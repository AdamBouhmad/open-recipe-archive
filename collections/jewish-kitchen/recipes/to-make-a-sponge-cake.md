## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 6 eggs well beaten
- 2 cups of flour
- rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Rasp the rind of a lemon
2. Beat the eggs well with the sugar
3. Place your dish in a pan of boiling water while you are beating them
4. Add the flour gently with the rasped lemon
5. Grease a tin well
6. Grease a thin paper and put round
7. Bake in a moderate oven
