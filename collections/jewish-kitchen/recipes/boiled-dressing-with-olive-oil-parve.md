## Ingredients

- three whole eggs
- two tablespoons olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- cayenne pepper
- one-half cup vinegar
- one teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Beat three whole eggs until very light, add two tablespoons of olive oil, stirring constantly, add a good pinch of salt, pepper, mustard and cayenne pepper. Heat one-half cup of vinegar with one teaspoon of sugar in it, stir while hot into the eggs and put it back on the stove in a double boiler or over hot water in another saucepan and stir until thick. Serve cold.
