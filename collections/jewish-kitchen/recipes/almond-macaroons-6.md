## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds ground fine
- one and one-half pounds of powdered sugar
- whites of five eggs
- grated rind of two lemons
- olive oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eggs to a snow
2. Add the sugar and the grated lemon rind and almonds
3. Mix it well together
4. Grease a very thin paper with olive oil
5. Sprinkle some powdered sugar over it
6. Place on a tin
7. Form the cakes and place them a little distance from each other
8. Bake in a very moderate oven
9. When done let them cool before you touch them
