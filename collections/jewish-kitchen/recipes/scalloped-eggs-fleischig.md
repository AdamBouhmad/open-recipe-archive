## Ingredients

- chopped tongue
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- a little parsley
- one tablespoon of melted fat
- soup stock
- eggs
- cracker dust

## Directions

1. Make a force-meat of chopped tongue, bread crumbs, pepper, salt, a little parsley, one tablespoon of melted fat, and soup stock enough to make a soft paste.
2. Half fill patty-pans with the mixture.
3. Break an egg carefully on the top of each.
4. Sprinkle with a little salt, pepper and cracker dust.
5. Put in the oven and bake about ten minutes.
6. Serve hot.
