## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- Two cups of sugar
- Four eggs, whites beaten separately
- One cup of milk
- Two teaspoons of baking-powder
- Three and one-half cups of flour
- Three large apples (Greenings preferred)
- The juice and peel of a lemon
- One cup of sugar
- One well-beaten egg

## Directions

1. Rub one cup of butter and two cups of sugar to a cream
2. Add four eggs, whites beaten separately
3. Add one cup of milk
4. Add two teaspoons of baking-powder and three and one-half cups of flour
5. Bake in layer tins
6. For filling: Pare and grate three large apples (Greenings preferred)
7. Add the juice and peel of a lemon, one cup of sugar and one well-beaten egg
8. Put in ingredients together and boil, stirring constantly until thick
9. Cool and fill in cake
