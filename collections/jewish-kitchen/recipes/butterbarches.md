## Ingredients

- one cake of compressed yeast
- one-half cup of lukewarm milk
- one teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of sugar
- eight cups of sifted flour
- about two cups milk
- one-half cup of sugar
- four tablespoons of butter
- the grated peel of a lemon
- two or three dozen raisins seeded
- two eggs well beaten

## Directions

1. Dissolve one cake of compressed yeast in one-half cup of lukewarm milk, add a teaspoon of salt, and a tablespoon of sugar and let it rise
2. Make a soft dough of eight cups of sifted flour and as much milk as is required to work it, about two cups
3. Add the yeast, one-half cup of sugar, four tablespoons of butter dissolved in the warm milk, the grated peel of a lemon, two or three dozen raisins seeded, and two eggs well beaten
4. Work this dough perfectly smooth with the palm of your hand, adding more flour if necessary
5. Work the dough as directed, set it aside covered until it is double the bulk of the original piece of dough
6. Then work again and divide the dough into two parts, and divide each of the pieces of dough into three parts
7. Work the six pieces of dough thoroughly and then roll each piece into a long strand; three of which are to be longer than the other three
8. Braid the three long strands into one braid (should be thicker in the centre than at the end), and braid the shorter strands into one braid and lay it on top
