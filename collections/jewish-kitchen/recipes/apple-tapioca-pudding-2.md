## Ingredients

- three-quarter cup of tapioca
- one quart of water
- sugar
- six apples
- nutmeg
- cream

## Directions

1. Soak three-quarter cup of tapioca and boil it in one quart of water until clear, sweetening to taste.
2. Pare and core six apples and place them in a baking dish.
3. Fill the cores with sugar, pour the tapioca around them and grate a little nutmeg over the top.
4. Cover and bake until the apples are soft.
5. Serve with cream.
