## Ingredients

- stale rye bread
- bunch of rhubarb
- baking-soda
- boiling water
- butter
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- nuts

## Directions

1. Grate some stale rye bread and take a bunch of rhubarb.
2. Cut rhubarb fine without peeling, put the cut rhubarb in a pan with a big pinch of baking-soda, and pour boiling water over to cover.
3. While that is steeping, grate the rye bread and butter pudding-form well, and put crumbs all over the pan about one-quarter inch deep.
4. Add one-half the rhubarb that has been well drained of the water.
5. Season with brown sugar, cinnamon, nuts and any other seasoning you like.
6. Then some more crumbs, and other one-half of rhubarb, and season as before.
7. Top with crumbs, put flakes of butter all over top.
8. Bake until done.
