## Ingredients

- 2 ounces unsweetened chocolate
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- stick cinnamon
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3 cups hot milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Scrape two ounces of unsweetened chocolate very fine
2. Add three tablespoons of sugar, small piece of stick cinnamon and one cup of boiling water
3. Stir over moderate heat until smooth
4. Add three cups of hot milk
5. Return to the fire for a minute, do not let it boil
6. Remove and add one teaspoon of vanilla
7. Beat with an egg-beater and serve
