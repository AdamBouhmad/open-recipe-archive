## Ingredients

- fine quinces
- sugar
- brandy
- white of eggs

## Directions

1. Boil and pulp some fine quinces
2. Pulp them through a sieve
3. Take equal weights of sugar and quince and put them in a preserving pan
4. Let it simmer gently, keeping stirring all the time to prevent it burning
5. When thoroughly done it looks quite glossy
6. Put it into saucers
7. When cold put over the top of the saucers fine tissue papers, dip them in brandy
8. Fasten the marmalade down with paper washed over with the white of eggs
9. When thoroughly cold it will cut out in pieces
