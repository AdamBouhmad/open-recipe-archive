## Ingredients

- rich fleishchig pie-crust
- one quart of berries
- four eggs
- one-half cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Make a rich fleishchig pie-crust and bake on the reverse side of pie-pan.
2. Pick a quart of berries, wash and drain, then sugar.
3. Take the yolks of four eggs beaten well with one-half cup of sugar and stir the beaten whites gently into this mixture.
4. Pour over strawberries.
5. Put in pie-crust and bake until brown.
