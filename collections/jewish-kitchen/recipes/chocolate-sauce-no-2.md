## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- two tablespoons of cornstarch
- one-half cup of cold milk
- three squares of chocolate
- three tablespoons of sugar
- three tablespoons of hot water
- whites of three eggs
- three-fourths of a cup of powdered sugar
- yolks of three eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Scald two cups of milk
2. add two tablespoons of cornstarch diluted with one-half cup of cold milk
3. cook ten minutes over boiling water
4. Melt three squares of chocolate over hot water
5. add three tablespoons of sugar and three tablespoons of hot water
6. stir until smooth
7. add to cooked mixture
8. Beat the whites of three eggs until stiff
9. add three-fourths of a cup of powdered sugar
10. add the yolks and stir into cooked mixture
11. cool and add vanilla
