## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- pinch of pepper
- one teaspoon of sugar
- one-half cup of milk
- one-half cup of cream
- yolks of five eggs
- whites of five eggs
- two tablespoons of butter

## Directions

1. Make a white sauce of butter blended with flour, salt, pepper and sugar
2. Add milk and cream
3. Beat the yolks of five eggs and stir them into the sauce
4. Add the stiffly beaten whites of the eggs, folding them in carefully
5. Melt two tablespoons of butter in the omelet pan
6. When hot put in the mixture and let it stand in a moderate heat for two minutes
7. Place in a hot oven and cook until set
8. Remove from the oven, turn on a hot platter and serve
