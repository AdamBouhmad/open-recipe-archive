## Ingredients

- one cup of oatmeal
- one tablespoon of butter
- one cup grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook one cup of oatmeal overnight
2. Just before serving add one tablespoon of butter and one cup grated cheese
3. Stir until the cheese is melted and serve at once
