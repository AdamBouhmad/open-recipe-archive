## Ingredients

- one large chicken
- four quarts of water
- parsley root
- an onion
- some asparagus
- salt
- the yolk of an egg
- one tablespoon of cold water

## Directions

1. Take one large chicken, cook with four quarts of water for two or three hours
2. Skim carefully, when it begins to boil add parsley root, an onion, some asparagus, cut into bits
3. Season with salt, strain
4. Beat up the yolk of an egg with one tablespoon of cold water, add to soup just before serving
5. Rice, barley, noodles or dumplings may be added
6. Make use of the chicken either for salad or stew
