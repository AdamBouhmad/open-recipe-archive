## Ingredients

- a tea-cup of biscuit powder
- beaten eggs
- white sifted sugar
- grated lemon peel
- a spoonful of orange flower-water

## Directions

1. Mix into a smooth batter a tea-cup of biscuit powder with beaten eggs, and sweeten with white sifted sugar
2. Add grated lemon peel, and a spoonful of orange flower-water, and fry of a light brown
3. The flavor may be varied by substituting a few beaten almonds, with one or two bitter, instead of the orange flower-water
