## Ingredients

- two pounds of plums, cherries, or red currants and raspberries
- a pint of water
- yolk of an egg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of plums, cherries, or red currants and raspberries and carefully pick and wash, and boil to a pulp with a pint of water.
2. Let it slightly cool and then stir in the beaten yolk of an egg and a little sugar.
3. Strain the soup, which should be served cold.
