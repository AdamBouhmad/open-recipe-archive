## Ingredients

- one pound of good light dough
- two eggs
- six ounces of butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Blend one pound of good light dough with two eggs, six ounces of butter, and add as much flour as may be needed to make the whole sufficiently dry.
2. Make it into the shape of a French roll, and cut off rather thin slices.
3. Place slices before the fire to rise.
4. Fry in oil.
5. Let them drain carefully.
6. When nearly cold dip each in very thick syrup flavored with essence of lemon.
