## Ingredients

- spinach
- drippings
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- soup stock or beef gravy
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of spinach about fifteen minutes
2. Drain thoroughly through a colander and chop extremely fine
3. Heat one tablespoon of drippings in a saucepan
4. Rub one tablespoon of flour in it
5. Add salt, pepper and ginger to taste
6. Add one cup of soup stock to the whole or some beef gravy
7. Put the spinach in the sauce
8. Let boil for five minutes
9. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs or use only the hard-boiled whites for decoration
10. Rub the yolks to a powder and mix through the spinach
