## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- potatoes
- butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Slit the pike open and cut it in pieces as near the shape of cutlets as possible
2. Season them with salt and pepper
3. Well beat in a plate 2 eggs
4. Have on another plate some crumbs of bread with chopped parsley
5. Dip the pieces in the eggs, then in the crumbs, then again in the eggs, and then in the crumbs
6. Fry them nicely
7. Dish them up in a dish that you have put in some potatoes mashed with butter, cutlet fashion
8. Garnish with slices of lemon and parsley
