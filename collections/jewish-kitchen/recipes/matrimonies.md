## Ingredients

- three cups of flour
- two scant cups of sour milk
- one teaspoon of salt
- yolks of three eggs
- whites of three eggs
- one level teaspoon of soda
- one teaspoon of flour
- butter or butter-substitute
- preserves

## Directions

1. Sift three cups of flour in a bowl
2. Pour in two scant cups of sour milk
3. Beat very thoroughly
4. Add one teaspoon of salt and the well-beaten yolks of three eggs
5. Mix well
6. Add the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs and one level teaspoon of soda sifted with one teaspoon of flour
7. Mix well and fry at once in very hot butter or butter-substitute
8. Baste the grease over them with a spoon until they are nicely browned
9. Serve with preserves
