## Ingredients

- one large Irish potato, or two medium-sized ones
- hot water
- white of one egg
- yolk of one egg

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and grate one large Irish potato, or two medium-sized ones.
2. Put it in a sieve and let hot water run over it until it is perfectly white.
3. Have the white of one egg beaten to a very stiff froth, then stir in the potatoes and twenty minutes before serving add it to the boiling soup.
4. Beat the yolk of one egg up in the soup tureen, and pour the hot soup over it, stirring carefully at first.
