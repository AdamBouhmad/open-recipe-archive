## Ingredients

- three stalks of celery
- one slice of onion
- three cups of milk
- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- one-fourth teaspoon of pepper
- one teaspoon of salt
- two-thirds of a cup
- one cup of cream

## Directions

1. Break three stalks of celery in one-inch pieces and pound in a mortar
2. Cook in double boiler with one slice of onion and three cups of milk for twenty minutes
3. Remove onion
4. heat two tablespoons of butter, add two tablespoons of flour, one-fourth teaspoon of pepper, one teaspoon of salt
5. add first two-thirds of a cup, and gradually the rest of the celery broth
6. add one cup of cream
7. cook until smooth and serve at once
