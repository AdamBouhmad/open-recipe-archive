## Ingredients

- two or three sticks of cinnamon
- salt
- pepper
- one-half teaspoon cloves
- one onion
- one bay leaf
- two cups of water
- three tablespoons of drippings
- five or six greening apples
- one medium-sized red cabbage
- two tablespoons sugar
- two tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Put two or three sticks of cinnamon, salt and pepper, one-half teaspoon cloves, one onion sliced thin, one bay leaf, two cups of water, three tablespoons of drippings in saucepan.
2. Add five or six greening apples, peeled and cut in quarters.
3. Put in one medium-sized red cabbage, cut in halves and then sliced very thin.
4. Cook three hours and then add two tablespoons each of sugar and vinegar; cook one minute more.
