## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, 8 to 10 lbs.
- force meat or veal stuffing
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Porg the leg
2. Make a nice stuffing of force meat or veal stuffing and put it in where the porging was taken from
3. Restore it to its original shape
4. Season well with pepper and salt
5. Sprinkle a little flour over it
6. Keep it well basted
7. Roast for two hours
8. Have a nice gravy for it
9. Send up with some currant jelly in a small glass dish
