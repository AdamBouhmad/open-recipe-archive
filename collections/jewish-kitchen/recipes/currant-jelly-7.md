## Ingredients

- Half ripe currants
- Granulated sugar, allowing one pint for every pint of juice

## Directions

1. Pick over half ripe currants, leaving stems on
2. Wash and place in preserving kettle
3. Pound vigorously with wooden masher until there is juice enough to boil
4. Boil slowly until fruit turns white and liquid drops slowly from the spoon
5. Stir to prevent scorching
6. Remove from fire
7. Take an enamelled cup and dip this mixture into the jelly bags, under which large bowls have been placed to catch the drip
8. Drip overnight
9. Next morning measure the juice
10. For every pint allow a pint of granulated sugar, which is put in a flat pan
11. Put juice in kettle and allow to come to boiling point
12. Place sugar in oven and heat
13. When juice boils add sugar
