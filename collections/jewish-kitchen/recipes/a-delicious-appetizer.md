## Ingredients

- slices of delicately browned toast
- several large, firm tomatoes
- one green pepper
- store cheese
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Place a slice of tomato on each slice of toast and season with salt and pepper and a dot of butter
2. Place several long, curly strips of pepper around the tomato, and cover with a thin slice of the cheese
3. Place in the oven until the cheese is melted
4. Serve piping hot
