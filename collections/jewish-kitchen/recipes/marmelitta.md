## Ingredients

- two cups of coarse cornmeal
- four cups of cold water
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- butter
- sharp cheese or pot cheese or cream cheese

## Directions

1. Take two cups of coarse cornmeal and four cups of cold water, put on to boil
2. Add one-half teaspoon of salt
3. Stir the cornmeal continually and when done place on platter
4. Spread with butter, sharp cheese or any cheese such as pot or cream cheese
5. To be eaten warm
