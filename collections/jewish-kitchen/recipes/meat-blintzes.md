## Ingredients

- potato flour
- eggs
- water
- fat
- meat from soup
- two small onions
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Use the same pancake batter as for Prune Blintzes
2. Fry two small onions with a little fat and chop with the meat
3. Add two eggs, salt and pepper to taste
4. Fill pancakes with meat mixture
5. Fold and fry
