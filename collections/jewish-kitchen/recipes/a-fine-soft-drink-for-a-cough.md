## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pint of new milk
- a beaten new laid egg
- a spoonful of capillaire
- a spoonful of rose water

## Directions

1. Add to a quarter of a pint of new milk warmed a beaten new laid egg with a spoonful of capillaire and the same of rose water
