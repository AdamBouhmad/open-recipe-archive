## Ingredients

- 1 lb. melted fat (veal or beef)
- 2 tablespoonfuls of oil
- 1 lb. flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Let the melted fat get warm and soft like butter, put with 2 tablespoonfuls of oil, mix it well and beat it till it is white as snow and just the consistency of butter
2. Place in a basin 1 lb. of flour to make puff-paste, it must be the best
3. Take up the lb. of fat you have beaten in the lump, lay it on the flour, turn it over to cover it with flour, lay it on the paste board
4. Make a paste with the remainder of the flour with a little cold water, not harder or softer than the fat
5. Roll it out on the board, then take the lump of fat, lay it on top and roll it over the paste
6. Sprinkle a little flour over, then fold it up on the board and cover it with the basin you made it in, and let it be for one hour
7. If in summer it must be put in a very cold place for the fat not to melt; if in winter, you can leave it in the kitchen, as the fat must not get hard
8. Be very particular not to handle it too much, and as light as you can; before beginning it in the summer let the cold water run well over your hands
9. Sometimes in the summer you have to put it on ice to get it the proper firmness
10. After it has laid for an hour, put it on your paste board, roll it out lightly 3 times
