## Ingredients

- fresh figs
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Lay fresh figs in water overnight.
2. Then simmer in water enough to cover them until tender, and spread upon dishes to cool.
3. Make a syrup of a pound of sugar to every pound of fruit.
4. Allow a small teacup of water to a pound of sugar.
