## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup of sifted powdered sugar
- one cup of sifted flour
- one-half teaspoon of baking-powder
- three tablespoons of water
- one-fourth pound of almonds
- three tablespoons of white sugar
- whites of three eggs
- one pound of pulverized sugar
- rose-water

## Directions

1. Beat up four eggs with one cup of sifted powdered sugar
2. Beat until it looks like a heavy batter
3. Stir one cup of sifted flour with one-half teaspoon of baking-powder
4. Stir it into batter gradually and lightly, adding three tablespoons of water
5. Bake in jelly tin
6. For filling: Scald one-fourth pound of almonds by pouring boiling water over them and remove skins
7. Put them on a pie plate and set them in the oven to brown slightly
8. Melt three tablespoons of white sugar, without adding water, stirring it all the while
9. Stir up the almonds in this
10. Remove them from the fire and lay on a platter separately to cool
11. Make an icing of the whites of three eggs, beaten very stiff, with one pound of pulverized sugar, and flavor with rose-water
12. Spread this upon layers and cover each layer with almonds
13. When finished frost the whole cake, decorating with almonds
