## Ingredients

- eight medium-sized apples
- one-half pound raisins
- one-half pound currants
- one-half pound sugar
- citron peel
- two or three cloves
- one teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
- puff paste
- two tablespoons of melted butter
- one teacup of thick syrup
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Chop finely apples, raisins, currants, sugar, citron peel, cloves and cinnamon to make a mince-meat
2. Cut puff paste into little triangles
3. Fill with the mince, turning the corners of the paste over it to make little puffs
4. Place these closely together on a buttered baking-dish until it is full
5. Mix melted butter with thick syrup flavored with essence of lemon
6. Pour it over the puffs
7. Bake until done in a rather slow oven
