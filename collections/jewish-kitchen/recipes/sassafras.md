## Ingredients

- sassafras wood
- stick liquorice
- aniseed
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Take some of the sassafras wood, with some of the stick liquorice and some aniseed tied in a piece of muslin
2. Put it all in a jug
3. Pour over boiling water
