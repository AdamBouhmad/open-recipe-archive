## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. ground rice
- a cup of cold milk
- 1 pint of milk
- 1/2 lb. of fresh butter
- 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar
- a tablespoonful of orange-flower water
- the yolks of 3 eggs
- the whites of 2 eggs
- 2 oz. of lemon and orange peel
- good puff paste
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Take a 1/2 lb. ground rice mixed with a cup of cold milk
2. Boil 1 pint of milk, stir in the rice, let it boil till it thickens
3. Put it out to get cold
4. Put in 1/2 lb. of fresh butter, 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar, a tablespoonful of orange-flower water, the yolks of 3 eggs, and the whites of 2
5. Add 2 oz. of lemon and orange peel cut in slices
6. Make a good puff paste, line the dish
7. Put in the rice, put over lemon and orange peel
8. Crossbar it with strips of paste, and between each put half of a blanched almond
9. Bake in a quick oven
