## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound salted sardellen
- one-half pound sweet butter
- toasted cracker or bread
- grated yellow and grated white of egg

## Directions

1. Take one-quarter pound salted sardellen and soak in water over night
2. Bone the next morning, put in cloth and press until dry
3. chop very fine, almost to a paste
4. take one-half pound sweet butter, stir to a cream and add the sardellen
5. Serve on toasted cracker or bread
6. Sprinkle with the grated yellow and grated white of egg
