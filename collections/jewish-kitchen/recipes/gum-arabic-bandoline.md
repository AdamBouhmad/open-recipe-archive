## Ingredients

- a tea-spoonful of gum arabic
- a little Irish moss
- half a pint of water

## Directions

1. Take a tea-spoonful of gum arabic with a little Irish moss
2. boil them in half a pint of water till half is boiled away
3. strain and perfume
