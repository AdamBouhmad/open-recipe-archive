## Ingredients

- fresh haddock
- forcemeat
- flour
- warmed butter
- pepper
- salt
- capers

## Directions

1. Carefully clean a fresh haddock, and fill it with a fine forcemeat, and sew it in securely
2. Give the fish a dredging of flour, and pour on warmed butter
3. Sprinkle it with pepper and salt
4. Set it to bake in a Dutch-oven before the fire, basting it, from time to time, with butter warmed, and capers
5. It should be of a rich dark brown
6. Dredge two or three times with flour while at the fire
7. The continual bastings will produce sufficient sauce to serve with it without any other being added
