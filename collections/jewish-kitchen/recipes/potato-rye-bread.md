## Ingredients

- one quart of potatoes (diced)
- boiling water
- one-quarter cup of salt
- one and one-quarter cups of cornmeal
- two tablespoons of any kind of fat
- two cups of flour
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one cake of yeast
- one cup of lukewarm water
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Cook one quart of potatoes diced, in boiling water until tender
2. Strain, reserving potato water
3. Measure and add enough more water to make three cups
4. Let come to a boil, add one-quarter cup of salt, and very gradually one and one-quarter cups of cornmeal
5. Cook two minutes, stirring constantly until thick
6. Remove from fire, add two tablespoons of any kind of fat, the potatoes riced or mashed and when cooled two cups of flour
7. Add one tablespoon of sugar and one cake of yeast dissolved in one cup of lukewarm water
8. Mix and knead to a stiff dough adding wheat flour to keep it from sticking
9. Cover, set aside in a warm place overnight, or until double its bulk
10. Shape into four loaves, let rise again
11. Glaze with egg diluted with water before putting in the oven
12. Bake in a moderate oven one hour or more, until well done
