## Ingredients

- coffee cake dough
- melted butter
- one cup of sugar
- one-half pound of ground poppy seed
- one-half cup of water

## Directions

1. Roll coffee cake dough out quite thin
2. spread with melted butter (a brush is best for this purpose)
3. Let it rise a little while
4. then sprinkle well with one cup of sugar
5. add one-half pound of ground poppy seed moistened with one-half cup of water
6. cut into strips about an inch wide and four inches long
7. roll and put in a well-buttered pan to rise, leaving enough space between each and brush with butter
8. Bake in moderate oven at first, then increase the heat; bake slowly
