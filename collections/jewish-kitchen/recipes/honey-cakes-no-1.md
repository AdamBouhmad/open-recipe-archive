## Ingredients

- one pound of real honey, not jar
- one cup of granulated sugar
- four eggs
- one tablespoon of allspice
- three tablespoons of salad-oil
- four cups of flour, well sifted
- three teaspoons of baking-powder

## Directions

1. Warm up or heat honey, not hot, just warm.
2. Rub yolks well with sugar, beat whites to a froth.
3. Mix ingredients, add flour and bake in moderate oven for one hour.
