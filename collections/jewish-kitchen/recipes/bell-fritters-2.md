## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cup sweet milk
- three cups sifted flour
- three teaspoons baking-powder

## Directions

1. Stir three eggs until very light
2. Stir in one cup of sweet milk
3. Sift in three cups sifted flour
4. Beat for ten minutes
5. Add three teaspoons of baking-powder
6. Fry by spoonfuls in hot oil
7. Serve with any sweet sauce
