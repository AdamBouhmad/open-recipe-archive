## Ingredients

- a small piece of vanille
- 1/2 a stick
- 2 pints of milk
- 1 pint of cream
- sugar to sweeten

## Directions

1. Pound a small piece of vanille, 1/2 a stick
2. Boil it in 2 pints of milk
3. When cold, strain it and add it to a pint of cream
4. Sweeten it to flavour
5. Put it in the freezer and work it
