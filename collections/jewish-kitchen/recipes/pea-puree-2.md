## Ingredients

- two cups of dried peas
- three pints of fresh, cold water
- one and one-half teaspoons of salt
- one-half teaspoon of sugar
- white pepper
- one-eighth of a pound of hard brisket fat
- one large onion

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash two cups of dried peas
2. Soak them overnight or for several hours in cold water
3. Put them on to boil in three pints of fresh, cold water and let them simmer until dissolved
4. Keep well scraped from the sides of the kettle
5. When soft, rub through a strainer
6. Add a little boiling water or soup stock
7. Add one and one-half teaspoons of salt, one-half teaspoon of sugar and a speck of white pepper
8. Beat the mixture well
9. Put hard brisket fat chopped in small pieces, about one-eighth of a pound, into a spider and cook until a light yellow
10. Add a large onion cut in dice and continue cooking with the fat until brown
11. Serve the puree like mashed potatoes
12. Pour the onion and fat over it before serving
13. Serve hot
