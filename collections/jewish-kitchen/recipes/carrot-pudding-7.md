## Ingredients

- one and a half cups of powdered sugar
- yolks of eight eggs
- one and a half cups peeled and grated raw carrots
- one cup of grated almonds
- rind of half a lemon chopped finely
- one tablespoon of wine
- whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Beat one and a half cups of powdered sugar and the yolks of eight eggs
2. Take one and a half cups peeled and grated raw carrots and stir all together
3. Add one cup of grated almonds, the rind of half a lemon chopped finely, one tablespoon of wine, and last the beaten whites of the eggs
4. Bake in a well-buttered and flour-sprinkled form at least one hour in a slow oven
