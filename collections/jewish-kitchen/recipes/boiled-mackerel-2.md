## Ingredients

- mackerel
- salt
- fennel
- fennel sauce
- apple sauce or gooseberry sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the mackerel well
2. put them on in cold water with salt
3. let them boil very gently
4. dish them on a fish napkin
5. garnish with fennel
6. serve with fennel sauce, and also a little apple or gooseberry sauce
