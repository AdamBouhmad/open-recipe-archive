## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread into cubes, place in pan and brown in the oven; or butter the bread, cut into cubes and then brown the same way.
2. Fry small cubes of stale bread in deep hot fat until brown or fry them in a little butter or fat in a hot spider until brown.
