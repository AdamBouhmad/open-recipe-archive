## Ingredients

- Three or four pounds of boned brisket
- a pint of water
- pepper and salt
- a tooth of garlic
- a small onion
- a dozen artichokes
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a little grated nutmeg
- little chopped parsley
- the juice of a lemon
- a little dried saffron

## Directions

1. Put boned brisket into a stewpan with a pint of water, pepper, salt, a tooth of garlic chopped fine, a small onion.
2. Put it on to simmer, it will take 3 hours.
3. Half an hour before it is done have ready peeled and washed a dozen artichokes as near one size as possible.
4. Let them drain from the water and put them in with the meat.
5. Rub in a basin a tablespoonful of flour, a little grated nutmeg, little chopped parsley, the juice of a lemon.
6. Turn it into a saucepan and mix it well with the liquor.
7. Colour with a little dried saffron.
8. Dish it up nicely, the meat in the centre, the artichokes round.
