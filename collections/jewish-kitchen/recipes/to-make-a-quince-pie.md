## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 quinces
- lemon rind
- ground cloves
- 1 lb. of moist sugar
- 2 eggs
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Peel and core 4 or 5 quinces, boil them with the rind of a lemon chopped fine, a little ground cloves, and 1 lb. of moist sugar
2. When done, beat it up to a pulp
3. Mix with the yolks of 2 eggs well beaten
4. Line a pie-dish with puff-paste
5. Lay in the fruit quite cold, cover over
6. Bake in a quick oven
7. Remember always to dust your pies with sifted sugar, and damp with white of egg and water
