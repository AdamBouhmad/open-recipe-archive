## Ingredients

- one cake of cream cheese
- one-quarter of a pound of chopped figs
- one-quarter of a pound of chopped walnuts
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Take one cake of cream cheese, one-quarter of a pound of chopped figs, one-quarter of a pound of chopped walnuts.
2. Roll into balls and serve on lettuce leaves.
