## Ingredients

- eggs
- noodle dough
- chicken fat
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough with as many eggs as desired
2. Roll out somewhat thin
3. Cut in strips four inches long by one inch wide
4. Have a skillet half full of boiling hot chicken fat
5. Drop in the strips, a few at a time
6. Baste with the hot grease until brown on both sides
7. Remove to a platter
8. Sprinkle generously with powdered sugar and cinnamon
9. Serve
