## Ingredients

- sieved meal
- eggs
- good sized apples
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a batter of some sieved meal and eggs
2. Peel some good sized apples
3. Cut them in nice slices
4. Take out the centre where the core is
5. Dip the slices in the batter both sides
6. Fry them
7. Arrange them prettily in a dish
8. Sprinkle over some cinnamon and sugar
