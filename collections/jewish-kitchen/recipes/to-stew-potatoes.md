## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 large potatoes
- 1 onion
- 2 oz. of butter
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- flour
- 1/2 pint of cold milk
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel 4 or 5 large potatoes, cut them in square pieces.
2. Chop an onion very fine, put it in a saucepan with 2 oz. of butter, let it fry.
3. Add the pieces of potatoes, with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg.
4. Sprinkle over some flour very lightly, and stir it all together with a spoon.
5. Pour over 1/2 pint of cold milk.
6. Set them on to cook, let them simmer till done, be careful the potatoes do not break.
7. When done the sauce will look like cream.
8. Turn them into a vegetable dish and sprinkle over chopped parsley; send up very hot.
