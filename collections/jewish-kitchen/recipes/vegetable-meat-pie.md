## Ingredients

- one-half cup of Lima beans
- one-quarter cup of almonds
- one-quarter cup of peanuts
- four potatoes
- hard-boiled egg
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- four tablespoons of hot milk
- one-quarter cup of flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Soak one-half cup of Lima beans overnight
2. In the morning let them boil rapidly for one-half hour
3. Drain, slip the beans from their skins and split them in halves
4. Blanch one-quarter cup of almonds and chop them with one-quarter cup of peanuts
5. Boil four potatoes, and when done cut two of them into small cubes
6. Mash the remaining two and use them for a dough, adding four tablespoons of hot milk, a little salt and one-quarter cup of flour
7. Put a layer of beans in the bottom of the baking-dish, a sprinkling of nuts, a little hard-boiled egg, then the potato blocks and one-half tablespoon each of chopped parsley and chopped onion, one-half teaspoon of salt and one-half saltspoon of pepper and so on until the material is all used
8. Roll out the potato dough the size of the baking-dish
9. Put it over the dish, brush with milk and bake half an hour in a moderately quick oven
