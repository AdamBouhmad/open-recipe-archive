## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Select fine, smooth potatoes and boil them about twenty minutes
2. Drain off the water and remove the skins
3. Pack in a buttered dish
4. Lay a small piece of butter on each potato
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Sprinkle fine bread crumbs over all with a few tablespoons of cream
7. Bake until a nice light brown
8. Serve in the same dish and garnish with parsley
