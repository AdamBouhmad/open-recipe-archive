## Ingredients

- five apples
- sugar
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Quarter and core five apples without paring
2. Put into a frying-pan one cup of sugar, one tablespoon of butter and three tablespoons of water
3. Let this melt
4. Lay in the apples with the skin up
5. Cover and fry slowly until brown
