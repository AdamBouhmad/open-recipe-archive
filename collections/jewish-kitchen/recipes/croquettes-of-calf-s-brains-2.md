## Ingredients

- brains
- salt water
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- beaten egg
- one-half cup of bread crumbs
- fat

## Directions

1. Lay the brains in salt water an hour, or until they look perfectly white
2. Take out one at a time
3. Pat with your hands to loosen the outer skin and pull it off
4. Beat or rub them to a smooth paste with a wooden spoon
5. Season with salt and pepper and a very little mace
6. Add a beaten egg and about one-half cup of bread crumbs
7. Heat fat in a spider
8. Fry large spoonfuls of this mixture in it
