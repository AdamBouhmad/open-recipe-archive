## Ingredients

- one ounce of coffee berries
- half a pint of water
- an equal quantity of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Grind the berries at the instant of using them in the proportion of one ounce to half a pint of water
2. Put the powder into a coffee biggin and press it down closely
3. Pour over a little water sufficient to moisten it
4. Add the remainder by degrees with the water perfectly boiling all the time
5. Let it run quite through before the top of the percolator is taken off
6. Serve with an equal quantity of boiling milk
