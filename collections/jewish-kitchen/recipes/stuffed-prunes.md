## Ingredients

- One pound of best prunes
- Sherry
- Browned almonds
- Marshmallow or another prune
- Granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Take one pound of best prunes, stone and soak in sherry for about an hour (do not cover with the wine)
2. Fill prunes with one large browned almond and one-half marshmallow or with another prune
3. Roll in granulated sugar
4. When all are finished, put in oven for two or three minutes
