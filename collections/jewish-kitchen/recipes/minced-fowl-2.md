## Ingredients

- remnants of cold fowl
- parsley
- onion
- rind of lemon
- a little marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- ground mace
- bones and remnants
- the juice of half a lemon
- sippets of toast

## Directions

1. The remnants of cold fowl chopped very fine with parsley, onion, rind of lemon, add a little marjoram, salt, pepper, ground mace.
2. When it is well mixed put on all the bones and remnants, stew them down to half a pint, strain it, put it in a small stewpan.
3. Put in your mince, stir in the juice of half a lemon, let it simmer till it thickens.
4. Have some sippets of toast made, place the mince in the dish and the sippets round.
5. Dish up very hot.
