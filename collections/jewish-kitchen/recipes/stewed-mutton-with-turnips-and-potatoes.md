## Ingredients

- 3 lb. of best end neck of mutton
- pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- three large turnips
- large onion
- 3 or 4 potatoes
- flour
- cold water
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Put 3 lb. of best end neck of mutton, cut off the fat into a stewpan with a pint of water, salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg
2. Peel three large turnips and cut in quarters
3. Slice a large onion
4. Let it all simmer for two hours very gently
5. Half an hour before the meat is done peel and halve 3 or 4 potatoes
6. Lay them on top so that the steam from the stew will cook them
7. Be sure that they do not break when ready for dinner
8. Put the mutton in the dish, turnips and potatoes round
9. Skim off the fat
10. Rub in a small basin a spoonful of flour with a little cold water
11. Turn it into the stewpan and let it have one boil
12. Put it over the mutton and vegetables
13. Chop a little green parsley and throw over the whole
14. Send up very hot with hot plates
