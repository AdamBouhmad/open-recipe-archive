## Ingredients

- two cups of brown sugar
- one cup of boiling water
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir two cups of brown sugar, one cup of boiling water, and cinnamon to taste together in a saucepan until the sugar is dissolved
2. Let the mixture simmer slowly until it thickens
