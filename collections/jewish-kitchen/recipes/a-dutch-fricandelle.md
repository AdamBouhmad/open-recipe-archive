## Ingredients

- two pounds of dressed fish
- two or three anchovies
- crumb of a French roll
- milk
- three eggs
- butter
- raspings
- anchovy sauce
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and bones from the fish, cut in small pieces with two or three anchovies, and season well
2. Soak the crumb of a French roll in milk
3. Beat it up with the fish and three eggs
4. Butter a mould and sprinkle it with raspings
5. Place the fish in the mould and bake it
6. When done, turn out and serve either dry or with anchovy sauce
7. If served dry, finely grated crumbs of bread should be sprinkled thickly over it, and it should be placed for a few minutes before the fire to brown
