## Ingredients

- cold cod
- parsley
- marjoram
- a little rind of lemon
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- a few crumbs of bread
- an egg
- fine bread crumbs
- oil
- slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Take all the white pieces of flesh free from bone from any cold cod that may be left
2. Chop it very finely with parsley, marjoram, and a little rind of lemon
3. Season with pepper, salt, grated nutmeg
4. Mix them up with a few crumbs of bread and an egg
5. Roll them into balls the size of a pippin apple
6. Have a plate with fine bread crumbs, and one with eggs
7. Have your pan with oil, and when boiling hot put the croquettes in
8. Fry a light brown
9. Serve this on a fish napkin garnished with parsley and slices of lemon
