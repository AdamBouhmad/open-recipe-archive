## Ingredients

- heart
- liver
- gizzard
- neck
- cold water

## Directions

1. Wash them; put them in cold water and cook until tender.
2. This will take several hours.
3. Serve with the chicken; or mash the liver, mince the heart and gizzard, and add them to the brown sauce.
4. Save the stock in which they are cooked for making the sauce.
