## Ingredients

- ratafia cakes
- wine
- a little brandy
- a thick custard
- white of eggs
- wine and white sugar

## Directions

1. Soak ratafia cakes in wine with a little brandy
2. Pour over a thick custard
3. Cover with a froth of the white of eggs, flavored with wine and sweetened with white sugar
