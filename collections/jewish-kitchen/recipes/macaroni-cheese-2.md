## Ingredients

- three ounces of macaroni
- salted water
- one-half pint of milk
- one-half pint of thick white sauce
- two ounces of grated cheese
- two well-beaten eggs
- browned bread crumbs
- stewed tomatoes and mushrooms

## Directions

1. Break three ounces of macaroni into small pieces
2. Boil in rapidly boiling salted water
3. When tender drain off the water and add half a pint of milk
4. Cook slowly till the macaroni has absorbed most of the milk
5. To half a pint of thick white sauce add two ounces of grated cheese and mix with the macaroni
6. Add two well-beaten eggs
7. Butter a pudding mold, sprinkle it with browned bread crumbs and pour in the macaroni mixture
8. Steam gently for about half an hour
9. Turn out and fill the centre with stewed tomatoes and mushrooms
