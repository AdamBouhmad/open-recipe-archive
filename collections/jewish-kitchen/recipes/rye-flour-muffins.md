## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of rye flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of baking soda
- one-half cup of molasses
- one well-beaten egg
- one-quarter cup of chopped raisins
- four tablespoons of melted shortening

## Directions

1. Sift one and one-half cups of rye flour with one-half teaspoon of salt and one teaspoon of baking soda
2. Add one-half cup of molasses and one well-beaten egg or one-half cup of water if the egg is omitted
3. Add one-quarter cup of chopped raisins and four tablespoons of melted shortening
4. Bake in muffin-pans in rather hot oven twenty-five minutes
5. Fill pans three-fourths full
