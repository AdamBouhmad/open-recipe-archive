## Ingredients

- about half a pound of finely grated cocoa nut
- half a pound of fresh butter
- half a pound of white sugar
- six whites of eggs
- rich puff paste

## Directions

1. Take about half a pound of finely grated cocoa nut
2. Beat up to a cream half a pound of fresh butter and add it to the cocoa nut
3. Add half a pound of white sugar and six whites of eggs beaten to a froth
4. Mix the whole well together
5. Bake in a dish lined with a rich puff paste
