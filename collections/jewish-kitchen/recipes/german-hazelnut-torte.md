## Ingredients

- yolks of eight eggs
- one-half pound of granulated sugar
- whites of eggs
- a small pinch of salt
- one-half pound of grated hazelnuts
- grated rind of one-half lemon

## Directions

1. Beat together for twenty minutes until very light the yolks of eight eggs with one-half pound of granulated sugar
2. Add the very stiffly-beaten whites of eggs
3. Place the bowl in which it has been stirred over a boiler in which water is boiling on the stove
4. Stir continually but slowly until all the batter is well warmed but not too hot
5. Add a small pinch of salt and one-half pound of grated hazelnuts
6. Add the nuts gradually and mix well
7. Pour into a greased spring form
8. Bake very slowly
9. Ice with boiled icing
