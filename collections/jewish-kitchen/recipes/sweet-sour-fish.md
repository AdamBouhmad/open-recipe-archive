## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- one and one-half cups water
- one cup vinegar
- one onion
- one dozen raisins
- one lemon
- two bay leaves
- six cloves
- three tablespoons granulated sugar
- two tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Cut up and salt the fish
2. Put on fish kettle with one and one-half cups of water and one cup of vinegar
3. Add one onion cut in round slices, one dozen raisins, one lemon cut in round slices, two bay leaves, six cloves
4. When this mixture begins to boil, lay in fish and cook thoroughly
5. When done remove fish to platter
6. Put liquor back on stove
7. Add three tablespoons of granulated sugar which has been melted and browned in a pie plate without water
8. Add two tablespoons of flour which has been rubbed smooth with a little water
9. Let boil well and pour over fish
10. If not sweet enough add more sugar
11. Serve cold
