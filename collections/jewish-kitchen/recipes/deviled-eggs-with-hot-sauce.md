## Ingredients

- six hard-boiled eggs
- one dessertspoon of melted butter
- Cayenne pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- a pinch of Cayenne pepper
- one pint of milk
- yolk of one egg
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Take six hard-boiled eggs, cut lengthwise
2. Remove yolk and add to same: one dessertspoon of melted butter, Cayenne pepper, salt and chopped parsley
3. Mash this mixture very fine
4. Refill the whites of the eggs
5. Turn over on platter
6. For sauce: put one tablespoon of butter, one tablespoon of flour, a pinch of Cayenne pepper, salt and one pint of milk in a pan
7. Stir this mixture continually until it thickens
8. Beat the yolk of one egg and pour the hot gravy over the same
9. Dress with chopped parsley and eat very hot
10. Sherry wine can be added if desired
