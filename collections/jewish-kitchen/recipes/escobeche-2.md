## Ingredients

- some cold fried fish
- half a pint of vinegar
- two table spoonsful of water
- one table spoonful of oil
- a little grated ginger
- allspice
- cayenne pepper
- two bay leaves
- a little salt
- a table spoonful of lemon juice
- sliced onions

## Directions

1. Take some cold fried fish, place it in a deep pan
2. Boil half a pint of vinegar with two table spoonsful of water, and one of oil, a little grated ginger, allspice, cayenne pepper, two bay leaves, a little salt, and a table spoonful of lemon juice, with sliced onions
3. When boiling, pour it over the fish
4. Cover the pan, and let it stand twenty-four hours before serving
