## Ingredients

- banana
- ice-cream
- chopped walnuts
- pistachio nuts
- marshmallows

## Directions

1. Cut a banana in four strips
2. Cross two over two in basket-shape
3. Fill centre square with a tablespoon of ice-cream
4. Sprinkle over all some chopped walnuts, pistachio nuts and marshmallows, cut in strips
