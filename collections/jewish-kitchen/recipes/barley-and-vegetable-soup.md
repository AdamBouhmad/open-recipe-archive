## Ingredients

- half cup of coarse barley
- two quarts of water
- two onions
- a bunch of carrots
- parsley
- two turnips
- one green pepper
- six tomatoes
- a few green peas
- lima beans
- two ears of corn
- pepper and salt
- a small piece of butter
- quarter teaspoon of sage and thyme

## Directions

1. Take a half cup of coarse barley and two quarts of water.
2. Let boil for one hour and skim.
3. Then add two onions, a bunch of carrots, parsley, two turnips, one green pepper and six tomatoes (all chopped fine).
4. Add a few green peas, lima beans, two ears of corn cut from cob; pepper and salt to taste.
5. Cook for one hour or more until done.
6. Then add a small piece of butter, quarter teaspoon of sage and thyme, if you like, and if soup is too thick add more water.
