## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of ground almonds
- 1/2 lb. sifted sugar
- 6 oz. flour
- 7 eggs
- raspings of 2 lemons
- oil of sweet almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put the almonds in a pan, with the sugar and yolks of the eggs
2. Mix it all together till it comes like a cream
3. Beat the whites to a strong snow
4. Mix it with the other
5. Put in the flour as light as you can
6. Grease your tin with the oil of sweet almonds
7. Sprinkle a little sugar on the tin
8. Bake it in rather a slow oven
