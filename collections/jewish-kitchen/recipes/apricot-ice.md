## Ingredients

- one dozen apricots
- kernels
- one pint of water
- juice of a lemon
- one-half pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and grate one dozen apricots
2. Blanch a few of the kernels
3. Pound them and add to the grated fruit
4. Pour a pint of water over them
5. Add the juice of a lemon also
6. Let them stand for an hour and strain
7. Add one-half pound of sugar just before freezing
