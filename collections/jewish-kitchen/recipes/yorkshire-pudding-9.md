## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- four eggs or two eggs
- one pint of milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix into a smooth batter half a pound of flour, four eggs, if intended to be rich, otherwise two, a pint of milk, and a little salt
2. it should be about an inch thick
3. it can be made with or without milk by using a greater proportion of eggs, but it is not so good
