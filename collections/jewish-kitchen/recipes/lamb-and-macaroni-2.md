## Ingredients

- one can of concentrated tomato sauce
- one quart of water
- two medium-sized onions
- olive oil or drippings
- one and one-half pounds of lean neck of lamb

## Directions

1. Dilute one can of concentrated tomato sauce with one quart of water
2. Mince two medium-sized onions very fine and fry slowly in olive oil or drippings until they are a golden brown, and add to tomatoes
3. Fry one and one-half pounds of lean neck of lamb in a little drippings until the meat is nicely browned all over and add to the tomatoes
