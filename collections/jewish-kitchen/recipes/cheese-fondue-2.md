## Ingredients

- one-half cup of bread crumbs
- one scant cup of milk
- a speck of bicarbonate of soda
- hot water
- one egg yolk and white beaten separately
- one-half cup of dry cheese grated
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt
- pepper
- dry crumbs moistened with butter

## Directions

1. Soak one-half cup of bread crumbs in one scant cup of milk
2. Dissolve a speck of bicarbonate of soda in a drop of hot water and add to the milk
3. Add one egg, yolk and white beaten separately
4. Add one-half cup of dry cheese grated
5. Add one tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste
6. Beat well, pour into a well buttered baking dish
7. Strew dry crumbs moistened with butter over the top
8. Bake in a hot oven until light brown
9. Serve at once in the dish in which it is baked
