## Ingredients

- shoulder
- vinegar
- fat or goose oil
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- onion
- cloves
- bay leaf
- tomatoes
- soup stock
- white wine
- potato pancakes

## Directions

1. Take a shoulder, have the bone taken out and then pound the meat well with a mallet
2. Lay it in vinegar for twenty-four hours
3. Heat some fat or goose oil in a deep pan or kettle which has a cover that fits air tight and lay the meat in the hot fat
4. Sprinkle the upper side with salt, pepper and ginger
5. Put an onion in with the meat; stick about half a dozen cloves in the onion and add one bay leaf
6. Turn the meat over and sprinkle the other side with salt, pepper and ginger
7. Cut up one or two tomatoes and pour some soup stock over all, and a dash of white wine
8. Cover closely and stew very slowly for three or four hours, turning the meat now and then
9. Do not add any water
10. Make enough potato pancakes to serve one or two to each person with Wiener Braten
