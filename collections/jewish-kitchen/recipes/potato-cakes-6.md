## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Take cold mashed potatoes or cold baked or boiled potatoes that have been mashed and seasoned
2. Roll into balls, dusting the hands well with flour first
3. Flatten into cakes
4. Saute in butter or place on a buttered tin with a small piece of butter on the top of each
5. Bake in a hot oven until golden brown
