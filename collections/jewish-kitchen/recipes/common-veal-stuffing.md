## Ingredients

- equal quantities of finely shred suet
- grated crumbs of bread
- chopped sweet herbs
- grated lemon peel
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Have equal quantities of finely shred suet and grated crumbs of bread
2. Add chopped sweet herbs, grated lemon peel, pepper, and salt
3. Pound it in a mortar
