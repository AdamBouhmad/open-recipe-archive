## Ingredients

- two pounds fat brisket of beef
- salt and pepper
- two bunches carrots
- boiling water
- two tablespoons fat
- two tablespoons flour
- one cup carrot and meat liquid

## Directions

1. Salt and pepper two pounds of fat brisket of beef and let stand several hours.
2. Wash and scrape two bunches of carrots and cut in small cubes.
3. Place in kettle with meat, cover with boiling water and cook several hours or until the meat and carrots are tender, and the water is half boiled away.
4. Heat two tablespoons of fat in a spider, let brown slightly, add two tablespoons of flour and gradually one cup of carrot and meat liquid.
5. Place in kettle with meat and carrots and boil until carrots become browned.
