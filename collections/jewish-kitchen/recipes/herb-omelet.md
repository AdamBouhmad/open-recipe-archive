## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two tablespoons of cold water
- a quarter of a teaspoon of salt
- pepper
- a teaspoon of chopped parsley
- a quarter of a teaspoon of grated onion
- a teaspoon of fine butter

## Directions

1. Take six eggs and beat well in a bowl.
2. Add two tablespoons of cold water and a quarter of a teaspoon of salt, a pinch of pepper, a teaspoon of chopped parsley, a quarter of a teaspoon of grated onion and a teaspoon of fine butter, shaved in little pieces.
3. Mix well with a wooden spoon.
