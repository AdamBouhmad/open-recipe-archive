## Ingredients

- yolks of five eggs
- one-half pound of sugar
- one tablespoon of water
- vanilla
- one-half pound of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- one-half of five cents worth anise seeds
- beaten whites of five eggs

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of five eggs, one-half pound of sugar, one tablespoon of water, vanilla, one-half pound of flour, one teaspoon of baking-powder, one-half of five cents worth anise seeds, and the beaten whites of the eggs
2. Butter square-tins and bake
3. When cooled cut in strips one inch wide and toast on both sides
