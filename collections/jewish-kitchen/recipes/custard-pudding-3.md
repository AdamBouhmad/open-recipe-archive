## Ingredients

- one desert spoonful of flour
- one pint of fresh milk
- yolks of five eggs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- lemon-peel
- two whites of eggs

## Directions

1. To one desert spoonful of flour, add one pint of fresh milk and the yolks of five eggs
2. flavor according to fancy, with sugar, nutmeg, or lemon-peel
3. beat to a froth two whites of eggs and pour to the rest
4. boil rather more than half an hour
