## Ingredients

- chicken
- almonds
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Steam chicken and when it is almost tender stuff it with the following: Take one-fourth pound of almonds, chopped; season with parsley, pepper and salt to taste, add one tablespoon of bread crumbs and bind this with one well-beaten egg.
2. Put chicken in roasting-pan and roast until done.
