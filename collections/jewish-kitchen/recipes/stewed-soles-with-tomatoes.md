## Ingredients

- soles
- onion
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- oil or butter
- red tomatoes
- lemon
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Slice in a stewpan an onion very thin, a tooth of garlic, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
2. Add 2 spoonfuls of oil or 2 oz. of butter, let it fry for 3 minutes
3. Lay in the soles with white skin on top with 4 or 5 red tomatoes sliced on the soles
4. Let it cook for 20 minutes, or 1 an hour if the soles are large
5. Take out the soles very carefully, lay them on the dish you intend serving them up
6. Rub the liquor through a sieve
7. When strained add the juice of a lemon and a little cayenne
8. Put it back in the saucepan to get hot, then lay it over the soles
