## Ingredients

- One-half cup of butter
- One and one-half cups of pulverized sugar
- Three eggs
- One-half cup of milk
- Two cups of flour
- Two teaspoons of baking-powder
- One grated cocoanut and all of its milk
- Beaten whites of two eggs
- One cup of powdered sugar
- Five tablespoons of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Rub to a cream one-half cup of butter and one and one-half cups of pulverized sugar
2. Add gradually three eggs, one-half cup of milk and two cups of flour
3. Add two teaspoons of baking-powder in last sifting
4. Bake in layers
5. For filling: Take one grated cocoanut and all of its milk
6. To half of it add the beaten whites of two eggs and one cup of powdered sugar
7. Lay this between the layers
8. Mix with the other half of the grated cocoanut five tablespoons of powdered sugar
9. Strew thickly on top of cake, which has been previously iced
