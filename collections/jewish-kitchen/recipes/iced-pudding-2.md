## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of Jordan almonds
- one quarter of bitter almonds
- three quarters of a pound of white pounded sugar
- six yolks of beaten eggs
- one quart of boiled cream

## Directions

1. Parboil three quarters of a pound of Jordan almonds, and one quarter of bitter almonds
2. Remove the skins and beat them up to a paste, with three quarters of a pound of white pounded sugar
3. Add to this six yolks of beaten eggs, and one quart of boiled cream
4. Stir the whole for a few minutes over a stove fire
5. Strain it, and pour it into a freezing pot, used for making ices
6. Work it with a scraper, as it becomes set by freezing
7. When frozen sufficiently firm, fill a mould with it, cover it with the lid, and let it remain immersed in rough ice until the time for serving
