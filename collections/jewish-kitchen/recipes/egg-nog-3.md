## Ingredients

- eggs
- sugar
- brandy or rum
- milk or cream
- whipped cream
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Separate the whites and yolks of the eggs
2. To each yolk add one tablespoon of sugar and beat until very light
3. Beat whites to a stiff froth
4. To each glass of egg-nog add two tablespoons of brandy or rum, then one-half cup of milk or cream
5. Add the whites of the eggs
6. Pour in glass
7. put a spoon of whipped cream over and grated nutmeg on top
