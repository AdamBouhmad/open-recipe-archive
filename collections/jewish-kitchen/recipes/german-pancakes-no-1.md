## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- two and one-half tablespoons of flour
- one cup of milk
- two tablespoons of butter
- sugar
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very thoroughly without separating yolks and whites
2. Add salt and sift in flour
3. Add milk gradually and beat the whole very well
4. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a large frying-pan
5. Turn mixture in and cook slowly until brown underneath
6. Grease the bottom of a large pie plate and slip the pancake on the plate
7. Add the other tablespoon of butter to the frying-pan
8. When hot, turn uncooked side of pancake down and brown
9. Serve at once with sugar and lemon slices or with any desired preserve or syrup
