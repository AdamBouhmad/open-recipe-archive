## Ingredients

- biscuits
- butter
- pepper
- chopped anchovies or fine cheese
- mustard
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Butter some biscuits on both sides, and pepper them well
2. make a paste of either chopped anchovies, or fine cheese, and spread it on the biscuit, with mustard and cayenne pepper
3. grill them
