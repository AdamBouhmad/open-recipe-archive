## Ingredients

- six eggs
- salt
- brandy
- huckleberries or other fruit compote
- rum

## Directions

1. Take six eggs, beat whites and yolks well, add a pinch of salt and a teaspoon of brandy.
2. Fry in a spider quickly and spread with a compote of huckleberries or any other fruit.
3. Roll up the omelet, pour a very small wineglass of rum over it, light it and serve at once.
