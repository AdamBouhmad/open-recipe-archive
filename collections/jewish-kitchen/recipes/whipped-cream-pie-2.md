## Ingredients

- rich crust
- jelly or jam
- one cup of sweet cream
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a crust as rich as possible and line a deep tin
2. Bake quickly in a hot oven and spread it with a layer of jelly or jam
3. Whip one cup of sweet cream until it is thick
4. Set the cream in a bowl of ice while whipping
5. Sweeten slightly and flavor with vanilla, spread this over the pie and put in a cool place until wanted
