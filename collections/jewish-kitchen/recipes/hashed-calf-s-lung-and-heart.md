## Ingredients

- lung
- heart
- water
- soup meat
- one tablespoon of goose fat
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- soup stock
- flour
- vinegar
- brown sugar
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- one tablespoon of molasses

## Directions

1. Lay the lung and heart in water for half an hour and then put on to boil in a soup kettle with your soup meat intended for dinner.
2. When soft, remove from the soup and chop up quite fine.
3. Heat one tablespoon of goose fat in a spider; chop up an onion very fine and add to the heated fat.
4. When yellow, add the hashed lung and heart, salt, pepper, soup stock and thicken with flour.
5. You may prepare this sweet and sour by adding a little vinegar and brown sugar, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon and one tablespoon of molasses.
6. Boil slowly; keep covered until ready to serve.
