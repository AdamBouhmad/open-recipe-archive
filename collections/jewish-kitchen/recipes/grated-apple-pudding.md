## Ingredients

- six good-sized apples
- six yolks of eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half pound of grated almonds or one-half cup of matzoth-meal
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- about three tablespoons of chicken or goose grease

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and leave them whole
2. Grate all the apple from the pulp
3. Add the yolks, almonds or matzoth-meal, salt, cinnamon and grease
4. Add the whites well beaten to a stiff froth
5. Mix very light
6. Bake in well-greased baking dish
