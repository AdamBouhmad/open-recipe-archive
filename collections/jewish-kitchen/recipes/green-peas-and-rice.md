## Ingredients

- one-half peck of green peas
- one-fourth cup of butter or other fat
- one cup of rice
- one quart of boiling water
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Shell one-half peck of green peas and wash them well; if canned peas are used pour off liquid and rinse with cold water
2. Heat one-fourth cup of butter or other fat in a spider, add one cup of rice and let simmer, stirring constantly until rice is a golden brown
3. Add one quart of boiling water, then the drained peas and one-half teaspoon of salt, and one-half cup of granulated sugar
4. Place in pudding dish, set in the oven and bake until rice is tender
5. Serve hot
