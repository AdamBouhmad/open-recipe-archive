## Ingredients

- one cup berry juice
- a scant half teacup of sago
- water
- whites of two eggs
- cream or berry juice

## Directions

1. Prepare one cup berry juice and sweeten to taste.
2. Have ready a scant half teacup of sago soaked one hour in water enough to cover.
3. Boil the sago in the fruit juice until thick like jelly.
4. Beat up the whites of two eggs and add to the sago while hot and remove immediately from the stove.
5. Mold and serve with cream or berry juice.
