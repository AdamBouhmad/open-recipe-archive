## Ingredients

- half a pound of mutton or goose fat
- two eggs
- a glass of rose-water
- a table-spoonful of honey
- oatmeal

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of mutton or goose fat well boiled down and beaten up well with two eggs, previously whisked with a glass of rose-water
2. Add a table-spoonful of honey, and as much oatmeal as will make it into a paste
