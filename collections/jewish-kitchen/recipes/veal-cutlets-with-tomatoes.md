## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 large red tomatoes
- a sliced Spanish onion
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 3 or 4 veal cutlets
- 1/2 pint of water
- juice of 1/2 a lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Slice 4 or 5 large red tomatoes in a stewpan with a sliced Spanish onion, salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
2. Lay on top 3 or 4 veal cutlets with 1/2 pint of water
3. Let it simmer for an hour
4. Take out the cutlets
5. Rub the tomatoes and onion through a sieve
6. Place the cutlets back into the stewpan
7. Pour over what you have pulped
8. Add the juice of 1/2 a lemon to the pulp
9. Serve it upon a hot dish with the cutlets first, then throw over the tomato sauce
10. Optionally chop a little parsley over it
