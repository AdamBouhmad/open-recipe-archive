## Ingredients

- two cups lentils
- cold water
- one sliced onion
- two tablespoons drippings
- three stalks celery, cut in small pieces
- celery seed
- pepper
- salt
- two quarts warm water

## Directions

1. Soak two cups of lentils over night in cold water
2. Drain and add to a sliced onion which has been browned in two tablespoons of drippings
3. When these have been fried for five minutes, add three stalks of celery cut in small pieces or some celery seed, pepper and salt to taste, and two quarts of warm water
4. Boil all these slowly, stirring occasionally until the lentils are quite soft
5. Pass all through a sieve, return to saucepan, heat again and serve
