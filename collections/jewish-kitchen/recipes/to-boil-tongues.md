## Ingredients

- plump tongue
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- white turnips
- carrots
- spinach
- rice
- lemon
- hard eggs
- parsley
- beetroot
- glaze

## Directions

1. Choose a nice plump tongue, if salted at home it is preferable
2. Put it on in cold water, if you have a tongue saucepan it will keep the shape better; if not, boil it in a long stock pot, it should lay flat
3. Let it boil gently for 2.5 hours, then it will be tender enough to skin
4. Be careful you do not tear the flesh, return it to the water to finish boiling
5. To garnish it, if you send it up for company, boil some very white turnips and some rich coloured carrots
6. Boil them separately and mash them separately, with a little salt and pepper to flavour
7. Arrange them in alternate colours, with strips of spinach boiled very green
8. Cut a frill for the root of the tongue to cover it, fasten it on with a plated skewer
9. Another garnish: boil some rice white and very dry, make a bed of it in the dish, lay on the tongue
10. Have slices of lemon with slices of hard eggs, lay on chopped parsley, and strips of beetroot
11. The tongue, when taken out of the water, must be put on one of the chopping boards, and plump it up by pushing from the point to the centre, that must rise higher than the other part
12. A little glaze on the top makes it look rich
13. A smoked tongue must be soaked for 2 hours in cold water before you boil it
