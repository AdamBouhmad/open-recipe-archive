## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound of sweet almonds
- heated sugar
- one-half pound of sifted powdered sugar
- whites of five eggs

## Directions

1. Roast one-quarter pound of sweet almonds, cut into strips lengthwise in a spider of heated sugar, not too brown
2. Beat one-half pound of sifted powdered sugar and the whites of five eggs to a very stiff froth
3. Mix all thoroughly and place teaspoonfuls of this mixture on waxed paper, and bake a light brown, in slow oven
