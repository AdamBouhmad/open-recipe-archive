## Ingredients

- 1 sheep's head, well trimmed and slit in halves
- turnip
- carrot
- celery
- onions
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- 1 blade of mace
- 3 pints water
- 1/4 lb. rice
- dried saffron
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- good dripping or soup fat
- 1 egg yolk
- vinegar
- parsley
- boiled turnips

## Directions

1. Put sheep's head in a deep saucepan with turnip, carrot, celery, onions, and a little garlic
2. Season with salt, pepper, marjoram, and a blade of mace
3. Add 3 pints of water
4. Wash 1/4 lb. of rice and put it in with a little dried saffron to colour
5. Let it cook gently for 2 hours
6. Dish up the sheep's head
7. Turn the soup in the tureen
8. In a small saucepan, rub a tablespoonful of flour with a little good dripping or soup fat
9. Add salt, pepper, and the yolk of an egg
10. Rub all till smooth
11. Stir in a little of the clear soup and flavor with a spoonful of vinegar
12. Let it boil until like melted butter
13. Chop some parsley and put in a sauce boat
14. Turn the boiling sauce over the parsley
15. Put round the head some boiled turnips cut in halves
