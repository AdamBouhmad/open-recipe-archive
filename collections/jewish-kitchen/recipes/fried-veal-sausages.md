## Ingredients

- a pound of veal sausages
- flour
- boiling fat
- 3 or 4 potatoes

## Directions

1. Take a pound of veal sausages and divide them
2. Roll them in flour
3. Cut 3 or 4 potatoes, wash them, and dry them on a cloth
4. Sift some flour lightly over them
5. Have a pan with boiling fat and fry the potatoes crisp and brown
6. Take them out of the pan with a slice and put them just inside of the oven to keep hot
7. Fry the sausages lightly
8. Have a dish very hot, put the sausages in, and the potatoes round
