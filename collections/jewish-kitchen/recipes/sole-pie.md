## Ingredients

- 1 good sized pair of soles
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- capers
- 2 oz. butter
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoonful flour
- 1 lemon
- dried saffron
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- butter puff-paste
- 1 egg yolk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Fillet a good sized pair of soles.
2. Put them in a stewpan with a little salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, a few fine capers, 2 oz. of butter, a cup of water.
3. Rub a teaspoonful of flour in a basin with the juice of a lemon, a little dried saffron.
4. Stir it in the saucepan to thicken the gravy.
5. When done, put them in a nice sized pie dish.
6. Cut 2 hard boiled eggs in quarters.
7. Make a good butter puff-paste to cover the dish.
8. Ornament it with a few leaves easily shaped out of pastry, and brush it over with the yolk of an egg.
9. Bake it in a hot oven.
10. Garnish with parsley.
