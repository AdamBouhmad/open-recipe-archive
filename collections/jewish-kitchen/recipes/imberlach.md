## Ingredients

- two cups of matzoth flour
- one-quarter pound of powdered ginger
- three eggs
- one-half pound of honey
- three-quarters pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of matzoth flour and one-quarter pound of powdered ginger together with three eggs
2. Set this dough aside until it dries
3. Take one-half pound of honey and three-quarters pound of sugar and boil until it gets a reddish color
4. Beat in the ginger and matzoth dough
5. Mix it with honey
6. Set back on stove, stirring constantly
7. When the mixture is thick and a reddish color, place on the board so as to cool
8. Roll and cut in two-inch lengths
