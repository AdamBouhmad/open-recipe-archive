## Ingredients

- 1/2 peck of beans
- a chopped onion
- salt
- pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1 lb of butter
- a tea cup of water
- a dessert spoonful of flour
- a spoonful of water
- a little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Pick the beans, and remove the inside shells
2. Put them in a stewpan with a chopped onion, salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg, 1 lb of butter, a tea cup of water
3. Let them cook till tender
4. Rub in a basin quite smooth a dessert spoonful of flour, with a spoonful of water, a little chopped parsley
5. Turn it into the beans, stir it with the liquor
