## Ingredients

- half a pound of pearl barley
- one quart of new milk
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Parboil half a pound of pearl barley in water
2. Pour off the water
3. Boil the parboiled barley in one quart of new milk
4. Sweeten with white sugar
