## Ingredients

- two yolks of eggs
- salt
- pepper
- finely-chopped parsley
- six blanched almonds
- sifted flour
- whites of eggs
- one-half teaspoon of baking

## Directions

1. Two yolks of eggs beaten very light, and a pinch of salt, pepper and finely-chopped parsley.
2. Add six blanched almonds grated, enough sifted flour to make stiff batter, then add the stiffly-beaten whites of eggs and one-half teaspoon of baking.
