## Ingredients

- one pound beef
- one chopped green pepper
- one large onion
- two blades of garlic
- pepper and salt
- water
- medium sized can of tomatoes
- two cups of cooked spaghetti or macaroni

## Directions

1. To one pound beef, free from fat and cut up as pan stew, add one chopped green pepper, one large onion, two blades of garlic (cut fine), pepper and salt, with just enough water to cover.
2. Let this simmer until meat is very tender, adding a little water as needed.
3. An hour or so before using, put in medium sized can of tomatoes.
4. Have ready two cups of cooked spaghetti or macaroni and put this into the meat until thoroughly heated.
5. Let water cook away just before adding the tomatoes. This must not be too wet.
