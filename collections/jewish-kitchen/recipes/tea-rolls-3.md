## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- one cake of compressed yeast
- one and one-half cups of flour
- one-quarter cup of sugar
- one and one-half teaspoons of salt
- two eggs
- one-third cup of butter

## Directions

1. Scald one cup of milk and when lukewarm dissolve one cake of compressed yeast
2. add one and one-half cups of flour
3. Beat thoroughly, cover and allow to stand until light
4. Add sugar, salt, eggs, butter and enough flour to knead
5. Allow to rise again until light
6. Shape into round or small oblong finger rolls, and place in buttered pans close together
7. when light bake in hot oven
