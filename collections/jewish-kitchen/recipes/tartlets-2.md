## Ingredients

- very rich light puff paste
- sweetmeat or jam

## Directions

1. Make a very rich light puff paste, and roll it out to half an inch of thickness
2. It should be cut with fluted paste-cutters, lightly baked, and the centre scooped out afterwards, and the sweetmeat or jam inserted
3. A pretty dish of pastry may be made by cutting the paste in ribbons of three inches in length, and one and a half in width
4. Bake them lightly, and pile them one upon another, with jam between each, in the form of a cone
