## Ingredients

- a pound of butter
- a pound of sifted loaf sugar
- eight eggs
- three quarters of a pound of flour
- currants

## Directions

1. Beat to cream a pound of butter and a pound of sifted loaf sugar
2. Add eight beaten eggs
3. Stir in lightly three quarters of a pound of flour
4. Beat well together
5. Bake for one hour in a brisk oven
6. Currants may be added if approved
