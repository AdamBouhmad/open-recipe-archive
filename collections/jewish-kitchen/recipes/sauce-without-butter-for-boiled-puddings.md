## Ingredients

- a table-spoonful of flour
- two table-spoonfuls of water
- a little wine
- lemon peel
- a small bit of clarified suet
- grated nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix a table-spoonful of flour, with two of water
2. Add a little wine, lemon peel grated, a small bit of clarified suet, of the size of a walnut, grated nutmeg, and sugar
3. Put on in a saucepan, stirring one way, and adding water if too thick
4. Lemon juice, or essence of noyeau, or almonds may be substituted to vary the flavour
