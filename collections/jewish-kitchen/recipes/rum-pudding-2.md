## Ingredients

- yolks of two eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of rum
- one pint of cream
- lady fingers
- one-half pound of marron glace

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of two eggs with one-half cup of sugar until light
2. Add stiffly-beaten whites
3. Flavor with one tablespoon of rum
4. Whip one pint of cream very stiff
5. Stir into beaten eggs
6. Line a melon mold with lady fingers, split in half
7. Put a layer of whipped cream over
8. Chop one-half pound of marron glace fine and sprinkle some over cream
9. Put another layer of lady fingers, cream and marrons, and so on until mold is filled
10. Close tightly and pack in rock salt and ice, from three to four hours
