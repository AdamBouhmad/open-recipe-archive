## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- milk
- tablespoon of fresh butter
- one cup of sifted flour
- yolks of three eggs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Break into a cup the whites of three eggs
2. Fill the cup with milk
3. Put it with a tablespoon of fresh butter and one cup of sifted flour in a spider
4. Stir as it boils until it leaves the spider clean
5. Set aside until cool and stir in the yolks of three eggs
6. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg and mix thoroughly
7. Drop by teaspoons in the boiling soup ten minutes before ready to be served
