## Ingredients

- four large, juicy oranges
- six tablespoons of sugar
- a very little water
- shaved ice
- water
- a little lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take four large, juicy oranges and six tablespoons of sugar.
2. Squeeze the oranges upon the sugar, add a very little water and let them stand for fifteen minutes.
3. Strain and add shaved ice and water, and a little lemon juice.
