## Ingredients

- bananas
- grated nuts or nutmeg
- white mayonnaise
- maraschino cherries

## Directions

1. Cut the bananas in half crosswise and arrange them on a plate, radiating from the center
2. Sprinkle with grated nuts or nutmeg and heap white mayonnaise in the center
3. Garnish with maraschino cherries
