## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of white sugar
- five eggs
- half a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of currants
- two ounces of candied orange and lemon peel
- a few drops of lemon essence

## Directions

1. Beat half a pound of butter with the same quantity of white sugar until it is like cream
2. Beat up five eggs and add them with half a pound of flour
3. Add a quarter of a pound of currants
4. Add two ounces of candied orange and lemon peel cut in thin slices
5. Add a few drops of lemon essence
6. When these ingredients are well mixed and beaten, butter a pudding tin
7. Pour in the mixture and bake in a moderately quick oven
