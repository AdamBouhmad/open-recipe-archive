## Ingredients

- yolks of six eggs
- one cup of granulated sugar
- three-fourths cup of sifted chocolate
- three-fourths cup of flour
- one and one-half teaspoon of vanilla
- whites of six eggs
- one cup of milk
- yolks of two eggs
- one cup of chopped walnuts
- rum

## Directions

1. Cream the yolks of six eggs with one cup of granulated sugar
2. Add three-fourths cup of sifted chocolate, three-fourths cup of flour sifted twice, and one and one-half teaspoon of vanilla
3. Add the beaten whites
4. Bake thirty minutes
5. When cold, cut in half
6. For filling: Boil one cup of milk with yolks of two eggs and one cup of chopped walnuts, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
7. Sweeten to taste
8. After removing from the fire add one tablespoon of rum
9. Spread while hot
