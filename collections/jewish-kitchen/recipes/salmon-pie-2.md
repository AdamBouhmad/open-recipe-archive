## Ingredients

- two pounds of fine fresh salmon
- five or six anchovies
- a small pottle of mushrooms
- a handful of fresh parsley
- a couple of shalots
- a little green thyme
- three ounces of butter
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- tarragon
- juice of a lemon
- half a pint of good brown gravy
- six eggs
- good short paste

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of fine fresh salmon in slices about three quarters of an inch thick
2. Clean and scrape five or six anchovies and halve them
3. Chop a small pottle of mushrooms, a handful of fresh parsley, a couple of shalots, and a little green thyme
4. Put these together into a saucepan, with three ounces of butter, a little pepper, salt, nutmeg, and tarragon
5. Add the juice of a lemon, and half a pint of good brown gravy
6. Let the whole simmer, gently stirring it all the time
7. Slice six eggs boiled hard
8. Line a pie-dish with good short paste
9. Fill it with alternate layers of the slices of salmon, hard eggs, and fillets of anchovies, spreading between each layer the herb sauce
10. Cover the dish with the paste
11. Bake in a moderately heated oven
