## Ingredients

- two broilers
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot fat
- six large red peppers
- two small onions
- three cloves of garlic
- one-fourth cup of capers
- water
- fat and flour

## Directions

1. Cut two broilers in pieces for serving
2. Season with salt and pepper, and dredge in flour
3. Brown in hot fat
4. Parboil six large red peppers until soft
5. Rub through a wire sieve
6. Chop two small onions fine, three cloves of garlic and one-fourth cup of capers
7. Combine and add to chicken
8. Cover with water and cook until chicken is tender
9. Thicken the sauce with fat and flour melted together
