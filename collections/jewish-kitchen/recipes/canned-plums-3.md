## Ingredients

- four quarts of plums
- one quart of sugar
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Wash, drain and prick the plums.
2. Make a syrup of the sugar and water.
3. Put part of the fruit in the boiling syrup; cook five minutes; fill and seal the jars.
4. Put more fruit in the syrup; remove and continue the process until all the fruit has been cooked.
