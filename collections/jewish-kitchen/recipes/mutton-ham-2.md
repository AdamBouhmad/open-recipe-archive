## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- brown sugar
- common salt
- saltpetre

## Directions

1. Choose a fine leg of mutton
2. Rub it in daily with a mixture of three ounces of brown sugar, two ounces of common salt, and half an ounce of saltpetre
3. Continue this process for a fortnight
4. Hang it to dry in wood smoke for ten days longer
