## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of roll dough
- 1/2 lb. of fresh butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 egg
- a cup of milk
- a small pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg into a cup of milk
2. Warm the butter in the milk
3. Mix all well together
4. Divide the mixture into 4 pieces
5. Shape them round
6. Bake them in a brisk oven for 20 or 30 minutes
7. If you make them hot for tea, cut them open and butter them, or toast them first
