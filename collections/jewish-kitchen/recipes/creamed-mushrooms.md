## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- black pepper
- cream
- flour
- milk
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Wash them thoroughly in cold water, peel them and remove the stems
2. cut them in halves or quarters, according to their size
3. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a saucepan over the fire
4. add the mushrooms and let them simmer slowly in the butter for five minutes
5. season them well with salt and black pepper, freshly ground
6. add a gill of cream
7. sift one tablespoon of flour in a bowl and add one-half pint of milk
8. Stir these briskly till flour is all dissolved
9. pour it gradually in the saucepan with the mushrooms and cream, stirring the whole constantly to keep it from lumping
10. Let it just bubble a moment
11. add another tablespoon of butter and pour the creamed mushrooms over hot buttered toast on a hot platter and serve
