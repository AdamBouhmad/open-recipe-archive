## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of almond milk
- a small piece of whole cinnamon
- rind of a fresh lemon
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of good chocolate
- 1/4 of an ounce of isinglass
- water
- yolks of 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Half pint of almond milk, a small piece of whole cinnamon, the rind of a fresh lemon, 1/2 lb. of sugar. Boil this.
2. Cut 1/2 lb. of good chocolate in a basin, pour over some of the boiled milk.
3. When the chocolate is soft, mix it well with the boiled milk, rub it through a sieve.
4. Dissolve 1/4 of an ounce of isinglass in a little water, strain it and add to it the milk and chocolate.
5. Let this get cold, beat up the yolks of 8 eggs to a froth, pour it through the sieve into the milk and chocolate, mixing it all the time.
6. When well mixed, pour it into the mould; put it in a flat saucepan on the fire like the vanille cream; attend to it just the same.
