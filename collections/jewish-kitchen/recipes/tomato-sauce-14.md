## Ingredients

- a dozen fine tomatos
- a little water or gravy
- a little vinegar
- lemon juice
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- the yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Skin a dozen fine tomatos
2. Set them on the fire in a little water or gravy
3. Beat them up with a little vinegar, lemon juice, cayenne pepper, and salt
4. Some persons like the yolk of an egg, well beaten added
5. Strain or not, as may be preferred
