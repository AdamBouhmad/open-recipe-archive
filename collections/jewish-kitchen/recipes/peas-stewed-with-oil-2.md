## Ingredients

- half a peck of peas
- half a lettuce
- mint
- small onion
- two table-spoonsful of oil
- dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Put half a peck of peas into a stew-pan, half a lettuce chopped small, a little mint, a small onion cut up, two table-spoonsful of oil, and a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar, with water sufficient to cover the peas
2. Watch, from time to time, that they do not become too dry
3. Let them stew gently, taking care that they do not burn, till perfectly soft
