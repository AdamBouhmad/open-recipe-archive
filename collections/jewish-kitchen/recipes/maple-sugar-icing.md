## Ingredients

- two cups of maple sugar
- one-half cup of boiling water
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Boil two cups of maple sugar with one-half cup of boiling water until it threads from the spoon
2. Pour it upon the beaten whites of two eggs and beat until cold
3. Spread between layers and on top of cake
