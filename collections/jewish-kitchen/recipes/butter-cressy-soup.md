## Ingredients

- 4 large carrots
- 1 onion
- 2 oz. butter
- 1 pint and 1/2 water
- 1 tooth of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- fish
- 1 spoonful flour
- 1/2 lemon
- grated nutmeg
- dried saffron
- 1 round of bread
- 2 oz. more butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Boil and rub through a sieve 4 large carrots to a pulp.
2. Fry an onion in a saucepan with 2 oz. of butter, fry it a light brown.
3. Put in pint and 1/2 of water, then add the pulp of the carrot with a tooth of garlic chopped very fine, salt and pepper.
4. Boil some fish and strain the liquor into the soup.
5. If not thick enough stir in a basin a spoonful of flour with the juice of 1/2 a lemon, a little grated nutmeg and a pinch of dried saffron.
6. Toast a round of bread, cut it in dice, put them in the tureen with 2 oz. more of butter and a little chopped parsley.
7. Throw over the soup, and stir it well.
8. Serve the fish with parsley, boil a cauliflower or some turnips or any vegetable you like to put round the fish with anchovy or caper or tomato sauce.
