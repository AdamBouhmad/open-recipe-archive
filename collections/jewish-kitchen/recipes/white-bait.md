## Ingredients

- white bait
- flour
- butter
- brown bread
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Throw white bait from the dish into a cloth with a handful of flour
2. Shake them lightly, but enough to cover them well with the flour
3. Turn them into a sieve expressly for bait to free them from too great a quantity of the flour
4. Throw the fish into a pan with plenty of boiling butter
5. They must remain but an instant, for they are considered spoilt if they become the least brown
6. Place them lightly on the dish piled up high in the centre
7. Serve with brown bread and butter
8. When devilled, sprinkle with cayenne pepper and a little salt, and serve with lemon juice
