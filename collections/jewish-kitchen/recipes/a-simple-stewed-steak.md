## Ingredients

- a fine steak
- a large piece of clarified suet or fat
- a couple of onions
- gravy or water
- a tea-cup full of button onions or a Spanish onion
- a little lemon peel
- pepper
- salt
- a little allspice
- a little browning
- a spoonful of mushroom ketchup
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Put a fine steak in a stewpan with a large piece of clarified suet or fat, and a couple of onions sliced
2. Let the steak fry for a few minutes, turning it several times
3. Cover the steak with gravy, or even water will answer the purpose
4. Add a tea-cup full of button onions or a Spanish onion sliced, a little lemon peel, pepper, salt, and a little allspice
5. Simmer till the steak is done
6. Remove the steak and carefully skim the gravy
7. Add to it a little browning and a spoonful of mushroom ketchup
8. Keep the steak on a hot stove or return it to the stewpan to warm up
9. If the gravy is not thick enough, stir in a little flour
