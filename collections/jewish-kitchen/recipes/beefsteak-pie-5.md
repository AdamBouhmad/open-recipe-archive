## Ingredients

- two pounds of chuck steak
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- juice of a lemon
- forcemeat balls
- potatoes
- four or five large floury potatoes
- chopped parsley
- melted fat
- two well-beaten eggs
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Cut up two pounds of chuck steak
2. Put it on to stew with salt, pepper and a little nutmeg and the juice of a lemon
3. Cook a few forcemeat balls, made very small, and a few potatoes cut in small pieces
4. Boil four or five large floury potatoes; when done, strain and mash with salt and pepper, a little chopped parsley and a little melted fat
5. Mix it with two well-beaten eggs
6. Put a layer of it around the bottom and sides of a deep pie-dish
7. Lay in the stew, cover with the balance of the potato
8. Brush it over with the yolk of an egg and bake in a quick oven till brown
