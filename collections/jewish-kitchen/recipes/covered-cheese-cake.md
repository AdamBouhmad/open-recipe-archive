## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- butter the size of an egg
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup water
- 2.5 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking-powder
- 2 eggs
- 0.5 cup sugar
- 0.5 pound pot cheese
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 cup milk
- lemon extract
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cream one cup of sugar with butter the size of an egg
2. Add two eggs well beaten and one cup of water alternately with two and one-half cups of flour in which has been sifted two teaspoons of baking-powder
3. For filling: Beat two eggs with one-half cup of sugar, add one-half pound of pot cheese, one tablespoon of cornstarch boiled in one cup of milk
4. Cool this and add, flavor with lemon extract
5. Put one-half of the batter in cake pan, then the filling and the other half of batter
6. Bake in slow oven thirty-five minutes
7. Sift sugar on top when done
