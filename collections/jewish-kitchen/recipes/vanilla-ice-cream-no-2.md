## Ingredients

- three whole eggs
- one cup of granulated sugar
- one pint of cream
- one cup of sweet milk
- vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Beat three whole eggs very light with one cup of granulated sugar until all grain is dissolved and mass is a light yellowish color
2. Whip one pint of cream until stiff
3. Add to eggs and sugar
4. Add one cup of sweet milk
5. Flavor with vanilla to taste
6. Put in freezer and turn until hard
