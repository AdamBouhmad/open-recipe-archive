## Ingredients

- brains
- salt
- onion
- celery root
- whole peppers
- crust of rye bread
- vinegar
- water
- lebkuchen
- brown sugar
- tablespoon of molasses
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- seedless raisins
- pounded almonds
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean brains
2. Lay in ice-cold salted water for an hour
3. Cut up an onion, a few slices of celery root, a few whole peppers, a little salt and a crust of rye bread
4. Lay the brains upon this bed of herbs and barely cover with vinegar and water
5. Boil about fifteen minutes
6. lift out the brains with a perforated skimmer, and lay upon a platter to cool
7. Take a lebkuchen, some brown sugar, a tablespoon of molasses, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon, a few seedless raisins and a few pounded almonds
8. Moisten this with vinegar and add the boiling sauce
9. Boil the sauce ten minutes longer and pour scalding over the brains
10. Eat cold and decorate with slices of lemon
