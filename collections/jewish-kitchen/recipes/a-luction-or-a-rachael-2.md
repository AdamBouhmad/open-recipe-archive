## Ingredients

- thin nouilles paste
- water
- beaten eggs
- white sugar
- fresh butter
- grated lemon peel
- preserved cherries
- custard or cream
- brandy

## Directions

1. Make a thin nouilles paste and cut into strips of about two inches wide
2. Leave it to dry
3. Boil the strips in a little water and drain through a colander
4. When the water is strained off, mix it with beaten eggs, white sugar, a little fresh butter, and grated lemon peel
5. Bake or boil in a shape lined with preserved cherries
6. When turned out pour over a fine custard or cream flavored with brandy and sweetened to taste
