## Ingredients

- rice
- stock
- chicken
- lemon
- egg

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of rice in a quart of stock for half an hour, stirring frequently.
2. Then add a chicken stuffed and trussed as for roasting; cover closely and cook thoroughly.
3. After removing the chicken, pass the liquor through a strainer, add the juice of a lemon and the beaten yolk of an egg, and pour over the bird.
