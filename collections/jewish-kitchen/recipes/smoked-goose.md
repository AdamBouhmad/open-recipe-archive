## Ingredients

- fat goose
- garlic
- salt
- sugar
- saltpetre
- water

## Directions

1. Remove skin
2. Place legs, neck and skin of neck of geschundene goose to one side
3. Scrape the meat carefully from the bones, neck, back, etc., of the goose
4. Remove all tendons and tissues and chop very fine
5. Fill this in the skin of the neck and sew up with coarse thread on both ends
6. Rub the filled neck, the legs and the breast with plenty of garlic
7. Sprinkle with three-eighths pound of salt and one tablespoon of sugar and one teaspoon of saltpetre and enough water to form a brine
8. Place the neck, legs and breast in a stone jar
9. Cover with a cloth and put weights on top
10. Put aside for seven days, turn once in a while
11. Take out of the brine, cover with gauze and send to the butcher to smoke
12. When done, serve cold, sliced thin
