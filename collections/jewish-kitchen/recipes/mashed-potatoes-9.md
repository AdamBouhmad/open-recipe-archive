## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt water
- large tablespoon of butter
- milk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Pare as many potatoes as required
2. Boil in salt water
3. Drain thoroughly when done
4. Mash in the pot with a potato masher
5. Work in a large tablespoon of butter and enough milk to make them resemble dough
6. Do not allow any lumps to form in your dish
7. Garnish with parsley
