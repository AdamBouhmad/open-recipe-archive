## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of melted butter
- two cups of grated cheese
- three-quarters of a cup of canned or grated fresh corn
- one ripe green pepper
- one egg yolk
- one-half cup of tomato puree
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-half teaspoon of paprika
- five slices of bread

## Directions

1. Into one tablespoon of melted butter stir two cups of grated cheese until it, too, is melted
2. Add three-quarters of a cup of canned or grated fresh corn
3. Add one ripe green pepper, stir them
4. Add one egg yolk mixed with one-half cup of tomato puree
5. Add one teaspoon of salt
6. Add one-half teaspoon of paprika
7. Toast five slices of bread and pour this mixture over it
8. Serve hot
