## Ingredients

- seven or eight potatoes
- two eggs
- salt
- flour
- butter
- milk
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil seven or eight potatoes, peel and let them stand several hours to dry
2. Grate them and add two eggs, salt, and enough flour to make a dough thick enough to roll
3. Roll into long, round noodles as thick as two pencils and cut to length of baking-pan
4. Butter pan and lay noodles next to each other
5. Cover with milk and lumps of butter and bake fifteen minutes, till yellow
6. Serve immediately with bread crumbs browned in butter
