## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- lettuce leaf
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Select tomatoes that are of uniform size, round, smooth and spotless
2. Scald and take off outer skin
3. Set away on ice until ready to serve
4. Serve on individual dishes, putting each on a lettuce leaf and pour a tablespoon of mayonnaise dressing over each tomato
