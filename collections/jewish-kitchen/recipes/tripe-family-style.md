## Ingredients

- two pounds tripe
- big kitchen spoon of drippings
- four large onions
- three small cloves of garlic
- water
- potatoes
- three tablespoons of flour
- catsup
- paprika
- ginger
- one teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Scald and scrape two pounds tripe and cut into inch squares.
2. Take big kitchen spoon of drippings and put in four large onions quartered and three small cloves of garlic cut up very fine.
3. Let steam, but not brown.
4. When onions begin to cook, put in tripe and steam half an hour.
5. Then cover tripe with water and let cook slowly three hours.
6. Boil a few potatoes and cut in dice shapes and add to it.
7. Half an hour before serving, add the following, after taking off as much fat from the tripe as possible: Three tablespoons of flour thinned with little water; add catsup, paprika, ginger, and one teaspoon of salt.
8. It should all be quite thick, like paste, when cooked.
