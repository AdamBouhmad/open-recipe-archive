## Ingredients

- milt
- soup meat
- cold water
- one tablespoon of drippings
- a clove of garlic
- one tablespoon of flour
- pepper
- salt
- soup stock
- a few caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Clean the milt thoroughly and boil with your soup meat
2. Set to boil with cold water and let it boil about two hours
3. Take it out and cut into finger lengths
4. Heat one tablespoon of drippings in a spider
5. When hot cut up a clove of garlic very fine and brown slightly in the fat
6. Add a tablespoon of flour, stirring briskly, pepper and salt to taste and thin with soup stock
7. Add the pieces of milt and let it simmer slowly
8. If the sauce is too thick add more water or soup stock
9. Some add a few caraway seeds instead of the garlic, which is a matter of taste
