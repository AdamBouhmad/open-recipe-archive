## Ingredients

- bread
- ice cold caviar
- one teaspoon of chopped onion
- one teaspoon chopped parsley
- quarters of lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the bread about one-quarter of an inch thick and two inches square or round
2. Toast the bread
3. Spread over each slice a teaspoon of ice cold caviar
4. Mix one teaspoon of chopped onion and one teaspoon chopped parsley
5. Spread the mixture over the caviar
6. Serve with quarters of lemon
