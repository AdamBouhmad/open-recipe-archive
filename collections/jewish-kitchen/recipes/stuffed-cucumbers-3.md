## Ingredients

- four cucumbers
- vinegar
- one cup pecans or Brazil nuts
- six tablespoons mashed potatoes
- one egg
- one teaspoon salt
- two tablespoons chopped parsley
- one saltspoon white pepper
- dash of nutmeg
- two tablespoons melted butter
- one cup white sauce
- dash of powdered cloves
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut four cucumbers in half lengthwise; remove the seeds with a spoon.
2. Lay the cucumbers in vinegar overnight.
3. Wipe dry and fill with a mixture made from one cup pecans or Brazil nuts chopped, six tablespoons of mashed potatoes, one well-beaten egg, one teaspoon of salt, two tablespoons of chopped parsley, one saltspoon of white pepper, dash of nutmeg and two tablespoons of melted butter.
4. Bake in a buttered dish until tender.
5. Serve hot with one cup of white sauce, dash of powdered cloves, one well-beaten egg, salt and pepper to taste.
