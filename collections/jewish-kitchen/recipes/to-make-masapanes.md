## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of good Valencia almonds
- 1 lb. of best loaf sugar
- half a pint of water
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 2 whole eggs
- 6 egg whites
- sufficient flour
- orange-flower water
- juice of a lemon
- a tablespoonful of the best oil

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound very fine 1 lb. of good Valencia almonds
2. Clarify 1 lb. of best loaf sugar in half a pint of water till it comes quite thick
3. Add the almonds, stirring it well together till it is well mixed
4. Put it away till the next day
5. Take the yolks of 6 eggs quite free from any of the whites
6. Mix them well with the mixture you put aside
7. Flavour with orange-flower water
8. Beat up 2 whole eggs with the 6 whites
9. Mix with sufficient flour to make a paste and add to the eggs
10. When beat, add the juice of a lemon and a tablespoonful of the best oil
11. Roll it out on your pasteboard and cut out rounds with a good size tumbler
12. Pinch up those rounds into a little square shape with 4 corners
13. Lay them on a baking tin slightly floured
14. Put a spoonful of the almond mixture into each
15. Place in a very quick oven for not more than 10 minutes to set the paste
16. When they come out of the oven, let them get cold
17. Brush over the top with thick clarified sugar
18. Sprinkle over each a few thousands
