## Ingredients

- pineapple
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. To one pineapple take three-quarters of its weight in sugar and one cup of water
2. Peel the pineapple and put it through the food-chopper
3. Weigh and add three-quarters of the weight in sugar
4. Bring slowly to a boil and simmer for about twenty minutes, or until the consistency of marmalade
