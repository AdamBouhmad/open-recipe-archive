## Ingredients

- fresh young cucumbers
- vinegar
- water
- veal broth
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take out the seeds of some fresh young cucumbers
2. Quarter them and cut them into pieces of two inch lengths
3. Let them lay for an hour in vinegar and water
4. Simmer them till thoroughly soft in a veal broth seasoned with pepper, salt, and a little lemon juice
5. When ready for serving, pour off the gravy and thicken it with the yolks of a couple of eggs stirred in
6. Add it to the saucepan and warm up, taking care that it does not curdle
