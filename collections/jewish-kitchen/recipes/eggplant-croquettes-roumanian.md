## Ingredients

- eggplant
- hot water
- two eggs
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons of matzoth or white flour or bread crumbs
- butter or oil

## Directions

1. Peel the eggplant
2. Place in hot water and boil until tender
3. Drain
4. Add two eggs, salt, pepper, two tablespoons of matzoth or white flour or bread crumbs
5. Beat together
6. Fry in butter or oil by tablespoonfuls
