## Ingredients

- Three pounds of the best end neck of mutton
- a quart of water
- 3 or 4 turnips
- a little celery
- a little salt, pepper
- 1/2 lb. of rice
- A large onion (Spanish)
- parsley
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put three pounds of the best end neck of mutton in your soup saucepan with a quart of water
2. Add 3 or 4 turnips cut in good size pieces
3. Add a little celery cut thin, a little salt, pepper
4. Add 1/2-lb. of rice, well washed
5. Let it boil well
6. Add a large onion (Spanish) boiled whole
7. Let it boil, skim it well, then let it simmer for two hours
8. Serve the mutton with the boiled onions in the dish with caper or tomato sauce
9. Have parsley and grated nutmeg in the tureen
