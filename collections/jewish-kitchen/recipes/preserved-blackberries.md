## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Weigh the fruit and allow a pound of sugar to every pound of fruit
2. Tie spices in a bag, such as cloves and cinnamon
3. Make a thick syrup of the sugar before you put in the berries
4. Boil half an hour and seal when cold
