## Ingredients

- the peel of six lemons
- the juice of one lemon
- a quarter of a pound of pounded almonds
- a quarter of a pound of preserved lemon and orange peel
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- six eggs well beaten

## Directions

1. Grate the peel of six lemons and add the juice of one
2. Mix with a quarter of a pound of pounded almonds, a quarter of a pound of preserved lemon and orange peel, half a pound of powdered white sugar, and six eggs well beaten
3. Mix all together and bake in a dish lined with a fine pastry
