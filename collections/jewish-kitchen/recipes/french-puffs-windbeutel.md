## Ingredients

- one cup of water
- one-quarter pound of butter
- one-quarter pound of sifted flour
- four eggs
- grated peel of a lemon
- rendered butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put one cup of water and one-quarter pound of butter on to boil
2. When it begins to boil stir in one-quarter pound of sifted flour
3. Stir until it leaves the kettle clean
4. Take off the fire and stir until milk-warm
5. Stir in four eggs, one at a time, stirring until all used up
6. Flavor with the grated peel of a lemon
7. Put on some rendered butter in a kettle
8. When the butter is hot, dip a large teaspoon in cold water and cut pieces of dough with it as large as a walnut
9. Drop into the hot butter
10. Try one first to see whether the butter is hot enough
11. Do not crowd as they want plenty of room to raise
12. Dip the hot butter over them with a spoon
13. Fry a deep yellow
14. Sprinkle powdered sugar over them
