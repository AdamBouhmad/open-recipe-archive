## Ingredients

- chicken
- one quart of water

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into small pieces and place it in a deep earthen dish
2. Add one quart of water; cover it and set over a kettle of boiling water, letting it steam until the meat of the chicken has become very tender
3. Strain off the broth and let it stand over night
4. In the morning remove the fat and return the liquid to the original earthen dish
