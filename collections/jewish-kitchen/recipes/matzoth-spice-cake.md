## Ingredients

- eggs
- one-half tablespoon of matzoth meal per egg
- one tablespoon of sugar per egg
- one-half tablespoon of ground ginger
- one-half tablespoon of cinnamon
- one-fourth tablespoon of cloves

## Directions

1. To every egg add one-half tablespoon of matzoth meal and one tablespoon of sugar
2. Sift meal five times, mix with sugar, one-half tablespoon of ground ginger, one-half tablespoon of cinnamon, one-fourth tablespoon of cloves
3. Mix with the well-beaten yolks
4. Cut and fold in gently the stiffly-beaten whites
