## Ingredients

- butter
- potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Grease a pan with butter
2. Choose the potatoes that are so big or misshapen you wouldn't want to use them for boiling or baking
3. Cut them in thin slices
4. Spread them in the pan in a layer an inch thick
5. Sprinkle with pepper and salt to taste
6. Dot with butter here and there, perhaps a half teaspoon for each layer
7. Four or six bits of butter should be sprinkled over each layer
8. Repeat the layers of the raw potatoes until the pan is full
9. Cover them with milk
10. Place in the oven
