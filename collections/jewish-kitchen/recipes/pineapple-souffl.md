## Ingredients

- nice ripe pineapple
- eggs
- cream

## Directions

1. Take a nice ripe pineapple
2. Grate it and sweeten to taste
3. Beat the whites of two eggs stiff
4. Mix with the pineapple
5. Before serving, whip half a pint of cream
6. Put on the pineapple
