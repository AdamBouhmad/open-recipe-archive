## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. sweet almonds
- about 1 dozen bitter almonds
- 2 pints of water
- orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 lb. sweet almonds and about 1 dozen bitter almonds
2. Pound them well in a marble mortar
3. Add to them 2 pints of water or more to taste
4. Flavour with orange-flower water
5. Put it into bottles for use
6. It will only keep for 2 or 3 days
7. It is very nice iced
