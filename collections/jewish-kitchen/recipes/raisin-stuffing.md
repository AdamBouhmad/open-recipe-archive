## Ingredients

- three cups of stale bread crumbs
- one-half a cup of melted chicken fat
- one cup of seeded raisins cut in small pieces
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-fourth teaspoon of white pepper

## Directions

1. Take three cups of stale bread crumbs; add one-half a cup of melted chicken fat, one cup of seeded raisins cut in small pieces, one teaspoon of salt and one-fourth teaspoon of white pepper.
2. Mix thoroughly.
