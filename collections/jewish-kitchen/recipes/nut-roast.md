## Ingredients

- one-half cup of lentils
- one-half cup shelled roasted peanuts
- one-half cup of toasted bread crumbs
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half saltspoon of pepper
- milk
- parsley or watercress

## Directions

1. Soak one-half cup of lentils overnight
2. In the morning drain, cover with fresh water and bring to a boil
3. Drain again, put in fresh water and cook until tender
4. Drain once more, throw away the water, and press the lentils through a colander
5. To them add one-half cup shelled roasted peanuts, either ground or chopped
6. Add one-half cup of toasted bread crumbs, one-half teaspoon of salt and one-half saltspoon of pepper
7. Add milk sufficient to make the mixture the consistency of mush
8. Put into a greased baking-dish
9. Bake in a moderate oven for an hour
10. Turn out on a heated platter
11. Garnish with parsley or watercress and serve
