## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one pound of brown sugar
- one-eighth pound of citron shredded
- one-eighth pound of shelled walnuts (broken)
- one and one-half cups of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- two teaspoons of cinnamon
- one-fourth teaspoon of allspice

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and brown sugar well.
2. Add citron and walnuts.
3. Add flour, baking-powder, cinnamon, and allspice.
4. Spread the dough in long pans with well-floured hands, have about one and one-half inches thick.
5. Bake in very moderate oven.
6. When baked, cut in squares and spread with icing.
7. Set in a cool stove or the sun to dry.
