## Ingredients

- about a pound of any kind of cooked fish except herring
- two eggs
- one cup of rice
- salt
- a good dessertspoon of butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut up in small pieces about a pound of any kind of cooked fish except herring.
2. Boil two eggs hard and chop up.
3. Take one cup of rice and boil in the following manner: After washing it well and putting it on in boiling water, with a little salt, let it boil for ten minutes, drain it almost dry and let it steam with the lid closely shut for ten minutes longer without stirring.
4. Take a clean pot and put in the fish, eggs, rice, a good dessertspoon of butter, and pepper and salt to taste.
5. Stir over the fire until quite hot.
6. Press into a mould and turn it out at once and serve.
