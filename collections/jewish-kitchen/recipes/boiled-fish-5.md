## Ingredients

- fish
- 4 yolks of eggs
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- ½ pint of the liquor the fish is cooked in
- juice of ½ a lemon
- chopped parsley
- white of one egg

## Directions

1. For the cooking of fish you must refer to the portion devoted to that cookery
2. Beat up eggs for a good sauce, you will require 4 yolks of eggs
3. Season it with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
4. Boil ½ pint of the liquor the fish is cooked in, and the juice of ½ a lemon
5. Have some chopped parsley in the sauce boat
6. When the sauce has thickened, have ready the white of one egg whipt to a snow
7. This will give it a creamy flavour
