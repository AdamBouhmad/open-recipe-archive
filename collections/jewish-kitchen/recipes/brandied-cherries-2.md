## Ingredients

- sweet cherries
- sugar
- brandy
- water

## Directions

1. Select the largest sweet cherries, leaving the stems on
2. Allow half a pound of sugar to every pound of fruit, and a pint of good brandy for every five pounds of fruit
3. Make a syrup of the sugar, using as little water as possible
4. Pour it over the cherries and let them remain in the syrup all night
5. Next day put them in a preserving kettle and heat slowly
6. Boil about eight minutes
7. Take up the cherries with a perforated skimmer and boil the syrup fifteen minutes
8. Add the brandy to the boiling syrup, remove from the fire and pour over the cherries hot, and seal
