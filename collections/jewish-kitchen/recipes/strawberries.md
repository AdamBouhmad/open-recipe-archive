## Ingredients

- ripe strawberries
- pulverized sugar
- oranges

## Directions

1. Pick nice ripe berries
2. Pile them in a fruit dish
3. Strew plenty of pulverized sugar over them
4. Garnish with round slices or quarters of oranges, also well sugared
