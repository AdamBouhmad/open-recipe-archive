## Ingredients

- a large white goose liver
- salt water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- goose-fat
- a piece of onion
- a few cloves
- a few slices of celery
- whole peppers
- one bay leaf
- mushrooms
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Lay goose liver in salt water for an hour
2. Wipe dry, salt, pepper and dredge with flour
3. Fry in hot goose-fat
4. Cut up a piece of onion, add a few cloves, a few slices of celery cut very fine, whole peppers, one bay leaf, and some mushrooms
5. Cover closely and stew a few minutes
6. Add lemon juice to sauce
