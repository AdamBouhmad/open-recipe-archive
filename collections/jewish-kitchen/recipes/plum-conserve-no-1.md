## Ingredients

- five pounds of blue plums or German Prunes
- four oranges
- rind of two oranges
- one pound of seeded raisins
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash five pounds of blue plums or German Prunes, cut them in halves and remove the stones.
2. Peel four oranges, slice them fine and cut each slice in half.
3. Cut the rind of two of the oranges into small squares.
4. Add one pound of seeded raisins.
5. Take a measure of sugar and a measure of the mixture, place in preserving kettle on the stove and let come slowly to the boiling point.
6. Cook steadily for several hours until the fruit is clear and thick.
7. Put in jelly glasses or jars.
