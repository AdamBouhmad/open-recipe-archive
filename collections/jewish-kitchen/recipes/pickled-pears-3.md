## Ingredients

- pears
- 4 pounds and a half white sugar per 10 pounds of fruit
- half a cup of water
- 1 quart strong vinegar
- stick cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Pears should always be peeled for pickling
2. If large cut them in half and leave the stems on
3. Throw each pear into cold water as you peel it
4. When all are peeled weigh them
5. Put into the kettle with alternate layers of sugar and half a cup of water and one quart of strong vinegar
6. Add stick cinnamon and a few cloves (remove the soft heads)
7. Heat slowly and boil until tender
8. Remove them with a perforated skimmer, and spread upon dishes to cool
9. Skim the boiling syrup and boil fifteen minutes longer
10. Put the pears in glass jars or a large earthen jar and pour the syrup and spices boiling hot over the fruit
11. When cold seal
