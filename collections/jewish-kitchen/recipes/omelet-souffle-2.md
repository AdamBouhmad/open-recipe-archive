## Ingredients

- yolks of six eggs
- six tablespoons of powdered sugar
- juice of one lemon
- grated rind of lemon
- whites of six eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of six eggs and six tablespoons of powdered sugar added gradually together until thick and smooth
2. Add juice of one lemon and a little grated rind
3. Beat whites as stiff as possible
4. Stir together
5. Put into a warm well-buttered dish
6. Bake in quick oven ten minutes
