## Ingredients

- Rich pie-crust
- Peaches
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Line a pie-plate with a rich pie-crust
2. Cover thickly with peaches that have been pared and sliced fine (canned peaches may be used when others are not to be had)
3. Add sugar and cover with strips of dough
4. Bake quickly
