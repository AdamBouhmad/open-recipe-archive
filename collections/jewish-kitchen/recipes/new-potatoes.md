## Ingredients

- six potatoes
- salted boiling water
- melted butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Brush and scrape off all the skin of six potatoes
2. Boil for half an hour in salted boiling water
3. Drain and salt
4. Dry for a few minutes
5. Pour melted butter over them
6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley
