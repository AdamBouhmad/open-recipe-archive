## Ingredients

- rinds of 4 very fresh lemons
- 2 quarts of water
- 4 lemons
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. Take the rinds of 4 very fresh lemons and cut up as fine as possible
2. Put it in 2 quarts of water
3. Cut the lemons in halves and squeeze them through a sieve into the water
4. Boil 1 lb. of loaf sugar with a pint of water to a syrup
5. When cold, turn the syrup into the water and stir it all well together
6. If not acid enough put more lemon, or more sugar if required
7. Let it stand for an hour before using
8. This drink is very nice iced in the summer
