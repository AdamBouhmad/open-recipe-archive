## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- One-half pint of water
- One-half cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of sugar with one-half pint of water until it ropes
2. Add one-half cup of vinegar and boil until it hardens
3. Dip in fruit, orange slices, nuts or green grapes with stems on
4. Put aside on a buttered platter to set
