## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 fine beetroots
- salt
- ground pepper
- a few allspice
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Parboil 2 or 3 fine beetroots
2. Skin them and let them drain well
3. Slice them
4. Put them in a jar with salt and ground pepper, a few allspice
5. Boil as much vinegar as you require to cover them
6. Pour it over boiling hot
7. Tie down when cold
