## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 onions
- 3 tablespoonfuls of oil
- pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- ground mace
- pieces of halibut head
- flesh of the halibut
- piece of the liver
- 4 eggs
- dried saffron
- chopped parsley
- juice of 2 large lemons or more

## Directions

1. Slice in a stewpan 3 or 4 onions with 3 tablespoonfuls of oil
2. Let it fry
3. Put in pint of water, salt, pepper, ground mace
4. Lay in the pieces of the head
5. Make a few balls of some of the flesh of the halibut and a piece of the liver
6. Lay them on top
7. Have a good egg sauce ready to pour over it
8. Rub in a basin 4 eggs, a little dried saffron, chopped parsley and the juice of 2 large lemons or more, according to taste
9. Dish it up very carefully
10. Lay the balls round
11. Pour over some of the sauce
12. Put the remainder in a sauce-boat
