## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. of butter
- 2 oz. of good Cheddar cheese
- 4 eggs
- pepper
- salt
- 2 tomatoes
- toast

## Directions

1. Put in your small butter tin 1/2 oz. of butter
2. Cut up small 2 oz. of good Cheddar cheese
3. As soon as the butter is warm lay that in
4. Beat in a basin 4 eggs with a little pepper, not much salt, as the cheese flavours
5. Let it cook in the oven, on the hob, or before the fire on a stand
6. You can cook this for a light lunch, with 2 tomatoes cut in slices at the bottom of the tin with the butter
7. Have 2 or 3 slices of well-made toast to send up
8. Place them in the toast rack as you make them
9. This must also be sent up in the dish or tin it is made in
