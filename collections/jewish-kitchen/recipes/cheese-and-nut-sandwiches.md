## Ingredients

- thin rounds from rye bread
- one cream cheese
- salt
- paprika
- one stalk of chopped celery
- one-fourth cup of chopped nut meats
- buttered bread
- stuffed olive

## Directions

1. Cut thin rounds from rye bread.
2. Rub one cream cheese to a cream, season to taste with salt and paprika.
3. Add one stalk of chopped celery, and one-fourth cup of chopped nut meats.
4. Spread on buttered bread and place a slice of stuffed olive on top, in the centre of each piece of bread.
