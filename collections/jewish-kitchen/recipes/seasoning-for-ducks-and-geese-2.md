## Ingredients

- chopped onion
- chopped sage
- grated stale bread
- shred suet
- pepper
- salt
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Mix chopped onion with an equal quantity of chopped sage
2. Add three times as much grated stale bread
3. Add a little shred suet, pepper, and salt
4. Add a beaten egg to bind it
5. The onions may be boiled first to render them less strong
