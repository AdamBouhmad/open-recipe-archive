## Ingredients

- yolks of ten eggs
- two cups of pulverized sugar
- one and three-fourth cups of sifted dry rye bread crumbs
- one-half pound of sweet almonds
- bitter almonds
- one-fourth pound of citron
- one cake of chocolate
- grated peel of one lemon
- juice of one orange and one lemon
- one tablespoon of cinnamon
- one teaspoon of allspice
- one-half teaspoon of cloves
- one wine glass of brandy
- whites of ten eggs
- chocolate icing
- white of one egg
- scant cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks and whites of ten eggs
2. Beat the yolks with two cups of pulverized sugar
3. When thick add one and three-fourth cups of sifted dry rye bread crumbs
4. Add one-half pound of sweet almonds and some bitter ones, grated or powdered as fine as possible
5. Add one-fourth pound of citron shredded fine
6. Add one cake of chocolate grated
7. Add the grated peel of one lemon
8. Add the juice of one orange and one lemon
9. Add one tablespoon of cinnamon, one teaspoon of allspice, one-half teaspoon of cloves
10. Add one wine glass of brandy
11. Fold in the beaten whites
12. Bake very slowly in ungreased form
13. For icing: Melt a small piece of chocolate
14. Beat the white of an egg stiff with scant cup of sugar
15. Stir into the melted chocolate and spread with a knife
