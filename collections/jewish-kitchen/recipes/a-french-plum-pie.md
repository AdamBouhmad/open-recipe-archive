## Ingredients

- one pound of fine dried French plums
- water
- one glass of port wine
- four ounces of white sugar
- rich puff paste

## Directions

1. Stew one pound of fine dried French plums until tender, in water, rather more than enough to cover, with one glass of port wine
2. Add four ounces of white sugar when the plums are quite tender
3. Pour them with the liquor into a pie-dish
4. Cover with a rich puff paste
5. Bake
