## Ingredients

- one pint drawn butter
- one tablespoon vinegar
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- two tablespoons chopped capers
- two tablespoons chopped pickles
- two tablespoons chopped olives
- one-half teaspoon onion juice
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. To one pint of drawn butter add one tablespoon each of vinegar and lemon juice and two tablespoons each of chopped capers, pickles, and olives, one-half teaspoon onion juice, a few grains cayenne pepper.
