## Ingredients

- grape fruit
- sugar
- white wine or sherry
- powdered sugar
- maraschino cherry

## Directions

1. Cut in half with a sharp knife
2. Remove seeds
3. Loosen pulp
4. Cut out pithy white centre
5. Wipe knife after each cutting, so that the bitter taste may be avoided
6. Pour in white wine or sherry and sprinkle with powdered sugar
7. Let stand several hours in ice-chest to ripen
8. Serve cold in the shell
9. Decorate with maraschino cherry
