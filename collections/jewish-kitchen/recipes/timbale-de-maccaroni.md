## Ingredients

- maccaroni
- water
- butter or grease for mould
- fricassee of chicken
- sweetbreads
- rich white sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the maccaroni in water till it slightly swells and is soft enough to cut
2. Cut it into short pieces about two inches in length
3. Grease a mould and stick the maccaroni closely together all over the mould
4. Fill up the space with fricassee of chicken, sweetbreads, or whatever may be liked
5. Close the mould carefully and boil
6. Serve with rich white sauce on the side, not poured over the timbale
