## Ingredients

- breast of mutton
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- dried saffron
- 1/2 lb rice
- potatoes
- water
- parsley
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Cut the breast of mutton in nice pieces, cut off the fat
2. Take a deep baking dish and slice an onion at the bottom
3. Add salt, pepper, a little dried saffron
4. Wash the rice and cut as many potatoes as you require
5. Put them in the dish
6. Put in the pieces of mutton
7. Fill the dish with water
8. Chop a little parsley and marjoram, put it in the oven to bake
