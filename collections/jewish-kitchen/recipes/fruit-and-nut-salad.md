## Ingredients

- two bananas
- two oranges
- one-half cup of English walnuts
- juice of one-half lemon
- French dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Slice two bananas, two oranges and mix them with one-half cup of English walnuts and the juice of one-half lemon with French dressing
2. Serve on lettuce leaves
