## Ingredients

- 1 lb. cod
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- capers
- 1 egg
- 1/2 lemon
- puff paste
- 1 egg yolk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil 1 lb. of cod, or if you have any cold left, mince it with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg.
2. Chop a few capers, mix it all up with an egg and juice of 1/2 a lemon.
3. Make a good puff paste, grease the little patty pans, line them with the pastry.
4. Put in each a little of the minced fish, cover them with pastry and turn the edges neatly.
5. Brush them over with the yolk of an egg.
6. Put them in a quick oven.
7. When done, take them out of the tins, dish them on a fish napkin, garnished with parsley.
