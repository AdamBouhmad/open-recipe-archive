## Ingredients

- one-half pound of lentils
- cold water
- one quart of water
- one small onion
- three or four ounces of smoked fat meat
- one pint of boiling water
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-quarter teaspoon of pepper

## Directions

1. Pick and wash one-half pound of lentils and soak them in cold water overnight
2. In the morning put them over the fire in a large saucepan with about a quart of water
3. As soon as the water begins to boil, the lentils will rise to the top
4. Remove them with a skimmer
5. put them in a baking dish with one small onion and three or four ounces of smoked fat meat in the centre
6. pour over them a pint of boiling water, in which one-half teaspoon of salt and one-quarter teaspoon of pepper have been mixed
7. Bake in a moderate oven four or five hours
8. The lentils must be kept moist and it may be necessary to add a little water from time to time
