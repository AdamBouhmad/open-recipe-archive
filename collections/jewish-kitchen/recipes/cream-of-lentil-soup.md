## Ingredients

- one cup of lentils
- one-half teaspoon of baking soda
- two tablespoons of butter or butter substitute
- two teaspoons of salt
- one-half teaspoon of sugar
- three cups of milk
- croutons

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of lentils over night
2. Drain and boil slowly for one hour in water containing one-half teaspoon of baking soda
3. drain and boil again very gently in fresh water
4. when the lentils are tender drain off most of the liquid and return to the fire
5. Add two tablespoons of butter, or butter substitute, two teaspoons of salt, and one-half teaspoon of sugar
6. Bring three cups of milk to a boil in the double-boiler
7. Just before serving mash the lentils through a strainer directly into the milk
8. Serve in cups and pass croutons with the soup
