## Ingredients

- a nice pair of soles
- flour
- eggs
- oil or butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Fillet a nice pair of soles into 8 fillets
2. Roll each fillet up and fasten with a fine piece of cotton
3. Dip them in flour, and then well in the eggs
4. Fry in boiling oil or butter
5. Dish on a fish napkin with little tufts of parsley
6. A few fried potatoes in the dish is an improvement
