## Ingredients

- 4 or 5-lb. of thin flank, free from bone
- 1 pound of chopped meat
- marjoram
- chopped onion
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- sausage
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Lay the beef on a chopping board and beat it quite flat
2. Have a pound of chopped meat well seasoned with marjoram, chopped onion, parsley, pepper, salt, and grated nutmeg
3. Spread this flat on the beef
4. Put a few slices of sausage cut thin on top of the forced meat
5. Add a couple of hard-boiled eggs cut in slices
6. Commence rolling it carefully
7. Sew it with a coarse needle and thread
8. Roll it in a coarse cloth and tie the ends tight with string
9. Put it in a long stock pot and let it boil for 4 hours gently
10. Take it out of the saucepan and unroll it from the cloth carefully
11. If to eat hot, make a nice liquor seasoned well and coloured with a little browning, and have some meat balls to garnish it
12. If cold, press it flat between two chopping boards by putting two heavy weights upon it
13. Cut the rough ends off and garnish it with parsley and slices of beetroot
