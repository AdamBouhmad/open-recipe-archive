## Ingredients

- port wine
- gravy
- vinegar
- oil
- shalot
- mustard
- fried bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mix a little port wine in some gravy
2. Add two table-spoonfuls of vinegar, one of oil, a shalot minced, and a spoonful of mustard
3. Just before the mutton is served, pour the sauce over it
4. Sprinkle it with fried bread crumbs
5. Baste the meat with the sauce
