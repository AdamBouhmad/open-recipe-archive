## Ingredients

- one pound of noodles
- two quarts of boiling water
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- two teaspoons of white pepper
- one ounce good butter

## Directions

1. Plunge one pound of noodles into two quarts of boiling water and cook for fifteen minutes.
2. Drain well, replace in the same pan, season with one-half teaspoon of salt, two teaspoons of white pepper, adding one ounce good butter.
3. Gently mix without breaking the noodles until the butter is thoroughly dissolved, and serve.
