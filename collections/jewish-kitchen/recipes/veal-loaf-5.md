## Ingredients

- two pounds of chopped veal
- four tablespoons of bread crumbs
- two beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- nutmeg
- water
- tablespoon of chicken-fat

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of chopped veal, four tablespoons of bread crumbs, two beaten eggs
2. Season with salt, pepper, ginger, nutmeg and a little water
3. Add a tablespoon of chicken-fat
4. Grease the pan, mix ingredients thoroughly, form into a loaf
5. Spread or lay piece of chicken-fat on top
6. Bake in oblong tin until done, basting frequently
