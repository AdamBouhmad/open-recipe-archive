## Ingredients

- eggplant, sliced
- chopped meat
- egg
- chopped parsley
- salt and pepper
- olive oil
- water

## Directions

1. Arrange in oiled pan in layers: one layer of sliced eggplant, one layer of chopped meat seasoned with egg, chopped parsley, salt and pepper
2. Repeat as many layers as desired
3. Add a little olive oil
4. Cover with water
5. Bake one-half hour
