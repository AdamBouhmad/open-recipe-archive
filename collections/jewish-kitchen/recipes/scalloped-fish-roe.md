## Ingredients

- three large roes
- water
- vinegar
- three hard-boiled eggs
- one cup of melted butter
- one teaspoon of anchovy paste
- one tablespoon of chopped parsley
- juice of half a lemon
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil three large roes in water with a little vinegar for ten minutes.
2. Plunge into cold water; wipe the roe dry.
3. Mash the yolks of three hard-boiled eggs into a cup of melted butter, teaspoon of anchovy paste, tablespoon of chopped parsley, juice of half a lemon, salt and pepper to taste.
4. Add a cup of bread crumbs and then mix in lightly the roe that has been broken into pieces.
5. Put all in baking dish, cover with bread crumbs and flakes of butter, and brown in oven.
