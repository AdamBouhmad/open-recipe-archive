## Ingredients

- One pint of stewed pumpkin
- Four eggs
- Scant cup of sugar
- One pint of milk
- One-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- Less than one-half teaspoon of mace
- Grated nutmeg
- Very rich crust

## Directions

1. Press through a sieve one pint of stewed pumpkin
2. Beat yolks and sugar together until very thick
3. Add one pint of milk to the beaten eggs
4. Add the pressed pumpkin, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon, less than one-half teaspoon of mace and grated nutmeg
5. Stir the stiffly-beaten whites in last
6. Bake in a very rich crust without cover
