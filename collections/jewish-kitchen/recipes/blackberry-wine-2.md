## Ingredients

- berries
- boiling water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Measure your berries and bruise them
2. to every gallon add one quart of boiling water
3. let the mixture stand twenty-four hours stirring occasionally
4. strain off all the liquor into a cask
5. to every gallon add two pounds of sugar
6. cork tightly and let stand till the following October
