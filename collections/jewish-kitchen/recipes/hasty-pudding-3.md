## Ingredients

- any kind of old cake
- wine
- one pint of milk
- four eggs
- one tablespoon of potato flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut any kind of old cake in slices and dip in wine or sprinkle some wine over all
2. Make a custard with one pint of milk and four eggs
3. Put one tablespoon of potato flour with the yolks, sweeten to taste
4. Boil the custard, flavor and pour over cake in pudding dish
5. Beat whites to a stiff froth, add sugar and spread over all
6. Put in oven to brown slightly
7. Eat cold
