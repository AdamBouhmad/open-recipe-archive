## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- currants
- shred suet
- brown sugar
- eggs
- brandy
- grated nutmeg
- lemon peel
- sweet sauce

## Directions

1. Make in a similar way to Cumberland pudding, with the omission of the apples
2. Make them in balls, and fry or bake in cups
3. A sweet sauce is served with them
