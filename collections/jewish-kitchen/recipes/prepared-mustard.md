## Ingredients

- one teaspoon of sugar
- saltspoon of fine salt
- one tablespoon of best salad oil
- two tablespoons of ground mustard
- vinegar
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Rub together one teaspoon of sugar, saltspoon of fine salt and one tablespoon of best salad oil
2. Do this thoroughly
3. Mix two tablespoons of ground mustard with vinegar enough to thin it
4. Then add to the mixture of sugar, and if too thick, add a little boiling water
