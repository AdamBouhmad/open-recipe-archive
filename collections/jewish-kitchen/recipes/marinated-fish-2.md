## Ingredients

- pickerel, pike or any fish that is not fat
- salt
- whole onion
- two cups of vinegar
- one cup of water
- four bay leaves
- twelve allspice
- pepper
- ginger

## Directions

1. Take pickerel, pike or any fish that is not fat, cut into two-inch slices, wash well, salt and set aside in a cool place for a few hours.
2. When ready to cook, wash slightly so as not to remove all salt from fish.
3. Take heads and set up to boil with a whole onion for twenty-five minutes.
4. Add the other pieces and two cups of vinegar, one cup of water, four bay leaves and twelve allspice, a little pepper and ginger.
5. Cook for thirty-five minutes longer.
6. Taste fish, add a little water or a little more vinegar to taste.
7. Remove fish carefully so as not to break the pieces and let cool.
8. Strain the sauce, return fish to same, adding a few bay leaves and allspice.
9. Set in a cool place until sauce forms a jelly around the fish.
