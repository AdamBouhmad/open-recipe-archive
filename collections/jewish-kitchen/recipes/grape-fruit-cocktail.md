## Ingredients

- grape-fruit
- powdered sugar
- sherry
- maraschino cherry or bright-colored jelly
- paper doilies or asparagus fern

## Directions

1. Cut the grape-fruit into halves, crosswise, and scoop out the pulp, rejecting the white inner skin as well as the seeds
2. Clean the shells
3. cut the edges with a sharp knife into scallops and throw them into cold water
4. Set the pulp on the ice
5. At serving time put a teaspoon of cracked ice in the bottom of each shell
6. fill with the pulp, mixed thoroughly with powdered sugar and a little sherry, if desired
7. place a maraschino cherry or bit of bright-colored jelly in the centre of each
8. Lay on paper doilies or surround with bits of asparagus fern
