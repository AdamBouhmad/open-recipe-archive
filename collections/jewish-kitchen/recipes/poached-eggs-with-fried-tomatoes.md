## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- slices of bread
- eggs
- salt water
- two tablespoons of cream
- one teaspoon of any pungent sauce
- one teaspoon of mushroom catsup
- juice of half a lemon
- one teaspoon of flour

## Directions

1. Fry tomatoes (cut one-half inch thick) in butter, pepper and salt
2. Prepare slices of bread cut round and fried in butter
3. Put on a hot platter with a slice of tomato on each
4. Poach as many eggs as required in boiling salt water
5. Lift out very carefully, placing one egg on each tomato
6. Add to the gravy in which tomatoes were fried two tablespoons of cream, one teaspoon of any pungent sauce, one teaspoon of mushroom catsup, juice of half a lemon, and one teaspoon of flour to thicken
7. Cook up once and pour over eggs
8. Serve very hot
