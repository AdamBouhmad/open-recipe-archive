## Ingredients

- hard red tomatoes, not too large
- good salad oil

## Directions

1. Select some hard red tomatoes, not too large
2. Wipe them carefully
3. Put them in a glass jar if you have one
4. Do not press them down
5. Fill the jar with good salad oil
6. The oil can be used for cooking purposes when you have used the tomatoes
