## Ingredients

- fowls
- salt
- pepper
- water
- white cauliflower
- egg sauce or mayonnaise
- boiled tongue or smoked beef

## Directions

1. Fowls are prepared for boiling the same as turkeys
2. Season the water well that they are boiled in
3. Dish up and garnish the same with small white heads or pieces of a large white cauliflower
4. Serve with fowls egg sauce or mayonnaise according to recipe for same
5. Boiled tongue or smoked beef should always accompany them
