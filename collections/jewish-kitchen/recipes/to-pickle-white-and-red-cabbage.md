## Ingredients

- white and red cabbage
- salt
- vinegar
- spices
- cochineal powder
- beet-root
- turmeric powder

## Directions

1. Take off the outside leaves, cut out the stalk
2. Shred the cabbage into a cullender
3. Sprinkle with salt
4. Let it remain for twenty-four hours
5. Drain it
6. Put it into jars
7. Fill up with boiling vinegar, prepared with the usual spices
8. If the cabbage is red, add a little cochineal powdered, or a slice or two of beet-root to make the pickle a fine colour
9. If it is white cabbage, add instead, a little turmeric powder
