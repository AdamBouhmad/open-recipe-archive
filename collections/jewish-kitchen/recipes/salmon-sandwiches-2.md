## Ingredients

- bread
- mayonnaise
- relishing sauce No 34
- salmon
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the bread very even
2. Spread on it some of the mayonnaise or relishing sauce No 34
3. Cut up the salmon small
4. Spread it on lightly
5. Press it down
6. Cut into four
7. Remember to put a little salt and pepper on all fish sandwiches
