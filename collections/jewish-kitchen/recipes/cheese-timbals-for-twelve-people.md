## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- four tablespoons of flour
- one-quarter pound imported Swiss cheese grated
- four egg yolks
- two egg whites
- tomato sauce
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take one pint of milk, four tablespoons of flour, and use enough of the milk to dissolve the flour
2. Put the balance in double boiler
3. When it boils, add the dissolved flour
4. Add one-quarter pound imported Swiss cheese grated
5. Let these two boil for fifteen minutes
6. When cool, add the yolks of four eggs, drop one in at a time and beat
7. Strain through a fine sieve about ten minutes before you put in the pans
8. Beat the whites of two eggs and put in the above and mix
9. Grease timbal forms, fill three-quarters full only
10. Bake in pan of boiling water twenty minutes
11. Let them stand about two minutes, turn out on little plates, and serve with tomato sauce with a sprig of parsley put on top of each one
