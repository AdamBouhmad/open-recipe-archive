## Ingredients

- sugar pea pods
- water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Gather the pods while the seeds are still very small
2. String like beans and cut into pieces
3. Cover with boiling water and boil gently for twenty-five or thirty minutes or until tender
4. Pour off most of the water, saving it for soup
5. Season the rest with salt and butter and serve
