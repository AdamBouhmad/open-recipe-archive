## Ingredients

- three whole eggs
- one cup of granulated sugar
- one pint of cream
- fruit
- pink coloring

## Directions

1. Make same as Vanilla Ice Cream No. 2, omitting the milk
2. If canned fruit is to be used, drain off the juice and add it to the eggs and cream
3. Mash the fruit through a sieve
4. Add it to rest of mixture
5. Freeze the whole
6. If fresh fruits are used, one pint is required
7. Mash fine, strain and sweeten before adding to the cream
8. For peach and strawberry a few drops of pink coloring may be added
9. Bananas must be mashed smooth, but not sweetened
10. Chop all fruits very fine
11. For pineapple, the sliced is preferred to the grated
