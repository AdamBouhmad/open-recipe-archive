## Ingredients

- three and one-half pounds of large red cherries
- two and a half pounds of sugar
- one-quarter pound of seeded raisins
- juice and pulp of three oranges

## Directions

1. Take three and one-half pounds of large red cherries, stone them and cook for fifteen minutes.
2. Heat two and a half pounds of sugar in the oven.
3. Add it to the cherries.
4. Also add one-quarter pound of seeded raisins and the juice and pulp of three oranges.
5. Cook until the mixture is as thick as marmalade.
