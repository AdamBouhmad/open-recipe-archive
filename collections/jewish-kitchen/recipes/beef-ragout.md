## Ingredients

- a small well cut piece of lean beef
- the fat of smoked beef
- good gravy
- allspice
- cloves
- pepper
- salt
- a glass of port wine
- the juice of a lemon
- half a tea-spoonful of cayenne pepper
- a little mushroom ketchup
- forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Take a small well cut piece of lean beef
2. Lard it with the fat of smoked beef
3. Stew it with good gravy, highly seasoned with allspice, cloves, pepper and salt
4. When the meat is well done, remove it from the gravy
5. Skim the gravy carefully and free from every particle of fat
6. Add to it a glass of port wine, the juice of a lemon, half a tea-spoonful of cayenne pepper, and a little mushroom ketchup
7. Glaze the beef when required to have an elegant appearance
8. Poach a few very small forcemeat balls in the gravy
9. Pour the gravy over the meat and arrange the balls round the dish
