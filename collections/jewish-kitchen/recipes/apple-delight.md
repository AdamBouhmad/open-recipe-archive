## Ingredients

- apple sauce
- butter
- chopped peaches and apricots
- blanched almonds
- peach juice

## Directions

1. Put a layer of apple sauce in a buttered pudding dish
2. Dot with butter
3. Add a layer of chopped peaches and apricots
4. Sprinkle with blanched almonds ground rather coarsely
5. Repeat until the pan is full
6. Pour the peach juice over the mixture
7. Bake for one hour
