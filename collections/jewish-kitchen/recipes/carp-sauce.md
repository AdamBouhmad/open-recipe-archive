## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of fresh butter
- dessert spoonful of flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- wine glass of cold water
- spoonful of fine capers

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan 1/2 lb. of fresh butter, a dessert spoonful of flour, salt, pepper, nutmeg
2. Add a wine glass of cold water
3. Put it on the fire and stir it till it comes smooth
4. Taste it
5. Add to it a spoonful of fine capers without the vinegar
