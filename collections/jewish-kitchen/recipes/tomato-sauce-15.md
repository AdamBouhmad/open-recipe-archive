## Ingredients

- one tablespoon butter
- one minced onion
- one tablespoon of flour
- two cups of tomatoes
- one teaspoon of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- pepper
- red pepper
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- one tablespoon of tomato catsup

## Directions

1. Brown one tablespoon butter with one minced onion
2. Add one tablespoon of flour
3. When brown stir in two cups of tomatoes which have previously been cooked and strained
4. Add also one teaspoon of sugar, a pinch of salt, pepper, and red pepper, also one tablespoon of vinegar and one tablespoon of tomato catsup
