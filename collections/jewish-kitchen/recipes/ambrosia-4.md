## Ingredients

- different kinds of fruit
- nuts
- marshmallows
- sugar
- sherry
- whipped cream
- cherry

## Directions

1. Cut up into small pieces different kinds of fruit.
2. Chop up nuts and marshmallows (not too fine).
3. Mix these and sugar, not allowing it to draw too much juice.
4. Flavor with sherry, if you like.
5. Serve individually, putting whipped cream on the top with a cherry.
