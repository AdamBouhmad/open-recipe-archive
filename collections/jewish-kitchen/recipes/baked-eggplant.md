## Ingredients

- eggplant
- salt
- water
- one tablespoon of butter
- one onion, minced
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- one egg yolk
- cracker crumbs (for topping, optional)

## Directions

1. Parboil eggplant until tender, but not soft, in boiling salted water
2. Cut in half crosswise with a sharp knife
3. Scrape out the inside and do not break the skin
4. Heat one tablespoon of butter, add a minced onion, brown
5. Add scraped eggplant, bread crumbs, salt and pepper to taste and an egg yolk
6. Mix well together
7. Refill shells
8. Place in dripping pan in oven
9. Baste with butter or sprinkle cracker crumbs on top with bits of butter
10. Baste often and brown nicely
