## Ingredients

- five eggs
- half a pound of white sugar
- six ounces of flour
- lemon-juice
- lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Beat together five eggs and half a pound of white sugar
2. Add six ounces of flour well dried and sifted, a little lemon-juice and grated lemon-peel
3. Bake in a moderate oven
