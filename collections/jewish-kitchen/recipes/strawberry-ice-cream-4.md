## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of strawberries
- juice of 1/2 a lemon
- 4 oz. of sugar
- 1 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Mash 1/2 pint of strawberries through a sieve
2. Mix with them the juice of 1/2 a lemon and 4 oz. of sugar
3. Add this to a pint of cream
4. Do not boil it, put it in the freezer and work it
