## Ingredients

- dough prepared according to white bread recipe
- beaten egg or sweetened water
- poppy seed

## Directions

1. Make dough according to the white bread recipe
2. Work small pieces of dough into strands a finger long, and take three strands for each loaf
3. Make small as possible
4. Brush with beaten egg or sweetened water and sprinkle with poppy seed
5. Allow them to rise before setting them in the oven
