## Ingredients

- green peppers
- chopped nuts
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Parboil peppers
2. stuff them with a forcemeat made of chopped nuts and bread crumbs moistened with salt and pepper
3. Bake, basting occasionally with melted butter for twenty minutes
