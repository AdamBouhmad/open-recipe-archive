## Ingredients

- eggs
- one tablespoon melted butter per six yolks
- one-half teaspoon mustard per six yolks
- one teaspoon salt per six yolks
- dash of cayenne pepper per six yolks

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs, drop into cold water, remove shells, cut each in half lengthwise
2. Turn out yolks into a bowl
3. Carefully place whites together in pairs, mash yolks with back of a spoon
4. For every six yolks put into bowl one tablespoon melted butter, one-half teaspoon mustard, one teaspoon salt, dash of cayenne pepper
5. Rub these together thoroughly with yolks
6. Make little balls of this paste the size of the yolks
7. Fit one ball into each pair whites
