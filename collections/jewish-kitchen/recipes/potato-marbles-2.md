## Ingredients

- one-half pound of plain mashed potatoes
- generous teaspoon of finely chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- one egg
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Mix one-half pound of plain mashed potatoes smoothly with a generous teaspoon of finely chopped parsley, pepper and salt to taste
2. Beat one egg, add it to the potato, mix well
3. Make it into little balls the size of a cherry
4. Lay a tiny sprig of parsley on each
5. Arrange the balls on a greased tin
6. Bake till a light brown
