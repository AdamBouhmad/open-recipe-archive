## Ingredients

- one-half cup of sugar
- one-fourth cup of water
- five large apples or six small ones
- rich milchig pastry
- sugar and cinnamon
- bits of butter

## Directions

1. Put in saucepan sugar and water and let it boil a few minutes
2. Lay in apples which have previously been peeled and quartered
3. Cover with a lid and steam until tender but not broken
4. Line pie-plate with rich milchig pastry
5. Lay on the apples
6. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and bits of butter
7. Drop a few drops of syrup over all
8. Bake
