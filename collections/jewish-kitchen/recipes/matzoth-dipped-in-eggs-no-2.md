## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one-half tablespoon of salt
- two tablespoons of goose fat or olive oil
- four matzoth
- sugar
- cinnamon
- grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs very light, add one-half tablespoon of salt
2. Heat two tablespoons of goose fat or olive oil in a spider
3. Break four matzoth into large, equal pieces
4. Dip each piece in the egg mixture and fry a light brown on both sides
5. Serve hot, sprinkled with sugar, cinnamon and a little grated lemon rind
