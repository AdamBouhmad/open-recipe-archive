## Ingredients

- soles
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash them well and let them lay in salt for 1/2 an hour before boiling
2. Put them in the fish kettle, just cover them with water
3. Lay them in with the white skin on top; that is the proper side to send them to table
4. 20 minutes will boil a good sized pair
5. As soon as the water boils skim it
