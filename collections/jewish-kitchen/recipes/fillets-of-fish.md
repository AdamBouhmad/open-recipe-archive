## Ingredients

- salmon, soles, or other fish fillets
- fish forcemeat
- good stock
- cayenne pepper
- lemon juice
- mushroom essence
- piccalilli

## Directions

1. Fry fillets of salmon, soles, &c., of a delicate brown according to the receipt already given
2. Serve with a fine gravy
3. If required to be very savory, make a fish forcemeat, and lay it thickly on the fish before frying
4. Fillets dressed in this way are usually arranged round the dish
5. Serve with a sauce made of good stock, thickened and seasoned with cayenne pepper, lemon juice, and mushroom essence
6. Piccalilli are sometimes added cut small
