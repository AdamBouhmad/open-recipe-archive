## Ingredients

- string beans
- salt water
- fresh water
- butter
- three or four large potatoes
- four large tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut off both ends of the beans, string them carefully and break into pieces about an inch in length
2. Boil in salt water
3. When tender drain off this brine and add fresh water boiling from the kettle
4. Add a piece of butter and three or four large potatoes cut into squares
5. Add four large tomatoes, cut up
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a spider, stir into it one tablespoon of flour
8. Thin with milk and add this to the beans
