## Ingredients

- two pounds of raisins
- three quarts of cold water
- a few tablespoons of sugar
- a handful of stick cinnamon

## Directions

1. To two pounds of raisins (cut in half if desired), add three quarts of cold water.
2. Either place the mixture on a corner of the range and let it simmer for two or three days or boil it until one-third of the water has evaporated.
3. A few tablespoons of sugar and a handful of stick cinnamon can be added if additional sweetness and flavoring are wished.
4. When cold strain through a fine cloth.
