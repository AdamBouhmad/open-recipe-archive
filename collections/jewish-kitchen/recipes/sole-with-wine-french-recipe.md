## Ingredients

- sole or fillets of any delicate fish
- white pepper
- salt
- shalot
- claret or white wine
- yolks of three eggs
- juice of a lemon
- two ounces of butter
- chopped parsley
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take a sole or fillets of any delicate fish
2. Lay on a fireproof dish, sprinkle with white pepper, salt and a little shalot
3. Cover with claret or white wine, and let it cook in the oven till done
4. Draw off the liquor in a saucepan and let it boil up
5. Have ready the yolks of three eggs, well stirred (not beaten), the juice of a lemon, and two ounces of butter
6. Put all together in a bowl
7. Little by little add the hot sauce, stirring all the time
8. Pour it over the fish, and sprinkle with chopped parsley
9. Serve very hot
10. A few mushrooms are a palatable addition to this dish
