## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- lemon juice
- lettuce leaves
- French dressing
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the cauliflower carefully, tie in a cloth and cook in boiling salt water until thoroughly tender
2. When done, remove the cloth, pour two tablespoons of lemon juice over the cauliflower and set it on the ice to cool
3. When ready to serve, separate the flowerets, lay them on lettuce leaves
4. Cover with French dressing and sprinkle one tablespoon of chopped parsley over the top
