## Ingredients

- hot cauliflower
- two tablespoons fine bread crumbs
- one tablespoon of hot butter or fat

## Directions

1. Drain and place the hot cauliflower in serving dish
2. Pour over it two tablespoons fine bread crumbs browned in one tablespoon of hot butter or fat
3. Serve hot
