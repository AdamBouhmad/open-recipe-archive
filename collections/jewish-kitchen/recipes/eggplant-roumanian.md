## Ingredients

- onion
- eggplant
- water
- salt
- white pepper
- sour salt
- red tomatoes
- one-fourth cup of rice

## Directions

1. Brown onion
2. Peel eggplant raw, cut in quarters, put in when onions are brown with a little water
3. Stew
4. Add salt, white pepper, sour salt, red tomatoes
5. When half done add one-fourth cup of rice
6. Cook until rice is tender
