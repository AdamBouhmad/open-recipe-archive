## Ingredients

- six large tomatoes
- six large potatoes
- two large onions
- salt
- pepper
- three pints of water
- one-half pint of cream
- one-fourth cup of butter
- paprika
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Omit fish and use same ingredients
2. sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve
