## Ingredients

- fish
- salt water
- vinegar
- onions
- celery root
- parsley
- eggs
- Colman's mustard
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- water
- fish gravy
- green onions
- capers
- shallots
- vinegar pickle
- astragon

## Directions

1. Cook any large fish in salt water, add one cup of vinegar, onions, celery root and parsley
2. When the fish is cooked enough, remove it from the fire, kettle and all, letting the fish remain in its sauce
3. Take the yolks of two eggs, one-half teaspoon of Colman's mustard (dry), salt, pepper, a tablespoon of butter, a tablespoon of vinegar, one-half glass water and some fish gravy
4. Boil in double boiler until thick
5. Take some parsley, green onions, capers, shallots and one large vinegar pickle and some astragon, chop all up very fine
6. Chop up the hard-boiled whites separately and then add the sauce
7. Mix all this together thoroughly, then taste to see if seasoned to suit
