## Ingredients

- brains
- ice-cold salted water
- onion
- celery root
- whole peppers
- salt
- crust of rye bread
- vinegar
- water
- lebkuchen
- brown sugar
- one tablespoon of molasses

## Directions

1. Clean as described above
2. Lay in ice-cold salted water for an hour
3. Cut up an onion
4. Cut a few slices of celery root
5. Prepare a few whole peppers
6. Lay the brains upon a bed of herbs with salt and crust of rye bread
7. Barely cover with vinegar and water
8. Boil about fifteen minutes
9. Lift out the brains with a perforated skimmer
10. Lay upon a platter to cool
11. Take a lebkuchen, some brown sugar, a tablespoon of molasses
