## Ingredients

- pineapple slices
- sherry or white wine
- sugar
- batter
- hot oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Soak slices of pineapple in sherry or white wine with a little sugar and let stand one hour
2. Drain and dip slices in batter
3. Fry in hot oil
4. Drain on brown paper
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
