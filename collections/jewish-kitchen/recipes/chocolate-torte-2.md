## Ingredients

- nine eggs
- one-half pound of pulverized sugar
- one-half pound of almonds, half cut and grated
- one-half pound of finest vanilla chocolate grated
- one-half pound of raisins, cut and seeded
- seven soda crackers, rolled to a powder
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- juice of three lemons
- one-fourth glass of wine

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth and stir in last
2. Beat yolks with sugar until very light
3. Add chocolate
4. Proceed as with other torten
