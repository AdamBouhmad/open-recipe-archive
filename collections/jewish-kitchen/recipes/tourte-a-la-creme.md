## Ingredients

- very rich puff paste
- a fine custard or any rich preserves
- well whipped cream

## Directions

1. Roll out some very rich puff paste to the thickness of one inch
2. Cut two pieces with the larger tin cutter
3. Press the smaller cutter through one of these pieces and remove the border which will be formed round it
4. Lay this border very evenly upon the other piece of paste and slightly press to make it adhere
5. Place the tourte in an oven to bake for about twenty minutes
6. Let it become cool, but not cold
7. Fill it with a fine custard or with any rich preserves
8. If the latter, a well whipped cream may be laid lightly over
9. The pastry may be glazed if approved
