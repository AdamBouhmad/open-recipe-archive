## Ingredients

- a pound of chuck steak
- a set of goose or turkey giblets
- a few small forced meat balls

## Directions

1. Take a pound of chuck steak and cut it in pieces the length of 2 inches long and square
2. Have a set of goose or turkey giblets well washed and cut in small pieces
3. The liver must be broiled first
4. Have ready a few small forced meat balls
