## Ingredients

- ten small, round potatoes
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- mustard seed
- celery seed
- sugar
- egg
- goose or chicken fat
- vinegar
- hot water
- hard-boiled eggs
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Boil ten potatoes (small, round ones preferred) in their skins
2. When done, peel them while still hot and slice in thin, round slices
3. Spread over the potatoes one onion, sliced fine
4. Sprinkle generously with salt and pepper
5. Add one tablespoon of mustard seed, one-half tablespoon of celery seed, and one-half tablespoon of sugar
6. Beat one egg until light
7. Pour two tablespoons of goose or chicken fat, melted, over the eggs
8. Stir well
9. Add one-half cup of vinegar
10. Pour over the seasoned potatoes
11. Add one-quarter cup of hot water and if necessary, add a little more vinegar, salt or pepper
12. One or two chopped hard-boiled eggs may be added to improve the salad
13. Line a salad bowl with lettuce leaves
14. Pour in the salad and decorate the top with grated hard-boiled eggs
