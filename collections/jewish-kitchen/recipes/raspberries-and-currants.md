## Ingredients

- raspberries
- currants
- pulverized sugar
- white or black currants or blackberries
- cream or wine

## Directions

1. Mix berries together
2. Set on ice until ready to serve
3. Pile in a mound, strewing plenty of pulverized sugar among them
4. Garnish the base with white or black currants (blackberries look pretty also) in bunches
5. Eat with cream or wine
