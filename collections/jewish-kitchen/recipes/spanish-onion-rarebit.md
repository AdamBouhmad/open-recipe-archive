## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- tabasco sauce
- prepared mustard
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Boil two large onions until very soft
2. drain and chop
3. return to the saucepan with a small piece of butter
4. Add milk, salt, pepper, a dash of tabasco sauce, one teaspoon of prepared mustard, one-half cup of grated cheese
5. Stir until of the consistency of custard
