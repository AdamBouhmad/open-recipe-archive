## Ingredients

- veal breast
- bread stuffing
- caraway seed
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Have the butcher cut a pocket to receive the stuffing
2. Prepare bread stuffing and sew up the pocket
3. Roast as directed for roast veal
4. Sprinkle a little caraway seed on top of the roast
5. A tablespoon of lemon juice adds to the flavor
6. Baste often
