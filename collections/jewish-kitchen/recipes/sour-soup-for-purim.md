## Ingredients

- one pound soup meat
- two soup bones
- boiling water
- two leeks, cut in slices like noodles
- cooked tomatoes, cooled and strained
- cauliflower
- two tablespoons sugar
- pinch sour salt
- pepper
- salt
- two egg yolks, beaten up with salt and cold water

## Directions

1. Take one pound of soup meat and two soup bones, put on to boil in boiling water
2. Cut two leeks in slices like noodles
3. Add some cooked tomatoes which have been cooled and strained, some cauliflower, two tablespoons of sugar, a pinch sour salt, pepper and salt
4. Let cook steadily
5. When the soup is done thicken it with two egg yolks that have been beaten up with a little salt and some cold water
6. Do not cook after adding yolks of eggs
