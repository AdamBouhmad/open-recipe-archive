## Ingredients

- pint of syrup
- juice of 4 lemons
- fresh rind of lemon
- lump of sugar

## Directions

1. Add to the pint of syrup the juice of 4 lemons well-strained
2. Grate some of the fresh rind on a lump of sugar
3. Put it in
4. Mix it well for 10 minutes
5. Put it in the freezer, and work same as last
