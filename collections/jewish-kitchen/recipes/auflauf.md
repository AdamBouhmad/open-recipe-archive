## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- one-half cup of sifted flour
- yolks of three eggs
- salt
- beaten whites of three eggs
- butter
- jelly or preserves

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of milk and when boiling stir in quickly one-half cup of sifted flour and work smooth until all lumps are out and it is the consistency of soft mashed potatoes.
2. Stir all the while over fire.
3. When smooth remove from stove and while yet warm break in, one by one, yolks of three eggs, a pinch of salt, then the beaten whites of three eggs.
4. Bake in well-buttered hot square pans, in very hot oven, from fifteen to twenty minutes.
5. Serve as soon as done with jelly or preserves.
