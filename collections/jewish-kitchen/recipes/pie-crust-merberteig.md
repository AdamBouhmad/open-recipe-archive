## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- four cups of sifted flour
- a pinch of salt
- a tablespoon of brown sugar
- the yolk of an egg
- a wine-glass of brandy
- one-half cup of ice-water

## Directions

1. Rub one cup of butter to a cream
2. Add four cups of sifted flour, a pinch of salt and a tablespoon of brown sugar; work these together until the flour looks like sand
3. Take the yolk of an egg, a wine-glass of brandy, one-half cup of ice-water and work it into the flour lightly
4. Do not use the hands; knead with a knife or wooden spoon, knead as little as possible
5. Roll out the dough using as little flour as possible
6. Work quickly, handle dough as little as possible and bake in a hot oven
