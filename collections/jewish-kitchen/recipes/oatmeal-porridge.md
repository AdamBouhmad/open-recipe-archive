## Ingredients

- one cup of oatmeal
- two cups of salted boiling water
- one teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Put a cup of meal into two cups of salted boiling water (a teaspoon of salt)
2. Let it cook in a double boiler the required time
3. Keep covered until done; then remove the cover and let the moisture escape
