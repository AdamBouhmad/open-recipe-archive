## Ingredients

- peaches
- sliced pineapple
- apricots
- strawberries
- raspberries
- juice of two lemons
- two oranges
- one cup of water
- one pound sugar
- one-half teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
- grated rind of lemon
- one cup red wine
- one-half glass of Madeira, arrak or rum

## Directions

1. Peel and pit some peaches, cut in slices and add as much sliced pineapple, some apricots, strawberries and raspberries, put these in a dish.
2. Prepare a syrup of juice of two lemons, two oranges, one cup of water and one pound sugar, a half teaspoon of powdered cinnamon, grated rind of lemon, add one cup red wine and a half glass of Madeira, arrak or rum.
3. Boil this syrup for five minutes, then pour over the fruit, tossing the fruit from time to time until cool.
4. Place on ice and serve cold.
