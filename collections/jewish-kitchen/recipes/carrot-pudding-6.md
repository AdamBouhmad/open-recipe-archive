## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one-third cup of butter
- one cup of grated carrots
- one cup of grated potatoes
- one cup of raisins
- one cup of currants
- two cups of bread crumbs
- one-half teaspoon of baking-soda
- one teaspoon of cloves
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one teaspoon of allspice
- a little syrup
- four tablespoons of whiskey

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Add a little syrup and four tablespoons of whiskey
3. Steam four hours
4. Serve with hard sauce
