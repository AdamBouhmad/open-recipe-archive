## Ingredients

- parsley roots (3 or 4)
- onions (several)
- water (1 pint)
- lemon juice
- vinegar
- saffron
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- fish
- flour
- butter
- egg (1 yolk)

## Directions

1. Take three or four parsley roots, cut them into pieces
2. Slice several onions and boil in a pint of water till tender
3. Season with lemon juice, vinegar, saffron, pepper, salt, and mace
4. Add the fish, and let it stew till nearly finished
5. Remove it, and thicken the gravy with a little flour and butter, and the yolk of one egg
6. Return the fish to the stew-pan, with balls made as directed in the preceding receipt, and boil up
