## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- two eggs or the yolks of three eggs
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Put the milk on to heat in a double boiler.
2. Beat the eggs thoroughly with the sugar.
3. Into them pour the hot milk, stirring to prevent lumps.
4. Return all to the double boiler and cook until the custard coats the spoon, but no longer.
5. If the mixture should curdle, set the boiler in a pan of cold water and beat with a wire egg-beater until smooth.
6. When the steam passes off add the vanilla or other flavoring.
