## Ingredients

- carp
- chopped onion
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- 2 or 3 potatoes
- 2 eggs
- parsley
- marjoram
- nutmeg
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Split open the carp and lay it in a baking dish with some chopped onion, a little oil, vinegar, pepper and salt
2. Mash 2 or 3 potatoes with 2 eggs, parsley, marjoram, a little nutmeg
3. Lay it over the carp
4. Let it bake a nice colour
5. Send it up with sauce made in the same way with no caper, but flavoured stronger of the anchovy sauce
