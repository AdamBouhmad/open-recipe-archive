## Ingredients

- pfarvel
- one-quarter cup of butter or other fat
- one quart of boiling water
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one can or one-half peck of green peas strained

## Directions

1. Make the pfarvel
2. Heat one-quarter cup of butter or other fat, add the pfarvel and when golden brown
3. Add one quart of boiling water, one-half cup of sugar, one-half teaspoon of salt, and one can or one-half peck of green peas strained
4. Set in moderate oven and bake one-half hour or until every kernel stands out separately
5. Serve hot
