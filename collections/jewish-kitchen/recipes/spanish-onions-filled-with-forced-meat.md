## Ingredients

- 3 Spanish onions
- salt
- 1 lb. forced meat
- pepper
- marjoram
- grated nutmeg
- onion hearts
- 1/2 pint stock
- 1 spoonful flour
- juice of 1 lemon
- browning

## Directions

1. Boil 3 Spanish onions in water with some salt for three-quarters of an hour
2. Take them out and put them to drain in a colander
3. When cold, make a slit down with a knife to enable you to strip each leaf without breaking them
4. Place each onion on a separate plate
5. Prepare 1 lb. of forced meat as for balls and season it well
6. Divide it in three and form them into balls
7. Begin with the smallest leaf, not the heart, and go on replacing the leaves till you make up the three onions again
8. Have in a stewpan the hearts of the onions, some salt, pepper, a little marjoram, grated nutmeg, and 1/2 pint of stock
9. Put in the onions and let them stew for an hour
10. Rub in a basin one spoonful of flour, the juice of a lemon, and a little browning to colour lightly
11. Serve with egg sauce or tomato sauce
