## Ingredients

- one-half pint glass of red raspberry or currant juice
- one quarter cup of sugar
- four eggs
- one quarter cup of powdered sugar
- cold whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take a half-pint glass of red raspberry or currant juice and mix it with a quarter cup of sugar.
2. Beat the whites of four eggs to a stiff froth and add gradually a quarter cup of powdered sugar.
3. Press the raspberries through a strainer to avoid seeds and by degrees beat the juice with the sugar and eggs until so stiff that it stands in peaks.
4. Chill it thoroughly and serve in a glass dish half filled with cold whipped cream.
5. Heap on the mixture by the spoonful, like floating island.
