## Ingredients

- perch
- steamed onion
- one well-beaten egg
- two tomatoes
- bread crumbs
- parsley or celery
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take perch and stuff with steamed onion to which has been added one well-beaten egg, two tomatoes cut up in small pieces, some bread crumbs, chopped parsley or celery, salt and pepper to taste.
2. Bake until the fish is nicely browned.
