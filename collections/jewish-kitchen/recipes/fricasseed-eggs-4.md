## Ingredients

- six hard-boiled eggs
- flour
- egg
- one-half teaspoon of oil
- one-half teaspoon of vinegar
- a few drops of onion juice
- one teaspoon chopped parsley
- a little nutmeg
- salt
- vermicelli
- deep beef drippings
- one tablespoon of fat
- one tablespoon of flour
- one-half cup of white wine
- one cup of bouillon
- cayenne
- one teaspoon chopped chives
- one teaspoon chopped parsley
- some chopped olives
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take six hard-boiled eggs, remove shells.
2. Roll them in flour, then in egg to which has been added one-half teaspoon of oil, one-half teaspoon of vinegar, a few drops of onion juice, one teaspoon chopped parsley, a little nutmeg and salt.
3. When quite covered, roll in vermicelli that has been broken into fine bits and fry in deep beef drippings.
4. For the sauce: brown one tablespoon of fat and one tablespoon of flour together.
5. Add one-half cup of white wine and a cup of bouillon.
6. Season with salt and cayenne and boil five minutes.
7. Add one teaspoon each of chopped chives and parsley, some chopped olives and mushrooms.
8. Bring to a boil again and pour over the eggs.
