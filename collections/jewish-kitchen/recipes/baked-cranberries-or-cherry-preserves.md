## Ingredients

- four quarts of large, perfect cranberries, or four pounds of large cherries
- four pounds of sugar
- one tablespoon of hot water

## Directions

1. Pick over, wash and drain four quarts of large, perfect cranberries; or stem and then stone four pounds of large cherries, use a cherry pitter so cherries remain whole.
2. Place a tablespoon of hot water in a jar, then alternately in layers cherries or cranberries and sugar (with sugar on top).
3. Cover closely.
4. Bake in a very slow oven two hours.
5. Let stand.
6. Keep in a cool, dry place.
