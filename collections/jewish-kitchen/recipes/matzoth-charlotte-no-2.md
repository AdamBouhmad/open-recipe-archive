## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup of sugar
- pinch of salt
- three matzoth
- one grated apple
- one lemon
- one-fourth cup of almonds
- one-fourth cup of raisins

## Directions

1. Prepare four egg yolks with one cup of sugar and pinch of salt
2. Soak three matzoth in water and squeeze out
3. Add one grated apple, one lemon rind and juice
4. Add one-fourth cup of almonds, and one-fourth cup of raisins
5. Put the stiffly-beaten whites of eggs in last, before putting into oven
6. Bake in an even oven about one-half to three-quarters of an hour
7. To be eaten warm
