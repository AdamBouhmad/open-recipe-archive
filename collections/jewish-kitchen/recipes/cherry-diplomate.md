## Ingredients

- white cake
- maraschino or fine brandy
- plain white ice-cream
- cherry ice
- candied cherries
- strawberry ice-cream or plain white vanilla
- ice

## Directions

1. Line a mold with white cake, thinly sliced
2. Previously dip cake in maraschino or some other fine brandy
3. Fill in with plain white ice-cream
4. Add a layer of cherry ice
5. Add a layer of candied cherries
6. Add a layer of cherry ice
7. Add a layer of strawberry ice-cream or the plain white vanilla
8. Finish it up with a layer of cake again
9. Be sure to dip the cake in the maraschino
10. Cover all up tight and pack in ice until wanted
