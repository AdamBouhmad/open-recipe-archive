## Ingredients

- cold fish
- onion
- parsley
- marjoram
- nutmeg
- pepper
- dried currants
- eggs
- oil or butter

## Directions

1. chop the cold fish nicely with onion, parsley, a little marjoram, grated nutmeg, pepper, and a few dried currants
2. mix it up with 2 or 3 eggs
3. fry in hot oil or butter
4. Dish them up on a fish napkin with parsley
