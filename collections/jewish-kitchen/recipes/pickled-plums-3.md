## Ingredients

- 7 pounds plums
- 4 pounds white sugar
- 2 ounces stick cinnamon
- 1 ounce cloves
- 1 pint best pickling vinegar

## Directions

1. Prick the plums with a large needle then weigh them
2. Boil the vinegar, sugar and spices
3. Pour boiling hot over the fruit, which must be packed in a large jar
4. Repeat this three times
5. While the vinegar boils the third time, pack the plums in glass jars and pour the syrup over the plums
6. When cold seal
