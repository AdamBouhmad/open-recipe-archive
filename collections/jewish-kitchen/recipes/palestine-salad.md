## Ingredients

- dozen fine Jerusalem artichokes
- salad mixture

## Directions

1. Take a dozen fine Jerusalem artichokes, boil till tender, let the water strain off
2. When cold cut them in quarters, and pour over a fine salad mixture
3. The artichokes should lay in the sauce half an hour before serving
