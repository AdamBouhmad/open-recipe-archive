## Ingredients

- sweet apples
- beef drippings
- brown sugar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Quarter and core five apples without paring
2. Rut into the pot and melt beef drippings
3. When hot, lay a layer of apples in, skin down
4. Sprinkle with brown sugar
5. When nearly done, turn and brown
6. Place on a platter
7. Sprinkle with sugar
8. Steam in a closely covered iron pot for three-quarters of an hour
