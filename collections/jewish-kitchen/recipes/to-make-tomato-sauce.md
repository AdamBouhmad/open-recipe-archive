## Ingredients

- a dozen large ripe tomatoes
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls of oil
- 2 teeth of garlic
- a little chili vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a dozen large ripe tomatoes
2. Slice them
3. Put them in a preserving pan
4. Let them simmer over a slow fire till all the juice is dry
5. Pulp it through a sieve
6. Add salt and white pepper
7. Fry 2 tablespoonfuls of oil with 2 teeth of garlic
8. When the garlic is brown take it out
9. Put in the pulp with a little chili vinegar to flavour
10. Do not make it too hot
11. Boil it for 10 minutes over a slow fire
12. When quite cold put it in small bottles
13. Cork tightly and seal them with melted rosin
