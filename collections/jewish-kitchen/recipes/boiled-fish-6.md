## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- vinegar
- onion
- celery head
- parsley roots

## Directions

1. Allow ten minutes to the first pound and five minutes for each additional pound
2. Set fish on to boil in hot water from the teakettle, adding salt and a dash of vinegar to keep the meat firm
3. Add an onion, a head of celery and parsley roots for flavor
4. If serving the fish whole, tie it in a napkin and lay it on an old plate at the bottom of the kettle
5. Fish is done when a fin pulled out comes easily and the meat is an opaque white
6. Avoid using too much water
