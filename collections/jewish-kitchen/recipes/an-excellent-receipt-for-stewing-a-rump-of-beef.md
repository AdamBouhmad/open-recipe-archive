## Ingredients

- a large onion
- four bay leaves
- a little parsley
- half an ounce of ground ginger
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- a blade of mace
- a little ground allspice
- some lemon sliced
- some lemon peel grated
- three parts of a cup of vinegar
- a calf's-foot cut in small pieces
- a pint of water

## Directions

1. Chop fine a large onion, four bay leaves, and a little parsley
2. add to these half an ounce of ground ginger, a tea-spoonful of salt, a blade of mace, a little ground allspice, some lemon sliced, and some of the peel grated
3. rub all these ingredients well into the meat
4. place it into a stew-pan with three parts of a cup of vinegar, a calf's-foot cut in small pieces and a pint of water
5. stew gently till tender
6. when the fat must be carefully skimmed off the gravy, which must be strained and poured over the meat
