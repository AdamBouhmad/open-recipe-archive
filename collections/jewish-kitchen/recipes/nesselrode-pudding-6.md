## Ingredients

- one-half pound of shelled and skinned chestnuts
- yolks of five eggs
- one-half pound of sugar
- one pint of sweet cream
- salt
- one-fourth pound of crystallized cherries
- one-fourth pound of crystallized pineapple
- one ounce of citron
- one-fourth cup of stoned raisins
- one-half cup of maraschino cordial
- one pint cream
- two tablespoons of powdered sugar
- rock salt and ice

## Directions

1. Put one-half pound of shelled and skinned chestnuts in cold water
2. Let them boil until very tender
3. Press them through a puree sieve
4. Beat the yolks of five eggs with one-half pound of sugar until light
5. Add the mashed chestnuts
6. Stir in one pint of sweet cream
7. Put on to boil in a double boiler
8. Add a few grains of salt
9. Stir until the mixture begins to boil
10. Remove at once from fire and set aside to cool
11. In a bowl put one-fourth pound of crystallized cherries, cut in half; one-fourth pound of crystallized pineapple cut up, one ounce of citron cut fine, one-fourth cup of stoned raisins and one-half cup of maraschino cordial
12. Put the chestnut cream in a freezer
13. Freeze ten minutes
14. Add one pint cream that has been whipped stiff with two tablespoons of powdered sugar
15. Turn until it begins to get stiff
16. Add the fruits and turn awhile longer
17. Pack in a pudding-mold in rock salt and ice two hours
