## Ingredients

- beer
- water
- sugar
- grated crust of stale rye bread
- stick cinnamon
- lemon juice
- zwieback

## Directions

1. Mix the beer with one-third water
2. boil with sugar and the grated crust of stale rye bread
3. add stick cinnamon and a little lemon juice
4. Pour over small pieces of zwieback (rusk)
