## Ingredients

- one cup of matzoth meal
- one cup of boiling soup stock or wine
- one tablespoon of chopped almonds
- one teaspoon of sugar
- salt
- yolks of four eggs
- whites of four eggs
- butter or goose grease
- powdered sugar
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Sift one cup of matzoth meal in a bowl
2. Stir into it one cup of boiling soup stock or wine
3. When mixed add one tablespoon of chopped almonds, one teaspoon of sugar, a pinch of salt and the yolks of four eggs well beaten
4. Add the stiffly-beaten whites of the four eggs
5. Fry by tablespoonfuls in boiling hot butter or goose grease
6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve with wine sauce
