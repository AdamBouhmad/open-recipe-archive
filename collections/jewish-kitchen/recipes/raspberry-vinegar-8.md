## Ingredients

- two quarts of raspberries
- two quarts of vinegar
- two quarts of fresh raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of raspberries in a bowl and cover them with two quarts of vinegar
2. cover and stand in a cool place for two days
3. Mash the berries
4. strain the vinegar through cheese-cloth
5. pour it over two quarts of fresh raspberries
6. let stand for another two days
7. strain and put in a preserving kettle with sugar, allowing a pound of sugar to a pint of juice
8. Heat slowly, skimming when the vinegar begins to boil
9. Boil twenty minutes and put in sterilized bottles
10. Serve as a drink, using two tablespoons to a glass of water
