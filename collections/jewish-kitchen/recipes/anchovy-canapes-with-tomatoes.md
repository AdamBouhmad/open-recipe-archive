## Ingredients

- thin slice toast
- anchovy paste
- egg
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. For each person take a thin slice toast covered with anchovy paste
2. Upon this place whole egg which has been boiled four minutes, so that it can be pealed whole and the yolk is still soft
3. Around the toast put tomato sauce
