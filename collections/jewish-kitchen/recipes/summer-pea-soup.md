## Ingredients

- a peck of peas
- good stock
- chopped lettuce
- cucumber
- mint
- pepper
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a peck of peas, separate the old from the young
2. Boil the former till they are quite tender in good stock
3. Pass them through a sieve, and return them to the stock
4. Add the young peas, a little chopped lettuce, small pieces of cucumber fried to a light brown, a little bit of mint, pepper, and salt
5. Two or three lumps of sugar give a fine flavor
