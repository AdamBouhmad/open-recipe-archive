## Ingredients

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 6 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1.5 teaspoons baking-powder
- 1 lemon or 1 orange
- 0.75 cup milk

## Directions

1. Take one cup of powdered sugar, one-half cup of butter rubbed to a cream
2. Add yolks of six eggs and stir until very light
3. Sift two cups of flour with one and one-half teaspoons of baking-powder sifted in well (sift the flour two or three times)
4. Grate in the peel of a lemon or an orange, add the juice also
5. Add three-quarters cup of milk alternately with the flour
6. Bake in moderate oven
