## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. To one pint of strawberries take one pint of sugar and one-half cup of water
2. Bring the sugar and water to a boil
3. Add the strawberries and cook ten minutes
4. Remove the berries carefully with a skimmer and cook the syrup until it is of the consistency of jelly
5. Return the berries to the syrup; bring all to a boil
6. When cool put in glass tumblers
