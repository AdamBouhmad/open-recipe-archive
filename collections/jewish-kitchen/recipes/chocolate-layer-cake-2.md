## Ingredients

- One scant half cup of butter
- One cup of sugar
- One-half cup of sweet milk
- Yolks of two eggs
- Whites of two eggs
- One-half cup of sifted flour
- One-half cup of milk
- One cup of grated chocolate
- One-half cup of granulated sugar
- Yolk of one egg
- One and one-half cups of sifted flour
- Two teaspoons of baking-powder
- One teaspoon of vanilla flavoring
- Plain white icing

## Directions

1. Stir one scant half cup of butter to a cream with one cup of sugar
2. Add alternately one-half cup of sweet milk and yolks of two eggs which you have previously beaten until quite light
3. Add whites of two
4. Add one-half cup of sifted flour
5. Make a custard of one-half cup of milk with one cup of grated chocolate and one-half cup of granulated sugar
6. Boil until thick
7. Add the yolk of one egg
8. Remove from the fire and stir until cool
9. Add this to the cake batter
10. Add one and one-half cups of sifted flour, two teaspoons of baking-powder and one teaspoon of vanilla flavoring
11. Bake in layers
12. Ice between and on top with plain white icing flavored to taste
13. You may substitute almond or colored icing
