## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- veal gravy made with the trimmings
- fresh button mushrooms
- a piece of lemon peel
- the yolks of two beaten eggs
- forcemeat in which mushrooms must be minced
- thin slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Cut them in proper shapes
2. Put them in a veal gravy made with the trimmings enough to cover them
3. Season delicately, and let them simmer till quite tender, but not long enough to lose their shape
4. Remove the meat when done
5. Take all fat from the gravy, and thicken it with the yolks of two beaten eggs
6. Poach small balls of forcemeat in which mushrooms must be minced in the gravy when about to be served
7. Return the meat to the saucepan to be made hot
8. When placed in the dish, garnish with thin slices of lemon
