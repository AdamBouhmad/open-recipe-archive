## Ingredients

- two eggs
- four eggs
- one cup of powdered sugar
- a quarter of a cup of pounded almonds
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Make noodles with two eggs
2. Boil in boiling salt water for ten minutes and drain
3. Beat the yolks of four eggs with one cup of powdered sugar until light
4. Add a quarter of a cup of pounded almonds and a pinch of salt
5. Add the drained noodles and the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth
6. Mix well
7. Pour into a greased pudding mold and boil one and one-half hours
