## Ingredients

- wine
- one egg per glass
- sugar to taste
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. To each glass of wine allow one egg, beat up, and add sugar to taste.
2. Add wine gradually and grated nutmeg.
3. Beat whites separately and mix.
