## Ingredients

- breast of veal
- good beef gravy
- white pepper
- salt
- couple of sliced onions
- oil
- piece of whole ginger
- rich forcemeat
- three cold hard boiled eggs
- lemon juice
- half a glass of white wine or a table-spoonful of walnut liquor
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Cut the breast of veal in pieces and fry them lightly
2. Put them into a stewpan with a good beef gravy, seasoned with white pepper, salt, a couple of sliced onions previously browned in a little oil, and a piece of whole ginger
3. Let it simmer very slowly for two hours taking care to remove the scum or fat
4. Spread rich forcemeat about an inch thick over three cold hard boiled eggs
5. Fry these for a few moments and put them in the saucepan with the veal
6. Before serving, cut these balls in quarters, and render the gravy more savory by the addition of lemon juice and half a glass of white wine, or a table-spoonful of walnut liquor
7. If the gravy is not sufficiently thick by long stewing, stir in a little browned flour
