## Ingredients

- an onion
- a tablespoonful of oil
- a pint of water
- a little salt
- pepper
- a little dried saffron
- a teacupful of rice
- 3 or 4 tomatoes

## Directions

1. Slice an onion in a small saucepan with a tablespoonful of oil
2. Let it fry
3. Put in a pint of water, a little salt, pepper, and a little dried saffron
4. Wash a teacupful of rice
5. Slice in 3 or 4 tomatoes
6. Let it simmer at the side of the fire till it has absorbed all the water and the rice is quite firm and dry
7. Dish this up round the knuckle of veal
