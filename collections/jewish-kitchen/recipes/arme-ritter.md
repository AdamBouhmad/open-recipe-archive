## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- two-thirds cup of milk
- six slices of stale bread
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs slightly
2. add one-half teaspoon of salt and two-thirds cup of milk
3. dip six slices of stale bread in the mixture
4. Have a griddle hot and well buttered
5. brown the bread on each side
6. Serve hot with cinnamon and sugar or a sauce
