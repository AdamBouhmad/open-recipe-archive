## Ingredients

- bread
- thin slices of Neufchatel cheese
- finely chopped olives
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Spread bread with thin slices of Neufchatel cheese.
2. Cover with finely chopped olives moistened with mayonnaise dressing.
