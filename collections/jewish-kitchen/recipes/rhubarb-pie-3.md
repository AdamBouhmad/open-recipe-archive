## Ingredients

- rich crust
- sugar
- flour
- rhubarb
- strawberries or raspberries
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a very rich crust, and over the bottom layer sprinkle a large tablespoon of sugar and a good teaspoon of flour.
2. Fill half-full of rhubarb that has been cut up.
3. Scatter in one-fourth cup of strawberries or raspberries.
4. Sprinkle with more sugar and flour.
5. Over the top dot bits of butter and another dusting of flour.
6. Use a good cup of sugar to a pie.
7. Pinch the crusts together well after wetting them, to prevent the juice from cooking out.
