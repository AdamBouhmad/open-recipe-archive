## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- a pair of sweetbreads or calf's brains
- celery
- one can of French peas
- mayonnaise
- lettuce leaf

## Directions

1. Take cucumbers and cut lengthwise to serve the salad in.
2. Scrape out the inside and salt well, then squeeze and use this to mix with the filling.
3. Take a pair of sweetbreads, or calf's brains, wash well, and boil.
4. When done, throw in cold water at once and skim them.
5. Chop fine, add bunch of celery (if you can get it), one can of French peas, scraped part of cucumber.
6. Mix all together and season.
7. Make a mayonnaise, mix with it, and fill the cucumber shells.
8. Keep all cold, and serve on lettuce leaf.
