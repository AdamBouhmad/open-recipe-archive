## Ingredients

- 1 white and fine skin turbot
- boiling water
- salt
- 1 lemon
- fried smelts
- horseradish
- parsley
- caper sauce or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. An hour before dinner, put the turbot into boiling water and salt
2. Rub the juice of a lemon over it
3. Let it boil fast at first, then draw the fish-kettle to the side
4. Slit the skin at the back a little to prevent it breaking
5. If done before wanted, lift the steamer out of the water and put it across the kettle with a cloth to keep it warm
6. Dish it on a fish napkin garnished with fried smelts, horseradish, and parsley
7. Serve with caper, anchovy sauce, or sauce No. 33
