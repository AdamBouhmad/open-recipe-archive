## Ingredients

- butter
- six muffin rings
- sliced apples
- one and one-half cups of pastry flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- three level teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-fourth of a cup of shortening
- three-fourths of a cup of milk or water
- cream
- sugar
- hard sauce
- fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Butter six muffin rings and set them on a shallow agate pan which has been well buttered
2. Fill the rings with sliced apples
3. Make a dough of one and one-half cups of pastry flour sifted several times with one-half teaspoon of salt and three level teaspoons of baking-powder
4. Chop into the dry ingredients one-fourth of a cup of shortening, gradually add three-fourths of a cup of milk or water
5. Drop the dough on the apples on the rings
6. Let bake about twenty minutes
7. With a spatula remove each dumpling from the ring, place on dish with the crust side down
8. Serve with cream and sugar, hard sauce or with a fruit sauce
