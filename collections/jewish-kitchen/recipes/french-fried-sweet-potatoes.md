## Ingredients

- uncooked sweet potatoes
- fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and cut small uncooked sweet potatoes into quarters.
2. Dry them and lower them into boiling hot fat.
3. Brown thoroughly; remove with a skimmer; drain and dry on paper.
4. Sprinkle with salt and serve.
