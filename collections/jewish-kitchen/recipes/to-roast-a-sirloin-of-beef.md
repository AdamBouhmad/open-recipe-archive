## Ingredients

- sirloin of beef, 14 to 16 lbs.
- salt
- pepper
- browning
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Prepare exactly the same as ribs
2. Roast for about 3 hours
3. Dish up in a well-dish with horseradish round
4. Serve with a good gravy, adding a little browning to make it look rich
5. Strain the gravy carefully
