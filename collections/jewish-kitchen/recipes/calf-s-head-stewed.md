## Ingredients

- calf's head
- quart of stock made from shin of beef
- eschalots
- small head of celery
- small bunch of sweet herbs
- onion
- carrot
- mace
- dozen cloves
- piece of lemon peel
- sprig of parsley
- salt and pepper
- fine forcemeat balls
- egg
- brains
- glass and half of white wine
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and soak the head till the cheek-bone can be easily removed
2. Parboil it and cut it into pieces of moderate size
3. Place them in about a quart of stock made from shin of beef
4. Season the gravy highly with eschalots, a small head of celery, a small bunch of sweet herbs, an onion, a carrot, a little mace, a dozen cloves, a piece of lemon peel, and a sprig of parsley, salt and pepper
5. Strain the gravy before the head is added
6. Prepare fine forcemeat balls rolled in egg and fried
7. Make small fritters with the brains
8. When ready for serving, add a glass and half of white wine and the juice of a lemon to the gravy
9. Serve the forcemeat balls and brain fritters in the dish
