## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 lb. of forced meat
- chopped onion
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- dark mushrooms
- quarter pint of water
- small meat balls
- flour
- juice of a lemon
- browning
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil 4 eggs very hard, throw them into cold water for 5 minutes, then peel them
2. Have ready 1 lb. of forced meat well seasoned, divide it in 4 equal portions
3. Roll each egg in one piece fixing them well so that you cannot see where the eggs were put in
4. Press them by putting your fingers in cold water
5. Chop an onion very fine, a tooth of garlic, salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg and a few of the dark mushrooms, if you can get them, chopped fine
6. Put all this in a stewpan with quarter pint of water, let it boil up
7. Put in the large balls and a few small meat balls, let them cook gently for 1 hour
8. When done rub in a basin a spoonful of flour with the juice of a lemon, a little browning
9. Stir it into the saucepan, take out the eggs, put them on a plate
10. Cut them each in halves, place them in the dish, put round the small balls
11. Pour the gravy round the dish, be careful not to put it over the eggs
12. Garnish with parsley
