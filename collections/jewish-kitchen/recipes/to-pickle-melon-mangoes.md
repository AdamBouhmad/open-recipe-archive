## Ingredients

- melons
- brine
- spices
- mustard-seed
- garlic
- spiced vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the melons in half
2. Remove the pulpy part and the seeds
3. Soak the halves for a week in strong brine
4. Fill them with the usual spices, mustard-seed and garlic
5. Tie them together with packthread
6. Put them in jars
7. Pour over boiling spiced vinegar
