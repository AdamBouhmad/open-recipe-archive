## Ingredients

- neck of mutton
- cutlet preparation
- vegetable puree or sauce

## Directions

1. Have a neck of mutton, cut the bones short, and remove the chine bone completely
2. Cut chops off so thin that every other one shall be without bone
3. Trim them carefully, that all the chops shall bear the same appearance
4. Flatten them well
5. Cover them with a cutlet preparation
6. Fry of a delicate brown
7. Serve with a fine puree of any vegetable that may be approved, or any sauce that may be selected
8. Arrange in various ways in the dish, either round the dish or in a circle in the centre
