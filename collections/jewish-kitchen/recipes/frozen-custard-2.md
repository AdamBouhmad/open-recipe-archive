## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- yolk of five eggs
- sugar to taste
- vanilla to taste
- beaten whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the milk first
2. Beat the yolks of eggs and stir into the milk
3. When cold add the beaten whites and vanilla
4. Put in freezer and turn
