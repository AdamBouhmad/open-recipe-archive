## Ingredients

- roe
- salted water
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- beaten egg
- cracker crumbs
- hot oil or butter substitute

## Directions

1. Parboil the roe in salted water ten minutes.
2. Drain; season with salt, pepper and melted butter; form into balls, roll in beaten egg and cracker crumbs and fry in hot oil or any butter substitute.
