## Ingredients

- small white onions
- salt
- vinegar
- white peppercorns
- cloves
- horseradish
- mustard seed
- dill
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour hot salt water over the onions, which should be small and perfectly white
2. Peel them with a silver spoon (a knife would injure their color)
3. Let them lay in a salt brine for two days
4. Drain the onions and boil enough vinegar to cover them
5. Throw the onions in the boiling vinegar and let them boil only a few minutes
6. Take from the fire and lay them in glass jars, with alternate layers of whole white peppercorns and a few cloves (removing the soft heads, which would turn the onions black), a stick of horseradish sliced, and mustard seed and dill (used sparingly)
7. When the jars are filled heat the vinegar and add a cup of sugar to a gallon of vinegar
8. Cover the jars to overflowing with the vinegar, and seal while hot
