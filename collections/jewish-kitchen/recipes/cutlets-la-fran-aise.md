## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- egg
- salt
- white pepper
- mushroom powder
- grated lemon peel
- pieces of bread, smoked meat or tongue
- puree of mushrooms or spinach

## Directions

1. Cut them thinner than the English, and trim them into rounds of the size of a tea-cup
2. Brush them over with egg, and sprinkle with salt, white pepper, mushroom powder, and grated lemon peel
3. Put them into a saute pan and fry of a very light brown
4. Cut pieces of bread, smoked meat or tongue of the same size as the cutlets, and prepare in the same manner
5. Lay them alternately in the dish with the cutlets
6. Serve without sauce and with a puree of mushrooms or spinach in the centre of the dish
