## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. butter
- 3/4 lb. castor sugar
- 9 fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Take half-a-pound of the best flour, put it before the fire to dry for half an hour.
2. Sift it through a sieve, put it aside.
3. Melt 1/2 lb. of butter to oil.
4. Put 3/4 lb. of castor sugar in a skillet or pan that is round at the bottom.
5. Break over the sugar 9 fresh eggs.
6. Stir the eggs and sugar till it froths.
7. Put in the butter by degrees while another person stirs the eggs and sugar.
8. When the butter is well worked in, then put the flour in the same way by spoonfuls.
9. When it is well worked in, grease your tin and sift a little fine sugar in the tin.
10. Put it in a slow oven.
