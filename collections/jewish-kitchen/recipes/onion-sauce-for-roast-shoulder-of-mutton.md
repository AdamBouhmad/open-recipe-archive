## Ingredients

- a large Spanish onion or two smaller ones
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- a teaspoonful of flour
- a tablespoonful of the fat from clear broth
- a 1/2 pint of the liquor the onions were boiled in

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and lay them in cold water for 1/2 of an hour
2. Put them on to boil with a little salt
3. When quite tender rub them through a sieve into a basin
4. Take a small basin and put in a teaspoonful of flour and a tablespoonful of the fat from clear broth
5. Rub it with the flour and season with a little salt and pepper
6. Take about a 1/2 pint of the liquor the onions were boiled in and put it in a nice clean saucepan with the onions you rubbed through the sieve
7. When it boils stir in the flour and fat you mixed together
8. Let it boil up and serve it
