## Ingredients

- fish
- four good-sized tomatoes
- chopped parsley
- scallions or leeks
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- four eggs
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Steam the fish and bone
2. Take four good-sized tomatoes and cut them up
3. Add chopped parsley, scallions or leeks cut in small pieces, a little celery, salt and pepper to taste and four eggs well-beaten
4. Mix all these ingredients very well with the boned fish
5. Form in omelet shape
6. Place in oven in pan greased with olive oil and bake until well browned
