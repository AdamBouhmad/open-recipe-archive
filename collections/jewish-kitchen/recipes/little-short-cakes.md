## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- four ounces of butter
- four ounces of white powdered sugar
- two eggs
- orange flower-water
- sweet wine

## Directions

1. Rub into a pound of flour four ounces of butter, four ounces of white powdered sugar, and two eggs
2. Make it into a paste and roll it thin
3. Cut into small cakes with tin cutters
4. A little orange flower-water or sweet wine improve the flavour of these cakes
