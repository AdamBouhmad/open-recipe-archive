## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pint of water flavoured with a little salt
- four beaten eggs
- half a pound of matso flour
- a couple of lumps of white sugar
- half a tea-cup of milk

## Directions

1. Warm a quarter of a pint of water flavoured with a little salt
2. In which mix four beaten eggs
3. Then mix half a pound of matso flour, and a couple of lumps of white sugar, and half a tea-cup of milk
4. Mix all well together
5. Bake in a tin
