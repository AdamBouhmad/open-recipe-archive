## Ingredients

- 1 pint of large white beans
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 onion
- 1 pint of water
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- browning
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- 1 teaspoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Lay in soak 1 pint of the large white beans the night before you require them.
2. Put in a stewpan 1/2 lb. of butter, and an onion chopped very fine, let it fry.
3. Add 1 pint of water, put in the beans.
4. Let them simmer gently till done.
5. Rub in a basin a teaspoonful of flour with a little browning and a little chopped parsley.
6. Stir it in, mix it well, season it with pepper and salt, and a teaspoonful of vinegar.
7. Serve as a vegetable for butter dinners.
