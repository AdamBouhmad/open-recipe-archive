## Ingredients

- breast of veal
- tendons
- slices of smoked beef
- shavings of veal
- herbs
- sliced lemon
- two or three onions
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut from the bones of a breast of veal the tendons which are round the front
2. Trim and blanch them
3. Put them with slices of smoked beef into a stewpan with some shavings of veal, a few herbs, a little sliced lemon, two or three onions, and a little broth
4. They must simmer for seven or eight hours
5. When done, thicken the gravy
