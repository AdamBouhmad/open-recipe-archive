## Ingredients

- fine strong well-seasoned beef stock
- two sets of giblets
- water
- white pepper
- salt
- rind of lemon
- white wine
- mushroom ketchup
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Add to a fine strong well-seasoned beef stock, of about three quarts, two sets of giblets
2. The giblets should be previously stewed separately in one quart of water
3. The gizzards require scalding for some time before they are put in with the rest
4. Season them with white pepper, salt, and the rind of lemon
5. When they are tender, add them with their gravy to the stock
6. Boil for about ten minutes together
7. Stir in a glass of white wine, a table spoonful of mushroom ketchup, and the juice of half a lemon
8. It will require to be thickened with a little flour browned
9. The giblets are served in the soup
