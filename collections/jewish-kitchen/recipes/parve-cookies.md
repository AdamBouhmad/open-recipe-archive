## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- four eggs
- one-quarter pound of poppy seeds
- three tablespoons of oil
- two pounds of sugar
- a little salt

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour take one teaspoon of baking-powder, four eggs, one-quarter pound of poppy seeds, three tablespoons of oil, two pounds of sugar and a little salt
2. Knead not too stiff
3. Put on tins and bake in hot oven till a nice brown (Do not let burn)
