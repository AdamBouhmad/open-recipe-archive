## Ingredients

- duck
- forced meat
- sheep's tongue
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- chopped onions
- garlic
- marjoram
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- pickled walnut
- cayenne
- small meat balls
- 2 or 3 slices of fat smoked beef
- flour
- juice of a lemon
- browning
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Take a duck and bone it
2. Make a pound of forced meat well seasoned
3. Have a sheep's tongue that has been salted, boil it, skin and trim it
4. Put it in the centre of the forced meat with 2 hard boiled eggs, peeled whole
5. Make a nice ball of that, put it inside of the duck and give it its original shape
6. Put it in a stewpan and stew it with chopped onions, garlic, marjoram, thyme, salt, pepper, a pickled walnut, a little cayenne
7. When done take out the duck, strain the liquor, put it over the duck
8. Cook with a few small meat balls and 2 or 3 slices of fat smoked beef
9. Rub in a basin a spoonful of flour, the juice of a lemon, a little browning if not dark enough
10. Stir it in, mix it well together
11. Dish up duck in the centre, with slices of beef under, balls round with a few quarters of hard boiled eggs
