## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of fat
- two tablespoons of flour
- one-half can of tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- finely-chopped parsley
- cayenne pepper
- brains
- bread crumbs
- chicken-fat
- green peas

## Directions

1. Put one tablespoon of fat in skillet
2. when hot add two tablespoons of flour, rub until smooth, and brown lightly
3. add one-half can of tomatoes
4. season with salt, pepper, finely-chopped parsley, and a dash of cayenne pepper
5. add the brains, which have previously been cleaned, scalded with boiling water, and cut in small pieces
6. Cook a few minutes
7. fill the shells with the mixture
8. Over each shell sprinkle bread crumbs, and a little chicken-fat
9. Put shells in pan and brown nicely
10. Serve with green peas
