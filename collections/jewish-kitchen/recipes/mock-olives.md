## Ingredients

- plums
- sea salt or rock salt

## Directions

1. Take plums when just beginning to ripen, but still green
2. Make a brine out of sea salt or rock salt strong enough to hold up an egg
3. Pour the brine over the fruit, hot, cover and let stand twenty-four hours
4. Pour off and make a new brine, heat, add the fruit, heat one minute and seal in the hot brine
