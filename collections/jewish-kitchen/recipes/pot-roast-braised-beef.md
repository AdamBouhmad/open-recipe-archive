## Ingredients

- fat or goose fat
- onion
- salt
- paprika
- hot water
- one-half a sweet green pepper
- one small carrot
- two tablespoons of tomatoes
- two onions
- two and a half pounds of brisket shoulder

## Directions

1. Heat some fat or goose fat in a deep iron pot.
2. Cut half an onion very fine and when it is slightly browned put in the meat.
3. Cover up closely and let the meat brown on all sides.
4. Salt to taste, add a scant half teaspoon of paprika and half a cup of hot water and simmer an hour longer, keeping covered closely all the time.
5. Add one-half a sweet green pepper (seeds removed), one small carrot cut in slices, two tablespoons of tomatoes and two onions sliced.
6. Two and a half pounds of brisket shoulder or any other meat suitable for pot roasting will require three hours slow cooking.
7. When the meat is tender, remove to a warm platter, strain the gravy, rubbing the thick part through the sieve and after removing any fat serve in a sauce boat.
