## Ingredients

- small, young string beans
- salt water

## Directions

1. Select small, young string beans, string them carefully
2. Boil in salt water, in a brass kettle, until tender
3. Throw them on a large, clean board to drip
4. Next morning press them into a jar, with alternate layers of salt and beans
