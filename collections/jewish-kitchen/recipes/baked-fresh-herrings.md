## Ingredients

- fresh herrings
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- grated nutmeg
- vinegar
- saffron
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the herrings well
2. take off the heads
3. cut in a baking dish some onion, salt, pepper, a little marjoram, grated nutmeg, a teacup of vinegar
4. place in the herrings a little saffron
5. let them bake till done
6. dish them up
7. put a little chopped parsley over
