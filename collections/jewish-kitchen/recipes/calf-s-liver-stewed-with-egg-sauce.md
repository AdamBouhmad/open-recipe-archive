## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- a good-sized onion
- white pepper
- salt
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1/2 pint of water
- 2 yolks of eggs
- a teaspoonful of flour
- the juice of 2 lemons
- a little chopped parsley
- a few pieces of marjoram
- a few small meat balls

## Directions

1. Prepare the liver as before
2. Put it on the gridiron for a short time
3. Cut it in pieces the size of a walnut
4. Slice a good-sized onion in a stewpan with white pepper, salt, and a little grated nutmeg
5. Add 1/2 pint of water and let it cook till done
6. Stir in a basin 2 yolks of eggs, a teaspoonful of flour, the juice of 2 lemons, a little chopped parsley, and a few pieces of marjoram
7. Turn the sauce over the liver and serve up very hot
8. You may put a few small meat balls in the dish
