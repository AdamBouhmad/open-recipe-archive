## Ingredients

- large white onions
- uncooked rice
- salt
- citric acid
- water
- green peppers
- tomatoes
- red cabbage
- onions
- fat

## Directions

1. Brown large white onions
2. add one-half cup of uncooked rice
3. add a little salt and piece of citric acid (size of a bean dissolved in a little water)
4. fill peppers
5. stew with tomatoes
6. Or fill peppers with red cabbage which has been steamed with onions and fat and add moistened rice
