## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- half an ounce of fine isinglass
- a handful of beaten almonds
- two or three bitter almonds
- a couple of bay leaves
- a piece of lemon peel
- four ounces of white sugar

## Directions

1. Add half an ounce of fine isinglass to a quart of milk
2. Add a handful of beaten almonds, two or three bitter almonds, a couple of bay leaves, and a piece of lemon peel
3. When the isinglass is dissolved, strain the milk into a basin
4. Sweeten with four ounces of white sugar
5. Pour into a mould
