## Ingredients

- four quarts of water
- salt
- four or five pounds brisket of beef
- whole and ground peppers
- one large clove of garlic
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one bay leaf
- one teaspoon of salt-petre

## Directions

1. Take four quarts of water, adding enough salt to float an egg, boil this salted water
2. When cool take four or five pounds brisket of beef, seasoned with whole and ground peppers, one large clove of garlic, pierced in different parts of the beef, one tablespoon of sugar, one bay leaf and one teaspoon of salt-petre
3. Put meat into deep stone pot, pour the boiled water over it and store in a cool place for ten days or two weeks
