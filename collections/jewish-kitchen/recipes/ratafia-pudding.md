## Ingredients

- the crumb of a French roll
- half a pound of ratafia cakes
- milk or cream
- three ounces of warmed fresh butter
- yolks of five and the whites of two eggs
- sugar
- one ounce of pounded almonds
- a few bitter almonds
- dried cherries
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Soak the crumb of a French roll and half a pound of ratafia cakes in milk or cream, then mix with them three ounces of warmed fresh butter, the yolks of five and the whites of two eggs, sweeten to taste
2. Add one ounce of pounded almonds, and a few bitter almonds
3. Boil in a shape lined with dried cherries, or bake in a cake-tin first well buttered, and sprinkled with bread crumbs
