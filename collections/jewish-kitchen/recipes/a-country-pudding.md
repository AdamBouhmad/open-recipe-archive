## Ingredients

- butter
- dried cherries
- citron
- bread
- dried currants
- pint and half of milk
- fresh lemon peel
- 4 ounces of castor sugar
- salt
- 6 eggs
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Butter your mould well
2. Arrange some dried cherries in strips, with some citron cut thin
3. Cut some slices of bread and butter and spread over some dried currants
4. Arrange the slices one over the other in the mould till you have filled it
5. Boil a pint and half of milk with some fresh lemon peel in it, 4 ounces of castor sugar, and a pinch of salt
6. Beat 6 eggs with this
7. When cold then fill up the mould
8. Let it soak in
9. Put the mould in a stewpan to boil for an hour
10. Serve it with wine sauce
