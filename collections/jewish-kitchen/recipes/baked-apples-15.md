## Ingredients

- large, juicy apples
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- cloves
- water
- sugar
- beaten white of an egg
- pulverized sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Wash and core apples well
2. Fill each cored place with brown sugar, cinnamon and raisins
3. Put a clove in each apple
4. Lay them in a deep dish
5. Pour a teacup of water in the dish
6. Put a little sugar on top of each apple
7. When well done the apples will be broken
8. Remove them carefully to the dish they are to be served in
9. Pour the syrup over them
10. To be eaten cold
11. If you wish them extra nice, glaze them with the beaten white of an egg, half a cup of pulverized sugar and serve with whipped cream
