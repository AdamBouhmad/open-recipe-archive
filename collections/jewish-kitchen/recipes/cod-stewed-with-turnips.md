## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 white, hard turnips
- onion
- 2 spoonfuls of oil
- 1/2 pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- cod
- 1 spoonful of flour
- 2 spoonfuls of vinegar or the juice of one lemon
- a little dried saffron
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel and cut turnips in nice size pieces, say one turnip in 4 pieces
2. Slice an onion in the stewpan with 2 spoonfuls of oil, let it fry
3. Put in a 1/2 pint of water, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
4. Put the turnips in, let them stew tender
5. Then lay the slices of cod, let them stew till done
6. Take out the cod, put it in the dish intended to send it to table
7. Mix in a basin a spoonful of flour, 2 of vinegar or the juice of one lemon, with a little dried saffron, some chopped parsley
8. Throw it in with the turnips, mix it well
9. Dish the turnips round the cod, and put all the sauce over fish and turnips with a spoon
10. Garnish with sprigs of parsley
