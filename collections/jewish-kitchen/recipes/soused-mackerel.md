## Ingredients

- mackerel
- vinegar
- cloves
- allspice
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne
- bay leaves
- fennel

## Directions

1. Wash them well
2. split them open
3. cut them in four
4. place them in a stewpan with some vinegar, 2 or 3 cloves, the same of allspice, pepper, salt, and a little cayenne, and 2 or 3 bay leaves
5. let them boil quickly for half an hour
6. put them in a deep dish
7. serve as required with a little fennel
8. turn them over each day
