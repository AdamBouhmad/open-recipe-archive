## Ingredients

- one cup broken macaroni
- two quarts boiling salted water
- cream sauce
- one cup grated cheese
- one cup buttered bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook one cup of broken macaroni in two quarts of boiling salted water for twenty or thirty minutes
2. Drain and pour cold water through the colander
3. Put the macaroni in a pudding-dish in layers, covering each layer with cream sauce and grated cheese
4. On the top layers sprinkle one cup of buttered bread crumbs
5. Bake in oven until the crumbs are brown
