## Ingredients

- eggplant
- red onion
- garlic
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Broil eggplant
2. When cool, skin and lay on platter
3. Cut with wooden spoon
4. Add a red onion cut fine, or garlic cut very fine, salt and a little vinegar
