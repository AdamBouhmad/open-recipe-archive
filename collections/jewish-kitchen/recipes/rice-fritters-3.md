## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- a small quantity of water
- six eggs
- three spoonfuls of flour
- a little grated lemon peel
- butter or oil
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of rice in a small quantity of water to a jelly
2. Let it cool and beat it up with six eggs, three spoonfuls of flour, a little grated lemon peel
3. Fry like fritters, either in butter or oil
4. Serve with white sugar sifted over them
