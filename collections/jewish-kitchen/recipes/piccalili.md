## Ingredients

- gherkins
- French beans
- cauliflower
- carrots
- onions
- capsicums
- white cabbage
- celery
- brine
- vinegar
- ginger
- whole black pepper
- whole allspice
- bruised chillies
- shalots
- garlic
- flour of mustard
- turmeric

## Directions

1. Pickle gherkins, French beans, and cauliflower, separately, as already directed
2. Cut the other vegetables (carrots, onions, capsicums, white cabbage, celery) in small pieces, and soak in brine; boil the carrots in it to make them tender
3. Into half a gallon of vinegar put two ounces of ginger, one of whole black pepper, one of whole allspice, and one of bruised chillies, three ounces of shalots, and one ounce of garlic
4. Boil together nearly twenty minutes
5. Mix a little of it in a basin, with two ounces of flour of mustard and one ounce of turmeric
6. Stir it in gradually with the rest
7. Pour the liquor over the vegetables
