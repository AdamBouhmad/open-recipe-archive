## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two tablespoons of flour
- one cup of cold milk
- butter
- powdered sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wet the flour with a little of the milk, then add the rest of the milk and the yolks of the eggs.
2. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and pour into the flour, milk and yolks.
3. Put a piece of butter into a spider and let it get hot, but not so hot that the butter will burn.
4. Then pour the mixture in and put in a moderate oven to bake in the spider.
5. It takes about ten minutes to bake.
6. Then slip a knife under it and loosen it and slip off on a large plate.
7. Sift powdered sugar on top and serve with a slice of lemon.
