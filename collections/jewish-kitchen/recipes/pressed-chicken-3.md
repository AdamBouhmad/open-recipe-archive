## Ingredients

- one or more chickens
- water
- salt
- parsley
- lemon
- pickles
- olives

## Directions

1. Boil one or more chickens just as you would for fricassee, using as little water as possible
2. When tender remove all the meat from the bone and take off all the skin
3. Chop as fine as possible in a chopping bowl
4. Add all the liquor the chicken was boiled in, which ought to be very little and well seasoned
5. Press it into the shape of a brick between two platters, and put a heavy weight over it so as to press hard
6. Set away to cool in ice-chest and garnish nicely with parsley and slices of lemon before sending to the table
7. It should be placed whole upon the table, and sliced as served
8. Serve pickles and olives with it
