## Ingredients

- potatoes
- flour
- butter or oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel them, cut them in quarters rather thin.
2. Dry them in a cloth, sprinkle flour over them.
3. Have the butter or oil frying ready for them, put them in.
4. Keep turning to have them all one colour.
5. Drain them, sprinkle them with salt and pepper mixed together.
