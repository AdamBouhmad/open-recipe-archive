## Ingredients

- steak
- carrot
- 2 turnips
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- teaspoonful of flour
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Put in a stewpan the steak you intend to stew
2. Cut up a carrot in strips of 2 inches long
3. Cut 2 turnips in pieces of an equal size
4. Add an onion with salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
5. Let it all stew till tender
6. Thicken with a teaspoonful of flour and a little dried saffron
