## Ingredients

- one cup of freshly cooked rice
- one cup of peanut butter
- four tablespoons of minced celery
- one teaspoon of grated onion
- one tablespoon of canned tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- white of one egg
- one tablespoon of water
- stale bread crumb dust

## Directions

1. To one cup of freshly cooked rice allow one cup of peanut butter, four tablespoons of minced celery, one teaspoon of grated onion, one tablespoon of canned tomatoes, and salt and pepper to taste
2. Mix well
3. Add the white of one egg, reserving the yolk for coating the croquettes
4. Shape into croquettes
5. Let stand in a cold place for an hour
6. Coat with the egg yolk mixed with one tablespoon of water
7. Roll in stale bread crumb dust until well covered
8. Fry in any hot oil or butter substitute
