## Ingredients

- saddle of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- paper, greased
- flour
- gravy
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Porg the saddle
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Lay over the back a sheet of paper well greased
4. Keep papered till within a half hour of sending it up
5. Remove the paper and dredge a little flour over it
6. Let it brown nicely
7. Roast very gently for about 2.5 hours
8. Send up very hot with a good gravy
9. Serve with currant jelly
