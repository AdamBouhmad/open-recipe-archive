## Ingredients

- strawberries
- white sugar powdered
- red currant juice

## Directions

1. Weigh an equal quantity of fruit and white sugar powdered.
2. Sift all the sugar over the fruit, so that half of it shall equally be covered.
3. Let it lay till the next day.
4. Boil the remainder with red currant juice.
5. Simmer the strawberries in the red currant juice until the jelly hangs about them.
6. Put the strawberries into pots, taking care not to break them.
7. Pour over the syrup.
