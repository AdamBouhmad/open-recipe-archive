## Ingredients

- one quart of green peas
- one quart of milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook one quart of green peas until very tender
2. Then mash through colander
3. To this amount heat one quart of milk in double boiler
4. Add butter, salt and pepper to taste, and last the mashed green peas
