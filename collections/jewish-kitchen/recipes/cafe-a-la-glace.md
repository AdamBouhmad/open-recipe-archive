## Ingredients

- five tablespoons of fresh-roasted and ground coffee
- four cups of boiling water
- one-half pound of sugar
- sweetened whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take five tablespoons of fresh-roasted and ground coffee
2. Pour four cups of boiling water over it
3. Cover quickly and put on the back of the stove
4. Add one-half pound of sugar
5. When cold, press through a sieve
6. Fill in the can to be frozen
7. Let it remain in freezer five minutes longer before you begin to turn the freezer
8. Serve in glasses, and put sweetened whipped cream on the top
