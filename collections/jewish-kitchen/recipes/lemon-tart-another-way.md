## Ingredients

- six lemons sliced
- sugar
- two savoy biscuits
- three ounces of orange and lemon peel
- three ounces of ground almonds
- one ounce of whole almonds blanched

## Directions

1. Slice six lemons and lay them in sugar all night
2. Mix with them two savoy biscuits, three ounces of orange and lemon peel, three ounces of ground almonds, one ounce of whole almonds blanched
3. Bake in a dish lined with pastry
