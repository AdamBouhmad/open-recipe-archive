## Ingredients

- peppers
- one onion
- fat
- one-fourth cup of cold cooked chicken or veal
- one-fourth cup of mushrooms
- one-half cup of water
- two tablespoons of bread crumbs
- salt and red pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a slice from the blossom end of each pepper, remove seeds and parboil ten minutes
2. Chop one onion fine and cook in fat until straw color
3. Add one-fourth cup of cold cooked chicken or veal, and one-fourth cup of mushrooms
4. Cook two minutes, add one-half cup of water and two tablespoons of bread crumbs
5. Cool, sprinkle peppers with salt and a pinch of red pepper
6. Fill with stuffing, cover with crumbs and bake ten minutes
