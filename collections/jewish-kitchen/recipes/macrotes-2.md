## Ingredients

- one pound of good light dough
- two eggs
- six ounces of butter
- flour
- oil
- thick syrup
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Blend one pound of good light dough with two eggs and six ounces of butter
2. Add as much flour as may be needed to make the whole sufficiently dry
3. Make it into the shape of a French roll
4. Cut off rather thin slices
5. Place before the fire to rise
6. Fry in oil
7. Let them drain carefully
8. When nearly cold dip each in very thick syrup flavored with essence of lemon
