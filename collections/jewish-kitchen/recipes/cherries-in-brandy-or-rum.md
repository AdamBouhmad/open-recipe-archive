## Ingredients

- fine Morelia cherries
- 1 oz. of sifted sugar per bottle
- brandy or rum

## Directions

1. Pick some fine Morelia cherries, the larger the better
2. Cut off half the stalk
3. Put them in dry bottles
4. Put 1 oz. of sifted sugar in each bottle
5. Fill up with cherries
6. Fill up with brandy or rum
7. Cork the bottles
8. Put skin over them tied tightly
