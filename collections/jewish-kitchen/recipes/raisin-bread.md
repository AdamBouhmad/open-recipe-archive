## Ingredients

- dough as directed for Butter Barches
- one-quarter cup of raisins
- egg diluted with water

## Directions

1. Make dough as directed for Butter Barches, using one-quarter cup of raisins and omitting the lemon and egg
2. Form in loaves, fill well-greased pans half full
3. Cover and let rise until light; about one hour
4. Glaze with egg diluted with water
5. Bake forty-five minutes
