## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a calf's foot
- two quarts of water
- a blade of mace
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- a turnip
- white pepper
- salt
- balls of forced meat
- egg balls
- yolks of four eggs
- a desert spoonful of lemon juice
- three ounces of sweet almonds
- a spoonful of powdered white sugar

## Directions

1. Put a knuckle of veal and a calf's foot into two quarts of water, with a blade of mace and a bunch of sweet herbs, a turnip, a little white pepper, and salt
2. When sufficiently done, strain and skim it
3. Add balls of forced meat, and egg balls
4. A quarter of an hour before serving beat up the yolks of four eggs with a desert spoonful of lemon juice, and three ounces of sweet almonds blanched and beaten with a spoonful of powdered white sugar
5. This mixture is to be stirred into the soup till it thickens, taking care to prevent its curdling
