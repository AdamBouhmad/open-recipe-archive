## Ingredients

- peas
- water
- salt
- butter
- one teaspoon of sugar (if peas are not the sweet variety)

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and cover them with water
2. Bring to a boil
3. Push aside until the water will just bubble gently
4. Keep the lid partly off
5. When the peas are tender add salt and butter
6. Cook ten minutes longer and serve
7. If the peas are not the sweet variety, add one teaspoon of sugar
