## Ingredients

- water
- tablespoon of shortening
- pinch of salt
- one egg
- spoon of sugar
- piece of compressed yeast
- flour
- white of egg
- teaspoon of peach compote

## Directions

1. Add compressed yeast previously dissolved in water to water, shortening, salt, egg, and sugar
2. Let the dough raise for three hours
3. In the meantime make a compote of peaches by stewing them with sugar and spices such as cinnamon and cloves, stewing enough to answer for both sauce and filling
4. When raised, flour the baking-board and roll out the dough half an inch thick
5. Cut cakes out of it with a tumbler
6. Brush the edges with white of egg
7. Put a teaspoon of peach compote in the centre of a cake and cover it with another layer of cake
8. Press the edges firmly together
9. Steam over boiling water and serve with peach sauce
