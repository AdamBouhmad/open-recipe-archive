## Ingredients

- two French rolls
- 1/2 pint of cold milk
- 2 or 3 eggs
- best oil
- sugar
- cinnamon
- good thick clarified sugar flavoured with orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Cut two French rolls in rounds (each roll will cut in four rounds)
2. lay them in a dish with 1/2 pint of cold milk
3. let them soak for 1/2 an hour
4. take them out, lay the rounds between a clean cloth
5. beat up in a plate 2 or 3 eggs
6. put the frying-pan on with the best oil
7. before putting in the fritters fry a piece of crumb of bread to take off the flavour of the oil
8. dip carefully each fritter in the eggs on both sides
9. fry them a light brown
10. when they are done put them in a deep dish
11. sprinkle over sugar and cinnamon
12. have some good thick clarified sugar ready flavoured with orange-flower water in a sauceboat
13. pour it over the fritters
