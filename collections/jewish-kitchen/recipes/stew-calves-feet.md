## Ingredients

- 2 feet
- a pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- marjoram
- a little dried saffron
- an onion
- a tooth of garlic
- meat balls
- 2 eggs
- the juice of a lemon
- a little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut up 2 feet in pieces and put them in a stewpan with water, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, marjoram, dried saffron, onion, and garlic
2. Let it simmer for 2 or 3 hours till quite tender
3. Add some meat balls 1 hour before it is done
4. Just before dishing up, add the egg sauce made from 2 beaten eggs, strained lemon juice, and chopped parsley
