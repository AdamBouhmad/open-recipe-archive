## Ingredients

- shin of beef
- various sorts of vegetables of whatever may be in season
- spices
- pepper
- salt
- two quarts of soup
- one quarter of a pound of rice
- two yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Make a good gravy from shin of beef
2. Cut up very small various sorts of vegetables of whatever may be in season
3. Add spices, pepper, and salt
4. Stew it all well down together
5. Set it to cool and take off the fat
6. Place it again on the fire to boil
7. Add to two quarts of soup one quarter of a pound of rice
8. Beat two yolks of eggs with a little of the stock
9. When the rice is quite tender, stir them into the soup
10. Take the precaution not to let the soup boil and to stir always the same way
