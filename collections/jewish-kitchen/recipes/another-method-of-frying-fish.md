## Ingredients

- six ounces flour
- one ounce olive oil
- egg yolk
- salt
- one gill tepid water
- egg white
- bread crumbs
- boiling oil

## Directions

1. Thoroughly mix six ounces of flour with an ounce of olive oil, the yolk of an egg, and a pinch of salt
2. Stir in one gill of tepid water and allow the whole to stand for half an hour in a cool place
3. Beat the white of an egg stiff and stir into the batter
4. Dip each fish into the mixture, then roll in bread crumbs
5. Cook in boiling oil
6. Do not allow the fish to remain in the spider after it has been nicely browned
7. Be sure that the fish is done
