## Ingredients

- one large sour apple
- three-quarters cup of white sugar
- white of one egg
- vanilla or grated rind of one-half lemon

## Directions

1. Peel and grate one large sour apple
2. Mix three-quarters cup of white sugar with the apple
3. Add white of one egg
4. Beat all together a long time
5. Flavor with vanilla or grated rind of one-half lemon
