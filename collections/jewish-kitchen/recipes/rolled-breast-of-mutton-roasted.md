## Ingredients

- breast of mutton
- forced meat
- lemon
- fat
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Lay the mutton on the board, take out the bones, beat it flat
2. Make a forced meat as for the balls and lay it on
3. Squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
4. Roll it up nicely, sew the seam along tight, sew the ends also
5. Put it down to roast, or if you have a good oven, bake it
6. Put some fat over it
7. Boil some potatoes carefully so they do not break
8. Put them in the tin with the mutton to brown
