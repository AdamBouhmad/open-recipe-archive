## Ingredients

- six bananas
- juice of one lemon
- three or more tablespoons of sugar
- whipped cream or boiled icing

## Directions

1. Mash six bananas, add juice of one lemon and three or more tablespoons of sugar
2. Or add mashed bananas with whipped cream or boiled icing
