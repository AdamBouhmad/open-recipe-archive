## Ingredients

- two tablespoons fat
- one onion
- two cups boiled potatoes
- one-half cup stock
- one tablespoon curry powder
- one-half teaspoon salt
- red pepper
- one teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of fat in a frying-pan
2. Add one onion chopped fine and cook until straw color
3. Add two cups of boiled potatoes cut in dice
4. Add one-half cup of stock and one tablespoon of curry powder
5. Cook until the stock has been absorbed
6. Add one-half teaspoon of salt, a dash of red pepper, and one teaspoon of lemon juice
