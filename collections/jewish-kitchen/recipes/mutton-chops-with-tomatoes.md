## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- 3 or 4 large tomatoes
- water
- lemon
- parsley
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Take some nice mutton chops with the fat trimmed off and put them in a stewpan with salt, pepper, an onion chopped
2. Slice 3 or 4 large tomatoes
3. Let it all cook together with about a cupful of water
4. When the chops are tender take them out and let them drain well from the gravy
5. Turn the tomatoes and liquor into a sieve, pulp it well
6. Put the juice of a lemon with it
7. Turn it all over the chops
8. Let it boil before you dish it up
9. Sprinkle over a little chopped parsley, and if liked a little cayenne
