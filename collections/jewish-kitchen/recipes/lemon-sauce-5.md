## Ingredients

- soup stock
- a few slices of lemon
- a little sugar
- grated nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- a teaspoon of flour or yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Boil some soup stock with a few slices of lemon, a little sugar and grated nutmeg
2. Add chopped parsley
3. Thicken with a teaspoon of flour or yolk of egg
