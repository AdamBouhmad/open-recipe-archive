## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- apples finely chopped
- currants
- shred suet
- brown sugar
- three eggs
- a little brandy
- grated nutmeg
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of bread crumbs, apples finely chopped, currants and shred suet
2. Sweeten with brown sugar, and mix all together with three eggs, a little brandy, grated nutmeg, and lemon peel
3. Boil in a round mould from one to two hours, according to the size of the pudding
