## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- sufficient flour to make a paste
- a pinch of salt
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- 1 lb. of raisins
- grated peel of a lemon
- 2 oz. of orange and lemon peel
- 1/2 lb. of melted suet
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Break 8 eggs, mix it with sufficient flour to make a paste and a pinch of salt
2. Roll the paste out as for frimsells, cut it in strips of an inch wide
3. Boil them for a few minutes
4. Put them in a sieve to drain
5. Put the noodle strips in a basin with the currants, raisins, grated lemon peel, orange and lemon peel, and melted suet
6. Mix all well together with 6 eggs well beaten
7. Grease and sugar a mould
8. Boil for 2 hours
9. Serve with wine or brandy sauce
