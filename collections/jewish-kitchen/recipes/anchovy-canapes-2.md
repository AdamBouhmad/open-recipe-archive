## Ingredients

- bread
- anchovy paste
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the bread as for caviar canapes
2. Spread with anchovy paste
3. Chop separately the yolks and whites of hard-boiled eggs
4. Cover the canapes, dividing them into quarters, with anchovies split in two lengthwise
5. Use yolks and whites in alternate quarters
