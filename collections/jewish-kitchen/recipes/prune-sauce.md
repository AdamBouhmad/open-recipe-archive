## Ingredients

- about one pound of Turkish prunes
- ground cinnamon
- juice of an orange

## Directions

1. Wash the prunes in hot water
2. put on to boil in cold water
3. Boil until they are very soft
4. Remove the pits or kernels
5. strain over them the water they were boiled in
6. sweeten to taste
7. Flavor with ground cinnamon
8. mash them until a soft mush
9. If too thick, add the juice of an orange
