## Ingredients

- one-half cup of white flour
- one teaspoon of soda
- three tablespoons of molasses
- one tablespoon of butter
- two cups of bran
- one and one-half cups of sweet milk
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of chopped dates or raisins

## Directions

1. Sift one-half cup of white flour with one teaspoon of soda
2. Mix three tablespoons of molasses with one tablespoon of butter
3. Add two cups of bran, one and one-half cups of sweet milk
4. Add the flour and one-half teaspoon of salt
5. Stir all together
6. One-half cup of chopped dates or raisins may be added if so desired
7. Bake in muffin-pans in a moderate oven thirty minutes
