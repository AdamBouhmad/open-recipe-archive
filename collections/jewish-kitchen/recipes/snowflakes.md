## Ingredients

- large cocoanut
- pulverized sugar
- whipped or plain sweet cream

## Directions

1. Grate a large cocoanut into a fruit dish
2. Mix it thoroughly and lightly with pulverized sugar
3. Serve with whipped or plain sweet cream
