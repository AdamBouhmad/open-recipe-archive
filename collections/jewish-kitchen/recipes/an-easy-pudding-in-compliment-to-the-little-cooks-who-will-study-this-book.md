## Ingredients

- butter
- dried cherries or candied peel
- thin slices of bread and butter
- raspberry or strawberry jam
- 1 pint of milk flavoured with lemon or orange-flower water
- 3 eggs
- ingredients for a good wine sauce

## Directions

1. Grease a mould well with butter
2. Arrange inside some dried cherries or candied peel with taste
3. Cut some thin slices of bread and butter
4. Spread on them some raspberry or strawberry jam
5. Put them in the mould one over the other
6. Make a nice custard with 1 pint of milk flavoured with lemon or orange-flower water
7. Beat up 3 eggs
8. Mix it altogether
9. Put it in the mould
10. Boil it for one hour
11. Turn it out
12. Make a good wine sauce
13. Turn it over
