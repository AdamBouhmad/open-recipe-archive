## Ingredients

- two cups of mashed potatoes
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- one egg
- salt
- nutmeg
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- hot oil or butter substitute

## Directions

1. Work into two cups of mashed potatoes, a tablespoon of melted butter, until smooth and soft
2. Add one egg well-beaten
3. Beat all together with a wooden spoon
4. Season with salt and nutmeg
5. Roll each in beaten egg then in bread crumbs
6. Fry in hot oil or butter substitute
