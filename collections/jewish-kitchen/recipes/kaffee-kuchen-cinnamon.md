## Ingredients

- butter
- dough
- melted butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- chopped almonds
- flour

## Directions

1. Butter long and broad cake-pans thoroughly, roll out enough dough to cover them, and let it rise about half an hour before baking, then brush it well with melted butter
2. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top and some chopped almonds
3. Take a small lump of butter, a very little flour, some sugar and cinnamon and rub it between the hands until it is like lumps of almonds, then strew on top of cakes
