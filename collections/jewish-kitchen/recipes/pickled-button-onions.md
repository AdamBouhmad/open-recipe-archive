## Ingredients

- bunches of button onions
- salt
- spice
- fresh lemon rind
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Get a few bunches of button onions as they come in
2. Skin them
3. Lay them in a colander
4. Well rub them with salt
5. Let them lay in salt for one day
6. Put them in glass bottles
7. Put very little spice to them
8. Add a large spoonful of salt to each bottle
9. Add a little of the rind of a fresh lemon
10. Fill up with cold vinegar
11. Cork them down tight
12. They will be fit for use in 6 weeks
