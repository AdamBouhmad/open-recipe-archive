## Ingredients

- stale bread
- sour milk
- one-half teaspoon salt
- two eggs
- two teaspoons baking soda
- finely sifted flour

## Directions

1. Soak stale bread overnight in sour milk
2. Mash the bread fine in the morning
3. Put in one-half teaspoon of salt, two eggs, two teaspoons of baking soda dissolved in hot water
4. Thicken with finely sifted flour
