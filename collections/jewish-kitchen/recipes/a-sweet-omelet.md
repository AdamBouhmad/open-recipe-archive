## Ingredients

- three or four eggs
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Beat up three or four eggs
2. Pour them into an omelet pan
3. Sprinkle a little white sugar over them while frying
4. Hold a salamander or hot shovel over the uppermost side of the omelet, as it must only be fried on one side
5. As soon as it is set, slide it onto a hot dish
6. Double it
7. Sprinkle sugar over it and serve quickly
