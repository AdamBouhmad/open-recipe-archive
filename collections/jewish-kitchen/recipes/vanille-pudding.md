## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 3 oz. flour
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 8 eggs
- a piece of vanille
- 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- butter and sugar for greasing

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, stir in the flour very dry, work it up well
2. Stir in the milk, stir it till it is quite thick
3. Let the butter get quite cold again
4. Beat in the yolks of 8 eggs one after the other
5. Pound a piece of vanille with the sugar, mix that in also
6. Beat the 8 whites to a snow, stir them in
7. Grease a shape well with butter and sugar, put the mixture in
8. Stand it in a flat stewpan half full of water
9. Let it boil slowly for 2 1/2 hours
10. Turn these puddings out very carefully, and always lay the sauce round in the dish, not over the pudding
