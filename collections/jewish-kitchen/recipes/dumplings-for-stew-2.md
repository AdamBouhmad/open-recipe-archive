## Ingredients

- two teaspoons baking powder
- two cups flour
- one egg
- one cup cold water
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix two teaspoons of baking powder with two cups of flour, one egg, one cup of cold water and a little salt
2. Stir all lightly together and drop the batter from the spoon into the stew while the water continues to boil
3. Cover closely and do not uncover for twenty minutes, boiling constantly, but not too hard
4. Serve immediately in the stew
