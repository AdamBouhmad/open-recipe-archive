## Ingredients

- one-half pound calf's liver
- salt water
- one onion
- one tablespoon parsley
- some mint
- two cloves
- a little cinnamon
- a little tabasco sauce
- one tablespoon olive oil
- one cup of soup stock
- one cup of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil one-half pound calf's liver in salt water
2. Drain and cut into small cubes
3. Chop one onion, one tablespoon parsley, some mint
4. Add two cloves, a little cinnamon, a little tabasco sauce, one tablespoon olive oil, and one cup of soup stock
5. Add one cup of bread crumbs which have been soaked in hot water and then drained
6. Mix all with the liver and bring to a boil
7. Serve with Spanish rice
