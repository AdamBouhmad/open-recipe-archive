## Ingredients

- stale bread or crumb of a French roll
- milk
- two or three eggs
- wine or essence of lemon or beaten almonds
- peel of one whole lemon
- juice of lemon

## Directions

1. Grate stale bread, or soak the crumb of a French roll in milk, which must be warmed
2. beat with it two or three eggs
3. flavor and sweeten to taste, sometimes with a little wine or essence of lemon, or beaten almonds
4. boil about half an hour
5. This pudding is excellent made as above, with the addition of the peel of one whole lemon grated, with its juice, and baked
