## Ingredients

- 1 quart of stock
- flesh of the foot
- small meat balls
- egg balls
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- juice of half a lemon
- a spoonful of browning
- a glass of sherry or madeira
- a little cayenne

## Directions

1. Put one quart of the stock into a moderate sized saucepan and let it warm gently.
2. Cut the flesh of the foot into nice pieces the size of a small biscuit and put that into the saucepan.
3. Have ready some small meat balls made according to the recipe, also a few egg balls.
4. Boil the meat balls first and throw them into the saucepan with the liquor they were boiled in.
5. Let the whole boil together.
6. Throw in the egg balls, which only take ten minutes to boil.
7. Have in a basin 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, the juice of half a lemon, and a spoonful of browning.
8. Rub it all together and turn it back into the saucepan.
9. Stir it well.
10. Turn it into the tureen with a glass of sherry or madeira.
11. Never let the wine boil in the soup.
12. Add a little cayenne according to taste.
