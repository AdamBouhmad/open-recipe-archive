## Ingredients

- pint of syrup
- juice of 6 good sized oranges
- 2 or 3 lumps of sugar
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Add to the pint of syrup the juice of 6 good sized oranges well strained
2. Rub off the essence from the rinds with 2 or 3 lumps of sugar
3. Dissolve it in the juice
4. Add the juice of 1 lemon to throw up the flavour of the oranges
