## Ingredients

- three cups of rye flour
- three cups of wheat flour
- two teaspoons of salt
- one-half cake of compressed yeast
- two cups of lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Sift three cups of rye flour, three cups of wheat flour and two teaspoons of salt in a bowl
2. Dissolve one-half cake of compressed yeast in two cups of lukewarm water
3. When the yeast is dissolved pour it into the flour and make into a dough
4. Lay it on a kneading board, and knead until smooth and elastic
5. Put it back into the bowl, cover with a towel, and set aside overnight to rise
6. Next morning, lay the dough on a biscuit or kneading board again and knead well
7. Make into a loaf, put into a pan, and when well risen, moisten the top with a little cold water and bake in a moderate oven
