## Ingredients

- one pint of flour
- five eggs
- salt
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- one tablespoon of pulverized sugar
- butter or fat
- sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Sift about one pint of flour in a bowl
2. Make a depression in the centre
3. Break in five eggs, a pinch of salt, one teaspoon of ground cinnamon and one tablespoon of pulverized sugar
4. Mix this as you would a noodle dough, though not quite as stiff
5. Roll out very thin
6. Cut into long strips with a jagging iron
7. Fry a light yellow
8. Roll on a round stick as soon as taken up from the fat or butter
9. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon or grated peel of a lemon
10. Mix both thoroughly
