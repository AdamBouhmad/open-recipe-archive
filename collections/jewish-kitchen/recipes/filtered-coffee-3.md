## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely ground coffee
- 6 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Place one cup of finely ground coffee in the strainer of the percolator
2. Place the strainer in the pot and place over the heat
3. Add gradually six cups of boiling water and allow it to filter
4. Serve at once
