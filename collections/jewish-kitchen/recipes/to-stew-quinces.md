## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar
- water
- rind of a lemon
- a few cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces, quarter and core them
2. Throw them into cold water as you do them and drain them
3. Lay them in a stewpan
4. Allow for every quince 2 ozs. of sugar, and to every 12 quinces add 1 pint of water, the rind of a lemon cut thin, and a few cloves
5. If the syrup is not thick enough add some more sugar
6. When nearly done add 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
7. Finish them off by quick boiling
