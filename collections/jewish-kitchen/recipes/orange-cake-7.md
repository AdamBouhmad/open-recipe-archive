## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 cups pulverized sugar
- 1 large orange
- 0.5 cup cold water
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking-powder
- 2 eggs
- sugar for icing

## Directions

1. Beat light the yolks of five eggs with two cups of pulverized sugar
2. Add juice of a large orange and part of the peel grated, one-half a cup of cold water and two cups of flour, sifted three times
3. Add two teaspoons of baking-powder in last sifting
4. Add last the stiff-beaten whites of three eggs
5. Bake in layers, and spread the following icing between and on top
6. For icing: beat the whites of two eggs stiff, add the juice and peel of one orange and sugar enough to stiffen
