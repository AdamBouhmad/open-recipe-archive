## Ingredients

- two cups of cold, cooked Lima beans
- two stalks of celery
- one dozen olives
- onion juice
- salt
- red pepper
- lettuce leaves
- French dressing
- green and red peppers

## Directions

1. Take two cups of cold, cooked Lima beans, two stalks of chopped celery, one dozen chopped olives, one teaspoon of onion juice, one teaspoon of salt, and a dash of red pepper
2. Mix thoroughly and serve on lettuce leaves with French dressing and garnish with green and red peppers cut in squares
