## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- very little water
- well-beaten yolks of eggs
- white sugar
- a shape of biscuit ice or any other cream ice
- brown-bread ice

## Directions

1. Put some gooseberries into a saucepan with very little water
2. When they are soft, pulp them through a sieve
3. Add several well-beaten yolks of eggs
4. Sweeten with white sugar
5. Have ready a shape of biscuit ice or any other cream ice that may be preferred
6. Take off a thick slice of the ice from the top carefully and without breaking
7. Scoop out a large portion of the ice which may be mixed with the gooseberry cream
8. Fill the hollow with it
9. Cover the shape with the piece that was removed and serve
