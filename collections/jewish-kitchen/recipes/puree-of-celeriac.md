## Ingredients

- celeriac
- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of cream

## Directions

1. Boil celeriac: pare the celeriac, cut in thin, narrow slices, and put into cold water
2. Drain from this water and drop into boiling water and boil thirty minutes
3. Drain and rinse with cold water
4. Press through a sieve
5. To one quart take two tablespoons of butter blended with two tablespoons flour and cooked until smooth and frothy, add the strained celeriac and cook five minutes, stirring frequently
6. Add one teaspoon of salt and a half cup of cream, cook five minutes longer and serve hot on toast or fried bread
