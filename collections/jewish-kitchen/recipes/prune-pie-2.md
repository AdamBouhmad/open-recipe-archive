## Ingredients

- rich pie paste
- prunes
- lemon
- sugar
- prune juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a rich pie paste.
2. Roll out the paste thin and line the pie-plate with it.
3. Put in a layer of prunes that have been stewed the day before with the addition of several slices of lemon and no sugar.
4. Split the prunes in halves and remove the pits before laying them on the pie crust.
5. After the first layer is in sprinkle it well with sugar, then pour over the sugar three or four tablespoons of the prune juice and dust the surface lightly with flour.
6. Repeat this process till there are three layers.
7. Cut enough of the paste in strips to cover the top of the fruit with a lattice crust and bake the pie in a rather quick oven.
