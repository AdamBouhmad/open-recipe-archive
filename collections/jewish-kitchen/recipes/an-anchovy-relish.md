## Ingredients

- anchovies
- eggs
- parsley
- garlic
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash well a dozen anchovies
2. halve them
3. have boiled 3 eggs hard
4. put them in cold water for 5 minutes
5. peel them
6. chop some parsley
7. take a large china plate
8. chop a tooth of garlic very fine
9. lay it in the plate with 3 spoonfuls of oil, 2 of vinegar
10. pepper it lightly
11. place the halves of the anchovies in strips round the plate (it forms like a star)
12. cut the eggs in round slices very thin with a sharp knife
13. place them in the rows round the plate
14. place the chopped parsley over
