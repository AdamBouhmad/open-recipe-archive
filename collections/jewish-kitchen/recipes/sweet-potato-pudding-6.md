## Ingredients

- one quart of grated, raw sweet potatoes
- one tablespoon of meat fat
- one tablespoon of chicken fat
- one half pound of brown sugar
- one-half pint of molasses
- one and one-half pints of cold water
- one saltspoon of salt
- black pepper
- grated orange peel
- ginger
- nutmeg
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pour into greased baking-pan
2. Bake in moderate oven until it jellies
3. May be eaten as a dessert, warm or cold
