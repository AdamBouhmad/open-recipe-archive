## Ingredients

- one ounce of spermacetti
- oil of almonds
- two or three grains of powdered cochineal

## Directions

1. Melt one ounce of spermacetti
2. Soften sufficiently with oil of almonds
3. Color it with two or three grains of powdered cochineal
4. Pour while warm into small toilet pots
