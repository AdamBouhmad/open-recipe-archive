## Ingredients

- six tablespoons of mustard
- one tablespoon of turmeric
- one cup of flour
- two cups of sugar
- two quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix six tablespoons of mustard, one tablespoon of turmeric, one cup of flour, two cups of sugar and two quarts of vinegar
2. These ingredients must be thoroughly mixed and then cooked until thick
3. Stir in the pickles
4. Heat thoroughly
5. Empty into glass jars and stand away until needed
