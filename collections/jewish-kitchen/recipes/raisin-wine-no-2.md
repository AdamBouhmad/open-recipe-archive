## Ingredients

- two pounds of raisins, seeded and chopped
- one pound of white loaf sugar
- one lemon
- six quarts of boiling water

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of raisins, seeded and chopped, one pound of white loaf sugar, and one lemon.
2. Put all into a stone jar, pour six quarts of boiling water over all and stir every day for a week.
3. Then strain and bottle.
4. Ready for use in ten or twelve days.
