## Ingredients

- one-half tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon chicken-fat
- one-half cup of soup stock
- one-half teaspoon onion juice
- one-half teaspoon lemon juice
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one-quarter teaspoon pepper
- one and one-half cups of veal or chicken
- one pair of brains
- one well-beaten egg
- beaten egg
- powdered bread or cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook one-half tablespoon of flour in one tablespoon chicken-fat
2. Add one-half cup of soup stock gradually, and one-half teaspoon each of onion juice, lemon juice, salt, and one-quarter teaspoon of pepper
3. Add one and one-half cups of veal or chicken, chopped very fine
4. Add one pair of brains which have been boiled
5. Mix these well
6. Remove from the fire and add one well-beaten egg
7. Turn this mixture out on a flat dish and place in ice-box to cool
8. Roll into small cones
9. Dip in beaten egg
10. Roll again in powdered bread or cracker crumbs
11. Drop them into boiling fat
12. Fry until a delicate brown
