## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- six onions
- a table spoonful of curry powder
- a desert spoonful of cayenne pepper
- salt
- a little Harvey's sauce
- lemon pickle

## Directions

1. Stew a knuckle of veal till half done
2. Cut off the greatest part of the meat
3. Continue to stew down the bone in the stock
4. Cut the meat into small pieces and fry with six onions thinly sliced and a table spoonful of curry powder
5. Add a desert spoonful of cayenne pepper and salt
6. Add the stock and let the whole gently simmer for nearly an hour
7. Flavour it with a little Harvey's sauce and lemon pickle
