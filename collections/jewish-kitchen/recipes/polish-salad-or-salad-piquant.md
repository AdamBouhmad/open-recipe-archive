## Ingredients

- half a dozen or more large salt pickles
- water
- two sour apples
- one large onion or two small ones
- white pepper
- sugar
- salt
- white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Lay half a dozen or more large salt pickles in water for about six hours, then drain off all the water.
2. Chop up two sour apples, one large onion or two small ones, chop the pickles and mix all thoroughly in a bowl.
3. Sprinkle over them a scant half teaspoon of pepper (white) and a tablespoon of sugar (either white or brown).
4. Add a pinch of salt if necessary.
5. Pour enough white wine vinegar over all to just cover.
6. Do not make more at a time than you can use up in a week, as it will not keep longer.
