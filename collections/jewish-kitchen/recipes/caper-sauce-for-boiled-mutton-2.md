## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of the liquor the mutton is boiled in
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1 spoonful of the vinegar from the capers
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 3 teaspoonfuls of capers

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 pint of the liquor the mutton is boiled in in a small saucepan and let it boil
2. Stir in a teaspoonful of flour mixed with a spoonful of the vinegar from the capers
3. Season nicely with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Chop 3 teaspoonfuls of capers and put them into the sauceboat
5. Turn the sauce over them and it is ready for table
