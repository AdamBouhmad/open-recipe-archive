## Ingredients

- fillets of flounder
- salt
- lemon juice
- chopped lettuce
- mayonnaise sauce
- slices of tomatoes
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Put some fillets of flounder into boiling water with a little salt and lemon juice, and cook until tender, then drain thoroughly.
2. When cold, put them in the center of some chopped lettuce, cover with mayonnaise sauce and garnish with slices of tomatoes and hard-boiled eggs.
