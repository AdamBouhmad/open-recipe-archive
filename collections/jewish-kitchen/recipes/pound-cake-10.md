## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 glass brandy
- 9 eggs
- 1.25 pounds sifted flour
- 0.5 teaspoon baking-powder

## Directions

1. Rub one pound of butter and one pound of powdered sugar to a cream
2. Add the grated peel of a lemon, a glass of brandy and the yolks of nine eggs, added one at a time
3. Add last one pound and a quarter of sifted flour with one-half teaspoon of baking-powder and the beaten whites of the eggs
4. Bake slowly
