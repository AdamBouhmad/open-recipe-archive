## Ingredients

- one fine fowl
- good gravy
- one pint of fresh button mushrooms
- mushroom powder
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Truss a fine fowl as if for boiling
2. Split it down the back and broil gently
3. When nearly done, put it in a stewpan with a good gravy
4. Add a pint of fresh button mushrooms
5. Season to taste
6. Add a little mushroom powder and lemon juice to improve the flavour
