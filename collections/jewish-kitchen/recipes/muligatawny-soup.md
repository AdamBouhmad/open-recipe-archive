## Ingredients

- two chickens
- flour
- four onions
- a piece of clarified fat
- pepper
- salt
- two table spoonfuls of curry powder
- three quarts of strong beef gravy
- the juice of a lemon
- rice
- a pinch of saffron

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens up small, as if for fricassee
2. Flour them well
3. Put them in a saucepan with four onions shred, a piece of clarified fat, pepper, salt, and two table spoonfuls of curry powder
4. Let it simmer for an hour
5. Add three quarts of strong beef gravy
6. Let it continue simmering for another hour
7. Before sent to table stir in the juice of a lemon
8. Some persons approve of a little rice being boiled with the stock and a pinch of saffron is also sometimes added
