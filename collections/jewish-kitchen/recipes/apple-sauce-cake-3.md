## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of butter
- one egg
- one cup of sugar
- a saltspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of allspice
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- a little grated nutmeg
- one cup of chopped raisins
- flour
- one cup of unsweetened apple sauce
- one teaspoon of baking-soda
- one tablespoon of boiling water

## Directions

1. In a big mixing bowl, beat to a creamy consistency four tablespoons of butter, one egg and one cup of sugar
2. Add a saltspoon of salt, one teaspoon of allspice, one teaspoon of vanilla and a little grated nutmeg
3. Beat and stir all these ingredients well together
4. Add one cup of chopped raisins, after dusting them with flour
5. Mix these well through the dough
6. Add one cup of unsweetened apple sauce which has been pressed through a fine wire sieve
7. Mix well with the other ingredients
8. Stir in one teaspoon of baking-soda dissolved in one tablespoon of boiling water
9. Last of all, stir in one cup of flour, sifting twice after measuring it
10. Bake forty-five minutes in moderate oven, or one hour if dough is too thin
