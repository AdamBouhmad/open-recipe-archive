## Ingredients

- fruit
- half a pound of sugar to a quart of fruit
- whites of eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Acid fruits are best stewed before putting into a pie
2. Use half a pound of sugar to a quart of fruit, not quite so much if the fruit is ripe
3. Lay the fruit high in the middle of the dish, to make the pie a good shape
4. Lay over the crust, when nearly baked, an icing of the whites of eggs whisked with sugar
5. Replace the tart or pie in the oven
