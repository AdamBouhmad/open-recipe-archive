## Ingredients

- cabbage
- one cup of boiled rice
- three dozen raisins
- salt
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- two tablespoons of drippings

## Directions

1. Boil cabbage whole for five minutes; drain, separate the leaves after it has cooled.
2. Mix one cup of boiled rice with three dozen raisins, pinch of salt, one teaspoon of cinnamon and two tablespoons of drippings.
3. Put two tablespoons of this mixture in three or four leaves, roll them and tie together with string.
4. Place in pan and let cook for an hour until done.
5. There must be sufficient fat and gravy to prevent the cabbage rolls from sticking to the bottom of the pan which must be kept closely covered.
