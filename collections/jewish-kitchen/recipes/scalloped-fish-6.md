## Ingredients

- dressed fish
- bread crumbs
- butter
- cream
- white pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- mushrooms
- anchovy

## Directions

1. Take any dressed fish and break it in small pieces
2. Put it into tin scallops with a few crumbs of bread, a good piece of butter, a little cream if approved, white pepper, salt, and nutmeg
3. Bake in an oven for ten minutes, or brown before the fire
4. Two or three mushrooms mixed, or an anchovy will be found an improvement
