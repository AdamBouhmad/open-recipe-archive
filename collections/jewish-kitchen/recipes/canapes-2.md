## Ingredients

- bread, two days old
- butter
- prepared sandwich filling
- watercress or lemon
- optional: stuffed olives
- optional for cheese canapes: grated cheese, salt, pepper

## Directions

1. Cut bread oblong, diamond shaped, in rounds, or with a fluted edge cutter.
2. Toast or fry the bread crouton fashion, or butter it lightly and set in a hot oven to brown delicately.
3. While the toast is quite hot, spread with the prepared mixture.
4. Serve on a small plate with sprigs of watercress or points of lemon as a garnish.
5. Alternative: cut the bread into delicate fingers, pile log-cabin fashion, and garnish the centre with a stuffed olive.
6. For cheese canapes: sprinkle the toast thickly with grated cheese, well seasoned with salt and pepper, set in a hot oven until the cheese melts and serve immediately.
