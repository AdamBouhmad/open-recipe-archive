## Ingredients

- six oranges (California)
- three pints of cold water
- three pounds of seeded raisins
- three quarts of currants (picked and washed)
- three pounds of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Peel six oranges, cut the skin in very small narrow strips, or run through a food chopper
2. Slice the oranges very thin and quarter the slices
3. Let it stand overnight in three pints of cold water
4. Place this in a preserving kettle with three pounds of seeded raisins, three quarts of currants and three pounds of granulated sugar
5. Boil all together for two hours and put in glass jars, closing them while hot
