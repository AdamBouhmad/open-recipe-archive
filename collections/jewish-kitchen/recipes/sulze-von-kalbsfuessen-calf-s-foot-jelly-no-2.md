## Ingredients

- one calf's head
- four calf's feet
- four quarts of water
- two or three small onions
- a few cloves
- salt
- one teaspoon of whole peppers
- two or three bay leaves
- juice of a large lemon
- one cup of white wine
- white wine vinegar
- one teaspoon of sugar
- whipped whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Clean calf's head and calf's feet carefully
2. Let them lay in cold water for half an hour
3. Set on to boil with four quarts of water
4. Add two or three small onions, a few cloves, salt, one teaspoon of whole peppers, two or three bay leaves, juice of a large lemon, one cup of white wine and a little white wine vinegar
5. Let this boil slowly for five or six hours until reduced one-half
6. Strain through a fine hair sieve and let it stand ten or twelve hours
7. Remove the meat from the bones and when cold cut into fine pieces
8. Add the boiled brains
9. Skim off every particle of fat from the jelly and melt slowly
10. Add one teaspoon of sugar and the whipped whites of three eggs and boil very fast for about fifteen minutes, skimming well
11. Taste and if not tart enough, add a dash of vinegar
12. Strain through a flannel bag, do not squeeze or shake it until the jelly ceases to run freely
13. Remove the bowl and put another under for the remaining jelly
14. Wet your mould, put in the jelly and set in a cool place
15. For variety, wet another mould and put in the bits of meat and the brains and lastly the jelly; set this on ice
