## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of isinglass
- a little water
- 1/2 pint almond milk
- vanille or orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 oz. of isinglass in a little water
2. Add 1/2 pint of almond milk sweetened to taste and flavoured with vanille or orange-flower water, or whatever may be preferred
