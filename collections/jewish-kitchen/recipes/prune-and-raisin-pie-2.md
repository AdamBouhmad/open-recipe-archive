## Ingredients

- One-half pound of prunes
- One-half cup of raisins
- Water

## Directions

1. Use one-half pound of prunes, cooked until soft enough to remove the stones
2. Mash with a fork and add the juice in which they have been cooked
3. Cook one-half cup of raisins in a little water for a few minutes until soft
4. Add to the prune
