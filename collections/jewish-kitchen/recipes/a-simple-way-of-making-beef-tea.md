## Ingredients

- one pound of gravy beef
- a pint of water
- a very little salt
- 2 cloves
- 2 allspice
- a small piece of crust

## Directions

1. Cut the gravy beef in pieces the size of dice, put into a pint of water with a very little salt, 2 cloves, 2 allspice
2. Let it boil for 1 hour only
3. Toast a small piece of crust
4. Let it boil with the crust to colour it
5. Strain it through a very clean sieve into a white basin
