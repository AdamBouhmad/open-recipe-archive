## Ingredients

- cold fish
- thin paste

## Directions

1. Make a force-meat of any cold fish
2. Form it into thin cakes and fry of a light brown
3. Or enclose them first in thin paste and then fry them
4. The roes of fish or the livers are particularly nice prepared in this way
