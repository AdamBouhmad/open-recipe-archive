## Ingredients

- one gallon of brandy
- two quarts of cherries
- one pound of cut loaf sugar

## Directions

1. To one gallon of brandy allow two quarts of cherries
2. Mash and pound them until all the stones are broken
3. put in the brandy and add a pound of cut loaf sugar
4. Set in the sun for two or three weeks
5. shake daily
6. strain and bottle
