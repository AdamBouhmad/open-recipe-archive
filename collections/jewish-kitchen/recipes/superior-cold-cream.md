## Ingredients

- one drachm of spermacetti
- one drachm of white wax
- two fluid ounces of oil of almond
- rose water

## Directions

1. Melt together one drachm of spermacetti, the same quantity of white wax, and two fluid ounces of oil of almond
2. While these are still warm, beat up with them as much rose water as they will absorb
