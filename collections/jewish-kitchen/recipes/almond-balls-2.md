## Ingredients

- one-eighth pound of almonds
- yolk of one egg
- pinch of salt
- grated rind of lemon
- white of egg
- boiling fat

## Directions

1. One-eighth pound of almonds chopped fine.
2. Yolk of one egg, well beaten.
3. Add almonds to egg, pinch of salt, little grated rind of lemon.
4. Beat white of egg stiff, then mix all together.
5. Drop a little from end of teaspoon into boiling fat.
6. Put in soup just before serving.
