## Ingredients

- salmon from the can
- one cup of hot cream sauce
- patty cups or toasted bread or crackers

## Directions

1. Remove salmon from the can, place it in a colander and wash under running water or scald with boiling water.
2. Break into small pieces, stir into one cup of hot cream sauce.
3. Bring all to a boil and serve in patty cups or on toasted bread or crackers.
