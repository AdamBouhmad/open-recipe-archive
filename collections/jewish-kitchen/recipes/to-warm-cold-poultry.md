## Ingredients

- cold poultry pieces
- seasoning for cutlets
- good gravy
- sippets of toast
- sliced lemon
- rice or mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut up the pieces required to be dressed
2. Spread over them a seasoning as for cutlets and fry them
3. Pour over a little good gravy
4. Garnish with sippets of toast and sliced lemon, or place them in an edging of rice or mashed potatoes
