## Ingredients

- two tablespoons fat
- onion
- two tablespoons flour
- one and one-half cups water
- salt
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- four medium-sized cooking pears
- six medium raw potatoes

## Directions

1. Heat two tablespoons of fat
2. Add chopped onion and two tablespoons of flour
3. When flour is brown, add one and one-half cups of water
4. Stir and cook until smooth
5. Add salt, brown sugar and a little cinnamon to taste
6. Quarter four medium-sized cooking pears, but do not peel
7. Cook them in the brown sauce
8. Add six medium raw potatoes, pared
9. Cook until tender
