## Ingredients

- a piece of fat the size of an egg
- two or three onions
- a little gravy
- flour
- a little vinegar
- a spoonful of mustard
- a little cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Throw into a saucepan a piece of fat the size of an egg, with two or three onions sliced, let them brown
2. Add a little gravy, flour, a little vinegar, a spoonful of mustard, and a little cayenne pepper
3. Boil it and serve with the steaks
