## Ingredients

- red mullet
- salt
- pepper
- butter or oil
- paper
- lemon juice
- cayenne
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the mullet well
2. take care of the liver
3. dry them
4. season well with salt, pepper
5. butter or oil a sheet of paper well to wrap up each mullet
6. broil before a clear fire
7. when done, take off the paper carefully
8. squeeze over them some lemon juice with a little cayenne
9. serve very hot, garnished with parsley
