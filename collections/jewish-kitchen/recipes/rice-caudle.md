## Ingredients

- half a pint of milk
- a spoonful of ground rice
- a little milk
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil half a pint of milk
2. Add a spoonful of ground rice mixed with a little milk till quite smooth
3. Stir it into the boiling milk
4. Let it simmer till it thickens
5. Carefully strain it and sweeten with white sugar
