## Ingredients

- one pint of flour
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- two tablespoons of butter
- milk
- soft butter
- granulated sugar
- powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, sugar, salt, and baking-powder
2. Rub in butter
3. Mix with milk to soft dough
4. Roll out one-half inch thick
5. Spread with soft butter, granulated sugar, and powdered cinnamon
6. Roll up like jelly roll
7. Cut in inch slices
8. Lay close together in greased pan
9. Bake in quick oven
