## Ingredients

- Beet-root
- Water
- Relishes
- Spices
- Whole eggs

## Directions

1. About three weeks before Pesach take twenty pounds of beet-root, which may be thoroughly washed and scraped.
2. Place the whole in a six-quart crock, cover with water.
3. Place the cover on the crock and over this cover put a clean cloth.
4. When ready for use the liquor is boiled with any relishes and spices that are liked.
5. Boil as much as required for the meal, for twenty minutes or longer if desired.
6. Thicken with beaten whole eggs that have been mixed with a little of the unboiled borsht.
7. Add the hot soup and serve.
8. Do not boil after adding the eggs.
9. To two quarts of borsht take three eggs.
