## Ingredients

- one cup of white flour
- one-half cup of corn-meal
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half teaspoon of soda
- one egg
- one cup of sour milk
- one tablespoon of melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of white flour, one-half cup of corn-meal, one tablespoon of sugar, one-half teaspoon of salt and one-half teaspoon of soda
2. Add one egg beaten into one cup of sour milk and one tablespoon of melted butter
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Bake in well-greased tins
