## Ingredients

- two pounds of sharp apples
- two pounds of quinces
- one pound of white sugar powdered
- half a pint of water
- a little bruised cochineal

## Directions

1. Peel, cut into quarters, and core two pounds of sharp apples, and the same quantity of quinces
2. Put them into a jar
3. Sprinkle one pound of white sugar powdered over them
4. Cover them with half a pint of water
5. Put in also a little bruised cochineal tied in a muslin
6. Set them in a slack oven till tender
7. Take out the cochineal
8. Pulp the fruit to a marmalade
