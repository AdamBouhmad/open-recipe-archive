## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- water
- two ounces of butter
- half a pound of butter total

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of flour into a stiff paste with a little water, first having rubbed into it about two ounces of butter
2. Roll it out
3. Add by degrees the remainder of the butter (there should be altogether half a pound of butter)
4. Fold the paste and roll about two or three times
