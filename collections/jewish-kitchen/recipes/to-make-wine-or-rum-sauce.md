## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 eggs
- a spoonful of sugar
- a cup of water or almond milk
- a glass of rum or 2 of wine
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Beat up 2 or 3 eggs well with a spoonful of sugar
2. Add a cup of water or almond milk
3. Add a glass of rum or 2 of wine and the juice of a lemon
