## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of white sugar
- three quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- two eggs
- one pound and a half of flour
- three spoonsful of yeast
- milk
- two ounces of citron
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut citron thin and mix into a light paste with all ingredients
2. Bake in a tin
3. Strew powdered sugar and cinnamon over it before baking
