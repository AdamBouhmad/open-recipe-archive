## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- three-fourths cup of powdered sugar
- one cup of ground sweet chocolate
- one cup of walnuts
- three tablespoons of flour

## Directions

1. Beat whites of three eggs to a snow
2. Add three-fourths cup of powdered sugar, one cup of ground sweet chocolate, one cup of walnuts chopped, three tablespoons of flour
3. Drop by teaspoonful on greased baking-tin
4. Bake in slow oven
