## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one-half cup of water
- rind of one lemon
- juice of two lemons
- one-half teaspoon of butter
- one scant teaspoon of cornstarch
- cold water

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of sugar with one-half cup of water, rind of one lemon, juice of two, and one-half teaspoon of butter
2. When boiling stir in a scant teaspoon of cornstarch dissolved in a little cold water
3. Serve hot
