## Ingredients

- soles
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter or oil
- caper or anchovy sauce
- plain butter
- lemon
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Take a fine pair of soles, wash and dry them well
2. Season them with salt and pepper
3. Dredge them lightly with flour
4. Rub the gridiron with butter or oil
5. Put them in before a very clear and bright fire, keep turning them
6. You must not have them done a minute before wanted
7. Slip them off the gridiron on to a very hot dish
8. Serve them with caper or anchovy sauce, or plain butter rubbed over them, with the juice of a lemon and a little cayenne
