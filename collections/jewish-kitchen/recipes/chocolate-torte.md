## Ingredients

- nine eggs
- one-half pound of pulverized sugar
- one-half pound of almonds
- one-half pound of finest vanilla chocolate
- one-half pound of raisins
- seven soda crackers
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- juice of three lemons
- one-fourth glass of wine

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth
2. Beat yolks with sugar until very light
3. Add chocolate
4. Add almonds (half cut and grated)
5. Add raisins (cut and seeded)
6. Add seven soda crackers rolled to a powder
7. Add one teaspoon of baking-powder
8. Add juice of three lemons and one-fourth glass of wine
9. Stir in the beaten whites last
10. Bake as with other torten
