## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- one cup of sugar
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- pinch of cloves
- pinch of allspice
- one cup of stale rye bread crumbs
- beaten whites
- one cup of claret or white wine
- sherry wine sauce or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of three eggs with one cup of sugar
2. Add one teaspoon of cinnamon, pinch of cloves, and pinch of allspice
3. Add one cup of stale rye bread crumbs gradually
4. Mix well and add beaten whites
5. Bake slowly
6. Half an hour before serving, add one cup of claret or white wine
7. Serve with sherry wine sauce or whipped cream
