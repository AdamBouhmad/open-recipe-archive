## Ingredients

- one pint of bread dough
- one well-beaten egg
- one-half cup of sugar
- a generous tablespoon of butter
- finely chopped nuts
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Knead into a pint of bread dough one well-beaten egg, one-half cup of sugar, and a generous tablespoon of butter
2. Roll flat and place in a shallow pan
3. Let rise again until very light
4. Sprinkle with finely chopped nuts, dust over with sugar and cinnamon
5. Bake in a quick oven
