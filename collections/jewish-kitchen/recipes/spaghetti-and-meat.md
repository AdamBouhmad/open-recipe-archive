## Ingredients

- spaghetti
- left-over meat
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Break spaghetti in small pieces and boil until tender.
2. Put left-over meat through chopper and mix with the spaghetti, salt, pepper and a little onion juice.
3. Grease a baking dish and put in the meat and spaghetti.
4. Sprinkle on top with bread crumbs and bake in a moderate oven.
