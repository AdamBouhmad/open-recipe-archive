## Ingredients

- diamond-shaped slices of stale bread
- six sardines
- two tablespoons of lemon juice
- a few drops of Worcestershire sauce
- a dash of pepper
- two teaspoons of chopped parsley
- four tablespoons of creamed butter
- whites of hard-boiled eggs
- shredded olives

## Directions

1. Toast lightly diamond-shaped slices of stale bread
2. Skin and bone six sardines, put them in a bowl and run to a paste with a silver spoon
3. Add two tablespoons of lemon juice, a few drops of Worcestershire sauce, a dash of pepper, two teaspoons of chopped parsley and four tablespoons of creamed butter
4. Spread the sardine mixture on the toasted bread
5. Garnish with a border of whites of hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped
6. Scatter shredded olives on top
