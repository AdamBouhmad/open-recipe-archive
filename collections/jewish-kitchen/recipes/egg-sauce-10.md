## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- a dessert spoonful of flour
- a little salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- the juice of a large lemon or two small ones
- a ladle of the liquor the fowls were boiled in
- chopped parsley or capers

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of 2 eggs in a basin with a dessert spoonful of flour well rubbed in a little salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
2. Have the juice of a large lemon, or two small ones, squeezed in a teacup, strain all the pips
3. Turn it gently, stirring all the time the egg and lemon till you have it quite smooth
4. If for boiled fowls take a ladle of the liquor the fowls were boiled in, stir it in
5. Let it boil up to thicken
6. Turn it into the sauceboat with some chopped parsley or capers
