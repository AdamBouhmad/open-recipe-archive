## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- toast

## Directions

1. Wash, peel caps and stems of one pound of mushrooms
2. drain dry between towels
3. Place in spider with two tablespoons of butter and one-quarter teaspoon of salt
4. Cover and cook twenty minutes, tossing them
5. Serve on hot slices of toast
