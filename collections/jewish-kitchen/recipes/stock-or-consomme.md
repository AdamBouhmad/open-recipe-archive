## Ingredients

- Shin of beef or ox-cheek
- good gravy-beef
- bones
- water
- gravy beef
- knuckle of veal
- veal or white poultry
- bones and trimmings of meat or poultry

## Directions

1. Break the bones and cut up the meat, as the juices are better extracted
2. Put on, at first, but very little water, and add more when the first quantity is nearly dried up
3. Boil six pounds of meat for about five hours; if bones, the same quantity will require double the time
4. Very tolerable stock can be procured by boiling down bones and the trimmings of meat or poultry
5. The liquor in which beef or mutton intended for the table has been boiled will also, with small additions and skilful flavoring, make an excellent soup at a trifling expense
