## Ingredients

- oil of almonds
- powdered sugar
- fresh strawberries
- isinglass
- basket of strawberries
- 1/2 lb. sifted sugar
- 1 pint of milk
- 1/2 an oz. of isinglass

## Directions

1. Oil a pretty jelly mould with the oil of almonds
2. Sift over some powdered sugar
3. Take some fine fresh strawberries and dip them in a little dissolved isinglass to make them stick to the mould
4. Have a basket of strawberries, pick off the stalks, rub them through a clean sieve with 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar, rub them through quite dry
5. Mix with them a pint of milk with 1/2 an oz. of isinglass dissolved in, stir it all together
6. Turn into the garnished mould
7. Put it in rough ice till you are ready to serve it
8. Turn it out very carefully and garnish the dish to fancy
