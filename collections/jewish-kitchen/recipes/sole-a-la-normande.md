## Ingredients

- sole
- mushrooms
- parsley
- fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- eggs
- lemon
- button mushrooms
- milk
- hard boiled eggs
- flour
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take a fine large thick sole, wash it well and dry it
2. Open it carefully, draw the bone as though you were going to cut off the fillets, slip the knife lightly not to cut the flesh, loosen the bone down the centre, get it out
3. Chop very fine 6 mushrooms and a little parsley
4. Put this into a small stewpan with 2 ounces of fresh butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg
5. Stir it on the fire for 5 minutes till the mushrooms are cooked
6. Beat 2 eggs and stir them in and put it over the fire till the eggs are set
7. Turn it all on a plate to cool
8. When cold put the mixture inside the sole in the place of the bone taken out
9. Lay the sole on a baking dish covered over with paper buttered, first sprinkle over the fish salt and pepper, then put the paper
10. Lay 2 oz. of butter in the dish, with the juice of a lemon
11. Boil a few button mushrooms in a little milk to keep them white, drain them dry when done
12. Cut 2 hard boiled eggs in slices, ready for garnishing, also a little chopped parsley
13. Lay the sole, take off the paper, in a very hot dish
14. Turn the liquor into a small saucepan, mix a spoonful of flour with a glass of white wine, stir it into the liquor, let it boil to get thick like another sauce
15. Pour it over the sole, and garnish one slice of egg with a little chopped parsley on and one button mushroom all round the dish
