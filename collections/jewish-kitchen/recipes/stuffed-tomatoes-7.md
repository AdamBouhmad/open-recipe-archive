## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- paprika
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Select tomatoes of uniform size, cut a slice from the stem-end and scoop out a portion of the pulp.
2. Have in readiness a dressing made from grated bread crumbs, parsley, a slice of minced onion, a high seasoning of salt and paprika and sufficient melted butter to moisten.
3. Fill this into the tomatoes and heap it up in the centers.
4. Place a bit of butter on top of each and bake in a quick oven until the vegetables are tender and the tops are delicately browned.
