## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar to taste
- wine or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the fruit well
2. put on the stove in a saucepan without adding any more water
3. Cover with a lid
4. let the fruit get thoroughly heated all through until it comes to a boil, but do not boil it
5. Stir occasionally
6. mash the fruit well with a wooden potato masher
7. strain through a fine sieve, being careful to get every drop of substance from the fruit
8. Sweeten the juice with sugar to taste
9. add a few drops of wine or lemon juice
10. put back on the stove
11. cook until it thickens, stirring occasionally
12. Serve with cake, fritters or puddings
