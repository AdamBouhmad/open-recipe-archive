## Ingredients

- one ounce of chocolate
- a small teacup full of water
- nearly a pint of good milk

## Directions

1. Cut one ounce of chocolate in small pieces
2. Boil it about six or seven minutes with a small teacup full of water
3. Stir it till smooth
4. Add nearly a pint of good milk
5. Give it another boil, stirring or milling it well
6. Serve directly
