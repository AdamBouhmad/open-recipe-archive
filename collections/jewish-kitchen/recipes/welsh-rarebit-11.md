## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- two cups finely cut American cheese
- one-half cup of milk or stale beer
- one-half teaspoon of English mustard
- two beaten eggs
- salt
- toast

## Directions

1. Melt one tablespoon of butter
2. Add two cups finely cut American cheese
3. When it melts add one-half cup of milk or stale beer
4. Keep stirring until it is smooth
5. Add one-half teaspoon of English mustard
6. Add two beaten eggs
7. Cook one minute longer and salt to taste
8. Serve on toast
