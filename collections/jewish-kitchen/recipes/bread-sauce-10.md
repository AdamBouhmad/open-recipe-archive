## Ingredients

- a large onion
- pepper
- milk
- grated stale bread
- a piece of butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a large onion and boil it, with a little pepper till quite soft, in milk
2. Take it out, and pour the milk over grated stale bread
3. Boil it up with a piece of butter, and dredge it with flour
4. It should be well beaten up with a silver fork
