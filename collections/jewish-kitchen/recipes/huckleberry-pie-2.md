## Ingredients

- two cups of huckleberries
- one egg
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one tablespoon of milk
- rich pastry or cookie dough

## Directions

1. Clean, pick and wash two cups of huckleberries, then drain them
2. Beat yolk of one egg and two tablespoons of sugar until light
3. Add one tablespoon of milk, then the drained berries
4. Line one pie-plate with rich pastry or cookie dough
5. Pour on it the berry mixture
6. Put in the oven and bake light brown
7. Remove from the oven and spread with a meringue made of the white of the egg beaten stiff, and two tablespoons of sugar added
8. Brown nicely
