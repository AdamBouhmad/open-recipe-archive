## Ingredients

- rich baking-powder biscuit dough
- cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a rich baking-powder biscuit dough and roll it out until it is about two-thirds of an inch thick
2. Pit and stew enough cherries to make a thick layer of fruit and add sugar to taste
3. Spread them over the dough thickly and roll it up, taking care to keep the cherries from falling out
4. Wrap a cloth around it and sew it up loosely with coarse thread
5. Allow plenty of room for the dough to rise
6. Lay the roley-poley on a plate, set it in a steamer and steam for an hour and a half
7. Serve in slices with cream or sauce
