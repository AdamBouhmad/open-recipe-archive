## Ingredients

- Pineapples
- Sugar, allowing half a pound to two pounds of fruit
- Water, allowing a tumblerful to a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the pineapples as in the first method
2. Steam the sliced pines in a porcelain steamer until tender
3. In the meantime make a syrup of the sugar, allowing a tumblerful of water to a pound of sugar
4. Skim the syrup carefully
5. Put in your steamed pineapples and can as above
