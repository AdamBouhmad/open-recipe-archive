## Ingredients

- cold poultry, meat, or fish
- minced mushrooms
- herbs
- parsley
- grated lemon peel
- minced anchovies
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs or pounded vermicelli

## Directions

1. Pound any cold poultry, meat, or fish, make it into a delicate forcemeat
2. Vary the flavor according to taste; use minced mushrooms, herbs, parsley, grated lemon peel for poultry and veal; use minced anchovies instead of mushrooms when the croquettes are made of fish
3. Form the mixture into balls or oval shapes the size of small eggs
4. Dip them into beaten eggs
5. Thickly sprinkle with bread crumbs or pounded vermicelli
6. Fry of a handsome brown
