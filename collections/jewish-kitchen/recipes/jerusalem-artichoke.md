## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichoke
- milk
- one small onion
- one tablespoon of butter
- one level tablespoon of flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-fourth teaspoon of pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the washed and peeled artichoke into cubes.
2. Put in a stew-pan, and cover with milk (a generous pint to a quart of cubes).
3. Add one small onion and cook twenty minutes.
4. Beat together one tablespoon of butter and one level tablespoon of flour, and stir this into the boiling milk.
5. Season with one teaspoon of salt and one-fourth teaspoon of pepper, and continue the cooking one-half hour longer.
6. The cooking should be done in a double boiler.
