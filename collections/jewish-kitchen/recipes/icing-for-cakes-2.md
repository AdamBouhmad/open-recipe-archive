## Ingredients

- half a pound of sifted white sugar
- one wine glass of orange flower-water
- the whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Whisk half a pound of sifted white sugar with one wine glass of orange flower-water
2. Add the whites of two eggs, well beaten and strained
3. Whisk until it is quite thick and white
4. When the cake is almost cold, dip a soft camel's hair brush into it
5. Cover the cake well
6. Set it in a cool oven to harden
