## Ingredients

- one-half cup of sweet almonds blanched
- four eggs
- four tablespoons of cream
- one tablespoon of butter
- powdered sugar
- salted almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare one-half cup of sweet almonds, blanched, chopped fine and pounded smooth.
2. Beat four eggs slightly, add four tablespoons of cream and turn it into a hot omelet pan on which you have melted one tablespoon of butter.
3. Cook carefully, drawing the cooked portion into the centre and tilting the pan to allow the liquid part to run over the bare pan.
4. When nearly all set, sprinkle the almonds over the surface and turn the edges over until well rolled.
5. Then slip it out on a hot dish and dredge with powdered sugar, and scatter several salted almonds over the top.
6. Serve immediately.
