## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- a cup of granulated sugar
- thirty prunes
- two tablespoons of sweet chocolate
- two tablespoons of grated almonds
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of four eggs and a cup of granulated sugar and stir to a cream
2. Chop fine thirty prunes (prunes being boiled without sugar)
3. Add two tablespoons of sweet chocolate and two tablespoons of grated almonds
4. Add the whites, which have been beaten to a snow
5. Boil two and one-half hours in a pudding form
6. Serve with whipped cream
