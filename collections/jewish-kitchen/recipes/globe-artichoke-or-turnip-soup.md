## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- one and one-half pounds of sliced turnips or artichokes
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt
- three cups of hot milk
- three cups of hot water
- sugar
- cream or butter

## Directions

1. Heat two tablespoons of butter, add one and one-half pounds of sliced turnips or artichokes and stir them in the butter
2. add one tablespoon of flour, a little salt, three cups of hot milk, three cups of hot water, stirring them in slowly
3. When the vegetables are done, rub them through a sieve
4. put them back in the saucepan, add a little sugar and more seasoning, if required, and heat thoroughly
5. A little cream or butter may be put into the tureen, and the soup stirred into it
