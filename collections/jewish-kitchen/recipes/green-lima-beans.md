## Ingredients

- shelled beans
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- butter or hot cream or cream sauce

## Directions

1. Cover the shelled beans with boiling water
2. Bring to a boil quickly
3. Then let them simmer slowly till tender
4. Drain and add salt, pepper and butter or hot cream or cream sauce
