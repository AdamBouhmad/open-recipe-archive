## Ingredients

- two cups cold potatoes
- two cups cream sauce
- one cup bread crumbs
- butter
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Cut cold potatoes into cubes
2. Mix well with cream sauce, adding more seasoning if necessary
3. Pour into a baking dish
4. Cover with bread crumbs
5. Dot with small pieces of butter
6. Bake for about half an hour
