## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 1 lb. castor sugar
- 12 eggs
- 1/2 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. ground rice

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in your pan, only warm, not to oil.
2. Add the sugar, stir it well till it is like a cream.
3. Break the eggs three at the time, and stir the paste all the time.
4. When all the eggs are worked in, add the ground rice and flour.
5. Bake it in a round tin buttered.
