## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- cold water
- a pinch of salt
- two cups of milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- the yolk of an egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and wash one cup of rice
2. Put on to boil with cold water, add a pinch of salt
3. When done drain off the water, if any
4. Add two cups of milk, stir in and let boil for five minutes
5. Dish up, then sprinkle sugar and cinnamon generously over the top
6. The yolk of an egg can be added just before serving if desired
