## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of cooked rice
- two cups of sweet milk
- one lemon
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs light with one cup of sugar.
2. Add one cup of cooked rice, two cups of sweet milk, juice and rind of one lemon, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon.
3. Pour in pudding-pan and place in a pan filled with hot water.
4. Bake until firm in moderate oven.
5. Serve with lemon sauce.
