## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes
- melted butter
- eggs
- cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Take two cups of cold mashed potatoes and stir into them one tablespoon of melted butter, beating to a white cream before adding anything else.
2. Put with this two eggs beaten extremely light, one cup of cream, and salt to taste.
3. Beat all well and pour into a deep dish, and bake in a quick oven until it is nice and brown.
