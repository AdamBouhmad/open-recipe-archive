## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- ginger or ground mace
- egg

## Directions

1. Rub in a basin 2 ounces of fresh butter with 3 spoonfuls of flour, a little salt, pepper, a little chopped parsley, and a little ginger or ground mace
2. Beat an egg and mix it well with the other ingredients
3. It ought to be like a thick batter
4. Take up a piece with a spoon and drop it into the boiling soup
