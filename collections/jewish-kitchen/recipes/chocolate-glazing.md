## Ingredients

- two sticks of bitter chocolate
- five tablespoons of powdered sugar
- three tablespoons of boiling water

## Directions

1. Grate two sticks of bitter chocolate, add five tablespoons of powdered sugar and three tablespoons of boiling water
2. Put on the stove, over moderate fire, stir while boiling until smooth, glossy and thick
3. Spread at once on cake and set aside to harden
