## Ingredients

- a piece of stale bread the size of half-penny roll
- 2 oz. of chopped suet
- a little parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- half an egg

## Directions

1. Soak a piece of stale bread the size of half-penny roll
2. Drain the water well from it by wringing it in a small piece of cloth
3. Put it in a basin with 2 oz. of chopped suet, a little parsley, marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
4. Mix with half an egg, make into balls
5. Use these balls as you require them to change the style of your cooking
