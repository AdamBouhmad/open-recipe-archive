## Ingredients

- four or five large tart apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- a few pounded almonds
- a little white wine
- grated peel of one lemon
- four eggs
- one cup of pulverized sugar
- one cup of flour

## Directions

1. Pare four or five large tart apples and cut off the top of each apple to use as a cover
2. Scrape out all the inside, being careful not to break the apples
3. Mix scrapings with sugar, cinnamon, raisins, a few pounded almonds and add a little white wine and the grated peel of one lemon
4. Fill up the apples with this mixture and put back the top of each apple, so as to cover each well
5. Grease a deep dish, set in the apples and stew a few minutes
6. Make a sponge cake batter of four eggs, one cup of pulverized sugar, one cup of flour and pour over the apples
7. Bake one-half hour
8. Eat warm or cold, with or without sauce
