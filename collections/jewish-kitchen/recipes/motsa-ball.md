## Ingredients

- 2 biscuits
- 2 oz. of fine chopped suet
- marjoram
- parsley
- grated nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- fat or oil
- couple of spoonfuls of sieved meal
- 2 eggs beaten with 2 spoonfuls of water

## Directions

1. Soak 2 biscuits
2. Squeeze the water well out of them
3. Put them in a basin with 2 oz. of fine chopped suet
4. Add a little marjoram, parsley, a little grated nutmeg, salt, pepper
5. Fry a little onion in a little fat or oil
6. Turn it into your mixture
7. Add a couple of spoonfuls of the sieved meal
8. Mix with 2 eggs beaten with 2 spoonfuls of water
9. Roll it up and form the balls
10. Boil 20 minutes
