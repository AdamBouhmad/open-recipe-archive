## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons flour
- one cup boiling water
- one-half teaspoon salt
- pepper
- paprika

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of butter and stir in two tablespoons of flour.
2. Add carefully one cup of boiling water.
3. Season with one-half teaspoon of salt and a dash of pepper and paprika.
