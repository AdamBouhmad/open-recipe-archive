## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of tapioca
- one quart of sweet milk
- three eggs
- one cup of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Soak four tablespoons of tapioca overnight in one quart of sweet milk.
2. In the morning beat the yolks of three eggs with one cup of sugar.
3. Put the milk and tapioca on in a double boiler, adding a pinch of salt.
4. When this comes to boiling point stir in the eggs and sugar.
5. Beat the whites to a stiff froth and stir quickly and delicately into the hot mixture.
6. Flavor with vanilla.
7. Eat cold.
