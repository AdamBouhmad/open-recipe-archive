## Ingredients

- one cup of sweet butter
- two cups of granulated sugar
- three eggs
- grated lemon rind
- four tablespoons of brandy
- three cups of flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- three teaspoons of baking-powder

## Directions

1. In a mixing bowl put a cup of sweet butter and two cups of granulated sugar; beat these ingredients to a cream, then add three eggs, grated lemon rind, and four tablespoons of brandy.
2. Beat the added ingredients thoroughly with the others till the mixture is smooth and creamy.
3. Sift three cups of flour in a big bowl with a teaspoon of salt and three teaspoons of baking-powder; stir this a little at a time in the bowl with the other ingredients, until the mixture is a light dough, just stiff enough to roll out.
4. Dust the pastry board well with flour, put part of the dough on the board, toss it lightly with your hands from side to side till the dough is covered with flour.
5. Dust the rolling-pin well with flour and roll the dough very thin; cut it in shapes with a cookie cutter.
6. Lift each cookie up carefully with a pancake turner, slip them quickly in a big baking-pan, the inside of which has been well rubbed with flour, and bake them in a moderate oven till light brown.
7. Just a moment before taking the pan out of the oven sprinkle the surface of the cookies lightly with granulated sugar.
8. When a little cool take the cookies out of the pan with the pancake turner and lay them on a big platter.
