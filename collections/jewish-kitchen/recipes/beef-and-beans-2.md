## Ingredients

- a piece of brisket of beef
- water
- one quartern of French beans cut small
- two onions cut in quarters
- pepper
- salt
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- one dessert-spoonful of coarse brown sugar
- a large tea-cup full of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a piece of brisket of beef, cover it with water
2. when boiling skim off the fat
3. add one quartern of French beans cut small, two onions cut in quarters
4. season with pepper and salt
5. when nearly done take a dessert-spoonful of flour, one of coarse brown sugar, and a large tea-cup full of vinegar
6. mix them together and stir in with the beans
7. continue stewing for about half an hour longer
