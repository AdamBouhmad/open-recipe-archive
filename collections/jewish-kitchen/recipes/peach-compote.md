## Ingredients

- peaches
- sweetened water
- cloves
- cinnamon bark
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pare the fruit, leave it whole
2. Put on to boil with sweetened water
3. Add a few cloves (remove the heads)
4. Add a stick of cinnamon bark
5. Boil the peaches until tender
6. Take up with a perforated skimmer
7. Lay them in your fruit dish
8. Boil the syrup until thick
9. Pour over the peaches
10. Eat cold with sweet cream
