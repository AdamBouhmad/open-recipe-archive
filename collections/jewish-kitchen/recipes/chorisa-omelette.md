## Ingredients

- eggs
- half a tea-cup full of finely minced chorisa

## Directions

1. Add to the eggs, after they are well beaten as directed in the last receipt, half a tea-cup full of finely minced chorisa
2. this omelette must be lightly fried on both sides, or the salamander held over long enough to dress the chorisa
