## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar

## Directions

1. Measure the fruit and sugar, allowing one pint of sugar to each quart of fruit
2. If the marmalade is made from berries the fruit should be rubbed through a sieve to remove the seeds
3. If large fruit is used have it washed, pared, cored, and quartered
4. Rinse the preserving kettle with cold water that there may be a slight coat of moisture on the sides and bottom
5. Put alternate layers of fruit and sugar in the kettle, having the first layer fruit
6. Heat slowly, stirring frequently
7. While stirring, break up the fruit as much as possible
8. Cook about two hours, then put in small sterilized jars
