## Ingredients

- two and one-half cups of flour
- four teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half cup of walnut meats
- one egg
- one cup of milk

## Directions

1. Mix two and one-half cups of flour, four teaspoons of baking-powder, one-half teaspoon of salt, one-half cup of sugar and one-half cup of walnut meats, broken
2. Add one egg beaten with one cup of milk
3. Let this mixture stand for about twenty minutes in well-greased breadpan before placing in a moderate oven
4. Bake about an hour
5. Better day after it is made
