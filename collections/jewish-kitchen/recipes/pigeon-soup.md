## Ingredients

- beef soup
- one pigeon
- vegetables
- beaten yolk of one egg
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a beef soup
2. An hour before wanted add a pigeon
3. Boil slowly, with all kinds of vegetables, provided your patient is allowed to have them
4. Strain
5. Add the beaten yolk of an egg, salt to taste
