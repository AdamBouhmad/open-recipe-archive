## Ingredients

- pickled tongue
- corned beef
- one egg
- onion
- soaked bread

## Directions

1. Take a pickled tongue and cut it open.
2. Chop or grind some corned beef.
3. Add one egg.
4. Brown a little onion, and add some soaked bread.
5. Fill tongue with it, and sew it up and boil until done.
