## Ingredients

- 3 lb. of raisins well stoned
- 3 lb. of currants well washed and dried
- 1 lb. of good fresh citron cut in slices
- 1 lb. of orange and lemon peel cut in slices
- 1/2 lb. of Valencia almonds blanched and cut in strips
- 2 lb. of moist sugar
- 2 oz. of ground cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg mixed together
- 3 lb. of fresh butter
- 1/2 pint of rum or brandy
- grated rind of 3 fresh lemons
- 3 lb. of flour
- 36 eggs
- salt
- white paper
- thin wood

## Directions

1. Have a very large pan lined with white
2. Put in the fruit and stir raisins and currants well together
3. Put in the peel and stir
4. Add the almond and spice, sugar and stir
5. Add grated lemon peel and stir this well with a large wooden spoon
6. Add a teaspoonful of salt
7. Keep stirring it till you see that it is well mixed
8. Warm the butter and beat it to a cream
9. Add the butter and stir again for a good half hour
10. Beat up the eggs well till they froth, cracking each one separately
11. Mix this all well together
12. Add the brandy before you put in the flour
13. Stir the flour by degrees till it is all mixed
14. The mixing of it will take about 3 hours and it must look like a rich plum pudding
15. Grease a proper tin well
16. Grease some large sheets of writing paper to line the tin with
17. Put about six thick at the bottom to prevent the cake burning
18. Bake it at home if you have a good oven, if not send it to the baker's
19. A cake of this weight will take 4 or 5 hours to bake
20. The oven must be well regulated, not too quick nor yet too slow
21. When it is well done it must be left to soak for nearly an hour in a very moderate oven
22. It must not be turned out of the tin till the next day
23. Must be kept in a very dry place
24. A bride's cake should be made 3 or 4 weeks before it is wanted
25. The almond and sugar icing should only be put on a week before the wedding
26. You should have a round of thin wood cut the size of the bottom of the cake
27. Cover the wood with white paper gummed on and let it dry well
28. Put the cake on that before you put on the icing
29. After the almond icing is dry, you can purchase the ornaments you would like for it from any of the good confectioners
30. After the sugar icing is nearly dry, you put on the ornaments
31. Cut some tissue paper very fine for the frilling round
32. You can buy the silver bands for round the cake where you buy the ornaments
