## Ingredients

- 12 whites of fresh eggs
- 1 lb. of fine sifted sugar
- lemon or vanille for flavouring

## Directions

1. Beat up the whites of eggs to a snow
2. When firm, add in the sifted sugar by degrees, flavoured with any flavouring you like
3. If using vanille, take a small piece of vanille and pound it with a little of the sugar
4. If using lemon, rasp the rind off with 2 or 3 pieces of loaf sugar, pound it and add it to the other
5. When it is beaten up quite firm, have a clean piece of board that will go in the oven, cover it with writing paper
6. dust the paper over lightly with sifted sugar
7. take up a spoonful of the mixture the exact shape of the bowl of the spoon
8. do not let one touch the other
9. Place the board in a very cool oven
10. when just a very light brown take them out, turn them over very lightly
11. press in the liquid part with the back of the spoon to form the hollow
12. place them back in the oven to dry that part
