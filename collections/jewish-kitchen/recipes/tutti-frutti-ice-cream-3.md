## Ingredients

- three pints of cream
- one pound of pulverized sugar
- yolks of nine eggs
- one-half pound of crystallized fruit
- peaches, apricots, cherries, citron
- one wineglass of pale sherry
- juice of an orange or lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare just like the other creams
2. When half frozen add one-half pound of crystallized fruit, peaches, apricots, cherries, citron, etc., chopped very fine
3. Put in also a wineglass of pale sherry and the juice of an orange or lemon
4. Finish freezing
