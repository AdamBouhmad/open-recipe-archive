## Ingredients

- turkey
- bread stuffing
- chestnut stuffing
- salt
- fat
- flour
- boiling water
- stock
- giblets

## Directions

1. Singe and clean the turkey the same as chicken
2. Fill with plain bread stuffing or chestnut stuffing
3. Tie down the legs and rub entire surface with salt and let stand overnight
4. Next morning place in large drippings or roasting-pan on rack
5. Spread breast, legs and wings with one-third cup of fat creamed and mixed with one-fourth cup of flour
6. Dredge bottom of pan with flour
7. Place in a hot oven and when the flour on the turkey begins to brown, reduce the heat
8. Add two cups of boiling water or the stock in which the giblets are cooking
9. Baste with one-fourth cup of fat and three-fourths cup of boiling water
10. When this is all used, baste with the fat in the pan
11. Baste every fifteen minutes until tender; do not prick with a fork, press with the fingers
12. If the breast meat and leg are soft to the touch the turkey is done
13. If the oven is too hot, cover the pan; turn the turkey often, that it may brown nicely
14. Remove strings and skewers and serve on hot platter
15. Serve with giblet sauce and cranberry sauce
