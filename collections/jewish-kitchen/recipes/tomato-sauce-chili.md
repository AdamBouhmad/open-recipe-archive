## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Green peppers
- Red peppers
- Onions
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Ginger
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Allspice

## Directions

1. Skin and cut into pieces forty-five large tomatoes.
2. Cut fine twenty green peppers, twenty red peppers, and six onions.
3. Mix all together with two tablespoons of salt, six small cups of vinegar, and two cups of sugar.
4. Boil two hours.
5. Add one tablespoon each of ginger, cloves, cinnamon and allspice, and boil up once.
6. Bottle and seal at once.
