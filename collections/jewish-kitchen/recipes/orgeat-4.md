## Ingredients

- three ounces of almonds
- a table-spoonful of orange-flour water
- one bitter almond
- one pint of new milk
- one pint of water
- sifted white sugar
- half an ounce of gum-arabic

## Directions

1. Beat three ounces of almonds with a table-spoonful of orange-flour water and one bitter almond
2. Pour one pint of new milk and one pint of water to the paste
3. Sweeten with sifted white sugar
4. Add half an ounce of gum-arabic for those who have a tender chest
