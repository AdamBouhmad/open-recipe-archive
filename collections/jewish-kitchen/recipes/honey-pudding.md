## Ingredients

- one-half cup of honey
- six ounces of bread crumbs
- one-half cup of milk
- one-half teaspoon of ginger
- grated rind of half a lemon
- yolks of two eggs
- two tablespoons of butter
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Mix one-half cup of honey with six ounces of bread crumbs
2. Add one-half cup of milk, one-half teaspoon of ginger, and grated rind of half a lemon
3. Add yolks of two eggs
4. Beat the mixture thoroughly
5. Add two tablespoons of butter and the whites of the eggs well beaten
6. Steam for about two hours in a pudding mold which is not more than three-quarters full
