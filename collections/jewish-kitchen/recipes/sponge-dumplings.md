## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cup of soup stock
- one-fourth teaspoon of salt
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Separate three eggs and beat the yolks
2. Add one cup of soup stock and one-fourth teaspoon of salt
3. Add the beaten whites
4. Pour into a greased cup and place in pan of hot water
5. Steam until firm
6. Cool and remove from cup and cut into small dumplings with a teaspoon
7. Pour the boiling soup over and just before serving add chopped parsley
