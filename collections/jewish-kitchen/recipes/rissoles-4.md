## Ingredients

- cold meat, veal or poultry
- cold smoked beef or sausage
- veal fat
- yolk of egg
- clarified dripping or melted veal fat
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a good forcemeat of cold meat, veal or poultry, well seasoned if of veal or poultry
2. Chop with it a little cold smoked beef or sausage you may have in the house
3. Make a fine puff paste of the veal fat
4. Roll it out very thin
5. You may make them in balls or small triangular shapes, or long narrow ribbons
6. The edges must be well pressed together and brushed over with yolk of egg to prevent their bursting in the frying
7. Fry them in good clarified dripping or melted veal fat
8. Flavour the forced meat with a little lemon juice
