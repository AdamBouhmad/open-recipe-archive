## Ingredients

- one pound meat
- water
- rice
- one beaten egg
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Take one pound of meat, cover with water and boil till meat is tender
2. Boil rice in another pan until it is creamy
3. When ready to serve, add one beaten egg and juice of half a lemon
