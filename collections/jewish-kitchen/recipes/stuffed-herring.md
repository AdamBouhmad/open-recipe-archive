## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of bread crumbs
- one tablespoon of chopped parsley
- two tablespoons of butter
- juice of one-half lemon
- pepper and salt to taste
- hot water

## Directions

1. Make a dressing of two tablespoons of bread crumbs, one tablespoon of chopped parsley, two tablespoons of butter, juice of one-half lemon, and pepper and salt to taste.
2. Add enough hot water to make soft.
3. Fill the herrings, roll up, tie in shape.
4. Cover with greased paper and bake ten to fifteen minutes.
