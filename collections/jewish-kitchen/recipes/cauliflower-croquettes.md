## Ingredients

- cold cooked cauliflower
- one small onion
- one small bunch of parsley
- one-half cup of bread crumbs
- one well-beaten egg
- cracker dust

## Directions

1. Finely chop cold cooked cauliflower
2. Mix in one small, finely chopped onion
3. Mix in one small bunch of parsley finely chopped
4. Mix in one-half cup of bread crumbs and one well-beaten egg
5. Carefully mix and mold into croquette forms
6. Dip in cracker dust
7. Fry in deep, smoking fat until a light brown
