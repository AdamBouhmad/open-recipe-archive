## Ingredients

- crisp Romaine or lettuce
- hard-boiled eggs
- boiled beets
- onion
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of the salad bowl with crisp Romaine or lettuce; arrange over the top alternate slices of hard-boiled eggs and boiled beets. Sprinkle with finely chopped onion, cover with French dressing, toss and serve.
