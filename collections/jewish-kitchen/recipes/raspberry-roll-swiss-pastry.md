## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup of castor sugar
- 1 cup of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- almond oil
- raspberry or strawberry jam or orange marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat 3 eggs thoroughly
2. Add a cup of castor sugar and 1 cup of flour
3. Stir them well together
4. Add 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar and 1 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda dissolved in a very little warm water
5. Grease the tin with almond oil
6. Pour the mixture in
7. Bake quickly
8. Place a sheet of writing paper over the top to prevent its scorching
9. It must be quite yellow when done
10. Slip it out of the tin on a clean cloth, the bottom side up
11. Spread over it raspberry or strawberry jam or orange marmalade
12. Gently roll it up and cut it as wanted
