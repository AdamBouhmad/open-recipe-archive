## Ingredients

- one-half peck of green tomatoes
- three red peppers
- one cup of salt
- five chopped onions
- one pound of brown sugar
- one-quarter ounce of allspice
- whole cloves
- one bunch of celery
- one-half ounce of mustard seed
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take one-half peck of green tomatoes, three red peppers, chopped
2. Put in one cup of salt
3. Let stand overnight, then strain off the water
4. Add five chopped onions, one pound of brown sugar, one-quarter ounce of allspice, and whole cloves put in a bag; one bunch of celery, one-half ounce of mustard seed
5. Cover with vinegar and boil three hours
