## Ingredients

- one cup sugar
- one cup water
- two cups carrots
- two tablespoons hot fat or butter

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of one cup of sugar and one cup of water by boiling ten minutes.
2. To this syrup add two cups of carrots diced, which have previously been browned in two tablespoons hot fat or butter.
3. Cook all together until carrots are tender.
4. Brown in oven and serve.
