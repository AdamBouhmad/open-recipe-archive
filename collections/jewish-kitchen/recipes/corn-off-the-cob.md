## Ingredients

- corn
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. With a sharp knife cut off the grains, not cutting closely enough to remove any of the woody portion of the skins.
2. With a knife press out all the pulp and milk remaining in the cob; add this to the corn.
3. Season well with salt, pepper and butter; add a little more milk if the corn is dry.
4. Cook, preferably in the oven, for about ten minutes, stirring occasionally. If the oven is not hot, cook over the fire.
