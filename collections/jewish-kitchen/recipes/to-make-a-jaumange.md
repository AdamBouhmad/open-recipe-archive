## Ingredients

- blancmange
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 2 oz. of sifted sugar

## Directions

1. When you have the blancmange quite ready, beat the yolks of 6 eggs with 2 oz. of sifted sugar
2. Stir the boiling mixture in, put it in the mould, turn out the same as the blancmange
