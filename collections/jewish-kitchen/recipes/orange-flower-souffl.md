## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of castor sugar
- a spoonful of the best flour
- 10 drops of orange-flower water
- almond oil
- sugar

## Directions

1. Break 6 eggs, the yolks in one dish, the whites in another
2. Add to the yolks 3 tablespoonfuls of castor sugar, a spoonful of the best flour, 10 drops of orange-flower water
3. Beat the yolks and sugar well together
4. Whip the whites to a stiff snow
5. Add the yolks to the whites, beating the snow all the time
6. Have ready a silver or plated dish, or for want of either a round cake tin
7. Grease it with the almond oil and sift some sugar over it
8. Place the mixture in, put it in a hot oven
9. Look that it rises, if so, run a knife round it, sift some more sugar lightly over it
10. Put it in the oven again
11. When well risen serve it quickly with a table napkin round the shape
