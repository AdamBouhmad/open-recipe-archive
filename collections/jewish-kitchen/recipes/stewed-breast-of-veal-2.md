## Ingredients

- 2 or 3-lb. of breast of veal
- soaked bread
- marjoram chopped
- lemon peel
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- an ounce of chopped suet
- one egg
- flour
- 1/2 pint of water
- dried marjoram
- a good-sized onion sliced
- a tablespoonful of flour
- juice of a lemon
- dried saffron
- button mushrooms or Jerusalem artichokes

## Directions

1. Lay breast of veal on the chopping board
2. Make a stuffing of soaked bread, marjoram chopped, lemon peel, parsley, pepper, salt, a little grated nutmeg, an ounce of chopped suet, and mix with one egg
3. Place stuffing between the flesh and bones
4. Sprinkle flour over the breast
5. Put it in a flat stew pan with 1/2 pint of water, salt, pepper, a little dried marjoram, and a good-sized onion sliced
6. Let it stew gently for 2 hours
7. Take out the veal and put it on a dish
8. Strain the liquor
9. Put the meat back into the stewpan and pour over the strained liquor
10. Let it boil up
11. In a basin mix a tablespoonful of flour rubbed smoothly with the juice of a lemon and a little dried saffron
12. Turn this over your stew and baste it backwards and forwards with a spoon
13. Dish it up, optionally with a few button mushrooms or Jerusalem artichokes
