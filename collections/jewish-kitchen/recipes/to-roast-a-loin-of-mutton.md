## Ingredients

- loin of mutton, 4 to 5 lbs.
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- jelly

## Directions

1. Porg the loin before using
2. Season well with pepper and salt
3. Roast for 1.5 hours
4. Serve with a good gravy and jelly
