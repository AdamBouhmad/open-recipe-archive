## Ingredients

- fresh young French beans, 1 lb.
- salt
- peppercorns
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Get 1 lb. when very fresh and young
2. Strip them both sides
3. Put them in bottles whole with salt and peppercorns only
4. Fill up the bottles with cold vinegar
5. Cork them tight
