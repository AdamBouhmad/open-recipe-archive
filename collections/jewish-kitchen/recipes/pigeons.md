## Ingredients

- pigeons
- lard
- forcemeat
- egg balls
- strong gravy

## Directions

1. Lard and stuff the pigeons
2. Glaze them if desired
3. Stew them in a strong gravy
4. When done enough, remove the pigeons and thicken the gravy
5. Add a few forcemeat and egg balls
6. Serve in the dish with the pigeons
7. Alternatively, split them down the back, broil, and then finish in the stew-pan
