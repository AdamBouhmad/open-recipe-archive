## Ingredients

- yolks of six eggs
- one cup of sugar
- juice of three lemons
- rind of two lemons
- one-half of a cocoanut grated
- whites of six eggs
- rich pastry
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of six eggs and one cup of sugar until very light
2. squeeze in the juice of three lemons and the rind of two of them, stir well
3. add one-half of a cocoanut grated
4. lastly add the whites of six eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Line a deep pie-plate with rich pastry, sprinkle a little flour over it
6. pour in the lemon mixture and bake
