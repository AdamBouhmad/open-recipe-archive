## Ingredients

- skin from neck of goose, duck or chicken
- mixture for Kischtke

## Directions

1. Remove skin from neck of goose, duck or chicken in one piece
2. Wash and clean well
3. Stuff with same mixture as for Kischtke
4. Sew at both ends
5. Roast in hot oven until well browned
