## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup cocoa
- salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve two cups of sugar in one cup of water and boil five minutes
2. Mix one cup of cocoa with one cup of water and add to the boiling syrup
3. Boil slowly for ten minutes
4. Add salt
5. Cool and bottle for further use
