## Ingredients

- seven or eight pounds of beef either brisket or a fillet off the shoulder
- water to cover
- sauer kraut

## Directions

1. Boil about seven or eight pounds of beef either brisket or a fillet off the shoulder, in enough water to cover it
2. when it has boiled for one hour, add as much sauer kraut, which is a German preparation, as may be approved
3. it should then stew gently for four hours
4. be served in a deep dish
