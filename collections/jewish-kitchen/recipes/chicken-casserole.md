## Ingredients

- chicken
- potatoes
- carrots
- green peas
- small white onions
- flour
- soup stock or water

## Directions

1. Bake chicken in covered casserole until nearly tender.
2. Add three potatoes cut in dice.
3. Boil small pieces of carrots, green peas, and small white onions — each to be boiled separately.
4. Just before serving, thicken gravy with a teaspoon of flour mixed with a half cup of soup stock or water.
5. Season to taste and place vegetables around the dish.
