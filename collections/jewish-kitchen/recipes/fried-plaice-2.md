## Ingredients

- plaice
- salt
- oil or butter

## Directions

1. Cut the plaice in slices, wash it well, salt it, and let it drain all the water
2. Lay it in your fish cloth for half an hour before you are ready to fry it
3. Prepare it just the same as other fried fish in oil or butter
