## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- seven heaping tablespoons of cocoa
- six level tablespoons of cornstarch
- one tablespoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Place milk and sugar up to boil.
2. When boiling, add cocoa, dissolved to a smooth paste.
3. Add cornstarch dissolved in cold water.
4. Let come to a boil, remove from fire and add vanilla.
5. Place in mold and allow to get cold.
6. Serve with whipped cream.
