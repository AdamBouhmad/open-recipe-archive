## Ingredients

- fine fresh suet, either beef or veal
- a sprig of rosemary, or a little orange flower water

## Directions

1. Melt down with care fine fresh suet, either beef or veal
2. put it into a jar, and set it in a stew-pan of water to boil
3. putting in a sprig of rosemary, or a little orange flower water while melting
4. it should be well beaten till quite light with a wooden fork
