## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- a desert spoonful of flour
- three table spoonfuls of water

## Directions

1. Mix with four ounces of butter, a desert spoonful of flour
2. When well mixed, add three table spoonfuls of water
3. Put it into a clean saucepan kept for the purpose, and stir it carefully one way till it boils
