## Ingredients

- Dried figs
- One-fourth cup of the pulp
- One-fourth cup of finely-chopped crystallized ginger
- One teaspoon of grated orange or lemon rind
- One tablespoon of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut a slit in the side of dried figs
2. Take out some of the pulp with the tip of a teaspoon
3. Mix with one-fourth cup of the pulp, one-fourth cup of finely-chopped crystallized ginger, a teaspoon of grated orange or lemon rind and a tablespoon of lemon juice
4. Fill the figs with the mixture, stuffing them so that they look plump
