## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a chopped onion
- salt
- pepper
- a blade of mace
- a bunch of marjoram
- lemon thyme
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put a knuckle of veal in a stewpan with a chopped onion, salt, pepper, a blade of mace, a bunch of marjoram, lemon thyme, and parsley
2. Let this cook for 3 hours
3. Remove the bunch of herbs when the meat is cooked
4. Make a sauce and throw over it
