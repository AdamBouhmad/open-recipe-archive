## Ingredients

- a dozen unpealed tomatas
- a bit of clarified suet or a little sweet oil
- a small Spanish onion
- flour
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- gravy or water
- rich stock
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a dozen unpealed tomatas, with a bit of clarified suet, or a little sweet oil, and a small Spanish onion
2. Sprinkle with flour, and season with salt and cayenne pepper
3. Boil them in a little gravy or water
4. It must be stirred to prevent burning, then pass it through a sieve
5. Thin it with rich stock to the consistency of winter pea-soup
6. Flavour it with lemon juice, according to taste, after it has been warmed up and ready for serving
