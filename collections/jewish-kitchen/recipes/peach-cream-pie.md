## Ingredients

- Rich crust
- Peaches
- Peach kernels
- One cup of rich cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Line a pie-plate with a rich crust and bake
2. Fill with a layer of sweetened grated peaches which have had a few pounded peach kernels added to them
3. Whip one cup of rich cream, sweeten and flavor and spread over the peaches
4. Set in ice-chest until wanted
