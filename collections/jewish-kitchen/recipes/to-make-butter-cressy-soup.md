## Ingredients

- 4 large carrots
- onion
- 2 oz. of butter
- water
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- fish
- flour
- lemon juice
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil and rub through a sieve 4 large carrots to a pulp
2. Fry an onion in a saucepan with 2 oz. of butter and fry it a light brown
3. Put in pint and 1/2 of water
4. Add the pulp of the carrot with a tooth of garlic chopped very fine, salt and pepper
5. Boil some fish and strain the liquor into the soup
6. If not thick enough stir in a basin a spoonful of flour with the juice of 1/2 a lemon, a little grated nutmeg
