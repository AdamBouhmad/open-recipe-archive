## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- slightly salted cold water
- salt
- pepper
- goose-fat

## Directions

1. Wash and lay your sweetbreads in slightly salted cold water for an hour
2. Pull off carefully all the outer skin, wipe dry and sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Heat some goose-fat in a spider
4. lay in the sweetbreads and fry slowly on the back of the stove, turning frequently until they are a nice brown
