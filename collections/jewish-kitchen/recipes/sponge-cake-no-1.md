## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- one pound of granulated sugar
- grated rind of a lemon
- six ounces of fine matzoth-meal

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs, sugar and lemon rind together until very light, to about the thickness of a custard
2. Add the meal, stirring it in without much beating
3. Bake in a moderately quick oven one-half hour
