## Ingredients

- fat skin from neck of a fat goose
- raw goose meat from breast or legs
- soft or linda fat
- garlic
- onion
- egg
- soaked bread
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- cold water

## Directions

1. Remove the fat skin from the neck of a fat goose, being careful not to put any holes in it
2. Clean carefully and sew up the smaller end
3. Grind fine pieces of raw goose meat, soft or linda fat, garlic, and onion
4. When fine add one egg and a little soaked bread
5. Season with salt, pepper, and ginger
6. Stuff through larger end
7. Sew up larger end
8. Lay in a pudding-pan
9. Pour a little cold water over it
10. Set in stove and baste from time to time
11. Let brown until crisp
12. Eat hot
