## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water or the juice of a lemon well strained
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat up 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar with 2 tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water or the juice of a lemon well strained
2. beat the whites of 2 eggs to a stiff snow
3. whisk it quickly into the sugar and flavouring
4. beat until it is thick and white
5. When the cake is nearly cold put it on smoothly with a brush all over the cake
6. put it in a cool oven to dry, mind that the oven is scarcely warm
