## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. cherries
- 1/2 pint of raspberries
- 1/2 pint of currants
- 1/2 lb. of moist sugar
- puff paste or medium paste

## Directions

1. Pick, wash, and drain your fruit well
2. Put to stew with 1/2 lb. of moist sugar
3. If too much syrup, strain off the syrup and put it in a bottle to drink with cold water
4. Let the fruit get cold
5. Line a dish (if for company with puff paste, if for ordinary occasions with medium paste)
6. Cover over, and follow out directions for baking
