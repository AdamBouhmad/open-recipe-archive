## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds
- two ounces of bitter almonds
- rose water

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of sweet almonds, and two ounces of bitter almonds
2. Pound them in a mortar
3. Make them into a paste with rose water
