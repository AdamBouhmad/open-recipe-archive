## Ingredients

- one and one-third cups flour
- two teaspoons baking-powder
- one-quarter teaspoon salt
- two-thirds cup milk or water
- one egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift one and one-third cups of flour, two teaspoons of baking-powder, one-quarter teaspoon of salt
2. Add two-thirds cup of milk or water gradually
3. Add one egg well beaten
4. For fruit batter add a little sugar, for vegetables pepper and salt
