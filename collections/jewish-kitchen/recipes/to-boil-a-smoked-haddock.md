## Ingredients

- haddock
- water
- butter
- made mustard
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put a haddock in a flat stewpan
2. barely cover it with boiling water
3. let it simmer for 10 minutes
4. dish it up
5. have ready in a teacup a small piece of butter, a teaspoonful of made mustard, and the juice of a lemon
6. let it get hot in the cup
7. pour it over
8. garnish with parsley
