## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- one small onion chopped fine
- one pint of tomatoes strained
- one-half pound mild cheese cut fine
- three eggs
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Place two tablespoons of butter in a pan after having the water boil to heat the pan
2. Let butter melt, add one small onion chopped fine and cook until soft
3. Add a pint of tomatoes strained and let come to a boil
4. Add one-half pound mild cheese cut fine, and stir until smooth
5. Break in three eggs and stir hard until eggs are done
6. Serve on buttered toast
