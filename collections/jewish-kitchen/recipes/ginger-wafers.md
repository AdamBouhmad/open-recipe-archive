## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of molasses
- half a cup of cold coffee
- two teaspoons of soda
- one teaspoon of ginger
- flour

## Directions

1. Take one cup of butter, one cup of sugar, one cup of molasses, half a cup of cold coffee, with two teaspoons of soda, one teaspoon of ginger, and flour enough to make a dough stiff enough to roll out thin
2. Shape with cutter and bake in quick oven
