## Ingredients

- about eight potatoes
- two eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- one wine-glass farina
- one tablespoon chicken fat
- one scant cup flour
- salted boiling water

## Directions

1. Boil about eight potatoes in their jackets and when peeled lay them on a platter overnight
2. When ready to use them next day, grate, add two eggs, salt, a little nutmeg if desired, one wine-glass of farina, a tablespoon of chicken fat, one scant cup of flour gradually
3. If not dry enough add more flour, but be sure not to make the mixture too stiff as this makes the balls heavy
4. Place balls in salted boiling water, cook until light and thoroughly done
5. Serve just as they are or fried in chicken fat until brown
