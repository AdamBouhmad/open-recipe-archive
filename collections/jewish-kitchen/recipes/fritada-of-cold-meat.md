## Ingredients

- cold meat
- salt
- pepper
- six eggs
- onion
- two spoonfuls of oil
- tomato
- parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare some cold meat as before
2. To about 1 lb. of the cold chopped meat, well seasoned, add 6 eggs well beaten
3. Take a deep pie dish, slice an onion thinly, with 2 spoonfuls of oil
4. Slice a tomato, or if not liked, do it without
5. Let it fry in the oven
6. When ready put your mixture, shaping it from the sides to be higher in the centre
7. Let it bake a nice brown
8. When done loosen it all round with a knife, and slip it on to a dish
9. Garnish with parsley, and send it to table with a lemon for use
