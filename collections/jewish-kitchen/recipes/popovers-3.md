## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- two cups of well-sifted flour
- yolks of three fresh eggs
- one teaspoon of salt
- butter
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Mix to a smooth batter two cups each of milk and well-sifted flour, the yolks of three fresh eggs and a teaspoon of salt
2. Butter well the inside of six or eight deep earthen popover cups and stand them in a pan in a hot oven
3. While the cups are heating, beat to a froth the whites of the three eggs
4. Stir the whites quickly into the batter
5. Open the oven door, pull the pan forward
6. Pour the batter in the hot buttered cups up to the brim
7. Push the pan back, close the oven door
8. Bake the popovers till they rise well and are brown at the sides where they part from the cups
9. Serve them hot, folded lightly in a napkin
