## Ingredients

- fish
- water (3 pints)
- parsley roots
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a portion of the fish intended to be dressed, and stew it down with three pints of water, parsley roots, and chopped parsley
2. Pulp them through a sieve
3. Add the rest of the fish, with pepper, salt, and seasoning
4. Serve in a deep dish
