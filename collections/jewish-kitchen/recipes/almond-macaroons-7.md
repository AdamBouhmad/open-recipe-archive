## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- one and one-half pounds of powdered sugar
- whites of five eggs
- grated rind of two lemons
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Grind almonds fine
2. Beat the whites of eggs to a snow
3. Add the sugar and the grated lemon rind and almonds
4. Mix it well together
5. Grease a very thin paper with olive oil, sprinkle some powdered sugar over it, place on a tin
6. Form the cakes and place them a little distance from each other
7. Bake in a very moderate oven
8. When done let them cool before you touch them
