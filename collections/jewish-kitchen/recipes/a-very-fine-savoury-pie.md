## Ingredients

- veal cutlet, cut in pieces and seasoned
- smoked beef fat
- fine cold jelly made from gravy-beef and veal
- hard boiled eggs
- chicken or sweetbread
- lemon-juice
- essence of mushroom
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- blade of mace
- small cakes of fine forcemeat
- fine puff paste
- egg

## Directions

1. Lay a fine veal cutlet, cut in pieces and seasoned, at the bottom of the dish
2. Lay over it a layer of smoked beef fat
3. Then a layer of fine cold jelly made from gravy-beef and veal
4. Then hard boiled eggs in slices
5. Then chicken or sweetbread, and then again the jelly, and so on till the dish is filled
6. Put no water, and season highly with lemon-juice, essence of mushroom, pepper, salt, and nutmeg; also, if approved, a blade of mace
7. Small cakes of fine forcemeat are an improvement
8. Cover with a fine puff paste, and brush over with egg, and bake
