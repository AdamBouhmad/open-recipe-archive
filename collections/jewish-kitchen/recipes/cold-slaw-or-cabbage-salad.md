## Ingredients

- small, compact cabbage
- hot dressing

## Directions

1. Select a small, compact cabbage and strip off the outside leaves
2. Cut the head in quarters
3. With a sharp knife slice very thin
4. Soak in cold water until crisp
5. Drain and dry between clean towels
6. Mix with hot dressing and serve when cold
