## Ingredients

- one cup of currant juice
- a pinch of salt
- one-quarter cup of cornstarch
- cold water
- cream

## Directions

1. Take one cup of currant juice, sufficiently sweetened, and a pinch of salt.
2. Let this boil and add to it enough cornstarch to render it moderately thick and then boil again for ten minutes.
3. Dissolve about one-quarter cup of cornstarch in cold water.
4. It should be eaten cold with cream.
