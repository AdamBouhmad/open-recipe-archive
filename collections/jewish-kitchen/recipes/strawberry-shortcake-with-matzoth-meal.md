## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- three-quarters of a cup sugar
- rind of one-half lemon
- a pinch of salt
- one-half cup of sifted matzoth meal
- whites of the eggs
- strawberries
- cream

## Directions

1. Beat until very light the yolks of four eggs and three-quarters of a cup sugar
2. Add rind of one-half lemon, a pinch of salt, one-half cup of sifted matzoth meal, and last the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
3. Bake in two shallow square pans in a moderate oven
4. When cold lay a cake on a platter, spread thickly with strawberries that have been well sugared
5. Put the other cake on top
6. Spread over the top and sides with cream that has been sweetened, flavored and whipped very stiff
