## Ingredients

- one-half pound of prunes
- cold water
- one-half cup of sugar
- three eggs
- one-half teaspoon of lemon juice
- thin custard or cream

## Directions

1. Soak one-half pound of prunes in cold water overnight.
2. In the morning let them simmer in this water until they are very soft.
3. Remove stones and rub through strainer.
4. Add one-half cup of sugar and cook five minutes or until the consistency of marmalade.
5. When the fruit mixture is cold, add the well-beaten whites of three eggs and one-half teaspoon of lemon juice; add this gradually.
6. Heap lightly in buttered dish and bake twenty minutes in a slow oven.
7. Serve cold with thin custard or cream.
