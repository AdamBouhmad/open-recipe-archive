## Ingredients

- cold veal
- fat of smoked beef
- an onion
- parsley chopped very fine
- a little of the rind of lemon
- grated nutmeg
- forced-meat balls
- a cupful of gravy

## Directions

1. Cut cold veal in nice pieces
2. Put it in a stewpan with some of the fat of smoked beef, an onion, and parsley chopped very fine
3. Add a little of the rind of lemon and grated nutmeg
4. Make a few forced-meat balls
5. Let it all simmer for 1/2 an hour with a cupful of gravy
