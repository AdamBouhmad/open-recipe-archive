## Ingredients

- cold fish
- chopped parsley
- marjoram
- 3 or 4 eggs
- 1 onion
- 2 or 3 tomatoes
- 2 spoonfuls oil
- 1 spoonful vinegar

## Directions

1. Take any cold fish you may have left, mince the same as for the patties.
2. Put some chopped parsley and marjoram, beat with 3 or 4 eggs.
3. Slice in a pie dish an onion, and, if you have them, 2 or 3 tomatoes with 2 spoonfuls of oil.
4. When it is fried, put in a spoonful of vinegar, stir it together.
5. Then add the fish and let it bake a nice brown.
6. Slip it out of the dish on to a hot dish.
7. Garnish it with parsley.
