## Ingredients

- tripe
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- 1 onion, sliced
- fat
- nutmeg, grated
- 1/2 pint water
- parsley, chopped
- capers
- 1 spoonful flour
- dried saffron
- 2 spoonfuls vinegar
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the tripe well
2. Boil it in a saucepan in thin flour and water to keep its colour, with salt and pepper
3. When done, drain it out of the water
4. Slice an onion in a stew pan with a little fat, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
5. Add 1/2 pint of the water in which the tripe was boiled
6. Let it cook for half an hour
7. In a basin, combine chopped parsley, a few capers, a spoonful of flour, a little dried saffron, and 2 spoonfuls of vinegar
8. Mix it all well together
9. Dish it up and serve with mashed potatoes
