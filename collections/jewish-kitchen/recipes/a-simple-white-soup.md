## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a piece of chorissa
- a carrot
- two onions
- three or four turnips
- a blade of mace
- two or three quarts of water or weak broth
- salt
- a sprig of parsley
- whole white pepper
- pounded vermicelli

## Directions

1. Break a knuckle of veal and place it in a stewpan
2. Also place a piece of chorissa, a carrot, two onions, three or four turnips, and a blade of mace
3. Pour over two or three quarts of water or weak broth
4. Season with salt, a sprig of parsley, and whole white pepper
5. When sufficiently boiled, skim and strain it
6. Thicken with pounded vermicelli
