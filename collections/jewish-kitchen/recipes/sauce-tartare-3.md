## Ingredients

- one-half pint well-made mayonnaise dressing
- two olives
- one gherkin
- one small onion
- parsley
- capers

## Directions

1. Add to a half pint of well-made mayonnaise dressing two olives, one gherkin and one small onion, chopped fine.
2. Chop sufficient parsley to make a tablespoonful, crush it in a bowl and add it first to the mayonnaise.
3. Stir in at least a tablespoon of drained capers and serve with fried or broiled fish.
