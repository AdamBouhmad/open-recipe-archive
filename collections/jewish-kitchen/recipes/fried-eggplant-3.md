## Ingredients

- eggplant
- salt
- flour
- butter
- egg and cracker (optional, for dipping)
- fat for deep frying (optional)

## Directions

1. Pare eggplant, cut in very thin slices
2. Sprinkle with salt, pile slices on a plate
3. Cover with a weight to draw out juice
4. Let stand one hour
5. Dredge with flour and fry slowly in a little butter until crisp and brown, or dip in egg and cracker and fry in deep fat
