## Ingredients

- six tablespoons melted butter
- one and one-half teaspoons anchovy paste
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix six tablespoons of melted butter and one and one-half teaspoons anchovy paste, place in double boiler and allow to boil for about six minutes.
2. Flavor with lemon juice.
