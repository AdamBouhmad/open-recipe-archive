## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of savoy biscuits
- a pint of milk
- 6 oz. of fresh butter
- the yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 oz. of candied orange and lemon peel
- 4 oz. of castor sugar
- the 4 whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Soak 1/2 lb. of savoy biscuits in a pint of milk
2. Beat them up, add 6 oz. of fresh butter, the yolks of 4 eggs, 2 oz. of candied orange and lemon peel, 4 oz. of castor sugar
3. Mix all well up together
4. Butter well a deep dish
5. Bake in a quick oven
6. Beat the 4 whites to a stiff snow
7. Just before the tart is done spread over the snow
8. Return it to the oven for it to colour a light brown
