## Ingredients

- 4 lb. boned brisket
- 3 pints of water
- salt, pepper
- a tooth of garlic
- a blade of mace
- a few vegetables in season: carrot, turnip, cabbage, peas, beans, celery, potatoes, leeks
- a bunch of marjoram, thyme, parsley
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Take 4-lb. boned brisket
2. Put it in a soup saucepan with 3 pints of water, with salt, pepper, a tooth of garlic, a blade of mace
3. Cut up a few of the vegetables in season: carrot, turnip, cabbage, peas, beans, celery, potatoes, leeks
4. Boil in a bunch of marjoram, thyme, parsley
5. Let it all boil quick
6. Skim it well, then let it boil slowly for 3 hours
7. Do not cut your vegetables too small
8. Make a few suet balls according to recipe and let them boil only 1 hour
9. Just before serving up for dinner rub a tablespoonful of flour with a little browning
10. Stir into the boiling soup
11. Turn it into the tureen
