## Ingredients

- 2 or 3-lb. of breast of veal
- soaked bread
- marjoram
- lemon peel
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- ounce of chopped suet
- egg
- flour
- 1/2 pint of water

## Directions

1. Take 2 or 3-lb. of breast of veal and lay it on the chopping board
2. Have ready a stuffing made of soaked bread, marjoram chopped, lemon peel, parsley, pepper, salt, a little grated nutmeg, and an ounce of chopped suet
3. Mix this with one egg
4. Place it between the flesh and bones
5. Sprinkle flour over the breast
6. Put it in a flat stew pan with 1/2 pint of water, salt
