## Ingredients

- very ripe black cherries
- two pounds of loaf sugar
- a quart of brandy
- a few cloves

## Directions

1. Put into a large wide mouthed bottle very ripe black cherries
2. Add to them two pounds of loaf sugar, a quart of brandy, and a few cloves
3. Bruise a few more cherries, and simmer with sugar
4. Strain and add the juice to the cherries in the bottle
5. Cork closely
6. Keep in a warm dry place
