## Ingredients

- plaice
- onions
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- dried marjoram
- flour
- dried saffron
- water
- small potatoes
- lemon
- parsley
- tomatoes
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Slice in your fish tin two or three onions with two spoonfuls of oil, with salt and pepper, put it in the oven to get hot
2. Wash and dry well a thick plaice notched as above, lay it on the tin, season it well, and sprinkle over a little dried marjoram
3. Dredge it with flour
4. Mix in a cup a little dried saffron with two spoonfuls of water, baste it over the plaice
5. Boil some small potatoes, and when done put them round the tin with the plaice to get brown
6. When you dish it up, squeeze the juice of a lemon over, and a little chopped parsley
7. Dish it up with the potatoes round, and garnish with parsley
