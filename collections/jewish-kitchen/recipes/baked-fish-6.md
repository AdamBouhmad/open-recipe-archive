## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- bread stuffing
- hot water
- butter
- lemon
- parsley
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and dry the fish, rubbing inside and outside with salt
2. Stuff with a bread stuffing and sew
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and place in a hot oven without water
4. As soon as it begins to brown add hot water and butter
5. Baste every ten minutes
6. Bake until done, allowing an hour or more for a large fish, twenty or thirty minutes for a small one
7. Remove to a hot platter and draw out the strings
8. Garnish with slices of lemon well covered with chopped parsley and serve with Hollandaise sauce
