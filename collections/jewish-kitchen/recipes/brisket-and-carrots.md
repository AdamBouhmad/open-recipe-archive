## Ingredients

- Four pounds of brisket
- a pint of water
- salt and pepper
- mace
- 3 or 4 good size carrots
- a tooth of garlic
- flour
- a little browning
- a spoonful of vinegar
- a little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put brisket on with a pint of water, salt, pepper, mace.
2. Cut up in strips of 2 inches long and one thick, 3 or 4 good size carrots.
3. Add a tooth of garlic chopped small.
4. Let them cook for three hours beef and carrots together.
5. When done thicken with flour and a little browning just to turn the colour.
6. Rub the flour with a spoonful of vinegar and a little chopped parsley.
7. Dish up with the meat in the centre and the carrots all round.
