## Ingredients

- mackerel
- salt
- fennel

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the mackerel well
2. Put them on in cold water with salt
3. Let them boil very gently, as it makes the flesh hard
4. Dish them on a fish napkin
5. Garnish with fennel
