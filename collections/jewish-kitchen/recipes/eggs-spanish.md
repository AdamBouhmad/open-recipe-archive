## Ingredients

- eggs
- fresh or canned tomatoes
- minced green onions
- garlic
- parsley
- laurel leaf
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard
2. After cooling, remove shells and halve lengthwise
3. Cook for thirty minutes fresh or canned tomatoes with minced green onions, garlic, parsley, a laurel leaf, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper to taste
4. Strain
5. Melt a slice of butter
6. Add a little flour
7. Add sauce gradually
8. Cook ten minutes
9. Place eggs carefully in sauce and serve
