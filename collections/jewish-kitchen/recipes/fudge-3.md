## Ingredients

- Two cups of granulated sugar
- One-eighth teaspoon of salt
- One cup of milk or cream
- One-half a cake of Baker's chocolate
- Two tablespoons of butter
- One teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil together two cups of granulated sugar, one-eighth teaspoon of salt and one cup of milk or cream, until when tried in cold water, it will form a soft ball (about eight minutes)
2. Add one-half a cake of Baker's chocolate, two tablespoons of butter and one teaspoon of vanilla
3. Beat until smooth and creamy
4. Pour into greased pans
5. Cool and cut in squares
