## Ingredients

- 8 oz. of chopped suet
- 8 oz. of bread crumbs
- 8 oz. of currants
- 6 oz. of apple
- 8 eggs
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well, the yolks and whites separately
2. Mix all ingredients well together
3. Beat for 20 minutes
4. Grease a pretty shape well and sift over some sugar
5. Boil for 2 hours
6. Serve with a rich rum sauce
