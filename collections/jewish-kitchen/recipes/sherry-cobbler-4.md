## Ingredients

- pineapple sliced in slices and quarters
- a few oranges sliced thin
- a lemon sliced thin
- one cup of powdered sugar
- one tumbler of sherry wine
- a few berries such as black and red raspberries and blackberries
- pounded ice
- water

## Directions

1. Cut pineapple in slices and quarters, a few oranges and a lemon, sliced thin.
2. Cover the fruit with the sugar, laid in layers at the bottom of your bowl with pounded ice.
3. Add the wine and twice as much water as wine.
4. Stir all up well before serving.
5. It is best to mix this in a large bowl and fill in glasses just before serving, and put a little of each kind of fruit in each goblet with pounded ice.
