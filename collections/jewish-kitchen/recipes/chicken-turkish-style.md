## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- salt
- chestnuts

## Directions

1. Brown a chicken, cover with water and season, cook until tender.
2. When chicken is tender; slash the skin of chestnuts, put them in oven and roast, then skin them, put in chicken and let come to a boil and serve with the chicken.
