## Ingredients

- desert spoonful of the best wheaten flour
- half a pint of fresh asses milk
- table-spoonful of the best honey
- tea-spoonful of rose water

## Directions

1. Boil a desert spoonful of the best wheaten flour with half a pint of fresh asses milk
2. When boiling, stir in a table-spoonful of the best honey, and a tea-spoonful of rose water
3. Mix smoothly, place in small pots, and use a little of it after washing
