## Ingredients

- half a pint of cream
- half a pint of new milk
- four ounces of rice-flour or potatoe-flour
- two to three ounces of fresh butter
- white sifted sugar
- grated lemon peel
- yolks of six to eight eggs
- whites of six to eight eggs

## Directions

1. Warm the cream and milk together in a clean saucepan
2. Make a smooth batter with rice-flour or potatoe-flour
3. Stir the batter into the milk
4. Let it simmer, stirring all the time till it thickens
5. Add fresh butter and white sifted sugar enough to sweeten
6. Add grated lemon peel
7. Take it off the fire and stir quickly the well-beaten yolks of six to eight eggs
8. Butter the pan and pour the mixture into it
9. When on the point of being placed into the oven, add the whites of the eggs thoroughly whisked
10. Fill the pan only half full, as it will rise very high
11. Bake in the oven
12. Serve immediately it is taken from the oven, holding a salamander over it to prevent its falling
