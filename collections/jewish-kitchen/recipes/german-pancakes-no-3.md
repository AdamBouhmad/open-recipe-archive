## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one-half cup of milk
- three-quarters cup of sifted flour
- one-eighth teaspoon of baking-powder
- salt
- two tablespoons of rendered butter
- powdered sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until very light
2. Add milk and stir in flour, baking-powder, and salt
3. Just before frying, add stiffly-beaten whites of eggs and mix well together
4. Put on fire an iron skillet with a close-fitting top
5. Heat in rendered butter
6. When very hot, pour in enough batter to cover the bottom of the skillet
7. Cover at once with the top
8. When the pancake is brown on one side, remove the top and let it brown on the other side
9. Take it up with a perforated skimmer and lay on a plate
10. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and some lemon juice
11. Serve at once
