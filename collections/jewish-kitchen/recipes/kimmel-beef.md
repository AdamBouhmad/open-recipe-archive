## Ingredients

- round of beef
- water
- 2 ounces carraway seeds
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- allspice
- brown paper

## Directions

1. Place a piece of round of beef in a deep baking pan with a pint of water
2. Tie 2 ounces of carraway seeds in a piece of muslin and add to the pan
3. Add salt, pepper, and a few cloves and allspice
4. Cover the pan down tight with the lid and tie brown paper over it
5. Put it in a moderate oven for 3 or 4 hours
