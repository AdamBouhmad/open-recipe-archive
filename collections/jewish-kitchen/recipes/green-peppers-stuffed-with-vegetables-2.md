## Ingredients

- large white onions
- one-half cup of uncooked rice
- salt
- citric acid (size of a bean dissolved in a little water)
- tomatoes
- red cabbage

## Directions

1. Brown large white onions, add one-half cup of uncooked rice, a little salt, piece of citric acid (size of a bean dissolved in a little water)
2. Fill peppers, stew with tomatoes like Arday-influs
3. Or fill peppers with red cabbage which has been prepared
