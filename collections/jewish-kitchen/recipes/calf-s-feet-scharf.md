## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- salt
- pepper
- clove or two of garlic

## Directions

1. Saw calf's feet into joints
2. Put on to boil within cold water and boil slowly until the gristle loosens from the bones
3. Season with salt, pepper, and a clove or two of garlic
4. Serve hot or cold to taste
