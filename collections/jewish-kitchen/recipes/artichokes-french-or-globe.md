## Ingredients

- French artichokes
- boiling water
- French salad dressing
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Remove all the hard outer leaves.
2. Cut off the stem close to the leaves.
3. Cut off the top of the bud.
4. Drop the artichokes into boiling water and cook until tender, which will take from thirty to fifty minutes.
5. Take up and remove the choke.
6. Serve a dish of French salad dressing with the artichokes, which may be eaten either hot or cold.
7. Melted butter also makes a delicious sauce for the artichokes if they are eaten hot.
