## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs, very fresh
- a tablespoonful of oil per yolk
- salt
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of white vinegar
- a few capers or chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of 2 eggs in your sauceboat
2. To each yolk put a tablespoonful of oil
3. Using a wooden spoon, rub the yolks and oil till it comes quite thick
4. Put in a little salt and pepper
5. Add a tablespoonful of white vinegar, the best that can be got
6. Rub all together
7. You can add a few capers or chopped parsley
