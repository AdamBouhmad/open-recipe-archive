## Ingredients

- carp
- strong rich gravy
- onion
- parsley roots
- allspice
- nutmegs
- beaten cloves
- ginger
- port wine
- vinegar
- coarse brown sugar
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean the fish thoroughly
2. Put it into a saucepan, with a strong rich gravy, season with onion, parsley roots, allspice, nutmegs, beaten cloves, and ginger
3. Let it stew very gently till nearly done
4. Mix port wine and vinegar in equal quantities, coarse brown sugar and lemon juice, a little flour, with some of the gravy from the saucepan, mix well
5. Pour over the fish, let it boil till the gravy thickens
