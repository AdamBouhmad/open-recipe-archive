## Ingredients

- one-fourth cup of butter
- one-fourth cup of sugar
- one cup of flour
- one egg
- half a pint of milk
- jam

## Directions

1. Beat one-fourth cup of butter to a cream with one-fourth cup of sugar.
2. Add one cup of flour.
3. Stir well.
4. Add one egg which has been beaten into half a pint of milk, a little at a time.
5. Fill buttered saucers with the mixture.
6. Bake.
7. When done, place the cakes one on top of another with jam spread between.
