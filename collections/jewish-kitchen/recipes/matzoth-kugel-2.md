## Ingredients

- three matzoth
- two tablespoons of fat
- one-quarter pound of matzoth meal
- yolks of five eggs
- two ounces of pounded almonds
- grated peel of one-half lemon
- one large sour apple
- a pinch of salt
- whites of eggs
- about a quarter pound of hot fat

## Directions

1. Soak three matzoth
2. Heat two tablespoons of fat in a spider
3. Press all the water out of the matzoth with your hands and dry them in the spider of heated fat
4. Add about one-quarter pound of matzoth meal
5. Stir the matzoth and matzoth meal well with a large spoon
6. Add by degrees the yolks of five eggs and two ounces of pounded almonds, and the grated peel of one-half lemon
7. Add one large sour apple, grated, a pinch of salt, and last the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
8. Line a kugeltopf well with fat, and pour about a quarter pound of hot fat over the kugel
9. Bake immediately
10. Serve with wine sauce
