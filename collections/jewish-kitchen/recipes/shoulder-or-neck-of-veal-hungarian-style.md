## Ingredients

- four onions
- tablespoon of fat
- one teaspoon mixed paprika
- veal
- one sweet green pepper
- water
- dumplings

## Directions

1. Brown four onions light brown in a tablespoon of fat
2. Add one teaspoon mixed paprika and the meat cut in pieces
3. Leave the pan uncovered for a few moments, cover
4. Add one sweet green pepper, cut up, and let cook
5. Add a little water whenever the gravy boils down
6. When the meat is tender serve with dumplings
