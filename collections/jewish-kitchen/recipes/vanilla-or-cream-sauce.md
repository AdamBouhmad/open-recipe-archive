## Ingredients

- one teaspoon cornstarch
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one cup of scalding milk
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- white of one egg

## Directions

1. Mix one teaspoon cornstarch and one tablespoon of sugar thoroughly
2. on them slowly pour one cup of scalding milk, stirring all the time
3. Cook and stir in a double boiler for ten minutes
4. set aside to cool
5. When ready to use stir in one teaspoon of vanilla and the white of one egg, stiffly beaten
6. Serve in place of whipped cream
