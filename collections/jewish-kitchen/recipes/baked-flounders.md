## Ingredients

- flounders
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- one onion
- celery
- bread crumbs
- one cup of hot water

## Directions

1. Clean, wipe dry, add salt and pepper and lay them in a pan.
2. Put flakes of butter on top, an onion cut up, some minced celery and a few bread crumbs.
3. Put a cup of hot water into the pan to prevent burning.
4. Baste often; bake until brown.
