## Ingredients

- white of one unbeaten egg
- one and one-fourth cups of pulverized sugar
- three drops of rose-water
- ten drops of vanilla
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. To the white of an unbeaten egg add one and one-fourth cups of pulverized sugar and stir until smooth
2. Add three drops of rose-water, ten of vanilla, and the juice of half a lemon
