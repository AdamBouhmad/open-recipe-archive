## Ingredients

- 7 pounds husk tomatoes
- 4 pounds sugar
- mace
- cinnamon
- cloves
- vinegar (not more than 1 pint per 7 pounds tomatoes)

## Directions

1. Prick each one with a needle, weigh
2. Put into the kettle with alternate layers of sugar
3. Heat slowly to a boil; skim and add vinegar
4. Add spices and boil for about ten minutes, not longer
5. Take them out with a perforated skimmer and spread upon dishes to cool
6. Boil the syrup thick, and pack as you would other fruit
