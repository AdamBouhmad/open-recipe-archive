## Ingredients

- large cucumber
- vinegar
- mustard-seed
- garlic
- spices
- highly-seasoned forcemeat
- rich gravy

## Directions

1. Cut a large cucumber in half, length ways, scoop out the seedy part, and lay it in vinegar that has been boiled with mustard-seed, a little garlic, and spices, for twenty-four hours
2. Fill the cucumber with highly-seasoned forcemeat
3. Stew it in a rich gravy, the cucumber must be tied to keep it together
