## Ingredients

- two cups of haricot beans
- two pints of water
- one onion
- fat
- one pound of beef
- one carrot
- turnip
- salt
- pepper
- pea meal or flour
- vinegar
- potatoes
- green vegetables

## Directions

1. Wash two cups of haricot beans and leave them covered with two pints of water overnight
2. Next day brown one coarsely chopped onion in a little fat and put it with the beans and their water into a casserole or stew-jar
3. Cook closely covered and rather slowly in the oven or by the side of the fire one hour
4. then put in a pound of beef in fairly large pieces
5. An hour later add one carrot cut into dice, half as many dice of turnip, and salt and pepper to taste
6. Continue the slow cooking until these vegetables are tender
7. a few minutes before serving thicken the stew with pea meal or flour previously baked to a fawn color
8. Flavor with vinegar
9. serve with an abundance of potatoes and green vegetables
