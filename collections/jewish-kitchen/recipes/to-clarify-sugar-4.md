## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- rind of a fresh lemon
- 1/2 pint of water

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar with some of the rind of a fresh lemon in a saucepan
2. Add 1/2 pint of water
3. Boil to a syrup
4. Serve in a sauce-boat
