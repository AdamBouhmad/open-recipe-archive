## Ingredients

- 1 ounce of the best green tea
- a cup of boiling water
- 1 pint of cream
- sugar to sweeten

## Directions

1. Steep an ounce of the best green tea in a cup of boiling water
2. Let it stand for 1/2 an hour
3. Strain it into a pint of cream
4. Sweeten it to flavour
5. Put it in the freezer and work it
