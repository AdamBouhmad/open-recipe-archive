## Ingredients

- rich crust
- apples
- four eggs
- two cups of milk

## Directions

1. Line your pie-plates with a rich crust
2. Slice apples thin, half fill your plates
3. Pour over them a custard made of four eggs and two cups of milk, sweetened and seasoned to taste
