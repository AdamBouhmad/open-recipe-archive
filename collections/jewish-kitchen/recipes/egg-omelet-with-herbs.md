## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- marjoram
- oil or butter

## Directions

1. Beat 4 or 5 eggs with a little salt and pepper, a little grated nutmeg, some chopped parsley and marjoram, a very little of each
2. Put a little oil or butter in the pan to grease it
3. Fry it like a pancake
4. Turn it on a hot dish
