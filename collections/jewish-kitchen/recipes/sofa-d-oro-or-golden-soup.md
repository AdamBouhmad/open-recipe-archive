## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- a quarter of a pint of water
- a quarter of a pint of orange flower-water
- a thin slice of toasted bread
- an ounce of sweet almonds
- the beaten yolks of four eggs
- pounded cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clarify a pound of sugar in a quarter of a pint of water and the same quantity of orange flower-water
2. Cut into pieces the size of dice a thin slice of toasted bread, or cut it into shapes with a paste cutter
3. Throw it, while hot, into the sugar with an ounce of sweet almonds pounded very finely
4. Take the beaten yolks of four eggs and pour over the sugar and bread
5. Stir gently and let it simmer a few minutes
6. Serve in a deep glass dish, sprinkled over with pounded cinnamon
