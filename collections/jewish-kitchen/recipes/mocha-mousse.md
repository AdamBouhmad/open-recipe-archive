## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- one-half cup of granulated sugar
- one-half pint of cream
- one-half cup of grated macaroons
- two tablespoons of mocha essence
- one teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream yolks of three eggs with one-half cup of granulated sugar
2. Add one-half pint of cream, whipped
3. Add one-half cup of grated macaroons
4. Add two tablespoons of mocha essence and one teaspoon of vanilla
5. Add lastly beaten whites
6. Put in a mold and pack in salt and ice for three hours
