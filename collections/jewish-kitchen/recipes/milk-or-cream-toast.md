## Ingredients

- stale light bread
- milk or cream
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Toast as many slices of stale light bread as desired a light brown
2. Heat milk or cream, allowing one-half cup for each slice
3. add small lump of butter
4. When just at the boiling point, pour over bread which has been placed in dish
5. sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. cover, and serve immediately
