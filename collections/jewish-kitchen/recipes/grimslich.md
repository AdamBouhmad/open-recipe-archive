## Ingredients

- half a loaf of bread, soaked and pressed
- two eggs
- one-half cup sugar
- one-fourth cup raisins
- one tablespoon cinnamon
- one-fourth cup almonds pounded fine

## Directions

1. Beat whites to a froth and add last
2. Drop by tablespoonful and fry
3. Serve with stewed fruit
