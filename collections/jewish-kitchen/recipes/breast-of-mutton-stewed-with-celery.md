## Ingredients

- nice breast of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- half pint of water
- large head of celery
- garlic
- flour
- juice of a lemon
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Take a nice breast of mutton, not too fat, put it in a stewpan with salt, pepper, nutmeg, half pint of water
2. Cut and wash well a large head of celery in good size pieces
3. Put it in with the mutton and a tooth of garlic chopped fine
4. Let it simmer nicely till tender
5. Skim off all the fat
6. Rub in a basin a spoonful of flour with the juice of a lemon and a little dried saffron
7. Put it into the gravy and stir it well
