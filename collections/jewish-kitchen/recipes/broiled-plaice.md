## Ingredients

- plaice
- salt
- sauce or plain butter
- lemon or vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a fine thick plaice, wash, scrape the skin well, and lay it in salt for half an hour
2. Drain it well, dry it in a cloth
3. Notch it in slices like for frying, but do not cut it through
4. Broil it like the soles, season it well
5. Serve it with sauce or plain butter, and lemon or vinegar
