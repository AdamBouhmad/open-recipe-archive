## Ingredients

- blackberries
- currants
- sugar
- flour
- pastry

## Directions

1. Mix as much fruit in a bowl as required, sweeten, stirring the sugar through the berries and currants lightly with a spoon
2. Dust in a little flour and stir it through the fruit
3. Cut one of the pieces of pastry in halves, dust the pastry-board with flour and roll the lump of pastry out very thin
4. cover the pie-plate, a big deep one, with the pastry, trim off the edges with a knife, cutting from you
5. Fill the dish with the fruit, dust the surface well with flour
6. Roll out the other piece for the top crust, fold it over the rolling pin, cut a few gashes in it for a steam vent
7. Carefully put on the top crust, trim it well about the edge of the pie-plate
8. Press it closely together with the end of your thumb or with a pastry knife
9. stand the pie in a moderate oven and bake till the surface is a delicate brown
10. Remove the pie and let it stand until it is cool
