## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- steak
- dripping
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Take some nice mushrooms and peel them
2. Wash them well and let them drain in a colander
3. Put on your meat frying-pan a little dripping and let it boil
4. Put in your steak and sprinkle over it salt and pepper
5. When nearly done put in the mushrooms to fry
6. Have a hot dish ready and put your steak in
7. Place the mushrooms round
8. Strain the fat from the frying-pan
9. Put in a little salt, pepper, and the juice of a lemon, and a little boiling water
10. Set it over the fire and when it boils turn it over the steak
