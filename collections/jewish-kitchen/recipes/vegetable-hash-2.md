## Ingredients

- one or many vegetables
- meat and fish (optional)
- potato
- salt and pepper
- parsley, onion, chives or green pepper (if desired)
- stock, milk or water (one-quarter cup to a pint of hash)
- one-half tablespoon of butter or savory drippings

## Directions

1. Chop the vegetables fine
2. Season well with salt and pepper, and parsley, onion, chives or green pepper if desired
3. Moisten with stock, milk or water, using a quarter of a cup to a pint of hash
4. Melt one-half tablespoon of butter or savory drippings in a pan
5. Put in the hash, spreading it evenly and dropping small pieces of butter or drippings over the top
6. Cover the pan
7. Let the hash cook over a moderate fire for half an hour
8. Fold over like an omelet and serve
