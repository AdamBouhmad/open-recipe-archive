## Ingredients

- a fine fat thick breast of veal
- pickle
- woodsmoke

## Directions

1. Take a fine fat thick breast of veal, bone it
2. lay it in pickle, according to the receipt to salt meat
3. hang it for three or four weeks in woodsmoke
4. it will prove a very fine savoury relish, either boiled and eaten cold, or fried as required
