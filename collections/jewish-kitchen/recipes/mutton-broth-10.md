## Ingredients

- three pounds of neck of lamb or lean shoulder
- three quarts of water
- two tablespoons well-washed rice
- flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut three pounds of neck of lamb or lean shoulder into small pieces
2. Cover closely and boil with three quarts of water, slowly, for two hours
3. Add two tablespoons well-washed rice to the boiling soup
4. Cook an hour longer, slowly
5. Watch carefully and stir from time to time
6. Strain and thicken it with a little flour
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
