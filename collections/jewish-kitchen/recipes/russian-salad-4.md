## Ingredients

- pickled cucumbers
- mustard pickles
- pickled beans
- capers
- six herrings
- one-half pound of smoked salmon
- six large apples
- one onion
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce leaves
- hard-boiled eggs
- nuts

## Directions

1. Cut up all kinds of pickled cucumbers, small and large, sweet and sour, also mustard pickles, into very small lengths, also pickled beans and capers.
2. Add six herrings, which you have soaked in water for twenty-four hours; skin and take out every bone, cut up as you did the pickles.
3. Add half a pound of smoked salmon, also cut into lengths, six large apples chopped very fine, and one onion grated.
4. Mix all thoroughly and pour a rich mayonnaise dressing over all.
5. Next day line a salad bowl with lettuce leaves, fill in the salad and garnish with hard-boiled eggs, nuts, and capers.
