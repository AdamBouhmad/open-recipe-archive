## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1.5 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking-powder
- 0.5 cup milk
- chopped almonds
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cream one-half cup of butter with one cup of sugar
2. Add three eggs, one and one-half cups of flour, two teaspoons of baking-powder, mixed with the flour, and one-half cup of milk
3. Mix well together
4. Bake in a long bread or cake pan, and have on top chopped almonds, sugar and cinnamon
