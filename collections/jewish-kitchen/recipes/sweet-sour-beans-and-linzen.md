## Ingredients

- beans
- salt
- pepper
- drippings
- flour
- soup stock
- vinegar
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Soak overnight and drain the beans
2. boil in salted water until tender
3. drain and prepare by adding salt and pepper to taste
4. thicken with one tablespoon of drippings in which has been browned one tablespoon of flour and some soup stock
5. If the beans are to be made sweet sour add two tablespoons of vinegar and two tablespoons of brown sugar
6. boil for a few minutes and serve
