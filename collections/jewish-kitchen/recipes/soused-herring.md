## Ingredients

- three herrings
- one cup of vinegar
- whole peppers
- salt
- cloves
- two bay leaves

## Directions

1. Split and half three herrings, roll and tie them up.
2. Place them in a pie plate, pour over them a cup of vinegar, add whole peppers, salt, cloves to taste and two bay leaves.
3. Bake in a slow oven until soft (about twenty minutes).
