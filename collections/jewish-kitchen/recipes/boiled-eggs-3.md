## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling water

## Directions

1. For soft-boiled eggs: drop carefully into boiling water and boil three minutes, or place in a covered vessel of boiling water and allow to stand in a warm place (but not on the stove) for ten minutes
2. For hard-boiled eggs: cook in boiling water for fifteen or twenty minutes and then drop in cold water to prevent the yolk from turning dark
