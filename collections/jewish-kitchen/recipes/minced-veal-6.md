## Ingredients

- cold veal
- a little onion
- parsley
- a little of the rind of lemon
- a cup of stock
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- a slice of bread

## Directions

1. Mince cold veal very fine with a little onion, parsley, and a little of the rind of lemon
2. Put it into a stewpan with a cup of stock, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
3. Let it simmer for 1/2 an hour
4. Toast a slice of bread and cut it in three-cornered pieces
5. Put it round the dish after you have turned in your mince
