## Ingredients

- four cups of raw apples cut in small pieces
- two cups of bread crumbs
- one-half cup of hot water
- two teaspoons of butter
- two teaspoons of cinnamon
- one-half cup of honey
- cream

## Directions

1. Put a layer of the apple in a well-buttered pudding dish
2. Then a layer of crumbs
3. Mix the honey and hot water
4. Pour part of this over the crumbs
5. Sprinkle with cinnamon and dot with a few bits of butter
6. Fill the dish with alternate layers of apples, crumbs, honey, etc., having a layer of crumbs on top
7. Cover and bake forty-five minutes
8. Serve with cream
