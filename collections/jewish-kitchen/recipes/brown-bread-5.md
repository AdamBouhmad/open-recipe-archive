## Ingredients

- one cup rye
- one cup graham flour
- one cup corn-meal
- one teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of soda
- one cup of molasses
- two cups of sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix and sift together one cup each of rye, graham flour, corn-meal and one teaspoon of salt
2. Dissolve one teaspoon of soda in one cup of molasses
3. Add alternately to flour with two cups of sour milk
4. Grease one-pound baking-powder cans and put in the dough
5. Boil two and one-half hours, keeping the water always three-fourths up around the tins
6. Turn out on baking-tins and place in the oven fifteen minutes to brown
7. Serve warm or steam again or toast leftovers
