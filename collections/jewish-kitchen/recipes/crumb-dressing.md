## Ingredients

- chicken fat
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- chopped parsley
- egg

## Directions

1. Take one tablespoon of chicken fat, mix in two cups of bread crumbs
2. Add pinch of salt and pepper, a few drops of onion juice, one tablespoon of chopped parsley
3. Add one well-beaten egg
4. Mix all on stove in skillet
5. Remove from fire and stuff fowl
