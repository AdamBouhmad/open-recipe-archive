## Ingredients

- sliced celery
- sliced apples
- one-quarter of a pound of pecans or English walnuts
- one tablespoon of lemon-juice
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Mix an equal quantity of sliced celery and apples
2. Add a quarter of a pound of pecans or English walnuts, chopped fine
3. Put over a tablespoon of lemon-juice and sufficient mayonnaise dressing to thoroughly cover
4. Serve without lettuce or dish on lettuce leaves
