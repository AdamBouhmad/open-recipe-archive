## Ingredients

- fat
- ½-lb. of sausage
- 3 tomatoes
- 4 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put a little fat into the fryingpan, when it is hot skin a ½-lb. of sausage, cut it in very thin slices, lay it in the pan
2. Slice 3 tomatoes lay with it
3. Beat up 4 eggs with a little salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
4. When done, slip it on to the dish
