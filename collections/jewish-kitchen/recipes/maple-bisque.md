## Ingredients

- one cup of maple syrup
- yolks of three eggs
- whites of eggs
- one pint of cream
- one-half cup of chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of maple syrup until quite thick
2. Beat yolks of three eggs
3. Add to syrup while hot, stirring constantly until well mixed
4. Let cool
5. Beat whites of eggs to a froth
6. Whip one pint of cream
7. Mix all together
8. Add one-half cup of chopped nuts
9. Have a pudding-mold buttered and see that the edges fit close
10. Pack in rock salt and ice four hours
