## Ingredients

- pint of cream
- four ounces of powdered white sugar
- rind of a lemon
- glass of sherry wine
- half an ounce of dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Put into a basin a pint of cream
2. Add four ounces of powdered white sugar
3. Add the rind of a lemon rubbed on a lump of sugar
4. Add a glass of sherry wine
5. Whisk them well
6. Mix with it half an ounce of dissolved isinglass
7. Beat it all thoroughly together
8. Fill the mould, which should be set in ice till wanted
