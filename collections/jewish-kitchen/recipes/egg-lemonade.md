## Ingredients

- two eggs
- juice of two lemons
- four tablespoons of sugar
- four cups of water
- ice

## Directions

1. Break two eggs and beat the whites and yolks separately.
2. Mix juice of two lemons, four tablespoons of sugar, four cups of water and ice as for lemonade.
3. Add the eggs.
4. Pour rapidly back and forth from one pitcher to another and serve before the froth disappears.
