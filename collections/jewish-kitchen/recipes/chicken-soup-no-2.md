## Ingredients

- the carcass of a cold, cooked chicken
- one-half cup of chopped celery
- one onion chopped fine
- cold water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take the carcass of a cold, cooked chicken and break into small pieces
2. Add one-half cup of chopped celery and one onion chopped fine
3. Cover with cold water; simmer slowly for two hours
4. Strain, add salt and pepper to taste
