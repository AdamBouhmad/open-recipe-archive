## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup butter
- 1.5 cups pulverized sugar
- 3 eggs
- 0.5 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking-powder
- 0.5 lemon

## Directions

1. Rub to a cream one-half cup of butter with one and one-half cups of pulverized sugar
2. Add gradually the yolks of three eggs, one at a time, and one-half cup of sweet milk
3. Sift two cups of flour with one teaspoon of baking-powder, add alternately with the milk and the stiffly-beaten whites of three eggs
4. Add the grated peel of one-half lemon and the juice of one lemon
5. Bake in moderate oven thirty minutes
