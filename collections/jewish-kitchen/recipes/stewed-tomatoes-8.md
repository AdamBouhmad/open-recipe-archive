## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- boiling water
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the tomatoes; remove the skins; cut into small pieces and place in a saucepan over the fire.
2. Boil gently for twenty or thirty minutes and season, allowing for each quart of tomatoes one generous teaspoon each of salt and sugar and one tablespoon of butter.
3. If in addition to this seasoning a slice of onion has been cooked with the tomatoes from the beginning, the flavor will be greatly improved.
