## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons tea leaves
- boiling water
- warm water
- lemon slice or lemon juice
- preserved strawberries, cherries or raspberries

## Directions

1. Use a small earthenware tea-pot, thoroughly clean
2. Put in two teaspoons of tea leaves
3. Pour over it boiling water to one-fourth of the pot
4. Let it stand three minutes
5. Fill the pot entirely with boiling water and let it stand five minutes
6. In serving dilute with warm water to suit taste, or serve cold, but always without milk
7. Allow a thin slice of lemon or a few drops of lemon juice for each cup
8. Consider preserved strawberries, cherries or raspberries an improvement
