## Ingredients

- five or six large strawberries per glass
- orange juice
- powdered sugar
- shaved ice

## Directions

1. Slice five or six large strawberries into each glass and squeeze over them the juice of an orange
2. At serving time add one heaping teaspoon of powdered sugar and one tablespoon of shaved ice
