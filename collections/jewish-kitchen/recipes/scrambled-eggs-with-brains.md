## Ingredients

- brains
- hot water
- fresh water
- a little fat
- one teaspoon of chopped parsley
- salt
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Scald brains with hot water, clean and skin, and boil a few minutes in fresh water.
2. Melt a little fat in skillet, put in brains, finely chopped, and stir well until dry and done.
3. Add one teaspoon of chopped parsley, pinch of salt, and three eggs well-beaten.
4. Stir with a fork until eggs are evenly cooked.
5. Put on hot platter, and serve immediately.
