## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Boil till perfectly tender; let them be quite dry, and press them through a cullender, or mash and beat them well with a fork
2. Add a piece of butter, and milk, or cream, and continue beating till they are perfectly smooth
3. Return them to the saucepan to warm, or they may be browned before the fire
4. The chief art is to beat them sufficiently long, which renders them light
