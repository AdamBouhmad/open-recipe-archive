## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- bread crumbs
- half an egg from meat balls

## Directions

1. Boil 2 eggs very hard
2. When done throw them into cold water for 5 minutes
3. Take the yolks only, rub them quite smooth with a spoon
4. Have about the same quantity of bread crumbs
5. Mix with a little salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
6. Mix them with the half of egg left from the meat balls
7. Make them very small, as they swell very much
