## Ingredients

- one pint of rice
- one quart of strong gravy
- one onion
- cloves
- sweet herbs
- one chicken
- forcemeat
- yolks of four eggs
- juice of a lemon
- saffron

## Directions

1. Stew gently one pint of rice in one quart of strong gravy till it begins to swell
2. Add an onion stuck with cloves and a bunch of sweet herbs
3. Add a chicken stuffed with forcemeat
4. Let it stew with the rice till thoroughly done
5. Take it up and stir in the rice, the yolks of four eggs, and the juice of a lemon
6. Color the rice to a fine yellow with saffron
7. Serve the fowl in the same dish with the rice
