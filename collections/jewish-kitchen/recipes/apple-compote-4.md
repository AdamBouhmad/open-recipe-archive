## Ingredients

- six apples
- water
- white wine
- white sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take six apples (Greenings, Baldwins or Bellflowers)
2. Pare, quarter, core and lay them in cold water as soon as pared
3. Take the parings and seeds
4. Put in a dish with a cup of water and a cup of white wine
5. Boil for about fifteen minutes
6. Strain through a fine sieve
7. Put on to boil again
8. Add half a cup of white sugar and the peel of half a lemon
9. Put in the apples
10. Let them stew for fifteen minutes longer
11. When the apples are tender, take up each piece carefully with a silver spoon and lay on a platter to cool
12. Let the syrup boil down to about half the quantity you had after removing the apples
13. Add to it the juice of half a lemon
14. Lay your apples in a fruit dish, pyramid shape
15. Pour the syrup over them
16. Serve
