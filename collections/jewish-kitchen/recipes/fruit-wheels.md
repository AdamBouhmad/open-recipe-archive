## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of sugar
- two large tablespoons of butter
- milk
- soft butter
- one teaspoon of flour
- four tablespoons of granulated sugar
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one-half cup of seeded and cut raisins
- one-half cup of chopped citron
- one-half cup of cleaned currants

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, baking-powder, salt, and sugar
2. Rub in butter
3. Mix to soft dough with milk
4. Roll out one-half inch thick
5. Spread thickly with soft butter
6. Dust with flour, granulated sugar, and cinnamon
7. Sprinkle over raisins, citron, and currants
8. Roll up
9. Cut in one-inch slices
10. Put one inch apart on greased, flat pans
11. Bake in hot oven
