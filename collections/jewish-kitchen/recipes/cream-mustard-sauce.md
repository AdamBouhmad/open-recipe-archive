## Ingredients

- white sauce
- one tablespoon of mustard
- a teaspoon of cold water

## Directions

1. Make white sauce as directed
2. Mix one tablespoon of mustard with a teaspoon of cold water
3. Stir into the sauce about two minutes before serving
4. The quantity of mustard may be increased or diminished, as one may desire the flavor strong or mild
