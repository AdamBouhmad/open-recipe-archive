## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- one pound of sugar
- one or two eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pound almonds
2. Mix with one pound of sugar, one or two eggs and enough cinnamon to give a strong flavor
3. Bake in a shallow pan and cut into small sections
