## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- olive oil or butter
- lemon
- Maitre d'Hotel butter

## Directions

1. For large fish, split down the back and remove head and tail
2. For salmon and halibut, cut into one-inch slices
3. For smelts and other small fish, leave whole
4. Wipe the fish as dry as possible
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. If the fish is dry and white, brush the flesh side well with olive oil or butter
7. Put in a well-greased broiler, placing the thickest parts toward the middle or back
8. Hold over a hot fire until the flesh side is nicely browned
9. Cook the skin side just long enough to make the skin crisp
10. Small fish require from ten to fifteen minutes, large fish from fifteen to twenty-five minutes
11. Loosen one side first, then the other, and lift carefully with a cake turner
12. Place on a platter and spread with butter
13. Stand in the oven for a few minutes
14. Garnish with lemon and serve with Maitre d'Hotel butter
