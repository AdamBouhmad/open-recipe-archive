## Ingredients

- A teacup of cream
- 6 oz. of fresh butter
- the yolks of 4 eggs
- a glass of brandy
- 6 oz. of castor sugar
- a very floury potato
- 1 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1/2 an oz. of bitter almonds
- 1 oz. of Barcelona nut
- the rind of 2 lemons
- the juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 grated nutmeg
- a rich puff paste

## Directions

1. Boil a very floury potato, and when cold rub it up to a flour quite smooth
2. Pound 1 oz. of sweet almonds, 1/2 an oz. of bitter almonds pounded very fine, pound also with them 1 oz. of Barcelona nut
3. Grate the rind of 2 lemons, the juice of 1, 1/2 grated nutmeg
4. Break the yolks of 4 eggs into a basin
5. Beat them with a glass of brandy
6. Add to this 6 oz. of castor sugar, beat this well together
7. Mix all well together, add the cream and butter, stir well together
8. Have a rich puff paste, line the little patty pans, trim the edges neatly
9. Put in some of the mixture
10. Bake quickly in a hot oven
