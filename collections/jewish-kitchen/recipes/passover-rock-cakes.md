## Ingredients

- Half a pound of butter
- 1/4 lb. sugar
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- rind of a lemon
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of the sieved meal
- 2 oz. of ground almonds
- almonds for decoration

## Directions

1. Mix the butter, sugar, currants and lemon with 2 oz. of ground almonds and the meal
2. Beat the eggs and add them
3. The mixture will form a thick paste, and if not thick enough, add more meal
4. Break them in pieces, and put them on a tin to bake
5. Chop a few almonds, and stick a few pieces in each rock
