## Ingredients

- 3 lb. of boned brisket
- a quart of water
- a little salt, pepper
- an onion
- a carrot
- a turnip
- a few pieces of celery
- a little of the green leaves
- half pound of barley
- a little chopped parsley
- a little grated nutmeg
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Put 3-lb. of boned brisket on with a quart of water
2. Add a little salt, pepper
3. Cut an onion small, a carrot, turnip, a few pieces of celery, with a little of the green leaves chopped
4. Wash them well
5. Also add half pound of barley
6. Let it boil quick
7. Skim it well, then only let it simmer
8. You can boil in the same soup a couple of carrots whole to garnish the beef for dinner
9. Have in the tureen a little chopped parsley and a little grated nutmeg
10. Colour the soup with dried saffron
11. It will take 3 hours to be well done
