## Ingredients

- 0.75 cup sugar
- butter the size of an egg
- 2 eggs
- 0.5 cup milk
- 1.5 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons baking-powder
- cinnamon
- flour
- sugar
- water
- chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Cream three-fourths cup of sugar with a piece of butter the size of an egg
2. Beat together
3. Then add two eggs, one-half cup of milk (scant), one and one-half cups of flour, one teaspoon of vanilla and two teaspoons of baking-powder
4. Put cinnamon, flour, sugar and a few drops of water together, and form in little pfarvel with your hand and sprinkle on top of cake
5. Also sprinkle a few chopped nuts on top
6. Do not bake too quickly
7. Bake in flat pan
