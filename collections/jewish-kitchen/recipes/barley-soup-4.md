## Ingredients

- one cup barley
- two onions, cut fine
- one-half cup carrots, diced
- one teaspoon salt
- pepper
- two quarts water
- one-half cup celery root

## Directions

1. Take one cup of barley, two onions cut fine, one-half cup of carrots diced, one teaspoon of salt, pepper to taste
2. Add two quarts of water and simmer two or three hours
3. When water has evaporated add soup; if you are making fresh soup, keep adding the "top soup," strained, to the barley and let boil until tender
4. One-half cup of celery root boiled with the barley improves the flavor
