## Ingredients

- fine, ripe melons
- vinegar
- 3 pounds and a half white sugar per dozen small cantaloupes
- 3 ounces stick cinnamon
- 1 ounce cloves
- 2 ounces allspice (whole spices)
- 1 pint fresh vinegar

## Directions

1. Take fine, ripe melons, pare, take out the seeds and wash
2. Cut into slices about three inches long and two inches wide
3. Lay them in a stone jar and cover with vinegar for twenty-four hours or longer
4. Lay the fruit on a clean board to drip
5. Throw away one quart of the vinegar to each quart remaining
6. Boil the spices, vinegar and sugar, adding a pint of fresh vinegar to the old
7. When well skimmed put in the melons, boil fifteen minutes, twenty is still better
8. Take out the fruit, put it in jars and boil the syrup awhile longer
9. Skim it again and pour boiling hot upon the fruit
10. Seal when cold
