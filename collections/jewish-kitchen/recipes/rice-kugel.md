## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- water
- one-fourth cup of chicken-fat
- scant cup of powdered sugar
- little cinnamon
- grated peel of one lemon
- yolks of three eggs
- one-half cup of raisins seeded
- one-half pound of stewed prunes pitted
- one-half cup of pounded almonds
- bitter almonds
- pudding sauce, either wine or brandy

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of rice in water until done, then let it cool
2. In the meanwhile rub one-fourth cup of chicken-fat to a cream
3. Add a scant cup of powdered sugar, a little cinnamon, the grated peel of one lemon, the yolks of three eggs adding one at a time
4. Add one-half cup of raisins seeded, one-half pound of stewed prunes pitted, then add the cold rice
5. One-half cup of pounded almonds mixed with a few bitter ones improves this pudding
6. Serve with a pudding sauce, either wine or brandy
7. This pudding may be eaten hot or cold and may be either baked or boiled
8. If baked, one hour is required; if boiled, two hours, the water must be kept boiling steadily
9. Left-over rice may be used, butter instead of the fat, and the rice may be boiled in milk
