## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- three-fourths of a cup of sugar
- one-third cup of flour
- one-eighth teaspoon of salt
- three slightly-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Scald two cups of milk
2. Mix together three-fourths of a cup of sugar, one-third cup of flour and one-eighth teaspoon of salt
3. Add to three slightly-beaten eggs and pour in scalded milk
4. Cook twenty minutes over boiling water, stirring constantly until thickened
5. Cool and flavor
6. This can be used as a foundation for most fillings, by adding melted chocolate, nuts, fruits, etc.
