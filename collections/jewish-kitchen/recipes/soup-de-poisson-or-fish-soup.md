## Ingredients

- a cod's-head
- water
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- celery
- parsley
- sweet herbs
- two or three onions
- cutlets of fish, sole or brill
- equal quantities of water and wine
- a large lump of butter
- the juice of a lemon
- cream
- flour
- force-meat balls of cod's liver

## Directions

1. Make a good stock, by simmering a cod's-head in water, enough to cover the fish
2. Season it with pepper and salt, mace, celery, parsley, and a few sweet herbs, with two or three onions
3. When sufficiently done, strain it
4. Prepare cutlets of fish: cut very small, well-trimmed cutlets from any fish, sole or brill are perhaps best suited
5. Stew them in equal quantities of water and wine, but not more than will cover them, with a large lump of butter, and the juice of a lemon
6. When they have stewed gently for about fifteen or twenty minutes, add them to the soup
7. Thicken with cream and flour
8. Serve the soup with the cutlets in a tureen; force-meat balls of cod's liver are sometimes added
