## Ingredients

- one pound of cooked kidney beans
- one-half pound of grated cheese
- salt and red pepper to taste
- bread crumbs
- butter and water for basting
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Mash one pound of cooked kidney beans and put them through a food chopper
2. Add one-half pound of grated cheese
3. Add salt and red pepper to taste
4. Add sufficient bread crumbs to make the mixture stiff enough to form into a ball
5. Bake in a moderate oven, basting occasionally with butter and water
6. Serve with tomato sauce
