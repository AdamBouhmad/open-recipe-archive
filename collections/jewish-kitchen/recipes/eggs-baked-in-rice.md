## Ingredients

- steamed rice
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Line a buttered dish with steamed rice
2. Break the eggs in the centre
3. Dot with butter
4. Sprinkle with salt, pepper
5. Bake in a moderate oven
