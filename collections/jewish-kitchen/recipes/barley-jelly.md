## Ingredients

- pearl barley
- water
- white sugar
- lemon juice
- white wine
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil in an iron saucepan, one tea-cup full of pearl barley, with one quart of cold water
2. Pour off the water when it boils, and add another quart
3. Let it simmer very gently for three hours over or near a slow fire
4. Stir it frequently with a wooden spoon
5. Strain it, and sweeten with white sugar
6. Add the juice of a lemon, a little white wine, and a quarter of an ounce of isinglass dissolved in a little water
7. Pour it into a mould
