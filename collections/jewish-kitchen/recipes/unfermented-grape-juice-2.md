## Ingredients

- ten pounds of Concord grapes
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and stem ten pounds of Concord grapes
2. put them in a preserving kettle and crush slightly
3. Bring to the boiling point and cook gently for one-half hour
4. Strain through cheese-cloth or jelly bag, pressing out all the juice possible
5. return to fire and with two pounds of sugar cook for fifteen minutes
6. strain again
7. reheat and pour into sterilized bottles thoroughly heated
8. Put in sterilized corks and dip the necks of the bottles in hot sealing-wax
