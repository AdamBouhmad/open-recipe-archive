## Ingredients

- two pounds of ribs of beef
- one chicken
- plenty of water
- a split carrot
- onion
- a head of celery
- a little parsley root
- pepper and salt to taste
- a pinch of saffron

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of ribs of beef and one chicken.
2. Place in a large cooking-vessel with plenty of water and add a split carrot and onion, a head of celery, a little parsley root, pepper and salt to taste, and a pinch of saffron.
3. Let the whole simmer for two hours.
4. The meat is then removed and can be used as a separate dish.
