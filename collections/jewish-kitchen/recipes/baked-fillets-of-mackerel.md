## Ingredients

- mackerel
- cotton
- chopped onion
- parsley
- marjoram
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- oil or butter
- vinegar
- fennel

## Directions

1. Wash the mackerel well
2. dry them
3. split them in halves
4. take out the bones
5. roll each half up
6. fasten with a piece of cotton
7. put in a deep dish some chopped onion, parsley and marjoram, pepper, salt, 3 or 4 cloves, with 2 spoonfuls of oil, or a lb. of butter, a cup of vinegar
8. let them bake for 1 hour
9. keep basting them
10. dish them up
11. garnish with fennel
12. put the sauce nicely over the mackerel when cold
