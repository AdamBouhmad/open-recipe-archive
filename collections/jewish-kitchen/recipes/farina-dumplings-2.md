## Ingredients

- one kitchen spoon of fresh butter
- one cup of milk
- farina
- yolks of two eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- one-half cup of grated almonds

## Directions

1. Put in a double boiler one kitchen spoon of fresh butter, stir in one cup of milk.
2. When it begins to boil stir in enough farina to thicken.
3. Take off the stove and when cold add the yolks of two eggs and the stiffly-beaten whites, and a little salt and nutmeg and one-half cup of grated almonds if desired.
4. Let cool, then make into little balls, and ten minutes before soup is to be served, drop in boiler and let boil up once or twice.
