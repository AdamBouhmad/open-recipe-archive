## Ingredients

- a fine thick steak
- flour
- a little good beef gravy
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- a quarter of a hundred good chestnuts
- a little Espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Take a fine thick steak and half fry it
2. Flour and place it in a stewpan with a little good beef gravy
3. Season with cayenne pepper and salt
4. When it has simmered for about ten minutes, add a quarter of a hundred good chestnuts, peeled and the inner skin scraped off
5. Let them stew with the steak till well done
6. A little Espagnole sauce heightens the flavor
