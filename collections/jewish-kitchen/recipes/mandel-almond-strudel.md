## Ingredients

- dough as for Apple Strudel No. 2
- one-half pound of almonds
- yolks of four eggs
- one-quarter pound of granulated sugar
- grated peel of one lemon
- oil, butter or fat

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for Apple Strudel No. 2
2. Blanch one-half pound of almonds and grind
3. When dried beat the yolks of four eggs light with one-quarter pound of granulated sugar
4. Add the grated peel of one lemon and mix in the almonds
5. Spread over the dough with plenty of oil, butter or fat and roll
6. Bake; baste very often
