## Ingredients

- one pound blanched almonds pounded
- eight eggs
- cinnamon
- lemon rind
- one and one-half cups of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs for twenty minutes
2. Add one and one-half cups of sugar gently
3. Add the almonds
4. Mix all together thoroughly
5. Bake in shallow pans and serve cold
