## Ingredients

- unblanched, pounded almonds
- grated apples
- chopped raisins
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- grated rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Mix unblanched pounded almonds, grated apples, chopped raisins, brown sugar, plenty of cinnamon and the grated rind of a lemon together
2. Fill the hollowed out center of the chrimsel with them
3. Place one chrimsel upon another, being careful not to let the filling escape from its hollow
4. Fasten the edges securely together with the fingers, keeping the rounded shape uninjured
5. Fry them in boiling fat
