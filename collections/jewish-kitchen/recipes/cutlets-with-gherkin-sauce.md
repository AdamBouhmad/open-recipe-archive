## Ingredients

- 4 plump chops
- bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- chopped parsley
- powdered marjoram
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- boiling fat
- 1 onion
- dripping
- juice of a lemon
- 2 green gherkins pickled
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Take 4 plump chops and take out the bones
2. Cut each chop into 3 small chops and flatten them very nicely
3. Have some bread crumbs in one plate and 2 eggs beat up in another plate
4. Add to the eggs some chopped parsley, a little powdered marjoram, pepper, salt, and grated nutmeg
5. Beat it all well
6. Have your frying-pan on the fire with some boiling fat
7. Dip the little cutlets first in the bread crumbs, then into the egg
8. Fry them a light brown
9. For the sauce, chop an onion and put it in a small saucepan with a small piece of dripping
10. Fry it and add some salt, pepper, the juice of a lemon, and two green gherkins pickled cut up small
11. Do not let the gherkins boil
12. Mix a teaspoonful of flour with the juice of the lemon, and 1/2 a teaspoonful of brown sugar
13. Stir all this well together
14. Place cutlets in a graceful pile in the centre of the dish and pour the sauce round
