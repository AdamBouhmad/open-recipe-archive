## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 tomatoes
- 1/2 oz. of butter
- 4 or 5 eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil 2 or 3 tomatoes, that is to say, cut them in slices
2. Put them in a small saucepan at the side of the fire with no water
3. Pulp them through your sieve kept for butter purposes
4. Put in another small saucepan 1/2 oz. of butter to fry
5. Then put in the tomato
6. Beat 4 or 5 eggs with salt and pepper
7. Stir it with the butter and tomatoes till done
8. Send up in a hot dish
