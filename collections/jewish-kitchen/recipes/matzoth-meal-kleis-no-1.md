## Ingredients

- matzoth meal
- egg
- ground ginger
- powdered cinnamon
- ground almond
- pepper and salt
- chicken fat or clarified dripping

## Directions

1. To each tablespoon of matzoth meal take one egg.
2. Beat the egg separately, adding a very little ground ginger, powdered cinnamon, ground almond, pepper and salt.
3. Now stir in the matzoth meal and make into a paste with chicken fat or clarified dripping.
4. Form this paste into small balls and boil them for twenty minutes in the Yom-tov soup.
