## Ingredients

- one package cream cheese
- one cup of chopped nut meats
- one teaspoon of chopped parsley
- two tablespoons of whipped cream
- salt
- red pepper
- parsley and chopped nuts for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix one package cream cheese with one cup of chopped nut meats, one teaspoon of chopped parsley, two tablespoons of whipped cream, salt and red pepper
2. Roll into balls and serve cold, garnished with parsley and chopped nuts
