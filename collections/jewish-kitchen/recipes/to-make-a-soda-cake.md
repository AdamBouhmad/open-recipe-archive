## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- a pinch of salt
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- a cup of milk
- 1/2 lb. currants
- 1/2 lb. raisins
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. peel
- 1 oz. mixed spice
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Warm 1/2 lb. of butter in a cup of milk
2. Mix all well together
3. Beat up for 20 minutes
4. Butter the tin
5. Bake in a quick oven
