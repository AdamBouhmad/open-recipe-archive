## Ingredients

- two small beets
- water
- citric acid (sour salt)
- sugar
- salt
- three-quarter cup of sour cream
- sweet cream
- yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Cut two small beets in strips, cover with water and let cook until tender, add citric acid (sour salt) and a little sugar to make sweet and sour, a little salt, and three-quarter cup of sour cream.
2. Serve cold.
3. Sweet cream may be used and while hot gradually poured over the well-beaten yolks of two eggs, keeping the soup over the stove and stirring all the time until thick and smooth.
4. Remove from stove and serve cold.
