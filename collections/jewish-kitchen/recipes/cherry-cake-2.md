## Ingredients

- butter
- cake-pan
- kuchen dough
- two pounds of cherries
- sugar
- four eggs
- one cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Line a cake-pan, which has been well-buttered, with a thin layer of kuchen dough
2. Stone two pounds of cherries and lay them on a sieve with a dish underneath to catch the juice
3. Sprinkle sugar over them and bake
4. In the meantime beat up four eggs with a cup of sugar, beat until light and add the cherry juice
5. Draw the kuchen to the oven door, pour this mixture over it and bake
