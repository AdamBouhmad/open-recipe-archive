## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- eight ounces of loaf sugar to every quart of rhubarb

## Directions

1. Strip the skins from the stalks, and cut into small pieces as you would for pies.
2. Set the sugar over the fire with as little water as possible.
3. Throw in the rhubarb and boil ten minutes.
4. Put in jars and seal.
