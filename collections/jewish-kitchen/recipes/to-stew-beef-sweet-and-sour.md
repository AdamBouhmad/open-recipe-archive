## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 pounds of beef
- an onion
- 2 teeth of garlic
- a few cloves
- allspice
- salt and pepper
- pint of vinegar
- a spoonful of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of brown sugar
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Put in a deep dish 4 or 5 pounds of beef with an onion sliced, 2 teeth of garlic, a few cloves, and allspice, salt, and pepper.
2. Pour over it pint of vinegar.
3. Leave it for four or five days, turning it each day.
4. When you are ready to cook it put it in a stewpan with the vinegar and all the other ingredients.
5. Let it simmer, it will only take an hour-and-a-half.
6. Take the meat out of the saucepan as it becomes tender.
7. Strain the liquor through a sieve.
8. Put the meat back in the saucepan and throw over what you strained.
9. Rub in a basin a spoonful of flour, 2 of brown sugar, a little browning to colour.
10. Mix it all well with the liquor in the saucepan.
