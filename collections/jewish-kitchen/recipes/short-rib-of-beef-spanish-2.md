## Ingredients

- small ribs of beef
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- garlic
- tomatoes
- red peppers or paprika

## Directions

1. Get the small ribs of beef and put on with water enough to cover
2. Season with salt, pepper, an onion and a tiny clove of garlic
3. Let it cook about two hours
4. Add a can of tomatoes and season highly either with red peppers or paprika
5. Cook at least three hours
