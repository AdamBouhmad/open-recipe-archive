## Ingredients

- one cup sour cream
- three eggs
- two tablespoons sugar
- one tablespoon mustard
- one-half cup vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- paprika
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of sour cream and three eggs, well beaten. Dissolve two tablespoons of sugar and one tablespoon of mustard in one-half cup of vinegar; salt, pepper and paprika to taste, and then stir this slowly into the cream and eggs. Put in double boiler, cook until thick, then add butter the size of an egg and cook about five minutes longer. Take from fire and bottle; this dressing will keep for months.
