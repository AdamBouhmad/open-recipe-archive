## Ingredients

- salmon trout
- oil
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- caper or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Split them open like a mackerel and wash it
2. Dry it well and lay on it a spoonful of oil, the juice of a lemon, salt, pepper, a little cayenne
3. Leave it for an hour
4. Broil it before a clear fire
5. You can put over it more oil and lemon, or caper or anchovy sauce
