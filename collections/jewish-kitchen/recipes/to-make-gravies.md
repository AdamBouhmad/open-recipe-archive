## Ingredients

- little bones of the rib or any little trimmings
- pepper and salt
- a few drops of browning

## Directions

1. Boil the little bones of the rib or any little trimming you do not want with a little pepper and salt.
2. Strain and colour with a few drops of browning.
3. Make it boiling hot and pour over your roast.
4. Baste it backwards and forwards with a spoon over the joint.
5. If for poultry, boil the legs and the neck with a small onion.
