## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 lb. of good chuck steak
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- juice of a lemon
- forced meat balls
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut up 1 or 2 lb. of good chuck steak
2. Put it on to stew with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
3. Put in the juice of a lemon
4. Stir it well till the liquor thickens
5. Cook a few forced meat balls made very small
6. Cook a few potatoes, cut in halves stewed with
7. Arrange the dish as for pigeon pie
8. Bake in a good oven a light brown
9. Remember as soon as the potatoes brown it is done
