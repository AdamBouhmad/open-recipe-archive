## Ingredients

- medium potatoes
- boiling water
- salt
- two tablespoons of butter
- one-half tablespoon of lemon juice
- one tablespoon of finely minced parsley
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Pare very thin, medium potatoes as near a size as possible
2. Have ready a pot of boiling water, salted
3. Drop in the potatoes and keep them at a quick boil until tender
4. Beat to a cream two tablespoons of butter, one-half tablespoon of lemon juice and one tablespoon of finely minced parsley
5. Add salt and a dash of cayenne pepper
6. Spread over the hot potatoes and it will melt into a delicious dressing
