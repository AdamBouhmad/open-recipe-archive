## Ingredients

- three pounds of pie plant
- three oranges
- two cups of sugar
- grated rind of one orange
- three pounds of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Cut three pounds of pie plant into small pieces (unpeeled).
2. Peel three oranges and cut into small pieces.
3. Put with this two cups of sugar and the grated rind of one orange.
4. Let stand overnight.
5. Cook until clear, stirring often.
6. Then add three pounds of granulated sugar heated in oven.
7. Cook until clear; ten to twenty minutes.
8. Pour into jelly glasses and cover with paraffin.
