## Ingredients

- sausages
- boiling water
- grated horseradish or French mustard

## Directions

1. Wash and put on in boiling water
2. Boil ten minutes
3. Fill a deep dish with hot water, put sausages in, cover, and serve in hot water
4. To be eaten with grated horseradish or French mustard
