## Ingredients

- six large, juicy lemons
- peel of three lemons
- two oranges
- juice of both oranges
- peel of one orange
- one pint of sugar

## Directions

1. Take six large, juicy lemons and grate peel of three lemons
2. Take two oranges, juice of both, and peel of one
3. Squeeze out every drop of juice
4. Steep the grated peel of lemon and orange in juice for an hour
5. Strain and mix in one pint of sugar
6. Stir until dissolved and freeze
