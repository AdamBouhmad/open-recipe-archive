## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- beaten whites of four eggs
- two cups of milk
- salt
- three tablespoons of fresh butter melted
- five level tablespoons of flour
- butter for cups

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs until very light, add the stiffly-beaten whites and then stir in two cups of milk, add a pinch of salt, three tablespoons of fresh butter melted, and five level tablespoons of flour that have been wet with a little of the milk.
2. Stir well together and divide equally between cups.
3. Butter the cups before pouring in the mixture.
4. Bake in hot oven until brown (generally twenty minutes).
5. Turn out carefully in the dish in which they are to be served, and pour over them the lemon sauce.
