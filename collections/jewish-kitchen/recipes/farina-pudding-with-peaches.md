## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- one-half cup of farina
- salt
- a small piece of butter
- yolks of four eggs
- four tablespoons of white sugar
- vanilla
- whites of the eggs
- pulverized sugar
- stewed or canned peaches
- rum sauce

## Directions

1. Add one-half cup of farina, salt, and a small piece of butter to one quart of milk
2. Boil in a double boiler until thick
3. Beat the yolks of four eggs with four tablespoons of white sugar, and add this just before taking off the fire
4. Stir it thoroughly, but do not let it boil any more
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth with pulverized sugar
7. Butter a pudding dish, put in part of the custard, in which you have mixed the whites (if you have any extra whites of eggs beat and use them also)
8. Add a layer of stewed or canned peaches
9. Cover with the remaining custard and bake
10. Eat with rum sauce
