## Ingredients

- Choice dates
- Preserved ginger
- Blanched nuts chopped
- Fondant or confectioner's sugar and ginger syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the stones from choice dates
2. Chop together equal measures of preserved ginger and blanched nuts chopped (hickory, pecan, or almond)
3. Mix with fondant or a paste of confectioner's sugar and ginger syrup
4. Use only enough fondant or paste to hold the ingredients together
5. Fill the open space in the dates, cover securely, and roll in granulated sugar
