## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of seed
- 1 lb. of honey
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1 lb. of almonds blanched and toasted

## Directions

1. Pick clean 1 lb. of seed, put it in a pan to roast, over a slow fire, stirring it all the while with a spoon
2. It will take an hour or more
3. When toasted, turn the seed out
4. put in a saucepan kept for sweets 1 lb. of honey, 1 lb. of loaf sugar, melt it
5. when dissolved, put in the toasted seed
6. let it simmer
7. have ready 1 lb. of almonds blanched and toasted a light brown
8. When the seed boils put in the almonds, stir it well all together to prevent it sticking
9. when it gets hard, which you can tell by putting a spoonful on a plate if it sets
10. oil a clean board, turn the sweet on it
11. damp the rolling-pin with cold water, roll the sweet out about 1 inch thick
12. cut it in strips, then cut those strips in squares, or any shape you like
