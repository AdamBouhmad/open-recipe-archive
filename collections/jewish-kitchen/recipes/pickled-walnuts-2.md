## Ingredients

- fine large walnuts
- salt
- allspice, 1/2 oz.
- cloves
- peppercorns
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Select some fine large walnuts
2. Prick them all over with a large darning needle
3. Lay them on a flat dish
4. Sprinkle them well with salt
5. Lay them out in the sun
6. Turn them every day and add more salt; in 5 or 6 days they will become quite black
7. Put them in a jar with 1/2 oz. of allspice, cloves, some peppercorns, and some more salt
8. Fill the jar with cold vinegar
9. Tie it down tight
10. Put it in a dry closet
11. They will be ready for use in 4 or 5 months
