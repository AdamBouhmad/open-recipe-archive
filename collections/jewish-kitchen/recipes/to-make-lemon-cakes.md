## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 14 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of rose water
- raspings and juice of 4 lemons

## Directions

1. The eggs must be very fresh
2. Separate the whites from the yolks
3. Beat the whites up to a strong snow
4. Beat the yolks well
5. Add the powdered sugar, the raspings of the lemon, and the rose water
6. Beat this altogether for half an hour till it becomes quite light
7. Beat the whites to a strong snow
8. Mix it very lightly with your other ingredients with a spoon
9. When well mixed add the flour
10. Put on the baking tin 3 greased sheets of paper
11. Bake these biscuits in oval small tins well greased, in a moderate oven; the less they are handled the better
