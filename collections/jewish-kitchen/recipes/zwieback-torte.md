## Ingredients

- yolks of six eggs
- one and one-eighth cups of sugar
- one-half box of zwieback
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- one tablespoon of rum or sherry wine
- one-half teaspoon of bitter almond extract
- whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of six eggs with one and one-eighth cups of sugar
2. Add one-half box of zwieback, which has been rolled very fine
3. Add one teaspoon of baking-powder
4. Season with one tablespoon of rum or sherry wine and one-half teaspoon of bitter almond extract
5. Fold in the stiffly-beaten whites of the six eggs
6. Bake in ungreased form in moderate oven three-quarters of an hour
