## Ingredients

- Knuckle of veal
- a brisket bone
- 3-lb. of clod
- 4 quarts of water
- pepper
- salt
- a blade of mace
- 1 large onion
- a tooth of garlic
- 1 leek
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- 2 or 3 sticks of celery

## Directions

1. Put knuckle of veal, brisket bone, and clod into a very clean stock-pot
2. Add 4 quarts of water, pepper, salt, a blade of mace, 1 large onion, a tooth of garlic, 1 leek, 1 carrot, 1 turnip, and 2 or 3 sticks of celery
3. Let it boil up quick and skim it well
4. Put it at the side to simmer for 3 hours
5. Remember that fast boiling spoils everything
6. After it is well boiled and you find the meat leaves the bones, strain it through a nice clean sieve
