## Ingredients

- one-half pound marshmallows
- one cup of sugar
- one-quarter cup of cold water
- white of one egg

## Directions

1. Melt one-half pound marshmallows over hot water
2. Cook together one cup of sugar and one-quarter cup of cold water until it threads thoroughly
3. Beat up the white of an egg and syrup and mix, then add to the melted marshmallows and beat until creamy and cool
4. Can be used for cake filling or spread between two cookies
