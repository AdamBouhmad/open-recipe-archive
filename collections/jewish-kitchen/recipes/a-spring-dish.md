## Ingredients

- one quart of young peas
- a little mint
- a few lumps of sugar
- a little salt and white pepper
- one pint of water
- paste for short crust
- a little brown sugar
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Take one quart of young peas, a little mint, a few lumps of sugar, a little salt and white pepper, simmer them gently in one pint of water
2. when the peas are half done, throw in small dumplings made of paste, as if for short crust, and sweetened with a little brown sugar
3. beat up two eggs, and drop in a spoonful at a time, just before serving
4. it will require a deep dish, as the liquor is not to be strained off
