## Ingredients

- seven peeled and cored apples
- six tablespoons of sugar
- two tablespoons of butter
- six eggs
- one pint of sour cream
- one tablespoon of vanilla
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- sugar to taste
- three cups of finely rolled zwieback
- lumps of butter

## Directions

1. Cook apples with six tablespoons of sugar and two tablespoons of butter together until apples are soft
2. Cream six eggs
3. Add to them one pint of sour cream, one tablespoon of vanilla, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon, and sugar to taste
4. Pour into the cooked apples and let all boil together till thick
5. Remove from stove
6. In the bottom of a well-greased pan put a layer of two cups of crumbs
7. Add a layer of the apple mixture
8. Add a layer of the remaining crumbs
9. Top with lumps of butter over all
10. Bake one hour
