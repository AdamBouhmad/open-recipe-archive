## Ingredients

- nice, large, well-shaped tomatoes
- pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Select nice, large, well-shaped tomatoes
2. Pare and slice
3. Put on ice
4. When ready to serve sprinkle each layer thickly with pulverized sugar
