## Ingredients

- a cup full of biscuit powder
- a little milk
- a couple of eggs
- three ounces of sugar
- two ounces of warmed butter
- a little shred of lemon peel
- a table-spoonful of rum

## Directions

1. Mix into a batter a cup full of biscuit powder, with a little milk and a couple of eggs, to which add three ounces of sugar, two ounces of warmed butter, a little shred of lemon peel, and a table-spoonful of rum
2. Pour the mixture into a mould, and boil or bake
