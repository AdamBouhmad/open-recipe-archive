## Ingredients

- one pound of cheese
- one and one-half tablespoons of cream
- one tablespoon of chopped parsley
- salt
- cold water
- waxed paper
- pimento
- lettuce leaves
- French dressing
- wafers

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly one pound of cheese, one and one-half tablespoons of cream, one tablespoon of chopped parsley and salt to taste
2. First fill a rectangular tin mold with cold water to chill and wet the surface
3. Line the bottom with waxed paper
4. Pack in three layers, putting two or three parallel strips of pimento between layers
5. Cover with waxed paper and set in a cool place until ready to serve
6. Run a knife around the sides and invert the mold
7. Cut in slices and serve on lettuce leaves with French dressing and wafers
