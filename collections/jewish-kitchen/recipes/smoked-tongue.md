## Ingredients

- smoked tongue
- cold water
- hot water

## Directions

1. Put on to boil in a large kettle, fill with cold water, enough to completely cover the tongue.
2. Keep adding hot water as it boils down so as to keep it covered with water until done.
3. Keep covered with a lid while boiling and put a heavy weight on the top of the lid so as not to let the steam escape.
4. It should boil very slowly and steadily for four hours.
5. When tongue is cooked set it outdoors to cool in the liquor in which it was boiled.
6. If the tongue is very dry, soak overnight before boiling.
7. In serving slice very thin and garnish with parsley.
