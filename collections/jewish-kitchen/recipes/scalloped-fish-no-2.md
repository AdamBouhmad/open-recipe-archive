## Ingredients

- cold cooked fish
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- white sauce
- flour
- milk
- buttered bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Butter a dish, place in a layer of cold cooked fish.
2. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, parsley, salt, butter and pepper.
3. Repeat.
4. Cover with white sauce, using one tablespoon of flour to two tablespoons of butter and one cup of milk.
5. Sprinkle top with buttered bread crumbs and bake.
