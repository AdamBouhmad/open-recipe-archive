## Ingredients

- figs
- 7 pounds fruit
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 ounces whole cinnamon
- 2 ounces whole peppers
- 1 ounce cloves
- 1 orange
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Boil the figs in water one and one-half hours, then drain and weigh
2. Boil syrup one-half hour
3. Add fruit and boil slowly two hours
