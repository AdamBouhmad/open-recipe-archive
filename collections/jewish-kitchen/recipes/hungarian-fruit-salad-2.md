## Ingredients

- equal parts of banana, orange, pineapple, grapefruit
- one-half cup of chopped nuts
- French dressing
- apple or orange skins
- watercress or lettuce leaves
- paprika

## Directions

1. Mix together equal parts of banana, orange, pineapple, grapefruit and one-half cup of chopped nuts.
2. Marinate with French dressing.
3. Fill apple or orange skins with mixture.
4. Arrange on a bed of watercress or lettuce leaves.
5. Sprinkle with paprika.
