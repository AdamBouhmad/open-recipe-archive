## Ingredients

- a pound of sour grass (sorrel)
- three potatoes
- a bunch of young onions
- salt
- two tablespoons of sugar
- a bit of sour salt
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Take a pound of sour grass (sorrel), remove leaves, wash well, cut and squeeze well.
2. Peel three potatoes, mince a bunch of young onions, salt and set on to boil, when boiling add the sour grass and let boil well, add two tablespoons of sugar, and a bit of sour salt, let simmer a bit, afterward add two well-beaten eggs.
3. Do not boil this soup after adding the eggs.
4. This soup is to be eaten cold.
5. It can be kept for some time in jars.
