## Ingredients

- soup stock
- one pint washed peapods
- one tablespoon drippings
- peas
- chopped parsley
- one teaspoon salt
- two teaspoons sugar
- drop dumplings

## Directions

1. Make your soup stock as usual, adding a pint of washed peapods to the soup
2. Heat a tablespoon of drippings, put in the peas, with a little chopped parsley
3. Cover closely and let simmer; keep adding soup stock when dry
4. When the peas are tender put into the strained soup
5. Season with one teaspoon of salt and two teaspoons of sugar
6. Add drop dumplings to this soup before serving
