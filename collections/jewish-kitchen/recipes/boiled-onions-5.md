## Ingredients

- onions
- cold water
- salt
- boiling water
- pepper
- butter
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and cut off the roots
2. drop each into cold water as soon as it is peeled
3. When all are ready, drain and put in a saucepan well covered with boiling water, adding a teaspoon of salt for every quart of water
4. Boil rapidly for ten minutes with the cover partly off
5. drain and return to the fire with fresh water
6. Simmer until tender
7. add pepper and butter and serve, or omit the butter and pepper and pour a cream sauce over the onions
