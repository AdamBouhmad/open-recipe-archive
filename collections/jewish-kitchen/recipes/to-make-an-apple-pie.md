## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 good cooking apples
- 4 lb. of loaf sugar
- cloves
- 1 dozen almonds
- 1 oz. of citron
- 2 tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water
- puff-paste
- powdered sugar
- white of egg
- water

## Directions

1. Peel and core 4 or 5 good cooking apples, boil them with 4 lb. of loaf sugar and a few cloves
2. When done, beat it up to a cream with a fork
3. Blanch and quarter a dozen almonds
4. Cut up 1 oz. of citron in small pieces
5. When cold, add to it 2 tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water
6. Line your dish with puff-paste, cover it over
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, and damp with white of egg and water
8. Bake in a quick oven
