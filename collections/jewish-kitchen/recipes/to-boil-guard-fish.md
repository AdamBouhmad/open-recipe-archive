## Ingredients

- guard fish
- salt
- water
- parsley
- caper or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Boil them in salt and water
2. Turn them round with the tail in the mouth
3. Put them on a fish napkin, garnished with parsley
4. Serve with caper or anchovy sauce
