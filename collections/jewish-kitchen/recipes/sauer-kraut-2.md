## Ingredients

- seven or eight pounds of beef, either brisket or a fillet off the shoulder
- water
- sauer kraut

## Directions

1. Boil about seven or eight pounds of beef either brisket or a fillet off the shoulder in enough water to cover it
2. When it has boiled for one hour, add as much sauer kraut as may be approved
3. Let it stew gently for four hours
4. Serve in a deep dish
5. Remove the fat for a more delicate dish
