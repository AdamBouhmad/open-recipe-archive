## Ingredients

- baker's bread
- butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Slice even slices of baker's bread, not too thin
2. put in a biscuit pan on the top rack of a very hot oven
3. brown nicely on one side, then turn and brown on the other
4. spread with butter, and a little powdered sugar, if desired
5. serve at once
