## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound of marshmallows
- contents of one-half can of pineapple
- one-quarter pound of shelled pecans
- one yolk of egg
- one-half cup of oil
- lemon juice
- two tablespoons of mayonnaise
- four tablespoons of whipped cream
- fresh green lettuce-leaves

## Directions

1. Cut up one-quarter pound of marshmallows into small squares, also contents of one-half can of pineapple
2. Let the marshmallows be mixed with the pineapples quite a while before salad is put together
3. Add to this one-quarter pound of shelled pecans
4. Make a drip mayonnaise of one yolk of egg into which one-half cup of oil is stirred drop by drop
5. Cut this with lemon juice, but do not use any sugar
6. To two tablespoons of mayonnaise, add four tablespoons of whipped cream
7. Serve on fresh, green lettuce-leaves
