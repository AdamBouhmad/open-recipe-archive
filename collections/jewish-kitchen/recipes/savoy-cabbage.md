## Ingredients

- savoy cabbage
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cut off the faded outside leaves and hard part of the stalk
2. Wash the vegetable well
3. Cook in boiling salted water
4. Drain and chop very fine
5. Proceed as with spinach in the foregoing recipe
