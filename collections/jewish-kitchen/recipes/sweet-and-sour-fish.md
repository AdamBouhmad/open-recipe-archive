## Ingredients

- fish
- salt water
- water
- vinegar
- sliced onions
- raisins
- brown sugar
- rye bread
- molasses
- sliced lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place the fish in strong salt water for one hour before cooking
2. Take three parts of water and one of vinegar
3. Put in saucepan with some sliced onions and some raisins
4. Let boil until tender
5. Add brown sugar to taste
6. Add a piece of rye bread from which the crust has been removed
7. Add some molasses
8. Boil the sauce, then place the fish in and let all cook twenty minutes
9. When done, arrange on platter with sliced lemon and chopped parsley
