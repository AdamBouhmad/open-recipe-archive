## Ingredients

- poultry drippings
- onion
- sweetbreads
- salt water
- salt
- pepper
- tomatoes
- soup stock
- string beans
- canned peas
- currants
- drippings
- ginger
- flour
- spaghetti

## Directions

1. Put on some poultry drippings to heat in a saucepan
2. cut up an onion, shredded very fine and then put in the sweetbreads, which have been picked over carefully and lain in salt water an hour before boiling
3. Salt and pepper the sweetbreads before putting in the kettle
4. slice two tomatoes on top and cover up tight and set on the back of stove to simmer slowly
5. Turn once in a while and add a little soup stock
6. Boil one-half cup of string beans, half a can of canned peas, one-half cup of currants, cut up extremely fine, with a tablespoon of drippings, a little salt and ground ginger
7. When the vegetables are tender, add to the simmering sweetbreads
8. Thicken the sauce with a teaspoon of flour
9. Have the sauce boiled down quite thick
10. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until tender
11. Serve with the sweetbreads
