## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- water
- pepper
- goose fat
- bread
- crust

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs
2. Lay them in salt water for about ten minutes and then rub dry with a clean towel
3. Split them down the back and broil over a clear coal fire
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Lay them on a heated platter, grease them liberally with goose fat and cover with a deep platter
6. Toast a piece of bread for each pigeon; removing the crust
7. Dip the toast in boiling water for an instant
8. In serving lay a squab upon a piece of toasted bread
