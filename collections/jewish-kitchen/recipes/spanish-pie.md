## Ingredients

- one pint of cold chicken, duck or any poultry
- thin crust
- sweet red peppers
- thinly sliced sausage
- a glass of claret
- two tablespoons of flour
- thin crust of pastry

## Directions

1. Take one pint of cold chicken, duck or any poultry
2. Cut it into flakes and place it in a pudding dish which has been lined with a thin crust
3. On the layer of meat place a layer of sweet red peppers (seeds removed), cut in slices
4. Next, a layer of thinly sliced sausage, and so on until the dish is full
5. Pour a glass of claret into which have been rubbed two tablespoons of flour over this
6. Cover with a thin crust of pastry, and bake
