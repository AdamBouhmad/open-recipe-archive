## Ingredients

- one pint of dried white peas
- half a pint of dried white beans
- a nice piece of brisket of about eight pounds weight
- three heads of celery
- water
- pepper
- salt
- four large lumps of sugar
- nearly a tea-cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the peas and beans for twelve hours, or longer if very dry
2. Put the brisket in a stew-pan with the peas and beans, and three heads of celery cut in small pieces
3. Add water enough to cover
4. Season with pepper and salt only
5. Let it all stew slowly till the meat is extremely tender and the peas and beans quite soft
6. Add four large lumps of sugar and nearly a tea-cup of vinegar
