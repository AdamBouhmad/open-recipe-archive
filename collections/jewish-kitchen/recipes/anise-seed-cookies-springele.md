## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one and one-half cups of granulated sugar
- two heaping cups of flour
- fourteen drops of anise seed oil

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs thoroughly.
2. Add one and one-half cups of granulated sugar, and beat for thirty minutes.
3. Add two heaping cups of flour and fourteen drops of anise seed oil.
4. Drop from a teaspoon on well-buttered pans.
5. Let them stand from two to three hours in the pans before baking.
6. Bake in a moderate oven.
