## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of suet
- 2 oz. of flour
- a little salt
- pepper
- a little ground ginger
- a little chopped parsley
- a little lemon thyme or marjoram
- a little cold water

## Directions

1. Chop 2 oz. of suet very fine, 2 oz. of flour, a little salt, pepper, a little ground ginger, a little chopped parsley, a little lemon thyme or marjoram
2. Mix it with a little cold water to a stiff paste
3. Divide it into small pieces for the balls
4. Throw them into the boiling soup
5. They require boiling for half an hour, in consequence of the raw suet
