## Ingredients

- one pint of boiled and minced new cabbage
- one-half pint of hot milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one teaspoon of flour
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one-half teaspoon pepper
- one teaspoon finely minced parsley
- sweet paprika

## Directions

1. To one pint of boiled and minced new cabbage add one-half pint of hot milk, one tablespoon of butter, one teaspoon of flour, one-half teaspoon each of salt and pepper, one teaspoon finely minced parsley and a generous dash of sweet paprika.
2. The butter and flour should be creamed together before stirring in.
3. Let simmer for about ten minutes, stirring occasionally to keep from burning.
4. Serve hot on toasted bread.
