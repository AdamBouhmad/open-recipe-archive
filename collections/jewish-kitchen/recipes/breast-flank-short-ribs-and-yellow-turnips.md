## Ingredients

- small ribs
- water
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- yellow turnip
- potatoes
- sugar

## Directions

1. Get the small ribs and put on with plenty of water, an onion, pepper and salt
2. After boiling about one and one-half hours add a large yellow turnip cut in small pieces
3. One-half hour before serving add six potatoes cut in small pieces
4. Water must be added as necessary
5. A little sugar will improve flavor
6. As it simmers the turnip will soften and give the whole dish the appearance of a stew
