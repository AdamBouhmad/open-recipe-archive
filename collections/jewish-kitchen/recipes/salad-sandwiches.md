## Ingredients

- mustard and cress
- eggs
- made mustard
- Tarragon vinegar
- white pepper
- salt
- oil
- bread
- boiled beetroot
- dry double parsley

## Directions

1. Use only mustard and cress as lettuces contain too much water
2. Wash and dry the salad in a cloth
3. Boil 3 or 4 eggs very hard
4. Take the yolks and pound them well in a mortar
5. Put in a dessertspoon of made mustard
6. Add 3 spoonfuls of Tarragon vinegar, a little white pepper and salt
7. When well mixed in the mortar, rub it through a sieve in a basin
8. Rub in 3 tablespoonfuls of the best oil
9. If not thick enough add more yolk of egg until it is thick
10. Lay it on the bread
11. Put in some of the salad with a piece of boiled beetroot to each sandwich
12. See that the salad does not hang out of the bread
13. Make the salad sandwich last of all
14. Garnish with dry double parsley, not too many on each dish
