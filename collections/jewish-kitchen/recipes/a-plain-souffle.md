## Ingredients

- six ounces of rice-flour, arrowroot, or tapioca
- half a pint of milk
- essence of almond
- lemon peel or orange-flour water
- white sugar
- beaten yolks of five eggs
- whisked whites of five eggs

## Directions

1. Mix well together six ounces of rice-flour, arrowroot, or tapioca with half a pint of milk flavoured with essence of almond and lemon peel, or orange-flour water
2. Let it thicken over the fire, stirring to keep it smooth
3. Sweeten with white sugar
4. Add the beaten yolks of five eggs
5. Add the whisked whites at the moment of placing the souffle into the oven
6. Bake in a souffle dish or cake-tin lined with fringed paper
7. Twist a napkin around it when brought to table
