## Ingredients

- about six pieces of celery root
- salt
- pepper
- onion powder
- one teaspoon of home-made mustard
- plenty of mayonnaise
- caviar or sardellen butter
- slice of beets
- lettuce leaf

## Directions

1. Boil about six pieces of celery root
2. When soft, peel and mash
3. Season with salt, pepper, a little onion powder, a teaspoon of home-made mustard and plenty of mayonnaise
4. Shape into pyramids, put mayonnaise on the top of the pyramid, and on top of that either a little well-seasoned caviar or some sardellen butter shaped in a pastry-bag
5. Serve on a slice of beets and a lettuce leaf
