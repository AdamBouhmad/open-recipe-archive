## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two cups of flour
- one cup of white flour
- three-quarters cup of corn-meal
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- pinch of salt
- boiling water
- sugar
- tepid milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites of two eggs separately
2. Add to this two cups of flour, of which one is a full cup of white and three-quarters of the corn-meal
3. Sift flour three times
4. Put into this flour two teaspoons of baking-powder, together with a pinch of salt
5. Mix the prepared flour with a little boiling water, adding the eggs
6. A little sugar may be put in, if desired
7. Add enough tepid milk to make the mixture into a batter
8. Pour into your pans or, if corn-bread is desired, into the plain pan thin
9. Bake in a quick oven
10. Butter your pan well, or the small gem-pans, and heat the pan a little
