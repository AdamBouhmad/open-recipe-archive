## Ingredients

- sardines
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice or vinegar
- melted butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and bones from the sardines.
2. Rub to a paste, adding an equal quantity of chopped hard-boiled eggs, seasoned with salt, cayenne, lemon juice or vinegar.
3. Moisten with melted butter and spread between slices of bread.
