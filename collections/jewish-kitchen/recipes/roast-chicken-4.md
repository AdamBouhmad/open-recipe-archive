## Ingredients

- chicken
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- chicken-fat
- hot water

## Directions

1. Stuff and truss a chicken, season with pepper and salt and dredge with flour.
2. Put in a roasting-pan with two or three tablespoons of chicken-fat if the chicken is not especially fat.
3. When heated add hot water and baste frequently.
4. The oven should be hot and the time necessary for a large chicken will be about an hour and a half.
5. When done, remove the chicken, pour off the grease and make a brown sauce in the pan.
