## Ingredients

- juice of four lemons
- twelve tablespoons of sugar
- eight cups of water
- one cup of maraschino liquor
- a few cherries

## Directions

1. Take the juice of four lemons, twelve tablespoons of sugar, eight cups of water, one cup of maraschino liquor and a few cherries.
