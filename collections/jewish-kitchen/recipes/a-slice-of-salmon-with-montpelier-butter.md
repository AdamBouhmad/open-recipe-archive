## Ingredients

- cold salmon slice
- 8 anchovies
- 1/4 lb. fresh butter
- 2 shalots
- 1 tooth of garlic
- tarragon
- fennel
- 4 egg yolks boiled hard
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Put 8 anchovies well washed and boned in a mortar with 1/4 lb. of fresh butter
2. Put into boiling water 2 shalots, 1 tooth of garlic, a little tarragon, a little fennel
3. Take them out and dip them in cold water
4. When quite cold put them in the mortar with the rest
5. Add to them the yolks of 4 eggs boiled hard, with salt and a little cayenne
6. Pound it all well and rub it through a sieve
7. Put it over the slice of cold salmon
