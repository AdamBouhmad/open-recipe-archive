## Ingredients

- one drachm of soda
- flour
- eggs
- butter
- fruit

## Directions

1. Mix with the common cake ingredients one drachm of soda, which should be rubbed in with the flour
2. This is reckoned a wholesome cake, and half the quantity of eggs are required
3. It may be rendered a fine rich cake by increasing the quantity of eggs, butter, and fruit
