## Ingredients

- one large, solid head of cabbage
- one quarter of a loaf of stale bread
- two tablespoons of drippings
- one large-sized onion
- one pound of chopped beef
- two eggs
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- sage

## Directions

1. Take a large, solid head of cabbage; take off the large top leaves, and scoop out the centre of the cabbage so as to leave the outside leaves intact for refilling.
2. Chop your cabbage fine as for slaw.
3. Take a quarter of a loaf of stale bread, soak it in water and squeeze very dry.
4. Heat two tablespoons of drippings in a spider, add a large-sized onion chopped fine, do not let the onion get too brown.
5. Add the bread, one pound of chopped beef well minced and the chopped cabbage and let it get well heated.
6. Take off stove and add two eggs, pepper, salt, nutmeg, a little parsley and a little sage, season very highly.
7. Put this all back in the cabbage, and cover this with the large leaves.
8. Put into small bread-pan and bake for two hours, put just enough water in to keep the pan from burning; don't baste.
