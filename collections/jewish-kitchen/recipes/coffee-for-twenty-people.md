## Ingredients

- 1 pound coffee finely ground
- 1 egg
- cold water
- 7 quarts boiling water
- cream

## Directions

1. Add and mix one pound of coffee finely ground, with one egg and enough cold water to thoroughly moisten it
2. Cover and let stand several hours
3. Place in thin bag and drop in seven quarts of boiling water
4. Boil five minutes
5. Let stand ten minutes
6. Add cream to coffee and serve
