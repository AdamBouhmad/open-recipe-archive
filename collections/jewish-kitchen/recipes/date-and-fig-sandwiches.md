## Ingredients

- equal quantities of dates and figs
- blanched almonds
- orange juice
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Wash equal quantities of dates and figs and stone the dates.
2. Add blanched almonds in quantity about one-fourth of the entire bulk.
3. Run the whole mixture through a food chopper.
4. Moisten with orange juice and press tightly into baking-powder tins.
5. When ready to use, dip the box in hot water and turn out the mixture.
6. Slice and place between thin slices of buttered bread.
