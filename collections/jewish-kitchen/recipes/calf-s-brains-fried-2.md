## Ingredients

- brains
- rolled cracker flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean as described in calf's brains cooked sour
2. Wipe dry
3. Roll in rolled cracker flour
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Fry as you would cutlets
