## Ingredients

- a half box of raisins and currants
- one-quarter pound of citron
- one-quarter pound of suet
- two eggs
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- a wineglass of brandy
- two cups of cider
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one teaspoon of ground cloves
- flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder

## Directions

1. Chop a half box of raisins and currants, one-quarter pound of citron, and one-quarter pound of suet very fine
2. Mix with two eggs, one and one-half cups of sugar, a wineglass of brandy, two cups of cider, one teaspoon of cinnamon and ground cloves
3. When all these are well mixed add enough flour with a teaspoon of baking-powder in it to thicken well
4. Cook in a greased mold and allow to steam for three hours
