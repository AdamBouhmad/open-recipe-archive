## Ingredients

- the feet of a fowl
- the neck and gizzard
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- parsley
- a breakfast-cup of water
- flour
- a little browning
- a small quantity of any store sauce

## Directions

1. Take the feet, wash them, cut them small, also the neck and gizzard
2. Season them with pepper and salt, onion, and parsley
3. Let them simmer gently for some time, in about a breakfast-cup of water
4. Strain
5. Thicken with flour
6. Add a little browning, and if liked, a small quantity of any store sauce at hand
