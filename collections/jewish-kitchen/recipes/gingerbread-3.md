## Ingredients

- one cup of molasses
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of soda
- one tablespoon of butter
- one or two eggs
- one teaspoon of ginger
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. To one cup of molasses add one cup of milk, sour or sweet
2. Dissolve one teaspoon of soda in the milk
3. Add one tablespoon of butter, one or two eggs, one teaspoon of ginger and one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
4. Add enough sifted flour to make a light batter
5. Bake in a shallow pan
