## Ingredients

- two cups white figs
- two cups of sugar
- sponge cake or lady fingers
- whipped cream
- candied cherries or angelica

## Directions

1. Soak two cups white figs overnight.
2. In the morning boil slowly until tender, add two cups of sugar and boil until a thick syrup is formed.
3. Line a dish with sponge cake or lady fingers.
4. Pour the figs in the centre and cover with whipped cream that has been sweetened and flavored.
5. Decorate with candied cherries or angelica.
