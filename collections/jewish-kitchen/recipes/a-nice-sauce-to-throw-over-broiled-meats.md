## Ingredients

- a little salad-oil
- a table-spoonful of vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- the yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Beat up a little salad-oil with a table-spoonful of vinegar, mustard, pepper and salt
2. Stir in the yolk of an egg
3. This sauce should be highly seasoned
