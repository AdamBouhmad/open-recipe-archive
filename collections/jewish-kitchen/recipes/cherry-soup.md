## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of sago
- one cup of boiling water
- one quart of large red or black cherries
- one cup of claret
- one tablespoon of broken cinnamon
- one-fourth cup of sugar
- one-half lemon
- yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Cook two tablespoons of sago in one cup of boiling water until tender, add more as water boils down.
2. Put one quart of large red or black cherries, one cup of claret, one tablespoon of broken cinnamon, one-fourth cup of sugar, and one-half lemon sliced fine, up to boil and let boil fifteen minutes; add the cooked sago, let boil up and pour very gradually over the well-beaten yolks of two eggs.
3. Serve cold.
