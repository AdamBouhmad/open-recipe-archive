## Ingredients

- goose
- salt

## Directions

1. Render the fat of goose
2. Remove and cut the skin into small pieces
3. The scraps, when brown, shriveled and crisp, are then Greben, and are served hot or cold
4. When fat is nearly done or clear, add the breast and legs of goose, previously salted
5. Boil in the fat until tender and browned
6. Place meat in crock and pour the clear, hot fat over it to cover
7. Cool
8. Cover crock with plate and stone and keep in a cool, dry place
9. Will keep for months
10. When ready to serve, take out meat, heat, and drain off fat
