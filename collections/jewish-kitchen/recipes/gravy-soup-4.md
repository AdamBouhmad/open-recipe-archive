## Ingredients

- about three quarts of any strong stock
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- a carrot
- turnip
- a head of celery
- Vermicelli, maccaroni, or thin slices of carrot and small sippets of fried bread cut in fancy shapes

## Directions

1. Take about three quarts of any strong stock, seasoned with a bunch of sweet herbs, a carrot, turnip, and a head of celery, which must not be served in the soup
2. Serve with Vermicelli, maccaroni, or thin slices of carrot and small sippets of fried bread cut in fancy shapes
