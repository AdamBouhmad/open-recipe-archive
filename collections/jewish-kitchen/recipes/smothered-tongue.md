## Ingredients

- tongue
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- drippings

## Directions

1. Scald tongue, and then skin.
2. Season well with salt and pepper and slice an onion over it.
3. Let it stand overnight.
4. Put some drippings in a covered iron pot, and then the tongue, with whatever juice the seasoning drew.
5. Cover closely and let it cook slowly until tender — about three hours.
