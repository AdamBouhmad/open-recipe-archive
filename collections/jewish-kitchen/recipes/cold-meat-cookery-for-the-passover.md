## Ingredients

- cold meat
- onion
- parsley
- a tooth of garlic
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 2 eggs
- potato crust

## Directions

1. If you have any cold meat left, either boiled, roasted or stewed, chop it very fine
2. Mix with onion, parsley, a tooth of garlic, marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
3. Mix it with 2 eggs
4. Make the potato crust as for pigeon pie
5. Put a layer of potatoes at the bottom
6. Put in the minced meat
7. Cover over and bake a nice colour
