## Ingredients

- three cups of warmed-over or freshly made coffee
- two tablespoons of powdered sugar
- one-half teaspoon of vanilla
- one cup of milk or cream
- one tablespoon of cornstarch
- cold water

## Directions

1. Put three cups of warmed-over or freshly made coffee in a small casserole, add two tablespoons of powdered sugar, one-half teaspoon of vanilla
2. When at boiling point (do not let it boil), add one cup of milk or cream
3. Add one tablespoon of cornstarch which has been moistened with cold water
4. Stir in while cooking till it is smooth and glossy
5. When the cake is cool, pour mixture over the layers
