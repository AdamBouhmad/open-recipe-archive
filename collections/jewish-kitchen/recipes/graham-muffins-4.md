## Ingredients

- one cup of Graham flour
- one cup of wheat flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- one-half cup of sugar
- one well-beaten egg
- one and one-half cups of milk

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of Graham flour, one cup of wheat flour, one-half teaspoon of salt, two teaspoons of baking-powder
2. Add to this one tablespoon of melted butter creamed with one-half cup of sugar and one well-beaten egg
3. Moisten with one and one-half cups of milk
4. Beat all well
5. Bake in muffin-tins in moderately hot oven one-half hour
