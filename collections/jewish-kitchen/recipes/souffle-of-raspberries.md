## Ingredients

- pint of raspberries
- rind of a lemon
- juice of 1/2 a lemon
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 3 stale sponge cakes
- snow of 3 eggs
- 2 oz. of veal fat
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rub through a sieve a pint of raspberries
2. Grate the rind of a lemon and the juice of 1/2 a lemon
3. Add 1 lb. of powdered sugar
4. Let it simmer till it thickens a little
5. Turn it out to get cold
6. Beat up the yolks of 8 eggs
7. Grate 3 stale sponge cakes
8. Beat the eggs and cakes together
9. Add the snow of 3 eggs slowly
10. Mix in 2 oz. of melted veal fat
11. Grease a mould and sift sugar over
12. Bake in a slow oven
