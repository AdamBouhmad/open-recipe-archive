## Ingredients

- berries
- sugar (1 pound per pound of fruit)
- red, black, and white cherries
- bananas
- pineapples
- oranges
- peaches
- 1 pint pure alcohol per 2 quarts berries and 2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Pick two quarts of the largest and soundest berries in the market and lay them in a new and perfectly clean two-gallon stone jar and cover with two pounds of the finest granulated sugar
2. Stone as many pounds of red, black, and white cherries as you wish to use, and add the same quantity of sugar
3. You may also use bananas, pineapples or oranges
4. Seed the latter carefully
5. Be sure to weigh all the fruit, and allow one pound of sugar to every additional pound of fruit
6. Pour over the fruit a pint of pure alcohol
7. Tie up the jar with thick paper
8. In season add peaches
