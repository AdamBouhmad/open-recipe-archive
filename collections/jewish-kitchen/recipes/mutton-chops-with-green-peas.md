## Ingredients

- 2 lbs mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- onion
- 3 or 4 lumps of sugar
- flour
- chopped mint
- parsley
- cold water

## Directions

1. Lay the chops in a stewpan with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, an onion chopped, and 3 or 4 lumps of sugar
2. Let them cook till tender
3. Rub in a basin a spoonful of flour, some chopped mint and parsley, and a little cold water
4. Skim off the fat
5. Put in the sauce when the mutton is done
