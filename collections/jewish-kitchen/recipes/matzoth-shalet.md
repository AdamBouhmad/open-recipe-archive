## Ingredients

- four soaked matzoth
- nine eggs
- one cup of sugar
- two grated apples
- one and one-half cups of seeded raisins
- one tablespoon of cinnamon
- grated rind of an orange or a lemon
- a few pounded almonds
- half a pound of rendered fat

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar, eggs, and cinnamon until light
2. Add all the ingredients, except the matzoth, mixing well
3. Drain the matzoth, gradually adding them to the mixture, beating until very light
4. Melt half a pound of rendered fat into the dish for baking, and then pour in the mixture
5. Bake in a moderately hot oven for one and one-fourth hours
6. Serve hot with wine, fruit, or prune sauce
