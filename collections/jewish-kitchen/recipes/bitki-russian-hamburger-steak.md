## Ingredients

- two cups of clear beef chopped
- two cups of bread crumbs
- water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take two cups of clear beef chopped and two cups of bread crumbs that have been soaked in a little water, leaving them quite moist.
2. Mix thoroughly with the beef.
3. Season with pepper and salt and shape into individual cakes.
4. Fry as directed for Hamburger Steak.
