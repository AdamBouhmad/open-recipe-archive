## Ingredients

- veal shoulder or breast
- salt
- ground ginger
- flour
- slices of onion
- goose-fat or drippings

## Directions

1. Choose the shoulder and breast of veal, always buy veal that is fat and white
2. Wash and then dry
3. Rub it well with salt, a very little ground ginger, and dredge it well with flour
4. Lay in roasting-pan and put slices of onion on top with a few tablespoons of goose-fat or drippings
5. Cover tightly and roast, allowing twenty minutes to the pound and baste frequently
6. Veal must be well done
