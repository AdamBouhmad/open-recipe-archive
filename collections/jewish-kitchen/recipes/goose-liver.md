## Ingredients

- goose liver
- fine salt
- pepper
- goose-fat
- a few cloves
- a little onion
- flour
- lemon

## Directions

1. If very large cut in half
2. Dry well on a clean cloth, after having lain in salted water for an hour
3. Season with fine salt and pepper
4. Dredge the liver with flour before frying
5. Fry in very hot goose-fat and add a few cloves
6. While frying cut up a little onion very fine and add
7. Cover closely and smother until you wish to serve
8. Turn occasionally
9. Serve with a slice of lemon on each piece of liver
