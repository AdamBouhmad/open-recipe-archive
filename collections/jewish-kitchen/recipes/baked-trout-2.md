## Ingredients

- trout
- mashed potatoes
- onions
- lemon juice
- cayenne
- salt
- white pepper
- bread raspings
- butter or oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash it very clean and dry it well
2. Make a nice stuffing of mashed potatoes, season the same as for baked pike
3. Sew it up
4. Take your fish tin and slice some onions, put them in the dish
5. Let them fry in the oven a light brown
6. Take out the dish and squeeze the juice of a lemon in with a little cayenne, salt and white pepper
7. Put over the salmon some raspings
8. Put in the baking dish ½ lb. of butter or ½ pint of oil
9. As it cooks keep basting it with a spoon
10. Have some parsley chopped and when you lay the trout in the dish throw over the gravy and the chopped parsley
