## Ingredients

- one cake of compressed yeast
- two level teaspoons of brown sugar
- two cups of lukewarm water
- one cup of milk
- two cups of buckwheat
- one cup of sifted white flour
- one and one-half teaspoons of salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast and brown sugar in lukewarm water and scalded and cooled milk
2. Add buckwheat and sifted white flour gradually
3. Add salt
4. Beat until smooth
5. Cover and set aside in a warm place, free from draft, to rise about one hour
6. When light stir well
7. Bake on a hot griddle
