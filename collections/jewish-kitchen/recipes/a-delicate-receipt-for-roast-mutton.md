## Ingredients

- mutton joint
- cold water
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Put the joint in a saucepan, cover it with cold water
2. let it boil for half an hour
3. have the spit and fire quite ready
4. remove the meat from the saucepan, and place it immediately down to roast
5. baste it well
6. dredge it repeatedly with flour, and sprinkle with salt
