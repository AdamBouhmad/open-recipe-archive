## Ingredients

- a large cup of cream
- essence of almonds
- cinnamon
- the yolk of three eggs
- powdered sugar
- beaten almonds

## Directions

1. Boil a large cup of cream and flavor with essence of almonds and cinnamon
2. Mix with it the yolk of three eggs, carefully beaten and strained, stirring one way to keep it smooth
3. Place it on a dish in small heaps
4. Strew over powdered sugar and beaten almonds and brown with a salamander
