## Ingredients

- small pieces of any vegetables
- pieces of smoked or salt beef
- any cold poultry, roast beef or mutton
- two or three quarts of water
- whole peppers
- allspice
- mace
- Jamaica pickles
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut small pieces of any vegetables
2. Add pieces of smoked or salt beef and also of any cold poultry, roast beef or mutton
3. Stew all these together in two or three quarts of water, according to the quantity of meat
4. Season highly with whole peppers, allspice, mace, Jamaica pickles, and salt
5. Thoroughly stew it
6. Serve without straining in a tureen
