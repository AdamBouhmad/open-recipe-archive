## Ingredients

- rice flour or arrow root
- eggs
- milk or cream
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make a batter as for a fondu, but use rice flour or arrow root instead of potatoe flour
2. Add the egg in the same manner as for a fondu
3. Pour the mixture into small paper trays fringed round the top
4. The mixture should only half fill the trays or cases
