## Ingredients

- one cup of yellow cornmeal
- three cups of cold water
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-half teaspoon of pepper
- one and one-half cups of grated cheese
- hot vegetable oil
- white or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Place one cup of yellow cornmeal and three cups of cold water in a double boiler
2. Add one teaspoon of salt, one-half teaspoon of pepper and cook for forty minutes
3. While still hot add one and one-half cups of grated cheese to the mixture and heat until it melts
4. Turn the mixture into a greased bowl and allow it to set
5. The meal may be sliced an inch thick or cut with a biscuit cutter and then fried in hot vegetable oil
6. Serve with white or tomato sauce as desired
