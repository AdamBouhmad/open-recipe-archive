## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1 glass of orange-flower water
- 1 lb. of grated cocoa-nut
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 lb. of loaf sugar with 1/2 pint of water and 1 glass of orange-flower water
2. Have ready 1 lb. of grated cocoa-nut
3. When the sugar has become a thick syrup, stir in the cocoa-nut
4. Let it boil till it looks quite a preserve
5. Part it in small glasses with a little cinnamon on top
