## Ingredients

- one glass water
- one-half glass vinegar
- two tablespoons of brown sugar
- one-half dozen cloves
- one-half teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- one onion
- one lemon
- one goblet of red wine
- one dozen raisins
- one tablespoon of pounded almonds
- a small piece Leb-kuchen or ginger cake
- four eggs
- fish (ghad or trout)

## Directions

1. Put on to boil in fish kettle one glass water, one-half glass vinegar, two tablespoons of brown sugar, one-half dozen cloves, one-half teaspoon of ground cinnamon, and one onion cut in round slices
2. Boil thoroughly, then strain and add to it one lemon cut in round slices, one goblet of red wine, one dozen raisins, one tablespoon of pounded almonds
3. Put on stove again, and when it comes to a boil, add fish that has been cut up and salted
4. Cook until done, remove fish to a platter
5. To the liquor add a small piece Leb-kuchen or ginger cake, and stir in the well-beaten yolks of four eggs
6. Stir carefully or it will curdle
7. If not sweet enough add more sugar
8. Pour over fish
