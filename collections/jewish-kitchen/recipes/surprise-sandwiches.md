## Ingredients

- orange marmalade
- pecan nuts
- cream cheese
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Take orange marmalade, pecan nuts and cream cheese in equal quantities.
2. After mixing thoroughly spread on thin slices of buttered bread.
