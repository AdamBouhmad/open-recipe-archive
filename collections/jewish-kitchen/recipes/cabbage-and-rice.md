## Ingredients

- fine summer white cabbage
- large cup of rice
- water
- large piece of butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scald till tender a fine summer white cabbage
2. Chop it up small, and put it into a stew-pan, with a large cup of rice, also previously scalded
3. Add a little water, a large piece of butter, salt and pepper
4. Let it stew gently till thoroughly done, stirring from time to time, and adding water and butter to prevent its getting too thick
5. There should be no gravy in the dish when served
