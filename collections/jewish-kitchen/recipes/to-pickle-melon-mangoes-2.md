## Ingredients

- melons
- strong brine
- spices
- mustard-seed
- garlic
- packthread
- spiced vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the melons in half, remove the pulpy part and the seeds.
2. Soak the halves for a week in strong brine.
3. Fill them with the usual spices, mustard-seed and garlic.
4. Tie them together with packthread.
5. Put them in jars, and pour over boiling spiced vinegar.
