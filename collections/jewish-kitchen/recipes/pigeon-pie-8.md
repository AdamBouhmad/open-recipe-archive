## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- pepper
- fat
- onion
- hard-boiled eggs
- minced parsley
- chicken fat
- flour
- rich paste
- crust

## Directions

1. Prepare as many pigeons as you wish to bake in your pie
2. Salt and pepper
3. Melt some fat in a stew-pan, and cut up an onion in it
4. When hot, place in the pigeons and stew until tender
5. Line a deep pie plate with a rich paste
6. Cut up the pigeons, lay them in, with hard-boiled eggs chopped up and minced parsley
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Put flakes of chicken fat rolled in flour here and there
9. Pour over the gravy the pigeons were stewed in
10. Cover with a crust
11. Bake slowly until done
