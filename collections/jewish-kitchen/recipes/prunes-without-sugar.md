## Ingredients

- prunes
- water

## Directions

1. Wash prunes thoroughly
2. Pour boiling water over same and let them stand for ten minutes
3. Drain and pour boiling water over them again
4. Put in sealed jar
5. See that prunes are all covered with water
6. Ready for use after forty-eight hours
