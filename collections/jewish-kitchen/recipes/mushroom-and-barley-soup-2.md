## Ingredients

- one quart hot bouillon
- one quarter pound barley, boiled in water
- one ounce dried mushrooms, thoroughly washed and cut in pieces
- one onion
- one carrot
- one bayleaf
- parsley
- dill
- meat from the bouillon

## Directions

1. Add the quarter pound barley which has been boiled in water to the hot bouillon
2. Add the dried mushrooms which have been thoroughly washed and cut in pieces, onion, carrot, bayleaf, parsley and dill
3. Boil all these
4. When the vegetables are nearly tender, remove from soup
5. Add the meat from the bouillon, cut up in small pieces
6. Let soup come to a boil and serve
