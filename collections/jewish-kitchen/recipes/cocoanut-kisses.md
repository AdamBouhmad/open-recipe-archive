## Ingredients

- white of one egg
- one-half cup of sugar
- vanilla
- one cup of shredded cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat the white of one egg
2. Add one-half cup of sugar with a flavoring of vanilla
3. Fold in one cup of shredded cocoanut
4. Drop by teaspoonfuls on a well-greased baking-pan, inverted
5. Bake for about ten or twelve minutes in a slow oven
6. Remove from pan when cookies are cold
