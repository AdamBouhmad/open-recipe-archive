## Ingredients

- six carrots
- some onions
- a lettuce
- a few sweet herbs
- good broth
- the crumb of a French roll

## Directions

1. Grate six carrots and chop some onions with a lettuce
2. Add a few sweet herbs
3. Put them all into a stewpan with enough of good broth to moisten the whole
4. Add broth occasionally
5. When nearly done, put in the crumb of a French roll
6. When soaked, strain the whole through a sieve
7. Serve hot in a tureen
