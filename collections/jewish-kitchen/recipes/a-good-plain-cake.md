## Ingredients

- 1 quartern of roll dough
- 1/2 lb. of currants well washed and dried
- 1/2 lb. raisins stoned, or 3/4 lb. sultanas
- 1/4 oz. of mixed spice
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- 1/2 lb. butter
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all well together
2. Beat it for half an hour
3. Butter well your tin
4. Bake in a good oven one hour and a half
