## Ingredients

- 6 fresh globe artichokes
- 1 pint of oil
- 2 teeth of garlic
- 1 tea-cup of boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- a little dried saffron
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Take 6 fresh ones, cut off the stalks, peel them, and cut them in pieces
2. Take off all the hard leaves, cut them in quarters, cut out the chokes, wash them well
3. Put in a stewpan 1 pint of oil, cut in 2 teeth of garlic, let it fry a light brown
4. Take out the garlic, put in a tea-cup of boiling water with salt and pepper
5. Lay in the artichokes with a little dried saffron, let them stew till quite tender
6. Add the juice of a lemon, mix it well with the liquor
7. Dish them up in a deep dish. To be eaten cold
