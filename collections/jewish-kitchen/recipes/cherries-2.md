## Ingredients

- cherries
- half a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as you would for preserving.
2. After putting the cherries into the syrup do not let them boil more than five minutes; then fill your cans to overflowing.
3. Seal immediately and then screw tighter as they grow cold.
4. Remove the little bag of stones which you have boiled with the syrup.
