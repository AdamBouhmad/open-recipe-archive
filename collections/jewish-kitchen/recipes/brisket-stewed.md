## Ingredients

- about six or seven pounds of brisket of beef
- water
- a little spice
- turnips
- carrots
- mushrooms
- cauliflowers
- or other vegetables in season

## Directions

1. Take about six or seven pounds of brisket of beef
2. Place it in a stewpan with only enough water to cover it
3. Season with a little spice tied in a bag
4. When the meat is tender and the spices sufficiently extracted to make the gravy rich and strong, part of it must be removed to another saucepan
5. Have ready a variety of vegetables cut into small shapes, such as turnips, carrots, mushrooms, cauliflowers, or whatever may be in season
6. Stew them gently till tender in the gravy
7. Glaze the meat and pour the gravy in the dish
8. Arrange the vegetables round
