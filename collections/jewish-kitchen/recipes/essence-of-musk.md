## Ingredients

- one dram of musk
- one dram of pounded loaf sugar
- six ounces of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Mix one dram of musk with the same quantity of pounded loaf sugar
2. add six ounces of spirits of wine
3. shake together and pour off for use
