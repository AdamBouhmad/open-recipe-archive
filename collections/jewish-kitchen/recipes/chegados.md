## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- half a pint of water
- a moderately sized cocoa nut
- rose water
- the beaten yolks of six eggs
- thin nouilles pastry
- light brown baking

## Directions

1. Clarify a pound of sugar in half a pint of water
2. Peel and grate a moderately sized cocoa nut and add it to the syrup
3. Let it simmer till perfectly soft, putting rose water occasionally to prevent its becoming too dry
4. Stir it continually to prevent burning
5. Let it cool and mix it with the beaten yolks of six eggs
6. Make a thin nouilles pastry and cut it into rounds of the size of a tea-cup
7. Pinch up the edges deep enough to form a shape and fill them with the sweet meat
8. Bake of a light brown
