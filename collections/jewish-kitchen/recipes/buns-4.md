## Ingredients

- tea roll dough
- egg
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make same as tea rolls
2. When well risen mold into small, round buns
3. place in well-greased pans, one inch apart
4. Cover, set aside to rise until light — about one hour
5. Brush with egg, diluted with water
6. bake twenty minutes
7. Just before removing from the oven, brush with sugar moistened with a little water
