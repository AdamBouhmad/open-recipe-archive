## Ingredients

- pineapple
- sugar
- orange
- leaves or small fruit

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple
2. Dig out all the eyes
3. Cut from the core downward, or chop in a chopping-bowl
4. Set on ice until ready to serve
5. Sugar the fruit well
6. Form into a mound in a dish
7. Garnish the base well with leaves or small fruit of any kind
8. Squeeze the juice of one orange over all
