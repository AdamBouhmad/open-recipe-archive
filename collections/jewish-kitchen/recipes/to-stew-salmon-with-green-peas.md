## Ingredients

- pint of very young green peas
- chopped onion
- 2 spoonfuls of oil
- salt
- pepper
- 2 lumps of loaf sugar
- 1/2 pint of water
- salmon slices
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- lemon juice
- chopped mint
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and drain well a pint of very young green peas
2. Fry in a fish stewpan a chopped onion with 2 spoonfuls of oil
3. When fried, put salt, pepper, 2 lumps of loaf sugar, with 1/2 pint of water
4. Let it cook well
5. When half done lay in the slices of salmon
6. Rub in a basin 1 tablespoonful of flour with the juice of a lemon, a little chopped mint and parsley
7. Turn it into the stewpan
8. Dish up salmon in the centre of the dish with the peas all round and some of the juice over the salmon
