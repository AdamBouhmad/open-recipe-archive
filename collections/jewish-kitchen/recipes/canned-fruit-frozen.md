## Ingredients

- can of pears
- ice and salt
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Without opening, pack a can of pears in ice and salt, as for ice-cream
2. Let it remain for three or four hours
3. When taken out, cut the can open around the middle
4. If frozen very hard, wrap around with a towel dipped in hot water
5. The contents can then be clipped out in perfect rounds
6. Cut into slices and serve with a spoonful of whipped cream on each slice
