## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 good sized tomatoes
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- steak

## Directions

1. Cut 3 or 4 good sized tomatoes and put them in a stewpan with an onion
2. Add salt and pepper
3. Put in the steak
4. As soon as it is tender take out the steak
5. Rub the tomatoes and onion through a sieve
6. Put back the steak in the saucepan and throw over the sauce
