## Ingredients

- bullock's heart
- large onion
- small bunch of sage
- pepper
- salt
- soaked bread
- suet
- egg
- gravy
- half pound of rice
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Wash well, after it has been soaked, every particle of blood
2. Dry it well on a clean coarse cloth
3. Fill it with the same stuffing as the poor man's goose
4. Put it down to roast, baste it well
5. It will take 1 hour and 1/4 to roast
6. Pour over it when dished up a good gravy
7. Boil a half pound of rice with salt, pepper, and a little dried saffron
8. Drain it well, make a bed of it in the dish
9. Lay on it the heart
10. Serve it up very hot
