## Ingredients

- 1 quartern of dough
- 1 lb. of sifted sugar
- the grated rind of a lemon
- 1 oz. of seeds, carraway or aniseed
- 1 pint of the best oil
- a small piece of bread or a piece of the dough
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the oil first with a small piece of bread or a piece of the dough
2. Let it cool before you put in the eggs
3. Beat the eggs well before you add them
4. Oil the tin
5. Bake in a good oven till done
