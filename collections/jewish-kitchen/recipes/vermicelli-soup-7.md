## Ingredients

- shin of beef or any other part preferred
- a handful of vermicelli
- vegetables
- herbs

## Directions

1. Make a fine strong stock from the shin of beef or any other part preferred
2. A short time before serving, add a handful of vermicelli, which should be broken so that it may be in pieces of convenient length
3. The stock should be more or less flavoured with vegetables and herbs according to taste
