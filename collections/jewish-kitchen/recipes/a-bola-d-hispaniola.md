## Ingredients

- one pound and a half of flour
- three spoonsful of yeast
- two ounces of fresh butter
- one table spoonful of essence of lemon
- eight eggs
- half a tea-cup full of water
- a quarter of a pound of citron
- three quarters of a pound of orange and lemon peel
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- powdered sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Take one pound and a half of flour, with three spoonsful of yeast, two ounces of fresh butter, one table spoonful of essence of lemon, eight eggs, and half a tea-cup full of water
2. Make it into a light dough
3. Set it to rise for about an hour
4. Roll it out and cut it into three pieces
5. Have previously ready, a quarter of a pound of citron, and three quarters of a pound of orange and lemon peel, cut in thin slices, mixed with powdered sugar and cinnamon
6. Form the Bola with the pieces of dough, layers of the fruit being placed between
7. Do not bake in a tin
8. Strew powdered sweet almonds and sugar over it before baking
