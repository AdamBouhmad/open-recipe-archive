## Ingredients

- 1 quart claret
- 1 pint water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon whole cloves
- 1 teaspoon whole cinnamon
- lemon rind

## Directions

1. Mix one quart claret, one pint water, two cups of sugar, one-half teaspoon of whole cloves, one teaspoon of whole cinnamon, lemon rind cut thin and in small pieces
2. Boil steadily for fifteen minutes and serve hot
