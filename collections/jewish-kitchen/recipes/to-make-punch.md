## Ingredients

- two fine fresh lemons
- sugar lumps
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- one pint and a half of very hot water
- a quarter of a pint of rum
- a quarter of a pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Rub off the outer peel of two fine fresh lemons upon a few lumps of sugar
2. Put the sugar into a bowl with four ounces of powdered sugar
3. Press the juice of the lemons upon it
4. Pour over one pint and a half of very hot water that has not boiled
5. Add a quarter of a pint of rum and the same quantity of brandy
6. Stir well together and strain it
7. Let it stand a few minutes before it is drank
