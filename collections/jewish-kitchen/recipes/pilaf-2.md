## Ingredients

- two cups of water
- juice of two tomatoes
- a pinch of salt
- one cup of rice
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Put two cups of water on to boil
2. Add juice of two tomatoes and a pinch of salt
3. When boiling, add one cup of rice
4. Let cook until the water has evaporated
5. Add melted butter, mix well
6. Keep in warm place, covered, until ready to serve
