## Ingredients

- unblanched, pounded almonds
- grated apples
- chopped raisins
- brown sugar
- plenty of cinnamon
- grated rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Mix the ingredients together and fill the hollowed out center of the chrimsel with them.
2. Then place one chrimsel upon another, being careful not to let the filling escape from its hollow and fasten the edges securely together with the fingers, keeping the rounded shape uninjured.
3. Fry them in boiling fat, turning them from one side to the other until a dark brown.
4. Serve hot with sugar syrup.
