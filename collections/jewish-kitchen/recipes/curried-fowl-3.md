## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 1 pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- one chopped onion
- a tooth of garlic
- 1/2-lb. of the best rice
- the juice of a large lemon
- 2 spoonfuls of curry powder
- slices of lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place a fowl in a stewpan with 1 pint of water, salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg, one chopped onion, a tooth of garlic.
2. Let it simmer gently for 1/2 of an hour.
3. After you have skimmed it, then take the fowl out, strain the liquor through a clean sieve, place the fowl back in the saucepan, wash and drain well.
4. Mix 1/2-lb. of the best rice with salt and pepper, the juice of a large lemon, 2 spoonfuls of curry powder, put it in the saucepan with the fowl.
5. Let it simmer gently till the rice is quite done and dry.
6. Lay the fowl in the dish and lay the rice round like a little wall.
7. Cut some slices of lemon thin, then cut them in halves, and make a border round the rice.
8. Chop some very green parsley, sprinkle a little on the lemon.
