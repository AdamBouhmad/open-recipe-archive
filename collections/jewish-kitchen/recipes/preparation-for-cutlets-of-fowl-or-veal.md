## Ingredients

- flour
- a little salad oil
- two eggs
- a little white pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- slices of lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a smooth batter of flour, and a little salad oil, and two eggs, a little white pepper, salt, and nutmeg
2. Turn the cutlets well in this mixture, and fry a light brown
3. Garnish with slices of lemon, and crisped parsley
4. Crisp the parsley by putting it in after the cutlets have been fried
5. Drain it to prevent its being greasy
