## Ingredients

- remains of cold chicken or turkey
- one tablespoon of olive oil
- one teaspoon of tarragon vinegar or lemon juice
- a few drops of onion juice
- salt
- pepper
- finely chopped parsley
- fritter batter
- boiling fat
- tomato or brown sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the remains of cold chicken (or turkey) into pieces about an inch long
2. Marinate them in a bowl containing one tablespoon of olive oil, one teaspoon of tarragon vinegar or lemon juice, a few drops of onion juice, salt and pepper
3. At the end of half an hour sprinkle with finely chopped parsley
4. Dip them in fritter batter, and fry in boiling fat
5. Drain on a brown paper
6. Serve with or without tomato or brown sauce
