## Ingredients

- one onion
- green pepper
- hot butter
- four tablespoons of flour
- two cups of strained tomatoes
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook one onion and green pepper chopped fine in hot butter
2. Add four tablespoons of flour, stir until smooth
3. Add two cups of strained tomatoes
4. Season with salt and pepper
