## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of boned brisket or double flank
- 1 quart of water
- salt
- pepper
- a blade of mace
- a tooth of garlic
- 2 carrots

## Directions

1. Put a nice piece of boned brisket, or double flank of 4 lbs., in a quart of water, some salt, pepper, a blade of mace, a tooth of garlic.
2. Boil 2 carrots with to garnish, let it boil fast, skim it, then let it simmer for 2 hours.
3. Dish it up, cut the carrots round the meat, put a spoonful of the liquor over to make it look moist.
4. Serve it up with horseradish or tomato sauce in the sauce tureen.
