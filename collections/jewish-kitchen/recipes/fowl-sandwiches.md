## Ingredients

- fowls
- bread
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Roast the fowls and let them get quite cold
2. Cut off the flesh as even as you can and thin
3. Have the bread for the sandwiches made expressly
4. Cut it very thin
5. Leave the crust on
6. Make a mayonnaise according to recipe for same
7. Spread it thinly over the bread
8. Lay on the fowl
9. See that all the edges look neat
10. Cut each 2 rounds into 4 sandwiches
11. Do not make the sandwiches until a couple of hours before you want them
12. Keep them covered with a damp cloth
