## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- milk
- one pound of flour
- six beaten eggs
- a quarter of a pound of beaten sweet almonds
- two table-spoonsful of yeast
- egg prepared as for Bola d'Amor
- slices of citron
- egg marmalade
- cinnamon
- rich puff paste
- sugar clarified with orange flower-water

## Directions

1. Warm one pound of butter over the fire with a little milk
2. Put it into a pan with one pound of flour, six beaten eggs, a quarter of a pound of beaten sweet almonds, and two table-spoonsful of yeast
3. Make these ingredients into a light paste
4. Set it before the fire to rise
5. Grease a deep dish
6. Place in a layer of the paste, then some egg prepared as for Bola d'Amor, then slices of citron, and a layer of egg marmalade
7. Sprinkle each layer with cinnamon
8. Fill the dish with alternate layers
9. Line the dish with a rich puff paste
10. Bake in a brisk oven
11. Pour sugar clarified with orange flower-water over till the syrup has thoroughly penetrated the Bola
