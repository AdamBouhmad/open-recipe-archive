## Ingredients

- small potatoes
- one onion
- goose-oil or drippings from roast meat
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Take small potatoes, pare and wash them very clean
2. Use one onion to about ten potatoes
3. Add goose-oil or any kind of drippings from roast meat
4. Put them in a pot or spider
5. When hot cut up an onion very fine and add to the boiling fat
6. Add the potatoes
7. Salt and pepper to taste
8. Pour some water over all
9. Cover up tight and let them simmer for about three-quarters of an hour
