## Ingredients

- 4 or 5-lb. of breast of veal
- a pound of chopped meat, well seasoned
- slices of boiled beetroot
- slice of smoked beef
- 3 or 4 gherkins
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- a small quantity of spinach
- a boiled carrot
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Order 4 or 5-lb. of breast of veal to be boned for collaring
2. Lay it flat upon your paste board
3. Beat it down with the rolling pin
4. Have a pound of chopped meat well seasoned
5. Lay it on the veal
6. Put some slices of boiled beetroot and slice of smoked beef
7. Add 3 or 4 gherkins and 2 hard boiled eggs cut in slices
8. Boil a small quantity of spinach and drain it from the water
9. Put that in and add a boiled carrot cut in halves
10. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper
11. Roll it up and sew it
12. Put it in a cloth and tie the ends tight with string
13. Put in a long stock pot
14. Let it boil for 3 hours
15. Take it out and untie the cloth
16. Lay a coarse clean cloth on it and press it down to the thickness of 4 inches
17. Let it remain till cold
18. Take it out, cut off the rough ends, and glaze the top
19. When served at table cut in very thin slices, garnished with meat jelly and parsley and strips of beetroot
