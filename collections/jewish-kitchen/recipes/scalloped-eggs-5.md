## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- three tablespoons of flour
- one and one-half cups of milk
- one teaspoon of salt
- pepper
- six hard-boiled eggs
- slices of toast
- one cup of bread crumbs
- one tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the eggs with cream dressing recipe
2. Mix one cup of bread crumbs with one tablespoon of butter
3. Sprinkle this over dish
4. Bake fifteen minutes in a hot oven
