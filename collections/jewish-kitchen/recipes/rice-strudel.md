## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of rice
- milk
- two ounces of butter
- the yolks of four eggs
- four ounces of sugar
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- salt
- the beaten whites of two eggs
- pounded almonds (optional)
- raisins (optional)
- sweet cream or butter for basting

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough same as for Apple Strudel
2. Leave it in a warm place covered, until you have prepared the rice
3. Wash a quarter of a pound of rice in hot water — about three times
4. Boil it in milk until very soft and thick
5. Let it cool, and then add two ounces of butter, the yolks of four eggs, four ounces of sugar and one teaspoon of vanilla, some salt and the beaten whites of two eggs, mix thoroughly
6. When your dough has been rolled out and pulled as thin as possible, spread the rice over it and roll
7. Add pounded almonds and raisins if desired
8. Put in a greased pan and bake until brown, basting with sweet cream or butter
