## Ingredients

- one-half cup of butter
- one cup and a half of sugar
- two eggs
- three-quarters of a cup of milk
- one-half cup of citron
- one teaspoon of allspice
- one teaspoon of cloves
- one heaping teaspoon of baking-powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Take one-half cup of butter and one cup and a half of sugar, and rub to a cream
2. Add two eggs, three-quarters of a cup of milk, one-half cup of citron cut up very fine, one teaspoon of allspice and one of cloves
3. Sift one heaping teaspoon of baking-powder into enough flour to thicken
4. Make stiffer than ordinary cup cake dough
5. Flavor to suit taste, and drop on large tins with a teaspoon
6. Grease the pans, and bake in a quick oven
7. Try one on a plate; if the dough runs too much add more flour
