## Ingredients

- one pint of chestnuts
- one pint of prunes
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel one pint of chestnuts and skin
2. Boil until tender
3. Boil one pint of prunes till tender
4. Mix chestnuts and prunes together, leaving whatever of sauce there is on the prunes
5. Season with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice
6. Cook all together
