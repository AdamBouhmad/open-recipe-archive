## Ingredients

- fine, ripe freestone peaches
- pulverized sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel fine, ripe freestone peaches
2. Cover plentifully with pulverized sugar
3. Serve with whipped cream (ice cold)
4. Do not slice until just before dining, or they will be very apt to change color
