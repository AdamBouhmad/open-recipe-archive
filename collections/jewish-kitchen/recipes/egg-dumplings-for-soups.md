## Ingredients

- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- one-half teaspoon of melted butter
- whites of two eggs
- a raw egg yolk

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs to a smooth paste
2. Add a little salt and grated nutmeg and one-half teaspoon of melted butter
3. Add the chopped whites of two eggs and a raw egg yolk to be able to mold the dough into little marbles
4. Put in boiling soup one minute
