## Ingredients

- one tea-spoonful of honey
- one tea-spoonful of spirits of wine
- one tea-spoonful of rosemary
- half a pint of rose-water or elder flower-water
- half a pint of soft water

## Directions

1. Mix one tea-spoonful of honey, one of spirits of wine, one of rosemary in half a pint of rose-water or elder flower-water, and the same quantity of soft water
