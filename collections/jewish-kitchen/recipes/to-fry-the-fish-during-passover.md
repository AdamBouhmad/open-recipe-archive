## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- egg
- finest meal

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the fish well
2. Lay it in salt
3. Wash it off and drain it well
4. Lay it in a nice dry cloth
5. Beat up the egg in a plate and some of the finest meal you can pound and sieve
6. Let the oil boil well
7. Put in a piece of the dry biscuit to get brown
8. When well fried, take it out and begin to fry your fish
