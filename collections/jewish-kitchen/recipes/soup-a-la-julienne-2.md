## Ingredients

- celery
- carrots
- turnips
- leeks
- cauliflower
- lettuce
- onions
- a little fine salad oil
- weak broth
- a few slices of bread
- a lump of white sugar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a variety of vegetables such as celery, carrots, turnips, leeks, cauliflower, lettuce, and onions
2. Cut them in shreds of small size
3. Place them in a stew-pan with a little fine salad oil
4. Stew them gently over the fire, adding weak broth from time to time
5. Toast a few slices of bread and cut them into pieces the size and shape of shillings and crowns
6. Soak them in the remainder of the broth
7. When the vegetables are well done add all together and let it simmer for a few minutes
8. Season with a lump of white sugar, pepper and salt
