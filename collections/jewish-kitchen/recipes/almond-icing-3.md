## Ingredients

- whites of two eggs
- one-half pound of sweet almonds, blanched and dried
- one-half pound of confectioner's sugar
- a few drops of rosewater
- plain icing
- flour

## Directions

1. Take the whites of two eggs and one-half pound of sweet almonds, which should be blanched, dried and grated or pounded to a paste
2. Beat the whites of the eggs, add half a pound of confectioner's sugar, one tablespoon at a time, until all is used
3. Add the almonds and a few drops of rosewater
4. Spread between or on top of cake
5. Put on thick, and when nearly dry cover with a plain icing
6. If the cakes are well dredged with a little flour after baking, and then carefully wiped before the icing is put on, it will not run and can be spread more smoothly
7. Put the frosting in the centre of the cake, dip a knife in cold water and spread from the centre toward the edge
