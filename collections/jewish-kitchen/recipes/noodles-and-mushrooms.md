## Ingredients

- broad noodles
- melted butter
- a tablespoon of butter
- one-half tablespoon of flour
- liquor of mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Make broad noodles, boil and serve with melted butter spread over the noodles and this sauce.
2. Brown a tablespoon of butter in the skillet, add one-half tablespoon of flour, then liquor of mushrooms, pinch of salt and pepper.
3. When smooth, add mushrooms.
4. Let boil and serve in a separate dish.
5. When serving, a spoon of mushrooms is to be put over each portion of noodles.
