## Ingredients

- eight apples
- sugar
- raisins
- cinnamon
- pounded almonds
- white wine
- eight eggs
- two scant cups of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- grated peel of a lemon
- one cup of matzoth flour

## Directions

1. Pare eight apples and cut off the tops carefully, so as to be able to use them as covers to the apples
2. Scrape out the inside with a knife, being careful not to break the apple
3. Mix the scrapings with sugar, raisins, cinnamon, pounded almonds and a little white wine
4. Fill this mixture into the hollow of the apple and clap on a cover for each apple
5. Grease a pudding dish, lay in the apples and stew them for a few minutes, but not long enough to break them
6. Make a sponge cake batter of eight eggs and two scant cups of sugar and a pinch of salt and add the grated peel of a lemon and beat until thick, at least half an hour
7. Fold in a cup of matzoth flour, sifted very fine
8. Pour this batter over the apples and bake in a moderate oven
9. Serve with wine sauce
