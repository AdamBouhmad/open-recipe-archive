## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- one-half cup of sifted sugar
- one-quarter pound of grated sweet almonds
- one-quarter pound of finely-grated vanilla chocolate
- one-quarter pound of raisins
- one-half cup of matzoth meal sifted fine
- juice of an orange
- one-quarter cup of wine
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs with one-half cup of sifted sugar
2. Add one-quarter pound of grated sweet almonds, one-quarter pound of finely-grated vanilla chocolate, and one-quarter pound of raisins
3. Add one-half cup of matzoth meal sifted fine, juice of an orange, one-quarter cup of wine, and lastly the stiffly-beaten whites
4. Bake one hour in a slow oven, in a form lined with greased paper
