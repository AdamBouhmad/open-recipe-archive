## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- a large piece of butter
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Clean some fine fresh mushrooms, put them in a saucepan with a large piece of butter, pepper and salt
2. let them simmer until tender, and serve them with no other sauce than that in which they have been dressed
