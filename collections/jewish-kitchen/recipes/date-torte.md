## Ingredients

- one-half pound of pulverized sugar
- yolks of six large eggs
- one-half pound of dates, cut very fine
- one teaspoon of allspice
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- one-fourth pound of chocolate grated
- juice and peel of one lemon
- three and one-half soda crackers, rolled to a fine powder
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- whites of eggs, stiff-beaten

## Directions

1. Beat pulverized sugar with the yolks of six large eggs
2. Beat long and steadily until a thick batter
3. Add dates, allspice, cinnamon, chocolate, juice and peel of lemon, soda crackers, and baking-powder
4. Add last the stiff-beaten whites
5. Bake slowly
6. Cake can be cut in half and put together with jelly
