## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 good apples
- currants
- raisins
- almonds
- candied lemon and orange peel
- chopped almonds
- cinnamon
- cloves
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Peel and core 3 or 4 good apples, cut them small
2. Mix with a few currants, raisins, and almonds, candied lemon and orange peel, a few chopped almonds, cinnamon, and cloves, 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
3. Let this stew well together
4. Turn it out to get cold
5. Make a puff-paste, line and cover the dish
6. Bake in a very brisk oven
