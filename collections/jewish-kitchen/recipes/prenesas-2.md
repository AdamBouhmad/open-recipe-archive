## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- flour
- ten eggs
- clarified butter
- clarified sugar
- lemon essence

## Directions

1. Take one pint of milk and stir in as much flour as will bring it to the consistency of hasty pudding
2. Boil it till it becomes thick and let it cool
3. Beat it up with ten eggs
4. When smooth, take a spoonful at a time and drop it into a frying-pan in which there is a good quantity of boiling clarified butter
5. Fry of a light brown and serve with clarified sugar flavored with lemon essence
