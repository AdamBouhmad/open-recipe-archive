## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- white sauce
- onion juice
- bread crumbs
- cream

## Directions

1. Saute mushrooms and prepare two cups of white sauce for one pound of mushrooms
2. add one teaspoon of onion juice
3. Into a well-greased baking dish place one-quarter of the mushrooms
4. then one-quarter of the sauce and one-quarter of the bread crumbs
5. continue in this way until all the sauce is used
6. pour one cup of cream over this and sprinkle the remaining crumbs over the top
7. Bake fifteen minutes in a moderate oven, or until the crumbs are browned
