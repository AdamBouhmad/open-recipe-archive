## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 ozs. of ground rice
- 1/2 pint more milk
- 2 ozs. of loaf sugar
- cinnamon
- 2 yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pint of milk with whatever flavouring you like
2. Mix 2 ozs. of ground rice with 1/2 pint more milk and 2 ozs. of loaf sugar
3. Let it boil till it thickens
4. Turn it out and put cinnamon over
5. Stir in 2 yolks of eggs to improve it
