## Ingredients

- brains
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- cup of beef broth
- marjoram
- saffron
- 2 eggs
- lemon
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains the same as the last
2. Slice in a stewpan some onion, pepper, salt, grated nutmeg, and a cup of beef broth if you have it
3. Put in the brains with a little marjoram, and a little saffron to colour
4. Let it cook gently for half an hour
5. Beat in a basin 2 eggs, the juice of a lemon, a teaspoonful of flour, a little chopped parsley
6. When it is rubbed smooth, stir it into the saucepan, basting it backwards and forwards to mix the sauce well
