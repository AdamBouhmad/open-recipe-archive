## Ingredients

- six thick slices of stale bread
- butter
- milk
- thinly sliced cheese
- one-half cup of milk
- one-half teaspoon of dry mustard

## Directions

1. Take six thick slices of stale bread, well buttered
2. Cut them in two
3. Dip into milk
4. Place in a baking dish with alternating layers of thinly sliced cheese, having cheese for top
5. Add half a cup of milk into which a half teaspoon of dry mustard has been put
6. Bake in quick oven fifteen minutes
7. Serve at once
