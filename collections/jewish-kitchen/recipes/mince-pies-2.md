## Ingredients

- fine puff paste
- mincemeat

## Directions

1. Grease and line tin patty-pans with a fine puff paste rolled out thin
2. Fill them with mincemeat, cover them with another piece of paste
3. Moisten the edges, close them carefully, cut them evenly round, and bake them about half an hour in a well-heated oven
