## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking-powder
- 1 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Cream one cup of butter with two cups of sugar
2. Add gradually the yolks of four eggs, one at a time
3. Sift three cups of flour, measure again after sifting, and add two teaspoons of baking-powder in the last sifting
4. Add alternately the sifted flour and one cup of sweet milk
5. Add last the beaten whites of the eggs
6. Flavor to taste
7. Bake in loaf or jelly-tins
