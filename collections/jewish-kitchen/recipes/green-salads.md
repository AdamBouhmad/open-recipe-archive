## Ingredients

- imported or domestic endive, chicory, escarole and Romaine or lettuce
- French dressing
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Wash endive, chicory, escarole and Romaine or lettuce, make crisp in cold water, and dry in a bag on the ice. Serve them with French dressing. Imported endive may be served with mayonnaise, if desired.
