## Ingredients

- one cup of granulated sugar
- one-half cup of butter
- three eggs
- one cup of milk
- two and one-half scant cups of sifted flour
- one teaspoon of vanilla extract
- two teaspoons of baking-powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar together as usual
2. break in three eggs and beat until very creamy
3. Add the flour and milk alternately, reserving a little of the flour to add after the vanilla and baking-powder
4. Beat well and bake in layer cake tins
