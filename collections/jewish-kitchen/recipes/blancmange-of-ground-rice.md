## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk or almond milk
- 1 lb. of sifted sugar
- a little cinnamon
- 2 oz. of finely pounded almonds
- 1/2 lb. of ground rice
- a little cold milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water
- oil
- raspberry or strawberry jam
- a few cut blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart of milk or almond milk, 1 lb. of sifted sugar, a little cinnamon, 2 oz. of finely pounded almonds
2. Set it at the side, when it boils mix in a basin 1/2 lb. of ground rice
3. Mix it stiff with a little cold milk and 2 tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water
4. Stir it into the boiling mixture
5. Let it boil till it is quite thick, keep stirring it all the time to prevent its burning
6. Have a pretty earthenware mould, slightly grease it with the oil, turn the mixture in
7. Set it in a very cold place
8. When ready, turn it out on a glass dish, and put round it some raspberry or strawberry jam
9. Stick it with a few cut blanched almonds
