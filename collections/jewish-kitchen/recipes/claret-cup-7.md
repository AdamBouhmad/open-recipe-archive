## Ingredients

- strained juice of one and one-half lemons
- two tablespoons of powdered sugar
- one tablespoon of red curagao
- three cups of claret
- one cup of apollinaris water
- a few slices of orange or pineapple, or both
- a few maraschino cherries
- rinds from two cucumbers
- a good-sized lump of ice

## Directions

1. Squeeze into a glass pitcher the strained juice of one and one-half lemons, add two tablespoons of powdered sugar, one tablespoon of red curagao.
2. Pour in three cups of claret, and one cup of apollinaris water.
3. Mix thoroughly, add a few slices of orange or pineapple, or both, and a few maraschino cherries.
4. Cut the rinds from two cucumbers without breaking them, hang them on the inside of the pitcher from the top.
5. Drop in a good-sized lump of ice and serve at once.
