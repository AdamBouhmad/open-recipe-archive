## Ingredients

- a cucumber
- orange flower-water
- a small piece of camphor
- a wine-glass of soft water

## Directions

1. Cut a cucumber into pieces after having peeled it
2. Let the juice drain from it for twelve hours
3. Pour it off, and add to it an equal quantity of orange flower-water, with a small piece of camphor dissolved in a wine-glass of soft water
4. Bottle the mixture
5. Wash the parts that have been exposed to the sun two or three times in the twenty-four hours
