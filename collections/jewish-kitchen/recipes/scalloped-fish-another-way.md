## Ingredients

- fish
- beaten egg yolk
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- lemon peel
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Break the fish into pieces
2. Pour over the beaten yolk of an egg
3. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
4. Strew with bread crumbs, chopped parsley, and grated lemon peel
5. Squeeze in the juice of lemon
6. Drop over a little warmed butter
7. Brown before the fire
