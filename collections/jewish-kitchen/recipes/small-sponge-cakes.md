## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one-half cup of granulated sugar
- one small lemon, juice and grated rind
- one scant cup of flour, sifted three times

## Directions

1. Separate the whites and yolks of four eggs
2. Beat the whites stiff, and beat into them one-half cup of granulated sugar
3. Beat the yolks to a very stiff froth and beat into them one-half cup of granulated sugar
4. Beat this last mixture for exactly five minutes
5. Add the juice and grated rind of one small lemon
6. Beat yolks and whites together well
7. Stir in very gently one scant cup of flour that has been sifted three times
8. Fold the flour in just enough to mix well
9. Bake twenty or twenty-five minutes in moderate oven in small patty pans
