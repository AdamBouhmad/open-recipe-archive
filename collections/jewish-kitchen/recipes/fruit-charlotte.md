## Ingredients

- strawberries
- liquid jelly
- pottle of scarlet strawberries
- half a pound of white sugar
- pint of whipped cream
- one ounce and a half of dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Line a jelly mould with fine picked strawberries, which must first be just dipped into some liquid jelly, to make them adhere closely
2. Fill the mould with some strawberry cream prepared as follows: take a pottle of scarlet strawberries, mix them with half a pound of white sugar, rub this through a sieve, and add to it a pint of whipped cream, and one ounce and a half of dissolved isinglass
3. Pour it into the mould, which must be immersed in ice until ready to serve
4. Carefully turn it out on the dish, and garnish according to fancy
