## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of savoy biscuits
- a quart of milk
- six ounces of fresh butter
- four eggs
- one ounce of candid orange peel
- one ounce of lemon peel
- one ounce of citron
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Soak three-quarters of a pound of savoy biscuits in a quart of milk
2. Add six ounces of fresh butter, four eggs, one ounce of candid orange peel, the same quantity of lemon peel, and one ounce of citron
3. Mix all well together and sweeten with white sugar
4. Bake in a quick oven
5. When nearly done, spread over the top the whites of the eggs well whisked
6. Return it to the oven
