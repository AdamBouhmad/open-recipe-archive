## Ingredients

- a cup and a half of thick yeast
- a little warm milk
- two pounds of flour
- one pound of fresh butter
- ten eggs
- a grated nutmeg
- a quarter of a pint of orange flower-water
- a little powdered cinnamon
- three pints of warm milk

## Directions

1. Mix the thick yeast with a little warm milk and set it with the flour before the fire to rise
2. Mix with them one pound of fresh butter, ten eggs, a grated nutmeg, a quarter of a pint of orange flower-water, a little powdered cinnamon, and three pints of warm milk
3. When the batter is perfectly smooth, butter the irons
4. Fill them with it and close them down tightly
5. Put them between the bars of a bright clear fire
6. When sufficiently done, they will slip easily out of the irons
