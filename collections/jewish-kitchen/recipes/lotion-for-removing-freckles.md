## Ingredients

- one dram of spirit of salts
- half a pint of rain water
- half a tea-spoonful of spirit of lavender

## Directions

1. Mix one dram of spirit of salts, half a pint of rain water, and half a tea-spoonful of spirit of lavender
2. Bottle for use
