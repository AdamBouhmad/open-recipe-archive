## Ingredients

- 1 quart of greengages
- 4 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1 oz. ground almonds
- cinnamon
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Wash and drain a quart of greengages, stew them with 4 lb. of loaf sugar, 1 oz. ground almonds, a little cinnamon
2. Remember not to put any water to stewed fruit for pies or tarts
3. Let it get cold
4. Line a dish with puff-paste, cover
5. Bake in a quick oven
6. These pies are always served with a baked custard
