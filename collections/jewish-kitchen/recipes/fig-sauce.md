## Ingredients

- figs
- loaf sugar
- port or other wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stew figs slowly for two hours, until soft
2. Sweeten with loaf sugar, about two tablespoons to a pound of fruit
3. Add a glass of port or other wine and a little lemon juice
4. Serve when cold
