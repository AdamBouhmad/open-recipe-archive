## Ingredients

- 1 pound macaroni
- boiling water
- salt
- 1/2-lb. Dutch cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Have a saucepan ready with boiling water with a little salt.
2. Put in the macaroni, let them boil till they look white and soft.
3. Take them out, lay them on a sieve to drain.
4. Have a deep dish ready, grate 1/2-lb. Dutch cheese, not too fresh.
5. Put a layer of macaroni in the dish with some pieces of butter and a layer of grated cheese.
6. Continue the layers till you have used the macaroni, cheese, and butter, 1/2-lb. will be required for this dish.
7. Put it into the oven for 20 minutes.
8. It must be sent to table in the same dish it is baked in.
