## Ingredients

- sour, juicy apples
- water
- white wine
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take sour, juicy apples, not too ripe, cut up in pieces, leave the skins on and boil the seeds also.
2. Put on enough water to just cover.
3. Boil on the back of the stove, closely covered, all day.
4. Then put in jelly-bag of double cheese-cloth to drip all night.
5. Next morning measure the juice.
6. Allow a wine-glass of white wine and juice of one lemon to every three pints of juice.
7. Boil a pint at a time, with a pound of sugar to every pint.
