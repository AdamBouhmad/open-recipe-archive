## Ingredients

- puff paste
- butter size of an egg
- one cup of granulated sugar
- one tablespoon of flour
- three-fourths cup of grated cocoanut
- one tablespoon of milk
- vanilla
- pinch of salt
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Line a pie-plate with puff paste
2. fill with the following custard: Butter size of an egg, creamed with one cup of granulated sugar, one tablespoon of flour, three-fourths cup of grated cocoanut, one tablespoon of milk, vanilla, pinch of salt, and the beaten whites of three eggs
