## Ingredients

- beets
- salt
- one-half cup of butter
- two tablespoons of strained lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash as many beets as required and cook in boiling water until tender
2. Drain and turn into cold water for peeling
3. Remove the skins and slice and sprinkle with as much salt as desired
4. Melt one-half cup of butter in a large frying-pan and add two tablespoons of strained lemon juice
5. Stir the butter and lemon juice until blended, keeping the fire low
6. Turn the beets into this sauce, cover the pan and shake and toss until the sauce has been well distributed
7. Serve hot at once
