## Ingredients

- cauliflower or asparagus
- salt water
- bread crumbs
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook in salt water until tender
2. Spread with bread crumbs and butter
3. Pour some sour cream over the vegetable and bake until the crumbs are a golden brown
