## Ingredients

- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- eight tablespoons of cream
- white pepper
- pinch of salt
- cayenne pepper
- two tablespoons of white vinegar

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs to a powder, then add eight tablespoons of cream very gradually to them, also white pepper, a pinch of salt and a mere suspicion of cayenne pepper.
2. Lastly add two tablespoons of white vinegar.
3. It is very important that this last ingredient be put in drop by drop, otherwise the mixture will curdle.
