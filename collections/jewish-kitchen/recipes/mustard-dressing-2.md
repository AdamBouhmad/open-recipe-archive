## Ingredients

- yolk of one hard-boiled egg
- two teaspoons French mustard
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- oil
- vinegar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Take yolk of one hard-boiled egg and rub smooth in a bowl. Add two teaspoons of French mustard, salt, pepper, and little sugar. Add a little oil, and then a little vinegar. Garnish top with the white, cut in pieces.
