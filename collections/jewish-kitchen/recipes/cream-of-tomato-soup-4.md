## Ingredients

- one quart tomatoes
- one pint water
- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- one and one-half cups of milk
- cream
- one teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of sugar
- one-quarter teaspoon of pepper
- chopped parsley
- celery
- one-fourth teaspoon of baking soda

## Directions

1. Cook one quart tomatoes (fresh or canned) with one pint water until done, and strain through a sieve
2. Meanwhile melt two tablespoons of butter, add two tablespoons of flour
3. add gradually one and one-half cups of milk (or half cream and half milk), one teaspoon of salt, one teaspoon of sugar, one-quarter teaspoon of pepper
4. add a little chopped parsley and celery, and let this boil for fifteen minutes
5. Just before ready to serve add one-fourth teaspoon of baking soda to the hot strained tomatoes
6. pour gradually into the cream sauce stirring constantly and serve at once
