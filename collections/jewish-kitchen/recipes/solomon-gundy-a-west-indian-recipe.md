## Ingredients

- pickled herring
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Soak a pickled herring and then scald it
2. pick out all the bones
3. chop it up very fine with a little onion and parsley
4. put over some oil, vinegar, and pepper, also a little cayenne
5. Make it an hour before it is wanted
6. put it in a small deep glass dish
