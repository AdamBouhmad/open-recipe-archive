## Ingredients

- pair of ribs, boned and rolled, or a piece of the round
- 1/2 pint of vinegar
- 1/2 pint of water
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- allspice
- 3 or 4 bay leaves
- a cup of raspings from the baker

## Directions

1. Put beef in a stewpan with vinegar and water
2. Season with onion, salt, pepper, allspice, 3 or 4 bay leaves, and a cup of raspings from the baker
3. Let it stew gently for 3 or 4 hours, according to the weight of the beef
4. Strain the gravy before sending up to table, and do not thicken it
