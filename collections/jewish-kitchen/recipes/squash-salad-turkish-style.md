## Ingredients

- long squash
- olive oil
- vinegar
- one-half teaspoon of prepared mustard
- two tablespoons of olive oil
- two shallots or leeks

## Directions

1. Grate off the skin of long squash
2. Cut the squash in slices, one-quarter of an inch thick, and fry in olive oil
3. Prepare a sauce with a little vinegar, one-half teaspoon of prepared mustard, two tablespoons of olive oil, and beat these ingredients very well
4. Add two shallots or leeks, cut in small pieces
5. Pour sauce over the squash and serve
