## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter or oil

## Directions

1. Wash some potatoes and dry them in a cloth.
2. Pare them in thin ribbons.
3. Fry them dry, lay them on a strainer to drain off the oil or butter.
4. Put them on a hot dish, sprinkle over salt and pepper mixed together.
