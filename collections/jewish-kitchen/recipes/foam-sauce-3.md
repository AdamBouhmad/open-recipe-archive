## Ingredients

- one-quarter cup of butter
- one cup of powdered sugar
- whites of two eggs
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- one-half cup of hot water

## Directions

1. Cream one-quarter cup of butter with one cup of powdered sugar, until very light
2. Add separately the unbeaten whites of two eggs, stirring briskly and beat again
3. Add one teaspoon of vanilla and one-half cup of hot water
4. Pour in sauceboat
5. place boat in a pan of boiling water on stove, until it becomes frothy
6. serve immediately
