## Ingredients

- gurnet
- onions
- tomatoes
- oil or butter
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- lemon
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the gurnet the same as the haddock
2. Slice the onions and tomatoes at once with 2 spoonfuls of oil, or 1/4 lb. of butter, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
3. Use no water to any fish that is stewed with tomatoes
4. When done, take out the fish
5. Rub the sauce through a sieve
6. Add the juice of a lemon and cayenne pepper
