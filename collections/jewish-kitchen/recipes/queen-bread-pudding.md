## Ingredients

- one cup of grated bread crumbs
- one pint of sweet milk
- three eggs
- one cup of sugar
- a small piece of butter
- vanilla
- fresh fruit (apricots or peaches or strawberry preserves)
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of grated bread crumbs in one pint of sweet milk
2. Break three eggs and separate the whites
3. Add to the yolks one cup of sugar and a small piece of butter
4. Beat it well
5. Squeeze the bread crumbs out of the milk, and add this to the yolks and flavor with vanilla
6. Grease the pans with butter, put the mixture in the pan, and pour the milk over it
7. Set in the oven to bake until nearly dry
8. Add a layer of fresh fruit (apricots or peaches are the best or strawberry preserves)
9. Add the whites of eggs that were beaten stiff
10. Serve cold with cream or milk. This can also be served hot
