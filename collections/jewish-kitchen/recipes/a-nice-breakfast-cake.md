## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- one ounce of butter
- a very little salt
- two eggs
- a table-spoonful of milk

## Directions

1. Make a paste of half a pound of flour, one ounce of butter, a very little salt, two eggs, and a table-spoonful of milk
2. Set it to rise before the fire
3. Roll it out
4. Cut it into cakes the size of small cheese plates
5. Sprinkle with flour
6. Bake on a tin in a brisk oven, or they may be fried in a clean frying pan
7. Cut in half, butter hot, and serve quickly
