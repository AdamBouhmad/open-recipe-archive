## Ingredients

- herring
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take off the head and skin them
2. put them in a tin whole with boiling water for 10 minutes
3. slit them down the back with a knife when done
4. put in a piece of butter with pepper
5. Dish upon a hot plate
