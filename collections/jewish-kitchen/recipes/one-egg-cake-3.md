## Ingredients

- one-fourth cup of butter
- one-half cup of sugar
- one egg
- one and one-half cups of flour
- two and one-half teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-half cup of milk
- vanilla or lemon

## Directions

1. Cream one-fourth cup of butter with one-half cup of sugar, add sugar gradually, and one egg, well-beaten.
2. Mix and sift one and one-half cups of flour and two and one-half teaspoons of baking-powder.
3. Add the sifted flour alternately with one-half cup of milk to the first mixture.
4. Flavor with vanilla or lemon.
5. Bake thirty minutes in a shallow pan.
6. Spread with chocolate frosting.
