## Ingredients

- venison
- broth
- seasoning
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- pounded mace
- allspice
- mutton fat
- strong beef gravy
- port wine
- thick puff paste

## Directions

1. Bake or boil venison two hours in broth, with a little seasoning
2. Cut the meat in pieces
3. Season with cayenne pepper, salt, pounded mace, and a little allspice
4. Place it into a deep dish
5. Lay over thin slices of mutton fat
6. Pour a little strong beef gravy flavored with port wine into the dish
7. Cover with a thick puff paste
8. Bake
