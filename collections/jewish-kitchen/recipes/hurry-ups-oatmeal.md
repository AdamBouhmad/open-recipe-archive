## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- one teaspoon of salt
- one cup of rolled oats
- one tablespoon of sugar
- two tablespoons of melted butter
- one-half cup of milk
- raisins or dates

## Directions

1. Sift one cup of flour with two teaspoons of baking-powder, one teaspoon of salt
2. Add one cup of rolled oats, one tablespoon of sugar and two tablespoons of melted butter
3. Mix with one-half cup of milk
4. Drop by teaspoons onto a greased pan
5. Press well into each two or three raisins, or a split date
6. Bake for twenty minutes in a hot oven
7. Can be served with butter, honey, or maple sugar
