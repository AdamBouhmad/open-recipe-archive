## Ingredients

- fresh herrings
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- lemon juice
- cayenne
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads
2. draw out the entrails
3. wash and dry them well
4. give 2 or 3 cuts across on each side
5. grease the gridiron
6. season the herrings with salt and pepper
7. dredge a little flour on them lightly
8. put them on the fish gridiron
9. broil them on both sides
10. put them on a hot dish with a piece of butter, lemon juice, a little cayenne and chopped parsley
11. serve on very hot dish and plates
