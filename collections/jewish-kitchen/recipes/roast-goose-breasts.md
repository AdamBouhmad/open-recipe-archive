## Ingredients

- goose breast
- skin from neck
- garlic or onion
- salt
- ginger

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the neck and sew it over the breast
2. Fasten it with a few stitches under the breast
3. Make an incision with a pointed knife in the breast and joints of the goose
4. Insert a little garlic or onion in each incision
5. Add a little salt and ginger
6. Keep closely covered all the time, so as not to get too brown
7. Cut up nicely cold for sandwiches
