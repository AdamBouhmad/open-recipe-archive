## Ingredients

- duck
- salt
- ginger
- pepper
- liver
- gizzard
- heart
- nutmeg
- celery root
- onion
- stale bread
- fat
- egg
- onions
- celery
- fat

## Directions

1. Singe off all the small feathers
2. Cut off neck and wings, which may be used for soup
3. Wash thoroughly and rub well with salt, ginger and a little pepper, inside and out
4. Take the liver, gizzard and heart and chop to a powder in chopping bowl
5. Grate in a little nutmeg
6. Add a piece of celery root and half an onion
7. Put all this into your chopping bowl
8. Soak some stale bread, squeeze out all the water and fry in a spider of hot fat
9. Toss this soaked bread into the bowl
10. Add one egg, salt, pepper and a speck of ginger and mix all thoroughly
11. Fill the duck with this and sew it up
12. Lay in the roasting-pan with slices of onions, celery and specks of fat
13. Put some on top of fowl
14. Roast two hours, covered up tight and baste often
15. Stick a fork into the skin from time to time so that the fat will try out
