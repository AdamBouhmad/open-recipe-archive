## Ingredients

- five ounces of white bread
- cold water
- three-fourths cup of soft goose fat in small pieces
- five whole eggs
- one cup of flour
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-fourth cup of cracker meal
- three apples
- two pears
- two dozen raisins with the seeds removed
- salt to taste
- tiny pinch of pepper
- one-quarter teaspoon each of cinnamon and allspice
- goose-fat
- one cup of cold water

## Directions

1. Soak five ounces of white bread in cold water
2. Squeeze out every bit of water and put it in a bowl
3. Add three-fourths cup of soft goose fat in small pieces
4. Add five whole eggs, one cup of flour, one-half cup of sugar, one-fourth cup of cracker meal, three apples and two pears cut in small pieces, two dozen raisins with the seeds removed, salt to taste, a tiny pinch of pepper, one-quarter teaspoon each of cinnamon and allspice
5. Mix all well together
6. Pour into an iron pan that has the bottom well covered with goose-fat
7. Stick a few pieces of cut apple or pear in the top of the pudding
8. Pour a cup of cold water over all
9. Place in the oven to bake
10. Bake slowly for five or six hours
11. If the water cooks out before it is ready to brown, add more
12. Bake brown, top and bottom
