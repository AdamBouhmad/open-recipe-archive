## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 dozen of picked large Spanish chestnuts
- 2 lbs. of loaf sugar
- 1 pint of water
- fine castor sugar
- vanille

## Directions

1. Parboil the chestnuts
2. Peel them very carefully with a small knife and pick out the pieces of inside skin with a darning needle
3. Put in a preserving pan 2 lbs. of loaf sugar with 1 pint of water and let it boil to a syrup
4. Put the chestnuts in the syrup and let them boil up
5. Take each out carefully and lay them on a large dish, one not to touch the other
6. Roll them well over with fine castor sugar
7. Let the syrup boil up again and dip the chestnuts in again
8. Take them out and put a few drops of vanille into the syrup
9. Let it boil till quite thick and dip each chestnut in again
10. Roll each chestnut in fine sugar and dip them again
11. Keep each chestnut separate and put them in a dry place till quite cold
