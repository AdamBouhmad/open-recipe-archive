## Ingredients

- a long round French roll
- boiling milk
- several eggs
- fine salad oil
- clarified sugar
- cinnamon
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Take off the crust of a long round French roll and cut the crumb in thin slices
2. Soak them in boiling milk, taking care they do not break
3. Have a dish ready with several eggs beaten up
4. With a fish slice remove the bread from the milk, letting the milk drain off
5. Dip them into the dish of eggs
6. Half fry them in fine salad oil
7. Then again soak them in the milk and dip in the egg
8. Fry of a handsome light brown
9. While hot, pour over clarified sugar flavored with cinnamon and orange flower water
