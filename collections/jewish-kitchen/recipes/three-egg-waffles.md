## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- yolks of three eggs
- one and one-fourth cups of milk
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of flour, one teaspoon of baking-powder, one-half teaspoon of salt
2. Sift these ingredients
3. Add the yolks of three eggs beaten and stirred into one and one-fourth cups of milk
4. Add one tablespoon of melted butter
5. Fold in the whites of the eggs
6. Bake and serve as directed under One-Egg Waffles
