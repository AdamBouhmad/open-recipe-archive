## Ingredients

- yolks of ten eggs
- whites of seven eggs
- half a pound of sweet almonds pounded finely
- half a pound of white sugar
- half an ounce of bitter almonds
- a table-spoonful of orange flower water
- powdered sugar
- clarified sugar with orange flower water

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of ten eggs, and the whites of seven
2. Add half a pound of sweet almonds pounded finely, half a pound of white sugar, half an ounce of bitter almonds, and a table-spoonful of orange flower water
3. Thoroughly mix
4. Grease a dish
5. Put in the pudding and bake in a brisk oven
6. When done, strew powdered sugar over the top, or pour over clarified sugar with orange flower water
