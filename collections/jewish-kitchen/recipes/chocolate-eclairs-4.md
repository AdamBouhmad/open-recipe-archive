## Ingredients

- Batter prepared as in cream puffs recipe
- One cup of brown sugar
- One-half cup of molasses
- One tablespoon of butter
- Two tablespoons of flour
- One-fourth pound of grated chocolate
- One-fourth cup of sweet milk
- Vanilla to flavor

## Directions

1. Spread the batter in long ovals
2. When done cover with frosting
3. For frosting: Boil one cup of brown sugar with one-half cup of molasses, one tablespoon of butter and two tablespoons of flour
4. Boil for one-half hour
5. Stir in one-fourth pound of grated chocolate wet in one-fourth cup of sweet milk
6. Boil until it hardens on the spoon
7. Flavor with vanilla
8. Spread this upon the eclairs
