## Ingredients

- 2 or 3-lbs. of beef
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1-lb. of chestnuts
- teaspoonful of flour
- juice of 1/2 a lemon
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Take a nice piece of beef and put it into a stewpan with salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg and 1/2 pint of water
2. Let it simmer gently for 2 hours
3. Boil 1-lb. of chestnuts for 1/2 an hour
4. Take them out of the water and peel them whole
5. 1/2 an hour before the meat is done put in the chestnuts to finish cooking
6. Rub a teaspoonful of flour with the juice of 1/2 a lemon and a little dried saffron
7. Be careful the chestnuts do not break when you put in the thickening
8. Dish them up nicely by putting them round the meat
