## Ingredients

- haddock
- mashed potatoes
- herbs
- spice
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- flour or raspings from the baker
- butter
- tomatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare a stuffing of mashed potatoes, herbs and spice as before described
2. Place it in the inside of the haddock and sew it up
3. Bake in a deep earthenware dish
4. Turn the haddock round with the tail in the mouth
5. Chop some onion very fine
6. Season the fish outside with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
7. Dredge with flour or some raspings from the baker
8. Put in the dish 1/4 lb. of butter to baste it well as it cooks
9. Add 2 or 3 tomatoes sliced with it
10. When dishing it up put on some chopped parsley
11. Prepare some potatoes as for baked plaice
12. When you put it in the dish, pour the gravy all over it with a spoon
