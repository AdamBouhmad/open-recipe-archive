## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sweet almonds
- 1.5 lb. of sifted sugar
- whites of 6 eggs
- grated rind of 2 lemons
- blanched almonds
- almond oil
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Pound the almonds very fine (they must be pounded, as the ground almonds are seldom fine enough)
2. Beat the whites of eggs to a snow
3. Add the sugar and grated lemon
4. Mix it all well together, it will be like a paste
5. Shape them as you please
6. Have some almonds blanched, cut them in quarters, and put a few on each cake
7. Grease a very thin paper with almond oil, and sift some fine sugar on it
8. Put it on the tin, and place the cakes at a distance from each other
9. Bake them in a moderate oven
10. When done, let them cool before you touch them
