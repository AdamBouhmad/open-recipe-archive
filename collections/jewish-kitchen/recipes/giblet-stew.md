## Ingredients

- 1 lb. edge-bone steak
- giblets
- small forced meat balls
- pickled walnut
- neck skin
- hard boiled eggs
- forced meat
- flour
- juice of half a lemon
- walnut liquor

## Directions

1. Cut the edge-bone steak in nice pieces larger than for a pie
2. Prepare all the seasoning and put it in the stewpan, with steak at bottom and giblets on top, with small balls
3. Cut up a pickled walnut
4. Take the skin of the neck, tie one end, put in a little forced meat, then a hard boiled egg peeled, then some more meat, then another hard egg, then some more meat, fill it well
5. Tie the other end and put it to stew with the other ingredients
6. When the stew is done take out the neck and cut it in even slices
7. Stir in a basin a spoonful of flour, the juice of half a lemon, and a little of the walnut liquor
8. Turn it on the stew and mix it well
9. Put the slices of the neck round as a garnish
10. Mind not to put the gravy over the eggs, it spoils the appearance
