## Ingredients

- a piece from the shoulder, about three to four pounds
- a couple of onions
- a carrot sliced
- sweet herbs
- two or three bay leaves
- a large piece of chorissa or a slice of the root of a tongue smoked
- a little whole pepper and salt
- gravy made from the trimmings of the veal

## Directions

1. Take a piece from the shoulder, about three to four pounds
2. Trim it and form it into a well shaped even piece, the surface of which should be quite smooth
3. Pique it thickly
4. Put it into a stewpan with a couple of onions, a carrot sliced, sweet herbs, two or three bay leaves, a large piece of chorissa or a slice of the root of a tongue smoked, a little whole pepper and salt
5. Cover it with a gravy made from the trimmings of the veal
6. Stew till extremely tender, which can be proved by probing it with a fine skewer
7. Reduce part of the gravy to a glaze
8. Glaze the meat with it and serve on a puree of vegetables
