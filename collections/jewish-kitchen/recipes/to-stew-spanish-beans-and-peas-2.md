## Ingredients

- beans
- cold water
- two table spoonsful of oil
- pepper
- salt
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the beans over night in cold water
2. Stew them in only sufficient water to cover them, with two table spoonsful of oil, a little pepper and salt, and white sugar
3. When done they should be perfectly soft and tender
