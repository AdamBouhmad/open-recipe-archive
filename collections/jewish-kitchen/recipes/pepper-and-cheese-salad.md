## Ingredients

- green peppers
- cream cheese
- olives
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Fill green peppers with a mixture of cream cheese and chopped olives
2. Set on the ice and then slice the peppers and serve a slice (shaped like a four-leaf clover) on a leaf of lettuce
