## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- ground ginger
- water
- rice

## Directions

1. Joint a chicken; season with salt and ground ginger and boil with water enough to cover.
2. Allow one-half pound of rice to one chicken.
3. Boil this after chicken is tender.
4. Serve together on a large platter.
