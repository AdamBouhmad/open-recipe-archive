## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one-half pound of granulated sugar
- a pinch of salt
- one-fourth pound of grated walnuts, whole pieces reserved for decorating
- two tablespoons of grated lady fingers or stale sponge cake
- almond or plain icing

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks and whites of six eggs, being very careful not to get a particle of the yolks into the whites
2. Sift one-half pound of granulated sugar into the yolks and beat until thick as batter
3. Add a pinch of salt to the whites and beat very stiff
4. Have ready one-fourth pound of grated walnuts, reserve whole pieces for decorating the top of cake
5. Add the pounded nuts to the beaten yolks and two tablespoons of grated lady fingers or stale sponge cake
6. Last add the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
7. Bake in layers and fill with almond or plain icing
