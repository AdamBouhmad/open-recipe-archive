## Ingredients

- one thick slice of cold, cooked salmon
- mayonnaise
- one tablespoon of French mustard
- one green onion
- one tablespoon of small Mexican peppers
- one tablespoon of pimentos

## Directions

1. Pick or grind one thick slice of cold, cooked salmon.
2. Make a dressing of mayonnaise, to which add one tablespoon of French mustard, one green onion chopped fine, one tablespoon of small Mexican peppers, one tablespoon of pimentos.
3. Mix this dressing into the picked salmon.
