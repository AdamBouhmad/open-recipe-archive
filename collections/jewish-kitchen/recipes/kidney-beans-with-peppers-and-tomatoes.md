## Ingredients

- two cups of kidney beans
- one quart of water
- one tablespoon of butter
- four green peppers
- one small red pepper
- one onion
- one pint of tomatoes
- one teaspoon of salt
- three tablespoons of uncooked rice

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash two cups of kidney beans, soak in one quart of water
2. Next morning bring to a boil in fresh water
3. Drain, cover beans with boiling water and cook until tender
4. Half an hour before beans are to be served, put one tablespoon of butter in a saucepan
5. Chop and add four green peppers, one small red pepper, one onion, one pint of tomatoes, one teaspoon of salt
6. Cook fifteen minutes
7. Add to beans with three tablespoons of uncooked rice
8. Simmer until thick
