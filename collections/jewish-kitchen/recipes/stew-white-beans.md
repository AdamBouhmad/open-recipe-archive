## Ingredients

- 1 pint large white beans
- 3/4-lb. butter
- 1 onion
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Lay in soak 1 pint of the large white beans the night before you require them.
2. Put in a stewpan 3/4-lb. of butter, and an onion chopped very fine, let it fry.
3. Add pint of water, put in the beans.
4. Let them simmer gently till done.
