## Ingredients

- one quart of new milk
- thinly pared rind of a lemon
- four ounces of lump sugar
- yolks of two eggs
- a little cold milk
- a tea-cup full of rum
- a tea-cup full of brandy

## Directions

1. Put into a quart of new milk the thinly pared rind of a lemon, and four ounces of lump sugar
2. Let it boil slowly
3. Remove the peel
4. Stir in the yolks of two eggs, previously mixed with a little cold milk
5. Add by degrees a tea-cup full of rum, the same of brandy
6. Mill the punch to a fine froth
7. Serve immediately in quite warm glasses
8. The punch must not be allowed to boil after the eggs have been added
