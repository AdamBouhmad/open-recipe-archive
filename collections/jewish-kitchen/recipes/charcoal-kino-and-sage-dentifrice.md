## Ingredients

- one ounce of finely powdered charcoal
- one ounce of red kino
- a table spoonful of the leaves of sage, dried and powdered

## Directions

1. Mix one ounce of finely powdered charcoal, one ounce of red kino, and a table spoonful of the leaves of sage, dried and powdered
