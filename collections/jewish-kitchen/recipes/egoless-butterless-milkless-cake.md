## Ingredients

- one package of seeded raisins
- two cups of sugar
- two cups of boiling water
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one teaspoon of cloves
- two tablespoons of Crisco, chicken schmalz or clarified drippings
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of soda
- three cups of flour

## Directions

1. Boil one package of seeded raisins, two cups of sugar, two cups of boiling water, one teaspoon of cinnamon, one teaspoon of cloves, two tablespoons of Crisco, chicken schmalz or clarified drippings, and one-half teaspoon of salt together five minutes
2. cool
3. add one teaspoon of soda dissolved in water
4. add three cups of flour
5. Bake forty-five minutes, make two cakes in layer pans
