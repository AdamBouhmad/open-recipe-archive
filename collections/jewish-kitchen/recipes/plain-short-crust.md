## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh butter
- a pound of flour
- yolks of two eggs
- powdered sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of fresh butter to a pound of flour
2. Add the yolks of two eggs and a little powdered sugar
3. Mix into a paste with water
4. Roll out once
