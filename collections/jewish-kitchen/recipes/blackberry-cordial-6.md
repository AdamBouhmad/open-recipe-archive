## Ingredients

- berries
- sugar
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- cloves
- allspice

## Directions

1. Simmer the berries until they break
2. strain and to each quart of juice add one pound of sugar
3. Let this dissolve by heating slowly
4. add one tablespoon each of cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and if desired, allspice
5. Simmer altogether twenty minutes
6. Bottle and seal
