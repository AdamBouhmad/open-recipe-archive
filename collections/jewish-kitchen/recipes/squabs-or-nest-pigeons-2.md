## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- ginger
- pepper
- bread dressing
- goose fat
- minced parsley
- chopped onion
- water

## Directions

1. Pick, singe, draw, clean and season them well inside and out, with salt mixed with a little ginger and pepper
2. Stuff them with well-seasoned bread dressing
3. Pack them closely in a deep stew-pan and cover with flakes of goose fat, minced parsley and a little chopped onion
4. Cover with a lid that fits close and stew gently, adding water when necessary
5. Do not let them get too brown. They should be a light yellow
