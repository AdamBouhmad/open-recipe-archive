## Ingredients

- a breast of veal
- a chopped onion
- currie powder
- a good gravy of veal and beef
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut a breast of veal into pieces
2. Fry lightly with a chopped onion
3. Rub the veal over with currie powder
4. Put it into a good gravy of veal and beef
5. Season simply with pepper, salt, and lemon juice
