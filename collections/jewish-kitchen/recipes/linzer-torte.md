## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- four whole eggs
- three-fourths pound of pounded almonds or walnuts
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one-fourth teaspoon of cloves
- one pound of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- a few drops of bitter almond essence
- apricot, strawberry, or raspberry jam
- pineapple marmalade

## Directions

1. Cream one pound of butter with one pound of sugar until foamy
2. Add one by one four whole eggs
3. Mix well
4. Stir in three-fourths pound of pounded almonds or walnuts, one teaspoon of cinnamon, one-fourth teaspoon of cloves, one pound of flour, one teaspoon of baking-powder, and a few drops of bitter almond essence
5. Put in four layer pans and bake in slow oven
6. Put together with apricot, strawberry, or raspberry jam and pineapple marmalade, each layer having a different preserve
7. Ice top and sides
