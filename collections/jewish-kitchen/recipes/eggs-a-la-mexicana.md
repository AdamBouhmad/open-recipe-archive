## Ingredients

- six dried Spanish peppers
- butter
- half an onion
- one clove of garlic
- one cup of uncooked rice
- one cup of water
- salt
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Boil six dried Spanish peppers twenty minutes
2. Drain and remove the seeds
3. Chop fine
4. Fry in butter half an onion and one clove of garlic
5. Add one cup of uncooked rice
6. Cover with one cup of water and cook till tender
7. Add a lump of butter and salt
8. When done, cover with six eggs
9. Scramble all together
10. Serve on a hot dish
