## Ingredients

- 2 lb. of gravy beef
- 2 or 3 cloves
- an onion
- a tooth of garlic
- salt and pepper
- stale crust of bread
- 1 pint and a half of stock 2
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a little browning
- a few meat balls

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan 2-lb. of gravy beef, cut small, with 2 or 3 cloves, an onion, a tooth of garlic, salt and pepper
2. Let it boil for 2 hours
3. If you have any stale crust of bread put it in also
4. Rub it all well through a sieve
5. Put to it pint and a half of stock 2
6. Rub a tablespoonful of flour with a little browning to colour
7. Turn it into the tureen with a few meat balls very small
