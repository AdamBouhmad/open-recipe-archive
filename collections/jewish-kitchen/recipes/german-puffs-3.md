## Ingredients

- quarter pound of flour
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 lb of sugar
- lemon
- orange-flower water
- vanilla
- jam

## Directions

1. Boil the milk
2. While boiling stir in the flour
3. Beat well 4 eggs and stir them into the paste of milk and flour
4. Have some teacups and grease them with butter
5. Drop in a spoonful of the mixture and put them on a baking sheet
6. Put them in a quick oven, they will rise over the top of the cup
7. Clarify 1/2 lb of sugar flavoured with lemon, orange-flower water, or vanilla
8. Serve the puffs hot or cold, with the sugar in a sauce tureen
9. You can if you like when you take them out of the oven put jam on the top
