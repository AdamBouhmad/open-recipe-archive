## Ingredients

- half a pound of well picked rice
- one quart of fresh milk
- white sugar
- whole cinnamon
- lemon peel
- a bay leaf
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil till tender half a pound of well picked rice in one quart of fresh milk, sweeten with white sugar, and flavour with whole cinnamon, lemon peel, and a bay leaf
2. When the rice is tender, place it in a deep dish, pour over a very little butter warmed in a little milk, and bake until brown
3. A slow oven is requisite unless the rice is extremely soft before it is put in the oven
