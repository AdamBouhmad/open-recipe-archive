## Ingredients

- two pounds of cold, boiled beets
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-eighth teaspoon of pepper
- one teaspoon of brown sugar
- one teaspoon of caraway seed
- one pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of cold, boiled beets, slice
2. Place in crock in layers
3. Sprinkle with one teaspoon of salt, one-eighth teaspoon of pepper, one teaspoon of brown sugar, one teaspoon of caraway seed, if you like, and cover with one pint of vinegar
4. Cold, hard-boiled eggs may be placed in the vinegar, and sliced over the beets for decorations
