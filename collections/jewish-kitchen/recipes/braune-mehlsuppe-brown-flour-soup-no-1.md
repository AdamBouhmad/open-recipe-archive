## Ingredients

- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of flour
- boiling water
- salt
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Heat a spoon of butter in a spider
2. add a spoon of flour, stir briskly, but do not let it get black
3. pour boiling water over it
4. add salt and caraway seeds
