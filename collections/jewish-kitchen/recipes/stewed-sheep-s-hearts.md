## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 sheep's hearts
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- marjoram
- water
- flour
- vinegar
- parsley
- saffron
- Jerusalem artichokes

## Directions

1. Clean the hearts well after soaking
2. Clear off all the blood settled in them
3. Cut them in slices and lay them in a stewpan with salt, pepper, onion, marjoram, and 1/2 pint of water
4. Let it cook well
5. Stir in a basin a spoonful of flour, 2 of vinegar, a little chopped parsley, and some saffron to colour
6. A few Jerusalem artichokes stewed with are very nice
