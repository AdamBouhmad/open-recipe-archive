## Ingredients

- two and one-half cups of granulated sugar
- three-fourths cup of hot water
- one-half salt-spoon of tartar

## Directions

1. Put two and one-half cups of granulated sugar in a saucepan
2. Add three-fourths cup of hot water and one-half salt-spoon of tartar
3. Stir until sugar is dissolved, but no longer
4. Boil without stirring until, when tried in cold water, it will form a soft ball
5. Wash down the edges of the pan with the finger first dipped in cold water, as the sugar boils up
6. Pour
