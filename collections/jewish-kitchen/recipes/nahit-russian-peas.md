## Ingredients

- one pound Russian peas
- one tablespoon of salt
- hot water
- one-quarter teaspoon of soda
- one pound of brisket of beef or back or neck of fat chicken
- two tablespoons of fat
- two tablespoons of flour
- two tablespoons of brown sugar
- one cup of the liquid from the peas

## Directions

1. Place one pound Russian peas in granite kettle
2. Add one tablespoon of salt and hot water to more than cover and let soak twelve hours or more
3. Drain, return to the kettle, cover with boiling water
4. Let cook fifteen minutes
5. Add one-quarter teaspoon of soda and one pound of brisket of beef or back or neck of fat chicken
6. Let cook slowly until peas are tender
7. Melt two tablespoons of fat
8. Add two tablespoons of flour and two tablespoons of brown sugar
9. Let brown
10. Add one cup of the liquid from the peas
11. Cook until thick and smooth
12. Pour over the peas
13. Cook thoroughly
14. Then place in casserole and bake in a moderate oven one-half hour
