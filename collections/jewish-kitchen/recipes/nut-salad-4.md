## Ingredients

- grapefruit
- finely chopped almonds or pecans
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Make a plain grapefruit salad.
2. When you have it ready to serve, cover the top thickly with finely chopped almonds or pecans mixed.
3. Pour over French dressing.
