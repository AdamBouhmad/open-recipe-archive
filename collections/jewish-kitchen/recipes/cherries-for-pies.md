## Ingredients

- cherries
- two tablespoons of sugar to a quart of fruit
- one-half cup of water to each quart of cherries

## Directions

1. Stem the cherries — do not pit them.
2. Pack tight in glass fruit jars.
3. Cover with syrup.
4. Let them boil fifteen minutes from the time they begin to boil.
