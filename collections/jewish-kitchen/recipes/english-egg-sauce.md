## Ingredients

- two eggs
- melted butter
- white pepper
- salt
- blade of mace
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil two eggs hard
2. Chop them finely
3. Warm them up in finely made melted butter
4. Add a little white pepper, salt, a blade of mace, and a very small quantity of nutmeg
