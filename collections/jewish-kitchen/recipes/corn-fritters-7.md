## Ingredients

- two cups of corn
- one egg
- one cup of sweet milk
- soda
- two teaspoons of melted butter
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Grate two cups of corn from the cob
2. Beat one egg light
3. Stir a bit of soda the size of a pea into one cup of sweet milk
4. Mix the corn with the egg, milk mixture, two teaspoons of melted butter, a pinch of salt and enough flour to make a thin batter
5. Beat well together
6. Fry on a griddle as you would cakes for breakfast
