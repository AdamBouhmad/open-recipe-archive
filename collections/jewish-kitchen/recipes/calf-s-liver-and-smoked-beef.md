## Ingredients

- a pound of calf's liver
- water
- boiling fat
- salt
- pepper
- slices of smoked beef
- potatoes
- the juice of a lemon
- a little ketchup

## Directions

1. After the liver has been properly prepared, lay it in water in another pan
2. Cut it in slices first and leave it for an hour
3. Take it out and dry it in a cloth
4. Put it on the gridiron before the fire for a few minutes
5. Have a pan on the fire with boiling fat
6. Salt and pepper the liver and fry it a nice brown
7. When nearly done put in the slices of smoked beef
8. Have some potatoes nicely mashed and lay the liver and beef upon them
9. Pour off the fat from the pan
10. Put in the juice of a lemon with a little ketchup and turn it over the liver, beef, and potatoes
