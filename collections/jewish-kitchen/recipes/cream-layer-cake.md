## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- Two scant cups of sugar
- Yolks of four eggs
- One cup of milk
- Three cups of sifted flour
- Three teaspoons of baking-powder
- Whites of four eggs
- Two tablespoons of cornstarch
- Cold milk
- One-half pint of milk
- One-half cup of sugar
- A pinch of salt
- Yolks of two eggs
- One teaspoon of sweet butter

## Directions

1. Rub one cup of butter and two scant cups of sugar to a cream
2. Beat in the yolks of four eggs well
3. Add gradually one cup of milk and three cups of sifted flour
4. Add three teaspoons of baking-powder in last sifting
5. Put whites in last
6. Bake in layers as for jelly cake
7. When cold, prepare filling by moistening two tablespoons of cornstarch with enough cold milk to work it into a paste
8. Scald one-half pint of milk with one-half cup of sugar and a pinch of salt
9. Beat the yolks of two eggs light
10. Add the cornstarch to this
11. As soon as the milk is scalded pour in the mixture gradually, stirring constantly until thick
12. Drop in one teaspoon of sweet butter
13. When this is mixed in, set away until cool
14. Spread between layers
