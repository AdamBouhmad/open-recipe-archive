## Ingredients

- one cup of seeded muscatel raisins
- one cup of nuts
- one cup of sugar
- one well-beaten egg
- one tablespoon of water
- juice and grated rind of one lemon
- pie dough

## Directions

1. Cut raisins and nuts in small pieces
2. Add sugar, egg, water, lemon juice and rind
3. Mix well
4. Line patty-pans with pie dough
5. Fill with mixture
6. Bake until crust is brown
