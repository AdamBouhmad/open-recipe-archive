## Ingredients

- One tablespoon of coffee per cup
- Boiling water
- Two tablespoons of cold water
- Egg shells

## Directions

1. Allow one tablespoon of coffee to each cup of boiling water
2. Mix coffee with two tablespoons of cold water
3. Clean egg shells and put in the pot
4. Allow this to come to a boil and add boiling water
5. Bring to a boil and boil for one minute
6. Add a tablespoon of cold water to assist the grounds in settling
7. Stand the pot where it will keep hot, but not boil, for five minutes
8. Serve at once, as coffee allowed to stand becomes flat and loses its aroma
