## Ingredients

- three layers of almond tart
- a wine glass of arrack
- one-half pound of sugar
- one-fourth cup of water
- a wine glass of rum
- cake crumbs
- crab-apple jelly or apple marmalade
- brandy

## Directions

1. Bake three layers of almond tart and flavor it with a wine glass of arrack
2. When baked, scrape part of the cake out of the thickest layer, not disturbing the rim, and reserve these crumbs
3. Boil one-half pound of sugar in one-fourth cup of water until it spins a thread
4. Add to this syrup a wine glass of rum and the crumbs
5. Spread over the layers, piling one on top of the other
6. Another way to fill this cake is to take some crab-apple jelly or apple marmalade and thin it with a little brandy
