## Ingredients

- one quart of hot bouillon
- one-quarter pound barley
- water
- one ounce of dried mushrooms
- an onion
- carrot
- bayleaf
- parsley
- dill

## Directions

1. Take one quart of hot bouillon, add a quarter pound barley which has been boiled in water
2. Add one ounce of dried mushrooms which have been thoroughly washed and cut in pieces, an onion, carrot, bayleaf, parsley and dill
3. Boil all these and when the vegetables are nearly tender, remove from soup
4. Add the meat from the bouillon, cut up in small pieces
5. Let soup come to a boil and serve
