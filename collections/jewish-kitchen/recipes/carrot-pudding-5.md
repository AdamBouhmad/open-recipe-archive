## Ingredients

- Six ounces of grated carrot
- 6 oz. of bread crumbs
- 2 oz. of orange and lemon peel
- 6 oz. of chopped suet
- 8 eggs
- 1 lb. ground almonds
- 6 oz. of sifted sugar
- wine or brandy sauce

## Directions

1. Beat all up well together
2. Grease an earthenware mould, and sift sugar over it
3. Put in the mixture and boil for 3 hours
4. Serve with wine or brandy sauce
