## Ingredients

- a bullock's foot
- two quarts of water
- salt
- pepper
- 2 cloves
- 2 allspice
- a few sticks of marjoram
- thyme
- parsley
- a large carrot
- a tooth of garlic
- 2 good sized onions
- any dry crumbs of bread
- a few meal balls
- a few egg balls
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a little browning
- the juice of a lemon
- a little cayenne

## Directions

1. Put the bullock's foot in a good size saucepan with two quarts of water, salt, pepper, 2 cloves, 2 allspice, a few sticks of marjoram, thyme, parsley, a large carrot, a tooth of garlic, 2 good sized onions (only wash them, do not peel them), and any dry crumbs of bread you may have
2. Put it on the fire for a quick boil, then let it simmer for 4 hours
3. Take out the foot, strip off all the flesh, put it in a plate, return the bones to the saucepan
4. Let the whole of it simmer till the last thing at night
5. Strain it in a nice clean pan, put it by till the morning
6. On Friday skim off all the fat, put the stock in the saucepan
7. Cut in pieces the flesh of the foot, make a few meal balls, let them boil in the soup
8. Have ready a few egg balls, boiling ten minutes
9. Put in a basin a tablespoonful of flour, a little browning, the juice of a lemon, a little cayenne
10. Let it all boil up, turn it into your pan, and on Saturday you will have a fine soup
