## Ingredients

- tongue
- eggs
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- oil or fat
- white paper

## Directions

1. Cut the slices very thin
2. Beat up 3 or 4 eggs with some chopped parsley, marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
3. Put in the frying pan a little oil or fat to grease it
4. You must not have too much in or your omelet will not turn out well
5. Mix the slices of tongue with the beaten eggs
6. Turn it into the pan, fry it nicely
7. Turn it on a dish with a sheet of white paper under
8. Garnish with parsley
9. Grated cold tongue is very nice in fowl salads
