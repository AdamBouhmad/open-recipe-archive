## Ingredients

- 1 sheep's head, well seasoned and slit in halves
- stale bread
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- onion, chopped
- 1 egg
- fat from soup
- potatoes, boiled

## Directions

1. Make a nice stuffing of stale bread with parsley, marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and chopped onion
2. Mix well with one egg
3. Lay the stuffing over the inside of the head
4. Put in a dish a good lot of fat from soup or any not required for pastry, with salt and pepper
5. Lay in the head
6. Flour over the dressing
7. Boil some potatoes and put them round the head
8. Bake all together in the oven for about 1 hour and 1/4
