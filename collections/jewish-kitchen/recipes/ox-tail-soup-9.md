## Ingredients

- a large ox tail
- 1 large carrot
- a tooth of garlic
- a little salt and pepper
- pint and half of water
- a few small meat balls
- a quart of stock
- the juice of a lemon
- a dessert spoonful of browning

## Directions

1. Nicely chop a large ox tail, making every joint into 2 pieces.
2. Put it into a saucepan with 1 large carrot, a tooth of garlic, a little salt and pepper, and pint and half of water.
3. Let it boil till quite tender.
4. Boil a few small meat balls with it.
5. When done take out the carrot and cut it up in pieces the size of dice.
6. Put it back into the saucepan with a quart of stock.
7. Let it simmer for an hour.
8. Have in your tureen the juice of a lemon and a dessert spoonful of browning.
9. When you turn it into the tureen stir it well.
