## Ingredients

- oranges and lemons
- cold water
- loaf sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Clear the insides out when you have squeezed the juice from them
2. Lay them in cold water for 3 or 4 days, change the water each day
3. Boil them tender
4. Put some loaf sugar in a clean saucepan with sufficient water to make a thin syrup
5. Boil the peels in that for 2 or 3 hours, simmer gently
6. Turn them into a basin
7. Put them on again the next day, let them simmer again for an hour
8. If the syrup is not thick enough, add some more sugar; the syrup must be thick
9. Put them in a jar, they are ready for use directly they are done
