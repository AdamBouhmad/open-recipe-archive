## Ingredients

- one level teaspoon baking-soda
- three cups sour milk
- one egg
- salt
- one-half cup flour
- one tablespoon syrup

## Directions

1. Mash fine and dissolve one level teaspoon of baking-soda in three cups of sour milk
2. Beat one egg well
3. Put in a little salt and one-half cup of flour
4. Stir in the milk and make a smooth batter
5. Last stir in one tablespoon of syrup
6. Bake on a hot griddle
