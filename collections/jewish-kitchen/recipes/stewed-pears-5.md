## Ingredients

- a dozen fine large baking pears
- half a pound of white sugar
- sufficient cold water
- a small quantity of the peelings
- a few cloves
- a little cochineal

## Directions

1. Peel, core, and quarter a dozen fine large baking pears
2. Put them into a stewpan with half a pound of white sugar and sufficient cold water to cover them
3. Add a small quantity of the peelings, a few cloves, and a little cochineal tied up in a muslin bag
4. Let them stew gently and closely covered until tender
