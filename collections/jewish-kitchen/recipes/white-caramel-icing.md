## Ingredients

- two cups of brown sugar
- one cup of milk
- a small lump of butter

## Directions

1. Put on to boil two cups of brown sugar, one cup of milk and a small lump of butter
2. Boil until it gets as thick as cream
3. Beat with a fork or egg whip until thick and creamy
4. Spread quickly on cake
