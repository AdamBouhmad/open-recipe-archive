## Ingredients

- one egg
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one cup water
- one cup sifted flour
- butter or oil
- one-half pound potcheef
- butter size of an egg
- one egg
- pinch of salt
- cinnamon
- sugar
- grated peel of a lemon

## Directions

1. Beat up one egg, one-half teaspoon of salt with a fork
2. Add one cup of water and one cup of sifted flour
3. Beat until smooth
4. Grease a frying-pan very slightly with butter or oil
5. Pour in two tablespoons of the batter, tilting the pan so as to allow the batter to run all over the pan
6. Fry over a low heat on one side only
7. Turn out the semi-cooked cakes on a clean cloth with the uncooked side uppermost and let cool
8. Prepare filling using one-half pound of potcheef, a piece of butter size of an egg, one egg, pinch of salt, a little cinnamon and sugar to taste and grated peel of a lemon
9. Spread this mixture on the cooled dough
10. Fold over and tuck the edges in well
11. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and cinnamon
12. Fry in plenty of oil or butter
13. Serve hot
