## Ingredients

- eight pounds of gravy beef
- five pints of water
- sweet herbs
- an onion
- pepper
- salt
- a bundle of asparagus
- chopped parsley
- mint
- fried bread
- spinach

## Directions

1. Take eight pounds of gravy beef, with five pints of water, a few sweet herbs, and an onion shred, with a little pepper and salt
2. When the strength of the meat is sufficiently extracted, strain off the soup
3. Add to it a bundle of asparagus, cut small, with a little chopped parsley and mint
4. The asparagus should be thoroughly done
5. A few minutes before serving, throw in some fried bread cut up the size of dice
6. Pound a little spinach to a pulp, and squeeze it through a cloth
7. Stir about a tea-cup full of this essence into the soup
8. Let it boil up after to prevent a raw taste
