## Ingredients

- turkey
- forcemeat
- veal stuffings
- sausage meat
- tongue
- mushrooms
- white sauce
- cradle spit

## Directions

1. Have the turkey boned by the poulterer
2. Fill it with a fine forcemeat, which may be varied in several ways
3. The basis should be according to the receipt given for veal stuffings, forcemeats, sausage meat, tongue, and mushrooms added as approved
4. If boiled, serve with any fine white sauce, French cooks use the veloute or bechamel
5. If roasted, use a cradle spit if available, or carefully tie the turkey to the spit
