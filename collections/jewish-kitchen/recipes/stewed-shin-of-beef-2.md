## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 lb. fleshy part of shin of beef
- 2 or 3 large sliced onions
- salt
- pepper
- 1 blade of mace
- marjoram
- dried saffron
- 1 pint water
- potatoes
- 1 spoonful flour
- 2 spoonfuls vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut off all the flesh and cut it in nice pieces
2. Put pieces in a saucepan with 2 or 3 sliced onions, salt, pepper, a blade of mace, a little marjoram, a little dried saffron, and a pint of water
3. Half an hour before it is done, cut some potatoes in halves and lay them on top of the meat so they will cook with the steam
4. When quite done, rub a spoonful of flour with 2 spoonfuls of vinegar in a basin and throw it over
5. Serve as a relishing family dish
