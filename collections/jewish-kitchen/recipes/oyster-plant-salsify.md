## Ingredients

- salsify
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- white sauce
- flour
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape salsify and put at once in cold water with a little vinegar to keep from discoloring
2. Cut one-half inch slices and cook in boiling, salted water until soft
3. Drain and serve in white sauce
4. Or boil in salted, boiling water until tender and cut in four pieces lengthwise
5. Dredge with flour and sprinkle with a little salt
6. Fry in hot butter or fat until nicely browned
