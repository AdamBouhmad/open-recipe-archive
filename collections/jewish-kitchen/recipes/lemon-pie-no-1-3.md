## Ingredients

- rich puff paste
- a large juicy lemon
- one cup of boiling water
- one cup of sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- one teaspoon of cornstarch
- one teaspoon of butter
- whites of eggs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cover the reverse side of a deep pie-plate with a rich puff paste, and bake a light brown
2. Remove from the oven until the filling is prepared
3. Take a large juicy lemon, grate and peel and squeeze out every drop of juice
4. put it into a cup of boiling water to extract every particle of juice
5. Put the cup of water on to boil with the lemon juice and grated peel, and a cup of sugar
6. beat up the yolks of four eggs very light and add to this gradually the boiling lemon juice
7. Return to the kettle and boil
8. wet a teaspoon of cornstarch with a very little cold water, and add also a teaspoon of butter
9. when the boiling mixture has thickened remove from the fire and let it cool
10. Beat up the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth, add half of the froth to the lemon mixture and reserve the other half for the top of the pie
11. Bake the lemon cream in the baked pie-crust
12. Add a few tablespoons of powdered sugar and half a teaspoon of cream of tartar
