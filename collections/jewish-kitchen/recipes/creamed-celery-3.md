## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- salt
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the stalks of celery
2. Scrape off all rusted or dark spots
3. Cut into small pieces and drop in cold water
4. Having boiling water ready, put the celery into it, adding one-half teaspoon of salt for every quart of water
5. Boil until tender, leaving the cover partly off
6. Drain and rinse in cold water
7. Make a cream sauce, drop the celery into it, heat thoroughly and serve
