## Ingredients

- six eggs
- seven tablespoons of granulated sugar
- seven tablespoons of bread crumbs
- one-eighth pound of chopped almonds
- one-half teaspoon of allspice
- one tablespoon of jelly
- grated rind and juice of one lemon
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one-half teaspoon of cloves
- one-half wine glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs well
2. Add sugar and beat until it blisters
3. Add bread crumbs, almonds, jelly, spice, lemon, and brandy
4. Add beaten whites
5. Bake slowly about forty minutes
