## Ingredients

- fat
- steak
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- potatoes
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Put a little fat in a pan
2. Season the steak with salt and pepper and sprinkle a little flour over the steak
3. Fry it a nice brown colour
4. Have boiled and washed a few potatoes and mashed with the yolk of an egg
5. Turn the potatoes out in the dish you intend sending up the steak
6. Shape it nicely and lay the steak upon it
7. Serve it on a very hot dish
