## Ingredients

- rich crust
- four eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- one pint of milk
- grated nutmeg or grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Line the pie-plate with a rich crust
2. Beat up four eggs light with one-half cup of sugar, a pinch of salt, one pint of milk and grated nutmeg or grated lemon peel
3. pour in shell and bake in slow oven
