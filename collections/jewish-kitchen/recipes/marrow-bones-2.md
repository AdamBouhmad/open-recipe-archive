## Ingredients

- Marrow-bones
- thick dough
- red pepper
- toast
- cloth

## Directions

1. Have the bones cut into pieces two or three inches long
2. Scrape and wash them very clean
3. Spread a little thick dough on each end to keep the marrow in
4. Tie each bone in a piece of cloth and boil them for one hour
5. Remove the cloth and paste, and place each bone on a square of toast
6. Sprinkle with red pepper and serve very hot
7. Or the marrow-bone can be boiled without being cut, the marrow then removed with a spoon and placed on squares of hot toast
8. Serve for luncheon
