## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- one and one-half cups of granulated sugar
- one cup of mixed matzoth-meal and potato flour
- flavoring to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar together until very light
2. Add the flavoring, matzoth-meal and potato flour
3. Add last of all the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth
4. Stir lightly and bake in a moderately quick oven
