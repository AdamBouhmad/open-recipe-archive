## Ingredients

- three pounds of granulated sugar
- one and one-half ounces of tartaric acid
- one quart of hot water
- well-beaten whites of three eggs
- bicarbonate of soda
- ice-water

## Directions

1. Take three pounds of granulated sugar and one and one-half ounces of tartaric acid, both dissolved in one quart of hot water.
2. When cold add the well-beaten whites of three eggs, stirring well.
3. Bottle for use.
4. Put two large spoonfuls of this syrup in a glass of ice-water, and stir in it one-fourth of a teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda.
5. Any flavor can be put in this syrup.
