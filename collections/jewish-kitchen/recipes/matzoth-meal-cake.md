## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- one and a half cups of pulverized sugar
- grated rind of a lemon
- two teaspoons of ground cinnamon
- two squares of chocolate
- one teaspoon of allspice
- juice of an orange
- one-half wine-glass of wine
- three-quarters of a cup of matzoth meal
- one-quarter pound almonds

## Directions

1. To the yolks of eight eggs add one and a half cups of pulverized sugar
2. Stir until the consistency of batter
3. Add the grated rind of a lemon, two teaspoons of ground cinnamon and two squares of chocolate grated, one teaspoon of allspice
4. Add the juice of an orange, and one-half wine-glass of wine
5. Add three-quarters of a cup of matzoth meal finely sifted, and one-quarter pound almonds finely pounded
6. Fold in the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
7. Bake in a moderate oven for three-quarters of an hour
8. Try with a straw
