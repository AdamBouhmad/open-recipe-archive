## Ingredients

- equal quantities of butter and flour
- an egg

## Directions

1. Mix in the same manner equal quantities of butter and flour, taking care to have the flour dried for a short time before the fire
2. It may be folded and rolled five or six times
3. An egg well beaten and mixed with the paste is sometimes added
