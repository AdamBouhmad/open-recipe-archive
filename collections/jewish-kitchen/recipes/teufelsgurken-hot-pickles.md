## Ingredients

- large, green cucumbers
- salt
- whole white and black peppercorns
- small pickling onions
- horseradish
- bay leaves
- fennel
- caraway seeds
- cloves of garlic
- Spanish pepper
- mustard seed
- pickling vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare large, green cucumbers, cut each one lengthwise
2. Take out the seeds with a silver spoon
3. Cut each piece again so as to have four pieces out of one cucumber
4. When all are pared salt well and let them remain in the salt for twenty-four hours or more
5. Dry each piece, put in layers in a stone jar with whole white and black peppercorns, small pickling onions, which have been previously pared and salted overnight, pieces of horseradish, a few bay leaves, a little fennel, caraway seeds, a few cloves of garlic and also some Spanish pepper
6. Have a layer of the spices at the bottom of the jar
7. A handful of mustard seed put on the top layer will be an improvement
8. Boil enough pickling vinegar to cover well
9. Add a cup of sugar to a gallon of vinegar, boil and pour over hot
10. Boil again in three days and pour over the pickles after it gets cold
11. In two days pour off the vinegar and boil again and pour over the pickles hot
12. Boil three times altogether
