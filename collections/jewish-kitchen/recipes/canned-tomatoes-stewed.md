## Ingredients

- canned tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- sugar
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Add salt and pepper to the tomatoes.
2. Add a lump of butter the size of an egg and add one tablespoon of sugar.
3. Thicken with one teaspoon of flour wet with one tablespoon of cold water, stir into the tomatoes and boil up once.
