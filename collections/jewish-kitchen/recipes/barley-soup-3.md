## Ingredients

- a knuckle of mutton, or four pounds of the neck
- three quarts of water
- a sprig of parsley
- a couple of sliced turnips
- a carrot
- an onion or more
- white pepper
- salt
- a breakfast cup full of barley

## Directions

1. Put in a stew-pan, a knuckle of mutton, or four pounds of the neck, with three quarts of water, boil it gently and keep it well skimmed
2. Add a sprig of parsley, a couple of sliced turnips, a carrot, an onion or more, if approved, with a little white pepper and salt
3. Scald a breakfast cup full of barley and put it in the stew-pan with the meat
4. If when done, the soup is thin and watery, a little prepared barley, mixed smoothly, should be stirred in
