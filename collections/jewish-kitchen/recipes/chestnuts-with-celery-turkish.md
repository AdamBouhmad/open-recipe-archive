## Ingredients

- table celery
- celery root
- roasted chestnuts
- two tablespoons of olive oil
- one tablespoon of lemon juice
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and cut table celery and some celery root
2. Take roasted chestnuts, season with two tablespoons of olive oil
3. Put on to boil with the celery and one tablespoon of lemon juice
4. Boil all until celery is tender
5. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot
