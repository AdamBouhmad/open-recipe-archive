## Ingredients

- fully-ripe strawberries
- raspberries
- currants
- red cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of fully-ripe strawberries, raspberries, currants and red cherries.
2. The cherries must be stoned, taking care to preserve the juice and add to rest of juice.
3. Mix and press through a jelly-press or bag.
4. Measure the juice, boil a pint at a time, and to every pint allow a pound of sugar and proceed as with other fruit jellies.
