## Ingredients

- four or five pounds of breast of veal, or fillet from the shoulder
- veal stuffing
- water
- calf's-foot
- one onion
- blade of mace
- white pepper
- salt
- sprig of parsley
- beaten yolks of four eggs
- juice of two lemons
- button mushrooms or pieces of celery
- egg and forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Stuff the veal with a finely flavoured veal stuffing
2. Put it into a stewpan with water sufficient to cover it
3. Add a calf's-foot cut in pieces if desired
4. Season with one onion, a blade of mace, white pepper and salt, and a sprig of parsley
5. Stew the whole gently until the meat is quite tender
6. Skim and strain the gravy and stir in the beaten yolks of four eggs, and the juice of two lemons previously mixed smoothly with a portion of the gravy
7. Add button mushrooms, or pieces of celery stewed with the veal if desired
8. Garnish the dish with egg and forcemeat balls
