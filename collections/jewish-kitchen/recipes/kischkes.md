## Ingredients

- beef casings
- fat
- flour
- chopped onions
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare as above.
2. Boil for three hours in plenty of water.
3. When done, put in frying-pan with one tablespoon of fat, cover and let brown nicely.
4. Serve hot.
