## Ingredients

- four sour apples
- fritter batter
- fat or oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Choose four sour apples
2. Pare, core and cut them into small slices
3. Stir into fritter batter
4. Fry in boiling hot fat or oil
5. Drain on paper
6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve
