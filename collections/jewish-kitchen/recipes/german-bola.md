## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. German yeast
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 lb. butter
- 1 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. sweetmeats
- 1 lb. clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 oz. of German yeast in a cup of milk and 2 eggs all beat together.
2. Work 3/4 lb. of butter into 1 lb. of flour with the yeast, milk and eggs, to a paste.
3. Set it to rise for an hour.
4. Roll it out and work in 1/2 lb. of sweetmeats.
5. Bake in a slow oven.
6. Have ready 1 lb. of clarified sugar and pour over the cake boiling hot, as it comes out of the oven.
