## Ingredients

- a pint of finely grated bread crumbs
- milk and water
- cinnamon or lemon peel grated
- a couple of beaten eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Simmer the bread crumbs in a little milk and water, flavour with cinnamon or lemon peel grated
2. Add a couple of beaten eggs, and sweeten to taste
3. Drop a small quantity into the pan and fry like pancakes
