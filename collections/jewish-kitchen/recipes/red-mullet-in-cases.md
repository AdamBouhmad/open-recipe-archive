## Ingredients

- four mullets
- one dozen button mushrooms
- one tablespoon of finely chopped parsley
- two shalots
- juice of a lemon
- salt
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Oil some pieces of foolscap paper
2. Lay the fish on them and sprinkle over them the mushroom, parsley, shalot, lemon juice, pepper and salt
3. Fold them in the cases and cook on a well-greased baking-sheet in a moderate oven for about twenty or thirty minutes
4. Send to the table in cases very hot
