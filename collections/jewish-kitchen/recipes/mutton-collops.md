## Ingredients

- knuckle of mutton
- forcemeat for cutlets
- potatoes or Jerusalem artichokes
- bread
- gravy
- fried parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take from a fine knuckle a couple of slices
2. Cut and trim them in collops the size of a tea cup
3. Flatten them and spread over each side a forcemeat for cutlets
4. Fry them
5. Prepare potatoes or Jerusalem artichokes cut in slices of the same size and thickness, or pieces of bread cut with a fluted cutter, prepared as the collops and fried
6. Place alternately in the dish with them
7. Serve with a pure simple gravy, or very hot and dry on a napkin, garnished with fried parsley and slices of lemon
