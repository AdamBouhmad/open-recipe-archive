## Ingredients

- hartshorn shavings
- water
- sherry wine
- white sugar
- eggs
- lemon juice
- saffron

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of hartshorn shavings in two quarts of water over a gentle fire until it becomes thick enough to hang about a spoon
2. Strain it into a clean saucepan and add half a pint of sherry wine, and a quarter of a pound of white sugar
3. Clear it by stirring in the whites of a couple of eggs, whisked to a froth
4. Boil it for about four or five minutes
5. Add the juice of three lemons, and stir all together
6. When it is well curdled, strain it and pour into the mould
7. If the color is required to be deeper than the wine will make it, a little saffron may be boiled in it
