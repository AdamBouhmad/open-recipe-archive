## Ingredients

- a quart of the best vinegar
- 2 dozen ripe chilis
- 1 dozen of ripe chilis to every wine bottle of vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of the best vinegar
2. Pour it over 2 dozen ripe chilis if not for immediate use
3. To every wine bottle of vinegar put in 1 dozen of ripe chilis
4. Cork it down
