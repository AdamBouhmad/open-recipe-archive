## Ingredients

- sweet green peppers
- one pound of chopped meat
- one egg
- one onion, grated
- salt
- citric acid (size of bean dissolved in a little water)
- one tablespoon of fat
- sliced tomatoes
- one-half cup of water
- sugar
- one-half tablespoon of flour
- one-half tablespoon of fat

## Directions

1. Take sweet green peppers, cut off blossom end
2. To one pound of chopped meat take one egg, grate in one onion, a little salt, citric acid (size of bean dissolved in a little water), mix all together
3. Place this mixture in the peppers, but do not fill too full
4. Set the entire top of peppers in place
5. Melt one tablespoon of fat in a saucepan, add sliced tomatoes, then the stuffed peppers and one-half cup of water
6. Let steam one-half or three-quarters of an hour
7. Make sweet sour with a little citric acid and sugar to taste
8. Thicken gravy with one-half tablespoon of flour, browned with one-half tablespoon of fat
