## Ingredients

- grape-fruit
- bananas
- oranges
- white mayonnaise dressing
- maraschino cherries
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the grape-fruit in halves and remove the pulp, being careful to get none of the tough white skin
2. Mix with bananas and oranges and stir in white mayonnaise dressing
3. Remove all skin from the inside of the grape-fruit and fill with the mixture, heaping it high and ornamenting with maraschino cherries
4. Lay each half in a bed of lettuce leaves and serve
