## Ingredients

- pair of ribs or round of beef
- 1/2 pint vinegar
- 1/2 pint water
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- allspice
- 3 or 4 bay leaves
- cup of raspings from the baker

## Directions

1. Have a pair of ribs, boned and rolled, or a piece of the round
2. Put it in a stewpan with 1/2 pint of vinegar and 1/2 pint of water
3. Season with onion, salt, pepper, allspice, 3 or 4 bay leaves, a cup of raspings from the baker
4. Let it stew gently for 3 or 4 hours, according to the weight of the beef
5. This dish is excellent when cold
