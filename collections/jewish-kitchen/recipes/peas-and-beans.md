## Ingredients

- 1 red bread pan with cover
- 1 bullock's foot
- 3 lbs. boned brisket
- 1 lb. worsht
- 1 pint Spanish peas
- 1 lb. small white beans
- 2 large onions, sliced
- 2 teeth of garlic
- 2 white sticks of celery, cut fine
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne
- 3 large potatoes
- 6 eggs
- dried saffron
- cold water

## Directions

1. Season the red bread pan by standing it on the hob with boiling water for 2 or 3 hours before using
2. Lay the peas and beans in soak from the night previous
3. Put the foot at the bottom of the pan
4. Layer in the peas and beans, onions, and all the seasoning
5. Put in the beef, the potatoes, and the eggs
6. Add some dried saffron and the worsht
7. Fill 3 parts with cold water
8. Put it in the oven as soon as ready so that it boils well
9. Leave in the oven all night
10. When dishing up, put the foot and meat in one dish and the eggs and potatoes in another
11. Remove the fat before putting the soup in the tureen
