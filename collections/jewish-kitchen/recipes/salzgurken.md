## Ingredients

- half-grown cucumbers
- fennel
- dill
- bay leaves
- whole peppers
- grape leaves
- cherry leaves
- salt
- water
- egg

## Directions

1. Lay cucumbers in water overnight
2. Wipe each one dry and reject all that are soft at the ends
3. Lay a layer of cucumbers in a new barrel or wine keg
4. Then a layer of the following spices: Fennel, dill, bay leaves, a few whole peppers
5. Cover with grape and cherry leaves
6. Begin again with a layer of cucumbers and fill in alternate layers until all are used
7. Boil enough salt and water to just cover them
8. Test the strength of the water by laying an egg in it, if it rises the water has enough salt in it, if not, add more salt
9. Pour this over the cucumbers when cold
10. Get a cooper to tighten up the barrel
11. Roll it in the sun and allow it to stay there for two weeks, turning over the barrel once each day
