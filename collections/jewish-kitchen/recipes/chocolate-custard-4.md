## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- three tablespoons of sugar
- one heaping tablespoon of grated unsweetened chocolate
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one tablespoon of hot water
- one pint of milk heated to boiling
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of three eggs, three tablespoons of sugar till light.
2. Dissolve one heaping tablespoon of grated unsweetened chocolate, one tablespoon of sugar and one of hot water.
3. When dissolved, add slowly one pint of milk heated to boiling.
4. Pour this hot mixture over the beaten eggs and sugar.
5. Cook in double boiler, stirring constantly till it thickens.
6. When cool, flavor with vanilla, and place on ice.
7. When ready to serve, half-fill small punch glasses with the custard, heap over them sweetened whipped cream, flavored; putting on top of each glass, and serve cold.
