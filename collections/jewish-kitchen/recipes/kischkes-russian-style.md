## Ingredients

- beef casings
- fat
- flour
- chopped onions
- salt
- pepper
- two large onions

## Directions

1. Make a filling of fat, flour (using one-third cup fat to one cup flour) and chopped onions.
2. Season well with salt and pepper.
3. Cut them in short lengths, fasten one end, stuff and then fasten the open end.
4. If they are not already cleaned plunge into boiling water and scrape the surface exposed after filling the casing until cleaned.
5. Slice two large onions in a roasting-pan, and roast the kischkes slowly until well done and well browned.
6. Baste frequently with liquid in the pan.
