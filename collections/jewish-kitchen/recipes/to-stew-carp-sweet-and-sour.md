## Ingredients

- carp
- large Spanish onion
- one tablespoonful of oil
- salt
- pepper
- 3 cloves
- 3 allspice
- ground mace
- 1/2 pint of ale or porter
- pennyworth of gingerbread
- brown sugar
- vinegar
- browning
- lemon
- chopped parsley
- port wine

## Directions

1. Wash and cleanse the carp from the blood
2. Cut a good sized carp in 6 pieces
3. Slice in a stewpan a large Spanish onion with one tablespoonful of oil, salt, pepper, 3 cloves, 3 allspice, a little ground mace
4. Put on 1/2 pint of ale or porter and set it on to boil
5. Add the carp with pennyworth of gingerbread, crumble it to dust
6. When the carp is done, lay it in the dish you intend sending to table
7. Rub the liquor through a sieve and put it back into the saucepan
8. Mix in a basin a spoonful of brown sugar with 3 of vinegar
9. If not brown enough from the gingerbread, add little browning
10. Stir it all smooth and put it over the fish with a spoon
11. Cover it all over alike
12. Cut slice of lemon and garnish the dish with them
13. On top of each slice put a little chopped parsley as green as possible
