## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Salt water
- Two cups of sugar
- One cup of water

## Directions

1. Halve oranges, scoop out, then scrape inside
2. Lay the peel in salt water overnight
3. Make syrup of two cups of sugar and one cup of water
4. When boiled thick, cut orange-peel in small strips and drop them into boiling liquid
5. Let them remain about ten minutes
6. Remove strips carefully, spreading them on waxed paper to dry
