## Ingredients

- lemon and orange peel
- boiling water
- sugar
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Take out the greater portion of the white inside
2. Throw the rinds into boiling water and simmer gently for twenty minutes
3. Drain, weigh, and take a pound of sugar to every pound of peel
4. Put a layer of sugar and a layer of fruit into the preserving kettle
5. Stand it over a slow fire until the sugar melts
6. When melted, cook slowly until the rinds are transparent
7. Lift them out
8. Drain them and when nearly dry roll in granulated sugar
