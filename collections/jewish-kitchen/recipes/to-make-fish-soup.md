## Ingredients

- onion
- 2 ounces of butter
- vegetables
- water
- salt
- pepper
- blade of mace
- gurnet
- turbot
- fish
- small fish
- bread balls
- flour
- browning
- butter sauce
- capers
- parsley

## Directions

1. Slice an onion thin in a saucepan and fry it in 2 ounces of butter
2. Have ready all kinds of vegetables, cut as for a julienne soup
3. Put in the saucepan pint of water, with salt, pepper, a blade of mace
4. Let it all stew well
5. Then put on a gurnet, a piece of turbot, or any other fish you intend dining off to boil with a little salt in a pint and half of water
6. When it is done, take it out and put it at the side to keep hot
7. Strain the water on to the vegetables
8. Have ready some small fish or bread balls and boil them in with the vegetables
9. Rub in a basin a spoonful of flour with a little browning to thicken the soup
10. Serve the gurnet with a good butter sauce with capers, and garnish with parsley
