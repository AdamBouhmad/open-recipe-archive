## Ingredients

- half a pint of cream
- half a pint of new milk
- four ounces of rice-flour or potatoe-flour
- two to three ounces of fresh butter
- white sifted sugar
- grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take half a pint of cream and the same quantity of new milk, and warm them together in a clean saucepan
2. meanwhile make a smooth batter with four ounces of rice-flour or potatoe-flour
3. stir into the milk
4. let it simmer, stirring all the time till it thickens
5. add two to three ounces of fresh butter, and white sifted sugar enough to sweeten, and a little grated lemon peel
6. take it off the fire
