## Ingredients

- breast of mutton
- stuffing
- sliced onion
- salt
- pepper
- French beans
- 1/2 pint of water
- flour
- moist sugar
- vinegar
- browning

## Directions

1. Prepare the breast just as you did for the peas, with the stuffing
2. Put it in a stewpan with sliced onion, salt, and pepper
3. Wash and cut the French beans
4. Put them on top of the mutton with 1/2 pint of water
5. When done, rub in a basin one spoonful of flour, one of moist sugar, two of vinegar, and browning to colour
6. Mind that you take off all the fat before you put the sauce in
