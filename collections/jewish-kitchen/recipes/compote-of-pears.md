## Ingredients

- pears
- sugar
- water
- stick cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Pare the fruit, leaving on the stems
2. Stew in sugar and a very little water
3. Flavor with stick cinnamon and a few cloves (take out the head of each clove)
4. When soft place each pear carefully on a platter until cold
5. Arrange them nicely in a glass bowl or flat glass dish, the stems all on the outer rim
6. Pour over them the sauce, which should be boiled thick like syrup
7. Eat cold
