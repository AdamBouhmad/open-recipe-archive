## Ingredients

- parsley
- savory
- thyme
- sweet marjoram
- shalot
- chervil
- sage

## Directions

1. Select a variety of herbs in equal quantities: parsley, savory, thyme, sweet marjoram, shalot, chervil, and sage
2. Dry these in the oven
3. Pound them finely and keep them in bottles well stopped
