## Ingredients

- one pound of carrots
- one heaping tablespoon of drippings or chicken fat
- four tablespoons of sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- grated peel of one lemon
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- grated nutmeg
- three tablespoons of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- pinch of salt
- beaten egg whites
- fat for pudding-dish

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of carrots, let them get perfectly cold before grating them.
2. In the meanwhile cream a heaping tablespoon of drippings or chicken fat and four tablespoons of sugar.
3. Add gradually the yolks of four eggs, the grated peel of one lemon, one teaspoon of cinnamon, a little grated nutmeg, three tablespoons of flour, one teaspoon of baking-powder, pinch of salt, and the beaten whites last.
4. Heat a few tablespoons of fat in a pudding-dish.
5. Pour in the mixture and bake in a moderate oven one hour.
6. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon and return to oven for a few moments to brown.
7. Serve hot.
