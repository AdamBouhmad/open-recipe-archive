## Ingredients

- Two pounds of chops, cut nicely, not too fat
- 1 lb. of good chuck steak, cut in pieces
- 4 lb. of large potatoes all one size, cut in thick slices
- 3 large onions
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. Put the steak at the bottom of your stewpan with a layer of the potatoes, and some thick slices of onions, then a layer of chops, potatoes, and onions, let the last layer only be onions and potatoes.
2. Add pepper and salt to taste and 1 pint of water.
3. Stew gently for 2½ hours, keep the lid close, not to let the steam out, shake the saucepan occasionally.
