## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of good rice
- salt
- four eggs
- 1/2 pint of water
- 3 ozs. of sugar
- grated nutmeg
- rind of a fresh lemon or chopped lemon and orange peel
- 1 oz. of dried currants
- 2 ozs. of melted suet

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 lb. of good rice in water with a dash of salt
2. When it has absorbed the water, beat up four eggs with 1/2 pint of water, 3 ozs. of sugar, a little grated nutmeg, and part of a rind of a fresh lemon or a little chopped lemon and orange peel, with 1 oz. of dried currants and 2 ozs. of melted suet
3. Mix all well together
4. Grease a dish and bake it
