## Ingredients

- six quarts of berries
- one quart of sugar

## Directions

1. Put one quart of the fruit in the preserving kettle; heat slowly, crushing with a wooden potato masher; strain and press through a fine sieve.
2. Return the juice and pulp to the kettle; add the sugar; stir until dissolved; then add the remaining quarts of berries.
3. Boil sixteen minutes, counting from the time they begin to boil.
4. Skim well while boiling, and put into jars as directed.
