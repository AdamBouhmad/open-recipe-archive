## Ingredients

- two quarts of spinach
- one-half cup of chopped onions
- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- two cups of milk
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash, pick over and cook two quarts of spinach for twenty minutes
2. drain, chop and rub through a sieve and return to the water in which it was cooked
3. add one-half cup of chopped onions, cook until thoroughly done
4. thicken with a white sauce made by melting two tablespoons of butter to which is added two tablespoons of flour
5. stir until smooth, add two cups of milk
6. season with one-half teaspoon of salt and pepper and add the spinach mixture
