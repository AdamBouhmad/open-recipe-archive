## Ingredients

- remains of any cold poultry or meat
- seasoning
- cold dressed vegetable
- one or more eggs
- gravy
- square of toast
- lemon-juice
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Mince any cold poultry or meat and season highly
2. Add to it any cold dressed vegetable
3. Mix it up with one or more eggs
4. Let it simmer till hot in a little gravy
5. Have ready a square of toast and serve it on it
6. Squeeze over a little lemon-juice and sprinkle with white pepper
