## Ingredients

- about six ounces of savoy biscuits
- three ounces of ratafias
- six ounces of sugar
- grated rind of half an orange
- grated rind of a lemon
- a small piece of cinnamon
- a wine-glass full of good maraschino
- a wine-glass full of fine noyeau
- one pint of cream
- well beaten yolks of six eggs
- half a pint more cream
- one ounce of dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Trim straitly about six ounces of savoy biscuits, so that they may fit closely to each other
2. Line the bottom and sides of a plain mould with them
3. Put into a stewpan three ounces of ratafias, six of sugar, the grated rind of half an orange, the same quantity of the rind of a lemon, a small piece of cinnamon, a wine-glass full of good maraschino, or fine noyeau, one pint of cream, and the well beaten yolks of six eggs
4. Stir this mixture for a few minutes over a stove fire
5. Strain it
6. Add half a pint more cream, whipped, and one ounce of dissolved isinglass
7. Mix the whole well together
8. Set it in a basin imbedded in rough ice
