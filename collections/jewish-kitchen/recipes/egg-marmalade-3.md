## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 pint of water
- 12 yolks of eggs
- 36 almonds, blanched and pounded
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil 1 lb. of loaf sugar with 1/2 pint of water to a syrup
2. Beat up 12 yolks of eggs with 36 almonds, blanched and pounded
3. When well beat up, stir the almonds and eggs into the boiling syrup
4. Keep stirring all the time in a jug, put in a saucepan of boiling water
5. When it becomes thick, pour it into custard glasses
6. Put a little cinnamon on the top
