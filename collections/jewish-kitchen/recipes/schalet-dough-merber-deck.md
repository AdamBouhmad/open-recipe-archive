## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of drippings
- pinch of salt
- two tablespoons of granulated sugar
- one egg
- one cup of sifted flour
- cold water, about three tablespoons

## Directions

1. Cream four tablespoons of drippings, add a pinch of salt, two tablespoons of granulated sugar.
2. Beat in well one egg.
3. Add one cup of sifted flour and enough cold water to moisten dough so that it can be rolled out — about three tablespoons will be sufficient; it depends on the dryness of the flour how much is required.
