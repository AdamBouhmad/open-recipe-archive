## Ingredients

- peach peelings
- peach kernels
- water
- juice of two or three lemons
- sugar

## Directions

1. Use the peelings of your peaches when you are through canning and preserving.
2. Add one-third of the peach kernels and put all on to boil in a stone jar on the back of the stove with a little water.
3. When soft, strain through a jelly-bag by letting it drip all night.
4. In the morning add the juice of two or three lemons and boil as you would jelly.
5. Set a pint of juice on to boil and boil for five minutes.
6. Add a pound of sugar and boil five minutes more, but it must boil very hard.
7. Bottle in wide-mouthed bottles or jars.
8. Seal.
