## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of flour
- one and one-half pints huckleberries
- one pint crumbed bread
- one quart milk
- three tablespoons of sugar
- pinch of salt
- flakes of butter

## Directions

1. Sprinkle four tablespoons of flour over one and one-half pints huckleberries and set aside for half an hour
2. Soak one pint crumbed bread in one quart milk
3. Add three tablespoons of sugar, pinch of salt, and the huckleberries
4. Put all into a greased pudding dish with flakes of butter on top
5. Bake forty-five minutes
6. Serve with hard sauce
