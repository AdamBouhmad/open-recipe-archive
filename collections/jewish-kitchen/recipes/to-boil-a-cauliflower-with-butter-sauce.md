## Ingredients

- 1 fine white cauliflower
- salt
- water
- melted butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put a fine white cauliflower in salt and water to boil, be careful it does not break
2. Put it to drain in the colander over the boiling water
3. Make a good sauce of melted butter with chopped parsley
4. Put the cauliflower in a dish, and pour over the sauce
