## Ingredients

- cold flaked fish
- salt
- pepper
- cold cooked rice
- butter
- cracker or bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Line a buttered baking-dish with cold flaked fish.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Add a layer of cold cooked rice, dot with butter.
4. Repeat and cover with cracker or bread crumbs.
5. Bake fifteen to twenty minutes.
