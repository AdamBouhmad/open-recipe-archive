## Ingredients

- coffee cake dough
- poppy seed mixture
- one-half pound of poppy seed
- milk
- one-quarter of a pound of sugar
- yolks of three eggs
- beaten whites of eggs
- two ounces of sifted flour
- one-quarter of a pound of melted butter

## Directions

1. Take coffee cake dough
2. Let the dough rise again for an hour
3. spread with a poppy seed mixture, after cutting into squares
4. fold into triangles and pinch the edges together
5. Lay in well-buttered pans, about two inches apart
6. let them rise again
7. spread with poppy seed filling
8. Take one-half pound of poppy seed (mohn) which have previously been soaked in milk and then ground
9. add one-quarter of a pound of sugar and the yolks of three eggs
10. Stir this all together in one direction until quite thick
11. then stir in the beaten whites to which you must add two ounces of sifted flour and one-quarter of a pound of melted butter
12. Fill the tartlets and bake
