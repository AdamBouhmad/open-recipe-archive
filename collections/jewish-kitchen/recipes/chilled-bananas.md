## Ingredients

- ice-cold bananas
- lemon
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cut ice-cold bananas down lengthwise
2. Lay these halves on a plate with a quarter of a lemon and a generous teaspoon of powdered sugar
3. Eat with a fork or spoon after sprinkling with lemon juice and dipping in sugar
