## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- three tablespoons of oil
- four eggs
- a pound of chopped nuts
- one-half pound of honey
- one-half pound of sugar
- cold water
- dry ginger

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, one teaspoon of baking-powder, three tablespoons of oil, and four eggs
2. Knead very well
3. Roll out in strips three inches long
4. Place on tins and bake
5. Take a pound of chopped nuts, one-half pound of honey, and one-half pound of sugar
6. Mix thoroughly with wooden spoon and boil with the cakes until brown
7. Take off the stove, wet with cold water, spread out on board
8. When cold, pat with the hands to make thin and sprinkle with dry ginger
