## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of savoy biscuits
- 2 oz. of sponge cake
- 2 oz. of ratafias
- 2 oz. of good raisins
- 2 oz. of lemon and orange peel
- almond oil
- sifted sugar
- 1 pint of almond milk
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- orange-flower or vanilla flavour
- 6 eggs
- raspberry, strawberry jam, or orange marmalade

## Directions

1. Grease a shape well with almond oil, with sugar sifted over
2. Make at the bottom a star with the finger biscuits
3. Ornament with a few of the raisins, a few pieces of the candied peel and ratafias
4. Crumble up the whole of the remainder of the cakes, mix with the peel and raisins
5. Mix in a basin a pint of almond milk, 1/4 lb. of sugar, orange-flower or vanilla flavour
6. Beat in the yolks of 6 eggs
7. Beat the whites to a snow and stir in
8. Stir in also a few spoonfuls of raspberry, strawberry jam, or orange marmalade
9. Put the cover on the mould with a piece of writing paper under
10. Stand it in a flat saucepan with boiling water
11. Boil 1.5 hours
12. Serve with a good sauce
