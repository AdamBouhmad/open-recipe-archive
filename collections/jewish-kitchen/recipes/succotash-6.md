## Ingredients

- equal parts corn
- equal parts any kind of beans
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of corn, cut from the ear, and any kind of beans.
2. Boil them separately.
3. Stir them lightly together, and season with butter, salt, and pepper and add a little cream if convenient.
