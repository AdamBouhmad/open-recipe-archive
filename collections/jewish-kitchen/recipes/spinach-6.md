## Ingredients

- spinach with large leaves
- salt
- water
- butter
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut off the roots and pick spinach over carefully, cutting off all the withered leaves and stems
2. Put the leaves in cold salt water to soak for half an hour
3. Shake the leaves about and turn them over several times
4. Drop them in a large pan of water and rinse well
5. Lift them out separately and drop back into a second pan of water
6. Continue washing in fresh water until there is not a grain of sand to be found in the bottom of the pan
7. Let a cup of water come to a boil
8. Lift the spinach out of the cold water pan with the water dripping from it and put it into the pot into the boiling water
9. Put the lid on the pot
10. Turn the fire a little low and let it cook slowly for fifteen minutes, stirring every now and then to keep it from sticking to the bottom of the pot
11. Just before taking up the spinach put some salt in it
12. Drain off the water and put a big tablespoon of butter and one-quarter teaspoon of pepper in it
13. Take it out of the pot and place it in a long, flat dish
14. Slice some hard-boiled eggs and place the slices all around the spinach for a kind of border
