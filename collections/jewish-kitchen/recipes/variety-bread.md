## Ingredients

- one cake of compressed yeast
- two cups of lukewarm water or milk
- two teaspoons of salt
- three cups of bread or wheat flour
- one cup of cornmeal
- one cup of rye flour
- one-half cup of dark molasses

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in water or milk
2. add salt, flour, cornmeal, rye flour and molasses, and mix very thoroughly
3. Let rise
4. shape into loaves
5. let rise again
6. bake in a moderate oven for forty-five minutes
