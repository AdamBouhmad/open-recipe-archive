## Ingredients

- one generous cup of butter
- two cups of New Orleans molasses
- two teaspoons of powdered ginger
- one teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
- half a large nutmeg
- two teaspoons of baking-soda
- half a cup of hot water
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put in a mixing bowl one generous cup of butter which has stood in a warm place until quite soft.
2. Add two cups of New Orleans molasses.
3. Whip these ingredients to a foam.
4. Add two teaspoons of powdered ginger, one teaspoon of powdered cinnamon and grate in half a large nutmeg.
5. Stir these spices well through the mixture.
6. Dissolve two teaspoons of baking-soda in half a cup of hot water.
7. Stir it through the mixture.
8. Stir in enough sifted flour to make a light dough just stiff enough to roll out.
9. Dust the pastry board well with flour and rub the rolling-pin well with flour, then flour the hands well.
10. Take out some of the dough, put it on the pastry board, quickly roll it out to the thickness of a quarter of an inch.
11. Cut the dough out with a round cutter, with or without scallops.
12. Put them in well-floured baking-pans and bake in a slow oven till a golden brown.
