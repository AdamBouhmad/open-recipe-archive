## Ingredients

- one yellow onion
- four tablespoons of chicken fat, melted
- salt
- slices of rye bread

## Directions

1. Chop one yellow onion very fine
2. Add four tablespoons of chicken fat, melted
3. Add salt to taste
4. Serve on slices of rye bread
