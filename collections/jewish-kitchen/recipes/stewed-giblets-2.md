## Ingredients

- one or more sets of giblets
- veal or chicken
- good gravy
- browning
- mushroom powder
- lemon-juice
- one glass of white wine
- forcemeat balls
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Scald one or more sets of giblets
2. Set them on the fire with a little veal or chicken or both in a good gravy
3. Season to taste and thicken the gravy
4. Color it with browning
5. Flavor with mushroom powder and lemon-juice and one glass of white wine
6. Add forcemeat balls a few minutes before serving
7. Garnish with thin slices of hard boiled eggs
