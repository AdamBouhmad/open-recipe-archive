## Ingredients

- large, very hard Lima beans
- two large onions
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one-quarter cup of rice

## Directions

1. Soak the large, very hard Lima beans overnight
2. To a pound of beans take two large onions
3. When the beans are soft add the onions browned in fat, salt, pepper, a tablespoon of sugar, a quarter cup of rice
4. let all simmer until the rice is done
