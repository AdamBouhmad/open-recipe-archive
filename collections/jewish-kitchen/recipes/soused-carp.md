## Ingredients

- carp
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1/2 pint of vinegar
- 3 cloves
- allspice
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- garlic

## Directions

1. Put it in a saucepan with 1/2 pint of water, 1/2 pint of vinegar, 3 cloves and allspice, salt, pepper and a little cayenne, a tooth of garlic
2. Let it cook till done
3. Lay the carp in a dish and strain the liquor over
