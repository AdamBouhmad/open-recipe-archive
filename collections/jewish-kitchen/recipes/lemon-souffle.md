## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of the crumb of bread
- pint of almond milk
- 2 oz. of melted fat
- rind of 2 fresh lemons
- 1/2 lb. powdered sugar
- juice of a lemon
- yolks of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Crumble 1 lb. of the crumb of bread
2. Pour over a pint of almond milk and 2 oz. of the melted fat
3. Beat this all up together till it is like a cream
4. Grate the rind of 2 fresh lemons
5. Add 1/2 lb. powdered sugar and the juice of a lemon
6. When this is all well mixed stir in the yolks of 6 eggs, well beaten
7. Then stir in the whites beat to a snow
8. Put it in a greased mould
9. Bake the same as the others
