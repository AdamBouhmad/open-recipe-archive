## Ingredients

- 6 ounces of melted veal fat
- 6 oz. of flour
- pint and a half of almond milk
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of preserved ginger
- almond oil
- sifted sugar
- brandy or rum sauce

## Directions

1. Mix 6 oz. of flour with 6 ounces of melted veal fat
2. Stir it over a slow fire
3. Add a pint and a half of almond milk gradually with the above over the fire
4. Beat up the yolks of 6 eggs
5. Cut 1/2 lb. of preserved ginger fine
6. Beat the whites of the eggs to a snow
7. Mix in well
8. Grease a mould well with almond oil and sifted sugar
9. Tie it down and let it steam for 2 hours
10. Serve with a brandy or rum sauce
