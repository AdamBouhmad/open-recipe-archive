## Ingredients

- one-half cup of granulated sugar
- one-fourth pound of stale macaroons
- one-half pint of heavy cream
- three eggs
- vanilla or sherry wine

## Directions

1. Stir yolks of eggs until thick and add sugar and stir again
2. Add whipped cream and whipped whites of eggs and grated macaroons
3. Flavor to taste
4. Put this all into freezer and pack outside with ice and salt alternately
5. Do not turn
6. Let stand five or six hours, adding ice from time to time
7. When serving put grated macaroons on top
