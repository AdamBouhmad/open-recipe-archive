## Ingredients

- one pint milk
- three tablespoons rice
- two tablespoons granulated sugar
- one tablespoon vanilla
- yolk of one egg
- one tablespoon flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil in a double boiler one pint of milk, three tablespoons of rice and two tablespoons of granulated sugar
2. Cook for fifty to sixty minutes until rice is thoroughly cooked and mixture thickens
3. Remove from fire and when a little cool, add one tablespoon of vanilla and the yolk of egg into which one tablespoon of flour has been smoothly stirred
4. Mix all thoroughly together
5. Pour by spoonfuls on hot buttered griddle
6. Let the cakes brown on one side, turn over and brown on the other
