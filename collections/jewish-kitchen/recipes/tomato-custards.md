## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- eggs
- onion
- bay leaf
- parsley
- water
- cream sauce
- green peas

## Directions

1. Simmer for fifteen minutes in a covered saucepan four cups chopped tomatoes, four eggs, one sliced onion, one bay leaf, and sprig of parsley.
2. Strain and if there be not two cups of liquid, add water.
3. Beat four eggs and add to liquid.
4. Pour into greased baking cups and stand them in a pan of water and bake until firm — about fifteen minutes.
5. Turn out and serve with cream sauce containing green peas.
