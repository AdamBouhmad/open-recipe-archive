## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and boil till tender, then squeeze and strain it
2. Press it in a towel till almost dry
3. Put it on a board, and chop it as finely as possible
4. Return it to the saucepan, with butter, pepper, and salt
5. Stir it all the time, and let it boil fast
