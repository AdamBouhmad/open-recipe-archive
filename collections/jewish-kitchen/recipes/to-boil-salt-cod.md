## Ingredients

- salt cod
- parsley
- boiled parsnips
- floury boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Mind that it is well soaked and looks very white
2. Put it to boil in cold water
3. As soon as it has boiled take it off the fire and let it stand at the side, simmering for 1/2 an hour
4. When done serve it on a fish napkin garnished with parsley
5. Serve with it boiled parsnips cut in nice pieces, and floury boiled potatoes
6. Make the sauce and serve separate in the sauceboat
