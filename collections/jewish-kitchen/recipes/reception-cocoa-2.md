## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 tablespoons cocoa
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- salt
- 3 cups milk
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- whipped cream or marshmallows

## Directions

1. Stir one cup of boiling water gradually onto two tablespoons of cocoa, two tablespoons of sugar and one teaspoon of cornstarch, a few grains of salt (that have been well mixed) in a saucepan
2. Let boil five minutes, stirring constantly
3. Heat three cups of milk in a double boiler
4. Add the cocoa mixture and one-half teaspoon of vanilla
5. Beat with egg-beater until foamy
6. Serve hot in chocolate cups, with a tablespoon of whipped cream on top of each cup, or place two marshmallows in each cup and fill cups two-thirds full of hot cocoa
