## Ingredients

- cold dressed veal
- a little water or gravy
- salt
- pepper
- grated or minced lemon peel
- sippets of toast

## Directions

1. Cut in small square pieces about the size of dice, cold dressed veal
2. Put it into a saucepan with a little water or gravy
3. Season simply with salt, pepper, and grated or minced lemon peel
4. Garnish the mince with sippets of toast
