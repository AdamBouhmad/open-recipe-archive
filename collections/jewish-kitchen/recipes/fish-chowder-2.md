## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of codfish or haddock
- six large tomatoes
- six large potatoes
- two large onions
- salt
- pepper
- three pints of water
- one-half pint of cream
- one-fourth cup of butter
- paprika

## Directions

1. Skin and bone one and one-half pounds of codfish or haddock
2. Cut six large tomatoes, six large potatoes, two large onions in small pieces
3. add salt, pepper, three pints of water and cook one hour
4. Add one-half pint of cream, one-fourth cup of butter, and paprika
5. Cook five minutes and serve
