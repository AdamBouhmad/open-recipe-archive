## Ingredients

- raised bread dough
- beaten egg yolk
- poppy seed

## Directions

1. Take pieces of raised bread dough, roll three-eighths inch thick and four or five inches long
2. Place in floured pan, far apart
3. brush tops with beaten yolk and poppy seed
4. Let rise
5. bake in a hot oven until brown
