## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Sift one cup of flour and one teaspoon of salt together
2. Chop in one tablespoon of butter, and add milk to make a very stiff dough
3. Chop thoroughly and knead until smooth
4. Make into small balls and roll each one into a thin wafer
5. Place in shallow greased and floured pans and bake in a hot oven until they puff and are brown
