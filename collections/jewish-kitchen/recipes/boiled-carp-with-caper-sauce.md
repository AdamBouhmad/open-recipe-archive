## Ingredients

- carp
- oil
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- caper sauce
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the carp well and slit it open
2. Dry it well
3. Lay it in a little oil and the juice of a lemon, with salt and pepper, for an hour before cooking it
4. Then put it on the gridiron before a slow and clear fire
5. Cook well through when broiled on both sides
6. Serve it with caper sauce in which put 2 teaspoonfuls of anchovy sauce
7. Put the sauce over the fish
