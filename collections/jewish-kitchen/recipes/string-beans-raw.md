## Ingredients

- string beans
- salt

## Directions

1. String the beans very carefully, and cut into fine short lengths
2. Sprinkle salt over and through them, mixing thoroughly, say to twenty-five pounds of beans, two pounds of salt
3. Let them remain in the salt overnight
4. Pack the shredded beans as tightly as possible into jars or kegs, without any of their juice
5. In two weeks look them over, remove the cloth and wash it
6. When cooking the beans, take out as many as may be required for a meal and soak them in cold water overnight
7. In the morning set on to boil in cold water
8. Boil for one hour
