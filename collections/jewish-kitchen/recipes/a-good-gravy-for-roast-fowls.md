## Ingredients

- a little stock
- the juice of a lemon
- a little mushroom powder
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a little stock, squeeze in the juice of a lemon
2. Add a little mushroom powder, cayenne pepper and salt
3. Thicken with flour
