## Ingredients

- half a pound of strained honey
- half a pound of sifted powdered sugar
- half a pound of almonds
- half a pound of finest flour
- one ounce of citron
- peel of a lemon
- a little grated nutmeg
- a pinch of ground cloves
- a wineglass of brandy

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of strained honey, half a pound of sifted powdered sugar, half a pound of almonds cut in half lengthwise, half a pound of finest flour, one ounce of citron cut or chopped extremely fine, peel of a lemon, a little grated nutmeg, also a pinch of ground cloves and a wineglass of brandy
2. Set the honey and sugar over the fire together
3. Put in the almonds, stir all up thoroughly
4. Put in the spices and work into a dough
5. Put away in a cold place for a week
6. Roll about as thick as a finger
7. Bake in a quick oven
