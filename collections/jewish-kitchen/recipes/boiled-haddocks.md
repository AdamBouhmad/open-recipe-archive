## Ingredients

- haddocks
- salt
- lemon
- parsley
- caper, anchovy, or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Wash haddocks well inside and out
2. Put them on in cold water with salt
3. Let them boil for half an hour
4. Let them drain well on the strainer before dishing them up on a fish napkin
5. Garnish with slices of lemon and parsley
6. Serve with caper, anchovy, or tomato sauce
