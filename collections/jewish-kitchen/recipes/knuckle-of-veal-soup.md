## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- 2 quarts of water
- salt
- pepper
- blade of mace
- turnip
- carrot
- celery
- leek
- onion
- tooth of garlic
- 1 lb. of potatoes
- a little dried saffron
- motsa balls

## Directions

1. Boil a knuckle of veal in water with salt, pepper, blade of mace, turnip, carrot, celery, leek, onion, and garlic
2. Let it boil till the meat leaves the bone
3. Take out the knuckle and cut all the flesh in small pieces
4. Put the flesh back in the soup
5. Boil and mash quite fine 1 lb. of potatoes and stir them well in
6. Make a few small motsa balls and boil with
7. Colour with a little dried saffron
