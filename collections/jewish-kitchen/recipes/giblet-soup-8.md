## Ingredients

- a set of giblets
- a quart of the dark stock
- a pint of water
- a little salt and pepper
- an onion
- a little marjoram
- a pickled walnut
- a few small meat balls
- a spoonful of flour
- a spoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the giblets well.
2. Skin the feet and chop them and the wings in several pieces.
3. Chop the neck in several pieces.
4. Cut the gizzard and heart in several pieces.
5. Put the giblets in a small saucepan with a pint of water, a little salt and pepper, an onion chopped fine, a little marjoram, and a pickled walnut chopped small.
6. Let them stew well and add a few small meat balls.
7. When all this is well cooked, put them into the quart of dark stock.
8. Mix a spoonful of flour with a spoonful of lemon juice in a small basin and mix it into the soup.
9. Stir it well.
10. Serve it up to table very hot.
