## Ingredients

- red mullet
- garlic
- oil
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- saffron
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the mullets
2. let them dry in a cloth
3. put in a stewpan a tooth of garlic fried in 2 spoonfuls of oil
4. when it fries a light colour take out the garlic
5. put in the juice of a large lemon
6. add a little salt, pepper, and a little cayenne, dried saffron to colour
7. put in the mullets
8. cook for half an hour
9. dish up
10. garnish with parsley
