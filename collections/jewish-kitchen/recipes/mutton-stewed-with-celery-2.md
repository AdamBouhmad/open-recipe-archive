## Ingredients

- best end of a neck of mutton or fillet from the leg or shoulder
- water
- carrot
- turnip
- forcemeat balls
- celery
- yolks of two eggs
- flour
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take the best end of a neck of mutton, or a fillet taken from the leg or shoulder
2. place it in a stewpan with just enough water to cover it
3. throw in a carrot and turnip, and season, but not too highly
4. when nearly done remove the meat and strain off the gravy
5. return both to the stewpan with forcemeat balls and some fine celery cut in small pieces
6. let all stew gently till perfectly done
7. stir in the yolks of two eggs, a little flour, and the juice of half a lemon
8. mix the yolks and lemon juice with a little of the gravy before pouring in the stewpan
9. take care to prevent curdling
