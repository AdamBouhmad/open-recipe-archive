## Ingredients

- best end of a neck of mutton
- flour
- carrots
- turnips
- button onions
- water
- pepper
- salt
- browning
- mushroom powder

## Directions

1. Cut off the best end of a neck of mutton into chops
2. flour and partly fry them
3. lay them in a stewpan with carrots, sliced turnips cut in small round balls, some button onions
4. cover with water
5. skim frequently
6. season with pepper and salt to taste
7. color the gravy with a little browning and a spoonful of mushroom powder
