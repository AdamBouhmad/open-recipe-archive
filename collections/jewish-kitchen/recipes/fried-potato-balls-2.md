## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 large potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- 2 eggs
- flour
- butter or oil

## Directions

1. Mash 4 or 5 large potatoes with a little salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg, and a little chopped parsley.
2. Mix it up with 2 eggs into a paste.
3. Roll it out on a pasteboard with flour the same as making paste.
4. Cut it in strips 2 inches wide and 6 long.
5. Roll up each strip, dust it with flour.
6. Fry them in butter or oil.
