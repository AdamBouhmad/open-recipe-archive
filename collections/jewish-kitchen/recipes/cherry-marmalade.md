## Ingredients

- three pounds of sweet cherries
- one pound of sour cherries
- two pounds of sugar
- cinnamon bark
- cloves
- brandied paper

## Directions

1. To three pounds of sweet and one pound of sour cherries allow two pounds of sugar.
2. Weigh the cherries when stemmed and pitted.
3. Make a syrup of the sugar, add cinnamon bark and cloves.
4. Put in the sweet cherries first, adding the sour ones half an hour later.
5. Boil down thick and cover the jars with brandied paper.
