## Ingredients

- fine white cauliflower
- salt
- cloves
- allspice
- mace, 1 or 2 blades
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Select a fine white cauliflower
2. Lay it in water for an hour with a handful of salt
3. Take it out and let it drain in the colander
4. Strip off all the green
5. Divide each little white bunch by itself
6. When dry, put them into a jar or bottles seasoned with salt, a few cloves and allspice, and a blade or two of mace
7. Fill up with cold vinegar
8. Cork jar or bottles very tight
