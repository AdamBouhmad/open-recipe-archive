## Ingredients

- one-half cup of rice
- one and one-half cups of hot milk
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- a few seeded or sultana raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the rice as for boiling in water
2. Cook one-half cup of rice with one and one-half cups of hot milk and one-half teaspoon of salt, adding a few seeded or sultana raisins if desired
3. Serve hot like boiled rice or press into small cups, cool and serve with cream and sugar
