## Ingredients

- large long black plum
- good moist sugar

## Directions

1. Choose the large long black plum; to each gallon of which add three pounds of good moist sugar.
2. Bake them till they begin to crack.
3. Put them in pots, of a size for once using, as the air is apt to spoil the jam.
