## Ingredients

- turnips
- 1 onion
- 1 ounce of butter
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 1 teacup of milk
- flour
- water
- parsley

## Directions

1. Turnips are stewed exactly the same as the artichokes.
2. When you dish up the turnips sprinkle parsley over.
