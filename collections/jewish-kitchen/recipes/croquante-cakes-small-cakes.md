## Ingredients

- three-fourths pound of shelled almonds
- one-half pound of citron
- flour
- three-fourths pound of sugar
- six eggs
- three-fourths pound of flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch and cut in halves three-fourths pound of shelled almonds
2. Slice one-half pound of citron
3. Mix well together and roll in a little flour
4. Add to them three-fourths pound of sugar
5. Add six eggs well beaten
6. Add the rest of the flour (three-fourths pound)
7. Butter shallow pans
8. Put in the mixture about two inches thick
9. Bake in a quick oven
10. Slice cake in strips three-fourths of an inch wide and turn each piece
11. Put back in oven and bake a little longer
12. When cold put away in tin box
