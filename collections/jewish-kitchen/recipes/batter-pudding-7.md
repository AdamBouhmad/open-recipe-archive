## Ingredients

- three ounces of flour
- four beaten eggs
- one pint of milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stir in three ounces of flour, four beaten eggs, and one pint of milk
2. sweeten to taste
3. mix to a smooth batter about the thickness of good cream
4. boil in a buttered basin
