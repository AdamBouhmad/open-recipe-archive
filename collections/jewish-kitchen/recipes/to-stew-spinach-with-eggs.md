## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt
- 1 onion
- 2 spoonfuls of oil or 2 ounces of butter
- 1 teacup of boiling water
- 5 or 6 eggs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and pick the spinach well, pass it through several waters, put it to drain.
2. Put it in a saucepan with some salt, no water, put it at the side to boil, spinach gives a great deal of water.
3. When done put it in a colander to drain, then chop it very fine on a board.
4. Chop very fine in a saucepan an onion, fry it with 2 spoonfuls of oil, or 2 ounces of butter.
5. Put a teacup of boiling water.
6. Lay in the spinach, let it simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Break 5 or 6 eggs, each one separately in a cup, then put the eggs on the spinach.
8. When all the eggs are in, sprinkle over some pepper and salt, let them boil with the spinach.
9. When they are set dish them up gently, not to disturb the eggs.
