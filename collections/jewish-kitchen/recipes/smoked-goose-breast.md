## Ingredients

- breast of a fat goose
- salt
- pepper
- saltpetre

## Directions

1. Take the breast of a fat goose; leave the skin on
2. Rub well with salt, pepper and saltpetre
3. Pack in a stone jar and let it remain pickled thus four or five days
4. Dry well
5. Cover with gauze and send away to be smoked
