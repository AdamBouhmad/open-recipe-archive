## Ingredients

- pound-cake
- jam or preserve
- whites of eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a pound-cake, cut it in slices about half an inch in thickness
2. spread each slice with jam or preserve
3. replace them to the original form
4. cover the cake with whites of eggs and sugar, whisked to a froth
5. set it in a cool oven to dry
