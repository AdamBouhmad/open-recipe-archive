## Ingredients

- one pound of cheese
- one-eighth pound of butter
- one-half glass of ale
- one teaspoon of mustard
- one egg well beaten
- salt
- paprika
- hot buttered toast

## Directions

1. Put butter in pan, and when melted add cheese cut up or grated
2. Stir, and as cheese melts, add ale
3. When it begins to bubble, add egg well beaten
4. Stir continually to keep from getting stringy
5. In two or three minutes it will be ready to serve
6. Pour over hot buttered toast
