## Ingredients

- one quart of milk or cream
- one tablespoon of sweet butter
- one spoon of flour or cornstarch
- cold milk
- pieces of toasted bread
- crackers

## Directions

1. Heat a quart of milk or cream, add a tablespoon of sweet butter and thicken with a spoon of flour or cornstarch, wet with cold milk
2. Pour, boiling, over pieces of toasted bread cut into dices; crackers may also be used
