## Ingredients

- six large tomatoes
- boiling water
- two onions
- butter
- water
- rice
- salt
- pepper
- cheese
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take six large tomatoes, pour boiling water over them and skin them.
2. Scrape all the inside out with a spoon, put in saucepan together with two onions, a tablespoon of butter, one pint of water; let this boil for a little while; strain, place back on stove.
3. Pour into this one-half pound of rice, let it cook tender; add salt, pepper, a tablespoon of butter and a little grated cheese.
4. Fill the tomatoes with this mixture, dip them in egg and bread crumbs, then fry till nice and brown.
