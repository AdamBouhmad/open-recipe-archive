## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one-eighth teaspoon of salt
- two tablespoons of matzoth meal
- fat

## Directions

1. Add one-eighth teaspoon of salt to two eggs, beat slightly, stir in two tablespoons of matzoth meal.
2. Heat a little fat in spider, pour in egg mixture; when cooked on one side turn on the other.
3. Roll the pancake and cut into noodles one-eighth inch wide.
4. Drop into boiling soup before serving.
