## Ingredients

- six large potatoes
- two eggs per pint of grated potato
- one tablespoon flour
- one-half teaspoon salt
- pepper
- onion juice

## Directions

1. Peel six large potatoes and soak several hours in cold water
2. Grate and drain
3. For every pint allow two eggs, about one tablespoon of flour, one-half teaspoon of salt, a little pepper, a little onion juice if desired
4. Beat eggs well and mix with the rest of the ingredients
5. Drop by spoonfuls on a hot greased spider in small cakes
6. Turn and brown on both sides
7. Serve with apple sauce
