## Ingredients

- cream sauce
- milk
- two cups boiled potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a cream sauce, a little thinner than usual by adding extra milk
2. Cut boiled potatoes into small cubes
3. Mix them thoroughly with the cream sauce
4. Cook in a double boiler until the potatoes are thoroughly hot
5. Add a little chopped parsley if desired
6. Serve
