## Ingredients

- a fine thick piece of brisket of beef not fat
- pickle
- ground all-spice
- black pepper
- cloves
- ginger
- nutmegs
- a little brown sugar
- pounded dried sweet herbs
- water

## Directions

1. Take a fine thick piece of brisket of beef not fat
2. Let it lay three days in a pickle
3. Take it out and rub in a mixture of spices consisting of equal quantities of ground all-spice, black pepper, cloves, ginger and nutmegs, and a little brown sugar
4. Repeat this daily for a week
5. Cover it with pounded dried sweet herbs
6. Roll or tie it tightly
7. Put it into a pan with very little water
8. Bake slowly for eight hours
