## Ingredients

- 1 lb. suet
- 1 lb. plums well stoned
- 1 lb. currants
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- 1/2 oz. of mixed spice
- a little salt
- 2 soaked biscuits
- 1 lb. of the fine sieved meal
- 8 eggs
- 2 oz. of chopped almonds
- rind of a fresh lemon chopped finely
- burnt rum
- Wine or rum sauce

## Directions

1. Whisk 8 eggs and mix well
2. Add 2 oz. of chopped almonds and the rind of a fresh lemon chopped finely
3. Mix in the suet, plums, currants, sugar, mixed spice, salt, soaked biscuits, and fine sieved meal
4. Beat it up for 1/2 an hour
5. Boil in a shape or cloth
6. Serve with burnt rum and Wine or rum sauce
