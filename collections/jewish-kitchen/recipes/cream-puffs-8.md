## Ingredients

- One cup of hot water
- One-half cup of butter
- One cup of sifted flour
- Three eggs
- One cup of milk
- One cup of sugar
- One egg
- Three tablespoons of flour
- Vanilla to flavor

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of hot water and one-half cup of butter together
2. While boiling stir in one cup of sifted flour dry
3. Take from the stove and stir to a thin paste
4. After this cools add three eggs unbeaten
5. Stir vigorously for five minutes
6. Drop in tablespoonfuls on a buttered tin
7. Bake in a quick oven twenty-five minutes
8. Open the oven door no oftener than is absolutely necessary
9. Be careful that they do not touch each other in the pan
10. For cream: Stir the flour in a little of the milk
11. Boil the rest
12. Turn this in and stir until the whole thickens
13. When both the puffs and cream are cool open the puff a little way with a sharp knife
14. Fill them with the cream
