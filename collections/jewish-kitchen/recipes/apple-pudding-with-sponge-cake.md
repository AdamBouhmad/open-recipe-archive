## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- two scant cups of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- grated peel of a lemon
- a cup of matzoth flour, sifted very fine
- apples

## Directions

1. Make a sponge cake batter of eight eggs and two scant cups of sugar and a pinch of salt and add the grated peel of a lemon and beat until thick, at least half an hour
2. Fold in a cup of matzoth flour, sifted very fine
3. Pour this batter over the apples and bake in a moderate oven
4. Serve with wine sauce
