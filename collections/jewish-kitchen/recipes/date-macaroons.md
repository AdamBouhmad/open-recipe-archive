## Ingredients

- thirty dates
- one-half pound of almonds
- whites of two eggs
- one cup of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Stone thirty dates and chop them fine
2. Cut one-half pound of almonds lengthwise in slices, but do not blanch them
3. Beat the whites of two eggs until foamy
4. Add one cup of powdered sugar, and beat until stiff
5. Add the dates, then the almonds, and mix very thoroughly
6. Drop mixture with teaspoon in small piles on tins, one-half inch apart
7. Bake thirty minutes in a very slow oven or until dry
8. They are done when they leave the pan readily
