## Ingredients

- red beetroots
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar
- salt
- yolks of eggs
- beef stock (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash beetroots thoroughly and peel
2. Boil in a moderate quantity of water from two to three hours over a slow fire until a strong red liquor is obtained
3. Strain off the liquor
4. Add lemon juice, sugar, and salt to taste
5. When cooled a little, stir in sufficient yolks of eggs to slightly thicken it
6. May be used either cold or hot
7. In the latter case a little home-made beef stock may be added to the beet soup
