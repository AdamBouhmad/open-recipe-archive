## Ingredients

- thin slices from any tender part of beef
- flour
- clarified fat
- good stock
- pepper
- salt
- pickled gherkins

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices off from any tender part
2. Divide them into pieces of the size of a wine biscuit
3. Flatten and flour them
4. Lightly fry in clarified fat
5. Lay them in a stew-pan with good stock
6. Season to taste
7. Chop pickled gherkins small and add to the gravy a few minutes before serving
