## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- chops
- 2 spoonfuls of curry
- 1 spoonful of flour
- juice of a lemon
- large rice
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Chop an onion in a stewpan with salt and pepper and a little nutmeg
2. Place in the chops and cut off some of the fat
3. Let them cook
4. When done stir in a basin 2 spoonfuls of curry and 1 of flour with the juice of a lemon
5. Stir this over the chops
6. Have some large rice boiled quite dry with a little salt
7. Put a bordering of rice round your dish
8. Throw a little chopped parsley over it
