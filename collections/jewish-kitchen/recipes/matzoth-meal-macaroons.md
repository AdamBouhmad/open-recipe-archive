## Ingredients

- egg yolk
- one teaspoon of matzoth meal
- pinch of salt
- egg white
- butter or fat
- prunes

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolk separately
2. Add one teaspoon of matzoth meal and pinch of salt
3. Whip white to a snow, fold in the whites
4. Fry by tablespoonfuls in butter or fat
5. Serve with prunes
