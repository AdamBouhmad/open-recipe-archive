## Ingredients

- brains
- parsley
- onion
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- rind of a fresh lemon
- crumbs of bread
- boiled potato
- 3 eggs
- dripping or oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains as before, then put them in a saucepan to boil
2. When cold chop them with a little parsley, a little onion
3. Put in a little fine marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and a little of the rind of a fresh lemon chopped fine
4. Add a few crumbs of bread, about an ounce, or one boiled potato mashed
5. Mix with 3 eggs
6. Fry them in dripping or oil
7. Serve them very hot, with lemon juice, and garnish with parsley
