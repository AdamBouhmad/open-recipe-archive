## Ingredients

- a three and one-half pound fat chicken
- salt
- one-fourth cup of fat
- one medium-sized onion
- paprika
- boiling water
- one cup of rice
- one teaspoon of salt
- three cups of chicken broth

## Directions

1. Cut a three and one-half pound fat chicken in pieces to serve
2. Salt it and let stand several hours
3. Heat one-fourth cup of fat in an iron kettle
4. Add one medium-sized onion, minced
5. Fry golden brown and set aside
6. Fry the chicken in the fat and when nicely browned, add paprika to taste and boiling water to cover
7. Let simmer one hour
8. Soak one cup of rice in cold water and drain
9. Add the fried onion and one teaspoon of salt
10. Gradually add three cups of chicken broth, more if necessary
11. When nearly done add the chicken and finish cooking in a slow oven, one-half hour
