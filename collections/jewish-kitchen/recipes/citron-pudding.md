## Ingredients

- two ounces of citron
- two ounces of candied orange and lemon peel
- four ounces of loaf sugar
- four ounces of fresh butter
- fine puff paste
- yolks of four eggs
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Cut in slices two ounces of citron and the same quantity of candied orange and lemon peel
2. Add to them four ounces of loaf sugar and four ounces of fresh butter
3. Line a shallow dish with fine puff paste
4. Beat up to a froth the yolks of four eggs and the whites of two
5. Fill the dish with these ingredients
6. Bake half an hour
