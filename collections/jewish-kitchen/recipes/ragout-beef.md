## Ingredients

- beef
- fat of smoked beef
- 1/2 pint beef gravy
- allspice
- cloves
- pepper
- salt
- garlic
- glass of port-wine
- lemon
- cayenne
- mushroom ketchup
- forced-meat balls

## Directions

1. Take a nice piece of beef, well cut, lard it with the fat of smoked beef
2. Place it in a stewpan with 1/2 pint of beef gravy highly seasoned with allspice, cloves, pepper, salt, and garlic
3. When the beef is cooked remove it from the gravy
4. Skim the gravy carefully and remove from it every particle of fat
5. Add to it a glass of port-wine, the juice of a lemon, a pinch of cayenne, and a little mushroom ketchup
6. Glaze the beef if you wish to cook extra nice
7. A few small forced-meat balls may be put round the dish
