## Ingredients

- a dozen carrots
- two heads of celery
- two onions
- a good stock, about two quarts
- a little lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a dozen carrots scraped clean and rasp them, but do not use the core
2. Take two heads of celery and two onions thinly sliced
3. Season to taste
4. Pour over a good stock, say about two quarts
5. Boil it
6. Pass it through a sieve
7. Return it to the saucepan
8. Boil it up and squeeze in a little lemon juice or add a little vinegar
