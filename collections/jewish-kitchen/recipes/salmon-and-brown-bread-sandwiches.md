## Ingredients

- one cup salmon
- mustard
- salt
- cayenne
- bread
- thin slices of cucumber
- parsley

## Directions

1. Flake one cup salmon and rub it to a paste.
2. Add mustard, salt, and cayenne.
3. Spread on the bread and cover with a layer of thin slices of cucumber.
4. Place another piece of bread on top, press lightly and arrange with sprigs of parsley on the platter.
