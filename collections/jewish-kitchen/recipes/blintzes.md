## Ingredients

- flour
- egg
- salt
- water
- oil or fat
- force meat
- one-half pound apples
- one ounce ground sweet almonds
- one ounce powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- juice of one-half lemon
- egg white

## Directions

1. Make dough as directed for cheese blintzes
2. Prepare filling of force meat, highly seasoned, or filling of one-half pound of apples peeled and cored then minced with one ounce of ground sweet almonds, one ounce of powdered sugar, a pinch of cinnamon, juice of one-half lemon
3. Mix well and bind with the beaten white of egg
4. Spread either of these mixtures on the dough
5. Fold over and tuck edges in well
6. Fry in plenty of oil or fat
7. Sprinkle those containing the fruit mixture with sugar and cinnamon
8. May be served either hot or cold
