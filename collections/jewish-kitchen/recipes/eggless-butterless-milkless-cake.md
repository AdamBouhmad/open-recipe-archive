## Ingredients

- One package of seeded raisins
- Two cups of sugar
- Two cups of boiling water
- One teaspoon of cinnamon
- One teaspoon of cloves
- Two tablespoons of Crisco, chicken schmalz or clarified drippings
- One-half teaspoon of salt
- One teaspoon of soda dissolved in water
- Three cups of flour

## Directions

1. Boil all together five minutes
2. Cool, add one teaspoon of soda dissolved in water
3. Add three cups of flour
4. Bake forty-five minutes
5. Make two cakes in layer pans
