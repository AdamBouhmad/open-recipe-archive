## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- bread crumbs
- thyme
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- one egg
- chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Have the blade removed, and fill the space with a stuffing made of bread crumbs, thyme, lemon juice, salt, pepper to taste and one egg, also chopped mushrooms if desired
2. Sew up the opening, press and tie it into good shape and roast
