## Ingredients

- the yolks of four eggs
- the juice of a fine lemon
- a tea-spoonful of flour
- a little cold water

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of four eggs with the juice of a fine lemon, a tea-spoonful of flour, and a little cold water
2. Mix well together
3. Set it on the fire to thicken, stirring it to prevent curdling
