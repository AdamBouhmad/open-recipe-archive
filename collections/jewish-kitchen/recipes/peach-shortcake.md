## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup of pulverized sugar
- a pinch of salt
- one cup of flour
- grated peel of a lemon
- peaches
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Make a sponge cake batter of four eggs, one cup of pulverized sugar, a pinch of salt and one cup of flour
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar until very light
3. Beat until the consistency of dough and add the grated peel of a lemon, and last the sifted flour
4. No baking-powder necessary
5. Bake in jelly tins
6. Cut the peaches quite fine and sugar bountifully
7. Put between layers
8. Eat with cream
