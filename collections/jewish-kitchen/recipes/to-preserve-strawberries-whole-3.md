## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of loaf sugar
- 2 pints of water
- 2 lbs. of fine, fresh, hard strawberries

## Directions

1. Put in a preserving-pan 3 lbs. of loaf sugar with 2 pints of water and let it boil to a syrup
2. Skim it as it boils up
3. Have 2 lbs. of fine, fresh, hard strawberries, pick off the stalks
4. Let them simmer in the syrup for 10 minutes
5. Do this for 2 days
6. Then boil up the syrup very stiff and put the strawberries in
