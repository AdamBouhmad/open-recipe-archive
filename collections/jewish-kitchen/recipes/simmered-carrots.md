## Ingredients

- one quart carrots
- one tablespoon of butter or drippings
- three tablespoons of sugar
- one teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape and slice one quart carrots roundwise
2. Put them in a saucepan with one tablespoon of butter or drippings, three tablespoons of sugar and one teaspoon salt
3. Cover closely and let simmer on a slow fire until tender
