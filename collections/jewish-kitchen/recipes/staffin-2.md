## Ingredients

- fruit prepared as for Lamplice
- paste
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Line the dish with the paste
2. Lay the fruit in alternate layers with paste till the dish is filled
3. The paste must form the top layer
4. Pour clarified sugar over before it is put into the oven
