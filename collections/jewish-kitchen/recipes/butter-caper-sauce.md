## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 spoonful of flour
- 1/2 a teacup of milk or water
- salt
- pepper
- good spoonful of capers

## Directions

1. Put in a small saucepan 2 oz. of butter, 1 spoonful of flour, 1/2 a teacup of milk or water, salt, pepper
2. Set it to warm and keep shaking the saucepan all the time till it thickens
3. Stir in a good spoonful of capers
