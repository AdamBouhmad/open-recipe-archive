## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- Two cups of powdered sugar
- Yolks of four eggs
- Three cups of flour
- Two teaspoons of baking-powder
- One cup of sweet milk
- One teaspoon of cinnamon
- Whites of four eggs
- Two cups of huckleberries

## Directions

1. Stir to a cream one cup of butter and two cups of powdered sugar
2. Add gradually the yolks of four eggs
3. Sift into this three cups of flour, adding two teaspoons of baking-powder in the last sifting
4. Add one cup of sweet milk alternately with the flour to the creamed butter, sugar and yolks
5. Spice with one teaspoon of cinnamon
6. Add the stiff-beaten whites of the eggs
7. Lastly, stir in two cups of huckleberries which have been carefully picked over and well dredged with flour
8. Be careful in stirring in the huckleberries that you do not bruise them
9. Use a wooden spoon for this purpose
10. Bake in a moderately hot oven
11. Try with a straw, if it comes out clean, your cake is baked
