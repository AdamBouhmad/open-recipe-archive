## Ingredients

- best pieces from the carcase
- boiling fat
- egg
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- slices of sausage
- potatoes
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut off the best pieces you can from the carcase left, make them of a good shape, the thigh will cut in two pieces, the drumsticks two pieces if you chop the stick off, and cut pieces off the breast.
2. Have a pan with boiling fat, dip the pieces in egg, mix with parsley, marjoram, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg, fry them a light brown.
3. Fry some slices of sausage in the same way.
4. Mash some potatoes nicely, lay them in a dish, put the fried fowl round.
5. Squeeze the juice of a lemon, strain over the fowl, garnish with parsley.
6. This is a nice dish for lunch.
