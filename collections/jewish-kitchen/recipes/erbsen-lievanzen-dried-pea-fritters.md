## Ingredients

- one cup of dried peas
- two ounces of butter
- soaked bread
- milk
- salt
- one teaspoon of sugar
- one yolk and one whole egg
- one ounce of blanched and pounded almonds

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of dried peas
2. Pass through a hair sieve
3. Pour into a bowl
4. Add two ounces of butter rubbed to a cream
5. Add soaked bread (soaked in milk)
6. Stir all into a smooth paste
7. Add salt, one teaspoon of sugar, one yolk and one whole egg, and one ounce of blanched and pounded almonds
8. If too thick add more egg, if too thin add more bread
9. Fry a nice brown
