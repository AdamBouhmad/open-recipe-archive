## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one cup of milk
- one and one-half cups of gluten flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of two eggs
2. Add one cup of milk
3. Add one and one-half cups of gluten flour, two teaspoons of baking powder
4. Beat well
5. Stir in the whites of the two eggs
6. Bake in hot buttered gem pans about twenty minutes
