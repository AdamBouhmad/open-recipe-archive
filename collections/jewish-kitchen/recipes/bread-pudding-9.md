## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of bread crumbs
- a pint of sweet milk
- two eggs
- a half cup of sugar
- grated peel of one lemon
- raisins
- powdered sugar
- jelly sauce or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak one and one-half cups of bread crumbs in a pint of sweet milk for half an hour
2. Separate the whites and yolks of two eggs, setting the whites in a cool place until needed
3. Beat the yolks with a half cup of sugar and add the grated peel of one lemon and stir into the bread crumbs
4. Put in some raisins and pour into a greased pudding dish and bake in a moderate oven, about half an hour
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, adding half a cup of powdered sugar
6. Spread this on top of pudding and return to the oven and brown delicately
7. May be eaten hot or cold, with jelly sauce or whipped cream
