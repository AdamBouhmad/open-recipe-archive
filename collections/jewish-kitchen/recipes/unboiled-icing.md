## Ingredients

- white of one egg
- water
- confectioner's sugar

## Directions

1. Take the white of one egg and add to it the same quantity of water
2. Stir into this as much confectioner's sugar to make it of the right consistency to spread upon the cake
3. Flavor with any flavoring desired
4. Color as desired by adding fruit coloring
