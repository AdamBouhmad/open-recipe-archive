## Ingredients

- string or wax-beans
- boiling water
- five sour apples per quart of beans
- thin peel of one lemon
- juice of one and one-half lemons
- small teaspoon of salt
- two tablespoons of cider vinegar
- quarter cup of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Put the beans into sufficient boiling water to just cover them
2. Cook for one hour and a half to two hours, depending upon the tenderness of the beans
3. Meanwhile, prepare five sour apples for each quart of beans; peel, core and cut in pieces
4. When the beans are done, add the apples, the thin peel of one lemon, the juice of one and one-half lemons, a small teaspoon of salt, and two tablespoons of cider vinegar
5. Let the apples cook on top of the beans until they are thoroughly done
6. Mix well with a good quarter cup of granulated sugar
7. Serve the next day warmed up for best results
