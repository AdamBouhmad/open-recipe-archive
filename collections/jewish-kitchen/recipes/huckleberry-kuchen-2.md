## Ingredients

- kuchen dough
- butter
- cornstarch
- milk
- sugar
- vanilla
- huckleberries
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Line cake-pans which should be long and narrow with rich kuchen dough, having previously greased them well
2. Make a paste of cornstarch, one cup of milk, one tablespoon of butter and one teaspoon of cornstarch wet with cold milk
3. Boil until thick, sweeten and flavor with vanilla and spread on top of the cake dough
4. Sprinkle thickly with huckleberries which have been carefully picked, sugared and sprinkled with ground cinnamon
5. Bake in a quick oven
