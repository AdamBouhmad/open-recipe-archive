## Ingredients

- one-half cup of molasses
- one-half cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of butter
- one-half teaspoon of ginger
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- one-half cup of milk
- two and one-half cups of sifted flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- one-fourth cup of currants or seeded raisins

## Directions

1. Mix one-half cup of molasses, one-half cup of sugar, one tablespoon of butter, and warm slightly
2. Beat up well and stir at least ten minutes
3. Add the following spices: one-half teaspoon each of ginger and cinnamon
4. Gradually add one-half cup of milk and two and one-half cups of sifted flour in which has been sifted two teaspoons of baking powder
5. One-fourth cup of currants or seeded raisins may be added
6. Bake in well-greased gem pans and eat warm for tea or lunch
