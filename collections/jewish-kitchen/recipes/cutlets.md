## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- egg
- flour
- a spoonful of flour
- a small cup of gravy
- salt
- pepper
- a little lemon juice or lemon pickle

## Directions

1. Cut them into proper shape and beat them with a roller until the fibre of the meat is entirely broken
2. Cover them with egg and sprinkle with flour, or a preparation for cutlets may be spread over them
3. Fry them of a fine brown
4. Remove the cutlets to a hot dish
5. Add to the fat in which the cutlets have been fried, a spoonful of flour, a small cup of gravy, salt, pepper, and a little lemon juice or lemon pickle
