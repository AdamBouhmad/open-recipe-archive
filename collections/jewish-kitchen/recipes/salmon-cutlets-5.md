## Ingredients

- boiled salmon
- mashed potatoes
- bread crumbs
- chopped parsley
- flour
- mace
- egg
- pepper
- salt
- oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take the remains of some boiled salmon or a small can of salmon, three tablespoons of mashed potatoes, one of bread crumbs, one of chopped parsley, a little flour, mace, an egg, pepper and salt
2. Mix the ingredients well together, bind with the egg, let stand an hour
3. Form into little flat cutlets, roll in bread crumbs and fry in hot oil
4. Drain on paper and send to table garnished with parsley
