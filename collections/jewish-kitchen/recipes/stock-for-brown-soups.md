## Ingredients

- the strained white stock
- a bullock's foot
- bunch of dried herbs such as marjoram, lemon thyme, parsley
- a little basil
- a large onion
- a tooth of garlic
- 3 or 4 cloves
- a few allspice
- pepper
- salt
- a carrot
- sticks of celery
- any cooked bones
- crust of bread
- 2 shalots
- 3 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Put the strained white stock back into the stock-pot with a bullock's foot, a bunch of dried herbs, a large onion, a tooth of garlic, cloves, allspice, pepper, salt, a carrot, sticks of celery, any cooked bones, crust of bread, and 2 shalots.
2. Add 3 quarts of water.
3. Let it simmer, without stopping, on the hob for 4 or 5 hours.
4. Take out the foot, remove the flesh from it, and put it in the larder.
5. Return the bones, and let it simmer for two hours longer.
6. Stir it all well up with the kitchen ladle.
7. Strain it into a pan, rubbing the ingredients well through the sieve.
