## Ingredients

- Rich crust
- Two cups of cranberries
- One cup of raisins
- Two cups of sugar
- Butter the size of walnut
- Flour
- Water

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of pie-plate with rich crust and reserve enough for upper crust
2. Cut cranberries in halves and raisins in pieces
3. Mix two cups of cranberries, one cup of raisins, two cups of sugar, butter the size of walnut
4. Dredge with flour, sprinkle with water
5. Bake thirty minutes in a moderate oven
