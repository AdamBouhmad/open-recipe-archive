## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- onions
- water
- paprika

## Directions

1. Slice and salt three pounds of carp
2. Steam four sliced onions with one cup of water, to which has been added one teaspoon of paprika
3. Add the sliced carp and cook very slowly until the fish is done
