## Ingredients

- large, fresh beef tongue
- saltpetre, size of walnut
- one teacup of salt
- one teaspoon of pepper
- three small cloves of garlic
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Soak in cold water one-half hour.
2. Crush a piece of saltpetre, size of walnut, one teacup of salt, one teaspoon of pepper, three small cloves of garlic cut fine; mix seasoning.
3. Drain water off tongue.
4. With a pointed knife prick tongue; rub in seasoning.
5. Put tongue in crock; add the balance of salt, etc.; cover with plate and weight.
6. Allow to stand from four to five days.
7. Without washing off the seasoning, boil in fresh water until tender.
