## Ingredients

- Remnants of cold fowl or duck
- peas
- 1 onion
- a little dripping
- a good size cup of gravy
- 1/2 peck of peas
- a couple of spoonfuls of sugar
- pepper
- salt
- saffron
- turnips, Jerusalem artichokes, or small new potatoes

## Directions

1. Fry an onion sliced in a little dripping, pour in a good size cup of gravy, made from odd pieces.
2. Wash and drain well 1/2 peck of peas, put them with a couple of spoonfuls of sugar, pepper, salt.
3. When nearly done lay in the pieces of fowl or duck.
4. When done dish up, poultry in the centre of the dish, and vegetables round.
5. They are very nice done with turnips, Jerusalem artichokes, or small new potatoes, for all these vegetables colour with saffron, and dish up very hot.
