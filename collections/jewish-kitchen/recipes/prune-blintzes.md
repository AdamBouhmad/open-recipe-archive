## Ingredients

- three cups of potato flour
- three eggs
- water
- fat
- prunes
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix three cups of potato flour with three eggs
2. Add a little water and mix well
3. Heat a small frying-pan, grease with a little fat
4. Pour into it enough batter to make thin pancakes
5. Chop prunes, add a little sugar
6. Fill each cake with this mixture
7. Fold into three-cornered pieces and fry
8. When done put in a pan, sprinkle with sugar and bake in oven
9. Do not let burn
