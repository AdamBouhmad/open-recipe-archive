## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 onion
- 1 ounce of butter
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 1 teacup of milk
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Before peeling the artichokes wash them well, free of all dirt, in 2 or 3 waters.
2. Dry them on a coarse cloth, then peel them and put them in cold water for 10 minutes.
3. Chop an onion very fine like a mince, put it in a saucepan with 1 ounce of butter, salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg, 1 teacup of milk.
4. Cut the artichokes in halves, let them simmer till done, careful they do not break.
5. Stir in a basin a teaspoonful of flour with a little water.
6. Pour it over the artichokes, dish them up.
