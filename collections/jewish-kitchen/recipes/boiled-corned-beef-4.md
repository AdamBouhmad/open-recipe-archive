## Ingredients

- corned beef
- cold water
- soup vegetables

## Directions

1. Put corned beef into cold water using enough to cover it well
2. Let it come slowly to the boiling-point then place where it will simmer only
3. Allow thirty minutes or more to each pound
4. It is improved by adding a few soup vegetables the last hour of cooking
5. If the piece can be used a second time, trim it to good shape
6. Place it again in the water in which it was boiled, let it get heated through
7. Set aside to cool in the water under pressure, a plate or deep dish holding a flat-iron being set on top of the meat
8. When cooled under pressure the meat is more firm and cuts better into slices
