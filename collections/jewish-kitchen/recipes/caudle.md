## Ingredients

- grits
- a few spices
- white wine
- a little brandy
- nutmeg
- lemon peel
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a fine smooth gruel of grits with a few spices boiled in it
2. Strain it carefully and warm as required
3. Add white wine and a little brandy, nutmeg, lemon peel, and sugar according to taste
