## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- pinch of salt
- one teaspoon of powdered sugar
- one cup of lukewarm water
- six apples chopped fine
- cinnamon
- sugar
- one-half cup of seedless raisins
- one-half cup of currants
- chicken-fat or oil

## Directions

1. Sift two cups of flour, add pinch of salt and one teaspoon of powdered sugar
2. Stir in slowly one cup of lukewarm water, and work until dough does not stick to the hands
3. Flour board, and roll as thin as possible
4. Do not tear
5. Place a tablecloth on table, put the rolled out dough on it, and pull gently with the hands, to get the dough as thin as tissue paper
6. Have ready six apples chopped fine, and mixed with cinnamon, sugar, one-half cup of seedless raisins, one-half cup of currants
7. Spread this over the dough with plenty of chicken-fat or oil all over the apples
8. Take the tablecloth in both hands, and roll the strudel, over and over, holding the cloth high, and the strudel will almost roll itself
9. Grease a baking-pan, hold to the edge of the cloth, and roll the strudel in
10. Bake brown, basting often with fat or oil
