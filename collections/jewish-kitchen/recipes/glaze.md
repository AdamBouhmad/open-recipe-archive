## Ingredients

- 1 pint dark stock No. 2
- a good spoonful of browning No. 23

## Directions

1. Put the dark stock at the side of the fire in a small iron saucepan with a good spoonful of browning No. 23 and let it simmer till it comes to 1/2 pint.
2. Then put it in a nice clean pot or small basin for use.
3. Keep a small brush for it.
