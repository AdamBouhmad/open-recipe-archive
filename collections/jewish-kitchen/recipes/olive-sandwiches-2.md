## Ingredients

- ripe or green olives
- Mayonnaise dressing
- whole-wheat or graham bread

## Directions

1. Take either ripe or green olives and remove the seeds.
2. Mince and mix thoroughly with Mayonnaise dressing.
3. Spread between slices of whole-wheat or graham bread.
