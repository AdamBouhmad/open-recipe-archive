## Ingredients

- two fine feet
- egg
- bread crumbs
- chopped parsley
- chalot
- ground cloves
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- two fine Spanish onions
- chopped truffles or mushrooms
- suet
- cayenne
- white pepper
- white sugar
- good strong stock
- wine glass of Madeira wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean, boil and split the two fine feet
2. Dip them into egg and bread crumbs mixed with chopped parsley and chalot, a few ground cloves, a little nutmeg, pepper and salt
3. Fry them a fine brown
4. Arrange them in the dish
5. To make the sauce: slice two fine Spanish onions and put them in a saucepan, with some chopped truffles or mushrooms, a little suet, cayenne and white pepper, salt, one or two small lumps of white sugar
6. Let all simmer in some good strong stock till the gravy has nearly boiled away
7. Stir in a wine glass of Madeira wine, and a little lemon juice
8. Return it to the saucepan, to be made thoroughly hot before serving
9. Pour the sauce over the feet
