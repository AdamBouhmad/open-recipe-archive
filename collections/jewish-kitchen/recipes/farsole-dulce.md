## Ingredients

- dried Lima beans
- cold water
- one tablespoon of fat
- peel of lemon or orange
- one tablespoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak dried Lima beans in cold water overnight
2. Drain, put on with very little water
3. add one tablespoon of fat, peel of lemon or orange
4. When beans are half done, add a tablespoon of sugar which has been browned in a pan
5. stew slowly until the beans are tender
