## Ingredients

- 4 good sized apples
- moist sugar
- 1 oz. dried currants
- 1 oz. raisins
- 1 oz. almonds chopped
- 2 oz. lemon and orange peel chopped
- cinnamon
- ground cloves
- 1/4 lb suet
- 6 oz. flour
- salt
- cold water
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Peel 4 good sized apples, cut them up rather small, put them in a stewpan with moist sugar, 1 oz. of dried currants, 1 oz. of raisins, 1 oz. of almonds chopped, 2 oz. of lemon and orange peel chopped, a little cinnamon, and a little ground cloves
2. Let it stew till the apples are quite soft
3. Chop 1/4 lb of suet very fine, put it in a dish with 6 oz. of flour, a little salt, mix with a little cold water
4. Make it into a paste, roll it out on your paste-board 3 times
5. Divide it in 4 pieces, grease a pudding basin well, and put in a little brown sugar with a little cinnamon
6. Roll out one of the pieces of dough, put it at the bottom of the basin
7. Put a layer of the stewed fruit, so on alternately, leaving the last layer of paste to form the top
8. Bake it, and turn it out of the basin to send it to table
