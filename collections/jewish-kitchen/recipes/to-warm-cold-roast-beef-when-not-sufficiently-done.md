## Ingredients

- cold roast beef
- beetroot or cucumber
- a little gravy
- two tablespoonsful of vinegar
- one of oil
- pepper
- salt
- a little chopped lettuce
- a few peas

## Directions

1. Cut the beef in slices
2. Slice some beetroot or cucumber
3. Put them in a saucepan with a little gravy which need not be strong
4. Add two tablespoonsful of vinegar, one of oil, pepper, salt, a little chopped lettuce and a few peas
5. Simmer till the vegetables and meat are sufficiently dressed
