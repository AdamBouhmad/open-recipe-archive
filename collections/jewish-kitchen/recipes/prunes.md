## Ingredients

- prunes
- lemon
- stick cinnamon
- sugar
- water
- potato flour
- juice of an orange

## Directions

1. Wash the prunes well, first in warm water, then in cold
2. Cut up half a lemon, some stick cinnamon and sugar to taste
3. Cook them in the oven, covered tight, allowing a liberal quantity of water
4. Stew slowly for two hours
5. Thicken with a teaspoon of potato flour, and wet the potato flour with the juice of an orange before adding
