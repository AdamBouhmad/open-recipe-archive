## Ingredients

- Normandy pippins
- water
- brown sugar
- rind of a lemon
- a few cloves

## Directions

1. Lay pippins in water for 2 or 3 hours
2. Use 1/2 pint of water to 1 lb. of pippins
3. Put 1 lb of brown sugar with a little of the rind of a lemon and a few cloves
4. Let them boil well
5. Put them in a dish and pour the syrup over the pippins
