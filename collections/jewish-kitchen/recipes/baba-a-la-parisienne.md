## Ingredients

- yeast
- a scant cup of butter
- four tablespoons of sugar
- grated peel of a lemon
- five eggs
- two cups of sifted flour
- a scant cup of lukewarm milk
- a pinch of salt
- one cup of flour
- one-half cup of almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the yeast as above
2. cream a scant cup of butter with four tablespoons of sugar, the grated peel of a lemon
3. add five eggs, one at a time, stirring each egg a few minutes before you add the next
4. Have ready two cups of sifted flour and add two spoonfuls between each egg until all is used
5. Make a soft dough of the yeast, a scant cup of lukewarm milk, add two spoonsfuls between each egg until all is used up, a pinch of salt, and one cup of flour
6. Let it rise for fifteen minutes
7. Now mix all well
8. rub the form with butter, and blanch one-half cup of almonds, cut into long strips and strew all over the form
9. Fill in the mixture or cake batter
10. let it rise two hours and bake very slowly
