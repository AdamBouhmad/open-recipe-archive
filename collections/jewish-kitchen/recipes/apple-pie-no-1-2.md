## Ingredients

- four apples
- plain pastry
- bread crumbs
- one-half cup of sugar
- cinnamon, nutmeg or lemon juice or two tablespoons of water

## Directions

1. Pare, core and slice four apples
2. Line a pie-plate with plain pastry
3. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
4. Lay in the apples
5. Sprinkle with sugar and flavor with cinnamon, nutmeg or lemon juice or water if apples are not juicy
6. Cover with upper crust, slash and prick
7. Bake in moderate oven until the crust is brown and the fruit is soft
