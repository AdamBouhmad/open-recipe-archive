## Ingredients

- green cucumbers
- salt
- cloves
- allspice
- black peppercorns
- mace, 2 or 3 blades
- cold vinegar
- garlic, 3 or 4 teeth

## Directions

1. Select a few green cucumbers when they get cheap
2. Lay them in cold water for 2 or 3 hours to make them crisp
3. Put them on a flat dish with salt for a day
4. Put them in a jar with a few cloves, allspice, black peppercorns, 2 or 3 blades of mace, and plenty of salt
5. Fill the jar up with cold vinegar
6. Cover it over air tight
7. Put it in a dry place
8. Optionally put 3 or 4 teeth of garlic in to make them a nice flavour
9. Choose the cucumbers as near one size as possible
