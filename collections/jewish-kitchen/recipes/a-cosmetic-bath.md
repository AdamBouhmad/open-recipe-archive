## Ingredients

- one pound of starwort
- two quarts of water
- half a pound of linseed
- six ounces of the roots of the water lily
- one pound of bean meal
- two quarts of milk
- one pint of rose water
- a wine glass of spirits of camphor

## Directions

1. Boil slowly one pound of starwort in two quarts of water, with half a pound of linseed, six ounces of the roots of the water lily, and one pound of bean meal
2. When these have boiled for two hours, strain the liquor
3. Add to it two quarts of milk, one pint of rose water, and a wine glass of spirits of camphor
4. Stir this mixture into a bath of about ninety-eight degrees
