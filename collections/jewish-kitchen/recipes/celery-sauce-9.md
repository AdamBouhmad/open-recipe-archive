## Ingredients

- about four to five heads of celery
- half a pint of veal gravy
- a spoonful of flour
- the yolks of three eggs
- white pepper
- salt
- the juice of one lemon
- a little water

## Directions

1. Cut in small pieces from about four to five heads of celery, which if not very young must be peeled
2. Simmer it till tender in half a pint of veal gravy
3. Add a spoonful of flour, the yolks of three eggs, white pepper, salt, and the juice of one lemon
4. Previously mix these together with a little water till perfectly smooth and thin
5. Stir in with the sauce
