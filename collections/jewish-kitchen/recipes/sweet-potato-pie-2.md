## Ingredients

- one cup of mashed, boiled sweet potatoes
- one pint of sweet milk
- three whole eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-quarter of a nutmeg
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one-half teaspoon of lemon extract
- pie crust

## Directions

1. Measure one cup of mashed, boiled sweet potatoes.
2. Thin with one pint of sweet milk.
3. Beat three whole eggs very light with one-half cup of sugar.
4. Mix with sweet potatoes.
5. Season with one-quarter of a nutmeg grated, one teaspoon of cinnamon, and one-half teaspoon of lemon extract.
6. Line pie-plate with crust, fill with mixture, and bake in quick oven.
