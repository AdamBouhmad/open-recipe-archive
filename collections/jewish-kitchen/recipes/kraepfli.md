## Ingredients

- butter
- egg
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- salt
- pounded almonds
- pot cheese or schmierkaese
- boiling milk
- melted butter or cream

## Directions

1. Knead dough on board and work it with the hollow of your hands, always toward you, until the dough is perfectly smooth
2. Roll out as thin as paper and cut into squares three inches in diameter
3. Prepare filling: stir up a piece of butter the size of an egg, adding one egg, sugar, cinnamon, grated peel of a lemon and pinch of salt, pounded almonds
4. Fill the kraepfli with a teaspoon, wet the edges with beaten egg, fold into triangles, pressing the edges firmly together
5. Boil in boiling milk
6. When done they will swim to the top
7. Eat with melted butter or cream
