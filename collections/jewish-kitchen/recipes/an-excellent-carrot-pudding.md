## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. chopped suet
- 1/2 lb. grated carrot
- 1/2 lb. of raisins stoned
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- 1 lb. of sifted sugar
- 2 eggs
- yolks of 4 more eggs
- gill of ale
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix in a basin 1/2 lb. flour, 1/2 lb. chopped suet, 1/2 lb. grated carrot, 1/2 lb. of raisins stoned, 1/2 lb. of currants well washed, 1 lb. of sifted sugar
2. Beat up 2 eggs, the yolks of 4 more, with a gill of ale, and a little grated nutmeg
3. Mix it all well together
4. Put it in a well-greased mould
5. Bake or boil it for 1 hour
6. Serve with wine or brandy sauce
