## Ingredients

- fowls
- stuffing
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- greased paper
- gravy
- bread sauce
- water-cresses

## Directions

1. Prepare fowls for roasting the same as turkeys, well dry them
2. Put in the stuffing at the breast
3. Sew the piece of skin on to the backbone, but not too tight
4. Season them well
5. Dredge with flour
6. Put greased paper over the breast
7. Remove the paper a quarter of an hour before you dish them up
8. Baste the part well where the paper was
9. Have a little gravy nicely coloured, pour some over the fowls boiling hot
10. Serve with bread sauce
11. Garnish them with water-cresses
12. One hour to roast a good-size fowl
