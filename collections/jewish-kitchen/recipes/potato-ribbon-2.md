## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fat

## Directions

1. Pare and lay in cold water, ice-water is best, for half an hour
2. Select the largest potatoes
3. Cut round and round in one continuous curl-like strip
4. Handle with care and fry a few at a time for fear of entanglement, in deep fat
