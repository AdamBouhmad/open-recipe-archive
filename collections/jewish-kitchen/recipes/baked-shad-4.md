## Ingredients

- three-pound shad
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean and split a three-pound shad.
2. Place in a buttered dripping pan.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, brush with melted butter and bake in a hot oven thirty minutes.
