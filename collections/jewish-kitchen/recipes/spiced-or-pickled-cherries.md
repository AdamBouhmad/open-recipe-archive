## Ingredients

- largest and freshest red cherries
- 1 cup best pickling vinegar per quart of cherries
- 1 pound sugar per 3 quarts of fruit
- 3 sticks whole cinnamon bark per 3 quarts of fruit
- one-half ounce cloves per 3 quarts of fruit

## Directions

1. Take the largest and freshest red cherries you can get, and pack them in glass fruit jars, stems and all
2. Put little splints of wood across the tops of the fruit to prevent rising to the top
3. Boil the vinegar and spices and sugar for five minutes steady
4. Turn out into a covered stoneware vessel, cover, and let it get cold
5. Pour over the fruit and repeat this process three days in succession
6. Remove the heads of the cloves, for they will turn the fruit black
7. You may strain the vinegar after the first boiling, so as to take out the spices, if you choose
8. Seal as you would other fruit
9. Be sure that the syrup is cold before you pour it over the cherries
