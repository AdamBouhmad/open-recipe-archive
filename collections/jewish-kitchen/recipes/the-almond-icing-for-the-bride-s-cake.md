## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- half-pint of water
- 1 lb. of ground almonds
- 2 oz. of bitter almonds
- wine-glass of orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan 1 lb. of loaf sugar with half-pint of water
2. Let it boil to a syrup
3. Stir in 1 lb. of ground almonds
4. Have in the lb. of almonds 2 oz. of bitter
5. Let it boil till it looks thick
6. Put in a wine-glass of orange-flower water
7. When it has all boiled, put a small piece on a plate to see if it is sticky
8. If so, lay it all over the top of your cake as thick as the quantity will allow
9. If not thick enough, you can make some more and lay it over
10. Have ready the sugar icing, as soon as the almond icing has set you can lay on the sugar
