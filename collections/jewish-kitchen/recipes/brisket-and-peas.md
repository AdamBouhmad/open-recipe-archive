## Ingredients

- Four pounds of boned brisket
- a pint of water
- salt and pepper
- a small piece of mace
- an onion
- 1/2 peck of peas
- 3 or 4 sticks of mint
- a little parsley
- 4 or 5 lumps of sugar
- a spoonful of flour
- a little cold water

## Directions

1. Put boned brisket in a stewpan with a pint of water, salt, pepper, a small piece of mace, an onion chopped small.
2. Let it simmer for 3 hours.
3. Half an hour before it is finished have 1/2 peck of peas carefully shelled and wash them through a colander.
4. Put them in with the beef, chop 3 or 4 sticks of mint, a little parsley, 4 or 5 lumps of sugar, put all in together.
5. Let it finish cooking.
6. Stir in a spoonful of flour rubbed smooth with a little cold water to thicken the liquor.
7. Have the beef in the centre and the peas all round.
