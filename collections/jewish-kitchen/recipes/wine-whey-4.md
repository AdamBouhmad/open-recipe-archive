## Ingredients

- a pint of milk
- white wine
- a little boiling water

## Directions

1. Set on the fire in a saucepan a pint of milk
2. When it boils, pour in as much white wine as will turn it into curds
3. Boil it up and let the curds settle
4. Strain off and add a little boiling water
5. Sweeten to taste
