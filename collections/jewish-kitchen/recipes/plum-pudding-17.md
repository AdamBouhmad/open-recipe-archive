## Ingredients

- one pound of currants
- one pound of raisins
- one pound of shred suet
- one pound flour or half a pound bread crumbs and half a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of candied orange and lemon peel
- a little citron cut thin
- half a pound of moist sugar
- six beaten eggs
- a glass of brandy
- ginger
- nutmeg
- cloves
- sifted sugar or whites of eggs beaten to a froth

## Directions

1. Add to one pound of currants one pound of raisins, one pound of shred suet, one pound flour (or half a pound bread crumbs and half a pound of flour), a quarter of a pound of candied orange and lemon peel, a little citron cut thin, half a pound of moist sugar
2. Mix all well together as each article is added, then stir in six beaten eggs and a glass of brandy
3. Beat the pudding well for half an hour, let it stand some time, then put it into a basin and boil six or seven hours in plenty of water
4. It should be seasoned according to taste with ginger, nutmeg, cloves, &c.
5. Serve with sifted sugar or whites of eggs beaten to a froth
