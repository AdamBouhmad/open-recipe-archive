## Ingredients

- matzoth
- cold water
- olive oil
- chopped meat
- egg
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- mashed potatoes
- well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Place some matzoth in cold water to soak
2. Take the matzoth out and dry them on a towel
3. Grease a pan with olive oil and put in matzoth enough to cover bottom of pan
4. Take chopped meat, bind with an egg, season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
5. Cover this with the matzoth, add some olive oil
6. Cover with mashed potatoes and one or two well-beaten eggs
7. Bake until brown
