## Ingredients

- a quart of white peas
- water
- broth
- a little piece of chorissa or smoked beef
- mint
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Soak a quart of white peas in water
2. Boil them till soft, in as much water as will cover them
3. Pass them through a sieve, and add them to any broth that may be ready
4. A little piece of chorissa or smoked beef will improve the flavour
5. This soup should be served with mint and fried bread
