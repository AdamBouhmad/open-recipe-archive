## Ingredients

- potatoes
- parsley
- fat

## Directions

1. Buy a potato cutter at a first-class hardware store
2. Cut the potatoes to the size of a hickory nut
3. Fry or steam them
4. Parboil and brown in fat, or boil and add parsley as you would with new potatoes
5. The remainder of the raw potatoes may be boiled and mashed or fried into ribbons
