## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of fresh butter
- four tablespoons of flour
- one quart water
- salt
- pepper
- a little nutmeg
- one pint of milk

## Directions

1. Heat two tablespoons of fresh butter in a spider
2. add four tablespoons of flour to it and brown to light golden brown
3. then add one quart water, stirring constantly
4. Season with salt and pepper and a little nutmeg
5. Add one pint of milk
6. let boil up once or twice and serve at once
