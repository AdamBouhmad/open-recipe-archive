## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- one teaspoon of salt
- one cup of sugar
- two well-beaten eggs
- one cup of cream of wheat or farina
- stewed prunes
- plain cream

## Directions

1. Take one quart of milk, one teaspoon of salt, one cup of sugar and two well-beaten eggs
2. Heat this and then pour in slowly one cup of cream of wheat or farina, stirring constantly
3. Boil fifteen minutes
4. Butter a deep pudding dish and put in a layer of stewed prunes that have been cut up in small pieces with a scissors on the bottom
5. Pour a layer of the above over this, alternating in this order until all has been used
6. Bake ten minutes in a hot oven
7. Serve with plain cream, not whipped or sweetened
