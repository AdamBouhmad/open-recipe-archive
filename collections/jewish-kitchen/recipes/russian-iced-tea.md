## Ingredients

- tea
- loaf sugar
- rum or brandy
- lemon slice
- preserved strawberries, raspberries, cherries or pineapple
- lemon or orange rind

## Directions

1. Make tea for as many cups as desired
2. Strain and cool
3. Place in ice-box and chill thoroughly
4. Serve in tall glass with ice and flavor with loaf sugar, one teaspoon of rum or brandy, one slice of lemon or one teaspoon preserved strawberries, raspberries, cherries or pineapple
5. Or loaf sugar may be flavored with lemon or orange and packed and stored in jars to be used later to flavor and sweeten the tea
6. Wash the rind of lemon or orange and wipe dry, then rub over all sides of the sugar
