## Ingredients

- one quart ripe tomatoes
- one quart okra, cut into small rings
- about two quarts water
- one piece soup meat (no bone)
- one onion
- one carrot
- one sprig parsley
- one chicken
- rice
- salt
- pepper
- corn
- Lima beans

## Directions

1. Take one quart of ripe tomatoes, stew with one quart of okra, cut into small rings
2. Put this on to boil with about two quarts of water and a piece of soup meat (no bone)
3. Chop up an onion, a carrot and a sprig of parsley, add this to the soup
4. Fricassee one chicken with some rice
5. Let the soup simmer four or five hours
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. A little corn and Lima beans may be added; they should be cooked with the soup for several hours
8. Cut the soup meat into small cubes and leave in the soup to serve
9. Dish up with the soup, putting a piece of chicken and one tablespoon of rice into each soup plate before adding the soup
