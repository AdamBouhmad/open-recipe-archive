## Ingredients

- strained juice of one and one-half lemons
- two tablespoons of powdered sugar
- one tablespoon of red curagao
- three cups of claret
- one cup of apollinaris water
- a few slices of orange or pineapple, or both
- a few maraschino cherries
- two cucumbers
- a good-sized lump of ice
- a bunch of mint

## Directions

1. Squeeze into a glass pitcher the strained juice of one and one-half lemons
2. add two tablespoons of powdered sugar
3. add one tablespoon of red curagao
4. pour in three cups of claret, and one cup of apollinaris water
5. Mix thoroughly
6. add a few slices of orange or pineapple, or both, and a few maraschino cherries
7. Cut the rinds from two cucumbers without breaking them
8. hang them on the inside of the pitcher from the top
9. drop in a good-sized lump of ice
10. serve at once in thin glasses
11. Place a bunch of mint at the top of the pitcher
