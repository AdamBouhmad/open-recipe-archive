## Ingredients

- one cup of soft, mild cheese
- one tablespoon of butter
- milk
- salt
- mustard
- paprika
- slices of bread

## Directions

1. Finely cut one cup of soft, mild cheese and stir over the fire with one tablespoon of butter until the cheese is melted.
2. Add enough milk to moisten, perhaps not more than one-eighth of a cup, with salt, mustard, and paprika to taste.
3. Stir until creamy and smooth.
4. Very thinly butter slices of bread and spread the cheese mixture on generously.
5. Cover each slice with another slice and set away until the filling cools and hardens.
6. Toast the sandwiches on both sides and serve hot.
