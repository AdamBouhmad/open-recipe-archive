## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 lb. of boned brisket
- 8 pints of water
- salt
- pepper
- a blade of mace
- a little dried saffron
- one turnip
- a carrot
- a stick of celery
- an onion
- a tooth of garlic
- 2 eggs
- a little chopped parsley
- the juice of a lemon
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil boned brisket with water, salt, pepper, blade of mace, dried saffron, turnip, carrot, celery, onion, and garlic
2. Let it boil fast, then skim it well
3. Let it simmer till the meat is tender enough for dinner
4. Beat up in a basin 2 eggs, chopped parsley, juice of lemon, and grated nutmeg
5. Stir it in while boiling
