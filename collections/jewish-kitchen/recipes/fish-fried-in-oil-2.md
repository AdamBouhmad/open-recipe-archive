## Ingredients

- Soles, plaice, or salmon
- beaten eggs
- flour
- the best frying oil

## Directions

1. When prepared by salting or drying, have a dish ready with beaten eggs
2. Turn the fish well over in them, and sprinkle it freely with flour, so that the fish may be covered entirely with it
3. Place it in a pan with a good quantity of the best frying oil at boiling heat
4. Fry the fish in it gently, till of a fine equal brown colour
5. When done, place it on a cloth before the fire for the oil to drain off
6. Fish prepared in this way is usually served cold
