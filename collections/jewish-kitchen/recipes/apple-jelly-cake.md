## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- four eggs
- one cup of milk
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- three and one-half cups of flour

## Directions

1. Rub one cup of butter and two cups of sugar to a cream
2. add four eggs, whites beaten separately
3. add one cup of milk
4. add two teaspoons of baking-powder and three and one-half cups of flour
5. Bake in layer tins
