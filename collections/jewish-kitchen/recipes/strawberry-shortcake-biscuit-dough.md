## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- four teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one-quarter cup of butter
- three-quarters of a cup of milk
- one to one and one-half boxes of strawberries
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix flour, baking-powder, salt, and sugar
2. Work butter in with tips of fingers
3. Add milk gradually
4. Toss on floured board and divide in two parts
5. Pat and roll out
6. Bake twelve minutes in hot oven in layer-cake tins
7. Split and spread with butter
8. Pick, hull, and drain berries
9. Sweeten strawberries to taste
10. Crush slightly and put between and on top of short cake
11. Serve with cream, plain or whipped
