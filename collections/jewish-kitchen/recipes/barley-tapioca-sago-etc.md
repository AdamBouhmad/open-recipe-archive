## Ingredients

- one teaspoon of salt
- one quart of boiling water
- one-half cup of barley or other hard grain

## Directions

1. Add one teaspoon of salt to one quart of boiling water
2. Pour gradually on one-half cup of barley or other hard grain and boil until tender, from one to two or more hours, according to the grain, and have each kernel stand out distinct when done
3. Add more boiling water as it evaporates
4. Use as a vegetable or in soups
