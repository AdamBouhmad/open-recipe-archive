## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- four ounces of loaf-sugar
- rind of three lemons
- juice of one lemon
- three well-beaten eggs
- nutmeg
- spoonful of brandy
- light puff paste

## Directions

1. Warm four ounces of butter, mix it with the same quantity of loaf-sugar sifted
2. Grate in the rind of three lemons, squeeze in the juice of one
3. Add three well-beaten eggs, a little nutmeg, and a spoonful of brandy
4. Put this mixture into small tins lined with a light puff paste, and bake
