## Ingredients

- half a pint of new milk
- thick slice of stale bread
- tea-spoonful of gum arabic
- spoonful of oil of almonds
- same quantity of honey
- pinch of common salt

## Directions

1. Boil in half a pint of new milk a thick slice of stale bread, and a tea-spoonful of gum arabic
2. When boiled, set it at a little distance from the fire to simmer almost to a jelly
3. Pass it through a folded muslin, and stir in a spoonful of oil of almonds, and the same quantity of honey, with a pinch of common salt
4. When cold it will be a stiff jelly
