## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one cup of cheese cut up
- four egg yolks beaten
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- pepper
- four egg whites beaten stiff
- a pinch of baking powder

## Directions

1. Cook in double boiler one cup of milk
2. Add one tablespoon of butter and one tablespoon of flour blended together
3. Cook till thick
4. Add one cup of cheese cut up and stir till dissolved
5. Remove from fire and stir in yolks of four eggs beaten
6. Add one-half teaspoon of salt and pepper
7. Fold in whites of four eggs beaten stiff and a pinch of baking powder
8. Bake in a buttered dish one-half hour
