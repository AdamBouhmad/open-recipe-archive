## Ingredients

- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- half a pint boiling water
- yolks of two eggs
- one slice onion
- one bay leaf
- one clove garlic
- four tablespoons vinegar
- half a teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix one tablespoon of butter and one of flour in a saucepan and add gradually half a pint of boiling water.
2. Stir until it just reaches the boiling point.
3. Take from the fire and add the yolks of two eggs.
4. Into another saucepan put a slice of onion, a bay leaf, and a clove of garlic; add four tablespoons of vinegar, and stand this over the fire until the vinegar is reduced one-half.
5. Turn this into the sauce, stir for a moment.
6. Strain through a fine sieve.
7. Add half a teaspoon of salt and serve.
