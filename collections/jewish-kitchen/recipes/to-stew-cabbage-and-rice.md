## Ingredients

- 1 white cabbage
- salt
- 1/2 lb. of rice
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of oil or 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 pint of boiling water
- pepper
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Get a nice white cabbage very hard, wash it well, lay it in water with a little salt, then drain it well.
2. Divide it into halves, slice very fine on a board.
3. Have washed 1/2 lb. of rice.
4. Take an onion, slice it into a stewpan with 2 tablespoonfuls of oil or 1/2 lb. of butter, let it fry.
5. Add 1 pint of boiling water, salt, and pepper, a little dried saffron.
6. Put in the chopped cabbage.
7. Let it cook for 1/2 an hour, then put in the rice, let it simmer till done, it will be set like a pudding, quite dry from the liquor.
8. Dish it up.
