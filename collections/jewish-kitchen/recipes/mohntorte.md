## Ingredients

- rich puff paste
- half a pound of white mohn (poppy seed)
- milk
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- beaten whites
- two ounces of sifted flour
- a quarter of a pound of melted butter
- vanilla or rose frosting

## Directions

1. Line a form with a rich puff paste.
2. Soak half a pound of white mohn (poppy seed) in milk and then grind.
3. Add a quarter of a pound of sugar and the yolks of six eggs.
4. Stir all together in one direction until quite thick.
5. Stir the beaten whites, to which add two ounces of sifted flour and a quarter of a pound of melted butter.
6. Fill and bake.
7. When done, frost either with vanilla or rose frosting.
