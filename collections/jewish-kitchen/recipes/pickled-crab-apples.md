## Ingredients

- three quarts tart, firm, red or yellow crab-apples
- three cups of cider vinegar
- three cups of sugar
- one cup of water
- two tablespoons of cloves
- two sticks of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Select tart, firm, red or yellow crab-apples, three quarts and remove all decayed spots but leave the stems
2. Put three cups of cider vinegar, three cups of sugar, and one cup of water in preserving kettle and let boil two minutes
3. Add two tablespoons of cloves and two sticks of cinnamon broken; these spices must be tied in a bag and let cook ten minutes
4. Lift out carefully with perforated skimmer and put in glass jars
5. When all the apples have been cooked, pour over enough syrup to cover and set spice bag away in a cup
6. Cover jars and let stand twenty-four hours
7. Pour off syrup and boil again
8. Wait two days then boil apples, sugar, with spice bag until apples are tender but firm
9. Place apples in jars and cover to keep hot
10. Boil down syrup a little and fill the jars to overflowing with the hot syrup and seal
