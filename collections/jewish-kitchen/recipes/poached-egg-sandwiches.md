## Ingredients

- bread from a round loaf
- pimentoes
- poached eggs
- Mayonnaise
- watercress

## Directions

1. Slice as many pieces of bread from a round loaf as you have persons to serve.
2. Toast these slices and let cool.
3. Across each slice place three strips of pimentoes (use the canned pimentoes).
4. On top of that place a cold poached egg.
5. Put a teaspoon of Mayonnaise on the top of the egg and surround with sprigs of watercress.
