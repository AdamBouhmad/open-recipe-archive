## Ingredients

- three or four chicken livers
- flour
- one minced onion
- one tablespoon of fat
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-eighth teaspoon of paprika
- one-half cup of strong soup stock
- one tablespoon claret or sherry
- toast

## Directions

1. Slice three or four livers from chicken or other fowl and dredge well with flour.
2. Fry one minced onion in one tablespoon of fat until light brown.
3. Put in the liver and shake the pan over the fire to sear all sides.
4. Add one-half teaspoon of salt, one-eighth teaspoon of paprika and one-half cup of strong soup stock.
5. Allow it to boil up once.
6. Add one tablespoon claret or sherry and serve immediately on toast.
