## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- beef gravy
- smoked meat
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- cloves
- allspice
- morels
- truffles
- forcemeat

## Directions

1. After soaking and blanching, fry them till brown
2. simmer gently in beef gravy seasoned highly with smoked meat, nutmeg, pepper, salt
3. add a small onion stuck with cloves, and a very little whole allspice
4. thicken the gravy slightly
5. add morels and truffles
6. small balls of delicate forcemeat are also an improvement
