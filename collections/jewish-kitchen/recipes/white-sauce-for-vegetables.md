## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons flour
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- pepper
- one cup milk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place two tablespoons of butter in a saucepan; stir until melted.
2. Add two tablespoons of flour mixed with one-fourth of a teaspoon of salt and a few grains of pepper.
3. Stir until smooth.
4. Add one cup of milk gradually and continue to stir until well mixed and thick.
5. Chopped parsley may be added.
