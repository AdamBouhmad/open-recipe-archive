## Ingredients

- one peck dandelions
- two quarts boiling salted water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash one peck of dandelions; remove roots.
2. Cook one hour in two quarts of boiling salted water.
3. Drain, chop fine; season with salt, pepper and butter.
4. Serve with vinegar.
