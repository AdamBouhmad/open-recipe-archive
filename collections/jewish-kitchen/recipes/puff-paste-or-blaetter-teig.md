## Ingredients

- one-half pound of butter
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half pound of flour
- one-fourth to one-half cup of ice-water

## Directions

1. Cut off one-third of the butter and put the remaining two-thirds in a bowl of ice-water
2. Divide this into four equal parts; pat each into a thin sheet and set them away on ice
3. Mix and sift flour and salt; rub the reserved butter into it and make as stiff as possible with ice-water
4. Dust the slab with flour; turn the paste upon it; knead for one minute, then stand it on ice for five minutes
5. Roll the cold paste into a square sheet about one-third of an inch thick; place the cold butter in the centre and fold the paste over it, first from the sides and then the ends, keeping the shape square and folding so that the butter is completely covered and cannot escape through any cracks as it is rolled
6. Roll out to one-fourth inch thickness, keeping the square shape and folding as before
