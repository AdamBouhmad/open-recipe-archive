## Ingredients

- sour milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat sour milk slowly until the whey rises to the top.
2. Pour it off, put the curd in a bag and let it dry for six hours without squeezing it.
3. Pour it into a bowl and break it fine with a wooden spoon.
4. Season with salt.
5. Mold into balls and keep in a cool place.
