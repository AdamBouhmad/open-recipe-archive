## Ingredients

- fine picked rice
- pure fresh milk
- bay leaf
- lemon peel
- stick of cinnamon
- apples
- whites of eggs
- lemon or noyau essence

## Directions

1. Boil well some fine picked rice in pure fresh milk
2. Sweeten and flavour with a bay leaf, lemon peel, and a stick of cinnamon
3. Remove the bay leaf, lemon peel, and cinnamon stick when the rice is done
4. Line a round dish or souffle dish with the rice
5. Have ready apples previously boiled, sweetened, and beat up smoothly
6. Place the apple lightly in the centre rather higher in the middle than at the sides
7. Beat up the whites of eggs to a froth
8. Sweeten and flavour with lemon or noyau essence
9. Place it in small heaps tastefully on the apple and rice
10. Brown delicately with a salamander
