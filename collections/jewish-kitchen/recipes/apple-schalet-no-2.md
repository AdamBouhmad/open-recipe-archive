## Ingredients

- schalet dough
- apples
- raisins, seeded
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Line an iron pudding-dish with schalet dough, greasing it well before you do so.
2. Chop up some apples quite fine, put on the crust, also some raisins (seeded), sugar and cinnamon.
3. Put another layer of pie and another layer of chopped apples, and so on until filled, say about three layers, the last being crust.
4. Bake slowly and long until a nice dark brown.
