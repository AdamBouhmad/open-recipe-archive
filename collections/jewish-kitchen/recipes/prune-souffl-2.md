## Ingredients

- stewed prunes
- eggs
- sugar
- butter
- whipped cream
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from a large cup of stewed prunes and chop fine
2. Add the whites of three eggs and a half cup of sugar beaten to a stiff froth
3. Mix well
4. Turn into a buttered dish
5. Bake thirty minutes in a moderate oven
6. Serve with whipped cream
7. If it is desired to cook this in individual cups, butter the cups, fill only two-thirds full, to allow for puffing up of the eggs, and set the cups in a pan of water to bake
8. Some like a dash of cinnamon in this
