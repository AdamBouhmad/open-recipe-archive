## Ingredients

- 1 ounce of isinglass
- 1/2 pint of water
- piece of vanilla
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 1/2 pint of almond milk
- milk
- savoy finger cakes
- ratafias
- dried cherries
- oil of sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Break isinglass small and boil it in 1/2 pint of water
2. Put in a piece of vanilla to boil with
3. When the isinglass is dissolved strain it through a sieve
4. Beat the yolks of 8 eggs
5. Stir in 1/2 pint of almond milk or if for a supper 1 pint of milk
6. Beat the whites to a strong snow
7. Stir it into the eggs, isinglass, and almond milk
8. Take a round mould and grease it well with the oil of sweet almonds
9. Line it with savoy finger cakes, crossing them at the bottom to form a star in the mould
10. Put several ratafias and a few dried cherries
11. When this is fixed well, pour your mixture in gently
12. Cover the top with the finger cakes like the bottom
13. Put it in ice or a very cold place
14. Pass a hot cloth round
15. Turn it out on your dish
