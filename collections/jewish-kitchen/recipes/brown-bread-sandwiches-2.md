## Ingredients

- one cup of cottage cheese
- salt
- cayenne
- one pimento
- white and brown bread

## Directions

1. Season one cup of cottage cheese with salt, cayenne, and add one pimento cut in shreds.
2. Cut white and brown bread in finger lengths about one inch wide.
3. Spread with cheese mixture and place a brown and white slice together.
