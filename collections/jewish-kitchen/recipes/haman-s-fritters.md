## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of the best Florence oil
- one pound of flour
- four beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Scald the oil
2. When hot, mix with it the flour
3. Add four beaten eggs and make it into a paste
4. Roll it out thin and cut it into pieces about four inches square
5. Let them dry and fry them in oil
6. The moment the pieces are put in the frying pan, they must be drawn up with two silver skewers into different forms according to fancy
7. Fry for a few minutes until crisp
