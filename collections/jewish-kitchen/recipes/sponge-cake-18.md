## Ingredients

- eggs
- sugar, same weight as eggs
- flour, one-half the weight of eggs
- grated rind of one lemon to five eggs
- juice of one lemon to five eggs
- powdered sugar
- rose-water (optional)

## Directions

1. Weigh any number of eggs; take the same weight of sugar and one-half the weight of flour
2. Add the grated rind and juice of one lemon to five eggs
3. For mixing this cake, see the directions given on page 246; the mixture should be very light and spongy, great care being used not to break down the whipped whites
4. The oven should be moderate at first, and the heat increased after a time
5. The cake must not be moved or jarred while baking
6. Bake forty to fifty minutes, according to size of cake
7. Use powdered sugar for sponge-cake
8. Rose-water makes a good flavoring when a change from lemon is wanted
