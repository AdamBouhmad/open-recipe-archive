## Ingredients

- cold fish
- beaten eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Shred finely any cold fish and season it
2. Mix with beaten eggs
3. Make it into a paste and fry in thin cakes like pancakes
4. Serve hot on a napkin
5. There should be plenty of boiling butter in the pan, as they should be moist and rich
6. There should be more eggs in the preparation for omelets than for fritters
