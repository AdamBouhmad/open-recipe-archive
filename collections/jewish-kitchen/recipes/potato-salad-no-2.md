## Ingredients

- one quart of small Bermuda potatoes
- cold water
- salt
- white onions
- oil
- vinegar
- tarragon vinegar
- pepper
- mayonnaise
- cream
- hard-boiled eggs
- boiled beets
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and scrub the potatoes thoroughly in cold water
2. Put them in a kettle with enough cold water, slightly salted, just to cover them
3. Stand them over a brisk fire with the kettle covered until the water begins to boil
4. Turn down the heat, lift the cover of the kettle slightly, and let the potatoes cook slowly till done
5. Drain off the water and stand the potatoes where they will get cold, but do not put them in a refrigerator
6. When quite cold, peel the potatoes and slice them very thin in a salad bowl
7. To every two layers of potato slices sprinkle over a very light layer of white onions sliced very thin
8. Pour over the salad a French dressing made of equal parts of oil and vinegar with tarragon vinegar, salt and pepper
9. When ready to serve, cover the surface of the salad with a stiff mayonnaise in which a suggestion of cream has been mixed
10. Ornament with quarters of hard-boiled eggs, boiled beets cut in fancy slices and a fringe of parsley around the edge of the bowl
