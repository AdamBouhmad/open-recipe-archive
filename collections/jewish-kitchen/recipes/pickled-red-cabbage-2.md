## Ingredients

- hard cabbage
- salt
- water
- allspice
- cloves
- peppercorns
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Select a very hard cabbage
2. Strip off the outside leaves
3. Cut it in halves
4. Lay it in salt and water for an hour
5. Drain it in a colander
6. Shred it very fine
7. Lay it on a dish with plenty of salt for 3 or 4 hours
8. Put in a jar with allspice, cloves, peppercorns and salt
9. Cover with cold vinegar
10. Cover over air tight
