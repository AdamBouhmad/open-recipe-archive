## Ingredients

- six big apples
- water
- lemon
- nutmeg
- eggs
- whipped or plain cream

## Directions

1. Peel six big apples and slice them
2. Put them in a saucepan with just enough water to cover them
3. Cook until tender
4. Put them through a colander
5. Add the grated rind and juice of half a lemon
6. Sweeten to taste
7. Stir in a trace of nutmeg
8. Fold in the stiffly beaten whites of four eggs
9. Put the dish on ice
10. Serve with whipped or plain cream
