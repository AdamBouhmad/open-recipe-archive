## Ingredients

- mackerel
- garlic
- oil
- lemon juice or vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- winter savory
- saffron
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and split the mackerel in halves
2. cut the halves in two
3. dry them in a cloth
4. cut in a stewpan 2 or 3 teeth of garlic, with 3 spoonfuls of oil
5. let it fry
6. take out the garlic when fried
7. put on the juice of 3 lemons or 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar, salt, pepper, and a little cayenne
8. lay in the mackerel with a spoonful of the dried leaves of winter savory, a little dried saffron
9. let it boil quickly for an hour
10. dish the mackerel up
11. pour the gravy over
12. garnish with parsley
