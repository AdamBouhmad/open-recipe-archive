## Ingredients

- good baking apples
- 1 lb. sugar
- cloves
- rind of a lemon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pick some good baking apples
2. Prick them with a fork
3. Lay them in a deep dish
4. Add 1 lb. of sugar, some cloves, and the rind of a lemon
5. Add a little of the juice
