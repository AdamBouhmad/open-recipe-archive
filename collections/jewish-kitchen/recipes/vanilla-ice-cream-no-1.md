## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- two cups of sugar
- one large tablespoon of flour
- cold milk
- two eggs
- one teaspoon of vanilla extract
- one quart of sweet cream
- vanilla, lemon and almond extracts

## Directions

1. Heat the milk in a double boiler
2. when it is at boiling point add the flour rubbed smooth in cold milk
3. add the eggs beaten light and one cup of sugar
4. Cook about twenty minutes, stirring very often
5. Let the mixture get cold
6. add the remaining sugar and the vanilla and cream
7. freeze
