## Ingredients

- salmon
- anchovies
- capers
- pickled cucumber
- 2 boiled eggs
- white lettuce
- 3 spoonfuls of oil
- 1 spoonful of vinegar
- parsley
- mint
- fennel
- beetroot

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon in pieces the size of dice
2. Halve some anchovies, wash them well, and roll each half up like a fillet
3. Add some fine capers
4. Cut up a pickled cucumber in pieces
5. Chop 2 boiled eggs
6. Have a nice white lettuce
7. Put all this in a salad bowl
8. Make a kind of mayonnaise by putting in 3 spoonfuls of oil, 1 of vinegar, some chopped parsley and mint, a little fennel
9. Beat all this together till it comes like a cream
10. Decorate your salad with some strips of beetroot
