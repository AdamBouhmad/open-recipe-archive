## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- two cups of water
- one small onion
- salt and pepper to taste
- two herrings

## Directions

1. Place two cups of milk, two cups of water, one small onion, salt and pepper to taste in a saucepan, and boil for ten minutes
2. add two herrings which have been previously soaked and cut in small pieces
3. cook until herring is tender
