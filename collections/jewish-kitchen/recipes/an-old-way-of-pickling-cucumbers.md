## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- cayenne pepper
- whole ginger
- whole pepper
- mustard seed
- West India pickles

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers in small pieces, length ways, with the peel left on
2. Lay them in salt for twenty-four hours
3. Dry the pieces with a cloth
4. Lay them in a deep dish
5. Pour over the following mixture: some vinegar boiled with cayenne pepper, whole ginger, a little whole pepper, and mustard seed
6. A few West India pickles may be added by some as an improvement
7. Let this mixture stand till nearly cold before covering the cucumbers
8. Bottle the cucumbers
