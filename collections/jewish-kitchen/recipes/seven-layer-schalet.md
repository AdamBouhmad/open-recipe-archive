## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one egg
- three tablespoons of fat
- one cup of water
- sugar
- pinch of salt
- one and one-half teaspoons of cinnamon
- three tablespoons of bread crumbs
- melted fat
- two ounces of sweet and bitter almonds, grated
- one-half pound of cleaned, seedless raisins
- jelly
- one-half pound of citron
- grated lemon peel
- juice of lemon

## Directions

1. Take two cups of flour, one egg, three tablespoons of fat, one cup of water, a little sugar, pinch of salt, and knead lightly.
2. Put dough aside in a cold place.
3. Prepare a mixture of one cup of sugar, one and one-half teaspoons of cinnamon and three tablespoons of bread crumbs.
4. Cut dough in seven pieces and roll out each piece separately.
5. Place one layer on a greased baking-tin and spread the layer with melted fat and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
6. Place upon this the second layer, sprinkle on this two ounces of sweet and bitter almonds which have been grated and mixed with sugar.
7. Place the third layer and spread with oil, sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar and one-half pound of cleaned, seedless raisins.
8. Place the fourth layer on and spread with jelly and one-half pound of citron cut up very small.
9. Cover over with another layer, spread fat and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar and grated lemon peel and juice of lemon.
10. Place the sixth layer and spread and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
11. Put on the last layer and spread with fat and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
12. Cut in four-cornered pieces and bake thoroughly and until a nice brown.
