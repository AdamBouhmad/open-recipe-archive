## Ingredients

- chicken
- mayonnaise
- chicken soup
- finger-rolls

## Directions

1. Grind up chicken in meat chopper
2. To each cup of chicken add one tablespoon of mayonnaise and one tablespoon of chicken soup
3. Mix into soft paste
4. Put in finger-rolls
