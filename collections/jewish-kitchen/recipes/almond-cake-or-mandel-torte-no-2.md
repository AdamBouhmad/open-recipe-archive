## Ingredients

- one-fourth pound of sweet almonds
- one-eighth pound of bitter almonds
- yolks of nine eggs
- eight tablespoons of granulated sugar
- grated peel of one lemon
- one-half teaspoon of mace or vanilla
- juice of one lemon
- whites of nine eggs
- four stale lady fingers

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds the day previous to using
2. Grate or pound them as fine as powder
3. Beat until light the yolks of nine eggs with eight tablespoons of granulated sugar
4. Add the grated peel of one lemon and one-half teaspoon of mace or vanilla
5. Beat long and steadily
6. Add the grated almonds and continue the stirring in one direction
7. Add the juice of the lemon to the stiff-beaten whites
8. Grate four stale lady fingers and add
9. Bake slowly for one hour at least
