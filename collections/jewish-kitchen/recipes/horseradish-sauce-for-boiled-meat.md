## Ingredients

- an egg
- salt
- pepper
- a cup of clear broth
- scraped horseradish
- a little vinegar
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat an egg and season it with salt and pepper
2. Stir in a cup of clear broth and make it smooth
3. Put in the scraped horseradish with a little vinegar and grated nutmeg
