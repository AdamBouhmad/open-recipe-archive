## Ingredients

- a nice piece of the tail of cod
- mashed potatoes
- butter
- yolk of eggs
- 2 or 3 onions
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- marjoram
- 2 eggs
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Take a nice piece of the tail of cod, cut all the fins off
2. Have some mashed potatoes with butter and yolk of eggs ready
3. Slice in your fish tin 2 or 3 onions, with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg, chopped parsley, and some marjoram
4. Notch the tail in slices just half down to the bone
5. Make layers of mashed potatoes and put where you have cut the slices of the fish to fill up, so that the tail will look twice the size
6. Lay the tail on the fried onions
7. Beat up 2 eggs with the juice of a lemon, and pour it over the fish
8. Put it in the oven to bake a light brown
9. Garnish with parsley
