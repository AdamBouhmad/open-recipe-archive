## Ingredients

- large celery roots
- beets
- French dressing
- sugar
- vinegar
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Buy large celery roots, parboil them and cut in shape of baskets and scallop the edge
2. Boil beets until soft and cut them in small balls (like potato-balls)
3. Set celery root baskets in French dressing for several hours to flavor, and the beet-balls in boiling sugar and vinegar
4. Fill the baskets with pickled beet-balls
5. Roll lettuce and cut it into shreds and put it around the celery root basket
