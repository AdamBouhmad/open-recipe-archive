## Ingredients

- Two cups of granulated sugar
- One-half cup of corn syrup
- One-half cup of water
- Two eggs
- One cup of nuts

## Directions

1. Boil two cups of granulated sugar, one-half cup of corn syrup and one-half cup of water until it will thread
2. Beat into the stiff whites of two eggs
3. Add one cup of nuts
4. Beat until cool and thick
5. Pour out, cool, and when set, cut into squares
