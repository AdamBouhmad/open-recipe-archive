## Ingredients

- one cup of rendered fat
- one cup of sugar
- one-half loaf of bread
- water
- little salt
- one-fourth cup of flour
- pears
- water
- sugar
- claret

## Directions

1. Cream one cup of rendered fat with one cup of sugar
2. Add one-half loaf of bread previously soaked and pressed dry, a little salt, one-fourth cup of flour
3. Grease pudding-dish and put in alternate layers of the mixture and pears that have been boiled with water, sugar and claret
4. Bake slowly three hours
