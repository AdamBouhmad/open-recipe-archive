## Ingredients

- one quart of peas
- one gallon of water
- a few potatoes
- a large celery root
- a little parsley
- a little onion
- a small carrot
- a small clove of garlic
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Soak peas in lukewarm water over night
2. Use one quart of peas to one gallon of water
3. Boil about two hours with the following vegetables: a few potatoes, a large celery root, a little parsley and a little onion, a small carrot cut up in cubes and a small clove of garlic
4. When boiled down to half the quantity, press all through colander
5. If soup is too thin, take a tablespoon of flour blended with a little cold water in a saucepan and add to the peas already strained
6. Serve with croutons
