## Ingredients

- cutlets
- greased paper
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- lemon peel
- thyme
- beaten eggs
- piquante sauce

## Directions

1. Make a paste of bread crumbs, chopped parsley, nutmeg, pepper, salt, grated lemon peel, and thyme, with a couple of beaten eggs
2. spread this seasoning on the cutlets
3. broil or fry cutlets in greased paper
4. serve a piquante sauce in a tureen
