## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of melted fat
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1/2 lb. of meal finely sifted
- 4 eggs
- clarified sugar
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 lb. of melted fat into a 1/2 pint of water
2. Have a 1/2 lb. of meal finely sifted
3. Stir it into the boiling fat and water
4. Beat up 4 eggs and stir it in
5. Grease your cups
6. Put a spoonful in each
7. Bake in a quick oven
8. Cover with clarified sugar
9. When you dish up the puffs, sprinkle over cinnamon and sugar
