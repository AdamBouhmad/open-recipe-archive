## Ingredients

- one cup of brown sugar
- one-half cup of butter or butter substitute
- one-half teaspoon of ground cloves
- one-half teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- one cup of sour milk
- one teaspoon of baking-soda
- two cups of flour
- one cup of raisins chopped

## Directions

1. Cream one cup of brown sugar and one-half cup of butter (or a little less of any butter substitute)
2. Add one-half teaspoon of ground cloves and ground cinnamon
3. Add one cup of sour milk
4. Add one teaspoon of baking-soda, two cups of flour and one cup of raisins chopped
5. Have ready a warm oven and bake three-quarters of an hour
