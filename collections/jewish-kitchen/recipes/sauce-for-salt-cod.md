## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 spoonful of flour
- a small tea-cup of the liquor the fish was boiled in
- parsley
- juice of a lemon
- 2 eggs boiled hard
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a small saucepan 1/2 lb. of butter and a spoonful of flour
2. Add a small tea-cup of the liquor the fish was boiled in
3. Chop a little parsley and add the juice of a lemon to it
4. Have 2 eggs boiled hard and chopped very small
5. Put them in the sauceboat with the chopped parsley, a little salt and pepper
6. Pour over the melted butter
