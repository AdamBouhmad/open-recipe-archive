## Ingredients

- onion, finely chopped
- oil
- water
- fish
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- ground allspice
- nutmeg
- ginger
- eggs (4 yolks)
- lemon juice (2)
- flour (1 tea spoonful)
- cold water (1 table spoonful)
- saffron
- fish liver and roe
- chopped parsley
- warmed butter
- crumbs of bread

## Directions

1. Put an onion, finely chopped, into a stew-pan, with a little oil, till the onion becomes brown
2. Add half a pint of water, and place the fish in the stew-pan, seasoning with pepper, salt, mace, ground allspice, nutmeg, and ginger
3. Let it stew gently till the fish is done
4. Prepare the beaten yolks of four eggs, with the juice of two lemons, and a tea spoonful of flour, a table spoonful of cold water, and a little saffron, mix well in a cup
5. Pour it into the stew-pan, stirring it carefully one way until it thickens
6. About twenty minutes before serving, throw in balls made from a little of the fish, the liver, and roe if there is any, beat it up finely with chopped parsley, and spread warmed butter, crumbs of bread, and seasoning according to taste
7. Form this into a paste with eggs, and make it into balls of a moderate size
8. Garnish with sliced lemon and parsley
