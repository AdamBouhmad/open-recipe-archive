## Ingredients

- fat of smoked beef
- lean veal
- chopped mushrooms
- minced parsley
- salt
- pepper
- grated lemon peel
- crumb of two French rolls
- good gravy

## Directions

1. Scrape half a pound of the fat of smoked beef and a pound of lean veal, free from skin, vein, or sinew
2. Pound it finely in a mortar with chopped mushrooms, a little minced parsley, salt and pepper, and grated lemon peel
3. Have ready the crumb of two French rolls soaked in good gravy
4. Press out the moisture and add it
