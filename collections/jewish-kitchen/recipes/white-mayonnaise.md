## Ingredients

- lemon juice
- whipped cream or egg white

## Directions

1. Follow the ordinary directions for mayonnaise, using lemon juice instead of vinegar, omitting the mustard and adding, when finished, a half cup of whipped cream or half an egg white beaten very stiff.
