## Ingredients

- a tablespoon drippings
- a little flour
- a cup of soup stock
- half a teaspoon of caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Heat a tablespoon drippings in a spider
2. Add a little flour
3. Stir smooth with a cup of soup stock, added at once, and half a teaspoon of caraway seeds
