## Ingredients

- one pint of cream
- two eggs
- one-half cup of maple syrup

## Directions

1. Whip one pint of cream until quite thick
2. Break two eggs into another bowl and beat until light
3. Add gradually one-half cup of maple syrup
4. When the two are well mixed, whip them gradually into the cream
5. Pour the whole into a freezer can, without the dasher
6. Cover
7. Pack in ice and salt and let stand for three hours
