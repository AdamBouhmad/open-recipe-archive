## Ingredients

- thin jelly
- one cup boiling water
- brandy or wine
- sugar
- one teaspoon cornstarch
- cold water
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Take thin jelly, add one cup boiling water and brandy or wine (one-half cup)
2. add a little more sugar
3. thicken with one teaspoon cornstarch dissolved in a little cold water
4. The beaten white of egg may be added
