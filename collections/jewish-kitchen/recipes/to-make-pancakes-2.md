## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of dry flour
- 1 teacupful of milk
- salt
- chopped rind of lemon cut very fine
- butter
- apple chopped fine
- powdered sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a batter of 3 eggs, 3 tablespoonfuls of dry flour, 1 teacupful of milk, a little salt, and a little chopped rind of lemon cut very fine
2. Take a very clean pan and put a very small piece of butter
3. Drop in some of the batter
4. A little apple chopped fine in the batter is a great improvement
5. They can be fried in oil
6. If for dinner, instead of the milk add another egg and a little water
7. Sprinkle over them powdered sugar and a little lemon juice
8. They must be sent up very hot
