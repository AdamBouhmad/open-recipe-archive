## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- a tea-cup full of cold water
- four table spoonsful of flour
- four well beaten eggs
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Put a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, and a tea-cup full of cold water into a saucepan
2. When the butter is melted, stir in, while on the fire, four table spoonsful of flour
3. When thoroughly mixed, put it in a dish to cool
4. Add four well beaten eggs
5. Butter some cups
6. Half fill them with the batter
7. Bake in a quick oven
8. Serve with clarified sugar
