## Ingredients

- one cup of white wine
- one-half cup of cold water
- a few pieces of stick cinnamon
- seven lumps of cut loaf sugar
- one cup of sweet cream
- yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Put one cup of white wine and one-half cup of cold water on to boil
2. add a few pieces of stick cinnamon and seven lumps of cut loaf sugar
3. while boiling scald a cup of sweet cream in double boiler
4. Have ready the well-beaten yolks of two eggs
5. pour over this the hot cream, stirring all the time
6. then pour in the boiling wine, being careful to stir well or it will curdle
7. Can be eaten hot or cold
