## Ingredients

- two and one-half tablespoons of melted butter
- one cup of granulated sugar
- two eggs
- one cup of milk
- one-half nutmeg grated
- sifted flour
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- one teaspoon of salt
- Crisco
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and milk
2. Add grated nutmeg to sifted flour along with baking-powder and salt
3. Add sifted flour mixture to wet ingredients to make a batter as stiff as biscuit dough
4. Flour your board well and roll dough out about half an inch thick
5. Cut into pieces three inches long and one inch wide
6. Cut a slit about an inch long in the centre of each strip and pull one end through this slit
7. Fry quickly in hot Crisco
8. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top of each doughnut
