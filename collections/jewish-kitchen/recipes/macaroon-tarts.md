## Ingredients

- rich pie dough
- preserve
- whites of three eggs
- one-half pound of powdered sugar
- one-half pound of grated almonds
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Line a gem or muffin-pan with rich pie dough
2. Half fill each tart with any desired preserve
3. Bake in a quick oven
4. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth
5. Add one-half pound of powdered sugar and stir about ten minutes or until very light
6. Gradually add one-half pound of grated almonds
7. Divide this macaroon paste into equal portions
8. Roll and shape into strips, dusting hands with powdered sugar
9. Place these strips on the baked tarts in parallel rows to cross each other diagonally
10. Return to oven and bake in a slow oven about fifteen minutes
11. Let remain in pans until almost cold
