## Ingredients

- one-half pound of pistachio nuts
- one tablespoon of brandy
- one pint of rich cream
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Take out the kernels of half a pound of pistachio nuts and pound them in a mortar with one tablespoon of brandy.
2. Put them in a double boiler with a pint of rich cream and add gradually the yolks of three eggs, well beaten.
3. Stir over the fire until it thickens and then pour carefully into a bowl, stirring as you do so and being careful not to crack the bowl.
4. Put a silver spoon into the bowl before pouring in the cream, as this will prevent it cracking.
5. When cold, stick pieces of the nuts over the cream and serve.
