## Ingredients

- best end neck of veal, 3 to 4 pounds
- smoked beef or choriza
- salt
- pepper
- gravy
- lemon juice
- cayenne
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Divide the flesh at the top from the bones gently with your hand
2. Have ready some slices of smoked beef or a piece of choriza, cut lengthways
3. Lay it in and skewer up the flesh to the bone
4. Season it with salt and pepper
5. Hang it down to roast to a brisk fire for 2 hours
6. Dish it up with a good gravy and the juice of a lemon
7. A little cayenne is a great improvement to veal
8. Tomato sauce is very relishing with roast veal
