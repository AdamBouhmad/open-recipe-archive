## Ingredients

- ducks
- stuffing
- greased paper
- gravy
- apple sauce

## Directions

1. Ducks are prepared precisely the same as geese for roasting
2. They are also stuffed at the lower end
3. Cover the breast with greased paper
4. Serve with boiling gravy and apple sauce
5. A good-sized duck will take 1 hour
6. Dish it up very hot
