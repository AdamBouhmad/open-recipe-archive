## Ingredients

- yolks of nine eggs
- one-half pound of powdered sugar
- one pound of walnuts
- two tablespoons of well sifted matzoth flour
- a pinch of salt
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- nine beaten whites

## Directions

1. Cream together the yolks of nine eggs and one-half pound of powdered sugar
2. Weigh one pound of walnuts before shelling; when shelled, grind
3. To the creamed yolks and sugar add two tablespoons of well sifted matzoth flour, a pinch of salt, and one teaspoon of vanilla
4. Mix in the ground walnuts
5. Fold in gently the nine beaten whites
6. Bake three-quarters of an hour
