## Ingredients

- a dozen fine large chesnuts
- milk
- rose water

## Directions

1. Boil a dozen fine large chesnuts, peeled and skinned, in milk
2. When soft beat them till perfectly smooth with rose water
3. Throw a tea-spoonful of this mixture into the water before washing the hands
