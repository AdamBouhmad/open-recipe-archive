## Ingredients

- one-half cup of goose-fat
- one medium-sized cabbage
- strudel dough
- one cup of raisins and currants mixed
- two cups of granulated sugar
- one-half cup of chopped almonds
- one teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Heat one-half cup of goose-fat, add one medium-sized cabbage and let it simmer until done, stirring constantly to keep from burning
2. While cooling prepare strudel dough
3. Fill with cabbage and one cup of raisins and currants mixed, two cups of granulated sugar, one-half cup of chopped almonds and one teaspoon cinnamon
4. Roll and put little pieces of grease on top
5. Bake in hot oven and baste frequently
6. The pans in which the strudel is baked must be greased generously
7. Serve this strudel hot
8. This strudel may be made for a milk meal by substituting butter for fat
