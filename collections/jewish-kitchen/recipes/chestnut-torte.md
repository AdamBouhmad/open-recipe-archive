## Ingredients

- one pound of chestnuts in the shells
- yolks of six eggs
- six tablespoons of sugar
- three teaspoons of baking-powder
- whites of six eggs
- one-half pint of cream

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of chestnuts in the shells
2. Peel them while warm
3. Put nuts through potato ricer or colander
4. Beat well the yolks of six eggs with six tablespoons of sugar
5. Add all the chestnut puree but two or three tablespoons reserved for top of torte
6. Add three teaspoons of baking-powder and the well-beaten whites of the six eggs
7. Bake in moderate oven fifteen to twenty minutes
8. Whip one-half pint of cream
9. Add to this the chestnut puree which was reserved and a little sugar
10. Garnish torte with this mixture
