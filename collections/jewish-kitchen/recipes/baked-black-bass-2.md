## Ingredients

- black bass
- salt
- one small cup of water
- butter
- pepper
- parsley
- one glass of sherry
- catsup
- one teaspoon of flour
- potatoes
- two tablespoons of butter
- one small cup of sweet cream
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. After having carefully cleaned, salt well and lay it in the baking-pan with a small cup of water, and strew flakes of butter on top, also salt, pepper and a little chopped parsley.
2. Bake about one hour, basting often until brown.
3. Serve on a heated platter; garnish with parsley and lemon and make a sauce by adding a glass of sherry, a little catsup and thicken with a teaspoon of flour, adding this to fish gravy.
4. Serve potatoes with fish, boiled in the usual way, making a sauce of two tablespoons of butter.
5. Add a bunch of parsley chopped very fine, salt and pepper to taste, a small cup of sweet cream thickened with a tablespoon of flour.
6. Pour over potatoes.
