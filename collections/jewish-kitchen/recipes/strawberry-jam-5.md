## Ingredients

- six pounds of fine fresh fruit
- very little water
- powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Bruise gently, with the back of a wooden spoon, six pounds of fine fresh fruit.
2. Boil them with very little water for twenty minutes, stirring until the fruit and juice are well mixed.
3. Put in powdered loaf sugar of equal weight to the fruit.
4. Simmer half an hour longer.
5. If the preserve is not required to be very rich, half the weight of sugar in proportion to the quantity of fruit may be used; but more boiling will be requisite.
