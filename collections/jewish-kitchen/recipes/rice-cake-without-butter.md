## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 12 oz. of castor sugar
- rind of 2 lemons
- 6 oz. of flour
- 6 oz. of ground rice
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites
2. Beat the whites to a strong snow
3. Mix the yolks and powdered sugar and the lemon rasping in a basin
4. Stand in a flat saucepan with boiling water over a slow fire till it comes thick and light; it will take 1/2 an hour
5. Take it off and stir it till cold
6. Stir in the flour and ground rice
7. Grease some paper with the almond oil to line the tin
8. Bake in rather a slow oven
