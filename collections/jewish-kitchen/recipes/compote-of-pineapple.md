## Ingredients

- pineapple
- sugar
- juice of one or two tart oranges
- brandy

## Directions

1. Cut off the rind of a pineapple
2. Core and trim out all the eyes
3. Cut into desired slices
4. Set on to boil with half a pound of sugar and the juice of one or two tart oranges
5. When the pineapple is tender and clear, put into a compote dish
6. Boil the syrup until clear
7. Pour over all and cool
8. The addition of a wineglass of brandy improves this compote very much
