## Ingredients

- a little good beef consomme, or stock
- a small piece of smoked beef, or chorissa
- a lemon sliced
- some chopped shalots
- a couple of onions shred
- a bay leaf
- two or three cloves
- a little oil
- a little minced parsley
- a few chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take a little good beef consomme, or stock, a small piece of smoked beef, or chorissa, a lemon sliced, some chopped shalots, a couple of onions shred, a bay leaf, two or three cloves, and a little oil
2. Simmer gently
3. Add a little minced parsley, and a few chopped mushrooms
4. Skim and strain
