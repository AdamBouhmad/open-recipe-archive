## Ingredients

- mackarel
- bay leaves
- whole pepper
- cloves (1/2 tea-spoonful)
- allspice (1 whole spoonful)
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads and tails
2. Open and clean them
3. Lay them in a deep pan with a few bay leaves, whole pepper, half a tea-spoonful of cloves, and a whole spoonful of allspice
4. Pour over equal quantities of vinegar and water
5. Bake for an hour and a half, in a gentle oven
