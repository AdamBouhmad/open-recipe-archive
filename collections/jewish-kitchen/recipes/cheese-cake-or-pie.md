## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of cheese
- sweet butter the size of an egg
- one-half cup of sugar
- yolks of three eggs
- pinch of salt
- peel of a lemon
- one-half cup of cleaned currants
- citron
- kuchen dough or pie dough
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Take one and one-half cups of cheese, rub smooth with a silver or wooden spoon through a colander or sieve
2. Rub a piece of sweet butter the size of an egg to a cream
3. Add gradually one-half cup of sugar and the yolks of three eggs, a pinch of salt
4. Grate in the peel of a lemon
5. Add one-half cup of cleaned currants and a little citron cut up very fine
6. Line two pie-plates with some kuchen dough or pie dough, roll it out quite thin
7. Butter the pie-plates quite heavily, and let the dough in them rise at least a quarter of an hour before putting in the cheese mixture
8. Whip up the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth and stir through the cheese mixture
9. Put the cheese into the plates and bake immediately
