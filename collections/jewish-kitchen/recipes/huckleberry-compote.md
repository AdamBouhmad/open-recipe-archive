## Ingredients

- huckleberries or blueberries
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cornstarch
- water

## Directions

1. Pick over a quart of huckleberries or blueberries
2. Wash them
3. Set to boil
4. Do not add any water to them
5. Sweeten with half a cup of sugar
6. Spice with half a teaspoon of cinnamon
7. Just before removing from the fire, add a teaspoon of cornstarch which has been wet with a little cold water
8. Do this thoroughly in a cup and stir with a teaspoon so as not to have any lumps in it
9. Pour into a glass bowl
10. Eat cold
