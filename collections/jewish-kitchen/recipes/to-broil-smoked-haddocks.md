## Ingredients

- thick haddock
- butter
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take a nice thick haddock
2. cut off the tail and the top pieces by the head
3. grease the gridiron
4. lay it on, and put it before a clear fire
5. lay it on a hot dish
6. rub a piece of butter over it
7. pepper it
8. squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
9. Send it up quickly
