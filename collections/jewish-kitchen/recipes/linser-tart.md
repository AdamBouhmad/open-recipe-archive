## Ingredients

- one-half pound flour
- one-half pound sugar
- one-half pound almonds grated with peel on
- two eggs
- allspice
- citron
- salt
- brandy
- strawberry jam

## Directions

1. Make a dough of flour, sugar, almonds, eggs, allspice, citron, salt flavored with brandy
2. Take a little more than half and roll it out
3. Line a pie-pan
4. Put strawberry jam on
5. Cut rest of dough in strips and cover the same as prune pie
6. Brush strips with yolk of egg
7. Bake in moderate oven
