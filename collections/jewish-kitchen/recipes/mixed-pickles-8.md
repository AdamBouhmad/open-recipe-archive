## Ingredients

- one quart of large cucumbers
- one quart of small cucumbers
- one quart small silver-skinned onions
- one quart small green tomatoes
- two red peppers
- one large cauliflower
- one quart of water
- one cup of salt
- six tablespoons of mustard
- one tablespoon of turmeric
- one cup of flour
- two cups of sugar
- two quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash one quart of large cucumbers and cut in cubes
2. Wash one quart of small cucumbers and leave whole
3. Prepare one quart small silver-skinned onions
4. Chop one quart small green tomatoes coarse
5. Chop two red peppers fine
6. Break one large cauliflower in small pieces
7. Pour over them a weak brine solution made of one quart of water and a cup of salt
8. Let stand twenty-four hours
9. Bring to a boil in same solution and drain
10. Mix six tablespoons of mustard, one tablespoon of turmeric, one cup of flour, two cups of sugar and two quarts of vinegar for the dressing
11. Thoroughly mix these ingredients and then cook until thick
12. Stir in the pickles
13. Heat thoroughly
14. Empty into glass jars and stand away until needed
