## Ingredients

- 12 or 14 ripe apricots
- apricot kernels
- 1/2 pint of milk
- flour
- 2 oz. of butter
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mash 12 or 14 ripe apricots through a sieve
2. Pound the kernels quite fine
3. Make a thick paste with 1/2 pint of milk and as much flour as it will take up
4. When cold put it in a basin
5. Dissolve in the milk 2 oz. of butter
6. Stir the apricots up with this paste
7. Mix with yolks of 6 eggs and 1 lb. of powdered sugar
8. Work this in with the paste and apricots
9. Beat the whites to a snow and stir in lightly
10. Grease the shape and put it in
11. Place in a middling oven
12. When it rises and is a good colour it is done
13. About 1/2 of an hour before you send it up sprinkle sugar over it
