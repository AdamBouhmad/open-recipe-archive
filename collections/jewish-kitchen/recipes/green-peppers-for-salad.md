## Ingredients

- whole, green sweet pepper
- boiling water
- vinegar
- salt
- sugar
- oil

## Directions

1. Put whole, green sweet pepper in boiling water and cook until tender
2. Place on platter and drain
3. Make a dressing of vinegar, salt, sugar and oil
4. Serve
