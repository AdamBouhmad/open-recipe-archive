## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- four tablespoons oil
- four tablespoons water
- one cup boiling water
- minced onion
- capers
- olives
- pickles
- parsley
- tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs with four tablespoons of oil and four of water.
2. Add a cup of boiling water and cook slowly until thick and smooth.
3. Take from the fire, and add minced onion, capers, olives, pickles, and parsley and a little tarragon vinegar.
