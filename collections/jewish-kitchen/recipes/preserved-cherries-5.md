## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar in equal proportion

## Directions

1. Lay them with the sugar the night before
2. Drain off the syrup to boil the sugar with before you put in the cherries
3. Keep them as whole as you can
4. Put them in pots
5. Cover with the syrup
6. Cover down the same as the others
