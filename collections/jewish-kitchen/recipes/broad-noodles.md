## Ingredients

- noodles
- fine noodles
- two tablespoons of sweet dripping
- soup stock (optional)

## Directions

1. Make noodles as above and when drained sprinkle with fine noodles which have been browned in two tablespoons of sweet dripping; serve as a vegetable.
2. If so desired, a cup of soup stock may be added and noodles browned in stove.
3. Serve hot.
