## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- a large piece of butter
- a tea cup full of water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and remove the outer leaves, slice it as thinly as possible
2. put it in a saucepan with a large piece of butter, and a tea cup full of water, salt and pepper
3. let it stew slowly till very tender
