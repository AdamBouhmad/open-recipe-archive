## Ingredients

- two large oxtails
- one onion
- one tablespoon drippings
- four quarts cold water
- one tablespoon salt
- one tablespoon mixed herbs
- four cloves
- four peppercorns
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Wash two large oxtails and cut into pieces
2. Cut one onion fine and fry in one tablespoon of drippings
3. When brown, add oxtails to brown
4. Put into soup kettle with four quarts cold water
5. Add one tablespoon of salt, one tablespoon of mixed herbs, four cloves, four peppercorns
6. Simmer for three or four hours
7. Skim off fat, strain
8. Cut vegetables into fancy shapes and boil twenty minutes, then add to soup
