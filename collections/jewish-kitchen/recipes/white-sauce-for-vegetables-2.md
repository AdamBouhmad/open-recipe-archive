## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- one-fourth of a teaspoon of salt
- a few grains of pepper
- one cup of milk
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Place two tablespoons of butter in a saucepan
2. Stir until melted
3. Add two tablespoons of flour mixed with one-fourth of a teaspoon of salt and a few grains of pepper
4. Stir until smooth
5. Add one cup of milk gradually and continue to stir until well mixed and thick
6. Chopped parsley may be added
