## Ingredients

- good-sized lemons
- one box of boneless sardines
- six anchovies
- two green peppers
- lemon-juice
- pimento
- horseradish
- watercress

## Directions

1. Select good-sized lemons
2. cut off tip to stand the lemon upright
3. cut top for cover
4. Scoop out all the lemon pulp, and put in a bowl
5. put shells in a bowl of cold water
6. For six lemons take one box of boneless sardines, six anchovies, and two green peppers, cut very fine
7. Wet with lemon-juice until moist
8. fill in shells after wiping dry
9. insert a pimento on top
10. put on cover of lemon
11. serve on doily with horseradish and watercress
