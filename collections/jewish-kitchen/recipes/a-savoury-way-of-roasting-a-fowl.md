## Ingredients

- fowl
- fine seasoning
- clarified veal suet
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice
- forcemeat fritters
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Fill it with a fine seasoning
2. Just before it is ready for serving, baste it well with clarified veal suet
3. Sprinkle it thickly with very dry crumbs of bread
4. Repeat this two or three times
5. Place it in the dish
6. Serve with a fine brown gravy well flavored with lemon juice
7. Delicate forcemeat fritters should be also served in the dish
