## Ingredients

- a pound of common salt
- half an ounce of saltpetre
- one ounce of coarse brown sugar

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of common salt, half an ounce of saltpetre and one ounce of coarse brown sugar
2. Rub the meat well with it, daily for a fortnight or less, according to the weather, and the degree of salt that the meat is required to have
3. Alternatively, boil eight ounces of salt, eight ounces of sugar, and half an ounce of saltpetre in two quarts of water, and pour it over the meat
4. Let it stand in it for eight or ten days
