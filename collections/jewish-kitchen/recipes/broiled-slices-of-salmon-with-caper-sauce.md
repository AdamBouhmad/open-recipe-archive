## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- caper sauce
- white paper

## Directions

1. Marinade your slices of salmon with oil, vinegar, pepper, and salt 3/4 of an hour before you want to broil it
2. Broil them before a very slow fire on both sides
3. Lay them on white paper at the bottom of the dish
4. Send up with caper sauce
