## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Boil a chicken till rather more than half done in a quart of water
2. Take of the skin, cut off the white parts when cold
3. Pound it to a paste in a mortar, with a small quantity of the liquor it was boiled in
4. Season with salt, a little nutmeg, and the least piece of lemon peel
5. Boil it gently, and make it with the liquor in which the fowl has been boiled of the required consistency
6. It should be rather thicker than cream
