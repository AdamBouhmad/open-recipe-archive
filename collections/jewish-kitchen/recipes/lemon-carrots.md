## Ingredients

- old carrots
- salted water
- one teaspoon of minced parsley
- loaf sugar
- one-half teaspoon of paprika
- one tablespoon of butter
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Pare and cut carrots into dice, and simmer in salted water until tender, but not pulpy
2. Drain, return to the fire, and for one pint of carrots add one teaspoon of minced parsley, a grating of loaf sugar, one-half teaspoon of paprika, one tablespoon of butter and the juice of half a lemon
3. Heat through, shaking the dish now and then, so that each piece of the vegetable will be well coated with the mixture or dressing
