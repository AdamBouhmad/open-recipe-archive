## Ingredients

- mackerel
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of butter
- two-thirds of a cup of milk

## Directions

1. Split fish, clean, and remove head and tail.
2. Put in buttered pan, sprinkle with salt and pepper and dot over with butter (allowing one tablespoon to a medium-sized fish), pour over two-thirds of a cup of milk.
3. Bake twenty-five minutes in a hot oven.
