## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- two cups corn-meal
- one heaping teaspoon of soda
- one-half cup sugar
- two eggs
- one and one-half cups of buttermilk
- one half cup of molasses
- one-half cup of shortening

## Directions

1. Mix one cup flour and two cups corn-meal, one heaping teaspoon of soda, one-half cup sugar
2. Add two eggs beaten with one and one-half cups of buttermilk, one half cup of molasses and one-half cup of shortening, melted
3. Beat all ingredients as fast as possible for a minute
4. Pour the dough into a warm, well-buttered pan
5. Bake quickly and steadily for half an hour
6. Serve hot
