## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- salt
- oil
- bread or garlic
- flour
- eggs
- cayenne
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut your salmon in slices and wash it well
2. Lay it in salt for half an hour
3. Wash it off and lay it in a cloth to dry
4. Have your fish pan on the fire with oil enough to cover the slices
5. Let it boil up
6. Put a very small piece of bread or a tooth of garlic to fry before you put the fish in, to take off the flavour of the oil
7. Have a plate with flour and one with some eggs beat in with a little salt and a little cayenne
8. Dip the fish first in the flour, cover it all over lightly
9. Dip it in the eggs
10. Put it carefully into the pan of oil
11. Let it fry nicely on one side then turn it on the other
12. Put it on a fish drainer
13. When the oil is drained off, if to eat cold, put it on a fish napkin and garnish with parsley
