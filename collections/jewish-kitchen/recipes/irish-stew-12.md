## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of lamb
- flour
- water
- one quart of potatoes
- one-half cup of carrots
- three onions
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons of flour

## Directions

1. Cut one and one-half pounds of lamb into small pieces
2. Dredge each piece of meat in flour
3. Brown in the frying-pan
4. Put in kettle, cover with water and cook slowly one hour or until tender
5. Add one quart of potatoes cut in small dice, one-half cup of carrots and three onions, after cooking thirty minutes
6. Season with salt, pepper, and thicken with two tablespoons of flour moistened in enough cold water to form a smooth paste
7. Serve with dumplings
