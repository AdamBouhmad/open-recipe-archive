## Ingredients

- fifteen large purple plums
- rich paste
- one cup of sugar
- powdered sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Select large purple plums, about fifteen plums for a good-sized pie; cut them in halves and remove the kernels and dip each half in flour.
2. Line your pie-tin with a rich paste and lay in the plums, close together, and sprinkle thickly with a whole cup of sugar.
3. Lay strips of paste across the top, into bars, also a strip around the rim, and press all around the edge with a pointed knife or fork, which will make a fancy border.
4. Sift powdered sugar on top.
5. Eat cold.
