## Ingredients

- one-half pound almonds
- one pint of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one of flour
- one cup of milk
- one cup of cream
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Blanch, and grind or pound one-half pound almonds
2. let simmer slowly in one pint of milk for five minutes
3. Melt one tablespoon of butter, blend with one of flour
4. Do not allow to bubble
5. Add one cup of milk and thicken slightly
6. Then add the almond mixture and simmer again until creamy
7. Remove from fire and add one cup of cream
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste
9. Cream may be whipped or left plain
