## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- butter
- parsley
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pare and quarter, and put on to boil.
2. When almost done drain off the water, add one cup of milk, one tablespoon of butter, a little chopped parsley and cook a while longer.
3. Thicken with a little flour (wet with cold water or milk), stir, and take from the fire.
