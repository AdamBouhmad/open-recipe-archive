## Ingredients

- three sponge cakes
- half a pound of macaroons and ratafias
- one wine glass of brandy
- three wine glasses of white wine
- nearly a pint of thick rich custard made of equal portions of milk and cream with seven eggs
- jam and sweetmeats
- cream
- whites of two eggs
- wine and sugar

## Directions

1. Soak three sponge cakes and half a pound of macaroons and ratafias in one wine glass of brandy and three of white wine
2. Lay them at the bottom of the trifle dish
3. Spread jam and sweetmeats over the cakes
4. Pour over nearly a pint of thick rich custard
5. Prepare a fine light froth with cream and the whites of two eggs, flavored with wine and sugar
6. Heap it over the trifle lightly
