## Ingredients

- one large soup bone or two pounds soup meat
- one or two onions
- a few potatoes
- a few carrots
- one turnip
- soup greens
- one can tomatoes or one quart fresh tomatoes
- two ears sweet corn
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon flour
- cold water
- noodles

## Directions

1. Take a large soup bone or two pounds of soup meat, the latter preferred, one or two onions, a few potatoes, a few carrots, a turnip, soup greens and a can of tomatoes or a quart of fresh ones
2. Cook two hours
3. In season add two ears of sweet corn grated
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Thicken with a tablespoon of flour, dissolved in cold water
6. A nice addition to this soup is a handful of noodles cut into round disks with a thimble
