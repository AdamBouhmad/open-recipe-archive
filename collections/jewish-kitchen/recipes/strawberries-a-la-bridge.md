## Ingredients

- large strawberries
- sugar
- marshmallows
- whipped cream
- shelled pecans

## Directions

1. Into a champagne-glass put large strawberries, halved and sugared, and an equal amount of marshmallows halved.
2. Place on top a mass of whipped cream, already sweetened and flavored.
3. Top with a single strawberry, sprinkle with shelled pecans.
