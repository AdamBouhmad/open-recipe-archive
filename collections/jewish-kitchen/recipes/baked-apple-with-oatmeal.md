## Ingredients

- apples
- leftover oatmeal mush
- sugar
- water
- cream

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples and fill the core space with leftover oatmeal mush
2. Put the apples in a baking dish
3. Sprinkle with sugar
4. Pour a little water into the bottom of the pan and bake in a moderate oven until the apples are tender
5. Serve warm with cream for breakfast or luncheon
