## Ingredients

- pigeons
- 4 or 5 large floury potatoes
- salt
- white pepper
- dried marjoram
- chopped parsley
- good dripping or melted fat
- 2 eggs
- egg yolk for washing

## Directions

1. Stew the pigeons the same as in No. 143
2. Boil 4 or 5 large floury potatoes, cut in pieces in a very little water
3. When done, strain them dry
4. Mash them with a fork, some salt and white pepper
5. Add a little dried marjoram and chopped parsley
6. Add a little good dripping or melted fat
7. Mix in 2 eggs
8. Put a layer round the bottom and sides of a pie dish
9. Lay in the stewed pigeons
10. Cover over with the potatoes
11. Shape the edge all round nicely
12. Form it well on the top just as another pie
13. Wash it over with the yolk of an egg
14. Bake in a good oven
15. Have a nice gravy with pigeons
