## Ingredients

- one duck
- one onion
- mint
- one pint of beef gravy
- one quart of green peas
- lump sugar

## Directions

1. Stuff and half roast a duck
2. Put it into a stew-pan with an onion sliced and a little mint
3. Add about one pint of beef gravy
4. Simmer for half an hour
5. Add a quart of green peas and a little lump sugar
6. Simmer another half hour
