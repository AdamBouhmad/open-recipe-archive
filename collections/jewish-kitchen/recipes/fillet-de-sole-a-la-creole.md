## Ingredients

- large flounders
- butter
- brown flour
- onion
- one-half can of tomatoes
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- green peppers
- parsley
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Fillet some large flounders, and have fishman send you all the bones.
2. Put the bones on to boil.
3. Wash, dry, and season the fillets; roll them (putting in some bits of butter), and fasten each one with a wooden toothpick.
4. Strain the water from the bones; thicken with a little brown flour and onion.
5. Add to this one-half can of tomatoes, a little cayenne pepper, salt, and chopped green peppers.
6. Let this sauce simmer for a couple of hours (this need not be strained).
7. Put the fillets in a casserole, and pour some of this sauce over them, and put in the oven for about fifteen minutes.
8. Pour over the rest of the tomato sauce, sprinkle a little chopped parsley and serve.
9. One can add a few mushrooms to the sauce. The mushrooms must be fried in butter before being added to the sauce.
