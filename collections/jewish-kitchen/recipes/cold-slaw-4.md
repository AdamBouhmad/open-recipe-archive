## Ingredients

- one firm, white head of cabbage
- one onion
- one sour apple
- salt
- white pepper
- white sugar
- one tablespoon goose oil or butter
- white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a firm, white head of cabbage; cut it in halves; take out the heart and cut as fine as possible on slaw-cutter.
2. Cut up one onion at the same time and a sour apple.
3. Sprinkle with salt and white pepper and a liberal quantity of white sugar.
4. Mix this lightly with two forks.
5. Heat one tablespoon of goose oil or butter, and mix it thoroughly in with the cabbage.
6. Heat some white wine vinegar in a spider; let it come to a boil and pour over the slaw, boiling.
7. Keep covered for a short time.
8. Serve cold.
