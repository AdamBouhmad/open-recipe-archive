## Ingredients

- six tender, sweet peppers
- bread crumbs (sufficient to make one pint when water is pressed out)
- one-fourth teaspoon basil
- herbs
- two teaspoons of salt
- two tablespoons of butter
- white sauce
- two cups of water

## Directions

1. Soak in water bread crumbs sufficient to make one pint when the water is pressed out
2. Mix with one-fourth teaspoon basil, herbs and two teaspoons of salt, add two tablespoons of butter
3. Cut off the stem end of each pepper
4. Carefully remove the interior and fill the peppers with the prepared dressing
5. Place in a shallow baking-pan and pour around them white sauce thinned with two cups of water
6. Bake about one hour, basting frequently with the sauce
