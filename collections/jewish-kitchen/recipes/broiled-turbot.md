## Ingredients

- small turbot
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Take a small turbot and lay it in a marinade of oil, vinegar, pepper, and salt
2. Let it lay for an hour
3. Broil it a nice colour
4. Remember to slit the skin on the black side
5. Dish it up and serve with caper sauce
