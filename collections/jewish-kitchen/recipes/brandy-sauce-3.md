## Ingredients

- one cup of water
- a quarter glass of brandy
- one cup of sugar
- juice of half a lemon
- yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Take one cup of water, a quarter glass of brandy, one cup of sugar, and juice of half a lemon
2. Boil all in double boiler
3. Beat the yolks of two eggs light
4. Add the boiling sauce gradually to them, stirring constantly until thick
