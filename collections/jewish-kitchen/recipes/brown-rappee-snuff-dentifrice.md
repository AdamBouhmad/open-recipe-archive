## Ingredients

- two ounces of brown rappee snuff
- one ounce of powder of bark
- one ounce and a half of powder of myrrh

## Directions

1. Mix together two ounces of brown rappee snuff, one ounce of powder of bark, and one ounce and a half of powder of myrrh
