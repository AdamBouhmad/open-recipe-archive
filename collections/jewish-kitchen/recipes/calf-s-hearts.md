## Ingredients

- calf's hearts
- bread dressing
- flour
- hot fat
- hot water

## Directions

1. Remove veins and arteries from the hearts
2. Stuff with a highly seasoned bread dressing and sew
3. Dredge in flour, brown in hot fat
4. Cover with hot water, and place on the back of the stove or in a hot oven
5. Cook slowly for two or three hours
6. Thicken the liquor with flour and serve with the hearts
