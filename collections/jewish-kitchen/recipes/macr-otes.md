## Ingredients

- one pound of French roll dough
- six ounces of fresh butter
- two eggs
- flour
- best Florence oil
- clarified sugar
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Take one pound of French roll dough, six ounces of fresh butter, two eggs, and as much flour as will be requisite to knead it together
2. Roll it into the form of a long French roll and cut it in thin round slices
3. Set them at a short distance from the fire to rise
4. Fry in the best Florence oil
5. When nearly cold, dip them in clarified sugar flavored with essence of lemon
