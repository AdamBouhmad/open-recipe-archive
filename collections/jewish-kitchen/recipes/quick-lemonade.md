## Ingredients

- two lemons
- four tablespoons of sugar
- one quart of water
- cracked ice
- a little fresh fruit or slices of lemon if convenient

## Directions

1. Wash two lemons and squeeze the juice.
2. Mix thoroughly with four tablespoons of sugar, and when the sugar is dissolved add one quart of water, cracked ice, and a little fresh fruit or slices of lemon if convenient.
3. If the cracked ice is very finely chopped and put in the glasses just before serving it will make a better-looking lemonade.
