## Ingredients

- veal
- salt
- ground ginger
- parsley
- caraway seed

## Directions

1. Prepare as above, but do not have the meat cut in small pieces
2. If desired one-half teaspoon of caraway seed may be used instead of the parsley
3. Mashed potatoes and green peas or stewed tomatoes are usually served with veal
4. Any of the flour or potato dumplings are excellent served with stewed or fricasseed veal
