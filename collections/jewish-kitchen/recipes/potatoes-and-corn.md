## Ingredients

- butter
- potatoes
- corn
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- cracker crumbs
- milk

## Directions

1. Butter well a deep baking dish, holding a quart or more
2. In the bottom place a layer of potatoes, sliced thin
3. Add a layer of corn, using one-half the contents of a can
4. Sprinkle a little grated onion and season with salt, pepper and bits of butter
5. Add another layer of potatoes, then the rest of the corn, seasoning as before
6. Cover the whole with a layer of cracker crumbs
7. Dot well with butter
8. Pour on milk until it comes to the top
9. Bake three-quarters of an hour
10. Use cooked potatoes, having them cold before slicing
