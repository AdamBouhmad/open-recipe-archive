## Ingredients

- one-half pint of water
- one-quarter pound of melted fat
- one-quarter pound of meal, finely sifted
- four eggs
- cinnamon
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Into one-half pint of water put one-quarter pound of melted fat
2. When boiling add one-quarter pound of meal, finely sifted
3. Beat up four eggs, remove the mixture from the fire and stir in the eggs
4. Grease some cups and put a spoonful in each
5. Bake in a quick oven
6. When done sprinkle with cinnamon and cover with clarified sugar
