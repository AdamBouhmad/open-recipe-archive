## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 oz. loaf sugar
- 1 teacupful of the best rice
- lemon rind or orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well
2. Let it soak for a quarter of an hour
3. Rinse out the saucepan
4. Put in 3/4 a teacupful of cold water
5. Put the milk and the rice well drained from the water
6. When you wish to flavour it with lemon, cut some of the rind very thin and boil it with the rice and milk
7. With orange-flower water, put that in when it is done
8. Turn it out in a china or glass dish
9. Put over some powdered cinnamon
