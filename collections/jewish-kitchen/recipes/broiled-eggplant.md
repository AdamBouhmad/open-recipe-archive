## Ingredients

- eggplant
- salt and pepper
- olive oil
- dried bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Slice the eggplant and drain it as for frying
2. Spread the slices on a dish
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Baste with olive oil
5. Sprinkle with dried bread crumbs and broil
