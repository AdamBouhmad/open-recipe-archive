## Ingredients

- one pound and one-half of tenderloin
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- soup stock or water
- carrot
- bay leaf
- tomato or brown sauce

## Directions

1. Take one pound and one-half of tenderloin, sprinkle it with parsley and onion.
2. Season with pepper and salt; roll and tie it.
3. Place it in a pan with soup stock (or water if you have no stock), carrot and bay leaf and pot roast for one and one-half hours.
4. Serve with tomato or brown sauce.
