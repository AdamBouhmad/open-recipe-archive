## Ingredients

- turkey
- water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- white paper, greased
- stuffing

## Directions

1. Put it in water and salt it
2. Let all the water drain out of the inside
3. Dry the outside well with a clean cloth
4. Prepare the stuffing and put it in at the breast
5. Plump it nicely and sew the skin with a needle and thread
6. Do not fill it too much or the skin might burst
7. Rub it well outside with mixed pepper and salt
8. Flour it lightly
9. Lay it in a dry dish and leave it for one hour before roasting
10. Put a sheet of white paper well greased over the breast to prevent it being scorched
11. Remove it an hour before sending it up
12. Dredge it lightly with flour
