## Ingredients

- fish (soles, slices of salmon, halibut, or plaice)
- oil
- onion
- water (1 cup)
- mushroom ketchup or powder
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- browned flour
- port wine (1 glass)
- truffles or mushrooms

## Directions

1. Fry some fish of a light brown, either soles, slices of salmon, halibut, or plaice
2. Let an onion brown in a little oil
3. Add to it a cup of water, a little mushroom ketchup or powder, cayenne pepper, salt, nutmeg, and lemon juice
4. Put the fish into a stew-pan with the above mixture, and simmer gently till done
5. Take out the fish and thicken the gravy with a little browned flour
6. Stir in a glass of port wine
7. A few truffles, or mushrooms, are an improvement
