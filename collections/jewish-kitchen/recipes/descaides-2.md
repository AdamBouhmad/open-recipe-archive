## Ingredients

- livers of chickens or any other poultry
- good gravy
- onion
- mushroom essence
- pepper
- salt
- yolks of one or two eggs
- round of toast

## Directions

1. Stew the livers gently in a little good gravy seasoned with a little onion, mushroom essence, pepper, and salt
2. When tender, remove the livers, place them on a paste board, and mince them
3. Return them to the saucepan, and stir in the yolks of one or two eggs, according to the quantity of liver, until the gravy becomes thick
4. Have a round of toast ready on a hot plate, and serve the livers on the toast
