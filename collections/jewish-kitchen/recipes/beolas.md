## Ingredients

- six eggs
- fine meal
- boiling olive oil or fat
- water
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take six eggs
2. Beat them until very light
3. Add a little fine meal, just enough to give it consistency
4. Drop this from the point of a spoon into boiling olive oil or fat
5. When light brown, take out, and drain
6. Serve cold with a syrup made of water, cinnamon and sugar
