## Ingredients

- string or green snap beans
- cold water
- salt
- one tablespoon of drippings
- part of an onion
- parsley
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt
- pepper
- meat gravy or soup stock
- potatoes
- cream or milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the tops and bottoms and string carefully
2. Break the beans in pieces about an inch long
3. Lay them in cold water with a little salt for ten or fifteen minutes
4. Heat one tablespoon of drippings in a stew-pan
5. Cut up part of an onion and some parsley in the stew-pan, cover and stew about ten minutes
6. Drain the beans, put into the stew-pan and stew until tender
7. Add one tablespoon of flour and season with salt and pepper
8. Meat gravy or soup stock will improve them
9. You may pare about half a dozen potatoes, cut into dice shape, and add to the beans
10. You may add cream or milk instead of soup stock and use butter
