## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. fine fresh salmon
- 5 or 6 anchovies
- dozen button mushrooms
- parsley
- lemon thyme
- 2 shalots
- 3 oz. butter
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 lemon
- 1 teacup water
- 4 eggs
- butter
- puff paste
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut 2 lbs. of fine fresh salmon in slices, about 3/4 of an inch thick.
2. Wash and clean 5 or 6 anchovies and halve them.
3. Chop a dozen button mushrooms, a little parsley, a little lemon thyme, a couple of shalots very fine.
4. Put them into a very clean stewpan with 3 oz. of butter, some pepper, salt, nutmeg, the juice of a lemon, a teacup of water.
5. Put in the salmon.
6. Let it simmer gently, not the anchovies.
7. Boil hard 4 eggs, when done put them in cold water, cut them in slices.
8. Grease a pie dish with butter, put layers of the slices of salmon with some of the anchovies, and the slices of hard egg.
9. When you have used it all throw over the gravy it was cooked in.
10. Have ready a good puff paste, make your pie as ornamental as you can, wash it over with yellow of eggs, with the paste brush.
