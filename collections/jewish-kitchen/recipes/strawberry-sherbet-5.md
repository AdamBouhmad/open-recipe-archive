## Ingredients

- a quart of ripe strawberries
- a quart of water
- juice of two lemons
- a pound of sugar
- one tablespoon of rose-water
- chopped ice

## Directions

1. Crush a quart of ripe strawberries, pour a quart of water over them, and add the juice of two lemons.
2. Let this stand about two hours, then strain over a pound of sugar, stir until the sugar is dissolved, and then set upon ice.
3. You may add one tablespoon of rose-water.
4. Serve with chopped ice.
