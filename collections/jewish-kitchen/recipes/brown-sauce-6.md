## Ingredients

- one tablespoon chopped onion
- one tablespoon fat
- one tablespoon of flour
- one cup of soup stock
- one teaspoon lemon juice
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry one tablespoon chopped onion in one tablespoon fat
2. Add one tablespoon of flour, one cup of soup stock, one teaspoon lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste
3. Strain before serving
