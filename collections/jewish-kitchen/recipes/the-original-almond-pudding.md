## Ingredients

- 16 eggs, leave out 8 whites
- 1 lb. of ground almonds (2 oz. of bitter are included)
- 1 lb. of sifted sugar
- two-pennyworth of orange-flower water
- almond oil
- 1 lb. of sugar and 1/2 pint water for clarifying (optional)
- blanched almonds and thousands for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well with the orange-flower water
2. Add the sugar and almonds gently
3. Beat it all for 1 hour till it bubbles
4. Grease the dishes with almond oil
5. Put in the mixture
6. Bake in rather a moderate oven
7. When half done and the mixture is set, put over them a paper greased with the almond oil for them not to get too dark
8. Loosen them and let them cool before you take them out of the dish
9. If you wish to have them very rich, clarify 1 lb. of sugar and 1/2 pint water, and when the pudding comes out of the oven pour it over
10. Garnish with blanched almonds and thousands
