## Ingredients

- one large chicken
- stock
- one cup of corn meal or farina
- one tablespoon of chili sauce
- three tablespoons of tomato catsup
- salt
- one teaspoon of Spanish pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Boil till tender one large chicken
2. Have two quarts of stock left when chicken is done
3. Remove chicken and cut into medium-sized pieces
4. Into the stock pour gradually one cup of corn meal or farina, stirring until it thickens
5. If not the proper consistency, add a little more meal
6. Season with one tablespoon of chili sauce, three tablespoons of tomato catsup, salt, one teaspoon of Spanish pepper sauce
7. Simmer gently thirty minutes
8. then add chicken
9. Serve in ramekins
