## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 2.5 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking-powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 0.25 cup small raisins

## Directions

1. Beat two whole eggs for ten minutes with two cups of sugar, two and one-half tablespoons of melted butter
2. Add one cup of milk, three cups of flour in which have been sifted two teaspoons of baking-powder
3. Flavor with one teaspoon of vanilla
4. One-fourth cup of small raisins may be added
5. Bake one hour
