## Ingredients

- butter
- cake-pans
- rich kuchen dough
- cornstarch
- one cup of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one teaspoon of cornstarch
- cold milk
- sugar
- vanilla
- huckleberries

## Directions

1. Line your cake-pans, which should be long and narrow, with a rich kuchen dough, having previously greased them well
2. Make a paste of cornstarch, one cup of milk, one tablespoon of butter and one teaspoon of cornstarch wet with cold milk
3. Boil until thick, sweeten and flavor with vanilla and spread on top of the cake dough
4. Sprinkle thickly with huckleberries which have been carefully picked, sugared
