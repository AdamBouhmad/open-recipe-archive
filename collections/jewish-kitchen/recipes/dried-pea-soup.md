## Ingredients

- one cup picked and cleaned dried split peas
- cold water
- two quarts cold water
- smoked beef-cheek or other smoked meat
- one-half cup celery, diced
- one small onion, cut fine
- one teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon pepper
- one tablespoon fat
- one tablespoon flour
- croutons

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of picked and cleaned dried split peas in cold water over night
2. Drain, put on with two quarts cold water, a smoked beef-cheek or any other smoked meat
3. Let boil slowly but steadily four hours or more
4. Add one-half cup of celery, diced, one small onion cut fine, one teaspoon of salt, one-eighth teaspoon of pepper
5. Cook until the meat and peas are tender
6. Remove meat when tender
7. Skim fat off the top of the soup
8. Heat one tablespoon of the fat in a frying pan, add one tablespoon of flour and gradually the rest of the soup
9. Season to taste and serve with the smoked meat, adding croutons
