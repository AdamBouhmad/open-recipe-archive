## Ingredients

- spinach
- cream sauce
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook spinach as directed
2. Drain through colander
3. Grind through machine
4. Make a rich cream sauce
5. Stir spinach in this sauce
6. Add pepper, salt, nutmeg to taste
7. Garnish with slices of hard-boiled egg
