## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound of goose-oil
- yolks of ten eggs
- three-quarters of a pound of sifted sugar
- grated peel of a lemon
- juice of a lemon
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half pound of potato flour
- whites of ten eggs

## Directions

1. Stir goose-oil to a cream
2. Stir in gradually the yolks of ten eggs and three-quarters of a pound of sifted sugar
3. Add the grated peel of a lemon, also its juice and one-half teaspoon of salt
4. Add last one-half pound of potato flour and the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
5. Have the pudding form well greased before putting in the mixture
6. Bake in a moderate oven
7. Serve with raspberry sauce
