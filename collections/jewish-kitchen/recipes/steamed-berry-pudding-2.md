## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one-quarter cup of sugar
- yolk of one egg
- one-half cup of milk
- one cup of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of berries or pitted cherries

## Directions

1. Roll berries or pitted cherries in flour
2. Put in a well-greased melon mold
3. Cook in boiling water steadily for two hours
4. Serve with hard sauce
