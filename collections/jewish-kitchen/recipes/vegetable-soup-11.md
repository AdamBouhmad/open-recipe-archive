## Ingredients

- one small soup bone
- cold water
- one-half cup celery
- one-half cup carrots
- one-half cup onion
- fat
- one tablespoon chopped parsley
- one-half cup potatoes
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a small soup bone, cover with cold water
2. Cut one-half a cup each of celery, carrots, and onion
3. Brown in fat, cooking five to ten minutes
4. Add one tablespoon of chopped parsley and one-half cup of potatoes
5. Add to soup bone and cook one hour
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Remove bone and serve
