## Ingredients

- three cups of cold, boiled beets
- one-half cup of grated horseradish
- one-quarter teaspoon of pepper
- one teaspoon of salt
- two tablespoons of sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take three cups of cold, boiled beets, grate and add one-half cup of grated horseradish
2. Season with one-quarter teaspoon of pepper, one teaspoon of salt and two tablespoons of sugar
3. Add all the vinegar the horseradish and beets will absorb, and place in covered jar or glass and it is ready for use
