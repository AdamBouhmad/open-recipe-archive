## Ingredients

- neck of turkey
- almonds
- walnuts
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Take neck of turkey
2. Chop almonds or walnuts fine and season with chopped parsley, pepper and salt
3. Put two hard-boiled eggs in the centre of this dressing
4. Stuff neck, sew up the ends
5. When roasted slice across so as to have a portion of the hard-boiled egg on each slice
6. Place on platter and surround with sprigs of parsley
