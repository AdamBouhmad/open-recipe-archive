## Ingredients

- yolks of two eggs
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one small cup of cold water
- one wineglass of rum
- juice of a lemon
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of two eggs with a tablespoon of sugar, and a small cup of cold water, a wineglass of rum and the juice of a lemon
2. Bring to boiling point, stirring all the time
3. The two whites of eggs may be whipped very firm and spread over the pudding just before serving
