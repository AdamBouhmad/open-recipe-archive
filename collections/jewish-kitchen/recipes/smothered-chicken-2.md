## Ingredients

- tender chickens
- salt
- pepper
- celery
- onion
- flour
- fat
- hot water

## Directions

1. Two tender chickens cut in half, split down the back.
2. Place the pieces in a colander to drain well, after having been well salted.
3. Season with pepper.
4. Grease well the bottom of a baking-pan.
5. Add one stalk finely chopped celery, onion.
6. Lay the chicken on breast, side up.
7. Sprinkle lightly with flour, fat.
8. Add two cups of hot water.
9. Have the oven very hot when putting chickens in.
10. As soon as browned evenly, cover with a pan, fitting closely.
11. Reduce the heat of the oven; allow to cook slowly.
