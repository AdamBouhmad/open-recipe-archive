## Ingredients

- one quart of carrots
- one quart of boiling water
- one teaspoon of salt
- two tablespoons fat
- one small onion
- one teaspoon of sugar
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Scrape, slice and cook one quart of carrots in one quart of boiling water to which has been added one teaspoon of salt, until tender
2. Drain
3. Heat two tablespoons fat, add one small onion, brown lightly, add the carrots, season with one teaspoon of sugar, one-quarter teaspoon of salt
