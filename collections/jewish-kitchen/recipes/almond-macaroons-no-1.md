## Ingredients

- half a pound of almonds
- one pound of pulverized sugar
- whites of four eggs
- water
- butter or waxed paper

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of almonds
2. Pound in mortar to a smooth paste
3. Add one pound of pulverized sugar and the beaten whites of four eggs
4. Work the paste well together with the back of a spoon
5. Dip your hands in water and roll the mixture into balls the size of a hickory nut
6. Lay on buttered or waxed paper an inch apart
7. When done, dip your hands in water and pass gently over the macaroons, making the surface smooth and shiny
8. Set in a cool oven three-quarters of an hour
