## Ingredients

- meal
- eggs
- lemon peel rasped
- 1 oz. of ground almonds
- boiling oil
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clarified sugar
- lemon juice
- rum

## Directions

1. Sieve some meal very fine
2. To every spoon of meal allow 1 egg
3. Mix it to a batter with some lemon peel rasped and 1 oz. of ground almonds
4. Have a pan with boiling oil
5. Take up a spoonful at the time and put them in as round as you can
6. They will form a round ball
7. Sprinkle over some sugar and cinnamon
8. Serve them with clarified sugar, flavoured with lemon juice and rum
