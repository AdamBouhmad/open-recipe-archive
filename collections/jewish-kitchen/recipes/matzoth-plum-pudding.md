## Ingredients

- one-half pound of chopped suet
- one-half pound of moist sugar
- one-half pound of raisins (stoned and chopped)
- one-half pound of currants
- one-half pound of mixed peel
- two matzoth soaked in cold water and well drained and beaten
- one-quarter pound of sifted meal
- rind of half a lemon
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- eight eggs
- a wineglass of rum

## Directions

1. Beat all ingredients thoroughly together
2. Boil for eight hours in a pudding mold or basin
3. Serve with rum sauce
