## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one-half cup of cold water
- one cup of white wine
- one ounce of chopped citron
- one teaspoon of pistache
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- one teaspoon of red Curaqoa or Maraschino liquor

## Directions

1. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a saucepan
2. stir in one tablespoon of flour
3. add one-half cup of cold water, stirring constantly until smooth
4. add one cup of white wine
5. add one ounce of chopped citron
6. Remove from fire
7. let cool
8. flavor with one teaspoon each of pistache and vanilla
9. If desired, one teaspoon of red Curaqoa or Maraschino liquor can be added for flavoring
