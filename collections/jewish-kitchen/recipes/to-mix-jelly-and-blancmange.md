## Ingredients

- 1 pint of jelly
- 1 pint of blancmange
- pink sugar from the confectioners

## Directions

1. Just slightly grease your 2 pint moulds
2. To make the blancmange pink you must get the pink sugar from the confectioners; they sell it all ready, and when you make the blancmange you colour it with that
3. Colour half of the blancmange and leave the other part white
4. Put in to one mould a portion of the pink, into the other a portion of the white
5. Put them both in a cold place to set
6. When quite cold, pour in a layer of the jelly (it need only be just warm like a thick liquid) into both moulds
7. Return them to the cold place
8. When set, use the remainder of the blancmange between the 2 moulds, let that set
9. Then divide the jelly between the two moulds
10. When turned out, they look very pretty
