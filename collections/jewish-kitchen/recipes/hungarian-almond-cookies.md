## Ingredients

- scant one-quarter of a pound of almonds, blanched and grated
- scant one-half pound of sweet butter
- not quite three-quarters of a pound of flour
- a little sugar
- a pinch of salt
- two yolks

## Directions

1. Mix this well, pound the dough well with the rolling-pin.
2. Roll out not too thin.
3. Bake.
