## Ingredients

- haddock
- onion
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- turnips
- water
- flour
- grated nutmeg
- dried saffron
- cayenne
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and clean well a fine haddock
2. Cut it in 7 or 8 pieces, do not slit it open
3. Put in a stewpan some onion sliced with 2 spoonfuls of oil, salt, pepper
4. Let it fry
5. Peel and cut some turnips as for stewed cod
6. Put the pieces in with a teacup of water
7. Let them boil till nearly tender
8. Put in the pieces of haddock
9. When cooked, take out the haddock and lay it in the dish ready
10. Stir in a basin a spoonful of flour, a little grated nutmeg, a little dried saffron, a little cayenne, and a little chopped parsley
11. Rub it all smoothly
12. Stir it in the stewpan with the turnip till it is a nice thickness
13. Place the turnip round the fish and spoon the sauce over both
14. Garnish with parsley
