## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh butter
- a pound of flour
- half a pound of sifted sugar
- a tea-spoonful of pounded cinnamon
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Rub half a pound of fresh butter into a pound of flour
2. Work it well together, then add half a pound of sifted sugar and a tea-spoonful of pounded cinnamon
3. Make it into a paste with three eggs
4. Roll it and cut into small cakes with tin cutters
