## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of butter
- one onion
- one tablespoon of curry powder
- one-fourth of a cup of water
- two tablespoons of flour
- one tablespoon of lemon juice
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- six hard-boiled eggs
- rice

## Directions

1. Melt four tablespoons of butter in a frying-pan
2. Add one onion chopped fine and cook until straw colored
3. Add one tablespoon of curry powder
4. Make a smooth paste of one-fourth of a cup of water and two tablespoons of flour
5. Add one tablespoon of lemon juice and one-half teaspoon of salt
6. Add to the first mixture
7. Boil five minutes
8. Arrange six hard-boiled eggs in a border of rice
9. Pour the dressing over all
