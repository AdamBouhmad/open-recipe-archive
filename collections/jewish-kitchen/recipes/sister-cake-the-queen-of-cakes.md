## Ingredients

- 1 lb. roll dough
- 1 lb. fresh butter
- 6 eggs
- 8 oz. ground almonds
- 1 lb. citron
- 1 lb. orange and lemon peel
- 3 lb. loaf sugar
- orange-flower water
- salt
- 2 oz. Jordan almonds
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Put the dough in a very nice pan, put it near the fire to warm.
2. Melt the 1 lb. of butter in a teacup of milk, taking a piece of the butter first to rub your dish with.
3. Beat up the 6 eggs, put them in the pan with the dough.
4. Put in the ground almonds, then the butter and milk, a small pinch of salt.
5. Beat it all well for 1 hour.
6. Have ready a nice round yellow dish, butter it well.
7. Cut up the citron in slices in one plate, then the orange and lemon in another.
8. Put at the bottom of your dish a layer of the mixture of dough, butter, almonds, etc., then a layer of citron, then dough, then orange and lemon.
9. Continue making layers till you have used up all your ingredients, making your layer very lightly.
10. Your oven must be ready for it as soon as it is made, not a fierce oven.
11. It will take an hour to bake and will rise very much.
12. Put an iron skewer through to see if it is done; if the skewer comes out clean it is done.
13. Have 2 oz. of Jordan almonds bleached, put them on a plate in the oven to dry and get a light brown colour.
14. Put the 3 lbs. of sugar in a very clean saucepan with a pint of water, let it boil till it comes like a syrup.
15. Throw the orange-flower water in.
16. Have the sugar ready when the cake is done, as it must be poured hot when the cake comes out of the oven.
17. Just pass a knife round to let it soak.
18. Let the last layer on the cake be citron, put it on in pattern.
19. Ornament it with the almonds and thousands.
20. When it is cold you can turn it in a china or deep glass dish.
