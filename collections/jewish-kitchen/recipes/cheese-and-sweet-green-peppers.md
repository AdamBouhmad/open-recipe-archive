## Ingredients

- two ounces of cheese
- one tablespoon of chopped peppers
- one tablespoon of butter
- paprika
- salt
- mustard
- hot buttered toast

## Directions

1. Melt two ounces of cheese
2. Add a tablespoon of chopped peppers and the same amount of butter
3. Add a little paprika, salt, and if liked, mustard
4. When the ingredients have been well blended pour the mixture on hot buttered toast and serve
