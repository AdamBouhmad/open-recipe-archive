## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- half a pint of water
- one ounce of sweet almonds blanched and pounded
- the yolks of twenty eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Clarify one pound of sugar in half a pint of water till it becomes a thick syrup
2. While clarifying, add one ounce of sweet almonds blanched and pounded
3. Let it cool and stir in gently the yolks of twenty eggs which have been previously beaten and passed through a sieve
4. Stir it continually the same way
5. When well mixed, place it over a slow fire till it thickens, stirring all the time to prevent burning
