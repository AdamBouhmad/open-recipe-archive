## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking-powder
- bitter chocolate
- lemon
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Take two cups of sugar, one cup of butter, four eggs (yolks), one cup of milk, three cups of flour, and three teaspoons of baking-powder (scant)
2. Cream the butter and sugar, and add the yolks of eggs
3. Then add the milk, flour, baking-powder, and the beaten whites of the eggs; flavor with lemon
4. To make the brown part; take a square of bitter chocolate and melt above steam, and mix with some of the white
5. Flavor the brown with vanilla
6. Put first a tablespoon of brown batter in the pan, and then the white
7. Bake in quick oven thirty-five minutes
