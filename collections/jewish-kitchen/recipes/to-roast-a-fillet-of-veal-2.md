## Ingredients

- fillet of veal
- round bone
- bread, thick piece
- suet, 2 oz.
- lemon rind
- parsley, chopped
- powdered marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take out the round bone
2. Make a good stuffing: soak a thick piece of bread, drain well from water, chop 2 oz. of suet with a little lemon rind, a little chopped parsley, a little powdered marjoram, salt, pepper, and a little grated lemon peel, mix well with an egg
3. Fill the cavity with the stuffing
4. Salt and pepper the joint well
5. Dredge it with flour
6. Roast for about 3 hours to a beautiful brown
7. Dish it up with a nice gravy mixed with the juice of a lemon well strained
8. Throw the gravy over the meat
