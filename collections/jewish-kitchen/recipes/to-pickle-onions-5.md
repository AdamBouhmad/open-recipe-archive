## Ingredients

- onions
- brine
- distilled vinegar
- white wine vinegar
- mace
- ginger
- whole pepper

## Directions

1. Choose all of a size and soak in boiling brine
2. When cold, drain them and put them in bottles
3. Fill up with hot distilled vinegar; if they are to be white, use white wine vinegar; if they are to be brown, use the best distilled vinegar
4. In both cases, add a little mace, ginger, and whole pepper
