## Ingredients

- fowl
- sweetbread
- meat
- jelly
- yolks of hard-boiled eggs
- lemon-juice
- white pepper
- salt
- half a pound of rice
- water
- yolk of one or two eggs

## Directions

1. Cut up fowl and sweetbread
2. Lay in the dish in alternate layers with meat, jelly, and the yolks of hard-boiled eggs without the whites
3. Flavor with lemon-juice, white pepper, and salt
4. Boil half a pound of rice in sufficient water to permit it to swell
5. When tender beat it up to a thick paste with the yolk of one or two eggs
6. Season with a little salt
7. Spread it over the dish thickly as cover
