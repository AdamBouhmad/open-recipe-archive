## Ingredients

- one and one-half tablespoons of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one and one-half cups of rich milk
- pepper
- salt
- four egg yolks
- three-quarters of a cup of grated cheese
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve one and one-half tablespoons of butter, add one tablespoon of flour, stir until it loosens from the pan.
2. Add one and one-half cups of rich milk, pepper and salt.
3. Take from the fire, add gradually four egg yolks and three-quarters of a cup of grated cheese, then the stiffly beaten whites of eggs.
4. Bake in a hot oven in china ramekins about fifteen minutes and serve immediately.
