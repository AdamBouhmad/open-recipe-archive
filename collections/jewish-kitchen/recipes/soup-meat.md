## Ingredients

- soup
- tablespoon of fat or drippings of roast beef
- slices of onion
- half a clove of garlic
- tablespoon of flour
- soup stock or rich gravy
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- one-half teaspoon of caraway seed

## Directions

1. Cook the meat until very tender then lift it out of the soup and lay upon a platter and season while hot.
2. Heat a tablespoon of fat or drippings of roast beef in a spider and cut up a few slices of onion in it, also half a clove of garlic.
3. Add a tablespoon of flour, stirring all the time.
4. Add soup stock or rich gravy and the soup meat, which has been seasoned with salt, pepper and ginger.
5. Sprinkle the spices on both sides of the meat and add one-half teaspoon of caraway seed to the sauce.
6. If too thick add more soup stock and a little boiling water.
7. Cover closely and let it simmer about fifteen minutes.
