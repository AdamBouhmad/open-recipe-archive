## Ingredients

- one ounce and a half of potatoe flour
- one ounce and a half of grated cheese
- one ounce and a half of butter
- a quarter of a pint of milk or cream
- salt
- pepper
- yolks of four fine fresh eggs
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Make into a batter one ounce and a half of potatoe flour, with the same quantity of grated cheese and of butter, and a quarter of a pint of milk or cream
2. Add a little salt, very little pepper, and the well-beaten yolks of four fine fresh eggs
3. When all this is well mixed together, pour in the whites of the eggs, well whisked to a froth
4. Pour the mixture into a deep soup plate or dish, used expressly for the purpose
5. Bake in a moderate oven
6. The dish should be only half filled with the fondu, as it will rise very high
7. Serve the moment it is ready, or it will fall
8. Hold a salamander over it while being brought to table
