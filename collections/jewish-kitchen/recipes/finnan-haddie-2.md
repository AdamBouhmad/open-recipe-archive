## Ingredients

- finnan haddie
- milk
- water
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Parboil ten minutes and then broil like fresh fish.
2. To bake, place the fish in a pan, add one cup of milk and one cup of water and cover.
3. Cook ten minutes in hot oven.
4. Remove cover, drain, spread with butter and season with pepper.
