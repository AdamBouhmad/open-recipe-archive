## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- two eggs
- salt
- water or milk
- two tablespoons butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Sift two cups of flour into a bowl, make a depression in the centre and break into it two eggs
2. Add a saltspoon of salt and enough water or milk to form a smooth, stiff dough
3. Set on some water to boil, salt the water
4. When the water boils, drop the spaetzle into it, one at a time, using the spoon with which you cut the dough, or roll it on a board into a round roll and cut them with a knife
5. When the spaetzle are done, they will rise to the surface, take them out with a perforated skimmer and lay them on a platter
6. Heat two tablespoons of butter and add bread crumbs, let them brown for a minute and pour all over the spaetzle
7. Alternatively, put the spaetzle right into the spider in which you have heated the butter, or scramble a few eggs over all, stirring eggs and spaetzle together
8. Serve hot
