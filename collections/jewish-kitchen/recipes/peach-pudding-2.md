## Ingredients

- two eggs
- three tablespoons of sugar
- two tablespoons of butter
- a saltspoon of salt
- half a grated nutmeg
- a cup of milk
- a cup and a half of flour
- three-quarters of a cup of Indian meal
- three teaspoons of baking-powder
- fine ripe peaches

## Directions

1. Whip to a cream two eggs, three tablespoons of sugar, and two tablespoons of butter
2. Add a saltspoon of salt and half a grated nutmeg
3. Stir these ingredients well into the mixture
4. Stir in a cup of milk
5. Add a little at a time, stirring well in to make a smooth batter, a cup and a half of flour and three-quarters of a cup of Indian meal which have been sifted together with three teaspoons of baking-powder
6. Butter well the inside of a two-quart pudding mold
7. Put a layer of the pudding batter an inch deep in the mold
8. Cover this with a layer of fine ripe peaches that have been peeled and cut in quarters or eighths
9. Sprinkle the layer of peaches with a light layer of sugar
10. Pour in a layer of batter, then a layer of peaches
11. Repeat this process till all the material is in, leaving a layer of batter on top
12. Steam for two hours
