## Ingredients

- boiled fish
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one egg
- one teaspoon of milk
- flour
- cracker crumbs or bread

## Directions

1. Take any kind of boiled fish
2. Separate it from the bones carefully
3. Chop with a little parsley, salt and pepper to taste
4. Beat up one egg with one teaspoon of milk and flour
5. Roll the fish into balls
6. Turn them in the beaten egg and cracker crumbs or bread
7. Fry a light brown
8. Serve with any sauce or a mayonnaise
