## Ingredients

- sprats
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- allspice
- cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash and clean them well and drain all the water from them
2. Dry them well
3. Put them in layers in a baking dish with salt, pepper, bay leaves, allspice, cloves, and ½ pint of vinegar
