## Ingredients

- a calf's foot
- salt
- pepper
- a little saffron
- a little marjoram
- grated nutmeg
- pint and a half of water
- the white of an egg
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take a calf's foot and put in a saucepan with some salt and pepper, a little saffron, a little marjoram, grated nutmeg, and pint and a half of water
2. Let it simmer for 3 or 4 hours
3. Strain it through a clean sieve into a clean pan
4. Put it by till next day
5. Skim off all the fat
6. Put it into a clean saucepan
7. Beat up the white of an egg and stir it well in
8. Let it boil till it all rises on top
9. Let it stand and throw in 2 tablespoonfuls of cold water
10. Strain it through a jelly bag the same as sweet jelly
11. When cold, cut it in any shape you like to garnish
