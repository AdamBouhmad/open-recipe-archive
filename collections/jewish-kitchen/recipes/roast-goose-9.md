## Ingredients

- goose
- salt
- ginger
- garlic
- bread dressing, or chestnut dressing, or dressing of apples
- onion
- celery

## Directions

1. All goose meat tastes better if it is well rubbed with salt, ginger and a little garlic a day previous to using
2. Stuff goose with bread dressing, or chestnut dressing, a dressing of apples is also very good
3. Sew up the goose
4. Line a sheet-iron roasting-pan with a few slices of onion and celery and place the goose upon these
5. Cover closely and roast three hours or more, according to weight
6. If the goose browns too quickly, cover with greased paper or lower the heat of the oven
7. Baste every fifteen minutes
