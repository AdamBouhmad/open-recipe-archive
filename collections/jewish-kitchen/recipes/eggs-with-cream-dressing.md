## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- three tablespoons of flour
- one and one-half cups of milk
- one teaspoon of salt
- pepper
- six hard-boiled eggs
- slices of toast

## Directions

1. Blend two tablespoons of butter with three tablespoons of flour
2. Place on range and stir until the butter is melted
3. Add one and one-half cups of milk, stirring all the time until the mixture is thick
4. Season with one teaspoon of salt and a few grains of pepper
5. Separate the whites of six hard-boiled eggs from the yolks
6. Chop the whites fine and add to the dressing
7. Arrange slices of toast on a hot platter
8. Pour the dressing over them
9. Force the yolks through a ricer onto the toast and dressing
10. Serve hot
