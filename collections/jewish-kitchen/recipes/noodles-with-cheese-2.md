## Ingredients

- two eggs (for homemade noodles) or one-quarter of a pound macaroni
- salt water
- cold water
- a cup of cheese
- a piece of fresh butter (about the size of an egg)
- a heaping tablespoon of flour
- a pint of rich milk
- butter
- cracker crumbs
- butter flakes

## Directions

1. If you make the noodles at home, use two eggs for the dough; if you buy macaroni use one-quarter of a pound, cut up and boil in salt water.
2. Boil about fifteen minutes; drain off the water and let cold water run through them.
3. Grate a cup of cheese.
4. Melt a piece of fresh butter, about the size of an egg, in a saucepan, stir in a heaping tablespoon of flour, add gradually to this a pint of rich milk, stirring constantly.
5. Take from the fire as it thickens.
6. Butter a pudding dish, lay in a layer of noodles, then cheese, then sauce, then begin with noodles again until all is used up.
7. Sprinkle cheese on top, a few cracker crumbs and flakes of butter here and there.
8. Bake until brown.
