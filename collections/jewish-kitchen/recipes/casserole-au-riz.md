## Ingredients

- rice
- one egg
- a couple of tablespoons of strong gravy
- fricassee of fowl and sweetbread
- rich white sauce

## Directions

1. Boil some rice till quite tender
2. Make it into a firm paste with one egg and a couple of tablespoons of strong gravy
3. Line the inside of a mould with the paste of sufficient thickness to turn out without breaking
4. After it is turned out the rice must stand till cold, before it is removed from the mould
5. Fill the rice with fricassee of fowl and sweetbread, with a rich white sauce
6. Place it in the oven to become hot and brown
