## Ingredients

- minced onion
- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- white pepper
- sour salt
- red tomatoes
- one-fourth cup of rice
- butter, fat or olive oil

## Directions

1. Brown a minced onion
2. Add cauliflower cut in pieces with a small quantity of water
3. Stew, add salt, white pepper, a little sour salt and red tomatoes
4. When half done add one-fourth cup of rice
5. Cook until rice is done
6. The onion may be browned either in butter, fat or olive oil, as desired
