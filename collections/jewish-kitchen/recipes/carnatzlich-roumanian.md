## Ingredients

- one pound of tenderloin
- an egg
- paprika
- black pepper
- salt
- four cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Chop one pound of tenderloin and add an egg, a little paprika, black pepper, salt and four cloves of garlic (which have been scraped, and let stand in a little salt for ten minutes, and then mashed so it looks like dough).
2. Form this meat mixture into short sausage-like rolls.
3. Boil one-half hour and serve at once.
