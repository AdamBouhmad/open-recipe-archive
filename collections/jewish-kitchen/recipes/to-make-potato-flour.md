## Ingredients

- fine large potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Have 2 very clean flat pans and a new grater
2. Peel and wash the potatoes, divide them in halves
3. Put some water in each pan
4. Grate the potatoes into one, then pour the water off into the other
5. Continue doing this until there is no more sediment left
6. Run the white sediment at the bottom of the pan onto clean white paper
7. Put the paper with the flour near the fire to dry
8. When dry, it will be a beautiful white powder
9. When thoroughly dry you can use it like ordinary flour for thickening sauces or gravies
