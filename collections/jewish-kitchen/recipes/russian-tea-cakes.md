## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one cup of eggs (about five)
- one cup of sour cream
- flour
- three-fourths pound of brick butter
- one cup of chopped almonds
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of sugar, one cup of eggs and one cup of sour cream with enough flour to roll
2. Toss on board and roll out one-fourth inch thick
3. Spread with a thin layer of butter, fold the dough over, roll and spread again; repeat three or four times, using altogether three-fourths pound of brick butter
4. Place dough in a bowl, cover, and let stand on ice to harden
5. Roll as thin as possible, strew with one cup of chopped almonds, sugar and cinnamon, and cut into seven-inch strips
6. Roll each strip separately into a roll, cut into squares and strew top with chopped almonds, sugar and cinnamon
7. Bake in a hot oven
