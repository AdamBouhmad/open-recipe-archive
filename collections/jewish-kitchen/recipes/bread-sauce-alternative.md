## Ingredients

- a large onion
- a little veal gravy
- grated bread
- pepper
- the yolk and white of an egg

## Directions

1. Slice a large onion thin
2. Put it into a little veal gravy
3. Add grated bread, pepper, &c.
4. Add the yolk and white of an egg well beaten
