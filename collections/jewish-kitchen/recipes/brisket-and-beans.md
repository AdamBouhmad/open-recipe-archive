## Ingredients

- Four pounds of boned brisket
- a large Spanish onion
- salt and pepper
- a tooth of garlic
- a small piece of mace
- 2 or 3 cloves
- a pint of water
- a pound of French beans
- a tablespoonful of flour
- one tablespoonful of brown sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Put boned brisket in a stewpan with a large Spanish onion sliced, salt and pepper, a tooth of garlic cut small, a small piece of mace, 2 or 3 cloves, and a pint of water.
2. Let it simmer gently till done, it will take nearly 3 hours.
3. When half cooked have a pound of French beans nicely cut, well washed, and drained, put them in with the meat.
4. When done rub in a basin a tablespoonful of flour, one of brown sugar, 2 of vinegar.
5. When quite smooth turn it into the saucepan and baste the sauce well over the beef and beans.
6. Let it stand for five minutes.
7. Colour the sauce with a little browning, not too deep a colour.
8. It is then ready to dish up.
