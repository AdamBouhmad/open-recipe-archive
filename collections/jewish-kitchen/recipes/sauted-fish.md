## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- flour or cornmeal
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Clean fish
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in flour or cornmeal
4. Cook in spider with just enough hot butter to prevent it sticking to the pan
5. Shake the pan occasionally
6. Brown well on under side
7. Turn and brown on the other side
