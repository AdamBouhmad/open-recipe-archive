## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of butter
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- two whole eggs
- one cup of milk
- two cups of flour
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- four tablespoons of powdered sugar
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cream four tablespoons of butter with one and one-half cups of sugar.
2. Beat in separately two whole eggs.
3. Add one cup of milk alternately with two cups of flour in which has been sifted two teaspoons of baking-powder.
4. Beat all thoroughly.
5. To make two layer cakes, spread with any of the cake fillings or icings.
6. To make small cakes omit one of the egg-whites.
7. Fill well-buttered gem pans a little more than half full.
8. Bake in a moderately hot oven until a delicate brown.
9. Beat the reserved white to a stiff froth.
10. Gradually stir in four tablespoons of powdered sugar and the juice of half a lemon.
