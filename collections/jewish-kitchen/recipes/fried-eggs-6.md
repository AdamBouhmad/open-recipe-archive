## Ingredients

- oil or butter
- bread
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Have a small frying-pan
2. Put in a little oil or butter
3. Let it boil
4. If oil, put a very small piece of bread to fry quite brown before you put in the eggs
5. Break each egg separately in a teacup
6. Be careful not to break the yolk
7. Put them into the pan
8. Keep them as round as possible
9. Have a very small slice to take them out of the pan
10. Put in a dish a sheet of writing paper
11. Let the dish be very hot
12. Put on the eggs
13. Garnish with nice little dry sprigs of parsley
14. Remember the hot plates
15. Sprinkle over the eggs salt and pepper
