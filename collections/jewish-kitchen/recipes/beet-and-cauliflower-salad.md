## Ingredients

- cooked beets
- cold cooked potatoes
- cold cooked cauliflower
- chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one level teaspoon mustard
- one teaspoon anchovy sauce
- one tablespoon milk or cream
- one dessertspoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Take some thin slices of cooked beets, some cold cooked potatoes, some cold cooked cauliflower, and a little chopped parsley. Put one level teaspoon of mustard, one teaspoon anchovy sauce, one tablespoon of milk or cream, and one dessertspoon of vinegar. Mix the mustard with the anchovy, then add the milk, and lastly the vinegar. Pour over the vegetables and add salt and pepper to taste.
