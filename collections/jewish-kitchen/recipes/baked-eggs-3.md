## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Butter individual baking dishes and break an egg in each, being careful to keep the yolk whole.
2. Put on each egg a bit of butter, a little pepper and salt.
3. Bake in moderate oven from four to six minutes.
