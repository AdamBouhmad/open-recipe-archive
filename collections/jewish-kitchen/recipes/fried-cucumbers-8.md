## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- water
- beaten egg
- cracker dust
- boiling fat

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the cucumbers lengthwise in about the same thickness observed with egg plant.
2. Lay these slices in salt and water for about an hour.
3. Dip in beaten egg and cracker dust.
4. French fry in boiling fat, taking care to carefully drain in a colander before serving.
