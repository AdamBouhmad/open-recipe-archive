## Ingredients

- sea-kale
- salt
- water
- bread
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Lay the sea-kale in salt and water for 1/2 an hour
2. Put it on to boil in a saucepan, cover it with boiling water and salt
3. Have ready a slice of bread toasted to lay the kale on
4. Serve it up with melted butter
