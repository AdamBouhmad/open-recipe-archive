## Ingredients

- a small onion minced
- one tablespoon of chicken fat
- a small liver chopped fine
- chopped parsley
- two tablespoons of flour
- nutmeg
- red and white pepper
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Brown a small onion minced in one tablespoon of chicken fat
2. Add a small liver chopped fine, chopped parsley, two tablespoons of flour
3. Season with nutmeg, red and white pepper, and add two eggs
4. Drop with teaspoon in the boiling soup
5. Let cook ten minutes and serve
