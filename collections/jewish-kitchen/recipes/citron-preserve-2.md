## Ingredients

- citron
- a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit
- four lemons and a quarter of a pound of ginger root for six pounds of fruit
- water

## Directions

1. Pare and core the citron
2. Cut it into strips and notch the edges; or cut it into fancy shapes
3. Allow a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit, and to six pounds of the fruit allow four lemons and a quarter of a pound of ginger root
4. Tie the ginger in a cloth, and boil it in a quart and a half of water until the flavor is extracted
5. Remove it, and add to the water the sugar and the juice of the lemons
6. Stir until the sugar is dissolved and the syrup is clear
7. Take off any scum
8. Add the citron and cook until it is clear, but not soft enough to fall apart
9. Can and seal while hot
