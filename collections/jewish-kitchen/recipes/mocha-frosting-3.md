## Ingredients

- one cup of pulverized sugar
- two dessertspoons of dry cocoa
- two tablespoons of strong hot coffee
- piece of butter the size of a walnut
- vanilla

## Directions

1. One cup of pulverized sugar into which sift two dessertspoons of dry cocoa
2. Mix two tablespoons of strong hot coffee in which is melted a piece of butter the size of a walnut
3. Beat well and add a little vanilla
