## Ingredients

- six macaroons
- one tablespoon of grated chocolate
- one pint of hot milk
- yolks of three eggs
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Pound and sift six macaroons
2. Add grated chocolate and hot milk
3. Let stand ten minutes
4. Add yolks of three eggs well beaten, sugar and vanilla
5. Line patty-tins with puff paste
6. Fill with the mixture
7. Bake twenty minutes
