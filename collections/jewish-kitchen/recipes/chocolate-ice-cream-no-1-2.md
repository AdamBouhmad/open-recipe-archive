## Ingredients

- three whole eggs
- one cup of granulated sugar
- one pint of cream
- one cup of sweet milk
- one-half pound of sweet chocolate
- one cup of sweet milk
- vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Make same as Vanilla Ice Cream, No. 2
2. Dissolve on stove one-half pound of sweet chocolate in one cup of sweet milk
3. Rub smooth and thick
4. Let get cold
5. Add to the eggs, just before putting in cream
6. Flavor with vanilla
