## Ingredients

- corn
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the corn
2. Cut it off the cobs
3. Pack in jars in alternate layers of salt and corn, using plenty of salt in packing
4. Pack the corn in this way: First a layer of salt, half an inch deep; then about two inches of corn; then salt again, and so on
5. The top layer must be salt
6. Spread two inches of melted butter over the top layer and bind with strong perforated paper
7. Keep in a cool cellar
