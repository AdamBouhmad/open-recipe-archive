## Ingredients

- cold dressed fish (turbot or salmon)
- small salad (1/2 pint)
- lettuce
- onions (2 boiled)
- truffles (thinly sliced)
- hard eggs
- cucumber
- hard boiled eggs (3 yolks)
- mustard (1 spoonful)
- salt
- cream (1 cup)
- vinegar (4 table-spoonsful)

## Directions

1. Cut in small pieces any cold dressed fish, turbot or salmon are the best suited
2. Mix it with half a pint of small salad, and a lettuce cut small
3. Add two onions boiled till tender and mild, and a few truffles thinly sliced
4. Pour over a fine salad mixture, and arrange it into a shape, high in the centre
5. Garnish with hard eggs cut in slices
6. A little cucumber mixed with the salad is an improvement
7. For the salad mixture, take the yolks of three hard boiled eggs, with a spoonful of mustard, and a little salt
8. Mix these with a cup of cream, and four table-spoonsful of vinegar
9. Add the different ingredients carefully and work them together smoothly
10. The whites of the eggs may be trimmed and placed in small heaps round the dish as a garnish
