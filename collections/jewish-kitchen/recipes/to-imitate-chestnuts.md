## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of the best lump sugar
- 1 pint of water
- yolks of 6 very fresh eggs
- a glass of maraschino
- fine powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 lb. of the best lump sugar
2. Boil it in 1 pint of water till it comes quite thick
3. Test by dipping a little from a spoon on a plate to see if it sticks like hardbake
4. Remove it from the fire and work it up with a spoon like you would a preserve
5. Beat the yolks of 6 very fresh eggs well
6. Add to them a glass of maraschino
7. Work them well with the sugar till you produce a stiff paste
8. Turn it on a piece of marble with some fine powdered sugar
9. Roll it up into small balls the size of a chestnut, imitating the shape and making the dents like you see in a peeled chestnut
10. Take a piece of iron wire and make it red hot
11. Draw it across several parts of the chestnut to imitate the roasted nut
12. Put these sweetmeats in glass dishes
