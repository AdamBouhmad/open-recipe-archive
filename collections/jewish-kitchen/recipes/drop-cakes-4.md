## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of currants
- a couple of eggs
- orange flower-water
- white wine

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with the same quantity of butter, sugar, and currants
2. Make these into a paste with a couple of eggs
3. Add a little orange flower-water and a little white wine
4. If the paste is likely to be too thin when two eggs are used, omit the white of one
5. Drop the mixture when ready on a tin plate
6. Bake
