## Ingredients

- carrot
- steak
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- salad oil

## Directions

1. Boil a carrot and cut it in the shape of dice
2. Put your dish to get hot
3. Take a tooth of garlic and rub it over the hot dish to give it a flavour
4. Lay the cut carrot in the dish
5. Sprinkle over it a little salt and pepper
6. Pour over it a teaspoonful of salad oil
7. Take your steak off the gridiron and place it on top
8. Season it with salt and pepper
9. Serve it up directly
