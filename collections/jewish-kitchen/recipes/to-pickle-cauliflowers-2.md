## Ingredients

- cauliflowers
- brine
- spiced vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks and leaves, break the flower into pieces
2. Parboil them in brine
3. Drain them, and lay them in a jar
4. Pour over boiling spiced vinegar
