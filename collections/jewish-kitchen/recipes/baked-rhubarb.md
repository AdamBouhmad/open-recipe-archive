## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into two-inch lengths three bunches of rhubarb
2. Dredge with flour
3. Put in baking dish with one cup of sugar sprinkled over
4. Bake in moderate oven three-quarters of an hour
5. Very nice served hot as a vegetable, or cold as a sauce
