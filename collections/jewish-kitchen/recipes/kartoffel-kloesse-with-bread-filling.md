## Ingredients

- about eight potatoes
- two eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- one wine-glass farina
- one tablespoon chicken fat
- one scant cup flour
- bread
- chicken fat
- onions
- salted boiling water

## Directions

1. Make dumplings of the same mixture as regular Kartoffel Kloesse
2. In the centre of each dumpling place stripes of bread one inch long and one-fourth inch thick which have been fried in chicken fat and onions
3. Flour your hands well and make into dumplings
4. Put into boiling salted water, boil about twenty-five minutes
5. Serve at once with chopped onions browned, or browned bread crumbs and chicken fat
