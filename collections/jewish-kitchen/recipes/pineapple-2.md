## Ingredients

- pineapple
- half a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit
- water to cover

## Directions

1. Take off rind and trim.
2. Cut into slices and divide into thirds.
3. Fill into glass jars and dissolve sugar in water enough to cover the jars to overflowing.
4. Pour this sweetened water over the pineapples; proceed as in canning fruit in a water bath.
5. Let them boil steadily for at least twenty minutes.
6. Draw the boiler aside or lift it off the coal range and allow the cans to cool in the water in which they were boiled.
7. Remove each can carefully, screwing each can as tightly as possible.
8. Wipe dry and put away in a cool place.
