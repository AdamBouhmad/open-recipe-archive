## Ingredients

- brains
- salt water
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- cup of stock
- marjoram
- yolks of two eggs
- juice of a lemon
- teaspoon of flour
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Wash brains well, skin
2. boil fifteen minutes in salt water
3. slice in stew-pan some onions, salt, pepper, ginger and a cup of stock
4. Put in the brains with a little marjoram
5. let it cook gently for one-half hour
6. Mix yolks of two eggs, juice of a lemon, a teaspoon of flour, a little chopped parsley
7. when it is rubbed smooth, stir it into saucepan
8. stir well to prevent curdling
