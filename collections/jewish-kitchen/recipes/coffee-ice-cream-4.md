## Ingredients

- 4 oz. of fresh roasted Mocha coffee
- 1 pint of cream
- 4 oz. of sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Grind 4 oz. of fresh roasted Mocha coffee
2. Put it into a pint of cream with 4 oz. of sifted sugar
3. Let it simmer over the fire
4. Let it stand for 1/2 an hour
5. When cool put it in the freezer
6. Work it the same
