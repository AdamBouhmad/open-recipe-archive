## Ingredients

- plums
- four pounds of white sugar
- two ounces of stick cinnamon
- one ounce of cloves
- one pint of best pickling vinegar

## Directions

1. Prick the plums with a large needle then weigh them
2. To every seven pounds of fruit use four pounds of white sugar, two ounces of stick cinnamon, one ounce of cloves and a pint of best pickling vinegar
3. Boil the vinegar, sugar and spices and pour boiling hot over the fruit, which must be packed in a large jar
4. Repeat this three times
5. While the vinegar boils the third time, pack the plums in glass jars and pour
