## Ingredients

- prunes
- water
- granulated sugar
- claret or sauterne
- lemon or orange

## Directions

1. Cleanse thoroughly
2. Soak in water ten or twelve hours, adding a little granulated sugar when putting to soak
3. After soaking, the fruit will assume its full size
4. Simmer on the back of the stove
5. Do not boil prunes
6. Simmer, simmer only
7. Keep lid on
8. Shake gently
9. Do not stir
10. Never let boil
11. When tender they are ready for table
12. Serve cold
13. A little cream will make them more delicious
14. A little claret or sauterne poured over the prunes just as cooking is finished adds a flavor relished by many
15. Added just before simmering, a little sliced lemon or orange gives a rich color and flavor to the syrup
