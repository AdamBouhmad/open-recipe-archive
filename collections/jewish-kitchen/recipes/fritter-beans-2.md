## Ingredients

- one egg
- three-fourths teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of flour
- two tablespoons of water
- deep hot fat
- hot broth

## Directions

1. Beat one egg until light
2. Add three-fourths teaspoon of salt, one-half cup of flour and two tablespoons of water
3. Put through colander into deep hot fat and fry until brown
4. Drain and pour hot broth over them
