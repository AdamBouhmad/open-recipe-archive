## Ingredients

- nine good-sized mushrooms
- two tablespoons of butter
- two finely chopped onions
- salt
- pepper
- paprika
- six whole eggs
- hot buttered toast

## Directions

1. Peel nine good-sized mushrooms without using the stems
2. Chop very fine
3. Fry two tablespoons of butter and two finely chopped onions without browning
4. Add the mushrooms
5. Steam them by covering the pan after seasoning with salt, pepper and paprika
6. Before serving, beat six whole eggs and scramble with the mushrooms
7. Serve on hot buttered toast
