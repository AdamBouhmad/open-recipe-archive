## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- one-half cup of sugar
- two whole eggs
- one-half cup of milk
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- flour
- one-half teaspoon cinnamon
- Crisco

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Beat in one at a time two whole eggs
3. Mix well, then add milk, baking-powder, and sufficient flour to make a soft batter to roll out
4. Add one-half teaspoon cinnamon last
5. Roll one-half inch thick
6. Cut in strips one inch wide and three inches long
7. Fry in hot Crisco
