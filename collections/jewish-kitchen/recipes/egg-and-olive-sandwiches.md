## Ingredients

- four eggs
- two tablespoons of chopped olives
- salt
- olive oil
- vinegar
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Chop four eggs which have been boiled fifteen minutes.
2. Add two tablespoons of chopped olives, season and moisten with olive oil and vinegar.
3. Spread between thin slices of buttered bread.
