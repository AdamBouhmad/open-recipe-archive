## Ingredients

- six cold boiled potatoes
- one tablespoon butter
- one onion
- one-half teaspoon salt
- two saltspoons white pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Finely hash up six cold boiled potatoes and keep on a plate
2. Heat one tablespoon of butter in a frying-pan
3. Add a finely chopped onion and lightly brown for three minutes
4. Add the potatoes
5. Season with one-half teaspoon of salt and two saltspoons of white pepper, evenly sprinkled over
6. Nicely brown them for ten minutes, occasionally tossing them meanwhile
7. Give them a nice omelet form
8. Brown for eight minutes more
9. Turn on a hot dish
10. Sprinkle a little freshly chopped parsley over and serve
