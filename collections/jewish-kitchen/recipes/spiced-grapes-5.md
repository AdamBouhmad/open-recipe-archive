## Ingredients

- seven pounds of Concord grapes
- four and one-half pounds of granulated sugar
- one pint of cider vinegar
- two tablespoons of ground cinnamon
- two tablespoons of ground cloves

## Directions

1. Pulp seven pounds of Concord grapes
2. Cook the pulp and skins until soft
3. Put them through a fine sieve
4. Add four and one-half pounds of granulated sugar, one pint of cider vinegar, two tablespoons of ground cinnamon, and two tablespoons of ground cloves
5. Bring to a boil then cook slowly for one and one-half hours
6. Put in an earthen crock when cool
