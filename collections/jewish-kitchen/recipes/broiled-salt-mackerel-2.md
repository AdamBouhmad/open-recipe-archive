## Ingredients

- salt fish
- cold water
- butter
- pepper
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Freshen the fish by soaking it over night in cold water, with the skin uppermost.
2. Drain and wipe dry, remove the head and tail.
3. Place it upon a butter broiler, and slowly broil to a light brown.
4. Place upon a hot dish, add pepper, bits of butter, a sprinkling of parsley and a little lemon juice.
