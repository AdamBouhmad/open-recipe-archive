## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- water
- 1/2 an oz. of aniseed

## Directions

1. Put them in a small saucepan covered with water
2. Tie in a piece of muslin 1/2 an oz. of aniseed
3. Boil with the chestnuts for 15 minutes
4. Hang up the aniseed bag for use several times
