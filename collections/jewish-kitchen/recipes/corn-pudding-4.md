## Ingredients

- six ears of green corn
- two cups of milk
- one-half cup of butter
- three eggs
- a little salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape with a knife six ears of green corn, cutting each row through the middle
2. Add two cups of milk, one-half cup of butter, three eggs with the whites and yolks beaten separately, a little salt and white pepper
3. Stir the yolks into the milk and corn
4. Pour into a baking dish, stir in the whites and bake one and one-half hours
