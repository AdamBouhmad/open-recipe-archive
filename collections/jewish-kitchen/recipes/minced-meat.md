## Ingredients

- one pound of tender roasted meat
- two pounds of shred suet
- three pounds of currants
- six chopped apples
- a quarter of a loaf grated
- nutmegs
- cloves
- pepper
- salt
- one pound of sugar
- grated lemon and orange peel
- lemon juice
- two wine glasses of brandy
- two wine glasses of white wine
- two ounces of citron
- two ounces of candied lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take one pound of tender roasted meat, two pounds of shred suet, three pounds of currants, six chopped apples, a quarter of a loaf grated, nutmegs, cloves, pepper, salt, one pound of sugar, grated lemon and orange peel, lemon juice, and two wine glasses of brandy, the same of white wine, and two ounces of citron, and the same of candied lemon peel
2. mix all well together
3. the ingredients ought to be added separately
4. Minced meat should be kept a day or two before using
