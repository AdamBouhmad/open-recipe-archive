## Ingredients

- one quart of chestnuts
- boiling water
- cold water
- one cup of raisins
- two bay leaves
- stick cinnamon
- salt
- one teaspoon of butter
- two tablespoons of white wine
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of vinegar
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Remove the outer shells from one quart of chestnuts
2. Pour boiling water over them and remove the skins
3. Put in cold water for half an hour
4. Drain and put on in a boiler with cold water and boil until tender
5. Do not add any salt as it toughens them
6. In another boiler put one cup of raisins which have been stemmed and cleaned
7. Cover with cold water
8. Add two bay leaves and some stick cinnamon
9. Boil until tender
10. Pour them into the boiler containing the chestnuts
11. Add a pinch of salt and one teaspoon of butter
12. Continue until chestnuts are done
13. Add two tablespoons of white wine, two tablespoons of sugar, one-half teaspoon of vinegar
14. Thicken with one tablespoon of flour dissolved in water
15. More sugar or vinegar may be added to suit taste
16. Boil a few minutes, then serve
