## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- bread stuffing
- water

## Directions

1. Have the butcher carefully remove the blade from the shoulder and fill the space with a bread stuffing.
2. Sew up the opening.
3. Roast in the oven with a very little water in the pan, and baste frequently.
4. Serve with the gravy from the pan after the grease has been carefully removed.
