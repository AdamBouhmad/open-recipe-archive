## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Boil 4 or 5 potatoes cut in pieces, put on with a very little water, a little salt and pepper.
2. Let them boil, when done mash them well with a fork.
3. Put in 2 oz. of butter, 1 egg, mash it quite smooth.
