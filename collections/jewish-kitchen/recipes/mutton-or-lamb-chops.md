## Ingredients

- mutton or lamb chops
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- ginger

## Directions

1. Trim off some of the fat and heat in the spider.
2. Season the chops with salt and pepper, or salt and ginger.
3. Have the spider very hot with very little fat in it.
4. To be nice and tender they must be sauted quickly to a nice brown.
5. Or the chops may be broiled over the hot coals or in gas broiler, eight or ten minutes is all the time required.
6. Serve at once.
