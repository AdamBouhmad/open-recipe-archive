## Ingredients

- five pounds of red raspberries (not too ripe)
- five pounds of loaf sugar
- one quart of currant juice

## Directions

1. Mash the whole well in a preserving kettle using a potato masher
2. Add one quart of currant juice, and boil slowly until it jellies
3. Try a little on a plate; set it on ice
4. If it jellies remove from the fire, fill in small jars, cover with brandied paper and tie a thick white paper over them
5. Keep in a dark, dry, cool place
6. If you object to seeds, press the fruit through a sieve before boiling
