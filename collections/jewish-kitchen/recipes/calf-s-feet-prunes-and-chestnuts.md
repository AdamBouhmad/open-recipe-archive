## Ingredients

- Two calf's feet
- one-half pound Italian chestnuts
- one-eighth pound of raisins
- one pound of fine prunes
- one small onion
- one small head of celery root
- two olives
- one-eighth teaspoon of paprika
- one cup of soup stock
- a wine glass claret
- a wine glass sherry
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Season calf's feet with pepper and salt a day before using
2. Place in an iron pot one-half pound Italian chestnuts that have been scalded and skinned, then the calf's feet, one-eighth pound of raisins, one pound of fine prunes, one small onion, one small head of celery root, two olives cut in small pieces, one-eighth teaspoon of paprika, one cup of soup stock
3. Stew slowly for five hours
4. One hour before serving, while boiling, add a wine glass claret and a wine glass sherry
5. Do not stir
