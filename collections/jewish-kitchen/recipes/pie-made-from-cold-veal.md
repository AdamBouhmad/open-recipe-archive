## Ingredients

- cold veal
- fat of smoked beef
- onion
- parsley
- rind of lemon
- grated nutmeg
- forced-meat balls
- cupful of gravy
- smoked beef
- hard-boiled egg
- pastry
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Cut cold veal in nice pieces and put it in a stewpan with some of the fat of smoked beef, an onion, and parsley chopped very fine, also a little of the rind of lemon, grated nutmeg
2. Make a few forced-meat balls
3. Let it all simmer for ½ an hour with a cupful of gravy
4. Turn it nicely into a pie dish, put the smoked beef at the bottom, the veal on top, the balls all round
5. Cut a hard-boiled egg in quarters, place it in the four sides of the dish
6. Make a nice crust according to the receipt you will find for pastry
7. Cover your dish with it, trim it all round nicely, shape it to rise in the centre
8. Cut a piece of pastry in 4 leaves, shape them on the dish
9. Wash it all over with the yolk of an egg well beat
