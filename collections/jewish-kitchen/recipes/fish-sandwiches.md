## Ingredients

- cold fish
- tartar sauce
- lettuce leaf
- hard-boiled egg
- buttered bread

## Directions

1. Spread one piece of bread with any kind of cold fish that has been shredded and mixed with tartar sauce.
2. Put a lettuce leaf on that and then a slice of hard-boiled egg that has been dipped in tartar sauce.
3. Cover with a slice of buttered bread.
