## Ingredients

- four herrings
- two apples
- one-half cup of coarsely chopped almonds
- one onion
- one-half cup of vinegar
- one teaspoon of sugar
- pinch of pepper
- sliced lemons

## Directions

1. Soak four herrings in cold water overnight, and then rinse several times in fresh cold water.
2. Skin, bone, and cut in one-half inch pieces.
3. Peel two apples, and cut in dice.
4. Mix with herring, then add one-half cup of coarsely chopped almonds and one onion chopped fine.
5. Remove the milsner or soft egg from the inside of herring, and mash perfectly smooth.
6. Add one-half cup of vinegar, one teaspoon of sugar, pinch of pepper.
7. Mix well, and then pour over herring, stirring with a fork to prevent mashing.
8. Set in ice-box until ready to serve.
9. Put sliced lemons on top.
