## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh beef heart fat
- one quart of flour
- two tumblers of ice-cold water
- one tablespoon of brown sugar
- saltspoon of salt
- tart apples
- raisins
- grated lemon peel
- citron
- pounded almonds
- melted drippings
- sugar
- brown sugar
- white sugar
- ground cinnamon
- wine
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Take one pound of fresh beef heart fat and shave it as fine as possible with a knife
2. Sift one quart of flour into a deep bowl
3. Add two tumblers of ice-cold water, one tablespoon of brown sugar, a saltspoon of salt, then add the shaved heart fat and work well into the sifted flour
4. Put it on a pie-board and work as you would bread dough with the palm of your hand until it looks smooth enough to roll
5. Do not work over five minutes
6. Take half of this dough, flour your pie-board slightly and roll out as you would pie dough about once as thick
7. Grease a deep pudding-dish (an iron one is best), one that is smaller at the bottom than the top, grease it well
8. Line the pudding-dish bottom and sides clear to the top
9. Fill this one-third full with chopped tart apples, raisins, part of a grated lemon peel, citron cut quite fine, pounded almonds and melted drippings here and there
10. Sprinkle thickly with sugar, half brown and half white, and a little ground cinnamon
11. Moisten each layer with one-half wine-glass of wine
12. Put another layer of dough, rolling out half of the remaining dough and reserving the other half for the top covering
13. Fill again with apples, raisins, etc. until full
14. Put on top layer and press the dough firmly together all round the edge using a beaten egg to make sure of its sticking
