## Ingredients

- biscuit powder
- beaten eggs
- sugar
- pounded cinnamon
- sifted sugar
- finely chopped almonds
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Make a thin batter of biscuit powder and beaten eggs
2. Drop it into a soufle pan, fry lightly, and strew over pounded cinnamon, sifted sugar, and finely chopped almonds
3. Hold over a salamander to brown the upper side
4. Slide the fritter on to a hot dish, and fold
5. Pour over, when in the dish, clarified sugar
