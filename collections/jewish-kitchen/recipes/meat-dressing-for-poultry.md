## Ingredients

- sausage meat
- fat
- veal
- beef
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- thyme
- celery root
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- stale bread
- egg

## Directions

1. Have sausage meat chopped, adding a little fat
2. Mix in some veal with the beef while chopping
3. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg or thyme
4. Grate in a piece of celery root and a piece of garlic about the size of a bean
5. Add a small onion, a minced tomato, a quarter of a loaf of stale bread, also grated
6. Mix up the whole with one egg
7. If you prefer, you may soak the bread, press out every drop of water and dry in a heated skillet with fat
