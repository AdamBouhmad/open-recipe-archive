## Ingredients

- dough from above recipe
- a tablespoon of butter
- prune sauce

## Directions

1. Make the dough just as you would in the above recipe, adding a tablespoon of butter
2. After they have risen steam instead of baking them
3. Put on a kettle of boiling water, set a colander on top of the kettle and lay in your dumplings, but do not crowd them
4. Cover with a close-fitting lid and put a weight on top of it to keep in the steam
5. When done they will be as large again as when first put in
6. Take up one at first to try whether it is done by tearing open with two forks
7. If you have more than enough for your family, bake a pan of biscuits out of the remaining dough
8. Serve dumplings hot with prune sauce
