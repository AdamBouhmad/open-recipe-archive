## Ingredients

- strained juice of two lemons
- grated peel of one lemon
- one cup of sugar
- one glass of white wine or water
- yolks of three eggs
- whites of three eggs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the strained juice of two lemons and the grated peel of one with a cup of sugar and one glass of white wine or water
2. When boiled to a syrup add the yolks of three eggs well beaten
3. also half of the whites beaten to a froth
4. Use the other half of the stiffly-beaten whites, sweetened with powdered sugar, to decorate the sauce
5. Serve immediately
