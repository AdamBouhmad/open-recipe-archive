## Ingredients

- 4 floury potatoes
- a little salt
- 4 eggs
- a cup of almond milk (optional)
- boiling oil
- cinnamon
- sugar
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 4 floury potatoes
2. Mash them with a little salt until smooth, like a cream
3. Beat up 4 eggs with or without a cup of almond milk
4. Mix it well with the mashed potatoes like a butter
5. Have a pan with boiling oil
6. Put them in by a spoonful for each fritter
7. Let them drain from the oil
8. Arrange them in a glass dish
9. Put over some cinnamon and sugar
10. Pour over some clarified sugar made like for German puffs
11. Turn them over so that both sides are soaked alike
