## Ingredients

- one pint of cranberries
- one and one-quarter cups of water
- three-fourths of a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Put the cranberries on with the water and cook until soft
2. Strain through a cloth
3. Weigh and add three-fourths of a pound of sugar to every pint of juice
4. Cook ten minutes
5. Pour into molds and set aside to cool
