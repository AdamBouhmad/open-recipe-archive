## Ingredients

- peaches
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- cloves
- hot butter sauce or cream

## Directions

1. Pare a number of peaches and put them whole into a baking-tin, together with layers of bread crumbs and sugar and add a few cloves
2. Bake until the top is brown
3. Serve with hot butter sauce or cream
