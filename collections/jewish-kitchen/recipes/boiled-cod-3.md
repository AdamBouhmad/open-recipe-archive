## Ingredients

- cod
- cold water
- salt
- parsley
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Put the cod in a fish kettle with cold water and a handful of salt
2. Let it boil fast at first, then draw it to the side
3. The time must be according to the size of the fish
4. When done lift the steamer across the kettle with a cloth to keep it warm
5. Lay it on a fish napkin garnished with parsley
6. Serve with caper sauce or sauce No. 33
