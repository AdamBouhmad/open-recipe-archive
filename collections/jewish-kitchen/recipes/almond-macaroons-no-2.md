## Ingredients

- one-half pound of almonds
- whites of five eggs
- three-quarters of a pound of pulverized sugar
- confectioner's sugar
- a tablespoon of rosewater or a teaspoon of essence of bitter almonds
- butter or waxed paper
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the almonds by blanching them in boiling water
2. Strip them of the skins and lay them on a clean towel to dry
3. Grate or pound one-half pound of almonds
4. Beat the whites of five eggs to a stiff, very stiff froth
5. Stir in gradually three-quarters of a pound of pulverized sugar (use confectioner's sugar if you can get it)
6. Add the pounded almonds
7. Add a tablespoon of rosewater or a teaspoon of essence of bitter almonds
8. Line a broad baking-pan with buttered or waxed paper
9. Drop upon this half a teaspoon of the mixture at a time, allowing room enough to prevent their running together
10. Sift powdered sugar over them
11. Bake in a quick oven to a delicate brown
12. Try one on a piece of paper before you venture to bake them all
13. If it runs add a little more sugar
