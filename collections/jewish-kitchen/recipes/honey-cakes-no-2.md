## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of honey
- one cup of blanched almonds chopped finely
- one teaspoon each of allspice, cloves, and cinnamon
- one cup of chocolate
- flour enough to make a thick batter
- one teaspoon of baking-soda

## Directions

1. Spread very thin on square, buttered pans.
2. Bake in a hot oven.
3. When done, spread with a white icing, cut into squares, and put a half blanched almond in the centre of each square.
