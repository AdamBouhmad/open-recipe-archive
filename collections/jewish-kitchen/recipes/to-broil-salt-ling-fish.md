## Ingredients

- salt ling fish
- butter
- made mustard
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Buy 2 lb. of the thick part
2. lay it in soak for 2 days
3. take it out
4. dry it nicely on a clean cloth
5. take the black skin off before you broil it
6. put it on the gridiron
7. let it broil each side a bright gold colour
8. have in a teacup 2 oz. of butter, a teaspoonful of made mustard, some pepper, and the juice of a lemon
9. pour it over directly it is done
