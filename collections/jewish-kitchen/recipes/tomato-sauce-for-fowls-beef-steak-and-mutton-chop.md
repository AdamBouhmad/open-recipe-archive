## Ingredients

- several large tomatoes
- a tooth of garlic
- a tablespoonful of good oil
- salt
- pepper
- a dash of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut several large tomatoes into a saucepan and let them simmer gently at the side of your fire
2. When quite soft rub them through a sieve into a basin
3. Chop a tooth of garlic and fry it in a tablespoonful of good oil
4. When fried take out the garlic
5. Stir in the pulped tomatoes with a little salt, pepper, and a dash of vinegar to taste
