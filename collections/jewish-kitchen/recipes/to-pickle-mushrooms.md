## Ingredients

- small button mushrooms
- salt
- distilled vinegar
- mace

## Directions

1. Choose small button mushrooms, clean and wipe them
2. Throw them into cold water
3. Put into a stewpan with a little salt
4. Cover them with distilled vinegar
5. Simmer a few minutes
6. Put them in bottles with a couple of blades or so of mace
7. When cold, cork them closely
