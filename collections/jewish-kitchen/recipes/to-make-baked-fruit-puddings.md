## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stew your fruit and let it get cold
2. Make a paste like the former, roll it out the three times
3. Grease the basin and sugar it
4. Roll out the paste in one round, line the basin with it, cut it off all round
5. Those pieces are to form the cover
6. Lay in your fruit, roll out what you cut off, cover it over, press it down
7. Bake it, turn it out like the last
8. These puddings can be filled with gooseberries, plums, damsons, cherries, currants and raspberries, in fact, any fruit in season
9. Always stew your fruit with sufficient sugar to sweeten
