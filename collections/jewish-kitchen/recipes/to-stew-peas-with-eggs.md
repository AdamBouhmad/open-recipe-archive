## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 2 spoonfuls of oil or 1/2 lb. of butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1 peck of peas
- 1 teacup of boiling water
- 3 or 4 lumps of sugar
- fresh mint
- 5 or 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Fry an onion sliced in 2 spoonfuls of oil, or 1/2 lb. of butter; salt and pepper.
2. Pick carefully 1 peck of peas, wash and drain them.
3. Put them in the saucepan with the oil and onion.
4. Add a teacup of boiling water, 3 or 4 lumps of sugar, chop a little fresh mint.
5. Let it all cook till the peas are done.
6. Break 5 or 6 eggs, each one separately in a cup, put them on the peas.
7. Let them cook till they are done, dish up carefully.
