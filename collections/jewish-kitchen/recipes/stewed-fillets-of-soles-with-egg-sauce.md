## Ingredients

- fillets of soles
- onions
- 2 spoonfuls of oil
- a cup of boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 1 spoonful of flour
- 2 eggs
- juice of a large or 2 small lemons
- a little dried saffron
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the fillets as for frying
2. Slice in a stewpan some onions with 2 spoonfuls of oil, let it fry
3. Then add a cup of boiling water
4. Lay in the fillets with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
5. Let them cook for an hour
6. Rub in a basin a spoonful of flour, 2 eggs, the juice of a large or 2 small lemons, a little dried saffron to colour
7. Rub it quite smooth
8. Turn it into the saucepan
9. Dish the fillets up and lay the sauce over them
10. Garnish with a little parsley
