## Ingredients

- 2 lb. of loaf sugar
- yolks of 20 eggs

## Directions

1. Clarify 2 lb. of loaf sugar to a thick syrup
2. Take the yolks of 20 eggs and mix them up quickly
3. Pass them through a sieve
4. Have ready a funnel, the hole must be the size of a vermicelli
5. Hold the funnel over syrup while it boils over a charcoal fire
6. Pass the egg through stirring the sugar at the same time
7. Take care to hold the funnel
