## Ingredients

- 1 lb. ground almonds
- 6 whites of eggs
- 1/2 lb. castor sugar
- nutmeg
- allspice
- sugar for rolling

## Directions

1. Pound the almonds in a marble mortar with the whites of eggs
2. Add the sugar
3. Take it out of the mortar and put it in a basin
4. Work in some nutmeg and allspice to colour like the real walnut
5. Roll up and break it in pieces the size of a walnut
6. Shape it
7. Roll up the walnuts in a little sugar to prevent their sticking
8. Bake them on plates in a slow oven; they are soon done
