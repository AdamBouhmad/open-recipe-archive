## Ingredients

- one cake Neufchatel cheese
- a piece of butter
- one tablespoon of cream
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- six dashes of Tabasco Sauce
- chopped pecan nuts

## Directions

1. Mix one cake Neufchatel cheese, a piece of butter the size of the cheese, one tablespoon of cream, one-quarter teaspoon of salt and six dashes of Tabasco Sauce.
2. Form one large ball or several small ones and roll in chopped pecan nuts.
