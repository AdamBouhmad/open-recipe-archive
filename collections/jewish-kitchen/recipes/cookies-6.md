## Ingredients

- one-half cup of matzoth meal
- one-fourth cup of potato flour
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-fourth cup of chopped almonds
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Sift together one-half cup of matzoth meal and one-fourth cup of potato flour
2. Add one-half cup of sugar, one-fourth cup of chopped almonds and two eggs
3. Roll out in potato flour mixed with sugar
4. Cut and bake on greased tins in a hot oven
