## Ingredients

- pike
- mashed potatoes
- butter
- eggs
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- lemon rind
- onion
- oil

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing of mashed potatoes with butter and 2 eggs, chopped parsley, marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, a little rind of lemon
2. Put this into the fish and sew it up
3. Chop an onion fine at the bottom of a round baking dish (or your fish tin) with salt, pepper, butter or oil
4. Lay the fish on it and squeeze a lemon over it
5. Put a few lumps of butter on top of it
6. Turn the fish round, by putting the tail in the mouth, fastened with a skewer
