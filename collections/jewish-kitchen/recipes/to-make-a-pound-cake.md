## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of dry sifted flour
- 1 lb. of castor sugar
- 2 oz. of candied peel
- 1 oz. of citron
- 1 lb. of currants well washed and dried
- a little grated nutmeg
- a small piece of salt
- the rind of a lemon grated
- 8 eggs

## Directions

1. beat up 8 eggs well
2. work the butter and sugar well together
3. add the currants, peel, and all the other ingredients
4. add the eggs
5. put the flour in lightly
6. beat it well for half an hour
7. grease your cake tin, butter some paper
8. lay a round at the bottom, and strips round
9. be sure the oven is well heated before you put it in, or the ingredients will sink to the bottom
10. It will take nearly 2 hours baking
