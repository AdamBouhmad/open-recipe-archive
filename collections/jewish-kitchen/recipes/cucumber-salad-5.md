## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- onion
- salt
- white pepper
- parsley
- vinegar
- white or red radishes

## Directions

1. Pare thickly, from end to end, and lay in ice-water one hour
2. Wipe them, slice thin, and slice an onion equally thin
3. Strew salt over them, shake up a few times, cover and let remain in this brine for another hour
4. Squeeze or press out every drop of water which has been extracted from the cucumbers
5. Put into a salad bowl, sprinkle with white pepper and scatter bits of parsley over them
6. Add enough vinegar to cover
7. You may slice up an equal quantity of white or red radishes and mix with this salad
