## Ingredients

- whites of two eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half cup of shredded cocoanut
- two cups of corn flakes
- a pinch of salt
- one-half teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix the whites of two eggs, beaten stiff, with one-half cup of sugar
2. Add one-half cup of shredded cocoanut
3. Fold in two cups of corn flakes, a pinch of salt, one-half teaspoon of vanilla
4. Make and bake same as kisses
