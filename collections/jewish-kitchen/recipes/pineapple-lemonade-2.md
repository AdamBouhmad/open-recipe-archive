## Ingredients

- a ripe pineapple
- juice of four lemons
- two cups of sugar
- same quantity of water
- a quart of water
- a cherry in each glass
- a few strawberries or raspberries

## Directions

1. Pare and grate a ripe pineapple.
2. Add the juice of four lemons and a syrup made by boiling together for a few minutes two cups of sugar and the same quantity of water.
3. Mix and add a quart of water.
4. When quite cold strain and ice.
5. A cherry in each glass is an agreeable addition, as are a few strawberries or raspberries.
