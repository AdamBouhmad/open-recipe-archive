## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 chops
- carrot
- turnip
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- 2 or 3 lumps of sugar
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Put 5 or 6 chops cut short in a baking tin in the oven
2. When they get a little brown take them out
3. Have in a stewpan cooking one large carrot and 1 turnip cut in strips about 2 inches long and 1 wide, with a chopped onion, pepper, salt, a little grated nutmeg
4. When this is nearly done put in the chops with 2 or 3 lumps of sugar
5. Let it all stew together
6. Skim off the fat
7. Rub a spoonful of flour with a little cold water and add to the pan
8. Dish up very hot
