## Ingredients

- six good-sized apples
- six yolks of eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half pound of grated almonds or one-half cup of matzoth-meal
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take six good-sized apples, six yolks of eggs, one-half cup of sugar (or to taste), one-half pound of grated almonds, or one-half cup of matzoth-meal, one-half teaspoon of salt, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
2. Pare the apples and leave them whole
3. Grate all the apple from the pulp
4. Add the remaining ingredients to the grated apple
