## Ingredients

- 1 pint of almond milk or plain milk
- 2 oz. of powdered sugar
- 2 laurel leaves or a little piece of vanille or orange-flower water
- 4 eggs
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pint of almond milk or plain milk with 2 oz. of powdered sugar, 2 laurel leaves or a little piece of vanille or orange-flower water
2. beat in a basin 4 eggs
3. stir the boiling milk into the eggs
4. put it in a jug in a saucepan of boiling water
5. keep stirring until it thickens, do not let it boil
6. pour it into custard glasses or cups
7. put a little ground cinnamon on each
8. stand them in a dish for table
