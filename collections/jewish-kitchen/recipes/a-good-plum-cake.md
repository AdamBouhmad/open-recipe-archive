## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 8 eggs
- 1 1/2 lb. of flour
- nutmeg
- 1 lb. of currants
- 1/2 lb. of citron and orange peel

## Directions

1. Beat 1 lb. of butter to a cream with a spoon
2. Add one pound of brown sugar
3. Beat for 10 minutes
4. Add 8 eggs one at the time until it is smooth
5. Stir in 1 1/2 lb. of flour, put in the flour a little at the time till it is well mixed
6. Add a little nutmeg and 1 lb. of currants
7. Add 1/2 lb. of citron and orange peel
8. Bake it 2 hours in a moderate oven
