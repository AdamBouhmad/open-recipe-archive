## Ingredients

- prime rib roast
- small onion
- celery root
- carrot
- parsley
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut up a small onion, a celery root and part of a carrot into rather small pieces and add to these two or three sprigs of parsley and one bay leaf
2. Sprinkle these over the bottom of the dripping-pan and place your roast on this bed
3. Heat the oven very hot when the roast is first put in
4. When the roast is browned sufficiently to retain its juices, moderate the heat and roast more slowly until the meat is done
5. Do not season until the roast is browned, then add salt and pepper
6. Baste frequently and turn occasionally, being very careful not to stick a fork into the roast
