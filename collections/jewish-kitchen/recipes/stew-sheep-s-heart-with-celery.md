## Ingredients

- 2 sheep's hearts
- celery
- a chopped onion
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- meat balls or motsa balls
- lemon
- browning

## Directions

1. Wash the hearts well and see that they are free from all blood
2. Cut them in slices
3. Cut and wash celery
4. Put it in a stewpan with chopped onion, pepper, salt, and grated nutmeg
5. Add a few meat balls or small motsa balls
6. Flavour with lemon and colour with browning
