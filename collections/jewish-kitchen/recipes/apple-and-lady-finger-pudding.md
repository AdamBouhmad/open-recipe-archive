## Ingredients

- apples
- almonds
- citron
- raisins
- butter
- sugar
- wine
- four yolks of eggs
- one cup of sugar
- beaten whites of eggs
- eight lady fingers
- juice of one whole lemon

## Directions

1. Core and peel apples, take top off, chop the top with almonds, citron and raisins.
2. Butter your pan, fill apples, sugar them and pour over a little wine, bake until tender.
3. When cool add four yolks of eggs beaten with one cup of sugar, then last, add beaten whites and eight lady fingers rolled, and juice of one whole lemon.
4. Pour over apples, bake.
5. Eat cold.
