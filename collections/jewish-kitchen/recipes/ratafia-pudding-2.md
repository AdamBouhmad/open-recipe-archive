## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of ratafias
- 6 sponge cakes
- 3 oz. of citron
- grated rind of a lemon
- 1/4 lb. of castor sugar
- 1/2 pint of almond milk
- 6 eggs, leave out 3 whites
- orange marmalade
- cut almonds

## Directions

1. Soak the cakes in 1/2 pint of almond milk
2. Beat in 6 eggs, leave out 3 whites
3. Grease and sugar your mould
4. Lay over it some orange marmalade
5. Turn in the mixture
6. Boil for an hour the same as the other puddings
7. Serve a sauce round
8. You can sometimes ornament these puddings with cut almonds
