## Ingredients

- goose
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- lemon peel
- goose fat
- flour
- vinegar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut up, after being skinned
2. Stew, seasoning with salt, pepper, a few cloves and a very little lemon peel
3. When done heat a little goose fat in a frying-pan
4. Brown half a tablespoon of flour
5. Add a little vinegar and the juice of half a lemon
