## Ingredients

- one cup of confectioner's sugar
- milk or water
- vanilla
- orange juice and rind
- lemon juice
- chocolate melted over hot water

## Directions

1. To one cup of confectioner's sugar add some liquid, either milk or water, to make it the right consistency to spread
2. Flavor with vanilla
3. Instead of the water or milk, orange juice can be used with a little of the rind
4. Lemon juice can be substituted in place of vanilla
5. Chocolate melted over hot water and added to the sugar and water makes a nice chocolate icing; flavor with vanilla
