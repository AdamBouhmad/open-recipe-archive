## Ingredients

- one cup of good red wine
- one-half cup of water
- sugar
- three whole cloves
- three small pieces of cinnamon bark
- yolk of one egg

## Directions

1. Put on to boil one cup of good red wine and one-half cup of water
2. sweeten to taste
3. add three whole cloves and three small pieces of cinnamon bark
4. let boil ten minutes
5. and pour while boiling over the well-beaten yolk of one egg
6. Eat hot or cold
