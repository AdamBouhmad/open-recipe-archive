## Ingredients

- eight ounces of walnuts
- eight ounces of blanched almonds
- yolks of twelve eggs
- three-fourths pound of sugar
- one-fourth pound of sifted flour
- whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Grate eight ounces of walnuts and eight ounces of blanched almonds
2. Beat light the yolks of twelve eggs and three-fourths pound of sugar
3. Add the grated nuts and one-fourth pound of sifted flour
4. Fold in the whites beaten to a stiff froth
5. Bake in layers and fill with sweetened whipped cream
