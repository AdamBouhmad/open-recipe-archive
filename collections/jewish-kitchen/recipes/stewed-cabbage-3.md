## Ingredients

- cabbage
- one tablespoon of drippings
- one-fourth of an onion
- one tablespoon of flour
- one-half pint of soup stock
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and drain cabbage, cut in small pieces and boil until tender.
2. Drain and rinse in cold water; chop fine.
3. Heat one tablespoon of drippings in spider, one-fourth of an onion cut fine and one tablespoon of flour; brown all together.
4. Add one-half pint of soup stock, add cabbage and cook ten minutes longer.
5. Salt and pepper to taste.
