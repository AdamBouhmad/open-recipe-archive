## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a calf's foot or a cow heel
- a thick slice of smoked beef
- a few dried herbs, marjoram, and lemon thyme
- a blade of mace
- salt
- pepper
- a little of the fresh rind of a lemon
- 2 quarts of water
- a tooth of garlic
- the white of an egg
- the shell
- the juice of 3 lemons
- a little saffron
- oil
- hard eggs
- slices of sausage
- sprigs of parsley
- a couple of salted sheep's tongues

## Directions

1. Put knuckle of veal, calf's foot or cow heel, smoked beef, dried herbs, mace, salt, pepper, lemon rind, water, and garlic into a saucepan
2. When it boils skim it well
3. Simmer for 6 or 7 hours
4. The next day skim off the fat
5. If not clear boil it for a few minutes with the white of an egg, beat to a snow and the shell
6. Pass it through a jelly bag with the juice of 3 lemons and a little saffron
7. Oil a mould and cut some hard eggs and slices of sausage, some sprigs of parsley
8. Pour in the mould some of the jelly, about a quarter full
9. Lay in a few pieces of the sausage, egg, parsley, and beetroot, tastefully arranged
10. Let that get cold
11. Put on the tongues gracefully with some more of the egg, sausage, parsley, and beetroot
12. Fill the mould up
13. Ensure the jelly is very stiff, if not in very cold weather set it on ice
14. When ready to serve pass a cloth dipped in boiling water round the mould
