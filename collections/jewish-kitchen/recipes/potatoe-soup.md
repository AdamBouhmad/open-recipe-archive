## Ingredients

- a pound of fine potatoes
- two quarts of water
- the trimmings of any meat, about a pound in quantity
- a cup of rice
- a few sweet herbs
- a head of celery
- a little thickening
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Grate a pound of fine potatoes in two quarts of water
2. Add to it the trimmings of any meat, amounting to about a pound in quantity
3. Add a cup of rice, a few sweet herbs, and a head of celery
4. Stew well till the liquor is considerably reduced
5. Strain it through a sieve
6. If when strained it is too thin and watery, add a little thickening
7. Flavour only with white pepper and salt
