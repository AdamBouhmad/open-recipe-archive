## Ingredients

- a fine thick piece of brisket of beef not fat
- salt
- sugar
- saltpetre
- water
- ground all-spice
- black pepper
- cloves
- ginger
- nutmegs
- brown sugar
- pounded dried sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Let the beef lay three days in a pickle made of eight ounces of salt, eight ounces of sugar, and half an ounce of saltpetre in two quarts of water
2. Take it out and rub in a mixture of spices consisting of equal quantities of ground all-spice, black pepper, cloves, ginger and nutmegs, and a little brown sugar
3. Repeat this daily for a week
4. Cover it with pounded dried sweet herbs, roll or tie it tightly
5. Put it into a pan with very little water, and bake slowly for eight hours
6. Take it out, untie it and put a heavy weight upon it
7. Serve eaten cold
