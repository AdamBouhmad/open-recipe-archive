## Ingredients

- one egg for each lily
- heart of bleached lettuce
- butter
- vinegar
- mustard
- salt
- paprika
- parsley
- stuffed olives
- cut-glass dishes

## Directions

1. Boil twenty minutes, one egg for each lily
2. Remove shell and while still warm cut with silver knife in strips from small end nearly to base
3. Very carefully lay back the petals on a heart of bleached lettuce
4. Remove yolks and rub them with spoon of butter, vinegar, a little mustard, salt and paprika
5. Form cone-shaped balls, and put on petals, sprinkling bits of parsley over balls
6. Two or three stuffed olives carry out the effect of buds
7. Serve on cut-glass dishes to give water effect
