## Ingredients

- bass
- one tablespoon of butter
- two cups of stale bread crumbs
- one tablespoon of chopped onion
- one teaspoon of chopped parsley
- two teaspoons of chopped capers
- one-fourth cup of sherry
- paprika
- salt
- sherry wine
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the scales and clean. Do not remove the head, tail, or fins.
2. Put into a double boiler one tablespoon of butter, two cups of stale bread crumbs, one tablespoon of chopped onion, one teaspoon of chopped parsley, two teaspoons of chopped capers, one-fourth cup of sherry.
3. Heat all the above ingredients, season with paprika and salt, and stuff the bass with the mixture.
4. Sew up the fish, put into a hot oven, bake and baste with sherry wine and butter.
