## Ingredients

- large sound tomatoes
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Cut large, sound tomatoes in halves and flour the insides thickly.
2. Season with a little salt and pepper.
3. Allow the butter to get very hot before putting in the tomatoes.
4. When brown on one side, turn, and when done serve with hot cream or thicken some milk and pour over the tomatoes hot.
