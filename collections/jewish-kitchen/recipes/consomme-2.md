## Ingredients

- three pounds of beef
- three quarts of cold water
- one-half cup carrots
- one-half cup celery
- one-half cup onion
- one-half teaspoon of peppercorns
- one tablespoon of salt
- one egg shell broken fine
- slightly beaten white of one egg

## Directions

1. Cut beef in dice and cover with three quarts of cold water
2. Simmer slowly for four hours
3. The last hour add one-half cup each of carrots, celery, onion, and season with one-half teaspoon of peppercorns and one tablespoon of salt
4. Strain, cool, remove fat and clear by allowing one egg shell broken fine and the slightly beaten white of one egg to each quart of stock
5. Add to the stock, stir constantly until it has reached the boiling point
6. Boil two minutes and serve
