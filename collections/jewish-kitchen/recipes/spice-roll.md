## Ingredients

- coffee cake dough
- melted butter
- leb kuchen or plain gingerbread
- one-half cup of almonds or nuts
- one cup of seeded raisins
- one cup of cleaned currants
- brown sugar
- syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Roll out coffee cake dough quite thin and let it rise half an hour
2. Brush with melted butter
3. Grate some leb kuchen or plain gingerbread; add one-half cup of almonds or nuts, one cup of seeded raisins and one cup of cleaned currants
4. Strew these all over the dough together with some brown sugar and a little syrup
5. Spice with cinnamon and roll
6. Spread with butter and let it rise for an hour
7. Bake brown
