## Ingredients

- macaroons
- lady fingers
- nuts
- crystallized cherries
- one cup of wine
- one cup of sugar
- one-half cup of water
- sweetened and flavored cream

## Directions

1. Fill a glass bowl with alternate layers of macaroons and lady fingers, sprinkle a layer of finely-chopped nuts over the cake, then a layer of crystallized cherries.
2. Boil one cup of wine, one cup of sugar and one-half cup of water together until syrupy and thick, pour it over the contents of the bowl.
3. Let this cool, then place a thick layer of thickly-whipped sweetened and flavored cream over all.
4. Serve very cold.
