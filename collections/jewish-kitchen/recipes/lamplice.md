## Ingredients

- half a pound of currants
- half a pound of raisins
- half a pound of sugar
- a little citron
- ground cloves
- ground cinnamon
- eight apples finely chopped
- a rich puff paste
- one ounce of fresh butter
- a tea-cup full of clarified sugar
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of currants, the same quantity of raisins and sugar, a little citron, ground cloves and cinnamon, with eight apples finely chopped
2. Mix all together
3. Cut a rich puff paste into small triangles
4. Fill them with the fruit like puffs and lay them in a deep dish
5. Place the pieces closely together
6. When the dish is full, pour over one ounce of fresh butter melted in a tea-cup full of clarified sugar, flavoured with essence of lemon
7. Bake in an oven not too brisk
