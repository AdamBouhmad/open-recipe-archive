## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- toasted bread crumbs
- one cup of boiling milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil and drain off the water
2. Grease a baking-dish, line with a layer of cauliflower, add a layer of toasted bread crumbs, another of cauliflower and so on alternately, letting the top layer be of bread crumbs
3. Over all pour one cup of boiling milk, dot the top with butter and bake in a moderate oven for twenty minutes
