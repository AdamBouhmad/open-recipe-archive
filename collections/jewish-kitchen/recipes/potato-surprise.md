## Ingredients

- large potatoes
- cooked mutton
- pepper
- salt
- potato pulp
- gravy
- fat

## Directions

1. Take large potatoes, parboil without peeling.
2. Cut a small piece of one end of the potato and scoop out the inside.
3. Mince two ounces cooked mutton, season with pepper and salt, mix with the potato pulp and a little gravy.
4. Return end of potato to its place and bake for about twenty minutes with a little fat on top of each potato.
