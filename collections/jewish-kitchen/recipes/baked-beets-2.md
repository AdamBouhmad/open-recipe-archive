## Ingredients

- large beetroot
- drippings
- pepper
- salt
- stock

## Directions

1. Boil large beetroot about two hours, being careful not to pierce it
2. When cold mash very smooth, add a little drippings, pepper, salt and stock
3. Place in a greased pan and bake one hour
