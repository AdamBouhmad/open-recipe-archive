## Ingredients

- three quarts of liquor in which fowls have been boiled
- three onions
- two carrots
- one head of celery
- one tablespoon of curry powder
- one tablespoon of flour
- chicken or veal

## Directions

1. Add to three quarts of liquor, in which fowls have been boiled, the following vegetables: three onions, two carrots, and one head of celery cut in small dice
2. Keep the kettle over a high heat until soup reaches the boiling point
3. Then place where it will simmer for twenty-five minutes
4. Add one tablespoon of curry powder, one tablespoon of flour mixed together; add to the hot soup and cook five minutes
5. Pass through a sieve
6. Serve with small pieces of chicken or veal cut in it
