## Ingredients

- brisket of beef weighing about two or three pounds
- two quarts water
- salt
- soup greens
- one cup soup stock
- sauerkraut
- one tablespoon caraway seed
- two raw potatoes
- one tablespoon brown sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take brisket of beef weighing about two or three pounds. Set it on to boil in two quarts of water, a little salt and the usual soup greens.
2. When the meat is tender take it out, salt it well and put on to boil again in a porcelain-lined kettle, having previously removed all the bones.
3. Add about a cup of the soup stock and as much sauerkraut as you desire.
4. Boil about one hour; tie one tablespoon of caraway seed in a bag and boil in with the kraut.
5. Thicken with two raw potatoes, grated, and add one tablespoon of brown sugar just before serving.
6. If not sour enough add a dash of vinegar.
