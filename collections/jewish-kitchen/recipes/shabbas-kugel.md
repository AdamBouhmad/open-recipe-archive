## Ingredients

- five wheat rolls
- water
- one cup of drippings or one-half pound of suet chopped very fine
- pinch of salt
- two eggs well beaten
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- one grated lemon rind
- one-half cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of water
- warm melted fat

## Directions

1. Soak five wheat rolls in water
2. Press the bread quite dry
3. Add one cup of drippings or one-half pound of suet chopped very fine, a pinch of salt, two eggs well beaten, one teaspoon of cinnamon, one grated lemon rind, one-half cup of sugar, one tablespoon of water
4. Stir all together thoroughly
5. Grease the kugel pot well with warm melted fat
6. Pour in the mixture and send it Friday afternoon to the bakery where it will remain till Saturday noon and it will then be baked brown
7. Alternatively, if one has a coal range that will retain the heat for the length of time required, it will be baked nicely
8. The kugel must be warm when served
