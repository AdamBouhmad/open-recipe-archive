## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 1 onion
- a tooth of garlic
- some marjoram
- lemon thyme
- a little walnut liquor or mushroom ketchup
- a few balls
- a spoonful of flour
- the juice of a lemon
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Cut up the fowl as for the last stew, put in a stewpan an onion chopped, also a tooth of garlic, some marjoram and lemon thyme, a little walnut liquor or mushroom ketchup.
2. Then place in the pieces of fowl, let it boil up, then put it at the side to simmer, put in a few balls.
3. When done rub in a basin a spoonful of flour, the juice of a lemon, a little browning to colour, stir it into the stew.
4. Dish it up.
