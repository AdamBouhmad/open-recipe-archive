## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- half a pint of water
- the whites of a couple of eggs

## Directions

1. Take the proportion of one pound of sugar to half a pint of water, with the whites of a couple of eggs
2. Boil it up twice
3. Set it by for the impurities to rise to the top
4. Skim it carefully
