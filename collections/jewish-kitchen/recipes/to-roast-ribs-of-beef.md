## Ingredients

- ribs of beef
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Roll them nicely, and skewer them light, if necessary tie them round with string
2. Put plenty of salt and pepper, and dredge them over with flour
3. Under the weight of 10 or 12 lbs. time for roasting will be 2½ hours, with a good fire
4. Keep basting constantly
5. When done dish them up in a well dish
6. Strain the fat from the dripping pan through a sieve
7. Take the brown part, add a little more salt and pepper
