## Ingredients

- peppers
- salt
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut the peppers in half and remove the seeds, stems and pith
2. cut them in neat, small pieces and throw into boiling salted water
3. Boil for half an hour
4. Drain them and then add salt to taste
5. add one tablespoon of butter and four tablespoons of cream to four peppers
6. Heat thoroughly and serve
