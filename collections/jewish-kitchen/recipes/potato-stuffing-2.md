## Ingredients

- hot mashed Irish potatoes
- sweet potatoes
- bread stuffing

## Directions

1. Add two cups of hot, mashed Irish or sweet potatoes to bread stuffing
2. Mix well and stuff in goose, stuffed veal or lamb breast, or in beef casings, cleaned and dressed
