## Ingredients

- 3 fine cucumbers
- vinegar
- a little good stock
- salt
- pepper
- 2 eggs
- a teaspoonful of flour
- the juice of a lemon
- lamb cutlets
- a little saffron

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the cucumbers in lengths of 3 inches
2. Lay them in vinegar for an hour
3. Stew them in a little good stock with salt and pepper till tender
4. See that there is not too much liquor
5. Beat 2 eggs with a teaspoonful of flour and the juice of a lemon
6. Stir it into the liquor and see that it does not boil
7. Have ready some lamb cutlets trimmed nicely and fried a light brown
8. Arrange the cucumber in the middle of the dish and put the cutlets round
9. Colour the sauce with a little saffron
