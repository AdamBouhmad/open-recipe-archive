## Ingredients

- cold veal or poultry
- a handful of fresh well cleaned button mushrooms
- a little veal gravy
- a piece of clarified veal fat about the size of the yolk of a hard boiled egg
- a piece of lemon peel
- very little white pepper and salt
- one small lump of white sugar
- a little nutmeg
- veal gravy
- a little lemon juice
- the beaten yolks of two eggs
- mashed potatoes
- a few truffles

## Directions

1. Cut into thin pieces of the size of shillings and half crowns, cold veal or poultry
2. Lay it in a small saucepan with a handful of fresh well cleaned button mushrooms
3. Pour over a little veal gravy, only enough to cover them, with a piece of clarified veal fat about the size of the yolk of a hard boiled egg
4. Flavor with a piece of lemon peel, very little white pepper and salt, one small lump of white sugar, and a little nutmeg
5. Stew all together for fifteen minutes
6. Pour over a sauce prepared in a separate saucepan, made with veal gravy, a little lemon juice, but not much, and the beaten yolks of two eggs
7. Let it simmer for an instant and then serve it up in the centre of a dish prepared with a wall of mashed potatoes, delicately browned
8. A few truffles renders this dish more elegant
