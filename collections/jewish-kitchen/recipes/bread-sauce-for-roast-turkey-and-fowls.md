## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of clear stock
- the crumb of a stale roll
- a small onion
- salt
- cayenne
- a pinch of ground mace
- a spoonful of the fat from plain broth

## Directions

1. Chop a small onion very fine
2. Soak the roll crumb in water first, then well drain it from the water
3. Beat it well up to a cream
4. Put the 1/2 pint of clear stock, the crumb, onion, salt, cayenne, and mace together
5. Let it all boil together
6. While it is boiling stir in a spoonful of the fat you have taken off plain broth that has no vegetable flavor
