## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Strip the skin off the stalks with care
2. Cut them into small pieces
3. Put into a saucepan with very little water
4. Stew slowly until soft
5. Sweeten while hot, but do not boil the sugar with the fruit
6. Eat cold
