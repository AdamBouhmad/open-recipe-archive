## Ingredients

- three egg-yolks
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one-quarter cup flour
- one cup cold milk
- two tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Stir three egg-yolks with one-half teaspoon of salt and one-quarter cup of flour until smooth
2. Add one cup of cold milk gradually
3. Fold in the beaten whites
4. Heat pan and add two tablespoons of butter
5. When hot pour in pancake
6. Let cook slowly and evenly on one side
7. Finish baking in oven
