## Ingredients

- matzoth-meal
- egg
- salt
- sugar
- ground almonds
- stoned and chopped raisins
- ground cinnamon
- oil or beef dripping
- water

## Directions

1. To each tablespoon of matzoth-meal take one egg, a pinch of salt, half a teaspoon of sugar, a teaspoon of ground almonds, a few stoned and chopped raisins, a pinch of ground cinnamon, a spoon of oil, or its equivalent of beef dripping, and just enough water to make the whole into a stiff paste
2. Mix the ingredients very thoroughly
3. Take a large enameled saucepan and about half fill it with oil or fat
4. Bring this to boiling point but do not let it burn
5. Shape the paste into small pieces and drop them into boiling fat
6. Turn them continually until well browned
7. Take out and drain carefully on a strainer
8. May be eaten hot or cold
