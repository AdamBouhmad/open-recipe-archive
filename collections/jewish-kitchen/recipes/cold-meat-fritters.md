## Ingredients

- cold meat
- egg
- fat or oil

## Directions

1. Prepare some cold meat as above, if too thick add another egg
2. Have your frying pan ready with boiling fat or oil
3. Put a large spoonful at the time as many as the pan will hold, only mind that they do not touch each other
4. Flatten them as you put them in, they ought to be flattened to the thickness of an inch
5. Fry them a light brown
6. Have a dish nice and hot at the side with a sheet of white paper
7. Lay them on with a slice of lemon upon each, and garnish with parsley
