## Ingredients

- two large cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- paprika
- one-half cup cream

## Directions

1. Pare two large cucumbers; remove seeds, if large; chop fine and squeeze dry.
2. Season with salt, vinegar, paprika and add one-half cup of cream.
