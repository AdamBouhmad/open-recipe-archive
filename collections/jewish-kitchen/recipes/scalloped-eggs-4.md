## Ingredients

- three or four eggs
- warm butter
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- crumbs of bread

## Directions

1. Poach lightly three or four eggs, place them in a dish, pour upon them a little warm butter
2. sprinkle with pepper, salt, and nutmeg, strew over with crumbs of bread
3. brown before the fire
