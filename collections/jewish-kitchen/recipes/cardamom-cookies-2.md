## Ingredients

- six eggs, hard-boiled
- one-half pound of sugar
- the grated peel of a lemon
- one-half wineglass of brandy
- one-half pound of butter
- flour
- about two cents worth of cardamom seed
- a little rosewater

## Directions

1. Boil six eggs hard.
2. When cold shell and grate the yolks.
3. Add one-half pound of sugar, the grated peel of a lemon and one-half wineglass of brandy.
4. Stir in one-half pound of butter which has been worked to a cream.
5. Sift in as much flour as you think will allow you to roll out the dough; take as little as possible, a little over half a pound, and flour the board very thick.
6. Put in about two cents worth of cardamom seed and a little rosewater.
7. Cut out with a fancy cake-cutter.
