## Ingredients

- pigeons
- melted veal fat
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- greased paper
- a very rich gravy

## Directions

1. Wash them, dry them well after they have been soaked.
2. Take a piece of the melted veal fat, rub it in salt, pepper, and chopped parsley, put it inside to keep it moist.
3. Secure both ends of pigeons so that it does not fall through, roll them in greased paper, put them back to back.
4. You can roast two at once by turning them.
5. Serve them up with a very rich gravy, and very hot.
