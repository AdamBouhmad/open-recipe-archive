## Ingredients

- six matzoth
- three whole eggs
- four tablespoons of goose fat or oil
- four tablespoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Break six matzoth in small pieces in a colander
2. Pour boiling water through them, drain quickly
3. They should be moist but not soggy
4. Beat three whole eggs well, fold the matzoth in lightly
5. Heat four tablespoons of goose fat or oil in a spider, add the egg mixture
6. Scrape and scramble carefully with spoon from the bottom of the pan, and while scrambling add four tablespoons of sugar
7. Cook gently until eggs are set
8. Serve at once
