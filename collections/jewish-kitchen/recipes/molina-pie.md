## Ingredients

- cold veal or chicken
- smoked beef or tongue
- lemon-juice
- nutmeg
- good beef or veal gravy
- yolks of four eggs
- rich pastry

## Directions

1. Mince finely cold veal or chicken, with smoked beef or tongue
2. Season well, add lemon-juice and a little nutmeg
3. Let it simmer in a small quantity of good beef or veal gravy
4. While on the fire, stir in the yolks of four eggs
5. Put it in a dish to cool, and then cover with a rich pastry, and bake it
