## Ingredients

- one pound of raw beef
- salt
- pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Take one pound of raw beef, cut off fat and stringy pieces and chop extremely fine.
2. Season with salt and pepper and grate in part of an onion or fry with onions.
3. Make into round cakes a little less than one-half inch thick.
4. Heat pan blue hot and grease lightly.
5. Add cakes and count sixty, then turn them and cook on the other side until brown.
6. When well browned they are done if liked rare, or cook ten minutes if liked well done.
