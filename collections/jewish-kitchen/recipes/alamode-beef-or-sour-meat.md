## Ingredients

- a piece of the ribs of beef boned and filletted, or a piece of the round
- vinegar diluted with water
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- whole allspice
- three or four bay leaves
- a cup full of raspings

## Directions

1. Cover a piece of the ribs of beef boned and filletted, or a piece of the round with vinegar diluted with water
2. season with onions, pepper, salt, whole allspice, and three or four bay leaves
3. add a cup full of raspings
4. let the whole stew gently for three or four hours, according to the weight of the meat
