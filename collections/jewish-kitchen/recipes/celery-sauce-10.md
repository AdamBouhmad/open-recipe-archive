## Ingredients

- heart of a white head of celery
- 1/2 pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- 1 yolk of an egg
- juice of a lemon
- a couple of spoonfuls of the liquor the turkey was boiled in
- a little of the green leaves of the celery

## Directions

1. Boil the heart of celery in 1/2 pint of water with a little salt and pepper
2. When tender rub it through a sieve
3. Beat in a basin one yolk of an egg with the juice of the lemon
4. Rub the celery in with a couple of spoonfuls of the liquor the turkey was boiled in
5. Chop a little of the green leaves of the celery and put them in the sauceboat
6. Send it to table
