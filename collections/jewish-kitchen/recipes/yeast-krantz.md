## Ingredients

- coffee cake dough
- one-fourth cup of washed currants
- beaten eggs
- sugar
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Take coffee cake dough, add one-fourth cup of washed currants
2. Let rise in warm place, then toss on floured board
3. Divide into three or four equal parts, roll each part into a long strand and work the strands together to form one large braid
4. Place braid in form of a circle in greased baking-pan or twist the braid to resemble the figure eight, pretzel shape
5. Let rise again in a warm place and bake in a moderate oven one-half hour or until thoroughly done
6. Brush with beaten eggs and sugar, sprinkle with a few chopped almonds
7. Return to oven to brown slightly
