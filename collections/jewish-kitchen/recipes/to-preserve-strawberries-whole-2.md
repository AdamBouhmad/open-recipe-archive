## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of loaf sugar
- 2 pints of water
- 2 lbs. of fine, fresh, hard strawberries
- brandy
- white of eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a preserving-pan 3 lbs. of loaf sugar with 2 pints of water, let it boil to a syrup, skim it as it boils up.
2. Have 2 lbs. of fine, fresh, hard strawberries, pick off the stalks, let them simmer in the syrup for 10 minutes, do this for 2 days.
3. Boil up the syrup very stiff, put the strawberries in pots, and pour over the thick syrup; let them get quite cold, therefore they must not be fastened down till the next day.
4. Cut round papers the size of the top of the jar, dip in brandy, then cover them over with papers brushed over with white of eggs.
