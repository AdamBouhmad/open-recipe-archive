## Ingredients

- six small tomatoes
- cream cheese
- pimento
- French mustard
- French dressing
- lettuce leaves
- thick boiled dressing

## Directions

1. Wash and skin six small tomatoes
2. Cut a piece from the stem end of each and when cold remove a portion of the pulp from the centre
3. Sprinkle with salt and invert on the ice to chill
4. Mash to a paste one small cream cheese
5. Add two tablespoons of chopped pimento, one tablespoon of French mustard
6. Blend well, moisten with a French dressing and fill into the tomato shells
7. Arrange on a bed of crisp lettuce leaves and pour over each tomato a tablespoon of thick boiled dressing
