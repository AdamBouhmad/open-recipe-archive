## Ingredients

- 6 apples
- water
- 2 oz. of raisins
- rind of lemon
- a glass of wine
- whites of 6 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and core 6 apples, boil them with a little water, 2 oz. of raisins, a little of the rind of lemon; let them get cold.
2. Add a glass of wine, beat up the whites of 6 eggs with 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar, lay it over the top and bake in a slow oven.
