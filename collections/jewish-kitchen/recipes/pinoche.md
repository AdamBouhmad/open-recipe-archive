## Ingredients

- One cup of packed medium brown sugar
- One-quarter cup of cream
- One-third cup of nut meats
- One-quarter pound pecans, weighed in shell
- One-third pound hickory in shell

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and cream to soft ball test
2. Cool until you can bear your hand on bottom of pan
3. Stir until it begins to thicken, add chopped nuts
4. When it is too thick to pour easily, spread quickly on a buttered pan
5. Cut in squares and cool
