## Ingredients

- five eggs
- six ounces of flour
- beaten almonds
- thin slices of citron

## Directions

1. Beat well five eggs
2. Add six ounces of flour
3. Flavour with beaten almonds
4. Add, if liked, thin slices of citron
5. Bake in a mould in a moderate oven
