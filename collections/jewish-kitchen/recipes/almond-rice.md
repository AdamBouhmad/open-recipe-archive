## Ingredients

- half a pound of whole rice
- milk
- sugar to taste
- a few pounded sweet almonds
- a few slices of citron
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of whole rice in milk until soft
2. Beat it through a sieve and set it on the fire with sugar according to taste, a few pounded sweet almonds and a few slices of citron
3. When it has simmered a short time, let it cool and place it in a mould
4. When sufficiently firm turn it out, stick it with blanched almonds, and pour over a fine custard
