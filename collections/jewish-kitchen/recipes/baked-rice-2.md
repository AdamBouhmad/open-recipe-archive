## Ingredients

- two cups of boiled rice
- grated cheese
- milk
- butter
- salt
- red pepper
- one cup of grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Arrange two cups of boiled rice in a baking dish in layers
2. Cover each with grated cheese, a little milk, butter, salt and red pepper
3. Spread one cup of grated bread crumbs over all
4. Bake in a moderate oven until the crumbs are browned
