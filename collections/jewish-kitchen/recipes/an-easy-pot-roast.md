## Ingredients

- four pounds of brisket
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- three tablespoons of tomatoes
- an onion
- water

## Directions

1. Take four pounds of brisket, season with salt, pepper and ginger.
2. Add three tablespoons of tomatoes and an onion cut up.
3. Cover with water in an iron pot and a close-fitting cover.
4. Put in oven and bake from three to four hours.
