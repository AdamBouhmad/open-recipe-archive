## Ingredients

- white meat of a turkey
- pickles
- beets
- carrots
- potatoes
- asparagus
- celery
- celery root
- salt
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce leaves or white cabbage leaves
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Take some white meat of a turkey, cut up fine.
2. Cut up a few pickles the same way, a few beets, one or two carrots, a few potatoes (the carrots and potatoes must be parboiled).
3. Also a few stalks of asparagus.
4. Chop up a bunch of crisp, white celery and a whole celery root (parboiled).
5. Sprinkle all with fine salt and pour a mayonnaise dressing over it.
6. Line the salad bowl with lettuce leaves or white cabbage leaves.
7. Add a few hard-boiled eggs and capers.
8. Garnish with sprigs of fresh parsley.
