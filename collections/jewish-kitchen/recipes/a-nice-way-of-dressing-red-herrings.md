## Ingredients

- red herrings
- hot water
- warmed butter
- egg (1 beaten)
- bread crumbs
- flour
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Open them, cut off the tails and heads
2. Soak them in hot water for an hour
3. Wipe them dry
4. Mix with warmed butter one beaten egg
5. Pour this over the herrings
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, flour, and white pepper
7. Broil them and serve them very hot
