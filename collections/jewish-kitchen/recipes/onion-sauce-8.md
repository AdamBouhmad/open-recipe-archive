## Ingredients

- finely-chopped onions
- fat
- half a clove of garlic
- a very little flour
- salt
- pepper
- soup stock

## Directions

1. Stew some finely-chopped onions in fat
2. You may add half a clove of garlic, cut extremely fine
3. Brown a very little flour in this
4. Season with salt and pepper and add enough soup stock to thin it
