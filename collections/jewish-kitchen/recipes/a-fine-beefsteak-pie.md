## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef steaks
- salt
- pepper
- mushroom powder
- one pint of strong broth
- a couple of table-spoonsful of Harvey-sauce
- good beef suet paste

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of beef steaks into large collops
2. Fry them quickly over a brisk fire
3. Place them in a dish in two or three layers, strewing between each, salt, pepper, and mushroom powder
4. Pour over a pint of strong broth, and a couple of table-spoonsful of Harvey-sauce
5. Cover with a good beef suet paste
6. Bake for a couple of hours
