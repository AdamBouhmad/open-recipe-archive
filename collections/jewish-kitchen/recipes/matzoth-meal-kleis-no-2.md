## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of chicken schmalz
- one cup of boiling water
- one egg
- matzoth meal
- chopped parsley
- ginger
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Beat one tablespoon of chicken schmalz till quite white; pour one cup of boiling water over one egg.
2. Add it to the dripping; stir these together, then add the flour, seasoning, a little chopped parsley, ginger, pepper and salt, and enough matzoth meal to form into small balls the size of a marble.
3. Drop these into the boiling soup and cook about fifteen minutes.
4. Test one in boiling water and if it boils apart add more meal.
