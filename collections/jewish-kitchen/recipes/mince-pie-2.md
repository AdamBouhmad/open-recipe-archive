## Ingredients

- Two pounds lean, fresh beef
- One-half pound chopped suet
- Two pounds of seeded raisins
- Two pounds of currants
- One-half pound of citron
- Two tablespoons of cinnamon
- Two tablespoons of mace
- One grated nutmeg
- One tablespoon of cloves
- Allspice
- Salt
- Two cups of white wine
- Two and one-half pounds of brown sugar
- Four apples
- Rich crust

## Directions

1. Boil two pounds lean, fresh beef
2. When cold, chop fine
3. Add one-half pound chopped suet, shredded very fine, and all gristle removed
4. Mix in a bowl two pounds of seeded raisins, two pounds of currants, one-half pound of citron, chopped very fine
5. Mix two tablespoons of cinnamon, two tablespoons of mace, one grated nutmeg, one tablespoon of cloves, allspice, and salt with meat and suet
6. Take two cups of white wine, two and one-half pounds of brown sugar and let stand
7. Chop fine four apples, and add meat to fruits
8. Mix wine with whole, stir well, and put up in small stone jars
9. Let stand at least two days before using
10. Line pie-plates with a rich crust, fill with mince meat mixture
11. Put a rich paste crust on top, or strips if preferred, prick slightly and bake
12. Serve warm, not hot
