## Ingredients

- two quarts of strong stock made of gravy beef
- carrots
- turnips
- leek
- celery
- brocoli
- peas
- French beans
- white sugar
- pepper
- salt
- force-meat balls

## Directions

1. Take two quarts of strong stock made of gravy beef
2. Add to this, carrots, turnips, leek, celery, brocoli, peas and French beans, all cut as small as possible
3. Add a few lumps of white sugar, pepper, and salt
4. Let it simmer till the vegetables are perfectly soft
5. Throw in a few force-meat balls
