## Ingredients

- a well-cleaned calf's head
- some good stock made of beef and veal
- flour
- fried shalot
- pepper
- parsley
- tarragon
- a little mushroom ketchup
- a pint of white wine
- balls of force-meat
- egg-balls
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Half boil a well-cleaned calf's head
2. Cut off all the meat in small square pieces and break the bones
3. Return it to the stew-pan with some good stock made of beef and veal
4. Dredge in flour and add fried shalot, pepper, parsley, tarragon, a little mushroom ketchup, and a pint of white wine
5. Simmer gently until the meat is perfectly soft and tender
6. Put in balls of force-meat and egg-balls a short time before serving
7. Stir in the juice of a lemon as an improvement
