## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- lemon rind
- cinnamon
- flour
- spice
- candied orange peel
- candied lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of butter and sugar, say half a pound of each
2. Grate the rind of a lemon and add a little cinnamon
3. Add as much flour as will form it into a paste with spice
4. Roll it out and cut it into two small cakes
5. Bake
6. A piece of candied orange or lemon-peel may be put on the top of each cake
