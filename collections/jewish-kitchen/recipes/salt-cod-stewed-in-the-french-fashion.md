## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 teeth of garlic
- 1/2 pint of oil
- pepper
- ground mace
- a little dried saffron
- juice of 3 lemons
- 2 lbs. of the thick part of the salt cod
- a few large potatoes

## Directions

1. Pound 2 or 3 teeth of garlic
2. Put it into the stewpan with 1/2 pint of oil, pepper, and ground mace, and a little dried saffron
3. Let it fry on the fire for five minutes, keep stirring it
4. Squeeze and strain the juice of 3 lemons, add them
5. Have 2 lbs. of the thick part of the salt cod, dry it well, put it in the saucepan and let it simmer gently for 1/2 an hour
6. The salt fish must be boiled first
7. When done put the cod in a dish
8. Have a few large potatoes cut into small ones, and ready boiled by the time the stew is done
9. Lay them round the fish and throw over the gravy
