## Ingredients

- eggs
- matzoth
- milk
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- grated peel of a lemon

## Directions

1. Beat up as many eggs as are required
2. Into these dip matzoth that have been soaked in milk
3. Fry quickly to a light brown on both sides
4. Lay on a large platter, sprinkle with a mixture of sugar, cinnamon and grated peel of a lemon
5. Fry in butter
