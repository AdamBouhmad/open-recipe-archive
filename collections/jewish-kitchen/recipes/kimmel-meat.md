## Ingredients

- a small piece of the rump of beef, or the under cut of a sirloin
- three pints of vinegar
- two ounces of caraway seeds
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Place a small piece of the rump of beef, or the under cut of a sirloin in a deep pan with three pints of vinegar
2. add two ounces of caraway seeds tied in a muslin bag, salt, pepper, and spices
3. cover it down tight
4. bake thoroughly in a slow oven
