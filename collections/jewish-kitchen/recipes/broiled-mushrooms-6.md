## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- buttered toast
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the mushrooms and remove the stems and peel the caps
2. Place them in a broiler and broil for five minutes, with the cap side down during the first half of broiling
3. Serve on circular pieces of buttered toast, sprinkling with salt and pepper and putting a small piece of butter on each cap
