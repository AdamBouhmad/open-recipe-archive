## Ingredients

- two cups of rolled oats
- boiling water
- one cake of compressed yeast
- one-fourth cup of brown sugar
- one-half cup of lukewarm water
- two tablespoons of shortening
- flour
- one teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over two cups of rolled oats, cover and let stand until lukewarm
2. Dissolve one cake of compressed yeast and one-fourth cup of brown sugar in one-half cup of lukewarm water
3. Add two tablespoons of shortening, the oatmeal and the water in which it has been swelling
4. Beat well, add enough flour to make a dough, also add one teaspoon of salt
5. Let rise until it doubles in bulk
6. Mold into two loaves in pan and bake forty-five minutes
