## Ingredients

- 2 calves' feet
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- marjoram
- pint of water
- meat balls
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut 2 calves' feet in several pieces
2. Slice an onion in a stewpan with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and a little marjoram
3. Put in the feet with a pint of water
4. Give them a quick boil to skim the liquor
5. Let them cook slowly for 2 hours
6. Have some meat balls, not too small
7. When the feet are very tender, put in the sauce made as directed before with a little chopped parsley
