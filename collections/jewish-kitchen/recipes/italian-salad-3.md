## Ingredients

- cold fowl
- cold smoked beef
- 4 eggs
- lettuce
- mustard and cress
- 2 spoonfuls of best salad oil
- 2 spoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 spoonful of tarragon vinegar
- 2 yolks of eggs raw
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- garlic
- beetroot

## Directions

1. Cut up the white part of any cold fowl that may be left
2. Grate some cold smoked beef
3. Boil 4 eggs hard
4. Have a lettuce nicely washed, also some mustard and cress
5. Take a salad bowl and put little heaps of the cold chicken, the grated smoked beef, the yolks of the eggs chopped, and some of the white
6. Arrange it tastefully, mixed with little portions of the lettuce and mustard and cress
7. Put round some slices of hard eggs
8. Make a mixture of 2 spoonfuls of the best salad oil, 2 of vinegar, 1 of tarragon vinegar, 2 yolks of eggs raw, some salt, pepper, a little mustard, and a tooth of garlic chopped very fine
9. Rub the mixture together like a cream
10. Do not put on till dinner or supper time
11. You can vary the style by putting the fowl and meat in the centre, and making walls of the salad round with strips of beetroot and hard eggs
