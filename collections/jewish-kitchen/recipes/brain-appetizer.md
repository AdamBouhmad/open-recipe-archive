## Ingredients

- brains
- salt
- chopped onions
- juice of one and a half lemons
- olive oil
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cook brains and let cool
2. Add salt
3. Beat up with chopped onions, juice of one and a half lemons and olive oil
4. Serve on lettuce leaves
