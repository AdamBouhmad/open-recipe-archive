## Ingredients

- 2 lb. loaf sugar
- 20 eggs
- 1 lb. ground sweet almonds
- 1/2 lb. clarified sugar
- orange-flower water
- candied citron

## Directions

1. Clarify 2 lb. of loaf sugar to a thick syrup.
2. Take the yolks of 20 eggs, mix them up quickly and pass them through a sieve.
3. Have ready a funnel, the hole must be the size of a vermicelli.
4. Hold the funnel over syrup while it boils over a charcoal fire.
5. Pass the egg through stirring the sugar at the same time, holding the funnel such a distance from the sugar to admit of the eggs dropping into it.
6. When the eggs have been in a few minutes it will become hard enough to take out with a silver fork and must be placed on a drainer.
7. Continue adding the eggs to the boiling sugar until you have used up the whole of them.
8. Prepare 1 lb. of ground sweet almonds, boiled in 1/2 lb. of clarified sugar flavoured with orange-flower water.
9. Place a layer of this paste in a dish, then a layer of candied citron cut thin, then a thick layer of the eggs prepared as above.
10. Continue in alternate layers of almond paste, citron, and the eggs until it is high enough to look handsome.
11. Pile it in the shape of a cone, with the eggs as the last layer.
12. Put it in a slow oven for it to set a little, and the last layer a little crisp.
13. It must be served in the dish it is baked in and is generally garnished with gold, silver, and myrtle leaves.
