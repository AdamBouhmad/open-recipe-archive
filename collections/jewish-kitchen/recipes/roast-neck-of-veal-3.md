## Ingredients

- Three pounds of the best end neck of veal
- stuffing
- a slice of fat smoked beef or a piece of large sausage
- flour
- lemon juice
- gravy
- cayenne pepper
- cresses

## Directions

1. Take three pounds of the best end neck of veal
2. Make a stuffing as for stewed veal, adding a slice of fat smoked beef or a piece of large sausage
3. Season it well and flour it over
4. Put it down to roast with a brisk fire for 1 hour
5. When roasted nice and brown squeeze the juice of a lemon over it and a little gravy and a little cayenne pepper
6. Serve it up very hot and put a few cresses round
