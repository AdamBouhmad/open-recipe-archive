## Ingredients

- apples
- milk
- butter
- salt
- pounded sugar
- lemon
- orange-flower water
- eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as Potato Soufflet, only that the apples must not be baked previously to being filled
2. Merely core the apples, and scoop the interior as directed
