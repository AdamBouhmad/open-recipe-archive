## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- veal gravy
- celery
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace
- lemon peel
- yolks of eggs
- mushroom essence
- Jerusalem artichokes

## Directions

1. After soaking and blanching, stew them in veal gravy
2. season with celery, pepper, salt, nutmeg, a little mace, and a piece of lemon peel
3. serve with a fine white sauce, the gravy in which they are stewed will form the basis for it
4. add yolks of eggs and mushroom essence
5. Jerusalem artichokes cut the size of button mushrooms, are a suitable accompaniment as a garnish
