## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of confectioner's sugar
- butter the size of a large egg
- two tablespoons of cocoa
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- cream or whipped cream or lukewarm coffee

## Directions

1. Cream the sugar and butter together thoroughly
2. add the cocoa and vanilla
3. Lastly add the cream or coffee to make the mixture the consistence of very thick cream
4. Mark the icing to represent the bark of a tree
5. Add a little green candy on it
