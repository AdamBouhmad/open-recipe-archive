## Ingredients

- one-half cup of flour
- two tablespoons of butter
- four tablespoons of grated cheese
- yolk of one egg
- dash of cayenne pepper
- ice-water

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, grated cheese, yolk of one egg and cayenne pepper as little as possible
2. Moisten with enough ice-water
3. Roll out about a quarter of an inch thick
4. Cut into long, narrow strips
5. Shake a little more cheese on top
6. Bake in hot oven
