## Ingredients

- one cake of compressed yeast
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one cup of milk (scalded and cooled)
- one cup of lukewarm water
- one level tablespoon of butter
- three cups of gluten flour
- one teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve one cake of compressed yeast and one tablespoon of sugar in one cup of milk, scalded and cooled, and one cup of lukewarm water
2. Add one level tablespoon of butter then three cups of gluten flour gradually, and one teaspoon of salt
3. Knead thoroughly until smooth and elastic
4. Place in well-greased bowl
5. Cover and set aside in a warm place, free from draught, to rise until light, which should be in about two hours
6. Mold into loaves
7. Place in greased pans, filling them half full
8. Cover, let rise again, and when double in bulk, which should be in about one hour, bake in moderate oven forty-five minutes
