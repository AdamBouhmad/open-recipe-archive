## Ingredients

- one calf's head
- four and one-half quarts of water
- two red peppers
- onions
- celery
- carrots
- cloves
- salt
- cabbage
- meat stock
- fat
- two large tablespoons of flour
- calf's tongue
- hard-boiled eggs
- one glass of sherry
- one lemon

## Directions

1. Take one calf's head, wash well
2. Put on to boil with four and one-half quarts of water
3. Add two red peppers, onions, celery, carrots, cloves, salt to taste, and a little cabbage
4. Boil six hours
5. Have ready some meat stock
6. The next day put fat in a skillet with two large tablespoons of flour; let it brown
7. Take the calf's head and cut all the meat from it in pieces
8. Add the calf's tongue, cut in dice
9. Slice hard-boiled eggs, one glass of sherry, and one lemon sliced
10. Put all in the stock
11. Allow it to come just to a boil
