## Ingredients

- five pounds of grapes
- three juicy oranges
- five pounds of sugar
- one pound of raisins
- one pound of shelled walnuts

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and skins from five pounds of grapes and boil the pulp until tender.
2. Press it through a sieve.
3. Boil the skins of three juicy oranges until tender, then chop fine.
4. Put the grape skins and the pulp into a saucepan.
5. Add the orange juice, the boiled skins, five pounds of sugar, one pound of raisins (the muscat seeded), and one pound of shelled walnuts.
6. Boil until quite thick.
