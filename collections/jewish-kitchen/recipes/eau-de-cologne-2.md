## Ingredients

- one ounce of essence of bergamot
- one ounce of essence of lemon
- one ounce of lavender
- one ounce of orange flower-water
- two ounces of rosemary and honey-water
- one pint of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Mix together one ounce of essence of bergamot, the same quantity of essence of lemon, lavender, and orange flower-water, two ounces of rosemary and honey-water, with one pint of spirits of wine
2. Let the mixture stand a fortnight
3. Put it into a glass retort, the body of which immerse in boiling water contained in a vessel placed over a lamp, while the beak of the retort is introduced into a large decanter
4. Keep the water boiling while the mixture distils into the decanter, which should be covered with cold wet cloths
