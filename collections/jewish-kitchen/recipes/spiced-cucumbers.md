## Ingredients

- firm cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- mixed spices
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take nice firm cucumbers, slice thin and salt overnight
2. In the morning take vinegar sufficient for covering the quantity prepared, mixed spices and sugar according to taste
3. Put on to cook and when boiling put in the cucumbers and cook for thirty minutes
4. Put in airtight jars
