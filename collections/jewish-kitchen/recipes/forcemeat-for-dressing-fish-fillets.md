## Ingredients

- anchovies
- grated bread
- chopped parsley
- the yolk of a hard boiled egg
- grated lemon peel
- a little lemon juice
- pepper
- salt
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Pound finely anchovies, grated bread, chopped parsley, and the yolk of a hard boiled egg
2. Add grated lemon peel, a little lemon juice, pepper and salt
3. Make into a paste with two eggs
