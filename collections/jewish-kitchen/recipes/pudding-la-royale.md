## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of savoy biscuits
- 2 oz. of almond cakes
- 1 oz. of ratafias
- 1/4 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 a tumbler of sherry
- 1 wine-glass of rum
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 pint of almond milk
- 1 oz. of isinglass
- 1 oz. of orange and lemon peel
- 1 oz. of angelica
- oil of almonds

## Directions

1. Boil the isinglass in a little water to dissolve, then strain it into the milk
2. Beat up the eggs, stir in the wine and rum
3. Stir it into the almond milk in which you have boiled the sugar
4. Let it all boil to a custard, turn it out to cool
5. Crumble up the whole of the cakes
6. Grease the mould with oil of almonds, dust it well with sifted sugar
7. Cut up the orange and lemon peel and angelica, all cut in nice strips
8. Ornament the mould well with them
9. Put a layer of the crumbled cake and one of custard till the mould is filled
10. Put it in the ice to set, turn out just before it is wanted
