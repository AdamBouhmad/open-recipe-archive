## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 1 1/2 lb. of sweetmeats
- 2 oz. allspice
- 1 oz. ginger
- 1/2 lb. moist sugar
- 2 lbs. of treacle
- 3 lbs. of flour
- orange and lemon peel
- citron
- blanched almond

## Directions

1. Sift the flour on your board and make a wall round, it means to make a hole in the centre of the flour, so that it is higher all round
2. Put in the butter, treacle, spices, the orange and lemon peel, and citron chopped very fine
3. Make this all into a paste, using as little extra flour as you can
4. Roll it out and cut in pieces the size of half-a-crown
5. Put them on your buttered tins
6. Flatten them with your finger and put on each half a blanched almond
7. Bake in a quick oven; they are soon done
