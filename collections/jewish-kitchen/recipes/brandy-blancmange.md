## Ingredients

- isinglass
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 oz. of castor sugar
- thin rind of a lemon
- a piece of cinnamon
- 1 pint of sherry
- 1/2 pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Make just the same as Wine Blancmange, by adding to the wine 1/2 pint of brandy
