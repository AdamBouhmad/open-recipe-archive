## Ingredients

- almonds
- butter or olive oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water on the almonds
2. cool and remove the skins
3. dry thoroughly and brown in a hot oven, using a half tablespoon of butter or olive oil to each cup of nuts, which must be shaken frequently
4. When brown, sprinkle well with salt and spread on paper to dry and cool
