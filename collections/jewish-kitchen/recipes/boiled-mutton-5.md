## Ingredients

- half a shoulder, the knuckle part, or 3 lb. of the best end of the neck
- an onion
- salt
- pepper
- a little nutmeg
- 2 or 3 turnips
- a white cauliflower

## Directions

1. Put it in the saucepan with an onion, salt, pepper, a little nutmeg.
2. Boil with 2 or 3 turnips to garnish, or boil in another saucepan a white cauliflower to garnish.
3. Serve with caper sauce in a sauce tureen made from recipes.
