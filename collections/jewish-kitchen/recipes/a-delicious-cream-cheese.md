## Ingredients

- sweet milk
- cheese-cloth
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Allow sweet milk to stand until it is like a jelly, but does not separate.
2. Pour it into a cheese-cloth bag and hang up to drain until all the water is out of it and only the rich creamy substance remains.
3. When all the water is out, turn the cheese from the bag into a bowl.
4. To every pint of the cheesy substance add a tablespoon of butter and enough salt to season it palatably.
5. Whip it up with a fork until it is a smooth paste.
6. Put enough on a plate to make a little brick, like a Philadelphia cheese.
7. With two knives, one in each hand, lightly press the cheese together in the shape of a brick.
8. Smooth it over the top and put it away to cool.
