## Ingredients

- squash
- cold water
- milk or cream
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel squash and cut in quarters
2. put on to boil in cold water and cook until tender
3. Drain and mash fine and smooth
4. add one-half cup of milk or cream, one tablespoon of butter, pinch of salt and pepper
5. put back on stove to keep hot
6. Beat well with a spoon to make light and smooth
