## Ingredients

- best end of a neck of lamb
- chopped onion
- pepper
- salt
- water
- peas
- lettuce
- mint
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the best end of a neck of lamb, either keep it whole or divide it into chops as may be preferred
2. Put it into a saucepan with a little chopped onion, pepper, salt, and a small quantity of water
3. When half done add half a peck of peas, half a lettuce cut fine, a little mint, and a few lumps of sugar
4. Let it stew thoroughly
5. When done, there must not be too much liquor
