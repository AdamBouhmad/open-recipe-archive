## Ingredients

- stock from calf's feet or bullock's foot
- almond milk
- thin rind of a lemon
- cinnamon
- cloves
- orange-flower
- vanille
- a glass of rum

## Directions

1. First have the stock ready if you make it from the jelly of calf's feet or bullock's foot
2. Put the quantity after you have warmed it into a saucepan, and add the same proportion of almond milk as you would wine and rum
3. You can flavour it with orange-flower, vanille, and add also a glass of rum
4. Prepare the stock as you would for calf's feet jelly, as far as boiling it with the thin rind of a lemon, cinnamon, and cloves
5. You need not put the white of eggs, as it does not need to be made clear for the almond milk
6. If you use isinglass, you dissolve it in water according to the directions for jelly, and add the almond milk and flavouring
