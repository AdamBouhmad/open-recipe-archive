## Ingredients

- small cucumbers or gherkins
- salt
- green or red peppers
- cloves
- mustard seed
- bay leaf
- ground mustard
- white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Choose small cucumbers or gherkins for this purpose
2. Reject all that are specked or misshapen
3. Wash them thoroughly; drain off all the water, and allow them to lay in a tub overnight, thickly salted
4. In the morning wipe the pickles carefully
5. Lay them in a stone jar or a wooden bucket, in this way: Put in a layer of pickles
6. Cut up a few green or red peppers; put a few pieces in each layer, also a few cloves and a tablespoon of mustard seed, and one bay leaf
7. Then proceed in this way until the pickles are used
8. Take half a pound of the very best ground mustard, tie it in a cloth loosely, and lay this mustard-bag on top of the pickles
9. Boil enough white wine vinegar
