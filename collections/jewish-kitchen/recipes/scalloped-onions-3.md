## Ingredients

- boiled onions
- cream sauce
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut boiled onions into quarters
2. put them in a baking dish and mix well with cream sauce
3. cover with bread crumbs and bits of butter and place in the oven until the crumbs are browned
