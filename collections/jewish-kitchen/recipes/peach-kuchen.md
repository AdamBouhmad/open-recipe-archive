## Ingredients

- clarified butter
- rich coffee cake dough
- peaches
- sugar
- meringue

## Directions

1. Grease your cake-pans thoroughly with good clarified butter
2. Line them with a rich coffee cake dough which has been rolled very thin and set in a warm place to rise
3. Pare and quarter enough peaches to cover the dough
4. Lay the peaches in rows and sweeten and set in oven to bake
5. Make a meringue quickly as possible and pour over the cakes and bake a light brown
