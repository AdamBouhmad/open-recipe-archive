## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- one-fourth teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- scant one-half cup of milk or water

## Directions

1. Sift one cup of flour, one-fourth teaspoon of salt, one teaspoon of baking powder, stir in scant one-half cup of milk or water and mix to a smooth batter.
2. Drop one teaspoonful at a time in the boiling soup; cover kettle, let boil five minutes and serve at once.
