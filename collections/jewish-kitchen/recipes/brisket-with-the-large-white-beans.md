## Ingredients

- Four pounds of boned brisket
- a pint of water
- salt and pepper
- 1 pint of the large white beans
- a large onion
- a tooth of garlic
- the white heart of a head of celery
- a few drops of browning

## Directions

1. Put in soak the night before 1 pint of the large white beans.
2. Strain them well from the water they were soaked in.
3. Put boned brisket in a stewpan with a pint of water, salt, pepper.
4. Put the beans on with the beef, a large onion sliced, a tooth of garlic, the white heart of a head of celery cut very fine.
5. Let all cook together, this will require no thickening.
6. Colour with a few drops of browning.
