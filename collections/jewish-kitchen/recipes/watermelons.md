## Ingredients

- ripe watermelon
- pale sherry or claret
- sugar
- whole spices

## Directions

1. Use only melons that are perfectly ripe
2. Do not select those that are very large in circumference
3. Select a rough melon with a bumpy surface
4. Either cut in half or plug
5. Put pale sherry or claret on to boil and boil down to quite a thick syrup with sugar
6. Pour this into either a plugged melon or over the half-cut melon
7. Lay on ice for a couple of hours before serving
8. If you use claret you may spice it while boiling with whole spices
