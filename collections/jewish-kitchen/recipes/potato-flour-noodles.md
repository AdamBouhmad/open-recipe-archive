## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one-half cup of potato flour
- one-half cup of water

## Directions

1. Take three eggs, beat until a light yellow and add one-half cup of potato flour and one-half cup of water, beat well.
2. Heat a frying-pan, grease well and pour in the batter; fry in thin leaves or wafers.
3. Cool, cut thin as noodles.
4. Just before serving soup, strain, then let it come to a boil and add noodles and let soup again come to a boil and serve.
