## Ingredients

- wings, neck, gizzard, heart and back of goose
- salt
- ginger
- pepper
- garlic
- cold water
- fat
- small piece of garlic
- heaping teaspoon of flour

## Directions

1. Rub wings, neck, gizzard, heart and back of goose with salt, ginger, pepper and garlic
2. Set on the fire in a stew-pan with cold water
3. Cover tightly and stew slowly but steadily for four hours
4. When done skim off all the fat
5. Put a spider over the fire and put into it about two or three tablespoons of the fat that you have just skimmed off
6. Add the fat to the meat again
7. Cut up fine a very small piece of garlic and add a heaping teaspoon of flour (brown)
8. Add the hot gravy and pour all over the goose
9. Cover up tightly and set on back of stove till you wish to serve
