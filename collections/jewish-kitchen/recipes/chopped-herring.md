## Ingredients

- herring
- one onion
- one sour apple
- a slice of white bread
- vinegar
- one teaspoon oil
- a little cinnamon
- pepper
- hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Soak herring a few hours
2. Wash and clean, bone and chop
3. To one herring take one onion, one sour apple, a slice of white bread which has been soaked in vinegar
4. Chop all these
5. Add one teaspoon oil, a little cinnamon and pepper
6. Put on platter in shape of a herring with head at top and tail at bottom of dish
7. Sprinkle the chopped white of a hard-boiled egg over fish
8. Then sprinkle the chopped yolk
