## Ingredients

- mackerel
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 2 ozs. fresh butter
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Wash them well in two or three waters
2. dry them in a cloth well
3. season them with salt, pepper, and rub some juice of a lemon over them
4. grease the gridiron
5. lay them on
6. let them broil both sides well
7. put in a cup to melt 2 ozs. fresh butter
8. dish them on a hot dish
9. squeeze the juice of a lemon over it with a little cayenne
10. send up hot plates with broiled fish
