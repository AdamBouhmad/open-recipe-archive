## Ingredients

- salt fish
- garlic

## Directions

1. Clean, salt fish one half hour, wash and dry with a clean cloth.
2. Cut garlic very thin, rub over fish.
3. Place in oven to bake.
4. Bake until odor of garlic has disappeared, then let fish cool.
