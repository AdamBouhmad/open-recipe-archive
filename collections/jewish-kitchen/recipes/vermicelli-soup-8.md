## Ingredients

- 1 quart of stock
- 1 lb. of vermicelli
- saffron
- a couple of tomatoes
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Warm a quart of stock
2. When it boils put in 1-lb. of vermicelli
3. Let it boil for 10 minutes
4. The stock is already coloured with the saffron
5. Cut a couple of tomatoes small and boil them
6. Put them through a sieve
7. Put it into the tureen before you dish up the soup
8. Add a little grated nutmeg
