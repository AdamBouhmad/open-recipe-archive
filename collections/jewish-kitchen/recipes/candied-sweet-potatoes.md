## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- butter or chicken-fat
- sugar
- water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil sweet potatoes, peel and cut into long slices.
2. Place in an earthen dish; place lumps of butter or chicken-fat if desired on each side, and sprinkle with sugar.
3. A little water or juice of half a lemon may be added.
4. Bake until the sugar and fat have candied and the potatoes are brown.
