## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of flour
- one egg
- one-half cup of milk
- butter
- two more eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Take three tablespoons of flour and stir with one egg and one-half cup of milk
2. Pour this in a pan in which some butter was melted
3. Stir until it loosens from the pan
4. When it is cold, add two more eggs and some salt
5. Shortly before needed form in little dumplings and put in boiling hot soup for five minutes
