## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one-half cup of sweet butter
- grated lemon peel or essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Take one cup of sugar, one-half cup of sweet butter and stir to a cream
2. Flavor with grated lemon peel or essence of lemon
3. Make into any shape desired
4. serve
