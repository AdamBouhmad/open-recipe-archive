## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- onion juice
- flour
- hot milk
- salt
- paprika
- egg yolk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash, pare and cut potatoes in one-third inch pieces, there should be three cups.
2. Parboil three minutes, and drain.
3. Add one-third cup of butter, and cook on back of range until potatoes are soft and slightly browned.
4. Melt two tablespoons of butter, add a few drops of onion juice, two tablespoons of flour, and pour on gradually one cup of hot milk.
5. Season with salt and paprika, then add one well-beaten egg yolk.
6. Pour sauce over potatoes and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
