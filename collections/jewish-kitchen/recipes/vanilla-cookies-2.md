## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- two eggs
- two level teaspoons of baking-powder
- flour enough to make a dough
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub one cup of butter and one cup of sugar to a cream.
2. Add two eggs and two level teaspoons of baking-powder, flour enough to make a dough.
3. Flavor with vanilla, roll very thin, spread with beaten white of egg and sugar.
4. Proceed as for sugar cookies.
