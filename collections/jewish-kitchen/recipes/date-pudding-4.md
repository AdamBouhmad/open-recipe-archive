## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of butter
- one-half cup of molasses
- one-half cup of milk
- one and two-third cups of flour
- one-half teaspoon of baking-soda
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- one-quarter teaspoon of cloves
- one-quarter teaspoon of cinnamon
- one-quarter teaspoon of nutmeg
- one-half pound of dates

## Directions

1. Melt three tablespoons of butter
2. Add one-half cup of molasses and one-half cup of milk
3. Add one and two-third cups of flour sifted with one-half teaspoon of baking-soda and one-quarter teaspoon of salt
4. Add one-quarter teaspoon each of cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg
5. Add one-half pound of dates, stoned and cut
6. Turn into a well-buttered mold
7. Butter the cover also and steam two and one-half hours
8. Keep at a steady boil
9. Serve with any kind of sauce
