## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- two tablespoons of cornstarch
- one-half cup of cold milk
- three squares of chocolate
- three tablespoons of sugar
- three tablespoons of hot water

## Directions

1. Scald two cups of milk
2. Add two tablespoons of cornstarch diluted with one-half cup of cold milk
3. Cook ten minutes over boiling water
4. Melt three squares of chocolate over hot water
5. Add three tablespoons of sugar and three tablespoons of hot water and stir until smooth
6. Add to cooked mixture
