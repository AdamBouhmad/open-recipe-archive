## Ingredients

- a tablespoon of drippings
- one or two cloves of garlic
- a tablespoon of flour
- a cup of soup stock or warm water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat a tablespoon of drippings, either of meat or goose in a frying-pan
2. Cut up one or two cloves of garlic very fine and let it brown slightly in the heated fat
3. Add a tablespoon of flour, a cup of soup stock or warm water, salt, pepper to taste
