## Ingredients

- long sweet potatoes
- water
- salt
- apples
- maple syrup
- butter
- rum
- candied cherry

## Directions

1. Wash and pare long sweet potatoes.
2. Cook in boiling salted water until almost soft; drain and cut slices crosswise, two inches high.
3. Core, pare and cut apples in one-half inch rounds.
4. Into a spider, place the potatoes upright, with a slice of apple on top of each.
5. Pour over one-half cup of maple syrup, one-fourth cup of water and two tablespoons of butter.
6. Baste frequently until apples are soft.
7. Pour one teaspoon of rum over each section, place a candied cherry in the center of each apple and bake ten minutes.
8. Remove to platter and if desired, pour more rum over and around.
9. Light the liquor and bring to the table burning.
