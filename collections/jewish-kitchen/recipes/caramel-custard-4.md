## Ingredients

- one-half cup of sugar
- four cups of scalded milk
- eggs
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt one-half cup of sugar until it is light brown in color.
2. Add four cups of scalded milk.
3. Beat the eggs, add the milk and sugar, one-quarter teaspoon of salt, one teaspoon of vanilla and bake in cups as directed for cup custard.
4. Serve with caramel sauce.
