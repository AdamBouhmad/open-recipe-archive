## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- four cups of water
- two cups of orange juice
- one-half cup of lemon juice
- grated rind of one orange and one lemon

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of two cups of sugar and four cups of water
2. Boil fifteen minutes
3. Add two cups of orange juice, one-half cup of lemon juice and the grated rind of one orange and one lemon
4. Freeze and serve in glasses
