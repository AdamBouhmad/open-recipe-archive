## Ingredients

- one-half teaspoon salt
- one-fourth teaspoon pepper
- one teaspoon sugar
- paprika
- two tablespoons vinegar
- four tablespoons olive oil

## Directions

1. Mix one-half teaspoon of salt, one-fourth teaspoon of pepper, one teaspoon of sugar, a dash of paprika, two tablespoons of vinegar and four tablespoons of olive oil. Stir until well-blended and use at once.
