## Ingredients

- grapes
- sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze the pulp into one bowl and put the skins into another.
2. Press the pulp through a sieve.
3. Weigh the grapes before you squeeze them and allow three-quarters of a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit.
4. Put the strained pulp and sugar on to boil, the skins also, and boil slowly until thick.
