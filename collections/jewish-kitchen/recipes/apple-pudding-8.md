## Ingredients

- three matzoth
- three good-sized apples
- one-quarter pound of currants
- one-quarter pound of raisins
- a little cinnamon
- some rind of lemon
- one-quarter pound of brown sugar
- two ounces of melted fat
- six beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Soak three matzoth and squeeze the water out well
2. Put them in a bowl with three good-sized apples cut in small thick pieces
3. Add one-quarter pound of currants, one-quarter pound of raisins, a little cinnamon, some rind of lemon cut thin, one-quarter pound of brown sugar and two ounces of melted fat
4. Mix all well together with six beaten eggs
5. Pour in a greased dish and bake in a moderate oven
6. Serve with rum sauce
