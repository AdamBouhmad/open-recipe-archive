## Ingredients

- one teaspoon of butter
- one-fourth teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one egg
- one tablespoon of cream
- one teaspoon of brandy
- flour
- butter or Crisco
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix one teaspoon of butter, one-fourth teaspoon of salt, one tablespoon of sugar with one egg
2. Add one tablespoon of cream and one teaspoon of brandy
3. Add flour to make stiff dough
4. Work the whole together with a spoon until the flour is incorporated with the other ingredients
5. Break the dough in pieces about the size of a walnut
6. Roll each piece out separately just as thin as possible without tearing
7. Make three lengthwise slashes in the centre of each piece of dough after rolling out
8. Heat a large deep skillet about half full with boiling hot butter or Crisco
9. Drop in the snowballs, not more than three at one time
10. Brown quickly on one side, then on the other
11. Turn carefully with a perforated skimmer as they are easily broken
12. Remove to a platter
13. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and cinnamon and a few drops of lemon juice
