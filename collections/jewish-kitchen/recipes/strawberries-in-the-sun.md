## Ingredients

- sugar
- berries
- water

## Directions

1. To two pounds of berries take two pounds of sugar and three-quarters cup of water
2. Put the syrup in the preserving kettle; bring it to a boil and cook for about ten minutes, or until it begins to thicken
3. Add the berries; cook for ten minutes and pour them out in shallow dishes or meat platters
4. Cover with sheets of glass, allowing a little air for ventilation; place in the sun until the juice is thick and syrupy
5. This will take two days or more
6. Put into small jars or tumblers and cover according to directions
