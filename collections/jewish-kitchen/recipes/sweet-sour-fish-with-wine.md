## Ingredients

- one glass water
- one-half glass vinegar
- two tablespoons brown sugar
- one-half dozen cloves
- one-half teaspoon ground cinnamon
- one onion
- one lemon
- one goblet red wine
- one dozen raisins
- one tablespoon pounded almonds
- fish

## Directions

1. Put on to boil in fish kettle one glass water, one-half glass vinegar, two tablespoons of brown sugar, one-half dozen cloves, one-half teaspoon of ground cinnamon, one onion cut in round slices
2. Boil thoroughly, then strain
3. Add to it one lemon cut in round slices, one goblet of red wine, one dozen raisins, one tablespoon of pounded almonds
4. Put on stove again
5. When it comes to a boil, add fish
