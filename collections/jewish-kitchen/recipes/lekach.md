## Ingredients

- three cups of sifted flour
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- three eggs
- one teaspoon of allspice
- one teaspoon of soda
- the grated rind and juice of one-half lemon
- three tablespoons of honey
- almond or walnut

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well.
2. Roll on board to one-fourth inch in thickness and cut with form.
3. Brush with white of egg or honey diluted with water.
4. On each cake put an almond or walnut.
5. Bake in moderate oven from fifteen to twenty minutes.
