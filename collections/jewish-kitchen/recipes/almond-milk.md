## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of good Valentia almonds sweet
- hot water
- cold water
- 1 pint of cold water

## Directions

1. Take 1 lb. of good Valentia almonds sweet
2. Lay them in hot water to get off the skins
3. As you peel them out of the hot water throw them into a basin with cold water
4. Have your marble mortar very clean, pound them by degrees
5. Have a very fine sieve, have a pint of cold water in a mug
6. As you put the pounded almonds in the sieve, throw a little cold water over them till you have used up the pint for the pound
7. Turn the milk with the almonds into a very clean saucepan, boil it up
8. Strain it again into a basin for use
