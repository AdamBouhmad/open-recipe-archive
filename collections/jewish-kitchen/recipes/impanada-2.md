## Ingredients

- halibut, plaice, or soles
- potatoes
- short-crust paste
- brown sugar
- small pickles
- peppers
- gherkins
- West India pickles
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut in small pieces halibut, plaice, or soles
2. Place them in a deep dish in alternate layers with slices of potatoes and dumplings made of short-crust paste, sweetened with brown sugar
3. Season well with small pickles, peppers, gherkins, or West India pickles
4. Throw over a little water and butter warmed
5. Bake it thoroughly
