## Ingredients

- three pounds of fresh salmon
- salt
- one teaspoon of salt
- one quart of water
- one-fourth cup of melted butter
- one tablespoon of parsley
- one-half pound of horseradish root
- one pint of cream

## Directions

1. Clean three pounds of fresh salmon, bone, salt and let stand several hours.
2. Place in fish kettle with boiling salt water (one teaspoon of salt to one quart of water), and let boil one-half hour or until well cooked.
3. Lift out carefully, place on hot platter and pour over one-fourth cup of melted butter and sprinkle well with one tablespoon of parsley.
4. Peel one-half pound of horseradish root, grate and mix well with one pint of cream beaten stiff.
5. Serve fish with sauce in a separate bowl, a large spoonful with each portion of fish. The fish must be hot and the sauce cold.
