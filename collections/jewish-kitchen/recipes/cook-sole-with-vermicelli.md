## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 1 teacup water
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 1 pint milk
- 1 large sole
- 1/2-lb. vermicelli
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Chop an onion and put it in a stewpan with a teacup of water, salt, and pepper, a little grated nutmeg.
2. Let it boil till the onion is quite tender, add a pint of milk.
3. Wash a large sole well, let it cook in the milk.
4. Then add a 1/2-lb. of vermicelli.
5. When done put the sole in a dish, and the vermicelli round, throw over a little chopped parsley.
6. This is an exquisite and delicate dish for an invalid.
