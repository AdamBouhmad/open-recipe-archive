## Ingredients

- fat cut into small pieces
- cold water
- raw potato slices (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the fat into small pieces.
2. Put in a deep, iron kettle and cover with cold water.
3. Place on the stove uncovered; when the water has nearly all evaporated, set the kettle back and let the fat try out slowly.
4. When the fat is still and scraps are shriveled and crisp at the bottom of the kettle, strain the fat through a cloth into a stone crock, cover and set it away in a cool place.
5. Alternatively, the water may be omitted and the scraps slowly tried out on back of stove or in moderate oven.
6. When fat is tried out, pour in crock.
7. Several slices of raw potato put with the fat will aid in the clarifying.
