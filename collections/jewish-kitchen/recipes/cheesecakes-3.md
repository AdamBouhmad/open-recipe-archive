## Ingredients

- 4 ozs. of butter
- 4 ozs. of castor sugar
- the rind of 3 lemons
- the juice of 1 lemon
- 3 eggs
- a spoonful of brandy
- a little grated nutmeg
- rich puff-paste

## Directions

1. Warm 4 ozs. of butter, mix with 4 ozs. of castor sugar
2. Grate in the rind of 3 lemons, the juice of one (remember always where the juice of lemon is used to strain it)
3. Beat well 3 eggs, a spoonful of brandy, a little grated nutmeg
4. Mix all well together
5. Line your tartlet pans with rich puff-paste
6. Put a small quantity in each
7. Bake in a quick oven
