## Ingredients

- roe of any fish
- salt
- two tablespoons of oil
- pepper
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove skin from roe and salt
2. Set aside over night
3. Next day beat roe apart
4. Pour boiling water over it and stir
5. When roe is white, pour off the water and let drain
6. Put in pan with two tablespoons of oil and salt, pepper, a little vinegar
7. Mix well
8. Let stand a few days before using
