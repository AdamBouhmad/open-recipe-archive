## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one-half cup of flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one cup of milk
- butter
- powdered sugar
- six lemon slices

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until very light
2. Add flour and salt and beat again
3. Add milk slowly and beat thoroughly
4. Heat a generous quantity of butter in a frying-pan
5. Pour all the batter into this at one time
6. Place on a hot stove for one minute
7. Remove to a brisk oven
8. When edges turn up on sides of pan, reduce heat and cook more slowly until light, crisp and brown, about seven minutes
9. Slide carefully on a hot plate
10. Sprinkle plentifully with powdered sugar
11. Send to the table with six lemon slices
