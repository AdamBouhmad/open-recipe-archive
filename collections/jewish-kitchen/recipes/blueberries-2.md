## Ingredients

- twelve quarts of berries
- one quart of sugar
- one pint of water

## Directions

1. Put water, berries, and sugar in preserving kettle; heat slowly.
2. Boil sixteen minutes, counting from the time the contents of the kettle begins to bubble.
