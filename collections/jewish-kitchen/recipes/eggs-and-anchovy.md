## Ingredients

- eggs
- anchovies
- bread
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Boil some eggs very hard
2. Lay them in cold water before you peel them for 5 minutes
3. Cut each egg in 4 or 5 slices
4. Wash and bone some anchovies
5. Dry them well
6. Spread on the bread some mayonnaise
7. Put on 1 piece of egg with 1/2 an anchovy to each sandwich
8. Place the 4 pieces of egg on the bread so that when you divide the 4 sandwiches you do not cut the egg
9. Dish them up on a paper expressly for sandwiches or a napkin
