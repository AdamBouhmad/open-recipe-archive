## Ingredients

- one fowl
- one fine sweetbread
- veal gravy
- white sauce
- forcemeat balls
- truffles
- morels
- beef gravy
- mushroom ketchup or lemon-pickle

## Directions

1. Blanch a fowl and a fine sweetbread
2. Parboil them
3. Cut off in smooth well shaped slices all the white part of the fowl
4. Slice the sweetbread in similar pieces
5. Place them together in a fine well-flavoured veal gravy
6. When done, serve neatly in the dish and pour over a fine white sauce
7. Cut the remainder of the fowl in small joints or pieces, not separated from the bone
8. Fry them to become brown
9. Place them in a stew-pan with forcemeat balls, truffles, and morels
10. Pour over half or three quarters of a pint of beef gravy and simmer till finished
11. Add a little mushroom ketchup or lemon-pickle if desired
