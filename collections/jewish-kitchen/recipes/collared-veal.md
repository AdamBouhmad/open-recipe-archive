## Ingredients

- a nice piece of veal, the breast is the best part
- chopped herbs
- mace
- pepper
- salt
- slices of smoked tongue
- beetroot
- chopped parsley
- hard yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the bones, gristle, &c., from a nice piece of veal, the breast is the best part for the purpose
2. Season the meat well with chopped herbs, mace, pepper, and salt
3. Lay between the veal slices of smoked tongue variegated with beetroot, chopped parsley, and hard yolks of eggs
4. Roll it up tightly in a cloth
5. Simmer for some hours till tender
6. When done, it should have a weight laid on it to press out the liquor
