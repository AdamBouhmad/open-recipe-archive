## Ingredients

- macaroni
- tomato or cream sauce
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into small pieces
2. Boil for half an hour
3. Drain and blanch in cold water
4. Reheat in tomato or cream sauce and serve
5. Grated cheese may be sprinkled over the dish if desired
