## Ingredients

- small firm green tomatoes
- dill
- whole peppers
- bay leaves
- whole ginger
- cloves
- horseradish
- salt water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Select small firm green tomatoes
2. Follow recipe for Dill Pickles, using the green tomatoes in place of the pickles
