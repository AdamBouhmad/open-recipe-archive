## Ingredients

- a cupful of linseed
- boiling water
- three table spoonfuls of rose-water

## Directions

1. Take a cupful of linseed
2. pour over it sufficient boiling water to cover
3. let it stand some hours
4. pour over three table spoonfuls of rose-water
5. stir the seeds well about
6. strain it off into a bottle
