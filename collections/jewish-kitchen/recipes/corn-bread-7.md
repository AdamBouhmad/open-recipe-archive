## Ingredients

- one cup of corn-meal
- one cup of flour
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- three teaspoons of baking-powder
- one tablespoon of butter
- one egg
- milk

## Directions

1. Mix and sift one cup of corn-meal, one cup of flour, two tablespoons of sugar, one-half teaspoon of salt, three teaspoons of baking-powder
2. Melt one tablespoon of butter and add to one egg
3. Mix milk and egg and beat this into the dry ingredients
4. Pour this mixture into well-greased tins
5. Bake in a hot oven one-half hour
6. Cut in squares and serve hot
