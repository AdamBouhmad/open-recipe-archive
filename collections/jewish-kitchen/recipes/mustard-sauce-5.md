## Ingredients

- two tablespoons vinegar
- one tablespoon mustard
- one teaspoon oil or butter
- pepper
- salt
- two hard-boiled eggs
- one small onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoons of vinegar and one of mustard, one teaspoon of oil or butter melted, pepper and salt to taste.
2. Add this to two hard-boiled eggs chopped fine, with a small onion and about the same quantity of parsley as eggs.
3. Mix all well together.
