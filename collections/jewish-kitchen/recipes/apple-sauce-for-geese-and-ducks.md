## Ingredients

- three or four large boiling apples
- a little water
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and core three or four large boiling apples
2. Cut them small into a saucepan with a little water, enough to moisten them
3. When soft beat them up with a fork to a pulp
4. Add some brown sugar
5. Turn them into the sauceboat
