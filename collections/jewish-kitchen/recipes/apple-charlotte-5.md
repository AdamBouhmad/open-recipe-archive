## Ingredients

- apples
- oval cake tin
- savoy cakes
- whites of eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the apples as in the last receipt
2. put the apples into an oval cake tin about the size of a small side dish, four or five inches high
3. when cold, turn it out
4. cover the apple-shape with savoy cakes placed closely together perpendicularly
5. all round the top of the charlotte should be covered with whites of eggs and sugar, beaten to a stiff froth, and placed in small balls
6. a salamander should be used to crisp them and to give a slight peach-like colour
7. a tasteful cook will, after crisping the first layer of these balls, add others over them to form a sort of cone high in the centre
