## Ingredients

- 6 or 7 large red tomatoes
- a quart of stock No. 1
- the juice of a lemon
- a little cayenne
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes in halves
2. Put them in a saucepan at the side of the fire with no water
3. Let them simmer till quite tender
4. Rub them through a sieve till only their skins remain
5. Have a quart of stock No. 1 ready at the side
6. Stir them well in till it is quite smooth
7. Have ready in your tureen the juice of a lemon and a little cayenne
8. Many persons like toasted bread with this soup
