## Ingredients

- one-half pound of pot cheese
- one-half pound of butter
- two cups of flour sifted four times
- salt
- jelly
- one-half pound of walnuts ground
- one-half cup of sugar
- hot milk
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Take pot cheese and butter and flour
2. Add a pinch of salt and work these ingredients into a dough
3. Make thirty small balls of it and put on a platter on the ice overnight
4. In the morning roll each ball separately into two-inch squares
5. Fill squares with a teaspoon of jelly put in the centre and fold over like an envelope
6. Or fill them with ground walnuts, sugar and moisten with a little hot milk
7. Roll and twist into shape
8. Brush with beaten egg
9. Bake in a moderately hot oven
