## Ingredients

- slices of leek or young onions
- one tablespoon of drippings
- neck or breast of lamb
- white pepper
- salt
- parsley
- dill

## Directions

1. Brown slices of leek or young onions in one tablespoon of drippings.
2. Add neck or breast of lamb, cut in small pieces.
3. Season with white pepper, salt and parsley.
4. Cook until tender.
5. Just before serving season with dill.
