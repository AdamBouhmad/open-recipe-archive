## Ingredients

- half a loaf of bread
- water
- one cup of suet
- tart apples
- sugar
- raisins
- cinnamon
- one-quarter cup of pounded almonds
- yolks of three eggs
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Soak half a loaf of bread in water and squeeze dry
2. Shave one cup of suet very fine and cut up some tart apples in thin slices
3. Add sugar, raisins, cinnamon, about one-quarter cup of pounded almonds and the yolks of three eggs
4. Mix all thoroughly
5. Add whites beaten to a stiff froth last
6. Bake one hour
