## Ingredients

- cold veal or poultry
- a little grated tongue, or smoked beef
- a few crumbs of bread
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- essence of lemon
- two or three eggs
- a very small quantity of good gravy

## Directions

1. Mince finely some cold veal or poultry
2. Add a little grated tongue, or smoked beef, a few crumbs of bread, sweet herbs, pepper, salt, parsley, and if approved, essence of lemon
3. Mix all well with two or three eggs, and a very small quantity of good gravy
4. Grease a mould, put in the above ingredients and bake for three-quarters of an hour
5. Turn out with care, and serve with mushroom sauce
