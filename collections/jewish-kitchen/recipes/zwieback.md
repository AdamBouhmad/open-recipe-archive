## Ingredients

- one-half cup of milk
- one cake of compressed yeast
- one-fourth cup of sugar
- one-fourth cup of melted butter
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- three eggs unbeaten
- one-half teaspoon of powdered anise
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald one-half cup of milk and when lukewarm add to one cake of compressed yeast
2. Add one-fourth cup of sugar, one-fourth cup of melted butter, one-half teaspoon of salt and three eggs unbeaten, one-half teaspoon of powdered anise and enough flour to handle
3. Let rise until light
4. Make into oblong rolls the length of middle finger and place together in a buttered pan in parallel rows, two inches apart
5. Let rise again and bake twenty minutes
6. When cold, cut in one-half inch slices and brown evenly in the oven
