## Ingredients

- pigeon
- pepper
- salt
- fat
- lemon
- rich gravy
- 2 or 3 large mushrooms

## Directions

1. Split the pigeon open down the back, dry it well
2. Season it with pepper and salt
3. Rub them well over with fat
4. Put them on the gridiron to a very clear fire
5. When done squeeze the juice of a lemon over, and a rich gravy
6. Broil 2 or 3 large mushrooms, lay them under
