## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Onion
- Black pepper
- Red pepper
- Whole cloves
- Salt
- Cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut eight quarts of tomatoes in pieces and stew them until soft.
2. Press through a sieve to remove the skins and seeds.
3. Add one head of garlic or one-half onion, one-half tablespoon of black pepper, one-quarter teaspoon of red pepper, one-half ounce whole cloves, three-quarters of a cup of salt and one of cider vinegar.
4. Mix thoroughly and boil about three hours or until reduced one-half.
5. Bottle without straining, then seal.
