## Ingredients

- mint
- 3 spoonfuls of mint
- 2 spoonfuls of brown sugar
- a teacup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the mint well and strip the leaves from the stalk
2. Chop it fine and put it in the sauce tureen
3. Add to it 3 spoonfuls of mint, 2 of brown sugar, and a teacup of vinegar
4. Stir it all well together
5. Make it half an hour before dinner
6. If not all used it can be put away for the next day to eat it with the cold lamb
