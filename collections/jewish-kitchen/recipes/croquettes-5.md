## Ingredients

- cold poultry or veal
- mushrooms
- two eggs
- fine bread crumbs or pounded vermicelli
- clarified dripping or melted veal fat

## Directions

1. Pound very fine cold poultry or veal and make it into delicate forcemeat
2. Vary the flavour according to taste; with poultry a few mushrooms chopped will improve it
3. Form the forcemeat into balls round or the shape of olives, the size of small eggs
4. Beat up two eggs and dip the balls well into the eggs
5. Dip into fine bread crumbs or pounded vermicelli
6. Fry them in clarified dripping or melted veal fat to a handsome brown colour
