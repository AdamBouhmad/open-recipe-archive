## Ingredients

- beets
- cold water

## Directions

1. Place beets in a stone crock, removing greens.
2. Cover with cold water and put in a warm place and let stand for three or four weeks or until the mixture becomes sour.
3. This is used as a vinegar during Pesach and to make beet soup, Russian style.
