## Ingredients

- rye bread
- mustard
- caviar
- smoked salmon
- pickle

## Directions

1. Take a piece of rye bread and cut round with a biscuit cutter.
2. Spread with mustard.
3. Put some caviar in centre of the bread.
4. Place strips of smoked salmon around the caviar and strips of pickle around the salmon.
