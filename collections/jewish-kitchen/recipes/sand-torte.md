## Ingredients

- one-half pound of butter
- one-half pound of sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- one small wine glass of rum
- one-fourth pound of corn-starch
- one-fourth pound of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Cream one-half pound of butter with one-half pound of sugar
2. Drop in, one at a time, the yolks of six eggs
3. Add one small wine glass of rum
4. Add one-fourth pound of corn-starch and one-fourth pound of flour that have been thoroughly mixed
5. Add one teaspoon of baking-powder
6. Fold in the beaten whites of six eggs
7. Bake one hour in a moderate oven
