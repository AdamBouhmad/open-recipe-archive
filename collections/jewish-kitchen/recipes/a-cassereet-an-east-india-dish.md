## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- water or gravy
- pepper
- salt
- cassereet
- rice

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of lamb chops
2. Place them in a stewpan
3. Cover with water or gravy
4. Season only with pepper and salt
5. When the chops are half done, carefully skim off the fat
6. Add two table spoonsful of cassereet
7. Stir it in the gravy which should not be thickened
8. Finish stewing gently till done enough
9. Rice should accompany this dish
