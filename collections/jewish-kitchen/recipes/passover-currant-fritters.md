## Ingredients

- biscuit powder
- beaten eggs
- well-washed and dried currants
- sweet sauce
- wine or shrub
- moist sugar

## Directions

1. Mix a thick batter, add some well-washed and dried currants, and fry of a rich brown
2. Serve with a sweet sauce, flavored with wine or shrub, and sweetened with moist sugar
3. These are often made in the shape of small balls, and fried and served in the same sauce
