## Ingredients

- cabbage
- fat
- one-fourth of a loaf of bread
- water
- one-half cup of flour
- one-half cup of brown sugar
- one-eighth pound of raisins
- finely chopped citron
- one-fourth pound of almonds
- bitter almonds
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- cinnamon
- allspice
- juice and peel of one lemon
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Chop up cabbage and let stew in fat slowly until quite brown, doing this the day previous to using
2. Next day mix in with the stewed cabbage one-fourth of a loaf of bread soaked in water and squeezed dry
3. Add one-half cup of flour, one-half cup of brown sugar, one-eighth pound of raisins, some finely chopped citron, one-fourth pound of almonds mixed with a few bitter almonds, one-half teaspoon of salt, some cinnamon and allspice about a teaspoon, juice and peel of one lemon and four eggs
4. Mix all thoroughly
5. Pour into well-greased iron pan (kugel pot) and bake slowly
