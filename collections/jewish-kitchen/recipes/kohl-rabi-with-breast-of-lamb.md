## Ingredients

- young kohl-rabi leaves
- salt
- water
- breast of mutton or lamb
- ginger
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Strip off the young leaves and boil in salt water
2. Peel the heads thickly, cut into round, thin slices, and lay in cold water for an hour
3. Put on to boil a breast of mutton or lamb, which has been previously well salted
4. Spice with a little ground ginger
5. When the mutton has boiled one-half hour add the sliced kohl-rabi
6. Boil covered
7. In the meantime, drain all the water from the leaves, which you have boiled separately, and chop them, but not too fine
8. Add them to the mutton
9. When done thicken with flour
10. Season with pepper and more salt if needed
11. You may omit the leaves if you are not fond of them
