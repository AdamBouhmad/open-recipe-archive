## Ingredients

- ripe apricots
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Halve and pare ripe apricots, or if not quite ripe, boil them till the skin can easily be removed
2. Lay them in a dish hollow downwards
3. Sift over them their own weight of white sugar
4. Let them lay for some hours
5. Put the fruit, with the sugar and juice into a preserving pan
6. Simmer till the fruit is clear
7. Take it out and put it carefully into pots
