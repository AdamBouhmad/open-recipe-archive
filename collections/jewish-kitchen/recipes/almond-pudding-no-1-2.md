## Ingredients

- whites of seven eggs
- yolks of ten eggs
- one-half pound of pulverized sweet almonds
- one-half ounce of pounded bitter almonds
- one-half pound of powdered sugar
- one tablespoon of orange-flower water
- olive oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well with the orange water
2. Add the sugar and almonds gradually
3. Beat all for one hour or until it bubbles
4. Grease deep pie-dishes with olive oil and pour in the mixture
5. Bake in a rather moderate oven
6. When the mixture is set and browned place over them a paper greased with olive oil to prevent them getting dark
7. Serve cold
8. Sprinkle powdered sugar freely over the pudding before serving
