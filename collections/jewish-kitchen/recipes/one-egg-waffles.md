## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of flour
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- one and three-fourths cups of milk
- one well-beaten egg
- two tablespoons of melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix one and one-half cups of flour, one teaspoon of baking powder, one-quarter teaspoon of salt
2. Add one and three-fourths cups of milk, add the milk slowly
3. Add one well-beaten egg and two tablespoons of melted butter
4. Drop by spoonfuls on a hot buttered waffle iron, putting one tablespoon in each section of the iron
5. Bake and turn, browning both sides carefully
6. Remove from the iron
7. Pile one on top of the other and serve at once
