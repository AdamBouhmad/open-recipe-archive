## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar
- peach kernels
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Weigh the peaches after they are pared and pitted
2. Allow a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit
3. Cook the peaches alone until soft
4. Add one-half of the sugar and stir frequently
5. In half an hour put in the remaining sugar
6. Watch carefully, stirring almost constantly for two hours
7. Boil slowly and add one-quarter of the peach kernels
8. Spice with cinnamon and cloves, using whole spices
