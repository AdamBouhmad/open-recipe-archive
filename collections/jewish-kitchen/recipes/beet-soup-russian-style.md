## Ingredients

- one large beet
- one-half pound of onions
- one pound of fat brisket of beef
- water
- three-fourths of a cup of sugar
- citric acid
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut one large beet and one-half pound of onions in thick pieces
2. Put in kettle with one pound of fat brisket of beef
3. Cover with water and let cook slowly two hours
4. Add three-fourths of a cup of sugar and a little citric acid to make it sweet and sour
5. Let cook another hour
6. Season and serve hot
