## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of olive oil
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one teaspoon of salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- six hot potatoes
- two small onions
- garlic
- parsley
- six stuffed olives
- three hearts of celery
- six radishes
- three slices of red beets
- two hard-boiled eggs
- mayonnaise dressing
- whole olives
- lettuce
- celery leaves

## Directions

1. Put into a bowl two tablespoons of olive oil, one tablespoon of sugar, one teaspoon of salt, some pepper and one tablespoon of vinegar and mix all together
2. Cut into this in slices six hot potatoes
3. Cut into small pieces two small onions, a little garlic, some parsley, six stuffed olives, three hearts of celery, six radishes, three slices of red beets and two hard-boiled eggs
4. Add this to the gravy in the bowl, mix well, and season to taste
5. Put all into a glass dish and pour over this a prepared mayonnaise dressing
6. Decorate with parsley, olives (whole), some lettuce and put in the centre some celery leaves
