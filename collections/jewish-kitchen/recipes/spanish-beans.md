## Ingredients

- two cups of beans
- one onion
- two cloves of garlic
- one tablespoon of olive oil
- one-half can of tomatoes
- one teaspoon chili powder
- salt to taste
- half a dozen olives
- smoked beef or tongue (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak two cups of beans overnight
2. Drain and boil until the skin cracks, and let one cup of water remain on the beans
3. Chop fine one onion and two cloves of garlic and fry a light brown in one tablespoon of olive oil
4. Add one-half can of tomatoes, one teaspoon chili powder dissolved in a little cold water, salt to taste and half a dozen olives chopped
