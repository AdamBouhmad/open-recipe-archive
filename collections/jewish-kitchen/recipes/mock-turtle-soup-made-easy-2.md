## Ingredients

- a bullock's foot
- two quarts of water
- salt, pepper
- 2 cloves
- 2 allspice
- a few sticks of marjoram, thyme, parsley
- a large carrot
- a tooth of garlic

## Directions

1. Put the bullock's foot in a good size saucepan with two quarts of water
2. Add salt, pepper, 2 cloves, 2 allspice
3. Add a few sticks of marjoram, thyme, parsley
4. Add a large carrot, a tooth of garlic
