## Ingredients

- small eggplants
- lemon juice
- parsley
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Use small eggplants
2. Place on end of toasting fork under broiler gas flame until the peel is black
3. Remove the skin
4. The eggplant will then be tender
5. Chop with wooden spoon
6. Add lemon juice, parsley chopped fine, and olive oil
