## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half cup of blanched and pounded almonds
- grated peel of one lemon
- juice of one lemon
- one-half pound of grated potatoes that have been boiled the day before
- beaten egg whites
- salt

## Directions

1. Stir the yolks of four eggs with one-half cup of sugar.
2. Add one-half cup of blanched and pounded almonds.
3. Grate in the peel, also the juice of one lemon.
4. Add one-half pound of grated potatoes that have been boiled the day before.
5. Lastly add the stiffly beaten whites, some salt and more potatoes, if necessary.
6. Grease your pudding-pan well, pour in the mixture and bake.
7. Set in a pan of water in oven; water in pan must not reach higher than one-half way up the pudding-form.
8. Bake one-half hour.
9. Turn out on platter and serve with a wine, chocolate, or lemon sauce.
