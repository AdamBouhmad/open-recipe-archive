## Ingredients

- four ounces vermicelli or maccaroni
- one pint of milk
- raisins
- sweet pudding sauce

## Directions

1. Boil till tender four ounces of either of the above articles, in a pint of milk
2. flavor as directed in the preceding receipt
3. boil in a mould, which may be lined with raisins
4. serve with any sweet pudding sauce
