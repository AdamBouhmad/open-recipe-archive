## Ingredients

- large sprats
- flour
- eggs
- boiling oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Choose some very large sprats and wash them well in 3 or 4 waters
2. Take off the heads and split them open by running your thumb up the centre
3. Take out the bone carefully
4. Wash them well when boned and lay them in salt for ½ an hour
5. When you wash off the salt, lay each one separately in a very clean cloth
6. When well dried, put two sprats together, dip them in flour, then in eggs
7. Fry them in boiling oil until a nice colour
8. Put them on a dish for the oil to drain if to be eaten cold; if hot, send them up on a fish napkin in rows one above the other, garnished with layers of parsley
