## Ingredients

- plums
- almonds
- best wine vinegar
- water
- sugar
- whole cinnamon
- cloves
- allspice

## Directions

1. Wash the plums, remove the stones and in place of the stones put in almonds
2. Take the best wine vinegar, water and sugar to taste
3. Tie in a bag some whole cinnamon, cloves, and allspice
4. Boil together with vinegar
5. After boiling, let it get lukewarm, then pour over the prunes
6. Let stand, and each day for nine days let vinegar come to a boil and pour over prunes
7. The last day cook the vinegar down some, then put in the prunes and let come to a boil
8. Keep in a stone or glass jar
