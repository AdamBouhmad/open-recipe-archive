## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. chuck steak
- a small piece of onion
- a little parsley
- a little marjoram
- a few crumbs of stale bread
- a little salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- a small piece of lemon peel chopped
- an egg

## Directions

1. Chop very fine 1/2 lb. chuck steak, a small piece of onion, a little parsley, a little marjoram, a few crumbs of stale bread, a little salt, pepper, nutmeg, a small piece of lemon peel chopped
2. Beat up an egg, use only half to mix these balls
3. Mix all the ingredients well, form them into very small balls, sometimes round and sometimes in the shape of small olives
4. Use them as you require
