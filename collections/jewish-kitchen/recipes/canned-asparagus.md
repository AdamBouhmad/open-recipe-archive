## Ingredients

- canned asparagus
- cold water
- boiling water
- hot platter
- salad dressing or Hollandaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Open one end of the can, as indicated on wrapper, so tips will be at opening.
2. Pour off the liquid and allow cold water to run over gently and to rinse.
3. Drain and pour boiling water over them in the can and set in a hot oven to heat thoroughly.
4. When ready to serve, drain and arrange carefully on hot platter and serve same as fresh asparagus, hot on toast or cold with salad dressing, or with Hollandaise Sauce poured over.
