## Ingredients

- pint of milk
- lemon-peel
- bay leaf
- yolks of four eggs
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Let a pint of milk simmer in a very clean saucepan
2. Flavor it with lemon-peel and a bay leaf
3. Sweeten to taste
4. Strain the milk
5. While gently boiling, add the beaten yolks of four eggs and the whites of two
6. Continue stirring until the custard thickens
7. Remove from the fire
8. Stir it until it cools
