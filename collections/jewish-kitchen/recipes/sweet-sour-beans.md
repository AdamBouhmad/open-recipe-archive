## Ingredients

- canned string beans
- fat
- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of brown sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- juice of pickled peaches or pears

## Directions

1. Heat some fat in a spider
2. Put in one tablespoon of flour and brown slightly
3. Add one tablespoon of brown sugar, a pinch of salt, some cinnamon and vinegar to taste
4. Add the beans and let them simmer on the back of stove
5. Do not let them burn
6. The juice of pickled peaches or pears is delicious in preparing this dish
