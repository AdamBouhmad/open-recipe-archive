## Ingredients

- mutton
- salt
- ground ginger
- cold water
- carrots
- potatoes
- fat
- flour
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- gravy

## Directions

1. Salt the mutton on both sides, adding a little ground ginger.
2. Put on to boil in cold water, cover up tightly and stew slowly.
3. In the meantime pare and cut up the carrots, add these and cover up again.
4. Pare and cut up about half a dozen potatoes into dice shape and add them three-quarters of an hour before dinner.
5. Cover up again, and when done, make a sauce as follows: Skim off about two tablespoons of fat from the mutton stew, put this in a spider and heat.
6. Brown a tablespoon of flour in the fat.
7. Add a heaping tablespoon of brown sugar, some cinnamon and pour the gravy of the stew into the spider.
8. Let it boil up once, and then pour all over the carrots and stew until ready to serve.
