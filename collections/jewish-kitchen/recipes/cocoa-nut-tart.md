## Ingredients

- a cocoa-nut
- 1 lb. very fine grated cocoa-nut
- a 1/2 lb. of castor sugar
- a 1/2 lb. of the clarified veal fat
- a tablespoonful of brandy
- 4 drops essence of vanilla
- the whites of 6 eggs
- good puff paste
- pounded sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Break a cocoa-nut, cut the brown skin off, cut the nut in pieces and put them in cold water
2. When ready to make the tart take it out of the water, dry it well
3. Grate 1 lb. very fine into a plate
4. Put in a 1/2 lb. of castor sugar, 1/2 lb. of the clarified veal fat well beaten
5. Stir the sugar and fat together till it comes like a cream
6. Add a tablespoonful of brandy, 4 drops essence of vanilla
7. Beat up the whites of 6 eggs well
8. Add gradually the fat and sugar, keep stirring it
9. Then add gradually the grated cocoa-nut
10. Line a pie dish with good puff paste
11. Put in the mixture, crossbar the top
12. Put it in a moderate oven for 1/2 an hour
13. Sift over some pounded sugar and cinnamon
