## Ingredients

- one teacup full of walnut pickle
- one teacup full of mushroom pickle
- three anchovies
- one clove of garlic
- half a tea-spoonful of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Pound three anchovies
2. Pound one clove of garlic
3. Mix all ingredients well together
4. Bottle for use
