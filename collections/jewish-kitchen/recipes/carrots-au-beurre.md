## Ingredients

- carrots
- a piece of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Boil them enough to be perfectly tender, then cut them in quarters, and again in lengths of three inches
2. drain them from the water, and put to them a piece of butter, salt and pepper
3. simmer them for a few minutes without boiling; a large piece of butter must be used
