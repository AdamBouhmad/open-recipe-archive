## Ingredients

- a little more than one pint of sweet milk
- a heaping tablespoon of butter
- a little nutmeg
- one cup of stewed prunes
- cream

## Directions

1. Heat a little more than a pint of sweet milk to the boiling point.
2. Stir in gradually a little cold milk in which you have rubbed smooth a heaping tablespoon of butter and a little nutmeg.
3. Let this just come to a boil, then pour into a buttered pudding-dish, first adding one cup of stewed prunes with the stones taken out.
4. Bake for fifteen to twenty minutes, according to the state of oven.
5. A little cream improves it when it is served in the saucers.
