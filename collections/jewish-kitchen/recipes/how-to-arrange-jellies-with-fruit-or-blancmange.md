## Ingredients

- calf's-foot jelly or jelly made from isinglass
- almond oil
- fresh strawberries or bunches of currants

## Directions

1. Rub the mould very slightly with 2 or 3 drops of almond oil, do it with a small piece of clean rag
2. Pour in some of the jelly just to take the pattern at the top of the mould
3. Set it to get cold; when set, take some fresh strawberries or bunches of currants
4. Wipe them well, see that they are quite clean; fix the fruit into the jelly lightly, so that they hang gracefully
5. Pour over another layer of warm jelly enough to cover them, let that set
6. Put in another layer of fruit, not too many
7. When this is quite set, fill your mould with the jelly, leave it in a cool place till you are ready for it
8. Before you turn it out just loosen it gently all round with a clean cloth, only the top
9. Wring a cloth out of boiling water quite dry, pass it all over the mould
10. Turn the mould on a dish, it will easily slip out
