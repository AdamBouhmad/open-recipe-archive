## Ingredients

- banana
- fresh or canned pineapple
- lemon or pineapple juice
- autumn leaves or sprays of green fern

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of banana and fresh or canned pineapple
2. cut into small cubes and cover with lemon or pineapple juice
3. Serve in glasses or orange shells placed on autumn leaves or sprays of green fern
