## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Break into a bowl as many eggs as required, add salt and pepper.
2. Have some very hot butter in the frying-pan on the stove; pour in the eggs, stir constantly until set, not stiff, and serve on a hot platter at once.
