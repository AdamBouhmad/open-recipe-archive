## Ingredients

- mashed cream cheese
- butter
- cream
- a little paprika
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Take mashed cream cheese
2. Add butter, cream and a little paprika
3. Chop either green peppers, almonds or olives in this mixture, or add the juice of an onion
4. Roll into small balls
5. Serve on lettuce leaves
