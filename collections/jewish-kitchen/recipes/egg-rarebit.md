## Ingredients

- cream sauce
- one-half pound American and Swiss cheese mixed or American alone
- three hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Make a cream sauce
2. Grate one-half pound American and Swiss cheese mixed, or American alone
3. Add to the sauce
4. Chop three hard-boiled eggs and add to the sauce
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Serve on buttered toast
