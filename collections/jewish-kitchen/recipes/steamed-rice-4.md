## Ingredients

- two cups of rice
- eight cups of cold water
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Wash two cups of rice carefully
2. Put in double boiler
3. Add eight cups of cold water and a pinch of salt
4. Steam for two hours; do not stir
5. Serve with any kind of stewed fruit or preserve
