## Ingredients

- small piece of mutton, shoulder or fillet of the leg
- beef gravy
- herbs
- carrot
- turnip
- Spanish onions

## Directions

1. Take a small piece of mutton, either part of a shoulder or a fillet of the leg
2. partly roast it
3. put it in a stewpan with beef gravy enough to cover it
4. previously season with herbs, a carrot and turnip
5. cut in quarters three large Spanish onions, and place in the stewpan round the meat
6. a stuffing will improve it
7. take care to free the gravy from every particle of fat
