## Ingredients

- one-half peck tart apples
- one quart cranberries
- cold water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cover with cold water and cook an hour.
2. Strain through a jelly-bag without squeezing.
3. There should be about three pints of juice.
4. Use a bowl of sugar for each bowl of juice.
5. When the juice is boiling add sugar which has been heated in oven and boil twenty minutes.
6. Skim and pour into glasses.
