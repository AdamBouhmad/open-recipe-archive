## Ingredients

- cinnamon
- ginger
- cloves
- water
- wine
- sugar
- nutmeg
- a strip or two of orange rind

## Directions

1. Boil a little spice, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves in water till the flavor is gained
2. Add wine as much as may be approved
3. Add sugar and nutmeg
4. A strip or two of orange rind cut thin is a great improvement
