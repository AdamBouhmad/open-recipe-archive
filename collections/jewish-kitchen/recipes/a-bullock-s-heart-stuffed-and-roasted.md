## Ingredients

- 1 bullock's heart
- stuffing (as referenced in previous recipe)
- salt
- pepper
- gravy
- 1/2 pound rice
- dried saffron

## Directions

1. Wash well after soaking, removing every particle of blood
2. Dry well on a clean coarse cloth
3. Fill with the same stuffing as the above
4. Put down to roast and baste well
5. Roast for 1 hour and 1/4
6. Boil half pound of rice with salt, pepper, and a little dried saffron
7. Drain rice well and make a bed of it in the dish
8. Lay the heart on the rice
9. Pour good gravy over it when dished up
10. Serve very hot
