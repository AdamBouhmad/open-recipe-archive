## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of stale bread
- 2 oz. of currants
- 2 oz. of raisins
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 2 oz. of melted fat
- grated nutmeg
- cinnamon
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup of water
- orange or lemon peel
- almonds

## Directions

1. Soak a piece of stale bread, drain it well from the water
2. Beat the bread with a fork
3. Add to it the currants, raisins, sugar, melted fat, nutmeg and cinnamon
4. Beat it up with the eggs and water
5. Grease a dish and place it in
6. Cut a few pieces of orange or lemon peel and a few almonds and put them on the outside with the peel
