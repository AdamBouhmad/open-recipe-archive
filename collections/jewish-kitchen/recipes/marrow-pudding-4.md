## Ingredients

- Half-pound of marrow
- 1/2 lb. sifted sugar
- 1/2 lb. of ground almonds
- 2 oz. of candied lemon and orange peel
- orange-flower water
- 8 eggs
- good puff paste

## Directions

1. Beat marrow to a froth
2. Mix in 1/2 lb. sifted sugar, 1/2 lb. of ground almonds
3. Cut up 2 oz. of candied lemon and orange peel
4. Flavour with orange-flower water
5. Beat in 8 eggs, each one beat in separately
6. Line a dish with good puff paste
7. Lay in the mixture, crossbar
8. Bake it in a quick oven
