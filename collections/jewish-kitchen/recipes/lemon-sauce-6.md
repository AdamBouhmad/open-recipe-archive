## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of water
- juice of two lemons
- sugar
- cinnamon bark
- three teaspoons of cornstarch
- cold water
- yolks of two eggs
- beaten whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Put on to boil one and one-half cups of water with juice of two lemons, sweeten to taste, add a few small pieces of cinnamon bark.
2. When boiling stir in three teaspoons of cornstarch that have been dissolved in a little cold water.
3. Boil a few minutes, then pour over the well-beaten yolks of two eggs, stirring all the time.
4. Stir in stiffly-beaten whites of eggs, and pour over and around puffs when cold.
5. Serve cold.
