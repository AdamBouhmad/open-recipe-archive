## Ingredients

- one quart carrots
- one quart boiling water
- one teaspoon salt
- two tablespoons fat
- one small onion
- one teaspoon sugar
- one-quarter teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon white pepper
- one and one-half cups soup stock
- one teaspoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape, slice and cook one quart of carrots in one quart of boiling water to which has been added one teaspoon of salt, until tender; drain.
2. Heat two tablespoons fat, add one small onion, brown lightly, add the carrots, season with one teaspoon of sugar, one-quarter teaspoon of salt, one-eighth teaspoon of white pepper and shake well over the fire for ten minutes.
3. Add one and one-half cups of soup stock, cover and simmer for one-half hour.
4. Add one teaspoon chopped parsley and serve hot.
