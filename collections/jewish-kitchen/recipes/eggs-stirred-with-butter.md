## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- an ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Break as many eggs as you require in a small basin
2. Beat them well with salt and pepper to taste
3. Have in a small saucepan an ounce of butter
4. Warm it well
5. Turn in the eggs
6. Keep stirring them with a fork
7. Dish up on a hot dish
8. Have hot plates ready
