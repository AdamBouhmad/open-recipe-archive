## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of flour
- one-half teaspoon of baking-powder
- a generous half cup of oil
- an exact half cup of very cold (or ice) water
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Sift into a mixing-bowl one and one-half cups of flour and one-half teaspoon of baking-powder
2. Make a depression in the centre; into this pour a generous half cup of oil and an exact half cup of very cold (or ice) water
3. Add pinch of salt, mix quickly with a fork
4. Divide in two portions; do not knead
5. Roll on a well-floured board, spread on pans, fill and bake at once in a quick oven
6. Instead of pinching or crimping the edge, trim the edges in the usual way, then place the palms of the hand on opposite sides of the pie and raise the dough until the edges stand straight up
