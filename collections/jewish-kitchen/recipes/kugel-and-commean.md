## Ingredients

- one pint of Spanish peas
- one pint of Spanish beans
- three pints of water
- two marrow bones
- a calf's-foot
- three pounds of fine gravy-beef
- one pound of flour
- half a pound of shred suet
- a little grated nutmeg
- ground ginger
- cloves
- allspice
- one pound of coarse brown sugar
- the crumb of a slice of bread

## Directions

1. Soak one pint of Spanish peas and one pint of Spanish beans all night in three pints of water
2. take two marrow bones, a calf's-foot, and three pounds of fine gravy-beef
3. crack the bones and tie them to prevent the marrow escaping
4. put all together into a pan
5. take one pound of flour, half a pound of shred suet, a little grated nutmeg and ground ginger, cloves and allspice, one pound of coarse brown sugar, and the crumb of a slice of bread, first soaked in water and pressed dry
6. mix all these ingredients together into a paste
7. grease a quart basin and put it in, covering the basin with a plate set in the middle of the pan with the beans, meat, &c.
8. Cover the pan lightly down with coarse brown paper
9. let it remain all the night and the next day (until required) in a baker's oven
10. when done, take out the basin containing the pudding, and skim the fat from the gravy which must be served as soup
11. The pudding must be turned out of the basin, and a sweet sauce flavored with lemon and brandy is a fine addition
