## Ingredients

- 2 large Spanish onions
- 1 lb. of chopped meat
- salt
- pepper
- a little ground mace
- the juice of a lemon
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Boil 2 large Spanish onions and lay them on a plate
2. Open them leaf by leaf
3. Make up 1 lb. of chopped meat according to recipe for forced meat
4. Form it into balls the size of a small apple
5. Roll each ball in one of the leaves
6. Chop an onion in the stewpan with salt, pepper, and a little ground mace
7. Let it boil and put in the balls, keeping the liquor very short
8. When done, stir in the juice of a lemon with a little browning
