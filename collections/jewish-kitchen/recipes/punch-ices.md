## Ingredients

- juice of two lemons
- three-quarters of a pound of loaf sugar
- two or three tablespoons of rum
- one pint of water
- rind of the lemons

## Directions

1. Rub the rind of the lemons onto the sugar
2. Boil the sugar and water together for fifteen minutes
3. Add the lemon juice and rum
4. Mix well
5. Strain and set aside to cool
6. Put the mixture into the freezing can and freeze till set
