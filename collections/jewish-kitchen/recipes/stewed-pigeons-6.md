## Ingredients

- pigeons
- chopped onion
- garlic
- marjoram
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- pickled walnut
- half a pint of water
- small meat balls
- 2 or 3 small mushrooms
- flour
- juice of half a lemon
- hard eggs

## Directions

1. Cut them in quarters, put them in a stewpan
2. Add chopped onion, garlic, marjoram, thyme, salt, pepper, a little cayenne, a pickled walnut cut in pieces, with half a pint of water
3. Let it stew till done, about half an hour if very young
4. Put in a very few small meat balls, and 2 or 3 small mushrooms chopped
5. When done rub in a basin a dessert spoonful of flour, the juice of half a lemon, and put it into the stew
6. Garnish with slices of hard eggs
