## Ingredients

- 8 pounds gooseberries
- 6 pounds sugar
- 3 cups water

## Directions

1. Cut the brush part from the berry, but leave the stem on
2. Wash thoroughly and let drip in colander overnight
3. Prepare a syrup of six pounds of sugar and three cups of water
4. When syrup has boiled till clear put in the berries and boil for three-quarters of an hour
5. Put in jars or glasses
