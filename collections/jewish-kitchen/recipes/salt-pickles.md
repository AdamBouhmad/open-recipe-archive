## Ingredients

- nice, large cucumbers
- coarse salt
- dill
- whole peppers
- grape leaves
- bay leaves
- water
- rye bread

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe cucumbers
2. Lay them in a jar or wooden pail
3. Sprinkle coarse salt over each layer
4. Add dill, whole peppers and grape leaves, if you have them, also a very few bay leaves
5. Cover with water up to the brim
6. Lay a piece of rye bread in the jar to help quicken the process of souring
7. Cover with a plate and put a clean, heavy stone on top of the plate to keep them well covered with the brine
8. Set them in a warm place, say back of the kitchen stove, for the first three days
9. They will be ready to use in a week
