## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of the best oil
- 3 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- a spoonful of mustard pickle

## Directions

1. Mix in a basin 2 tablespoonfuls of the best oil and 3 of vinegar
2. Add a little salt and pepper
3. Rub it well with a spoon till it comes like a cream
4. Chop a spoonful of mustard pickle and put them in your sauceboat
5. Turn the sauce over them
