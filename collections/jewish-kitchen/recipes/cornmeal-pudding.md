## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- one-half cup of Indian meal
- three eggs
- one tablespoon of flour
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half teaspoon of ginger
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- pinch of salt
- one pint cold milk
- hot maple sugar or cream

## Directions

1. Bring one pint of milk to the boiling point
2. Pour it gradually on one-half cup of Indian meal, stirring all the while to prevent lumps
3. When cool add three eggs well beaten, and one tablespoon of flour, one-half cup of sugar, one-half teaspoon of ginger, one teaspoon of cinnamon, pinch of salt and one pint cold milk
4. Pour into buttered pudding dish and bake an hour and a half
5. Serve with hot maple sugar or cream
