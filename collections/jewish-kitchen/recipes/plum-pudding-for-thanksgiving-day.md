## Ingredients

- a small loaf of bread
- one cup of suet
- yolks of six eggs
- one cup of currants
- one cup of raisins
- one-half cup of citron
- three-quarters cup of syrup
- one wineglass of brandy
- one cup of sifted flour
- whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Soak a small loaf of bread and press out every drop of water
2. Work into this one cup of suet shaved very fine, the yolks of six eggs, one cup of currants, one cup of raisins seeded, one-half cup of citron shredded fine, three-quarters cup of syrup, and one wineglass of brandy
3. Add one cup of sifted flour and the stiffly-beaten whites of eggs last
4. Boil four hours in greased melon mold
