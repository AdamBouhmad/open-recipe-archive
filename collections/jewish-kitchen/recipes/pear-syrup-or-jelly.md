## Ingredients

- pears
- coarse brown sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the pears in a saucepan over the fire until the pulp, skins, &c., have separated from the juice.
2. Strain the juice.
3. Boil the juice with coarse brown sugar to the thickness of treacle.
