## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Have the turnips peeled and sliced
2. Drop the slices into a stew-pan with boiling water enough to cover generously
3. Cook until tender, then drain well
4. Mash with a wooden vegetable masher as metal is apt to impart an unpleasant taste
5. Season with salt, butter, and a little pepper
6. Serve at once
