## Ingredients

- spinach
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- brown sugar
- vinegar
- poached eggs or small forcemeat fritters

## Directions

1. Scald and chop some spinach small
2. Cut up an onion
3. Add pepper and salt and brown sugar, with a little vinegar
4. Stew all together gently
5. Serve with poached eggs or small forcemeat fritters
