## Ingredients

- a cup of water
- vinegar
- an onion
- a little rasped horse-radish
- pepper
- two or three cloves
- a couple of anchovies
- the beaten yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Put on, in a small saucepan, a cup of water, well flavored with vinegar, an onion chopped fine, a little rasped horse-radish, pepper, and two or three cloves, and a couple of anchovies cut small
2. When it has boiled, stir carefully in the beaten yolks of two eggs, and let it thicken, until of the consistency of melted butter
