## Ingredients

- drippings or fat
- onion
- stale baker's bread
- cold water
- soup stock
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- finely chopped parsley
- fat
- egg

## Directions

1. In a fryer on the stove heat two tablespoons of drippings or fat
2. Drop in one-half onion cut fine and brown lightly
3. Add one-quarter loaf of stale baker's bread which has previously been soaked in cold water and then thoroughly squeezed out
4. Cook until it leaves the sides of the fryer, stirring occasionally
5. If too dry add a little soup stock
6. Remove from the fire, put in a bowl
7. Season with salt, pepper, ginger, and finely chopped parsley
8. Add a small lump of fat, break in one whole egg
9. Mix well and fill the fowl with it
