## Ingredients

- 3 biscuits
- 3 good sized apples
- 2 oz. of currants
- 2 oz. of stoned raisins
- a little cinnamon
- rind of lemon cut thin
- 1/2 lb. of moist sugar
- 2 oz. of melted fat
- 6 eggs
- wine or rum sauce or shrub
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak 3 biscuits and squeeze the water out well
2. Put them in a basin
3. Cut up 3 good sized apples in small thick pieces
4. Add 2 oz. of currants, 2 oz. of stoned raisins, a little cinnamon, some rind of lemon cut thin, 1/2 lb. of moist sugar, and 2 oz. of melted fat
5. Mix all well together with 6 eggs
6. Grease a dish and bake in a good oven
7. This pudding can be boiled if preferred
8. Serve with wine or rum sauce or shrub, remembering always to put lemon juice in the sauce
