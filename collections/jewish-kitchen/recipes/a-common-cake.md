## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- six ounces of butter
- two tea-spoonsful of yeast
- five eggs
- half a pound of sugar
- a quarter of a pint of milk
- currants
- carraways
- candied citron
- lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Rub in with one pound of flour six ounces of butter and two tea-spoonsful of yeast to a paste
2. Set it to rise
3. Mix in five eggs, half a pound of sugar, and a quarter of a pint of milk
4. Add currants or carraways and beat well together
5. If required to be richer, put more butter and eggs, and add candied citron and lemon-peel
