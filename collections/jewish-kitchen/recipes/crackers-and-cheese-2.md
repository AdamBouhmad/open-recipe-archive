## Ingredients

- Bent's water biscuits
- hot water
- melted butter
- French mustard
- grated cheese
- paprika or cayenne

## Directions

1. Split in two some Bent's water biscuits
2. Moisten them with hot water
3. Pour over each piece a little melted butter and French mustard
4. Spread with a thick layer of grated cheese
5. Sprinkle with paprika or cayenne
6. Place them in a hot oven until the cheese is soft and creamy
