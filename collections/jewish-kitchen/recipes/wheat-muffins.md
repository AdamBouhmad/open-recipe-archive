## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- three teaspoons of baking-powder
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one tablespoon of butter
- one egg
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of flour, one-half teaspoon of salt, three teaspoons of baking-powder, two tablespoons of sugar
2. Sift these ingredients twice
3. Rub in one tablespoon of butter
4. Separate one egg
5. Beat the yolk and add it to one cup of milk and one teaspoon of molasses
6. Mix with the dry ingredients
7. Stir until smooth
8. Fold in the beaten white of egg
9. Pour into hot, well-greased muffin-tins
10. Bake fifteen to twenty minutes in hot oven
