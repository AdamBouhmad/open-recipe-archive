## Ingredients

- One-half cup of raisins
- One-half cup of nuts
- One-half cup figs or dates
- Honey or corn syrup

## Directions

1. Chop coarsely one-half cup of raisins, one-half cup of nuts, one-half cup figs or dates
2. Add enough honey or corn syrup to make a stiff loaf, about two tablespoons
3. Place in ice-box for one hour
4. Slice and serve in place of candy, rolling each slice in cornstarch
