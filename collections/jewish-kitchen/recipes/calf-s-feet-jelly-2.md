## Ingredients

- two calf's feet
- two quarts or five pints of water
- lump sugar
- lemon juice
- white wine
- lemon peel
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Boil two feet in two quarts or five pints of water, till the water has half wasted
2. Strain, and when cold, take off the fat
3. Put it in the saucepan with lump sugar, lemon juice, and white wine to taste
4. Add a little lemon peel
5. When simmered a few minutes, throw in the whites of two eggs and their shells broken
6. Let it boil about ten minutes after the scum rises
7. Pour it through a flannel bag or thick cloth, dipping the bag or cloth first into hot water
8. Pass the jelly through it until clear
9. Pour it into moulds and put them in a cool place to set
