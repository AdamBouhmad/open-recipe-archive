## Ingredients

- one-half cup of rye bread crumbs
- yolks of four eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- a pinch of cloves
- allspice
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- grated rind of one-half lemon
- one-quarter pound of chopped almonds
- three tablespoons of whiskey or brandy
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Dry one-half cup of rye bread crumbs in oven
2. Beat the yolks of four eggs very light with one-half cup of sugar
3. Add a pinch of cloves and allspice, one-half teaspoon of cinnamon, grated rind of one-half lemon and one-quarter pound of chopped almonds
4. Moisten crumbs with three tablespoons of whiskey or brandy, add to eggs, then add stiffly-beaten whites of four eggs
5. Put in mold and boil three hours
6. Serve with a brandy or whiskey sauce
