## Ingredients

- one pint of rich thick cream
- one-quarter of a pound of powdered sugar
- one-half teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. To one pint of rich thick cream add one-quarter of a pound of powdered sugar and one-half teaspoon of vanilla.
2. Put in a large platter in a cool place and whip with a wire egg-whip until perfectly smooth and velvety.
3. Set on ice until wanted.
4. In the summer set the cream on ice before whipping.
5. A good plan is to set the bowl in another one filled with ice while whipping.
