## Ingredients

- butter
- crumbed bread
- dried English currants
- three eggs
- one pint of milk
- four tablespoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Brush small molds with butter, fill with crumbed bread and dried English currants
2. Beat three eggs without separating, add one pint of milk and four tablespoons of sugar
3. Pour carefully over the bread and let stand five minutes
4. Place molds in baking-pan of boiling water and bake in the oven thirty minutes, or steam half an hour
5. Serve with liquid pudding sauce
