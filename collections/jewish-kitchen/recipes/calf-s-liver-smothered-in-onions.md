## Ingredients

- goose fat
- onion
- calf's liver
- fine salt
- flour
- bay leaf
- five cloves
- two peppercorns
- hot water

## Directions

1. Heat some goose fat in a stew-pan with a close-fitting lid.
2. Cut up an onion in it and when the onion is of a light yellow color, place in the liver which you have previously sprinkled with fine salt and dredged with flour.
3. Add a bay leaf, five cloves and two peppercorns.
4. Cover up tight and stew the liver, turning it occasionally and when required adding a little hot water.
