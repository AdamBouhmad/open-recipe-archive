## Ingredients

- Two pounds of soup fat
- three pints of flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- two-thirds cup of granulated sugar
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- two teaspoons of vanilla
- flour
- beer
- One and one-half cups of brown sugar
- two tablespoons of honey
- two pounds of walnuts
- one pound of stewed prunes
- two cups of sponge cake crumbs
- juice of one lemon
- spices
- few raisins and currants
- a little citron
- a little wine
- a little chicken schmalz

## Directions

1. Render two pounds of soup fat a day or two before using
2. Mix three pints of flour, one teaspoon of salt, two-thirds cup of granulated sugar, one teaspoon of baking-powder, two teaspoons of vanilla
3. Knead well
4. Add enough beer to be able to roll
5. Let it stand two hours
6. Roll, cut in long strips three inches wide
7. Make filling: Mix one and one-half cups of brown sugar, two tablespoons of honey, two pounds of walnuts chopped fine, one pound of stewed prunes chopped fine, two cups of sponge cake crumbs, juice of one lemon, spices to taste, few raisins and currants, and a little citron chopped fine
8. Add a little wine, a little chicken schmalz
9. Heat a few minutes
10. You may use up remnants of jellies, jams, marmalades, etc.
11. Put plenty of filling in centre of strips
12. Fold over
13. With a round stick (use a wooden spoon), press the dough firmly three inches apart
14. Then with a knife cut them apart
15. Grease the pan and the top of cakes
16. Bake in moderate oven
