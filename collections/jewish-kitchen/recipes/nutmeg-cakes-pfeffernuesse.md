## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of pulverized sugar
- four eggs
- a piece of citron grated or chopped very fine
- the peel of a lemon
- one whole nutmeg grated
- one tablespoon of ground cinnamon
- one-half teaspoon of ground cloves
- half a teaspoon of allspice
- a heaping teaspoon of baking-powder

## Directions

1. Sift one pound of flour and one pound of pulverized sugar into a large bowl.
2. Add four eggs, a piece of citron grated or chopped very fine, also the peel of a lemon, one whole nutmeg grated, one tablespoon of ground cinnamon, one-half teaspoon of ground cloves, and half a teaspoon of allspice.
3. Mix all thoroughly in a deep bowl.
4. Sift a heaping teaspoon of baking-powder in with the flour.
5. Work into little balls as large as hickory nuts with buttered or floured hands.
6. Bake on waxed or buttered tins, an inch apart.
