## Ingredients

- 5 to 6 lbs. prime bola
- large onion
- small bunch of sage
- pepper
- salt
- soaked bread
- 2 oz. of suet
- egg
- dripping
- melted suet
- apple sauce
- potatoes
- flour
- boiled cabbage

## Directions

1. Take a piece of prime bola from 5 to 6 lbs., according to the family
2. Chop a large onion, a small bunch of sage, pepper, salt, a piece of soaked bread, 2 oz. of suet
3. Mix it all well together with an egg
4. Open the crevices of the meat, fill them up with the stuffing
5. Skewer them tight together, tie it round with string
6. Put it down to roast with some dripping, baste it well with melted suet
7. Serve it hot with some apple sauce
8. Parboil as many potatoes as you require, then put them in a tin dish
9. Take a few spoons of the fat from the dripping pan, put them in the oven to brown
10. Sprinkle over them salt, pepper, and a little flour
11. Serve with a good large boiled cabbage
12. Roast for 3 hours
