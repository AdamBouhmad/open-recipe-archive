## Ingredients

- a loaf of stale bread
- three eggs
- one quart berries
- brown sugar
- salt
- hard sauce

## Directions

1. Take a loaf of stale bread; cut off the crust and soak in cold water, then squeeze dry
2. Beat three eggs light, yolks and whites together, add one quart berries and mix all together with a little brown sugar and a pinch of salt
3. Boil steadily one hour, serve with hard sauce
