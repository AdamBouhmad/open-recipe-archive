## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling water
- salt
- bread

## Directions

1. Put on the pan with some boiling water
2. Add enough salt to season the water
3. Crack each egg separately into a teacup
4. Slip them into the pan
5. See that the water covers them
6. Have a round of bread toasted and laid in the dish at the side of the fire
7. Take out each egg separately and lay it on the toast
8. Each egg should look like a snowball
9. Lay them on the toast
10. Send up with hot plates
