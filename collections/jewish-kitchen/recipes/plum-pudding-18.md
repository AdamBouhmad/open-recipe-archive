## Ingredients

- Half lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. soaked bread
- 1 lb. well washed and dried currants
- 1 lb. well stoned raisins
- 1/2 lb. citron, orange, and lemon peel
- 1/2 oz. of mixed spice
- 1 lb. of finely chopped suet
- 2 oz. of almonds blanched and cut in long strips
- the rind of a fresh lemon
- 1/2 lb. of moist sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 glass of good ale
- a good wine or brandy sauce
- blanched almonds
- castor sugar

## Directions

1. Beat all well together for an hour
2. Grease a mould or basin well, and sift some sugar over it
3. Tie it in a cloth (if a mould it will have a cover)
4. Let it boil for 6 or 7 hours
5. Serve with a good wine or brandy sauce, have some almonds blanched and cut in equal sizes to stick it like a porcupine, or whip some whites of eggs to a snow with a little castor sugar, and flavoured with lemon, or put some rum or brandy in the dish, and light it at the door of the dining-room before it goes in
