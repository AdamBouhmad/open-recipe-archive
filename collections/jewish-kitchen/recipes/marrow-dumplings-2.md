## Ingredients

- one tablespoon marrow
- pinch of salt
- little nutmeg
- yolk of one egg
- finely chopped parsley
- matzoth meal

## Directions

1. One tablespoon marrow creamed.
2. Add a pinch of salt, little nutmeg and the yolk of one egg mixed in gradually; some finely chopped parsley and then enough matzoth meal to hold.
3. Wet the hands and roll the mixture into small balls.
4. Add to the boiling soup, and boil fifteen minutes.
