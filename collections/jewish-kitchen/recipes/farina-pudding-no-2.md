## Ingredients

- one and one-half pints of milk
- nine level tablespoons of sugar
- five bitter almonds chopped fine
- five sweet almonds chopped fine
- twelve level tablespoons of farina
- vanilla
- beaten whites of five eggs
- fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Bring one and one-half pints of milk with nine level tablespoons of sugar, five bitter and five sweet almonds chopped fine to boiling point
2. Drop in slowly twelve level tablespoons of farina and stir constantly
3. Cook for twelve minutes
4. Add vanilla to taste
5. Add slowly the beaten whites of five eggs
6. Put it in a form and when cold serve with a fruit sauce
