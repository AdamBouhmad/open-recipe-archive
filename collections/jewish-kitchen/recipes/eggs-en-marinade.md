## Ingredients

- water
- good meat gravy
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- two egg yolks
- half dozen poached eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Mix equal quantities of water and good meat gravy, two tablespoons each, with a teaspoon of vinegar and a seasoning of pepper and salt.
2. Put in a stew-pan and stir in gradually two well-beaten yolks of eggs.
3. When it thickens and before it boils, have ready a half dozen nicely poached eggs and pour the sauce over them.
4. Garnish with parsley.
