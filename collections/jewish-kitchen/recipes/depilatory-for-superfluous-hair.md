## Ingredients

- one ounce of finely powdered pumice-stone
- one ounce of powdered quick-lime

## Directions

1. Mix one ounce of finely powdered pumice-stone with one ounce of powdered quick-lime
2. rub the mixture on the part from which the hair is to be removed, twice in twenty-four hours
