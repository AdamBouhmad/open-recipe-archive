## Ingredients

- asparagus
- gravy made from beef, veal, or mutton
- salt
- white pepper
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Cut some asparagus into half inch lengths
2. Wash them
3. Throw them into half a pint of gravy made from beef, veal, or mutton thickened
4. Season with salt, white pepper, and a lump of white sugar
5. Fry the chops delicately and serve the sauce in the centre of the dish
