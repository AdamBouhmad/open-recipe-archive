## Ingredients

- three Neufchatel cheeses
- one-half cup of thick cream
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one rounding teaspoon of sugar
- preserved figs
- lettuce leaf

## Directions

1. Take three Neufchatel cheeses
2. Mash the cheese to a smooth paste
3. Add one-half cup of thick cream, one-half teaspoon of salt and one rounding teaspoon of sugar
4. Place in a small square mold
5. Bury in salt and ice and let stand several hours
6. When ready to serve unmold and cut in squares
7. Place each on a lettuce leaf
8. Decorate the centre of the cheese square with a preserved fig and serve at once
