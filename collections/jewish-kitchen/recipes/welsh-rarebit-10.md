## Ingredients

- good Cheddar cheese
- butter
- 1/2 teacup of ale or porter
- made mustard
- pepper
- hot toast

## Directions

1. Melt in the cheese tin some good Cheddar cheese with a small piece of butter
2. Add 1/2 teacup of ale or porter
3. Stir in a little made mustard and pepper
4. Have ready hot dry or buttered toast to send up with
