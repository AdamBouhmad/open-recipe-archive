## Ingredients

- two pounds of dough
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a couple of eggs

## Directions

1. Work into two pounds of dough a quarter of a pound of sugar and the same of butter
2. Add a couple of eggs
3. Bake in a tin
