## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- vinegar
- stock
- eggs
- flour
- essence of lemon
- lamb cutlets

## Directions

1. Take two fine cucumbers, peel and cut them lengthways
2. Lay them in vinegar for an hour
3. Stew them in good stock till tender
4. Stir in the yolks of two or three eggs, a little flour and essence of lemon, which must all be first mixed up together with a little of the stock
5. Have ready some cutlets trimmed and fried a light brown
6. Arrange them round the dish and pour the cucumbers in the centre
