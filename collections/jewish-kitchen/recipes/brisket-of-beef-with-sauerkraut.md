## Ingredients

- three pounds of fat, young beef
- salt
- one quart of sauerkraut
- vinegar
- grated raw potato
- brown sugar
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Take about three pounds of fat, young beef (you may make soup stock of it first), then take out the bones, salt it well and lay it in the bottom of a kettle.
2. Put a quart of sauerkraut on top of it and let it boil slowly until tender.
3. Add vinegar if necessary, thicken with a grated raw potato and add a little brown sugar.
4. Some like a few caraway seeds added.
