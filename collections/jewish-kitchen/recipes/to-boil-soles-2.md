## Ingredients

- soles
- salt
- water
- parsley
- caper or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Wash soles well and let them lay in salt for half an hour before boiling
2. Put them in the fish kettle, just cover them with water, lay them in with the white skin on top
3. 20 minutes will boil a good sized pair
4. As soon as the water boils skim it, and when you lift it see that all scum is washed off
5. Have a dish ready with a fish napkin, slip them off the drainer on to the napkin, garnish round with parsley
6. Serve with caper or anchovy sauce in the sauce tureen
