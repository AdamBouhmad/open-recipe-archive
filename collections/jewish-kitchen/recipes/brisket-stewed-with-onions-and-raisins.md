## Ingredients

- about five pounds of brisket of beef
- water
- allspice
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- four large onions
- half a pound of raisins
- a tea-spoonful of brown sugar
- a table spoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Stew about five pounds of brisket of beef in sufficient water to cover
2. Season with allspice, pepper, salt, and nutmeg
3. When nearly done, add four large onions cut in pieces and half a pound of raisins stoned
4. Let them remain simmering till well done
5. Just before serving, stir in a tea-spoonful of brown sugar and a table spoonful of flour
