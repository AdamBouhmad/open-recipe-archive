## Ingredients

- 1 pint of raspberries
- lemon
- loaf sugar
- 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 3 grated sponge cakes
- 2 oz. of melted veal fat
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Rub a pint of raspberries through a sieve
2. Rub the rind of a lemon on some loaf sugar and add to the raspberries 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar
3. Let it simmer thick
4. When cold beat the yolks of 8 eggs with 3 grated sponge cakes and 2 oz. of melted veal fat
5. Mix the whites of 3 eggs to a stiff snow
6. Mix gently together
7. Grease a mould and bake in a slow oven
