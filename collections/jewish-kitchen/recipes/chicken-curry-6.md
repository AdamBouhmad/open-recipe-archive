## Ingredients

- chickens
- flour
- hot fat
- one onion
- one tablespoon of curry powder
- three-fourths of a tablespoon of salt
- one tablespoon of wine vinegar
- boiling water
- steamed rice

## Directions

1. Cut chickens in pieces for serving
2. Dredge in flour and sauté in hot fat
3. Cut one onion in thin pieces
4. Add one tablespoon of curry powder, three-fourths of a tablespoon of salt and one tablespoon of wine vinegar
5. Add to chicken and cover with boiling water
6. Simmer until chicken is tender
7. Thicken sauce and serve with steamed rice
