## Ingredients

- very fresh lemons
- salt
- 2 or 3 teeth of garlic to each bottle
- 3 or 4 cloves
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Take some very fresh lemons
2. Cut them in quarters
3. Put them in glass bottles with plenty of salt, 2 or 3 teeth of garlic to each bottle, 3 or 4 cloves
4. Fill the bottle up with cold vinegar
5. Cork tight
