## Ingredients

- one pint of white haricot beans
- one pint of coarse barley
- fat meat
- marrow bone or backs of two fat geese
- ginger
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash one pint of white haricot beans and one pint of coarse barley
2. Put them into a covered pot or pan with some pieces of fat meat and some pieces of marrow bone, or the backs of two fat geese which have been skinned and well spiced with ginger and garlic
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Add sufficient water to cover
5. Cover the pot up tightly
6. Place in the oven on Friday afternoon and let remain there until Saturday noon
7. This takes the place of soup for the Sabbath dinner
