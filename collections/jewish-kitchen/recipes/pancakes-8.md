## Ingredients

- eggs
- flour
- milk or water
- boiling butter or clarified suet
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix a light batter of eggs with flour and milk or water
2. Fry in boiling butter or clarified suet
3. They may be fried without butter or fat, by putting more eggs and a little cream, the pan must be very dry and clean
4. Those fried without butter should be fried of the very lightest colour
5. They are good also made of rice, which must be boiled in milk till quite tender; then beat up with eggs, and flavoured according to taste, and fried like other pancakes
