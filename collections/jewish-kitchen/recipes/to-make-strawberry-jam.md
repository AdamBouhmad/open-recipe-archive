## Ingredients

- strawberries
- 1 lb. sugar to 1 lb. of strawberries

## Directions

1. Get the strawberries dry
2. Lay the quantity of fruit you intend to preserve the day before in an equal quantity of sugar
3. The next morning put it in the preserving-pan or a new saucepan
4. Stand it on a clear fire
5. Let it simmer for a little while
6. If you find too much syrup, drain it off, and boil it up, strain it, bottle it
7. Give it a quick boil till finished
8. Drop a little on a plate, and let it get cold
9. If you find it hard, put it in the jam pots
10. Let it stand till next day
11. Put on a little tissue paper steeped in rum or brandy
12. Cover them over with paper brushed over with white of egg
13. Keep in a dry place
