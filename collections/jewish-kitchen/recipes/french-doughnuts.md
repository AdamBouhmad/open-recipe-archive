## Ingredients

- one-half pint of water
- one-half pint of milk
- six ounces of butter
- one-half pound of flour
- six eggs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the butter, milk, and water
2. When it boils remove from the fire and stir in the flour, using a wooden spoon
3. When well mixed, stir in the eggs, whipping each one in separately until you have a hard batter
4. Pour your dough into a pastry bag with a small fluted or fancy tin funnel
5. Pour the paste on buttered paper, making into ring shapes
6. Fry in hot oil or butter substitute
7. Dust with powdered sugar
