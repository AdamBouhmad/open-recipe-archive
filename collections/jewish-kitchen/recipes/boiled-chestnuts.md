## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- salt
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the chestnuts a few minutes
2. Drain and remove the shells and skins
3. Boil again until tender, adding sufficient salt to make them palatable
4. Drain again
5. Shake over the fire until dry
6. Cover with cream sauce and serve at once
