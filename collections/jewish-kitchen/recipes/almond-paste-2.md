## Ingredients

- half a pound of fine almonds
- water
- half a pound of white sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of fine almonds and pound them to a paste
2. Add a few drops of water from time to time, or they become oily
3. Mix thoroughly with it half a pound of white sifted sugar
4. Put it into a preserving pan and let them simmer very gently until they become dry enough not to stick to a clean spoon when touched
5. Stir constantly
