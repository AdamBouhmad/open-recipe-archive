## Ingredients

- medium-sized potatoes
- two tablespoons butter or fat
- salt
- one teaspoon caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Boil medium-sized potatoes in their jackets until tender
2. Peel while hot
3. Put two tablespoons of butter or fat in spider
4. When hot add potatoes and brown well all over
5. Drain
6. Sprinkle with salt and one teaspoon of caraway seeds
7. Serve hot
