## Ingredients

- salsify
- butter
- cheese
- salt
- pepper
- milk or cream
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil and slice the salsify as in preceding recipe
2. Butter a baking dish
3. Fill it by adding alternate layers of salsify and small bits of cheese
4. Season with salt, pepper and butter
5. Pour over it a sufficient quantity of milk or cream to moisten thoroughly
6. Bake one-half hour
7. Bread crumbs may be added if desired
