## Ingredients

- one cup of powdered sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- one cup of sifted flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- three tablespoons of cold water
- one-half teaspoon of vanilla
- one tablespoon essence of mocha
- stiffly-beaten whites
- one-half pint of cream
- one tablespoon of mocha essence
- one and one-half tablespoon of powdered sugar
- pounded almonds

## Directions

1. Beat one cup of powdered sugar with the yolks of four eggs
2. When very light, add one cup of sifted flour in which has been mixed one teaspoon of baking-powder
3. Add three tablespoons of cold water
4. Add one-half teaspoon of vanilla
5. Add one tablespoon essence of mocha
6. Add the stiffly-beaten whites
7. Bake fifteen to twenty minutes in two layer pans in a moderate oven
8. Spread when cold with one-half pint of cream to which has been added one tablespoon of mocha essence, one and one-half tablespoon of powdered sugar and then well whipped
9. Garnish with pounded almonds
