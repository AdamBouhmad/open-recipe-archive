## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of French plums
- water
- 1 glass of port wine
- 4 ounces of loaf sugar
- lemon peel
- rich puff crust

## Directions

1. Stew 1 lb. of French plums until tender, in a very little water
2. When done add 1 glass of port wine and 4 ounces of loaf sugar
3. Put them into a pie dish
4. Line it and cover it with a rich puff crust
5. A little grated lemon peel adds to the flavour
6. Bake in a hot oven
