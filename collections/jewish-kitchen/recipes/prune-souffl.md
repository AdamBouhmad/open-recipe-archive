## Ingredients

- a large cup of stewed prunes
- whites of three eggs
- a half cup of sugar
- butter
- whipped cream
- cinnamon (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from a large cup of stewed prunes and chop fine.
2. Add the whites of three eggs and a half cup of sugar beaten to a stiff froth.
3. Mix well, turn into a buttered dish and bake thirty minutes in a moderate oven.
4. Serve with whipped cream.
5. If it is desired to cook this in individual cups, butter the cups, fill only two-thirds full, to allow for puffing up of the eggs, and set the cups in a pan of water to bake.
6. Some like a dash of cinnamon in this.
