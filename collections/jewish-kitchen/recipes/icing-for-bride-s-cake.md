## Ingredients

- whites of 4 fresh eggs
- 1 lb. of sifted sugar
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Break the whites of 4 fresh eggs in a deep basin
2. Mix in 1 lb. of sifted sugar
3. Add the juice of a lemon well strained
