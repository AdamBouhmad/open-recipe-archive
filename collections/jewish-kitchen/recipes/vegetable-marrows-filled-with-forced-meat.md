## Ingredients

- vegetable marrows
- salt
- onion
- two tablespoonfuls of oil
- pepper
- tomatoes
- cold meat
- two eggs
- lemon or two tablespoonfuls of good vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape lightly the vegetable marrows, wash them well
2. Put them on in a saucepan with boiling water and a little salt
3. Feel them with a fork, and when tender take them out carefully
4. Lay them on your pasteboard, divide them in halves lengthways
5. Scoop out the inside, let it drain well from the water
6. Slice in a deep pie dish an onion, with two tablespoonfuls of oil, a little salt and pepper
7. Put it in the oven to fry
8. If you have some tomatoes slice 2 or 3, and put with the onion
9. Take some cold meat, mince it like for the forcemeat balls, with the same seasoning
10. Put in a dish, mix it up with two eggs
11. Take the dish from the oven, lay the vegetable marrows in, do not put them too close
12. Fill them with the mixture you have just made
13. Beat in a plate two more eggs with the juice of a lemon, or two tablespoonfuls of good vinegar
14. Put it over the vegetable marrow, with a spoon
15. Put them in a rather brisk oven to bake until a nice light brown colour
16. Dish them up carefully
17. Put the eggs and lemon or vinegar custard round them and garnish with parsley
