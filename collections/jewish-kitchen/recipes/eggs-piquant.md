## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- half a cup of vinegar
- fresh eggs
- cold water
- two hard-boiled eggs
- one tablespoon of butter
- sugar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Pour into the kettle water to the depth of about one inch, adding salt and half a cup of vinegar
2. Bring to boil
3. Break in as many fresh eggs one at a time as desired, carefully so as not to break the yolks
4. As soon as the whites of the eggs are boiled, take up carefully with a perforated skimmer
5. Lay in cold water
6. Remove to a large platter
7. Strain the sauce the eggs were boiled in and set away
8. Rub or grate two hard-boiled eggs, yolks only
9. Add one tablespoon of butter rubbed very hard and also some sugar and part of the strained sauce
10. Boil up once and pour over the eggs
11. Garnish with parsley
