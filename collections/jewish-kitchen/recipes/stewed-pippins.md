## Ingredients

- pippins
- a little water
- white sugar
- a little lemon peel
- preserve

## Directions

1. Peel the pippins
2. Stew them gently with a little water, white sugar, and a little lemon peel
3. Use preserve to ornament the top of each apple
4. They should when done look white and rather transparent
