## Ingredients

- one ounce of pearl barley
- one ounce of sweet almonds
- a piece of lemon peel
- a little syrup of lemon and capillaire

## Directions

1. Wash and rinse extremely well one ounce of pearl barley
2. Put to it one ounce of sweet almonds beaten fine and a piece of lemon peel
3. Boil together till the liquor is of the thickness of cream and perfectly smooth
4. Put in a little syrup of lemon and capillaire
