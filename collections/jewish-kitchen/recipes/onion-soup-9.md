## Ingredients

- two or three large onions
- one tablespoon of butter
- three tablespoons of flour
- one pint of boiling water
- three good-sized potatoes
- one quart of scalded milk
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- croutons

## Directions

1. Slice two or three large onions
2. fry them in a tablespoon of butter until they are soft and red
3. then add three tablespoons of flour and stir until it is a little cooked
4. To this add slowly a pint of boiling water, stirring all the time, so it will be smooth
5. Boil and mash three good-sized potatoes
6. Add to them slowly a quart of scalded milk, stirring well so it will be smooth
7. Add the potato and milk mixture to the onion mixture
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Let it get very hot, and pass it through a strainer into the tureen
10. Sprinkle over the top a little parsley chopped very fine, and a few croutons
