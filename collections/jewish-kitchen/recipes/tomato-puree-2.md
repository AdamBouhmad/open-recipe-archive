## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald the tomatoes, take off the skins carefully and stew with one teaspoon each of butter and sugar; salt and pepper to taste.
2. When the tomatoes are very soft strain through a coarse sieve and if necessary thicken with one teaspoon of flour.
