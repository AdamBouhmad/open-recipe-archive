## Ingredients

- mixed pickles from No. 411
- best mustard, 1/2 lb.
- coarse brown sugar, 1/2 lb.
- cold vinegar
- salt
- ground marjoram
- lemon thyme
- fresh lemon rind, chopped
- dried saffron, a pennyworth

## Directions

1. Prepare a jar of mixed pickles and let it stand for 6 weeks
2. Take 1/2 lb. of the best mustard
3. Mix it in a basin with 1/2 lb. of coarse brown sugar and a little cold vinegar and salt
4. Pour off the whole of the jar of pickles that you had prepared
5. Let them drain through a sieve or colander
6. Put that vinegar in a clean saucepan with a pint more, some ground marjoram and lemon thyme, and a little chopped fresh rind of a lemon
7. Let it boil
8. Stir in the mixed mustard and sugar with a pennyworth of dried saffron
9. Let it boil, it will come quite thick
10. Let it cool
11. Return the pickles to the jar and pour this mixture over
12. Let the jar be uncovered till the next day
13. Tie it up and place it away for 6 weeks
