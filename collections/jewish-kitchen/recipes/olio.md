## Ingredients

- eight pounds of beef
- water to cover
- two fine white cabbages without any of the stalk or hard parts
- pepper
- salt
- a tea-spoonful of white sugar
- half a pound of chorisa

## Directions

1. Put eight pounds of beef in sufficient water to cover it
2. when the water boils take out the meat, skim off the fat
3. return the meat to the stew-pan, adding at the same time two fine white cabbages without any of the stalk or hard parts
4. season with pepper, salt, and a tea-spoonful of white sugar
5. let it simmer on a slow fire for about five hours
6. about an hour before serving, add half a pound of chorisa, which greatly improves the flavor
