## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- salted water
- chicken fat
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- soup stock

## Directions

1. Shell and blanch two cups of chestnuts
2. Cook in boiling salted water until tender
3. Drain and force through a colander or a potato ricer
4. Add one-fourth cup of melted chicken fat, one-fourth teaspoon of pepper, three-fourths of a teaspoon of salt, one cup of grated bread crumbs
5. Add enough soup stock to moisten
