## Ingredients

- beets
- water
- one pound of honey
- one pound of sugar
- white ginger
- one-quarter pound of almonds

## Directions

1. Cut beets in strips like noodles
2. Wash
3. Cook in water one-half hour
4. To three pounds of beets take one pound of honey and one pound of sugar
5. When the beets have cooked on slow fire until white, strain off and add the honey
6. Let cook well and add sugar
7. Cook
8. Add white ginger to taste, stirring continually
9. Add one-quarter pound of almonds, cut in slices
10. One-quarter of an hour before being done, mix
11. When the beets brown put in jars
