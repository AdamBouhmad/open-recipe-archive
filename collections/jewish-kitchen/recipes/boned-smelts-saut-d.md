## Ingredients

- smelts
- salt
- butter
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take a dozen raw smelts
2. Split them from the back lengthwise, leaving the head and tail intact
3. Take out the large center bone without opening the stomach and season with salt
4. Put four ounces of butter into a saucepan, and when quite hot place the smelts in it, so that the side which was cut open is underneath
5. When they have attained a nice color, turn them over and finish cooking
6. When ready, arrange them on a very hot dish, pour the butter in which they were cooked over them
7. Squeeze a little lemon on them, then add over all some finely chopped green parsley
8. Serve
