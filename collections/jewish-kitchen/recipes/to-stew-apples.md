## Ingredients

- 12 small pippins
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 pint of cold water
- orange-flower water
- marmalade

## Directions

1. Take 12 small pippins and peel them very thin
2. Lay them carefully in a stewpan with 1 lb. of loaf sugar, 1/2 pint of cold water, and a little orange-flower water
3. When the apples are nearly done, take them out and place them in a dish
4. When the syrup is cold, pour it over the apples
5. Place a little marmalade on each one
