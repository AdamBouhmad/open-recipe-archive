## Ingredients

- isinglass
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 oz. of castor sugar
- thin rind of a lemon
- a piece of cinnamon
- 1 pint of sherry

## Directions

1. Dissolve the isinglass as above
2. Beat the yolks of 4 eggs with 1/2 oz. of castor sugar
3. Boil in the isinglass the thin rind of a lemon and a piece of cinnamon
4. Skim it
5. Add to it the eggs and 1 pint of sherry
6. Grease your mould well
7. Put it in ice or a cool place
