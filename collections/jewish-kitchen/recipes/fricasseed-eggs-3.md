## Ingredients

- six hard-boiled eggs
- flour
- egg
- one-half teaspoon of oil
- one-half teaspoon of vinegar
- onion juice
- one teaspoon chopped parsley
- nutmeg
- salt
- vermicelli
- beef drippings

## Directions

1. Take six hard-boiled eggs and remove shells
2. Roll them in flour
3. Roll in egg to which has been added one-half teaspoon of oil, one-half teaspoon of vinegar, a few drops of onion juice, one teaspoon chopped parsley, a little nutmeg and salt
4. When quite covered, roll in vermicelli that has been broken into fine bits
5. Fry in deep beef drippings
