## Ingredients

- 4 good sized apples
- moist sugar
- 1 oz. dried currants
- 1 oz. raisins
- 1 oz. almonds chopped
- 2 oz. lemon and orange peel chopped
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel 4 good sized apples, cut them up rather small, put them in a stewpan, with moist sugar, 1 oz. of dried currants, 1 oz. of raisins, 1 oz. of almonds chopped, 2 oz. of lemon and orange peel chopped, a little cinnamon
