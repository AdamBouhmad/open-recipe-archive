## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one tablespoon of sugar
- four eggs
- two tablespoons of oil
- one pound of honey
- one-quarter pound of poppy seeds
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take two cups of flour, one tablespoon of sugar, add four eggs and two tablespoons of oil
2. Knead all these together
3. Roll out not very thin
4. Cut in squares
5. Close two sides
6. Prick with a fork so they will not blister
7. Put on tins and bake well
8. Take one pound of honey, boil, and put the squares in this and let boil a bit
9. Drop in one-quarter pound of poppy seeds and put back on fire
10. When nice and brown sprinkle with a little cold water
11. Take off and put on another dish so they do not stick to each other
