## Ingredients

- slices from a fine knuckle
- forcemeat
- potato or Jerusalem artichokes
- bread

## Directions

1. Take from a fine knuckle a couple of slices
2. cut and trim them in collops the size of a tea cup
3. flatten them and spread over each side a forcemeat for cutlets
4. fry them
5. serve with potato or Jerusalem artichokes cut in slices of the same size and thickness, or pieces of bread cut with a cutter
