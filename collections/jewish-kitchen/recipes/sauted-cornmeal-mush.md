## Ingredients

- left-over mush
- flour
- one-half teaspoon of drippings
- syrup

## Directions

1. Put left-over mush into a dish and smooth it over the top
2. When cold cut into slices one-half inch thick
3. Dip each slice into flour
4. Melt one-half teaspoon of drippings in a frying-pan and let it get smoking hot
5. Brown the floured slices on each side
6. Drain if necessary and serve on a hot plate with syrup
