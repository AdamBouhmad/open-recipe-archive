## Ingredients

- two cups of boiled squash
- half a cup of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- one egg
- two tablespoons of milk
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Use cold, left over portion of squash that has been prepared as a vegetable with seasoning and a little butter
2. Mix baking-powder with the flour
3. Add flour mixture to the squash
4. Add milk and stir all together
5. Beat egg and stir in
6. Have hot fat in pan
7. Drop fritters from spoon into pan
8. When browned on both sides remove to hot platter
