## Ingredients

- bananas
- sugar
- wine or orange juice
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Slice according to fancy, either round or lengthwise
2. Set on ice until required
3. Add sugar, wine or orange juice
4. In serving, dish out with a tablespoon of whipped cream
