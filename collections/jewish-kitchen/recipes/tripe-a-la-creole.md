## Ingredients

- tripe
- onion
- parsley
- celery
- seasoning
- one tablespoon of fat
- two tablespoons of flour
- one can of boiled and strained tomatoes
- one can of mushrooms
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil tripe with onion, parsley, celery, and seasoning.
2. Cut in small pieces.
3. Brown fat with two tablespoons of flour.
4. Add one can of boiled and strained tomatoes, one can of mushrooms, salt and pepper to taste.
5. Boil up the tripe in this sauce.
6. Serve in ramekins.
