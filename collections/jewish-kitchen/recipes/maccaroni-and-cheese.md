## Ingredients

- maccaroni
- milk or water
- butter
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil some maccaroni in milk or water until tender, then drain them and place on a dish with bits of butter and grated Parmesan cheese
2. when the dish is filled grate more cheese over it and brown before the fire
