## Ingredients

- six firm red tomatoes
- lettuce leaves
- salt
- pepper
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Take six firm red tomatoes, wash and wipe them neatly
2. Slice them in thin slices with a very sharp knife
3. Line a salad bowl with lettuce leaves
4. Lay the sliced tomatoes in
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Serve with French dressing
