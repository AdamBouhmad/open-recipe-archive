## Ingredients

- shoulder of lamb
- lemon juice
- grated bread crumbs
- egg
- parsley
- lemon pickle
- mint

## Directions

1. Half boil it
2. Score it and squeeze over lemon juice
3. Cover with grated bread crumbs, egg and parsley
4. Broil it over a clear fire and put it to brown in a Dutch oven, or grill
5. Serve with a sauce seasoned with lemon pickle and chopped mint
