## Ingredients

- thick fat of a fat goose
- salt
- cold water
- soft or linda fat

## Directions

1. Cut the thick fat of a fat goose in pieces as big as the palm of your hand
2. Roll together and run a toothpick through each one to fasten
3. Put a large preserve kettle on top of hot stove
4. Lay in the cracklings
5. Sprinkle a tiny bit of salt over them
6. Pour in a cup or two of cold water
7. Cover closely and let cook not too fast, until water is cooked out
8. Then add the soft or linda fat
9. Keep top off and let all brown nicely
10. About one to two hours is required to cook them
11. If you do not wish the scraps of Greben brittle, take them out of the fat before they are browned
12. Place strainer over fat crock, to catch the clear fat and let greben drain
13. If greben are too greasy place in baking-pan in oven a few minutes to try out a little more
14. Serve at lunch with rye bread
