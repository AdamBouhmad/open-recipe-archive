## Ingredients

- four pounds of small yellow or green tomatoes
- three pounds of sugar
- one-half cup boiling water
- juice and rind of two lemons

## Directions

1. Wash and dry four pounds of small yellow or green tomatoes and prick each one in five or six places
2. Stir three pounds of sugar in one-half cup boiling water until dissolved
3. Add the tomatoes and cook until clear
4. When half done add the juice and the rind of two lemons sliced very thin
5. When the fruit is clear remove it with a skimmer and put in small jars, filling them two-thirds full
6. Boil the syrup fast for a few minutes longer or until thick and syrupy, fill up the jars
7. Cover with a cloth until the next day then cover closely and stand away in a cool place
