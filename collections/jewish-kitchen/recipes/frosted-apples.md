## Ingredients

- six large apples
- water
- sugar
- egg
- lemon juice
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare and core six large apples
2. Cover with one pint of water and three tablespoons of sugar
3. Simmer until tender
4. Remove from the syrup and drain
5. Wash the parings and let simmer with a little water for one-half hour
6. Beat the white of one egg to a stiff froth and add one tablespoon of sugar
7. Coat the top of the apples lightly with the meringue
8. Place in a cool oven to dry
9. Strain the juice from the parings
10. Add two tablespoons of sugar
11. Return to the fire and let boil for five minutes
12. Add a few drops of lemon juice and a little nutmeg
13. Cool and pour around the apples
