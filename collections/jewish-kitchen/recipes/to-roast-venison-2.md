## Ingredients

- venison
- salt
- writing paper
- clarified fat
- flour
- water
- packthread
- white kitchen paper

## Directions

1. Wipe the venison dry
2. Sprinkle with salt
3. Cover with writing paper rubbed with clarified fat
4. Cover this with a thick paste made of flour and water
5. Tie with packthread white kitchen paper, so as to prevent the paste coming off
6. Set the venison before a strong fire
7. Baste it directly and continue until it is nearly done
8. Remove the paper, paste, etc.
9. Draw the venison nearer the fire
10. Dredge it with flour
11. Continue basting
12. It should only take a light brown, and should be rather under than over-done
13. A large haunch requires from three to four hours roasting, a small one not above three
14. Serve with the knuckle, garnished with a fringe of white paper, and with gravy and red currant jelly, either cold or melted, in port wine, and served hot
