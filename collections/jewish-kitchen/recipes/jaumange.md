## Ingredients

- half a pound of white sugar
- three-quarters of a pint of water
- thinly cut peel of two lemons
- one ounce of dissolved isinglass
- juice of three lemons
- one glass of brandy
- three glasses of sherry
- yolks of five or six eggs

## Directions

1. Simmer half a pound of white sugar in three-quarters of a pint of water, with the thinly cut peel of two lemons
2. when the sugar is melted, add an ounce of dissolved isinglass, and the juice of three lemons, a glass of brandy and three of sherry
3. beat up with this the yolks of five or six eggs
4. Place the basin in which it is mixed into a pan of boiling water to thicken it
5. pour it into a mould and set it to cool
6. if it does not thicken by being put in a pan of boiling water, set the pan on the fire and stir it for a few minutes
