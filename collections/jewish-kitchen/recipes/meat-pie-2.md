## Ingredients

- left-over beef, lamb or veal
- one green pepper
- two cups of potatoes
- one-half cup of carrots
- one onion
- one-fourth cup of flour
- cold water
- one cup of flour
- one heaping teaspoon of drippings
- pinch of salt
- one-fourth teaspoon of baking powder
- one teaspoon of sugar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cut any left-over beef, lamb or veal in small pieces, removing all excess of fat.
2. Parboil one green pepper (seeds removed) cut in strips, two cups of potatoes and one-half cup of carrots cut in dice, and one onion chopped fine.
3. Add to the meat.
4. Thicken with one-fourth cup of flour moistened in cold water.
5. Put in a baking dish.
6. For the crust: Mix one cup of flour, one heaping teaspoon of drippings, pinch of salt, one-fourth teaspoon of baking powder, one teaspoon of sugar and cold water to mix, about one-third cup.
7. Roll out to fit baking dish and cut holes for steam to escape, after covering the contents of the dish.
8. Bake in a quick hot oven one-half hour.
