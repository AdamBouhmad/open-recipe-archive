## Ingredients

- six large raw potatoes
- one gallon of water
- one and one-half cups of hops
- a scant cup of sugar
- one-half cup of salt
- a yeast cake or a cup of fresh yeast

## Directions

1. Grate six large raw potatoes
2. Have ready a gallon of water in which you have boiled one and one-half cups of hops
3. Strain through a fine hair sieve, boiling hot, over the potatoes, stirring well, or the mixture will thicken like starch
4. Add a scant cup of sugar and one-half cup of salt
5. When cold, add a yeast cake or a cup of fresh yeast
6. Let it stand until a thick foam rises on the top
7. Bottle in a few days
8. If kept in a cool place, this yeast will last a long time
9. Use one cup of yeast for one large baking
