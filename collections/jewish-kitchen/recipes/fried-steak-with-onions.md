## Ingredients

- steak
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- fat
- white onions
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Season the steak with salt and pepper, and dredge with flour
2. If tough, chop on both sides with a sharp knife
3. Lay in a pan of hot fat; when brown on one side, turn and brown on the other
4. While the steak is frying, heat some fat in another fryer and drop in four or five white onions that have been cut up
5. Fry crisp but not black
6. Remove the steak to a hot platter
7. Stir one tablespoon of flour in the fryer until smooth
8. Add one-half cup of boiling water
9. Lay the crisp onions over the steak, then over all pour the brown gravy
