## Ingredients

- 1/2 peck of peas
- 1/2 pint of milk
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 1 oz. of butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Pick the peas, wash them well, let them drain
2. Put them in a saucepan with 1/2 pint of milk, seasoned with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
3. Let it simmer till the peas are cooked
4. Mix in a basin 1 oz. of butter, 2 eggs, with two spoonfuls of flour into a very loose paste
5. As the peas and milk boil, take up a dessert spoonful at the time of the mixture, drop it into the peas
6. The kleis will adhere to the peas; dish it up in a deep dish very hot
