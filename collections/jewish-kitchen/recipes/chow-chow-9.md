## Ingredients

- pickles
- cauliflower
- beans
- little onions
- green and red peppers
- salt
- one gallon or more of best pickling vinegar
- one pound of ground mustard
- cold water
- cloves
- whole peppers
- mustard seed
- curry powder
- cloth

## Directions

1. Take pickles, cauliflower, beans, little onions and a few green and red peppers
2. Cut all up fine, except the onions
3. Salt well overnight
4. Drain off next morning and put in a large jar
5. Mix one gallon or more of best pickling vinegar with a pound of ground mustard
6. Wet the mustard with cold water before using
7. Put in a bag the following spices: Cloves, whole peppers and mustard seed
8. Boil the vinegar and spices and then throw over pickles boiling
9. Add a tablespoon of curry powder
10. When cold tie up, having previously put a cloth with mustard seed over all
