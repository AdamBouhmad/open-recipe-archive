## Ingredients

- one cup of cold rice
- two eggs
- one cup of sweet milk
- one teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of sugar
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- flour
- one tablespoon of melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat one cup of cold rice, two eggs, one cup of sweet milk, one teaspoon of salt, one tablespoon of sugar, two teaspoons of baking-powder
2. Add enough flour to make a stiff batter
3. Add lastly one tablespoon of melted butter
4. Bake in muffin-tins
