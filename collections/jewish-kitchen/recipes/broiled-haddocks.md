## Ingredients

- haddock
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- lemon
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash a haddock and dry it well
2. Lay it in the marinade (oil, vinegar, pepper, and salt)
3. Prepare the gridiron as for other broiled fish
4. Dish it up with one of the sauces or the plain oil and vinegar, or butter and lemon
5. Serve with a dish of mashed potatoes for lunch
