## Ingredients

- one pound of chestnuts
- two cups of milk
- salt and pepper
- one teaspoon of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put one pound of chestnuts, which have been shelled and skinned, on to boil in two cups of milk
2. Cook until tender
3. Mash smooth
4. If necessary add more milk while boiling
5. Strain and season with salt and pepper and one teaspoon of fresh butter
6. Serve hot
