## Ingredients

- a small piece of butter
- six or eight leeks
- water
- salt
- pepper
- piece of stale bread
- croutons
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Put a small piece of butter in saucepan and then six or eight leeks cut in small pieces
2. Keep turning for about five minutes so they will get brown
3. add water for amount desired
4. season with salt and pepper and put in piece of stale bread
5. Strain through the strainer
6. Put in croutons and serve with grated cheese
