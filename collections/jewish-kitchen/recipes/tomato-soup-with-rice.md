## Ingredients

- one minced onion
- one tablespoon of butter
- one can of tomatoes or one quart of medium sized tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of sugar
- a pinch of paprika
- one tablespoon of flour
- cold water
- one cup of boiled rice

## Directions

1. Brown slightly one minced onion in one tablespoon of butter
2. add one can of tomatoes or a quart of medium sized tomatoes cut in small pieces
3. season with salt, pepper, one tablespoon of sugar and a pinch of paprika
4. Simmer a half hour
5. strain and thicken with one tablespoon of flour moistened with cold water
6. add the strained tomatoes and one cup of boiled rice
7. let come to a boil and serve
