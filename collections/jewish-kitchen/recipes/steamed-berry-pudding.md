## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter or other shortening
- one-quarter cup of sugar
- yolk of one egg
- one-half cup of milk
- one cup of flour
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of berries or pitted cherries

## Directions

1. Take one tablespoon of butter (or other shortening), one-quarter cup of sugar, yolk of one egg, one-half cup of milk, one cup of flour, one teaspoon of baking-powder, one-quarter teaspoon of salt, one-half cup of berries or pitted cherries rolled in flour
2. Put in a well-greased melon mold and cook in boiling water steadily for two hours
3. Serve with hard sauce
