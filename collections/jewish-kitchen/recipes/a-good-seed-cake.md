## Ingredients

- 1 quartern of roll dough
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 oz. of carraway or aniseed well picked
- a pinch of salt
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all well together
2. Beat for half an hour
3. Bake in a good oven one hour and a half
