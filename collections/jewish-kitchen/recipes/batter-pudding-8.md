## Ingredients

- one teacup of matzoth-meal
- one pint of milk
- two eggs
- three ounces of brown sugar
- two ounces of butter
- rind of a lemon
- one tablespoon of rum

## Directions

1. Mix the meal into a batter with the milk and eggs
2. Add the sugar, melted butter, grated rind of a lemon and a tablespoon of rum, if desired
3. Pour the mixture in a greased basin or mold, and boil for one hour or bake for one-half hour
