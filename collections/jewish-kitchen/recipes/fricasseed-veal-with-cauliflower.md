## Ingredients

- veal breast or shoulder
- fine salt
- ginger
- tablespoon of goose-oil or poultry drippings
- one onion
- one or two tomatoes
- canned tomatoes
- water
- teaspoon of flour
- parsley
- cauliflower
- salt

## Directions

1. Use the breast or shoulder for this purpose, the former being preferable, and cut it up into pieces, not too small
2. Sprinkle each piece slightly with fine salt and ginger
3. Heat a tablespoon of goose-oil or poultry drippings in a stew-pan, and lay the veal in it
4. Cut up an onion and one or two tomatoes (a tablespoon of canned tomatoes will do), and add to this a little water
5. Stew two hours, closely covered
6. When done mix a teaspoon of flour and a little water and add to the veal
7. Chop up a few sprigs of parsley, add it and boil up once and serve
8. Place the cauliflower around the platter in which you serve the veal
9. Boil the cauliflower in salt and water, closely covered
