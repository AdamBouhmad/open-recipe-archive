## Ingredients

- a piece of meat off the neck, about two and one-half pounds
- three quarts of water
- an onion
- one celery root
- two carrots
- a large potato
- some parsley
- three tomatoes
- the giblets of poultry
- salt
- noodles

## Directions

1. For six persons, select a piece of meat off the neck, about two and one-half pounds; add three quarts of water, an onion, one celery root, two carrots, a large potato, some parsley, three tomatoes and the giblets of poultry
2. Cook in a closely covered kettle, letting the soup simmer for four or five hours
3. Remove every bit of scum that rises
4. Strain; add salt and remove every particle of fat
5. Put in noodles; boil about five minutes and serve at once
6. If allowed to stand it will become thick
