## Ingredients

- Rich pastry
- One pint of huckleberries
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Butter

## Directions

1. Line a pie-plate with rich pastry
2. Pick, clean and wash one pint of huckleberries, drain and lay them thickly on the crust
3. Sprinkle thickly with sugar, lightly with cinnamon, and drop bits of butter over the top
4. Bake a nice even brown
