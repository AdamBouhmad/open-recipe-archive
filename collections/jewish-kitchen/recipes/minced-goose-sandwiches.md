## Ingredients

- boiled or roast goose
- pickles or catsup
- bread or fresh rolls
- goose oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Take either boiled or roast goose (which has been highly seasoned) and mince in a chopping bowl
2. Add one or two pickles, according to quantity, or a teaspoon of catsup
3. Spread thin slices of bread or nice fresh rolls with a thin coating of goose oil, slightly salted
4. Spread the minced goose and cover with a layer of bread which has been previously spread
