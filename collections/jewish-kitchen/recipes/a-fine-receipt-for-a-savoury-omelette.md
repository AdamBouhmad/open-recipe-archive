## Ingredients

- four eggs
- a piece of butter, about an ounce and a half
- finely minced herbs
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Break four eggs, beat them up till thin enough to pass through a hair sieve, then beat them up till perfectly smooth and thin
2. a small omelette frying-pan is necessary for cooking it well
3. Dissolve in it a piece of butter, about an ounce and a half, pour in the egg, and as soon as it rises and is firm, slide it on to a warm plate and fold it over
4. it should only be fried on one side, and finely minced herbs should be sprinkled over the unfried side with pepper and salt
5. A salamander is frequently held over the unfried side of the omelette to take off the rawness it may otherwise have
