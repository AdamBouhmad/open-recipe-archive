## Ingredients

- young okra pods
- boiling salted water
- cream
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- tomatoes
- onion
- lemon juice
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Wash and cut off the ends of young pods
2. cover with boiling salted water and cook about twenty minutes, until tender
3. Drain
4. add cream (a scant cup to a quart of okra), a tablespoon of butter, and salt and pepper to taste
5. Another way of stewing is to cook it with tomatoes. To a pint of okra pods, washed and sliced, allow a dozen ripe tomatoes, peeled and sliced, and one medium-sized onion
6. Stew slowly for an hour, adding one tablespoon of butter, a scant teaspoon of salt and pepper to season
7. In the West Indies lemon juice and cayenne are also added to stewed okra
