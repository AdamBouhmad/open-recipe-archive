## Ingredients

- two well cleaned tails
- a neat's foot
- a large piece of clarified suet or fat
- between three and four quarts of cold water
- four onions
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- a carrot
- a turnip
- a head of celery
- whole pepper
- allspice
- two or three cloves
- salt
- browned flour
- a glass or more of port wine
- a little mushroom ketchup

## Directions

1. Have two well cleaned tails and a neat's foot, cut them in small joints and soak them in water
2. Put them in a stew-pan with a large piece of clarified suet or fat, and let them simmer for ten minutes
3. Put to them between three and four quarts of cold water, four onions, a bunch of sweet herbs, a carrot, a turnip, a head of celery
4. Season with whole pepper, allspice, two or three cloves, and salt
5. Let it stew till the meat is tender enough to leave the bones, then remove it from them
6. Thicken if necessary with browned flour
7. Just before serving, add a glass or more of port wine, and a little mushroom ketchup
