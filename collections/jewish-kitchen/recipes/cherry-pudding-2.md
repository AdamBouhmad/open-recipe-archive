## Ingredients

- one pint of crackers or bread crumbs
- one quart of boiling milk
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a good pinch of salt
- four eggs
- a cup and a half of sugar
- a little ground cinnamon
- a quart of stoned cherries

## Directions

1. Scald a pint of crackers or bread crumbs in a quart of boiling milk
2. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg, a good pinch of salt, four eggs, a cup and a half of sugar, a little ground cinnamon and a quart of stoned cherries
3. Bake in quick oven
