## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- 4 oz. of the best chocolate
- 4 oz. of sifted sugar
- a cup of boiling water

## Directions

1. Dissolve in a cup of boiling water 4 oz. of sifted sugar
2. Mix it all with 1 pint of cream and 4 oz. of the best chocolate
3. Stir it over a slow fire
4. When cold, put it in the freezing pot and work it according to directions
