## Ingredients

- parboiled spinach
- sorrel
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- drippings

## Directions

1. Take equal portions of parboiled spinach and sorrel
2. Season to taste with ground nutmeg, pepper and salt
3. Add sufficient drippings to make all moist enough
4. Place in a covered dish in a slow oven
