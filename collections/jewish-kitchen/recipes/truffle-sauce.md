## Ingredients

- truffles
- a little butter
- good white or brown consomme
- lemon juice
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- a very little white wine

## Directions

1. Peel and slice as many truffles as required
2. Simmer them gently with a little butter
3. When they are tender, add to them good white or brown consomme, lemon juice, pepper, salt, nutmeg, and a very little white wine
