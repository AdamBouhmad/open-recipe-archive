## Ingredients

- two sliced onions
- two ounces of sugar
- spices
- pepper
- salt
- one pint of pure malt vinegar
- six beaten eggs
- crumbled ginger-bread

## Directions

1. Place two sliced onions with two ounces each of sugar and spices, pepper and salt to taste in a pint of pure malt vinegar
2. Boil gently until the onions are nearly done
3. Let it cool a little
4. Stir in six beaten eggs and sufficient crumbled ginger-bread to make the whole quite thick
5. Place again over the fire for a few minutes, stirring frequently and mashing the mixture into a uniform paste
6. Be very careful that it does not boil
