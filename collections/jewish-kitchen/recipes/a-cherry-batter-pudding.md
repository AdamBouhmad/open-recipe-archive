## Ingredients

- fine cherries
- butter
- fine batter well sweetened
- sweet sauce

## Directions

1. Stone and pick some fine cherries, put them into a buttered mould, and pour over them a fine batter well sweetened
2. Tie over the mould closely, and boil one hour and a half
3. Serve with sweet sauce
4. Plums or damsons are sometimes used instead of cherries
