## Ingredients

- dough as for Apple Strudel No. 2
- one quart of thick sour milk
- one cup of grated bread crumbs
- two cups of granulated sugar
- one cup of chopped almonds
- one cup of raisins
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- butter
- vanilla sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for Apple Strudel as directed in the foregoing recipe
2. Drip one quart of thick sour milk on it lightly, with a large spoon
3. Put one cup of grated bread crumbs over the milk
4. Add two cups of granulated sugar, one cup of chopped almonds, one cup of raisins, and one teaspoon of cinnamon
5. Roll and place in well-buttered pan
6. Put small pieces of butter over the top
7. Baste frequently
8. Serve warm with vanilla sauce
9. One-half this quantity may be used for a small strudel
