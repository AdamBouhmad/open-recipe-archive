## Ingredients

- meat strips
- two cups of potatoes
- one-half cup of carrots
- one onion
- one-fourth cup of flour
- cold water
- one cup of flour
- one heaping teaspoon of drippings
- pinch of salt
- one-fourth teaspoon of baking powder
- one teaspoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut meat in strips, potatoes and carrots in dice, and onion chopped fine and add to the meat
2. Thicken with one-fourth cup of flour moistened in cold water
3. Put in a baking dish
4. Make crust: mix one cup of flour, one heaping teaspoon of drippings, pinch of salt, one-fourth teaspoon of baking powder, one teaspoon of sugar and cold water to mix, about one-third cup
5. Roll out to fit baking dish, cut holes for steam to escape, after covering the contents of the dish
6. Bake in a quick hot oven one-half hour
