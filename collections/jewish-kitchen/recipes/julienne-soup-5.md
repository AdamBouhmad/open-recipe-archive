## Ingredients

- soup stock
- one cup green peas
- three carrots
- some cabbage
- two tablespoons of flour
- hot fat
- livers and gizzards of fowls

## Directions

1. Have soup stock ready
2. Boil in water until tender one cup green peas, three carrots cut up in small pieces, and some cabbage chopped fine
3. Brown two tablespoons of flour in a skillet in hot fat, then stir in the vegetables
4. Fry some livers and gizzards of fowls, if handy, and add
5. Stir in the strained soup stock
