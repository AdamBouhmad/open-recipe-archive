## Ingredients

- melon
- crushed ice
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cut melon in half, seed and put on ice one hour before serving
2. When ready to serve, fill with crushed ice and sprinkle with powdered sugar
3. Allow one-half melon for each person
4. For dinner serve before soup
