## Ingredients

- half a carrot
- half a turnip
- a leek
- 2 sticks of celery
- a Jerusalem artichoke
- a potato
- several French beans
- a few green peas
- a small piece of lettuce
- a little cabbage
- a pint of water
- a little salt
- pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- a few meat balls
- a quart of stock
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Cut in thin long strips the length of 2 inches: half a carrot, half a turnip, a leek, 2 sticks of celery, a Jerusalem artichoke, a potato, several French beans, a few green peas, a small piece of lettuce, and a little cabbage.
2. Wash them well and strain through a colander.
3. Put them into a clean saucepan with a pint of water, a little salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg.
4. Boil a few meat balls with the vegetables.
5. When quite done put it all into a quart of the stock.
6. Put in a little browning to colour it.
