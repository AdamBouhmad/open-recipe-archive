## Ingredients

- lettuce
- dandelion
- chicory
- chopped beet
- chopped celery
- tomato
- French dressing
- onion
- garlic

## Directions

1. Mix lettuce, dandelion, chicory, a little chopped beet, chopped celery, and a bit of tomato and cover with French dressing. The dressing is usually flavored both with onion and garlic.
