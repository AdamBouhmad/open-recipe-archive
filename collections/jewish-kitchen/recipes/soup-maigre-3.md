## Ingredients

- three lettuces
- a large handful of spinach
- a little chervil
- a head of celery
- two or three carrots
- four onions
- half a pound of butter
- salt
- sifted white sugar
- white pepper
- five pints of boiling water
- yolks of four eggs

## Directions

1. Chop three lettuces, a large handful of spinach, a little chervil, a head of celery, two or three carrots, and four onions
2. Put them on the fire with half a pound of butter
3. Let them fry till slightly browned
4. Season with a little salt, sifted white sugar, and white pepper
5. Stew all gently in five pints of boiling water for about two hours and a half
6. Just before serving the soup, thicken it with the beaten yolks of four eggs, mixed first with a little of the soup, and then stirred into the remainder
