## Ingredients

- round, very firm and even sized tomatoes
- cold slaw
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Select round, very firm and even sized tomatoes
2. Cut off the top (reserve to use as a cover)
3. Scrape out the inside, being very careful to not break the tomato
4. Fill each tomato with some finely prepared cold slaw
5. Cover with the top of the tomato
6. Lay them on lettuce leaves and pour a mayonnaise dressing over each
7. You may lay them en masse on a decorated platter, heaping them in the shape of a mound, or serve individually
