## Ingredients

- one-half pound of sweet butter
- one pound flour (four cups sifted)
- four tablespoons of powdered sugar
- salt
- four egg yolks
- one-half cup of sour cream
- two pounds of sour apples
- one-half cup of light-colored raisins
- sugar and cinnamon
- four tablespoons of bread crumbs
- white of one egg
- two tablespoons of powdered almonds

## Directions

1. Rub together butter and flour on a pastry-board
2. Add powdered sugar, salt, egg yolks and moisten with sour cream
3. Cover and set aside in the ice-box for one-half hour
4. Peel sour apples and cut fine
5. Mix with light-colored raisins, sugar and cinnamon to taste
6. Cut the dough in two pieces
7. Roll out one piece and place on greased baking pan
8. Spread over this bread crumbs and the chopped sugared apples
9. Roll out the other half of dough and place on top
10. Spread with white of one egg
11. Sprinkle with powdered almonds
12. Bake in hot oven
