## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon tea per person, and 1 extra
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald the tea-pot
2. When the water boils, pour off the water with which the pot was scalded
3. Put in the tea and pour boiling water over it
4. Let it draw three minutes
5. Tea should never be allowed to remain on the leaves
6. If not drunk as soon as it is drawn, it should be poured off into another hot tea-pot, or into a hot jug, which should stand in hot water
