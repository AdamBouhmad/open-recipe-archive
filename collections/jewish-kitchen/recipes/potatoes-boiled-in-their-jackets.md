## Ingredients

- potatoes
- boiling water
- cloth

## Directions

1. Brush potatoes well
2. Put on to boil in a saucepan of boiling water
3. Continue boiling at the same degree of heat until done, when a fork will easily pierce them
4. This will take from twenty-five to thirty minutes
5. Drain the potatoes
6. Draw the saucepan to a low flame
7. Place a clean cloth folded over the top of the saucepan and press the lid down over it
8. This dries the potatoes and makes them a good color
9. Hold the potatoes in a cloth and peel them
10. Reheat for one minute and serve
