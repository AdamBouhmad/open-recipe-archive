## Ingredients

- one-half cup of butter
- three eggs
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- two and one-half cups of flour
- two and one-half level teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-half cup of milk
- one cup of any kind of nuts

## Directions

1. Rub the butter and sugar to a light white cream
2. add the eggs beaten a little
3. add the flour sifted with the powder
4. Mix with the milk and nuts into a rather firm batter
5. Bake in a paper lined tin in a steady oven thirty-five minutes
