## Ingredients

- 6 fine-looking pears
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 1 pint of water
- rind of a lemon
- a few cloves
- 2 spoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take 6 fine-looking pears, halve them, and take out the core
2. Lay them in a stewpan with 1 lb. of brown sugar, 1 pint of water, the rind of a lemon, a few cloves, and 2 spoonfuls of vinegar
3. Stew them gently for 4 or 5 hours
4. The vinegar will make them red as coral
