## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- yolks of six eggs, hard-boiled
- flour enough to make a dough that is not too stiff
- three cents worth of ammonia (hartshorn)
- one cup of scalding milk
- one-half cup of cold milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve three cents worth of ammonia (hartshorn) in scalded milk. Place the ammonia in a large bowl and pour one cup of scalding milk over it.
2. After this has cooled add it to the dough with one-half cup of cold milk. Flavor to taste.
3. Flour the pans and the cookie dough. Roll and proceed as with sugar cookies.
