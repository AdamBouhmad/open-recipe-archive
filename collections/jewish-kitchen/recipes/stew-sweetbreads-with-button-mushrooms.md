## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 throat sweetbreads
- boiling water
- button mushrooms
- very small meat balls
- a couple of sheep's tongues
- 2 eggs
- the juice of a lemon
- a little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put sweetbreads in a saucepan with boiling water for 10 minutes
2. Take them out and shape them nicely
3. Stew them exactly like calf's feet
4. Add a few button mushrooms and a few very small meat balls
5. Add sheep's tongues cut in pieces after they have been boiled
6. Add the egg sauce made from beaten eggs, lemon juice, and chopped parsley
