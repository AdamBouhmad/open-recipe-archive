## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare the potatoes and throw them into cold water until needed
2. Dry them with a towel
3. Cut into small pieces lengthwise of the potato
4. Drop them into hot fat and remove when lightly browned
5. Fry only a few at a time, letting those done stand in a colander in the oven to keep hot
6. When all are done, sprinkle with salt and serve at once
