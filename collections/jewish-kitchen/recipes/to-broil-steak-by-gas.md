## Ingredients

- steak
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- melted chicken fat

## Directions

1. Wipe steak with a damp cloth
2. Trim off the surplus fat
3. Heat the oven for from five to seven minutes
4. Lay steak on a greased rack, as near the flame as possible, the position of the rack depending on the thickness of the steak
5. Let the steak sear on each side, thereby retaining the juice
6. Lower the rack somewhat, and allow the steak to broil to the degree required
7. Just before taking from the oven, salt and pepper and spread with melted chicken fat
