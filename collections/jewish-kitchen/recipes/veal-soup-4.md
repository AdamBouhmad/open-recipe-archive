## Ingredients

- one piece veal from the neck
- one or two veal bones
- two quarts water
- one sprig parsley
- one onion, cut up into small pieces
- yolks of two eggs
- one tablespoon cold water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of veal, off the neck, and one or two veal bones in two quarts of water
2. Add a sprig of parsley, one onion, cut up into small pieces
3. Strain and thicken with the yolks of two eggs slightly beaten with a tablespoon of cold water
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
