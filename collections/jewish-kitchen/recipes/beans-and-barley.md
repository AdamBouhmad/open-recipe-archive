## Ingredients

- one-half cup of navy beans
- cold water
- one quart boiling water
- one teaspoon of salt
- one-half cup of barley
- fat soup stock

## Directions

1. Soak one-half cup of navy beans in cold water overnight
2. Drain and cook in one quart boiling water with one teaspoon of salt, until tender but not broken
3. add one-half cup of barley and let cook slowly until barley is tender, about one-half hour
4. Add fat soup stock as the water evaporates
5. Season to taste and bake in medium oven about one-half hour or until dry but not browned
