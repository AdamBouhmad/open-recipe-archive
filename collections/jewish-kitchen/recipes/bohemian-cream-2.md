## Ingredients

- one pint of double cream
- one pint of grape juice or grape jelly melted
- lady fingers

## Directions

1. Stir together and whip one pint of double cream and one pint of grape juice or grape jelly melted, this must be whipped to a froth.
2. Drain if needed.
3. Put in cups and set on ice for several hours.
4. Serve with lady fingers.
