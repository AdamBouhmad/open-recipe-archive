## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cup of granulated sugar
- one cup of flour
- two teaspoons of baking-powder
- one-half cup of boiling water
- jelly
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cream three eggs with one cup of granulated sugar
2. Sift flour with two teaspoons of baking-powder
3. Add one-half cup of boiling water
4. Bake in broad pan
5. While hot, remove from pan and lay on cloth wet with cold water
6. Spread with jelly and roll quickly
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
