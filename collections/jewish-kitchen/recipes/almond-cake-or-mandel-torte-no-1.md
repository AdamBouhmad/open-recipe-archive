## Ingredients

- one-half pound of almonds
- yolks of eight eggs
- one cup of sugar
- one-half lemon
- one tablespoon of brandy
- four lady-fingers
- whites of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds by pouring boiling water over them
2. Pound in a mortar or grate on grater
3. Beat yolks of eight eggs vigorously with one cup of sugar
4. Add grated peel and juice of one-half lemon
5. Add one tablespoon of brandy
6. Add four lady-fingers grated
7. Add the almonds
8. Fold in the stiffly-beaten whites of eggs
9. Bake in moderate oven one hour
