## Ingredients

- one quart of water
- one ounce of isinglass
- peel of one lemon and one orange
- juice of a lemon and six fine oranges
- half a pound of white sugar
- a Seville orange

## Directions

1. Simmer the isinglass in the water
2. Add the peel of one lemon and one orange
3. When the isinglass is dissolved, add the juice of a lemon and six fine oranges
4. Sweeten with half a pound of white sugar
5. Add a Seville orange if there should not be much flavor in the others
6. Pour it through a flannel bag or thick cloth dipped first into hot water
7. Pass the jelly through it until clear
8. Pour it into moulds and put them in a cool place to set
