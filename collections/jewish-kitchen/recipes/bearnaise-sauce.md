## Ingredients

- two tablespoons vinegar
- two tablespoons water
- one slice onion
- yolks of three eggs
- salt
- pepper
- four tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Bring to the boil two tablespoons each of vinegar and water.
2. Simmer in it for ten minutes a slice of onion.
3. Take out the onion and add the yolks of three eggs beaten very light.
4. Take from the fire, add salt and pepper to season, and four tablespoons of butter beaten to a cream, and added slowly.
