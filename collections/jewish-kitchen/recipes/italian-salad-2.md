## Ingredients

- white parts of a cold fowl
- mustard and cress
- lettuce
- equal quantities of vinegar and the finest salad oil
- salt
- mustard
- yolks of hard boiled eggs
- yolk of one raw egg
- tarragon vinegar
- whites of the eggs
- grated smoked beef
- two or three hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut up the white parts of a cold fowl, and mix it with mustard and cress, and a lettuce chopped finely
2. Pour over a fine salad mixture, composed of equal quantities of vinegar and the finest salad oil, salt, mustard, and the yolks of hard boiled eggs, and the yolk of one raw egg, mixed smoothly together
3. Add a little tarragon vinegar, and pour the mixture over the salad
4. Mix the whites of the eggs, and serve to garnish the dish, arranged in small heaps alternately with heaps of grated smoked beef
5. Cut up two or three hard boiled eggs with the chicken in small pieces and mixed with the salad
6. Alternatively, pile the fowl in the centre of the dish, pour over the salad mixture, and make a wall of any dressed salad, laying the whites of the eggs (after the yolks have been removed for the mixture), cut in rings on the top like a chain
