## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- salt
- sour apple
- one tablespoon goose or soup drippings
- onion
- cloves
- vinegar
- brown sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut fine on slaw-cutter, put cabbage in a colander, pour boiling water over it and let it stand over another pan for ten minutes.
2. Salt, mix well, and cut up a sour apple in the cabbage.
3. Heat one tablespoon goose or soup drippings, brown in this an onion cut fine.
4. Add the cabbage and stew slowly, keep covered.
5. Add a little hot water after it has boiled about five minutes.
6. When tender add a few cloves, vinegar, brown sugar and cinnamon to taste, and serve.
