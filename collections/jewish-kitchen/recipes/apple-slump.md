## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar
- one pint of flour
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- salt
- two cups of milk
- cream and sugar or soft custard

## Directions

1. Pare, core and quarter apples, add a little water and sugar to taste, stew until tender
2. Sift one pint of flour and one teaspoon of baking powder, add a pinch of salt and two cups of milk, mix and turn out onto a lightly floured board
3. Roll to one-half inch thickness and place over the stewed apples
4. Cover and cook for ten minutes without lifting the lid
5. Serve hot with cream and sugar or soft custard
