## Ingredients

- one-half pound of almonds
- whites of four eggs
- one pound of pulverized sugar
- one tablespoon of cinnamon
- boiled frosting

## Directions

1. Grate one-half pound of almonds
2. Beat the whites of four eggs to a stiff froth
3. Add gradually one pound of pulverized sugar and a tablespoon of cinnamon
4. Roll out this dough into half finger lengths and about as thick as your little finger
5. Bake
6. When done ice each one with boiled frosting
