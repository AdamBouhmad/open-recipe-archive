## Ingredients

- beer
- water
- sugar
- grated crust of stale rye bread
- stick cinnamon
- lemon juice
- small pieces of zwieback (rusk)
- a handful of dried currants

## Directions

1. Mix the beer with one-third water, boil with sugar and the grated crust of stale rye bread, add stick cinnamon and a little lemon juice.
2. Pour over small pieces of zwieback (rusk).
3. Some boil a handful of dried currants.
4. When done add both currants and juice.
