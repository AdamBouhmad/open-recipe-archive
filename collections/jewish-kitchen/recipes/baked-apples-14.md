## Ingredients

- large, juicy apples
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- clove
- water
- sugar
- egg
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take large, juicy apples
2. Wash and core them well
3. Fill each place that you have cored with brown sugar, cinnamon and raisins
4. Put a clove in each apple
5. Lay them in a deep dish
6. Pour a teacup of water in the dish
7. Put a little sugar on top of each apple
8. When well done the apples will be broken
9. Remove them carefully to the dish they are to be served in
10. Pour the syrup over them
11. To be eaten cold
12. If you wish them extra nice, glaze them with the beaten white of an egg, half a cup of pulverized sugar and serve with whipped cream
