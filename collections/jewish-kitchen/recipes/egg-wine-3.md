## Ingredients

- a fresh egg
- a tumbler of white wine and water
- sugar
- spices

## Directions

1. Beat a fresh egg
2. Add it to a tumbler of white wine and water, sweetened and spiced
3. Set it on the fire and stir it gently one way until it thickens
