## Ingredients

- one pint of carrots
- one pint cooked green peas
- two tablespoons of flour
- salt, pepper and sugar to taste
- two tablespoons of fat or butter
- one-half cup of milk or soup stock

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape and cut one pint of carrots in small cubes
2. Cook until tender
3. Drain and reserve one-half cup of carrot water
4. Mix carrots well with one pint cooked green peas
5. Sprinkle with two tablespoons of flour, salt, pepper and sugar to taste
6. Add two tablespoons of fat or butter, one-half cup of milk or soup stock and carrot water
7. Boil a little longer and serve
