## Ingredients

- trout, pickerel, or pike
- one-quarter pound of butter
- two tablespoons chopped parsley
- soaked white bread
- one onion
- salt
- pepper
- pounded almonds
- two eggs
- nutmeg
- salt water
- celery root
- parsley
- one onion
- flour
- bits of butter

## Directions

1. After the fish has been scaled and thoroughly cleaned, remove all the meat that adheres to the skin, being careful not to injure the skin
2. Take out all the meat from head to tail, cut open along the backbone, removing it also, but do not disfigure the head and tail
3. Chop the meat in a chopping bowl
4. Heat about a quarter of a pound of butter in a spider
5. Add two tablespoons chopped parsley, and some soaked white bread
6. Remove from the fire and add an onion grated, salt, pepper, pounded almonds, the yolks of two eggs, also a very little nutmeg grated
7. Mix all thoroughly and fill the skin until it looks natural
8. Boil in salt water, containing a piece of butter, celery root, parsley and an onion
9. The fish should be cooked for one and one-quarter hours, or until done
10. Remove from the fire and lay on a platter
11. Thicken the sauce with yolks of two eggs, adding a few slices of lemon
12. This fish may be baked but must be rolled in flour and dotted with bits of butter
