## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of flour
- 2 tablespoons of good oil
- a teaspoonful of made mustard
- salt
- pepper
- a small shalot
- 2 anchovies
- 3 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- a pint of the liquor the fish was boiled in

## Directions

1. Put into a small saucepan a tablespoonful of flour and 2 tablespoons of good oil
2. Rub the flour and oil together to a paste
3. Add a teaspoonful of made mustard, a little salt, pepper, a small shalot chopped fine, 2 anchovies washed and boned, and 3 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
4. Stir all well together
5. Stir in a pint of the liquor the fish was boiled in
6. Let it simmer gently and when it boils it is ready to serve
7. For mackerel add some chopped fennel; for salmon, some capers; for haddock, chopped parsley
