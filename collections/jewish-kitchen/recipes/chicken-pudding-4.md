## Ingredients

- good beef-suet paste
- a small chicken
- thin slices of chorissa
- sliced smoked veal
- good beef gravy
- mushroom essence or powder
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace

## Directions

1. Line a basin with a good beef-suet paste
2. Cut up a small chicken
3. Lightly fry the pieces
4. Place them in a stew-pan, with thin slices of chorissa, or, if at hand, slices of smoked veal
5. Add enough good beef gravy to cover them
6. Season with mushroom essence or powder, pepper, salt, and a very small quantity of nutmeg, and mace
7. Simmer gently for a quarter of an hour
8. Fill the pudding
9. Pour over part of the gravy and keep the rest to be poured over the pudding when served in the dish
10. Cover closely with the paste, the ends of which should be wetted with a paste brush to make it adhere closely
