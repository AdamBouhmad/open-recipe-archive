## Ingredients

- one egg
- two cups of boiled or steamed rice
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of cold water
- fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Separate the white and yolk of one egg and reserve about half the yolk for coating the croquette
2. Beat the rest with the white
3. Mix with two cups of boiled or steamed rice and one-half teaspoon of salt
4. Form into oblong croquettes or small balls
5. Mix the reserved part of the egg yolk with a tablespoon of cold water
6. Dip croquettes in this and then roll in fine bread crumbs
7. Repeat until well-coated
8. Fry brown in deep oil
