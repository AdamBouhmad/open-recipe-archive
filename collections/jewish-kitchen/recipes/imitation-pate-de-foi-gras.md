## Ingredients

- livers and gizzards of fowl
- three tablespoons of chicken or goose fat
- a finely chopped onion
- one tablespoon of pungent sauce
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Take as many livers and gizzards of any kind of fowl as you may have on hand
2. Add to these three tablespoons of chicken or goose fat, a finely chopped onion, one tablespoon of pungent sauce, and salt and white pepper to taste
3. Boil the livers until quite done and drain
4. When cold, rub to a smooth paste
5. Take some of the fat and chopped onion and simmer together slowly for ten minutes
6. Strain through a thin muslin bag, pressing the bag tightly
7. Turn into a bowl and mix with the seasoning
8. Work all together for a long time
9. Grease a bowl or cups and press this mixture into them
10. When soft cut up the gizzards into bits and lay between the mixture
11. Season this highly, or to suit taste
