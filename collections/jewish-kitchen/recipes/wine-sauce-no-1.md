## Ingredients

- one-half cup of white wine
- one and one-half cups of water
- yolks of two eggs
- two teaspoons of white sugar
- grated nutmeg or cinnamon bark or grated rind of half a lemon
- one teaspoon of flour

## Directions

1. Take one-half cup of white wine and one and one-half cups of water
2. put on to boil in double boiler
3. beat up the yolks of two eggs very light, with two teaspoons of white sugar
4. some grated nutmeg or three small pieces of cinnamon bark, or the grated rind of half a lemon
5. add a teaspoon of flour to this gradually
6. When perfectly smooth add the boiling wine, pouring very little at a time and stirring constantly
7. Return to boiler and stir until the spoon is coated
