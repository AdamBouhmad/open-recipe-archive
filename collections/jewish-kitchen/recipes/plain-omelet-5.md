## Ingredients

- three eggs
- three tablespoons of sweet milk
- a saltspoon of salt
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Whip the yolks of the eggs, the milk and salt to a light foam with an egg whip.
2. Slowly add the yolk mixture to the whites of the eggs, which should be beaten to a stiff froth in a big bowl.
3. After the yolks and milk are well whipped through the whites, beat the whole together for a few minutes with the egg-beater.
4. In an omelet pan or a large frying-pan put a tablespoon of good butter.
5. When the butter is bubbling hot, pour in the omelet mixture.
6. Stir it lightly for the first minute with a broad-bladed knife, then stop stirring it; and, as the mixture begins to stiffen around the edge, fold the omelet toward the centre with the knife.
7. As soon as it is properly folded, turn it over on a hot platter.
8. Decorate with sprigs of parsley and serve.
