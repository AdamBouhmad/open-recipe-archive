## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons prepared cocoa
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3 cups scalded milk

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoons prepared cocoa with two tablespoons of sugar and a few grains of salt
2. Dilute with one-half cup of boiling water to make a smooth paste
3. Add one-half cup of boiling water and boil five minutes
4. Turn into three cups of scalded milk and beat two minutes, using Dover beater
5. Serve
