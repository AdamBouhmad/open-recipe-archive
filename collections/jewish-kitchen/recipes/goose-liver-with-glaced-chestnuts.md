## Ingredients

- goose liver
- chestnuts
- goose-fat
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare goose liver as directed in the basic recipe
2. For the chestnuts: Scald until perfectly white
3. Heat some goose-fat, add nuts, a little sugar and glaze a light brown
4. Garnish the liver with the glaced chestnuts
