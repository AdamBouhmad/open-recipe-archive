## Ingredients

- flour
- milk or water
- butter
- salt
- yolk of an egg
- beaten white

## Directions

1. Scald some flour with milk or water
2. Mix in a small piece of butter and salt
3. Boil until thick
4. When cool beat in yolk of an egg
5. If too stiff add the beaten white
