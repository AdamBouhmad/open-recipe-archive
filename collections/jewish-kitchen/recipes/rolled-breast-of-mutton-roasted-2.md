## Ingredients

- breast of mutton
- forced meat
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Lay the mutton on the board
2. Take out the bones and beat it flat
3. Make a forced meat as for the balls
4. Lay it on and squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
5. Roll it up nicely and sew the seam
