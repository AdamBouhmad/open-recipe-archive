## Ingredients

- seven lemons
- water
- two pounds of sugar
- two cups of water
- one-half pound of large raisins

## Directions

1. Take seven lemons, slice thin and remove seeds
2. Draw string through slices, fasten ends
3. Lay them in a pan with water
4. Boil a short time
5. Remove the lemon, pour off water
6. Cook two pounds of sugar with two cups of water
7. When the sugar is syrupy add one-half pound of large raisins
8. Put in the lemon and let cook until the syrup is thick
