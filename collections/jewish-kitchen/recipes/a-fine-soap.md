## Ingredients

- two ounces of bitter almonds
- a small piece of camphor
- one ounce and a half of tincture of Benjamin
- one pound of curd soap in shavings

## Directions

1. Blanch and beat to a paste two ounces of bitter almonds, with a small piece of camphor, and one ounce and a half of tincture of Benjamin
2. Add one pound of curd soap in shavings
3. Beat and melt well together
4. Pour into moulds to cool
