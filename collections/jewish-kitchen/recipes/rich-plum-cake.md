## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of sifted loaf sugar
- one pound of fine flour
- ten eggs
- half a nutmeg
- one pound of well-washed currants
- half a pound of mixed candied peels
- a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream one pound of butter
2. Add the same quantity of sifted loaf sugar and of fine flour
3. Add the whites of ten eggs beaten to a froth and the yolks of the same also beaten till quite smooth and thin
4. Add half a nutmeg grated
5. Work in one pound of well-washed currants, half a pound of mixed candied peels cut small, and a glass of brandy
6. Bake for two hours
