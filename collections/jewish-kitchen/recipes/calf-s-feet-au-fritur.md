## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- water
- pepper
- salt
- egg
- grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Simmer them for four hours in water till the meat can be taken easily from the bone
2. Cut them in handsome pieces
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Dip them in egg, and sprinkle thickly with grated bread crumbs
5. Fry of a fine even brown
6. They may be served dry or with any sauce that may be approved
7. Continue to stew the liquor with the bones, and it can be used for jelly
