## Ingredients

- onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of oil
- ½ pint of boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- ½-lb. of rice
- saffron
- ¾-lb. of large sausage
- 2 or 3 tomatoes

## Directions

1. Slice an onion in a small stewpan with 2 tablespoonfuls of oil, let it fry
2. Put in a ½ pint of boiling water, a little salt and pepper
3. Wash well ½-lb. of rice, the best, well drain it from the water
4. Put it in the saucepan with a little saffron to colour it
5. Have a ¾-lb. of the large sausage, take the skin off, put it in also
6. Let it simmer very gently till the rice swells and soaks up all the water
7. Take the sausage out of the saucepan, lay it on a plate, cut it in round slices
8. Put the rice in the dish, lay the slices of sausage round it
9. If tomatoes are in season slice 2 or 3 and cook with the rice
