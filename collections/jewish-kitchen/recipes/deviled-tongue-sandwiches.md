## Ingredients

- tongue
- mustard
- soup
- mayonnaise
- white bread

## Directions

1. Grind up tongue (root will do) in meat chopper
2. To a cup of ground tongue add one teaspoon of mustard, one tablespoon of soup, and one teaspoon of mayonnaise
3. Mix into soft paste
4. Spread on white bread cut very thin
