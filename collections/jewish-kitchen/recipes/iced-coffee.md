## Ingredients

- boiled coffee
- sugar
- 1 quart coffee
- 1/2 cup cream
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take boiled coffee and strain
2. Add sugar to taste and chill
3. When ready to serve, add one quart of coffee, one-half cup of cream and pour in pitcher
4. Serve in tall glasses
5. Have ready a small bowl of whipped cream and place a tablespoon on top of each glass if desired
