## Ingredients

- one large sour apple
- three-fourths cup of powdered sugar
- two eggs
- cold boiled custard

## Directions

1. Peel and grate one large sour apple, sprinkling over it three-fourths cup of powdered sugar as it is grated to keep it from turning dark.
2. Add the unbeaten whites of two eggs; beat constantly for half an hour.
3. Arrange mound fashion on a glass dish with cold boiled custard around it.
