## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one-third cup of boiling water
- white of one egg

## Directions

1. Pour water on sugar until dissolved
2. Heat slowly to boiling point without stirring
3. Boil until syrup will thread when dropped from tip of spoon
4. As soon as it threads, pour slowly over beaten white
5. Beat with heavy wire spoon until of proper consistency to spread
6. Flavor
