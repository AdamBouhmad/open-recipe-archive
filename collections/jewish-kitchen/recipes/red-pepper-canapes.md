## Ingredients

- two chopped hard-boiled eggs
- one tablespoon of chopped red peppers canned
- a saltspoon of salt
- a tiny pinch of mustard
- two tablespoons of grated American cheese
- melted butter
- rounds of fried bread
- watercress

## Directions

1. Mix together two chopped hard-boiled eggs, one tablespoon of chopped red peppers, a saltspoon of salt, a tiny pinch of mustard and two tablespoons of grated American cheese with sufficient melted butter to form a paste
2. spread over the rounds of fried bread and place in a very hot oven for about three minutes
3. Serve on a folded napkin, garnished with watercress
