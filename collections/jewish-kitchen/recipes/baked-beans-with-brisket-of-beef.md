## Ingredients

- two cups of navy beans
- one and one-half pounds of brisket of beef
- one-half tablespoon of mustard
- one teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of molasses
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one-half cup of boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash, pick over and soak overnight in cold water, two cups of navy beans
2. In the morning, drain and cover with fresh water
3. heat slowly and let cook just below the boiling point until the skins burst
4. When done, drain beans and put in a pot with one and one-half pounds of brisket of beef
5. Mix one-half tablespoon of mustard, one teaspoon of salt, one tablespoon of molasses, two tablespoons of sugar, one-half cup of boiling water and pour over beans
6. add enough more boiling water to cover them
7. Cover pot and bake slowly six or eight hours
