## Ingredients

- four cups of water
- two cups of sugar
- one can grated pineapple
- juice of six lemons
- four cups of ice-water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of four cups of water and two cups of sugar
2. Boil fifteen minutes
3. Add one can grated pineapple and juice of six lemons
4. Cool and add four cups of ice-water
5. Freeze until mushy, using half ice and half salt
