## Ingredients

- little wild cherries
- sugar
- whole spice
- cloves
- stick cinnamon
- good whiskey

## Directions

1. Wash carefully the little wild cherries
2. sugar plentifully
3. add whole spice, cloves (with the heads removed) and stick cinnamon
4. Fewer cloves than the other spices
5. Get good whiskey and allow one-half as much cherries as whiskey
6. To a quart bottle allow scant half pint sugared cherries to one and one-half pints of whiskey
7. Bottle and seal
8. Let stand at least two months
9. Open, shake bottle well and taste
10. if necessary add more sugar
11. Seal again, and let stand another month
