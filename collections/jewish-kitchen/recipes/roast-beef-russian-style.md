## Ingredients

- cross-rib or shoulder
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- water
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Place a piece of cross-rib or shoulder weighing three pounds in roasting-pan
2. Slice some onions over it, season with salt and pepper, add some water and let it cook well
3. Peel a few potatoes and put them under the meat
4. When the meat becomes brown, turn it and cook until it browns on the other side
