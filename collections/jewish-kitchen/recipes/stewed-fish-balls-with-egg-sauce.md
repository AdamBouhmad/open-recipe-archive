## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. cod
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- lemon rind
- parsley
- marjoram
- soaked bread
- 1 egg
- 1 large onion
- 3 tablespoonfuls salad oil
- 1 teacupful boiling water
- 3 eggs
- 2 large lemons
- dried saffron
- chopped parsley
- 3 or 4 tomatoes

## Directions

1. Take 2 lbs. of cod and free it from all the bones.
2. Chop it and season it with pepper, salt, grated nutmeg, and a little of the rind of lemon chopped fine, parsley, and marjoram, a little soaked bread, with the water drained from it.
3. Mix well together with an egg, make them into nice-sized balls the size of an apple.
4. Slice in the stewpan a large onion, add 3 tablespoonfuls of salad oil, let it fry.
5. Add a teacupful of boiling water, let it boil up, put in the balls.
6. When done beat in a basin 3 eggs, strain the juice of 2 large lemons, with a little dried saffron and a little chopped parsley.
7. Stir and mix it all well together.
8. Dish them up by first taking out the balls, then strain the sauce over them.
9. Garnish with parsley.
10. 3 or 4 tomatoes added to the balls is a great improvement and makes it a pretty dish.
