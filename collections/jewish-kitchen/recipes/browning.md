## Ingredients

- one pound of coarse brown sugar
- a lump of clarified suet
- a wine-glass of port wine
- half an ounce of black pepper
- a little mace
- four spoonsful of ketchup or Harvey's sauce
- a little salt
- the peel of a lemon grated

## Directions

1. Put one pound of coarse brown sugar in a stew-pan with a lump of clarified suet
2. When it begins to froth, pour in a wine-glass of port wine, half an ounce of black pepper, a little mace, four spoonsful of ketchup or Harvey's sauce, a little salt, and the peel of a lemon grated
3. Boil all together
4. Let it grow cold, when it must be skimmed and bottled for use
