## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- one finely minced onion
- one-quarter pound of well-boiled rice
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Melt two ounces of butter in a stew-pan
2. Fry in the butter a finely minced onion
3. When this is of a nice golden color stir into it a quarter of a pound of well-boiled rice
4. Work it well with a fork and then pour all into a buttered pie dish
5. Dredge over with a good coating of grated cheese
6. Sprinkle the surface with melted butter and bake until nicely browned
