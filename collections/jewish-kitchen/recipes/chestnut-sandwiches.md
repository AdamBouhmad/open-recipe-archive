## Ingredients

- one slice each of white and brown bread
- butter
- chestnuts
- hard-boiled eggs
- Mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Boil chestnuts tender, peel and rub through a sieve.
2. Mash with hard-boiled eggs to a paste and moisten with Mayonnaise.
3. Cut white and brown bread thin and butter.
4. Spread with the chestnut mixture.
