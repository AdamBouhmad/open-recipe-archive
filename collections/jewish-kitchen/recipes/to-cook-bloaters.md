## Ingredients

- bloaters
- butter
- pepper
- vinegar
- mustard

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads
2. in so doing draw out the entrails
3. split them open down the back
4. lay them in a tin with a piece of butter and a little pepper
5. dish them on a hot plate
6. A little mixture of vinegar and mustard makes them very nice
