## Ingredients

- a nice piece of smoked beef
- ½ peck of broad beans

## Directions

1. Soak it first for an hour, put it in a saucepan, only half covering it, let it boil and skim it, then let it cook very gently according to its weight.
2. Have ready picked ½ peck of the beans, put them in with the meat.
3. Dish up the beef, strain the beans into a colander, put them round the beef.
