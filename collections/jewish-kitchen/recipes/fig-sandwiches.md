## Ingredients

- figs
- water
- lemon juice
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and chop the figs fine.
2. Put in a double boiler with a little water and cook until a paste is formed.
3. Add a few drops of lemon juice and set aside.
4. When cool spread on thin slices of buttered bread.
