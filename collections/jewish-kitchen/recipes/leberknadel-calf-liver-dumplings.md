## Ingredients

- one-half pound calf's liver
- four ounces marrow
- thyme
- one clove garlic
- pepper
- salt
- lemon peel
- yolk of two eggs
- one whole egg
- grated bread crumbs or rolled crackers
- boiling salt water
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Chop and pass through a colander one-half pound of calf's liver
2. Rub to a cream four ounces of marrow, add the liver and stir hard
3. Add a little thyme, one clove of garlic grated, pepper, salt and a little grated lemon peel, the yolks of two eggs and one whole egg
4. Add enough grated bread crumbs or rolled crackers to permit its being formed into little marbles
5. Drop in boiling salt water and let cook fifteen minutes
6. Drain, roll in fine crumbs and fry in hot fat
