## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- two cups of soup stock
- one-quarter of a white onion
- one-half can of tomatoes
- olive oil or chicken-fat
- salt
- pepper
- a jointed chicken slightly parboiled, or slices of cold cooked chicken or turkey
- two tablespoons of olive oil or chicken-fat

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of rice in cold water for one hour
2. Pour off the water
3. Put the rice with two cups of soup stock and one-quarter of a white onion on to boil
4. Stew until the rice absorbs all the stock
5. Stew one-half can of tomatoes thoroughly and season with olive oil or chicken-fat, salt and pepper
6. Mix it with the rice
7. Sauté in chicken-fat to a light color, a jointed chicken slightly parboiled, or slices of cold cooked chicken or turkey
8. Make a depression in the rice and tomato and put in the chicken
9. Add two tablespoons of olive oil or chicken-fat, and stew all together for twenty minutes
10. Serve on a platter in a smooth mound, the red rice surrounding the fowl
