## Ingredients

- one quart of cream
- one pint of new milk
- two eggs
- one teacup of grated chocolate
- two cups of pulverized sugar
- one teaspoon of cornstarch
- one teaspoon of extract of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and sugar and let them come to a boil
2. Take them quickly from the fire
3. Dissolve the chocolate in a little milk over the fire, stirring all the time
4. When smooth mix with the milk and eggs
5. Add the cream and vanilla
6. Freeze when cold
