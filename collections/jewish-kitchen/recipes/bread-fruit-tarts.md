## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- fruit
- brown sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Line a dish with thin slices of bread
2. Lay the fruit with brown sugar in alternate layers with slices of bread
3. When the dish is filled, pour over half a tea-cup full of water
4. Let the top be formed of thin pieces of bread thickly strewed over with brown sugar
5. Bake until thoroughly done
