## Ingredients

- Dates
- English walnuts, blanched almonds, or pecans
- Granulated sugar or powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a cut the entire length of dates and remove stones
2. Fill cavities with English walnuts, blanched almonds, pecans or with a mixture of chopped nuts
3. Shape in original form
4. Roll in granulated sugar or powdered sugar and serve on small plate or bonbon dish
