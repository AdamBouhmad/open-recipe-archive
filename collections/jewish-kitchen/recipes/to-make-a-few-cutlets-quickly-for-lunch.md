## Ingredients

- 2 large chops
- mashed potato or stewed spinach
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take 2 large chops
2. Cut out the bones free from any of the flesh
3. Cut each chop with a sharp knife into 3 small ones
4. Fry them
5. Have a little mashed potato or stewed spinach in a dish
6. Lay them on
7. Season with salt and pepper
