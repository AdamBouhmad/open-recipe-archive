## Ingredients

- strudel or roley-poley dough
- half a pound of cheese
- salt
- yolks of two eggs
- one whole egg
- grated peel of one lemon
- two ounces of sweet almonds
- four bitter almonds
- four ounces of sultana raisins
- citron chopped fine
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Make a strudel or roley-poley dough and let it rest until you have prepared the cheese
2. Take half a pound of cheese, rub it through a coarse sieve or colander
3. Add salt, the yolks of two eggs and one whole egg, sweeten to taste
4. Add the grated peel of one lemon, two ounces of sweet almonds, and about four bitter ones, blanched and pounded, four ounces of sultana raisins and a little citron chopped fine
5. Roll out as thin as possible
6. Spread in the cheese
7. Roll and bake, basting with sweet cream
