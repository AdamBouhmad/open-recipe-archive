## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a calf's foot
- one pound of chorissa
- a large fowl
- four quarts of water
- a piece of fresh lemon peel
- six Jerusalem artichokes
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- a little salt
- white pepper
- a little nutmeg
- a blade of mace
- flower or ground rice

## Directions

1. Stew a knuckle of veal and a calf's foot and one pound of chorissa and a large fowl in four quarts of water
2. Add a piece of fresh lemon peel, six Jerusalem artichokes, a bunch of sweet herbs, a little salt, white pepper, a little nutmeg, and a blade of mace
3. When the fowl is thoroughly done, remove the white parts to prepare for thickening
4. Let the rest continue stewing till the stock is sufficiently strong
5. Pound the white parts of the fowl and sprinkle with flower or ground rice
6. Stir them in the soup after it has been strained until it thickens
