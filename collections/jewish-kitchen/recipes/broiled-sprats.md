## Ingredients

- large sprats
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pick the largest sprats you can get and take off the heads
2. Put them in layers on the gridiron and let them broil nicely both sides
3. Have a dish at the side of the fire with a good lump of butter
4. Turn them on the dish and season well with salt and pepper and the juice of a lemon
5. Serve with very hot plates; no garnish
