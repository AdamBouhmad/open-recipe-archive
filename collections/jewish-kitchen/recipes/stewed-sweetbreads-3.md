## Ingredients

- one pair of sweetbreads
- warm water
- boiling water
- two cups of veal stock
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- one-half lemon
- one teaspoon of potato flour

## Directions

1. Soak one pair of sweetbreads for two or three hours in sufficient warm water to cover them, then drain
2. Put them in a stew-pan, with boiling water to cover them, and then boil gently for seven or eight minutes
3. Lay the sweetbreads in a stew-pan, pour two cups of veal stock over them
4. Add salt and cayenne pepper to taste, and simmer gently for one hour
5. Lift them out on to a very hot dish
6. Add juice of one-half lemon and one teaspoon of potato flour to the gravy
7. Stir smoothly, and boil up
8. Pour over the sweetbreads and serve at once
