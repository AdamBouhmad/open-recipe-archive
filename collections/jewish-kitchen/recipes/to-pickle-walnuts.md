## Ingredients

- walnuts
- brine
- spiced vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak in brine for a week
2. Prick them
3. Simmer in brine
4. Let them lay on a sieve to drain, and to turn black
5. Place them in jars
6. Pour over boiling spiced vinegar
