## Ingredients

- one-quarter cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- one-quarter pound of raisins
- one-quarter pound of currants
- juice and peel of one lemon
- one spoon of rum
- twenty blanched and grated almonds
- two cups of flour
- one-half teaspoon of baking-powder
- two stiffly-beaten whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Cream one-quarter cup of butter with one cup of sugar, yolks of six eggs, one-quarter pound of raisins, one-quarter pound of currants, juice and peel of one lemon, one spoon of rum, twenty blanched and grated almonds
2. Mix with two cups of flour mixed with one-half teaspoon of baking-powder
3. Fold in two stiffly-beaten whites of eggs
4. Bake in an ungreased form one to one and one-half hours
