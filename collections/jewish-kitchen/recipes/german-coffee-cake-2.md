## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking-powder
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 0.67 cup milk
- melted butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take three cups of flour sifted, one teaspoon of salt, three tablespoons of sugar, three teaspoons of baking-powder, two eggs, two tablespoons of butter, and two-thirds of a cup of milk
2. Stir well together, adding more milk if necessary
3. Keep batter very stiff
4. Sprinkle with melted butter (generously) sugar and cinnamon, and again with melted butter
5. Put into well-buttered shallow pans and bake about half an hour
