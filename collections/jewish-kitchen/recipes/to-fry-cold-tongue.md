## Ingredients

- cold tongue
- fat
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. If you have any left cut it in thin slices, and a few slices from the fat part
2. Have some fat boiling in your pan
3. Dip the slices in bread crumbs seasoned, and then into egg
4. Fry it a light brown
5. Have some potatoes well mashed, spread them in the dish
6. Place the slices on arranged nicely
7. Garnish with parsley
