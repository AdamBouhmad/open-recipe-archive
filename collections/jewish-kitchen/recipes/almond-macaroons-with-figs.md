## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- one-half pound of sugar
- one-half pound of finely cut figs
- one-half pound of blanched almonds or walnuts
- ironing-wax
- waxed paper

## Directions

1. Beat stiff the whites of three eggs
2. Add one-half pound of sugar
3. Add one-half pound of finely cut figs
4. Add one-half pound of either blanched almonds cut into long slices, or cut up walnuts
5. Heat a large pan
6. Pass ironing-wax over surface
7. Lay in waxed paper
8. Drop spoonfuls of mixture on paper, same distance apart
9. Bake very slowly in very moderate oven
10. Remove and let cool
11. Take paper out with the macaroons
12. Turn over and place hot cloths on wrong side
13. When cakes will drop off
