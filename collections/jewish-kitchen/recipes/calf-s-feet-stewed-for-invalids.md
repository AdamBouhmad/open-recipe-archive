## Ingredients

- fine foot
- water
- salt
- white pepper
- sprig of parsley or pinch of saffron
- thin egg sauce
- lemon juice
- sippets of toast or well boiled rice

## Directions

1. Clean and soak a fine foot
2. Put it on in very little water
3. Let it simmer till tender
4. Cut it in pieces, without removing the bone
5. Continue stewing for three hours, till they become perfectly soft
6. If the liquor boils away, add a little more water, but there should not be more liquor than can be served in the dish with the foot
7. Season with a little salt and white pepper, and a sprig of parsley, or a pinch of saffron to improve the appearance
8. A little delicately-made thin egg sauce, with a flavor of lemon juice, may be served in a sauce-tureen if approved
9. Garnish the dish with sippets of toast or well boiled rice
