## Ingredients

- macaroni
- finnan haddie
- drawn butter sauce
- cornstarch or milk or cream dressing
- bread or cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Break up and cook until tender about a package of macaroni.
2. Pick up the finnan haddie until you have about three-quarters as much as you have macaroni.
3. Mix in a greased baking-dish and pour over a drawn butter sauce, made with cornstarch or with any good milk or cream dressing.
4. Cover with bread or cracker crumbs or leave plain to brown in oven.
5. Bake from twenty to thirty minutes.
