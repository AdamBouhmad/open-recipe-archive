## Ingredients

- one-half peck of perfect red Siberian crab-apples
- four pounds of sugar
- one tablespoon of water
- Manila paper

## Directions

1. Wash, wipe and remove the blossom ends of one-half peck of perfect red Siberian crab-apples.
2. Pour one tablespoon of water in bottom of one gallon stone jar.
3. Place in alternate layers of apples and sugar, using four pounds altogether (with sugar on top).
4. Cover with two thicknesses of Manila paper, tied down securely or with close fitting plate.
5. Bake in a very slow oven (that would only turn the paper a light brown), two or three hours.
6. Let stand to cool.
7. Keep in cool, dry place.
