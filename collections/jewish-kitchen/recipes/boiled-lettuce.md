## Ingredients

- four or five heads of lettuce
- salt
- boiling salted water
- butter
- salt and pepper
- white sauce
- grated nutmeg
- yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Wash four or five heads of lettuce, carefully removing thick, bitter stalks and retaining all sound leaves
2. Cook in plenty of boiling salted water for ten or fifteen minutes
3. Blanch in cold water for a minute or two
4. Drain and chop lightly, and heat in stew-pan with some butter, and salt and pepper to taste
5. If preferred, the chopped lettuce may be heated with a pint of white sauce seasoned with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
6. After simmering for a few minutes in the sauce, draw to a cooler part of the range and stir in the well-beaten yolks of two eggs
