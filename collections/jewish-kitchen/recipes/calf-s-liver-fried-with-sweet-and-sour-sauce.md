## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- boiling fat
- a large Spanish onion
- slices of sausage
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a spoonful of brown sugar
- 2 of vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- a cup of water
- a little browning

## Directions

1. Prepare the liver as before when it comes from the gridiron
2. Put it on a plate
3. Have on the fire a pan with boiling fat
4. Slice a large Spanish onion very fine and fry this first a light brown
5. Have at the side of the fire a sieve on a plate
6. When the onion is done put it on the sieve to drain
7. Fry the liver and some slices of sausage
8. Take them out and pour off the fat from your pan
9. Rub in a basin a tablespoonful of flour with a spoonful of brown sugar, 2 of vinegar, some pepper and salt, with a cup of water
10. Let this all boil in the pan with a little browning to colour
11. Put back the onions, then the liver and sausage
12. Have a very hot dish and dish up the liver and sausage
13. Put the onions round and the sauce over
