## Ingredients

- some mushrooms, young and fresh
- salt
- a little gravy
- the trimmings of the fowl, or of veal
- a blade of mace
- a little grated lemon peel
- the juice of one lemon
- flour
- a table-spoonful of white wine

## Directions

1. Chop some mushrooms, young and fresh, salt them
2. Put them into a saucepan with a little gravy, made of the trimmings of the fowl, or of veal
3. Add a blade of mace, a little grated lemon peel, the juice of one lemon
4. Thicken with flour
5. When ready to serve, stir in a table-spoonful of white wine
