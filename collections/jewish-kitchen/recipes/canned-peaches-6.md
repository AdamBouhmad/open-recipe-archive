## Ingredients

- Eight quarts of peaches
- One quart of sugar
- Three quarts of water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of the sugar and water
2. Bring to a boil and skim it
3. Draw the kettle aside where the syrup will keep hot but not boil
4. Pare the peaches, cutting them in halves or not as desired; if in half leave one or two whole peaches for every jar, as the kernel improves the flavor
5. Put a layer of fruit in the kettle
6. When it begins to boil skim carefully
7. Boil gently for ten minutes
8. Put in jars and seal
9. Then cook more of the fruit in similar fashion
10. If the fruit is not ripe it will require a longer time to cook
