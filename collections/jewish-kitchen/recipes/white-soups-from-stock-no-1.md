## Ingredients

- a quart of stock
- 1-lb. of vermicelli
- a couple of tomatoes
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Warm a quart of stock.
2. When it boils put in 1-lb. of vermicelli and let it boil for 10 minutes.
3. Cut a couple of tomatoes small and boil them.
4. Put them through a sieve and put it into the tureen before you dish up the soup.
5. Add a little grated nutmeg.
