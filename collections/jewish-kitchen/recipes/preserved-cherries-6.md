## Ingredients

- sour red cherries
- sugar
- cherry stones

## Directions

1. Stone the cherries, preserving every drop of juice, then weigh the cherries
2. For every pound take three-quarters of a pound of sugar
3. Set the sugar and juice of the cherries on to boil, also a handful of the cherry stones pounded and tied in a thin muslin bag
4. Let this boil about fifteen minutes
5. Skim off the scum that rises
6. Now put in the cherries, and boil until the syrup begins to thicken like jelly
7. Remove from the fire, fill in pint jars
8. When cold, cover with brandied paper and screw on the cover tight
