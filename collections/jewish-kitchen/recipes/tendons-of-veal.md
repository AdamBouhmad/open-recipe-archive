## Ingredients

- tendons from the bones of a breast of veal
- slices of smoked beef
- shavings of veal
- a few herbs
- a little sliced lemon
- two or three onions
- a little broth
- white wine
- mushrooms
- egg-balls
- a few peas
- a piece of mint
- a little white sugar

## Directions

1. Cut from the bones of a breast of veal the tendons which are round the front
2. Trim and blanch them
3. Put them with slices of smoked beef into a stewpan with some shavings of veal, a few herbs, a little sliced lemon, two or three onions, and a little broth
4. Let them simmer for seven or eight hours
5. When done, thicken the gravy and add white wine and mushrooms and egg-balls
6. Add a few peas with the tendons
7. Add a piece of mint and a little white sugar
