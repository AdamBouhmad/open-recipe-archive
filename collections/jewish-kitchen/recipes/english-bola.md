## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 teacup milk
- 1/2 oz. German yeast
- 6 oz. sugar
- 1 lb. sweetmeat
- salt

## Directions

1. Work 1 lb. flour, 1/2 lb. butter, 5 eggs, 1 teacup of milk, and 1/2 oz. of German yeast into a paste.
2. Let it stand for 1 hour.
3. Work in 6 oz. of sugar and 1 lb. of sweetmeat.
4. Grease your tin.
5. Bake it in a slow oven.
6. Remember in all sweets to put a little salt.
