## Ingredients

- one and a half matzoth
- one tablespoon of fat
- one-half cup of matzoth meal
- two eggs
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one-eighth teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Soak one and a half matzoth and press dry
2. Heat one tablespoon of fat and add the soaked matzoth
3. When dry add one-half cup of matzoth meal, two eggs, two tablespoons of sugar and one-eighth teaspoon of salt
4. Mix well and press into pie-plate with hands
5. Have dough one-quarter inch thick
