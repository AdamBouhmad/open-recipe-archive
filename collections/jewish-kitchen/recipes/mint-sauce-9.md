## Ingredients

- mint
- half a cup of vinegar
- one tablespoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Chop some mint fine
2. Boil half a cup of vinegar with one tablespoon of sugar
3. Throw in the mint and boil up once
4. Pour in a sauceboat and cool off a little before serving
