## Ingredients

- fowl
- water
- soup stock
- mushrooms
- carrots
- small potatoes
- peas
- sherry wine

## Directions

1. Take fowl and brown in a skillet the desired color
2. Add to this enough water or soup stock preferred
3. Put it in casserole and add vegetables; add first those that require longest cooking
4. Use mushrooms, carrots, small potatoes and peas
5. If you like flavor of sherry wine, add small wine glass; if not, it is just as good
6. Season well and cook in hot oven not too long, as you want fowl and vegetables to be whole
7. You may add soup stock if it is too dry after being in oven
