## Ingredients

- about a pint of fine young button mushrooms
- a white veal gravy
- salt
- pepper
- a blade of mace
- the grated peel of half a lemon
- flour
- the yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Let about a pint of fine young button mushrooms stew gently in a white veal gravy seasoned with salt, pepper, a blade of mace, and if approved, the grated peel of half a lemon
2. Thicken with flour and the yolk of an egg stirred in it, just before serving
