## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Green ginger root
- Peppercorn
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Mace

## Directions

1. Wash and look over one pint of mushrooms carefully.
2. Put them in an earthen jar with alternate layers of salt.
3. Let stand for twenty-four hours in a comparatively warm place.
4. Put through a fruit press and add one-fourth ounce of green ginger root cut in small pieces.
5. Measure the mushroom liquor; to one pint of liquor add one-half ounce of peppercorn.
6. Simmer for forty minutes.
7. Add one-fourth ounce of allspice and of cloves and one blade of mace and boil for fifteen minutes.
8. Take from fire and cool.
9. Strain through a cloth, bottle and seal.
