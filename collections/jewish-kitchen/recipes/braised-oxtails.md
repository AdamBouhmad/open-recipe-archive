## Ingredients

- two oxtails
- six onions
- water
- two carrots
- one green pepper
- sprig of parsley
- one-half cup of tomatoes
- six small potatoes
- browned flour
- eight lengths of macaroni

## Directions

1. Joint and wash the oxtails.
2. Slice and brown six onions in pot with oxtails.
3. When nicely browned add water enough to cover and stew slowly one hour.
4. Add two carrots, one green pepper, sprig of parsley, one-half cup of tomatoes and six small potatoes, and cook until tender.
5. Thicken with browned flour.
6. Cook separately eight lengths of macaroni.
7. Place cooked macaroni on dish and pour ragout over it and serve hot.
