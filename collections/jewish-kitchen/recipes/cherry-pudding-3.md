## Ingredients

- one-half pound of stale rye bread
- a wineglass of red wine
- two tablespoons of almonds
- yolks of four eggs
- half a cup of powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- one-half pound of sweet cherries
- one-half pound of sour cherries
- butter

## Directions

1. Grate one-half pound of stale rye bread and wet with a wineglass of red wine
2. Pound two tablespoons of almonds
3. Stir the yolks of four eggs with half a cup of powdered sugar and flavor with cinnamon
4. Add the grated bread and almonds
5. Stone one-half pound each of sweet and sour cherries
6. Mix all thoroughly with the beaten whites added last
7. Do not take the juice of the cherries
8. Butter the pudding mold well before putting in the mixture
9. To be eaten cold
