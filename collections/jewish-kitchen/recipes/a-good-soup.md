## Ingredients

- 3 lb knuckle of veal
- 2 quarts of water
- a little salt, pepper, a blade of mace
- a carrot
- a turnip
- a leek
- an onion
- a small white cabbage
- 3 sticks of celery
- 1/2 lb. of rice
- a little dried saffron
- a little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put in a good sized soup saucepan 3-lb knuckle of veal, 2 quarts of water, a little salt, pepper, a blade of mace
2. Cut and wash a carrot, turnip, leek, onion, a small white cabbage, 3 sticks of celery
3. Put them all in the saucepan
4. Wash 1/2-lb. of rice and put with
5. Let it simmer for 3 hours
6. Take out the knuckle, strip off the flesh, cut it in nice pieces
7. Put the flesh back in the saucepan
8. Let it all boil for half an hour longer
9. Colour with a little dried saffron
10. Have a little chopped parsley in the tureen
