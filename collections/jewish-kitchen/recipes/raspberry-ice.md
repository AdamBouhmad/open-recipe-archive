## Ingredients

- strong lemonade
- raspberry juice
- grated pineapple

## Directions

1. Make a strong lemonade
2. Add raspberry juice to taste, and some grated pineapple
3. Put into freezer and turn like ice cream and pack
4. Let stand five hours
