## Ingredients

- large onion
- oil or butter
- guard fish
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- dried saffron
- flour
- lemon juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. Chop very fine a large onion and put it in a stewpan with 2 spoonfuls of oil or ½ lb. butter
2. Cut the guard fish and lay it in with salt and pepper, a little grated nutmeg, a little dried saffron
3. Put it on to stew
4. When done rub in a basin a dessert spoonful of flour with the juice of a lemon
5. Turn it over the fish
6. When dished up, chop some parsley and sprinkle it over
