## Ingredients

- Nice pie crust
- Two slices of bread about two inches thick
- One cup of boiling water
- One dessertspoon of butter
- Juice and rind of one lemon
- One cup of sugar
- Yolks of two eggs
- Salt
- White of two eggs
- Four tablespoons of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Line a deep pie-plate with nice crust
2. Remove the crust from two slices of bread about two inches thick, pour over it one cup of boiling water
3. Add one dessertspoon of butter, and beat until the bread is well soaked and smooth
4. Add the juice and rind of one lemon, one cup of sugar, the yolks of two eggs well beaten, and a little salt
5. Mix well
6. Fill pie with mixture and bake in hot oven until firm
7. Beat white of two eggs to a stiff froth, add four tablespoons of powdered sugar and spread on top and brown
