## Ingredients

- one pint (two cups) of kidney beans
- one teaspoon butter
- one cup of brown sauce

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash one pint (two cups) of kidney beans
2. Let soak overnight in cold water
3. Drain and cook in fresh salted water till tender
4. Drain; shake in saucepan with one teaspoon butter three minutes
5. Add one cup of brown sauce and simmer five minutes
