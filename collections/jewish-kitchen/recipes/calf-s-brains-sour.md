## Ingredients

- brains
- ice-water
- cold salted water
- vinegar
- water
- crust of rye bread
- one onion
- ten whole peppers
- one bay leaf
- one or two cloves
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Lay the brains in ice-water and then skin
2. Skin by taking them up in your hands and patting them
3. Lay in cold salted water for an hour at least
4. Put on to boil in half vinegar and half water
5. Add crust of rye bread
6. Add one onion, cut up fine
7. Add ten whole peppers
8. Add one bay leaf
9. Add one or two cloves and a little salt
10. Boil altogether about fifteen minutes
11. Serve on a platter and decorate with parsley
12. Eat cold
