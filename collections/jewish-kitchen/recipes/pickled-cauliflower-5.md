## Ingredients

- four heads of cauliflower
- one cup of salt
- one-quarter of a cup of mixed pickle spices
- two quarts of vinegar
- two cups of sugar

## Directions

1. Separate flowerettes of four heads of cauliflower
2. Add one cup of salt, and let stand overnight
3. Place in colander, rinse with cold water and let drain
4. Tie one-quarter of a cup of mixed pickle spices in a thin bag and boil with two quarts of vinegar and two cups of sugar
5. Throw in the cauliflower
6. Boil a few minutes and pour to overflowing in wide-mouthed bottles or cans
7. Cork or cover and seal airtight
