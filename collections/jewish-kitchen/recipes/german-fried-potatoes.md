## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut up raw potatoes quite thin
2. Salt and pepper them
3. Drop in boiling fat
4. Cover up at first to soften them
5. Turn frequently to prevent burning
6. Remove the cover to brown slightly
