## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- grated cheese
- well-beaten eggs
- boiling oil
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Take some mashed potatoes, grated cheese, well-beaten eggs; make a good paste
2. Take tablespoonfuls of this mixture and drop in boiling oil
3. Fry until brown
4. Serve with a syrup made of sugar and water
