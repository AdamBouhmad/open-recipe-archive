## Ingredients

- cold meat
- tomatoes
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- floury potatoes
- water
- grated nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- egg
- lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare some cold meat exactly as you did for the marrows, with the difference that you chop the tomatoes and onion with the meat
2. Season it well, and add to the mixture two extra eggs
3. Boil some floury potatoes according to the size you wish to make it
4. Cut them in pieces, put a little salt and very little water
5. When they are done drain them well
6. Mash them in the saucepan, taste and see if they are salted enough
7. If so, put some pepper, a little grated nutmeg, a little chopped parsley, 1 egg
8. Mash it all well together
9. Lay some at the bottom of your dish, grease it well first
10. Then put in the forced meat
11. Sprinkle the juice of half a lemon
12. Cover it over with the remainder of the mashed potatoes
13. Smooth it well, and crossbar it with a knife
14. Put in the oven to bake
15. Serve it in the same dish with another dish under
