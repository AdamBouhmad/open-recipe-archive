## Ingredients

- remnants of cold turkey or fowl
- 1/2 pint of stock
- 1 onion
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- 2 or 3 allspice
- marjoram
- thyme
- a few pieces of smoked beef
- a pickled walnut
- a little flour
- the juice of a lemon
- a little browning
- slices of hard eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the pieces nicely and neatly, put them in a stewpan with 1/2 pint of stock, an onion, garlic, pepper, salt, mace, 2 or 3 allspice, marjoram, thyme, a few pieces of smoked beef, let it cook well, add a pickled walnut.
2. When done take out the fowl, strain the liquor through a sieve, return the fowl and gravy to the saucepan.
3. Rub in a little flour with the juice of a lemon, a little browning, stir it all well together.
4. Dish it up, and garnish with slices of hard eggs.
