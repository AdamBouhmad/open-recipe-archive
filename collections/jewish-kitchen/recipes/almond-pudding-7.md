## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- one-half cup of sugar
- one-half cup of grated walnuts or almonds
- one-half cup of grated white bread crumbs
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs very light with one-half cup of sugar
2. Add one-half cup of grated walnuts or almonds, one-half cup of grated white bread crumbs, then the stiffly-beaten whites of four eggs
3. Put in pudding form and steam from one and one-half to two hours
4. Serve with wine or fruit sauce
