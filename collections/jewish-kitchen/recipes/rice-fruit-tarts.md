## Ingredients

- whole rice
- milk
- white sugar
- currants, raspberries, cherries, or any other fruit
- the whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Boil in milk some whole rice till perfectly soft and sweeten with white sugar
2. When nearly cold, line a dish with it
3. Have ready some currants, raspberries, cherries, or any other fruit, which must have been previously stewed and sweetened, and fill the dish with it
4. Beat up the whites of three eggs to a froth, mixed with a little white sugar
5. Lay over the top and place it in the oven for half an hour
