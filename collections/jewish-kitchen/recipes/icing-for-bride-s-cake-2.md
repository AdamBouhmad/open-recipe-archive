## Ingredients

- whites of 4 fresh eggs
- 1 lb. of sifted sugar
- juice of a lemon well strained

## Directions

1. Break the whites of 4 fresh eggs in a deep basin
2. Mix in 1 lb. of sifted sugar and the juice of a lemon well strained
3. Whisk it all well together till it comes very thick, and feels quite hard
4. Lay it all over the cake
5. Stand it inside of a cool oven or in a warm place to harden the sugar
6. When you put on the ornaments do not disturb the sugar more than you can help
