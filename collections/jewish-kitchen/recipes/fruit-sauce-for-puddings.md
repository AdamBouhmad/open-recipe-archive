## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs
- teaspoonful of arrowroot
- jam
- hot water
- spoonful of lemon juice
- teacupful of almond milk

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of 2 eggs with a teaspoonful of arrowroot very smooth
2. If fresh fruit is not in season take some jam
3. Pour over it a little hot water to enable you to run it through a sieve
4. Stir it into the egg with a spoonful of lemon juice
5. Boil a teacupful of almond milk
6. Stir it in and let it boil to thicken
7. Turn it round your pudding
