## Ingredients

- stale bread crumbs
- chicken fat
- seeded raisins
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Take three cups of stale bread crumbs
2. Add one-half a cup of melted chicken fat
3. Add one cup of seeded raisins cut in small pieces, one teaspoon of salt and one-fourth teaspoon of white pepper
4. Mix thoroughly
