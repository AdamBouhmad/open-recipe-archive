## Ingredients

- salmon trout
- salt
- fennel
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the salmon trout in boiling water with a good spoonful of salt, no vinegar
2. When it is done, drain it well
3. Serve it up on a fish napkin, garnished with fennel
4. Serve with anchovy sauce or the sauce made expressly for fish No. 33
