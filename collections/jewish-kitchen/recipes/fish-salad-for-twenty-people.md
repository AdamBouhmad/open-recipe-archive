## Ingredients

- four pounds of halibut
- six hard-boiled eggs
- one pint bottle of pickles or chow-chow
- celery
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Boil four pounds of halibut, cool and shred fish.
2. Marinate the fish as directed.
3. When ready to serve add six hard-boiled eggs chopped, and one pint bottle of pickles or chow-chow.
4. The pickle may be omitted and celery cut fine be added.
5. When these are well mixed serve on lettuce leaves with mayonnaise dressing, of which one pint will be required.
