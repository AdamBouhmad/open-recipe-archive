## Ingredients

- ripe cranberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and pick ripe cranberries and set on to boil in a porcelain-lined kettle closely covered
2. When soft strain the pulp through a fine wire sieve
3. Measure the juice and add an equal quantity of sugar
4. Set it on to boil again and let it boil very fast for about ten minutes — but it must boil steadily all the time
5. Wet a mold with cold water, turn the jelly into it and set it away to cool
6. When firm turn it into a glass salver
