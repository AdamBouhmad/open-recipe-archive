## Ingredients

- gherkins, 50 or 100 nice sized ones
- salt
- cloves
- allspice
- peppercorns
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick 50 or 100 gherkins, nice sized ones
2. Put them in glass bottles with salt, cloves, allspice and peppercorns
3. Fill the bottles up with cold vinegar
4. Tie down air tight
5. Keep them in a dry place
6. Do not put too much of each spice
