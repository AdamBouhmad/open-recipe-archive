## Ingredients

- hot baked sweet potatoes
- one teaspoon of butter or drippings
- yolks of two eggs
- pepper
- salt
- fine bread crumbs
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Press through a ricer sufficient hot baked sweet potatoes to measure one pint
2. Place over the fire
3. Add one teaspoon of butter or drippings, the beaten yolks of two eggs, pepper and salt to taste
4. Beat well with a fork until the mixture leaves the sides of the pan
5. Cool slightly
6. Form into cones
7. Roll in fine bread crumbs
8. Dip in beaten eggs
9. Roll again in crumbs
10. Fry in hot oil or fat
