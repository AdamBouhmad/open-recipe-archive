## Ingredients

- Corn ears
- Cabbage
- Salt
- Green peppers
- White mustard-seed
- Vinegar
- Granulated sugar
- Turmeric
- Cornstarch
- Dry mustard

## Directions

1. Boil nine ears of corn and cut from cob.
2. Chop fine large head of cabbage and salt it.
3. Chop six green peppers.
4. Dissolve two tablespoons of cornstarch and one tablespoon of dry mustard in three pints of vinegar.
5. Put on to thicken.
6. Strain salt-water from the cabbage.
7. Mix all the ingredients (two tablespoons of white mustard-seed, one cup of granulated sugar, two tablespoons of turmeric) and stir in pot of vinegar.
8. Let all get very hot and seal in pint jars.
