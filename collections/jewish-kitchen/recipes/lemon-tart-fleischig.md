## Ingredients

- rich crust
- three whole eggs
- yolks of three eggs
- one cup of sugar
- juice of three lemons
- grated rind of one lemon
- juice of one orange
- three egg whites
- three tablespoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Make a rich crust and bake in small spring form
2. Beat three whole eggs and yolks of three very light with one cup of sugar
3. Add juice of three lemons and grated rind of one and juice of one orange
4. Put whole on stove and stir until it comes to a boil
5. Put on baked crust
6. Spread a meringue made of the remaining three whites and three tablespoons of sugar on top
7. Put in oven to brown
