## Ingredients

- two calf's feet
- one pound of beef
- one calf's head
- one quart water
- one cup of white wine
- one celery root
- three small onions
- a bunch of parsley
- one dozen whole peppercorns
- half a dozen cloves
- two bay leaves
- one teaspoon of fine salt
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Set on to boil two calf's feet chopped up, one pound of beef and one calf's head with one quart water and one cup of white wine
2. Add one celery root, three small onions, a bunch of parsley, one dozen whole peppercorns, half a dozen cloves, two bay leaves and a teaspoon of fine salt
3. Boil steadily for eight hours and then pour through a fine hair sieve
4. When cold remove every particle of fat and set on to boil again, skimming until clear
5. Break two eggs, shells and all, into a deep bowl and beat them up with one cup of vinegar
6. Pour some of the soup stock into this and set all back on the stove to boil up once, stirring all the while
7. Remove from the fire and pour through a jelly-bag as you would jelly
8. Pour into jelly-glasses or one large mould
9. Set on ice
