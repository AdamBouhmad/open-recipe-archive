## Ingredients

- cinnamon to taste
- allspice to taste
- one cup of hot water
- three eggs
- sugar
- one pint of wine

## Directions

1. Put cinnamon and allspice (to taste) in a cup of hot water to steep.
2. Add three eggs well beaten with sugar.
3. Heat to a boil a pint of wine, then add spice and eggs.
4. Stir for three minutes and serve.
