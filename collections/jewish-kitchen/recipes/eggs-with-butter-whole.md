## Ingredients

- butter
- salt
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Have a small white dish or plate that will stand the fire
2. Put it on the hob quite close to the fire
3. Put a good piece of butter (1 an ounce will be enough for 6 eggs)
4. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper
5. Crack the eggs in a cup
6. Slip each one separately on the dish that is ready to cook them
7. They will set nicely
8. Put over them a little salt and pepper so that they will be seasoned right through
9. Send them up to table on the same dish
10. Another dish under
