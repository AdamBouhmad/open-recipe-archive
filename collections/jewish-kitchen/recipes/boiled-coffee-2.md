## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon coffee per cup of boiling water
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- clean egg shells
- 1 tablespoon cold water for settling
- cream or scalded milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix coffee with two tablespoons of cold water
2. Clean egg shells and put in the pot
3. Allow this to come to a boil and add boiling water
4. Bring to a boil and boil for one minute
5. Add a tablespoon of cold water to assist the grounds in settling
6. Stand the pot where it will keep hot, but not boil, for five minutes
7. Serve at once
8. Fill the cup one-fourth full of hot scalded milk
9. Pour on the freshly made coffee, adding sugar
