## Ingredients

- cold roast or boiled chicken
- gravy
- hard-boiled eggs
- thin slices of bread
- mustard

## Directions

1. Mince some cold roast or boiled chicken in a chopping bowl.
2. Mix the gravy with it, adding a few hard-boiled eggs, which have been minced to a powder.
3. Mix all into a soft paste.
4. Cut thin slices of bread and spread the chicken between the slices (if desired you may add a little mustard).
5. Press the pieces gently together.
