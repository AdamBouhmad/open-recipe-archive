## Ingredients

- sheep's hearts
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- 2 eggs
- fat
- potatoes
- walnut liquor or ketchup
- cabbage or Brussels sprouts

## Directions

1. Prepare the hearts and cut them in slices
2. Dry them on a coarse cloth
3. Have in a plate some bread crumbs mixed with parsley chopped, some marjoram, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg
4. Mix this all well together
5. Have in another plate 2 eggs well beaten
6. Put in the frying pan some fat and heat until boiling
7. Dip the slices well in the eggs and then in the crumb mixture
8. Put them into the boiling fat
9. Have some potatoes boiled and mashed with a little salt and pepper
10. Make a bed of them in the dish
11. Lay the heart upon the mashed potatoes
12. Strain the fat from the frying pan into a basin
13. Pour a little walnut liquor or ketchup on to the pan
14. Set it over the fire and when it boils pour it over the heart and potatoes
15. Alternatively, use boiled cabbage or Brussels sprouts instead of potatoes
