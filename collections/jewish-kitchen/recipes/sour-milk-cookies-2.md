## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- two or three eggs
- two-thirds of a cup of sour milk
- a teaspoon of soda
- hot water
- flour
- a teaspoon of cardamom seed

## Directions

1. Dissolve a teaspoon of soda in a little hot water.
2. Add part of it at a time to the milk until it foams as you stir it. Be careful not to get in too much.
3. Mix up soft, only using flour sufficient to roll out thin.
4. A teaspoon of cardamom seed may be sprinkled into the dough.
