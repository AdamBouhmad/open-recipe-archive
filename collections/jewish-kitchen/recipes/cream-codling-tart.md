## Ingredients

- apples
- 6 eggs
- almond milk
- rind of a lemon
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stew the apples
2. Lay them in a dish
3. Make a custard of 6 eggs and almond milk
4. Flavour with the rind of a lemon
5. Sprinkle over some cinnamon
