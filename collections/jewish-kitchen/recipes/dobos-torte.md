## Ingredients

- Yolks of six eggs
- One-half pound of powdered sugar
- Three-fourths cup of flour
- Whites of six eggs
- Butter
- One-half pound of sweet butter
- One-half pound of sweet chocolate
- One cup of strong liquid coffee
- One-half pound of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Cream yolks of six eggs with one-half pound of powdered sugar
2. Add three-fourths cup of flour sifted three times
3. Add beaten whites of six eggs lightly and carefully into the mixture
4. Butter pie plates on under side
5. Sprinkle with flour lightly over the butter
6. Spread the mixture very thin
7. This amount makes one cake of twelve layers
8. Remove layers at once with a spatula
9. For filling: Cream one-half pound of sweet butter and put on ice immediately
10. Take one-half pound of sweet chocolate and break it into a cup of strong liquid coffee
11. Add one-half pound of granulated sugar
12. Let it boil until you can pull it almost like candy
13. Remove from fire and stir the chocolate until it is quite cold
14. When cold add the chocolate mixture to the creamed butter
15. Spread filling thin between the layers
16. Spread the icing thicker on top and sides of the cake
17. Bake and make filling a day or two before needed
