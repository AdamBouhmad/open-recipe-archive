## Ingredients

- cream cheese
- cream
- preserved gooseberries
- biscuits

## Directions

1. Moisten cream cheese with cream and beat to a froth
2. Arrange in a mound shape on a dish and turn preserved gooseberries over it
3. Serve with biscuits
