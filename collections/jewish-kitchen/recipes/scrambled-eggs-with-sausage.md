## Ingredients

- one pound of cold, boiled sausage
- two tablespoons of hot fat
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Take one pound of cold, boiled sausage, skin and slice in half-inch pieces.
2. Place in a frying-pan with two tablespoons of hot fat.
3. Brown on both sides a few minutes.
4. Just before serving add three eggs, beaten slightly.
5. Mix and cook until the eggs are set and serve immediately.
