## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two tablespoons of cold water
- a quarter of a teaspoon of salt
- a pinch of pepper
- a teaspoon of chopped parsley
- a quarter of a teaspoon of grated onion
- a teaspoon of fine butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well in a bowl
2. Add cold water, salt, pepper, chopped parsley, grated onion and butter
3. Mix well with a wooden spoon
4. Dissolve butter in the spider
5. Add at once the beaten eggs
6. Incline the spider to the handle for an instant
7. Shake the omelet into the centre
8. Turn up the right edge, then the left
9. Fry briskly five minutes
10. Serve
