## Ingredients

- a pound of chopped meat
- grated onion
- an egg
- matzoth flour
- white pepper
- one-half cup of water
- fat
- sugar
- a quarter cup of large black raisins
- a few slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Take a pound of chopped meat and add grated onion, an egg, matzoth flour, white pepper, mix and form into small balls.
2. Put in pot with one-half cup of water, fat, sugar, a quarter cup of large black raisins and a few slices of lemon and let stew one-half hour.
3. Thicken gravy with tablespoon of flour browned in a tablespoon of fat and serve.
