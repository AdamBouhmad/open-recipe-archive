## Ingredients

- thin slices of fat smoked beef
- dripping
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put in your meat fryingpan a little dripping, fry the beef, put it on a plate at the side to keep hot.
2. Then fry the eggs nice and round a light brown, put the beef on the dish and one egg on each slice, turn a little of the hot fat in the dish.
3. Serve very hot.
