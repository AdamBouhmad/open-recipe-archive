## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. flour
- 1/4 lb. chopped suet
- salt
- cold water
- 1 lb. sausage
- yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/4 lb. flour, 1/4 lb. of chopped suet, and a pinch of salt
2. Mix it with a little cold water
3. Roll it out three times
4. Boil 1 lb. sausage and skin it
5. Roll the paste out the size of the sausage and lay it on the sausage
6. Roll it up and fasten the ends with a little yolk of an egg
7. Brush the roll all over with the egg
8. Bake it in a brisk oven
9. This is very nice for lunch, or for a side dish cut in rounds and garnished with parsley
