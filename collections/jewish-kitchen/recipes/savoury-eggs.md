## Ingredients

- eggs
- grated hung beef
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Boil some eggs hard, put them into cold water, cut them into halves, take out the yolks
2. beat them up in a mortar with grated hung beef, fill the halves with this mixture
3. fry lightly, and serve with brown gravy
