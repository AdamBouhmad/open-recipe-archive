## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- salt
- water
- butter
- pepper
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Remove any wilted leaves from the outside of the sprouts
2. Let them stand in cold salted water from fifteen to twenty minutes
3. Put the sprouts into salted, rapidly boiling water
4. Cook, uncovered, fifteen or twenty minutes or until tender, but not until they lose their shape
5. Drain them thoroughly in a colander
6. Place them in a saucepan with butter, pepper and salt
7. Toss them until seasoned or mix them lightly with just enough white sauce to coat them
