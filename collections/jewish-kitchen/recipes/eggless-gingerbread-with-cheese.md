## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one teaspoon of soda
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- two teaspoons of ginger
- three-fourths cup of grated cheese
- one-half cup of hot water
- one-half cup of molasses

## Directions

1. Sift two cups of flour, one teaspoon of soda, one-half teaspoon of salt and two teaspoons of ginger
2. Melt three-fourths cup of grated cheese in one-half cup of hot water
3. Add one-half cup of molasses and blend perfectly
4. Add the flour and seasonings very gradually and beat thoroughly
5. Bake in muffin rings for fifteen minutes
6. Serve while warm
