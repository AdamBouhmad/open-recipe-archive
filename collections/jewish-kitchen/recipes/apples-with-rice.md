## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- water or milk
- butter
- salt
- about six apples
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of rice in water or milk
2. Rub the kettle all over with a piece of butter before putting in the rice
3. Season with salt and add a lump of butter
4. When cooked, add about six apples, pared, quartered and cored, sugar and cinnamon
5. This makes a nice side dish, or dessert, served with cream
