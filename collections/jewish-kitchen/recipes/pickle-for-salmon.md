## Ingredients

- vinegar
- white wine
- water
- mace
- clove
- ginger root
- whole peppers
- grated horseradish
- salmon
- strong salt and water

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of vinegar, white wine and water.
2. Boil these with a little mace, a clove or two, a bit of ginger root, one or two whole peppers and some grated horseradish.
3. Take out the last named ingredient when sufficiently boiled.
4. Pour the pickle over the salmon, previously boiled in strong salt and water.
