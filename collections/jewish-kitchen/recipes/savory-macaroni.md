## Ingredients

- flour
- half a pound macaroni
- salted water
- butter
- one level tablespoon finely chopped onion
- three or four sliced tomatoes
- half teaspoon powdered mixed herbs
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- one pint milk
- two tablespoons browned flour
- water
- two tablespoons grated or finely chopped cheese

## Directions

1. Bake some flour to a pale fawn color and pass it through a sieve or strainer to remove its gritty particles
2. Break half a pound of macaroni into short pieces, boil them in salted water until fairly tender, then drain
3. In a little butter in a saucepan brown a level tablespoon of very finely chopped onion
4. Add three or four sliced tomatoes, a half teaspoon of powdered mixed herbs, a little nutmeg, salt and pepper
5. When the tomatoes are reduced to a pulp add one pint of milk and allow it to come to the boiling point
6. Mix in two tablespoons of the browned flour moistened with water
7. Stir and boil till smooth, press the whole through a strainer and return to the saucepan
8. When boiling, add the macaroni and a few minutes later stir in two tablespoons of grated or finely chopped cheese
9. Serve at once or keep the pan for half an hour by the side of the fire in an outer vessel of water, or turn into a casserole and finish off in the oven
