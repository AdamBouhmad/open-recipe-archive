## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- cold water

## Directions

1. Wash the rhubarb thoroughly in pure water; cut it into pieces and pack it in sterilized jars.
2. Cover with cold water; let it stand ten minutes; pour off the water.
3. Fill again to overflowing with fresh cold water; seal with sterilized rubber rings and covers.
4. Set away in a cool, dark place.
