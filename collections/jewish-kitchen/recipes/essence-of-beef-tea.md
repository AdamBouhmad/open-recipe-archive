## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of beef
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Cut up 1 lb. of beef into a mince, put it into a small jug with only a pinch of salt
2. Stand the mug in a saucepan with boiling water, let it boil at the side of the fire for several hours
3. Tie down the top of the mug for no steam to escape
4. From the 1 lb. of meat you will have half a teacup of juice
