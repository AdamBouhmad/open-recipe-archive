## Ingredients

- one-half cup of rice (brown preferred)
- an equal quantity of bread crumbs
- one level teaspoon of salt
- one-half saltspoon of black pepper
- one cup of chopped nuts (pecans or peanuts)
- one tablespoon of chopped parsley
- one egg

## Directions

1. Boil one-half cup of rice (brown preferred)
2. Drain and dry it
3. Mix with an equal quantity of bread crumbs
4. Add level teaspoon of salt and one-half saltspoon of black pepper
5. Stir in one cup of chopped nuts — pecans or peanuts
6. Add one tablespoon of chopped parsley and one egg
7. Mix thoroughly and pack in bread-pan to mold it
8. Turn it from pan into baking-pan and bake slowly three-quarters of an hour
9. Serve with cream sauce or puree of peas
