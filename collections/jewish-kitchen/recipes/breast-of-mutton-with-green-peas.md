## Ingredients

- breast of mutton
- soaked bread
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- grated nutmeg
- grated rind of a lemon
- one egg
- chopped onion
- peas
- mint
- flour
- cold water
- lumps of sugar

## Directions

1. Take a breast of mutton not too fat and lay it on your board
2. Make a nice dressing of soaked bread, parsley, salt, pepper, marjoram, grated nutmeg, the grated rind of a lemon
3. Mix up with one egg
4. Put it between the bone and flesh
5. Skewer it through with a fine iron skewer
6. Put it in a stewpan with some chopped onion, salt and pepper
7. When it has simmered for an hour, have washed some peas and put them in when done
8. Chop some parsley and mint and put it in a basin with a spoonful of flour mixed with a little cold water
9. When you put in the peas, put three or four lumps of sugar
10. Stir in the sauce just before you dish it up
