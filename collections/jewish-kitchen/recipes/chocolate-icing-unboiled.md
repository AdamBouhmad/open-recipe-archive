## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- one and one-half cups of pulverized sugar
- four tablespoons of grated chocolate
- two teaspoons of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs and one and one-half cups of pulverized sugar, added gradually while beating
2. Beat until very thick, then add four tablespoons of grated chocolate and two teaspoons of vanilla
