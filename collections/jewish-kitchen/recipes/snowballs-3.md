## Ingredients

- nice large baking apples
- preserves or jam
- sugar
- rich pie crust
- boiled icing

## Directions

1. Pare and core nice large baking apples
2. fill the holes with some preserves or jam
3. roll the apples in sugar and cover with a rich pie crust and bake
4. When done, cover with a boiled icing and set back in the oven, leaving both doors open to let the icing dry
