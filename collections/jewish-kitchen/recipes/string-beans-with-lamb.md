## Ingredients

- small breast of lamb
- two large onions
- one-quarter peck of beans
- six large tomatoes
- two cups of water
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Take a small breast of lamb, two large onions, one-quarter peck of beans (string and cut in long thin pieces)
2. Skin six large tomatoes
3. Add two cups of water
4. Cook until the beans are tender
5. Add one tablespoon of flour to thicken
