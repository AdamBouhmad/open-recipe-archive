## Ingredients

- turkey
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- blade of mace
- lemon
- veal stuffing
- celery or mushroom sauce
- boiled tongue or smoked beef
- boiled cauliflower
- water cresses

## Directions

1. Put the turkey in a saucepan, cover it with boiling water, as the boiling water sets the colour of the flesh at once
2. Put salt into the water and a little pepper, a blade of mace
3. Rub the turkey over with the juice of half a lemon before you put in the water
4. Stuff it with a veal stuffing
5. Let it simmer for 2 hours gently
6. If a large turkey, send up in the sauce tureen celery or mushroom sauce
7. Boiled tongue or smoked beef must always accompany this dish
8. Garnish the turkey with small pieces of boiled cauliflower and water cresses
