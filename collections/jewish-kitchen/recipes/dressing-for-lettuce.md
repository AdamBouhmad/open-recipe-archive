## Ingredients

- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- one teaspoon salad oil or melted butter
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one-half teaspoon prepared mustard
- pepper
- two tablespoons white sugar
- one-half teacup vinegar

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs to a paste, adding one teaspoon of salad oil or melted butter, being careful to add only a few drops at a time. Add one-half teaspoon salt, one-half teaspoon of prepared mustard, very little pepper, two tablespoons of white sugar. Stir very hard, then pour in gradually one-half teacup of vinegar.
