## Ingredients

- one quart beans
- salt
- one tablespoon flour
- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons strained lemon juice

## Directions

1. String and remove the ends from one quart of beans. Cut into short lengths. Cover with boiling water, add one level tablespoon of salt and cook until tender, but not soft. Drain and save one cup of the liquor. Cream one tablespoon of flour with two tablespoons of butter. Pour the liquid over the flour and butter, stirring constantly to avoid lumping. Cook this sauce for five minutes, remove from stove and stir in two tablespoons of strained lemon juice. Pour this over the beans and serve.
