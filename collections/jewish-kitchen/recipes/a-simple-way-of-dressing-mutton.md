## Ingredients

- fillet from a small leg or shoulder of mutton
- egg
- seasoning
- strong gravy
- balls or fritters

## Directions

1. Take the fillet off a small leg or shoulder of mutton
2. rub it well over with egg and seasoning
3. partly roast it
4. place it in a stewpan with a little strong gravy
5. stew gently till thoroughly done
6. garnish with a few balls or fritters
