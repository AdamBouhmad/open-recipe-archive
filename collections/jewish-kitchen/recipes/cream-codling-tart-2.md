## Ingredients

- 6 codling apples
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 2 or 3 cloves
- 1 wine-glass of orange-flower water
- puff paste
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sifted sugar
- 1/2 pint of almond milk

## Directions

1. Peel and core 6 codling apples, cut them in small thick pieces
2. Put them in a saucepan with 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar, 2 or 3 cloves
3. Let them boil to a pulp, then beat them well with a spoon till they are quite smooth
4. Mix with them a wine-glass of orange-flower water
5. Line a pie dish with puff paste
6. When the apples are cold turn them into the pie dish
7. Put a border of paste round, crossbar the top with pastry
8. Bake it in a quick oven
9. Mix in a basin 3 eggs, with 3 tablespoonfuls of sifted sugar, mix it well together
10. Boil 1/2 pint of almond milk, and stir your eggs in with a little more orange-flower water
11. Let it cool, then turn it over the top
