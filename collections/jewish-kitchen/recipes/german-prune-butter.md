## Ingredients

- prunes
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Remove pits and wash prunes.
2. Take three-quarters of a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit, and enough water to keep from burning.
3. Do not stir but remove from the sides of the kettle occasionally.
4. Let boil for hours.
5. When done, place in glasses.
6. Let cool.
7. Cover with paraffin.
