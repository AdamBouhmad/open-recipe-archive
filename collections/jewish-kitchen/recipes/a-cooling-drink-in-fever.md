## Ingredients

- a little tea-sage
- a couple of sprigs of balm
- a lemon
- a quart of boiling water

## Directions

1. Put a little tea-sage and a couple of sprigs of balm into a jug with a lemon thinly sliced and the peel cut into strips
2. Pour over a quart of boiling water
3. Sweeten and let it cool
