## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- juice of a lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Lay the fish in a marinade after it is washed and dried in a little oil, pepper, salt, vinegar, or juice of a lemon
2. When done, rub some butter over, or more oil and lemon
