## Ingredients

- four teaspoons of soda
- two teaspoons of salt
- four cups of white flour
- four cups of bran flour
- one cup of molasses
- four cups of sweet milk
- chopped nuts or raisins

## Directions

1. Sift four teaspoons of soda, two teaspoons of salt with four cups of white flour
2. Add four cups of bran flour and mix well
3. Add one cup of molasses and four cups of sweet milk
4. Use chopped nuts or raisins or both as desired
5. Place in greased pans (four and a half by nine inches)
6. Bake one hour in a moderate oven
