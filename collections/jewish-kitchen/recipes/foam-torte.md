## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- one cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of vanilla
- sliced bananas, peaches and cream or strawberries

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites well
2. Add one tablespoon of vinegar drop by drop
3. Add one cup of sugar and one tablespoon of vanilla
4. Beat for twenty minutes
5. Line spring form with this batter on all sides
6. Reserve a little of the mixture and drop by drops on top of torte
7. Bake forty-five minutes in moderate oven
8. Serve with sliced bananas, peaches and cream or strawberries
