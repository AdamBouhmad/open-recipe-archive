## Ingredients

- a piece of melted fat, the size of an egg
- 2 onions
- a little beef gravy
- a small spoonful of flour
- a little cayenne
- salt

## Directions

1. Put melted fat into a small saucepan
2. Add 2 onions sliced thin and let them brown
3. Add a little beef gravy with a small spoonful of flour and a little cayenne
4. Add salt and boil it well
5. Throw it over the steak
