## Ingredients

- grated stale bread
- an equal quantity of chopped parsley
- clarified suet
- beaten yolks of eggs
- dry and fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Add to grated stale bread, an equal quantity of chopped parsley
2. Season it well, and mix it with clarified suet
3. Brush the cutlets with beaten yolks of eggs
4. Lay on the mixture thickly with a knife
5. Sprinkle over with dry and fine bread crumbs
