## Ingredients

- a gill of fresh cream
- a spoonful of beaten almonds
- spirit of camphor

## Directions

1. Mix with a gill of fresh cream a spoonful of beaten almonds
2. When perfectly smooth put it in toilette pots
3. Use as ointment for chaps
4. Add a little spirit of camphor to it if keeping for a week
