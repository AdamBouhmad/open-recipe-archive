## Ingredients

- one-half cup of sugar
- one-quarter teaspoon of salt
- two cups of flour
- two cups of milk
- one-half cup of butter
- one quart of sliced apples

## Directions

1. Mix one-half cup of sugar, one-quarter teaspoon of salt, two cups of flour and gradually two cups of milk to make a smooth batter
2. Melt one-half cup or a little less of butter in a large shallow dripping-pan and let it spread all over the pan to grease it well
3. Pour one-half cup of butter and one quart of sliced apples to the batter
4. Mix and pour into pan or pans not more than three-quarters of an inch deep
5. Bake in a moderate oven, thirty to forty-five minutes, until a golden brown
