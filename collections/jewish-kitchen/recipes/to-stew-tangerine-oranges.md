## Ingredients

- 12 tangerine oranges
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 pint of water

## Directions

1. Take 12 tangerine oranges, peel them very carefully, and peel off all the white skin
2. Boil 1 lb. of loaf sugar in 1/2 pint of water to a syrup
3. Put in the oranges to simmer for half an hour
4. Dish them up
5. When the syrup is cold pour it over the oranges
6. Cut a little of the peel in very thin strips and boil in with the sugar
