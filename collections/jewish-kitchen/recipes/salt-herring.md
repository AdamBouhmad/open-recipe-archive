## Ingredients

- salt herring
- cold water
- boiled potatoes
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak salt herring over night in cold water, that the salt may be drawn out.
2. Drain and serve with boiled potatoes, or bone and place in kettle of cold water, let come to a boil and let simmer a few minutes until tender.
3. Drain and pour melted butter over them and serve hot with boiled or fried potatoes.
