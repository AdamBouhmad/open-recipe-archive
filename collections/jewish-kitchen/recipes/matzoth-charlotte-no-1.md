## Ingredients

- one matzoth
- two eggs
- one-fourth teaspoon of salt
- one-fourth cup of chopped almonds
- one-fourth cup of raisins
- one-fourth cup of currants

## Directions

1. Soak one matzoth
2. Beat and add to the beaten yolks of two eggs
3. Add one-fourth teaspoon of salt, one-fourth cup of chopped almonds, one-fourth cup of raisins, one-fourth cup of currants
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Fold in the stiffly-beaten whites of two eggs
6. Bake in a greased baking-dish
