## Ingredients

- about three matzoth
- handful of raisins
- almonds
- salt
- peel of a lemon
- yolks of four eggs
- one cup of sugar
- cinnamon
- oil
- whites of four eggs
- stewed prunes

## Directions

1. Soak about three matzoth
2. Seed a handful of raisins and pound as many almonds as you have raisins
3. Press every drop of water out of the matzoth, put them in a bowl and stir them to a cream
4. Add a pinch of salt, the peel of a lemon, yolks of four eggs and a cup of sugar, the raisins and almonds, and also a little cinnamon
5. Heat some oil in a spider; the more fat the lighter the chrimsel will be
6. Last add the stiffly-beaten whites to the dough
7. Fry a light brown on both sides; use about a tablespoonful of batter for each chrimsel
8. Serve with stewed prunes
9. Lay the chrimsel on a large platter and pour the prunes over all
10. Eat hot
