## Ingredients

- pippins
- water
- white sugar
- lemon peel
- preserve

## Directions

1. Peel the pippins and stew them gently with a little water, white sugar, and a little lemon peel
2. Preserve is usually used to ornament the top of each apple
3. They should, when done, look white and rather transparent
