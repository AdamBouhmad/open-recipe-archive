## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- Salt
- Flour
- One cup of ice-water
- One whole egg
- Yolks of two eggs
- One tablespoon of brown sugar
- Two teaspoons of baking-powder
- Pulverized sugar
- Compote of peaches
- Whipped cream or ice-cream

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of butter and a little salt with just enough flour to thoroughly mix
2. Add one cup of ice-water, one whole egg, and the yolks of two eggs mixed with a tablespoon of brown sugar to the flour in which you have previously sifted two teaspoons of baking-powder
3. Handle the dough as little as possible in mixing
4. Bake in round rings in a hot oven until a light brown
5. When baked, sift pulverized sugar over the top and fill the hollow centre with a compote of peaches
6. Heap whipped cream or ice-cream on top of each one
