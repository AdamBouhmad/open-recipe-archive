## Ingredients

- two or three onions
- a little oil
- a little beef gravy
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Slice finely, and brown in a little oil, two or three onions
2. Put them in a little beef gravy
3. Add cayenne pepper, salt, and the juice of a lemon
