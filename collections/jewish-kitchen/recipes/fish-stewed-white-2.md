## Ingredients

- an onion, finely chopped
- a little oil
- half a pint of water
- fish
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- ground allspice
- nutmeg
- ginger
- the beaten yolks of four eggs
- the juice of two lemons
- a tea spoonful of flour
- a table spoonful of cold water
- a little saffron

## Directions

1. Put an onion, finely chopped, into a stew-pan, with a little oil, till the onion becomes brown
2. Add half a pint of water, and place the fish in the stew-pan
3. Season with pepper, salt, mace, ground allspice, nutmeg, and ginger
4. Let it stew gently till the fish is done
5. Prepare the beaten yolks of four eggs, with the juice of two lemons, and a tea spoonful of flour, a table spoonful of cold water, and a little saffron, mix
