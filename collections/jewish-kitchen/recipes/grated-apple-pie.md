## Ingredients

- rich puff paste
- four or five large tart apples
- yolks of two eggs
- about half a cup of sugar
- a few raisins
- a few currants
- a few pounded almonds
- a pinch of ground cinnamon
- grated peel of a lemon
- whites of the eggs
- about three tablespoons of pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Line a pie-plate with a rich puff paste
2. Pare and grate four or five large tart apples into a bowl into which you have stirred the yolks of two eggs with about half a cup of sugar
3. Add a few raisins, a few currants, a few pounded almonds, a pinch of ground cinnamon, and the grated peel of a lemon
4. Bake in a quick oven with no top crust
5. In the meantime, make a meringue of the whites of the eggs by beating them to a very stiff froth and add about three tablespoons of pulverized sugar
6. Spread this over the pie when baked and set back in the oven until brown
7. Eat cold
