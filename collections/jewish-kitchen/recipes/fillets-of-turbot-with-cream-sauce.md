## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. butter
- tablespoonful of flour
- salt
- cayenne
- water from boiled turbot
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. When the turbot comes from table take off the fillets and skin them at once while hot
2. The next day cut your fillets as even as possible
3. Put them in a deep dish at the side of the fire with some salt and pepper, a little water, to keep them warm
4. Put into a small saucepan 1/4 lb. of butter, a tablespoonful of flour, salt, a little cayenne
5. Do not let this sauce boil, only melt it till it is thick, and season high
6. Have some mashed potatoes in a dish for table
7. Take the fillets out carefully and lay them on the potatoes
8. Pour the sauce over and send it up
