## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet butter
- half a pound of sifted powdered sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- grated peel of one lemon
- half a pound of raisins
- a little citron
- half a pound of the finest flour
- whites of six eggs
- blanched and pounded almonds

## Directions

1. Rub to a cream half a pound of sweet butter and half a pound of sifted powdered sugar
2. Add the yolks of six eggs, one at a time, and the grated peel of one lemon
3. Stone half a pound of raisins and add also a little citron, cut very fine
4. Add gradually half a pound of the finest flour, sifted three or four times
5. Add the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
6. Pour this mixture into a well-buttered mold, into which you have strewn some blanched and pounded almonds
7. Boil fully three hours
8. Serve with sweet brandy or fruit sauce
