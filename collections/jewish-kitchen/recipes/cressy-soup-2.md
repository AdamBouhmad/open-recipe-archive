## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 large carrots
- a pint of water
- salt and pepper
- a quart of stock No. 1
- flour
- a little cayenne pepper
- a few very small pieces of toasted bread
- a little fine chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut carrots in small pieces
2. Put them in a saucepan with a pint of water, a little salt and pepper
3. Let them boil to a pulp
4. Rub them well through a sieve
5. Have ready a quart of stock No. 1
6. When hot, put in the liquor the carrots were boiled in and the pulp
7. Let it all boil together if not thick enough
8. Rub a couple of spoonfuls of flour with a little cold water and stir it in
9. The soup should be as thick as good cream
10. Add a little cayenne pepper
11. Have ready in the tureen a few very small pieces of toasted bread cut in the shape of dice and a little fine chopped parsley
