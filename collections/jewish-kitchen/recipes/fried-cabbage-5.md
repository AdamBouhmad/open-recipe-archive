## Ingredients

- one medium head of cabbage
- three tablespoons of fat (from top of soup stock preferred)
- one sour apple
- caraway seed to taste
- salt
- paprika
- one-half onion

## Directions

1. Cut one medium head of cabbage fine, soak ten minutes in salt water.
2. Drain, heat three tablespoons of fat (from top of soup stock preferred), add cabbage, one sour apple peeled and cut up, caraway seed to taste, salt, paprika and one-half onion minced.
3. Cover very closely and cook slowly for one hour.
