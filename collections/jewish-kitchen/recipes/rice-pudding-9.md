## Ingredients

- three cups of milk
- half a cup of rice
- yolks of three eggs
- three tablespoons of white sugar
- salt
- vanilla
- whites of the eggs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. To three cups of milk, add half a cup of rice, which you have previously scalded with hot water
2. Boil in a double boiler until quite soft
3. Add salt to the rice while boiling
4. Beat the yolks of three eggs with three tablespoons of white sugar, add this just before taking it off the fire
5. Stir it thoroughly with a wooden spoon, but do not let it boil any more
6. Flavor with vanilla
7. Beat the whites of the eggs with powdered sugar to a stiff froth
8. Put the custard into the pudding dish in which you wish to serve it
9. Spread with the beaten whites and let it brown slightly in the oven
