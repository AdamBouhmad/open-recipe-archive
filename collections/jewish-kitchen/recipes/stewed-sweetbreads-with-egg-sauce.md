## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 throat sweetbreads
- boiling water
- fat of smoked beef
- onion
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- small meat balls
- button mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take 2 or 3 throat sweetbreads and put them in boiling water to plump them up
2. Take them out and put them on your paste board and shape them nicely
3. Cut a piece of the fat of smoked beef in length of two inches
4. Put each piece through the larding needle and run it through the sweetbread till it looks like a porcupine
5. Place the sweetbreads in your stewpan with an onion sliced, a little marjoram, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
6. Put in a few small meat balls and a few button mushrooms
7. Boil the mushrooms first so that they do not make the stew dark
8. Make an egg sauce as before directed
