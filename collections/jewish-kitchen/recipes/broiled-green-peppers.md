## Ingredients

- green peppers
- cold water
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Broil on all sides
2. place the broiled peppers in a dish of cold water so that the skin can be easily removed
3. When the peppers are all peeled put in a bowl or crock
4. add French dressing and cover closely
