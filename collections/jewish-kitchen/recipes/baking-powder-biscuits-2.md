## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- four teaspoons of baking-powder
- four tablespoons of butter
- one-half to two-thirds cup of water or milk

## Directions

1. Sift two cups of flour with one-half teaspoon of salt and four teaspoons of baking-powder
2. Sift in four tablespoons of butter
3. Cut butter in with two knives and mix with one-half to two-thirds cup of water or milk
4. Stir this in quickly with a knife
5. When well mixed place on a well-floured board and roll out about one inch thick, work quickly
6. Cut with a biscuit cutter or the cover of a half-pound baking-powder can
7. Place on a greased pan
8. Bake quickly in a well-heated quick oven ten to fifteen minutes
