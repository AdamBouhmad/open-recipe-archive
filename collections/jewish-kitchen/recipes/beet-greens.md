## Ingredients

- beet greens
- one teaspoon of salt for every two quarts of greens
- boiling water
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Examine the leaves carefully to be sure that there are no insects on them.
2. Wash thoroughly in several waters.
3. Put over the fire in a large kettle of boiling water.
4. Add one teaspoon of salt for every two quarts of greens.
5. Boil rapidly about thirty minutes or until tender.
6. Drain off the water.
7. Chop well and season with butter and salt.
