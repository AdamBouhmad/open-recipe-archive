## Ingredients

- sweetbread
- one tablespoon of fat
- one tablespoon of flour
- canned mushrooms
- soup stock
- catsup
- mushroom catsup
- kitchen bouquet
- clove of garlic
- small onion
- salt
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Clean sweetbread, boil until tender, and cut in small pieces
2. Take one tablespoon of fat, blend in one tablespoon of flour
3. add half the liquor of a can of mushrooms and enough soup stock to make the necessary amount of gravy
4. add a little catsup, mushroom catsup, and a few drops of kitchen bouquet, a clove of garlic, and a small onion
5. salt and pepper to taste
6. Cook this about an hour, and then remove garlic and onion
7. Add sweetbreads, mushrooms, and two hard-boiled eggs chopped very fine
