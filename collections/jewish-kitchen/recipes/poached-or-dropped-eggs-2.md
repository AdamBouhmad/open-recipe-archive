## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling salted water
- salt

## Directions

1. Fill a pan with boiling, salted water
2. Break each egg into a wet saucer and slip it into the water
3. Set the pan back where water will not boil
4. Dip the water over the eggs with a spoon
5. When the white is firm and a film has formed over the yolk, they are cooked
6. Take them up with a skimmer, drain and serve hot, on toast
7. Season with salt
