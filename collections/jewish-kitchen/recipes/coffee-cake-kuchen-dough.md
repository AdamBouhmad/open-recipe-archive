## Ingredients

- one-half ounce of yeast
- one-half cup of lukewarm milk
- one cup of sifted flour
- one teaspoon of sugar
- one-fourth teaspoon of salt
- one cup of sugar
- three-fourths cup of butter
- three eggs
- five cups of sifted flour
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Soak one-half ounce of yeast in one-half cup of lukewarm milk; when dissolved put in a bowl, or round agate pan, and stir in one cup of sifted flour, one teaspoon of sugar and one-fourth teaspoon of salt, mix thoroughly, and put in a warm place (not hot) to rise, from one to two hours
2. When well risen, cream well together one cup of sugar and three-fourths cup of butter, then add three eggs, five cups of sifted flour, one cup of milk and one teaspoon of salt, mix together until light, then stir in the risen yeast, and with a spoon work well for ten minutes, and set aside to rise again, five or six hours or all night
