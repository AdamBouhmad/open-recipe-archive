## Ingredients

- one pint of rose-water
- one ounce of oil of almonds
- fifty drops of oil of tartar

## Directions

1. Put into a bottle one pint of rose-water and one ounce of oil of almonds
2. shake well together
3. then add fifty drops of oil of tartar
