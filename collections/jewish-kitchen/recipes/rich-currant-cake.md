## Ingredients

- 1 lb. fresh butter
- 1 lb. castor sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of currants
- 1 lb. mixed citron, lemon and orange peel
- 1/2 lb. ground almonds
- 10 eggs
- 1/2 oz. of allspice
- 1/2 oz. of cinnamon
- a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Melt the butter to a cream
2. Put in the sugar and mix it well together
3. Add the ground allspice and cinnamon
4. Take the yolks of the eggs, 2 or 3 at the time, and work them in
5. Beat the white to a strong snow
6. Work in gently
7. Cut the sweetmeats in long pieces
8. Add the currants, first well-washed and dried
9. Add the flour and a glass of brandy
10. Bake it in a round deep tin with 3 or 4 rounds of paper at the bottom well buttered; it will take 3 hours to bake
