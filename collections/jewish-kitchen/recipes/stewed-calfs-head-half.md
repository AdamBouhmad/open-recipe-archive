## Ingredients

- 1/2 calf's head
- 1 quart water
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- tooth of garlic
- 2 cloves
- 2 allspice
- marjoram
- thyme
- parsley bunch
- rind of lemon
- small meat balls
- egg balls
- mushrooms
- browning
- juice of 1/2 large lemon
- 2 eggs
- lemon slices
- green parsley
- beetroot strips

## Directions

1. Take half calf's head and put it in a stewpan with quart of water, salt, pepper, onion, tooth of garlic, 2 cloves, 2 allspice, marjoram, thyme and parsley a bunch, and a little rind of lemon
2. Let it simmer gently till tender, not to break at all
3. Do it the day before you want it
4. When done take out the head carefully and take out the brains
5. Put them by themselves with the half tongue
6. Strain all the liquor into a basin
7. Put half of the liquor in a flat stewpan and put the head carefully
8. Make a few small meat balls and boil them separately
9. Add a few egg balls and a few mushrooms
10. Let it all simmer together
11. Put in a little browning to make it quite a light brown
12. The liquor must be quite transparent and will be quite like a syrup
13. Remember that it must be very nicely seasoned
14. Do not forget the juice of half a large lemon well strained
15. Put that in when you stir in the browning
16. Be very particular in the dishing up
17. Place the half head in the centre of the dish with the cheek up
18. Place the balls and mushrooms alternately
19. Remember the mushrooms must be buttons
20. Boil two eggs very hard
21. When cold cut them in slices in a plate and cut some thin slices of lemon
22. Chop a little green parsley
23. Put a slice of lemon, on top of that a slice of hard egg, on the egg a little chopped parsley, and on the top cross it with narrow strips of beetroot
