## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes
- small cup of milk
- 2 oz. of butter
- salt
- 1/2 lb. of pounded sugar
- lemon
- orange-flower water
- yolks of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash potatoes well, select the best shaped, put them in the oven
2. When baked sufficiently make a hole at the top of each
3. Scrape out the insides with a spoon, and put it in a stewpan with a small cup of milk, 2 oz. of butter, a pinch of salt, 1/2 lb. of pounded sugar
4. Rasp a lemon on a piece of sugar, or flavour with a little orange-flower water
5. Add the yolks of 2 eggs
6. Beat the white to a snow, mix it with the rest
7. Fill the potatoes with this mixture
