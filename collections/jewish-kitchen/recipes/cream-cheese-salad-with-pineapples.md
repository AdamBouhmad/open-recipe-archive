## Ingredients

- one slice of Hawaiian pineapple
- lettuce leaves
- cream cheese
- chopped walnuts
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Serve one slice of Hawaiian pineapple on lettuce leaves
2. On the pineapple slice place a spoon of cream cheese and some chopped walnuts
3. Top off with a dash of mayonnaise dressing
