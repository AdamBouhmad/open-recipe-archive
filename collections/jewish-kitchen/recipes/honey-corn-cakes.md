## Ingredients

- one pound of pure honey
- one pound of cornmeal
- ground allspice
- cloves
- pepper
- one wineglass of brandy
- white flour
- one pound of chopped nuts
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of pure honey
2. Take one pound of cornmeal mixed with a little ground allspice, cloves, and pepper
3. Add the boiled honey
4. Make a loose batter
5. Add one wineglass of brandy
6. Mix all, and cool
7. Wet the hands with cold water, take pieces of the dough and knead until the dough comes clear from the hand
8. Afterwards knead with white flour so it is not too hard
9. Add one pound of chopped nuts
10. Sprinkle flour on tins, spread dough, not too thin
11. Leave the stove door open till it raises
12. Then close door, and when done take out
13. Spread with brandy and cut in thin slices
