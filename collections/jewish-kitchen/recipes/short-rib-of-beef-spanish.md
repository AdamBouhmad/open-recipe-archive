## Ingredients

- small ribs of beef
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- tiny clove of garlic
- can of tomatoes
- red peppers or paprika

## Directions

1. Get the small ribs of beef and put on with water enough to cover, seasoning with salt, pepper, an onion and a tiny clove of garlic.
2. Let it cook about two hours, then add a can of tomatoes and season highly either with red peppers or paprika.
3. Cook at least three hours.
