## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- flour
- beaten eggs
- oil or butter

## Directions

1. Scale the fish with the utmost thoroughness
2. Remove the entrails and wash very thoroughly
3. Salt both inside and out
4. Cut the fish into convenient slices
5. Place them on a strainer and leave for an hour
6. Place some flour in one plate and some beaten eggs in another
7. Heat a large frying-pan half full of oil or butter
8. Wipe fish slices thoroughly with a clean cloth
9. Dip them first in flour and then in beaten eggs
10. Fry until browned
11. Put fish in a frying basket, then into the hot oil and cook five minutes
12. Drain on brown paper and arrange on platter
13. Do not stick knife or fork into fish while it is frying
