## Ingredients

- one cake compressed yeast
- a pinch of salt
- one tablespoon of sugar
- about two tablespoons of lukewarm water
- two and one-half cups of flour
- a scant cup of lukewarm milk
- one-half cup of butter
- three-fourths cup of powdered sugar
- three eggs
- peel of a lemon or an orange
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Take one cake compressed yeast, add a pinch of salt, one tablespoon of sugar, and about two tablespoons of lukewarm water. Stir the yeast until it is a smooth paste and set it in a warm place to rise
2. Sift two and one-half cups of flour, make a depression in the centre, stir in the yeast and a scant cup of lukewarm milk, make batter, and let it rise until you have prepared the following
3. Rub one-half cup of butter and three-fourths cup of powdered sugar to a cream, just as for cup cake, then add gradually one egg at a time, using three altogether, and stirring all the time in one direction
4. Work in the risen batter two or three spoons at a time between each egg
5. Grate in the peel of a lemon or an orange
6. Butter the bunt-form well; blanched almonds may be set in the grooves of the cake-form after buttering it
7. Put in the dough, set it in a warm place and let it rise for an hour and a half or two hours
8. Bake in a moderate oven one full hour, covered at first
