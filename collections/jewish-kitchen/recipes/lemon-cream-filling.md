## Ingredients

- yolks of five eggs
- one-half cup of granulated sugar
- juice of three lemons
- grated rind of one lemon
- water
- beaten whites of five eggs

## Directions

1. Put on to boil the yolks of five eggs, one-half cup of granulated sugar, the juice of three lemons and grated rind of one, and about a brandy glass of water
2. Stir constantly so as to prevent curdling
3. When it has thickened and comes to a boil take it from the range and add the beaten whites of eggs
