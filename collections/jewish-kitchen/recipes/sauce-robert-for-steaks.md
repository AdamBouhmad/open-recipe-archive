## Ingredients

- some onions
- a bit of clarified fat
- pepper
- salt
- a little gravy
- mustard
- lemon juice
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop up some onions, throw them into a saucepan with a bit of clarified fat, let them fry till brown
2. Add pepper, salt, a little gravy, mustard, lemon juice, and vinegar
3. Boil it all, and pour over the steaks
