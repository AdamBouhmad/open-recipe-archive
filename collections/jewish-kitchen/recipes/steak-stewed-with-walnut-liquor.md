## Ingredients

- 1 pound of the first cut of chuck steak
- 1/2 pint of water
- salt
- pepper
- small onion
- pickled walnut

## Directions

1. Take a pound of the first cut of chuck steak
2. Put it in a stewpan with 1/2 pint of water, salt, and pepper
3. Chop a small onion very fine to stew with
4. Let it stew gently for 1 hour till the liquor is quite reduced
5. When quite done rub a pickled walnut quite smooth and stir it in the liquor
6. When you put the steak in the dish put the liquor over
