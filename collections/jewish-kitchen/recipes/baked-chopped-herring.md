## Ingredients

- herring
- water
- sweet milk
- medium-sized onion
- butter
- cream
- two egg yolks
- one-fourth cup of white bread crumbs
- crumbs or cracker dust
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak herring one hour in water and then one and a half in sweet milk.
2. Skin, bone and chop herring.
3. Cut up a medium-sized onion, fry in butter until golden brown.
4. Add a cup of cream, two egg yolks and one-fourth cup of white bread crumbs, then put in a little more cream.
5. Butter pan, sprinkle with crumbs or cracker dust, then put in herring, pepper slightly.
6. Bake in moderate oven three-quarters of an hour.
