## Ingredients

- fresh cucumbers
- butter or clarified fat
- strong beef gravy
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- chopped onion

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in thick slices one or more fresh cucumbers
2. Fry them until brown in a little butter or clarified fat
3. Add to them a little strong beef gravy, pepper, salt, and a spoonful of vinegar
4. Some cooks add a chopped onion browned with the cucumbers
