## Ingredients

- one-half cup of butter
- one-half cup of confectioner's sugar
- three whole eggs
- one-half pound of chopped nuts
- one tablespoon mocha essence or one square of bitter chocolate melted, or one teaspoon of vanilla
- two dozen lady fingers
- one dozen macaroons
- one-half pint of cream

## Directions

1. Cream one-half cup of butter with one-half cup of confectioner's sugar.
2. Add three whole eggs, one at a time, beat these all for twenty minutes.
3. Add one-half pound of chopped nuts, one tablespoon mocha essence or one square of bitter chocolate melted, or one teaspoon of vanilla.
4. Grease a spring form, put two dozen lady fingers around the edge, at the bottom put one dozen macaroons, then add the filling and let this all stand for twenty-four hours in ice-box.
5. When ready to serve, pour one-half pint of cream, whipped, over all and serve.
