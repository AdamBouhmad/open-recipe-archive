## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- a pint of milk or water
- a tablespoon of shortening
- salt
- one egg
- a spoon of sugar
- a piece of compressed yeast
- water
- peaches
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Make a dough of a quart of flour and a pint of milk, or water, a tablespoon of shortening, a pinch of salt, one egg and a spoon of sugar
2. Add a piece of compressed yeast, which has previously been dissolved in water
3. Let the dough raise for three hours
4. In the meantime make a compote of peaches by stewing them with sugar and spices, such as cinnamon and cloves
5. Stew enough to answer for both sauce and filling
6. When raised, flour the baking-board and roll out the dough half an inch thick
7. Cut cakes out of it with a tumbler, brush the edges with white of egg
8. Put a teaspoon of peach compote in the centre of a cake and cover it with another layer of cake and press the edges firmly together
9. Steam over boiling water
