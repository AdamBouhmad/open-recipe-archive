## Ingredients

- puff paste
- white fricassee of chicken, sweetbread, or other filling

## Directions

1. It should be rolled out about an inch and a half in thickness
2. Cut it with a fluted tin of the size of the dish in which it is to be served
3. Also cut a smaller piece, which must be rolled out considerably thinner than an inch, to serve as a lid for the other part
4. Bake both pieces, and when done, scoop out the crumb of the largest, and fill it with a white fricassee of chicken, sweetbread, or whatever may be selected
5. The sauce should be well thickened, or it would soften, and run through the crust
