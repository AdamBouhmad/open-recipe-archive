## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one and one-half cups of canned tomatoes
- a pinch of soda
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- eggs
- squares of toast

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour, canned tomatoes rubbed through a strainer, soda, salt, pepper and sugar to taste
2. Cook sufficiently
3. Drop in the required number of eggs
4. Cook until the white is firm, basting the eggs often with the sauce
5. When done, lift the eggs carefully to squares of toast
6. Pour the sauce around them
