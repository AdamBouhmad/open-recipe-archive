## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- salt
- paprika
- pepper
- cream
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Butter a baking dish of a size necessary for number of eggs desired, break eggs into dish, add salt, paprika, pepper to taste, one tablespoon of cream, and two tablespoons of grated cheese.
2. Place dish in a pan of hot water in moderate oven for five minutes until eggs are set.
