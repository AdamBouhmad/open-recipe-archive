## Ingredients

- one cup of powdered sugar
- one-half cup of butter
- one cup of pastry flour
- one-quarter of a teaspoon of baking-powder
- peel and juice of one lemon
- five or six eggs

## Directions

1. Beat sugar with two whole eggs
2. add butter, beat until foamy
3. add the flour mixed with baking-powder, lemon and four yolks
4. Last the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs
5. Mix this well, bake in form in a moderately hot oven
