## Ingredients

- pigeons
- chopped onion
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- half a pint of liquor from beef
- half a pint of French olives
- dried saffron
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Dry the pigeons well, plump them up nicely
2. Put in a stewpan a chopped onion, a tooth of garlic, pepper, salt, a little nutmeg
3. Place the pigeons in whole, with half a pint of liquor from beef
4. Let them stew gently till nearly done
5. Take half a pint of French olives, cut them round and round off the stone, so that they will form the olive again
6. Put in the olives a little dried saffron, and the juice of half a lemon
7. When done put the pigeons in the centre of the dish, the olives round, and the gravy poured over
