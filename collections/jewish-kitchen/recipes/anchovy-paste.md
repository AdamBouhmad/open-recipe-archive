## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- fresh eggs
- anchovies

## Directions

1. take 2 oz. of fresh butter
2. the yolks of 2 fresh eggs boiled hard rubbed with the butter very smoothly
3. take 6 anchovies well cleaned and boned
4. pound them well with the eggs and butter in a marble mortar
5. when very smooth put it into small pots
6. run over them a little plain butter warmed to oil
7. this will keep for a week
