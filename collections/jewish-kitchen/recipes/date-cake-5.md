## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- one and one-quarter cups of pulverized sugar
- two tablespoons of ground cinnamon and cloves mixed
- one cup of matzoth-meal
- one-half pound seeded dates
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and sugar together until very light
2. Add the matzoth-meal, spices, dates and lemon
3. Put in the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Bake in a moderate oven three-quarters of an hour
