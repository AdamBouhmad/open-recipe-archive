## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of sifted dry rye bread crumbs
- one teaspoon of baking-powder
- a pinch of salt
- one-half cup of ground almonds
- two tablespoons of sherry
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs very light with one cup of sugar
2. Add one cup of sifted dry rye bread crumbs to which one teaspoon of baking-powder and a pinch of salt have been added
3. Moisten one-half cup of ground almonds with two tablespoons of sherry
4. Add and lastly fold in the beaten whites of eggs
5. Bake in ungreased form in moderate oven
