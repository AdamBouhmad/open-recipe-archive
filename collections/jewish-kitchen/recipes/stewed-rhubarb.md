## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- sugar
- rind of lemon
- almond milk
- eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stew the rhubarb, cut very thin and peeled, with plenty of sugar and rind of lemon
2. Turn it in a dish
3. Make a custard of almond milk and eggs, flavoured with cinnamon
4. Pour over it
