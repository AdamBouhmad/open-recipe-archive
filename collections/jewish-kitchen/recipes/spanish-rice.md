## Ingredients

- one cup of washed rice
- four or five tablespoons of poultry fat
- three onions
- two cloves of garlic
- one red pepper or one canned pimento or one teaspoon of paprika
- three ripe tomatoes or two cups of strained tomatoes
- one teaspoon of salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Put one cup of washed rice in frying-pan with four or five tablespoons of poultry fat
2. Add three onions chopped and two cloves of garlic minced fine
3. Fry ten minutes
4. Add one red pepper or one canned pimento chopped, or one teaspoon of paprika, and three ripe tomatoes or two cups of strained tomatoes and one teaspoon of salt
5. Cook slowly about one hour
6. As the water evaporates, add more boiling water to keep from burning
