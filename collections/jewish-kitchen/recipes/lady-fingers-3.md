## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- one-quarter pound of powdered sugar
- flavoring to taste: vanilla, lemon juice, grated rind of lemon or orange
- whites of three eggs
- one-half saltspoon of salt
- one-half cup of flour
- powdered sugar
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of three eggs until light and creamy
2. Add one-quarter pound of powdered sugar (sifted) and continue beating
3. Add flavoring to taste, vanilla, lemon juice, grated rind of lemon or orange
4. To the whites of the three eggs add one-half saltspoon of salt and beat until very stiff
5. Stir in lightly one-half cup of flour and then fold in the beaten whites very gently
6. Press the mixture through a pastry tube on a baking-tin, covered with paper in portions one-half inch wide by four inches long, or drop on oblong molds
7. Sift a little powdered sugar on top of each cake
8. Bake from ten to fifteen minutes in a moderate oven
9. Do not let brown
10. Remove immediately from pan
11. Brush the flat surface of one cake with white of egg and press the underside of a second cake upon the first
