## Ingredients

- hard yolks of eggs
- the yolk of a raw egg

## Directions

1. Beat the hard yolks of eggs in a mortar
2. Make it into a paste with the yolk of a raw egg
3. Form the paste into very small balls
4. Throw them into boiling water for a minute or so, to harden them
