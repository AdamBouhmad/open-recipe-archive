## Ingredients

- one egg
- one-eighth teaspoon of salt
- three tablespoons of flour
- one-fourth cup of water
- one teaspoon of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Beat one egg
2. Add one-eighth teaspoon of salt, three tablespoons of flour and one-fourth cup of water
3. Stir until smooth
4. Pour slowly from a considerable height from the end of a spoon into the boiling soup
5. Cook two or three minutes and serve hot
6. Add one teaspoon of chopped parsley to the soup
