## Ingredients

- Two tender chickens
- salt
- pepper
- one stalk celery
- onion
- flour
- fat
- two cups of hot water
- level tablespoon of flour
- minced parsley
- large cup of strong stock
- one can mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut chickens in half, split down the back
2. Place the pieces in a colander to drain well, after having been well salted
3. Season with pepper
4. Grease well the bottom of a baking-pan
5. Add one stalk finely chopped celery and onion
6. Lay the chicken on breast, side up
7. Sprinkle lightly with flour and fat
8. Add two cups of hot water
9. Have the oven very hot when putting chickens in
10. As soon as browned evenly, cover with a pan, fitting closely
11. Reduce the heat of the oven and allow to cook slowly an hour or so longer, until tender
12. Place on a hot platter and set in oven until sauce is made
13. Put the pan on top of stove in which chickens were smothered
14. Add level tablespoon of flour, thinned in cold water
15. Add minced parsley
16. Let this all cook two or three minutes, then add large cup of strong stock to the chickens
17. Broil one can mushrooms, and pour these over chicken when ready to serve
