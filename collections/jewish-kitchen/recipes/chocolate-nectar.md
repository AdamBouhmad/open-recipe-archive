## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
- cream or milk
- chopped ice
- soda water, apollinaris, or milk
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Put into a glass two tablespoons of chocolate syrup, a little cream or milk and chopped ice
2. Fill up the glass with soda water, apollinaris, or milk
3. Drop a little whipped cream on top
