## Ingredients

- two yolks of eggs
- a pinch of salt
- pepper
- finely-chopped parsley
- six blanched almonds grated
- sifted flour
- whites of eggs
- one-half teaspoon of baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat two yolks of eggs very light with a pinch of salt, pepper and finely-chopped parsley
2. Add six blanched almonds grated and enough sifted flour to make stiff batter
3. Add the stiffly-beaten whites of eggs and one-half teaspoon of baking powder
4. Drop by teaspoons in soup ten minutes before serving
