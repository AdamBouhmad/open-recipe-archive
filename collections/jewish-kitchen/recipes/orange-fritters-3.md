## Ingredients

- yolks of two eggs
- two spoons of sugar
- juice of quarter of a lemon
- flour
- beaten whites of eggs
- oil or butter-substitute
- pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of two eggs with two spoons of sugar
2. Stir into this the juice of quarter of a lemon and just enough flour to thicken like a batter
3. Add the beaten whites
4. Dip in one slice of orange at a time
5. Take up with a large kitchen spoon and lay in the hot oil or butter-substitute
6. Fry a nice brown
7. Sprinkle pulverized sugar on top
