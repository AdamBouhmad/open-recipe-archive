## Ingredients

- bread and butter from a French roll
- yolks of four eggs beaten
- four ounces of moist sugar
- a few soaked ratafias
- a table-spoonful of brandy
- a few currants
- milk
- whites of the eggs beaten to a froth

## Directions

1. Lay in a deep dish alternate layers of bread and butter cut from a French roll, and a mixture of the yolks of four eggs beaten, four ounces of moist sugar, a few soaked ratafias, a table-spoonful of brandy and a few currants
2. Fill up the dish with these layers, and pour over a little milk
3. The last layer should be of bread and butter
4. The whites of the eggs beaten to a froth may, if an elegant appearance is wished for, be laid over the top when the pudding is nearly baked
