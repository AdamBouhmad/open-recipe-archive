## Ingredients

- seven or eight large potatoes
- salt
- four whole eggs
- one and one-half tablespoons of melted chicken fat
- a little more than a cup of matzoth meal
- one tablespoon of prune jam
- boiling salt water
- cup of hot melted chicken fat
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel and cook seven or eight large potatoes, place in a bowl
2. Add salt, four whole eggs, one and one-half tablespoons of melted chicken fat and a little more than a cup of matzoth meal
3. Knead in bowl to smooth consistency
4. Take a handful at a time, pat smooth and flat
5. In the centre put a tablespoon of prune jam
6. Form into a dumpling
7. Place dumplings in boiling salt water, kettle half covered
8. Allow to cook twelve to fifteen minutes
9. Take out with strainer and serve hot
10. Have ready a cup of hot melted chicken fat and sugar and cinnamon
11. Serve over knoedel to taste
