## Ingredients

- one medium-sized onion
- fat
- a cup of strong beef gravy
- a cup of claret or white wine
- pepper
- salt
- finely-chopped parsley
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Take one medium-sized onion, chopped very fine and browned in fat
2. Add a cup of strong beef gravy and a cup of claret or white wine
3. Add pepper, salt and a trifle of finely-chopped parsley
4. Allow this to simmer and thicken with a little browned flour
