## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter and core the apples
2. Set on to boil in cold water
3. Boil them over a very brisk fire
4. When they are soft mash with a potato masher
5. Pass the mashed apples through a sieve
6. Sweeten to taste
7. Flavor with a teaspoon of vanilla
