## Ingredients

- One-half pound of loaf sugar
- One breakfast cup of water
- Cherries or desired fruit

## Directions

1. Boil, but do not stir, one-half pound of loaf sugar in one breakfast cup of water
2. Pit some cherries, or prepare any desired fruit, and string them on a thread, then dip them in the syrup
3. Suspend them by the thread
4. When pineapples are used, slice them crosswise and dry them on a sieve or in the open air
5. Oranges should be separated into sections and dried like pineapple
