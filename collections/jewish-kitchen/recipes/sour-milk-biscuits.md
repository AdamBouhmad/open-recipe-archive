## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one-half teaspoon of soda
- one tablespoon of butter
- sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix and sift two cups of flour, one-half teaspoon of salt and one-half teaspoon of soda
2. Cut in one tablespoon of butter
3. Stir in with a knife enough sour milk to make a soft dough
4. Roll one-half inch thick
5. Cut in small rounds
6. Bake in a quick oven about twenty minutes
