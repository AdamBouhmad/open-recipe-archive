## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- milk
- a pound of flour
- six eggs
- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds
- two tablespoonsful of yeast
- pounded almonds
- sugar
- citron
- cinnamon
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Warm butter over the fire with a little milk
2. Put it into a pan with a pound of flour, six eggs, a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds finely pounded, and two tablespoonsful of yeast
3. Beat these ingredients well together into a light paste
4. Set it before the fire to rise
5. Butter the inside of a pan and fill it with alternate layers of the paste and of pounded almonds, sugar, citron, and cinnamon
6. When baked and while hot, make holes through the siesta with a small silver skewer, taking care not to break it
7. Pour over clarified sugar till it is perfectly soaked through
