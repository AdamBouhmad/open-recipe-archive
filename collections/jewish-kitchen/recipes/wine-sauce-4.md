## Ingredients

- one cup of white wine
- one-half cup of water
- grated peel of lemon
- one teaspoon of potato flour
- cold water
- yolks of two eggs
- beaten whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Combine one cup of white wine, one-half cup of water, and grated peel of lemon
2. Wet one teaspoon of potato flour with cold water
3. Add the yolks of two eggs, stirring constantly
4. When thick, add the beaten whites and serve
