## Ingredients

- noodles
- three eggs
- one-fourth cup of sugar
- pounded almonds
- grated peel of one lemon
- raisins
- goose grease or drippings
- melted fat

## Directions

1. Make the quantity of noodles desired, then boil.
2. When done, drain through colander, pouring cold water over the noodles.
3. When all the water has drained off, beat up three eggs in a large bowl.
4. Mix the noodles with the beaten eggs.
5. Grease an iron pudding dish with plenty of goose grease or drippings.
6. Put in a layer of noodles, then sprinkle one-fourth cup of sugar, some pounded almonds, the grated peel of one lemon and a few raisins.
7. Sprinkle some melted fat over this, then add another layer of noodles, some more sugar and proceed as with the other layer until all the noodles are used.
8. Bake two hours.
