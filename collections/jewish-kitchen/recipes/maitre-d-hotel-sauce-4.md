## Ingredients

- a heaping tablespoon of drippings or goose-fat
- two teaspoons of flour
- a small cup of hot soup or water
- chopped parsley
- the juice of a lemon
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a heaping tablespoon of drippings or goose-fat, heat it in a spider
2. Stir two teaspoons of flour into this
3. Add gradually and carefully a small cup of hot soup or water
4. Add some chopped parsley, also the juice of a lemon
5. Add salt and pepper
6. Stir up well
