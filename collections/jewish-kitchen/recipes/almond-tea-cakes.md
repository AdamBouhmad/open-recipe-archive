## Ingredients

- half-a-pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- half a pound of sugar
- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds
- a couple of eggs
- ground almonds
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Put aside three ounces of flour for rolling out the cakes
2. Set the other five ounces with a quarter of a pound of fresh butter before the fire for a few minutes
3. Mix with it half a pound of sugar, a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds chopped fine, and a couple of eggs
4. Make these ingredients into thin cakes
5. Strew over them ground almonds and white sugar
6. Bake in a brisk oven
