## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. sugar
- half a pint of boiling water

## Directions

1. Put into a small yellow basin 1/4 lb. sugar
2. Put it into your oven and let it get almost like hardbake, a deep brown colour
3. While it is bubbling up pour over it half a pint of boiling water off the fire
4. Let it stand, then stir it well
5. Strain it through a sieve
6. When quite cold bottle it for use
