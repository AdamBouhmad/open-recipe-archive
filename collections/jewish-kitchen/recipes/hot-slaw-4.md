## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- one tablespoon of butter
- one-half to one cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into thin shreds as for cold slaw.
2. Boil it until tender in salted fast-boiling water.
3. Drain it thoroughly, and pour over it a hot sauce made of one tablespoon of butter, one-half teaspoon of salt, dash of pepper and of cayenne, and one-half to one cup of vinegar, according to its strength.
4. Cover the saucepan and let it stand on the side of the range for five minutes, so that the cabbage and sauce will become well incorporated.
