## Ingredients

- nine eggs
- one and three-quarter cups of sugar
- juice and rind of one lemon
- one scant cup of potato flour

## Directions

1. Separate the whites and yolks of nine eggs
2. Beat the whites of seven eggs very stiff
3. To the well-beaten yolks of nine eggs and the whites of two, add one and three-quarter cups of sugar and juice and rind of one lemon
4. Beat thoroughly
5. Add one scant cup of potato flour, and beat again
6. Fold in the beaten whites very carefully
7. Bake slowly in a moderate oven forty to fifty minutes
