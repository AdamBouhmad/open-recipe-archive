## Ingredients

- two cups of soft bread crumbs
- one cup of chopped walnut meats
- six tablespoons of butter or butter substitute
- one-half cup of hot water
- one and one-half teaspoons of salt
- one-quarter teaspoon of pepper
- one tablespoon of chopped onion
- a sprig of parsley chopped
- one egg

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of soft bread crumbs and one cup of chopped walnut meats with six tablespoons of butter or any butter substitute
2. Add one-half cup of hot water, one and one-half teaspoons of salt, one-quarter teaspoon of pepper, one tablespoon of chopped onion, a sprig of parsley chopped
3. Bind with one egg
4. Shape into a loaf
5. Place in a greased baking-dish and bake in a moderate oven one hour
6. As the liquor boils out of the loaf it may be used for basting
7. A brown sauce may be made in the dish in which the loaf is cooked
