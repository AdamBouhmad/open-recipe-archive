## Ingredients

- two large potatoes
- two-thirds cake of yeast
- cup of sugar
- large lump of butter
- teaspoon of salt
- pint of flour
- currants or raisins

## Directions

1. Boil two large potatoes and strain the water into a pitcher
2. dissolve two-thirds cake of yeast in a cup
3. Put potatoes in a pan with a cup of sugar, large lump of butter, and teaspoon of salt
4. The heat of potatoes will melt the sugar and butter
5. Mash with large masher to a cream
6. pour in rest of potato water
7. add pint of flour and mix together
8. cover and set in a warm place all night
9. In the morning add more flour, mix quickly and put currants or raisins in as you turn the dough
10. Put in hot pans and bake in a hot oven
