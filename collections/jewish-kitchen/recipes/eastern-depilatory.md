## Ingredients

- one ounce of quick-lime
- one ounce of orpiment
- barley-water
- sweet oil or cold cream

## Directions

1. Mix with one ounce of quick-lime, one ounce of orpiment
2. put the powder in a bottle with a glass stopper
3. when required for use, mix it into a paste with barley-water
4. apply this over the part, and let it remain some minutes
5. then gently take it off with a silver knife
6. the part should then be fomented to prevent any of the powder being absorbed by the skin
7. a little sweet oil or cold cream should be wiped over the surface with a feather
