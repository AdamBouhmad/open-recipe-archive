## Ingredients

- goose
- large onion
- sage leaves
- bread crumbs
- chopped suet
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- greased paper
- gravy
- apple sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the goose first by drying it well with a clean cloth
2. Parboil a large onion, chop it with about a dozen leaves of sage
3. Mix 2 oz. of bread crumbs, 1 oz. of chopped suet, pepper, salt, season rather high
4. Mix it well together, no eggs
5. Put it into the bottom part of the goose
6. Turn the rump inside, sew the bottom part together to secure the stuffing
7. Put on the pepper and salt
8. Dredge it over with flour
9. Skewer a greased paper over the breast
10. Remove it half an hour before serving it
11. Must be constantly basted, and roasted before a brisk fire to keep it crisp
12. Pour over a good gravy boiling hot, do not put too much in the dish
13. Send up a sauce tureen with some hot gravy, and one with apple sauce
14. A large goose will take 1 hour and a half to cook
