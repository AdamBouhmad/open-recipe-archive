## Ingredients

- entire breast of a goose
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- grated stale bread
- eggs
- goose fat

## Directions

1. Take the entire breast of a goose, chop up fine in a chopping bowl
2. Grate in part of an onion
3. Season with salt, pepper and a tiny piece of garlic
4. Add some grated stale bread and work in a few eggs
5. Press this chopped meat back on to the breast bone
6. Roast, basting very often with goose fat
