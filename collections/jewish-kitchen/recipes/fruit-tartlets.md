## Ingredients

- canned or fresh fruit
- sugar
- water
- puff paste

## Directions

1. If using canned fruit, drain syrup and put in saucepan with equal quantity of sugar
2. Cook until it forms a syrup, then pour in the fruit which has been stoned if necessary
3. Cook until the whole is a syrupy mass
4. If using fresh fruit, put two parts sugar to one of water and cook until syrupy
5. Add fruit which has been peeled, sliced and stoned
6. Cook until the whole is a thick syrupy mass
7. Line patty cases or plain muffin rings with puff paste
8. Put a spoonful or two of fruit in each one
9. Bake a nice brown
