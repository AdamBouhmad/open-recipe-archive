## Ingredients

- one cup of chopped meat
- one cup of cold mashed potatoes
- one-half an onion
- one well-beaten egg
- one-half cup of soup stock
- salt
- paprika
- celery salt

## Directions

1. Mix together one cup of chopped meat, one cup of cold mashed potatoes, one-half an onion minced, one well-beaten egg and one-half cup of soup stock.
2. Season rather highly with salt, paprika and celery salt.
3. Turn into greased baking dish and bake for twenty minutes in a well-heated oven.
