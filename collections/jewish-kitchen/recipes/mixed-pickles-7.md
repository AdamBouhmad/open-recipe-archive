## Ingredients

- a little of each kind of pickling vegetables
- salt
- peppercorns
- cloves
- allspice
- mace, a few blades
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare a little of each kind of pickling vegetables
2. Put them in a jar with salt, peppercorns, cloves, allspice, and a few blades of mace
3. When you have filled the jar, put in as much vinegar as it will take cold
4. Tie it down tight
5. Put it in a dry place
6. It will be fit for use in 3 months
