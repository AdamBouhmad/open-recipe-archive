## Ingredients

- bread
- mustard
- tongue

## Directions

1. Cut the bread exactly the same as fowl sandwiches
2. Spread mustard instead of mayonnaise
3. Cut them very even
4. Cut the tongue very thin
5. Arrange them on a dish with cut paper or a white napkin under
