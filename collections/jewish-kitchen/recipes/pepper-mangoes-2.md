## Ingredients

- large green peppers
- one head of cabbage
- one cup of mustard seed
- two tablespoons of grated horseradish
- one nutmeg
- one clove of garlic
- ground ginger
- one dozen whole peppercorns
- half a tablespoon of prepared mustard
- one teaspoon of sugar
- half a teaspoon of best salad oil
- salt brine
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take large green peppers
2. Extract the seeds and core with a penknife, being careful not to break the peppers
3. Chop up one head of cabbage after boiling it in salt water
4. When cold add one cup of mustard seed, two tablespoons of grated horseradish, one nutmeg grated, one clove of garlic grated, a pinch of ground ginger, one dozen whole peppercorns, half a tablespoon of prepared mustard, one teaspoon of sugar and half a teaspoon of best salad oil
5. Lay the peppers in strong salt brine for three days
6. Then drain off the brine and lay them in fresh water for twenty-four hours
7. Fill the peppers with the above mixture
8. Sew or tie them up with strong thread
9. Pack them in a large stone jar and pour scalding vinegar over them
10. Repeat this process three times more, at intervals of three days
11. Then tie up the jar and set it away in a cool, dry place for three months
