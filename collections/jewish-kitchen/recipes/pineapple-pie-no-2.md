## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Sugar
- Rich pie dough
- One-half pint of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pare and core the pineapple and cut into small slices
2. Sprinkle abundantly with sugar and set it away in a covered dish to draw enough juice to stew the pineapple in
3. Bake two shells on perforated pie-plates of a rich pie dough
4. When the pineapple is stewed soft enough to mash, mash it and set it away to cool
5. When the crust is baked and cool whip half a pint of sweet cream and mix with the pineapple and fill in the baked shell
