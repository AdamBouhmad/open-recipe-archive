## Ingredients

- two cups cold boiled potatoes
- two cups hot cream sauce
- three tablespoons grated cheese
- salt
- pepper
- buttered bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Slice cold boiled potatoes
2. Add them to hot cream sauce
3. Bring all to a boil
4. Remove and add grated cheese, salt and pepper to taste
5. Pour all into a baking dish
6. Sprinkle buttered bread crumbs over the top
7. Set in the oven to brown
