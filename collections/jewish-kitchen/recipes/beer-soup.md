## Ingredients

- one pint of beer
- one cup of water
- salt
- cinnamon
- two egg yolks
- a little sugar
- flour
- one cup of milk
- whites of two eggs
- croutons

## Directions

1. To one pint of beer add one cup of water, let come to a boil
2. season with salt and cinnamon if desired
3. Beat two egg yolks well with a little sugar and flour mixed
4. add one cup of milk, stir until smooth
5. stir all together in the hot beer mixture
6. let come almost to the boiling point
7. fold in the beaten whites of the two eggs and serve at once with croutons
