## Ingredients

- milk
- two generous tablespoons of flour
- butter
- maple sugar

## Directions

1. Let the milk stand until it jellies, but does not separate
2. Put it into a saucepan and let simmer one minute
3. Then thicken with two generous tablespoons of flour
4. blend to a smooth paste with butter
5. Strain through a fine sieve and serve in cups or soup plates
6. sprinkle the top with maple sugar
