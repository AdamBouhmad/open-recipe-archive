## Ingredients

- perch
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- flour
- eggs
- oil or butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Split them open from the back and wash them well
2. Dry them
3. Season them well with salt and pepper, with the juice of a lemon
4. Let them lay in that for 1/2 an hour
5. Dip them in flour and eggs
6. Fry them in oil or butter
7. Send them up hot on a fish napkin garnished with parsley
