## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Cut top from tomatoes, remove seeds, put a raw egg in each tomato, dust with salt, pepper, and finely chopped parsley.
2. Place in moderate oven until egg is set.
3. Serve with cream sauce.
