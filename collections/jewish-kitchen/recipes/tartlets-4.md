## Ingredients

- puff paste
- jam
- meringue (made with egg whites and powdered sugar)
- beaten egg diluted with one teaspoon of water (optional for glaze)

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste one-eighth of an inch thick
2. Cut it into squares
3. Turn the points together into the middle and press slightly to make them stay
4. Bake until thoroughly done
5. Place a spoonful of jam in the centre of each
6. Cover the jam with meringue and brown the meringue in a quick oven
7. For a glazed appearance, brush the top of the paste with beaten egg diluted with one teaspoon of water
