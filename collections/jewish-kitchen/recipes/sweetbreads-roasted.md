## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- warm water
- egg
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- clarified veal suet
- puree of vegetables
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. First soak them in warm water, and then blanch them
2. cover with egg and bread crumbs, seasoned with salt and pepper
3. finish in a Dutch oven or cradle spit
4. frequently baste with clarified veal suet
5. serve either dry with a puree of vegetables, or with a brown gravy
