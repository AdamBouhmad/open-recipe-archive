## Ingredients

- one pineapple, or one can of grated pineapple
- one cup of boiling water
- two cups of freshly made tea (one heaping tablespoon of Ceylon tea, steep for five minutes)
- one dozen lemons
- three oranges sliced and quartered
- one quart bottle apollinaris water
- three cups of sugar
- one and one-half cups of water
- one quart of water
- ice
- Pieces of pineapple, strawberries, mint-leaves or slices of banana

## Directions

1. Grate the pineapple, add the one cup of boiling water, and boil fifteen minutes.
2. Strain through jelly-bag, pressing out all the juice; let cool, and add the lemon and orange juice, the tea and syrup.
3. Add apollinaris water just before serving.
4. Pieces of pineapple, strawberries, mint-leaves or slices of banana are sometimes added as a garnish.
