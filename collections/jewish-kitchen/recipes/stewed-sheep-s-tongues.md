## Ingredients

- 4 sheep's tongues
- ½ pint of clear stock
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- garlic
- 3 cloves
- 3 allspice
- meat balls
- carrot
- dessert-spoonful of flour
- brown sugar
- vinegar
- browning

## Directions

1. Boil 4 sheep's tongues till tender enough to skin
2. Have ready in a stewpan ½ pint of clear stock, pepper, salt, an onion chopped, a tooth of garlic, 3 cloves, 3 allspice
3. Put in the tongues when skinned, and as soon as it boils put in a few meat balls
4. Boil a carrot with it
5. When quite done put a sieve over a deep dish or basin, lay the tongues and balls in the under dish, strain and rub the liquor through the sieve over them
6. Put back the tongues and balls, and turn the gravy over them
7. Cut the carrot in strips 2 inches long and 1 wide
8. Rub in a basin a dessert-spoonful of flour, one of brown sugar, one of vinegar, and a little browning to colour
9. Stir it in the liquor quite smooth
10. Dish it up very nicely, arranging the balls and carrots
