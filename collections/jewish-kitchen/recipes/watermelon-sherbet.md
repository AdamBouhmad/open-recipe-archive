## Ingredients

- good, pale sherry
- loaf sugar
- chopped meat of a very fine, sweet melon
- claret

## Directions

1. Take good, pale sherry and boil down to quite a thick syrup, with loaf sugar
2. Allow to cool
3. When cold mix with the chopped meat of a very fine, sweet melon
4. Use only the heart of the soft red part, not any near the white rind
5. Freeze in a freezer as you would ice, but do not allow it to get too hard
6. Serve in glasses
