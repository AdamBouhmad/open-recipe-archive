## Ingredients

- carrots
- salt
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper or cream sauce

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots lightly
2. Cut them into large dice or slices and drop them into salted boiling water, allowing one teaspoon of salt to one quart of water
3. Boil until tender
4. Drain and serve with butter and pepper or with cream sauce
