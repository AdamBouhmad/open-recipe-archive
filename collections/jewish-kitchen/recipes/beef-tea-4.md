## Ingredients

- beef
- water
- salt
- lemon peel
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut one pound of fleshy beef in dice, or thin slices
2. Simmer for a short time without water, to extract the juices
3. Then add, by degrees, one quart of water, a little salt, a piece of lemon peel, and a sprig of parsley
