## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter or butter substitute
- one tablespoon of chopped onion
- one tablespoon of chopped carrot
- one tablespoon of chopped turnip
- fish-bones, head, and trimmings
- one stalk of celery
- sprigs of parsley
- sprigs of thyme
- one bay-leaf
- one tomato or one slice of lemon
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a tablespoon of butter or butter substitute
2. Add a tablespoon each of chopped onion, carrot and turnip
3. Fry them without browning, then add fish-bones, head, and trimmings, a stalk of celery, sprigs of parsley and of thyme, a bay-leaf, a tomato or a slice of lemon
4. Cover with water and let them simmer for an hour or more
5. Season with salt and pepper and strain
