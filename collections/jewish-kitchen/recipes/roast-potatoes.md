## Ingredients

- sweet or Irish potatoes
- salt

## Directions

1. Take either sweet or Irish potatoes, or both
2. Pare, wash, and salt them
3. Lay them around the meat
4. Let them roast for about three-quarters of an hour
5. Turn them about once so they will be nicely browned
