## Ingredients

- Red currants
- White of egg
- Boiled sugar syrup
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pick fine, even, large bunches of red currants (not too ripe)
2. Dip each bunch, one at a time, into a mixture of frothed white of egg, then into a thick, boiled sugar syrup
3. Drain the bunches by laying on a sieve
4. When partly dry dip again into the boiled syrup
5. Repeat the process a third time
6. Sprinkle powdered sugar over them and lay on a sheet of paper in a slightly warm oven to dry
