## Ingredients

- five yolks and two whites of eggs
- preserve
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs as directed for sweet omelet, using about five yolks and two whites, all of which require being finely beaten and strained
2. Soften a little preserve by holding it over the fire, or mixing a little warm water with it
3. Spread it slightly over the omelette
4. Whisk the remainder of the whites to a froth with white sugar
5. Lay the whisked whites on the preserve
6. Slide the omelette onto a hot dish
7. Double it and serve directly
