## Ingredients

- cake of unleavened bread
- raw onion
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Rub a cake of unleavened bread with a raw onion, sprinkle lightly with salt
2. Place in the oven for a few minutes to dry
3. Butter and eat hot
