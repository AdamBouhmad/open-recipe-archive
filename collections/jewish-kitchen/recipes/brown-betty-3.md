## Ingredients

- four medium-sized apples
- one-quarter cup of butter
- juice of half a lemon
- one cup of bread crumbs
- one-half teaspoon of cinnamon
- grated rind of one-half lemon
- one-quarter cup of sugar
- one-quarter cup of boiling water
- cream
- soft custard or lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter, core and slice four medium-sized apples
2. Melt one-quarter cup of butter and pour it with the juice of half a lemon over one cup of bread crumbs
3. Mix one-half teaspoon of cinnamon, grated rind of one-half lemon and one-quarter cup of sugar together
4. Butter a baking dish
5. Put in alternate layers of apple and bread crumbs, sprinkling the apples with the sugar mixture, and making the last layer of crumbs
6. Pour one-quarter cup of boiling water on before adding the last layer of crumbs
7. Cover and bake for thirty minutes or until the apples are soft
8. Uncover and brown the crumbs
9. Serve with cream or with soft custard or lemon sauce
