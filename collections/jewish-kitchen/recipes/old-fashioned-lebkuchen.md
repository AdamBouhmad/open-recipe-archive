## Ingredients

- one cup of molasses
- two cups of brown sugar
- three eggs
- one level teaspoon of baking-soda dissolved in a little milk
- flour
- one cup of milk
- one tablespoon of mixed spices
- half cup of brandy
- one small cup of chopped nuts
- one small cup of citron

## Directions

1. Heat one cup of molasses.
2. Mix it with two cups of brown sugar and three eggs, reserving one white for the icing.
3. Add one level teaspoon of baking-soda that has been dissolved in a little milk.
4. Put in alternately a little flour and a cup of milk.
5. Add one tablespoon of mixed spices, half cup of brandy, one small cup each of chopped nuts and citron.
6. Add lastly, flour enough to make a stiff batter.
7. Place in shallow pans and bake slowly.
8. When done, cover with icing and cut in squares of strips.
