## Ingredients

- One pound salt
- ½ lb. of coarse brown sugar
- ½ oz. saltpetre
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. When the meat or tongue is ready lay it in the pan, rub in the salt and sugar, and the powdered saltpetre, put over the ½ pint of water, press it down and put a heavy weight on it.
2. It must be turned every day.
3. This quantity is only for 1 tongue, 5 or 6 lbs. of meat, and a small shoulder of mutton; if you wish to put beef, tongue, and mutton at the same time, you must double the quantity, it will then be ready in a week.
