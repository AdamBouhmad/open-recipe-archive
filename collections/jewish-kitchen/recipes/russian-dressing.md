## Ingredients

- one-half pint mayonnaise dressing
- two hard-boiled eggs
- two to four tablespoons tomato catsup
- one tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- one teaspoon finely chopped or grated white onion or shallot
- one cup mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Make one-half pint of mayonnaise dressing and add the following: Two hard-boiled eggs chopped fine, two to four tablespoons of tomato catsup, one tablespoon of finely chopped parsley, one teaspoon of finely chopped or grated white onion or shallot, after these ingredients are mixed, fold them into one cup of mayonnaise and serve.
