## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 parsley roots
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- blade of mace
- perch
- water
- melted butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan 2 or 3 parsley roots, an onion in slices, some salt, pepper, blade of mace
2. Wash the perch well free from scales
3. Boil in the liquor for 3/4 of an hour, only 1 pint of water
4. Take them out and put them in a dish
5. Pour over them a little melted butter and chopped parsley
