## Ingredients

- three tablespoons vinegar
- one sliced onion
- six whole peppers
- salt
- one piece stick cinnamon
- water
- sliced fish
- two egg yolks
- two lemons
- sugar
- twelve grated almonds
- parsley
- sliced lemons

## Directions

1. Boil three tablespoons of vinegar, one sliced onion, six whole peppers, salt, one piece of stick cinnamon, and a little water
2. Add sliced fish
3. When fish has boiled twenty minutes remove and arrange on platter
4. Strain the gravy and add the well-beaten yolks of two eggs, juice of two lemons, sugar to taste and twelve grated almonds
5. Let all come to a boil
6. Pour over the fish
7. Sprinkle finely chopped parsley on top and garnish with sliced lemons
