## Ingredients

- six eggs
- half a pound of lump sugar
- half a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pint of water
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Mix six eggs and half the whites
2. Flavour a quarter of a pint of water by having lemon peel in it for some hours
3. Boil the sugar and water together
4. Pour over the eggs after they have been well whisked
5. Continue whisking while the liquid is being poured over them, and until they become quite thick and white
6. Stir in the flour, which must be warm and dry
7. Pour the mixture into a couple of cake tins
8. Bake in a gentle oven
