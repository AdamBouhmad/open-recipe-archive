## Ingredients

- pigeons
- chopped onion
- garlic
- marjoram
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- pickled walnut
- water
- 1/4 lb. of first cut of chuck steak
- small meat balls
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- puff paste
- yolk of egg
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Stew the pigeons the same as stewed pigeons recipe
2. Add 1/4 lb. of first cut of chuck steak, put it at the bottom of the dish
3. Put the pigeons on top, the small balls round
4. Add 2 hard boiled eggs, cut in quarters intermixed with the balls
5. Make a fine puff paste, ornament it with 4 leaves
6. Put on the top crust, wash over with yolk of egg
7. Bake in a brisk oven
8. Put a few pieces of nice green parsley on the crust to garnish when you send up
9. Cut off the little feet, and when you have put on the crust, before you put it in the oven, stick the bone of the feet in at each end leaving out their little claws
