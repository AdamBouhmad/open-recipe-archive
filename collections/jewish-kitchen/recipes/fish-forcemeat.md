## Ingredients

- any kind of fish
- a couple of anchovies or a little anchovy essence
- the yolk of a hard boiled egg
- a little butter
- parsley or any other herb
- grated lemon peel
- a little lemon juice
- a little bread previously soaked

## Directions

1. Chop finely any kind of fish
2. Pound it in a mortar with a couple of anchovies, or a little anchovy essence, the yolk of a hard boiled egg, a little butter, parsley or any other herb which may be approved, grated lemon peel, and a little of the juice
3. Add a little bread previously soaked, and mix the whole into a paste
4. Form into balls, or use for stuffing
