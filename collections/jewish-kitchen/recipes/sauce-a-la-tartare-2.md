## Ingredients

- egg yolk
- oil
- vinegar
- chopped parsley
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- pate de diable or French mustard

## Directions

1. Mix the yolk of an egg with oil, vinegar, chopped parsley, mustard, pepper, and salt
2. A spoonful of pate de diable or French mustard renders the sauce more piquante
