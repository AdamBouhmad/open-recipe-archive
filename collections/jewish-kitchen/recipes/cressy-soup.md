## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 nice fresh-looking large carrots
- a pint of water
- a little salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take 4 or 5 nice fresh-looking large carrots.
2. Cut them in small pieces and put them in a saucepan with a pint of water, a little salt and pepper.
3. Let them boil to a pulp and rub them through.
