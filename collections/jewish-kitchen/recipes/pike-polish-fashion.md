## Ingredients

- pike
- butter or oil
- carrots
- parsley roots
- onions
- mace
- cloves
- thyme
- bay leaves
- water
- flour
- dried saffron
- lemon juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the pike clean and cut it in slices
2. Put in a stewpan ½ lb. of butter or 2 spoonfuls oil
3. Cut 2 carrots in square pieces, a few roots of parsley, and 2 or 3 onions seasoned with mace, cloves, thyme, and 2 bay leaves
4. When this has fried a little in the butter, moisten it with ½ pint of water and boil it for an hour gently
5. Strain it through a sieve over the slices of pike
6. Let them stew till done, be careful they do not break
7. Rub in a basin a tablespoonful of flour with a little dried saffron and the juice of a lemon
8. Stir it in and dish it up garnished with parsley
