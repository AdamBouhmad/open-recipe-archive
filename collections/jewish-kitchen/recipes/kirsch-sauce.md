## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- two cups of water
- two tablespoons of cornstarch
- one-quarter cup of cold water
- one cup of Kirsch wine

## Directions

1. Put one cup of sugar and two cups of water on to boil
2. Mix two tablespoons of cornstarch in one-quarter cup of cold water
3. when the water in the saucepan is boiling, add cornstarch and stir for two minutes
4. Remove from stove
5. add one cup of Kirsch wine and stir again
6. Strain and serve with pudding
