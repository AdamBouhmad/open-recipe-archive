## Ingredients

- sponge cakes
- almond cakes
- ratafias
- dried cherries
- raspberry or strawberry jam
- custard
- 1 pint of sherry
- 2 wineglasses of brandy
- 1 pint of cream
- 2 ozs. of sifted sugar
- whites of 2 fresh eggs
- thousands and pieces of green citron
- whites of 4 eggs
- juice of 1/2 a lemon

## Directions

1. Put in a large glass dish some sponge cakes broken in pieces, break some almond cakes, put them in with a few ratafias, a few dried cherries, and pieces of raspberry or strawberry jam.
2. Then put a layer of the custard, continue so till you have piled the dish.
3. You must have a pint of sherry, into which put 2 wineglasses of brandy; upon each layer of the cakes pour some to soak them.
4. When it is all finished, if for butter, whip a pint of cream, with 2 ozs. of sifted sugar, the whites of 2 fresh eggs, lay that on at the last, garnish it with thousands and pieces of green citron, dried cherries.
5. If for meat, heat the whites of 4 eggs, with 2 ozs. of sifted sugar, and the juice of 1/2 a lemon; put it on as you would the cream.
