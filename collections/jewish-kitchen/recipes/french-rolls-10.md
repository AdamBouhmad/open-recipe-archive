## Ingredients

- yeast
- flour
- salt
- one-quarter cup of butter
- one-quarter cup of sugar
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- mace or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare the yeast as for bread and work just the same
2. add one-quarter cup of butter, one-quarter cup of sugar, one whole egg and one egg yolk beaten very light
3. flavor with mace, or a few gratings of lemon peel
4. work until it leaves the hands perfectly clean
5. form into rolls
6. let raise
7. brush with beaten egg
8. place rolls in pan close together and bake
