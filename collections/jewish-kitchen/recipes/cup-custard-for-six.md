## Ingredients

- four eggs, yolks and whites
- four tablespoons of sugar
- four cups of scalded milk
- pinch of salt
- one teaspoon of good vanilla

## Directions

1. Stir until quite light four eggs, yolks and whites, and four tablespoons of sugar.
2. Have ready four cups of scalded milk.
3. Mix, add pinch of salt and one teaspoon of good vanilla.
4. Pour into cups and place cups into pan of boiling water.
5. Put into oven and bake exactly twenty-five minutes.
