## Ingredients

- one-half pound of prunes
- one-half cup of raisins
- one-half cup of sugar
- ground clove or lemon juice
- pie crust

## Directions

1. Cook prunes until soft enough to remove the stones.
2. Mash prunes with a fork and add the juice in which they have been cooked.
3. Cook raisins in a little water for a few minutes until soft.
4. Add raisins to the prune mixture with one-half cup of sugar and a little ground clove or lemon juice to improve the flavor.
5. Bake with two crusts.
