## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 3/8 pint of water
- 8 yolks
- juice of three large lemons
- rind of one lemon
- whites of eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. 1 lb. of loaf sugar melted in 3/8 pint of water.
2. Beat 8 yolks well, squeeze the juice of three large lemons, scrape the rind of one on several lumps of sugar, throw them into the water.
3. Put the juice of the lemon into the sugar and water, mix it well, then stir in the yolks of the eggs, stirring well all the time.
4. Strain through a fine sieve, put it into a jug with a spout, stand the jug in a saucepan of boiling water over the fire, stirring all the time till it thickens like a custard.
5. Have the whites beat to a stiff snow, stir it when you take it off, till it looks quite light.
6. Fill your custard glasses, and put a little cinnamon on top.
