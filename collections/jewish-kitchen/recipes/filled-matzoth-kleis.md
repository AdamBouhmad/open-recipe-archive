## Ingredients

- matzoth dough
- cooked prunes
- goose fat
- onion

## Directions

1. Prepare a matzoth dough as for the soup kleis.
2. Make round flat cakes of it with your hands, and fill with cooked prunes (having previously removed the kernels).
3. Put one of the flat cakes over one that is filled, press the edges firmly together and roll until perfectly round.
4. Boil them in salt water — the water must boil hard before you put them in.
5. Heat some goose fat, cut up an onion in it and brown; pour this over the kleis and serve hot.
