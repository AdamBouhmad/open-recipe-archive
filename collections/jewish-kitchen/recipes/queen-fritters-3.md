## Ingredients

- one cup of water
- one-fourth cup of fresh butter
- one cup of sifted flour
- three eggs
- salt
- butter or Crisco
- preserves
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put in a deep skillet on the fire one cup of water and one-fourth cup of fresh butter
2. When it comes to a boil, stir in one cup of sifted flour
3. Continue stirring until the dough leaves the side of the skillet clean
4. Remove from the fire and when cool break in three eggs, one at a time, stirring continually
5. Add a little salt
6. Mix all well
7. Drop pieces about the size of a walnut into plenty of boiling butter or Crisco
8. Fry a light brown
9. Drain
10. Make an opening in each and fill with preserves
11. Sprinkle with sugar and serve at once
