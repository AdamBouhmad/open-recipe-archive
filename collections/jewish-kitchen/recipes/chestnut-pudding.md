## Ingredients

- one pound of chestnuts
- a cup of milk
- apple sauce
- currant jelly
- lemon juice
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of chestnuts fifteen minutes
2. Shell and skin them, then put back on stove with a cup of milk and boil till tender
3. Rub through a colander
4. Butter a mold, line it with the pulp, then add a layer of apple sauce that has been colored with currant jelly, then another layer of chestnuts, and again apple sauce
5. Squeeze lemon juice over all, and bake in a moderate oven
6. Turn out on a platter and serve with whipped cream colored with currant jelly
