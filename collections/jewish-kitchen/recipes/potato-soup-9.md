## Ingredients

- three or four potatoes
- one tablespoon of butter
- one-half tablespoon of flour
- one teaspoon of chopped onion
- one pint of milk
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- croutons

## Directions

1. Boil and mash three or four potatoes, one tablespoon of butter, one-half tablespoon of flour, and one teaspoon of chopped onion, letting the onion cook in the butter a few minutes before adding the flour
2. When this is cooked add to it a pint of milk, making a thin, white sauce
3. Add this to the mashed potato and pass the whole through a strainer
4. Return it to the fire for a few minutes to heat and blend it
5. Season it with salt and pepper
6. Sprinkle on the soup chopped parsley and a few croutons
