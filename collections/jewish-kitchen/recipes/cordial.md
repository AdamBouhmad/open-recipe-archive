## Ingredients

- two quarts of water
- two and three-quarter pounds of sugar
- one quart of alcohol

## Directions

1. Boil two quarts of water and two and three-quarter pounds of sugar for thirty minutes
2. Take off stove and add one quart of alcohol
3. Color and flavor to taste
