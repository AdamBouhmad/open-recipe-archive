## Ingredients

- pint of syrup
- juice of 3 large lemons
- 2 wine-glasses of rum

## Directions

1. Add to the pint of syrup the juice of 3 large lemons well strained, and 2 wine-glasses of rum
2. Stir it all well together for about 10 minutes
3. Put it in the freezer, and work the same as described before
