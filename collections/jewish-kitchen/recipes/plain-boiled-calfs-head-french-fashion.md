## Ingredients

- 1/2 calf's head
- 1 quart water
- salt
- pepper
- bunch of marjoram, thyme, and parsley
- large onion
- flour
- chopped onion
- fine chopped parsley
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil half a calf's head very carefully in a quart of water seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Add a bunch of marjoram, thyme, and parsley, and a large onion whole
3. Be sure to take out the bunch of herbs
4. Flour the head lightly before putting it on to boil
5. Be careful of the brains and tongue
6. When done serve it on a very hot dish
7. Send up in another small dish an onion chopped very small and some very fine chopped parsley
8. The only sauce used with this is plain oil and vinegar
9. When the head is carved each person is served with a little of the onion and parsley, and then they assist themselves to the oil and vinegar to taste
