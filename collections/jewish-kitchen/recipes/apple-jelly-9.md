## Ingredients

- fine baking apples
- water
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and cut small any kind of fine baking apples—say six pounds in weight.
2. Put them in a preserving pan with one quart of water.
3. Boil gently till the apples are very soft and broken.
4. Pass the juice through a jelly bag.
5. To each pint, add half a pound of loaf sugar.
6. Set it on the fire to boil twenty minutes, skimming it as the scum rises.
7. It must not be over boiled, or the colour will be too dark.
