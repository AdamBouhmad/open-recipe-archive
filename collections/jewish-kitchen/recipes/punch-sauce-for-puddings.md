## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 oz. of sifted sugar
- 1 teacupful of water
- juice of a lemon
- 1/2 a teaspoonful of arrowroot
- 1 wine-glass of good rum

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of 3 eggs with an ounce of sifted sugar
2. Boil a teacupful of water with the juice of a lemon
3. Add 1/2 a teaspoonful of arrowroot to the eggs
4. When you have beaten them, pour the beaten eggs and sugar into the boiling water and lemon
5. When it is thick, stir in a wine-glass of good rum
