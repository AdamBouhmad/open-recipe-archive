## Ingredients

- chauebo head
- garlic
- pepper
- chili
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Partially cook the head to debone it
2. Remove all the meat and cut into regular pieces
3. Remove the skin from the tongue and cut into pieces
4. Cook in water with garlic, pepper, chili, cumin, salt and vinegar
5. After the meat is cooked place it in a mold
6. Cover above and below with the skin of the head which should also be cooked
7. Put the mold lid on to be able to place a weight on top
8. When cold remove the weight
