## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- liver
- bacon
- truffles
- mushrooms
- capers
- anchovies
- fine herbs
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- butter
- cream or milk
- bread crumbs
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After scalding and preparing like roasted suckling pig, fill with its own liver chopped, removing the bile, bacon, truffles, mushrooms, capers, anchovies, fine herbs seasoned with pepper and salt, bread crumbs toasted on the fire in cream or milk, a bit of butter, bread crumbs crushed and dissolved in three egg yolks
2. Fill the belly of the suckling pig with this mixture
3. Tie it, put on the spit and do the same as with the aforementioned
4. Serve accompanied with pebre sauce
