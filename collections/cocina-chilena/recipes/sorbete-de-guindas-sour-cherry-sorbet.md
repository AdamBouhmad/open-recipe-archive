## Ingredients

- Sour cherries
- 2 pounds sugar per 1 pound cherry juice

## Directions

1. Wash these in two waters and place them to drip in a basket, then remove the stems and crush them with the hand in a pot.
2. Let them rest four hours.
3. Then strain them through cloth; when strained, place in a cloth of three layers letting it drip until the next day.
4. For every pound of juice, add 2 pounds of sugar.
5. When the syrup reaches point, add the juice.
6. Let it boil until it is thick.
