## Ingredients

- ribs
- 2 tablespoons fat
- chopped onion
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- rice
- oil
- bacon
- onion head
- saffron
- broth

## Directions

1. Place well-washed ribs in a pot with 2 tablespoons fat, some chopped onion, parsley, pepper and salt
2. Cover the pot and place over heat
3. Separately, fry some rice with oil; add some bacon, an onion head cut in pieces, a little saffron, pepper, parsley and broth added gradually so it does not dry out
4. When serving, combine everything together
