## Ingredients

- oil
- onion
- rice
- whole peppers
- water
- salt
- pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Fry a little oil and onion, and when well sautéed add the rice
2. Sauté also a little and then place the whole peppers
3. Fill the pot with water, and season with salt, a little pepper and saffron
