## Ingredients

- juice of some oranges
- sugar
- clove
- cinnamon
- colapiz

## Directions

1. Extract the juice from some oranges and season with sugar, clove and cinnamon
2. Put to boil
3. Add dissolved colapiz
4. Strain and let cool
