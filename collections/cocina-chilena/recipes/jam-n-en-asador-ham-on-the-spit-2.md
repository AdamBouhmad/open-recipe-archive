## Ingredients

- ham
- good white wine
- onion
- carrots
- thyme
- bay leaf
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare and place the ham in good white wine with onion, sliced carrots, thyme and bay leaf.
2. Ensure the vessel is hermetically sealed with a cloth and its lid or another vessel of equal size.
3. Place on the spit and while roasting, baste it with its own marinade.
4. When half cooked, remove the skin, dust with bread crumbs and return to the fire to brown it.
5. Serve with its marinade, which should be reduced to a consistent sauce in a pan.
