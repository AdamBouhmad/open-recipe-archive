## Ingredients

- chicken
- piece of butter
- two tablespoons oil
- carrot
- onions
- parsley branches
- broth

## Directions

1. Disjoint the chicken
2. In a pot place a piece of butter and two tablespoons oil
3. Place over a very hot fire and add the chicken pieces to take color
4. When half cooked, add carrot cut in rounds and two onions in slices with parsley branches
5. Add broth and let cook slowly for a quarter hour
