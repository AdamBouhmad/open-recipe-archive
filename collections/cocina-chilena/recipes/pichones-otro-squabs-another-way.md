## Ingredients

- lard
- squabs
- tomato pieces
- water
- saffron
- pepper
- bread crumb

## Directions

1. Fry the squabs with tomato pieces in lard until they are tender
2. Add water and when they are soft, add saffron, pepper and bread crumb
