## Ingredients

- pescado seco (dried fish)
- aceite (oil)
- miga de pan (bread crumbs)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- cebollas (onions)
- ajo (garlic)
- tomate (tomato)

## Directions

1. Soak the dried fish cut into pieces from the day before.
2. The next day, cook it.
3. Heat four tablespoons of oil in a pot with bread crumbs soaked in vinegar to remove the oil's smell.
4. Fry a plate of onions cut into large pieces and one garlic clove until they begin to brown.
5. Add about a plate of fresh chopped tomato (or resoaked dried tomato if fresh is not available, or preserved if desired).
6. When the tomato begins to wrinkle and has released all its juices, add the well-shredded fish and let it boil briefly.
7. It should remain quite dry.
