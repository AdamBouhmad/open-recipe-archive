## Ingredients

- squabs
- giblets
- tomato
- water
- broth
- spices
- soaked bread crumb

## Directions

1. Clean and remove the giblets
2. Fry a little tomato with the whole squabs until they are well browned
3. Add the corresponding water and two cups of broth
4. Crush the giblets with some spices and soaked bread crumb, dilute everything and add it to the squabs
5. Let it boil once
