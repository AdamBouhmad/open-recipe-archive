## Ingredients

- equal quantity of milk and sugar as for the coffee custard
- lemon peel
- a small piece of vanilla
- six egg yolks
- two egg whites

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of milk and sugar as for the coffee custard
2. Add enough lemon peel to flavor it and a small piece of vanilla
3. Make the rest as with the coffee custard and cook the same way
