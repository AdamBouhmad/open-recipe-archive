## Ingredients

- onions
- cow butter
- potatoes
- broth

## Directions

1. Fry onions in cow butter
2. Cut half-cooked potatoes
3. Finish cooking them in the fried onion
4. Mix with broth from the stew or fish
5. Cook very slowly
