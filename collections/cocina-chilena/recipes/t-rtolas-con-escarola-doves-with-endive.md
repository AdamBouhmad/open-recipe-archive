## Ingredients

- Doves
- Butter
- Endive
- Potatoes
- Carrots
- Bread
- Bacon

## Directions

1. Fry endive in butter with sliced potatoes, carrots, and bread
2. Arrange this in a serving dish
3. On top arrange the parboiled doves with very thin slices of bacon on the breast
4. Place in the oven to brown
