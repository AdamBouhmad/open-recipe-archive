## Ingredients

- tallerines (noodles)
- water
- salt
- stewed onion
- grated cheese
- broth from the stew
- wine

## Directions

1. Cook the noodles in water and salt
2. Arrange in a serving dish alternating layers of noodles and stewed onion, grated cheese and broth from the stew
3. For this soup it is necessary to make a stew with very good wine and substantial ingredients
