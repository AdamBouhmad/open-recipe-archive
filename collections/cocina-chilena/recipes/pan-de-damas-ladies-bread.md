## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds flour
- thirty-five egg whites
- one and a half pounds sugar
- twelve ounces butter
- one ounce bitter almond

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites four at a time until they resemble meringues
2. Add flour, powdered sugar, and butter combined with almond ground as for paste
3. Mix well and pour into buttered molds
4. Bake in a moderate oven
