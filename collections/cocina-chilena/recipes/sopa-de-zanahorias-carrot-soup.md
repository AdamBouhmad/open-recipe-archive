## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Broth
- A little flour
- Butter
- Fresh peas

## Directions

1. Cook carrots in broth and pass them through a strainer and add the broth from the soup
2. Fry a little flour in butter and add it to the broth to thicken it
3. Add fresh peas
4. When these are cooked, pour the soup into the tureen which should be prepared with small pieces of bread fried in butter
