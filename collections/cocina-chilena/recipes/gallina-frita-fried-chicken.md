## Ingredients

- Hen, disjointed
- Onions
- Parsley
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Lemon juice
- Flour, one tablespoon
- Broth
- Butter
- Fried eggs
- Parsley
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Oregano
- Whole pepper and ground pepper
- One glass of vinegar
- Butter
- Broth
- Breadcrumbs or flour

## Directions

1. Disjoint the hen and place in a saucepan with onions, parsley, salt, whole pepper and lemon juice
2. Leave for one hour in this sauce, either raw or cooked
3. Remove and place the pieces in a frying pan with one tablespoon of flour, a little broth and butter so they brown
4. Serve with fried eggs around them and a sauce made as follows: put in a saucepan parsley, onions, bay leaves, oregano, whole and ground pepper, one glass of vinegar and butter
5. Bring to a boil and add broth
6. Strain through a sieve and thicken with breadcrumbs or flour
