## Ingredients

- One pound of plums
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of plums with a little water and sugar until they soften when pressed with a finger
2. Place in a compote dish
3. If the syrup is not the right consistency, boil until it thickens
4. Pour the syrup over the plums
