## Ingredients

- bread
- sweet pepper
- almonds peeled
- cooked partridge
- onion heads
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Soak bread and sweet pepper and grind with peeled almonds
2. Add this sauce to the cooked partridge with onion heads
3. At the time of serving, beat in an egg yolk
