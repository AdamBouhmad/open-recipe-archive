## Ingredients

- partridges
- carrots
- onion
- celery
- parsley
- bacon piece
- sausage
- cabbage
- salt
- nutmeg
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges well
2. Put carrots, onion, celery, parsley, the partridges, a piece of bacon, a sausage and a good cabbage (which should have been soaked in boiling water beforehand, seasoned with salt and well squeezed) in a pot
3. Moisten with a little broth and add a little nutmeg
4. Boil for three hours with fire below and above if the partridges are old, and one and a half hours if they are new
5. Put the cabbage on a platter and the partridges on top with the bacon and sausage around it cut into small pieces
