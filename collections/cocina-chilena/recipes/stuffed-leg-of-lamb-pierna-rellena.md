## Ingredients

- Lamb leg
- fat or lard
- broth
- onion pieces
- herbs
- whole pepper
- cooked potatoes
- hard-boiled eggs
- onion, finely chopped
- hot water
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the leg in a little fat
2. When golden, add broth, onion pieces, herbs and whole pepper
3. When cooked, cut a square in the middle
4. Fill with mashed cooked potatoes
5. Add chopped hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped onion that has been passed through hot water, oil and vinegar
6. Add the leftover filling to the broth the leg was cooked in to make sauce
