## Ingredients

- 4 ounces apples
- 3 ounces butter
- 4 ounces ground sugar
- juice and peel of half a lemon
- 6 to 8 chopped bitter almonds
- 4 eggs, removing the white from 2 of them

## Directions

1. Chop the apples very finely
2. Combine all ingredients well
3. Place in small pastries
