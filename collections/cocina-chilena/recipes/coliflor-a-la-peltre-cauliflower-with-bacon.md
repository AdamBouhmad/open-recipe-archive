## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- bacon
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry it with bacon and butter and a little flour
2. Add a little broth, salt, pepper and nutmeg to this
3. Cook on low heat
