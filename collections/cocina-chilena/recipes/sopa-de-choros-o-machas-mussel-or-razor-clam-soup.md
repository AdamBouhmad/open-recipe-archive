## Ingredients

- Mussels or razor clams
- Water
- Sliced bread
- Ground garlic
- One onion head in segments
- Six ounces of rendered fat or butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the mussels or razor clams well and place them to cook in a regular amount of water.
2. When it has boiled for a quarter hour, remove them from their shells.
3. In an earthenware dish prepared for this purpose, place sliced bread, ground garlic, onion head in segments, and six ounces of rendered fat or butter.
4. Add the mussels or razor clams and pour in the stock in which they were previously cooked.
5. Let it boil for another half hour if necessary.
6. Serve.
