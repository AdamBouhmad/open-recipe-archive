## Ingredients

- One pound of lentils
- Stock from puchero
- A little bacon
- Two carrots, thinned
- Two onions, thinned and browned in butter
- Two cloves
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of lentils in stock from puchero with a little bacon, two thinned carrots, two thinned onions browned in butter, and two cloves.
2. When everything is cooked, pass through a sieve.
3. Thin the puree with stock.
4. Cook in a pot to skim it.
5. Serve over fried bread crusts.
6. For fasting days, this puree can be made by substituting oil for bacon.
