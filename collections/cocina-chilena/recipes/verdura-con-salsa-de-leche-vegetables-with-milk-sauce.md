## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Fry a tablespoon of flour in butter and then add the milk and make a not-too-thick mixture.
2. This sauce goes well with stalks and the thick part of the Swiss chard, cauliflower, and green beans. All these vegetables must be cooked beforehand before using with this sauce; the green beans should be cut before cooking them.
