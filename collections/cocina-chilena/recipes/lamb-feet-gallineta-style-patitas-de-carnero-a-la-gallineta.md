## Ingredients

- Lamb feet
- Salt
- Water
- Chicken sauce

## Directions

1. Scald and clean the lamb feet carefully
2. Remove the large bones
3. Cook in salted water for four or five hours
4. When tender and the bones come loose easily, they are cooked
5. Make a chicken sauce and let the feet simmer in it for about half an hour to take on flavor
6. Thicken the sauce and serve very hot
