## Ingredients

- Ham
- Oil
- Three tomatoes
- Sweet pepper
- Onion
- Eggs
- Oil
- Finely chopped ham
- Meat
- Spices

## Directions

1. Fry the ham with oil
2. Then fry three tomatoes, one sweet pepper and an onion in oil
3. Once fried, add the eggs beaten with the very finely chopped ham
4. Flip it as stated in the previous recipe and let it brown well
5. If made with meat, it must be previously cooked and with fine spices
