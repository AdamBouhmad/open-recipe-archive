## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- onion
- hard-boiled eggs
- oil
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Cook the peas with salt
2. Drain the water and let cool
3. Partially cook a little onion, wash well in cold water and add to the peas
4. Chop hard-boiled eggs and add to the mixture
5. Season to taste with oil and orange juice
