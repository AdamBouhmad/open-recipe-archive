## Ingredients

- Wood pigeons
- Parsley
- Color
- Celery
- Bread
- Onion
- Seasonings
- Eggs
- Broth

## Directions

1. Cook the pigeons well
2. Remove the backbone from the top
3. Place in a serving dish with parsley, color, and celery
4. Put in the oven to brown
5. Make a sauce from soaked bread, fry some onion with seasonings, beat two eggs, mix with the soaked bread adding a little broth to make it clear
6. Pour this sauce over the pigeons and let cook briefly
