## Ingredients

- Brains
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the brains, cut them into slices and arrange them in paper cases with a piece of butter, salt, pepper and plenty of bread crumbs
2. Place them in the oven to brown
