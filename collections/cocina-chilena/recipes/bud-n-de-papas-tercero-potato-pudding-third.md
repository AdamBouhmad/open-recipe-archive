## Ingredients

- six eggs
- six ounces of sugar
- one large tablespoon of flour
- three large tablespoons of ground potatoes
- some raisins

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar
2. Add the potatoes and flour
3. Last, fold in the beaten egg whites
4. Place everything in a dish coated with butter
5. Bake in the oven and serve with wine sauce
