## Ingredients

- One well-fattened hen, killed the day before
- Apple, finely chopped
- Butter
- Bread, small pieces
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the hen and ensure no fat or lard remains when cleaning it
2. Fill with finely chopped apple fried in butter
3. When the apple is well fried, add small pieces of bread
4. Once this is well cooked, add a little sugar and stuff the raw hen
5. Roast early, adding a piece of butter
6. Roast slowly
7. Remove and let rest for a while
8. Baste with the same broth the hen releases and with a piece of butter
9. Repeat this basting several times
