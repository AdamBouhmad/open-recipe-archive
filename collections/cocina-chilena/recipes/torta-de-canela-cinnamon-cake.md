## Ingredients

- eighteen eggs
- half pound ground almonds
- one pound sugar
- one and a half ounces cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat eighteen eggs well
2. Add half pound ground almonds, one pound sugar, and one and a half ounces cinnamon
3. Mix everything well and place in an earthen pot between two fires
4. Dust with sugar and cinnamon
