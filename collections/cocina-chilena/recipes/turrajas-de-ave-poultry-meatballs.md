## Ingredients

- Chicken meat
- Breadcrumbs
- Cream
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes and tendons from chicken meat
2. Chop it with breadcrumbs moistened in cream and egg yolks
3. When well incorporated, season
4. Form small meatballs and roll in breadcrumbs
5. Coat again with egg and fry
6. Serve with parsley prepared the same way
