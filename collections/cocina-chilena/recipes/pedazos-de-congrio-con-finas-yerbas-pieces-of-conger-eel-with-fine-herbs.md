## Ingredients

- congrio (conger eel)
- manteca (lard)
- cebollas (onions)
- perejil (parsley)
- setas (mushrooms)
- sal (salt)
- pimienta (pepper)
- nuez moscada (nutmeg)
- zumo de limón (lemon juice)

## Directions

1. Take a piece of conger eel from the end of the belly toward the tail.
2. Cut it into slices half a finger thick after removing the skin.
3. Lightly fry the pieces in lard with finely blanched onions, parsley, and sliced mushrooms seasoned with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
4. Cook over good heat for about twenty minutes without adding water.
5. Remove the fish and let the herbs soften a bit more by adding more sauce, a little lard, and lemon juice.
6. Serve the conger eel with the remaining sauce on the side.
