## Ingredients

- trout
- bacon
- lettuce
- bread
- pepper
- orange juice
- lemon

## Directions

1. Scaled, split down the middle and cut into rounds, fry with fatty and lean bacon
2. Cook in seasoned water some heads of white lettuce
3. When the trout are fried in their own fat, fry some slices of bread and then the lettuce heads so they do not dry out
4. Remove and place on slices of bread over other heads of cabbage and pieces of trout
5. Sprinkle with pepper and orange juice, add thin slices of lemon between the cabbage and trout
6. Serve hot
