## Ingredients

- 100 strawberries
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar
- 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Remove hulls from strawberries and pass through a sieve
2. Add 1/2 pound powdered sugar and 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
3. Bring to a boil and when the preparation begins to thicken, let cool and pour into a mold
4. Place mold in snow for two hours
5. Beat 6 egg whites as for meringues and add to the mixture before molding
6. To serve, place mold in a pan with warm water that can be held comfortably by hand, then quickly turn out onto the serving plate
