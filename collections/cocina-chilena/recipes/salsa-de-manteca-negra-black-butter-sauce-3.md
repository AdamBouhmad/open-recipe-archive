## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. In a pan or saucepan fry butter until it becomes almost black
2. Then fry a little parsley in it
3. After frying, add vinegar and salt
4. Let it cook near the fire for an instant
