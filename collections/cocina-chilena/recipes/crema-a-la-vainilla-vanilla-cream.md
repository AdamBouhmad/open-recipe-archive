## Ingredients

- One vanilla bean
- Four glasses of boiled milk
- Half a pound of sugar
- A grain of salt
- Eight egg yolks
- Half an ounce of clarified isinglass

## Directions

1. Cut a vanilla bean into small pieces
2. Add to four glasses of boiled milk and leave on the fire for a quarter hour
3. Add half a pound of sugar and a grain of salt
4. Cover the milk so it does not lose its perfume and when lukewarm, strain through a cloth
5. Pour the milk over eight egg yolks and mix well with a spoon
6. Cook over gentle heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon
7. When the cream is thick and begins to stick to the spoon, pass through a strainer
8. When cooled, mix with half an ounce of clarified isinglass
9. When the cream is cold, pour into a mold like gelatin
