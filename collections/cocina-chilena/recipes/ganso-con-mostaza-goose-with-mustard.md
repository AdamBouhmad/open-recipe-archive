## Ingredients

- One tender goose
- Goose liver
- Small onions
- Laurel
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Butter
- One tablespoon of mustard
- Bread crumbs
- Mustard
- A little vinegar
- Broth
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Stuff the tender goose with its liver, small onions, laurel, parsley and thyme, all finely chopped
2. Spit it and roast it, basting occasionally with butter
3. When almost roasted, add one tablespoon of mustard to the butter and spread it all over
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and return to the fire to finish roasting and browning
5. Serve with the following sauce: put butter in a saucepan with one tablespoon of mustard, a little vinegar, broth, pepper and salt, and let it cook until it thickens
