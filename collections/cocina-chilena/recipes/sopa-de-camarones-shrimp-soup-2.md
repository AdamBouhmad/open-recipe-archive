## Ingredients

- shrimp shells (excluding head shells)
- butter
- flour
- broth
- bread crumbs
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- nutmeg
- salt
- shrimp tails

## Directions

1. Put shrimp shells (less those from the head) in the oven to toast well
2. Grind them to powder
3. Put a tablespoon of butter in a saucepan, when melted add the shell powder with a tablespoon of flour
4. Stir well over the fire and add about four tablespoons broth
5. Remove from fire and add plenty of broth
6. Add bread crumbs and bring to boil
7. Strain, and if too thick add more broth
8. At the time of serving add small balls made from bread crumbs, eggs, nutmeg and a small piece of butter cooked separately in broth
9. Beat a tablespoon of butter until foamy, add three yolks and two whites, a little nutmeg and salt, stir adding bread crumbs gradually until thick, wet hands in water and form balls, add to boiling broth, taking care not to cover the pot until serving
