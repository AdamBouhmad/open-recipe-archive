## Ingredients

- Cheese
- Bread
- Eggs
- Cheese
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a crust composed of half cheese and half bread
2. Break the eggs over it
3. Sprinkle with cheese, pepper and nutmeg
4. Place over low heat, browning them with a palette made of hot coals
