## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lemon peel
- Sugar
- Rasped sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs as in the previous ones, mix with lemon peel, sugar and put in the pan
2. Serve immediately on a plate sprinkled with rasped sugar
3. Cover the omelette also with sugar and brown it with a very hot iron
