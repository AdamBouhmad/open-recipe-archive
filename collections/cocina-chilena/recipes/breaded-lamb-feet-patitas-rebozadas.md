## Ingredients

- Lamb feet
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Cream of milk
- Broth
- Onion
- Hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb feet
2. Roll them in beaten egg and fry them
3. Make a light cream with three egg yolks and a little sugar
4. When serving, unite the lamb feet with the sauce
5. Alternatively, serve with broth with chopped onion and hard-boiled egg
