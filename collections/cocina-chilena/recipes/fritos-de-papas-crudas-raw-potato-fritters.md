## Ingredients

- raw potatoes, peeled
- milk (one small cup)
- salt
- bread crumb
- four eggs
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Grate raw peeled potatoes
2. Squeeze out the water they release
3. Add one small cup of milk, a little salt, bread crumb, and four eggs to the potatoes
4. Beat everything well
5. Fry by spoonfuls in hot lard or butter
