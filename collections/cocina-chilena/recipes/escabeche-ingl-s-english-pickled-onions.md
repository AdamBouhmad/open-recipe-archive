## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper of two kinds
- cloves

## Directions

1. Cut the onions into pieces and wash them very well
2. Place them in a vessel and add ground salt
3. Separately, boil the vinegar with pepper of two kinds and cloves
4. Pour this hot mixture over the onions and cover well
