## Ingredients

- meat
- water
- salt
- burnt fat
- potatoes
- squash
- green beans
- peas
- cabbage
- seasonings
- egg

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces and put it to cook in a regular amount of water with salt
2. When it boils, skim it
3. Remove the meat and fry it in burnt fat
4. Dice potatoes, squash, green beans and peas if available, or cabbage, fry all this with the meat
5. Add the same broth and cook again
6. Add all the seasonings desired and let it rest for one or more hours
7. At the time of serving, season with egg
