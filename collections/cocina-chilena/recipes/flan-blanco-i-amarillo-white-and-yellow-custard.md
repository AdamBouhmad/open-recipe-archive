## Ingredients

- fifteen eggs
- two ounces of sugar dissolved in two and a half glasses of milk

## Directions

1. Beat fifteen eggs
2. For the white, beat the egg whites like for meringues and the yolks for the yellow like for sponge cake
3. Dissolve two ounces of sugar in two and a half glasses of milk and add to the eggs
4. Let the white sit for a while until the foam dissolves
5. Pour into caramelized molds
6. The white needs little boiling, the yellow is done when it pulls away from the mold
7. Cook in water like the previous recipe
