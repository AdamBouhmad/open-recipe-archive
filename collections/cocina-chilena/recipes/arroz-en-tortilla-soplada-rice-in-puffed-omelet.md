## Ingredients

- half pound of rice, clean and washed
- milk
- sugar
- salt
- six egg yolks
- orange blossom water
- lemon zest
- butter, about half an ounce
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with the appropriate amount of milk, sugar and a little salt
2. After well cooked, pass it through a metal strainer
3. Put the puree in a pot with six egg yolks, a little orange blossom water, lemon zest and a little butter, about half an ounce
4. Beat the egg whites well and add to the rice
