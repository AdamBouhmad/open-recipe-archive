## Ingredients

- garlic
- butter
- white wine
- water
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. After roasting and removing from shells, put in a pot with a piece of butter, white wine and water, bread crumbs, lemon juice and a little nutmeg
2. Bring to boil
