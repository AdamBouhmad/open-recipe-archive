## Ingredients

- one lemon
- four ounces of sponge cake, grated
- four ounces of liquid butter
- juice of one lemon
- four eggs
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Peel one lemon, removing the skin as thinly as possible and chop it well
2. Grate four ounces of sponge cake and add four ounces of liquid butter
3. Add the juice of one lemon, four eggs, and sugar to taste
4. Mix everything well and place in a dish in the oven or in pastries
