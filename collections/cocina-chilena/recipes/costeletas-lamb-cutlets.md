## Ingredients

- lamb ribs meat
- mashed potatoes
- 3 eggs
- parsley
- celery
- pepper
- rib bone

## Directions

1. Scrape the meat from lamb ribs and fry it
2. Pound it and mix with as much mashed potato as meat
3. Add 3 eggs, parsley, celery and pepper, mix well
4. Place the cleaned small bone in the middle giving it the shape of a cutlet
5. Roll in egg, breadcrumbs and fry
