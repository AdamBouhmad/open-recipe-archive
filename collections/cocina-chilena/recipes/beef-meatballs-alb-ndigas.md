## Ingredients

- Ground meat from cooked beef
- Breadcrumbs
- Milk
- Spices
- Flour or breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. To make more delicate meatballs, soak breadcrumbs in hot milk
2. Mix with the ground meat until forming a homogeneous paste
3. Taste to check if it has sufficient spices
4. Form balls, roll them in flour or breadcrumbs
5. Fry them
6. These meatballs can be kept several days and eaten on grills or reheated with a white sauce
