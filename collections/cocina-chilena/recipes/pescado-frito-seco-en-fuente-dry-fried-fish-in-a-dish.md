## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- pimienta (pepper)
- cebollas (onions)
- ajo (garlic)
- harina (flour)
- huevo (egg)
- papas (potatoes)

## Directions

1. Soak the fish overnight.
2. Cut it into pieces.
3. Add pepper, sliced onions, and a little garlic.
4. Bring to a boil.
5. Make a flour sauce with the water in a silver or ceramic dish.
6. Layer the fish, onions, hard-boiled eggs sliced, and fried potatoes in slices.
7. Cover everything with the flour sauce.
8. Put it in the oven or over fire.
