## Ingredients

- gherkins
- flour
- butter
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Chop the gherkins a lot, putting them afterwards in a saucepan with a pinch of flour, butter, pepper and sufficient broth
2. After the first boil, remove from the fire
