## Ingredients

- ten well-beaten egg yolks
- six ounces sugar
- four ounces butter
- one glass sherry or white wine
- a little milk
- cinnamon
- grated lemon
- sufficient flour for a soft dough

## Directions

1. To ten well-beaten egg yolks add six ounces sugar
2. Add four ounces butter, one glass sherry or white wine, a little milk, cinnamon, and grated lemon
3. Add sufficient flour for a soft dough
4. Knead everything a little and form the rings
