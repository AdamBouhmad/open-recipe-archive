## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- pepper, powdered
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. To two egg yolks add one tablespoon of lemon juice and powdered pepper
2. Season and put on very low heat, stirring constantly
3. Add a little oil
