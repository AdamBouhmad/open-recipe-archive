## Ingredients

- seven hard egg yolks
- two raw egg yolks
- half a bottle of oil
- salt
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Dissolve seven hard egg yolks with a spoon and unite them with two raw egg yolks
2. Add half a bottle of oil little by little until they are well beaten
3. Add salt and vinegar to taste
4. If it breaks add a little vinegar and a splash of water until the broken appearance is removed
5. Arrange as the previous recipe
