## Ingredients

- green beans
- onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Tear green beans into four pieces
2. Put in a pot with cold water
3. Cook over active heat; at the first boil the beans are cooked and without losing color
4. Drain the water and cool
5. Add a little blanched onion if desired
6. Dress to taste
