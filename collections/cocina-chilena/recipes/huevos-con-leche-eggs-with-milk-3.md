## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- flour
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. To the melted butter add very finely chopped parsley, salt, pepper and a glass of milk, in which a spoonful of flour has been dissolved beforehand
2. When the sauce is ready, place the cooked eggs in it
