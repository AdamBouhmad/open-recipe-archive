## Ingredients

- About two bottles of milk
- One quarter of a half of burned fat
- Flour as needed
- Fourteen eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the milk with the quarter part of a half of burned fat
2. Bring to a boil and add handfuls of flour until forming a not very hard dough
3. Boil and stir constantly until it separates from the pot or pan
4. Empty into a dish or bowl and when lukewarm, add fourteen eggs that will be incorporated with the dough
5. The fat for frying should not be too inert; when adding to fry it is good to wet the fingers with cold water so the batter does not stick
6. Strike them so they split and when removed already fried, place a little fire underneath so they do not dampen
7. Pass through syrup or honey raised to point to make the figure desired
8. When already lukewarm, knead into small pieces and like little rolls, have very fine powdered sugar on hand to take in the hands each figure made so it does not stick to the fingers, and afterwards pick with very fine tongs
