## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- strong vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Score the onions in a cross pattern
2. Add salt and let them soften in the sun for an entire day
3. Place them in strong vinegar with a little salt and plenty of pepper
4. Put them in glass jars
