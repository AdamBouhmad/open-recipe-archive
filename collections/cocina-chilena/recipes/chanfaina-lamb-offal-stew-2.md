## Ingredients

- Lamb liver
- lamb lungs
- cooked lamb blood
- butter
- onions
- spices
- parsley, chopped
- paprika
- water
- flour
- eggs, beaten (optional)

## Directions

1. Take pieces of liver, lungs and cooked blood
2. Sauté in butter
3. Add onions, spices, chopped parsley and paprika
4. After everything is well sautéed, add water and cook
5. Mix flour dissolved in water when it is boiling and add to the chanfaina to thicken the broth
6. If desired, a beaten egg mixture can be added
