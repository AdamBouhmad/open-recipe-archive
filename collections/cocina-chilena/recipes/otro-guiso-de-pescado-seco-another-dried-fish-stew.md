## Ingredients

- pescado seco (dried fish)
- aceite (oil)
- mantequilla (butter)
- ajo (garlic)
- cebollas (onions)
- perejil (parsley)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- dos yemas de huevo (two egg yolks)
- caldo (broth)

## Directions

1. Prepare as above.
2. Put equal amounts of oil and butter in a pot with one minced garlic clove, onions, and parsley all finely chopped.
3. When half-fried, add the fish in pieces and a little vinegar.
4. Once everything is well fried, add water and let it cook.
5. When ready to serve, beat two egg yolks and cook them in a little fish broth.
6. Pour this mixture over the top, being careful it does not boil so the egg does not curdle.
