## Ingredients

- hen
- water
- butter
- parsley branches
- small onions
- mushrooms
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces
2. Blanch three minutes in boiling water
3. Drain and place in a pot with butter, parsley branches, small onions, and mushrooms
4. Reheat everything
5. Add one tablespoon flour and moisten with broth
