## Ingredients

- cod
- butter
- nutmeg
- flour
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. After well cleaned, cook it; when about to boil, skim
2. Remove from fire, cover well, leaving it a quarter hour, then remove from water and drain
3. Put in a pot a bit of butter, nutmeg, flour and pepper
4. Dissolve everything in a bit of milk and add the cod afterwards so it takes on the taste of this stew and serve
