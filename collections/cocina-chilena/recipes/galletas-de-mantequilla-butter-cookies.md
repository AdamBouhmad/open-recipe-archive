## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- half pound of butter
- half pound of powdered sugar
- one small cup of milk
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add half pound of butter and half pound of powdered sugar, one small cup of milk, cinnamon and cloves
2. If the dough becomes too soft, add flour until it does not stick
3. Cut the cookies and place in a very hot oven
