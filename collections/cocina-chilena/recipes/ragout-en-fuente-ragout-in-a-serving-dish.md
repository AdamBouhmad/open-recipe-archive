## Ingredients

- Partridges or chickens
- Butter
- Lemon peel
- Broth
- Wine
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut up partridges or chickens into pieces
2. Place in a serving dish with butter, lemon peel, broth, and a little wine and breadcrumbs
3. Let simmer
4. When cooked, add lemon juice and serve
