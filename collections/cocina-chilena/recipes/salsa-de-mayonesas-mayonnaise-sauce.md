## Ingredients

- one or two egg yolks
- juice of one lemon
- salt
- spices
- oil
- strong aromatic vinegar
- mustard (optional)

## Directions

1. Dissolve one or two egg yolks with the juice of one lemon
2. Add salt and spices mixed with these eggs
3. Add oil little by little while beating without ceasing
4. This will begin to thicken very soon
5. From time to time add a little strong aromatic vinegar
6. If desired add mustard
