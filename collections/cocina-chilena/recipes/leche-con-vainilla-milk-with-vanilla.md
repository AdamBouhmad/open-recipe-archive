## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- vanilla
- lemon
- six egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook milk with plenty of sugar, cinnamon, vanilla and lemon
2. When it has boiled well, add six well-beaten egg yolks
3. Stir for a moment and add the beaten egg whites
4. Mix well and place in the oven
