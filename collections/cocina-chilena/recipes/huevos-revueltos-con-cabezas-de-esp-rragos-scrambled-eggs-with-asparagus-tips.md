## Ingredients

- eggs
- asparagus tips
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat as the previous ones, mixing them with clean asparagus tips that have been scalded beforehand
2. Beat all together, finish and place on the serving plate garnished with other asparagus tips
