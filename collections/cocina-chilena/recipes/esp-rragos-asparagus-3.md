## Ingredients

- Tender asparagus
- Salt
- Water
- Eggs
- Fried oil
- Pepper
- Spices
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the most tender asparagus in water and cook with salt and half or less water
2. Add eggs so they set without becoming hard
3. To serve, place in a dish with a little water and drizzle fried oil over the top, pepper, spices, and a few drops of vinegar if desired
4. Alternatively, serve the asparagus simply cooked with salt, arranged in bundles, accompanied by vinegar cruets
