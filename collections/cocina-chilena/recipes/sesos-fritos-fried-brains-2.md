## Ingredients

- cooked brains
- frying batter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. If after cooking brains to eat à la marinière or with black butter some leftovers remain, divide them into pieces
2. Dip or soak them in a batter for frying
3. Fry this batter until it takes on a golden color and serve at the table with fried parsley
