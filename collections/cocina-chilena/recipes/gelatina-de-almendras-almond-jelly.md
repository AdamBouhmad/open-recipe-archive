## Ingredients

- Four ounces of almonds
- A little milk
- Lemon peel
- Whole cinnamon
- Two cups of milk
- One ounce of isinglass
- A little water
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Peel four ounces of almonds and sprinkle them with a little milk in a saucepan with lemon peel and whole cinnamon, and boil for a while
2. In another saucepan, put the almonds without straining with two cups of milk and put to boil over low heat
3. Place one ounce of isinglass with a little water at the edge of the fire to dissolve
4. Combine the two milks, strain, and pour into the saucepan where the isinglass is
5. Sweeten to taste, let boil for a while, and pour into a mold
