## Ingredients

- Four tablespoons of tea
- Four glasses of milk
- Two ounces of sugar
- A little boiling water
- Eight egg yolks
- Half a pound of powdered sugar
- A grain of salt

## Directions

1. Add four tablespoons of tea to four glasses of boiling milk
2. Cover the infusion so it does not lose its perfume and when cold, strain through a cloth
3. Make a caramel with two ounces of sugar and a little boiling water
4. Add the caramel to the infusion
5. Mix with eight egg yolks that have been beaten with half a pound of powdered sugar and a grain of salt
6. Mix everything and pass through a strainer
7. Pour into a mold and place in a bain-marie
