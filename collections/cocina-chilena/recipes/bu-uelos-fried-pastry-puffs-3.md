## Ingredients

- One and a half bottles of water
- Four ounces of butter
- Two ounces of sugar
- Zest of one lemon
- A little cinnamon
- Twelve ounces of flour
- Eight eggs
- Four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Bring water to boil in a pot with butter, sugar, lemon zest, and cinnamon
2. When boiling, add flour little by little and stir constantly until the dough separates from the pot
3. Pour into a spread dish to cool
4. When lukewarm, pass to a cup or bowl to incorporate eight eggs and four yolks
5. When well incorporated, fry the puffs in very hot lard and place in a dish that has heat from below so they do not dampen
6. These puffs can be passed through syrup at point, or if you wish to serve with ground sugar, add five ounces of sugar instead of two to the water
