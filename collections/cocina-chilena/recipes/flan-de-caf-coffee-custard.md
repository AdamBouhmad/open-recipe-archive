## Ingredients

- a little roasted unground coffee
- three glasses of milk
- six egg yolks
- two egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil a little roasted unground coffee in three glasses of milk
2. Let it boil until the milk is well colored and has the full flavor of coffee
3. Strain and sweeten to taste
4. Beat six egg yolks and two egg whites and mix together
5. Pour into the caramelized mold
6. Cook in a pan with hot water
7. When it has boiled for a while, cover the mold with a small tin and place some embers on top so it cooks evenly
