## Ingredients

- Twelve ounces of flour
- Half a pound of powdered sugar
- About a large nut of butter
- A little salt
- Lemon zest
- Eight whole eggs
- Blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Mix all together; if not enough to moisten, add one more white
2. The dough should be hard
3. Beat with the wooden spoon to soften
4. Cut the rolls long, placing some split almonds that are flattened with the wooden spoon
5. Butter the baking sheets and bake
