## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- rama de apio (celery branch)
- perejil (parsley)
- pimienta (pepper)
- sal (salt)
- papas (potatoes)
- mantequilla (butter)
- mostaza francesa (French mustard)
- harina (flour)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and cook it in a fish poacher with a celery branch, parsley, pepper, and salt.
2. Serve at the table with large boiled and peeled potatoes.
3. For the sauce: melt a tablespoon of butter and add a teaspoon of French mustard and finely chopped parsley.
4. Add a little flour and dilute with the water in which the fish was cooked to the desired thickness.
5. Serve the cooked fish with this sauce.
