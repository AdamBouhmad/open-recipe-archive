## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- one egg white
- one cup of milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil milk with sugar, cinnamon and vanilla
2. Strain and mix with two half-boiled egg yolks and one egg white that have been beaten
3. Cook in cups in a pan with water
