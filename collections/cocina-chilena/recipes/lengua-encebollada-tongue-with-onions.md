## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- onions
- tomato
- corn
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue and cut into thin slices
2. Fry until browned
3. Separately fry also until browned: onions, tomato and chopped corn
4. Mix everything with the tongue and add a little broth
