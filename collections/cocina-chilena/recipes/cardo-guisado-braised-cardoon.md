## Ingredients

- cardoon
- salt
- water
- pine nuts
- hazelnuts
- oil
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean and remove the strings and membranes
2. Cut it, placing the pieces in cold water so they do not blacken
3. Card them and cook with salt
4. Then drain them and add a sauce of pine nuts and hazelnuts, drizzling oil over the broth
