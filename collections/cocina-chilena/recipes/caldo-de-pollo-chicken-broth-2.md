## Ingredients

- Half a chicken without fat
- One liter of water
- Lettuce leaves
- Chervil
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Place half a chicken without fat to boil in one liter of water for one hour.
2. Add lettuce leaves and chervil.
3. Season with salt.
