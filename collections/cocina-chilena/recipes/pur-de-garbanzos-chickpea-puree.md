## Ingredients

- Two pounds of chickpeas
- Stock from meat
- One onion
- Bacon
- Carrot
- A small bundle of herbs

## Directions

1. Soak chickpeas the day before with a little salt.
2. Cook with meat stock, adding one onion, bacon, carrot and a small bundle of herbs.
3. Cook gently and slowly.
4. Finish as instructed for lentil puree.
