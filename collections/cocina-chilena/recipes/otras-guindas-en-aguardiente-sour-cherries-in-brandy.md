## Ingredients

- Cherries, with pits
- Brandy (aguardiente)
- Whole cherries
- Sugar: two pounds per one pound of cherries

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries, including the pits
2. Place them in an earthenware pot and cover with brandy
3. Leave to infuse for one week
4. Strain and press out the liquid
5. In this liquid, add whole cherries and sugar (two pounds of sugar per one pound of cherries)
