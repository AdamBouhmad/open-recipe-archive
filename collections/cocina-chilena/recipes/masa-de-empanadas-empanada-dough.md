## Ingredients

- four pounds flour
- two pounds cow butter
- one pound lard
- six egg yolks
- sufficient amount of very hot water

## Directions

1. Take four pounds flour, two pounds cow butter and one pound lard
2. Skim well before incorporating them into the flour
3. Add six egg yolks and sufficient amount of very hot water
4. Knead for a long time and roll many times
