## Ingredients

- loin
- broth
- fat
- soaked bread
- red tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the loin well
2. With a spoon remove all the fat from the top of the broth in which it has been cooked
3. Place this fat in a casserole and add soaked bread and some red tomatoes ground and placed with the bread
4. Mix together and gradually add the broth
5. Place the loin in this sauce and let it rest
