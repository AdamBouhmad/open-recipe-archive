## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 4 ounces sugar
- 4 ounces burnt fat
- 1 glass of white wine
- cinnamon
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar
2. When well thickened, gradually add the hot fat little by little
3. Add cinnamon, wine and sufficient flour to make a white dough
