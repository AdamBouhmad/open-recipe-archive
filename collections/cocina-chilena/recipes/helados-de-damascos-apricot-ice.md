## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Milk, cold and cooked
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apricots, wash them, and crush them in earthenware pots, first removing the pits
2. Have ready the desired amount of milk, which should be cold and cooked
3. Pass the apricot through a sieve, combine with the milk, and sweeten to taste
4. These ices can also be made with water
