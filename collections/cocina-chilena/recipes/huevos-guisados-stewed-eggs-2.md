## Ingredients

- bread crumb
- parsley
- onion
- butter
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- cream or milk cream

## Directions

1. Make a hash with bread crumb, parsley, onion, butter and egg yolks, with which to cover the bottom of a plate
2. On top pour the eggs one by one and place over gentle heat
3. Brown with a shovel made of hot coals, sprinkle with a little ground salt and a pinch of pepper and serve
4. Pour four spoonfuls of cream or milk cream on a plate and mix ten eggs into it, taking care that the yolks do not burst
5. Add a little grated cheese and a pinch of pepper
6. Cook in a portable oven and serve soft
