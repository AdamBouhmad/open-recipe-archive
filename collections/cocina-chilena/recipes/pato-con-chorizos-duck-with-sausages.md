## Ingredients

- Sausages (chorizos)
- Duck
- Broth
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Salt
- Onion
- Clove
- Mushrooms (callampas)
- Flour
- Potatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry sausages alone
2. Cook the duck in broth with pepper, parsley, salt, one onion, and one clove
3. In the fat rendered by the sausages, fry the mushrooms and add a little flour
4. Place the duck in this mixture until it browns slightly
5. Small potatoes may be fried together with the mushrooms
