## Ingredients

- One and a half ounces of fine cinnamon
- Water
- Sugar
- Gum arabic

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the cinnamon and let it infuse quietly for a while in sufficient water
2. When well infused, strain the clear part
3. Add sugar and a little gum arabic dissolved in hot water
