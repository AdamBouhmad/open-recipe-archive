## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Small onions
- Small radishes
- Broth
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as the macaroni soup, adding asparagus and small onions scalded beforehand, and small radishes
2. To remove the bitterness of these vegetables, add a little sugar to the broth
