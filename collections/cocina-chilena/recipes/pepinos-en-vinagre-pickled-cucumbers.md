## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- whole pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them in half to remove the seeds
2. Add plenty of salt and press for one hour so they release all the water
3. Fill the jars in layers with whole pepper and salt
4. Add vinegar and cover
5. The next day, uncover, refill with vinegar and tie a piece of bladder over the top
