## Ingredients

- two and a half pounds flour
- one bottle milk
- half pound butter
- five eggs
- three tablespoons yeast
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Make a dough with two and a half pounds flour, one bottle milk, half pound butter, five eggs, three tablespoons yeast, sugar, cinnamon, and lemon zest
2. Pour into a mold
3. Let rise until it lifts
4. Then bake in the oven
