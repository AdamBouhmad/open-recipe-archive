## Ingredients

- Duck
- Butter
- Turnips
- Flour, one tablespoon
- Broth
- Parsley sprigs
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Place a little butter in the casserole and turn the duck several times, along with some turnips
2. Remove them, then add one tablespoon of flour to the butter and brown this mixture
3. Increase with broth from a pot
4. Return the duck and turnips with parsley sprigs, salt, and pepper
5. If the turnips are already tender, do not add until the duck is nearly cooked
6. Let the stew reduce if necessary and serve everything together
