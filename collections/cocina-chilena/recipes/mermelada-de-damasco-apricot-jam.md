## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Equal quantity of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the apricots in water
2. When cooked, remove and pass through a sieve
3. Mix with an equal quantity of powdered sugar
4. Put over the fire and cook until it reaches the point of setting
5. You know it is ready when a little placed on a plate does not run
6. This jam keeps well for a long time and can be used in frozen desserts
