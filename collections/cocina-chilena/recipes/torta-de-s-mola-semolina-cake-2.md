## Ingredients

- four glasses milk
- semolina
- orange blossom water
- lemon zest or vanilla
- sugar to taste
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites, well beaten
- butter
- finely grated bread

## Directions

1. Bring four glasses of milk to boil and gradually add semolina until thick
2. Let cook a little and then season with orange blossom water, lemon zest or vanilla, and sugar to taste
3. Add four egg yolks and four well-beaten egg whites
4. Butter a mold or pot and dust with finely grated bread
5. Pour everything in and finish cooking by surrounding the mold with hot ashes and moderate heat on top of the lid
6. Serve hot and well browned
