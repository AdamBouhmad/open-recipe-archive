## Ingredients

- Cooked meat, fried
- A little butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- One glass of milk
- Aromatics (herbs)
- Beaten egg
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat well, which should be fried
2. Put it in the pan with a little butter
3. When somewhat fried, add one tablespoon of flour, stir constantly so it does not burn
4. Add one glass of milk for it to cook, add aromatics
5. When everything is well cooked and buttery, let it cool
6. Then form little balls the size of an egg, roll them in beaten egg and then in bread crumbs
