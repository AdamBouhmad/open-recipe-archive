## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- parsley
- cucumbers
- hard-cooked eggs
- capers
- gherkins

## Directions

1. Remove the top point of the tomato to extract the seeds with a small spoon
2. Squeeze out the water with light pressure of the fingers
3. Fill with a paste made of chopped parsley, cucumbers, hard-cooked eggs, capers and gherkins, all chopped very finely
