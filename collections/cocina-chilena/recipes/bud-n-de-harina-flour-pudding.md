## Ingredients

- Seven eggs
- Four ounces of butter
- Four ounces of sugar
- Four ounces of flour
- One and a half bottles of milk
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with the butter and gradually add the flour, stirring constantly until the mixture detaches well from the pan
2. When the mixture is cool, add the seven beaten yolks, four ounces of sugar, lemon zest, and a little cinnamon
3. Mix well and add the beaten egg whites
4. Stir again and pour into a buttered pudding mold lined with bread crumbs
5. Place in the oven
6. Can also be made with semolina or rice flour
