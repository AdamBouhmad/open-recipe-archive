## Ingredients

- cooked rice
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- cognac
- six egg yolks
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook rice in milk and season with sugar, cinnamon, raisins, cognac and six yolks
2. Pour into a mold lined with butter and breadcrumbs
3. Place in the oven
