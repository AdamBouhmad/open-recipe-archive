## Ingredients

- rabbit
- lard
- fine herbs
- rabbit liver
- egg yolks
- bacon
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into pieces and cook in lard.
2. Place in paper packets with a filling containing fine herbs, the liver of the same rabbit and a couple of egg yolks for consistency.
3. Cover the intervals in the packet with this filling, then cover all with strips of bacon and paper spread with butter.
4. Prepared this way, place in the oven and serve with an appropriate sauce.
