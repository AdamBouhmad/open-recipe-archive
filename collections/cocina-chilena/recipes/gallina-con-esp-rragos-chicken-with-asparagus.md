## Ingredients

- Fat hen
- A little pot broth
- Onions in slices
- Asparagus
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Salt
- Pepper
- Three potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the hen in a little pot broth with onions in slices and asparagus, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper and three potatoes
2. Leave on the fire until well cooked
