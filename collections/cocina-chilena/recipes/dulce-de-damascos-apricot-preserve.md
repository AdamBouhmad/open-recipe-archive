## Ingredients

- 6 pounds sugar
- 6 pounds apricots

## Directions

1. Weigh 6 pounds sugar and 6 pounds apricots
2. Remove the pit by pushing with a small stick
3. Place them in an earthenware cup
4. Cover with more than half the sugar, which should be very well ground and white
5. Let sit until the next day
6. Place on low heat and remove when they begin to boil
7. When cold, place again on low heat and remove when they begin to boil again
8. When cold, place the apricots in jars and boil on heat until thick
9. Then pour the hot syrup over the fruit
