## Ingredients

- Sour cherries
- Spirits
- Thick syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the guindas and wash them in spirits.
2. Place them in a jar and fill it with spirits.
3. Cap it well so no air penetrates.
4. When you want to eat them, uncap and remove all the spirits and fill with thick syrup.
5. Cap and leave for fifteen days without moving them so they become impregnated with sugar; if desired they can be made another way.
6. After washing the guindas, arrange them in the jar with a layer of guindas and another of ground sugar, fill the jar well with spirits and cap it well.
