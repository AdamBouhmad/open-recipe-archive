## Ingredients

- flour
- sugar
- fat
- sweet wine
- salt
- cinnamon
- egg yolks
- egg white
- milk
- raw meat
- onion
- flour
- black pepper
- raisins
- olives
- hard-boiled eggs
- cooked chicken pieces
- chicken broth
- thick syrup
- ground almonds
- candied sour cherries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough with one pound of flour, half pound of sugar, four pounds of fat, and half cup of sweet wine in which the salt and a little cinnamon are dissolved, six yolks and one white. Form a dough and if it is too hard add a little milk and knead slightly.
2. For the filling, chop raw meat into small pieces and fry in a pan with a little color. Once the meat is cooked add the browned onion. Remove from heat and add a tablespoon of raw flour, roll it around, and season with salt, whole pepper, and a little sugar.
3. Form the empanadas and decorate the filling on top with raisins, olives, sliced hard-boiled eggs, pieces of hard-cooked chicken, and onion also cooked in chicken broth.
4. Put in the oven and when about to serve at the table, top with thick syrup mixed with ground almonds and candied sour cherries.
5. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top of everything and serve.
