## Ingredients

- tripe
- cooked pork blood
- spices
- pieces of fat pork meat
- salt water
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the tripe to be filled, tying it at the point with small strings.
2. Fill with cooked pork blood mixed with ground and toasted spices, adding some small pieces of fat pork cut into very small pieces.
3. Mix everything together and after filling, tie another string at the mouth.
4. Boil in salted water and when done, remove and cut into pieces.
5. Fry and add fried tomato sauce.
