## Ingredients

- two carrots
- one onion
- butter
- two cabbage leaves
- four to six chard leaves
- water
- a dozen lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots into thin slices
2. Cut the onion into very thin and small pieces and fry in a little butter
3. Chop two cabbage leaves and four to six chard leaves and cook them in boiling water
4. When cooked, wash and dry them in a napkin and add to the broth with the carrot and onion
5. When everything has boiled for three hours, add a dozen chopped lettuce leaves, let it boil a bit more and serve
6. If using chard, cook it separately and add at the time of serving
