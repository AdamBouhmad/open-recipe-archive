## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of chickpeas
- broth from meat
- 1 onion
- bacon
- carrot
- a bundle of herbs

## Directions

1. Soak 2 pounds of chickpeas the day before with a little salt
2. Cook them with meat broth, adding 1 onion, bacon, carrot and a bundle of herbs
3. Ensure the cooking is done slowly
4. When cooked, remove the onion, carrot and bacon
5. Pass everything through a sieve, pressing well
6. Remove the fat and season
7. Heat and serve the purée
