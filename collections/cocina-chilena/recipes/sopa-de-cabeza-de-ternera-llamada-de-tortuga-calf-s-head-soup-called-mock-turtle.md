## Ingredients

- Pieces of cooked calf's head with skin
- Thyme
- Shallot
- Laurel
- Mushroom
- Cured ham
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Take pieces of cooked calf's head, keeping the skin and cutting it into small square pieces.
2. Add thyme, shallot, laurel, mushroom, cured ham, cloves, and pepper.
3. Mix everything with butter, making a roux with sufficient water for the soup, which should be fairly thick.
4. Serve.
