## Ingredients

- Fine lettuce leaves
- Butter
- Broth
- Bread

## Directions

1. Cut fine lettuce leaves and fry them in butter
2. Pour broth over them and let it boil
3. In the tureen place small pieces of toasted bread fried in butter
4. Pour the broth over the bread
