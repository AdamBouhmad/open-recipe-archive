## Ingredients

- One tablespoon of onion, finely chopped and washed
- Four ounces of butter
- One tablespoon of chopped parsley
- One tablespoon of flour
- A little milk
- Salt
- One whole clove
- Fish, poultry or meat cooked beforehand with salt, pepper, celery and onion

## Directions

1. Fry the finely chopped and washed onion in four ounces of butter until it is softened
2. Add one tablespoon of chopped parsley and another of flour, fry a little
3. Add a little milk, salt and a whole clove
4. Leave to boil for a while with the fish, poultry or meat that has been previously cooked with salt, pepper, celery and onion
