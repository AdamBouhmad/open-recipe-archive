## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh butter
- 5 eggs
- 3/4 pound sugar
- lemon rind
- 1 pound flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pound fresh butter with 5 eggs and 3/4 pound sugar and the rind of a lemon
2. This should be beaten much
3. Add 1 pound flour
4. Grease the mold with butter and bake in a gentle oven
