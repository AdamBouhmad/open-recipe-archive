## Ingredients

- three ounces rice flour
- six cups broth
- four egg yolks
- one glass milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Add rice flour to a saucepan with six cups broth, adding gradually while taking care to dissolve the rice well in the broth
2. Place over fire stirring continuously until cooked; one hour of cooking is sufficient for serving
3. Add a mixture of four egg yolks dissolved in a glass of milk and a small piece of butter
