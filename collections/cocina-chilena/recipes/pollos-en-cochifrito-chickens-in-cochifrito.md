## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- flour
- water
- salt
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley branch
- onion
- mushrooms
- asparagus
- three egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After washing the chickens well, disjoint them and soak for one or two hours in cold water to whiten the meat
2. Drain them
3. In a pot place a piece of butter and one tablespoon flour, stir until the butter melts
4. Add one glass of water, salt, ground pepper, nutmeg, a parsley branch, and onion
5. Place the chickens there and let cook three quarters of an hour
6. Separately make the sauce with three egg yolks and lemon juice
7. When the chickens have cooked a little, add chopped mushrooms and small pieces of asparagus
8. Unite the sauce with the chickens
