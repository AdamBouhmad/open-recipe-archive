## Ingredients

- squabs
- vine leaf
- bacon strip

## Directions

1. After plucking, emptying and cleaning well the feather shafts, tie them, being careful to leave their legs lengthwise
2. Place a vine leaf on the breast, if it is in season, and over it a strip of bacon
3. Put them on the spit: three quarters of an hour are sufficient for them to be roasted
