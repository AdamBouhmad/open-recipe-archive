## Ingredients

- sierra fish
- three tablespoons of oil
- onion
- tomato, finely chopped
- celery sprigs
- parsley
- oregano
- salt
- hard-boiled egg, chopped
- whole pepper

## Directions

1. Fry some onion in three tablespoons of oil, then add finely chopped tomato, celery sprigs, parsley and oregano
2. Place the clean fish on a serving platter
3. Put all of this underneath the fish, then add salt, chopped hard-boiled egg and whole pepper
