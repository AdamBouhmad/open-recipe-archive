## Ingredients

- two tablespoons flour
- butter
- milk
- broth
- oyster broth
- oysters
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Fry about two tablespoons flour in butter
2. Add a little milk to make a paste
3. Dilute with broth and oyster broth, should be regular thickness
4. At the time of sending to the table, add the oysters (which should boil only an instant) and some toasted fried bread
