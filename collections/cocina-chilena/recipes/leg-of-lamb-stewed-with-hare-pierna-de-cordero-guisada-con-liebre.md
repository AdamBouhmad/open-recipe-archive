## Ingredients

- Leg of lamb
- onion pieces
- bay leaves
- cloves
- whole pepper
- red wine (one bottle)
- vinegar (one glass)
- butter
- fine herbs for larding
- potatoes
- cooked potatoes
- bread crumbs
- eggs
- nutmeg
- chuño (potato starch)

## Directions

1. Cut the tip of the lamb leg to give it a round shape
2. Remove the fat and skin
3. Pound it well and rub with salt on all sides
4. Place in a deep dish with onion pieces, bay leaves, two cloves, whole pepper, a bottle of red wine and a glass of vinegar
5. Leave for three days entirely in this marinade, well covered, turning it from side to side occasionally
6. Remove from marinade and lard finely on both sides
7. Place a piece of butter in a pot and put the leg there just to take a nice color
8. Add the same marinade gradually with a little hot water
9. Leave to boil until cooked, basting occasionally with the sauce
10. Remove the meat and strain the sauce and thicken with a little chuño
11. Place the meat in a dish and sauce on top, surrounded by potato balls
12. For the balls: mix cooked grated potatoes, bread crumbs, eggs and nutmeg
