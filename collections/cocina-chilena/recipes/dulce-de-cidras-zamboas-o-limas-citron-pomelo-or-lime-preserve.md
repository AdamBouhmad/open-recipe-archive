## Ingredients

- Citrons, pomelos, or limes
- Salt
- Water
- Sugar, one and a half libras per one libra of fruit

## Directions

1. Peel them, place to cook in boiling water with a handful of salt, and when very tender, remove.
2. Drain in cold water until they lose the bitterness, squeeze in a basket and then weigh them; for one libra of fruit use one and a half libras of sugar.
3. Make this in syrup at half point and let cool.
4. Add the fruit, bring to point in three boils.
