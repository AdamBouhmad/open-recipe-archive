## Ingredients

- Roasted hen
- Lard
- Onion, chopped
- Parsley
- Broth
- Wine or vinegar
- Fresh lard
- Spices
- Vegetables, chopped
- Sorrel, in particular

## Directions

1. After roasting, cut into pieces and brown with lard, chopped onion and parsley
2. When in point, add the necessary broth to cover the pieces and a little wine or vinegar and fresh lard
3. Season with spices
4. If desired, add some chopped vegetables, or particularly sorrel, which make a good stew
