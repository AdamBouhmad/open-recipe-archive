## Ingredients

- Culen wood shavings
- Guindo wood shavings
- A little quinoa
- Sugar
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Toasted corn kernels

## Directions

1. Scrape some culen wood sticks and others of guindo and with a little quinoa put them to boil.
2. Remove from the fire, add as much sugar as desired, clarify it, give it a short boil and strain.
3. When it is warm, pour it into a calabash with allspice, cloves and cinnamon and some toasted corn kernels.
4. Quinoa can be replaced with corn.
