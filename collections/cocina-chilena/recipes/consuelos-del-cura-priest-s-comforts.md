## Ingredients

- 1 whole egg
- 4 ounces powdered sugar
- a little lemon zest
- 3 ounces melted butter
- 1 glass of rum
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the whole egg with powdered sugar and lemon zest
2. Add the melted butter and rum
3. Add sufficient flour to make a white dough
4. Knead and extend with a rolling pin until the thickness of a finger
5. Cut the dough into small squares with a rounded cutter
6. Fry in not very hot lard
7. Serve dusted with powdered sugar and may be placed in the oven
