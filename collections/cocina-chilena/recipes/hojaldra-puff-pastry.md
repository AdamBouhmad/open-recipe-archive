## Ingredients

- 6 whole eggs
- sufficient flour
- 1 large spoonful of well-rendered lard
- 1 spoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Knead and roll out the dough very thin
2. Add 1 spoonful of vinegar so the dough remains soft
3. Stretch with your hands, cut, and fry
