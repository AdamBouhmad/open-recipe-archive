## Ingredients

- bacon
- onion
- leg meat
- salt
- pepper
- warm water
- parsley
- mint
- lettuce heart
- soaked pine nuts
- spices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry bacon cut in dice-like pieces with some finely chopped onion
2. Cut the meat into pieces and place everything in a pot with salt and pepper
3. Reheat over gentle heat and then add warm water
4. When cooked, add parsley, a mint leaf, a lettuce heart, soaked pine nuts, and spices, all finely chopped
5. When serving, add a little lemon juice
