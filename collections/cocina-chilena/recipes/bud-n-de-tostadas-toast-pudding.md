## Ingredients

- Beer bread
- Butter
- Preserved fruit in syrup, preferably quince
- Five egg yolks
- Two egg whites
- Sugar
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut beer bread into slices and butter half of them
2. Arrange in a pudding mold prepared like the previous ones
3. Alternate layers of buttered toast with preserved fruit in syrup
4. Beat five yolks and two whites, add necessary sugar and enough milk to well cover the toast
5. Pour the beaten mixture over the toast and let soak for two hours
6. Place in the oven
