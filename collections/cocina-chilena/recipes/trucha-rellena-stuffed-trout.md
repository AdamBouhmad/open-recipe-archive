## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- melted butter
- onion, chopped
- bread crumbs
- vegetables of choice
- laurel leaves
- capers sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the trout well, cut it into pieces, add salt and after a while dry it with a cloth
2. Make a filling with melted butter, chopped onion, bread crumbs and the vegetables you wish
3. In a platter well-coated with butter, place the stuffed trout pieces with the filling, onion slices and laurel leaves
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and put in the oven
5. Serve with capers sauce
