## Ingredients

- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- onion
- pepper, powdered
- bread
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. In a saucepan put vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, onion and powdered pepper
2. Fry a little bread that is dissolved in broth
3. Add salt and let it boil for a quarter hour
4. Pass it through a sieve
