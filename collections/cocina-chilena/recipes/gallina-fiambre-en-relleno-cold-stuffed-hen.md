## Ingredients

- hens
- vinegar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- pimientos
- garlic
- raw meat
- bacon, chopped
- salt

## Directions

1. Kill the hens carefully so the neck does not break
2. Pluck in hot water, split along the backbone from the neck to the rump and remove the eggs carefully so they come out whole
3. Wash the carcasses well and place them in a little vinegar seasoned with cinnamon, cloves, two pimientos and some crushed garlic cloves; keep in this vinegar for four to six hours
4. Meanwhile mash well a piece of raw meat and mix with part of the pimientos that were added to the vinegar and a little chopped bacon
5. Stuff the hens with this and strain the seasoned vinegar over them and cook in water with a little salt
