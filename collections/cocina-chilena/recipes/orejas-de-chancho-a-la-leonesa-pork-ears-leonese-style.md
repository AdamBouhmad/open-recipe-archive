## Ingredients

- pork ears
- onions
- lard
- flour
- broth
- vinegar or lemon juice
- fried croutons (optional)

## Directions

1. In a sauce of onions chopped and sautéed in lard, place the ears cut into strips.
2. Add a little flour, moisten with broth and reduce.
3. Serve adding a few drops of vinegar or lemon juice, and if desired with fried croutons.
