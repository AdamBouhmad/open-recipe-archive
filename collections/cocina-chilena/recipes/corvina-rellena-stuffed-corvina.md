## Ingredients

- corvina
- onion
- tomato
- hard boiled egg
- celery
- parsley
- water
- egg

## Directions

1. Chop onion and tomato very finely, fry well in color
2. Add chopped hard boiled egg, a bit of celery and parsley, well seasoned
3. Layer this with raw corvina in a dish
4. Add a bit of water; when ready to bring to table, add a beaten egg
