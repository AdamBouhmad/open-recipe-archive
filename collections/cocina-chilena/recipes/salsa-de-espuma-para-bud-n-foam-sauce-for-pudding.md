## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- ten ounces of sugar
- one glass of sherry or white wine
- juice of two lemons
- zest of one lemon
- eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix well eight egg yolks with ten ounces of sugar
2. Add one glass of sherry or white wine, the juice of two lemons, and the zest of one
3. Place over heat and beat constantly until foamy
4. Fold in the eight well-beaten egg whites
5. Give it a light boil, beating constantly
6. Serve with the pudding
