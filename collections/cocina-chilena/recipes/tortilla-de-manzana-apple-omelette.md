## Ingredients

- Butter
- Three or four apples
- Eggs
- Milk
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Fry three or four finely chopped apples in butter
2. When they are golden, pour over them a beaten egg mixture prepared like the previous one
3. Fry the same way, but do not brown it
