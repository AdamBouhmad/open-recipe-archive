## Ingredients

- conger
- water
- salt
- garlic
- pepper
- flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in salted water and let the broth reduce
2. Prepare a sauce of roasted garlic and a bit of pepper thickened with flour
3. Add to the conger and at the same time of serving sprinkle with vinegar
