## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar
- 2 ounces bitter almonds reduced to very fine paste with a little egg white
- 4 whole eggs
- zest of 1 lemon
- 2 ounces flour

## Directions

1. Beat all ingredients together for half an hour
2. Then add 2 ounces of flour
3. Place small pieces of this dough on sheets of paper buttered and placed on baking tins
4. Top with almonds cut lengthwise
5. Bake in a gentle oven for half an hour
