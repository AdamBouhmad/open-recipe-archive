## Ingredients

- fish
- flour
- oil or lard
- eggs

## Directions

1. In general, for all kinds of fried fish, it must be scaled, opened and cleaned
2. Then cut into rounds, coat with flour and fry in a pan with very hot oil
3. Carp and other fish are also fried with pork lard and served with fried eggs
4. Small river fish and others of this kind are served whole and cleaned
