## Ingredients

- Four ounces of almonds
- Milk
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon stick
- One ounce of isinglass
- Water

## Directions

1. Peel four ounces of almonds and sprinkle with a little milk in a pot with lemon peel and whole cinnamon
2. Boil briefly
3. In another pot, place the almonds without straining with two cups of milk and bring to a slow boil
4. Place one ounce of isinglass at the edge of the fire with a little water to dissolve
