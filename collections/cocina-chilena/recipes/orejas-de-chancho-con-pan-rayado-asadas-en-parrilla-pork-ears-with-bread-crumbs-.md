## Ingredients

- pork ears
- bread crumbs
- eggs
- clear juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Place over hot coals after passing through the flame, washed, cleaned and blanched.
2. When tender and cold, coat in bread crumbs, dip in beaten eggs, pass again through bread crumbs and grill on the grill.
3. Serve dry or with clear juice.
