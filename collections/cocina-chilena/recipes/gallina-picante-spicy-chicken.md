## Ingredients

- Raw hen
- Butter
- Two sweet or green peppers, or dried peppers, seeded and cut
- Ground saffron
- Flour, about one tablespoon
- Broth

## Directions

1. Put the raw, disjointed hen in a saucepan with butter
2. Add peppers cut without seeds, ground saffron, flour like one tablespoon and broth
3. Cover and cook on low heat
