## Ingredients

- one hen or capon
- water
- onion heads
- sweet peppers
- whole peppercorns
- two tablespoons oil
- vinegar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the bird with water, onion heads, sweet peppers, and some whole peppercorns
2. When tender, add two tablespoons of oil and vinegar to taste
