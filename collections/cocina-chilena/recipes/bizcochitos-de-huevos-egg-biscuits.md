## Ingredients

- Twenty egg yolks, well beaten
- Four ounces of sugar
- Four ounces of unsalted butter
- Flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks well
2. Add the sugar and butter
3. Add flour as necessary to form a soft dough
4. Knead well
5. Cut the biscuits and bake
