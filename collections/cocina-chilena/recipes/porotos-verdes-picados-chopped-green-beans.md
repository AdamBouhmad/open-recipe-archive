## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- onion
- abundant fat
- meat (to roast in advance)

## Directions

1. Break the beans into small pieces
2. Cook them in salted water
3. Once cooked, discard the water
4. Make a sofrito of onion and abundant fat
5. Put the beans in a dry platter and break them up with a spoon until somewhat ground
6. Pour everything into the onion sofrito
7. Leave on low heat
8. Just before serving, add the meat that was roasted in advance
