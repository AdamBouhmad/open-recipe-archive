## Ingredients

- Lamb leg
- linen cloth
- bay leaves
- thyme
- spices
- pepper
- water
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Wrap in linen with bay leaves, thyme, spices and pepper
2. Place in boiling water
3. Cook as many hours as the leg weighs in pounds
4. Remove and unwrap
5. Serve with caper sauce
