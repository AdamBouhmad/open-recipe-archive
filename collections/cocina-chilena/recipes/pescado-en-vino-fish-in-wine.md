## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- pimienta (pepper)
- mantequilla (butter)
- cebollas (onions)
- two tablespoons of color (coloring agent)
- half a cup of vino (wine)
- harina (flour)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and wash it.
2. Season inside and outside with pepper and butter.
3. Place sliced onions in a dish with two tablespoons of coloring agent.
4. Put the fish on top of the onions.
5. Add another layer of onions above the fish and half a cup of wine.
6. Cover the dish and bring to a boil.
7. If preserved, add half a tablespoon of flour when it boils.
