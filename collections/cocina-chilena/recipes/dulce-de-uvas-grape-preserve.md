## Ingredients

- White Italian grapes, the sweetest and most golden
- A small amount of whey (lagrimilla)
- A little ash

## Directions

1. Deseed the grapes, leaving no stem.
2. Place in a saucepan over the fire with a small amount of whey and a little ash to remove the acidity.
3. Keep the grapes on the fire until the seed loosens; remove immediately so they do not overcook.
4. Extract the seed and rinse in the same whey in which they were blanched until no ash remains.
5. Transfer to a thick syrup that should be ready, because the preserve must be completed on the same day.
6. Do not add too much grape to the syrup as it will become poor and unattractive; it should be well saturated with syrup, and to bring it to point, do not place a large quantity in the saucepan.
