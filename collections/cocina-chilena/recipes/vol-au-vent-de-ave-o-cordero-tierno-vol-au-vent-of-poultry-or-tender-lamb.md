## Ingredients

- Poultry or lamb
- Butter
- Onion
- White wine
- Salt
- Broth
- Flour
- Mushrooms
- Chestnuts
- Sausages
- Brains
- Meat
- Pepper
- Asparagus

## Directions

1. Cut up the bird or lamb and place in a pot with butter, very finely chopped onion, a glass of white wine, and a little salt
2. Fry for five minutes and add the necessary broth to cook
3. When well cooked, arrange the pieces in a serving dish
4. Strain the broth and thicken with a little flour fried in butter
5. In the same dish arrange raw washed mushrooms, cooked chestnuts, fried sausages, pieces of cooked brains, and small balls of meat and pepper
6. If in season, add asparagus tips, cover with puff pastry, and place in the oven
