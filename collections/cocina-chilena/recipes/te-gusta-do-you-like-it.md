## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound peeled and ground almonds
- powdered sugar (some amount)
- 1/2 ounce ground cinnamon
- 1/2 ounce ground cloves
- zest of 1 lemon
- 2 candied lemons
- 3 ounces toasted bread, ground very fine
- 12 eggs
- butter
- ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Grind 1/2 pound peeled almonds with some powdered sugar
2. Add 1/2 ounce ground cinnamon, 1/2 ounce ground cloves, zest of 1 lemon, 2 candied lemons, and 3 ounces toasted bread ground very fine
3. Add 12 eggs
4. Beat everything together, always in one direction
5. Coat the mold with butter and ground biscuit
6. Bake in the oven for 3/4 hour
