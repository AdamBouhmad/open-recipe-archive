## Ingredients

- four carrots
- broth
- butter
- nutmeg
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook four carrots and when cooked mash with a spoon and pass through a sieve
2. Prepare the broth and half an hour before serving add the carrot purée, a piece of butter and a little nutmeg
3. Fry some toasted bread pieces the size and shape of a dime and place in the soup tureen when serving
