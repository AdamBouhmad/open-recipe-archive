## Ingredients

- half a pound of potatoes
- four ounces of butter
- six ounces of powdered sugar
- lemon zest and juice
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Mash half a pound of potatoes
2. Melt four ounces of butter
3. Add six ounces of powdered sugar and lemon zest and juice
4. Mix everything well with four eggs
5. Butter the dish and place it in the oven for half an hour
