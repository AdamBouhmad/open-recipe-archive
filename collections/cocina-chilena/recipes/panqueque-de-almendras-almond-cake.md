## Ingredients

- six eggs with shells, weighed
- sugar equal to the weight of the eggs
- five ounces butter
- four ounces almonds
- flour equal to the weight of four whole eggs

## Directions

1. Weigh six eggs without breaking them and measure sugar equal to their weight
2. Combine the butter with the flour and almond
3. Beat the egg yolks well with the sugar
4. Combine all ingredients and add the egg whites beaten separately as for meringues
5. Pour into a buttered mold and place in a not-too-hot oven
