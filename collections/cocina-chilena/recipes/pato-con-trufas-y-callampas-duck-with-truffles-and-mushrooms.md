## Ingredients

- Flour
- Butter
- Broth
- Truffles, finely chopped
- Mushrooms (callampas), finely chopped
- Pepper, ground
- Salt
- Duck, raw and cut into pieces
- Onion heads, cut into small pieces
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Fry a little flour in butter
2. Add broth and when the sauce is cooked and of regular thickness, add finely chopped truffles and mushrooms
3. Season with ground pepper and salt
4. Place raw duck pieces, onion heads cut into small pieces, and chopped parsley with the sauce
5. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently so it does not stick
