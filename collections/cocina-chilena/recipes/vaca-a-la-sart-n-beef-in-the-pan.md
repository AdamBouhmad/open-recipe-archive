## Ingredients

- onion
- beef
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut an onion in half, separating the head and tail, place in the pan without letting it turn black
2. After cutting each half into thin strips, add the piece of meat, separating the bones, skin and sinews
3. Heat gently, tossing the slices
4. When serving hot, add a few drops of vinegar
5. For more economy, the fat of the meat can be used in place of lard
