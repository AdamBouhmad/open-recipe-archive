## Ingredients

- Hen
- Bacon strips
- Oil or fat for the paper
- Watercress
- Salt
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Lard with bacon strips
2. Wrap in oiled paper and put on the roaster
3. When almost roasted, remove the paper so it takes color
4. Baste the bacon leaves
5. If possible, place on watercress on a plate, sprinkled with salt, with a little vinegar and serve watered with the same
