## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- fresh butter
- onions
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut hard boiled eggs in two pieces lengthwise
2. Mash the yolks with an equal amount of bread crumb soaked and squeezed of milk
3. Add the same amount of fresh butter
4. Add chopped onions and parsley, salt, pepper, fine spices
5. Mash this seasoning and soften with raw egg yolks
6. Fill the whites with these and put the rest on a fire-resistant plate, forming a layer one finger thick
7. Arrange the eggs on top, place the plate in the oven with gentle heat above and below so it takes color
