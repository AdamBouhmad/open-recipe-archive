## Ingredients

- Milk
- Peeled and ground walnuts
- Syrup (almíbar)
- Eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook milk with peeled and ground walnuts
2. After cooking, pass through a sieve
3. Sweeten with syrup
4. Beat eight egg yolks and add to the milk
5. Place back on the fire
6. Remove, let cool, and freeze
