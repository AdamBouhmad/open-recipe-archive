## Ingredients

- slices of roasted loin from the day before
- onion slices
- plenty of tomato
- slices of fried potatoes
- fried bread toasts
- fat
- celery
- parsley
- whole pepper
- two spoonfuls of color

## Directions

1. Cut slices of a roasted loin from the day before
2. Put in a dish slices of onions, plenty of tomato, slices of fried potatoes, and fried bread toasts in fat, celery, parsley, whole pepper, two spoonfuls of color
3. Cover well and let it boil
