## Ingredients

- Lamb tongue
- bacon
- oil
- parsley
- bay leaf
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Blanch the tongues and add with thin bacon
2. When half cooked, remove the skin
3. Continue cooking for half an hour over low heat
4. Serve hot with a sauce made of oil, parsley, bay leaf, pepper and vinegar
