## Ingredients

- Two cups of thick orgeat
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- A piece of butter
- Six egg yolks
- Two egg whites

## Directions

1. Make two cups of thick orgeat and sweeten to taste
2. Add a little cinnamon, lemon zest, and a piece of butter
3. Thicken the orgeat with six yolks and two whites
4. Mix everything together and pour into a mold lined with butter and bread crumbs
5. Place in the oven
6. Serve with a wine or foam sauce
