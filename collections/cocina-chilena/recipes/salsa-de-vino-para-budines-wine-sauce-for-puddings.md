## Ingredients

- one glass of wine
- water
- sugar
- lemon peel
- pieces of whole cinnamon
- one tablespoon of chuño dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Boil one glass of wine with water, sugar, lemon peel, and pieces of whole cinnamon
2. Add one tablespoon of chuño dissolved in water
3. Let it boil and serve with the pudding
