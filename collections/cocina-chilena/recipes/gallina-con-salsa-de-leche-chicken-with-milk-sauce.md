## Ingredients

- A little oil
- One tablespoon of butter
- One glass of milk
- Hen, cut into pieces
- Carrots in slices
- One egg
- Flour

## Directions

1. Put a little oil, one tablespoon of butter and one glass of milk in a saucepan
2. Once the butter is dissolved, put the hen cut into pieces and carrots in slices
3. After everything boils, add one egg beaten with flour
