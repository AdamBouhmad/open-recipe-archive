## Ingredients

- one pound potatoes
- nine ounces almonds
- nine eggs
- lemon zest
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat sugar with egg yolks
2. Add almonds and potatoes
3. Finally add beaten egg whites
4. Bake in the oven
