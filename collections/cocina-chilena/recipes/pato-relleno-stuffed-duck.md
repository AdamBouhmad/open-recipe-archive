## Ingredients

- Duck, boned
- Duck breast meat
- Duck heart
- Duck liver
- Four eggs
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Lemon zest
- Beef, finely chopped (optional)
- Milk (optional)
- Extra egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Bone the duck
2. Take meat from the breast, heart, and liver, chop very finely
3. Warm a little butter and beat until foamy
4. Mix four eggs, the chopped meat, bread crumbs, and lemon zest
5. To increase volume, may add finely chopped beef, milk, and another egg
6. Combine everything together
7. Stuff the duck and stitch closed so the filling does not escape
8. Place in oven to roast
9. Make a mushroom sauce, to which chestnuts may be added
10. At meal time, remove the thread from the duck, place over the sauce, and serve
