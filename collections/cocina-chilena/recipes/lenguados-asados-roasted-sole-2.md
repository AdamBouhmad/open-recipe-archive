## Ingredients

- sole
- butter
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- mustard
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put in a pot a sauce of butter and fine herbs, salt and chopped pepper
2. Place the sole on top covered with a light layer of grated bread crust and a little melted mustard
3. Pour a little white wine and place in the oven over low heat
4. When done, remove and serve with lemon juice
