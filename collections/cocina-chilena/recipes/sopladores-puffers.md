## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 4 ounces fat
- 4 eggs
- 2 ounces sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a mound with 2 pounds flour and leave a hollow in the middle
2. Place the 4 eggs without beating in the hollow
3. Add a little salt dissolved in boiling water and the hot fat
4. Stir with a knife so everything combines with the flour
5. The dough should be firm and is softened by kneading a lot
6. Cut the puffers
7. Place in moderate oven
