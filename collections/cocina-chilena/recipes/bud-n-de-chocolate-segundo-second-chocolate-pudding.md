## Ingredients

- Six ounces of powdered sugar
- Eight eggs
- Four ounces of peeled and finely chopped almonds
- Four ounces of shredded chocolate
- Two ounces of fine flour
- Butter
- Cream sauce

## Directions

1. Beat eight yolks with sugar as for sponge cake
2. When the mixture is well thickened, beat eight whites separately as for meringue
3. Unite the two mixtures and quickly add the flour, almonds, and chocolate
4. Stir and immediately place in a mold lined with butter
5. Bake in a not very hot oven
6. Make a cream sauce to serve hot
7. It is inadvisable for the oven to be hot before beginning the beating
