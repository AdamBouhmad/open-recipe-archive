## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- Cooked brains
- Flour or breadcrumbs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Instead of soaked breadcrumbs, use cooked and chopped brains
2. Wrap the meatballs in flour or breadcrumbs
3. Brown them in a pan with a little lard
