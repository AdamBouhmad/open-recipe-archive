## Ingredients

- three slices of bread
- lard or butter
- three or four hard egg yolks
- broth
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take three slices of bread and fry them in lard or butter
2. After they are well browned mix them with three or four hard egg yolks
3. Dissolve with a little broth
4. Add pepper and parsley and place over the poultry or meat
