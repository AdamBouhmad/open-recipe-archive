## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Salt
- Oil or butter
- Chopped parsley
- Fried crusts or bread, optional

## Directions

1. Cook lentils with salt.
2. Place in a pot with oil or butter and chopped parsley.
3. Brown slightly.
4. Add water.
5. Serve with or without fried bread crusts.
