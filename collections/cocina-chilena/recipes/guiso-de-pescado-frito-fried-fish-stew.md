## Ingredients

- fried fish
- long onion
- tomato
- potatoes
- cheese
- broth

## Directions

1. Fry long onion with a little tomato; separately fry very thin slices of potatoes
2. Arrange one layer in a serving dish or pot and grate a little cheese on it
3. Place the fish and cover it with a layer equal to the first
4. Over all pour one cup of broth, place over low heat and do not stir but only move it
