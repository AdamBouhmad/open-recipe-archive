## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- four ounces of sugar
- half small glass of pisco or strong liquor
- one and one-half small glasses of water with a little salt and cinnamon
- flour as needed
- two ounces of hot fat, well burned

## Directions

1. Beat six egg yolks with four ounces of sugar like sponge cake
2. Add one and one-half small glasses of water with a little salt and cinnamon and enough flour so the dough is not hard
3. Add two ounces of hot fat, well burned, knead much, stretch and cut the cookies
4. These are fried well and dried in a pot of boiling water, taking out those that float on top
5. Wrap in a hot cloth and then place in the oven
