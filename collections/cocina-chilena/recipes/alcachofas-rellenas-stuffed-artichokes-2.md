## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- lard
- parsley
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- ground meat
- egg yolks
- hazelnut sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and boil them once in salted water
2. Place them on a board to drain well
3. Have lard in a pot, and after filling the artichokes with a mixture of chopped parsley, oil and grated breadcrumbs or very finely ground meat, place them in it on low heat
4. Cover the pot with an iron lid with coals until they brown
5. If desired with sauce, pass them through a beaten mixture of egg yolks and add a hazelnut sauce
