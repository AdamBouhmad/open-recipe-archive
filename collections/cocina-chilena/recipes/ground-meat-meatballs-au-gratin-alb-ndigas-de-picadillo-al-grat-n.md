## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- Flour
- Lard
- Breadcrumbs
- Broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the ground meat as described before
2. Form balls and roll them in flour
3. Coat a dish well with lard that can be placed on fire
4. Place the balls in it, one against the other without touching
5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and moisten with a little broth
6. Place a small piece of lard on each ball
7. Brown in the oven
8. The broth is not absolutely necessary
9. Eat these balls plain or with a sauce
