## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- bacon slice
- vine leaves
- butter
- water
- sour cream
- bread crumb

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges and lard them very finely
2. Add a little salt and a thin slice of bacon, wrap each one in two vine leaves, tie and put them in boiling butter with a little water so they do not roast
3. Then add sour cream (aged like butter) and a little bread crumb that has been fried a moment in butter
4. Remove the leaves and bacon and serve apart
