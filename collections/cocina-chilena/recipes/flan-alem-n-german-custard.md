## Ingredients

- one and a half bottles of milk
- half a pound of sugar
- vanilla
- four ounces of ground almond
- lemon peel
- twelve egg yolks
- ten ounces of chuño

## Directions

1. Boil one and a half bottles of milk with half a pound of sugar, vanilla, four ounces of ground almond, and lemon peel
2. Strain and beat twelve egg yolks
3. When the milk is lukewarm, gradually add the yolks with the chuño
4. Keep on the heat until cooked, stirring constantly so it doesn't stick
5. Pour into a mold that should be moistened with cold water
6. Let it cool and serve with wine sauce
