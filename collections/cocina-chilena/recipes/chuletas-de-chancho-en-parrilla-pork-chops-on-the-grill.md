## Ingredients

- pork chops
- salt
- peppery sauce with pickles

## Directions

1. Cut the chops like veal chops, leaving some fat
2. Flatten them and give them a good shape
3. Sprinkle with salt and place on the grill
4. Serve with a peppery sauce with pickles
