## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- onion
- hard-boiled egg
- oil
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Cook peas with salt
2. Drain the water and cool
3. Blanch a little onion, wash well in cold water and add to peas
4. Add chopped hard-boiled egg
5. Dress with oil and orange juice to taste
