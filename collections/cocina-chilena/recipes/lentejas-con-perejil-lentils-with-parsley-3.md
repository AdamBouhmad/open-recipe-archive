## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cook in water and salt
2. Drain and place in a pan with abundant butter, chopped parsley, salt, and a little pepper
3. Sauté for several minutes and serve with fried bread crusts, all very hot
