## Ingredients

- parsley shoots
- mint
- bread, soaked
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Take parsley shoots, mint and soaked bread
2. On the stone where everything will be ground, add vinegar and pepper
3. When it is well ground, add vinegar again, salt and oil, balancing the oil with the vinegar
