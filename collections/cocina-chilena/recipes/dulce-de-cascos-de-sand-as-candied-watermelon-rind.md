## Ingredients

- Watermelon rinds
- 1.5 pounds sugar per 1 pound watermelon rind
- Almond water

## Directions

1. Peel the watermelon rinds and place them in the sun for half a day.
2. Give them whatever shape you desire.
3. Soak them for one day in warm almond water.
4. Place them to cook in boiling water, remove them and drain in cold water until they lose the salt.
5. Strain them in a basket and weigh 1.5 pounds of sugar for every 1 pound of watermelon.
6. When the syrup is at medium point and cooled, add the rinds and bring to point over low fire.
7. If you want to make them candied, remove them from the syrup to dry; heat them and pass them through syrup at caramel point.
8. Melon rinds are prepared the same way.
