## Ingredients

- A good piece of butter
- Pieces of chopped pepper
- Flour
- A little saffron (optional)
- Broth
- Nutmeg zest
- Hot pepper powder (optional)

## Directions

1. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with pieces of chopped pepper, flour, and if desired a little saffron
2. Add a little broth and bring to a boil
3. Add nutmeg zest and serve very hot
4. This sauce is of very good taste and is very suitable for arranging leftover poultry from other days
5. It is very digestive and gives much warmth to the stomach
6. It can be simplified by simply adding a pinch of hot pepper
