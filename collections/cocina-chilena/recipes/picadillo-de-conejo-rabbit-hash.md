## Ingredients

- rabbit
- rabbit bones
- veal scraps
- lean bacon
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Remove bones and membranes from the rabbit and mince very finely what remains.
2. Sauté in lard the crushed bones with veal scraps, lean bacon, salt and pepper, dusting everything with a little flour.
3. Mix well and add milk.
4. After it has boiled for one hour, remove and pass through a sieve, reducing to the consistency of a stew, mixing with the hash which should cook alone.
