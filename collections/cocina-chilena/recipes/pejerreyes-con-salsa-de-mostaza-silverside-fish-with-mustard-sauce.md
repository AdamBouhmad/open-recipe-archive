## Ingredients

- Silverside fish
- Flour
- Warm brine
- Four egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Oil
- Fat

## Directions

1. Bone the silverside fish well
2. Make a batter of flour with a little warm brine and mix four egg yolks with egg whites beaten as for meringues
3. Add one and a half spoonfuls of oil and combine everything
4. Dip the fish in this sauce before putting it in the hot fat
