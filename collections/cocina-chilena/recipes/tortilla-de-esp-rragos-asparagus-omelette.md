## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Fat
- Eggs
- Raw flour
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the tender part of the asparagus into small pieces and fry in plenty of fat, being careful to stir it constantly until the asparagus is half cooked
2. Then beat four eggs and add one tablespoon of raw flour, salt and pepper
3. Remove the asparagus from the fat and add to the beaten mixture
4. Keep the pan with very hot fat and fry the omelette
