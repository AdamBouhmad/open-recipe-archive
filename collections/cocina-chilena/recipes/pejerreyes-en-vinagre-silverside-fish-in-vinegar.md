## Ingredients

- Silverside fish
- Butter
- Vinegar
- Laurel leaves
- Oil
- Garlic
- Black peppercorns

## Directions

1. Fry the silverside fish (not much) in butter
2. Place in vinegar that has been boiled beforehand with laurel leaves and is now cold
3. Pour over it half a bottle of oil boiled with ground garlic
4. Add finely ground black peppercorns over all this
