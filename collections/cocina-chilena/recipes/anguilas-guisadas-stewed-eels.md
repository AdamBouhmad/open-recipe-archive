## Ingredients

- eels
- parsley
- pepper
- clove
- saffron
- cinnamon
- water
- salt
- pine nuts or hazelnuts
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces; after washing, put in a pot
2. Add chopped parsley, pepper, clove, saffron and cinnamon
3. Cook with water and salt
4. As they cook, make a sauce of pine nuts or hazelnuts with soaked breadcrumbs
5. Let this sauce boil a couple of times, stirring everything well so it does not burn
6. Season in proportion
