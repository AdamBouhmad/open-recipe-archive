## Ingredients

- 12 ounces unpeeled almonds, ground not very finely
- 12 ounces ground sugar
- half an ounce of fine cinnamon
- the peel of a candied lemon
- 4 ounces butter
- half an ounce of nutmeg
- 5 beaten egg whites
- a little clove

## Directions

1. Mix everything together
2. Spread on wafers
3. Place in a pastry oven for half an hour with heat from below, but not very strong
