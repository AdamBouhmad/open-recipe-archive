## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Bacon
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pass the cauliflower with bacon, butter, and a little flour
2. Add a little broth, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Place on low heat
