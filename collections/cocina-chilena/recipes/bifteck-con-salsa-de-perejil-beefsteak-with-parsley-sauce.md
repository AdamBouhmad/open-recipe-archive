## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- handful of parsley
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the beefsteak on a grill
2. When it is done on one side, turn it
3. Grind a handful of parsley until it becomes a paste
4. Mix it with a piece of butter and the juice of a lemon
5. When placing the beefsteak on a platter, spread the sauce on the other side
