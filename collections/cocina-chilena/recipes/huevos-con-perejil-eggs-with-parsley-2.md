## Ingredients

- parsley
- saffron
- salt
- pepper
- mint leaf
- fine herbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. In a wide vessel put parsley, saffron, salt, pepper, a mint leaf and fine herbs, all in branch
2. Pour the fried eggs and let them come to a boil
3. Remove with a large skimmer
