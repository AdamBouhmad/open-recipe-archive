## Ingredients

- beef lard
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take one pound of beef lard per pound of flour. Mix the dough very soft by adding cold water.
2. Flatten with the rolling pin until it is the thickness of a silver coin. Coat the surface well with beef lard, fold in half, pass the rolling pin again and continue.
