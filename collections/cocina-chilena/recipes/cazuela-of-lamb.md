## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- water
- garlic
- cumin
- pepper
- potatoes
- rice
- green beans
- peas
- egg

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and put it to cook
2. When well skimmed, remove the broth and fry the meat in color with ground garlic, cumin and a few grains of pepper
3. Add the broth to the pot, add potatoes, rice, green beans and peas
4. Before serving at table, season with beaten egg
