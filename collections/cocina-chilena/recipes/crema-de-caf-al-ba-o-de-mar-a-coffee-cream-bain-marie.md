## Ingredients

- Four ounces of roasted coffee
- Four glasses of milk
- Eight egg yolks
- Half a pound of sugar
- A grain of salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Add four ounces of roasted coffee to four glasses of boiling milk
2. Cover the infusion and when cold, strain through a cloth
3. Beat eight egg yolks and mix with a spoon
4. Add half a pound of sugar and a grain of salt
5. Mix everything well and pass through a strainer
6. Pour the cream into a mold greased with butter
7. Place the mold in a pan and cook over very gentle heat, ensuring the water does not boil
8. It is done when it is firm to the touch
