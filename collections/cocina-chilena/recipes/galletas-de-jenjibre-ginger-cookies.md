## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- two pounds of chancaca or cane honey
- half pound of dark sugar
- candied orange peel
- one and one-half ounces of ginger
- twelve ounces of melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix all together and let rest for one hour
2. Then place in small pieces on a baking sheet, flatten with the hand, and place in the oven for ten minutes
