## Ingredients

- potatoes
- beaten eggs
- flour
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- cow meat
- raisins
- olives
- chicken
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes and grind them on stone
2. Beat eggs into them, add a little flour, sugar, cinnamon and a little butter
3. Knead everything well until forming a paste with which to cover the cake
4. Prepare the hash as before and use fire in the same manner
