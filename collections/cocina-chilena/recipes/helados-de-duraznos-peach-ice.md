## Ingredients

- Four pounds of sliced peaches
- Two pounds of sugar
- Juice of four lemons

## Directions

1. Boil the sliced peaches for a short time
2. Pass them through a sieve
3. Mix in two pounds of sugar made into syrup and the juice of four lemons
4. Mix all together and freeze
