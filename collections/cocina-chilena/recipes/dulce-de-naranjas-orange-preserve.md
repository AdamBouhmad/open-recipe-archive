## Ingredients

- Oranges, fresh
- Sugar, four libras per one dozen regular-sized oranges
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, leaving the rind, and place in cold water so they do not turn black; then cut in half, removing the seeds and place to cook in boiling water.
2. When tender, place to drip in a basket.
3. Place in cold syrup to bring to point, and at the time of removing them add a little lemon juice so they become bright.
4. Use four libras of sugar per one dozen oranges of regular size.
