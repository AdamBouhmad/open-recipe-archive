## Ingredients

- raw chicken
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- breadcrumb

## Directions

1. After cleaning the raw chicken well, season with salt and pepper
2. Melt a piece of butter and coat all sides
3. Add considerable breadcrumb
4. Three quarters of an hour before eating, place on the grill to roast
5. Serve with some vegetable stew
