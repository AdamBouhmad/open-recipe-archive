## Ingredients

- Lamb tongue (cooked as described above)
- onions
- butter
- flour
- broth or white wine
- parsley
- scallions
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut cooked tongues into slices
2. Slice two or three onions and sauté in butter with a handful of flour
3. Soak in broth or white wine
4. Add finely chopped parsley and scallions, salt, pepper and lemon juice
5. Place on the grill until done
