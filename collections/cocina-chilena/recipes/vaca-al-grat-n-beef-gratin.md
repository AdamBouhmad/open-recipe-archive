## Ingredients

- bacon or broth or lard with water
- mushrooms
- parsley
- small onions or minced garlic
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- broth
- white wine or sherry or rum

## Directions

1. In a dish that can be placed on the fire, put pieces of bacon, or lacking that, broth or lard with a little water
2. Add mushrooms, parsley, small onions or minced garlic
3. Sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs, salt, pepper and spices
4. Place in that base the slices of cooked beef, covering them the same on top as below
5. Sprinkle with a little broth and heat over low heat
6. A little before serving, place coals above and below to gratin it
7. A little white wine or a small glass of sherry or rum can be added
