## Ingredients

- codfish
- cooked potato, peeled

## Directions

1. Soak codfish from the day before
2. Cook and shred the codfish well
3. Take an equal quantity of cooked peeled potato
4. Mix with the codfish and grind together until a smooth paste forms
5. Add sugar to taste
6. Fry in lard
