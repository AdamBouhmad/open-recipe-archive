## Ingredients

- 4 ounces whole roasted coffee
- 2 glasses boiling milk
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
- 1 plate whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take 4 ounces of whole roasted coffee and pour 2 glasses of boiling milk over it
2. Cover the infusion and when cold, pass through a napkin
3. Add 1/2 pound sugar and 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
4. Pass a second time through a napkin
5. Let thicken and add 1 plate of whipped cream
