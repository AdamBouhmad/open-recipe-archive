## Ingredients

- two pounds flour
- a pound and a half butter
- half ounce salt
- four beaten eggs
- two glasses of water

## Directions

1. Make a dough with two pounds flour, pound and a half butter, half ounce salt, four beaten eggs and two glasses of water
2. Mix it gradually without kneading
3. Stretch with the roller or kneader, like puff pastry
