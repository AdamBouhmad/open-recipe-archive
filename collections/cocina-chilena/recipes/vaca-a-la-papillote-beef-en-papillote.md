## Ingredients

- cooked beef slices
- lard
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Between two wide and thick slices of cooked beef, spread lard mixed with seasonings
2. Wrap the two surfaces of the meat with a sheet of buttered or oiled paper and place on the grill over low heat
