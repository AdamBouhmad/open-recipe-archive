## Ingredients

- Flour
- Fresh eggs
- A little salt
- Mixed meats such as fish, poultry, veal
- Cheese
- Butter
- Broth

## Directions

1. Make a dough with flour, fresh eggs and a little salt, thin it until it is the thickness of paper
2. Have ready a filling made of whatever meats are at hand: fish, poultry, veal, etc.
3. Form small mounds at equal distances on half of the dough
4. Moisten and cover the filling with the other half, pressing with your fingers the space between each mound to isolate them and seal the dough
5. Cut this into regular-sized quarters and place them on the lid of the pot, then drop into broth to cook for five minutes
6. Serve these ravioli accompanied by a plate of grated cheese
7. Alternatively, place a layer of ravioli in the tureen, over these another layer of grated cheese and melted butter and continue alternating until the tureen is full, pouring over it the broth in which they were cooked
