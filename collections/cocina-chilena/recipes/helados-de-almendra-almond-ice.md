## Ingredients

- Two pounds of almonds for a regular mold
- Water or milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Toast the almonds, rub with a cloth, and grind with water or milk
2. Strain, adding sufficient milk before straining
3. If it becomes too thick, increase the water or milk in which the sugar is dissolved
4. Calculate the amount needed for the mold and season with cinnamon
5. These ices can also be made and are very good without toasting the almonds
