## Ingredients

- codfish
- potatoes
- carrots
- onion

## Directions

1. Soak codfish from one day to the next
2. Cook and cool it
3. Separately cook peeled potatoes and carrots
4. Chop onion as for salad and blanch it
5. When everything is cooked, shred the codfish into small pieces
6. Slice the carrot thinly
7. Slice the cooked potato similarly
8. Squeeze the onion and add everything together
