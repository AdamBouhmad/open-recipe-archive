## Ingredients

- trout
- water
- salt
- parsley sprigs
- lemon juice
- pepper
- oil
- spices

## Directions

1. Cook the trout, after scaling it, in salted water with some parsley sprigs
2. When cooked, drizzle a little lemon juice over it and dust with pepper
3. Alternatively, cook the trout in water and salt, oil and all spices; when half-cooked, remove that broth and add new broth to finish cooking
4. Make a sauce of egg yolks and lemon juice and serve
