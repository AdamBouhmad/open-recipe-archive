## Ingredients

- rabbit
- white wine
- lemon juice
- thyme
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces and place in a marinade of white wine.
2. Add lemon juice, thyme, bay leaf, salt and pepper.
3. After two hours, drain and coat with flour.
4. Should be served with an appropriate sauce.
