## Ingredients

- lentils
- water
- salt
- abundant butter
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in water and salt
2. Drain them
3. Put them in a saucepan with abundant butter, chopped parsley, salt and a little pepper
4. Stir for a few minutes
5. Serve with fried bread crusts, all very hot
