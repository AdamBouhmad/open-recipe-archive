## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt
- batter for coating
- parsley sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a cabbage for about a quarter of an hour
2. Place it afterwards in cold water
3. Remove the leaves one by one and bread them
4. Then replace as if it were whole, fastening it with a cord or thread
5. Cook it, and at serving time squeeze it, placing it on a plate, and on top pour a parsley sauce
