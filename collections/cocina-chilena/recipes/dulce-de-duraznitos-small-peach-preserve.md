## Ingredients

- Small peaches
- Ash from thorn wood
- Water
- Sugar, five libras per one hundred peaches

## Directions

1. When a mild lye made from ash of thorn wood is boiling, place the small peaches in a basket and submerge in this lye for a short while.
2. Rinse in two tepid waters and cook in boiling water; remove and place in cold water to drain.
3. Place in warm syrup of half point.
4. Use five libras of sugar per one hundred peaches.
