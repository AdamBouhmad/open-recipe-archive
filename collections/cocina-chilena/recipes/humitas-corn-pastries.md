## Ingredients

- fresh corn kernels
- fat
- sweet or hot pepper

## Directions

1. Cut some fresh corn kernels and grind them. Fry in a little hot fat and add sweet or hot pepper as desired.
2. Arrange some corn husks, place a tablespoon of fried corn mixture in them, and cook in water or brown in the oven.
