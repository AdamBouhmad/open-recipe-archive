## Ingredients

- 1 pound lemon juice
- 1 pound 12 ounces ground sugar

## Directions

1. Place in a bain-marie until the sugar dissolves and the syrup becomes crystal clear.
2. Remove, let it cool and bottle it.
3. Be careful that it does not boil.
