## Ingredients

- squabs
- four ounces butter
- seven to eight egg yolks
- four ounces almonds
- one glass milk
- sugar
- nutmeg
- salt
- ten ounces bread crumb
- egg whites

## Directions

1. After cleaning the squabs, roast them in the oven, dry them and arrange them in a dish, whole or split in half
2. Beat four ounces of butter until it looks like foam
3. Add seven to eight egg yolks, four ounces of almonds, one glass of milk, a little sugar, nutmeg, salt and about ten ounces of bread crumb
4. At the end, fold in the beaten egg whites until it becomes soft
5. Pour it over the squabs and put them in the oven
6. The squabs should be so well covered that it looks like a pastry
