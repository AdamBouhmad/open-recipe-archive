## Ingredients

- Duck
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- Cinnamon
- Broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning, leave in open air for twenty-four hours
2. Cook in a pot
3. When half-cooked, drain and place on a roasting spit coated with butter and dusted with salt and pepper
4. Insert cloves into the breast and legs
5. Wrap well in straw paper, tie securely, and place over low heat
6. When well roasted, make a sauce with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, pepper, and broth, and pour over the top
