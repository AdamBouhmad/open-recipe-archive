## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- broth
- vinegar
- pickles
- parsley
- small onion
- chervil
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Roast the tongue and then cut into thin wheels
2. In a plate or platter where it is to be served, place a little broth, a dash of vinegar, pickles, parsley, a small onion and chervil, all finely chopped and seasoned with salt, pepper and a little breadcrumbs
3. Layer the wheels on top, and scatter the same condiments on each layer, covering everything with breadcrumbs
4. Place the plate in an oven at low heat and let it cook until it browns on the bottom
5. Serve pouring a little broth over it
