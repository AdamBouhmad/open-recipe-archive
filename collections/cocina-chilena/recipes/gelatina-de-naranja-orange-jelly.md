## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- One ounce of isinglass

## Directions

1. Squeeze some oranges and season the juice with sugar, cinnamon, and cloves
2. Bring to a boil
3. Add one ounce of dissolved isinglass, strain, and let cool in molds
