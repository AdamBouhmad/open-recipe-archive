## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. Take one pound of butter and one pound of flour and four egg yolks. Take half of the flour and form a small mound, place the yolks in the middle; a little water and a small piece of butter that is not salted, join all this together and make a very soft dough.
2. Beat it well with the hand until it becomes very smooth. Add a little flour, knead well and roll it out, fold in three times.
3. Then divide the butter into three parts, spread the dough and add the butter in small pieces, sprinkle with flour, fold, and roll out with the rolling pin. Repeat this three times. Use a very hot oven.
