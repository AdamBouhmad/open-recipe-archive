## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 4 ounces finely grated chocolate
- a little vanilla powder

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and powdered sugar as for meringues
2. Add 4 ounces of very finely grated chocolate and a little vanilla powder
3. With a spoon, place portions of this dough on buttered baking tins
4. Bake in a gentle oven
