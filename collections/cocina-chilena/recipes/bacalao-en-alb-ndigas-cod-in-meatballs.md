## Ingredients

- cod
- bread
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- eggs
- fish broth
- oil
- saffron

## Directions

1. Chop the cod after cooking and mix with breadcrumbs, salt, pepper and parsley, all ground and beaten eggs in proportion so it takes a regular consistency
2. After well mixed, make balls, wrap in breadcrumb and fry
3. Can afterwards be stewed with fish broth and oil, a piece of bread mixed with parsley, pepper and a bit of saffron, scattered over the meatballs
