## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar, one libra four onzas per libra of quince
- Egg white for clarification

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces, cook them and pass through a sieve.
2. Make syrup and clarify with an egg white, strain and place on the fire until it reaches sufficient point.
3. Add the quince, stir and place on the fire again, which should be brisk, so that the preserve does not turn black.
4. You will know it is good when, placing a little on a plate, it does not run.
