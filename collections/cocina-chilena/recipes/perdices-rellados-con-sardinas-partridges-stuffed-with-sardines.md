## Ingredients

- partridges
- sardines
- lard or bacon
- tomato
- lemon juice
- pepper
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place two clean sardines without heads inside the body of each partridge
2. Roast them with lard or bacon, and if there is tomato, add it on top, or lemon juice, a pinch of pepper, salt and parsley
3. Roast them between two fires and when serving, remove the sardines
