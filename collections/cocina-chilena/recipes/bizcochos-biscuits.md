## Ingredients

- Twelve egg yolks
- Six egg whites
- Half a pound of sugar
- Half a pound of beaten butter
- Half a cup of milk
- Flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks, whites, and sugar well
2. Add the beaten butter and milk
3. Mix all together with sufficient flour
4. Knead well
5. Cut into squares and bake
