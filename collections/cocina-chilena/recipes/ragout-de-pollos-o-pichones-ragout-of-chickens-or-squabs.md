## Ingredients

- Chicken or squab
- Balls made for German vol-au-vent
- Caper sauce
- Puff pastry or other delicate dough

## Directions

1. Cook the bird and cut it into pieces
2. Place in a serving dish with balls made for German vol-au-vent
3. Add a small caper sauce
4. Cover with puff pastry or other delicate dough
5. Place in the oven
