## Ingredients

- one pound melted butter
- ten egg yolks
- one pound powdered sugar
- seven ounces Corinth raisins
- ten egg whites
- one pound flour
- one small cup cognac

## Directions

1. Beat one pound melted butter with ten egg yolks
2. Add one pound powdered sugar
3. Add seven ounces Corinth raisins
4. Add ten egg whites beaten as for meringues
5. Add one pound flour and one small cup cognac
6. Combine all and pour into a buttered mold
7. Place in an oven heated as for bread
