## Ingredients

- One dozen eggs
- Nine ounces of ground sugar
- One pound of flour
- One and a half pounds of sugar for syrup
- One large glass of sweet wine
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with nine ounces of ground sugar
2. Add one pound of flour and fill large boxes made of paper
3. Bake in a mild oven
4. Make a syrup from one and a half pounds of sugar
5. When ready, add a large glass of sweet wine and bring to caramel point
6. When the biscuits are removed, let them cool a little, cut into pieces and with two forks soak in the wine syrup until well soaked
7. Sprinkle a little ground cinnamon and air dry in wicker baskets
