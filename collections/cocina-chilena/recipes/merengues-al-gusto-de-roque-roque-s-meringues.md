## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 4 ounces powdered sugar
- 4 ounces sweet almonds made into paste

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg whites with 4 ounces powdered sugar, evaporating everything over hot ash and stirring constantly
2. Add 4 ounces of sweet almonds made into paste
3. Once the mixture is done, form a round or oval meringue the size of a spoon, being careful to leave a hollow in the middle of each one
4. Dust with very fine sugar and place in the oven
5. When they are rising, remove them and place inside whipped cream or preserves to cover with the other half
