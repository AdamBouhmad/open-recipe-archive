## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Raw meat
- Bread
- Nutmeg
- Eggs
- Salt
- Milk
- Sausages
- Brains
- Broth
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken, cut into pieces, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in butter
2. Mince some raw meat, grate bread and a little nutmeg, add two eggs, salt, and a little milk
3. Make a dough from this mixture, form small balls, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in butter
4. Also fry some sausages and brains
5. Arrange everything in a serving dish
6. If desired, place fried meat slices underneath
7. Make a caper sauce with the chicken broth and pour over the top
