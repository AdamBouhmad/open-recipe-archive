## Ingredients

- egg white
- one tablespoon of flour per egg white
- one tablespoon of powdered sugar per egg white
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. For each egg white put one tablespoon of flour and one tablespoon of powdered sugar
2. Mix these ingredients well
3. When the butter for frying is hot, put one tablespoon of this mixture in a funnel that has three tubes the thickness of a grain of wheat, and let it fall into the butter, turning the funnel to form circles the size of a tea cup opening
4. When golden, remove with a skimmer and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
