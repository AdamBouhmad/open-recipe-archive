## Ingredients

- Fresh legumes of choice
- Stock
- Small slices of carrot and onion
- A little butter

## Directions

1. Cook any of these fresh legumes in stock, adding small slices of carrot and onion with a little butter.
2. When cooked, crush in a mortar and pass the juice through a sieve.
3. Add more stock if it is too thick.
4. Pour this juice over a few small pieces of fried bread and when well arranged, serve.
5. To preserve the green color of the legume, the juice of crushed spinach may be added.
6. On fasting days, use fish stock and oil instead of butter.
