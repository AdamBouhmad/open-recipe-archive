## Ingredients

- corn
- butter
- beaten egg
- flour
- sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- cow meat
- olives
- chicken
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the corn for two days
2. Then grind it on stone, sieve it in a coarse sieve to remove the husk
3. Prepare a little butter and fry the corn until half cooked
4. Then add beaten egg, a little flour, sugar, cinnamon and raisins
5. Prepare the hash as before, assemble the cake and put it to fire in the same manner
