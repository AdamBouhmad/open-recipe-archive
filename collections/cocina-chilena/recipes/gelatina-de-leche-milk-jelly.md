## Ingredients

- Milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- One ounce of isinglass
- A little milk

## Directions

1. Season the milk with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon peel and bring to a boil
2. Dissolve one ounce of isinglass in a little of this milk
3. Combine everything and pour into a mold
