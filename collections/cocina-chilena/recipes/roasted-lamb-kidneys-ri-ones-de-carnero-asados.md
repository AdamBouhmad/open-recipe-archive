## Ingredients

- Lamb kidneys
- Lard
- Butter
- Fine herbs
- Anchovy butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and split the kidneys without separating them completely
2. Place them on a small skewer to keep them open
3. Put a little lard at the bottom of a casserole with the kidneys inside
4. Place on a strong fire
5. When nearly done, place inside a small meatball of butter dissolved with fine herbs or anchovy butter and a little lemon juice
