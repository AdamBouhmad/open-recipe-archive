## Ingredients

- eight to ten egg yolks
- oil
- two tablespoons of oil
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat eight to ten egg yolks for a regular serving dish
2. Add a little oil and continue beating
3. When combined, add two tablespoons of oil
4. Beat well for about three hours, adding two tablespoons of oil from time to time until you calculate about half a bottle of regular size
5. Once the beating is done and by taste you can see that the oil is cooked, add vinegar to taste
6. Stir well and add the necessary salt
7. Arrange a dish with lettuce leaves, tender radishes and pieces of celery opened in water
8. In the middle place preserved shellfish or some cold roasted poultry
9. Pour the beaten mixture on top of everything and cover with lettuce hearts, radishes and celery
