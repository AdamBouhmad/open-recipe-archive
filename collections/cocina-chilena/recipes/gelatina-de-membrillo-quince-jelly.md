## Ingredients

- Twelve quinces
- More than one jar of water
- Four pounds four ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from twelve quinces with a cloth and cook them
2. Place in cold water, remove from the water, and break down dry with the pulp
3. Add more than one jar of water to this, stir, and pour into a straining bag
4. Take four pounds four ounces of sugar and make a syrup, strain it, and in the same strainer strain the distilled broth
5. After both are strained they should be almost the same amount
6. Test the point and when it does not run on a plate it is ready
7. Pour into a mold to cool
