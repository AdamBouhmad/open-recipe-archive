## Ingredients

- vinegar
- water
- salt
- cumin
- oregano

## Directions

1. Use a regular amount of vinegar, if it is very strong add a little water
2. Season with salt, cumin and a little oregano
3. Leave the guachalomos in this seasoning for four hours
4. Remove and sprinkle oregano on them and place in a regular press
5. Leave there as long as desired
