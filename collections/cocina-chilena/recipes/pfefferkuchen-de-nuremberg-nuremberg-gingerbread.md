## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- one pound powdered sugar
- eight egg whites
- one pound four ounces peeled almonds, sliced very thin
- twelve ounces flour
- four ounces candied lemon, chopped
- cinnamon
- clove
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks with one pound powdered sugar for half an hour
2. Add eight egg whites without beating
3. Add one pound four ounces peeled almonds sliced very thin, twelve ounces flour, four ounces candied lemon chopped, cinnamon, clove, and cilantro
4. Mix all these ingredients well
5. Spread the mixture on the dough, extending it to the thickness of half a finger
6. Cut into long pieces and place on buttered baking pans in the oven
