## Ingredients

- bread (beer bread)
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- four eggs
- a little flour
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut beer bread into slices and soak overnight in milk seasoned with sugar and cinnamon
2. When ready to fry, make a batter with four eggs and a little flour
3. Pass the bread slices through the batter
4. Fry in butter or lard
5. Top with sugar or honey
