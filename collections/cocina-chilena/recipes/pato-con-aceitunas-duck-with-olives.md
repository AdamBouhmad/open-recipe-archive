## Ingredients

- Duck, well prepared and arranged
- Bacon pieces
- Half pound olives

## Directions

1. Turn the duck several times in a casserole with bacon pieces
2. Add half a pound of olives to the casserole where the duck is and leave for a few minutes
3. Serve the duck surrounded by olives
