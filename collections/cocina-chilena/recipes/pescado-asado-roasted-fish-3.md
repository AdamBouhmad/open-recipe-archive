## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- tres huevos duros (three hard-boiled eggs)
- perejil (parsley)
- orégano (oregano)
- pimienta (pepper)
- mantequilla (butter)
- miga de pan (bread crumbs)

## Directions

1. Fill the fish with finely chopped hard-boiled eggs, parsley, oregano, and pepper.
2. Place it in a buttered pan lined with bread crumbs.
3. Coat the fish with butter on all sides.
4. Roll it in bread crumbs.
5. Put it in the oven.
