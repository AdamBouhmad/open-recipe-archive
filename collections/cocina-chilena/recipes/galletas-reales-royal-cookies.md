## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter, desalted the day before by pouring boiling water over it
- six egg yolks
- half pound of sugar
- flour as needed
- egg white
- sugar

## Directions

1. The next day, remove the butter and place in a cup over the mouth of a pot so it melts a little
2. Beat with a spoon until white
3. Add six egg yolks
4. When they are combined with the butter, add half pound of sugar and stir a little
5. Add enough flour to make a very soft dough, place flour on the table and mix the dough well
6. Form a large ball and take small pieces, shaping them into an S directly on a buttered baking sheet
7. On top, brush with egg white and a little sugar using a feather
