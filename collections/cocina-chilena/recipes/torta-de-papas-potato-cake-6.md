## Ingredients

- 14 eggs (7 whole and 7 yolks)
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 12 ounces grated potatoes
- lemon rind
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat 14 eggs (7 whole and 7 yolks) with 1/2 pound sugar and 12 ounces grated potatoes for one hour
2. Potatoes should be cooked the day before without skin and left without water
3. The next day pass through a grater and weigh
4. Add lemon rind and lemon juice
5. When about to pour into the mold to bake it, taking care that it is not too strong, grease the mold with butter and sprinkle with flour
