## Ingredients

- one pound butter
- one pound flour
- one pound sugar
- half pound raisins
- thirteen eggs, whites well beaten and yolks half beaten
- half pound almonds in thin slices
- zest of one lemon
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Butter a mold
2. Pour all ingredients, well mixed, into the mold
3. Bake in the oven
