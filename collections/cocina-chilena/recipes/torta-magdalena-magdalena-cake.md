## Ingredients

- two ounces fresh butter
- four ounces flour
- five ounces sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- one tablespoon orange blossom water
- three egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Place two ounces of fresh butter on the heat in a plate
2. When melted, add four ounces flour, five ounces sugar, and lemon zest
3. Add one tablespoon of orange blossom water and three egg yolks
4. Beat the egg whites perfectly and mix everything with a spoon
5. Bake in a mold in a camp oven with moderate heat for one hour
