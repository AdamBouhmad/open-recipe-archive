## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- lard
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- egg yolks
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut into larger pieces
2. After roasting, pass through lard and moisten with water or broth
3. Add salt, pepper and a bay leaf
4. Make a liaison of egg yolks at the time of serving and add lemon juice
