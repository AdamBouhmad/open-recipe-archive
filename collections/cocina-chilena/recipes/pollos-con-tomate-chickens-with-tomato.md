## Ingredients

- onion
- tomato
- corn
- bay leaves
- oregano
- chicken

## Directions

1. Chop a little onion and fry until colored
2. When it begins to brown, add about one plate of chopped tomato and a little corn cut up, bay leaves, and oregano
3. When everything is fried, add the chicken, either raw or roasted and disjointed
