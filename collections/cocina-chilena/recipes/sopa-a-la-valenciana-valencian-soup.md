## Ingredients

- one plate of rice
- four tablespoons oil
- saffron
- onion
- tomato
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well and put in a serving dish with four tablespoons oil and a little saffron
2. Add onion in slices, tomato and pepper, all fried in oil
3. When the rice grain begins to open, fill the dish with broth and let boil until cooked
