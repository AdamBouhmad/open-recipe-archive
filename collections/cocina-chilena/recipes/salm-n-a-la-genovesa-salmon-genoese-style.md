## Ingredients

- salmon wheel or steak
- red wine
- broth
- equal quantity of wine and broth
- parsley, chopped
- spices
- salt
- nutmeg powder
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook a salmon wheel in equal quantities of red wine and broth
2. Add chopped parsley, spices, salt and nutmeg powder
3. When cooked, remove the salmon
4. To the liquid remaining in the pan, add a piece of butter mixed with flour
5. Reduce the sauce after passing it through a sieve
6. When it reaches the right consistency, add the salmon
