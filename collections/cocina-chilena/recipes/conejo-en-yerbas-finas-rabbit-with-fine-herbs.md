## Ingredients

- rabbit
- lard
- mushrooms
- parsley
- bay leaf
- flour
- wine
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- rabbit liver

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit meat into pieces and sauté in lard with mushrooms, parsley and chopped bay leaf.
2. Add a spoonful of flour, wine and broth in equal parts.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. When serving, add the liver which has also been cooked, and mix with the sauce.
