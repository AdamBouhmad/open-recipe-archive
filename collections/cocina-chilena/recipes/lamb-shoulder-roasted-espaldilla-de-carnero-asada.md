## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- bacon slices
- meat scraps
- carrots
- onion
- herbs bouquet
- broth

## Directions

1. Break the bone on top with a blade
2. Cook on a spit prepared with bacon slices
3. Add meat scraps, carrots, onion and a bouquet of herbs
4. Pour broth over it
5. Serve with the reduced cooking liquid, or over endives or other vegetables with the desired seasoning
