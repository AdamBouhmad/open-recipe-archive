## Ingredients

- Egg white
- Lemon zest and juice
- Ground sugar as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white
2. Add the lemon zest and juice
3. Add ground sugar until forming a thick dough
4. Place on wafers, cut into pieces and dry with gentle heat
