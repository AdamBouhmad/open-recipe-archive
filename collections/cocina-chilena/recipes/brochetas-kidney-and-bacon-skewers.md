## Ingredients

- thin slices of bacon
- raw kidneys
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of bacon and raw kidneys
2. Thread alternating slices of kidney and bacon onto a stick
3. Coat thoroughly with melted butter and roll in breadcrumbs
4. Place on a grill in the oven to roast
