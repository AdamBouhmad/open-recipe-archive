## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- oil
- salt
- lemon juice
- pepper
- or salted salmon soaked twenty-four hours in water
- or salmon on a spit with salt, oil and pepper, served with a sauce of beef butter and capers

## Directions

1. If the salmon is fresh, cook it and drizzle raw oil over it, seasoning with salt, lemon juice and a little pepper
2. If it is salted, soak it twenty-four hours in water, preferably changing the water; then remove it and scrub it well to prepare it as described or with any other dish
3. Alternatively, place the salmon on a spit with salt, oil and pepper, and when it is ready, serve it with a sauce of beef butter and capers
