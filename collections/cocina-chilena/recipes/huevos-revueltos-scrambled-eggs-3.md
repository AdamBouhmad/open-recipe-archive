## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- cream or broth

## Directions

1. Put in the pan, seasoning with salt and pepper
2. Add a spoonful of strained broth, cream or broth
3. Place over gentle heat, stirring until they thicken
4. Remove immediately
