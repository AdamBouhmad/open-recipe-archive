## Ingredients

- beef hindquarters
- bones
- bird and game bones
- bird and game necks
- salt
- roots

## Directions

1. Preferably choose the hindquarters or part of it, remove the bones, roll and tie with string
2. Place in a pot with the bones, pasta and necks of birds and game used for entrees
3. Skim well over high heat
4. Once it has cooked a little, add salt and all sorts of roots
5. After removing the beef, serve it surrounded with parsley sprigs, or chopped in sauce garnished with onions and vegetables, or with small pastries
