## Ingredients

- 1 pound almonds
- Water
- 2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the almonds and grind them like paste with a little water.
2. After grinding well, add a little water, enough to extract all the juice from the almonds, which should result in a thick milk.
3. Strain it, make a syrup of 2 pounds of sugar, and when it reaches the thread stage, add the milk and stir well over the fire until it reaches point.
4. Let it cool and bottle it.
