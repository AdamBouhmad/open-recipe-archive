## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks for each 1 egg white
- butter
- clear syrup
- peeled almonds
- candied cherries

## Directions

1. For every 12 yolks, add 1 egg white and beat well
2. Butter the mold so it does not stick
3. Pour the batter into the mold and place it to cook in a saucepan with water
4. Cover the mold with a metal sheet with fire on top
5. Be very careful when removing it so that air does not hit it and cause it to fall
6. Make a clear syrup and pour the chimbos cut into pieces into it and let them soak for two days
7. Top with peeled almonds cut lengthwise and some candied cherries
