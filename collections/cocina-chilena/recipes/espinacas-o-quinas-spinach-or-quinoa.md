## Ingredients

- Spinach or quinoa
- Salt
- Water or milk
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook with a little salt
2. Drain and chop well
3. Cook in a little water or milk with breadcrumbs and a little butter
4. Add the vegetables and let them boil for a moment
