## Ingredients

- partridges
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- raw oil
- bay leaves
- lemon or orange slices
- broth
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Well cleaned and buttered, broil them on the grill
2. Then put them in a pot with parsley, pepper, salt, raw oil, bay leaves, some slices of lemon or orange and broth that covers them
3. When they are cooked, set them aside, and for each bird beat an egg yolk and add to the sauce
