## Ingredients

- Half pound of butter
- Lemon zest
- Nutmeg
- Cinnamon
- One pound of sugar
- Two egg yolks and one white
- Two pounds four ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Beat half pound of butter and add lemon zest, nutmeg, cinnamon, and one pound of sugar
2. Add two egg yolks and one white, stir everything and beat well with two pounds four ounces of flour
3. When well combined, remove from the bowl, place on a board, roll out, and cut into rounds somewhat thick
4. Place on pans and in a very mild oven
