## Ingredients

- Lamb meat
- croutons
- fried eggs
- tomato sauce or other sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very finely
2. Place on a plate
3. Serve with fried croutons around and fried eggs on top of a tomato sauce or other sauce
