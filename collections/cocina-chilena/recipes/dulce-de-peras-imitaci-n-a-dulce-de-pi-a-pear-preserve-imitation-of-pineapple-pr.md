## Ingredients

- Pears
- Lemon juice
- Sugar, equal weight to the pears

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, cut into thin slices and soak in lemon juice, which should be ready in a plate.
2. Place in a saucepan one layer of pears and another layer of sugar.
3. Let rest for half a day, or a full day if the pear is hard.
4. Place on the fire, taking care to stir so it does not stick; give a light boil.
5. Repeat this same process three days in a row, and on the third day bring to point.
