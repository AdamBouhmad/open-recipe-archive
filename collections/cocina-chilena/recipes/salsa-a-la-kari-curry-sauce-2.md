## Ingredients

- butter
- pepper, chopped
- flour
- saffron
- broth
- nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. In a saucepan put a good piece of butter, some pieces of chopped pepper, flour and if desired a little saffron
2. Add a little broth and make it boil
3. Add grated nutmeg and serve very hot
