## Ingredients

- pork meat
- eight tablespoons wine
- four tablespoons vinegar
- ground garlic
- cumin
- onions
- carrots
- two hot peppers

## Directions

1. Cut the pork meat and put to fry
2. Add eight tablespoons of wine and four of vinegar, ground garlic, cumin, onions in pieces, carrots in slices, two hot peppers
3. Cover everything in a pot, taking care that steam does not escape, and let it boil gently for one hour
