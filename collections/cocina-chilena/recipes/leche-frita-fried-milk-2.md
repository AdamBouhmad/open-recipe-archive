## Ingredients

- two bottles of milk
- four ounces of sifted rice flour
- sugar to taste
- three ounces of good grated cheese
- five egg yolks
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- lemon zest
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bring everything to a boil until it reaches the consistency of mazamorra, stirring constantly so the rice does not settle
2. Remove from heat and add three ounces of good grated cheese
3. Add five beaten egg yolks, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon zest, stirring for some time until all is incorporated
4. Spread on a platter to cool
5. Cut into pieces that are dipped in well-beaten egg yolks and rolled in breadcrumbs
6. Fry
