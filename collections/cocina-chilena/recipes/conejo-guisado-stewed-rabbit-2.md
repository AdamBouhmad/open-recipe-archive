## Ingredients

- rabbit
- raw oil
- parsley
- pepper
- hot water
- bay leaf
- clove
- cinnamon powder
- capers or lemon slices

## Directions

1. After skinning and cleaning without wetting it, cut into pieces.
2. Place in a pot or pan with raw oil, parsley and pepper to stew over gentle heat.
3. Add hot water and when half cooked, add bay leaf, clove and cinnamon powder.
4. Very good with capers or lemon slices.
