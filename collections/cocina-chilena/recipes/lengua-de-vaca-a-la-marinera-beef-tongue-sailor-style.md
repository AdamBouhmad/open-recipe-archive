## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- broth
- two glasses of red or white wine
- small onions
- butter
- flour
- fried bread

## Directions

1. After cooking the tongue, remove the skin
2. Cut into wheels and strain the broth in which it was cooked, skim off fat and put everything in a casserole with two glasses of red or white wine, small onions fried in butter with a little flour
3. When the sauce is reduced to its point, place on pieces of fried bread, pouring the sauce on top
