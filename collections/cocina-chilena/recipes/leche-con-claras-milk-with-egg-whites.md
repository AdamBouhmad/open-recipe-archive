## Ingredients

- three cups of milk
- cinnamon
- vanilla
- lemon peel
- sugar
- eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook three cups of milk with cinnamon, vanilla, lemon peel and sugar as for manjar blanco
2. Let boil for a good while until the milk begins to thicken
3. Remove, let cool and strain
4. Add eight well-beaten egg whites, stir and place on the fire until it begins to clear
5. Remove and strain
6. Let cool and serve
