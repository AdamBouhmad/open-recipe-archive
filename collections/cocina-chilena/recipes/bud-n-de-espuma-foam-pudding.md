## Ingredients

- Four ounces of butter
- Half a bottle of milk
- Eight ounces of flour
- Ten egg yolks, beaten
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Lemon zest
- Egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Boil all ingredients together, stirring always in the same direction, until it detaches from the pan
2. Let cool for a moment
3. Add ten beaten egg yolks, cinnamon, sugar, and lemon zest, all to taste
4. At the time of pouring into the pudding mold, add the beaten egg whites
5. Place in the oven
