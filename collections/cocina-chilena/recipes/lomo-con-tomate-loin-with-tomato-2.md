## Ingredients

- loin
- fat from cooking broth
- soaked bread
- red tomatoes, ground
- cooking broth

## Directions

1. Cook the loin well
2. With a spoon, remove all the fat from the top of the broth in which it has been cooked
3. Put this fat in a saucepan and add soaked bread and some ground red tomatoes mixed with the bread
4. Gradually add the broth little by little
5. Place the loin in this sauce and let it rest
