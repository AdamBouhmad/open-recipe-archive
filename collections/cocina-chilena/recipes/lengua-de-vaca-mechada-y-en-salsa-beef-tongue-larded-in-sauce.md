## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- bacon strips
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- brown sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Soak in cold water for twenty-four hours, changing the water several times
2. Scald in boiling water, scraping to remove the skin
3. Lard with bacon strips, seasoning with pepper, salt and chopped parsley
4. Let it cook slowly for about four or five hours
5. Remove from heat, open it down the middle without dividing it and place on the plate
6. Remove the fat from the cooking liquid and soak in a brown sauce or tomato sauce
