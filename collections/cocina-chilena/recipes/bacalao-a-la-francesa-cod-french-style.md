## Ingredients

- cod
- onion
- parsley
- lemon
- pepper
- oil
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. After the cod is cooked, take the serving dish, which should be somewhat deep
2. Put in it a bit of onion, parsley, lemon slices without skin or seed
3. Peppercorns, two spoonfuls of oil and a piece of butter, if beef better than pork, the size of an egg
4. Place the cod on top and cover with a layer of the same mixture, sprinkle with breadcrumbs
5. Then place the dish over low heat so it boils little by little
6. Add a bit of lemon juice and pepper on top
