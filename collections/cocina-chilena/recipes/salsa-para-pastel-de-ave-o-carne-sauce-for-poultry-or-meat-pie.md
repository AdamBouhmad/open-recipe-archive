## Ingredients

- chicken
- aromatics and celery
- water
- butter
- onion
- flour
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Fry the chicken with aromatics and celery
2. Let it boil with a little water
3. Fry a little chopped onion in butter
4. Add one tablespoon of flour and let it fry
5. Gradually add a little chicken broth to the sauce and let it boil until it thickens a little
6. Pour it into the pastry dish and when it is fried put the dough on top
