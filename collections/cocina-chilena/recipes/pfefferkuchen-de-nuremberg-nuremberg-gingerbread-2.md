## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 8 egg whites
- 1 pound 4 ounces peeled almonds cut into very fine slices
- 12 ounces flour
- 4 ounces candied lemon, chopped
- cinnamon
- clove
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 1 pound powdered sugar for half an hour
2. Then add 8 unbeaten egg whites, 1 pound 4 ounces of peeled almonds cut into very fine slices, 12 ounces of flour, 4 ounces of candied lemon and chopped, cinnamon, clove, and cilantro
3. Mix all these ingredients well, place on the dough
4. Extend the dough until 1/2 finger thick
5. Cut into long pieces and place in the oven on buttered baking tins
