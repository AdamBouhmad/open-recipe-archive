## Ingredients

- Leg of mutton
- Linen cloth
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Spices
- Pepper
- Water
- Capers

## Directions

1. Wrap the leg of mutton in linen cloth with bay leaves, thyme, spices and pepper
2. Place it in boiling water and cook for as many hours as pounds the leg weighs
3. Remove and unwrap it
4. Serve with a caper sauce
