## Ingredients

- Two calf's feet
- Two pounds of sugar
- Juice of five or six lemons
- Ten eggs with shells
- Half a glass of sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's feet well until the meat separates from the bone
2. Strain and when cold, remove the fat by passing a cloth through it
3. Bring to a boil with two pounds of sugar, lemon juice, and ten eggs (with shells) broken into pieces
4. Leave everything quiet on the fire until the egg is well cooked
5. Add half a glass of sherry wine and remove from heat
6. Pour through a flannel cloth strainer over a mold that must be very dry
7. If it does not come out clear, pour back through the flannel
8. If it cools and cannot finish straining, put everything back on the fire to clarify and pour into the flannel again
