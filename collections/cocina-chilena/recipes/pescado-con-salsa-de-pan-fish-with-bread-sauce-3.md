## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- cebollas (onions)
- sal (salt)
- perejil (parsley)
- mantequilla (butter)
- miga de pan (bread crumbs)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and cook it with onions and salt.
2. Add parsley and butter.
3. Thicken with bread crumbs.
