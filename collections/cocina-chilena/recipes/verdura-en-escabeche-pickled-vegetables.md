## Ingredients

- onions
- asparagus
- peppers
- vinegar
- salt
- whole pepper

## Directions

1. Peel medium-sized onions and prick them all over with a fork or brush
2. Take asparagus, cut off the stems and prick them together with peppers cut in half to remove the seeds
3. Wash everything in vinegar and arrange in jars in layers with salt and whole pepper
4. Add vinegar, and the next day refill and cover well with bladder
