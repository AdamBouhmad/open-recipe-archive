## Ingredients

- A dozen eggs
- Clean bladder

## Directions

1. Break a dozen eggs and separate the yolks from the whites
2. Take a clean bladder and fill it with well-beaten yolks
3. Tie it very well so air does not penetrate and suspend it in boiling water until the yolks take on consistency
4. Remove it, cool it and carefully break it open
5. Take another larger bladder and put in the whites, placing the already curdled yolks in the middle
6. Cook again and when you think it convenient, remove it, and breaking the bladder the egg will be done, which can be served with a poultry sauce or in rounds
