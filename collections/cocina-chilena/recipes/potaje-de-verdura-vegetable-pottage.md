## Ingredients

- Two ounces of rice
- Two ounces of peeled chickpeas
- Chard
- Carrots, cooked and finely chopped
- One spoonful of coloring
- A little stock
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook two ounces of rice in water.
2. Cook two ounces of peeled chickpeas and cooked chard and carrots.
3. Finely chop the chard and carrots.
4. Mix everything together in a dish with one spoonful of coloring, a little stock, salt and pepper.
5. Place over low heat to keep warm.
