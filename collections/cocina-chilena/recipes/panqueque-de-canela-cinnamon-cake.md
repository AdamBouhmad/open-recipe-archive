## Ingredients

- one pound butter
- one pound sugar
- twelve eggs
- a small piece of vanilla
- cinnamon
- one pound flour

## Directions

1. Beat one pound butter with one pound sugar until foamy
2. Add twelve beaten eggs
3. Add a small piece of vanilla, cinnamon, and one pound flour
4. Combine all and pour into the mold
5. Bake in an oven as for the previous cakes
