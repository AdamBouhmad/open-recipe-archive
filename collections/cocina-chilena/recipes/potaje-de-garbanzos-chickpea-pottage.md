## Ingredients

- Two onions, cooked
- Oil
- Beaten eggs or bread soaked and dissolved in water
- A little clove and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook two onions well.
2. Stew with oil.
3. Add beaten eggs or bread soaked and dissolved in water.
4. Add a little clove and pepper.
5. Serve.
