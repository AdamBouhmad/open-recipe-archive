## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the artichokes
2. Place them in cold water
3. Reheat them at serving time, placing them in boiling water after removing the outer part and interior
4. Place them on a plate, pouring white sauce into the hollow of the heart
5. The sauce can also be served separately in a sauce boat
