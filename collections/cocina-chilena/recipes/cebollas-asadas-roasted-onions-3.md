## Ingredients

- Onions
- Oil
- Sugar
- Ground pepper
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Cut both ends off the onions and remove the outer layers
2. Place in a pan with oil, a little sugar, a grain of ground pepper, and a little cinnamon powder
3. Roast on low heat
4. Cover the pan with an iron lid and place some coals on top until they are well browned
