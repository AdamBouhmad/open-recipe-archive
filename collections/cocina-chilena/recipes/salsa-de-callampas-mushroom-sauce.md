## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash them well
2. Fry them in butter
3. Once they have released most of their water
4. Add bread crumbs, salt and pepper
5. Serve with some meat dish or as previously mentioned
