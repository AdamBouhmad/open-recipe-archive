## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- water
- capers
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Dissolve a tablespoon of flour in another of butter
2. Add water to make a regular porridge
3. Add the capers with a little vinegar
4. Serve with the dishes already mentioned
