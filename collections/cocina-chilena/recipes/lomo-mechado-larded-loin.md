## Ingredients

- loin
- bacon strips
- potatoes
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- celery branches
- parsley
- cup of brandy

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with bacon strips and place in a casserole with potatoes, onions, pepper, salt, celery branches, parsley and a cup of brandy
2. Cover and bring to a boil
