## Ingredients

- one teacup of cold bread crumbs
- three finely chopped apples
- four ounces of very finely chopped fat
- sugar to taste
- four eggs, well beaten
- half a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fill one teacup with cold bread crumbs
2. Chop finely three apples and four ounces of fat
3. Add sugar to taste and four well-beaten eggs
4. Add half a nutmeg and mix everything
5. Place in a buttered cup
6. Moisten a cloth with hot water, place the cup in it, wring out, tie, and boil upside down for several hours
