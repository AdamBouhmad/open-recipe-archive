## Ingredients

- Pork lard
- Ham pieces
- Onion, chopped
- Squabs
- Water
- Egg yolk
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry pieces of ham and chopped onion in pork lard
2. Once everything is fried, remove and brown the squabs in the same lard
3. When tender, mix with water and the previously fried ingredients
4. Add an egg yolk and lemon juice
