## Ingredients

- Good piece of butter
- Three egg yolks
- Grated lemon
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Water from the oysters
- Minced mushrooms
- Capers
- Breadcrumbs
- Piece of roasted veal or cold fowl
- Egg whites
- Broth
- Puff pastry shells
- Oysters
- Lemon juice
- Egg yolk
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Beat butter until it resembles foam
2. Mix with three egg yolks, grated lemon, nutmeg and salt, and the water from the oysters
3. Add minced mushrooms, capers, breadcrumbs and a finely minced piece of roasted veal or cold fowl
4. At the last moment add half the beaten egg whites; if too dry add a little broth
5. Fill uncooked puff pastry shells with this mixture
6. Place three or four oysters on top of each one
7. Brush with a little lemon juice, beaten egg yolk and bread crumbs
8. Place in the oven, which should be set as for baking bread
