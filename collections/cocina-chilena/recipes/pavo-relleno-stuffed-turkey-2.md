## Ingredients

- turkey
- lamb, pork or chicken meat
- vinegar
- pepper
- garlic
- cumin
- salt

## Directions

1. Debone the turkey
2. Remove the meat from the breast and legs and chop it, adding a piece of lamb, pork or chicken meat
3. Season this with desired seasonings
4. Stuff the turkey and cook in water seasoned with a little vinegar, pepper, garlic, cumin and salt
