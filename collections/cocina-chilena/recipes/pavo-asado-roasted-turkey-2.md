## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Brine
- Vinegar
- Bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Place turkey in brine three days before roasting
2. On the third day, add a little vinegar
3. Three hours before the meal, soak bread in vinegar and stuff the crop well
4. Place in a hot oven
5. When it begins to brown, remove from oven, coat with butter on all sides so it comes out juicy, and return to oven
