## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- broth
- thyme
- mint
- parsley
- aromatic herbs
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- mushrooms
- flour
- white wine
- onions
- cloves

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with broth and a bouquet of thyme, mint, parsley and other aromatic herbs, salt and pepper
2. Place lard, mushrooms and flour in a pot
3. Brown with broth, white wine, parsley, onions and cloves
4. After cooking, pour the sauce over the ribs and serve
