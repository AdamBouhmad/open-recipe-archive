## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- water
- burnt fat
- potatoes
- rice
- green beans
- peas
- 2 heads of onions
- 1 head of garlic
- egg
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken well, gut it, wash it and cut it into pieces
2. Put it to cook in a regular amount of water
3. When it is boiling, skim well so the broth remains clean and clear
4. Remove the pieces and fry them in burnt fat
5. Add seasonings and diced potatoes, a little rice, green beans and peas if available, 2 heads of onions and 1 head of garlic
6. After this operation, add the broth in which the chicken has boiled and cook over low heat until dinner time
7. Before serving, season with beaten egg and vegetables
