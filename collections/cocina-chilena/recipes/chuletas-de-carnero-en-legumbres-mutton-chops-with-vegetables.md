## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- butter
- parsley
- cloves
- white wine
- ham
- legumes

## Directions

1. Reheat chops in butter with parsley and cloves
2. Add white wine and a small piece of ham
3. After cooking, remove the chops; let the sauce thicken
4. Use the sauce to dress the vegetables that have been prepared beforehand, and arrange the chops in a crown shape on top
