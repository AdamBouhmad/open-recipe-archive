## Ingredients

- three or four glasses good broth
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon mustard

## Directions

1. Reduce three or four glasses of good broth with salt and pepper to half in a saucepan
2. Add one tablespoon of mustard
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Serve very hot
