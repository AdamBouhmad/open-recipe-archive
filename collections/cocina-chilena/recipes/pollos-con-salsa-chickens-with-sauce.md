## Ingredients

- chickens
- two tablespoons butter
- carrot
- chopped onion
- parsley
- vinegar
- flour if needed

## Directions

1. Place chickens in a pot with two tablespoons butter, carrot, chopped onion, and parsley
2. Bring everything to boil with a little vinegar
3. Strain the sauce afterward
4. If the sauce is clear, add a little flour
