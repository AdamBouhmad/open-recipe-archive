## Ingredients

- potatoes
- carrots
- beans
- squash
- peas
- chard
- corn
- shrimp
- sea urchins
- oysters
- lobsters

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes, carrots, beans, squash, peas, chopped chard and sliced corn
2. When all is cooked, drain the water, fry in color and add trimmed corn so it fries as well
3. For shrimp add the broth in which they have already been cooked, also add the bodies so they boil with everything
4. When serving add the tails and if the locro has not become thick enough, mash some potatoes
5. For sea urchins prepare in their own broth and the tongues are not added until serving time
6. For other shellfish prepare the locro in water
7. Note that oysters and lobsters should be soaked from the day before and the water changed frequently
