## Ingredients

- chicken
- egg
- bread crumb
- lard

## Directions

1. Disjoint the raw chicken
2. Beat an egg with breadcrumb
3. Fry in very hot lard
4. Serve with pebre or peas, mushrooms, or chard, in short with any vegetable stew desired
