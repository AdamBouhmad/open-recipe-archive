## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- garlic
- oregano
- potatoes
- curd
- raw shrimp
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put a pot on the fire with a little butter, chopped onion, a little garlic and a little oregano, fry a little
2. Then add raw potatoes cut in half, fry a little more
3. Add a large quantity of water and let the potatoes boil until well cooked
4. Add a little dissolved curd
5. Add raw shrimp to cook with the potatoes
6. When everything is well cooked, add two beaten eggs
7. When these are cooked, add six or eight whole eggs on top of the stew
8. Can be made with fresh silverside fish, oysters or fresh lobsters
