## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- slightly less cow butter
- a little cold water
- sufficient amount of salt

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with slightly less cow butter
2. Add a little cold water and sufficient amount of salt
3. Knead with the roller and ensure it becomes very hard
