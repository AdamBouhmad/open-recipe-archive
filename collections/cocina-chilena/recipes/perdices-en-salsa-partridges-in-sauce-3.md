## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Butter
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- Raw oil
- Bay leaves
- Lemon or orange slices
- Broth
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges well and coat them with butter
2. Sear them on a grill
3. Place them in a pot with parsley, pepper, salt, raw oil, bay leaves, lemon or orange slices, and broth to cover
4. When cooked, remove them
5. For each bird, add one beaten egg yolk
