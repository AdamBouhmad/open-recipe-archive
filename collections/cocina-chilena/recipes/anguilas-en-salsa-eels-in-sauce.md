## Ingredients

- eels
- oil
- paper
- butter
- fine herbs
- breadcrumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place on the grill wrapped in paper coated with oil
2. Must be divided along the back at the time of serving
3. Can be filled with a paste made of butter, fine herbs and breadcrumbs
4. Can also be made into a white sauce stew, served in a sauce boat separately
5. The eels in another plate, so each person can season to their taste
6. Lemon juice is very acceptable for this dish
