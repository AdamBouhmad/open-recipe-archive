## Ingredients

- celery
- salt
- butter
- flour
- puchero broth

## Directions

1. When peeling the celery root, cut it equal in thickness and length
2. Wash with great care and blanch in lightly salted water
3. Refresh and drain well
4. Make a light paste of butter and flour, which dissolve in puchero broth, stirring continuously
5. When it is boiling add the celery without breaking it and cook on high heat for about half an hour
6. Serve covered with sauce
