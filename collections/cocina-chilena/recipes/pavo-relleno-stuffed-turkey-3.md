## Ingredients

- Ground meat and ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two eggs
- Parsley
- Dried apricots or prunes
- Raisins
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of ground meat and ham, salt, pepper, two eggs with parsley, dried apricots or prunes, and raisins
2. Fry with butter
3. Stuff the turkey and then roast
