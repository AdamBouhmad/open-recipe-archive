## Ingredients

- two onions
- water
- one cup oil
- half cup wine
- salt
- pepper
- cooked partridges

## Directions

1. Cut two onions into long, thin slices and soften them with boiling water
2. Squeeze them and fry them in a pan where one cup of oil should already be hot
3. When they are well fried, add half a cup of wine, salt and pepper
4. Add the cooked partridges so they heat through and serve immediately
