## Ingredients

- Four ounces of butter, well beaten
- Grated lemon
- Four egg yolks
- Half of half an ounce of cinnamon
- Four ounces of ground almonds
- Sufficient flour to make a soft dough
- Flour for dusting
- One egg white
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- A little flour

## Directions

1. Beat four ounces of butter well and add grated lemon and four egg yolks
2. Add half of half an ounce of cinnamon, stir well, and add four ounces of ground almonds
3. Add sufficient flour to make a soft dough
4. Follow the same procedure as for the others, cut somewhat thick
5. Place on greased and flour-dusted pans
6. Beat one egg white and add sugar, plenty of cinnamon, and a little flour
7. After removing the cookies from the oven, spread this mixture on top with a spoon and return to the oven to dry
