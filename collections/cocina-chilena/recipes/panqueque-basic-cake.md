## Ingredients

- fifteen egg yolks
- nine egg whites
- one pound powdered sugar
- one pound butter
- one pound starch powder
- lemon zest
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place fifteen egg yolks and nine egg whites in an earthenware bowl
2. Beat all with one pound powdered sugar as for cake
3. Take one pound of butter and beat well with a spoon
4. Mix everything and beat again by hand
5. Add one pound starch powder, beating constantly
6. Add lemon zest and cinnamon
7. Pour into molds
