## Ingredients

- two or three leeks
- butter
- one quarter of a glass vinegar
- gherkins in vinegar
- capers in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut two or three leeks very fine, which will be fried with a little butter
2. Add one quarter of a glass of vinegar, which will be allowed to reduce
3. Let everything cook on low heat for a quarter hour at the corner of the stove
4. Degrease it
5. When serving, add finely cut gherkins and capers in vinegar
