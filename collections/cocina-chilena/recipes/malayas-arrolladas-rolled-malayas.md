## Ingredients

- malayas
- salt
- vinegar
- cumin
- oregano
- garlic
- chili
- water
- string

## Directions

1. Season these in the same seasoning as for guachalomos
2. Remove and air dry
3. Roll by tying with string
4. Cook in seasoned water and serve cold
