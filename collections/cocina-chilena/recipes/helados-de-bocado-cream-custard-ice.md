## Ingredients

- Milk for a two-pound mold
- Eight eggs, well beaten
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Coffee infusion (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the necessary milk for a two-pound mold
2. When cooked, add eight well-beaten eggs and let cook
3. When the eggs are cooked, remove and strain
4. Sweeten and add cinnamon and some lemon zest
5. A little coffee infusion can also be added to the milk
6. Freeze
