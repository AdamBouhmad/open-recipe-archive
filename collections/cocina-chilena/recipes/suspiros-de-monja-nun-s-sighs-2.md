## Ingredients

- 1/4 of a cuartillo water
- sugar the size of 2 walnuts
- butter the size of 2 walnuts
- grated lemon zest
- flour
- eggs
- hot fat
- sugar or honey for serving
- orange blossom water (optional)

## Directions

1. Place in a pot 1/4 of a cuartillo water, sugar the size of 2 walnuts, butter the size of 2 walnuts, and grated lemon zest
2. Bring to a boil for a moment
3. Add handfuls of flour until the paste thickens well
4. Stir constantly until cooked, which is known when touching it with your finger it does not stick
5. Remove from heat and let cool
6. Break an egg and add it, stirring gently
7. When well incorporated, add another egg, and then another, until the dough is softer and the spoon releases easily when removed
8. Take with a spoon a piece of dough the size of a walnut and place it in hot fat
9. Strike a little with a stick so it rises
10. Serve the fritters with sugar or glazed with honey
11. If desired, add orange blossom water when adding the first egg
