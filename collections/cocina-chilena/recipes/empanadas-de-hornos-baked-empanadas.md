## Ingredients

- onion
- ground raw meat
- flour
- water
- flour
- rendered fat
- eggs
- salt
- wine

## Directions

1. Chop onion and fry it until slightly softened.
2. Add finely chopped raw meat without tendons and fry until cooked.
3. Add a tablespoon of flour, mix well, and add a little water so it becomes a sauce.
4. Let cool. Make a dough with a little flour, rendered fat, two eggs, and salted water.
5. Form the empanadas with a little wine inside and put in a very hot oven.
