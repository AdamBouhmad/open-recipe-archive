## Ingredients

- Chard
- Leeks
- Carrots
- Parsnips
- Salt
- Eggs
- Butter
- Milk
- Flour
- Butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook chard, leeks, carrots and parsnips, season with salt
2. Make a beaten mixture of six to seven eggs, fry in butter
3. It should remain thick
4. When golden on one side, empty it onto a platter, then return it to the pan to fry on the other side so it becomes dry
5. Serve with a small milk sauce that is thickened with one tablespoon of flour fried in butter, season with pepper
