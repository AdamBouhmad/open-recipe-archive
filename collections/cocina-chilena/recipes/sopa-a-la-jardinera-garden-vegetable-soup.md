## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Turnips
- Lettuce
- Sorrel
- Chervil
- Lard or oil
- Broth
- Peas
- Asparagus heads
- Bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cut thin strips of carrots and turnips
2. Take lettuce, sorrel and chopped chervil, and sauté everything with lard or oil
3. Then add broth from the stew, throwing in a handful of peas and some asparagus heads
4. Once these vegetables are well cooked, skim and pour over bread crusts
