## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites as for meringues and add them to boiling milk to cook
2. Beat egg yolks well in the same manner and add a little milk and the necessary sugar dissolved in the same milk
3. When the whites are cooked, remove them and add the beaten yolk mixture
4. Boil this gently over low heat, not stirring but moving the milk from side to side
5. It should not be very thick
6. Place in a platter with a little cinnamon on top
