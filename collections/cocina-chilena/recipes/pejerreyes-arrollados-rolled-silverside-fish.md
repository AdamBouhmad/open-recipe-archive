## Ingredients

- Onion
- Hard-boiled egg
- Bread crumbs
- Silverside fish
- Water
- Salt
- Chopped egg
- Minced onion

## Directions

1. Mince onion very finely and hard-boiled egg, add bread crumbs
2. Fry this mixture and use to fill the silverside fish, which should already be prepared, cleaned and boned
3. Arrange in a dish, add salted water and bring to boil
4. Top with chopped egg and finely minced onion
