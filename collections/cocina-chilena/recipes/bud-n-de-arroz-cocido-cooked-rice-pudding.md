## Ingredients

- Three tablespoons of rice
- Milk
- Four egg whites
- Two egg yolks
- Sugar to taste
- Flavorings as desired
- Butter or caramel

## Directions

1. Cook three tablespoons of rice in milk over low heat
2. Add four whites and two yolks of eggs, sugar to taste and desired flavorings
3. Pour into a mold prepared with caramel or butter
4. Place the mold in a pan of boiling water, cover with fire on top, and let boil for one hour
