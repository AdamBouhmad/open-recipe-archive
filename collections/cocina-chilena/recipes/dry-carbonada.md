## Ingredients

- raw beef
- potatoes
- squash
- corn
- green beans
- wine
- vinegar
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Mince raw beef and fry it a little
2. Once fried, add diced potatoes, squash, corn and green beans
3. Calculate equal amounts of meat and vegetables
4. Add 2 spoonfuls of wine, 1 of vinegar and all kinds of seasonings
5. Leave to cook over low heat until dinner time
