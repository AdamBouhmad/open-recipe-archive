## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 4 ounces hot fat
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat 12 eggs with 1/2 pound sugar
2. When well beaten, add necessary flour to make a dough
3. Add 4 ounces hot fat
4. Work the dough, form rings, and coat them
5. Pass through boiling water and remove as they rise
6. Coat again by placing fire underneath and with great quickness place in the oven
7. They are also very good if lard is used instead of fat
8. Cut with a knife on the sides when placing them in the oven
