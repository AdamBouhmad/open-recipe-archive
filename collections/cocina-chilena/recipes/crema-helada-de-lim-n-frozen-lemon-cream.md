## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Lemon zest
- Four glasses of milk
- Half a pound of sugar
- Twelve bitter almonds, ground
- Eight egg yolks
- Two ounces of butter
- Salt and saltpeter

## Directions

1. Scrape the zest of a fresh lemon onto a piece of sugar
2. Boil four glasses of milk and add the sugar with lemon zest and half a pound more sugar
3. Add twelve ground bitter almonds
4. Cover the infusion and when lukewarm, pour over eight beaten egg yolks
5. Mix well and cook over gentle heat, stirring constantly when it begins to thicken
6. As soon as it boils, remove from fire and add two ounces of butter
7. Strain through a colander
8. When cold, place in a freezer set in snow with salt and a little saltpeter
