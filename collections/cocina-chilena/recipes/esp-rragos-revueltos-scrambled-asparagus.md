## Ingredients

- Small asparagus
- Beef tallow
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Sugar
- Flour
- Water or broth
- Egg yolks
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Choose the smallest asparagus and cut into pieces the size of a chickpea
2. Cook as described previously
3. After cooking, remove from boiling water and place in cold water
4. Drain in a strainer to release all water
5. When well dried, place in a pan with beef tallow, salt, whole pepper, and a little sugar
6. Place on high heat, stirring constantly
7. Lightly sprinkle with flour and add a few drops of water or broth
8. A moment later remove from heat and while hot mix with two egg yolks
9. Serve surrounded by hard-boiled eggs in slices or with fried bread crusts
