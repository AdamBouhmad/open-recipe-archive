## Ingredients

- whole onion
- bacon pieces
- two tomatoes
- two tablespoons oil
- two tablespoons vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- half glass brandy
- partridge

## Directions

1. Put plenty of whole onion, pieces of bacon, two tomatoes, two tablespoons of oil and two of vinegar, salt, pepper and half a glass of brandy in a pot
2. Add the partridge and cover well so no steam escapes
3. Do not turn it but shake it frequently
