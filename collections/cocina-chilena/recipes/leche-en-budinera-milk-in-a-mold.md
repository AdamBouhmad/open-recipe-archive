## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- three egg whites
- half a pound of sugar
- cinnamon
- one-third mold of cooked and cold milk
- caramelized sugar

## Directions

1. Add three egg whites at half beat to twenty egg yolks, half a pound of sugar, a little cinnamon, and about one-third mold of cooked and cold milk
2. Have the mold ready with caramelized sugar so it does not stick
3. Place in boiling water
4. To know if it is cooked, insert a small feather which should come out dry
5. To remove it from the mold let it cool
