## Ingredients

- Doves
- Sweet chilies
- Tomatoes
- Walnuts
- Bread
- Wine
- Broth
- Seasonings

## Directions

1. Take some sweet chilies, toast half and soak the other half
2. Grind the soaked chilies with two roasted tomatoes, walnuts, and breadcrumbs soaked in wine
3. Place this mixture in a pot with the doves, broth, and seasonings
4. Let simmer well
