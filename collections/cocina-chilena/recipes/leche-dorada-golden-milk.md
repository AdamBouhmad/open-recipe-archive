## Ingredients

- five glasses of cooked milk
- lemon peel
- cinnamon sticks
- ground cinnamon
- sugar to taste
- two tablespoons of chuño
- ten egg yolks
- ten egg whites

## Directions

1. Bring five glasses of cooked milk to a boil with lemon peel, cinnamon sticks, and a little ground cinnamon; sugar to taste
2. After boiling for a while, add two tablespoons of chuño dissolved in milk
3. When cooked, remove and let cool
4. Add ten well-beaten egg yolks
5. Place on the fire to cook the eggs
6. Remove, let cool for a moment, add ten beaten egg whites
7. Pour into a platter and place in the oven
