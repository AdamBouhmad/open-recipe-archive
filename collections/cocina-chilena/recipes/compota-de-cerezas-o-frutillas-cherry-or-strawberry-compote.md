## Ingredients

- Cherries or strawberries
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries
2. Cook with water and sugar in proportion to the quantity being made
3. Once cooked, place in a compote dish
4. Pour the syrup over them and serve cold
