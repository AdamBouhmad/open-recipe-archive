## Ingredients

- Raw potatoes
- Milk
- Salt
- Bread crumbs
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Grate raw peeled potatoes and remove the water they release
2. Add to the potatoes a small cup of milk, a little salt, bread crumbs and four eggs
3. Beat all of this
