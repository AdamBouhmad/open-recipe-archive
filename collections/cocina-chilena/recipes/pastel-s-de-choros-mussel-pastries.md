## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Onion rings
- Olives
- Oil
- Butter
- Coloring
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Whole peppercorns
- Grated cheese
- Breadcrumbs
- Broth from the mussels
- Puff pastry

## Directions

1. Roast the mussels until they release their broth
2. Cook them in the same broth after removing from the shell
3. When cooked, place in a dish with onion rings, olives, a little oil, butter about half a spoonful, a little coloring, sliced hard-boiled eggs and whole peppercorns
4. Over all this a layer of grated cheese and another of breadcrumbs
5. Pour the broth from the mussels over it, place puff pastry on top and put in the oven
