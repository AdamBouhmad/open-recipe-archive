## Ingredients

- lobster or shrimp
- onion
- tomato
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. It is advisable that for this dish the shellfish be as fresh as possible
2. Parboil so the shells loosen and peel them
3. Place in a serving dish with onion slices, tomato, whole pepper, bread crumbs, all in small amounts
4. When they have boiled, add oil and vinegar
