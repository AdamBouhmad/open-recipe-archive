## Ingredients

- pejerreyes (silverside fish)
- harina (flour)
- salmuera tibia (warm brine)
- four egg yolks
- egg whites
- one and a half tablespoons of aceite (oil)

## Directions

1. Clean the pejerreyes well and remove the spines.
2. Make a batter by mixing flour with a little warm brine.
3. Beat four egg yolks and whip the egg whites as for meringues.
4. Add one and a half tablespoons of oil to the mixture.
5. Combine all ingredients together.
6. Dip the pejerrey in this sauce before frying it in hot fat.
