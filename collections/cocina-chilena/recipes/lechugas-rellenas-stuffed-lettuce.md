## Ingredients

- lettuces
- salt
- water
- filling (minced)

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks at the base of the leaves
2. Wash them
3. Put them to cook in salted water
4. When half-cooked, remove them carefully and let them drain on a board, squeezing them one by one
5. Place filling about the size of an egg in the middle
6. With the same minced filling, gather the leaves from one side to the other so that they turn toward the stalk
7. Place them loosely in a vessel
8. Cook them with broth from the bones of the minced meat or from the stew
9. Finally make them boil with a hazelnut sauce
