## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- four eggs minus one white
- a little raw flour
- sugar
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve a little flour in cold milk and another portion of milk in a pan to heat
2. Combine with the first and cook until it pulls away from the pan; it should not be very hard
3. Remove from heat and put in a dish
4. Add four eggs minus one white, a little raw flour, and sugar
5. Beat everything together well
6. Fry in lard that should not be very hot
7. Strike with a stick while frying
