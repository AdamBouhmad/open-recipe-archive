## Ingredients

- Five cents worth of beef head
- Noodles
- Whole pepper
- Peppermint
- Onion

## Directions

1. Put five cents worth of beef head to cook and let it boil until the bones come loose
2. Strain this broth so it is clean and put it to boil again
3. Then add the noodle in the quantity desired and put it to cook again on low heat
4. Add whole pepper and peppermint, onion and let it rest
5. This soup can be made with any kind of noodle or macaroni
6. When you want to add bread to this soup, before serving it, toast some slices and add them, letting it rest a little so it takes on good flavor
