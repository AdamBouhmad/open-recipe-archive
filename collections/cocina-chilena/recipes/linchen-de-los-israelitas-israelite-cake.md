## Ingredients

- 2 ounces butter
- 5 eggs
- 3/4 pound sugar
- 3/4 pound flour
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1 egg yolk
- ground almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and mix with 5 eggs and 3/4 pound sugar and beat a little
2. Then add 3/4 pound flour and the zest of 1/2 lemon
3. Butter the mold and place the dough inside
4. Brush the top with 1 egg yolk mixed with a little ground almonds mixed with sugar and place on top
