## Ingredients

- fourteen eggs (seven whole and seven yolks)
- half pound sugar
- twelve ounces grated potatoes
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes the day before without skin and drain thoroughly
2. The following day, grate the potatoes and weigh them
3. Beat the seven whole eggs and seven yolks with sugar for one hour
4. Add lemon zest and lemon juice to the beaten mixture
5. Butter a mold and dust with toasted and ground bread
6. Pour the mixture into the mold
7. Bake in a gentle oven, taking care the heat is not too strong
