## Ingredients

- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 22 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 1/2 pound almond paste
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1 1/2 pounds flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pound powdered sugar with 22 egg yolks and 4 egg whites
2. When beaten and white, add 1/2 pound almond paste, the zest of 1 lemon, and 1 1/2 pounds flour
3. When everything is well incorporated, place pieces of this dough the size of a peso on buttered baking sheets
4. Sprinkle chopped almonds on top
5. Place in a moderate oven
