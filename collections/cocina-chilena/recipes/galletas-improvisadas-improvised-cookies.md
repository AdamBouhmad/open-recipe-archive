## Ingredients

- two and one-half pounds of flour
- ten ounces of sugar
- half pound of butter
- seven whole eggs
- one cup of milk with lemon zest
- cinnamon
- anise

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together and knead well
2. Cut the cookies and place in the oven
