## Ingredients

- Two whole eggs
- Half pound of sugar
- Half pound of flour
- Lemon zest
- Flour
- Butter and bacon
- Whole anise

## Directions

1. Beat two whole eggs and half pound of sugar with a wooden spoon for half an hour
2. Add half pound of flour and lemon zest
3. Place a little flour on the table and place the dough on top, spread without overworking
4. Roll not too thin and cut
5. Grease the pans with butter and bacon and sprinkle with whole anise
6. Place the cookies and put in the oven
