## Ingredients

- veal
- butter
- wine
- onion
- cloves
- pepper
- bay leaves
- veal feet

## Directions

1. Lard the meat very finely and leave it with a bottle of wine overnight
2. The next day add a piece of butter to a saucepan until it begins to brown
3. Then add the meat with onion, cloves, pepper, bay leaves, veal feet and the wine in which the meat has been soaked
4. Cover the saucepan and place over low heat, being careful that it does not burn
