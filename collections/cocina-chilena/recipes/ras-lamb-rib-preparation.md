## Ingredients

- lamb ribs
- flour
- milk
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb ribs and arrange leaving the meat at the point
2. Make a batter of flour mixed with milk and 3 eggs
3. Roll the ribs in it and fry
