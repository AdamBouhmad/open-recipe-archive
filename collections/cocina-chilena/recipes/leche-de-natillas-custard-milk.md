## Ingredients

- milk
- one pound of sugar
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- fifteen egg yolks
- four ounces of cornstarch

## Directions

1. Fill a regular-sized platter well with raw milk
2. Cook and cool it
3. Add one pound of sugar, lemon peel, and cinnamon and boil a little
4. When lukewarm, add fifteen egg yolks, stirring gently, and four ounces of cornstarch dissolved in milk
5. Place on the fire stirring constantly
6. Let cool and garnish with the same milk reduced to a thicker consistency
