## Ingredients

- 2 dozen ripe apricots
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar
- milk
- 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
- 1 plate whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take 2 dozen of the best ripe apricots and remove the pits
2. Place in a bowl with 1/2 pound powdered sugar
3. After two hours, pass through a sieve
4. Add a little milk and 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
5. When it begins to curdle, add 1 plate of whipped cream
6. Finish as with the previous recipes
