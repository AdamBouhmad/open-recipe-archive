## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- three egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook rice with milk and a little salt
2. Add a little sugar and three egg yolks
3. Mix well
4. Add beaten egg whites
5. Fry by small spoonfuls in very hot lard
