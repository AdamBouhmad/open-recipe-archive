## Ingredients

- Four ounces of almonds
- Two glasses of milk
- Half a pound of sugar
- Isinglass

## Directions

1. Place four ounces of almonds in boiling water until the skin can be removed
2. Place in cold water so they do not become oily and grind with a little sugar
3. Add to two glasses of milk that have been boiled with half a pound of sugar
4. Let the infusion cool, strain through a cloth and add isinglass as in the previous recipes
5. When it begins to thicken, add snow
