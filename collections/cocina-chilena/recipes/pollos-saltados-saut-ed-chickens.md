## Ingredients

- chicken
- peeled potatoes
- good piece of butter
- potatoes
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken with peeled potatoes
2. When well cooked, place a good piece of butter in a pot and heat
3. Fry the disjointed chicken
4. When browned, add potatoes cut in slices, finely chopped onion and parsley
5. Stir until the potatoes are browned
