## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Broth
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Drop the macaroni into boiling broth, stir and skim continuously, letting them cook for a quarter of an hour, then remove them, letting them boil on low heat until well cooked
2. Before serving, add cheese shavings over them
3. The soup should be thick; cheese can also be served grated on a plate and this is preferable
4. Macaroni remains very tasty when cooked in stew broth
