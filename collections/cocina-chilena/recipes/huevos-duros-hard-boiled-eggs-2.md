## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs in their shells and when they are hard, remove them and take off the shells
2. Cut them into rounds
3. Place them on a plate and serve with salt, oil, salt, vinegar and pepper
