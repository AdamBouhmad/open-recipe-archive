## Ingredients

- Onion
- Mushrooms
- Cumin
- Pepper
- Salt
- Bread
- Grated cheese
- Broth

## Directions

1. Fry a little onion until it begins to brown
2. Add the mushrooms
3. Add a little cumin, pepper, salt, bread, and grated cheese
4. Add a little broth and let it simmer
