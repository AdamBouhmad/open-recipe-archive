## Ingredients

- two ounces of chocolate
- a little boiling water
- four ounces of sugar
- a little cinnamon
- a little milk
- six egg yolks
- two egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve two ounces of chocolate in a little boiling water
2. Dissolve four ounces of sugar and a little cinnamon in another bit of water
3. Put a little milk on the heat and let it boil briefly
4. Let it cool and mix with six yolks and two egg whites, well beaten
5. Before combining everything, strain the boiled mixture
6. Cook like the coffee custard
