## Ingredients

- A little butter
- Flour
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Some pieces of ham
- Garlic
- Onion
- Raw poultry or meat
- Salt
- Chopped parsley
- Juice of one lemon
- Three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a little butter and flour in a saucepan with cloves, cinnamon and some pieces of ham set apart
2. Chop garlic and onion
3. After frying, add broth to the raw poultry or meat, adding sufficient salt, chopped parsley and the juice of one lemon
4. At the time of serving add three beaten egg yolks
