## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- butter
- bay leaf
- thyme
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- paper

## Directions

1. Cook and cut the tongue into more or less thick pieces
2. Cover with butter mixed with bay leaf and thyme, breadcrumbs, pepper and salt
3. Then cover with paper spread with butter
4. Toast over gentle heat
