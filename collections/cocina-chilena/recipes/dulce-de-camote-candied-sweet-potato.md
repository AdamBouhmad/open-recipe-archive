## Ingredients

- Sweet potato
- Sugar in equal quantity to the sweet potato

## Directions

1. Cook the sweet potato and pass it through a sieve.
2. Weigh an equal quantity of sugar to the sweet potato.
3. Make a syrup and mix it with the sweet potato.
4. Stir well and it is done when the bottom of the pan becomes visible.
