## Ingredients

- Four ounces of butter
- Four ounces of sugar
- Six ounces of flour
- Three egg yolks
- Lemon zest
- Three egg whites
- Eight ounces of powdered sugar for topping
- Eight ounces of sweet almonds
- Two ounces of bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Form a dough with butter, sugar, flour, egg yolks, and lemon zest after mixing and kneading well
2. Roll out and form into a round cake the size of a plate
3. Place on a buttered baking sheet
4. Prepare topping by beating three egg whites and mixing with powdered sugar and almonds reduced to paste
5. Cover the cake with this topping
6. Bake in a gentle oven
