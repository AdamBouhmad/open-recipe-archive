## Ingredients

- oranges
- sugar
- cloves
- cinnamon
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Extract the juice from some oranges
2. Season with sugar, cloves, and cinnamon
3. Bring to a boil
4. Add dissolved isinglass
5. Strain and let cool
