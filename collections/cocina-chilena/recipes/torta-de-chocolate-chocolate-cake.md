## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- One quarter ounce of cloves
- Six ounces of chocolate
- Four eggs
- Fourteen egg yolks
- Fourteen egg whites
- Seven ounces of dried dark bread, ground
- Butter

## Directions

1. Grind the sugar, cinnamon, cloves, and chocolate together
2. Beat for one hour with four eggs and fourteen egg yolks
3. Add the fourteen egg whites, beaten separately
4. Add seven ounces of dried dark bread that has been ground
5. Grease the mold with butter
6. Add the batter when ready to place in the oven
7. Bake in a not-too-hot oven for one hour
