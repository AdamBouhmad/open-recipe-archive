## Ingredients

- One pound of beef or veal, cut into small pieces
- Onion
- Bacon
- Half a glass of water
- Salt to taste
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut one pound of beef or veal into small pieces.
2. Brown in a pot with onion, bacon, and half a glass of water until it begins to stick.
3. Add boiling water and season with salt.
4. Cook for three-quarters of an hour.
5. Strain through a sieve.
