## Ingredients

- desalted pork leg
- white wine
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a desalted pork leg and soak in white wine for ten or twelve hours.
2. Place on the spit and roast for six hours over moderate heat, continuously basting it with its own drippings.
3. When done, remove the skin and dust with bread crumbs.
4. When serving, add lemon juice.
