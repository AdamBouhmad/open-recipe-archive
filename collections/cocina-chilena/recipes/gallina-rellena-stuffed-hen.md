## Ingredients

- One fat hen, killed the day before
- Apple, finely chopped
- Butter
- Small pieces of bread
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Take a fat hen killed the day before, being careful not to remove its fat or lard when plucking
2. Stuff it with finely chopped apple fried in butter
3. When well fried, add small pieces of bread
4. Once this is well cooked, add a little sugar and stuff the raw hen
5. Begin roasting early, putting a piece of butter on it, roasting slowly
6. Remove it, let it rest for a while and baste it with the same broth it releases and with a piece of butter
7. Repeat this several times
