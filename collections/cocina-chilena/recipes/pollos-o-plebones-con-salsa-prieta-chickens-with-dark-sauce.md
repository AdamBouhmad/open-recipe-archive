## Ingredients

- bird
- onion
- celery stalks
- salt
- bird blood
- vinegar
- flour
- butter
- bird broth

## Directions

1. Cook the bird with onion, celery stalks, and salt
2. Receive the bird blood in a cup with vinegar so it doesn't clump
3. Make a sauce of flour with butter and bird broth
4. When cooked, add the blood, taking care it doesn't clump and remains smooth
5. Place the bird on a platter with the onion and celery
6. Pour the sauce over it
