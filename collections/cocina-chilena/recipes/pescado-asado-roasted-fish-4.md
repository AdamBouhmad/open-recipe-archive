## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- pan rayado (grated bread)
- aceite (oil)
- agrio de limón (lemon juice)
- pimienta molida (ground pepper)
- hígado del pescado (fish liver)

## Directions

1. Place the whole fish in a pan.
2. Sprinkle grated bread, oil, lemon juice, and ground pepper on top.
3. Put it in the oven.
4. The ground fish liver can also be added if desired.
