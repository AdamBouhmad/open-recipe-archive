## Ingredients

- pejerreyes (silverside fish)
- water
- aceite (oil)
- ácido de limón or vinagre (lemon juice or vinegar)
- perejil (parsley)
- pimienta (pepper)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over the pejerreyes to clean them, but do not remove the backbone.
2. Use the water that drains after washing to cook them.
3. Place the fish in a pot with this water.
4. When the fish is partially cooked, add a seasoning composed of oil, lemon juice or vinegar, parsley, pepper, and salt.
5. Continue cooking over low heat until done.
