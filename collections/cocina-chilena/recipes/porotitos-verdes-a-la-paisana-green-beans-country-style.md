## Ingredients

- butter
- chopped parsley
- small green beans
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan with chopped parsley
2. Add the small green beans, which should be cooked beforehand
3. Stir and reduce to a sauce
4. When serving, dress them with lemon juice or vinegar
