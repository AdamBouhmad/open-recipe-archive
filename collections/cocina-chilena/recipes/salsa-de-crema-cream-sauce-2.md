## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt
- pepper
- one and a half glasses of cooked milk
- two or three egg yolks
- one tablespoon of milk

## Directions

1. Melt a little fresh butter and add one tablespoon of flour
2. Stir well and season with salt and pepper
3. Add one and a half glasses of milk cooked beforehand and let it boil
4. Remove from heat at once and slowly add while stirring two or three egg yolks dissolved in one tablespoon of milk
