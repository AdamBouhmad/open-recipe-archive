## Ingredients

- 6 pounds powdered sugar
- 6 pounds ripe sour cherries

## Directions

1. Take 6 pounds powdered sugar and 6 pounds ripe sour cherries
2. Place in a pan, arranging with a little sugar at the bottom, then cherries and sugar alternately
3. Place on low heat and when they begin to boil, leave for twenty minutes
4. Remove from heat and let cool
5. Then empty into jars, boil the syrup, skimming it until thick, and pour hot over the fruit
