## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Milk
- Sugar
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take as many eggs as tablespoons of milk and a little sugar and salt
2. Beat for a moment
3. Put this in a frying pan where there will be one tablespoon of butter
4. Open it with a knife so that the raw part on top passes to the bottom
5. Empty it folded onto a plate, sprinkle generously with sugar and brown it with a very hot iron
