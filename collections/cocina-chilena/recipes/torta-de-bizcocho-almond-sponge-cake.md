## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 3/4 ounce bitter almonds
- 3/4 pound ground sugar
- 18 egg yolks
- 14 egg whites
- lemon rind
- nutmeg rind
- butter

## Directions

1. Grind very fine 1 pound sweet almonds and 3/4 ounce bitter almonds, which should first be washed and well dried
2. Mix with 3/4 pound ground sugar and 6 egg yolks, which are beaten with 12 more yolks for half an hour
3. Beat separately the whites of 14 eggs and very lightly fold in the mixture with lemon rind and a little nutmeg rind
4. Grease the mold with melted butter and bake in a gentle oven for one hour
5. Strips of fine paper greased with butter can be placed in the mold to remove the cake more easily
