## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Cold water
- Sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces until half done
2. Remove and place in cold water
3. Cut into four pieces and peel carefully, removing the core
4. Make a syrup, removing all foam that forms
5. Add the quinces to finish cooking
6. Serve cold with little syrup
