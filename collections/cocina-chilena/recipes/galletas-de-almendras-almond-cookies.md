## Ingredients

- Half pound of almonds with shell
- Three egg whites
- Half pound of sugar
- Half ounce of cinnamon
- Flour with sugar
- Flour and sugar for the mold
- Butter for the pans
- Flour
- Egg yolk and sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Grind half pound of almonds with shell
2. Beat three egg whites, half pound of sugar, half ounce of cinnamon, and combine all
3. Mix a little flour with sugar and place on the table with the dough on top
4. Roll not too thin and cut, always greasing the mold with flour and sugar
5. Grease the pans with butter, a little flour, and put in the oven
6. When cooked, brush the top with a beaten egg yolk and sugar using a feather
