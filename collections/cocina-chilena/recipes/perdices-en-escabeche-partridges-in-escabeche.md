## Ingredients

- onion
- two partridges
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- garlic
- allspice
- black pepper
- parsley
- celery
- oregano
- salt
- orange leaves

## Directions

1. Cut onion into large pieces
2. Place a layer of onion in an earthenware pot, place two raw partridges, another layer of onion
3. Add one cup of oil and half cup of vinegar, four ground bay leaves, one head of garlic, whole allspice and black pepper, parsley, celery, oregano and salt
4. Cover with orange leaves
5. Well covered, cook over low heat, moving them often so they do not stick
6. Before serving let them cool for four hours so the broth incorporates
7. For two partridges use four onions
