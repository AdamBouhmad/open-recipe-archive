## Ingredients

- Two pounds of milk
- Twelve ounces of sugar
- Zest of a lemon
- Zest of a citron
- Zest of an orange
- Two tablespoons of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Bring everything to a boil
2. When sufficiently thick, let it cool and freeze
