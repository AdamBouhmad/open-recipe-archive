## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Broth
- Carrots
- Onions
- Two cloves
- Bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook lentils in broth with carrots, onions and two cloves
2. When well cooked, strain them and pour into the tureen over small pieces of bread fried in butter
