## Ingredients

- squabs
- bacon strips
- meat broth
- parsley bundle
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Empty and scald them, fold their legs inside, then leave them in cold water
2. Put them in a pot between two strips of bacon with meat broth and a bundle of parsley and fine herbs
3. When they are cooked, skim the broth and put it in a sauce that is clarified and thickened
