## Ingredients

- four ounces sifted rice flour
- four ounces ground almond
- four ounces powdered sugar
- seven beaten egg whites
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Combine four ounces sifted rice flour, four ounces ground almond, and four ounces powdered sugar
2. Mix with seven beaten egg whites and a little milk
3. Pour into a buttered mold and place in the oven
