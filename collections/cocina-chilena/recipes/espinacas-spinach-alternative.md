## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Butter
- Onion
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cook and chop very fine
2. Fry a little chopped onion in butter
3. Add the spinach, a little bread crumbs, salt, and milk, being careful that it is not clear
