## Ingredients

- thirty-six eggs, with only three egg whites
- twelve ounces of sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon
- anise
- one glass of white wine
- flour as needed
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat thirty-six eggs with only three egg whites together with twelve ounces of sugar, cinnamon, lemon and anise
2. When the mixture is like sponge cake batter, add one glass of white wine and enough flour to make a soft dough
3. Remove the dough onto a table and add a piece of butter and sugar
4. When the butter and sugar are granulated like meringue batter, add the egg little by little, always beating
5. After finishing this operation, sift in one pound of cake flour, mix well and put into two loaf pans and place in the oven
