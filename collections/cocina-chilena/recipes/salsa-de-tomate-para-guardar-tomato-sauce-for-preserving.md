## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper, ground

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes in half and salt them
2. Leave them from one day to the next
3. Squeeze and strain them through fine cloth, pressing until only the seeds remain in the cloth
4. Put this to cook until it boils and reaches the desired thickness
5. Before bottling, add plenty of ground pepper
