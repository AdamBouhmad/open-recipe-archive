## Ingredients

- Lamb breast
- bacon strips
- onions
- bay leaf
- parsley
- thyme
- salt
- broth
- butter
- bread crumbs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare a pot with bacon strips
2. Place the meat on top and cover with more bacon strips
3. Add three onions in pieces, bay leaf, parsley, thyme, salt and a plate of broth
4. Leave on low heat for three hours, with heat also from above
5. It can be served two ways: first way - brown with melted butter, soak with bread crumbs, sprinkle with pepper and salt, place on the grill and serve with spicy sauce; second way - remove the sauce and present garnished with endive, celery or other vegetables
