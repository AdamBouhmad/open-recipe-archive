## Ingredients

- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lamb blood
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cut onions lengthwise in abundance and season with salt and pepper
2. When the lamb is slaughtered, stir the blood and mix it with the onion
3. Put everything in a roasting pan and place in the oven
4. When well toasted, remove and it can be eaten hot or cold
5. Alternatively, if not using the oven, roast the pan over strong fire with coals on the lid
6. Or heat oil in a pan and when the blood is freshly drawn, add it by spoonfuls until it forms like fritters, serve with pepper and salt
