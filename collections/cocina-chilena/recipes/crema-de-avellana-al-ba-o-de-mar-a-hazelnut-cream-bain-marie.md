## Ingredients

- Four ounces of hazelnuts
- Four glasses of milk
- Half a pound of sugar
- A grain of salt
- Eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Toast four ounces of hazelnuts until golden
2. Grind them in a mortar
3. Heat four glasses of milk with half a pound of sugar and a grain of salt
4. Add the ground hazelnuts to the hot milk
5. When the infusion is cold, strain through a cloth
6. Mix with eight beaten egg yolks
7. Pass everything through a strainer and pour into a mold as in the previous recipe
