## Ingredients

- beef kidney
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut a kidney into thin strips
2. Put on heat with lard, salt, pepper, parsley and onion
3. After cooking, add a little vinegar and some strained broth, or in its place, pot broth
4. Do not cook further so it does not become tough
