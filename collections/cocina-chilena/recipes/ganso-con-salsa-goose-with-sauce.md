## Ingredients

- One goose
- Butter
- Potatoes
- Flour
- Broth
- Chopped parsley
- A little vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the goose
2. Fry potatoes cut into thin slices in butter
3. Make a sauce of flour, butter and broth from the goose
4. Add chopped parsley and a little vinegar to the sauce
5. At the time of sending to table add the potatoes
