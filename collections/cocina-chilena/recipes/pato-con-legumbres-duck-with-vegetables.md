## Ingredients

- duck
- onion
- celery
- whole pepper
- whole peeled potatoes
- carrot slices
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After washing the duck well, add onion, celery, whole pepper, whole peeled potatoes, carrot slices and vinegar to the duck
2. Cover the pot well and move it from time to time
