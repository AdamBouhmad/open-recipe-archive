## Ingredients

- sierra fish
- salt
- celery branches
- parsley
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- lemon
- oil
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- dark sauce

## Directions

1. Clean carefully and cook with salt, celery branches, parsley, a bay leaf and peppercorns
2. When it is seen to be cooked remove the skin, bones and all the dark meat
3. While still hot squeeze a lemon over it, add plenty of oil, vinegar, salt, ground pepper, a bay leaf and a little dark sauce
4. Let the fish rest about three hours, then serve with that sauce
5. It is also good with mayonnaise sauce, cold of course
