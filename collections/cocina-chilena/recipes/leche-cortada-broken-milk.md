## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- eight egg yolks
- cinnamon
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Heat a little milk and sweeten it
2. Beat about eight egg yolks, add one-third to the boiling milk
3. Stir for a moment and add half of the remaining beaten mixture
4. Leave on the fire until it comes to a boil, remove and add the rest of the beaten mixture flavored with cinnamon and lemon peel
5. Pour into a platter and place in the oven
