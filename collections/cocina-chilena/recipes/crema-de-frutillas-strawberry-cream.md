## Ingredients

- Four glasses of milk
- One tablespoon of cochineal grains
- Ten ounces of sugar
- A grain of salt
- Eight egg yolks
- One pound of ripe red strawberries

## Directions

1. Boil four glasses of milk with one tablespoon of cochineal grains, ten ounces of sugar, and a grain of salt
2. Pour over eight egg yolks and mix well with a spoon
3. Cook the cream, stirring continuously until it begins to thicken, then strain through a colander
4. Pass one pound of ripe red strawberries through a sieve
5. When the cream is cold, mix with the strawberries and pour into the mold
