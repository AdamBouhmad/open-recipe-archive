## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of veal
- 2 pounds of beef
- bacon
- cold water
- lettuce
- 2 or 3 turnips
- carrots
- chickpeas
- turkey scraps
- half chicken
- lamb bones
- parsley
- 3 cloves
- 1 roasted onion

## Directions

1. Take a pot containing about 2 jars of cold water
2. Add 2 pounds of veal, 2 of beef and half a pound of bacon, put on low heat
3. Add 2 jars of water when the first portion has lost its coldness
4. Increase the heat to produce foam
5. Remove this as it rises until no more appears
6. After boiling about 8 hours over regular heat, when it has been cooking 4 hours, add half a cabbage, 1 lettuce, 2 or 3 turnips, equal quantity of carrots, a handful of soaked chickpeas, turkey scraps, half a chicken and some roasted lamb bones, a little parsley, 3 cloves and 1 roasted onion
7. Add hot water if needed, continuing until 8 hours total
