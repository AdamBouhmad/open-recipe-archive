## Ingredients

- three ounces well-washed butter
- six ounces powdered sugar
- nine egg whites
- six ounces flour
- lemon zest
- Corinth raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat three ounces well-washed butter with six ounces powdered sugar until foamy
2. Beat nine egg whites as for meringues
3. Beat six ounces flour, a little lemon zest, and Corinth raisins if desired
4. Combine all ingredients
5. Pour into a buttered mold and place in an oven heated as for bread
