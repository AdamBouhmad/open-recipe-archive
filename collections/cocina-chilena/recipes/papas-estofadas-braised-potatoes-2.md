## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chopped onion
- flour
- meat or fish broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in water
2. Peel and cut them into slices
3. Put them in a saucepan with butter, salt, pepper, parsley and chopped onion with a little flour
4. Add meat or fish broth and a good glass of wine
5. Serve with little sauce
