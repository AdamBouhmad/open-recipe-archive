## Ingredients

- Leftover lamb meat
- fat
- fine herbs, chopped
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Heat a little fat in the pan
2. Add fine chopped herbs, a glass of white wine, salt and pepper
3. Add a little broth
4. Only let it heat without boiling
