## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Celery
- Parsley
- Small beans
- Onions
- Potatoes
- Poultry livers
- Good broth
- A little flour
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Chop very finely a little cabbage, celery, parsley, small beans, onions and potatoes
2. Also add chopped poultry livers
3. Put all of this to cook in good broth
4. Once well cooked, thicken it with a little flour dissolved in cold water
5. Stir and leave it a little while on the fire
