## Ingredients

- loin
- strips of bacon
- potatoes
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- celery sprigs
- parsley
- a glass of brandy
- broth

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with strips of bacon
2. Place it in a saucepan with potatoes, onions, pepper, salt, celery sprigs, and parsley
3. Add a glass of brandy
4. Cover and bring to boil
5. If it becomes too dry, add a small cup of broth
