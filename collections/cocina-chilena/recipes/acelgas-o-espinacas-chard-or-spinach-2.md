## Ingredients

- chard or spinach
- butter
- flour
- sugar
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- lemon acid
- bread fried in butter

## Directions

1. After cooking the chard, grind well and fry in a little butter with a spoonful of flour, two of sugar, a little pepper and salt.
2. Add milk so it boils and forms a thick sauce.
3. When cooked, add a little lemon juice.
4. Top with slices of bread fried in butter.
5. For spinach, do not add sugar.
