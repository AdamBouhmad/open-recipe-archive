## Ingredients

- 4 ounces almonds
- 2 glasses milk
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
- 1 plate cream

## Directions

1. Peel 4 ounces almonds in hot water and place in cold water to blanch
2. Dry in a napkin and grind in a mortar, adding a little water to prevent them from turning to oil
3. Add 2 glasses of milk that has been boiled with 1/2 pound sugar
4. Let infuse until cool and pass through a napkin, pressing well to extract all the almond juice
5. Add 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass and when it begins to thicken, add 1 plate of cream and freeze as with the others
