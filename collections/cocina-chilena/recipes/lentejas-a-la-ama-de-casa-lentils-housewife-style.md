## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fried bread crust

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in slightly salted water
2. Drain and place in a pan with a good piece of butter, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper
3. Sauté for several minutes
4. Place in the dish garnished with fried bread crust and serve very hot
