## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- oil
- salt
- onion pieces
- parsley
- white sauce with capers or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Place raw salmon slices in a marinade of oil to which salt, pieces of onion and parsley have been added
2. After one hour, remove and place on a grill, moistening it with the said marinade
3. Serve with a white sauce of capers or a tomato sauce
