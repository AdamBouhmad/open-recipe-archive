## Ingredients

- truffles
- butter
- broth or stock
- more butter
- cornstarch or flour

## Directions

1. Make by chopping some truffles and sautéing them in butter
2. Add broth or stock
3. When the sauce has reduced, add more butter
4. Let it boil, skim it and let it thicken naturally, or by means of cornstarch or flour
