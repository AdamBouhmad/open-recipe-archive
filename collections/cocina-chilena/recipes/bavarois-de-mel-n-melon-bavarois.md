## Ingredients

- 1 ripe yellow sweet melon
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
- 1 plate cream

## Directions

1. Choose a ripe yellow sweet melon
2. Cut into thin slices and remove seeds and rind
3. Cut into small pieces and place in a pan with 1/2 pound sugar
4. Boil until it becomes a jam and pass through a sieve
5. Add 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass and mix well
6. When it begins to take consistency, add 1 plate of cream
7. Pour into mold and place in snow
