## Ingredients

- Cooked poultry
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Lettuce
- Pickles
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Anchovies

## Directions

1. Cook a bird and remove the meat after cooling
2. Chop the meat coarsely
3. Place in a marinade with salt, pepper, and vinegar
4. After a few moments, dress with chopped lettuce, pickles, hard-boiled eggs, and strips of anchovies
5. Pour the substance over the top
