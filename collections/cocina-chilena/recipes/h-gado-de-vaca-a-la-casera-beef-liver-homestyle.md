## Ingredients

- beef liver
- thick pieces of bacon
- bacon strips
- veal scraps
- bay leaves
- clove
- good broth
- half bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Lard the liver with thick pieces of well-seasoned bacon
2. Place in a casserole on bacon strips with veal scraps, bay leaves, and clove
3. Add good broth and half a bottle of white wine
4. Skim everything
5. Cover with other slices of bacon
6. Seal the casserole hermetically and place fire on top also
7. Ensure it does not boil in large bubbles
8. When cooked, strain the sauce taking half of it, reduce it, thicken with butter and serve pouring this sauce over it
