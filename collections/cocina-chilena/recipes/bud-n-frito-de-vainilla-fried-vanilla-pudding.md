## Ingredients

- one bottle milk
- five ounces sugar
- vanilla
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- seven ounces cornstarch
- water
- seven egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put milk, sugar, vanilla, lemon zest and cinnamon on the fire and cook slowly until it boils
2. Strain and return to the fire, adding seven ounces cornstarch dissolved in water little by little
3. Stir until it boils for five minutes
4. Let cool and add seven well-beaten yolks
5. Place on the fire for a moment without stopping to stir and pour into a pudding mold to cool
