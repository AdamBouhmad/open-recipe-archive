## Ingredients

- cooked apples
- sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Once well cooked, pass the apples through a fine sieve
2. Add sugar, juice of 1 lemon, and 10 beaten egg whites
3. Mix everything well and pour into a baking dish
4. Bake in the oven for 1/4 hour
5. Remove and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
