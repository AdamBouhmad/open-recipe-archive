## Ingredients

- machas (razor clams)
- onion
- bread
- broth from the clams
- pepper
- parsley
- celery
- chili pepper

## Directions

1. Wash well and cook with the shells
2. When well cooked, remove from shells and wash again to remove sand
3. Fry a little onion in slices until colored, then fry a little bread (calculating that the soup not be too thick)
4. Add the broth in which the clams were cooked, well strained
5. Season with pepper, parsley, celery and a little chili pepper
6. Add the clams and let boil over low heat
