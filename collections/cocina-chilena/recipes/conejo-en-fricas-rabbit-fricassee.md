## Ingredients

- rabbit
- beef lard
- bacon
- flour

## Directions

1. Skin, gut and clean the rabbit, then cut into pieces.
2. Heat a good piece of fresh beef lard in a pan, through which pass square pieces of bacon.
3. Remove immediately, put two spoonfuls of flour in the lard and knead.
