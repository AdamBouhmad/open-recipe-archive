## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour at good point
- 4 ounces almonds
- 1.5 handfuls of powdered sponge cake
- 1 glass of good wine
- egg prepared for this purpose
- cinnamon and ground clove

## Directions

1. Add the almonds and sponge cake powder to the flour at good point
2. Boil everything for a short time
3. Lower from heat and add the sponge cake
4. Add a glass of good wine
5. Bring to point by adding the prepared egg
6. When done, add cinnamon and ground clove
7. Pour onto a table
