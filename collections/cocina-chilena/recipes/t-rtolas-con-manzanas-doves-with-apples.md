## Ingredients

- Doves
- Color
- Onion
- Apples

## Directions

1. Place a tablespoon of color and chopped onion in a pot
2. Add raw, well-washed doves
3. Chop a dozen apples on top
4. Cover the pot and place on low heat from early on, stirring occasionally so they do not stick
