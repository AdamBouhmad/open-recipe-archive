## Ingredients

- cod
- potatoes
- oil
- water
- parsley
- pepper
- saffron
- bread

## Directions

1. Clean and desalt the cod
2. Fry in oil until golden on both sides
3. Remove it, and in the same oil fry the peeled and cut potatoes
4. Add the cod with the potatoes and a pitcher of water, bringing it to a boil
5. When everything is cooked, take a bit of parsley, pepper, a few strands of saffron and a soaked breadcrumb
6. Dissolve all this in the cod broth and add to the stew
7. Season, and when it boils once, remove from heat and serve
