## Ingredients

- Lamb leg
- butter
- flour
- broth from the leg
- celery
- parsley
- onion heads
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Melt a tablespoon of butter until it takes a dark color
2. Dissolve a tablespoon of flour in it
3. Gradually add the broth from the leg to make a regular sauce
4. Cook the leg with celery branches, parsley and onion heads
5. Fry thin slices of potatoes in butter or fat
6. Combine everything and serve
