## Ingredients

- lamb loin
- parsley
- potatoes or bread
- color
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb loin and make the sauce in this broth
2. Grind plenty of parsley with potatoes or soaked bread, fry in a little color and then combine with the broth and meat pieces
3. Leave to make a delicate sauce
