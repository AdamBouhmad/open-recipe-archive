## Ingredients

- 30 Zaragoza peaches
- 3 pounds sugar
- Spirits
- Water

## Directions

1. Choose thirty healthy and beautiful Zaragoza peaches that are not very ripe; clean them well with a cloth and prick them to the pit with a needle.
2. Make a syrup with 3 pounds of sugar and before it reaches point add the peaches, stir them and let them cook until they yield to the finger.
3. Remove from the fire and peel them.
4. Let the syrup boil until it reaches thick point and cool it.
5. When the peaches are cold, place them in jars.
6. For every cup of spirits calculate two of thick syrup, fill the jars well so that the peaches remain well covered with syrup and spirits.
7. Cap the jars and place soaked bladder on top, tie well with twine so that no air penetrates.
8. If desired, calculate the spirits this way: for three of thick syrup one of spirits about thirty degrees. This method is perhaps safer.
