## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Oil or butter
- Pepper
- Nutmeg powder
- Milk

## Directions

1. Wash cabbage well and cook with salt.
2. Fry and drain the cabbage, cutting it into thick pieces.
3. Brown with oil or butter, salt, pepper and nutmeg powder.
4. Add a little milk and cook over low heat until the sauce reduces and the cabbage is well bound.
