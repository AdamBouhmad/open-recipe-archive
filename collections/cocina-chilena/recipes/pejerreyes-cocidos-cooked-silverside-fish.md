## Ingredients

- Silverside fish
- Hot water
- Oil
- Lemon juice or vinegar
- Parsley
- Peppercorns

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over them to remove the bones, but without removing the spine
2. The water that drains after washing is sufficient for them to cook
3. Place them thus in the saucepan
4. When somewhat cooked, pour over them a dressing composed of oil, lemon juice or vinegar, parsley and peppercorns
