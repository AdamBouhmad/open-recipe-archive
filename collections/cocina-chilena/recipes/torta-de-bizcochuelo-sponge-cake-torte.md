## Ingredients

- sponge cake (bizcochuelo)
- lukewarm light syrup
- sweet preserve of choice
- meringue
- orange blossom water
- glazed candies, pasta figures, or caramelized pieces (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Arrange the sponge cake on a serving dish
2. Pour lukewarm light syrup over it
3. When soaked, arrange in layers, alternating one layer of sponge cake and one of preserve of your choice
4. Build the figure somewhat high and coat with meringue
5. Garnish with the same meringue or with glazed candies, pasta figures, or caramelized pieces
6. Can also be made with caramel or egg yolk
7. Orange blossom water suits the meringue very well
