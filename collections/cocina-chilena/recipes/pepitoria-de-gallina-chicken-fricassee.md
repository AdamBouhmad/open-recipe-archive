## Ingredients

- Hen, cut into pieces
- Butter
- Parsley sprigs
- Small onions
- Mushrooms (setas)
- Flour, one tablespoon
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut hen into pieces
2. Scald for three minutes in boiling water, drain
3. Place in a casserole with butter, parsley sprigs, small onions, and mushrooms
4. Brown everything together
5. Add one tablespoon of flour and moisten with broth
6. Season with salt and pepper, cook
7. Remove the hen quarters and reduce the sauce
8. Strain the sauce and thicken with egg yolks without boiling
9. Pour over the hen pieces with lemon juice
