## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley
- ground pepper
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage very finely
2. Put in a dry pot
3. Pour boiling water over it and add salt
4. Cover for half an hour
5. Wash in cold water and squeeze well
6. Put in a salad bowl
7. Add chopped hard-boiled eggs, parsley, and ground pepper
8. Add olives and dress to taste
