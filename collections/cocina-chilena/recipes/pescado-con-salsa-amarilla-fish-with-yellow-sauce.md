## Ingredients

- harina (flour)
- agua (water)
- agrio de limón (lemon juice)
- dos yemas de huevo (two egg yolks)
- mantequilla (butter)
- pescado cocido (cooked fish)

## Directions

1. Dissolve a little flour in water.
2. Add lemon juice and two egg yolks.
3. Mix well.
4. Add a piece of butter and a little water.
5. Serve with cooked fish.
