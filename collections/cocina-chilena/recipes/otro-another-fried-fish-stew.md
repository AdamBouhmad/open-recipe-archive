## Ingredients

- fish
- parsley sprigs
- bread
- chili pepper
- pepper
- salt
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the fish and fry it
2. Make a sauce from parsley sprigs, bread soaked and ground, half a hot or sweet chili pepper, pepper or salt
3. Fry all this and add a little broth
4. Pass through a sieve and pour over the fried fish
