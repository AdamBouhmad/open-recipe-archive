## Ingredients

- trout
- pepper
- lemon sauce
- eggs
- onions
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the trout as described in previous recipes
2. Dust with a little pepper
3. Serve with lemon sauce in a sauce boat and garnish the plate with eggs sliced into rounds, all over a bed of finely chopped onions and parsley
