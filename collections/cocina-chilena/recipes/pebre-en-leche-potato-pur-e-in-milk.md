## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled potatoes
2. Mash them with a spoon until they become like paste
3. Add milk to make it like a porridge
4. Put it in a saucepan with a piece of butter
5. Put it on the fire for a while
6. If desired, empty it onto a platter and put it in the oven to brown
