## Ingredients

- a little bread without crust
- cold milk
- a little butter
- three eggs with egg whites
- sugar
- spices

## Directions

1. Soak a little bread without crust in cold milk
2. When well soaked, mix it with a spoon and add a little butter
3. Beat three eggs with egg whites and add sugar and spices
4. Mix everything well and place in a buttered dish
5. Bake in the oven
