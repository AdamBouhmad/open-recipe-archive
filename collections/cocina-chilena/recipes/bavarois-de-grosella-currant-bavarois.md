## Ingredients

- 1 pound currants
- some raspberries
- 10 ounces powdered sugar
- 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass
- 1 plate whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take 1 pound currants and some raspberries
2. Place in a bowl and break apart with a wooden spoon
3. Add 10 ounces powdered sugar
4. Let sit for a couple of hours and pass everything through a sieve
5. Add 1/2 ounce clarified isinglass and when it begins to thicken, add 1 plate of whipped cream
6. Finish as with the previous recipes
