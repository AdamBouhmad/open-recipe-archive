## Ingredients

- Lamb head
- liver
- lungs
- feet
- heart
- salt
- hot water
- spicy sauce or preferred sauce
- two egg yolks
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the jaws and snout from the head
2. Cut the offal into slices
3. Drain and wash well with hot water
4. Place in a pot and cook with salt
5. Just before serving, drain them
6. Except for the head, add to the rest a spicy sauce or preferred sauce made with two beaten egg yolks, a little butter and lemon juice
7. Arrange in a serving platter with the head in the middle
