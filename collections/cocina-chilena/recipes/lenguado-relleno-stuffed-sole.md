## Ingredients

- sole
- flour
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- nutmeg
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning well, make a lengthwise cut along the spine next to the dark part
2. Coat with flour and fry
3. Remove the spine and introduce the following filling: one tablespoon of butter kneaded with chopped parsley and onion, ground pepper, nutmeg and vinegar
4. Cook all this and place the fish in it
