## Ingredients

- ham
- white wine
- onions
- carrots
- thyme
- bay leaf
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare and place in adobo in good white wine with onions, carrots in slices, thyme and bay leaf
2. Be sure the vessel is hermetically sealed with a cloth and its lid or another vessel of equal size
3. Place in the roaster and while roasting, sprinkle with its own adobo
4. When it is half cooked, remove the rind, sprinkle with grated bread and return to the fire to brown it
5. Serve with its adobo, which will have been placed and reduced to consistency
