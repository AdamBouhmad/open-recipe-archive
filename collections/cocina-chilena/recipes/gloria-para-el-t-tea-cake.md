## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- Equal weight of powdered sugar
- Equal weight of flour
- Equal weight of butter
- Four egg yolks
- Four egg whites
- Peeled almonds
- Currants
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Place four eggs on one side of a balance scale and weigh out equal amounts of powdered sugar, flour, and butter
2. Beat the butter, add the four egg yolks, and beat again
3. Add the sugar and flour
4. Fold in the four egg whites, beaten separately as for meringues
5. Place the batter in a mold greased with butter
6. Cover the top with peeled almonds that have been cut, currants, and cinnamon powder
7. Bake in the oven
