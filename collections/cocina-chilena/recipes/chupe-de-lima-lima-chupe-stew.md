## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- garlic
- oregano
- potatoes
- water
- cheese curds
- shrimp

## Directions

1. Put a pot on the fire with a bit of butter, chopped onion, a little garlic and a bit of oregano
2. Fry a bit and then add raw potatoes halved
3. Fry a bit more, add a large amount of water
4. Let the potatoes boil until well cooked
5. Then add a bit of cheese curds dissolved
6. Add raw shrimp so they cook with the potatoes
7. When everything is very well cooked
