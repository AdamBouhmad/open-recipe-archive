## Ingredients

- laurel leaves
- bacon
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- broth
- lettuce
- chicory

## Directions

1. Prepare a pot with laurel leaves, bacon, onion, parsley, salt, pepper, a glass of white wine and the same amount of broth
2. Add the mutton tail and let it cook over gentle heat
3. When done, remove and let it drain
4. Serve with lettuce and chicory
