## Ingredients

- 1 hen
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- raw meat
- bread
- nutmeg
- 2 eggs
- salt
- milk
- sausages
- brains
- fried meat slices
- capers
- hen broth
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Cook the hen, cut it into pieces, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in butter
2. Chop some raw meat, grate bread and a little nutmeg and add 2 eggs, salt and a little milk
3. Make a paste from this mixture, form small balls, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in butter
4. Also fry some sausages and brains
5. Arrange everything on a platter; if desired, place some fried meat slices at the bottom
6. Make a caper sauce with the hen broth and pour it over
7. Cover the pastry with puff pastry
