## Ingredients

- small artichokes
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose the smallest artichokes
2. Cut them into quarters
3. Remove the outer leaves and cut the upper part
4. Cook them in cold water with a sauce of vinegar, oil, salt and pepper
