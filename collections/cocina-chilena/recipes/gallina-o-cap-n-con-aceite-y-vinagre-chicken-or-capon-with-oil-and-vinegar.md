## Ingredients

- Bird
- Water
- Onion heads
- Sweet peppers
- Whole peppercorns
- Two tablespoons of oil and vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the bird with water, onion heads and sweet peppers and some whole peppercorns
2. When tender, add two tablespoons of oil and vinegar to taste
