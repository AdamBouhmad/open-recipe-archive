## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- one pound sugar
- one pound cooked and grated potatoes
- half pound sweet almonds and a few bitter almonds
- zest and juice of one lemon
- twenty egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat twenty egg yolks with one pound sugar
2. Add one pound of cooked and grated potatoes, half pound sweet almonds and a few bitter almonds, and lemon zest and juice
3. Finally add twenty beaten egg whites
4. Butter the mold and dust with grated bread
5. Bake in the oven
