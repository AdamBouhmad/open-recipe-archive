## Ingredients

- four glasses of milk
- half a pound of sugar
- a grain of salt
- eight egg yolks
- half an ounce of clarified isinglass
- half a glass of Italian maraschino

## Directions

1. Bring four glasses of milk to a boil
2. Add half a pound of sugar and a grain of salt
3. Dissolve eight egg yolks with a spoon and add them to the milk, stirring to mix well
4. Heat until it begins to thicken and pass through a strainer
5. Let cool
6. Add half an ounce of clarified isinglass that is only lukewarm at the moment of pouring into the mold, which should be when it is cold
7. Add half a glass of Italian maraschino
8. Place in ice and remove as customary
