## Ingredients

- Duck
- Onion heads
- Celery sprigs
- Whole pepper
- Whole peeled potatoes
- Carrot slices
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place well-washed duck in a casserole with onion heads, celery sprigs, whole pepper, whole peeled potatoes, carrot slices, and vinegar
2. Cover well and shake occasionally
