## Ingredients

- Half a veal leg
- A piece of beef
- Some poultry
- Stock from puchero
- Whole cloves

## Directions

1. Place half a veal leg, a piece of beef and some poultry to cook with stock from puchero.
2. Stir and skim carefully, adding more stock and whole cloves.
3. Cook over low heat for five hours.
4. Pass through a sieve.
