## Ingredients

- Oysters without shells
- Beef butter
- Flour
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Broth from the pot
- Salt
- Black peppercorns
- Lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Blanch the oysters in boiling water
2. Pass them to cold water and drain well
3. In a saucepan, place a piece of beef butter mixed with flour, mushrooms and minced parsley
4. Turn them a few times on the fire
5. Moisten with broth from the pot and cook this sauce
6. When sufficiently reduced, place the oysters in it
7. Season with salt and black peppercorns
8. A moment before serving, add lemon juice or at least vinegar
