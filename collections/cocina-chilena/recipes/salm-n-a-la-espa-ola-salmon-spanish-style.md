## Ingredients

- salmon
- leeks
- pepper
- fish broth
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon with leeks and pepper in a light white fish broth
2. Serve with the same leeks, or with a mixture of chopped parsley and onion, dusted with pepper
