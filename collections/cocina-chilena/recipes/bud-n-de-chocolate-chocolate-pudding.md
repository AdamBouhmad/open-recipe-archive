## Ingredients

- Half a pound of peeled almonds
- Half a pound of sugar
- Water for syrup
- Lemon zest
- One egg
- Four egg whites
- Five egg yolks
- Three ounces of ground chocolate
- Two tablespoons of cinnamon
- Butter without salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Peel half a pound of almonds and dry well with a cloth
2. Make a light syrup from half a pound of sugar with necessary water, stirring always in one direction
3. Pour the syrup into a cup and cool slightly
4. Add the almonds and lemon zest
5. Add one egg and beat well
6. Continue until four whites and five yolks are incorporated and beaten until thickened
7. Add three ounces of ground chocolate and two tablespoons of cinnamon
8. Line the mold with unsalted butter and dust with flour
