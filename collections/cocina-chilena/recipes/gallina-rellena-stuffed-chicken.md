## Ingredients

- Hen or chickens
- Bread crumbs
- Eggs
- Onions
- Parsley
- Livers and gizzards, finely chopped
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- Two tablespoons of broth
- One small tablespoon of flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Fill the hen or chickens with bread crumbs, eggs, onions, parsley, livers and gizzards, all finely chopped
2. Place in a saucepan with one tablespoon of vinegar, two of broth and one small tablespoon of flour dissolved in the same broth
3. Put on the fire with butter
