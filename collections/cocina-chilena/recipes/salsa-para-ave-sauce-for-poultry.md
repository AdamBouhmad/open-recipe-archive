## Ingredients

- four onions
- butter
- broth or water
- poultry, partridge or hen
- a little sugar water
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Cook four onions well and pass them through a sieve
2. Add a small piece of butter and a little broth or water sufficient so it becomes of regular thickness
3. Put on low heat with the poultry, partridge or hen with a little sugar water and the seasonings you wish
