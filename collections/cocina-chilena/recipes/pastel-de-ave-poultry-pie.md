## Ingredients

- Chicken or pullet
- Butter
- Parsley
- Seasonings
- Salt
- Onion
- Water
- Flour
- Color

## Directions

1. Cut up a chicken or pullet into pieces
2. Heat butter in a pot and place the pieces on top
3. Add parsley, seasonings, salt, and chopped onion
4. When well fried, add about two cups of hot water
5. Let simmer slowly
6. Add a little flour and about four tablespoons of color
7. Arrange in a serving dish
