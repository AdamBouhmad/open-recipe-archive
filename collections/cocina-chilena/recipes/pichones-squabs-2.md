## Ingredients

- lard
- ham pieces
- chopped onion
- squabs
- egg yolk
- lemon acid

## Directions

1. Fry ham pieces and chopped onion in lard
2. Once everything is fried, remove it and brown the squabs in the same lard
3. When they are tender, mix them with water and the fried ingredients
4. Add an egg yolk and lemon acid
