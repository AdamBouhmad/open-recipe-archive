## Ingredients

- One tablespoon of butter
- About six chopped apples
- A little bread crumbs
- Peeled and ground almonds
- A few raisins
- Sugar to taste
- One glass of wine
- One raw goose

## Directions

1. Put one tablespoon of butter in a pan and fry about six chopped apples there
2. Once they are fried, add some bread crumbs, peeled and well ground almonds, a few raisins, sugar to taste and one glass of wine
3. Let it cook well and fill the raw goose with this mixture
4. Put it in the oven
