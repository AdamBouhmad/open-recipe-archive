## Ingredients

- yellow squash, cooked
- three roasted tomatoes without skin
- salt
- pepper
- half a small cup vinegar
- double the amount of broth
- small onion
- parsley, finely chopped
- sweet pepper

## Directions

1. Take yellow squash cooked in a pot, three roasted tomatoes without skin, sufficient salt and some grains of pepper
2. Pound everything in a mortar
3. Add half a small cup of vinegar with double the quantity of broth
4. Add a small onion, a little finely chopped parsley and a sweet pepper, all raw and cold
