## Ingredients

- soup broth
- peppercorns
- potatoes
- carrots
- turnips
- butter or fat
- bread
- cooked meat

## Directions

1. In the soup broth add a handful of peppercorns and very thin slices of potatoes, carrots and turnips fried in butter or fat
2. Add plenty of bread in pieces and chopped cooked meat also fried in butter
3. Let everything boil until mealtime
4. Pass through a strainer, taking care it is not too thick
