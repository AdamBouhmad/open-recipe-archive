## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 10 to 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of 1 pound sugar
2. Beat the egg yolks until they turn white
3. Combine with the warm syrup and place over gentle heat
4. Stir constantly until the egg is cooked
