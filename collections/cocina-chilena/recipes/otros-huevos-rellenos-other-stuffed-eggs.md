## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- bread crumb soaked in cream
- butter
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- fish
- oil

## Directions

1. Harden the eggs and cut in two halves
2. Remove the yolks and crush in a mortar with bread crumb soaked in cream
3. Add a part of butter, one or two yolks, salt and pepper
4. Fill the eggs with this mixture and cover with bread crumb moistened with butter
5. Pour the rest of the filling, which is thinned with a little broth and let it boil
6. It can also be filled with chopped fish and oil is used
