## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds flour
- 2 egg whites
- salt
- sufficient water
- 1 pound butter

## Directions

1. To 1.5 pounds flour add 2 egg whites, salt, and sufficient water, being careful that it does not become too soft
2. Mix everything and knead for a while
3. Take 1 pound of butter and squeeze it with your hand in pieces to remove all the water
4. Join them together and make a cake of it and place on a cloth to dry it more
5. Roll out the dough until it is the size of the butter cake and place on top, leaving a small border of dough around it
