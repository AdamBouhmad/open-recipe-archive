## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- milk
- lemon zest
- a little vanilla
- sugar to taste
- a little salt
- four to six egg yolks
- egg whites
- orange blossom water (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the rice with hot water
2. Cook with milk, adding more milk as it thickens
3. Add lemon zest, vanilla, sugar to taste, and salt
4. When open and thick, remove from heat
5. Remove the lemon zest and vanilla
6. Add four to six egg yolks
7. Beat egg whites well and add them to the mixture, with orange blossom water if lemon zest or vanilla was not used
8. Mix everything very well
9. Butter a mold and dust with ground sugar
10. Pour in the rice
11. Bake three quarters of an hour in the oven
