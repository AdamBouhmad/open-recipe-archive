## Ingredients

- Three dozen eggs
- Three egg whites only
- Twelve ounces of sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon
- Anise
- One cup of wine

## Directions

1. Beat three dozen eggs with only three egg whites together with twelve ounces of sugar, cinnamon, lemon, and anise
2. When the beaten mixture is like sponge cake consistency, add one cup of wine
