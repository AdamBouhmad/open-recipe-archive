## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- potato starch
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- anchovy or capers (optional)

## Directions

1. Have the cooked and cut potatoes as hot as possible
2. Dissolve potato starch with butter, broth, salt and pepper on low heat
3. As soon as this sauce is sufficiently thickened, add chopped anchovy or capers if desired
4. Dress the potatoes with this sauce
5. Serve as hot as possible
