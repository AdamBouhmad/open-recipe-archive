## Ingredients

- Capon
- Butter
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Pepper
- Broth
- Mushrooms
- Lemon peel
- Oysters
- Flour
- Liver
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place butter, onion, bay leaves, whole pepper, and a little broth in a pot
2. Cook the capon in this
3. Make a sauce from broth, ground mushrooms, lemon peel, and oysters
4. Cook briefly and thicken with flour fried in butter
5. If desired, stuff the capon before cooking with its liver, breadcrumbs, butter, some oysters, and pepper
