## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- one pound sugar
- ten egg whites
- twenty-two ounces chuño (freeze-dried potatoes)

## Directions

1. Stir twenty egg yolks in the same direction for one hour with one pound of sugar
2. Beat ten egg whites
3. Unite to the beaten mixture with twenty-two ounces of chuño, alternating spoonfuls of white with spoonfuls of chuño
4. When all is united, stir only as much as necessary and pour into a buttered mold
5. Bake in the oven and when removing, place sugar on top
