## Ingredients

- half a mold of milk
- 4 ounces sugar
- a piece of vanilla
- 1 ounce isinglass
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites well beaten
- sponge cake toasts for lining

## Directions

1. Measure half a mold of milk and bring to a boil with 4 ounces sugar
2. When boiling, add a piece of vanilla
3. When tepid, add 1 ounce of isinglass and stir until it dissolves
4. Add 6 egg yolks beaten slightly and return to heat until it boils
5. Pour this over the well-beaten egg whites and mix everything well
6. Pour into the mold, which should be prepared with sponge cake toasts arranged around it
