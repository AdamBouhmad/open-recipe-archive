## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- pimienta (pepper)
- perejil (parsley)
- vino (wine)
- salsa de callampas (mushroom sauce)

## Directions

1. Place the fish in the pot in which it will be cooked.
2. Add pepper and parsley.
3. Add a layer of wine and another layer of mushroom sauce.
4. Cook over low heat.
