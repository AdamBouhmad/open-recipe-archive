## Ingredients

- Five tablespoons of tapioca
- Milk
- Five whole eggs
- Sugar to taste
- Nutmeg
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- A small glass of white wine
- A small teaspoon of butter

## Directions

1. Soak five tablespoons of tapioca in milk
2. Cook until thick
3. Beat five whole eggs separately with sugar to taste, nutmeg, cinnamon, lemon zest, a small glass of white wine, and a small teaspoon of butter
4. Prepare the mold and place in the oven
