## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 6 ounces sugar
- 4 ounces butter
- 1 glass sherry or white wine
- a little milk
- cinnamon
- grated lemon
- sufficient flour

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg yolks well
2. Add 6 ounces sugar, 4 ounces butter, 1 glass sherry or white wine, a little milk, cinnamon, and grated lemon
3. Add sufficient flour for a soft dough
4. Knead everything a little
5. Form rings
6. Bake in the oven
