## Ingredients

- chicken or mutton
- chickpeas
- ham
- broth

## Directions

1. It is made as a rule with chicken or mutton, adding a little bit so it is substantial, some chickpeas or ham
2. The broth should be strained before serving
3. If greater substance is desired, pound the chicken in a mortar with bread crumb and thin it in broth
4. Strain it and once seasoned, put on heat but without boiling
