## Ingredients

- three eggs with shells
- flour equal in weight to the eggs
- butter, the freshest possible, equal in weight to the eggs
- powdered sugar equal in weight to the eggs
- three ounces almonds, peeled with boiling water
- lemon zest or orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Weigh three eggs with their shells and use equal weights of flour, butter, and powdered sugar
2. Grind the sugar with three ounces of blanched almonds
3. Unite this mixture with the flour, butter, lemon zest or orange blossom water, and the eggs
4. When all is well incorporated, butter a mold and pour in the mixture
5. Bake slowly between two fires
6. Serve cold or hot with ground sugar on top
