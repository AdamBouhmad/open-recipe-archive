## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Finely chopped onion
- Parsley
- Ground pepper
- Salt
- Sea urchin tongues
- Fat

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs and add a little finely chopped onion and parsley, ground pepper and salt
2. Remove the tongues from the sea urchins and add to the beaten mixture
3. Beforehand have the fat very hot
4. Pour all this beaten mixture into the pan and be careful to flip it so it fries well
