## Ingredients

- Apricots, peeled
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pass the peeled apricots through a sieve.
2. Make a syrup that is thick until the thread stage.
3. Measure one glass of syrup and another of apricot juice.
4. Stir lightly, and when it has cooled, bottle it without putting it over the fire.
