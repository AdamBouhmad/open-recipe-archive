## Ingredients

- six egg yolks with five egg whites
- half pound of sugar
- four ounces of butter
- a little anise water broken
- a little orange blossom water
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat six egg yolks with five egg whites like sponge cake, half pound of sugar, four ounces of butter, a little anise water, a little orange blossom water and enough flour to make a very soft dough
