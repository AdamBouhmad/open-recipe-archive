## Ingredients

- Oysters with their liquid
- Piquant sauce
- Oil
- Parsley
- Flour
- White wine
- Broth
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the shell and reheat for a few minutes in the water they contain
2. Drain and place in piquant sauce
3. Reheat in oil and parsley
4. Add a spoonful of flour
5. Add white wine and broth, letting it reduce until little sauce remains
6. Pour a little lemon juice over them
