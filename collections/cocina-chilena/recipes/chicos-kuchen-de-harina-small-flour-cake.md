## Ingredients

- 6 ounces butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- half a tablespoon of cinnamon
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Make a thick dough with the butter, egg yolks, sugar, cinnamon and precise flour
2. Form small round rolls
3. Place on paper greased with butter and grated sponge cake
4. Top the rolls with beaten egg whites
5. Bake in a pastry oven with heat from below
