## Ingredients

- poultry
- garlic
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- thin strips of bacon
- lamb or beef meat
- cloth
- string

## Directions

1. Freshly killed birds, pluck without scalding in hot water so the skin does not soften
2. Begin to remove the skin with a fine knife, starting from the tip of the backbone near the neck and making a cut from this part to the rump
3. Remove the crop from inside to avoid tearing the front
4. Remove the bones so the legs remain boneless without breaking them; the wings are not deboned as they give shape to the birds
5. Place the bird meat, thin strips of bacon and lamb or beef meat in a marinade of garlic, vinegar, salt, pepper and oregano to increase the filling
6. Leave there the necessary time for it to marinate well
7. Then spread the bird skin on a table and arrange the meat and bacon on it
8. After completing this operation, pound it so it unites
9. Wrap, starting from the rump, then roll with fine string, wrap in cloth and cook in boiling water seasoned with salt and vinegar from the same in which the bird has been, being careful to turn it when it is boiling
