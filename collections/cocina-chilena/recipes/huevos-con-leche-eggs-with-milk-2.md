## Ingredients

- Melted butter
- Finely chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- One glass of milk
- One tablespoon of flour
- Hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. To the melted butter add very finely chopped parsley, salt, pepper and a glass of milk in which one tablespoon of flour has been dissolved beforehand
2. When the sauce is ready, place the hard boiled eggs cut into rounds in it
