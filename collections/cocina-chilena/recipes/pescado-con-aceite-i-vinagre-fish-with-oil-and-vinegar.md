## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- cebollas (onions)
- tomate (tomato)
- apio (celery)
- ajíes dulces (sweet peppers)
- escabeches (pickled vegetables)
- aceite (oil)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Cut large slices of onions, tomatoes (fresh or dried if fresh is not available), and celery branches.
2. Place these in a dish.
3. Put the whole fish or pieces of fish on top.
4. Cover with another lid of equal size.
5. Add sweet peppers (fresh halved or soaked if dried) and pieces of pickled vegetables.
6. When it comes to a boil, add oil and vinegar.
