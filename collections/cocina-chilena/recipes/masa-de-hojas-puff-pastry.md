## Ingredients

- one pound cow butter
- one pound flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take one pound cow butter per pound of flour
2. Mix the dough very soft, adding cold water
3. Flatten with the roller until it reaches the thickness of a strong peso
4. Coat the surface well with cow butter
5. Fold in half and pass the roller over it again
6. Coat with butter and flour as many times as desired to give the dough layers. The minimum is three
