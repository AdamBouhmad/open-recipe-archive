## Ingredients

- butter kneaded with flour
- white wine
- broth
- parsley sprigs
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- toasted bread slices
- brandy
- truffle

## Directions

1. Melt, without letting it turn red, a piece of butter kneaded with flour
2. Add in equal quantity white wine and broth with a sprig of parsley, pepper and salt
3. Make it boil, then place the foods to be cooked with a little lemon juice and remove from the fire as it boils
4. Place the pieces of meat or pieces of poultry you want to prepare on toasted bread slices and pour the sauce over them
5. A small glass of brandy and a truffle cut into very fine pieces make an admirable dish
