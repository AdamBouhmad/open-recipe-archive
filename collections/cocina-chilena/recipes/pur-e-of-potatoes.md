## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of white potatoes
- water
- salt
- butter
- poultry broth
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook 4 pounds of white potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters, in water and salt
2. Before they are completely cooked, drain well all the water
3. In the same covered pot, place under the heat so they finish cooking and become floury
4. Stir with a spoon, adding a little butter
5. Pass through a sieve
6. Mix with poultry broth and pass through a fine sieve
7. At the time of serving, add 4 beaten egg yolks
