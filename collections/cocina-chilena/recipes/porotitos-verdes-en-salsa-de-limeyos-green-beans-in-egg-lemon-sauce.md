## Ingredients

- butter
- chopped parsley
- small green beans
- egg yolks
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan with chopped parsley
2. Add the small green beans
3. Stir and reduce the sauce
4. Before serving, add a beaten mixture of egg yolks diluted with lemon juice or a little vinegar
