## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Water
- One sprig of parsley
- One sprig of celery
- Three carrots
- Pepper
- One garlic clove
- Onion head
- Three ounces of butter
- Two tablespoons of wine
- Three egg yolks
- One tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the chickens in three warm waters
2. Put them to cook in a little water with a sprig of parsley, one of celery and three carrots, pepper, one garlic clove and onion head
3. Take three ounces of butter, dissolve it in two tablespoons of wine
4. Add all of this to the broth half an hour before serving
5. Add three egg yolks with one tablespoon of vinegar
