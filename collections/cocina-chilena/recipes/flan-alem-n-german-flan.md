## Ingredients

- One and a half bottles of milk
- Half a pound of sugar
- Vanilla
- Four ounces of ground almonds
- Lemon zest
- Twelve egg yolks
- Ten ounces of chuño

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with half a pound of sugar, vanilla, four ounces of ground almonds, and lemon zest
2. Strain the mixture
3. Beat twelve egg yolks and mix them with ten ounces of chuño
4. When the milk is lukewarm, gradually add the egg yolks and chuño while stirring
5. Keep on the fire until it is set, stirring constantly so it does not stick
6. Pour into a mold that has been moistened with cold water
7. Allow to cool and serve with wine sauce
