## Ingredients

- Fish
- Vinegar
- Onion pieces
- Whole peppercorns
- Butter or fat
- Breadcrumbs
- Nutmeg
- Hard-boiled egg
- Broth
- Puff pastry or delicate pastry

## Directions

1. Place fish in vinegar from the day before with onion pieces and whole peppercorns
2. The next day remove the vinegar, remove the bones and mince finely
3. Fry in butter or fat
4. Add breadcrumbs, a little nutmeg, minced hard-boiled egg and sufficient broth so it is not too thin
5. Place in a dish with thin puff pastry or any other delicate pastry
