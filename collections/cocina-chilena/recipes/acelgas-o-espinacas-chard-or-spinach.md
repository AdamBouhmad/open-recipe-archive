## Ingredients

- Chard or spinach, cooked
- butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- two tablespoons of sugar
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- lemon juice
- bread slices, fried in butter

## Directions

1. After the chard has been cooked, grind it well and fry it in a little butter with one tablespoon of flour, two tablespoons of sugar, a little pepper and salt
2. Add milk so that it boils and becomes a thick sauce
3. When it is cooked, add a little lemon juice
4. On top place some slices of bread fried in butter
5. For spinach, do not add sugar
