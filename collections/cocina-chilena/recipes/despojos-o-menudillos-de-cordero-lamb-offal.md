## Ingredients

- lamb head
- liver
- lungs
- feet
- heart
- salt
- spicy sauce or preferred sauce
- 2 egg yolks
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the jaws and snout from the head
2. Cut the offal into slices, drain of blood and wash well with hot water
3. Place in a pot and cook with salt
4. Just before serving, drain the offal and, except for the head, add a spicy sauce or preferred sauce with 2 beaten egg yolks, a little butter and lemon juice
5. Place the dish in a platter with the head in the middle
