## Ingredients

- Two onion heads
- Two loaves of bread
- Twenty cents worth of roasted and finely chopped meat
- Half a glass of wine
- Whole pepper
- Salt
- Broth

## Directions

1. Cut two onion heads into slices and fry them until they take on color
2. Cut two loaves of bread into slices and toast them
3. Arrange in a pan in layers of onion and bread, roasted and finely chopped meat, half a glass of wine, whole pepper, salt and a little broth
4. Place on the fire to boil well
