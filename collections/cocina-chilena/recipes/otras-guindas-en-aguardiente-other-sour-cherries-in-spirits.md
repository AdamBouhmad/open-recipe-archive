## Ingredients

- Cherries
- Cherry pits
- Spirits
- 2 pounds sugar per 1 pound guindas

## Directions

1. Crush cherries, even the pits; place them in a pot and cover with spirits and let them infuse for one week.
2. Strain them, filter and in this liquid add whole cherries and sugar: for every pound of guindas, add 2 pounds of sugar.
