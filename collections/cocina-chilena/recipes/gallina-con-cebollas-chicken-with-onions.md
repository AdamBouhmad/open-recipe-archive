## Ingredients

- Hen
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Clove
- Bay leaf
- Fifteen or sixteen small onions
- Broth
- Starch

## Directions

1. Brown the hen in lard with salt, pepper, thyme, clove and bay leaf
2. Put in the saucepan fifteen or sixteen small onions
3. Let them brown, add a little broth and cook on low heat five or six hours until the hen is well cooked
4. Then thicken the sauce with starch and serve the hen surrounded by the onions
