## Ingredients

- One chicken, cleaned
- Aromatics (herbs)
- A little bread crumb

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken, cut all the meat into thin strips and place in a marinade with all the aromatics and a little bread crumb
2. After coating with all the aromatics, fry them
3. Serve at table together with a vegetable stew
