## Ingredients

- Twenty apples
- Ten ounces of water
- Twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- Juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Cut the apples in half and remove the interior
2. Crush them and place them in a pot with ten ounces of water
3. Let them boil until one part is consumed
4. Pass through a sieve
5. Add twelve ounces of powdered sugar
6. When cooled, add the juice of two lemons and freeze
