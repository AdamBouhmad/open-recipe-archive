## Ingredients

- Acallóte
- 1 pound 4 ounces sugar per 1 pound acallóte
- Chopped almond

## Directions

1. Cook the acallóte in water and strain it in a basket.
2. Weigh 1 pound 4 ounces of sugar for every 1 pound of acallóte.
3. Make the syrup with the sugar and add the acallóte before it reaches point; stir constantly so it does not stick and add a little bit of chopped almond.
4. Remove when it reaches point.
