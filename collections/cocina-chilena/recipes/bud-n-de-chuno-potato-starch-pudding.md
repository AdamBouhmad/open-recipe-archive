## Ingredients

- thick chuño made in milk
- sugar
- a small piece of butter
- lemon zest
- three eggs with egg whites
- spices

## Directions

1. Make a thick chuño in milk and pour it into a cup to cool
2. Add sugar, a small piece of butter, and lemon zest
3. When cold, beat three eggs with egg whites
4. Mix with the chuño, adding some spices
5. Place in the oven
