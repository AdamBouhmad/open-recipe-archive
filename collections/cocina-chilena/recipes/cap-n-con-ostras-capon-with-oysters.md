## Ingredients

- One capon
- Water and salt for cooking
- A good piece of butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Whole pepper
- Oyster broth
- Oysters
- Three eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the capon in water and salt
2. In a saucepan put a good piece of butter, one tablespoon of flour and whole pepper
3. Add the oyster broth
4. At meal time add the oysters and three eggs and bring to a light boil
5. Be careful to stir frequently so it does not stick
6. The sauce should be of regular thickness; if it is too thick, thin it with the broth in which the capon was cooked
7. Place the capon on a platter and pour the sauce over it
