## Ingredients

- Cardoon
- Salt
- Pine nuts
- Hazelnuts
- Raw oil
- Water

## Directions

1. Clean the cardoon and remove the strings and membranes
2. Cut into pieces and place in cold water so they do not blacken
3. Blanch and cook with salt
4. Drain and add a sauce of pine nuts and hazelnuts
5. Drizzle raw oil over the broth and bring to a boil a couple of times
6. Alternatively, serve the cardoon dressed after it has been cooked
