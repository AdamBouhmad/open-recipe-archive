## Ingredients

- squash
- flour
- onion
- tomato
- cheese
- water or broth
- salt
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into thin slices, put in the sun or oven for a while
2. Fry rolling them in flour together with onion slices and a little tomato
3. Arrange in a serving dish in layers of squash, layers of onion and grated cheese on top
4. Before putting on the fire add boiling water or broth with salt
5. Shortly before taking to the table, beat three eggs with broth and scatter over the entire dish
