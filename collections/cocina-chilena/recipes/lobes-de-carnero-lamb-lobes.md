## Ingredients

- lamb lobes
- lard
- salt
- tomato
- water
- spices

## Directions

1. After boiling, cut into small slices and reheat with lard and salt, being careful to skim frequently
2. Then fry with tomato, and before serving, stew with warm water and spices
