## Ingredients

- Milk for a regular mold
- One pound of roasted unground coffee
- Lemon zest
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Calculate the milk needed for a regular mold
2. Bring it to boil with one pound of roasted unground coffee and a piece of lemon zest
3. Give it three boils and strain
4. Sweeten to taste and freeze
