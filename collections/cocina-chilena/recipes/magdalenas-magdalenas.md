## Ingredients

- 12 ounces butter
- 6 eggs
- 12 ounces powdered sugar
- 1 pound flour
- zest of lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the butter with the eggs and sugar for half an hour
2. Then add the zest of 1 lemon and the flour
3. When incorporated, place the dough 1/2 finger thick on a bordered baking tin
4. Dust with sugar and place in a hot oven for half an hour
5. Immediately after removing from the oven, cut into pieces and let cool on the same tin
