## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- White sauce
- Butter
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces all the part of the asparagus that can be eaten
2. Place white sauce with butter in a pan
3. Add eggs and cook them, stirring constantly
4. At the last moment add the pieces of asparagus
