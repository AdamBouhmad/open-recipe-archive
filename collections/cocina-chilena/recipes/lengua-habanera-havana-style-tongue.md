## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt
- parsley
- small onions
- flour
- two glasses of good white wine
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well in hot water, remove the skin and cut into wheels
2. Cook in a casserole with salt
3. When it is seen to be cooked, strain the broth and add it back with the tongue
4. Add a little chopped parsley, small onions fried in lard with a little flour, two glasses of good white wine and wheels of potatoes
5. Season with salt, let it cook for a while
