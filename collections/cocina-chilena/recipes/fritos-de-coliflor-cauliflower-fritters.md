## Ingredients

- three or four egg yolks
- cooked cauliflower
- egg whites
- flour (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat three or four egg yolks slightly
2. Mix with cooked cauliflower, removing thick stems first
3. Stir until cauliflower is well broken up
4. Add beaten egg whites
5. If too thin, add a very little flour
6. Fry in lard
