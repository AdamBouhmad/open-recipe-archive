## Ingredients

- 1 pound almonds
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 10 egg whites
- 1 heaping spoonful flour
- butter

## Directions

1. When the almond is peeled, dry it on a cloth until very dry
2. Reduce the almond to very fine paste
3. Add the 10 egg whites without beating and incorporate them with the paste
4. Add the flour last
5. Spread this paste very thin on buttered baking sheets
6. Place in the oven until golden
7. At this point remove the sheets to the mouth of the oven and there cut with a knife the squares of the size desired
8. Shape over a round stick
