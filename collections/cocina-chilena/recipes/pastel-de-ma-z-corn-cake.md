## Ingredients

- tender corn
- corn meal
- burned fat
- sugar
- raisins
- cinnamon
- egg yolks
- cow meat
- olives
- chicken
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut tender corn and grate or grind the corn
2. Put the corn in burned fat until it is cooked
3. Add sugar, raisins, cinnamon and some beaten egg yolks
4. If desired, make a hash of cow meat with raisins, olives, chicken, slices of hard eggs, cinnamon and sugar
5. Empty the hash into the pastry dish or pan, taking care to put plenty of fat on the bottom
6. Then cover with the corn that has been prepared and on top sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
7. Then cover with a sheet, put plenty of fire on top and very little on the bottom
8. Let it rest until it browns well and serve
