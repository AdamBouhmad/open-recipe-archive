## Ingredients

- One small pineapple
- Syrup
- Six egg yolks
- One tablespoon of rice flour
- One glass of milk
- Two ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple and cut into small pieces
2. Add to the syrup and boil until it becomes a jam
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Mix six egg yolks with one tablespoon of rice flour, one glass of milk, and two ounces of sugar
5. Cook over gentle heat, stirring continuously until it begins to thicken
6. Remove from fire and beat well with a spoon
7. Return to heat for a few minutes, then transfer to another pan
8. Allow to cool and place in the snow
9. When ready, place the jam on a plate of whipped cream and serve in small cups placed again in the snow
