## Ingredients

- Strawberries
- Sugar, equal weight to the strawberries

## Directions

1. Remove the stem from the strawberry and clean well with a cloth.
2. Weigh an equal quantity of sugar as strawberries.
3. Arrange in a saucepan layers of strawberries, layer of sugar.
4. Leave thus until the next day.
5. Place on the fire and bring to point.
