## Ingredients

- beef
- oil
- salt
- white rice
- plantains

## Directions

1. Take sufficient oil for two pounds of beef and heat in the pan
2. Cut the beef into finger-thick strips and crush slightly with the mortar pestle
3. Add a little salt and fry
4. Serve with white rice and plantains cut into small rounds
