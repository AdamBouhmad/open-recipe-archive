## Ingredients

- butter
- finely chopped onion
- water
- peeled potatoes cut into small pieces
- pepper
- salt
- mint
- parsley
- tarragon

## Directions

1. Put butter and very finely chopped onion in a saucepan
2. Let it fry until it takes a good color
3. Add a glass of water and the peeled potatoes in small pieces with pepper and salt
4. Add a bundle made of mint, parsley, tarragon, etc.
5. Let everything cook slowly
6. Before serving this dish, remove the bundle
