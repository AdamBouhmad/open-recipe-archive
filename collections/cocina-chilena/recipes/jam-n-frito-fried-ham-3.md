## Ingredients

- ham
- bread slices
- tomato sauce or eggs

## Directions

1. Before frying it is necessary to give it a boil to desalt it
2. Then cut into slices and thin strips and fry so that they do not become hardened or too toasted
3. Serve with slices of bread fried in the same substance, with a tomato sauce or with fried eggs
