## Ingredients

- mussels
- parsley
- salt
- bread
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After washing well, cook in water with parsley and salt
2. Remove from shells and place each mussel on a very thin slice of bread
3. Season the mussel broth with beaten egg yolks and plenty of chopped parsley until it becomes a little thick
4. Place the mussels on a serving dish and pour the sauce over them
5. Serve
