## Ingredients

- eels
- salt
- water
- breadcrumbs
- egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. After well prepared, open and cut into pieces
2. Cook in little water with a bit of salt
3. When cold, remove all the water
4. Coat in grated breadcrumbs
5. Then in two beaten egg yolks mixed with melted butter
6. Coat again in grated breadcrumbs
7. Let them take a little color on the grill and serve with a sauce
