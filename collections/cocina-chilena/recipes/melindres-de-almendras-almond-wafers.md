## Ingredients

- 2 ounces ground almonds
- 1/2 shell of very thick quince paste
- grated lemon
- ground cinnamon
- ground clove
- 1 pound refined powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together and make a small paste
2. Place on a table with powder on top and bottom
3. Roll out and cut to about the thickness of a peso coin
4. Pinch the shape you want to cut on one side
5. When they are aired, place them in a hot oven on papers and a board
6. After they are cooked and browned on one side, remove and let cool
7. Turn them and brown again
8. Put together in pairs and fill with desired sweet
9. When the filling is aired, dust with powder
10. These wafers can also be made with 2 more ounces of almonds and 1 egg white
