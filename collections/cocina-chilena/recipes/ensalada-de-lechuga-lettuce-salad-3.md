## Ingredients

- lettuce
- hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Wash lettuce well
2. Cut to desired size
3. Put in a very clean cloth and squeeze well until all water is removed
4. Put in a bowl and dress to taste
5. Add chopped hard-boiled egg
