## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- sugar
- three egg yolks
- egg whites
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes
2. Grind them
3. Season with salt, sugar, and three egg yolks
4. Add beaten egg whites when ready to fry
5. Fry in very hot lard
