## Ingredients

- four ounces well-washed butter (to remove salt)
- half pound powdered sugar
- six ounces flour
- ten egg whites, well beaten
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Mix the well-washed butter with powdered sugar
2. Add flour, beaten egg whites, and lemon zest
3. Pour everything together into a buttered mold
4. Bake in the oven
