## Ingredients

- Large shells
- Oysters
- Minced oysters and corresponding sauce
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Choose large shells
2. In each shell place four or six oysters with their minced mixture and corresponding sauce
3. Cover with breadcrumbs
4. Place on the grill and serve roasted
