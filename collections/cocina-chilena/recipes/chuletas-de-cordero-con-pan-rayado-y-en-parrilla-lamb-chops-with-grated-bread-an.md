## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- hot butter
- egg yolks
- clear sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pass through very hot butter without allowing them to brown
2. Remove and let the butter cool slightly, then mix with egg yolks
3. Soak the chops in this sauce, then place on the grill and roast over gentle heat
4. Serve with clear sauce and lemon juice
