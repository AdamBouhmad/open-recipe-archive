## Ingredients

- two large loaves of beer bread
- milk
- six beaten eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- cow meat
- raisins
- olives
- chicken
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Take two large loaves of beer bread and remove the crumb
2. Soak this crumb in milk
3. Add six beaten eggs, sugar, cinnamon
4. Everything is fried in butter
5. Then prepare the hash as before and assemble the cake, putting it immediately to fire in the same manner
