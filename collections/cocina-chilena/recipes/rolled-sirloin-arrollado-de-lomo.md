## Ingredients

- Sirloin steak
- Breadcrumbs
- Nutmeg
- Bacon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut long thin pieces of sirloin, beat them well
2. On one side place breadcrumbs and nutmeg
3. Cut bacon pieces of the same size
4. Roll and tie with string
5. Cook slowly in a casserole and baste with the juice they release
6. Make a sauce by adding a spoonful of flour
