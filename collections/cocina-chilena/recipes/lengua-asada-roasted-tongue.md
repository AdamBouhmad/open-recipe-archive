## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- paper

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue
2. Spread butter on it and roll in breadcrumbs
3. Wrap in paper and place in the oven to brown
4. Serve with pebre or any other vegetable stew
