## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 4 ounces almonds
- 1.5 handfuls of sponge cake powder
- 1 glass good wine
- ground cinnamon
- ground clove

## Directions

1. To 1 pound of flour at the proper point, add 4 ounces of almonds and 1.5 handfuls of sponge cake powder, and bring everything to a boil again for a short time
2. Remove from heat and add the sponge cake
3. Add 1 glass of good wine and bring to the proper point by adding the prepared egg
4. When done, add ground cinnamon and ground clove
5. Pour onto a table with powder on top and mark the sponge cakes so that after they cool they can be cut with a knife
