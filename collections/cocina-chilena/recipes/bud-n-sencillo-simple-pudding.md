## Ingredients

- One pound of raisins
- Half a pound of flour
- Half a pound of sugar
- Half a pound of suet, trimmed and finely chopped
- Four whole eggs
- One glass of white wine
- A little cognac
- Lemon peel
- Various spices
- Sufficient milk to moisten

## Directions

1. Combine raisins, flour, sugar, suet, whole eggs, white wine, cognac, lemon peel, and spices
2. Add sufficient milk to moisten
3. Wrap in a napkin
4. Place in boiling water for six hours
