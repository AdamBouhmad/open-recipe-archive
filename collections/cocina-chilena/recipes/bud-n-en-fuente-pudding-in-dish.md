## Ingredients

- Twelve eggs, half with whites
- Two pounds of sugar
- Cinnamon
- Two reales of milk

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs, half with whites and two pounds of sugar, until thick
2. Add a little cinnamon
3. Make cream with two reales of milk and mix with the beaten mixture
4. Place in the oven, sprinkling sugar on top
