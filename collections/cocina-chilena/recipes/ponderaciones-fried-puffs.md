## Ingredients

- four eggs
- four heaping tablespoons flour
- a little melted butter
- a little salt
- powdered sugar to taste
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs a little
2. Add four heaping tablespoons flour, a little melted butter, a little salt, and powdered sugar to taste
3. Add a little milk to form a pancake batter
4. Dip the ponderaciones mold into the batter, ensuring it enters only halfway into its height
5. Submerge in very hot lard
6. When the ponderación is golden, place on a serving dish and immediately sprinkle with sugar mixed with a little cinnamon
7. Note: the first two or three ponderaciones may not separate from the mold, but continuing the operation they will come out easily
