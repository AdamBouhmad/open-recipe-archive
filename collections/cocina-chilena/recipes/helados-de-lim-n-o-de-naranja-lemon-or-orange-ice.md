## Ingredients

- Fruit (lemon or orange)
- Sugar
- Water
- Gum arabic (for lemon variety)
- Egg white (for lemon variety)

## Directions

1. Peel the fruit and cut it to squeeze well
2. Add sugar made into syrup and the necessary water so it is not too acidic
3. For lemon ice, a little gum arabic dissolved in hot water and a well-beaten egg white can be added when it begins to set
