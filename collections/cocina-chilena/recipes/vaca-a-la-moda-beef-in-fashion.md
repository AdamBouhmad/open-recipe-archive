## Ingredients

- good piece of beef
- strips of bacon
- parsley
- minced onion
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- white wine
- bacon pieces
- small whole onions
- carrot slices
- whole pepper

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef, remove the bones
2. Lard it with strips of bacon, parsley and minced onion, salt, pepper and fine spices
3. Put white wine in the saucepan, bacon pieces, small whole onions, carrot slices, whole pepper and a little salt
4. Place the meat on these ingredients
5. Cover the pot well and put on low heat
6. Leave it for five or six hours and serve with all its seasoning
