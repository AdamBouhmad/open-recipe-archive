## Ingredients

- six hard eggs
- two tablespoons vinegar
- one tablespoon English mustard
- onion, very fine and well washed
- parsley, plenty
- celery, very finely chopped
- pickled vegetables
- salt
- pepper, peppery
- one quarter of a bottle of oil

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of the six hard eggs and dissolve them with two tablespoons of vinegar
2. Add one tablespoon of English mustard and a little very fine and well washed onion
3. Add plenty of finely chopped parsley and celery and some pickled vegetables
4. Season with salt and plenty of peppery pepper
5. Add one quarter of a bottle of oil
