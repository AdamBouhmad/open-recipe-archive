## Ingredients

- milk
- six ounces of sugar
- vanilla
- one pound of bread crumb
- a piece of butter
- two whole eggs
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Heat milk with six ounces of sugar and vanilla and bring to four boils
2. Add one pound of bread crumb to the hot milk
3. Drain and cool
4. Pass through a cloth and grind a piece of vanilla with a little strained milk and combine with the rest of the milk
5. Add a piece of butter, two whole eggs and four egg yolks
6. When combined, strain
7. Pour all into a platter and beat the four egg whites, stir and place in the oven
