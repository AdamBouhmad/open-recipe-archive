## Ingredients

- cod, soaked overnight
- potatoes, peeled
- carrots
- onion
- hard-boiled eggs
- egg yolk
- parsley, chopped
- fresh oregano
- ground pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak cod overnight, then cook and let cool
2. Cook peeled potatoes and carrots separately
3. Chop onion as for salad and partially cook it
4. Once everything is cooked, break cod into small pieces
5. Dice the carrot into slices, likewise the cooked potato
6. Squeeze the onion and add everything together
7. Cook hard-boiled eggs and cut into slices to place on top of the salad
8. Make a sauce with beaten egg yolk mixed with chopped parsley, fresh oregano, ground pepper, plenty of oil, vinegar and salt
9. Mix everything well and serve
