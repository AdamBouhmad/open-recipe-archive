## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Half a pound of refined powdered sugar
- Four ounces of butter
- Seven egg yolks
- Half a cup of milk
- Three pinches of salt
- Three pinches of anise
- A little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks as for sponge cake
2. Mix all ingredients until in state to knead
3. If too soft, add more flour
4. Knead well, cut and bake in not very hot oven
