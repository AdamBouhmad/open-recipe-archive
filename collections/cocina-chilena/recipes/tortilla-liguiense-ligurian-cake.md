## Ingredients

- Fourteen egg yolks and whites
- One pound of butter, beaten
- One pound of flour
- Twelve hard-boiled egg yolks, finely chopped
- Four ounces of sweet almonds
- Two ounces of bitter almonds, ground
- One pound of sugar
- Zest of two lemons
- Jam or cream for filling

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Spread on baking sheets in a round form
3. Make the dough the thickness of one finger
4. Bake in the oven
5. When cooked, layer the cakes in a dish, spreading jam or cream between each layer
6. Let cool in the air
