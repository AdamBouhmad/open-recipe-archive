## Ingredients

- Two well-cooked sweet potatoes
- A little milk
- Three or four tablespoons of flour
- Twelve eggs, yolks and whites separated
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Sugar to taste
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Strain the cooked sweet potatoes with a little milk
2. Add three or four tablespoons of flour
3. Beat twelve eggs, yolks and whites separately, and mix together with the sweet potato
4. Add a little cinnamon, lemon zest, and sugar to taste
5. Prepare the pudding mold with butter and bread crumbs
6. Place in the oven
