## Ingredients

- Lamb feet or testicles
- Onion
- Grease
- Rice
- Eggs
- Water

## Directions

1. Parboil the lamb feet or testicles
2. Fry a little onion in two spoonfuls of burned grease
3. Add the rice and fry it a little
4. Add the lamb feet or testicles with mashed eggs
5. Add plenty of water and let it boil
