## Ingredients

- butter, well beaten
- shallots or sorrel, crushed

## Directions

1. In a lightly heated serving dish put a good piece of well beaten butter
2. According to each person's taste, with crushed shallots or sorrel
3. When the mixture is completely made, place on top the fish or meat, which must have been kept constantly hot
