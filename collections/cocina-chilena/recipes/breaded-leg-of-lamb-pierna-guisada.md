## Ingredients

- Lamb leg
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- potatoes
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the leg as for a stew
2. After cooking, cut into large pieces
3. Roll in melted butter, then in salt, ground pepper and bread crumbs
4. Arrange in a dish
5. Add plenty of the sauce in which it cooked
6. Add cooked potatoes cut into slices and hard-boiled eggs also cut
7. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and remaining butter
8. Place in the oven to brown
