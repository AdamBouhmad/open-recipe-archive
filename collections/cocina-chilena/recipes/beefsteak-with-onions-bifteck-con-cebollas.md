## Ingredients

- Beefsteak
- Grease
- Salt
- Onion

## Directions

1. After preparing the meat, place it in a pan with burned grease, salt and finely chopped onion
2. Let it brown
3. Add the sauce and let it reduce somewhat on the fire
4. Remove and serve on a plate
