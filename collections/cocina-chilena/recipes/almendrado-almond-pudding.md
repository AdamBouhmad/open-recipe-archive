## Ingredients

- half pound of almonds
- milk
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- sugar
- one large tablespoon of ground and sifted rice
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Reduce half pound of almonds to paste, place in a napkin, pour boiling milk on top and squeeze to make the milk come out
2. Put to boil with some lemon peel, cinnamon, sugar and one large tablespoon well full of ground and sifted rice, not stopping to stir so the rice does not settle
3. When it has boiled well and is thick, beat four egg yolks a little, add to the milk and let it boil
4. A little later, pour into a serving dish and sprinkle cinnamon on top
