## Ingredients

- 1 to 1.5 dozen egg yolks
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 ounce isinglass
- 1 large bottle of milk
- egg whites beaten like meringue
- vanilla or orange blossom water
- sponge cake crumbs

## Directions

1. Put everything (egg yolks, sugar, isinglass, and milk) in a saucepan and place over regular heat
2. Stir continuously with a wooden spoon
3. When it begins to stick to the spoon, remove from heat and cool a little
4. Pour from one pan to another so it does not curdle
5. Mix the beaten egg whites like for meringues with the milk when already almost cold
6. Add vanilla or orange blossom water
7. Place some sponge cake crumbs on the bottom and sides of the mold, then pour the mixture
8. When solid, remove from the mold by first immersing it in cold water
