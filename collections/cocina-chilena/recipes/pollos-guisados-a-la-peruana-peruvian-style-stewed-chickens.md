## Ingredients

- chicken
- pork lard
- ham slices
- green peppers
- parsley
- tomatoes
- onions
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- vinegar
- raisins
- toasted and crushed almonds
- capers
- fried plantain slices
- two small cups chicken broth

## Directions

1. Pluck well and pass a flaming paper over it to burn any remaining down
2. Open and remove the entrails and clean well, then cut into quarters
3. Fry in lard with ham slices
4. When half browned, add green peppers in pieces, parsley, tomatoes, and onions, all chopped
5. Fry all this with the chickens, adding a little cinnamon, pepper, and ground clove with some vinegar
6. When the chickens are half cooked, add raisins, toasted and crushed almonds, whole capers, fried plantain slices, and two small cups of chicken broth
7. Let simmer, ensuring the sauce becomes half thick
