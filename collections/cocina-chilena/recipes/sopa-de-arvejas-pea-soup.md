## Ingredients

- one hen
- salt
- celery
- leeks
- parsley
- lettuce leaves
- raw peas
- butter
- two tablespoons flour
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the hen with salt, celery and leeks until tender
2. Clean parsley and lettuce leaves and grind together with raw peas on a stone
3. Put a piece of butter and two tablespoons flour in a saucepan
4. When melted and combined with the flour, add the ground vegetables and let heat for a while
5. Pour the hen broth over it and let boil
6. It should be somewhat thick
7. Strain into the soup tureen and can add small pieces of bread fried in butter and hen pieces
