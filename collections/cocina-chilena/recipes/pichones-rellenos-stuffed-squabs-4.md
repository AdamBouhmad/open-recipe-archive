## Ingredients

- squabs
- veal
- poultry breast
- bacon
- onion fried
- eggs
- parsley
- pepper
- cumin

## Directions

1. Remove the wings and necks, trim the tail, and make a fine hash of veal, poultry breast, bacon and fried onion, eggs and parsley, ground pepper and cumin
2. Stuff two squabs with this mixture and cross their legs over the opening to prevent the filling from coming out
3. Then wrap them in brown paper and roast them in a pot between two slow fires
