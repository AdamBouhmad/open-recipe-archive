## Ingredients

- Hen to be stewed
- Tender cabbage, chopped
- Half a glass of white wine
- Seasonings
- Pepper
- Cloves
- A little chili

## Directions

1. Place the hen to be stewed in a saucepan in which tender cabbage will be chopped
2. Pour over it half a glass of white wine, seasonings, pepper, cloves and a little chili
3. Cover the saucepan and cook on low heat
