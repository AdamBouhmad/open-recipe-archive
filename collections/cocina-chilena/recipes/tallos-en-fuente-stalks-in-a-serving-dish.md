## Ingredients

- stalks
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the stalks without peeling them. When they are tender, peel them and cut into small pieces.
2. Place them in a serving dish with a piece of butter and breadcrumbs on top.
3. Put in the oven to brown them.
