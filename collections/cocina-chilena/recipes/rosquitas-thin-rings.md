## Ingredients

- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 6 whole eggs
- 1 teaspoon yeast
- cinnamon
- flour
- more than 1 ounce butter
- burned fat

## Directions

1. Add 6 whole eggs to 1 pound powdered sugar and beat a little
2. Add 1 teaspoon yeast, plenty of cinnamon, and sufficient flour to make a dough, with more than 1 ounce butter and burned fat
3. Form thin ring shapes
4. Place in boiling water until they rise to the surface of the water
5. Remove and place on baking sheets
6. Bake in the oven
