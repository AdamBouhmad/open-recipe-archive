## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one pound of rice
- two ounces of butter
- five eggs
- a little milk
- egg white
- a little water
- almond, finely ground mixed with sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough of regular thickness from all these ingredients, as for puff pastry
2. Cut the cookies, brushing them with a beaten egg and a little water
3. Brush others with a little milk using a feather and sprinkle on top a little ground almond mixed with sugar
4. Place on butter-lined baking sheets in an oven heated for sponge cake
