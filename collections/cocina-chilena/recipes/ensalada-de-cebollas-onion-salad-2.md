## Ingredients

- onion
- ground pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut onion thinly
2. Put in a pot with cold water
3. Heat carefully without cooking it through
4. Remove and wash in cold water
5. Squeeze well
6. Dress as with the previous salads
