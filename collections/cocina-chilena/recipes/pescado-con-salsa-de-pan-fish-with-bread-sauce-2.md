## Ingredients

- pescado (fish)
- pan (bread)
- papas (potatoes)
- harina (flour)
- caldo or agua caliente (broth or hot water)
- cebollas (onions)
- pimienta (pepper)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Fry the fish.
2. Break bread into pieces and cut potatoes into thin slices; coat the potatoes in flour.
3. Place the potatoes in a pot and put the fish on top.
4. Grind the bread and add a little broth or hot water, minced onions, pepper, and vinegar.
5. Put the fish on top and keep it on the fire until serving time.
