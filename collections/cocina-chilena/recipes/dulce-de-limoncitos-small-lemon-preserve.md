## Ingredients

- limoncitos (small lemons)
- salt
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Scrape these with glass to remove the bitter coating and place in cold water so they do not turn black
2. Make a cross cut on the side of the stem and place to precook in boiling water with a little salt
3. After precooked, drain in 2 or 3 cold waters until all salt and acid are gone
4. Place to drain in a basket
5. Then add to syrup that should be cold and of light consistency
6. Give 4 boilings, letting 2 days pass between each one, at the fourth give final point
7. For 1 pound of precook and drained limoncitos, add 1 1/2 pounds sugar
8. If the syrup is seen to be insufficient, 1 or 2 pounds sugar can be added, as it seems best
