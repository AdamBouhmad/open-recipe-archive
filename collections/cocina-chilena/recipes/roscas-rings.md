## Ingredients

- sixteen eggs with six and a half egg whites
- half pound sugar
- one large tablespoon lard
- sufficient flour

## Directions

1. Beat sixteen eggs with six and a half egg whites well
2. Add one large tablespoon lard
3. Add sufficient flour to make a regular dough
4. Knead, form rings, and place in the oven
