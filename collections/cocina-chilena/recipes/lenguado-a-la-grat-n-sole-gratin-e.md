## Ingredients

- sole
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- white wine
- brandy
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the sole well
2. Put butter kneaded with flour in the serving dish to be used for cooking
3. Add to this mixture parsley, onions and mushrooms, all finely chopped and seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Place the fish on top, putting another layer equal to the one below and surrounding it with half a glass of broth, white wine or one tablespoon of brandy
5. Cover the dish and place in the oven
6. Can also be topped and bottomed with bread crumbs and put fire on the lid so it sticks to the bottom of the dish, which is the Gratinée preparation
