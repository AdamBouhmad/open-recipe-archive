## Ingredients

- two carrots
- one onion
- butter
- two leaves of cabbage
- four to six leaves of chard

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots into thin slices
2. Cut the onion into very thin and small pieces and fry in a little butter
3. Chop two leaves of cabbage, four to six leaves of chard and put to cook in boiling water
