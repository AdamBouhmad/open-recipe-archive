## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- lard
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- bread crumbs
- flour
- white wine
- pickles

## Directions

1. Cut, prepare and flatten the ribs and place in the roaster with a little lard
2. While roasting, cover them with breadcrumbs mixed with salt, pepper and fine herbs
3. Remove when done, adding grated bread, a little flour and a glass of white wine
4. Let it reduce a little and add pickles cut into slices
