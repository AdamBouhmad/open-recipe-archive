## Ingredients

- peas, raw
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Shell the raw peas well and then pass them through a sieve
2. Fry onion in butter
3. Once the onion is fried, mix it with the peas and continue frying until done
4. If you want to make this sauce with meat or poultry, cook it in very little water and add the resulting broth to the sauce, being careful that it does not remain too light
