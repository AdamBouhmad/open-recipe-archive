## Ingredients

- One pound of almonds
- Four egg yolks
- Cinnamon infusion

## Directions

1. Make a light almond milk (horchata) from one pound of almonds and place on the fire
2. When it boils, add four well-beaten egg yolks that have first been dissolved in a little of the same almond milk
3. Leave it on the fire for a while and beat with a chocolate mill (molinillo)
4. When the egg is cooked, remove it, sweeten, and add a little cinnamon infusion
