## Ingredients

- beer bread
- milk
- eight egg yolks
- five egg whites
- four ounces powdered sugar
- four ounces ground almond
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- raisins
- half ounce butter

## Directions

1. Soak a beer bread in milk for two hours and pass it through a sieve
2. Beat eight yolks with five whites and four ounces powdered sugar
3. Add four ounces ground almond, lemon zest, cinnamon, raisins, and half ounce butter
4. Mix everything together with the bread and pour into a mold prepared with butter and breadcrumbs
5. Place in the oven
