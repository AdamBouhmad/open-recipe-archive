## Ingredients

- veal brains
- bacon strips
- white wine
- broth
- carrots
- parsley
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Remove the coagulated blood, veins and tissue covering the brains
2. Place in warm water to clean them
3. Cover with two bacon strips and cook in two glasses of white wine and the same amount of broth with carrots, parsley, onions, thyme, bay leaf, pepper and salt
4. After cooking, place butter in another pot and sauté the liquid in which the brains were cooked, strained through a sieve
5. Add some mushrooms and let everything cook together
