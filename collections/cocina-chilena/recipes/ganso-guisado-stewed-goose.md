## Ingredients

- One goose
- Broth
- Apple
- A little burnt flour
- Lemon zest
- Raisins
- Almonds
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces and cook the goose
2. In a little broth in which the pieces have been cooked, add chopped apple, a little burnt flour, lemon zest, raisins and some almonds
3. When the apples are very soft, add the pieces to this sauce and add a little sugar
4. Let it boil for a moment and serve
