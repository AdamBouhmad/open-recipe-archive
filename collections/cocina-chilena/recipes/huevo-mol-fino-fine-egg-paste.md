## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour, well reduced
- 4 ounces almonds reduced to paste
- 14 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the almonds reduced to paste
2. Lower the pan when the syrup is at good point
3. Add the egg yolks all at once and stir well
4. Place over low heat, scraping the bottom so it does not stick
5. It should move very slowly and will be done when it fries from the bottom and separates from the sides
