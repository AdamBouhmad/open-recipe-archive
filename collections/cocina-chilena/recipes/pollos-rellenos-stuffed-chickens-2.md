## Ingredients

- Gizzard, chopped
- Liver
- A little meat
- A little chopped onion
- Bread crumbs
- Parsley
- Eggs (the egg and meat cooked)
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- Two tablespoons of broth
- One small tablespoon of flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Chop the gizzard, liver and a little meat
2. Add a little chopped onion, bread crumbs, parsley and eggs (the egg and meat cooked) and with this stuff the chickens
3. Put in a saucepan one tablespoon of vinegar, two of broth, one small tablespoon of flour and boil with butter
