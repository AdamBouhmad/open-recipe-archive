## Ingredients

- bird
- one tablespoon flour
- butter
- broth
- cheese
- one glass cognac or sherry

## Directions

1. Cook the bird
2. In the same broth make the sauce by dissolving one tablespoon flour in a little butter
3. Add the necessary broth so it reaches a regular thickness
4. Arrange the bird in pieces on a platter
5. Pour the sauce over it and add one glass of cognac or sherry
6. Grate cheese so the pieces are well covered
7. Place in the oven
