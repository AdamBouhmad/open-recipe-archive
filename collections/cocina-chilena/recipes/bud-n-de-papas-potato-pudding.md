## Ingredients

- Two pounds of potatoes with skin
- Half a pound of melted butter
- One glass of cream
- Six whole eggs
- Half a pound of ground sugar
- One glass of white wine
- A little salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of potatoes with skins
2. Mash in half a pound of melted butter, mixing well
3. Add one glass of cream
4. Beat six whole eggs and add
5. Add half a pound of ground sugar, one glass of white wine, a little salt, nutmeg, and other seasonings
