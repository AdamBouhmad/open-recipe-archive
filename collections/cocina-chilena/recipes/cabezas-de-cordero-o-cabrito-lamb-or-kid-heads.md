## Ingredients

- lamb or kid heads
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolk
- bacon
- lemon juice
- butter
- twine
- paper

## Directions

1. Split heads in half and stuff with a filling prepared as follows: chop some parsley and add salt, pepper, an egg yolk and bacon, sauté the mixture in a pan with butter
2. Once the heads are filled, coat the outside with lemon juice and butter, then tie with twine and wrap in paper
3. Place on the spit and leave until tender
