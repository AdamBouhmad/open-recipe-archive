## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- parsley
- cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- pickled gherkins
- anchovies
- salt
- oil
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the top point of the tomato to extract the seeds with a teaspoon and squeeze out the water with a light pressure of the fingers.
2. Fill with a paste made of finely chopped parsley, cucumbers, hard-boiled eggs, capers, and pickled gherkins, along with anchovies in small pieces, salt, and oil.
3. Once the tomato is filled, wrap it in breadcrumbs and put in the oven.
