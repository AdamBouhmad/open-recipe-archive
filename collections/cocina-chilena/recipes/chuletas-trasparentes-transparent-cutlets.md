## Ingredients

- rib meat
- grated bread
- parsley
- pepper
- 2 hard eggs
- egg white
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Remove meat from the ribs and arrange in the shape of a chop
2. Prepare some grated bread, parsley, pepper and 2 hard eggs according to the amount of meat
3. Wrap the chops in this preparation and then pass through a lightly beaten egg white
4. Fry in butter or not very hot fat
