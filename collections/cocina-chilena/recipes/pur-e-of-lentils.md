## Ingredients

- 1 pound of lentils
- broth from puchero
- a little bacon
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions
- butter
- 2 cloves

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of lentils in puchero broth with a little bacon, 2 carrots, 2 onions thinned and sautéed in butter, and 2 cloves
2. When everything is cooked, pass through a sieve
3. Thin the purée with broth
4. Cook in a pot to skim it
5. Serve over fried bread crusts
6. This purée can be made for fast days by substituting oil for the bacon fat
