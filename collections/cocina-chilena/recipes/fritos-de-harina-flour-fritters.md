## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- butter
- five egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a cooked paste with flour, salt, and butter
2. When it pulls away well from the pan, let it cool
3. Add five egg yolks
4. When ready to fry, mix in beaten egg whites
5. Drop by small spoonfuls into the pan
6. Strike with a stick so they puff up
