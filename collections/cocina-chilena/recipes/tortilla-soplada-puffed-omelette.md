## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Sugar
- Lemon peel
- Butter
- Beef fat

## Directions

1. Break eggs separating the whites and yolks
2. Dust the yolks with powdered sugar, adding finely chopped lemon peel
3. Mix everything, then beat the whites and unite them to the yolks and mix as well as possible
4. Put sufficient butter in a pan and place over low heat
5. Once the butter is melted, add the eggs, stir the omelette so the liquid stays on top
6. When you see the butter is absorbed, give it good form and pour it onto a platter spread with beef fat, which is placed over hot ashes
7. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top of the omelette and put in the oven
