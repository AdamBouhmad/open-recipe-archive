## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- water
- oil
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Place in a pot of water slightly warmer than lukewarm
2. After breaking the tusks, rub with the hand to remove the bristles, putting it in and taking it out of the water several times until all the hair disappears
3. Remove the hooves
4. Remove the belly being careful not to remove the kidneys
5. Make some cuts in the neck and other larger ones on the back
6. Introduce the tail into the skin and flesh
7. Gather the legs and shoulders by means of two iron skewers passed one through the thigh and the other through the chest
8. Place immediately for twenty-four hours in cold water
9. Hang and let it dry
10. Cover the belly with flour mixed with lard and put on the spit
11. While on the fire, brush with good oil using feathers
12. After roasting, make other incisions in the neck
