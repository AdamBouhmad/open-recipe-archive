## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sliced peaches
- 2 pounds sugar
- Juice of 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Place the sliced peaches to boil for a little while and then pass them through a sieve.
2. To this add 2 pounds of sugar made into syrup and the juice of four lemons; mix everything and freeze it.
