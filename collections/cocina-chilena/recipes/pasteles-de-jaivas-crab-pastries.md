## Ingredients

- Onion
- Butter
- Crab meat
- Three hard-boiled eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Broth
- Sugar
- Puff pastry
- Crab shells
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Fat

## Directions

1. Mince onion very finely, wash and fry in butter
2. When fried, add the crab meat, three finely minced hard-boiled eggs and breadcrumbs
3. When slightly fried, add broth so it reaches regular thickness and add a little sugar
4. Empty into a dish and cover with puff pastry, place in the oven
5. Alternatively, fill well-washed crab shells, roll in eggs and bread crumbs and fry in very hot fat
