## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- onions
- seasonings
- beaten egg
- grated cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage finely and cook it with salt
2. Remove and squeeze it well
3. Make a sofrito of onions and seasonings
4. Add the cabbage to this
5. Fry it well for an hour
6. Add beaten egg and grated cheese if desired
7. Serve
