## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- ground meat
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- grated lemon
- capers sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue until very tender
2. Split it lengthwise in half
3. Make a mixture of ground meat, breadcrumbs, eggs, butter, salt and grated lemon
4. Place this mixture on one half of the tongue and cover with the other half
5. Roll in egg and breadcrumbs
6. Pour melted butter on it and place in the oven
7. Serve with capers sauce
