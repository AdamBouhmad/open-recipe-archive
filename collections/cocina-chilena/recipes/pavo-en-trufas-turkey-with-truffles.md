## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Two pounds truffles, cleaned and drained
- One pound bacon, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Aromatic herbs
- Truffle peelings, crushed

## Directions

1. In a casserole, combine one pound chopped bacon, salt, pepper, nutmeg, aromatic herbs, and crushed truffle peelings
2. Keep over fire for half an hour
3. Insert this mixture into the well-emptied turkey through the crop and tie
4. Prepare turkey this way two days before roasting so it has time to absorb the truffle flavor
5. Place on a roasting spit for two hours until tender
6. Let it brown and serve on a platter decorated with the reserved truffle peelings
