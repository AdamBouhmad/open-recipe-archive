## Ingredients

- Clean hen
- Three onion heads of regular size
- Half a small cup of oil
- Half a small cup of wine
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Celery
- One clove of garlic
- A little rosemary

## Directions

1. Put the clean hen in a pot
2. Add three onion heads of regular size, half a small cup of oil, half a small cup of wine, pepper, parsley, celery, one clove of garlic and a little rosemary
3. Seal the lid with paste and put on regular heat
