## Ingredients

- One plate of rice
- Four tablespoons of oil
- A little saffron
- Onion in slices
- Tomato
- Pepper
- Broth
- Roasted peppers
- Green celery sprigs

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well and place it in a pan with four tablespoons of oil and a little saffron
2. Also add sliced onion, tomato and pepper, all fried in oil
3. When the rice grain begins to open, fill the pan with broth and let it boil until all the broth is consumed
4. The rice should remain so dry that it separates into grains
5. The soup can also be decorated with roasted peppers filled with rice from the soup and green celery sprigs
