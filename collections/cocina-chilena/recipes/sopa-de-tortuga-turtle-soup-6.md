## Ingredients

- Two pounds turtle meat, well washed
- Three ounces of butter
- Two bay leaves
- Salt to taste
- Onions, chopped
- A small bundle of parsley, chopped
- Two cloves
- Ground pepper
- Toasted and crushed hazelnuts
- Four plates of stock
- One glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Fry the cleaned turtle meat in a pot with three ounces of butter.
2. Add two bay leaves, appropriate salt, and chopped onions.
3. When everything is fried, add a small bundle of chopped parsley, two cloves, ground pepper, toasted and crushed hazelnuts, four plates of stock and one glass of white wine.
4. Season and let simmer until the meat is cooked.
5. Serve.
