## Ingredients

- Whole hen
- Onion, whole
- Celery branches
- Peppercorns
- Potatoes, whole and peeled
- Carrot slices
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- Two tablespoons of oil
- Two tablespoons of broth

## Directions

1. Put the whole hen in a saucepan after washing well
2. Add whole onion, celery branches, peppercorns, whole peeled potatoes, carrot slices, one tablespoon of vinegar, two of oil and two of broth
3. Put on the fire well covered
4. Move from time to time so it doesn't stick
