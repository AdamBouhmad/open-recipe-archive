## Ingredients

- Stock from puchero or meat extract
- Semolina

## Directions

1. Cook the stock from puchero or meat extract and add the semolina.
2. Note that this pasta is more prone to clumping, so it must be stirred more continuously than other pastas.
3. Cook for half an hour until done.
4. For semolina in milk, prepare the same way but use milk instead of stock.
