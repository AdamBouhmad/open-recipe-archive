## Ingredients

- pork feet
- wine
- water
- pepper
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- salt
- vinegar
- egg
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and cook with one part wine and two parts water.
2. Make a marinade with pepper, parsley, thyme, bay leaf, salt and a little vinegar.
3. If serving this way, serve very hot.
4. To fry them, coat with a batter of beaten egg and flour and a dusting of sugar.
