## Ingredients

- vinegar
- salt
- chili
- cumin
- garlic
- oregano

## Directions

1. Season a regular vinegar with salt, chili, cumin, garlic and a little oregano
2. Place the loins there for two days
3. Remove and place in a heavier press than that used for guachalomos
