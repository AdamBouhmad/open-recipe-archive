## Ingredients

- Fava beans
- Water
- Salt
- Oil
- Roasted hazelnuts
- Toasted bread
- Water
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Eggs

## Directions

1. When small, remove only the ends
2. When large, peel entirely
3. Boil in water and salt
4. Drain and drizzle with oil
5. Make a sauce with roasted hazelnuts, a little toasted bread soaked in water and vinegar, pepper, and eggs corresponding to the sauce
6. Dilute the sauce with seasoned water
7. Pour over the beans and bring to a boil on gentle heat
