## Ingredients

- Apples or peaches
- Cold water
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon sticks
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the fruits and remove the core without damaging them
2. Place in cold water to prevent browning
3. Cook with a glass of cold water, lemon peel, cinnamon sticks, and sugar
4. When the fruit is cooked, remove and place in a compote dish
5. Return the syrup to the heat and reduce it to a jelly
6. Pour into a plate and let cool
7. Once cold, dip the plate in boiling water to loosen the jelly
8. Cover the fruit with this jelly and serve cold
