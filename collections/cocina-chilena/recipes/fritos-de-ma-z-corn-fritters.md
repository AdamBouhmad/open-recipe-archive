## Ingredients

- corn
- salt
- sugar
- three egg yolks
- flour (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut and grind corn
2. If too coarse, strain through a coarse sieve
3. Fry in a little lard
4. When cooked, add a little salt and sugar to taste
5. When slightly warm, add three egg yolks
6. If they do not bind well when frying, add a little flour to the corn
