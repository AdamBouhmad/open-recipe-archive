## Ingredients

- one hen
- onion slices
- two tablespoons flour
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook the hen with onion slices
2. When the broth is good, remove the fat from the top
3. Dissolve two tablespoons of flour in this fat and add enough broth for the soup
4. Add the hen meat to the broth
5. At the time of sending to the table, add a glass of white wine
