## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 24 egg yolks
- milk
- desired flavorings

## Directions

1. Beat 24 egg yolks with 1 pound sugar until well thickened with desired flavorings
2. Pour everything into a regular-sized pastry pan and fill with milk
3. Place at the oven mouth, stirring until well heated
4. Push into the oven and leave until baked
