## Ingredients

- artichokes
- ground meat
- sausages
- shallots
- celery
- parsley
- aromatics
- onion
- breadcrumbs
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and cut off the top with a knife
2. Round them well and remove the heart from the middle
3. Prepare a filling composed of ground meat, sausages, shallots, celery, parsley, all kinds of aromatics, onion and breadcrumbs, all finely chopped
4. Fill the artichokes, arrange them in a dish, cover each one with a little oil and the remaining filling
5. Blanch them before filling
6. Cook them covered in steam
