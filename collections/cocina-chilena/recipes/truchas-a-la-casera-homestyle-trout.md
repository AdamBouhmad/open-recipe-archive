## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- white wine
- butter
- flour
- onion
- parsley
- mint
- cloves
- basil
- thyme

## Directions

1. Cover the trout with salt one hour before cooking
2. Cook at high heat with white wine, butter and flour, onion, parsley, mint, cloves, basil and thyme
3. Once cooked, set aside and serve in the same sauce, removing the onion and thyme
