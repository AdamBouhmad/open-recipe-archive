## Ingredients

- one hard egg yolk
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- oil
- olives, anchovies, or smoked herring fillets (optional)

## Directions

1. Dissolve one hard egg yolk in a few drops of vinegar with pepper and salt
2. Finish dissolving by pouring oil drop by drop while always stirring
3. When the mixture is well made clarify it to the consistency of sauce by adding vinegar little by little and always stirring
4. Olives, anchovies or smoked herring fillets can be added
