## Ingredients

- Fat duck
- Large whole peeled potatoes
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Bread crumbs
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Celery, finely chopped
- Broth

## Directions

1. Place a fat duck in a casserole
2. Add whole peeled potatoes, a piece of butter, lemon juice, bread crumbs, and finely chopped parsley and celery
3. Cover with broth
4. Cover the casserole well and place over low heat
