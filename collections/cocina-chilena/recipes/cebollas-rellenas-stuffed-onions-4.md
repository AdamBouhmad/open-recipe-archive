## Ingredients

- Ground lean meat
- Fat bacon
- Parsley
- Fried onion
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt
- Pine nuts
- Spices
- Grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of ground lean meat, fat bacon, parsley, fried onion, breadcrumbs, salt, pine nuts, spices, and grated cheese
2. Remove the inner layers of the onions with a knife and stuff them
3. Place in a pot without pressing them down
4. Cook and serve
