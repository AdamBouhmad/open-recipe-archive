## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- parsley
- ground pepper
- hard-boiled eggs
- olives

## Directions

1. Cook cauliflower in boiling salted water
2. When cooked, remove from water and cool
3. Chop finely or as desired
4. Add parsley, ground pepper, hard-boiled eggs in slices, and olives
5. Dress to taste as the others
