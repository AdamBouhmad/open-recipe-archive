## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- butter
- cheese
- grated bread
- parsley
- pepper
- onion head

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs into pieces and place in a pot
2. Add a little butter, cheese and grated bread, parsley, pepper and an onion head cut in pieces
3. Place over gentle heat
