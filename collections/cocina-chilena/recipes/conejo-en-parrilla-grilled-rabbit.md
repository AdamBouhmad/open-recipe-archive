## Ingredients

- rabbit
- butter
- fine herbs or anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Open lengthwise after gutting and flatten with the flat of the knife.
2. Place on the grill, surrounded by a sheet of paper spread with butter.
3. When done, remove the paper and serve mixed with fine herbs or with anchovy butter if available.
