## Ingredients

- pork pieces
- lard
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- egg yolks
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into larger pieces
2. After roasting, pass it through lard
3. Moisten with water or broth and add salt, pepper, and a bay leaf
4. Make a liaison of egg yolks and add lemon juice at the time of serving
