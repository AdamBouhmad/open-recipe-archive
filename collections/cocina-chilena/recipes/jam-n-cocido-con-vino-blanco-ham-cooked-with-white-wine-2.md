## Ingredients

- ham
- white wine
- cinnamon strips
- whole cloves
- bay leaves
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak the ham for twelve or more hours.
2. Cut off the shank and boil in water for at least an hour and a half.
3. Remove and drain, then place in a pot or suitable vessel with white wine to cover it.
4. Add some cinnamon strips, half a dozen whole cloves and some bay leaves.
5. If sweetness is desired, add one pound of sugar.
6. Can be served cold, but sprinkle sugar on top and toast it with a hot iron.
