## Ingredients

- thick end of loin
- thin strips of bacon
- butter
- four carrots
- four onions
- parsley sprigs
- celery sprigs
- two bay leaves
- oregano
- salt
- two glasses of water
- whole pepper
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Take the thickest end of a loin and cut thin strips of bacon
2. Put them on one side of the loin and arrange it in a saucepan buttered, making sure the side with the bacon goes down
3. Add four carrots, four onions, parsley and celery sprigs, two bay leaves, a little oregano, salt, two glasses of water and some whole pepper
4. If the piece of meat is of regular size, boil for three hours
5. When the loin is somewhat softened, increase the heat to reduce the sauce
6. A little before serving, remove the carrots, onions, pieces of bacon and meat
7. Dissolve a spoonful of flour in the broth and add a glass of water
8. Let this sauce cook and pour it over the meat which should be arranged on a platter with carrots and onions
