## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Sugar for syrup
- Water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup
2. Before it thickens, add peeled apricots, whole or halved
3. Let them cook and remove the foam
4. Remove from heat and arrange in a compote dish
5. Bring the syrup to the proper consistency and pour over the fruit
