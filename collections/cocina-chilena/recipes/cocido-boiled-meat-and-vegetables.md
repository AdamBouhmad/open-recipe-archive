## Ingredients

- water
- chickpeas
- beef or mutton
- ham
- bacon
- blood sausage
- salt
- chicken
- meat scraps

## Directions

1. In a pot proportionate to the food to be cooked, put water and when hot add chickpeas and well-washed, well-skinned meat, adding for each pound of beef or mutton half a pound of chickpeas
2. When it begins to boil, skim, being careful not to overdo it so as not to deprive the cocido of its substance
3. After two hours, a little ham, bacon and small blood sausage can be added
4. Let everything boil over low heat, seasoning with salt and adding warm water from time to time
5. If half a chicken, meat scraps, etc. are added to this mixture, with such a simple and easy method the best that exists in ordinary cocido will be achieved
6. The broth serves to improve all stews in which a substance is needed without resorting to water
7. The vegetables are cooked separately with bacon or chorizo
