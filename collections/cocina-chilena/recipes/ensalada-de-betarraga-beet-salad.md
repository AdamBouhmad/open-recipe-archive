## Ingredients

- beets
- onion
- ground pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook beets until done
2. Cool and peel them
3. Cut into thin slices
4. Cut onion thinly and blanch it
5. Wash the onion in cold water and squeeze well
6. Add to the beets
7. Season with ground pepper and parsley
8. Dress to taste
