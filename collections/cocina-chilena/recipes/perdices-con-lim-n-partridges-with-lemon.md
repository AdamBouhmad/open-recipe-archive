## Ingredients

- raw partridges
- butter
- onion
- lemon juice
- pepper
- parsley
- oregano
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put the raw partridges in the pot
2. Take a little more than one tablespoon of butter, one onion, the juice of half a lemon, pepper, parsley and oregano
3. A little while before serving, beat two egg yolks
4. When they are beaten, add the juice of the other half lemon and pour this sauce over the partridges
5. Move the pot well so it does not stick and the sauce cooks
