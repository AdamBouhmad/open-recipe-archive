## Ingredients

- Milk
- Cinnamon
- Vanilla
- Lemon zest
- Sugar
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Make an infusion in a little boiling milk with cinnamon, vanilla, and a piece of lemon zest
2. Add the milk necessary for two pounds of sugar
3. Also add the juice of half a pound of almonds, which have been ground with the same milk
