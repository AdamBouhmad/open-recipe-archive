## Ingredients

- corvina or conger
- onion
- tomatoes
- corn
- aromatics
- broth
- oil

## Directions

1. Put in an earthenware dish, covered, chopped onion, tomatoes and corn and all kinds of aromatics
2. Then add the fish divided into pieces
3. Cover with another layer of the same mixture, cover well and cook
4. If it dries too much, add broth until it finishes cooking
5. At the time of serving, if desired, add oil
