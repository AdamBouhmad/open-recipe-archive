## Ingredients

- piece of loin
- half tablespoon of butter or color
- breadcrumbs
- broth
- pepper
- salt
- bay leaves
- half glass of wine

## Directions

1. Place a piece of loin in a casserole with half a tablespoon of butter or color and let it brown
2. Add a little breadcrumbs, broth, pepper, salt, bay leaves and half a glass of wine
3. Simmer until tender
