## Ingredients

- chicken giblets
- chicken liver
- some chicken meat
- small amount of chopped onion
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- eggs
- one tablespoon vinegar
- two tablespoons broth
- small tablespoon flour
- sufficient butter

## Directions

1. Chop the giblets, liver, and some meat
2. Mix with chopped onion, breadcrumbs, parsley, and cooked eggs and meat
3. Stuff the chickens with this mixture
4. In a pot combine one tablespoon vinegar, two tablespoons broth, and a small amount of flour
5. Bring to boil with sufficient butter
6. Place the raw chickens in the pot and cook
