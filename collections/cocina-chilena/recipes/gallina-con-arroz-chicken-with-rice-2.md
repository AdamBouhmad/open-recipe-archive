## Ingredients

- Hen
- Well-washed rice
- Salt
- Onion in large pieces
- Peppercorns
- Water
- One tablespoon of color

## Directions

1. Cook the hen with well-washed rice, salt, onion in large pieces and peppercorns
2. Calculate sufficient water for the rice to cook well, but so it is not clear
3. Once cooked, add one tablespoon of color
