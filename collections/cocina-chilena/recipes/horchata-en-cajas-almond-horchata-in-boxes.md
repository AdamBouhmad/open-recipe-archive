## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds peeled almonds
- Slightly more than half a liter of water
- 4 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Grind the peeled almonds like paste and place in a basin, add slightly more than half a liter of water and stir well.
2. Strain it and add an equal quantity of water to the coarse almonds; strain again and add this milk to the syrup when it is crystallizing and let it boil over high fire.
3. The syrup is made from 4 pounds of sugar; it is done well when placing a little on a plate it does not run.
4. The box should be left three days uncovered so it forms a crust.
