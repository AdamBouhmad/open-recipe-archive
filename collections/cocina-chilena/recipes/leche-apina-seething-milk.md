## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- twenty-four egg yolks
- flavoring to taste
- milk

## Directions

1. Beat one pound of sugar with twenty-four egg yolks until they are well thickened with the flavorings desired
2. Pour into a regular-sized pastry pan and fill with milk
3. Place at the oven mouth stirring until it is well heated
4. Then push it inside the oven
