## Ingredients

- egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, put an egg yolk, salt, pepper and some drops of vinegar
2. Beat well to mix
3. Add oil drop by drop, always without stopping stirring
4. Incorporate vinegar, adding it little by little and without ceasing to stir the beaten mixture
5. This sauce needs a quarter hour of beating to be made
6. Serve with all kinds of cold meat and poultry
