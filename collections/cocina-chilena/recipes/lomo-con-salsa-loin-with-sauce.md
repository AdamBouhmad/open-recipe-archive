## Ingredients

- piece of meat
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- celery sprigs
- parsley
- large onion
- butter or color
- flour
- vinegar
- broth
- French mustard

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of meat with salt, pepper, vinegar, celery sprigs and parsley
2. Cut a large onion into slices and fry in butter or color until well fried and golden
3. Add a spoonful of flour and another of vinegar and a cup of broth
4. Let it cook
5. At the time of placing it on the meat, add a spoonful of French mustard diluted in broth
