## Ingredients

- Squash
- Tender squash
- Corn
- Tomato
- Onion
- Spices
- Cheese
- Eggs
- Fat

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and remove the seeds
2. Chop finely with a little tender squash
3. Cook and when done, drain the water
4. Empty into a dry dish and break up with a ladle
5. Make a fry in fat with tender corn, tomato, onion, and spices
6. Add the squash and place on low heat
7. Grate cheese, beat some eggs, and add this before serving
