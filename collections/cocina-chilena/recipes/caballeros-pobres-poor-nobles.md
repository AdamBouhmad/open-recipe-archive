## Ingredients

- Beer bread
- Milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Eggs
- Flour
- Butter
- Fat
- Sugar or honey

## Directions

1. Cut a loaf of beer bread into slices and soak them from the night before in milk seasoned with sugar and cinnamon
2. At the time of frying, make a beaten mixture of four eggs with a little flour
3. Pass the slices through it and fry in butter or fat
4. Sprinkle with sugar or bee honey on top
