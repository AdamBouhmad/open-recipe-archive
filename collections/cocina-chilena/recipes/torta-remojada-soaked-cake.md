## Ingredients

- Twelve eggs
- Half pound of refined powdered sugar
- Half pound of flour
- Three pounds of sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs with half pound of powdered sugar until very white, using a wooden spoon and copper spatula
2. When well combined, add half pound of flour and mix until fully incorporated
3. Place in a greased earthenware dish with a sheet of paper underneath
4. Bake in a moderate oven
5. Once cooked, remove from paper and scrape any stuck portions with a knife
6. Return to the same dish
7. Make a syrup with three pounds of sugar
8. Prick the cake with a thin stick and gradually pour hot syrup over it so it penetrates throughout
9. When cold, dust with sugar and cinnamon
