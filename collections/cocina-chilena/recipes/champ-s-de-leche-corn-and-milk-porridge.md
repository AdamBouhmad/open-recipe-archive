## Ingredients

- 1 quarter of corn kernels (mote maiz)
- 2 reales of milk
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 pound ground rice
- a little rice cooked with very fine cinnamon
- half a pound of peeled almonds, cut very finely
- cinnamon and almonds or whole corn for topping

## Directions

1. Take the corn kernels and boil until they begin to fall apart
2. Drain the water in which they were cooked
3. Without letting it cool, add to the milk with the sugar
4. Once the milk has boiled, add the ground rice so that it becomes as smooth as possible
5. Once it comes to its first boil, place on low heat so it boils slowly until it is heated through
6. Add a little rice cooked with very fine cinnamon
7. Shortly before removing from heat, add the finely cut peeled almonds
8. Pour into a serving dish and top with cinnamon and almonds or whole corn
