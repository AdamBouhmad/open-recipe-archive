## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut a portion of the tender part from all the asparagus
2. Tie in a bundle
3. Blanch in boiling water and then cook
4. Remove all the water and place on a plate in a single bundle
5. Serve with vinegar cruets
