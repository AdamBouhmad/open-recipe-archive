## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of green beans
- water
- salt
- sugar
- broth
- fried bread pieces

## Directions

1. Cook 2 pounds of green beans in boiling water with salt over strong heat so they do not lose their color
2. After cooking, remove the beans and place in cold water, passing them through a sieve
3. Place in a clean pot with a little sugar
4. Add enough broth so it is neither too thin nor too thick
5. Serve very hot with some pieces of fried bread
