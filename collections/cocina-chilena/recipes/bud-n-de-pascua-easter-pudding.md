## Ingredients

- one cup of cold bread crumbs, well broken up
- six ounces of finely chopped fat
- two pounds of small potatoes and one of large potatoes
- eight eggs
- one small cup of cognac
- candied lemon and orange peel
- four finely chopped apples
- a little corn
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Take one cup of cold bread crumbs, well broken up
2. Add six ounces of finely chopped fat
3. Add two pounds of small potatoes and one large potato
4. Add eight eggs, one small cup of cognac, candied lemon and orange peel, four finely chopped apples, a little corn, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar to taste
5. Place in cloth and boil for six hours
