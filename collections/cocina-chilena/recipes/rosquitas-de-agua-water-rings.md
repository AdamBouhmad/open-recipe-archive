## Ingredients

- one pound powdered sugar
- six whole eggs
- one small teaspoon baking powder
- abundant cinnamon
- sufficient flour
- more than one ounce butter and rendered fat

## Directions

1. Add six whole eggs to one pound powdered sugar and beat a little
2. Add one small teaspoon baking powder and abundant cinnamon
3. Add sufficient flour to make the dough with more than one ounce butter and rendered fat
4. Form thin little rings
5. Place in boiling water until they rise above the water
6. Then place on baking pans and in the oven
