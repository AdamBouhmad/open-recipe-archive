## Ingredients

- Beef steak
- Oil
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat the day before eating it
2. It should be clean and without nerves, beaten until it takes a square or round shape
3. Two hours before grilling, coat well with a mixture of raw oil and a little salt
4. Place on the grill over strong fire, turning it twice
5. Collect carefully the juice that comes from the meat and with a little melted butter dissolved in it, baste each portion when serving
