## Ingredients

- Beef feet
- Eight egg whites
- Juice or peel of eight lemons
- Eight oranges
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Half bottle of port

## Directions

1. Cook beef feet and strain the broth
2. Season with eight egg whites, juice or peel of eight lemons and eight oranges, cloves, cinnamon, and half a bottle of port
3. Bring to a boil, then pour into a flannel bag of two or three layers to strain
4. If it comes out cloudy the first time, strain again
5. Pour into a mold and let cool
