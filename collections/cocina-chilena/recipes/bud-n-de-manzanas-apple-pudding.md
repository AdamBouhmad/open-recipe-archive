## Ingredients

- Apples
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Twelve egg yolks
- Twelve egg whites
- Butter

## Directions

1. Peel apples and cook with sugar, a little cinnamon, and clove
2. After cooking, let cool
3. Beat twelve yolks lightly
4. Separately, beat twelve whites as for meringue
5. Incorporate everything together
6. Line the pudding mold with butter
7. Pour in the beaten mixture and place in the oven
