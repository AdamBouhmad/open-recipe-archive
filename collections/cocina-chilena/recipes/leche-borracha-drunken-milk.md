## Ingredients

- sponge cakes
- cognac or sherry
- milk
- sugar
- lemon peel
- vanilla
- flour
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Arrange a platter with sponge cakes and moisten with cognac or sherry
2. Cook a little milk with sugar, lemon peel and vanilla
3. Thicken with a little flour dissolved in milk
4. When cooked, remove and cool for a moment
5. Add six egg yolks and place on the fire
6. When the eggs are cooked, pour over the sponge cakes
7. Let rest and serve
