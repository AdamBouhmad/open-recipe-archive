## Ingredients

- two pounds syrup
- one pound paste, not too finely ground

## Directions

1. Boil two pounds of syrup with one pound of paste not too finely ground
2. When it reaches the thread stage, remove the pan from heat and beat until it becomes white
3. Boil again once more
4. Pour quickly onto a damp table and spread to desired thickness
5. Before it cools, cut into squares or biscuits
6. Separate with a knife and place in a basket
7. This can also be made with Panama coconut or local coconut, grating the coconut instead of grinding it
