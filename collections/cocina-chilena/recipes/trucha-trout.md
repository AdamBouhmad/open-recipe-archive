## Ingredients

- trout
- water
- half a glass of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- laurel
- onion
- carrot in slices
- butter
- mushrooms
- parsley
- small onions, chopped
- half a cooked bread in fish

## Directions

1. Cook the trout in water with half a glass of vinegar, salt, pepper, laurel, onion and carrot slices
2. Prepare the sauce: in a pan, put a piece of butter with mushrooms, parsley and small chopped onions
3. When it has cooked a little, add half a bread cooked in fish, stir everything well so it breaks down
4. Add a little of the trout cooking broth, pass through a strainer
5. Serve the sauce with the trout
