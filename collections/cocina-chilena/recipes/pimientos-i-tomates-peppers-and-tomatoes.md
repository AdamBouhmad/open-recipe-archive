## Ingredients

- finely chopped onion
- oil
- tomato (peeled)
- fine peppers cut into small pieces

## Directions

1. Chop the onion finely and put it to simmer in oil
2. Add peeled tomato
3. Add fine peppers cut into small pieces
4. Let it simmer a lot until reduced
5. Serve with fried eggs or with meat, as an appetizing side dish
