## Ingredients

- four eggs
- powdered sugar equal in weight to four eggs
- flour equal in weight to four eggs
- butter equal in weight to four eggs
- peeled and sliced almonds
- Corinth raisins
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Place on the scale plate of a balance four eggs and equal weights of powdered sugar, flour, and butter
2. Beat the butter and add four egg yolks
3. Beat again for a time and finally add the sugar, flour, and four beaten egg whites separated as for meringues
4. Place this dough in a buttered mold and cover on top with peeled and sliced almonds, Corinth raisins, and cinnamon powder
5. Bake in the oven
