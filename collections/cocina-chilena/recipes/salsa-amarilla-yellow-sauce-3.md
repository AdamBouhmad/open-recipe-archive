## Ingredients

- fried bread
- cumin
- clove
- saffron threads
- broth
- bread crumb

## Directions

1. Grind in a mortar fried bread and cumin, a grain of clove and some saffron threads
2. Dissolve in broth, thicken with bread crumb and give it some boils
