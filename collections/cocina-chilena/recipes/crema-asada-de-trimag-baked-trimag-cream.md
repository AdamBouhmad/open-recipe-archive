## Ingredients

- Just over one bottle of milk
- Six eggs
- A little nutmeg
- Lemon zest
- A little cognac
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and add to just over one bottle of milk
2. Add a little nutmeg, lemon zest, a little cognac, and sugar to taste
3. Mix everything and pour into a mold
4. Cook with very gentle heat and also with heat from above
5. Ensure it does not boil or it will curdle
6. As soon as it is set, remove from the fire and serve
