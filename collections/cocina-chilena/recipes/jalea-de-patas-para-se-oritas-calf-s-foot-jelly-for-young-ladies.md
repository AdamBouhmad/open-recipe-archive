## Ingredients

- Two large beef feet, parboiled
- Twelve egg whites with shells
- Juice of twelve oranges
- Juice of twelve lemons
- Peel of one lemon
- Peel of one orange
- Sticks of cinnamon
- A little cloves
- About three pounds of refined sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the two large parboiled beef feet into pieces, removing all the black part and fat
2. Wash this with three waters and put to cook in a pan with the bones until they are well cleaned
3. It is necessary to have boiling water to add to the pan as it reduces
4. Pass this broth through a sieve and leave in a tureen until the next day
5. If it has fat on top, remove it with a spoon until the jelly is completely clean of it
6. Put this in a pan and add twelve egg whites with shells, the juice of twelve oranges and twelve lemons, the peel of one lemon and one orange, some sticks of cinnamon, and a little whole cloves
7. Stir to combine everything and in the middle add about three pounds of refined sugar
8. Place the pan over the fire and without stirring let boil until it sets
9. Lower the pan from the fire and strain the jelly through a flannel bag that has been moistened beforehand in boiling water and wrung out
