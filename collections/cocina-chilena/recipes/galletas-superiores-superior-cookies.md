## Ingredients

- twenty-four egg yolks
- one pound of powdered sugar
- grated lemon
- cinnamon
- one tablespoon of butter
- one glass of white wine or cognac
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat twenty-four egg yolks with one pound of powdered sugar, grated lemon and cinnamon
2. When beaten like sponge cake batter, add one tablespoon of butter, one glass of white wine or cognac and enough flour to make a white dough
3. Cut into thin pastry sheets and place in the oven
