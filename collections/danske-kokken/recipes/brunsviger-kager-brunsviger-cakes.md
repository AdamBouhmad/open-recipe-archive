## Ingredients

- 125 grams (1/4 lb) margarine
- 2 egg yolks
- 125 grams (1/4 lb) icing sugar
- 75 grams (15 quintal) ground almonds
- zest of one lemon or a few drops of lemon essence
- 125 grams (1/4 lb) flour
- 2 egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Mix the margarine well with 2 egg yolks, icing sugar, ground almonds, lemon zest or lemon drops, and flour
2. Then mix in the 2 beaten egg whites
3. Spread the dough thinly on a cold, buttered baking sheet
4. Sprinkle with chopped almonds, sugar and caraway seeds
5. Bake quickly
6. Cut into small square cakes while still warm
