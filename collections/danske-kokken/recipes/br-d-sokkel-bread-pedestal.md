## Ingredients

- Malted bread
- Egg white
- Flour

## Directions

1. Use the bottom crust of malted bread to create the pedestal
2. Split the bread and give the pedestal the desired height
3. Glue it to the dish with egg white mixed with flour
4. Fasten the decorative skewers at the ends, decorated with flowers, lettuce or available garnish
5. Arrange vegetables on the sides
