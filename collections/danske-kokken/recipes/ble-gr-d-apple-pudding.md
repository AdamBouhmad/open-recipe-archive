## Ingredients

- 3 kg good large apples
- ¼ L water
- Sugar to taste
- Rice flour (optional for thickening)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut away the core
2. Rinse them
3. Place in a well-enameled pot with ¼ L water on the bottom
4. Cook the apples over low heat without stirring until tender
5. Mash them, sweeten, and pour into bowls
6. Can be thickened with a little thinned rice flour
7. Alternatively, cook the apples with the skin on and press the pudding through a coarse sieve
8. Serve lukewarm with cream or crème
