## Ingredients

- 1 kg cloudberries
- 5.5 L water
- raisins
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- potato flour or sago flour
- white wine
- whipped cream
- small macarons
- almonds

## Directions

1. Cook berries in water and strain
2. Bring soup to boil again with raisins, whole cinnamon and lemon peel
3. Thicken with potato flour or sago flour mixed with water
4. Season with a little white wine
5. Serve with whipped cream and small macarons or coarsely chopped or lengthwise-cut almonds
