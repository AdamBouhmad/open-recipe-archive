## Ingredients

- Equal parts of pressed or strained juice, for example of strawberries, currants, and raspberries
- 375 g (3/4 S) powdered sugar Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of pressed or strained juice, such as strawberry, currant, and raspberry juice.
2. Boil with the sugar until it has skimmed.
3. Pour the juice into bottles and bind immediately.
