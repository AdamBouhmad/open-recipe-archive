## Ingredients

- 3 Kg (6 S) lamb forequarter
- 1 Kg (2 S) asparagus
- 1 L (1 Pot) unpeeled shrimp
- Salt
- Broth bundle

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into suitably small pieces, wash in cold water and blanch it.
2. Place it over heat in cold water with salt and blanch it and cook with a good broth bundle.
3. When it is tender, strain off some of the broth, the rest stays with the meat so it can be kept warm.
4. Peel the asparagus and cut it into about 3 centimeter (1 inch) long pieces.
