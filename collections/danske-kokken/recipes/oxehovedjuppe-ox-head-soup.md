## Ingredients

- 1 ox head
- Butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 4 carrots
- 6 sliced parsnips
- 2 celeries
- 4 cloves
- 1 teaspoon mace
- strong pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- Soya
- Port wine

## Directions

1. Clean the head thoroughly and soak in water for 10 hours
2. Crush the bones and place the head in the pot with the meat side down
3. Simmer gently for 1 hour with 13 quarts water
4. Add nearly a gallon of water and add the salt
5. Cook a few minutes, skim well, and add vegetables and seasonings
6. When the meat is tender, remove the head
7. Clean the meat and cut into large cubes
8. Return the bones to the pot and cook the soup until about 3 quarts remain
9. Strain the soup and add butter, flour, 1 tablespoon soya and 1 glass port wine
10. Add the meat to the soup
11. Cook for 15 minutes and it is ready to serve
