## Ingredients

- Good, true Sauce-hollandaise (see Nr. 793)
- Whipped cream from 1 1/4 Dl (1/4 Pægl) cream

## Directions

1. Make a good, true Sauce-hollandaise (see Nr. 793), into which whipped cream from 1 1/4 Dl (1/4 Pægl) cream is stirred for 1/2 dozen people when it is to be served
2. Eaten with boiled vegetables
3. Lemon juice, which is used in hollandaise sauce, is omitted in this
