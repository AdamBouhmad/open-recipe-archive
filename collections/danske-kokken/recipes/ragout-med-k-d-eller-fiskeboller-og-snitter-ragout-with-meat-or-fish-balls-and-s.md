## Ingredients

- Ragout (prepared as above)
- Meat or fish balls
- Puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Arrange the ragout on a flat serving dish
2. Instead of covering the entire dish with forcemeat, place a wreath of meat or fish balls around it
3. Place puff pastry strips around the outside of the balls
4. This method avoids using the oven and creates an attractive presentation
