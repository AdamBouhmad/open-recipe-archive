## Ingredients

- good 1/4 L (good 1 Pægl) medium-fine sago grains
- 4 1/2 L (4 1/2 Pot) water
- lemon peel
- whole cinnamon
- raisins
- 7 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 Kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) sherry
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) rhubarb juice or white wine

## Directions

1. When the water boils, add lemon peel, whole cinnamon and raisins together with the grains under constant stirring and cook until the grains are clear.
2. In a tureen, beat 7 egg yolks and 3 egg whites with 1/2 Kg (1 S) powdered sugar until white and foaming.
3. Pour the boiling soup into the egg mixture while stirring well.
4. Season the soup with 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) sherry and 1/4 L (1 Pægl) rhubarb juice or white wine.
