## Ingredients

- lamb hindquarter
- parsley
- butter
- breadcrumb dough (Nr. 492)
- almonds (optional)
- currants (optional)
- cardamom (optional)

## Directions

1. When the flap remains with the roast, it is filled either with parsley that has been picked and rinsed, and into which a piece of butter has been added, or with a breadcrumb dough (see Nr. 492), which can optionally be mixed with ground almonds, currants and a small amount of cardamom.
2. The flap is opened on one side as needed.
