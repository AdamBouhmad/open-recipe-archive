## Ingredients

- Beef tenderloin
- Bacon or pork fat for larding
- Fine salt
- Ground pepper
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Fresh mushrooms, coarsely chopped (optional)
- Wheat flour (optional)
- Cream (optional)
- Madeira wine (optional)

## Directions

1. After the tenderloin is prepared and larded as mentioned in the previous recipe, beat it lightly with a meat mallet.
2. Rub it with fine salt and ground pepper.
3. Heat abundant butter in a pan and place the fillet in it, frying uncovered at strong heat with constant dripping.
4. Thin the sauce with bouillon and arrange it with the roast.
5. Optionally, when the roast is removed, add some coarsely chopped fresh mushrooms to the sauce and thicken it with a little wheat flour, a tablespoonful of cream, and bouillon.
6. If desired, add a little Madeira to the sauce.
