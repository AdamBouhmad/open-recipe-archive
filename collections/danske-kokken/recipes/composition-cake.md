## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 2¾ cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 4 cups flour
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 1 cup butter with 5 egg yolks, then 2¾ cups sugar, 1 cup milk, 2 teaspoons cream of tartar, 1 teaspoon soda and 4 cups flour
2. Finally add the beaten whites
