## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg chocolate
- 1 L (1 pot) sweet milk
- 6 sheets isinglass
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) heavy cream
- cherry jam
- small cakes

## Directions

1. Cook chocolate with 1 L sweet milk.
2. After removing from heat, dissolve 6 sheets isinglass in it.
3. Pour the chocolate and stir until it begins to set.
4. Fill into a glass bowl and let become completely cold.
5. Before serving, cover with whipped cream and decorate with cherry jam.
6. Serve small cakes on the side.
