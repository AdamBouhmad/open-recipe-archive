## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaves

## Directions

1. Cook the mussels in the usual manner
2. Remove them from the shells and discard the beard and black intestine
3. Place the mussels in a crock
4. Cook the mussel broth with nutmeg, pepper, cloves and bay leaves, skim it and strain it
