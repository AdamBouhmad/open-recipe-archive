## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 ounces raisins
- 4 ounces sugar
- 1 pint lukewarm milk
- 2 cents yeast
- butter
- cinnamon
- currants
- sliced almonds

## Directions

1. Knead 6 egg yolks, 4 ounces raisins, 4 ounces sugar, 1 pint lukewarm milk and 2 cents yeast dissolved in a little of the lukewarm milk into 2 pounds flour.
2. Knead for a good half hour and set overnight covered in a cold place.
3. Next day turn the dough out onto a baking sheet smeared with butter and set for half an hour in the heat.
4. While it stands there, pour some melted butter over the bread, and stick small pieces of butter here and there into it, then sprinkle it with sugar, crushed cinnamon, currants and sliced almonds.
5. Bake at even heat light brown for approximately half an hour.
