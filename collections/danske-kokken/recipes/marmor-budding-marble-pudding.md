## Ingredients

- 1 bottle white wine
- 12 ounces white sugar
- Juice of 3 lemons and zest of 2
- 14 ounces gelatine
- 20 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook white wine with white sugar, lemon juice and zest, and gelatine
2. As soon as the gelatine is dissolved, remove from heat and strain if necessary
3. Mix with 20 egg yolks, first beaten white
