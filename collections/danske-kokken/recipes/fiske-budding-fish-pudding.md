## Ingredients

- 0.25 kg scraped fish for forcemeat
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish forcemeat as described in the fish preparation method.
2. Butter a pudding mold and dust with bread crumbs.
3. Fill with the fish forcemeat.
4. Cook as described for meat pudding, by placing in boiling water and simmering until set.
5. Serve with choice of sauce: melted butter, oyster sauce, mushroom sauce, lobster sauce, tomato sauce, caper sauce, curry sauce, chutney sauce, or shrimp and asparagus sauce.
