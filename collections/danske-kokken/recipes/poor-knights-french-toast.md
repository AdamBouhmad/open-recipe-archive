## Ingredients

- French bread
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- fruit jelly

## Directions

1. Dip slices of French bread in milk
2. Cover them for an hour
3. Roll in sugar and cinnamon and fry in butter on both sides
4. Then spread with a good layer of fruit jelly and arrange
