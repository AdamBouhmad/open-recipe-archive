## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram dried Russian peas
- Sugar
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the peas in several changes of water and soak them in lukewarm water overnight; if soaked for 2 days they will be better.
2. Cook them in water with sugar, salt, and a good slice of butter.
3. The cooking time depends on the quality of the peas, but usually at least 2 hours are needed.
4. Stew them as green peas and can be prepared with finely chopped parsley.
