## Ingredients

- 1 white cabbage head
- 1.5 kg prepared forcemeat (serves 12 people)
- butter
- sugar
- coloring
- broth or water
- flour
- sago flour

## Directions

1. Shred a white cabbage head and brown it in butter with a little sugar and coloring
2. When the cabbage is brown, add a little broth or water and make a depression in the center
3. Place the forcemeat in this depression, shaping it as round as possible
4. Pack the cabbage around the forcemeat
5. Cook both together under a closed lid over low heat for 2 to 2.5 hours
6. Make a brown sauce from the gravy, either by cooking butter and flour in it or by thickening with sago flour
