## Ingredients

- small macaroons
- lemon slices
- a couple of sticks cinnamon
- white wine
- ice-cold pure water
- sugar to taste
- ice (if available)

## Directions

1. An hour before the cold soup is to be served, put small macaroons in a tureen together with some lemon slices without pits and a couple of sticks of cinnamon.
2. Pour over them white wine mixed with ice-cold pure water and sugar to taste.
3. If you have some pieces of clean ice, add them.
4. Allow 1 to 1.5 pints per portion.
