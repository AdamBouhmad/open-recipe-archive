## Ingredients

- 2 cups good, not very salty, melted butter
- 2 cups flour
- a little cayenne pepper
- a little salt
- 1 gallon nearly boiling milk
- 1 quart fresh oysters
- water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Stir 2 cups of melted butter with 2 cups flour, a little cayenne pepper, and a little salt
2. Gradually add 1 gallon of nearly boiling milk to the roux while stirring well to prevent lumps
3. Pour a little water over 1 quart of fresh oysters, set over good heat and simmer gently
4. Pour the water with the oyster liquor into the soup
5. Rinse the oysters in cold water to clean them of all small shells
6. Bring the soup to a boil and remove immediately from heat so it does not curdle
7. Add the oysters to the soup
8. Have everything at hand before beginning, as the soup must be stirred constantly
