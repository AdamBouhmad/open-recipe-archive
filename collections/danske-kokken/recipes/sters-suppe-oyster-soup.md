## Ingredients

- 40 oysters
- 3 L soup stock
- 2 L boiling water
- oyster liquid and beards
- 125 gr butter
- 125 gr flour
- lemon juice
- fish, veal or spare soup
- 6 egg yolks
- Rhine wine
- small fish balls (optional)

## Directions

1. Open the oysters and collect the water from the shells in a large saucepan along with the removed beards
2. Place the oysters in a colander set over the saucepan containing the beards
3. Pour 2 L boiling water over the oysters and place them in a tureen
4. Cook the beards for a few minutes with a little lemon juice in the oyster water
5. Strain the soup and mix with 3 L fish, veal or spare soup
6. Brown 125 gr butter and 125 gr flour together and thin with the soup
7. When it boils, thicken it with 6 beaten egg yolks and season with Rhine wine
8. Pour the boiling soup over the oysters in the tureen
9. Serve with Butter dough slices or Simels
