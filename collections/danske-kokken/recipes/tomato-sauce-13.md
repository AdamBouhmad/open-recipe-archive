## Ingredients

- 1 Pint Tomatoes
- 1 Ounce Carrot
- 1 Ounce Onion
- 1 Ounce salt Meat
- 1 Bay leaf
- 4 Cloves
- 1 Teaspoon Salt
- 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
- 1 Gill Soup, Syrup or Water

## Directions

1. In a thick Saucepan or Casserole put 1 Pint Tomatoes, 1 Ounce Carrot and the same amount of Onion sliced, further 1 Ounce salt Meat cut into small pieces, 1 Bay leaf, 4 Cloves, 1 Teaspoon Salt, 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper and 1 Gill Soup, Syrup or Water.
2. Boil slowly for about one Hour and strain through a Sieve with a Spoon.
