## Ingredients

- Fresh mussels
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Mussel broth
- Wine
- Lemon juice
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Examine each mussel to ensure it is fresh and good - it should be closed; if it has opened, discard it and do not put it in the pot with the others
2. Clean mussels with a brush
3. Place on heat with a little water and salt on the bottom of the pot, covered with a tight-fitting lid
4. As soon as they open, they are cooked
5. Remove from shells and remove the beard and black intestine
6. Strain the broth and use for the sauce
7. Prepare sauce and add wine, lemon juice, and egg yolks in the same way as in recipe 13
