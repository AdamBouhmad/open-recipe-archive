## Ingredients

- plums
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar per pound of fruit
- just over 1 measure vinegar per pound of fruit
- cinnamon sticks
- cloves

## Directions

1. Wipe the plums, remove the stems, and prick the fruit with a needle.
2. For each pound of fruit, use 1/2 pound powdered sugar and just over 1 measure vinegar.
3. Heat the vinegar and sugar over the fire, bring to a boil, skim, and pour hot over the plums.
4. Let sit until the next day.
5. The next day, place everything over the fire and cook with some cinnamon sticks and some cloves at even heat for half an hour.
6. Skim the plums well during cooking, then pour them with the syrup into the preserving jar.
