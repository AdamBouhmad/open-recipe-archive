## Ingredients

- 0.5 pint milk
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- 3 egg yolks
- A little vanilla
- Salt
- Pie dough
- Beaten egg whites
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 0.5 pint milk.
2. Stir into it 1 tablespoon corn starch, 3 egg yolks, a little vanilla, and salt.
3. Line the plate with ordinary dough.
4. Pour in the filling and set in the oven and bake at good heat.
5. Beat the whites with sugar and place on top when done, after which set it back in the oven for a moment.
