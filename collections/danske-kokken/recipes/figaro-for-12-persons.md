## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg Bavarian sausages
- Boiled cold potatoes
- Butter
- Flour
- Sausage cooking water
- 5 to 6 large onions
- Salt
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Rinse the sausages well and place them in boiling water to cook for 4 to 5 minutes.
2. Make a roux from butter and flour, then thin it with the water in which the sausages were cooked until it becomes a regular smooth white sauce.
3. Chop the onions coarsely and cook them tender in the sauce.
4. On a slightly deep plate, layer slices of boiled potatoes, then slices of the cooked sausages, then another layer of potato slices.
5. Season the sauce with salt, Maggi Bouillon cubes, and pepper, and pour it over generously, as some will be absorbed by the potatoes.
6. Sprinkle the dish with bread crumbs and place it in a warm oven to heat through and brown slightly on top.
7. If no warm oven is available, cut the potatoes and sausages while warm and pour the hot sauce over them.
8. Serve as soon as it is arranged. Figaro can be served as a lunch or dinner course.
