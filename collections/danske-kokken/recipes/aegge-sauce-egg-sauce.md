## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 heaping teaspoon flour
- Juice of 1 lemon and rind of half a lemon
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- Powdered sugar
- A piece of butter the size of a small walnut
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks until thick
2. Add the flour
3. Add lemon juice and rind, vinegar, powdered sugar, and butter
4. Add water
5. Place on heat and stir until it boils
6. This portion is suitable for 4 pounds of cooked fish
