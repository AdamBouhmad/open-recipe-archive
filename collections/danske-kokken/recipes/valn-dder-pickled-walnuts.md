## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg walnuts
- 625 gr ground icing sugar
- 51/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Pick walnuts in the first half of July before the stone has hardened
2. Pierce the meat and outer green shell with a coarse larding needle or bacon needle
3. Place in cold water, which must be renewed daily
4. After standing thus for about ten days, place over heat in cold water and cook covered until tender enough to pierce easily with a needle
5. Remove and place on a cloth, then weigh them
6. Boil sugar and water to a thin syrup and pour over the nuts, which stand thus for a couple of days
7. Drain off the juice, reduce well, and give the walnuts another boil in it
8. Place in glasses and reduce the juice further before pouring over
9. The walnuts can be flavored with various additions: either cook a stick of vanilla in the syrup, or stick the nuts with small pieces of cinnamon and some whole cloves after they have been cooked tender in the water. The spices mentioned can also be simply boiled in the syrup
