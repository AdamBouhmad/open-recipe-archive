## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 20 grams white sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 pound fine wheat flour
- 1/2 pound potato flour
- 1/4 pound washed raisins
- 15 grams finely cut citron peel
- a little cardamom
- 1 tablespoon yeast

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and stir with the white sugar and 3 whole eggs.
2. Add 1 cup sweet milk, 1 pound fine wheat flour, 1/2 pound potato flour, 1/4 pound washed raisins, 15 grams finely cut citron peel, a little cardamom, and 1 tablespoon yeast.
3. Stir well and knead with the hands until the dough releases and begins to rise.
4. Let it rise until well leavened, then shape as a Christmas cake.
5. Long strips of citron peel can be placed on top.
6. Brush with egg white and bake for 1 hour at good heat.
