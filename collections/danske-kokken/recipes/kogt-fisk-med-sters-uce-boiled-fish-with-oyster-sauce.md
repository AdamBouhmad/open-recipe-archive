## Ingredients

- 2 pounds cod
- salt
- peppercorns
- 1 pint oysters

## Directions

1. Clean 2 pounds cod and boil according to directions in cold water
2. As soon as the fish is done, lift the pot from the fire and set it back
3. Meanwhile set 1 pint oysters over the fire to cook in their own sauce
4. When the oysters are cooked, strain them
