## Ingredients

- cooked cod
- flour
- butter
- fish stock
- a little Graves wine
- mushrooms or tomato paste or lobster and crayfish extract (optional)
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pick the cooked cod into small pieces and free it from skin and bones.
2. Brown flour and butter together, thin it with fish stock and add a little Graves wine.
3. The sauce can be made either with mushrooms, tomato paste, or lobster and crayfish extract; wine alone can also be sufficient.
4. Last, add the fish and turn it carefully in the sauce.
5. If mushrooms are used, place one on top in each crust case and bind the sauce with egg yolks.
