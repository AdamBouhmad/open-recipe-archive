## Ingredients

- Young carrots
- Celery
- Leeks
- Parsley roots
- Corn salad
- 5 pots good clear bouillon
- Toasted French bread slices

## Directions

1. Clean the carrots, celery, leeks, parsley roots, and corn salad
2. Cut into thin strips the thickness of a straw and a couple of inches long
3. Cook covered over gentle heat with 5 pots good clear bouillon
4. Serve with toasted French bread slices
