## Ingredients

- green peas
- water
- sugar
- butter
- cooked smoked ham or beef tongue (leftovers)

## Directions

1. Steam the green peas tender in very little water, with a little sugar added.
2. Drain them on a sieve, then toss them in butter.
3. If you have leftovers of cooked smoked ham or beef tongue, cut it very fine and stir it into the peas.
4. Fill the peas into the crust cases and arrange according to previous instructions.
5. The peas can also be braised.
