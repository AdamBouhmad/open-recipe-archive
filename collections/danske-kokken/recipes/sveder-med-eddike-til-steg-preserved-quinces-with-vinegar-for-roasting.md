## Ingredients

- Quinces, well ripened
- Cinnamon pieces
- Cloves
- 2 pounds quinces
- 1 pint water
- 3 pints vinegar
- 1½ pounds powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces, which must be well ripened, cut them into 4 to 6 pieces according to their size, remove the cores
2. Weigh them and stud with small pieces of cinnamon and cloves
3. For 2 pounds quinces, take 1 pint water, 3 pints vinegar, and 1½ pounds good powdered sugar
4. If the quinces are not completely covered, take a little more of each ingredient
5. Heat until the quinces are soft, then remove them and place in a jar
6. Boil the liquid until it is perfectly smooth, cool it, and pour over the quinces
7. Seal the jar the following day
