## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- Water
- Salt
- Whole peppercorns
- Sour butter sauce
- Boiled potatoes, freshly peeled

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the fish
2. Place in boiling water with salt and whole peppercorns to cook
3. When cooked, serve with sour butter sauce and freshly peeled boiled potatoes
