## Ingredients

- 10 egg whites
- 1 pound finely sifted powdered sugar
- butter for greasing the pan
- flour for dusting
- coarse sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Slowly stir in 1 pound finely sifted powdered sugar while continuing to whip
3. Grease a baking sheet with butter and dust with flour
4. Place small cakes on the sheet using a silver spoon
5. Sprinkle with coarse sugar and dry them in a very weak oven heat until they release from the pan
6. Note: Blanched sweet almonds cut into long thin strips (40 quintal) can be added to the mixture, but then use only 75 quintal sugar. This mixture can also be used to make a ring cake.
