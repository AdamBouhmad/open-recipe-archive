## Ingredients

- 1 quart potatoes
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Nutmeg
- Breadcrumbs
- Egg
- Water
- Fat or melted butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the potatoes in the usual way.
2. Press them through a fine sieve and stir with 2 egg yolks, a little butter, salt, a little white pepper and nutmeg.
3. Spread a thick layer of breadcrumbs on the table and beat one egg with a spoonful of water.
4. Dip the hands in cold water and form the dough like corks, which are dipped in the egg and rolled in breadcrumbs.
5. Fry golden brown in fat or melted butter and serve around roasted chicken or other meat.
