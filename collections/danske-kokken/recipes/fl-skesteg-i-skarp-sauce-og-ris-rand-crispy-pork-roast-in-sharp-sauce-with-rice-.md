## Ingredients

- Browned butter
- Flour
- Stock or soup
- Sherry
- Salt
- Paprika
- Culinary color
- Slices of crispy pork roast
- Cooked rice
- Onion slices
- Butter for browning
- Potato puree (see No. 336)

## Directions

1. Make a sauce from browned butter and flour, thinned with stock or soup
2. Add sherry, salt, paprika and culinary color to the sauce
3. Warm slices of crispy pork roast in this sauce
4. Place the warmed meat on a flat dish and surround it with a border of loosely cooked rice
5. Sprinkle the rice border with onion slices browned in butter, or with a potato puree baked on the dish (see No. 336)
