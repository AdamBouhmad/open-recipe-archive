## Ingredients

- Cucumbers, peeled, halved and hollowed
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Fresh bay leaves

## Directions

1. Salt the peeled, halved and hollowed cucumbers and let them stand for 24 hours
2. Dry them and press firmly in thick cloths
3. Cut them into strips, weigh them and place them in a crock
4. For each pound of cucumbers, boil 1 pint of vinegar and pour it hot over them
5. Let them stand for 24 hours, then dry them again
6. For each pound of cucumbers, boil 1 pint fresh vinegar with 1/2 pound sugar, 3 cloves, some small pieces of cinnamon and 6 to 8 fresh bay leaves
7. When it has simmered, add the cucumbers, and when it comes to a boil again, pour it out and cover with a cloth
8. After a few days, strain off the liquid and boil it until it has simmered and is clear
9. Pour it hot over the cucumbers, cover with a cloth and tie up the next day
