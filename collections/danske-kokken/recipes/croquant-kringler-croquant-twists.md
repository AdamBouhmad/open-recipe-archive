## Ingredients

- 354 grams (1/4 pound) flour
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) margarine
- 175 grams (35 quintals) powdered sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- Sugar for dipping

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, egg, and egg yolk together well
2. Roll and form into small twists
3. Dip them in sugar and bake until light brown
4. They are very crisp
