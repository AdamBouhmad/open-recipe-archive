## Ingredients

- 100 Gr (20 Kvint) pistachios (green almonds)
- 1 L (1 Pot) cream or milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pound the pistachios (green almonds) and place them in the cream.
2. Bring to a boil, then strain out the pistachios.
3. Cook the cream to cream with 8 egg yolks, stirred with 125 Gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar.
4. When the cream is cold, pour it into the freezer.
