## Ingredients

- 2 pounds peeled rhubarb stalks, or 11 pots unripe gooseberries
- 3 pots water
- a couple of sticks cinnamon
- a little lemon peel
- sugar to taste
- 2 spoonfuls rice flour
- a little cold water
- a little grated lemon peel
- a little white wine (optional)
- small rusks or baked wheat bread cubes

## Directions

1. Put the peeled, cut-up rhubarb stalks or the picked gooseberries over the fire with the water, a couple of sticks of cinnamon, and a little lemon peel in an enameled pot.
2. Cook them until they are completely tender, then strain the soup, add sugar to taste, and put it back over the fire to come to a boil.
3. Thicken the soup with 2 spoonfuls of rice flour mixed with cold water, and add a little grated lemon peel, after which it is ready.
4. If you like, you can also add a little white wine when serving.
5. Serve small rusks or baked wheat bread cubes with the soup.
