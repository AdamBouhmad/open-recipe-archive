## Ingredients

- 12 pounds sugar
- 6-7 oranges
- 1 bottle Rhine wine
- 1 bottle Champagne

## Directions

1. Dip the sugar in water and place it in a bowl together with the juice of 4 oranges and some of the peel. Add the Rhine wine and Champagne gradually. Peel the remaining oranges and add them. Set on ice until ready to serve.
