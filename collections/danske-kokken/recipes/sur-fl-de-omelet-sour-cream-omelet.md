## Ingredients

- 3 pints sour cream
- 0.5 pint sweet cream
- 6 egg yolks
- White sugar
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat sour cream and sweet cream to a foam
2. Combine with egg yolks, white sugar, and flour
3. Fold in beaten egg whites just as it goes into oven
