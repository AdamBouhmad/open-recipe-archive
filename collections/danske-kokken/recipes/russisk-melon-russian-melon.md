## Ingredients

- Unripe melon, cut into smaller pieces
- Vinegar
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut an unripe melon into smaller pieces and cover with vinegar.
2. The next day, remove the melon and sprinkle it with sugar.
3. After 4 to 5 hours, put everything in the pot, and cook the melon until it is clear, before removing it and placing it in the jar.
4. Bring the liquid to a boil and pour it over. Add some whole cloves and whole cinnamon as desired.
5. Seal immediately.
