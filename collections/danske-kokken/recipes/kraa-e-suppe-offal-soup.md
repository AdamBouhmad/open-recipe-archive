## Ingredients

- offal
- bunch of herbs
- root vegetables
- flour
- vinegar
- apples
- sugar
- bread or flour balls

## Directions

1. Clean the offal, cut it into pieces, wash it in several changes of cold water so it stands well covered; cook bunch of herbs and root vegetables with it; when these and the offal are done, take them out
2. Strain the soup, place it on the fire, and when it boils again and is skimmed, add a roux of very little flour stirred in vinegar and chopped apples and sugar
3. When the apples are cooked enough, take them out so there is room to add bread or flour balls; pour the soup over the offal
4. You can also serve these on a plate and thus offer them at table
