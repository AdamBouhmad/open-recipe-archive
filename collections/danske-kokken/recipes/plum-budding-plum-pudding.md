## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram flour
- 0.25 kilogram chopped beef kidney fat
- 125 grams beef bone marrow, cut into smaller pieces
- 0.25 kilogram raisins
- 125 grams currants, plucked and scalded
- 125 grams finely cut citron peel
- 0.25 kilogram powdered or caster sugar
- 1.25 deciliters rum
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful soda, dissolved in 1.25 deciliters milk
- margarine

## Directions

1. Into 0.5 kilogram of flour, stir 0.25 kilogram of chopped beef kidney fat, 125 grams of beef bone marrow cut into smaller pieces, 0.25 kilogram of raisins, 125 grams of currants, both plucked and scalded, 125 grams of finely cut citron peel, 0.25 kilogram of powdered or caster sugar, 1.25 deciliters of rum, 4 eggs, and 1 teaspoonful of soda dissolved in 1.25 deciliters of milk.
2. The batter can be placed in a cloth that is buttered in the middle and sprinkled with flour.
3. The cloth is then gathered and tied with a ribbon.
4. It is cooked in the cloth in a pot with plenty of water for about 3 hours.
5. Plum pudding can, like any other pudding, be cooked in a buttered and floured mold.
6. At serving, a hole is cut in the top; into it are placed some pieces of sugar, which are covered with rum, just as the sides of the pudding are covered.
7. It is lit and must be brought to the table burning.
8. Serve with fruit sauce.
