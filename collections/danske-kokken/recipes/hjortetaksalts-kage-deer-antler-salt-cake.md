## Ingredients

- 3 quarter-pounds butter
- 7 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 1/4 pound sweet almonds, cut into narrow strips
- 12 quintals bitter almonds, finely ground
- a little grated lemon zest
- 7 egg whites, beaten
- 8 ore deer antler salt

## Directions

1. Wash and clarify 3 quarter-pounds butter, then beat well with 7 egg yolks and 1 pound powdered sugar.
2. Beat in 1 pound flour, 1/4 pound sweet almonds cut into narrow strips, 12 quintals finely ground bitter almonds and a little grated lemon zest.
3. Finally add the 7 beaten egg whites and 8 ore deer antler salt.
4. Fill the whole mixture into a greased form and bake hard.
