## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter (1 pint) water
- 1/2 kg (1 pound) berries

## Directions

1. Crush the berries in a wooden or stone mortar
2. Place them in a pot together with the water and boil for a good quarter hour
3. Strain the juice, pour into dry bottles, and cork them
4. Bind with twine, wrap with hay, and place in a kettle to boil for 5 minutes
5. Seal the bottles with plaster the next day
