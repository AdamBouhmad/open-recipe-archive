## Ingredients

- 4 L spare soup
- 2 L shrimp
- shrimp butter from shells and roe
- 100 gr flour
- Madeira wine
- ground white pepper
- asparagus
- Butter slices or Simels

## Directions

1. Cook and pick the shrimp
2. Pound the shells and roe and cook as instructed for Lobster Soup
3. Thicken with 100 gr flour baked in the shrimp butter
4. Season with Madeira and ground white pepper
5. Serve with the picked shrimp and asparagus which has been cooked in some of the spare soup
6. Serve Butter slices or Simels with it
