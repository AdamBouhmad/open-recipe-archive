## Ingredients

- 4 good celery roots
- Some of the innermost fine leaves
- 5 pots bouillon
- A little nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- Toasted French bread slices

## Directions

1. Clean the celery roots and cut into pieces with some of the innermost fine leaves
2. Place over slow heat with 5 pots bouillon and cook until very tender
3. Press through a sieve while thinning with the soup
4. Return the soup to heat with a little nutmeg and bring to a boil
5. In the tureen, whisk 3 egg yolks
6. While continuously whisking, pour the soup into the egg yolks
7. Serve with toasted French bread slices
