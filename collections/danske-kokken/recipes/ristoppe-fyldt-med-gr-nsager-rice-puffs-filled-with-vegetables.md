## Ingredients

- Rice
- Cooked vegetables such as Brussels sprouts, peas, or asparagus
- Parsley or chervil, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the rice as described in recipe 502, but omit the raisins
2. Wet small molds lightly and line them with the rice mixture
3. In the center, place cooked finely chopped vegetables such as Brussels sprouts, peas, or asparagus, or any cooked vegetables that suit soup
4. Add finely chopped parsley or chervil
5. Cover with another layer of cooked rice
6. Turn these small puddings out onto a warm platter while still warm
7. Serve with the soup, allowing one rice puff per person
8. Small timbale molds can be used
