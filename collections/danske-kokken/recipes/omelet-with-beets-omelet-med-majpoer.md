## Ingredients

- Cooked beets
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Leek

## Directions

1. Cooked warm beets can either be cut into small cubes and stirred into the eggs, or they can be cut into slices, dipped in egg and bread, and fried in butter.
2. Place a strip of this over the omelet, which is then closed together.
3. Coarsely cut leek goes well with the beets and is stirred into the egg mixture.
