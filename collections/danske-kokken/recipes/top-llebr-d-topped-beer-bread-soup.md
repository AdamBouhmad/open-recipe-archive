## Ingredients

- beer prepared as in Norsk Øllebrød
- 1 pound grated rye bread
- 20 drams browned butter
- a quarter pound powdered sugar
- a little ground cinnamon
- a little grated lemon peel
- half a quarter pound washed currants

## Directions

1. Prepare the beer as in Norsk Øllebrød
2. For the bread topping: bake 1 pound of grated rye bread in 20 drams of light brown browned butter in a pan together with a quarter pound of powdered sugar, a little ground cinnamon, a little grated lemon peel, and half a quarter pound of washed currants until it resembles a good dough and is almost dry
3. Let it cool slightly in the pan, and when half-cold, press it into small cups that have been moistened inside
4. When cold, loosen it around the edges with a knife and turn out onto a plate
5. Serve the bread topping with the beer soup
