## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound chocolate
- 2 quarts sweet milk
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- a few drops good vanilla extract
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate 1/4 pound chocolate and boil in 2 quarts of sweet milk
2. Stir regularly until it boils
3. After some cooking time, pour it into a terrine in which 3 egg yolks have been previously whisked with sugar and a few drops of good vanilla extract
4. Whisk constantly while pouring in the soup
5. If desired, the egg whites can be whisked, cooked and placed on top
