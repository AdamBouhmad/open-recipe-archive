## Ingredients

- Round beef
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Onion

## Directions

1. Use round beef, and in winter it is good if it has hung for several days.
2. Scrape the meat from the sinews, making sure to get everything while leaving only the clear sinews, which are not used.
3. Chop the meat together with the back of a knife into round, flat cakes.
4. Sprinkle salt and pepper on them and fry in well-browned butter; it is good to fry some chopped onion at the same time as the meat.
5. Serve with browned butter and potatoes.
