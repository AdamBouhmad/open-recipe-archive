## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 36 quintal finely ground and sifted powdered sugar
- 24 quintal finely ground and sifted chocolate

## Directions

1. Whip 4 egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Stir in 36 quintal finely ground and sifted powdered sugar and 24 quintal finely ground and sifted chocolate
3. Place small dollops on a wax-greased sheet
4. Bake or dry at slow heat
