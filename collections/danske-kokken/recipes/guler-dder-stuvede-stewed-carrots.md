## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Meat broth
- Butter (3 ounces per plateful)
- Flour (4 ounces per plateful)
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Sugar
- Young green peas (optional)

## Directions

1. Choose carrots with a nice red color and truncated roots (typically called Carrots). Clean and scrape the carrots, cut into small dice. For each plateful, use 3 ounces butter and 4 ounces flour. Stew in meat broth that is thickened with flour and butter. When tender, add some chopped parsley and salt and sugar to taste. When stewed together with young green peas, they are well suited to salt dishes in summer.
