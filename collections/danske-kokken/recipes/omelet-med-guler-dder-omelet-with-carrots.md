## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 10 tablespoons milk or cream
- salt
- egg whites, stiffly beaten
- butter
- cooked whole warm carrots
- parsley, chopped
- cooked kale, optional

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks well
2. Stir in 10 tablespoons milk or cream and a small amount of salt
3. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Place a good slice of butter on the pan and when it boils, pour in the eggs
5. With a fork or knife, lightly prick the eggs and constantly shake the pan so the omelet does not burn or form a hard crust
6. Bake the omelet only on one side
7. Slice the cooked warm carrots with a fluted knife and toss in butter
8. Lay the carrot slices over the middle of the omelet, sprinkle with chopped parsley, and fold together
9. Alternatively, cooked kale can be warmed, cut, and placed in the omelet in the same manner
10. Serve with stirred butter on the side
