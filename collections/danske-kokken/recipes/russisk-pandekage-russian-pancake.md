## Ingredients

- 1/4 Pound potato flour
- 0.5 Pound butter
- 1 Pint water
- 1 Pint cream
- 6 to 8 Egg yolks
- Egg whites, whipped
- A little vanilla

## Directions

1. Bake the potato flour in 0.5 pound butter and 1 pint water.
2. Set aside to cool.
3. When cooled, stir it up with 1 pint cream and 6 to 8 egg yolks, and finally with the whipped egg whites.
4. A little vanilla enhances the flavor.
5. Bake 3 pancakes on a pan until they are light brown.
6. Stack them on top of each other with preserves between them and powdered sugar on top.
7. Serve warm.
