## Ingredients

- 8 pounds of pressed juice
- 5 pounds of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in the juice to dissolve 12 hours before cooking
2. Then put the juice on to cook for half an hour
3. Stir constantly and skim the foam carefully
4. When it is cooked and clear, filter it and fill it cold into warm bottles, which are immediately corked
