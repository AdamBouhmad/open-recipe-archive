## Ingredients

- vanilla cream as Nr. 1060
- 3 1/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) very strong coffee extract
- 14 sheets of isinglass, dissolved in the extract
- whipped cream for decoration

## Directions

1. Stir the very strong coffee extract into the vanilla cream as Nr. 1060, before the whipped cream is added.
2. Dissolve the isinglass in the extract.
3. Decorate the cream with whipped cream foam.
