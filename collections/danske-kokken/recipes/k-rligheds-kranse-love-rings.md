## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 375 grams (1/4 pound) flour
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) margarine
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) powdered sugar
- Vanilla essence
- Egg for dipping
- Sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Crumble the hard-boiled egg yolks with the flour
2. Knead in the margarine, powdered sugar, and vanilla essence
3. Roll the dough into a thin rod and form small rings
4. Dip them in egg and sugar
5. Place them not too close together on the baking sheet
