## Ingredients

- 3 dekaliters green peas
- Carrots, quantity to taste
- Sugar
- Salt
- 125 grams butter
- 2 good tablespoons flour
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots and cut them into small, fine cubes about the size of a pea; rinse them.
2. Cook the carrots together with the peas in very little water with sugar and salt.
3. Melt the butter and brown 2 good tablespoons of flour in it.
4. Thin this with the pea water and add the peas together with finely chopped parsley.
5. Bring to a boil and serve.
