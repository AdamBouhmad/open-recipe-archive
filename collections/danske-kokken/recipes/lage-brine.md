## Ingredients

- 12 quarts water
- 3 pounds coarse salt
- 0.5 pound powdered sugar
- 3 ounces saltpetre

## Directions

1. Place water over heat and add saltpetre first, as it dissolves most easily
2. Then add the rest and bring the whole to a boil, then skim and pour out
3. When cold, all kinds of meat and flesh can be preserved in it and do not need to be leached before cooking
