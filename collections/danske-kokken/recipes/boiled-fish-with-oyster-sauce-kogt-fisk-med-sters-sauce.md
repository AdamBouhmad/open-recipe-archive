## Ingredients

- 2 pounds cod
- 1 pint oysters
- 1 ounce butter
- 1 ounce flour
- 1 egg yolk
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Ground white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and boil 2 pounds of cod in salt water according to instructions.
2. Once the fish is done, remove the pot from heat and set aside.
3. In the meantime, place 1 pint of oysters over heat to cook in their own sauce.
4. Once cooked, strain the oysters and return the sauce to heat to bring it to a boil.
5. Melt 1 ounce of butter in a frying pan over heat, stir in 1 ounce of flour, and when it bubbles, stir in the boiling oyster sauce.
6. Even out the sauce with 1 well-beaten egg yolk, ½ teaspoon salt, and a little ground white pepper.
7. Add the oysters and serve the sauce in a sauce boat.
