## Ingredients

- 6 pounds fresh beef
- 1 pound suet
- 5 pounds apples
- 2 pounds raisins
- 2 pounds currants
- 1 pound citron
- 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 1 quart wine
- Broth from the beef

## Directions

1. Cook 6 pounds fresh beef tender in barely covered water.
2. When cold, chop it fine.
3. Chop 1 pound suet fine.
4. Peel and chop 5 pounds apples.
5. Remove stones from 2 pounds raisins.
6. Mix together the beef, suet, apples, raisins, 2 pounds currants, 1 pound citron, 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon, 1 tablespoon grated nutmeg, 1 tablespoon cloves, 1 tablespoon allspice, 1 tablespoon salt, 3 pounds brown sugar, 1 quart wine, and the broth in which the meat was cooked.
7. Put in a stone jar with a double layer of paper over it.
8. It must be made at least a day before it is to be used.
9. When the pies are prepared, add a little more wine and a little more chopped apple to what you take for the pies.
10. This kind of pie is used especially for Christmas and other holidays and is therefore made in large batches; it will also keep a long time and is eaten both hot and cold.
