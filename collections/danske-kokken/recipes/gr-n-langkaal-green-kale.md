## Ingredients

- Green kale
- Water
- Broth
- Butter
- Potatoes, browned

## Directions

1. Use only the innermost, fine, curled leaves. Rinse well in cold water and cook half-tender in a little water. Drain this water, pour cold water over the kale, and when drained in a colander, cook tender in a little broth and butter. Arrange with browned potatoes on top.
