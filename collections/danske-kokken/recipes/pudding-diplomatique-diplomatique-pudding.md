## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound raisins
- 1 good lemon
- 1/2 pound currants
- A prepared sponge cake
- 9 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the raisins and remove the pits
2. Slice the lemon into rounds
3. Wash and clean the currants
4. Prepare a sponge cake beforehand
5. Slice the cake and layer it with the fruit in a buttered earthenware mold
6. Make a custard: whisk 9 eggs with 1 pint milk, 3 pounds sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla
7. Pour this custard over the pudding and distribute 1 tablespoon butter in small pieces on top
8. Fill the baking pan with enough boiling water to come two-thirds up the mold
9. Place the mold in the water and cook gently for 1 hour
10. Serve usually with fruit sauce
