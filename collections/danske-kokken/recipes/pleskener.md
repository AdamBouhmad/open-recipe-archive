## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- wheat flour, equal weight to eggs
- sugar, equal weight to eggs

## Directions

1. Stir eggs, sugar, and wheat flour (of equal weights) well together in a bowl
2. Set small cakes on a greased baking pan using a tablespoon
3. Bake at even heat
