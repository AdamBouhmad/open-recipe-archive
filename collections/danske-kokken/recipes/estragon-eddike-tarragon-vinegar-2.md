## Ingredients

- 2 liters finest grape vinegar
- tarragon leaves dried in shade
- lemon peel
- 5 husks Spanish pepper

## Directions

1. Use the peel of 0.5 lemon and 5 husks of Spanish pepper.
2. Pour the vinegar over the leaves and let stand for 1 month.
3. Strain through a tight cloth.
