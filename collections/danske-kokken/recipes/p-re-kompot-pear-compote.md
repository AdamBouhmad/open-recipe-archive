## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.5 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Peel not fully ripe gray pears or bergamot pears, remove the blossom end and scrape the stem.
2. During peeling, place the pears in water with a little vinegar added.
3. In a thin syrup made from the sugar and water, boil the pears until a knitting needle can be easily inserted through them.
4. Remove them and place in sulfured jars.
5. Boil the juice for 6 to 8 minutes before pouring it hot over the fruit.
6. The pears can also be cut in 2 or 4 parts and the core removed, but the fruit is most beautiful when boiled whole.
