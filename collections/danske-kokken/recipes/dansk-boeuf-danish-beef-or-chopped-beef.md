## Ingredients

- Beef from the inner leg, approximately half a pound per portion
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut beef from the inner leg into pieces, 6 to 8 pieces per pound.
2. Remove the sinews and beat each piece well with a meat mallet and chop it with a knife like carbonade.
3. Place the pieces on a platter as they are finished, but they must not touch each other.
4. Place a frying pan over the heat with some butter, and when it is browned, add the beef to it.
