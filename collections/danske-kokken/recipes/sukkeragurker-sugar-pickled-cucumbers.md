## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, split them lengthwise, soak them in cold water for a while and drain on a sieve
2. Boil water, place the cucumbers in it for one boiling and drain on a cloth
3. Cook together 2 parts vinegar, 1 part sugar by weight, some whole cloves and a little cinnamon to make a syrup, then cook the cucumbers in this syrup until done
