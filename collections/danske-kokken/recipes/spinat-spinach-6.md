## Ingredients

- Fresh-picked spinach
- Water
- Salt
- Soda
- Cold brine
- Sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the spinach and cook in very little water with salt and soda for 3 to 4 minutes
2. Pack this cooked mass in canning jars, tapping them lightly against the table
3. If there is not enough liquid with the spinach, i.e. it does not cover the spinach in the jars, then add a little cold brine with sugar addition
4. Close the jars, cook for 30 minutes and cool
5. The spinach is stewed when it is to be used
6. It can also, after being cooked, be poured over with cold water, pressed well and chopped finely, then set over heat to reduce until the liquid with it is gone
7. Pack the spinach in jars or crocks and seal carefully with butter
8. The butter is used in the preparation of the dishes for which the spinach is used
