## Ingredients

- Good smoked herring
- Milk
- Shallot
- Powdered sugar
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove bones and skin from good smoked herring and split it along the backbone into two equal halves
2. Soak them in milk for a couple of hours
3. Remove and cut each half into 3 to 4 pieces across
4. Layer these pieces with thinly sliced shallot in a crock, sprinkling a very thin layer of powdered sugar over each layer of herring
5. Pour enough vinegar into the crock to cover the herring
6. Let them stand for a day before using
