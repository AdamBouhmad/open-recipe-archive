## Ingredients

- 1 pound sifted flour
- barely 1/2 pound butter
- 1/4 ounce sugar (or slightly less)
- 2 eggs
- barely 1 gill cream
- 1/2 ounce salt

## Directions

1. Place the flour on the table and make a depression as usual; place in it the sugar, salt, butter in small pieces, eggs and cream; work together well and moisten with a little cream that has been held back.
2. This dough should be loose, so if it has become too stiff, add even more cream.
3. Before use, it should rest 1 to 2 hours in a cool place.
