## Ingredients

- Karudsen (crucian carp)
- Scales (optional)
- Cream and horseradish or thyme sauce

## Directions

1. Use only when the fish has reasonable size
2. Remove the innards but keep the head on
3. Cook whole either with scales on or scrape the scales off
4. Serve with cream and horseradish or thyme sauce
