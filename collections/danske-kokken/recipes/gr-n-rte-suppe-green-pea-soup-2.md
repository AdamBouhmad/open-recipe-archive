## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) dried green Dutch peas
- 4 to 5 pots good white meat broth
- 4 carrots
- 1 bunch

## Directions

1. Soak the peas the evening before.
2. Cook them in water and press through a sieve when they are tender.
3. Dilute the purée with 4 to 5 pots good white meat broth, in which 4 carrots and 1 bunch have been cooked.
4. Bring the soup to a boil.
5. Serve with the carved carrots or with small meat balls.
