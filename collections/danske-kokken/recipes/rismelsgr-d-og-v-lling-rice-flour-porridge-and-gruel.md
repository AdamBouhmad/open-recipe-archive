## Ingredients

- 4 pots milk
- 1 pound rice flour
- ground cinnamon
- powdered sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Take as much of 4 pots of milk as needed to stir 1 pound of rice flour into
2. Boil the remaining milk
3. When it boils, add the rice flour while stirring constantly, as it tends to sink to the bottom
4. Serve sprinkled with ground cinnamon and powdered sugar and with butter on the side
5. For gruel, use half the amount of rice flour
