## Ingredients

- Beef
- Soup or water
- Salt
- Horseradish sauce (No. 784)
- Cooked prunes

## Directions

1. Warm the meat first in soup or water with salt
2. Cut the meat
3. Place on a hot dish
4. Pour a good horseradish sauce over it (see No. 784)
5. The dish can be decorated with a ring of cooked prunes, or these can be served separately
