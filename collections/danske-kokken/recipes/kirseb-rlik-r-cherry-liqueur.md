## Ingredients

- 9 pounds crushed preserved cherries
- 6½ pounds white sugar
- 40 quarts bitter almonds
- 3 quarts cinnamon
- 5 pails rum

## Directions

1. Let the crushed cherries, white sugar, bitter almonds, cinnamon, and rum stand well covered for one week
2. Strain and pour into bottles
3. Pour the sediment with 1 pail of boiling water and set aside to steep
4. Strain this as well; do not mix this liqueur with the first and do not store it long
