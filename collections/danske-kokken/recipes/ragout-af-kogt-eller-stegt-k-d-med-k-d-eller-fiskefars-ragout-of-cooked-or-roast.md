## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onions, finely chopped
- Anchovies, chopped
- Flour
- Gravy or soup
- Sherry
- Paprika
- Pickled shallots
- Cooked or roasted meat
- Meat or fish forcemeat
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Brown butter and sauté finely chopped onions in it
2. If using anchovies, chop a few pieces and sauté with the onions
3. Brown flour in the mixture
4. Dilute with gravy or soup to the desired consistency
5. Season the sauce with sherry, paprika, and pickled shallots
6. Give the meat a quick boil in the sauce
7. Pour onto a flat serving dish
8. Cover the ragout with a layer of meat or fish forcemeat
9. Brush with egg, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and salt
10. Bake in a hot oven until the forcemeat is cooked, which takes about 1/2 hour
