## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound finely sifted flour
- a little hartshorn salt
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks for about an hour with 1 pound powdered sugar and 1 pound butter
2. Then stir in 1 pound finely sifted flour and finally fold in the 10 beaten egg whites
3. Mix a little hartshorn salt into the flour
4. Place the mixture in a form buttered and dusted with flour, and bake 1 hour in a moderately warm oven
5. When the cake is removed from the oven, let it rest in the form for an hour, covered with a warm folded cloth
6. Only when it is cold should it be turned out of the form, as otherwise it easily becomes sticky inside
