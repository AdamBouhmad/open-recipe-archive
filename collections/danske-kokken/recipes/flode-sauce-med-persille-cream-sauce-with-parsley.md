## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Cream
- Chopped parsley
- A small piece of white sugar
- Salt
- Milk (optional, as substitute for cream)

## Directions

1. Melt butter and stir flour into it
2. Add cream gradually until the sauce is smooth
3. Add chopped parsley and bring to a boil
4. Add a small piece of white sugar and salt to taste
5. If using milk instead of cream, use more butter
