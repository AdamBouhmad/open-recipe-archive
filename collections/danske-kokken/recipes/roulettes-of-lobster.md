## Ingredients

- lobster
- butter
- flour
- nutmeg
- sugar
- broth
- salt
- milk or water

## Directions

1. Cook the lobsters, remove the meat from the bones and cut it into cubes
2. Pound the shells with butter, sauté over the fire and add water
3. When it has cooked a quarter hour, strain it, and put the lobster butter back on the fire with flour, a little nutmeg and a little sugar and add broth until it becomes a thick sauce
4. Stir in the lobster meat and a little salt at the end
5. When cold, cook as described for roulettes of meat
6. The shells can also be cooked with milk, in which case the lobster butter is diluted with cream or good milk
