## Ingredients

- 3.5 L (3.5 pots) good white stock
- 1 kg (2 lb) asparagus
- 100 grams (20 quintal) butter
- 100 grams (20 quintal) flour
- 4 egg yolks
- Sherry or Madeira to taste
- Sugar
- Salt
- 0.5 kg (1 S) cooked medium asparagus

## Directions

1. Wash thin asparagus well and cut into small pieces.
2. Cook tender in 2 L (2 pots) water.
3. Strain the asparagus and pass through a sieve with 3 L (3 pots) good soup along with the asparagus water.
4. Brown 100 grams butter and 100 grams flour together and dilute with the strained soup.
5. When it boils, bind with 4 egg yolks.
6. Season with sherry or Madeira, sugar, and salt.
7. Serve with 0.5 kg of cooked, cut medium asparagus and serve with toasted bread.
