## Ingredients

- Leftover veal roast
- Stock from the roast
- Curry
- Paprika
- Cooked rice

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover veal roast into cubes or slices and place in the bottom of a deep dish that can be placed in the oven
2. Make a good smooth sauce from the stock of the roast
3. Season the sauce with curry and paprika
4. Pour some of the sauce over the meat
5. Lay a thick layer of cooked rice (prepared as for curry) over this
6. Pour the remaining strongly spiced sauce over the rice
7. Place the dish in the oven until the ragout is heated through
