## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 3 teaspoons royal baking powder
- 1 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup butter, 2 cups sugar, 4 cups flour, 4 eggs, 3 teaspoons royal baking powder, and 1 cup sweet milk
2. Bake in layers
3. For the filling: heat half a cake of baker's chocolate with half a cup powdered sugar and enough hot water to cover, boil for a couple of minutes, cool, and add half a teaspoon vanilla
4. Spread between each layer and over the top in the usual way
