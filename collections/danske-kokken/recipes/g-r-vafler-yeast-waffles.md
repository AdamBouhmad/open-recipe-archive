## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 Q) flour
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) melted margarine
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 good tablespoon yeast
- lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Mix into 1/2 kg (1 Q) flour, 1/4 kg (1/2 S) melted margarine, 6 egg yolks, and 1 good tablespoon of yeast so much lukewarm milk that it becomes a light batter like for apple pancakes; set to rise.
