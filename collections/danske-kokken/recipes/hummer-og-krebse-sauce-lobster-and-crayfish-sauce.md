## Ingredients

- Fresh cooked lobster
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock
- Parsley
- Lemon juice
- White pepper
- Salt
- Lobster and crayfish extract (alternative method)

## Directions

1. Shell the meat from a fresh, cooked lobster and pound the shells in 100 gr (20 Kvint) of butter
2. Put the whole in a casserole together with a whisk of parsley and cover with 1 L (1 Pot) of stock
3. After it has boiled a few minutes, strain off the stock
4. Skim the butter and use it to bake the flour for the sauce, which is thinned with the strained stock
5. Season with lemon juice, white pepper, and salt
6. Cut the lobster meat into smaller pieces and add to the sauce
7. Alternatively, instead of pounding the shells, simply add a little of the excellent lobster and crayfish extract available in Danish wine and preserved foods factories
8. Dissolve the extract in the hot sauce, which it gives a beautiful color and excellent lobster flavor
9. Good for lobster soup made from thin clear stock with fish balls
10. Asparagus can be added to lobster sauce and soup
