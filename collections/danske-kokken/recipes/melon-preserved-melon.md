## Ingredients

- Ripe melon, peeled and sliced
- Powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Layer the ripe, peeled, and sliced melon with sugar and set aside until the next day.
2. Pour the liquid that has formed into a pot, bring to a boil, and skim before pouring hot over the melon slices.
3. Repeat this for the 2 following days.
4. The melon must not be cooked.
