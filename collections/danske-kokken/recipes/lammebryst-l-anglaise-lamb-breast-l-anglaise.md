## Ingredients

- Lamb breast with brisket
- Egg
- Bread
- Pepper
- Salt
- Palmin or lard
- Broth
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Puff pastry slices

## Directions

1. When the breast with brisket is cooked tender, brush it with egg, sprinkle with bread, pepper and salt, and brown it on a pan in abundant Palmin or lard.
2. Make a brown mushroom sauce from the broth (see Nr. 758), which is served separately.
3. Cut the meat and garnish with parsley and mushrooms, and place puff pastry slices around it.
