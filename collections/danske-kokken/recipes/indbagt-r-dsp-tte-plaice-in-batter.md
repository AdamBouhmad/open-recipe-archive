## Ingredients

- plaice fillets
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- margarine
- sugar
- milk
- butter
- lard or Palmin
- lemon
- lettuce
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut out fillets and dry after standing sprinkled with salt
2. Mix batter: 1/4 kg flour, 3 eggs, 50 gr melted margarine, salt, slight sugar and 2½ dl milk stirred together to a thick pancake batter
3. Take fish with a fork and dip into batter so it completely covers it
4. Place directly into hot lard or Palmin
5. Cook quickly until batter has a nice light brown color
6. Serve with stirred butter, chutney or tarragon butter, or with mayonnaise or maître d'hôtel butter
7. Place stirred butter in a bowl in center of platter, arrange fish around it decorated with lemon, green salad or parsley
