## Ingredients

- 4.5 L (4 pots) soup
- Pea pods
- Water
- Bicarbonate of soda
- 125 grams (0.25 lb) butter
- 125 grams (0.25 lb) flour
- Whole cooked peas
- Sliced carrots
- Spinach juice (optional for color)
- Baked bread

## Directions

1. Cook pea pods 1/2 hour in water with a little bicarbonate of soda.
2. Place in a hay box for 4-5 hours to become very tender.
3. Press through a sieve with stock.
4. Brown 125 grams butter and 125 grams flour together and dilute with the strained soup.
5. Add some whole cooked peas and sliced carrots.
6. For a nice green color, add juice from pressed spinach strained through a sieve.
7. Serve with baked bread.
