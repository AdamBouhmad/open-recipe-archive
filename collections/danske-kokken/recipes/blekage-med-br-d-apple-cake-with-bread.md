## Ingredients

- apples
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- sugar
- white wine or water
- 1 pound grated French bread
- butter
- powdered sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and remove the cores
2. Cook them with lemon zest, cinnamon, sugar, and a little white wine or water into a compote or porridge
3. Bake 1 pound grated French bread golden brown in butter
4. Butter a form and dust with grated bread
5. In the bottom of the form lay first a layer of bread, then a layer of apple porridge, over which sprinkle a layer of powdered sugar, then bread again, apple porridge, sugar, and so forth, until the form is full
6. Place it in the oven and bake 1 hour
7. When done and somewhat cooled, turn it onto a dish and serve sprinkled with white powdered sugar and with whipped cream on the side
8. Instead of sugar, one can also place sweet preserves, for example, raspberry marmalade, between the layers
