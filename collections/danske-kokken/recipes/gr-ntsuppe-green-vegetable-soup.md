## Ingredients

- 5 Potter tynd Kjødsuppe (5 pots of thin meat broth)
- Et stort Hvidkaalshoved (a large head of white cabbage)
- Unge, rensede Supperødder (young, cleaned soup vegetables)
- Nogle Porrer (some leeks)
- En Suppevisk (a bunch of herbs)
- 2 Pægle unge, pillede Grønærter (2 pecks of young, shelled peas)
- 1 Pund ituskaarne Asparges (1 pound of cut-up asparagus)
- Lidt itupillet Blomkaal (some separated cauliflower)

## Directions

1. Bring 5 pots of thin meat broth to a boil.
2. Add strips of a large cabbage head along with young cleaned soup vegetables cut into strips, some leeks, and a bunch of herbs.
3. When the vegetables are half tender, add 2 pecks of young shelled peas, 1 pound of cut-up asparagus, and some separated cauliflower.
4. Cook until all the vegetables are tender.
