## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) icing sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) half-skimmed boiling milk
- 12 leaves isinglass
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) white rum
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream
- cold fruit juice sauce, preferably of raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Stir 5 egg yolks with 125 Gr (1/4 S) icing sugar and whisk into 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) half-skimmed boiling milk
2. Cook to custard consistency
3. Dissolve 12 leaves of isinglass in 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water and stir into the custard
4. When the custard has cooled, add 1/4 L (1 Pægl) white rum and cream foam from 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream
5. When the mixture begins to set, pour it into a wetted mold
6. Make this pudding the day before
7. Serve with cold fruit juice sauce, preferably of raspberry juice
