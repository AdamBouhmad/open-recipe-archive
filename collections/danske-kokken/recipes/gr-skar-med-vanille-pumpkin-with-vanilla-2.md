## Ingredients

- 1/4 dl vinegar
- 1/4 dl water
- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg peeled pumpkin
- 1 vanilla pod (for every 3 kg pumpkin)

## Directions

1. Boil vinegar, water, and sugar together to make a syrup.
2. Add the pumpkin cut into pieces as previously explained.
3. Boil it. Skim the liquid and remove the pumpkin pieces when they are translucent.
4. Boil the liquid together with the chopped vanilla until it reaches the right consistency.
5. Add the pumpkin back into the boiling liquid.
6. Pour into jars or glasses and tie them up when the preserve has cooled.
