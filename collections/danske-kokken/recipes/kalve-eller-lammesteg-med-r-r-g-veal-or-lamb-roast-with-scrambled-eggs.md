## Ingredients

- Veal or lamb roast
- Stock from the roast
- Butter
- Flour
- Scrambled eggs
- Chives, chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the roast into cubes or slices
2. Warm these in a brown stock sauce made from the stock of the roast
3. Arrange the warmed roast on a hot, flat dish
4. Garnish with a ring of scrambled eggs
5. Sprinkle with chopped chives
