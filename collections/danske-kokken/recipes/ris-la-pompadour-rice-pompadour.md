## Ingredients

- ¼ kilo (½ pound) rice
- 1 liter (1 pot) half-skimmed milk
- 125 grams (¼ pound) powdered sugar
- vanilla essence
- orange essence
- 12 sheets isinglass
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) boiling milk
- 10 stiffly beaten egg whites
- powdered sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in half-skimmed milk until soft.
2. Stir in powdered sugar along with vanilla and orange essence to taste and isinglass dissolved in boiling milk.
3. When the pudding is almost cold but not set, stir in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the rice into a wetted mold sprinkled with powdered sugar.
5. Place the pudding in a cold location and serve with chocolate sauce (see no. 803).
