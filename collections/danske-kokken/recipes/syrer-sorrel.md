## Ingredients

- sorrel
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove the greenest stems from the sorrel and cook them in barely enough water if they are not very sour, but if they are quite sour use plenty of water, some of which should be poured off before they are fully cooked
2. When tender, pour off the water and press them through a sieve and place on heat with a good piece of butter, sugar to taste and a little salt
