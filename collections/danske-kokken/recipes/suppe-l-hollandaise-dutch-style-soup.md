## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) economical stock
- 4 carrots
- 4 parsnips
- 0.5 L (0.5 pot) shelled peas
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots and parsnips into small dice and cook tender in some of the stock together with 0.5 L (0.5 pot) shelled peas
2. Brown 125 g (1/4 lb) butter with 125 g (1/4 lb) flour and dilute with 5 L (5 pots) stock in which the vegetable stock has been mixed
3. When it has cooked, remove from heat while 6 well-beaten egg yolks are stirred in
