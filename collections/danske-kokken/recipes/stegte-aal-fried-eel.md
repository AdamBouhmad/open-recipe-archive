## Ingredients

- Eel
- Salt
- Flour
- Egg or egg white
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or claret
- Lemon juice
- Parsley
- Boiled potatoes or potato purée

## Directions

1. After the eels are skinned, cut them into 8 centimeter (3 inch) long pieces
2. Rinse the pieces and sprinkle them with salt, let them stand for one hour, and then dry them in a fish cloth
3. Turn the pieces first in flour, then dip them in egg or just in egg white, and finally in breadcrumbs
4. Fry them in a pan in browned butter or claret
5. Serve with browned butter mixed with lemon juice and boiled potatoes or potato purée
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and lemon slices
