## Ingredients

- 1 can preserved oysters, containing approximately 375 grams (1/4 pound)
- 1 large English celery
- Oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Finely ground white pepper
- Ice
- Mayonnaise (made with 2 egg yolks)
- Watercress

## Directions

1. Marinate the oysters in a mixture of oil, lemon juice, salt and a small amount of finely ground white pepper.
2. Set aside for a couple of hours in an icebox or cold place.
3. Rinse the celery and cut out the most tender and best of it in very thin slices.
4. Place them in a bowl and add a piece of ice.
5. When the salad is to be served, drain the celery together with the oysters.
6. Place a layer in the salad bowl, cover with mayonnaise, then again celery and oysters and mayonnaise as the top layer.
7. Garnish with watercress.
8. Make the mayonnaise from 2 egg yolks.
