## Ingredients

- 150 grams butter
- 200 grams Pillsbury flour
- 4 liters milk
- 100 grams almonds
- salt
- sugar
- almond essence (optional)
- bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Melt 150 grams of butter or margarine
2. Roast 200 grams of Pillsbury flour in the butter
3. Dilute gradually with at least 4 liters of boiling, sweet milk
4. Cook 100 grams of chopped almonds with some bitter almonds in the mixture, or use almond essence instead
5. Season with salt and sugar
6. Serve with sugar and cinnamon, or with jam or fruit compote
