## Ingredients

- 1 pair calf sweetbreads
- 1/2 kg calf meat
- butter
- flour
- stock from the sweetbreads
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 leaves dissolved isinglass
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour and thin with the stock from the sweetbreads to a thick sauce.
2. Bind this with 2 egg yolks and add 4 leaves dissolved isinglass, salt, pepper, and a few drops of lemon juice.
3. Last, add the coarsely chopped sweetbreads and the cooked, chopped calf meat.
4. Form the cold stew and otherwise treat as Roulettes (Nr. 16).
5. These croquettes are placed on the platter as garnish around braised vegetables.
