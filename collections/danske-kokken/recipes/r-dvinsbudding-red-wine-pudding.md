## Ingredients

- 1 bottle red wine (St. Julien)
- 1 pail Cognac
- 2 pails water
- 3/4 pound white sugar
- rind of 1 lemon
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 2 cardamom pods
- 10 quintals isinglass dissolved in boiling water

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil 1 bottle red wine, 1 pail Cognac, 2 pails water, 3/4 pound white sugar, the rind of 1 lemon, the juice of 2 lemons, 1 piece cinnamon, and 2 cardamom pods.
2. Dissolve 10 quintals isinglass in boiling water and add it cooled to the rest.
3. Mix the whole well, stir from time to time, strain, and pour into a jelly mold.
4. Let it stand for several hours on ice; if you do not have ice, the pudding must be made the day before it is to be used, and the mold should stand in cold water.
5. Serve with cream sauce.
