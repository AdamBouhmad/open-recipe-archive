## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 4½ pounds white sugar
- 6 kvint isinglass
- a couple of teaspoons powdered vanilla
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pot sweet cream, 4½ pounds white sugar and 6 kvint isinglass until the sugar is dissolved.
2. Stir in a couple of teaspoons powdered vanilla.
3. Pour this mixture into 12 egg yolks, which have first been stirred until white.
4. When nearly cold, pour into a mold and preferably let stand until the next day.
5. Serve with sweet fruit sauce.
