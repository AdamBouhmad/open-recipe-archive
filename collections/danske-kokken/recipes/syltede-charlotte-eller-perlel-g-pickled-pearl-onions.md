## Ingredients

- pearl onions
- sweet milk
- cold water
- vinegar
- white pepper
- nutmeg
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions with the help of a silver or glass knife and place them gradually in sweet milk as they are peeled
2. Rinse them completely clean in cold water
3. Heat vinegar with a little white pepper and nutmeg over heat, boil and skim
4. Place the onions in this and cook until they are tender
5. If you want the onions to be sweet and sour, boil the vinegar the first time with sugar in the proportion of 1 pound granulated sugar to 4 pails vinegar
