## Ingredients

- Sauce as in recipe 2
- Small fish balls
- Asparagus water
- Gravy
- Lobster and crab extract (optional)

## Directions

1. Make sauce as in recipe 2
2. Cook small fish balls in asparagus water
3. Add gravy to the sauce
4. Lobster and crab extract may be added to the white, smooth sauce; egg yolks can then be omitted
5. See fish farce recipe 205-206 for preparation of fish balls
