## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 8 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- egg whites
- 3 ounces bacon

## Directions

1. Whip 10 egg yolks until they are thick, into which then stir 8 tablespoons of sweet milk, in which 1 tablespoon of flour has first been dissolved, and finally the whipped egg whites.
2. Fry 3 ounces of bacon in slices in a pan, and drain the fat.
3. Pour the egg batter over the bacon, and the bacon cake is baked until it is light brown on the underside.
4. It can now either be served as is or with the help of a lid be turned and baked light brown on the other side as well.
5. Some like to sprinkle the bacon cake with chopped chives before baking.
