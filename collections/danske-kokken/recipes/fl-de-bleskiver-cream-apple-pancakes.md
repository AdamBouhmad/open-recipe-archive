## Ingredients

- 1 Pint sweet cream
- 2 Egg yolks
- 0.5 Pint fine wheat flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls powdered sugar
- A little finely powdered cardamom
- 2 Egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pint sweet cream well with 2 egg yolks, 0.5 pint fine wheat flour, 2 tablespoonfuls powdered sugar, and a little finely powdered cardamom.
2. Beat the 2 egg whites to stiff foam and fold into the rest.
3. Bake the æbleskiver in a hot pan in melted butter.
4. Serve with caster sugar over.
