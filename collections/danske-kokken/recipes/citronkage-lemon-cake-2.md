## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- 1/4 hour time
- 3/4 pound powdered sugar
- Zest of 3 lemons
- Juice of 3 lemons
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks for 1/4 hour with 3/4 pound powdered sugar and the grated zest of 3 lemons
2. Add the juice of the 3 lemons and the whipped egg whites
3. Bake the cake until pale brown in a pan at even heat and serve with whipped cream over top
