## Ingredients

- Large mealy potatoes
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- White pepper, ground
- Milk

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and boil them tender in salted water.
2. Drain the water.
3. Mash the potatoes with a spoon or potato masher.
4. If lumps remain, press them through a sieve or coarse strainer.
5. Place the mash over heat with a piece of butter, salt, ground white pepper, and enough milk to achieve the proper consistency.
6. When arranged, decorate with a fluted butter knife if desired.
