## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- zest of 2 oranges
- zest of 1 lemon
- juice of 4 oranges
- juice of 1 lemon
- 14 egg yolks
- 9 quintals cold dissolved isinglass
- 14 stiffly beaten egg whites
- raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 pound sugar and the grated zest of 2 oranges and 1 lemon for 1 hour with the juice of 4 oranges, juice of 1 lemon, 14 egg yolks in which 9 quintals cold dissolved isinglass has been mixed, and finally with 14 stiffly beaten egg whites.
2. Serve with raspberry juice.
