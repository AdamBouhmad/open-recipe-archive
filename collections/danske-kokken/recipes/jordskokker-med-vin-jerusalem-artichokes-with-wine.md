## Ingredients

- small Jerusalem artichokes
- butter
- flour
- water from cooking the artichokes
- egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- Maggi seasoning
- wine

## Directions

1. Peel small Jerusalem artichokes
2. Cook them
3. Make a white, smooth sauce of butter and flour
4. Dilute the sauce with the water in which the artichokes were cooked
5. Thicken this sauce with 1 to 2 egg yolks
6. Add salt, sugar, Maggi seasoning and wine to taste
