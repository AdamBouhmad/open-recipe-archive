## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound rye bread
- 3 quarts water
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- 1 glass white wine
- juice and zest of 2 lemons
- sugar
- 1/2 pint thick cream

## Directions

1. Slice the rye bread small and boil in 3 quarts of water until smooth
2. Rub through a sieve and set back on heat with lemon zest and cinnamon
3. When the soup boils, add 1 glass of white wine, juice and zest of 2 lemons, and sugar
4. Whip 1/2 pint of thick cream to foam in the terrine and pour the soup over it while whisking with a whisk
5. If you do not have sliced bread to use, the bread can be grated, and then the soup does not need to be strained
