## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe berries
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1 pound sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Dust 1 quart of ripe berries with 1/2 pound of sugar.
2. Let them stand for 3 hours, then crush them and press through a thin cloth.
3. Add 1 pound of sugar, and when it is dissolved, beat in the cream little by little.
4. Then freeze as quickly as possible.
