## Ingredients

- Mutton meat
- Butter
- Flour
- Eggs
- White sauce
- White root vegetables
- Asparagus (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop the mutton as for fricassee and cook it, but not too thoroughly.
2. Clean the pieces of outer skin and remove any loose bones.
3. Brown them lightly in butter.
4. Prepare a dumpling dough without sugar and with one or two more eggs.
5. Spread this dough around all sides of the pieces.
6. Brown them again very lightly in butter.
7. Serve with white sauce and white root vegetables; asparagus is also suitable.
