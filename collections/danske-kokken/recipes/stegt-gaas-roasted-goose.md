## Ingredients

- Goose
- Apples
- Prunes
- Salt
- Pepper
- Little water
- Wheat flour
- Bouillon
- Potatoes
- Red cabbage

## Directions

1. Pluck the goose, singe over burning spirits, rinse and dry well.
2. Chop off the neck, wings and feet, which are used with giblets, liver and heart for giblet soup.
3. Open the goose and remove the entrails. Wash well and rub inside with salt and pepper, then fill with peeled and chopped apples and rinsed prunes, and sew together at both the neck and the rear.
4. Roast with very little water for 2 to 3 hours in a warm oven without basting until tender.
5. When serving, remove the thread. Skim the fat off the sauce, thicken with flour and bouillon and serve with the roast along with potatoes and red cabbage.
