## Ingredients

- 1 plate full of chopped eggs
- butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- meat stock

## Directions

1. When a plate full of chopped eggs is browned in butter, stir in 6 tablespoons flour; thereafter gradually add stock and let it cook for an hour
2. Strain it, skim off the fat, and give it another boil
3. Eaten with baked bread
