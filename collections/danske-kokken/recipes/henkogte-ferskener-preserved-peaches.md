## Ingredients

- Peaches, ripe but firm
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Peaches should be ripe but firm in the flesh; the large white ones are best
2. Peel them, cut in half and remove stones, place immediately in cold water
3. In an enamelled pot place fruit and sugar in layers (a large tablespoon of sugar or a little more to each quart of fruit)
4. Bring slowly to a boil and cook only 3 minutes or until all fruit is well heated through
5. Fill immediately into jars and screw shut
