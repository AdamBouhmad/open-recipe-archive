## Ingredients

- pike or cod
- butter
- flour
- soup or bouillon
- capers
- mushrooms
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook pike or cod and pick it free from skin and bones. Bake butter and flour together and thin with soup, adding capers, mushrooms, pepper, and salt. When the sauce is finished, add the fish and stir carefully, but it must not boil. Then strain it to cool, and when it is cold, form roulettes from it as in No. 203. The roulettes are served hot as a side dish with mushroom or caper sauce.
