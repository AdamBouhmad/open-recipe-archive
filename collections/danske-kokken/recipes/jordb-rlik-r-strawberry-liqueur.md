## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 pounds strawberries
- 2 bottles good rum or cognac
- 1½ pounds white sugar
- ½ pound white sugar or crushed rock candy dissolved in 15 pails rum and filtered

## Directions

1. Let strawberries, rum or cognac, and 1½ pounds sugar stand to steep for 5 to 6 days
2. Strain and add ½ pound white sugar or crushed rock candy, which has been dissolved in 15 pails rum and filtered
3. Pour the liqueur into bottles
