## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of cherries and currants
- 565 gr sugar
- 1 1/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Use equal quantities of cherries and currants.
2. Remove the pits from the cherries, which are pounded in the mortar, and pick over the currants before pressing them.
3. Mix the cherries and currant juice now in the jelly cloth, where it remains until the next day; then weigh the juice and cook into jelly.
4. See its preparation in No. 93.
