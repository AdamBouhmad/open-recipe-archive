## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons oil
- 3 tablespoons tomato puree
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- Mustard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tarragon vinegar
- 10 anchovies
- 6 to 8 pickled shallots
- 1 deep plate finely cut meat

## Directions

1. Mix together the oil, tomato puree, chopped parsley, mustard, salt, pepper and tarragon vinegar.
2. Chop the meat from 10 anchovies and 6 to 8 pickled shallots and add to the sauce together with a deep plate of finely cut meat.
