## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) powdered flour sugar
- a small amount of cream of tartar, just the amount on the tip of a teaspoon

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to very stiff peaks
2. Carefully fold in the powdered flour sugar and a small amount of cream of tartar
3. Place on buttered baking sheets with a serving spoon or pipe onto the sheets
4. Dry for a couple of hours in a warm oven
5. Store in a tin box in a dry place
