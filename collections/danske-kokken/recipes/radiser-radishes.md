## Ingredients

- Radishes
- Salt
- Butter
- Mayonnaise (Nr. 753)

## Directions

1. Remove the outermost coarse leaves, leaving only the heart leaves
2. Scrape the radish well around these leaves, rinse, and serve with salt
3. Alternatively, arrange them on a larger plate, in the center of which place a smaller dish with creamed butter or Mayonnaise (Nr. 753), which is eaten with the radishes
