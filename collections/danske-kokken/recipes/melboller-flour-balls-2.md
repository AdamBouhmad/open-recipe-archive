## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- approximately 1/4 pound wheat flour
- 1 pail boiling water
- 4 eggs
- sugar to taste
- salt to taste
- nutmeg or cardamom to taste
- currants, rinsed and dried (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and stir in enough wheat flour to make it stiff
2. Bake while gradually adding the boiling water until the dough no longer sticks to the pan or spoon
3. Place the dough over coals and bake for another half hour while stirring
4. Remove from heat to cool slightly
5. Stir in the eggs one at a time until the dough becomes smooth and white
6. Add sugar, salt, nutmeg or cardamom to taste, and if desired, some rinsed and washed currants
7. Place the balls on the soup and cook for at most 10-15 minutes over steady heat
