## Ingredients

- 2 to 2.5 kg pears
- 4.5 L water
- Graves wine, rhubarb juice, or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook like apple soup
2. Add acidity with Graves wine, rhubarb juice, or lemon juice as pears often need some sourness
