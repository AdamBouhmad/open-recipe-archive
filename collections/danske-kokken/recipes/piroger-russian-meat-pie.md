## Ingredients

- Three quarters pound cooked beef, preferably ox beef
- a little onion
- 2 tablespoons flour
- a piece of butter
- salt
- pepper
- gravy or soup
- a little chopped anchovy fish
- some wine
- 1 pound flour
- 3/4 pound butter
- 4 tablespoons thick cream
- 3 eggs
- a splash of rum
- egg yolk
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked beef with a little onion and 2 tablespoons flour. Brown a piece of butter and add the chopped beef along with salt, pepper, and gravy or soup, cooking it together into a thick hash. A little chopped anchovy fish and some wine may be added if desired. Make a dough from 1 pound flour and 3/4 pound butter, kneaded well together. Stir 4 tablespoons thick cream, 3 eggs, and a splash of rum into the dough with a spoon. Knead until it releases from the hands, then roll out very thinly, using flour outside the weight. Place a spoonful of hash on a piece of dough and fold it over to form a half-moon shape, cutting the edges with a small cutter. Brush the outside with egg yolk, bake at a brisk heat in the oven or in a baking pan for about a quarter hour. Serve hot with a strong gravy sauce. This amount is for 4 pieces or 10-12 portions.
