## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg beef
- 0.5 kg pork
- 8 rusks
- 2 eggs
- salt
- a few spoonfuls of cream
- smoked bacon for larding
- milk for basting
- butter
- flour
- gravy
- raw cream
- food coloring
- browned potatoes
- parsley
- currant or lingonberry jelly

## Directions

1. Scrape and mince the meat finely together with the crushed rusks, eggs, salt, and a few spoonfuls of cream.
2. Form the forcemeat in the shape of a hare saddle.
3. Lard it deeply and densely with smoked bacon.
4. Roast in the oven for 1 hour, basting frequently with milk.
5. For the sauce, brown butter and flour, thin with gravy, and before serving add a little raw cream and food coloring.
6. Garnish with browned potatoes and parsley.
7. Serve with currant or lingonberry jelly.
