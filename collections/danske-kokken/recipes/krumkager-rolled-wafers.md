## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- ½ pound sugar
- ½ pound butter, clarified
- 4 eggs
- approximately 1½ pint milk
- ⅓ gill cream, whipped to foam
- a little jam

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pound flour, ½ pound sugar, ½ pound butter (clarified), 4 eggs, and approximately 1½ pint milk well together and bake
2. The iron does not need to be greased for each cake
3. When the cake is taken from the iron, roll it like a cone
4. Whip ⅓ gill cream to foam; put 1 spoonful in each cake and on top a small teaspoonful jam
5. The foam must be put in the cakes just before they are to be used, otherwise they become soft; you can also place jam on a dish, put the foam on it and serve it together with the cakes
6. This makes approximately 70 cakes
