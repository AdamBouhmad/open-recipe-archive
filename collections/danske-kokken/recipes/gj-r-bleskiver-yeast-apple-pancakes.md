## Ingredients

- 2 Pints lukewarm milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls yeast
- A little powdered sugar
- Cardamom
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pint melted butter
- Fine wheat flour

## Directions

1. Stir 2 tablespoonfuls yeast into 2 pints lukewarm milk.
2. Add a little powdered sugar, cardamom, 4 eggs, 1 pint melted butter, and so much fine wheat flour that the mixture has the thickness of gruel.
3. Set the dough on a warm place to rise.
4. Bake the æbleskiver light brown in butter.
5. Serve with powdered sugar.
