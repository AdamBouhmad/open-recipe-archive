## Ingredients

- 1 liter (1 pot) cream
- approximately 375 g (1/4 tb) flour
- 1 good tablespoon sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the cream to foam and then carefully beat in approximately 375 g (1/4 tb) flour, but only a little at a time, together with a good tablespoon of sugar.
2. Heat the waffle iron thoroughly and butter it with a cloth.
3. During baking, turn the iron so the waffles bake evenly light brown on both sides.
4. Sprinkle with crushed powdered sugar and serve preferably warm. Sour or sweet cream or half of each can be used. They become soft when stacked on top of each other.
