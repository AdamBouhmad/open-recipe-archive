## Ingredients

- Scrape meat
- Kidney fat
- Fat
- Salt
- Ground nutmeg
- Ground white pepper
- Onion
- Cloves
- Ginger
- Intestines

## Directions

1. The filling can be made from various kinds of meat but must be softer than the filling for meatballs and should stand for one day.
2. From the waste of the scrape meat, cook a soup with onion, cloves and a piece of ginger.
3. Knead the scrape meat with finely chopped kidney fat and fat along with salt, ground nutmeg and white ground pepper until it becomes a tough mass.
4. Dilute and knead it with the lukewarm soup, from which the fat has been skimmed.
5. When the filling is ready, it should be quite smooth, after which it is filled into intestines and stuffed fairly loosely.
6. During cooking, the sausages must be pricked with a needle so they do not burst.
7. The sausages can be preserved for some time if they are simmered in strong soup with melted fat on top.
