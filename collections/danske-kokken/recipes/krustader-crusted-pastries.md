## Ingredients

- 40 ounces fine flour
- 6 ounces clarified butter
- 1 egg
- 2 cups white beer
- 1 cup water

## Directions

1. Mix flour, clarified butter, egg, white beer, and water to form a smooth batter of the consistency of pancake batter
2. Heat the croustade iron well in boiling butter or clarified fat
3. Dip the iron in the batter
4. Place it back in the hot butter or fat and fry until the croustade becomes a beautiful light brown
5. Remove from the iron carefully with a thin knife at the edge of the iron
6. Place upside down on a plate until needed (preferably the same day)
7. If croustades must be kept, cover them so they do not lose their crispness
8. Fill with similar filling as used for small pâtés
9. Allow 8 to 10 per serving
