## Ingredients

- Venison liver
- Salt and pepper
- Flour
- Butter
- Onion
- Game stock or soup

## Directions

1. Soak the liver.
2. Remove the skin and cut the liver into thin slices.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Dip in flour and fry in butter on the pan.
5. Slice onion, brown it in butter, and brown some flour for the sauce.
6. Dilute with game stock or other good soup or stock.
7. Pour the sauce over the liver at serving.
8. Serve with cooked, peeled potatoes.
