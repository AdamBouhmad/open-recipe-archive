## Ingredients

- Large shells (Coquille shells)
- Oysters
- Bread crumbs
- White wine
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Cooked and boned Cod (optional)

## Directions

1. Place 4 oysters in each large shell, with the beard removed.
2. Add a little bread crumbs, white wine, a few drops of lemon juice and a little butter to each shell.
3. Cover with a thin layer of bread crumbs with small pieces of butter on top.
4. Keep the oyster liquid in the shells.
5. Bake in the oven for about a quarter hour until the bread crust is a nice light brown.
6. Optionally, the above ingredients can be mixed with a little fine, cooked and boned cod.
7. Cook the beards in a little water, strain it and store in bottles for use in oyster sauce.
