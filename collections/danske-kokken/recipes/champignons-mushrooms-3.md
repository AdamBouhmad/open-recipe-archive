## Ingredients

- Small closed mushrooms
- Wool cloth
- Lukewarm salt water
- Clean salt water
- Boiling water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- 10 gr (2 Kvint) salt to 1 l (1 Pot) water

## Directions

1. Cut off the bottom of the stems of small closed mushrooms
2. Wash them with a wool cloth and lukewarm salt water
3. As they are cleaned, place them in clean salt water
4. Do not remove the gills from small partially closed mushrooms, but remove them from large, open ones, and also remove the skin from these
5. Take the mushrooms out of the salt water and pour boiling water mixed with a little vinegar and salt over them
6. Let them stand for 1.5 hours, then drain on a sieve
7. This treatment should be done before canning in all cases
8. Give the mushrooms a 3 minute boiling in water with salt, 10 gr (2 Kvint) to 1 l (1 Pot)
9. When the mushrooms are filled in the jar, pour the water in which they were boiled over them, but let it be well cooled beforehand
10. Attach the lid, rubber ring and clamp and boil the jars for 30 to 35 minutes
11. Cool before storing
