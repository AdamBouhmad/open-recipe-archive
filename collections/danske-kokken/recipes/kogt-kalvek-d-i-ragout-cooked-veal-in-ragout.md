## Ingredients

- White mushroom sauce (see Nr. 775)
- Egg yolks
- White wine or sherry
- Cooked veal, cut into pieces
- Puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Make a white mushroom sauce bound with egg yolks and seasoned with a little white wine or sherry
2. Add the cut cooked meat
3. Garnish with puff pastry strips when serving
4. Tomato sauce (see Nr. 790) and rice border can be used instead of mushrooms
