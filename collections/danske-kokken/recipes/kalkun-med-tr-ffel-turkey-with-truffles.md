## Ingredients

- young turkey
- veal meat from the leg
- kidney fat
- beef marrow
- truffles
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- cognac

## Directions

1. Clean a young, fat turkey as usual and remove the innards.
2. Pat dry but do not wash.
3. Finely chop 4 pounds of veal meat from the leg with 3 pounds of kidney fat, 4 pounds of beef marrow, and a small glass of truffles, setting aside some of the largest truffle pieces.
4. Work the stuffing together with 3 eggs, salt, pepper, and a glass of good cognac.
5. While the turkey is still warm, fill the neck skin with the stuffing and sew it closed.
6. Fill the inside cavity with the remaining truffles and any leftover stuffing, being careful not to overfill as the stuffing will expand.
7. Let the turkey rest for a couple of days to absorb the truffle flavor.
8. Roast over gentle heat and arrange whole with truffle sauce.
9. Mushrooms may be substituted for truffles if desired.
