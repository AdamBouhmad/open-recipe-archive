## Ingredients

- 2.5 pails red wine
- juice of 1 lemon
- grated rind of 1/2 lemon
- 4 pounds sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 4.5 quintals dissolved isinglass
- 3/4 pail red liqueur
- 1.5 pails sweet cream, whipped to foam

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil 2.5 pails red wine, the juice of 1 lemon, and the grated rind of 1/2 lemon with 4 pounds sugar.
2. Mix 10 egg yolks with 4.5 quintals dissolved isinglass and whip together with the remaining ingredients until it is about to boil.
3. Add 3/4 pail red liqueur, then pour the entire pudding into a dish.
4. Whip 1.5 pails sweet cream to foam and add it, stirring until cold.
5. Serve with cream sauce.
