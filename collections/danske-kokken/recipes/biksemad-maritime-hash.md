## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked or fried beef, fatty beef with some bacon
- Potatoes, cooked and diced
- Onions, sliced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter or cooking fat

## Directions

1. Cut the meat small and brown it well in a pan.
2. Cut the potatoes into small cubes; they should be cooked, firm, peeled potatoes, not mealy.
3. Slice the onions in a good proportion to the meat and potatoes, and brown them as for beef steak.
4. Brown all three components together and warm through on the pan.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve very hot.
