## Ingredients

- Asparagus water in which finely cut, peeled asparagus has been cooked
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make a baked white, smooth, bound sauce as a mock hollandaise sauce (see No. 777)
2. Use the asparagus water, in which the finely cut, peeled asparagus has been cooked, to thin this sauce
3. Bind with egg yolks - use 3 to 4 egg yolks per sauce for 12 persons
4. This sauce pairs well with lobster meat, shelled shrimp, and crayfish tails
