## Ingredients

- butter, as much as 8 eggs weigh
- powdered sugar, as much as 7 eggs weigh
- 8 egg yolks
- flour, as much as 6 eggs weigh
- 8 egg whites, beaten to stiff foam

## Directions

1. Beat as much washed and slightly lukewarm butter as 8 eggs weigh well with as much powdered sugar as 7 eggs weigh, then with 8 egg yolks, so with as much flour as 6 eggs weigh, and finally carefully with the 8 egg whites beaten to stiff foam.
2. Bake in a greased form for 1 hour at good heat.
