## Ingredients

- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl red currant juice
- 1/2 kg picked berries

## Directions

1. Treat the gooseberries as per the previous instructions
2. The only difference is that the sugar is cooked with red currant juice instead of water and the Cognac is omitted
