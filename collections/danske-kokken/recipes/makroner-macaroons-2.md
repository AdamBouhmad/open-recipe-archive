## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 15 quintal bitter almonds
- 1 pound fine white sugar or Demerara sugar
- peel of 1 lemon, finely grated
- 4 beaten egg whites
- oblate wafers or buttered baking sheet

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and grate them finely on a sharp, fine grater
2. Chop the larger pieces finely
3. Mix in 1 pound fine white sugar and the finely grated lemon peel
4. Stir in the 4 beaten egg whites
5. Place the macaroons on oblate wafers or on a buttered sheet
6. Bake for a fairly long time at weak oven heat
7. When removed from the oven, press them together slightly with your hand
