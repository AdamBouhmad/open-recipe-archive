## Ingredients

- 3 parts raspberries
- 1 part white currants, picked over

## Directions

1. Use 3 parts raspberries and 1 part white currants, which are picked over.
2. Mix the berries together in a pitcher and place in a pot of boiling water, which must be kept at a boil until they release their juices well and fall together in the pitcher.
3. Pour into the jelly cloth, and the next day cook as No. 93.
4. This jelly obtains a beautiful clear color; the refuse is used for marmalade.
