## Ingredients

- Forlorn Mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 tablespoon pickled shallots
- Tarragon or other vinegar
- Pepper
- 1¼ dl (½ pint) whipped cream

## Directions

1. To the forlorn mayonnaise, stir in parsley, capers, and pickled shallots—1 tablespoon of each kind, all finely chopped.
2. The sauce can be given tarragon or other vinegar and pepper to taste and 1¼ dl whipped cream.
3. It is placed on ice or in a cold place until it is to be served.
