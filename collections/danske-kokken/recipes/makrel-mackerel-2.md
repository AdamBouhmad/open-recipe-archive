## Ingredients

- fresh mackerel
- salt
- whole peppercorns
- sour butter sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the mackerel, rinse it, and place it in boiling water with salt and several whole peppercorns
2. Cook until done
3. Serve with sour butter sauce and freshly peeled boiled potatoes
