## Ingredients

- 2 pounds melted and clarified butter
- 5 eggs
- ¾ pound powdered sugar
- 3 pounds wheat flour
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Stir melted and clarified butter well with eggs and ¾ pound powdered sugar until even
2. Work in 3 pounds wheat flour
3. Spread the dough into small oblong cakes on a greased pan
4. Sprinkle with chopped almonds
5. Bake light brown at slow heat
6. When taken out, curl them over a bottle
