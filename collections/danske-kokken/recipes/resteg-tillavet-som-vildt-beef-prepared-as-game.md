## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the hind leg
- Vinegar
- Bacon strips
- Butter
- Brandy
- Cream

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef from the hind leg, pound it well on all sides, and place it in vinegar overnight.
2. Remove it and dry it, but do not stuff it, and then lard it densely with bacon strips.
3. Brown butter in a pot large enough to accommodate the meat, which should be appropriately sized in proportion to the meat.
4. Brown the meat in the butter, add a little brandy to the butter, and cook it gently until it is tender.
5. When done, stir a little cream into the sauce.
6. For tough and lean meat, you can cut it into elongated pieces about two fingers thick and treat them in the same way.
