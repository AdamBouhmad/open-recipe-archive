## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg (1.5 S) chestnuts
- 5 L (5 pots) brown spare broth
- 75 grams (15 skein) butter
- 50 grams (10 skein) flour
- Sherry
- couleur
- glazed chestnuts or small fillets of roasted or cooked chicken, squab, doves or game birds
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Place the chestnuts in boiling water for a few minutes until the shell and the innermost brown skin are easily removed.
2. Then cook them tender in some of the brown spare broth and rub them through a sieve with constant thinning with the rest of the broth.
3. Brown 75 grams (15 skein) butter, and bake 50 grams (10 skein) flour in it, which is thinned with the chestnut soup, which then only is brought to a boil.
4. Add a little Sherry and couleur to the soup if needed.
5. It can be served with glazed chestnuts or with small fillets cut from roasted or cooked chicken, squab, doves or game birds.
6. Serve with toasted bread.
