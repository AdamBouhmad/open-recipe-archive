## Ingredients

- Corn
- Milk
- Flour
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the husks and strip the kernels from the cob.
2. Boil the kernels; afterwards stew them in good milk.
3. Make a roux of flour and butter; add a little salt at the end.
