## Ingredients

- bones, tendons or trimmings of meat, already roasted or cooked
- salt
- 1 quart water for each round of bone and meat

## Directions

1. When you have bones, tendons or meat trimmings that have already been roasted or cooked but are cleanly handled, chop and cut them into small pieces
2. Cook them for a pair of hours with salt and 1 quart water to each round of bone and meat
3. This produces a soup that can be used for sauces, dishes and soups that do not need to be strong
