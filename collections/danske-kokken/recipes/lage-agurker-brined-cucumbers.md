## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- soft water
- dill
- pepper
- horseradish
- cherry leaves and vine leaves
- salt
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the cucumbers in clean water, changing it every other hour.
2. After 6 hours, remove them and place them in a jar layered with dill, pepper, horseradish, and cherry and vine leaves.
3. Preferably place the leaves between the cucumbers so they do not touch each other.
4. Cook a brine of soft water with 30 parts salt and 1 gill wine vinegar added for each pot.
5. When it is cold, pour it over the cucumbers.
6. Immediately place a lid with a weight on them, then tie them and place them in a dry cellar.
7. They can also be stored in a wooden vessel, preferably made of oak.
