## Ingredients

- 1 ripe melon
- 1/2 Kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægel) white rum
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the melon into slices about 1 good centimeter (1/2 inch) thick
2. Cut the slices into pieces of 5 centimeters (2 inches) length
3. Give the melon pieces a brief boiling in water
4. Cook the powdered sugar to syrup with the white rum and lemon juice
5. Cook the melon in this syrup
6. Remove the melon when cooked and place in the serving bowl
7. If necessary, boil the syrup a bit longer before pouring it over the melon
