## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet milk
- 1/4 pound sago grits
- 1/4 pound cold butter
- 15 drams powdered sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- grated lemon zest
- 10 drams ground sweet almonds
- 5 drams ground bitter almonds
- 10 egg whites
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Heat 1 pot sweet milk, and when boiling, add 1/4 pound well-cleaned sago grits
2. Cook with constant stirring until the porridge is thick and leaves the sides of the pan
3. Pour out and when slightly cooled, stir in 1/4 pound cold butter, first beaten to a salve with 15 drams powdered sugar
4. Stir in 10 egg yolks one at a time, together with the grated lemon zest, 10 drams ground sweet almonds and 5 drams ground bitter almonds
5. After stirring for half an hour, fold in the 10 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks
6. Pour the entire mixture into a buttered dish, sprinkled with bread crumbs
7. Sprinkle powdered sugar over the omelet and bake immediately for 1 hour
8. Serve hot with either powdered sugar and jam or with sweet fruit sauce
