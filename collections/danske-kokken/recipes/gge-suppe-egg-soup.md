## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 Potter) water
- whole cinnamon
- lemon peel
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) sago flour stirred in 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cold water
- 8 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 375 Gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) sherry
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) rhubarb juice or white wine
- small rusks

## Directions

1. Boil the water for a few minutes with whole cinnamon and lemon peel and thicken with 125 Gr (1/4 S) sago flour stirred in 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cold water.
2. In a tureen, beat 8 egg yolks and 3 egg whites with 375 Gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar until the egg mixture is white and foaming.
3. Pour the hot soup into the egg mixture under vigorous whisking with a whisk.
4. Add 1/4 L (1 Pægl) sherry and 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) rhubarb juice or white wine to the soup.
5. Serve small rusks with it.
