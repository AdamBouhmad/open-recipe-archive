## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 1 L (1 pot) water
- Juice of 8 lemons
- Zest of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Make a syrup from the powdered sugar, water, juice of 8 lemons, and zest of 2 lemons.
2. Treat it as the preceding types.
