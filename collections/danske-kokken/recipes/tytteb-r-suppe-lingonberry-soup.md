## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg lingonberries
- 5 L water
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- potato flour
- sugar
- pears
- apples

## Directions

1. Cook berries with lemon peel and whole cinnamon and strain after cooking for half an hour
2. Thicken soup with potato flour
3. Peel pears, divide in 4 pieces and cook in very little water with sugar
4. When tender, add to soup and sweeten further
5. Can also add apples
