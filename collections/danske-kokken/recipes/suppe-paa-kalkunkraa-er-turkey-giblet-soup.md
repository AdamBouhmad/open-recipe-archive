## Ingredients

- Giblets from 3 turkeys
- 7 to 8 leeks
- 1 bunch chervil
- 1 pint Madeira wine
- 4 to 5 quarts veal bouillon
- Toasted bread cubes

## Directions

1. Bring the bouillon to a boil and add the mixed turkey giblets, except for the livers.
2. Pour in the wine and continue cooking over moderate heat.
3. Cook the white part of the leeks in very little butter, cut into pieces and place in the soup tureen together with the cleaned and cut giblets and the cooked livers.
4. Skim the soup and strain through a fine sieve, pour over them and add the chervil.
5. Serve with toasted bread cubes.
