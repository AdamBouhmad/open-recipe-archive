## Ingredients

- brown sauce
- red wine
- salt
- sugar
- lemon juice
- olives
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Reduce brown sauce with red wine.
2. Add a little salt, sugar, and lemon juice.
3. Add several spoonfuls of olives with the pits removed.
4. Bring the whole to a boil.
5. A glass of Madeira can be added.
