## Ingredients

- 6 large celery stalks (serves 12 people)
- forcemeat
- water
- salt
- butter
- flour
- broth
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- white pepper
- wine
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel large celery stalks and boil half tender in salted water
2. Cut a lid from the top of each celery stalk and hollow it out
3. Fill with forcemeat and bind the lids on, placing them upside down in the pot
4. Cook again in the celery water, but only as much as needed for the sauce
5. Make the sauce from butter and flour, thin with the broth into a white sauce, add Maggi bouillon cubes, white pepper, and wine if needed, and thicken with 2 to 3 egg yolks
6. Preferably cook the celery in a thin broth, though this can be omitted
7. Remove the lids and arrange the celery on a napkin on the platter
8. Place small meat balls in the spaces between
9. Garnish the top with celery leaves, parsley, or salad
10. The mass hollowed from the celery can be used for celery soup
