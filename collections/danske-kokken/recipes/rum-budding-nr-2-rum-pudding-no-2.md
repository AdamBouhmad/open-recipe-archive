## Ingredients

- 3 pints cream
- 2 ounces gelatine
- A little boiling water
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 ounces white sugar
- 1 pint rum

## Directions

1. Place cream on the fire with 2 ounces gelatine, first dissolved in a little boiling water
2. When it boils, pour it in under rapid whisking into 12 egg yolks, first stirred thick with 12 ounces white sugar
3. Pour back into the saucepan and whisk until it just begins to boil
4. Remove from heat and stir in 1 pint rum
5. Pour into a dish and stir constantly until cold, then pour into the mold
