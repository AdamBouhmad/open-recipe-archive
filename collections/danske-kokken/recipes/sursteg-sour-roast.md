## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef (inner loin, for 12 persons)
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- A little cloves
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) vinegar
- 1 L (1 pot) beer
- A few whole peppercorns
- 3 bay leaves
- 2 onions, sliced
- A good slice of butter or Palmin
- Water
- A little soy sauce or color
- Flour for the sauce
- Sugar
- Currant juice
- Color if necessary
- Boiled potatoes for serving

## Directions

1. Beat the meat well with a meat mallet and rub with a mixture of salt, ground pepper and a little cloves.
2. Place the meat in a marinade of 1/4 L (1 pægl) vinegar, 1 L (1 pot) beer, a few whole peppercorns, 3 bay leaves and 2 onions sliced into slices for 3 to 4 days and turn regularly.
3. Brown a good slice of butter or Palmin in a pot, and place the meat in it after the marinade has drained well.
4. When sufficiently brown, pour in water and a little soy sauce or color.
5. Baste the roast regularly with the gravy, turn it and roast very slowly covered for 2.5 to 3 hours.
6. Strain the gravy and skim the fat.
7. In the fat that is skimmed, brown flour for the sauce and dilute with the gravy and season with salt, a little sugar, currant juice and color if necessary.
8. Boiled potatoes can be served alongside.
9. Sursteg can also be roasted in milk.
