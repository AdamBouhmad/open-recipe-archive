## Ingredients

- 2 pints water
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1 pound white powdered sugar
- Juice of 5 lemons
- 1 glass maraschino or 1 pint arrack

## Directions

1. Boil 2 pints water, the zest of 1 lemon, and 1 pound white powdered sugar to a syrup and skim well, then cool the syrup.
2. Add the juice of 5 lemons and strain the whole mixture several times to become clear.
3. Then freeze it, and when it is half-frozen, stir in either a glass of maraschino or a pint of arrack, then freeze it thoroughly.
4. Maraschino ice is served garnished with small meringues, arrack ice is served in small ice glasses.
