## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) meat sausage
- Water
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the fresh or lightly salted sausages in water for approximately 10 minutes.
2. Serve decorated with parsley and eat with stewed vegetables and various types of cabbage, Russian peas, etc.
3. The water in which they are cooked should not be discarded but used for soup.
