## Ingredients

- 1 liter (1 pot) heavy cream
- 125 grams (¼ pound) finely chopped candied citrus peel
- 6 to 8 tablespoons cherry jam without juice
- 125 grams (¼ pound) small macaroons
- a little vanilla essence
- 8 sheets isinglass
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) boiling water
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) maraschino liqueur
- ¼ liter (1 pægel) heavy cream for decoration
- a few drops of fruit coloring

## Directions

1. Whip 1 liter of heavy cream into stiff foam.
2. Stir in the chopped citrus peel, cherry jam without juice, small macaroons, and a little vanilla essence.
3. Dissolve isinglass in boiling water and stir into the whipped cream along with the maraschino liqueur.
4. When the pudding is so stiff that it can bear the cherries and citrus peel, pour it into the wetted mold.
5. It can be made a few hours before it is to be eaten and decorated with whipped cream made from ¼ liter heavy cream, mixed with a few drops of fruit coloring.
6. Small macaroons or biscuits are served separately.
