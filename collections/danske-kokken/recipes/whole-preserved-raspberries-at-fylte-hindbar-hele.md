## Ingredients

- Raspberries
- Powdered sugar at 2 pounds per pound of berries

## Directions

1. Layer the raspberries in an earthenware pot alternating with powdered sugar, with sugar on top and bottom
2. Let stand 2 days
3. Put on a gentle fire and cook very slowly for a short time
4. After several days, drain the juice and bring to a boil
