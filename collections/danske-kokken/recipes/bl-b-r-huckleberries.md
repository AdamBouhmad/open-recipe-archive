## Ingredients

- 1 pound ripe blueberries
- ½ pound powdered sugar
- Cinnamon
- Paper dipped in cognac

## Directions

1. Place 1 pound ripe blueberries over the fire with ½ pound powdered sugar and boil for about ¼ hour
2. Pour the berries onto a strainer
3. Boil the juice for a quarter hour with cinnamon, skim it, and pour hot over the berries
4. The next day, cover with paper dipped in cognac and seal
