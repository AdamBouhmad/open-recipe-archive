## Ingredients

- 1 beef tongue (prepared as Kogt Oxetunge)
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the beef tongue as in Kogt Oxetunge.
2. Bread it by dipping in beaten egg and grated breadcrumbs.
3. Place in an oven to brown.
4. Serve with tomato sauce.
