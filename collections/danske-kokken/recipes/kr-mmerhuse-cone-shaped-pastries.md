## Ingredients

- 10 egg whites
- 1 pound finely powdered sugar
- 5 pounds flour
- 10 egg yolks
- whipped cream with vanilla sugar
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Whip the egg whites until stiff and stir in the finely powdered sugar, then the flour and finally the egg yolks, 2 at a time. Grease and butter the baking sheet carefully; with a spoon place the cakes on it, smooth well with a knife and bake light golden at moderate heat. Remove them from the sheet and form into cone shapes, which are left in a dry place. When they are to be served, fill them with whipped cream with vanilla sugar, and place a little currant jelly on each cone. On the dish place them with the points inward and the openings outward.
