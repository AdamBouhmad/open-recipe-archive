## Ingredients

- Grapes
- Whole sugar, 2½ pounds per quart of juice
- Vanilla drops

## Directions

1. Place the berries in a jar and treat as stiff currant juice
2. However, since grape juice difficult becomes stiff, take 2½ pounds whole sugar per quart of juice, which is dipped in the juice and boiled to syrup according to instructions
3. Lift off the white of the egg while the remaining juice is stirred in together with a few vanilla drops
4. Cook this now for half an hour
5. It seldom becomes so stiff that it can be turned out of a cup
