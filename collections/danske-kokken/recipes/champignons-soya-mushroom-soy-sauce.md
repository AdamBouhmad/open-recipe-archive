## Ingredients

- mushroom trimmings from pickled mushrooms
- salt
- 10 chopped anchovies
- 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- 1/2 kg (1 s) strongly browned powdered sugar
- 1/2 bottle red wine
- 1 1/2 L (1 1/2 pot) mushroom liquid

## Directions

1. If you have pickled mushrooms, all the trimmings can be used for soy sauce. Rinse them well and chop finely, then sprinkle with salt and let stand under a press for 3 to 4 days.
2. Wring out the liquid from a cloth.
3. To 1 1/2 L (1 1/2 pot) liquid take 10 chopped anchovies, 1 teaspoon ground white pepper, 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves, 2 bay leaves and 1/2 kg (1 s) strongly browned powdered sugar.
4. Cook all this and skim, before pouring 1/2 bottle red wine into it.
5. When it has boiled for at least 1 quarter of an hour after the wine is added, strain it and pour into dry bottles, which are stoppered when it is cold.
6. This soy sauce is very tasty in various dishes.
