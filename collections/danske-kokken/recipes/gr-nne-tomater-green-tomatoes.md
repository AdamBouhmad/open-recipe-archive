## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg (1 S) crushed powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl (1/4 Pægl) vinegar
- 1/2 kg (1 S) green tomatoes
- Vinegar and water in equal parts
- Vanilla bean or cinnamon stick and whole cloves

## Directions

1. Select small, oblong green tomatoes
2. Cook them in a mixture of equal parts vinegar and water until the skin can be removed
3. Boil sugar and vinegar together to make a syrup
4. Cook the tomatoes in this syrup for about 3 to 4 minutes, then pour the fruit and liquid into a crock
5. The following day, pour everything into the pot and give it one more boil, then remove the tomatoes with a slotted spoon and place in the crock
6. Let the syrup boil until very thick and pour it boiling over the tomatoes
7. The syrup can be cooked either with a vanilla bean for 2 1/2 to 3 kg (5 to 6 S) tomatoes or with a few pieces of whole cinnamon and 1/2 dozen whole cloves, according to taste
8. Seal immediately
