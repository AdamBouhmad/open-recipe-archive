## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Salt
- Water
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Place them in cold water with a little salt, and cook until they can be cut through with the side of a fork; strain the water once. Mash them, crush them and heat them in hot water, pepper and butter.
