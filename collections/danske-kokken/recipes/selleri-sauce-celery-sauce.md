## Ingredients

- Two celeries
- Salt
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Butter
- Flour
- 2 egg yolks
- A little sherry
- Ground white pepper
- Insignificant amount of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the celeries into small cubes and cook them tender in soup or in water with salt and Maggi bouillon cubes
2. Make a roux with butter and flour and dilute with the strained soup to a sauce that is not too thick, which is thickened with 2 egg yolks and seasoned with a little sherry, salt, ground white pepper and insignificant sugar
3. Put the cubes in the sauce, which is served with boiled fowl and with boiled beef, veal, and lamb
