## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 1/2 kg (5/8 S) powdered sugar
- juice and zest of 3 lemons
- 15 sheets isinglass
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) boiling water
- egg whites, whipped stiff
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream for whipped topping

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks white with powdered sugar
2. Add lemon juice and zest to the mixture along with isinglass dissolved in boiling water
3. Fold in stiffly whipped egg whites immediately after adding isinglass
4. Pour into glass bowls at once
5. Can be prepared one hour before serving or the day before
6. Whip cream to stiff peaks and use for decoration
