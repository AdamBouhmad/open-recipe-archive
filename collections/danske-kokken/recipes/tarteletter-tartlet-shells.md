## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) flour
- 1/4 kg (1/2 pound) cold butter or margarine
- 1 1/2 dl (1/10 pint) cold water
- 3 egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- dried peas for filling forms during baking

## Directions

1. Mix flour, cold butter or margarine, cold water, egg yolks, and salt together quickly and knead together
2. Place in a cloth and set in a cold place to rest for at least 1/2 hour before using
3. Roll out the dough thinly and line the inside of small fluted cake forms with it
4. Fill the forms with dried peas to prevent the dough from collapsing during baking
5. Place the forms on a baking sheet and bake at good heat until light brown
6. Pour out the peas and invert the forms to remove the shells easily
