## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Vinegar
- Raw cherry juice
- Sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, hollow them, weigh them and put them on the stove with enough vinegar to cook them in
2. When they have had one boil, remove them, press in cloths and cut into strips
3. If they are small cucumbers, you do not need to cut them into strips
4. For 1/2 pound of cucumbers, take 1 gill raw cherry juice, 2 small spoonfuls of vinegar, 1/2 pound sugar, 5 cloves and some small pieces of cinnamon
5. Put this on the stove and boil until it just simmers
6. Add the cucumbers, and when it boils again, remove them and place in the crock
7. Boil the liquid for a few more minutes and pour it hot over
8. Cover with a cloth and tie up the next day
