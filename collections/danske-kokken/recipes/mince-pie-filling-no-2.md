## Ingredients

- 2 pounds raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound suet
- 2.5 pounds brown sugar
- 0.5 pound citron
- 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- 3 lemons
- 1 nutmeg
- Wine
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Mix together 2 pounds raisins, 1 pound currants, 1 pound suet, 2.5 pounds brown sugar, 0.5 pound citron, 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon, the grated zest and juice of 3 lemons, 1 grated nutmeg, wine, and cloves to taste.
