## Ingredients

- Herring
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Lemon peel
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- Cloves
- Thyme

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, rinse, and dry it.
2. Brush with oil and season.
3. Roast on both sides.
4. Layer in a glass jar with the seasonings.
5. Pour over vinegar that has been brought to a boil and cooled.
6. Seal well and store in a cool place.
