## Ingredients

- 3 quarter-pounds butter
- 3 quarter-pounds powdered sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 pound flour
- 1 quarter-pound finely ground sweet almonds
- 14 quintals deer antler salt
- egg white
- powdered sugar for topping
- 1 quarter-pound sweet almonds, sliced into strips

## Directions

1. Beat 3 quarter-pounds butter, 3 quarter-pounds powdered sugar and 6 eggs well together, then beat again with 1 pound flour, 1 quarter-pound finely ground sweet almonds and 14 quintals deer antler salt.
2. Grease a form well with butter and fill with the dough, then brush with egg white on top and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
3. Upon that lay 1 quarter-pound sweet almonds cut into strips.
4. Bake for 1 hour at even oven heat.
