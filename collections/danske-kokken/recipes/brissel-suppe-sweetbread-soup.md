## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) economical stock
- 0.5 sweetbreads
- Cold water
- 1 bunch of herbs
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter or vegetable margarine
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour
- 6 egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Madeira wine
- Ground white pepper
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Place the sweetbreads in cold water and blanch before adding to the stock in which a good bunch of herbs has been cooked
2. When the sweetbreads are white and firm, remove them and cut into very fine, small dice, placing them in the tureen
3. Brown 125 g (1/4 lb) butter or vegetable margarine with 125 g (1/4 lb) flour and dilute with 5 L (5 pots) stock
4. Thicken with 6 egg yolks and season with lemon juice, Madeira and ground white pepper
5. Pour the soup over the sweetbreads when serving
6. Serve with toasted bread
