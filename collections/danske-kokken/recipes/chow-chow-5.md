## Ingredients

- Two white Cabbage heads
- two Cauliflower heads
- a dozen Cucumbers
- four Celery knobs
- four pieces red Pepper
- a Quart small Onions
- two Quarts green Tomatoes
- two Gallons Vinegar
- 4 Pounds Mustard
- 4 Pounds Mustard Seeds
- one can French Mustard
- one Ounce Cloves
- two Ounces Turmeric

## Directions

1. Cut all vegetables into small pieces
2. Cook each type of vegetable separately until soft
3. Sieve
4. Place vinegar and spices in a kettle and bring to a boil
5. Mix the vegetables and pour the vinegar over
