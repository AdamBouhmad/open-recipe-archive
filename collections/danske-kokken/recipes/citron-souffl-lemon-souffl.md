## Ingredients

- Yellow zest of 4 lemons
- 4 pounds white sugar, crushed
- 20 egg yolks
- 5 pounds white sugar, crushed
- Juice of 6 lemons, strained
- 16 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Rub the yellow zest of 4 lemons onto 4 pounds of crushed white sugar.
2. Remove the white pith completely.
3. Whisk the egg yolks for one hour with 5 pounds of crushed white sugar.
4. Pour the strained lemon juice into the mixture.
5. Fold in the whipped egg whites.
6. Pour the whole mixture into a deep cake pan and place in a gentle oven heat so the soufflé rises.
7. Serve dusted with powdered sugar.
