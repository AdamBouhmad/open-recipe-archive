## Ingredients

- 1 quart sweet cream
- 15 egg yolks
- Two large heaped spoonfuls sugar per portion
- A little vanilla
- Grated lemon zest
- Beaten egg whites
- 4 ounces white sugar
- A little water

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream and egg yolks separately, each with two large heaped spoonfuls of sugar
2. Then whisk together with a little vanilla and grated lemon zest
3. Finally add a couple beaten egg whites
4. Meanwhile, melt 4 ounces white sugar in a little water and stir over the fire until light brown
5. Pour this into the dry pudding mold and turn it around until it covers all sides
6. Pour the batter into the mold and cook for a little hour
7. Serve cold with white sweet sauce
8. Turn out of the mold when serving
