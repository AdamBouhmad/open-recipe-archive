## Ingredients

- plaice
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- lemon
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut plaice into pieces as desired - crosswise into 3-4 pieces, lengthwise into 2 halves, or cut out as fillets
2. Treat and fry as for fried plaice fillets
3. Serve with browned butter, lemon and boiled potatoes, or with vegetables such as spinach, peas, asparagus or stewed carrots, or with browned butter and potato salad, or with browned butter, chutney sauce and boiled rice
