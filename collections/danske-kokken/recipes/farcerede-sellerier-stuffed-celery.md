## Ingredients

- Large celery
- Meat forcemeat
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Nutmeg or curry
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Egg yolk (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare large celery in the same way as stuffed onions, but cook them in a little less water, because this shall be used for the sauce
2. Remove them when they are tender, melt a little butter, bake flour in it, thin it with some of the water in which the celery is cooked
3. Warm these in the sauce, or, if they are warm, pour the sauce over them
4. Add a little ground nutmeg or curry and salt
5. The sauce can be thickened with an egg yolk, and a little pepper can be added to it
6. The part that was hollowed out can be used to help make celery soup or for stewing
