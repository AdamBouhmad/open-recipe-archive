## Ingredients

- Simple veal
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Since one often receives several calves at once in the country than one has use for, one can prepare veal cheese from the simpler meat, which can later be used as a topping for open-faced sandwiches.
2. The meat is boiled and treated as aspic in gelée, pressed firmly in a bowl and pressed by means of a lid with a heavy object on top.
