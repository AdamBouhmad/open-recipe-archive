## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Onions
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes the day before, peel and cut them into thin slices
2. Cut a couple of onions likewise into thin slices and cook tender in a little water in the bottom of the pan
3. Add a good piece of butter, salt, pepper, and vinegar
4. When the butter is melted and the sauce sufficiently seasoned, turn the potatoes gently into it without breaking
5. Serve warm with meat
