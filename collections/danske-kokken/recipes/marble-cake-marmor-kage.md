## Ingredients

- White part: 1 cup white powdered sugar, ½ cup butter, ½ cup sweet milk, whites of 4 eggs, 2¾ cups flour, 2 heaped teaspoons baking powder
- Dark part: 1 cup brown sugar, ½ cup molasses, ½ cup sour milk, 2¾ cups flour, 1 level teaspoon soda dissolved in the milk, 4 egg yolks, ½ cup butter, ½ teaspoon cinnamon, allspice, cloves

## Directions

1. For the white part: stir the butter and sugar together until cream, beat the whites stiff and add last along with ½ cup of the flour in which the baking powder is sifted. Any kind of drops can be added.
2. For the dark part: stir ½ cup butter together with the sugar; add ½ teaspoon cinnamon, allspice and cloves.
3. Either kind can be used by itself and will make good cakes, or they can be mixed together so they represent marble.
