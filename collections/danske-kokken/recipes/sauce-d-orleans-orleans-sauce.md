## Ingredients

- Fish stock
- Butter
- Flour
- Lobster and crayfish extract
- Cold butter
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Use fish stock to thin flour and butter to a smooth, white sauce
2. The sauce does not need to be bound, but add some lobster and crayfish extract, a piece of cold butter, and a little paprika
