## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- 3 3/4 dl (1 1/2 cup) water
- 1/4 L (1/4 pot) strawberry juice or 1 kg (2 lb) fresh strawberries
- Juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook the powdered sugar to a syrup with the water and skim.
2. When it is cold, stir in the strawberry juice or fresh strawberries grated through a sieve, along with the juice of 2 lemons.
3. Pour the mixture through a fine sieve directly into the freezing container.
