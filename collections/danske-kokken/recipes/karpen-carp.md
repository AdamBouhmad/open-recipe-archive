## Ingredients

- Carp
- Wine vinegar
- Salt
- Water
- Horseradish cream

## Directions

1. Scrape the fish along the scales, but leave the scales and head on
2. Remove the innards
3. Pour wine vinegar over both sides and sprinkle with a little salt
4. Let stand to turn blue until ready to cook
5. Cook in water with a little salt and some of the vinegar used to pour over the fish
6. Serve with horseradish cream
7. To remove any muddy taste, place some bread crumbs in the cooking water
