## Ingredients

- Cooked peas
- Melted butter
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Drain the water from the cooked peas.
2. Toss them in melted butter together with a little finely chopped parsley.
3. This preparation is commonly used as a garnish around cutlets, roasts, fillets, and similar dishes.
