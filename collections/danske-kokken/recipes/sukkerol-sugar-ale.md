## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 5 quarts cold fresh water
- 1 bottle lager

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, water, and ale together for 1 hour. Pour into bottles without cooking beforehand and cork well. After 10 days it is ready to serve.
