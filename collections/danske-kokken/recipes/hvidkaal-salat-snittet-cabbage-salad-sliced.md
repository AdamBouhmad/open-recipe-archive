## Ingredients

- 1 small white cabbage head
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Oil

## Directions

1. Slice the white cabbage into thread-fine strips without cutting any of the stem or the coarse ribs in the leaves.
2. Sprinkle with salt, place in a press for 1 hour, just as one treats cucumber salad.
3. Prepare as this with vinegar and pepper.
4. Add oil to taste.
