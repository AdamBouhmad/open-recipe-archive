## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked beef brisket
- Pepper
- Salt
- Egg white
- Breadcrumbs
- Flour
- Parsley, chopped
- Claret
- Mixed vegetables
- Brown stock sauce or browned butter

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover cooked beef brisket into slices
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
3. Dip first in egg white and then in crushed bread mixed with a little flour and chopped parsley
4. Fry the meat light brown in claret on the pan
5. Serve with mixed vegetables and brown stock sauce or browned butter
