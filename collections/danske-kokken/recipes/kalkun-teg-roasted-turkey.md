## Ingredients

- One turkey
- Cold water
- Hot water
- Several slices of wheat bread
- Milk
- Cold butter
- Salt
- Ground ginger
- A couple of cloves
- Three eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the turkey in cold water, then pour boiling water over it, let it lie for a couple of minutes and place it again in cold water.
2. If desired, you can stuff turkeys.
3. To fill the space where the crop has been, prepare a farce of several slices of wheat bread soaked in milk and then covered and left to stand for one hour.
4. Work a piece of cold butter until creamy, then stir in the bread after it has been crushed with a spoon, salt, a little ground ginger, a couple of cloves and three eggs.
5. When well mixed, you can add the crop, liver and heart, which are finely chopped, fill it in the skin and sew it, but the farce must not press down into the turkey, so a piece of wheat bread must be placed in the internal hole below the neck.
6. The oven must be well heated before the turkey is set in, and it must have an even heat, but not too strong, otherwise it will become too dark brown.
7. It cannot be roasted in a pot when it is stuffed.
8. Set it in with water and a little salt, with which it is gradually basted; later butter is placed on it, with which it is then basted.
9. A turkey requires two to three hours of roasting according to its size.
