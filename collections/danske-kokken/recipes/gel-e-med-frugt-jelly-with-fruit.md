## Ingredients

- Wine jelly (Recipe 424)
- Small, preserved fruits without any juice, for example yellow cherries and strawberries

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a mold with wine jelly (Recipe 424) to a finger's height and set to cool.
2. When the jelly is stiff, lay small, preserved fruits, without any juice, for example yellow cherries and strawberries in small piles on top of it.
3. Pour 2 fingers of the same kind of jelly over, and when this layer is stiff, lay fruits again, and thus continue until the mold is full.
4. Set it in a cold place, and when the jelly is stiff and is to be served, turn it out on a dish and lay preserved fruits around it.
