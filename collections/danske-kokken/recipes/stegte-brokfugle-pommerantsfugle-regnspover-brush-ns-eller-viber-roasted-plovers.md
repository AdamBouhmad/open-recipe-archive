## Ingredients

- Young birds (plovers, dotterels, ringed plovers, grouse, or lapwings)
- Cream
- Potatoes
- Currant jelly

## Directions

1. Handle and roast them as you would woodcock, but do not use the entrails and bend the head under the wing.
2. During roasting, baste them with cream.
3. Serve with browned potatoes and currant jelly.
