## Ingredients

- Calf sweetbreads
- Butter
- Sweetbread cooking liquid
- Coloring
- Paprika
- Sherry
- Tomato paste
- Sago flour
- Small meatballs of veal
- Olives or mushrooms
- Puff pastry

## Directions

1. Half-cook the sweetbreads, split them, and brown in butter.
2. Pour some of the sweetbread cooking liquid over them.
3. Add coloring, paprika, sherry, and tomato paste to taste.
4. Thicken the sauce with sago flour.
5. Arrange small veal meatballs alongside the sweetbreads and pour the sauce over.
6. The sauce can be further improved with either olives or mushrooms.
7. Serve with puff pastry, either baked as a lid, border, or slices.
