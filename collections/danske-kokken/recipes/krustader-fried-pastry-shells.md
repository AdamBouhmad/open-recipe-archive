## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg flour
- 1/4 L cold water
- 1 1/4 dL egg white
- 2 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 4 eggs
- Clarified butter or Palmin for frying

## Directions

1. Mix flour, cold water, egg white, powdered sugar, and eggs into a thin pancake batter
2. Heat clarified butter or Palmin in a pot with a krustader iron
3. Do not heat the Palmin so strongly that it steams
4. Pour batter into as many small cups as there are forms on the iron
5. Dip the iron into the batter, not quite to the rim
6. Keep in the hot fat and fry until the shells are light brown
7. Loosen with a fork or larding needle and place upside down on paper to drain the fat
8. Pour the remaining fat through a sieve
9. The shells can be stored in metal boxes for a long time, but must be placed in a warm oven before filling is added
