## Ingredients

- 1 bottle red wine
- ¼ pound fine sugar
- 25 quintals orange essence

## Directions

1. Dissolve ¼ pound fine sugar in 1 bottle red wine
2. Add 25 quintals orange essence, which is bought at the apothecary
