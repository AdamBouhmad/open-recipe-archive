## Ingredients

- 1 Pint sour Cream
- 12 Tablespoons Flour
- 4 Tablespoons light Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Whip 1 Pint sour Cream well and stir with 12 Tablespoons Flour and 4 Tablespoons light Powdered Sugar
2. Set on a hot pan with a Spoon in small dollops and bake until light brown
3. If the Cream is good, Butter on the pan is needed only for the first cakes
