## Ingredients

- 1¼ L pearl barley
- 4½ L milk
- salt
- sweet beer

## Directions

1. Soak the pearl barley overnight.
2. The water poured over them must be warm and is drained before the grains are poured into 4½ L boiling milk.
3. When the porridge boils again, cover the lid and it can stand over very low heat without stirring for about 3 hours, or place it in a hay box.
4. Salt the porridge and serve with sweet beer.
