## Ingredients

- Thrushes
- Game farce
- Butter
- Short pastry
- Stock or gravy
- Maggi Bouillon cubes (optional)
- Sago flour
- Apple compote or apple sauce

## Directions

1. Cut open the thrushes along the back and remove bones except the backbone
2. Fill each bird with game farce and add a small ball of farce
3. Sew together along the back, brown in butter, and roast until tender in the oven or pot with a little good gravy or stock for basting
4. Line a mold with short pastry and farce
5. Remove the threads from the birds and layer them in the mold with a thin layer of farce between each
6. Top with farce and then short pastry decorated with figures cut with a pastry wheel
7. Brush the pâté with egg and bake for 1 hour in a hot oven
8. Make a strong gravy sauce from the bird gravy, improved with Maggi Bouillon cubes if needed, and thickened with sago flour
9. Serve with apple compote or apple sauce
