## Ingredients

- Crabs or lobster
- Salad oil
- Bread crumbs
- Nutmeg
- Cayenne pepper
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Sweet cream
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs and carefully remove the meat from the shell, keeping the shell intact.
2. Rub the shell with salad oil.
3. Mix bread crumbs (the same amount as the meat), a little nutmeg, cayenne pepper, lemon juice, butter, and a little sweet cream.
4. For lobster, rub the shell with cream instead.
5. Place the meat back in the shells, lightly cover with bread crumbs and butter, and bake brown in the oven.
6. Arrange with parsley and lemon slices around it.
