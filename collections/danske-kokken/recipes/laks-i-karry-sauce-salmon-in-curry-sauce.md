## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked salmon
- Butter
- Flour
- Curry
- Fish stock
- Cooked rice
- Dry curry

## Directions

1. Cut leftover cooked salmon into smaller pieces
2. Warm the pieces in a curry sauce made from butter, flour, curry and diluted with fish stock
3. Arrange a border of loose cooked rice around the edge of the dish
4. Sprinkle the rice with a little dry curry
