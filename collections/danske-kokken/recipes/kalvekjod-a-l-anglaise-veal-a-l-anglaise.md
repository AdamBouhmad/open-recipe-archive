## Ingredients

- Butter
- Veal
- Water
- Flour
- Cream

## Directions

1. Put a large tablespoon of butter over the heat in a pot; when it begins to brown, add two pounds veal, which can be simple meat, shoulder or neck, and brown it
2. Then pour water over it so it stands just above the meat; stew as much as necessary
3. When the meat is tender, stir a few tablespoons of flour mixed in good cream and thicken the broth with it
4. It is an inexpensive and well-flavored dish
