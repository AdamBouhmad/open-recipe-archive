## Ingredients

- 3 handfuls peas
- 5 L (5 pots) soup
- Parsley
- Water
- Salt
- 100 grams (20 quintal) butter
- 50 grams (10 quintal) flour
- Small carrots
- Fine chopped parsley
- Small whole ripe tomatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and cook tender with a bunch of parsley in salted water.
2. Reserve some whole peas for serving; strain the rest through a coarse sieve.
3. Brown 100 grams butter and 50 grams flour together and dilute with 5 L (5 pots) soup.
4. When boiling, add the pea purée and bring to a boil.
5. Cut small carrots that have been cooked in some of the soup and add to the soup with whole peas and finely chopped parsley.
6. If using coarser peas, sort during shelling and cook the finer ones separately in salted, sweetened water, adding whole to the tureen while the others are pressed through.
7. Season with salt and sugar.
8. Serve with toasted bread. Small whole ripe tomatoes may be added to the soup.
