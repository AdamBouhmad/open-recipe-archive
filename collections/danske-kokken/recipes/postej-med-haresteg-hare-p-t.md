## Ingredients

- Hare meat, cooked leftovers
- Short pastry or butter pastry
- Farce of game, beef, veal, or pork
- Milk
- Butter
- Flour
- Sauce or gravy from the roast
- Maggi Bouillon cubes (optional)
- Cream (optional)
- Egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Line the mold with short pastry or butter pastry and farce
2. Pick the hare meat from the bones and cook the bones in milk
3. Blend this milk gravy with any remaining gravy or sauce from the roast
4. Brown butter and flour and dilute with the gravy to make a good game sauce of light brown color
5. Improve the sauce if necessary with Maggi Bouillon cubes or cream
6. Stir a small piece of cold butter into the sauce at the end
7. Place the meat in the center of the mold and pour some sauce over it
8. Layer with farce, then cover with short pastry brushed with egg
9. Decorate with pastry figures cut out with a pastry wheel
10. Bake for 1 hour in good heat
11. Serve the remaining sauce with the pâté
12. Accompany with redcurrant jelly, lingonberry jam, or apple sauce
