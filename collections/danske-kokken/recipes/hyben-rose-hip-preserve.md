## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl vinegar
- 3/4 dl rose water
- 1/2 kg rose hips

## Directions

1. Select beautiful large red garden rose hips that are firm in flesh and dry carefully with a cloth.
2. Keep the stems on but remove the sepals.
3. Cut a slit lengthwise into each fruit with a knife and remove the small seeds and hairs through the slit.
4. Boil sugar, rose water, and vinegar together to make a syrup and skim.
5. Add the fruit and boil until tender.
6. Pour everything through a sieve and boil the liquid again over heat until well reduced.
7. Mix the fruit with the syrup and pour into glasses or jars and tie them up when the preserve is done.
8. When serving, pour a little rose water over it for a fine effect.
