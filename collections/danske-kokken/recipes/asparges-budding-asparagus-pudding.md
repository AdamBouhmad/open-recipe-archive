## Ingredients

- 150 grams margarine
- 225 grams Pillsbury flour
- 1 liter boiling milk
- 14 egg yolks
- 0.5 kilogram cooked asparagus, drained, or equivalent fresh-cut cooked asparagus
- salt
- a small amount of sugar
- egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. In 150 grams margarine, bake 225 grams Pillsbury flour, which is diluted with 1 liter of boiling milk.
2. When cooled, gradually stir in 14 egg yolks and 0.5 kilogram of cooked asparagus, from which the water has been well drained, or else use an equivalent amount of fresh-cut cooked asparagus.
3. Finally, stir in a little salt, an imperceptible amount of sugar, and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
