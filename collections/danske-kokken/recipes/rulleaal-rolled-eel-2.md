## Ingredients

- Large eel
- Finely chopped shallot
- Salt
- Pepper
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Twine
- Half vinegar and half water
- Bay leaves
- Peppercorns
- Chopped onion
- Isinglass (4.5 quint per pot of broth)

## Directions

1. Skin the eel and remove the head and tail
2. Split the eel along the backbone and remove the backbone
3. Sprinkle with finely chopped shallot, salt, pepper, allspice and cloves
4. Roll it lengthwise and bind it with twine
5. Cook the eel in half vinegar and half water with some bay leaves, peppercorns, chopped onion and salt
6. Place it in a light press, then remove the twine and cut it into slices across the fish
7. Place these slices in a dish
8. Prepare the broth into aspic as described in recipe 443, using 4.5 quint isinglass for each pot of broth
