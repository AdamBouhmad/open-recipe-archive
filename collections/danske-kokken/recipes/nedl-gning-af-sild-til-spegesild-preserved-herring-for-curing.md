## Ingredients

- Fresh, newly caught autumn herring
- Kitchen salt
- 1.5 kg (3 S) Lüneburg salt
- A wooden barrel or butter churn, well-cleaned and sealed

## Directions

1. Lay fresh, newly caught autumn herring without cleaning them in a barrel and sprinkle with kitchen salt
2. Leave them for a couple of hours and stir regularly with a stick so that the salt reaches between the herring properly and the blood is drawn out of them
3. Take them out and drain them, then lay them again in a well-cleaned and sealed butter churn
4. Lay the herring with the back downward and pack them in tight layers, between which is sprinkled at least 1.5 kg (3 S) Lüneburg salt
5. The top layer must be salt, and over it lay the loose wooden bottom with a good weight on it so that the brine can stand over them
6. The herring are usable after 4 to 6 weeks
