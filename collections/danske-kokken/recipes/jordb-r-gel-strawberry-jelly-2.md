## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 S) fresh strawberries
- 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) water
- 10 sheets isinglass per liter (pot) of juice
- juice of 1 lemon (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the strawberries, powdered sugar, and water together until the berries are cooked.
2. Pass them through a jelly bag or a dense cloth.
3. For each liter (pot) of juice, take 10 sheets of isinglass, dissolved in a little of the warm juice without allowing it to boil.
4. Stir it into the remaining juice, which is poured into a moistened and sugar-strewn ring mold.
5. Place on a cold spot or on ice until the next day.
6. At serving, turn the ring out onto a plate, and place a dollop of whipped cream in the center.
7. The jelly ring can be decorated around the outside with fresh, beautiful strawberries that have been standing for an hour, strewn with sugar.
