## Ingredients

- 1 pint sour cream
- 3 bottles light beer or double beer (not bitter)
- 1 whole bottle Bavarian beer
- a little ice-cold water
- a little finely chopped lemon peel
- rye rusks or wheat rusks

## Directions

1. Whip 1 pint of sour cream in a tureen with 3 bottles of light beer or double beer (that is not bitter) and 1 whole bottle of Bavarian beer and a little ice-cold water.
2. A little finely chopped lemon peel gives a good flavor.
3. Serve with rye rusks or wheat rusks cut into large cubes and added to the cold soup just before serving.
