## Ingredients

- Beef from the tenderloin, sliced finger-thick
- Grated bread
- Wheat flour
- Finely chopped onions
- Ground pepper
- Butter
- Chard onions
- Cloves
- Ginger
- Bay leaves
- Bouillon
- Browned flour
- Port wine
- Lemon juice
- Grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cut beef into finger-thick slices and beat and chop lightly with a knife.
2. Mix grated bread and wheat flour with finely chopped onions and ground pepper.
3. Coat the beef in this mixture.
4. Heat butter in a pan over the fire until slightly browned.
5. Place the beef in the pan and fry it half-tender under a lid.
6. Transfer the beef to a casserole with grated Charlotte onion, cloves, pepper, a little ginger, and a few bay leaves over even heat.
7. Add broth or water until the beef is covered.
8. When the beef is tender, thicken the sauce with browned flour and butter mixed in bouillon.
9. Shortly before serving, add a glass of Port wine, some lemon juice, and a little grated lemon zest to the sauce.
