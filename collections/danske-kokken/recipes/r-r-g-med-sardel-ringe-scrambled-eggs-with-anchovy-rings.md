## Ingredients

- Scrambled eggs
- Tomatoes, coarsely chopped
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt
- Anchovy rings (canned)
- Lettuce heart leaves

## Directions

1. Around the scrambled eggs spread on a small flat platter, place a border of coarsely chopped tomatoes tossed in a mixture of oil, vinegar, pepper and salt. On the tomatoes arrange the small anchovy rings (available in cans), and tuck the heart leaves of head lettuce around the edge of the platter.
