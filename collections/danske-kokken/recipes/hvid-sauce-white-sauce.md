## Ingredients

- 1 Quart Bouillon
- 1 Tablespoon Wheat Flour
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Cayenne
- white Roots
- small Meat balls
- preserved Oysters, Capers or Anchovies

## Directions

1. For 1 Quart Bouillon take 1 Tablespoon good Wheat Flour, which is stirred out in a piece of Butter.
2. This must first be melted in a Pot, and when it begins to boil, put the Flour in and rub it out.
3. It must absolutely not become brown, and it is gradually thinned with the Broth.
4. Add Nutmeg, Cayenne, white Roots, small Meat balls, and otherwise what you have of preserved Oysters, Capers or Anchovies.
5. If it is for Chickens, Turkeys or Calf's Breast, it is also good to whisk a Spoon of Cream into the Sauce when it is to be served; but for Geese and Ducks it does not suit.
6. Have the Sauce a little sharper with Cayenne for these, and do not use Oysters.
7. If you want to make the White Sauce for Geese sour and sweet, then do not make it so sharp, and use only white Roots and Apples.
8. Some use sliced Almonds and Currants for this.
