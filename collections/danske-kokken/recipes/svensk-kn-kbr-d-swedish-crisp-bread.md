## Ingredients

- 2 measures sweet milk
- 1/2 pound butter
- half wheat flour and half rye flour
- 2 measures good yeast
- two handfuls caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Place 2 measures sweet milk and 1/2 pound butter over heat and warm.
2. Work half wheat flour and half rye flour with 2 measures good yeast together into a dough and knead well until it begins to rise.
3. Add more flour and two handfuls caraway seeds and knead with the hands until the dough releases from the hands and the dough trough.
4. Let it rise.
5. When it has risen, punch it down.
6. Roll out a piece of dough the size of a goose egg to the thickness of a straw, trim the edges with a pastry wheel and prick with a fork so it does not puff up during baking.
7. Let the cakes rise further and bake in the oven at good heat.
8. Store in a dry place.
