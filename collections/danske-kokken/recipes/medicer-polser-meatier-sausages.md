## Ingredients

- small pieces of lean and fat meat
- salt
- crushed allspice
- cloves
- pepper
- bouillon cooked on meat stock
- pork lard or meat, cut into cubes
- narrow pork intestines
- warm water

## Directions

1. Chop small pieces of lean and fat meat until it releases from the board and chopping knife
2. Salt it and stir it with crushed allspice, cloves, pepper and bouillon cooked on meat stock (which should be lukewarm) or water
3. The mixture should be quite soft and stirred well with pork lard or meat cut into cubes
4. Do not stuff the intestines too tightly
5. Place the intestines in a trough with warm water while filling
6. Use narrow pork intestines
7. Dry them well, place them on the roasting pan in boiling water and prick with a needle so they do not burst
8. When they have cooked a little on both sides, remove them, drain the water from the pan into a bowl and pour it gradually over the sausages during roasting
9. Wipe the pan well and brown 1 spoonful of butter on it
10. Brown the sausages in it and fry them until they are completely fried through
11. Serve with stewed potatoes, red cabbage or green cabbage
