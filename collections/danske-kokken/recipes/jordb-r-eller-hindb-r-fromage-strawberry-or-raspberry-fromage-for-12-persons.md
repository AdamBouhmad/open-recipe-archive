## Ingredients

- 1 L (1 pot) whipped cream
- strawberry or raspberry juice to taste, or candied berries
- 10 sheets isinglass
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) water
- small macaroons
- whipped cream for decoration

## Directions

1. Stir strawberry or raspberry juice or candied berries into the whipped cream to taste
2. Dissolve isinglass in water and stir into the cream
3. Serve with small macaroons and decorate with whipped cream
