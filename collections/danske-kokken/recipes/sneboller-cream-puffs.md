## Ingredients

- 125 Gr (1/4 S) melted margarine
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 tt) flour
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) boiling milk or water
- 8 eggs
- Palmin
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. In melted margarine, add 1/4 Kg (1/2 tt) flour, which is baked and thinned with 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) boiling milk or water.
2. When the dough has cooled, stir 8 eggs into it, after which it is dropped into small balls with a teaspoon or dessert spoon and placed in hot Palmin.
3. When the cream puffs have a nice brown colour and are cooked through, serve them as hot as possible, sprinkled with powdered sugar.
4. The dough can be flavoured with an essence.
