## Ingredients

- cherries
- powdered sugar equal in weight to the berries

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and extract the stones using the help of a cut quill.
2. Weigh the berries and take as much powdered sugar as they weigh.
3. Cook the sugar in the usual manner into a thick syrup.
4. Place the berries in the syrup and cook for 1/4 hour, skim well, and remove with a slotted spoon, placing them in a deep dish.
5. Cook the syrup again until it is like a fairly thick syrup.
6. Return the berries to the syrup and give it one more boil.
7. Remove the pot from the fire and shake the berries well, then let them rest until they appear clear and full, then divide into smaller jars and treat in the usual manner.
8. If desired, the stones can be crushed, the kernels removed and mixed with the berries.
