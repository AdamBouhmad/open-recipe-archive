## Ingredients

- Fried eggs
- Strong gravy
- Madeira
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Sago flour

## Directions

1. Make a strong, clear brown gravy
2. Add a glass of Madeira
3. The gravy can be made from Maggi bouillon cubes and thickened slightly with sago flour
4. Serve the eggs in a ring around the plate and pour the gravy in the center
