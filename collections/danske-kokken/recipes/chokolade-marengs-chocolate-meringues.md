## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 375 gr (1/4 lb) powdered flour sugar
- 125 gr (1/4 lb) grated chocolate
- a small amount of cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Fold in the powdered flour sugar, grated chocolate, and a small amount of cream of tartar
3. Place on a buttered baking sheet with a teaspoon
4. Bake in a warm oven
