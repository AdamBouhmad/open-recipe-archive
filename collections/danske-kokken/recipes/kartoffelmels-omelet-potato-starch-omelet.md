## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon potato starch
- 5 tablespoons milk
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks well and stir in the potato starch, milk, and finally the beaten egg whites.
2. Melt a little butter on a pan and pour in the eggs.
3. As soon as the omelet is baked on one side, cover it with jam and fold together.
