## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 cents yeast
- fine wheat flour
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 cup raisins
- candied orange peel, chopped
- lemon zest
- sugar
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Warm 1 pint milk and set with 2 cents yeast dissolved in a little of the milk plus as much fine wheat flour to make dough of the usual thickness and evenness.
2. Set in a warm place, and when well risen (which takes 2 to 3 hours), stir in 1/2 cup melted butter, 1 cup raisins, a generous amount of chopped candied orange peel, lemon zest, and a handful of sugar and 3 eggs.
3. Stir in flour until the dough is stiff enough to knead but not more.
4. When kneaded so it no longer sticks to the fingers, divide into three parts, roll out long and thin, and braid the dough in an ordinary braid that is long enough to fit around inside a bread pan.
5. Set to rise again, and when risen, brush with egg.
