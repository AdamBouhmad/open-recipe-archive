## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Salt
- Butter
- Grated cheese
- Oysters

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni in salted water and strain through a sieve.
2. Butter a deep dish and layer macaroni alternately with grated cheese and oysters.
3. The top layer should be macaroni with small pieces of butter on top.
4. Bake until brown.
