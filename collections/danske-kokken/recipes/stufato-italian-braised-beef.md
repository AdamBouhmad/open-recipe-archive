## Ingredients

- 4 kg beef chuck or brisket
- ¼ kg bacon strips
- Salt
- Pepper
- Finely chopped onions
- Palmin or claret
- ½ L white wine
- 1 L soup or water
- Coloring
- 3 to 4 apples
- Flour
- Butter
- Sugar
- Cooked macaroni
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Remove all the tallow and then beat the meat well with a meat mallet and lard with bacon strips dipped in salt, pepper and finely chopped onions
2. Rub the meat with salt and brown in palmin or claret in a pot
3. Pour ½ L white wine and 1 L soup or water with added coloring over the meat and cook it until tender, turning it regularly
4. One hour before serving, add 3 to 4 apples to the pot and cook together with the meat
5. When the meat is tender, remove it; cooking usually takes 3 hours
6. Strain the gravy and with it dilute the baked flour and butter to make the sauce, which is seasoned with salt and a little sugar
7. Serve cooked macaroni with grated Parmesan cheese with the meat
