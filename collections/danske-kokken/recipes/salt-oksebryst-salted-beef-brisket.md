## Ingredients

- Beef brisket
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Boil the meat before it is salted to obtain clear, fresh broth
2. Place the beef brisket over heat in boiling water, skim it, and let it boil for a couple of hours
3. Remove it directly from the boiling broth and place it in the brine to salt for 8 to 10 days
4. When ready to serve, whether hot or cold, remove it from the brine and place it in a pot with gently boiling salted water
5. Cook for half an hour or longer depending on the thickness of the meat
6. If the meat is not to be served hot with vegetables, let it cool in the broth
7. This meat will not have as fine an appearance, but the flavor is good
