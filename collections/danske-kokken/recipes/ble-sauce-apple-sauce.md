## Ingredients

- Apples
- Water
- sweet Almonds
- Lemon Juice and Shell
- Sugar
- Corn Starch
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut the Apples into pieces and boil them in Water together with some crushed sweet Almonds.
2. Then rub them through a Colander, put them back on with a little Lemon Juice and Shell, Sugar and a little Corn Starch, stirred in Water, and boil quite dry.
