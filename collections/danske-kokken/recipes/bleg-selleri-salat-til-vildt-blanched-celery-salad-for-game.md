## Ingredients

- One blanched celery
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream
- Mustard
- Sugar
- Lemon juice or tarragon vinegar
- Green salad leaves
- Fruit coloring (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut out all the tender and good parts of a rinsed and dried blanched celery into very small pieces
2. Lay in a salad bowl on a ring of green salad leaves
3. Whip 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream to a foam
4. Mix the foam with mustard, sugar, and lemon juice or tarragon vinegar to taste
5. The foam sauce can be colored pale pink with a few drops of fruit coloring
6. The salad must be eaten when prepared
7. If using root celery, it must be cooked
