## Ingredients

- 13 pounds syrup
- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound clarified butter
- 12 quintal sweet almonds
- 3 quintal bitter almonds
- 1 quintal cardamom
- 6 quintal potassium carbonate, stirred in lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Warm the syrup and mix it with the flour, clarified butter, sweet and bitter almonds, cardamom, and potassium carbonate stirred in lukewarm milk. Knead the whole together and let it stand for 12 hours in a warm place. Then knead it again, roll it out, and form it into small balls, which are baked at good heat.
