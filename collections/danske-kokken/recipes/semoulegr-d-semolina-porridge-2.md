## Ingredients

- 4 pots milk
- 1 pound semolina grits
- salt to taste
- a little crushed almonds (optional)
- 1 tablespoon butter (optional)
- ground cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Heat 4 pots of milk over the fire
2. When it boils, sprinkle 1 pound of semolina grits into it while stirring constantly until the porridge boils
3. Add salt at the end
4. Optionally, add a little crushed almonds and 1 tablespoon of butter at the same time as the grits
5. Cooking takes approximately a quarter hour after the grits are added
6. Serve sprinkled with ground cinnamon and sugar
