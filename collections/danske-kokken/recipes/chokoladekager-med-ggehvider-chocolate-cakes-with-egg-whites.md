## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 1/4 pound grated chocolate
- 1 1/2 pounds sifted powdered sugar
- 1 small teaspoon cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites to stiff peaks.
2. Stir in 1/4 pound grated chocolate, 1 1/2 pounds sifted powdered sugar, and 1 small teaspoon cream of tartar.
3. When all is just mixed, place the dough immediately on a buttered and cooled pan with a teaspoon.
4. The oven must be just warm enough so the cakes can stand in it overnight.
5. Makes 25 cakes.
