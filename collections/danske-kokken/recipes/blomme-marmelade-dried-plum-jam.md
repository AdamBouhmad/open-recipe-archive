## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 2 kg prunes
- 1/2 kg apples
- 1/2 kg quinces
- Peel of 1 lemon cut into fine strips
- 1 or 2 pieces of whole cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scald the plums and press out the stones
2. Peel the apples and quinces and cut into 4 to 6 pieces, removing the seed cores; then weigh the fruit
3. Cook the lemon peel and cinnamon together with the fruit and sugar over even heat, stirring, until the fruit is cooked out and the jam is like a smooth porridge
4. Place in glasses, cover with paper, and seal
