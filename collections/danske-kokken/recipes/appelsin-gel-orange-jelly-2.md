## Ingredients

- 1.5 oranges (approximately 14 oranges total)
- 375 gr (3/4 S) powdered sugar
- 1/4 L water
- 10 sheets isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil 375 gr powdered sugar with 1/4 L water.
2. Pour in the squeezed and strained juice of 8 oranges and strain through a jelly bag.
3. To each liter of juice add 10 sheets isinglass dissolved in a little boiling water.
4. Peel the remaining 6 oranges, cut into slices, remove the seeds, and layer them in the mold with the jelly.
