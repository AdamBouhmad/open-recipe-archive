## Ingredients

- 6 fish, weighing about half a pound each
- 1/4 pound salt pork
- 2 quarts water
- chopped parsley
- 1 bunch celery or celery salt
- 1 pint milk
- 2 beaten eggs
- a small piece of butter
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and cut into pieces
2. Cut 1/4 pound salt pork into small pieces
3. Put fish and pork together in the pot with 2 quarts water, chopped parsley, and 1 bunch of celery or celery salt
4. Boil until the fish and meat are in pieces
5. Strain the soup and pour back into the pot with 1 pint of warmed milk
6. Add 2 beaten eggs and a small piece of butter last
7. When it has come to a boil, it is ready and is served with toasted bread
