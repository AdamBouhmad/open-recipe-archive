## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Short pastry
- Fish farce
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish stock
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the mussels as in recipe 8
2. Handle in the same manner as Oyster Pâté
3. Line mold with pastry and farce
4. Fill with prepared mussels
5. Make sauce from butter, flour, and fish stock
6. Bake for about one hour
