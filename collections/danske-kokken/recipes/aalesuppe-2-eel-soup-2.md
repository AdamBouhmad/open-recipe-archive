## Ingredients

- Several small eels
- Clear, strong fish soup
- A handful of chervil
- A handful of sorrel
- A handful of celery leaves
- Butter
- Toasted bread
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the eels and cut into pieces and cook in the fish soup.
2. Cook the vegetables separately, then let them drain.
3. Add them to the soup together with a piece of butter and the whisked egg yolks.
4. Place some toasted bread slices in the soup tureen and pour the soup over.
