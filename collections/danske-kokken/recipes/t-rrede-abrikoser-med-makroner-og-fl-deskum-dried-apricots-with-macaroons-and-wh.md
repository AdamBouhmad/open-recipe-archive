## Ingredients

- 575 gr (1/4 lb) dried apricots
- Macaroons
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream for foam

## Directions

1. Soak the apricots the evening before, cook and sweeten to taste.
2. Layer them cold with macaroons and garnish with foam from 1/2 L cream.
