## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup coffee
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 nutmeg
- a little candied peel
- cinnamon
- cloves
- allspice
- 1 glass brandy

## Directions

1. Dissolve the soda in the coffee
2. Mix all ingredients together
3. Bake in a hot oven
