## Ingredients

- 1 chicken or capon
- oysters
- water
- salt
- lemon juice
- stock
- flour
- butter
- egg yolks
- white wine

## Directions

1. Select an excellent, fat, and good chicken or capon. After cleaning and removing the skeleton, fill it with oysters and sew it closed along the back.
2. Place it on a rack in the pot to be steamed tender.
3. Boil the beard from the oysters in water with salt and lemon juice.
4. Mix it with a little stock made from the skeleton and neck, and add the oyster water from the shells.
5. Strain this stock and pour it over the chicken, which must not boil hard.
6. Within 1½ hours the meat will certainly be steamed tender, provided it is a young chicken.
7. For the sauce, make a beurre manié with flour and butter, dilute with the stock from the chicken, and bind with 2-3 egg yolks, seasoning with lemon juice and white wine.
8. Oysters can be added to the sauce, placing them in the hot sauce without bringing to a boil.
9. Remove the thread before serving the chicken.
