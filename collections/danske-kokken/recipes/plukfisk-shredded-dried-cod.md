## Ingredients

- Smør (Butter)
- Mel (Flour)
- Mælk (Milk)
- Salt
- Sennep (Mustard)
- Fisk (Shredded fish from klipfisk)
- stødt Brød (Crushed bread)
- smeltet Smør (Melted butter)

## Directions

1. Bake butter and flour together and dilute with good milk to make an even sauce.
2. Season the sauce with salt and stirred mustard or the remaining mustard water from the dried cod.
3. Stir in the shredded fish and pour the stew onto a platter.
4. Sprinkle with a good layer of crushed bread and pour a little melted butter over it.
5. Place the platter in the oven and bake the stew until the bread is brown.
