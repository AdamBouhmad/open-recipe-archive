## Ingredients

- Flæsk, Ben, Flæskesvær og Saltmad (pork, bones, pork crackling, and salt meat)
- Koldt Vand (cold water)
- Et lille Stykke Ingefær (a small piece of ginger)
- Grove Byggryn eller Perlegryn (coarse barley or pearl barley)
- Selleri (celery)
- Persillerødder (parsley roots)
- Gulerødder (carrots)
- Raaskrællede Kartofler (raw peeled potatoes)
- Lidt Hvedemel (a little wheat flour)

## Directions

1. Soak the salt meat in cold water for two days before cooking, renewing the water three times daily.
2. If there are sausages among the salt meat, prick them occasionally with a fork during cooking to prevent them from bursting.
3. Place the meat in a pot with enough cold water to cover it by a couple of inches.
4. Add a small piece of ginger to the water.
5. Bring to a boil and skim the soup carefully several times during cooking.
6. When the meat is tender, strain the soup and let it rest for a quarter hour to allow fat to rise.
7. Rinse the barley in cold water.
8. Return the soup to the heat and add the barley, celery, parsley roots, carrots, and raw peeled potatoes.
9. Skim the fat that rises and mix it with a little wheat flour to thicken the soup.
10. Cook until the vegetables and barley are tender.
