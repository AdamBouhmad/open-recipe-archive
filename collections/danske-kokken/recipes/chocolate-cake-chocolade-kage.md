## Ingredients

- 1 Cup Butter
- 2 Cups Sugar
- 5 Eggs (less than the whites of 2)
- 1 Cup Sweet Milk
- 1 Teaspoon Cream of Tartar dissolved in the milk
- ½ Teaspoon Soda dissolved in the milk
- 3½ Cups Flour, scant measure
- Whites of two eggs
- 1½ Cups powdered Sugar
- 6 large Tablespoons grated Chocolate
- 2 Teaspoons Vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream together the butter and sugar
2. Add the eggs
3. Add the sweet milk
4. Mix in the cream of tartar and soda dissolved in the milk
5. Stir in the flour
6. Bake the cake
7. When baked, cover it while hot with a layer made from the whites of two eggs, 1½ cups powdered sugar, 6 large tablespoons grated chocolate and 2 teaspoons vanilla
8. Place it in a moderately warm oven for a few minutes
