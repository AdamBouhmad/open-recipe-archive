## Ingredients

- Potato purée (as in Nr. 333)
- Egg
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pipe the purée onto a dish that can be exposed to heat in the oven
2. Bake the beautifully piped purée until light yellow
3. Alternatively, the purée can be shaped on the dish with 2 spoons, brushed with egg and sprinkled with breadcrumbs, then baked until light yellow
4. If baking the purée in a ring form, butter the form and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
5. Mix the potato purée with egg, a very little butter, milk, pepper and salt
6. Bake in the ring form in the oven for approximately 1/2 hour
7. Turn out and fill with a meat dish or ragout
