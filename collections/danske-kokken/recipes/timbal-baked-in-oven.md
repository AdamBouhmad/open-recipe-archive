## Ingredients

- Butter pastry
- Decorated pastry figures
- Brown coloring or soya
- Butter
- Water
- Paper for baking
- Ragout filling

## Directions

1. Butter a smooth mold (timbal mold) with cold butter
2. Cut figures from thinly rolled butter pastry, color with brown coloring or soya, press firmly against the mold sides, and brush with water
3. Roll out a larger sheet of pastry and cut out a bottom piece to line the mold
4. Line the sides with a thin layer of rolled pastry, pressing carefully against the figures
5. Butter a piece of paper the size of the mold
6. Place the buttered paper buttered side down in the mold
