## Ingredients

- three quarter pounds potato starch
- 4 pots sweet milk
- a quarter pound butter
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Stir three quarter pounds of potato starch little by little with 4 pots of sweet milk
2. Place over the fire together with a quarter pound of butter
3. Cook while stirring continuously
4. When thoroughly cooked, add salt
5. Serve immediately, as it otherwise becomes stringy
