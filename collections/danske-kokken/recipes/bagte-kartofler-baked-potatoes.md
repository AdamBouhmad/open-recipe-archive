## Ingredients

- Small potatoes of equal size
- Cold water
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Select small potatoes of equal size.
2. Wash them well in cold water and clean with a stiff brush.
3. Place them in a hot oven, which will bake them quickly.
4. They are ready in 20 to 35 minutes depending on their size and ripeness.
5. They are done when they give way easily when pressed between the fingers.
6. Serve immediately as soon as they are finished and never cover them; if they stand after finishing, they will become heavy, and if covered, they become watery.
7. Eat with cold butter and salt.
