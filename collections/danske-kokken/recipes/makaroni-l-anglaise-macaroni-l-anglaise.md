## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter good rich gravy
- A little sago flour
- 2 truffles, sliced
- 1/4 kg cooked macaroni, broken into pieces
- Cooked salted tongue, cut out

## Directions

1. In good rich gravy thickened with a little sago flour, add 2 truffles cut into slices and bring to a boil in the gravy, which gains an excellent taste from this.
2. Warm the cooked macaroni pieces in the sauce and serve in a border.
3. Around this can be arranged cooked salted tongue cut into pieces.
