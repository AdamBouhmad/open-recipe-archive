## Ingredients

- Roe from pike, cod, or preferably salmon
- Fine, dry salt
- Per half pound of roe: 3 finely chopped shallots
- Crushed pepper
- Crushed cloves
- Elderberry paste (optional, for darkening)

## Directions

1. Remove the membranes from the roe very carefully
2. Sprinkle with fine, dry salt and beat with it
3. Let stand a few hours to stiffen
4. To half a pound of roe, add 3 very finely chopped shallots, a little crushed pepper, and crushed cloves
5. Mix well together and let stand for 1 day, after which it is ready to use
6. The color will be light, but if you wish it to be dark like genuine caviar, you can purchase a little elderberry paste from the apothecary and stir it in
