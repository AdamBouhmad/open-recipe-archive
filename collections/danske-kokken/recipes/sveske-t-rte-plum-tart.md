## Ingredients

- Two thin layers of butter dough
- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) prunes
- Sugar
- A few drops of lemon oil
- Egg for brushing
- Cream (see recipe 1120)
- Whipped cream for topping

## Directions

1. Roll out two thin layers of butter dough and cut to the size of the serving pan
2. Lattice one layer with thin strips of butter dough, brush with egg, and bake in a hot oven
3. Blanch the prunes, remove the pits, and cook them until tender in a small amount of water with sugar and a few drops of lemon oil, then chop them
4. Place this mixture along with a layer of cream between the cake layers
5. Decorate the top with whipped cream peaks
