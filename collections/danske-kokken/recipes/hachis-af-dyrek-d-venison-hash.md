## Ingredients

- Roasted or cooked venison
- Butter
- Flour
- Game stock
- Color (food coloring)
- Red wine
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Chop or mince the roasted or cooked meat finely as for salad.
2. Turn it in a brown, smooth sauce made of butter, flour, game stock, color and red wine.
3. Place browned potatoes in a ring around the hash.
