## Ingredients

- Bread soup
- 3-4 egg yolks
- Powdered sugar
- Ribs or rhubarb juice
- Sherry

## Directions

1. The bread soup for this should be rather thin, as the eggs thicken it considerably.
2. Stir 3-4 egg yolks in the serving bowl with powdered sugar, and add the hot soup to it while stirring constantly.
3. Prepare with currant or rhubarb juice and sherry.
