## Ingredients

- Very ripe wild or garden raspberries
- 1/2 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. Very ripe berries can either be wrung out in a cloth or mashed with a spoon, then poured into a jelly bag or fine sieve.
2. Measure the juice that runs through and place on heat together with the sugar.
3. Boil and skim for 8 to 10 minutes, pour hot into bottles and tie immediately.
4. The refuse left behind is boiled in water, and the juice is used in the household for fruit pudding or fruit soup, or served cold with porridge instead of beer.
