## Ingredients

- Large leeks, 1/4 to 1/5 per person
- Water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Use the largest leeks for sweet leeks, which can be served at breakfast, as a first or second course at dinner, or in the evening
2. Cut away the upper green part of the leeks (which can be used in soup)
3. Thoroughly clean the lower part of soil, which is often found in abundance in leeks
4. Rinse the leeks, tie them in bundles like asparagus, and cook them tender in salted water for 1/2 to 1/4 hour
5. Arrange them as hot as possible on a platter with a napkin, and remove the bands
6. Serve sweet leeks with creamed butter
