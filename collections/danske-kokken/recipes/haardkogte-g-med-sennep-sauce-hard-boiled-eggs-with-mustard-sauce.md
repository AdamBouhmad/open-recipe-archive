## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth or Maggi bouillon cubes
- Water
- Mustard
- Milk
- Fried potato slices
- Palmin

## Directions

1. Boil and arrange the eggs as in No. 119. Brown butter and a very little flour and dilute with meat broth or dissolved Maggi bouillon cubes; add mustard sauce to taste, and finally stir a little cold butter into the sauce, which is poured over the eggs. The sauce can be made with milk instead of meat broth; it is not as sharp and uses less butter. The dish can be garnished with breaded potato slices, fried in a pan in Palmin.
