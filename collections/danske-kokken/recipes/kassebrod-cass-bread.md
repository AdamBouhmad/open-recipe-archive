## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound butter
- 3 tablespoons cream
- 3 tablespoons rum
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stir together flour, butter, cream, rum, 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks into a stiff dough.
2. Roll out and place on a baking sheet.
3. Sprinkle generously with sugar and bake at even heat.
4. When baked enough, cut it into oblong pieces.
