## Ingredients

- Capercaillie
- Game stock, soup, or water with meat extract
- Flour
- Butter
- Sauce
- Fresh or preserved mushrooms

## Directions

1. Baste the capercaillie with game stock, soup, or water with meat extract
2. When tender, strain the gravy and use it with the baked flour and butter to make the sauce
3. Add fresh or preserved mushrooms to the sauce
