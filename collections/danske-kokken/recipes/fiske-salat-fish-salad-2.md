## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 pint cream
- Mustard
- 3 to 4 tablespoons vinegar
- White wine
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lobster or anchovy essence (optional)
- 1 large deep plate cooked, picked fish
- 1/4 pound small broken cooked macaroni

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks in a pint of cream and cook as for cream
2. When cold, stir in mustard, 3 to 4 tablespoons vinegar, white wine, a little pepper and salt, and if desired some lobster or anchovy essence
3. In this sauce, which must be smooth, put a large deep plate of cooked, picked fish and 1/4 pound small broken cooked macaroni
4. Mix well together and should preferably stand a couple of hours before use
