## Ingredients

- Cooked Brussels sprouts
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth or water
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Salt
- Browned potatoes

## Directions

1. After the Brussels sprouts are cooked and the water is well drained, pour over a sauce made of butter and flour.
2. Bake together, then dilute with broth or water with Maggi bouillon cubes and bind with 1 egg yolk per head of cabbage.
3. Season the sauce with sugar and salt to taste and toss the cabbage in it.
4. Serve browned potatoes around the cabbage or separately.
5. Alternatively, Brussels sprouts can be stewed in milk.
