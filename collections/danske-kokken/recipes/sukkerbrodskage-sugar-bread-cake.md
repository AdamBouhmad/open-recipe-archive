## Ingredients

- 16 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- egg whites
- 2 ounces corn starch

## Directions

1. Stir 16 egg yolks with 1 pound powdered sugar for half an hour
2. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form
3. Blend the whipped egg whites with 2 ounces corn starch
4. Carefully stir the corn starch mixture into the egg yolks
5. Pour into a form lined with buttered paper
6. Bake for a couple of hours with gentle heat at first, then increased heat later
