## Ingredients

- 14 pounds sweet almonds
- 15 pounds powdered sugar
- 1.5 pints water

## Directions

1. Scald the sweet almonds and chop them somewhat fine and place them in a pan together with the powdered sugar and water. Cook it with constant stirring until the sugar has melted and thereafter become dry and finally the whole has taken on a beautiful light brown color. Then pour another good half pint of water on it and let it cook again until it is like a good, thick porridge. Then pour it into a tin form that must be dipped in cold water, or it will stick to it. Quickly press the sugar firmly in the form with a silver spoon dipped in cold water and let it then stand for 5-6 hours until it is cold, then pour it out of the form.
