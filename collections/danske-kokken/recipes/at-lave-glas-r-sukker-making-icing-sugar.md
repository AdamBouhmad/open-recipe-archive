## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 3 ounces white sugar (finely ground and sifted)
- juice of one lemon or half a lemon
- rose water (a little)

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white to foam and stir it well with 3 ounces of very good, finely ground and sifted white sugar.
2. If it does not become as thick as a thin porridge, more sugar should be added, as otherwise it will not be able to cover the cake.
3. Stir in the juice of a lemon or half a lemon and a little rose water.
4. To make the cake, which is to be iced, even, rub it with flour before coating it with the icing.
