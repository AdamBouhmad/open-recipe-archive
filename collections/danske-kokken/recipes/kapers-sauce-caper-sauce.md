## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls capers
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) even gravy sauce
- Wine
- A little paprika
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of capers to 1/2 L of even gravy sauce
2. Prepare with wine, a little paprika, salt and sugar to taste
