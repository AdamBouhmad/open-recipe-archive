## Ingredients

- Good ripe apples
- White wine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel good ripe apples and cut into 2 or 4 pieces
2. Give them one cooking in white wine and sugar
3. Then boil whole sugar fast and thick and pour lukewarm over the apples; the wine is not used but can be used for soup
4. They should not be placed in a narrow jar the first time, but the sauce should be poured over them in a deep dish where they do not lie much on top of each other
5. Two days later they can be placed in the jar
6. The sauce is then given one cooking and poured cold over
7. They cannot be kept as long as other sweet preserves
