## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 bottles pale ale
- 3/4 L (3 Pægle) cream
- old rye and wheat bread
- lemon peel
- whole cinnamon
- smoked herring or anchovies
- raw cream

## Directions

1. Soak old rye and wheat bread (mixed if desired) for a few hours in water and cook it out.
2. Press it through a coarse sieve and dilute with the ale.
3. The ale bread soup can be cooked for a few minutes with lemon peel and whole cinnamon and sweetened to taste.
4. Serve smoked herring or anchovies and raw cream with it. Like with bread soup, the sugar can be stirred with 3 to 4 egg yolks.
