## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Meat stock
- Horseradish root
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. In a white, smooth sauce made from butter and flour and thinned with meat stock, add cleaned and blanched horseradish root, vinegar, sugar, salt, and grated horseradish
2. Bring the sauce just to a boil
