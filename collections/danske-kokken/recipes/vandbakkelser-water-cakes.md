## Ingredients

- 40 quintal melted butter, with the salt removed
- 3/4 pound flour
- 2 pints boiled water
- 10 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Bake the melted butter with the salt removed together with the flour and thin it with the boiled water. Bake it until it releases from the pan, then pour it out to cool. When it has cooled somewhat, stir in 10 whole eggs, 2 at a time, and continue stirring for half an hour. Grease the baking sheet with cold butter, and with a teaspoon place small, round cakes, about 25 in all, on it and bake at good oven heat for 1.5 hours. While they bake, the oven must not be opened, as they will fall. They can be served sprinkled with powdered sugar and with a dollop of currant jelly on each plus cream sauce.
