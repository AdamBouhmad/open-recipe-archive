## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg Pillsbury flour
- 125 g potato starch
- 125 g icing sugar
- 125 g margarine
- 3 teaspoons deer salt, dissolved in 1/4 L cold water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together
2. Let the dough rest for a quarter hour
3. Roll out the crackers very thinly
4. Prick them well throughout
5. Cut with a pastry wheel
6. Bake until pale golden
