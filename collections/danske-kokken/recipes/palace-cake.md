## Ingredients

- 250 grams margarine
- 250 grams fine sugar
- 4 eggs
- 125 grams currants
- 50 grams finely chopped preserved orange peel
- a little orange essence
- 250 grams Pillsbury flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 250 milliliters milk

## Directions

1. Mix margarine, fine sugar, and eggs well
2. Add currants, finely chopped preserved orange peel, and orange essence
3. Stir in Pillsbury flour, baking powder, and milk
4. Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes
5. Grease the form
