## Ingredients

- 1 pound clarified butter
- 1 pound light, dried powdered sugar
- 1 pound and 6 tablespoons flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and stir until stiff with 1 pound light, dried powdered sugar, 1 pound and 6 tablespoons flour, and 1 egg.
2. When too hard to stir, knead with hands until it releases from them, then roll with floured hands into small round cakes.
3. Flatten with a knife, place on a pan, and bake very light.
4. Makes 70 cakes.
