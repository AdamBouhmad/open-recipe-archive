## Ingredients

- Twenty peeled shallots
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 20 whole peppercorns
- 10 allspice berries
- 5 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- parsley
- chervil
- horseradish
- paprika
- 1/4 L (3 pægle) wine vinegar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) tarragon vinegar
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) Graves wine

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled shallots, garlic, peppercorns, allspice, cloves, bay leaf, parsley, chervil, horseradish and a little paprika in a mixture of 1/4 L (3 pægle) wine vinegar, 1/2 L (1/2 pot) tarragon vinegar and 1/4 L (1 pægl) Graves until the onions are tender and the essence has reduced to approximately 1 L (1 pot) during cooking.
2. Strain and pour cooled into bottles, which are stoppered.
3. Use for sauce. Can be purchased ready-made.
