## Ingredients

- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl pear water
- 1 lemon
- 1/2 kg peeled pears

## Directions

1. Select unripe cooking pears or Bergamots as for compote.
2. The pears can be preserved whole or cut into 2 to 4 pieces with the core removed.
3. If preserved whole, cut off the blossom; scrape the stem that remains.
4. Cook the pears in water until tender enough to pierce easily with a knitting needle.
5. For each 1/2 kg sugar, use 3/4 dl of the water in which the pears were cooked, along with the juice of 1 lemon, and boil to a thin syrup in which the pears are cooked for at least 5 minutes together with half the lemon peel.
6. Remove the pears and place in a jar, removing the lemon peel.
7. Cook the syrup until smooth, then pour it over the pears in the jar, which is sealed immediately.
8. The water in which the fruit was cooked can be used with other juices for fruit soup.
