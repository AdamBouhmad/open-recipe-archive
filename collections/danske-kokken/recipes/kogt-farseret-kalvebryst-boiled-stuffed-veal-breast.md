## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg veal breast
- Forcemeat of veal or pork
- Salt
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the bones with a sharp knife.
2. Spread a layer of forcemeat (which should not be beaten too fine) over the meat.
3. Roll the breast tightly together and sew it closed to prevent the forcemeat from escaping during cooking.
4. Place in boiling salted water and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours.
5. Remove the thread and slice the meat, garnishing with parsley.
6. Use the cooking liquid partly for sauce and partly for a light soup that does not require much strength.
