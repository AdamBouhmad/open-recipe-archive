## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- juice of 3 lemons
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1/2 kg carrots, cut into pieces
- herb water (1/4 liter)

## Directions

1. Scrape and rinse the carrots, weigh them and cut into thin slices.
2. Cut the slices into fine strips.
3. Cut the lemon zest very finely.
4. Cook the lemon zest under a lid together with the carrots in enough water to barely cover them for about 15 minutes.
5. Drain the carrots through a sieve.
6. Boil 1/2 kg sugar and 1/4 liter of herb water together with the juice of 3 lemons to a thin syrup.
7. Add the carrots and boil for 5-6 minutes.
8. Drain the carrots again through a sieve.
9. Boil the syrup until it is very smooth to avoid having to boil it multiple times.
10. Add the carrots directly into the boiling syrup and mix well.
11. Pour into glasses and tie them up as soon as the preserve is ready.
