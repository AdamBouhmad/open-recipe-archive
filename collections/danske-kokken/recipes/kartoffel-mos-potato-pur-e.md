## Ingredients

- Peeled, cooked potatoes
- Eggs (approximately 1 per 1/2 kg potatoes)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter (a small amount)

## Directions

1. Mash the cooked potatoes thoroughly
2. Press them through a coarse sieve
3. Mix in eggs (ca. 1 to each 1/2 kg potatoes), salt, pepper and a small amount of butter
4. Using a pastry bag, pipe the mixture into roses, small mounds, wreaths or other figures on a cold buttered baking sheet
5. Bake until light brown at gentle heat
