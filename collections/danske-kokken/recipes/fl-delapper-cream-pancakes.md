## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) cream
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) flour
- 3 to 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- essence of choice
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the cream well and stir in the flour, 3 to 4 tablespoons of powdered sugar, and a little essence if desired.
2. Bake in butter on a pan and set off with a spoon in small cakes.
3. Serve warm with sugar.
