## Ingredients

- butter dough
- raspberry, strawberry, or cherry marmalade
- 6 egg whites
- ¾ pound finely ground powdered sugar
- vanilla, ground
- finely chopped almonds
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Line small molds with butter dough
2. Place a little fruit marmalade in each mold
3. Whip 6 egg whites to stiff peaks
4. Fold in ¾ pound finely ground powdered sugar mixed with ground vanilla
5. Fill the molds with the foam and smooth with a knife
6. Sprinkle with finely chopped almonds mixed with powdered sugar
7. Bake in a medium-warm oven
