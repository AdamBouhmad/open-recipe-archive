## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- a little butter
- 3 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Break 8 eggs into a bowl well-buttered with butter
2. Add pepper, salt, a little butter and 3 tablespoons cream
3. Set in the oven, bake 20 minutes and serve warm
