## Ingredients

- Juice of 2 large lemons
- 0.5 Pint water for each bottle of good wine
- 12 Pounds powdered sugar (for 2 Quarts of the mixture)
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 1.5 Ounces gelatine, dissolved in warm water

## Directions

1. Mix together the lemon juice and water with the wine
2. Take 2 Quarts of this mixture and add the powdered sugar, lemon zest, and gelatine dissolved in warm water
3. Cook the mixture
4. Stir both during cooking and afterward in a stone bowl until cold
5. Pour into the mold
6. Serve with yellow Crème sauce with some finely crushed almonds
