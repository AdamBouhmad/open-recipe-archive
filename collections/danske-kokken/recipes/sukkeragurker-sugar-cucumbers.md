## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and split lengthwise
2. Place in cold water for a while and drain on a sieve
3. Boil water and place the cucumbers in for a preliminary cooking and drain on a cloth
4. Take 2 parts vinegar to 1 part sugar by weight
