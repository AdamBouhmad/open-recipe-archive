## Ingredients

- 20 sheets isinglass
- 1⁄3 dl (½ pægel) boiling water
- ½ kilo (1 pound) powdered sugar
- 1 liter (1 pot) cold rhubarb juice
- ¼ liter (3 pægel) heavy cream

## Directions

1. Dissolve isinglass in boiling water along with powdered sugar and pour into cold rhubarb juice.
2. When it begins to set, stir in whipped cream made from the heavy cream.
3. Pour it into a wetted and sprinkled mold.
4. Place in a cool location or on ice for a few hours.
5. Serve with small macaroons.
6. Either red currant, raspberry, strawberry, or cherry juice can be used instead of rhubarb.
7. If the juice is sweet, use less sugar.
8. Can be decorated with preserved fruits around the pudding.
