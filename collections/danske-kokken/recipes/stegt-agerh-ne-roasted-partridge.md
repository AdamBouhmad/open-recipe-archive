## Ingredients

- Partridge (1 bird for 2 portions)
- Twine
- Linen cloth
- Bacon, either for larding or in slices
- Butter
- Salt
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Currant jelly
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the partridge as usual; do not cut off the head, but fasten it with twine to the legs.
2. Do not wash it, but dry it well with a linen cloth.
3. Either lard it, wrap it with bacon slices, or brown it first in a casserole with butter on both sides of the breast.
4. Then place it on its back in the pan, sprinkle with a little salt, and roast tender in about half an hour.
5. The less liquid under the partridges, the better.
6. Make the sauce by boiling down the pan drippings with bouillon, thicken it with a little flour, and stir it up with bouillon.
7. Serve with currant jelly and browned potatoes.
