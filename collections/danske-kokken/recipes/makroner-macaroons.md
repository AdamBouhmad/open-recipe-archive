## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) blanched almonds
- 7 egg whites
- 3/4 kg (1 1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Grind the blanched almonds very fine
2. Mix together well the egg whites, powdered sugar and baking powder
3. Place on a cold, buttered sheet
4. Bake at low heat
