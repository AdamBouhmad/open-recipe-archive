## Ingredients

- pie dough
- zest of 2 lemons
- 1 peeled apple
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup sugar
- a small piece of melted butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- juice of 2 lemons
- a small cup of hot water
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Line the mold with ordinary pie dough
2. Grate the zest of 2 lemons together with a peeled apple
3. Stir 2 egg yolks with 1 cup sugar, a small piece of melted butter, 2 tablespoons flour and the juice of 2 lemons
4. Stir in a small cup of hot water and when all is well mixed, pour onto the dough
5. Treat entirely as an egg pie: bake, brush with beaten egg whites and place in the oven again
