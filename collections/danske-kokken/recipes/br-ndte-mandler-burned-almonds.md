## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- half a pint water
- 60 quintal sweet almonds
- 4 quintal ground Ceylon cinnamon
- 1/2 quintal ground cloves
- 1/2 quintal ground cardamom
- 1/2 quintal borax
- coarse powdered sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in water and cook to syrup. Then add the sweet almonds, Ceylon cinnamon, ground cloves, ground cardamom, and borax. Cook them with constant stirring until they become light brown and begin to crack, then spread them on a sheet and sprinkle with coarse powdered sugar.
