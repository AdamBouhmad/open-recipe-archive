## Ingredients

- 1 pint cream
- sugar
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Whip 1 pint cream to stiff peaks with a little sugar.
2. Stir in 1 tablespoon vinegar.
3. Finally add the grated horseradish, which should be grated only when it is to be used so it does not become dark.
4. Serve cold.
