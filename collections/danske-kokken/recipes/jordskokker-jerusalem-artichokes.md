## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- water
- vinegar
- skimmed milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel the Jerusalem artichokes and place them gradually in cold water with a little vinegar
2. Cook until tender in skimmed milk
3. Serve on a napkin on a plate
4. Serve with melted butter
