## Ingredients

- 75 grams margarine
- 200 grams powdered sugar
- 125 grams maizena
- 125 grams cake flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/4 deciliters sweet milk
- 3 egg whites
- 125 grams cake flour with powdered sugar
- 125 grams coconut flour
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Melt the margarine and stir in the powdered sugar, maizena, cake flour, baking powder, and sweet milk
2. Fold in the 3 beaten egg whites
3. Grease small molds with margarine and place 1 teaspoon of batter in each
4. Mix the 2 beaten egg whites with the cake flour with powdered sugar and coconut flour
5. Distribute this mixture on top of the cakes using a teaspoon
6. Bake until light brown. Makes approximately 35 cakes
