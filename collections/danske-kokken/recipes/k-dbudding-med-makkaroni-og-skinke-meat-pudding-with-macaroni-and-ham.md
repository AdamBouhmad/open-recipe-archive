## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound macaroni
- Thin bouillon
- Smoked ham
- Meat farce
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook 0.5 pound macaroni in just enough thin bouillon to barely cover it until tender, but not overcooked.
2. Cook smoked ham well and it should not be too salty.
3. When cold, thickly line a pudding mold with meat farce.
4. Layer small slices of the lean part of the ham and the macaroni with their sauce, then meat farce over.
5. You can either stir a little rinsed butter into the macaroni while hot, or leave a little of the fat on the ham pieces as desired.
6. It must cook for at least 3 hours.
