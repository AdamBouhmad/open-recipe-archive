## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) stone sugar
- 3/4 dl (3/4 cup) water for 1/2 kg (1 pound) fruit juice
- ripe, picked, and rinsed lingonberries

## Directions

1. Place the ripe, picked, and rinsed berries over gentle heat before they burst.
2. Pour them into a jelly cloth.
3. The juice that has run through by the following day is weighed.
4. After sugar and water have been cooked as for the previous jellies, add the juice and cook and skim for 10 minutes.
5. Pour into cups and bind when it begins to set.
6. The berries remaining in the cloth can be cooked with sugar and served with roast, or cooked in water and used for lingonberry pudding.
