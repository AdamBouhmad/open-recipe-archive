## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt
- water
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Rinse the tongue well and boil it in salted water
2. Skim the stock frequently during cooking
3. When fully cooked, remove and allow to cool
4. Strain the stock and remove the skin from the tongue, cutting away the lower part
5. Return to heat and bring briefly to a boil
6. Serve either whole or sliced with sharp brown sauce
