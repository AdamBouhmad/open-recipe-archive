## Ingredients

- 10 Egg whites
- 1 Pound fine sifted powdered sugar
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg whites to stiff foam.
2. Slowly stir in 1 pound fine sifted powdered sugar while constantly beating.
3. Grease a clean baking sheet with butter and flour it.
4. With a silver spoon, place the small cakes on the sheet.
5. Sprinkle with caster sugar.
6. Dry before putting them in a very gentle oven heat, where they stay until they release from the sheet.
7. This mass can also be used to make small cakes filled with vanilla ice cream.
8. To the foregoing mixture you can also add 40 qvint scalded, long, fine strips cut sweet almonds, but then use only 75 qvint sugar.
9. From the latter mentioned mixture you can also make Kransekage.
