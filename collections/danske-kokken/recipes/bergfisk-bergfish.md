## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces, clean it, and soak it overnight.
2. Place in boiling water and cook for a short time.
3. When the pot is removed from the heat, add a little salt and some cold water, then immediately take the fish out and place it on a dish with the skin facing up.
4. Serve with melted butter and mustard, and with potatoes.
