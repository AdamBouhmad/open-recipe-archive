## Ingredients

- fresh rose petals
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. In summer, fill bottles halfway with fresh rose petals.
2. Fill the bottles with good vinegar and cork them.
3. Let stand for some time before the vinegar can be used.
4. Strain from the petals.
5. You will have an aromatic, light pink vinegar for salad, mayonnaise, and similar dishes.
