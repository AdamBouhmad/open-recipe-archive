## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg chestnuts
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the brown shell from large selected genuine chestnuts.
2. Place them for a few minutes in boiling water and then in cold water so that the inner thin seed coat can be removed more easily.
3. Place the peeled chestnuts in boiling water with lemon juice and boil until almost tender.
4. Remove them with a slotted spoon or place on a sieve.
5. Melt 1/2 kg sugar in 1 1/4 dl of the water in which the chestnuts were boiled and add lemon juice to taste.
6. Bring the chestnuts to a boil in this syrup and immediately pour into a jar and set aside until the next day.
7. The next day, drain the syrup, bring to a boil and pour boiling over the chestnuts.
8. On the third day, place the syrup back on the heat to reduce until smooth.
9. Add the chestnuts to the boiling syrup and pour into jars and tie them up while the preserve is still hot.
