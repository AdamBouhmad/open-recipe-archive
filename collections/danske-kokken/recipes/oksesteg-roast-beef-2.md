## Ingredients

- A piece of beef (thick roast) of 5 to 6 kg
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Half white beer and half water for the gravy

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water with salt into the oven pan before placing the meat.
2. Place the roast in the pan with the outer fillet side facing down towards the pan.
3. Roast for 3 hours, basting frequently and evenly.
4. Half an hour before serving, remove most of the gravy from the roast and skim off the fat, then pour a little water back into the pan to prevent the roast from burning while it remains in the oven.
5. For the sauce, brown butter and bake flour in it, then thin with the gravy to make a fairly thin sauce. Let it boil for about 10 minutes before serving.
6. The sauce should have a nice brown color.
7. Arrange the roast on a hot platter with the tender side facing up, as it lay in the oven.
8. Serve with boiled potatoes, peas, cucumbers, pickles, preserves, or compote.
9. Optionally, rub the roast with garlic or place a couple of onions on the pan to roast together, and roast some fresh or salted tomatoes with the roast.
