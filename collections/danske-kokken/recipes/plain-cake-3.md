## Ingredients

- 1 Cup cornmeal, sifted
- 1 Cup wheat flour
- 1 Cup Sugar
- 1 Teaspoon Salt
- 1 Egg
- a piece of Butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar into the butter first
2. Dissolve the soda in the milk
3. Mix all ingredients together
