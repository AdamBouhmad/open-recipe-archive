## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 12 quintals bitter almonds
- ½ pound fine sugar
- 1 pitcher boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald 1 pound sweet almonds and 12 quintals bitter almonds and grind them very finely with ½ pound fine sugar
2. Pour 1 pitcher boiling water over it
3. Let it stand well covered for a couple of hours, then strain it, cool and drink cold
