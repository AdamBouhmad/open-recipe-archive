## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg powdered sugar for 1 kg plums without stones
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Scald the plums, remove the skin, halve them, and remove the stones, then weigh them
2. Place the plums, sugar, and juice of 1 lemon in the pot
3. Cut the lemon peel into very fine strips and mix with the plums
4. Cook over even heat with constant stirring until the fruit is cooked out and the jam is a firm mass
5. Ladle into glasses, cover with paper, and seal
