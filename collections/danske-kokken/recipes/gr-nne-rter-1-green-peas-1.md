## Ingredients

- Good, carefully shelled peas
- Salt
- Natron (baking soda)
- Cold water
- 10 grams (2 quintals) salt to 1 liter (1 pot) water
- A small amount of powdered sugar (Melis)

## Directions

1. Rinse the shelled peas in cold water
2. Boil them for 3 minutes in boiling water with a little salt and natron
3. Drain the water from them and rinse the peas in cold water
4. Pack them in jars and cover with a cold brine made of 10 gr (2 quintals) salt to 1 l (1 pot) water with a small amount of powdered sugar
5. Close the jars with ring and clamp and boil in water for 1/2 hour
6. The peas must be boiled in the jars 2 times with 1 day in between; after each time they must be cooled in the water in which they were boiled, or covered with a woolen blanket
