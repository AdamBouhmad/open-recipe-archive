## Ingredients

- 1 ounce butter
- 1 ounce flour
- 1 gill boiling water
- 3 egg yolks
- a saltspoonful dry mustard
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 3 tablespoons oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Set 1 ounce butter over the fire
2. Sprinkle 1 ounce flour in it until it bubbles
3. Gradually add 1 gill boiling water
4. When it boils, remove it from the fire
5. Add 3 egg yolks, one at a time, 1 saltspoonful dry mustard, 1 tablespoon vinegar, 3 tablespoons oil gradually drop by drop, stirring constantly, until it is smooth
6. For boiled fish
