## Ingredients

- 125 grams (1/4 pound) chocolate
- 2 tablespoons cocoa
- 1 liter (1 pot) half-skimmed milk
- 2 to 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons potato starch
- Cognac, wine, or liqueur to taste (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix chocolate and cocoa into 1 liter of half-skimmed milk and bring to a boil with constant stirring until smooth.
2. Whisk 2 to 3 egg yolks white with 2 tablespoons powdered sugar and 2 teaspoons potato starch.
3. Add some of the hot chocolate sauce to the egg mixture.
4. Under strong whisking with a whisk, pour the egg mixture into the pot with the remaining sauce, but do not allow it to boil.
5. Serve both warm and cold with puddings.
6. Add Cognac, wine, or liqueur if desired.
7. For this portion, 1/4 liter (1 pint) of cream may be whipped to a foam and placed on top in a warm sauce bowl, or stirred into the cold sauce.
