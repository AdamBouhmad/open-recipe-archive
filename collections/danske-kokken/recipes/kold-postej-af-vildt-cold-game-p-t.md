## Ingredients

- Butter
- Butter dough
- Thin bacon slices
- Game bird, plucked and cleaned
- Breast meat from game bird, finely chopped
- Pork fatback, finely chopped, equal amount to breast meat
- 2 onions
- Pepper
- 4 whole eggs
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- Thyme, crushed
- 3 cups white beer
- Pitted olives
- Bouillon, good and strong
- Root vegetables

## Directions

1. Grease a casserole with butter and line with butter dough
2. Line the bottom and sides with thin bacon slices
3. Pluck and clean the game bird thoroughly
4. Scrape all meat from the breast and chop very finely with an equal amount of pork fatback, 2 onions, pepper, 4 whole eggs, 2 tablespoons vinegar, a little crushed thyme, and 3 cups white beer
5. Mix everything together well
6. Layer the forcemeat mixture in the bottom, then a layer of game, then forcemeat again, then a layer of pitted olives, and continue alternating until the mold is nearly full
7. Top with a layer of butter dough, pressed firmly at the sides
8. Place in a warm oven and bake for 30 minutes
9. Remove and cut an opening in the center
10. Fill this opening with good, strong hot bouillon
11. Return to the oven and bake for another 2 hours, occasionally pouring hot bouillon into the opening
12. Remove from oven and let cool until half-cold
13. Place a light press on top and leave until the next day
14. Place the casserole in hot water to loosen the pâté and turn it out onto a serving dish
15. Serve with robust vegetables
