## Ingredients

- Beef
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Wine
- Paprika
- Tomato purée to taste
- Rice border
- Boiled rice or macaroni

## Directions

1. For tomato sauce, make a roux with butter and flour, which is thinned with broth.
2. Add wine, paprika and tomato purée to taste.
3. Arrange the beef on a dish on which the rice border has been baked, and pour the sauce over.
4. Alternatively, loose boiled rice or macaroni can be used, laid around the edge of the dish.
