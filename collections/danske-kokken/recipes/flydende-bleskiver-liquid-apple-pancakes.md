## Ingredients

- 1 heaping Cup Flour
- 2 large Tablespoons Butter (clarified)
- 1 Gill Ale
- 1 Gill Milk
- 4 Eggs

## Directions

1. Stir 1 heaping Cup Flour with 2 large Tablespoons clarified Butter, 1 Gill Ale, 1 Gill Milk, and 4 Eggs (whip the Whites and fold in last)
2. Peel and hollow out Apples, cut into slices and they can be given a short boil in Sugar and Water
3. Dip each slice in the batter and fry
