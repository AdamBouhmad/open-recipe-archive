## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Asparagus water
- Salt
- Lemon juice or white wine
- Nutmeg
- Egg yolks
- Cold butter
- Asparagus

## Directions

1. In 100 gr (20 Kvint) of butter, bake 50 gr (10 Kvint) of flour
2. Thin with 1/4 L (3 Pægle) of asparagus water
3. Bring the sauce to a boil, season with salt, juice of 1 lemon or a little white wine, and a pinch of ground nutmeg if desired
4. Just before serving, bind with 6 well-beaten egg yolks, then stir in 50 gr (10 Kvint) of cold butter
5. Pour the sauce immediately over the asparagus
