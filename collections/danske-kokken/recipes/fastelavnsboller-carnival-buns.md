## Ingredients

- 875 Gr (1 1/4 S) cornstarch flour
- 30 Gr (6 Kvint) powdered sugar
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) sultana raisins
- 75 Gr (15 Kvint) finely chopped candied citrus peel
- a little cardamom
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) lukewarm milk in which 40 Gr (8 Kvint) yeast is dissolved

## Directions

1. Mix flour, powdered sugar, sultana raisins, finely chopped candied citrus peel, a little cardamom and lukewarm milk in which yeast is dissolved
2. Roll out the dough as for Danish pastry and layer with 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) margarine
3. Fold together, roll out and form into buns
4. Brush with egg and let rise
5. Place in a very hot oven to bake
