## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound butter
- salt
- 2 cups milk
- 5 tablespoons yeast
- flour
- 0.75 pound light powdered sugar or powdered sugar
- cardamom
- lemon zest
- ground cinnamon
- cut candied peel to taste
- 12 eggs
- 1 pound good raisins

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and drain off the salt
2. In the warm butter place 2 cups milk and then 5 tablespoons yeast, and gradually pour this mixture into the flour and knead well together with 0.75 pound light powdered sugar or powdered sugar, a little cardamom, grated lemon zest, ground cinnamon, and cut candied peel to taste
3. Then add 12 eggs, 2 at a time, and when the dough is nearly done kneading, add 1 pound good raisins
4. When the dough is ready, pour it into a buttered and floured form, cover well, and set in a warm place to rise, as its quality depends essentially on its being sufficiently risen before it is placed in the oven or cake pan
5. Bake for about 1.5 hours and it should be a beautiful light brown
6. Serve with coffee and tea
