## Ingredients

- 1 ounce sugar
- a couple of tablespoons currant jelly
- 1 pint boiling water
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1/2 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Melt 1 ounce sugar and a couple of tablespoons of currant jelly over heat in 1 pint boiling water
2. Stir in 2 tablespoons cornstarch dissolved in 1/2 cup cold water
3. As soon as it boils up, it is ready
