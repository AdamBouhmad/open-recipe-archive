## Ingredients

- forcemeat
- butter
- asparagus or cauliflower
- tomato slices
- onion slices
- leeks
- peas
- peeled apples
- stewed prunes
- gravy or soup
- food coloring or soya
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- lettuce head

## Directions

1. Spread a layer of forcemeat on a roasting pan buttered with cold butter, press it flat.
2. Arrange chosen vegetables over the forcemeat.
3. Cover with another layer of forcemeat and smooth it.
4. Roast in the oven for 1 hour, basting with gravy, soup, or broth from the forcemeat trimmings, seasoned with food coloring or soya.
5. Slice the roast and arrange the slices together on a platter.
6. Garnish with vegetables matching the filling and a lettuce head at the ends.
7. Make sauce from good brown gravy prepared from the drippings, adding Maggi bouillon cubes.
