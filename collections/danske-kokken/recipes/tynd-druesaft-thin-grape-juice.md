## Ingredients

- Grapes, completely ripe
- Water
- White or brown sugar, 1 pound per quart of juice

## Directions

1. The grapes can be completely ripe; treat them like the previous ones
2. Strain the juice in a cloth and boil for half an hour with 1 pound white or brown sugar per quart of juice
3. Then put it in small bottles and cork
