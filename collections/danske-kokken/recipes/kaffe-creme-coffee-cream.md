## Ingredients

- strong extract of good coffee
- 1 quart fresh cream
- 5 ounces sugar
- 10 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Brew or strain a strong extract of good coffee.
2. Whisk 1 quart fresh cream with 5 ounces sugar.
3. Beat 10 egg yolks first a little alone, then with the cream.
4. Place in an enameled pot along with 1/2 pint of coffee extract (or adjust to taste).
5. Stir constantly until it reaches a boil and likewise when poured into a dish, until it becomes cold.
6. Serve in small cups with cake.
