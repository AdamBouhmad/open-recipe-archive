## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- ½ pound butter
- 4 eggs
- ½ pound powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pound flour, ½ pound butter, 4 eggs, and ½ pound powdered sugar together into a stiff dough
2. Place a small piece in the center of the iron and bake
3. Grease the iron for each cake
4. This makes 24 cakes
