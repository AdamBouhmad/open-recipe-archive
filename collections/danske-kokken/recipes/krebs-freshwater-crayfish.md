## Ingredients

- Freshwater crayfish
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Dill

## Directions

1. Freshwater crayfish are caught in fresh water.
2. Cook them as you would cook lobster, but for at most 5 minutes.
3. Crayfish are used very much for decoration in various dishes.
4. For eating crayfish, serve with oil, vinegar and pepper, and arrange them in a pyramid, decorated with parsley and dill.
