## Ingredients

- 1 Pound grated potatoes
- 10 Egg yolks
- 1½ Cups powdered Sugar
- 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
- Sweet almonds (for 5 cents)
- Bitter almonds (for 1 cent), finely ground
- Juice of 2 Lemons
- Zest of 1 Lemon, grated
- 10 beaten Egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil and peel the potatoes the day before grating
2. Grate them with a fine grater, using only what falls behind the grater to avoid lumps
3. Beat the egg yolks well with 1½ cups powdered sugar
4. Mix in the baking powder
5. Mix in the potatoes
6. Add the sweet and bitter almonds
7. Add the lemon juice and grated lemon zest
8. Stir and beat well in a bowl
9. Gently fold in the beaten egg whites
10. Bake immediately with moderate heat, especially at first until it rises, then with stronger heat, so it is done in one hour's time
11. Serves 10 people
