## Ingredients

- Onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Gravy, soup, or water
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Pepper
- Salt
- Sugar
- Coloring
- Gherkins, pickles, lemon sauce, pearl onions, or chutney
- Veal roast, sliced
- Potato border (Nr. 336)

## Directions

1. Brown onions in butter
2. Brown flour in the mixture
3. Dilute with gravy, soup, or water and Maggi bouillon cubes
4. Add pepper, salt, a little sugar, and coloring to the sauce
5. Season with gherkins, pickles, lemon sauce, pearl onions, or chutney
6. Place slices of veal roast in the sauce to warm through
7. Serve garnished with potato border
