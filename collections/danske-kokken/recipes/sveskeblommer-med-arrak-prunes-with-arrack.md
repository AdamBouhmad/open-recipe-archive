## Ingredients

- ¾ kg Demerara sugar
- ½ l water
- prunes

## Directions

1. Set the dried prunes, from which the stones have been removed, over heat with the stated measure of water.
2. Remove them as soon as they begin to boil, and place in a sieve.
3. When the water has drained well, put the prunes in the jar.
4. Put the sugar in the pot together with the water from the prunes and cook to syrup, which should be smooth enough to hang in threads from the spoon; pour it boiling over the prunes.
