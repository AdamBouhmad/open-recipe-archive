## Ingredients

- 1 pound washed and soaked pearl barley
- 3 pots boiling water
- several sticks cinnamon
- 1.5 pounds prunes
- a couple of lemon slices
- sugar to taste
- 1/2 pint sharp French wine

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of washed and soaked pearl barley in 3 pots of boiling water and cook until tender, together with several sticks of cinnamon.
2. Meanwhile, wash 1.5 pounds of prunes well in several changes of cold water and add them to the soup along with a couple of lemon slices (with pits removed).
3. Add sugar to taste and 1/2 pint of sharp French wine.
4. If the soup becomes too thick, thin it with boiling water.
