## Ingredients

- Fried onion cutlets
- Softened butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. On each of the fried cutlets place a small ball of softened butter with finely chopped parsley in it. Make these small balls with a teaspoon dipped in hot water, and serve the dish before the butter melts.
