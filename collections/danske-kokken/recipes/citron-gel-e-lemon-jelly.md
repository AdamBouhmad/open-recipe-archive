## Ingredients

- Zest of 3 lemons
- Juice of 5 lemons
- 2 pounds white sugar
- 2 pints water
- 12 quarters isinglass
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make exactly as Recipe 421, except that instead of the zest of oranges use the zest of 3 lemons, and instead of orange juice use the juice of 5 lemons.
