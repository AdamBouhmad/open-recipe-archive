## Ingredients

- a pie dish with compote of dried or fresh fruit
- batter (choice of three types as specified)

## Directions

1. Place the pie dish with compote in the oven to warm through
2. Pour one of the batter types over the compote
3. Bake the pie at good heat for 35 minutes
4. Serve in the dish, wrapped with a napkin, unless the dish is too nice to need covering
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
