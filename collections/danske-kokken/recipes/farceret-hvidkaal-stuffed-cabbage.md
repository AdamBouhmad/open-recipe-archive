## Ingredients

- Cabbage head
- Meat forcemeat
- Water
- Salt
- Large cabbage leaves
- Fine twine or thin cloth
- Butter
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. When the outermost leaves are peeled from the cabbage head, cut a lid from one side where the stalk is
2. Hollow out the cabbage head and fill with meat forcemeat, place the lid on, over it a couple of the large leaves, and bind it with fine twine or a piece of thin cloth and place in boiling salted water, in which it cooks 1½ to 2 hours depending on the size of the head
3. Serve with brown butter mixed with nutmeg
4. The cabbage that was hollowed out can be cooked in the water and minced for soaked cabbage or stewed
