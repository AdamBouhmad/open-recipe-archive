## Ingredients

- 1 pound good raspberries
- 1 pound white sugar or good powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pit the raspberries and sprinkle with 1 pound white sugar or powdered sugar
2. The next day put the whole thing on the fire and cook 10 to 12 minutes with a couple of boils, skimming
3. Pour through a coarse sieve so the juice can drain
4. Cook and skim the juice 8 to 10 minutes
5. When taken off the fire, add the berries to blend with the juice
6. Pour into a pot
