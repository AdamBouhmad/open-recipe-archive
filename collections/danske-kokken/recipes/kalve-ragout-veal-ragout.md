## Ingredients

- Onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Stock (Sky)
- Pepper
- Salt
- Rock sugar
- Pickled cucumber or gherkin strips
- Cooked veal
- Tarragon vinegar (optional)
- Pickled onions or mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice onions, fry them in butter and brown them golden with a couple of tablespoons of flour.
2. Thin with bouillon and stock, adding a little pepper, salt, a piece of rock sugar and some strips of pickled cucumber or gherkin, and cook the whole until it becomes a smooth sauce.
3. Place slices of cooked meat in this and warm until the whole is boiling hot.
4. A little tarragon vinegar, pickled onions or mushrooms improve the flavor.
