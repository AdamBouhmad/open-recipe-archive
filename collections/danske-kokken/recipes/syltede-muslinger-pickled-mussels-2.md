## Ingredients

- Mussels, cooked
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the mussels in the usual manner (see recipe 116)
2. Remove the mussels from their shells
3. Remove the beard and the black intestine
4. Place the mussels in a jar
5. Cook the mussel broth with nutmeg, pepper, cloves and bay leaves
6. Skim and strain the broth
7. Blend the broth with vinegar
8. Pour the mixture over the mussels
