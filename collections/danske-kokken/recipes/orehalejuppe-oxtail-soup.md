## Ingredients

- 2 ox tails cleaned of all waste
- 1 carrot
- 3 parsnips
- 1 celery
- 1 leek
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- 12 whole peppercorns
- 1 cup flour
- 3 ounces butter
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtails into joints and wash thoroughly in cold water
2. Place butter on the bottom of a casserole and add the oxtail pieces with the vegetables, seasonings and ½ pint water
3. Place over strong heat, stir now and then until the bottom of the casserole is covered with a thick, brown sauce or jus
4. Add the flour and blend well
5. Fill the casserole with 1 gallon water, season with salt and stir now and then until it comes to a boil
6. Move the casserole to a gentle heat, skim carefully, and the soup cooks until the oxtail pieces are so tender that the meat easily slips from the bones
7. Remove the pieces and place in the soup tureen
8. Strain the soup over them through a fine sieve
9. Add a celery that has been previously cooked in bouillon and cut into pieces
10. The soup is ready to serve. If you omit the flour, the soup will be clear, and many prefer it this way
