## Ingredients

- Salad heads
- White sugar
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the salad heads and wash them thoroughly; cut them into 7 to 8 pieces.
2. Rub white sugar into hard-boiled egg yolks, and add a little vinegar mixed with water.
3. Add the salad and let it stand for some time before using.
