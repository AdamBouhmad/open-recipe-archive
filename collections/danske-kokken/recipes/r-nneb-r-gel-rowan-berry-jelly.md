## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg rowan berries
- 625 gr sugar
- 1.75 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Gather the berries when they have a beautiful red color and are juicy.
2. Remove them from their stems, rinse, and place them in a pot with 2.5 dl water for each 0.5 kg berries.
3. Cook them well before pouring into the cloth, and weigh the juice that has run through the next day.
4. Cook the specified amount of sugar and water as directed in No. 93, then pour in the juice.
5. Cook and skim for 10 minutes and then pour into jelly cups moistened with Cognac, which are sealed when the jelly has begun to set.
6. Serve especially with game. Rowan berry juice can be mixed with half the amount of currant juice or with juice of cooked, unripe apples, and the jelly tastes just as good as from rowan berry juice alone.
