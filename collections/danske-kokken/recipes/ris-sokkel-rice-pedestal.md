## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram rice
- 2 liters water
- Lemon slices
- Parsley
- Salad
- Red peppers (pimientos)
- Tomatoes
- Mayonnaise
- Fish forcemeat or cooked rice (optional for filling)

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in 2 liters of water
2. Work the porridge well with a spoon in the pot in which it is cooked, frequently dipping the spoon in cold water
3. Arrange the porridge either in the form of long or round pedestals or simply cover the dish with it
4. Around the edge, decorate with lemon slices and parsley or salad
5. Hollowed tomatoes can be filled with mayonnaise to garnish a fried fish dish
6. Red, ripe, fresh peppers can be used as follows: dry them and cut them to an even height, remove the stems and inner seeds, these cases can now be filled with mayonnaise and placed around fried fish or vegetables
7. If filled with fish forcemeat or cooked rice, bake them in the oven
8. Tomatoes can be used in the same way as peppers
