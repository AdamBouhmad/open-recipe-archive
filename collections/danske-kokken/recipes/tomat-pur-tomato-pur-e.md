## Ingredients

- 125 gr (0.25 S) salt to 3 kg (6 S) tomatoes
- Beautiful, red tomatoes without bruised spots
- Salicylic acid

## Directions

1. Dry beautiful, red tomatoes without bruised spots, cut them once through and place them gradually in the preserving pot, sprinkled with salt
2. As soon as they are cooked out, rub the mash through a coarse sieve and pour it back into the pot to just bring to a boil
3. Dissolve 1 teaspoon of salicylic acid in the purée after it is lifted from the heat
4. Pour it boiling onto jars or bottles and bind immediately with parchment paper
5. If salicylic acid is added, the jars only need to be bound with parchment paper, and then the Hercules jar is not necessary, but if the salicylic acid is omitted, the tomato purée must absolutely go into canning jars and be canned, otherwise it is ruined
6. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes
