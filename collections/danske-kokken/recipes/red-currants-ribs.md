## Ingredients

- Currants
- 1.5 Pd. Melis per 1 Pd. currants

## Directions

1. Strip the currants from the stems
2. Boil the Melis into syrup in the usual manner
3. Add the berries and simmer until clear
4. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place in a deep dish
5. Boil the syrup until thick and pour over the berries
6. Shake them until they begin to swell
7. Skim well and preserve in the usual manner
