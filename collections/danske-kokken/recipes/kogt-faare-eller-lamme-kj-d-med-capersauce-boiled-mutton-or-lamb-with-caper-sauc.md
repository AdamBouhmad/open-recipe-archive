## Ingredients

- Salt
- A lamb shank with clear, white and hard fat
- Milk
- Flour for thickening
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Capers

## Directions

1. Put a handful of salt in a roomy pot with boiling water.
2. Choose a lamb shank with clear, white and hard fat, wash it and rub it thoroughly with salt.
3. Boil for three hours or more, according to the size of the shank.
4. Make a sauce from a pint of boiling milk, thickened with flour; add butter, salt, pepper and 2 spoonfuls of capers, and serve the meat with it.
