## Ingredients

- Chicken, cooked until meat separates easily from bones
- Green lettuce
- 1 tablespoon salad oil
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1/2 teaspoon each of salt, dry mustard, and white pepper
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Cayenne pepper to taste
- 3 gills salad oil
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- Olives
- Capers
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook chicken until meat separates easily from bones
2. Remove all meat from bones, free from skin and fat, and cut into pieces about 3/8 inch or smaller
3. Wash green lettuce, break into small pieces, and dry on a cloth
4. Mix meat and lettuce in a deep dish and season with 1 tablespoon salad oil, 1 teaspoon vinegar, and 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper
5. Prepare a mayonnaise as follows: blend 1 raw egg yolk with 1/2 teaspoon each of salt, dry mustard, and white pepper, juice of 1/2 lemon, and cayenne pepper to taste
6. Mix well with a whisk, then add 3 gills salad oil and juice of 1/2 lemon drop by drop while stirring constantly
7. When two-thirds of the oil is incorporated, begin adding vinegar, totaling 4 tablespoons
8. Cover the salad with the mayonnaise and decorate with olives, capers, and hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
