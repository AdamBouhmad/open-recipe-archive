## Ingredients

- 2½ to 3 Kg (5 to 6 S) lamb meat
- ½ Skp. peas
- Salt
- Butter
- Sugar
- Small boiled carrots for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into smaller pieces as for fricassée.
2. After washing them, scald them in boiling water and place them in a pot.
3. Pour 1 L (1 Pot) boiling water over the meat with a little salt, and only when the broth is completely skimmed, add the peas.
4. Cook the meat with the peas tender under a lid and over gentle heat, but it must be constantly watched so the water does not evaporate and the meat and peas burn on.
5. If the liquid shrinks away, pour a little more broth or water into the pot.
6. The meat must be stirred to become tender, so that the top pieces come to the bottom of the pot.
7. When it is nearly done, add 125 Gr (¼ S) butter along with salt and sugar, if the peas are not sufficiently sweet in themselves.
8. Serve meat and peas together on a platter, and the dish can be garnished with small boiled carrots.
