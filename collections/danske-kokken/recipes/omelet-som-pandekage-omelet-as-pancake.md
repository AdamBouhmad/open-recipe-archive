## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 4 teaspoons potato flour
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks well with 4 teaspoons powdered sugar, 4 teaspoons potato flour and 4 tablespoons cream
2. Finally beat in the 4 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks
3. Bake over slow heat
4. Serve with powdered sugar and jam or jelly on top
5. This portion makes 2 omelets; beat the egg whites fresh for each omelet, just before baking
