## Ingredients

- Margarine
- Butter

## Directions

1. A mixture of the best margarine and good butter can be browned together in a pan just before serving.
2. It is sufficiently brown as soon as it has stopped foaming; then, if desired, the sediment can be strained out before it is poured into the sauce bowl.
3. To browned butter can be added lemon juice or vinegar, if it suits the dish it is to be served with.
4. Naturally, it is preferable to use only butter for this, but margarine is excellent for mixing with it.
