## Ingredients

- Scalded chestnuts
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock
- Salt
- Sugar
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Scalded chestnuts, from which the shell and brown skin have been removed, are boiled tender in stock. Brown butter and flour and dilute with the stock in which they are cooked. Season with salt, sugar and Madeira. Give the chestnuts a brief cooking in this before serving.
