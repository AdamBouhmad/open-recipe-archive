## Ingredients

- Old rye bread
- ½ L cream
- Raisins
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon
- Rhubarb juice
- Sherry
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Soak old rye bread in water for a few hours and cook it.
2. Press it through a coarse sieve with the help of boiling water until the soup becomes suitably thin and there is sufficient quantity.
3. Return the bread soup to the fire to cook with raisins, lemon peel and whole cinnamon.
4. Prepare with rhubarb juice, sherry and sugar or with jam.
5. Bread soup can be served with cooked apples, or add a well-sweetened gooseberry compote to the serving bowl.
6. Serve with cream foam.
