## Ingredients

- leeks
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut pieces of leeks 2 to 3 centimeters long and cook them until almost tender in water with salt
2. Drain them in a colander so the water can run off, before tossing them in a mixture of oil, vinegar, pepper and salt
