## Ingredients

- pike or other fish
- cold butter
- melted butter
- eggs
- wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Scrape 4 pounds of fish free of skin and bone, then chop it with half a pound of cold butter
2. Place it in a mortar to be pounded and thin during pounding with a pound of melted butter until it becomes like a stiff dough and releases from the mortar
3. Add 8 eggs one at a time while stirring constantly, then add 4 spoonfuls of wheat flour, salt and pepper to taste, and enough sweet cream so that the mixture becomes appropriately thick
