## Ingredients

- Calf tongues
- Bread crumbs
- Eggs
- Butter
- Vegetables
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongues as for Fresh Calf Tongues with Mayonnaise.
2. In the center of the plate on which the tongues are arranged, place the vegetables.
3. The vegetables may either be stewed or eaten with stirred butter.
4. If vegetables are stewed, serve browned butter with the tongues.
