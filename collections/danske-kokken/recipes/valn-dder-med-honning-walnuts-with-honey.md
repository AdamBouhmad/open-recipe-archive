## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cooked walnuts
- 1/2 kg honey

## Directions

1. Prick the walnuts, place in cold water and cook until tender in water as described in the previous recipe
2. Weigh them and can be studded with cloves and cinnamon if desired
3. Bring the honey to a boil and give the nuts another boil in it
4. Let stand overnight, then drain the honey
5. Bring the honey to a boil and pour immediately over the nuts
6. Boil the honey a third time and finally give the walnuts another boil in it before placing in glasses and covering with the boiling honey
7. Bind immediately
