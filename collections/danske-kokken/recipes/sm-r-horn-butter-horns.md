## Ingredients

- 375 g margarine
- 1/2 kg flour
- 1 tablespoon icing sugar
- 15 g yeast, mixed with 1 teaspoon salt
- just over 1/4 L half-skimmed milk
- egg for brushing
- sugar and chopped almonds for coating

## Directions

1. Crumble the margarine into the flour
2. Add the icing sugar, yeast mixed with salt, and milk
3. Mix everything together and let the dough rise
4. Roll out the dough to 1/4 inch thickness
5. Cut into small triangles
6. Roll up from the wide side, shape and curve like horns
7. Brush with egg and dip in sugar and chopped almonds
8. Bake at good heat for approximately 20 minutes
