## Ingredients

- 1 small wheat bread, approximately 1 pound in weight
- 6 large celeries
- Meat broth
- White root vegetables
- Small meat dumplings

## Directions

1. Grate the bread and cook it in barely simmering water together with 6 large celeries until these can easily be pressed apart. Then dilute the whole with meat broth, cook a little, press through a colander, then through a sieve and place back on the fire with some cut white root vegetables. When it boils, add small meat dumplings. In the absence of meat dumplings, the soup can be eaten with toasted bread.
