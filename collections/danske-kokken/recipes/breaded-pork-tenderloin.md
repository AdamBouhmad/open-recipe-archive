## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloin
- Egg
- Bread
- Butter
- Parsley
- Lemon slices
- Tomato slices
- Cooked vegetables

## Directions

1. Beat the pork tenderloin flat, dip it in egg and bread, and roast it on the pan in browned butter.
2. Arrange with the butter poured over.
3. Garnish with parsley, lemon and tomato slices.
4. Pork tenderloin can be served garnished with cooked vegetables.
5. Serve with creamed butter.
