## Ingredients

- Small beet roots
- Butter
- Sugar
- Coloring
- Broth or gravy
- Salt
- Ground pepper

## Directions

1. Usually choose the smallest roots, which are less often tough
2. Peel, rinse, and cut them into long thin strips
3. Brown them in butter and sugar
4. When they have taken on a brownish color, a little coloring can be added, as well as a small amount of broth or gravy, and cook the roots tender in this
5. Usually the liquid will have evaporated; season with salt and ground pepper
