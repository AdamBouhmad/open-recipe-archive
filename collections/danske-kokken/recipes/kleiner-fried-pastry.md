## Ingredients

- 6 Egg Yolks
- 2 whole Eggs
- half a Pound white Sugar or dried and crushed Powdered Sugar
- 2 Tablespoons Cream
- 1 Tablespoon Butter (clarified)
- 1 Pound Flour

## Directions

1. Whip 6 Egg Yolks, 2 whole Eggs, and half a Pound white Sugar or dried and crushed Powdered Sugar well and stir with 2 Tablespoons Cream and 1 Tablespoon clarified Butter
2. Add as much of 1 Pound Flour as you can incorporate; work the rest in on the board until the batter releases from the board and hands
3. Roll out very thin with Flour on the outside and cut into oblong pieces, best with a pastry wheel, so the cakes become notched at the edge
4. Cut a slit in the middle of each piece and push one end of the cake through it, so that it becomes twisted in the middle
5. Fry very light
