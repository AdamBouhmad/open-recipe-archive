## Ingredients

- Eel
- Butter
- Spinach
- Peas
- Carrots
- Cauliflower
- Potato purée
- Scrambled eggs

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the eel before cutting it into smaller pieces
2. Fry the pieces in butter in a frying pan
3. Serve the browned butter in which they were fried alongside the smoked eel
4. Serve stewed vegetables such as spinach, peas, carrots, cauliflower, potato purée, or scrambled eggs as garnish
