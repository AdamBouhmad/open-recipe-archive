## Ingredients

- 2 quarts cornmeal
- 3 pints hot water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 large tablespoons homemade or 1 cent baker's yeast
- 1 3/4 cups wheat flour
- 3/4 pint hot water

## Directions

1. Blend 2 quarts cornmeal with 3 pints hot water.
2. Add 1 tablespoon salt, 2 tablespoons sugar, and 2 large tablespoons homemade or 1 cent baker's yeast.
3. Let stand 5 hours in a warm place.
4. Add 1 3/4 cups wheat flour and 3/4 pint hot water.
5. Let it rise again for 1 1/2 hours, knead lightly, and divide into bread pans, letting them stand a few minutes before putting them in.
6. Bake in a moderate oven for 1 1/2 hours.
