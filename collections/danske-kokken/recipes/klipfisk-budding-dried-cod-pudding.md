## Ingredients

- Dried salted cod
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 rusks
- Flour
- Milk
- 0.5 pound melted butter
- Salt
- 1 cup cleaned currants
- Egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Soak the dried cod in water the evening before or at least in the morning.
2. Boil it and chop it fine; for each deep plate full of chopped fish use 8 egg yolks.
3. Soak 8 rusks in flour, let stand covered for some time, and then rub them together with the egg yolks and enough milk to make a consistency like ordinary pancake batter.
4. Add 0.5 pound of melted butter (reserve the salt), 1 cup of well-cleaned currants, then the fish, and lastly the stiffly beaten egg whites.
5. Immediately pour into the buttered form and boil for 2 hours.
