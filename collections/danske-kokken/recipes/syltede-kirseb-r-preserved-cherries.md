## Ingredients

- 5 pounds cherries with or without pits
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- 3 ounces cinnamon
- ½ ounce cloves

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar together with the vinegar and spices (these crushed and tied in a piece of muslin)
2. Pour the whole hot over the berries
