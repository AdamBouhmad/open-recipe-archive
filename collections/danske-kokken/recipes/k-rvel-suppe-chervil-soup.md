## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) stock
- 5-6 tablespoons chopped chervil
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour

## Directions

1. Bake 125 g (1/4 lb) butter and 125 g (1/4 lb) flour together and thin with the stock.
2. When it boils, add the shelled and finely chopped chervil, which is given just one boil.
3. New carrots and asparagus, cooked in some of the stock, can be added, then mixed with the remaining soup.
