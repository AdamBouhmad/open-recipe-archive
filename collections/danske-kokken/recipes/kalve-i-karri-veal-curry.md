## Ingredients

- Veal breast or other meat-rich cuts
- Butter
- Onion
- Wheat flour
- Curry powder
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Chop the meat into small pieces and wash it in several changes of water.
2. Melt a large spoonful of butter in a pot over heat; when melted but not browned, add a small grated onion and cook for a couple of minutes.
3. Stir in two large spoonfuls of wheat flour and curry powder to taste, along with salt (use sparingly, about half a teaspoon, as more can be added later).
4. Cook this together and gradually add boiling water until it becomes a thick sauce.
5. Add the meat and gradually add more boiling water as needed, keeping the sauce fairly thick.
6. Cook covered as much as possible, but skim thoroughly.
7. The dish is ready when the meat is tender, which takes about an hour for a couple of pounds of meat.
8. Serve immediately with rice; if the sauce has become lumpy, remove the meat first and pour the sauce over it.
