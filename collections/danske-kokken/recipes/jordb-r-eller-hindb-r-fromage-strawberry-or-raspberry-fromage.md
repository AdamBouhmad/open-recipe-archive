## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilo (1/2 S) vanilla chocolate
- 12 sheets isinglass
- 5 teaspoons cacao
- 175 gr (35 quintal) powdered sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) water
- 3/4 L (3 pægle) cream
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) cream for decoration

## Directions

1. Melt the vanilla chocolate, isinglass, cacao and powdered sugar in water
2. When almost cold but not yet set, stir in whipped cream
3. Decorate at serving with whipped cream
4. Prepare the same day it is to be used
