## Ingredients

- Well-cleaned and dried raspberries
- 2 to 3 pounds of powdered sugar per pot of juice

## Directions

1. Mash the berries in a pot and wring them in a clean linen cloth
2. Place the sugar in the juice to dissolve overnight
3. Before putting it on to cook, skim it with the utmost care
4. Cook for half an hour, skim well and pour through a filter bag or piece of flannel
5. Fill it cold into bottles
6. Berries can be gathered over several days in a pot and should then be mashed gradually and stirred a couple of times daily
7. Otherwise treat as described above
