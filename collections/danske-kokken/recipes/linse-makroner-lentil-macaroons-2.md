## Ingredients

- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) blanched, peeled, finely chopped almonds
- 1/4 Kg (1.5 lb) powdered sugar
- 6 raw egg whites
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- thin rolled butter dough for lining the molds

## Directions

1. Line the molds with thin rolled butter dough
2. Mix the almond paste made from the blanched, peeled, finely chopped almonds with the powdered sugar, raw egg whites, and baking powder
3. Place the mixture in the molds with a teaspoon
4. Bake the cakes light brown at even heat
