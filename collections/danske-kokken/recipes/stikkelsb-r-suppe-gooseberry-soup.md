## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg green gooseberries
- water
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- egg yolks
- sugar
- sago grains

## Directions

1. Rinse gooseberries without removing husks and cook in water with a little lemon peel and whole cinnamon
2. Strain and thicken
3. Season with egg yolks like rhubarb soup
4. Can cook fine sago grains in the strained gooseberry soup
