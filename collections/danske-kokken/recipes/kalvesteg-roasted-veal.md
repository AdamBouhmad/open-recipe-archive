## Ingredients

- Veal roast
- Butter
- Flour
- Cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. The roast becomes whiter and thicker by blanching. If you wish to take on that trouble, it is good to lard the veal roast.
2. It is roasted in a shorter time than beef roast and should, in our opinion, not be red inside.
3. In the sauce stir cream and a piece of sugar.
4. It is good to let some butter and flour, in which a couple of teaspoonfuls of wheat flour have been rubbed, come to a boil in a roasting pan, and then pour it over the roast after it has been browned in butter; with this it is basted often.
