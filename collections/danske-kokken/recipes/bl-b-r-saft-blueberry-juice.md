## Ingredients

- Blueberries
- Brandy

## Directions

1. Place the berries over the fire and boil for a quarter hour
2. Stir often so nothing sticks to the bottom
3. When slightly cooled, wring them out in a cloth
4. When the juice is completely cold, put it in small bottles that have been well filled with brandy; also pour a layer of brandy on top of each bottle, which is then immediately corked
5. This inexpensive juice keeps well all winter
