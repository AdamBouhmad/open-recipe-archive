## Ingredients

- Hvidling (Whiting)
- Salt
- smeltet Smør (Melted butter)
- hakket Persille (Chopped parsley)
- kogte Kartofler (Boiled potatoes)

## Directions

1. Clean the whiting as you would cod.
2. Place whole in boiling salted water.
3. Cook for only a few minutes.
4. Serve with melted butter and chopped parsley along with boiled potatoes.
