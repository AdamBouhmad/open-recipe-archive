## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef chuck or middle cut
- Onion slices
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Flour
- Brown butter or Palmin
- Gravy, broth or boiling water
- Brown butter or margarine
- Flour for sauce
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Food coloring or soya
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Brown a good portion of onion slices, then scrape them from the pan.
2. Cut the meat into smaller pieces and grind once through a meat grinder.
3. Form into round patties with a knife to create well-minced small beef pieces.
4. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper and dredge in flour.
5. Fry the patties in brown butter or Palmin in a pan; they should be red and juicy but not bloody.
6. Deglaze the pan with gravy, broth or boiling water.
7. Dry the pan, and in a little brown butter or margarine, make a roux with flour, which is thinned with the deglazed liquid to make a smooth sauce.
8. Season the sauce with Maggi bouillon cubes, salt and food coloring or soya.
9. Let the onions simmer in the sauce, which is poured over the patties.
10. Serve with boiled potatoes.
11. Optionally, sinews and bones from the meat can be simmered in very little water, and this broth can be used for the sauce.
