## Ingredients

- One good pork liver
- Water
- Salt
- Pork fat
- Fine salt
- Ground white pepper
- Shallot onions
- Fat
- Sweet cream or cold bacon soup
- Intestines

## Directions

1. Place a good pork liver in water and let it stand for one day so that the blood is completely drawn out.
2. Cook it then in some water with salt until it is tender, then take it up, set it aside to become cold, and remove the skin.
3. Then grate the liver finely, making sure that there are no small hard pieces in between.
4. From pork fat remove all sinews and skins, then scrape the fat so it becomes like butter.
5. Mix equal weight of this and of the grated liver well together with finely ground salt and white pepper.
6. Some shallot onions are cooked down in a little fat, and this is strained into the mass.
7. Stir with sweet cream or in the absence thereof with cold bacon soup until it has reached an appropriate thickness, then stuff firmly into cleaned intestines.
8. Tie these and sprinkle with salt, after which they stand 1 day.
9. They then get a brief cooking of a few minutes in warm water, are taken up and stirred.
10. Eaten cold with open-faced sandwiches.
