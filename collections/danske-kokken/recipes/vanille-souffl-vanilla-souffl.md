## Ingredients

- 3 measures sweet cream
- 60 drams white sugar
- vanilla
- 14 egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil 3 measures sweet cream and 60 drams white sugar with a vanilla stick, split lengthwise
2. Beat 14 egg yolks and pour the boiling cream into them
3. Pour everything back into the pan and bring to a boil with constant beating
4. Pour out to cool and beat
