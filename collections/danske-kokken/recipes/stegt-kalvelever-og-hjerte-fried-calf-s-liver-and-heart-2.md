## Ingredients

- fresh calf liver
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- onions
- stock

## Directions

1. Place the fresh calf liver in cold water for a quarter hour, then rinse and remove the membrane
2. Cut into slices of suitable size, approximately the size of cutlets, and rinse these slices in several changes of cold water
3. Beat lightly on both sides with a mallet, sprinkle with salt, pepper and flour, and place in a pan with browned butter
4. Turn and fry until completely cooked through
5. Remove from the pan and brown some chopped onions in the remaining butter, then pour this sauce over the liver
6. If necessary, thin the sauce with stock
7. The liver can also be chopped finely instead of being beaten
