## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef sirloin
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Cloves
- ¼ L vinegar
- 1 L beer
- Whole peppercorns
- 3 bay leaves
- 2 onions
- Butter or palmin
- Water
- Soya or coloring

## Directions

1. Beat the meat well with a meat mallet and rub with a mixture of salt, ground pepper and a little cloves
2. Place the meat for 3 to 4 days in a brine of ¼ L vinegar, 1 L beer, some whole peppercorns, 3 bay leaves and 2 onions cut into slices, turning it regularly
3. Brown a good slice of butter or palmin in a pot and place the meat in it after the brine has drained well
4. When sufficiently browned, pour water and a little soya or coloring over it
5. Baste the roast regularly with the gravy, turn it and roast very gently covered for 2½ to 3 hours
6. Strain the gravy
