## Ingredients

- pointed cabbage
- ragout of game animals and game birds, or geese, hens or pigeons
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. The mold is decorated as in recipe 54. The cabbage is prepared in the same manner as directed in that recipe.
2. In the middle, ragout of game animals and game birds, of geese, hens or pigeons can be filled, and on top a layer of cabbage.
3. There must not be too much sauce with the meat. The rest is served to the Chartreuse, which is turned out onto a serving dish with great care.
