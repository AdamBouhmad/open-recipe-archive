## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- 3/4 pound cold butter
- 1 measure milk
- a handful caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Work 1 pound wheat flour, 3/4 pound cold butter, 1 measure milk and a handful caraway seeds together.
2. Roll out into very thin cakes, cut into oblong, square pieces with a pastry wheel, double the size of a playing card.
3. Prick with a fork and bake at good heat.
