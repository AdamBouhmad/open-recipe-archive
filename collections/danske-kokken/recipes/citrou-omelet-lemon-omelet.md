## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound clarified butter
- 0.5 pound powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon fine wheat flour
- 12 beaten egg yolks
- 1 quart sweet cream
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Grated peel of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir clarified butter with powdered sugar
2. Stir in wheat flour
3. Add beaten egg yolks
4. Add sweet cream
5. Add lemon juice, strained to avoid seeds
6. Add grated lemon peel
7. Beat well together
8. Gently fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
