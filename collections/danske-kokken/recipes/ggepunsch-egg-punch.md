## Ingredients

- 4 whole eggs
- 9 egg yolks
- 1½ pounds ground, sifted white sugar
- peel of 1 lemon
- juice of 4 lemons
- 5 measures white wine
- 6 measures boiled water
- 4 measures arrack

## Directions

1. Whisk 4 whole eggs and 9 egg yolks to foam in a large saucepan
2. Add 1½ pounds ground, sifted white sugar, to which the peel of a lemon has been grated
3. Whisk again until quite white
4. Press the juice of 4 lemons into it
5. Pour in 5 measures white wine, 6 measures boiled water and 4 measures arrack
6. Set the whole over embers
7. Continue whisking until it begins to rise and foam up
8. Pour the punch into a tureen and serve immediately warm
