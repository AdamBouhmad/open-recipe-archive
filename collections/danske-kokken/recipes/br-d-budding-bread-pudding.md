## Ingredients

- one large French bread
- milk
- 200 grams margarine
- 12 egg yolks
- egg whites, beaten
- salt
- sugar
- crushed almonds, finely cut citron peel, raisins, orange essence, grated lemon zest, or 3 crushed vanilla tablets (optional)

## Directions

1. From a large French bread, cut off the crust, and cut the bread into thick slices, which are dipped in milk and left covered for a couple of hours, after which they are mashed with a spoon.
2. 200 grams of margarine is melted, and the bread is baked in it until it is well combined.
3. When the batter is slightly cooled, it is stirred with 12 egg yolks and finally with the beaten egg whites.
4. A little salt and sugar are added to taste, and the pudding can be seasoned either with crushed almonds, finely cut citron peel, raisins, orange essence, grated lemon zest, or 3 crushed vanilla tablets.
5. Serve with fruit sauces, ginger sauce, or red wine sauce.
