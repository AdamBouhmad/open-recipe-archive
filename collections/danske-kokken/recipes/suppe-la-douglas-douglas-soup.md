## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 Pots) soup
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Sago flour
- Water
- Sherry or Madeira
- 1/2 Kg (1 S) finely diced asparagus
- Veal forcemeat

## Directions

1. Take a good, clear, brown spare soup and add Maggi Bouillon cubes if needed
2. Thicken with sago flour mixed in water
3. Season with a little Sherry or Madeira to taste
4. Add 1/2 Kg (1 S) finely diced asparagus and some very small balls of veal forcemeat (Nr. 201) to the soup
5. Serve with small water pastries
