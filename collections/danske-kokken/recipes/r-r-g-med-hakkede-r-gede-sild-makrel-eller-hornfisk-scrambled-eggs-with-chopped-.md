## Ingredients

- Scrambled eggs
- Smoked herring, mackerel, or garfish, chopped

## Directions

1. Place the set scrambled eggs in a bowl as they are taken from the pan. At serving, turn this mound over onto a smaller platter, and place the chopped fish around it.
