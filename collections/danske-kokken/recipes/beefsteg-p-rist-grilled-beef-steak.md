## Ingredients

- Porterhouse beef
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Use Porterhouse beef for steak. Skin it and scrape it from the sides; make cuts here and there in the meat so that it cooks through more easily.
2. Place it on the grill and cook over clear coals of hardwood. When you think it is cooked enough on one side, turn it.
3. Do not let it become too dry; it should be red inside.
4. When done, place it on a platter, lay cold butter and salt over it and let it rest a moment in the oven, but serve it before the butter is completely melted. Eat it with potatoes.
