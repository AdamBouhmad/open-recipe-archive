## Ingredients

- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg mulberries

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the ripe mulberries with sugar and let sit until the next day.
2. Pour everything into the pot and boil for only 4 to 5 minutes.
3. Remove the mulberries with a slotted spoon and boil and skim the syrup for at least 10 minutes.
4. Pour the boiling syrup over the berries and seal the jar.
5. After a few days, check it, and if the syrup is too thin, pour it off, bring to a boil, and pour immediately back over the berries.
