## Ingredients

- 6 whole eggs
- 1 pound flour
- 4 pounds sugar
- lukewarm cream
- lemon zest
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs in a bowl and stir together with the flour and sugar
2. Thin the mixture with lukewarm cream
3. Add a little grated lemon zest and ground cinnamon if desired
4. The batter should be thick enough to cling to a spoon
5. Drop into boiling butter or fat as small balls
6. Fry and serve hot
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving
