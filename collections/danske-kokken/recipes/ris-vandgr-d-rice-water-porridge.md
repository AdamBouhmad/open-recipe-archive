## Ingredients

- ½ kg rice, rinsed and blanched
- 4 L water
- apples, blanched and cut, or rhubarb treated the same way, or blanched raisins
- butter
- salt
- butter
- sugar
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Stir the rinsed and blanched rice into the water when it boils, and after 1 hour apples (blanched and cut), rhubarb (treated the same way), or blanched raisins can be cooked in it.
2. Stir a little butter and salt into the porridge.
3. Serve with butter, sugar and sweet milk.
