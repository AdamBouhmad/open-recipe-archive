## Ingredients

- 15 pails vinegar
- 1 pound granulated sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- ginger
- cloves
- 2 pounds tomatoes, peeled and cut into smaller pieces or slices

## Directions

1. Boil vinegar and granulated sugar with cinnamon sticks, ginger, and cloves until a smooth syrup forms
2. Add the prepared tomatoes to the syrup
3. Boil and skim for 10 minutes
4. Pour into jars and bind when the tomatoes have cooled
