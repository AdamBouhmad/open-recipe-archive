## Ingredients

- Ripe or overripe pears
- Water (amount as specified)

## Directions

1. Do not peel the pears, but cut them through and remove the flower and stem
2. Rinse the fruit and place it in a pot with the specified amount of water
3. Cook the pears until completely soft so that the juice can be pressed out using a fruit press or through a sieve
4. Pour the juice into the pot and cook until it is thick as syrup and hangs in threads from the spoon, at which point it will have darkened in color
5. Pour the syrup into jars and seal with parchment paper; it can be preserved for years
