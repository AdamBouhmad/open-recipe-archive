## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg quinces
- water
- sugar
- sago flour
- white wine or bottled rhubarb juice

## Directions

1. Wipe quinces, cut off blossom and stem, cut into smaller pieces
2. Rinse and cook until tender in water so they easily pass through a sieve
3. Bring the strained fruit soup to boil, sweeten and thicken with sago flour mixed with water
4. Season with white wine or a little bottled rhubarb juice
