## Ingredients

- 2 lamb chops per person
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Often 2 bones are taken so that the pieces do not become too small; during preparation one is cut away.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and fry like veal chops, but not as long.
3. Arrange stewed spinach in the center of the dish and place the chops around it on edge.
4. Practically all vegetables go well with this.
5. They can also be served with gooseberry compote and cucumber salad or head lettuce, prepared with cream or with oil, vinegar, and pepper.
