## Ingredients

- Veal roast
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock from the veal roast
- Raw cream
- Browned potatoes
- Lettuce or cucumber salad
- Pickled vegetables
- Preserved fruit compote

## Directions

1. Make a good brown sauce from butter, flour and stock from the veal roast
2. Stir a little raw cream into this sauce
3. Warm the cut meat in this sauce
4. Place the warmed meat on a flat dish
5. Garnish with browned potatoes
6. Serve with lettuce or cucumber salad, pickled vegetables, preserves and fruit compote
