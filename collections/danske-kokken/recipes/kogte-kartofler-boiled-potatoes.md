## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar (1 teaspoon per 6 potatoes for winter potatoes)

## Directions

1. For early new potatoes: wash well and dry in a rough cloth. Place over heat in hot but not boiling salted water. They require very little cooking.
2. For winter potatoes: peel and leave in cold water for several hours before cooking. Place in an enameled pot in cold water with salt and vinegar, which will make them white and attractive.
3. Once cooked, drain the water and place over low heat, shaking them dry until water is completely evaporated.
4. If cooked ahead of serving, drain water and immediately cover with a cloth to keep warm.
5. Serve with onion sauce (recipe 796) and with matjes herring, salt herring, spiced herring, or smoked ham.
