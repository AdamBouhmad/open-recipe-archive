## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Water
- White sugar, 1 pound per pound of peels
- ¼ pint of the water the peels were cooked in

## Directions

1. Peel the lemons and remove the pulp, place them in soft water for 9 days, giving them fresh water every day
2. Put them to cook in the same water until they are so soft that a straw can be pierced through them
3. For 1 pound peels, take 1 pound white sugar, which with ¼ pint of the water the peels were cooked in, boil to a syrup
4. Give the lemon peels one cooking in this syrup; four days later boil the syrup and repeat this twice more
