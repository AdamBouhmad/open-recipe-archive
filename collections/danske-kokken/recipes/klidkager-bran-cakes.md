## Ingredients

- 4 pounds coarse rye bran
- a few spoonfuls gluten flour (if available)
- 1 pound butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons sodium bicarbonate
- 5 quints pressed yeast
- a handful salt
- a handful caraway seeds
- 5.5 measures boiled, cooled water
- 0.5 measure vinegar

## Directions

1. The evening before the cakes are to be baked, work together 4 pounds coarse rye bran, a few spoonfuls gluten flour if available, 1 pound butter, 4 eggs, 2 teaspoons sodium bicarbonate, 5 quints pressed yeast, a handful salt, a handful caraway seeds, 5.5 measures boiled, cooled water and 0.5 measure vinegar into a dough.
2. Before baking, press the dough out on a pan with the hand to the thickness of a biscuit, roll thin and smooth with a bottle and cut into round or square small cakes before the pan is placed in the oven.
3. When the dough has dried a little on the pan, loosen all the cakes with a thin knife and later turn them so they can also dry on the side facing the pan.
