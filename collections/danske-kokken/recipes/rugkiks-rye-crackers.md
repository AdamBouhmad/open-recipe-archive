## Ingredients

- 1 pound rye flour
- 3/4 pound butter
- 1 cup cream
- crushed dried caraway
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Knead the rye flour, butter, cream, crushed dried caraway, and sugar to taste well together.
2. Roll out into thin dough and cut out crackers with a glass.
3. Prick with a fork and bake at even heat.
4. This yields approximately 100 pieces.
