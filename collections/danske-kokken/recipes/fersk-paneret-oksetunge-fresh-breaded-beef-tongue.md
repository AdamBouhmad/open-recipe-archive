## Ingredients

- One cooked tongue, skin removed
- Bread crumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from a cooked tongue and sprinkle with bread crumbs, then pour melted butter over it.
2. Place it in a hot oven to brown.
3. Slice the tongue into slices, then reassemble them so it lies whole.
4. Arrange and garnish either with endive, lettuce, or parsley.
5. Serve with one of the following sauces: Sauce piquante, Sauce à la diable, Tomato Sauce, or Brown Mushroom Sauce.
