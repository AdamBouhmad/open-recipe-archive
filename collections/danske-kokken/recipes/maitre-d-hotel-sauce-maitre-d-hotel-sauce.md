## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Meat or fish stock
- Chervil
- Parsley
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Cold butter

## Directions

1. In 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) of white, smooth sauce made with meat or fish stock, add 1 tablespoon of finely chopped chervil and 1 tablespoon of parsley
2. Bind with 2 egg yolks
3. Stir in salt, lemon juice, and a slice of cold butter
4. This sauce suits fish, poultry, veal, and lamb
