## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) flour
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) icing sugar
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) margarine
- 2 eggs
- 1 small teaspoon baking soda, mixed in 1 1/4 dl (1/4 pint) water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together into a dough
2. Pipe and shape into S's or sticks
