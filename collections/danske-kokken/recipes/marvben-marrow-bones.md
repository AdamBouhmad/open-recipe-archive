## Ingredients

- Marrow bones
- Bouillon or boiled water
- Maggi Bouillon cube
- Linen cloth
- Rye bread
- Kitchen salt
- Horseradish
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Order the marrow bones from the butcher in advance.
2. Scrape the bones well on the outside and place them in cold water for 10 minutes.
3. Bind the ends with washed small linen cloths.
4. Place the bones in a pot and cover with bouillon or boiled water, adding one Maggi Bouillon cube for each 1/4 liter (1 pint).
5. Bring to a boil, then place the pot over low heat and simmer gently for at least one hour; it must not boil hard.
6. When serving, remove the linen cloths and place the bones on a hot plate, wrap with a warm napkin, and serve as quickly as possible.
7. Serve with hot toasted rye bread, kitchen salt, grated horseradish, or chopped parsley.
