## Ingredients

- 3 Bottles White Beer
- Juice and Peel of 1 Lemon
- whole Cinnamon
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) cold Water
- Sugar
- Sherry or white Wine
- Cognac
- 500 Gr (1 lb) Rye Rusks

## Directions

1. Put the beer in the tureen together with juice and peel of 1 lemon, whole cinnamon and 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) cold water
2. Add sugar, sherry or white wine and cognac to taste
3. Cover the cold soup and let it stand thus for a couple of hours in a cold place
4. Shortly before serving, 250 Gr (1/2 Q) rye rusks are placed in the tureen, while the rest is served on the side
