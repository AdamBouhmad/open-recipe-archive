## Ingredients

- Venison head, neck and breast
- Claret
- Carrots
- Onions
- Water
- Salt
- Kulør (caramel coloring)
- Paprika
- Butter
- Flour
- Madeira or red wine
- Veal or fish forcemeat for balls
- Hard-boiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop the venison head, neck and breast into smaller pieces
2. Brown in claret together with carrots and a couple of onions
3. Pour in enough water so it is level with the meat
4. Add salt, kulør and paprika
5. When the soup is well skimmed and the meat is cooked tender but not shredded, remove and use for hachis
6. The bones can then be put back in the soup to cook longer with it
7. Brown butter and flour and dilute with the strained soup
8. Season with Madeira or red wine and serve with balls made of either venison or fish forcemeat
9. Hard-boiled egg yolks can be added to the soup
