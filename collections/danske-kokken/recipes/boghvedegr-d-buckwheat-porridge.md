## Ingredients

- 625 Gr (1 1/2 S) fine, hulled buckwheat grains
- 5 L (5 Potter) skimmed milk
- 3 bottles pale ale

## Directions

1. When the milk boils, sprinkle the grains into it while stirring.
2. This porridge only needs 1/4 hour of cooking.
3. Serve with butter and sweetened pale ale.
4. Leftover porridge can be used for ale porridge.
