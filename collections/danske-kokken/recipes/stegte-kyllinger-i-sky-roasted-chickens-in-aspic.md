## Ingredients

- roasted chickens
- aspic
- herb salad

## Directions

1. Portion roasted chickens in 4 pieces and place in a ring mold
2. Pour the prepared aspic over them
3. When the mold is turned out onto the dish, place an herb salad in the middle
