## Ingredients

- Pie dough
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 1 grated apple
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 small lump melted butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1 small cup hot water
- Beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Line the pie form with ordinary pie dough.
2. Grate together the zest of 2 lemons with a grated apple.
3. Stir together 2 egg yolks with 1 cup sugar, a small lump of melted butter, 2 tablespoons flour, and the juice of 2 lemons.
4. Stir in 1 small cup of hot water and beat the whole mixture well.
5. Pour the mixture onto the dough.
6. Bake as you would an egg pie: bake, brush with beaten egg whites, and set in the oven again.
