## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 1/2 pound white sugar
- 6 quintals isinglass dissolved in boiling water
- 2 pails thick cream, whipped to stiff foam
- Maraschino liqueur
- biscuits
- candied fruits

## Directions

1. Heat 1 pot sweet cream with 1/2 pound white sugar and pour out.
2. Dissolve 6 quintals isinglass in boiling water and add it to the cream.
3. Whip 2 pails thick cream to stiff foam.
4. When the cooked cream is approximately cold, add enough Maraschino to give the cream a strong flavor of it, and then add the whipped cream.
5. Let the whole stand for a couple of hours in ice in a jelly mold, then turn it out and garnish with biscuits and candied fruits.
