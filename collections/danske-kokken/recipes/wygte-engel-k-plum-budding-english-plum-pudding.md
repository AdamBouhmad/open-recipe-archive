## Ingredients

- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound raisins, pitted
- 1 pound almonds, blanched and chopped
- 1 pound chopped suet
- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound breadcrumbs
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon and cloves mixed
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 2 pints rum
- 12 well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix together currants, raisins, almonds, suet, flour, breadcrumbs, cinnamon and cloves, lemon zest, and rum
2. Add 12 well-beaten eggs
3. Boil in a napkin well dusted with flour for 4.5 hours
4. Serve with melted butter with sherry and sugar, or brandy sauce
