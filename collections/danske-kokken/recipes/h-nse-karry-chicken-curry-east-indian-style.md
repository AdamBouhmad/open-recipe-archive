## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- a good slice of butter
- 5 tablespoons chopped onions
- 1 tablespoon curry
- 1½ liters boiling water
- salt
- rice flour for thickening
- water for thickening
- mashed potatoes
- rice for serving
- dry curry or browned onions or chutney for garnish

## Directions

1. Brown a good slice of butter in a pot.
2. Sauté the chopped onions and curry in the butter.
3. Add the raw chickens cut into 4 to 6 pieces.
4. Pour in 1½ liters of boiling water and add salt.
5. Cook the chickens until tender.
6. Thicken the sauce with rice flour mixed in water and allow to boil well.
7. Serve with both mashed potatoes and rice.
8. Place the mashed potatoes in a mound in the center of a platter, arrange the chicken in curry around it, and place rice in a ring around the outside.
9. Sprinkle with dry curry or garnish with browned onions or chutney.
