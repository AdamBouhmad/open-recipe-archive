## Ingredients

- Beef, chopped, 2 pounds
- Beef kidney fat (Nyretalg), 4.5 pounds
- Marrow, a little, scraped free of sinews
- Salt
- Fine flour (Flormel), 4 tablespoons
- Eggs, 2 to 3
- Onion, fairly abundant
- Sweet milk, a little
- Nutmeg, grated, a little
- Bread, crushed, for coating the mold
- Butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Scrape the beef and kidney fat, along with a little marrow, free of sinews and chop very finely with a little salt.
2. Then add 4 tablespoons of fine flour, 2 to 3 eggs, and fairly abundant onion, chopping it all together until it is quite fine and cohesive.
3. After this, stir it well with a little sweet milk and a little grated nutmeg.
4. Butter a pudding mold, sprinkle it with crushed bread, put the mixture in it, and cook for 2 hours.
