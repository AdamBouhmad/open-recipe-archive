## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 lb) green plums
- 250 gr (1/2 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- white rum

## Directions

1. Dry the plums and cut off the outer part of the stems before placing them in glass jars or crocks.
2. Sprinkle them with sugar and pour white rum over them.
3. Seal the jars with a bladder in which several holes are pricked with a needle, then wrap the jars with straw.
4. Place them over heat in a pot with cold water and let them boil for 1 quarter hour.
5. Leave them standing in the water until they are approximately cold.
