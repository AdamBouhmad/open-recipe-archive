## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- Pancake batter

## Directions

1. Clean the mackerel and cut from the bones
2. Cut into smaller pieces
3. Dip the pieces in pancake batter
4. Cook in clarified butter as explained in recipe 560
5. Arrange and serve with sauce as for battered plaice
