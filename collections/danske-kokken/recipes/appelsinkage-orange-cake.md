## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 5 egg yolks
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 3 1/2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- rind of 1 orange with all juice on a spoonful

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately.
2. Stir the yolks with 2 cups sugar and 1/2 cup butter.
3. Add 1/2 cup cold water and then 3 1/2 cups flour with 2 tablespoons baking powder and the beaten whites and grated orange rind with juice on a spoonful.
4. Bake in two square pans.
5. For the filling: beat 2 egg whites with 2 small cups sugar and the remaining orange juice.
6. When the cake is cold, spread the filling between the layers and over the top.
