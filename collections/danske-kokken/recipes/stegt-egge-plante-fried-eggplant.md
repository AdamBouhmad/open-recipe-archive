## Ingredients

- eggplant
- salt
- water
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Cut the eggplant into slices at least 1/4 inch thick
2. Clean each piece and place it in salt and water, laying a plate on top to keep them under the liquid
3. Let them stand an hour or more
4. Dry each slice, dip it in whisked eggs and breadcrumbs and fry in butter or fat until thoroughly fried and brown
