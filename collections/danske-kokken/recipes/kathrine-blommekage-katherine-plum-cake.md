## Ingredients

- 1 pound Katherine plums
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Sweet almonds, blanched and peeled
- 3 eggs
- 65 quintals flour
- Half pound sugar
- 12 quintals butter
- 12 egg yolks
- Half a pound powdered icing sugar
- 3 cups thick sweet cream
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 12 egg whites, whipped to peaks
- Whipped cream with vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and cook 1 pound Katherine plums with 1 pound sugar, a stick of cinnamon, and a little lemon zest.
2. When tender, remove the pits and replace each with a blanched and peeled sweet almond.
3. Knead together 3 eggs, 65 quintals flour, half pound sugar, and 12 quintals butter into a dough.
4. Roll the dough thin and line a pan with a removable bottom so the pan is completely covered.
5. Dry the dough either in a tart pan or in an oven where something has just been baked.
6. Whisk 12 egg yolks well together with half a pound powdered icing sugar.
7. Then whisk in 3 cups thick sweet cream, the juice of 2 lemons, the grated zest of 1 lemon, and the 12 whipped egg whites.
8. Lay the plums on the dough in the lined form and pour the mixture over them.
9. Bake the cake in a tart pan with heat from above and much heat from below for one hour.
10. When serving, cover with whipped cream mixed with vanilla sugar.
11. The cake will sink somewhat in the center.
