## Ingredients

- Roasted beef filet
- Gravy from the filet
- Sago flour
- Truffles
- Madeira wine
- Roasted or glazed chestnuts (see recipes 663-664)

## Directions

1. When the filet is roasted, pour off the gravy; strain it, skim it and thicken with sago flour stirred smooth
2. Cut truffles into slices and braise briefly in Madeira, then place in the gravy and bring to a boil
3. Reserve a few slices and place on top of the filet
4. Garnish with roasted or glazed chestnuts
