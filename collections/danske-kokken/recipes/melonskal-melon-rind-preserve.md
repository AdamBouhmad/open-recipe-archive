## Ingredients

- Melon rind, cut into oblong pieces
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Vinegar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- White peppercorns

## Directions

1. Peel the melon very thinly, and cut the rind into oblong pieces.
2. Cook sugar and vinegar to a syrup and skim before adding the melon rind to cook until tender.
3. Cook some whole cloves, a few pieces of cinnamon, and 1 teaspoon of whole white pepper together with the rind.
4. Cool and seal. Serve.
