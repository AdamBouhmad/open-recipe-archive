## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked meat or roast
- Onions, chopped
- Butter
- Flour
- Good stock or soup
- Paprika
- Salt
- Tomato puree
- Culinary color
- Potato puree

## Directions

1. Brown some chopped onions in butter
2. Brown flour in this mixture
3. Thin with good stock or soup
4. Season with paprika, salt, tomato puree and culinary color
5. Add the meat to the sauce and let it cook well
6. Arrange potato puree in a mound in the center of the dish
7. Place the goulash around this mound
