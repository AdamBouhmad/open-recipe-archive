## Ingredients

- Thin yellow zest of oranges
- Cognac or wine spirits
- 1 kg (2 S) ground icing sugar
- 1/4 l (3 pægles) water
- 25 gr (5 qvints) citric acid
- 1 1/4 l (1 pægl) hot water
- 1/4 l (1 pægl) of the orange essence

## Directions

1. Cover the thin yellow zest of oranges with cognac or wine spirits.
2. After 6 weeks, strain off the extract.
3. Boil 1 kg (2 S) ground icing sugar with 1/4 l (3 pægles) water to make a thin syrup.
4. When cool, blend the syrup with 25 gr (5 qvints) citric acid dissolved in 1 1/4 l (1 pægl) hot water, and with 1/4 l (1 pægl) of the orange essence.
5. Fill into bottles and cork. Can be used in soda water or ordinary cold drinking water.
6. The zest can be poured over with cognac or spirits again, but since the essence becomes less potent, a larger quantity of the sugar syrup must be added.
