## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onions
- Rice
- Meat broth or water
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Cooked smoked ham, finely diced
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. After the butter and onions are browned and the rice is cooked tender, stir in the finely diced cooked smoked ham.
2. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
