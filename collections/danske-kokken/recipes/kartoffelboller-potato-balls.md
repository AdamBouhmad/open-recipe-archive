## Ingredients

- 80 grams (16 dram) butter or margarine
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) grated, cooked, cold potatoes
- 25 grams (5 dram) potato flour
- 3 eggs
- Salt
- Sugar
- Zest of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine
2. Bake the grated cooked cold potatoes in the butter
3. Sprinkle with potato flour
4. Bake until the dough releases from the pot and spoon
5. Lift the pot from the heat and stir in 3 eggs one at a time
6. Add a little salt, sugar, and grated lemon zest
7. Cook in salted water
