## Ingredients

- Crab meat
- Fairly smooth, clarified hollandaise sauce
- Salt
- Tarragon vinegar or lemon juice
- Ground white pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- Melted butter
- Parsley or salad leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare a fairly smooth, clarified hollandaise sauce, seasoned with salt, tarragon vinegar or lemon juice and with ground white pepper.
2. Turn the meat in this sauce, fill it into the shells, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and brown the surface in the oven.
3. The breadcrumbs can be mixed with grated Parmesan cheese and drizzled with a little melted butter.
4. Serve directly from the oven on a platter, covered with a napkin and decorated with parsley or salad leaves.
