## Ingredients

- Fully developed but unripe gooseberries
- Water

## Directions

1. Pick the gooseberries when they are fully developed but not ripe. The green or white ones are the best
2. Remove the flower and stem
3. Pour water over them so that it reaches a certain level (recipe text cuts off)
