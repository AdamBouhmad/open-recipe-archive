## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons powdered sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks and powdered sugar together very well, always stirring the same direction, until the mixture becomes thick
2. Stir in enough flour to make a loose dough
3. Roll out and cut into long strips
4. Form small wreaths from the strips
5. Fry until golden in melted butter
6. When removed and drained
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving
