## Ingredients

- 1 kg blackcurrants
- 4.5 L water
- cloves
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- potato flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse blackcurrants and cook for half an hour with a few whole cloves, whole cinnamon and lemon peel
2. Strain the soup, sweeten, and when it boils again, thicken with potato flour mixed with water
