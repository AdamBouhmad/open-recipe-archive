## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- Same quantity of celery as chicken
- 3 or 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon cold butter
- 2/3 cup vinegar
- 5 tablespoons salad oil
- 2/3 cup cold cream

## Directions

1. Cut cooked chicken meat and celery into small pieces
2. Beat the whites and yolks of eggs separately
3. Whisk oil slowly into the yolks
4. Mix all ingredients (except cream) in a casserole and place over heat
5. Cook until fairly thick
6. When cool again, whisk the cream and pour over the salad just before serving
