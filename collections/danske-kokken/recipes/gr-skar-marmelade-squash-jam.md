## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg powdered sugar for 1 kg peeled squash
- 2 lemons
- 50 grams powdered ginger

## Directions

1. Cut the mature, hollowed, and peeled squash into small pieces and cook for 1/2 hour without any additions
2. Add the sugar, juice of 2 lemons, and powdered ginger
3. Cook the jam under constant watch for about 2 hours until ready
