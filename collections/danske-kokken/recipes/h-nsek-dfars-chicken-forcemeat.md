## Ingredients

- Chicken breast meat
- 125 g cold butter or margarine

## Directions

1. From chickens, which need not be young for this, take only the breast meat, which is chopped and pounded with 125 g cold butter or margarine per 1/2 kg meat.
2. Otherwise stir and handle it as for beef and veal forcemeat.
3. The remainder of the chickens can be used for soup, chicken salad and various fillings or fricassée.
