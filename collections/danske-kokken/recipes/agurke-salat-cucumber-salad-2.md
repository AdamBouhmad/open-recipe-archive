## Ingredients

- Thin cucumbers without seeds
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Powdered sugar
- White pepper
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them into thin slices
2. Sprinkle with a little salt and place under a light press for one hour
3. Press off the liquid from the cucumbers and pour vinegar over them
4. Add a little powdered sugar and sprinkle with white pepper and chopped parsley
5. Can be arranged in small hollowed-out tomatoes
