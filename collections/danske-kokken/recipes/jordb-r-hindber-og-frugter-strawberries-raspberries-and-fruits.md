## Ingredients

- Strawberries, raspberries, or apples, pears, and all kinds of fruit
- Water
- Sugar
- Corn-starch

## Directions

1. Boil them in very little water with sugar and a small amount of cornstarch thickening.
2. Peel apples and pears and cut them into pieces; remove the cores.
3. For cherries, add a few whole cloves and use no thickening.
