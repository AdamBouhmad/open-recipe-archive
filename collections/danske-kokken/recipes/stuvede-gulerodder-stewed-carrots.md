## Ingredients

- carrots
- butter
- sugar
- parsley
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots, cut them into slices and place them in boiling water
2. Cook until they are so tender that you can press them through a sieve with a spoon
3. Place them again over heat with a piece of butter and sugar and let them have a brief boiling
4. When arranged, sprinkle chopped parsley over
5. Some like a little vinegar with this dish
