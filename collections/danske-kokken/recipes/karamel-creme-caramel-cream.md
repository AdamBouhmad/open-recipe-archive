## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 Q) powdered sugar
- 3 tablespoons water
- 1 L (1 pot) skimmed milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 125 gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 10 sheets isinglass
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) cream for whipped topping
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Heat powdered sugar with water in a pot, stirring until it becomes dark caramel
2. Add skimmed milk and bring to a boil
3. Whisk egg yolks with powdered sugar and whisk into the boiling milk, then remove from heat immediately
4. Pour into a dish to cool and add isinglass, stirring until melted
5. Keep the cream moving gently while it begins to set to prevent a skin from forming
6. Pour into glass bowls and refrigerate until the next day
7. Decorate with whipped cream mixed with vanilla extract
