## Ingredients

- 3 pounds fine flour
- 1 egg
- ¼ pound washed butter
- ¾ pound finely ground powdered sugar
- ½ stick ground vanilla
- 1 pound sweet almonds, finely ground
- 3 quintal bitter almonds, finely ground
- flour for rolling

## Directions

1. Knead on the baking board 3 pounds fine flour with 1 egg
2. Add ¼ pound washed butter that has been patted dry in cloth
3. Add ¾ pound finely ground powdered sugar
4. Add ½ stick ground vanilla
5. Add 1 pound finely ground sweet almonds and 3 quintal finely ground bitter almonds
6. Knead all together well until it leaves your hands
7. Roll in flour on the board into small rings or form using a pastry bag
8. Bake slowly until nicely brown
9. From the stated amount you will get about 70 rings
