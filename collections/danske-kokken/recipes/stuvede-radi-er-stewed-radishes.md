## Ingredients

- Radishes
- Bouillon
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Chopped parsley
- Coarsely ground white pepper

## Directions

1. Remove stems and small roots
2. Wash the radishes and cook quickly in bouillon or strong meat stock with fresh butter and spices
3. Thicken the sauce with flour and parsley and bring to a boil, then add egg yolks
4. Serve, typically with cutlets
