## Ingredients

- Duck
- Olives
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Wheat flour
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Prepare and roast the duck as described in Nr. 163.
2. Remove the duck from the pan and add peeled olives to the sauce; it should not boil but only stand briefly over the heat.
3. Cut the duck into pieces and place on a platter with peeled olives distributed among them.
4. Skim the fat off the sauce and brown with flour until it becomes sufficiently thick; add some peeled olives and a glass of Madeira, and pour the sauce over the duck.
