## Ingredients

- Good quality veal liver
- Fresh lard
- Butter
- Milk
- Culinary coloring
- Salt
- Flour
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Choose a good pale veal liver with a skin that bursts easily when pressed with a finger.
2. Soak in water for half an hour to draw out the blood, and remove the skin and veins as much as possible.
3. Lard the liver with fresh lard, brown it in butter in a pot, and add milk with culinary coloring and salt.
4. Roast gently for 1.5 hours, basting constantly.
5. Make a roux with butter and flour, and thin with the strained pan drippings until the sauce has the right consistency.
6. Add a little cold butter just before serving and pour a spoonful of sauce over the liver before it is served.
7. Serve with boiled potatoes.
