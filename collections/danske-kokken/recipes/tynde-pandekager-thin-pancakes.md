## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) fine flour or Pillsbury flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) oil
- just over 1/2 L (just over 1/2 Pot) milk
- sugar
- salt
- cardamom
- egg whites

## Directions

1. In fine flour or Pillsbury flour, stir 4 egg yolks, 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) oil, just over 1/2 L (just over 1/2 Pot) milk, sugar, salt and cardamom to taste, lastly the beaten egg whites.
2. On the hot pan, add either a little butter, margarine or Palmin and a spoonful of the dough.
3. Turn the pan quickly so the dough can spread out into a thin cake, which is baked on both sides.
4. Jam or compote and powdered sugar are served with it.
