## Ingredients

- melon, fully grown but not ripe
- 4 gills vinegar
- 1 pound sugar (Melis)

## Directions

1. The melon must be fully grown but not ripe.
2. Peel it and cut it into 8 pieces.
3. Remove the interior.
4. Give the pieces a brief boil in water, remove and place on a clean linen cloth.
5. Meanwhile cook a sugar brine of 4 gills vinegar and 1 pound sugar.
6. Place the melon pieces in it to cook a little until the brine is as thick as syrup.
7. Place the melon pieces in a jar and pour the brine over them, as warm as it is.
8. Tie the jar.
