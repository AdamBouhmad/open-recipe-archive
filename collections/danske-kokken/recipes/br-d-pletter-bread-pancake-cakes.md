## Ingredients

- Some slices not too fresh French bread
- Milk
- Powdered sugar
- Powdered cinnamon
- Crushed bitter almonds
- Wheat flour
- 4 Eggs
- Sweet cream
- 0.5 Pint melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak some slices of not too fresh French bread in milk.
2. After taking them up, press them apart with a spoon.
3. Add powdered sugar, a little powdered cinnamon, some crushed bitter almonds, and a couple of tablespoonfuls wheat flour.
4. Beat 4 eggs with a little sweet cream and add to the rest.
5. Finally stir in 0.5 pint melted butter.
6. Bake somewhat thick cakes from this.
7. Put butter on for each new pan full.
8. Serve with powdered sugar and powdered cinnamon.
