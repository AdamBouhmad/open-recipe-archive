## Ingredients

- turbot
- coarse salt
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the turbot by making a cross-wise incision below the gill cover on the white side, and remove the entrails and gills through it
2. Rub it well with coarse salt, and brush the scraped skin with a stiff brush, while the white side is brushed and scraped until all slime is removed
3. Clip off the fins
4. Wash the fish again in several changes of cold water, then sprinkle it with salt and let it stand for half an hour
5. Tie a strong string around the backbone below the head and make an incision in the meat for the string
6. Place it over the heat in cold water, which is quickly brought to a boil; the water must contain a good handful of salt
7. When the water begins to boil, skim it, reduce the heat, and cook the fish over gentle heat for about an hour, until the meat easily loosens from the bones
8. Take it up and lay it so the water can run off, then arrange it on a platter with a napkin
9. Turn the white side upward
10. Serve with peeled boiled potatoes and sour butter sauce
