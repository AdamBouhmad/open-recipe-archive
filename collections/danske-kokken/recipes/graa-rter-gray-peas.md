## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) peas
- 2 bay leaves
- 100 gr (20 Kvint) butter
- 50 gr (10 Kvint) flour
- smoked pork or smoked pork head broth
- ground pepper
- coloring
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the peas the day before and cook them together with 2 bay leaves in the same water in which they have stood.
2. Brown 100 gr (20 Kvint) butter, and bake 50 gr (10 Kvint) flour in it, which is diluted with the smoked broth, from which the fat must be well skimmed.
3. Add the peas and bring to a boil.
4. Season the soup with ground pepper and coloring.
5. Add vinegar and sugar to taste.
6. The pork is eaten with the soup.
