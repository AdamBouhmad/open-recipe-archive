## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 2 pounds powdered sugar
- 2 pounds flour
- 4 ore deer antler salt
- a little grated lemon zest
- 8 egg whites, beaten
- cream for layering
- white sugar for glazing
- jam for decoration

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 pound butter and beat white.
2. Beat 8 egg yolks with 2 pounds powdered sugar and add to the butter, likewise 2 pounds flour which has first been mixed with 4 ore deer antler salt and a little grated lemon zest.
3. When well beaten together, carefully add the 8 beaten egg whites.
4. Bake the cakes in paper forms as layer cake, and between each layer place cream.
5. Glaze the top with white sugar and decorate with jam.
