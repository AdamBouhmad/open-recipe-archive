## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Bread
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs for 6 to 7 minutes, as they must not be completely hard-boiled
2. Cover with cold water, shell and cut into slices
3. Spread hot toasted bread with butter and place the egg slices on it
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
