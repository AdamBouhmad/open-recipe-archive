## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons mustard
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Rub the mustard and hard-boiled egg yolks together in the oil until combined.
2. Add vinegar, pepper, and a little salt.
3. Treat the salad in the same manner as above.
