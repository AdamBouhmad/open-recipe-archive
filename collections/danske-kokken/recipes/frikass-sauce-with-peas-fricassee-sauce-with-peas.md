## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Stock in which the meat was cooked
- Egg yolks
- Cooked, shelled peas
- Parsley
- Cooked, sliced carrots

## Directions

1. Make the sauce as described in the preceding recipes and add cooked, shelled peas
2. If desired, add finely chopped parsley and cooked, sliced carrots
