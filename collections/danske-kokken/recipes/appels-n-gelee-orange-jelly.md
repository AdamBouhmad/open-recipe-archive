## Ingredients

- 1 ounce gelatine
- 1/2 pint cold water
- juice of 3 lemons
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 pint orange juice
- 3 oranges

## Directions

1. Let 1 ounce of gelatine stand for one hour in 1/2 pint of cold water.
2. Then add the juice of 3 lemons, 2 pounds of sugar, and 1 quart of boiling water.
3. When everything is dissolved, add 1 pint of orange juice.
4. Strain carefully and set on a cold place, preferably on ice, until it is to be used.
