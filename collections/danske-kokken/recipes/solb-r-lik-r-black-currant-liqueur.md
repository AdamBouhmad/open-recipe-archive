## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) black currants
- 1 l (1 pot) white rum
- Ground icing sugar to taste, approximately 1/4 kg (1/2 S) per 1 l (1 pot) rum

## Directions

1. Place 1 kg (2 S) black currants in a jar and pour 1 l (1 pot) white rum over them.
2. Set the jar in the warmth for 4 to 5 weeks.
3. Strain the rum through a clear piece of cloth and sweeten with ground icing sugar to taste, approximately 1/4 kg (1/2 S) per 1 l (1 pot) rum.
4. Pour the black currant rum into dry bottles, cork them, and seal with gypsum.
