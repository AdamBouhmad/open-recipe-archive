## Ingredients

- ¼ kilo (½ pound) rice
- 1 liter (1 pot) half-skimmed milk
- 75 grams (15 quintals) margarine
- vanilla essence
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- the stiffly beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the rice tender in half-skimmed milk and stir in the margarine and vanilla essence.
2. Stir the egg yolks with powdered sugar and add the cooled pudding along with the beaten egg whites.
3. Prepare the pudding mold as for Caramel Pudding, no. 1037, and cook the pudding for about an hour until it has set, or place in a warm oven.
4. Serve cold the next day and eat with caramel syrup.
5. The caramel can be mixed with a little rum or sherry and a little whipped cream.
