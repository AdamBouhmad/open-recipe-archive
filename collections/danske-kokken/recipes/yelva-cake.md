## Ingredients

- 250 grams margarine
- 250 grams fine sugar
- 3 eggs
- a little cardamom
- a few drops bitter almond oil
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 750 grams Pillsbury flour
- 250 milliliters semi-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix margarine, fine sugar, eggs, cardamom, bitter almond oil, and baking powder well
2. Add Pillsbury flour and semi-skimmed milk
3. Bake in a greased form for 1 hour 15 minutes
