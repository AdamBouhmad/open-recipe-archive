## Ingredients

- 3 pounds butter
- 3½ pounds powdered sugar
- 2 pounds wheat flour
- 8 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Stir butter with powdered sugar
2. Add wheat flour
3. Finally fold in 8 whipped egg whites
4. Bake the small cakes light brown
