## Ingredients

- cooked macaroni
- carrots
- celery or salsify roots
- pickled gherkins
- cooked green peas
- pickled onions
- lost mayonnaise
- mustard
- watercress

## Directions

1. Cut cooked macaroni, carrots, celery or salsify roots and pickled gherkins small, and mix in cooked green peas and small sliced pickled onions
2. Mix all these ingredients in lost mayonnaise made with mustard
3. Sprinkle the salad with watercress
