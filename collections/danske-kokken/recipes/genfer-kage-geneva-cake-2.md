## Ingredients

- 122 grams (1/4 pound) butter
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) sugar
- 4 eggs
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) flour
- Cream (see recipe 1120)
- Vanilla
- Water glaze
- Roasted, chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Cream together the butter and sugar until fluffy
2. Add the eggs and flour to the mixture
3. Divide the batter into 2 portions and bake in a greased layer cake form
4. Once baked, layer them together with cream flavored with vanilla
5. Brush the cake with water glaze and sprinkle with roasted, chopped almonds that have been dried in the oven
