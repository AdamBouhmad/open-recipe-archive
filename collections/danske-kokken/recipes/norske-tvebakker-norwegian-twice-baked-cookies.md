## Ingredients

- 1 pint cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- half a pound flour
- cardamom
- rind of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Whip 1 pint cream to foam.
2. When 4 egg yolks are stirred white, stir 3 tablespoons white sugar into them, together with half a pound flour.
3. Finally add the whipped egg whites, as well as a little cardamom and grated rind of half a lemon.
4. Bake best in paper forms in a good warm oven.
