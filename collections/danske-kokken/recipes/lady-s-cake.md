## Ingredients

- 500 grams fine sugar
- 12 whole eggs
- 500 grams margarine
- 65 grams finely chopped or ground sweet almonds
- 35 grams finely chopped or ground bitter almonds
- 500 grams Pillsbury flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix fine sugar, eggs, margarine, and almonds for at least 15 minutes
2. Stir in Pillsbury flour and baking powder
3. Pour batter into a greased form and bake at good heat for about 1 hour
