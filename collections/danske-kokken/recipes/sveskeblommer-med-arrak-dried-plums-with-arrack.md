## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) dried plums, pitted
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) Demerara sugar
- 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water
- arrack

## Directions

1. Place the dried pitted plums over heat with the specified amount of water.
2. Remove them as soon as they begin to boil and place in a sieve.
3. Once the water has drained well, place the plums in a crock.
4. Put the sugar in a pot together with the water from the plums and boil until it becomes a syrup that hangs in threads from the spoon.
5. Pour the boiling syrup over the plums and let them sit until the next day.
6. The next day, pour everything into the pot and remove the plums as soon as they boil.
7. Reduce the syrup before pouring it over the fruit.
8. The next day, pour enough good arrack over the plums to completely cover them and seal the crocks.
