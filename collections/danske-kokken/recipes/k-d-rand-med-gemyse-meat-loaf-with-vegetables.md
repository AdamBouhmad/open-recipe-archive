## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) ground meat for the loaf
- Butter
- Vegetables, either stewed or sautéed in butter
- Mushrooms, sautéed in butter
- Spicy brown gravy sauce

## Directions

1. Butter a loaf mold well and fill it with the ground meat
2. Place the mold upside down in a pot of boiling water, ensuring the water does not come over the top, or place it in the oven in a roasting pan with water
3. Cook for 20 to 25 minutes
4. Arrange vegetables in the center of the turned-out loaf, or fill with sautéed mushrooms
5. Serve with spicy brown gravy sauce
