## Ingredients

- 1 pound washed butter
- 1 pound wheat flour
- 1 pound finely ground white or powdered sugar
- 1 pound sweet almonds finely ground
- a few bitter almonds
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Knead together the butter, flour, sugar, sweet almonds, bitter almonds, and lemon zest well on a board
2. Roll out with flour into thin sheets
3. Cut to the size of a plate or whatever size suits the pan where the cake will be served
4. Bake the sheets slowly, one at a time, until light brown
5. Trim the edges while still warm
6. When cold, layer them with different jams, apple pulp, or very thick cream between each layer
7. Do not bake the assembled cake, but it can be covered with icing sugar and placed in a lukewarm oven to dry
8. It is more palatable to cover the whole cake with whipped cream just before serving
9. Serves approximately 20 persons
