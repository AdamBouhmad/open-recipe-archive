## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 175 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 kg margarine
- 1 level teaspoonful ground cardamom
- 4 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- just under 1/4 L semi-skimmed milk
- 1 kg flour

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs well with the powdered sugar, margarine, ground cardamom, baking powder and salt.
2. Finally add the semi-skimmed milk and flour and knead into a dough.
3. Roll a piece of the dough into a long sausage shape and cut off pieces the size of a small walnut.
4. Roll into small, smooth balls, and if necessary dust lightly with flour, place on the sheet and bake pale brown in a well-heated oven.
5. When the balls have cooled, cut them through with a sharp knife and place again on the sheet with the soft, cut side upwards.
6. Put in a not-too-hot oven to dry out, brown and become crisp.
7. Yields about 1 1/4 kg rusks.
