## Ingredients

- 2 pounds wheat flour
- 1/2 pound crushed powdered sugar or light farina
- 1 pound butter
- 4 eggs
- a little crushed cinnamon
- cloves
- grated lemon zest
- 1 egg for brushing
- 4 pounds finely cut almonds
- candied citrus peel

## Directions

1. Work 2 pounds wheat flour, 1/2 pound crushed powdered sugar or light farina, 1 pound butter, 4 eggs, a little crushed cinnamon, cloves and grated lemon zest together well.
2. Roll out the dough on a baking board.
3. Brush the dough with 1 egg and sprinkle with 4 pounds finely cut almonds.
4. Cut candied citrus peel into thin slices and press them here and there on the surface of the dough.
5. Bake until beautifully light brown.
