## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Water
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Asparagus is usually picked from the middle of May to the end of June.
2. The largest are cooked as green asparagus.
3. Peel the asparagus from the middle up, without damaging the tips, but so that only the outermost tough skin is removed.
4. Rinse them, tie them in bundles with about twenty pieces in each, so that the tips point to the same side, and place them in boiling salted water.
5. Allow half an hour for cooking.
6. Arrange the asparagus on a platter with a folded napkin.
7. They can either be placed on a grill or lifted with a spoon, and the water is removed.
8. Melted butter is served with them.
