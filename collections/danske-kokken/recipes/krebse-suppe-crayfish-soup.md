## Ingredients

- 60 crayfish
- soup stock
- asparagus
- small fish balls
- wine
- parsley
- ground white pepper
- Butter dough slices or Simels

## Directions

1. Follow the same procedure as for Lobster Soup (Nr. 376)
2. When the crayfish are cooked and cold, pick out the meat from the tails and remove the black vein
3. Place the crayfish tails in the soup together with asparagus and fish balls
4. Season with wine, chopped parsley and ground white pepper
5. Serve as Lobster Soup with Butter dough slices or Simels
