## Ingredients

- Eel
- Butter
- Spinach, stewed
- Peas
- Carrots
- Cauliflower
- Potato puree
- Scrambled eggs

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the eel before cutting into smaller pieces
2. Fry the pieces in butter in a frying pan
3. Serve with the browned butter in which they were fried
4. Serve with vegetables such as: stewed spinach, peas, carrots, cauliflower, potato puree, or scrambled eggs
