## Ingredients

- 1 small quart milk
- 1 cent compressed yeast
- wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Warm 1 small quart milk and pour into the dough pan. Dissolve 1 cent compressed yeast in a little lukewarm water and add to the milk.
2. Sift in as much flour while stirring to make a thick, even dough that cannot be kneaded, only stirred.
3. Let it rise for the usual time during the day or overnight.
4. When risen, add 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon salt, and 2 tablespoons butter.
5. Sift in enough flour to make a good, soft dough that can be handled.
6. Knead well and place in the pan, allowing it to rise again.
7. When sufficiently risen, divide into as many loaves as desired—2 large or 3 small—let stand for a quarter hour in a warm place, then bake as usual.
