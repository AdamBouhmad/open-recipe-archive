## Ingredients

- Cooked veal breast with bones removed
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs, crushed
- Butter or claret for browning

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the cooked veal meat.
2. Brush the meat with egg, sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs, and brown in a pan with butter or claret.
3. Serve with browned butter, compote, and cucumber salad.
4. Vegetables can be served as puree, stewed, or simply boiled au naturel.
