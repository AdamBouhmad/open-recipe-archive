## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- Butter
- Small glazed onions
- Cooked macaroni
- Sharp brown sauce
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Turtle sauce

## Directions

1. Butter a loaf mold and fill it about halfway with ground meat
2. Press small glazed onions into the meat
3. Cook the loaf, turn it out, and arrange it
4. Fill with cooked macaroni and serve with sharp brown sauce
5. Alternatively, arrange hard-boiled eggs the same way and serve with turtle sauce
6. Chestnuts may be used instead of macaroni
