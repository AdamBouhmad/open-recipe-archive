## Ingredients

- Six egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons strong broth or fish broth
- 1 snapseglas Béarnaise essence
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) cold butter

## Directions

1. Stir together six egg yolks with 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley and 2 tablespoons strong broth, or fish broth if the sauce is to be served with a fish dish
2. Finally stir in 1 snapseglas Béarnaise essence (see Nr. 734) and 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) cold butter
3. Place the sauce in a water bath like Sauce-hollandaise, and add the butter in small portions in the same manner
