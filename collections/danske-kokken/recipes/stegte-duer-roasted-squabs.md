## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Salt
- Butter
- Soup or water
- Coloring
- Heart, liver, and gizzard
- Salad or parsley
- Compote and salad for serving
- Butter
- Flour
- Strained gravy
- Raw cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the squabs, clean, and dress like other poultry
2. Lard the breast with 2 rows of lardoons
3. Brown the squabs in butter in a pot
4. Sprinkle with salt and pour in a little soup or water and coloring
5. Cook the heart, liver, and gizzard with the squabs
6. Roast for a good half hour, basting well during cooking
7. Split and arrange garnished with salad or parsley
8. Serve with compote and salad
9. For the sauce, brown butter and flour, dilute with the strained gravy, and add a little raw cream
