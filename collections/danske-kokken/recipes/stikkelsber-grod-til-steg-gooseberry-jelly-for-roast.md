## Ingredients

- unripe Gooseberries
- a little Water
- washed Butter
- whole Cinnamon
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Take unripe berries, but do not scald them first.
2. When the stems and seed-cases are removed, place them on the fire with very little water and a piece of washed butter the size of a walnut for 1 quart berries.
3. They should only be cooked more, but not broken.
4. A few pieces of whole cinnamon are cooked with the berries, and the sugar is added when the jelly is cooked, but it should not be very sweet.
