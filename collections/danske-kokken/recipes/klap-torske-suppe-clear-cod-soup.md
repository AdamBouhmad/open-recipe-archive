## Ingredients

- 2 L (2 pots) fish broth
- 2 L (2 pots) clear meat broth
- 4 to 5 carrots
- 1 celery
- 4 leeks
- a good bunch
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Mix the broths together and cook with 4 to 5 carrots, 1 celery, 4 leeks and a good bunch.
2. When the vegetables are tender, remove them, cut them up and add them to the tureen, over which the broth is poured.
3. Finely chopped parsley can be added to this.
