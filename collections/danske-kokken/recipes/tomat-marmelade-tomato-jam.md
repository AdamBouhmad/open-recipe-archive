## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Powdered sugar (1/4 kg to 1/2 kg tomatoes)

## Directions

1. Wipe the tomatoes dry, cut them in 4 parts and cook over low heat.
2. When they are completely cooked down, press the pulp through a coarse sieve and pour it back into the pot to be cooked together with the sugar until the jam is as stiff as desired.
3. Fill it into jars, cover with paper and tie up.
