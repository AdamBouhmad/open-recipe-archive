## Ingredients

- Sea cabbage stalks
- Water
- Salt
- Sauce for braised asparagus (recipe 587-589)

## Directions

1. Cut the stalks into smaller pieces.
2. Boil them in water with salt.
3. Prepare sauce as for braised asparagus (recipe 587-589).
4. Turn the cabbage in the sauce.
