## Ingredients

- White cabbage
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Milk
- Cream

## Directions

1. Prepare like cauliflower, cut into 4 parts for cooking, and when they are to be stewed, cut them again into smaller pieces and keep warm while the sauce is made. A good piece of butter is stirred with a little nutmeg over heat and served in a sauce bowl or poured over the cabbage when it is arranged. You can also stir flour into melted butter, approximately 1 ounce flour in 2 ounces butter, spread it with bouillon and give the cabbage a brief cooking in this together with salt and nutmeg. You can stew the cabbage in milk, then melt a little butter, in which flour is stirred out, cream or milk, and it is cooked evenly. The cabbage then gets a brief cooking in this with a piece of sugar and finally salt. In everything where there is milk, salt is added last and only after it is taken off the heat.
