## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 2 pounds butter
- 1 spoonful yeast
- cream as the dough will absorb

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, butter, and yeast with as much cream as the dough will take. Knead the dough together the evening before the kringler are to be baked and let it stand overnight in a covered dish to rise. The next day, roll out the dough into small kringler and bake them light brown.
