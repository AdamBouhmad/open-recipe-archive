## Ingredients

- Apples
- Lemon juice
- Pie dough
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. The apples for this should preferably be stewed like cooked apples.
2. Lemon juice is dripped between them; sometimes they are also merely cut into slices, but the first is to be preferred, because the pie in the latter case is apt to become too hard.
3. When the plate is lined with dough, spread a thick layer of apples over, and a lid of dough is spread over again.
4. Holes are pricked here and there in the lid so the steam can escape.
5. Just before it is done, take it out a moment, sprinkle with powdered sugar, and set in for 5 minutes.
