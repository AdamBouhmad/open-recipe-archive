## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet milk
- vanilla
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon salep
- egg whites
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pot of sweet milk with a little vanilla, which is soon removed to avoid giving too strong a taste.
2. Whip 2 egg yolks and stir them in.
3. Dissolve 1 tablespoon of salep and whip it into the milk.
4. When the milk is almost cold, whip the egg whites together with 1 tablespoon of powdered sugar into foam and whip this into the milk.
