## Ingredients

- 28 kvint flour
- 28 kvint butter
- 12 kvint powdered sugar
- 3 pægle sweet milk
- 12 egg yolks
- a little ground nutmeg
- egg whites
- 15 large asparagus
- salt
- water
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bake together the flour, butter, powdered sugar and sweet milk.
2. Stir in 12 egg yolks, two at a time, a little ground nutmeg, and finally the beaten egg whites.
3. Cook 15 large asparagus until tender in salted water and place on a colander to drain.
4. Cut each asparagus into 3-5 pieces.
5. Layer the asparagus pieces with the batter in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold.
6. Cook the pudding for 2 hours and serve warm with melted butter.
