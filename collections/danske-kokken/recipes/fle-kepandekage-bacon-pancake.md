## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Cream
- Butter
- Bacon
- Water
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Use, for example, 8 eggs, and to each egg take a spoonful of cream.
2. Beat the eggs first and then together with the cream.
3. Brown a little butter in a frying pan, then pour a large spoonful of the batter onto it and lay slices of bacon (which must first be soaked in water) on it.
4. Pour the batter gently over so the bacon is covered and fry the cake over gentle heat.
5. Sometimes you must insert a little butter at the edge, as you must not have too much underneath at first.
6. When it begins to stiffen on top, it is brown underneath and must be turned; it may also happen that it browns while it is still soft on top, in which case the heat is too high.
7. Slide it from the pan, preferably onto a lid with a handle; place the pan on top of the cake, on which a little butter has first been placed, and hold the lid firmly to the pan as you turn it around.
8. You can sprinkle chopped parsley on top.
