## Ingredients

- 2 pounds wheat flour
- 4 pounds butter
- 6 eggs
- 2 measures milk
- a little crushed cardamom
- grated lemon zest
- 2 tablespoons yeast
- 1 small cup crushed powdered sugar
- 3/4 pound cleaned raisins
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Work 2 pounds wheat flour, 4 pounds butter, 6 eggs, 2 measures milk, a little crushed cardamom, grated lemon zest, 2 tablespoons yeast and 1 small cup crushed powdered sugar together cold with the hand until it comes together.
2. Place the dough in a strong, firm cloth strewn with flour and submerge it in a pail with cold water.
3. Let it stand from evening to the next morning.
4. Knead 3/4 pound cleaned raisins into the dough.
5. Form the dough into a large ring cake, brush it with melted butter and sprinkle with sugar before baking.
