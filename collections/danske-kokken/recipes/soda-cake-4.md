## Ingredients

- 250 grams cold margarine
- 500 grams Pillsbury flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 250 grams fine sugar
- 375 milliliters semi-skimmed boiling milk
- 3 whole eggs, well whipped
- 65 grams chopped preserved orange peel or lemon drops
- 125 grams currants

## Directions

1. Rub cold margarine completely into Pillsbury flour together with baking soda and fine sugar
2. Add boiling semi-skimmed milk and whipped eggs
3. Add chopped preserved orange peel or lemon drops and currants
4. Grease form and bake for 1 hour
