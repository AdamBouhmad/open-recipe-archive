## Ingredients

- Parsnips, scraped and cooked
- Milk
- Broth or the water in which they were cooked
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the scraped, cooked roots and stew them either in a milk sauce, in broth, or in the water in which they were cooked.
2. Thicken the sauce with a couple of egg yolks.
3. In the milk sauce, stir in a piece of butter rolled in finely chopped parsley.
