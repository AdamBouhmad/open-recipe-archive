## Ingredients

- Small Spanish onions or red onions
- Butter
- Sugar
- Stock, gravy, or water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over small Spanish onions or red onions to make them easier to peel. Brown butter and sugar in a pot, and add the onions, which must be shaken regularly so they do not burn. When the sugar has adhered to the onions, add a little stock, gravy or water, and boil them tender with regular shaking.
