## Ingredients

- good pie dough
- approximately 10 good large apples, peeled and sliced
- 1 pound raisins, chopped
- 2 to 3 cups sugar
- cinnamon
- small pieces of butter
- fine sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Bake preferably in a two-quart mold with roughly the same circumference at top and bottom
2. First make good pie dough
3. Place a layer of this in the bottom but not on the sides
4. Spread a layer of chopped and peeled apples two inches thick
5. Then spread a layer of chopped raisins
6. Sprinkle sugar, cinnamon and small pieces of butter over this
7. Then lay another layer of dough, then fruit and so forth until the mold is full
8. The top layer must be dough
9. Bake evenly for a couple of hours
10. When done, turn the bottom upward and before serving, sprinkle fine sugar over it
11. It is equally good when reheated
