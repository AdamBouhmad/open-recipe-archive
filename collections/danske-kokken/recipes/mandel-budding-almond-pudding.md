## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 150 Gr (30 Kvint) icing sugar
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) finely ground sweet almonds
- 25 Gr (5 Kvint) bitter almonds
- 8 leaves isinglass
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water
- 1 L (1 Pot) cream
- cold fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Stir 5 egg yolks with 150 Gr (30 Kvint) icing sugar
2. Add 125 Gr (1/4 S) finely ground sweet almonds and 25 Gr (5 Kvint) bitter almonds
3. Dissolve 8 leaves of isinglass in 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water and stir into the egg mixture along with the foam from 1 L (1 Pot) cream
4. Make the pudding a few hours before serving in a glass bowl
5. Serve with cold fruit sauce
