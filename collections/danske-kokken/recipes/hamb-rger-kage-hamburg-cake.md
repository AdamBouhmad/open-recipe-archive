## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 pound finely ground powdered sugar
- ground cardamom
- ginger, ground
- lemon zest, grated
- potash (baking soda), 1 nut-sized piece or the value of 1 ore
- 1 pound wheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat 5 eggs in a bowl
2. Stir in 1 pound finely ground powdered sugar along with ground cardamom, ground ginger, grated lemon zest, and potash
3. Work in as much of the wheat flour as the mixture will take
4. Place the remaining flour on the work board, put the mixture on top, and work together with hands until the dough releases and shows eyes
5. Roll out the dough with a rolling pin, slightly thicker than for kleiner
6. Cut into small square cakes the size of a playing card
7. Place a blanched almond in the middle of each
8. Flour the baking pan before placing the cakes on it
9. Bake at good oven heat
10. If the stated amount of flour is not sufficient, more can be added
