## Ingredients

- 1 pound macaroni
- Salt
- Water
- 6 large onions
- 6 carrots
- Plenty of butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 5 pots good broth

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces and place with a little salt in plenty of boiling water
2. Cook just a moment, and before they are half tender, drain in a sieve
3. Cool by pouring cold water over them
4. Cut the macaroni into smaller pieces about a couple of inches long
5. Clean, peel, and cut 6 large onions and 6 carrots into slices
6. Brown them in plenty of butter
7. When well browned, add 3 tablespoons flour and stir well until everything is properly browned
8. Thin with 5 pots good broth
