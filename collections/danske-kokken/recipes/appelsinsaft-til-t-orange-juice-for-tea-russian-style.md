## Ingredients

- Orange peel
- Powdered sugar (1/4 kg or 1/2 lb powdered sugar or farina for juice of 3 oranges)

## Directions

1. Rub the orange peel on a piece of sugar
2. Press out the juice, strain it through a fine sieve into the saucepan and boil it with the sugar for at most 5 minutes
3. Skim it and reheat it the next day for another boiling
4. Pour the juice into bottles and seal them while still warm
