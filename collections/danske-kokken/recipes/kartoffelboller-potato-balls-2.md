## Ingredients

- 1 pound boiled potatoes
- 3 ounces flour
- 2 whole eggs
- Sugar
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the potatoes and stir in the flour and the rest.
2. Mix well together and place on the soup.
