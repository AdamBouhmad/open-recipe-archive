## Ingredients

- Peas
- Butter
- Water
- Sugar
- Salt
- Flour
- Parsley

## Directions

1. If the peas are cooked by steam, then when they are tender, place them on the dish, where they are stirred with a good piece of butter and a little salt. If you do not use steam, you can cook them with sugar and very little water until they are tender; then stir a good piece of butter into them, and arrange them immediately. You can also cook them with a little more water and add a roux of plenty of butter and a little flour together with chopped parsley, salt and a little sugar.
