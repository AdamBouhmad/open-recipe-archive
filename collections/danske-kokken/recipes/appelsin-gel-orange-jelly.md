## Ingredients

- Half the peel of the oranges to be used
- Juice of oranges
- Corresponding weight of sugar to the juice
- 1 1/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Use only half of the peels from the oranges to be used in the jelly.
2. Rub the outer thin yellow peel on some sugar and place together with the pressed juice in the jelly cloth.
3. Weigh the juice after it has run through the cloth.
4. Cook the corresponding weight of sugar with water according to instructions in No. 93, and add the juice when the sugar has been cooked dry.
5. Cook and skim for 10 minutes, pour into jelly cups moistened with cognac, and tie up when the jelly has set.
6. The remaining peels may be preserved as No. 29.
