## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Cottin mixture
- Chicken broth

## Directions

1. Chickens can be prepared like hen with a layer of cottin; or you can divide them so that the breast is cut out completely by itself, and when it is cooked, it is coated with cottin and roasted on plates in the oven until the cottin is roasted
2. The rest of the chicken is then used only for broth for the sauce
3. However, this is very wasteful, and one should at least take the legs with it, placing them with the thick end against the narrow end, and with cottin over
4. Chickens can otherwise be used for fricassee, curry and with white sauce like hen
