## Ingredients

- game birds
- soup or gravy for basting
- isinglass
- fresh cabbage leaves
- herb salad
- apple compote or apple puree

## Directions

1. During roasting, baste the birds with soup or gravy
2. From game birds, generally only the breast is used, which is sliced and placed in the mold
3. Prepare the aspic with isinglass
4. Cut fresh cabbage leaves in the form of shells and fill them with herb salad
5. Arrange the shells around the edge of the dish
6. Apple compote or apple puree can be served with the game
7. Use the remaining meat for game salad
