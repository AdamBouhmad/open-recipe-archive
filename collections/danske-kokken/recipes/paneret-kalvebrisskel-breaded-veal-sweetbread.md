## Ingredients

- 2 veal sweetbreads
- 2 to 3 ounces butter
- 2 eggs
- Grated breadcrumbs
- 1/2 pint cream
- Toasted breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in water 1 to 2 hours, then in boiling water for a few minutes.
2. Remove, cool, and cut into 1-inch thick slices.
3. Dip in egg and grated breadcrumbs.
4. Place on a tray, lightly butter, place in a warm oven for a quarter hour.
5. Arrange on slices of toasted breadcrumbs with cream around it.
