## Ingredients

- 125 Gr (1/4 S) macaroni
- Milk or water
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream
- Salt
- Sugar
- Vinegar
- Grated horseradish
- Watercress for garnish
- Cooked celery (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni tender in milk or water
2. Drain the cooked macaroni through a colander, rinse with cold water, and cut into 2-3 centimeter (1 inch) long pieces
3. Whip 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream to stiff foam
4. Mix salt, sugar, vinegar, and grated horseradish into the foam
5. Turn the macaroni into it
6. Garnish the salad with watercress and prepare it just before serving
7. Cooked, finely cut celery can be used instead of macaroni
