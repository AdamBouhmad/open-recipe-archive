## Ingredients

- 2 L (2 pots) fish broth
- 2 L (2 pots) good white meat broth
- 1 good bunch
- some carrots
- 1 celery
- 125 gr (1/4 S) butter
- 125 gr (1/4 S) flour
- 1/2 kg (1 S) fresh or preserved mushrooms
- 5 egg yolks
- ground white pepper
- chopped parsley
- Graves or sherry
- lemon juice
- small fish balls

## Directions

1. Mix the fish broth and meat broth together, and cook 1 good bunch, some carrots and 1 celery in it.
2. Melt 125 gr (1/4 S) butter, and bake 125 gr (1/4 S) flour in it, which is thinned with the strained broth.
3. Add 1/2 kg (1 S) fresh or preserved mushrooms to the soup and bring to a boil, after which it is thickened with 5 egg yolks and seasoned with ground white pepper, chopped parsley, Graves or sherry and lemon juice.
4. Small fish balls are added to the soup.
