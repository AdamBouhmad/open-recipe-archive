## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 2 pounds flour
- a little white sugar
- 4 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip 1 quart cream to foam
2. Constantly remove the foam from the top and there remains a little thin part, in which stir 2 pounds flour
3. Mix in the foam with a little white sugar and 4 beaten egg whites
4. Grease the iron from time to time and bake the cakes light brown
5. They are equally good whether the cream is sweet or sour, or take half of each kind
6. The egg whites can be omitted
7. This makes approximately 18 waffles
