## Ingredients

- 4 whole eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 6 ounces washed butter
- half a pint milk
- 1 cent yeast
- 1 pound fine, sifted flour

## Directions

1. Whip the 4 whole eggs well with 3 tablespoons sugar.
2. Melt the 6 ounces butter and add half a pint milk in which 1 cent yeast is dissolved and 1 pound fine, sifted flour, which is all stirred together with the whipped eggs.
3. Knead it properly and set it aside to rise.
4. Then knead the dough a little, but without more flour, and cut it into small pieces, which are rolled into round buns and placed on a baking sheet, covered with a thin cloth and set aside again in the heat.
5. When they have risen, salt them in the oven and bake for half a dozen minutes, then remove the sheet, and when the buns are cold, cut them across with a thin, sharp knife, place them with the cut side up and put them in a warm, not hot oven, until the upper side is light brown.
