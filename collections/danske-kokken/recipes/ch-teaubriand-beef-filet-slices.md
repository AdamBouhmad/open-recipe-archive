## Ingredients

- Beef filet slices, 3 centimeters thick
- Salt
- Pepper
- Brown butter
- Gravy
- Stirred butter or piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Châteaubriand consists of simple slices of beef filet 3 centimeters thick.
2. Pound lightly on both sides, sprinkle with salt and pepper and fry like beef.
3. Can be served as beef with stirred butter or with another piquant sauce, after which it is named accordingly.
4. Can be varied with the different additions mentioned in beef filet and pork tenderloin prepared as beef.
