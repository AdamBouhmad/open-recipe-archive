## Ingredients

- 1½ Cups Sugar
- 1½ Cups Butter
- 1 Egg
- 1 Cup Milk
- 1⅔ Cups Flour
- 1⅔ Teaspoons Baking Powder
- Chocolate

## Directions

1. Mix together the sugar and butter
2. Add the egg
3. Add the milk
4. Mix in the flour and baking powder
5. Bake mostly in thin cakes
6. Layer with chocolate between and on top
