## Ingredients

- Raisins (parboiled)
- blanched Almonds
- Apples (in small pieces)
- Batter for liquid Æbleskiver

## Directions

1. On good thread thread alternately Raisins (which are first parboiled slightly), blanched Almonds, and small pieces of Apple
2. When about 1 Yard is threaded full and tight, but so that there is a piece of thread outside at each end for holding, dip it in Batter prepared according to the recipe for liquid Æbleskiver
3. Lay in boiling clarified fat to fry until light brown
4. Pull the thread out before the cake is cold
