## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- 3 pounds butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 1 gill sand sugar (if the butter is not salted, 1/2 ounce salt)

## Directions

1. Place the flour on the table.
2. Mix the butter into it in small pieces; make a depression in the center, into which pour the water, 2 egg yolks, 1 egg white, the cognac (and the salt, if necessary).
3. Mix everything well together, fold in thirds and roll out, which is repeated 4 times.
