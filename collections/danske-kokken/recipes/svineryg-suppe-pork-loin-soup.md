## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (8 lb) pork loin
- 1 medium head of cabbage
- 1 celery
- 4 carrots
- 20 peppercorns
- 1 bunch of herbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the pork loin and place in a pot of cold salted water over heat
2. Once it comes to a boil and is well skimmed, add 1 large head of cabbage cut into 4 pieces, 1 celery, 4 carrots, 20 peppercorns, and a good bunch of herbs
3. Simmer for approximately 3 hours
4. When the cabbage is tender, remove it and cut away from the core into smaller pieces
5. Clean and cut the carrots into pieces and place in the tureen
6. Strain the soup and skim off the accumulated fat
7. Cut the pork loin into smaller pieces and serve hot with the soup
8. Leftover pork loin can be prepared with a good curry sauce (recipe No. 780)
