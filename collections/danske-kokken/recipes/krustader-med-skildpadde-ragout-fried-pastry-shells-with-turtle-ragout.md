## Ingredients

- 1 split calf head with tongue, brain, and eyes removed
- Water
- Salt
- Margarine or fat (soup fat)
- 1 slice smoked bacon or bacon fat
- 4 onions
- 4 carrots

## Directions

1. Soak the split calf head in cold water for a few hours to draw out the blood
2. Clean and blanch it and cook the day before
3. The tongue can either be used for the turtle ragout or salted and used as a cold cut
4. Place on heat in cold water with a little salt, skim well and simmer until it loosens from the bones
5. Remove the meat and place on a flat plate with a light press on top
6. When cold, cut into cubes of about 2 1/2 centimeters
7. Brown margarine or fat (soup fat) and a slice of smoked bacon or just bacon fat
8. Add 4 onions and 4 carrots
