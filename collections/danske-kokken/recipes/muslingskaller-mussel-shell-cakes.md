## Ingredients

- 325 grams flour
- 190 grams margarine
- 190 grams powdered sugar
- 75 grams crushed almonds
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix flour, margarine, powdered sugar, crushed almonds, and egg into a dough
2. Roll out the dough very thinly
3. Line small mussel shell molds (baking forms) with the dough
4. Bake the cakes light brown in the oven
5. Serve as cornucopias
