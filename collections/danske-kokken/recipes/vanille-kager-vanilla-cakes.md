## Ingredients

- 375 gr (0.75 lb) margarine
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar, crushed
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon hartshorn salt
- vanilla essence
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) Pillsbury flour
- 1/4 Kg (1/6 lb) potato flour

## Directions

1. Mix the margarine well with the powdered sugar, eggs, hartshorn salt, and vanilla essence
2. Stir in the Pillsbury flour and potato flour
3. Place on a cold baking sheet in small dollops with spacing between them
4. Bake light brown
