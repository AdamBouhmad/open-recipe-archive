## Ingredients

- White cabbage, pointed cabbage, or savoy cabbage
- Butter
- Sugar
- Broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare cooked white cabbage, pointed cabbage, or savoy cabbage the same way as cauliflower. Generally it is browned by cutting the head into pieces, which are placed in browned butter with sugar and cooked tender under a lid over even heat. Salt and broth are added to the sauce.
