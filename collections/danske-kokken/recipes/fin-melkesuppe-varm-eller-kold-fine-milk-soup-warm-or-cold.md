## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 1 small dessert spoon corn starch or rice flour
- 2 egg yolks
- white sugar
- salt
- a couple of drops of vanilla (or a couple of pieces of whole cinnamon)
- soft poached egg whites (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the corn starch or rice flour with some of the cold milk
2. Heat the rest of the milk on the fire, and when it is hot, stir in the flour and cook the milk until it is smooth
3. Stir the egg yolks with white sugar, thin them with warm milk, and pour into the pot and stir until it begins to cook
4. Then pour it into a tureen with a little salt and a couple of drops of vanilla added (or cook a couple of pieces of whole cinnamon in the milk instead of vanilla)
5. Soft poached egg whites may be laid on top in the tureen; the milk in which they cook should contain sugar and a little cinnamon, which milk is then strained and added to the rest of the milk
6. Serve the soup with small rusks or baked bread, and one generally counts one quart of milk for 3 people
