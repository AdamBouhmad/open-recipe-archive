## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Bordeaux wine
- 2 wine glasses Sherry
- 1 wine glass Maraschino
- Crushed sugar
- 1 half bottle soda water

## Directions

1. Mix the wine, sugar, and liqueur to taste and chill on ice, then pour into a bowl or terrine. The soda water, also chilled, is added just before serving. Instead of Maraschino liqueur, one can use a glass of Curacao and a little lemon, or instead of lemon, 1 pint of strawberries or raspberries, or 4-5 peaches.
