## Ingredients

- Lamb liver, cut into slices
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Palmin or butter

## Directions

1. Dip the cut liver slices in flour mixed with salt and pepper.
2. Fry in Palmin or butter.
3. Make sauce as for calf liver.
