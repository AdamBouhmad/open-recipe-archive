## Ingredients

- 1 pint lemon or citron jelly
- strawberries
- cherries
- raspberries

## Directions

1. Clarify a pint of lemon or citron jelly prepared as usual and pour it through flannel onto a dish.
2. Arrange all sorts of fruits on it, such as strawberries, cherries, raspberries, etc., in the most ornamental order one can manage.
3. Let the jelly set firmly.
