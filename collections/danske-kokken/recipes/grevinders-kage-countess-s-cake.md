## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- 4 pounds butter
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 1 egg
- Jam or rhubarb compote

## Directions

1. Knead 1 pound fine flour, 4 pounds butter, 1/2 pound sugar, and 1 egg well together and roll out into a fairly thick sheet, cut to fit the pan
2. Roll together the remaining dough, twist it, and lay it as a wreath on the edge of the bottom
3. Bake until pale brown, then fill the inside space with jam or rhubarb compote
