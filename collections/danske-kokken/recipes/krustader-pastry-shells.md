## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg flour
- 1/4 L cold water
- 1 1/4 dl egg white
- 2 teaspoonfuls powdered sugar
- 4 eggs
- good lard or Palmin for frying

## Directions

1. Stir together the flour, cold water, egg white, powdered sugar, and eggs into a pancake batter
2. Fry approximately 80 shells in good lard or Palmin
