## Ingredients

- Roasted beef filet
- Good olive sauce (see recipe 767)
- Olives for garnish
- French potatoes (recipe 661)

## Directions

1. Serve the roasted filet with a good olive sauce
2. Garnish the filet with olives and serve with French potatoes
