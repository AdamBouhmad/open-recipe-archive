## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. well-scraped beef
- 1 lb. finely cleaned chopped kidney fat
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- salt
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- pale ale
- bouillon
- short crust dough
- roasted hens or other birds
- onions
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound 2 lbs. beef and 1 lb. finely cleaned chopped kidney fat very finely in a mortar together with 2 tablespoons wheat flour, a little salt, ground pepper, nutmeg, pale ale and bouillon until it becomes spongy and smooth.
2. Line a mold with short crust dough (Nr. 184).
3. Spread the forcemeat on it and place roasted hens or other birds, cut into pieces, on top together with onions that have been braised in butter and cut into strips, and continue in this manner.
4. On top place a layer of short crust dough.
5. Place the pie in a hot oven and bake for 1.5 hours.
6. There must be an opening in the middle of the lid, as otherwise the pie easily cracks.
7. Serve with brown sauce.
