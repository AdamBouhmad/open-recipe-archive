## Ingredients

- Butter
- Beef, veal, or game forcemeat
- Game ragout as recipe No. 11
- Game sauce with red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Butter the molds and line them with forcemeat made from beef, veal, or game
2. Fill them with game ragout as recipe No. 11
3. Treat and cook as described above
4. Serve with game sauce mixed with a little red currant jelly
