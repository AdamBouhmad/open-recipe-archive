## Ingredients

- Various types of apples
- Generous 1/2 liter (1/2 Pot) water per 1/2 kg apples
- 1/2 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice

## Directions

1. Wipe the apples, cut them into 4 to 6 pieces, and cook them out in generous 1/2 liter (1/2 Pot) water per 1/2 kg apples.
2. Pour the mass into the jelly cloth.
3. Only the next day, measure the juice, boil it with the sugar for 10 minutes, and fill into bottles that are bound immediately.
4. The apple juice will have a beautiful clear color and makes an excellent dessert.
