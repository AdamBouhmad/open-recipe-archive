## Ingredients

- 1 bottle best white rum
- 1 bottle white French wine (Cerons)
- juice of 1 lemon
- 6 measures boiling water
- 1 pound white top sugar

## Directions

1. Add juice of 1 lemon and 6 measures boiling water in which 1 pound white top sugar is dissolved to 1 bottle best white rum and 1 bottle white French wine (Cerons)
2. Drink warm or cold
