## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Kg (2 1/2 S) Cherries
- 3/4 Kg (1 1/2 lb) Powdered Sugar
- 2 1/2 L (2 1/2 Pot) Water
- 1 whole Cinnamon stick
- Peel of 1/2 Lemon
- 1/2 Bottle Sherry

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the cherries, which are given a boil with the sugar and poured into the tureen
2. Crush the pits in a mortar and place over the fire with 2 1/2 L (2 1/2 Pot) water, 1 whole cinnamon stick and peel of 1/2 lemon
3. This must boil for a few minutes and is then poured through a sieve into the tureen over the berries
4. Place the cold soup on ice and finish just before serving with 1/2 Bottle Sherry and, if necessary, with more sugar or water to taste
5. Serve with Vanilla Pastries
