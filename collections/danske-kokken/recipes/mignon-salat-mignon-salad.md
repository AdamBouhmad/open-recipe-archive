## Ingredients

- celery
- tomatoes
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise
- celery leaf stalks

## Directions

1. Rinse a head of celery in cold water and cut it very small
2. Remove the skin from 250 grams of tomatoes and cut them into dice
3. Mix the celery and tomato together and arrange in small piles on lettuce leaves laid on a platter
4. On each pile place a little real mayonnaise, which should not completely cover the vegetables, and stick the top of the leaf stalks in the piles of salad for garnish
5. Keep in a cool place until ready to serve
