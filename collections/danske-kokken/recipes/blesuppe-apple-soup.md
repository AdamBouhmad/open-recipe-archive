## Ingredients

- 3 pounds apples
- 3 pots water
- a little lemon peel
- a couple of sticks cinnamon
- a little rice flour or potato flour
- sugar to taste
- lemon juice
- 1 spoonful old wine
- small rusks

## Directions

1. For this soup, one preferably uses dried apples, though fresh ones can also be used.
2. Peel and cut 3 pounds of apples into pieces and cook under a lid with 3 pots of water and a little lemon peel, until they are so tender that they can be mashed through a sieve.
3. Put the strained soup back over the fire with a couple of sticks of cinnamon, let it come to a boil, and thicken it with a little rice flour or potato flour.
4. Before serving, add sugar and lemon juice according to how sweet or sour the soup is, and 1 spoonful of old wine.
5. Serve the soup with small rusks.
6. For fresh fruit, large juicy varieties are preferable; a smaller portion can also be pears.
