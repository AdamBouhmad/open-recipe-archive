## Ingredients

- 175 gr (35 quintal) blanched almonds
- 250 gr (1/2 S) powdered sugar
- 4 tablespoons water
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Cut blanched almonds lengthwise into fine strips
2. Place in a saucepan with powdered sugar, water and a little butter
3. Stir gently over heat to prevent the almonds from breaking or burning
