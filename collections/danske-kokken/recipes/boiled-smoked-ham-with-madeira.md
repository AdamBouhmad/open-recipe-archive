## Ingredients

- One smoked, lightly salted ham, approximately 4 kg (8 lbs)
- 1 bottle Madeira
- Butter
- Flour
- Strong broth, reduced
- Vegetables

## Directions

1. Blanch the smoked, lightly salted ham over heat in abundant boiling water, where it must boil very gently for 2½ hours, or until the rind easily comes off.
2. Transfer the ham to another pot that is no larger than strictly necessary, and cover with 1 bottle of Madeira.
3. Let it boil for ½ hour very gently under a very tightly fitting lid, so that all the wine does not evaporate.
4. At serving, cover it with a gentle, reduced, strong broth and serve hot with vegetables.
5. Make a sauce by baking butter and flour together, and thin it with a mixture of the reduced broth and the Madeira in which the ham was cooked.
