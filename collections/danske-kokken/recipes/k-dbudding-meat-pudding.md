## Ingredients

- Meat farce
- Butter
- Green vegetables
- Browned butter
- White sauce
- Root vegetables
- Asparagus tips
- Oysters (optional)

## Directions

1. The farce for pudding should be softer than for meatballs.
2. Grease the mold with butter; there should be at least 3 inches lacking to make it full so the pudding can rise during cooking.
3. It needs about an hour of cooking.
4. Serve it either with green vegetables and browned butter sauce, or also use white sauce with white root vegetables, asparagus tips and, if desired, oysters.
