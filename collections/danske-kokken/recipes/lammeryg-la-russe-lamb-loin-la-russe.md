## Ingredients

- Lamb loin
- Butter or fat
- Parsley for garnish
- Potato croquettes (Nr. 14)
- Reduced gravy from roasting
- Sago flour (optional for thickening)
- Maitre d'hôtel or chutney butter

## Directions

1. Prepare a lamb loin covered with butter or fat and roast it as described in the previous recipe.
2. When ready to serve, carve the meat as indicated above and decorate with parsley and garnish with a ring of potato croquettes.
3. Serve the reduced gravy from the roasting as sauce, which, if necessary, can be thickened with a little sago flour.
4. Along with the gravy, serve maitre d'hôtel or chutney butter, which is stirred into the sauce.
