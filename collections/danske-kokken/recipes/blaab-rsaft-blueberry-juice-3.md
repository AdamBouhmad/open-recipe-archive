## Ingredients

- Blueberries
- 1 pound of powdered sugar per pot of juice
- A little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place the berries over the fire until they burst, then pour them out and cool a little
2. Wring them in a cloth
3. Cook the juice afterwards for 1 hour with the sugar and cinnamon
4. Skim it well
5. When it is cold, pour it into bottles
