## Ingredients

- 3 L milk
- 375 g rice flakes
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir 375 g rice flakes into the boiling, sweet or semi-skimmed milk.
2. The porridge cooks for 20 minutes, is salted and served.
3. Serve with butter, sugar and cinnamon.
