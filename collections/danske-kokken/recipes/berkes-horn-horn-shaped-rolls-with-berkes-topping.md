## Ingredients

- 100 g margarine
- 1 kg Pillsbury flour
- 1 teaspoon salt with 25 g yeast dissolved in it
- 2 teaspoons icing sugar
- a very small amount of cardamom
- just over 6.5 dl half-skimmed milk
- egg for brushing
- berkes for coating
- butter for serving

## Directions

1. Crumble the margarine into the flour
2. Add salt with dissolved yeast, icing sugar, cardamom, and milk
3. Shape the dough as horns and place on the baking sheet
4. Brush with egg and sprinkle with berkes
5. Let rise for approximately 1.5 hours
6. Bake at good heat for circa 20 minutes
7. Split and serve spread with butter
