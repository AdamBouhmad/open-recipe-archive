## Ingredients

- 0.5 Pound washed butter
- 1 Pound fine wheat flour
- 3 Pints lukewarm water
- A little powdered sugar
- A little cardamom
- 1 scant pint cold water

## Directions

1. Stir the washed butter to a salve.
2. Then stir with 1 pound fine wheat flour, and when well mixed, with 3 pints lukewarm water, a little powdered sugar, and a little cardamom.
3. Finally add 1 scant pint cold water.
4. Meanwhile heat the waffle iron and grease with butter, but only for the first waffle, after which baking begins.
5. Bake light brown over brisk heat.
6. Serve hot with caster sugar.
7. The quantity given makes about 16 pieces.
