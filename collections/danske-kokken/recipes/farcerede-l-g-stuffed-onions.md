## Ingredients

- Large yellow onions
- Meat forcemeat
- Thin cloth
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Black pepper
- Cucumbers or gherkins
- Bouillon, broth or soy sauce
- Meatballs (optional)

## Directions

1. When large yellow onions are peeled, cut a lid from them, hollow them out, fill with meat forcemeat, place the lids on and bind them in a piece of thin cloth
2. Place them in boiling water and cook until they are tender, then remove them and untie the binding
3. Afterwards make a brown sauce from browned butter, in which mix flour; pepper and cucumbers or gherkins, which is thinned with bouillon, broth or soy sauce
4. Give the onions a boil in it or keep them warm on the platter and pour the sauce over them
5. If you do not have enough onions, you can cook some meatballs in the water together with the onions and place them in the middle of the platter with the onions around them
