## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg green plums
- 625 gr sugar
- 1.25 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice

## Directions

1. Wipe the green plums and place them in a pot set over very low heat until the fruit is cooked through, then pour into the jelly cloth.
2. When the juice has dripped through the next day, weigh and cook it into jelly. (See Sugar treatment in No. 93.)
