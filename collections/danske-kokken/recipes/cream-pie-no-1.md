## Ingredients

- 1 scant pint fresh milk
- 2 small tablespoons corn starch
- 2 eggs
- 1 scant teacup sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1.5 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 8 tablespoons cold water

## Directions

1. Boil 1 scant pint fresh milk.
2. In a little of the cold milk, stir 2 small tablespoons corn starch and beat together with 2 eggs.
3. When the milk has boiled, slowly stir this in with 1 scant teacup sugar, 0.5 cup butter, and 2 tablespoons lemon juice.
4. For the cake, stir together 3 eggs with 1 cup white sugar, 1.5 cups milk, 1 teaspoon baking powder sifted together with 8 tablespoons cold water.
5. Pour into two ordinary pie plates and bake in a hot oven.
6. When the cake is done, take it out, split while still warm, and fill the cream between both layers.
7. If desired, it can be glazed again with egg white and sugar.
