## Ingredients

- 4.5 liters buttermilk
- 2.5 deciliters rice flour
- citrus peel
- whole cinnamon
- scalded raisins or currants
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- crushed almonds (optional)
- whipped cream and strawberry or raspberry jam (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with some of the buttermilk in the bottom of the pot
2. Add the remaining cold buttermilk with citrus peel, cinnamon, and scalded raisins or currants
3. Place over heat and stir continuously from the start, as the mixture will separate if not stirred constantly
4. Beat 3 egg yolks with sugar in a tureen
5. Pour the soup into the egg mixture
6. Serve immediately
7. Optional: add finely crushed almonds
8. Optional: serve separately with whipped cream and strawberry or raspberry jam, omitting the raisins and currants
