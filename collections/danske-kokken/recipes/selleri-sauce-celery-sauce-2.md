## Ingredients

- Celery
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock or salted water
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut two celeries into small cubes and cook tender in stock or in salted water with Maggi bouillon cubes
2. Bake butter and flour and thin with the strained stock to a not too smooth sauce, which is bound with egg yolks
