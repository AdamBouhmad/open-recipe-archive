## Ingredients

- White cabbage
- Butter or lard
- Sugar
- Coloring (optional)
- Liquid (broth, gravy, or water)
- Salt

## Directions

1. Slice the white cabbage and place it in a pot in which a good slice of butter or lard has been browned.
2. Add a spoonful of sugar and brown the cabbage with regular stirring so it does not burn.
3. To speed up browning, coloring may be added.
4. Once the cabbage has sufficient color, add a little liquid, which can be broth, gravy, or water.
5. Steam the cabbage until tender under a lid.
6. Season with salt and more sugar if necessary.
7. Serve with boiled sausages, boiled and fried bacon.
8. A piece of lightly salted, striped bacon can be rinsed, placed in the browned cabbage, and cooked in it, to be eaten with the cabbage.
