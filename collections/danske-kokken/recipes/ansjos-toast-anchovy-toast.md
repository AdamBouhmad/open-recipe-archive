## Ingredients

- 10 anchovies
- 50 grams butter
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- Paprika
- Lemon juice
- Bread

## Directions

1. Pound the meat of 10 anchovies, butter, hard-boiled egg yolks, paprika, and a few drops of lemon juice well in a mortar
2. Spread on warm toasted bread
