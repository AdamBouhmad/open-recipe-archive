## Ingredients

- Butter dough, rolled thin according to the size of the pan
- Fresh strawberries
- Powdered sugar (Melis)
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Roll out a layer of butter dough thinly and bake it in a very hot oven
2. Once the cake is cold, place a layer of fresh strawberries on top, which have been well dusted with powdered sugar
3. Cover the cake with whipped cream
