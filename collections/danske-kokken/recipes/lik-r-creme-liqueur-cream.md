## Ingredients

- vanilla cream as Nr. 1060
- Maraschino, Noyau, Cacao, Chartreuse, Orange, or Strawberry liqueur to taste
- whipped cream for decoration
- food coloring (pink, green, or yellow)

## Directions

1. Stir either Maraschino, Noyau, Cacao, Chartreuse, Orange, or Strawberry liqueur to taste into the vanilla cream (see Nr. 1060).
2. Fill the cream into larger or smaller molds, turn them out, and decorate with small peaks of whipped cream colored pink, green, or yellow with food coloring.
