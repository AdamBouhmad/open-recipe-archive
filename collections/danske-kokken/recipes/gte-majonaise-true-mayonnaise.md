## Ingredients

- 2 raw or hard-boiled egg yolks or preferably one of each kind
- a knife point of salt
- 1 tablespoon fine culinary oil
- juice of half a lemon
- white wine
- salt
- white pepper (optional)
- white powdered sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir well 2 raw or hard-boiled egg yolks or preferably one of each kind and a knife point of salt with a wooden spoon.
2. Gradually let 1 tablespoon fine culinary oil drip down drop by drop onto the eggs with constant stirring until the mixture is properly smooth.
3. Finally add juice of half a lemon, white wine and salt.
4. The sauce, which should be properly thick and smooth, is then cooled.
5. It can also have a little powdered white pepper and white powdered sugar added, but this is not necessary.
