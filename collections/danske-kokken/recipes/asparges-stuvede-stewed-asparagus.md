## Ingredients

- Smaller asparagus
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Asparagus cooking water
- Nutmeg
- Milk
- Cream
- Sugar
- Shrimp (optional)
- Shrimp shells

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus and cut into pieces about one inch long
2. Cook in water with salt or in thin broth
3. The stalks can be cooked longer than the heads
4. Make a roux of flour and butter baked together and thin with a little of the asparagus water, add the drained asparagus pieces with a little salt and nutmeg, or alternatively make a roux of flour and butter baked together and thin with milk and cream
5. The second method is preferable when asparagus is stewed with shrimp
6. Cook and shell the shrimp, crush the shells, bake in butter, cook in water and strain, then skim off the butter, bake with flour, thin with cream and a little asparagus water, add the shelled shrimp and cooked asparagus pieces along with a little sugar and nutmeg
