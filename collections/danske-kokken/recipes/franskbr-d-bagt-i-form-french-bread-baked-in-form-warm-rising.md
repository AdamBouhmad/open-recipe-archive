## Ingredients

- 50 Gr (10 Kvint) yeast
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 Kg (2 S) Pillsbury flour
- 1/4 L (3 Pægle) semi-skimmed, lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Mix yeast with salt and sugar
2. Add Pillsbury flour and semi-skimmed lukewarm milk
3. Work well with a spoon until the dough releases from the bowl
4. Grease two French bread forms with butter
5. Place dough in forms and let rise for 2-2 1/2 hours
6. Bake in a very hot oven for approximately 1 hour
