## Ingredients

- green, white, or red cabbage or spinach
- meat or beef
- flour
- butter
- meat balls
- potatoes (optional)
- sugar
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. First boil a piece of meat or beef, or a little of each
2. When this is tender and the broth strained, add the finely chopped cabbage with a little flour
3. When the cabbage is tender, add a binding of butter and flour together with meat balls
4. If desired, potatoes can be added, which are added at about the same time as the cabbage, or a little before
5. Previously cooked potatoes, either small or cut in pieces, browned in butter and sugar, are particularly suitable
6. For green and white cabbage: cook it in water with a little salt until it begins to soften, then squeeze out the water, chop it with flour and treat as before. This removes the bloating quality the cabbage contains without loss of flavor
7. The broth from smoked meat or fish can be used, but then the meat, after it has soaked half the time in water, must be carefully scraped, placed again in clean water and scraped again before boiling, and the fat must be skimmed off during cooking as the smoked taste is mostly there
8. Salt meat and pork must likewise be soaked before use
