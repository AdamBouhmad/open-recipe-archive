## Ingredients

- leftover game of all kinds, both animals and birds
- butter
- flour
- stock or water or milk
- meat extract or Maggi seasoning
- salt
- truffles or olives or mushrooms
- currant jelly or a little red wine

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small cubes.
2. Make the sauce of browned butter and flour, thinned with stock.
3. If you do not have stock left over, crush the bones and cook them in stock, water, or milk.
4. Add meat extract or Maggi seasoning and salt to the sauce so it becomes sufficiently strong.
5. The sauce can be seasoned with various things, such as finely chopped truffles, peeled olives, or mushrooms.
6. Currant jelly or a little red wine stirred into the sauce gives a good flavor.
