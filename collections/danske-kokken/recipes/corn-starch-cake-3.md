## Ingredients

- 7 egg whites
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup corn starch (mixed in the milk)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- almond extract

## Directions

1. Stir 7 egg whites with 2 cups powdered sugar, 1 cup butter, 1 cup flour, 1 cup corn starch (mixed in milk), 2 teaspoons baking powder sifted with 2 cups flour, and almond extract
