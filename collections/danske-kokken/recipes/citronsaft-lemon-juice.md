## Ingredients

- Lemons
- 3/4 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. The pared lemon peels can be preserved as No. 29, and the juice of the lemons is pressed out and then poured through a fine sieve or cloth.
2. Boil with the sugar for 10 minutes and pour hot into small bottles, which are tied or corked with clean, scalded corks that are well dried.
