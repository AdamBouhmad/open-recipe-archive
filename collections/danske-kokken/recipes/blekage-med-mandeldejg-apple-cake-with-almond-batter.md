## Ingredients

- apples
- wine or water
- butter
- powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 3 pounds sweet almonds
- 3 quintal bitter almonds
- 8 egg whites
- whipped cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and remove the cores carefully
2. Cook the apple pieces tender with very little wine or water and a piece of butter on the bottom of the cooking pot, stirring only when tender
3. When tender, add powdered sugar to taste
4. Lay the porridge on the bottom of the dish in which it is to be baked, in a layer about 2 fingers thick
5. Make a batter of 8 egg yolks, 3 pounds sweet almonds and 3 quintal bitter almonds and finally the 8 beaten egg whites, which is stirred well together and poured over the apple porridge
6. Bake 1 hour until it is a beautiful light brown, and serve warm with whipped cream with sugar over it
