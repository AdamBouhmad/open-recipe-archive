## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) icing sugar
- 1 L (1 Pot) boiling half-skimmed or sweet milk
- 10 leaves isinglass
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water
- 1 L (1 Pot) cream
- finely chopped ginger
- cream foam with ginger juice and liqueur

## Directions

1. Stir 10 egg yolks with 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) icing sugar
2. Whisk this mixture into 1 L (1 Pot) boiling half-skimmed or sweet milk
3. Cook to custard consistency
4. Dissolve 10 leaves of isinglass in 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water and stir into the custard
5. When the custard is partially cooled, stir in the foam from 1 L (1 Pot) cream along with finely chopped ginger
6. Pour the pudding into a wetted mold sprinkled with sugar
7. Serve with cream foam flavored with ginger juice and a little liqueur of some kind
