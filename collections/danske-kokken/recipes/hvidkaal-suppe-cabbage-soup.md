## Ingredients

- meat stock
- 20 whole peppercorns
- root vegetables
- 1 large cabbage head, cut into 4 parts
- 4 to 5 tablespoons oat flour or wheat flour

## Directions

1. When the stock is skimmed, add approximately 20 whole peppercorns, the root vegetables and a large cabbage head that has been cut into 4 parts
2. When both are done, take them out; if you wish the soup to be smooth, add 4 to 5 tablespoons of stirred oat flour or wheat flour to the soup, with which it cooks until the meat is tender and the soup is smooth
3. After this is strained, place it back on the fire together with the root vegetables and cabbage, which are cut into smaller pieces and give it a good boil
4. It is eaten with toasted bread
