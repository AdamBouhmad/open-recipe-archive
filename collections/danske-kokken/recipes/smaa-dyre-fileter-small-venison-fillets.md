## Ingredients

- Venison fillets from the back, nearly one inch thick
- Butter
- Flour
- Salt and pepper
- Game stock or water
- Color (food coloring)
- Currant, lingonberry, or redwine jelly (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut small fillets from the back, nearly one inch thick.
2. Chop the backbone into pieces, sauté in butter and cook in thin stock or water.
3. Brown the flour in butter for the sauce, dilute with the strained stock.
4. Color the sauce and finish with currant, lingonberry jelly or red wine if desired.
5. Beat the fillets lightly with a meat mallet, season with salt and pepper.
6. Fry on the pan in brown butter over high heat.
7. Variations in sauce can be made with different additions, given only one boil, but omit the jelly in these cases.
