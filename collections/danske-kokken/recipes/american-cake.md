## Ingredients

- 500 grams powdered sugar
- 250 grams margarine
- 5 eggs
- 10 lemon drops
- 125 grams cleaned currants
- 125 grams sultana raisins
- 65 grams finely chopped preserved orange peel
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 875 milliliters semi-skimmed milk
- 1 kilogram Pillsbury flour

## Directions

1. Mix powdered sugar, margarine, and eggs well
2. Add lemon drops, currants, sultana raisins, and finely chopped preserved orange peel
3. Dissolve baking powder in semi-skimmed milk and add to the mixture
4. Stir in Pillsbury flour last
5. Bake in a greased form for approximately 1 hour 30 minutes
