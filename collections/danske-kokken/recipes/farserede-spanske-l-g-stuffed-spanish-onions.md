## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 large Spanish onions (serves 10 people)
- forcemeat
- broth or water
- salt
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- browned butter
- flour
- paprika
- lemon juice
- coloring

## Directions

1. Peel large Spanish onions and cut off the top
2. Hollow them out and fill with forcemeat
3. Place the top back on and bind with string
4. Cook until tender in broth or water with salt and Maggi bouillon cubes, using only enough liquid for the sauce
5. Make the sauce from browned butter, flour, the broth, paprika, lemon juice, and coloring
