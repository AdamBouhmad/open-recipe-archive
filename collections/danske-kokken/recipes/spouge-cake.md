## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks with 2 cups sugar, then with 2 cups sifted flour with 2 teaspoons baking powder in it
2. Then add 1 teaspoon vanilla and the beaten whites
