## Ingredients

- 2 pints syrup
- 3 quintal ground cinnamon
- 1 quintal ground ginger
- cloves and cardamom to taste
- 10 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 10 tablespoons melted butter
- 10 beaten eggs
- 6 quintal soda, dissolved in cold water
- flour as needed for a standard dough

## Directions

1. Give the syrup one boil with the cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and cardamom, then add the powdered sugar and melted butter. Whip it until it forms bubbles, and when the syrup has cooled, pour this mixture into the beaten eggs while constantly beating, then stir in the soda dissolved in cold water and as much flour as is needed for a standard dough. The dough must stand for a couple of days in a cold place before the cakes are baked in a well-heated oven.
