## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg cloudberries
- Cognac

## Directions

1. These Norwegian berries are picked and sprinkled with sugar, then left until the next day.
2. Pour everything into the pot and bring just to a boil.
3. Remove the berries with a slotted spoon and place in a jar.
4. Cook the syrup until it is smooth, then add 1 sherry glass of cognac for each 1/2 kg fruit.
5. Pour the boiling syrup over the berries and seal once the preserve has cooled.
