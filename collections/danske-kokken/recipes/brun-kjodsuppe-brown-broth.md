## Ingredients

- Lean meat and pieces unsuitable for other uses
- Butter
- Boiling water
- Salt
- Small root vegetables
- Celery fibers
- Water
- Meat dumplings or fish dumplings

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces, but not thin ones. Brown them in butter with a little boiling water and add salt and roots. Use small roots as they cook apart anyway. For celery, use the fibers since the broth should not be white. Wash and rub the roots in several waters and let them soak in clean water for several hours before adding. Skim the broth well as it cooks. Thin it first with water, then with white meat broth. The roots that should be in the finished broth are cooked in white broth or water. It is most common to have only dumplings in the broth, either meat dumplings or fish dumplings.
