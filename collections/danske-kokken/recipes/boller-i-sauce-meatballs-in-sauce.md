## Ingredients

- Broth and water in equal parts
- Meat or fish meatballs
- Butter
- Onion
- Black pepper
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Flour
- Oysters, mussels, mushrooms, capers, celery, or other additions as desired
- Wine and lemon juice (optional)
- Egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Put broth and water in equal parts on the heat, in which cook meat or fish meatballs
2. When cooked, remove them and strain the broth
3. Heat a good piece of butter on the heat, in which stir some grated onion, some ground pepper, nutmeg, salt and flour, which is thinned with the broth until it has a proper thickness
4. Add the meatballs and cook them a little in it
5. In the same way the meatballs can be prepared with oysters, mussels, mushrooms, capers, celery, or whatever you wish to use; the onions can be omitted and you can then add a little wine and lemon juice and thicken the sauce with a couple of egg yolks
