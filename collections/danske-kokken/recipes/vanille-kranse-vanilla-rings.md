## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 375 grams (3/4 pound) margarine
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) powdered sugar
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) sweet, blanched, and finely ground almonds
- 1 egg
- Vanilla essence
- A small amount of deer salt, optional

## Directions

1. Knead together the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, almonds, egg, and vanilla essence
2. The dough can be piped through a pastry bag or meat grinder and formed into small rings
3. The rings can be placed fairly close together on the baking sheet
4. If a small amount of deer salt is added, they become equally good
5. Vanilla rings and almond rings can be baked using special baking flour, adding only water and margarine
