## Ingredients

- 375 g (3/4 S) powdered sugar
- 1/4 L (3 pints) water
- 8 oranges, juice extracted and strained
- 10 sheets isinglass per liter (pot) of juice, dissolved in a little boiling water
- 6 oranges, peeled and sliced
- green grapes for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the powdered sugar with 1/4 L (3 pints) water.
2. Pour in the extracted and strained juice of 8 oranges and pass the whole through a jelly bag.
3. For each liter (pot) of juice, add 10 sheets of isinglass dissolved in a little boiling water.
4. Peel the remaining 6 oranges, cut them into slices, remove the pips, and layer them in the mold with the jelly; but the previous layer must be stiff before a new one is laid over it.
5. Place the mold on ice or set it in a cold place until the next day.
6. Turn out, garnish with green grapes, and serve with cream sauce or whipped cream.
7. Serve with macaroons.
