## Ingredients

- Equal parts potatoes and beets
- Mustard
- Oil
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Raw egg yolk
- Pepper
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Celery, raw apples, cooked egg whites and mushrooms (optional)
- Onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut equal amounts of potatoes and beets into thin slices, place on the salad dish and pour over with a sauce made from a little stirred mustard, stirred with oil or with hard-boiled egg yolks, which are first mashed with one raw egg yolk, pepper, salt and vinegar, half of it from the beets
2. You can add celery, raw apples, cooked egg whites and mushrooms; it is then best to cut everything small and stir it in the sauce
3. If you prefer onion, these must first be cooked and chopped
