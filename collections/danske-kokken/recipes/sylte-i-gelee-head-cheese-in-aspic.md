## Ingredients

- Pork head
- Veal knuckle
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Bay leaves
- Whole pepper
- Egg whites and shells

## Directions

1. Cook head and other parts with a veal knuckle in water
2. Simmer, then add salt, a little vinegar, bay leaves and whole pepper
3. Cook slowly; then remove
4. Remove bones, pick head clean and cut into smaller pieces, separate feet
5. Rinse slightly in broth and place either gathered in one pot or in several smaller ones
6. Skim fat well off the broth, which is heated over fire; if it is not clear, cook a couple of egg whites and shells in it
7. Pour it through a fine sieve over the head cheese, which is lifted a little so the aspic can penetrate everywhere
8. The head cheese is eaten cold with vinegar and pepper
