## Ingredients

- 1 pound cold cooked potatoes
- a quarter pound butter
- 3/4 cup cream
- half a pound ground sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 18 quintal sweet almonds
- 9 quintal bitter almonds
- a little grated lemon zest
- cardamom
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel 1 pound cold cooked potatoes and grate or pound very finely, then bake in a quarter pound butter. Then thin and stir with 3/4 cup cream. Then stir for about a quarter hour first with half a pound ground sugar, then with 10 egg yolks, 18 quintal sweet almonds and 9 quintal bitter almonds, a little grated lemon zest, cardamom, and finally with the beaten egg whites. Boil the pudding in a water bath for 2 hours and serve with sweet fruit sauce with a little red wine in it or with melted butter.
