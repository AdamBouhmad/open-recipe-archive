## Ingredients

- 0.25 kg (0.5 lbs) butter
- 0.25 kg (0.5 lbs) flour
- 8.5 dl (3.5 pints) boiling milk
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 onion, grated
- Salt
- Pepper
- 0.5 kg (1 lbs) chopped meat
- Egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and bake the flour in it.
2. Add the boiling milk gradually to the flour mixture.
3. When cooled, stir in the egg yolks, grated onion, salt, pepper and chopped meat.
4. Finally fold in the beaten egg whites.
5. Pour into a buttered and floured pudding mold.
6. Boil for 2.5 hours.
7. Alternatively, bake in the oven in a gratin dish for approximately 1 hour or in a ring mold.
8. Serve with a brown gravy sauce made with gherkins, anchovies, pickles, pearl onions, chutney, Maggi bouillon cubes or lemon juice.
