## Ingredients

- Lamb liver (1 liver serves 3-4 people)
- Flour
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter or lard
- Browned butter
- Boiled new potatoes
- Gooseberry compote, cucumber salad, or lettuce salad

## Directions

1. Soak the liver in water for ½ hour so the blood drains out well.
2. Remove the membrane and cut the liver into thin slices.
3. Dip in flour, then in egg and bread crumbs mixed with salt and pepper.
4. Fry until crispy and brown in butter or lard.
5. Serve with browned butter and newly boiled potatoes, along with either gooseberry compote, cucumber salad, or lettuce salad. Most vegetables are suitable as accompaniments.
