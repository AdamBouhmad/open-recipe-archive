## Ingredients

- 2 pounds butter
- 0.5 pound flour
- 1 pint milk
- Preserved fruit or jam
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Bake butter and flour until it leaves the spoon
2. While still over heat, stir in milk
3. Finally fold in beaten egg whites
4. Grease pan with cold butter
5. Divide batter into 3 parts and bake as 3 thick pancakes
6. Spread jam between each layer
7. Sprinkle white sugar on top
8. Serves about 8 persons
9. Serve warm with powdered sugar
