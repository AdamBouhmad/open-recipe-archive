## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Gravy from the roast
- Salt
- Sugar
- Coloring (if needed)

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and bake flour in it, which is thinned with the gravy from the roast; this must be strained beforehand and all fat well skimmed
2. The sauce must simmer very gently for 1 quarter hour after it is thinned with the gravy
3. It should be made fairly thin, and the portion should be generously large, since the sauce reduces somewhat during cooking, but in return becomes thicker the longer it cooks
4. Prepare the sauce with salt, a little sugar and coloring if it needs more color
