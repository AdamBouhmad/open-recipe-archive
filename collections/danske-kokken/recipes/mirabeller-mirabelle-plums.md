## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl water
- 3/4 dl cognac
- 1/2 kg mirabelle plums

## Directions

1. These small yellow plums must not be completely ripe.
2. Pour boiling water over them, then cold water.
3. Boil sugar and water to a thin syrup and add the mirabelles, giving them just one boil.
4. Remove the plums and place in a jar, and cook down the syrup before pouring it hot over the fruit.
5. Cover the preserve immediately.
6. After 2 days, pour off the syrup to cook down further, and add 1/4 dl cognac as soon as the pot is removed from heat, then immediately pour the syrup over the mirabelles and seal.
7. Mirabelles are excellent for making into compote.
