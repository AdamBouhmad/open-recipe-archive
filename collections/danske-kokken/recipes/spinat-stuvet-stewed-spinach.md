## Ingredients

- New Zealand spinach
- water
- salt
- flour
- nutmeg
- sugar
- cream or milk
- butter

## Directions

1. So-called New Zealand spinach should be preferred as the best
2. After the spinach is picked and rinsed, cook it until tender in salted water
3. Lay in a sieve until the water has drained off
4. Chop it very fine with a little flour and stew with a little nutmeg and sugar in cream or milk
5. Finally add a little butter and salt
