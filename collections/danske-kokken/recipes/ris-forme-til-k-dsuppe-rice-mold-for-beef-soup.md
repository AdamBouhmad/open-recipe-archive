## Ingredients

- 3.5 deciliters (11/10 pails) rice
- 0.5 liter (0.5 pot) boiling water
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) blanched and scalded raisins
- Butter
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the rice and place it over heat in boiling water
2. After the rice has cooked for 15 minutes, add the blanched and scalded raisins
3. Cook until the rice is tender but not completely cooked
4. During cooking, gradually add boiling water as needed
5. Stir in a good portion of butter, a little sugar, and salt
6. Rinse a mold or bowl with water
7. Pour the rice mixture into it to be turned out onto a platter later and served with the soup
8. The mixture can also be formed into small bowls, allowing one for each person
