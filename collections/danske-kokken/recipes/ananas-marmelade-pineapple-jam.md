## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar for every 1/2 kg peeled fruit
- Ripe pineapples

## Directions

1. Peel ripe pineapples, cut into slices, and pound in a mortar
2. Cook sugar and fruit together for 25 minutes with constant stirring
3. Fill into glasses, immediately cover with paper, and seal with parchment paper
