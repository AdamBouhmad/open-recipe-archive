## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Milk
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. One egg and 2 tablespoons of milk are generally calculated for each person.
2. Beat the eggs well so that the yolks and whites come together.
3. In a flat saucepan or on a pan, place a slice of butter which is melted and pour in the eggs, which must be placed over very low heat.
4. As the scrambled eggs stiffen, scrape them from the bottom of the pan and place them on the warm platter on which they are to be arranged.
5. The scrambled eggs must not come to a boil and only very little salt should be added, as both of these cause them to easily become grainy and watery.
