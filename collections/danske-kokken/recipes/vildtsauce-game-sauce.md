## Ingredients

- cream
- butter
- gravy or soy sauce
- red currant jelly (optional)

## Directions

1. Put cream and butter in a saucepan over heat and stir until it boils.
2. Add gravy or soy sauce.
3. The sauce should be fairly smooth.
4. A little red currant jelly can also be stirred in, though this is better served unmixed with the roast.
