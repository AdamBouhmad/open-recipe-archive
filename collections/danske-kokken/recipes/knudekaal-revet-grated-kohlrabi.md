## Ingredients

- Cooked kohlrabi
- Milk or cream sauce
- Salt
- Sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Peel the whole cooked kohlrabi thickly and grate it.
2. Toss gently in a good milk or cream sauce that has been seasoned with salt, a little sugar, and a small slice of cold butter before the kohlrabi is stirred in.
