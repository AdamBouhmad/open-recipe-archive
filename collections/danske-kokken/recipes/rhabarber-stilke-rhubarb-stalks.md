## Ingredients

- Rhubarb stalks
- Water
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Corn starch

## Directions

1. Peel them carefully as for porridge, cut into small pieces and place for a couple of hours in cold water.
2. Then place them on the fire in fresh water and if the stalks are very sour there should be enough that half can be drained off when the stalks have cooked until they begin to fall apart.
3. Then rub them out with sugar and a little cinnamon.
4. A thickening can be added of an insignificant amount of corn starch, dissolved in a little cold water, but this can also be omitted if one simply does not have too much water.
