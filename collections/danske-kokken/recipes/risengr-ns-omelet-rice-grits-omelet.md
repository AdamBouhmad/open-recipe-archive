## Ingredients

- 35 drams rice grits
- 1 pot sweet milk
- 1.5 measures cream
- vanilla
- 35 drams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- sugar
- salt
- 7 egg whites

## Directions

1. Clean rice grits well and cook almost tender with 1 pot sweet milk
2. Stir 1.5 measures cream with a vanilla stick cooked in it and 35 drams butter into the warm grits
3. When cooled somewhat, add 8 beaten egg yolks with sugar and salt to taste
4. Finally stir in the 7 beaten egg whites
5. Bake the omelet for 2 hours
6. Serve with sprinkled powdered sugar
