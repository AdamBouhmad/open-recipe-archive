## Ingredients

- Mutton, cut into pieces
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Green peas
- White root vegetables
- Small potatoes
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare as for calf fricassee, but as the meat is stronger, more water can be added.
2. Use green peas, white root vegetables, small potatoes and chopped parsley.
