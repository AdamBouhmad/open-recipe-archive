## Ingredients

- 190 g oat groats
- 4½ L water
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- approximately ¼ kg powdered sugar
- ¼ L good sherry
- ¼ L rhubarb juice
- Whole cinnamon
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the grains into the boiling water and cook ½ to 1 hour.
2. Strain the soup and pour into a serving bowl in which 2 egg yolks and 1 egg white have been stirred with approximately ¼ kg powdered sugar.
3. Into this thickened soup pour ¼ L good sherry and ¼ L rhubarb juice.
4. Whole cinnamon and lemon peel can be cooked in the soup or rather together with the grains.
5. This can be served with compote of fresh, dried or preserved fruit.
