## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Salt
- 0.5 small loaf
- Cold water
- A little pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 2 pounds fish
- Butter

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes in well-salted water.
2. Soak 0.5 small loaf in cold water and wring dry in a clean cloth.
3. Stir in a little salt, pepper, and 1 tablespoon chopped parsley.
4. Clean 2 pounds fish, cut into thin slices, and place in cold, salt water.
5. When the potatoes are sufficiently cooked, pick them, press through a colander, and season with pepper and salt.
6. Grease a pudding form on the bottom with butter and lay alternating layers of bread and fish in it.
7. On top place a neat layer of the potato mixture and bake for one hour in a not too hot oven.
