## Ingredients

- 200 grams (40 quintals) Gorgonzola cheese
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) butter

## Directions

1. Place the cheese in a bowl, set it over boiling water, and as soon as the cheese is soft and easily stirred, blend it after cooling into the stirred butter
2. Give this a slight green color and place in a pastry bag as soon as it is sufficiently stiffened to pipe
3. Set off in small stars or roses over the crackers
4. Arrange on parchment paper
5. Use not very thin crackers
