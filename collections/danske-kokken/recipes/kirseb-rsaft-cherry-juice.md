## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 S) berries per batch
- 1/4 liter (1 Pægl) water per 1/2 kg berries

## Directions

1. Crush the berries in a wooden or stone mortar and place them in the pot together with the water.
2. Boil for a good quarter of an hour.
3. Sift the juice, pour into dry bottles, cork them, bind with sailcloth, wrap with hay, and place in the pot to boil for 5 minutes.
4. Plaster the bottles the day after.
