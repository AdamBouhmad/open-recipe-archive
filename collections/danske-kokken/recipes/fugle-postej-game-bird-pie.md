## Ingredients

- liver forcemeat
- 0.5 lbs. veal
- 1 lb. pork belly
- a fairly large calf liver
- game birds or pigeons
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cover the mold with a liver forcemeat made of 0.5 lbs. veal, 1 lb. pork belly and a fairly large calf liver.
2. Half-roast the birds in a casserole and set aside the sauce.
3. Place the birds in the forcemeat and cover with a layer of it.
4. Bake the pie for 1 hour.
5. Serve with a strong sauce of meat broth without onion and small puff pastry bits or strips.
6. Game birds and pigeons are best suited for this pie.
