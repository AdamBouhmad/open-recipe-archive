## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 pots) clear soup or boiling water with Maggis Bouillon cubes
- 4 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 3 leeks
- 1 celery
- 1 head of lettuce
- 50 g (10 drams) butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon chopped chervil
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots, turnips, leeks, celery, and lettuce into approximately 2 centimeter (1 inch) long strips, as fine as knitting needles
2. Melt 50 g (10 drams) butter in a pot and sauté the vegetables with 1 teaspoon sugar
3. Pour the soup over the vegetables and cook them completely tender
4. Skim all the fat from the soup carefully
5. A tablespoon of chopped chervil can be added and brought to a boil
6. Serve with toasted bread
