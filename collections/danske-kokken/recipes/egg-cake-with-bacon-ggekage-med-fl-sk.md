## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 12 tablespoons whole milk
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- 375 grams bacon
- Leek

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks and milk into the flour together with finely cut leek and finally the beaten egg whites.
2. Fry slices of lightly salted, striped bacon from which the rind has been removed in the pan.
3. When done, remove it and pour the fat from the pan, which is cleaned of the salt that has collected on it.
4. Pour the fat back into the pan, which is again heated over the fire.
5. Pour in the eggs and arrange the bacon on top.
6. Turn the egg cake onto a lid and bake on the other side.
