## Ingredients

- Smoked ham
- Parsley or lettuce
- Braised vegetables or boiled vegetables
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the smoked ham into very thin slices, which are laid in strips on a flat platter and garnished with parsley or lettuce.
2. Serve with most types of braised vegetables. If boiled in water, serve them with melted butter or toss them in butter.
3. Sliced smoked ham can be served with onion sauce and boiled potatoes.
4. Smoked pork tenderloin can be sliced and used in place of sliced smoked ham.
