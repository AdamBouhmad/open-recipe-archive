## Ingredients

- Elderberries
- 3 pounds of powdered sugar per pot of juice
- A few pieces of cinnamon
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Strip and place the berries over the fire, preferably in a clean earthenware pot
2. When they begin to burst, take them out, set them aside to cool and wring them in a clean linen cloth
3. Filter the juice if necessary and place it over the fire with the sugar and cinnamon
4. Cook with regular skimming until after about a quarter of an hour it begins to appear clear, then remove it from the fire and add a little Cognac
5. When the juice has cooled, fill it into bottles, whose upper part is filled again with Cognac
6. Cork the bottles well
