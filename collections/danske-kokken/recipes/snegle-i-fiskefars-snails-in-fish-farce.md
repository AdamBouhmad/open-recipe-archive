## Ingredients

- Cooked snails (from No. 572)
- Fish farce
- Water
- Salt
- Chopped mushrooms
- 1 grated onion
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Ground white pepper
- White wine
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. After the snails have undergone the cleaning and cooking described in No. 572, place them in fish farce, ensuring a little of the snail is visible above the smoothed farce
2. Place snail shells in a flat casserole with boiling salted water, farce side up
3. Cook for 10 minutes
4. Arrange on a napkin or on a dish covered with a layer of salt
5. Use the broth in which the snails were cooked to make a white, bound sauce
6. Before adding egg yolks, add some chopped mushrooms, 1 grated onion and 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley to the sauce
7. Season the sauce with salt, ground white pepper, white wine and lemon juice
8. Serve the sauce separately
