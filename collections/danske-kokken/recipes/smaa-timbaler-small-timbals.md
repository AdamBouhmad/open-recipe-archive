## Ingredients

- Butter
- Fish forcemeat
- Various stews mentioned previously

## Directions

1. Butter small cup molds and line them with fish forcemeat
2. Fill with various stews mentioned previously, covering them with forcemeat and paper
3. Place the molds in a hot oven in a baking pan, then fill the pan with boiling water
4. They will be ready in approximately ½ hour
5. Small timbals can be served as a course with sauce or as decoration around a larger timbal
