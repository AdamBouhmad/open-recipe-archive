## Ingredients

- 125 grams (1/4 pound) grated cheese
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) cold butter
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) Pillsbury flour
- 2 egg yolks
- a small amount of paprika

## Directions

1. Rub butter and flour together with the cheese
2. Knead the egg yolks into it
3. Roll out the dough very thinly
4. Cut into squares with a pastry wheel
5. Prick and bake light brown
6. Serve hot
7. They can be stored in a tin box but must be refreshed in the oven before serving
8. The dough can be rolled into small sticks; they are baked light brown and likewise served with coffee or at dinner
