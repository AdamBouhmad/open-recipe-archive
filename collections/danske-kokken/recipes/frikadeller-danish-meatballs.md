## Ingredients

- 3 pounds ground beef
- 1 pound beef kidney fat
- 4 eggs
- 4 hardtacks soaked in sweet milk
- finely chopped onion
- ground pepper
- salt
- sweet milk and cream for consistency
- fat or butter for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the beef very finely.
2. Mix the beef with kidney fat, eggs, hardtacks soaked in sweet milk, finely chopped onion, ground pepper and salt.
3. Work the mixture together until it becomes well aerated.
4. Thin with sweet milk and cream to achieve proper consistency.
5. Fry the meatballs light brown in a pan with fat or butter, using a spoon dipped in cold water to shape them.
6. Turn them on the pan and fry over gentle heat.
7. Brown butter in the pan, thin with a little water and serve with the meatballs along with stewed vegetables or carrots.
8. May also be made from other kinds of meat, fish forcemeat, or cooked or fried meat; in the latter case, mix the meat with 1 pound of fat, smoked, finely chopped bacon instead of kidney fat.
