## Ingredients

- 500 grams margarine
- 375 grams fine sugar
- 5 eggs
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- zest and juice of 1 lemon
- a little orange essence
- 1 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 750 milliliters semi-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix margarine, fine sugar, eggs, baking powder, lemon zest, lemon juice, and orange essence well
2. Add Pillsbury flour and semi-skimmed milk
3. Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes in a moderately warm oven
4. Grease and line the form with paper
