## Ingredients

- 375 g prunes
- 4½ L water
- 125 g small crushed macaroons
- ½ L cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes, soak them for one day and cook them in the water in which they have been standing.
2. Pass the fruit through a coarse sieve, dilute to the specified measure, sweeten and thicken with sago flour.
3. Whip ½ L cream in which 125 g small crushed macaroons have been stirred, and serve with the soup.
