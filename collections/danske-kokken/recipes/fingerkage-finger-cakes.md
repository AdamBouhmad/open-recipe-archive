## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar (less than 1 tablespoon)
- 1 scant teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 as much soda
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs lightly and add 1 cup granulated sugar (less than 1 tablespoon).
2. Sift in a very scant teaspoon of cream of tartar and half as much soda and a little salt.
3. Sift in flour until a stiff dough is formed.
4. Roll the dough fairly thin and spread the remaining sugar over it.
5. Cut the dough into strips about the size of a finger and place on a buttered pan without touching each other.
6. Bake in a quick oven and watch that they do not burn.
7. Vanilla may be stirred into the dough.
