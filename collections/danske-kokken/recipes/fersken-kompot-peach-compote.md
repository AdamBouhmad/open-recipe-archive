## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.25 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Place the peaches in boiling water so the skin comes off more easily.
2. Split them and remove the stones.
3. Treat the fruit and kernel as described for apricots.
