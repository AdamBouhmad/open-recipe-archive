## Ingredients

- spinach
- a little flour
- nutmeg
- cream, milk or broth
- butter
- salt
- poached or hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the spinach and cook it until soft
2. Press the water out through a sieve and chop it fine with a little flour
3. Place it in the pot in which it will be stewed, with a little nutmeg, mix well and thin gradually with cream, milk or broth until it becomes like a thin porridge
4. Cook it briefly and finally add butter and salt
5. Serve with poached or hard-boiled eggs around it
