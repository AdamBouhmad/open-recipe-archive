## Ingredients

- Thick sour cream
- White powdered sugar
- Vinegar
- Grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Whip thick sour cream
2. Add white powdered sugar and vinegar according to the sourness of the cream
3. Add grated horseradish until it becomes sufficiently sharp
4. This sauce is used for cold fish and pike; some even use it for warm fish
