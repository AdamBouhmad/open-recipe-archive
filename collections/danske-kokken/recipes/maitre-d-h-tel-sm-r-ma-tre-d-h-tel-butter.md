## Ingredients

- ¼ kg (½ pound) stirred butter
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- White pepper

## Directions

1. To ¼ kg stirred butter, add 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and a little white pepper.
2. This butter is used for French beef as well as for other meat and vegetable dishes.
3. A teaspoon is dipped in lukewarm water, and with it small butter balls are formed and placed on the beef just as it is served.
