## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 lb) dark syrup
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 125 gr (1/4 lb) margarine
- 125 gr (1/4 lb) chopped almonds
- 65 gr (13 quintals) chopped candied citrus peel
- 65 gr (13 quintals) chopped orange peel
- 5 gr (1 quintal) cloves
- 10 gr (2 quintals) cinnamon
- 5 gr (1 quintal) ginger
- 1.25 dl (1/2 pint) rose water
- 15 gr (3 quintals) potash
- 1/4 Kg (1.5 lb) flour

## Directions

1. Place the syrup, powdered sugar, margarine, chopped almonds, candied citrus peel, orange peel, cloves, cinnamon, ginger, and rose water in a pot
2. Bring to a boil, then add the potash and remove from heat
3. Sprinkle in the flour and work well with a spoon, as the dough is too soft to handle by hand
4. Let it rest covered for one week
5. Work the dough well on a board and roll very thin with a little flour
6. Cut the cakes with a glass or with cake cutters in various shapes
7. Butter the baking sheet and place the cakes not too close together
8. Brush with egg and top each with an almond
9. Bake at good heat
10. Store in tin boxes for an extended period. Note: candied citrus peel and almonds can be omitted from the dough and the cakes will still be good
