## Ingredients

- pears
- soft water
- wine vinegar
- sugar (Melis)
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Halve the pears, hollow them out and wipe them clean.
2. Place them in a jar and pour boiling soft water over them.
3. Let them stand for 5 to 6 hours.
4. Remove them, place on a colander, and when the water has drained, press them gently with a linen cloth.
5. Bring wine vinegar to a boil and briefly boil the pears in it.
6. Remove them and place them layered in a jar on whose bottom has been spread a layer of ground sugar.
7. Between each layer of pears place a layer of sugar; the topmost layer must be sugar.
8. Pour the wine vinegar over the pears.
9. It gives a pleasant taste if the vinegar is boiled with cinnamon and cloves, but the pears will become dark.
