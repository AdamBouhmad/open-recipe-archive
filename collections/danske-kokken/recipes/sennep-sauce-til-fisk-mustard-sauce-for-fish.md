## Ingredients

- A slice of butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Fish broth
- Water mustard
- Milk

## Directions

1. Melt a slice of butter and bake 1 tablespoon flour in it, which is diluted with fish broth
2. Stir in water mustard when it boils
3. The mustard sauce becomes milder when it is diluted with milk
4. Melted butter is served at the same time as the mustard sauce
