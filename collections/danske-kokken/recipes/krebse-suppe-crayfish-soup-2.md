## Ingredients

- 25 crayfish of medium size
- Butter
- Meat broth
- Rice flour
- White root vegetables or asparagus
- Bread dumplings or fish dumplings

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish as usual.
2. Pick out the meat from the tails, heads and claws; crush the shells and all the small bones finely with a little butter, place on the fire and blanch well, but not too long, so as to avoid a bad taste, and moisten slightly with meat broth, which does not need to be strong, until it stands over this.
3. When it has cooked a little, strain it, place it back on the fire with the same amount of broth, and after a good boiling strain it again into the dark portion.
4. Let the soup stand awhile so that you can skim off the red butter; in this stir in approximately two ounces of rice flour, which is added to the crayfish soup when this, mixed with the remaining meat broth, boils again.
5. Use only white root vegetables or asparagus.
6. It is eaten either with bread dumplings, into which the chopped crayfish claws and heads are stirred, or with fish dumplings.
7. The shells of the crayfish heads are filled with some of the dumpling dough and cooked in the soup.
8. After the dark vein is removed from the tails, these whole tails are placed on the finished soup, but must not be cooked with it.
