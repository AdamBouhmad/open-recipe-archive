## Ingredients

- 1/6 kg (1 Skp) rinsed and scalded rice
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 1 L (1 Pot) white vegetable broth
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) tomato purée
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pour 1 L white vegetable broth over the 1/6 kg rinsed and scalded rice, together with 1 carrot and 1 onion
2. Set over gentle heat to steam tender under a lid for approximately 1 hour
3. If there is still liquid remaining after this time, remove the lid and cook further until it has evaporated
4. Remove the carrot and onion, and stir 1/4 L tomato purée into the rice
5. Serve this vegetable with meat and fish dishes
