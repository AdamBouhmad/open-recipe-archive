## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 2 teaspoons saleratus

## Directions

1. Dissolve 2 teaspoons saleratus in the molasses.
2. Mix the whole together with flour until stiff, and cut into round cakes.
