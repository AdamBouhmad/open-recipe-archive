## Ingredients

- 4 well beaten egg yolks
- 25 quintals butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- cold water
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Set a pudding mold in a pot of boiling water.
2. Add 4 well beaten egg yolks to it.
3. Stir in 25 quintals butter which has first been stirred to a salve, and 1 tablespoon flour.
4. Pour in several spoonfuls of cold water and then gently boiling water under constant stirring so the sauce becomes smooth.
