## Ingredients

- 8 large whole carrots
- meat stock
- 1/2 pint rice flour
- white root vegetables, a large bunch
- butter
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped carrots
- 1 tablespoon onion

## Directions

1. Cook 8 large whole carrots in the stock, mash them and press through a colander together with the stock, which should be strained, then place it back on the fire with 1/2 pint rice flour and cook until it has evened the soup
2. Serve with toasted bread or small meat balls
3. Alternatively, brown a piece of butter in the pot, add 2 tablespoons full of finely chopped carrots and 1 tablespoon onion and sauté well until light brown; then gradually add stock and let it cook evenly until the carrots can be pressed apart; then press it through a colander with more stock, finally through a sieve and give it a boil with cut white root vegetables and small meat balls
