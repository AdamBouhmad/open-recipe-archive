## Ingredients

- 1 ounce butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 0.25 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 ounce butter, 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, 1 teaspoon lemon juice and 0.25 teaspoon pepper
2. Mix together cold and spread on what you wish to use it for
3. It is excellent for any kind of fried fish
