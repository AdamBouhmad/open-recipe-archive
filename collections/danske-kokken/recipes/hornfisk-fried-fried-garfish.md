## Ingredients

- Hornfisk (Garfish)
- Salt
- Mel (Flour)
- Æg (Egg)
- Brød (Bread)
- Smør or Palmin (Butter or Margarine)
- brunt Smør (Brown butter)
- Citron or Eddike (Lemon or Vinegar)
- Kartofler (Potatoes)
- Gemyse (Vegetables)

## Directions

1. Split the fish along the belly and remove the bone.
2. Partition into smaller pieces.
3. Rinse the pieces and sprinkle with salt.
4. Dip in flour, then in egg, then in bread.
5. Fry in butter or Palmin.
6. Serve with brown butter, lemon or vinegar, and potatoes.
7. Vegetables can be served alongside.
