## Ingredients

- Peeled asparagus
- Lightly salted water
- Salt solution (10 gr or 2 Kvint per liter)

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled asparagus into small pieces and rinse
2. Cook in lightly salted water for 5 minutes and pour cold water over them
3. Place in jars and pour a salt solution (as described above) over them
4. Cook the closed jars for 1/4 hour and then place to cool in the pot or cover
