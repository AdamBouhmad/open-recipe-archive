## Ingredients

- 1.5 Pint good red wine
- 1.5 Pint clear raspberry juice, not cooked with sugar
- 1 Pint water
- 1.5 Pound whole sugar
- 1.5 Ounces gelatine, good quality

## Directions

1. Cook the red wine, raspberry juice, water, and sugar together with the gelatine
2. Allow it to cool and stir until it is quite cold
3. Pour it into the mold
4. Serve with Crème sauce
5. Serves 10 persons
