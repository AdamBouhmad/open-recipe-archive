## Ingredients

- ¼ kg (½ pound) butter
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped chervil
- 2 tablespoons parsley
- 1 tablespoon watercress
- 1 tablespoon leek

## Directions

1. ¼ kg butter is mixed with 1 tablespoon finely chopped chervil, 2 tablespoons parsley, 1 tablespoon watercress, and 1 tablespoon leek.
