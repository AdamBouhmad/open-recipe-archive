## Ingredients

- Veal shoulder or chopped veal
- Fatty pork
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread
- Butter
- Gravy made from meat sinews
- Food coloring
- Vegetables, browned potatoes, compote, or salad
- Macaroni
- Paper frills

## Directions

1. Use either shoulder or chopped veal finely scraped or ground once through the meat grinder; preferably half should be fatty pork.
2. When ground and collected with a knife, form it into the shape of cutlets.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and bread and roast in browned butter.
4. Pour gravy made from the meat sinews into the butter on the pan.
5. The sauce must be well reduced, mixed with food coloring and poured over the rissoles or served in a sauce bowl.
6. Serve with vegetables, browned potatoes, compote, or salad.
7. Small pieces of macaroni wrapped with a small piece of cut paper or paper frills can be inserted into the rissole after it is roasted as mock bones; these can be bought ready-made or can be easily cut oneself.
