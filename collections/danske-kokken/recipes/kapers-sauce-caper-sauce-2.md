## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Meat or fish stock
- Capers
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- White pepper
- Sugar
- Wine

## Directions

1. Bake butter and flour and thin with meat or fish stock to a white, smooth sauce
2. Add 2 tablespoons of capers
3. Bind with 2 egg yolks per 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) of sauce
4. Season to taste with salt, ground white pepper, sugar, and a little wine
