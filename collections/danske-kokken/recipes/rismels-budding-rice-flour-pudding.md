## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) melted margarine
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) rice flour
- 125 g (1/4 S) sweet, ground almonds
- 1 liter (1 pot) boiling milk
- 125 g (1/4 Q) powdered sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Bake the rice flour and sweet ground almonds in the melted margarine and dilute with 1 liter (1 pot) of boiling milk.
2. When the batter has cooled, stir it with the powdered sugar and 12 egg yolks, and finally add the stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. Serve the pudding with cream or fruit sauce. Sweet almonds may be replaced with a few bitter almonds.
