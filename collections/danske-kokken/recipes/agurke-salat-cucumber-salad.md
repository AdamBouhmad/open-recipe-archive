## Ingredients

- 2 medium cucumbers
- 2 spoonfuls oil
- 4 spoonfuls vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Let the cut cucumbers stand beforehand, sprinkled with salt, under a light press.
2. Mix together the oil, vinegar, salt and pepper and pour over the pressed cucumbers.
3. Sprinkle chopped parsley on the salad.
