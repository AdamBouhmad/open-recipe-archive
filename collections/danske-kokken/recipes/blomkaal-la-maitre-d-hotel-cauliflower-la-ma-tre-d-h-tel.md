## Ingredients

- cooked cauliflower
- butter
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place the cooked, steaming hot cauliflower in the center of a dish
2. On the edge of the dish place slices of lemon
3. On the lemon place small balls of butter made with a teaspoon and dipped in hot water
4. Roll the butter balls in finely chopped parsley
5. Arrange before serving the cauliflower
