## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 300 grams (60 quintals) margarine
- 175 grams (35 quintals) powdered sugar
- 6 tablespoons cream
- Cream for dipping
- Powdered sugar for coating
- Butter for greasing the pan

## Directions

1. Knead together the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, and cream
2. Roll the mixture into small rings
3. Dip them in cream and powdered sugar
4. Grease the baking sheet with butter and place the rings at some distance from each other on the sheet
