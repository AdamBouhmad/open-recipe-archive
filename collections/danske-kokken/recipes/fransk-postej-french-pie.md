## Ingredients

- butter
- breadcrumbs
- veal forcemeat
- cooked macaroni
- Parmesan cheese
- fricassee of turkey or hens
- short crust dough

## Directions

1. Grease the mold with cold butter and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
2. Fill the bottom and sides with veal forcemeat, then with a layer of cleaned, cooked, finely cut macaroni and a layer of Parmesan cheese.
3. Over this place a strong fricassee of turkey or hens.
4. Then cover the whole with macaroni and finally with a layer of short crust dough.
5. Bake the pie for a good hour in a hot oven.
6. Serve with mushroom sauce.
