## Ingredients

- zest of 1 lemon
- 1/4 pound white sugar
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 glasses white wine
- 1 glass water
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon potato flour

## Directions

1. Beat together the grated zest of 1 lemon and 1/4 pound white sugar with the juice of 2 lemons, 2 glasses white wine, 1 glass water, 4 egg yolks and 1 teaspoon potato flour.
2. With constant whisking bring to a boil until it froths.
3. Remove the sauce from heat and continue whisking until it is approximately cooled.
