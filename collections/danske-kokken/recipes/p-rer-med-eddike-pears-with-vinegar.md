## Ingredients

- 575 gr powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl vinegar
- 1/4 dl pear water
- 1/2 kg peeled pears

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and either halve them or cook whole as in recipe 70.
2. Boil sugar, vinegar, and water to a thin syrup in which the fruit is cooked for a few minutes, then removed and placed in a jar.
3. Cook the syrup until smooth before pouring it boiling over the pears.
4. Seal the preserve once it has cooled.
