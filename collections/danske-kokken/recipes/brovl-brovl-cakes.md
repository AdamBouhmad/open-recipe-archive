## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- half a pound powdered sugar
- 4 ounces butter
- 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 whole eggs
- good thick cream

## Directions

1. Blend together 1 pound flour, half a pound powdered sugar, 4 ounces butter, 1.5 teaspoons baking powder and 3 whole eggs.
2. Add as much good thick cream as it can take.
3. Roll out in whatever molds you wish.
4. Bake at even heat.
