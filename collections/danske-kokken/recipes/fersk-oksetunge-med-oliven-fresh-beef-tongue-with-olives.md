## Ingredients

- Fresh beef tongue
- Water
- Olives
- Butter
- Flour
- Coloring
- Tongue soup
- Madeira
- Maggi bouillon cubes (if needed)
- Roasted chestnuts (optional)
- Butter pastry strips (No. 34, optional)

## Directions

1. Place fresh beef tongue in boiling water
2. Skim and cook until tender and the skin comes off
3. Remove the root and cut the tongue into fairly thick slices
4. Lard one side of each slice with olives that have been peeled lengthwise and cut into strips, inserting them into the cooked slices
5. Make a good brown sauce from butter, flour, coloring and tongue soup, add Madeira and peeled olives
6. Strengthen the sauce with Maggi bouillon cubes if needed
7. Place the tongue slices in the sauce with the larded side up and bring to a boil
8. Arrange in a ring on the platter and pour the sauce over
9. Can be served with roasted chestnuts and butter pastry strips
