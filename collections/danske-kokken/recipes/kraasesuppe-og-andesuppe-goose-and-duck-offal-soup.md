## Ingredients

- Offal from 2 geese or 4 ducks
- 5 pots cold water
- Salt
- Soup vegetables and roots
- Peeled apple pieces
- Washed prunes
- A small piece of ginger
- Sugar
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the offal thoroughly, removing the thick inner membrane and cutting into 2 or 4 pieces
2. Clean the heads by splitting and removing the tongues, neck, and outermost wing joints, carefully plucking out fine feathers
3. Scald the feet and remove the outer skin
4. Clean the liver and heart
5. Place all cleaned and rinsed offal in a pot with 5 pots cold water and a little salt
6. Bring to boil and skim
7. Add soup vegetables and roots
8. Simmer until the offal is tender
9. Remove the offal and keep warm for serving with the soup
10. Remove the roots, cut into pieces, and place in the tureen
11. Strain the soup and return to heat for a boil with peeled apple pieces, some washed prunes, and a small piece of ginger
12. When the apples are tender, remove from heat and season with sugar, vinegar, and salt to taste
13. For duck soup, old ducks may also be used; one duck is usually sufficient for 6 portions
