## Ingredients

- 375 gr Demerara sugar
- ¾ dl vinegar
- prunes
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cover the prunes with boiling water and place on a sieve.
2. When sugar and vinegar are cooked to a thin syrup, add the prunes, but not more at once than can be ensured they do not break during cooking.
3. Cook only a couple of minutes, remove with a slotted spoon and spread on flat dishes until they have cooled and a glaze has formed over them; only then are they placed in the jar.
4. A little whole cinnamon is added to the liquid, which is cooked until smooth, which takes about 1 hour.
5. Treated in this way the prunes become excellent, and both pricking and drying are avoided.
6. If the liquid boils sufficiently long, it need not be reduced more often, and the prunes are sealed.
7. Should it happen that the liquid has become thin after some days, it is only the top that needs to be poured off and reduced.
