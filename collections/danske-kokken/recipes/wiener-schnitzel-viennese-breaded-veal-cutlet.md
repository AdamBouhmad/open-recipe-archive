## Ingredients

- veal cutlet from the inner side of shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- crushed zwieback
- butter
- stock
- lemon
- horseradish
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the inner side of the shoulder near to the bone in the shape and thickness of cutlets
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, brush with beaten eggs using a feather and turn in crushed zwieback
3. Fry to a light golden brown in a pan with lightly browned butter
4. Serve with a sauce made from the pan drippings thinned with stock
5. Place a lemon slice on top of each piece when serving and garnish the dish with grated horseradish
6. Serve with boiled, peeled potatoes
