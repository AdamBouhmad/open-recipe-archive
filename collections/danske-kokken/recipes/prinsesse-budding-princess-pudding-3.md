## Ingredients

- 36 quintal butter
- 36 quintal flour
- 36 quintal ground sugar
- 1 pot cooked sweet milk
- 20 egg yolks
- 20 egg whites
- salt
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Bake 36 quintal butter, 36 quintal flour, and 36 quintal ground sugar together and thin with 1 pot cooked sweet milk. When it has cooled, stir in 20 egg yolks, 2 at a time, and lastly stir in the 20 egg whites, beaten to a foam, gently. Then stir the mixture continuously for half an hour, pour into a pudding mold that is buttered and sprinkled with grated bread, and boil for 2 to 2.5 hours. You can also add a little salt and a little vanilla to the pudding, which is served with sweet fruit sauce.
