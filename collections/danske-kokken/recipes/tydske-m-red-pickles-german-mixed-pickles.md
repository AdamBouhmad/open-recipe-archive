## Ingredients

- Young fresh asparagus
- Small carrots
- Cauliflower heads
- Green beans
- Artichokes
- White beans
- Turkish wheat or corn
- Shallots
- Young parsley roots
- Green Spanish pepper pods
- Pearl onions
- Brussels sprouts
- Small cucumbers
- Very small whole decorative gourds
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Water
- Red pepper
- French tarragon vinegar
- Spices

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean all the pieces
2. Cut the asparagus into pieces, keep the carrots and parsley roots with a little tuft of heart leaves
3. Separate the cauliflower by the skin, trim the beans and cut into pieces if large
4. Slice the artichokes, scald the large beans and clean them by the skin
5. The corn should be the smallest, and the washed cucumbers are salted and placed in water overnight
6. Shell the shallots and pearl onions by the outer skin and free the Brussels sprouts of the outer loose leaves
7. Boil everything, each separately, with vinegar and water, first sprinkling well with salt, drain on a sieve
8. Arrange according to colors with strips of red Spanish pepper in glasses
9. Pour over with French tarragon vinegar, which has been heated with some spices and cooled, filling the glasses
10. Close with stoppers and paper, and dip in sealing wax
