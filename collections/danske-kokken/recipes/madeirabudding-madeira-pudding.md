## Ingredients

- 12 quintals isinglass dissolved in boiling water
- 1 stick vanilla
- 4 pounds white sugar
- 1 bottle Madeira
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Dissolve 12 quintals isinglass in boiling water and set aside to cool.
2. Add the cooled isinglass along with 1 stick vanilla, 4 pounds white sugar, 1 bottle Madeira, and 12 egg yolks (first whipped with a little of the wine) to the heat.
3. Bring to a boil while stirring constantly, then pour out.
4. When cold, stir in 1 pot cream whipped to foam.
5. Pour the whole into a porcelain mold greased with salad oil and let it stand until the pudding is stiff.
6. Serve with raspberry juice or mixed fruit juice.
