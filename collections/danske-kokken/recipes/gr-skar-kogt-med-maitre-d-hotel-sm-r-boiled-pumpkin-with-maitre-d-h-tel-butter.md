## Ingredients

- peeled and hollowed pumpkin
- salt
- lightly salted boiling water
- parsley or lettuce for garnish
- maitre d'hôtel butter

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled and hollowed pumpkin into long strips like asparagus
2. Strew them with salt and let them stand for 1 hour if possible
3. Rinse off the salt
4. Tie them into bundles and place on a rack
5. Lower the rack into a pot of boiling, lightly salted water
6. Cook for about 1 quarter hour until tender
7. Arrange the pumpkin on a napkin on a hot plate
8. Garnish with a little parsley or lettuce
9. Serve with maitre d'hôtel butter
