## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 125 gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) boiling water
- 6 sheets isinglass
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) white rum
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) cream for whipped topping

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks with powdered sugar
2. Dissolve isinglass in boiling water and add to egg mixture along with white rum
3. Fold in whipped cream
4. Pour into a mold, turn out when set, and serve decorated with whipped cream
