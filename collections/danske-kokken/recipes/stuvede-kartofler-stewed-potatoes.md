## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- Potatoes
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour and thin with milk until it becomes a proper thick sauce, which is prepared with salt and a little sugar. Add very small, cooked, peeled potatoes or larger ones, cut into 2 to 4 pieces. Heat the potatoes in the sauce. A little chopped parsley in the sauce is good for new potatoes. For all vegetables that are stewed in milk, it has a good effect if a little cold butter is stirred into the sauce just before serving.
