## Ingredients

- One pound cooked or roasted beef
- Half a quarter pound of butter
- Flour
- Four gills good broth
- One tablespoon vinegar
- Sugar
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Apples (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut beef into small cubes.
2. In half a quarter pound of butter, mix half as much flour.
3. Thin with 4 gills of good broth and one tablespoon of vinegar.
4. Add the beef and heat through.
5. Before serving, season the finker with sugar, salt, and ground pepper to taste.
6. Optionally, cook peeled, quartered apples in the broth before adding the beef for good flavor.
