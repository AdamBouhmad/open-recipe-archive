## Ingredients

- 2 Gallons Vinegar
- 1 Pint white Mustard Seeds
- 1 Ounce Allspice
- 3 Ounces Peppercorns
- 4 Ounces Ginger
- 1 Ounce Nutmeg Flower
- 1 Ounce Nutmeg
- 2 Ounces Cloves
- 2 Ounces Turmeric
- 1 Handful chopped Garlic
- 1 Handful grated Horseradish
- 4 Pounds Sugar
- 2 Ounces Celery Seeds
- 3 Lemons thinly sliced
- white Cabbage
- Brine
- Salt
- cold Vinegar
- soaked Turmeric

## Directions

1. Mix the vinegar, mustard seeds, allspice, peppercorns, ginger, nutmeg flower, nutmeg, cloves, turmeric, garlic, horseradish, sugar, celery seeds and sliced lemons together well and expose to the sun for 3 days
2. Remove the outer green leaves from the cabbage, cut into 4 pieces and cook for 3 minutes in a kettle with boiling brine
3. Remove and sieve and cover with a good layer of salt
4. Spread in the sun to dry
5. Shake off the salt and place in cold vinegar that has been colored with soaked turmeric
6. Let stand for 2 weeks
7. Then place in the spiced vinegar
8. Can be used after 5-6 weeks
