## Ingredients

- Veal or shank meat
- Cold margarine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Grated onion
- Chopped mushrooms or truffles (optional)

## Directions

1. Use either cut meat or shank meat, treating it the same as for beef forcemeat.
2. Instead of tallow, use the same quantity of cold margarine, which is chopped together with the meat.
3. Use salt, pepper and grated onion as seasonings; chopped mushrooms or truffles also have an excellent effect, for example for small balls for soup, for pasties etc.
