## Ingredients

- Roasted partridges
- Mushrooms
- Madeira
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the partridges as directed
2. When tender, strain the sauce
3. Simmer some mushrooms in this sauce
4. Season the sauce to taste with Madeira and a little lemon juice
