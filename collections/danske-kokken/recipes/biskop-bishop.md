## Ingredients

- 3 fresh bitter oranges
- 4 bottles red wine
- 13 pounds sugar
- 1 bottle Champagne
- 1 bottle soda water

## Directions

1. Peel the bitter oranges, place them with their peels in a bowl and pour 1 bottle of red wine over them. Let stand for 24 hours, then remove the fruit and pour the wine over sugar that has been dipped in water. Add the 3 remaining bottles of red wine and after 1 hour also add the Champagne and soda water.
