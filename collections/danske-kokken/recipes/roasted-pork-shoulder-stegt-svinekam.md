## Ingredients

- 2½ kg (5 lbs) pork shoulder, with all fat trimmed
- Salt
- Water
- Flour
- Coloring

## Directions

1. Wash the pork shoulder in cold water, sprinkle with salt, and place on a roasting pan, which is set in a hot oven.
2. Add boiling water to the pan, and baste the shoulder well during roasting, which lasts 1½ to 2 hours, depending on its thickness.
3. If roasting in a pot, it must be browned first.
4. Strain the broth, and in some of the skimmed fat, cook the flour, which is thinned with the broth to a suitable smooth sauce, colored with food coloring.
5. Serve with vegetables and preserves as mentioned for roast pork belly.
