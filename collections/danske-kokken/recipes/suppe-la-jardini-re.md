## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 pots) clear soup
- 4 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 3 leeks
- 1 celery
- 1 head of lettuce
- 50 g (10 drams) butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Small whole onions
- Cooked vegetables of the season: asparagus, peas, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, etc.
- Bread-croutons (recipe No. 500)

## Directions

1. Prepare as for the Julienne or Vegetable Soup
2. In addition to the vegetables, sauté some small whole onions together with the other vegetables
3. Add cooked seasonal vegetables such as asparagus, peas, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, etc. to the tureen
4. Serve with bread-croutons (recipe No. 500)
