## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 5 tablespoons milk
- egg whites
- butter
- powdered sugar
- rum

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with milk and stir in the foam from the beaten egg whites.
2. Melt a slice of butter on a pan and add the eggs.
3. The omelet, which should only be baked on one side, is folded together and sprinkled with a generous layer of powdered sugar.
4. Serve on a warm dish, pour a wine glass of rum over it and ignite. Set the omelet on fire at the table immediately.
