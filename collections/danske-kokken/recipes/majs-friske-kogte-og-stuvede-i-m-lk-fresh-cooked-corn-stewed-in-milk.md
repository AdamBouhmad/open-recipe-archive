## Ingredients

- Kernels from cooked corn cobs
- Cold sweet milk
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the kernels off the cooked cob.
2. Cover them with a little cold sweet milk and let them cook over a low fire until they thicken.
3. Stir in cold butter, salt and pepper.
4. Salted corn is treated in the same manner.
