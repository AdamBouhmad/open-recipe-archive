## Ingredients

- 13 pounds butter
- 4 pounds syrup
- 5 pounds flour
- 1/2 pound sweet almonds, sliced into fine strips
- zest of 2 lemons, chopped
- 5 quintal ground cloves
- 6 quintal potassium carbonate, dissolved in rose water
- 6 quintal chopped orange peel
- 8 quintal dried yeast, first dissolved in a cup of lukewarm milk
- 1 pound coarsely crushed candy sugar

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and give it just one boil with the syrup, then while it is still warm, stir in: the flour, sweet almonds in fine strips, the chopped lemon zest, ground cloves, potassium carbonate dissolved in rose water, chopped orange peel, and the dried yeast first dissolved in lukewarm milk. The entire mixture must be well stirred and thoroughly kneaded the evening before the cakes are to be baked, and stand in a warm place overnight. The next day, knead the dough again with the coarsely crushed candy sugar, and then bake the cakes very brown in the oven.
