## Ingredients

- 375 g dried apricots
- 4½ L water

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and soak it the day before it is to be cooked in the water in which it has been standing.
2. When the fruit is tender but not overcooked, remove some whole fruits to add to the soup; dust them with powdered sugar and place in the serving bowl.
3. Cook the remaining fruit. Strain the soup, sweeten it and thicken it with sago flour stirred in water.
4. Serve with rusks or baked French bread cubes.
