## Ingredients

- Cooked snails
- Sauce (as in previous recipe)
- Chopped parsley
- Lemon
- Salad leaves

## Directions

1. Serve the cooked snails in a sauce similar to the previous dish
2. The sauce should be velvety and not too thin
3. Arrange on a flat dish, in gratin shells or in small casseroles
4. Sprinkle the ragout with chopped parsley, which is then omitted from the sauce
5. Garnish with lemon and salad leaves
