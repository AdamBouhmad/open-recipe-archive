## Ingredients

- leftover veal roast
- old French bread
- sweet milk
- eggs
- mushrooms
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- sweet cream
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from leftover veal roast into cubes, which are mixed with old French bread, a little sweet milk, some eggs, mushrooms, a little vinegar, salt, pepper and 1 pint sweet cream.
2. After everything is well mixed, put it in a mold that has been greased with butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs.
3. Place the mold in a hot oven for an hour.
4. Serve with remoulade sauce.
