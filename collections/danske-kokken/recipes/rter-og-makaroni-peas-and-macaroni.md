## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked macaroni
- Green peas, fresh or preserved
- Pea water
- Butter
- Salt
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover cooked macaroni very finely and mix it with an equal portion of green peas, fresh or preserved.
2. Add some of the pea water, a slice of butter, salt, and finely chopped parsley.
3. Heat the vegetables through and serve with ham.
