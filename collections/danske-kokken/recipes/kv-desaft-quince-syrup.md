## Ingredients

- Quinces
- 575 gr (1/4 lb) powdered sugar per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. Dry the quinces and cut into very small pieces, cutting away the core.
2. Put them in a pot and pour on enough water so it is level with the fruit.
3. Boil the quinces until completely soft, then strain through a jelly cloth and let hang for several hours.
4. Measure the drained juice and boil with the sugar for a quarter hour.
5. Skim well during boiling; pour hot into dry bottles, which are tied with parchment paper as soon as they are filled.
6. The quince pulp left in the cloth is boiled in water and used for soup or similar dishes.
7. Quince syrup can be made into an excellent dessert by adding isinglass and filling into molds.
