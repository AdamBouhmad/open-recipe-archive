## Ingredients

- 6 pounds lean meat
- 6 pounds fresh beef from the inner loin
- 2 pounds salt
- 1½ ounces ground saltpeter
- ½ ounce ground pepper
- ½ ounce ground allspice
- ½ ounce ground cloves
- 3-4 pounds pork fat, cut into small cubes
- 5 bottles red wine
- Ox intestines

## Directions

1. Mince the lean meat and beef very finely
2. Place in a trough and knead with hands for ½ hour
3. Add 2 pounds salt, 1½ ounces ground saltpeter, ½ ounce ground pepper, ½ ounce ground allspice and ½ ounce ground cloves
4. Knead again for ½ hour and spread in the trough
5. Add 3-4 pounds pork fat cut into small cubes and pour in 5 bottles of red wine
6. Knead thoroughly
7. Use the widest and thickest ox intestines, cleaned and prepared like pork intestines
8. Sew one end closed and place in lukewarm water
9. Stuff very tightly so that no empty spaces remain
10. After stuffing, rub well with salt and keep for 24 hours
11. Then pack the sausage meat more tightly together and tie the end with twine to form a loop for hanging in smoke
12. Let lie another 24 hours, then dry off, wrap in thin paper and hang in smoke for 14 days
13. Then hang in a dry place
14. Do not make smoked sausages unless you are certain of having completely trichinosis-free meat
