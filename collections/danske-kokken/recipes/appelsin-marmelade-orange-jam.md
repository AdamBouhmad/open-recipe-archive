## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 2 fresh bitter oranges
- 1 lemon
- At least 1/4 kg powdered sugar per 1/2 kg fruit meat
- 1/4 liter of the last cooking water in which the fruit was boiled

## Directions

1. Cook the fruit in water until completely soft so it can easily be pierced with a straw or stick
2. Change the water 2 to 3 times during cooking, which lasts about one hour
3. Dry the fruit and cut the yellow peel as thin as possible, then cut into thread-fine strips
4. Remove the bitter white pith and cut the fruit open, carefully removing the seeds
5. Weigh the fruit meat with the juice
6. Cook sugar and water for 10 minutes, then add the orange mixture and cook for another 10 minutes
7. Finally add the finely cut peel and cook the jam for 10 minutes more until ready to pour into glasses and seal
8. The best time for making this jam is February, March, or April when fresh bitter oranges are available
