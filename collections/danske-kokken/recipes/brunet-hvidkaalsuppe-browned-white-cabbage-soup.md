## Ingredients

- Et Fjerdingspund Smør (a quarter pound of butter)
- 2 Spiseskefulde Puddersukker (2 tablespoons powdered sugar)
- Et ituskaaret, fast Hvidkaalshoved (a cut-up firm head of white cabbage)
- 5 Potter Suppe (5 pots of broth)
- Kjødboller (meatballs)

## Directions

1. Brown the butter with 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar until light brown.
2. Add the strips of cabbage (from a firm head with outer leaves removed) and brown under a lid, stirring carefully, until the cabbage is tender.
3. Gradually thin the cabbage with 5 pots of broth.
4. When the broth boils, top with meatballs.
