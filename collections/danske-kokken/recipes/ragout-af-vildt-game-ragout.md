## Ingredients

- Leftover roasted or boiled game
- Butter, 1/4 pound
- Onions, finely chopped
- Wheat flour, 2 tablespoons
- Wine, 1 pægel
- Vinegar, 1 wine glass
- Stock
- Sugar, a small piece
- Soup made from simple game meat (if using previously cooked meat)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a casserole.
2. Brown the finely chopped onions in it until tender.
3. Add 2 tablespoons of wheat flour, or as much as the butter will take.
4. Brown the whole strongly to a yellow color and thin it with a pægel of wine, a wine glass of vinegar, stock, a small piece of sugar, and if using simpler meat that was first boiled, the soup made from it.
5. After straining the sauce, give the meat a little boil in it and arrange it in the sauce.
