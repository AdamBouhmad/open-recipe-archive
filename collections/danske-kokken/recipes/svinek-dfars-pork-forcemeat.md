## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg pork
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 1/2 liter milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. The meat must be cut pork, pork tenderloin or small cuts; this is bought most cheaply and as a rule there is sufficient fat with the meat.
2. Prepare approximately 1 kg of forcemeat from 1/2 kg pork, 2 tablespoons potato flour, about 1/2 liter milk and 1 egg.
