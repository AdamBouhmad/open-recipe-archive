## Ingredients

- 1 pot white beer
- 2 pots milk
- 2 spoonfuls (10 drams) wheat flour
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pot of white beer and likewise 2 pots of milk in which 2 spoonfuls (10 drams) of wheat flour have been stirred.
2. Mix both parts together, add sugar to taste, give it a boil, and take it off the fire immediately so that it does not separate.
