## Ingredients

- Butter Dough
- Egg
- Filling of choice

## Directions

1. Roll out butter dough to approximately ½ centimeter thickness.
2. Cut out rounds using a pastry cutter or glass at least 6 centimeters (2½ inches) in diameter.
3. Place half the rounds on a baking sheet.
4. Cut the other half in the center with a small glass to form rings.
5. Brush the bases with egg and place the rings on top.
6. Place the small lids beside them on the baking sheet.
7. Brush both the pastry cases and lids with egg, being careful not to let egg run down the sides as this will prevent them from rising.
8. Bake at high heat until light brown.
9. Carefully remove the risen butter dough from the center of each pastry case to make room for filling.
10. Fill with the same fillings used for Krustader, which should be hot before filling.
11. Alternatively, omit the lids and place a mushroom or olive on top to suit the filling.
