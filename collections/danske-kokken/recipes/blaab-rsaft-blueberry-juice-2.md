## Ingredients

- Blueberries
- Powdered sugar or farina (1/2 kg or 1/2 lb powdered sugar or farina to 1 liter juice)

## Directions

1. Pick and rinse the berries and place them over the fire, stirring regularly so they do not burn on the bottom of the pot
2. When they release their juice well, remove the pot from the fire and press the berries through a sieve after they have cooled sufficiently
3. Measure the juice and bring it to a boil with the sugar, skim it and boil for 10 minutes
4. Pour it into dry bottles and seal them immediately with parchment paper
