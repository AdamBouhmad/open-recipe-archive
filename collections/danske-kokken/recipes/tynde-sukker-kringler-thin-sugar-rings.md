## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) flour
- 125 grams (1/4 lb) icing sugar
- 175 grams (35 quintal) margarine
- 1 egg
- 1 level teaspoon baking soda, dissolved in a little water
- a little ground cardamom

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Roll out the dough into very thin sticks and form as rings
3. Roll very flat with a cookie roller
4. Dip in egg mixed with cream, then in plenty of soft icing sugar
5. Place far apart on the baking sheet as they spread considerably
6. Bake until light brown and remove quickly from the sheet while still warm from the heat
7. Can also be sprinkled with caraway seeds
