## Ingredients

- 4 pounds clarified butter
- ¾ pound powdered sugar
- lemon zest, grated
- Ceylon cinnamon, ground
- 3 whole eggs
- 2 pounds wheat flour
- chopped sweet almonds
- coarsely ground white sugar

## Directions

1. Stir clarified butter with ¾ pound powdered sugar, grated lemon zest, and ground Ceylon cinnamon
2. Stir for half an hour with 3 whole eggs
3. Add 2 pounds wheat flour
4. Form a square mold from paper, grease it with butter, and place the dough in it to the thickness of a two-crown coin
5. Sprinkle with chopped sweet almonds and coarsely ground white sugar
6. Bake at even heat for half an hour until light brown
7. Cut into small square cakes
