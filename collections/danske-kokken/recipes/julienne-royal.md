## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 pots) clear soup or boiling water with Maggis Bouillon cubes
- 4 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 3 leeks
- 1 celery
- 1 head of lettuce
- 50 g (10 drams) butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Egg-royal (recipe No. 507)

## Directions

1. Prepare the soup as for the Julienne or Vegetable Soup
2. Serve with Egg-royal (recipe No. 507)
