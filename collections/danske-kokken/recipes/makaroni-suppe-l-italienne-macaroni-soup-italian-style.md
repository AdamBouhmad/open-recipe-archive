## Ingredients

- 200 gr thin macaroni
- 5 L soup stock
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter raw cream
- 75 gr grated Parmesan cheese
- ground white pepper
- Madeira or Graves wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the thin macaroni until tender in half of the soup for approximately 1/2 hour
2. Drain the macaroni and once cooled, cut it into very small pieces
3. Mix the soup in which the macaroni was cooked with the remaining soup and return the macaroni to it
4. As soon as it boils, lift the pot from the heat
5. Beat 6 egg yolks with 1/4 liter raw cream, 75 gr grated Parmesan cheese and a little ground white pepper
6. Add this mixture to the soup while stirring vigorously
7. Set the pot back over the heat for a couple of minutes while continuing to stir, but do not let it boil
8. Season with Madeira or Graves wine if desired
9. Serve immediately
