## Ingredients

- 2 Pints sweet cream
- 3 Eggs
- A little powdered sugar
- Fine wheat flour
- 5 Pints melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat 2 pints sweet cream in a bowl with 3 eggs and a little powdered sugar.
2. Beat in so much fine wheat flour that the batter becomes suitably thick.
3. Stir in 5 pints melted butter.
4. Set the pan over the heat to become really warm through.
5. Grease with butter, but only for the first cake.
6. Serve as hot as possible with preserves.
