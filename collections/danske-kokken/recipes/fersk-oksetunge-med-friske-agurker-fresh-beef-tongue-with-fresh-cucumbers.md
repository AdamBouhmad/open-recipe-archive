## Ingredients

- Cooked beef tongue
- Brown sauce
- Paprika
- Glass of Sherry
- Fresh cucumbers, peeled and hollowed

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked beef tongue and arrange it together again in its original form
2. In a brown sauce base, add paprika, a glass of Sherry and thin slices of fresh peeled and hollowed cucumbers
3. Bring to a boil in the sauce
