## Ingredients

- Cleaned leeks
- Butter
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned leeks into smaller pieces and give them a good boil in salted water
2. Drain the water from them
3. Place a good slice of butter in the pot
4. Steam the leeks in this under a lid and over gentle heat
5. Arrange them with no other addition than salt; this is a tasty vegetable
