## Ingredients

- Fine, good peas
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the peas with a little fine salt, let stand until the next day and then fill into dry bottles, which are corked and placed in a dry spot
2. When they are to be used, cook them a little in plenty of water, which is poured off completely, and they are cooked tender in scant water, in which they are stewed
