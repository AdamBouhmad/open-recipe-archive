## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onion
- Anchovies
- Salt
- Pepper
- Curry or other spices
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Brown 2 ounces of butter (or more if the meat is lean).
2. Cook a grated onion in it first.
3. Then add 2 anchovies, scraped from the bones and chopped with a knife.
4. Season with a little salt, pepper or a little curry.
5. Add the juice of half a lemon.
