## Ingredients

- 125 grams flour
- 100 grams margarine
- 25 grams powdered sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- Cream filling
- apple compote
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Knead together flour, margarine, powdered sugar, and egg yolk
2. Roll out into a thin sheet and bake on paper cut to the size of the dish
3. Cover this base with a layer of cream
4. Over that a layer of apple compote and again cream
5. Before serving, decorate with whipped cream tops
