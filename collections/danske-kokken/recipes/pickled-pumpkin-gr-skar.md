## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- salt
- spices as for pickled cucumbers (recipe 232)
- raw vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkin and cut it into long pieces of the same shape and thickness as pickled cucumbers
2. Scrape out the seeds with a silver spoon
3. Sprinkle the pumpkin with salt and let it stand until the next day
4. Remove it and dry it well before layering it with spices in the jar
5. Use the same spices as for pickled cucumbers (recipe 232)
6. Pour raw vinegar over the pumpkin and bind the jar with parchment paper
7. The vinegar can be boiled as for pickled cucumbers, but the pumpkin often becomes soft over the winter
