## Ingredients

- 3 pairs of veal sweetbreads
- Filling for Krustader

## Directions

1. Clean, blanch, and cook the sweetbreads in the same manner as described in recipe 6.
2. Prepare the filling as for Krustader, preferably cutting the sweetbreads into slightly larger pieces.
3. Garnish the vol au vent filled with sweetbreads with mushrooms.
