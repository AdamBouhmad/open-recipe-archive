## Ingredients

- celery
- water or stock
- meatballs made from forcemeat
- butter
- flour
- strained stock
- 2 egg yolks
- ground white pepper
- 1 glass sherry

## Directions

1. Peel the celery, rinse well and cut into small cubes
2. Boil the celery cubes in water or stock
3. When the celery is cooked, remove it
4. Boil the meatballs in the stock
5. For the sauce, brown butter and flour, thin with the strained stock and bind at the end with a couple of egg yolks; add ground white pepper and a glass of sherry
6. Serve with puff pastry strips
