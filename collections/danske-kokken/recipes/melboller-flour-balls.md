## Ingredients

- 125 grams (1/4 pound) margarine
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) Pillsbury flour
- 2.5 deciliters (1 good pail) boiling water
- 5 egg yolks
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cardamom
- Egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Melt the margarine and bake the flour in it
2. Add the boiling water gradually
3. When the dough has cooled, stir in 5 egg yolks, a little salt, sugar, and cardamom
4. Finally fold in the beaten egg whites
5. Immediately drop spoonfuls into boiling salted water
6. Cook only very gently for a moment
7. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain
8. Place in a tureen
9. The balls can be cooked several hours ahead and reheated just before serving by giving them a quick boil in the soup or water
