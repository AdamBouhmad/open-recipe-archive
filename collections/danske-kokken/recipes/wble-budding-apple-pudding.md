## Ingredients

- 3 pounds apples
- A little water
- Lemon zest and cinnamon
- Sugar
- Half a pound wheat bread
- Milk
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter
- 4 egg yolks
- A good half cup sugar
- A little bitter almonds, blanched and finely chopped
- Cardamom and cinnamon
- Egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the apples in a little water with lemon zest and cinnamon
2. Press through a sieve and sweeten with sugar, but not too sweet
3. Grate half a pound of wheat bread and mix with milk, not too thin
4. Add clarified butter, egg yolks, sugar, almonds, cardamom, and cinnamon
5. When well mixed, add the apple puree and finally the beaten egg whites
6. Cook for 1.5 hours
7. Serve with sugar
8. Instead of apples, gooseberries or rhubarb stalks can be used
