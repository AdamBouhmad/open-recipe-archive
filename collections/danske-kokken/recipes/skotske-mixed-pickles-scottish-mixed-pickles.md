## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Small heads of Brussels sprouts
- Very small young carrots
- Young beans
- Green beans, shelled
- Small cucumbers
- Nasturtium seeds
- Radish skins
- Pearl onions
- Small shallots
- Whole lemons
- Horseradish
- Pepper
- Tarragon leaves
- Dill
- Bay leaves
- Wine vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the first five items, each in its own way
2. Divide the carrots only once, leave the beans whole
3. Boil everything quite soft in water with some salt, but not too soft
4. Drain on a sieve
5. Wash the cucumbers the evening before, sprinkle with salt and dry the next day
6. Arrange everything alternately in square glass jars with wide mouths, layer by layer with spices between
7. Fill the glasses with hot and cooled wine vinegar
