## Ingredients

- Beef
- Strong gravy sauce
- Capers
- Wine
- Butter dough strips

## Directions

1. In a well-made, strong gravy sauce, add capers and wine.
2. Pour over the beef at serving.
3. Serve with butter dough strips.
