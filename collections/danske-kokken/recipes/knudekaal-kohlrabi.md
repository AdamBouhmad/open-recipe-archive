## Ingredients

- Kohlrabi
- Water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi thickly, removing all the woody parts as these are inedible, indigestible, and cannot be made tender.
2. Cut lengthwise like asparagus.
3. Tie in bundles and place over heat in boiling water with salt.
4. The kohlrabi must boil for at least 1 hour.
5. Arrange and serve like asparagus with stirred butter.
