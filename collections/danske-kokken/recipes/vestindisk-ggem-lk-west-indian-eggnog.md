## Ingredients

- 4 liters semi-skimmed milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 0.25 kg powdered sugar
- 5 to 6 teaspoons cornstarch
- 2.5 deciliters white rum
- vanilla essence
- additional rum and sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks white with 0.25 kg powdered sugar
2. Bring the milk to a boil
3. Mix 5 to 6 teaspoons cornstarch in 2.5 deciliters white rum
4. Pour the boiling milk over the egg mixture while whisking continuously
5. Add vanilla essence to taste, and more rum and sugar if needed
6. Serve with small rusks
