## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- 1 Kg (2 lb) peas
- Butter or fat from chicken broth
- Flour
- Chicken broth
- Pea cooking water
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and blanch the chickens, then cook until tender.
2. Leave them in the broth to cool (removing them immediately makes the meat dry).
3. When cold, cut the chicken meat into small pieces without bones.
4. Use the butter or fat skimmed from the chicken broth to make the sauce.
5. Make a sauce with flour and dilute with broth and pea water to make a good fricassée sauce.
6. Bind the sauce with 4 egg yolks for this portion.
7. Make enough sauce to fill the sauce boats before heating the chicken meat in it.
8. Arrange the fricassée high in the center of each small vol au vent and garnish with peas on top.
9. If using canned peas, simply heat the can in boiling water. It is unnecessary to sauté the peas in butter for this dish.
