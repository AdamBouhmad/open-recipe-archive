## Ingredients

- Cooked, chopped spinach
- Cold butter
- Salt
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Place the cooked, chopped spinach in a pot and bring to a boil so that the water attached to it can evaporate.
2. Stir in cold butter, salt, and a little sugar.
3. Serve the spinach immediately.
