## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Butter
- Sugar
- Stock or water

## Directions

1. Scald them, boil a little, and peel off the shell and the brown skin. Brown butter and sugar together on a frying pan, and add the chestnuts, which must be shaken regularly to avoid burning. As soon as they begin to become glazed, add a little stock to the pan, in which they are steamed tender, until the liquid has reduced and formed a glaze around them. Use as garnish around meat dishes.
