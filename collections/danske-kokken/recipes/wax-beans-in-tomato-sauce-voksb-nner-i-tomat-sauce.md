## Ingredients

- wax beans or green beans
- butter
- flour
- meat broth or cooking water from beans
- tomato purée
- salt
- pepper
- Maggis bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Rub, rinse the beans, and if small cook them whole, otherwise cut them once
2. Brown butter and flour together and thin with meat broth or the water in which the beans were cooked
3. Stir tomato purée, salt, pepper, and Maggis bouillon cubes into the sauce to taste
4. Pour the sauce over the warm beans or turn the beans in the sauce without breaking them
