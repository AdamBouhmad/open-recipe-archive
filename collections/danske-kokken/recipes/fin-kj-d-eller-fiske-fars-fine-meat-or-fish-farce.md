## Ingredients

- 1 pound scraped meat or fish
- 15 quints marrow (for meat fars) or 15 quints butter (for fish fars)
- 4 beaten egg whites
- 2 pails sweet cream

## Directions

1. Work the meat or fish very strongly, chop it and pass through a fine colander together with marrow (for meat) or butter (for fish)
2. Stir the farce with beaten egg whites and sweet cream
