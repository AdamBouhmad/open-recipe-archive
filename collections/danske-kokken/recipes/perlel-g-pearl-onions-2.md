## Ingredients

- Small onions
- Cold water
- Cold brine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Scald the onions, peel and pour cold water over them
2. Place in jars and fill with the mentioned cold brine with a small sugar addition
3. Cook for a good half hour and cool
