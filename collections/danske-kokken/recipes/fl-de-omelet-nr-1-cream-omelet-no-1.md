## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup clarified butter
- 0.25 pint cream or good milk
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Bake flour in clarified butter
2. Gradually add cream or milk and cook with strong stirring and beating with spoon
3. Cool slightly
4. Stir strongly with egg yolks and vanilla extract
5. Finally fold in beaten egg whites
6. Bake in oven for about 3 quarters of an hour
7. Serves 8 to 10 persons
