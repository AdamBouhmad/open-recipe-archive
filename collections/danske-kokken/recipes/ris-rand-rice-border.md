## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram rice
- 2 liters milk
- 125 grams butter
- 4 eggs
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Cook the rice until approximately tender in 2 liters of milk
2. While the porridge is still warm, stir in the butter, eggs and salt
3. Brush a flat dish with a broad rim with egg white
4. Form the border on it and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
5. Bake for approximately 1/4 hour at good top heat
6. The dough can also be filled into a ring form that is buttered and sprinkled with breadcrumbs
7. Bake in the oven and turn out onto a dish
8. Fill and serve immediately
