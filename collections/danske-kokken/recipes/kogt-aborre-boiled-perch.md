## Ingredients

- Perch
- Salt
- Parsley
- Boiled potatoes
- Melted butter
- Grated horseradish
- Parsley sauce

## Directions

1. Scrape off the scales using a grater, as they adhere firmly
2. Clean the fish and cut off the fins, and after rinsing well, sprinkle it with salt until it is cooked
3. Place in cold water with salt and a bunch of parsley
4. Cook until the fins begin to loosen
5. Serve with boiled potatoes and melted butter with grated horseradish or parsley sauce made from the cooking liquid
