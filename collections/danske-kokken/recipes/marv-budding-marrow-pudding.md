## Ingredients

- Ingredients as for bread pudding
- 1/2 pound marrow, cut into fairly large pieces

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter the same way as for bread pudding, but with only 4 ounces butter
2. When poured into the mold, distribute 1/2 pound marrow, cut into fairly large pieces, throughout
3. Serve with red wine sauce or another sweet sauce
