## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 36 grams butter
- 18 grams light powdered sugar
- 2 eggs
- 4 to 5 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Knead 1 pound flour, 36 grams butter, 18 grams light powdered sugar, and 2 eggs well with 4 to 5 tablespoons water.
2. Roll out and bake as described for rye crackers.
