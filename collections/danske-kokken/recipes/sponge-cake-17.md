## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 2¾ cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks and whites
2. Stir 2¾ cups granulated sugar into the yolks
3. Add 1 cup boiling water, then 3 cups flour and 1 teaspoon baking powder sifted with the flour
4. Fold in the beaten egg whites last
