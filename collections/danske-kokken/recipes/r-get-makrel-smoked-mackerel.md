## Ingredients

- Smoked mackerel
- Butter

## Directions

1. Pat the smoked mackerel dry with a damp cloth
2. Cut into pieces
3. Toast in butter in a pan
4. Place pieces on a platter with skin side up
5. Reassemble in the fish's original form
6. Garnish with salad or parsley
