## Ingredients

- small fresh or cooked mushrooms
- butter
- flour
- lemon juice
- bouillon
- egg yolks
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cook small cleaned fresh or cooked mushrooms together with butter, a little flour, and lemon juice.
2. Thin with bouillon.
3. After bringing to a boil, thicken the sauce with a few egg yolks and sweet cream.
