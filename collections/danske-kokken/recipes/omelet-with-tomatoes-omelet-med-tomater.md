## Ingredients

- Large, ripe tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut large ripe tomatoes into slices and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Dip them in egg and bread and fry in plenty of butter in the pan.
3. Pour the egg mixture over the tomatoes.
4. This omelet must be baked on both sides.
