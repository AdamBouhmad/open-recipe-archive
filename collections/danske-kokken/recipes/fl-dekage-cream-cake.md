## Ingredients

- 3 pints thick sweet or sour cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 8 egg whites, beaten
- a little ground Ceylon cinnamon
- a little grated lemon zest
- clarified butter
- a little raspberry or currant juice (optional)
- powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Beat 3 pints thick sweet or sour cream to stiff foam, and stir in 8 egg yolks which have first been beaten well with 4 tablespoons powdered sugar and then with 4 tablespoons flour.
2. Finally stir in the 8 beaten egg whites together with a little ground Ceylon cinnamon and a little grated lemon zest.
3. Grease a dish well with clarified butter and pour in the dough, then bake for about an hour.
4. If you wish, you can place a little raspberry or currant juice in the bottom of the dish.
5. Serve warm, well sprinkled with powdered sugar.
