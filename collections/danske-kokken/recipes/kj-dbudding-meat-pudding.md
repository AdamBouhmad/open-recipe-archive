## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. beef, chopped
- 4.5 lbs. kidney fat
- a little marrow
- salt
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 to 3 eggs
- a considerable amount of onion
- a little sweet milk
- grated nutmeg
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Scrape the kidney fat and marrow free of sinews and chop very finely with a little salt.
2. Add 4 tablespoons flour, 2 to 3 eggs, and a considerable amount of onion, and chop together until quite fine and cohesive.
3. Stir well with a little sweet milk and a little grated nutmeg.
4. Grease a pudding mold with butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and put the mixture in it to cook for 2 hours.
5. Serve with a strong sauce of meat broth with a little Madeira or caper sauce.
