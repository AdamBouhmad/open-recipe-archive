## Ingredients

- Onions
- Fat from a roasted pork roast
- Cooked pork shank, shredded
- Flour
- Pepper
- Salt
- Mustard
- Coloring
- Cooked potatoes, small diced
- Meat, shredded

## Directions

1. Brown onions in a pan with the fat from a roasted pork roast and the shredded cooked pork shank
2. Mix in flour
3. Dilute to make a smooth sauce
4. Season with pepper, salt, and a little mustard
5. Give it color
6. Add small diced cooked potatoes
7. Let the shredded meat cook through in the sauce
