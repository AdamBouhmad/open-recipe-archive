## Ingredients

- 12 Qvint melted butter
- 12 Qvint sugar
- 24 Qvint flour
- 6 Eggs
- 30 Tablespoonfuls milk

## Directions

1. Stir the melted butter with the sugar.
2. Add the flour, eggs, and finally 30 tablespoonfuls of milk.
3. Place 1 tablespoonful of the batter on the pan for each pancake.
