## Ingredients

- 5 to 6 kg veal back, trimmed like venison back
- Butter or claret
- Thin cream, broth, or water
- Salt
- Fresh or preserved vegetables
- Flowers, salad, or parsley for decoration

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs so they are the same length and not visible beyond the fillet.
2. Remove the skin and lard the back in tight strips, then blanch.
3. Place butter or claret on a roasting pan in a hot oven to brown, then lay the back with the larded side up.
4. Sprinkle with salt and cover with thin cream, broth, or water, basting frequently during roasting, which takes about one hour.
5. When carving, loosen the fillet on both sides of the spine and place on a carving board to slice crosswise into smaller pieces, then reassemble on the back to restore its original shape.
6. Decorate with a decorative skewer through a bouquet of flowers, salad, or parsley, and garnish along the sides with fresh or preserved vegetables.
