## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) cream of the best sort
- Liqueur or vanilla (optional)
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Keep the cream on ice or in a cold place until it is to be whipped.
2. This is done most easily when the cream is in a jug or pail, which is either placed on ice or in cold water.
3. Whip it with a cream whisk to very stiff foam.
4. Add liqueur or vanilla and a little sugar as desired.
5. Use as decoration around cheese, cold puddings and cooked fruit, or serve separately.
