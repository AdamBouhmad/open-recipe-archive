## Ingredients

- small firm brussels sprout heads
- salt
- water
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the small firm heads and discard any loose leaves
2. Cook the heads tender in boiling salted water
3. Lay them for a moment on a colander and shake them while still warm with melted butter
4. Serve warm with salt dishes, or they can also be cooked in soup with a little butter
