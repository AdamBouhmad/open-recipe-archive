## Ingredients

- 3 pounds butter
- 1 pound wheat flour
- almost 3 quarts boiling water
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt 3 pounds butter
2. Bake 1 pound wheat flour in it until it comes off the spoon easily
3. Gradually add almost 3 quarts boiling water, so the porridge blends well between each time it is stirred, and with vigorous stirring so it does not become lumpy
4. The porridge will be very thin after this amount of water, but it will thicken when served, which must happen as soon as it is cooked
5. Add salt last
6. Serves 8 to 9 persons
