## Ingredients

- Leftover chicken meat
- Cooked celery
- Chicken carcasses
- Watercress for garnish
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pick the meat from the bones and cut it very fine together with a cooked celery
2. Boil the carcasses to make the sauce, prepared exactly as for Fish Salad (No. 278)
3. Garnish the salad with watercress and slices of hard-boiled eggs
