## Ingredients

- 2 pints red wine
- water
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- 2 cardamom pods
- half pound white sugar
- 4 tablespoons potato starch
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Mix 2 pints of red wine with a little water and cook it with a little lemon zest, cinnamon and 2 cardamom pods.
2. Let it cook well through, strain it and set it again over the heat with half a pound of white sugar.
3. When it boils, dissolve 4 tablespoons of potato starch in a little water and whip it into the boiling mixture, after which it is poured onto a dish to become cold.
4. It is garnished with whipped cream, which is laid in strips over the cream and served with biscuits.
