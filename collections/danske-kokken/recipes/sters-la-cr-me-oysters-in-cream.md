## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 1 pint cream
- flour
- cold milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place the oysters in an enameled pot and boil until the oyster liquid begins to flow out.
2. Strain the oysters and place the cream over the fire.
3. When the cream is nearly boiling, thicken it with flour mixed in a little cold milk.
4. Add the oysters and boil for five minutes.
5. Serve hot with toast.
