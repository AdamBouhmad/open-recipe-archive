## Ingredients

- grated rye bread
- butter
- sugar
- apple sauce or jam
- whipped cream
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Bake grated rye bread with butter and sugar in a pan and spread on a flat dish while still warm.
2. Layer with apple sauce or jam, then another layer of bread.
3. Cover completely with whipped cream and decorate with currant jelly.
