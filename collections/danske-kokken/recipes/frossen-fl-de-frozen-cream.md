## Ingredients

- Whipped cream
- Vanilla
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Sweeten the whipped cream and add a little vanilla.
2. Freeze it.
3. Serve in slices from the mold with a spoon dipped in warm water.
4. Arrange around a whole carved and folded pineapple and sprinkle with candied violets.
5. Frozen cream is eaten with fresh strawberries.
