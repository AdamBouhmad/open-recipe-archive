## Ingredients

- 2½ Kg (5 S) lamb breast
- Salt
- Broth bundle

## Directions

1. From a lamb forequarter, remove the brisket, cut off the chops, and break and remove the breastbones along with the brisket in smaller pieces.
2. Wash the pieces well and place them over heat in cold water with salt and a broth bundle.
3. Skim well and cook tender in the course of about an hour.
4. Boiled lamb meat, especially the leg, is excellent served with cream horseradish sauce (see Nr. 798).
