## Ingredients

- 5 egg whites
- 3 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
- ½ pound sweet almonds
- 1 lemon, finely grated peel
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Stir in 3 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
3. Add ½ pound sweet almonds, half finely ground and half sliced
4. Add the finely grated peel of 1 lemon
5. Grease a warm baking sheet with butter
6. When cool, spread the batter very thinly over the sheet
7. Insert into the oven and bake
8. When removed, cut immediately while still warm into small cakes
