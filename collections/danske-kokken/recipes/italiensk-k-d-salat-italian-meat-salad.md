## Ingredients

- Cooked, finely chopped macaroni
- beets
- carrots
- potatoes
- canned asparagus
- salt herring
- cooked smoked ham
- veal
- lost mayonnaise (see recipe 244)
- mustard
- capers
- pepper
- tarragon vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Use equal parts of each ingredient: cooked finely chopped macaroni, beets, carrots, potatoes, canned asparagus, salt herring, cooked smoked ham, and veal
2. Toss in lost mayonnaise seasoned with mustard, capers, pepper, tarragon vinegar, and oil to taste
