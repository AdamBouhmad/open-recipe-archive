## Ingredients

- large onions
- salt
- water
- butter
- sugar
- milk or cream
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil large onions in salt and water, chop or slice them, and put them in melted butter with a little more salt and 1 tablespoon sugar.
2. Cook while stirring and thinning with milk or cream.
3. If desired, the sauce can be thickened with a little flour and a little ground pepper added.
