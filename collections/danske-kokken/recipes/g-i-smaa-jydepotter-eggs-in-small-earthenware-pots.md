## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Parmesan cheese
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Butter the earthenware pots
2. Pour an egg into each one
3. Sprinkle with a little salt, white pepper, and grated Parmesan cheese if desired
4. Place the pots in a hot oven in a baking pan with boiling water poured in
5. When the eggs are set, serve immediately
6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley
