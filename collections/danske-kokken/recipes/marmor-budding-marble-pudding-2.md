## Ingredients

- 1 Bottle white wine
- 12 Ounces white sugar
- Juice of 3 lemons and zest of 2 lemons
- 1.5 Ounces gelatine or isinglass
- 20 Egg yolks
- 3 Teaspoons vanilla
- 0.5 Pint Madeira wine
- 1 Pint sweet cream, whipped to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Cook the white wine with the white sugar, lemon juice, and zest and gelatine until dissolved
2. Remove from heat and strain if necessary
3. Mix with the egg yolks, which have been beaten white
4. Return to heat and stir constantly until it becomes thick
5. Add the vanilla and Madeira wine
6. Cool, stirring occasionally, and when nearly cold, fold in the whipped cream
7. Pour into the mold
