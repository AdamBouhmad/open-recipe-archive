## Ingredients

- cod fish
- butter
- flour
- fish stock
- egg yolks
- white pepper, ground
- salt
- wine (Sherry or Graves)
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The fish is cooked and picked from the bones in larger pieces while still warm.
2. Butter and flour are baked and thinned with fish stock to a fairly smooth sauce, which is bound with a couple of egg yolks.
3. To this is added ground white pepper, salt, wine (either Sherry or Graves) and a little lemon juice.
4. The mixture is placed in the shells and baked. Salmon, pike and halibut can be prepared in a similar manner.
