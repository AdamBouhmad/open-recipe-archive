## Ingredients

- Zest of 5 oranges
- ½ pound white sugar
- Juice of 5 oranges
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 2 pints water
- 2 pints white wine
- 12 quarters dissolved isinglass
- 3 to 4 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 5 oranges on ½ pound white sugar.
2. Strain the juice of 5 oranges and 2 lemons and pour into a saucepan with 2 pints water, 2 pints white wine, 12 quarters dissolved isinglass, and 3 to 4 beaten egg whites.
3. Set over a brisk heat and bring to a boil, lift off for a moment, bring to a boil again, and repeat this a third time, and after skimming it should appear clear.
4. Pour it through a filter bag to clarify and fill what drains into clear glass bowls, which are set on ice or in a cold place.
