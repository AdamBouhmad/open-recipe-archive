## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 4 ounces butter
- 7 ounces wheat flour
- 1 ounce sugar
- 5 bitter almonds
- 3 pints milk

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil. Add the sugar, butter and crushed almonds.
2. Whip the porridge until it leaves the casserole, cool and then work in the eggs.
3. Dip a spoon in water; use it to take balls from the dough and place them in boiling water.
4. Serve in boiled milk.
