## Ingredients

- cod
- salt
- cold water
- vinegar
- boiled potatoes
- melted butter or mustard sauce or sour butter sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the cod thoroughly with salt and cold water
2. If the liver is beautifully white and the roe is firm, cook both with the fish
3. Cut the cleaned fish into suitable pieces, sprinkle with salt, and let it rest for a quarter hour
4. Place it in boiling water with salt and a couple of spoonfuls of vinegar
5. Cook thoroughly, but not so much that the meat falls from the bones
6. Remove carefully and arrange on a platter
7. Serve with freshly peeled boiled potatoes and melted butter, or mustard sauce, or sour butter sauce
8. The head is considered the best piece
