## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) butter dough
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) grated Parmesan or half Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. Roll out the dough thinly
2. Sprinkle with cheese; thus roll out, sprinkle and layer it 3 to 4 times
3. Ramequins can be cut in any shape desired, as half-moons, round or elongated
4. Place on a baking sheet, brush with egg and bake a few minutes in a hot oven until light brown
5. Arrange in a pyramid on a napkin as hot as possible
6. Serve with soup or instead of cheese course
