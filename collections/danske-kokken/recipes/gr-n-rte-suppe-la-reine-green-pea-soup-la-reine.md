## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls peas
- 5 L (5 pots) chicken stock
- 2 chickens
- 100 grams (20 quintal) butter
- 50 grams (10 quintal) flour
- Salt
- Sugar
- Fine chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare pea purée as in recipe 394.
2. Cook 2 chickens tender.
3. Pick meat from bones and cut into small, even pieces; keep warm until serving.
4. Brown 100 grams butter and 50 grams flour and dilute with chicken stock, adding the pea purée and bringing to a boil.
5. Season with salt, sugar, and finely chopped parsley.
6. Pour into tureen over the small cut chicken filets.
