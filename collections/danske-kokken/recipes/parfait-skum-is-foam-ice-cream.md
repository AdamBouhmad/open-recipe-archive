## Ingredients

- 1 L (1 Pot) cream or foam
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make stiffly whipped cream, and blend this foam into the well-stirred egg yolks and sugar.
2. Rinse an ordinary pudding or ice cream mold.
