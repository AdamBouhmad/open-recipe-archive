## Ingredients

- Leftover game roast
- Butter, 1/4 pound
- Onions, chopped
- Pepper
- Flour
- Stock, preferably from game
- Sherry, 1 glass
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover game roast into very small cubes.
2. Brown the butter and brown chopped onions in it with a little pepper.
3. Thicken with flour and thin with stock, preferably game stock, and a glass of sherry.
4. When the sauce is ready, strain it over the meat, which is arranged garnished with browned potatoes.
