## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg (3 S) kale
- 4 L (4 pots) broth
- 4 egg yolks
- cooked, cut kale

## Directions

1. The cut kale is cooked so tender that it is easily rubbed through a sieve (at least 1.5 hours), poured back into the pot, brought to a boil and bound with 4 egg yolks.
2. Cooked, cut kale can be placed in the tureen.
