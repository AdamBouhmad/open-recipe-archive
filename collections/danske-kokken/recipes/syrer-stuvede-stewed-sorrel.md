## Ingredients

- sorrel
- flour
- butter
- bouillon
- spinach (optional)

## Directions

1. The treatment is nearly the same as for spinach (No. 537), but stew with flour, butter and bouillon
2. If they are very sour, they can be mixed with a little spinach
