## Ingredients

- 4-5 L (4.5-5.5 pots) soup
- 0.5 kg (1 lb) fresh mushrooms
- Water
- Salt
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter
- Lemon juice
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour
- 6 egg yolks
- Rhenish wine
- Bread croutons

## Directions

1. Select only small closed fresh mushrooms
2. Rinse with a woolen cloth in salted water and pour boiling water over them, allowing them to stand for a quarter hour
3. Discard the water and place the mushrooms in 0.5 L (0.5 pot) boiling water with 125 g (1/4 lb) butter and a little lemon juice
4. Give them one boil
5. Skim the butter and brown 125 g (1/4 lb) flour in it, which is diluted with the soup and the water in which the mushrooms have been cooked
6. Thicken the soup with 6 egg yolks, add a little Rhenish wine and serve with bread croutons arranged on a napkin on a platter
