## Ingredients

- beet greens
- chicory
- cowslips
- dandelion
- sorrel
- horseradish tops
- turnip greens
- salt
- soda
- cold water

## Directions

1. Thoroughly clean the greens in several changes of cold water, after removing roots, dry stems and similar
2. Cook them in salted water with a little soda added
3. When they are tender, pour off the boiling water and dry them in a sieve, pressing the water out with a spoon or similar
