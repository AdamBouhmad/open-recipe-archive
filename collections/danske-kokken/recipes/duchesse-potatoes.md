## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes and press through a coarse sieve.
2. Mix them with a piece of butter, salt, very little white pepper, a little grated nutmeg and two egg yolks.
3. Spread the dough on a plate and shape with a knife into small cakes two inches long and one inch wide.
4. Place them on a buttered plate, brush on top with egg white and place in the oven until golden brown.
5. The oven must not be very hot.
