## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound butter
- 0.75 pound powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 pounds rice flour
- 0.5 pound flour
- 10 quintal bitter almonds
- 4 quintal powdered cardamom or a little grated lemon zest
- 1 baking or raising powder
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Wash the butter well and stir together with 0.75 pound powdered sugar
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks, 2 at a time, then 4 pounds rice flour, 0.5 pound flour, 10 quintal bitter almonds, 4 quintal powdered cardamom or a little grated lemon zest, 1 baking or raising powder
3. Finally fold in the 6 egg whites beaten to peaks
4. Bake 1 hour at even good heat in a prepared form
5. Do not remove from the form immediately, but let it rest a moment, wrapped with a warm piece
