## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg apples
- powdered sugar
- 1/4 kg blanched, sweet ground almonds
- bitter almonds
- 1/4 kg powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites (beaten)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut into 4 pieces, remove the core, rinse, and cook over heat with a little water.
2. When cooked, sweeten the apple sauce with powdered sugar.
3. Make an almond paste from 1/4 kg blanched, sweet ground almonds, a few bitter almonds, 1/4 kg powdered sugar, 6 egg yolks, and finally the beaten egg whites.
4. Spread the apple sauce on flat baking sheets, cover with the almond paste, and place in a moderate oven.
5. Serve lukewarm, decorated with whipped cream.
