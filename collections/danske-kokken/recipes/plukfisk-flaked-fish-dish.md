## Ingredients

- cooked fish (cod, pike, or salt fish preferred)
- butter
- wheat flour
- milk
- white sugar
- salt
- mustard (if using salt fish)

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the bones of the cooked fish in small pieces
2. For each pound of fish, take half a quarter pound of butter and 9 quintal of wheat flour, baked together with the butter and thinned with 2 pails of milk
3. Add a little white sugar and salt, then the fish, and give it a boil
4. If making plukfisk from salt fish, a little mustard can also be added to the sauce
