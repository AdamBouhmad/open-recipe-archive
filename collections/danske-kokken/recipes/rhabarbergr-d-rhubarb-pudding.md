## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- sugar to taste
- sago flour
- cold sweet cream

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb stalks, cut into pieces and cook to extract juice in the same manner as described for Stikkelsbærgrød
2. Treat 3 pots of juice in the same manner and proportions as described for gooseberries
3. Serve with cold sweet cream
