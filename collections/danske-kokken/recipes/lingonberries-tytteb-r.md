## Ingredients

- Berries
- 1.5 Pd. Melis per 1 Pd. berries
- whole cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean the berries by placing them on a coarse cloth and rolling them down to remove stems and fuzz
2. Place in cold water
3. Pound the Melis and place layered in the pot with the berries along with some pieces of whole cinnamon
4. Boil for 1/2 hour, skim frequently, and preserve in the usual manner
