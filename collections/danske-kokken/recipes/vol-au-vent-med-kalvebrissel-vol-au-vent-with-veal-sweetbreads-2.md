## Ingredients

- 3 pairs veal sweetbreads
- Champignons

## Directions

1. Clean the sweetbreads, blanch and cook in the same manner as Nr. 6.
2. Prepare the filling in the same way as for Croustades, though the sweetbreads should preferably be cut into somewhat larger pieces.
3. Fill the vol au vent with sweetbreads and garnish with Champignons.
