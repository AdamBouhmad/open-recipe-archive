## Ingredients

- red Juice, preferably Raspberry with a little Cherry
- Water
- Sugar
- 1 Pint
- 1 Tablespoon Corn Starch
- cold Water
- Preserves or Raisins
- sliced Almonds

## Directions

1. Take red Juice, preferably Raspberry with a little Cherry, Water and Sugar to taste.
2. For 1 Pint of this take 1 Tablespoon Corn Starch, which is stirred out in cold Water and put in the Juice when it boils.
3. You may add Preserves or Raisins, also sliced Almonds in it; these last Things are boiled in the Juice before the Thickening is added.
4. The Preserve is added after it is boiled, and must not boil.
5. Such Sauce is used for most Puddings.
