## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/4 liter Jamaica rum
- 1 kg rose hips

## Directions

1. Prepare the rose hips according to the previous instructions.
2. Place them in boiling water and boil until tender, then immediately strain through a sieve.
3. Melt the sugar in 1/4 liter of the water in which the rose hips were boiled and pour the warm syrup over them.
4. Over the next two days, drain the syrup and reboil it, pouring it over the fruit only after it has cooled.
5. On the fourth day, drain the syrup again and boil it down to a very thick syrup.
6. Add the rose hips without boiling them again.
7. After partially cooling, pour the rum over the preserve.
8. Pour into glasses and tie them up.
