## Ingredients

- Hvidling (Whiting)
- Salt
- Mel (Flour)
- Æg (Egg)
- Brød (Bread)
- Smør or Palmin (Butter or Margarine)
- brunt Smør (Brown butter)
- kogte Kartofler (Boiled potatoes)
- Salat or Persille (Salad or Parsley)
- Citronskiver (Lemon slices)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and remove the head and fins.
2. Make a few cuts on the sides.
3. Rinse well and sprinkle with salt.
4. Before frying, dry thoroughly in a fish cloth.
5. Dip in flour, then in egg, then in bread.
6. Fry in butter or Palmin on a pan.
7. Serve with brown butter and boiled potatoes.
8. Garnish with salad or parsley and lemon slices.
