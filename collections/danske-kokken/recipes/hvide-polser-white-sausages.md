## Ingredients

- 1 pound scalded rice grains
- 2 quarts milk
- ½ pound butter
- ½ pound powdered white sugar
- 8 eggs
- ½ pound scalded, coarsely ground almonds
- peel of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- salt
- 1 pound scalded raisins

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound scalded rice grains almost completely soft in 2 quarts milk
2. Pour it into a bowl and stir in ½ pound butter, ½ pound powdered white sugar, 8 eggs, ½ pound scalded, coarsely ground almonds, the peel of 1 lemon finely chopped, a little salt and 1 pound scalded raisins
3. Stir well
4. Fill the intestines about half full with the mixture and cook for ½ hour
5. Since they are usually made at the same time as the black sausages, the white ones should be cooked first in the water
6. If they are to be warmed on another day, both kinds of sausages can be warmed together
7. Serve with the same sauce as the black ones
