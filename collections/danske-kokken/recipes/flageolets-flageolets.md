## Ingredients

- Young beans from fully developed snap beans
- Boiling water
- Salt solution (10 gr or 2 Kvint per 1 liter)

## Directions

1. Scald the shelled beans in boiling water, pour cold water over them and fill into jars
2. Pour a cold salt solution over the beans in the jar, close and cook for 30 to 35 minutes
3. Cool the jars
