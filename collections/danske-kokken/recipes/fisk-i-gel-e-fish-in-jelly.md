## Ingredients

- salmon
- water
- bay leaves
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. After the fish is cleaned, cut it into appropriate slices across, so that the skin comes with each piece, as the skin is fatty and helps the piece hold its shape
2. Rinse the slices only once in cold water so the fat is not lost
3. Set the fish to cook and when it comes to a boil, skim the broth
4. Add some bay leaves and whole peppercorns
5. Do not let it boil for long
6. Cook until the fish is tender
