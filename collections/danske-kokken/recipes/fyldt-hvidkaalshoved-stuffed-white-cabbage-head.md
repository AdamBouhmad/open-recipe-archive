## Ingredients

- One good, firm white cabbage head
- Ground beef
- Kidney, finely chopped
- Linen cloth or twine
- Butter
- Salt
- Water
- Melted butter or brown sauce

## Directions

1. Take a good, firm white cabbage head and remove the outer loose leaves. Cut off a lid the size of a saucer and hollow out the head. Fill with forcemeat of ground beef mixed with finely chopped kidney. Replace the lid and bind with a clean linen cloth or wrap with the removed large leaves and tie around it. Boil the head in about two hours in water with a little salt and butter. Before serving, drain the water from it. Serve with melted butter or brown sauce. To brown the head: after filling and binding with cooking twine, place it with a piece of butter in a pot with a tight lid to cook in its own juice over gentle heat until golden brown and tender, checking frequently so it does not burn. Place the head on a dish and remove the twine. Stir the sauce in the pot with a teaspoon of flour and pour over the head. The hollowed-out cabbage can be browned separately.
