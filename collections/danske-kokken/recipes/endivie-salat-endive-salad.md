## Ingredients

- Endive heads
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt
- Chopped chervil

## Directions

1. Remove the outermost coarse, green leaves and use only the inner yellow ones.
2. Rinse and shake them well dry in a cloth.
3. Cut away the stem and section the salad and place it in the salad bowl.
4. Mix together oil, vinegar, pepper and salt as for the previous salads and pour over.
5. Endive salad can be sprinkled with chopped chervil.
