## Ingredients

- 2 kg (4 lb) mildly salted or partly fresh striped pork
- 2 medium heads white cabbage

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of mildly salted or partly fresh striped pork first for ½ hour in water, which is then roughly poured out of the pot.
2. Cut 2 medium white cabbage heads coarsely and put them in the pot with the pork in the middle and cover with the cabbage.
3. Cook for about 3 hours under a lid over very low heat so it does not burn.
4. Turn the cabbage once in a while.
5. When done, cut the pork and arrange it in the center of the serving dish with the cabbage around it.
