## Ingredients

- 125 grams mushrooms, finely chopped
- 1 onion
- 125 grams butter
- Grated French bread
- Strained snail broth, reduced
- Salt
- Pepper
- White wine or lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- Snail shells, cleaned
- Cooked snails (from No. 572)
- Breadcrumbs, finely sifted
- Melted butter
- Salad for garnish
- Lemon for garnish

## Directions

1. Finely chop 125 grams mushrooms together with 1 onion
2. Sauté in 125 grams butter
3. Add as much grated French bread as the butter can accommodate
4. Thin with the strongly reduced, strained snail broth until it reaches the consistency of a not too thin batter dough
5. Season with salt, pepper and a little white wine or lemon juice
6. Stir in 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
7. Ensure snail shells are well cleaned inside with boiling and cold water and well drained
8. Place a little of the dough in each shell
9. Add the cooked snail (from No. 572)
10. Cover with dough and smooth it
11. Sprinkle with finely sifted breadcrumbs
12. Drizzle with melted butter
13. Place in a moderately warm oven
14. Bake for about 15 minutes on a baking pan covered with a layer of kitchen salt, or on a flat dish
15. Arrange very hot on a napkin, decorated with salad and garnished with lemon, whose juice is eaten on the snails
