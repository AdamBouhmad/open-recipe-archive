## Ingredients

- cooked cauliflower
- batter for cauliflower gratin (Nr. 64)
- milk
- breadcrumbs
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. After the cauliflower is cooked, separate it into small florets
2. Dip them in a batter prepared as for cauliflower gratin Nr. 64, thinning the batter with a little milk if needed
3. Place the dipped florets on a flat dish without touching each other
4. When the batter has cooled, dip them in ground breadcrumbs
5. Fry them light brown in fat
6. They can be used as a garnish around meat dishes or served as a first or middle course with butter
