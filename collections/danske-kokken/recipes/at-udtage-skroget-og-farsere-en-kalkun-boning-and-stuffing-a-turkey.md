## Ingredients

- 1 turkey, plucked, cleaned and washed
- salt
- stuffing (see options below)

## Directions

1. Place the plucked, cleaned and washed turkey on a cloth with the breast facing down toward the table.
2. With a pointed, sharp knife, slit the skin along the back.
3. With great care, cut out the back and breast bone in such a way that only the thighs and wings remain attached to the meat, but this must be done without damaging the skin.
4. When the turkey is opened in this manner, remove the entrails at the same time as the skeleton.
5. Spread the meat out on the cloth, sprinkle with a little salt and cover with enough stuffing that the turkey, when sewn together along the back, has taken on a beautiful round shape.
