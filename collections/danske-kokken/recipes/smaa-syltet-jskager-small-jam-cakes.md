## Ingredients

- 5 egg whites
- egg yolks
- 15 quintal finely ground powdered sugar
- 6 quintal fine flour
- paper for forms
- melted butter
- sugar and egg white for glazing
- jam for serving

## Directions

1. Whip 5 egg whites to stiff peaks and stir in the yolks
2. Add 15 quintal finely ground powdered sugar and 6 quintal fine flour
3. When well mixed, curl round pieces of paper at the edges, dip in melted butter
4. Place a small teaspoonful of batter in each paper cup
5. Insert into a moderately warm oven
6. When risen, remove from the paper and glaze with a mixture of sugar and egg white beaten together
7. Insert into a lukewarm oven to dry
8. Serve with jam
