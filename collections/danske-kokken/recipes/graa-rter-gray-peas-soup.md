## Ingredients

- 3 pounds gray peas
- Cold water
- Bouillon, broth, or ham stock
- 1/4 pound butter
- 3 tablespoons wheat flour
- Crushed cloves
- A couple of bay leaves
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the peas and soak overnight in cold water
2. Place peas over heat with enough cold water to stand a couple of inches above them
3. Cook 2 to 3 hours under a lid, skimming well but without stirring
4. When almost tender, place a cast-iron pan over heat with 1/4 pound butter
5. Brown the butter to a light brown color
6. Add 3 tablespoons wheat flour and let it brown dark brown with the butter
7. Meanwhile, the soup should be cooked, strained, and fat skimmed
8. Place the browned flour in a pot and thin with soup until it becomes a smooth sauce
9. Add crushed cloves, a couple of bay leaves, vinegar, and sugar to taste
10. Bring to a boil
11. Drain the cooked peas in a sieve
12. Place peas in the tureen and pour the sauce over them
13. If the soup was made with smoked ham, serve the ham at the same time after removing the rind
