## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound raisins
- 2 lemons
- several sticks cinnamon
- 1 pint cleaned small sago grains
- 3.5 pots boiling water
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 spoonfuls powdered sugar
- sugar to taste
- 1/2 bottle old white wine

## Directions

1. Pick and wash the raisins.
2. Peel the lemons; put the yellow peel together with several sticks of cinnamon, the raisins, and 1 pint of cleaned small sago grains (see recipe 36) in a pot with 3.5 pots of boiling water and cook for just over half an hour under a lid, until the grains are tender.
3. Beat 5 egg yolks with 5 spoonfuls of powdered sugar, then whisk this mixture into the soup.
4. Just before serving, add the lemon slices (with pits removed), sugar to taste, and half a bottle of old white wine.
