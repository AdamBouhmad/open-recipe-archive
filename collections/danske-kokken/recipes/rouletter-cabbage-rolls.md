## Ingredients

- Cabbage leaves
- Meat forcemeat
- Meat broth or water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Half-cook nice white cabbage leaves, and when they are cold, take meat forcemeat and roll it in them
2. The thickness should be like a large chestnut ball and the length 3 inches
3. Bind them with thread, cook in meat broth or salted water, and brown them on the outside in butter
4. The thread cannot be removed until they are ready to be served
5. Serve with brown butter
