## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Salt
- Water
- Vinegar
- Whole pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the oysters and throw them into strongly boiling salted water, stew them well and place in cold water
2. Bring vinegar and whole pepper to a boil and then cool
3. Place the oysters in a stone crock; to each quart of the water in which the oysters were scalded, take one cup of vinegar, both cold, which is poured over the oysters so it stands over them
