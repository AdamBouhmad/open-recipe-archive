## Ingredients

- 7 Pounds lean pork
- 2 Pounds good beef
- 0.5 Pound salt
- 7 Quintals white pepper
- 4 Quintals saltpeter
- 5 Pounds bacon cut into fine dice
- Beef intestines

## Directions

1. Chop the lean pork, beef, salt, white pepper and saltpeter finely and knead until it becomes spongy.
2. Let it stand until the next day, then knead it again, after which the 5 Pounds of bacon cut into fine dice are kneaded in and mixed well with the rest of the mass.
3. Fill it into wide, well-cleaned beef intestines, stuff firmly, let it lie for 24 hours with salt, press together well again, tie it up and bring it to a boil.
4. In the country they are best preserved in malt in the malt box.
