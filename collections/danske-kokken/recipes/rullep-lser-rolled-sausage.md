## Ingredients

- Meat from beef or sheep
- Salt
- Ground saltpeter
- Ground pepper
- Ginger
- Cloves
- Allspice
- Fat meat for filling

## Directions

1. Spread out the meat and cut into pieces 1 quarter wide and 13 quarters long
2. The top surface should be scraped so it becomes evenly thick; on the thin spots, place flat slices of fat meat
3. Coat everywhere with salt, a little ground saltpeter, ground pepper, ginger, cloves and allspice
4. Roll together into a thick, oblong sausage, stuff well at the ends, and sew around with strong thread, with a loop at one end for those you want to smoke
5. Rub well with salt and saltpeter and place with the salted meat or in cooked brine
6. After 3 weeks, remove the sausages you want to smoke
7. Both salted and smoked rolled sausages must be soaked the day before they are to be used; cook very gently and place in a press between two wooden spatulas, with weight on the upper one
8. The next day remove from the press and strip off the outer skin and threads
