## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- Cream or good milk
- Butter
- Salt
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Beat 8 eggs well, first alone, then with 8 small spoonfuls of cream or good milk.
2. Put it in the pot or frying pan with an ounce of butter and a little salt.
3. It tastes good to add a little finely chopped parsley.
4. There must not be a high flame and do not stir, but scrape continually from the bottom with a spatula; if you stir round, the pieces will break apart and that is not the intention.
5. When you find them suitably stiff, take them off the fire.
6. They must not be too hard.
7. You can add cooked asparagus heads in them.
8. Count 3 eggs per person.
