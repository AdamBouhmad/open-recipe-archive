## Ingredients

- 16 Egg yolks
- 1 Pound powdered Sugar
- Egg whites
- 2 Ounces Corn Starch

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the powdered sugar for half an hour
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks
3. Mix the corn starch with the beaten whites
4. Carefully fold into the egg yolks
5. Pour into a form lined with buttered paper
6. Bake for a few hours with low heat at first, then more
