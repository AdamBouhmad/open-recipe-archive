## Ingredients

- Pork head or mixed meat with some fat
- Salt
- Cloves
- Ground pepper
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Use pork head, which should be flayed, cleaned and cooked as for Sylte
2. Remove bones, innards and all unusable parts and remove most of the fat from the jelly
3. Place one piece of jelly in a bowl of suitable size
4. Cut the innards into strips or cubes along with the fat cut from the jelly and mix with ears and snout, cut into thin pieces
5. Place over heat with salt, cloves, ground pepper and a cup of bouillon
6. Stir well and as soon as it begins to boil, remove from heat and place immediately in the jelly
7. Place the other piece of jelly over and then again a bottom that fits well in the bowl
8. For the first hour place a light weight on top, later a heavier one, but not so heavily that fat presses out the sides
9. The next day remove from bowl and place in brine, or it can be cut into slices and placed in aspic
