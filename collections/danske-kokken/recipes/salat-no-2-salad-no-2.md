## Ingredients

- Mustard
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Rub together 2 teaspoonfuls of mustard and 2 hard-boiled egg yolks in 2 spoonfuls of oil; add vinegar, pepper and a little salt.
2. Treat the salad as before.
