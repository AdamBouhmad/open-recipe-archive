## Ingredients

- Four egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) milk
- A few drops of vanilla essence
- 4 pieces of isinglass dissolved in insignificant water
- 2 glasses of sherry
- White wine or rhubarb juice
- 1/2 L (1 Pægl) cream for whipped cream
- Fruit coloring
- Strawberry juice
- Raspberry juice
- Cherry marmalade
- Ginger
- Orange essence
- Maraschino or cacao liqueur

## Directions

1. Whisk together four egg yolks white with 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
2. Bring 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) milk to a boil and stir it into the egg mixture, which is again poured into the pot and stirred well until it comes to a boil
3. Pour it out and add a few drops of vanilla essence
4. Stir in 4 pieces of isinglass dissolved in insignificant water together with 2 glasses of sherry and as much white wine or rhubarb juice so that the sauce gets a fresh, tart flavor
5. Stir regularly in the cream until it is cold
6. When it is to be served, stir in whipped cream made from 1/2 L (1 Pægl) cream, and if the sauce is desired thinner, it can simply be diluted with a little cream
7. The sauce can be colored pale pink with fruit coloring and served with various cold puddings
8. It can be varied with: strawberry juice, raspberry juice, cherry marmalade, ginger, orange essence, or maraschino or cacao liqueur
