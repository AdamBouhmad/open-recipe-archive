## Ingredients

- good, beautiful apples of equal size
- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 3.75 dl water
- ladyfinger cake, macarons, or small cakes

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, remove the blossom, scrape the stem, and cook without peeling.
2. Boil 1/2 kg powdered sugar with 3.75 dl water to make a syrup.
3. Cook the apples tender in the syrup.
4. Arrange the apples in a pyramid while as hot as possible.
5. Strain the syrup in which they were cooked and pour over them.
6. Serve immediately with ladyfinger cake, macarons, or small cakes.
