## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.5 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Scald the plums in boiling water until the skin comes off easily.
2. Split them and remove the stones.
3. Give the plums just one boil in the syrup as described in the previous recipe, then place them in jars.
4. Boil the juice for a few minutes before pouring it over them.
5. Crush the stones and boil some of the kernels as described for apricots.
