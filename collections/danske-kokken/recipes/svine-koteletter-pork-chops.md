## Ingredients

- 1½ kg pork shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread
- brown butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut from the shoulder as mentioned for veal chops, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and bread, and fry in brown butter or lard.
2. The chops can be served with brown butter and the gravy from the pan, or with spiced sauces and vegetables, for example: stewed cauliflower and brown butter, red cabbage and gravy sauce, browned potatoes, apple sauce and gravy sauce, potato croquettes and pickles sauce, mashed potatoes and piquant sauce, or macaroni and tomato sauce or lemon sauce.
