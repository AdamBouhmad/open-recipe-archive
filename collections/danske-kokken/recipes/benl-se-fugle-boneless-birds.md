## Ingredients

- Slices of good beef (not too freshly slaughtered, preferably inner loin)
- Ground meat forcemeat
- Bacon strips
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Butter or margarine
- Onions
- Flour
- Stock or water
- Coloring
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Beat out slices of beef and spread a thin layer of forcemeat on each.
2. Place a strip of bacon in the center, dipped in a mixture of salt, pepper, and cloves.
3. Roll up the meat, shape it like a bird's body, and join together with bacon strips.
4. Rub the birds externally with the spices and dip in flour.
5. In a pot with browned butter or margarine into which a couple of grated onions have been added, brown the birds.
6. Pour in enough stock, broth, or water so that it reaches the level of the meat.
7. Add coloring to the liquid and cook the birds for 2 hours.
8. Brown flour in the fat skimmed from the liquid, then thicken the sauce with it.
9. Arrange the birds in the sauce and serve with boiled, peeled potatoes.
