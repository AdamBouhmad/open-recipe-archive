## Ingredients

- Ham roast
- Salt
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Requires roasting longer than veal roast; it must not be red inside.
2. After washing, the roast is rubbed very thoroughly with salt, which helps make the rind crispy, and this is cut so that the whole rind becomes narrow strips.
3. The oven must be very hot when the roast is put in, so that the rind can become crispy; and it is not basted.
4. The rind can also be cut off together with a layer of fat, if the ham is very fatty.
5. The roast is then put in to become warm, and taken out again, while it is sprinkled with crushed allspice mixed with crushed cloves, pepper and salt, after which it is put into the oven again and roasted.
