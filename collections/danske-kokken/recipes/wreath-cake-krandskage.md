## Ingredients

- 1½ Pounds sweet almonds
- 2 Ounces bitter almonds
- 1½ Pounds dry powdered Sugar
- Zest of 1 Lemon, grated
- 6 Egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Blanch and dry the sweet and bitter almonds
2. Grind them slightly
3. Mix with the dry powdered sugar
4. Add the grated lemon zest
5. Fold in the beaten egg whites
6. Form wreaths of the batter, each one smaller than the previous, so they can be stacked on top of each other when they are done
