## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Butter

## Directions

1. Scrambled eggs can be made with the same amount of water as milk is used above.
2. Made with water, the scrambled eggs become lighter and get a more beautiful yellow color, but in this case a little more butter must be used.
