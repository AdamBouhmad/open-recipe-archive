## Ingredients

- 20 medium tomatoes
- 0.5 kg powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon whole white pepper
- a few fresh bay leaves
- 10 peeled shallots
- raw vinegar

## Directions

1. Layer the tomatoes in a jar alternately with the powdered sugar, whole white pepper, bay leaves, and peeled shallots.
2. Pour enough raw vinegar over the tomatoes to cover them.
3. Cover the jar and let stand for 3-4 days.
4. Drain off the vinegar and heat it to boiling.
5. Pour the hot vinegar over the tomatoes.
6. Cover with cloth and seal the jar the next day with parchment paper.
7. Serve with meat as a pickle.
