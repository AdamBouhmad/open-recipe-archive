## Ingredients

- 8 whole eggs
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar, preferably superfine sugar
- 1 L (1 Pot) boiling cream or sweet milk
- vanilla beans (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir the eggs and powdered sugar together with a whisk until it is a foamy mass.
2. Whip 1 L (1 Pot) boiling cream or sweet milk into this mixture.
3. Bring the cream just to the boiling point while continuing to work it—it must not boil.
4. Whip it until it is cold to prevent it from forming a skin.
5. This cream can have the kernels of 1 vanilla bean added and can serve as the base for all the following types of cream ice cream.
