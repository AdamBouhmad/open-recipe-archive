## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 12 egg yolks
- 36 quarters powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pot of sweet cream with 1 vanilla pod.
2. Whip 12 egg yolks white with 36 quarters of powdered sugar.
3. When the cream has boiled but is cooled enough that it no longer boils, pour it with whisking into the egg yolks, which are placed in the pot and again under constant whisking are set over the heat and warmed until the mixture is just about to boil.
4. Remove it from the heat and pour it through a large sieve, stirring it through into a terrine.
5. You must continue to stir it in the terrine until it is cold, otherwise it will separate.
6. The cream is then set to cool.
