## Ingredients

- Cardoon stalks
- Boiling water with salt
- Salt solution (10 gr or 2 Kvint per 1 liter)

## Directions

1. Cut only the fleshy stalks into finger-length pieces
2. Place in boiling salted water and cook until the outer skin can easily be removed by rubbing with salt
3. Pour the cold salt solution over the cardoons
4. Cook for 30 to 35 minutes in carefully closed jars and cool
