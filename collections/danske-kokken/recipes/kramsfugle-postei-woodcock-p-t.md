## Ingredients

- For 4 persons: Pastry dough or fine pastry dough
- 6 woodcocks
- Meat farce
- Pork fat (lard)
- Gravy

## Directions

1. Clean the birds thoroughly, remove the head and wings, then roast the birds.
2. Line a mold with dough.
