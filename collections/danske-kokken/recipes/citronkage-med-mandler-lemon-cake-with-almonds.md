## Ingredients

- zest of 6 lemons
- 3 quarter-pounds powdered sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 3/4 pound bitter almonds
- egg white or a little water
- 8 eggs
- juice of 6 lemons
- butter dough (No. 186)

## Directions

1. Rub the yellow zest of 6 lemons onto 3 quarter-pounds powdered sugar, then grind very finely.
2. Melt 1 pound butter and remove the salt from it.
3. Scald and grind 1 pound sweet and 3/4 pound bitter almonds very finely either with the white of an egg or with a little water.
4. Beat 8 eggs well and stir with the almonds, then with the sugar, until the whole is properly well mixed, which will take about 1 hour.
5. Then press the juice of the 6 lemons through a sieve into the mixture.
6. Grease a form with butter and line with butter dough (No. 186), then fill with the dough and crimp the butter dough at the edge.
7. Insert immediately in the oven and bake for 2 hours.
