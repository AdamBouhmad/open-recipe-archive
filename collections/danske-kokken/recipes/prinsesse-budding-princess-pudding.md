## Ingredients

- 100 g (20 quintal) Pillsbury flour
- 100 g (20 quintal) margarine
- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) boiling milk
- 8 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- a little salt
- cardamom to taste
- egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Bake the flour in the margarine and dilute with 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) of boiling milk.
2. When cooled, stir in the egg yolks, sugar, a little salt, and cardamom to taste, and finally the beaten egg whites.
3. Boil the pudding for 2 hours and serve with fruit or cream sauce.
