## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- salt
- butter or margarine
- pan drippings or broth or salted water
- butter
- flour
- Sagomel (sago flour)

## Directions

1. The male turkey is best for roasting.
2. Clean and truss the turkey, lard the breast and thighs, sprinkle with salt and cover with butter or margarine.
3. Place breast-side up in the roasting pan and roast approximately 2.5 hours in the oven, but must be basted frequently with either pan drippings, broth or salted water.
4. If the breast begins to brown too much, cover it with paper spread with butter.
5. The sauce can be made from butter and flour, diluted with the pan drippings, or the drippings can simply be thickened with Sagomel, but for this it must be more reduced to be sufficiently strong.
