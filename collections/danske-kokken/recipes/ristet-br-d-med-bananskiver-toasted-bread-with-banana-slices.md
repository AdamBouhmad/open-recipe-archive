## Ingredients

- Toasted bread
- Banana slices
- Violets for sprinkling
- Orange pieces
- Venushair for garnish

## Directions

1. Spread the toasted bread with butter.
2. Cover with banana slices and sprinkle with violets.
3. Arrange on cake paper and garnish with orange pieces and venushair.
