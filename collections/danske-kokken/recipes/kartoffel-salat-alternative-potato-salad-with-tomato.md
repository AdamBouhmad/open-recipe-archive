## Ingredients

- tomato purée
- salt
- ground white pepper
- mustard
- parsley
- grated onion
- small cooked, cold, peeled chestnut potatoes
- watercress

## Directions

1. Make a sauce from tomato purée, salt, ground white pepper, mustard, chopped parsley and a little grated onion
2. Toss small cooked, cold, peeled chestnut potatoes in the sauce
3. Sprinkle watercress over the salad
