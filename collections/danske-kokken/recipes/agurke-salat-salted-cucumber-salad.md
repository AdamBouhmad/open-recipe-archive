## Ingredients

- firm, green cucumbers without seeds
- salt
- kitchen salt
- parchment paper

## Directions

1. The cucumbers must be firm and green and preferably without seeds.
2. Peel them and cut into thin slices, as cucumbers are otherwise handled for salad.
3. Sprinkle with salt and let stand until the next day.
4. Partially squeeze out the brine.
5. Pack the cucumber salad tightly in stone jars in layers with a little kitchen salt.
6. Place a light press over them and seal the jar with parchment paper.
7. As you need them, remove the cucumbers and rinse them in fresh, cold water before preparing them with pepper, vinegar, or oil.
