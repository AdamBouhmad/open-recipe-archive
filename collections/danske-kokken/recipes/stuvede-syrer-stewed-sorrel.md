## Ingredients

- 1/2 bushel sorrel
- A little natron
- Butter
- Flour
- Soup stock
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the sorrel, rinse it, and place it in plenty of boiling water with a little natron.
2. When the leaves are tender, drain them on a colander and press out the water.
3. Chop the sorrel finely or press it through a coarse sieve.
4. The sorrel mash can either be stewed like spinach, for which butter and flour are browned and thinned with soup stock, and the sorrel is stirred in and prepared with sugar; or a piece of cold butter is simply stirred into the mash along with sugar to taste.
5. Bring the sorrel to a boil with this and serve with roasted meat, cutlets, etc.
