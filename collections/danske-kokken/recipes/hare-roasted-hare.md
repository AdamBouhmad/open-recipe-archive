## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed hare weighing 5.5 kg (for 6 to 8 persons)
- Fine larding strips
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Skin the hare as described for venison.
2. Cut off the head and forelegs, and place the hare on its back, then cut off the ribs down to the fillet.
3. On the inside of the back, break the spine in several places to facilitate carving.
4. Loosen all the membranes of the back, legs and forelegs with a sharp knife and remove them all at once without any meat following or being damaged.
5. Lard the hare on the back, legs and forelegs with fine larding strips, cover with butter or margarine and roast in a well-heated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes.
6. Serve with browned potatoes and red currant jelly or apple compote.
7. Carve and reassemble before serving.
