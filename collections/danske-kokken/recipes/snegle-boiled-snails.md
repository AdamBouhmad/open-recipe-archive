## Ingredients

- snails in shells
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the snail shells well, boil them for several minutes in water with plenty of salt, until the snail easily pulls out with a larding needle or similar pointed object.
2. Remove the foot and the internal organs with a small knife.
