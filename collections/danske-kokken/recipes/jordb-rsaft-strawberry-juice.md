## Ingredients

- 6 pots of strawberries
- 3 pounds of currant juice
- 4 pots of water
- 0.5 pound of powdered sugar per pound of juice

## Directions

1. Add the currant juice and water to the strawberries and cook for half an hour, then press through a sieve
2. Cook the berries that remain with 2.5 measures of water for half an hour and press through the sieve again
3. Filter the juice, weigh it and cook for half an hour with 0.5 pound of powdered sugar for each pound of juice
4. This juice becomes very stiff
