## Ingredients

- Good, carefully shelled peas
- Cold water
- Boiling water
- Salt
- Soda
- Cold brine

## Directions

1. Rinse good, carefully shelled peas in cold water and give them a 3 minute boiling in boiling water with a little salt and soda
2. Drain the water from them and rinse the peas in cold water
3. Pack in jars and pour over with a cold brine, 10 gr (2 Kvint) salt to 1 l (1 Pot) water
