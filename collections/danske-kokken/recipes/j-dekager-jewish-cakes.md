## Ingredients

- 625 grams (1 1/4 lb) Pillsbury flour
- 375 grams (3/4 lb) margarine
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) icing sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking soda, dissolved in a small amount of water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Roll out thinly and cut out with a glass
3. Place fairly close together on the sheet
4. Brush with egg
5. Sprinkle with a mixture of sugar and cinnamon and with chopped or blanched, scalded almonds
6. Bake light in a not too hot oven
7. Very crisp Jewish cakes can be made with my baking flour
