## Ingredients

- quince fruit
- water
- 375 gr (0.75 lb) powdered sugar to 1/2 kg (1 lb) strained pulp
- almond kernels (optional)

## Directions

1. Dry the fruit, cut into 4 pieces, and cut away the stem, blossom and core house.
2. Cook the quinces until completely tender in water, remove and pass through the sieve.
3. Weigh the pulp and cook with the sugar until it is so firm that it does not immediately flow together after the spoon is drawn through it.
4. Scalded, broken almond kernels can be cooked in the jam.
5. Pour it out, dry and store as previously explained.
6. Scraps from quince jelly can be passed through the sieve; it is weighed and cooked with sugar and otherwise treated as the previously mentioned Russian jams.
