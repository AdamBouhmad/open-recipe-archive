## Ingredients

- 1 quart hot water
- Graham flour
- 2 cents compressed yeast
- fine wheat or rye flour
- 1 small cup molasses or brown sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. To 1 quart hot water add as much Graham flour as necessary to mix it into a thick dough, plus 2 cents compressed yeast.
2. Let it rise for the usual time.
3. Stir in as much fine wheat or rye flour (as desired) as can be kneaded, along with 1 small cup molasses or brown sugar (if preferred) and a little salt.
4. When well kneaded, set to warm for a few hours.
5. Shape into loaves and bake with good heat.
