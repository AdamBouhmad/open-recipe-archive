## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple
- wine
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Mix the pineapple juice with wine and prepare as Wine Jelly.
2. Layer the fruit cut into small cubes in the jelly and let sit until the next day.
3. Serve either with cream sauce, whipped cream, or garnish with pineapple slices and can be decorated with meringue.
