## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 measure sweet cream or milk
- 1 teaspoon flour
- ground pepper
- salt
- 14 drams butter

## Directions

1. Beat 6 eggs well with 1 measure sweet cream or milk, 1 teaspoon flour, and a little ground pepper and salt
2. Melt 14 drams butter in a pan
3. Pour the egg mixture into the melted butter
4. When stiff, the omelet is ready
5. Serve with powdered sugar
