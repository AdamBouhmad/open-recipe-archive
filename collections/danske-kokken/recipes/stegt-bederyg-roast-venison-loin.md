## Ingredients

- Venison loin
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Salt
- Blood
- Vinegar
- Cold milk
- White writing paper
- Butter

## Directions

1. The venison loin must hang for several days before roasting; remove the internal fat and kidneys, then rub with ground pepper, ginger and salt.
2. Alternatively, soak it in cold blood mixed with vinegar and salt for several days. For each pound of meat, reckon 2 measures of blood and half a measure of vinegar and a small tablespoon of salt.
3. The blood should cover the loin, and all impurities that collect on the surface should be skimmed off daily.
4. When you want to use the loin, remove it, rinse with cold, skimmed milk and soak in it for an hour.
5. When roasting, cover the loin with a piece of white writing paper smeared with butter; baste and roast as usual.
