## Ingredients

- One pound unripe peaches, peeled
- One pound white sugar
- One gill vinegar
- One half pint water (scant)

## Directions

1. Peel 1 pound unripe peaches, divide into 4 parts, and remove the stones.
2. Take 1 pound white sugar and cook with 1 gill vinegar and a scant half pint water.
3. When this is skimmed, add the peaches and cook about 5 minutes (longer if very unripe); remove and place in a jar.
4. Cook the syrup a bit longer, about 6 minutes, pour hot over the peaches, and cover with cloth.
5. After several days, cook the syrup again about 10 minutes, skim well, pour hot over, and cover again with cloth.
6. Tie as usual.
