## Ingredients

- One hen
- One pound rice
- One quarter pound good fat (clarified) or butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Boil the hen until there is a good quart of broth.
2. Remove the meat from the bones and cut into small pieces.
3. Cook one pound of rice in the reserved quart of broth with one quarter pound of good fat or butter.
4. Add salt to taste.
5. When the rice is cooked (it should remain whole, not broken apart), add the meat and warm through.
6. Stir everything together and place in a round mold, turning out onto a serving dish so it resembles a pudding.
