## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) peeled pumpkin
- 1/4 dl (1/4 pint) vinegar
- 1.5 dl (1/4 pint) water
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil the vinegar, water and sugar into a syrup; add the pumpkin, cut in pieces as previously described.
2. Boil it, skim the syrup, and remove the pumpkin when the pieces are clear.
3. Reduce the syrup together with sliced vanilla, and only when it is boiled quite smooth, add the pumpkin to then cool in crocks or jars, which are sealed when the preserve has cooled.
4. Calculate 1 vanilla pod for 3 kg (6 lb) of pumpkin.
