## Ingredients

- Fresh fish
- Salt pork slices
- Crackers
- Red pepper
- Onions
- Water

## Directions

1. Any kind of fresh fish can be used, although many prefer cod and bass.
2. Clean in the usual way, cut into pieces 1 inch or more thick and 2 inches wide and long.
3. Fry a suitable number (for example, 8) fairly large slices of salt pork until crisp in an iron pot; remove the pork so that only the fat remains.
4. In the pot place a layer of fish, a layer of coarsely crushed crackers, some of the finely chopped pork with red pepper and chopped onions; then a new layer of fish, a new layer of crackers, etc., etc. until the pot is full.
5. Pour water over it and stew the fish until it is cooked enough; then take it up and place it in the serving dish; the sauce (the gravy) is thickened with crushed cracker, brought to a boil and poured over the chowder.
