## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg cherries
- 4.5 L water
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- potato flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pound 1 kg of the cherries in a mortar until the pits are crushed
2. Heat in water and cook at least half an hour with whole cinnamon and lemon peel
3. Mix 2 tablespoons potato flour with water and thicken the soup when it boils
4. Remove pits from remaining cherries and cook in very little water with sugar
5. Mix with the strained soup and sweeten
