## Ingredients

- walnuts
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- whole black pepper
- ground ginger
- shallots

## Directions

1. Prick the walnuts with a fork and place them in a brine made of 0.5 kg salt to 1.25 liters water.
2. Every third day, pour fresh brine over the nuts.
3. After 9 days total, remove the nuts, pat them dry, and place on trays.
4. Set the trays in the sun until the nuts are completely black after 3-4 days.
5. Place them in jars.
6. Heat enough vinegar to cover the walnuts and boil for 10 minutes.
7. For each liter of vinegar, add 50 gr whole black pepper, 25 gr ground ginger, and 5 shallots.
8. Pour the hot vinegar with spices over the walnuts, ensuring they are completely covered.
9. When the vinegar cools, seal the jar.
10. After one month, the walnuts are ready to eat and can be stored for several years.
11. Use in brown sauces, with fish and meat.
