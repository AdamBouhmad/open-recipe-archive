## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg good, fine, large apples
- 1/4 kg flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 50 g melted margarine
- 1/4 L semi-skimmed milk
- a little sugar
- salt
- cardamom
- egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into slices about 1/4 inch thick, removing the core from the center
2. Make a batter from the flour, egg yolks, melted margarine, semi-skimmed milk, a little sugar, salt, cardamom and the whipped egg whites
3. Take the apple slices with a fork and dip them in the batter, making sure they are completely covered
4. Fry them in boiling clarified butter until they have a nice light brown color
5. The batter covering the center can be shaped so that the wreaths are nicely formed
6. Serve very hot with powdered sugar
