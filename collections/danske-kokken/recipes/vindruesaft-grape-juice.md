## Ingredients

- Grapes
- 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice

## Directions

1. Crush the grapes in a wooden or stone mortar and pour them into the jelly cloth so that the juice can run off.
2. Measure the juice and boil it with the sugar until no more foam appears during cooking.
3. Then pour it out and fill into bottles, which are bound immediately.
4. This juice is particularly suitable for jelly.
