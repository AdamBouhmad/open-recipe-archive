## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Soup or water
- Mushroom sauce (see Nr. 758)
- Puff pastry border or strips

## Directions

1. Lard and roast the squabs, basting with soup or water as described in recipe 913
2. Make a good brown mushroom sauce from the gravy
3. Pour over the split squabs
4. Garnish with puff pastry border or strips
