## Ingredients

- 2 ounces butter
- 1 onion, grated
- 2 anchovies, scraped from the bones and chopped
- salt
- pepper or a little curry
- juice of half a lemon
- 1 biscuit, crushed
- 1 teaspoon flour
- a little soy
- 1 gill bouillon
- strong gravy
- ½ pound finely chopped, cooked or fried meat
- approximately 1 gill white wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown 2 ounces butter (more if the meat is lean)
2. Cook the grated onion in it first, then add 2 anchovies scraped from the bones and chopped with a knife, a little salt, pepper or curry, juice of half a lemon, 1 crushed biscuit and 1 teaspoon flour
3. When it boils, add a little soy, 1 gill bouillon, strong gravy and ½ pound finely chopped, cooked or fried meat
4. Finally add approximately 1 gill white wine, though this may be omitted
5. Cook the whole for a good quarter hour
6. When serving, either place browned potatoes on top, or place slices of baked French bread on the dish and pour the hachis over
7. For 4 persons
