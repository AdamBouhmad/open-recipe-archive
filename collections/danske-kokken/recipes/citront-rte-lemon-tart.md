## Ingredients

- Zest of 4 lemons
- 1 pound white sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- Juice of 4 lemons, strained
- 10 egg whites, whipped to peaks

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 4 lemons onto 1 pound white sugar, then crush it fine.
2. Whisk this with 20 egg yolks and the strained juice of 4 lemons for one hour.
3. Whip 10 egg whites to stiff peaks and gently fold in.
4. Bake the mixture in a buttered pan at moderate heat for three-quarters to one hour.
