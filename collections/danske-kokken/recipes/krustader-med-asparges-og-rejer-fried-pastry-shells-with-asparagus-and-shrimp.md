## Ingredients

- 1 liter shrimp, cooked and shelled
- 1/2 kg asparagus, peeled
- Salt
- Flour
- Butter
- Asparagus broth
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook and shell the shrimp
2. Peel the asparagus and cut into 2 centimeter pieces
3. Cook the asparagus tender in salted water
4. Make a roux with flour and butter
5. Dilute with asparagus broth and thicken with 2 egg yolks
6. Add the asparagus and shrimp to the sauce
