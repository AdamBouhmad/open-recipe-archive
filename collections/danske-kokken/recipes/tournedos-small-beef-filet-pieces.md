## Ingredients

- Small beef pieces cut from the filet
- Salt
- Pepper
- Brown butter
- Gravy
- Vegetables for garnish

## Directions

1. Small beef pieces cut from the filet, which are altogether handled, fried and served like French Beef No. 134.
2. Tournedos can be garnished with vegetables.
