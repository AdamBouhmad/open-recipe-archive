## Ingredients

- Small to medium-sized fine white Onions
- strong Brine
- new Brine
- cold Water
- white Peppercorns
- Cloves
- whole Nutmeg Flower
- Vinegar
- 1 Cup Sugar per Gallon of Vinegar

## Directions

1. Choose smaller or medium-sized fine white onions
2. Peel and place in strong brine for four days, changing the brine twice
3. Bring new brine to a boil, add onions and cook for 3 minutes
4. Place in cold water for 4 hours
5. Remove and pack in crocks, layering with white peppercorns, cloves and whole nutmeg flower
6. Cook vinegar with 1 cup sugar per gallon of vinegar and pour hot into the crocks while still warm, then bind
7. After one month the onions can be used but are better later
