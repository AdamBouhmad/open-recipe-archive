## Ingredients

- Bread, preferably rye bread
- Ale or Beer and Lager in equal parts with Water
- Brown sugar
- whole Cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Cream

## Directions

1. In the morning, soak as much bread as desired, preferably rye bread, in cold water to soften; if using fresh bread, soaking is not necessary.
2. If homemade ale is available, use it exclusively, but for beer or lager, take equal parts water.
3. Place the bread over the fire with ale and water, and let it cook until well blended.
4. Stir occasionally, and do not use too much bread as it may easily burn on the bottom.
5. When cooked, strain it and place it over the fire again with brown sugar, whole cinnamon and lemon peel, and after it has come to a boil, it is ready to serve.
6. Cream is offered at the table with this dish.
