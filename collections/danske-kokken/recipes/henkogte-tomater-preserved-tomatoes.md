## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the fruit to loosen the skin, then peel them and remove all the juice you can without pressing them too hard
2. Then bring slowly to a boil in a pot and cook 10 minutes
3. Remove excess juice with a cup or ladle, and fill the tomatoes boiled into glass jars
