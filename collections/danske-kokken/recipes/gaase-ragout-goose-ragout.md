## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- salt
- color (culinary coloring)
- butter
- flour
- water
- wine
- paprika
- boiled apple halves
- puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Either boil or roast the goose whole, or cut it into smaller pieces.
2. After rinsing the pieces in cold water, place them over heat in just enough boiling salted water with color so that the sauce becomes more concentrated.
3. Brown a slice of butter and bake flour in it, then thin with the strained and skimmed cooking liquid.
4. Give the sauce color with culinary coloring and add wine and paprika to taste.
5. Bring the meat to a boil in the sauce, serve with the sauce poured over, and garnish with boiled apple halves and puff pastry strips.
6. If making ragout from leftovers of roast goose, use the leftover sauce from the roast along with other good stock, meat extract, or Maggi bouillon cubes.
7. The sauce can also be made with pickled cucumbers, pickles, truffles, mushrooms, olives, and chestnuts.
