## Ingredients

- 1 pound fish
- salt
- 2 ounces cold butter, or half butter and half marrow
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 eggs
- milk
- cream
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Rinse the fish and cut it open from the back
2. Rinse it again on the inside so that the fish smell does not become too strong
3. Dry it with a cloth
4. Scrape and mince 1 pound of fish first with salt, then with 2 ounces of cold butter or half butter and half marrow, 1 tablespoon of flour and two eggs
5. When it is minced well and thoroughly combined, stir it with milk, cream and nutmeg
6. If the forcemeat is not fine, it can be pressed through a fine sieve or open sieve
7. The fish can be kept until the next day by tying it at the tail so blood and slime can drain away
8. Wash it again before scraping
