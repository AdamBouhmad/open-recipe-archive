## Ingredients

- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) boiling stock
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) cold butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- One tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- Tarragon vinegar or juice of one lemon
- Egg yolks
- Thin potato slices

## Directions

1. Stir the cold butter, salt, pepper, finely chopped parsley, and tarragon vinegar or lemon juice into the boiling stock
2. Thicken the sauce with 2-3 egg yolks
3. Turn thin slices of potato into it
4. Serve warm with veal and lamb roasts, veal cutlets, etc.
