## Ingredients

- String beans
- Salt

## Directions

1. Choose beans that are not too coarse.
2. Dry, remove seams, and cut finely, placing them as cut in a bowl in layers with salt.
3. The next day, press down the layers and pack the beans tightly together in a stone jar, always with a little salt sprinkled between layers and over the top.
4. Place a piece of linen cloth on top, then a perforated wooden bottom fitting the size of the jar.
5. Finally, place a press on top and put the jar in a dry place.
6. Each time the jar is opened and beans are taken out, rinse the cloth in warm water and dry the inside of the jar.
7. It is common to cook the beans just as they are taken from the jar and rinsed in cold water, drying them after cooking if necessary.
