## Ingredients

- brown stock
- cooked calf's head, cut into thin strips
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- meat and fish balls
- nutmeg
- ginger
- cayenne pepper
- raw egg yolks
- Madeira or port wine

## Directions

1. Make brown stock as previously described; add thin strips of cooked calf's head; if lacking strong stock, you can also take the soup in which the calf's head was cooked, when it is well cleaned of hair, but it is better to avoid this; add hard-boiled egg yolks, meat and fish balls, whose size should be like the egg yolks, and nutmeg, ginger and cayenne; root vegetables and parsley are not used
2. At serving, smooth it with a couple of raw egg yolks, and add Madeira or port wine according to the portion size
