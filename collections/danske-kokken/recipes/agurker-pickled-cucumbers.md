## Ingredients

- Cucumbers, large or small
- Strong salt brine
- Vinegar
- Dill seeds or sprigs
- Red and black pepper
- Bay leaves
- Black peppercorns

## Directions

1. Place the cucumbers in strong salt brine
2. The next day, remove them and dry them thoroughly
3. Bring vinegar to a boil and give the cucumbers a quick boil, a few at a time
4. Remove them with a fish slice and add new ones
5. Continue until all cucumbers have had one boil
6. Discard the vinegar
7. As the cucumbers are placed in the crock, add dill seeds (which may be dried), red and black pepper, and some bay leaves between them
8. Pour new vinegar over in the proportion of 23 quarts to one peck small cucumbers, boiled with black peppercorns and poured hot over the cucumbers
9. Add dill on top (or you can have sprigs of dill throughout, as it looks nice when served)
10. Place a cloth over and tie the crock after the vinegar has cooled
