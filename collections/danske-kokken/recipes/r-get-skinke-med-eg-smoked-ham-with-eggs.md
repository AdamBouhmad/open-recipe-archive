## Ingredients

- Butter
- Smoked ham (the lean part only)
- Eggs
- Parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown a little butter in the pan, in which fry slices of soaked smoked ham (but only the lean part) until it is slightly browned.
2. Break eggs and place them between the meat in the same way as for poached eggs, but so that it can form into a cake.
3. Bake until the eggs are set (but the yolks must not be hard), and with care so it does not burn.
4. Do not turn it, but slide it from the pan onto the plate.
