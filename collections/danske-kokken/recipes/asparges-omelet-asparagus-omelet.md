## Ingredients

- good asparagus
- water
- 12 drams butter
- 15 drams flour
- 1 measure cream
- salt
- sugar
- nutmeg
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel good asparagus, cut into thumb-length pieces and cook in water until tender
2. Make a sauce of 12 drams butter and 15 drams flour, thin with 1 measure cream
3. Add the cooked asparagus with salt, sugar and a little nutmeg
4. Stir well together with 6 egg yolks
5. Carefully fold in the beaten egg whites
6. Pour the entire mixture into a buttered and breadcrumbed dish
7. Bake for half an hour in a warm oven
