## Ingredients

- 4-5 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 spoonful anchovies made into anchovy butter
- 1 cup chopped herbs such as celery, leek and a little thyme
- 3 apples
- 4 spoonfuls fine culinary oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 3 teaspoons mustard
- capers

## Directions

1. Rub the hard-boiled egg yolks fine in a bowl with a spoonful of anchovies made into anchovy butter, chopped herbs such as celery, leek and a little thyme, 3 apples grated on a grater, 4 spoonfuls fine culinary oil and 2 tablespoons vinegar.
2. Mix well together and let stand for 2 hours to draw, then strain through a sieve.
3. Add 3 teaspoons mustard and some capers.
4. Chill on ice until serving.
