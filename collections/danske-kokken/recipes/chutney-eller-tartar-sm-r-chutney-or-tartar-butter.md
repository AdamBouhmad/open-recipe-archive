## Ingredients

- ¼ kg (½ pound) stirred butter
- 2 tablespoons chutney
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon mixed mustard
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. To ¼ kg stirred butter, mix in 2 tablespoons chutney that has first been pressed through a coarse sieve, 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, 1 tablespoon mixed mustard, and a little lemon juice.
2. For fried flounder, hornfish, etc., the chutney can be omitted.
