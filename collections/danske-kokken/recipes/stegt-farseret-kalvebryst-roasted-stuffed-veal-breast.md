## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Forcemeat of veal or pork
- Butter
- Broth or water
- Culinary coloring
- Salt
- Parsley and puff pastry for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal breast the same way as for boiled stuffed veal breast.
2. Brown in butter and roast either in the oven or in a pot, basting with broth or water in which culinary coloring and salt have been added.
3. When serving, remove the thread and slice the meat, then reassemble the slices and garnish with parsley and puff pastry strips, or serve in a puff pastry border.
4. Make a sauce from the pan drippings as for veal roast, and serve with vegetables.
