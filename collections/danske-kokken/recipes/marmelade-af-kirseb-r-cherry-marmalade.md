## Ingredients

- Black preserved cherries with pits removed
- 5 pounds of sugar per 1 pound of cherries
- Kernels from some of the broken pits

## Directions

1. Pit the cherries
2. Break some of the pits, remove the kernels and cook them with the berries
3. Otherwise the treatment is as described in recipe 480, but using only 5 pounds of sugar per 1 pound of cherries
