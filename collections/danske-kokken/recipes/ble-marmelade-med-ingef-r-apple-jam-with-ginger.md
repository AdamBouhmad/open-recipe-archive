## Ingredients

- Chopped apples with seed cavity and blossom end removed
- Powdered sugar (1 1/2 kg to 2 kg apple pulp)
- Water (1/2 l)
- Ground ginger (50 gr), bound in a cloth

## Directions

1. Put the chopped apples in the pot together with 1/2 l water and the ground ginger, bound in a piece of cloth.
2. When the apples are completely cooked, press the pulp through the sieve.
3. Weigh it and cook with the sugar until it is sufficiently stiff; then fill into jars, cover with paper and tie up.
