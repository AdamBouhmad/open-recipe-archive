## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 2 pints water
- a little egg white
- flavoring such as cognac, rum, lemon juice, orange juice, chocolate, peppermint water, or vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar with water and a little egg white. During cooking, skim it well, and to test if it is cooked enough, dip a spoon in water and then in the sugar. When you bite the sugar you get on the spoon, it must release from your teeth; as long as it sticks, it is not cooked enough. The sugar can either be poured hot onto a buttered sheet and when it is almost cold, cut into square pieces; or it can be molded in forms created from dry starch on a sheet. Different flavors are given by the addition of cognac, rum, lemon or orange juice, chocolate, peppermint water, vanilla, and similar.
