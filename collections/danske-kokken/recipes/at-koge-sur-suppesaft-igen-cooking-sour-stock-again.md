## Ingredients

- sour meat stock
- raw or cooked, chopped meat bones (fresh and good)

## Directions

1. If preserved meat stock from one day to the next becomes sour in summer, boil the soup for a quarter hour or longer if needed with some raw or cooked, chopped meat bones.
2. The sourness will be boiled away by the chalky substance contained in the bones, and the soup will become good and usable.
3. Alternatively, let the sour food boil quite dry with a teaspoon of magnesia, which will remove all sourness.
