## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 0.75 pound powdered sugar
- zest and strained juice of 1 lemon
- lightly pounded almonds
- 0.75 pound rice flour
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the powdered sugar
2. Add the finely grated zest and strained juice of 1 lemon, some lightly pounded almonds, and 0.75 pound rice flour
3. Finally fold in the 6 egg whites beaten to peaks
4. Bake the entire mixture in a pan at even oven heat
