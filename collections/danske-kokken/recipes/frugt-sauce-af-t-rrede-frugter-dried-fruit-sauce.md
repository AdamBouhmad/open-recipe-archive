## Ingredients

- Dried apples, pears, or apricots
- Water
- Sugar
- Sago starch
- Vanilla essence, lemon zest, or cinnamon (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the dried fruit, soak it the day before, and cook in water with sugar until so tender that it can be pressed through a sieve.
2. Smooth the sauce with mixed sago starch.
3. Add vanilla essence, lemon zest, or cinnamon if desired.
4. For dried cherries: crush them before cooking.
