## Ingredients

- 3 ounces gelatine
- 1 quart boiling water
- juice of 3 large lemons
- 1 pint white sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve 3 ounces of gelatine.
2. Add 1 quart of boiling water, the juice of 3 large lemons, and 1 pint of white sugar.
