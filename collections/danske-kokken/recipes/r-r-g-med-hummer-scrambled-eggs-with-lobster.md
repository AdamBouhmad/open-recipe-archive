## Ingredients

- Fresh lobster
- Butter
- Water
- Beaten eggs
- Watercress

## Directions

1. If using fresh lobster, boil and pick the meat. Pound the shells together with a slice of butter. Heat this over the fire, boil it in water and strain it. Skim off the lobster butter that floats on top and put it in a pan to be used for the scrambled eggs. Mix the lobster meat into the beaten eggs. Make the scrambled eggs as usual. Garnish with watercress. Canned lobster can be used very well for this; replace the lobster butter with a little lobster and crayfish extract.
