## Ingredients

- Firm, not overripe tomatoes
- Cold salt brine
- 10 gr (2 Kvint) salt to 1 l (1 Pot) boiling water
- Icing sugar

## Directions

1. Dry firm, not overripe tomatoes and place them in the canning jars
2. Pour the cold salt brine, 10 gr (2 Kvint) to 1 l (1 Pot) boiling water, and a very little icing sugar over them
3. Close the jars with ring and clamp and cook in the kettle for 15 minutes
4. Cool in the kettle or under a woolen blanket
