## Ingredients

- slices of veal or pork tenderloin
- veal or pork forcemeat
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- soup or gravy
- sago flour
- wine
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Pound the meat slices flat and coat with a layer of forcemeat.
2. Roll up and tie with string.
3. Dip in beaten egg and bread crumbs.
4. Brown in butter in a pot.
5. Pour a little soup or gravy over them and braise gently, basting frequently.
6. Strain the sauce and thicken with sago flour mixed with wine, adding mushrooms.
7. Alternatively, serve with vegetables and gravy sauce, omitting wine and mushrooms.
