## Ingredients

- Cold roasted pheasant or leftovers thereof
- Game stock or soup
- Arrowroot
- Sherry or Madeira
- Coloring
- Truffles
- Toasted bread, bread croutons, or slices

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones of a cold roasted pheasant or the leftovers in smaller pieces
2. Crush the bones and cook them in soup or game stock
3. Dilute arrowroot in Sherry or Madeira and stir into the stock along with coloring
4. Let the sauce boil for at least 10 minutes, then strain it
5. Add the meat to warm it through, but do not let it boil
6. Serve on a hot dish garnished with toasted bread, bread croutons, or slices
7. Truffles may be added to the sauce
8. The salmi may be served warm or cold
