## Ingredients

- Icelandic ptarmigan
- 1¼ dl (½ pint) vinegar
- 1 l (1 pot) water
- Milk
- Sauce (see Pheasant, No. 963)

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the ptarmigan and soak for 4 to 5 hours in a mixture of 1¼ dl vinegar to 1 l cold water.
2. Rinse well and soak in milk overnight.
3. Lard and brown the ptarmigan, then braise in a pot and drip with a previously made sauce (see Pheasant, No. 963).
4. Roast over gentle heat for approximately 1 hour, which will result in excellent flavour.
