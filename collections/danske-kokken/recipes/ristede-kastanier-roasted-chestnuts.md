## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Coarse salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a cross at the point of each chestnut. Place them on a frying pan with plenty of coarse salt and roast them tender over a brisk fire until they easily slip from the shell. Serve very hot in a folded napkin and eat with softened butter.
