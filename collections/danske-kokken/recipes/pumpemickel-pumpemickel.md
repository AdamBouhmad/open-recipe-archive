## Ingredients

- 8 medium eggs
- 1/2 Kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- 25 gr (5 quintals) hartshorn salt
- 125 gr (1/4 lb) crushed almonds
- 1 Kg (2 lb) flour

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs well with the powdered sugar, hartshorn salt, and crushed almonds
2. Work the flour in
3. Roll the dough and cut into strips approximately 2 finger-widths wide and the length of the baking sheet
4. Brush with egg and bake at good heat
5. Cut into smaller pieces after baking, while still warm
