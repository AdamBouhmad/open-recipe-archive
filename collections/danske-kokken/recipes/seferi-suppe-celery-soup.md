## Ingredients

- a small wheat bread, about one pound by weight
- 6 large celeries
- meat stock
- white root vegetables, cut into pieces
- small meat balls

## Directions

1. Grate the wheat bread and cook it in a small meat stock together with 6 large celeries until they can easily be pressed apart
2. Dilute the whole mixture with meat stock, cook a little, press through a colander, then through a sieve and place it back on the fire with some cut white root vegetables
3. When it boils, add small meat balls
4. If meat balls are lacking, the soup can be eaten with toasted bread
