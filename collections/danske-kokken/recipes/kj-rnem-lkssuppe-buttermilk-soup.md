## Ingredients

- 4 pots buttermilk
- One-third pound rice flour
- A little lemon peel
- Whole cinnamon
- A little chopped almonds
- Half a pot rinsed raisins
- Sugar to taste
- Cold sweet cream for serving

## Directions

1. Mix a couple of pots of buttermilk with one-third pound of rice flour and place over heat together with the rest of the buttermilk, a little lemon peel, whole cinnamon, a little chopped almonds, and half a pot of rinsed raisins.
2. Stir continuously during cooking.
3. When ready, add sugar to taste.
4. At serving, cold sweet cream can also be added.
