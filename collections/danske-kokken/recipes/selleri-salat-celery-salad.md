## Ingredients

- cooked finely chopped celery
- lost mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Toss cooked finely chopped celery in lost mayonnaise
2. This salad is given with game roasts, woodcocks, pheasants, partridges, etc.
3. It can be arranged in shells cut from fresh cabbage leaves, or in small clam shells, which are placed around the roast
