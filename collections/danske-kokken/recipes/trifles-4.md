## Ingredients

- macarons
- sherry or rum
- cream (vanilla, liqueur, or with lemon or orange essence)
- whipped cream
- jam (optional in this English version)

## Directions

1. Place some macarons dipped in sherry or rum on the bottom of a glass bowl.
2. Pour a cream over them, which can be vanilla or liqueur cream, or cream with lemon or orange essence.
3. Over the set cream, layer whipped cream.
4. Note: In this English version, jam is omitted, though it is generally layered with macarons and cream in the traditional recipe.
