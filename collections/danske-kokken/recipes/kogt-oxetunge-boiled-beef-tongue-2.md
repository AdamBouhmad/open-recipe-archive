## Ingredients

- 1 fresh beef tongue
- 1/3 ounce salt
- Pepper
- 2 cloves
- 4 ounces onion

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue in water for one hour.
2. Boil for 3 hours with 3 quarts of water and the seasonings.
3. When well cooked, remove the skin.
4. Serve with tomato sauce.
