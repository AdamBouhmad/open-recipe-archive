## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds scalded rice groats
- 3 Pots sweet milk
- 20 Quintals melted butter
- 10 beaten eggs
- 0.5 Pound washed raisins without seeds
- 0.5 Pound finely chopped lard
- 12 Quintals sweet finely ground almonds
- 5 Quintals bitter finely ground almonds
- 18 Quintals ground icing sugar
- Ground nutmeg
- Intestines
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. 2 Pounds of scalded rice groats are cooked half-soft into a stiff porridge in 3 pots of sweet milk.
2. When the porridge is somewhat cooled, add 20 Quintals of melted butter, 10 beaten eggs, 0.5 Pound of washed raisins without seeds, 0.5 Pound of finely chopped lard, 12 Quintals of sweet and 5 Quintals of bitter, finely ground almonds, 18 Quintals of ground icing sugar and a little ground nutmeg.
3. Stir everything well and stuff into cleaned intestines that have first lain for one hour in soak in lukewarm milk.
4. They are then cooked in water with a little salt in it.
5. The sausages should not be stuffed too firmly; the intestines should only be three-quarters full.
6. They are arranged whole, either boiled or roasted with melted butter.
