## Ingredients

- Long cabbage leaves
- Water
- Salt
- Soda
- Butter
- Flour
- Cream or good milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves and rinse them well.
2. Cook them in water with salt and a small amount of soda.
3. When the cabbage is tender, drain it through a colander and press out the water well.
4. Chop it very finely.
5. Bake butter and a small amount of flour together and dilute with cream or good milk.
6. Add the chopped cabbage and bring to a boil.
7. Season with sugar and salt.
8. Finish with a piece of cold butter.
9. Serve in a ragout dish with a wreath of glazed chestnuts or browned potatoes arranged around the cabbage.
