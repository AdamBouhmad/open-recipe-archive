## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 24 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 12 egg whites
- Butter
- Paper
- Fine powdered sugar
- Water
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound 1 pound sweet almonds
2. Stir 24 egg yolks with 1 pound powdered sugar until white and foaming, then fold in the pounded almonds carefully
3. Whip 12 egg whites to peaks and fold carefully into the aforementioned mixture
4. Butter a paper and use it to line the form, then pour in the mixture
5. Bake the tart for 1 hour in a moderate oven
6. When somewhat cooled, turn out of the form and decorate on top with sugar glaze made from fine powdered sugar moistened with a little cold water and mixed with a few drops of lemon juice, then dry in the oven
