## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice grains
- 3 cups sweet, boiling cream
- 1 pound white sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scald the rice grains in several changes of water and pour into 3 cups of sweet, boiling cream with 1 pound white sugar.
2. Cook gently for approximately 2 hours until the rice is completely tender.
3. Press through a coarse sieve.
4. Add the grated lemon zest and juice of 2 lemons.
5. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and gently fold in.
6. Pour the mixture into a deep cake pan, sprinkle with crushed white sugar, and place in a moderately warm oven.
7. When it has risen, it is done. Serve either without sauce or with raspberry sauce.
