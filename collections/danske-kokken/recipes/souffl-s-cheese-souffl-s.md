## Ingredients

- 125 grams (1/4 pound) grated Parmesan cheese
- 50 grams (10 quintals) butter
- 50 grams (10 quintals) potato flour
- 4 eggs
- 3/4 deciliter (1/2 pint) milk

## Directions

1. Stir the flour and milk together and place in a saucepan along with the cold butter
2. Set over heat and bring to a boil with constant stirring until thickened
3. Lift from heat and stir in the cheese, egg yolks and a little salt
4. Let the dough cool
5. Only when ready to bake, fold in the beaten egg whites
6. Fill into paper capsules and bake in a hot oven approximately 1/4 hour
7. Arrange as quickly as possible in a pyramid on a folded napkin, placed on a platter
