## Ingredients

- ¼ kg (½ pound) stirred butter
- 4 to 6 tablespoons thick, strongly reduced tomato purée
- Paprika

## Directions

1. ¼ kg stirred butter is given 4 to 6 tablespoons of thick, strongly reduced tomato purée and a little paprika.
2. Served with roasted meat and fish.
