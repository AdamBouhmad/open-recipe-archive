## Ingredients

- Good quality beef, cut into cubes of nearly one inch
- Bacon, weighing one quarter of the beef weight
- Onions, weighing half the bacon weight
- Ground pepper
- Ground caraway
- Salt
- Broth
- Red wine

## Directions

1. Cut beef into small square cubes of nearly one inch.
2. Cut bacon into slightly smaller cubes.
3. Cut onions into pieces.
4. Place the bacon in a casserole and fry it.
5. When the bacon begins to brown and becomes crisp, add the onions.
6. When the onions are also brown and tender, add the beef.
7. After the beef has fried for 15 minutes, pour over enough broth and red wine to cover it.
8. Add ground pepper, ground caraway, and salt.
9. Boil the goulash well covered until the meat is tender and the broth is nearly evaporated.
10. Serve with mashed potatoes.
