## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine wheat flour
- 1/4 pound butter
- 12 grams sugar
- 12 grams washed raisins
- 12 grams washed currants
- 12 grams sweet, scalded and chopped almonds
- 3 eggs
- a little cardamom
- 1 tablespoon yeast
- milk

## Directions

1. Lay the flour on a baking table and add 36 grams cold butter, the eggs, cardamom, currants, yeast, sugar, and enough milk to make a soft wheat bread dough.
2. Knead well with the hands.
3. Roll out the dough to half a finger's thickness in a round or oval shape.
4. Fold up the dough around the edge into tongues; in every other tongue place almonds, in the other raisins with a small lump of remaining butter.
5. Melt the rest of the butter and brush the cake with it, then sprinkle a good layer of ground cinnamon and powdered sugar.
6. If desired, thin slices of citron peel can be placed here and there.
7. Let the cake rise for 1/2 hour before it goes in the oven.
