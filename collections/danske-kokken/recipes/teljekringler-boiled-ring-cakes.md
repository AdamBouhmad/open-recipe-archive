## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- 4 measures sweet milk
- 5 whole eggs
- 4 spoonfuls sugar
- a little crushed cardamom
- 1 spoonful dissolved yeast

## Directions

1. Work 1 pound fine flour, 4 measures sweet milk, 5 whole eggs, 4 spoonfuls sugar, a little crushed cardamom and 1 spoonful dissolved yeast together well until it shows eyes and begins to rise.
2. Form small rings from the dough and immediately place them in boiling water.
3. When they float up, remove them from the water and let them drain for a moment.
4. Place them on a buttered pan and bake in a moderately warm oven.
5. When they are removed from the oven, brush them with boiling water to make them glossy.
6. These rings can be stored for a long time without spoiling.
