## Ingredients

- sandeel
- salt
- cold water
- milk

## Directions

1. Scrape and clean the sandeel, clip off the fins, then let it soak in ice-cold water with salt for about an hour
2. Place it over the heat in cold water, salt, and milk
3. When the water boils, skim it
4. Cook over even heat for about an hour, then remove the pot from the heat and let the sandeel rest in the hot fish water for a quarter hour before arranging it
5. Serve with boiled peeled potatoes and sour butter sauce
