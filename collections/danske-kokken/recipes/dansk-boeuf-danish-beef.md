## Ingredients

- Beef from the tenderloin, approximately half a pound per portion
- Wheat flour
- Fine salt
- Ground pepper
- Butter
- Onions
- Water or beer

## Directions

1. Cut beef from the tenderloin into pieces, 6 to 8 pieces per pound.
2. Remove the sinews from each piece and beat well with a mallet.
3. Chop with a knife like a cutlet.
4. Place finished pieces on a plate without letting them touch each other.
5. Heat butter in a frying pan over the fire until browned.
6. Turn each piece of beef in wheat flour mixed with fine salt and ground pepper.
7. Place the beef in the pan and fry it.
8. As the beef is fried, place it on a warm serving dish.
9. Add a good piece of butter to the pan along with peeled onions sliced into slices.
10. Bake the onions until tender over gentle heat.
11. When done, thin the sauce with a little water or beer.
12. Add salt to taste.
13. Pour the sauce over the beef.
14. Serve with peeled, boiled potatoes.
