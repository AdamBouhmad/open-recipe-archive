## Ingredients

- Perch
- Parsley
- Farce

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the fish, cut it open along the belly, and remove the backbone
2. Spread the fish out and coat it with a layer of farce made from smaller perches
3. Finely chopped parsley may be mixed into the farce
4. Sew the belly together and cook the fish in salted water
5. Arrange garnished with parsley, and remove the threads with which they were sewn
6. Serve with sauce hollandaise and boiled potatoes
