## Ingredients

- Berries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Bring slowly to a boil in a spacious pot
2. If they give off much juice, remove it with a cup when they begin to boil; when they are almost dry, add sugar, 1 spoonful to each quart of berries
3. Cook for a quarter hour and fill as usual
4. This method can be used for all kinds of berries
