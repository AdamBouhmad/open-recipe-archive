## Ingredients

- 4 ounces rice
- Water
- 4.5 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- 3 quarts milk

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well in two waters and place in a form, which may be earthenware.
2. Add the sugar and lemon extract; pour 3 quarts of milk over it.
3. Place in a moderate oven to bake for 1.5 hours, or until it reaches a creamy consistency.
