## Ingredients

- Fresh lobster
- Butter
- Eggs
- Watercress

## Directions

1. If using fresh lobster, cook and shell them and pound the shells together with a slice of butter.
2. Place it over the fire, cook down in water and strain, and the lobster butter, which floats on top, is skimmed off and put in the pan to be used for the scrambled eggs.
3. Stir the lobster meat into the beaten eggs.
4. Garnish the scrambled eggs with watercress.
5. Canned lobster can be used excellently for this, lobster butter is then replaced with some lobster and crayfish extract.
