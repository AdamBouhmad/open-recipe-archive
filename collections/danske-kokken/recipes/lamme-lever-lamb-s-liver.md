## Ingredients

- Lamb's liver, cut into fairly thick pieces
- Flour or breadcrumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter or good frying fat
- Gravy
- Cream
- Butter for sauce
- Vegetables: turnip greens, gooseberry sauce or rhubarb compote

## Directions

1. Treat as calf's liver and cut into fairly thick pieces.
2. Lay them spread out so they are not too wet, then turn them in flour or breadcrumbs mixed with pepper and salt.
3. Fry on a pan in butter or good frying fat.
4. Make the sauce as for calf's liver but without onions.
5. You can cut the fat from raw smoked bacon in large cubes, fry it along with the liver and place it on the platter with it.
6. This requires less butter and fat for frying the liver.
7. Serve with vegetables, preferably with turnip greens, gooseberry sauce or rhubarb compote.
