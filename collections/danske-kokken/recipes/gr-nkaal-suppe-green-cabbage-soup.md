## Ingredients

- 1.5-2 kg (3-4 lb) salted pork belly or brisket
- 4 heads green cabbage

## Directions

1. The soup for green cabbage is generally cooked in salted, striped pork belly or brisket.
2. While this cooks, remove the cabbage from the stems; rinse it well, squeeze out the water, then chop it very finely.
3. When the pork is cooked, skim the fat from the soup, and in this fat bake flour, which is thinned with the soup as in the previous recipes.
4. Alternatively, chop a couple of spoonfuls of flour together with the cabbage, which is added to the soup and cooked for several minutes.
5. The pork is served either cold or hot with the cabbage.
