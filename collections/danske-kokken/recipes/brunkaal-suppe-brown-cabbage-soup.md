## Ingredients

- 1 medium white cabbage head
- Butter
- 1 Dessertspoonful Powdered sugar
- 5 L (5 Pots) thin spare soup
- Kulør (caramel coloring)

## Directions

1. Slice the white cabbage finely
2. Brown in butter and 1 dessertspoonful powdered sugar
3. Pour 5 L (5 Pots) thin spare soup over it
4. If the soup does not have sufficient color, add a little kulør
5. Let it boil for at least 2 hours
