## Ingredients

- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) strong, even gravy sauce
- A couple of chopped, lightly browned onions
- 4 tablespoonfuls of finely cut Piccalilly (Pickles), pickled in mustard
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mustard
- Salt
- Wine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the strong, even gravy sauce
2. Add the chopped, lightly browned onions and bring to a boil in the sauce together with the finely cut Piccalilly and mustard
3. Season with salt, wine and sugar to taste
4. The sauce should have a nice, brown color
