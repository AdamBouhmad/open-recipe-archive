## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- 125 grams sugar
- 4 eggs
- 125 grams flour
- Cream filling
- vanilla
- water glaze
- browned, chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar into foam, add eggs and flour
2. Divide this mixture into 2 bases and bake in a greased layer cake form
3. Put them together with cream flavored with vanilla
4. Coat the cake with water glaze and sprinkle with browned, chopped almonds
