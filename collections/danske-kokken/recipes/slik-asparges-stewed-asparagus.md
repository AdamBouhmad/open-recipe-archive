## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus very well while it is raw and place it in cold water as you scrape it; the tip or head is not scraped, so you hold it and scrape downward. It is best to cook large and small separately, as the large ones require longer cooking. Tie them in bundles and cook them in salt and water. They are eaten with melted butter.
