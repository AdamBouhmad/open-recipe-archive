## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg blackcurrants
- 625 gr sugar
- 2.5 dl water per 0.5 kg berries
- 1.75 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice

## Directions

1. Rinse 0.5 kg blackcurrants and cook with 2.5 dl water until the berries are cooked apart, then pour into the jelly cloth.
2. When the juice has run through, weigh it.
3. Cook sugar and water (see No. 93), and add the juice at the end to cook for 10 minutes.
4. Skim well during cooking.
5. Pour the jelly into jars, which are sealed when it has begun to set.
6. The berries remaining in the cloth can be cooked with sugar and served with roast.
