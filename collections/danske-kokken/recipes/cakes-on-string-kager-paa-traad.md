## Ingredients

- raisins, cooked slightly
- blanched almonds, folded in
- small pieces of apple
- batter for Apple Slices

## Directions

1. Thread raisins, blanched almonds, and small pieces of apple alternately on a good string
2. Thread until about 1 yard is full and tight, leaving a piece of string at each end to hold
3. Dip in batter made according to the method for liquid Apple Slices
4. Place in boiling clarified fat and cook until light brown
5. Draw out the string before the cake is cold
