## Ingredients

- Plums
- 250 grams (1/2 pund) powdered sugar
- 375 milliliters (1/2 pægel) water

## Directions

1. Scald the plums, prick them, and give them one boil in a sugar syrup made from the sugar and water.
2. Green plums, prune plums, and small mirabelles can be treated in the same way.
