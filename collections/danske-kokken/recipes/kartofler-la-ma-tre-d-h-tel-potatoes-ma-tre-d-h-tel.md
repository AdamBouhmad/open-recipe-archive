## Ingredients

- About 12 large potatoes
- 1/4 liter (1 pint) soup or Maggi bouillon cube dissolved in boiling water
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- Salt
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) butter
- 2-3 egg yolks
- Juice of 1 lemon, or tarragon or ordinary vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the soup or bouillon with water, chopped parsley, salt and butter.
2. Bring to a boil and bind with 2-3 egg yolks.
3. Add the juice of 1 lemon or tarragon or ordinary vinegar to taste.
4. Slice firm, cold potatoes into thin slices.
5. Warm them gently in the sauce.
6. Serve with open-faced sandwiches or as a vegetable alongside fried fish and meat.
