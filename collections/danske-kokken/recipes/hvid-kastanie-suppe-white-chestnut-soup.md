## Ingredients

- white spare broth
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 L (1 mug) raw cream
- fillets of cooked or roasted chicken or squab

## Directions

1. The difference is only that white spare broth is used for this, and that it is bound with 4 egg yolks stirred in 1/4 L (1 mug) raw cream.
2. Fillets of cooked or roasted chicken or squab can be placed in the soup.
