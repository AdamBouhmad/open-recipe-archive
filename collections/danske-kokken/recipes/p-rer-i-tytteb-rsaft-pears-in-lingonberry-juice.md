## Ingredients

- 250 gr powder or Demerara sugar
- 1 l lingonberry juice
- peeled whole or halved pears

## Directions

1. When picking lingonberries for preserving, separate the less good ones, rinse them and put them in a pot with enough hot water to cover them
2. Cook for at least 1/4 hour and strain through a fine sieve or through a juice cloth without pressing so the juice is clear
3. Measure the juice and give it a boil with the specified amount of sugar
4. Add the peeled whole or halved pears to cook until tender
5. Remove pears and place in jar while the juice cooks again until thick
6. Pour the hot juice over the fruit in the jar and seal immediately
7. The lingonberries can be cooked again with more water and the juice strained off for use in fruit soup
