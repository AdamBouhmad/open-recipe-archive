## Ingredients

- 3 Pints sweet cream
- A little sugar
- 2 Pints flour
- 4 Egg yolks
- 2 Pints melted butter
- 1 Pint cold water

## Directions

1. Whip the sweet cream with a little sugar.
2. Add 2 pints flour, 4 egg yolks, and 2 pints melted butter.
3. Thin with 1 pint cold water.
4. Bake these pancakes so thinly that you do not need to turn them, and they will still be stiff.
5. If you wish to serve them rolled up, roll them warm around a wooden stick or similar.
6. Grease the pan with butter only the first time; not afterwards.
7. Serve with powdered sugar sprinkled over.
