## Ingredients

- 2½ to 3 Kg (5 to 6 S) lamb forequarter
- Butter
- Salt
- Paprika
- 1 L (1 Pot) broth, soup or water
- 1¼ Dl (½ Pægl) Madeira
- 6 to 8 tablespoons tomato purée
- Coloring
- Fresh or preserved mushrooms
- Sago flour
- Puff pastry slices for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces as for fricassée and wash well in cold water.
2. On a roasting pan, brown the meat in a good slice of butter, then place in a pot, sprinkle with salt and paprika and pour over 1 L (1 Pot) broth, soup or water, 1¼ Dl (½ Pægl) Madeira, 6 to 8 tablespoons tomato purée and coloring so the broth gets a nice color.
3. Cook the meat in this until tender; then add fresh or preserved mushrooms and give it a good boil in the sauce, which is thickened with sago flour.
4. Serve the meat in the sauce and garnish with puff pastry slices.
