## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound rice grains
- 3 pints milk
- Handful of finely chopped sweet almonds
- 2 ounces butter
- Nutmeg flower
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 4 eggs
- Chicken meat
- Sauce with thickening and mustard, cayenne or curry
- Beaten eggs
- Crushed rusks
- 3 to 4 tablespoons clarified butter

## Directions

1. Cook the rice grains in the milk to make a porridge that should not be overcooked, and cook a handful of finely chopped sweet almonds in it
2. Stir in 2 ounces of butter, nutmeg flower and 1 teaspoon white sugar
3. When cold, add 4 eggs
4. When the chicken meat is half-cooked and cold, place it in a dish as for pâté
5. Pour the cold sauce, which is made with only a thickening and with mustard, cayenne or curry, over it
6. Spread the cottin over 2 finger-widths thick and make a hole in the middle for steam
7. Coat with beaten eggs and sprinkle with crushed rusks
8. The oven should not be hotter than for wheat bread; when it has set until slightly browned on top, take it from the oven and coat with 3 to 4 tablespoons of clarified butter
9. When the meat is presumed to be tender, the cottin is also roasted
