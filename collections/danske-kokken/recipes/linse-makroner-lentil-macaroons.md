## Ingredients

- thin rolled puff pastry
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) blanched, peeled, finely chopped almonds
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 6 raw egg whites
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Line the molds with thinly rolled puff pastry
2. Mix together the almond paste of blanched, peeled, finely chopped almonds with powdered sugar, 6 raw egg whites and 1 teaspoon baking powder
3. Place with a teaspoon in the molds
4. Bake the cakes until light brown at even heat
