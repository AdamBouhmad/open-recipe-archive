## Ingredients

- A little flour
- butter
- soup stock
- a couple of egg yolks
- oil
- pickled shallots
- potato slices
- watercress, chervil, or parsley

## Directions

1. Cook a little flour and butter together, thin with soup stock to make a thin white sauce, thickened with a couple of egg yolks
2. Stir the sauce until cold, then add 2 tablespoons of oil, pickled shallot vinegar to taste, and some finely chopped pickled shallots
3. Toss the potato slices in the sauce
4. At serving, sprinkle the salad with watercress, finely chopped chervil, or parsley
5. Serve cold with hot fried fish and roasts
