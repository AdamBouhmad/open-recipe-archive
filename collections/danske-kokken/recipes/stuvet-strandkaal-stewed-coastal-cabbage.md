## Ingredients

- Coastal cabbage stalks
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cut the stalks into smaller pieces and cook in water with salt.
2. Use a sauce like that for stewed asparagus to serve with it.
