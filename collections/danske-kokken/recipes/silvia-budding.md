## Ingredients

- 1 Quart milk
- 5 Ounces blanched and finely cut almonds
- 2 Egg yolks
- 4 Spoonfuls white sugar
- 3 Spoonfuls corn starch
- 2 Egg whites, beaten
- 5 Pints rum
- Cranberry sauce or another red sauce

## Directions

1. Reserve a good half cup of the milk
2. Heat the remaining milk with the blanched and finely cut almonds
3. Mix the egg yolks with white sugar
4. Mix the corn starch with the cold milk
5. When the milk is about to boil, add the egg yolks, pouring them in gradually and then pouring into the pot
6. Then add the corn starch mixture
7. When this has come to a boil, remove the pot from the heat
8. Add the beaten egg whites and the rum
9. Let it come to a boil again and pour either into a mold or into small cups, from which it is turned out and eaten cold
10. Cranberry sauce is good with this, or any other red sauce
11. Alternatively it can be made without rum with lemon juice
12. Serves 5 persons
