## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Kg (3 S) Melon
- 1/4 Kg (1 1/2 S) Powdered Sugar
- 2 Lemons

## Directions

1. Peel the ripe melon, scrape out the pits and cut the fruit into small pieces, sprinkle with 1/4 Kg (1 1/2 S) powdered sugar and press the juice of 2 lemons over them
2. Leave this for 1 hour and finish it with wine as in Nr. 486
