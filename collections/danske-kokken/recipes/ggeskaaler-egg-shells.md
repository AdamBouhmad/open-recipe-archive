## Ingredients

- 1 measure good, thick cream
- 4 eggs
- fine flour
- melted butter
- light powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Work 1 measure good, thick cream and 4 eggs together well with enough fine flour so that the dough can be rolled very thin to the thickness of a straw.
2. Cut into square pieces, double the size of a playing card.
3. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with light powdered sugar.
4. Bake light brown.
