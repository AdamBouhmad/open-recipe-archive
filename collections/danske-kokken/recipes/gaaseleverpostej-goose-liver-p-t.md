## Ingredients

- 8 to 10 goose livers of medium size
- small-cut truffles
- spices as in the preceding recipe, but only half as much clove
- finely chopped pork shoulder (optional, to lighten the color)

## Directions

1. Wash the livers well and inspect them carefully; cut away any green parts often found from the gallbladder, as the pâté would otherwise taste less good
2. Prepare the farce (forcemeat) as in recipe No. 259, with small-cut truffles and spices as mentioned, but only half as much clove
3. If using Danish goose livers, add some finely chopped pork shoulder to lighten the color, though this diminishes the good taste; select the lightest livers for this purpose
4. Bake the pâté as in recipe No. 259
5. Garnish the pâté with aspic (Sky)
6. Serve as a middle course with bread and butter
