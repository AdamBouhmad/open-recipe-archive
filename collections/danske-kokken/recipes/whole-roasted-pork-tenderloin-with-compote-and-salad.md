## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) pork tenderloin
- Butter, clarified butter, or Palmin
- Thin broth or water
- Food coloring
- Salt
- Flour
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove fat and membranes from the pork tenderloin, beat lightly with a meat mallet and lard if desired.
2. Brown a slice of butter, clarified butter, or Palmin in a pot and place the meat inside.
3. When sufficiently browned, drip either thin broth or water mixed with food coloring and salt over it.
4. Roast over low heat.
5. When the meat is tender, strain the gravy and skim off the fat; use this fat to brown the flour, which is then thinned to make a good sauce, which can be given more strength with the help of Maggi bouillon cubes.
6. When serving, coat the pork tenderloin with some of the sauce, garnish with parsley and serve with compote and salad.
