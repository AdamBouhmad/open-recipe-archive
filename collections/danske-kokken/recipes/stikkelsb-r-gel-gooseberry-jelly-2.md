## Ingredients

- 3 liters (3 pots) green gooseberries
- 1 L (1 pot) water
- 625 g (1 1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) water
- 20 sheets isinglass

## Directions

1. Hull, rinse, and cook the gooseberries with 1 L (1 pot) water and pass through a jelly bag.
2. Cook 625 g (1 1/4 S) powdered sugar and 1/2 L (1/2 pot) water to a syrup, and melt 20 sheets of isinglass in it.
3. Pour the isinglass mixture into the juice.
4. Serve and arrange as Rhubarb Jelly.
