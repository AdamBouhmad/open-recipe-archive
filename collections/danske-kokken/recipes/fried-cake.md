## Ingredients

- ½ pint sour milk or buttermilk
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 2 spoonfuls warm fat
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix together ½ pint sour milk or buttermilk, 2 beaten eggs, 1 cup sugar, ½ teaspoonful soda, and 2 spoonfuls warm fat
2. Add flour until you find it pleasantly thick so it can be handled
3. Press it together well, but do not roll it
4. Place on baking sheet and roll out with a roller, not too thin
5. Cut out cakes with a glass or similar object
6. Cut these out again with a smaller glass so they form rings with small cakes
7. Form them like doughnuts when frying
8. This dough works well for Kleiner too
