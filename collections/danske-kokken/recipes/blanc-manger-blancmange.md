## Ingredients

- 1 pot thick, sweet cream
- 6 quarters isinglass
- Drops of vanilla and bergamot oil
- Sugar to taste, about 1 pound
- Brown caramel sugar, dissolved cochineal, saffron decoction, spinach decoction for coloring

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pot thick, sweet cream until very stiff foam.
2. Add 6 quarters isinglass, first dissolved in boiling water, a few drops of vanilla and bergamot oil, and sugar to taste—about 1 pound.
3. Color one part brown with brown caramel sugar, another red with dissolved cochineal, one yellow with saffron decoction, one green with spinach decoction, and thus you can vary the colors as desired in the different layers with which the jelly mold is filled; but each layer must be set before the next comes on.
4. Set the whole blancmange in a cold place to become stiff.
5. It can also be filled in small molds, but then given only one color.
