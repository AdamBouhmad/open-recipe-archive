## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- Salt
- 4 ounces or 6 large tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 0.5 teaspoon lemon extract
- 0.5 pint milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour together with baking powder and salt.
2. Rub the sugar and butter together well; when mixed but not creamy, gradually add the flour.
3. Make a hollow in the center of the dough and add an egg, lemon extract, and milk.
4. Mix to a soft dough, place in a form greased well with butter and sprinkled well with flour.
5. Boil for approximately 3 hours; test with a stiff straw pushed down into it; if nothing sticks to the straw, it is done.
6. Turn the pudding out of the form and serve with crème sauce.
