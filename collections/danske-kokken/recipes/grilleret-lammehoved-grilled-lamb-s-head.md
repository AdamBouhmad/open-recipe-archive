## Ingredients

- Lamb's head
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread
- Parsley
- Butter, lard or Palmin

## Directions

1. Split the head and remove the brain. Wash it well in several changes of water, then soak it at least 1 hour in cold water.
2. Place it along with the tongue over the fire in salted water. It must cook until the skin can easily be pulled off both the head and the tongue.
3. Pick it clean carefully and remove the palate; cut out the eyes and ears, but the fat behind the eye must remain.
4. Split the tongue and dip it together with the head in egg and bread mixed with salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
5. Brown it in a frying pan in butter, lard or Palmin.
