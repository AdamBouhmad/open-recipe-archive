## Ingredients

- Boiled corned beef
- Dry mustard

## Directions

1. After the meat is boiled, or when you have enough left over from the midday meal, it is chopped up while still warm, not excessively fine, but so that fat and lean are well mixed together; a little dry mustard is stirred in to give it flavor.
2. It is then placed in a pan that can give it form, and pressed together by a strong weight on top of a lid that must be able to slide down into the pan, touching its sides.
3. The next day it can be sliced into well-held together slices.
