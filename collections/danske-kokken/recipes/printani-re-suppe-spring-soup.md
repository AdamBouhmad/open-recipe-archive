## Ingredients

- 3 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 1 celery
- 3 leeks
- 75 g (15 drams) butter
- 4 L (4 pots) soup
- Cooked peas or asparagus
- Water in which peas or asparagus were cooked

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots, turnips, celery, and leeks into small dice and brown them lightly in 75 g (15 drams) butter
2. Pour the soup over the vegetables and cook until tender
3. Skim the soup
4. Just before serving, add cooked peas or asparagus to the tureen along with the water in which they were cooked
