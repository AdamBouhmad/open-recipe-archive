## Ingredients

- Meat farce
- Butter
- Stewed green vegetables
- Cream

## Directions

1. Place farce in an oval dish, either of sheet metal or tinned copper, greased with butter.
2. Make a long hole in the middle so that there is only about a hand's breadth thickness of farce around all sides.
3. Fill this hole with stewed green vegetables that should not be overcooked.
4. Spread farce about as thick as a finger over this and brush with butter, then place it in the oven on a plate to cook through.
5. The oven should have heat like for wheat bread.
6. Of all kinds of green vegetables, shelled green peas and white or green cabbage are the best, because they are improved by the meat flavor and do not spoil it with their taste. The peas should then be stewed with a little cream.
