## Ingredients

- 2 chickens, cooked
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- clarified butter for frying
- vegetables such as cauliflower, peas, asparagus, sea kale, beans, carrots, or fresh mushrooms
- melted butter
- parsley or lettuce for garnish
- brown butter for sauce

## Directions

1. Split the cooked chickens lengthwise and cut each half into 2 or 3 pieces depending on size.
2. Dip the meat in egg, then in a mixture of breadcrumbs, pepper, and salt.
3. Brown in a pan in plenty of clarified butter.
4. Arrange vegetables in the center of the platter, cooked tender in salted water and served with melted butter.
5. Arrange the chicken pieces around the vegetables, garnished with parsley or lettuce.
6. Serve with brown butter as sauce.
