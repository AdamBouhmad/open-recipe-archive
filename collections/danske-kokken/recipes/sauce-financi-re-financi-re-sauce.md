## Ingredients

- Madeira sauce
- Chopped, cooked chestnuts
- Mushrooms
- Truffles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Prepare a Madeira sauce
2. Bring to a boil with chopped, cooked chestnuts, mushrooms, truffles and olives, using just a small amount of each type
