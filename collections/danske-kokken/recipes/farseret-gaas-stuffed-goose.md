## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- veal or pork forcemeat, neither too lean nor too fat

## Directions

1. Make a cut along the middle of the back in the goose's skin.
2. Remove the carcass as instructed for turkey.
3. Spread the meat and cover it with good veal or pork forcemeat that is neither too lean nor too fat.
4. Sew the goose closed and roast or boil it.
5. If boiled, serve with Mushroom Sauce or Tomato Sauce.
6. If roasted, prepare the sauce with mushrooms, truffles, olives, chestnuts, and pickles.
