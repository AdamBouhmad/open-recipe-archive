## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Lightly salted water
- Cold water
- Cold brine

## Directions

1. Use preferably carrots
2. Scrape them, rinse and cook in lightly salted water for 5 minutes
3. Take out the carrots, cool in cold water
4. Then place them in jars and pour cold brine over them
5. Close the jars hermetically with rubber ring and clamp, then boil for 30 to 35 minutes and cool
6. A little icing sugar can be added to the brine
