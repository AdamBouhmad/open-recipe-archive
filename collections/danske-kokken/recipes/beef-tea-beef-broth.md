## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef, finely chopped
- 1 pint cold water
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the lean beef finely and place it in 1 pint of cold water for one hour
2. Set over the fire in the same water and bring slowly to a boil
3. Simmer for 20 to 30 minutes
4. Strain and season with a little salt
5. Note: The longer the meat can lie in cold water, the more nourishing matter is extracted
