## Ingredients

- Beef roast
- Thick pancake batter (No. 560)
- Palmin
- Mixed vegetables
- Preserved fruit compote
- Salad
- Browned butter

## Directions

1. Cut the beef roast into slices
2. Dip them in a thick pancake batter (see No. 560) which must completely cover the meat
3. As soon as it is dipped, place it immediately on the frying pan in hot Palmin
4. Remove when it is brown on both sides
5. Serve with mixed vegetables, preserved fruit compote and salad
6. Browned butter is served alongside
7. Cooked beef can be battered in the same manner
