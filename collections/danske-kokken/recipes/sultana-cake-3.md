## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 250 grams margarine
- 250 grams fine sugar
- 125 grams pitted and blanched sultana raisins
- 50 grams finely chopped candied orange peel
- cardamom
- orange essence
- 500 grams Pillsbury flour
- 500 milliliters semi-skimmed milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, margarine, and fine sugar well
2. Add pitted and blanched sultana raisins, finely chopped candied orange peel, cardamom, and orange essence to taste
3. Stir in Pillsbury flour, semi-skimmed milk, and baking powder
4. Bake in a greased form for 1 hour
