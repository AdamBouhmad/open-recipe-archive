## Ingredients

- Peel of 8 oranges (thinly peeled, yellow part only)
- 2 liters (2 pots) white rum
- 1 kg (2 pounds) lump sugar
- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) water

## Directions

1. Place the thinly peeled yellow peel of 8 oranges in a crock
2. Pour 2 liters (2 pots) of white rum over the peel
3. Tie the crock closed and place in warmth for 3 to 4 days
4. Strain the rum through a clear but fairly tight piece of cloth
5. Make a syrup by boiling 1 kg (2 pounds) of lump sugar with 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) of water
6. Pour the syrup over the strained rum
7. Fill into dry bottles, cork them, and seal with plaster or lac
8. See rules for sealing and storing preserves
