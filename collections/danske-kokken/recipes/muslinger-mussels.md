## Ingredients

- mussels
- butter
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mussels are only available in winter. Cook them as instructed in Nr. 8 and can be used like oysters in many dishes.
2. Cooked mussels should be brought steaming to the table; they are arranged unopened in a deep dish, covered with a napkin.
3. Serve with melted butter, vinegar, and pepper.
