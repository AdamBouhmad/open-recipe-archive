## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 36 quarters chocolate
- 2 pounds white sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 pot cream

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pot sweet cream with 36 quarters chocolate and 2 pounds white sugar and pour it while hot under beating over 12 beaten egg yolks.
2. Set the whole mixture over heat to smooth under continued beating.
3. Let it cool.
4. Beat 1 pot cream until stiff foam and stir it in when the rest is cooled.
5. Then freeze it.
