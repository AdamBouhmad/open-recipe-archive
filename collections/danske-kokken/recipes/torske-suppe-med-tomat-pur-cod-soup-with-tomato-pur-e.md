## Ingredients

- 2 L (2 pots) fish broth
- 2 L (2 pots) good white meat broth
- tomato purée

## Directions

1. Prepare as No. 418.
2. Instead of mushrooms, add tomato purée to taste; but this soup does not need to be thickened.
