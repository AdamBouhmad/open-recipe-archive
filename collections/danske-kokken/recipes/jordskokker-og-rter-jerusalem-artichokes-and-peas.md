## Ingredients

- cooked Jerusalem artichokes
- cream or hollandaise sauce
- cooked green peas

## Directions

1. Stew the cooked Jerusalem artichokes in a cream or hollandaise sauce
2. Arrange on a dish
3. Garnish with a wreath of cooked green peas
