## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar or Demerara sugar
- 1 kg (2 S) tomatoes
- 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- Vinegar
- Cinnamon stick pieces
- Whole cloves
- 1/4 kg (1/2 pound) sugar
- Salicylic acid, dissolved

## Directions

1. Select large, partly ripe tomatoes and dry them in cloth, then cut them in half
2. Dip 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar directly into vinegar and place in a pot to cook down to a syrup
3. Add as many tomatoes at once as can lie side by side without stacking on top of each other
4. Shortly after the tomatoes begin to boil in the syrup, the skin will split; then immediately remove them and place in glasses with a few pieces of whole cinnamon and some whole cloves
5. Add the remaining 1/4 kg (1/2 pound) sugar to the liquid and boil it down until fairly thick
6. Pour the boiling liquid over the tomatoes, then pour a little dissolved salicylic acid over them as described in recipe Nr. 81
7. Seal the glasses immediately
