## Ingredients

- strong extract of tea
- 1 quart fresh cream
- 5 ounces sugar
- 10 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare and serve the same as coffee cream, only using strong tea extract instead of coffee.
2. It can be made without eggs; in that case use less tea extract for the same portion.
