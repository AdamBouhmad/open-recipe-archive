## Ingredients

- 250 gr powdered sugar
- 1/2 l vinegar
- Melon rind

## Directions

1. Peel the melon very thinly and cut the rind into elongated pieces.
2. Boil sugar and vinegar to a syrup and skim before adding the melon rind to cook until tender.
3. Cook a few whole cloves, a couple pieces of cinnamon, and 1 teaspoon whole white pepper together with the rind.
4. Pour into jars and seal. Serve with roasted meat.
