## Ingredients

- Pear quinces
- Melis, equal weight to the strained juice

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the quinces
2. Cut into pieces and boil along with the cores in a little water until completely soft and can be mashed into a pulp
3. Pour into a sieve so the juice runs off
4. Filter the juice through a jelly bag and weigh it
5. Boil the juice with an equal weight of Melis until the jelly appears clear and thick
6. Skim and pour into jelly cups that have been previously moistened with cognac
7. Treat further as noted for raspberry jelly
