## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) icing sugar
- 125 grams (1/4 lb) lukewarm margarine
- a couple tablespoons cream
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) flour
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) Palmin for frying

## Directions

1. Mix eggs with icing sugar and lukewarm margarine
2. Add a couple tablespoons cream and 1/2 kg flour, mix with hands
3. Roll out, form and fry as described above
