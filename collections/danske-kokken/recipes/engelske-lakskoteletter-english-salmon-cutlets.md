## Ingredients

- Raw salmon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread
- Butter
- Chutney
- English fish sauce
- Lemon juice
- Cooked potatoes
- Parsley
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Clean the raw salmon and split it from the back, remove the bone
2. Cut into slices of 2½ centimeters (1 inch) thickness
3. Beat the slices very lightly and remove the skin
4. Sprinkle the cutlets with salt and pepper, dip in egg and bread
5. Fry on the pan in browned butter
6. Serve with browned butter mixed with chutney, English fish sauce or simply lemon juice, accompanied by cooked potatoes
7. Garnish the dish with parsley and lemon slices
