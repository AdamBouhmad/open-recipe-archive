## Ingredients

- various fruit juices
- potato flour
- red wine
- fresh fruit such as cherries or raspberries
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Use various fruit juices, boil and thicken with a little potato flour first stirred in red wine.
2. If using fresh fruit, particularly cherries or raspberries, first boil and strain, then boil the juice again with sugar to taste and thicken as mentioned above.
3. In cherry sauce one may also add a little cinnamon and lemon zest.
