## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg quince
- 625 gr sugar
- 0.5 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice

## Directions

1. Wipe the quinces and cut them into 4 to 6 pieces, removing the blossom end.
2. Place them in a pot and cover with approximately 0.5 l water for each 0.5 kg fruit, or with enough water so it reaches the level of the quinces in the pot.
3. They must be completely cooked before pouring into the jelly cloth.
4. When the juice has run through the following day, cook the jelly like all other varieties. (See description in No. 93.)
