## Ingredients

- Brussels sprout heads
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the heads from the stalks and take off all loose leaves.
2. Pour boiling water over the cabbage.
3. Let it stand for 5 minutes.
4. Pour off with cold water and drain on a colander so the water runs off.
5. Place the cabbage in jars, sprinkle with a little salt, and pour raw vinegar over it.
6. The next day, drain off the vinegar, boil it, and add sugar to taste.
7. Pour the hot vinegar over the cabbage in the jars.
8. Seal the jars.
