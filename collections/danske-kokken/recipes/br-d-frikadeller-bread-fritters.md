## Ingredients

- 5 Cents Toast or equivalent amount of old bread
- 1 Pint Milk
- 3 Eggs
- 3 Tablespoons melted Butter
- grated Lemon Peel
- Powdered Sugar
- a little Salt
- a little Currants (optional)

## Directions

1. Pound the toast or bread fine and mix with 1 Pint Milk, letting it stand for one hour
2. Stir well with 3 Eggs (added 3 at a time), 3 Tablespoons melted Butter, grated Lemon Peel, Powdered Sugar, a little Salt, and if desired, a little Currants
3. Fry in clarified fat for a fairly long time so they become hard on the outside
4. Serve with powdered sugar sprinkled on top
