## Ingredients

- 1 French bread loaf
- Milk
- 100 grams (20 dram) butter
- 6 eggs
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cardamom

## Directions

1. Cut away the crust from a French bread loaf and cut the bread into thick slices
2. Dip the slices in milk and let stand covered for a couple of hours
3. Rub the bread with a wooden spoon until well combined
4. Melt the butter and bake the bread in it until it is well bound together
5. When the dough has cooled slightly, stir it with 6 eggs, salt, sugar, and cardamom
6. Form into balls and cook in salted water
