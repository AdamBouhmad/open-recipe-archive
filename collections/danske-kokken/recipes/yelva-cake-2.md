## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram margarine
- 1/4 kilogram powdered sugar
- 3 eggs
- a little cardamom
- a few drops bitter almond oil
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 1/4 liter half-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix the margarine, powdered sugar, eggs, cardamom, bitter almond oil, and baking powder well together
2. Add the Pillsbury flour and half-skimmed milk
3. Bake in a greased form for 5 quarters of an hour
