## Ingredients

- 1 very large cabbage head
- 2 1/2 kg (3 lb) prepared ground meat
- Sailcloth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer loose leaves from a good firm cabbage head
2. Cut out a lid around the stalk large enough to hollow out the head
3. Fill with ground meat, taking care to leave room for it to rise
4. Place the lid on and cover with a couple of the removed leaves
5. Tie the head with sailcloth
6. Place in a pot with abundant boiling salted water and cook for 3 to 3 1/2 hours
7. Remove the lid at serving time
