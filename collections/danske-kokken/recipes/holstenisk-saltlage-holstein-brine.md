## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram kitchen salt
- 10 grams saltpeter
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 4 liters boiling water

## Directions

1. Dissolve the salt, saltpeter and powdered sugar in 4 liters of boiling water
2. Place the meat to be salted in the brine when it is completely cold
3. After 14 days it is ready to be cooked
