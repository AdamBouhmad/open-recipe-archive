## Ingredients

- Gherkins not yet seeded
- Salt
- 2 liters (2 pots) vinegar
- 50 grams (10 quintals) whole black pepper
- 50 grams (10 quintals) whole ginger

## Directions

1. Cut the gherkins on the bias into slices 2-3 centimeters (about 1 inch) thick
2. Sprinkle with salt and set aside until the next day
3. Drain the gherkins in a sieve until the brine is completely drained before placing in crock
4. Cover after pouring boiling vinegar over them
5. After 8 days, drain the vinegar and bring it to a boil with whole black pepper and whole ginger
6. Pour the boiling vinegar over the gherkins, cover and bind when they are partially cooled
7. These sliced gherkins are suitable for use after a few days
