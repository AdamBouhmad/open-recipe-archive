## Ingredients

- 250 grams margarine
- 4 eggs
- 375 grams fine sugar
- 2 good teaspoons baking powder
- 500 grams Pillsbury flour
- 500 milliliters semi-skimmed milk
- cardamom
- vanilla essence
- orange essence

## Directions

1. Mix margarine, eggs, fine sugar, and baking powder well
2. Add Pillsbury flour and semi-skimmed milk
3. Season with cardamom, vanilla essence, and orange essence to taste
4. Bake for about 1 hour in a greased cake form
