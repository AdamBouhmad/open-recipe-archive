## Ingredients

- Hen
- Water
- Salt
- 12 peppercorns
- Carrot
- Onion
- 2 cloves
- Herb bouquet or dried vegetables
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolk
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the hen and chop it up; first take off the legs and wings
2. Split the breast and back, and chop these into suitable pieces
3. Place over heat in boiling water that must come just over them
4. Skim as often as foam rises, then add a tablespoon of salt, a dozen peppercorns, a carrot, an onion, a couple of cloves, and a bunch or bouquet of dried vegetables
5. Cook gently until the hen is tender, about one hour, then remove it and keep it warm while the sauce is made
6. Strain the soup and return it without vegetables to heat; stir together a lump of butter and a lump of flour over the heat
7. Gradually stir in 0.5 pint of the soup, adding a beaten egg yolk and stirring until the sauce is smooth
8. Add salt and pepper, and serve on a warm dish
