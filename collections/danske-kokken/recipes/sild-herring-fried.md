## Ingredients

- herring
- salt
- beaten egg
- grated bread
- flour
- clarified butter
- parsley
- boiled potatoes
- fried sauce
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the herring
2. Make several cross-wise cuts and sprinkle with salt
3. Let them rest for 2-4 hours
4. Dry them, dip in beaten egg, roll in grated bread mixed with flour, and fry in clarified butter
5. Serve on a platter garnished with parsley, with peeled boiled potatoes, fried sauce, and vinegar
