## Ingredients

- Herring
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Cream
- Vinegar
- Mustard
- Salt
- Allspice
- Bay leaves

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, rinse, drain and lay tightly in a casserole with the above spices except mustard. Add a little water and a spoonful of vinegar, and cook the herring covered.
2. Brown butter and flour, whisk in a little vinegar and mustard. Smooth the sauce with egg yolk and cream and serve with the herring.
