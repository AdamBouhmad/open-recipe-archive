## Ingredients

- 1 kg dried white beans
- Maggis bouillon cubes
- butter
- ground white pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the beans in lukewarm water overnight
2. The next day, drain the water and cook the beans in bouillon or water with Maggis bouillon cubes, adding a slice of butter
3. The beans must remain whole and not be overcooked
4. At serving time, drain the cooking liquid and pour melted butter mixed with ground white pepper over the beans
5. Season with salt and lemon juice to taste
