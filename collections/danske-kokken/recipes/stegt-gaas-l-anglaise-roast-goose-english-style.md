## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- 6 fairly large onions
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the 6 onions into dice and brown them in a pan with butter, salt, and pepper.
2. Fill the cleaned and prepared goose with this hot onion mixture.
3. Roast the goose as an ordinary roast goose.
4. Make the sauce from butter, flour, and the pan drippings from the roast.
5. Serve with boiled potatoes.
