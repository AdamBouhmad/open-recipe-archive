## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons mustard
- Boiling water
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg yolk (well whipped)
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Pour a little boiling water over the mustard
2. Add salt
3. Stir in olive oil gently
4. Add sugar
5. Stir in a well-whipped egg yolk
6. Add vinegar to taste
7. Should be made the day before it is used
