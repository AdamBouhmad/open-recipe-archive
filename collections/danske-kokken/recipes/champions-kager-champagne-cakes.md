## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons finely powdered sugar
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg whites
- 1 whole egg
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix the powdered sugar and flour with the egg whites and whole egg
2. Thin with a little cream, taking care the batter does not become too thin
3. Fry in a mold iron in fat
4. Before beginning to fry, dip the iron in the boiling fat and repeat this for each cake
5. When arranging, place a little currant jelly in the bottom of each cake
6. Top with whipped cream
