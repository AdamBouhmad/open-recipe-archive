## Ingredients

- Norwegian ptarmigan
- Browned potatoes
- Currant or lingonberry jelly

## Directions

1. Pluck and lard the ptarmigan and prepare as for grouse.
2. Divide the ptarmigan into 2 parts.
3. Serve with browned potatoes and currant or lingonberry jelly.
