## Ingredients

- Leftover game or poultry
- Good game sauce
- Red wine
- French mustard (moutarde)
- Watercress
- Tartlets filled with apple compote, apple sauce, lingonberry jelly, or currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cut leftover game or poultry meat into 2 to 3 centimeter (1 inch) long, fine strips.
2. Make a good, smooth game sauce and add red wine and a little French mustard.
3. Turn the meat in this sauce and arrange it, garnished with watercress.
4. Place the salad bowl on a platter and arrange a wreath of tartlets filled with apple compote, apple sauce, lingonberry jelly, or currant jelly around the edge.
