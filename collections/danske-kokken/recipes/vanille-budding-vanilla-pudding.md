## Ingredients

- 100 g (20 quintal) margarine
- 100 g (20 quintal) flour
- 3 3/4 dl (1 1/2 pint) boiling milk
- 7 egg yolks
- 125 g (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- egg whites, beaten stiff
- vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Melt the margarine and bake the flour in it, then dilute with about 3 3/4 dl (1 1/2 pint) of boiling milk.
2. When the batter has cooled, stir in the 7 egg yolks together with the powdered sugar, and finally add the stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. Vanilla essence may be stirred into the batter.
4. Serve with chocolate sauce (Nr. 803) or fruit sauce.
