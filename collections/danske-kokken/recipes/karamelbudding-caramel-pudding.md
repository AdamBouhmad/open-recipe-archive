## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 10 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 15 quintals finely ground melis sugar
- 1 quarter-pound white sugar

## Directions

1. Heat 1 pot sweet cream.
2. Whip together 10 egg yolks and 6 egg whites with 15 quintals finely ground melis sugar.
3. When the cream is hot, remove from heat and add the eggs while stirring constantly.
4. In a separate pan, dip 1 quarter-pound white sugar in water and bring to a boil until it becomes dry and again liquid.
5. Pour this caramel into a mold, turning and tilting it to coat all sides, ensuring no dry spots remain.
6. If any caramel remains, pour it into the cream.
7. Pour the cream into the caramel-lined mold, place the mold in water, and boil for one hour.
8. Prepare this pudding the day before, turn it out of the mold onto a dish, and serve cold.
