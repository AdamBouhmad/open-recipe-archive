## Ingredients

- Fish forcemeat
- Cooked Lobster
- Butter
- 1 Tablespoon Wheat Flour
- 1 Pail strong Broth
- 1 Pail Cream
- Small cooked Fish balls
- Cooked Asparagus pieces one inch in length

## Directions

1. Fill a greased ring mold with fish forcemeat and place it in a pan with enough water to reach halfway up the mold.
2. Cover with a piece of paper greased with butter and cook for about a quarter hour.
3. Turn the mold onto a serving dish and let it stand for a few minutes before lifting off the fish border.
4. Prepare the lobster stewing: Pick the cooked lobster and cut the meat into small pieces.
5. Melt a piece of butter and stir in one tablespoon of wheat flour.
6. Add one pail of strong broth and one pail of cream, and cook together.
7. Pour this sauce over small cooked fish balls and lobster meat to warm it.
8. Add small pieces of cooked asparagus about one inch in length to the mixture.
9. Fill the fish border with the lobster mixture and serve as hot as possible.
