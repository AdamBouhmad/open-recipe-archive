## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg cooked macaroni, broken into pieces
- 100 grams butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- About 1/2 liter broth or water with Maggi bouillon cube
- Ground white pepper
- Salt
- Cayenne pepper
- Mustard, stirred
- 1/4 kg grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Make a white sauce with butter, flour and about half a liter of broth or water with a Maggi bouillon cube.
2. When the sauce has boiled, season it with ground white pepper, salt, cayenne pepper and stirred mustard.
3. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and bring back to a boil.
4. Finally warm the cooked macaroni in the sauce.
5. Serve as a vegetable with meat dishes.
