## Ingredients

- 1 L (1 pot) cream or foam
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- Optional: finely chopped pineapple, nuts, chocolate or cocoa, nougat, cold caramel, ginger, coffee extract, almonds, or fruit puree

## Directions

1. Whip the cream until stiff frozen.
2. Blend this foam into the well-stirred egg yolks and sugar.
3. Rinse an ordinary pudding or ice mold with cold water and dust with powdered sugar.
4. Fill with the foam, cover with a piece of paper before placing the lid firmly on.
5. Pack the mold in the ice mixture as described in recipe 1067.
6. Do not stir this ice like cream ice; spread a woolen blanket over the packed ice and let it stand on its own.
7. Only check the ice mixture and pack ice firmly around it or renew it if necessary.
8. Allow 6 hours.
9. If adding peach puree, stir 1/2 kg (1 lb) peach puree into the foam.
10. For striped parfait, layer 2 to 3 types of foam, for example with cocoa, raspberry juice with a few drops of red fruit coloring, and vanilla added to the uncolored white foam.
11. Dip the mold briefly in hot water when turning the ice out of it.
12. Parfait can be garnished with whipped cream, white or colored.
