## Ingredients

- veal or mixed veal and pork
- pepper
- salt
- butter or Palmin
- gravy
- lemon
- herring or anchovies
- capers
- potato salad or browned potatoes
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. The meat used can be either cut veal, cut like fricandeau, or minced veal and pork, prepared like veal carbonade. Sprinkle with pepper and salt and fry in butter or Palmin on a pan.
2. When the meat is fried, add more butter and gravy to the pan; boil it down to a good gravy sauce, which at serving is poured over the meat.
3. On each piece place a slice of lemon and in the center of it a rolled, boneless herring or a flaked anchovy along with some capers.
4. Either potato salad or browned potatoes and grated horseradish can be eaten with this. Wiener-Schnitzel can be made from pork tenderloin, but then the gravy must be mixed with Maggi bouillon cubes.
