## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- butter
- water
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Bake 1 tablespoon flour with a little butter and thin with water.
2. Finish with vinegar and salt and stir in butter, but must not boil. For 10 portions: half a pound butter and half a tablespoon flour.
