## Ingredients

- Equal amounts of beets, potatoes, carrots, celery, apples, and pickles or aïoli
- False mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut equal amounts of beets, potatoes, carrots, celery, apples, and pickles or aïoli into small cubes and turn in false mayonnaise.
2. This salad can be arranged in a puff pastry ring and served as a vegetable accompaniment to a roast.
