## Ingredients

- 1 orange
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Press juice from orange and grate the yellow peel
2. Beat egg yolks white and add peel and juice with powdered sugar
3. Beat egg whites until stiff foam
4. Heat pan over heat and add a piece of butter about the size of a small walnut
5. Pour in omelet with folded whites and fry, constantly lifting from bottom with fork and gathering in center
6. When ready, push to one side of pan, hold hot plate to it and slide omelet over quickly
7. Sprinkle with fine sugar and serve hot
