## Ingredients

- 4 to 5 portions of goose giblets
- a good slice of butter
- 1 pair of grated onions
- flour for coating
- a pair of whole carrots
- paprika
- salt
- a little curry
- color
- boiling water as needed
- sherry
- potato starch for thickening
- puff pastry strips, cooked apple halves, or glazed chestnuts for garnish

## Directions

1. Chop the giblets, clean and blanch them as described for giblet soup.
2. Brown a good slice of butter in a pot together with grated onions.
3. Coat the giblets in flour and place them in the pot.
4. Braise well together with whole carrots, paprika, salt, a little curry, and color.
5. Pour in as much boiling water as needed for the sauce.
6. Cook the giblets until tender under a lid for at least 2 hours.
7. Remove the carrots and skim the fat from the gravy.
8. Finish the sauce with sherry and thicken further with potato starch mixed in water.
9. Serve very hot, garnished with puff pastry strips, cooked apple halves, or glazed chestnuts.
