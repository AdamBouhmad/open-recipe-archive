## Ingredients

- approximately 1/4 kg (1 1/2 Q) leftover cold semolina porridge
- 7 sheets isinglass
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) boiling water
- 75 gr (15 quintal) powdered sugar
- 20 sweet, blanched and grated almonds
- 8 bitter, blanched and grated almonds
- 3 3/4 dl (1 1/2 pægl) cream for whipping
- fruit compote

## Directions

1. Add isinglass dissolved in boiling water to the leftover semolina porridge
2. Stir in powdered sugar and almonds
3. Fold in whipped cream with a spatula
4. Pour into 2 wetted ring molds
5. After a few hours, turn out and fill with fruit compote
