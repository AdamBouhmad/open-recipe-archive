## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) well-dried rhubarb stalks
- 1/2 l (1/2 pot) wine spirits
- 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water
- 1/4 stick vanilla
- Red fruit coloring or wine coloring (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut 1 kg (2 S) well-dried rhubarb stalks very finely or crush in a wooden or stone mortar and place in a jar.
2. Pour 1/2 l (1/2 pot) wine spirits over them. Tie the jar with parchment paper and let stand for one week.
3. Pour the entire contents into a jelly bag to obtain completely clear liquid; if not completely clear, filter through a piece of sterilized cotton placed in a funnel.
4. Boil 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar with 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water and 1/4 stick vanilla.
5. Remove the pan from the heat before pouring in the strained liquid.
6. The liqueur is now ready to be poured into bottles. This portion yields 2 bottles of liqueur.
7. Color slightly with a few drops of red fruit coloring or wine coloring if desired.
