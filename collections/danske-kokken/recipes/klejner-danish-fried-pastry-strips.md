## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 4 pounds powdered sugar
- 7 quintal butter
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1 pound flour

## Directions

1. Mix together egg yolks, egg whites, powdered sugar, butter, and lemon zest with a wooden spoon
2. When well mixed, stir in the flour until a smooth dough forms
3. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a five-øre coin
4. Cut strips 2 fingers wide and 4 inches long using a pastry wheel
5. Cut a slit in the center of each strip and pull one end through
6. Fry in melted butter or fat until golden brown
7. Turn during frying, remove and drain on paper so the butter runs off
