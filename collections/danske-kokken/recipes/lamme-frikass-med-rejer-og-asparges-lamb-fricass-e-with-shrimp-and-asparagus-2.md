## Ingredients

- 3 kg lamb forequarter
- 1 kg asparagus
- 1 liter unpeeled shrimp
- salt
- butter
- flour
- 3-4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into appropriately small pieces, rinse in cold water and blanch.
2. Place over heat in cold water with salt, skim and cook with a good soup whisk; when tender, some of the broth is drained off, the rest remains with the meat so it can be kept warm.
3. Peel the asparagus and cut into approximately 3 centimeter long pieces, which are cooked in water with salt.
4. Brown butter and flour and thin with broth mixed with asparagus water into a smooth sauce, which is thickened with 3-4 egg yolks.
5. At serving, arrange the asparagus and the cooked, peeled shrimp between the meat, and pour the sauce over.
