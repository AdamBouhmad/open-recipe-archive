## Ingredients

- Pastry dough
- Butter
- a few slices of veal
- 2 fat chickens, cooked
- Boiled ham
- Parsley
- Egg

## Directions

1. Grease a mold with butter; layer in it first the slices of meat, then the chickens, cut as for fricassée, and between them the ham and parsley.
2. Cover the mold with dough, which is brushed with egg.
3. Bake in a hot oven for 1 hour and serve on the same mold.
