## Ingredients

- sago
- 4 pots sweet boiling milk
- a couple of cinnamon sticks
- a little lemon peel
- a vanilla stick

## Directions

1. Bring 4 pots of sweet milk to a boil with a couple of cinnamon sticks, a little lemon peel, and a vanilla stick
