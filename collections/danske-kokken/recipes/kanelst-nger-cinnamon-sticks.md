## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 0.5 pound crushed white sugar
- 3 quints crushed Ceylon cinnamon
- 1 egg
- 0.75 pound wheat flour

## Directions

1. Soften 1 pound butter and work together with 0.5 pound crushed white sugar, 3 quints crushed Ceylon cinnamon, 1 egg and 0.75 pound wheat flour until the dough releases from the hands.
2. Roll out the dough in flour on a baking board, cut into oblong sticks and bake for half an hour at good, even heat.
