## Ingredients

- Wrung out berries from the previous recipe
- Vinegar
- Powdered sugar, ½ pound per pint

## Directions

1. The wrung out berries that remain from the previous one can be cooked with vinegar to taste
2. Since only very little juice comes from this, it is best to crush some more berries and cook them with the vinegar
3. When it has boiled well for 8 to 10 minutes under a lid, wring it in a cloth
4. Then boil the juice like the previous one
