## Ingredients

- Hvidling (Whiting)
- Maître d'hôtel-filer
- Tartar-Smør (Tartar butter)

## Directions

1. Prepare the whiting fillet as Torske-Filet No. 567.
2. Serve with maître d'hôtel fillet and tartar butter (No. 745-746).
