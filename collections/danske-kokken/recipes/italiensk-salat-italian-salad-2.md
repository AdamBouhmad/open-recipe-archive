## Ingredients

- Equal parts beets
- potatoes
- carrots
- celery
- apples
- canned asparagus or pickles

## Directions

1. Cut the beets, potatoes, carrots, celery, apples, and canned asparagus or pickles into small dice
2. Toss in lost mayonnaise
3. This salad can be arranged in a puff pastry ring and used as a vegetable side dish for roasts
