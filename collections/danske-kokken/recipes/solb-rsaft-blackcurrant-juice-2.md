## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of blackcurrants
- 1 pot of water
- 0.75 measures of Cognac
- 1 pound of powdered sugar per pot of juice

## Directions

1. Cook the blackcurrants with the water and Cognac and skim well
2. After 1 hour the berries are cooked through, place them in a sieve
3. Press off the juice and cook the berries once more with two measures of water and mix this juice with the first
4. Cook the combined juice for half an hour with the sugar, skim, filter and pour into containers
5. The next day distribute the juice into bottles
