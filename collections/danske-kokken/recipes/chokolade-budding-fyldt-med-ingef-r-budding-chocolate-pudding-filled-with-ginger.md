## Ingredients

- 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) strong coffee
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) vanilla chocolate
- 8 leaves isinglass
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) cream, whipped to stiff foam
- salad oil

## Directions

1. Dissolve the vanilla chocolate and 8 leaves of isinglass in the strong coffee
2. Allow to cool and stir in 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) cream whipped to stiff foam
3. Coat the sides and bottom of the mold with this mixture in a one-inch thick layer
4. Rub the mold beforehand with a cloth dipped in a little salad oil
5. Turn the mold around until the chocolate has set in the mold
6. Fill the center either with Ginger Pudding (Nr. 1036) or with Rum Fromage (Nr. 1057), Pineapple Fromage (Nr. 1048), or Kan ikke la'  være (Nr. 1054)
