## Ingredients

- 375 grams (1/4 lb) flour
- 175 grams (35 quintal) margarine
- 125 grams (1/4 lb) icing sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Directions

1. Mix together the flour, margarine, icing sugar, egg and baking soda
2. Form into rings
3. Dip in egg and sugar
4. Bake until light brown
