## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram white beans
- Water
- 2 spoonfuls oil
- 4 spoonfuls tomato puree
- 1 spoonful chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the white beans overnight.
2. Cook them tender in water.
3. Mix together the oil, tomato puree, chopped parsley, salt and pepper and pour over the beans.
