## Ingredients

- cooked fruits such as apples, pears and apricots, very tender
- sugar to taste
- 1 L (1 pot) cream for whipping
- raw fruits such as strawberries and raspberries
- 1/2 kg (1 S) strawberries or raspberries to 1 L (1 pot) cream

## Directions

1. For cooked fruit: strain through a coarse sieve and sweeten to taste, then fold in whipped cream just before serving
2. Arrange high in the center of a dish and serve immediately
3. For raw fruit: strain through a sieve, sweeten, and stir into 1 L whipped cream using the proportion of 1/2 kg fruit to 1 L cream
