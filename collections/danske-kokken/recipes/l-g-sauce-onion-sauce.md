## Ingredients

- Three medium onions
- Water
- A good slice of butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Thin cream or sweet milk
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the onions into thin slices or chop them and cook them tender in a little water on the bottom of the pot
2. Add a good slice of butter and bake 1 tablespoon flour in it, which is diluted with thin cream or sweet milk to a smooth sauce
3. After it has boiled, salt it and it should preferably be served immediately
4. This sauce is eaten with cured herring and cured ham
