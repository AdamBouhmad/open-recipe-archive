## Ingredients

- egg white
- 5 cents gum tragacanth
- white sugar
- 2 ounces finely ground sweet almonds

## Directions

1. The evening before baking, pour egg white over 5 cents gum tragacanth and let it stand until the next morning.
2. Beat well and stir with as much white sugar as it can take and 2 ounces finely ground sweet almonds.
3. Roll small pieces of dough with hands against the cake board into cylinders, cut like peppernuts, and bake light brown on a buttered pan.
