## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 30 kvint powdered sugar
- rind of 2 lemons
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 pægle white French wine
- 6 kvint dissolved isinglass
- 2 pægle sweet cream

## Directions

1. Stir 10 egg yolks, 30 kvint powdered sugar and the grated rind of 2 lemons with the juice of 2 lemons until it becomes white.
2. Add 2 pægle white French wine and 6 kvint dissolved isinglass.
3. After stirring this well, carefully fold in 2 pægle sweet cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Fill the mixture into a mold and chill for about an hour in ice and salt.
5. Turn out onto a platter and garnish with small oblong sugar breads.
