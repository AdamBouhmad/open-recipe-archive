## Ingredients

- Cured herring
- Milk
- Ground pepper
- Onions
- White writing paper, half sheets
- Butter
- Twine

## Directions

1. Soak the cured herring in milk the day before
2. Skin the herring and cut off the head and tail
3. Open them with a slit down the back and remove the bone, then scrape them clean in the belly
4. Lay the herring flat with the inside facing upward, sprinkle with ground pepper and cover with thin onion slices
5. Layer them back into their original form, wrap them in half sheets of white writing paper well buttered, and bind them with twine
6. Fry the herring on both sides either on a grill or in a pan with butter over a brisk fire until the paper is brown
7. Remove the bands and serve the herring in the paper
8. Eat with stewed potatoes
