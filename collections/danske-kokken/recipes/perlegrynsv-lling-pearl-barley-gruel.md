## Ingredients

- 1 pint pearl barley
- 4 pots milk
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse the pearl barley and soak it in cold water the day before use
2. Boil 4 pots of milk
3. When the milk boils vigorously, sprinkle the grains into it
4. Cook the gruel over even heat under a lid for approximately 3 hours until the grains are tender, stirring occasionally
5. Add salt when done
