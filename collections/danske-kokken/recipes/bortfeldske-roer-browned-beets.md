## Ingredients

- Beets
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Sugar
- Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. The beets should not be too large, but rather firm and crisp
2. Cut into strips and place in boiling water, then immediately remove to a colander and rinse with cold water
3. Brown some butter in a saucepan and cook the beets in it until they become golden brown
4. Thin with bouillon and cook until tender
5. Add sugar and salt to taste and thicken with a tablespoon of flour first mixed in cold water
6. Do not leave them cleaned for long before using as they will otherwise become black unless well covered
7. Store best in the cellar in a pile of sand
