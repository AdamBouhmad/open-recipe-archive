## Ingredients

- 3 pounds veal breast
- Cold water
- White pepper
- Salt
- Green herbs
- Carrot
- Turnip
- Onion
- Cloves
- Butter
- Flour
- Nutmeg
- Egg yolks
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut 3 pounds veal breast into small pieces and place in sufficient cold water to cover them; at the same time add half a teaspoon white pepper, a teaspoon salt, a good bunch of green herbs, half a scraped carrot, a peeled turnip and an onion in which 3 cloves are stuck
2. Bring to a gentle boil and skim carefully as long as scum appears, and cook until the meat is tender
3. Strain it, keeping the broth on the heat and wash the meat in cold water
4. Meanwhile make a white sauce by melting a little butter and stirring flour into it and then gradually adding 1½ pints of the broth
5. Add a little more pepper and salt if needed, and half a teaspoon grated nutmeg
6. When the sauce comes to a boil, whisk it with a whisk, add two small egg yolks, put the meat in and cook it a few minutes while stirring occasionally
7. It can be served with a tablespoon of chopped parsley sprinkled over it after it is poured onto a warm platter
