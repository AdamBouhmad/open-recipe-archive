## Ingredients

- 2½ kg (5 lbs) pork shoulder, with all fat trimmed
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- 1 bottle ordinary, good red wine
- Milk
- Flour
- Cream
- Butter
- Browned potatoes
- Lingonberries or lingonberry jelly

## Directions

1. The shoulder can be browned and roasted in milk in a pot. Another method is to sprinkle the pork shoulder, with all fat removed, with a little salt and ground pepper, and pour over it 1 bottle of ordinary good red wine.
2. Place the meat side down, and leave it in this way until the next day; then place it in the pan, which is set in a well-heated oven, and pour the marinade in which it has lain over it. It must be basted well with it.
3. Make the sauce from flour baked in some of the fat skimmed from the strained broth, which is used to thin it. Finally give it a boil with a little raw cream, and stir in cold butter.
4. Serve with browned potatoes and either lingonberries or lingonberry jelly.
