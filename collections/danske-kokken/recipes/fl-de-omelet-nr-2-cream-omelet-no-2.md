## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 1 small teaspoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 pint cream
- A couple drops vanilla

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks white with sugar
2. Stir in flour
3. Gradually add cream
4. Add vanilla
5. Finally fold in beaten egg whites
6. Bake for 0.5 hour
7. Serves 4 persons
