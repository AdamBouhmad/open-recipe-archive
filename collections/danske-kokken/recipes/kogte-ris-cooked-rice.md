## Ingredients

- Rice (1 1/4 L or about 1 pint per 10 persons)
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice first in cold and then in warm water
2. Place it in abundant boiling salted water
3. When it is so tender that it can easily be pressed apart, drain the water from it
4. Fill the pot with cold water and stir the rice until it is completely separated
5. Drain the water and cover with a lid
6. Keep at heat until serving
7. Alternatively, after cooking, spread the rice on a colander and cover with cold water, then place in a warm oven, stirring regularly with a fork until it is separated and all water has evaporated
