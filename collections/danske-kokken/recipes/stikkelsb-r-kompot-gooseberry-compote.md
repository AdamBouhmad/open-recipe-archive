## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) green, thin-skinned gooseberries
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 Q) Melis sugar
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægel) water

## Directions

1. Use green gooseberries with thin skins
2. Remove the stems and blossoms
3. Rinse the berries
4. Make a syrup from the Melis sugar and water
5. Give the berries a brief boiling in the syrup, but they must remain whole
6. The syrup can be thickened with stirred sago flour
7. If the berries are particularly sour, they can be given a brief boiling in water before being put in the syrup
