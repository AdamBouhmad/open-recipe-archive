## Ingredients

- leftover milk rice porridge from previous day
- eggs
- melted butter
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- sauce

## Directions

1. If you have milk rice porridge left over from the previous day, you can use it as follows: If you have enough porridge for four persons, stir it with a spoon, 2 whole eggs and a piece of melted butter
2. Put it into a deep dish that has first been buttered and sprinkled with stiff bread crumbs
3. Smooth the top, pour melted butter over, and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
4. Bake until light brown, and serve hot with sauce
5. Serve on the same dish with a napkin around it
6. Alternatively, instead of baking the whole porridge, after preparation it can be fried on a griddle in flat cakes
7. Serve hot with sugar, jam, boiled apples or mushrooms
