## Ingredients

- Hornfisk (Garfish)
- Persille (Parsley)
- Smør (Butter)
- Sky or Suppe (Stock or Soup)
- Sagomel (Sago flour)
- Maggis-Bouillon-Tærninger (Maggi Bouillon cubes)
- Kartofler (Potatoes)
- grøn Salat (Green salad)

## Directions

1. Split the fish along the belly and remove the bone.
2. Divide into 3 or 4 pieces.
3. Fill with parsley and a little butter.
4. Sew the belly together.
5. Brown the garfish in butter.
6. Add either stock or soup.
7. Cook briefly as the fish cooks quickly and falls apart easily.
8. Skim the stock and thicken with stirred sago flour.
9. Season with Maggi bouillon cubes.
10. Remove the thread before serving.
11. Serve with boiled or browned potatoes along with green salad.
12. This dish is delicious served either cold or warm.
