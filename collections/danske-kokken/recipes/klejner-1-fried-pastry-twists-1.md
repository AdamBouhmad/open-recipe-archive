## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 150 grams (30 quintal) icing sugar
- 150 grams (30 quintal) lukewarm margarine
- ground cardamom
- zest of one lemon, chopped or grated
- 3 tablespoons raw cream
- approximately 1/2 kg (1 lb) flour
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) Palmin for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs well with icing sugar
2. Add lukewarm margarine, cardamom and lemon zest
3. When this is mixed into a tough dough, add 3 tablespoons raw cream and approximately 1/2 kg flour
4. Roll the dough very thinly
5. Cut with a cake cutter into long cakes
6. Make a slit in the middle of each cake with a cake cutter and draw one end of the cake through the slit, tying it into a knot in the middle
7. Fry this portion in 1/4 kg Palmin in a frying pan
