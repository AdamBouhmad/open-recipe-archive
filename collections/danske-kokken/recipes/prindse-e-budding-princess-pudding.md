## Ingredients

- 1 quart thick, sour cream
- 1 good cup sugar
- 5 tablespoons butter
- 6 ounces flour
- 20 egg yolks
- Cardamom
- Lemon peel
- A little chopped almonds
- 20 egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip the cream slightly and bring to a boil with the sugar.
2. Melt the butter without clarifying it; when it begins to boil, brown 6 ounces of flour in it.
3. Gradually add the boiling cream to this mixture.
4. When almost cold, add 20 egg yolks which have been whipped slightly, cardamom, lemon peel, and a little chopped almonds.
5. Beat the 20 egg whites stiff and fold them in just before pouring into the form.
6. Boil for about 2 hours; serves 20 persons.
7. It can be made in half size. Serve with red sauce.
8. 16 eggs can be used, but then it serves only 16 persons.
