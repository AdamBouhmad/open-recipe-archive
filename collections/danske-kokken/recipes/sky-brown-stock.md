## Ingredients

- tough meat such as neck, brisket or shank
- leftover meat from game, veal, lamb, and pork
- bones and sinews, all finely chopped
- plenty of fat
- peppercorns
- onions, cut into pieces
- slices of carrots
- celery

## Directions

1. Brown plenty of fat in a pot
2. Add meat, bones, etc., and brown together with peppercorns, onions cut into pieces, slices of carrots and celery
3. When well browned, pour water or thin stock over it; a little salt soup can be mixed in if available
4. Skim during cooking, which takes 5-6 hours
5. Strain the stock and set aside until the next day
6. Remove the fat when the stock is to be used
7. After straining, the stock can be reduced further if necessary
