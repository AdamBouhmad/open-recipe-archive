## Ingredients

- Calf liver
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Butter or Palmin
- Onion slices
- Stock, broth, or water
- Coloring
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Boiled potatoes
- Salt pork slices

## Directions

1. After the liver is drained and the membrane removed, cut it into very thin slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in flour, and fry on a pan in browned butter or Palmin.
3. In a good portion of butter, brown slices of onion and bake a spoonful of flour in it.
4. Thin the flour with stock, broth, or water, adding coloring, Maggi bouillon cubes, and salt.
5. Once the sauce has boiled, pour it over the liver.
6. Serve with boiled potatoes.
7. Many people fry slices of salt pork alongside the liver, which improves the flavor of both. Serve both together in the sauce.
