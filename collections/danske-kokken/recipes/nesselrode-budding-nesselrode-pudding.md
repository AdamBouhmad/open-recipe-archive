## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 stick vanilla
- 1/2 pound white sugar
- 3 pails Madeira
- 6 quintals dissolved isinglass
- 1 pail sweet cream, whipped to stiff foam
- finely chopped candied citron
- cleaned raisins without pits
- candied strawberries without juice

## Directions

1. Heat 1 pot sweet cream with 8 egg yolks, 1/2 stick vanilla, and 1/2 pound white sugar, stirring well while bringing to a boil.
2. Pour out when done and while still warm, add 3 pails Madeira and 6 quintals dissolved isinglass.
3. Before it cools completely, stir in 1 pail sweet cream whipped to stiff foam, as well as a handful of finely chopped candied citron, an equal amount of cleaned raisins without pits, and some candied strawberries without juice.
4. In a mold, layer candied strawberries at the bottom, then a layer of the mixture, then again a layer of jam, and so forth until the mold is full.
5. Let the pudding stand for several hours in a cold place or freeze in an ice box.
6. Quickly dip the mold in hot water, turn it out onto a dish, and serve the pudding with raspberry jelly on top.
