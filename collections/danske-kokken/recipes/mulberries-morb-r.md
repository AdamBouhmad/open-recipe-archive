## Ingredients

- 1 Pd. mulberries
- 2 Pd. Melis

## Directions

1. Boil the Melis to a very thick syrup
2. Place the berries in a shallow dish and pour the sugar syrup over them
3. Cover and let stand for 2 days
4. Pour off the syrup and boil it
5. When slightly cooled, pour the syrup back over the berries and let stand until the next day
6. Give the syrup a final boiling, then add the berries and boil gently together
7. When cooled, pour into a jar and seal in the usual manner
8. After 8 days, check if the syrup needs to be poured off and boiled again
9. When cooled, distribute into smaller jars
