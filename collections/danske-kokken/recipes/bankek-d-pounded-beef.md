## Ingredients

- Beef, preferably from the hind leg
- Butter
- Onion
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Flour
- Bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Cut beef into slices as thick as a finger, pound with a mallet and hack slightly with a knife.
2. Place a piece of butter on the bottom of a casserole, then layer beef slices sprinkled with grated onion, salt, ground pepper, cloves, a couple of bay leaves and a little butter; repeat layers of meat, spices and butter.
3. Place over even heat and cook until the meat releases its juices, then gradually pour in boiling water until it barely covers the meat.
4. After cooking for about an hour, the meat should be tender. Mix a little flour with a little bouillon or water and add it.
5. Alternatively, you can flour the meat, brown it in butter, add the same spices and enough boiling water to cover the meat. Cook for a couple of hours until tender.
