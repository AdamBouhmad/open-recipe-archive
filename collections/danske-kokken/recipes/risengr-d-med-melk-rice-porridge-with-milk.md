## Ingredients

- 3 pounds grain
- 2.5 quarts milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- sweet cream or milk

## Directions

1. Pick through 3 pounds of grain, rinse in cold water and scald twice in boiling water
2. Let the grain stand for a short time in boiling milk, as the grains are often sour, which causes the porridge to separate; the boiling milk extracts the sourness and gives the porridge a whiter appearance
3. Do not use this milk for the porridge
4. For 3 pounds grain take 2.5 quarts milk
5. Shake the grain in the boiling milk and cook without stirring according to directions until soft, usually about an hour
6. Add salt
7. Serve with sugar, cinnamon, and butter, along with sweet cream or milk
