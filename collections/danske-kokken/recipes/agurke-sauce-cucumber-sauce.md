## Ingredients

- Plain brown sauce
- Small pickled cucumbers, cut in pieces
- Wine
- Paprika
- Sugar
- Salt
- Fresh cucumber slices
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. A plain brown sauce is given a boil with small, cut pickled cucumbers, wine, paprika, sugar, and salt.
2. Slices of fresh cucumbers can be cooked in the sauce, which is then finished with a little lemon juice.
