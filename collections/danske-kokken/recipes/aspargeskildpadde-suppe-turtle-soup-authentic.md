## Ingredients

- 8 L (8 pots) good brown stock
- Paprika
- 1/2 bottle Madeira
- Cognac to taste
- Turtle meat (pre-cooked)
- Whole hard-boiled egg yolks
- Chicken, veal, or fish forcemeat balls
- Sago flour (optional for thickening)

## Directions

1. Reduce 8 L (8 pots) good brown stock without fat to approximately half its volume.
2. Season with paprika, 1/2 bottle Madeira, and cognac to taste.
3. Cut the turtle meat into appropriate pieces and bring to a boil in the soup.
4. Serve with whole hard-boiled egg yolks and forcemeat balls made from chicken, veal, or fish.
5. If desired, thicken the soup with sago flour mixed with water.
