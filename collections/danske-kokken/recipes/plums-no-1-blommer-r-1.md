## Ingredients

- Fully ripe plums
- 1/2 gill vinegar per pound of plums
- 1/4 pound powdered sugar per pound of plums

## Directions

1. Dry the plums, prick them with a sharp stick or needle and place in a pot
2. Cover with 1/2 gill vinegar per pound of plums and let stand until the next day
3. Drain the vinegar over 1/4 pound powdered sugar for each pound of plums
4. Put on the fire and when just melted, add the plums and turn a couple of times
