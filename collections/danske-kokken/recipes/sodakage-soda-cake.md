## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 12 quintals butter
- 3 pounds powdered sugar or white farina
- a little finely ground bitter almonds
- 1 pound flour
- 3 quintals cream of tartar
- 15 quintals baking soda
- 2 pints cold sweet milk
- 4 egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks white with 12 quintals butter and 3 pounds powdered sugar or white farina, a little finely ground bitter almonds and then with 1 pound flour which has first been mixed with 3 quintals cream of tartar and 15 quintals baking soda.
2. Gradually stir in 2 pints cold sweet milk and finally the 4 beaten egg whites.
3. Put the cake immediately in the oven and bake for 1 and a quarter hours at even heat.
