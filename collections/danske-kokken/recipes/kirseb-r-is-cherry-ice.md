## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 lb) pitted cherries
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- 3 3/4 dl (1 1/2 cup) water

## Directions

1. Make the ice from sweet cherries, remove the pits and crush them.
2. Crush the kernels and cook them with the syrup made from the powdered sugar and water.
3. Crush about 1 kg (2 lb) pitted cherries before working them through a sieve.
4. Strain and freeze.
