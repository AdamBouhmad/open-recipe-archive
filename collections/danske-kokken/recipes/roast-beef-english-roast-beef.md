## Ingredients

- Rib of beef or tenderloin roast
- Brown gravy
- Parsley
- Scraped horseradish

## Directions

1. Roast as beef roast in about 2 hours
2. Serve a strong brown gravy with the roast beef, which is garnished with parsley and scraped horseradish
3. Peel, rinse and scrape the horseradish lengthwise into thin shavings with a sharp knife
4. When scraping, press it firmly against the cutting board and scrape from top to bottom
