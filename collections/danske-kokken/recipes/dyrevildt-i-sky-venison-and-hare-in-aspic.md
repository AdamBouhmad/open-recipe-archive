## Ingredients

- Leftover venison or hare roast
- Aspic or broth
- Additional good aspic or Maggi bouillon cubes
- Italian salad
- Trotters (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones into small pieces.
2. Arrange the meat in a ring-shaped mold.
3. Fill the center with Italian salad.
4. Arrange chopped aspic around the edge of the ring.
5. Prepare the aspic by cooking it on the roasting bones with additional good aspic, broth, or Maggi bouillon cubes.
6. Cook trotters in the aspic for garnish and decoration on top of the salad.
