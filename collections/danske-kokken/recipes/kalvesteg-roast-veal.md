## Ingredients

- Veal roast
- Butter
- Flour
- Wheat flour
- Cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. The roast becomes whiter and thicker by blanching.
2. If you are willing to do this, it is good to lard the veal roast.
3. Roast in a shorter time than beef roast and should not, in our opinion, be red inside.
4. Stir cream and a piece of sugar into the sauce.
5. It is good to let some butter and flour, in which a couple of teaspoons of wheat flour have been worked, come to a boil in a roasting pan and then pour it over the roast after it has been browned in butter; baste it often with this.
