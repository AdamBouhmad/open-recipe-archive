## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Butter
- Strong gravy
- Chopped truffles
- Madeira
- Warm boiled salted beef tongue
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Treat and roast the cutlets as in recipe 153.
2. Add chopped truffles and Madeira to a strong gravy and bring to a boil.
3. When serving, place a slice of warm boiled salted beef tongue on each cutlet and pour the sauce with truffles over it.
