## Ingredients

- Savoy cabbage
- Water
- Salt
- Milk
- Sugar
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut into 4 pieces and remove some of the core.
2. After rinsing, cook only for a very short time in water with salt; if cooked too long, it loses its fine cabbage flavor.
3. Stew in milk and season with a little sugar and salt.
4. Drain the water well from the cabbage, place in a ragout dish, and pour the sauce over.
