## Ingredients

- 2 pounds round beef
- Butter
- 2 ounces flour
- 1 carrot
- 3 small onions
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- Boiling water
- Parsley

## Directions

1. 2 pounds round beef is cut into square pieces, two inches on each side, and browned in butter.
2. Two ounces of flour is stirred in and browned.
3. One carrot is cut into pieces about an inch in size, three small onions are peeled, and a bouquet garni is made.
4. These ingredients are added to the meat together with a tablespoon of salt, 1/2 teaspoon of pepper and boiling water enough that it stands over the meat.
5. It is boiled until the bouquet garni is tender, then this is removed, you taste if there is enough salt in it, and add what is missing.
6. A teaspoonful of chopped parsley can be sprinkled over it, or some slices of fried bread are placed on top.
