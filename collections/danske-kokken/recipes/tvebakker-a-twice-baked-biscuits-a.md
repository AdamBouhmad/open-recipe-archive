## Ingredients

- 3.5 pounds fine wheat flour
- 1 pound butter
- 1 measure sweet milk
- spices and sugar to taste
- 16 quints pressed yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve 16 quints pressed yeast in 1 measure sweet milk.
2. Work together 3.5 pounds fine wheat flour, 1 pound butter, the milk with yeast, spices and sugar to taste well until the dough releases from the hands.
3. Let it rise a little.
4. Form small buns, place on a pan and let them rise a little before baking.
5. Continue with the treatment described in Nr. 558.
