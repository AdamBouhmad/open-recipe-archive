## Ingredients

- raw tomatoes
- oil
- tarragon vinegar or lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- lettuce leaves
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Toss slices of raw tomatoes in a sauce of oil, tarragon vinegar or lemon juice, salt, pepper and chopped parsley
2. Lay the tomato slices in a heap in the salad bowl, garnish it with lettuce leaves and let the heart leaves of a head of lettuce form the top figure
3. Hard-boiled eggs, cut into slices, are stood on edge along the sides of the salad
