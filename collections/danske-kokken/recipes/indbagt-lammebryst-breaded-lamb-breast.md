## Ingredients

- Lamb meat
- Batter (Nr. 560)
- Palmin or lard
- Butter
- Vegetables
- Salad

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into smaller pieces and cook them, then dip them in a batter (see Nr. 560) which must completely cover the meat.
2. Fry in Palmin until it has a nice, light brown color.
3. Breaded meat is eaten either with browned butter and garnished with vegetables or with a piquant sauce and green salad.
