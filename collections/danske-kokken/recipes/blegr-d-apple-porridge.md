## Ingredients

- 10 pounds good apples (not too juicy)
- 1 pot water
- A couple of cinnamon sticks
- A little lemon peel
- Vanilla
- Cold butter
- Powdered sugar
- Cold sweet cream or creme for serving

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the apples into pieces, removing the cores, and place the pieces in cold water.
2. Smear the bottom of an enameled pot with cold butter and place it over heat with 1 pot of water, a couple of cinnamon sticks, a little lemon peel, and vanilla.
3. When the water boils, add the apple pieces and cook until completely tender.
4. Remove from heat and mash the pieces into a smooth porridge, or press them through a colander; in the latter case, give the porridge a boil.
5. Add powdered sugar and serve either lukewarm or cooled with cold sweet cream or creme.
