## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces tapioca
- 1 Pint milk
- 3 Eggs
- 0.5 Pound sugar

## Directions

1. Put the tapioca over the heat in the milk
2. Let it heat and become soft gradually, stirring often so it does not burn
3. When the grains are quite soft, beat the eggs with half a pound of sugar
4. Mix the cooked tapioca and half a pint of cold milk together
5. Bake the pudding for half an hour in a moderately warm oven
6. Serve warm or cold
