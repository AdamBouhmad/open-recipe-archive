## Ingredients

- Firm cauliflower heads
- Cold water
- Boiling water with salt
- Salt solution (10 gr or 2 Kvint per 1 liter)

## Directions

1. Divide firm cauliflower heads into smaller florets and rinse in cold water
2. Place in a pot with boiling salted water
3. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes, remove and pour the cold salt solution over them
4. Place the hermetically sealed jars in a pot and cook for 30 minutes and cool
