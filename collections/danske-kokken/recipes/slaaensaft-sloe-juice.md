## Ingredients

- Sloes
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar or Farin per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice
- Water

## Directions

1. The berries must have been exposed to frost before preserving.
2. Rinse the berries and place them in a pot with enough water to cover them.
3. Let them boil until they begin to burst, then pour them into a jelly cloth or a fine sieve.
4. Boil the strained juice with the sugar for a quarter of an hour and skim during cooking.
5. Pour the juice hot into dry bottles and bind them immediately.
6. The waste can be poured into a jar, covered with boiling water, and left until the next day.
7. Pour everything through the cloth and boil the juice obtained here with a little sugar; this must be used first.
