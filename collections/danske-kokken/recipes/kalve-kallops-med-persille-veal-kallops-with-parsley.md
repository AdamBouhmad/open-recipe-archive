## Ingredients

- good veal
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- stock or gravy
- cold butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut good veal into small pieces, beat them lightly, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and brown on both sides in approximately 8 to 10 minutes.
2. In the butter in which it is fried, add flour, which is thinned with stock or gravy, and boil well together.
3. Just before serving add a piece of cold butter and a tablespoon of chopped parsley. Pour the sauce over the meat.
4. Serve with potatoes. Kallops can be made from pork tenderloin.
