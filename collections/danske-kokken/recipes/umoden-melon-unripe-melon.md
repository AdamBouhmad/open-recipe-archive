## Ingredients

- Unripe melon, peeled and cut into oblong pieces
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Water
- Alum

## Directions

1. Place the peeled melon, cut into oblong pieces, in boiling water with a slight amount of alum, and bring to a boil.
2. When the melon is soft, remove it onto a colander and cover with cold water.
3. Place the fruit in a jar and cover with a thin syrup, made from the specified amount of sugar and water.
4. The next two days, pour off the liquid, cook and skim it, but the last time place the fruit in it to bring to a boil.
5. After the melon is removed, the liquid drained, and the fruit placed in the jar, reduce the liquid further to make it completely even.
6. Pour it warm over the melon.
7. Seal as soon as the preserve has cooled.
