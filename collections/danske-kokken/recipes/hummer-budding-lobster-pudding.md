## Ingredients

- fish forcemeat
- meat from 2 cooked lobsters, coarsely chopped
- lobster shells
- cooked asparagus (optional)

## Directions

1. This pudding can be prepared from fish forcemeat, into which the coarsely chopped, cooked meat from 2 lobsters is stirred.
2. The shells are crushed and cooked down for sauce (see Lobster Sauce).
3. Cooked asparagus can be added to the sauce.
