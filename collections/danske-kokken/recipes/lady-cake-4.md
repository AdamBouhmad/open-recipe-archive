## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- ½ pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- whites of 16 eggs
- 1½ teaspoons soda
- 1½ teaspoons cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar together first
2. Then stir in the egg whites and finally the flour, sifted twice, with soda and cream of tartar
