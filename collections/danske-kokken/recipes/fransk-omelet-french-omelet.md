## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 dessertspoons powdered sugar
- juice and grated zest from half a lemon
- egg whites
- butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks for half an hour with 6 dessertspoons powdered sugar and the juice and grated zest of half a lemon
2. Beat the egg whites stiff and fold in gently
3. Butter a mold, pour in the batter and let stand five minutes at low heat to set
4. Then place it in a hot oven and bake one hour
5. Cannot be removed from the mold
6. Serve with white sugar
7. The eggs must be stirred and beaten well and long, otherwise it will be heavy
8. Serves 5 people
