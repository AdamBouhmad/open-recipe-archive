## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Salt
- Water
- Pepper
- Pork fat from the loin, finely chopped, 3/4 pound per pound of liver
- Broth from cooking the liver
- Sweet cream
- Butter

## Directions

1. Wash and cook the veal liver
2. Cool it thoroughly
3. Grate it finely
4. For each pound of grated liver, take 3/4 pound of finely chopped pork fat from the loin
5. Work everything together well with pepper and salt
6. Thin with some of the broth in which the liver was cooked and with sweet cream until it becomes smooth and spongy
7. Place in a buttered mold
8. Bake for 1 hour in a warm oven
9. Cool thoroughly
10. When the pâté is to be served, dip the mold in boiling water to loosen it
11. Serve as an accompaniment to open-faced sandwiches
12. Optional: add finely chopped onion to the liver mixture, or line the mold with very thin bacon slices
13. Note: Pork liver or game liver can be used instead of veal liver
