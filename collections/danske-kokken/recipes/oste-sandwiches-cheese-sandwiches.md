## Ingredients

- rye bread
- butter
- a tasty, rich cheese

## Directions

1. Cut not too thin slices of rye bread and spread with butter
2. Layer with a tasty, rich cheese
3. Place on a baking sheet after cutting into the desired shape
4. Bake in the oven until the bread is toasted
5. Arrange quickly on a napkin on a platter and serve with tea or coffee
