## Ingredients

- 1.5 Pints Milk
- 6 small Spoonfuls Butter
- 1 Pound Flour
- 6 Eggs
- grated Lemon Peel
- 2 Tablespoons white Sugar
- 2 Cents Yeast

## Directions

1. Set 1.5 Pints Milk on the fire with 6 small Spoonfuls Butter and when melted, add it little by little to 1 Pound Flour and beat well with 6 Eggs (one at a time), a little grated Lemon Peel, 2 Tablespoons white Sugar, and finally 2 Cents Yeast dissolved in a little warm Milk
2. Treat as the preceding recipe
3. If you wish Apples baked in, peel and hollow out the Apples, cut them in round slices and cook in Water with Sugar and, if desired, Wine; on the pan put first a little Batter, then a little Apple and then again a little Batter
