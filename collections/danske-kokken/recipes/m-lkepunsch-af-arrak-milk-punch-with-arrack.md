## Ingredients

- 1½ pounds white sugar
- 2 pots boiled water
- 12 measures arrack
- juice of 12 lemons and peel of 5 lemons
- 1 pot boiling sweet milk with a half vanilla pod boiled in it

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1½ pounds white sugar in 2 pots boiled water
2. Add 12 measures arrack (white rum can be used instead) and juice of 12 lemons and peel of 5 lemons
3. Pour in 1 pot boiling sweet milk in which a half vanilla pod has been boiled
4. Let the punch stand until the next day, then filter and pour into bottles, which are corked
5. Drink cold
