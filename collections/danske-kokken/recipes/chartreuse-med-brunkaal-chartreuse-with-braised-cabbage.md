## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- browned butter
- sugar
- striped, lightly salted bacon, or medister sausage, or poached duck
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. White cabbage is cut and browned in a pot in browned butter and a very little sugar.
2. When the cabbage is nicely brown, a layer of it is laid in the buttered mold.
3. In the middle, either striped, lightly salted bacon, medister sausage, or poached duck can be placed. What is used for this must be cooked and cut into suitable pieces.
4. It is baked or cooked for 1 hour. Brown sauce is eaten with it; this can be made from Maggi bouillon cubes.
