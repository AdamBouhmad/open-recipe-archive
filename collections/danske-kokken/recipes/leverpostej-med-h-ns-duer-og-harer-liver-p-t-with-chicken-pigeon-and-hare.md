## Ingredients

- leftovers of cooked or roasted meat (chicken, pigeon, or hare)
- liver pâté as in recipe No. 259

## Directions

1. Cut the leftovers of cooked or roasted meat into small fillets
2. Distribute the fillets in the pâté before it is placed in the oven for baking
3. Prepare the liver pâté as in recipe No. 259
