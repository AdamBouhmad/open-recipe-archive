## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg (1 tb) crushed powdered sugar
- 1 1/4 dl (3/4 Pægl) water
- 1/4 vanilla bean
- 1/2 kg (1 S) tomatoes
- Vinegar
- Salicylic acid

## Directions

1. Blanch beautiful, ripe tomatoes and peel away the skin
2. Place them over heat with enough vinegar to cover them
3. Give them only one boil and place on a sieve to let the vinegar drain off
4. Boil the sugar, water, and vanilla together
5. Give the tomatoes one boil in this syrup, being careful to keep them whole as they will be placed in the jars
6. If the liquid is not thick enough, reduce it further before pouring over the fruit
7. Seal as soon as the preserve is poured into the jars
8. Dissolve 1/2 teaspoon salicylic acid in some of the liquid for every 3 kg (6 Q) fruit
