## Ingredients

- 1 pint hops
- 2 gallons water
- 2 level teaspoons salt
- 0.5 pint best powdered sugar
- 0.5 pint wheat flour
- 3 pounds cooked and mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Monday morning steep 1 pint hops in 2 gallons water for half an hour
2. Strain into a crock and let cool until lukewarm
3. Add 2 level teaspoons salt and 0.5 pint best powdered sugar
4. Stir 0.5 pint wheat flour into some of the liquid, pour in and stir all together well
5. Wednesday morning add 3 pounds cooked and mashed potatoes, stir well and let stand until Thursday
6. Then strain and put into crocks with the lid loose for a day or two
7. Should be made two weeks before it is used, as it improves with standing
8. Store in a cool place and shake the crock so the yeast does not settle
