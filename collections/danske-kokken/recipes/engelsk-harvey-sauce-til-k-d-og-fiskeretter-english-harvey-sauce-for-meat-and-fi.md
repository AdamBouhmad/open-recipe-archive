## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 L (1 1/4 pot) water
- 1/4 L (1/4 pot) soy sauce
- 75 gr (15 kvint) crushed celery seed
- 75 gr (15 kvint) mustard seed
- 15 gr (3 kvint) ground ginger
- nutmeg
- cayenne pepper
- 20 gr (4 kvint) garlic
- 75 gr (15 kvint) pickled, chopped cucumbers
- 1 1/2 L (1 1/2 pot) vinegar
- dried peel of one lemon

## Directions

1. Place 1/4 L (1/4 pot) soy sauce, 75 gr (15 kvint) crushed celery seed, 75 gr (15 kvint) mustard seed, 15 gr (3 kvint) ground ginger, a little nutmeg and cayenne pepper, 20 gr (4 kvint) garlic, 75 gr (15 kvint) pickled, chopped cucumbers and 1 1/2 L (1 1/2 pot) vinegar in 1 1/4 L (1 1/4 pot) water.
2. Cook all this together until it has reduced to approximately half.
3. Remove from heat and add dried peel of one lemon.
4. Let the sauce stand covered for a few days and strain before pouring into dry bottles, which are stoppered well.
5. This sauce, as well as No. 740, can be used instead of the expensive English sauces available in commerce.
6. Use in sauce for meat and fish dishes and is used much for cold buffets.
