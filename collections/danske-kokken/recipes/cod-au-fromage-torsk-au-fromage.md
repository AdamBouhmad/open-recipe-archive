## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of cold, cooked macaroni, broken into small pieces
- 1 cupful of cold, cooked cod
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Cold, melted butter or milk
- Grated cheese

## Directions

1. Mix the macaroni and cod well.
2. Place in a buttered dish or bowl and lay a small lump of butter on top.
3. Season with salt and pepper, moisten with cold melted butter or milk.
4. Sprinkle several spoonfuls of grated cheese over it and bake until done, then serve in the pan.
