## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 skålpund) cauliflower, divided into small florets
- Salt
- 1 1/2 liters (1 1/2 pots) vinegar
- 3 pods Spanish pepper
- 1 tablespoon whole white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the cauliflower divided into small florets
2. Give it one good boil in salted water
3. Drain in sieve
4. Boil the vinegar with Spanish pepper pods and whole white pepper
5. Place the cauliflower in a crock, cover with the boiling vinegar, and cover
