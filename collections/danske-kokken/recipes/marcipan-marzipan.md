## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg good sweet almonds
- 25 gr bitter almonds
- 375 gr flour sugar
- 375 gr flour sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls rose water or rum

## Directions

1. Blanch the sweet and bitter almonds, remove the skin, rinse and dry in a cloth.
2. Pass this mass twice through an enameled meat grinder and then 2 more times with the flour sugar.
3. Now knead the almond paste with the flour sugar and rose water or rum.
4. Can be stored in parchment in a cool place.
5. Form figures from this fresh marzipan and decorate with a brush using confectioner's colors, which can be bought in small portions at the grocer's.
6. Place the finished marzipan on dishes and set in a cool place for a couple of days.
7. The marzipan should not be baked.
