## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Thin cream or sweet milk
- Chopped parsley
- Salt
- Insignificant amount of sugar
- A little cold butter

## Directions

1. Make a roux with butter and flour and dilute with thin cream or sweet milk to a properly smooth sauce, into which is stirred chopped parsley, salt, insignificant sugar and a little cold butter
