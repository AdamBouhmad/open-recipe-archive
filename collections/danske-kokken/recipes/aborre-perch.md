## Ingredients

- perch
- salt
- boiled peeled potatoes
- melted butter
- parsley
- eggs
- cream
- breadcrumbs
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Scrape the fish on a grater to remove the scales and clean it. If boiled, serve with boiled peeled potatoes, melted butter, and parsley.
2. If they are to be fried, which is best for the smallest ones, fill them with a little parsley and butter, dip in eggs, a little cream and breadcrumbs, then fry hard in browned butter and serve with browned butter and lemon slices.
