## Ingredients

- Cured or spiced herring
- Milk
- Powdered sugar
- Ground white pepper
- Onions
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. If using cured herring, soak them in milk overnight; spiced herring generally need not be soaked
2. Skin and clean the herring, cut off the head and tail
3. Split the herring from the back and remove the bone and entrails
4. Cut the herring halves crosswise into small pieces, lay them together again and turn them with the skin side upward when arranging
5. Sprinkle the herring with a little powdered sugar and ground white pepper and cover with thin onion slices
6. Pour vinegar over them a few hours before serving
