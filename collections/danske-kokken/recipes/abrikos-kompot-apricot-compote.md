## Ingredients

- 15 to 20 ripe apricots
- 250 grams (1/2 pund) powdered sugar
- 375 milliliters (1/2 pægel) water

## Directions

1. Split the fruit, remove the stones and crush them; the kernels are cooked together with the apricots, which must not cook longer than until the fruit is beautifully whole when removed.
2. The syrup is reduced and poured over the fruit.
