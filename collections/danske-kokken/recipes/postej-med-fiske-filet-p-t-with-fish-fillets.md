## Ingredients

- 6 plaice, at least 625 grams (1.25 pounds) each
- Short pastry
- Fish farce
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish stock
- Graves (fish roe)
- Champignons
- Capers
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Line a mold with short pastry (see Nr. 21) and fish farce (as in Nr. 35).
2. Skin and clean plaice weighing at least 625 grams (1.25 pounds) each.
3. Remove the fillets from the bones; from each plaice you will get 4 fillets.
4. Roll each fillet individually like a snail and place them tightly together in the mold.
5. Pour over a sauce made from butter and flour, thinned with the stock cooked from the fish bones.
6. The sauce must be quite smooth and seasoned with a little graves and champignons or capers.
7. For the sauce poured over the fish in the mold, eggs are not necessary; however, thin the rest and bind it with 3 to 4 egg yolks before serving.
