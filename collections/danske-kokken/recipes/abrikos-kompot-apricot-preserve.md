## Ingredients

- Not fully ripe apricots
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 1.25 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Split the apricots and remove the stones.
2. Prepare a thin syrup by cooking the sugar and water together.
3. Give the fruit only a good boil in the syrup and place in the sulphured jars.
4. Reduce the syrup for a few minutes before pouring it boiling over the fruit.
5. Tie the jars immediately as instructed above.
6. The stones can be crushed, and the kernels can be scalded and cooked for 5 minutes, either together with the fruit or by themselves in a little of the syrup.
7. Some of the kernels can be mixed with the preserve when serving; the preserve keeps just as well without being preserved together with the kernels, which easily cause it to ferment.
8. Another use of apricot kernels is to use them instead of almonds; the stones are crushed as the kernels are needed.
