## Ingredients

- Venison cutlets from the back
- Salt and pepper
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Game stock made from bones and venison trimmings

## Directions

1. Cut small cutlets from the back.
2. Beat lightly with a meat mallet.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Dip in egg and breadcrumbs.
5. Fry as veal cutlets.
6. Pour a good brown sauce made of game stock cooked on bones and venison trimmings into the center of the serving dish.
7. Arrange the cutlets in a ring around the sauce.
