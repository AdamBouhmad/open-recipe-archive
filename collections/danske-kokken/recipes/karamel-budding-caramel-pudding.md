## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) icing sugar
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) water
- 12 egg yolks
- 75 Gr (15 Kvint) icing sugar
- 1 L (1 Pot) boiling cream
- vanilla essence
- 6 egg whites
- cream foam
- cognac

## Directions

1. Make the caramel the day before: cook 1/2 Kg (1 S) icing sugar with 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) water until dry, stirring constantly until it becomes liquid again and a beautiful brown color (do not let it become too dark or it will taste burnt)
2. Pour some of the caramel sugar into the dry mold and turn it well until a layer of sugar has set on both the sides and bottom
3. For the pudding: stir 12 egg yolks with 75 Gr (15 Kvint) icing sugar and whisk well with a whisk while stirring 1 L (1 Pot) boiling cream into the mixture and adding vanilla essence
4. Stir regularly in the custard until it is cold, then fold in 6 whipped egg whites
5. Pour the pudding into the prepared mold and cook for approximately 2 hours until completely set
6. Keep the pudding in the mold until the next day
7. Serve on a not too flat platter with the caramel that comes with the pudding
8. For the remaining caramel sugar: dilute with water and stir with a little cream foam and a glass of cognac the next day, then serve with the pudding
