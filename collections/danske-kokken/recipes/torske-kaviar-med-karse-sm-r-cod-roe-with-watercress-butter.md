## Ingredients

- Cod roe
- Watercress butter (butter mixed with chopped watercress)
- Butter
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Remove the roe from the tin or jar and press it into a couple of small, moistened molds or cups
2. Turn it out onto plates and decorate these small puddings with watercress butter
3. Decorate with piped butter and place small rosettes of it on lemon slices, which are laid around the roe
4. It should be very cold
