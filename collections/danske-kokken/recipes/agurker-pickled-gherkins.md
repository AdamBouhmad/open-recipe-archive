## Ingredients

- Medium-sized gherkins
- Cold water
- Strong salt brine
- 2 1/2 liters (2 1/2 pots) good vinegar
- 1/4 kg (1/2 skålpund) powdered sugar (Melis)
- 4 pods Spanish pepper
- 2 tablespoons whole yellow mustard
- About 20 small peeled shallots
- A little dill
- A couple fresh bay leaves

## Directions

1. Soak the gherkins in cold water for one day
2. Remove and place in strong salt brine for a couple of days
3. Remove from brine, dry, and cover with boiling vinegar
4. Heat 2 1/2 liters good vinegar with powdered sugar, Spanish pepper pods, whole yellow mustard, small peeled shallots, dill, and fresh bay leaves
5. Bring the gherkins to a boil in this mixture, remove, and place in crock
6. Pour the vinegar with spices over them
7. Cover the crock immediately with cloth and bind with parchment paper the next day
