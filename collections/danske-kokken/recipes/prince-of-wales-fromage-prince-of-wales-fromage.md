## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 375 gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 12 sheets isinglass
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) water
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) white rum
- juice of 1 lemon
- stiffly whipped egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks well with powdered sugar
2. Dissolve isinglass in water, add white rum and lemon juice
3. Stir this mixture into the eggs
4. Fold in stiffly whipped egg whites
5. Serve with fruit sauce
