## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice grains
- 4 pots milk
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean 1 pound of rice grains
2. Grease the bottom of a pot with butter to prevent sticking
3. Add 4 pots of milk and bring to the fire
4. When the milk boils, sprinkle the drained rice grains into it from a sieve
5. Cook until the grains are tender, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking
6. Serve sprinkled with powdered sugar and cinnamon and with butter on the side
7. For gruel, use half the amount of rice grains
