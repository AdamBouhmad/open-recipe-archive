## Ingredients

- Leeks
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. After the lower white part of the leeks is cut away for sweet leeks, some of the firm yellow-green part above can be cooked and placed in the baked omelet, which is then folded together.
2. Sweet leeks look more beautiful in this and taste even better.
3. Serve with creamed butter.
