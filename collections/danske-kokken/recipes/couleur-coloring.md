## Ingredients

- 1 pound St. Croix sugar
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Moisten 1 pound St. Croix sugar in a kettle and boil with constant stirring to a thick, brownish syrup.
2. Dilute with boiling water and boil again for 1 hour.
3. When cold, pour into clean, dry bottles, cork and store in a dry place.
4. This produces a golden-brown color.
