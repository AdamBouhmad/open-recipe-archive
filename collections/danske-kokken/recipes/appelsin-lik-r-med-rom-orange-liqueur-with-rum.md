## Ingredients

- Thinly peeled yellow zest of 8 oranges
- 2 l (2 pots) white rum
- 1 kg (2 S) powdered sugar
- 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water

## Directions

1. Place the thinly peeled yellow zest of 8 oranges in a jar and pour 2 l (2 pots) white rum over it. Tie the jar and set it in the warmth for 3 to 4 days.
2. Strain the rum through a clear but fairly tight piece of cloth.
3. Prepare a syrup by boiling 1 kg (2 S) powdered sugar and 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water together.
4. Pour the syrup over the rum, then fill into dry bottles, cork them, and seal with gypsum or lacquer.
