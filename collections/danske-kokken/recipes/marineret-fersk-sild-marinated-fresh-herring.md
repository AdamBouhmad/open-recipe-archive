## Ingredients

- Herring
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Lemon peel
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- Cloves
- Thyme

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, rinse, dry and brush with oil, season, roast on both sides.
2. Layer in a glass jar with the spices and pour over with vinegar that has previously been brought to a boil and cooled.
