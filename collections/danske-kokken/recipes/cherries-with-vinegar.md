## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cherries
- 315 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl vinegar

## Directions

1. The sugar can be chosen between powdered sugar, icing sugar or Demerara sugar, the latter gives the best juice.
2. Place the sugar in the pot in layers with the pitted berries, and pour the vinegar over.
3. The pot should initially be placed over low heat, while stirring occasionally.
4. As soon as the cherries boil, skim them and remove them on a colander, but not before they begin to wrinkle; usually one counts 10 minutes of boiling.
5. Pour the cherries, from which the juice must have drained well, into a jar, and reduce the juice until it is fairly thick.
6. Pour it boiling over the cherries, which can then be sealed immediately with parchment paper.
7. If desired, some pieces of whole cinnamon and some cloves can be boiled with the juice.
