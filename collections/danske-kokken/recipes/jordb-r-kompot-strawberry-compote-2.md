## Ingredients

- 250 grams (1/2 pund) powdered sugar
- 375 milliliters (1/2 pægel) water
- 500 grams (1 pund) strawberries
- Sago flour

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and water to a syrup and add the strawberries.
2. Remove them from the syrup before they cook.
3. Thicken the syrup with sago flour and pour it over the berries.
