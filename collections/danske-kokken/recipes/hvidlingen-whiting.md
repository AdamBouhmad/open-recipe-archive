## Ingredients

- Hvidlingen (whiting)
- Water
- Parsley
- Butter, melted

## Directions

1. Does not keep well; must be eaten fresh immediately
2. Place in boiling water
3. Cook and serve with parsley and melted butter or sour butter sauce
