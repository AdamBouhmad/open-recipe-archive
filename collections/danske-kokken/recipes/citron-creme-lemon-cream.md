## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- juice of 4 lemons
- 1/2 pint old wine
- powdered sugar to taste
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks well; add the corn starch, juice of 4 lemons, 1/2 pint old wine and powdered sugar to taste.
2. Place over heat with constant stirring until it begins to boil and becomes thick.
3. Whisk 4 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold them in.
4. Pour out and continue stirring until it becomes cold.
5. This is for 8 to 10 persons.
