## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 375 grams (¾ pound) powdered sugar
- 1 liter (1 pot) boiling sweet milk
- 16 sheets isinglass
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) water
- 3¾ dl (1½ pægel) Madeira wine
- ¼ dl (¼ pægel) cognac
- 125 grams (¼ pound) finely ground sweet almonds
- 125 grams (¼ pound) cleaned, scalded currants
- 3¾ dl (1½ pægel) heavy cream

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks well with powdered sugar and pour into boiling sweet milk, whisking with a whisk while standing over heat until the cream is smooth but does not come to a boil.
2. Dissolve isinglass in water and add Madeira, cognac, ground almonds, and currants to it.
3. Pour this mixture into the cream, stirring until it is so stiff that it can bear the currants.
4. Then add whipped cream made from the heavy cream.
5. Pour the pudding into a wetted mold and preferably make it the day before.
6. Serve with cold fruit sauce.
