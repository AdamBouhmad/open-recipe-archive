## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Salt
- Water
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or fat

## Directions

1. Cut the plant into slices at least 1 inch thick; clean each piece and place it in salt and water, laying a plate on top to keep them submerged; let them stand for an hour or more. Dry each slice thoroughly, dip it in beaten eggs and breadcrumbs and fry in butter or fat until they are completely fried and brown.
