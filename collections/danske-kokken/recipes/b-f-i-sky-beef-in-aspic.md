## Ingredients

- Cooked beef
- Good aspic
- Isinglass
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Egg yolk and egg white

## Directions

1. Refer to recipe 132 for beef preparation.
2. Deglaze the pan in which the beef was cooked with good aspic.
3. Cook the aspic while stirring constantly until it begins to thicken.
4. Pour the aspic onto the bottom of the dish.
5. Arrange the beef on top.
6. When cold, cover with good aspic mixed with isinglass.
7. When serving, once the aspic has set, garnish with chopped egg yolk and egg white arranged in strips.
