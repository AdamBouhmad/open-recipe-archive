## Ingredients

- 1 kg lump sugar
- 1/2 kg orange peel

## Directions

1. If a large number of lemons or oranges are used in the household, the peels can be preserved and used in baking where candied orange peel or other citrus peel is otherwise used.
2. Remove the peels in 2 halves and soak in cold water for 3 to 4 days, changing the water daily.
3. Cook the peels tender in water under a lid, then remove and place on a strainer.
4. Pour the sugar over the peels.
5. After 3 to 4 days, pour off the syrup and cook until very smooth, then pour back over the peels.
6. 3 days later, pour everything into the pot and boil for 5 to 6 minutes.
7. Remove the peels and place in a jar, then bring the syrup to a boil before pouring over. Seal.
