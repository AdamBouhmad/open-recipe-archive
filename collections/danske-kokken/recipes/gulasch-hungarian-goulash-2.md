## Ingredients

- ½ kg veal, cut into 3-centimeter (1-inch) cubes, per person
- Butter or margarine
- Onions, chopped
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Stock, broth, or water
- Soya
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Tomato paste
- Paprika
- Potato puree
- Pickled cucumbers or pickles

## Directions

1. Heat a good portion of butter or margarine in a pot and brown it together with some chopped onions.
2. Add the meat, which has been tossed in flour mixed with salt and pepper.
3. Brown the meat.
4. Pour in enough stock, broth, or water so that it just reaches the level of the meat.
5. Add soya, Maggi bouillon cubes, several spoonfuls of tomato paste, and paprika.
6. Cook covered until the meat is tender. The sauce will usually be sufficiently thickened by this point.
7. Arrange the goulash with potato puree either placed in the middle of the dish or as a border around the meat.
8. The border can be decorated with chopped pickles or pickled cucumbers.
