## Ingredients

- 1 pair calf sweetbreads
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth from sweetbreads
- 2 egg yolks
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Gravy or sherry
- Mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak sweetbreads in water for one hour
2. Rinse carefully
3. Pour boiling water over them (blanch) and place on heat with cold water, only as much as is needed for the sauce
4. Cook until firm, then remove
5. Remove the membranes and cut into small cubes
6. Make a roux with butter and flour
7. Dilute with the strained broth to a thick sauce
8. Thicken (liaise) with 2 well-beaten egg yolks without bringing to a boil
9. Add salt, ground pepper, a little lemon juice, gravy or sherry, and if desired, mushrooms
10. Add the sweetbreads last
11. Serve krustader as quickly as possible after filling
12. Arrange on a plate with a folded napkin underneath
