## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Broth or milk
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes raw, cut them into slices and place on the fire with just enough broth or milk so it comes level with the potatoes.
2. Cook until completely cooked through, strain and press through a sieve.
3. Cook the mash again briefly with butter, salt, and if it is too thick, with more broth or milk.
4. Cooked potatoes can be grated; they then do not need to be pressed through the sieve.
5. Instead of broth, water can be used, but then more butter must be used.
