## Ingredients

- 4 whole eggs
- 200 Gr (40 Kvint) powdered sugar
- 2 small teaspoons cardamom
- 1 small teaspoon cinnamon
- 100 Gr (20 Kvint) yeast
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) lukewarm, sweet milk
- 1 Kg (2 S) good flour
- a little salt
- 400 Gr (80 Kvint) cold butter or margarine
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) scalded and pitted raisins
- 150 Gr (30 Kvint) finely chopped candied citrus peel
- melted butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Stir eggs with powdered sugar, cardamom and cinnamon
2. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm sweet milk and add to the eggs together with flour and salt
3. Knead the dough together, place on a floured baking board and roll out with a rolling pin on a sheet
4. Distribute 400 Gr (80 Kvint) cold butter or margarine in small lumps on the dough
5. Fold the dough together, roll out and fold together 3 times
6. The last time, cover the rolled-out sheet with 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) scalded and pitted raisins and 150 Gr (30 Kvint) finely chopped candied citrus peel
7. Roll it up as a very long sausage, roll this as a snail, place on a cold, floured sheet and let rise
8. When it has risen and become half again larger, brush over with melted butter and bake at good heat for 1 hour
9. Can also be baked in a form
