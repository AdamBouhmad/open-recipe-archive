## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Cognac, Rum, or Arrack
- Eggs
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Beat 1-2 egg yolks well with 3-4 tablespoons of crushed sugar. While stirring constantly, add boiling water and finally either Cognac, Rum, Arrack, Port wine, or Madeira.
