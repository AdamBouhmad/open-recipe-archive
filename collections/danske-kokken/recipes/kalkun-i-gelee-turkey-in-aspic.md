## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Water
- Salt
- Gelatine
- Beets

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey whole until nearly done
2. Cut off the legs and wings, removing the outermost joints which are not used in the aspic
3. Cleave the breast lengthwise and cut each half into 2 or 3 pieces according to size
4. Do not include the back, but cook it and the bones longer in the stock
5. The good pieces should only cook until properly done
6. The aspic should be white and is made stiff with gelatine, using 1 ounce to each quart of soup when the meat is cooked in barely sufficient water
7. Test by straining a couple of spoonfuls
8. Decorate the aspic with beets
