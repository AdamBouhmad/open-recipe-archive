## Ingredients

- 4 pounds fish
- 4 quarts water
- Salt
- A bundle of herbs
- Root vegetables
- A small handful of leeks
- Parsley
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Almost half a pint of sour cream
- Bread or flour dumplings
- Vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and boil in 4 quarts of water with salt, a bundle of herbs and root vegetables.
2. When these are soft, remove them, rinse them and put them back in the soup when it is strained.
3. Cook out the fish completely, crush it and strain it; pour a little hot water on it in the strainer to extract the flavor.
4. Chop a small handful of leeks and parsley finely with two tablespoons of flour, mix it in almost half a pint of sour cream, thin somewhat with the soup, which is again placed on the fire, add it when it is heated, and stir until it boils; then add bread or flour dumplings to it.
5. If you do not have sour cream, you can stir in a couple of spoonfuls of vinegar into the finished soup.
