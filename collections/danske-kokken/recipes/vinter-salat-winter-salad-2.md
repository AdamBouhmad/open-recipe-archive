## Ingredients

- Cooked macaroni
- Carrots
- Celery or salsify roots
- Pickled gherkins
- Cooked green peas
- Small pickled onions, sliced
- Mayonnaise made with mustard
- Watercress

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked macaroni, carrots, celery or salsify roots, and pickled gherkins into small pieces.
2. Mix in the cooked green peas and small sliced pickled onions.
3. Blend all these ingredients into the diluted mayonnaise made with mustard.
4. Sprinkle the salad with watercress.
