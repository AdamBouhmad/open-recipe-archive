## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 375 grams flour (approximately 1/4 lb)
- Egg white
- Flour for binding

## Directions

1. Mix eggs and flour to form a fairly firm dough that can be easily rolled out
2. Roll out a strip 5 centimeters (2 inches) wide, fairly thin and long enough to reach around the inside edge of the dish
3. Cut the border perfectly straight
4. Form figures on the upper edge with a border iron, or cut simply into points with a knife, and make a clear pattern with a pastry bag
5. Lay the border to dry and set it up only when it has begun to stiffen
6. Fix it to the dish with egg white mixed thick with flour
7. Suitable for meat, fish and vegetables
