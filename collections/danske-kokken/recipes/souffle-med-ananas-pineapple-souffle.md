## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 150 g (30 quintal) fine powdered sugar
- egg whites
- butter
- stewed pineapple, cut into very small pieces, without juice

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks until foamy with the fine powdered sugar.
2. Beat the egg whites to very stiff foam and carefully fold into the stirred egg mixture.
3. Butter a shallow baking dish that can withstand oven heat and pour in the egg mixture mixed with the stewed pineapple pieces.
4. Bake the omelet within 25 minutes. It must go straight from the oven to the table.
5. Arrange and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
