## Ingredients

- Venison haunch
- Lard strips
- Margarine or fat

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the haunch.
2. Lard the haunch well with fine strips of lard.
3. Cover with margarine or fat.
4. Roast and serve as with venison leg.
