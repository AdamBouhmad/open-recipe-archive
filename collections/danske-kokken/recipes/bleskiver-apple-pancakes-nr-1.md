## Ingredients

- half a Cup Butter
- half a Cup Sugar
- 4 Egg Yolks
- 1 Quart Milk
- 2 Cents Yeast
- Flour as needed
- Egg Whites (whipped)

## Directions

1. Stir half a Cup Butter well with half a Cup Sugar, then with 4 Egg Yolks
2. Warm 1 Quart Milk and dissolve 2 Cents Yeast in it
3. Add the stirred mixture and sift in as much Flour as will make the batter properly stiff (somewhat thicker than for pancakes)
4. Fold in the whipped Egg Whites
5. Beat well in a crock which, covered, stands by the heat for a couple of hours so the batter can rise
6. When the pan is hot, put a little Butter in each hole and fill the hole a little more than half
7. When baked enough on one side, turn them with two Forks
