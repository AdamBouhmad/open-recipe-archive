## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal shoulder or neck, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 125 g butter
- 1 dessert plate finely chopped onions
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon curry
- 3/4 liter water
- Potato puree (see recipe 650)

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into small 1-inch cubes and coat in flour.
2. Heat 125 g butter in a pot and sauté the finely chopped onions.
3. Add the meat along with salt, 1 tablespoon curry, and 3/4 liter water.
4. Cook the meat gently under a lid for approximately 1.5 hours, or until very tender.
5. Arrange potato puree in a mound in the center of a flat dish and surround with the curry, or alternatively place the meat in the center and arrange the puree around it as a border, which can be placed in the oven and baked before serving.
6. In East India, potato puree and rice are served simultaneously with this dish.
