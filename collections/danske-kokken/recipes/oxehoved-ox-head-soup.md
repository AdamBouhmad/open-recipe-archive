## Ingredients

- Meat from a cooked ox head
- Ox lung
- Ox heart
- Ox liver
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Apples
- Ginger
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Whole allspice
- Flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Wash the ox parts well and soak them several hours in water to draw out the blood
2. Boil them in salted water and skim carefully
3. After sufficient cooking, remove all unusable parts; mince the lung and heart, and grate the liver to more easily remove the sinews
4. Strain the broth in which they were cooked and bring the minced meat to a boil in this broth along with the head meat cut into cubes
5. Place in crocks and seal when cold
6. When ready to use, add vinegar and sugar along with sliced apples, if you have them, some pieces of ginger, whole pepper and cloves, and whole allspice and thicken with flour mixed in butter
