## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Kg (3 S) good, fine, large apples
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) fine flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 50 Gr (10 Kvint) melted margarine
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) half-skimmed milk
- a little sugar
- salt
- cardamom
- egg whites
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Peel 1 1/2 Kg (3 S) good, fine, large apples and cut them into 1/4 inch thick slices, from which the core is removed from the middle.
2. For this portion, stir a dough of 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) fine flour, 3 egg yolks, 50 Gr (10 Kvint) melted margarine, 1/4 L (1 Pægl) half-skimmed milk, a little sugar, salt, cardamom and the beaten egg whites.
3. Take the apples with a fork and dip them into the dough, which must completely cover them; from there, place them in boiling clarified butter until they have a nice, light brown colour.
4. The dough covering the middle can be removed so that the rings are nicely shaped.
