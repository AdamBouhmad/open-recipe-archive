## Ingredients

- Venison haunch
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Crushed cloves
- Butter
- Water
- Wheat flour
- Bouillon
- Red wine
- Cream
- Roasted potatoes
- Redcurrant jelly

## Directions

1. Beat the haunch well, rinse and dry with a clean linen cloth.
2. Lard first on the underside and then in the middle of the thigh's upper side in the form of a star or rose.
3. Pour vinegar over the haunch and sprinkle with salt mixed with finely crushed cloves. Cover with butter and place buttered white paper over it.
4. Pour a little water over it and roast with regular basting for approximately 3 to 4 hours until tender.
5. Remove the paper. Brown the sauce with flour, thin with good bouillon, and add a little red wine and cream, then strain.
6. Serve with roasted potatoes and redcurrant jelly.
