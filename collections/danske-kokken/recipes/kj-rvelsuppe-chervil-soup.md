## Ingredients

- 3 til 4 Haandfulde Kjørvel (chervil)
- 5 Potter Bouillon (beef or lamb broth)
- Et Fjerdingspund Smør (butter)
- Rengjorte Asparges (cleaned asparagus)
- Nogle smaa kogte Gulerødder (small cooked carrots)
- Forlorne Æg (poached eggs)
- Ristede Skiver af Franskbrød (toasted French bread slices)

## Directions

1. Rinse and finely chop 3 to 4 handfuls of chervil.
2. Heat 5 pots of bouillon (made from veal or lamb meat) and skim it.
3. Melt a quarter pound of butter in a saucepan and brown it for about a quarter hour.
4. Gradually thin the roux with the bouillon.
5. Cut cleaned asparagus into one-inch pieces, boil in water, and add to the soup along with some of the cooking water.
6. Add small cooked carrots and the chopped chervil to the soup.
7. Bring the soup to a boil and serve with poached eggs and toasted slices of French bread.
