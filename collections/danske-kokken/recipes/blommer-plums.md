## Ingredients

- Plums
- Powdered sugar (optional, for serving as compote)

## Directions

1. Choice, unblemished plums must be used, and the stems must be removed carefully so as not to damage the fruit.
2. Fill the plums into jars with wide mouths, seal them, wrap with hay, and cook according to the preceding instructions for 20 minutes.
3. The jars remain in the pot until the water has cooled, or they are removed, covered, dried, and marked.
4. The plums may be served as compote, but first sprinkle with powdered sugar and let them stand for a couple of hours.
5. Suitable in grain soup or for plum soup.
6. Crags, a type of small plums, may be treated the same way, but must not cook more than 10 minutes.
