## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound flour
- 1/2 pound clarified butter
- 1 pint boiling water
- 10 eggs

## Directions

1. These cakes require strong heat to be quick, but must not become brown at all.
2. Bake the flour well in 1/2 pound clarified butter.
3. Add 1 pint boiling water and when it has baked with it, cool it a little, stir for half an hour with 10 eggs, two at a time, and place preferably in small molds, but if not available, with a spoon in small lumps on the pan.
4. Makes 25 cakes.
5. They can be filled with Vanilla Cream or Pastry Cream.
