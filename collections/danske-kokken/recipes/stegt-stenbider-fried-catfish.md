## Ingredients

- catfish
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- Palmin
- browned butter
- tomato slices
- cucumber salad

## Directions

1. Skin fish and cut from backbone into smaller pieces
2. Dip in eggs and breadcrumbs
3. Fry in Palmin
4. Serve with browned butter and garnish with tomato slices topped with cucumber salad
