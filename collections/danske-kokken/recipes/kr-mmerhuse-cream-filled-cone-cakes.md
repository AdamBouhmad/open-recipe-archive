## Ingredients

- Egg whites
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Margarine
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Powdered sugar (Melis)
- 140 Gr (28 Quintal) Flour (Flormel)
- 8 beaten egg whites
- Whipped cream
- Preserved strawberries, cherries, raspberry jam, currant or gooseberry jelly

## Directions

1. Melt the margarine; while hot, stir in the powdered sugar and flour, and finally 8 beaten egg whites.
2. The baking sheet must be warm; brush it well and place the dough on it with a teaspoon, with large intervals.
3. Spread the dough with a spoon into elongated cakes and bake quickly in a well-heated oven; they should have a beautiful golden-brown color.
4. When the cakes are finished but still in the oven, loosen them with a knife and twist into the form of a cone, which must be done very quickly, as the cakes otherwise break and cannot be bent once cooled.
5. Store in a tin box and fill with whipped cream just before serving, as they otherwise become soft. Preserved strawberries, cherry jam, raspberry jam, or currant or gooseberry jelly may be served alongside.
