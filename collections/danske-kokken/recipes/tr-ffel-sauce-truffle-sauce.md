## Ingredients

- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) strong gravy sauce
- 2 tablespoonfuls finely cut truffles
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Take 2 tablespoonfuls of finely cut truffles for 1/2 L of strong gravy sauce
2. Steam the truffles in Madeira before adding them to the sauce
