## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Eggs

## Directions

1. The mushrooms used here must not be sour, that is, not preserved with vinegar or lemon juice.
2. Best are those that are preserved in butter.
3. Use the mushroom butter in the scrambled eggs.
