## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 1.5 cups best oatmeal
- salt
- cream or milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. In 2 quarts of boiling, slightly salted water, add 1.5 cups best oatmeal little by little; stir to prevent it from settling on the bottom
2. Should be cooked well for an hour or more
3. Serve with cream or milk and sugar
