## Ingredients

- 1/2 Liter (1/2 Pot) cream
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) small macaroons
- 65 Gr (13 Kvint) blanched sweet almonds, sliced into strips
- candied pumpkin with ginger or Chinese ginger
- a little strawberry jam
- liqueur or 1 wine glass cognac
- 5 leaves isinglass
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) water
- cream foam, half colored pink with a few drops of fruit coloring and flavored with vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Whip 1/2 Liter (1/2 Pot) cream to foam
2. Stir in 125 Gr (1/4 S) small macaroons, 65 Gr (13 Kvint) blanched sweet almonds sliced into strips, candied pumpkin with ginger or Chinese ginger, and a little strawberry jam
3. Flavor the pudding with liqueur, or if not available, add 1 wine glass of cognac
4. Dissolve 5 leaves of isinglass in 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) water and stir into the pudding
5. Pour into a mold and decorate with cream foam, half of which is colored pink with a few drops of fruit coloring and flavored with vanilla essence
