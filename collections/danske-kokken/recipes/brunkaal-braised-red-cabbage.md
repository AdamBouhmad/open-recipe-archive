## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- butter or wine
- sugar
- color
- broth, gravy or water
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice white cabbage
2. Brown a good slice of butter or wine with a spoonful of sugar in a pot
3. Add the cabbage and brown it with regular stirring so it does not burn
4. If needed, add color to make it brown faster
5. When it has sufficient color, add a little liquid which can be broth, gravy or water
6. Braise it tender under a lid
7. Season with salt and more sugar if necessary
