## Ingredients

- 14 pounds wheat bread
- 5 ounces butter
- 10 eggs
- 4 ounces raisins
- Nutmeg, salt, and a little sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape off the crust of 14 pounds of wheat bread on a grater, cut into smaller pieces
2. Dip in cold water and let sit covered for a couple hours
3. Press the bread with a spoon, add 5 ounces butter and stir warm, not hot, for half an hour or until the batter becomes white
4. Mix in 10 well-beaten eggs, raisins, nutmeg, salt, and a little sugar
5. Cook for 2.5 hours and serve with sweet sauce
