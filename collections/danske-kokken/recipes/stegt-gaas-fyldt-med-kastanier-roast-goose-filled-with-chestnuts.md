## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- about 750 grams chestnuts, shelled
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- water
- olives

## Directions

1. After cleaning the goose and rubbing the inside with salt and pepper, fill it with the 750 grams of chestnuts, whose shells have been removed.
2. Scald the chestnuts so that the thin brown skin can be removed.
3. Sew the goose closed and roast it.
4. Serve with ordinary brown pan sauce, to which olives can be added.
5. Serve the chestnuts with the roast.
