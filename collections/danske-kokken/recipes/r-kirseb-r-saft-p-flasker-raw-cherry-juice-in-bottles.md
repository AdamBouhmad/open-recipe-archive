## Ingredients

- Good freshly picked cherries
- Brandy

## Directions

1. The berries, which must be good and freshly picked, are crushed and wrung out in a cloth and placed in completely dry bottles
2. Pour as much brandy as stands a few inches above the juice
3. The bottles should preferably be small, as the juice does not keep once they have been opened
