## Ingredients

- Half a pound almonds, of which approximately 6 ounces are bitter
- A little lemon peel
- 4 pots sweet milk
- 1 pound rice flour
- One-quarter pound vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Scald and chop the almonds with a little lemon peel.
2. Place 3 pots of the sweet milk in an enameled pot smeared with cold butter at the bottom and cook over gentle heat.
3. Mix 1 pound of rice flour with the remaining 2 pots of cold milk.
4. When the milk in the pot begins to boil, add the mixed flour and almonds.
5. Stir the porridge continuously until fully cooked.
6. Stir in one-quarter pound of white vanilla sugar.
7. Pour into a large bowl to cool.
8. Serve cold with mixed fruit juice or diluted wine with sugar.
