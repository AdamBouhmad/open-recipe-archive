## Ingredients

- Butter
- Soy sauce or Sky
- Cold butter
- Sour or sweet cream or buttermilk (for beef or veal roast)

## Directions

1. Heat the pan before adding butter
2. When the butter is brown, stir in some soy sauce or Sky
3. Remove the pan from heat and stir in a piece of cold butter strongly until smooth
4. For beef or veal roast, sour or sweet cream or buttermilk can be used instead of cold butter
5. Remove the pan from heat while stirring in the cream, then place on heat again for a small boil while stirring constantly
