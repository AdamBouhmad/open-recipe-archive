## Ingredients

- 125 g (1/4 S) margarine
- 125 g (1/4 S) Pillsbury flour
- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) boiling milk
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites
- jam
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Bake the margarine and flour together and dilute with 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) of boiling milk.
2. When the batter has cooled, stir it with 6 egg yolks and finally the beaten egg whites.
3. Bake the batter in 3 thick pancakes with a layer of jam between them. Sprinkle the top with powdered sugar.
