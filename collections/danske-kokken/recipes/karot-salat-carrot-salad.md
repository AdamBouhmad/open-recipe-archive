## Ingredients

- small carrots
- lettuce leaves
- oil
- vinegar or lemon juice
- pepper
- parsley
- cooked green peas

## Directions

1. Scrape small carrots or cook them first, and rub off the skin as you would with hot cooked beets
2. Cut them into slices with a fluted vegetable knife and arrange them on a bed of lettuce leaves
3. Pour a sauce of oil, vinegar or lemon juice, and pepper over them and sprinkle chopped parsley on top, or arrange cooked green peas in a ring on top of the salad
