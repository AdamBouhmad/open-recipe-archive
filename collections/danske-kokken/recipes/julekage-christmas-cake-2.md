## Ingredients

- 3 ounces butter (3 tablespoons)
- salt
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 pint fresh milk
- 13 pounds flour
- 2 cents yeast dissolved in a little milk
- half a pound raisins
- cardamom
- sugar

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and hold back most of the salt.
2. Stir it with two tablespoons white sugar and 3 whole eggs.
3. Add 1 pint fresh milk.
4. Add 13 pounds flour, 2 cents yeast dissolved in a little milk, half a pound raisins, cardamom and sugar.
5. When it becomes too hard to stir, knead it with the hands until the dough begins to rise and releases from the board.
6. Set it aside until it has risen, then place it in a mold or shape it into a Christmas cake without kneading further.
7. Brush with sugar or spread with butter or egg white and bake in a good hour at good heat.
