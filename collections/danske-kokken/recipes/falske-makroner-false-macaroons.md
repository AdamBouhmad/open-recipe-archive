## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 5 ounces bitter almonds
- 2 pounds wheat flour
- 1½ pounds powdered sugar
- 4 whole eggs
- lemon zest, grated
- Ceylon cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds and grind them fine
2. Mix together with 2 pounds wheat flour, 1½ pounds powdered sugar, 4 whole eggs, grated lemon zest, and ground Ceylon cinnamon
3. Roll the dough into small balls
4. Press them flat as they are placed on the pan
