## Ingredients

- Peeled and cut celery root
- Water or soup
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled and cut celery root until tender in water or soup with salt
2. Brown butter and flour together and thin with the strained celery cooking liquid to make a sauce
3. Thicken the sauce with a couple of egg yolks
4. Add salt, pepper and a little sugar to taste
