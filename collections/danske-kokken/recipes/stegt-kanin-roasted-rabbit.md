## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Basilicum
- Thyme
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Sauce

## Directions

1. After the rabbit is skinned, cleaned and washed like a hare, place it for 24 hours in a cool place under a tightly fitting lid in a deep basin with basilicum and thyme.
2. Lard densely as the meat is dry, and roast as a hare (Nr. 159), but pour a little vinegar over it during roasting and baste very frequently.
