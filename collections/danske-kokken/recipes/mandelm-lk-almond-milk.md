## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 12 quintals bitter almonds
- 5 pound powdered sugar
- 1 can boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald the sweet and bitter almonds and pound them very fine together with the powdered sugar.
2. Pour 1 can of boiling water over the mixture.
3. Let it stand covered for a couple of hours.
4. Strain it, cool it, and drink it cold.
