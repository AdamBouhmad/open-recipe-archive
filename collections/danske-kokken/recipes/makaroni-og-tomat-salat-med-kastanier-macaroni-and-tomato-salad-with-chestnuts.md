## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- tomato purée
- paprika
- sugar
- salt
- a little wine
- cooked finely chopped macaroni
- cooked chestnuts
- cooked salted beef tongue
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour together, thin with tomato purée to make an even sauce, seasoned with paprika, sugar, salt and a little wine
2. Mix the cooked finely chopped macaroni with the sauce and arrange the salad on a platter
3. Garnish with lettuce leaves and arrange a ring of cooked chestnuts around it
4. Slices of cooked salted beef tongue can be placed around it
