## Ingredients

- haricots verts
- dill
- tarragon leaves
- fresh bay leaves
- raw vinegar
- powdered sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Remove the seams from the back and belly of the beans
2. Dry the beans and layer them in jars with dill, tarragon leaves and some fresh bay leaves
3. Pour raw vinegar over them, seasoned with a little powdered sugar to taste
4. The next day, drain the vinegar, bring it to a boil, and once cooled, pour it over the beans
5. Bind the jar
6. Serve with roasted meat
