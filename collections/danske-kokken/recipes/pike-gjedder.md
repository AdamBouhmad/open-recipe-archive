## Ingredients

- Pike
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Butter
- Horseradish
- Browned butter
- Capers

## Directions

1. Pike are good except in March and are fattest in February.
2. They are best boiled whole with the scales left on, brushed with a little vinegar and sprinkled with salt.
3. The tail is considered the best and fattest piece.
4. Serve with butter and horseradish.
5. They can be boiled in fricassée like perch, but then the scales must be scraped off.
6. A large pike can be roasted whole, the skin is removed, it is stuffed, roasted in the oven and basted frequently.
7. Serve with browned butter in which capers are added.
