## Ingredients

- Pike-perch
- Salt
- Water or thin soup
- Food coloring
- Butter
- Flour
- Paprika
- Madeira
- Mushrooms
- Butter pastry slices

## Directions

1. Clean and split the fish, remove the bone and cut into 2½ centimeter (about 1 inch) thick slices
2. Wash the slices and sprinkle with salt
3. Cook the head and backbone in water or thin soup, color it brown with food coloring
4. Brown a slice of butter and bake a good spoonful of flour in it
5. Dilute with the strained fish soup until there is enough to cook the fish in
6. Season the sauce to taste with paprika and Madeira
7. Rinse the fish and lay the pieces side by side in a flat casserole or pot
8. Pour the prepared sauce over them
9. Cook very gently and remove when done
10. Finish the sauce with an addition of mushrooms and pour over the fish
11. Garnish with butter pastry slices
