## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- broth
- forcemeat
- small round flat baking forms
- water
- paper
- brown gravy sauce
- Madeira wine
- sago flour
- small fish balls
- salad leaves

## Directions

1. Shred white cabbage finely and brown it in butter with a little sugar and salt
2. Steam it tender in a little gravy
3. Butter small round flat baking forms and line them with forcemeat
4. Place in the oven in a roasting pan covered with boiling water and topped with paper brushed with butter
5. When the forcemeat is cooked, carefully remove from the forms
6. Arrange the cabbage inside in the form of a nest with 3 small fish balls in the center
7. Serve with a brown gravy sauce to which 1 glass of Madeira has been added and which is thickened with sago flour
8. Garnish with salad leaves between the bird nests
