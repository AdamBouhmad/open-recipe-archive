## Ingredients

- 1 pint raw fruit juice, preferably raspberries
- sugar
- 3 ounces gelatine
- 1 quart fresh cream

## Directions

1. Boil the fruit juice with sugar and 3 ounces gelatine until the gelatine is dissolved.
2. Allow to cool and fold in 1 quart fresh cream that has been whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Continue stirring until it is cold, then pour into a mold that has first been wetted, from which it can be turned out again.
4. Serve without sauce with small, dry cakes.
5. Can be made the same way from stewed juice, which then must be heated.
6. For 8 persons.
