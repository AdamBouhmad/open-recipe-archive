## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Vinegar
- Unsalted butter of best quality

## Directions

1. Clean and extract the mushrooms in water with vinegar (see previous recipe)
2. Bring unsalted butter of best quality to a boil, and cook the well-drained mushrooms in it for 10 minutes
3. Take out the mushrooms, place them in jars, and cook the butter for 5 minutes over gentle heat; thereby some of the moisture which the mushrooms contain will evaporate, and they will be better preserved
4. After being packed in jars, the butter must completely cover them
5. Bind the jars very carefully with parchment paper; this can be painted over with egg white
6. The butter in which they are cooked is used in the dishes in which the mushrooms are used
