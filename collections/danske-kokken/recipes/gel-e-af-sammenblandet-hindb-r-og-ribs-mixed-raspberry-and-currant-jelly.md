## Ingredients

- Juice from 5 pounds of red currants
- Juice from 2 pots of raspberries
- Powdered sugar (equal weight to the combined juice)

## Directions

1. Filter the raspberry juice and cook it with an equal weight of powdered sugar as the juice weighs
2. Otherwise the treatment is the usual (see recipe 474)
