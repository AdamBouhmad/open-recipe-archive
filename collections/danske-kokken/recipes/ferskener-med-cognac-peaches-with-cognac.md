## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) peaches
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- 1.5 dl (1/3 pint) water
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water into a fairly smooth syrup in which the whole peaches boil for 3 minutes and are then placed in glass jars or crocks, which are left covered for 3 to 4 days.
2. After this time has elapsed, pour off the syrup, reduce it until it is very smooth, and pour it over the peaches.
3. The jars must not be filled completely, as after the fruit has cooled, a generous amount of good Cognac must be added before the jars are sealed.
