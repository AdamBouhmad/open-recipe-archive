## Ingredients

- 1¼ L grains
- 4½ L water

## Directions

1. This soup is cooked and prepared as Barley Soup.
2. It tolerates 3 hours of cooking, and if it should still not be sufficiently thickened, a little potato flour stirred in can be added, which will also prevent the grains from settling to the bottom.
