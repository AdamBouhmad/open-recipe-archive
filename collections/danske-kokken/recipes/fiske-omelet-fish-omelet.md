## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound flour
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 measures sweet milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 2 pounds cooked fish
- oyster or mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Bake 1/4 pound flour with 1/4 pound butter
2. Thin with 2 measures sweet milk
3. When well baked, stir in 8 egg yolks
4. Finally stir in the beaten egg whites
5. Stir in 2 pounds cooked fish in large pieces
6. Bake the omelet for 1 hour, it should be light brown
7. Serve with oyster or mushroom sauce
