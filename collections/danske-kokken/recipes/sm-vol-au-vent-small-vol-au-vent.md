## Ingredients

- Butter Dough
- Egg
- Filling of choice

## Directions

1. Roll out butter dough to the same thickness as for small pastry cases.
2. Cut out rounds approximately 8 centimeters (3 inches) in diameter.
3. Brush with egg as described for pastry cases.
4. Use the same fillings as for pastry cases, though cut less finely.
5. Vol au vent do not have lids.
