## Ingredients

- Pork kidneys
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Onion slices
- Stock or soup
- Coloring
- Cold butter
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Place kidneys in cold water
2. Remove the skin
3. Cut into long slices
4. Soak lightly and place on an inverted sieve, press in a cloth
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Fry in butter on a fairly hot pan over brisk heat
7. A good portion of onion slices can be browned before the kidneys are fried and distributed over the fried meat
8. Deglaze the pan with a little stock or soup and a little coloring
9. Stir in a piece of cold butter
10. Pour this reduced stock over the meat
11. Serve with boiled potatoes
12. Veal kidneys can be prepared the same way
