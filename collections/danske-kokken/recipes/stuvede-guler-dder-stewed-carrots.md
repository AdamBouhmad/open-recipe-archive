## Ingredients

- carrots
- water
- Maggi bouillon cubes or thin broth
- butter
- flour
- ground white pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- sugar

## Directions

1. Small carrots are preferred for stewed carrots
2. If using larger carrots, cook them whole first, then cut them
3. They can be split and cut into smaller pieces or into slices, preferably with a fluted vegetable knife
4. Scrape the carrots and place in enough water with Maggi bouillon cubes or thin broth to provide sufficient liquid for stewing
5. Make a roux of butter and flour, dilute with the carrot broth
6. Season with ground white pepper, salt, chopped parsley, and if needed, a little sugar
