## Ingredients

- dried peaches
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the dried fruit in water for 2 days
2. Rinse the dried fruit well in several changes of lukewarm water before soaking
3. Cook the fruit in the water in which it has been soaked with lemon juice
4. Add the sugar only after the fruit is cooked and removed, allowing the sugar and juice to boil together
5. Pour the boiling syrup over the fruit
