## Ingredients

- Cooked chicken or leftovers from chicken dishes
- Sauce as in recipe 6
- Mushrooms (optional)
- Lobster and crab extract (optional, as a cheaper alternative)

## Directions

1. Use cooked chicken or leftover chicken dishes
2. Prepare sauce as in recipe 6
3. Turkey can be used in the same way
4. Lobster and crab extract can be used instead of mushrooms as it is cheaper
