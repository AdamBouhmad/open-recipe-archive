## Ingredients

- Lingonberries
- 1/2 liter (1/2 Pot) water per 1/2 kg berries
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar or Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice

## Directions

1. Stem, weigh, and rinse the berries.
2. When the water has drained well, place them over heat with 1/2 liter (1/2 Pot) water per 1/2 kg berries.
3. Cook them through, pour into the jelly cloth.
4. Measure the juice that has run through.
5. Boil it with the sugar for 10 minutes, skim during cooking, and pour into bottles, which are bound immediately.
