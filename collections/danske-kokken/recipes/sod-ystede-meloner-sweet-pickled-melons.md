## Ingredients

- Small, not overly ripe musk melons
- One quart water
- Two tablespoons fine salt
- One quart water
- One pint good white vinegar
- One pound melons (prepared)
- One pound sugar
- One pint white wine

## Directions

1. Place the melons in one quart of water in which two tablespoons of fine salt is dissolved; let sit for 12 hours.
2. Transfer to a mixture of one quart water and one pint white vinegar; leave for 24 hours.
3. Remove, peel, and halve the melons; remove the pulpy parts.
4. Dry with fine cloth and cut into long pieces.
5. For 1 pound of melons, take 1 pound sugar and 1 pint white wine; cook sugar and wine together and skim well.
6. Add the cut melon and bring to a boil.
7. Pour into a glass and add the syrup while hot.
8. If it becomes thin after several days, cook it again and pour cold over the melons.
