## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal
- 0.5 pound pork
- 1 large cupful finely grated breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs
- Pepper
- Salt
- Grated nutmeg
- Thyme (pulverized or chopped)

## Directions

1. Mince the veal and pork finely.
2. Stir in a large cupful of finely grated breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, pepper, salt, grated nutmeg, and a small amount of pulverized or chopped thyme.
3. Place the mixture in a deep roasting pan with browned butter and brown it well on both sides.
4. Pour bouillon over it and roast slowly in the oven for about 2 hours, turning occasionally.
