## Ingredients

- 2 measures sweet cream
- 1/2 pound white sugar
- 4 tablespoons pulverized orris root
- 8-10 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 2 measures sweet cream with 1/2 pound white sugar and 4 tablespoons pulverized orris root
2. Beat 8-10 egg yolks and pour the boiling cream into them
3. Pour the mixture back into the pan and heat with constant beating until thick
4. Pour out and continue beating until cold
5. Fold in 6 beaten egg whites
6. Put the soufflé in a gentle oven heat to rise slowly
7. Serve on a cake dish, sprinkled with pearl sugar
