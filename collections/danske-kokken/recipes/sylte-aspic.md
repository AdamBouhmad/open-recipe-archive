## Ingredients

- Pig head
- Water
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Ginger
- Finely chopped onion
- Linen cloth
- Laurel leaves
- Allspice

## Directions

1. A pig head is best; but slices of boiled, striped bacon can also in a similar way be made into pressed aspic.
2. Split the head lengthwise, take out the eyes, and clean the ears well, after which it is set over the fire in cold water.
3. When it is about to come to a boil, pour out this water and put fresh water and a little salt on it.
4. Cook it with this until it is tender, then take it up and place it in cold water.
5. Take the meat from the bones and lay it in layers in a clean linen cloth, alternating with salt, finely ground pepper, ginger and finely chopped onion.
6. Fold the linen cloth together and bind it firmly, after which the aspic is placed in a press.
7. If one wishes to have the aspic in gelée, one can reduce the soup with salt, laurel leaves and allspice, skim off the fat and pour the reduced, cold soup over the aspic that has first been cut into appropriate pieces.
