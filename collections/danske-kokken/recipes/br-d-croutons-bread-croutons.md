## Ingredients

- Old French bread, crust removed
- Butter or Palmin

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices
2. Form into various shapes using a knife or cookie cutter
3. Bake until light golden brown in butter or Palmin on a frying pan
4. Place on paper to drain the fat
5. Serve with soup or as garnish around various dishes
