## Ingredients

- Fresh calf's liver
- Calf's heart
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Browned butter
- Onions
- Stock (Sky)
- Smoked bacon (optional)

## Directions

1. Place fresh calf's liver in cold water for a quarter hour, then rinse and remove the membrane.
2. Cut the liver into appropriate pieces, about the size of cutlets, and rinse these slices in several batches of cold water.
3. Beat the slices lightly on both sides with a meat mallet, sprinkle with salt, pepper and flour, and place in a pan with browned butter, turning and frying until cooked through.
4. Remove from the pan, and in the remaining butter, brown some chopped onions, then pour the sauce over the liver.
5. If necessary, thin the sauce with stock.
6. Instead of beating the liver, it can be hacked slightly with the back of a knife.
7. The liver gains in flavor when some slices of smoked bacon are fried alongside it.
8. Clean the heart and cut into slices like the liver, then fry it with the liver.
