## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- ¾ pound sugar
- 6 kvint isinglass
- 1 vanilla stick
- water
- jam (strawberry, raspberry or similar)
- oblong sugar breads

## Directions

1. Whip 1 pot sweet cream to stiff peaks.
2. Boil ¾ pound sugar, 6 kvint isinglass and 1 vanilla stick with a little water until all is dissolved.
3. When nearly cold, gently stir it into the whipped cream.
4. Decorate the bottom of the mold with jam (strawberry, raspberry or similar) and place oblong sugar breads around the inside of the mold.
5. Fill with the blancmange.
6. Let stand for a few hours in a cold place, turn out and serve with cold wine sauce.
