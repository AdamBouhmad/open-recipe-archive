## Ingredients

- 1/2 calf's kidneys
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Flour
- 4 L (4 Pots) brown spare soup
- 125 Gr (1/4 Q) Macaroni
- Water

## Directions

1. Rinse the kidneys well and cut them into small cubes
2. Brown and steam them partially tender in 125 Gr (1/4 S) butter with a little salt and pepper
3. Brown 125 Gr (1/4 S) butter and 125 Gr (1/4 S) flour and dilute with 4 L (4 Pots) brown spare soup
4. When it boils, add the kidneys together with their juices and bring to a boil
5. Cook 125 Gr (1/4 Q) macaroni in salted water, cut into small rings and place in the tureen
6. Serve with salt sticks
