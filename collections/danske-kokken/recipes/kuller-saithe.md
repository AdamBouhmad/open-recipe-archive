## Ingredients

- Saithe
- Water
- Salt
- Whole peppercorns
- Liver and roe
- Mustard sauce or sour butter sauce

## Directions

1. Scrape and clean the fish, cut into pieces
2. Cook the liver and roe with the fish
3. Place over heat in boiling water with a little salt and whole peppercorns
4. Cook over brisk heat
5. Serve with mustard sauce or sour butter sauce
