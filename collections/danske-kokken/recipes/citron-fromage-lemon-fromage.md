## Ingredients

- 1 pound powdered icing sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- juice of 6 lemons
- zest of 1 lemon
- 12 quintals isinglass
- 2 pints boiling water
- 20 beaten egg whites
- fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 pound powdered icing sugar with 20 egg yolks, juice of 6 lemons, zest of 1 lemon, and 12 quintals isinglass which has first been dissolved in 2 pints boiling water.
2. Bring the mixture to a boil.
3. Fold in the 20 beaten egg whites.
4. Serve cold in glass bowls with fruit sauce.
