## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 0.5 cup white sugar
- Juice of one large or two small lemons
- Grated peel of one lemon
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks white with sugar
2. Stir in lemon juice
3. Stir in grated lemon peel
4. Fold in beaten egg whites, stirring strongly to blend well with batter
5. Bake for half an hour
6. Serves 3 to 4 persons
