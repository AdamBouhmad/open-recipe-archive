## Ingredients

- White roots (turnips)
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Parsley
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Roots are not considered a particularly healthy food and can often have a bitter taste, so as soon as they come to a boil, discard the first water
2. Peel and cut them into smaller pieces and cook tender in salted water
3. Stew them like potatoes in butter, flour, and milk
4. Prepare the sauce with chopped parsley, sugar, and salt
5. Drain the roots well on a colander before placing them in the stew
