## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg ripe red tomatoes
- 625 gr sugar
- 2 dl water per 2 kg tomatoes
- 2.25 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice

## Directions

1. Wipe the ripe red tomatoes and place over heat with 0.5 l water per 2 kg tomatoes.
2. Cook until they can be easily passed through a sieve.
