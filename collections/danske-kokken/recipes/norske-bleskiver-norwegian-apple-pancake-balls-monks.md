## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 tb) fine flour
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) melted margarine
- 1 L (1 Pot) sweet milk
- 8 egg yolks
- cardamom
- grated lemon zest
- sugar

## Directions

1. In fine flour, stir 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) melted margarine, 1 L (1 Pot) sweet milk, 8 egg yolks, cardamom, grated lemon zest and sugar to taste.
2. Beat the egg whites and add them last.
3. Bake as soon as the dough is mixed and can, as previously mentioned, be filled with apples, or the fruit is served separately.
