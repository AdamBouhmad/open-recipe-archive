## Ingredients

- 1 pound anchovies
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- ½ pound washed butter

## Directions

1. Remove the spines from 1 pound anchovies and weigh the meat
2. Mince the meat finely and mix with 3 hard-boiled egg yolks and ½ pound washed butter
3. When well combined, spread on toasted French bread
