## Ingredients

- Veal or lamb meat, preferably from the breast
- Eggs
- Bread
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Dip veal and lamb meat first in beaten eggs, then in breadcrumbs mixed with salt, pepper and parsley, and fry quickly in a pan.
2. Alternatively, instead of eggs you can dip the meat in the butter and water melted on the pan, sprinkle it with bread and fry it.
3. Serve with vegetables.
