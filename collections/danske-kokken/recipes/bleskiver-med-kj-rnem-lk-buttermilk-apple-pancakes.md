## Ingredients

- 5 Pints buttermilk
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pound wheat flour
- 2 Teaspoonfuls sodium bicarbonate

## Directions

1. Stir 4 eggs into 5 pints buttermilk.
2. Add 1 pound wheat flour and 2 teaspoonfuls sodium bicarbonate.
3. Bake and serve in the usual manner.
