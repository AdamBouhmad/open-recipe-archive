## Ingredients

- Fried eggs
- Butter
- Flour
- Beef broth
- White pepper
- Sherry
- Tomato puree

## Directions

1. Arrange the fried eggs on a warm plate
2. Pour over a tomato sauce made of butter and flour, thinned with beef broth
3. Add white pepper, a glass of sherry, and tomato puree to taste
