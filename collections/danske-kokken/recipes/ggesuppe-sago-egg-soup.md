## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound large sago grains
- cold water
- 2 quarts boiling water
- zest of half a lemon
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 3 eggs
- white sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 glass white wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grains in cold water and sprinkle into 2 quarts of boiling water
2. When it has boiled until the grains are swollen, add the zest of half a lemon and 1/2 cup of raisins
3. Stir 3 egg whites in the terrine with white sugar, then with the juice of 1 lemon and 1 glass of white wine
4. When the grains are clear and cooked, heat the soup to boiling over the eggs, stirring constantly and briskly in the terrine
5. It is a wholesome and good-tasting dish and is made in about half an hour's time
6. The wine can be omitted
