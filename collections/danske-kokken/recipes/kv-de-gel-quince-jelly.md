## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg quinces
- Approximately 1/2 liter water per 1/2 kg fruit (or enough to cover level with the fruit in the pot)
- 1/2 kg sugar (or corresponding weight to juice)
- 1 1/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Dry the quinces and quarter or divide into 4 to 6 pieces, cutting away the blossom end.
2. Place them in the pot and cover with approximately 1/2 liter water per 1/2 kg fruit or with enough so it stands level with the quinces in the pot.
3. They must be completely cooked through before they are poured into the cloth.
4. When the juice has run through the next day, cook as instructed in No. 93.
