## Ingredients

- Leeks
- Butter
- Flour
- Leek water or beef broth
- Eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the leeks and cut them into thumb-length pieces, which are cooked in salted water
2. Make a smooth sauce from butter and flour, diluted with leek water or beef broth
3. Thicken this sauce with 1 to 2 eggs depending on the size of the portions
4. Warm the cooked leeks in this sauce
5. This is a good vegetable to serve with boiled soup meat
