## Ingredients

- Eight egg yolks
- 5 to 6 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 L (1 Pot) sweet milk
- A few drops of vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Whisk together eight egg yolks white with 5 to 6 tablespoons powdered sugar
2. When 1 L (1 Pot) sweet milk boils, pour some of it into the eggs, which are poured back into the pot under vigorous whisking with a whisk
3. As soon as it comes to a boil, remove it quickly from heat and pour it out
4. Stir a few drops of vanilla essence into the cream, which must be stirred regularly until it is cold so it does not form a skin
