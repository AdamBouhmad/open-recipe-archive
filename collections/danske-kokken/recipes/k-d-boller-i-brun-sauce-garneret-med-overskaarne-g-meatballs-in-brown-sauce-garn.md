## Ingredients

- Sinews and trimmings from meat used for forcemeat
- salt
- water
- browned butter
- 1 large grated onion
- paprika
- flour
- stock from the meat
- wine
- food coloring
- tomato purée
- Maggi bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Boil the sinews and other trimmings from the meat in salted water
2. When strained and brought to a boil again, form oval-shaped balls with a spoon
3. To make good balls, ensure they do not boil longer than necessary, which depends on the size of the balls, as the forcemeat otherwise easily loses its juiciness and becomes hard and dry
4. Make the sauce from browned butter, add a large grated onion, paprika and flour; thin with the stock and add wine, food coloring and a little tomato purée
5. Pour the sauce over the balls
6. Since the sauce for the balls should be strong and piquant, use Maggi bouillon cubes to improve it
