## Ingredients

- 3 medium heads of white cabbage
- 2 l vinegar
- 0.5 kg salt
- 2 l boiling water
- 20 peeled shallots
- 75 grams East Indian curry
- 50 grams cayenne pepper
- 25 grams ground ginger
- powdered sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage heads into fine strips and place in cold salt brine consisting of 0.5 kg salt to 2 liters boiling water for 4 to 5 days
2. Drain the cabbage in a sieve and press out the brine well before placing the cabbage in the pot together with the vinegar
3. Add 20 peeled shallots, 75 grams East Indian curry, 50 grams cayenne pepper, 25 grams ground ginger and powdered sugar to taste
4. Cook all of this with regular stirring for 1.5 hours, then fill into jars and bind
5. This cabbage is very piquant; it is served with meat, similar to how pickled cucumbers and gherkins are otherwise used
