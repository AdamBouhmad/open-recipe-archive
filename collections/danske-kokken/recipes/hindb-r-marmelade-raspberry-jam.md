## Ingredients

- Ripe raspberries
- Powdered sugar (1/2 amount of berries)

## Directions

1. Press the ripe raspberries through a coarse sieve.
2. Put the pulp in a pot together with the sugar and cook down to an even mass.
3. Fill it hot into jars, cover with paper and tie up.
