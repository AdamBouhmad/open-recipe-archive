## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- Half a pound white sugar
- 1.5 pints cream
- 1/2 ounce gelatine
- Cold water
- 3 gills good, preferably white rum

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks white with half a pound white sugar
2. Boil 1.5 pints cream and pour over 1/2 ounce gelatine, first dipped in cold water
3. When the gelatine is dissolved, set it back over the fire until it steams
4. Remove from heat, add 3 gills good white rum, and stir until cold
5. Pour into a mold rinsed with rum
6. Serve with red sauce
