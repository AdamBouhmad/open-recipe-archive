## Ingredients

- 2 Pots lukewarm milk
- 1 Tablespoonful yeast
- 2 Pounds barley or wheat flour

## Directions

1. Spread the lukewarm milk with a tablespoonful of yeast in a bowl.
2. Pour in 2 pounds barley or wheat flour.
3. Work the dough well through until it forms bubbles.
4. Set the covered dough on a warm place to rise.
5. When it has risen to one and a half times its original size, it is ready for baking.
6. Bake the pancakes in browned fat on the pan over gentle heat and turn to make them sufficiently brown on both sides.
7. Grease the pan when you turn them or put a new pancake on.
8. Do not put too much dough on at once, as it rises during baking.
