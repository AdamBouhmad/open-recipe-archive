## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- fish forcemeat
- cooked lobster
- lobster butter

## Directions

1. Line a mold with puff pastry dough.
2. In the bottom place fish forcemeat with pieces of cooked lobster pressed into it.
3. Over this place forcemeat mixed with lobster butter.
