## Ingredients

- 5 pails sweet milk
- 20 quintals sweet almonds, blanched and finely ground
- 10 quintals bitter almonds, blanched and finely ground
- 30 quintals wheat starch
- 10 egg yolks
- 3/4 pound white sugar
- 1 tablespoon rum
- whipped egg whites

## Directions

1. Heat 5 pails sweet milk over the fire, and when the milk boils, add 20 quintals sweet and 10 quintals bitter blanched and finely ground almonds, which are half-cooked.
2. Mix 30 quintals wheat starch, 10 egg yolks, 3/4 pound white sugar, and 1 tablespoon rum in 1 pail cold sweet milk.
3. Stir this mixture into the boiling milk and let it cook for approximately 5 minutes.
4. Remove from heat, and when it stops boiling, stir in the whipped egg whites.
5. Serve the pudding cold with fruit sauce.
