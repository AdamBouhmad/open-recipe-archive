## Ingredients

- 300 grams rice
- 6 liters semi-skimmed milk
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rinse and scald the grains beforehand
2. Pour the grains into the boiling milk, stirring until it comes to a boil
3. Place a tight-fitting lid on the pot to prevent a skin from forming
4. Cook for 2 hours
5. Serve with sugar and cinnamon
