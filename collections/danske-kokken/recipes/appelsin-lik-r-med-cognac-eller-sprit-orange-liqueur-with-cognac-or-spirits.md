## Ingredients

- Thinly peeled yellow zest of 6 oranges
- 1 l (1 pot) cognac or wine spirits
- 1 kg (2 S) icing sugar
- 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water
- Vanilla essence to taste

## Directions

1. Pour 1 l (1 pot) cognac or wine spirits over the thinly peeled yellow zest of 6 oranges and let it stand for one month.
2. Boil 1 kg (2 S) icing sugar with 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water to make a syrup.
3. Blend the syrup with the strained orange essence and flavor to taste with vanilla essence.
4. Pour the liqueur into bottles, cork them, and seal with gypsum.
