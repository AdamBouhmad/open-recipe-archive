## Ingredients

- lobster or crayfish shells
- butter
- water
- flour
- bouillon
- salt
- lobster or crayfish meat

## Directions

1. After the lobsters or crayfish are cooked, pound the shells fine with butter, set to boil over steady heat and dilute with boiling water.
2. Let them boil until all the butter has risen to the surface, then skim off, strain and set to boil again with flour.
3. Dilute with bouillon and add a little salt.
4. Finally add the crayfish meat or lobster meat cut into suitable pieces.
