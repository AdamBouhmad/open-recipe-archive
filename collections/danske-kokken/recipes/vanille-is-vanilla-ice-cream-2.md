## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 4½ pounds powdered sugar
- 1 pot good, sweet cream
- 1 vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks white with 4½ pounds powdered sugar.
2. Boil 1 pot good, sweet cream and scrape the inside of a vanilla bean into it, while the outside is well boiled with it, but then taken out.
3. When the cream is boiled, take it off the heat and pour it little by little and with constant beating into the egg yolks.
4. Put the whole into the pot and bring to a boil with uninterrupted beating.
5. When the cream is ready, let it stand until cold, then pour it into the ice cream freezer to freeze.
