## Ingredients

- 250 grams margarine
- 2 eggs
- 375 grams powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- zest of 1 lemon
- 250 grams cleaned currants
- finely chopped candied orange peel to taste
- 250 milliliters semi-skimmed milk
- 500 grams cake flour

## Directions

1. Mix margarine well with eggs, powdered sugar, cinnamon, ground cloves, baking soda, and lemon zest
2. Add cleaned currants and finely chopped candied orange peel to taste
3. Stir in semi-skimmed milk and cake flour
4. Bake in a greased form at moderate heat
