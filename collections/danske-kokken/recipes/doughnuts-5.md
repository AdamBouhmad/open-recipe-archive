## Ingredients

- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 tablespoon soda
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 3 eggs
- salt
- cinnamon (if desired)
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups sour milk, 1 tablespoon soda, 2 cups sugar, 1 tablespoon melted butter, 3 eggs, salt, and cinnamon if desired with enough wheat flour to make a dough that can be rolled.
2. Bake in hot lard and dry afterwards.
