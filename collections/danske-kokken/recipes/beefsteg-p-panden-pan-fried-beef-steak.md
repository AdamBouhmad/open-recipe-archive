## Ingredients

- Porterhouse or sirloin beef
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Wheat flour
- Onion
- Bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Use Porterhouse or sirloin. Have the butcher cut it into thick or thin slices as you prefer.
2. Pound thick slices lightly on both sides, then sprinkle with salt and pepper and fry in brown butter.
3. When done, which varies according to taste, place on a platter.
4. Put a piece of butter on the pan, stir in 1 teaspoon wheat flour, some grated onion and a little ground pepper, then thin with boiling water.
5. If you have bouillon, make a better sauce as follows: When the meat is taken from the pan, add butter and stir well with a spoon; when it is brown, add grated onion and cook a little. Then stir in good bouillon and ground pepper.
