## Ingredients

- Peaches, nearly ripe
- One pound peaches
- One pound sugar
- One-quarter pint white wine
- Peach kernels, sliced

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches and remove the stones.
2. For each pound of peaches, take 1 pound sugar, dip in water and cook to a syrup.
3. When the syrup is ready, add one-quarter pint white wine for each pound of sugar.
4. Add the peaches a few at a time, bring to a boil, and carefully remove with a fish slice; place on a colander to drain.
5. When the syrup has drained, return it to the pot and cook again.
6. Skim well, then add the kernels, sliced into thin slices.
7. Place the peaches in glass jars and pour the syrup over them.
8. If the syrup becomes watery after a few days, cook it again and pour cold over the peaches.
