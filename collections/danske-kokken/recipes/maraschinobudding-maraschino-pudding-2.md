## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- half a pound white sugar
- 6 quintals isinglass
- 2 pints thick cream
- Maraschino

## Directions

1. Boil the sweet cream with half a pound white sugar and strain it.
2. Dissolve 6 quintals isinglass in boiling water and add to the cream.
3. Whip 2 pints thick cream to stiff peaks.
4. When the boiled cream is approximately cold, add enough Maraschino to give the cream a strong flavor of it.
5. Add the whipped cream.
6. Let the whole stand for a couple of hours in ice in a gelatin mold.
7. Turn it out and garnish with biscuits and preserved fruits.
