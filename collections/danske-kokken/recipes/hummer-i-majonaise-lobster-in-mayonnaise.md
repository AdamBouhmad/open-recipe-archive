## Ingredients

- Cold, cooked lobster tail and claws
- Mayonnaise sauce
- Green salad leaves

## Directions

1. Serve the tail and claws of cold, cooked lobster covered with proper mayonnaise sauce
2. Garnish the dish with fine, green salad leaves
