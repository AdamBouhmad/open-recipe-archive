## Ingredients

- Game birds
- Broth or aspic
- Isinglass (for the aspic)
- Fresh cabbage leaves
- Herb salad
- Apple compote or apple puree

## Directions

1. During roasting, baste the birds with broth or aspic.
2. For game birds, use only the breast, which is sliced and arranged in the dish.
3. Prepare the aspic with isinglass.
4. Cut fresh cabbage leaves in the shape of scallop shells and fill them with herb salad.
5. Arrange the shells around the edge of the dish.
6. Serve apple compote or apple puree with the game.
