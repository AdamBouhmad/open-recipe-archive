## Ingredients

- Quinces, well ripe
- Small pieces of cinnamon
- Cloves, peeled
- Two pounds quinces (prepared)
- One pint water
- Three pints vinegar
- One and one-third pound powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces, which must be well ripe, and cut into 4 to 6 pieces according to size.
2. Remove the hard matter and weigh the pieces; stud with small pieces of cinnamon and peeled cloves.
3. For 2 pounds quinces, take 1 pint water, 3 pints vinegar, and 1 and one-third pound good powdered sugar.
4. If the quinces are not completely covered, take a bit more of each ingredient.
5. Place everything together on heat until the quinces are tender; remove the quinces and place in a jar.
6. Cook the syrup until completely even, cool it, and pour over the quinces; tie the next day.
