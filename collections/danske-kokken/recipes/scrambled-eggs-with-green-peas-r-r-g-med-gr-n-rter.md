## Ingredients

- Cooked peas
- Eggs
- Parsley
- Butter

## Directions

1. Pour the cooked peas through a colander and when the water has drained, mix them into the beaten eggs.
2. The pea water can be used in meat soup or used in the scrambled eggs instead of milk or water.
3. Sprinkle the serving with chopped parsley.
