## Ingredients

- Cherries
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar or farina per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the berries, which are then crushed in a wooden or stone mortar; then press the juice through a fine sieve or pour through a jelly bag.
2. Measure the juice and boil with the sugar for 10 minutes.
3. Skim well during boiling, fill into dry bottles and tie immediately.
4. The refuse is boiled in water, and the juice is used in the household.
