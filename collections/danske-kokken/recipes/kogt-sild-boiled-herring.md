## Ingredients

- herring
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- laurel leaves
- whole pepper
- boiled potatoes
- parsley sauce or caper sauce or melted butter sauce with vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean herring by removing gills and entrails
2. Cut off head and some of tail fin
3. Scrape well to remove scales
4. Keep roe and milt in the herring
5. Rinse well and sprinkle with salt
6. Place over heat in boiling water with vinegar, salt, laurel leaves and whole pepper
7. Serve with boiled potatoes and parsley, caper, or melted butter sauce with vinegar
