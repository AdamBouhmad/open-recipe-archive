## Ingredients

- 10 white, fresh celery plants
- Salt
- Pepper
- Carrot (2 Ounces)
- Butter (2 Ounces)
- Flour (½ Ounce)
- Bouillon (1 Quart)

## Directions

1. Remove the green leaves from the stalks, cut the stalks to a length of 4 inches
2. Boil for 10 minutes in water, place in cold water, clean well, rinse in several changes of water, and set to drain
3. Tie the stalks together in groups of two or three, cook with ½ Pint bouillon, ½ Pint water, carrot, spices, and a little butter
4. Cook very gently for 2 hours covered
5. Drain and arrange
6. Make a roux with butter and flour, thin with the remaining bouillon, which is reduced to 3 spoonfuls, strain, and pour over the stalks
7. Serve as a vegetable or as an independent course
