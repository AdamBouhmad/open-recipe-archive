## Ingredients

- cherries
- white sugar
- water
- white wine
- lemon peel
- potato starch

## Directions

1. Treat as for plum compote
2. To make the sauce slightly smoother, a teaspoon of potato starch dissolved in water may be added, and the berries gently shaken with it
