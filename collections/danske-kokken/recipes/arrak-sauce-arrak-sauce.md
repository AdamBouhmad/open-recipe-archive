## Ingredients

- Half a liter (1/2 Pot) milk
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon arrowroot flour
- 5 tablespoons arrak

## Directions

1. Boil half a liter (1/2 Pot) milk and whisk it into 4 egg yolks, which are whisked white with 4 tablespoons powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon arrowroot flour
2. Place the sauce back over heat and blend with 5 tablespoons arrak and whisk well until it is smooth, but without boiling
