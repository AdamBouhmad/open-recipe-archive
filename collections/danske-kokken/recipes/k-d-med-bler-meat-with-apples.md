## Ingredients

- Sliced cooked or roasted meat
- Butter
- Apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Brown the sliced meat in butter in a pan
2. Arrange on a flat serving dish
3. Add more butter to the pan
4. Fry slices of peeled and cored apples
5. If the apples are very sour, sprinkle with a little sugar while frying
6. Place the apples in the center of the dish
7. Serve browned butter with the meat
