## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork
- 375 g fresh bacon
- ginger
- pepper
- salt
- 6-8 boiled potatoes, grated
- 2-3 eggs
- cream or good milk
- soup made from bones and trimmings

## Directions

1. Mince the meat very finely together with the bacon.
2. Season with ginger, pepper, and salt.
3. Knead the mixture by hand or with a spoon.
4. Add 6-8 grated boiled potatoes and 2-3 eggs.
5. Add enough cream or good milk to achieve a proper consistency for forming into a fillet shape.
6. Place in a roasting pan, give it an oblong shape, and roast in a hot oven for one hour.
7. Baste frequently with soup made from bones and trimmings.
8. Make sauce from browned butter and flour thinned with gravy.
9. Slice the roast and garnish with warm potato salad.
