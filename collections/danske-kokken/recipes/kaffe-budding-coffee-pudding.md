## Ingredients

- 3/4 L (3 Pægle) very strong coffee
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) vanilla chocolate
- 16 leaves isinglass
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) cream, whipped to stiff foam
- 1/4 L (3 Pægle) half-skimmed or sweet milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 150 Gr (30 Kvint) icing sugar
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour or powdered starch
- vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Dissolve the vanilla chocolate and 16 leaves of isinglass in the very strong coffee
2. Allow to cool and begin to set
3. Stir in 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) cream, whipped to stiff foam
4. Pour into a wetted and coated mold
5. The pudding can be prepared the day before
6. For the cream sauce: whip 5 egg yolks with 150 Gr (30 Kvint) icing sugar and 2 teaspoons wheat flour or powdered starch
7. Heat 1/4 L (3 Pægle) half-skimmed or sweet milk and whisk in the egg mixture
8. Keep on low heat and stir regularly until almost cold
9. Add vanilla essence
10. Serve the pudding with this cream sauce
