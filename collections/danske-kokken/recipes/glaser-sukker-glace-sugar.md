## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 3 ounces powdered and sifted white sugar
- juice of 1 lemon or half lemon
- a little rose water

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg white until it forms a foam
2. Stir in 3 ounces of very good, pounded and sifted white sugar
3. If the mixture does not become as thick as a thin porridge, add more sugar, as otherwise it will not be able to cover the cake properly
4. Stir in the juice of a lemon or half a lemon and a little rose water
5. To ensure the cake to be glazed is even, rub it with flour before coating it with the sugar
6. Place it in a lukewarm oven until the glaze is dry
7. The glace sugar can be given a red color with a little cherry juice or currant juice if desired
