## Ingredients

- Two finely chopped onions
- 125 grams butter
- 1/2 kg rice, blanched
- Good meat broth or water
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Truffle (optional)
- Tongue, boiled and sliced
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Brown the chopped onions in butter.
2. Add the blanched rice along with good meat broth or water flavored with Maggi Bouillon cubes.
3. While the rice cooks, continually add more broth so the rice does not cook dry and become mealy, but remains whole and tender.
4. Risotto can be arranged in a Bordure ring (see recipe 334), and a wreath of cooked sliced tongue can be placed around it, garnished with parsley.
5. The dish can be improved by preparing it with truffles; slice the truffles and give them only a brief cooking in the rice before serving.
6. If truffles are used, omit the onions.
