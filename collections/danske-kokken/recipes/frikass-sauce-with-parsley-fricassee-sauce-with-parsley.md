## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Stock in which the meat was cooked
- Egg yolks
- Cooked, sliced carrots
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Calculate at least 1 L (1 Pot) of sauce for 12 persons
2. Bake butter and flour and thin with the stock in which the meat was cooked, bind with 3 egg yolks
3. Pour the sauce over the cut-up meat for fricassee
4. Just before serving, add 2 tablespoons of finely chopped parsley and some cooked, sliced carrots to the sauce
