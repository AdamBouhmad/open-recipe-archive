## Ingredients

- ½ kg hominy grains
- approximately 5 L milk
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- beer, fruit juice and water, or red wine

## Directions

1. If cooking the grains in milk, soak them as described above, drain the water, and add the grains to the boiling milk; they require the same cooking time.
2. Season with salt.
3. Serve with butter, sugar, cinnamon and either beer, fruit juice and water or red wine.
4. The corn grains produce a beautiful white porridge.
