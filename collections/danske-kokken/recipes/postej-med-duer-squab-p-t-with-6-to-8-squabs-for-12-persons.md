## Ingredients

- Squabs, roasted beforehand
- Short pastry
- Farce of veal or pork
- Butter
- Flour
- Gravy from the squabs
- Pearl onions, pickled

## Directions

1. Line the mold with short pastry and veal or pork farce
2. Cut the roasted squabs into 4 pieces and remove the breastbone, or use the breast alone
3. Place the meat in the center of the mold
4. Pour a good brown sauce made from butter, flour, and squab gravy over the meat
5. Add pickled pearl onions to the sauce
6. Layer with farce and short pastry
7. Serve the remaining sauce with the pâté
