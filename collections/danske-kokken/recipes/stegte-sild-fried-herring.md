## Ingredients

- herring
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- Palmin
- browned butter
- vinegar
- lemon
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean herring
2. Make several cuts on the sides with a knife
3. Dry in fish cloth
4. Dip in flour, eggs and breadcrumbs
5. Fry in butter or Palmin
6. Serve with browned butter with vinegar or lemon and boiled potatoes
