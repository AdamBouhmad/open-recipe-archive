## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, about 2 pounds
- 2 quarts cold water
- salt and light seasoning, if the doctor permits

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken and remove all the fat; cut it open and clean carefully so as to avoid breaking the gall and entrails
2. Cut up the meat and break the bones into pieces
3. Place in a casserole with 2 quarts of cold water, bring slowly to a boil and skim until clear
4. Cover with a lid and simmer gently until the chicken has become a collapsed mass, about 2 to 4 hours
5. Strain through a flannel cloth until clear and return to the fire to boil gently until it has become a complete jelly
6. Test by placing a spoonful of jelly on a saucer; if it is stiff when cool, it is ready
7. Season lightly if the doctor permits and pour into cups; serve cold or slightly warmed
