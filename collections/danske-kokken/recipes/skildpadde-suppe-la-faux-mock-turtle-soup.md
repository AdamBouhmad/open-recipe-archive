## Ingredients

- 1 large calf's head
- Good brown stock
- Sago flour
- Wine to taste
- Fish balls
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the head stock and mix it with good brown stock, as the soup requires more liquid than a ragout.
2. Reduce the soup well.
3. Thicken with sago flour mixed in water.
4. Season with wine to taste.
5. Cut the head meat and tongue into dice and bring to a boil in the soup.
6. Serve with fish balls and hard-boiled eggs, either whole yolks or cut in pieces.
