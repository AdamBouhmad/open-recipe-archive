## Ingredients

- Eggs
- White sugar, one tablespoon per egg
- Wheat flour, half a tablespoon per egg
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Preserved fruit or jam

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks first
2. Beat with sugar
3. Add flour
4. Finally add beaten egg whites
5. Grease paper forms well with butter and sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Forms should be about three fingers high with two fingers of batter
7. If baking in oven and top heat is too strong, cover with paper to prevent over-browning
8. Serve cold, stacked with jam between layers
9. Can be decorated with whipped cream and jam on top
