## Ingredients

- 20 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- zest and juice of 2 lemons
- 20 egg whites, beaten
- 1 pound potato flour
- 16 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 pint good Arack or Rum
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Beat 20 egg yolks for 3 quarter-hours with 1 pound powdered sugar.
2. Add the grated zest and strained juice of 2 lemons.
3. Stir in the 20 beaten egg whites and finally 1 pound potato flour.
4. Bake 7 thin cakes as for layer cake in greased paper forms.
5. Make the cream as follows: beat 16 egg yolks well with 1 pound powdered sugar, and when well beaten, add 1 pint good Arack or Rum and the strained juice of 2 lemons.
6. Heat the cream over the fire and cook with constant stirring until the whole is well cooked.
7. Set aside to cool so that it becomes cold before spreading between the layers of the cake.
8. Stack the cakes on top of each other with cream between.
