## Ingredients

- Cauliflower heads
- Salt
- Butter
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. The heads must be white and firm
2. Clean well and remove all green leaves
3. Cut off the stalk close to the head without separating it
4. Place with the stalk facing up in a little water and sprinkle generously with salt so that insects and worms fall off into the water
5. Cook the head in water with salt until tender
6. Carefully remove and serve with melted butter in which an egg yolk has been beaten
