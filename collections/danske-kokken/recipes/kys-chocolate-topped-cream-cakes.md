## Ingredients

- Ingredients for water cakes (Nr. 1117)
- Vanilla cream (Nr. 1060)
- Chocolate glaze

## Directions

1. Bake as Water cakes (Nr. 1117), open and fill with Vanilla cream (Nr. 1060).
2. Brush the cakes with Chocolate glaze.
