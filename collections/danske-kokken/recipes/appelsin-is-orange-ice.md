## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (1 1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 1 L (1 pot) water
- Juice of 8 oranges
- Finely grated zest of 4 oranges
- Juice of 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook the powdered sugar with the water.
2. When it is cold, add the juice of 8 oranges and finely grated zest of 4 oranges, as well as the juice of 4 lemons.
3. Strain and pour into the freezing container.
