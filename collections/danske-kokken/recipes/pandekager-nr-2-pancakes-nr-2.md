## Ingredients

- 8 Egg Yolks
- 3 Tablespoons white Sugar
- 12 Tablespoons Flour
- 6 small Tablespoons melted Butter
- a little grated Lemon Peel
- crushed Cardamom
- 1.5 Pints Milk or thin Cream
- Egg Whites (whipped)

## Directions

1. Whip 8 Egg Yolks well with 3 Tablespoons white Sugar
2. Stir in 12 Tablespoons Flour, 6 small Tablespoons melted Butter, a little grated Lemon Peel, crushed Cardamom, 1.5 Pints Milk or thin Cream, and finally the whipped Egg Whites
