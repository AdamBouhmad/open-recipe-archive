## Ingredients

- Scrambled eggs
- Watercress
- Smoked salmon

## Directions

1. Arrange the scrambled eggs on a platter and sprinkle with watercress. Roll slices of smoked salmon together like snails and place them around the eggs. Place small bunches of watercress between them.
