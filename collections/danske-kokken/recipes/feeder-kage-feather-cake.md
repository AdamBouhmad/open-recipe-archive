## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- a couple of grains of salt

## Directions

1. Sift the powder as always with the flour
