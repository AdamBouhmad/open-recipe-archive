## Ingredients

- One large, fat calf heart (serves 2-3 people)
- Parsley
- Butter
- Water
- Coloring
- Salt
- Flour
- Maggi bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Open the heart by cutting it lengthwise and remove the blood vessels and tendons, then wash it thoroughly.
2. Stuff the heart like a chicken with parsley and butter.
3. Sew the heart together, brown it in butter, and add a little water along with coloring and salt.
4. Roast for about 3 hours.
5. Brown butter and flour together, then thin with the cooking liquid to make a sauce.
6. Strengthen the sauce with Maggi bouillon cubes if needed.
7. Cut the heart in half and cover with the sauce.
8. Serve with compote, currant jelly, salad, or browned potatoes.
