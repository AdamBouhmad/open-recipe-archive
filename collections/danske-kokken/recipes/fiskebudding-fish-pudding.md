## Ingredients

- 4 Pounds Fish, preferably Pike, scraped free of skin and bone
- Half a Pound cold Butter
- 1 Pound melted Butter
- 8 Eggs
- 4 Tablespoons Wheat Flour
- Salt and Pepper to taste
- About 2 Pots sweet Cream

## Directions

1. Scrape the fish free of skin and bone, then chop it with half a pound of cold butter.
2. Place the mixture in a mortar and pound it, adding the melted butter gradually during pounding until it becomes like a tough dough and releases from the mortar.
3. Add 8 eggs one at a time, stirring constantly.
4. Add 4 tablespoons of wheat flour, salt and pepper to taste, and enough sweet cream so that the mixture becomes appropriately thick.
5. Test the consistency by placing a spoonful in boiling water; if too thin, add more flour; if too firm, add more cream.
6. Grease a pudding mold with butter and fill it with the mixture.
7. Cook for a couple of hours.
8. Serve with Caper sauce or Crayfish sauce.
