## Ingredients

- 1/3 kg powdered sugar for 1/4 kg scraped carrots
- 4 lemons
- Peel of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots, rinse them, and cut into thin slices
2. Cook tender in very little water and press through a puree sieve or food mill
3. Cook sugar, juice of 4 lemons, and finely cut peel of 2 lemons together with the puree until the jam is sufficiently smooth
4. Fill into glasses, cover with paper, and seal with parchment paper
