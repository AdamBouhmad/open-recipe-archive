## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked, chopped salt cod
- 3 tablespoons cooked, grated potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- A little ground nutmeg
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 gill cream
- A couple spoonfuls chopped raisins
- A small teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix the salt cod and potatoes thoroughly with butter and nutmeg
2. Stir in 3 whole eggs, cream, and a couple spoonfuls of chopped raisins
3. Finally add a small teaspoon of baking powder
4. Pour the batter into a buttered earthenware dish and place immediately in the oven
5. Serve with stirred or melted butter
