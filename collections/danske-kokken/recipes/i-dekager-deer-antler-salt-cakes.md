## Ingredients

- ½ pound fine flour
- 2 pounds butter
- 4 pounds sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons deer antler salt dissolved in a little cold water
- egg yolk for brushing
- chopped almonds
- coarse powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Knead together ½ pound fine flour, 2 pounds butter, 4 pounds sugar, 3 eggs, and 2 tablespoons deer antler salt dissolved in a little cold water
2. Roll out on the baking board
3. Cut into small round cakes using a glass
4. Brush with egg yolk
5. Sprinkle with chopped almonds mixed with coarse powdered sugar
6. Bake at good heat
