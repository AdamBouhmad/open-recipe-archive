## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. After rinsing well, boil the tongue in salted water
2. Skim the stock frequently
3. When the tongue is fully cooked, remove it and set aside to cool
4. Strain the stock, and remove the skin from the tongue and cut away the lower part
5. Return to heat and bring to a boil once more
6. Serve either whole or sliced with sharp brown sauce
7. Salted beef tongue is treated similarly but served with cooked vegetables without sauce
