## Ingredients

- old Wine mixed with Water and Sugar
- 1 Pint
- Juice and grated Shell of half a Lemon
- 1 Teaspoon Corn Starch
- 3 well-beaten Egg yolks
- cold Wine

## Directions

1. For this take old Wine mixed with Water and Sugar.
2. For 1 Pint of this use Juice and grated Shell of half a Lemon; thicken it with 1 Teaspoon Corn Starch, and when it is to be served, add 3 well-beaten Egg yolks, thinned with a little cold Wine.
3. It is used warm, preferably for Bread Pudding and Potato Pudding, but can also be used for others, if you wish.
