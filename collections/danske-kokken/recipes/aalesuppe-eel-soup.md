## Ingredients

- 3 til 4 Pund mellemstore Aal (3 to 4 pounds of medium-sized eels)
- Salt
- Vand (water)
- 5 Potter tynd Kjødsuppe (5 pots of thin meat broth)
- Rene, ituskaarne Supperødder (clean, cut-up soup vegetables)
- En halv Snes Peberkorn (half a dozen peppercorns)
- Et Laurbærblad (a bay leaf)
- Skrællede, ituskaarne Æblestykker (peeled, cut-up apple pieces)
- Smaa Kjødboller (small meatballs), optional
- Sukker (sugar)
- Eddike (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Skin the eels, clean them, remove the head and cut off the fins.
2. Cut the eels into pieces about one inch long and sprinkle with salt.
3. Place the eel pieces in a little salted water, bring to a boil, skim well, and drain in a colander.
4. Heat 5 pots of thin meat broth with clean cut-up soup vegetables, half a dozen peppercorns, and a bay leaf over gentle heat.
5. When the vegetables are half tender, add peeled, cut-up apple pieces and, if desired, small meatballs.
6. When these are almost tender, add the eel pieces and bring to a boil until completely tender.
7. Remove from heat and season with salt, sugar, and vinegar to taste.
8. Note: the soup will become discolored if vinegar is added while it is boiling.
