## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- ½ pound finely ground powdered sugar
- ½ stick vanilla, ground
- 1 pound potato flour
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks carefully with ½ pound finely ground powdered sugar and half a stick ground vanilla
2. When mixed, add 1 pound potato flour
3. Finally add the beaten egg whites
4. Bake in even oven heat
