## Ingredients

- Small French bread rolls, crust scraped off
- Stewed sweetbreads
- Egg white
- Milk
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or Palmin
- Sauce from the stewing of sweetbreads
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut a lid from small French bread rolls from which the crust has been scraped, and hollow them out.
2. Fill with stewed sweetbreads while they are still hot.
3. Glue the lid on with egg white.
4. Dip the breads in milk and the scraped bread crumbs.
5. Fry in butter or Palmin.
6. Serve with sauce made at the same time as the sauce for the stewing, thinned and bound with a couple of egg yolks.
