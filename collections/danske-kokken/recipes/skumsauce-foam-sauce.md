## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 11 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons powdered icing sugar
- 1/4 glass old white wine
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Whisk together 3 whole eggs and 11 egg yolks in a saucepan with 2 tablespoons powdered icing sugar.
2. Gradually add 1/4 glass old white wine and 1 glass water.
3. Set the saucepan over heat and continue whisking the sauce until smooth.
4. Remove from heat and keep in a warm place until needed.
