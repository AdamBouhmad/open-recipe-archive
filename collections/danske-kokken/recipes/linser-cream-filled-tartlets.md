## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) Pillsbury flour
- 375 Gr (3/4 S) Margarine
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Powdered sugar (Melis)
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 egg yolks for filling cream
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar (Melis)
- 1 tablespoon flour (Flormel)
- Vanilla essence
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) half-cream

## Directions

1. Knead together the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, and 4 egg yolks until a dough forms; let it rest for half an hour.
2. Roll out thinly and line small buttered baking forms.
3. Place 1 teaspoon cream in each form; arrange these closely together on the kitchen counter and cover with a large sheet of dough, thinly rolled out.
4. Trim the edges of the forms and place in the oven.
5. Bake for approximately half an hour. They can be prepared early in the day and baked later.
6. For the filling cream: beat 3 egg yolks with 3 tablespoons powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, and vanilla essence. When 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) half-cream is added, continue as directed.
7. This portion yields 40 cakes.
