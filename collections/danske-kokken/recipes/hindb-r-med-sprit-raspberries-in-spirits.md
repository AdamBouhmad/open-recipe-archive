## Ingredients

- 1/4 dl spirits of wine
- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg raspberries

## Directions

1. Remove stems from the berries.
2. Layer them in glasses or jars alternately with the sugar.
3. Pour the specified amount of spirits over them and cover well.
4. For the next two days, gently turn the fruit around with a spoon, and tie up the glasses.
