## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 lb) Pillsbury flour
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) margarine
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon hartshorn salt
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, hartshorn salt, and eggs together
2. Form into various small shapes, not placed too close on the baking sheet
3. Bake. Note: Vrøvl can be baked from baking flour made for vanilla or almond wreaths. Even better is half almond and half vanilla baking flour, baked in wreaths
