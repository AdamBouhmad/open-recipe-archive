## Ingredients

- 3 pounds lean veal, cut into square pieces
- 1 pound ham
- Roots
- Mixed vegetables, cut into pieces
- Spices
- Parsley
- 1 pint Madeira
- Turtle meat
- Turtle fat, cut into cubes
- Cayenne pepper
- 1 glass spice extract
- Lemon slices
- Lemon slices for serving
- Fish balls
- Egg balls

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into square pieces. Bring to a boil, then move over a gentler fire. Add 1 pint Madeira and continue cooking for 1 hour until the soup is clear and light yellow in color.
2. Skim the soup and strain it through a sieve into another casserole.
3. Blend it with the necessary amount of turtle meat and the lungs, cut into cubes.
4. Allow it to boil for 12 to 15 minutes, then add a little Cayenne pepper, a glass of spice extract and some of the turtle fat, cut into cubes.
5. Pour the soup into the tureen and serve with lemon slices.
6. Prepare the spice extract as follows: In a casserole, place 1 part dried thyme, the same amount of basil and of marjoram. Pour over a good half pint of boiling Madeira. Boil for 5 minutes, then strain through a sieve into the soup.
7. Garnish with fish balls and egg balls.
