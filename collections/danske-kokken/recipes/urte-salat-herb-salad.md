## Ingredients

- lost mayonnaise
- Russian mustard
- beet vinegar
- cooked finely chopped celery
- cooked beets
- watercress

## Directions

1. Add Russian mustard and beet vinegar to lost mayonnaise
2. Add equal parts of cooked finely chopped celery and beets
3. Garnish the salad with watercress
4. It can be served as a vegetable side dish with meat
