## Ingredients

- pumpkin, cut up
- thin meat broth or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
- butter
- flour
- blanched grated horseradish
- vinegar or lemon juice
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the cut-up pumpkin in thin meat broth or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
2. Use this broth for the sauce
3. Make a white sauce as usual for boiled meat
4. Bring a little blanched grated horseradish to a boil in the sauce
5. Add grated horseradish, vinegar or lemon juice, sugar, and salt
6. Warm the pumpkin in the sauce
7. Serve as a vegetable side dish with boiled meat
