## Ingredients

- 62 quintal flour
- 36 quintal light powdered sugar
- 37 quintal butter
- a handful of scalded, finely chopped almonds
- 1 egg
- whipped cream with vanilla sugar
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, sugar, butter, almonds, and egg until the dough releases from the dish; then press the dough into small shell molds that have been buttered beforehand. Bake them in a moderately warm oven and serve cold, filled with whipped cream with vanilla sugar and a little currant jelly on top.
