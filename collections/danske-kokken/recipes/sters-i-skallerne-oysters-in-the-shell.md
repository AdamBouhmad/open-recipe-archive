## Ingredients

- Oysters in shells
- Hardtack

## Directions

1. Wash the shells and bake in a hot oven; then open the shells with an oyster knife, being careful not to spill any of the juice, and arrange them quickly on warm plates with small hardtack.
2. Oysters can in the same way be baked by steam in the shells.
3. If you want to preserve oysters in the shell, they should be placed in a cool cellar, in upright ascending piles, and from time to time sprinkled with cornmeal and sprinkled with salt water.
4. If you also ensure that they do not freeze, they can keep for a long time.
