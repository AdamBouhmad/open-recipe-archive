## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoons Flour
- 1 Teacup Butter
- 1 Pint boiling Milk
- 3 Celery heads
- Water

## Directions

1. Mix 2 Tablespoons Flour with 1 Teacup Butter.
2. Have 1 Pint boiling Milk ready.
3. Stir the Flour and Butter into the Milk.
4. Boil 3 Celery heads cut into small pieces in Water, strain off the Water.
5. Put the Celery into the Milk and set it over the Fire for 5 to 10 Minutes, stirring constantly.
