## Ingredients

- 1 pound of raspberries
- 3/4 pound of powdered sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place the sugar over the fire with a little of the juice from the berries
2. When the sugar is melted, pour in the berries
3. Cook for nearly three-quarters of an hour, then pour the berries onto a colander and cook the juice for 1 hour with a little cinnamon
4. Then return the berries together with the kernels, but pour it out immediately before it begins to boil
5. Distribute the marmalade into pots in the usual manner
