## Ingredients

- 9 egg yolks
- 1/2 Kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon hartshorn salt
- either lemon, almond, or vanilla essence
- 1/2 Kg (1 lb) flour
- the beaten egg whites
- blanched almonds, split

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks well with the powdered sugar and hartshorn salt
2. Add either lemon, almond, or vanilla essence, as well as the flour and finally the beaten egg whites
3. Place the cakes on buttered baking sheets with a teaspoon, with generous spacing between them
4. Top each with a split almond
5. Bake very light
