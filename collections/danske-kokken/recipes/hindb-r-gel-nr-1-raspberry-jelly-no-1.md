## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg raspberries (wild or garden)
- 1/2 kg sugar (or corresponding weight to juice)
- 1 1/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Both wild and garden raspberries may be used.
2. Gently crush the berries with a spoon and pour into the jelly cloth.
3. When the juice has run through, weigh it, and cook a corresponding weight of sugar with water as described in No. 93.
4. Cook the juice for about fifteen minutes with the sugar and it must be well skimmed during this time.
5. Pour into the cups, which are tied up when the jelly begins to set.
6. The pulp may be cooked with sugar and made into marmalade.
