## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cherries
- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Either pit the cherries or preserve them as whole berries, of which only a little of the stems are cut off.
2. Boil sugar and water together to a thin syrup, add the cherries, boil and skim for 6 to 8 minutes, then remove them and place on a colander.
3. Pour the cherries into a jar after they have drained well, and set the juice over the fire to reduce until it is very thick, then pour it boiling over the fruit, which should be sealed as soon as possible.
4. A vanilla bean can be boiled in the juice to taste if desired.
