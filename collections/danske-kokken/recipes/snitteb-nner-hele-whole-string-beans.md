## Ingredients

- String beans
- Salt

## Directions

1. After drying, remove the seams from the back and front of the beans.
2. Pack the beans tightly on top of each other in a completely clean and dry bucket or in a stone jar, covered with a cloth.
3. Place a perforated bottom on top with a press.
4. Store in a dry place.
5. Cut the beans as needed for use.
6. Princesse beans are preserved in the same manner.
