## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Gravy from veal roast
- Wine
- Mushrooms
- Veal roast, carved
- Slices of cooked or roasted sweetbreads
- Fish balls
- Puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Make a brown gravy sauce from butter, flour, and the gravy from the veal roast
2. Season with wine and mushrooms
3. Heat the carved meat in the sauce together with slices of cooked or roasted sweetbreads and fish balls
4. Garnish the ragout with puff pastry strips
