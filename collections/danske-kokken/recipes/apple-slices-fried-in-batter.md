## Ingredients

- 1 heaping cup flour
- 2 large spoonfuls butter, clarified
- 1 gill ale
- as much milk
- 4 eggs, whites whipped and stirred in firmly
- apples

## Directions

1. Mix 1 heaping cup flour with 2 large spoonfuls clarified butter, 1 gill ale, and as much milk
2. Whip the egg whites and stir them in firmly
3. Peel and core the apples, cut them into slices
4. Give them a quick boiling in sugar and water
5. Dip each slice in the batter and cook
