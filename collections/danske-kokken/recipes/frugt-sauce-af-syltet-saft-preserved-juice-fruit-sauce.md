## Ingredients

- Any kind of preserved juice
- Water
- Sago or potato starch
- Sugar (if needed)
- Sliced or chopped almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Dilute the preserved juice with water.
2. When it boils, smooth it with mixed sago or potato starch.
3. Add sugar if needed.
4. Sliced or chopped almonds may be added to the fruit sauce.
5. Serve cold or warm.
