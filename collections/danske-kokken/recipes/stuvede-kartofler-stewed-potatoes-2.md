## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Milk or broth
- Cooked potatoes
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Salt

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour and milk or bouillon; when this has boiled until smooth, give the cooked and peeled potatoes a boiling in it together with nutmeg, pepper and parsley.
2. Add salt at the end.
3. Alternatively, peeled potatoes can be placed on the fire with milk or thin soup and cooked until the sauce becomes smooth, then stir in butter, seasonings and salt.
