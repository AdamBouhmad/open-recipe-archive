## Ingredients

- 1 Kg (2 lb) good, not too sweet apples
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) Melis sugar
- 1/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægel) water
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut in half, and remove the cores
2. Rinse in water with a little vinegar
3. Make a syrup from 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) Melis sugar and 1/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægel) water
4. Place the apples in the syrup
5. Watch carefully during cooking, remove each piece as it becomes cooked, and place in the serving bowl
6. If the juice is not sufficiently smooth, boil it a bit longer and strain before pouring over the apples
7. Just before serving, the hollow where the core was removed can be filled with a little currant, gooseberry, or apple jelly, but the fruit pieces must not lie on top of each other
