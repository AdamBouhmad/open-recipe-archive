## Ingredients

- ½ pound butter (softened first)
- ½ pound sugar
- 12 eggs
- 20 drops vanilla
- 1 pound flour

## Directions

1. Line the pan with buttered paper
2. Put ½ pound softened butter, ½ pound sugar and 12 eggs in a bowl and beat until cream
3. Add 20 drops vanilla
4. Finally fold in 1 pound flour, sifted as usual
5. Bake in a moderately warm oven in half an hour
