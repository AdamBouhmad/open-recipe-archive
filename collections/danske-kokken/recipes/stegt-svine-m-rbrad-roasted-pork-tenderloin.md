## Ingredients

- pork tenderloin
- salt
- ground pepper
- meat drippings
- wine
- boiled peeled potatoes
- stewed vegetables

## Directions

1. Roast the tenderloin whole; cut away the fat and membrane.
2. Sprinkle with salt mixed with a little ground pepper.
3. Roast finished under constant basting with drippings.
4. Serve with the drippings in which it was roasted, to which a little wine may be added, along with boiled, peeled potatoes and stewed vegetables.
