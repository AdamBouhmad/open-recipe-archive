## Ingredients

- 3 hens or young roosters
- 2½ kg (5 lbs) veal shank
- 1 celery
- 4 carrots
- 1 good bunch of herbs

## Directions

1. Place the hens and veal shank in cold water with salt, equal in amount to the poultry.
2. Once the scum has been removed, add 1 celery, 4 carrots, and a good bunch of herbs.
3. Cook until the hens are tender.
4. Strain the soup through a fine sieve, leaving only a little liquid in the pot to keep the hens steaming hot until serving.
5. Skim the fat well from the soup, and only when it has been returned to the fire and comes to a boil again, pour it into the tureen.
6. Serve with small cut filets of meat, meatballs, vegetables, and similar garnishes.
