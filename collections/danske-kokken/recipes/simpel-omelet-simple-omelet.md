## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- A pinch of pepper
- A piece of butter the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Put egg yolks in one dish, egg whites in another
2. Add salt and pepper
3. Beat for half a minute
4. Heat butter in a clean omelet pan or frying pan over heat
5. Beat egg whites until stiff foam
6. Gently fold into yolks
7. Pour into pan
8. Stir with a fork along the bottom so omelet gathers in the center
9. When cooked, slide from one side of pan onto a warm plate
10. Serve immediately
11. An omelet of 3 eggs serves 2 persons; if more is wanted, make another omelet of 3 eggs
