## Ingredients

- 4 well-beaten egg yolks
- 25 quint butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- cold water
- boiling water
- 1 slice lemon
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Set a pudding mold in a pot of boiling water.
2. Add 4 well-beaten egg yolks to the mold.
3. Stir in 25 quint butter that has been stirred to a paste, and 1 tablespoon flour.
4. Pour in several tablespoons of cold water and then slowly boiling water with constant stirring, so the sauce bubbles up somewhat.
5. Add a slice of lemon, 2 tablespoons vinegar and a little sugar.
6. Keep the pot over heat and stir the sauce until smooth, but do not allow it to boil.
