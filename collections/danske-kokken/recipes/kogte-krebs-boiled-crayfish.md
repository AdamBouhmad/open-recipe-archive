## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the crayfish and cook them like lobster in boiling salted water.
2. If cooking a large quantity at once, heat an iron implement (such as fire tongs) until red-hot and stir it in the boiling water as soon as the crayfish are added, to prevent the temperature from dropping too much.
