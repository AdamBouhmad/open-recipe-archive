## Ingredients

- 12 egg whites
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 pail good cream
- a little cardamom
- 3 quarter-pounds powdered sugar
- 1 pound flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg whites into a foam
2. Add the egg yolks together with the cream, a little cardamom, powdered sugar, and flour
3. Bring butter to boiling in a small saucepan
4. Pour a spoonful of the batter through a funnel held high over the butter, moving it back and forth in circles
5. Fry the cake and when golden brown, turn it carefully
6. Remove and drain the butter off
7. Arrange on a plate
8. Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving
