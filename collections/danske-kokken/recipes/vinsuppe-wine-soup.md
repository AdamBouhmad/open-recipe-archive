## Ingredients

- 3 pints French white wine
- 4.5 pints water
- a couple of lemon slices
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 4 egg yolks
- 6 spoonfuls pounded powdered sugar
- small rusks

## Directions

1. Cook 3 pints of French white wine and 4.5 pints of water with a couple of lemon slices (with pits removed) and a stick of cinnamon.
2. Beat 4 egg yolks with 6 spoonfuls of pounded powdered sugar until they are white.
3. While stirring constantly, add a few spoonfuls of the soup to the eggs, and continue this until all the soup is whisked together with the eggs in a tureen.
4. Serve small rusks with it.
5. The soup must be served as soon as it is ready.
