## Ingredients

- 15 to 20 grams tartaric acid
- 2 liters (2 pints) cold water
- 7.5 kg (15 pounds) berries
- 1/2 kg (1 pound) powdered sugar per liter of juice

## Directions

1. Dissolve the tartaric acid in the cold water
2. Pour over the fruit, which stands undisturbed for 24 hours
3. Pour through a jelly cloth
4. Measure the juice that runs through
5. For each liter of juice, take 1/2 kg powdered sugar, which is stirred into the cold juice until completely dissolved
6. Pour the juice immediately into bottles, cork and seal with plaster
