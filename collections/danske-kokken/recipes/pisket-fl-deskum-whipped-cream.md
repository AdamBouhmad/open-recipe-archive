## Ingredients

- thick, sweet cream
- white sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Only thick, sweet cream can be whipped into foam. The cream must be well chilled and held in a spacious container, and the whipping must take place in a cool room.
2. If you cannot get the cream to foam, try adding a few drops of lemon juice or vinegar.
3. White sugar and vanilla are whipped in according to taste.
