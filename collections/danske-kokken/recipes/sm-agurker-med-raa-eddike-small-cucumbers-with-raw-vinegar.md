## Ingredients

- Small cucumbers
- Strong salt brine
- White pepper
- Whole nutmeg
- Bay leaves
- Raw vinegar

## Directions

1. The cucumbers must be very small
2. Dry them and place in strong brine for 24 hours
3. Dry them again and place in a crock
4. For 100 small cucumbers, take 1 spoonful white pepper, a little whole nutmeg and 10 bay leaves
5. Cover everything with enough raw vinegar that it stands well over them
6. Tie them up immediately and they will keep well, though they will not be very green in appearance
