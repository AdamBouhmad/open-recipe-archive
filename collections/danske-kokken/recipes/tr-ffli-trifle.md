## Ingredients

- 5 pounds small macaroons
- 1 pint Madeira
- jam or preserves
- 1 pot cream
- 10 egg yolks
- 3 quintals isinglass
- half a pint water
- half a pound white sugar
- vanilla
- 1 pint cream
- colored sugar crystals

## Directions

1. Lay 5 pounds small macaroons in a bowl, pour over 1 pint Madeira and let stand covered for a couple of hours until they are thoroughly soaked but not dissolved.
2. Place them in a glass bowl and cover with a layer of jam.
3. Pour over a cream made of 1 pot cream, 10 egg yolks, 3 quintals isinglass dissolved in half a pint water, half a pound white sugar, and vanilla prepared as directed in No. 272.
4. Whip 1 pint cream to stiff peaks, spread over the whole, and sprinkle with colored sugar crystals.
