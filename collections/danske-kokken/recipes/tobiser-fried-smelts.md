## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- Palmin (cooking fat)
- parsley
- lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and sprinkle with a small amount of salt and let it stand briefly.
2. Dry the smelts, dip them in flour, then in egg and breadcrumbs, and fry in Palmin.
3. Arrange them garnished with parsley and lemon.
4. Place a dish of soft butter in the center of the platter, which can be either Tartar or maître d'hôtel butter.
5. Alternatively use browned butter and boiled potatoes.
