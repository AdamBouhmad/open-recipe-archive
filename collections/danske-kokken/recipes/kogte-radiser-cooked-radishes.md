## Ingredients

- Long English radishes
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the radishes, and remove the outermost coarse leaves
2. Tie them in bundles like sweet asparagus and place in boiling salted water
3. They require only a few minutes of cooking
4. Arrange on a napkin with the tops toward the center of the platter
5. Since these lose their fresh appearance during cooking, garnish the dish with a bouquet of parsley
6. Serve creamed butter with this
