## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the asparagus into small pieces, and sort the tips separately and cook them a few minutes later in the water, as they otherwise boil away easily.
2. Blanch the asparagus in boiling salted water, and when cooked, place them on a colander so the water can drain off well so it does not thin the sauce.
3. Brown the butter and flour together and add milk to make a white, smooth sauce, which is seasoned with a little sugar and salt.
4. Toss the asparagus in the warm sauce.
5. The water in which they were cooked can be used in soup and will have good effect.
