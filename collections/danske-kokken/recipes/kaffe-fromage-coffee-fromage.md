## Ingredients

- 1/4 L (1 pægl) very strong coffee
- 12 sheets isinglass
- 125 gr (1/8 S) powdered sugar
- 3/4 L (3 pægle) cream
- whipped cream for decoration

## Directions

1. Melt isinglass with powdered sugar in very strong coffee
2. When cooled, stir in whipped cream
3. Decorate with whipped cream
