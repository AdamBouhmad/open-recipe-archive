## Ingredients

- ½ pound sweet almonds
- 6 kvint bitter almonds
- 36 kvint powdered sugar
- a couple of tablespoons water
- 1 pægle melted butter
- ¾ pægle finely ground wheat bread
- 8 beaten eggs
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scald the sweet and bitter almonds, remove the skins and pound them fine in a mortar.
2. When nearly fine, add 36 kvint powdered sugar and a couple of tablespoons water, and pound again until the whole becomes like a stiff dough.
3. Take it out and stir in 1 pægle melted butter, ¾ pægle finely ground wheat bread and 8 beaten eggs.
4. Beat 5 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into the mixture.
5. Butter a mold and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
6. Pour the mixture in and place the mold in boiling water.
7. Cook the pudding for 2 hours.
8. Turn out onto a platter and serve with powdered sugar.
