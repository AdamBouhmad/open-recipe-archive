## Ingredients

- Small chestnut potatoes
- Powdered sugar
- Water
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Boil the small potatoes and peel them while hot.
2. Brown a good spoonful of powdered sugar in a frying pan.
3. As soon as it is brown but not burnt, add a couple of spoonfuls of water and a small piece of butter or margarine and let it boil together with the sugar.
4. Pour in the potatoes and turn them carefully.
5. Let them boil in the sugar until they acquire a beautiful brown color.
6. For a more attractive result, use small chestnut potatoes rather than large mealy ones cut into pieces.
