## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- butter (equal weight to eggs)
- powdered sugar very finely ground (equal weight to eggs)
- flour (equal weight to eggs)

## Directions

1. Weigh the eggs and take the same weight of each of the following: butter, very finely powdered sugar, and flour
2. Mix the egg yolks and sugar together
