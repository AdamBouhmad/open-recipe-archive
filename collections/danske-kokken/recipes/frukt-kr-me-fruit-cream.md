## Ingredients

- apple sauce or juice of cherries, currants, raspberries, or strawberries and raspberries
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon juice
- wine
- sago starch or potato starch
- whipped cream
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Can be made from apple sauce or from juice of cherries, currants, raspberries, or strawberries and raspberries, which are cooked and strained.
2. The juice is cooked with sugar according to taste, a little cinnamon and lemon juice, and wine.
3. When the juice boils, thicken it by stirring in some sago starch or potato starch, which has first been dissolved in cold juice, such that it does not lump.
4. The cream is covered with whipped cream and over it powdered sugar, and it is served with cream.
