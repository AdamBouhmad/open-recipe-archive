## Ingredients

- Striped, slightly salted pork
- Egg
- Bread
- Clarified butter (Klaret)
- Stewed vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut striped but only slightly salted pork into slices and remove the rind.
2. Beat the slices with a meat mallet, dip them in egg and then in bread and fry on the pan in clarified butter.
3. Serve with stewed vegetables.
4. If the pork is saltier than desired, after cutting it, soak it in milk for a few hours, press it in a cloth and bread it.
