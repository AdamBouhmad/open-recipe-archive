## Ingredients

- Medium-sized pike
- Salt
- Fish farce
- Parsley
- Small boiled potatoes
- Melted butter
- Grated horseradish
- Sauce hollandaise
- Mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Cut open the pike along the belly, remove the entrails, clean and rinse well
2. Cut out the backbone with a sharp knife, leaving the head and tail intact
3. Rub the inside with salt and coat with a layer of fish farce
4. Sew the belly together and cook as described in previous pike recipes
5. Remove the band when serving and garnish with parsley and small boiled potatoes
6. Serve with either melted butter mixed with grated horseradish, sauce hollandaise, or mushroom sauce
