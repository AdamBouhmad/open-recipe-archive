## Ingredients

- chopped parsley
- water
- butter
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Set chopped parsley on the fire with a little water
2. When it boils, remove the pot and stir in the butter until it is melted
3. The sauce becomes smooth from this
4. To save on butter, use less and mix a little flour in it, and it will then have a boiling in the sauce
