## Ingredients

- 3 pails white rum
- 1 pound shelled blackcurrants
- 1 pound white sugar
- A little cinnamon
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a kettle and pour enough water over it so it covers the sugar
2. Boil until it becomes like thin syrup and skim well during boiling
3. Add a little cinnamon and cloves along with the blackcurrants
4. When boiled, remove from the fire to cool
5. When cold, pour in the rum
6. Let the blackcurrant rum stand for 6 weeks in a well-corked jug and shake occasionally
7. After this time, strain and pour into bottles
