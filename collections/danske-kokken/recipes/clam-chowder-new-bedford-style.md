## Ingredients

- 50 clams
- 1/2 pound salted pork, finely diced
- Onions, finely chopped
- Potatoes, sliced thin
- 3 pints water
- 1 quart hot water
- Crackers, crushed
- Sweet milk

## Directions

1. Rinse the clams thoroughly and place in a pot with 3 pints water.
2. When the shells open, remove the clams and chop them finely, reserving all the clam water.
3. Fry the pork slowly, remove the pieces, and fry or toast the onions.
4. Keep the chowder pot very clean to avoid scorching.
5. Add the onions with 1 quart hot water.
6. Pour in the clams, clam water, and pork pieces.
7. When boiling, add the potatoes.
8. When the potatoes are cooked, the dish is done.
9. Add a little crushed crackers and sweet milk before serving.
