## Ingredients

- Very strong, clear, brown gravy
- Pickled pearl onions
- Paprika
- Sugar
- Salt
- Madeira
- Sago flour

## Directions

1. Add pickled pearl onions, paprika, sugar, salt and Madeira to taste to a very strong, clear, brown gravy
2. Boil the gravy for a few minutes
3. Thicken with sago flour stirred in water
4. Sauce piquante is a thin, clear sauce with a slightly sour taste
5. Suitable for roasted meat dishes, especially veal, lamb and pork, as well as for boiled and roasted chicken, spring chicken and turkey
