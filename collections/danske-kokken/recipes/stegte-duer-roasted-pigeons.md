## Ingredients

- pigeons
- meat stuffing
- bacon slices
- cream

## Directions

1. Fill the pigeons with fine meat stuffing (recipe 75).
2. Cover with bacon slices on the breast.
3. Roast as chickens are roasted.
4. During roasting, baste with cream, and also stir cream into the sauce.
