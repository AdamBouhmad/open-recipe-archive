## Ingredients

- Small cubes of lean, smoked bacon or ham
- Water
- Finely chopped carrots
- Finely chopped white turnips
- Finely chopped celery
- Finely chopped potatoes
- Shelled green peas, about double the amount of other vegetables
- Flour mixed with water for thickening, optional
- Cold butter
- Finely chopped parsley
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook small cubes of lean, smoked bacon or ham until nearly tender in a little water
2. Add equal parts of finely chopped carrots, white turnips, celery and potatoes, and about double the amount of shelled green peas
3. Cook until tender together with the bacon
4. Optionally add a little thickening of flour mixed with water and bring to a boil
5. Finally add a piece of cold butter, finely chopped parsley, sugar and salt as needed
6. This dish can be served as an accompaniment to a meat course or filled in the middle of a ring of forcemeat, or as a dessert course
