## Ingredients

- 4 to 5 pots skimmed milk
- 1 pot medium-fine barley grits
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Heat 4 to 5 pots of skimmed milk in a pot that is greased inside with lard
2. Rinse 1 pot of medium-fine barley grits in cold water
3. When the milk is boiling vigorously, sprinkle the grits into the milk
4. Cover the pot and let the porridge cook for a couple of hours over even heat until the grits are tender, stirring occasionally
5. Season with salt to taste
6. For gruel, use the same method but use only 4.5 pots of grits to 4 pots of milk
