## Ingredients

- 1 pot light beer
- 2 pots milk
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- sugar to taste
- baked rye bread in cubes

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pot of light beer
2. Boil 2 pots of milk with 2 tablespoons of wheat flour stirred in
3. Blend both parts together
4. Add sugar to taste
5. Bring to a boil once more and immediately remove from heat to prevent it from separating
6. Serve with baked rye bread in cubes
