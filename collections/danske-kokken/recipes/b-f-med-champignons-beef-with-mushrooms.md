## Ingredients

- Fresh or preserved mushrooms
- Strong, slightly thickened brown gravy sauce
- Wine to taste
- Fried beef
- Butter dough for garnish as border or as strips

## Directions

1. Give fresh or preserved mushrooms a simmer in a strong, slightly thickened brown gravy sauce, to which wine is added to taste.
2. Arrange the sauce over the fried beef, which is decorated with butter dough in the form of a border or as strips.
