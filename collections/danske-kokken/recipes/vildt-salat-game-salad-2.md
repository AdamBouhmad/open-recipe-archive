## Ingredients

- Leftover venison or game bird
- Good game sauce
- Red wine
- French mustard
- Watercress for garnish
- Tartlets with apple compote

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover meat into 2-3 centimeter (1 inch) long, fine strips
2. Prepare a good, smooth game sauce and add red wine and a little French mustard to it
3. Turn the meat into it and arrange garnished with watercress
4. Place the salad bowl on a platter and arrange a ring of tartlets filled with apple compote around the edge
