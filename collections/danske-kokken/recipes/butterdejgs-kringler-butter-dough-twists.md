## Ingredients

- Butter dough (see recipe 22)
- Egg for brushing
- Sugar and chopped almonds, optional

## Directions

1. Roll out a thin layer of butter dough
2. Cut out small twists using a metal twist cutter
3. Brush with egg and can be sprinkled with sugar and chopped almonds
4. Place close together on the baking sheet and bake with good heat
