## Ingredients

- 1 pot water
- 1 pound white top sugar
- 1 bottle good Cognac
- 1 bottle white French wine (Sauternes)
- 1 bottle Sherry

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pot water with 1 pound white top sugar and skim well
2. Let it cool
3. When cold, filter it and pour into a tureen with 1 bottle good Cognac, 1 bottle white French wine (Sauternes) and 1 bottle Sherry
4. Stir and pour into bottles, which are corked
5. Drink the punch cold, mixed with seltzer water
