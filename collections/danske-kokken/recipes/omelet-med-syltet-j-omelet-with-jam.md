## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound potato flour
- 1/4 pound flour
- 1/2 pound butter
- 6 measures sweet, boiling milk
- 20 egg yolks
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 20 egg whites
- vanilla
- cardamom
- jam

## Directions

1. Bake 1/4 pound potato flour and 1/4 pound flour with 1/2 pound butter
2. Thin with 6 measures sweet, boiling milk
3. Stir in 20 egg yolks, first beaten white with 1 cup powdered sugar
4. Then stir in the 20 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks, and a little vanilla and cardamom
5. Line a dish with jam at the bottom
6. Pour the mixture over the jam
7. Bake in the oven at good heat for 1 hour until light brown
8. Serve with a napkin around the dish
