## Ingredients

- Salted mackerel
- Cold butter
- Pepper
- Lemon slices
- Fine wheat bread

## Directions

1. Soak the fish for a few hours in lukewarm water, changing the water several times.
2. Then wrap it loosely in cloth and place in cold water, set over heat and let it just come to a boil.
3. Pour out all the water at once and pour boiling water over the fish.
4. Let this come to a boil, then remove it, dry it and place on a platter.
5. Add cold butter and pepper and place in the oven for a couple of minutes.
6. Serve with lemon slices and fine wheat bread.
