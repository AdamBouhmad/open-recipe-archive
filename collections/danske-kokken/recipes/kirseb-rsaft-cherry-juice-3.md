## Ingredients

- Good, fully ripe preserved cherries
- 3 pounds of powdered sugar per pound of juice

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries in a mortar so that the pits are also crushed
2. Fill the berries into a pot and let them stand for a day
3. Filter the juice and cook it with 3 pounds of powdered sugar for each pound of juice for 1 hour, skimming it regularly
4. When it is cold, pour it into bottles and cork them
