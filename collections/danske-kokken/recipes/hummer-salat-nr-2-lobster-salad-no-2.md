## Ingredients

- Lobster with its greenness
- Cream or fine salad oil
- Juice from inside the lobster
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Lobster meat
- Lettuce leaves, small and curled
- Watercress
- Lobster roe

## Directions

1. Grind the green part of the lobster in a little cream or fine salad oil
2. Add the juice that flows from the lobster when opened (collected carefully) and a little vinegar and pepper
3. Cut the lobster meat into small pieces and arrange between small curled lettuce leaves in the sauce
4. Garnish with watercress and some of the finest and lightest lettuce leaves
5. Decorate with the internal red firm roe, cut into figures and arranged among the greens; if roe is scarce, place it in the center and corners of the salad dish
