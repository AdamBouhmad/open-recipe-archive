## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 S) unripe, rinsed, unpeeled berries per batch
- 1/2 liter (1/2 Pot) water per 1/2 kg berries
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice

## Directions

1. For each 1/2 kg (1 S) unripe, rinsed, but unpeeled berries, take 1/2 liter (1/2 Pot) water.
2. The berries boil until they burst.
3. Pour them into the jelly cloth.
4. Only the next day, measure the juice and boil it for 10 minutes with the sugar.
5. Skim it during cooking and pour it into bottles, which are bound immediately.
