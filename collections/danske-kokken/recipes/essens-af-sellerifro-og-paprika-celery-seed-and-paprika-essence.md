## Ingredients

- Celery seed or paprika
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Place the spice in a small bottle and cover it with enough Cognac so that it stands above it.
2. Shake the bottle regularly, and after a few days the essence can be strained and stoppered well.
3. In this way essence can be made from most spices.
4. A few drops of celery seed essence make a good effect in soup and sauce and can often replace celery.
5. Paprika (Hungarian pepper), prepared as an essence, is a spice which is now used much more than cayenne pepper. It is easy to have it in liquid form, as only a small amount need be used.
