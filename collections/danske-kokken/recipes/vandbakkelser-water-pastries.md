## Ingredients

- 40 ounces melted butter
- ¾ pound wheat flour
- 2 pints boiled water
- 10 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Bake 40 ounces melted butter (with salt removed) together with ¾ pound wheat flour and thin with 2 pints boiled water
2. Bake until the dough releases from the pan
3. Pour out to cool
4. When somewhat cooled, stir in 10 whole eggs, 2 at a time
5. Continue stirring for half an hour
