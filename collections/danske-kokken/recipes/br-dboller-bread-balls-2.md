## Ingredients

- 5 small French breads or wheat rolls
- 1/2 pound butter
- 5 eggs
- salt to taste
- sugar to taste
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scrape off the crust from the breads with a grater
2. Cut into 4 pieces, soak in water and press in a clean linen cloth
3. Melt the butter in a saucepan and bake the bread while stirring constantly until it releases
4. Pour the bread onto a dish and while still hot, stir in 5 eggs one at a time
5. Add salt, sugar and a little grated nutmeg
