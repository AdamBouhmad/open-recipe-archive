## Ingredients

- 1 Pint sour cream
- 3 bottles light beer or double beer (not bitter)
- 1 bottle Bavarian beer
- a little ice-cold water
- a little finely chopped lemon peel
- rye bread cubes or wheat bread cubes

## Directions

1. Whisk 1 pint of sour cream in a bowl with 3 bottles of light beer or double beer (not bitter) and 1 bottle of Bavarian beer along with a little ice-cold water
2. Add a little finely chopped lemon peel for good flavor
3. Just before serving, add rye bread cubes or wheat bread cubes cut into large pieces to the cold soup
4. Serve immediately
