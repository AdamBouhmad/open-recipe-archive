## Ingredients

- Cooked veal breast
- Melted butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Pepper
- Salt
- Egg
- Claret
- Mixed vegetables
- Salad or parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked breast into pieces with 1 or 2 bones
2. Dip them in melted butter and turn in crushed bread mixed with pepper and salt
3. Then dip in egg and again in bread
4. Cook the cutlets light brown in claret
5. Place mixed vegetables in the center of the dish
6. Arrange the cutlets around this, decorated with salad or parsley
