## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce smoked Ham
- 1 Ounce Fat
- 2 Ounces Carrot
- 2 Ounces Onion
- 2 Ounces dried Flour
- 1 Quart Bouillon or Water and 1/2 Pound finely chopped Meat
- 1 Teaspoon Salt
- Pepper
- 1 Bay leaf
- 1 Gill Wine

## Directions

1. Cut 1 Ounce smoked Ham into small pieces about 2 Inches in size and fry in 1 Ounce Fat.
2. As soon as it is brown, add 2 Ounces Carrot sliced and the same amount of Onion, likewise sliced.
3. Stir in 2 Ounces dried Flour and brown it.
4. Then add 1 Quart Bouillon; if you do not have it, then 1 Quart Water and 1/2 Pound finely chopped Meat.
5. Season with 1 Teaspoon Salt, a little Pepper and 1 Bay leaf.
6. Boil slowly for one Hour, and skim properly.
7. Then strain it and 1 Gill Wine may be added.
8. This Sauce can keep for over a Week in a cold Place and is used for all kinds of dark Meat, Game or fried Fish.
