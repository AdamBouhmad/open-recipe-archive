## Ingredients

- One turkey, slaughtered a couple of days before use
- Finely grated breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Two eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Sage
- Marjoram
- Water
- Flour
- Egg white
- Gooseberry sauce

## Directions

1. Slaughter a couple of days before use and hang it up by the legs.
2. Prepare in the usual manner.
3. Fill with finely grated breadcrumbs that are soaked with butter and two eggs and seasoned with salt, pepper, parsley, sage and marjoram.
4. Sew it up and roast in a roasting pan, preferably on a rack in the same; drip constantly with butter, pepper, salt and water and turn it.
5. A couple of minutes before it is done, glaze it with the white of an egg.
6. Take it out.
7. Most of the fat is poured off, and into it are put the chopped giblets with the water in which they were cooked, which is thickened with flour rubbed with butter; everything is stirred in the roasting pan and is given a good boil before it is served.
8. Gooseberry sauce is served with it.
