## Ingredients

- Plums
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 1.25 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Place the plums in a sieve and pour boiling water over them.
2. In the sugar and water, which have been boiled to a thin syrup, give the plums only a couple of minutes of cooking, as they must remain whole when filled into the jars.
3. Reduce the syrup for 6 to 8 minutes and pour it immediately over the plums.
