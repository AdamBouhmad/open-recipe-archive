## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- small macaroons
- apple porridge
- raspberry marmalade
- 6 egg yolks
- 1.5 cups sweet cream
- 6 egg whites
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Butter a form and flour it
2. On the bottom place a layer of small macaroons dipped in melted butter, then a layer of apple porridge, then a layer of raspberry marmalade, then again macaroons, and so forth, until the form is almost full
3. Beat 6 egg yolks with 1.5 cups sweet cream and then with the 6 previously beaten egg whites
4. Pour this mixture over the rest, dust with powdered sugar, and then bake the cake 1 hour in a moderately warm oven
5. When done, turn it onto a dish
