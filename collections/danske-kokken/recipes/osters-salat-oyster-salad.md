## Ingredients

- 25 large oysters
- 3 pounds chestnuts
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 raw egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fish balls or cooked fish (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the beard from 25 large oysters and cut into 4 pieces each
2. Boil 3 pounds of chestnuts, shell them and cut according to their size into 2 or 4 pieces
3. Make the sauce from 3 hard-boiled egg yolks, which are mashed and stirred with 2 raw ones, then with 2 tablespoons of oil, 1 tablespoon vinegar, salt and a little pepper
4. Stir the chestnuts in this sauce together with the cut oysters together with the sauce that comes with them
5. You can either cut 3 to 4 fish balls into small pieces or pick cooked fish into pieces and stir into the sauce, of which a little more must then be made
