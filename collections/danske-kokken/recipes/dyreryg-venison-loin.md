## Ingredients

- Loin of a roe deer
- Lard
- Butter
- Salt
- Cream
- Bouillon
- Water
- Wheat flour
- Roasted potatoes
- Redcurrant jelly

## Directions

1. Hang the venison for several days. Lard the roast well with lard and place in a roasting pan with browned butter, sprinkle with salt and cover with butter.
2. During roasting, baste well, initially also with cream and bouillon. For each year the animal is old, add one more branch of larding.
3. Roast at even heat for approximately 2.5 to 3 hours until tender, basting the meat regularly.
4. When the roast is tender and removed, brown the sauce with a little wheat flour, strain through a sieve, and stir in a cold piece of butter quickly.
5. Serve with roasted potatoes and redcurrant jelly.
