## Ingredients

- prepared forcemeat
- half-cooked cauliflower
- butter

## Directions

1. Treat as for meat pudding with asparagus, layering half-cooked cauliflower in bouquets with the forcemeat
2. When the pudding is turned out, it can be further decorated with a wreath of cooked cauliflower
3. Serve with melted butter
