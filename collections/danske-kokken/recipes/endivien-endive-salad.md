## Ingredients

- endive
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and any insects
2. Remove the stalk and cut the lettuce into fine strips
3. Rinse in water and pat dry in a cloth
4. Prepare either with vinegar and oil alone or as the oil salad recipe
