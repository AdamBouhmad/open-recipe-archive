## Ingredients

- Small Spanish onions or red onions
- Butter
- Sugar
- Broth, gravy, or water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the small Spanish onions or red onions to make them easier to peel.
2. Brown butter and sugar in a pot, then add the onions, which must be shaken regularly so they do not burn.
3. When the sugar has adhered to the onions, add a little broth, gravy, or water.
4. Cook them until tender with regular shaking.
5. When they are tender and a glaze has formed around them, they are ready to serve.
6. Glazed onions are used as a garnish around many meat dishes, to which they taste excellent.
