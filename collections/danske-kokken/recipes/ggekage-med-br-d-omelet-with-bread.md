## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 6 tablespoons milk
- lemon zest
- 6 egg whites
- French bread
- butter
- powdered sugar
- raspberry jam

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks until they are white, and then mix with 2 tablespoons of flour, 6 tablespoons of milk, a little grated lemon zest, and 6 egg whites whipped to foam.
2. A French bread is cut into small slices, which are baked in butter in a pan, and when they are baked, pour the egg yolks over them, after which the cake is baked beautifully light brown.
3. It is served with powdered sugar and raspberry jam.
