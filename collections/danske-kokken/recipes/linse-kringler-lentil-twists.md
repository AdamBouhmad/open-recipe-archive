## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 375 grams (3/4 pound) margarine
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) powdered sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- Glaze made from 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) flour mixed with 4 tablespoons water and a few drops of vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Knead together the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, and egg yolks
2. Roll out into small, thin twists
3. Place fairly close together on the baking sheet in a not-too-hot oven
4. Bake until very pale
5. Once the twists are cold, brush them with glaze using a knife and place in a barely warm oven to dry
6. The oven door must be left open for the glaze to become shiny, but if the twists are left in too long, the glaze will crack
