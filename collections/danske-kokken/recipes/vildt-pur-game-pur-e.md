## Ingredients

- leftovers of all kinds of game
- 3 carrots
- 1 celery
- 2 onions
- whole peppercorns
- salt
- water or thin brown spare broth
- 125 grams (0.25 S) butter
- 125 grams (0.25 S) flour
- Sherry
- game balls (no. 204)
- bread croutons (no. 500)

## Directions

1. The meat is picked from the bones, which are chopped and cooked for an hour in water with salt or in thin brown spare broth together with 3 carrots, 1 celery, 2 onions and some whole peppercorns.
2. The meat is finely pounded in a mortar and rubbed through a sieve with some of the broth.
3. Brown 125 grams (0.25 S) butter, and bake 125 grams (0.25 S) flour in it, which is thinned with the sieved broth.
4. Add the purée and bring to a boil and add Sherry to taste.
5. Game purée can be served with small balls of game farce (no. 204). Bread croutons (no. 500) can be served with it.
