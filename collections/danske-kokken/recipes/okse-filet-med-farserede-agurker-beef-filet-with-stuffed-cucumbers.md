## Ingredients

- Beef filet, roasted
- Stuffed cucumbers (see recipe 218)
- Fresh tomato slices
- Salad or parsley for garnish
- Gravy from the filet
- Gravy from the cucumbers

## Directions

1. Around the roasted filet, arrange slices of stuffed cucumbers alternately with slices of fresh tomatoes
2. Garnish the dish with salad or parsley
3. Make a smooth brown sauce from the gravy of the filet and the gravy from the cucumbers
