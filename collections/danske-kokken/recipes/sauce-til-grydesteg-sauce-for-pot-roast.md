## Ingredients

- Ginger
- Whole peppercorns
- Onion
- Flour
- Brown butter
- Meat broth
- Sugar
- Vinegar
- Sour and sweet sauce ingredients

## Directions

1. The ordinary sauce is what forms itself in the pot when you knead the roast well before putting it in the brown butter and add whole pieces of ginger, whole peppercorns, and whole onion, which are then strained
2. Some prefer sour and sweet sauce, in which case less flour is put on the roast
3. Prepare a small browned thickening as for red wine sauce, dilute it with the broth and add sugar and vinegar
4. In this case use neither onion nor spices, only a little ginger
