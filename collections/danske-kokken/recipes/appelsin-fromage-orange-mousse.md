## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 375 grams (¼ pound) powdered sugar
- 16 sheets isinglass
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) boiling water
- juice of 6 oranges, pressed and strained
- juice of 1 lemon
- 10 stiffly beaten egg whites
- ¼ liter (½ pot) heavy cream for whipping
- orange pieces

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks well with powdered sugar.
2. Dissolve isinglass in boiling water and add the pressed and strained juice of oranges and lemon.
3. Pour this mixture into the eggs along with the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the mousse into a wetted mold to be turned out and decorated with whipped cream and with some orange pieces that have been left covered with sugar for an hour.
5. The oranges should be peeled before pressing and the peel can be used for essence.
