## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and whites separately
2. Instead of salt and pepper, add powdered sugar to yolks
3. Cook the same way as simple omelet
4. While omelet cooks, clean the fire shovel and make it red-hot
5. When omelet is slid onto plate, sprinkle with powdered sugar
6. Stiffen the top with the hot iron, browning the sugar without burning it
7. Serve hot
