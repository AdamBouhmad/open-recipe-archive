## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.5 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. The apples used must be fine and firm apples that do not easily fall apart.
2. Peel them, place in water with vinegar and cook as pears.
3. Since fresh apples can be obtained throughout the winter, it is probably the fruit least used in the household for preserving as compote.
