## Ingredients

- cooked finely chopped celery
- grated horseradish
- oil

## Directions

1. Make according to the same recipe as Sildesalat (Herring Salad, recipe 285) with the addition of cooked finely chopped celery, a little grated horseradish and oil, stirred into the sauce
