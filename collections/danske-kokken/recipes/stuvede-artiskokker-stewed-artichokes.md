## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Strong broth
- Paprika
- Lemon juice
- Mushrooms
- Madeira
- Sugar
- Salt
- Meat glaze if needed
- Artichoke hearts
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and cook flour in it, then add a strong broth to make a smooth sauce, seasoned with paprika and a little lemon juice.
2. Add mushrooms, Madeira, sugar, salt and meat glaze if the sauce needs more body.
3. The hearts, which have previously been blanched in bouillon, are taken up with a spoon, arranged on a warm platter, and the sauce is poured over them.
