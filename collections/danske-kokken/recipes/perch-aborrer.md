## Ingredients

- Perch
- Butter
- Parsley
- Egg sauce or perch fricassée: good beef broth, celery, parsley roots, stock, salt, butter, flour, nutmeg, oysters, wine or lemon juice, egg yolk

## Directions

1. Scales are scraped off.
2. Perch are eaten boiled with butter and parsley or with egg sauce.
3. For fricassée: boil a good beef broth, celery and parsley roots in water and a little stock.
4. Once done, remove them and add the fish with salt, cooking until done.
5. Place the fish on a platter along with the roots, cut into pieces.
6. Strain the soup, place on heat with a roux of butter and flour and a little nutmeg and pour over the fish.
7. For oysters version, use no roots but a little wine or lemon juice added to the sauce, which is thickened with egg yolk.
