## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- 4½ pounds butter
- ½ pound powdered sugar
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix wheat flour, butter, powdered sugar, and eggs together
2. Form into small tea cakes shaped like an S using a pastry bag
3. Bake brown at strong heat
