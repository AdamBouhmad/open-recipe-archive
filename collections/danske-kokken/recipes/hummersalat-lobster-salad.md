## Ingredients

- Diced, cooked lobster meat (about as much as is usually in a tin)
- 2 pints cream or sweet milk
- 7 egg yolks
- 25 pound cooked, cold cod, free of bones and skin
- Capers worth 16 øre
- 3 celery stalks, diced
- Vinegar and sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Mix diced, cooked lobster meat with a crème made from 2 pints cream or sweet milk and 7 egg yolks
2. Blend in 25 pound cooked, cold cod (free of bones and skin), capers worth 16 øre, 3 diced celery stalks, and vinegar and sugar to taste
