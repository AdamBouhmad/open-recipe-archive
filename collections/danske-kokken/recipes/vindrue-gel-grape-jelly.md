## Ingredients

- 625 grams (1 1/2 pounds) stone sugar
- 1 1/2 dl (1/2 cup) water for 1/2 kg (1 pound) fruit juice
- partially ripe grapes

## Directions

1. Pick the grapes from the stems and fill them into a jar which is placed in a pot of boiling water that must be kept boiling until the grapes burst and release their juice well.
2. Pour the entire contents into a jelly cloth.
3. Weigh the juice that has run through.
4. Cook sugar and water as in recipe 93.
5. Pour the juice, which is then poured into the pot, and cook for 10 minutes, skim and pour into cups moistened with cognac.
6. Bind as soon as the jelly is cooled and has begun to set.
