## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- cleaned soup vegetables, sliced
- chopped red onion
- parsley
- 4 pounds cod, flounder, or eel, cleaned and cut into pieces
- water
- salt
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf

## Directions

1. Place 1/4 pound butter in a casserole together with the soup vegetables sliced clean, chopped red onion and parsley, and steam covered until the vegetables are half-tender.
2. Then add 4 pounds of well-cleaned cod, flounder, or eel cut into pieces.
3. Pour in enough water so that it just covers the fish, then boil and skim the soup.
4. Add salt, 10 peppercorns and a bay leaf, then cook the whole covered until the fish is thoroughly tender.
5. Remove the fish, strain the soup and it can be used for thinning meat soup and for fish sauce.
