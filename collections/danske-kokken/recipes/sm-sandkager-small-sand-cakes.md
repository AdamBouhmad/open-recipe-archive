## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 3 pounds powdered sugar
- ¼ pound wheat flour
- 9 egg yolks
- 9 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and remove the salt
2. When half cooled, stir in 3 pounds powdered sugar
3. Stir in ¼ pound wheat flour
4. Add 9 egg yolks
5. Finally fold in 9 whipped egg whites
6. Bake in small molds at fairly strong heat
