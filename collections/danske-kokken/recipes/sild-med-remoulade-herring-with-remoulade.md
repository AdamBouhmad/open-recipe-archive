## Ingredients

- Four cured herring
- Milk
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon stirred mustard
- 3 spoonfuls oil and vinegar
- 5 chopped shallots
- 1 bunch finely chopped parsley
- 1 chopped cucumber

## Directions

1. Lay the cured herring in milk the day before and prepare as for marinated herring
2. Stir together 3 hard-boiled egg yolks with 1 tablespoon stirred mustard and 3 spoonfuls oil and vinegar until it becomes a liquid sauce
3. Stir into this 5 chopped shallots and 1 bunch finely chopped parsley
4. Pour the sauce over the herring and sprinkle with 1 chopped cucumber
