## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- salt
- mustard
- pepper
- allspice
- cloves
- gray pepper
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut ripe tomatoes in half and bring to a boil.
2. Press them through a sieve to separate skin and seed.
3. For every 3 pots of strained tomatoes take 4 tablespoons salt, 3 tablespoons mustard, 2 tablespoons pepper, 1 tablespoon allspice, 4 tablespoons cloves, and 4 tablespoons gray pepper, as well as 3 pints wine vinegar.
4. Boil the whole for 4 hours and then fill into hot bottles, which are immediately corked.
5. The sauce is used to strengthen other sauces.
