## Ingredients

- Herring
- Flour
- Butter or clarified fat

## Directions

1. After the herring is cleaned, washed and dried, dip it in flour and fry in butter or clarified fat.
2. Brown butter in a pan and dip the herring in flour and place in the pan with one head to another's tail, as long as there is space, and press them so tightly together that they form a cake.
3. When one side is fried enough, carefully slide the cake onto a lid, or if you have a sufficiently large plate, place small pieces of butter on the unfried side, place it over the pan and turn it over.
4. Be careful to hold the lid firmly to the pan so there is no opening through which the cake can slide out.
5. Do not press the herrings too hard together, as the herrings might become soft and the flour white.
6. This must be checked as the cake is taken from the pan.
7. Serve hot with greens or cold as an open sandwich.
