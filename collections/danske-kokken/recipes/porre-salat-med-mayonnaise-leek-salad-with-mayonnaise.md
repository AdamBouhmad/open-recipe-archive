## Ingredients

- leeks
- salt
- real mayonnaise
- mustard
- raw cream or whipped cream
- tomatoes
- watercress

## Directions

1. Cut the lower white piece of the leeks into 1.5 centimeter long pieces and cook them in lightly salted water, but not so long that they lose their firm appearance and become stringy
2. Real mayonnaise made with mustard can have a little raw cream or whipped cream added to it, and arrange the leeks on a platter and cover with it
3. Cut 2-3 tomatoes in slices, with which the salad is lined
4. On each tomato slice place mayonnaise in very small pearls, and place watercress in the center
