## Ingredients

- Bacon side
- Rinds and waste
- Salt
- Water
- Cream or cream and soup
- Fine, dry salt
- Ground white pepper
- Ground nutmeg
- Pig intestines

## Directions

1. The bacon side is best; chop it very finely.
2. Set the rinds and waste to cook with some salt and water, and when this soup is strained and cooled, knead the filling with it so it becomes tough and supple.
3. Even better than soup alone is half soup and half cream.
4. Add fine, dry salt, ground white pepper and nutmeg, after which the whole is filled into cleaned pig intestines.
5. The intestines should only be stuffed loosely, otherwise they will burst during roasting.
6. These sausages can also be smoked.
