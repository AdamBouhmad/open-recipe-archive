## Ingredients

- pears
- white wine
- water
- lemon peel
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and split them lengthwise so a piece of the split stalk remains with each half
2. Cut out the core and scrape the stalk a little with a penknife or replace it with a piece of cinnamon
3. Set a saucepan over heat with half white wine and half water, lemon peel, and white sugar
4. When this has boiled a little, place the pear pieces in it and cook until tender, which if the pears are hard may sometimes take over two hours
5. Gray pears are best for compote
