## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- half a bottle white French wine
- 3 bottles water
- juice of 2 lemons
- zest of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Stir together 12 egg yolks, half a bottle white French wine, 3 bottles water, juice and zest of 2 lemons.
2. Place over heat and whisk with a whisk until it boils.
