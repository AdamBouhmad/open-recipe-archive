## Ingredients

- Wild ducks
- Butter
- Good milk
- Cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and skin the ducks completely.
2. Roast in butter for one hour, browning the butter.
3. Add enough good milk so that it comes halfway up the duck, basting constantly.
4. Add cream to the sauce before serving.
