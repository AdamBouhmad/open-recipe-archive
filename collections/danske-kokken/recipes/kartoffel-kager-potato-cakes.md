## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked potatoes
- Eggs
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Milk
- Butter

## Directions

1. Use potatoes left over from the previous day; grate them and mix with a couple of eggs, a little salt and nutmeg, and, if necessary to even them out, a little milk.
2. Fry them in browned butter either in flat cakes or like patties.
3. They should be beautiful, light brown and eaten with all kinds of preserves or cooked fruits.
