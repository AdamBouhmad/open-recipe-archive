## Ingredients

- 4 pints broth
- 4-5 quintal dissolved isinglass
- vinegar
- pepper
- 2 egg whites
- cooked chicken

## Directions

1. To the broth add the dissolved isinglass and vinegar and pepper to taste
2. Add 2 egg whites which have been whisked until white and stiff
3. Filter the whole and pour over cooked chicken cut into appropriate pieces
