## Ingredients

- Blood sausages
- Apples, peeled and sliced
- Sugar
- Butter, melted

## Directions

1. Slice the sausages and fry them in a pan in butter together with peeled apples that have been sliced and sprinkled with sugar.
2. Arrange the apples warm in the center of the dish and the sausages around them.
3. Serve with melted butter and sugar.
