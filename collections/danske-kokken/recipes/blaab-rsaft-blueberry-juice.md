## Ingredients

- Blueberries
- 1 liter (1 Pot) water per 2 to 2½ kg berries (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the berries for a quarter of an hour with frequent stirring.
2. Press the juice through a very fine sieve.
3. Pour it into bottles, which are wrapped with hay, corked, and boiled for 5 minutes in the pot.
4. When the bottles have partially cooled, wipe them dry and plaster them the next day.
5. Alternatively, the blueberries can be boiled with 1 liter (1 Pot) water per 2 to 2½ kg berries.
6. Sift off the juice and treat as described above.
