## Ingredients

- 2 pints fine, rinsed buckwheat grits
- 4 pots milk (preferably sweet)
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Heat the pot and grease it inside with lard to prevent sticking
2. Bring 4 pots of milk to a boil
3. Sprinkle the buckwheat grits quickly into the milk at the side where it boils most vigorously, stirring constantly until it returns to a boil
4. Continue stirring regularly until the porridge is done
5. Add salt at the end
6. If the porridge becomes too thick, thin it with a little boiling milk
7. Most can be served as an accompaniment to this porridge
