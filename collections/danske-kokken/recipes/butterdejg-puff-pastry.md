## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) cake flour
- 1/2 kg (1 pound) cold margarine
- 1/2 glass vinegar
- 1/4 liter (1 pint) cold water

## Directions

1. Place flour on a pastry board and blend half of the cold margarine with the flour until very finely crumbled, using a large knife to chop the flour and butter together to keep hands cool
2. Make a well in the center of the flour and pour in the vinegar and cold water
3. Work together with the knife until a tough dough forms
4. Gather the dough together with quick, light hands and roll out with a pastry pin into a large sheet
5. Distribute the remaining margarine in small lumps over the dough
6. Fold the dough first into 3 parts and then again into 3 parts in the opposite direction, as if folding a napkin
7. Wrap in a cloth and set in a cold place for 1 hour
8. Roll out and fold the puff pastry 4 to 5 times with a few minutes between each folding, ensuring this is done in a cool kitchen and using only minimal flour for the rolling pin and board
9. The dough is then ready for use in various baked goods
10. A larger portion of puff pastry can be made at once but must be stored in a cold place and well covered to prevent it from developing a dry crust
11. The pastry is typically baked the day it is to be served, but it can also be baked the day before and reheated in the oven before serving
