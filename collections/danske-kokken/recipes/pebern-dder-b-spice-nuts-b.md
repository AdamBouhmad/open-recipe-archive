## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound dried, light powdered sugar
- 4 whole eggs
- ground ginger, cinnamon, and cloves to taste
- up to 1 pound butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, powdered sugar, eggs, and spices well together and roll out and form into spice nuts of appropriate size. Bake the spice nuts light golden brown. They can be made more palatable by adding up to 1 pound butter; but it is not necessary.
