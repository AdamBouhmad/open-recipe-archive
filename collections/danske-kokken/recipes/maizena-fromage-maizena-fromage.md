## Ingredients

- 625 gr (1 1/4 S) cold maizena porridge
- ground almonds or vanilla extract to taste
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 3/4 L (1 pægl) cream
- apple or apricot compote
- 125 gr (1/4 S) small macaroons
- 1/4 L (1 pægl) cream for whipped topping, colored pale pink

## Directions

1. Stir ground almonds or vanilla extract and powdered sugar into the cold maizena porridge
2. Fold in whipped cream
3. Spread on a flat dish covered with apple or apricot compote
4. Top with small macaroons
5. Spray whipped cream tinted pale pink over the fromage
