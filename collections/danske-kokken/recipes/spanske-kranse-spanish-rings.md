## Ingredients

- 354 grams (1/4 pound) flour
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) margarine
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- Coarse sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Knead together the flour, margarine, and hard-boiled egg yolks
2. Form into rings
3. Dip them in coarse sugar
4. Place them not too close together on the baking sheet
