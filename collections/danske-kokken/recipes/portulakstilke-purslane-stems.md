## Ingredients

- Purslane stems
- water
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the stems into smaller pieces and pour boiling water over them.
2. Place them in a strong brine.
3. Let the stems stand until the next day.
4. Boil them in as much vinegar as needed to cover them.
5. Strain the stems and pack them in jars.
6. Heat the vinegar and pour it over them.
7. Seal the jars.
