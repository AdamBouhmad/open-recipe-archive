## Ingredients

- Wild ducks
- Butter
- Milk

## Directions

1. Pluck and flay them completely; the gamey flavor often found in wild ducks is in the skin
2. Roast in one hour with butter, which is browned, and into which is added enough good milk so it comes halfway up the roasting bird, which is constantly
