## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- Salt
- Water
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes thoroughly in water and peel them
2. Shape them like small pears and cook in water with a little salt
3. Drain on a sieve and arrange with melted butter over them
