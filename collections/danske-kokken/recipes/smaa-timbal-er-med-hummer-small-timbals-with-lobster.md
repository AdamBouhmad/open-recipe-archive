## Ingredients

- Butter
- Fish forcemeat
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish stock made from fish bones
- Curry
- Lobster meat, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Butter the small timbal molds and line them with fish forcemeat
2. Make a roux with butter and flour and thin with fish stock made from fish bones, the meat being used for the forcemeat
3. Add curry to the sauce and finally the finely chopped lobster meat
4. Fill the molds with this stew, cover with forcemeat, and place a piece of buttered paper on top
5. Cook as recipe No. 51
6. Turn the molds out onto platters and pour curry sauce over them
7. Decorate with some of the remaining lobster meat
8. Serve with curry sauce or a lobster and crayfish sauce
