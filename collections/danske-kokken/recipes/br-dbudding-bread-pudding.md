## Ingredients

- half a pound French bread
- sweet milk or cold water
- a quarter pound butter
- a quarter pound cleaned raisins
- a quarter pound ground sugar
- salt
- cardamom or lemon zest
- 10 egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate half a pound French bread, cut into pieces and dip very lightly in sweet milk or cold water, then let it stand covered for an hour. Then mash it with a spoon and bake with a quarter pound butter until it releases from the spoon, then place it in a deep dish to cool somewhat. When this is done, but while it is still warm, stir in a quarter pound cleaned raisins with the kernels removed, a quarter pound ground sugar, a little salt, cardamom or lemon zest, and 10 egg yolks. Lastly stir in the egg whites, which have been beaten to a foam, also carefully. The stirring takes about half an hour. Then put the mixture in a mold that is buttered and sprinkled with grated bread. Set the mold in boiling water, and the pudding must boil for 2 hours. Serve with sweet fruit sauce.
