## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Salt
- Water
- Fat

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cut into thin slices and place in well-salted cold water for at least one hour.
2. Wash them well in several changes of water and dry in a clean cloth.
3. Fry golden brown in hot fat, then place on gray paper to drain of fat and serve hot.
