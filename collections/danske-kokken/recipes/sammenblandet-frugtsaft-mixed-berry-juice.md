## Ingredients

- strawberries
- raspberries
- currants
- cherries
- 37 parts juice
- 63 parts sugar (Melis)
- vanilla (optional)

## Directions

1. Crush the berries and let them ferment for approximately 3 days at a temperature of 15 to 20 degrees Reaumur.
2. When gas development stops, press out the juice.
3. Give it a brief boil and let it rest quietly for 2 days.
4. Filter it through filter paper, first wetted with water.
5. Weigh the drained juice.
6. Add 63 parts sugar (Melis) to 37 parts juice and dissolve it in.
7. Give it a brief boil and filter while still warm.
8. Give it another short boil and fill into bottles.
9. When boiling the juice a second time, a little vanilla may be added.
