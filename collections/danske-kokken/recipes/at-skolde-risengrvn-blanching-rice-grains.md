## Ingredients

- rice grains
- cold water
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Pick over the grains and place them in a little cold water to draw out the sourness.
2. Afterwards pour boiling water over them three times, letting them stand each time until nearly cold.
3. Finally, drain well through a colander.
