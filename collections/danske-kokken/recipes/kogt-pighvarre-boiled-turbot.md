## Ingredients

- Turbot
- Salt
- Bay leaves
- Parsley or green salad
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean by removing entrails through an incision near the head, keeping the head on
2. Brush in salt water to clean the dark bumpy skin on top
3. Scrape the white underside until clear and free of slime
4. Polish the fins
5. Wash in several changes of cold water
6. Sprinkle with salt
7. Cook in a fish kettle on a rack or on a platter, starting in cold salted water
8. Once it comes to a boil, reduce heat
9. After about half an hour of cooking, when the fins begin to loosen, the fish is done
10. Arrange on a platter with a porcelain rack under the folded napkin
11. Garnish at the head and tail with a bouquet of parsley or green salad
12. Place small boiled potatoes on the sides
