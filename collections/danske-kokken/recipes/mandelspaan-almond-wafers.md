## Ingredients

- ½ pound blanched almonds
- 6 egg whites
- 4 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
- 4 pounds fine flour
- sliced almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Finely pound the blanched almonds with 6 egg whites
2. Add 4 pounds finely ground powdered sugar and 4 pounds fine flour
3. Spread this batter on oblate wafers
4. Sprinkle with sliced almonds
5. Bake in a moderately warm oven
6. When removed, curl them over a cake roll or over a bottle
