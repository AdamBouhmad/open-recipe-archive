## Ingredients

- 4-5 L (4.5-5.5 pots) economical stock
- 0.5 sweetbreads
- Water
- Butter or vegetable margarine
- 100 g (3.5 oz) flour
- 6 egg yolks
- Paprika
- Madeira wine
- Salt sticks

## Directions

1. Blanch the sweetbreads and cook them tender in the economical stock
2. Cut half of the sweetbreads into very small dice and place in the tureen
3. Finely chop the remaining sweetbreads and sauté in a very little butter or vegetable margarine
4. Brown 100 g (3.5 oz) flour and dilute with the stock, thickened with 6 egg yolks
5. Add a little paprika and Madeira to taste
6. Press the soup through a coarse sieve into the tureen
7. Serve with salt sticks
