## Ingredients

- 350 gr Farine or powdered loaf sugar
- 1¾ dl vinegar
- 1¾ dl water
- peeled pears
- whole cloves
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, split them, hollow them out and then cover them repeatedly with boiling water, in which they must stand until they are cold.
2. Pour them onto a sieve and dry them in a cloth, before cutting them into strips as before.
3. Cook sugar, vinegar and water to syrup together with a few whole cloves and a couple of pieces of whole cinnamon.
4. Cook the pears in this for about 5 minutes, pour into a jar and cover.
5. After 3 to 4 days, pour off the syrup and reduce it until very smooth.
6. Pour it hot over the pears and seal.
