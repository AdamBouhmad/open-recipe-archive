## Ingredients

- 625 grams (1 1/4 lb) Pillsbury flour
- 375 grams (3/4 lb) margarine
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) icing sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking soda, dissolved in a small amount of water
- chocolate glaze

## Directions

1. Bake the cakes from the dough described above
2. When slightly cooled, spread with chocolate glaze
3. Then dry in the oven
4. Bake at not too strong heat
