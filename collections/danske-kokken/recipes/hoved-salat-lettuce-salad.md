## Ingredients

- Five lettuce heads
- Three egg yolks
- Two tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the outer coarse leaves from the lettuce heads
2. Rinse well and place in cloth, shaking them until approximately dry
3. Mix 3 egg yolks well with 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
4. Pour in 1/4 L (1 Pægl) cream and vinegar to taste
5. Cut the lettuce into portions, place in a bowl, and pour the sauce over
