## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Cold water
- Yellow and white root vegetables
- A bundle of herbs
- Whole small onions
- Salt
- Ground black pepper or Cayenne pepper
- Salted meat or meat broth (optional)
- Fresh meat, diced (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cut them into pieces and put them on the fire with enough cold water that it roughly stands over them.
2. When they have cooked somewhat, add yellow and white root vegetables and a bundle of herbs; this is removed when soft and pressed well.
3. Cook several whole small onions in this with salt and a little ground black pepper or Cayenne pepper.
4. You can add less water at first and later thin the soup with broth from salted meat or meat.
5. If desired, fresh meat can be cut into small dice, which are cooked with the potatoes and served in the soup.
