## Ingredients

- Leftover pâté
- Good, clear aspic
- Isinglass
- Hard-boiled eggs (optional)
- Truffles (optional)
- Lemon slices
- Parsley
- Aspic buddings (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Use small molds or small coffee cups with the bottom lined with good, clear aspic mixed with isinglass.
2. Once set, place a small piece of liver pâté in the mold.
3. Pour lukewarm aspic over it so as not to melt the aspic on the bottom.
4. Optionally, place a slice of hard-boiled egg or truffle on the set aspic before adding the pâté.
5. A ring-shaped mold can also be filled with liver pâté in aspic.
6. Turn out onto a dish and decorate the center with lemon slices arranged in a star pattern.
7. Place parsley between the slices.
8. A pudding mold can also be used, garnished with small aspic buddings.
