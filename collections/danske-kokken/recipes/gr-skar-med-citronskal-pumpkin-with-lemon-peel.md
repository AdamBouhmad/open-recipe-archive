## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) peeled pumpkin
- 250 gr (1/2 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- 1.5 dl (1/3 pint) vinegar
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. After the pumpkin is prepared as described in the general pumpkin recipe, boil it until clear in a syrup made from the vinegar and sugar together with finely sliced peel of 2 lemons.
2. Pour the pumpkin onto a colander to later blend with the strongly reduced syrup before filling it into the jars.
3. Seal as soon as it has cooled.
