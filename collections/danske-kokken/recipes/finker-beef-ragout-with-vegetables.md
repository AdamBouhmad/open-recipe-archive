## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked or roasted beef
- half a quarter pound butter
- half as much flour
- 4 pints good stock
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- ground pepper
- peeled apples, chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into small cubes
2. In the butter, stir in half as much flour
3. Thin with 4 pints of good stock and 1 tablespoon of vinegar
4. Add the meat, reheat thoroughly
5. Before serving, season the ragout with sugar, salt and ground pepper to taste
6. It gives a good flavor to cook some peeled, chopped apples in the stock before adding the meat
