## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg fresh plums
- 4.5 L water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- potato flour
- red wine or Graves wine

## Directions

1. Rinse plums and cook in 4.5 L water with sugar and cinnamon until cooked through
2. Strain through a coarse sieve
3. Thicken with potato flour mixed with water
4. Season with red wine or Graves
