## Ingredients

- For 8 persons: Pastry dough
- 2 cleaned goose giblets
- 2 carrots
- 1 celery root
- 2 onions with 2 cloves in each
- Parsley
- Thyme
- a few peppercorns
- Salt
- Bouillon or water
- Soy sauce
- Farce of ox meat

## Directions

1. Simmer the giblets for 5 minutes in water with a little salt, rinse, drain on a sieve, place in a casserole with the vegetables and seasonings, moisten with sufficient bouillon or water and a little soy sauce and stew very gently over a weak heat 1.5 hours or longer or shorter according to the age of the geese.
2. When the giblets are cooked, drain them; cut them together with the vegetables into pieces of a couple of inches length. The liver is cooked less. Arrange in a pâté mold that has been lined with farce.
3. Reduce the bouillon in which the giblets were cooked to a suitably thick sauce, from which the fat is skimmed well. Pour the sauce into the mold, cover the mold with pastry dough and bake. Serve.
