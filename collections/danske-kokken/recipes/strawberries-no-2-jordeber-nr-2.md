## Ingredients

- 1 pound pitted strawberries
- 1 pound white sugar
- 1 gill water or young wine

## Directions

1. Cook sugar into a syrup with a gill of water or young wine
2. Add the berries and cook 15 to 20 minutes with a couple of boils
3. Remove the berries and cook the juice 4 to 5 minutes
4. Pour the juice hot over the berries
5. The next day put the whole thing on the fire, and when the berries have cooked a little, strain them into a pot
6. Cook the juice until thick, skim, and pour cold over the berries
7. The next day cover with paper dipped in brandy and tie up
8. Powdered sugar may also be used for the berries
