## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- fat from the shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- twine
- butter
- carrots
- celery root
- onions
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut 2 or 3 fillet pieces from a veal shoulder, place each on a board
2. Place some of the fat from the shoulder on each fillet, sprinkle with salt and pepper, roll together and tie with twine
3. Place the fillets in a casserole with butter that has been lightly browned, together with 2 carrots, a celery root and a couple of onions
4. Chop the shoulder bones, brown them in butter and salt, and thin with bouillon
5. Use this stock to baste the fillets during cooking
6. When done and a light golden color, remove the twine and slice the fillet, but arrange it in its original form for serving
7. Thin the stock in the casserole with bouillon and thicken the sauce with flour, strain and serve with the fillet alongside browned potatoes
