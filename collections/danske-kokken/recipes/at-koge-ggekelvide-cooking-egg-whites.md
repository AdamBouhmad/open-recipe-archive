## Ingredients

- milk
- egg whites (beaten to stiff foam)
- sugar (optional)
- vanilla or cinnamon (optional)

## Directions

1. Place milk on the fire in a shallow pan; when it boils, add the egg whites, which have been beaten to stiff foam, one spoonful beside the other.
2. They need only boil a moment to become large, then remove them and place them on an inverted sieve so the milk can drain off.
3. Sugar, a piece of vanilla or cinnamon can be added to the milk, which gives the whites a good taste.
4. The milk can then be strained off and used for egg milk and the like.
