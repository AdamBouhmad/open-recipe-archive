## Ingredients

- Six egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 125 Gr (1/4 tb) cold butter
- 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) cold water
- Insignificant amount of sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Stir together six egg yolks with 1 tablespoon flour, the juice of 1 lemon, 125 Gr (1/4 tb) cold butter, 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) cold water and insignificant sugar
2. Stir continuously until the sauce comes to a boil
3. Then season it with salt and with more acid if needed
4. This sauce is eaten with boiled meat and fish dishes
