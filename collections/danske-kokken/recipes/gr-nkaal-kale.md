## Ingredients

- Grønkaal (kale)
- Saltvand (salt water)
- 3 Spiseskefulde Hvedemel (3 tablespoons wheat flour)
- 5 Potter Kjød- eller Saltmadssuppe (5 pots of meat or salt meat broth)
- Nogle pillede Løg (some peeled onions)
- Raaskrællede, kogte og ituskaarne Kartofler (raw peeled, cooked, and cut potatoes)
- Boller (meatballs), optional

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the kale with salt water and let it sit for a few hours so that any insects fall off.
2. Pick the good leaves from the stalk and rinse well in cold water.
3. If desired (especially in autumn when kale has a strong taste), blanch the leaves: bring to a boil with water, drain in a colander, and rinse with cold water until completely drained.
4. Finely chop the kale with 3 tablespoons of wheat flour.
5. Heat 5 pots of meat or salt meat broth.
6. When the broth boils, gradually thin the kale with a little of it until properly diluted.
7. Pour the kale into the boiling broth along with some peeled onions.
8. Cook for about 10 minutes.
9. Before serving, add raw peeled, cooked, and cut potatoes.
10. If desired, add meatballs as you would for meat soup.
