## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) fine barley grains
- 5 L (5 Potter) skimmed milk

## Directions

1. When the milk boils, sprinkle the grains into it while stirring.
2. After the porridge has cooked for about 2 hours, salt it and serve with butter and sugar.
3. Serve sweetened pale ale with it.
