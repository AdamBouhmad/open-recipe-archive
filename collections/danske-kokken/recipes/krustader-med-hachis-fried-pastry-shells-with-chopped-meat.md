## Ingredients

- Butter
- 1 onion, chopped
- Flour
- Stock
- Tomato purée
- Paprika
- Pickled vegetables or spiced vinegar
- Maggi bouillon cube
- Sugar
- Salt
- Wine
- Cognac
- Coloring
- Chopped cooked or fried meat

## Directions

1. Brown butter, chopped onion, and flour
2. Dilute with stock and tomato purée to a thin consistency
3. Add paprika, chopped pickled vegetables or spiced vinegar, Maggi bouillon cube, sugar, salt, wine, a little cognac, and coloring so the hachis becomes a beautiful brown color
4. Add the chopped meat (cooked or fried) last
5. Fill into warm krustader and arrange as usual
