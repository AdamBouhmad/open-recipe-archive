## Ingredients

- 65 grams (13 dram) wheat flour
- 65 grams (13 dram) grated Parmesan cheese
- 40 grams (8 dram) cold butter
- Cayenne pepper
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix flour and grated Parmesan cheese together
2. Work a little cayenne pepper into the cold butter and add to the flour mixture
3. Bring together with 1 egg
4. Roll out very thinly and cut into pieces the size of a crown coin
5. Place on a buttered baking sheet
6. Bake until golden brown
7. Arrange cooled on a platter lined with parchment paper
