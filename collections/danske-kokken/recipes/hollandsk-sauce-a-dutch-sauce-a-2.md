## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- a little butter
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- butter for finishing

## Directions

1. Bake 1 tablespoon flour with a little butter and dilute with water.
2. Mix in vinegar and salt and stir in butter, but do not allow to boil.
3. For 19 portions use 1 pound butter and half a tablespoon flour.
