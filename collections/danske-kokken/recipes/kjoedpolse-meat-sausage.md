## Ingredients

- 12 pounds beef
- 4 pounds chopped suet
- Salt
- Seasonings
- Strong pepper
- Ginger
- 1/2 quart milk

## Directions

1. Cut and mince the beef finely.
2. Cut the suet into fine cubes, season with ground pepper and a little ginger.
3. Mix everything well and work for a good half hour.
4. Add the milk (which should be boiled the day before) gradually while working the mixture for another half hour.
5. Season with salt to taste.
6. Stuff the sausage fairly firmly and salt it in a tub.
