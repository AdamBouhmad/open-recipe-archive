## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 lb) flour
- 375 gr (0.75 lb) margarine
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon hartshorn salt, dissolved in a little water

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, margarine, powdered sugar, eggs, and hartshorn salt solution together
2. Roll into very thin sticks
3. Twist into snail shapes, about the size of a two-crown coin
4. Place slightly apart on a baking sheet
5. Brush with egg and sprinkle with either chopped almonds or chopped candied citrus peel
