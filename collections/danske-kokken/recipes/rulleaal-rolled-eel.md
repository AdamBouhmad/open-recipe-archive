## Ingredients

- one very large eel
- salt
- pepper
- allspice
- whole pepper
- bay leaves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Skin the eel, cut off the head and tail, and clip off the fins with scissors
2. Cut the eel lengthwise along the back and remove the bone
3. Rinse it well and dry it in a fish cloth
4. Sprinkle it with salt, pepper and allspice and roll it tightly together from the tail
5. Bind it with yarn and sew it into a thin cloth
6. Place it over the fire in cold water with salt, whole pepper, bay leaves and vinegar
7. When cooked, remove it and place it in a press like rolled sausage
8. When the eel is cold, remove the bands, cut it into slices and place on a platter
9. Pour aspic (see recipe No. 247), made from the broth in which the eel was cooked, over it
10. Garnish the dish with lemon and parsley
