## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 10 tablespoons milk or cream
- salt
- egg whites, stiffly beaten
- butter
- well-prepared hachis
- turtle ragout, forlorn or authentic
- kidney ragout
- calf's sweetbread ragout
- leftover goulash

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks well
2. Stir in 10 tablespoons milk or cream and a small amount of salt
3. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Place a good slice of butter on the pan and when it boils, pour in the eggs
5. With a fork or knife, lightly prick the eggs and constantly shake the pan so the omelet does not burn or form a hard crust
6. Bake the omelet only on one side
7. Place a well-prepared hachis in the middle of the omelet, ensuring it is not too thin
8. Alternatively, place warmed leftover turtle, kidney ragout, calf's sweetbread ragout, or goulash in the omelet
9. Do not serve with additional sauce
10. Fold together and serve immediately
