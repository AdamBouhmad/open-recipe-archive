## Ingredients

- Beef (Thick steak, tenderloin, or thin steak)
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. When purchasing, allow 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) per person.
2. Beat the meat well with a meat mallet before rinsing.
3. Place it in the roasting pan and put it in the hot oven for a few minutes before pouring boiling water with some salt into the pan.
4. This way the pores contract, and the meat becomes juicier.
5. When placing the meat in the pan, turn the tenderloin upward, i.e., with the outer fillet (the most meaty side) down toward the pan.
6. It must be basted frequently during roasting, which must proceed very evenly.
7. A beef roast (thick steak) of 5 to 6 kilograms (10 to 12 pounds) needs 3 hours of roasting and is still juicy and red in the meat without being bloody.
8. Half an hour before serving, skim most of the gravy from the meat, and remove the fat, while pouring a little more water onto the pan to prevent the meat from browning too much.
