## Ingredients

- 2 pots of pressed juice
- 1 measure of wine vinegar
- 1.5 pounds of white sugar

## Directions

1. Pour the juice into a glass vessel, add the vinegar, and let the mixture stand for 2 days so impurities can settle to the bottom
2. Filter the clear liquid and add the white sugar, which is dissolved over a gentle fire
3. Give it one boil and skim it
4. When the juice is cold, fill it into bottles and cork them well
