## Ingredients

- ½ kg dried apples
- 5 L water
- Whole cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Rice flour
- Sherry

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, soak them overnight and cook together with whole cinnamon and lemon peel in the water in which they have been standing.
2. When the apples are completely tender, pour them into a coarse sieve and press through.
3. Thicken the soup with a little rice flour stirred in water, sweeten and prepare with sherry.
4. Serve with rusks, baked bread or hardtack.
