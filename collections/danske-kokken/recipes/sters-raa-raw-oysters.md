## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Serve raw oysters with lemon slices.
2. They look best when they stay on the flat shell when opened, but the liquid is better when left in the deep shell.
3. They must be fresh; this is known by the shells being tightly closed.
4. They are only good in winter.
