## Ingredients

- Svineryg (pork back) or other pork or salt meat, 8 til 10 Pd.
- Vand (water)
- Et fast Hvidkaalshoved (a firm head of white cabbage)
- Gulerødder (carrots)
- Persillerødder (parsley roots)
- En Selleri (celery)
- 20 hele Peberkorn (20 whole peppercorns)
- En lille Spiseskefuld stødt Kommen (a small tablespoon of ground caraway)
- Raaskrællede, kogte og ituskaarne Kartofler (raw peeled, cooked, and cut potatoes)
- Salt

## Directions

1. Break the pork back at the joints before cooking.
2. Place 8 to 10 pounds of pork in a pot with enough water to cover it well.
3. Remove the outer leaves and tough stalk from the cabbage head; cut the rest into fine strips.
4. When the broth has boiled and been skimmed, add the cabbage strips along with carrots, parsley roots, and celery.
5. Place 20 whole peppercorns and a small tablespoon of ground caraway in a small tied linen bag and add to the soup.
6. Remove the bag before serving.
7. When the cabbage and vegetables are tender, remove them and cut the vegetables in pieces.
8. Strain the broth and bring to a boil again.
9. Pour into a tureen with the cabbage, vegetables, and raw peeled, cooked, and cut potatoes.
10. Sprinkle the pork back with a little salt and serve alongside the soup.
