## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloin
- Parsley
- Cold butter
- Liquid for braising
- Flour
- Food coloring
- Cream
- Browned potatoes
- Cucumber or tomato salad

## Directions

1. Slit the pork tenderloin lengthwise and place parsley and a little cold butter inside as for chicken.
2. Bind or sew the meat lightly together.
3. Brown it, pour liquid over it and roast until tender, then remove the strings.
4. Make the sauce from butter, flour, and the strained gravy.
5. When serving, a piece of cold butter or a little cream can be added to the sauce, and it is given a nice brown color with food coloring.
6. Serve with browned potatoes and cucumber or tomato salad.
