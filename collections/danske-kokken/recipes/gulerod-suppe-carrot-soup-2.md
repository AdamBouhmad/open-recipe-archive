## Ingredients

- 10 carrots
- 3 onions
- 5 L (5 pots) soup
- 2 L (2 pots) soup
- A good bunch of herbs
- Salt
- Ground white pepper
- 100 grams (20 quintal) butter
- 100 grams (20 quintal) flour
- Bread croutons

## Directions

1. Scrape, rinse, and slice the carrots along with the onions.
2. Place over heat with 2 L (2 pots) of the soup, a good bunch of herbs, salt, and ground white pepper.
3. Cook until tender enough to easily pass through a coarse sieve.
4. Brown 100 grams butter and 100 grams flour together and dilute with the strained purée.
5. Bring to a boil.
6. Serve with bread croutons.
