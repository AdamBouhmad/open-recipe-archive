## Ingredients

- Pickled mushroom trimmings
- Salt
- 1½ liters (1½ pots) mushroom liquid
- 10 chopped anchovies
- 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
- ¼ ounce ground cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- ½ kg (1 pound) darkly browned sugar
- ½ bottle red wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the mushroom trimmings well and chop finely.
2. Sprinkle with salt and let stand under a press for 3 to 4 days.
3. Strain the liquid through a cloth.
4. Take 10 chopped anchovies, 1 teaspoon ground white pepper, ¼ ounce ground cloves, 2 bay leaves, and ½ kg darkly browned sugar.
5. Cook all of this and skim it before pouring in ½ bottle of red wine.
6. Once it has boiled for at least a quarter hour after the wine is added, strain it and pour into dry bottles, which should be corked when cold.
7. This soya is very tasty in various brown, piquant sauces and in many meat dishes.
