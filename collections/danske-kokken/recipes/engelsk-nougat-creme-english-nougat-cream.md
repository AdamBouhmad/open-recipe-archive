## Ingredients

- 175 Gr (35 Kvint) scalded almonds
- 250 Gr (1/2 S) powdered sugar
- 4 tablespoons water
- a little butter
- vanilla cream as Nr. 1000
- cream foam for decoration
- candied fruit crowns for decoration

## Directions

1. Cut the scalded almonds lengthwise into fine strips and place in a saucepan together with the powdered sugar, water, and a little butter.
2. While it stands over the fire, stir carefully so that the almonds do not break apart or burn.
3. Once the sugar is sufficiently brown like caramel sugar, remove from the fire and quickly place in small lumps on a buttered or oiled tray.
4. When the nougat is hard, crush it and stir it into a vanilla cream as Nr. 1000.
5. Decorate with cream foam and candied fruit crowns.
