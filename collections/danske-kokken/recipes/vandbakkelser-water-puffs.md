## Ingredients

- 250 grams margarine
- 250 grams Pillsbury flour
- 500 milliliters boiling water
- 10 egg yolks
- egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Melt the margarine and bake the flour in it
2. Add the boiling water gradually
3. When cooled, stir well with egg yolks
4. Finally add the stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Place the dough with a teaspoon on a cold baking sheet, not too close together
6. Bake in a very hot oven for approximately half an hour
7. Make a small opening in the cakes with a knife and fill with cream or whipped cream, which can be flavored with various things such as chopped pineapple, candied ginger, pineapple, vanilla, or a liqueur
8. Jam is served separately if used
9. Alternatively, the dough can be filled into a pastry bag and piped into oblong cakes, which can also be filled with whipped cream
10. For small water puffs for soup, place the dough on the sheet in very small drops the size of a large nut and bake quickly
