## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- lemon zest, grated
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Whip 4 egg whites to as stiff peaks as possible
2. Stir in grated lemon zest
3. Add 6 tablespoons sugar, one at a time
4. Place greased paper on the baking sheets and sprinkle with sugar
5. Set small cakes on this using a teaspoon, twice the size of a walnut
6. Space them a finger's width apart
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
8. Bake at even heat
