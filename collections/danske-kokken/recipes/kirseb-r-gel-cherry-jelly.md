## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cherries (sour cooking cherries or a mixture of sour and sweet)
- 625 gr sugar
- 1 1/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Either sour cooking cherries may be used, or a mixture of sour and sweet berries.
2. Pick the cherries from their stems and pound in a mortar so the pits are crushed.
3. Pour the mass into the jelly cloth, where it remains overnight.
4. The next day, weigh it and cook into jelly as mentioned in No. 93.
5. The refuse from this as well as from the following jelly must be pressed through a coarse sieve before it can be used for marmalade.
