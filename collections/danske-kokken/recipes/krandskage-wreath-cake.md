## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds sweet almonds
- 2 ounces bitter almonds
- 1.5 pounds powdered sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Fold together the sweet and bitter almonds
2. Toast and pound them
3. Stir the almonds with 1.5 pounds powdered sugar, lemon zest, and egg whites that have been whipped to stiff peaks
4. Form rings from the dough, each one smaller than the last so they can be stacked together when baked
5. Bake at an even temperature until light golden
6. If making small wreaths, this yields approximately 40 pieces
