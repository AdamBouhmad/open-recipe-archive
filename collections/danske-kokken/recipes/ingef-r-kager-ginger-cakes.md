## Ingredients

- 125 grams (1/4 lb) margarine
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) flour
- 375 grams (1/4 lb) icing sugar
- 3 eggs
- 30 grams (6 quintal) ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon baking soda, dissolved in 2 tablespoons milk
- candied orange peel

## Directions

1. Crumble together margarine, flour and icing sugar
2. Mix in the 3 eggs along with ground ginger and baking soda dissolved in milk
3. Roll the cakes thinly and cut out with a glass or mold
4. Top with a small piece of candied orange peel
5. Place on buttered sheets and bake in a well-heated oven
