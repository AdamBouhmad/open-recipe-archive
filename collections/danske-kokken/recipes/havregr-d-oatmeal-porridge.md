## Ingredients

- 1½ kg oats
- approximately 4 L water
- salt

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the oats into the boiling water, add a little salt and cook covered over low heat for about half an hour.
2. The oats can also soak overnight and be cooked in the morning.
3. Serve with fruit juice or red wine and water, or with sweet, semi-skimmed or buttermilk.
