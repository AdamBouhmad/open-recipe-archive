## Ingredients

- peeled, hollowed, and cooked pumpkin
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter or Palmin
- parsley for garnish
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked pumpkin into slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in egg and breadcrumbs
4. Fry on a pan in butter or Palmin
5. Garnish with parsley
6. Serve with melted butter
