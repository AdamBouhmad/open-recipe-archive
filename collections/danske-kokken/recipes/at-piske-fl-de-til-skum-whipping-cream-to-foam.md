## Ingredients

- cream (preferably cooked)
- lemon juice or vinegar (optional)
- ground vanilla or vanilla extract (optional)
- white sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. The cream should preferably be cooked, as long as it is good, because thin cream cannot become foam.
2. Whip it in a roomy pan, especially in warm weather, place it in cold water both before and while the cream is whipped.
3. If it still does not become foam, a little lemon juice or vinegar can be whipped with the cream.
4. If it does not become entirely foam throughout, let it stand still, take the foam from the top and place it on an inverted sieve so the liquid can drain off; whip this again along with the remaining cream; when it has become foam again, place it on the sieve again and so forth.
5. Ground vanilla or a few drops of it, when dissolved in spirits, and white sugar can be whipped with it.
