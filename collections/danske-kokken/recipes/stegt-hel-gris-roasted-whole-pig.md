## Ingredients

- one pig aged 4 to 6 weeks
- ground pepper
- salt
- butter or bacon fat
- bouillon
- apples
- prunes
- flour
- cold butter

## Directions

1. Wash, scald and scrape the pig.
2. Remove the eyes and insert a wooden spoon in their place.
3. Open the mouth slightly and keep it open during roasting with a stick.
4. Rub the inside with ground pepper and salt, the outside with salt.
5. Bend the legs under the pig.
6. Fill it with wooden spoons and chopped apples.
7. Sew it together.
8. Make the oven very hot.
9. When the pig has roasted about a quarter hour or has begun to heat, rub the skin well with butter or preferably with bacon fat or fat bacon rind.
10. Put a little bouillon in the bottom of the pan.
11. Roast for approximately 14 hours, regularly basting and rubbing the skin as described.
12. At serving, place a lemon in the mouth after removing the stick.
13. Brown the pan sauce with flour and strain it; finally stir a little cold butter into it, and serve this sauce with the pig, which is placed whole on the table after removing the thread.
