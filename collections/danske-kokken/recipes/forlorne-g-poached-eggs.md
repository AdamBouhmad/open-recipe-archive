## Ingredients

- fresh eggs
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Fresh eggs are cracked on the edge of a plate and placed in a pan with boiling water, which stands over the heat.
2. You must not put too many in at once, and if you want the yolk clear, there must not be so much water in the pan that it stands over the eggs.
3. They are loosened with a knife, taken up and wiped off.
4. For breakfast, they can be served with anchovies, one of which is laid around the yolk on each egg, after the head and backbone have first been removed, or gravy can be laid over them in strips.
5. If the eggs are not newly laid, add a little vinegar to the water.
