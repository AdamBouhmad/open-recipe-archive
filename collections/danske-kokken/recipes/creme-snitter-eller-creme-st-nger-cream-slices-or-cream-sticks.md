## Ingredients

- 375 Gr (1/4 S) Flour
- Cream (made from 3 1/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) semi-skimmed milk, 2 egg yolks, stirred with 1 tablespoon powdered sugar and 1 tablespoon cornstarch)
- chopped almonds or candied citrus peel and raisins
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Roll out puff pastry made from 375 Gr (1/4 S) flour thinly on a large baking sheet
2. Cut the dough into strips 1/4 quarter inch wide
3. Brush each strip with a thin layer of cream
4. Sprinkle with chopped almonds or candied citrus peel and raisins and cover with a thin layer of dough
5. Brush with egg and sprinkle with chopped almonds
6. Bake at strong heat
7. Cut into appropriate pieces when cooled
8. The cream is made from 3 1/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) semi-skimmed milk, 2 egg yolks, stirred with 1 tablespoon powdered sugar and 1 tablespoon cornstarch
