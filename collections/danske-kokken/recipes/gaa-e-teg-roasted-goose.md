## Ingredients

- One goose
- Apples and good prunes
- Or: Eggs, bread and milk
- Or: Salt, pepper and anchovies
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. The best filling for a goose is apples and good prunes, which gives it the most pleasant taste, but others prepare a dough of eggs, bread and milk and fill with that.
2. You can also simply rub it inside with salt and pepper and then place a couple of anchovies inside; these are consumed entirely during roasting, but they give the goose a fresh and piquant taste.
3. Set it in the oven with water over it, which soon becomes fat, with which the goose is often basted.
4. It requires two to three hours of roasting depending on the condition of the oven and the size of the goose.
5. For sauce, sharp red wine sauce can be used, but in general a brown sauce prepared in the usual manner is used.
6. The fat is well skimmed off before the sauce is made.
