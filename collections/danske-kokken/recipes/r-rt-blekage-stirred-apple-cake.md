## Ingredients

- 1.5 Pounds cold cooked potatoes
- 3 Pounds butter
- 8 Egg yolks
- 0.5 Pound powdered sugar (or white farina)
- 1 Lemon, juice and grated peel
- 2 Deep plates peeled apples, without cores, cut into pieces
- 8 Egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Grate the cold cooked potatoes and bake them in 3 pounds butter.
2. Beat the 8 egg yolks well with 0.5 pound powdered sugar (or white farina), the lemon juice, and grated lemon peel.
3. When well mixed, add the potatoes together with the 2 deep plates of peeled, cored, and cut apples.
4. Mix the whole together.
5. Finally, stir in the 8 whipped egg whites.
6. Bake the cake for one hour in a form with removable bottom, or even better in an omelet pan.
7. Serve sprinkled with powdered sugar.
