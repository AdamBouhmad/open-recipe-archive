## Ingredients

- fat, butter or margarine
- 1 fairly large cabbage head
- potatoes
- 1.5 kg meat (lamb breast, beef brisket, or lightly salted striped pork)
- ground pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. On the bottom of a pot place a little fat, butter or margarine.
2. A fairly large cabbage head is cut very coarsely, and the bottom of the pot is covered with some of this cabbage.
3. Thereon is laid a layer of raw, peeled potatoes cut into 2-4 pieces.
4. 1.5 kg meat is cut into smaller pieces; this can be either lamb breast, fat beef brisket or lightly salted striped pork cut into slices.
5. The meat is laid over the potatoes, sprinkled with ground pepper, and for fresh meat also with salt.
6. The meat is covered again with potatoes, and on top cabbage is laid.
7. Melted butter is poured over, the amount depending on the fattiness of the meat.
8. The lid is placed on the pot and must close well, since the dish must be softened by steam.
9. The pot, which must be shaken regularly during cooking, which lasts 3 hours, is placed over low heat to prevent the cabbage from burning on.
10. Meat and cabbage are arranged together on a dish.
