## Ingredients

- cold fried herring
- onion slices
- white pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- sugar
- meat or fish stock

## Directions

1. Layer cold fried herring with onion slices, white pepper, salt, vinegar, a little sugar and meat or fish stock
2. In this marinade the herring can keep for several days
3. Serve as breakfast dish
