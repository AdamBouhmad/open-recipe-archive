## Ingredients

- A couple of egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar
- a little wine
- salt
- pepper
- watercress

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks with oil, vinegar, a little wine, salt and pepper
2. Clean the watercress well and pour the sauce over it
