## Ingredients

- 375 g butter or margarine
- 1/2 kg flour
- 1/4 L cold water
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- cooked apple pieces or raw peeled apples
- powdered sugar
- egg white for brushing
- egg for brushing decoration

## Directions

1. Make a pastry dough as for butter dough, using the specified amounts of butter, flour, water and baking powder
2. Roll out the dough 3 times, handling it as little as possible with the hands but working it with a broad knife
3. Roll out a very thin sheet of dough and line a buttered form with it
4. Lay cooked apple pieces or raw peeled apples in layers with powdered sugar in the lined form until almost full
5. Lay another sheet over and press the edges together; this can be crimped with the fingers
6. Roll out the remaining dough and cut into strips or shapes; arrange these over the pie, brushed with egg white; brush the decoration with egg as well
7. Bake for 1 hour at even top and bottom heat
8. All kinds of fruit can be used
