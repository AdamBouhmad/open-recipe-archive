## Ingredients

- Skimmings from the roast
- Butter
- Finely chopped shallots
- Soy sauce
- Mushroom slices
- Mushroom juice

## Directions

1. Take as much skimmings from the roast as possible by pouring everything out of the roasting pan or pot when the sauce has become strong
2. Add a small piece of new butter
3. When the poured-out sauce has stood for a while, skim off all the fat from it and pour it back on the roast so that only the strength remains
4. Place this in a pan with finely chopped shallots and cook well
5. When ready to serve, remove the pan from heat and stir in a good piece of cold butter
6. This makes the sauce smooth; add a little soy sauce for color
7. You can also add strips of mushrooms and a little mushroom juice
8. This is good for all roasts
