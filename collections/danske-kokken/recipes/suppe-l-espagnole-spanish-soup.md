## Ingredients

- 1/4 Kg (1 Sj) pearl onions
- 75 Gr (15 Quintal) Butter
- 1 Dessertspoonful Powdered sugar
- Soup
- Paprika
- Wine
- 1/2 Kg (1 tt) fish forcemeat
- Parsley or watercress, chopped

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the onions
2. Brown 75 Gr (15 Quintal) butter, and shake the onions in it until they begin to brown
3. Sprinkle 1 dessertspoonful of powdered sugar over them and shake the onions in it until they are glazed
4. Cook them tender in a little of the soup, which is later mixed with the remainder
5. Just before serving, season with paprika and wine
6. Place small balls of 1/2 Kg (1 tt) fish forcemeat, mixed with chopped parsley or watercress, on top of the soup
