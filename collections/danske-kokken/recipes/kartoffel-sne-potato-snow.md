## Ingredients

- Very large, floury potatoes
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Boil very large, floury potatoes in salted water. As soon as they are tender, drain the water and keep the pot warm while peeling the potatoes and then pressing them through a purée sieve or grating over a very hot serving dish. Serve the potato snow as quickly as possible, and it must not be touched after it has been pressed through the sieve or grated on the grater, as handling it will cause it to lose its light appearance. Serve with spiced meat dishes such as breaded meat, goulash, boneless birds, etc.
