## Ingredients

- 20 apples
- wine
- sugar
- water
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 8 eggs
- 8 rusks

## Directions

1. Peel, slice, and cook 20 apples to a pulp with wine, a little sugar, water, cinnamon, and lemon zest
2. Whip 8 whole eggs well with sugar
3. Mix the whipped eggs with 8 crushed rusks and the hot apple pulp
4. Bake slowly, being careful to avoid too strong an oven heat
