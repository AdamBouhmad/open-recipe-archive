## Ingredients

- 1 kg powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg raspberries
- 3/4 kg currants

## Directions

1. Remove stems from the raspberries and currants.
2. Layer them in the pot alternately with the sugar.
3. Place over low heat until the berries begin to release juice.
4. Skim well and boil for 3/4 hour.
5. Pour into jars and tie them up immediately.
