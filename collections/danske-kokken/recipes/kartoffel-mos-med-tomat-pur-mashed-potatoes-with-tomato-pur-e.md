## Ingredients

- Mashed potatoes
- Tomato purée
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Mix the mashed potatoes with tomato purée instead of milk.
2. Add salt and pepper as the vegetable requires.
3. Serve with fried fish dishes.
