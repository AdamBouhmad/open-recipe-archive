## Ingredients

- Soaked and well-dried kidneys
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread
- Claret or Palmin
- Warm potato salad

## Directions

1. Cut the soaked, well-dried kidneys into slices across the width
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in egg and bread
4. Fry in Claret or Palmin
5. Arrange around warm potato salad in the middle of a platter
