## Ingredients

- ripe fruit
- water

## Directions

1. Scald the ripe fruit for a few minutes in boiling water until the skin can be easily removed.
2. Split the apricots and remove the stones.
3. Place the fruit in the pot after the bottom has first been covered with water.
4. Cook the jam as previously described, pour it out and cover with paper, then seal.
5. Alternatively, split the fruit, remove the stones and cook the fruit completely; but first the bottom of the pot must be covered with water.
6. Pass the mass through a coarse sieve, then pour it back into the pot to reduce under continuous stirring as previously explained.
7. What remains in the sieve can be covered with water, boiled and used in fruit soup.
