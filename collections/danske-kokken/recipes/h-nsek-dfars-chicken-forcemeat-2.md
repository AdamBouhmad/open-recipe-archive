## Ingredients

- Chicken breast meat
- 125 grams cold butter or margarine per 1/2 kg meat

## Directions

1. Take only the breast meat from chicken
2. Chop and pound the meat with cold butter or margarine (125 grams per 1/2 kg meat)
3. Treat and handle as for beef and veal forcemeat
4. The remaining parts of the chicken can be used for soup, chicken salad, various fillings, or fricassée
