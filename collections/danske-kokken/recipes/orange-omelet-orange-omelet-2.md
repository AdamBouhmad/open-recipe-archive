## Ingredients

- juice from one orange
- zest of one orange
- yolks from 3 eggs
- zest and juice from orange
- two tablespoons powdered sugar
- egg whites
- a piece of butter the size of a small walnut

## Directions

1. Press the juice from an orange and grate the yellow zest
2. Beat the yolks of 3 eggs white and add the zest and juice along with two tablespoons powdered sugar
3. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks
4. Heat a pan over the fire and when hot, add a piece of butter the size of a small walnut
5. Pour the omelet batter into the pan and fry, constantly lifting from the bottom with a fork and heaping toward the center
6. When it seems done, slide it to one side of the pan, hold a warm plate against it and quickly slide the omelet onto it
7. Sprinkle with fine sugar and serve hot
