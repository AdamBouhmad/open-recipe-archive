## Ingredients

- 1 liter (1 pot) heavy cream
- 5 sheets isinglass
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) boiling water
- a little vanilla essence
- jam (preserved fruit)

## Directions

1. Whip the heavy cream into stiff foam.
2. Dissolve the isinglass in boiling water and stir into the cream foam along with a little vanilla essence.
3. Layer the whipped cream alternately with jam in a glass bowl or a mold from which the pudding can be turned out.
4. Make the same day it is to be eaten.
