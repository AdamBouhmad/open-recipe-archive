## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 3 pounds smoked ham
- 5 onions
- 1 celery
- 1 leek, all cut into pieces
- 1 tablespoon peppercorns
- A little cloves
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 pound flour
- 4 to 5 quarts turtle bouillon
- 1 flask rum
- 1 flask Madeira or sherry
- Salt
- Sugar
- Turtle meat
- Fish balls
- Egg balls

## Directions

1. Brown 1 pound butter in a casserole with 3 pounds smoked ham, 5 onions, 1 celery, 1 leek, all cut into pieces, and 1 tablespoon peppercorns, a little cloves, thyme, marjoram and 2 bay leaves.
2. Blend in 1 pound flour.
3. When well blended, add 4 to 5 quarts of turtle bouillon, 1 flask rum, 1 flask Madeira or sherry.
4. Stir over the fire until it boils, then set aside to cook slowly for 1 hour and skim well.
5. Season with salt and sugar to taste and strain into a casserole.
6. Cut the turtle meat into suitable pieces and heat in the strained soup.
7. Garnish with fish balls and egg balls.
