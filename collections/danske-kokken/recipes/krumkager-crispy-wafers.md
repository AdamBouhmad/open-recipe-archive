## Ingredients

- ½ pound butter
- ½ pound powdered sugar
- ¾ pound wheat flour
- 3 egg whites, whipped to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and remove the salt
2. Stir well
3. Stir in ½ pound powdered sugar
4. Stir in ¾ pound wheat flour
5. Finally fold in 3 egg whites whipped to stiff peaks
6. Bake the cakes light brown
