## Ingredients

- half a pound butter
- 1 pound wheat flour
- 4 pots boiling milk
- salt to taste
- ground cinnamon
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Bake half a pound of butter and 1 pound of wheat flour together until it leaves the spoon
2. Gradually thin it with 4 pots of boiling milk, letting the porridge boil between each addition
3. Stir well during thinning to prevent lumps
4. Salt at the end
5. Serve immediately sprinkled with ground cinnamon and powdered sugar, as it becomes long and stringy if it stands
