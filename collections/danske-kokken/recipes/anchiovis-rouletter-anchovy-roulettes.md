## Ingredients

- 20 hard-boiled egg yolks
- softened butter
- 20 anchovies
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Mash 20 hard-boiled egg yolks with softened butter, then take the scraped meat of 20 anchovies, chop it finely, and stir it in. When it is cold, form roulettes from it in the usual manner as in No. 203. When serving, garnish the platter with curly parsley.
