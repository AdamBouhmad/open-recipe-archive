## Ingredients

- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) gravy sauce
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Sago flour
- Paprika
- Salt
- Madeira
- Mushrooms, truffles, olives, or chopped chestnuts (for variations)

## Directions

1. Prepare a very strong, clear, brown gravy, which can be further improved with Maggi bouillon cubes if necessary
2. Thicken with a little stirred sago flour and season with paprika, salt and Madeira
3. This gravy can be varied with different additions, after which it takes its name: Madeira Gravy with Mushrooms, Truffles, Olives, or Chestnuts
4. These additions are brought to a boil in the gravy
5. Can be served with veal and beef filet, with cutlets, and with roasted and boiled poultry
