## Ingredients

- 1½ pints water
- 1 pound sugar
- Zest of 2 lemons
- Juice of 6 lemons
- 2 egg whites
- Alternative: 2 tablespoons thick cream foam

## Directions

1. Boil 1½ pints water and 1 pound sugar and skim.
2. Add the finely grated zest of 2 and the strained juice of 6 lemons.
3. When cold, stir in 2 egg whites beaten until stiff, then freeze.
4. Instead of egg whites, you can also add 2 tablespoons thick cream foam.
