## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg red currants
- 625 gr sugar
- 1.25 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice

## Directions

1. For this jelly only the green leaves are removed from the berries, after which the currants are placed in a pot set over low heat.
2. Stir regularly until the currants begin to burst, then remove the pot from heat and crush the berries with a spoon before pouring into the cloth.
3. What has run through the next day is weighed and treated as the preceding varieties of jelly. (See Cooking of sugar for jelly No. 93.)
4. The currant juice must likewise cook for only 8 minutes, and is skimmed during cooking.
5. What remains in the cloth is boiled in water and used for fruit soup and the like.
