## Ingredients

- Large prunes
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Lemon peel
- Sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Select the largest and best prunes.
2. Cook them in barely enough water until soft; then add lemon juice and peel, sugar and a very little butter.
3. The stones can be removed when the prunes are cooked.
4. You can be satisfied with adding only sugar.
