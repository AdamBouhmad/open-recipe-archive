## Ingredients

- Very large, uniform potatoes
- Kitchen salt
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Brush very large, uniform potatoes clean in lukewarm water, dry in a coarse cloth and place on a baking sheet, covered with a layer of kitchen salt. Bake for 1 hour in a hot oven, turning them occasionally. Arrange them as hot as possible in a napkin on a dish. Serve with cold or softened butter. Serve with salt and pepper. Eat with a spoon from the skin.
