## Ingredients

- chicken breast
- butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 shallot
- 2 spoonfuls wheat flour
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Chop the breast meat very finely and pound in a mortar together with butter, eggs, shallot, wheat flour, and salt and pepper to taste until it becomes appropriately cohesive and spongy
2. Stir in a little sweet cream, a little at a time
3. Form small balls to be poached in soup
