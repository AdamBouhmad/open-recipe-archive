## Ingredients

- 12 ounces good rice
- wheat flour
- lukewarm water
- salt
- 2 cents yeast

## Directions

1. Boil 12 ounces good rice in a linen cloth or bag, ensuring sufficient room for it to swell.
2. Cook for 3 to 4 hours or until it becomes a complete dough.
3. While still warm, blend with 7 pounds flour, as much lukewarm water as necessary, a little salt, and 2 cents yeast.
4. Knead the dough well and set to rise in warmth.
5. Divide into loaves, knead and handle again in the usual manner.
