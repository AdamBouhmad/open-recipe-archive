## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 handfuls chervil
- 5 pots bouillon made from veal or lamb meat
- 1/4 pound butter

## Directions

1. Clean and finely chop 3 to 4 handfuls of chervil.
2. Place 5 pots of bouillon, which can be made from veal or lamb meat, over heat and skim it.
3. Place 1/4 pound butter in a casserole and bake for approximately a quarter hour, then gradually thin the roux.
