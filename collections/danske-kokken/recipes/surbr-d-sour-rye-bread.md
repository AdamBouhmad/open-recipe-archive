## Ingredients

- 50 Gr (10 Kvint) yeast
- 1/2 Kg (1 S) rye sieve flour
- 1/4 L (3 Pægle) semi-skimmed milk
- salt
- 1/2 Kg (1 S) rye sieve flour
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Crumble yeast into 1/2 Kg (1 S) rye sieve flour
2. Stir in 1/4 L (3 Pægle) semi-skimmed milk and a little salt
3. Let the dough rise for 3-4 hours
4. Knead with 1/2 Kg (1 S) rye sieve flour
5. Form into 2-3 loaves, which rise again
6. Brush with milk or egg and sprinkle with caraway seeds
7. Bake well for 1/4 hour
