## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 0.5 pound fine Hungarian flour
- Zest of 1 large lemon
- Juice of 1 large lemon
- Egg whites
- White wine cream
- Sugar glaze

## Directions

1. Stir 1 pound butter to a salve and add 1 pound powdered sugar, stirring for a good half hour until white
2. Stir for half an hour with 8 egg yolks until quite light
3. Add 1/2 pound fine Hungarian flour and the grated yellow zest and strained juice of 1 large lemon
4. Finally add the egg whites after whipping to stiff peaks
5. Roll out this batter and bake into 8 sheets, either in 8 special forms or in 8 paper forms; in both cases butter the forms and the oven should be very hot
6. When the layers are baked a fine pale brown, remove from the oven and set to cool
7. The given amount is for 2 cakes, as each uses 4 layers, between which white wine cream is placed (vanilla cream easily becomes sour)
8. Decorate on top with sugar glaze
