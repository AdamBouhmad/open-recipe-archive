## Ingredients

- 5 to 6 large rhubarb stalks
- 4.5 to 5 L water
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- sugar
- potato flour
- egg yolks
- sago grains

## Directions

1. Cut rhubarb stalks in pieces, rinse and cook in water with lemon peel and a little cinnamon until cooked through
2. Strain and bring to boil again
3. Thicken with potato flour mixed with water
4. For improved flavor, beat sugar with 3 egg yolks and pour into soup while stirring constantly
5. Can also cook sago grains in the soup by beating sugar with 3 egg yolks
