## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Stock or soup
- Sherry
- Salt
- Paprika
- Coloring
- Sliced pork roast
- Cooked rice, loose
- Onion slices, browned in butter

## Directions

1. Make a sauce with browned butter and flour, lighten with stock or soup.
2. Add Sherry, salt, paprika and coloring to the sauce.
3. Warm slices of pork roast in this sauce.
4. Arrange the meat on a flat dish and surround it with a border of loose cooked rice.
5. Sprinkle the rice with onion slices browned in butter or with mashed potatoes baked on the dish.
