## Ingredients

- Thin, fine sugar peas
- Salt
- Sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Rub the peas, rinse them, and tie them in bundles.
2. Place the bundles in boiling water with salt and sugar and cook until very tender.
3. Arrange them as hot as possible on a napkin placed on a hot dish.
4. Serve with stirred butter.
