## Ingredients

- Fatty, good pickled herring
- Sweet milk
- Fine oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Chopped shallot
- Cooked beets
- Carrots
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Skin and bone the herring
2. Soak in sweet milk for one hour, strain and dry on a cloth
3. Cut into very small cubes and dress with fine oil, vinegar, pepper, and chopped shallot (though some dislike onion or oil, in which case use only vinegar and pepper)
4. For garnish, use cooked beets, carrots, and eggs, all cut into fine cubes and mixed with the salad if desired
5. Arrange garnishes on top in points or stripes, with each item separate
6. Chop hard-boiled egg whites and yolks separately, not finely
7. Can arrange alternating rows of egg white and beets, with egg yolk and carrots between, and can also show a stripe of herring
