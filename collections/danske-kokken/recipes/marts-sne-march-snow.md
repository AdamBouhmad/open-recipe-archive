## Ingredients

- 1 Quart sweet cream
- 6 Ounces fine sugar
- Zest and juice of 2 lemons
- 1.5 Ounces gelatine, dissolved in warm water

## Directions

1. Whip the sweet cream well with the fine sugar, lemon zest, and lemon juice
2. Add the gelatine dissolved in warm water
3. Do not cook, but allow to set
