## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloin
- Milk
- Food coloring
- Butter
- Flour
- Browned potatoes
- Redcurrant or lingonberry jelly or apple sauce

## Directions

1. Lard the pork tenderloin, brown it and drip milk mixed with food coloring over it.
2. Make the sauce from butter, flour, and the strained gravy.
3. Cut the meat into suitable pieces and arrange them together on the platter.
4. Coat with sauce and garnish with browned potatoes.
5. Serve with redcurrant or lingonberry jelly or apple sauce.
