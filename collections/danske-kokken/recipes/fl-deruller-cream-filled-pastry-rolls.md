## Ingredients

- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) Flour
- 3/4 L (3/4 Pot) Cream, whipped
- Butter dough (see Nr. 22)
- Egg
- Powdered sugar (Melis)
- Maraschino liqueur
- Pineapple, finely chopped
- Chocolate, dissolved

## Directions

1. Roll out butter dough as thinly as possible and cut into strips 2.5 centimeters (1 inch) wide.
2. Thin metal tubes of the oven's length can be ordered from the tinsmith; spiral the butter dough strips around these in 8 centimeters (3 inches) length and break off for each cake.
3. Brush with egg, sprinkle with powdered sugar, and bake golden brown in a good hot oven.
4. When finished, gently push them off the tubes and fill at serving with whipped cream, into which may be added maraschino liqueur, finely chopped pineapple, or dissolved chocolate.
