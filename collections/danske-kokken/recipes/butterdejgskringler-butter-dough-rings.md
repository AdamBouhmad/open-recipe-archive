## Ingredients

- 3 pounds washed butter
- ½ pound fine flour

## Directions

1. Divide the 3 pounds of washed butter into 3 parts
2. Rub one part with ½ pound fine flour
3. Place the remaining butter in this mixture
4. Treat as with other butter dough (see No. 186)
5. Form into rings using a ring iron
