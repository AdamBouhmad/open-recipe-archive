## Ingredients

- Roasted beef filet
- Scraped horseradish
- Watercress
- Browned potatoes
- ½ L whipped cream
- 60 grams grated horseradish
- Brown gravy with Madeira

## Directions

1. On the roasted filet place a strip of scraped horseradish, interspersed with small bunches of watercress
2. Place a similar garnish around it and outside this again browned potatoes
3. Or instead of the scraped horseradish, make a horseradish parfait from ½ L whipped cream, prepared with 60 grams grated horseradish
4. Freeze this parfait in approximately 4 hours and garnish the roasted filet with it
5. Place the ice either in gratin shells or in hollowed-out tomatoes; it can also be arranged on salad leaves
6. Serve with a brown gravy sauce with Madeira
