## Ingredients

- Rib section with most fat removed
- Peeled apples
- Scalded, pitted prunes

## Directions

1. The rib section is used for this dish, with most of the fat removed.
2. Break the bones in the middle and lay a layer of peeled, cut apples and scalded, pitted prunes on them.
3. Bend the bones toward each other and sew them together.
4. The rib roast can be roasted either in the oven or in a pot, but in the latter case must be browned before adding water or broth.
5. The flour for the sauce can be baked in some of the fat from the broth; it must cook well and have a nice brown color.
6. When serving, pull out the thread and serve the roast with red cabbage or potatoes and beets.
