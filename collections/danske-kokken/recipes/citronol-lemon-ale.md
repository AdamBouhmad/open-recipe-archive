## Ingredients

- 8 fresh lemons
- 10 pounds sugar
- Water
- 0.5 pint yeast

## Directions

1. Pour the sugar together with 37 quarts of water into a clean kettle, boil for 1 hour, skim well, cool. When lukewarm, add the yeast. Cover the vessel with a lid, not a cloth, which would cause off-flavor. Let stand 2 or more days for fermentation. Carefully skim off the yeast foam, pour the ale into barrels. Slice the lemons without peeling and add them. Bung the barrel and place in the cellar. After 14 days the ale is ready for bottling. Pour 23 quarts of fresh spring water into the barrel and bottle it.
