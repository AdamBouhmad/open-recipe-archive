## Ingredients

- French bread
- butter
- broth
- flat anchovies
- poached eggs
- meat gravy sauce

## Directions

1. Slice French bread and bake in butter and broth
2. On each piece place a flat anchovy with the bones removed, and beside it a poached egg
3. Arrange them side by side on a platter and serve with a strong meat gravy sauce
