## Ingredients

- 8 good apples
- dough as for cream puffs
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- a little vanilla essence
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Peel 8 good apples, cut them into very small pieces and stir into a dough as for cream puffs (Nr. 1024); add 1 tablespoon powdered sugar and a little vanilla essence.
2. Drop it with a dessert spoon into oblong balls, which are cooked light brown in clarified butter.
3. Serve hot, sprinkled with powdered sugar.
