## Ingredients

- 1 pound macarons with some bitter almonds
- 1 pint good wine
- preserved fruit
- whipped cream
- vanilla
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place 1 pound of macarons, in which there are some bitter almonds, in a deep dish, not too thickly, and cover with 1 pint of good wine.
2. Let them stand covered for one hour, but turn them when they have stood half the time.
3. Then take them out of the wine, place them closely together in the dish in which they are to be served, and cover with preserved fruit and then with stiff whipped cream mixed with vanilla and sugar.
