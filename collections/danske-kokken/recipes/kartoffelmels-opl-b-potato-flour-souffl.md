## Ingredients

- 12 drams powdered sugar
- 18 drams butter
- 1 measure thin cream
- 18 drams potato flour
- 9 egg yolks
- 9 egg whites
- jam

## Directions

1. Heat 12 drams powdered sugar, 18 drams butter and 1 measure thin cream in a pan and boil together
2. Mix 18 drams potato flour with 1 measure thin cream and bake with the first mixture in the same way as making buns, until the dough leaves the pan
3. Remove from heat and stir in 9 egg yolks one by one
4. When cold, stir in the 9 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks
5. Butter a dish and lay a layer of jam at the bottom
6. Pour the dough over the jam and bake the omelet in the dish for 1 hour
7. Bake slowly, especially at the beginning
8. Serve warm, sprinkled with powdered sugar with a napkin around the dish
