## Ingredients

- vegetables, meat, fish, or poultry to be cooked
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Obtain a simple, deep tin pot with a lid that fits tightly and well.
2. Place a tin sieve inside that goes only one quarter of the way down into the pot (it must not have a handle or shaft).
3. Fill one quarter of the space below with water.
4. Place the sieve in, add what is to be cooked, sprinkle with salt, place the lid on, and keep the water in continuous boil until the vegetables are tender.
5. The water becomes a strong stock that can be used in many ways.
