## Ingredients

- 10 Egg yolks
- 0.75 Pound powdered sugar
- 0.5 Pound fine sifted fine or potato flour

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg yolks for half an hour with three-quarters pound powdered sugar, constantly in the same direction.
2. Then add 0.5 pound fine sifted fine or potato flour.
3. Pour the mixture into small, oblong forms.
4. Brush with dissolved sugar.
5. Bake the cakes for one hour at even heat.
