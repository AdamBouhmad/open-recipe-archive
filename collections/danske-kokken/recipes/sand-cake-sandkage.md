## Ingredients

- 1 Pound clarified Butter
- 1 Pound powdered Sugar
- 1 Pound fine Flour
- 10 Egg yolks
- beaten Egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir the butter until creamy
2. Stir in the powdered sugar for a quarter hour
3. Stir in the fine flour and egg yolks one at a time
4. Lastly fold in the beaten egg whites
5. Pour into a greased form
6. Bake with moderate heat, especially at first, for 1½ hours
