## Ingredients

- one piece horseradish the length of a finger
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 dessertspoon whole white pepper
- 1 whole yellow mustard seed
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- a few fresh or dried bay leaves
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1 liter vinegar

## Directions

1. Scrape the horseradish and cut into smaller pieces.
2. Place the horseradish, garlic, pepper, mustard seed, ginger, bay leaves, salt, sugar, and vinegar together in a jar.
3. After about 10 days, strain the vinegar and use it in salad, hachis, ragout, and similar dishes.
4. Fresh vinegar can be poured over the spices for reuse.
