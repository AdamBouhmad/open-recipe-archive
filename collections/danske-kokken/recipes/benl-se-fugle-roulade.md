## Ingredients

- Tender beef from the tenderloin or sirloin
- Bacon strips
- Flour
- Ground pepper
- Cloves
- Salt
- Butter
- Onions
- Hot water and bouillon
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into long slices of a hand's width and a finger's thickness.
2. Beat the slices lightly.
3. Place a strip of bacon in the middle of each slice and wrap the beef around it.
4. Coat in flour mixed with ground pepper, cloves, and salt.
5. Tie each roll with string.
6. Brown butter with a couple of chopped onions.
7. Place the beef rolls in the pan and brown them lightly.
8. Add enough hot water and bouillon to cover the meat.
9. Boil until tender and remove, keeping warm.
10. Strain the sauce and thicken it with egg yolk.
11. Pour the sauce over the roulades, removing the string.
