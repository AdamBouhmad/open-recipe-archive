## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Bones and membranes
- Madeira
- Food coloring
- Meat extract or Maggi bouillon cubes
- Boiled, smoked ham

## Directions

1. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and roast on the pan in browned butter.
2. Boil the bones and membranes to make a gravy, which is mixed with Madeira, food coloring, and meat extract or Maggi bouillon cubes.
3. Pour the gravy over the cutlets, which are arranged in the center of the platter.
4. Arrange slices of boiled, smoked ham in a ring around them and eat together with the cutlets.
