## Ingredients

- 1 head fresh lettuce
- thick sour cream
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the head of lettuce
2. Rinse it in several changes of cold water and place in a colander to drain
3. Cut into 4 pieces, remove the stalk, and place the leaves in thick sour cream mixed with vinegar and sugar to taste
