## Ingredients

- A small half loaf of wheat bread, 1/2 pound by weight
- Milk
- 4 tablespoons sifted flour
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 3 gills old rum
- 10 eggs
- 4 ounces beef marrow, removed and chopped
- 2 ounces finely chopped beef suet
- A handful good raisins
- A small handful cleaned currants
- Half a teaspoon coarse salt

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from the wheat bread, grate it on a grater
2. Soak in boiling milk until well soaked through
3. When cold, grind it out and mix with sifted flour, powdered sugar, and old rum
4. Add eggs two at a time, beef marrow, beef suet, raisins, currants, and coarse salt
5. Cook for 2.5 hours and serve mostly with red sauce
6. Pour a couple spoonfuls of rum over the pudding and set it alight when serving
