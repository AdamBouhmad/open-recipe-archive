## Ingredients

- fresh pineapples
- sugar
- 2 pounds fine sugar
- 1 bottle Madeira
- 1 bottle Sauternes
- 1 bottle Graves
- 1 bottle ice-cooled Champagne

## Directions

1. Cut fresh pineapples into thin slices, place in sugar and let stand 24 hours, or use pineapples cooked with sugar
2. Add 2 pounds fine sugar and cover with 1 bottle Madeira, 1 bottle Sauternes and 1 bottle Graves
3. Let the mixture stand well covered for a couple of hours on ice
4. Then strain through a fine sieve or filter
5. Immediately before serving, add 1 bottle ice-cooled Champagne
