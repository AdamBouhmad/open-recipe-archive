## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg flour
- 1/4 kg margarine
- 1 good teaspoon baking powder
- 4 to 5 tablespoons water
- 3 eggs
- a little salt
- a small amount of sugar
- a handful of caraway seed

## Directions

1. Crumble the margarine and baking powder into the flour
2. Mix in the water, eggs, salt, sugar, and caraway seed
3. When the dough releases from the hands, roll it out very thinly
4. Prick with a fork
5. Bake for a quarter hour
6. The bread can be baked in one large sheet and broken into smaller pieces when baked, or cut into small squares before baking
7. Serve spread with butter
