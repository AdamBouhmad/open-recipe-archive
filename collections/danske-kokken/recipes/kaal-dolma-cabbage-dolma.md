## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg prepared forcemeat (serves 12 people)
- 1 white cabbage head
- twine
- butter
- broth or water
- salt

## Directions

1. Separate the cabbage leaves and place them briefly in boiling water to soften and make them pliable
2. Remove the coarsest part of the stems from the leaves
3. Place a spoonful of forcemeat in each leaf
4. Roll the cabbage carefully around the forcemeat to form long rolls and bind with twine
5. Place the dolmas side by side in a pot with browned butter
6. When browned, pour a little broth or salted water over them
7. Cook under a closed lid for at least 2 hours
8. When tender, strain the gravy and thicken it into sauce with a nice brown color
9. Pour the sauce over the dolmas when serving
10. Alternative: use a Bavarian sausage instead of forcemeat, or place a cooked leek on a piece of white cabbage leaf, wrap the leaf around it, and place another leaf on top, then bind, brown, and cook as for cabbage dolma with forcemeat
