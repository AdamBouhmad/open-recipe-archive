## Ingredients

- Young birds, cleaned and rinsed
- Butter
- Salt
- Bouillon
- Cream
- Currant jelly
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. After removing the entrails, clean and rinse the birds.
2. Lard the birds and place them in a pan in which butter has been browned with a little salt.
3. During roasting, place them breast-down and baste with bouillon and cream.
4. Turn them from time to time.
5. When done, stir a piece of butter into the sauce, skim it, and strain it.
6. Serve with currant jelly and browned potatoes.
