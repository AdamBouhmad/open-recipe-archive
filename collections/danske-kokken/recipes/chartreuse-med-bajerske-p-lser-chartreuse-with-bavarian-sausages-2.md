## Ingredients

- 2 heads of pointed cabbage
- ¼ kg (½ lb) sausages
- Butter
- Yellow and white carrots, parboiled

## Directions

1. A chartreuse can be cooked in a pudding mold without a center post or baked in an oven in a mold with a fixed bottom
2. In the latter case, place a piece of buttered paper over it with some small holes cut in it to allow steam to escape
3. Butter the mold well with cold butter
4. Cut parboiled yellow and white carrots into thin slices
