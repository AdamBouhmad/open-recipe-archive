## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.25 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water to a thin syrup.
2. Place large, whole, selected raspberries in the syrup.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon as soon as they reach a boil and place them in sulfured jars.
4. Reduce the juice for 6 to 8 minutes and pour over the berries.
5. Place sticks and sulfur in the jars, light them, and seal the jars as quickly as possible.
