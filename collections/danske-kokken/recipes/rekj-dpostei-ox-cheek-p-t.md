## Ingredients

- For 5 persons: Pastry dough
- 2 pounds meat
- Flour
- 3 teaspoons pepper
- 1 crushed bay leaf
- Salt
- chopped onion and parsley, 2 teaspoons of each
- Butter
- 1 gill water

## Directions

1. Roll out the dough to 1/4 inch thickness and line a well-buttered mold with it.
2. Cut the meat into slices, pound them, dip in flour and layer them in the mold.
3. Between each layer place some of the seasonings.
4. Pour in the water, cover the mold with dough and bake in a moderately hot oven for 3 hours.
5. When done, shake it gently so that the sauce mixes, then turn out the pâté.
6. Can be made from any kind of meat.
