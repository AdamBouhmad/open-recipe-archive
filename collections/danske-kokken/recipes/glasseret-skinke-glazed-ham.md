## Ingredients

- fresh ham
- water
- salt
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the fresh ham in salted water.
2. When tender, remove the skin.
3. Polish it, sprinkle with white sugar and place in a warm oven to brown.
4. When the sugar has melted and looks glossy, it is done.
5. At serving, wrap crinkled paper around the shank.
