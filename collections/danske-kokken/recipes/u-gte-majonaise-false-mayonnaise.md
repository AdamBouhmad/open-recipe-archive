## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- flour
- strong bouillon
- 4 egg yolks
- fine culinary oil
- mustard
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Bake a piece of butter with flour and dilute with strong bouillon so it becomes fairly thick.
2. Let it boil clear, and when cold, stir in 4 egg yolks one at a time along with fine culinary oil, a little mustard, salt, pepper and fairly substantial lemon juice.
3. Finally carefully fold in 3 egg whites whipped to foam.
