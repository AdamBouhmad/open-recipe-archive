## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 2 lemons
- 1/2 kg ripe tomatoes

## Directions

1. Tomatoes are available until late autumn but as soon as night frost begins they stop
2. Ripe but not overripe tomatoes are dried well pricked with a needle and placed in jar in layers with the powdered sugar
3. The next day the juice is poured off and cooked together with the juice of 2 lemons then poured hot over the tomatoes
4. The two following days the cooking of the juice is repeated and both times it is poured hot over
5. To 2 1/2 kg tomatoes dissolve 1/2 teaspoon salicylic acid in a little Cognac and pour this solution over the glasses or jars which are sealed as soon as possible
6. The salicylic acid must not be omitted with tomatoes as it greatly helps preserve them
