## Ingredients

- 40 quintal sweet almonds
- 10 quintal bitter almonds
- 4 egg whites
- 1 pound flour
- 1 quintal ground cardamom

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds and pound them fine with the egg whites, flour, and ground cardamom. If necessary, knead a little more egg white into it. Roll the dough in long strips and cut pieces of the size of a nut, place on a buttered sheet and bake at moderate heat.
