## Ingredients

- Wild boar back
- Butter or margarine
- Red wine
- Good stock
- Butter
- Flour
- Lemon or orange peel
- Red currant or raspberry jelly

## Directions

1. Cut the back as you would venison back, remove the skin, and lard the back thickly and cover with ample butter or margarine.
2. During roasting, baste it with a mixture of red wine and good stock.
3. For the sauce, brown butter and flour, thin with the stock from which the fat has been skimmed.
4. Add a very small amount of lemon or orange peel, bring to a boil, and remove before serving.
5. Stir red currant or raspberry jelly into the sauce.
