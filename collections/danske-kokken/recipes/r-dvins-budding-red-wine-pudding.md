## Ingredients

- 1.5 pints good red wine
- 1.5 pints clear raspberry juice, not cooked with sugar
- 1 pint water
- 1.5 pounds white sugar
- 1.5 ounces gelatine

## Directions

1. Cook the red wine, raspberry juice, water, and white sugar with gelatine
2. Let it cool and stir until completely cold
3. Pour into the mold
4. Serve with cream sauce
