## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Ground saltpeter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Finely chopped onion
- Meat (optional)

## Directions

1. The shoulder of lamb is best, though beef shoulder can also be used.
2. It is cut from the roast, laid on a board and sprinkled with ground saltpeter, salt, pepper, ginger and finely chopped onion.
3. If one thinks that the rolled sausage becomes too thin, some meat is laid in the middle.
4. Roll it up well and sew it on the side and at the ends and sprinkle with salt or place it in brine in the salt barrel with a press on top.
5. When it is to be used, place it first in soak in cold water and then cook it until it is tender.
6. Then place a press on it, which remains lying until the sausage is cold so that it becomes flat; finally remove the thread.
