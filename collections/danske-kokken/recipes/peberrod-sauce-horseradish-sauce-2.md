## Ingredients

- butter
- grated horseradish
- flour
- cream or milk
- currants

## Directions

1. Melt butter over heat.
2. Before it boils, add the grated horseradish and stir until it boils.
3. It can also be thickened with half as much flour as butter, and with cream or milk.
4. Sometimes it is made sweet and sour, and a little cleaned currants are cooked in it.
