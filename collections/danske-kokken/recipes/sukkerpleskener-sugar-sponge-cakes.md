## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 pound patent flour

## Directions

1. Beat 12 eggs and 1 pound powdered sugar well together.
2. Stir in 1 pound patent flour.
3. When well mixed, stick out small cakes with a spoon, place on a buttered pan, sprinkle with sugar, and bake.
