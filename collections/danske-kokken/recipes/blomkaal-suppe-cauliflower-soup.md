## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) soup
- 2 medium cauliflower heads
- Water
- Salt
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter or vegetable margarine
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour
- Cauliflower cooking water
- Veal stock or economical stock
- 1 bunch of herbs
- 6 egg yolks
- Ground white pepper
- A couple spoonfuls grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower heads and cook in salted water, but not too soft
2. Divide into small florets and place in the tureen
3. Brown the butter or margarine with the flour and dilute with 5 L (5 pots) of liquid, a mixture of the cauliflower water and veal stock or economical stock, in which a bunch of herbs has been cooked
4. Thicken the soup like asparagus soup with 6 egg yolks and season with a little ground white pepper
5. Grated Parmesan cheese can be added to the soup if desired
6. Serve with baked or toasted bread
