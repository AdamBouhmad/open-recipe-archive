## Ingredients

- Corn
- Salt
- Fat

## Directions

1. Remove the husks and cook the cob until no white juice flows out when the kernels are pricked
2. Remove the kernels and pack in stone jars as follows: at the bottom a layer of salt 1 inch high, then corn in a 2 inches high layer, and thus alternately until the jar is nearly full
3. The top layer of salt should be 1 inch high
4. Pour melted but not hot fat over the whole
5. Press a layer of thick white paper that fits in the jar on top, which is tied securely with paper or a bladder
6. Store in a cold place
7. Soak overnight before use
8. Should the top layer show mould, remove it
9. The rest will not be affected
