## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) wild or garden strawberries
- 1 bottle white rum
- 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- 1/4 l (1 pægl) water

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as Raspberry Liqueur.
