## Ingredients

- 9 oranges
- sugar
- 20 macarons
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) heavy cream

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the oranges, removing the seeds.
2. Sprinkle with sugar and let sit for one hour.
3. Layer the orange slices in a glass bowl with 20 macarons and let sit for 1/2 hour so the macarons soften slightly.
4. Just before serving, top with whipped cream made from 1/2 L (1/2 pot) heavy cream.
