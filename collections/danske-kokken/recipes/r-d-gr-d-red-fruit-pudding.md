## Ingredients

- Equal parts currants and raspberries, or currants and blackcurrants, or currants and cherries
- 2 L (2 pots) water
- Sugar to taste
- 60 gr (12 quintals) flour per liter (pot) of juice, half sago flour and half potato flour
- Chopped almonds (optional)
- Vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Pick and rinse the berries
2. Cook the berries in 2 L water
3. Strain the juice
4. Sweeten the juice
5. Thicken with 60 gr flour per liter of juice, using half sago flour and half potato flour
6. If using cherries, pound them in a mortar
7. Add chopped almonds to the strained juice before thickening, or sprinkle them on the finished pudding
8. Stir vanilla essence into the finished pudding
9. Serve with fresh cream or crème
