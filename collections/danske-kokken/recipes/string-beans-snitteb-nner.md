## Ingredients

- fresh tender bean pods with underdeveloped beans
- salt
- a small amount of baking soda
- butter
- flour
- good milk
- chopped parsley
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select freshly picked thin pods with underdeveloped beans
2. Dry and rub them
3. Stack several pieces and cut crosswise with a sharp knife into very thin, fine strips
4. Cook tender in water with salt and a small amount of baking soda
5. Brown butter and flour together and thin with good milk to make a smooth sauce
6. Season the sauce with chopped parsley, salt, and sugar
7. Stir in the string beans without adding any liquid
