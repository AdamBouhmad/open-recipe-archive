## Ingredients

- Thrushes
- Bacon slices
- Butter
- Cream
- Maggis bouillon cubes
- Food coloring
- Cold butter
- Heart, gizzard, and liver

## Directions

1. Pluck the birds and remove the entrails.
2. Wrap the breast with thin slices of bacon.
3. Brown in butter and braise in a pot for a quarter hour, basting with cream during cooking.
4. The sauce should reduce well and can have Maggis bouillon cubes and coloring added to achieve a light brown color.
5. At the end, stir cold butter into the sauce.
6. The heart, gizzard, and liver can be made into a forcemeat (see Snæpper, No. 979), which can either be stuffed into the birds or prepared as woodcock bread.
