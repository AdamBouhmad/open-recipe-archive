## Ingredients

- Hornfisk (garfish)
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Whole peppercorns
- Parsley sauce
- Vinegar (for sauce)
- Roe

## Directions

1. Remove the head and discard it
2. Salt the fish before cooking
3. Cut the fish into pieces 3 inches in length
4. Cook in water with salt, vinegar, and whole peppercorns
5. Arrange with parsley sauce containing a little vinegar
6. Cook the roe with the fish
