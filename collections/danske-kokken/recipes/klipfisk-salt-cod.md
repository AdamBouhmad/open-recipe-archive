## Ingredients

- Salt cod
- Soda
- Cold water
- Salt
- Butter
- Mustard
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut off the fins from the fish, which is cut into pieces and placed in water in the evening before it is to be used.
2. In the water is placed a small piece of soda, which is completely harmless and causes the fish to be cooked faster and whiter in appearance.
3. The next day scrape it on both sides so the scales, not the skin, come off, and give it fresh water, in which it stands until it is to be cooked; then place it on the heat in cold and plenty of water without salt, skim and cook until it is tender, usually 2 or 3 hours.
4. It must not remain in the water after it is cooked, but place it immediately on the plate with the skin upward.
5. Serve with butter and mustard, and hard-boiled chopped eggs, which are added to the sauce at the table, and boiled potatoes.
