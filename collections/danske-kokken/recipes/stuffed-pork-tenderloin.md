## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloin
- Pork filling (see recipe 202)

## Directions

1. Trim the pork tenderloin and use the trimmed pieces for the filling.
2. Slit open the tenderloin and place the filling inside.
3. Sew it closed and lard it.
4. Roast as in the previous recipe.
5. Serve with potato salad and brown sauce made from the gravy.
