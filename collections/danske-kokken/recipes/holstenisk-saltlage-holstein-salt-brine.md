## Ingredients

- 1 Kilo (2 lb) Kitchen salt
- 10 Gr (2 Dram) Saltpeter
- 125 Gr (1/4 lb) Powdered sugar
- 4 L (4 Pots) Boiling water

## Directions

1. Dissolve the kitchen salt, saltpeter, and powdered sugar in 4 liters of boiling water
2. Place the meat to be salted in the brine when it is completely cold
3. After 14 days it is ready to be cooked without needing to be desalted
4. This mild brine is excellent for salting tongues, beef brisket, salt meat, rolled sausages, and hams
5. Veal and lamb meat require only a few days of salting
6. Hams must lie in it for at least 3 weeks
