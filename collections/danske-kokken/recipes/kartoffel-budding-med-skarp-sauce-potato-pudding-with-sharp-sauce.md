## Ingredients

- 2 pounds potatoes
- 0.5 pound butter
- 15 eggs
- Onion
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. When the potatoes are cooked, not overcooked, peel them and grate them with a light hand so no lumps remain.
2. Bake them in butter; when baked, stir in 15 eggs and plenty of onion, pepper, and salt.
3. Boil for 3 hours and serve with strong brown sauce.
