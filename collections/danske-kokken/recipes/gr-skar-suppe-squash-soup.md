## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg (3 lb) squash
- 4 L (4 pots) soup
- A bunch of herbs
- Yellow and white root vegetables
- 5-6 egg yolks
- Salt
- Ground white pepper
- Sliced cooked leeks, meat balls, or baked French bread cubes (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the ripe squash, remove seeds, and cut into small pieces.
2. Cook together with a bunch of herbs and some yellow and white root vegetables in some of the soup.
3. When vegetables are tender, remove and cut into pieces; place in tureen.
4. Remove the bunch of herbs and press well.
5. Pass the cooked squash through a coarse sieve by pressing with the remaining soup.
6. Bring to a boil.
7. Bind with 5-6 beaten egg yolks.
8. Season with salt and ground white pepper and arrange immediately.
9. In addition to the vegetables, add sliced cooked leeks, meat balls, or baked French bread cubes to the soup if desired.
10. Alternatively, the soup may be thickened with butter and flour baked together and diluted with the strained soup, in which case the eggs may be omitted.
