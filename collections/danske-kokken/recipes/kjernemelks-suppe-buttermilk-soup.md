## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 2 large tablespoons corn starch
- currants
- whole cinnamon
- sliced almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the corn starch in the milk while it is cold
2. Place it on the fire with currants, whole cinnamon, sliced almonds and sugar
3. Whisk it with a whisk or fork until it comes to a boil, which need only be very brief
4. It will curdle during cooking but will come together again
