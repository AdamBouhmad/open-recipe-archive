## Ingredients

- 1⅓ kg rice flour
- 4½ L milk
- crushed almonds
- salt
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- beer, red wine, or fruit juice and water

## Directions

1. Mix the rice flour with ¼ L of the cold milk.
2. When the remaining milk boils, stir a little of the hot milk into the liaison before adding it to the pot with strong stirring.
3. Cook for 10 minutes.
4. Crushed almonds can be stirred in with the salt.
5. Serve with butter, sugar, cinnamon and either beer, red wine or fruit juice and water.
