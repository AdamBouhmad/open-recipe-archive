## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the asparagus in cold water
2. Peel from top to bottom to remove the woody outer part at the lower end
3. Trim the bottom
4. Tie them in bundles of about twenty pieces
5. Cook in water with a little salt for about a quarter hour until tender, but not so tender that the heads fall off
6. Remove the ties from the bundles and arrange on a flat platter with a napkin underneath
7. If they are barely tender enough, cover them with a warm napkin and let them stand for a moment until done
8. For sauce asparagus, always choose the largest and most tender with light heads
