## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg quinces, peeled
- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 l vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces and cut them into 8 pieces, and cut away the core.
2. Place them over the fire together with sugar and vinegar and boil soft, while at the same time skimming.
3. Remove the quinces and further reduce the juice before pouring it boiling over the fruit.
4. Seal as soon as the preserve has cooled.
