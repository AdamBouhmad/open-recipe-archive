## Ingredients

- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) port wine
- 1/4 L (1/4 pot) pickles vinegar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) mushroom soy sauce
- 1 1/4 dl (1/8 pot) ordinary soy sauce
- 30 gr (6 kvint) finely chopped shallots
- 30 gr (6 kvint) finely chopped lemon peel
- 30 gr (6 kvint) grated horseradish
- 15 gr (3 kvint) ground pepper
- 15 gr (3 kvint) ground allspice
- 10 chopped anchovies
- cayenne pepper
- celery seed

## Directions

1. Place 1/2 L (1/2 pot) port wine, 1/4 L (1/4 pot) pickles vinegar, 1/2 L (1/2 pot) mushroom soy sauce and 1 1/4 dl (1/8 pot) ordinary soy sauce in a small barrel, along with 30 gr (6 kvint) finely chopped shallots, 30 gr (6 kvint) finely chopped lemon peel, 30 gr (6 kvint) grated horseradish, 15 gr (3 kvint) ground pepper, 15 gr (3 kvint) ground allspice, 10 chopped anchovies, and a very little cayenne pepper and celery seed.
2. Stopper the barrel well and shake it daily for a period of 14 days.
3. Strain the sauce, pour into bottles and stopper.
4. A few spoonfuls of this is an excellent addition to sauce.
