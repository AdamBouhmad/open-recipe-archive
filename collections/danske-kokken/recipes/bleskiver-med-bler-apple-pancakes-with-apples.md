## Ingredients

- Peeled apples, without cores, cut into pieces the size of a large nut
- 5 Eggs
- 1 Pint cream
- 0.5 Pint cold water
- 1 Pint melted butter
- 1 Pint fine wheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat 5 eggs with 1 pint cream and 0.5 pint cold water.
2. Add 1 pint melted butter and 1 pint fine wheat flour.
3. When the æbleskiver pan is well heated, put butter in it.
4. Dip the apple pieces in the batter and place them in the holes.
5. Fill the holes with more batter.
6. Bake beautifully golden brown.
7. Turn the æbleskiver and serve hot with caster sugar over.
