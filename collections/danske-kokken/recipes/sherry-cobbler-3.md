## Ingredients

- Ice
- 2 wine glasses Sherry
- 1 tablespoon Cognac
- 2 teaspoons crushed sugar
- Lemon peel
- Strawberries, if available

## Directions

1. Fill a large glass more than halfway with ice chunks. Add the remaining ingredients except the strawberries. Pour the mixture quickly several times from one glass to another, then add the strawberries. Drink through a glass straw or drinking tube.
