## Ingredients

- buttermilk
- cornstarch
- powdered sugar
- egg yolks
- vanilla essence

## Directions

1. For each liter of buttermilk, use one good tablespoon of cornstarch
2. Mix the cornstarch in some of the cold milk
3. Pour under stirring into the remaining boiling milk
4. Bring to a boil
5. Stir in a tureen where 1 egg yolk per liter of milk has been mixed with powdered sugar
6. Add a little vanilla essence
7. Optional: the soup can be served cold as a dessert after a meat course, with whipped cream folded in, and strawberry or raspberry jam and small macaroons served alongside
