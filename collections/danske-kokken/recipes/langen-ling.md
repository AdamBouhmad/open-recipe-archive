## Ingredients

- Ling fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Palmin (cooking fat)
- Browned butter
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Split the fish which is bought in flayed condition
2. Remove the bone and cut out the two long, meaty fillets into smaller pieces
3. Pound the very thick pieces with a meat mallet
4. Sprinkle the fish generously with salt and let it stand for half an hour
5. Rinse and dry the fish
6. Sprinkle with pepper
7. Dip it in egg and breadcrumbs
8. Fry it thoroughly in Palmin
9. Serve with browned butter and lemon
