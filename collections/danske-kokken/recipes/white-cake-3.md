## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup butter
- whites of 4 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 small teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Stir sugar and butter until cream
2. Then stir in the milk and flour, little by little
3. Finally fold in the lightly beaten whites
4. The cake must be stirred well before the whites are added
