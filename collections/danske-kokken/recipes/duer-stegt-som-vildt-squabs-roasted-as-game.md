## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Milk
- Butter
- Flour
- Strained gravy
- Cold butter
- Redcurrant jelly
- Roasted potatoes
- Parsley
- Redcurrant jelly, lingonberry jelly, or apple sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Lard and brown the squabs, then roast as in the previous recipe
2. Baste with milk instead of soup
3. Split and arrange garnished with roasted potatoes and parsley
4. The sauce can be reduced until smooth, or brown butter and flour and dilute with the gravy it was roasted in
5. Stir in a little cold butter and redcurrant jelly at the end
6. The sauce should have a nice, light brown color
7. Serve with redcurrant jelly, lingonberry jelly, or apple sauce
