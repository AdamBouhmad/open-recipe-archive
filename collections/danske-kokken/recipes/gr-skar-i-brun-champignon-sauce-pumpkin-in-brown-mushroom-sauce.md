## Ingredients

- peeled and hollowed pumpkin
- strong brown sauce or water with Maggi bouillon cubes and salt
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) fresh cleaned mushrooms for 6 persons
- Sherry
- lemon juice
- sugar
- paprika
- potato starch for thickening

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled and hollowed pumpkin into small, slightly elongated pieces
2. Cook until tender in a strong brown sauce or in water with Maggi bouillon cubes and salt
3. Season the sauce with Sherry, lemon juice, a small amount of sugar, and paprika
4. Bring the mushrooms to a good boil in the sauce
5. Thicken the sauce very slightly with potato starch
6. Serve as a vegetable side dish with fried fish
