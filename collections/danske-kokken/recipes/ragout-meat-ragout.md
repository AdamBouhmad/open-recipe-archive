## Ingredients

- butter
- 1 onion, grated
- anchovies, cut into small pieces
- pepper
- flour
- gravy
- bouillon
- currant juice, sweetened
- meat or roast
- oysters, stuffed lemons or pearl onions (optional)
- meat or fish forcemeat (optional)
- egg yolk
- water

## Directions

1. Brown a little butter and cook the grated onion in it with small pieces of anchovies, pepper and flour
2. Thin it with gravy, bouillon and a little sweetened currant juice
3. Chop or cut the roast or meat into pieces and add it to the sauce
4. Remove it as soon as it is well heated through
5. When the sauce boils again, strain it through a sieve over the meat
6. Vary by adding oysters, stuffed lemons or pearl onions to the sauce
7. Meat or fish balls may also be cooked in this ragout, or a layer of forcemeat may be spread over the hot ragout when it is placed on the serving dish; brush the forcemeat with egg yolk mixed with a little water
8. Set the dish in the oven and bake for about half an hour
