## Ingredients

- Raw, ripe tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Split raw, ripe, dried tomatoes and place them on a round platter.
2. Sprinkle them with salt and pepper and place the platter in the warm oven for about ten minutes.
3. Bake the omelet on one side and turn it over onto the platter so that the tomatoes are covered by it, with the baked side facing upward.
4. Serve with open-faced sandwiches or with creamed butter.
