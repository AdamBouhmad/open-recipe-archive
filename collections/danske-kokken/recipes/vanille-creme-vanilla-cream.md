## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla
- 8 egg yolks
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Place the cream on heat with 2 tablespoons vanilla.
2. When the cream boils, remove it from heat and stir in 8 egg yolks that have first been stirred white with a little white sugar.
3. Return to heat and stir constantly until it is just about to boil and has become thick.
4. Pour out and stir evenly until it is so cold that it no longer forms a skin, then pour into a dish and serve.
5. If the cream becomes thick quickly while on heat and begins to separate, pour it out at once and stir strongly so that it can come together again; it helps to stir in a little cold cream or good milk and pour it through a sieve.
6. Add a little salt and serve with small cakes.
7. You can cook egg whites according to instructions and place them over the cream.
8. For 18 persons.
