## Ingredients

- 500 grams cake flour
- 375 grams powdered sugar
- 250 grams melted margarine
- 125 grams currants
- 35 grams finely chopped preserved orange peel
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cardamom
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 250 milliliters semi-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix cake flour, powdered sugar, melted margarine, currants, finely chopped preserved orange peel, eggs, salt, cardamom, cinnamon, and baking powder
2. Add semi-skimmed milk
3. Pour into a greased form and bake for 1 hour
