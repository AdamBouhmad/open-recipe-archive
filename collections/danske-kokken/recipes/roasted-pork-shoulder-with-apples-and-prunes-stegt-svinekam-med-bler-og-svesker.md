## Ingredients

- Pork shoulder
- Apples, peeled and chopped
- Prunes, scalded and pitted
- Broth from roasting
- Butter
- Flour
- Boiled potatoes or red cabbage

## Directions

1. At the thin side of the shoulder, that is at the ribs, loosen the meat so much that some few peeled and chopped apples can be inserted there as well as some scalded prunes without stones.
2. Sew the meat together and roast it either in a pot, but then it must be browned first, or in a good oven.
3. Apples and prunes can also be larded into the shoulder, just as one lards a lamb roast with parsley.
4. Serve with sauce made from the broth and browned potatoes or red cabbage.
