## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- gravy or strong bouillon
- port wine or sherry (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour to a light brown in a saucepan.
2. Thin with gravy or, if lacking, with strong bouillon.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. If desired, add a little port wine or sherry.
