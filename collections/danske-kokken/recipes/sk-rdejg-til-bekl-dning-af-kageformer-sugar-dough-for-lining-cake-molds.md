## Ingredients

- 0.5 lbs. butter
- 30 quintals white sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the salt out of 0.5 lbs. butter.
2. Work it together with 30 quintals white sugar, 1 lb. flour and 2 eggs.
3. When well kneaded together, roll it out on a board sprinkled with flour.
