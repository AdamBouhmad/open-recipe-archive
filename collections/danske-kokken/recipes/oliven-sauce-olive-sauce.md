## Ingredients

- Olives
- Madeira
- Even, brown gravy sauce

## Directions

1. Peel olives from the pits in a spiral and twist them back into their original form
2. Bring them to a boil in a little Madeira
3. Add the Madeira and olives to an even, brown gravy sauce and bring to a boil again
