## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs out onto a plate so that the yolks are whole, rather than placing them on the pan one at a time
2. Place the frying pan over the heat with a little butter
3. When it boils, pour the eggs carefully onto it
4. As soon as the whites are set, they are done
