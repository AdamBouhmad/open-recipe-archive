## Ingredients

- Fresh vegetables: peas, carrots, cauliflower, beans, and asparagus
- Sugar
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth or vegetable water
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Salt
- 2 egg yolks
- Raw cream
- Smoked salmon (for garnish)
- Smoked goose breast (for garnish)
- Small sausages (for garnish)
- Parsley (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Cook whole vegetables in water with a little sugar
2. Make a sauce with butter and flour, diluted with meat broth or vegetable water with Maggi bouillon cubes and salt
3. Thicken with 2 well-beaten egg yolks mixed with a few spoonfuls of raw cream
4. Separate cauliflower into small pieces and cut other vegetables with a Chartreuse knife
5. Carefully turn vegetables in the sauce so they remain whole
6. Fill as hot as possible into the krustader
7. Garnish the plate with different things: thinly sliced smoked salmon rolled together and arranged in a ring around the krustader, thinly sliced smoked goose breast, or very small sausages
8. Decorate everything with parsley
