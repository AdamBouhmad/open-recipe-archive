## Ingredients

- potatoes
- cold water
- carrot and white root vegetables
- a large bunch of herbs
- small whole onions
- salt
- ground black pepper or cayenne pepper
- meat or bacon stock (optional)
- fresh bacon in small cubes (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cut them into pieces and place them on the fire with so much cold water that it stands well over them
2. When they have cooked some, add carrot and white root vegetables and a large bunch of herbs; take this out when it is done and press it well
3. Cook some small whole onions in it together with salt and a little ground black pepper or cayenne pepper
4. You can add a little less water at first and later dilute the soup with stock from salt meat or bacon
5. You can also cut fresh bacon in small cubes, which is cooked with the potatoes and eaten in the soup
