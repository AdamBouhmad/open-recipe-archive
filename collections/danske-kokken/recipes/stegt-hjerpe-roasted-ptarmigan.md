## Ingredients

- Ptarmigans, cleaned
- Salt
- Fine bacon
- Butter
- Flour
- Sweet cream
- Currant jelly
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. After cleaning the ptarmigans, scald them.
2. Pull the skin from the neck and breast downward and wrap it around the legs.
3. Set them up so the breast is high.
4. Lard with fine bacon and place them side by side, breast up.
5. Sprinkle with a little salt and roast in butter over gentle heat for about a quarter hour.
6. When roasted, remove them and brown the sauce with a little flour.
7. Thin it with sweet cream and strain it; finally stir in a piece of cold butter.
8. Serve with currant jelly and browned potatoes.
