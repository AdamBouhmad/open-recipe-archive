## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 Q) flour
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) melted margarine
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 good tablespoon yeast
- lukewarm milk
- spices as for Æbleskiver

## Directions

1. In flour, melted margarine, egg yolks and a good tablespoon of yeast, add so much lukewarm milk that it becomes a soft dough as for Æbleskiver; set this aside to rise.
2. Apply spices to taste as for Æbleskiver, and bake the waffles as the previous ones.
3. Keep them warm until serving and serve sprinkled with powdered sugar.
4. Instead of yeast, 2 small teaspoons of my baking powder can be used, but then they are baked immediately without being set to rise.
5. After use, the iron is dried and wrapped in paper, and paper is also placed between it.
