## Ingredients

- Lamb chops with bone
- Ground pepper
- Fine salt
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Green peas, spinach or potato purée

## Directions

1. Cut the chop piece from the breast, and each chop must have a bone.
2. Give the chops a blow with a meat mallet; sprinkle with ground pepper and fine salt, dip in butter and turn in grated bread, then fry them golden brown in butter.
3. Arrange them in a ring on the platter and serve with green peas, spinach or potato purée alongside.
