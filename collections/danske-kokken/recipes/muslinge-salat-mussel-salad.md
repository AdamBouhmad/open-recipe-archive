## Ingredients

- Cooked mussels
- Pickled fish or small fish balls
- Mustard seed vinegar
- Tarragon vinegar
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as in No. 8, adding mustard seed and tarragon vinegar
2. Place the cooked mussels in the sauce along with some flaked fish or finely cut fish balls
3. Lay some mussels and lemon slices around it
