## Ingredients

- Carp, cleaned, scaled and cut into pieces
- Bay leaves
- Whole pepper
- Fine cloves
- Salt
- Water
- Red wine
- Flour
- Grated rye bread

## Directions

1. Place cleaned, scaled and cut carp pieces in a casserole with bay leaves, whole pepper, fine cloves and salt.
2. Place the heads on the bottom.
3. Add a little water and enough red wine so it just covers the fish.
4. Once it has boiled a little, even it out with a little flour and some grated rye bread, which is mixed with a little of the hot soup and pressed through a sieve.
