## Ingredients

- 2½ to 3 kg pike
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Green salad or parsley
- Boiled potatoes
- Melted butter
- Grated horseradish
- Cream and grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Clean the pike by removing the gills and entrails, and rinse well inside
2. The scales should remain on the fish; rub them well with vinegar and salt
3. When cooking, tie the head and tail together with a piece of twine passed through with a needle, so the fish lies curved when served
4. Place in cold water and cook until the fins begin to loosen
5. Serve on a platter with a fish rack and a folded napkin underneath
6. Remove the band and place either green salad or parsley in the mouth of the fish
7. Serve with boiled potatoes and sauce of melted butter and grated horseradish, or cream and grated horseradish
