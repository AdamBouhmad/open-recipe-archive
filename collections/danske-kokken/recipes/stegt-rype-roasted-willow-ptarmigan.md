## Ingredients

- Willow ptarmigans
- Butter
- Salt
- Ginger, crushed
- Cream, thin and thick, sour
- Currant jelly
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Roast as you would ptarmigans, but do not flay them and place them in the pan breast up.
2. When nicely browned, turn them.
3. Thin the sauce with a little boiling, thin cream in which a little salt and crushed ginger have been added.
4. Roast them tender under a lid and baste regularly with thick, sour cream.
5. Stir the sauce up with thick cream.
6. Serve with currant jelly and browned potatoes.
