## Ingredients

- Pears
- Powdered sugar (1 1/2 kg to 2/3 kg pear pulp)
- Water
- Vinegar
- Lemon zest (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the pears.
2. Remove the blossom end and seed cavity, and place the pears in cold water with a little vinegar as they are peeled.
3. Remove the pears from the water, put them in a pot and set over heat with enough water so they can just float in it.
4. Cook the pears until soft enough to easily pass through a coarse sieve.
5. Weigh the pulp, pour it into the pot and cook it down together with the sugar with regular stirring, until the jam is cooked to a porridge consistency.
6. Fill it into jars, cover with paper and tie up.
7. To this portion, the zest of 1 lemon can be added, which is cooked at the same time as the sugar.
