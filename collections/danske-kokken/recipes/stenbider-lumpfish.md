## Ingredients

- lumpfish
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- peppercorns
- bay leaves
- cream and horseradish sauce or sour sauce
- flour
- light brown butter
- butter
- flour
- soup or water
- finely chopped onion
- white liver
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. To cook: skin the fish, rinse it, and dry it in a clean cloth
2. Place it to cook with water, salt, a little vinegar, some peppercorns, and a couple of bay leaves
3. Serve with cream and horseradish sauce or sour sauce
4. To fry: flour the fish as usual and fry it in light brown butter
5. For the sauce: bake a good spoonful of butter and a spoonful of flour together over the heat and thin with soup or water
6. Add finely chopped onion, the white liver (blanched), and lemon slices, cook well together, then strain the sauce
7. Slice the fried fish thinly and lay it in this sauce, which is then brought to a boil
