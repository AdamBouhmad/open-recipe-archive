## Ingredients

- 3 medium cauliflower heads
- butter
- flour
- milk
- egg yolk
- grated Parmesan cheese
- sugar
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Place the cooked cauliflower on a sieve to remove water
2. When well drained, arrange the heads in a pyramid on an oven-safe dish
3. Make a smooth white sauce of butter and flour diluted with milk
4. Thicken with 1 egg yolk per cauliflower head
5. Season the sauce with grated Parmesan cheese, sugar and salt to taste
6. Pour the sauce over the cauliflower, ensuring it is completely covered
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and drizzle with melted butter
8. Bake at strong oven heat for about 15 minutes until light brown
9. Serve as a vegetable or as an independent dish for a first or middle course
