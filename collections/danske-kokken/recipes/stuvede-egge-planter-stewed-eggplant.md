## Ingredients

- eggplant
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Place them in cold water with a little salt and cook until they can be cut through with the side of a fork
2. Change the water once
3. Peel them, mash them and warm them in salt water, pepper and butter
