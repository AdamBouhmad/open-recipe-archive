## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet cream
- 12 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons stiff currant or raspberry juice
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip 1 cup sweet cream to peaks, and add to it 12 whipped egg yolks, 3 tablespoons stiff currant or raspberry juice, the juice of 2 lemons, and 1 pound powdered sugar
2. Stir the whole well together
3. Finally add 6 whipped egg whites and fold in gently
4. Bake the cake in a pan at even heat, and when cold, cover with whipped cream with vanilla sugar and decorate with jam jelly
