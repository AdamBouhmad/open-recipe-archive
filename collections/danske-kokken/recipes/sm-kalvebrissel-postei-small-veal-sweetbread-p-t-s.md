## Ingredients

- Morde dough
- 2 to 3 veal or lamb sweetbreads, blanched
- Ham
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Spices
- 1 Gill red wine
- Egg yolk
- Meat stock

## Directions

1. Cut the sweetbreads into large cubes and mix with equal amounts of cubed ham and mushrooms
2. Sauté with butter over even heat for 4 to 5 minutes, stirring constantly
3. Add spices and wine, bring to a boil over brisk heat, then remove from heat
4. Line small fluted molds with dough
5. Lay a layer of forcemeat, then fill with the stewed mixture and cover with forcemeat and a lid of dough
6. Brush with egg yolk and bake in an evenly warm oven
7. When done, remove the lid, pour in a little sauce or stock, replace the lid, and serve
