## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons vinegar
- 12 tablespoons strong gravy
- 14.5 tablespoons salad oil
- 3 chopped eggs
- Chopped onion equal in amount to the eggs
- 4 roughly chopped anchovies without bones
- Finely chopped beets and shallot
- 3 teaspoons ground pepper
- 2 knife-tips cayenne pepper
- 4 spoonfuls soy sauce
- Diced meat of roasted goose, duck, turkey or veal

## Directions

1. Fill a terrine with 6 tablespoons vinegar, 12 tablespoons strong gravy, 14.5 tablespoons salad oil, 3 chopped eggs, chopped onion equal in amount to the eggs, 4 roughly chopped anchovies without bones, finely chopped beets and shallot, 3 teaspoons ground pepper, 2 knife-tips cayenne pepper and 4 spoonfuls soy sauce
2. Place it in heat so everything melts together but does not become hot
3. Place diced meat of roasted goose, duck, turkey or veal in a dish and pour the sauce over it
4. Let this salad stand for several hours before serving
