## Ingredients

- 1 Pot berries
- 1 Pd. Melis
- 1/2 Pægl water
- whole cinnamon
- vanilla
- cognac

## Directions

1. Clean the berries well
2. Boil the Melis to syrup with water
3. Add the berries along with some whole cinnamon and vanilla
4. Boil gently with a couple of boilings for 1/2 hour
5. When removed from heat, add a little cognac before distributing into jars or glasses
