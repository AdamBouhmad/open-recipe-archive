## Ingredients

- brill
- hot water
- salt
- sour butter sauce or cream and horseradish sauce
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Scrape and scald the brill in several changes of hot water to remove the slime
2. Clean them and cook whole
3. Serve with sour butter sauce or cream and horseradish sauce
4. If they are muddy, cook some bread crumbs in the water to remove the muddy taste
