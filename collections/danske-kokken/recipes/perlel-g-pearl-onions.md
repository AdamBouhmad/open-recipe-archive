## Ingredients

- Small onions
- 1 l (1 pot) vinegar
- 2.5 gr (1/4 oz) sugar to 1 kg (2 lb) onions

## Directions

1. Place the small onions in boiling water and after boiling for 5 minutes, remove them and cover with cold water.
2. Peel them and boil them for 5 minutes in the vinegar which has had the sugar added to it.
3. Pearl onions can be pickled without spices or as in recipe No. 232 and boiled together in the vinegar.
4. After the onions are poured into the glasses together with the vinegar, tie them shut with parchment paper.
