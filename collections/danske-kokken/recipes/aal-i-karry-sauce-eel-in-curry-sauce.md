## Ingredients

- Eel, skinned, cleaned, and cut into pieces
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Onion
- Flour
- Curry
- Boiled loose rice or potato puree
- Onions, roughly chopped and browned

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the eel as for Fricassee (Nr. 510), boiling in salted water with parsley
2. Melt butter and sweat a grated onion in it
3. Brown a good spoonful of flour together with curry to taste in the butter
4. Add the fish stock to create a sauce
5. Arrange the eel in a ring of loose boiled rice or potato puree
6. Pour the sauce over the eel
7. Sprinkle the rice or puree ring with roughly chopped browned onions
