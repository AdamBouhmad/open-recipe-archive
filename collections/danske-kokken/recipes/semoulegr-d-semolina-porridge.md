## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg semolina grains
- 2.25 to 2.5 liters sweet or semi-skimmed milk
- essence (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare and serve as Rice Flour Porridge
2. Add essence if desired
3. Leftover porridge can be used for dessert the same way as rice porridge
