## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound finely ground melis sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 stick vanilla, chopped
- 1 pot boiling cream
- 7 quintals dissolved isinglass
- 5 bottles white rum
- 12 whipped egg whites
- 1 pail cream, whipped to foam

## Directions

1. Whip 1/2 pound finely ground melis sugar together with 12 egg yolks for about a quarter hour and place over heat with 1 chopped stick vanilla.
2. Whip in 1 pot boiling cream along with 7 quintals dissolved isinglass.
3. When it has come to a boil, remove from heat and continue stirring, adding 5 bottles white rum.
4. When the cream is almost cold, add the 12 whipped egg whites and 1 pail cream whipped to foam.
5. Pour the pudding into glass dishes and let it stand in a cold place for at least one day before using.
6. Serve with sauce made from fruit juice.
