## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) peeled, floury potatoes
- 4 L (4 pots) spare broth
- 1 whisk
- 4 carrots
- 75 grams (15 skein) butter
- 50 grams (10 skein) flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 L (1 mug) raw cream
- ground white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes tender in water with salt.
2. Drain the water, and rub the potatoes through a sieve with 4 L (4 pots) of broth in which 1 whisk and 4 carrots have been cooked.
3. Bake 75 grams (15 skein) butter and 50 grams (10 skein) flour together and thin with the potato soup, which is bound with 4 egg yolks beaten with 1/4 L (1 mug) raw cream, but without bringing it to a boil afterwards.
4. Season the soup with ground white pepper and salt.
5. The soup can be served with meat balls cooked in the soup, cut carrots cooked in the soup, cut Bavarian sausages cooked in the soup, small cut leeks sautéed in butter, or onion cut in cubes and sautéed in butter.
6. Serve with toasted bread.
