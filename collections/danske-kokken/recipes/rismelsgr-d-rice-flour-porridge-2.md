## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg rice flour
- 4.5 liters milk
- salt
- crushed almonds (optional)
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- oil, red wine, or juice and water

## Directions

1. Mix the rice flour in 0.25 liters of cold milk
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil
3. Stir a little of the hot milk into the flour mixture before adding it to the pot with strong stirring
4. Cook for 10 minutes
5. Optional: stir in crushed almonds with the salt
6. Serve with butter, sugar, cinnamon, and either oil, red wine, or juice and water
