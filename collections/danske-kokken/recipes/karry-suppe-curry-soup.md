## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 pots) thin vegetable stock
- 1 celery
- 4 carrots
- 2 medium onions
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter
- 1 teaspoon curry
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour
- 1/4 L (1 pint) rice
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook 1 celery and 4 carrots until tender in the stock, then peel, cut in pieces, and place in the tureen.
2. Grate 2 medium onions and sauté in 125 g (1/4 lb) butter together with 1 teaspoon curry.
3. Add 125 g (1/4 lb) flour and thin with the stock.
4. When it has boiled, it is ready, but more curry may be added to taste if desired, first stirred in water or stock.
5. 1/4 L (1 pint) rice, cooked in ample water with salt, can either be placed in the tureen or served separately.
