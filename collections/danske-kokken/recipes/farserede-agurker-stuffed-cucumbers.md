## Ingredients

- Fresh medium-sized cucumbers
- Ground meat filling
- Maggis bouillon cubes
- Browned butter
- Flour
- Food coloring
- Paprika
- Wine

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, split them, and scrape out the cores with a spoon
2. Fill the cucumbers with the ground meat filling and tie them together
3. Cook them in stock or water with Maggis bouillon cubes
4. Make a sauce from browned butter, flour, the cooking stock, food coloring, and paprika
5. Add wine when the sauce is finished and pour it over the cucumbers at serving
6. The stuffed cucumbers may be sliced and used as garnish around a roast
