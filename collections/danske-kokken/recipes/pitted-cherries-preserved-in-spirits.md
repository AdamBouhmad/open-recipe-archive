## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg pitted cherries
- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl brandy

## Directions

1. Pit the cherries and treat the kernels as described above.
2. Sprinkle the sugar over them as they are laid in the jar.
3. Finally pour the spirits over them and cover the jar well.
4. For the first 4 to 5 days stir once daily so that the sugar can mix with the spirits and juice, then seal it airtight.
