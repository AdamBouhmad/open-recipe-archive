## Ingredients

- 300 grams margarine
- 300 grams fine sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- a little almond essence
- 250 milliliters semi-skimmed milk
- 625 grams Pillsbury flour
- egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Mix margarine with fine sugar
2. Add egg yolks, baking powder, almond essence, and semi-skimmed milk
3. Stir in Pillsbury flour
4. Fold in whipped egg whites last
5. Grease form well and line with paper
6. Pour batter in, sprinkle sugar and chopped almonds on top, and bake in a moderately warm oven for about 1 hour
