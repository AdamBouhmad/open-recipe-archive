## Ingredients

- 250 grams cornstarch
- 0.25 liter cold milk
- 0.5 liter milk, brought to boil
- 75 grams cold margarine
- 8 egg yolks
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 50 grams finely cut orange peel
- zest of 0.5 lemon, grated
- egg whites, beaten
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. 250 grams of cornstarch is stirred into 0.25 liter of cold milk.
2. Just over 0.5 liter of milk is brought to a boil, the mixture is quickly stirred in, and cooked for a couple of minutes.
3. The pot is lifted from the heat, while 75 grams of cold margarine is stirred into the mass; this is poured into a dish in which 8 egg yolks have been stirred with 125 grams of powdered sugar, 1 teaspoonful of salt, 50 grams of finely cut orange peel, and grated zest of 0.5 lemon.
4. The beaten egg whites are stirred in last, and the batter is immediately poured into a mold buttered and sprinkled with breadcrumbs.
5. Bake 0.25 hour in the oven and serve in the mold.
6. Serve with fruit sauce or fruit compote.
