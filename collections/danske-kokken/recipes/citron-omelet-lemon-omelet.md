## Ingredients

- grated lemon zest
- 6 drams powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 6 egg whites
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat together the grated lemon zest, 6 drams powdered sugar and 6 egg yolks
2. Beat in 6 tablespoons melted butter and 2 tablespoons flour
3. Finally beat in the 6 egg whites, first beaten to stiff peaks, and a few drops of lemon juice
4. Bake the omelet in a moderate oven for 2 hours
