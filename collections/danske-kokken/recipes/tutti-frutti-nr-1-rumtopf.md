## Ingredients

- 5 kg (10 S) crushed powdered sugar
- 1 bottle Jamaica rum
- 5 kg (10 S) fruit: strawberries (1/2 kg (1 S) berries and 1/2 kg (1 S) sugar), raspberries, various types of cherries (pitted), apricots (halved and pitted), peaches (skin removed and pitted), mirabelles (halved and pitted), Reine-Claude plums (halved and pitted), prune plums (halved and pitted), melon (peeled and cut into small pieces), mulberries, blackberries

## Directions

1. Select fruits that are completely spotless and not overripe
2. Do not peel them until just before layering with sugar and rum
3. Begin with strawberries: place 1/2 kg (1 S) berries and 1/2 kg (1 S) sugar plus some rum on the bottom of a large crock and stir once so the sugar can dissolve
4. Cover the crock with a well-fitting lid and wrap with parchment paper
5. Continue layering with other berries and fruits throughout the summer in the same manner
6. Stir occasionally
7. After one month from when the last fruit was added, it is ready for use
8. Serve with roasts as a compote or use as a dessert
9. The liquid that forms can be strained through a fine sieve if not used with the fruit and served as an aromatic liqueur
