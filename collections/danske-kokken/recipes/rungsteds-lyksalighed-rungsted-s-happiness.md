## Ingredients

- 10 egg whites
- 10 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons raspberry jam (without juice)
- 10 egg yolks
- 125 gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) boiling milk
- vanilla essence
- 4 sheets isinglass
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) heavy cream
- macarons

## Directions

1. Beat the 10 egg whites until very stiff.
2. Carefully stir in 10 tablespoons powdered sugar and 2 tablespoons raspberry jam without juice.
3. Pour onto flat baking sheets and dry in the oven like meringue. This must be done the day before.
4. On the day of serving, stir the 10 egg yolks (which have been covered) with 125 gr powdered sugar and add to 1/2 L boiling milk.
5. Make a good cream and add vanilla essence and 4 sheets isinglass dissolved in a small amount of water.
6. Stir the cream regularly until it is almost completely cold.
7. Pour over the egg whites and when set, top with whipped cream made from 1/2 L heavy cream.
8. Serve with macarons.
