## Ingredients

- 1 hen
- white root vegetables
- beef
- water
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 pint white wine (optional)
- nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook a hen with white root vegetables and beef in so much water that it stands over it
2. When it is done, take it out and cut the meat from the bones in small pieces; the bones are cut up and cook somewhat longer in the soup
3. Strain this and place it back on the fire with the cooked white root vegetables and chicken meat to get a little boil
4. Then pour it into the tureen in which 4 egg yolks have been beaten
5. It is good to have 1/2 pint white wine and a little nutmeg in the eggs
6. Eaten with toasted bread
