## Ingredients

- peeled pumpkin
- water or thin meat broth with salt
- piece of lemon zest
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- finely chopped parsley
- cold butter

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled pumpkin into cubes
2. Cook these in water with salt or preferably in thin meat broth
3. Drain the pumpkin through a sieve
4. Use the cooking liquid to make a white, smooth sauce
5. Bring a small piece of lemon zest to a boil in the sauce to flavor it
6. Thicken the sauce with a couple of egg yolks
7. Give it a sour taste with lemon juice
8. Add finely chopped parsley and a slice of cold butter to the sauce
9. Allow the butter to melt before pouring the sauce over the hot cooked pumpkin
10. Remove the lemon zest before serving
11. Serve as a vegetable side dish with fried fish
