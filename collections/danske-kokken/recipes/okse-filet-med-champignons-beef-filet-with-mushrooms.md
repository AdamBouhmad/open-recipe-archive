## Ingredients

- Beef filet, roasted
- Brown mushroom sauce (see recipe 758)
- Stuffed tomatoes (see recipe 236)
- Parsley for garnish
- Mushroom
- Salad
- Tomato
- Whole mushrooms

## Directions

1. Serve the roasted filet with a brown mushroom sauce made from the gravy
2. Garnish the dish with stuffed tomatoes interspersed with parsley
3. A skewer can be passed through a mushroom, salad and a tomato, and used to garnish the filet
4. Some whole mushrooms can be placed on top of the meat
