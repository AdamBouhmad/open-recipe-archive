## Ingredients

- Fried eggs
- Mushrooms
- Strong gravy
- Madeira
- Maggi bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Give some mushrooms a boil in a strong gravy
2. Add a little Madeira and Maggi bouillon cubes
3. Pour the gravy around the fried eggs
