## Ingredients

- Scrambled eggs
- Cooked spinach
- Watercress
- Smoked salmon
- Hard-boiled egg, sliced

## Directions

1. These 2 types of prepared warm vegetables are placed side by side in a warm gratin shell. Over the eggs sprinkle watercress, and place 2 thin slices of smoked salmon rolled together like snails on the eggs. Cover the spinach with a slice of hard-boiled egg. Place the shells on a platter lined with a nicely folded napkin.
