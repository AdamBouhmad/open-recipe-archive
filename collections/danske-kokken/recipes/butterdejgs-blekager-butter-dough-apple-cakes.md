## Ingredients

- 375 grams flour
- 375 grams margarine
- 175 milliliters water
- 1 shot glass vinegar
- 1.25 kilograms sour apples
- cinnamon
- sugar
- egg
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make butter dough from flour, margarine, water, and vinegar
2. Roll out a very thin layer that covers the baking sheet completely
3. Peel the apples, divide into 4 pieces, and remove the cores
4. Rinse them and place in a saucepan with a little water and cook until partly tender, but not until they become mush
5. Sweeten and cool before spreading over the dough and sprinkling with cinnamon
6. Roll out the remaining dough into a similar sheet and cover the apples with it
7. Press the cake together along the edges, brush with egg, and sprinkle with sugar
8. Bake in a well-heated oven for approximately half an hour
9. When the sheet is taken from the oven, cut the cake into small, square cakes
10. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve lukewarm
11. Can be decorated with whipped cream
12. These cakes can be made with different fruits, first cooked with sugar, such as gooseberries, rhubarb, plums, and dried apricots
