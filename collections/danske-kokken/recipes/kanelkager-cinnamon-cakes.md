## Ingredients

- 6 whole eggs
- 1 pound light sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 1 ounce ground cinnamon
- 1 small teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat 6 whole eggs, one at a time, with 1 pound light sugar until white.
2. Stir in 1 pound flour, 1 ounce ground cinnamon, and 1 small teaspoon baking powder.
3. Place in small lumps on a cold pan that has first been buttered or greased with a spatula, and bake with good heat.
4. Makes approximately 80 cakes.
