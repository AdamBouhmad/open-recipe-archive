## Ingredients

- 175 grams (3/4 lb) Pillsbury flour
- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) margarine
- 100 grams (20 quintal) icing sugar

## Directions

1. Mix together flour, margarine and icing sugar
2. Roll out and cut with a cake cutter into long cakes
3. Brush with egg
4. Sprinkle with icing sugar and caraway seeds
5. Bake in a well-heated oven
