## Ingredients

- Kallun, Fedetarme og Fødder af 5 Lam (tripe, mesentery, and feet from 5 lambs)
- 5 til 6 Potter Vand (water)
- Salt
- En Suppevisk (a bunch of herbs)
- Smaa, fine Supperødder (small soup vegetables)
- Gulerødder (carrots)
- Grønærter (peas)
- Lidt afvadskede Rosiner (raisins, rinsed)
- Et godt Fjerdingspund Ris (a good quarter pound of rice)
- Finthakket Kruspersille (finely chopped parsley)

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe, mesentery, and feet from 5 lambs with great care.
2. Scrape the tripe several times, placing it in cold salted water between each scraping.
3. Clean the mesentery first on the outside, then cut it open and clean the inside.
4. When the feet are cleaned, split the bones.
5. Cut the tripe into small cubes and place with the feet in 5 to 6 pots of water.
6. Bring to a boil and skim; add the mesentery (also cut into small cubes), salt, and a bunch of herbs.
7. Cook until the tripe is tender.
8. Add small soup vegetables, especially carrots, shelled peas, and rinsed raisins, along with rice prepared as described in recipe No. 6.
9. When the vegetables are tender, serve as hot as possible with finely chopped parsley.
