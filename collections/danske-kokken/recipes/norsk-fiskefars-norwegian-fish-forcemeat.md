## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg scraped fish
- 100 grams cold butter
- 2 tablespoons flour, pounded
- ground white pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. In Norway, pollack is much used for forcemeat
2. Split and clean the fish, remove the bone
3. Scrape very carefully (do not chop) and pound in a mortar until it is like a very fine dough, finally pound it with salt to make it even more tough and cohesive
4. Stir the cold butter until it is white
5. Add 2 tablespoons pounded flour, ground white pepper and a little milk until it becomes a soft porridge
6. When well stirred together, work in the scraped fish and stir the forcemeat with quite small amounts of milk at a time until it reaches proper firmness, which is tested by cooking a test ball
7. This makes an excellent forcemeat
