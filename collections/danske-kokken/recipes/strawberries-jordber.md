## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- A few spoonfuls brandy
- 1 pound strawberries
- 1 pound large, not too ripe strawberries
- 1/2 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar into syrup with a few spoonfuls of brandy
2. Add 1 pound of strawberries and bring to a boil once
3. Pour out and let stand 3 to 4 days
4. Take large, not too ripe strawberries and pour the juice from the first batch over them
5. Let stand several more days
6. Carefully remove the strawberries into a sieve
7. Strain the juice again so the seeds come out and bring to a boil with 1/2 pound sugar
8. Pour this cold over the berries
9. The strawberries should be picked the same day they are preserved, otherwise they will taste bitter when preserved
