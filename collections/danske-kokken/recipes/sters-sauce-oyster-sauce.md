## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Water or thin Soup
- Butter
- Flour
- White Wine
- Lemon Juice
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Meat Soup
- small Fish balls
- Chestnuts
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Remove the beards from the Oysters, boil them very briefly in a little Water or thin Soup and strain.
2. Melt a piece of Butter, stir the Flour into it, and thin it with the strained Soup, together with white Wine, a little Lemon Juice, Salt, Nutmeg and so much Meat Soup that it becomes appropriately thick.
3. Add the Oysters last, and they must not be boiled, but only become warm.
4. Very small Fish balls may be added, which must be boiled in the Soup before it is used for the Sauce.
5. Boiled and peeled Chestnuts may also be added together with the Oysters.
6. Thicken it with a little Egg yolk.
