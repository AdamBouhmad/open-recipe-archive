## Ingredients

- 1/2 quarter pound butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 4.5 pounds powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon pounded bitter almonds
- 1 pound fine flour
- 3 quintals cream of tartar
- 1.5 quintals baking soda
- 2 cups boiled, cold milk
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir the butter white with 4 egg yolks, 4.5 pounds powdered sugar, and 1 tablespoon pounded bitter almonds
2. Mix 1 pound fine flour with 3 quintals cream of tartar and 1.5 quintals baking soda, and gradually stir in 2 cups boiled, cold milk
3. Finally add the whipped egg whites
4. Pour the batter into a form and bake for 1 hour at even heat
