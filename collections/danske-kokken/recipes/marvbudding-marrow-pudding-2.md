## Ingredients

- half a pound ox marrow
- cold water
- two old 10-ore French breads
- sweet milk or thin cream
- half a pound butter

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound ox marrow into pieces, place in cold water and then on a colander so the water can drip off, then place the marrow on a clean linen cloth. Two old 10-ore French breads, from which the crust has been scraped off, are soaked in sweet milk or thin cream, pressed out and baked with half a pound butter.
