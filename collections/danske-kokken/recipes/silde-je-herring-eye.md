## Ingredients

- Herring (Matjes, cured, spiced, boneless herring or anchovies)
- Finely chopped onion
- One raw egg yolk

## Directions

1. Chop the herring and lay them in a ring on a small plate or compote plate
2. Around the chopped herring lay very little finely chopped onion; too much of it easily makes the flavor too prominent
3. In the middle of this arrangement lay one whole raw egg yolk and place it on the table thus
4. Place a spoon on each plate, and with it mix the whole together after sitting down to table
5. Herring Eye is eaten like caviar, spread on bread or biscuits, and is used in sandwiches
