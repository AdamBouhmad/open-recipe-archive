## Ingredients

- turkey
- medisterpølse sausage
- bread crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. If using a male turkey, after cleaning, make a small cut in the leg joint without cutting through it.
2. Push the leg bone through the crack on the back of a door, and while one person presses the door together on the bone, another person pulls off the lower leg so that the hard sinews in the thigh come out with it.
3. Fill the turkey with medisterpølse sausage stuffing mixed with bread crumbs and a beaten egg.
4. Roast as usual.
5. Serve with small medisterpølse sausages.
