## Ingredients

- White ginger, fine and smooth
- Water
- Soda lye
- Powdered sugar, 1 pound per pound of ginger

## Directions

1. Beat the white ginger for a while to check if it is dried; discard any dried pieces, but keep the good ones
2. Weigh the good ginger and place in cold water for 24 hours
3. Prepare a weak soda lye; when the liquid is strong enough that it feels smooth to the fingers, strain off the clear liquid
4. Place the ginger in this liquid until it feels soft to the touch
5. Rinse it for 8 days, changing the water every day
6. Weigh the sugar, 1 pound for every pound of ginger
7. Boil the sugar and water clear as usual, then boil the ginger in it until it is so soft that it can be pierced with a fine straw
8. Pour together but do not seal until it has cooled
