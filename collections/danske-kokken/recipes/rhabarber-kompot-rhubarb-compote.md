## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) rhubarb stalks
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) powdered Melis sugar
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægel) water

## Directions

1. Use stalks of medium size
2. Remove the outer skin and cut the stalks into thumb-length pieces
3. Rinse the cut stalks
4. Make a syrup by cooking the powdered Melis sugar with the water
5. Cook the rhubarb in this syrup
6. Remove the pieces gradually and place in the serving bowl
7. Either boil the juice down to a smooth syrup or thicken slightly with a little sago flour and pour over the fruit
