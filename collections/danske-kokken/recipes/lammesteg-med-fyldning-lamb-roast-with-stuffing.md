## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Parsley, picked and rinsed
- Butter
- Bread dough (Nr. 492)
- Ground almonds (optional)
- Currants (optional)
- Cardamom (optional)

## Directions

1. If the shoulder remains attached to the roast, fill it either with parsley that has been picked and rinsed with a piece of butter added, or with a bread dough which may be seasoned to taste with ground almonds, currants, and a small amount of cardamom.
2. Open the shoulder on one side enough to get your hand between the membranes to loosen the outer layer.
3. If there is a hole in the membrane, it must be carefully sewn up before filling is inserted.
4. Sew tightly before the dough, as if too much is filled, the membrane will easily burst.
5. Sprinkle the roast with parsley and roast as described.
6. When carving, ensure that some of the stuffing is cut and served along with the meat.
