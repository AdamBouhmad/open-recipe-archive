## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) Quince
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) powdered Melis sugar
- 2 1/2 Pint (1/2 Pægel) water from cooking the quince

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces until almost tender in water
2. Peel and cut into 4 pieces
3. Mix the powdered Melis sugar with 2 1/2 Pint (1/2 Pægel) of the water in which they were cooked and boil together
4. Cook the quince pieces in this syrup until completely tender
5. Remove the fruit and place in a serving bowl
6. Either boil the juice a bit longer or thicken slightly with a little sago flour and pour over the fruit
