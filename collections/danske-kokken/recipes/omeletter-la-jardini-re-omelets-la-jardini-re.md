## Ingredients

- 12 eggs or similar amount
- butter
- various cooked vegetables
- artichoke hearts or cardoons
- head lettuce for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks well
2. Stir in milk or cream and salt
3. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Divide the egg mixture into 3 omelets
5. As each omelet is completed and cooked, arrange it on a warm platter
6. Belege each omelet with different vegetables before folding
7. As each omelet is finished, folded, and topped with vegetables, place side by side on the warm platter and keep in the oven until all are ready to serve
8. In addition to the usual vegetables, artichoke hearts or cardoons can be used
9. Place a stripe of cooked vegetables on top of each omelet to indicate its contents
10. Garnish the ends of the platter with head lettuce
11. Serve immediately
