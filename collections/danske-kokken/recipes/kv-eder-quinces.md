## Ingredients

- Quinces
- One pound quinces (prepared)
- One pound sugar, white or brown
- One pint water

## Directions

1. Place the quinces in boiling water and cook until they begin to soften.
2. Remove, peel, and cut according to size into 4 or 8 pieces.
3. Remove the seed cavities and all the hard matter surrounding them; weigh the pieces.
4. For 1 pound quinces, take 1 pound sugar, white or brown, and place on heat with one pint water.
5. When the first scum is removed, add the quinces and skim well.
6. Cook evenly for one hour or longer, if needed, until they are completely tender and the syrup is even.
7. Pour into a vessel, spread cloth over, and after a day, cover with paper dipped in brandy and tie.
