## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg strawberries
- 4.5 L water
- potato flour
- sugar
- wine

## Directions

1. Cook strawberries in water
2. Strain and bring to boil again
3. Sweeten and thicken with 2 tablespoons potato flour mixed with water
4. Season with wine to taste
