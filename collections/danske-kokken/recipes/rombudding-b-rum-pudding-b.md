## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons finely ground melis sugar
- 1 pail rum
- 1 pot cream
- 10 quintals isinglass dissolved in boiling water

## Directions

1. Stir 4 egg yolks well with 4 tablespoons finely ground melis sugar.
2. Add 1 pail rum.
3. Whip 1 pot cream to thick foam and stir it into the egg yolks.
4. Finally add 10 quintals isinglass that has been cooled after dissolving in 1 pail boiling water.
5. Pour the mixture into a glass bowl and set to cool, and half an hour later the pudding is stiff and can be arranged.
6. Serve with sauce made from fruit juice.
