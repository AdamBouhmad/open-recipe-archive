## Ingredients

- Beef loin
- Butter
- Paper
- Flour
- Reduced gravy
- Food coloring
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Cooked vegetables (Nr. 733)
- Glazed onions (Nr. 671)
- Potato croquettes (Nr. 14)
- Purée (Nr. 725-732)
- English sauces

## Directions

1. Remove the loin, roast and carve it like lamb loin (Nr. 858).
2. The flaps must remain attached to the loin and be tucked underneath it, and the whole is bound with a band, covered with butter and wrapped with paper.
3. During roasting, which takes approximately 2 to 3 hours depending on the size of the loin, it must be frequently basted.
4. Make a sauce by cooking butter and flour together, which is diluted with the strained and skimmed gravy.
5. Give the sauce color with food coloring and season with pepper, salt, and lemon juice.
6. Garnish with cooked vegetables, glazed onions, potato croquettes, and purée.
7. Serve English sauces mixed with the gravy sauce.
