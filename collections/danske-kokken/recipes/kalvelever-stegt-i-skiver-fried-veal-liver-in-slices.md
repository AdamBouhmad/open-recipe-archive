## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices, season, dip in beaten egg and grated breadcrumbs, and fry in butter.
2. Alternatively, dip in flour and fry in butter.
3. Serve with slices of fried bacon and applesauce that is not too sweet.
