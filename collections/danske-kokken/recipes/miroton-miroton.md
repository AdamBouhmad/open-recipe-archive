## Ingredients

- Cut beef roast
- Stock from the roast
- Potato puree (No. 651)
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Pickled cucumbers, chopped

## Directions

1. Place the cut beef roast on a flat dish that can be exposed to heat in the oven
2. Pour a good beef sauce made from the stock of the roast over the meat
3. Cover the meat with a layer of potato puree (No. 651), raised in the middle
4. Brush with egg
5. Sprinkle with bread
6. Place in the oven until it has a brownish color
7. Chopped, pickled cucumbers can be placed as decoration on top of the puree
