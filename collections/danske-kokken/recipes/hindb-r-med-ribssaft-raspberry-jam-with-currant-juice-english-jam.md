## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/4 liter raw strained currant juice
- 1/2 kg raspberries

## Directions

1. Remove stems from the raspberries and place them in a pot.
2. Place over low heat and boil for 15 minutes, stirring and skimming constantly.
3. Add the currant juice and sugar.
4. Boil again for 1/2 hour.
5. Pour the preserve into jars and tie them up immediately.
