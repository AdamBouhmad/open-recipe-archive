## Ingredients

- Peeled pineapple
- Sugar
- Water
- Juice of 1 or 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Cut peeled pineapple into very small pieces and cook with sugar and water.
2. Strain the syrup, add the strained juice of 1 or 2 lemons and freeze as explained in the previous recipe.
