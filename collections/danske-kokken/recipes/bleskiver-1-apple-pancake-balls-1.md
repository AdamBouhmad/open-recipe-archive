## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 tb) fine flour
- 1 L (1 Pot) half-skimmed milk
- 4 egg yolks
- a little sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- lemon or orange essence
- cardamom
- 1 teaspoon Hjortetaksalt
- egg whites
- clarified butter or butter

## Directions

1. In fine flour, stir 1 L (1 Pot) half-skimmed milk, 4 egg yolks, a little sugar, 1 teaspoon salt, lemon or orange essence, cardamom and 1 teaspoon Hjortetaksalt together, and lastly add the beaten egg whites.
2. The apple pancake ball pan must be hot, and the holes well supplied with clarified butter or butter or a mixture of both, which is easiest done if melted in a small pot.
3. The dough is best poured from a pitcher, and the holes are filled halfway with dough.
4. When the apple pancake balls are baked on one side, before they are turned, a little apple sauce, a piece of cooked apple or rhubarb compote can be placed in each, and the apple pancake balls are then baked finished on the other side, but it is preferable to serve the fruit separately, as the apple pancake balls will rise better that way.
5. They must be served as hot as possible and sprinkled with sugar.
6. If yeast is used for the apple pancake balls, use 40 Gr (8 Kvint), 4 eggs, 2 to 3 tablespoons powdered sugar, cardamom, 1/2 Kg (1 S) flour and 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) lukewarm milk, in which 75 Gr (15 Kvint) margarine is melted. Set to rise for a couple of hours.
