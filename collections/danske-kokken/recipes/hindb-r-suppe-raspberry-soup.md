## Ingredients

- 1 kg raspberries
- 0.5 kg currants
- 4.5 L water
- potato flour
- vanilla essence or vanilla tablets

## Directions

1. Cook raspberries and currants for half an hour in 4.5 L water
2. Strain and bring to boil again
3. Thicken with potato flour mixed with water
4. Add a few drops of vanilla essence or a couple of vanilla tablets
