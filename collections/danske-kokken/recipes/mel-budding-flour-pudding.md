## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons flour
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 3.5 gills milk
- 8 egg yolks
- A handful sugar
- A little cardamom
- A little salt
- Egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Brown the flour well in the butter; heat the milk and pour it in gradually, little by little.
2. When this is well baked, remove from heat and cool it.
3. Stir the egg yolks, sugar, cardamom, and a little salt together very well for some time; lastly add the beaten egg whites.
4. Boil for 2 hours and serve with fruit or wine sauce.
5. Cannot be baked.
