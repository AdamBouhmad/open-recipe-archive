## Ingredients

- veal cutlets from shoulder
- egg
- crushed zwieback
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- stock

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets from the shoulder as described previously, chop with the back of a knife and shape
2. Dip them in melted butter, coat lightly with crushed zwieback, salt and pepper
3. Fry over high heat in a pan with lightly browned butter, turning frequently until they are firm and a light golden brown
4. If the butter in the pan diminishes and begins to steam, add more butter
5. Deglaze the remaining butter with a little stock and serve as sauce with the cutlets alongside boiled, peeled potatoes or stewed mushrooms
