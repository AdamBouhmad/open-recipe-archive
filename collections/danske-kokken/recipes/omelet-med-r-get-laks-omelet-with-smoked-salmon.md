## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 10 tablespoons milk or cream
- salt
- egg whites, stiffly beaten
- butter
- smoked salmon, cut into very small pieces
- cooked smoked ham, optional
- watercress

## Directions

1. Cut the smoked salmon into very small pieces and stir into the eggs
2. Beat egg yolks well with the salmon pieces
3. Stir in 10 tablespoons milk or cream and a small amount of salt
4. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Place a good slice of butter on the pan and when it boils, pour in the eggs
6. With a fork or knife, lightly prick the eggs and constantly shake the pan so the omelet does not burn or form a hard crust
7. Bake and fold together
8. Alternatively, cooked smoked ham can be chopped coarsely or cut small and used in the same manner
9. Sprinkle watercress over the omelet
10. Serve immediately
