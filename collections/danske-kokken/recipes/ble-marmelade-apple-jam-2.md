## Ingredients

- Apples
- Powdered sugar (125 gr to 1/2 kg apple pulp)
- Water (1 1/4 dl per 1/2 kg apples)

## Directions

1. The apples for this can either be peeled first with the seed cavity and blossom end removed, or they can simply be wiped dry and cut in 4 pieces.
2. The cooking must take place over very low heat and covered until the apples are completely cooked down.
3. Press the pulp through a coarse sieve and weigh it.
4. The sugar must be added only after the pulp has been strained through the sieve.
5. Cook the jam down until it is sufficiently stiff.
6. Fill it into jars, cover with paper and tie up.
7. The English often add a portion of lemon juice to the apple pulp. Chopped or grated almonds or nuts can be stirred into the jam when using it.
