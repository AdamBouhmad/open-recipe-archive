## Ingredients

- 2 pints French white wine (Graves)
- 1 pint water
- 16 beaten egg yolks
- 1 pound white sugar
- zest of 3 lemons
- juice of 3 lemons
- 8 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, place 2 pints of French white wine (Graves), 1 pint of water, 16 beaten egg yolks, 1 pound of white sugar, on which the zest of 3 lemons has been grated, and the strained juice of 3 lemons.
2. Set all this over the heat and warm it until it begins to boil, then remove it, and stir in 8 beaten egg whites.
3. The cream is whisked until it is cold.
4. It is arranged in a deep dish and garnished with powdered sugar.
5. Biscuits are served with it.
