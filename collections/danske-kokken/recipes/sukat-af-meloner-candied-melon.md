## Ingredients

- A melon that is not fully ripe
- Sugar brine

## Directions

1. Peel a melon that is not fully ripe, hollow it out and cut into finger-long pieces, about an inch wide and half as thick, weigh them, place in a brine that is so strong that it can just make an egg float, and let stand for 4 days
2. Then take the pieces out and place for 3 days
