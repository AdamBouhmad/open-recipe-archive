## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Melted butter
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook mussels as directed in No. 8
2. Bring cooked mussels steaming to the table
3. Arrange unopened in a deep dish, covered with a napkin
4. Serve with melted butter, vinegar and pepper
