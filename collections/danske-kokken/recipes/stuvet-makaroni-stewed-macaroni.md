## Ingredients

- Macaroni, broken into pieces and cooked
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Salt
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Make a sauce with butter and flour.
2. Dilute with good milk and season with salt and a little sugar.
3. Turn the cooked macaroni into the sauce.
