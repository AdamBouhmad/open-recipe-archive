## Ingredients

- Large Spanish onions
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Brown butter

## Directions

1. Peel large Spanish onions and cut into not too thin slices, which are dipped in egg and ground bread and fried light brown and tender in butter on a pan. Arrange the cutlets on a hot dish, and a round fried egg is placed on each. Browned butter can be poured on the dish or served in a sauce boat.
