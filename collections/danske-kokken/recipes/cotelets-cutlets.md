## Ingredients

- Veal, lamb, or pork ribs
- Green vegetables or potatoes
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cutlets are made from veal as well as from lamb and pork
2. For this, ribs are bought
3. The butcher cuts it into slices with a bone to each; from lamb meat one must place two bones to each piece so that it becomes thick enough, but only one remains attached
4. Serve with green vegetables or potatoes and no other sauce than that which remains on the platter, for which reason a little good butter is placed on it
