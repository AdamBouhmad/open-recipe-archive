## Ingredients

- 5 bottles pale ale
- 7 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 Kg (1 S) sugar
- rye bread or French bread cubes baked in butter and sugar, or small rusks

## Directions

1. While the ale is brought to a boil, beat 7 egg yolks and 3 egg whites with 1/2 Kg (1 S) sugar in a tureen until foaming.
2. When pouring, place the tureen with the egg mixture down on the floor, and pour the ale into it under constant whisking with a whisk.
3. At first hold the pot right down to the tureen, but raise it as high as possible during pouring. In this way the egg drink foams well.
4. Serve with rye bread or French bread cubes baked in butter and sugar, or with small rusks.
