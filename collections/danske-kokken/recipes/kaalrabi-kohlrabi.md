## Ingredients

- Kohlrabi
- Bouillon
- Fresh butter
- A little sugar
- Salt
- Flour
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove all hard parts, cut the kohlrabi into half-moon shaped pieces and clean them
2. Place over heat with bouillon, fresh butter, a little sugar, and salt, and cook gently
3. Thicken the stock with flour and chopped parsley and bring to a boil
4. Serve with veal or lamb cutlets
