## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Parsley finely chopped
- Cold fresh butter
- Brown butter for sauce

## Directions

1. Scald the chicken in hot water so the feathers come off easily; place them immediately in lukewarm water when they are opened
2. To remove the small pointed breast bone that gives the chicken a lean appearance when roasted, insert your hand into the chicken with the thumb on the outside of the breast; press on the breast bone and a small triangle of it will give way immediately and turn inward when removed
3. Turn the head back under one wing
4. Place the giblets and heart under the other wing; then insert a pin through one wing and fasten it
5. Press the legs back into the skin and turn the bone back from the knee joints; insert a pin through these as well so they stay in this position
6. Fill with finely chopped parsley mixed with cold fresh butter
7. Roast best in a pot because it has no fat, so it can easily become too dry in a roasting oven if not constantly basted
8. For sauce use only brown butter
9. Can be roasted in about one hour
