## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 4½ pounds sugar
- rind of 2 lemons
- juice of 2 lemons
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir together 10 egg yolks, 4½ pounds sugar and the grated rind of 2 lemons for approximately 1 hour.
2. Add the juice of the 2 lemons and the egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour this mixture into a large, deep dish buttered well.
4. Place in a gently heated oven until it has risen.
5. Serve warm with a napkin around the dish and powdered sugar.
