## Ingredients

- Beef rib section
- String
- Small onion
- Half carrot
- Half turnip
- Three parsley stems
- One thyme stem
- Three bay leaves
- Salt
- Half pint hot water
- Flour (optional)

## Directions

1. Have the butcher prepare the rib section, removing the bones and holding the piece in place with strong string tied around it.
2. Place the roast on a roasting pan on a bed of vegetables: one small onion, half a carrot, half a turnip, three parsley stems, one thyme stem, and three bay leaves.
3. Do not add water to the pan.
4. Do not add salt until the surface has contracted.
5. If making sauce, remove the vegetables and add half a pint of hot water to the roasting pan.
6. Boil rapidly for a few minutes with the pan juices until the sauce is thick enough, then salt to taste.
7. Some thicken the sauce with one teaspoon of flour mixed with two tablespoons of cold water before stirring it into the sauce.
