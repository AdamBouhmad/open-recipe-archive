## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt
- A little baking soda
- Cream, milk or soup
- Butter
- Flour
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach and remove the coarse stalks, rinse in several changes of water
2. Place in boiling water with salt and a little baking soda and cook quickly
3. Drain on a colander and rinse with cold water, pressing well to remove moisture
4. Spinach can be chopped very fine or pounded in a mortar
5. Make a sauce with butter and a little flour, thin with cream, milk or soup
6. Add the spinach and bring to a boil, season with sugar, salt and cold butter
7. Alternatively, chop the spinach with a spoonful of flour, place in a pot, bring to a boil and add cream, milk or soup plus butter, sugar and salt
8. Or chop raw spinach using a meat grinder and stir into a sauce as described above, then cook and reduce well
9. Arrange in a serving dish with a ring of sliced hard-boiled eggs around it
