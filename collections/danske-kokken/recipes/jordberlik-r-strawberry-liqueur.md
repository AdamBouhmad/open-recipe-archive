## Ingredients

- 24 pounds strawberries
- 23 quarts sugar solution (approximately 0.5 pound sugar to scarcely 3 quarts water)
- 13 pints fine spirits

## Directions

1. Let the strawberries stand in the sugar solution for 2 days, press through a sieve, mix with the spirits and filter.
