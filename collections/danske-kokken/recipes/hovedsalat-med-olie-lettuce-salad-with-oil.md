## Ingredients

- 1 head lettuce
- fine cooking oil
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- wine vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the lettuce head as described in the cream salad recipe
2. Pour some spoonfuls of fine cooking oil over it and gently toss with a fork and spoon
3. Work 2 hard-boiled egg yolks well with 2 to 3 spoonfuls of wine vinegar, pepper, and salt, and a little powdered sugar if desired, all to taste
4. Pour this sauce over the salad
