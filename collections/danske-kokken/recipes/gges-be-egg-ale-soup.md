## Ingredients

- 1 gallon ale
- 12 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- sugar
- rye bread cubes
- butter

## Directions

1. Place 1 gallon of ale over the heat
2. Stir 12 egg yolks and 4 whites with sugar until white and thick
3. When the ale boils, pour it in under strong whisking, first a little, then more
4. Continue whisking for a couple of minutes, then pour into the terrine
5. Rye bread cubes fried in butter and sugar are served with it
6. If only lager ale or beer is available, it should first be diluted with water
7. Serves 8 persons
