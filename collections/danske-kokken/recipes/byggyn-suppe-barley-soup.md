## Ingredients

- 1¼ L fine barley grains
- 4½ L water
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon
- Prunes
- Peeled apples
- Fruit juice

## Directions

1. When the water boils, add lemon peel, whole cinnamon and the grains while stirring until it boils again; then cover to prevent skin from forming on the soup.
2. It must cook very gently for at least 2 hours.
3. Prepare the soup with juice.
4. Cook prunes and peeled, cut apples in the soup.
5. It can be given more color with fruit coloring if the juice used to prepare it does not color it.
