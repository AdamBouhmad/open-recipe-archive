## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 375 grams margarine
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 liter half-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix the Pillsbury flour and baking powder together
2. Add the margarine, powdered sugar, eggs, and half-skimmed milk
3. Knead the batter well and roll out to a 1/4 inch thick sheet
4. Cut out round cakes and place close together on a baking sheet
5. Bake immediately in a fairly warm oven for approximately 20 minutes
6. While warm, split the cakes, spread with butter, and fold together
7. Serve immediately or reheat in the oven for a few minutes before serving. Makes 30 muffins
