## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg lingonberries
- 1½ L water
- 1 piece of cinnamon
- Sugar to taste
- Potato flour and sago flour

## Directions

1. Pick and rinse the berries
2. Place over heat with 1½ L water, cinnamon stick, and sugar to taste
3. When cooked through, thicken the juice with a little thinned potato flour and sago flour
4. Serve cold with cream or crème
5. Alternatively, the pudding can be strained and thickened like other fruit puddings
