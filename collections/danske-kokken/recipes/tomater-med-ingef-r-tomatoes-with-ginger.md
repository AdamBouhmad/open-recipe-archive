## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 Q) crushed powdered sugar
- 25 gr (5 Kv.) whole ginger
- 3/4 dl (1/4 Pægl) vinegar
- 1/2 kg (1 Q) tomatoes
- Rum with dissolved salicylic acid

## Directions

1. Peel the skin from very large ripe tomatoes, then section them like oranges
2. Layer them with sugar in a crock and pour vinegar over them
3. Let them sit for one day
4. Pour off the syrup and boil it for 10 minutes together with the blanched and scraped ginger
5. Pour the boiling liquid over the tomatoes
6. The next day, pour off the liquid and boil it again
7. On the third day, remove the tomatoes, place them in jars, and pour the boiling, newly reduced liquid over them
8. Pour a generous amount of rum with dissolved salicylic acid into each jar (1/2 teaspoon to 2 1/2 to 3 kg (5 to 6 S) tomatoes)
9. Seal immediately
