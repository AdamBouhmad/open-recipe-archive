## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- forcemeat, preferably of veal or pork
- beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- Palmin
- brown gravy sauce
- lettuce head
- small roasted sausages

## Directions

1. Cover hard-boiled eggs with forcemeat
2. Smooth them, roll in beaten egg and breadcrumbs
3. Fry in Palmin for a few minutes until the forcemeat is golden brown
4. Cut the hot eggs once lengthwise and arrange them with the cut side up in a circle on a hot platter
5. Place a rinsed and well-dried lettuce head in the center of the platter
6. Serve with a piquant brown gravy sauce
7. Small roasted sausages can be placed around the eggs
