## Ingredients

- 2 large eels
- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- 4 quarts strong bouillon
- About 1 pint port wine
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons Harvey sauce
- 1 tablespoon anchovy essence
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones along the entire length of the eel and cut into pieces of 1 inch square.
2. Brown the butter, onions and flour over gentle heat for 5 minutes and dilute with good bouillon.
3. When it boils, add the eel bones, let them cook for a few minutes, then skim the soup and strain it into a casserole.
4. Add the eel pieces and at the same time add the Harvey sauce, parsley, port wine, salt and sugar.
5. Return the soup to the heat, boil for a few minutes, skim well and serve.
