## Ingredients

- 100 Gr (20 Kvint) green almonds (pistachios)
- a little sugar
- 1 L (1 Pot) milk
- egg yolks and isinglass as in vanilla cream Nr. 1060
- whipped cream for decoration
- macaroons

## Directions

1. Pound the green almonds (pistachios) very finely with a little sugar and place in 1 L (1 Pot) milk.
2. When it boils, strain it through a sieve onto the stirred egg yolks.
3. Stiffen and treat the cream as vanilla cream Nr. 1060.
4. Serve with whipped cream and macaroons.
