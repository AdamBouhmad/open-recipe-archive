## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) peas
- 1/2 kg (3 S) lightly salted pork
- 1 bunch
- 2 celeries
- 5 leeks

## Directions

1. Soak the peas in lukewarm water and let them stand until the next day.
2. Cook them in 3 L (3 pots) water without salt.
3. Cook the pork separately together with a good bunch, 2 celeries and 5 leeks.
4. When the pork is tender, remove it and keep it warm, while the peas are pressed through a sieve with continued dilution with the broth, from which the fat has first been skimmed.
5. Cut the celeries and leeks into small pieces and add them to the peas, which must be served very hot.
6. The pork is eaten with the peas.
