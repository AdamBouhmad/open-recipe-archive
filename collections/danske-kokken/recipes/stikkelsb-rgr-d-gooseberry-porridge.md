## Ingredients

- 3 pots unripe, green gooseberries
- 2 pots water
- The peel of one lemon
- A cinnamon stick
- 1 pot white wine
- Powdered sugar
- Thickening agent: 30 ounces sago flour mixed with a little cold water
- Cold sweet cream for serving
- Vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Pit and rinse the green gooseberries well in cold water, then cook them with 2 pots of water, the lemon peel, and cinnamon stick until completely cooked.
2. Press through a colander several times and then through a fine sieve.
3. Place the 3 pots of strained juice over heat to boil with 1 pot of white wine and powdered sugar to taste.
4. Thicken like Rødgrød with 30 ounces of sago flour mixed first with a little cold water.
5. Serve cold with cold sweet cream in which a little vanilla sugar has been mixed.
6. The wine can be omitted.
