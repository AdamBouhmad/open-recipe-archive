## Ingredients

- Capercaillie and hen (1 pair for 10 to 12 people)
- Lard
- Game sauce
- Cold butter
- Raw cream
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Lingonberry or currant jelly

## Directions

1. Thaw overnight in the kitchen if frozen.
2. Pluck and clean the birds and soak in milk for a few hours.
3. Lard and brown the birds, then roast like pheasant for about 2 hours.
4. Serve with the game sauce in which they were roasted, which can be improved with cold butter, raw cream, and Maggi bouillon cubes.
5. Carve the breast into thin slices and reassemble.
6. Serve with lingonberry or currant jelly.
