## Ingredients

- 2 L (2 Potter) pale ale
- 2 L (2 Potter) milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 Q) powdered sugar
- egg whites
- toasted rye bread cubes

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and ale separately and thicken like Nr. 450.
2. Beat 5 egg yolks in a tureen with 1/4 Kg (1/2 Q) powdered sugar, and pour the boiling ale and milk into it under constant whisking with a whisk.
3. The egg whites, which must be whipped to foam before serving, are added on top of the tureen.
4. Serve toasted rye bread cubes with it. Can also be thickened with Maizena.
