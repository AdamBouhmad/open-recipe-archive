## Ingredients

- Water
- Vinegar
- Eggs

## Directions

1. A frying pan, which must be well filled with boiling water with a tablespoon of vinegar added, is used for this. The eggs are broken in one at a time and carefully so that the yolks remain whole. When the whites are set, the eggs are done. If you wish the yolks to be visible and lie uncovered by the whites, boil the eggs in less water. Take them now out of the water with a slotted spoon, and clean the loose and uneven edges. Poached eggs can be boiled in an egg poacher. The eggs are broken into the cups after the pan is placed in the frying pan, approximately filled with boiling water.
