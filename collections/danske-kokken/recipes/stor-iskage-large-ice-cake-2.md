## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- Equal weight of butter
- Equal weight of very finely powdered sugar
- Equal weight of flour
- 7 to 8 egg whites
- Equal amount of sifted sugar to egg whites, measured in tablespoons
- Vanilla ice cream (Recipe 413)

## Directions

1. Weigh 12 eggs, and take the same weight of each of the following: butter, very finely powdered sugar, and flour.
2. Beat the egg yolks and sugar together in a large bowl until it becomes a white salve.
3. Stir the flour into it and finally the butter, which has first been melted and somewhat cooled.
4. Beat the whites until white foam and blend with the rest.
5. Butter 8 to 10 pieces of paper of the size the ice cake should have, and spread a finger-thick layer of the batter on them, with a suitable opening in the middle so the baked cake has the form of a fairly broad wreath.
6. When the cakes are baked, take them from the paper and lay them on top of each other.
7. Beat 7 to 8 egg whites until foam and blend with the same number of tablespoons of sifted sugar, and brush the cakes with this both on the sides and on top.
8. Put it in a moderately warm oven to dry and lay it on the serving dish.
9. Immediately before serving, fill the opening in the middle with vanilla ice cream (Recipe 413).
