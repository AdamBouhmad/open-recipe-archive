## Ingredients

- Palmin or margarine
- Ground meat
- Cooked leeks of even length
- Browned butter
- Cooked or browned potatoes

## Directions

1. Melt the palmin or margarine on a frying pan
2. Spread a layer of ground meat over the pan
3. Before the meat is cooked on the underside, arrange a star of evenly long cooked leeks on top with their root ends pointing toward the edge
4. Turn the cake onto a lid, fry on the other side
5. Serve with browned butter and cooked or browned potatoes
