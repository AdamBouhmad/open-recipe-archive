## Ingredients

- 1 Pineapple
- 2 Bottles white Wine
- 1/2 Bottle Sherry
- a little Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut a preserved pineapple into slices, which are cut into 4 pieces, sprinkle with a little powdered sugar and let stand with it for a couple of hours, after which it is poured over with the wine and placed on ice to be properly cold when it is to be served with small Macaroons, Flutes or Ice Wafers
2. The cheap, white country wine can be used for this
3. The cold soup can be diluted with Soda Water or Apollinaris to taste
