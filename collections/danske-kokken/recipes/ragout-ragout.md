## Ingredients

- Gravy or soup
- Pepper
- Allspice
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Coloring
- Meat, sliced
- Cooked potatoes or potato croquettes (see Nr. 14)

## Directions

1. Make a brown, smooth gravy sauce from gravy or soup
2. Add pepper, allspice, and a little vinegar
3. Add sugar and coloring to taste to give it a nice color
4. Heat the sliced meat in the sauce
5. Serve with cooked potatoes or potato croquettes
