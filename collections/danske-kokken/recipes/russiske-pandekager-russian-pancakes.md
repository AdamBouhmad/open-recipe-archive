## Ingredients

- 8 Egg Yolks
- 4 Ounces Flour
- 1 Quart Milk
- 3 Tablespoons Sugar
- Egg Whites (whipped)

## Directions

1. Stir 8 Egg Yolks, 4 Ounces Flour, and 1 Quart Milk well together with 3 Tablespoons Sugar
2. Whip the Egg Whites and fold in last
3. Pour the batter very thin on the pan, and when the cake is baked on one side, fold it together without baking on the other, and lay it straight on the plate
4. Preserve can be placed inside
5. Brisk fire is needed
