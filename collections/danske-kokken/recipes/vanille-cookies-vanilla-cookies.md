## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup butter and 2 cups sugar well together.
2. Add 1 cup cold water, 1 teaspoon soda, 2 teaspoons vanilla, and flour to make the dough very stiff.
3. Roll very thin, cut, and bake the cakes brown.
