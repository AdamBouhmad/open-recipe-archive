## Ingredients

- 1 quart thin good cream
- 1 pint grated chocolate
- sugar according to the chocolate's sweetness
- 6 beaten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place 1 quart of thin, good cream, 1 pint of grated chocolate, sugar according to the sweetness of the chocolate, and 6 beaten egg yolks on the fire and beat until it boils and then until it becomes cold.
2. Then treat it as other ice cream.
