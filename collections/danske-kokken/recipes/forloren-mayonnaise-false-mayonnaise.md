## Ingredients

- 30 grams butter
- 60 grams flour
- Broth
- 5 well-beaten egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tarragon vinegar
- 4 tablespoons salad oil

## Directions

1. Make a roux from butter and flour and thin it with enough broth to make a very thick sauce, thickened with the well-beaten egg yolks.
2. Pour out the sauce and stir until it is cold.
3. Under constant stirring add salt, pepper, tarragon vinegar, and 4 tablespoons of salad oil, all to taste.
4. This mayonnaise is easy to make and is preferred by many over the genuine version since it does not contain as much oil.
