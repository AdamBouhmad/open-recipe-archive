## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Larding strips
- Butter or margarine
- Parsley

## Directions

1. The meat is light but fairly dry, so it is recommended to lard and baste it well.
2. Skin, clean, cut up and roast the rabbit as you would hare.
3. Serve as hare or as lamb roast, larded with parsley.
