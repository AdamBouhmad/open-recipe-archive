## Ingredients

- 5 liters sweet milk
- 200 grams cornstarch
- 20 bitter almonds
- 5 eggs
- 0.25 kg powdered sugar
- salt
- vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Bring 5 liters of sweet milk to a boil
2. Mix 6 tablespoons (200 grams) cornstarch in 1 liter cold milk
3. Stir 20 blanched, scalded, grated bitter almonds and the cornstarch mixture into the boiling milk
4. Bring to a boil, season lightly with salt, and pour into a tureen
5. In the tureen, beat 5 egg yolks with 0.25 kg powdered sugar
6. Fold the whipped egg whites gently into the hot gruel with the rice
7. Serve with sugar and cinnamon, or with raspberry jam
