## Ingredients

- Equal amount of cooked fresh corn kernels cut from the cob, or salted corn soaked as described
- Fresh scalded tomatoes, cut into pieces and with the skin removed
- Salt
- Pepper
- A little sugar
- Butter
- Tomato puree (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook equal parts of fresh corn kernels (or soaked salted corn) and fresh tomatoes together gently for about half an hour.
2. Season with salt, pepper and a little sugar.
3. Cook gently for another quarter hour.
4. Stir in a piece of butter, bring to a boil and serve.
5. Tomato puree may be used instead of fresh tomatoes if these are not available at reasonable prices.
