## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Short pastry
- Fish farce
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish stock made from fish bones
- Oyster beards
- Oyster water
- Lemon juice
- Tarragon
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Line the mold as for the preceding pâtés
2. Prepare the oysters and sauce as in recipe 13
3. Fill the pâté with oysters
4. Make a sauce from butter, flour, fish stock made from fish bones from the farce, oyster beards, oyster water, and a few drops of lemon juice
5. Add tarragon to the sauce
6. Thin the remaining sauce and bind with eggs
7. Oysters may be placed in the sauce boat if first scalded with boiling water
8. Bake for about one hour
