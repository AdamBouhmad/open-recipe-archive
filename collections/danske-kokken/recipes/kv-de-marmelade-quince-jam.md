## Ingredients

- Ripe quinces
- Powdered sugar (575 gr to 1/2 kg cooked pulp)
- Water

## Directions

1. Wipe the ripe quinces dry and cut them into 4 to 6 pieces.
2. Put these in a pot with enough water so they can just float in it, and cook until completely soft.
3. Towards the end, stir regularly so that the pulp does not burn on the bottom.
4. Press the quinces through a coarse sieve, then weigh the strained pulp and put it back in the pot together with the sugar.
5. Cook the pulp with stirring, and the jam should, when it is finished cooking, be like a thick, concentrated mass.
6. Fill it into jars, cover with paper and tie up.
