## Ingredients

- 25 gr whole ginger
- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl vinegar
- 1/4 dl water
- 1/2 kg peeled pears

## Directions

1. Soak the ginger overnight
2. Scrape and scald the ginger and cook it together with sugar, vinegar and water to a thin syrup
3. Cook the peeled pears in this syrup until tender
4. Remove pears with a slotted spoon and place in jar
5. Pour the syrup over them unless it is not sufficiently thick and needs further reduction
6. The ginger can either remain in the jar with the pears or be removed
7. Seal the jar as soon as the fruit is poured into it
