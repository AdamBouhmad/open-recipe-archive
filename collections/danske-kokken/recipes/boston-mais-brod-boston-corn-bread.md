## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups sour milk
- 2/3 cup molasses
- 1 cup wheat flour
- 4 cups cornmeal
- 2 teaspoons soda

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sweet milk with 2 cups sour milk.
2. Add 2/3 cup molasses, 1 cup wheat flour, 4 cups cornmeal, and 2 teaspoons soda.
3. Bake for 3 hours.
