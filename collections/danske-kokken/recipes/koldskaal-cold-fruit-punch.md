## Ingredients

- 1 Bottle red wine
- 1 Quart cold Water
- Juice and peel of 1 Lemon, sliced
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the red wine with 1 quart cold water, the juice and sliced peel of 1 lemon, and white sugar to taste.
2. When served, place a piece of ice in the tureen.
3. Serve with rusks.
4. This can also be used as a refreshing summer drink and can be made with ale.
