## Ingredients

- Onion slices
- Butter
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Brown a good portion of onion slices in butter in the pan.
2. These can either be stirred into the eggs or placed inside the omelet after it is baked.
3. Hold back some of the onion slices and sprinkle them over the omelet.
4. Serve with open-faced sandwiches.
