## Ingredients

- Half the back with kidney from veal without leg piece
- Bacon
- Butter
- Egg
- Grated breadcrumbs
- 1 carrot
- Onion
- Capers
- Parsley
- Lemon peel
- Cream
- 1/2 pint water
- Seasonings
- Anchovies (if available)

## Directions

1. Remove the kidney, backbone ribs, and ribs.
2. Make a forcemeat from veal, bacon, egg, anchovies, grated breadcrumbs, onion, parsley, lemon, cream, and seasonings.
3. Spread this forcemeat on the inside of the meat piece.
4. Roll it together, bind with twine, brown with butter, and add water.
5. Add the carrot.
6. Roast for 1 to 2 hours, basting frequently with the sauce.
7. When done, remove the binding.
8. Skim the fat from the sauce, add cream, and add 2 spoonfuls of capers.
9. Serve.
