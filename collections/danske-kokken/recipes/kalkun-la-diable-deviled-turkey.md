## Ingredients

- leftover turkey or turkey meat
- pan drippings from roasting
- broth cooked from the bones
- sauce à la diable (recipe 760)
- Maggis bouillon cubes
- coloring

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones.
2. Warm it in a sauce à la diable made from the pan drippings from the roast together with the broth cooked on the bones from which the meat was first removed.
3. The sauce can be improved with Maggis bouillon cubes and colored with food coloring.
