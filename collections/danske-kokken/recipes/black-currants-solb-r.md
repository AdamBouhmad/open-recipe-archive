## Ingredients

- Ripe berries
- 1 Pd. Melis per 1 Pd. berries
- 1 Pægl vinegar

## Directions

1. Strip the ripe berries from the stem and cut off the flower
2. Boil the Melis with vinegar to a thin syrup
3. Add the berries and boil for about 1 hour until they appear clear
4. Remove from heat and shake until they begin to swell, then pour off
5. Boil the syrup and give the berries a boil in it
