## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 6 tablespoons milk
- grated lemon zest
- 6 egg whites
- 1 French bread
- butter
- powdered sugar
- raspberry jam

## Directions

1. Beat the 6 egg yolks until white
2. Mix with 2 tablespoons flour, 6 tablespoons milk, and grated lemon zest
3. Beat the 6 egg whites until stiff and fold in
4. Slice the French bread into small pieces and bake in butter in a pan
5. When baked, pour the egg mixture over the bread slices
6. Bake the cake until light brown
7. Serve with powdered sugar and raspberry jam
