## Ingredients

- Smoked herring
- Milk
- Onion
- Cooked potatoes
- Cooked beets
- Veal roast
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Mustard
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Remove bones and skin from smoked herring and let it soak in milk for a couple of hours
2. Finely chop the herring, onion, cooked potatoes, cooked beets and veal roast, all cut into small cubes
3. Mix and stir with vinegar, pepper, mustard and a little powdered sugar if necessary
4. When serving, garnish the salad on top with small, neatly cut pieces of beets
