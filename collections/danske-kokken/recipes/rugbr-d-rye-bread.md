## Ingredients

- 4 pounds coarse rye flour (and if available, a couple of spoonfuls of gluten flour)
- 1 pound butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 5 quarts baker's yeast
- a handful of salt
- a handful of caraway
- 5½ pails cooked, cooled water
- ½ pail vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together thoroughly the evening before baking to form a dough
2. Before baking, press the dough by hand onto the pan to the thickness of crackers
3. Roll thin and smooth with a bottle
4. Cut into round or square small cakes before placing the pan in the oven
5. When the dough has dried slightly, loosen all cakes with a thin knife
6. Later turn them so they become dry on the side facing the pan as well
