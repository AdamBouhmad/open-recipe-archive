## Ingredients

- Globe artichokes
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the lower leaves as they cannot be eaten and clip the remaining ones half off
2. Place the artichokes inverted in a pot and add just enough boiling water to reach one-third of the way up
3. Cover with a lid and place over moderate strong heat so sufficient steam develops to cook the artichokes better and preserve their flavor
4. When done, arrange quickly with fresh butter or another suitable sauce
