## Ingredients

- ripe fruit, half of each
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the ripe fruit, half of each, and cut away the core house and blossom.
2. Place the fruit in the pot, whose bottom is covered with water, and reduce the jam to a firm mass.
3. Alternatively, dry the fruit before cutting it into 4 pieces, from which the core house is cut away.
4. Cook until well reduced, then pass through a coarse sieve and pour back into the pot to reduce to jam.
5. Pour out and seal as previously explained.
