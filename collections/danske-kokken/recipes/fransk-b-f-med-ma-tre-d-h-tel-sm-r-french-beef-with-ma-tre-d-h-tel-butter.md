## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg beef tenderloin, or veal or pork tenderloin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Brown butter
- Gravy or stock
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- 0.25 kg butter
- 3 teaspoons chopped parsley
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- Potatoes cooked in Palmin

## Directions

1. Beef, veal or pork tenderloin can be used; in this case pork tenderloin is used.
2. Cut the tenderloin across into suitable pieces and pound flat.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and fry in a pan with brown butter over a brisk fire.
4. Arrange the beef on a very hot dish and pour over it the butter and gravy deglazed from the pan.
5. A few Maggi bouillon cubes can be dissolved in it.
6. Stir the butter and add the lemon juice.
7. With a teaspoon dipped in warm water, form small balls; these are rolled in the chopped parsley, and one ball is placed on each piece of beef.
8. Garnish with potatoes cooked in Palmin.
