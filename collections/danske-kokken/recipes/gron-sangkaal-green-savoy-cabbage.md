## Ingredients

- Savoy cabbage inner leaves
- Water
- Soda
- Bouillon
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Chestnuts or browned potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the innermost and finest leaves of savoy cabbage nearly tender in a little water with a little soda; discard this water, and add a little bouillon, some butter—half of this can be fat from freshly made soup—nutmeg and a little salt, with which it is cooked tender. Cooked chestnuts or in their absence browned potatoes can be placed on top of the cabbage.
