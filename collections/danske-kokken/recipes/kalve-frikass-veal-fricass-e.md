## Ingredients

- Veal breast or brisket
- Salt
- Water
- Bouquet garni
- Peppercorns
- Onions
- Root vegetables for soup
- Flour
- Butter
- Asparagus (optional)

## Directions

1. Use breast or brisket, chop into smaller pieces, rinse well and set to cook with cold water and a little salt and a bouquet garni.
2. When it boils, skim the soup several times; add some peppercorns and a couple of onions.
3. When the meat is cooked, remove it and strain the soup and reduce it with root vegetables and thicken with a tablespoon of flour mixed with butter until the thickness is appropriate.
4. Place the meat in the soup and warm it.
5. If you have asparagus, you can add cooked pieces about two inches long to the fricassée along with a little of the water in which they were cooked.
