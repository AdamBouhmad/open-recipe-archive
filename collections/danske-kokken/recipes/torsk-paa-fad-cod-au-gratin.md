## Ingredients

- small cod
- salt
- butter
- flour
- fish stock from fish heads
- egg yolks
- shallots
- lemon juice
- white pepper
- crushed hardtack (Tvebak)
- puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Clean small cod, cut them in pieces, and cut off the heads. After rinsing the fish, sprinkle with salt.
2. Let them stand with salt for about an hour, then rinse the fish pieces and place them on a slightly deep dish that can withstand heat in the oven.
3. Make the sauce from butter and flour, which are baked and thinned with fish stock cooked from the fish heads.
4. Bind it with a few egg yolks and season with shallots, lemon juice, salt, and ground white pepper to taste.
5. Pour the sauce over the fish, which is sprinkled with crushed hardtack and drizzled with melted butter.
6. Place the dish in a warm oven for a good quarter hour.
7. Serve the fish dish on the platter, which is garnished with puff pastry strips.
