## Ingredients

- 1 pound melted butter
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- grated lemon peel or vanilla
- 8 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 pound melted butter, 1 pound powdered sugar, and 4 egg yolks with a little grated lemon peel or vanilla
2. Finally add the 8 beaten egg whites
3. Place on the sheet in small dollops using a teaspoon
