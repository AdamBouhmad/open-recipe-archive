## Ingredients

- apples, cooked as described previously
- lemon, orange, wine, or vanilla as flavoring

## Directions

1. After the apples have been cooked as described above, add one of the aforementioned flavorings to the clear juice, and then weigh it.
2. Cook the jelly as with the other types of jelly.
3. Cook the apple pulp with sugar to make Russian marmalade.
