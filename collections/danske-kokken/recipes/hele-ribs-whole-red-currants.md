## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl water
- 1/2 kg picked red currants

## Directions

1. Choose large ripe currants for this
2. Pick them carefully from the stems and only then weigh them
3. Cook sugar and water to a very thick syrup in which the currants get one boil
4. Remove them before they begin to burst and fill into glasses
5. Reduce the juice and pour it hot over them
6. Seal immediately
