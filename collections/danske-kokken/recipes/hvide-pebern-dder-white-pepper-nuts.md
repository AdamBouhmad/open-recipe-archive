## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 625 gr (1.25 lb) powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon cardamom
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 4 teaspoons chopped lemon zest
- 3 teaspoons hartshorn salt, dissolved in a little rose water
- 20 drops lemon oil
- 1 Kg (2 lb) flour

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs with the powdered sugar, cardamom, ginger, chopped lemon zest, hartshorn salt dissolved in rose water, and lemon oil
2. Add the flour
3. Roll the dough into thin sticks and cut very small
4. Place on a buttered baking sheet, not too close together
5. Bake until very crisp. This yields approximately 2.5 Kg (5 lb) of nuts
