## Ingredients

- White cabbage head
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Ground white pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the cabbage head and cut the head into 4 pieces, cutting away the core.
2. Cook the cabbage until tender in water with salt.
3. Place on a colander and press the water out of it.
4. Make a roux by baking butter and flour together, then dilute with milk to make a stewing sauce.
5. Season the sauce with salt and often with ground white pepper.
6. Cut the cooked cabbage into smaller pieces or, as many do, chop it coarsely.
7. Add the cabbage to the sauce.
