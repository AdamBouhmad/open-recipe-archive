## Ingredients

- Pike-perch
- Salt
- Cooked potatoes
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it and wash it well
2. Cut off the fins
3. Sprinkle with salt and let it stand for a couple of hours before cooking
4. Serve on a platter with a napkin, garnished with small cooked potatoes sprinkled with chopped parsley
