## Ingredients

- 1 Pound dark red Carrots
- 4 Lemons
- cold Water approximately 1 Quart
- 1 Pound white Sugar
- 1 Pint of water from cooking the carrots
- Juice of two Lemons
- 3 Ounces Sugar
- Lemon juice to taste
- Cognac paper

## Directions

1. Choose dark red carrots from August
2. Scrape clean, cut into slices as thick as a straw, then cut these into strips of equal thickness and weigh
3. Peel lemons very thinly so none of the white remains, then cut the peelings into thin black strips
4. Place the peelings and carrots in cold water, approximately 1 Quart, cook covered for about 1.5 hours or until carrots are soft but do not fall apart
5. Drain on a colander
6. Cook 1 pound white sugar to a thin syrup with 1 pint of the water in which the carrots were cooked
7. Add the carrots and juice of two lemons and cook about 10 minutes
8. Place in a crock and let stand two days
9. Drain on a colander and cook the syrup for 8-10 minutes with 3 ounces sugar and more lemon juice to taste
10. Place carrots in but do not cook further
11. Place in jars and cover with cognac paper and bind
