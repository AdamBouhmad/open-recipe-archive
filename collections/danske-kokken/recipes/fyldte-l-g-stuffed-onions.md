## Ingredients

- onions
- meat forcemeat
- brown butter
- flour
- pepper
- bouillon or gravy
- salt
- thread for binding

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and cut off a lid
2. Hollow out the onions
3. Fill with meat forcemeat
4. Replace the lids and bind the onions with thread
5. Cook either in meat broth or in salted water until tender
6. Remove the thread
7. Make a brown sauce from browned butter with a little flour and pepper, thinned with bouillon or gravy
8. Give the onions a boil in this sauce
9. Arrange with the sauce poured over
