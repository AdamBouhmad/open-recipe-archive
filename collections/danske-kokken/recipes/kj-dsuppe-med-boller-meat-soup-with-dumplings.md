## Ingredients

- 10 pounds soup meat
- 4 pounds simpler beef, such as shank
- 12 pots cold water
- salt
- 1 soup bunch
- cleaned whole soup vegetables
- 10 whole peppercorns
- a small piece of ginger
- a small piece of candy sugar
- dumplings

## Directions

1. For 10 pounds of soup meat, take 4 pounds of simpler beef, such as shank; after washing, chop it into pieces and place it over heat with 12 pots of cold water.
2. When this soup has been brought to boil over slow heat, skim it, and add a little salt, then boil the soup evenly for one hour.
3. Only then add the proper soup meat together with a soup bunch and some cleaned whole soup vegetables, 10 whole peppercorns and a small piece of ginger, then cook the soup evenly again until the vegetables are tender.
4. Remove these vegetables, cut them up and place them in the tureen.
5. When the meat is tender, remove it and keep it warm until serving.
6. Strain the soup and let it rest quietly for a quarter hour, then skim off the fat.
7. Now pour the soup carefully back into the pot so that no sediment comes with it.
8. Add a small piece of candy to the soup and, if necessary, salt it.
9. Add the dumplings, give it a good boil, and when the dumplings are tender, pour it into the tureen over the vegetables.
10. The portions mentioned here will be sufficient for 12 persons.
11. The soup meat is sprinkled with a little salt and can be served as a second course with horseradish sauce.
12. If soup is left over, the vegetables must be strained out, as it will easily turn sour during storage.
