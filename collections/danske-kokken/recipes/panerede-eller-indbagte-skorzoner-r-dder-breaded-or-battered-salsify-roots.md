## Ingredients

- Scraped, cut, and cooked salsify roots
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Pancake batter, optional
- Clarified butter or Palmin for frying

## Directions

1. Drain the cooked roots on a colander and when the water is well drained, pour them onto a coarse cloth to remove the remaining moisture
2. Dip in egg and breadcrumbs, or alternatively in pancake batter
3. Fry in clarified butter or Palmin until beautifully light brown
4. Use as garnish around a meat course, or serve with melted butter as a side dish
