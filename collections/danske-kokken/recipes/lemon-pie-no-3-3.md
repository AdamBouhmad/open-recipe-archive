## Ingredients

- Pie dough
- 6 eggs, fewer than 2 whites
- 2 cups white sugar
- A little salt
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons corn starch
- Zest of 2 large lemons
- Juice of 2 large lemons
- 2 egg whites for topping

## Directions

1. Treat the same as the previous pies.
2. Stir together 6 eggs (fewer than 2 whites) with 2 cups white sugar and a little salt.
3. In 1 cup sweet milk, stir 2 tablespoons corn starch, then add the grated zest and juice of 2 large lemons.
4. Use the 2 whites for the topping.
