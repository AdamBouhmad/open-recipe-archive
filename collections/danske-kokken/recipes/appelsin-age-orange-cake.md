## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 5 egg yolks
- 2 cups sugar
- half a cup butter
- half a cup cold water
- 2.5 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Whip 3 egg whites and 5 egg yolks separately
2. Stir the yolks with 2 cups sugar and half a cup butter, add half a cup cold water and 2.5 cups flour with 2 teaspoons baking powder, then fold in the whipped egg whites
