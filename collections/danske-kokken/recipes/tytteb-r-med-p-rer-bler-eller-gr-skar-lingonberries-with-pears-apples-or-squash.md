## Ingredients

- 200 gr (40 Kv.) Demerara sugar
- 1/2 kg (1 S) lingonberries
- Peeled and cut fruit (pears, apples or squash)
- Whole cinnamon pieces

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lingonberries, layer them with sugar in the pot and bring to a boil with constant stirring
2. Now add the peeled and cut fruit along with some whole cinnamon and cook together with the lingonberries for about 1/4 hour
3. Pour the preserve into crocks and seal as soon as it has cooled
4. Use pear varieties such as Graapærer, Bergamottes, or Figenpærer, which are commonly cooked with lingonberries, though any cooking pear variety can be used
5. Peel the fruits, cut them into 4 pieces, remove the cores, rinse them, then add to the pot to cook with the lingonberries
6. Pears can be bought when cheapest, peeled, cut and cooked with the lingonberries
