## Ingredients

- Good, fat autumn herring
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the herring by removing the gills
2. Sprinkle generously with salt and let stand for 3 days
3. Remove the herring and brush off the salt
4. Layer the herring in a small barrel or half-barrel with the belly side up, packed tightly together
5. Between each layer of herring, place a good layer of salt, and top with salt
6. Once layered, place a weight on top
