## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- 4 Ounces sugar
- 3 Ounces corn starch
- 1 Teaspoonful vanilla or lemon
- 1 Quart milk

## Directions

1. Mix together the eggs, sugar, corn starch, and vanilla or lemon
2. Add the milk gradually
3. Heat until nearly boiling
4. Pour into cups and place in a roasting pan filled halfway with water
5. Place the pan in a moderately warm oven and bake the small puddings for half an hour until they are firm and light brown
6. They may be eaten warm from the cups or turned out and eaten cold
