## Ingredients

- 2 kg (4 lbs) blood sausages
- Salt
- Water
- Melted butter
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Apple sauce or apple compote

## Directions

1. Place whole in boiling salted water, and boil them for a few minutes until they are thoroughly heated through.
2. Cut them into pieces, which are reassembled, and serve as hot as possible, garnished with parsley.
3. Serve with melted butter as well as cinnamon and sugar. Apple sauce or apple compote is very much eaten with blood sausages.
