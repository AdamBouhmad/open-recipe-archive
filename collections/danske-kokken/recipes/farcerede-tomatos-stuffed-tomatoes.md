## Ingredients

- Medium-sized tomatoes
- Bread
- Melted butter
- Salt
- Sugar
- Paper

## Directions

1. Wash medium-sized tomatoes, cut a slice from the top of each, and hollow out this and the tomato with a teaspoon
2. Mince the cut-out portions finely and mix with an equally large quantity of wheat bread (which has been soaked in water or meat broth), a little melted butter, salt and a little fine sugar
3. Fill the tomato with the mixture and bake, covered with a little paper, in a hot oven
