## Ingredients

- Small, closed mushrooms
- Butter
- Fish forcemeat formed into cubes or small balls
- Cooked, finely cut celery
- Mayonnaise
- Salad leaves

## Directions

1. Cook small closed mushrooms in butter; form cubes or small balls of fish forcemeat and cook them.
2. Mix equal amounts of mushrooms, forcemeat balls, and finely cut cooked celery together.
3. Turn in a mayonnaise.
4. Arrange the salad and garnish with salad leaves and a few mushrooms.
