## Ingredients

- 60 herring
- 1 good handful of salt
- 25 quints light powdered sugar
- 3 handfuls salt
- 3 quints Spanish hops
- 1 handful coarsely crushed pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaves, cut into pieces
- 1 pot vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the gills from the herring and rinse in 6 changes of water
2. Dry them in a cloth
3. Choose the male herring or milk herring, which are the fattiest and best
4. Sprinkle with a good handful of salt and let stand for 24 hours
5. Dry again with a cloth
6. Mix together the light powdered sugar, salt, Spanish hops, coarsely crushed pepper, cloves, and bay leaves cut into pieces
7. Layer the herring in this mixture
8. Pour the vinegar over everything
9. Place a board with a stone on top
10. After 6 weeks they are ready to use
