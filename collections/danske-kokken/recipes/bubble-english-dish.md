## Ingredients

- Cooked Savoy cabbage, finely chopped
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter, browned
- Cooked meat, sliced and fried brown in butter

## Directions

1. Place the finely chopped cooked Savoy cabbage, sprinkled with pepper and salt, on a pan with browned butter.
2. Turn and warm through the cabbage in the butter.
3. Place it in the center of a flat dish.
4. Arrange slices of cooked meat that have been fried brown in butter around it.
5. Either pour a strong brown gravy sauce over the dish or serve the sauce separately.
