## Ingredients

- Plaice, at least 625 grams per piece
- Short pastry (pâte brisée)
- Fish farce
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish stock made from fish bones
- Tarragon
- Mushrooms or capers
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Line a mold with short pastry and fish farce
2. Clean and fillet plaice of at least 625 grams each, yielding 4 fillets per fish
3. Roll each fillet into a spiral and arrange tightly in the mold
4. Make a sauce from butter, flour, and fish stock made from the bones
5. Season the sauce with tarragon and add mushrooms or capers
6. Pour some of this sauce over the fish in the mold
7. Thin the remaining sauce and bind with 3 to 4 egg yolks before serving
8. Bake for about one hour
