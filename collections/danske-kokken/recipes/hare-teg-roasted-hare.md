## Ingredients

- One young hare
- Half an onion
- Three cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Some peppercorns
- A lump of fresh butter or the same amount of roasting fat
- Salt
- Cream

## Directions

1. Skin and clean a young hare, removing all the innards and cutting off the head and bones.
2. Remove the inner skin from the meat, lard the roast and place it in the roasting pan with half an onion, three cloves, two bay leaves, some peppercorns and a lump of fresh butter or the same amount of roasting fat.
3. Sprinkle with a little salt and roast slowly, with repeated basting, approximately three quarters of an hour or somewhat more.
4. Add cream to the meat gravy, let it come to a boil, strain it, and serve it with the roast.
