## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups sugar
- half a cup butter
- half a cup milk
- 1 egg
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Stir 1.5 cups sugar with half a cup butter, half a cup milk, 1 egg, and the yolks of 4 eggs well
2. Sift 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder into it
3. Bake into five cakes
4. For the filling: cook together 1 cup powdered sugar and 2 tablespoons water, remove from heat and stir in a stiffly whipped egg white, then combine with desiccated coconut (soaked for half an hour in warm milk)
5. Spread a good layer between the cakes and over the top
