## Ingredients

- 100 g margarine
- 1 kg Pillsbury flour
- 1 teaspoon salt with 25 g yeast dissolved in it
- 2 teaspoons icing sugar
- a very small amount of cardamom
- just over 6.5 dl half-skimmed milk
- milk for brushing

## Directions

1. Crumble the margarine into the flour
2. Add salt with dissolved yeast, icing sugar, cardamom, and milk
3. Roll the dough into balls
4. Score a cross on each with a sharp knife
5. Let rise
6. Brush with milk and bake for 15 to 20 minutes
