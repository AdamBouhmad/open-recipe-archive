## Ingredients

- 125 Gr (1/4 S) scalded almonds or nut kernels
- 1 L (1 Pot) milk
- egg yolks and isinglass as in vanilla cream Nr. 1060
- macaroons
- whipped cream for decoration

## Directions

1. Pound the scalded almonds or nut kernels very finely and place in 1 L (1 Pot) milk.
2. When it boils, add egg yolks and isinglass exactly as in vanilla cream Nr. 1060.
3. Serve with macaroons and decorate with whipped cream.
