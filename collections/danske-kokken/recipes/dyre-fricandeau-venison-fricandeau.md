## Ingredients

- Venison leg
- Lard
- Butter
- Flour
- Game stock
- Sherry, Madeira or red wine
- Potatoes
- Currant jelly
- Green salad or herb salad

## Directions

1. Cut fricandeau from the leg, lard it, brown and roast as directed in Nr. 163.
2. From the pan drippings, make a brown, smooth sauce.
3. Add sherry, Madeira or red wine to the sauce.
4. Pour the sauce over the meat, which is garnished with browned potatoes.
5. Serve with currant jelly, green salad or herb salad.
