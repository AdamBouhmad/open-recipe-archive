## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg (3 pounds) stone sugar
- 3/4 dl (3/4 cup) water for 1/2 kg (1 pound) fruit juice
- 2 kg (4 pounds) ripe red tomatoes
- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) water to 2 kg (4 pounds) tomatoes

## Directions

1. Wipe the ripe red tomatoes and place them over heat with 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) water to 2 kg (4 pounds) tomatoes.
2. Cook until they can be completely mashed with ease.
3. Pour them into a jelly cloth.
4. The juice that has run through by the next day is weighed.
5. Cook sugar and water as described in recipe 93.
6. Pour the juice into the sugar after it has been cooked completely dry.
7. Cook for 10 minutes, then pour into jelly cups and bind as soon as it has partially set.
