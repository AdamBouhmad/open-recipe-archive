## Ingredients

- string beans
- water
- butter
- cream or milk
- flour
- parsley
- sugar
- pepper
- mace
- salt

## Directions

1. String the beans, in which the kernels should not be too large, and cut at slant angles into small fine strips
2. Cook in water until just tender
3. Pour into a sieve to drain the water
4. Make a white sauce from melted butter, cream or milk, flour, chopped parsley, a little sugar, pepper, mace and finally a little salt
5. Give the string beans a boil in this sauce and serve with it
6. To salt them: string the beans and place in a crock that has first been sprinkled with salt inside
7. Layer the beans with salt, either whole or usually cut in strips, and press down with a weight
8. When needed, rinse first in several changes of cold water or give a boil in boiling water which is then drained, then cook tender in fresh water and otherwise treat as fresh
