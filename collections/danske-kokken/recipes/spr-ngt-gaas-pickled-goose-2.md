## Ingredients

- Goose
- Salt
- Saltpeter
- Powdered sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. When the goose is cleaned, rub it inside and outside with a quarter pound of salt mixed with 1 tablespoon of saltpeter and 1 tablespoon of powdered sugar, then place it in a cool place for several days.
2. When ready to use, rinse it and boil in plentiful water for approximately 1.5 hours.
3. Serve sprinkled with salt and with beet roots or Brussels sprouts. The broth is used for mixing with other soups, such as giblet soup, split pea soup or similar.
