## Ingredients

- Beef roast, approximately ten pounds
- Hot water
- Salt

## Directions

1. A beef roast of approximately ten pounds requires three hours of roasting, sometimes more, sometimes a little less.
2. There may be some red inside, but it should not be bloody.
