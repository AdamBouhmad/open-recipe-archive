## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) white spare broth
- 125 grams (0.25 S) butter
- 125 grams (0.25 S) flour
- 1 whisk
- tomato purée
- paprika
- wine
- cauliflower, fish balls, flour balls, or loose cooked rice (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt 125 grams (0.25 S) butter, and bake the same amount of flour in it, which is thinned with the broth in which a whisk has been cooked.
2. Add tomato purée (see the preserve book) to the soup to taste as well as paprika and a little wine.
3. It can be served with cauliflower, fish balls, flour balls, or loose cooked rice.
