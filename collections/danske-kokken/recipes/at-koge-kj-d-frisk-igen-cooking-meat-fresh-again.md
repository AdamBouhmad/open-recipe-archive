## Ingredients

- meat (raw, cooked, fresh, or salted)
- charcoal

## Directions

1. In warm weather, if meat and flesh develop what is called a 'taint' in kitchen language, place it on the fire in a little water along with some charcoal.
2. Boil for a quarter hour with the charcoal, which will restore the meat to freshness.
3. To prevent the charcoal from leaving marks on the meat, wrap it in a very thin piece of cloth.
