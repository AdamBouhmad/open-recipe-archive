## Ingredients

- 3 liters semi-skimmed milk
- 125 grams cocoa
- 3 level teaspoons cornstarch
- vanilla essence
- sugar
- rusks

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil
2. Mix the cocoa in some of the hot milk
3. Mix 3 level teaspoons cornstarch in a little cold milk and blend into the cocoa
4. Stir the cocoa mixture into the milk
5. Bring to a boil
6. Add a little vanilla essence
7. Sweeten to taste
8. Serve with rusks
