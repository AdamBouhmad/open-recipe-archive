## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- Half pound powdered icing sugar
- Zest of 3 lemons
- Juice of 3 lemons
- Egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Whisk 1 egg yolk for half an hour with half pound powdered icing sugar and the grated zest of 3 lemons.
2. Add the juice of 3 lemons and the whipped egg whites.
3. Bake the cake light brown in a pan at even heat and serve with whipped cream.
