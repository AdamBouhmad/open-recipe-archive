## Ingredients

- 1 pint tomatoes
- 2 quarts good, clear broth, free from fat
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon pepper
- 4 ounces rice

## Directions

1. Boil the tomatoes until soft and press them through a sieve with a spoon.
2. Stir them into 2 quarts of good, clear broth, free from fat; season with one teaspoon of salt and 0.5 teaspoon of pepper.
3. Add 4 ounces of rice, well washed in cold water, and now cook the soup slowly for half an hour before it is served.
