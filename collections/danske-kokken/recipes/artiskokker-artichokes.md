## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Salt
- Water
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the outermost tips and trim the stems.
2. Rinse the artichokes and place them upside down in a pot.
3. Pour boiling salted water over them.
4. Most of an hour's cooking time is needed.
5. Serve them upside down on a platter with a napkin as warm as possible.
6. Melted butter is served with them.
