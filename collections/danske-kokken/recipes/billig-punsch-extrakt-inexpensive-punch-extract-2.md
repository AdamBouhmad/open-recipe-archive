## Ingredients

- 1 pot water
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 quintals citric acid
- 1 drop citron oil
- 1 drop Peruvian balsam
- 1 pot white rum

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pot water with 1 pound sugar and 2 quintals citric acid
2. When the sugar is dissolved, skim it and remove from heat
3. Add 1 drop citron oil and 1 drop Peruvian balsam, and 1 pot white rum
4. Filter the extract and preserve in bottles
5. When it shall be used, add at least as much boiling water; drink it warm
