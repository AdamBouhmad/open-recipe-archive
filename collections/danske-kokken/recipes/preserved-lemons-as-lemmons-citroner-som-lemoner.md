## Ingredients

- lemons
- salt brine
- 0.5 dozen whole cloves per liter of vinegar
- 1 tablespoon whole white pepper per liter of vinegar
- 1 tablespoon ground ginger per liter of vinegar
- 2 tablespoons whole yellow mustard per liter of vinegar
- 2 pods Spanish pepper per liter of vinegar
- a little sliced horseradish per liter of vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic per liter of vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the lemons in a strong salt brine for 6 days
2. Place them in a pot with boiling water and boil for 1 quarter hour
3. Remove them and place on a cloth until completely dry, then place them in the jar
4. Measure the water used for the brine to determine exactly how much vinegar is needed to completely cover the lemons
5. For each liter of vinegar, take 0.5 dozen whole cloves, 1 tablespoon whole white pepper, 1 tablespoon ground ginger, 2 tablespoons whole yellow mustard, 2 pods Spanish pepper, a little sliced horseradish and 2 cloves of garlic
6. Boil the vinegar with the spices for 5 minutes, then pour it boiling over the lemons
7. Bind the jar when they are cold
8. Preferably store for 1 season before using in sauce or serving as pickles
