## Ingredients

- Hornfisk (Garfish)
- Salt
- Kartofler (Potatoes)
- Sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the head and tail from the garfish.
2. Clean and cook as Mackerel (No. 551).
3. Serve with boiled potatoes and sauce as described for mackerel.
