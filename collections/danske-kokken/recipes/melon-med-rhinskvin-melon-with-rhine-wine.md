## Ingredients

- Unripe netted melon, peeled
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Rhine wine
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Peel an unripe netted melon, remove the seeds, and cut the melon into smaller pieces, either with an ordinary knife or with a fluted herb knife.
2. Layer the melon pieces in a jar together with finely cut lemon peel (peel of 1 lemon to 1.5 kg melon).
3. Cook the sugar with the Rhine wine to a thin syrup, and pour it hot over the fruit.
4. The next day, pour off the liquid and reduce it to a very even syrup, which is again poured hot over the fruit.
5. Should the fruit after this treatment be too hard, it can simply be brought to a boil in the syrup.
6. Seal the jar as soon as the preserve is finished.
