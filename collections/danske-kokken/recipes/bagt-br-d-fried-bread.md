## Ingredients

- Butter
- Sugar
- French bread, cut into small cubes

## Directions

1. Place butter and sugar on a frying pan
2. Brown small cubes of French bread in the mixture
3. Serve with sweet soups, or for beef soup, fry the bread in butter only
