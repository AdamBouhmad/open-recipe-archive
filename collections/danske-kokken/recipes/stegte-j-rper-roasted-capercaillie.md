## Ingredients

- 6 capercaillie
- Butter
- Claret
- Flour
- Game stock or soup
- Cream
- Coloring
- Potatoes for browning
- Apples, halved and cooked
- Currant or gooseberry jelly
- Apple sauce

## Directions

1. Skin the capercaillie, remove innards, and rinse well
2. If frozen, soak them in milk for a few hours before larding
3. Lard them and brown in butter or claret in a pot
4. During roasting, which lasts approximately 1 hour, baste frequently with game stock, soup, or cream
5. Strain the gravy and use it to make a thin sauce with the browned butter and flour, stirring in a little cold butter and coloring to achieve a nice golden-brown color
6. Alternatively, roast them as pheasants in a pre-prepared sauce, stirring in only a little cold butter at service
7. Serve the breast, which is split lengthwise, as the back is rarely served at dinner
8. Garnish with browned potatoes and with a wreath of cooked apple halves filled with currant or gooseberry jelly
9. Apple sauce may be served with the capercaillie
