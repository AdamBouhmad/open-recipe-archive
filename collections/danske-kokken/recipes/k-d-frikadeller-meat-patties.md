## Ingredients

- Ground meat, firmer than for meatballs
- Flour or breadcrumbs
- Clarified butter

## Directions

1. Form the ground meat mixture with a spoon and shape in flour or breadcrumbs, or place directly on the pan and smooth with the spoon
2. Fry the patties in clarified butter over low heat so they do not brown too much before cooking through
3. Serve with browned butter
