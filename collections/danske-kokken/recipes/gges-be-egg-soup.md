## Ingredients

- egg yolks from 10 eggs
- powdered sugar
- 3 pots light beer or double beer
- rye bread or wheat bread cubes, toasted

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks from 10 eggs with powdered sugar until they resemble a white salve
2. Heat 3 pots of light beer or double beer over the fire
3. As soon as it boils, add a little of it to the whisked eggs
4. While whisking continuously, pour the egg mixture into the beer until it begins to boil, then immediately remove from heat and pour into a serving bowl
5. Serve with toasted rye bread or wheat bread cubes
