## Ingredients

- 4 onions
- Butter
- 4 L (4 pots) meat soup cooked with herbs and vegetables
- 60 g (12 drams) tapioca grains
- Finely chopped chervil
- Carved cooked carrots
- Poached eggs

## Directions

1. Slice 4 onions and sauté lightly in butter until they do not darken
2. Pour 4 L (4 pots) of meat soup cooked with herbs and vegetables over the onions
3. As soon as the soup comes to a boil, sprinkle 60 g (12 drams) tapioca grains while stirring continuously
4. Cook the soup until the grains become clear, which takes about a quarter hour
5. Stir in a small amount of finely chopped chervil and bring to a boil once
6. Arrange with carved cooked carrots
7. Serve with poached eggs
