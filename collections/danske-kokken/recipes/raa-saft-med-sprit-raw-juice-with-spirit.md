## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) powdered sugar
- 3/4 deciliter (3/4 pint) wine spirit
- 1/2 kg (1 pound) picked berries

## Directions

1. Pick and weigh the berries and pour into a large crock
2. Layer the berries with the sugar and pour the spirit over at the end
3. Cover well and let stand for 4 to 5 days, stirring gently once daily so the sugar dissolves properly
4. Pour through a sieve or fairly open jelly cloth until the juice has only partly run off so the berries do not become too dry
5. Pour the juice into dry bottles, cork and seal with plaster
6. Fill the berries into crocks or jars and tie with parchment paper
