## Ingredients

- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) medium-fine barley grains
- 6 L (6 Potter) water
- butter and sugar for serving

## Directions

1. When the water comes to a boil, sprinkle the grains into it under quick stirring to prevent lumps from forming.
2. Water porridge tolerates about 3 hours of cooking.
3. Serve with butter and sugar.
4. Serve with sweet milk; in summer thick milk is used a lot and is served separately in small dessert plates.
5. A few peeled and scalded berries can be cooked in the porridge a few minutes before serving.
