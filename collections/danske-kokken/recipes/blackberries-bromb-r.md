## Ingredients

- berries
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar per pound of berries
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Clean the berries.
2. For each pound of berries, use 1/2 pound powdered sugar, which is sprinkled in layers between the berries.
3. After standing for several hours, place on the fire to cook together with some whole cinnamon sticks.
4. Cook the berries for approximately half an hour until they appear clear.
5. Skim frequently during cooking.
6. Remove the berries carefully and place in the preserving jar.
7. Continue cooking the syrup until it is like a thick syrup, then pour over the berries and cover the next day.
