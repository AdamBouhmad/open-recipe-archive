## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) vegetable stock
- 4 carrots
- 4 turnips
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) shelled peas
- 125 g (1/4 lb) butter
- 125 g (1/4 lb) flour
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 L (1 pint) raw cream

## Directions

1. Cut 4 carrots and 4 turnips into small cubes and cook until tender in some of the stock together with 1/2 L (1/2 pot) shelled peas.
2. In a pot, bake 125 g (1/4 lb) butter and 125 g (1/4 lb) flour together and thin with 5 L (5 pots) stock in which the vegetable stock has been mixed.
3. When it has boiled, remove from heat.
4. Whisk together 6 well-beaten egg yolks with 1/4 L (1 pint) raw cream and whisk into the soup.
5. Return the pot to the heat and bring the soup to a boil with strong stirring, then pour immediately into the tureen over the vegetables.
