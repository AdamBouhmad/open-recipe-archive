## Ingredients

- Scraped, cooked parsnips
- Milk sauce, soup, or water from cooking the parsnips
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the scraped, cooked parsnips and stew them either in a milk sauce, in soup, or in the water in which they were cooked
2. Thicken this sauce with a couple of egg yolks
3. In the milk sauce, stir in a piece of butter rolled in finely chopped parsley
