## Ingredients

- Veal
- Water
- Bones from mutton or preferably beef

## Directions

1. Veal becomes tender much faster than beef, so less water is needed for it. This broth and lamb broth have a weak flavor in themselves, so it is good if you have bones from mutton or preferably beef to cook with them.
