## Ingredients

- 575 g dried rose hips
- 4½ L water
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak the rinsed and dried fruit the evening before it is to be cooked.
2. Use the water in which it has stood for cooking.
3. Cook the soup, strain it, thicken with sago flour, sweeten and serve with baked bread.
4. Cook lemon peel and cinnamon together with the berries.
5. If using fresh rose hips, slit the fruit lengthwise and remove the small nuts and hairs through the slit.
6. Cook as other fruit soup and serve with raisins added.
