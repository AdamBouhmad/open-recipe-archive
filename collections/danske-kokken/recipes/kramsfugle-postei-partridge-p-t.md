## Ingredients

- Pâté dough or Morde dough
- 6 Partridges
- Meat forcemeat
- Bacon
- Meat stock
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean the birds thoroughly and remove the heads and wings
2. Roast the birds
3. Line a mold with dough
4. Layer alternately the birds and meat forcemeat in the mold
5. First lay a layer of bacon slices, which are removed before serving
6. Bake for 2 hours over heat
7. Serve warm with sauce made from strong meat stock
