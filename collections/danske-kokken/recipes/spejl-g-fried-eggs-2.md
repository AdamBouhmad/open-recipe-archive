## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. To make the eggs lie more beautifully and become more evenly fried, it is best to first break them out onto a platter so that the yolks are whole, rather than placing them on the pan one at a time; for as often happens, the first ones are done before the last ones are even placed on. Place the frying pan over the heat with a little butter; when it boils pour the eggs carefully onto it, and as soon as the white is set, they are done.
