## Ingredients

- 5 calf tongues
- Water
- Salt
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or claret
- Mayonnaise sauce
- Chopped anchovies
- Stirred mustard
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the calf tongues and cook them tender in water and salt.
2. Remove the skin.
3. Split the tongues lengthwise into 2 to 3 pieces.
4. Dip in egg and bread, then fry in butter or cook in claret.
5. Serve the tongues garnished with parsley.
6. Serve Mayonnaise sauce on the side, with chopped anchovies and stirred mustard added to it.
