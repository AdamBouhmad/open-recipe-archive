## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) pounded fine sugar to 1/2 kg (1 pound) fruit without pits
- various types of stone fruits such as apricots, plums, peaches, and cherries

## Directions

1. Different varieties of stone fruit such as apricots, plums, peaches, and cherries can be cooked together.
2. Remove the pits from the last-mentioned fruits, whereas the other varieties are peeled or scalded in boiling water until the skin can be easily removed.
