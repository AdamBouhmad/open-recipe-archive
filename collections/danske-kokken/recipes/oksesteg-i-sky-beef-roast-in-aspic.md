## Ingredients

- Slices of beef roast
- Good aspic (from roasting or made fresh)
- Roasting bone
- Onions
- Black pepper, whole
- Lemon juice or tarragon vinegar
- Isinglass (12 leaves per 1 liter)
- Horseradish
- Fresh tomato slices or pickled cucumbers
- Salad leaves

## Directions

1. Arrange slices of beef roast on a flat dish.
2. Prepare aspic from the leftover aspic from roasting or cook it on the roasting bone with a couple of onions, whole black pepper, lemon juice or tarragon vinegar, and isinglass.
3. Once it has boiled, strain the aspic and clarify it with egg whites if necessary.
4. Pour the aspic over the beef.
5. When the aspic has set, scrape horseradish into thin shavings with a knife and streak the dish with the scraped horseradish.
6. Arrange slices of fresh tomatoes or pickled cucumbers and salad leaves around the edge.
