## Ingredients

- 20 egg yolks
- 55 quintal powdered sugar
- 25 quintal best flour
- 12 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks together with 55 quintal powdered sugar for an hour
2. Then add 25 quintal best flour, first dried and sifted
3. Beat 12 egg whites to peaks and fold in with the rest
4. Pour the whole into a form lined with butter paper and bake 1 hour in a moderately warm oven
