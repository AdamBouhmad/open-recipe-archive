## Ingredients

- 2 raw egg yolks
- 1.5 tablespoons stirred smooth mustard
- Best salad oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- A little Cayenne
- Half a teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Whip together 2 raw egg yolks (no white may be included) and stirred smooth mustard
2. Add the best salad oil drop by drop while stirring constantly
3. Continue until the bowl or cup can be turned over without spilling
4. Add vinegar, a little Cayenne, and half a teaspoon salt
5. Stir strongly until it becomes lightly colored
6. This is used for lobster, green salad, or fish
