## Ingredients

- 1 Quart cream
- 5 Ounces grated chocolate
- Powdered sugar to taste
- 6 Ounces isinglass or nearly 2 Ounces gelatine

## Directions

1. Put the cream on the heat with the grated chocolate, powdered sugar, and isinglass or gelatine
2. Let it boil for about 15 minutes
3. Pour it out and stir until it is cold, but still fluid enough to be poured into the oiled mold so that a layer forms all over it, about as thick as a finger
4. Continue to turn the mold until the mixture is so stiff that it no longer moves
5. Smooth out the last layer evenly with a knife so that a hollow space remains in the center
6. Let the mold stand in this way until the next day
7. Then whip 1 Pint cream to stiff peaks with 2 Ounces white sugar, vanilla, and finally 1 Ounce dissolved gelatine
8. Fill the pudding with this mixture
9. After a couple of hours it can be turned onto a plate, but it must first be loosened on the sides with a knife; if it still does not slip out, place a hot cloth around the mold for a moment
10. A mold that holds 2.5 Quarts is adequate for this portion, which serves 16 persons
