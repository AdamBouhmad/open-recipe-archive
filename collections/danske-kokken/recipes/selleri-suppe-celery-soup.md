## Ingredients

- 4 celeries
- 4 L (4 pots) broth
- 100 grams (20 skein) butter
- 100 grams (20 skein) flour
- 3 egg yolks
- ground white pepper
- Sherry
- salt
- water for cooking
- peas, asparagus or meat balls (optional)
- toasted bread
- wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the celeries, rinse them, cut them into 4 pieces and cook them tender in water.
2. Take one of them aside and cut it into smaller cubes, which are placed in the tureen.
3. Rub the others through a sieve.
4. Bake 100 grams (20 skein) butter and 100 grams (20 skein) flour and thin with the broth and 1 L (1 pot) of the celery water, after which the celery purée is added and brought to a boil.
5. Stir 3 egg yolks with a couple of spoonfuls of water, and bind the soup with this without bringing it to a boil.
6. Season it with ground white pepper, a little Sherry and salt.
7. Peas, asparagus or meat balls can be added to the soup. Serve with toasted bread. Wine can be added to the soup.
