## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef, finely cut
- 1 onion
- 3 carrots
- 3 quarts water
- a large bunch of herbs
- root vegetables
- 1/2 pound meat for meat balls

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound beef finely and cook it slowly together with 1 onion and 3 carrots in 3 quarts water until the meat can easily be pressed apart, which requires nearly 3 hours
2. To prevent the soup from boiling away, it is necessary that it cooks evenly in a pot with a tight-fitting lid
3. Add a large bunch of herbs and root vegetables and take them out when they are done
4. Strain the soup, place it back on the fire, skim it, and when it boils, add meat balls made from 1/2 pound meat
5. The soup can be smoothed with egg and eaten with toasted bread; this portion is for 3 persons
