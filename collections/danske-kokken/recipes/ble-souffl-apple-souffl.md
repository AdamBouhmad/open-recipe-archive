## Ingredients

- Apples
- Water
- Lemon zest
- Sugar
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the apples in a little water with lemon zest and press through a colander.
2. Blend with sugar to taste.
3. Finally fold in egg whites that have been whipped to stiff peaks with sugar.
4. Treat otherwise as with other soufflés.
