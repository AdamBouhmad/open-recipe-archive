## Ingredients

- 12 large apples
- Water
- 0.5 pound butter
- 0.5 pound white sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel and slice apples, remove cores
2. Cook to a pulp with a little water
3. When cooked, stir in butter and white sugar
4. When cooled, stir in egg yolks
5. Finally fold in beaten egg whites
6. Sprinkle form or pan with bread crumbs
7. Bake to a light brown
8. Serves about 8 persons
9. Serve warm with powdered sugar
