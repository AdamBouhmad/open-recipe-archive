## Ingredients

- 35 drams rice flour
- 3 measures cream or sweet milk
- 35 drams butter
- 35 drams powdered sugar
- 25 drams ground sweet almonds
- 12 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound washed currants
- 1/4 pound finely cut candied citrus peel
- 12 egg whites
- liqueur

## Directions

1. Mix 35 drams rice flour with 3 measures cream or sweet milk
2. Bake well together with 35 drams butter, 35 drams powdered sugar and 25 drams ground sweet almonds, heating the butter gently but not hot
3. Gradually stir into the mixture 12 egg yolks, first beaten white
4. Stir in 1/4 pound washed currants and 1/4 pound finely cut candied citrus peel
5. Finally fold in the 12 egg whites, first beaten to stiff peaks
6. As an alternative to the dried fruits, half a measure of liqueur can be added to the mixture
7. Bake the omelet and serve with sweet fruit sauce, liqueur can be added to taste according to preference
8. This amount makes 10-12 portions
