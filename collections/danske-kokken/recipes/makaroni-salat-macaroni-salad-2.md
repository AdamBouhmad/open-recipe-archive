## Ingredients

- 0.25 pound macaroni
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Cream
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Cook 0.25 pound macaroni tender in water with salt
2. Mix a lump of butter and 0.5 pint cream with vinegar, sugar and grated horseradish to taste
3. Add the macaroni (drained on a colander) to this sauce
4. Serve this salad cold
5. To serve warm, break 0.5 pound macaroni into fairly large pieces and cook slowly for about 3 hours in water with a little salt, butter and onion
6. Heat 1 pint sweet cream over heat with 30 quint grated Parmesan cheese, a little nutmeg and a little white pepper
7. Add the macaroni (drained on a colander) and steam for 20 minutes, then serve immediately
