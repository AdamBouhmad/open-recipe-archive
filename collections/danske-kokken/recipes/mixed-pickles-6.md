## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Cauliflower
- Sour cucumbers
- Carrots
- Onions
- Green melon
- 20 peppercorns
- Spanish pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Give all the vegetables one boiling, and let them drain carefully
2. Layer them together with the pepper in square glass bottles with wide necks
3. Pour the vinegar over and cork the bottles tightly
