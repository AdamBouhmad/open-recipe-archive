## Ingredients

- 1 quart cranberries
- Cold water
- Sugar
- 2 teaspoons corn starch
- Pie dough
- 1 peeled and cut apple

## Directions

1. Pick the cranberries clean, wash in cold water, and set over heat with cold water that covers them.
2. Let them boil gently until they are soft.
3. Press them through a sieve with a spoon.
4. Set them over heat again with sufficient sugar and 2 teaspoons corn starch dissolved in a little of the juice that has been held back and must be cold.
5. When it comes to a boil it is done.
6. Line the form with dough prepared meanwhile.
7. Pour the berries over and lay strips of dough on top in various figures.
8. Bake in a moderately heated oven.
9. To take away the bitter taste of the cranberries, one may boil a peeled and cut apple among them.
