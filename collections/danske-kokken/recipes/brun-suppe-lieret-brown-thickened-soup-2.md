## Ingredients

- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Butter
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Flour
- 4 L (4 Pots) brown spare soup
- Asparagus water
- 4 Egg yolks
- Cold water
- Sherry
- 1 Kg (2 S) cooked asparagus

## Directions

1. Brown 125 Gr (1/4 S) butter, and bake 125 Gr (1/4 S) flour in it
2. Dilute with 4 L (4 Pots) brown spare soup together with the asparagus water
3. Whisk 4 egg yolks with a couple of spoonfuls of cold water and stir into the soup, which must not then come to a boil
4. Season with a little Sherry, and add the asparagus
