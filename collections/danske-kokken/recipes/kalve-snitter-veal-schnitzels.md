## Ingredients

- inner loin of veal
- butter
- good gravy or stock
- spinach, sorrel, peas, asparagus, or other vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut from the inner loin into small pieces and give a blow with the meat mallet, trim, and lard.
2. Brown the schnitzels in butter in a pot and pour over them a little good gravy or stock, but no more than is needed to braise them tender.
3. Baste them frequently with the gravy, which gradually must fry away until it becomes a good, smooth, and strong sauce, which is strained and served with the meat.
4. Arrange the schnitzels in a line lengthwise on the dish, which is decorated at each end with a bouquet of salad or parsley.
5. Spinach, sorrel, peas, asparagus, or other vegetables can be served with this.
