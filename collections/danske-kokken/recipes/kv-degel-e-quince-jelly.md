## Ingredients

- Pear quinces
- Kernels from the fruit
- Water
- Powdered sugar (equal weight to the strained juice)
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the pear quinces, then cut them into pieces
2. Cook the pieces together with the kernels in a little water until they are completely soft and can be mashed into a pulp
3. Pour them into a sieve so the juice can run off
4. Filter the juice through a jelly bag and weigh it
5. Cook the juice with an equal weight of powdered sugar until the jelly appears clear and thick
6. Skim it and pour it into jelly cups that have been previously moistened with Cognac
7. Treat it as described in recipe 474
