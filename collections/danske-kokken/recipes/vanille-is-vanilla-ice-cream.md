## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 4.5 pounds powdered sugar
- 13 pots good sweet cream
- 1 vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks until white with the powdered sugar
2. Boil the sweet cream and scrape the inside of a vanilla bean into it while the outside is boiled with it but then removed
3. When the cream is boiled, remove from heat and pour slowly into the egg yolks while whisking constantly
4. Put the whole mixture back in the pot and bring to a boil while whisking continuously
5. When the cream is done, let it cool and pour into an ice bucket to freeze
