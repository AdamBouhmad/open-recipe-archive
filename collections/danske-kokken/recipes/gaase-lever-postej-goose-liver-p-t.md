## Ingredients

- Fresh goose livers
- Veal liver, up to half the amount of goose liver if needed
- Salt
- Water
- Pork fat from the loin, equal amount to liver
- White pepper
- 1/2 cup Madeira
- Truffles, finely chopped
- Salt to taste
- Melted fat, half goose fat and half pork fat

## Directions

1. Wash the fresh goose livers in cold water
2. If there is not enough goose liver, up to half veal liver may be used
3. Cook the liver in salt and water until tender
4. Remove and pound finely in a mortar with an equal amount of pork fat from the loin
5. Press carefully through a sieve so that no membrane passes through
6. Add a little white pepper
7. Stir in 1/2 cup Madeira
8. Add finely chopped truffles
9. Mix everything according to taste with a little salt
10. Fill the mixture into small jars or terrines
11. Cover with melted fat, half goose fat and half pork fat
12. Place the lid on while still warm so it seals airtight
13. Store in a cool, airy place where it will keep for a long time
