## Ingredients

- Wild boar back
- 2 bottles red wine
- 2 onions
- 1 celery
- 2 carrots
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 lemon
- Whole peppercorns
- Salt
- Juniper berries
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut the back as you would venison back and remove the skin.
2. Place it in a marinade for 2 to 3 days made of 2 bottles red wine, 2 onions, 1 celery, 2 carrots, 2 bay leaves, 1 lemon (all cut up), some whole peppercorns, a little salt and juniper berries.
3. Remove the roast from the marinade, cover with butter, baste with the marinade, and roast at least 3 hours in a well-heated oven.
4. Strain the stock and thicken browned butter and flour with it to make the sauce.
