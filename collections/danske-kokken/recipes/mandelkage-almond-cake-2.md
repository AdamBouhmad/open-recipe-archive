## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 12 pieces bitter almonds
- 62 quintals powdered, sifted sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 10 tablespoons melted butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 10 tablespoons sweet cream
- 12 quintals finely grated bread
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Blanch 1 pound sweet and 12 pieces bitter almonds, dry and pound finely
2. Work into this 62 quintals powdered, sifted sugar and the grated yellow zest of 1 lemon
3. Stir 10 tablespoons melted butter into this until cold, then add 5 egg yolks and 10 tablespoons sweet cream
4. Stir both mixtures together for approximately an hour
5. Finally add 12 quintals finely grated bread and the 5 egg whites whipped to stiff peaks
6. Pour the whole mixture into a buttered form and bake for 1.5 hours at moderate heat
