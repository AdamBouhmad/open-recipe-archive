## Ingredients

- 2 kg meat from inner loin (for 8 to 10 persons)
- Butter or fat for larding
- Water or broth
- Flour
- Cream
- Brown sauce
- Salt and pepper
- Browned potatoes
- Currant jelly or apple compote for serving

## Directions

1. Cut the meat piece from the inner loin.
2. Remove the skin and blanch the fricandeau.
3. Lard the fricandeau and roast it like veal roast, but it is best roasted in a pot.
4. Make the sauce as a brown, smooth gravy sauce, to which a little cream is added just before serving.
5. Browned potatoes, currant jelly or apple compote are served with the fricandeau.
