## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) Pillsbury flour
- 190 grams (38 quintals) cold butter or margarine
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) grated cheese
- 2 eggs
- 2 beaten egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 50 grams (10 quintals) grated cheese
- 1/4 liter (1 pint) sweet milk

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients together into a dough and let stand 1/2 hour
2. Roll out and line small lentil molds with it
3. In each mold place 1 teaspoon of cream made from 2 beaten egg yolks, 1 tablespoon flour and 50 grams (10 quintals) grated cheese stirred together
4. Bring 1/4 liter (1 pint) sweet milk to a boil, pour into the eggs, whisk over heat until smooth, then cool before placing in the lentils
5. Cover the cream with dough and bake the lentils 1/2 hour
6. Arrange hot for dinner
7. Serve them on cut parchment paper on a platter
