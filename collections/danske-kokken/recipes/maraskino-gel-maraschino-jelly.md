## Ingredients

- wine, isinglass, sugar (same quantities as in White Wine Jelly, Nr. 1102)
- 1/4 L (1 pint) maraschino liqueur

## Directions

1. Prepare using the same method as Wine Jelly, Nr. 1102, adding 1/4 L (1 pint) maraschino liqueur.
2. Serve as Wine Jelly.
