## Ingredients

- Mashed potatoes (for 12 people)
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) butter
- 3 egg yolks
- Salt
- Ground white pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Pickled cucumbers, optional

## Directions

1. Stir 125 grams of butter, 3 egg yolks, salt and ground white pepper into the mash.
2. Butter a baking dish and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
3. Fill the dish with the mash.
4. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top again.
5. Place the dish in a warm oven until the mash has a beautiful light brown color.
6. Arrange in the dish with a napkin around it.
7. Chopped pickled cucumbers may be arranged in a star shape on top.
8. Serve with meat dishes.
