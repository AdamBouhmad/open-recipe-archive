## Ingredients

- 175 grams potato starch
- 100 grams melted margarine
- 0.75 liter boiling milk
- 10 egg yolks
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. 175 grams of potato starch is baked in 100 grams of melted margarine and diluted with 0.75 liter of boiling milk.
2. While the batter is warm, it is stirred with 10 egg yolks, 125 grams of powdered sugar, and finally with the beaten egg whites.
3. It is cooked for 2.5 hours and served with red wine or fruit sauce.
4. The batter can have essences added as indicated for other puddings.
