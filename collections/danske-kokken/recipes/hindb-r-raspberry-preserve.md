## Ingredients

- 375 g powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl water
- 1/2 kg raspberries

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together.
2. Add the berries and bring to a boil.
3. Drain the berries through a sieve.
4. Boil the drained juice until it is smooth.
5. Pour the berries back into the juice and bring to a boil again.
6. Pour into glasses and tie them up as soon as the preserve has cooled.
