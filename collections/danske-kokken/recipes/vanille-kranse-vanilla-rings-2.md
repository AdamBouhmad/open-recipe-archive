## Ingredients

- ½ pound fine flour
- 3 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
- ¼ pound washed butter
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 raw egg yolks
- vanilla sugar for 25 øre
- coarse white powdered sugar
- chopped almonds
- flour for rolling

## Directions

1. Knead ½ pound fine flour, 3 pounds finely ground powdered sugar, ¼ pound washed butter, 2 hard-boiled and 2 raw egg yolks, and vanilla sugar for 25 øre together
2. Roll with flour into rings
3. Dip in coarse white powdered sugar and chopped almonds
4. Bake at even heat on a floured sheet
5. Best served freshly baked
