## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups sugar
- half a cup butter
- half a cup sweet milk
- 2.5 cups flour
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Stir 1.5 cups sugar with half a cup butter, half a cup sweet milk
2. Whip 3 eggs separately and stir the whites in last
3. Sift 2.5 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder into the mixture
4. Bake in pie or jelly pans in the usual way
5. For the filling: cook together 1 cup sugar, 1 egg, grated lemon zest, 1 tablespoon water, and 1 teaspoon flour, then cool and place in thick layers between the cakes as usual
