## Ingredients

- Cooked tongue, skin removed
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Endive, salad or parsley for garnish
- Sauce piquante (No. 768) or Sauce à la diable (No. 760) or Tomato sauce (No. 749) or Brown mushroom sauce (No. 758)

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the cooked, skinned tongue with breadcrumbs and pour melted butter over it
2. Place in a warm oven to brown
3. Cut the tongue into slices and reassemble so it lies whole
4. Arrange and garnish with endive, salad or parsley
5. Serve with one of the suggested sauces: sauce piquante, sauce à la diable, tomato sauce, or brown mushroom sauce
