## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onion
- Flour
- Meat or fish stock
- Curry powder
- Rice

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and sweat a grated onion in it before adding the flour
2. Bake the flour and thin with meat or fish stock, depending on what the sauce is to be used for
3. When the sauce has boiled, add curry powder stirred in to taste
4. Serve cooked rice with curry
5. If the curry sauce is to be used for fish dishes, the onion is usually omitted
