## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Water or asparagus cooking liquid
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolk
- Sugar
- Maggi bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Drain the asparagus after it has been cooked as described above.
2. Brown the butter and flour and add the water in which the asparagus was cooked to make a smooth sauce, which is thickened with one egg yolk for every half pound of asparagus.
3. Add salt, a little sugar and Maggi bouillon cubes to the sauce.
4. Warm the asparagus in this sauce, without letting the sauce come to a boil.
