## Ingredients

- Parsnips
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Cream or milk
- Parsley
- Sugar
- Salt
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Peel, cut into pieces, cook tender by steam or in water and drain; melt butter, in which flour is mixed, cream or milk, which is cooked to a smooth sauce, into which the roots are placed together with chopped parsley, a small piece of sugar and finally salt. You can also cook the roots with a little bouillon and a little water. When they are tender, make a roux of butter and flour and chopped parsley.
