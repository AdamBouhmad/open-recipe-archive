## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Asparagus water
- Finely cut, peeled asparagus
- Egg yolks
- Lobster meat, peeled shrimp, and crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Prepare a baked white, even, thickened sauce as for false Hollandaise sauce
2. For thinning this use the water in which the finely cut, peeled asparagus has been cooked
3. Serve together with asparagus, lobster meat, peeled shrimp and crayfish tails
4. For sauce for 12 persons use 3 to 4 egg yolks for thickening
