## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef, finely chopped
- salt and other seasonings, if the doctor permits

## Directions

1. Chop the lean beef finely and place it in an enameled pot with a lid, without water
2. Place in a moderately warm oven for 4 hours
3. Strain off the juice and season, if the doctor allows it
4. Note: Beef prepared this way retains all the nourishing properties of the meat
