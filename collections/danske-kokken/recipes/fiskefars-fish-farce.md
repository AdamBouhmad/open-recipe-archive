## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound pike and 1/4 pound cod (or 3/8 pound total of one type of fish)
- 20 to 25 quints butter
- 3 small spoonfuls wheat flour
- 3 eggs
- sweet milk (approximately 3 pails, of which half can be cream)
- nutmeg to taste
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean and fillet the fish, remove the bones
2. Soak in cold water which is changed several times for a couple of hours
3. Carefully scrape the meat from the skin and chop fairly finely
4. Blend with butter and wheat flour, then chop finely
5. Stir the farce with eggs and sweet milk as needed
6. Add nutmeg and salt to taste
7. This makes approximately 100 small balls
8. If baked in browned butter as frikadeller, this amount will make 8 portions, served with browned butter as sauce
