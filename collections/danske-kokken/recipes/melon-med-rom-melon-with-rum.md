## Ingredients

- Melon, peeled and cut into pieces
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Water
- White rum

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water to a syrup, then add the rum.
2. Bring the peeled and cut melon to a boil in this.
3. Remove the fruit onto a colander, and reduce the liquid until it is very even to avoid boiling the next day.
4. Pour the liquid hot over the melon.
5. Seal the jar when the preserve has cooled.
