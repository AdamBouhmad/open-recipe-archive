## Ingredients

- 2 cents caraway seeds
- water
- fine rye flour
- 2 cents baker's yeast
- wheat flour or rye flour

## Directions

1. The evening before baking, boil 2 cents caraway seeds in about 3 cups water until the water turns brown.
2. Pour the hot liquid over fine rye flour and stir quickly to make an even dough.
3. When cooled, stir in 2 cents baker's yeast.
4. Cover well and let stand until the next day.
5. Knead up with wheat flour (and also rye flour if desired) and set to rise again before shaping into loaves.
6. Bake with good heat.
