## Ingredients

- Twenty fresh herring
- 6 coarsely chopped onions
- 25 gr (5 quintal) whole peppercorns
- 75 gr (15 quintal) sugar
- 100 gr (20 quintal) salt
- 15 to 20 bay leaves
- Vinegar water

## Directions

1. Clean the fresh herring and remove the bone
2. These herring, which are cut in the belly, are spread out
3. Mix 6 coarsely chopped onions with 25 gr (5 quintal) whole peppercorns, 75 gr (15 quintal) sugar and 100 gr (20 quintal) salt and distribute this mixture on the herring
4. Roll the herring from the tail upward and bind them with bands or gather them with a sausage pin
5. Cook the herring in vinegar water in which 15 to 20 bay leaves are added
6. The herring remain in the broth they are cooked in and are also arranged in this
7. Serve cold
8. If using cured herring instead of fresh herring, economize on the salt that is placed inside the herring and cook them in as little liquid as possible
