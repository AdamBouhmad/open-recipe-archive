## Ingredients

- eggs
- sweet milk or sweet cream
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. For each egg, take 2 tablespoons of sweet milk or sweet cream, which is whisked well together with a little salt. The mixture is then poured into a saucepan with melted butter, but only when it foams.
2. When the eggs begin to set, they are done.
3. 15 quarters of butter is calculated for 10 eggs.
