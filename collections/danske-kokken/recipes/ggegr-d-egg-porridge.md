## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 175 grams potato starch
- 175 grams wheat flour
- 4.5 liters semi-skimmed milk
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar
- red wine or juice and water

## Directions

1. Heat the milk to boiling
2. Mix the flour with the eggs in some of the cold milk to make a smooth mixture
3. As soon as the milk boils, stir the mixture in quickly
4. Bring to a boil again while stirring continuously
5. Cook for only 5 minutes
6. The porridge should be thin, as it becomes stiff quickly after being served
7. Season with salt and serve immediately
8. Serve with butter, cinnamon, and sugar
9. Serve red wine or juice and water on the side
