## Ingredients

- Berries (best of currants and raspberries together)
- Water
- white Sugar
- Isinglass
- blanched and sliced sweet Almonds

## Directions

1. Rod-grod is made from all sorts of berries, but is most delicious of currants and raspberries together.
2. Currants must be picked to avoid the taste of the stems.
3. Press the berries raw through a fine flannel cloth; when all the juice is wrung out, place it over the fire with equal amount of water as juice.
4. When hot, stir in sufficient white sugar.
5. Dissolve isinglass in lukewarm water, and when the juice is near boiling, stir this in.
6. It only needs to boil a couple of minutes to become thick, but must be stirred gently so it becomes smooth.
7. Thereafter pour it either into dishes or small moulds and cups, from which it can be turned out the next day.
8. Blanched and sliced sweet almonds seem pleasant to most in rod-grod.
