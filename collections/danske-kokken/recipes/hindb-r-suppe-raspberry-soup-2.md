## Ingredients

- 1 Quart Raspberries
- 3 Pint Water
- Sugar
- 1 large Spoonful Corn-Starch

## Directions

1. Pick, clean and place the raspberries over the fire in 3 pints water.
2. This boils as long as until all juice is extracted from the berries, then strain it through a fine sieve and place it over the fire again with sugar according to how sour the berries are; it may well be brown sugar.
3. Reserve a little of the soup after it is strained, and stir a large spoonful of corn-starch into it.
4. When the soup is near boiling, stir this in, it boils up again and is then ready to serve.
5. Serve with rusks.
