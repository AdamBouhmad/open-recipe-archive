## Ingredients

- 2 pots sweet milk
- ½ pound sago flour
- 6 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 wine glass rum
- a little chopped orange peel
- vanilla
- a little raspberry jam
- 9 egg whites
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Bring 2 pots sweet milk to boil.
2. Mix ½ pound sago flour in a third pot of milk and bring it to a boil in the first pot of milk.
3. Add 6 tablespoons powdered sugar, 1 wine glass rum, a little chopped orange peel, vanilla and a little raspberry jam.
4. While this boils, gently stir in 9 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks, and bring it to a boil.
5. At the very end add a little salt.
6. Turn the pudding out of the mold and serve with fruit sauce.
