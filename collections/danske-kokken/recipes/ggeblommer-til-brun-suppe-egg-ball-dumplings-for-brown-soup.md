## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites
- 10 tablespoons sweet cream
- a little grated nutmeg
- a very little sugar
- a couple of tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg yolks, 5 egg whites, and 10 tablespoons sweet cream well together.
2. Stir in a little grated nutmeg, a very little sugar, and a couple of tablespoons butter.
3. Pour the mixture into a mold that has been coated with butter and breadcrumbs.
4. Cook or bake like a pudding, then cut into oblong pieces and place in the soup when serving.
