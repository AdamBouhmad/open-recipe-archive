## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 250 grams (1/2 pund) powdered sugar
- 375 milliliters (1/2 pægel) water

## Directions

1. Cook a syrup from the sugar and water.
2. Peel the oranges, remove the white pith, slice them, and remove the seeds before placing them in bowls and pouring the syrup over them.
