## Ingredients

- White sugar
- Arrack

## Directions

1. Crush white sugar and place it in a saucepan or other suitable vessel. Pour the Arrack over it and ignite it, stirring and moving it up and down with a spoon. When the sugar is melted, extinguish the flame by placing a lid over it, and the drink is ready to serve.
