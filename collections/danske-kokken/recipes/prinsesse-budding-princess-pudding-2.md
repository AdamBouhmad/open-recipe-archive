## Ingredients

- 100 grams Pillsbury flour
- 100 grams margarine
- 0.5 liter boiling milk
- 8 egg yolks
- sugar
- salt
- cardamom
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. 100 grams of Pillsbury flour is baked in 100 grams of margarine and diluted with 0.5 liter of boiling milk.
2. Then 8 egg yolks, sugar, a little salt, and cardamom to taste, and the beaten egg whites are stirred in.
3. The pudding is cooked for 2 hours and served with fruit or cream sauce.
