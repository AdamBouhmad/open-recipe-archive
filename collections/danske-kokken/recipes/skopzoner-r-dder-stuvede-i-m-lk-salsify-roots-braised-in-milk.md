## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Lukewarm water
- Wheat flour
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Milk
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots and gradually place them in lukewarm water in which wheat flour and some vinegar have been dissolved to prevent them from darkening
2. Cut the roots like asparagus
3. Place in boiling salted water and as soon as tender, drain on a colander
4. Place in a milk sauce like that for braised potatoes and asparagus
