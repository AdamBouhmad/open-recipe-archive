## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- A good piece of butter
- Strong fish broth
- 2-3 egg yolks
- A couple spoonfuls of Madeira or other wine
- A little nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Knead cornstarch into a good piece of butter
2. Place this in a large cup of sieved strong fish broth and bring to a boil
3. Whip 2-3 egg yolks and stir into the sauce while it is removed from the heat
4. When it is about to boil again, add a couple spoonfuls of Madeira or other wine
5. Whip well until it is poured out
6. Add a little nutmeg and salt if needed
