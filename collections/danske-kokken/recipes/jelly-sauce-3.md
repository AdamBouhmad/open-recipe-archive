## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce Sugar
- a couple of Teaspoonfuls Currant Jelly
- 1 Pint boiling Water
- 2 Teaspoonfuls Corn Starch
- 1/2 Cup cold Water

## Directions

1. Melt 1 Ounce Sugar and a couple of Teaspoonfuls of Currant Jelly over the Fire in 1 Pint boiling Water.
2. Stir in 2 Teaspoonfuls Corn Starch dissolved in 1/2 Cup cold Water.
3. As soon as it boils up, it is done.
