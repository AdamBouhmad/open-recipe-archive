## Ingredients

- prepared puff pastry dough
- eggs
- meat or fish

## Directions

1. Roll out a base from ready-made puff pastry dough according to the size of the dish.
2. Around it place a strip a couple of fingers wide, brush it with beaten eggs and fasten it to the edge around the base.
3. Place it in a hot oven to bake.
4. When done, take it out and place a meat or fish dish in the opening.
