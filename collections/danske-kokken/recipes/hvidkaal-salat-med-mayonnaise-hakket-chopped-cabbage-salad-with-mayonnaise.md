## Ingredients

- 1 small, raw white cabbage head
- Mayonnaise made with mustard
- Cooked carrots
- Green head lettuce

## Directions

1. Chop a small, raw white cabbage head fairly fine and turn this chopped cabbage in mayonnaise made with mustard.
2. Decorate the salad with coarsely chopped cooked carrots and a little sliced green head lettuce.
