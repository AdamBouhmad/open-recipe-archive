## Ingredients

- smoked or green-salted ham
- water
- salt
- ground hardtack
- green cabbage

## Directions

1. Soak the smoked or green-salted ham 24 hours in cold water, or if very salty, in lukewarm water.
2. After scraping and rinsing well, boil in abundant water for 2.5 to 5 hours depending on its size.
3. When tender, remove it; take off the skin except at the shank where a small strip is kept.
4. Sprinkle the ham with ground hardtack and place in a hot oven for a quarter hour.
5. After the meal, cover the remaining part of the ham with the removed skin.
6. Serve with green cabbage.
