## Ingredients

- 24 egg whites
- 36 quintals powdered sugar
- 0.5 pound raspberry marmalade

## Directions

1. Whip 24 egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Stir 36 quintals powdered sugar into 1/2 pound raspberry marmalade and pour into a cake pan
3. Add the egg whites one tablespoon at a time and stir carefully into the jam
4. Bake in a moderate oven for approximately 1.5 hours and serve with whipped cream over top
