## Ingredients

- 2 pints cream
- 6 egg yolks
- Capers
- Chopped shallot
- Pepper
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Pieces of cold, cooked fish, freed of skin and bones

## Directions

1. Stir 2 pints cream and 6 egg yolks into a crème
2. Add capers, chopped shallot, pepper, salt, vinegar and sugar to taste
3. Place pieces of cold, cooked fish, freed of skin and bones, in this sauce
4. Alternatively, use diced cooked chicken or veal meat mixed with diced cooked celery
5. Pickled mushrooms may be used instead of capers if desired
