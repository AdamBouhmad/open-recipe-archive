## Ingredients

- Dried cod
- Water
- Boiled potatoes
- Butter
- Egg

## Directions

1. Cut the scraped and cleaned dried cod into small pieces, and place these in lukewarm water for about an hour.
2. Remove the skin and bones and pick it finely; place it in cold water and set over the heat; when it boils, change the water and let it boil again.
3. Have ready cooked, mashed and butter-stirred potatoes.
4. Stir the fish and potato mixture together while both are hot; stir in a well-beaten egg and make balls or thick cakes from the mixture, drop these in enough melted fat so they float in it, and fish them out.
