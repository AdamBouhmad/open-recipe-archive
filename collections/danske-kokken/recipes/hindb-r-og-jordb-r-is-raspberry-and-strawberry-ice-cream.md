## Ingredients

- Raspberries and strawberries
- Powdered sugar
- 1 to 2 pints water
- 1 pound sugar
- Optional: lemon juice, a shot of rum, a beaten egg white

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the berries with powdered sugar and let them stand thus for half a day.
2. Boil a sugar syrup of 1 to 2 pints water and 1 pound sugar, which is poured boiling over the berries, which are first crushed into pieces.
3. When the mixture is cooled, strain it and freeze the juice.
4. You can, if you wish, add a little lemon juice, a shot of rum, and a beaten egg white.
