## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Dl (1/2 Pægl) water
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 tb) powdered sugar
- Zest of 3 oranges
- Juice of 6 oranges
- Graves or Rhine wine
- A very little arrowroot flour

## Directions

1. Make a sugar syrup cooked from 1 1/2 Dl (1/2 Pægl) water and 1/4 Kg (1/2 tb) powdered sugar
2. Stir in grated zest of 3 and juice of 6 oranges together with Graves or Rhine wine to taste
3. The sauce can be thinned with water if desired
4. Smooth it with a very little mixed arrowroot flour and can be served both warm and cold
