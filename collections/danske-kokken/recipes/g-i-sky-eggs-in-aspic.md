## Ingredients

- Hard-boiled eggs
- Good aspic
- Isinglass
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs until hard, then peel them.
2. Cut a little off one end so the eggs can stand upright on the plate.
3. Pour good aspic stiffened with isinglass between the eggs.
4. Alternatively, halve the eggs and place them with the cut side down.
5. Place a little parsley on top of each egg.
