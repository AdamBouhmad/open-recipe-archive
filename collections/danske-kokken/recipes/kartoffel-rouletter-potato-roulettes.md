## Ingredients

- cooked, cold potatoes
- 2 eggs
- 4 spoonfuls butter
- a little sweet cream
- salt
- pepper
- fine sifted bread crumbs
- egg whites
- clarified butter for frying

## Directions

1. Grate cooked cold potatoes and mix with 2 eggs, 4 spoonfuls butter, a little sweet cream, salt, and pepper. Mix well together, and form the mixture into oblong or round roulettes, which are rolled in fine sifted bread crumbs and set aside for half an hour. Then dip them in egg whites and roll in bread crumbs, and thus they are breaded twice. Fry them in clarified butter, 5 to 6 pieces at a time, until they become beautifully golden brown. Take them out with a slotted spoon and place on a sieve so the butter can drip off. Use them as garnish for roasted meat and similar dishes.
