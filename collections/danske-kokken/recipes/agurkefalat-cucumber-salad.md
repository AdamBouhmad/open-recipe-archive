## Ingredients

- Large, green cucumbers
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them into slices as thin as possible.
2. Lay the slices on a plate with coarse salt between them and place a press over them; let them stand for several hours, then press them hard between the hands so no salt water follows.
3. Place them in a dish and add vinegar and pepper.
4. If they must be made immediately before the meal, sprinkle salt on the slices, lay them between two deep plates and shake them strongly so the salt spreads around, then press and prepare them.
