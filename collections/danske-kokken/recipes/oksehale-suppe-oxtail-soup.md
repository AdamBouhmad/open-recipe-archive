## Ingredients

- 3 oxtails
- 5 L (5 Pots) spare soup
- Butter or Claret
- 1 pair of onions, diced
- 4 whole carrots
- Paprika
- Sherry
- Tomato Purée
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Sago flour
- Water
- Kulør (caramel coloring)
- Sliced eggs or hard-boiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. Order the oxtails from the butcher
2. Cut them into pieces joint by joint, wash in cold water and scald in boiling water
3. Brown butter or claret in a pot, and in it fry 1 pair of diced onions, 4 whole carrots and a little paprika
4. Brown the tails together with the carrots
5. Cook them tender in 5 L (5 Pots) thin, brown spare soup or water with salt for at least 3 hours or until the meat easily separates from the bones
6. Remove them
7. Strain the soup, skim and season with Sherry, a little Tomato Purée and Maggi Bouillon cubes if needed
8. Thicken with sago flour mixed in water
9. Put the tails back in the soup to keep warm until serving and serve in the soup, which is given a nice brown color with kulør
10. For Oxtail Soup serve sliced eggs or hard-boiled egg yolks, 1 in each plate
