## Ingredients

- Lamb's heads
- Salt
- Water
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Butter
- Browned butter or parsley sauce
- Salad
- Vegetables

## Directions

1. After the heads are split and the brain removed, clean them, rinse and place in cold water for an hour.
2. Then boil them in water with fairly abundant salt until the skin can be removed, then clean them carefully.
3. Cut off the ears and remove the eyes, but do not remove the fat in the eye sockets behind the eye.
4. Split the tongues and fry them alongside the heads.
5. Brush these with beaten eggs and sprinkle with ground bread mixed with ground pepper, salt and finely chopped parsley.
6. Fry them a light golden brown in butter in a pan.
7. Serve lamb's heads either with browned butter or with parsley sauce, and serve salad and other vegetables alongside.
