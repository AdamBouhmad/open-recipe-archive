## Ingredients

- rye bread
- boiling water
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Slice rye bread and toast it on both sides
2. Break it into pieces
3. Pour boiling water over it
4. Prepare the bread water with lemon juice and sugar
5. When cold, strain it
