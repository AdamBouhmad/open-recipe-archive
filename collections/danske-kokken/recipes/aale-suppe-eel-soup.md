## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) eel
- butter
- flour
- a mixture of eel and white meat broth
- carrots
- bunch
- flour dumplings
- cooked apples
- prunes
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. After the eels have been slaughtered, skinned, cleaned, rinsed and the fins cut off as shown in No. 508, cut them into 5 centimeter (2 inch) long pieces, which are cooked in salted water.
2. When the eels are cooked, remove them and keep them warm.
3. Brown butter and flour as for Kræse Soup (No. 372).
4. Dilute it with a mixture of eel and white meat broth, in which carrots and bunch have been cooked.
5. Cut the carrots in half and add them to the tureen together with the eels, flour dumplings, as well as cooked apples and prunes.
6. Season the soup with vinegar and sugar.
7. It must be served very hot.
