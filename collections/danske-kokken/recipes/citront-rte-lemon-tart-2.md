## Ingredients

- Zest of 4 lemons
- 1 pound white sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- Juice of the 4 lemons
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 4 lemons onto 1 pound white sugar, then pound it finely and stir for an hour with 20 egg yolks and the strained juice of the 4 lemons
2. Whip 10 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in carefully
3. Bake the mixture in a buttered form at moderate heat for up to 1 hour
