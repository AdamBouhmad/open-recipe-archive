## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 anchovies
- butter
- 2 to 3 spoonfuls cooked water
- flour
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pound 6 to 8 anchovies in a mortar together with a piece of butter.
2. Add 2 to 3 spoonfuls cooked water and strain.
3. Put butter and flour on heat, bake together and thin with the anchovy mixture.
4. When it has come to a boil, thicken it with 3 egg yolks.
