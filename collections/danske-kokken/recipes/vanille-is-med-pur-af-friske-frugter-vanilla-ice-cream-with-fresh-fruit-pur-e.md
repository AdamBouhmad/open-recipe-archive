## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) fruit purée for 1 L (1 Pot) cream or milk
- cream as Nr. 1068
- fresh fruit (strawberries, raspberries, cherries, apricots, or peaches)

## Directions

1. Cook the cream as Nr. 1068 and freeze, but only after it is frozen to the point that it is a thick mass, add 1/2 Kg (1 S) purée of one or another sort of fruit.
2. Stir it well around in the mixture and then pack it in the freezer.
3. The fruit must be fully ripe and pressed through a sieve.
4. It can be either strawberries, raspberries, cherries, apricots, or peaches.
