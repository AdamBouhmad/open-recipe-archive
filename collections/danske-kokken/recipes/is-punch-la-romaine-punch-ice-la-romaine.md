## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) water
- Thinly pared and sliced zest of 1 orange
- Juice of 2 oranges
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1/4 bottle good aged rum or arrack
- 1 bottle white wine or champagne
- 100 gr (20 quintals) powdered sugar
- Foam from 4 to 5 stiffly beaten egg whites
- Or alternatively foam from 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream

## Directions

1. Cook the powdered sugar with the water to a thin syrup.
2. When cold, add the thinly pared and sliced orange zest along with the juice of 2 oranges and 2 lemons.
3. After 15 minutes pass, strain the whole mixture and pour it into the freezing container.
4. Pack this in a mixture of finely chopped ice and Spanish salt; treat as otherwise done when freezing ice.
5. Into the frozen mass work in 1/4 bottle of good aged rum or arrack along with 1 bottle of white wine or champagne.
6. Sprinkle 100 gr powdered sugar into the foam of 4 to 5 stiffly beaten egg whites, stir with a light hand until well blended, and stir this egg white mixture into the punch shortly before serving.
7. Alternatively cream foam can be used instead of the egg white mixture.
8. Serve this thick punch preferably in flat champagne glasses; pour into glass decanters.
