## Ingredients

- very small cod
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- Palmin (cooking fat)
- parsley
- lemon
- capers sauce
- mushroom sauce
- tomato sauce
- chutney sauce
- butter
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. From very small cod, cut off the head and remove the innards; scrape the fish, split it, clean it, and cut off the fins.
2. Rinse the fillets several times in cold water and sprinkle with a little salt.
3. After drying in a fish cloth, dip them in flour, then in egg and breadcrumbs, and fry in Palmin until crispy and nicely brown.
4. Arrange them immediately, as they otherwise become soft.
5. As soon as the fish is cleaned, cook the heads and backbones for the sauce, which is prepared while the fish is frying.
6. Garnish the fish with parsley and slices of fresh tomatoes.
