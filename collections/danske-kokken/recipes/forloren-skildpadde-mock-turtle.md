## Ingredients

- Head of a fattened calf, cleaned and cooked
- Beef neck meat or good shank, 245 Pd.
- Onions
- Beets
- Carrots
- Small celery root
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Salt
- Bouquet garni
- Calf stock
- Lemon juice
- Sugar
- Soya
- Cayenne pepper
- Madeira wine, half a bottle, or half a measure of good cognac
- Madeira wine, one more measure, before serving
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Small fish balls made from fish farce of 24 Pd.

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the head of a fattened calf the day before it is to be used (see recipe 23), then place the cut meat on a platter.
2. Brown beef neck meat or good shank with abundant onions, beets, carrots, a small celery root, pepper, ginger, salt and a bouquet garni, and let the whole simmer until the meat is very tender.
3. Finally thin it with a little of the calf stock and let it cook for a long time, but gently.
4. Let it stand for a while to settle, then strain it.
5. In this sauce, warm the meat from the calf's head when it is to be used, together with a little lemon juice, sugar, soya and a small amount of cayenne pepper, and half a bottle of Madeira or half a measure of good cognac.
6. Immediately before serving, add one more measure of Madeira.
7. Serve the mock turtle with hard-boiled egg yolks and small fish balls.
8. From a good head, reckon 10-12 portions.
