## Ingredients

- 3 quarter-pounds cleaned rice grains (No. 6)
- 1 can sweet milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 4 pounds powdered sugar
- 1 quarter-pound finely ground sweet almonds
- 10 quintals bitter almonds, finely ground
- 1 pound clarified butter
- 8 egg whites, beaten
- apples, peeled and cored
- jam
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil 3 quarter-pounds cleaned rice grains in 1 can sweet milk.
2. When they are cold, stir in 8 egg yolks one by one, then 4 pounds powdered sugar, 1 quarter-pound finely ground sweet and 10 quintals bitter almonds, 1 pound clarified butter and finally the 8 beaten egg whites.
3. Make a compote from apples that are peeled and cored removed.
4. Layer in the form: first a layer of the gruel, then jam, then apple compote, then gruel again and so on until the form is full.
5. Sprinkle the cake with grated bread and bake for 1 hour.
