## Ingredients

- 125 g powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg strawberries
- Sherry or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Choose large fleshy strawberries, preferably light-colored pineapple strawberries, pick them in dry weather and remove stems.
2. Place them immediately into completely dry glasses with wide necks.
3. Sprinkle sugar between the berries.
4. Tap the glasses on the table so the fruit settles tightly together.
5. Pour as much Sherry or Madeira into the filled glasses as there is room for.
6. Tie up immediately.
