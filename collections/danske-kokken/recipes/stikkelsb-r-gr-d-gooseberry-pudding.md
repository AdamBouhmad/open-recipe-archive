## Ingredients

- 1½ kg gooseberries
- ½ kg sugar
- ¼ L water
- Sago flour
- Wine or water for thinning
- Vanilla essence (optional)
- Fresh cream or crème for serving

## Directions

1. For unsieved pudding: melt the sugar in water
2. Cook the picked and rinsed berries in this liquid until they begin to fall apart
3. Stir in a little sago flour thickening, thinned in wine or water
4. The pudding will then come to a boil
5. Adjust sweetness to taste according to the tartness of the fruit
6. For sieved pudding: cook the berries in 2 L water, strain, sweeten, and thicken
7. Add vanilla essence and serve with raw cream or crème
