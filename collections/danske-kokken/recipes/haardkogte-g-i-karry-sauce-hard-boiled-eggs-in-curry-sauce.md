## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth or Maggi bouillon cubes
- Water
- Curry
- Cooked rice

## Directions

1. For this, boil the eggs for approximately 10 minutes. Remove them and pour cold water over them so the shell comes off better. Peel and arrange whole on a platter. While the eggs boil, make the curry sauce from butter and flour and dilute it with meat broth or Maggi bouillon cubes dissolved in water. Stir in curry to taste. Pour the sauce over the eggs, which can be garnished with cooked rice.
