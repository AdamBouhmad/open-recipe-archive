## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- cream
- chopped parsley
- white sugar
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Melt butter, stir flour in it and dilute with cream until smooth
2. Give chopped parsley a boiling in it
3. Add a small piece of white sugar and salt at the end
4. Milk can be used instead of cream, but then more butter is used
