## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 12 quarters sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- 25 quarters grated chocolate, not too sweet

## Directions

1. Place 1 pot of sweet cream, 12 quarters sugar, the zest of half a lemon, and 25 quarters of grated chocolate, not too sweet, over the heat and give it a boil under constant whisking, after which it is strained through a hair sieve and set to cool.
2. The cream is poured into a deep cake dish, which is set in boiling water and stands there for a few minutes until the cream has become firm, after which it is set to cool.
3. It is served with sweet bread.
