## Ingredients

- Thin slices of beef
- Beef forcemeat
- Carrot or fatty meat
- Spices
- Butter
- Onion
- Water
- Broth
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Thin slices of beef are cut and beaten very little.
2. On each piece is spread a spoonful of beef forcemeat, into which is placed a narrow piece of carrot or fatty meat, which is first dipped in spices.
3. The meat is folded together and formed in the shape of a bird without head and legs.
4. The meat is packed together with meat and bound with twine.
5. The birds are rubbed with flour, pepper, a little crushed cloves and salt.
6. A piece of butter is browned, in which a grated onion is boiled, then the birds, until they are slightly brown.
7. This is thinned with water and broth until it stands over them, and boiled gently for a couple of hours or until they are tender, then they are placed on a platter and kept warm, while the sauce is strained, put back on the fire with a little cream and flour if it is not strong and even enough, and poured over the birds.
8. These can be made in the same way without forcemeat, only with a piece of carrot or meat in the middle.
