## Ingredients

- Fresh parsley
- Clarified butter or Palmin

## Directions

1. Remove the coarse stems from the parsley
2. Rinse the parsley in water and allow it to drain well
3. Place it in hot clarified butter or Palmin and cook until crisp
4. Use the cooked parsley to garnish roulettes, croquettes, etc.
