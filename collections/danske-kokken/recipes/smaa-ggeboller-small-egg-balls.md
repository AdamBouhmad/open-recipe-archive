## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 10 grams (2 dram) wheat flour

## Directions

1. Mash the 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
2. Stir with melted butter, a little salt and pepper, and wheat flour
3. Roll into small balls
4. Cover until ready to serve in brown or clear soup or on top of mock turtle
