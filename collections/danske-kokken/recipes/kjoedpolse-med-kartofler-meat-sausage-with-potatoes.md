## Ingredients

- 5 pounds finely minced meat
- 2.5 pounds bacon in fine cubes
- 5 pounds finely minced raw potatoes
- Salt
- Strong pepper
- Seasonings
- Oil

## Directions

1. Mince the meat finely like dough and the potatoes like grains.
2. Mix well and work in the bacon, pepper, salt, and oil.
3. Do not stuff the sausage firmly.
4. Salt and boil as in the previous recipe.
