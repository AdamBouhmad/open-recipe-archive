## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Gravy
- Paprika
- Salt
- Madeira
- Maggi bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and bake flour in it, thin with good gravy and season as in the Madeira Gravy with paprika, salt, Madeira and Maggi bouillon cubes
