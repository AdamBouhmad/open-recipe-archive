## Ingredients

- 4 L soup
- 1 kg cooked asparagus
- 125 gr butter
- 125 gr flour
- 4 egg yolks
- asparagus water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Brown 125 gr butter and bake 125 gr flour in it
2. Thin with 4 L brown spare soup together with the asparagus water
3. Beat 4 egg yolks with a couple of spoonfuls of cold water and stir into the soup
