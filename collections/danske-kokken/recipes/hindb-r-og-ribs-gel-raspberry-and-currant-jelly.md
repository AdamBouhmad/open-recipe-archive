## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg raspberries (half)
- 1/2 kg red currants (half)
- 1/2 kg sugar (or corresponding weight to combined juices)
- 1 1/4 dl water

## Directions

1. Mix half raspberries and half red currants together and treat as above.
2. Cook as No. 93.
3. The berries are cooked to Russian marmalade, see No. 147.
