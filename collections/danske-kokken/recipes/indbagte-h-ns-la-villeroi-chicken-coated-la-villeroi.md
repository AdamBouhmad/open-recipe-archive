## Ingredients

- chickens, cooked
- batter (as in recipe 560)
- clarified butter or Palmin for frying
- cooked rice
- Chutney sauce (recipe 759) or Macaroni and Tomato sauce (recipe 790)

## Directions

1. Cook and portion the chickens as for breaded chicken.
2. Dip them in batter.
3. Fry light brown in clarified butter or Palmin.
4. Serve with cooked rice and Chutney sauce, or with macaroni and Tomato sauce.
