## Ingredients

- grain
- 2 quarts water (proportionally less than milk)
- butter
- currants (optional)

## Directions

1. Use proportionally less water than milk would be used for the milk version; measure about 2 quarts water to the same amount of grain
2. When cooked, stir in a spoonful of butter
3. Add currants if desired
