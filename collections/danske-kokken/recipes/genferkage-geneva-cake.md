## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds butter
- 20 eggs
- 1.5 pounds dried, sifted, pounded white sugar
- 1.5 pounds finely pounded sweet almonds
- 1 pound finest fine flour
- Grated lemon zest
- Jam or marmalade, preferably cherry
- White sugar
- Egg white
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Wash 1.5 pounds butter in cold water and set aside by the heat so it can be stirred
2. Stir it well with 20 eggs and 1.5 pounds dried, sifted, pounded white sugar, 1.5 pounds finely pounded sweet almonds, 1 pound finest fine flour, and a little grated lemon zest
3. Form rounds from white paper, brush with butter, and spread the batter to the thickness of a thick pancake on them
4. Bake until quite pale brown, remove from the paper with great care using a knife, and trim the edges
5. Let each layer cool separately, then layer them together with jam between them, preferably cherry
6. Pound the trimmings from the edges and stir into thick currant jelly, and use this to coat the edge of the cake all around
7. Glaze the top with white sugar and egg white mixed with the strained juice of 1 lemon, then set to dry
8. Bake in a tart pan, and do not trim the edges until the cake has been baked approximately a day, as it is very brittle
