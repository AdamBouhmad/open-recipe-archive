## Ingredients

- Peeled and cooked mealy potatoes
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Shallots
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Palmin (cooking fat)

## Directions

1. Mash the peeled and cooked potatoes.
2. Mix in a little pepper, salt, finely chopped parsley, shallots, and a couple of eggs.
3. Roll in egg and breadcrumbs.
4. Fry in Palmin.
5. Serve hot with roasts or meat dishes.
6. May also be made with finely chopped ham.
