## Ingredients

- ground mustard
- water
- butter
- flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Stir ground mustard with a little water and cook under constant stirring with a slice of butter.
2. Alternatively, add as much flour by weight as mustard, and twice as much butter, and thin the sauce with bouillon.
