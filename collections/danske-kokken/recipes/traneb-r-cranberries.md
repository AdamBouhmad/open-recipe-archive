## Ingredients

- 375 gr (3/4 S) powdered sugar or Demerara sugar
- 2 1/2 dl (1 Pægl) water
- 1/2 kg (1 tt) fruit

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water to a thin syrup
2. Add the cleaned and rinsed berries and cook until they change color
3. Remove them and place in a crock, then reduce the liquid until very thick
4. Mix the berries with the liquid
5. Pour into jars and seal immediately
6. American cranberries can be preserved the same way
