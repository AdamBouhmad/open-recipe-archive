## Ingredients

- Venison haunch
- Butter
- Flour
- Game stock
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. After the haunch has been larded and roasted, cut it into smaller pieces.
2. Make a good sauce from the pan drippings by browning butter and flour.
3. The sauce can be finished as with venison fillets (Nr. 942).
4. Leftover ragout can be put in a pastry (see Nr. 41), and bones can be cooked in milk or thin soup for sauce to the pastry.
