## Ingredients

- Half a pound good chocolate
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3 cups sweet cream
- 4 pounds sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites, whipped with powdered icing sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the chocolate in boiling water.
2. Add 3 cups sweet cream and 4 pounds sugar to the chocolate.
3. Boil the mixture for one hour, stirring constantly.
4. Whip the egg yolks and pour the warm chocolate mixture into them.
5. Bring to a boil while constantly whisking until the mixture becomes thick and smooth.
6. Pour into a dish to cool.
7. When cold, fold in the whipped egg whites mixed with powdered icing sugar.
8. Place the mixture in a deep porcelain pan in a moderately warm oven.
9. When it has risen, remove and serve either without sauce or with vanilla sauce.
