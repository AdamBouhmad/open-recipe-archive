## Ingredients

- fine apples
- sugar
- water
- jam (raspberry or cherry)
- 1 quart cream
- 8 egg yolks
- vanilla
- half a tablespoon corn starch

## Directions

1. Peel apples and cut off a lid, hollowing them out
2. Simmer in sugar and a little water so they do not fall apart
3. Arrange them close together in a fairly deep pan and fill with jam without liquid, preferably raspberry or cherry marmalade
4. Cover with the cut-off lids
5. Make a cream from 1 quart cream, 8 egg yolks, a little vanilla, half a tablespoon corn starch, and sugar to taste
6. When cold, pour the cream over the apples
7. Place the pan in the oven and bake the cake slowly
8. When the batter begins to set and becomes light brown, it is done
