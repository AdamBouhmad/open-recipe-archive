## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- flour
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. To remove the skin from ripe tomatoes, pour boiling water over them, then drain it off at once and cover with cold water
2. When peeled, stew them with flour, butter and pepper in an enameled pot or earthenware vessel
