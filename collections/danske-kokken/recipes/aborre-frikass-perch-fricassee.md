## Ingredients

- 2 kg perch
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Sherry or Graves
- Lemon juice
- White pepper
- Mushrooms or oysters (optional)

## Directions

1. Skin and split the perches, remove the backbone, and cut into smaller pieces
2. Rinse the pieces well and let them stand sprinkled with salt for one hour
3. Cook the fish in very little water with salt so the sauce becomes better
4. Brown the butter and flour together and dilute with the fish stock to a white, smooth sauce
5. Thicken the sauce with a few egg yolks and season with Sherry or Graves, a little lemon juice, and ground white pepper
6. The sauce may be varied with the addition of mushrooms or oysters
7. Pour the sauce over the fish and garnish with puff pastry strips
