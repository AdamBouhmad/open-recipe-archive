## Ingredients

- Plums
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prick each plum with a needle so the skin does not burst
2. For each 3 quarts fruit prepare a sugar solution of 0.5 gill water and 0.5 pound sugar
3. When the water is well warmed and all sugar dissolved, add the plums
4. Bring slowly to a boil and cook very slowly for 5 minutes
5. Fill into jars as directed above
