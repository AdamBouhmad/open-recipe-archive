## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- ½ pound powdered sugar
- 3 pounds wheat flour
- 1 good tablespoon water
- chopped almonds
- coarsely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Mix butter, powdered sugar, and wheat flour together until it crumbles
2. Add a good tablespoon of water
3. Roll the dough very thin
4. Cut it into small cakes
5. Sprinkle with chopped almonds and coarsely ground sugar
6. Bake at even heat
