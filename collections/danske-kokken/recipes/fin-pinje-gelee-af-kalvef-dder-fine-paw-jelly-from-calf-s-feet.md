## Ingredients

- 12 large calf's feet
- 12 quarts wine
- 3 pounds sugar
- a small piece of broken cinnamon
- juice of 12 lemons
- yellow peel of 3 lemons
- 6-8 lightly beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the 12 calf's feet according to the instructions given above.
2. Add 12 quarts of wine, 3 pounds of sugar, a small piece of cinnamon broken into pieces, the juice of 12 and the yellow peel of 3 lemons, and 6-8 lightly beaten egg whites.
3. Can be eaten from small glasses, or placed in molds that are first rinsed, and turned out.
