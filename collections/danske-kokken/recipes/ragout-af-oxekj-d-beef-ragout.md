## Ingredients

- Slices of leftover boiled beef or roasted beef
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Egg yolk
- Capers
- Lemon capers
- Pickled onions
- Soy sauce

## Directions

1. Brown butter with half as much flour.
2. Thin with broth to make a sauce of the right consistency.
3. Add egg yolk, capers, lemon capers, pickled onions, and soy sauce to taste.
4. Place the beef slices in the sauce and bring to a boil together.
5. Do not boil longer than until the meat is heated through, otherwise the beef becomes tough.
