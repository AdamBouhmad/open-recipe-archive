## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 400 grams fine sugar
- 500 grams margarine
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- vanilla essence
- 500 grams cake flour
- egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks, fine sugar, margarine, baking powder, and vanilla essence into a cream
2. Add cake flour
3. Fold in whipped egg whites last
4. Bake in a greased form for 1 hour
