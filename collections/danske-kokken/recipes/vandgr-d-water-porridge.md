## Ingredients

- water
- barley grits or rice grains

## Directions

1. Instead of milk, use water to cook barley grits or rice grains in the proportions given for those dishes
2. To improve the flavor, cook a tablespoon of washed caraway seeds with barley water porridge or some washed currants with rice water porridge
