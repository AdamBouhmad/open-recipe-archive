## Ingredients

- apricots
- water
- white sugar
- white wine

## Directions

1. Boil the apricots in water
2. Remove them and strip off the skin and split them
3. Remove the stones and place the apricot pieces in a sauce of white sugar boiled with a little water
4. Crack some of the stones, remove the kernels, scald them and remove the outer skin, then place them in the compote
