## Ingredients

- Large, best berries, 1 quart
- Powdered sugar, ½ pound
- Cinnamon or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook the berries with the powdered sugar and a little cinnamon or lemon peel, and skim until they are no longer sour, usually in a quarter of an hour
2. Strain them, lay a cloth over and tie them up the next day
3. Small pieces of apple can be cooked together with the berries, which greatly removes their bitter taste
