## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter, clarified
- 8 egg yolks, 1 at a time
- 1 pint cream
- ½ pound flour
- 5 egg whites, whipped to foam

## Directions

1. Clarify and stir 1 pound butter until stiff
2. Stir in 8 egg yolks, 1 at a time
3. Stir in 1 pint cream and ½ pound flour
4. Whip 5 egg whites to foam and add when beginning to bake
5. Grease the iron from time to time
6. This makes approximately 18 waffles
7. The butter can also be rinsed out and stirred to cream, in which case stir in 4 egg yolks, ½ pound flour, 1 pint warm milk, and the whipped whites; grease the iron for each cake
