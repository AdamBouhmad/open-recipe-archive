## Ingredients

- 1 coconut, peeled and grated
- 1 pound sugar
- 1/2 pint water
- 1 pound sugar
- egg yolks from 7 well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound sugar and 1/2 pint water for fifteen minutes.
2. Stir in the grated coconut and cook for fifteen minutes more.
3. While hot, stir in 1 pound sugar and the yolks from 7 well-beaten eggs.
4. Bake in small, half-oval cake pans.
