## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Stock
- Two egg yolks
- Two dessertspoons powdered sugar
- Beetroot vinegar
- French or Russian mustard
- Pepper
- Salt
- Wine
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- One grated onion
- Two apples
- Salted herring
- Meat
- Pickled cucumbers
- Potatoes
- Beetroots
- Chopped eggs for garnish
- Pickled cucumbers or small pickles for garnish
- Parsley or watercress

## Directions

1. Make a white, smooth sauce from butter and flour, diluted with good stock
2. Mix 2 egg yolks with 2 dessertspoons powdered sugar and pour in the warm sauce, stirring until cooled
3. Season with beetroot vinegar, French or Russian mustard, pepper, salt, a little wine, Maggi bouillon cubes, and one grated onion
4. Cut 2 apples, salted herring, meat, pickled cucumbers, potatoes, and beetroots in equal parts into 1½ centimeter (½ inch) long, narrow fillets
5. Turn these gently into the sauce
6. The salad should be made a few hours before serving
7. Garnish with chopped eggs and pickled cucumbers or small pickles arranged in a pattern, with parsley or watercress between
8. A few drops of red food coloring can be added if necessary
