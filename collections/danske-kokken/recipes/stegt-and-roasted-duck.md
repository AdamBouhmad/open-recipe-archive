## Ingredients

- Duck
- Butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- Apples
- Prunes
- Giblets
- Liver
- Wheat flour
- Bouillon
- Duck gravy
- Roasted potatoes
- Red cabbage

## Directions

1. Pluck the duck, singe and remove the entrails. Cut off the head and feet, rinse and rub inside with pepper and salt.
2. Brown butter in the pan and roast the duck at very even heat for approximately 1 good hour until it is a beautiful light brown.
3. Baste with the sauce from time to time, which is thinned with bouillon or gravy if necessary.
4. Like the goose, it can be filled with apples and prunes, and in that case place giblets and liver inside the duck, otherwise place them under the wings but they will roast too quickly then.
5. The duck should not be roasted too much; it is roasted enough when the leg easily loosens from the side.
6. Brown the sauce with flour, thin with duck gravy and strain. Serve with roasted potatoes and red cabbage.
