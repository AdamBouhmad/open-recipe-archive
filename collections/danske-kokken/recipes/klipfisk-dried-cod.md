## Ingredients

- Klipfisk (dried cod)
- Water
- Soda
- Salt
- Boiled peeled potatoes
- Mustard sauce or melted butter
- Hard-boiled eggs, chopped

## Directions

1. The day before use, trim the fins and cut the fish into suitable pieces
2. Soak in cold water with a small piece of soda for 16 to 24 hours
3. The next day, scrape the scales without removing the skin
4. Place in fresh cold water again
5. Cook slowly for about 1 hour, placing in fresh cold water before cooking
6. When cooked, remove and arrange with skin side up
7. Serve with boiled peeled potatoes and mustard sauce or melted butter with chopped hard-boiled eggs
