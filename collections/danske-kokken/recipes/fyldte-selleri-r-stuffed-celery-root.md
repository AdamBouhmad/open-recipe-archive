## Ingredients

- Medium-sized celery roots
- Forcemeat
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Peel the roots and cut smooth, turn them into pear shapes
2. Cut out a round lid and hollow out the roots with a knife
3. Cook until a larding needle passes easily through them
4. Fill with forcemeat and replace the lid
5. Place closely together in a low pot and cook slowly with bouillon
6. Serve with their own sauce alongside meat dishes
