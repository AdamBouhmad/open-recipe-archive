## Ingredients

- oranges
- spirits
- 1½ pounds fine sugar
- 2 pots boiling water
- 4 quintals citric acid
- 2 drams orange essence
- 14 quintals cochineal drops

## Directions

1. Peel oranges very thinly and place the peel in a bottle in which you pour so much spirits that it stands somewhat over the peel
2. Let it stand corked for 3 months to infuse
3. When you want to make the lemonade, dissolve 1½ pounds fine sugar in 2 pots boiling water with 4 quintals citric acid
4. When it is dissolved and cooled, add 2 drams of the orange essence with 14 quintals cochineal drops
5. This lemonade is especially suitable for children
