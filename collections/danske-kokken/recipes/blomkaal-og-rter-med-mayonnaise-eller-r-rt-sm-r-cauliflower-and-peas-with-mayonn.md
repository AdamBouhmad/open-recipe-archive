## Ingredients

- cooked cauliflower heads
- cooked peas
- parsley
- mayonnaise or butter

## Directions

1. Arrange a large or several smaller cooked cauliflower heads in the center of a dish
2. Garnish with a wreath of cooked peas
3. Rinse a couple of bunches of parsley, press dry in a cloth, chop finely and sprinkle over the vegetable dish
4. Serve with mayonnaise or butter
5. For a more decorative presentation, place the mayonnaise or butter in tartlets arranged around the vegetable dish, ensuring the tartlets are cold
