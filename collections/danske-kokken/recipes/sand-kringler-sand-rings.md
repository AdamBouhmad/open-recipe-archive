## Ingredients

- 105 grams (35 quintal) margarine
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 kg (1/10 lb) icing sugar
- a little cardamom
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) flour

## Directions

1. Mix the margarine with 2 eggs, 1/4 kg icing sugar and a little cardamom until it is a tough dough
2. Then mix in 1/2 kg flour
3. Form the dough into small rings
4. Bake until light brown at even heat
