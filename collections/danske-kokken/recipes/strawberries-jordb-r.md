## Ingredients

- berries
- 1 pound powdered sugar per pound of berries
- brandy

## Directions

1. Clean the berries and for each pound use 1 pound powdered sugar, which is sprinkled in layers between the berries the day before preserving.
2. The next day, place the berries over the fire.
3. When the sugar is well dissolved, carefully pour the berries into a colander and cook the drained syrup for 1/2 hour while skimming frequently.
4. Then add the berries to the syrup and cook the whole for 8 minutes, then remove the berries again and cook the syrup alone for 1 hour more while skimming.
5. Now return the berries to the syrup and stir gently with 4 tablespoons of brandy.
6. Pour everything into a large jar and let stand until the next day, then divide into smaller glasses.
