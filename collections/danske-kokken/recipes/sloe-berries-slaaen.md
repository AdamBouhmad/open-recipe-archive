## Ingredients

- Sloe berries
- Water

## Directions

1. The berries must not be picked before they have been touched by frost
2. Fill them into a barrel
3. Pour water over them and insert the bung
4. During the first month, roll the barrel daily across the floor
5. As the juice is used, more water can be added
