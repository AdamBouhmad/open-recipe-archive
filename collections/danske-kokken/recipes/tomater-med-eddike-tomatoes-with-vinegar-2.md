## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg soft sugar
- 1 kg tomatoes

## Directions

1. Large partly ripe tomatoes are dried in a cloth and cut in half
2. 1/2 kg soft sugar is dipped directly into vinegar and put in pot to cook to syrup
3. Then as many tomatoes as can lie side by side without lying on top of each other are added
4. Shortly after the tomatoes are put in the syrup and begin to cook the skin bursts and they are then removed immediately and placed in glasses with a few pieces of whole cinnamon and some whole cloves
5. The juice is mixed with the remaining 1/4 kg sugar and reduced until fairly thick
