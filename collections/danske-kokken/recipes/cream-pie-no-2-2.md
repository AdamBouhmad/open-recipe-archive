## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 0.5 teaspoon soda
- Beaten egg whites
- 1 pint milk
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- A little cold milk
- 3 eggs
- A little vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks thick, then stir in 1 cup sugar and then 1 cup flour sifted together with 1 teaspoon cream of tartar and 0.5 teaspoon soda.
2. When stirred together, fold in the beaten egg whites.
3. Divide into four ordinary pie plates and bake light brown. This will be sufficient for 2 pies.
4. For the cream, use 1 pint milk, 1 tablespoon corn starch dissolved in a little cold milk, 3 eggs, and a little vanilla.
5. When cold, divide into two parts and spread between two layers of cake.
