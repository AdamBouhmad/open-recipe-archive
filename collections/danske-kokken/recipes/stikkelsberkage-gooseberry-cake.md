## Ingredients

- 1 quart unripe gooseberries
- 6 egg yolks
- cinnamon
- powdered sugar
- old wheat bread
- 2 small cups of grated bread
- 3 tablespoons butter
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Pick, rinse, and place unripe gooseberries in the oven until they turn yellow, or alternatively simmer in water and strain through a colander until dry
2. Stir 6 egg yolks well with a little ground cinnamon and powdered sugar to taste
3. Grate old wheat bread and use 2 small cups full, baking it in 3 tablespoons butter until slightly browned
4. Stir the bread mixture into the eggs along with the gooseberries and whipped egg whites
5. Bake for one hour
6. Serves 8 persons
