## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chopped parsley
- Mustard (stirred smooth)
- Cooked, chopped, or raw grated onions
- Strong broth

## Directions

1. Press the hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve
2. Stir with a raw egg yolk
3. Add oil, then vinegar
4. Season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
5. Add stirred smooth mustard
6. Add cooked, chopped, or raw grated onions
7. Add strong broth until the sauce reaches the desired consistency
