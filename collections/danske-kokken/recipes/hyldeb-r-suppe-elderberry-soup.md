## Ingredients

- 1 kg elderberries
- 4.5 L water
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- rhubarb juice, currant juice, or lemon juice
- vinegar
- rhubarb stalks

## Directions

1. Remove elderberries from stems, rinse and cook for half an hour in water with lemon peel and cinnamon
2. Strain the soup
3. Add acidity with rhubarb juice, currant juice, lemon juice, or vinegar
4. Can cook rhubarb stalks together with berries in autumn
