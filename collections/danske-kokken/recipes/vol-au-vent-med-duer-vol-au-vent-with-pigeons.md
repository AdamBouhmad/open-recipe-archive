## Ingredients

- Roasted pigeons
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock
- Pickled pearl onions
- Shallots
- Pickles
- Olives
- Champignons

## Directions

1. Place roasted pigeons, portioned into 2 or 4 pieces, in the vol au vent.
2. Make a brown sauce from butter, flour, and stock.
3. Pickled pearl onions, shallots, pickles, olives, or champignons may be added to the sauce.
4. Pour the sauce over the pigeons, arrange them in the vol au vent, and serve as hot as possible.
