## Ingredients

- cold roast meat, sliced thin
- a handful chopped parsley
- 1 root onion cut into pieces
- ½ pint bouillon
- a piece of butter
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 glass port wine or sherry
- 1 spoonful vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices and cook it gently for a quarter hour together with a handful chopped parsley, a root onion cut into pieces, ½ pint bouillon, a piece of butter, salt and pepper
2. Quickly stir together 2 egg yolks, 1 glass port wine or sherry, and 1 spoonful vinegar
3. Pour this mixture into the meat
