## Ingredients

- large beautiful celery knobs
- salt
- water
- meat forcemeat
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- mace
- white wine
- meat balls

## Directions

1. Clean and peel large beautiful celery knobs and place them in salted water as you work to prevent them from turning black
2. Cut off a lid and hollow out the celery
3. Fill with meat forcemeat
4. Cook very little in water or soup until tender, then remove them
5. Melt butter and cook flour in it to make a white sauce, thin with bouillon and some of the water in which the celery was cooked
6. Add a little ground mace, salt and white wine
7. Warm and serve the celery in this sauce
8. Alternatively, cut the celery into fairly large pieces, cook in soup stock and serve in the above sauce together with fairly large meat balls that have been boiled in water
