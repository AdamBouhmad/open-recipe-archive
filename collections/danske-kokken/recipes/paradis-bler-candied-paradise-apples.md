## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl water
- 1/2 kg paradise apples

## Directions

1. Cut off half of the stems from the paradise apples.
2. Place over heat in cold water and bring to a boil.
3. Remove and place in cold water.
4. Boil sugar and water to a syrup in which the apples are cooked until tender enough to pierce easily with a knitting needle.
5. Remove the apples and place in a jar, and cook the syrup until smooth.
6. Pour the boiling syrup over them and seal while the apples are still hot.
