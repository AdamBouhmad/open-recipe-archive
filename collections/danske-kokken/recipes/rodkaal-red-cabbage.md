## Ingredients

- Red cabbage
- Vinegar
- Butter or fat
- Sugar
- Cherry juice or currant juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and cores from the red cabbage, which is cut into fine strips and placed on the heat with some vinegar, which makes it red, and cook with a lid on over even heat. If the cabbage becomes dry, add more vinegar or soup, but not more than makes the cabbage moist. Before it is tender, add butter or fat from freshly made soup, and finally sugar, a little cherry juice or currant juice and salt.
