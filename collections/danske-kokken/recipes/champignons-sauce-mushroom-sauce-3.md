## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Meat or fish stock
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sherry or Graves wine
- Lemon juice
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Bake butter and flour as in the preceding sauce and thin with meat or fish stock, depending on what the sauce is to be served with
2. Bind with egg yolks and season with salt, pepper, sherry or Graves wine, and lemon juice if using mushrooms that are not already sour
3. Give the mushrooms a boil in the sauce
4. Mushrooms kept in butter should be heated so the butter can melt; use it to bake the flour in
