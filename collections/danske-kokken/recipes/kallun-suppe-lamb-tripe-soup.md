## Ingredients

- 1.5 times lamb tripe
- 1/2 bushel shelled peas
- 6 L (6 pots) water
- salt
- 100 g (20 skein) butter or vegetable margarine
- 100 g (20 skein) flour
- 20 new carrots, cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Kallun soup is a summer dish which requires fresh vegetables to achieve the right flavor.
2. Cleaning tripe is work that must be done with great care and requires considerable time.
3. Split the bones before putting them in salted water.
4. Scrape the tripe at least 3-4 times, pouring boiling water over it in between.
5. Scrape and clean the fat intestines on the outside, then split and clean inside.
6. Cut these together with the tripe into small, square pieces.
7. Place the bones and cut tripe and fat intestines in a pot in 6 L (6 pots) salted water.
8. When the soup boils, skim well before adding vegetables, then simmer gently for about 3 hours under a closed lid or until the tripe is nearly tender; then add the shelled peas and about 20 cut new carrots and cook until tender.
9. In a pot, bake 100 g (20 skein) butter or vegetable margarine and 100 g (20 skein) flour and thin with some of the soup until it becomes a thick liaison, which is poured into the tripe soup.
10. Season with salt and ground pepper.
