## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) Pillsbury flour
- 175 grams (35 quintal) margarine
- 75 grams (15 quintal) icing sugar
- a little cardamom
- a few drops of orange essence
- 2 teaspoons baking powder, mixed in 1/4 dl (1/4 pint) milk

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Roll out thinly
3. Cut with a cake cutter into long, narrow cakes
4. Brush with egg
5. Can be sprinkled either with caraway seeds or with chopped almonds
6. Bake in a well-heated oven
