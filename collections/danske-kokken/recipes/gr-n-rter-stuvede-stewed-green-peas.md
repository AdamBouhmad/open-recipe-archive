## Ingredients

- shelled green peas
- water
- butter
- sugar
- flour
- parsley
- salt
- young carrots (optional)

## Directions

1. The shelled green peas, which should not be too large, are cooked tender in water with generous butter and a little sugar
2. Add the peas only when the water boils
3. When tender, thicken with a little flour, chopped parsley and salt
4. A good flavor and economy with sugar is gained by mixing the peas with some slices of young carrots
