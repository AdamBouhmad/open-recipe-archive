## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Gravy from the game
- Cream or unsweetened condensed milk
- Cold butter
- Salt
- Coloring
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Currant jelly

## Directions

1. Brown butter and bake just a little flour in it, thin with the gravy from the game to make a thin sauce
2. Add good cream or unsweetened condensed milk and cook well
3. Finally stir in a slice of cold butter together with salt, coloring and Maggi bouillon cubes if needed
4. Currant jelly stirred into the sauce gives an excellent taste
