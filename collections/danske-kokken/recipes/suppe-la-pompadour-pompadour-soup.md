## Ingredients

- 1 Celery
- 2 Parsley roots
- 2 Root leeks
- 4 Carrots
- Butter
- 4 L (4 Pots) good brown spare soup
- Toasted bread or small water pastries

## Directions

1. Cut the celery, parsley roots, root leeks and carrots into small cubes
2. Fry in a very little butter
3. Pour 4 L (4 Pots) good brown spare soup over the vegetables
4. Boil for a quarter hour
5. Serve with toasted bread or small water pastries
