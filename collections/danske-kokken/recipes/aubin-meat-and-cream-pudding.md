## Ingredients

- 0.5 lbs. cooked meat
- some onions
- 0.5 lbs. butter
- 0.5 lbs. flour
- 3 pints cooked milk
- 15 egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat very finely with some onions.
2. Bake it with 0.5 lbs. butter, 0.5 lbs. flour and 3 pints cooked milk.
3. When baked, pour it into a dish, and when it has cooled, stir 15 egg yolks into it.
4. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in last.
5. Put the mixture in a pudding mold greased with butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs and cook for 1.5 hours.
6. Serve with a strong sauce of meat broth, to which a very little Sherry can be added.
