## Ingredients

- Salt cod, soaked and cooked
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 tablespoons melted butter, dissolved in flour
- Milk
- 1/2 pound melted butter
- 1 cup cleaned currants
- Egg whites, stiffly beaten
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the salt cod the evening before use, or at least in the morning
2. Cook the cod and chop it finely
3. For a deep plate full of chopped fish, use 8 egg yolks
4. Dissolve 8 tablespoons in flour, let it sit covered for some time, then mix with the egg yolks and enough milk to achieve a consistency like ordinary pancake batter
5. Add 1/2 pound melted butter (salt held back)
6. Add 1 cup of well-cleaned currants
7. Add the fish and finally the stiffly beaten egg whites
8. Pour immediately into a buttered mold and cook for 2 hours
9. Alternatively, bake in the oven in an earthenware dish, ready in 3 quarters of an hour
10. Serve with melted or stirred butter with pepper
