## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) wild or garden raspberries
- 1 bottle white rum
- 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar
- 1/4 l (1 pægl) water

## Directions

1. Pour 1 bottle of white rum over 1 kg (2 S) raspberries and let stand well covered for 8 days.
2. Boil 1/2 kg (1 S) powdered sugar and 1/4 l (1 pægl) water to make a syrup.
3. Pour the hot syrup into the strained rum.
4. Pour the liqueur into dry bottles, cork them, and seal with gypsum.
