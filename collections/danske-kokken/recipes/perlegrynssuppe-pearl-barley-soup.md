## Ingredients

- 1/4 measure pearl barley
- 4 pots water
- cinnamon stick
- cloves
- prunes
- fruit juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the pearl barley in cold water the day before use.
2. Boil 4 pots of water and add the drained grains along with a piece of cinnamon and some cloves.
3. Cook covered over even heat.
4. When the grains are almost tender, add rinsed prunes to the soup.
5. When ready to serve, season with fruit juice and sugar to taste.
