## Ingredients

- Pears
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. While 1 pint water with 0.5 pound sugar for each quart of fruit is heating, peel pears and place in cold water so they do not discolour
2. When the solution has come to a strong boil, add pears carefully so they do not fall apart; they cook until they can be easily pierced with a fork, whereupon they together with the solution, while it is still boiling, are filled into jars which are immediately screwed shut
3. Coarse and hard pears must first be cooked more in water and then placed hot in the heated solution, in which they cook 10 minutes before being filled into jars
4. Apples are treated the same way
