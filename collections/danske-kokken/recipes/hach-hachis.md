## Ingredients

- Cooked beef
- Onions
- Ground pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- Wheat flour
- Anchovies
- Beef broth
- Egg yolk
- Potatoes
- Eggs
- Rum or cognac

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked beef finely with onions, ground pepper, and salt to taste.
2. For each pound of beef, brown half a quarter pound of butter with 2 grated onions.
3. Finely chop the beef of 2 anchovies.
4. Brown the mixture with a little salt, ground pepper, and 2 teaspoons of wheat flour until it leaves the spoon.
5. When it boils, add one gill of beef broth for each pound of meat, egg yolk, and the chopped beef.
6. The hachis should be the consistency of a thin porridge.
7. Cook for approximately a quarter hour.
8. Just before serving, add a little rum or cognac.
9. Serve garnished alternately with browned potatoes and hard-boiled eggs cut lengthwise.
