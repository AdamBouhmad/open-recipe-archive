## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head
- Small fish balls
- Eggs
- Chestnuts
- Truffles
- Champignons
- Olives

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes of approximately 3 centimeters (1 inch) in size.
2. Prepare the ragout according to Nr. 9.
3. Serve the ragout in vol au vent.
4. Garnish with very small fish balls arranged in a star pattern on top, or with eggs, chestnuts, truffles, champignons, or olives.
5. Alternatively, use leftovers of goose and duck roasts, boiled or roasted turkey, pigeons, or any kind of game for the ragout filling.
