## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Lemon pieces
- Salt
- Endive salad, parsley or maidenhair fern for garnish

## Directions

1. Calculate six oysters per person for dinner service
2. Serve either after the soup or begin the meal with them
3. Arrange in gratin shells in their juice
4. Place the shell on a medium or dessert plate on which a small napkin has been laid
5. Garnish with lemon pieces and a salt cellar
6. Decorate with endive salad, parsley or maidenhair fern
