## Ingredients

- 2 Teaspoons Soda
- 1 Dessertspoon Ginger
- 2 Cups Molasses
- 4 well-beaten Eggs
- 1 Cup melted Butter
- 1 Cup sour Milk with 1 Teaspoon Soda dissolved in it
- Flour

## Directions

1. Sift the soda and ginger into the molasses
2. Stir for a long time
3. Add the well-beaten eggs
4. Add the melted butter
5. Add the sour milk with dissolved soda
6. Mix all together
7. Add flour until the consistency is like pound cake
8. Bake in 2 cakes
