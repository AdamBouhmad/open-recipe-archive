## Ingredients

- Quinces with peelings
- One pound peels
- One pint water
- One pound quinces (prepared)
- One pound white or brown sugar

## Directions

1. Dry the quinces, remove the peelings, and weigh them.
2. For 1 pound peelings, take 1 pint water; cook very evenly for one hour, then strain.
3. Cut the quinces into 4 to 8 pieces, remove the seed cavities and all hard matter, and weigh.
4. For 1 pound quinces, take 1 pound white or brown sugar and place in the water that was strained from the peelings.
5. When melted, add the quinces and cook covered over a good, even heat, not flame, stirring regularly until tender.
6. This may take a couple of hours.
7. By this even cooking under closed lid, the quinces will turn red; if not, a little cherry juice may be added.
8. Pour into a vessel and tie as usual.
