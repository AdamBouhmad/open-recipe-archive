## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple in syrup
- 10 egg yolks
- 175 grams (35 quintals) sugar
- 18 sheets isinglass
- 1¼ dl (½ pægel) boiling water
- juice of 2 lemons
- juice from the canned pineapple
- 10 stiffly beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks well with sugar.
2. Dissolve isinglass in boiling water and add the juice of 2 lemons as well as the juice from the canned pineapple.
3. Cut the pineapple into small cubes and stir into the eggs along with the isinglass solution and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the mousse into a wetted mold, which is turned out at serving and decorated with whipped cream.
