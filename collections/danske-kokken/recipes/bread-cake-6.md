## Ingredients

- eggs
- cream
- lemon rind
- salt
- French bread
- milk
- butter
- white sugar

## Directions

1. For each egg, take a spoonful of cream
2. Whip them together and add grated lemon rind and salt
3. Dip slices of French bread in milk and fry in butter
4. When fried on both sides, pour the batter over and bake over good heat, but not over a flame from fire, until it shines on top
5. It does not need to be turned, and is arranged with white sugar on top
