## Ingredients

- Pear quinces
- 1 Pd. Melis per 1 Pd. quinces

## Directions

1. Boil the quinces in water
2. Place in a colander, peel, and cut into 2 or 4 pieces lengthwise
3. Dissolve the Melis in water and boil to a smooth syrup
4. Boil the quinces in this syrup until tender
5. Remove the quinces and place in preserve jars
6. Continue boiling the syrup with constant skimming until smooth, then pour over the quinces
