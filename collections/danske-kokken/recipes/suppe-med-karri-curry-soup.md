## Ingredients

- nearly 1 pound beef, veal, or lamb meat, or about double that amount of tender veal meat per portion
- rice
- cold water
- salt
- soup vegetables
- curry powder to taste
- sour onion sauce

## Directions

1. This soup is primarily cooked with veal or lamb meat.
2. The treatment is otherwise quite as described in No. 5, except that the meat barely tolerates cooking as long, and instead of dumplings, rice is used.
3. Soak the rice in cold water, and renew this repeatedly while stirring well with your hands.
4. When all impurities have been removed in this way, place the rice over heat in cold water, bring to boil and skim repeatedly.
5. Then pour it into a colander and rinse with cold water.
6. Now pour the soup onto the rice, which is given a boil until it is tender, but still whole.
7. Add curry powder to taste.
8. Serve the soup first and then the soup meat, to which sour onion sauce can be served.
