## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl water
- 1/2 kg peeled rhubarb stalks

## Directions

1. From the well-dried stalks remove the skin and cut the stalks into pieces the length of a thumb
2. Cook sugar and water to a thin syrup and cook the stalks in it until soft but completely whole
3. If too many stalks are put in the pot at once it is difficult to prevent them from breaking up
4. Remove with slotted spoon and place in jar
5. Cook the syrup afterward until very thick then pour it hot over them
6. Seal the jar while the preserve is boiling hot
7. 1/2 teaspoon salicylic acid to 3 kg stalks is stirred into the warm juice
