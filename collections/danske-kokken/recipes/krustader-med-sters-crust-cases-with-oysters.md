## Ingredients

- 1 to 2 oysters per crust case
- oyster liquor from the shells
- oyster beards
- fish stock
- lemon juice
- bouillon or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
- butter
- flour
- Graves wine
- ground white pepper
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Open the oysters; pour the water in the shells into a saucepan together with the removed beards and give it a boil in fish stock with a few drops of lemon juice, in bouillon, or water with Maggi bouillon cubes added.
2. Strain the stock and use it for the sauce, which is made of butter and flour and thinned to an even sauce.
3. Add 1 glass of Graves wine, ground white pepper, and any liquid that may still be with the oysters; bind it with a couple of egg yolks.
4. Pour boiling water over the oysters and place them in the warm sauce.
5. Arrange as soon as possible.
