## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 herrings
- 8 ounces salt

## Directions

1. Place 3 quarts water over the heat with the salt. When it boils, add the herrings and simmer for a quarter hour, then let them drain.
2. They should be served and eaten immediately, otherwise they become heavy and difficult to digest.
