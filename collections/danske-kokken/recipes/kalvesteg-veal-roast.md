## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Water

## Directions

1. The veal leg is used for this roast. A typical weight for a leg is about 10 kg, and it can be roasted whole or divided.
2. The piece with the shank is the most advantageous, as it includes the large, tender piece of meat that is excellent both for the main course and later for use as cold cuts.
3. Veal can be stored for a few days in buttermilk or skim milk, which must be renewed every day or every other day, though it is best to prepare it without this preservation method.
4. When ready to use, rinse the meat well, pour boiling water over it, remove the skins, and lard it.
