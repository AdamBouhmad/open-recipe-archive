## Ingredients

- 8 medium, juicy apples
- 2 pots water
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the apples and cut them into four pieces
2. Boil them with 2 pots of water until they are very soft
3. Strain the juice from the pulp
4. Sweeten with powdered sugar
5. Serve cold
