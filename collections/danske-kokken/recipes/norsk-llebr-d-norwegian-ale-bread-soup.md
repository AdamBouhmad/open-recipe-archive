## Ingredients

- 2 L (2 Potter) pale ale
- 2 L (2 Potter) milk
- a good tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Stir a good tablespoon of flour in some of the cold milk, which is stirred in when the milk boils.
2. The ale is cooked separately, sweetened and poured simultaneously with the milk into a tureen, while whisking vigorously with a whisk.
3. It is better thickened with Maizena.
4. Serve rye bread cubes baked in butter and sugar with it.
