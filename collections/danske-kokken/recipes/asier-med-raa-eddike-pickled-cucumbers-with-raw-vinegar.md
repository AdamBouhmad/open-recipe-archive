## Ingredients

- About 20 peeled, split and hollowed cucumbers
- Spices as in the previous recipe
- A couple handfuls of salt
- Raw vinegar to cover

## Directions

1. Arrange the cucumbers in layers in a crock with spices as in the previous recipe and a couple handfuls of salt
2. Pour enough raw vinegar to cover the cucumbers
3. Place a light press on them
4. Bind the crock
