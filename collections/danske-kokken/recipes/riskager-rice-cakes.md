## Ingredients

- ½ pound Caroline rice
- sweet cream
- ¼ pound butter
- 4 eggs
- 12 quintal finely ground powdered sugar
- fine grated bread for dusting

## Directions

1. Clean the ½ pound Caroline rice as previously mentioned (No. 6)
2. Cook it soft in sweet cream until it becomes like a thick porridge, then pour into a dish
3. Stir ¼ pound butter white, adding 4 eggs one at a time
4. Add 12 quintal finely ground powdered sugar
5. When well stirred, mix with the cooked rice and stir well
6. Grease small forms with butter and sprinkle with fine grated bread
7. Fill with the batter
8. Bake in a moderately warm oven
