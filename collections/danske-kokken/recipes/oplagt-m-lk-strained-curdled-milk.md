## Ingredients

- 1 L (1 Pot) sweet Milk per person
- 1 1/4 Dl (3/10 Pægl) boiling Water per Liter (Pot) of Milk
- Cream
- Sweet Cream
- fresh Strawberries or Raspberries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pour the milk into a vessel, and to make it curdle more quickly, pour 1 1/4 Dl (3/10 Pægl) boiling water into each Liter (Pot) of milk
2. Usually about 1.5 hours is required for this
3. When it is thick, skim the cream off first, and take slices of the milk with a spoon and place in a sieve, in which a piece of clear cloth is spread
4. Thus leave the sieve for 4 to 5 hours, so that the whey can drain well; thereafter turn the strained curdled milk from the cloth over onto a dish
5. Whip the skimmed cream and pour it over
6. Add sweet cream, fresh strawberries or raspberries and sugar
