## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.25 pound butter
- 3 tablespoons cream
- 0.5 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Divide the sugar in two parts, stir half into the butter and the other half into the egg yolks
2. Mix in the cream, half a teaspoon vanilla, and fold in the beaten egg whites
3. This mixture is enough for 2 pies
4. Line the molds preferably with fine dough, then pour in the mixture and bake in a not too hot oven
