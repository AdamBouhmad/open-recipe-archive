## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds peeled and cut sour apples
- 1 pint water
- 1/2 ounce red gelatine
- 1/2 pint white wine
- rind of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the 1 1/2 pounds of peeled and cut sour apples very soft in an enameled pot with 1 pint of water.
2. Then press them through a sieve and blend while warm with 1/2 ounce of red gelatine dissolved in 1/2 pint of white wine.
3. Add the grated peel of 1/2 and the juice of 2 lemons, 1 pound of powdered sugar, and some cinnamon.
4. Keep the whole warm while stirring constantly and pour it into a mold first rinsed with salad oil.
5. When completely cold, turn it out onto a chilled dish and vanilla sauce can be served with it.
