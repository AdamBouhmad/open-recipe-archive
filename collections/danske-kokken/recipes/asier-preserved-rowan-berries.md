## Ingredients

- Rowan berries
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Spices (as in recipe Nr. 232)

## Directions

1. Peel and split the rowan berries, and scrape out the seeds with a silver spoon.
2. They can be salted in the autumn and left with a light press on top.
3. As needed during winter, prepare a smaller portion at a time.
4. Cook the spices (as in recipe Nr. 232) in vinegar, then add the well-dried rowan berries briefly and remove them immediately.
5. Bring the vinegar to a boil before pouring it over.
6. Cover the jar with a cloth and bind it with parchment paper the next day.
7. This tastes excellent as freshly preserved rowan berries during winter.
