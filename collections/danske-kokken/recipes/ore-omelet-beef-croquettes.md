## Ingredients

- 4 pounds round beef
- 6 eggs
- Five or six soda crackers
- Butter
- Chopped celery
- Black pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scrape and mince 4 pounds round beef finely, beat 6 eggs, crush five or six soda crackers finely, add a little butter, a little chopped celery, pepper and salt, mix all this together
2. Form two small loaves, roll them in crackers and bake one hour
3. When cold, slice them into pieces
