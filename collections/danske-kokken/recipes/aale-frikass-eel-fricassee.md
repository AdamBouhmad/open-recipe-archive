## Ingredients

- Skinned, cleaned eel, cut into pieces
- Water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned eel pieces in boiling salted water with a bunch of parsley, using only as much water as necessary to make a stronger sauce
2. Strain the cooking liquid and keep the eel warm
3. Make a roux with butter and flour, then add the fish stock to create a smooth sauce
4. Thicken the sauce with a couple of egg yolks and add chopped parsley
5. Pour the sauce over the eel when serving
6. Serve with boiled potatoes
