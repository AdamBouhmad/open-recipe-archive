## Ingredients

- English salsify roots
- Water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. The roots are about the thickness and length of a small finger and very ridged
2. Scrub the thin skin off as much as possible with a brush before cooking
3. Cook in salted water
4. Arrange as steaming hot as possible, laid in a folded napkin
5. Serve with melted butter
