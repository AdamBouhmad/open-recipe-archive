## Ingredients

- 2 pints cherry juice
- 2 pints raspberry juice
- 1 pint water, or more if the juice is very thick
- 12 quintal dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil the cherry juice, raspberry juice, and water together with the dissolved isinglass in an enameled pot
2. Strain through a new fine sieve
3. Set in a mold on ice to freeze for 2–3 hours
