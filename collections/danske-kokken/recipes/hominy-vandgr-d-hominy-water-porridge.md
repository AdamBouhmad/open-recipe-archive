## Ingredients

- ½ kg hominy grains
- 4 L water
- salt
- butter
- sugar
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. For ½ kg grains, use 2 L cold or lukewarm water; let them stand until the next day.
2. Pour everything into an enameled pot with approximately 2 L boiling water.
3. Cook the porridge for 1 hour over the heat and then place it in a hay box, where it stands for about 4 hours.
4. All grain types must be well cooked.
5. Salt the porridge and serve with butter, sugar and sweet milk.
6. Hominy is American corn grains, very nourishing and cheap.
