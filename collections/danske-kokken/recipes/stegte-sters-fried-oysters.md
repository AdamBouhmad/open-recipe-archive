## Ingredients

- Large oysters
- Clean cloth
- Butter
- Beaten egg
- Bread crumbs or crackers
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Use the largest and best oysters.
2. Place them on a clean cloth and press another cloth over them to remove all moisture.
3. Melt enough butter in a frying pan to nearly cover the oysters.
4. Dip the oysters first in beaten egg and then roll them in bread crumbs or crushed crackers.
5. Fry them light brown.
6. Serve dry on a hot dish with lemon slices around them.
