## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Set a pot over the fire with plenty of water and salt.
2. When the water is boiling, add the live lobsters with their claws tied together, after rinsing them in cold water.
3. Boil over strong heat until the legs can be removed. Do not cook longer or the meat will become stringy.
4. Skim the water during cooking.
5. Remove the pot from the fire and let the lobsters cool until half-cold.
6. Remove the lobsters and crack the back and tail lengthwise and the claws crosswise.
7. Serve cold with chopped parsley, pepper, vinegar and fine oil, along with fine green salad leaves.
