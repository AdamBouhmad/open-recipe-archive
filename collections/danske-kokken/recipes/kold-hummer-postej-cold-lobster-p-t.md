## Ingredients

- Butter dough
- Fish forcemeat
- Cooked lobster pieces
- Lobster butter
- Lobster sauce

## Directions

1. Line a mold with butter dough
2. Place fish forcemeat in the bottom and press pieces of cooked lobster into it
3. Alternate layers of forcemeat mixed with lobster butter and lobster meat until the mold is nearly full
4. Top with a layer of butter dough, rolled out and pressed firmly at the edges
5. Place in a warm oven and bake for 1.5 hours
6. When the pâté is cold, turn it out onto a serving dish
7. Slice and serve with hot lobster sauce
