## Ingredients

- 5 bottles pale ale
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) fine sago grains
- lemon slices
- whole cinnamon
- 6 egg yolks
- 375 Gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) fine sago grains into the boiling ale and cook together with lemon slices and whole cinnamon until the grains are clear.
2. Beat 6 egg yolks white with 375 Gr (1/4 S) powdered sugar.
3. Pour the sago ale into the eggs, stirring well with a whisk.
