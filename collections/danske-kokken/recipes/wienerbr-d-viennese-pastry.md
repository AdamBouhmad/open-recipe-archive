## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg flour
- 75 gr powdered sugar
- 1/2 L lukewarm milk
- 50 gr yeast
- salt
- 1/4 kg margarine
- ground cardamom
- egg
- chopped almonds
- powdered sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and powdered sugar with the lukewarm milk and yeast mixed with a little salt.
2. Roll out the dough on a board and cover with margarine distributed in lumps, sprinkle with ground cardamom and fold together as for butter dough (see No. 22), repeating 3 to 4 times.
3. Set aside in a cool place to become firmer and easier to handle, then roll out to 1/4 inch thickness.
4. Cut into strips 1 inch wide.
5. Twist and shape as rings, spirals, or braids, or cut the dough into squares.
6. Place on baking sheets, brush with egg, sprinkle with chopped almonds and set to rise.
7. As soon as sufficiently risen, sprinkle with plenty of powdered sugar and bake in a very hot oven.
8. The finished, lukewarm bread can be glazed.
