## Ingredients

- 100 Gr (20 Kvint) yeast
- a handful of kitchen salt
- 1 Kg (2 S) rye sieve flour
- 1 Kg (2 S) 'Best Bakery' flour
- 1 1/2 L (1 1/2 Pot) semi-skimmed milk
- optional: a handful of caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Mix yeast with a handful of kitchen salt
2. Add rye sieve flour, 'Best Bakery' flour and semi-skimmed milk
3. The dough can be kneaded the evening before and set on the kitchen table overnight
4. The next morning, knead it with a little flour not counted in the weight
5. Form into 1 larger and 2-3 smaller loaves, which must rise again
6. Brush with milk and bake 1/4 hour in a well-heated oven
7. A handful of caraway seeds can be kneaded into the dough
