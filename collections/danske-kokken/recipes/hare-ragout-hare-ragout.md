## Ingredients

- 1 roasted hare or leftovers of one
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Truffles, mushrooms, olives, or chestnuts (optional)
- Butter pastry strips

## Directions

1. Cut up the roasted hare or the leftovers, and boil the bones in milk to make stock for the sauce.
2. Brown butter and flour and thin with the strained stock to make a smooth sauce in which to warm the meat.
3. The sauce can be garnished with truffles, mushrooms, olives or chestnuts and butter pastry strips.
