## Ingredients

- 1 liter (1 pint) vinegar
- 1/4 kg (1/2 pound) berries
- 1/2 kg (1 pound) powdered sugar per liter of juice

## Directions

1. Place the berries in a crock and cover with vinegar
2. Tie the crock well and let stand for 3 to 4 days in a cold place
3. Pour the berries through a jelly cloth
4. Let the juice that runs through stand until the next day
5. Measure it and boil for 10 to 12 minutes with 1/2 kg powdered sugar per liter of juice
6. Skim carefully during cooking
7. Pour into bottles, cork and seal with plaster
