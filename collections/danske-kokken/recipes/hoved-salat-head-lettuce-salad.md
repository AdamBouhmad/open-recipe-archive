## Ingredients

- 5 heads of lettuce
- 4 spoonfuls oil
- 2 spoonfuls vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pour the oil, vinegar, salt and pepper mixture over the rinsed and prepared salad.
2. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs, either cut into quarters or sliced.
