## Ingredients

- Short, thick, heavy green cucumbers
- Salt
- 5 liters (5 pots) vinegar
- 1/2 kg (1 skålpund) powdered sugar (Melis)
- 50 grams (10 quintals) long pepper
- 50 grams (10 quintals) whole white pepper
- 50 grams (10 quintals) whole yellow mustard seeds
- 6 pods Spanish pepper
- A little dill
- A couple dozen small peeled shallots

## Directions

1. Choose short, thick, heavy green cucumbers for pickling
2. Peel them, split lengthwise, and scrape out the seeds with a silver spoon
3. Sprinkle with salt and set aside until the next day
4. Remove from the brine, drain well, and dry in cloth
5. Heat the vinegar with the powdered sugar, long pepper, whole white pepper, whole yellow mustard seeds (wrapped in a clear piece of cloth), Spanish pepper pods, dill, and peeled shallots
6. Boil all of this in the vinegar, then remove after 2 minutes before the cucumbers are added
7. Arrange the cucumbers in a crock and after all are arranged, bring the vinegar to a boil before pouring it over the cucumbers
8. Place the mustard seeds on top of the crock, cover with cloth, and the next day bind with parchment paper
9. Cucumbers pickled this way become hard, crisp, and very durable
