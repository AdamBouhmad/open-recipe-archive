## Ingredients

- Large potatoes
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Scoop small, round balls from large potatoes using a potato scoop.
2. Boil them tender in salted water.
3. Drain the water and steam them dry.
4. Layer a good slice of butter rolled in finely chopped parsley between them.
5. Swing the potatoes several times in the pan without breaking them.
6. Once the butter is melted, quickly arrange them in a warm serving dish.
7. Serve with cooked fish dishes.
