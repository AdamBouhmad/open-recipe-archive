## Ingredients

- 1 Pd. cleaned, ripe cherries
- 4 Pd. sugar
- 3/4 Pægl vinegar
- whole cinnamon
- whole cloves

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar with the vinegar, skimming the vinegar well during cooking
2. Add the cherries and boil until they are no longer sour at the pits
3. Remove the cherries and place in a colander so the liquid drains from them
4. Boil the syrup for 8 minutes until it is clear
5. Return the cherries to the syrup along with some whole cinnamon and boil together
6. Pour into preserve jars and add a little cognac to each
7. If the syrup becomes too thin after some time, boil it again with more sugar
