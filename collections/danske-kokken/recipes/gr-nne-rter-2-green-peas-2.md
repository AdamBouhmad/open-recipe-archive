## Ingredients

- Peas
- Natron (baking soda)
- A little salt
- Water where peas were cooked
- Cold water
- A small amount of powdered sugar (Melis)

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and boil them until almost tender in water with natron and a little salt
2. Drain the water from them and pour cold water over them
3. Fill the peas into jars
4. Take the water in which the peas were boiled, add a small amount of powdered sugar, and pour it over the peas
5. Close and boil the jars
6. Cool as usual
7. These peas must also be boiled several times as described in recipe 228
