## Ingredients

- A couple of handfuls of sorrel
- A handful of chervil
- The same amount of lettuce
- A little parsley
- 1/4 pound butter
- 5 pots chicken broth
- Small chicken dumplings or toasted French bread

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and finely chop the sorrel, chervil, lettuce, and parsley
2. Place over heat with 1/4 pound butter and braise for a quarter hour
3. Add 5 pots chicken broth and cook for 3/4 hour
4. Serve with small chicken dumplings or with toasted French bread
