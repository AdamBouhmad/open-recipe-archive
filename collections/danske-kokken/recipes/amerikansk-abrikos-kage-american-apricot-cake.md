## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- lemon or orange essence
- 75 grams flour
- egg whites, beaten stiff
- 500 milliliters semi-skimmed milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 100 grams powdered sugar
- 4 sheets isinglass
- 125 grams cold butter
- 3 egg whites, beaten stiff
- 125 grams dried apricots

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks well with powdered sugar, cream of tartar, lemon or orange essence, and flour
2. Finally add the beaten egg whites
3. Divide the dough into 2 parts and bake in a greased layer cake form
4. Bake only a few minutes until set, then immediately remove from the form and place on the dish on which it will be served
5. Boil the semi-skimmed milk and add 3 egg yolks beaten well with powdered sugar
6. Bring the cream to a boil, remove from heat, and add the isinglass sheets rinsed in cold water to melt
7. Blend cold beaten butter into the cooled cream, then the 3 beaten egg whites, and use this to coat the cakes
8. Soak dried apricots and cook them in compote, sweeten well, and only when cold, coat the cakes with them
9. Place the half fruits with the rounded side up
10. The cakes can be baked the day before, but the fruit must only be added the day the cake is served
