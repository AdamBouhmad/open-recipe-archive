## Ingredients

- Thrushes or small birds
- Bacon slices, thin
- Butter
- Salt
- Bouillon or cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the birds and remove the entrails.
2. Cover them with thin slices of bacon and roast them in butter that has first been browned in a casserole.
3. After adding a little salt, roast them over brisk heat for about 10 minutes, basting and dripping with bouillon or cream.
4. Stir the sauce up with sweet cream and a piece of butter and strain it.
5. Smaller birds are roasted in a similar manner.
