## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg (3 pounds) stone sugar
- 3/4 dl (3/4 cup) water for 1/2 kg (1 pound) fruit juice
- tart apples
- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) water to each 1/2 kg (1 pound) apples

## Directions

1. Use tart apples; do not peel them as the skin especially contains the jelly substance.
2. Cut them into 4 to 6 pieces and remove the core, blossom, stem, and bruised spots.
3. Rinse the fruit and cook completely soft in 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) water to each 1/2 kg (1 pound) apples.
4. Pour into a jelly cloth where it remains overnight.
5. The next day weigh the clear juice.
6. Cook sugar and water as in recipe 93 and, after it is again dry, pour the juice into it, cook and skim for 10 minutes.
7. Pour the jelly into cups and bind when it has begun to thicken.
