## Ingredients

- Roasted beef fillet
- Stuffed cucumbers
- Fresh tomatoes
- Salad or parsley for garnish
- Gravy from the fillet
- Gravy from the cucumbers

## Directions

1. Around the roasted fillet, arrange slices of stuffed cucumbers alternating with slices of fresh tomatoes.
2. Make a smooth, brown gravy sauce from the gravy of the fillet and the gravy from the cucumbers.
3. Garnish the dish with salad or parsley.
