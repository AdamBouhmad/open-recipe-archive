## Ingredients

- Skinned and cleaned eel, cut into 5-8 centimeter pieces
- Salt
- Water
- Parsley
- Horseradish, grated
- Butter, melted
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Place the prepared eel pieces in boiling salted water with a good bunch of parsley
2. When cooked, arrange on a serving platter garnished with grated horseradish sprinkled with chopped parsley
3. Serve with melted butter and boiled potatoes
