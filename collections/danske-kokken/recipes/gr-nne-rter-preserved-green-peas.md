## Ingredients

- Green peas
- Salt

## Directions

1. Coarse peas are generally used for salting.
2. As they are shelled, place them in layers with salt in jars, and as mentioned for beans, place a piece of linen cloth on top, then a perforated wooden bottom, and finally a light press.
3. The brine should preferably cover the peas.
4. Rinse before cooking in water with a little sugar.
5. Suitable for example in Pea Purée (see the Cookbook).
