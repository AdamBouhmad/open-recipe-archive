## Ingredients

- Butter dough
- Peas
- Various fillings as specified

## Directions

1. Roll out the butter dough very thinly
2. Line small tartlet forms with the dough
3. Fill with peas
4. Bake until golden
5. When the tartlets are baked and cooled, fill with one of the following: Chocolate Fromage decorated with piped whipped cream, Nougat Cream served when set with pale red whipped cream roses around the edge, Rum Fromage or a set Vanilla Cream mixed with a glass of rum while still liquid
6. For Swedish Tartlets, fill the baked tartlets with compote without liquid or fruit puree, covered with Cream, with a whipped cream rose in the center
