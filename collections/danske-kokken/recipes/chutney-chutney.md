## Ingredients

- 1 l vinegar
- 40 plums
- 3 pods Spanish pepper
- 0.25 kg sultana raisins
- 20 peeled shallots
- 1 small clove of garlic
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Place 3 pods of Spanish pepper in boiling water to soften and make them easier to chop fine
2. Chop together with 0.25 kg sultana raisins, 20 peeled shallots and 1 small clove of garlic
3. Cook all of these together with the vinegar under cover for 1 hour
4. Then add the split plums with the stones removed, 1 tablespoon salt, 3 tablespoons sugar, 2 teaspoons ground ginger and 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
5. After the plums are added, cook the mixture again under cover for approximately 2 hours
6. When the chutney is ready, it should resemble a thin porridge
7. Pour it into jars and bind with parchment paper
8. Can be stored for years
