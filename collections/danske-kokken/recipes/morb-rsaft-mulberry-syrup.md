## Ingredients

- Mulberries
- 1/3 kg (1/2 lb) powdered sugar per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. Press the berries with a spoon and pour into a sieve or jelly cloth.
2. Measure the juice that runs through, boil with the sugar and skim for 10 minutes.
3. Pour hot into bottles which are tied immediately.
