## Ingredients

- leftover rice pudding from dinner
- 2 eggs or just egg whites
- currants, raisins, ground almonds, lemon essence or grated lemon zest
- flour

## Directions

1. Rice pudding fritters are made from the leftover rice pudding from dinner.
2. In a portion for 6 people, add 2 eggs, or just the whites, to the pudding along with either currants, raisins, ground almonds, lemon essence or grated lemon zest.
3. Stir in a couple of spoonfuls of flour or in any case enough so the dough can hold together during baking.
4. It must not sit after it is stirred, as it will become too soft and require more flour to be added.
5. Bake them as cream puffs and serve hot, sprinkled with sugar.
6. Jam can be served with them.
