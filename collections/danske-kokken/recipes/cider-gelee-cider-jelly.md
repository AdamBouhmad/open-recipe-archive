## Ingredients

- 1 ounce gelatine
- rind of 1 lemon
- juice of 3 lemons
- 1 pint cold water
- 2 pounds whole sugar
- 3 pints boiling water
- 1 pint cider

## Directions

1. Add 1 ounce of gelatine to the grated peel of 1 lemon and the juice of 3.
2. Add 1 pint of cold water and let it stand for one hour.
3. Then add 2 pounds of whole sugar, 3 pints of boiling water, and 1 pint of cider.
4. Set to cool as usual.
