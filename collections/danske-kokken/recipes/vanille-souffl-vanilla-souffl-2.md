## Ingredients

- 3 cups sweet cream
- 60 quintals white sugar
- 1 vanilla pod, split lengthwise
- 14 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites, whipped to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Bring the sweet cream and white sugar to a boil with the split vanilla pod.
2. Whip the egg yolks and pour the boiling cream mixture into them.
3. Pour the entire mixture back into the saucepan and bring to a boil while constantly whisking.
4. Pour into a dish to cool, whisking constantly until cold.
5. Gently fold in the whipped egg whites.
6. Bake the soufflé in a gentle oven heat and serve as in Nr. 292, dusted with powdered sugar.
