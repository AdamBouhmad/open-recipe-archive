## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef chuck or mixed cut
- butter, lard, or Palmin
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- water or thin stock
- coloring
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices, which are beaten out with a meat mallet and dipped in a mixture of salt, pepper, and flour.
2. In a pot, brown a good slice of butter, lard, or Palmin, and in it brown a dessert plate of finely chopped onion, after which the floured meat is placed in the pot.
3. Pour over it 1 liter water or thin stock and coloring, sufficient to color the sauce.
4. Simmer the meat over low heat and under a closed lid for about 3 hours.
5. Add Maggi bouillon cubes in this and the following 2 dishes if the sauce is not sufficiently piquant.
6. Serve with boiled potatoes or mashed potatoes.
7. When the meat is about tender, raw, peeled, cut potatoes can be added to cook in the sauce.
