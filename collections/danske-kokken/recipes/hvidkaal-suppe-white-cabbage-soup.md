## Ingredients

- 5 L (5 pots) stock
- 1 large head white cabbage
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- 20 peppercorns
- salt
- flour
- butter
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cooked mostly the same as green cabbage soup on salted pork.
2. Divide 1 head white cabbage into 4 parts, which are scalded in boiling water.
3. Cut away the stem and chop the cabbage finely.
4. A couple of spoonfuls of flour can be chopped into the cabbage as directed for green cabbage, or it can be baked as with other thickened soups.
5. Cook the cabbage for a quarter hour in the stock together with 1 teaspoon caraway and 20 peppercorns.
6. Peeled potatoes cooked in the stock can be served with it; the pork is served alongside.
