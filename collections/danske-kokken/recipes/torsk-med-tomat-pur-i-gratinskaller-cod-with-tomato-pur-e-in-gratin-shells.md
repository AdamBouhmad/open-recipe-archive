## Ingredients

- cod fish
- butter
- flour
- fish stock
- tomato purée
- wine

## Directions

1. The cod is prepared as in the previous recipe. If the sauce is well smooth, it does not need to be bound with egg yolks, but only tomato purée and a glass of wine are added.
