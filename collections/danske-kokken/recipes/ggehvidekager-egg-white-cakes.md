## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 7 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
- 4 pounds fine flour
- 5 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Warm 1 pound butter slightly and stir to a salve
2. Stir in 7 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
3. Add 4 pounds fine flour
4. Finally add 5 beaten egg whites
5. Place on the sheet in small dollops
6. Curl slightly
