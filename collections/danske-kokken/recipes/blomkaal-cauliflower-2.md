## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- flour
- water or milk
- butter
- cream
- milk
- nutmeg
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower very well of the small leaves that sit between the florets
2. Cut away the stalks around the outer leaves
3. Wrap it in a piece of white cloth to prevent it from becoming reddish when cooked
4. Place it in cold water gradually as you clean it before cooking
5. Cook by steam or in water
6. Rub 2 rounded teaspoons of flour into a large spoonful of butter, add enough milk or cream to make a thick, even sauce and let the cauliflower have a brief boiling in this with nutmeg, a piece of sugar and finally a little salt
7. Alternatively, melt a good piece of butter over heat and stir a little nutmeg in, then pour it over the cauliflower when arranged on the plate
