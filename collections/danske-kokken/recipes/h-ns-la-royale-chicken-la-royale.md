## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- good, smooth ragout of calf sweetbreads and mushrooms

## Directions

1. Remove the skeleton from the chickens and fill them with a good, smooth ragout of calf sweetbreads and mushrooms.
2. Sew them closed tightly.
3. The chickens can either be boiled or roasted; if roasted, lard the breast with fine, fresh strips of fat.
4. For roasted chicken, make a brown mushroom sauce from the gravy. For boiled chicken, make a white mushroom sauce from the stock.
5. Serve the chickens with puff pastry strips or border.
