## Ingredients

- 3 L (3 Pots) semi-skimmed Milk
- 125 Gr (1/4 S) Cocoa
- 3 heaping Teaspoonfuls Maizena
- a little cold Milk
- Vanilla Essence
- Sugar
- 2 1/10 Dl (1 Pægl) Cream

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil
2. In some of this hot milk, stir the cocoa
3. In the cocoa, blend 3 heaping teaspoonfuls of Maizena stirred in a little cold milk; stir this into the milk, which then receives another boil
4. Add a little Vanilla Essence
5. Sweeten the soup and serve with Rusks
6. 2 1/10 Dl (1 Pægl) Cream can be whipped into foam and served on top or placed in the Tureen
