## Ingredients

- Butter dough (see recipe 22)
- Egg for brushing
- Chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Roll out a thin layer of butter dough
2. Cut out round cakes with a beer glass
3. Remove the center of each with a small liqueur glass to form rings
4. Brush the rings with egg, sprinkle with chopped almonds, and bake in a very hot oven
5. These cakes cannot be stored for long like other small cakes, only a few days, and must be warmed in the oven before serving
6. They can be placed quite close together on the baking sheet
