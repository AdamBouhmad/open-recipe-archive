## Ingredients

- Chopped parsley
- A little water
- Butter
- Flour (optional)

## Directions

1. Place chopped parsley on the heat with a little water
2. When it boils, remove the pot from heat
3. Stir in butter until melted and the sauce becomes smooth
4. If you wish to economize on butter, use less butter and knead a little flour into it, then bring to a boil in the sauce
