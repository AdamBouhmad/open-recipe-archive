## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Flour
- Butter
- Milk
- Cream
- Salt
- Sugar
- Nutmeg
- Egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower first, then cut into smaller pieces
2. Make a roux of flour and butter, thin with milk or cream and add a little salt, sugar, and nutmeg
3. Whipped egg yolks can also be added to the roux
