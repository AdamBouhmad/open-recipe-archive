## Ingredients

- Onion cutlets
- Browned potatoes
- Parsley
- Brown butter
- Gravy sauce

## Directions

1. Lay the cutlets in strips in the middle of the hot dish, garnish them on the sides with browned potatoes and decorate the dish with parsley. Serve with browned butter or a good gravy sauce.
