## Ingredients

- 50 gr (10 kvint) chervil
- 50 gr (10 kvint) watercress
- 50 gr (10 kvint) tarragon leaves
- 50 gr (10 kvint) salad burnet
- 200 gr (40 kvint) powdered yellow mustard
- water

## Directions

1. Place chervil, watercress, tarragon leaves and salad burnet in boiling water for a couple of minutes and pour onto a strainer to drain.
2. Pound the vegetables very finely in a mortar and mix into 200 gr (40 kvint) powdered yellow mustard that has been stirred in water.
3. Store this mustard in a well-stoppered and bound glass.
