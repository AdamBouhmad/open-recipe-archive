## Ingredients

- 1 liter (1 pint) water
- 2 kg (4 pounds) berries

## Directions

1. Pick and rinse the berries
2. Place them in a preserving pot together with the water
3. They must be completely cooked before pouring into a jelly cloth or fine sieve so the juice can drain
4. The next day, pour into bottles and cork them
5. Wrap with hay and boil for 5 minutes in a kettle
6. Seal with plaster the next day
