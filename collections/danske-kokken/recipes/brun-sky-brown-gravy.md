## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound beef kidney, finely chopped
- approximately 6 pounds beef, medium cut
- peeled onions
- cleaned carrots, sliced
- celery, sliced
- meat and bone scraps
- roasting bones, chopped
- salt
- 1 soup bunch
- about 10 peppercorns
- water
- bouillon as needed

## Directions

1. In a heavy-bottomed casserole, melt 1/4 pound butter and let it run around the bottom.
2. Add 1/2 pound finely chopped beef kidney.
3. Slice approximately 6 pounds of medium-cut beef and lay it on the bottom of the casserole.
4. Over this, place peeled onions and slices of cleaned carrots and celery.
5. On top of this, layer scraps of meat and bone and well-rinsed roasting bones that have been chopped.
6. Place the whole over gentle heat for one hour, or better yet in a fairly warm oven, and watch that it does not burn. If necessary, thin the mixture with a little bouillon.
7. Then place the whole briefly over stronger heat, and pour in enough water so that it just covers the mixture.
8. Add a little salt, a small soup bunch, and about 10 peppercorns, then cook covered under gentle heat for approximately 4 to 5 hours.
9. Remove the vegetables and soup bunch, and when the gravy is done, strain it and set it aside to cool.
10. The longer the gravy cooks, the firmer it becomes, and the longer it can be kept.
