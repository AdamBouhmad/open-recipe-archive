## Ingredients

- 36 quintal sugar
- 36 quintal fine flour
- 25 quintal butter
- 2 eggs
- chopped almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Stir 36 quintal sugar, 36 quintal fine flour, 25 quintal butter, and 2 eggs into a batter
2. Form small rings with chopped almonds on top
3. Bake light brown
