## Ingredients

- plaice
- salt
- laurel leaves
- water
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Choose larger plaice, clean and rinse well several times in cold water
2. Sprinkle with salt and let stand until cooking
3. Cut the fish lengthwise along the backbone into 2 parts, or cut crosswise into several pieces
4. Place over heat in boiling water with salt and some laurel leaves
5. Cook for several minutes until done
6. Arrange on serviette and garnish with small boiled potatoes
