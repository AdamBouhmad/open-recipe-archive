## Ingredients

- duck
- bouillon or gravy
- coloring
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- a glass of sherry
- tarragon vinegar
- isinglass
- egg whites
- eggshells
- cooked apples
- currant jelly
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Roast and portion the duck when cold according to the instructions for aspic
2. Skim the fat carefully from the gravy, which is mixed with good bouillon or gravy and boiled together
3. Give it a nice brown color with coloring and add Maggi bouillon cubes if needed
4. While it boils, add a glass of sherry along with a little tarragon vinegar and isinglass (12 leaves to 1 L)
5. Clarify with egg whites and eggshells
6. Pour a thin layer of gravy into the bottom of the mold, and when it is set, place the meat in it and cover with the rest of the gravy
7. The next day turn it out of the mold
8. Decorate the dish with a ring of cooked apples cut in 2 pieces; place a little currant jelly on each piece and garnish with lettuce leaves
9. If you have decorative skewers, arrange it in the middle of the mold by running it through a hard-boiled egg and binding it with lettuce leaves
