## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 20 ounces powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon potato flour
- A few crushed almonds
- 6 pots sweet milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- Salt to taste
- Ground cinnamon and powdered sugar for garnish

## Directions

1. Whisk 6 egg whites until foamy and blend in 20 ounces powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon potato flour, and a few crushed almonds.
2. Bring 6 pots of sweet milk to a boil over heat.
3. Using a spoon, drop balls of egg white mixture the size of an egg into the boiling milk, as many at once as will fit.
4. Let them cook for a few minutes, carefully turning them during cooking, then remove them.
5. Continue until all the egg white mixture is cooked into balls or snow eggs.
6. Mix the 6 remaining egg yolks, 1 teaspoon flour, and 1 tablespoon sugar until it resembles a paste.
7. Whisk this mixture into the boiling milk so it becomes smooth.
8. Add salt to taste, place the finished snow eggs in the milk, and serve the porridge with ground cinnamon and powdered sugar.
