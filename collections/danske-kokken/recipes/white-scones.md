## Ingredients

- 3/4 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 200 grams margarine
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 65 grams powdered sugar
- a little cardamom
- 2 eggs
- 125 grams sultana raisins
- 3 3/4 deciliters half-skimmed milk
- milk

## Directions

1. Mix the Pillsbury flour, margarine, baking powder, powdered sugar, and cardamom together
2. Stir in the eggs, sultana raisins, and half-skimmed milk
3. Knead the batter and roll out into 6 round, flat cakes of 1/4 inch thickness
4. Cut each cake into 4 pieces
5. Place the triangles on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes in a hot oven
6. 5 minutes before removing from the oven, brush with milk to brown
7. Serve as fresh as possible, split while warm, spread with butter, and eat folded together
