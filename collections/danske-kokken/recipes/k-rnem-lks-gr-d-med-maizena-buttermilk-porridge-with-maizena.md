## Ingredients

- 4 L buttermilk
- 1½ packages maizena
- 4 egg yolks
- ¼ kg powdered sugar
- 4 egg whites
- salt
- orange essence or vanilla essence
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix 4 L buttermilk with 1½ packages maizena and bring to a boil while stirring.
2. Let the porridge boil for a few minutes.
3. Remove the pot from heat.
4. Beat 4 egg yolks with ¼ kg powdered sugar and then blend with the 4 beaten egg whites; stir this into the boiling porridge.
5. Season with salt and a little orange essence or vanilla essence.
6. Serve immediately with butter, sugar and cinnamon.
