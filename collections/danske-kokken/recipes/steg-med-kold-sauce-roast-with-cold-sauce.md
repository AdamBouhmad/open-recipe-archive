## Ingredients

- Roast of any kind
- Butter
- Stock
- Cream
- Gelatine (if needed)

## Directions

1. Cut roast into pieces and arrange neatly on a flat dish
2. For the sauce, brown butter in a pan, stir in strong stock, and when it has boiled a little, remove from heat
3. Stir in enough good cream to make the sauce light
4. Return to heat and simmer, stirring constantly
5. Season and when smooth, continue stirring until nearly cold, then pour over the roast
6. If the sauce is not smooth, it can be stirred with a very little gelatine
7. Cut strong aspic or stiff stock into strips and arrange in strips around the roast
8. The aspic can also be melted and poured, nearly cold, over everything
9. Alternatively, strong stock can be melted and stirred with good cream until nearly cold and beginning to become smooth, then poured over diced roast
