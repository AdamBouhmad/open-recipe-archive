## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg prunes
- Very little water
- Sugar to taste
- Sago flour

## Directions

1. Scald the prunes
2. Remove the stones before cooking, unless using stone-free prunes
3. Cook the prunes in very little water under a lid over low heat
4. When tender, sweeten them
5. Thicken with sago flour
6. Pour into glass bowls
7. Serve cold with raw cream or crème
