## Ingredients

- 1 Quart good sweet Cream
- 7 Ounces Sugar
- a little Vanilla
- 8 well-beaten Egg yolks
- whipped Cream, about 5 to 6 Tablespoons

## Directions

1. Boil 1 Quart good sweet Cream with 7 Ounces Sugar and a little Vanilla.
2. Thicken it with 8 well-beaten Egg yolks, and whip it well.
3. When it is cold, add whipped Cream, about 5 to 6 Tablespoons, which is gently stirred in and should be seen on top.
4. This Sauce is used cold for Red Wine Pudding, Lemon Pudding, Fromage and several similar Things.
5. It is also used for Blanc-Manger; but then it is mixed with twice as much whipped Cream.
