## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter (1 pint) water
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) powdered sugar
- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) rinsed fruit (apples, pears, apricots, peaches, or various sorts of plums)
- Tapioca starch

## Directions

1. Set 1/4 liter water over heat with 125 grams powdered sugar, and when it is melted, add 1/2 kilogram rinsed fruit and cook until it can easily be pressed through a sieve.
2. Set the sauce over heat again and smooth it with mixed tapioca starch.
3. The amount of water and sugar must be adjusted according to the fruit's sweetness.
4. Some of the fruit may be left whole in the sauce; they are then cooked with the rest and removed before the remainder is pressed through the sieve.
5. Apples and pears must be peeled before cooking, and the cores removed.
6. Apricots and peaches are halved, the stones removed, and the kernels placed in the sauce.
