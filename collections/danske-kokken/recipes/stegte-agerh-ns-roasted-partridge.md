## Ingredients

- 6 young partridges
- Fresh bacon for larding
- Butter
- Flour
- Cream
- Milk
- Game stock or soup
- Salt
- Sugar
- Coloring
- Potatoes for browning
- Salad or parsley for garnish
- Currant, lingonberry, or rhubarb jelly

## Directions

1. Pluck or skin the young partridges, which are identifiable by their yellow legs; remove and dry them thoroughly inside with a cloth
2. Lard the breasts with fine strips of fresh bacon
3. Brown the partridges in a pot and cover with a sauce made as for pheasant
4. Cook over low heat for about 1 hour
5. Strain the sauce and stir in a little cold butter
6. Split the partridges and arrange them again on a hot dish
7. Garnish with browned potatoes and decorate with salad or parsley
8. Serve with currant, lingonberry, or rhubarb jelly
