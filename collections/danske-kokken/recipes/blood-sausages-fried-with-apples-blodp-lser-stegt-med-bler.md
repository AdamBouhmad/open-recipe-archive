## Ingredients

- Sausages
- Apples, peeled and sliced
- Sugar
- Butter
- Melted butter with sugar

## Directions

1. Slice the sausages and fry them in butter in a pan together with peeled and sliced apples, which are sprinkled with sugar.
2. Place the apples hot in the center of the platter and the sausages around them.
3. Serve with melted butter and sugar.
