## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- fine breadcrumbs
- butter
- boiled peeled potatoes
- frying sauce

## Directions

1. After the eel is slaughtered, skin it as follows: with a sharp knife, make an incision under the head and loosen the skin. Bind a thin, strong twine just under the head and hang up the eel. With a rough cloth, take the skin and pull it off.
2. Rub the skinned eel with salt, and cut off the fins with scissors, then remove the innards that are not used.
3. Rub the fish with salt and treat it as mentioned in the introduction, after first cutting it into pieces about 2 inches long and discarding the head.
4. After it has soaked for a couple of hours in water and the pieces are rinsed, dry them, roll in flour, then in beaten eggs, and then in fine sifted breadcrumbs.
5. Fry in a pan with very little butter and serve with boiled peeled potatoes and frying sauce.
