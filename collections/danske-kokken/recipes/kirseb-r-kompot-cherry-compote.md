## Ingredients

- 500 grams (1 pund) cherries
- 250 grams (1/2 pund) sugar
- 375 milliliters (1/2 pægel) water
- Sago flour

## Directions

1. The berries can be cooked with or without pits.
2. Cook the berries in the sugar and water syrup, but not so long that they become tough.
3. Thicken the syrup with sago flour.
