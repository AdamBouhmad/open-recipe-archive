## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Lightly salted water
- Lemon juice
- Salt (10 gr or 2 Kvint per 1 liter)
- Cold water

## Directions

1. After the artichokes have been treated as instructed, remove the flower parts from the bases
2. Give the bases a good cooking in lightly salted water with a little lemon juice added to prevent darkening
3. Remove and pour cold water over them and when cooled place them in dry, clean jars
4. Pour over them a cold solution of 10 gr (2 Kvint) salt to 1 liter boiling water, close the jars, place in a pot with cold water and cook for about 35 minutes
5. Cool in the water in which they were cooked, or remove and cover with a woolen blanket
