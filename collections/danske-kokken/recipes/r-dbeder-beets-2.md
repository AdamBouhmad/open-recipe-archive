## Ingredients

- beets
- water
- caraway
- salt
- powdered sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the beets in very little water
2. When tender, remove them
3. Scrape off the skin while hot so they remain whole
4. When cooled, cut into slices and place in a jar with a little caraway and salt if needed
5. Pour over with diluted vinegar
