## Ingredients

- Pig's head
- Pig's feet
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- Vinegar
- Pepper for serving

## Directions

1. Fleece the head, remove the ears and brain, cut off the jowls, and clean the place where they were situated
2. Remove unusable parts from inside the head, wash it and soak for a few hours in cold water so the blood can drain out
3. Boil it together with the feet in warm water and scrape well
4. Split the feet and put everything together in a pot on the heat in cold water with salt
5. Skim and cook until tender, remove, and while still warm take the bones out of the head, pick it clean, and when cold place it and the feet in a cold brine seasoned with whole pepper, bay leaves and a little cloves
6. Remove as needed for use and serve with vinegar and pepper
