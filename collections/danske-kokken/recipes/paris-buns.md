## Ingredients

- 3/4 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 125 grams margarine
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 3/4 deciliters half-skimmed milk
- sugar
- chopped candied citrus peel

## Directions

1. Mix together the Pillsbury flour, baking powder, margarine, and powdered sugar
2. Add the eggs and half-skimmed milk
3. Knead the batter well and shape into small, flat buns
4. Brush with egg and sprinkle with sugar and chopped candied citrus peel
5. Bake for approximately 1 quarter of an hour
