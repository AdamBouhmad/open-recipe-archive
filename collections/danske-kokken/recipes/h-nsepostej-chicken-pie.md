## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- fat hens
- veal forcemeat
- mushroom stew

## Directions

1. Line a smooth mold with puff pastry dough.
2. Cook fat hens and cut into suitable pieces.
3. Mix in veal forcemeat that has been cooked, cooled and cut into suitable pieces.
4. Dip each piece in a good mushroom stew.
5. In the mold, layer alternately a layer of forcemeat and a layer of chicken, so that the top layer becomes chicken.
6. Over this place a layer of puff pastry dough.
7. Bake the pie for one hour in a hot oven.
8. When it is to be served, loosen it with a knife at the sides of the mold and carefully turn it onto the dish.
9. Serve with mushroom sauce.
