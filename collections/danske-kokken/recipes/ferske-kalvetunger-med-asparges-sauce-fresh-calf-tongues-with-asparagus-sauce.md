## Ingredients

- Cooked, split calf tongues
- 1½ kg (1 S) asparagus
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Tongue broth
- 2 to 3 egg yolks
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus, cut it into small pieces, and cook in salted water.
2. Brown butter and flour together, then thin with some of the tongue broth to make a sauce of proper consistency.
3. Bind the sauce with 2 to 3 egg yolks.
4. Arrange the cooked, split tongues in a circle on the plate, with parsley for garnish in the center.
5. Pour the asparagus sauce over the tongues.
