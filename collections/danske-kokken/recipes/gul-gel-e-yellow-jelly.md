## Ingredients

- 1 pot Wine Jelly (Nr. 424)
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Set the Wine Jelly over heat
2. Whisk in the egg yolks, and while constantly whisking, bring to a boil
3. Remove from heat and continue whisking the jelly until cold
4. Pour into a glass bowl and set in a cold place until the jelly becomes stiff
