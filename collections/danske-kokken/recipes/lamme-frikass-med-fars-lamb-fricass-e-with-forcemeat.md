## Ingredients

- lamb fricassée
- fish or veal forcemeat
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. When the fricassée is prepared, pour it onto a flat dish and cover with a good layer of fish or veal forcemeat, which is brushed with egg and sprinkled with breadcrumbs.
2. Bake in the oven at high heat until the forcemeat is light brown and cooked through, and garnish afterwards with parsley.
