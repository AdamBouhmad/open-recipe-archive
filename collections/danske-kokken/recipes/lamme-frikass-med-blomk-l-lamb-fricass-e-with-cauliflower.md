## Ingredients

- lamb forequarter
- cooked cauliflower
- butter
- flour
- salt
- parsley
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Around the edge of the dish place a wreath of cooked cauliflower divided into small florets.
2. Chopped parsley can be stirred into the sauce.
3. It does not need to be thickened, but tastes best if it is.
