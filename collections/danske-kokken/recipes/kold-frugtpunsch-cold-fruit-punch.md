## Ingredients

- 2 pounds white sugar
- 1 pot water
- ½ pound fruit juice from crushed cherries, raspberries, strawberries or currants
- 1 pot boiling water
- juice of 2 lemons and yellow peel of 1 lemon
- 1 bottle red wine (or Mosel wine)
- 2 measures arrack

## Directions

1. Boil 2 pounds white sugar with 1 pot water to a syrup and skim
2. Add ½ pound fruit juice from crushed cherries, raspberries, strawberries or currants as desired
3. Add 1 pot boiling water, juice of 2 lemons and yellow peel of 1 lemon, 1 bottle red wine (or Mosel wine) and 2 measures arrack
4. Filter and let it stand well covered until it is completely cold
5. Drink cold
