## Ingredients

- chickens
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- cooked, salted beef tongue
- cooked vegetables (peas, asparagus, cauliflower, carrots, beans)
- sago flour

## Directions

1. Rub the cleaned and well-dried chickens inside with pepper and salt.
2. Place a walnut-sized piece of butter inside each chicken.
3. Lard the breast and thighs and roast as instructed in recipe 931.
4. When serving, cut the chickens into 4-6 pieces depending on size and arrange in strips on the platter, alternating with strips of sliced, warm, cooked, salted beef tongue.
5. Garnish around with various cooked vegetables such as peas, asparagus, cauliflower, carrots, beans, or only some of these.
6. Purée of vegetables can also be served with this.
7. Reduce the gravy to make a sauce and thicken with sago flour.
