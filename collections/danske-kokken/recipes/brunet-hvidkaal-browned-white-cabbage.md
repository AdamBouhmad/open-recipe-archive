## Ingredients

- Butter
- Sugar
- White cabbage
- Salt
- Meat gravy

## Directions

1. A good piece of butter is browned, and sugar is stirred into it; the cabbage must be cut very fine and cooked in it over even heat with a closed lid until it is tender, then salt is added. It is good, if you have it, to add a little meat gravy to the cabbage when it is finished.
