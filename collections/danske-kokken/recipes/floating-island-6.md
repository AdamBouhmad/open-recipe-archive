## Ingredients

- egg whites
- preserves
- fresh cream

## Directions

1. For each egg white take a small spoonful of currant or cloudberry preserves.
2. Whisk together for a long time, even after it becomes stiff peaks, as this makes it whiter and finer in appearance.
3. From 4 whites makes a fairly large portion.
4. Pile it in a high mound on a dish into which enough raw cream has been poured so that the mound can float in it.
5. Serve with the cream in which it floats and with small cakes.
6. You can also place the whites on a dish without cream, and then serve vanilla cream alongside.
