## Ingredients

- Calf sweetbreads, cleaned and boiled
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter or lard
- Stewed vegetables

## Directions

1. After the sweetbreads are cleaned and boiled, cut them into slices.
2. Dip the slices in egg and bread crumbs mixed with salt and pepper.
3. Fry in butter or lard and arrange as a garnish around stewed vegetables.
