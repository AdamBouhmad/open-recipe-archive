## Ingredients

- Live shrimp
- Salt
- A little sugar
- A live ember or red-hot iron bar

## Directions

1. The shrimp must be alive; rinse them in cold water before putting them into boiling water with salt and a little sugar.
2. While they cook, stir in the pot with a live ember on an iron rod to deepen the shrimp's color.
3. Cook for about 5 minutes, remove with a slotted spoon and hold under running water until they are cooled; then toss them in salt and remove.
4. In this way the meat becomes firm.
5. The shrimp are easy to shell, and they keep fresh longer.
6. They can be shelled and eaten with vinegar and pepper like lobster.
7. Otherwise, as mentioned with lobster, they can find use in many dishes.
