## Ingredients

- Meat or roast, sliced very thin
- 1 pound flour
- 3 beaten eggs
- 2 ounces melted butter
- 1 pint milk
- a little sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat or roast into very thin slices and dip them in a pancake batter made from 1 pound flour, 3 beaten eggs, 2 ounces melted butter, 1 pint milk, a little sugar and salt
2. Fry the slices on the griddle in clarified butter
3. Serve with vegetables such as turnip, gooseberry, rhubarb stalks or other sorts of vegetables
4. This amount of batter is sufficient for 8 persons
