## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- Water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the small, firm, green heads from the stalks and remove all yellow or wilted leaves.
2. Rinse well.
3. Place in boiling water with salt and cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
4. The heads must remain whole when cooked.
5. Drain through a colander, then place in a warm ragout dish and pour melted butter over them.
