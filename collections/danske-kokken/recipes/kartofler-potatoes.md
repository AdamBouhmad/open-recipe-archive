## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place them on the heat in as much water as it stands over them, salt is added and they cook continuously until they are tender, then drain the water, salt is sprinkled on and the pot is shaken well so the salt gets around, thereafter place the pot for half a dozen minutes at the back of the oven without a lid, so they can steam off and become dry. It is best that they are thereafter eaten immediately, but if you are obliged to wait, then as they are peeled, they can be placed in hot water with plenty of salt in; they are covered and placed at the back of the oven and in this way they can be kept good for a couple of hours. If they are very large potatoes, then you can peel them raw and either cook them whole or cut them in half; as you peel them they must be placed in cold water so they do not become dark. They are cooked and dried off like the previous ones.
