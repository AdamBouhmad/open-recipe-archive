## Ingredients

- Very ripe strawberries
- Powdered sugar (1/4 amount of berries)

## Directions

1. Hull the very ripe strawberries and cook them with the sugar until it is an even, fully cooked pulp.
2. Fill into jars and tie up as with the other jams.
