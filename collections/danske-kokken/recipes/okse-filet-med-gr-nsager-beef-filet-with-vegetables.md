## Ingredients

- Beef filet
- Butter
- Flour
- Cooking gravy
- Vegetables of the season, either preserved or fresh
- Butter for piping

## Directions

1. Lard, brown and roast the filet as in recipe 814
2. Make the sauce from browned butter and flour, thinned with the strained cooking gravy to a smooth sauce of appropriate consistency
3. Serve vegetables either preserved or whatever the season brings, and with these garnish the base on which the filet is laid
4. Serve the sauce in a sauce bowl
5. Small tartlets can be filled with creamed butter for the vegetables and placed around them
6. Pass the butter through a piping bag
