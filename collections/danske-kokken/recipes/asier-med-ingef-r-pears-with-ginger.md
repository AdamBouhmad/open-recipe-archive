## Ingredients

- 75 gr whole ginger
- ¾ dl water
- ¾ dl vinegar
- ½ kg powdered loaf sugar
- peeled pears

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, split them, hollow them out with a silver spoon and dry them in a cloth, before cutting them across into narrow strips, either with an ordinary knife or with an herb knife.
2. Soak the ginger in water until the next day; then scald, scrape, rinse and cook it together with the vinegar and sugar to a thin syrup, into which the pear pieces are placed.
3. Cook for about 5 minutes and drain on a sieve, and the syrup is cooked until smooth, together with the ginger.
4. The pears are put back into the pot to blend with the syrup, before they are filled into the jar, which is sealed when the preserve has cooled.
5. The ginger may remain in the jar among the pears, or it may be removed and wrapped in a clear cloth to be placed on top of the jar.
