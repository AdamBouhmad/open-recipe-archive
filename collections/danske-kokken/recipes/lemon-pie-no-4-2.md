## Ingredients

- Pie dough
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- A little cold water
- 1 cup boiling water
- 7 tablespoons sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- Zest of 2 lemons
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Egg whites for topping

## Directions

1. Treat the same as the previous pies.
2. Dissolve 1 tablespoon corn starch in a little cold water.
3. Add 1 cup boiling water and bring to a boil.
4. Remove from heat and stir in 7 tablespoons sugar, 4 egg yolks, grated zest and juice of 2 lemons.
5. Use the whites for the topping.
