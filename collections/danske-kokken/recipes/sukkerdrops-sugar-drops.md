## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 gill rose water

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together.
2. Place on paper with a teaspoon.
3. Makes approximately 60.
