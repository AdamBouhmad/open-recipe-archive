## Ingredients

- Wild ducks
- Thin slices of bacon
- Sauce
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the wild ducks as you would other fowl.
2. Do not stuff them, but bind thin slices of bacon over the breast.
3. Place in the oven and roast for approximately 15 minutes per pound of weight.
4. Serve with sauce and lemon slices.
