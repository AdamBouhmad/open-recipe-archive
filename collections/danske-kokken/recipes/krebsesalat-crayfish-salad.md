## Ingredients

- Crayfish meat
- Cooked potatoes
- Beets
- Cucumbers
- Onion
- Sugar
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mustard
- Crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Mix crayfish meat with cooked potatoes, beets, cucumbers and onion, all cut into small cubes, and with sugar, vinegar, salt, pepper and mustard to taste
2. Line a cone on the inside with crayfish tails so they face toward the cone
3. Fill it with all the chopped ingredients
4. When serving, turn the cone over onto the dish and garnish it with green salad leaves
5. The salad may also be prepared as in recipe 451
