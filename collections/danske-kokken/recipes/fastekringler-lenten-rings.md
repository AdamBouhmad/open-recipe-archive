## Ingredients

- 100 g margarine
- 1 kg flour
- a little salt and sugar
- 2 eggs
- 40 g yeast, dissolved in 1/2 L half-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Crumble the margarine into the flour
2. Add salt, sugar, eggs, and yeast dissolved in milk
3. Form the dough into elongated rings and place on a floured baking board
4. Let them rise
5. When risen, place them in a pot of boiling water
6. Remove with a slotted spoon as soon as they float to the surface
7. Place on buttered baking sheets
8. Brush with egg and bake for a quarter hour at good heat
9. Split and serve spread with butter
