## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 2 pounds powdered sugar
- zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 1 pound grated cooked cold potatoes
- 18 quintals sweet almonds, finely ground
- 12 quintals bitter almonds, finely ground
- 12 egg whites, beaten to stiff foam
- apple compote
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 12 egg yolks for 3 quarter-hours with 2 pounds powdered sugar, and add the strained juice and grated zest of 1 lemon, 1 pound grated cooked cold potatoes, 18 quintals sweet and 12 quintals bitter finely ground almonds.
2. When beaten for a quarter-hour more, fold in the 12 egg whites beaten to stiff foam and mix carefully.
3. In a greased tin form with loose bottom, lay a layer of the dough and on top a layer of apple compote and lastly again a layer of dough.
4. Bake slowly at even heat for 1 hour.
5. The apple compote should be cooked with a little cinnamon, and the apples should be whole and not cooked too soft.
