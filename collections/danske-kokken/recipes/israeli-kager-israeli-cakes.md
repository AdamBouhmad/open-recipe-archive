## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 10 eggs
- 1 pound wheat flour
- sliced almonds
- washed currants
- Ceylon cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, clarify it, and stir into a smooth paste with 1 pound powdered sugar
2. Add 10 eggs, 2 at a time, and stir well for 1 hour
3. Work in 1 pound wheat flour
4. Place the dough in a flat mold to the thickness of a two-crown coin
5. Sprinkle with sliced almonds, a few washed currants, and ground Ceylon cinnamon
6. Bake light brown
7. As soon as the cake is taken from the oven, cut it into oblong square pieces
8. Return them to the oven to dry at low heat
