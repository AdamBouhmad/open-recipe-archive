## Ingredients

- 30 quintals ground fine sugar
- ½ quintals citric acid
- ½ drops citron oil
- 25 drops vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Mix 30 quintals ground fine sugar well with ½ quintals citric acid
2. Drop ½ drops citron oil and 25 drops vanilla essence onto this mixture
3. Preserve the mixture in a glass jar with glass stopper
4. Add to cold, fresh water in appropriate quantity when you want to make lemonade
