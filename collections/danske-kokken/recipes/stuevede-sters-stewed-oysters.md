## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Oysters
- 2 cups cream or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix butter with flour.
2. Place this mixture and the oysters in a glazed pot and stir until it boils.
3. Gradually add cream or milk, stirring constantly.
4. Season with salt to taste.
5. The oysters' own liquid is not used when stewing them.
