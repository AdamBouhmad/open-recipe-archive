## Ingredients

- Very ripe gooseberries
- Powdered sugar (575 gr to 1/2 kg berries)

## Directions

1. Put the very ripe gooseberries in a jar, and place this in a pot of boiling water, which must be kept boiling until the berries are completely soft and the juice can easily be pressed through a coarse sieve.
2. The strained juice is then cooked with the sugar until one finds the jam sufficiently stiff.
3. Fill it into jars, which are covered with paper and tied up.
4. What remains in the sieve can have water added to it and be cooked down to gooseberry porridge.
