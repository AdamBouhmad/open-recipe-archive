## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 250 grams margarine
- 125 grams fine sugar
- zest of 1 lemon or lemon drops
- cardamom
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 500 grams Pillsbury flour
- 125 grams sultana raisins
- 125 grams currants
- 500 milliliters semi-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, margarine, fine sugar, lemon zest or lemon drops, cardamom, and baking powder well
2. Add Pillsbury flour, sultana raisins, currants, and semi-skimmed milk
3. Pour batter into a greased form
