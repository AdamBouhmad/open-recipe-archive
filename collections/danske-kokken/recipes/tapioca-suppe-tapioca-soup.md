## Ingredients

- 4 L (4 pots) good, strong bouillon
- 125 g (1/4 lb) tapioca grains
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Sprinkle 125 g (1/4 lb) tapioca grains into 4 L (4 pots) of boiling bouillon while stirring constantly
2. Simmer for at least a quarter hour or until the grains are clear
3. Fine sago grains can be used instead of tapioca
4. Serve with toasted bread
