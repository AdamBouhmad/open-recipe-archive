## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- ¼ pound powdered sugar
- ¾ pound rinsed butter
- 1 egg
- lemon zest, grated
- egg yolk
- coarsely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Mix together wheat flour, powdered sugar, rinsed butter, egg, and grated lemon zest
2. Roll into small rings
3. Brush with egg yolk
4. Sprinkle with coarsely ground sugar
5. Bake light brown
