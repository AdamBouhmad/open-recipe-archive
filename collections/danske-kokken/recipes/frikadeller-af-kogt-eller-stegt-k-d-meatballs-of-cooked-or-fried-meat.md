## Ingredients

- 125 grams (0.25 lbs) fatty, smoked or fresh bacon, finely chopped
- 0.5 kg (1 lbs) cooked or fried meat
- 1 onion
- 1 French bread roll, sliced and soaked in milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- 4-5 eggs
- Egg for dipping
- Breadcrumbs
- Clarified butter or Palmin oil

## Directions

1. Finely chop the bacon with the cooked or fried meat and onion.
2. When it forms a cohesive mass, work in with the chopper a small French bread roll that has been sliced and soaked in milk for one hour.
3. Finally chop in salt, pepper and 4-5 eggs until it becomes a firm dough.
4. Shape with a spoon into balls, dip in egg and breadcrumbs and form into round or slightly flattened oval shapes.
5. Fry in clarified butter or Palmin oil over low heat.
6. Serve with a sharp brown sauce and cooked macaroni or with other vegetables.
