## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- olives
- strained stock from cooking the tongue
- bouillon or gravy

## Directions

1. After cooking the tongue as described in the previous recipe, cut it into slices
2. Peel the olives and cut them into long strips
3. Insert these strips into one side of each tongue slice
4. Lay the slices close together with the olive-studded side upward and pour over some of the strained stock in which the tongue was cooked
5. Cook until done over gentle heat
6. Prepare an olive sauce (see recipe 252) and arrange the tongue slices in a ring around the edge of the dish, pouring the sauce in the center
7. When the olives are peeled, boil the interior in bouillon or gravy, strain and add to the olive sauce
