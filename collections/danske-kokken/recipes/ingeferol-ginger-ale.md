## Ingredients

- 13 pounds sugar
- 13 ounces ginger
- 23 ounces cream of tartar
- 1 lemon, sliced and seeded
- 7 quarts boiling water
- 0.5 gill yeast

## Directions

1. Place the sugar and spices in a stone crock, pour the boiling water over, stir, cover well and let stand for 24 hours in a cool place. Then open the crock, add the yeast and stir well, let stand for another 24 hours. Then pour into bottles, cork and seal with wax. Can be served after 3 days.
