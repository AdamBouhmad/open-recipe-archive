## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg (1 pound) can of canned Sweet Corn
- 4 L (4 pots) spare broth
- 50 grams (10 skein) butter
- 50 grams (10 skein) flour
- 3 to 4 egg yolks
- salt
- ground white pepper
- wine
- tomato purée
- toasted bread
- fresh corn cobs (optional)

## Directions

1. Bake 50 grams (10 skein) butter and 50 grams (10 skein) flour together and thin with the broth, pour the contents of the can into it, and let the thinned soup simmer for 5 minutes before sieving.
2. Pour it into the pot, bring it to a boil, bind it with 3 to 4 egg yolks, season with salt, ground white pepper, wine and tomato purée to taste.
3. Serve with toasted bread. Can be made from fresh corn cobs.
