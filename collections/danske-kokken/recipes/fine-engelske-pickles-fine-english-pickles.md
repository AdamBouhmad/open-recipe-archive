## Ingredients

- 5 Quart Vinegar
- 2 Pounds Mustard Seeds
- 1 Pound Long Pepper
- 1 Pound Short Pepper
- 1 Quart Wine Vinegar
- 1 small bottle Cayenne Pepper
- 6 pieces Horseradish sliced
- 2 Stalks Garlic
- Carrots cleaned and flaked
- Cabbage
- Sliced Cauliflower
- Brussels Sprouts
- Green Beans
- Small Corn
- Young Elderberry Stems
- Celery
- Slices of 2 Apples
- Cucumbers
- Small whole peeled Mushrooms
- 1-2 Bags Spanish Pepper per glass
- 1 Teaspoon whole Mustard Seeds per glass

## Directions

1. Cook all these parts and place in a large crock, let it stand covered until cooled
2. Add as many cleaned and flaked carrots as needed to color the vinegar yellow
3. Clean and polish the various vegetables such as cabbage, sliced cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, green beans, small corn, very young elderberry stems, celery, apple slices, cucumbers, small whole peeled mushrooms and the like
4. Pour boiled vinegar over them repeatedly, cooling each time, until they are soft
5. Drain on a sieve and let stand overnight
6. Place in pickle glasses and fill with the aforementioned vinegar
7. Stopper and bind with bladder
8. Cover with sealing wax
9. Add 1-2 bags of Spanish pepper and one teaspoon of whole mustard seeds to each glass
