## Ingredients

- Filling as for Medisterpølse
- Fine lamb intestines

## Directions

1. These sausages are prepared like Medister (No. 510), only with the difference that the filling is put into fine lamb intestines and the sausages are smoked.
2. They are boiled 3 minutes in boiling water with salt in it or in boiling bacon soup and are served with coarse, grated horseradish and toasted French bread.
