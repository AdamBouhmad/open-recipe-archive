## Ingredients

- 125 g oats
- 1 L water
- 2 teaspoons dry cocoa
- 2 to 3 teaspoons flour

## Directions

1. Cook the oats and water.
2. Stir in the cocoa and flour; the porridge comes to a boil.
3. Serve with sweet milk.
