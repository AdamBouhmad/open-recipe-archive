## Ingredients

- 1 pound powdered sugar
- rind of 2 oranges and 2 lemons
- juice of 6 oranges and 2 large lemons
- 1 bottle white wine
- 12 kvint isinglass
- 6 egg yolks
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. To 1 pound powdered sugar add the grated rind of 2 oranges and 2 lemons, also the juice of 6 oranges and 2 large lemons, 1 bottle white wine, 12 kvint isinglass and 6 egg yolks.
2. Stir everything well together and place over the fire, bringing to a boil while stirring constantly.
3. Pour the mixture into a mold and let stand for a few hours in a cold place.
4. The pudding is served with vanilla cream.
5. When the pudding is taken from the fire, one can also carefully fold in 2 pægle sweet cream that has been whipped to stiff peaks.
