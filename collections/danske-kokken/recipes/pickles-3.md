## Ingredients

- small thumb-length cucumbers
- very small onions
- small peeled carrots
- small green peas
- fine pieces of cauliflower
- a little saltpeter
- salt water
- good wine vinegar
- scraped horseradish cut into smaller pieces
- Spanish pepper
- black pepper
- yellow mustard
- mustard seed

## Directions

1. Place the cucumbers, onions, carrots, peas, and cauliflower along with a little saltpeter in a salt brine and let stand for 1 week
2. Place on a sieve so the brine can drain off
3. Let sit for 6 hours in fresh water
4. Place in the sieve again, then on a clean, dry linen cloth to further remove the water
5. Remove the cauliflower and carrots; place the remaining items over heat with enough good wine vinegar to cover well
6. Add scraped horseradish cut into smaller pieces, a little Spanish pepper, black pepper, and yellow mustard to the pot
7. Boil until the cucumbers have a beautiful green color
8. Cook the carrots and cauliflower separately in a salt brine
9. Arrange everything in layers in a jar and cover with the brine
10. Cover the jar with a serviette and place a plate or similar over it so the vapors remain until everything has cooled
11. When cooled, cover with a fine linen cloth and place a small bag filled with mustard seed on top, then bind the jar
