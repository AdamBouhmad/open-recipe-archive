## Ingredients

- 200 Gr (40 Kvint) chocolate
- a little milk
- 1 L (1 Pot) cream or milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar
- cocoa (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt the chocolate over the fire in a little milk.
2. Stir 1 L (1 Pot) cream or milk into this for the cream.
3. Cook this as vanilla cream Nr. 1068, with 8 egg yolks and 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar.
4. Cocoa can be used in this recipe.
