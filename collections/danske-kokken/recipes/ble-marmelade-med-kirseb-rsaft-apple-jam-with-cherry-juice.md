## Ingredients

- Apples
- Powdered sugar (125 gr)
- Cherry juice (1/4 l to 1/2 kg pulp)

## Directions

1. After the apples are cooked and the pulp pressed through the sieve, weigh it, then add sugar and cherry juice.
2. Cook the jam down to a suitable consistency, fill into jars or crocks, cover with paper and tie up.
