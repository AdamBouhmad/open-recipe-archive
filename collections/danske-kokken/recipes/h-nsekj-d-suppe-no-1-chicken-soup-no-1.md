## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- white root vegetables
- bread or flour balls
- parsley
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. It is made like other meat stock; however, take only 1/4 water over the portion of soup you want if the chickens are young; if they are old and fatty, nearly double of what you want to keep must be taken
2. Cut up the chickens so that the legs and wings are taken off; next cut the backbone from the breast; cut it over in 3 parts, the breast is split, and each half is divided in two; legs, offal and outer wings are taken along
3. On chicken soup only white root vegetables are used, and bread or flour balls, parsley and a little nutmeg
