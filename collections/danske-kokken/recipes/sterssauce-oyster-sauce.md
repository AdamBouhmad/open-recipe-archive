## Ingredients

- 30 oysters with their beards
- 18 quint butter
- 12 quint flour
- oyster liquor
- white wine
- lemon juice
- soup or broth
- 2 egg yolks (optional for binding)

## Directions

1. Set the beards of 30 oysters along with all the liquor collected carefully to boil, then strain.
2. Stir 18 quint butter with 12 quint flour, and pour the oyster liquor onto it with white wine, a little lemon juice and enough soup so the whole becomes properly smooth.
3. Add the 30 oysters and warm through without allowing to boil.
4. The sauce may be bound with 2 egg yolks.
