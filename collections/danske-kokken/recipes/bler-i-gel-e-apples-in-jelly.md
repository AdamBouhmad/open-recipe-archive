## Ingredients

- Wine Jelly (Nr. 424)
- apples, peeled and cored
- sugar
- cinnamon
- white wine

## Directions

1. Pour the Wine Jelly into small molds and set to stiffen
2. Using a silver spoon dipped in hot water, remove enough jelly from the center of each mold to make room for a piece of apple
3. Peel the apples, remove the cores, and cook the pieces with sugar and cinnamon in a little white wine
4. Melt the removed jelly and use it to fill the holes where the apple pieces are placed
5. Set the jelly to cool
6. When ready to serve, quickly dip the molds in hot water, dry them, and turn the jelly out onto a dish so that the whole forms a pyramid
