## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Chicken or veal ground meat
- Mushrooms, chopped
- Parsley
- Breadcrumbs
- Melted butter
- Strong stock with Madeira
- Butter-coated paper
- Arrowroot flour

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the artichokes and cut level with the base of the flower head
2. Clean and rinse them, then cook in salted water with a little lemon juice for about an hour so they do not darken
3. Remove and place on cloth to drain
4. Scrape away the fuzzy choke with a spoon handle
5. Mix chopped mushrooms and parsley into good chicken or veal ground meat
6. Fill the artichokes with this mixture, mounding it high in the center
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs, drizzle with melted butter, and place in the oven in a roasting pan
8. During cooking, drizzle frequently with good strong stock mixed with Madeira
9. Cover with butter-coated paper and bake for about 15 minutes
10. Strain the stock, skim it, add more food coloring if needed, and thicken with arrowroot flour
11. Arrange the artichokes on a folded napkin on a platter and serve the sauce separately
