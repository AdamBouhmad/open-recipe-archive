## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fine wheat flour
- 2 measures cream
- 15 quints melted butter
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 tablespoons yeast

## Directions

1. Warm 2 measures cream slightly.
2. Work together 2 pounds fine wheat flour, the warm cream, 15 quints melted butter, 1 pound sugar and 2 tablespoons yeast well until the dough releases from the hands.
3. Let it rise in a warm place.
4. When the dough seems sufficiently risen, form it into small, round buns and place them on a pan.
5. Cover with a thin cloth and let them rise again in a warm place.
6. Place them in the oven and bake light brown, then remove and let cool.
7. When the buns are cold, cut them in half with a sharp knife and place them back on the pan with the cut side up.
8. Place them in a warm, but not too hot oven, until the cut side is light brown.
