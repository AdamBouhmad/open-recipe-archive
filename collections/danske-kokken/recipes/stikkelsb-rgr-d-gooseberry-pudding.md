## Ingredients

- 3 pots unripe green gooseberries
- 2 pots water
- peel of 1 lemon
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 1 pail white wine
- crushed sugar (about 14 pounds), to taste
- 30 quints sago flour
- cold sweet cream
- a little vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Pick and rinse the gooseberries well in cold water
2. Cook them with 2 pots water together with the lemon peel and cinnamon stick until completely cooked
3. Press through a colander several times and then through an open sieve
4. Heat the 3 pots of strained liquid with 1 pail white wine and crushed sugar to taste
5. Thicken like rødgrød with 30 quints sago flour, first mixed in a little cold water
6. Serve cold with cold sweet cream in which a little vanilla sugar has been added
