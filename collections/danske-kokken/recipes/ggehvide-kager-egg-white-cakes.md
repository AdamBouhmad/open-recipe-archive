## Ingredients

- ½ pound clarified butter
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- ½ pound wheat flour
- 5 egg whites, whipped to stiff peaks
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Stir clarified butter for 20 minutes with 1 pound powdered sugar
2. Gradually stir in ½ pound wheat flour
3. Finally fold in 5 egg whites whipped to stiff peaks
4. Place on the pan with a teaspoon
5. Sprinkle with chopped almonds
6. Bake at even oven heat
