## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 pound white sugar
- 6 tablespoons sifted flour
- 1/4 pound grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar dissolved in a little rose water

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs for a quarter hour with 1/2 pound white sugar.
2. Mix in 6 tablespoons sifted flour, 1/4 pound grated chocolate, and 1 teaspoon cream of tartar dissolved in a little rose water.
3. Butter the pan with a spatula and it must be cold again before the dough is placed on it in small lumps, not too close together as they spread out.
4. They are generally done in half an hour and are removed when they loosen from the pan.
5. Makes 50 cakes.
