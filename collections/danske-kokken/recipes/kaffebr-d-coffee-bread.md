## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds wheat flour
- 0.75 pound butter
- 3 tablespoons cream
- 3 tablespoons rum
- 3 tablespoons white crushed sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Stir together 1.5 pounds wheat flour, 0.75 pound butter, 3 tablespoons cream, 3 tablespoons rum, 3 tablespoons white crushed sugar, 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks into a firm dough.
2. Roll out the dough, sprinkle with sugar and bake at even heat.
3. When the cake is baked, remove it from the oven and cut it, while warm, into oblong pieces.
