## Ingredients

- One cod weighing 6 pounds
- Butter
- Grated bread
- Shallots
- Chopped parsley
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into 4 to 5 pieces, remove the bones. Marinate the meat with melted butter, lemon juice, parsley and shallots.
2. Lay with marinade above and below, sprinkle thickly with grated bread, pour over with warm butter and roast in the oven.
3. Serve with sauce made of white wine, smoothed with egg yolks and seasoned to taste.
