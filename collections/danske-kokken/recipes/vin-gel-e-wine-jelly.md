## Ingredients

- Juice of 5 lemons
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 2 pounds white sugar
- 2 pints water
- 12 quarters isinglass
- 4 egg whites
- 1 bottle Rhine wine
- 1 pint Madeira

## Directions

1. Set the strained juice of 5 and zest of 2 lemons, 2 pounds white sugar, 2 pints water, 12 quarters isinglass, and 4 egg whites over heat in an enameled pot.
2. Bring to a boil in the manner mentioned in Recipe 421 and skim.
3. Then add 1 bottle Rhine wine and 1 pint Madeira, and pour the whole through a filter bag to clarify.
4. Arrange the jelly preferably in tall glass bowls as cold as possible.
