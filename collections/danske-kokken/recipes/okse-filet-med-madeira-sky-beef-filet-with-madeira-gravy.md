## Ingredients

- Good soup or gravy
- Madeira wine
- Beef filet
- Sago flour

## Directions

1. To baste the filet you must have a good soup or gravy which gives a beautiful brown color, is well reduced and prepared with Madeira to taste
2. With this gravy baste the browned filet and roast it in the gravy
3. When the filet is nearly done, strain the gravy, skim off fat and thicken if necessary with sago flour
4. Add more Madeira to taste
