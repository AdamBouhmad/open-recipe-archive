## Ingredients

- Halibut meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread
- Butter or Palmin
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thick slices lengthwise with the grain of the meat, without bone and skin
2. Beat the pieces with a meat mallet
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and bread
4. Fry on the pan in butter or ample Palmin
5. Arrange standing upright in a point in the middle of the platter, covered with a napkin and garnished with lemon
6. Serve with browned butter and cooked potatoes or with various kinds of compound butter
