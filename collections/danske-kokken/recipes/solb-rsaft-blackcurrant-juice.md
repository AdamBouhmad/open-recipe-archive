## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 S) blackcurrants per batch
- 1/4 liter (1 Pægl) water per 1/2 kg blackcurrants
- 1/4 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice

## Directions

1. Each 1/2 kg (1 S) blackcurrants is covered with 1/4 liter (1 Pægl) water and must boil completely through before pouring into the jelly cloth.
2. Measure the juice and boil it with the sugar for a quarter of an hour.
3. Skim it and pour it immediately into bottles, which are then bound.
