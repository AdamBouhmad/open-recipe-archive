## Ingredients

- firm red cabbage head
- water
- vinegar
- butter or meat fat
- salt
- sugar
- currant juice
- caraway seeds (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the outer loose leaves and the stalk from a firm head
2. Cut the cabbage into fine strips
3. Rinse in cold water and lay on a colander to drain
4. Place over heat in a little cold water and vinegar, not too much
5. Cook covered until nearly tender
6. Add a little butter or meat fat, salt and sugar to taste
7. If the color is not a good red, add a little currant juice
8. Many like to mix in a tablespoon of caraway before cooking, which is also healthful
