## Ingredients

- 125 grams margarine
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 kilogram powdered sugar
- a few drops lemon oil
- a little cardamom
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 1/2 liter half-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix the margarine with the eggs, powdered sugar, lemon oil, cardamom, and baking powder
2. Stir in the Pillsbury flour and half-skimmed milk
3. Pour the batter into a greased form and bake for 1 hour
