## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram maizena
- 1/2 kilogram cake flour
- 1/4 kilogram cold margarine
- 100 grams powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- vanilla essence
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 liter milk

## Directions

1. Mix the maizena, cake flour, cold margarine, powdered sugar, baking powder, and vanilla essence together on a pastry board until the margarine is completely worked into the flour
2. Stir in the eggs and milk with a spoon and mix the dough together
3. Roll out as thin as possible, as the biscuits rise considerably during baking
4. Prick the dough and cut out with a tin can, placing very close together on a cold greased baking sheet
5. Bake light brown or golden. Makes approximately 150 excellent round tea biscuits, suitable for butter and cheese or marmalade
