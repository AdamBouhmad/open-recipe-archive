## Ingredients

- 1 pitcher water
- 1 pound fine sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 drop citron oil
- 2 measures Graves

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pitcher water and 1 pound fine sugar together
2. Press the juice of 1 lemon through a sieve into it
3. Add 1 drop citron oil and 2 measures Graves
