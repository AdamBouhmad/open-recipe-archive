## Ingredients

- 10 carrots
- 3 onions
- 5 L (5 pots) broth
- a good whisk
- salt
- ground white pepper
- 100 grams (20 skein) butter
- 100 grams (20 skein) flour
- bread croutons (no. 500)

## Directions

1. After the carrots are scraped, rinsed and sliced together with the onions, place them over heat with 2 L (2 pots) of the broth, a good whisk, and salt and ground white pepper.
2. Cook them until tender so that they are easily rubbed through a coarse sieve.
3. Brown 100 grams (20 skein) butter and bake 100 grams (20 skein) flour, and thin with the sieved purée, which is brought to a boil.
4. Serve the soup with bread croutons (no. 500).
