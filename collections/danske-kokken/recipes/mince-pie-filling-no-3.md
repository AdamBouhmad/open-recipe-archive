## Ingredients

- 3 pints peeled and chopped apples
- 1 pint cooked chopped meat
- 0.5 pint butter or chopped suet
- 1 pint molasses
- 0.5 pint water
- 1.5 tablespoons allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon cloves
- 2.5 large tablespoons vinegar
- 3 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix together 3 pints peeled and chopped apples, 1 pint cooked chopped meat, 0.5 pint butter or chopped suet, 1 pint molasses, 0.5 pint water, 1.5 tablespoons allspice, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 teaspoon salt, 0.5 teaspoon cloves, 2.5 large tablespoons vinegar, and 3 nutmeg.
