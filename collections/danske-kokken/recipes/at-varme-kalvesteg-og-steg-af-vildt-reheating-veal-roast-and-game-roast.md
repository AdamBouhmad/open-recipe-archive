## Ingredients

- veal or game roast, sliced
- butter
- gravy or cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices and place it in browned butter on the pan
2. When it is well heated, remove it and place it on the serving dish
3. Remove the pan from heat while you stir a little gravy or cream into the sauce
4. Return the pan to heat and stir until it boils, then pour it over the meat
5. Serve with stiff currant jelly
6. For as much meat as serves 5 persons, use a gill of good cream
7. It does not matter if the cream is sour
