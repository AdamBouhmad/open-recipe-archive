## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- water
- vinegar
- milk
- soup stock
- flour
- butter or cream
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots well and immediately place them as you work in lukewarm water with a little vinegar and milk, otherwise they will turn black
2. Cut them into pieces of similar shape and size as asparagus to be stewed
3. Cook in soup stock and they will be tender in a moment
4. Stew them in a white sauce made from flour and butter or in cream with pepper and nutmeg to taste
