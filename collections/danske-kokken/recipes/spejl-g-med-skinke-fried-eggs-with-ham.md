## Ingredients

- Fried eggs
- Cooked ham
- Butter
- Watercress or finely sliced leeks
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut cooked ham into slices
2. Fry on the pan in butter and arrange on a warm plate
3. Place one fried egg on each slice of ham
4. Watercress or finely sliced leeks are much appreciated with this dish; sprinkle lightly over the eggs
