## Ingredients

- 1 large lobster or 2 small lobsters
- 1 quart milk
- 1 pint water
- 1 pound butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Remove all meat from the lobster bones and chop finely
2. Warm 1 quart milk together with 1 pint water
3. Add the lobster meat to the milk and water along with 1 pound butter mixed with 1 tablespoon flour
4. Season with salt and red pepper to taste
5. Boil for 10 minutes
