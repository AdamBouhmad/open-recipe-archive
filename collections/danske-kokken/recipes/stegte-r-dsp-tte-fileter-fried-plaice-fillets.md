## Ingredients

- plaice fillets
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- Palmin or lard

## Directions

1. Let cut fillets stand for about an hour sprinkled with salt
2. Dry in fish cloth
3. Dip first in flour, then in egg, and finally in finely sifted, pounded breadcrumbs
4. Fry in Palmin or lard, or fry in pan until fish is crispy and has a nice brown color
5. Serve immediately
6. Alternatively, place fillets in a frying basket and lower into boiling fat
