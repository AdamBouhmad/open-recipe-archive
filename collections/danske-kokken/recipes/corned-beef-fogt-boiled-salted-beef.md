## Ingredients

- Corned beef
- Water

## Directions

1. The meat is placed in water overnight if it is very salty, but if the meat is young and only lightly salted, such prolonged soaking is not necessary.
2. The salt is washed off, and cold water is poured over to completely cover it.
3. It should be placed over so slow a fire that it will only simmer, and remain thus for 4 to 6 hours according to the size of the piece.
4. If it is tough, let it stand over until the next day and bring it to the boiling point just before serving.
5. Breast pieces are simmered until the bones are easily removed, folded together and placed under a weight until cold; then it can be sliced.
