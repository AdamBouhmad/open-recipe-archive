## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds cooked, peeled, cold potatoes
- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 5 quintals finely pounded bitter almonds
- 12 egg yolks
- 35 quintals powdered sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate 1.5 pounds cooked, peeled, cold potatoes and stir, always in the same direction, with 1 pound sweet and 5 quintals finely pounded bitter almonds, 12 egg yolks, 35 quintals powdered sugar, the grated zest of 1 lemon, and the juice of 2 lemons
2. When this mixture is well stirred together, gradually stir in the whipped egg whites just before placing the cake in a buttered form
3. Immediately place in the oven
4. Bake for one hour and should be a fine pale brown
