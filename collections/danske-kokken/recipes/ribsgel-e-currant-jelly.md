## Ingredients

- Currants
- Powdered sugar (2 pounds per pot of juice)
- Water

## Directions

1. Place the currants in pots in a warm oven so they burst and the juice can run off, making it clearer than by the usual pressing method
2. Pour the juice through a jelly bag
3. Dip the sugar briefly in water, place it over a gentle fire and dissolve it with regular stirring
4. When it is melted, place it over stronger fire and cook until it resembles an even pulp
5. Remove the pot from the fire and pour in the juice
6. Cook the whole together for 10 minutes, after which the jelly is ready to be poured into containers
