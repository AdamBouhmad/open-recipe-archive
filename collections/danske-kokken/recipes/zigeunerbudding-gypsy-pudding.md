## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 6 quintals isinglass dissolved in water
- 1 quarter-pound sugar
- currant or raspberry jam

## Directions

1. Whip 1 pot sweet cream to foam.
2. Bring to a boil 6 quintals isinglass dissolved in water and 1 quarter-pound sugar.
3. When almost cold, carefully stir this into the whipped cream along with some currant or raspberry jam.
4. Line the bottom of a mold with jam and place some oval sugar cookies around the sides.
5. Fill the pudding into the mold and let it stand in a cold place for several hours.
6. Turn the pudding out of the mold onto a dish and place some sugar cookies around it.
