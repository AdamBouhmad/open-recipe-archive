## Ingredients

- Pear quinces
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Water
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Peel the pear quinces, remove the cores, and cut the fruit into 8 pieces, which are gradually placed in cold water.
2. Cook the quinces until partially tender in water; remove them, place on a sieve, and only when the water has well drained, pierce each piece with a thin strip of lemon peel and a small piece of cinnamon.
3. Weigh the fruit and cook the sugar to an even syrup with the measured amount of the water in which the quinces were cooked.
4. Pour the hot syrup over the fruit.
5. After a couple of days have passed, pour off the liquid, which has become considerably thinner, and cook it until it is again very even; then pour it into the jar over the quinces.
6. Finally pour in enough Cognac to cover the fruit. Can be sealed immediately.
