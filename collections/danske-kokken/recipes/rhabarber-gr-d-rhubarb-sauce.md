## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare this the same way as gooseberry sauce
2. When desired for roasted meat, do not strain it, and do not thicken it either, but only cook it in very little water and sufficient sugar
3. In all cases, strip the stalks by taking hold with a knife at the top of the red end and then drawing off the skin, and cut the stalks into small pieces
