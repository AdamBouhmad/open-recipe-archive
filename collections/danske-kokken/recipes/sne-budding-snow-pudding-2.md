## Ingredients

- 1 Liter (1 Pot) cream
- 5 leaves isinglass
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water
- a little vanilla essence
- jam

## Directions

1. Whip 1 Liter (1 Pot) cream to stiff foam
2. Dissolve 5 leaves of isinglass in 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water and stir in along with a little vanilla essence
3. Layer the cream foam with jam in a glass bowl or in a mold from which the pudding can be turned out
4. Make on the same day it is to be served
