## Ingredients

- Dough from recipe 1120
- Cream from recipe 1120
- Chocolate glaze

## Directions

1. Bake two layers using the same dough and quantity as in recipe 1120
2. Place cream between the layers
3. Cover the cake with chocolate glaze
