## Ingredients

- Beef tenderloin
- Fat from the surface of the tenderloin
- Sinew from the side of the tenderloin
- Skin of the tenderloin
- Bacon or pork fat for larding
- Butter
- Sugar
- Thin gravy or stock
- Onions, approximately 30-40

## Directions

1. Cut off the fat that sits on the surface of the tenderloin as well as the sinewed string at the side.
2. Remove the skin and lard the fillet with bacon.
3. Roast it as a beef roast, but proportionally not for as long.
4. Brown 30-40 onions with butter and sugar in a pan and thin them with thin stock.
5. Drip the fillet alternately with this sauce and with the juice that runs from the fillet itself.
6. Serve with the onions on top.
7. Cook together the sauce from the roast and the sauce from the onions and serve with it.
