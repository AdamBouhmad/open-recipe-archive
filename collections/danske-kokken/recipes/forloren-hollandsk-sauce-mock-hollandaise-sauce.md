## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Water
- Maggi bouillon cubes or fish or meat stock
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt 100 gr (20 Kvint) of butter
2. Bake 50 gr (10 Kvint) of flour in it
3. Thin with approximately 3/4 L (1/4 Pot) of water mixed with Maggi bouillon cubes, or with fish or meat stock
4. Bind the sauce with 6 egg yolks and season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
5. Finally, add a little cold butter to it
6. The sauce must be bound just before serving
