## Ingredients

- 250 grams Pillsbury flour preferably
- 3 eggs
- 50 grams melted butter or margarine
- 250 milliliters milk
- salt
- sugar
- peeled and cooked Jerusalem artichokes
- Palmin for frying
- parsley
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour with 3 eggs, melted butter or margarine, milk, and a small amount of salt and sugar
2. Dip the peeled, cooked Jerusalem artichokes in this batter
3. Take them one by one on a fork and place them directly from the batter into hot Palmin
4. Cook until light brown
5. Place these battered Jerusalem artichokes briefly on paper before arranging on a hot plate with a napkin
6. Parsley, cooked in the Palmin, can be sprinkled over the dish
7. Serve with melted butter
