## Ingredients

- 2 kg inner loin of veal
- salt
- pepper
- brown butter
- stock or cream

## Directions

1. Cut the inner loin into smaller pieces and beat lightly with a meat mallet and lard it. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, place with the larded side down against the pan, and fry in brown butter.
2. Within about 10 minutes they are as a rule fried. The gravy that has stuck to the pan is boiled off with stock or cream until it becomes smooth.
3. The sauce can also be made from butter and flour, which is thinned with the gravy. Serve with currant jelly, compote, salad, or vegetables.
4. Alternatively: prepare beforehand a sauce of butter and flour, browned well, thin this with stock, gravy, or boiling water and Maggi bouillon cubes, add coloring, and let the meat, previously browned, simmer in it. Before serving, some mushrooms are given a boil in the sauce; this is prepared with salt, sugar (very slightly) and wine.
