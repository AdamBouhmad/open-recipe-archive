## Ingredients

- Plaice
- Salt
- Bay leaves
- Boiled potatoes
- Butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Purchase the plaice preferably alive
2. Cut off the head and remove entrails through the neck opening
3. Cut off the side and tail fins
4. Loosen the dark skin at the head and pull it off with a quick motion
5. Scrape the white skin side well to remove slime
6. Choose larger plaice for boiling
7. After cleaning, rinse well in several changes of cold water
8. Sprinkle with salt and let stand until cooking time
9. The fish can either be cut lengthwise along the backbone and divided in two, or filleted crosswise into several pieces
10. Bring to boil in salted water with some bay leaves
11. Cook until done in a few minutes
12. Arrange on a napkin and garnish with small boiled potatoes
13. Serve with melted butter and finely chopped parsley
