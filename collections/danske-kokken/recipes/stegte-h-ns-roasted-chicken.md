## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken
- butter
- gravy, stock, or water
- salt
- coloring
- sago flour

## Directions

1. Select preferably capons or young roosters. After cleaning, dry them in a cloth and lard the breast with 2-3 rows of fat.
2. Brown a good slice of butter in a pot, in which the meat is browned without becoming tough or dry.
3. Pour in only a little gravy, stock, or water with salt and coloring.
4. Roast gently under a lid and must be frequently basted.
5. Strain the gravy and reduce it until smooth, or improve it with Maggi bouillon cubes and thicken with sago flour.
6. Alternatively, serve a smooth gravy sauce to which butter and flour are added, diluted with the gravy and mixed with a little raw cream, along with compote, salad, and browned potatoes.
