## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound white sugar
- 1 lemon
- cold water
- 2 glasses white wine
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 1 lemon onto 1/4 pound white sugar; dip the sugar quickly in cold water and place in a saucepan with 2 glasses white wine, 3 whole eggs, 3 egg yolks and a little salt.
2. Beat all this well together and then set over heat, where it is constantly whisked until it is about to boil.
3. Remove the sauce and serve immediately.
