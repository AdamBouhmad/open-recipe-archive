## Ingredients

- Day-old French bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread from the previous day into slices that are not too thin.
2. Place the slices on an asbestos plate, turn them, and toast until they are light brown on both sides; the bread must be turned regularly.
3. Do not stack the toasted bread on top of each other as it will become tough; instead stand it on edge or in a stand made for this purpose.
4. Serve the bread warm, spread with butter.
