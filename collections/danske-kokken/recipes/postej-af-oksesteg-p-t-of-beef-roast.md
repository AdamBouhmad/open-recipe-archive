## Ingredients

- Puff pastry (No. 22)
- Slices of beef roast
- Warm stock sauce

## Directions

1. Line a form with a thin layer of puff pastry (see No. 22) that must be large enough to extend over the edge of the form
2. Lay slices of beef roast in the form
3. Pour a little warm stock sauce over them
4. Lay a layer of puff pastry on top
5. Fold up the hanging pastry over the lid
6. The lid can be decorated with figures cut out of pastry
7. Brush the whole with egg white
8. Place in the oven until the puff pastry is baked
9. Serve the remaining sauce in a sauce bowl
