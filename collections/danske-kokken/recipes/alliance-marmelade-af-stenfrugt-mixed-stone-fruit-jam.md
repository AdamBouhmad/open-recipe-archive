## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg powdered sugar for every 1/2 kg fruit without stones
- Various types of stone fruit such as apricots, plums, peaches, and cherries

## Directions

1. For cherries, remove the stones; for other types of fruit, scald in boiling water until the skin comes off easily
2. Halve the fruits and remove the stones, crush them, and scald the kernels before cooking them together with the fruit and sugar for about 1/2 hour
3. Alternatively, halve the fruits, remove and crush the stones, scald the kernels, and after the jam has cooked, press them through a coarse sieve
4. Add the kernels to the strained finished jam and cook for 5 minutes before filling into glasses, covering with paper, and sealing while warm
5. The kernels may be omitted
