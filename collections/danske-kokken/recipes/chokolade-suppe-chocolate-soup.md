## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound Chocolate
- 2 Quarts sweet Milk
- 3 Egg yolks
- Sugar
- a few drops good Vanilla Extract
- Egg whites (optional)

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate and cook it in 2 quarts sweet milk.
2. Stir evenly until it thickens.
3. After some time of cooking, pour it into a tureen in which 3 egg yolks have been whisked beforehand with sugar and a few drops good vanilla extract.
4. Whisk it in until the soup is poured in.
5. If desired, the egg whites can be whisked, cooked and laid on top in the tureen.
6. Serve with cooked bread or small rusks, and allow 1 pint of milk per person.
