## Ingredients

- 8 pounds beef (top round)
- Bacon for larding
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- 1 quart thin oil
- 3 pints vinegar
- White or black pepper
- Salt
- 3 to 4 bay leaves
- Cream
- 1 ounce sugar
- Juniper berries

## Directions

1. Free the meat from bone and rub with salt and ground pepper.
2. Mix oil and vinegar with some pepper grains, bay leaves, salt, and 50 juniper berries.
3. Place meat in this brine and let stand 2 to 3 days.
4. When ready to roast, rinse the meat in fresh water.
5. Lard with bacon strips rolled in salt and pepper.
6. Brown the meat well in butter in a roasting pan.
7. Add broth and roast under a lid.
8. Add sugar and cream from time to time, and baste occasionally with the sauce.
9. Thicken the sauce with a little flour, bring to a boil, strain, and serve.
