## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 plaice
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish stock made from fish bones
- Egg yolks
- Madeira or Graves wine
- Mushrooms
- Lemon
- Tomato slices

## Directions

1. Prepare a timbal mold as described in the general timbal instructions
2. Arrange cooked fish fillets high in the center of the timbal
3. Make mushroom sauce from butter and flour, thin with fish stock made from fish bones
4. Bind the sauce with egg yolks and add Madeira or Graves wine
5. Pour the mushroom sauce over the fish fillets
6. Decorate the top of the timbal with mushrooms
7. On the serving platter, arrange a wreath of fried fish fillets decorated with lemon and raw tomato slices
8. Different types of fish can be used, either as a stew or as fillets
