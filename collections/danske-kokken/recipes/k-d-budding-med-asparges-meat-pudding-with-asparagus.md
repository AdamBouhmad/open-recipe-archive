## Ingredients

- cold butter
- prepared forcemeat
- cooked asparagus

## Directions

1. Butter a pudding mold well with cold butter
2. Layer the prepared forcemeat alternately with cooked asparagus
3. The top and bottom layer must be forcemeat, which can be made of beef, veal or pork
4. Place the pudding mold in boiling water in a pot over even heat, keeping it at a boil
5. As the water reduces, continually replenish with boiling water; but ensure the water does not rise too high so that it seeps into the pudding
6. The pudding can be cooked without coals on the lid
7. It can also be cooked in the oven in a pudding or cake mold by placing it in boiling water in a roasting pan
8. The pudding can cook for 2 hours, but if placed in a good oven, it needs only 1 hour
9. Serve with asparagus sauce
