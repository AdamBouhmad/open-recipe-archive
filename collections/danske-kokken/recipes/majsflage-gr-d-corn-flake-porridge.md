## Ingredients

- ½ kg corn flakes
- 2½ L milk
- 20 bitter almonds, grated and blanched
- salt
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- fruit juice and water

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the corn flakes into the boiling, sweet milk together with 20 grated blanched bitter almonds.
2. The porridge cooks for 10 minutes, is salted and served with butter, sugar, cinnamon and fruit juice and water.
