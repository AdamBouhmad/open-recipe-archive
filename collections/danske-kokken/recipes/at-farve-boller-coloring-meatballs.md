## Ingredients

- veal forcemeat
- cooked and strained spinach
- raw beetroot
- saffron solution

## Directions

1. Meatballs made from veal forcemeat can be colored green with a little cooked and strained spinach, which is stirred into the forcemeat before the balls are cooked.
2. Color the forcemeat red by grating raw beetroot over it until the color is as desired.
3. Color it yellow by adding a little dissolved saffron in boiling water.
