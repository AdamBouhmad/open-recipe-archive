## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- cold water

## Directions

1. Scald the calf's feet, clean them and wash them well and place them overnight in lukewarm water.
2. Then place them over heat with cold water; 4 calf's feet are boiled in 4 1/2 quarts water for approximately 3 hours.
3. Skim well, then strain through an earthenware or porcelain dish and set aside until it becomes cold.
4. If there is a layer of fat on top, it must be removed before using, as well as any sediment at the bottom.
