## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet raspberry juice
- 1 pot thick, sweet cream
- 3 pounds very small macaroons the size of a ten-øre coin

## Directions

1. Blend 1 pot sweet raspberry juice with 1 pot thick, sweet cream that has been beaten until foam.
2. When this is well mixed, add 3 pounds very small macaroons the size of a ten-øre coin and blend well with the rest.
3. Freeze it.
4. Serve the ice whole on the serving dish, garnished with macaroons.
