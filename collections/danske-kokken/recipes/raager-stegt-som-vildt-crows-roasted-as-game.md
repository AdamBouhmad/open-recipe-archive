## Ingredients

- Crows
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Potatoes
- Rhubarb compote or currant jelly
- Lettuce or parsley

## Directions

1. Flay them, brown them, and roast as game (see Doves as Game, No. 914).
2. When serving, split them, garnish with lettuce or parsley, and serve with browned potatoes and rhubarb compote or currant jelly.
