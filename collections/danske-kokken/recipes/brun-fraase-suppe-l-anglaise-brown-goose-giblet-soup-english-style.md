## Ingredients

- 1/2 goose giblets
- 125 Gr (1/4 tt) Butter, Margarine or Palmin
- 2 chopped onions
- 6 L (6 Pots) boiling water
- Salt
- Kulør (caramel coloring)
- Paprika
- Sherry
- Sago flour or Butter and Flour
- Egg royal (Nr. 507)
- Puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Clean the giblets as described for Giblet Soup (Nr. 372)
2. Brown the giblets in 125 Gr (1/4 tt) butter, margarine or Palmin together with 2 chopped onions
3. Add 6 L (6 Pots) boiling water, salt, kulør and a little paprika to the pot
4. Boil the giblets 2 to 3 hours
5. When tender, remove and keep warm, strain the soup, skim off the fat, and season with Sherry
6. The soup must have a nice brown color and can be thickened either with sago flour or with butter and flour browned together and diluted with the soup
7. The giblets are eaten with the soup and can be placed in the tureen or served separately on a platter, garnished with puff pastry strips
8. Top the soup with egg royal (Nr. 507)
