## Ingredients

- cleaned rice grains
- boiling water
- strong bouillon
- butter
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse cleaned rice grains in several batches of boiling water and then place to cook in strong bouillon until they begin to burst. Bake butter and flour together, and add the grains along with a little pepper. Stir the mixture during a small boil and set aside to cool. Form roulettes from this, which are treated in the manner mentioned in No. 203. Rice roulettes are used as garnish for boiled chicken and turkey.
