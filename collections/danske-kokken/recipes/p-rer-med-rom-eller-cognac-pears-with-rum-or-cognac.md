## Ingredients

- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 3/4 dl pear water
- 1/2 kg peeled pears
- Rum or cognac

## Directions

1. Cook the whole peeled pears partially tender in water.
2. Boil sugar and water to a smooth syrup in which the pears are cooked completely tender, then remove and place in a jar.
3. The syrup is cooked until very smooth, then flavored to taste with rum or cognac, but without bringing to a boil after.
4. Pour the syrup over the pears so they are completely covered. Seal as soon as possible.
