## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons water
- Juice of one lemon or an equivalent amount of vinegar
- 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) cold butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Fish broth or cream

## Directions

1. Carefully separate the whites from the yolks, which are whisked well together with 4 tablespoons water and the juice of one lemon or an equivalent amount of vinegar
2. Pour the egg mixture into a smaller saucepan, which is placed inside a larger one filled with enough boiling water to come up the sides of the inner saucepan
3. Place over heat and stir continuously in the eggs, stirring in 1/4 Kg (1/2 S) cold butter in small portions
4. When the sauce has become smooth, salt it and add white pepper and dilute with fish broth or cream until it reaches the proper consistency
5. The sauce cannot boil in this way, and in this manner one avoids it separating
6. Stir the sauce continually until it is to be served
