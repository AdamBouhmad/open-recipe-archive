## Ingredients

- Beaten eggs
- Milk
- Swiss cheese, diced
- Watercress
- Small radishes

## Directions

1. Into the beaten eggs prepared with milk, add small dice of Swiss cheese. Spread the finished, set scrambled eggs out on a platter, and watercress garnishes the edge of the eggs. In the middle, place a wreath of very small radishes without leaves. Served with open-faced sandwiches.
