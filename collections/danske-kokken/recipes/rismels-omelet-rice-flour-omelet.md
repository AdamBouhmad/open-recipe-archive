## Ingredients

- 3 cups sweet milk
- 2 ounces butter
- 3 ounces white sugar
- 3 ounces rice flour
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Bring milk, butter, and sugar to a boil
2. Bake rice flour in the mixture
3. When cooled, add egg yolks first
4. Then fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Bake for about half an hour
6. Serves 6 persons
