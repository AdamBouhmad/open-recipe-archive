## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 S) large, red tomatoes
- 5 L (5 pots) broth
- 0.25 kg (0.5 S) rice
- salt
- 2 onions
- Sherry
- paprika
- finely chopped parsley
- fish balls, flour balls, or small pieces of leeks cooked in the soup (optional)

## Directions

1. Scald 0.25 kg (0.5 S) rice and cook it tender in water with salt as a thin porridge.
2. Cook the tomatoes together with 2 onions in some of the broth and rub them through a sieve together with the rice porridge and the remaining broth.
3. Place it over heat and bring to a boil and season with Sherry, paprika and finely chopped parsley.
4. Fish balls, flour balls, or small pieces of leeks cooked in the soup can be added to the tomato soup.
