## Ingredients

- Butter dough
- Egg yolk, beaten
- Well-washed and cooked veal sweetbread
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mushrooms, chopped
- Madeira or Sherry

## Directions

1. Roll out butter dough to 3 inches thick
2. Cut out with a round form or large wine glass
3. From a piece of the same size, cut out the center with a smaller glass to form a ring
4. Cut out a smaller third piece to form the lid
5. Brush beaten egg yolk on the bottom piece
6. Place the ring on top and brush the surface with egg (not the sides)
7. Brush the lids (baked separately) with egg as well
8. Wet the baking sheet with a little water before placing the pâtés on it
9. Bake in the oven at good heat
10. Remove the skin and membrane from the cooked veal sweetbread
11. Cut into pieces the size of a pea
12. Brown butter and flour together and thin with bouillon
13. Add salt, pepper, and chopped mushrooms
14. Add the chopped veal sweetbread
15. Bake everything together
16. Stir in a little Madeira or Sherry
17. Fill the warm pâtés with this mixture
18. Place the lids on top
19. Serve immediately
20. Allow 2 to 3 pâtés per person
