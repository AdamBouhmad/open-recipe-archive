## Ingredients

- hen
- water
- salt
- celery root
- parsley roots
- flour
- butter
- rice
- curry

## Directions

1. Cut the hen into pieces and wash in lukewarm water.
2. Boil until tender in water and salt.
3. Make a sauce from the strained cooking broth with celery root and parsley roots.
4. Thicken the sauce with flour and butter.
5. Clean half a pound of rice and cook slowly over steady heat with constant stirring.
6. Place the chicken in the sauce and arrange with a ring of cooked rice around the edge of the platter.
7. If desired, add a little curry to the sauce.
8. Alternatively, arrange the boiled hen in a mushroom sauce (see recipe 240).
