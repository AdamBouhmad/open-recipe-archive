## Ingredients

- puff pastry (see recipe 22)
- 1 egg
- sugar
- chopped almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Roll out a thin sheet of puff pastry
2. Cut out small cakes with a shot glass
3. Dip in egg and plenty of sugar
4. Bake until light brown in a well-heated oven
5. Chopped almonds can be sprinkled on the cakes, which are placed close together on the sheet
6. Best served the same day they are baked
