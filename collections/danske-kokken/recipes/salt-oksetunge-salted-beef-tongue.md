## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Brine (see Holstein Salt Brine recipe)

## Directions

1. Scald the tongue in boiling water
2. Let it lie in brine for 14 days
3. Place it over heat in boiling water
4. It is tender when the skin can be easily removed
5. Place the tongue back in the broth until cold, unless it is to be served warm
6. For serving as a cold cut, it is most economical to begin cutting from the tongue tip, as nothing goes to waste
