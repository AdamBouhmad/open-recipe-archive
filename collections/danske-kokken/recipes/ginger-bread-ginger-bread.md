## Ingredients

- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 15 grams ground ginger
- 125 grams margarine
- 375 grams cake flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 250 milliliters semi-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Mix powdered sugar with eggs, ground ginger, and margarine
2. Add cake flour and baking powder dissolved in semi-skimmed milk
3. Bake for 1 hour in a greased form
