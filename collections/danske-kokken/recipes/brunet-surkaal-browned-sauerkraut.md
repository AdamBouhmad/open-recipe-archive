## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Pepper
- Flour
- Brown syrup

## Directions

1. Rinse the sauerkraut and drain it, brown with butter over gentle heat
2. Add bouillon and pepper, then cook until done
3. Thicken with a roux of butter and flour that has been fried together, and add a little brown syrup
4. Bring to a boil
