## Ingredients

- Unpeeled potatoes
- Salt
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salad oil or melted butter
- Chopped shallot
- Pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook unpeeled potatoes in salted water and drain
2. Cut the potatoes into thin slices
3. Pour some vinegar over them and add a little salad oil or melted butter, chopped shallot, salt, pepper and sugar to taste
