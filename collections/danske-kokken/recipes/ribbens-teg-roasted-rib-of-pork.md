## Ingredients

- Rib of pork
- Pepper
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Roast on a spit for one hour.
2. Crack it crosswise over the bones in two places.
3. Rub it with pepper and salt.
4. Add only as much water from the beginning as can drip in and go over to baste it.
5. Many use only the fat drippings for sauce, prepared at table with mustard and vinegar.
