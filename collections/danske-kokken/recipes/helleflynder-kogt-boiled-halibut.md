## Ingredients

- Halibut
- Salt
- Milk
- Water
- Carrot strips
- Parsley
- Cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Scale and portion the halibut by the pound
2. Remove the dark skin as on plaice, scrape the white side well, wash the fish in several changes of cold water
3. The flayed side can be larded with fine carrot strips; turn it upwards during cooking
4. Place the fish in cold water with salt, or as many prefer, in half milk and half water, which makes it whiter
5. Garnish the fish with parsley at serving, place cooked potatoes on the sides
6. Cook for 6 minutes per ½ kg (1 lb) of fish
