## Ingredients

- 1 quart tomatoes
- 1 quart water
- 1 quart milk
- butter
- salt
- red pepper
- 1 teaspoon saleratus
- crackers

## Directions

1. Boil the tomatoes in the water
2. Heat the milk to boiling point in a bowl set over a pot of hot water
3. When the tomatoes are soft, add 1 teaspoon of saleratus, which will cause strong effervescence
4. It is best to place the pot first in a pan or other vessel to prevent spillage
5. When it has cooled again, add the milk, butter, and seasonings
6. The soup becomes better when more milk than water is used, and cream instead of butter is used
7. Serve with crackers
