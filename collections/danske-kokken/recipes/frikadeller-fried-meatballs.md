## Ingredients

- fars (minced meat mixture)
- double the amount of wheat flour compared to the mixture for balls
- fat or butter
- cold water

## Directions

1. Use the same fars as for balls, but with double the wheat flour
2. Heat a frying pan over the fire with a little fat or butter, and when it is light brown, place the frikadeller on the pan using a spoon dipped in cold water
3. Place as many on the pan as can fit without touching each other
4. When half cooked through on gentle heat, turn them carefully
5. When fully fried, place them on a dish and keep warm until ready to serve
6. When finished frying all the frikadeller, brown butter in the pan, thin with a little water and serve as sauce
