## Ingredients

- Parsley leaves
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the stems.
2. Pack the parsley tightly in a jar in layers with salt.
3. Place a piece of cloth on top, and finally a light press.
4. Before use, rinse the parsley in cold water.
5. After a couple of days, the brine that has formed can be poured off and boiled, then poured cold over the parsley, which is bound the next day.
