## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg (3 S) fresh plums
- 4 1/2 L (4 1/2 pots) water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- potato starch
- red wine or Graves

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums and cook them in 4 1/2 L (4 1/2 pots) water with sugar and cinnamon until they are cooked out; then strain them through a coarse sieve and thicken with stirred potato starch.
2. Season the soup with red wine or Graves.
