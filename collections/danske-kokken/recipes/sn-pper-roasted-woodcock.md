## Ingredients

- Woodcock (2 for 3 people)
- Hearts, gizzards and livers of the woodcock
- Thin strips of bacon

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcock, singe, remove the eyes, and pluck only the head.
2. When cleaning the birds, collect the hearts, gizzards and livers to prepare as a farce.
3. Dry the birds well inside with a cloth.
4. Cut off the feet and truss: press the thighs tight against the body and bend the head back, then insert the long pointed beak through the thigh and body and out again through the other thigh.
5. Lard or wrap the breast with thin strips of bacon.
