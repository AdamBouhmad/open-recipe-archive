## Ingredients

- lobsters
- flour
- bouillon
- lobster butter
- cooked hens
- butter
- chicken bouillon
- white wine
- sugar
- salt
- lemon zest
- egg yolks
- fish forcemeat
- salt water
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Break the lobsters and pound the shells, from which cook lobster butter, strain and set to cook with flour and dilute with bouillon; the sauce should be fairly thick.
2. Cut the lobster meat into pieces and place in it.
3. Cut cooked hens into pieces; make a sauce of butter and flour and dilute with chicken bouillon, to which add a little white wine, sugar, salt, grated lemon zest and 4 egg yolks, with which to bind the sauce.
4. Place the meat in the sauce and set to cool.
5. Cook small balls of fish forcemeat in salt and water, which are likewise set to cool.
6. Line a mold with puff pastry dough on the bottom and sides.
7. Over this place a layer of fish forcemeat, then a layer of the chicken fricassee, then a layer of the balls, then of lobster and so on until the mold is full.
8. Cover this with a layer of fish forcemeat and finally with a lid of puff pastry dough.
9. Place in a hot oven, bake for a good hour and serve with lobster sauce.
