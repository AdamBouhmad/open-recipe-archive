## Ingredients

- 8.5 to 9 kg (7 to 8 lb) veal rib section
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Egg
- Bread
- Gravy or water
- Salad or parsley
- Vegetables, compote, or salad

## Directions

1. Use the rib section, cut so that the cutlet bones are 8 to 10 centimeters (3 to 4 inches) long.
2. Remove the vertebrae and the thick membrane covering the fillet.
3. Generally allow one bone per cutlet, though individual pieces without bone can sometimes be cut.
4. Loosen and scrape the meat from the top of the bone, beat with a meat mallet and shape with a knife into a rounded form.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and roast approximately 6 to 8 minutes in browned butter over a brisk fire.
6. The cutlets can also be dipped in egg and bread.
7. When serving, place a little cold butter under them on the platter, place the bones toward the center and garnish with a bouquet of salad or parsley.
8. Pour a little gravy or water on the pan on which the cutlets were roasted to cook out the strength, which is then poured over them.
9. Serve with either vegetables, compote, or salad.
10. Paper frills can be placed around the bones of the roasted cutlets.
