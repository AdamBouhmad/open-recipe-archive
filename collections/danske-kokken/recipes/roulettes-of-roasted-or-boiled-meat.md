## Ingredients

- 2 chopped onions
- 1.5 ounces butter
- 0.5 ounces flour
- 1 pint bouillon
- 1 pound cooked, finely chopped meat
- ground pepper
- salt
- ground bread
- eggs
- parsley
- 5 rusks
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Sauté two chopped onions in 1.5 ounces butter
2. Bake 0.5 ounces flour in it and gradually add 1 pint bouillon
3. Cook until smooth, then stir in 1 pound cooked, finely chopped meat, ground pepper and salt
4. When completely cold, take a little at a time and roll in ground bread, then in well-beaten eggs, then in bread again
5. Cook them in broth together with a little whole parsley to garnish
6. About 16 roulettes are made from this portion, using 5 rusks and 4 eggs
7. For a more piquant taste, 3 chopped anchovies can be sautéed with the onions, and a little white wine, a little lemon juice and nutmeg can be added to the mixture
8. Serve with a sharp sauce and boiled potatoes or eat with vegetables
