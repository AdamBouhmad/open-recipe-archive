## Ingredients

- 125 grams macaroni
- celery
- lost mayonnaise (see recipe 244)
- tarragon vinegar
- mustard
- capers
- lean ham

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni and cut it small together with celery
2. In lost mayonnaise, stir in tarragon vinegar, mustard and 2 tablespoons of capers
3. Toss the macaroni and celery in the sauce
4. On the salad you can place strips of finely chopped lean ham, or the salad can be arranged on a platter with slices of cooked ham around it
