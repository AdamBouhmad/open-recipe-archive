## Ingredients

- raw scraped carrots
- butter
- sugar
- gravy or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
- salt
- coloring
- potato starch

## Directions

1. Cut the raw scraped carrots into not too thin slices
2. Braise them in a little browned butter and sugar on the bottom of a pot
3. Pour over them a little gravy or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
4. Add salt and coloring, but do not add more water than absolutely necessary
5. When the carrots are tender from gentle cooking, thicken the sauce with a small amount of potato starch
6. Season the vegetable with what it still needs of salt and sugar
