## Ingredients

- 1 pint green peas
- small bundle of asparagus
- 1 pint string beans
- a couple of cents carrots
- parsnip roots
- a very small cauliflower head
- 1 large beet
- 1 tablespoon salad oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Shell 1 pint of green peas
2. Use the heads of a small bundle of asparagus
3. Cut 1 pint of string beans in different forms
4. Clean a couple of cents of carrots and parsnip roots and cut them into pieces about an inch in size
5. Cut a very small cauliflower head into small florets
6. Cook all these things and then place them in cold water to preserve their color
7. Wash and cook 1 large beet without breaking the skin or cutting off the top and root
8. When tender, peel it and cut it like the carrots
9. Arrange all the vegetables on a plate and prepare with 1 tablespoon salad oil, 1 tablespoon vinegar, 1 teaspoon salt and a little pepper
