## Ingredients

- 5 pots of well-cleaned, fully developed but unripe gooseberries
- 2 pots of water
- 2 pounds of powdered sugar per pot of juice
- Vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the gooseberries with the water until they fall apart and are cooked into a pulp
2. Press this through a jelly bag
3. Cool the juice, place it on the fire again and cook and skim it for 10 minutes
4. Add 2 pounds of powdered sugar and vanilla to taste for each pot of juice
5. Cook this for half an hour and treat it afterwards in the usual manner
