## Ingredients

- Butter
- Ground meat
- Leeks cut to the height of the mold
- Water and salt
- Stirred or melted butter or brown spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Butter a loaf mold and fill it about halfway with ground meat
2. Cut leeks to the height of the mold and give them a good boil in salted water
3. Insert the leeks root-end down with 2.5 centimeters (1 inch) spacing
4. Cook the loaf as usual
5. Serve with stirred or melted butter or a brown spicy sauce
