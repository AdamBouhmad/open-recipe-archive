## Ingredients

- potato flour
- wheat flour
- butter
- milk
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- cooked flaked fish
- salt
- melted butter or caper sauce

## Directions

1. Bake a quarter pound of potato flour and a quarter pound of wheat flour with half a pound of butter and 6 pails of milk
2. Stir in 20 egg yolks which have first been stirred until pale
3. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and add them last to the mixture along with about 3 pounds of flaked cooked fish and a little salt
4. Pour the gratin into a deep dish smeared with butter and bake for an hour in the oven
5. Serve with a napkin around the dish, and serve with melted butter or caper sauce
