## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloin
- Peeled and halved apples
- Prunes
- Boiling water
- Brown gravy sauce
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut a slit in the pork tenderloin.
2. Fill it with peeled halved apples and prunes; these must first be placed in boiling water so that the pits can be pressed out better, if pitted prunes are not used.
3. Serve with brown gravy sauce and potatoes.
