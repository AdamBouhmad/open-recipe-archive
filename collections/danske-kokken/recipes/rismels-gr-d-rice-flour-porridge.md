## Ingredients

- half a pound rice flour
- 2 quarts milk
- sliced almonds (worth 5 cents), finely sliced
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- cinnamon
- vanilla (if served cold)

## Directions

1. Mix the flour in some of the cold milk, adding it little by little to prevent lumps
2. Place the rest on the fire
3. When it boils, thin the mixed flour with a little of the hot milk and then pour it into the milk while stirring vigorously back and forth
4. Add the finely sliced almonds and salt when serving
5. It is cooked for a good quarter hour and eaten with sugar, butter and cinnamon
6. This porridge is particularly delicious eaten cold; then take a little less flour and a couple of drops of vanilla, pour it into a mold from which it can be turned and eaten with fruit juice over it
7. As warm porridge without butter and almonds it is good for children
