## Ingredients

- Homemade liver pâté (see No. 259)
- Slices of French bread without crust
- Beaten egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or Claret
- Lemon
- Pickled cucumber (optional)

## Directions

1. Spread the homemade liver pâté in a good layer over slices of French bread without crust
2. Dip the spread side in beaten egg and then in breadcrumbs
3. Place the bread slices on a frying pan with boiling Claret or preferably a little butter, breadcrumb side down
4. Fry until light brown, turn briefly onto the bread side
5. Arrange on a warm platter
6. Top with a piece of lemon from which the juice is pressed as they are eaten
7. A thin slice of pickled cucumber can replace the lemon
