## Ingredients

- 5 Ounces corn starch
- 1 Quart milk
- Vanilla
- 1 Ounce white sugar
- 12 well-beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the corn starch in some of the milk
2. Heat the remaining milk with vanilla and white sugar
3. When it boils, add the corn starch mixture
4. While still on the heat, add the beaten egg whites
5. The pot or saucepan must be large enough to have good space for mixing the egg whites quickly
6. Pour immediately into the mold, from which it can be turned out after a few hours
7. For the sauce, beat 12 egg yolks with white sugar until stiff, then add 2 teaspoons cognac and a little good wine, or use wine Crème sauce or strong sweet sauce
8. Serves approximately 12 persons
