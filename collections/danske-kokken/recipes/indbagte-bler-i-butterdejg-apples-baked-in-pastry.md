## Ingredients

- 18 large fine apples
- 375 gr (3/4 S) puff pastry
- preserved fruits (strawberry, plum, or prune)
- blanched almond
- egg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the blossom and stem, and hollow out the core cavity so the apples remain whole.
2. Fill the cavity with various fruits such as a large preserved strawberry, plum, or prune (with the pit removed and a blanched almond inserted in its place), or leave empty.
3. Cover each apple completely with a thin layer of puff pastry, sealing it at the bottom near the stem, which should be turned downward on the baking sheet.
4. Brush the apples with beaten egg and sprinkle with a good layer of sugar.
5. Place on the baking sheet in a well-heated oven and bake for approximately 3/4 hour so the apples become very tender.
6. Serve very hot, placed on a napkin on a plate.
7. Whipped cream can be served separately.
