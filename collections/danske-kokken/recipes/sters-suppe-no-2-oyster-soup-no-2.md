## Ingredients

- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 quart good milk
- 1 teacup of grated crackers
- Pepper and salt to taste
- 1 quart good, fresh oysters
- 1 piece of good butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Mix one quart of boiling water in the pot with one quart of good milk; stir in one teacup of grated crackers along with a little pepper and salt to taste.
2. When it boils, add one quart of good, fresh oysters; you must stir so that it does not scorch.
3. Finally add a piece of good butter the size of an egg, and as soon as it boils up, the soup is ready and is removed from the fire.
