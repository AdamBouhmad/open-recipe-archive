## Ingredients

- Young flying-capable crows
- Parsley
- Cold butter
- New boiled potatoes
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. In early summer, flying-capable young crows taste excellent.
2. Either flay or pluck them, stuff with parsley in which a little cold butter has been placed, and roast like chickens (No. 940).
3. Split them and garnish with newly boiled potatoes sprinkled with chopped parsley.
4. Cucumber or head lettuce and compote are suitable accompaniments.
5. Crows are very inexpensive.
