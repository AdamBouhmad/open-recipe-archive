## Ingredients

- apple sauce
- crushed macarons
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Cook the apple sauce and arrange lukewarm as in the previous recipe.
2. Instead of almond paste, top with a layer of crushed macarons.
3. Top with whipped cream.
4. Do not place in the oven.
