## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 1/4 pound coarse oat grains
- sugar
- whole cinnamon stick
- lemon zest
- baked bread

## Directions

1. Place 2 quarts of water on heat
2. When it boils strongly, sprinkle 1/4 pound of coarse oat grains into it
3. When they are completely cooked, strain the soup through a fairly fine sieve so that no grains pass through, and set it back on heat
4. Add sugar to taste, a little whole cinnamon stick and lemon zest
5. When it has come to a boil, pour it into the terrine
6. Prepare with preserved juice and serve with baked bread
