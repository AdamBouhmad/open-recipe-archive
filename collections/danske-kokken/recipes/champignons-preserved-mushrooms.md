## Ingredients

- Small, closed mushrooms
- Salt (100 grams per 1/2 kilogram of mushrooms)

## Directions

1. Extract the small, closed mushrooms first in boiled water as clarified in Nr. 217, then drain them on a sieve.
2. After draining well, place them in jars; for a larger portion one can use a well-cleaned butter tub.
3. Arrange the mushrooms on blocks with caps facing up and sprinkle salt between the layers.
4. Spread a piece of linen over them, then cover with a perforated wooden bottom of appropriate size to fit the tub and place a stone or other press on top.
5. Leave in this manner in a cool place.
6. Soak the mushrooms a few hours before use, changing the water several times during this period.
7. Salted mushrooms can be preserved throughout the winter if kept covered with a press on top.
8. Some of the brine that forms can be used in Mushroom-Soya (see the Cookbook).
