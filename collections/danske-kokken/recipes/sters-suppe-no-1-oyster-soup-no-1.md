## Ingredients

- 1 quart water
- 1 teacup butter
- 1 pint milk
- 2 teaspoons coarse salt
- 0.5 teaspoon pepper
- 1 quart oysters

## Directions

1. Let the whole come to a rapid boil and then add one quart of oysters, let it boil rapidly again and then remove it from the fire.
