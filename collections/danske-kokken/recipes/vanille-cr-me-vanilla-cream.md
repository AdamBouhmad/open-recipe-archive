## Ingredients

- 1 L (1 pot) sweet milk
- 5-6 egg yolks
- 100 gr (20 quintal) powdered sugar
- 10 sheets isinglass
- 1 1/4 dl (1/2 pægl) boiling water
- vanilla flavoring to taste
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream for whipping

## Directions

1. Heat the milk and pour it in a thin stream while whisking into the egg yolks mixed with powdered sugar
2. Return the cream to the pot and whisk well until it just comes to a boil, then remove from heat quickly
3. Stir in isinglass dissolved in boiling water and vanilla flavoring to taste
4. Continue stirring until the cream is approximately cold
5. Fold in whipped cream
6. Pour into a wetted mold sprinkled with powdered sugar and chill on ice or in a cool place until ready to turn out and serve
7. Can be decorated with: whipped cream, pineapple, stewed fruits, candied fruits, sugared fruits, Russian marmalade, grapes, meringue, macaroons, small cakes, or biscuits
