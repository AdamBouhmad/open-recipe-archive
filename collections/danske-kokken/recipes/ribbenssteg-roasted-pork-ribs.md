## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- salt
- ground pepper
- apples
- prunes
- bouillon
- flour
- boiled peeled potatoes
- red cabbage

## Directions

1. Break the ribs, rub with salt and ground pepper and fill with peeled, chopped apples and prunes.
2. Sew the roast together.
3. Roast at gentle heat, and if necessary thin the sauce with a little bouillon.
4. Brown the sauce with flour and strain it.
5. Remove the thread at serving.
6. Serve with boiled, peeled potatoes and red cabbage.
