## Ingredients

- 2 pounds white sugar
- 2 fresh oranges
- 1 bottle Mosel wine
- 1 bottle white Port wine
- 1 bottle Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Dip 2 pounds white sugar in cold water and place in a tureen or punch bowl with 2 fresh cut oranges
2. Cover with 1 bottle Mosel wine
3. Let it stand for 2 hours
4. Strain it and add 1 bottle white Port wine and 1 bottle Rhine wine
5. Cool the mixture strongly in ice
