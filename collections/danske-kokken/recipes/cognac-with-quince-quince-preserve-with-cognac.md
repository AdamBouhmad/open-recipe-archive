## Ingredients

- Quince
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Vinegar
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Place over the fire together with sugar and vinegar and cook until tender, while skimming at the same time.
2. Remove the quinces and reduce the liquid further before pouring it hot over the fruit.
3. Seal as soon as the preserve has cooled.
