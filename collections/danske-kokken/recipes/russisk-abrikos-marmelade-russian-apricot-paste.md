## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) apricot pulp from jelly straining
- 0.5 kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- apricot kernels (optional)

## Directions

1. The scraps from apricot jelly are passed through a puree sieve, after which the pulp is weighed.
2. Stir the sugar into it, and place the pulp over low heat to reduce to a fairly firm mass.
3. Pour it out onto flat pans smeared with oil, dry and cut out as directed above.
4. The apricot kernels can be scalded, chopped and cooked in the jam.
