## Ingredients

- rice
- cooked cauliflower heads
- tomato sauce made with wine
- raw tomatoes
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook rice as for curry and arrange as a border on a larger dish
2. Place the rice in the oven until dry
3. Place cooked cauliflower heads inside the rice border
4. Pour a good tomato sauce made with wine over the cauliflower
5. Dip slices of raw tomato in egg and breadcrumbs mixed with pepper and salt
6. Fry the tomato slices in butter in a pan
7. Place these fried tomato slices on the rice border
