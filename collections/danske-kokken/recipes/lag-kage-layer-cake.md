## Ingredients

- 124 grams (37 quintals) margarine
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) powdered sugar
- 8 eggs
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) flour
- 5 grams (1 quintal) deer salt
- A few drops of lemon oil
- Butter for greasing paper
- Cream (see recipe 1120)
- Jam, if desired
- Water glaze
- Piped glaze and jelly for decoration

## Directions

1. Heat the margarine in the bowl where the dough will be mixed
2. Stir in the powdered sugar and eggs, and finally add the flour, deer salt, and a few drops of lemon oil
3. Spread the dough on 4 sheets of buttered and cut paper according to the size of the pan, and place on baking sheets
4. Bake the cakes quite pale, and as soon as they are done, remove the paper
5. Stack them on top of each other with cream between the layers and jam if desired
6. Trim the edges of the cake, glaze with water glaze, dry slightly in the oven, and decorate with piped glaze and jelly
