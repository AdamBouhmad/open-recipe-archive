## Ingredients

- 12 pots gooseberries, green or white glass berries preferred
- water to cover the berries well
- 10 pounds sugar (Melis)

## Directions

1. Pick the gooseberries when fully developed but not ripe.
2. Remove the flowers and stems.
3. Pour enough water over the berries so it stands well above them.
4. Cook for 1 hour.
5. Mash the berries.
6. Strain the whole mixture through a jelly bag.
7. This yields approximately 9 pots of juice.
8. Cook the juice for 1 hour with 10 pounds sugar.
9. Skim thoroughly and pour into bottles.
10. The next day, fill the cooled juice into bottles.
11. From each pot of berries, expect to get about 1 bottle of juice.
