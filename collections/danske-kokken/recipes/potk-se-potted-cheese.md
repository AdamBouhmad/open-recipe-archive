## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram (2 pounds) inexpensive dairy cheese
- 1 liter (1 pot) semi-skimmed milk
- 1/4 liter (1 pint) simple cognac
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the cheese into smaller pieces and place in a crock
2. Pour 1 liter (1 pot) semi-skimmed milk over the cheese and add 1/4 liter (1 pint) simple cognac
3. Stir the potted cheese at intervals of several days and ensure it does not become dry; if it does, pour more milk on top
4. After 6 to 8 weeks you can try to press it through a coarse sieve, and what is not yet dissolved is poured back into the crock
5. The contents can be increased over the year with what remains of cheese scraps and regularly topped up with milk and occasionally with 1 glass of cognac
6. When the potted cheese has been pressed through the sieve, season it with a little salt and sugar and, if it is too thin, let it stand a few days
