## Ingredients

- Small green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Soda (in small amount)
- Salt solution (10 gr or 2 Kvint per 1 liter)

## Directions

1. Pat dry or rinse the beans; remove the ribs along the back and belly seams
2. Place in boiling water with salt and a slight amount of soda and cook for 5 minutes
3. Remove and pour cold water over them, let cool and place in jars
4. Pour over them a cold solution of 10 gr (2 Kvint) salt to 1 liter water, close the jars and place in a pot with cold water
5. Cook for 30 to 35 minutes and cool
