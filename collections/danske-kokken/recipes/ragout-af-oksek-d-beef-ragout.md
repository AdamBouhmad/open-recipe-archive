## Ingredients

- 2 pounds round beef
- 2 ounces flour
- Butter
- 1 carrot
- 3 small onions
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon pepper
- Boiling water
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut 2 pounds round beef into square pieces, 2 inches on each side, and brown in butter.
2. Stir in 2 ounces flour and brown it.
3. Cut 1 carrot into pieces about 1 inch in size and peel 3 small onions, and make a bouquet garni.
4. Add these ingredients to the meat along with 1 tablespoon salt, 0.5 teaspoon pepper and boiling water until it covers the meat.
5. Simmer until the bouquet garni is soft, then remove it.
6. Taste and adjust salt if needed.
7. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon chopped parsley over it, or arrange some slices of fried bread around the dish when serving.
