## Ingredients

- 1 Pint Cream
- Pepper, preferably white
- Shell of half a small Lemon
- 2 to 3 Ounces Butter
- 1 Tablespoon Flour
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Boil 1 Pint Cream very gently with a little crushed Pepper, preferably white, and the Shell of half a small Lemon.
2. When it has boiled a little, add a Thickening of 2 to 3 Ounces Butter (depending on how thick the Cream is), stirred with 1 Tablespoon Flour.
3. Stir constantly until it has become thick, and then thin it with Bouillon until the Sauce has obtained a suitable Thickness.
4. When serving, press the Juice of half a Lemon into it.
5. Good Milk may be used instead of Cream, but then use more Butter.
6. Instead of Lemon Shell and Juice, cook so much strong Broth or good Soy in the Sauce that it gets a reddish Colour.
7. For 8 to 10 Persons.
