## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg macaroni
- Salt
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into short pieces and place in plenty of boiling salted water or in milk, cooking until tender but not overcooked.
2. Drain through a colander and transfer to a serving dish.
3. Pour melted butter over the macaroni.
4. Serve grated Parmesan cheese separately.
