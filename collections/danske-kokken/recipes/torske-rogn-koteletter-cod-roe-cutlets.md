## Ingredients

- cooked cod roe
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- butter or Palmin (cooking fat)
- lemon
- pickled cucumber
- tomato slices
- cucumber salad

## Directions

1. The roe should be cooked, and the membrane is removed from the cold roe, which is cut into slices, not too thin, dipped in egg and breadcrumbs mixed with salt and pepper, and fried in butter or Palmin.
2. Home-cooked cod roe is treated the same way and served freshly fried on open-faced sandwiches, just as one serves fried fish.
3. Top with lemon, pickled cucumber, or tomato slices topped with cucumber salad.
