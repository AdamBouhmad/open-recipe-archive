## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar per pound of fruit
- Nearly 1 cup water per pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the fruit, free it from the cores and cut it into slices.
2. Place it layer by layer in the pot with the addition of nearly 1 cup water per pound of sugar.
3. As soon as it has just begun to boil, take up the fruit and spread it on platters exposed to the sun.
4. The syrup continues to boil and simmer for half an hour. Then add the fruit again and let it boil for a quarter hour.
5. Then put the fruit in crocks and pour the boiling syrup over it. Cover it with a cloth until it is cold, then place paper soaked in cognac over it, and tie up the crocks.
