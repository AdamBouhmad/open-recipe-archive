## Ingredients

- 4 pots sweet milk
- A couple of pieces of lemon peel
- One-quarter pound chocolate
- 12 egg yolks, whisked
- 1 pot cream
- Powdered sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Boil 4 pots of sweet milk with a couple of pieces of lemon peel.
2. Grate one-quarter pound of chocolate and dissolve it in 12 whisked egg yolks and 1 pot of cream; add powdered sugar to taste.
3. When the milk boils, pour this mixture into it with continuous whisking and stirring until the whole comes to a boil again.
