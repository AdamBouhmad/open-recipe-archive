## Ingredients

- 75 grams (15 quintals) rice
- ½ liter (½ pot) half-skimmed milk
- 100 grams (20 quintals) coarsely chopped or ground sweet almonds
- 15 sheets isinglass
- ¼ dl (¼ pægel) sherry
- vanilla essence
- 3 to 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 liter (1 pot) heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in half-skimmed milk.
2. Stir in the coarsely chopped almonds along with isinglass dissolved in sherry, vanilla essence, and powdered sugar.
3. When the pudding has cooled somewhat, stir in whipped cream made from the heavy cream.
4. Pour the rice pudding into the glass bowl in which it is to be served.
5. Place in a cold location for a few hours before eating.
6. Cold sauce made from cherry jam is particularly good with this.
