## Ingredients

- 4.5 chickens
- butter
- 1 liter of spare broth, gravy, or water
- salt
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- 5 to 6 tablespoons tomato puree
- mushrooms, canned or fresh
- paprika
- 1.25 deciliters Madeira or sherry
- color for the sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the chickens and cut them into 6 pieces.
2. Brown them in a slice of butter and cover with 1 liter of spare broth, gravy, or water mixed with salt and Maggi bouillon cubes.
3. Stir in 5 to 6 tablespoons of tomato puree and some canned or fresh mushrooms, paprika, 1.25 deciliters of Madeira or sherry, and color to give the sauce a beautiful brown color.
4. Cook the chickens until tender in this mixture.
