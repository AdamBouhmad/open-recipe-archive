## Ingredients

- 625 g rice
- 5½ L semi-skimmed milk
- 3 bottles wheat beer
- salt

## Directions

1. When the milk boils, stir in the grains after they have been rinsed in cold water and then blanched in boiling water.
2. Only stir the porridge until it boils; then put on the lid and place the pot over very low heat so it just maintains a boil.
3. A quarter hour before serving, check the porridge; if it is too stiff, thin it with boiling milk, stirring it in gently along with salt.
4. The porridge cooks for 1½ hours.
5. Leftover porridge can be used for ale porridge, rice grain fritters or a cold rice dessert with fruit.
