## Ingredients

- Meat farce
- Butter
- Green vegetables
- Sharp sauce
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. The farce should be as firm as for meatballs, and from it fry small round cakes in brown butter.
2. Place them on the pan with a spoon, dipping the spoon in warm water each time so the dough slips off.
3. Serve with green vegetables or with sharp sauce and potatoes.
