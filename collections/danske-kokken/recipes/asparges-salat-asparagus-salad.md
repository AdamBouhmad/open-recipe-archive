## Ingredients

- Peeled asparagus
- Water
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) butter
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt
- Asparagus water

## Directions

1. Peel and cut asparagus small and cook tender in a small amount of water.
2. Set aside until cold.
3. Cream 125 grams butter and mix in 2 tablespoons oil, a little vinegar, pepper, salt and enough asparagus water to make the sauce properly smooth.
4. Turn finely cut chicken, veal or lamb meat together with the asparagus in the sauce.
5. Alternatively: turn the finely cut, cold asparagus in a true or false mayonnaise, top with lobster meat and garnish with salad leaves.
