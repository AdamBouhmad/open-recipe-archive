## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg apples
- 5 L water
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- rice flour
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Use sour apples, do not peel but divide in 4 pieces removing blossom, stem and core
2. Cook in 1 L water with cinnamon and lemon peel, then press through a coarse sieve with the remaining 4 L water
3. Thicken with rice flour mixed with water and cook at least 5 minutes
4. Season with wine and sweeten to taste
