## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Egg
- Bread
- Parsley
- Shallots
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Gravy
- Potatoes cooked in clarified butter

## Directions

1. Dip the cutlets first in egg, then in a mixture of crushed bread, chopped parsley, shallots, salt, and pepper and roast in butter.
2. Cook the pan with gravy, which is poured over the cutlets.
3. Garnish with potatoes cooked in clarified butter.
