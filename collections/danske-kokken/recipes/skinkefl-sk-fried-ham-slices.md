## Ingredients

- slices from a smoked ham
- fat bacon or butter
- flour
- pepper
- onion
- boiled peeled potatoes

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from slices cut from a smoked ham.
2. Beat the slices a little with a mallet and soak in water for a couple of hours.
3. Take them out and let the water drain off.
4. Add a little fat bacon or butter to the pan, depending on whether the ham is fatty or lean.
5. Turn the pieces in flour and sprinkle with a little pepper.
6. Add onion, sliced as for beef, in abundance.
7. Fry brown and serve with boiled, peeled potatoes.
