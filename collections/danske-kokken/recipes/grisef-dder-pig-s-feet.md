## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- salt
- whole pepper
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Scald the pig's feet, scrape them, clean them carefully and place them over the fire in cold water with salt, whole pepper and a couple of bay leaves
2. The feet must cook for at least 3 to 4 hours under a lid to be properly tender
3. Remove them, split them lengthwise, arrange them on a platter and pour the strained broth over them
4. Serve with spices as for aspic
5. If served in aspic, simply add isinglass as in recipe No. 263
6. They can also be cooked, boned and used as calf's head for mock turtle
