## Ingredients

- 100 very small gherkins
- Strong salt brine
- 1 liter (1 pot) vinegar
- 125 grams (1/4 skålpund) powdered sugar (Melis)
- About 20 small peeled shallots
- 2 pods Spanish pepper
- 5 pieces long pepper
- 1 tablespoon whole yellow mustard

## Directions

1. Place the very small gherkins in strong salt brine
2. Leave until the next day, then remove and dry
3. Place in jars or crocks
4. Boil the vinegar with powdered sugar, small peeled shallots, Spanish pepper pods, long pepper, and whole yellow mustard
5. Pour the boiling vinegar over the gherkins
6. Cover with cloth and bind with parchment paper the next day
