## Ingredients

- 1 pound gluten flour
- 4 whole eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons vinegar
- 3.5 measures boiled, cooled water
- 1 pound fine rye flour

## Directions

1. Work 1 pound gluten flour, 4 whole eggs, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon salt and 2 teaspoons vinegar together.
2. Stir in 3.5 measures boiled, cooled water, adding it little by little.
3. Finally work in 1 pound fine rye flour.
4. Knead well until the dough releases from the hands.
5. Form 4 small, oblong loaves and bake at good, even heat for approximately 2 hours.
6. When removed from the oven, cover them and let them cool.
