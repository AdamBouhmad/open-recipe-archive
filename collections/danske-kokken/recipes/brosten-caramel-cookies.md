## Ingredients

- ¾ pound butter
- 1 pound finely ground powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 stick ground vanilla
- 1 pound potato flour
- 6 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Clarify ¾ pound butter and stir well
2. Add 1 pound finely ground powdered sugar and stir well for 1 hour
3. Add 6 egg yolks while stirring constantly
4. Add 1 stick ground vanilla
5. Add 1 pound potato flour
6. Finally add 6 beaten egg whites
7. Bake at good heat in small forms
