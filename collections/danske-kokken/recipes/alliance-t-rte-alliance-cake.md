## Ingredients

- 9 whole eggs
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 3 quarter-pounds blanched sweet almonds, finely ground
- 10 quintals bitter almonds, finely ground
- half pound washed butter
- half pound flour
- 1 egg
- a little white rum
- a little crushed rusks
- vanilla cream
- jam

## Directions

1. Beat the 9 whole eggs well for a quarter hour, then beat again for a quarter hour with 1 pound powdered sugar and lastly with 3 quarter-pounds finely ground sweet and 10 quintals finely ground bitter almonds.
2. Make a butter dough (see No. 186) from half pound washed butter, half pound flour, 1 egg and a little white rum.
3. Line the form with this butter dough, then sprinkle with a little crushed rusks.
4. On the bottom of the form, lay a layer of vanilla cream, upon that a thin layer of crushed rusks; then a layer of jam, so a thin layer of crushed rusks, after which fill the form with the aforementioned mixed dough.
5. Bake immediately at even good heat.
6. Test by inserting a straw; when it comes out dry, the cake is sufficiently baked.
7. When it has rested a little, remove it from the form.
