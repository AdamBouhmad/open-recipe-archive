## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- 4 Ounces sugar
- 3 Ounces corn starch
- 1 Teaspoon vanilla or lemon flavoring
- 1 Quart milk

## Directions

1. Mix together the eggs, sugar, corn starch, and vanilla or lemon
2. Gradually add the milk
3. Heat over the stove until nearly boiling
4. Pour into cups and set in a baking pan half full of water
5. Place the pan in a moderately warm oven and bake for half an hour until firm and light brown
6. May be eaten warm from the cups or turned out and eaten cold
