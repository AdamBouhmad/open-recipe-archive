## Ingredients

- Pheasant
- Fresh bacon for larding
- Veal forcemeat
- Truffles, chopped
- Sauce as for roasted pheasant

## Directions

1. Bone the pheasant as directed for turkey
2. Lard the pheasant and fill with a good veal forcemeat to which chopped truffles may be added
3. Sew it closed so that none of the forcemeat comes out during roasting
4. Preferably roast in the oven, basting with a sauce prepared as for roasted pheasant
5. If there are truffles in the forcemeat, small pieces may be added to the sauce
