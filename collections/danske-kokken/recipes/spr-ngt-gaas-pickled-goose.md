## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned goose with head, neck, wings, and legs removed
- 2 to 3 large handfuls of salt
- 1 good teaspoon of ground saltpeter
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- water for boiling
- vegetables for serving

## Directions

1. Rub the goose well inside and outside with salt mixed with ground saltpeter and sugar.
2. Set the goose in a cool place for 2 to 3 days.
3. Rinse the goose when ready to cook.
4. Place in boiling water and cook for approximately 2 hours.
5. Serve with vegetables. Use the broth for peas or cabbage.
