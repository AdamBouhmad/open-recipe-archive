## Ingredients

- carrots
- butter
- flour
- vegetable cooking water
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- cold butter
- Maggi seasoning

## Directions

1. Wash the carrots and cook them
2. Scrape and cut them preferably into slices with a fluted vegetable knife
3. Make a sauce of butter and flour, diluted with the vegetable cooking water
4. Thicken with egg yolks, 1 or 2 depending on portion size
5. Add lemon juice, chopped parsley, a piece of cold butter, and a few drops of Maggi seasoning
6. Serve with fried and boiled meat dishes
