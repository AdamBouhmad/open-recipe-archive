## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound melted butter
- 1/4 pound potato starch (or half potato starch and half wheat flour)
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Stir the potato starch (or flour mixture) into the melted butter and bake as described for Melboller
2. Stir in 6 eggs, one egg at a time
