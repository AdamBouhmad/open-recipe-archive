## Ingredients

- 2.5 pounds fine wheat flour
- 1/2 pound butter
- 5 eggs
- 15 cups cold, sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons yeast
- 1/2 pound sugar
- washed raisins
- finely cut citron peel
- a little cardamom
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Knead 2.5 pounds fine wheat flour, 1/2 pound butter, 5 eggs, 15 cups cold sweet milk, and 2 tablespoons yeast well together the evening before the wreath is to be baked.
2. Wrap the dough in a linen cloth and let it lie in cold water overnight.
3. The next morning, knead in 1/2 pound sugar, washed raisins, finely cut citron peel, and a little cardamom.
4. Shape as a wreath, brush with melted butter, and sprinkle with powdered sugar before baking.
