## Ingredients

- 7 egg yolks
- half a cup butter
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons water
- half a teaspoon salt
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 7 egg yolks first with half a cup butter, 2 cups white sugar, 2 cups flour, 2 tablespoons baking powder, 2 tablespoons water, and half a teaspoon salt
2. Finally add the whipped egg whites
3. Bake in pie pans in a hot oven
4. For the filling: stir together 1 egg, 1 cup sugar, 3 grated apples, and 1 lemon until it boils and becomes thick, then cool before placing between each cake layer
5. When served, sprinkle with white sugar
