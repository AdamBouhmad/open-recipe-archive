## Ingredients

- 2 medium red cabbage heads
- Butter or lard
- Salt
- Sugar
- Vinegar (beet or regular)
- Caraway seeds (optional)
- Currant or cherry juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and cut the cabbage in quarters and slice into fine strips, without including any of the core.
2. Melt a slice of butter or lard on the bottom of a pot, add a little salt and sugar.
3. Pour in several spoonfuls of beet vinegar or regular vinegar.
4. Steam the cabbage until tender in this for at least 2 hours, the last part without a lid and with regular stirring.
5. At serving time, season to taste with more vinegar, sugar, salt, and butter.
6. Many prefer to cook a little caraway seed with the cabbage and season it with currant or cherry juice; however, the juice is unnecessary, as the cabbage can be equally delicious without it.
