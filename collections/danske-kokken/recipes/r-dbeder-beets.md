## Ingredients

- Beets
- Salt
- Powdered sugar
- Caraway seeds, optional
- Vinegar, diluted

## Directions

1. Rinse the beets and cook in water, or place in a hay box to soften, which takes several hours
2. While warm, rub the skin off lightly by hand
3. Best results come from placing them on a dry pan and roasting in the oven until the skin can be easily rubbed off
4. As they are sliced, place them in a jar and sprinkle with powdered sugar and optionally a little caraway seeds
5. Finally pour diluted raw vinegar over them
6. The jar should preferably stand uncovered but with plenty of vinegar over the beets
7. Serve with roasted meat and use frequently in salads
