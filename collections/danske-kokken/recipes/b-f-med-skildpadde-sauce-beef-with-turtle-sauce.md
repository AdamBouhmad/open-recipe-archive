## Ingredients

- Beef
- Turtle sauce
- Stock from roasted bones for cooking vegetables
- Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- Small fish balls

## Directions

1. A good turtle sauce can be made as recipe No. 9.
2. For this, stock from roasted bones can be used for cooking the vegetables.
3. Arrange over the beef, which is garnished with sliced hard-boiled eggs and small fish balls.
