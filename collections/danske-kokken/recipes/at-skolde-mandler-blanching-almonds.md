## Ingredients

- almonds
- hot water
- cold water

## Directions

1. After the almonds have been in hot water for a little while, the brown skin can be slipped off with the fingers.
2. Rinse them in cold water and dry them in a cloth before grinding or grating.
3. Bitter almonds should always be ground fine.
