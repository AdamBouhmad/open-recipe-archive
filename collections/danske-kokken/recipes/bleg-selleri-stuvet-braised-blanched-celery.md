## Ingredients

- Cooked celery stalks, cut in pieces
- Butter
- Flour
- Celery cooking water
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Egg yolks
- Raw cream
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pour a white, smooth sauce made of butter, flour and celery water with Maggi Bouillon cubes over the cut cooked stalks
2. Thicken the sauce with egg yolks beaten with raw cream
3. Add salt and a little sugar to taste
