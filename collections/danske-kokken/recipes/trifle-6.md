## Ingredients

- 1 Pound Macaroons with some bitter almonds
- 1 Pint good wine
- Jam
- Whipped cream
- Vanilla
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Place the macaroons in a deep dish, not too crowded, and cover with 1 pint good wine
2. Let them stand covered for one hour, turning them after half the time
3. Remove them from the wine and layer them tightly in the dish in which they shall be served
4. Cover with jam and then with stiff whipped cream mixed with vanilla and sugar
