## Ingredients

- Pork shoulder with the fat layer
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth from roasting
- Paprika
- Pickles
- Sherry
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Mashed potatoes
- Onions
- Butter for browning onions
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Roast the shoulder with the fat layer, scoring the rind as on roast pork belly, and roast it like that in the oven. The rind must be crispy.
2. Make a good brown sauce from butter, flour, and broth, seasoned with paprika, chopped pickles, a little sherry, and Maggi bouillon cubes.
3. Slice the roast, then arrange the slices together and place them in the center of the platter; around it, garnish with mashed potatoes sprinkled with onions, sliced and browned in butter.
4. Decorate the dish with parsley. Serve the sauce separately.
