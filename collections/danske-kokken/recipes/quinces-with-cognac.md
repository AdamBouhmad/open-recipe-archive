## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg pear quinces, peeled
- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Peel the pear quinces, cut away the cores, and cut the fruit into 8 pieces, which are placed in cold water as needed.
2. Boil the quinces partly soft in water, remove them, place on a sieve, and only when the water has drained well, pierce each piece with a thinly cut strip.
