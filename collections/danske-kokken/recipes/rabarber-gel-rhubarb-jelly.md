## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg rhubarb stalks
- 625 gr sugar
- 1.25 dl water per 0.5 kg fruit juice

## Directions

1. Wipe the stalks and cut them into smaller pieces, which are cooked through over very low heat, after which the mass is poured into the jelly cloth.
2. The strained juice is weighed and cooked into jelly. (See No. 93.)
