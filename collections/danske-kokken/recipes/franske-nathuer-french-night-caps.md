## Ingredients

- 2 French breads
- half-skimmed milk
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- bread crusts
- butter or Palmin
- fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from a French bread.
2. Split them, dip in half-skimmed milk and let stand covered for a couple of hours, before they are dipped in egg and bread crumbs mixed with the removed crusts.
3. Fry them in a pan in butter or Palmin and serve hot with fruit sauce.
