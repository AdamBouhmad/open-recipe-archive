## Ingredients

- cold veal roast, sliced medium thickness
- butter
- eggs, beaten
- crushed biscuits

## Directions

1. Cut cold veal roast into moderately thick slices
2. Warm these in butter that is very light brown, then remove them from the pan and drain off the butter
3. Brown fresh butter and dip the pieces in beaten eggs, then in crushed biscuits
4. Brown them in the fresh butter, and when they are turned, the first butter can be used to help
5. Serve with browned butter and greens
