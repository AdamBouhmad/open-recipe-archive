## Ingredients

- Pie dough
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- Yolk of 3 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- Beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix well together the grated zest and juice of 1 lemon with 1 cup sugar and the yolk of 3 eggs.
2. Stir well.
3. In 2 cups flour, stir 1 tablespoon corn starch; bring to the heat and let it just come to a boil.
4. Stir it into the other ingredients and pour into a pie form lined with good dough.
5. When baked, brush the egg whites on, beaten with sugar.
