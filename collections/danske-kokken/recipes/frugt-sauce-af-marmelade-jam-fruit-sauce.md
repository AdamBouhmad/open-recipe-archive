## Ingredients

- Preserved fruit jams (cherry, strawberry, raspberry, blackberry, or mulberry)
- Boiling water
- Potato or tapioca starch

## Directions

1. Make a thickening of boiling water and potato or tapioca starch.
2. Add the jam to this mixture.
3. The jam should preferably not boil, as the berries will lose in taste and appearance.
