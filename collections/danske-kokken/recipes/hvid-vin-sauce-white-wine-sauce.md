## Ingredients

- 1/2 bottle Rhine wine or Graves
- 125 grams (1/4 pound) powdered sugar
- 5 egg yolks (whites carefully separated)

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 bottle Rhine wine or Graves into a fairly large saucepan with 125 grams powdered sugar and 5 egg yolks, with the whites carefully separated.
2. Set the sauce over heat and whisk strongly with a whisk until it is about to boil.
3. Then lift it quickly off and serve immediately.
