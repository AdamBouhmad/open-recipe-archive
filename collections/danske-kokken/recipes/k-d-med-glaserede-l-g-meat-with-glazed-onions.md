## Ingredients

- Cut meat
- Brown stock sauce
- Glazed onions (see No. 671)
- Potato croquettes (see No. 14)

## Directions

1. Warm the cut meat in a brown stock sauce
2. Place on a hot, flat dish
3. Garnish with glazed onions (see No. 671) and potato croquettes (see No. 14)
