## Ingredients

- Calf tongues
- Water
- Salt
- Larding bacon
- Butter
- Broth, soup, or water
- Maggis Bouillon cubes
- Food coloring
- Madeira
- Tapioca flour
- Water
- Optional: mushrooms, pearl onions, shallots, or lemon slices
- Bread croutons or sliced bread

## Directions

1. Boil the tongues in water just enough so the skin can be removed.
2. Lard the tongues and brown in butter.
3. Pour over as much broth, soup, or water with Maggis Bouillon cubes as needed for the sauce.
4. Give the sauce a nice color with food coloring.
5. Let the tongues simmer gently and baste frequently until they are tender.
6. If desired, add Madeira to the sauce.
7. Thicken the sauce with tapioca flour mixed in water.
8. To make it more piquant, add mushrooms, pearl onions, shallots, or lemon slices.
9. When serving, pour some of the sauce over the tongues and garnish with bread croutons or sliced bread.
