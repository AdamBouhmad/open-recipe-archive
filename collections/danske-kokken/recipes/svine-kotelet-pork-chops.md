## Ingredients

- pork chops with bone
- salt
- ground pepper
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the chops from each other so that a bone follows with each one.
2. Beat them well with a meat mallet.
3. Sprinkle with salt and a little ground pepper.
4. Dip in beaten eggs.
5. Turn in grated bread.
6. Fry golden brown in butter.
7. Serve with mashed potatoes.
