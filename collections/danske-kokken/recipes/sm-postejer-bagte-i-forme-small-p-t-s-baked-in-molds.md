## Ingredients

- Butter pastry
- Peas for filling during baking
- Ragout filling (as previously mentioned)
- Egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Line small fluted or smooth molds with thinly rolled butter pastry
2. Fill with peas during baking to prevent collapse
3. Cut lids the size of the molds with a pastry wheel and bake them separately until light brown
4. When the pâtés are baked, drain the peas
5. Invert the mold bottom upward and release the pâtés easily from the molds
6. Fill with any of the previously mentioned ragouts
7. Place the lid on top and brush the pâté with egg
8. Return to the oven to dry and gain gloss
9. Arrange on a platter with a napkin
10. These pâtés can also be used for a cold buffet
