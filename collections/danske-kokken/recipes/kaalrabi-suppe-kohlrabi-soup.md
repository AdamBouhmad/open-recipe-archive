## Ingredients

- Kaalrabi (kohlrabi)
- Bouillon
- Fresh butter
- Sugar
- Salt
- Flour
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove all hard parts
2. Cut kohlrabi into crescent-shaped pieces and clean them
3. Place over heat with bouillon, fresh butter, a little sugar and salt
4. Cook until tender
5. Thicken soup with flour and chopped parsley
6. Add a dash of seasoning
7. Serve with veal or lamb cutlets
