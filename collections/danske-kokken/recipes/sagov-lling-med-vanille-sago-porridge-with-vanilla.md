## Ingredients

- 4 pots sweet milk
- A couple of cinnamon sticks
- A little lemon peel
- A vanilla stick
- Half a pound small, white sago grains, cleaned
- Salt to taste
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Bring 4 pots of sweet milk with cinnamon sticks, lemon peel, and vanilla stick to a boil over heat.
2. Simultaneously, whisk in half a pound of small, white sago grains as the milk comes to a boil.
3. The porridge cooks thereafter for approximately 3 quarters of an hour, until the grains are tender.
4. Add salt and sugar to taste.
5. Do not serve this porridge too hot.
