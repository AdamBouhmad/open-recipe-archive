## Ingredients

- Bavarian sausages
- Eggs
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Arrange the scrambled eggs on a platter and garnish with the sausages, which have been cooked for at most 5 minutes.
2. Sprinkle the scrambled eggs with chopped parsley.
3. The sausages can also be roasted in butter in the pan.
