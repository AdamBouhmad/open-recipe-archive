## Ingredients

- Leftover roast, ham, and soup meat
- Boiled potatoes
- Onions
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Peppercorns
- Bay leaves
- Worcestershire sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut leftover meat into cubes.
2. Cut boiled potatoes into pieces.
3. Peel and chop onions.
4. Mix together equal amounts of meat cubes, potato pieces, and chopped onions.
5. Brown the onions in butter in a casserole.
6. Add the meat and fry for approximately 5 minutes.
7. Add the potatoes and a little bouillon with salt, whole peppercorns, and a few bay leaves.
8. Cook everything together until it forms an even mass.
9. Before serving, stir in a lump of butter.
10. A little Worcestershire sauce can be added for a sharper taste.
