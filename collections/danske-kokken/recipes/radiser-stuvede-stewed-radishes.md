## Ingredients

- Radishes
- Water
- Salt
- Beef broth or Maggi bouillon cubes
- Butter
- Flour
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the radishes and cook them for a few minutes in salted water, or in thin beef broth or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
2. Make a sauce from butter, flour, and the broth
3. Thicken it and prepare it with salt and finely chopped parsley
4. Serve with roasted meat dishes
