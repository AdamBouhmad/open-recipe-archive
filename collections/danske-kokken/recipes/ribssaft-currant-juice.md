## Ingredients

- Currants
- 4 kg (1/2 S) powdered sugar or Melis per 1 liter (1 Pot) juice
- 1 small teaspoon salicylic acid per 4 liters (4 Pots) juice

## Directions

1. Remove only the green leaves from the currants; the berries need not be stemmed.
2. Wring the berries in a cloth, but to do this more easily, place them in the preserving pot set over low heat until the berries burst.
3. Measure the juice and place it in the pot together with the sugar and boil for a quarter of an hour.
4. Stir 1 small teaspoon of salicylic acid per 4 liters (4 Pots) juice into the warm juice.
5. Pour the juice into bottles and bind them immediately with parchment paper.
