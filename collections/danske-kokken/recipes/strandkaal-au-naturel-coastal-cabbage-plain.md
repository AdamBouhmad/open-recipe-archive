## Ingredients

- Coastal cabbage stalks with leaves
- Water
- Salt
- Butter or hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Rinse the stalks with leaves.
2. Split lengthwise if needed.
3. Cook immediately in water with salt under a tight-fitting lid.
4. Many use milk to cook them in.
5. Arrange on a napkin like asparagus, but as hot as possible.
6. Serve with stirred butter or hollandaise sauce.
