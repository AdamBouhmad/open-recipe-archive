## Ingredients

- trout
- warm vinegar
- white paper
- napkin

## Directions

1. Lay the trout on a wet kitchen board and do not scrape it; remove the innards, being careful not to touch them too much with your hand so as not to lose the slime that produces the blue color.
2. Rinse in cold water, place on a dish, and pour warm vinegar over them, which will make them blue.
3. Cover with a clean piece of white paper and a napkin over it, and let them stand in the vinegar for about 1 hour.
