## Ingredients

- Calf tongues
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or claret
- Lemons
- Madeira broth, slightly thickened
- Compote of dried or fresh fruit
- Lettuce or parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the calf tongues, split them, bread them, and brown in butter or claret.
2. Place a star of lemons, cut into four pieces, in the center of the plate; the lemon juice is eaten with the meat.
3. Serve with slightly thickened Madeira broth.
4. Serve with compote of dried or fresh fruit.
5. Garnish the dish with lettuce or parsley.
