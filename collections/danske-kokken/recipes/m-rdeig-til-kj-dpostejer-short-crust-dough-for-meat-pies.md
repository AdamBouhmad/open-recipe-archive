## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 pint sweet cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter free of salt and stir until quite white with 5 eggs and 1 pint sweet cream.
2. When well mixed, add enough flour to make a suitably thick dough.
3. Set on a cold place to stiffen.
4. This mixture can be used for all kinds of meat pies.
