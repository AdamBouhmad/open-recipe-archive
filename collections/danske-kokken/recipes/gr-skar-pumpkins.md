## Ingredients

- Mature pumpkins
- Well water
- Brine

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkins, hollow them out and cut into strips of the thickness of cucumbers and the length of a finger
2. Place in well water for 24 hours
3. Remove and place in brine for 24 hours
4. Place between two boards with a light press for a few hours so the brine can drain out
5. Dry thoroughly and treat and preserve as with cucumbers
