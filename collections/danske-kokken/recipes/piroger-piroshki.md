## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound cooked beef, preferably
- Onion
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth or light gravy
- Anchovy, finely chopped, optional
- Wine, optional
- 1 pound flour
- 3/4 pound butter
- 4 tablespoons thick cream
- 3 eggs
- 1 shot of rum

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked beef finely with a little onion and 2 tablespoons flour
2. Brown a piece of butter and add the chopped meat along with salt, pepper, and broth or light gravy
3. Cook together until it forms a thick hash
4. Optionally add finely chopped anchovy and some wine
5. Make a dough from 1 pound flour and 3/4 pound butter, worked together well
6. Stir in 4 tablespoons thick cream, 3 eggs, and a shot of rum with a spoon
7. Work it until it releases from the hands
