## Ingredients

- Kohlrabi
- Milk
- Butter
- Flour
- Parsley
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut peeled kohlrabi into elongated strips.
2. Stew on the same manner as asparagus.
3. Add a little finely chopped parsley to the milk sauce.
4. Serve mainly with salted meat dishes.
