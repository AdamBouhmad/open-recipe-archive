## Ingredients

- Sauce (as per Nr. 13)
- Raw oysters
- Cooked cod
- Lemon slices
- Watercress

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as Nr. 13.
2. Cut the raw oysters in half and add them along with a little cooked cod to the sauce when it is cold.
3. Place some whole oysters and lemon slices on top of the salad.
4. Garnish with a little watercress.
