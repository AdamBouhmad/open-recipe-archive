## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) lemon or orange peel
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the peel from the fruit in 2 or 4 pieces and soak them in cold water for 5 days, changing the water daily.
2. After this time, place them over heat in cold water and cook them gently under a lid, usually for almost 1/2 hour.
3. Remove the peels and cut away all the white part before weighing.
4. Boil the sugar into a thin syrup with 1/2 l (1/2 pot) water, which is only poured over the peels once it has cooled, and let them sit covered for 2 to 3 days.
5. Pour the syrup into a pot and boil it until smooth together with the juice of 1 lemon.
6. Finally, give the peels one boil in it before placing them in the jars.
7. Reduce the syrup again until it is completely smooth, pour it boiling over the peels, and seal the jars immediately.
