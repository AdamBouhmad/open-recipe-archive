## Ingredients

- Powdered mustard
- cooked, lukewarm water
- sugar
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Whether using Russian Sarepta, English Mustard, or Danish mustard, it is stirred in the same way, but not in too large portions at once, as it loses flavor and strength when standing.
2. It must never be stirred the same day it is to be used, as it will then have a bitter taste.
3. Stir the powdered mustard in cooked, lukewarm water and add sugar, salt and a little vinegar.
4. If it becomes too thick after standing for some time, it can be thinned with water or vinegar.
