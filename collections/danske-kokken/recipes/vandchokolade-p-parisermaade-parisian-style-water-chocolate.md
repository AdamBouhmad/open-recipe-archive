## Ingredients

- 4 Unzer fin Chokolade
- 1 Pint Band
- 2 Æggeblommer
- Sukker (optional)

## Directions

1. Dissolve 4 Unzer fine chocolate in 1 Pint water
2. Pour into a high kettle and place on gentle heat and boil
3. After it has boiled for 10 minutes, whisk it with an egg whisk, which is twirled between the hands therein, until it froths strongly
4. Pour gradually into 2 beaten egg yolks, which have been beaten throughout the work
5. Return to the kettle and whisk continuously with the egg whisk
6. All the thin foam is placed with a skimmer in the heated cups, which are placed in a warm spot, and this is continued until it stands piled up in the form of a dome over the cups
7. Hold a red-hot fire shovel very close over the foam until a kind of crust forms, after which the chocolate is served
8. Note: The chocolate froths even better when some sugar is stirred into the egg yolks, or if foam balls are used instead, prepared as follows: A sufficient number of egg whites are whisked to very stiff foam, and therein is stirred fine powdered sugar until the mass forms a stiff dough, which is spread on a baking sheet, and when it is nearly dried, small balls are formed of the size of a medium nut, which are then dried completely in the sun or at room temperature. After the egg yolks are whipped together with the chocolate in the manner described above, for each cup one such foam ball is placed in the chocolate pot and whisked on the fire until the entire chocolate mass is dissolved into stiff foam. One must observe the precaution that the chocolate is only heated until nearly boiling; it must absolutely not boil.
