## Ingredients

- 6 medium cauliflower heads
- 1/2 kg (3 lb) prepared ground meat
- Clear cloth
- Sailcloth band
- Salt
- Parsley
- Stirred butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the cauliflower
2. Place a cloth and cover it with a thin layer of ground meat
3. Place the cauliflower head down against the meat and tie the cloth at the bottom of the stalk with a sailcloth band
4. Place in boiling salted water and cook, usually about 3 quarters of an hour until tender
5. Arrange in a pyramid, garnish with parsley or salt, and serve with stirred butter
6. The cauliflower may be boiled for a few minutes before coating with meat, which then requires less cooking
7. Cauliflower may be prepared the same way with fish ground meat and served with lobster, crab, or shrimp sauce
