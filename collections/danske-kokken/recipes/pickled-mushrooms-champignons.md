## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg mushrooms
- 0.25 l vinegar
- 0.25 l water
- a small amount of salt

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms as described in recipe 217
2. Place them in boiling vinegar and water with a small amount of salt
3. Boil the mushrooms for only 5 minutes and fill them into jars, where the boiling vinegar must completely cover them
4. Bind immediately with parchment paper
