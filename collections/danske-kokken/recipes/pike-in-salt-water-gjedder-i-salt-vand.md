## Ingredients

- 1 pike of 3-4 pounds
- Salt water
- Finely chopped parsley
- Melted butter
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Scrape the pike clean, clean and dress it; cut off the fins and stick the tail between the teeth in the mouth so the fish forms a ring.
2. Place the necessary salt water over heat; when it boils, put in the pike, let it come to a boil once and then let it simmer gently for a quarter hour under a lid.
3. Carefully lift it out, let the water drain off, place on a serving platter and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
4. Melted butter and boiled potatoes are served with it.
