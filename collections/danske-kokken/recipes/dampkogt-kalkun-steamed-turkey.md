## Ingredients

- 1 stuffed turkey
- slices of bacon
- salt
- a little broth or water
- carrots, cut in half
- celery
- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- turkey broth from cooking

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a pot with slices of bacon.
2. Place the turkey on top and steam until tender for approximately 3 hours in a little broth or water to which salt, a few halved carrots, and a celery stalk have been added.
3. Alternatively, cook on a rack with only a little liquid on the bottom of the pot.
4. With a fine knitting needle, one can pierce the turkey to ascertain that it is tender.
5. Make a sauce by browning butter and flour together, then dilute with the broth from the turkey and thicken with a few egg yolks.
6. The sauce can be given various additions according to recipe 935.
