## Ingredients

- good ¼ L medium-fine sago grains
- 4½ L water
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon
- Raisins
- 7 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- ½ kg powdered sugar

## Directions

1. When the water boils, add lemon peel, whole cinnamon and raisins together with the grains while stirring constantly and cook until the grains are clear.
2. Stir 7 egg yolks and 3 egg whites in the serving bowl with ½ kg powdered sugar until it is white and foamy; then pour the hot soup into it while stirring.
