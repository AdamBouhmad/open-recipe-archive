## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- sugar
- essence
- 8 good apples
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and add sugar and essence as mentioned in Nr. 1012.
2. Peel and cut 8 good apples into smaller pieces and fry them on a pan in butter and as much sugar as the tartness of the apples requires.
3. When they are lightly browned, pour the egg mixture over them and turn and bake the omelet on both sides.
4. Sprinkle with sugar and serve immediately on a warm dish.
