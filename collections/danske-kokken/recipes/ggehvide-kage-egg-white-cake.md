## Ingredients

- 3/4 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 1/4 kilogram margarine
- 1/4 kilogram powdered sugar
- a little cardamom
- 3 3/4 deciliters half-skimmed milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 125 grams currants
- chopped candied citrus peel
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix together the Pillsbury flour, margarine, powdered sugar, cardamom, half-skimmed milk, baking powder, currants, and chopped candied citrus peel
2. Beat the egg whites and fold into the batter at the end
3. Pour into a greased form and bake for approximately 1 1/4 hours
