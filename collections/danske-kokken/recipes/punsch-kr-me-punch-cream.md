## Ingredients

- 2 pints French white wine
- 1 pint rum, cognac, or arrack
- 1 pint water
- 16 beaten egg yolks
- 1 pound white sugar
- zest of 3 lemons
- juice of 3 lemons
- 8 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Made like Citron-Kréme (No. 273); but instead of 1 pint wine, take 1 pint rum, cognac, arrack, or similar.
