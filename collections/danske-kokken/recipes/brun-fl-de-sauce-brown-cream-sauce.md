## Ingredients

- Equal parts butter and thick sour cream
- 1 teaspoon flour
- Meat skimmings
- Soy sauce

## Directions

1. Take equal amounts of butter and thick sour cream
2. Brown the butter in a pan and brown 1 teaspoon flour in it
3. Add a couple spoonfuls of skimmings
4. When this boils, add the cream and stir strongly until the sauce boils
5. If you have no skimmings, do not use flour either, but then the cream must be good
6. Should it separate, take a little more cream, and if it becomes too white from this, remedy it with soy sauce
7. This sauce is for veal and poultry and can be used for mutton and beef roast if necessary
8. For poultry avoid soy sauce
