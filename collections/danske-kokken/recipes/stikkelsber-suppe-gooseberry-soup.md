## Ingredients

- 1 Quart Gooseberries
- 2 Quart Water
- 3 Egg yolks
- white Sugar
- whole Cinnamon
- Bread

## Directions

1. This can be made from both unripe and ripe gooseberries. It is best made with eggs.
2. Place 1 quart gooseberries over the fire in 2 quarts water; they need not be picked.
3. Meanwhile whisk 3 egg yolks in the tureen with much white sugar.
4. When the berries are cooked, strain the soup and place it on again with a little whole cinnamon and sugar, as what is in the eggs will scarcely be sufficient to diminish the berries' sourness.
5. When it has cooked a little, whisk in the eggs and serve with bread.
