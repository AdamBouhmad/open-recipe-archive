## Ingredients

- white beans cooked as in recipe 590
- butter
- flour
- cooking broth from the beans
- 2 egg yolks per 0.5 kg beans
- salt
- ground white pepper
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in broth as in recipe 590
2. When tender, drain the broth and reserve it
3. Brown the butter and flour together and thin with the reserved cooking broth
4. Thicken the sauce with 2 egg yolks per 0.5 kg of beans
5. Season the sauce with salt, ground white pepper, lemon juice, and chopped parsley
6. Turn the beans in the sauce to coat
