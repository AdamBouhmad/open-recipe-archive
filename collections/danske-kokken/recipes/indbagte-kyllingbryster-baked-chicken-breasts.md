## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Meat forcemeat
- Puff pastry
- Broth
- Thickening for white sauce
- Stuffed celery (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the head with neck, bones and the outermost wing from the chicken
2. Divide it into 4 parts, first down the middle of the breast bone and then across
3. Both these and simpler pieces are cooked; but the best ones are removed before they are too tender
4. When cold, coat each piece with meat forcemeat and wrap puff pastry around it so the outside is smooth and the seam is on the underside
5. Roast on plates in the baking oven which should be as hot as for wheat bread; but be prepared to cover them with paper as puff pastry should never brown
6. Prepare sauce from the broth as white sauce usually is; sometimes stuffed celery is used for this dish; then no root vegetables are used in the sauce itself
