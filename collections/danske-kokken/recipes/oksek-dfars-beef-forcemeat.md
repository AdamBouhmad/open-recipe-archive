## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg scraped beef
- 125 g tallow or fresh fat pork
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 to 3 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 small onion, grated
- 1/4 to 1 liter milk

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and if you do not have a meat grinder, pound it with a meat hammer to remove sinews more easily.
2. Chop 1/4 kg scraped beef very finely with 125 g tallow or fresh fat pork.
3. Work in 3 tablespoons flour, 2 to 3 eggs, salt, pepper and 1 small grated onion.
4. Add milk very gradually, stirring constantly in the same direction, as the forcemeat will easily separate otherwise. For this portion use 1/4 to 1 liter milk, but this depends on how long the forcemeat is being worked.
5. If you have a meat grinder, it is much easier to prepare the forcemeat.
6. If you want the forcemeat even finer, after stirring, press it through a coarse sieve.
7. When ready, cook a test ball to ensure the forcemeat is sufficiently soft.
