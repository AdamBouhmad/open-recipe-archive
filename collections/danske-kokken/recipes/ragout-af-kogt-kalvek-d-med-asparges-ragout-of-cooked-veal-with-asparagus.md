## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Soup
- Asparagus water
- Egg yolks
- Parsley, chopped
- Asparagus
- Cooked veal, cut into pieces
- Puff pastry border, strips, or bread croutons

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour
2. Dilute with soup and some of the asparagus water to make a smooth sauce
3. Bind the sauce with a couple of egg yolks
4. Add chopped parsley
5. Add the asparagus and cut meat, which can be either sliced or cubed
6. Garnish the ragout with puff pastry border, strips, or bread croutons
