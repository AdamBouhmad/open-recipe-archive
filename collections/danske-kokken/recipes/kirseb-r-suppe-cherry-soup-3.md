## Ingredients

- 1 Pound Cherries
- 1 Quart Water
- 1 Pint Water (second cooking)
- whole Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Sugar
- Bread dumplings or baked Bread

## Directions

1. Pit and cook the cherries with 1 quart water, strain, cook again with 1 pint and pour into the first cooked soup.
2. To make the soup clear, lay a thin cloth in the sieve and pour the soup through it, after which place it on the fire with whole cinnamon, cloves and sugar to bring to a good boil.
3. Serve with bread dumplings, which are added when the soup boils, or with baked bread.
4. A few of the berries can be removed and cooked in the strained soup.
5. If you want the soup smooth, stir a heaping spoonful of corn-starch dissolved in water or in some of the soup while cold, diluted with the hot and stirred in when the soup boils.
6. The starch must only boil a few minutes to not become stringy.
