## Ingredients

- 1 Liter (Pot) cream
- 200 Gr (40 Kvint) icing sugar
- 16 leaves isinglass
- 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water
- 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) white rum
- cold fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to foam
2. Stir in 200 Gr (40 Kvint) icing sugar
3. Dissolve 16 leaves of isinglass in 1 1/4 Dl (1/2 Pægl) boiling water
4. Stir the isinglass solution and 3 3/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) white rum into the cream
5. Stir the pudding until it begins to set before pouring into the mold or bowl
6. The pudding can be made a few hours before serving
7. Serve with cold fruit sauce
