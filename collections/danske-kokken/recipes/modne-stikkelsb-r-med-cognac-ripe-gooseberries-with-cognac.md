## Ingredients

- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl red currant juice
- 1/2 kg picked berries
- Cognac

## Directions

1. From ripe thin-skinned gooseberries cut off the flower and stem
2. Pour sugar and water in the pot and when melted add the berries to cook for 1/4 hour
3. Remove the gooseberries with slotted spoon and place on a colander then cook the juice again and skim until thick
4. Add 1 glass of Cognac per 1/2 kg of berries
5. Put the berries in jar and pour the hot juice over them
6. Seal when the preserve is done
7. Instead of Cognac the gooseberries can be cooked with a few pieces of whole cinnamon and a few whole cloves
