## Ingredients

- A couple of very finely chopped onions
- A small amount of garlic
- Butter
- Flour
- Butter to brown
- Finely chopped mushrooms
- Olives

## Directions

1. Brown a couple of very finely chopped onions and just a small amount of garlic in butter
2. The butter should be browned and combined with flour for the Madeira sauce
3. Finely chopped mushrooms and olives are brought to a boil in it
