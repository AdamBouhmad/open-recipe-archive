## Ingredients

- 150 Gr (30 Kvint) almonds
- 1 L (1 Pot) cream or milk
- nougat as prepared in Nr. 1062

## Directions

1. See preparation of nougat, Nr. 1062.
2. Stir the crushed nougat into vanilla cream made with 1 L (1 Pot) cream.
3. Set aside for freezing.
