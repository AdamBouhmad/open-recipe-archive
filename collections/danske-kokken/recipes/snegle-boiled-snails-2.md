## Ingredients

- Snail shells
- Salt
- Water
- 1 onion
- 1 bay leaf
- Thyme
- Black peppercorns
- Good bouillon

## Directions

1. Wash snail shells well
2. Boil for several minutes in salted water until snails can be easily pulled out with a larding needle or similar pointed object
3. Remove the mantle and viscera sacs with a small knife
4. Place snails in warm water and rinse in cold water
5. Press dry in a cloth
6. Place over heat in a little good bouillon
7. Cook together with snails 1 onion, 1 bay leaf, a small amount of thyme and a couple of whole black peppercorns for about 1 hour
8. Strain the broth from the cooked snails for use in further preparation
