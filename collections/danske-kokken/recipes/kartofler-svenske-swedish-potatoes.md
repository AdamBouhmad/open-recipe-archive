## Ingredients

- Onion slices
- Butter
- Cooked, firm potatoes
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Brown onion slices in butter on a frying pan and turn and heat slices of cooked, firm potatoes in it. They should not be fried hard, but should really only be heated in plenty of butter. Season them with salt, if the butter is not salty enough, and sprinkle with ground pepper. With this potato vegetable, for example cutlets or fried meatballs can be garnished.
