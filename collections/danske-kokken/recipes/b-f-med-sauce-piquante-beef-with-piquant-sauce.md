## Ingredients

- 0.25 liters strong gravy
- 4 tablespoons preserved small onions or shallots
- Paprika
- Wine
- Salt
- Sugar
- Sago flour for thickening
- Water
- Butter dough border or strips

## Directions

1. For a sauce for 8 to 10 people, use 0.25 liters of very strong gravy, which must simmer for 5 to 10 minutes with 4 tablespoons preserved small onions or shallots, paprika, wine, salt and sugar.
2. Give the sauce a nice brown color and thicken with sago flour mixed in water.
3. On the dish place either a butter dough border or strips.
4. Pour the sauce over the beef.
