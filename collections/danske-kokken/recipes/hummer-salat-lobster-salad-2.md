## Ingredients

- 1 large lobster
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt
- Cooked picked fish or fish balls (optional)

## Directions

1. Pick the lobster as previously shown
2. Make the sauce from 2 tablespoons of oil, stirred with 1 tablespoon vinegar, pepper and salt
3. Stir the picked lobster in this
4. You can add cooked and picked fish or cut fish balls, but then a little more sauce must be made
