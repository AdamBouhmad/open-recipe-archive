## Ingredients

- 1 liter (1 pint) water
- 7.5 kg (15 pounds) pears

## Directions

1. Use ripe or even overripe pears that are otherwise unusable
2. Do not peel them, but cut them in half
3. Remove the blossom and stem
4. Rinse the fruit and place in a pot with the specified amount of liquid
5. Boil the pears until completely tender so the juice can be pressed out using a fruit press or through a sieve
6. Pour the juice into the pot and boil until it is thick as syrup and hangs in threads from the spoon, at which point it has changed color and become dark
7. Pour the syrup into crocks and can be preserved for years
8. Tie with parchment paper
