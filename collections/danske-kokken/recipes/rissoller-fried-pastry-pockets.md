## Ingredients

- Butter Dough
- Filling (made the same way as for Roulettes)
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Palmin (cooking fat)

## Directions

1. Roll out the butter dough fairly thinly and cut into 6 to 7 centimeter (2½ inch) squares with a pastry cutter.
2. Prepare the filling in the same manner as for Roulettes, made stiff and cold.
3. Place a dessert spoonful of filling on one half of each piece of dough.
4. Brush the edges with egg, fold the dough together, and press firmly.
5. Brush the Rissoller with beaten egg, dip in breadcrumbs, and fry in Palmin.
6. Arrange in a pyramid on a platter lined with a folded napkin, and garnish with parsley.
7. Serve with a sauce suited to the filling, though they are most often eaten without sauce.
