## Ingredients

- 0.25 kilogram flour
- 0.25 kilogram margarine
- 375 grams finely chopped, cooked salt cod
- just under 0.75 liter boiling milk
- 10 egg yolks
- salt
- ground white pepper
- 125 grams plucked and scalded raisins
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. 0.25 kilogram of flour is baked in 0.25 kilogram of margarine together with 375 grams of finely chopped, cooked salt cod and diluted with just under 0.75 liter of boiling milk.
2. When the batter is slightly cooled, it is stirred with 10 egg yolks, salt, ground white pepper, 125 grams of plucked and scalded raisins, and finally with the beaten egg whites.
3. The pudding is eaten with melted or creamed butter.
4. Salt cod pudding can also be made as bread pudding, with the fish stirred into it.
