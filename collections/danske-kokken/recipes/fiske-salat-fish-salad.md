## Ingredients

- Salmon, pike, turbot or cod
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock from the cooked fish
- Egg yolks
- Mushrooms or mushroom vinegar
- Vinegar
- White pepper
- Sugar
- Sherry
- Cooked celery
- Watercress for garnish

## Directions

1. Make a roux from butter and flour and dilute with the stock in which the fish was cooked
2. Thicken the sauce with 2-3 egg yolks so it is like a thick porridge
3. Season with mushrooms or their vinegar, vinegar, salt, white pepper, a little sugar, and sherry to taste
4. Pick the cooked fish into fairly large pieces while still warm
5. Turn the fish gently into the prepared sauce
6. For a deep plate full of fish, take one large cooked celery, cut it into very small cubes and add it
7. The salad is best the same day it is prepared
8. It can be garnished with watercress
