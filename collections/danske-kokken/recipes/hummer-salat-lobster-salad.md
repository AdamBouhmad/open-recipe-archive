## Ingredients

- Preserved lobster
- Mayonnaise made with 2 egg yolks, 1/4 liter oil, 4 spoonfuls tomato puree, salt, ground white pepper, mustard and tarragon vinegar
- Fresh salad leaves
- Cooked, flaked cod
- 125 grams cooked, diced macaroni
- Lobster meat
- Watercress
- Toasted French bread slices
- Caviar

## Directions

1. Place a wreath of fresh salad leaves on a platter.
2. In the center place finely cut lobster meat, a flat plate of cooked, flaked cod, and 125 grams of cooked, diced macaroni.
3. Pour the mayonnaise over.
4. Some of the lobster meat can be placed on top of the salad and sprinkled with watercress.
5. Toasted French bread slices topped with caviar can be arranged around the salad leaves.
