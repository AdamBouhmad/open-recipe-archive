## Ingredients

- Elder berries
- 125 gr (1/4 lb) farina per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. Pick the berries and put them in a pot; place over very low heat until they release their juice well, then strain through a fine sieve.
2. Measure the juice and boil for a quarter hour with the sugar; skim it and pour hot into bottles, which are tied immediately with parchment paper.
3. The elder berries are then boiled with water, with a little vinegar added, and the juice, after being strained, can be poured into dry bottles, corked, and gradually used in the household for elder berry soup.
