## Ingredients

- Butter Dough
- Liver Pâté
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Palmin (cooking fat)

## Directions

1. Use liver pâté as the filling for the Rissoller.
2. Place a piece of liver pâté on each square of butter dough and treat as described in the basic Rissoller recipe.
