## Ingredients

- Butter
- Bread or cracker crumbs
- 1/2 cup cream or milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- 1 quart oysters with liquid

## Directions

1. Butter the bottom of a baking dish and spread a layer of bread or cracker crumbs over it.
2. Pour 1/2 cup cream or milk over this, spread with a tablespoon.
3. Add salt, pepper, and butter; then add 1 quart of oysters with their liquid.
4. Sprinkle with a little pepper and butter, then another layer of crumbs and butter.
5. Cover and bake for 2 or 3 hours or until the oyster liquid bubbles up.
6. Remove the lid and place the baking dish in the hottest part of the oven for a few minutes.
7. Serve in the baking dish.
