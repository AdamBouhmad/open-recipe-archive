## Ingredients

- 1 very large firm cabbage head
- 2.5 kg prepared forcemeat (serves 12 people)
- salt
- water
- melted butter or sauce made from butter, flour, and broth

## Directions

1. Remove the outer loose leaves from a good firm cabbage head
2. Cut a lid around the stem large enough to hollow out the head
3. Fill the forcemeat inside, leaving room for it to rise
4. Place the lid on and cover with a few of the removed leaves
5. Bind the head with twine and place in a pot with plenty of boiling salted water
6. Boil for 3 to 3.5 hours
7. When serving, remove the lid and place the head with forcemeat facing down on the platter
8. Serve with melted butter or sauce made from butter, flour, and the broth
9. The hollowed cabbage can be cooked with the head and arranged around it, or prepared together with cabbage broth into a brown cabbage soup with the addition of Maggi bouillon cubes
