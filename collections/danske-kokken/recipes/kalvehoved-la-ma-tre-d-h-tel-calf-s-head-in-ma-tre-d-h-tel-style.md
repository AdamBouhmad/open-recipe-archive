## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Salt
- Bouillon
- Butter
- Flour
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Mushrooms
- White pepper
- Sugar
- Egg yolks
- Parsley

## Directions

1. After the calf's head has been soaked, cleaned and cooked in very little water with salt and good bouillon, remove it from the bones and cut it into cubes when it is cold.
2. Brown butter and flour together and dilute with the bouillon to make a good, white, smooth sauce, and if necessary add Maggi Bouillon cubes.
3. Add mushrooms, white pepper, salt and a little sugar to the sauce, and lastly add the meat to warm it.
4. Just before serving, thicken the sauce with 3 to 4 egg yolks.
5. Serve in a puff pastry border or with slices and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
