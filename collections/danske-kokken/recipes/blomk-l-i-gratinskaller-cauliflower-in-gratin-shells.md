## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- ham (optional)
- eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. The cauliflower is cooked nearly tender in salted water.
2. It is divided into small florets, which are placed in the shells, over which melted butter is poured, and they are sprinkled with breadcrumbs and baked.
3. Around the warm cabbage, around the edge of the shell, chopped ham can be placed. Chopped egg is sprinkled over the cauliflower.
