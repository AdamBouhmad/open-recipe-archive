## Ingredients

- Fish of various kinds
- 1 pint cream (or sour cream)
- 2 tablespoons mushrooms (or capers as alternative)
- Vinegar from the mushrooms
- A little ground nutmeg
- Salt
- 3 tablespoons oil (as alternative to cream)

## Directions

1. Pick the cooked fish from skin and bones into flat pieces
2. For a deep plate half full of picked fish, make a sauce from a pint of cream, which can be sour, stirred with 2 tablespoons of mushrooms together with the vinegar that comes with them, a little ground nutmeg and salt
3. If the sauce is not tart enough, a little vinegar can be added
4. Instead of cream, the salad can be made with 3 tablespoons oil
5. Instead of mushrooms, capers can be used
