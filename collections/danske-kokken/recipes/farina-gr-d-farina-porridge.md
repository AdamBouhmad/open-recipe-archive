## Ingredients

- 1.5 quarts milk
- 1 ordinary cup farina
- salt
- butter
- sugar
- milk or juice and water (as desired)

## Directions

1. When the milk boils, sprinkle the grains in; stir evenly, and it needs only very brief cooking
2. Stir in salt afterward
3. Eat it with butter and sugar along with milk or juice and water as desired
4. Without butter and juice, it is also an excellent dish for small children, as is farina gruel, for which fewer grains are needed
