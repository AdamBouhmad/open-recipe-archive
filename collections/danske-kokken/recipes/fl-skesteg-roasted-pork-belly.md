## Ingredients

- ham (with shank removed)
- bacon fat
- salt
- ground pepper
- water
- flour
- cold butter

## Directions

1. Before roasting, rub the skin well with the fatty side of a piece of bacon fat and then with salt mixed with a little ground pepper.
2. Cut scores in the skin, but not into the meat, first lengthwise and then crosswise, so that the skin appears divided into squares no larger than a 25-øre piece.
3. Place the roast in the pan, sprinkle with a little salt and ground pepper.
4. Add a little water to the pan.
5. Roast for approximately 4-5 hours.
6. The oven must be very hot when the roast is placed in it, but not so hot that the roast burns.
7. Roasting occurs at even heat, and the roast is not basted.
8. Check occasionally that there is not too much steam in the oven, as the skin will not become crispy otherwise.
9. Pour off the sauce from the roast, skim off the fat and brown it with flour in a casserole and thin with the remaining sauce.
10. Skim it, and just before serving stir a small piece of cold butter into the sauce.
