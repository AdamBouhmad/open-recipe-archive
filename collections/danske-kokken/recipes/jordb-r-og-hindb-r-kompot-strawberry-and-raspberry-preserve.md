## Ingredients

- Strawberries and raspberries
- 125 grams powdered sugar

## Directions

1. The strawberries and raspberries can either be laid in layers in the jars with 125 grams powdered sugar, or the sugar can be melted in a small amount of water and poured boiling over the fruit well shaken together in the jars.
2. If the sugar is dissolved in a small amount of boiling water, the same amount of fruit will fit in the jars.
3. Cook for 15 minutes.
