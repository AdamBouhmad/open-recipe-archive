## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg (1 S) dried apricots
- 1 1/2 L (1 1/2 pots) water
- sago flour for thickening
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and soak it for 2 days before cooking it in the water in which it has been standing.
2. Thus soaked, it needs only a few minutes of cooking before it is thickened with stirred sago flour, sweetened, and poured out.
3. Serve cold with cream or an inexpensive cream made as follows: to 1 L (1 pot) of sweet or semi-skimmed milk, use 1 tablespoon maizena, which is stirred into some of the cold milk; the rest is brought to the boil, and the thickening is given one boil in it.
4. 2 eggs are stirred with 75 g (15 quintal) powdered sugar, the milk is poured in, and the cream is poured into the pot to be brought to the boiling point.
5. It is whisked well with a whisk, poured out, and flavored with vanilla essence.
6. A slight pinch of salt is stirred in.
