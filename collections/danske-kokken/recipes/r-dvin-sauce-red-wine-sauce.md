## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) red wine
- 1/4 liter (1 pint) cherry juice
- 1 1/4 deciliter (1/2 pint) water
- 1 shot glass Cognac
- Sago or potato starch
- Scalded, chopped almonds (optional)
- Sugar to taste (optional)

## Directions

1. Combine 1/2 liter red wine, 1/4 liter cherry juice, 1 1/4 deciliters water, and 1 shot glass Cognac.
2. When it boils, smooth it with a little mixed sago or potato starch.
3. Scalded, chopped almonds may be added to the sauce and more sugar to taste.
