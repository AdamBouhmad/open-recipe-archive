## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Sugar, ¼ pound per pint of juice
- 5 to 6 lemons per pint juice

## Directions

1. Remove all the insides of the lemons and press them through a sieve or wring them out in a cloth
2. For 1 pint juice (from 5 to 6 lemons), take ¼ pound sugar
3. Put it on the fire at once, boil and skim well for 10 minutes
4. Pour into a jar and seal
