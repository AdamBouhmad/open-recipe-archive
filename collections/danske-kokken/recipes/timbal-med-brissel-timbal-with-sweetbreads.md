## Ingredients

- 3 pairs of sweetbreads
- Butter
- Truffles, sliced
- Veal forcemeat, approximately 2½ centimeters thick
- Sweetbread ragout
- Mushroom sauce
- Egg yolks
- Salad or parsley
- Sweetbread cutlets
- Breaded tomatoes

## Directions

1. Use a pudding mold without a center post if a timbal mold is not available
2. Butter the mold well with cold butter and decorate with truffle slices pressed firmly against the sides
3. Line the mold with a layer of veal forcemeat approximately 2½ centimeters thick, pressing it firmly against the truffles with a spoon dipped in hot water without disturbing the truffles
4. Fill the mold with sweetbread ragout prepared as recipe No. 6, cutting the sweetbreads into pieces of at least 2½ centimeters
5. Cover the ragout with a layer of forcemeat and place a piece of buttered paper on top
6. Put the lid on and cook like any other pudding for approximately 1 hour
7. Turn the timbal out onto the serving platter, let it stand briefly, then carefully lift the mold and remove any butter that has collected on the platter
8. Serve with mushroom sauce held back from the ragout, thinned and bound with egg yolks
9. Garnish with salad or parsley, sweetbread cutlets, and breaded tomatoes
