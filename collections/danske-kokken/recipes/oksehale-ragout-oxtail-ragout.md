## Ingredients

- Oxtail
- Madeira
- Paprika
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Tomato purée
- Egg

## Directions

1. Prepare as for soup (see Nr. 389), but use significantly less liquid for the ragout, as the sauce should be more robust.
2. The sauce should have a fine color and is made with good Madeira, paprika, Maggi Bouillon cubes and tomato purée.
3. Arrange the ragout on a flat, warm platter and garnish with egg.
