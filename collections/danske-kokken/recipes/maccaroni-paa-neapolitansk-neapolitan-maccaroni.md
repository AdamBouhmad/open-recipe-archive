## Ingredients

- Maccaroni
- Water or thin bouillon
- Finely chopped ham
- Meat
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Let maccaroni cook in water or thin bouillon until almost completely cooked.
2. Then drain the water so only a little remains with which they can stand and soak up.
3. In another pot cook finely chopped and drained ham and meat in thin bouillon until well cooked.
4. Remove it from the sauce, then layer everything in a deep baking dish as follows: first a layer of maccaroni, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, then a thin layer of ham, then maccaroni with cheese and then meat and ham, continuing thus as long as you have something; but the last must be cheese.
5. Place the dish in a warm baking oven for about 10 minutes, that is, until it is well heated and has taken on a golden brown color on top.
6. Serve hot.
