## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- dry salt

## Directions

1. Cucumber salad can be preserved for a long time when peeled and sliced cucumbers in the usual way are placed in a jar, layered with dry salt and tied over.
2. When the salad is to be used, soak the pieces thoroughly and prepare them afterwards in the usual manner.
