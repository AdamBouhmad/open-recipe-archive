## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg butter
- 625 grams flour
- 4 liters boiling water
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Melt the butter
2. Roast the flour in the melted butter
3. Gradually add the boiling water, allowing the porridge to boil between each addition
4. Stir strongly to avoid lumps
5. Serve with butter, sugar, and cinnamon
6. Serve juice and water or sweet white beer on the side
