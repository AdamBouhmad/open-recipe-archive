## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) raspberry pulp from jelly straining
- 0.5 kg (1 lb) powdered sugar

## Directions

1. The raspberry refuse which remains in the jelly cloth is passed through the sieve.
2. The sugar is added, and the jam is reduced, poured out and dried as previously described.
