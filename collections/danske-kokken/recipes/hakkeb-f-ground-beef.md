## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef from the rib or middle cut
- Onion slices
- Butter or Palmin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Gravy, broth or boiling water
- Maggi bouillon cubes

## Directions

1. Brown a good portion of onion slices and scrape off the pan
2. Cut the meat into smaller pieces and grind once through a meat grinder
3. Form into round pieces with a knife to make well-hacked small beef steaks
4. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper and turn in flour
5. Fry on the pan in browned butter or Palmin; it should be red and juicy but not bloody
6. Deglaze the pan with gravy, broth or boiling water
7. Dry the pan and brown flour in a little browned butter or margarine, thin with the deglazed liquid to a smooth sauce
8. Season with Maggi bouillon cubes, salt and pepper
