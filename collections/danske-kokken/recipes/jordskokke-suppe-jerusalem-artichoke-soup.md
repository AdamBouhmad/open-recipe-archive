## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg (3 S) Jerusalem artichokes
- 4 L (4 pots) broth
- 60 grams (12 skein) flour
- 75 grams (15 skein) butter
- 4 to 5 egg yolks
- salt
- ground white pepper
- Sherry
- baked French bread cubes
- cooked, cut Jerusalem artichokes

## Directions

1. Peel the Jerusalem artichokes, rinse them, cook them in water or broth, and rub them through a sieve.
2. Bake 60 grams (12 skein) flour in 75 grams (15 skein) butter, thin this with the Jerusalem artichoke purée, which is brought to a boil.
3. Bind it with 4 to 5 egg yolks, season with salt, ground white pepper and Sherry.
4. Serve with baked French bread cubes. Cooked, cut Jerusalem artichokes can be served in the soup.
