## Ingredients

- 3 bottles Graves wine
- 1½ pounds top sugar
- peel of 2 lemons
- peel of 2 oranges
- 3 measures boiling water
- ½ bottle Sherry
- a good dram old rum

## Directions

1. Set 3 bottles Graves wine over heat with 1½ pounds top sugar, peel of 2 lemons and peel of 2 oranges
2. When it begins to boil, pour 3 measures boiling water into it
3. Remove from heat and add ½ bottle Sherry and a good dram old rum
4. Drink
