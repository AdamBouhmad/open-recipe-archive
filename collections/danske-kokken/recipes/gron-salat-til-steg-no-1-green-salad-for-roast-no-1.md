## Ingredients

- Salad heads
- White sugar
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Pick through salad heads to find no bad leaves on them; wash them well and cut into 7 or 8 parts.
2. Rub white sugar into hard-boiled egg yolks, and a little vinegar in water is added.
3. Add the salad and it must stand some time before it is used.
