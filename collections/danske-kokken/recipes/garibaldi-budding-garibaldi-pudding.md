## Ingredients

- 6 pægle sweet cream
- 1½ pounds white sugar
- 1 vanilla stick
- 4 pounds butter
- 14 pounds flour
- sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons powdered bitter almonds
- 12 to 14 eggs
- brown sugar for glazing

## Directions

1. Boil 6 pægle sweet cream with 1½ pounds white sugar, 1 vanilla stick and 4 pounds butter.
2. Add 14 pounds flour, first mixed with a little sweet milk.
3. Stir the whole mixture over the fire until it releases from the pan and spoon, then remove and let cool.
4. Add 2 tablespoons powdered bitter almonds and 12 to 14 eggs, stirring well until it looks white.
5. Glaze a mold with brown sugar and fill with the mixture.
6. Place the mold in boiling water and cook the pudding for 2 hours.
7. Serve with Chantilly sauce.
