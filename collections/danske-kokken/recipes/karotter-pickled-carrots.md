## Ingredients

- Small carrots
- Whole white pepper
- Spanish pepper husks
- Pickling vinegar

## Directions

1. Scrape the small carrots, rinse them, and half-cook them in water.
2. Drain them in a colander and rinse them with cold water.
3. Dry the roots in a cloth and place them in glasses or jars together with the spices, which consist of some whole white pepper and a few Spanish pepper husks.
4. Pour over as much good pickling vinegar as is necessary to cover the vegetables.
5. Let them stand until the next day, then pour off the vinegar, give it a boil, and immediately pour it over the roots, after which the jar is tied shut with parchment paper.
6. Carrots can be peeled with a fluted herb knife and thereby get a beautiful appearance.
