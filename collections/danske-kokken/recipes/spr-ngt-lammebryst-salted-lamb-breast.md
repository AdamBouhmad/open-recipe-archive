## Ingredients

- lamb breast or brisket
- salt
- 1 teaspoon saltpeter
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Break the ribs in a couple of places, and rub the meat with salt.
2. Rub with 1 teaspoon of saltpeter and 2 teaspoons of sugar, and place on a flat dish to be regularly turned in the brine that draws from the meat.
3. Let it stand thus for 3-4 days.
4. Rinse the brine well from the meat before placing it in the pot in boiling water and cooking until tender.
5. Serve with stewed vegetables. The broth from the meat can be used for a smooth soup or purée.
