## Ingredients

- ¾ pound butter, clarified
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 vanilla pod, ground
- 1 pound potato starch
- 6 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and stir well
2. Add 1 pound powdered sugar and stir well for 1 hour
3. While stirring continuously, add 6 egg yolks
4. Add 1 ground vanilla pod
5. Add 1 pound potato starch
6. Finally fold in 6 whipped egg whites
7. Bake in small molds at good heat
