## Ingredients

- ½ Pound rice grains, ground
- 3 Pints Milk
- 4 Ounces Butter (4 Tablespoons, not heaped)
- 9 Egg yolks
- ½ Pound sweet almonds, ground
- a few Bitter almonds, ground
- Zest of Lemon, grated
- Sugar to taste
- Salt to taste
- beaten Egg whites

## Directions

1. Grind the rice grains and cook to stiff porridge in 3 pints milk
2. Stir in the warm porridge the butter
3. Add the egg yolks
4. Add the sweet and bitter almonds
5. Add the grated lemon zest
6. Add sugar and salt to taste
7. Lastly fold in the beaten egg whites
8. Pour into greased and floured form
9. Sprinkle with sugar
10. Bake for one hour
11. Serves 10 people
