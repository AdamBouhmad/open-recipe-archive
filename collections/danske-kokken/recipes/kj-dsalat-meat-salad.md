## Ingredients

- Diced cooked chicken meat
- Celery root
- Finely diced ham or ox tongue
- 10 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 pint good, sweet cream
- 16 anchovies
- 1 small shallot
- 1 good teaspoon strong mustard
- Capers
- French vinegar

## Directions

1. Combine diced chicken meat, celery root and finely diced ham or ox tongue
2. Mash 10 hard-boiled egg yolks and mix with 1 pint good, sweet cream
3. Remove bones from 16 anchovies and finely chop them together with 1 small shallot, 1 good teaspoon strong mustard, capers and French vinegar to taste
4. Mix all together and strain through a sieve
5. Place the meat in the sauce and let it sit for a couple of hours before serving
