## Ingredients

- 4 chickens
- 1 L (1 pot) stock, gravy, or water
- salt
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- 5-6 tablespoons tomato purée
- preserved or fresh mushrooms
- paprika
- 1¼ dl (1/10 pægl) Madeira or Sherry
- butter
- sago flour
- puff pastry strips or border

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the chickens and cut them into 6 pieces each.
2. Brown the pieces in a slice of butter in a pot.
3. Pour over with 1 L (1 pot) stock, gravy, or water, seasoned with salt and Maggi bouillon cubes.
4. Stir in 5-6 tablespoons of tomato purée and some preserved or fresh mushrooms, paprika, 1¼ dl (1/10 pægl) Madeira or Sherry, and coloring to give the sauce a beautiful brown color.
5. Cook the chickens gently in this sauce.
6. Thicken the sauce with sago flour.
7. Pour the sauce over the chickens when serving and garnish with puff pastry strips or border.
