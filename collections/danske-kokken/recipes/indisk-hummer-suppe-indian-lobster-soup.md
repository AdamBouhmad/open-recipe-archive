## Ingredients

- 2 lobsters
- 4 L spare soup
- 125 gr butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 teaspoons curry
- 1/4 L raw cream

## Directions

1. Cook the lobsters, pick out the meat and pound the shells and roe with 125 gr butter
2. Boil these in the specified quantity of soup
3. Skim off the lobster butter
4. Thicken the soup with 6 beaten egg yolks in which 2 teaspoons curry and 1/4 L raw cream have been stirred
5. Do not let the soup boil after the eggs are added
6. Finally, whisk the lobster butter into the soup and pour it over the lobster meat in the tureen
7. Serve immediately with boiled rice served separately
