## Ingredients

- Wild ducks
- Thin slices of bacon
- Sauce
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare wild ducks like other fowl; do not stuff them
2. Bind thin slices of bacon over the breast and place in the oven
3. Calculate about 15 minutes roasting time for each pound the fowl weighs
4. Serve with sauce and lemon slices
5. Note: The book recommends they be fully roasted as half-roasted wild duck is considered unpalatable
