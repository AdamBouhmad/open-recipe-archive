## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) blackberries
- 375 gr (3/4 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- 1.5 dl (1/2 pint) water

## Directions

1. Once the sugar is boiled together with the water, add the berries and give them only one boil in the syrup.
2. Pour them onto a colander and reduce the syrup until it is smooth.
3. Then add the berries to blend with the syrup and give them one boil before pouring them into the crock.
4. Seal immediately.
