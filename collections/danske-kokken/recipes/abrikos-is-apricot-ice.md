## Ingredients

- 20 to 25 ripe apricots
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered sugar
- 3 3/4 dl (1 1/2 cup) water
- Juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Split the apricots and remove the pits and crush them, as the kernels must be cooked with the syrup.
2. Cook the powdered sugar to a syrup with the water, the crushed apricot kernels, juice of 2 lemons, and the flesh of the apricots which has been grated through a sieve.
3. Pour the whole mixture through a fine sieve into the freezing container.
4. If the fruit is not sufficiently ripe, it must first be cooked and the water from this should be used to cook the sugar in.
