## Ingredients

- 375 gr powdered sugar
- 1/2 kg picked berries

## Directions

1. Pick the berries from the stems and if desired cut off the flowers
2. Weigh them, place on a colander to rinse well and then put in pot sprinkled with sugar
3. Cook the berries for 10 minutes, skim and remove on a colander
4. Reduce the juice until thick then add the berries to mix with it
5. Cool and seal immediately
