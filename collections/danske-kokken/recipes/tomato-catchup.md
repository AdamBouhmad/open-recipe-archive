## Ingredients

- good ripe Tomatoes
- boiling Water
- Salt
- ground Mustard
- short Pepper
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Cayenne Pepper
- white Wine or Cider Vinegar

## Directions

1. Choose good ripe tomatoes
2. Throw in boiling water and rub through a coarse sieve to remove skin and seeds
3. When cooled to each gallon add 4 Tablespoons salt, 3 tablespoons ground mustard, 2 tablespoons short pepper, 1 tablespoon allspice, 4 tablespoons cloves, 4 tablespoons cayenne pepper and 1 pint white wine or cider vinegar
4. Simmer gently for 4 hours
5. Place on bottles and stopper well
