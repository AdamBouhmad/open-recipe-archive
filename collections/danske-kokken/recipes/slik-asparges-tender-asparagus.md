## Ingredients

- Peeled fresh asparagus
- Boiling water
- Salt (10 gr or 2 Kvint per liter)
- Cold salt solution (10 gr or 2 Kvint per liter)

## Directions

1. Bind the peeled, fresh asparagus in bundles or stand them heads upward in a frying basket, all of the same length
2. Pour boiling salted water into the pot where the asparagus is arranged, but do not let the water reach the heads
3. Cook for 5 minutes, then remove the basket and pour cold water over the asparagus
4. Place them immediately in jars with the root ends down, cover with cold salt solution to 3 to 4 centimeters from the rim of the jar, close and place in a pot or steam cooker
5. Fill the pot with cold water up to the neck of the jars and cook for 1 hour
6. Leave the jars in the pot until cooled, or remove and cover with a woolen blanket
