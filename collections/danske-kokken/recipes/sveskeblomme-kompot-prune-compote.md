## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.5 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Place the plums in a sieve and pour boiling water over them.
2. Cook the sugar and water to a thin syrup.
3. Give the plums only a couple of minutes of boiling, as they must remain whole when filled into jars.
4. Reduce the syrup for 6 to 8 minutes and pour immediately over the plums.
5. Fit the jars with sticks and sulfur, light them, and then seal the jars.
