## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cherries
- 250 gr powdered sugar
- 1/4 dl cognac

## Directions

1. Yellow Spanish morello cherries or ordinary table cherries can be used, but they must be completely dry, whole and without blemishes.
2. Cut off some of the stems before placing them in dry jars, which are tapped lightly on the table so that the berries can settle.
3. Stir the sugar into the cognac, pour it over the cherries, and bind the jars immediately with parchment paper.
