## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.5 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Cut off half of the stems.
2. Cook sugar and water to a thin syrup in which the cherries boil for about 5 minutes.
3. Fill them into sulfured jars.
4. Boil the juice for 8 to 10 minutes before pouring it over the berries.
5. The stones can, if desired, be removed from the cherries before preserving.
6. Seal as previously instructed.
