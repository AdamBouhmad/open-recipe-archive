## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 35 pounds finely ground powdered sugar
- cochineal dye
- rose oil
- butter for greasing
- colored coarse sugar for decoration

## Directions

1. Whip 6 egg whites over weak heat with 35 pounds finely ground powdered sugar until it becomes frothy
2. Remove from heat and whip until cold
3. Stir in a few drops of cochineal dye and rose oil
4. Grease the sheet as usual
5. Place the cakes on the sheet in a lukewarm oven to dry until they release easily from the sheet
6. Place on the sheet using a teaspoon in size of a five-øre coin
7. Can be decorated with colored coarse sugar and egg white
