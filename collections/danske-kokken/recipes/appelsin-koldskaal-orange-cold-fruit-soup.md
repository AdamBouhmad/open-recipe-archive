## Ingredients

- 6 Oranges
- 1/4 Kg (1 1/2 lb) Powdered Sugar
- Wine as in the preceding Cold Soup

## Directions

1. Rub the peel of 1 orange on a piece of sugar, which is placed in the tureen together with the pressed juice of 2 oranges, whereas the remaining fruits are peeled, cut into thin slices, freed of the pits and sprinkled with sugar
2. Otherwise treated as the preceding cold soup
3. Serve with Sugar Biscuits
4. Dilute with Apollinaris
