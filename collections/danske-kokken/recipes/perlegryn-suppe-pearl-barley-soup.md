## Ingredients

- 1¼ L pearl barley
- 5 L water
- Lemon peel
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak the grains in 2 L water the evening before they are to be used.
2. Bring 3 L water to a boil, add the grains together with lemon peel and cinnamon and cook for 3 hours.
