## Ingredients

- 1 pound powdered icing sugar
- 2 knife tips cayenne pepper
- 2 tablespoons curry
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 4 tablespoons Sarepta mustard
- 2 tablespoons ground nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons ground ginger
- 4 bottles tarragon vinegar
- Madeira (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pound powdered icing sugar, 2 knife tips cayenne pepper, 2 tablespoons curry, 2 tablespoons salt, 4 tablespoons Sarepta mustard, 2 tablespoons ground nutmeg, 1 tablespoon white pepper, and 2 tablespoons ground ginger.
2. Add enough tarragon vinegar so that the total makes 4 bottles full.
3. Ordinary vinegar or vinegar from mushrooms and asparagus can also be used.
4. A little Madeira enhances the flavor.
