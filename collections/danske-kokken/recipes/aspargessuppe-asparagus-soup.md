## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds cleaned asparagus
- a little water
- a little salt
- butter
- 3 Pots good bouillon
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons good sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned asparagus into pieces about two inches long and cook quickly in a little water with a little salt and butter until tender.
2. Dilute with 3 pots of good bouillon and bring to a boil.
3. In a tureen, beat 6 egg yolks with 6 tablespoons of good sweet cream.
4. Under continuous beating, carefully add the soup a little at a time to the egg mixture.
5. Serve with toasted French bread cut into cubes.
