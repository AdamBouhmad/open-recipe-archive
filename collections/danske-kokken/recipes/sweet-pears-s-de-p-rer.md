## Ingredients

- Gray pears
- 1 Pd. Melis per 1 Pd. pears
- 1 Pægl water
- vanilla
- optional: Madeira or Sherry

## Directions

1. Peel the pears; if large, cut in 2 pieces and remove the core
2. Boil the Melis to syrup with water
3. Add the pears and boil until tender
4. Remove the pears and place on a colander
5. Boil the syrup with a piece of vanilla and skim until clear and thick
6. When the syrup is cooled, pour over the pears which have been placed in preserve jars
7. Seal the jars the next day
8. It is recommended to add a little Madeira or Sherry to the syrup after the final boiling to enhance the taste
