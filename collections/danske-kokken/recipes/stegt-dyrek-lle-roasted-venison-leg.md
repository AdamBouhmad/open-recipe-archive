## Ingredients

- Venison leg from fallow deer
- Lard strips
- Margarine or fat
- Potatoes
- Currant jelly
- Apple sauce or apple compote

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the leg.
2. Lard densely with fine lard strips and cover with margarine or fat.
3. Roast and baste as with venison back, allowing 2 to 3 hours of roasting depending on size.
4. Serve with browned potatoes, currant jelly, apple sauce or apple compote.
5. Make the sauce as ordinary game sauce.
