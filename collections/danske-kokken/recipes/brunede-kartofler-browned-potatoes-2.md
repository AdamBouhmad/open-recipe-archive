## Ingredients

- Butter
- Powdered sugar or syrup
- Cooked peeled potatoes

## Directions

1. Brown a piece of butter in the pan, stir in powdered sugar or syrup and cooked peeled potatoes cut into pieces and cook slowly until they are brown.
