## Ingredients

- pigeons
- aspic with isinglass
- small pickled pearl onions
- cooked salted tongue
- parsley or lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. When the pigeons are roasted, portion them in 2 or 4 pieces
2. Cover them with the aspic, mixed with isinglass (see roasted duck in aspic recipe), in which small pickled pearl onions have been cooked
3. On the dish where the mold is turned out, arrange a ring of cooked salted tongue
4. Between the tongue pieces, insert parsley or lettuce leaves
