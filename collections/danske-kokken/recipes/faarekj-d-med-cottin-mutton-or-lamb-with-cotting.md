## Ingredients

- Fat mutton or lamb meat
- Butter
- Flour
- Nutmeg
- Cayenne
- Cotting (a type of coating)

## Directions

1. Cook the fat mutton or lamb meat first, but not too thoroughly, and let it cool.
2. Prepare a sauce with a roux of butter and flour, slightly thinner than for fricassee, seasoned with nutmeg and cayenne; let this sauce also cool.
3. Arrange the pieces tightly together in a deep dish so that the upper side is reasonably flat without protruding bones.
4. Pour the sauce over the meat.
5. Spread the cotting over to a finger's thickness.
6. Make a hole in the center to allow steam to escape.
7. Brush the whole with beaten egg.
8. Place paper over once the crust is golden; it must not become brown.
9. Keep more sauce in the dish than goes on the meat, as it reduces considerably.
