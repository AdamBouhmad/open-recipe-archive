## Ingredients

- half a Pound Wheat Flour
- sweet Milk as needed
- a small piece melted Butter
- 3 Eggs
- a small handful Sugar
- a very little Cinnamon
- grated Lemon Peel

## Directions

1. Sift half a Pound Wheat Flour and stir in as much sweet Milk that the batter becomes a little thicker than it should be
2. Add a small piece melted Butter, 3 Eggs (one at a time), a small handful Sugar, and a very little Cinnamon and grated Lemon Peel
