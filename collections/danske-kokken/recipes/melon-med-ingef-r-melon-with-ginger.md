## Ingredients

- Melon, peeled and cut into finger-thick pieces
- Powdered loaf sugar
- Ground ginger
- Ground white pepper
- Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Briefly place the peeled melon cut into finger-thick pieces in boiling water for a boil.
2. Pour it onto a colander and cover with cold water.
3. Layer the melon pieces in a jar with a mixture of sugar, ginger, pepper, and a very slight amount of cayenne pepper, and leave covered until the next day.
4. Pour off the liquid, cook and skim it, then place the melon in it for a single minute, remove onto a colander, and pour it from the jar, but only after the liquid has well drained.
5. Reduce this liquid until it is even, and pour it hot over the fruit, which is then sealed.
