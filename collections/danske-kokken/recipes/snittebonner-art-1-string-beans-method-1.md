## Ingredients

- string beans
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place the beans in boiling water and cook without a lid until tender
2. Shake the water off them and place them again over heat with 1 ounce butter, 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, 1 teaspoon chopped onion and a little salt and pepper
3. Serve warm
