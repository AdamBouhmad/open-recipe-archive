## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 12 ounces bitter almonds
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg white
- 1 egg
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- oblat wafer slices

## Directions

1. Grind sweet and bitter almonds with 1 egg yolk and 1 egg white until it becomes a tough dough
2. Stir 1 egg well with 1 pound powdered sugar and mix together with the almond dough
3. Spread the dough to the thickness of a straw on oblong slices of oblat
4. Bake the cakes light brown at slow heat
