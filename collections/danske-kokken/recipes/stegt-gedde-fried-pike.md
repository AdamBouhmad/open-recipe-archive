## Ingredients

- Pike
- Lard strips
- Gravy
- Potato flour
- Sherry
- Paprika
- Chutney
- Oyster or lobster sauce

## Directions

1. The pike can be fried whole or split, but if split the backbone is removed
2. After cutting a crosswise slit at the nape, the skin is pulled off
3. After the pike is well rinsed, lard it thickly with fine, fresh lard strips and place it in a hot oven
4. Baste frequently with good gravy during roasting, otherwise it will easily become dry
5. When fried, strain the gravy, skim it, and thicken it with potato flour mixed in Sherry
6. Season the sauce further with paprika and chutney, which are very piquant with fried fish
7. Oyster or lobster sauce can also be used
