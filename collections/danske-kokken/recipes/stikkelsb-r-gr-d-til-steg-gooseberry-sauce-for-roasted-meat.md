## Ingredients

- unripe berries
- very little water
- a piece of rinsed butter, the size of a walnut, per quart of berries
- a few pieces of whole cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take unripe berries, but do not peel them first
2. After stems and flower buds have been removed, place them on the fire with very little water and a piece of rinsed butter the size of a walnut per quart of berries
3. They should only be cooked until soft, but not broken
4. Cook a few pieces of whole cinnamon with the berries
5. Add sugar after the sauce is cooked, but it should not be very sweet
