## Ingredients

- 2 pints sweet cream
- quarter pound powdered sugar
- 5 quarters dissolved isinglass
- half pint Maraschino liqueur or Madeira
- preserved plums for garnish

## Directions

1. Whip 2 pints of sweet cream to stiff foam with a quarter pound of powdered sugar, and into this mix 5 quarters of dissolved isinglass and half pint of Maraschino liqueur or Madeira.
2. The cream is poured into tall glass bowls and garnished with preserved plums.
3. Biscuits are served with it.
