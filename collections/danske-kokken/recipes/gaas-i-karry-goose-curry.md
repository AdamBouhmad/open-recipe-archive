## Ingredients

- 1 whole goose
- salt
- water
- curry sauce ingredients (as per Nr. 780)
- cooked rice

## Directions

1. Cut the goose into pieces and boil in water with salt and a whisk.
2. When tender, prepare the curry sauce as Nr. 780.
3. Place the meat in the sauce and serve in a ring formed of loose cooked rice, sprinkled with curry.
