## Ingredients

- oysters
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or Palmin
- lemon
- oyster water

## Directions

1. Clean the oysters and remove from shells.
2. Dip in beaten egg and bread crumbs.
3. Brown in butter or Palmin.
4. Serve with lemon.
5. Cook the oyster water and beards to make sauce, which can be preserved in well-stoppered bottles.
6. Arrange on napkin garnished with lemon and toasted bread.
