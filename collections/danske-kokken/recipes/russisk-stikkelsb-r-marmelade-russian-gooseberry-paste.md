## Ingredients

- Gooseberry jelly scraps
- Sugar
- Powdered sugar (1½ kg to ½ kg of sieved pulp)
- Oil (for greasing)
- Optional: various essences, colors, chopped or sliced almonds, or nut kernels

## Directions

1. Press the scraps from gooseberry jelly through a sieve.
2. Cook the pulp with sugar until a fairly firm mass forms.
3. Color the mass before spreading it out on flat pans greased with oil.
4. Let the marmalade dry and store as explained in the first recipe.
5. The marmalade may have various essences and colors added to it, and chopped or sliced almonds or nut kernels may be stirred in if it is not to be used for cutting out as decoration on cakes and cheeses.
