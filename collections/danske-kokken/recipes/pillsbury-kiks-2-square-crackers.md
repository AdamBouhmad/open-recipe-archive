## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg Pillsbury flour
- 150 g cold margarine
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons deer salt
- just over 1/4 L cold water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together into a dough
2. Let the dough rest 10 minutes in a cold place
3. Roll out very thinly
4. Prick well
5. Cut out and bake as previously instructed
6. The crackers should be pale golden
