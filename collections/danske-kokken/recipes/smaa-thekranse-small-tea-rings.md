## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- ½ pound butter
- 1 cup cream
- a little yeast

## Directions

1. Knead together 1 pound fine flour, ½ pound butter, 1 cup cream, and a little yeast into a batter
2. Form small rings
3. Bake light brown
