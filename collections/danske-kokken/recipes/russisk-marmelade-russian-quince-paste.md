## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar
- Blanched and chopped nut kernels (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the quinces and cook them completely soft in water.
2. Press the cooked quinces through a sieve.
3. Weigh the resulting pulp and cook it with sugar until it is so firm that it does not immediately flow back together after a spoon is drawn through it.
4. If desired, blanched and chopped nut kernels may be cooked into the marmalade.
5. Spread it out, let it dry, and store as previously explained.
6. Any scraps from quince jelly may be pressed through a sieve, weighed, cooked with sugar, and treated as the other Russian marmalades.
