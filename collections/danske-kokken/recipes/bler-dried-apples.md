## Ingredients

- Firm, slightly tart and partly ripe apples
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and dry them whole, cut into 4 pieces, or cut into rings with the core removed using a core remover
2. As the fruit is peeled, immediately place it in salt water (6 to 8 grams salt to 1 liter cold water)
3. Before drying, place the fruit on a sieve so all water can drain off
4. Place whole apples in the oven side by side with the stem end facing upward
5. Place quartered pieces with the round side against the tray
6. Lay rings in rows so they overlap slightly, as they will shrink as they dry
7. Drying time for whole fruits is up to 4 to 5 hours, while cut pieces take at most 2 to 3 hours
8. The rings should be spongy when broken to check if they are finished
9. Store the apples in boxes or linen bags
