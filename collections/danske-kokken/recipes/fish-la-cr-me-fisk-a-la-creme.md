## Ingredients

- Salt water fish such as cod or mackerel
- Salt
- 1 ounce flour
- 1 quart cream or good milk
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 bunch parsley
- 1 finely chopped onion
- Salt and pepper
- ½ pound butter
- Egg whites from 6 eggs

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish, rub it with salt and place it whole in enough boiling water to completely cover it, then place over heat.
2. Once it boils, move it to the back of the oven where it can simmer gently for 1 hour.
3. Remove and pick out all the bones.
4. In a casserole, blend 1 ounce flour with 1 quart cream or good milk, adding it little by little.
5. Add the juice of 1 lemon, 1 bunch parsley, 1 finely chopped onion, and salt and pepper.
6. Place over heat and stir until it becomes a thick sauce, then add ½ pound butter.
7. Bring to a boil and strain.
8. Pour a little sauce into a deep dish, place the fish in it and pour the remaining sauce over.
9. Beat the whites of 6 eggs to a foam, spread over the top and bake until light brown in the oven.
