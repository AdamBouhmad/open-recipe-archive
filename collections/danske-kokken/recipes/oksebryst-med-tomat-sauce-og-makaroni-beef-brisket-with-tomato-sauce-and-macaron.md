## Ingredients

- Leftover beef
- Tomato Sauce (see Nr. 790)
- Cooked macaroni
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Heat leftover beef in tomato sauce
2. Serve with cooked macaroni sprinkled with Parmesan cheese
