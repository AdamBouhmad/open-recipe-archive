## Ingredients

- 6 pounds beef shank, chopped
- 2 pounds veal shank
- 1 not too young hen
- 8 pots water

## Directions

1. Chop the beef shank and veal shank. If the hen is to be used whole later, do not chop it; otherwise chop it as well.
2. Place all the meat over heat with 8 pots water and cook in the same manner as Bouillon No. 1.
3. When the soup is strained, pour it back into the pot without the sediment, and now cook it over gentle heat with regular stirring until it becomes brown and even, approximately like syrup.
4. It is then ready and poured onto a flat dish to cool and can be kept for a long time.
5. When you wish to use it, cut off appropriate pieces, which are dissolved in boiling water.
