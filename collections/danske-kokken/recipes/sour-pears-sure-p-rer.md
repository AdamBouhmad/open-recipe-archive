## Ingredients

- 23 Pd. Melis
- 5 Pægl vinegar
- 5 Pd. peeled, chopped gray pears

## Directions

1. Dissolve the Melis in vinegar
2. Add the chopped pears and boil for 3 hours at even heat, skimming them well
