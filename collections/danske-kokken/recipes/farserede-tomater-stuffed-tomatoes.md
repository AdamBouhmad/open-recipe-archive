## Ingredients

- large red tomatoes of uniform size
- fish forcemeat
- fish stock made from trimmings
- butter
- flour
- salad or parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a cap from the top of each tomato.
2. Hollow out the tomatoes and fill with fish forcemeat, leaving space for it to rise during cooking.
3. Replace the cap.
4. Place the tomatoes in a shallow casserole with boiling water.
5. Cook until the forcemeat is thoroughly cooked.
6. Cook the hollowed-out tomato flesh in fish stock made from the forcemeat trimmings.
7. Strain the stock and use to thin the sauce made from butter and flour.
8. Garnish with salad or parsley.
