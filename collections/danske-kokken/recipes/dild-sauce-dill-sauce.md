## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- egg yolks
- fresh dill
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bake butter and flour together and thin with bouillon.
2. Thicken with a couple of egg yolks.
3. Finally add finely chopped dill and a little lemon juice.
