## Ingredients

- Pie dough
- Mince pie filling

## Directions

1. Line the form with good dough.
2. Put in a thick layer of filling.
3. Lay dough over as a lid.
4. Prick holes in the lid and bake in a hot oven; they should be quite light brown, but not hard.
