## Ingredients

- 1 bottle white French wine (Graves)
- 3 quarter-pounds white sugar
- juice of 3 lemons
- rind of 2 lemons
- 8 quintals dissolved isinglass
- 16 egg yolks
- 1 pail Madeira or Sherry
- 2 pails sweet cream, whipped to stiff foam

## Directions

1. Boil 1 bottle white French wine with 3 quarter-pounds white sugar, the juice of 3 lemons, and the rind of 2 lemons, along with 8 quintals dissolved isinglass.
2. When the sugar is dissolved, strain through a sieve.
3. Whip 16 egg yolks until white and add them to this mixture.
4. Place the whole over heat again and bring to a boil while stirring constantly.
5. Add 1 pail Madeira or Sherry.
6. When almost cold, stir in 2 pails sweet cream whipped to stiff foam.
7. Pour the mixture into a wet mold and let it stand for several hours in a cold place.
8. Serve cold with cream sauce.
