## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Salt
- Onions
- Nutmeg
- Peppercorns
- Bay leaves
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the mussels in several changes of cold water and remove the black tuft.
2. Place them in a pot over the fire without water, but with a little salt and some onions between them.
3. Cook covered with their own liquid and shake occasionally.
4. When cooked and all opened, serve hot in the shells with stirred butter, ground pepper, vinegar and lemon.
5. To preserve them for use in sauces or cold dishes: Remove the mussels from the shells and cut off the beard and black intestine.
6. Cook the trimmings along with the juice from the mussels with nutmeg, peppercorns, some bay leaves and a little vinegar.
7. Strain and pour over the mussels, which are meanwhile placed in a crock or glass.
8. For longer storage, seal the glass with butter.
