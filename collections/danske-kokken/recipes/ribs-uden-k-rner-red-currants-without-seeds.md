## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg soft sugar
- 2 1/2 dl water
- 1/2 kg picked red currants without seeds

## Directions

1. Pick large ripe currants carefully from the stems so they remain whole
2. Remove the seeds with a piping bag
3. Cook sugar, water and the juice from the berries to a very thick syrup in which the fruit gets one boil
4. Remove the berries on a colander and after the juice has drained well pour them into a jar while the juice is further reduced and poured hot over them
5. Seal as soon as possible
