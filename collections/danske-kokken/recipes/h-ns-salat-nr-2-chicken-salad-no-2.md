## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into very small pieces
- Celery, cut into same-sized pieces
- Equal parts chicken and celery
- Hard-boiled eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard per egg
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon oil per egg
- 1 wine glass vinegar per egg

## Directions

1. Cut chicken meat from bones into very small pieces, almost like peas
2. Cut celery the same way
3. Use equal parts chicken and celery
4. Grind hard-boiled egg yolks finely; to each egg add 1/2 teaspoon mustard, same amount salt, 1 tablespoon oil, and 1 wine glass vinegar
5. Mix chicken and celery well in a deep dish
6. Pour the sauce (to which a little cream has been added at the end) over them
7. Do not pour the sauce over until the salad is to be served
