## Ingredients

- 25 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered icing sugar
- Half a pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- Zest of 5 lemons
- Egg whites, whipped to stiff peaks
- Orange or lemon cream (prepared as Nr. 273)
- White sugar glaze with orange juice
- Orange slices
- Powdered sugar glaze for decoration

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with 1 pound powdered icing sugar.
2. Melt half a pound butter in a warm oven and sift 1 pound flour on white paper.
3. When the sugar and eggs are whisked completely white, add the warm butter, then the flour, and finally the whipped egg whites and the grated zest of 5 lemons.
4. Cut paper to fit the pan size as molds, crimp the edges and brush with cold butter.
5. Distribute the mixture into these molds and bake until light brown.
6. Between the layers from the molds, place the orange or lemon cream.
7. Carefully trim the edges and cover the entire tart with a glaze of white sugar dissolved in water and mixed with a little orange juice.
8. Place in the oven to dry.
9. Dry orange slices (with seeds removed) in a lukewarm oven, glaze them by dipping with a thin pointed stick into boiled white sugar, and when dry, arrange in a ring on the outer edge of the cake's top layer.
10. Decorate the whole cake with decorative sugar glaze in figures, in whose larger spaces you can place jelly.
