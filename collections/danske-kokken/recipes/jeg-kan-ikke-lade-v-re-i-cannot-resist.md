## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 4 pounds powdered, sifted sugar
- Juice of 2 large or 3 small lemons
- Egg whites
- 6 quintals cold, dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Stir 10 egg yolks well with 4 pounds powdered, sifted sugar
2. Stir in the juice of 2 large or 3 small lemons
3. Add the stiffly whipped egg whites and 6 quintals cold, dissolved isinglass
4. Let it stand for an hour and serve with whipped cream over and small biscuits on the side
