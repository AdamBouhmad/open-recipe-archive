## Ingredients

- Canned artichoke hearts
- Hollandaise sauce
- Breadcrumbs
- Egg
- Clarified butter
- Salad leaves or parsley
- Lemon slices
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Open the cans and empty the artichoke hearts into a saucepan so they can be warmed in the liquid they are in.
2. Dip the warm hearts in a good, warm hollandaise sauce and lay them on a flat platter to cool.
3. Then dip them in finely ground bread, then in egg, and again in bread and cook in clarified butter until they get a nice brown color.
4. Serve on a warm platter with a nicely folded napkin, decorated with salad or parsley and lemon slices.
5. Serve with melted butter.
