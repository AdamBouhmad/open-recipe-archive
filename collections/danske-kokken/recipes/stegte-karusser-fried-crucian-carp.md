## Ingredients

- Small crucian carp
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Browned butter or Palmin

## Directions

1. Scrape off the scales and clean the fish thoroughly, then rinse.
2. Sprinkle with salt.
3. Dry the fish in a cloth before frying.
4. Dip in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
5. Fry in browned butter or Palmin.
6. Serve with browned butter and lemon, or English fish sauce, accompanied by boiled potatoes.
