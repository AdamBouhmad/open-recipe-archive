## Ingredients

- 1½ kg (3 lb) striped side pork, mildly salted
- Onion slices
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Soup or broth
- Ground pepper
- Coloring (kulør)
- Potato puree or boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. The pork should preferably be mildly salted striped side pork, which is cut into thin slices with the rind removed.
2. Fry the slices on a hot pan until they are brown, then turn them and place them on the serving dish.
3. Pour the fat from the pan so that only the salty sediment remains, and rinse the pan before pouring some of the fat back to brown the onion slices in.
4. When the onions are brown and crispy, add 1 tablespoon of flour to the pan and dilute this with soup or broth and add ground pepper and coloring.
5. The sauce can be poured over the pork or served separately.
6. Serve with potato puree or boiled potatoes.
7. If the pork is very salty, the slices can be boiled in water in the pan before frying.
