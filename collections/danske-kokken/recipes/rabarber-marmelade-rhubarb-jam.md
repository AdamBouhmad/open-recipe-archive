## Ingredients

- Rhubarb stalks, peeled and cut very small
- Powdered sugar (1 1/2 kg to 1/2 kg chopped stalks)

## Directions

1. Strip the outer skin from the stalks, which are then cut into very small pieces, much smaller than for compote; rinse them and put them in the pot together with the sugar.
2. The cooking must take place over low heat and with constant stirring to prevent the stalks from burning on the bottom of the pot.
3. Cook the stalks for about 1/2 hour, fill into jars, which are covered with paper and tied up.
4. This jam can in many cases be used instead of apples, especially in baking and the like.
