## Ingredients

- pumpkin, cut into very small pieces
- salt
- water
- sauce ingredients as for stewed potatoes
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Let the pumpkin pieces stand strewn with salt for 1 hour
2. Rinse the pumpkin
3. Place it in boiling water
4. As soon as it is cooked, drain it
5. Turn it into a sauce as prepared for stewed potatoes
6. Add chopped parsley to the sauce
7. Serve with salted meat dishes
