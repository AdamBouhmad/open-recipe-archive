## Ingredients

- 2 ounces butter
- ½ pound raisins
- ½ pound currants
- 2 ounces candied peel
- 6 ounces sugar
- 6 eggs
- 6 ounces flour

## Directions

1. Heat 2 ounces butter near the fire until soft without becoming oily
2. Line a cake pan internally with paper that is buttered
3. Remove the stones from ½ pound raisins and wash and dry ½ pound currants thoroughly
4. Cut 2 ounces candied peel into thin small slices
5. Stir 6 ounces sugar to cream with 2 eggs; then add 2 more, stir for a couple of minutes and finally a fifth egg, then beat again slightly
6. Sift 6 ounces flour into this and stir evenly; then add the butter and fruit, and when well mixed, pour into the pan and bake in a moderately warm oven for approximately half an hour
7. Test in the usual way
