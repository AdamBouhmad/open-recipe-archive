## Ingredients

- Klipfisk (Dried salted cod)
- Natron
- lunkent Vand (Lukewarm water)
- Smør (Butter)
- haardkogte Æg (Hard-boiled eggs)
- Persille (Parsley)
- smeltet Smør (Melted butter)
- Sennep-Sauce (Mustard sauce)
- kogte Kartofler (Boiled potatoes)

## Directions

1. Cut the dried salted cod in half.
2. Soak for 1 day in lukewarm water with a small amount of sodium bicarbonate.
3. If very salty, change the water several times.
4. Before cooking, scrape the grey skin well and remove the fins.
5. Place in cold water over heat and cook for about half an hour, skumming well.
6. Arrange on a platter with skin side up, garnished with sliced hard-boiled eggs and decorated with parsley.
7. Serve with melted butter and mustard sauce (No. 789) along with boiled potatoes.
8. The eggs can also be chopped and served separately with the fish.
