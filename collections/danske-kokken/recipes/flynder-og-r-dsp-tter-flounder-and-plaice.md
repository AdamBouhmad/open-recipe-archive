## Ingredients

- flounder or plaice
- salt
- boiled potatoes
- melted butter
- parsley
- eggs
- cream
- breadcrumbs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and discard it along with the innards. Skin the flounder, but not the plaice.
2. Both can be boiled by placing them in boiling water with a little salt. If they are large, cut them into pieces.
3. Serve with either melted butter with parsley or sour butter sauce, along with boiled potatoes.
4. If they are to be fried, use the method described for Perch (No. 85), but without parsley filling.
