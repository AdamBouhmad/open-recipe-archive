## Ingredients

- ½ pound fine flour
- 36 quintal butter
- 12 quintal finely ground powdered sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- egg white for dipping
- coarse white powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Knead together ½ pound fine flour, 36 quintal butter, 12 quintal finely ground powdered sugar, and 1 egg yolk
2. Form into rings
3. Dip in egg white and coarse white powdered sugar
4. Bake at slow heat
5. From this you will get about 60 rings
