## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram sifted rye flour
- 125 grams margarine
- 1/4 liter cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Mix together the rye flour, margarine, cream, salt, and caraway seeds
2. Roll out thin and prick with a fork
3. Bake as the previous recipe. These are cheaper but not as crispy
