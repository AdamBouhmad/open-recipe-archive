## Ingredients

- Veal
- Salt
- Water
- Onion
- Melted butter
- Flour
- Curry
- Rice

## Directions

1. Chop and cut the meat into small pieces, rinse in several batches of cold water and blanch by placing in boiling water, immediately remove and pour cold water over it.
2. Cook grated onion in just under 10 minutes in melted butter, then add flour, curry and salt, cook the whole together and thin it with boiling water until it becomes as smooth as thin gruel.
3. Place the meat in this and cook covered until tender, thinning occasionally with boiling water.
4. To clear the sauce, strain it after the meat has been removed.
5. Serve rice cooked in water (see recipe 6) alongside.
