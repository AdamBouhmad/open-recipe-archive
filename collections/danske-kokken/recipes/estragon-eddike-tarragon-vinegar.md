## Ingredients

- Fresh tarragon leaves
- good grape vinegar

## Directions

1. Dry fresh tarragon leaves in the shade and then place them in a bottle, which is filled with good grape vinegar.
2. Store the bottle in a warm place and shake it daily.
3. Let it stand for one month's time, then strain the vinegar through a fine cloth, pour into a bottle, and stopper it.
4. It is then ready for use. Fresh vinegar can again be poured over the leaves.
