## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram (2 pund) squash
- 375 milliliters (1/4 pægel) vinegar
- 500 grams (1 pund) powdered sugar
- 75 grams (15 kvint) whole ginger

## Directions

1. Peel the squash, hollow it out, and cut it like melon.
2. Cook a syrup from the vinegar and sugar.
3. Cook the squash together with whole ginger wrapped in a clean cloth until the squash is clear.
4. Remove the fruit and reduce the syrup until it is even, then pour it over.
