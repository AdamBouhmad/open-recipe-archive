## Ingredients

- A piece of inner thigh, well beaten and larded
- Half vinegar and half water
- Butter or claret
- Milk
- Flour
- Couleur (coloring)
- Browned potatoes for serving
- Redcurrant jelly for serving

## Directions

1. The evening before use, place the meat in a mixture of half vinegar and half water.
2. Brown butter or claret in a pot and add the meat, browning it on all sides.
3. Pour milk over the meat and roast it covered for about 3 hours.
4. Strain the gravy from the roast and thicken it with browned butter and flour mixed together to make a sauce.
5. Color the sauce with couleur.
6. Pour a couple of spoonfuls of sauce over the roast and serve with browned potatoes and redcurrant jelly.
