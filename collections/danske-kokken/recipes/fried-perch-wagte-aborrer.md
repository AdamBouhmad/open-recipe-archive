## Ingredients

- Perch
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley
- Chopped onion
- Beaten egg whites
- Bread
- Caper sauce

## Directions

1. After the fish is cut and washed, split it in two parts.
2. Remove the bones, peel off the skin and add vinegar, oil, pepper, salt, parsley and chopped onion, letting it stand for 2-3 hours.
3. Then take out the fish, dry it in a cloth, dip it in beaten egg whites, then in bread and fry in a pan.
4. Serve with caper sauce.
5. Alternatively, split cleaned perch from the back, lay them in brine or sprinkle with salt.
6. Dry them, coat with capers mixed with parsley, ground pepper and butter.
7. Put them together, spread butter on the outside, roast on a grate and serve with the same sauce.
