## Ingredients

- 1 kg cauliflower, divided into small florets
- 0.5 l vinegar
- 3 pods Spanish pepper
- 1 tablespoon whole white pepper

## Directions

1. Divide the cauliflower into small florets, rinse and give it a good boil in salted water
2. Drain the cauliflower in a sieve
3. Boil the vinegar with 3 pods of Spanish pepper and 1 tablespoon whole white pepper
4. Place the cauliflower in a jar, pour the boiling vinegar over it and cover with a cloth
5. The next day, bind the jar with parchment paper
