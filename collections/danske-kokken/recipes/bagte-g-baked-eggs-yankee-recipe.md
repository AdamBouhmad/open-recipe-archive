## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- Butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- A little butter
- 3 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Break 8 eggs into a well-buttered bowl
2. Add pepper, salt, a little butter, and 3 tablespoons cream
3. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes and serve hot
