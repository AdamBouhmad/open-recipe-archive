## Ingredients

- Damaged cherries not suitable for whole preservation
- Powdered sugar, ½ pound per pint juice

## Directions

1. All the worst berries that are not suitable for preserving whole are pitted, crushed, and wrung out in a cloth
2. Weigh the juice, place on the fire with ½ pound powdered sugar per pint juice
3. Skim it, boil well for 10 to 15 minutes and pour onto bottles
