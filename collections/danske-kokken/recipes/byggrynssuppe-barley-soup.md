## Ingredients

- 2 measures medium-fine barley grits
- 4 to 5 pots water
- peeled apple pieces
- dried fruit
- prunes
- raisins
- sour fruit juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the barley grits in cold water by stirring with your hands, then pour away the dirty water.
2. Set 4 to 5 pots of water over the fire (less water is needed if the grits are finer) and bring to a boil.
3. Add the grits and cook covered until tender.
4. Add peeled apple pieces, dried fruit, prunes and raisins that have first been rinsed in cold water.
5. When ready to serve, season the soup with sour juice and sugar to taste.
