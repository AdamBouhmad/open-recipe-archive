## Ingredients

- Veal tenderloin
- Lard
- Butter
- Broth
- Madeira
- Sago flour
- Vegetables for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the inner fillet before the back is larded and roasted.
2. Beat the tenderloin lightly and remove the skin.
3. Lard and brown the meat, then roast for just under half an hour, basting regularly with pan drippings.
4. Make a good Madeira sauce from the pan drippings of the fillet, thickened with a little sago flour and flavored with Madeira.
5. Garnish the tenderloin with vegetables.
