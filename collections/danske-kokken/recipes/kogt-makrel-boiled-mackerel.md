## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- Salt
- Bay leaves
- Peppercorns
- Parsley
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Ensure the mackerel is firm and stiff when purchasing
2. Clean as you would salmon
3. Keep the head on
4. Boil the mackerel whole in cold water with salt, bay leaves and whole peppercorns for a quarter of an hour
5. Remove from water immediately once cooked
6. Arrange on a platter
7. Garnish with parsley and cooked potatoes
