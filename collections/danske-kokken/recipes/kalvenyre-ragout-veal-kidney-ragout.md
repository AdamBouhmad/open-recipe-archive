## Ingredients

- Roasted or boiled kidneys, finely chopped
- Other finely chopped meat (optional)
- Browned butter
- Flour
- Stock or soup
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Paprika
- Wine
- Coloring
- Mushrooms or a little of the pickling liquid they are preserved in

## Directions

1. Cut roasted or boiled kidneys very finely as for salad
2. Mix in other finely chopped meat if desired
3. Make a fairly thick sauce from browned butter, flour, stock or soup, Maggi bouillon cubes, paprika, wine, and coloring
4. Add the chopped kidney meat to the sauce along with mushrooms or a little of their pickling liquid
5. Bring to a boil
6. Serve on slices of French bread fried in butter, or fill into rolls, Roseljer (No. 27), or croustades (No. 1), etc.
7. Can also be served on breaded potatoes
8. Use the same preparation for lamb kidneys
