## Ingredients

- Seeds or berries
- 125 gr (1/4 lb) salt
- 1 l (1 pot) boiling water
- Vinegar, as much as needed to cover the seeds
- Several whole white peppercorns

## Directions

1. Place the seeds or berries in a brine of 125 gr (1/4 lb) salt to 1 l (1 pot) boiling water.
2. The next day, drain them in a colander and let them stand so the brine drains well.
3. Bring as much vinegar as is necessary to cover the seeds to a boil together with some whole white peppercorns.
4. Place the seeds directly into the boiling vinegar and then pour into jars or glasses, which are covered with a cloth and tied shut.
