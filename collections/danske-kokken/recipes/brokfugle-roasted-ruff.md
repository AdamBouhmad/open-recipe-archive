## Ingredients

- Ruff
- Gizzard, heart and liver
- French bread slices

## Directions

1. Pluck and roast the ruff as for partridge (see No. 960).
2. The gizzard, heart and liver can be prepared like woodcock farce and used to stuff the birds before roasting, or the farce can be served on slices of French bread placed around the birds. (See Baking woodcock bread, No. 979).
