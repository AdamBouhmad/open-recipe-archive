## Ingredients

- veal or lamb from the leg or best shank
- beef fat
- salt
- pepper
- shallot
- wheat flour
- eggs
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Prepare exactly as described for Fars af Oxekjød
2. The meat is taken from the leg or the best shank and beaten strongly with a mallet before scraping
