## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef sirloin
- Pickled shallots
- Bacon strips
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Palmin or claret
- Water
- Coloring
- Butter
- Flour
- Cooked potatoes or mashed potatoes
- Pickles or preserved vegetables

## Directions

1. The beef, which must not be freshly slaughtered, is beaten on all sides and larded with the help of a pointed knife very densely with pickled shallots and fairly thick bacon strips dipped in a mixture of salt, ground pepper, allspice and cloves
2. The meat is rubbed with the spices
3. Brown it in palmin or claret in a pot and then pour water with coloring over it
4. Roast it covered for 3 hours and turn during roasting
5. Make the sauce as usual from butter, flour and gravy
6. When serving, pour just a little sauce over the roast and serve the rest in a sauce bowl
7. Cooked potatoes or mashed potatoes and either preserved vegetables or pickles can be served with it
