## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Brown caper sauce or other sauce
- Veal or lean pork for the filling
- Meat ring (No. 215)
- Butter pastry ring (No. 34, optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the beef tongue and cut into slices
2. Prepare with one of the above-mentioned sauces
3. Arrange in a meat ring (see No. 215) with filling made from veal or lean pork
4. Can be further decorated with a butter pastry ring around the meat ring (see No. 34)
