## Ingredients

- butter dough
- raspberry jam
- thick cold vanilla cream
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Line small forms with butter dough
2. Add a little raspberry jam
3. Top with a thick, cold vanilla cream
4. Lay a cross of butter dough over the cream
5. Brush the cakes with egg
6. Bake in a warm oven
