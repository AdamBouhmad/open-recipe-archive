## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Soup
- Anchovy or sardine butter
- Filleted anchovies
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour and thin with soup
2. Just before serving, add a good spoonful of anchovy or sardine butter and stir until it melts
3. Cut some filleted anchovies (with the bone removed) into small pieces and add to the sauce together with chopped parsley
4. This sauce can be prepared as a brown sauce by browning the butter, in which the flour is baked, and thinning it with gravy
