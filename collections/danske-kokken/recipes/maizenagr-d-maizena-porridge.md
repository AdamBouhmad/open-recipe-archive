## Ingredients

- 1 package maizena
- 2½ L sweet or semi-skimmed milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- ¼ L cold milk
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 3 egg whites
- almond, lemon, vanilla or orange essence
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- fruit juice and water or beer

## Directions

1. Bring 2 L milk to a boil.
2. Make a liaison by beating 3 egg yolks with 1 tablespoon powdered sugar, mix in the flour and ¼ L cold milk, and stir this into the boiling milk.
3. Let it come to a good boil, then stir in 50 g butter or margarine.
4. After removing from heat, stir in the 3 beaten egg whites.
5. A little almond, lemon, vanilla or orange essence can be stirred in.
6. Serve with butter, sugar and cinnamon, fruit juice and water or beer.
7. Cold maizena porridge can be served as a pudding with cold fruit sauce or compote.
