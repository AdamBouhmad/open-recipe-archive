## Ingredients

- slices of good beef
- farce
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- flour
- brown butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Slices of good beef (not freshly slaughtered and preferably inner loin) are beaten out, and on them is spread a thin layer of farce.
2. In the center place a strip of bacon, which is dipped in a mixture of salt, pepper, and cloves.
3. Roll up the meat, shape like the body of a bird, and fasten together with a row of lard strips.
4. Rub the birds on the outside with the aforementioned spices and dip in flour.
5. In a pot with brown butter or margarine, [preparation continues but is cut off]
