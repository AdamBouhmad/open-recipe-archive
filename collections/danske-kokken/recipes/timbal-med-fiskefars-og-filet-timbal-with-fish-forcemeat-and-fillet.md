## Ingredients

- 4 to 6 plaice
- Butter
- Fish forcemeat
- Fish fillets
- Wine
- Egg yolks
- Crayfish
- Optional: mushrooms, lobster, crayfish extract, mussels, shrimp, asparagus, or tomato puree

## Directions

1. Butter the mold and line it with fish forcemeat
2. Fill the center with fillets prepared as described in recipe No. 37
3. Treat and cook as instructed in recipe No. 48
4. Thin the sauce from the fillets and bind with eggs, adding more wine
5. Decorate the timbal with crayfish
6. The sauce can be varied in many different ways, such as with mushrooms, lobster, crayfish, lobster and crayfish extract, mussels, shrimp and asparagus, or tomato puree
