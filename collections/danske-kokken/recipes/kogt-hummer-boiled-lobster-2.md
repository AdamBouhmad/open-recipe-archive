## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Place the lobster in cold water and bring to a boil.
2. The lobster suffers less this way as it dies as soon as the water becomes warm, and its meat will be more tender.
3. Cook for 15 to 30 minutes depending on its size; very large ones may need an hour.
4. When cooked, remove it and split lengthwise from head to tail.
5. Remove the stomach (which follows right after the head) and the dark or bluish vein that runs from near the head to the end of the tail.
