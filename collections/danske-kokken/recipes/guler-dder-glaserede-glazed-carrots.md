## Ingredients

- cooked large carrots
- butter
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. If small carrots are not available, cut cooked large carrots into slices with a fluted vegetable knife
2. On a pan, brown a little butter
3. Add a small amount of sugar and salt
4. Toss the carrot slices in this mixture
5. Serve with boiled and fried meat dishes
