## Ingredients

- 2 lobsters
- 4 heads fresh green lettuce
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- Cayenne pepper
- Salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 2 cups vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick lobster meat finely
2. Cut fresh green lettuce finely; layer with lobster meat in a fairly deep dish
3. Grind hard-boiled egg yolks and mix with 3 tablespoons melted butter, 1 teaspoon mustard, cayenne pepper and salt to taste, 2 teaspoons sugar, and 2 cups vinegar
4. Heat the mixture over the stove and pour over the salad while warm
