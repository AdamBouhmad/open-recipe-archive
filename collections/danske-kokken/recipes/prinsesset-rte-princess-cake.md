## Ingredients

- 1 pound clarified butter
- 0.5 pound powdered sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 40 quintal potato flour
- 30 quintal flour
- zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. When the butter is cooled, stir it well with the egg yolks, one by one, then with the sugar and lemon zest
2. Stir in the flour, and finally fold in the 6 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
3. Bake 6 to 7 round, flat cakes as for layer cake either in the oven or in a cake pan on paper dipped in melted butter
4. When done, remove carefully from the paper as they are very brittle, and trim immediately
5. Between the layers place a mixture made of 1 pound powdered sugar, the zest and juice of 1 lemon, and 4 cups good white rum
6. When the cake is assembled, glaze it in the usual manner
