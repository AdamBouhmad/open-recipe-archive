## Ingredients

- Kraasemaden af 2 Gæs eller 4 Ænder (giblets from 2 geese or 4 ducks)
- Hovederne (heads)
- Tungerne (tongues)
- Halsen (neck)
- De yderste Led af Vingerne (the outer joints of the wings)

## Directions

1. Use giblets from 2 geese or 4 ducks.
2. Clean the giblets carefully, remove the thick inside membrane by scraping, and cut into 2 or 4 pieces.
3. Split the heads and clean them along with the tongues.
4. Clean the neck and the outer joints of the wings.
