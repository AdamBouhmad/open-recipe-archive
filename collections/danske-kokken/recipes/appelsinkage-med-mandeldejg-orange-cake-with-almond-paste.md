## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- Powdered icing sugar
- Half a pound clarified butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered icing sugar
- 1 pound sweet almonds, crushed with 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Remove the peel and white pith from 6 oranges and divide into small pieces.
2. Lay the pieces on the bottom of a pan and sprinkle with powdered icing sugar.
3. Whisk the clarified butter to a smooth paste and add 4 egg yolks, 1 pound powdered icing sugar, and the sweet almonds crushed with 2 whole eggs.
4. Mix well together and finally fold in the whipped egg whites.
5. Pour the dough over the oranges.
6. Place the pan in the oven and bake the cake at good heat from above.
7. Serve with whipped cream.
