## Ingredients

- 1 Pound Butter
- 8 Egg yolks
- 2 Pounds powdered sugar
- 2 Pounds fine wheat flour
- 1/4 Kilo hart's horn salt
- Grated lemon peel
- 8 Egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and stir it white.
2. Beat the egg yolks with 2 pounds powdered sugar and add to the butter.
3. Add 2 pounds fine wheat flour that has first been mixed with 1/4 kilo hart's horn salt and a little grated lemon peel.
4. When this is well mixed together, carefully fold in the 8 whipped egg whites.
5. Bake the cakes in paper forms like layer cake.
6. Place cream between the individual layers.
7. Glaze the top with white sugar and decorate with preserves.
