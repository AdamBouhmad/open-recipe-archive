## Ingredients

- Homemade liver pâté
- French bread slices without crust
- Egg, beaten
- Bread crumbs
- Lard or butter

## Directions

1. Spread a good layer of the homemade liver pâté over French bread slices without crust.
2. Dip the spread side in beaten egg and then in bread crumbs.
3. On a frying pan with boiling lard or preferably a little butter, place the bread slices with the bread crumb side down.
4. Fry until light brown and turn for a moment.
