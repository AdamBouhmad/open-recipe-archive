## Ingredients

- Butter
- Puff pastry
- Large oysters
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks, finely chopped
- Bread crumbs
- 1/2 pint cream
- Flour

## Directions

1. Butter a large pie dish and line the sides and corners with puff pastry.
2. Use the largest and finest oysters possible and strain all liquid from them.
3. Season the oysters with pepper, salt, and butter.
4. Place them on the dish with as much of their own sauce as desired, and sprinkle with the chopped hard-boiled egg yolks and grated bread crumbs.
5. Cover with a pastry lid and press the edges with a fork.
6. Bake at high heat.
7. You may also add 1/2 pint of cream mixed with a little flour.
