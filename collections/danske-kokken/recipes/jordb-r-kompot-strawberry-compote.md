## Ingredients

- 125 gr powdered sugar
- 1.25 dl water for 0.5 kg fruit

## Directions

1. Choose preferably the very large, firm and fleshy berries.
2. Treat them as raspberries. Pineapple strawberries are particularly beautiful for preserving.
