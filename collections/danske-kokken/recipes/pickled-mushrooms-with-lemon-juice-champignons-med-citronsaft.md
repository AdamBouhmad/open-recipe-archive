## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg mushrooms
- juice of approximately 6 lemons

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms and cook according to the instructions for pickled mushrooms, with the difference that these are cooked in the pressed and strained lemon juice
2. There must be enough juice to cover the mushrooms after they are filled into the jars
