## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) unripe peaches
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) powdered loaf sugar
- 1.5 dl (1/3 pint) water

## Directions

1. Dry the peaches and prick or peel them.
2. Split them and remove the pits.
3. Boil and skim the sugar and water before adding the fruit to boil for several minutes, depending on its ripeness.
4. Remove the peaches and place them in the jars, and boil the syrup again for several minutes before pouring it over the fruit.
5. After 3 to 4 days have elapsed, pour off the syrup, skim it and boil it until smooth; then pour it over the peaches.
6. Seal the jars as soon as the preserve has cooled.
