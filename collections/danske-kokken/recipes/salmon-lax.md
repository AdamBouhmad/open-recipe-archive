## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Bay leaves
- Whole pepper
- Parsley
- Butter
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Fine chopped parsley
- Oil, vinegar and pepper for cold service

## Directions

1. Salmon retains the scales and should not be split, but cut into fairly thick pieces crosswise.
2. In the water in which the pieces are boiled, add bay leaves, whole pepper and parsley.
3. They can be boiled whole, but if it is a thick middle piece, it takes a long time to cook through.
4. Serve with butter and vinegar; pepper and finely chopped parsley are added to the sauce at the table.
5. When cold, serve with oil, vinegar and pepper.
