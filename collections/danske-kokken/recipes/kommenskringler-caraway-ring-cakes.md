## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- 6 quints butter
- 6 quints yeast
- 1 measure milk
- caraway seeds
- coarse salt (optional)

## Directions

1. Work 1 pound fine flour, 6 quints butter, 6 quints yeast and 1 measure milk together well and let rise for a moment.
2. Form the dough into fairly large rings and sprinkle with caraway seeds.
3. Let them rise again for a short time and place in boiling water.
4. When they float up, remove them, place on a pan and put in a hot oven to bake light brown.
5. Before baking, you can also sprinkle some coarse salt on them.
