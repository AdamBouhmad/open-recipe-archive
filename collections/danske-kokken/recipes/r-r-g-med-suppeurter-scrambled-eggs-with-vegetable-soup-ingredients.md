## Ingredients

- Cooked soup vegetables, cut into strips
- Beaten eggs
- Chopped parsley or finely cut leek greens

## Directions

1. A portion of cooked soup vegetables cut into strips can be mixed into the egg mixture along with a little chopped parsley or finely cut leek greens, and then the scrambled eggs are made as usual.
