## Ingredients

- ¼ kg (½ pound) stirred butter
- 3 to 4 tablespoons grated horseradish

## Directions

1. To ¼ kg stirred butter, use 3 to 4 tablespoons grated horseradish, which is mixed together with the butter.
2. Used for beef, cutlets, and other fried meat dishes.
