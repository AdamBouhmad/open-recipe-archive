## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 12 bitter almonds
- 62 quintal finely ground powdered sugar, sifted
- zest of 1 lemon
- 10 tablespoons melted butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 10 tablespoons sweet cream
- 12 quintal finely grated bread
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, dry them, and pound them finely
2. Work in the powdered sugar and lemon zest
3. Stir in the melted butter until cool, with 5 egg yolks and 10 tablespoons sweet cream
4. Combine both mixtures and stir together for about an hour
5. Finally add the finely grated bread and the 5 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
6. Pour the entire mixture into a buttered form and bake 1.5 hours at medium heat
