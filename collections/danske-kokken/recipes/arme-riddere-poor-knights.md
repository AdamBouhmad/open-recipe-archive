## Ingredients

- slices of French bread
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or Palmin
- jam

## Directions

1. Dip slices of French bread in milk and let them stand covered for 1/2 hour.
2. Before frying, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, dip in egg and bread crumbs, and fry on a pan in butter or Palmin.
3. Serve hot, covered with jam.
