## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onion
- Paprika
- Flour
- Thin meat or fish stock
- Food coloring
- Skinned and cut eel
- Potato starch
- Sherry
- Simlers or puff pastry strips

## Directions

1. Brown a piece of butter with a grated onion, some paprika, and a spoonful of flour in a pot
2. Add thin meat or fish stock and add coloring to achieve a nice brown color
3. Add the cleaned and cut eel and cook until tender
4. Remove the eel pieces with a skimming ladle and place on the serving platter
5. Skim the fat from the sauce and thicken with a little potato starch if needed
6. Finish the sauce with sherry
7. Pour the sauce over the eel pieces
8. Serve with Simlers or puff pastry strips, or arrange in a puff pastry or potato puree ring
