## Ingredients

- Brown, smooth stock sauce made from stock or soup
- Pepper
- Pickled vegetables
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a brown, smooth stock sauce from stock or soup
2. Add pepper, pickled vegetables and a little vinegar, sugar to taste and culinary color to give it the desired appearance
