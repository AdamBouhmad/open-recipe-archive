## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- half a cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1.5 cups flour
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon cream of tartar
- half a teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Stir sugar and butter first in the usual way
2. Add the rest, and sift the soda with the flour, adding it last
3. Between each cake layer spread a filling made like the coconut jelly cake filling but omitting the coconut
4. Over each layer sprinkle split and blanched almonds approximately one inch apart
5. Bake in layers
