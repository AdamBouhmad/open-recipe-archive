## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon fine salad oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mustard
- Vinegar
- Broth from the chicken
- 1 pint Madeira
- Fine, green salad leaves for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken until very tender, then let it cool
2. Remove the bones and cut the meat into small cubes
3. Place 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, 1 tablespoon fine salad oil, salt, pepper, mustard, vinegar and broth from the chicken over heat, stirring until it begins to boil
4. Remove from heat, strain through a sieve, and add 1 pint Madeira
5. Place the meat in the sauce and cover it well until it is cold and ready to be served
6. Garnish with fine, green salad leaves
