## Ingredients

- Cooked turnips
- False mayonnaise
- Watercress
- Spinach juice

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked turnips into strips and turn them in false mayonnaise and garnish with watercress.
2. Alternatively, color the mayonnaise green with spinach juice.
