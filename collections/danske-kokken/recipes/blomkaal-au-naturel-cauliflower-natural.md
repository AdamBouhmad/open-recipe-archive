## Ingredients

- cauliflower heads
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Select white and firm cauliflower heads
2. Remove the green leaves
3. Cut away the bottom of the stalks
4. Soak the cauliflower in cold water with a little vinegar to remove larvae and insects
5. Place the cauliflower in boiling salted water with the flower facing down
6. Cook for approximately 20 to 25 minutes
7. To keep it white, cook in a mixture of milk and water or wrap in a clear cloth
8. Serve either arranged alone in a pyramid or together with other vegetables such as peas, asparagus, beans, radishes, carrots and leeks, arranged around the cauliflower with carrots placed between them with points upward
9. Serve with butter
