## Ingredients

- Meat or roast
- Flour
- Eggs
- Butter
- Milk
- Sugar
- Salt
- Clarified butter
- Apple sauce, gooseberry sauce, rhubarb stalks, or other vegetables

## Directions

1. Slice the meat or roast into very thin slices.
2. Make a pancake batter from one pound flour, 3 beaten eggs, two ounces melted butter, one pint milk, a little sugar and salt.
3. Dip the meat slices in the batter and fry on the pan in clarified butter.
4. Serve with apple sauce, gooseberry sauce, rhubarb stalks or other vegetables.
5. This portion of batter is sufficient for 8 persons.
