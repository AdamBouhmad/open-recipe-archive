## Ingredients

- Medium-sized crabs
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Ground white pepper
- Cayenne pepper
- Mustard
- Lemon slices
- Parsley or salad leaves

## Directions

1. Medium-sized crabs are best, and the crab should, like the lobster, be heavy when it is full of meat.
2. Cook the crab as you would cook lobster.
3. When cold, split them and pull the meat out.
4. The shell, that is the back shell, is used to arrange the meat in.
5. Cut the meat into small pieces and lay it on one side, while the soft mass in the crab, stirred together with oil, vinegar and salt and with a little ground white pepper and cayenne pepper or with a little mixed mustard, is laid in a stripe on the opposite side.
6. Arrange the shells, garnished with the claws, lemon slices and parsley or salad leaves on a platter, covered with a napkin.
7. The crab meat can also be served in mayonnaise, seasoned with English fish sauce, tarragon vinegar and a little mixed mustard, or the mayonnaise is laid in stripes over the meat, arranged in salad leaves, with which the shell is covered.
