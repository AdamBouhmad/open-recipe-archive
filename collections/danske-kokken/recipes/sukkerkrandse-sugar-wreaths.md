## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 raw egg yolks
- 1/4 pound white or powdered sugar
- 1/4 pound clarified butter
- 1/2 pound flour

## Directions

1. Rub the hard-boiled yolks and mix well with the 4 raw yolks, 1/4 pound white or powdered sugar, 1/4 pound clarified butter, and 1/2 pound flour.
2. Mix to a stiff dough.
3. Take small pieces, dip in flour, roll with hands, and form into small wreaths.
4. Brush with egg white on one side, sprinkle with white coarse sugar, and bake light brown.
5. Makes 30 wreaths.
