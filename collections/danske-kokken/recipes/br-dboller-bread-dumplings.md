## Ingredients

- 1 French Bread
- 100 Gr (20 Kvint) Butter
- 6 Eggs
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cardamom

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from a French bread, and cut the bread into thick slices, which are dipped in milk and left covered for a couple of hours, after which it is worked with a wooden spoon
2. Melt 100 Gr (20 Kvint) butter, and bake the bread in it until it is well cohesive
3. When the dough is slightly cooled, stir it with 6 eggs, salt, sugar and cardamom
4. Boil dumplings in salted water
