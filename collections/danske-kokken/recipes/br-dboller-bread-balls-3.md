## Ingredients

- 5 French bread rolls (or small wheat bread rolls)
- 5 eggs
- 1 good pound butter
- Grated bread
- Flour

## Directions

1. Remove all crust and hard edges from the bread, which is dipped in milk and stirred with the melted butter, egg yolks and finally the egg whites, which are whipped to stiff peaks.
2. Pour the mixture into a form that is greased with butter and sprinkled with grated bread, and cook in the oven.
3. When done, take it out, cut into pieces and serve in the soup.
4. Note: From the same mixture small round balls can be made, which are placed in boiling salted water and are done when they rise to the top.
