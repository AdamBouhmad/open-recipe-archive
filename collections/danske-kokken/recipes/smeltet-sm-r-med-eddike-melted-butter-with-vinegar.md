## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 4 tablespoons water
- ¼ kg (½ pound) cold butter

## Directions

1. Bring 4 tablespoons vinegar and 4 tablespoons water to a boil and remove from the heat.
2. Stir in ¼ kg cold butter while it stands by the heat, but it must not boil.
