## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) flour
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- cardamom
- salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) milk
- egg whites
- butter or Palmin

## Directions

1. From 1/2 Kg (1 S) flour, approximately 15 pancakes are made.
2. Stir 5 egg yolks with 3 tablespoons sugar, a little cardamom, salt and 2 teaspoons baking powder.
3. Mix in 1/2 Kg (1 S) flour along with 1/2 L (1/2 Pot) milk and lastly the beaten egg whites.
4. Bake the dough immediately in thick cakes with butter on the pan for each pancake.
5. Granulated sugar and compote or jam are served with it.
6. Thick pancakes must be baked slowly and turned on a lid.
7. If yeast is used, take 50 Gr (10 Kvint). Set the dough to rise before baking.
