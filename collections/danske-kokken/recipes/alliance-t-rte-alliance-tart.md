## Ingredients

- 9 whole eggs
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 3 quarter-pounds pounded sweet almonds
- 10 quintal bitter almonds
- 0.5 pound softened butter
- 0.5 pound flour
- 1 egg
- white rum
- vanilla cream
- rusks
- jam

## Directions

1. Stir 9 whole eggs well for 0.25 hour and then again for 0.25 hour with 1 pound powdered sugar and finally with 3 quarter-pounds pounded sweet almonds and 10 quintal bitter almonds
2. Make a butter dough (see recipe 186) of 0.5 pound softened butter, 0.5 pound flour, 1 egg, and a little white rum
3. Line the form with this butter dough and dust it with a little pounded rusks
4. On the bottom of the form place a layer of vanilla cream, over it a thin layer of pounded rusks; then a layer of jam, then a thin layer of pounded rusks, after which fill the form with the aforementioned mixed batter
5. Bake the tart immediately at even good heat
6. Test it by inserting a straw; when this comes out dry, the tart is done
