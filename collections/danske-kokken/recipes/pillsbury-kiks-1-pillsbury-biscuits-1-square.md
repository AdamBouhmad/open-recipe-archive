## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram Pillsbury flour
- 125 grams potato flour
- 125 grams powdered sugar
- 125 grams margarine
- 3 teaspoons hartshorn salt, dissolved in 1/4 liter cold water

## Directions

1. Mix the Pillsbury flour, potato flour, powdered sugar, margarine, and dissolved hartshorn salt together
2. Let the dough rest for a quarter of an hour
3. Roll out the biscuits very thin
