## Ingredients

- Lamb meat
- Water
- Salt
- Bouquet garni
- Peppercorns
- Onions
- Flour
- Butter
- Root vegetables for soup
- Green peas
- Cabbage (optional)

## Directions

1. Make as Calf's Fricassée (recipe 140); but to it is usually added a little fresh, cooked and chopped root vegetables such as carrots and parsley roots, as well as shelled peas.
2. The meat can also be prepared as Cabbage Fricassée. Cut the meat into pieces, and each piece is smeared with a little butter, then laid in layers in a pot.
