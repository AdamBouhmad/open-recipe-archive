## Ingredients

- 1 wine glass Banko
- 1 tablespoon vanilla ice cream
- 2 pounds fine sugar
- 1 pot boiling water
- 3 measures arrack (Api-api)
- 4 measures Sherry
- vanilla

## Directions

1. In a wine glass of Banko, place 1 tablespoon vanilla ice cream and drink the Banko thus
2. To make Banko: dissolve 2 pounds fine sugar in 1 pot boiling water
3. When it has cooled, add 3 measures arrack (Api-api), 4 measures Sherry and a little vanilla
4. Filter it
