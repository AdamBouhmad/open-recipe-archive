## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg flour
- 2 eggs
- 50 g sugar
- a little cardamom
- 50 g yeast, dissolved in 1/4 L lukewarm, half-skimmed milk
- 1/4 kg margarine

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, eggs, sugar, cardamom, and yeast dissolved in milk well together
2. Roll out the dough with a rolling pin
3. Spread with 1/4 kg margarine, fold together and roll out repeatedly
4. Form into balls
5. Make a small indentation on top of each and place a small ball the size of a small nut in it
6. Brush with egg and let rise
7. Bake in a well-heated oven
