## Ingredients

- Leftover beef brisket
- Water or soup
- Salt
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Flour
- Parsley, chopped
- Soup or stock
- Claret or Palmin
- Chutney or cucumber sauce (No. 759 and 756)
- Cooked rice or other mixed vegetables

## Directions

1. Give the leftover beef brisket a reheat in water with salt or in soup
2. Brush with egg
3. Sprinkle with a mixture of crushed bread, a little flour and chopped parsley
4. Place the meat in the oven in the roasting pan or on a frying pan
5. Pour a little soup or stock on the pan so it does not burn, or brown it on the frying pan in claret or Palmin
6. As soon as it is browned, serve it with chutney or cucumber sauce (see No. 759 and 756) and cooked rice or other mixed vegetables
