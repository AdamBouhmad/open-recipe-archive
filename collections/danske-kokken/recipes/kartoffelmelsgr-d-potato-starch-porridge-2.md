## Ingredients

- Three-quarter pound potato starch
- 4 pots sweet milk
- One-quarter pound butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix the potato starch gradually with 4 pots of sweet milk and place over heat together with one-quarter pound of butter.
2. Cook with continuous stirring.
3. When fully cooked, add salt and serve immediately, as it becomes long and stringy if allowed to stand.
