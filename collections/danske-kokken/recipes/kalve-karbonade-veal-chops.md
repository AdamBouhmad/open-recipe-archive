## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- egg yolks and whites
- crushed zwieback
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- stock

## Directions

1. Cut the shoulder as close to the back as possible, cut the ribs into finger-length pieces, and divide the meat with a sharp knife so each piece has a bone
2. Remove skin and sinews
3. Scrape the meat from the thinnest part of the bone to make it clean, but ensure the meat is not removed from the thick part of the bone
4. Beat the meat lightly with a mallet, chop it with the back of a knife and shape it into a rounded form
5. Brush each piece with beaten egg yolks and whites using a feather, then coat both sides with crushed zwieback mixed with pepper and salt
6. Fry the chops in a pan with lightly browned butter over high heat, turning during cooking
7. In the remaining browned butter in the pan, add butter and stock, cooking until it begins to separate, then serve as sauce with the chops alongside boiled, peeled potatoes
8. Alternatively, chops can be made from other veal meat mixed with an equal amount of fat pork or bacon, chopped together, and formed into chops without bone
