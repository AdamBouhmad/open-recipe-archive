## Ingredients

- 1 pound blanched sweet almonds
- 5 pounds sweet almonds
- 10 quintals bitter almonds
- 4 egg whites
- 2 pounds powdered sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Caramel
- Decorative sugar
- Sugar figurine

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound blanched sweet almonds into small cubes
2. Pound 5 pounds sweet and 10 quintals bitter almonds finely in a mortar after drying them slightly in the oven overnight
3. Whip 4 egg whites to stiff peaks, then whip with 2 pounds powdered sugar, the zest of 1 lemon, and finally the almonds
4. Form wreaths from this batter using a pastry bag and bake at gentle oven heat either on a wax-buttered plate or on wafers
5. Join the wreaths together using caramel and decorate the whole cake with decorative sugar and a sugar figurine on top
