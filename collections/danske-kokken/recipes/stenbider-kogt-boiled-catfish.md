## Ingredients

- catfish
- salt
- water
- hollandaise sauce
- pickles
- mustard

## Directions

1. Skin the fish
2. Remove entrails and cut from backbone into 2 fillets
3. Let stand sprinkled with salt for 1/2 hour
4. Place in cold salted water
5. Serve covered with a broken hollandaise sauce in which a little finely cut pickles preserved in mustard can be stirred
