## Ingredients

- plaice
- water
- salt
- butter
- flour
- curry
- fish stock
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Cut and cook plaice as in boiled plaice recipe, but not in too much water so the fish stock can be used for the curry sauce
2. When fish is cooked, brown butter, flour and curry together
3. Add fish stock to make a smooth sauce
4. Pour sauce over fish at serving
5. Garnish with a ring of loose boiled rice sprinkled with dry curry
