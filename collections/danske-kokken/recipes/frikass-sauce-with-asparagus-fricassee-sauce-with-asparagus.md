## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock or salted water
- Egg yolks
- Cooked, shelled shrimp

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus, cut into small pieces and cook in very little stock or in salted water
2. Use the stock in which they were cooked for the sauce, which is bound with egg yolks
3. Cooked, shelled shrimp can be added to the sauce together with the asparagus
4. Shrimp shells can be pounded and boiled; see Lobster Sauce No. 778
