## Ingredients

- 15 gills vinegar
- 1 pound sugar (Melis)
- cinnamon sticks
- ginger
- cloves
- 2 pounds tomatoes, not fully ripe, peeled and cut into smaller pieces or slices

## Directions

1. The tomatoes must not be fully ripe.
2. Cook 15 gills vinegar and 1 pound sugar with some pieces of cinnamon, ginger and cloves to a smooth syrup.
3. Place 2 pounds peeled tomatoes, cut into smaller pieces or slices, in it.
4. Cook and skim for 10 minutes.
5. Pour into jars and tie them when the tomatoes have cooled.
6. They go well with roast.
