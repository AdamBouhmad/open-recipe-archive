## Ingredients

- Scrambled eggs
- Cooked leeks
- Bavarian sausage
- Butter

## Directions

1. Arrange the scrambled eggs in the warm shells, and place 2 steaming hot, cooked leeks in a cross over the scrambled eggs with 1 Bavarian sausage on each side. The sausages can be fried in butter in a pan. If serving this dish on a larger platter, place the eggs on one side and stripe the other alternately with sausages and leeks.
