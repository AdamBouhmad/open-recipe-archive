## Ingredients

- 5 pægle sweet milk
- 20 kvint sweet almonds, skinned and finely ground
- 10 kvint bitter almonds, skinned and finely ground
- 30 kvint wheat starch
- 10 egg yolks
- ¾ pound white sugar
- 1 tablespoon rum

## Directions

1. Place 5 pægle sweet milk over the fire, and when the milk boils, add 20 kvint sweet almonds and 10 kvint bitter almonds, skinned and finely ground, and half-cook them.
2. Mix 30 kvint wheat starch, 10 egg yolks, ¾ pound white sugar and 1 tablespoon rum in 1 pægle cold milk.
