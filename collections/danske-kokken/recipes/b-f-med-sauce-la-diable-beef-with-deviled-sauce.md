## Ingredients

- Beef
- 2 large shallots, finely chopped
- Butter
- Flour
- Good, strong gravy or broth
- 3 tablespoons finely cut piccalilli pickles in mustard
- 1 to 2 tablespoons of the mustard
- Paprika
- Wine
- Salt
- A little sugar
- Food coloring if needed
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Brown 2 large, finely chopped shallots in butter, and in this make a roux with flour, which is thinned with good, strong gravy or broth.
2. Add to the sauce 3 tablespoons finely cut piccalilli pickles in mustard, 1 to 2 tablespoons of the mustard, paprika, wine, salt, a little sugar and food coloring if needed.
3. Let the sauce simmer with the seasonings for a few minutes.
4. Arrange over the fried meat.
5. Serve with boiled potatoes.
