## Ingredients

- 50 Gr (10 Kvint) yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 level teaspoon cardamom
- 5 eggs
- 175 Gr (35 Kvint) margarine
- 1/4 Kg (1 1/2 S) flour
- 3 1/4 Dl (1 1/2 Pægl) thin cream
- finely chopped candied citrus peel and raisins to taste
- egg for brushing
- powdered sugar
- sliced almonds

## Directions

1. Mix yeast with salt
2. Knead together cardamom, eggs, margarine, flour and thin cream
3. Add finely chopped candied citrus peel and raisins to taste
4. Mix the dough the evening before, place in a cloth and bind with a band, but so that the dough has ample space to rise during the night
5. Place the cloth with the dough in a bowl or crock with ample cold water and set until the next morning
6. On the baking board place a little flour and pour out the dough, which with a light hand is rolled into a sausage
7. Form it as a kringle by placing it on the sheet and pressing it fairly flat
8. Brush with egg, sprinkle with abundant powdered sugar and cover with sliced almonds
9. When it has risen well again, place it in a well-heated oven and bake 1/2 hour
