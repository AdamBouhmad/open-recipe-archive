## Ingredients

- Apples
- 1 Pd. sugar per 1 Pd. apples

## Directions

1. Wipe the apples and cut off half the stem
2. Carefully give them a brief boil in water
3. Place on a colander to drain
4. Boil and skim the sugar, then cook the apples in the syrup
5. The next day pour off the syrup, give it a boil, and pour it back over the apples
6. Repeat this process 3 times over 3 days
7. On the final day the apples should also have a gentle boiling together with the syrup
