## Ingredients

- Even, brown gravy sauce
- Mushrooms (fresh, pickled in butter, or pickled in vinegar, well rinsed)
- Sherry
- Maggi bouillon cubes
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Add mushrooms to an even, brown gravy sauce
2. The mushrooms can be either fresh, pickled in butter, or if pickled in vinegar, well rinsed so the sauce does not take on a strong, sour taste
3. Prepare with Sherry, Maggi bouillon cubes, salt and a little sugar
4. Bring to a boil with the mushrooms
