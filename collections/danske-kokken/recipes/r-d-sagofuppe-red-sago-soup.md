## Ingredients

- sago grains
- water
- jam or preserves
- sweet almonds (optional)
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the preceding soup, but instead of eggs and wine add jam or preserves and, if desired, sliced sweet almonds
2. Whipped cream, preferably whipped, can be offered at table so that each person can add some to their plate
