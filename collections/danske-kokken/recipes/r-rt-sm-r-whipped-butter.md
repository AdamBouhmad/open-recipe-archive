## Ingredients

- Butter
- Water
- Butter and sweet cream (alternative method)
- Half a teaspoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Stir butter strongly on heat with a drop of water until it becomes thick
2. This tastes best with asparagus and cabbage, but is expensive and has the disadvantage that it tends to turn into melted butter on a warm plate
3. Alternative method: take equal amounts of butter and sweet cream, knead half a teaspoon cornstarch into the butter, pour the cream into a small pot and stir until it boils
4. This is used for many types of fish
