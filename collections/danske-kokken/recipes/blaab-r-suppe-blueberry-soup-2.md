## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg (2 1/2 S) blueberries
- 5 L (5 pots) water
- lemon
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook blueberries with lemon and cinnamon, strain and thicken as with the other fruit soups.
2. Blackberries are cooked the same way as blueberries.
