## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or Palmin
- Lemon
- Oyster water
- Oyster beard

## Directions

1. Clean the oysters and remove them from their shells.
2. Dip them in egg and bread crumbs.
3. Brown them in butter or Palmin.
4. Serve with lemon.
5. Cook the oyster water and beard to make a sauce and bottle it, well corked.
6. Arrange on a napkin, garnished with lemon, toasted bread, and butter.
