## Ingredients

- 5 grams pressed yeast
- 13 cups lukewarm milk
- 1 pound flour
- a little salt
- egg white or lukewarm water for brushing
- poppy seeds (optional)

## Directions

1. Dissolve the pressed yeast in the lukewarm milk and stir into 1 pound flour along with a little salt.
2. Knead the dough well until it releases from the hands and shows eyes.
3. When it has risen, shape without further kneading into small loaves.
4. Brush with egg white or lukewarm water and bake light brown with good, even heat.
5. If poppy seeds are sprinkled over the loaves before baking, they become the so-called Berkesbrød.
