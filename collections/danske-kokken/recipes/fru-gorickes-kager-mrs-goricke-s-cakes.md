## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- almond drops

## Directions

1. Mix well thoroughly.
2. Add almond drops.
3. Place on a buttered pan with a teaspoon.
