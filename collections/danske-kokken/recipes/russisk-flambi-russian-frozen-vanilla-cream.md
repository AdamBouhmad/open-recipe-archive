## Ingredients

- 1 pot sweet cream
- 8 egg yolks
- half a pound powdered sugar
- 1 vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Make a vanilla cream from the sweet cream, egg yolks, powdered sugar, and vanilla bean
2. Set over heat and bring to a boil while whisking, then set aside to cool
3. When completely cold, add 2 pails of sweet cream that has been whipped to stiff peaks
4. Gently stir the mixture together so it appears striped
5. Pour into an ice bucket and put the lid on
6. Set in salt and ice and let stand for 3 hours, but do not turn it
7. Turn out onto a plate as usual and arrange whole with small meringues alongside
8. Instead of vanilla cream, thick sweet raspberry juice may be used, but the treatment is otherwise the same
