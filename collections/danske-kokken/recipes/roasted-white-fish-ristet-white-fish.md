## Ingredients

- White fish
- Fine salt and pepper
- Cold butter
- Smoke from wood

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and dry the fish.
2. Sprinkle a little fine salt and pepper over it, place it with the inside down on a grate and roast over fresh, clear coals.
3. When it is nicely brown, turn it for a short time, then remove it and spread cold butter over it.
4. A little smoke under the fish enhances its flavor.
