## Ingredients

- peeled and rinsed peas
- 4 quarts water per bushel of medium-sized peas
- a good and large bunch of herbs
- many carrot and parsley roots
- approximately 23 ounces flour
- 3 ounces butter
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- purslane or parsley

## Directions

1. Place the peeled and rinsed peas on the fire with 4 quarts water to a bushel of medium-sized peas
2. When they boil, add a good and large bunch of herbs and many carrot and parsley roots
3. When these are done, take them out and cut into smaller pieces, and press the bunch well
4. Mix approximately 23 ounces flour and 3 ounces butter into a roux, which is thinned with the soup, and cook it together with salt, 1 tablespoon sugar, purslane or parsley
