## Ingredients

- A slice of butter
- A little sugar
- A few tablespoons of gravy, soup or water
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Peeled and sliced white turnips

## Directions

1. Brown a slice of butter together with a little sugar
2. Add a few tablespoons of gravy, soup or water with Maggi Bouillon cubes
3. Boil this together before adding the peeled and sliced turnips
4. Cook until tender and the sauce has reduced and formed a glaze around the turnips
