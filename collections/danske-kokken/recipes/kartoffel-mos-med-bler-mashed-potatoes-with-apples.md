## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Apples

## Directions

1. Either cut some pieces of apples and stew them together with the peeled potatoes for the mash and press through a sieve with them, or soften small pieces of peeled, good apples in the warm, prepared mash. Serve with roast pork, cutlets and similar dishes.
