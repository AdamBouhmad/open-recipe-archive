## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kilograms (5 pounds) apples
- Rusks for 30 øre
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) melted butter
- Jam, if desired
- Whipped cream from 1/2 liter (1/2 pot) cream
- Red currant jelly for decoration

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters, and remove the cores
2. Rinse and cook them until tender in a small amount of water under a closed lid over low heat
3. Sweeten them and layer them in a greased form alternating with crushed rusks, starting and ending with crushed rusks
4. Press the cake firmly together with a spoon before pouring 1/4 kilogram of melted butter over it
5. Jam can be placed between the layers
6. Place the cake in a warm oven for about half an hour
7. Once cold, turn it out of the form and decorate with whipped cream and red currant jelly
8. The cake does not need to be placed in the oven if the bread has been previously baked in butter on a griddle, and butter can be saved in that case
