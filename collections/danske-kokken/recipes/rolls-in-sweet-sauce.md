## Ingredients

- flour or bread rolls
- water
- salt
- strong sweet sauce
- pears or apples
- dried fruit or various jams

## Directions

1. Make flour or bread rolls according to instructions
2. Cook them in water with a little salt and place them on a platter
3. Pour over them a strong sweet sauce in which pears or apples (which can be dried) or various jams have been cooked
