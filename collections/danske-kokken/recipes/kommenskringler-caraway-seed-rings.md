## Ingredients

- 75 g margarine
- 2/4 kg flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon caraway seed
- 30 g yeast, dissolved in 3 1/4 dl lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Crumble the margarine into the flour
2. Add salt, caraway seed, and yeast dissolved in milk
3. Mix the dough well and form into rings
4. Let rise, then cook, brush, and bake as Fastekringler
