## Ingredients

- 1 cup melted butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup lukewarm water
- 2 tablespoons Dr. Price's Baking Powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix melted butter, sugar, lukewarm water, and 2 tablespoons Dr. Price's Baking Powder sifted together with as much flour as needed for a stiff dough.
2. Roll out very thin and cut with a round cake cutter or a glass.
3. Bake in a hot oven.
