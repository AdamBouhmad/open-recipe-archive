## Ingredients

- Beaten eggs
- Watercress, rinsed and cut
- Small red tomatoes

## Directions

1. Mix the rinsed, cut watercress into the beaten eggs and make scrambled eggs as usual. Arrange them warm and place a wreath around them made of small red tomatoes, halved and laid with the round side up. A bunch of watercress is placed between each tomato.
