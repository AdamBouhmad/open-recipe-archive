## Ingredients

- celeriac
- salt
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Peel the celeriac and split them.
2. Remove the cores with a silver spoon.
3. They can be salted in autumn and stand with a light press on.
4. As needed during winter, prepare a smaller portion at a time.
5. Boil spices as for the recipe No. 232 in vinegar.
6. Add the well-dried celeriac pieces just long enough to heat through, then remove them immediately.
