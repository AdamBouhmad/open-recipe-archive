## Ingredients

- Game birds (partridge, eider duck, wild duck, capercaillie, or snipe)
- Milk
- Short pastry
- Farce
- Butter
- Flour
- Game sauce
- Apple compote, redcurrant jelly, or apple sauce

## Directions

1. Lard and roast the birds in milk
2. Cut the meat from the breast of each bird
3. Cook the bones in milk for the sauce
4. Line the mold with short pastry and farce
5. Use the same method as for the preceding pâtés
6. Serve with game sauce, apple compote, redcurrant jelly, or apple sauce
