## Ingredients

- Water puff dough
- Butter dough
- Water glaze

## Directions

1. Place a teaspoon of water puff dough on a small piece of square, thinly rolled butter dough
2. Bend the four pointed corners up around the dough
3. Bake as water puffs
4. While lukewarm, brush the cakes with water glaze
