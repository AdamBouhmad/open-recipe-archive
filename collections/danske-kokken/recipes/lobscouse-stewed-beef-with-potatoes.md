## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked beef
- 1 pound lean bacon
- 1 knap ounce butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 onion
- Parsley
- 2 pounds potatoes
- 1 quart broth

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into 2-inch cubes.
2. Cut the bacon into 2-inch slices and brown lightly with the butter.
3. Add the broth.
4. Layer in raw sliced potatoes, onion, parsley, and seasonings.
5. Simmer gently for 15 minutes.
6. Add the meat and simmer gently for 10 minutes.
7. Remove the onion and seasonings.
8. Ensure the potatoes are fully cooked and adjust seasoning before serving.
