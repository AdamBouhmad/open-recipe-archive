## Ingredients

- ½ liter (½ pound) cream
- 125 grams (¼ pound) grated horseradish
- Sugar
- Salt
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. When the cream is whipped to stiff peaks, stir in 125 grams grated horseradish along with sugar, salt, and vinegar to taste.
2. The sauce is made just before serving.
3. It is eaten with boiled pike, carp, chicken, and veal and lamb meat, etc.
4. What remains of the sauce can be used for macaroni salad.
