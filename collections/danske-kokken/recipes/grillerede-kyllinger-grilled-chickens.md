## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Butter
- Beaten egg yolks
- Crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Divide the chicken as mentioned and half-cook it, but only the best pieces; the rest should be saved for soup
2. Brown butter very lightly in a pot
3. Coat the pieces, which can be lukewarm but not hot, with beaten egg yolks, dip in crushed rusks, and fry in the pot
