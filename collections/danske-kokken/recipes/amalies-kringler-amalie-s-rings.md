## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 2 pounds butter
- 1 spoonful yeast
- cream, as the dough will take

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, and yeast with as much cream as the dough will take
2. Mix the dough together the evening before the rings are to be baked
3. Let it stand overnight in a covered bowl to rise
4. The next day, roll the dough into small rings
5. Bake light brown
