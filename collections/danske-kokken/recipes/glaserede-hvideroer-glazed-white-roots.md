## Ingredients

- Butter
- Sugar
- Broth, soup, or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
- Peeled white roots

## Directions

1. Brown a slice of butter together with a little sugar
2. Add a few tablespoons of broth, soup, or water with Maggi bouillon cubes
3. Cook this together, then place the peeled and rinsed roots in it to cook tender until the sauce has reduced and formed a glaze around the roots
