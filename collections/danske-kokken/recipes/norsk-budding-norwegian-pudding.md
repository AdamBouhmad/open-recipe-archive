## Ingredients

- 5 ounces rice flour
- 5 ounces butter
- 5 ounces white sugar
- 1.5 pints thin cream
- 18 egg yolks
- 4 ounces sweet, finely ground almonds
- 6 to 8 bitter almonds
- 2 ounces finely cut citron
- 1 stick ground vanilla
- 2 ounces currants
- A little chopped lemon peel
- Egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Cream the rice flour, butter, and white sugar together well and gradually add the thin cream.
2. Pour this mixture and stir in 18 egg yolks, then add the sweet and bitter almonds blended together, the citron, vanilla, currants, and chopped lemon peel.
3. Finally fold in the beaten egg whites.
4. Boil for 2.5 hours.
5. Serve with sweet sauce; serves 14 persons.
