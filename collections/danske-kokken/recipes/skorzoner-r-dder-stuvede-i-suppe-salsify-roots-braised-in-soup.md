## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Butter
- Flour
- Soup or cooking water from the roots
- Maggi Bouillon cubes
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Sugar
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter and flour, diluted with soup or the water in which the roots were cooked
2. Add Maggi Bouillon cubes
3. Thicken with egg yolks
4. Season with salt, sugar and lemon juice
5. Warm the roots in the sauce
