## Ingredients

- 1 Lemon
- 1 Ounce Butter
- 1 Ounce Sugar
- 1/4 Pint boiling Water
- 1 Ounce Sugar

## Directions

1. Press out the Juice from a Lemon and grate the Shell.
2. Put 1 Ounce Butter over the Fire and mix it with 1 Ounce Sugar until it boils.
3. Then stir in 1/4 Pint boiling Water, 1 Ounce Sugar, Lemon Shell and Juice.
4. The Sauce is served for Puddings.
5. It must not boil after the Lemon is added, as it will otherwise become bitter.
