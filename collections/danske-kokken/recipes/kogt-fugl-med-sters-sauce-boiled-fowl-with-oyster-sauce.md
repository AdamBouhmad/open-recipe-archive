## Ingredients

- Fowl
- Boiling water
- One quart of oysters
- One ounce butter
- One ounce flour
- Oyster sauce
- Broth from the fowl
- Salt
- White pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the fowl but do not stuff.
2. Place in enough boiling water to cover the fowl.
3. Simmer carefully until the fowl is cooked.
4. When nearly done, prepare the oyster sauce: bring one quart of oysters to boil in their own liquid.
5. Strain the oysters; reserve the liquid and wash the oysters in cold water.
6. Mix one ounce of butter and one ounce of flour together over heat until they form a white paste, being careful not to brown.
7. Stir in enough of the oyster sauce and enough broth from the fowl to make the sauce the consistency of melted butter.
8. Season with salt, white pepper, and grated nutmeg.
9. Add the oysters and pour over the fowl.
10. Sprinkle finely chopped parsley over all.
11. This dish can also be made with hens and chickens.
