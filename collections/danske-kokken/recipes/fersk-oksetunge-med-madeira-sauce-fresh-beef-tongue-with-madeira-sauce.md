## Ingredients

- Good brown sauce made from butter, flour, and stock or tongue soup
- Paprika
- Coloring
- Madeira
- Butter pastry ring (No. 34)
- Truffles (optional)
- Mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Make a good brown sauce from butter, flour, stock or the tongue soup, paprika, coloring and Madeira
2. Pour the sauce over the sliced tongue
3. Garnish with butter pastry ring (see No. 34)
4. Truffles can be added to the sauce, and mushrooms also pair well with Madeira sauce
