## Ingredients

- 3/4 L very strong coffee
- 1/4 kg vanilla chocolate
- 16 sheets isinglass
- 1/2 L cream, whipped to stiff foam
- 1/4 L semi-skimmed or sweet milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 150 g powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour or powdered starch
- vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Dissolve the vanilla chocolate and isinglass in the very strong coffee
2. When it has cooled and begins to set, stir in the whipped cream
3. Pour into a moistened and dusted form
4. For the cream sauce: whisk the egg yolks into the milk together with the powdered sugar and wheat flour or starch
5. Place the cream on the heat and stir regularly until it is almost cold
6. Add vanilla essence to the sauce
7. Serve the pudding with the cream sauce
