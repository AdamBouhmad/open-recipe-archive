## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons sweet cream
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 2 crushed cardamom seeds

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs well with 1 tablespoon powdered sugar.
2. Add 2 tablespoons sweet cream, 2 tablespoons melted butter, 6 tablespoons flour, and 2 crushed cardamom seeds.
3. Work all together well and set aside for one hour to cool.
4. When the soup for which the balls are intended boils, place them in with a teaspoon.
5. Cook for 2 minutes.
