## Ingredients

- Blueberries
- 9 0/2 parts powdered sugar or farina per 1 liter (1 pot) juice

## Directions

1. Pick over the berries, rinse them, and place over heat, stirring regularly so they do not burn on the bottom of the pot.
2. When they release their juice well, remove the pot from heat and press the berries through a sieve after they have cooled sufficiently.
3. Measure the juice and bring to a boil with the sugar, skim it, boil for 10 minutes, and then pour into dry bottles.
4. Tie the bottles immediately with parchment paper.
