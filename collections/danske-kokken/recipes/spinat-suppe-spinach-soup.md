## Ingredients

- 2 quarts spinach
- Water for cooking
- Salt
- 2 quarts milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- A little white pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach in sufficient salted water.
2. When it is completely soft, so that it can easily be pressed, drain the water through a strainer; chop the spinach finely and rub it through a sieve with a spoon.
3. Boil 2 quarts of milk, stir the spinach into it, and when it is near boiling, thicken it with one tablespoon of cornstarch, mixed in cold milk.
4. Season with a little teaspoon of salt, a little white pepper and nutmeg.
5. It must be served as soon as it boils up.
