## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg dried apricots
- macarons
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) heavy cream

## Directions

1. Soak the apricots the evening before.
2. Cook the apricots and sweeten to taste.
3. Layer the cooled apricots with macarons in a glass bowl.
4. Decorate with whipped cream made from 1/2 L (1/2 pot) heavy cream.
