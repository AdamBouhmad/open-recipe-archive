## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Thin cream or sweet milk
- Salt
- A little sugar
- Grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Make a roux with butter and flour and dilute with thin cream or sweet milk until the sauce is properly smooth
2. Then season it with salt, a little sugar and grated horseradish to taste
3. Cream horseradish sauce is served with boiled fowl, turkeys and with boiled veal and lamb
