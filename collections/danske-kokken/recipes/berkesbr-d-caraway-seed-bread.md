## Ingredients

- French bread dough
- caraway seeds
- egg beaten and mixed with milk

## Directions

1. Form French loaves and shape
2. Make a twist of dough and lay along the length of the loaves
3. Brush with beaten egg mixed with milk
4. Sprinkle with caraway seeds
5. Let rise before placing in oven
