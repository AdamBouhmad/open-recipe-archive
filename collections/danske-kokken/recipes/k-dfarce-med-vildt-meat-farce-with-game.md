## Ingredients

- Partridge backs and wings
- Partridge thighs
- Partridge breast
- Raw meat farce
- Sharp brown sauce

## Directions

1. Take the backs and wings from the partridges, and from the thighs cut off the lower joint. Divide the breast into 4 pieces.
2. When they are well roasted and cold again, place raw meat farce in a sheet metal dish, then place a layer of the roasted birds, then again meat farce.
3. Roast it in a roasting oven until the farce is roasted.
4. Serve with sharp brown sauce.
5. The birds can also be stuffed inside with meat farce.
