## Ingredients

- 1 quart white or red wine
- 12-14 ounces sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix the wine with sugar that has been dipped in water, the cinnamon, and the peel of 1 lemon. Heat over the fire, stirring until warm, and strain through a fine sieve. Add the two sliced lemons after the seeds have been removed. Instead of lemons, many prefer a few tablespoons of Kirschwasser.
