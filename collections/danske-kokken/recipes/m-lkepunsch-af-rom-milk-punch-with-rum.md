## Ingredients

- 3 pounds white sugar
- 3 pots boiling water
- 3 bottles (9 measures) best white rum
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 vanilla pod
- juice of 10 lemons and thinly pared yellow peel of lemons
- 1½ pots boiling sweet milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve 3 pounds white sugar in 3 pots boiling water
2. Add 3 bottles (9 measures) best white rum, 1 grated nutmeg, 1 vanilla pod, juice of 10 lemons and thinly pared yellow peel
3. Add 1½ pots boiling sweet milk
4. Let the mixture stand, covered, until the next day
5. Filter it a couple of times and pour into bottles, which are corked
6. Drink cold
