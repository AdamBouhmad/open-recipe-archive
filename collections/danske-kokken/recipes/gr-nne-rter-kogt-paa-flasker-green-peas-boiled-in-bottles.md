## Ingredients

- Carefully shelled fresh peas
- Hay

## Directions

1. Put the peas in completely dry bottles
2. Wrap them with hay
3. Boil for approximately 3 quarters of an hour or place in a steam pot
4. Cork with new corks
5. Cool the bottles in the pot or under a blanket
