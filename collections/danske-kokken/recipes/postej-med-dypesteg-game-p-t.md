## Ingredients

- Game farce (or beef, veal, or pork as substitute)
- Leftover game meat
- Short pastry
- Butter
- Flour
- Game sauce
- Olives, chestnuts, or truffles (optional)

## Directions

1. Line the mold as described for the fish pâté recipe
2. Use leftover game meat in the same manner as for Hare Pâté
3. Layer the meat with farce in the mold
4. Between the meat layers, place olives, chestnuts, or truffles if desired
5. Cover with short pastry and bake
