## Ingredients

- Peeled, firm heads of Brussels sprouts
- Cold water
- Cold brine

## Directions

1. Rinse the peeled, firm heads and cook for a couple of minutes
2. Cool with cold water and fill in the jars
3. Pour the usual cold brine over and cook the cabbage in the hermetically closed jars for 45 minutes and cool
