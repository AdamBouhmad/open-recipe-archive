## Ingredients

- 1/2 Kg (1 S) fine flour
- 1 L (1 Pot) buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- salt
- grated lemon zest
- cardamom
- Palmin or butter

## Directions

1. In fine flour, stir 1 L (1 Pot) buttermilk, 1 teaspoon baking soda, salt, grated lemon zest and cardamom to taste together and bake immediately in generous Palmin or butter.
