## Ingredients

- 2 sheets puff pastry
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Roll out two sheets of puff pastry to just under 1 centimeter (1/4 inch) thick.
2. Take a measurement of the dish on which the vol au vent will be served; this is easiest done by cutting a shape from paper and using it to cut the pastry with a pastry wheel.
3. As the dough shrinks during baking, the dough must be at least 1.5 centimeters (1/2 inch) larger in circumference than the measurement.
4. Place the thinner sheet of pastry on a baking sheet and brush with egg white.
5. Cut the second, slightly thicker sheet into a ring approximately 5 to 6 centimeters (2 inches) wide, and place it on top of the base sheet and brush with egg as well, but ensure nothing gets on the edges.
6. Cut the remaining dough into small strips or decorative figures; bake these together with the vol au vent in a well-heated oven and use them for garnishing.
7. When the puff pastry is to be a nice light brown, it is often necessary to turn the vol au vent so that it bakes evenly.
