## Ingredients

- bread pudding batter ingredients (with 50 grams less margarine)
- 0.25 kilogram beef bone marrow

## Directions

1. The batter is made as for bread pudding, except that about 50 grams of margarine is saved.
2. 0.25 kilogram of beef bone marrow is well cleaned of bone splinters and distributed in small pieces in layers in the pudding.
3. Red wine or fruit sauce is served with it.
