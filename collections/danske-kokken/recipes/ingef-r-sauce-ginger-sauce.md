## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter (1 pint) water
- 1 tablespoon potato starch
- 1/4 liter (1 pint) Graves wine
- Finely chopped preserved Chinese ginger with some of its syrup, to taste
- Preserved pumpkin with ginger (optional)

## Directions

1. When the water boils, smooth it with 1 tablespoon mixed potato starch.
2. Pour in 1/4 liter Graves wine and finely chopped preserved Chinese ginger with some of its syrup, to taste.
3. Add diced preserved pumpkin with ginger if desired.
4. A cheaper sauce can be made by using rhubarb juice instead of wine.
