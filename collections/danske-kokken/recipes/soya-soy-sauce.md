## Ingredients

- 3 liters (3 pots) good meat broth
- water
- 3 teaspoons ground white pepper
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 2 chopped red onions
- 3 bay leaves
- 1/4 kg (1 1/2 s) powdered sugar
- 1/2 bottle sherry
- potato starch

## Directions

1. Place 3 liters (3 pots) good meat broth over the fire and bring to a boil, skim well and strain through a fine cloth once it has finished skimming.
2. Dilute the broth with so much water that it becomes appropriately salty.
3. Return 3 L (3 pots) of this to the pot along with 3 teaspoons ground white pepper, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1 teaspoon ginger, 1 teaspoon cloves, 2 chopped red onions and 3 bay leaves.
4. Simmer the broth with these ingredients for at least 3 quarters of an hour and strain again.
5. While the soy sauce is cooking, brown 1/4 kg (1 1/2 s) powdered sugar in a pan and add it to the hot soy sauce to give it color. The sugar must be dark brown, but not burnt, as it can otherwise easily give a bad taste.
6. Finally add 1/2 bottle sherry.
7. If it is preferred to have the soy sauce smooth, stir potato starch in some of the wine and bring the soy sauce to a boil again.
