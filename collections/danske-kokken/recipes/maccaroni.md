## Ingredients

- Maccaroni
- Bouillon
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt

## Directions

1. Break the maccaroni into fairly long pieces and cook in bouillon, which does not need to be very strong.
2. There should not be more broth than one can imagine will be suitable as it will cook down.
3. When one takes one out with the edge of a fork and it thereby breaks in the middle, they are done.
4. Then stir a little cold butter into them until the butter is melted.
5. Serve immediately and eat with grated sharp cheese and, if one wishes, melted butter.
