## Ingredients

- 3 chickens
- Butter
- Cooked, salted beef tongue, sliced
- Fish or veal forcemeat
- Chicken ragout

## Directions

1. Prepare the mold as in recipe No. 48
2. Instead of truffles, use slices of cooked, salted beef tongue
3. Line the mold with either fish or veal forcemeat
4. Fill the center with a good chicken ragout prepared as recipe No. 35
5. Cover with forcemeat and paper and cook as the previous recipe
6. Serve with the same sauce used for the ragout, but thinned
