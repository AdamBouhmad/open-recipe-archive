## Ingredients

- 1 Plate rice pudding
- A couple of tablespoonfuls potato flour
- 3 Whole eggs
- A little raisins
- Crushed almonds

## Directions

1. Stir the rice pudding with a couple of tablespoonfuls potato flour, 3 whole eggs, a little raisins, and crushed almonds.
2. Bake small, flat cakes or cakes in butter on the pancake pan.
3. Serve with powdered sugar over.
