## Ingredients

- 375 g (1/4 S) powdered sugar
- 15 sheets isinglass, rinsed in cold water
- 1/4 L (1 pint) boiling water
- 1/4 L (3 pints) cold, preserved rhubarb juice
- 1/4 L (1 pint) sherry
- 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- a few drops of vanilla essence
- stewed fruit (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt the powdered sugar and isinglass in 1/4 L (1 pint) boiling water.
2. Pour this into 1/4 L (3 pints) cold, preserved rhubarb juice together with 1/4 L (1 pint) sherry.
3. Fill into 2 moistened and sugar-strewn ring molds.
4. When the jelly is stiff and ready to serve, turn it out and decorate with foam made from 1/2 L (1/2 pot) cream, to which 1 tablespoon powdered sugar and a few drops of vanilla essence are added.
5. The jelly can be garnished with stewed fruits.
6. If the jelly is made from fresh stalks, use 125 g (1/4 S) more sugar for each liter (pot) of juice.
