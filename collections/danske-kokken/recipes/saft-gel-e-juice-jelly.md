## Ingredients

- 2 pints cherry juice
- 2 pints raspberry juice
- 1 pint water, or more if the juice is very thick
- 12 quarters dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil 2 pints cherry juice, 2 pints raspberry juice, and 1 pint water, or more if the juice is very thick, together with 12 quarters dissolved isinglass in an enameled pot.
2. Strain through a new flour sieve and set in the mold on ice to freeze 2-3 hours.
