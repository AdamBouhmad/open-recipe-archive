## Ingredients

- good 1/4 L (good 1 Pægl) medium-fine sago grains
- 4 1/2 L (4 1/2 Pot) water
- whole cinnamon
- lemon peel
- preserved plums and cherries or juice of plums, cherries, currants, rhubarb, raspberries or strawberries

## Directions

1. Cook the soup with whole cinnamon and lemon peel.
2. Season with preserved plums and cherries or with juice of either plums, cherries, currants, rhubarb, raspberries or strawberries.
3. Sweeten to taste.
