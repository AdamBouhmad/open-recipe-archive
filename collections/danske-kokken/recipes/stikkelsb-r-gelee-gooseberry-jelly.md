## Ingredients

- 1 quart unripe good gooseberries
- 1 pint water
- 4 quarts berries (to yield 1 quart juice)
- 2 pounds loaf sugar
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Cook 1 quart unripe good gooseberries in 1 pint water until they burst
2. Pour into a flannel jelly bag that has been dipped in hot water and wrung out in a dry cloth while still warm
3. Let the bag hang quietly until the juice has run off, which may take until the next day
4. For 1 quart juice (typically obtained from 4 quarts berries), take 2 pounds loaf sugar and cook it into syrup with 1 pint water
5. Add the juice to the syrup and cook until stiff when tested on a cup
6. If the juice is not very thick, you may add the sugar to it and let it cook until stiff when tested
7. The spent berries can be used for gooseberry soup
