## Ingredients

- Pear or Apple Syrup (before it is completely reduced)
- Cooked, strained apple sauce without any sugar added

## Directions

1. Before the Pear or Apple Syrup is completely reduced, stir in the cooked, strained apple sauce without any sugar added
2. Cook the juice a while longer before pouring into jars
3. The apple sauce produces an excellent effect; this is called Apple Butter and will certainly become highly valued as it becomes more known
4. Spread on French bread or crackers it tastes refreshing and is also a very healthy food; can be preserved for years
