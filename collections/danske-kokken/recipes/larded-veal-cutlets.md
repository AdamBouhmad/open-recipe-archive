## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Thin bacon strips
- Butter
- Brown gravy
- Vegetables, asparagus, or peas
- Parsley or salad

## Directions

1. After the cutlets are formed, lard them with very fine bacon strips.
2. Brown them on the pan in butter and drip frequently with a little good brown gravy to obtain a nice brown glaze.
3. Place vegetables, asparagus, or peas in the center of the platter.
4. Arrange the cutlets around them, garnished with parsley or salad.
