## Ingredients

- Lamb heads (or mutton heads if from young sheep)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Beaten egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or lard
- Brown butter
- Salad or other vegetables
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Although lamb heads are best, mutton heads can be used if from young animals.
2. Scald, pluck, and clean them, remove the brains, wash well and soak for an hour in cold water to let the blood drain out.
3. Put them in boiling salted water and cook until the skin can be removed.
4. Pick them carefully, cut off the ears and sinews, remove the palate and split the tongue and fry it along with the heads if desired.
5. Fry the heads in butter or lard after dipping them in beaten egg and rolling in ground breadcrumbs mixed with salt and pepper.
6. Fry them light brown and serve with brown butter and salad or other vegetables and potatoes.
