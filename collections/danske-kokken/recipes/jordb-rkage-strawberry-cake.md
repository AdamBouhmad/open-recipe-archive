## Ingredients

- 14 egg whites
- 1 cup preserved fresh strawberries
- 14 dessert spoonfuls powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Whip 14 egg whites
2. Stir together 1 cup preserved fresh strawberries and 14 dessert spoonfuls powdered sugar and place in a cake pan
3. Gently fold in the egg whites
4. Immediately place the pan in a warm oven or tart pan
5. Bake for half an hour and serve immediately, as it will fall otherwise
6. Arrange with whipped cream over top
