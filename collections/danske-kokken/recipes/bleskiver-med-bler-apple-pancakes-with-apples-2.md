## Ingredients

- Peeled apples, without cores
- 5 Eggs
- 1 Pint cream
- 0.5 Pint cold water
- 1 Pint melted butter
- 1 Pint flour

## Directions

1. Peel apples, remove the cores, and cut into pieces the size of a large nut.
2. Beat 5 eggs with 1 pint cream and 0.5 pint cold water.
3. Add 1 pint melted butter and 1 pint flour.
4. When the æbleskiver pan is hot, put butter in it.
5. Dip the apple pieces in the batter and lay them in the holes.
6. Fill the holes with more batter.
7. Bake to a beautiful golden brown.
8. Turn the æbleskiver and serve warm with caster sugar over.
