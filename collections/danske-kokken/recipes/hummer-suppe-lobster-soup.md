## Ingredients

- 2 large female lobsters with roe
- 4.5 L soup stock
- 175 gr butter
- 100 gr flour
- ground white pepper
- Madeira wine
- 1/2 kg asparagus
- small fish balls
- Butter dough slices or Simels

## Directions

1. Rinse the lobsters and place them in boiling water
2. When the legs come off easily, remove the lobsters
3. Cool them, split them open and remove the meat from the tail and claws in pieces as large as possible
4. Cut the meat into small, uniform pieces and place in a tureen
5. Pound all remaining parts of the lobsters - shells, bones and roe - in a mortar together with 175 gr butter
6. Pour this mixture into 4 L spare soup together with a good whisk and cook for 10 minutes so that the red lobster butter can collect on top
7. Skim off the red butter, then bake 100 gr flour in the soup and thin with the lobster soup
8. Add ground white pepper and Madeira
9. Add 1/2 kg asparagus, the lobster meat and small fish balls to the soup
10. Serve with Butter dough slices or Simels and add the asparagus water to the soup
