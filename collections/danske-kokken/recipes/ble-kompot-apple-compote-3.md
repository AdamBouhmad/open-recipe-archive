## Ingredients

- fresh, juicy apples
- white wine
- water
- lemon peel
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into 4 pieces, and remove the cores
2. Treat as for pear compote, but they do not tolerate boiling as long as pears and must be removed carefully so they do not fall apart
3. If making a larger portion, divide the apples into more pieces and cook a batch at a time in the same sauce
4. The sauce must be reduced slightly before pouring over the apples
