## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 to 2 Kg (3 to 4 lb) Strawberries
- Powdered Sugar
- nearly 1 L (1 Pot) Water per 1/2 Kg (Pound) of Berries
- a little white Wine or Sherry

## Directions

1. Select some of the most beautiful berries and sprinkle with powdered sugar, which must stand with them for a couple of hours
2. Pass the rest through a fine sieve with nearly 1 L (1 Pot) water for each 1/2 Kg (Pound) of berries
3. Sweeten the cold soup and add a little white wine or sherry to taste
4. It is served with small Macaroons or Rusks and must be very cold
5. Either the tureen can be placed in an ice chest, or a small piece of ice is placed in it
6. Just before serving, the whole berries are placed in the tureen
7. Raspberries can be treated in a similar manner
