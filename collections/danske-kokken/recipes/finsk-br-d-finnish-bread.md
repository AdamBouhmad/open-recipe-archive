## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg (1/2 lb) margarine
- 375 grams (1/4 lb) flour
- 100 grams (20 quintal) sugar

## Directions

1. Mix together margarine, flour and sugar
2. Roll into sticks of finger thickness
3. Cut into pieces 5 centimeters (2 inches) long
4. Dip in egg, sugar and chopped almonds
