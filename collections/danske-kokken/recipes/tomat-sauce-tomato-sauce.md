## Ingredients

- Plain white sauce
- Tomato puree
- Ground white pepper
- Salt
- Sugar
- Wine

## Directions

1. Stir tomato puree into a plain white sauce to taste and bring it to a boil
2. Season the sauce with ground white pepper, salt, sugar and wine
3. It can be served with dishes of boiled and fried fish as well as with boiled fowl, veal, and lamb
4. The sauce does not need to be thickened with egg
