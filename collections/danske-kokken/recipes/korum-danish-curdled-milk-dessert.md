## Ingredients

- nearly 1/2 L (1/10 Pot) sweet Milk per person
- 1/2 Dl (3/10 Pægl) sweet Cream per person
- Grated Rye Bread
- Sugar
- crushed Almonds
- Jam, if desired

## Directions

1. Set the milk up in a vessel, and when it has become thick, skim off the top cream layer and whip it together with 1/2 Dl (3/10 Pægl) sweet cream for each person
2. Pour the milk into the tureen and whip it with the whisk
3. Stir in grated rye bread, sugar, crushed almonds and, if desired, a little jam together with the whipped cream
