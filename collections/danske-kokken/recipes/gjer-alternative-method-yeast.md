## Ingredients

- 2 ounces hops
- 4 quarts water
- 0.5 pound brown sugar
- salt
- 1 pound sifted wheat flour
- 3 pounds potatoes

## Directions

1. Steep 2 ounces hops 0.25 hour in 4 quarts water
2. Strain and let stand until lukewarm
3. Dissolve 0.5 pound brown sugar and salt in it
4. Gradually stir this mixture into 1 pound sifted wheat flour until even
5. Pour into a crock or deep vessel and let stand four days in a warm place to ferment
6. On the third day, cook, shell and mash potatoes (about 3 pounds), stir into the yeast and let stand one more day
7. Next day strain and put into bottles which must be stored in a cold place
