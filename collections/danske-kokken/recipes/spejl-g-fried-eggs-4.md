## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs
- Stuffed oysters or mushrooms (optional)
- Sliced anchovies (optional)
- Green leaves for garnish (optional)

## Directions

1. Put butter in the holes of an omelet pan; when light brown, break an egg and slide gently into the butter
2. Fry only until the white is firm and the yolk is soft like poached eggs
3. Eggs can be placed on a small flat dish with stuffed oysters or mushrooms around each egg, or with sliced anchovies between the eggs
4. Can also be served alone
5. Alternatively, use a frying pan; when butter is light brown, break eggs gently side by side so they appear separate but connected like a cake
6. Garnish with green leaves around the edge
