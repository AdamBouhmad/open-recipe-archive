## Ingredients

- slices of French bread
- milk
- butter
- egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 10 tablespoons milk
- a little salt
- sugar
- egg whites
- powdered sugar
- jam

## Directions

1. Dip slices of French bread in milk and fry them in butter on a pan.
2. Beat the egg yolks and stir in 1 tablespoon of flour, 10 tablespoons of milk, a little salt and sugar, and finally the foam from the egg whites.
3. Melt a little butter on a pan before pouring the egg mixture onto it.
4. When it begins to set a little, distribute the bread slices. Turn the bread cake and bake on both sides.
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve with jam.
