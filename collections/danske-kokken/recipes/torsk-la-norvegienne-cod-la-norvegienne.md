## Ingredients

- 2 cod
- Salt
- 1.5 pints cream
- About 3 pounds butter
- 1 to 2 ounces flour

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, rinse and cut into slices, which are placed in cold water with 1 pound fine salt for 6 hours.
2. Boil 8 quarts water separately with 12 ounces salt. Place the fish in it and bring to a boil, then remove the kettle from the heat and let it stand for 5 minutes.
3. Arrange and serve with sauce made from the above ingredients, which are whisked together during boiling and strained.
