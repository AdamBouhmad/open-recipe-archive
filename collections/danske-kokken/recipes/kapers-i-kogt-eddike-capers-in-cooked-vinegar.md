## Ingredients

- Seeds or berries
- Brine
- Raw vinegar

## Directions

1. Pour the aforementioned brine over the seeds, where they stand until the next day.
2. Then pour away the brine and pour raw vinegar over them.
3. The day after, pour fresh vinegar over the seeds and tie the jar shut.
