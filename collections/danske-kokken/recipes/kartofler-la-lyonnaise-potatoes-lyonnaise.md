## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onions
- Potatoes, boiled the previous day
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- White pepper, ground
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place thin slices of onion in a pan with plenty of butter and brown them lightly.
2. Slice the potatoes, which must not be mealy, and brown them in the pan until they acquire a brownish-yellow color.
3. Before serving, stir in chopped parsley, salt, ground white pepper, and vinegar to taste.
4. Serve as hot as possible with roasted meat, cutlets, etc.
