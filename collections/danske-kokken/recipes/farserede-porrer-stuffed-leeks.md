## Ingredients

- cooked leek pieces
- forcemeat
- breadcrumbs or flour
- egg
- butter or Palmin
- browned butter
- small whole cooked carrots or browned potatoes

## Directions

1. Wrap each cut cooked leek piece in forcemeat
2. Sprinkle a cutting board with breadcrumbs or flour, place spoonfuls of forcemeat on it, flatten it, and place a cooked leek on each
3. Gather the forcemeat around the leek, roll in bread, dip in egg, then in bread again
4. Fry in a pan with butter or Palmin
5. Serve with browned butter
6. Garnish the stuffed leeks with small whole cooked carrots or browned potatoes
