## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) medister sausage
- Butter

## Directions

1. Place the sausages in a pot or on a frying pan and pour hot water over them.
2. Allow them to boil for approximately 5 minutes.
3. Remove them and dry the pan.
4. Brown the sausages in a good amount of butter.
5. Pour the butter in which they were browned into a sauce bowl.
6. Serve with stewed potatoes or a vegetable stew of various types of cabbage.
