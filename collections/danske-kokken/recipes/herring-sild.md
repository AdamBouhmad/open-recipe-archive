## Ingredients

- Herring
- Flour
- Butter or clarified butter

## Directions

1. Herring can be used for soup, but they are mostly fried.
2. After the herring is cleaned, washed and dried, dip it in flour and fry in butter or clarified butter.
3. Often herring are fried in the following way: brown butter in a pan and then dip the herring in flour and place in the pan with the head of one to the tail of another as long as there is space, and they are packed so tightly together that they stick.
