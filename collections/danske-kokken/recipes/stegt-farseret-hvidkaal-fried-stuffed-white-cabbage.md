## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage head
- forcemeat
- butter
- broth or water
- coloring
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Stuff the head as described in recipe 221
2. Brown the head in butter in a pot
3. Add broth or water, a little coloring, salt, and liquid as typically used for a pot roast
4. Turn the head once during cooking under a closed lid for 3.5 to 4 hours
5. Make the sauce from browned butter and flour, thinned with the gravy
6. Can also be roasted in the oven
