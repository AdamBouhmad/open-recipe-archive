## Ingredients

- Lightly salted beef tongue
- Water

## Directions

1. Place the lightly salted beef tongue in boiling water over heat
2. Cook very gently for 2 to 3 hours
3. When the skin comes off easily, the tongue is usually tender
4. Place it back in the soup to keep it warm until serving
5. Cut slightly on the slant into almost 1 centimeter (1/4 inch) thick slices
6. Arrange the slices so the tongue lies whole on the platter with vegetables around it
7. The vegetables can either be tossed in butter after boiling or stewed
8. For serving cold, the tongue will be more juicy if cooled in the soup
9. Smoked beef tongue should be soaked for a few hours before cooking
