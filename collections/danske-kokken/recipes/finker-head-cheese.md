## Ingredients

- Meat from a cooked ox head
- Ox lung
- Ox heart
- Ox liver
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Apples
- Ginger
- Whole pepper

## Directions

1. Use the meat of a cooked ox head along with lung, heart and liver of an ox.
2. These parts must be well rinsed and soak for several hours in water so the blood can be extracted, then cook in salted water and skim carefully.
3. After sufficient cooking, cut away all unusable parts; lung and heart are chopped, while liver is grated to make it easier to remove the sinews.
4. Strain the soup in which it cooked, and in it give the chopped mixture a re-boil together with the head meat cut into cubes.
5. Place in crocks and seal when cold.
6. When it is to be used, add vinegar and sugar along with sliced apples if you have them, some pieces of ginger, whole pepper and other seasonings as desired.
