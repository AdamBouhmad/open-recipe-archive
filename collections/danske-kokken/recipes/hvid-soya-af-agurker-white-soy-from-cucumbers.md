## Ingredients

- flesh, seeds and juice from hollowed cucumbers
- Salt
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the flesh, seeds and juice hollowed from cucumbers on the stove with a little salt and cook for 1/2 hour with constant stirring so nothing sticks, which would give a burnt or musty taste; if this happens the whole must be discarded
2. Press through a sieve
3. Return the juice to the stove with one gill vinegar per pint of juice and cook until it is smooth, about 1/2 hour
4. Place in small bottles or jars and bind well the next day
5. This soy gives a pleasant flavor in all kinds of white sauces, fricassees and the like, and must be added early so it cooks a bit with the sauce
