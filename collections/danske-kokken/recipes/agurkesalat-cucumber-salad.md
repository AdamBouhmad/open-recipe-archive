## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Use white, long cucumbers if possible; whether using white or green, make sure they are not spotted as these are bitter
2. Peel thinly and cut into thin slices across the cucumber
3. Place the slices on a plate, sprinkle with salt, cover with another plate and let stand for one hour
4. Gently press the slices and prepare with vinegar, pepper, and salt, and with oil if desired, added before the vinegar
