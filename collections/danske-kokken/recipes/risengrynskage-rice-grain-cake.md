## Ingredients

- 1 pound best Carolina rice
- 2 pots sweet milk
- 1 pound melted butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 9 quintal bitter pounded almonds
- 25 quintal powdered sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Clean the rice (see recipe 6) and cook it with 2 pots sweet milk into a soft porridge
2. When cold, stir in 1 pound melted butter, 10 egg yolks one by one, 9 quintal bitter pounded almonds, 25 quintal powdered sugar, and lemon zest
3. Finally fold in the 10 egg whites beaten to peaks
4. Place the whole in a buttered form and bake 0.75 hour in a moderately warm oven
