## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon flour
- pinch salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Put the milk on the stove
2. When hot, add the egg and sugar beaten with flour
3. Cook
