## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- milk
- 2 cups cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- butter
- 1/2 cup sweet cream

## Directions

1. Stew a nice chicken till tender, remove the larger bones, salt and pepper
2. Thicken gravy with flour made smooth with milk and have plenty of gravy or the pie will be dry
3. For Crust: Use one pint or 2 cups cream; if sour add 1 teaspoon soda, if sweet 1/2 teaspoon soda
4. Add 1 teaspoon baking powder in flour enough to roll
5. Line 3 quart pan around sides, put in chicken and piece of butter, with 1/2 cup sweet cream, butter size of an egg
6. Cover with remainder of crust dough, cut a slit in top for steam escape and bake
7. When nice and brown remove from oven, cover for ten minutes and serve
