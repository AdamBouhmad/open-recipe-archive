## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds veal fat and lean
- 1 slice salt pork
- 3 common crackers
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 small teaspoons pepper
- pinch of cloves
- herbs
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop the veal and salt pork together raw.
2. Pound the crackers fine and mix with egg, butter, pepper, cloves, and any herb to suit taste.
3. Mix it well together and make into a loaf.
4. Put in a shallow baking pan with a little water, cover with bits of butter, and sift flour over it.
5. Bake slowly for 1.5 hours.
