## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- a little water
- 1 cup dates, chopped fine
- white of 1 egg, well-beaten

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and a little water together until brittle when dropped into cold water
2. Remove from stove and stir quickly into the well-beaten white of an egg
3. Add 1 cup dates chopped fine
