## Ingredients

- 24 bitter oranges
- 6 sweet oranges
- 11 qts. water
- 22 lbs. sugar
- Juice 6 lemons

## Directions

1. Cut up the oranges very thinly
2. Cover with twenty-two pints of cold water
3. Allow to stand for thirty-six hours
4. Boil quickly for two hours
5. Add sugar, and boil steadily for one hour
6. If you care to, add one wineglass of brandy as you are taking from the stove, to clear the jelly
7. Put into pots, cool, and cover
