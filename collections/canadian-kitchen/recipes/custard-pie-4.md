## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups milk
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly
2. Add sugar, salt and milk
3. Line plate with paste and build up a fluted rim
4. Strain in the mixture and sprinkle with nutmeg
5. Bake in quick oven to set rim, then decrease heat
