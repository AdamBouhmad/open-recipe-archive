## Ingredients

- three pounds of beefsteak
- fifteen soda biscuits
- half a cupful of sweet milk
- half a cupful of butter
- one teaspoonful of salt and pepper
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the beefsteak fine.
2. Roll the soda biscuits well.
3. Mix all ingredients well in a loaf and bake two hours.
