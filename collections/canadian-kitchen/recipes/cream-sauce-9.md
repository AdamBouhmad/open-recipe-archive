## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt butter in saucepan
2. Add the flour and stir until frothy
3. Add milk
4. Stir constantly until it boils a minute
5. Add seasoning
