## Ingredients

- 15 large cucumbers (green)
- 15 large green tomatoes
- 2 heads cabbage
- 2 heads celery
- 1 pint horseradish
- salt
- 3.5 gallon vinegar
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 1 small tin mustard
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons tumeric
- 3/4 teaspoon cayenne

## Directions

1. Chop up fine as mincemeat and as you chop, sprinkle with salt
2. When it is all chopped, drain off the water
3. Add vinegar, brown sugar, mustard, black pepper, tumeric, and cayenne
4. Boil 25 minutes; stir to prevent burning
5. Seal tight
