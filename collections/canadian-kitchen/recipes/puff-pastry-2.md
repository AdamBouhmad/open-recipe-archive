## Ingredients

- ¾ pound flour
- ¾ pound butter
- Ice-cold water

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, form it into a flat, round cake, and put it away to chill
2. Make the flour into a stiff dough with ice water, and knead until perfectly smooth
3. Roll into two pieces a trifle larger than the butter pat; place the butter on one, moisten the edge, place the other on top, and press the edges together
4. Put aside to chill
5. Roll it out in one direction only, until about three times as long as broad, fold into three or four layers, turn around and roll again in the other direction, then set aside to chill
6. Roll and chill twice more in the same way, but fold it in half the last time
7. Roll out and cut into shape for baking
8. Bake in a rather hot oven at first, then cooler
