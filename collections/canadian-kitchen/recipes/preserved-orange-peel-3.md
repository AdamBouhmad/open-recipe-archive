## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges and cut rinds into narrow shreds
2. Boil till tender, change the water 3 times
3. Squeeze the juice of orange over sugar
4. Put pound to pound of sugar, and peel
5. Boil all together 20 minutes
