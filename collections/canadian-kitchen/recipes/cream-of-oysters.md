## Ingredients

- One quart of oysters in their own liquor
- One tablespoonful of butter
- One heaping tablespoonful of flour
- Pepper, salt and paprica to taste
- One half pint of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Scald a quart of oysters in their own liquor
2. Remove the oysters; chop and pound them in a mortar, then press as much of them as possible through a puree sieve
3. Make a roux of one tablespoonful of butter and a heaping tablespoonful of flour
4. Dilute it with the oyster juice
5. Add the oyster pulp; season it with pepper, salt and paprica, and keep it hot until ready to serve
6. Just before serving add a half pint of whipped cream, and beat it well into the soup
