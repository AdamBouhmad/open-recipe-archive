## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 3 cups flour
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- fruit
- cloves
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Combine 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup molasses, 3 cups flour, 1/4 cup sour milk, 2 eggs, 1 teaspoon soda, fruit, cloves and nutmeg.
2. Bake in moderate oven.
