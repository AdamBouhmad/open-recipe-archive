## Ingredients

- sour or loppered milk
- cloth or towel
- sieve
- salt
- butter
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put a pan of sour or loppered milk on the stove or range, where it is not too hot
2. Let it scald until the whey rises to the top; be careful that it does not boil, or the curd will become hard and tough
3. Place a clean cloth or towel over a sieve, and pour this whey and curd into it
4. Leave it covered to drain two or three hours
5. Put it into a dish and chop it fine with a spoon
6. Add a teaspoonful of salt, a tablespoonful of butter and enough sweet cream to make the cheese the consistency of putty
7. With your hands make it into little balls flattened
8. Keep it in a cool place
