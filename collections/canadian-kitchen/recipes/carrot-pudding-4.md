## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup grated potatoes
- 1 cup grated carrots
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- a little salt
- pinch of soda

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Pour into a buttered basin
3. Steam 3 hours
4. Eat with sweet melted butter
