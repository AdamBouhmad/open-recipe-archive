## Ingredients

- One cup of maple syrup
- One pint of cream
- Yolks of four eggs

## Directions

1. Boil syrup five minutes
2. Remove from stove and add beaten yolks of eggs
3. When cold stir in cream, which has been whipped
4. Pour into mould
5. Pack in ice and salt for three hours
