## Ingredients

- 1 quart buttermilk freshly churned
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to make a good batter

## Directions

1. Combine buttermilk, sour cream, egg, and soda. Add flour to make a good batter.
