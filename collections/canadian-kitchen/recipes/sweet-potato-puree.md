## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- hot milk
- egg
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Mash thoroughly the boiled potatoes, and season them well with salt, pepper, and butter.
2. Add enough hot milk to moisten them.
3. Serve it the same as mashed white potato; or put it in a pudding-dish, brush the top with egg, and brown it in the oven.
4. Serve with it a tomato sauce, and use as a luncheon dish.
5. Either boiled or baked potatoes may be used.
