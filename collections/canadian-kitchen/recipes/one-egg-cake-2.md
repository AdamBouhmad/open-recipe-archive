## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- butter size of an egg
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour (sifted)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and butter well together
2. Add egg and beat together again
3. Add milk
4. Add sifted flour and baking powder
5. Bake in jelly tins
