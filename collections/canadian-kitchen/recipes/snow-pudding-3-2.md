## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot water
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1/2 cup sugar
- a pinch of salt
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup or 1/2 cake grated chocolate
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook 2 cups hot water with 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1/2 cup sugar and a pinch of salt
2. Beat whites of 3 eggs to a stiff froth
3. Pour into boiling starch
4. For custard part, boil 1/2 cup or 1/2 cake grated chocolate in 3/4 cup sweet milk
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. When cool add to the above
7. Bake slowly
