## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 cup raspberries
- 2 cups flour
- cloves, cinnamon and nutmeg for seasoning

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake in 2 layers
