## Ingredients

- Juice and rind of 3 lemons
- 3 eggs
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well
2. Beat together juice and rind of 3 lemons, 3 eggs well beaten, 1 pound sugar, 1 cup water, 1 teaspoon butter
3. Boil 5 minutes
4. Keep in covered jelly glasses
