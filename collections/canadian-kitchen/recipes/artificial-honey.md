## Ingredients

- 10 pounds white sugar
- 3 pounds clear bees' honey
- 1 quart hot water
- 3/8 ounce cream of tartar
- 2 or 3 drops otto roses
- clear yellow honeycomb

## Directions

1. Mix together 10 pounds white sugar, 3 pounds clear bees' honey, 1 quart hot water, 3/8 ounce cream of tartar
2. When cool flavor with 2 or 3 drops otto roses and sprinkle in a handful of clear yellow honeycomb broken up
