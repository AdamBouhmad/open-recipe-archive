## Ingredients

- 1 qt. tomatoes
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 small onion chopped
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- cayenne
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut a piece from the top of each tomato.
2. Scoop out the inside, leaving a hollow shell.
3. Chop the pulp fine, mix with the crumbs, butter, sugar, pepper, salt, and onion.
4. Fill the cavities of the tomatoes with this stuffing, heaping and rounding each.
5. Scatter fine crumbs on the top, and arrange in a bake-dish.
6. Set the dish, covered, in an oven, and bake half an hour before uncovering.
7. After which brown lightly, and send to table on a hot platter.
