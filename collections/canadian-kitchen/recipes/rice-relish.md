## Ingredients

- 1 large cup rice
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup cold meat, any kind
- pepper and salt
- 2 tablespoons catsup
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash rice in cold water
2. Add 1 quart boiling water and 1 teaspoon salt
3. Boil quickly until tender and water is all boiled away
4. Mince 1 cup cold meat of any kind
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Place in granite dish
7. Spread over the boiled rice 2 tablespoons catsup
8. On top place a few lumps of butter and some bread crumbs
9. Bake 20 or 30 minutes
