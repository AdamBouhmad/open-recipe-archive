## Ingredients

- 1 pint corn (grated)
- 1 teacup flour
- 1 egg
- pepper and salt to taste
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Grate corn to make 1 pint
2. Mix with flour, egg, pepper and salt
3. If too thick, add a little milk
4. Fry in butter
