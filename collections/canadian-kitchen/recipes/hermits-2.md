## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 large cup butter or lard
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon mixed spice
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins chopped
- 1 cup nuts
- 5 teaspoons sour milk
- 3.5 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Beat sugar, butter and eggs together
2. Add other ingredients, flour and soda last
3. Drop in a long tin and bake in a moderate oven
