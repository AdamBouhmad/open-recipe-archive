## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1 cup cream and milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup currants (if preferred)
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Bake in 1 cake
