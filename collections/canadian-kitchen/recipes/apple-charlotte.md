## Ingredients

- Slices of bread and butter, with the crust trimmed off
- Six apples
- Grated rind of a lemon and the juice
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Butter a pie-dish
2. Place a layer of bread-and-butter at the bottom
3. Layer apples, peeled, cored and cut into slices
4. Sprinkle over with sugar, a little of the lemon peel, and a few drops of the juice
5. Repeat until the dish is full
6. Cover well over with the peel of the apples to prevent it burning
7. Bake in a quick oven about three-quarters of an hour
8. When done, remove the peel
9. Turn it out on a dish
10. Sprinkle with white sugar
11. Serve at once
