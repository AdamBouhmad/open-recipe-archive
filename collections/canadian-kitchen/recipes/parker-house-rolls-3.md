## Ingredients

- 1 egg, well beaten
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cake yeast
- A little salt
- Flour to make light dough

## Directions

1. Beat egg and salt, add sugar, dissolve yeast in a little milk, and add to egg and salt and sugar.
2. Add flour and set to rise for an hour or an hour and one-half.
3. Turn out and knead.
4. Roll out with a rolling pin about one-half inch thick.
5. Cut with a ring, brush over with water, fold in two and brush over with egg.
6. Set to rise to twice original size and bake.
7. Use same amount of yeast for twice the quantity.
