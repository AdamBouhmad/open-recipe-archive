## Ingredients

- 12 bitter oranges
- 1 lemon
- 6 quarts water
- 7 or 8 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Cut bitter oranges and one lemon very thin
2. Put into water
3. Let stand over night or thirty-six hours
4. Boil rather fast two and one-half hours
5. Add seven or eight pounds of white sugar
6. Boil one hour longer
7. Take out all seeds and hard ends
