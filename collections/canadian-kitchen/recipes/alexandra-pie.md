## Ingredients

- 2 crusts
- 1 egg
- 1 small cup sugar
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- a little nutmeg
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Stir all well together
