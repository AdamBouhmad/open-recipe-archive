## Ingredients

- brook trout
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- fat

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and dry the fish, handling tenderly, not to mar its beauty or flavor, roll in salted and peppered flour, and fry in deep fat to a delicate brown.
2. Serve up on folded tissue-paper in a hot-water dish, if you have one.
