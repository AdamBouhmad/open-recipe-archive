## Ingredients

- 6 eggs or more
- pepper
- salt
- chopped onion
- sage
- teacup milk
- good tablespoon of butter
- can of tomatoes

## Directions

1. Break eggs into a bowl, beat lightly and season with pepper, salt, chopped onion, and sage.
2. Add teacup milk.
3. Turn into a hot frying-pan in which you have heated a good tablespoon of butter.
4. Proceed as for scrambled eggs, only do not stir too much, as the larger the pieces of egg are, the nicer the dish will look.
5. Take a can of tomatoes and let it heat in a granite pan while your eggs are cooking, and season well with pepper and salt.
6. Turn the eggs out on a hot platter and pour the tomatoes over them.
7. Serve hot.
