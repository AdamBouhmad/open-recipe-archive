## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, buttermilk, eggs, flour, and butter
2. flavor with lemon, bake on lined plate same as custard
