## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon milk
- salt
- flour to make stiff

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together and bake.
