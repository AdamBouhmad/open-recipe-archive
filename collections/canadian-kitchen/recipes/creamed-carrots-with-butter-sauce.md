## Ingredients

- carrots
- salt
- water
- 2 medium teaspoons flour
- 2 medium teaspoons butter
- 1 cup water

## Directions

1. Scrape carrots, cut into cubes or slice thinly lengthwise
2. Cook in boiling salted water forty minutes, drain and pour the sauce over carrots in a warm dish
3. For Butter Sauce: mix 2 medium teaspoons flour, 2 medium teaspoons butter, 1 cup water and season to taste
