## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Take cold mashed potatoes
2. Season with pepper, salt and onion chopped fine
3. Beat in 1 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons milk
4. Shape carefully into cones
5. Brown fifteen minutes in the oven
