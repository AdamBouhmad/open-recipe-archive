## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of the crumbs of stale bread
- a gill of milk
- glycerine or sweet oil

## Directions

1. Put a tablespoonful of the crumbs of stale bread into a gill of milk, and give the whole one boil up
2. Or, take stale bread-crumbs, pour over them boiling water and boil till soft, stirring well
3. Take from the fire and gradually stir in a little glycerine or sweet oil, so as to render the poultice pliable when applied
