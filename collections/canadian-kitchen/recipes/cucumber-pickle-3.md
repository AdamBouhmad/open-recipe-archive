## Ingredients

- ripe cucumbers
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- oil of cloves
- oil of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers and cut lengthwise
2. Take out seeds
3. Soak in salt and water 24 hours
4. Take them out and wash them
5. Soak them in vinegar and water for 6 hours
6. Drain them
7. Make a syrup of 1 pound sugar and 1 quart vinegar
8. Flavor with oil of cloves and oil of cinnamon
9. Boil till tender
