## Ingredients

- 2 large lemons
- 6 large apples
- 3/4 pound suet
- 1 pound currants
- 3 1/2 pound sugar
- 2 ounces candied lemon peel
- 1 ounce citron peel
- mixed spice to taste

## Directions

1. Pare the lemons and squeeze them
2. Boil the peel tender enough to mash
3. Add to the mashed lemon peel the apples minced, then the chopped suet, currants, sugar, peel and spice
4. Strain the lemon juice to these ingredients
5. Stir the mixture well, put in a jar with a closely fitting lid
6. Stir occasionally
7. In one week it will be ready for use
