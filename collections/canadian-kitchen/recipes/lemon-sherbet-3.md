## Ingredients

- Six lemons
- Four eggs (the whites)
- Two pints sugar
- Water
- Vanilla
- Citric acid or cream tartar

## Directions

1. Make a thick syrup of one pint sugar and about one pint water
2. When cold, thin with the juice of six lemons, and water enough to make it a rich lemonade
3. When it is half frozen add boiled icing made as follows: One pint sugar moistened with water, and boiled until it is a soft candy
4. Whilst hot add the stiff beaten whites of four eggs
5. Flavor with vanilla and a little citric acid or cream tartar
6. Beat hard until thick and smooth
7. Add to the half frozen lemonade
