## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- whites of 5 eggs beaten light
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon almond extract

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. add the whites, then the milk, lastly the flour sifted with the baking powder
3. Beat until very light
4. Bake in moderate oven
