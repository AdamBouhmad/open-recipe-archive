## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 3 eggs beaten
- melted butter the size of a good big egg
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 cups of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take out the bones and skin
2. Drain off oil
3. Add beaten eggs, melted butter, salt, pepper, and bread crumbs
4. Mix all well
5. Put in a buttered dish
6. Steam an hour
