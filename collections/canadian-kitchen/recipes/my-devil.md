## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of Worcestershire sauce
- 1 small dessertspoonful anchovy sauce
- 3 teaspoonfuls made mustard
- 1 saltspoonful of salt
- 1 saltspoonful pepper
- 0.5 saltspoonful cayenne
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful brown sugar
- one squeeze of lemon
- one glass port wine

## Directions

1. Mix together and heat in saucepan, and pour over grilled turkey legs, or any cold meat grilled for breakfast or lunch.
