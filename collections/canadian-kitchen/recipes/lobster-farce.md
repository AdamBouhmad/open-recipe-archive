## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiled lobster meat
- 1/2 cup milk or cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon bread crumbs
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1-6 teaspoon paprika
- lobster shells

## Directions

1. Put butter in pan
2. When melted stir in flour and milk or cream
3. Let boil
4. Remove from fire
5. Add lobster meat cut in pieces
6. Fill shells from which the lobster meat was taken with farce
7. Cover with buttered crumbs
8. Brown in oven
9. Arrange on bed of parsley
