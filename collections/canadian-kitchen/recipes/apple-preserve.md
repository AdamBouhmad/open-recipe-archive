## Ingredients

- equal quantities of good brown sugar and apples
- 1 pint of water to 3 pounds of sugar
- 1 or 2 lemons
- a little whole white ginger

## Directions

1. Weigh equal quantities of good brown sugar and apples
2. Peel, core and cut the apples into small square pieces
3. Make syrup of 1 pint of water to 3 pounds of sugar
4. Boil until pretty thick
5. Add the apples, the grated peel of a lemon or two, and a little whole white ginger
6. Boil until the apples are clear and begin to fall
