## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 even tablespoon flour
- 1 pint milk

## Directions

1. Cut a cabbage into quarters
2. Put into boiling salt water and cook 20 minutes
3. Remove the pieces and put into cold water till cool
4. When cold, chop fine and season with pepper and salt
5. Mix butter with flour and make into a sauce by adding milk
6. Put all together and let simmer half an hour
7. Serve
