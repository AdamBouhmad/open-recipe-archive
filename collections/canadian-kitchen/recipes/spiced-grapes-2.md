## Ingredients

- 7 lbs. grapes
- 1 pt. vinegar
- 4 lbs. sugar
- 4 teaspoonfuls ground cinnamon
- 5 teaspoonfuls ground cloves

## Directions

1. Remove skins of grapes and boil pulp till soft.
2. Strain through a bag or fine colander.
3. Add skins and ingredients, and boil two hours.
4. Bottle.
