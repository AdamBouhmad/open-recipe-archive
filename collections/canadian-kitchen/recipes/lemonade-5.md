## Ingredients

- 4 lemons
- 4 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 qt. water or a bottle of Apollinaris

## Directions

1. Roll, peel, and slice four lemons
2. Put lemons (sliced) and sugar into a pitcher
3. Let them stand for an hour
4. Add water and ice
5. If you substitute Apollinaris for plain water you have a most refreshing drink
