## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons yellow sugar
- 1 scant cup molasses
- butter or lard size of an egg
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup yellow sugar
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- a little water

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well
2. Mix in 3 tablespoons yellow sugar and 1 scant cup molasses
3. Add butter or lard size of an egg
4. Add 2 cups flour measured before sifting
5. Mix well
6. Add 1 cup boiling water into which has been dissolved 1 teaspoon baking soda
7. Bake in layers
8. For frosting: Mix 1 cup yellow sugar, 1 tablespoon molasses and a little water
9. Boil till it threads
