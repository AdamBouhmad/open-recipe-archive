## Ingredients

- 1 quart raw cabbage chopped fine
- 1 quart boiled beets chopped fine
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon red pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Cover with vinegar
