## Ingredients

- turkey or chicken
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- bread and cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Pick and draw the fowl
2. Wash them well in 2 or 3 waters
3. Wipe them dry
4. Dredge them with a little flour inside and out
5. Sprinkle on a little pepper and salt
6. Prepare a dressing of bread and cracker crumbs
7. Fill the bodies and crops of the fowls
8. Bake them from 2 to 3 hours
9. Baste them frequently while roasting
