## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup cold water
- 2 tablespoons flour
- juice and grated rind of one lemon

## Directions

1. Combine 1 cup sugar, 1 egg, 1 cup cold water, and 2 tablespoons flour
2. Add juice and grated rind of one lemon
