## Ingredients

- 10 pounds ripe tomatoes
- 3 pounds ripe apples
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 ounce ginger
- 0.25 ounce cayenne pepper
- 1 ounce allspice
- 3 lemons
- quarts vinegar
- 1 large onion

## Directions

1. Boil tomatoes, apples, raisins and lemons together
2. Strain
3. Add vinegar and spice
4. Tie spice in muslin bags
