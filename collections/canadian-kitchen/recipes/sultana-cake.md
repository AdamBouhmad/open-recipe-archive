## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound flour
- 6 ounces butter
- 1 cup sultanas
- small piece lemon peel
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- 6 ounces white sugar

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar together
2. Add eggs well beaten
3. Add the other ingredients
4. Bake in slow oven 1 1/2 or 2 hours
