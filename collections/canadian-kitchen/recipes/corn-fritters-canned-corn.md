## Ingredients

- one can of corn
- two eggs
- seasoning to taste
- two tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- one tablespoonful of butter
- thickening

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well; add corn by degrees, beating very hard; salt to taste; one tablespoonful of butter; stir in milk and thickening enough to hold together for frying.
