## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- celery or cucumber
- salt and pepper
- oil
- lettuce leaves or cucumber shells

## Directions

1. Parboil sweetbreads twenty minutes
2. Drain and cool
3. Cut in one-half inch cubes
4. Mix with celery or cucumber cut in small pieces
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Moisten with oil
7. Arrange on lettuce leaves or serve in cucumber shells
