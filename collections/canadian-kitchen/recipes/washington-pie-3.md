## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- butter size of walnut
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- a few drops vanilla
- 1 pint milk
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch

## Directions

1. Bake cake in a round tin using egg, milk, sugar, butter, flour, baking powder, and vanilla
2. When baked, cut in two
3. For filling: put milk on the stove and when it boils add the cornstarch dissolved with a little of the milk
4. Add the beaten eggs, sugar and vanilla to the filling
5. Fill the cake with this filling
