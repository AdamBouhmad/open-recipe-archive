## Ingredients

- very stale bread
- finely chopped onions
- apples
- raw eggs
- salt
- pepper
- allspice

## Directions

1. Steam very stale bread according to size of fowl.
2. Add finely chopped onions and apples, raw eggs, salt, pepper and allspice to taste.
3. For a duck: use small bowl bread, one onion, two apples, three eggs, mixed well.
