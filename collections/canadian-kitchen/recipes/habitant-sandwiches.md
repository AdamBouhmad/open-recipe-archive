## Ingredients

- buttered slices from roll loaf
- large apples
- cheese
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Prepare buttered slices from roll loaf
2. Pare large apples and slice through so each slice may fit on bread
3. Remove the seeds and core
4. Place on lower slice
5. Sprinkle a trifle of cheese over the apple, or spread with a rich mayonnaise
