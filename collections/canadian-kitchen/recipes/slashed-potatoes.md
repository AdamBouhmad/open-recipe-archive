## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- cream
- butter
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Take 2 quarts mashed potatoes, 1/2 cup cream, 1/2 cup butter
2. Season with salt and white pepper
3. Beat until the consistency of cream
4. Serve
