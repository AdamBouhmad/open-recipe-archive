## Ingredients

- whitefish
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- sliced eggs
- flour
- boiling water
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Lay the fish open
2. put it in a dripping pan, with the back down
3. nearly cover with water
4. to one fish put two tablespoonfuls of salt
5. cover tightly and simmer (not boil) one-half hour
6. dress with gravy, butter and pepper
7. garnish with sliced eggs
8. for sauce use a piece of butter the size of an egg, one tablespoonful of flour, one-half pint boiling water
9. boil a few minutes, and add three hard-boiled eggs, shred
