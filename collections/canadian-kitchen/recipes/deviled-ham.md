## Ingredients

- lean boiled ham
- mustard
- flour
- boiling water
- egg
- sweet milk or cream
- sugar
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop lean boiled ham very fine
2. To each pint of ham add 1 teaspoon mustard, 1 tablespoon flour, 3/4 cup boiling water
3. Press into moulds to slice or for sandwiches
4. Beat 1 egg well with 1/2 cup sweet milk or cream, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 teaspoon salt
5. Cook all together till thick
6. When cold add 3/4 cup strong vinegar
