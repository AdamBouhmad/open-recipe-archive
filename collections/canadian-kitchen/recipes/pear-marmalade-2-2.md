## Ingredients

- 7 pounds pears
- 7 pounds sugar
- 3/4 pound preserved ginger cut fine
- juice of 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Peel and cut fine pears
2. Add sugar and let stand 24 hours
3. Add preserved ginger cut fine
4. Boil 3 hours
5. Add juice of 3 lemons and boil a few minutes longer
