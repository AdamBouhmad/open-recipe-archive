## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup sweet milk
- ½ cup butter
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- whites of 5 eggs
- freshly grated coconut

## Directions

1. Beat sugar, milk, butter, flour, baking powder, and egg whites well
2. Bake in a deep square tin
3. Cut in 2-inch squares
4. Remove the outside
5. Frost all sides
6. Roll in freshly grated coconut
