## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 cups rice, ground
- 2 large tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter or cream
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 2 large cups milk

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, add sugar; melted butter or cream; and milk
2. Mix into a stiff batter with ground rice and flour in proportion of three cups of rice to one of flour
3. Add baking powder last; the sugar may be melted if desired
