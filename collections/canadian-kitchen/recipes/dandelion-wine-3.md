## Ingredients

- water (3 quarts)
- dandelion flowers (4 quarts)
- sugar (4 pounds)
- lemons (3)
- White Swan Yeast Cake (1)
- toast

## Directions

1. Boil water and dandelion flowers until soft
2. Strain and add 4 pounds sugar and 3 lemons sliced
3. Put it on again and boil 15 minutes
4. Set to cool and when about lukewarm set on top of it a slice of toast and spread 1 White Swan Yeast Cake on it
5. Let it stand 48 hours in a warm place
6. Strain again and bottle, but do not cork down
7. Let it stand 4 or 5 weeks, then cork and put away
