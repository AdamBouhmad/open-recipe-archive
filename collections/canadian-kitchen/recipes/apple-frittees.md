## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 2 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- Milk for thin batter
- 1 oz. butter
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- Apples
- Boiling lard or clarified dripping
- Sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare some apples, as for a pie, or mince them if preferred
2. Add these to the batter
3. Drop a large tablespoonful at a time into a pan of boiling lard or clarified dripping
4. Fry till of a light brown, turning when required
5. When done, lay them on a sheet of blotting-paper before the fire to absorb the grease
6. Dish them, piled high, one above the other
7. Strew with sifted sugar
8. Serve as hot as possible
