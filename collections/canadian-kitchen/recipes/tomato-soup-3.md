## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 qt. stock
- 1 gill cream or milk
- 1 oz. butter
- 1 oz. flour
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Boil together the stock and tomatoes for fifteen minutes, then rub them through a sieve
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, stir in flour and strained stock, boil two minutes
3. Allow the boil to go off, then add cream, and do not allow it to boil again or the cream will curdle
