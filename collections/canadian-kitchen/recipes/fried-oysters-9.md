## Ingredients

- large oysters
- cracker crumbs or cornmeal
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Select the largest oysters for frying
2. Take them carefully from the liquor, lay them on a cloth and press another lightly upon them to absorb the moisture
3. Roll in cracker crumbs or cornmeal
4. Fry quickly to a light brown in hot butter
5. Serve at once in a warm dish
