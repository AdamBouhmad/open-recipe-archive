## Ingredients

- 1 quart beets cooked and ground
- 1 quart cabbage ground fine
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- pepper to taste
- cold vinegar to cover

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Cover with cold vinegar
