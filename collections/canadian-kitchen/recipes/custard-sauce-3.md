## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- pinch salt
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Heat the milk in a double boiler
2. Beat the egg just enough to mix it thoroughly
3. Stir in the sugar and the salt
4. Stir the hot milk into the egg
5. Return to the double boiler
6. Stir constantly until it thickens and coats the spoon
7. Turn immediately into the bowl
8. Add the flavoring and stir a moment or two
