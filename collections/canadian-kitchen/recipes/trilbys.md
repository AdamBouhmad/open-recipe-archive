## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 cups oatmeal
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar

## Directions

1. Mix butter and sugar
2. Add sour milk
3. Mix flour, oatmeal, soda, and cream tartar
4. Add flour enough to roll out
5. Bake as cookies
6. When cool spread date filling
