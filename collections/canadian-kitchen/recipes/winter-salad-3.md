## Ingredients

- 2 quarts green cucumbers
- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 quarts green cabbage
- 2 large cauliflowers
- a pinch of red pepper
- salt
- vinegar and water
- 6 bunches of celery
- 1 gallon vinegar
- 3/4 pound mustard
- 3 teaspoons turmeric
- 5 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 heaping cup flour

## Directions

1. Let cucumbers, tomatoes, cabbage, cauliflowers and red pepper stand over night in salt in separate dishes
2. Drain in the morning and scald in vinegar and water
3. Add 6 bunches of celery, 1 gallon vinegar, 3/4 pound mustard, 3 teaspoons turmeric, 5 1/2 cups sugar, 1 heaping cup flour
4. Mix mustard, flour and turmeric in cold vinegar
5. Put on and scald
