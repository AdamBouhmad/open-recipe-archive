## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon lard
- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- hot potato water
- 1/2 cup warm water
- flour

## Directions

1. At noon mix sugar, salt, lard and potatoes in crock, pouring over this the hot potato water.
2. When cool add yeast cake which has been dissolved in a half cup warm water.
3. By night this should look foamy.
4. In the morning warm the mixture and mix stiff with flour.
5. When light knead and put in pans.
