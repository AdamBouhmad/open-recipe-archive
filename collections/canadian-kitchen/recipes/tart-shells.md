## Ingredients

- white of 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 cup lard
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 2 small teaspoons baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Put lard in dish and add sugar and mix together
2. Add the beaten white of egg and water
3. Sift baking powder with flour and mix stiff enough to roll
4. Cut with large biscuit cutter
5. Take 2 off sides and turn towards centre which has been dampened with a little water
6. Turn other 2 sides and press down in centre with finger
7. Bake in very hot oven
8. Fill with good jelly or jam when needed
