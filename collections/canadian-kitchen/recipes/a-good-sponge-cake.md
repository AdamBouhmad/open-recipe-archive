## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- Weight of 10 in sugar
- Weight of 6 in flour
- Flavor with lemon extract

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks stiff, then add the sugar
2. beat with the yolks until very light
3. now essence
4. now gently stir in whites which have been beaten stiff, and last put in flour
5. Stir it in, do not beat it in
6. have buttered pans ready
7. Bake in moderate oven
