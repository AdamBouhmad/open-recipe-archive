## Ingredients

- tea
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Allow 1 teaspoon of tea to each person
2. Have the teapot hot and then put in the tea
3. Pour over this boiling water until steeper is a little more than half full
4. Cover tightly and let it stand where it will keep
