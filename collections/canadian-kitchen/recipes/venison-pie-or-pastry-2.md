## Ingredients

- neck, breast and shoulder of venison
- fat and lean meat together
- bones and trimmings
- pepper and salt
- water or veal broth
- good rich paste
- nutmeg and mace
- a glass of port wine
- bits of butter rolled in flour
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces (fat and lean together) and put the bones and trimmings into the stew-pan with pepper and salt, and water or veal broth enough to cover it.
2. Simmer it till you have drawn out a good gravy.
3. Strain it.
4. Make a good rich paste, and roll it rather thick.
5. Cover the bottom and sides of a deep dish with one sheet of it, and put in your meat, having seasoned it with pepper, salt, nutmeg and mace.
6. Pour in the gravy which you have prepared from the trimmings, and a glass of port wine.
7. Lay on the top some bits of butter rolled in flour.
8. Cover the pie with a thick lid of paste and ornament it handsomely with leaves and flowers formed with a tin cutter.
9. Bake two or more hours according to the size.
10. Just before it is done, pull it forward in the oven, and brush it over with beaten egg; push it back and let it slightly brown.
