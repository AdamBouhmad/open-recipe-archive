## Ingredients

- 1 pint sweet milk
- 1 teacup yeast or 1 compressed yeast cake
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- A little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Add butter, sugar, soda, little nutmeg to milk and yeast mixture
2. Let rise
3. Knead and mould into shape
4. Let rise and bake
5. When done wet top with eggs, sprinkle with sugar, and return to oven again for a moment
6. Serve hot
