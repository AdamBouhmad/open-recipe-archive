## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- pinch salt
- heaping teaspoon baking powder
- sweet milk
- whites of 2 eggs beaten stiff
- preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pint flour, pinch salt and heaping teaspoon baking powder into a soft batter with sweet milk, add whites of 2 eggs beaten stiff
2. Have ready well-greased cups, this quantity requires 6 or 7
3. Into each put a couple teaspoons batter, then a little preserved fruit, then more batter, leaving plenty room to rise
4. Steam 20 or 30 minutes
5. Serve hot with sauce
