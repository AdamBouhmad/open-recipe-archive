## Ingredients

- 1 pint of sweet milk
- 1 1/2 pints bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- flour to thicken

## Directions

1. Mix sweet milk, bread crumbs, eggs, baking powder, salt, and flour to thicken. Fry, turning carefully as they are very tender.
