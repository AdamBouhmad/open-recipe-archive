## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound macaroni
- 2 cups white sauce
- 1 egg
- 1 pound dried beef
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Boil macaroni until tender
2. Drain
3. Tear beef into small pieces
4. Add egg to sauce
5. Put in layers in buttered mould
6. Cover and steam 2 hours
7. Turn out and serve with tomato sauce
