## Ingredients

- Frogs' legs, hind legs only
- Milk
- Pepper
- Salt
- Flour
- Deep boiling fat
- Egg
- Pounded cracker

## Directions

1. Skin, wash, and lay in milk for fifteen minutes
2. Without wiping them, pepper and salt, and coat with flour
3. Fry in deep boiling fat to a light brown
4. Or, wipe off the milk, dip in egg and pounded cracker, and fry
