## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound chopped walnuts
- 1/4 pound prunes
- 1/4 pound figs
- 1/2 pound dates
- 1/2 pound raisins
- 2 handfuls cocoanut

## Directions

1. Chop very fine and mix well
2. Pack in mould
3. Cut in squares
4. Roll in white sugar
