## Ingredients

- 1 qt. green tomatoes
- 1 qt. gherkins
- 1 qt. ripe cucumbers
- 1 qt. celery
- 3 cups vinegar
- 3 cups brown sugar
- 1 qt. white onions
- 1 qt. string beans
- 1 large cauliflower
- 6 green peppers
- 6 tablespoonfuls mustard
- 4 tablespoonfuls flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls turmeric
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Chop all very fine and cover with a good sprinkling of salt and water over night
2. Bring to a boil and drain
3. Boil together vinegar, sugar, mustard, flour and turmeric
4. Pour boiling hot over vegetables and bottle
