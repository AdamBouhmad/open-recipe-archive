## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 3/8 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter
2. add sugar gradually and flavoring
3. The beaten yolk or white of egg may be added; beat mixture well
4. Form on serving dish and put in cool place until used
