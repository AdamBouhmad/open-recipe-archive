## Ingredients

- 2 slices of bread
- 1 egg
- pinch salt
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour to make batter
- butter and lard mixed

## Directions

1. Soak bread slices for 1/2 hour in cold water. Pour water off dry and beat up bread fine. Add egg, pinch salt, brown sugar, milk, and baking powder. Add flour to make batter. Fry in butter and lard mixed.
