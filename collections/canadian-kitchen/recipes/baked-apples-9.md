## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- water
- nuts
- jelly
- cream
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Pare and core, leaving whole as many apples as required
2. Fill the centres with sugar
3. Pour a little water in the dish
4. Cover and cook in a hot oven
5. As a variation add some finely chopped nuts to the sugar or a lump of jelly in each apple
6. Serve hot or cold with cream
