## Ingredients

- 1 egg beaten
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons molasses
- 2 small teaspoons mixed spice
- small ½ cup shortening
- large half cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix egg, sugar, molasses, spice, shortening, and sour milk
2. Dissolve soda in sour milk
3. Add flour to make thick batter
4. Bake in a small dripping pan
5. Ice and cut in square pieces
