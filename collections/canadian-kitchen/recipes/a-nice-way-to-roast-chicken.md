## Ingredients

- young chicken
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a young chicken and prepare as for cooking in the kettle and roll each piece in flour till your pan is full, or till you have plenty
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt and a liberal sprinkling of butter
3. Set in the oven and roast till done, which takes about 1 hour
