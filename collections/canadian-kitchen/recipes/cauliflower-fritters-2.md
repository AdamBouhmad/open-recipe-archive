## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk (sweet)
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- a little salt
- flowerets of cauliflower
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Mix into a batter
2. Dip flowerets of cauliflower previously boiled tender in a little salted water
3. Fry in hot fat till brown
