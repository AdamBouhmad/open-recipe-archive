## Ingredients

- large cauliflowers
- salt water
- vinegar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 pound mustard
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon turmeric

## Directions

1. Cut large vegetables into small pieces and put into a jar
2. Pour over them boiling salt water, let stand until cold
3. Pour into a bag, drain all night
4. Pour boiling vinegar over them, let stand until cold
5. Pour off vinegar, put on stove, add 1 tablespoon salt
6. To each quart of vinegar take 1/4 pound mustard, 1 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons flour, and mix to a smooth paste in cold vinegar
7. Pour into the boiling vinegar and cook five minutes
8. Add 1 teaspoon turmeric
9. Bottle before cold
