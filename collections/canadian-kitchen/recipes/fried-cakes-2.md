## Ingredients

- 2 good-sized potatoes
- butter the size of a walnut
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder to each cup of flour
- flour enough so it will not stick to hands
- a little nutmeg
- pinch of salt
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Boil and mash potatoes fine
2. Add butter, sugar, and eggs beaten well
3. Add sweet milk
4. Add baking powder to flour
5. Use flour enough so it will not stick to hands, try not to get too stiff
