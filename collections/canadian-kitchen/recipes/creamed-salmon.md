## Ingredients

- A tin of salmon
- One pint of milk
- Butter size of an egg
- One teaspoonful of flour
- Salt and cayenne pepper to taste
- Two well-beaten eggs
- Cracker or bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Take a tin of salmon, empty on a dish and flake with a fork
2. Have ready a sauce made in double boiler from one pint of milk, butter size of an egg, one teaspoonful of flour, with salt and cayenne pepper to taste
3. Melt butter and flour until smooth and add milk slowly
4. When it boils remove from fire and add two well-beaten eggs
5. Put alternate layers of salmon and sauce in baking dish until all is used
6. Cover with layer of cracker or bread-crumbs
7. Bake fifteen minutes and serve hot
