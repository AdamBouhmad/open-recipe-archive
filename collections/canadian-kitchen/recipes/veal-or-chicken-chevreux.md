## Ingredients

- carrot
- peas
- beet
- meat
- warm aspic jelly

## Directions

1. Garnish small moulds with carrot, peas and beet, cooked and chopped with fancy cutter
2. Cut meat in small squares
3. Pack in moulds and fill with warm aspic jelly
