## Ingredients

- 3 potatoes
- 4 cups milk
- 1 onion
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 stalks celery

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in salted water with the onion
2. When done, mash smooth
3. Scald the milk with the celery
4. Remove the celery
5. Add the butter and flour, creamed together
6. Add the prepared potatoes to the milk
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Let come to a boil and serve at once
