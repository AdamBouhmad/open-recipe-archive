## Ingredients

- 3 even-sized tomatoes
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 ounce butter
- a little chopped parsley
- 6 rounds of buttered toast

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes in halves through the centre, not from stem to top
2. Take out some of the pulp into a saucepan with 1 ounce butter, and cook lightly
3. Beat eggs and add to milk and put in saucepan with pulp and allow all to cook, but not set
4. Place the tomato shells in the oven and let them cook until tender
5. Arrange them on toast and place mixture in them
6. Sprinkle parsley over them and decorate the dish with parsley leaves
