## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 1/4 teaspoons cloves
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix butter, sugar and water
2. Add eggs
3. Add flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves
4. Add baking powder
5. Bake in layers
6. Spread with icing
