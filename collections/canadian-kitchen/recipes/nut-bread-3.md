## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sweet milk
- a large pinch salt
- 4 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 10 cents' worth chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Let rise 20 minutes
3. Bake in a moderate oven 3/4 hour
