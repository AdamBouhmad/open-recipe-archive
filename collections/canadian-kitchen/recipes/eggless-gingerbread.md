## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour cream
- 3 cups flour
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- hot water

## Directions

1. Combine sugar, molasses, and sour cream
2. Add flour and butter
3. Add ginger and cinnamon
4. Dissolve soda in hot water and add last
5. Beat hard
6. Bake in a slow oven
