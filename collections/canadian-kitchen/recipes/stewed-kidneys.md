## Ingredients

- kidneys
- flour
- water
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil kidneys the night before until very tender
2. Turn meat and gravy into a dish and cover over
3. Next day boil a few minutes
4. Thicken with flour and water
5. Add part of an onion chopped fine, pepper, salt and a lump of butter
