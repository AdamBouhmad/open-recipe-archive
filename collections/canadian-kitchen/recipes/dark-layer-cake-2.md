## Ingredients

- Yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 3/4 cups sifted flour
- a pinch of salt
- 2 tablespoons cocoa or chocolate

## Directions

1. Bake in layers and put together with white filling and frost with chocolate
