## Ingredients

- 1 cup suet or butter
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- lemon peel to suit taste

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Steam 3 hours
