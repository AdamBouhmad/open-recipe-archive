## Ingredients

- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 coffee cup of milk
- 1 crust
- 2 bananas
- beaten whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a custard from the yolks of 3 eggs, 1 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon butter, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2. beat well and add 1 coffee cup of milk
3. bake in 1 crust
4. when done slice 2 bananas thin over the top
5. cover with the beaten whites of 2 eggs and serve fresh
