## Ingredients

- butter
- crushed cracker-crumbs
- oyster liquor
- milk
- oysters
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Butter a pudding-dish
2. Cover the bottom with a layer of crushed cracker-crumbs, moistening them with oyster liquor and a little milk
3. Put a layer of oysters on this, dotting it with bits of butter and seasoning with salt and pepper
4. Cover with another layer of crumbs
5. Repeat the layer of oysters
6. Continue in this order until the dish is full, making the top layer of crumbs
7. Strew thickly with bits of butter
8. Bake covered for 20 minutes and brown
