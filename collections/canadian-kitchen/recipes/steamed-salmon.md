## Ingredients

- 1 can of salmon
- 1 cup of fine breadcrumbs
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- A little milk
- Anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Steam in a mould one hour
2. Make a white sauce
3. Season with teaspoonful anchovy sauce
4. Pour over the salmon when served
