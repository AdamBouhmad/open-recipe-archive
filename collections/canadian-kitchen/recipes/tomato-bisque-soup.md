## Ingredients

- 1 qt. tomatoes
- 3 pts. milk
- 1 large tablespoonful of flour
- Butter size of an egg
- 1 scant teaspoonful of soda
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes on to stew and the milk in a double boiler, reserving half a cup to mix the flour
2. Mix flour smoothly and stir in boiling milk and cook ten minutes
3. Add soda to tomato, stir well and strain, add butter, salt and pepper to milk and then the tomato
