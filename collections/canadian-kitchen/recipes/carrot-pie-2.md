## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup grated carrots
- 1 cup water
- 2 eggs
- a small piece of butter
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- vanilla
- salt
- pie shell or light cake

## Directions

1. Boil same as lemon pie, adding pinch of salt
2. Whip egg whites for top
3. Can be put in pie shell or light cake for bottom
