## Ingredients

- 1 gallon cider vinegar
- 3/4 cup cloves
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 3/4 cup mustard
- 2 tablespoons tumeric
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 cup brown sugar
- flour to thicken
- cucumbers, soaked in brine over night

## Directions

1. Mix vinegar, cloves, salt, mustard, tumeric, curry powder, and brown sugar
2. Thicken with flour
3. Bring to a boil
4. Add cucumbers which have been soaked in brine over night
5. Cook 5 minutes
