## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch
- vanilla
- cocoanut enough to make stiff

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs very light and stiff
2. Add sugar and cornstarch dissolved in a very little water and stirred into the eggs and sugar
3. Put on a double boiler and cook over water (boiling) for about twenty or twenty-five minutes
4. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking
5. Add cocoanut enough to stand up well when dropped on buttered tins
6. Flavor with vanilla
7. Drop on tins and bake eight or ten minutes in a moderate oven
