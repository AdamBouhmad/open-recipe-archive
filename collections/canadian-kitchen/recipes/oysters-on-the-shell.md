## Ingredients

- oysters in shell
- toast

## Directions

1. Wash the shell
2. Put them on hot coals, or upon the top of a hot stove, or bake them in a hot oven
3. Open shells with an oyster knife, taking care to lose none of the liquor
4. Serve quickly on hot plates with toast
5. Oysters may be steamed in the shells, and are excellent eaten in the same manner
