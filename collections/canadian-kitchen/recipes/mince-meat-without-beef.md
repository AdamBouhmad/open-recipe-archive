## Ingredients

- 1 lb. chopped suet
- 2 lbs. seeded raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 2 lbs. apples
- 2 1/2 lbs. sugar
- 2 nutmegs grated
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 4 lemons boiled (with seeds removed)
- Grated yellow rind of 2 raw lemons
- 1/2 lb. citron
- 1/2 lb. orange
- 1/2 pt. sherry
- 1 teaspoonful each powdered mace and ginger
- 1/2 pt. brandy

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Stir in well half sherry and brandy
3. Cook several hours
