## Ingredients

- asparagus stalks
- salt
- white sauce or Hollandaise sauce or melted butter

## Directions

1. Scrape the stalks and lay them in cold water for half an hour
2. Tie into rather loose bundles with soft string
3. Cook in hot, salted water for half an hour
4. Undo the strings and arrange the stalks upon a hot dish
5. Pour white or Hollandaise sauce over it, or pass this separately, or serve melted butter with it
