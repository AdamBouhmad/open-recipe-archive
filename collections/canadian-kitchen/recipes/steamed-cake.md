## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon mixed spice
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking soda
- 2 teaspoons vinegar
- buttermilk
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, sugar, and butter well together
2. Mix in currants, raisins, molasses, vanilla, and mixed spice
3. Dissolve baking soda in vinegar, fill cup with buttermilk and add to cake
4. Mix stiff with flour
5. Put in a dish and steam 2 hours
6. Then bake slowly an hour
