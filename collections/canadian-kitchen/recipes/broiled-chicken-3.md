## Ingredients

- young spring chickens
- salt
- pepper
- soft butter
- maitre d'hotel butter
- parsley or watercress
- lemon

## Directions

1. Split them down the back, remove the entrails and the breast bone, and wipe them clean.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and rub them with soft butter.
3. Place them on a broiler over a slow fire, the inside down.
4. Cover with a pan, and let cook for twenty minutes to twenty-five minutes.
5. Turn, to let the skin side brown when nearly done.
6. Place them on a hot dish, and spread them with maitre d'hotel butter.
7. Garnish with parsley or watercress and thin slices of lemon.
