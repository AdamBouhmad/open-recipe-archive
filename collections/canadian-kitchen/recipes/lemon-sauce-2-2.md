## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. mix sugar and cornstarch
2. add nutmeg
3. add water gradually, stirring constantly
4. boil 5 minutes
5. Add other material
