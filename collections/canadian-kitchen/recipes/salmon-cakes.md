## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 3 eggs
- 8 soda biscuits rolled fine
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- salt and pepper
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Break eggs on salmon
2. Stir thoroughly
3. Fry in butter
