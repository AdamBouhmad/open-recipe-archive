## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- Butter size of egg
- 1 qt. water
- 1 qt. milk
- Salt and pepper
- 1 teaspoonful soda

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes thoroughly in water; have milk scalding in a double boiler to prevent scorching
2. When tomatoes are done, add a large teaspoonful of soda, which will cause a violent effervescence
3. Set vessel in a pan before adding soda, to prevent waste
4. When the commotion has ceased add milk and seasoning
