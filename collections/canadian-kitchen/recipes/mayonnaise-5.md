## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- Yolk of 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Dash of cayenne
- 1 cup olive oil
- three-quarters cup of olive oil
- two tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- one-half cup of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Mix well.
2. Then add drop by drop three-quarters cup of olive oil, beating constantly during mixing.
3. If it grows too thick add two tablespoonfuls of vinegar.
4. If it is to be used immediately add one-half cup of whipped cream.
