## Ingredients

- 1 dozen cucumbers
- 1 dozen onions
- weak brine
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 dessertspoon tumeric
- 1 dessertspoon curry powder

## Directions

1. Pare and slice cucumbers in ¼-inch rings
2. Slice onions in equal quantity
3. Let stand for 24 hours in weak brine
4. Make dressing of 1 pint vinegar, 2 cups brown sugar, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 dessertspoon tumeric, 1 dessertspoon curry powder
5. Dissolve in cold vinegar
6. Heat together until boiling and well blended
7. Put in pickles that have been well drained
8. Allow to come to a scald
9. Bottle hot
