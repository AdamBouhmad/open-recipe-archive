## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 to 3/4 cup ice water

## Directions

1. Wash butter in ice water until soft and waxy
2. Place in napkin and knead it a little to free from moisture
3. Reserve 2 tablespoons butter and shape remainder into a circular piece 1/4 inch thick and keep on ice
4. Work 2 pounds butter into flour and moisten to a dough with ice water
5. Turn on a slightly floured board and knead 1 minute
6. Roll on a napkin and place on ice for 15 minutes
7. Pat and roll into a piece 1/8 inch thick
8. Place butter in centre of lower half and fold upper half of paste over it
9. Fold right side of paste over enclosed butter and left side under
10. Turn half way round, cover and let stand 5 minutes
11. Pat and roll 1/8 inch thick and fold from ends towards centre making 3 layers
12. Cover and let stand 5 minutes
13. Repeat twice, turning paste half-way round each time before rolling
14. Fold from end to centre and double making four layers
15. Put in cold place to chill or fold in a towel
16. Put in dripping pan and place between pans of crushed ice
17. After shaping, chill thoroughly before baking
18. Bake in very hot oven with greatest heat coming from the bottom
19. While rising, decrease the heat as needed by opening check to stove or placing basin of ice water in oven
20. Turn the paste frequently that it may rise evenly
21. Do not open door for first five minutes
22. When risen to full height, slip a pan under sheet on which paste is baking
23. Bake on a tin sheet covered with double thickness of brown paper or in a dripping pan
