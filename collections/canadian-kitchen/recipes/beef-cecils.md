## Ingredients

- 3 cups cold beef
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- a small onion
- a little sage
- pepper
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Chop cold beef
2. Add bread crumbs
3. Add egg
4. Add small onion chopped fine
5. Add a little sage, pepper and salt to taste
6. Add enough milk to moisten the mixture so it will hold together in balls
7. Flatten into balls
8. Fry in drippings or butter
