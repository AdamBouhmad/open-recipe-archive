## Ingredients

- rice
- grated cheese
- milk
- celery salt or table salt

## Directions

1. Boil the quantity of rice desired
2. Put into a baking dish a layer of rice and a layer of grated cheese alternately
3. Add milk as preparing for macaroni or spaghetti
4. Season with celery salt (table salt may be used)
