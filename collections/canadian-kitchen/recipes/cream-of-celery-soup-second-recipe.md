## Ingredients

- Pint of milk
- Flour
- Cornstarch
- A dash of red pepper
- A slice of onion
- A blade of mace
- A head of celery
- Salt
- Lump of butter
- A cupful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Boil together pint of milk thickened with flour and cornstarch, a dash of red pepper, a slice of onion, and a blade of mace
2. Add a head of celery previously cut in pieces, boiled and mashed, in salted water in which boiled
3. When well blended strain
4. Add lump of butter; stir over fire till blended
5. If very special add a cupful of whipped cream after soup is dished in very hot tureen
