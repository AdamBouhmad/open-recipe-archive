## Ingredients

- pieces of dry bread
- fresh buttermilk
- 1 beaten egg
- teaspoon baking soda
- pinch of salt
- flour (wheat) enough to make a batter

## Directions

1. Cover dry bread with fresh buttermilk and let stand until soft. For 1 quart of mixture, add 1 beaten egg, teaspoon baking soda, pinch of salt, and flour enough to make a batter.
