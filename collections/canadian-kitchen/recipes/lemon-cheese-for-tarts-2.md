## Ingredients

- 1 pound white sugar
- rind of 2 lemons
- juice of 3 or 4 small lemons
- 6 eggs (leaving out the whites of 3)
- 6 ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Boil until the thickness of honey
2. Put into sealers and keep in a cool place
