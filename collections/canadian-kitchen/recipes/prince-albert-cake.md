## Ingredients

- 2 eggs beaten together
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup sour milk mixed with 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup seeded raisins
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat eggs together
2. Cream butter and brown sugar
3. Mix sour milk with soda
4. Combine all ingredients
5. Add raisins, flour, nutmeg, and cinnamon
6. Bake in 2 jelly tins
