## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- One-half pound butter
- One-quarter pound sugar, fine granulated

## Directions

1. Put flour on board, add butter and sugar, and knead with the hand until a nice dough is formed.
2. Roll into a round cake any thickness desired and bake in a moderate oven about half an hour, or until it is nicely browned.
3. Cut into shapes while hot.
