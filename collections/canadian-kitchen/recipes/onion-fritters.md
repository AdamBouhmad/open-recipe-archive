## Ingredients

- onion
- whites of two eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 heaping tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Slice onion across and then pull apart.
2. Make a batter — the whites of two eggs, half cup milk; put in slowly two heaping tablespoonfuls of flour and a little salt; mix in onion thoroughly and fry in boiling lard; dry on brown paper.
