## Ingredients

- three slices of bread
- sardines
- two eggs
- one quarter ounce of butter
- one teaspoon Tarragon vinegar
- one quarter teaspoon made mustard
- salt
- Worcester sauce

## Directions

1. Cut three slices of bread a little larger than the size of a sardine
2. Fry a delicate brown on both sides
3. Place a sardine on each and make them hot in the oven
4. Beat up two eggs and mix with a quarter ounce of butter, one teaspoon Tarragon vinegar, quarter teaspoon made mustard, salt to taste, and a little Worcester sauce
5. Put these in a small saucepan and stir over the fire until it thickens (not boils)
6. Pour over the bread and sardines
