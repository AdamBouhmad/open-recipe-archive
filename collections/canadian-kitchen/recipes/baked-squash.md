## Ingredients

- one squash
- two tablespoonfuls butter
- one egg
- one cup milk
- bread-crumbs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Boil and mash the squash
2. Stir in butter, and egg beaten light
3. Add milk, and pepper and salt to taste
4. Fill a buttered pudding-dish with this
5. Strew fine bread-crumbs over the top
6. Bake to a nice brown
