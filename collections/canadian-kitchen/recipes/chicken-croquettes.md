## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1 pt. milk
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- pepper, salt and a dash of cayenne
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil chicken, remove skin and chop fine
2. Cook the sauce and add the chopped chicken
3. Mix well, then set aside to cool
4. When cool mould into shape
5. Dip in egg and bread-crumbs and boil in hot fat
