## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup chopped suet
- 1 cup chopped onions
- cold water
- hot roast beef
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, suet, and onions thoroughly
2. Add enough cold water to mix stiff
3. Steam 3 hours
4. Serve with hot roast beef and brown gravy
