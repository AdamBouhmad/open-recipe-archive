## Ingredients

- large mushrooms
- bread crumbs
- seasoning
- butter
- toast
- cream

## Directions

1. Select the largest mushrooms
2. Chop remainder and mix with crumbs and seasoning
3. Stuff the large ones with this mixture
4. Saute in butter
5. Place piece of toast under it in pan
6. Add some cream and cook
