## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated carrots
- 1 cup grated potatoes
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup suet
- a little salt
- 1 dessertspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix grated carrots, grated potatoes, sugar, flour, currants, raisins, suet, salt, and soda
2. Steam 1 1/2 hours
