## Ingredients

- 1 small chicken or tender meat
- 2 tablespoons lard or drippings
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup broth
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 small cupful raisins
- about 1 cupful olives
- 3 cups cornmeal
- 1 cup flour
- salt
- 3/4 cup lard or drippings
- 1 beaten egg
- 1 cup broth

## Directions

1. Boil until tender one small chicken, or that amount of any tender meat
2. Strain off the broth, remove the bones, and break meat into small pieces
3. Put into a frying-pan 2 tablespoons of lard or drippings, and when hot add 2 tablespoons flour, and let brown a little
4. Then add the meat, salted to taste, with 1 cup broth and 2 tablespoons of chili powder
5. Stir and cook 10 minutes
6. Set off to cool, and add a small cupful raisins (whole) and about the same of olives
7. Make a stiff batter of 3 cups of cornmeal and 1 of flour, salted, into which is rubbed 3/4 of a cup of lard or drippings, moistened with 1 beaten egg and 1 cup broth
8. Line a deep pan with the batter, and bake 1 hour
