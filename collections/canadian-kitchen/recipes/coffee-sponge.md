## Ingredients

- 1½ cups strong cold coffee
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon gelatine soaked for ½ an hour in ¾ cup water
- pinch salt
- yolks of 2 eggs
- ¼ cup sugar
- beaten whites of 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- whipped cream or cream

## Directions

1. Heat coffee, milk, ½ cup sugar, and soaked gelatine in a double boiler
2. When fully heated add ¼ cup sugar, pinch salt, and yolks of eggs
3. Cook until mixture thickens
4. Remove from fire and stir in the beaten whites of the eggs
5. Add vanilla
6. In summer set on ice to mould
7. Serve with whipped cream or cream
