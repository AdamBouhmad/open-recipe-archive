## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup cooked berries
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix 2 eggs, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, 3/4 cup butter, 1 cup cooked berries, 1 1/2 cups flour, and 1 teaspoon soda
2. Bake in loaf
