## Ingredients

- 1 pt. sour milk or buttermilk
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- ½ teaspoonful salt
- ½ cup soft lard
- Flour to make dough

## Directions

1. Take buttermilk and stir in it a rounding teaspoonful of baking soda
2. Pour into the flour bowl where there has been made a hole in the middle of the flour
3. Add salt and lard
4. Mix with the fingers into a soft dough
5. Do not get in too much flour — it must be quite soft
6. Roll out one inch thick and cut
7. Place not too close together on a tin and bake in a very hot oven
