## Ingredients

- 1 pound salt
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 ounce saltpetre
- water
- coarse salt
- beef

## Directions

1. Boil salt, brown sugar, and saltpetre with water
2. Skim and let cool
3. First sprinkle and rub beef with coarse salt
4. Let stand 24 hours
5. Wash off salt
6. Pack in tub or barrel
7. Pour pickle over meat
8. Will be ready to use in a week
9. When ready to cook, simply wash off and put on to cook
