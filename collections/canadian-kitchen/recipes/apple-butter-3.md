## Ingredients

- 5 gallons sweet cider
- 1 peck tart apples
- 2 pounds brown sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples, then quarter, and cut across to shorten the fibre
2. Boil the cider down to about half its original quantity
3. Add the apples and sugar, and continue to stir the mixture well from the bottom
4. Boil until the apples and cider form one solid mass and do not separate
5. Simmer without burning
