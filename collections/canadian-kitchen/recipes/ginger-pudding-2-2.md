## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon butter melted
- 1 egg
- 1 cup flour
- 1 level teaspoon soda
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Put 1 level teaspoon soda in a cup and then half fill cup with boiling water
2. Mix 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup molasses, 1 teaspoon ginger, 1/4 teaspoon cloves, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 tablespoon butter melted, 1 egg, 1 cup flour
3. Stir in soda water quickly
4. Bake in angel cake tin
5. Do not move while baking
6. Serve with sauce
