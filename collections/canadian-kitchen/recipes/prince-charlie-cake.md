## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup sour cream or sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- spice

## Directions

1. Rub flour, white sugar, and butter together like pie paste and keep out 1 cup
2. To the balance add egg, raisins, sour cream or sour milk, soda, and a little spice
3. Pour the batter in the pan
4. Pour the cupful saved out on the top of batter; latter serves as icing
