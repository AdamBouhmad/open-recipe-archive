## Ingredients

- can of tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- sugar
- onion juice
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Season a can of tomatoes with one teaspoonful of salt, and one-quarter teaspoonful of pepper.
2. Spread a shallow baking dish with a thin layer of breadcrumbs.
3. Pour in the tomatoes, sprinkle over them a tablespoonful of sugar, and a few drops of onion juice.
4. Cover the top with a cupful of breadcrumbs which have been moistened with a tablespoonful of melted butter.
5. Bake in a hot oven for fifteen minutes.
6. Serve in the same dish.
