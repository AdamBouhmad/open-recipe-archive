## Ingredients

- large firm tomatoes
- butter
- onion
- chopped meat
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread
- water

## Directions

1. Select large, firm tomatoes; do not remove the skins; cut a small slice off the stem end, and scoop out the inside.
2. Put butter in a saucepan; when hot add onion chopped fine.
3. Let it color slightly; then add meat, chicken, or livers, parsley, breadcrumbs, the pulp taken from the tomatoes, salt, pepper, egg.
4. Stir it over the fire until it is consistent.
5. Dust the inside of the tomatoes with salt and pepper, and fill them, letting the stuffing rise half an inch above the tomato, and place a piece of butter on it.
6. Cut slices of bread one-half inch thick into circles the size of the tomato.
7. Dip them quickly in water, and place in a baking-pan.
8. Place a tomato on each piece of bread, and bake in oven about fifteen minutes, or until the stuffing is browned.
9. A brown sauce may be served with this dish.
10. The meat may be omitted from the stuffing if desired.
11. If convenient it is better to use oil instead of butter with tomatoes.
