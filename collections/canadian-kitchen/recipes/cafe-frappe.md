## Ingredients

- 1 qt. strong coffee
- white of 1 egg
- sugar to taste
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. To coffee sweetened, add the beaten white one egg and freeze.
2. Serve in glasses with whipped cream on top, or with vanilla ice cream.
