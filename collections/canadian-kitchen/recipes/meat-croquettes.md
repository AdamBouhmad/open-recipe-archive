## Ingredients

- 2 cups meat chopped fine
- 1 small onion
- pepper and salt
- white sauce
- beaten egg
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. mix to a good paste with white sauce
2. take tablespoon
3. roll in beaten egg
4. then roll in cracker crumbs and fry in hot fat
