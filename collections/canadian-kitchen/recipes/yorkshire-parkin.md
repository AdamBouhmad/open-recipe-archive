## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 1 pound Scotch oatmeal
- 2 pounds syrup
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound butter
- 1 ounce ginger
- 1 teaspoon carbonate soda
- a little milk if required
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Bake in a buttered tin and cut into squares
