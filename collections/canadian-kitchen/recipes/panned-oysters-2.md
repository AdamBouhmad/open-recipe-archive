## Ingredients

- oysters
- toast
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Clean, place on small pieces of toast in baking pan
2. Season, bake until plump
3. Serve with lemon butter, made with 3 tablespoons butter creamed, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, cayenne
