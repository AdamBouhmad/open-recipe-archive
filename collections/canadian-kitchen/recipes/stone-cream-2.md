## Ingredients

- Preserve, strawberry, raspberry or peach
- Three large tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
- One pint of cream
- Three-quarters of an ounce of isinglass
- Very little warm milk
- Wine
- Loaf sugar
- Blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Put some preserve into a glass dish with three large tablespoonfuls of lemon juice on the top
2. Boil a pint of cream with three-quarters of an ounce of isinglass dissolved in a very little warm milk
3. Add wine and loaf sugar
4. When new milk warm, keep moving it round on the sweetmeats on the dish
5. When quite cold cut some blanched almonds lengthwise and stick them all over it
