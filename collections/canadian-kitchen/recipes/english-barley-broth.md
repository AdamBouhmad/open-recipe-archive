## Ingredients

- One quart of strong stock made by boiling the bones of a rib-roast, or steak well broken, with a pound of underdone beef for six hours
- One pound of chopped lean beef and the cracked cooked bones
- Three pints of water
- One onion
- One carrot cut into dice
- One small turnip cut up small
- Half a cup of barley soaked for an hour
- Minced parsley
- Sweet marjoram
- Pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Parboil the vegetables, drain them and put into the soup-kettle with the barley and the cold stock
2. Bring to a slow boil and keep this up for an hour, before the parsley goes in
