## Ingredients

- chopped parsley
- finely cut white loaf
- butter
- sprig of parsley
- onion-juice

## Directions

1. Butter slices
2. Sprinkle with parsley
3. Pile sandwiches crosswise on plates
4. Decorate with a sprig of parsley in centre of pile
5. The parsley may be tossed in a very little onion-juice if relished
