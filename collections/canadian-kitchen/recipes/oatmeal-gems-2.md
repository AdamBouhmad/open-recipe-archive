## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 1/2 cups granulated oatmeal
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 teaspoon baking soda in 1/2 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. cut into squares
2. when done place 2 together with jelly or other filling between
