## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons soda in 1 cup boiling water
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- yolks of 4 eggs well beaten
- 10 tablespoons boiling water
- 2 cups sugar
- whites of 4 eggs beaten
- 1 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Mix molasses, brown sugar, and melted butter together
2. Add cloves and cinnamon
3. Dissolve soda in 1 cup boiling water and add to mixture
4. Add flour
5. Add yolks of 4 eggs well beaten last
6. Bake in layers
7. For filling, boil 10 tablespoons boiling water and 2 cups sugar until it thickens
8. Pour over the beaten whites of 4 eggs and 1 cup raisins
9. Mix and spread over layers and on top
