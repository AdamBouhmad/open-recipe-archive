## Ingredients

- Well-beaten yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch
- 1 cup cold water
- Juice and rind of 2 lemons
- thin stale cake
- three eggs (the whites)

## Directions

1. Boil together until thick the yolks of 3 eggs, 1 cup white sugar, 1 tablespoonful cornstarch, 1 cup cold water, and juice and rind of 2 lemons
2. Stir well and pour while hot into a pudding dish, lined first with thin stale cake
3. When cooked cover the top with three eggs (the whites) well whisked
4. Brown in the oven
5. Serve cold
