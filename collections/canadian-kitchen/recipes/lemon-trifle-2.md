## Ingredients

- 2 lemons (juice)
- Grated rind of 1 lemon
- 1 cupful sherry
- 1 large cup of sugar
- 1 pt. cream sweetened and whipped stiff
- A little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Strain the lemon juice over the sugar and grated peel
2. Let them lie together two hours before adding the wine and nutmeg
3. Strain again
4. Whip gradually into the frothed cream
5. Serve in jelly glasses
