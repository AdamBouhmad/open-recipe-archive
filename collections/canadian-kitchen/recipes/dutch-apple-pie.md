## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 4 sour apples
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt and rub in butter
2. beat egg, add to milk, stir into the above and put in a deep pie plate
3. core apples, quarter them and partly cube apples in the batter, sprinkle sugar on apples only
4. Bake 15 minutes in a hot oven and serve with lemon sauce
