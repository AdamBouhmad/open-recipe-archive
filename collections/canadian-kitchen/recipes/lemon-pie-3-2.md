## Ingredients

- juice and grated rind of 1 large lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Put the sugar into the yolk and beat
2. Add flour and beat again
3. Add milk and fold in the stiffly beaten whites
4. Be careful not to beat the mixture after the whites are added
5. Bake so the whites rise to the top while baking and form a sort of meringue
