## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 2 eggs
- butter the size of an egg
- milk
- jam or jelly

## Directions

1. Break 2 eggs into a cup
2. Add a piece of melted butter the size of an egg to the eggs
3. Fill the cup with milk
4. Mix with flour, sugar, cream tartar, and soda
5. Bake in 2 large layers
6. Place jam or jelly between layers
