## Ingredients

- 1 level teaspoon of soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 pint of flour
- 1 tablespoon lard
- sour milk

## Directions

1. Sift 1 level teaspoon of soda and 1 teaspoon salt in a pint of flour
2. Rub into flour 1 tablespoon lard
3. Wet with sour milk to a nice batter that will drop from the spoon
4. Have the meat prepared as for meat pie, with it hot in dish when crust is ready
5. Take a piece of white paper, make a tube with paper, place in centre of meat dish and spread batter over top of pie
6. Bake 1/2 hour
