## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- butter size of an egg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup seeded raisins

## Directions

1. Bake in two layers
2. Make filling with 1 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons cream, 2 tablespoons water, 1½ tablespoons butter
