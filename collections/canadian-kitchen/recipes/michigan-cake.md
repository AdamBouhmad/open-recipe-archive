## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sour milk
- 4 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ cup currants
- 1 tablespoon syrup
- ¾ teaspoon allspice or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, butter and sugar together until light
2. Add milk and flour
3. Put baking powder and soda in flour
4. Beat all for five minutes
5. Divide in three parts
6. In one put ¾ cup currants, 1 tablespoon syrup, and ¾ teaspoon allspice or cinnamon for centre layer
