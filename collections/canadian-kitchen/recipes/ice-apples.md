## Ingredients

- 1 dozen large apples
- sugar
- butter
- nutmeg
- cake icing
- cream

## Directions

1. Pare and core 1 dozen large apples
2. Fill with sugar, a little butter and nutmeg
3. Bake until nearly done
4. Let cool
5. Remove to another plate
6. Ice top on sides with cake icing
7. Brown lightly
8. Serve with cream
