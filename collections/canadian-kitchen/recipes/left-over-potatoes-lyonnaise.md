## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- cold boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- brown stock
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook for 5 minutes in 3 tablespoons butter with 1 small onion, cut in slices
2. Add 3 cold boiled potatoes cut in 1/2-inch cubes
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Let stand until well browned underneath
5. Fold and turn on hot plate
6. To this might be added 2 tablespoons brown stock and chopped parsley
