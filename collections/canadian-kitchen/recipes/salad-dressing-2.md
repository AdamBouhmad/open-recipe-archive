## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful brown sugar
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- A little salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat egg, add dry ingredients and melted butter.
2. Mix in gradually half a cup of vinegar and three-quarters of a cup of milk, then boil till thick.
3. Have quite cold before using.
