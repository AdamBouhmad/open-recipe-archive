## Ingredients

- fruit (3 pounds)
- water (about 1 cup)
- sugar (1.5 pounds)
- vinegar (1 quart)
- salt (1 teaspoon)
- spices to taste

## Directions

1. Quarter and core the fruit
2. Boil with about 1 cup of water
3. Rub through a colander
4. Add 1.5 pounds sugar, 1 quart vinegar, and 1 teaspoon salt
5. Add spice to taste and boil till thick
