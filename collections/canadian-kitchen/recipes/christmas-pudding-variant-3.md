## Ingredients

- 1 pint raisins
- 1 pint currants
- 1 pint suet
- 1 pint flour
- 1/2 pint bread crumbs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup sugar
- 5 eggs
- a little candied orange or lemon peel
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Boil 3 hours
3. Eat with wine sauce
