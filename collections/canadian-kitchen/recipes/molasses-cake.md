## Ingredients

- 1 scant cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 4 cups sifted flour
- 1 tablespoonful ginger
- 2 tablespoonfuls soda

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in a little cold water.
2. Bake in moderate oven.
