## Ingredients

- 1 knuckle of veal or chicken or turkey bones
- 2 quarts cold water
- 1/2 cup pearl barley
- 1/2 pint boiling cream
- salt
- pepper
- 15 beef balls

## Directions

1. Boil knuckle of veal or bones in cold water very gently for three hours
2. Skim and strain
3. Wash pearl barley in cold water
4. Cover it with the white broth
5. Cover saucepan and cook very gently until barley is tender
6. Remove one-third of the barley and set aside
7. Rub remaining portion through a sieve
8. Place whole barley grains in saucepan with barley and stock passed through sieve
9. Add boiling cream and season to taste with salt and pepper
10. Throw 15 beef balls into soup
11. Boil up once and serve 3 balls to each person
