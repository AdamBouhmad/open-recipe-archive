## Ingredients

- left-over meat
- onion
- sage
- pepper and salt
- sliced raw potatoes
- gravy or water

## Directions

1. Cut meat in pieces
2. lay in baking dish
3. slice an onion
4. add a sprinkling of sage, pepper and salt to taste
5. fill dish to top with sliced raw potatoes
6. add gravy or water
7. bake in hot oven 1 1/2 hours
