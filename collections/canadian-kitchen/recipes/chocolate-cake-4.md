## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 slice of butter, one inch thick, off a print
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- 5 heaping tablespoonfuls chocolate
- 3 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls milk
- 1 pint cream whipped very stiff
- sugar to taste
- 1 teaspoonful coffee extract
- chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar together
2. add the beaten yolks of eggs
3. then add the above melted mixture
4. then the flour, which must be sifted five times, with the baking powder in it
5. Add alternately with the milk until both are used up
6. Then the vanilla, and lastly the beaten whites of the eggs
7. This can be baked in one good-sized tin as a loaf, or made into three layers, when the following filling is used: One pint cream whipped very stiff, sugar to taste and flavor with one teaspoonful coffee extract
8. Put a layer of chopped walnuts, then the cream, between the layers, and on top
9. Bake in a medium oven
