## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1 lb. brown sugar
- 1/2 lb. blanched almonds
- 1/2 lb. citron, sliced
- 1/2 teaspoonful soda
- 1/2 lb. lemon peel
- 1 teacup molasses
- 1/2 lb. butter
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 cupful milk
- cloves
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. Put soda in last
2. Flour to make it stiff enough not to run
3. Brown the flour on the stove; it must be sifted before using
4. Bake in a very slow oven
