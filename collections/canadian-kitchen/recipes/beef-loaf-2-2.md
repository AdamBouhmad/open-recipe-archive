## Ingredients

- 3 pounds raw beef
- 1/2 pound suet
- 1 pound bread crumbs
- 4 tablespoons sweet cream
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 teaspoons summer savory
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Grind beef and suet
2. Mix with bread crumbs, sweet cream, and eggs
3. Add butter, summer savory, pepper, and salt
4. Work into a loaf, using flour to bind the ingredients together
5. Bake in a pan for 2 hours
6. Baste frequently with melted butter or hot water
