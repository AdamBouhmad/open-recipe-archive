## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- five tablespoons sugar
- yolks of 2 eggs
- a little cold milk
- whites of eggs for icing

## Directions

1. Scald 1 pint milk
2. Wet 2 tablespoons starch with a little cold milk
3. Beat the eggs and sugar till light
4. Stir the whole into the scalding milk
5. Use the whites of eggs for icing
