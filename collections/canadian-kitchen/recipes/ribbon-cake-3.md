## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 4 cups flour (scant)
- 4 eggs
- ⅜ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 4 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 cup currants
- ½ pound citron cut fine
- jelly for spreading

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, add the sugar gradually, beating all the while, then the flavoring
2. Beat the eggs very light, add them and the milk
3. Sift the flour, soda and cream tartar with it, beat quickly
4. Take 3 sheet pans of the same size, and in each of 2 put ⅓ of the mixture and bake
5. To the other ⅓ add 4 teaspoons cinnamon, 1 cup currants, ½ pound citron cut fine, and bake in the remaining pan
6. When done, spread the light cake with a thin layer of jelly while warm
7. Place on this the dark cake and spread with jelly, then other sheet of light cake
8. Press all together by putting a weight on top
