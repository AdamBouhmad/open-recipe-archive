## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 2 large onions
- 2 heads cabbage
- salt
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 4 tablespoons celery seed
- 1 tablespoon ground mace
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop all fine, and sprinkle with salt
2. Stand over night and drain
3. Add brown sugar, horseradish, mustard, celery seed, and ground mace
4. Cover with vinegar and boil slowly 1 hour
