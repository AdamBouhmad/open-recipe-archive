## Ingredients

- 1 cup cream
- 1 spoon flour
- whites of 2 eggs
- 3 spoons vinegar
- 2 spoons salad oil
- 2 spoons powdered sugar
- 1 spoon salt
- 1/2 spoon pepper
- 1 spoon made mustard
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Heat cream almost to boiling
2. Stir in flour previously wet with cold milk
3. Boil 2 minutes, stirring constantly
4. Add sugar and remove from fire
5. When half cold beat in whipped whites of eggs
6. Set aside to cool
7. When quite cold, whip in the oil, pepper, mustard and salt
8. If the salad is ready, add vinegar and pour at once over it
