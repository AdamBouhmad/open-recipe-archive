## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- 5 eggs
- Juice and grated rind of 2 lemons
- 1 lb. butter
- one rolled biscuit
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Place over a slow fire, stirring until dissolved
2. add one rolled biscuit
3. place in a jar for use
4. Make small shells of puff paste and fill with the lemon filling
