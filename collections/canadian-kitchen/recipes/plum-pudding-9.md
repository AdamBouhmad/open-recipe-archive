## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. breadcrumbs
- 1 lb. raisins
- 1 lb. currants
- 1 lb. chopped suet
- 8 eggs (well beaten)
- 2 apples (chopped fine)
- 1/2 lb. mixed peel
- 1/2 lb. brown sugar
- 1 small teaspoonful salt
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 wineglass brandy
- a little milk or molasses

## Directions

1. Boil six hours, or, if some days before, four, and when wanted two hours
