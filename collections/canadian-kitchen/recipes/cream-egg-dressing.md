## Ingredients

- 2 eggs well beaten
- 6 tablespoons cream
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6 teaspoons vinegar
- a small piece of butter
- 1/2 head of cabbage chopped fine
- salt for sprinkling
- 2 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Put on the fire and cook, stirring constantly until quite thick, or cook in a double boiler
2. Have a half head of cabbage chopped fine, sprinkle with salt
3. Add to the dressing when cold 2 tablespoons cream and pour over the cabbage
