## Ingredients

- 4 quarts stoned plums
- 2 1/2 pounds sugar
- 1/4 pound English walnuts
- 1 pound raisins
- 2 oranges

## Directions

1. Boil about 20 minutes
2. Be careful not to scorch
3. Seal in jelly glasses
