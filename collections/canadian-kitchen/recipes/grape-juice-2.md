## Ingredients

- 10 pounds grapes
- water
- 3 pounds white sugar

## Directions

1. Weigh grapes before taking them off the stems
2. Pick off the stems, put in a porcelain kettle
3. Add a little water, and cook until stones and pulp separate
4. Press and strain through thick cloth
5. Return juice to kettle and add 3 pounds white sugar
6. Heat to simmering, bottle hot and seal
