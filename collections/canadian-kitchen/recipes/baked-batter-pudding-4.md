## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pints milk
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 ounces butter
- 4 eggs
- a little salt
- sweet sauce or stewed fruit
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with a little cold milk
2. Make the rest hot (but not too hot)
3. Pour it on the flour
4. Keep the mixture well stirred
5. Add butter, eggs and salt
6. Beat the whole well and put the pudding into a buttered pie dish
7. Bake 3/4 hour
8. Serve with a sweet sauce or stewed fruit, or add sugar to taste before cooking and serve with cream
