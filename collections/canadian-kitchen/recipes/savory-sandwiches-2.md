## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped chicken
- 1 slice boiled ham, minced
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1/4 teaspoonful mace
- A few drops onion juice
- A few spoonfuls oyster juice
- Graham bread slices

## Directions

1. Mix chicken, boiled ham (minced), butter, mace, and onion-juice into a soft paste with a few spoonfuls of oyster-liquor
2. Set in a saucepan of boiling water and stir until smoking hot
3. Set aside to get cold
4. Spread between thin slices of Graham bread
