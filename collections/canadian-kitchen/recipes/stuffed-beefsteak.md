## Ingredients

- flank or round steak
- pepper
- salt
- dressing
- twine
- water

## Directions

1. Pound steak well
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Spread with a nice dressing
4. Pull out and tie closely with twine
5. Put in a kettle a quart of boiling water
6. Boil slowly 1 hour
7. Take out and place in a dripping pan, adding water in which it was boiled
8. Baste until a nice brown
9. Make gravy of the drippings
10. Slice and serve
