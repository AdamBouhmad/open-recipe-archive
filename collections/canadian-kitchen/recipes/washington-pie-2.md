## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- butter the size of an egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup flour
- strawberry or raspberry jam
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a stiff batter using sugar, egg, milk, butter, baking powder, and flour as for cake
2. Bake in jelly pan
3. Slip it off and spread with strawberry or raspberry jam
4. Spread the whites of 2 eggs beaten to a stiff froth with a little sugar over the jam
5. Put in oven till set
6. Eat with or without cream while hot
7. Can also be cut when cold and used for a cake
