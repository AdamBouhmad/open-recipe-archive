## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Knox's gelatine (slightly rounded)
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- speck salt
- 8 tablespoons cold water
- 3/4 cup water
- flavoring to taste

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in 8 tablespoons cold water
2. Heat the sugar with 3/4 cup of water until dissolved
3. Add gelatine to syrup and just bring it to the boil
4. Take off the stove and let it stand in a bowl until partially cool
5. Add salt and flavoring
6. Beat with a whip until soft, then with a large spoon, until only soft enough to settle into a sheet
7. Dust granite pans thickly with fine powdered sugar
8. Pour in the candy about 1/2 inch deep, and set to cool until it will not come off on the finger
9. Turn out on powdered paper
10. Cut in cubes and roll in sugar
