## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oats
- 1 quart hot water
- ⅜ cup molasses
- salt
- yeast cake
- 1 quart flour

## Directions

1. Put rolled oats to soak in 1 quart hot water at noon
2. Let stand until night
3. Add ⅜ cup molasses, salt, yeast cake, 1 quart flour
4. This makes 2 loaves
