## Ingredients

- two pounds almonds
- three tablespoonfuls best olive oil
- one tablespoonful fine salt

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds
2. pour oil over them, adding salt
3. Let them stand for an hour or two, stirring frequently
4. Place in a pan large enough for the almonds to rest on the surface, not being crowded one on another
5. Place in an oven sufficiently slow to allow twenty minutes for the nuts to brown nicely
6. shake the pan frequently that they may color evenly
