## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon lard
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup dark molasses
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 tablespoons ginger
- 1 large teaspoon baking soda
- 2 good cups flour

## Directions

1. Stir lard and butter with brown sugar
2. Add dark molasses and egg yolk
3. Beat well
4. Add boiling water
5. Add ginger and baking soda
6. Add flour
