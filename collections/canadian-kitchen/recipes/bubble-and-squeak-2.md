## Ingredients

- cold roast or boiled beef
- pepper
- salt
- cabbage
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut cold roast or boiled beef into pieces convenient for frying
2. Add pepper and salt and fry them
3. Boil and chop cabbage small
4. Fry cabbage with a little pepper and salt
5. Keep stirring so all may be equally done
6. Sprinkle over cabbage when taken from the fire a very little vinegar
7. Place cabbage in centre of dish and arrange meat neatly round it
