## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiled rice
- 1 cup finely chopped left-over meat (beef)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. put milk on to boil
2. add meat, rice and seasoning
3. when boiling add beaten egg
