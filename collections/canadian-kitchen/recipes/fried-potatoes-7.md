## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- butter
- flour
- raw potatoes
- hot fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Cold boiled potatoes are sliced, then put into a saute-pan with butter, and cooked until browned on both sides.
2. If rolled in flour they will form a crisp crust.
3. Raw potatoes are sliced or cut into any shape, and put into cold water for half an hour.
4. Dry them well on a napkin, and immerse in hot fat until done.
5. Too many must not be put in the basket at once, as it cools the fat.
6. Fry them to an amber color; then drain, and place them on a paper in the oven until all are done.
7. Serve them at once, as they lose their crispness if kept.
