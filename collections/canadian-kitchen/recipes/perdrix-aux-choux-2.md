## Ingredients

- partridge
- turkey dressing
- pork fat
- lard
- pepper
- salt
- four onions
- cloves
- one small cabbage
- four slices of pork and bacon
- hot water
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Stuff partridge with turkey dressing
2. Stick on breast pieces of pork fat
3. Put lard down in pot and brown the partridge alone with pepper and salt
4. Roast well for twenty minutes in a covered pot
5. Take out the partridge and put in the pot four onions stuck with cloves, one small cabbage cut in four, four slices of pork and bacon; fry brown
6. Put back the partridge and cover with hot water
7. Simmer gently for an hour
8. Before taking off put in a tablespoonful of browned flour
