## Ingredients

- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1 large sour apple
- sugar to sweeten
- milk to moisten
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop raisins and apples fine
2. Combine with bread crumbs
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
4. Sweeten with sugar and moisten with milk
