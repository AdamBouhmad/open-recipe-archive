## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat 3 eggs and 1 cup sugar together to a stiff froth
2. Add 2 tablespoons cold water
3. Mix 2 teaspoons baking powder with 1 1/4 cups flour
4. Bake in 2 layers
