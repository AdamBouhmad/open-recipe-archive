## Ingredients

- cucumbers (100 pickles)
- 1 cup salt
- 1 cup mustard
- 2 cups sugar
- green peppers
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe pickles dry
2. Take salt, mustard, sugar and some green peppers
3. Mix all together
4. Put the vinegar in this mixture
5. Pour it over the pickles cold
6. Use enough vinegar to cover pickles
