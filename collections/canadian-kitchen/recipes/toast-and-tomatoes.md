## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- butter
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 pint milk
- a lump of soda
- 0.5 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Toast and butter some thin slices of bread
2. Place in a deep dish
3. Take 1 can tomatoes, put in a porcelain dish and heat
4. Add 1 pint milk, a lump of soda, 0.5 teaspoon salt
5. When hot pour over toast
6. Serve at once
