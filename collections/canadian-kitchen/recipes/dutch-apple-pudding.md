## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 4 sour apples, cored and cut into eighths

## Directions

1. Mix flour, salt and baking powder
2. Rub in the butter
3. Beat the eggs and mix with the milk
4. Stir with the dry mixture
5. Turn the mixture into a shallow pan
6. Press the sharp apples into the dough slightly
7. Bake in a quick oven 20 or 30 minutes
