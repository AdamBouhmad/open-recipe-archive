## Ingredients

- fresh pork
- fat
- salt
- sage
- pepper

## Directions

1. Use 1/2 fat to 3/4 lean fresh pork
2. To each pound of meat add 1 even tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon sifted sage, a scant 3/4 teaspoon pepper
3. Cut in thin strips and put through sausage machine or meat chopper
4. Add the seasoning and mix thoroughly
