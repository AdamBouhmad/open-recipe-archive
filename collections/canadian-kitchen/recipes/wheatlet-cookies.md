## Ingredients

- 2 cups wheatlets
- 7½ cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup lard
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ cup sweet milk
- dates optional

## Directions

1. Rub wheatlets, flour, baking powder, brown sugar, lard and butter all together
2. Add sweet milk
3. Mix into dough and roll out thin
4. Optional: place dates between cookies
5. Bake
