## Ingredients

- fresh tomatoes
- boiling water
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- butter
- cornstarch
- cold water
- cracker or breadcrumbs
- chopped onions

## Directions

1. If fresh tomatoes are used remove the skins by placing them in boiling water a few minutes; they will then peel off easily.
2. Cut them in pieces, and stew in a granite-ware saucepan until tender.
3. To one quart of tomatoes add one teaspoonful each of salt and sugar, one-quarter teaspoonful of pepper, and a tablespoonful of butter.
4. Thicken with a teaspoonful of cornstarch wet in cold water, or with one-half cupful of cracker or breadcrumbs.
5. Chopped onions are relished in this dish and should be well cooked.
