## Ingredients

- 20 clams
- 2 slices salt pork
- A little white pepper
- Slices of Graham toast

## Directions

1. Fry the pork or bacon crisp in the blazer, and when the dice begin to brown push them to the side of the pan and lay in the clams
2. Saute them, turning once or twice, and serve on Graham or Boston brown bread toast
