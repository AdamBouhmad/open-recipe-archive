## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sweet cream
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 3 1/2 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in a double boiler and stir until thick
2. When cold, or when you want to use it, add 3 1/2 cup vinegar, and serve on chopped cabbage
