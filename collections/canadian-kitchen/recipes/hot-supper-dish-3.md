## Ingredients

- vegetable bits
- a small onion
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- loaf bread
- milk
- butter
- egg
- water

## Directions

1. Save all the bits left in the vegetable dishes at noon
2. Mince finely
3. Add a small onion if liked
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Moisten with cream
6. Put in a hot oven and serve as soon as browned
7. If there are not enough vegetables, crumb up a piece of loaf and add
8. If cream is not obtainable, use milk with a few bits of butter scattered over the top of the dish, or take 1 egg, beat until light, add 2 tablespoons water and pour over the mixture, using the bits of butter as with the milk
