## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- one tablespoonful of flour
- half a teaspoonful of baking powder
- one tablespoonful of grated cheese
- four eggs
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- salt
- pepper (cayenne)

## Directions

1. Half cook in the spider, then spread on the whites
2. Cook on the top of the stove for about ten minutes
3. Put in the oven to brown
