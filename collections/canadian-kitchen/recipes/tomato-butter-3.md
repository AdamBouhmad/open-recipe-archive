## Ingredients

- 9 pounds ripe tomatoes peeled
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Boil all together for 3 hours
