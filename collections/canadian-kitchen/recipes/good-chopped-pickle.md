## Ingredients

- 12 onions
- 12 green tomatoes
- 12 apples, all uniform size
- vinegar
- 3 cups granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard

## Directions

1. Chop onions, green tomatoes, and apples
2. Cover with vinegar
3. Add sugar and mustard
