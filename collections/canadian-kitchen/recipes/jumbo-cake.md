## Ingredients

- 2 cups bread dough
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in water
- 1 pound raisins well flavored

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add beaten yolks of eggs, then spices
3. Mix with bread dough
4. Add raisins and well-beaten whites of eggs, then soda
5. Bake in a slow oven 1 1/2 hours
