## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 cup chopped and seeded raisins

## Directions

1. Let 1 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup sweet milk, and 1 teaspoon butter boil 5 minutes
2. Add 1 cup chopped and seeded raisins
3. Boil a few minutes longer
4. Stir until cool
