## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup barley
- 4 quarts cold water
- neck of mutton
- 2 carrots
- 1 large onion
- small head cabbage
- 1 bunch parsley
- 1 sprig celery top
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring barley and cold water to the boil and skim
2. Put in a neck of mutton and boil 1 hour
3. Skim well the sides of the pot
4. Chop carrots, onion, cabbage, parsley, and celery top fine
5. Add your chopped vegetables, pepper and salt to taste
6. Cook for two hours total
