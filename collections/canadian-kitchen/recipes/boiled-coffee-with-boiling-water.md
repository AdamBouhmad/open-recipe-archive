## Ingredients

- 2 ounces coffee
- 1 pint water
- 1 raw egg
- 4 tablespoons cold water

## Directions

1. Beat dry coffee well into 1 raw egg mixed with 4 tablespoons of cold water
2. Add boiling water
3. Boil 3 minutes
4. Place where it will keep hot for 10 minutes
5. Serve
