## Ingredients

- chicken
- stuffing
- salt
- pepper
- salt pork
- water
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- giblet sauce

## Directions

1. Truss the chicken as directed, or use skewers, doubling the cord across the back and around the ends of the skewers to hold them in place.
2. Dredge the chicken with salt and pepper, and place it on slices of salt pork in a baking-pan.
3. Add a very little water.
4. Bake in hot oven, allowing fifteen minutes to the pound.
5. Baste frequently.
6. Fifteen minutes before it is done, rub it over the top and sides with butter, dredge it with flour, and replace it in the oven until it becomes a golden brown and looks crisp.
7. Draw out the trussing cords, and garnish with parsley.
8. Serve with it a giblet sauce.
