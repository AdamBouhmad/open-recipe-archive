## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated potatoes
- 1 cup grated carrots
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup yellow sugar
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients
2. Place in a mould
3. Steam 4 hours
