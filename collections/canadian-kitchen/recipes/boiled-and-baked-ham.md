## Ingredients

- ham
- raw egg white
- sugar
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil your ham tender
2. Cover it with the white of a raw egg and sprinkle sugar or bread crumbs over it
3. Put it in the oven and brown
4. It is also delicious covered with a regular cake icing and browned
