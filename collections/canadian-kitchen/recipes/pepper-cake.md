## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup syrup
- 3/4 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 3/4 nutmeg
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
