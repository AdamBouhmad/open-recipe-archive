## Ingredients

- meat of another fowl or veal, or a mixture of both
- 1 cup crumbs
- some chopped ham or tongue
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- stock

## Directions

1. Chop meat fine and add to the mince meat 1 cup crumbs and if convenient, some chopped ham or tongue
2. Season with 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, 1 teaspoon onion juice, 1 teaspoon salt, 3/4 teaspoon pepper, 1 teaspoon thyme
3. Moisten with stock if veal is used; take it from knuckle, and use the bone in braizing pot, as it will leave a good jelly
4. In stuffing, be careful that too much is not used, as allowance must be made for the swelling
