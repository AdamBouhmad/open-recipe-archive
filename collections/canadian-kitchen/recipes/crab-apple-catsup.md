## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. apples (which have been cored)
- 1½ lbs. sugar
- 1 qt. vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful cloves
- 1 tablespoonful cinnamon
- 1 tablespoonful pepper
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Scald the fruit, run it through the sieve
2. Mix all ingredients together
3. Boil until it is almost as thick as jam
