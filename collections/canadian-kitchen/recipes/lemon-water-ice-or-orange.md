## Ingredients

- Four large juicy lemons
- One quart water
- One orange
- One and one-quarter pounds sugar
- White of one egg
- One tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Put sugar and water on to boil
2. Chip yellow rind from three lemons and the orange
3. Add to the syrup
4. Boil five minutes and stand to cool
5. Squeeze juice from lemons and orange
6. Add to cold syrup
7. Strain and freeze
8. Just before removing, add white of one egg beaten very stiff with one tablespoon sugar, to make white and frothy
