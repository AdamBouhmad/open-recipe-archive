## Ingredients

- 4 eggs beaten separate
- 2 even tablespoons mustard
- 4 even tablespoons white sugar
- 4 even tablespoons butter
- ½ cup vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- dash cayenne pepper
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. mix butter, sugar, mustard, salt and pepper well
2. add beaten yolks of eggs
3. stir in vinegar, beat it well
4. cook in double boiler until it becomes soft like custard
5. when done add the white of eggs well beaten
6. when ready for use add cream or milk
