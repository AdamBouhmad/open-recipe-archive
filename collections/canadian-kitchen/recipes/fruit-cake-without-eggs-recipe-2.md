## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups sour milk
- 4 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon soda
- 1/2 cup strong coffee
- 2 pounds currants
- 2 pounds raisins
- 1/2 pound mixed peel
- 1 pound dates
- 1/4 pound almonds
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Combine 2 cups brown sugar, 2 cups sour milk, 4 cups flour, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon soda, 1/2 cup strong coffee, 2 pounds currants, 2 pounds raisins, 1/2 pound mixed peel, 1 pound dates, 1/4 pound almonds, 1 tablespoon vanilla, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon cloves, 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg and 1 teaspoon salt.
2. This makes 2 good loaves.
