## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 pt. cream
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 1 glass rye whisky
- rind of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Melt sugar to a light brown
2. Line a mould with it by putting some in and turning the mould till cool
3. Sweeten cream to taste and put in the grated rind of one-half a lemon
4. Put all into a double boiler
5. When it comes to a boil pour into a basin and let cool
6. Add the yolks of eggs and whisky
7. Stir all together and strain into the prepared mould
8. Tie kitchen paper over mould and steam three-quarters of an hour
9. Serve with whipped cream
