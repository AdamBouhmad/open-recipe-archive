## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons tapioca
- cold water
- 1 quart milk
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons cocoanut
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak 4 tablespoons tapioca 4 hours in cold water
2. Pour off water and stir tapioca into 1 quart milk
3. Boil together 10 minutes
4. Add yolks of 3 eggs, 1 cup sugar, and 3 tablespoons cocoanut
5. Cook 5 minutes longer
6. Use whites of eggs for meringue for top
