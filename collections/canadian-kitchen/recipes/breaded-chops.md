## Ingredients

- chops
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- cracker-dust
- boiling fat

## Directions

1. Trim and flatten, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and then in cracker-dust, and fry to a fine brown in deep boiling fat.
2. Drain and serve dry and hot.
