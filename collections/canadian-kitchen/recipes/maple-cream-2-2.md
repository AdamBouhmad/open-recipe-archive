## Ingredients

- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 pound almonds, blanched and cut in pieces
- 3/4 pint sweet cream

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and cream together until, when dropped into a little cold water, it forms into a firm ball
2. Add nuts
3. Beat until cold, or firm enough to spread on buttered tins
