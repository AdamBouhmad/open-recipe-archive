## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 large teaspoon baking powder
- butter size of an egg
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 4 apples
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups flour, 1 large teaspoon baking powder, and butter size of an egg well
2. Beat 1 egg light and mix with 3/4 cup milk, pour into dry mixture and stir
3. Spread 3 1/2 inch thick in baking pan
4. Peel 4 apples, cut and stick into dough and sprinkle with sugar
5. Bake
6. Serve with cream
