## Ingredients

- 1 can of tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- small onion
- pepper
- salt
- teaspoonful of sugar
- 3 teaspoons soft butter
- 3 teaspoons flour
- 1.5 pints of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Turn tomatoes into a saucepan
2. Add butter and chopped onion
3. Let boil 3/4 hour
4. Strain through a coarse sieve
5. Return to the saucepan
6. Season with pepper, salt and sugar
7. Rub soft butter with flour and add to the puree
8. Let cook about 3 minutes
9. Pour into the soup tureen
10. Add boiling milk
11. Stir well and serve hot
