## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound suet
- 6 ounces bread crumbs
- 1 cup flour
- 1/4 pound dates
- 2 ounces sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Stone and chop dates
2. Mix bread crumbs, flour and sugar, add dates
3. Beat eggs well and add to milk
4. Mix all well for 10 minutes
5. Put into a mould and steam for 3 hours
6. Serve with sweet sauce
