## Ingredients

- sweet peppers
- force-meat of tongue, chicken, ham, or veal
- boiled rice
- salt
- onion-juice
- butter
- weak stock
- brown roux

## Directions

1. Make an incision in one side, and extract the seeds through this with a bit of stick
2. Stuff with a force-meat of tongue, chicken, ham, or veal, mixed up with boiled rice, and seasoned with salt, a dash of onion-juice, and a little butter
3. Sew up the peppers with a few stitches
4. Pack them into a bake-dish
5. Pour in enough weak stock to keep them from burning
6. Cover and bake in a moderate oven for an hour
7. Dish, withdrawing the strings
8. Keep hot while you add to the gravy in the dish a tablespoonful of brown roux
9. Boil up once and pour over the peppers
10. Should the gravy have boiled away too much, put in a little boiling water to thin the roux
