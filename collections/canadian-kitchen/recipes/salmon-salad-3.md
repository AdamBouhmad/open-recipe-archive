## Ingredients

- salmon
- chopped celery
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- salt
- 1 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick the salmon to pieces
2. Add twice as much chopped celery as fish
3. Mix well
4. For dressing: mix eggs, sugar, mustard, salt and vinegar
5. Boil until it thickens
6. Combine with salmon and celery
