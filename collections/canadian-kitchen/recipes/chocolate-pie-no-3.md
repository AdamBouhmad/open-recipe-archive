## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Cowan's Perfection Chocolate (grated)
- 1 cup sweet milk
- yolks of 3 eggs
- ¾ cup sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Let 2 tablespoons chocolate and 1 cup sweet milk come to a boil
2. When cold add yolks of 3 eggs
3. Add ¾ cup sugar
4. Beat to a cream
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Bake with under crust
7. Save whites of eggs for icing
