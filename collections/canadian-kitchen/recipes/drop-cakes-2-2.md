## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 pound raisins or dates
- a little salt
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop in teaspoonfuls on greased paper
3. Bake
