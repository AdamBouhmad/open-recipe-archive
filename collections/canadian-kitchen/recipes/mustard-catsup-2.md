## Ingredients

- 1 gallon tomato juice
- 1 pound cooking sugar
- 1/2 pound mustard
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Boil 30 minutes
