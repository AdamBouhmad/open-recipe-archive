## Ingredients

- 1 cup sultanas
- 1 cup thick sour cream
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon allspice
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Heat to thicken
3. Cool and put in shell
4. Make meringue
