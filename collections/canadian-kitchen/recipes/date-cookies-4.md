## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- dates

## Directions

1. Mix rolled oatmeal, flour, brown sugar, butter, cold water and soda
2. Spread dates between layers
3. Bake like cookies
