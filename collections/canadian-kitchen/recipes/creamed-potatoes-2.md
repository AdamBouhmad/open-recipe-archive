## Ingredients

- baked potatoes
- cream or rich milk
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Slice baked potatoes
2. Heat sufficient cream or rich milk to moisten the potatoes well
3. Add salt, pepper and parsley
4. Let cream boil up and put in the potatoes
5. Leave them until well heated and serve at once
