## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 cup dark sugar
- 1 large cup flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful each of cloves, allspice and cinnamon
- 2 tablespoonfuls molasses
- 0.5 cup sour milk

## Directions

1. Bake in two layers
2. Ice and fill with icing made by boiling sugar and water
