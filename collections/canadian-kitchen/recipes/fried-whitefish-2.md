## Ingredients

- Whitefish
- Flour
- Very hot dripping

## Directions

1. Scale and draw the fish
2. Wash in cold water, cut off the head and fins
3. Split down both sides of the back
4. Cut the lengths into sections of the desired size
5. Roll in flour, and cook in plenty of very hot dripping
6. Cook until well browned on both sides
