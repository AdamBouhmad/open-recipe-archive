## Ingredients

- Slices of undercooked roast beef
- breadcrumbs
- sweet herbs
- pepper and salt
- gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the slices of meat very thin, spread upon each slice a stuffing made from the above ingredients, roll up tightly, and tie with string
2. Have ready in a saucepan some good brown gravy, lay in the olives, and let them simmer for about half an hour
3. Take up, remove the string carefully that the shape may not be spoiled, pour the gravy over, and serve hot
