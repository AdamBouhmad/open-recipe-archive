## Ingredients

- 1 ox tail
- 2 lbs. lean beef
- 4 carrots
- 3 onions
- Thyme
- 4 qts. cold water
- Butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of browned flour

## Directions

1. Cut the tail into several pieces and fry brown in butter
2. Slice the onions and carrots, and when you remove the ox-tail from the frying-pan, put in these and brown also
3. When done tie them in a bag with a bunch of thyme and drop into a soup pot
4. Lay the pieces of ox-tail in the same, then the meat cut into small slices
5. Grate over them the two whole carrots, and add four quarts of cold water with pepper and salt
6. Boil four to six hours, in proportion to the size of the tail
7. Strain fifteen minutes before serving, and thicken with two tablespoonfuls of browned flour
8. Boil ten minutes longer
