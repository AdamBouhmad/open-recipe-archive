## Ingredients

- potatoes
- hot fat

## Directions

1. To make balls use a potato scoop; press it well into the potato before turning it.
2. To make straws cut the potato into slices lengthwise, and then into strips, making each one about one-eighth of an inch thick.
3. Slices or strips cut with a fluted knife are good forms for fried potatoes.
4. Fry the potatoes in hot fat, using a basket.
5. Fancy fried potatoes are used to garnish any broiled meat dish.
