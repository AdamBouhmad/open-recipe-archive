## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, whites
- 1 heaping cup cocoanut
- 0.75 cup sugar, large
- 1 dessertspoonful cornstarch
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Stir into sugar one dessertspoon cornstarch
2. Stir this into whites of eggs beaten very stiff
3. Set over steam of kettle and stir till sugary around edges, about twenty minutes
4. Take off and stir in one heaping cup shredded cocoanut
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Drop on buttered paper
7. Bake in rather quick oven
