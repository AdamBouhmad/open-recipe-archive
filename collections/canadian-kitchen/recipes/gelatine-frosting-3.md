## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon gelatine
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 2 tablespoons hot water
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- lemon to taste

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water until soft
2. Add hot water until entirely dissolved
3. Add 1 cup powdered sugar
4. Beat while yet warm until white and light
5. Add lemon to taste
6. This frosts 1 sheet of cake
