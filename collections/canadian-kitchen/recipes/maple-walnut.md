## Ingredients

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup walnuts, broken in pieces
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat sugar, maple syrup, milk, and butter and let boil until a soft ball may be made when mixture is tried in water
2. Remove from fire and beat until creamy
3. Add walnuts broken in pieces with a little salt
