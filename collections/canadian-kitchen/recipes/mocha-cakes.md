## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Whites 4 eggs
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful water and vanilla
- 1 lb. almonds, chopped, or peanuts

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar, beating all the time, slowly add the milk, then the flour and baking powder sifted together, gently fold in well-beaten whites.
2. Bake in a long pan or in two square layer-cake tins.
3. Beat sugar, butter, water with vanilla; if not soft enough add more water.
4. When cake is cold cut in pieces about an inch square, which hold on a two-pronged fork, ice on five sides and dip in a bowl of finely rolled peanuts or chopped parched almonds, previously prepared.
