## Ingredients

- juice and rind of 1 orange
- 1 cup sugar
- yolk of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teacup milk
- paste
- 4 tablespoons pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Line the dish with paste
2. Pour in the custards and bake till done
3. Beat the whites of eggs to a stiff froth
4. Add 4 tablespoons of pulverized sugar
5. Spread it on the pie and brown lightly
