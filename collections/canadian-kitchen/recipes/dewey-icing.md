## Ingredients

- One cup icing sugar
- Butter the size of a walnut
- White of an egg
- One teaspoonful of vanilla
- One teaspoonful of lemon

## Directions

1. Mix butter and sugar, and add the egg; then the other ingredients, and spread on the cake when cold.
