## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare potatoes, put on to cook with barely enough water to cover
2. Cut up one large or 2 small onions, put in pot with potatoes
3. Let cook until water is all cooked up and potatoes and onions are soft
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, 1 tablespoon butter
5. Beat thoroughly
