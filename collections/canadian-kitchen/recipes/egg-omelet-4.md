## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup cracker crumbs
- 3 eggs
- salt
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak the cracker crumbs in the milk.
2. Beat 3 eggs to a froth.
3. Stir eggs into crackers and add a little salt.
4. Turn all into the hot frying-pan that contains a little melted butter.
5. When the omelet is browned on one side turn and brown the other.
