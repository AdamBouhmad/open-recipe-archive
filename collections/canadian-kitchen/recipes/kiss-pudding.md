## Ingredients

- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
- a little cold milk
- 2 cups boiling milk
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of 3 eggs with 1/2 cup sugar until light.
2. Add 1 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch, wet with a little cold milk.
3. Stir in 2 cups boiling milk until thick, then let cool.
4. Beat the whites of 3 eggs with 1 cup sugar.
5. Spread over the top and brown in oven.
