## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 2 large white cabbages
- 1 dozen large onions
- salt
- 1 teaspoon tumeric
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 1 tablespoon cayenne
- 1 tablespoon red pepper
- ½ pound mustard
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop green tomatoes, white cabbages, and large onions all fine
2. Sprinkle with salt
3. Hang up in a sack over night
4. In the morning put in a large granite kettle or boiler
5. Add 1 teaspoon each of tumeric and celery seed, 1 tablespoon each of cloves, cinnamon, allspice, cayenne and red pepper, ½ pound mustard, 2 pounds brown sugar
6. Almost cover with vinegar
7. Boil 2½ hours
8. Do not put in brown sugar until nearly done to prevent burning
