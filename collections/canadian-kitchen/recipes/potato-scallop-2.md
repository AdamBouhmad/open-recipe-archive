## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onions
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Grease a deep pudding dish
2. Place in the bottom a layer of potatoes, peeled, and sliced very thin
3. Next, a layer of thin sliced onions
4. Then a layer of bread crumbs
5. Sprinkle some pepper over this and a little pinch of salt
6. Next, layer of potatoes, and repeat until dish is full, having the potatoes on top
7. Cover with sweet milk
8. Bake in a hot oven for an hour, or until the potatoes can be pierced with a fork
