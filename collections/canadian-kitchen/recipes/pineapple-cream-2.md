## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- a large cup of sugar
- one-half box gelatine
- 1 cup of milk
- a tiny pinch of salt
- 1 pineapple (or 1 can of pineapple)

## Directions

1. Whip one pint of cream to a stiff froth.
2. Add a large cup of sugar.
3. Add one-half box gelatine soaked in a cup of milk for one hour.
4. Add a tiny pinch of salt.
5. Scrape one pineapple with a silver fork until it is in fine shreds, and add it to the cream (or one can of pineapple).
6. Set mould on ice to stiffen.
