## Ingredients

- A round of beef
- 1/4 lb. of saltpetre, powdered
- 1/2 lb. of common salt
- 1 oz. of black pepper
- 1/2 lb. of brown sugar
- 1 oz. of allspice
- A little cayenne
- Beef suet
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. Bone and bind the beef tight
2. If large rub into it a quarter of a pound of saltpetre, powdered
3. Let it stand a day
4. Season it with half a pound of common salt, one ounce of black pepper, half a pound of brown sugar and an ounce of allspice
5. Add a little cayenne
6. Let it remain in the pickle a fortnight, turning it every day (and about three times a week add a small quantity of common salt)
7. Wash off the salt and spice and put in a granite or tin dishpan deep enough to cover the beef entirely
8. Lay some beef suet at the bottom and a great deal at the top
9. Put in a pint of water and cover it with a thick crust
10. Bake seven or eight hours
11. When it comes out of the oven pour off the gravy
12. Do not cut it till cold
