## Ingredients

- 3.5 lbs. beef or veal chopped fine
- 3 slices salt pork chopped fine
- 3 raw eggs
- 1 teaspoonful salt and pepper
- 1 nutmeg
- A piece of butter size of butternut
- 6 crackers rolled fine

## Directions

1. Mix all with flour into a deep loaf, sprinkle with bread crumbs and small pieces of butter.
2. Bake two hours in meat-pan with a little water.
3. Baste while baking.
