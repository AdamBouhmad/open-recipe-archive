## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup molasses
- ½ cup cold coffee
- 1 egg
- ½ cup butter
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 cup raisins
- a little citron or lemon peel
- 2¾ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar

## Directions

1. Bake in layers and ice
