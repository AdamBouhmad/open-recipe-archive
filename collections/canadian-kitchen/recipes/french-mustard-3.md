## Ingredients

- 1 good-sized onion
- cold vinegar
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- mustard

## Directions

1. Slice up a good-sized onion
2. Cover it with cold vinegar
3. After it has stood for an hour, pour off the vinegar
4. Add a little cayenne pepper and salt
5. Add 1 teaspoon sugar
6. Add mustard enough to thicken
7. Mix and set on the stove
8. Stir until it boils
