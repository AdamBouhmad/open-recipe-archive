## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup of butter and lard mixed
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 2 cups bran
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Bake in muffin tins
