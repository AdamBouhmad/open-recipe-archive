## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ¾ cup sour milk
- 1½ cups flour
- ¾ cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Bake 30 minutes in pan
