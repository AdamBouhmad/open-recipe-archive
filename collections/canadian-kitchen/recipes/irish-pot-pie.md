## Ingredients

- 4 pounds fresh beef
- 4 carrots
- 1 turnip
- 1 dozen potatoes
- 2 onions
- 2 quarts flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- milk or cold water
- salt
- pepper
- summer savory

## Directions

1. Make dough with 2 quarts flour, 2 teaspoons cream tartar, 1 teaspoon soda, mixed with milk or cold water as for biscuit with no lard
2. Roll about 1 inch thick and cut into 4-inch squares
3. Cut meat into small pieces
4. Put in a layer of beef in bottom of pot
5. Add alternating layers of carrots, turnip, potatoes, dough and meat
6. Add salt, pepper, and a little summer savory
7. Add water until you see it through the vegetables
8. Cook slowly 3 hours
