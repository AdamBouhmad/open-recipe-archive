## Ingredients

- 3 cups sifted flour
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- ½ teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 3 teaspoonfuls cold butter
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. To sifted flour, add baking-powder, and salt; then sift again.
2. Mix with cold butter (cut in dice), and milk, add the flour to the mixture, handling as little as possible.
3. Cut into three portions, make with a knife a + and bake twenty minutes.
4. On removing from oven rub over with a little butter.
