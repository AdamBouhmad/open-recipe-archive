## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- enough water to dissolve it
- 1 cup stoned and chopped raisins
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Put 1 cup white sugar into a basin with enough water to dissolve it
2. Let boil till it will harden in cold water
3. Have 1 cup stoned and chopped raisins ready
4. Beat the white of 1 egg to a stiff froth
5. Mix with the raisins into boiling sugar
6. Stir briskly
7. While warm put between and on top of cake
