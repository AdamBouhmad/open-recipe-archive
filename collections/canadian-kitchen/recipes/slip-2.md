## Ingredients

- milk
- prepared rennet
- powdered sugar
- nutmeg
- cream

## Directions

1. Make a quart of milk moderately warm
2. Stir into it one large spoonful of the preparation called rennet
3. Set it by, and when cool again it will be as stiff as jelly
4. In summer set the dish on ice after it has jellied
5. It must be served with powdered sugar, nutmeg and cream
