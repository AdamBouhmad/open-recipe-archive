## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup cleaned currants
- grated rind of 1 lemon
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients together
2. Rub in butter
3. Add currants and lemon rind
4. Mix to a thick batter with cold milk
5. Turn into a well-greased pan
6. Bake 1 hour in moderate oven
