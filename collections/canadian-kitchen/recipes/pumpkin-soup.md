## Ingredients

- 1 cup of cooked pumpkin
- 1 quart milk
- salt
- 1 tablespoon hot melted butter
- croutons
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut pumpkin in small pieces, sprinkling lightly with salt
2. Cook in boiling water for five minutes
3. Drain off the water
4. Mash thoroughly
5. Stir in hot melted butter
6. Pour boiling milk over croutons arranged in soup dish and lightly sugared
7. Add the pumpkin
8. Stir the whole well together and serve
