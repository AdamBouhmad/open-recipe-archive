## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound turmeric
- 2 ounces ground ginger
- 0.5 pound mustard
- 2 ounces allspice
- 0.5 pound mustard seed
- 0.5 ounce cayenne
- 1 ounce curry powder
- 4 quarts malt vinegar
- Vegetables

## Directions

1. Boil these ingredients five minutes with vinegar
2. Pour over the vegetables while the mixture is hot
3. Soak vegetables at least eight days in strong salt and water, then dry them
4. Put them into jars, not too closely packed
5. Pour over them the above mixture
