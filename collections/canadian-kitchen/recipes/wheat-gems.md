## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 quart milk
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls (small) butter

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with the salt
2. Stir the milk in smoothly
3. Beat the yolks and whites well and separately
4. Stir first the yolks in the milk and flour, then the whites, then the melted butter
5. Half fill the gem-pans and bake in a deep pan or on a baking-sheet in a moderate oven for twenty-five minutes; if baked in earthen cups, forty-five minutes
6. Let them be thoroughly baked, or they will fall on being taken from the oven
