## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix butter, sugar, and milk
2. Add flour and baking powder
3. Add vanilla and eggs
4. Bake in layer cake or sponge cake form
