## Ingredients

- yellow carrots
- milk
- sugar
- eggs
- ginger
- cloves
- cinnamon or allspice

## Directions

1. Cook yellow carrots until soft, then sift and make as pumpkin pie with milk, sugar and eggs
2. season with ginger, cloves, and cinnamon or allspice
