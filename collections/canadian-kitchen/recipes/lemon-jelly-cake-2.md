## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs well beaten
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add well beaten eggs, milk, flour and baking powder sifted together
3. Bake in layers
4. For jelly: combine sugar, egg, lemon juice, rind and cold water with cornstarch
5. Place the dish in boiling water until it thickens
6. Spread between the layers and ice the top
