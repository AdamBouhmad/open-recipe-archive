## Ingredients

- 6 or 7 good-sized potatoes
- 1 large cup of boneless codfish
- boiling water
- egg
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Pare 6 or 7 good-sized potatoes
2. Quarter them
3. Cover with boiling water
4. Put a large cup of boneless codfish above the potatoes
5. Cook till tender
6. Drain
7. Mash and season to taste
8. Add an egg and beat well
9. Shape into balls or flat cakes (about 1/2 inch thick)
10. Fry in hot fat
