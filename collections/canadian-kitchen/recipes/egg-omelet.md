## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/2 pint milk
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks separately.
2. Add the whites beaten to a stiff froth last.
3. Cook in a little butter.
