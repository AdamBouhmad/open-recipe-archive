## Ingredients

- smelts
- salt
- beaten egg
- bread or cracker-crumbs
- boiling fat
- parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the smelts by drawing them between the finger and thumb, beginning at the tail
2. wash them and drain in a colander
3. salt well and dip in beaten egg and bread or cracker-crumbs
4. dip first in the egg and then roll in the crumbs
5. fry in boiling fat deep enough to float them
6. they should be a handsome brown in two minutes and a half
7. take them up and place them on a sheet of brown paper for a few minutes to drain
8. pour on a hot dish
9. garnish with parsley and a few slices of lemon
