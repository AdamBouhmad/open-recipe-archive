## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1 lb. citron or lemon peel
- 1 teacup sugar
- 2 lbs. beef suet
- Butter size of orange
- 2 lbs. flour
- 2 thick slices bread crumbed fine
- 7 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful ground cloves
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1 nutmeg grated
- milk or water

## Directions

1. Mix it all with milk or water
2. Boil four hours in a bag
3. This makes two large puddings, and may be kept a month steamed when wanted, and eaten with a sauce
