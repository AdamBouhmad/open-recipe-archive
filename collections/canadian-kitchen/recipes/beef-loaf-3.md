## Ingredients

- 2 3/4 pounds beefsteak
- 2 eggs
- 2 soda biscuits
- 1/2 cup cream
- 6 large chopped onions
- small piece of suet chopped fine
- 1/2 cup butter
- chopped parsley
- sage
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put beefsteak through grinder
2. Crumble soda biscuits
3. Chop parsley and sage
4. Mix all ingredients together
5. Bake in oven about 2 hours
