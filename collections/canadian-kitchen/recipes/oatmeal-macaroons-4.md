## Ingredients

- 2 cups oatmeal
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth
2. Drop from a teaspoon on a buttered tin
3. Bake in a quick oven
