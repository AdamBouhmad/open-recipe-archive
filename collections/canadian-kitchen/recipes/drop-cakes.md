## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful whisky
- Flour enough to make stiff batter
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Beat butter to a cream, then add yolks well beaten, then part of the flour
2. whites beaten stiff
3. whisky
4. rest of flour
5. Bake in a quick oven, on a buttered paper
