## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of potatoes
- 2 quarts of water
- 2 cups of flour
- 2 quarts cold water
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup salt
- 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes

## Directions

1. Peel and slice 2 quarts of potatoes, boil in 2 quarts of water
2. When cooked, scald 2 cups of flour with water of potatoes and add 2 quarts cold water
3. Add 1 cup brown sugar and 1/4 cup salt, along with mashed potatoes
4. Add 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes dissolved in a cup of warm water
5. Set in a warm place, stirring 3 or 4 times
6. For making each loaf use a cup of yeast and 1 of warm water
