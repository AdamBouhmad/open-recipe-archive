## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup sour milk or buttermilk
- 1/2 cup baking syrup
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- a little nutmeg and allspice
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs
3. Mix soda with sour milk or buttermilk
4. Add soda mixture and syrup to batter
5. Add cloves, nutmeg, and allspice
6. Add flour to reach desired consistency, not too stiff
7. Bake in layers in moderate oven
8. Fill with date filling
