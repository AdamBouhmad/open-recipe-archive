## Ingredients

- 1 pound prunes
- beaten whites of 5 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil prunes until tender and dry
2. remove the pits and crack, then chopping the nut with the prune pulp
3. add the beaten whites of eggs, and white sugar
4. bake in a moderate oven for 20 minutes
