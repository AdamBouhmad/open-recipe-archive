## Ingredients

- 48 good-sized tomatoes
- 8 onions
- 16 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 4 cups vinegar
- 4 tablespoonfuls salt
- 6 green peppers
- 2 tablespoonfuls allspice
- 2 tablespoonfuls cloves
- 2 tablespoonfuls cinnamon
- 2 tablespoonfuls mustard

## Directions

1. Have spices ground.
2. Peel tomatoes, and onions, stem peppers; chop together and boil till onions are soft.
