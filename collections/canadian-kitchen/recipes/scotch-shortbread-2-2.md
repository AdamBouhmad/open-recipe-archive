## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 2 pounds flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pound butter to a cream, and add by degrees 1/4 pound sugar and 2 pounds flour
2. Work this dough until smooth, roll out, cut into squares and pinch up edges
3. Prick it well with fork
4. Bake in slow oven
