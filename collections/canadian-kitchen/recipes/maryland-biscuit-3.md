## Ingredients

- Two sifters of flour
- Two tablespoons of lard
- One saltspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Mix to as stiff a dough as possible.
2. Beat with sadiron or rolling pin until soft and blistering.
3. Cut out and press the knuckles into each biscuit.
4. Bake in a quick oven.
