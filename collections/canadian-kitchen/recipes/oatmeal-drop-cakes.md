## Ingredients

- 3 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup melted butter
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 5 teaspoons sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix oatmeal, flour, brown sugar and cinnamon together dry
2. Add 1 cup melted butter, 2 eggs well beaten, 1 teaspoon soda, 5 teaspoons sour milk
3. Drop in small pieces on a pan
4. Bake in a hot oven
