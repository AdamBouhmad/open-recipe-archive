## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 yolk of egg
- 2 cups chopped cooked macaroni
- 2 tablespoons cheese
- pepper and salt to taste
- egg and crumbs for rolling
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare a white sauce
2. Add butter, flour, and milk together
3. Add macaroni, cheese, yolk of egg, pepper and salt
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Cool
6. Make into croquettes
7. Roll in egg and crumbs
8. Fry
9. Serve with tomato sauce
