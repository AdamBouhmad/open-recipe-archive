## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of butter per quart
- corn-starch
- fine cracker-dust
- 1 teaspoonful of salt per quart
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper per quart
- 1/2 teaspoonful of grated onion per quart

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water upon tomatoes to loosen their skins, and peel them.
2. Slice, or cut into dice, and cook in a porcelain or agate-iron saucepan for twenty minutes.
3. Drain off the superfluous liquid, pepper and salt it, and keep for sauces, stews, and soups.
4. Stir into the hot tomatoes, for each quart, a tablespoonful of butter rolled in corn-starch or in fine cracker-dust, a teaspoonful each of salt and pepper, and half a teaspoonful of grated onion.
5. Cook three minutes longer and serve.
