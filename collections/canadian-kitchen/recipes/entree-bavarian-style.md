## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. beef collops
- 1/4 lb. sausage
- 1 small onion
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper
- cabbage leaves
- lard and butter

## Directions

1. Form into croquettes.
2. Have some half-boiled leaves of cabbage, wrap each croquette in one large leaf, completely covering the meat, tie firmly and fry slowly in lard and butter (half and half) until brown on both sides.
3. Serve on a flat platter with gravy poured over, which may be increased by addition of a little boiling water.
