## Ingredients

- pickerel
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- cracker-dust
- cottolene
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean, wipe dry, roll in salted and peppered flour, or dip in egg and roll in seasoned cracker-dust, and fry quickly in deep cottolene or oil brought slowly to the boil.
