## Ingredients

- 10 qts. water
- 3 lbs. sugar
- 3 oz. ginger
- 3 oz. cream tartar
- juice and rind 6 lemons
- 6 teaspoonfuls brewer's yeast or 6 cakes of yeast

## Directions

1. Pour ten quarts of boiling water on sugar, ginger bruised, cream of tartar, the thin rind and juice of six lemons.
2. When cool add yeast; let stand two days, bottle and cork.
