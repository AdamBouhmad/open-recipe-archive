## Ingredients

- 1/4 cake chocolate
- Yolk of 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Take chocolate, yolk of egg, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup milk
2. Let boil until it thickens
3. Set aside until cool
4. Beat 2 eggs with 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup butter
5. Add 1 cup milk
6. Mix in 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder
7. Put in the chocolate mixture
8. Mix thoroughly
9. Bake in layers
10. Join with icing
