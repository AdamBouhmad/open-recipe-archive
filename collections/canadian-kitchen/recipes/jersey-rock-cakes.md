## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds flour
- 1/2 pound butter
- 10 ounces sugar
- 4 eggs
- a few drops of essence of lemon
- currants

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, sugar, eggs, essence of lemon and currants well together
2. Drop on baking tin
