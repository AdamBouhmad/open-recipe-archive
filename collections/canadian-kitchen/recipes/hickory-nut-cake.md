## Ingredients

- 3 eggs, leaving out white of 1
- 1 cup sugar, flavor if desired
- butter size of an egg
- 1½ cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup nut meats
- a little sweet milk

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. Add sugar and butter; beat together well
3. Add flour, in which sift baking powder
4. Add enough sweet milk to make rather thin batter
5. Lastly add part of nuts chopped
6. Bake in layers
7. Put together with remaining white of egg beaten with granulated sugar till stiff, and cover with halved nuts
