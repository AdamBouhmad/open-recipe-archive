## Ingredients

- 1 round steak, pounded
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- cream
- sage

## Directions

1. Make a dressing by crumbling bread crumbs with pepper and salt
2. Wet dressing with a little cream and any other seasoning such as sage
3. Spread over top of steak
4. Roll and tie with string
5. Put in pan and roast 40 minutes
