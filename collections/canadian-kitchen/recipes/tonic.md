## Ingredients

- one quart of rye whisky
- one ounce fluid extract of celery
- two ounces fluid extract of hops
- half ounce fluid extract of rhubarb
- four quarts of cold water

## Directions

1. Mix well, and bottle for use
2. One tablespoonful before each meal and at bedtime
