## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 hard crackers baked quite brown
- 1 quart water
- sugar
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Break in pieces three or four hard crackers that are baked quite brown
2. Let them boil fifteen minutes in one quart of water
3. Remove from the fire, let them stand three or four minutes
4. Strain off the liquor through a fine wire sieve
5. Season it with sugar
6. For invalids recovering from a fever, add a little wine and nutmeg
