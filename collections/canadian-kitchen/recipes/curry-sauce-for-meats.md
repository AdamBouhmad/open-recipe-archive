## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon curry powder
- 1 tablespoon Chutney sauce
- 1 cup stock

## Directions

1. Brown butter
2. Add onion and Chutney sauce
3. Cook thoroughly before adding stock and curry
4. In this sauce, heat up cooked egg or meat cut in small pieces
5. Serve with cooked rice
