## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 egg well beaten
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- a little salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- Graham flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1 egg well.
2. Mix 1 cup sour milk, beaten egg, 1/2 teaspoon soda, a little salt and 1 tablespoon sugar.
3. Add Graham flour enough for stiff batter.
4. Add 1 tablespoon butter (melted) lastly.
