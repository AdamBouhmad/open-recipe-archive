## Ingredients

- 2 pounds soup meat
- 1 cup barley
- Salt and pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 3 potatoes
- 1/4 cabbage cut up fine
- 2 cups lima beans
- 2 cups corn
- 3 or 4 tomatoes
- A little parsley

## Directions

1. Chop vegetables up fine, all but potatoes and tomatoes
2. Cut potatoes and tomatoes up and add when near done
3. Cook until done
