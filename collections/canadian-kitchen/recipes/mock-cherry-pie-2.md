## Ingredients

- 1 cup cranberries chopped fine
- 3½ cup raisins seeded and chopped fine
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake with cover
