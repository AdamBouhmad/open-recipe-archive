## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 3/2 teaspoon soda
- cassia, nutmeg and cloves

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Stir in the well-beaten eggs
3. Mix the raisins, walnuts and spices with the flour and add with the soda dissolved in warm water
4. Beat thoroughly
5. Drop a teaspoon on baking pan
6. Bake
