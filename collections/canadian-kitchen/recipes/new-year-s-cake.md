## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 cups raisins
- 1/4 pound walnuts
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and butter together
2. Add the milk
3. Add eggs well beaten
4. Add 1 1/2 cups flour, 2 cups raisins, 1/4 pound walnuts, and 2 teaspoons baking powder
5. Bake in moderate oven
