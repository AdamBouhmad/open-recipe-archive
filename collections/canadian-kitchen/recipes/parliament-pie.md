## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 5 eggs
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 pound bread crumbs fine
- 1 pound currants
- Pie paste

## Directions

1. Dry and roll the bread crumbs fine
2. Cream together 1 pound sugar, 1 pound butter, 5 eggs, 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, 1 pint sweet milk, and 1 teaspoon baking soda
3. Line your bake dish with pie paste
4. Pour in the mixture
