## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter (2 cups)
- 1 pound sugar (2 cups)
- 12 yolks eggs
- 2 cups molasses
- 1 pound flour (4 cups)
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 2 teaspoons mace
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- whites of 12 eggs
- 2 pounds seeded raisins
- 2 pounds sliced citron
- 2 1/2 pounds currants
- 3/4 pound candied peel chopped
- 1/4 pound chopped blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 round sponge-cake pans, with tube, about 2 hours and 40 minutes
2. Each loaf weighs 5 pounds
