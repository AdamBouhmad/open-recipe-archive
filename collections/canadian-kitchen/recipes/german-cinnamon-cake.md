## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 3 teaspoons butter
- 1 egg
- 1 pint sweet milk
- lemon or vanilla flavoring
- sugar
- cinnamon
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Make a rich biscuit dough with flour, salt, baking powder, sugar, butter, egg, and sweet milk
2. Flavor with lemon or vanilla
3. Mix all into a soft dough
4. Roll 3/4 inch thick
5. Put dough in a shallow pan
6. Cover top with a mixture of sugar, cinnamon and melted butter
7. Bake in quick oven
