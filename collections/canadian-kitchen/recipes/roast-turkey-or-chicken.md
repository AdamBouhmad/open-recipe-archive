## Ingredients

- flour
- pepper
- salt
- bread and cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Having picked and drawn the fowl, wash them well in 2 or 3 waters
2. Wipe them dry
3. Dredge them with a little flour inside and out
4. Sprinkle on a little pepper and salt
5. Prepare a dressing of bread and cracker crumbs
6. Fill the bodies and crops of the fowls
7. Bake them from 2 to 3 hours
8. Baste them frequently while roasting
9. Stew the giblets in a saucepan
10. Just before serving chop the giblets fine, after taking up the chicken and the water in which the giblets are boiled, and the chopped giblets to a gravy of the roast fowl
11. Thicken with a little flour which has been previously wet with the water
12. Boil up and serve in a gravy dish
13. Roast chicken and turkey should be accompanied with celery and jellies
