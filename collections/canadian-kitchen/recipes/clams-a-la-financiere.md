## Ingredients

- 2 doz. clams
- 1 slice mild ham
- 1 teaspoonful chopped parsley
- 1/4 teaspoonful chopped onion
- 1 tablespoonful chopped celery
- 1/4 can French mushrooms
- 1/4 can French peas
- Salt and red pepper
- Butter
- Mashed potatoes
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Open clams, taking care to retain all the liquor found in shell, also shells to serve up in
2. Stew in their own liquor for five minutes, cut into pieces the size of peas
3. Take ham, cut into small dice; place in a small saucepan on fire with sufficient butter to prevent burning
4. Fry to a light brown color, then add chopped onions, chopped celery; mushrooms, peas; parsley
5. Mix in the clams and their liquor
6. Season to taste with red pepper and salt; stew for fifteen minutes
7. Serve in their own shell made hot, with borders of mashed potatoes and garnished with parsley and lemon
