## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold meat minced
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley or sage
- 1 very small onion chopped fine
- pepper
- salt
- beaten eggs
- brown gravy or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 cup cold meat minced, 1 cup bread crumbs, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley or sage, 1 very small onion chopped fine, pepper and salt to taste
2. Moisten with beaten eggs
3. Bake in hot oven 15 or 20 minutes
4. Serve with brown gravy or tomato sauce
