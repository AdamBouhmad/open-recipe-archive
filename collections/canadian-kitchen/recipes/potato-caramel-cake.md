## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 4 yolks eggs
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup hot mashed potatoes
- 1/4 cup grated chocolate
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/8 teaspoon cloves
- 4 beaten whites
- 1 cup walnuts

## Directions

1. Beat butter, sugar, and egg yolks to a cream
2. Add sweet milk and hot mashed potatoes
3. Add grated chocolate
4. Add flour and baking powder
5. Add ground cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves
6. Fold in 4 beaten whites
7. Add walnuts
8. Bake slowly
