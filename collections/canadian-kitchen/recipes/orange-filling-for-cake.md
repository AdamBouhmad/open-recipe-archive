## Ingredients

- 0.5 pt. milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- Yolks 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Grated yellow rind of 1 orange
- 2 tablespoonfuls orange juice

## Directions

1. Put milk in a double boiler
2. Moisten cornstarch with a little cold water
3. Add it to the scalded milk
4. Stir constantly until smooth and thick
5. Beat the yolks of eggs with sugar, until light
6. Add it to the cornstarch
7. Take from the fire
8. When cool add the grated yellow rind of one orange and two tablespoons of orange juice
