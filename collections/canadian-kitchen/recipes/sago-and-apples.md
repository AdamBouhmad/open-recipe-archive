## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sago
- water
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- small piece butter
- heaping tablespoon sugar
- apples

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 cup sago in about 5 times its bulk of water for hour
2. Add 1/8 teaspoon salt, a small piece of butter, and a heaping tablespoon sugar
3. Pare and core as many apples as will cover the bottom of the baking dish
4. Pour sago over them
5. Cook in a moderate oven until the apples are done
6. If the apples are large they can be quartered, and the baking dish can be about filled with them, and the sago put over them the same way
