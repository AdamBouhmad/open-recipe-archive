## Ingredients

- 2 pounds white sugar
- 2 quarts water
- 2 ounces tartaric acid
- 10c. bottle vanilla
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water 10 minutes
2. When cold, add tartaric acid
3. Add vanilla
4. Add beaten egg whites
