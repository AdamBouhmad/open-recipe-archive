## Ingredients

- 1 peck Damson plums
- 3/4 pound sugar to 1 pound plums
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cloves
- 3 dessertspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1 dessertspoon salt

## Directions

1. Boil the vinegar and plums well
2. Strain through sieve
3. Add the other ingredients
4. Boil till it thickens
