## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons flour
- 3 ounces butter
- 1 egg
- 1 eggspoon baking powder
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 pint milk
- flavoring to taste
- 1 1/2 pounds apples
- cold stewed apples

## Directions

1. Have ready some cold stewed apples
2. Rub butter into flour
3. Add baking powder
4. Beat up egg with sugar
5. Add milk
6. Stir all into the flour
7. Mix well
8. Pour on the top of the apples
9. Bake in moderate oven for hour
10. Sift sugar over
