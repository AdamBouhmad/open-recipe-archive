## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 pounds lobster
- four pounded crackers
- 1 quart boiling milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop lobster fine
2. Take the green part and add to it four pounded crackers
3. Stir into 1 quart boiling milk
4. Add lobster
5. Add a piece of butter 1/2 size of an egg
6. Add a little pepper and salt
7. Bring to a boil
