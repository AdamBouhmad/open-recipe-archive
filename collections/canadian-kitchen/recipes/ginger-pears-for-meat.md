## Ingredients

- 5 pounds pears (winter ones preferred)
- 5 pounds white sugar
- ¼ package seeded raisins
- 1 ounce crystallized ginger
- 2 whole lemons

## Directions

1. Chop pears
2. Boil 1 hour
3. Boil 2 whole lemons for 1 hour separately
4. Chop lemons fine
5. Add to pears
6. Boil 20 minutes more
7. Seal
