## Ingredients

- Butter the size of an egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 1/2 cup blackstrap
- 1 teaspoon mixed spice
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter with brown sugar
2. Add well-beaten eggs
3. Add blackstrap, mixed spice, soda, and buttermilk
4. Sift in flour
5. Bake in layers
