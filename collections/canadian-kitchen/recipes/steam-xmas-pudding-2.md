## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- season to taste
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to make not too stiff

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Steam
