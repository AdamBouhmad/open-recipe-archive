## Ingredients

- Two cups of boiled fish
- One cup of milk or cream
- One large tablespoonful of butter
- Three tablespoonfuls of flour
- Yolks of two eggs
- One tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- Pepper and salt to taste
- Little onion juice and nutmeg, if you wish

## Directions

1. Put milk on to boil, rub butter and flour together well and add to milk, then parsley
2. Add yolks of eggs, then the fish and stir until well mixed, then season
3. When cold form into cutlets
4. Roll with a little flour, dip in egg and then in breadcrumbs
5. Fry in dripping
6. For the sauce: Tablespoonful of melted butter, one of flour, stir smoothly, add one cup of milk; salt and pepper
