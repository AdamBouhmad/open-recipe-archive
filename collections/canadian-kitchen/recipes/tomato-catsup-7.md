## Ingredients

- 1 bu. tomatoes
- 6 onions
- 1 qt. vinegar
- 1 teacup salt
- 2 lbs. sugar
- 1 lb. whole black peppers
- 1 lb. whole allspice
- 1 oz. whole cloves
- 1 oz. whole cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut up tomatoes and onions and boil soft, strain, tie all spices in a bag and boil together three hours.
2. Bottle and cork while hot.
