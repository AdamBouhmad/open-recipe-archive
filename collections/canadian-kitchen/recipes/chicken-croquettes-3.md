## Ingredients

- 2 cups finely chopped chicken
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1/2 cup cream or milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- a little mace, nutmeg, pepper, and salt

## Directions

1. Mix the butter and flour together
2. When the milk and stock have come to a boil add them to the butter and flour
3. Add the meat, and let all simmer together ten minutes
4. Add eggs pretty well beaten
5. Leave on the back of stove for a few minutes, but do not allow it to simmer
6. Pour in a dish to cool
7. When quite cool form into small rolls and roll in bread-crumbs, then in egg, and again in bread-crumbs
8. Cook in boiling lard
