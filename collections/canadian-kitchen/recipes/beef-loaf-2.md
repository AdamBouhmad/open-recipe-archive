## Ingredients

- Three pounds of beefsteak, chopped fine
- Fifteen soda biscuits, well rolled
- Half a cupful of sweet milk
- Half a cupful of butter
- One teaspoonful of salt and pepper
- Two eggs

## Directions

1. Mix well in a loaf
2. Bake two hours
