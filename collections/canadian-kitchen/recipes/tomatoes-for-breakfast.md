## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 cup sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes through colander
2. Bring to a boil on the stove
3. Season with pepper, salt, and sugar
4. Mix flour with half a cup of sweet cream
5. Stir it in with the tomatoes while boiling
6. Serve hot
