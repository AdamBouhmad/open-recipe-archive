## Ingredients

- beef dripping
- onion, large or several small ones
- liver
- boiling water
- brown gravy
- pepper
- salt
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Place in a stewpan enough beef dripping to fry either a large onion or several small ones.
2. Cut your liver in nice squares, not too thin, brown them nicely in the stewpan.
3. Add a little boiling water and a little brown gravy if you have it.
4. Stew till tender, and season to taste.
5. Before dishing, thicken the gravy with cornstarch.
