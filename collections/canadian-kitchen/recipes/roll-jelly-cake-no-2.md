## Ingredients

- 3 eggs beaten to a froth
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 small tablespoons cold water
- 1 cup sifted flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- jelly

## Directions

1. Beat eggs to a froth
2. Add white sugar and beat again
3. Add cold water, sifted flour and baking powder
4. Spread with jelly and roll
