## Ingredients

- any left-over chicken or tomatoes
- 2 cups chicken, cut into small pieces
- 2 cups tomatoes, cut into small pieces
- 1 cup water
- 1 onion
- 1 cup boiled rice
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Stew chicken pieces and tomatoes gently in 1 cup water for 20 minutes
2. Add onion while stewing if desired
3. Put the chicken, tomatoes, and 1 cup boiled rice in a kettle and heat to boiling point
4. Serve hot, garnish the platter with parsley
