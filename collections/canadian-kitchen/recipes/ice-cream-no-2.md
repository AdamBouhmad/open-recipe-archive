## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 6 ounces sugar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 pint whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Scald milk with yolks of 2 eggs, 6 ounces sugar, and 1 tablespoon cornstarch until thick
2. When cold, add 1 pint whipped cream and whites of 2 eggs beaten stiff
3. Flavor and freeze
