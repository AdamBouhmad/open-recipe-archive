## Ingredients

- One ounce of glaze
- One tablespoonful of boiling water
- One quarter of a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Put one ounce of glaze and one tablespoonful of boiling water into saucepan over fire till melted
2. Brush it over ham or tongue; two coats if not dark enough
3. Beat one quarter of a pound of butter to a cream
4. Put into icing tubes and ornament with it
