## Ingredients

- one-quarter cake unsweetened chocolate
- three-quarters of a cup of powdered sugar
- one-half cup of boiling water

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate with powdered sugar and boiling water, stirring all the time
2. Cook in a double boiler to the consistency of molasses
3. Serve hot
