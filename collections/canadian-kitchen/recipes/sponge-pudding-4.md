## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 2 oz. sugar
- 2 oz. flour
- 6 eggs, beaten separately
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix milk, sugar and flour and heat but not boil.
2. Take off the fire and put in butter, the yolks of eggs thoroughly beaten, then the whites.
3. Bake twenty-five minutes in a pan or mould set in hot water.
4. Eat with foam sauce.
