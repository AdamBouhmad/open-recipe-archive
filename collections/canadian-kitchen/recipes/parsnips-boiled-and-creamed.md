## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salted water
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape parsnips
2. Cut in pieces 2 inches long and inch wide
3. Cook until soft in boiling salted water
4. Drain and add 1 cup white sauce to every 2 cups parsnips
