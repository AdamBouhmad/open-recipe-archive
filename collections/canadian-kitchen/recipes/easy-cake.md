## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- piece of butter size of an egg
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 heaping teaspoonful baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat all together until light.
2. Add milk, flour and baking powder and beat again.
3. Bake in two layers.
