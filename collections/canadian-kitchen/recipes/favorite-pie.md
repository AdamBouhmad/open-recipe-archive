## Ingredients

- 6 stewed apples
- 1 tablespoon butter
- beaten yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup cream
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Mash 6 stewed apples and while hot stir in a tablespoon butter
2. When cool add the beaten yolks of 4 eggs, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup cream and juice of one lemon
3. Line pie tins with good crust and fill with above mixture
4. Bake in a quick oven and spread the beaten whites of the eggs sweetened a little over top
5. Place in oven and brown lightly
