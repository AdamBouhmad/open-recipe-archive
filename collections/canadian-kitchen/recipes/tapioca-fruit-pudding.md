## Ingredients

- one cup tapioca
- one quart of water
- one cup of sugar
- rind and juice of one lemon
- box and a half of fresh fruit (red currants delicious)

## Directions

1. Steep tapioca over night in one quart of water
2. Drain off water in the morning and add one cup of sugar and the rind and juice of one lemon to tapioca
3. Put fruit in pudding-dish and add tapioca and mix
4. Bake in a slow oven for one hour
5. Serve cold
