## Ingredients

- milk
- 1 scant tablespoonful sifted flour
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Thicken milk with a scant tablespoonful of sifted flour
2. Let it boil five minutes, stirring it constantly
3. Add a little cold milk
4. Give it one boil up, and it is ready for use
