## Ingredients

- Butter
- Lobster
- Cracker-crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Milk

## Directions

1. Butter the dish, chop the lobster quite fine
2. Layer of lobster and cracker-crumbs alternately
3. Add salt, pepper, and butter
4. Moisten with milk
5. Bake for twenty minutes
