## Ingredients

- Two ounces each of butter, flour, bread crumbs and grated cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Mix these ingredients into a paste, roll it a quarter of an inch thick, cut into narrow strips.
2. Bake until a light brown color.
3. Serve cold.
