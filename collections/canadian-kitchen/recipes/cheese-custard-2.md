## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 3/4 cup grated stale cheese

## Directions

1. Put the seasonings and the eggs into a saucepan and beat thoroughly
2. Add the milk and stir in the cheese
3. Put over the fire and stir constantly until it begins to thicken and coat the spoon
4. Take at once from the fire, and pour over soda biscuits, strips of buttered toast, or shredded wheat biscuits arranged on a hot platter
