## Ingredients

- citron
- alum
- water
- 3/4 pound sugar to 1 pound citron
- ginger root
- 1 lemon
- 2 medium-sized citrons

## Directions

1. Cut the citron in dice
2. Boil in water with a small piece of alum until tender and clear
3. Make a syrup, allowing 3/4 pound sugar to 1 pound citron
4. Boil a piece of ginger root in the syrup
5. Put the citron in and boil for a few minutes
6. Put in 1 lemon with 2 medium-sized citrons
