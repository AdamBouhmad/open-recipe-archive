## Ingredients

- smooth, solid tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes into slices and drain well
2. Dust each with pepper and salt
3. Beat an egg in a saucer until light
4. Add to it a tablespoon of boiling water
5. Dip each slice first in this, then in crumbs
6. Fry brown on both sides
7. Take up carefully and serve very hot
