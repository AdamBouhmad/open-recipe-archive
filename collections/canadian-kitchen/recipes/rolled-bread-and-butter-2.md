## Ingredients

- bread loaf
- butter

## Directions

1. Butter the loaf, not a fresh one, having first decrusted it with a very sharp knife
2. Cut a slice as thin as possible
3. Roll each slice with flat of hand
4. Pile the rolls log-fashion, or in a pyramid, on a doyley-covered bread plate
5. Garnish daintily with parsley or cress
