## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter washed in warm water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 5 eggs
- 4 cups flour sifted
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix butter washed in warm water, sugar, and milk.
2. Add eggs mixed in one by one.
3. Sift in flour and baking powder.
4. Bake.
