## Ingredients

- chunk steak 1/2 inch thick
- stuffing
- brown sauce
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut chunk steak into pieces 4 inches square
2. Season both sides
3. Put stuffing on pieces and roll
4. Tie pieces
5. Put in double roasting pan with brown sauce underneath
6. Cook in aladdin or slow oven 2 hours
7. Season with salt and serve
