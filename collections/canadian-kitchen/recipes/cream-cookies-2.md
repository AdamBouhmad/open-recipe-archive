## Ingredients

- 2 cups sour cream
- 3 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 teaspoons soda
- a nutmeg grated
- 1 teaspoon lemon
- flour enough to roll out

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Bake in a quick oven
