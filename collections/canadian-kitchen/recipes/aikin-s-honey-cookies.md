## Ingredients

- 1 cup extracted honey
- 1 pint sour cream
- scant teaspoon soda
- flavoring if desired
- flour to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Mix honey, sour cream and soda
2. Add flavoring if desired
3. Mix with flour to make a soft dough
