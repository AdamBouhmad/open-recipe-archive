## Ingredients

- 1/2 can tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- bit of bay-leaf
- 8 peppercorns
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 4 teaspoons flour
- 4 teaspoons butter
- 1 cup stock

## Directions

1. Cook first 5 ingredients together until tomatoes are soft
2. Strain and add stock
3. Melt butter, add flour, and when browned add liquid gradually
