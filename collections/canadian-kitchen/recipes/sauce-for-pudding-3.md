## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- a little cold water
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 1 3/4 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and cornstarch with a little cold water
2. Add maple syrup and boiling water
3. Cook about 5 minutes
