## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. pineapple
- 4½ lbs. sugar
- 3 pts. water
- brandied paper

## Directions

1. Peel and remove all dark spots on the pineapples
2. Shred with a fork
3. Boil sugar and water ten minutes, skim and add the shredded pineapple
4. Let it boil up, then seal in self-sealers with a brandied paper between the fruit and glass tops
