## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 pounds brown sugar
- 1 gallon clean soft water
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake

## Directions

1. Let brown sugar and water boil
2. Put in a crock and when cool, not cold, add 1 White Swan Yeast Cake (1 yeast cake is enough for 3 gallons)
3. Stir every day, and when it has stopped working, let it stand to get sour
4. Keep in a warm place all the time
