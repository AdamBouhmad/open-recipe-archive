## Ingredients

- sugar
- milk of cocoanut
- cocoanut (finely grated)
- butter

## Directions

1. Put sugar and milk of cocoanut together, heat slowly until the sugar is melted, then boil five minutes
2. Add cocoanut (finely grated), boil ten minutes longer, stir constantly to keep from burning
3. Pour on buttered plates, cut in squares
4. Will take about two days to harden
5. Use prepared cocoanut when other cannot be had
