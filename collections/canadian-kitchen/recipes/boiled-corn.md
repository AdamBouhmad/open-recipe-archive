## Ingredients

- corn
- salt

## Directions

1. Husk, clearing the ear of every strand of silk, and trim off stem and top neatly
2. Boil fifteen or twenty minutes, according to the age of the corn
3. Drain, sprinkle the corn with salt, and serve upon a hot napkin upon a platter
4. Fold the corners of the napkin over the corn
