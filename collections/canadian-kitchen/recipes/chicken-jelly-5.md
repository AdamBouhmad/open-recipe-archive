## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- peppercorn
- water

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into joints and put into a pan or stew-jar
2. Put in a very little salt and a peppercorn, and just cover with water
3. Let it stew gently for two or three hours or more, adding a little water if required
4. When the meat falls from the bones take off the meat and pound up the bones, and give them an extra boil
5. Strain the liquid from the meat (and bones) and when cold take off any fat
