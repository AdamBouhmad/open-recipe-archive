## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 1/4 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 pound dates
- 1 pound nuts
- 1 pound raisins

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop on buttered tins
