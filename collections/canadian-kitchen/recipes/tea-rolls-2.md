## Ingredients

- 2 quarts flour
- 1 large tablespoon lard
- 1 pint cold boiled milk
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake
- butter

## Directions

1. Into 2 quarts flour rub 1 large tablespoon lard
2. Make a hole in flour, pour in 1 pint cold boiled milk, 3/4 cup sugar, 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake
3. Let rise over night
4. Knead in morning and let rise until noon
5. Knead and roll out
6. Cut with round cake cutter
7. Butter one half
8. Turn other half over on to it
9. Let rise until tea time
10. Bake 25 minutes in a quick oven
