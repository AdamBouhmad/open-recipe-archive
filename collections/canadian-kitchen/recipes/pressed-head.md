## Ingredients

- pig's head
- salt
- upper parts of shanks
- pepper
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Soak the pig's head in cold water with a little salt in it over night
2. When it has soaked enough, add the upper parts of the shanks
3. Boil until the meat will drop from the bone
4. Chop fine, season with pepper, salt, cinnamon and cloves to taste
5. Put in a mould and press while warm
