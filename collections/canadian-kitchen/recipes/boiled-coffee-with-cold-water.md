## Ingredients

- 2 ounces coffee
- 1 pint water
- 1 raw egg
- 4 tablespoons cold water

## Directions

1. Beat dry coffee well into 1 raw egg mixed with 4 tablespoons of cold water
2. Add remainder of water
3. Heat slowly to boiling
4. Let boil 3 minutes
5. Let settle 5 minutes
6. Serve
