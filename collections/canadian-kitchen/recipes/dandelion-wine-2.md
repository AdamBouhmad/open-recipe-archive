## Ingredients

- dandelion flowers (1 gallon)
- boiling water (2 gallons)
- lemons (4)
- sugar (6 pounds)
- White Swan Yeast Cake (1)
- toast

## Directions

1. To 1 gallon of dandelion flowers add 2 gallons of boiling water
2. Let stand 24 hours, then strain and see that there is 2 gallons of liquid
3. Add 4 lemons, sliced, and 6 pounds sugar
4. Soak 1 White Swan Yeast Cake and spread on a piece of toast and float on liquid, yeast side down
5. Let it stand 3 weeks, only stirring till the sugar is all off the bottom of the crock
6. Bottle
