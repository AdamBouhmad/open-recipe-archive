## Ingredients

- Cold roast beef
- 3 tablespoonfuls of walnut catsup
- 1 teaspoonful of vinegar
- A little salt and pepper
- A dessertspoonful of currant jelly
- A little warm water

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of cold roast beef
2. Lay them in a tin saucepan set in a pot of boiling water
3. Cover them with gravy made of the walnut catsup, vinegar, salt, pepper, currant jelly, and warm water
4. Cover tightly, and steam for half an hour, keeping the water in the outer vessel on a hard boil
