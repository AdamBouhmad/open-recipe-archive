## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 2 lbs. flour
- 1/4 lb. brown sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat butter to cream and dredge in flour and sugar gradually
2. Must be well beaten
3. Roll out an inch thick
4. Pinch edge all around
5. prick with a fork
6. cut in small pieces
