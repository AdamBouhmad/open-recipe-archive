## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 3 eggs
- 8 soda biscuits
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Roll 8 soda biscuits fine
2. Break 3 eggs on salmon
3. Stir thoroughly with rolled biscuits, 3 tablespoons sweet milk, a little salt and pepper
4. Fry in butter
