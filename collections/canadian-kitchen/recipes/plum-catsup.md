## Ingredients

- 3 pounds blue plums
- 1 3/8 pounds brown sugar
- 3/4 pound mustard
- 1 pound raisins chopped fine
- 1 ounce garlic
- 1 1/2 dozen ripe tomatoes
- 1 1/4 dozen large apples
- 1 1/4 dozen large onions
- 2 red peppers

## Directions

1. Boil 1 hour
2. Strain through sieve
