## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- water
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- milk
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Put brown sugar in pudding dish with just enough water to keep from burning
2. Put on stove to brown
3. Beat yolks of 3 eggs well
4. Stir in 2 tablespoons cornstarch
5. If too thick, add a little milk
6. Pour in the sugar and cook
7. Have the whites of the eggs beaten stiff
8. Pour over the rest, stirring briskly
9. Serve with or without cream, as preferred
