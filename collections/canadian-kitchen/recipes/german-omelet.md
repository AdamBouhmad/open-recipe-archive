## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 teaspoonfuls cornstarch
- 1 pt. milk
- salt
- celery salt

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately.
2. Mix beaten yolks, milk, cornstarch and seasoning and divide into two parts.
3. Pour half into a hot buttered frying-pan and cook slowly.
4. When beginning to set, put on the top of the omelet half the beaten whites, and when cooked turn over so that the white of the eggs is enrolled in the omelet.
5. Serve very hot, with brown side up.
6. Proceed in the same manner with the remaining half.
