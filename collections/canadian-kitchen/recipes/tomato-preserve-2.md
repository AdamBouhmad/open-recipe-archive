## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 1/2 peck ripe apples
- 3 1/2 dozen lemons
- 2 ounces whole ginger
- 2 ounces cinnamon
- 1 pint good vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and stew the tomatoes with a pint of good vinegar, until the water is pretty well boiled out of them
2. Add a pound of sugar to each pint of the mixture and boil until thick
