## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- ½ can salmon
- 1 egg
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook rice till tender
2. Add salmon, egg, season with pepper and salt
3. Form into oblong shape
4. Fry brown in butter
