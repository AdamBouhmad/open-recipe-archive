## Ingredients

- tea
- boiling water
- lemon

## Directions

1. Make tea in the same way as regular tea
2. Serve with a slice of lemon either in cup and tea poured over it, or placed on saucer beside cup
3. Rind is always left on
4. Do not use cream in this tea
