## Ingredients

- 1 cup citron shaved thin
- 1 cup nuts
- 1 cup raisins chopped
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/8 cup flour
- butter size of an egg
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Combine citron, nuts and chopped raisins
2. Sift over fruit 1/4 cup flour, 1/2 cup sugar and 3/8 cup flour
3. Add butter size of an egg, yolks of 3 eggs and 1/4 cup milk
4. Add 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
5. Add this to the fruit
6. Bake in layers
