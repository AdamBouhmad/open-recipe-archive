## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped nut meats
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 whites
- 3/4 cup fine cracker crumbs
- vanilla or almond flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very light
2. Add sugar gradually
3. Fold in nuts and vanilla and crumbs
4. If eggs are large, add more crumbs
5. Drop on unbuttered paper
6. Bake in a moderate oven
