## Ingredients

- 1 cup salmon
- 8 biscuits
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Roll 8 biscuits
2. Mix 1 cup salmon, rolled biscuits, 1 egg, 1/2 cup milk, pepper and salt to taste together
3. Fry in butter till a rich brown
