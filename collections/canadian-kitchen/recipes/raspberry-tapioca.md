## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup tapioca
- 4 cups boiling water
- 1 pint fresh berries
- sugar to taste
- cream

## Directions

1. Put 3/4 cup tapioca into a kettle
2. Cover with 4 cups boiling water
3. Cook until transparent
4. Stir into it 1 pint of fresh berries
5. Add sugar to taste
6. Pour into a mould
7. Serve cold with cream
