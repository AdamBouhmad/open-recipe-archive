## Ingredients

- A knuckle of veal
- Three quarts of water
- A little salt
- One tablespoonful of rice
- Yolk of one egg
- One cup of cream
- A piece of butter the size of a hickory nut

## Directions

1. Put a knuckle of veal into three quarts of water, a little salt and one tablespoonful of rice
2. Boil slowly, hardly above simmering, until liquor is reduced one-half
3. Remove from the fire
4. Into a dish put the yolk of one egg; stir well into it a cup of cream; add a piece of butter the size of a hickory nut
5. Into this strain the soup boiling hot, stirring all the time
6. Just at last beat well for one minute
