## Ingredients

- 1 pound flank of mutton
- 1 small onion
- 3 slices carrot
- 3 slices turnip
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- pepper
- salt
- hot water

## Directions

1. Cut mutton into small pieces, season and place in earthen dish.
2. Add vegetables, mix butter and flour.
3. Add hot water and pour over meat, cover with boiling water, add salt to taste and cook 5 hours in aladdin pail.
