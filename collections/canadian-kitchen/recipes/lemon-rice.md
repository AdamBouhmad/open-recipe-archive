## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 2 cups water
- grated rind of 1 lemon
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- white sugar to taste
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Boil rice tender in water.
2. Make a custard of grated rind of lemon, yolks of eggs, and milk.
3. Add to rice and sweeten to taste.
4. Boil until the custard is set.
5. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth with 1 cup white sugar and juice of 1 lemon.
6. Put over the pudding and brown very slightly before serving.
