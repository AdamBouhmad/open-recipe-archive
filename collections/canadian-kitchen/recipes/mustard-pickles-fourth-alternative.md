## Ingredients

- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 1 quart small onions
- 2 quarts cucumbers
- 2 quarts cider vinegar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 ounce red pepper
- 1 ounce turmeric powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes and cucumbers up in pieces
2. Sprinkle with salt and let stand over night
3. Scald in the morning in their own pickle and drain
4. Pare onions and scald with the rest
5. While draining, let the vinegar come to a boil
6. Pour over tomatoes, cucumbers and onions
7. Let stand till next morning
8. Drain off vinegar
9. Stir to a paste sugar, mustard, turmeric and flour with a little vinegar
10. Stir well and add to vinegar
11. When hot add pickles
12. Stir and scald on top of stove
13. On no account allow it to boil
