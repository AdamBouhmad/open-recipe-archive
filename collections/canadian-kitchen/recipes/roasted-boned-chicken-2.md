## Ingredients

- chicken
- force meat
- salt
- pepper
- salt pork
- flour
- giblet or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Spread chicken on board, skin side down
2. Turn the flesh of legs and wings right side out and stuff with force meat into shape
3. Equalize the meat as well as possible, placing against the little strip of white meat only the dark meat, dredge with salt and pepper
4. Make a roll of the force meat and lay it in the chicken
5. Draw the skins up and sew it together securely
6. Turn it over, place legs and wings into the position of a trussed fowl
7. Press the body into natural shape and tie securely
8. Cover with slices of salt pork and roast, allowing 20 minutes to the pound, baste frequently
9. Remove pork last 15 minutes, dredge with flour, let brown, serve with giblet or tomato sauce
