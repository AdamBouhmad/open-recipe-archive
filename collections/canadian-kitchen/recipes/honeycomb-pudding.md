## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup beef suet chopped or 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a little salt
- spice to taste
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Steam 3 hours.
3. Serve with hard sauce made by stirring together a cup of brown sugar and 1/2 cup butter flavored with vanilla.
