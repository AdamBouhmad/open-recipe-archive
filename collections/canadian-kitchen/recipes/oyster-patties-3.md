## Ingredients

- rich puff paste
- large fresh oysters
- cloves
- mace
- nutmeg
- 1 yolk of egg, boiled hard and grated
- butter
- oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Make some rich puff paste
2. Bake in very small patty pans
3. When cool, turn out upon a large dish
4. Stew some large, fresh oysters with a few cloves, a little mace and nutmeg
5. Add the yolk of one egg, boiled hard and grated
6. Add a little butter, and as much of the oyster liquor as will cover them
7. When they have stewed a little while take them out of the pan and set them to cool
8. When quite cool, lay 2 or 3 oysters in each shell of puff paste
