## Ingredients

- 2 cups German chopped cheese
- 1/4 cup milk (sweet)
- yolks of 2 eggs
- salt and pepper
- cayenne to taste
- square slices of bread with crust removed
- butter

## Directions

1. Toast carefully square slices of bread with crust removed
2. While hot, butter them
3. Plunge into bowl of hot water
4. Place in a heated dish and stand in the oven to keep warm
5. Put the milk into a granite saucepan
6. Stand it over a moderate fire
7. When boiling hot add the cheese
8. Stir constantly until the cheese is melted
9. Add salt and pepper and yolks of eggs
10. Pour over the toasted bread
