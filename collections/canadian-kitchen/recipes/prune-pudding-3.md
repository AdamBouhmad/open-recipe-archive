## Ingredients

- One pound prunes
- Whites of four eggs beaten very stiff
- Three tablespoons white sugar
- Cream

## Directions

1. Stew prunes and remove the pits.
2. Put sugar and whites of eggs together.
3. Add prunes.
4. Bake one-half hour.
5. Serve with cream.
