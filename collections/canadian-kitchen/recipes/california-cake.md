## Ingredients

- Yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- 1 cup cold water
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs and brown sugar together
2. Add shortening, cold water, cinnamon
3. Mix soda and cream of tartar in a little water
4. Add to mixture
5. Add flour
6. Bake until done
