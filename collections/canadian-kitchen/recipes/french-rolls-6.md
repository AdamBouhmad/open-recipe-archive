## Ingredients

- 2 cups sweet milk
- 1/2 cup butter and lard mixed
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup yeast or 1/2 cake dissolved in 1/2 cup warm water
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- flour to make stiff dough

## Directions

1. Let rise over night.
2. In the morning add well-beaten eggs, knead and let rise again.
3. Make into balls the size of an egg.
4. Roll each one between the hands to make a long roll (about three inches long).
5. Place close together in even rows on well-buttered pans.
6. Cover and let rise again.
7. Glaze with sweet milk before baking.
8. Bake in a quick oven to a delicate brown.
