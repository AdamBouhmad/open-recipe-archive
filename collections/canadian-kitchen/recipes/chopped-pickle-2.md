## Ingredients

- 1 gallon green tomatoes, chopped fine
- 1 gallon cabbage
- 3/4 gallon onions
- 2 dozen large green peppers
- 1 dozen large red peppers
- salt
- 1 pint white mustard seed
- 1 gallon vinegar
- 1 1/2 pounds brown sugar

## Directions

1. Chop all vegetables fine
2. Put all together in a vessel
3. Cover with salt
4. Let stand over night
5. Next morning put all in a bag and drain 24 hours
6. After draining, add white mustard seed
7. Boil vinegar with brown sugar
8. While boiling hot pour over chopped ingredients
9. Put all on the stove together and cook a few minutes
