## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups milk
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- pinch salt
- flavoring
- whites of 2 small eggs

## Directions

1. Put 2 cups milk to heat in a double boiler
2. Mix the cornstarch, sugar, salt and remaining milk, and stir them into the scalding milk
3. Stir constantly until it thickens, then cover closely and cook 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
4. Beat the egg whites stiff
5. add the flavoring to the milk mixture, and then stir in the egg whites
6. Mix thoroughly but quickly, and stir as little as possible
7. Pour at once into a wet mould and set away to chill thoroughly before turning it out to serve
8. Serve with a custard sauce made with the egg yolks, or with a red syrup from canned fruit such as cherries or raspberries
