## Ingredients

- ground nuts, almonds, brazils and walnuts
- beaten yolks of 2 eggs
- a little cream
- puff or good short pastry shells

## Directions

1. Mix ground nuts, almonds, brazils and walnuts with the beaten yolks of 2 eggs and a little cream
2. Sweeten slightly
3. Pour into shells of puff or good short pastry
4. Bake in a quick oven
