## Ingredients

- 4 cups warm water
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- a little salt
- coarse Graham flour
- bread flour

## Directions

1. Add 1 cup molasses, 1 White Swan Yeast Cake, and a little salt to 4 cups warm water
2. Add enough coarse Graham flour to make a thick batter
3. Set to rise
4. When light, stir in enough of bread flour to knead on board
5. Shape in loaves
6. Put in pans
7. When light bake about one hour in a medium hot oven
