## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- water
- toast
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the tough, hard end from the asparagus
2. Wash carefully
3. Tie in bunches
4. Set the bunches on end in a saucepan of boiling, salted water, having the tender ends above the water
5. Cover and boil gently for 1 hour
6. Drain
7. Slightly moisten pieces of toast with a little of the water in which asparagus was cooked
8. Spread with butter
9. Arrange these on a hot dish
10. Untie the bunches of asparagus and place them on the toast
11. Put a little butter over the tender ends of the stalks and serve very hot
