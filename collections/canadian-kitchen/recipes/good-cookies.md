## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour
- granulated sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat well together the sugar, butter and egg, then add cream with soda in it.
2. Add to this sufficient flour to make nice soft dough, roll thin.
3. Sprinkle granulated sugar over top of them before going into oven.
4. Bake lightly.
