## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar (0.75 pound for every pound of fruit)
- water (1 pint to 1.5 pounds)
- brandied paper

## Directions

1. Stone the fruit and weigh it
2. Dissolve sugar in water, a pint to the pound and a half
3. Add fruit and let it boil as fast as possible for 0.5 hour, till it begins to jelly
4. As soon as it begins to thicken, put in pots or earthen jars and cover with brandied paper
5. Next the fruit and then cover closely from the air
