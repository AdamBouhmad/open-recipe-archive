## Ingredients

- One lemon, juice and rind grated
- yolks of two eggs
- one cup of water
- one cup of sugar
- one heaping teaspoonful of cornstarch

## Directions

1. Put lemon juice and rind grated, yolks of two eggs, one cup of water, one cup of sugar, and one heaping teaspoonful of cornstarch in a double boiler and boil thick.
2. Save the whites for top.
