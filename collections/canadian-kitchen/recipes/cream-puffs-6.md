## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 3 unbeaten eggs

## Directions

1. Boil water and butter together
2. When boiling, stir in 1 cup flour
3. When almost cold stir in 3 unbeaten eggs
4. Drop on buttered tins
5. Bake 25 minutes
6. Fill with whipped cream
