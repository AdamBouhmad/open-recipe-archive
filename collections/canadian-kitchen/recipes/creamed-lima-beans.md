## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried Lima beans
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak over night
2. drain and cook slowly in salted water until soft
3. drain, add cream, salt and pepper
4. Re-heat before serving
5. When cooked they may be popped from their skins before adding the cream
