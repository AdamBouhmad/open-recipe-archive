## Ingredients

- cold meat
- bread crumbs
- 1 well-beaten egg
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Mince fine any pieces of cold meat
2. Add bread crumbs, enough for meal, and 1 well-beaten egg
3. Press together
4. Fry in slices in hot fat
