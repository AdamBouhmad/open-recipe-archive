## Ingredients

- 3 pts. pumpkin pulp
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 tablespoonful ginger
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 qts. milk
- pie crust

## Directions

1. Make crust and line pie dish.
2. Cook pumpkin pulp, flour, eggs, sugar, ginger, salt and milk all together until well thickened.
3. Bake in a moderate oven one hour.
