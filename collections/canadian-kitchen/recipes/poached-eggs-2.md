## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Break as many eggs as desired, one at a time.
2. Drop carefully into a spider filled with boiling water.
3. When the whites of the eggs are well set, slip a spoon carefully under and take out.
4. Lay a small piece of butter on each egg, a slight dash of pepper, and serve immediately.
