## Ingredients

- purified, shredded codfish
- vinegar
- mashed potato
- egg
- pepper
- flour
- fat for deep frying

## Directions

1. Soak codfish for two or three hours
2. Boil for fifteen minutes in water that has had a tablespoonful of vinegar stirred into it
3. Spread upon a sieve to get cold
4. Allow to each cupful of fish half as much mashed potato whipped to a soft cream
5. Mix them together well
6. Make very hot over the fire and beat in a frothed egg for every cupful of fish
7. Season with pepper
8. Let the mixture get quite cold
9. Make into balls
10. Roll in flour
11. Set in a cold place to stiffen
12. Roll again in flour
13. Fry in deep fat to a yellow-brown color
