## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup sour milk
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- butter size of a walnut
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour enough to thicken

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Steam 1 hour
