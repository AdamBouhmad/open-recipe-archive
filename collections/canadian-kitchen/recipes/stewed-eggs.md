## Ingredients

- 2 Spanish onions
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1/2 pt. milk or cream
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry onions, sliced in butter in a covered pan till very tender, but do not brown
2. Add flour and mix well
3. Add milk and stir until thickened
4. Season
5. Add eggs cut in quarters
6. Simmer ten minutes
7. Serve with garnish of fried strips of bread
