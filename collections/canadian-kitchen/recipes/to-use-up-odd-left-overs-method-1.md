## Ingredients

- meat scraps
- butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- pepper
- salt
- 1 small onion

## Directions

1. Cut all meat off the bones and chop fine
2. If fat put in frying-pan and let fry till brown; if not fat, add a little lump of butter
3. When brown add a tablespoon flour, pepper and salt to taste
4. Last, add 1 small onion
5. Cook for a few minutes
