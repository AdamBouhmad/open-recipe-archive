## Ingredients

- summer squashes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- 1 tablespoon cream

## Directions

1. Cook squashes whole
2. When tender, if large, skin and remove the seeds; if small, this will not be necessary
3. Drain and press the water out with a plate
4. Put in a stewpan and season well with butter, pepper and salt
5. Add a tablespoon of cream
