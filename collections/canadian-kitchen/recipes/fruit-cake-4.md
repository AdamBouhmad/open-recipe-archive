## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 2 pounds raisins
- 2 pounds currants
- 1/2 pound mixed peel
- 3/8 pound almonds
- 8 eggs
- spices to taste
- soda

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add eggs gradually
3. Add spices, fruit and flour
4. Beat well
5. Add peel and almonds
6. Just before baking add the soda
