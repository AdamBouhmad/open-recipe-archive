## Ingredients

- 3 potatoes
- 1 cup of cream
- 1 egg
- 1 pint of flour
- chopped parsley
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pare and slice potatoes
2. Boil them until they can be rubbed through a colander
3. Add cream
4. Rub egg into flour and sprinkle it into the boiling soup
5. Flavor with chopped parsley
6. Add butter, salt and pepper to taste
