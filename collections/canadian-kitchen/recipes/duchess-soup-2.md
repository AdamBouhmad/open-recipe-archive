## Ingredients

- One pint white stock
- One pint brown stock
- Salt and cayenne
- Four teaspoonfuls rice-flour
- Yolks of two eggs
- Half a cup of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Strain stock and pour over well beaten yolks of two eggs
2. Soup must not be allowed to boil after this
3. Before serving add half a cup of whipped cream
