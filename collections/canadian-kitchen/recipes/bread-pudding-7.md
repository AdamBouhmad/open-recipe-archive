## Ingredients

- Pieces of bread or toast
- 2 eggs
- sugar to taste
- milk to fill dish

## Directions

1. Put milk in dish
2. Add beaten eggs
3. Add sugar to taste
4. Put in toast or bread
5. When done, sprinkle with white sugar and nutmeg
