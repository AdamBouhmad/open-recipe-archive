## Ingredients

- 2 gallons cabbage chopped fine
- 1 gallon green tomatoes chopped fine and drained
- 12 large onions chopped fine
- 1.5 pounds brown sugar
- 1/2 pound mustard seed
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1 ounce cloves
- 1 ounce allspice
- 1 ounce pepper
- 1 ounce tumeric
- 1 gill salt
- 1 gallon good vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil 30 minutes slowly
