## Ingredients

- turnips
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- cottolene
- beaten egg
- cracker-dust

## Directions

1. Pare and slice crosswise a quarter of an inch thick.
2. Lay in ice-cold water half an hour, then cook tender, but not too soft, in boiling water without salt.
3. Drain, lay upon a soft cloth until dry and lukewarm, sprinkle with pepper and salt, flour, and fry in hot cottolene; or, dip in beaten egg, then in cracker-dust, and fry.
