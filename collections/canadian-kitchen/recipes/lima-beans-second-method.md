## Ingredients

- lima beans
- boiling water
- salt
- butter (a lump half the size of an egg)
- pepper

## Directions

1. After shelling, cook about half an hour in boiling water with a little salt.
2. Drain dry, and after dishing stir in a lump of butter half the size of an egg and pepper and salt to taste.
