## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup golden syrup
- 1/4 cup suet
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking-powder
- 2 teaspoonfuls ginger
- water

## Directions

1. Mix with water to a thin batter.
2. Steam two hours and eat with sweet sauce.
