## Ingredients

- 6 pounds shredded pineapple
- 2 pounds granulated sugar
- 3 pints water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of 2 pounds granulated sugar and 3 pints water
2. Boil 5 minutes, then skim
3. Add the fruit
4. Boil 5 minutes more and seal while hot
