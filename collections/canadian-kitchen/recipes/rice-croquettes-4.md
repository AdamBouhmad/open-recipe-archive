## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup of milk
- 1 generous tablespoonful of butter
- 1 heaping teaspoonful of sugar
- 1 1/2 cups of boiled rice
- 1 saltspoonful of powdered cinnamon or nutmeg
- salt
- 1 beaten egg
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Put three-quarters of a cup of milk in a sauce-pan over the fire with a generous tablespoonful of butter, a heaping teaspoonful of sugar, and, when it comes to a boil, add a cup and a half of boiled rice, a saltspoonful of powdered cinnamon or nutmeg, and salt to taste.
2. Mix well, let it come to a boil.
3. Add a beaten egg, remove from the fire, turn into a plate to get cold, form into cylinders and fry in hot butter.
