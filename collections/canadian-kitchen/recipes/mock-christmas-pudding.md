## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint milk
- stale bread
- 1 cup currants
- butter the size of egg
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- pinch of salt
- 2 tablespoons molasses or table syrup
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 pint milk and pour over some stale bread
2. Add 1 cup currants, butter the size of egg, 1/2 cup brown sugar, pinch of salt, 2 tablespoons molasses or table syrup, 1 teaspoon baking soda, and spice to taste
3. Steam for 2 hours
4. Serve with or without sauce
