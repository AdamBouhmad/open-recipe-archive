## Ingredients

- large tomatoes
- stewed celery
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- salt and pepper
- browned bread crumbs
- hot buttered toast

## Directions

1. Cut out circular pieces from stem end of large tomatoes
2. Remove part of pulp
3. Fill tomatoes with stewed celery to which has been added 1 cup sweet cream, 1 tablespoon melted butter, season to taste
4. Cover the openings with browned bread crumbs
5. Bake 3/4 hour in a moderate oven
6. Serve on rounds of hot buttered toast
