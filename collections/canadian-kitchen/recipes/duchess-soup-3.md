## Ingredients

- 1 quart white stock
- 2 slices carrot and onion
- 2 blades of mace
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 2 cups milk
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Cook some of vegetables in some of butter 3 minutes
2. Add stock and mace
3. Simmer 15 minutes, strain into white sauce made of rest of butter, flour and milk
4. Season, add cheese
