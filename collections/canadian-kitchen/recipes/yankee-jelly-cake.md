## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup brown syrup
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 eggs
- lemon filling
- maple cream icing
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- butter size of egg
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix brown sugar, butter, brown syrup, buttermilk, ginger, soda, cinnamon and eggs
2. Bake in layers
3. Use lemon filling and maple cream icing
4. For icing: combine brown sugar, sweet milk, and butter size of egg
5. Flavor with vanilla and let boil until it begins to thread
6. Beat until it begins to cool
