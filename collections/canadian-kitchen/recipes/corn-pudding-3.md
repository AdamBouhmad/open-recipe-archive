## Ingredients

- 1 pint canned corn chopped fine
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 2 cups rich sweet milk
- 1 small teaspoon sugar
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Mix corn, cornstarch, eggs, milk, sugar, pepper and salt together
2. Bake until set
