## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- Butter size of an egg
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Stir sugar and butter to a cream
2. Add sweet milk
3. Add flour, cream of tartar, and soda
4. Mix together
5. Bake until done
