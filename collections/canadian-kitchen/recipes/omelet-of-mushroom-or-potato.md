## Ingredients

- eggs
- cream
- mushroom or potato

## Directions

1. Make an ordinary egg and cream omelet
2. As soon as it is set sprinkle the finely chopped scalded mushroom or hot-cooked potato cut fine
3. Fold the omelet over once
4. Dish immediately
