## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 teaspoon each soda, cinnamon, ginger, allspice and salt
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 3/2 cup sweet milk
- 3/2 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Sift all together, add 1/4 cup molasses and 3/2 cup each sweet milk and raisins
2. Steam 3 hours and serve warm with cream or sauce
