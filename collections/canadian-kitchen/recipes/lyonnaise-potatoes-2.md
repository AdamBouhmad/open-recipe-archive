## Ingredients

- one and a half tablespoonfuls of butter
- a scant tablespoonful of chopped onion
- two cupfuls of cold boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put one and a half tablespoonfuls of butter in a frying pan.
2. When melted add a scant tablespoonful of chopped onion; let it slightly color.
3. Add two cupfuls of cold boiled potatoes cut into dice.
4. Stir until the potato has absorbed all the butter, and become slightly browned.
5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a tablespoonful of chopped parsley.
6. Mix well, and serve very hot.
