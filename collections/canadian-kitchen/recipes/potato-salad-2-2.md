## Ingredients

- 1 cup vinegar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon each of salt and pepper
- 4 onions chopped
- enough cold cooked potatoes fine to make about 2 quarts
- 3 eggs boiled and sliced

## Directions

1. Put vinegar, 2 eggs, flour, mustard, sugar, salt and pepper all together on the stove and cook until it boils
2. Chop 4 onions and enough cold cooked potatoes fine to make about 2 quarts
3. Cool the dressing and pour over the potatoes
4. Boil 3 eggs and slice and lay over top of salad
