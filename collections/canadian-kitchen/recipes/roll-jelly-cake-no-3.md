## Ingredients

- 4 eggs well beaten
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- pinch of salt
- jelly

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well
2. Add sugar and flour
3. Add baking powder and salt
4. Bake in long pan
5. Spread jelly on while hot
