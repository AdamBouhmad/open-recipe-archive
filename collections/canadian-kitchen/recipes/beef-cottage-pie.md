## Ingredients

- old beef scraps
- small onion
- salt and pepper
- gravy or butter
- boiling water
- mashed potatoes
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop beef scraps fine with a small onion, salt and pepper
2. Place meat in a deep dish moistened with gravy or a little butter dissolved in boiling water
3. Put enough mashed potatoes over the top to make a thick cover over the meat
4. Put a few bits of butter over the potatoes before putting into the oven
5. Bake a light brown
