## Ingredients

- five grains of oil of anise-seed
- two grains of peppermint
- half an ounce of lump sugar
- a drachm of magnesia

## Directions

1. Drop five grains of oil of anise-seed and two of peppermint on half an ounce of lump sugar
2. Rub it in a mortar, with a drachm of magnesia, into a fine powder
3. A small quantity of this may be given in a little water at any time
