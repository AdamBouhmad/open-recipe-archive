## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- oil
- maitre d'hotel sauce
- hot Mayonnaise
- Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes horizontally in two; leave the skins on.
2. Place them on a broiler with the skin side down; dust with salt and pepper, and broil without turning, over a moderate fire, fifteen to twenty minutes, or until tender.
3. Lay them on a hot dish, and spread each piece with either butter, oil, maitre d'hotel sauce, hot Mayonnaise or Bearnaise.
4. Alternatively, the tomatoes may be cut into thick slices, covered with oil, and then broiled, turning frequently.
