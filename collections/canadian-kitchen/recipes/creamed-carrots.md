## Ingredients

- lard
- 1 onion
- carrots
- salt and pepper
- water
- 3/4 cup sweet cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Put in your pot a little lard; when hot slice in 1 onion
2. Put in carrots, salt and pepper, cover with water, let boil till tender
3. Add 3/4 cup sweet cream, mixed with a little flour
4. If too thick add a little hot water
