## Ingredients

- 1 quart beets
- 1 pint celery
- 1 small head cabbage
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- 3/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil and chop fine the beets
2. Chop fine the celery
3. Chop fine the cabbage
4. Mix all ingredients
5. Cover with cold vinegar
