## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- butter size of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
- handful of currants (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix flour, soda, cream tartar, salt, and butter
2. Add milk and egg
3. Add sugar and currants if wished sweet
4. Bake
