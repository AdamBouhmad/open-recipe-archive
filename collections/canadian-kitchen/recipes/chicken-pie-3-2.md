## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- chicken broth
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Cook chicken until tender, add salt and pepper to taste
2. Place the chicken flat in a stone baking dish
3. Make a gravy of chicken broth, not enough to cover
4. For Crust: Mix 2 cups of flour, sifted, with 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 3 tablespoons butter mixed with flour
5. Beat 1 egg, add to it 1 cup sweet milk, stir into flour
6. Dip in spoonfuls over chicken
7. Bake in moderate oven from 1/2 to 3/4 hour
