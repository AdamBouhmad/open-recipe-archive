## Ingredients

- stale cake
- butter
- a large cup preserved pineapple
- 1 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Butter a baking-dish and line it with stale cake, take a large cup preserved pineapple and put in with more cake, in layers, with cake on top
2. Pour over this a cup of cold water, cover with a plate and bake slowly for 2 hours
3. Serve with sauce
