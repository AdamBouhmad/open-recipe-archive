## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon mixed spices
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 3/4 cup lard
- 1 dessertspoon soda
- hot water
- flour
- 1 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, sugar, molasses, cloves, mixed spices, and ginger together
2. Add lard
3. Dissolve soda in hot water and add
4. Stir in flour until stiff as sponge cake
5. Add 1 cup boiling water last
