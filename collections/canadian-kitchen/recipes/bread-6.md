## Ingredients

- 4 quarts warm water
- 3 level tablespoons salt
- 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes
- flour
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. At night put in the bake dish 4 quarts warm water, 3 level tablespoons salt, 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes, mix all together with enough good flour to make a thin batter.
2. Next morning, when nice and light, put in 1 cup sugar, mix stiff and knead a few minutes, let rise and make into loaves.
3. Let them rise and bake in moderate oven for one hour.
