## Ingredients

- large red apples
- hot water
- 1 cup sugar
- orange or lemon rind
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Select large red apples, wash or wipe clean as many as will set in bottom of pudding dish or saucepan
2. Pour in hot water until about half-way up the apples
3. Boil on top of range, taking care to turn often, pricking with a fork to keep the skin as whole as possible
4. When cooked, take from fire, and when slightly cooled remove the skins carefully and arrange in a pretty dish
5. To the water add 1 cup sugar and boil down to about 1 cup
6. If an orange or lemon is handy some of the rind grated is an improvement
7. Pour over apples
8. Serve when cold with whipped cream
