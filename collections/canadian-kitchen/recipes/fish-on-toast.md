## Ingredients

- 2 cups codfish or any cold leftover fish
- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Pepper
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- Buttered toast

## Directions

1. Heat milk to boiling point
2. Add cornstarch, butter and pepper, cook for 5 minutes
3. Prepare buttered toast, place fish on it
4. Cover with the milk mixture and garnish with sliced egg
