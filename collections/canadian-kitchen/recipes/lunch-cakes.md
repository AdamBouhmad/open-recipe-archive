## Ingredients

- 1 qt. flour
- 2 heaping tablespoonfuls butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup currants
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- baking powder

## Directions

1. Into flour rub butter.
2. Add sugar, baking powder and currants.
3. Beat eggs, add to them one cupful of milk and stir into the dry mixture, adding more milk, if it is necessary, to mix to a soft dough.
4. Roll out as for biscuits; cut into three-inch squares.
5. Rub the top of each with a mixture of milk and sugar.
6. Bake in a hot oven.
7. Split, butter and serve while hot.
