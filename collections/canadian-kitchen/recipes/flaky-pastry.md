## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1½ to ¾ cup butter
- Ice-cold water

## Directions

1. Rub 2 ounces of the butter into the flour
2. Cut the remainder into ¼-inch dice, and stir through the flour
3. Mix the water in with a broad-bladed knife, using only enough to hold the flour and butter together in shreds
4. Turn the mixture out on the board in a pile, pack it together with the hands, and roll out into a sheet
5. Use a broad-bladed knife, and fold it into three or four layers, tucking in all the loose shreds about the edges
6. Roll out again
7. Fold and roll out again
8. Fold in half and set away to chill, if necessary, before rolling it out to line pie-plates, etc.
