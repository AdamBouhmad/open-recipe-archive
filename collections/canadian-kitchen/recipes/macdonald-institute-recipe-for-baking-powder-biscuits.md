## Ingredients

- 4 level cups flour
- 1 level teaspoon salt
- 2 level tablespoons shortening
- 2 1/2 level tablespoons baking powder
- about 1 1/2 cups milk

## Directions

1. Place the board, mixing knife, rolling pin, cutter, and pan ready
2. See that the oven is hot
3. Sift the salt and flour into a bowl
4. Rub the shortening into it with the fingers
5. Sift in the baking powder, and mix well
6. Mix into a soft dough with the milk, using a broad-bladed, flexible knife, in order to cut and mix it quickly
7. Flour the board lightly, turn the dough out, and roll it round to coat it with flour
8. Knead just enough to make the dough smooth
9. Roll out about an inch thick, and cut into small biscuits
10. Place them in the pan, wet the tops only with milk or water, and bake in a hot oven about 20 minutes
