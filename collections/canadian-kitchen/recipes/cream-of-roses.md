## Ingredients

- Olive oil, one pound
- Attar of roses, fifty drops
- Oil of rosemary, twenty-five drops
- Alkanet root for coloring

## Directions

1. Mix olive oil, attar of roses, and oil of rosemary
2. Color it with alkanet root
