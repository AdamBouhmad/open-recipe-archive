## Ingredients

- 2 eggs (whites only)
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar (white)
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix butter and sugar together
2. Add milk and flour
3. Add lastly beaten egg whites
4. Flavor
5. Bake
