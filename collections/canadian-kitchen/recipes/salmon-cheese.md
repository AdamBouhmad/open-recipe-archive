## Ingredients

- 1 can good salmon
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup cream
- 6 soda biscuits
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Roll 6 soda biscuits fine
2. Mix 1 can good salmon, 4 eggs, 1 cup cream, rolled biscuits, pepper and salt and a little vinegar to taste together
3. Steam 2 hours
4. When cold slice down and serve
