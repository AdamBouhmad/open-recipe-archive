## Ingredients

- eggs
- cold fish
- cream
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a plain omelet
2. When ready to fold, spread over it fish prepared as follows
3. Add to a cupful of any kind of cold fish, broken fine, cream enough to moisten it
4. Season with a tablespoonful of butter, then pepper and salt to taste
5. Warm together
