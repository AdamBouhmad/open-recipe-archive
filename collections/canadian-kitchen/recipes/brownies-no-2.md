## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 cups bran
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup melted butter
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 2 eggs slightly beaten
- 2 tablespoons sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- dates

## Directions

1. mix all together like pie crust and cut in rounds and place dates between
