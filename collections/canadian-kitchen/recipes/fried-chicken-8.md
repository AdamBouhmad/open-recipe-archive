## Ingredients

- good plump chicken
- deep hot fat
- 1 large potato
- 1 large onion
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut chicken in half lengthwise and cut each half across to make four pieces of the body
2. Cut off the legs and open them to the bone
3. Place the fowl in the frying pan in deep hot fat
4. Slice over it 1 large potato and 1 large onion
5. Season well with pepper and salt
6. Cover and fry gently for 20 minutes or 3/4 an hour, turning meat to brown on both sides
