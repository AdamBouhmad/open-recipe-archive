## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 heaping cup shortening
- ¼ cup sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- seasoning to taste
- currants optional

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar, shortening, sweet milk, and vinegar together
2. Add soda and baking powder
3. Season to taste
4. Stir all the flour you can in with a spoon before taking on the board
5. Have them stiff enough not to stick to board
6. Optional: add currants to part of the dough
7. Bake
