## Ingredients

- cold roast mutton
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- oysters
- bread crumbs
- tomato juice or brown gravy
- cold boiled macaroni

## Directions

1. Remove fat and skin from cold roast mutton, cut in small, thin slices, season with pepper and salt.
2. Butter a small low dish, put in a layer of meat, then a layer of oysters, strained and seasoned, then a layer of bread crumbs, then a second layer of meat, oysters and crumbs for the top.
3. Strew a spoonful of butter cut in small pieces over the top, and moisten the whole with tomato juice or brown gravy.
4. Cold boiled macaroni, cut in inch pieces, may be used instead of oysters.
