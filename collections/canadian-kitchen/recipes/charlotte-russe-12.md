## Ingredients

- One pint cream
- one-quarter cup sugar
- whites of two eggs
- one teaspoonful granulated gelatine
- lady fingers

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in as little boiling water as possible.
2. Whip cream and beat eggs very light.
3. Add all together and pour into mould lined with lady fingers.
