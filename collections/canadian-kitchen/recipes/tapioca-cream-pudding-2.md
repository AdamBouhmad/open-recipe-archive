## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons tapioca
- 1 cup water
- 1 pint sweet milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- whites of 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- pinch salt
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Soak 4 tablespoons tapioca in 1 cup water over night
2. Add 1 pint sweet milk, yolks of 2 eggs, 3/4 cup sugar, pinch salt, and flavor with vanilla
3. Boil till thick
4. Heap beaten whites on top
