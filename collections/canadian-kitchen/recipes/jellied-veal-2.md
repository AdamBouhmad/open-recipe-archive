## Ingredients

- A shank of veal
- Water
- Salt and pepper
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cover with water and cook a shank of veal slowly until the meat comes easily from the bones
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Meanwhile hard-boil two eggs and chill in cold water
4. Moisten a mould, cut the eggs in slices and lay in the bottom
5. Take out the bones and gristle and pour the remainder on the eggs
6. Put in a cool place, or on ice, and it will jelly quickly
