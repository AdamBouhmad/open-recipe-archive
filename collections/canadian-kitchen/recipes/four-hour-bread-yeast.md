## Ingredients

- 12 medium-sized potatoes
- 4 heaping tablespoons flour
- 2 heaping tablespoons salt
- 2 heaping tablespoons sugar
- potato water
- 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes
- warm water

## Directions

1. Boil 12 medium-sized potatoes, have ready in a 2-gallon crock 4 heaping tablespoons flour, 2 heaping tablespoons salt, 2 heaping tablespoons sugar.
2. Scald with potato water, mash potatoes through colander, then add enough water to make about 1 1/2 gallons, lukewarm.
3. Add 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes which have been soaked in a little warm water.
4. Set in a warm place to rise.
