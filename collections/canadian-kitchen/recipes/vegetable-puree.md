## Ingredients

- onions
- carrots
- potatoes (boiled first)
- beans of any kind
- parsnips
- celery
- peas
- leek
- turnips
- cauliflower
- 1 teacup dripping or stock-fat
- 1 large spoonful flour
- 1 pint hot water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut up a large plateful of any and all kinds of vegetables, having potatoes or beans for thickening
2. Put teacup of dripping or stock-fat into saucepan and when very hot add sliced onions
3. Stir well to prevent burning, and when onions are red stir in large spoonful of flour till it is the same color
4. Stir in pint of hot water and some pepper and salt
5. Add the rest of the vegetables and let them simmer, adding more hot water as necessary, for two hours
6. Press them through a colander
7. Return them to the range and let them simmer till the moment of serving
