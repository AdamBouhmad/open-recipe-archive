## Ingredients

- 1 cup of mashed potatoes
- 1 pint of hot milk
- 1 cup white sauce
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- onion juice

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup of mashed potatoes with 1 pint of hot milk
2. Add 1 cup white sauce
3. Flavor with salt, pepper, celery salt and onion juice
