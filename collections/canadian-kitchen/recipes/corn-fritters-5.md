## Ingredients

- 1 can corn
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup flour
- milk to make thick batter

## Directions

1. Mix the corn, yolks of eggs, salt, pepper together, and add the flour sifted with baking powder
2. Add milk, beat well
3. Fold in whites, drop by spoonful into hot fat
