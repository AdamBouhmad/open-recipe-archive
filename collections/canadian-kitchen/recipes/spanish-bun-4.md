## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- little nutmeg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients and mix
2. Bake
