## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup buttermilk
- ½ cup molasses
- ½ teaspoon allspice
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- ¾ teaspoon butter
- water
- 1 cup seeded raisins

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 layers
2. For filling and icing: boil 1 cup granulated sugar, ¾ teaspoon butter, and water till it strings
3. Add 1 cup seeded raisins cut up fine
4. Partly cool, then spread some between the layers, and put the rest on the top of cake
