## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoonfuls sugar
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of grated nutmeg
- good sweet milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Break an egg into a coffee-cup
2. Put in two teaspoonfuls of sugar
3. Beat it up thoroughly with a pinch of salt and a pinch of grated nutmeg
4. Fill up the cup with good sweet milk
5. Turn it into another cup, well buttered
6. Set it in a pan of boiling water, reaching nearly to the top of the cup
7. Set in the oven
8. When the custard is set it is done
