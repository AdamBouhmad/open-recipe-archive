## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 6 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix egg, melted butter, and sweet milk
2. Sift flour with 3 teaspoons baking powder and add salt
3. Combine ingredients
