## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 heaping teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix cinnamon with flour and baking powder
2. Combine all ingredients and mix
