## Ingredients

- fruit (4 pounds)
- sugar (1.5 pounds)
- vinegar (pints)
- cloves (1 tablespoon)
- cinnamon (1 tablespoon)
- black pepper (1 tablespoon)
- salt (1 teaspoon)
- ginger

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients and cook until thick
