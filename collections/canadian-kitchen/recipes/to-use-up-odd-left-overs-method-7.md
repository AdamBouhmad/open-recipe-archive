## Ingredients

- cold meat
- cold potatoes
- onion
- bread
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- biscuit crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop cold meat, a few cold potatoes, an onion and a little bread
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Roll in balls
4. Dip in well-beaten egg and biscuit crumbs
5. Fry in butter
