## Ingredients

- 6 pounds ripe cucumbers, peeled and seeded
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and seed cucumbers
2. Combine with sugar, cloves, allspice, cinnamon, and black pepper
3. Add salt
4. Cover with vinegar
5. Boil together till tender
