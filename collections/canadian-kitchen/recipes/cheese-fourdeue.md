## Ingredients

- 1 cup stale crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 pound grated cheese
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- speck of cayenne pepper
- 1 yolk of egg
- toast

## Directions

1. Soak crumbs 6 hours in 1 cup milk
2. Cook over hot water
3. Melt butter, add soaked crumbs mixed with egg, then seasonings and cheese
4. Stir constantly
5. When smooth and hot serve on toast
