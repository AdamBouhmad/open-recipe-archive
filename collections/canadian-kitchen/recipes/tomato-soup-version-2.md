## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 medium-sized ripe tomatoes
- butter the size of an egg
- pinch of soda
- 1 quart sweet milk
- 1/2 cup sweet cream
- pepper
- salt
- broken biscuits

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter tomatoes
2. Put butter in a soup kettle and let it get hot
3. Add tomatoes and let boil till tender
4. When tender add a pinch of soda to prevent curdling
5. Add sweet milk and sweet cream
6. Add pepper and salt to taste
7. Add enough broken biscuits to thicken nicely
8. Let boil 3 minutes and serve hot
