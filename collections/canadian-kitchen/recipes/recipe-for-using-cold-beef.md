## Ingredients

- cold boiled or roasted beef
- 3 carrots
- 1 large onion
- dripping or butter
- 1 cup hot water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take cold boiled or roasted beef and chop with food chopper or a knife into small bits
2. Take 3 carrots and 1 large onion and cut up fine
3. Have the frying pan hot with good dripping or butter, and 1 cup of hot water
4. Drop in the vegetables and boil until tender, without the addition of more water
5. Add the meat and pepper and salt to taste
6. If any gravy left over, add when the meat is put in
7. If no gravy is on hand, water may be added, just in proportion as required, either for hash or a soup
