## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup pork fryings
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- nutmeg for flavoring
- flour enough to roll out thin

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in sour milk
2. Add flour enough to roll out thin with baking powder
3. Flavor with nutmeg and bake in hot oven
