## Ingredients

- 1 heaping cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sour milk
- a small teaspoon soda
- as little flour as will roll them
- vanilla or lemon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix butter, sugar, eggs, and sour milk together
2. Add soda and flour
3. Roll out thin
4. Flavor with vanilla or lemon
5. Bake
