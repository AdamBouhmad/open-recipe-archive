## Ingredients

- 2 cups Graham flour
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter and lard
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- enough sour milk to roll out thin
- 2 pounds chopped dates

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, butter and lard with soda and enough sour milk to roll out thin
2. Spread over 2 pounds chopped dates
3. Put 1 layer on top of dates
4. Bake
