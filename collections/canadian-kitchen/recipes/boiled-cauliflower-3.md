## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- hot, salted water
- a tablespoonful of vinegar
- a cupful of drawn butter
- lemon-juice
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower, tied in a net, in plenty of hot, salted water, in which has been stirred a tablespoonful of vinegar
2. When done, drain and dish, the flower upward
3. Pour over it a cupful of drawn butter seasoned with lemon-juice, pepper, and salt
4. Serve very hot
