## Ingredients

- 4 pints butter beans
- salted water
- 3 pints vinegar
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 dessertspoon celery seed
- 1 dessertspoon turmeric powder
- 3/4 cup mustard
- 1 cup flour
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil 4 pints butter beans in salted water
2. drain
3. prepare mixture of 3 pints vinegar, 1 pound brown sugar, boil together
4. add 1 dessertspoon celery seed, 1 dessertspoon turmeric powder, 3/4 cup mustard, 1 cup flour
5. mix together with cold vinegar
6. add to boiling vinegar and sugar
7. boil all for 5 minutes, stirring constantly
