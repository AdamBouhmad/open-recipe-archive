## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup currants
- 1 quart flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Use more flour if necessary to make it stiff.
2. Roll thin and bake in a pretty hot oven.
