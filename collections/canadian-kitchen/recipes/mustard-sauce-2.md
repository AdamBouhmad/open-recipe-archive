## Ingredients

- 12 ripe tomatoes
- 12 onions
- 12 apples
- a little salt
- 3/4 pound best mustard
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 cups granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes, onions, and apples well
2. Put through colander
3. Add 3/4 pound best mustard, 1 quart vinegar, and 2 cups granulated sugar
4. Boil 10 minutes
