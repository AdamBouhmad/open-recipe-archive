## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. cheese
- 1/4 cup cream or milk
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- Salt and cayenne
- 4 slices of toast

## Directions

1. Break the cheese in small pieces, or if hard grate it
2. Put it with the milk in a double boiler
3. Toast the bread and keep it hot
4. Mix the mustard, salt and pepper; add the egg, and beat well
5. When the cheese is melted, stir in the egg and butter, and cook two minutes, or until it thickens a little, but do not let it curdle
6. Pour it over the toast
