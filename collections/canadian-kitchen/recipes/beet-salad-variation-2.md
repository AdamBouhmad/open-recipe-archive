## Ingredients

- 1 quart boiled beets
- 1 pint celery
- 1 pint cabbage
- 1 cup horseradish
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon red pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop all ingredients
2. cover with vinegar and keep from air
