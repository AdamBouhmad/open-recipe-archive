## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- cold water
- boiling water
- 5 tablespoons sugar
- whites of 3 eggs
- teaspoon salt
- vanilla
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 2 cups sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Take 4 tablespoons cornstarch and wet with a little cold water
2. Pour in boiling water to thicken
3. Stir in 5 tablespoons sugar
4. Have whites of 3 eggs beaten to stiff froth
5. Beat all together and add teaspoon salt and some vanilla
6. Set away to cool
7. Make sauce with yolks of 3 eggs, 2 cups sweet milk, 1 tablespoon cornstarch
8. Sweeten and put in a dish and boil in water
9. Flavor with vanilla
10. Serve with sauce
