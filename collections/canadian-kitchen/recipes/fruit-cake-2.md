## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- 0.75 lb. butter
- 1 cup molasses
- 10 eggs
- 2 lbs. seeded raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1 lb. citron
- 0.5 lb. almonds
- 0.5 oz. mace
- 0.5 teaspoonful rose water
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 tablespoonful each cloves and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and butter together
2. Scorch the flour
3. Mix all together
4. Cook till done
5. Try with a straw
