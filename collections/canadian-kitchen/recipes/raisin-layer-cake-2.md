## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 cups chopped raisins
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar, butter, and sour milk
2. Add soda
3. Add chopped raisins
4. Add flour, cloves and cinnamon
5. Bake in layers
