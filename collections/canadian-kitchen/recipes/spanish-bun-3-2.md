## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 3/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3/4 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons mixed spice
- Whites of 2 eggs for icing
- 3/4 cup brown sugar for icing

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients and mix
2. For icing: beat whites of 2 eggs with 3/4 cup brown sugar until stiff
3. Apply icing to cake
