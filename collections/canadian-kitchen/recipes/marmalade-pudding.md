## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls marmalade
- 3 cups breadcrumbs
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup suet
- Pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Steam and serve with sauce
