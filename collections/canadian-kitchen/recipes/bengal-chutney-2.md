## Ingredients

- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1/2 pound salt
- 3/4 pound mustard seed
- 3/4 pound onions chopped fine
- 3/4 pound stoned raisins chopped fine
- 3/4 teaspoon red pepper
- heaping teaspoon ginger
- 2 ounces ground mustard
- 1 ounce tumeric
- 13 large apples
- 9 large tomatoes
- 3 pints good vinegar

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter, and core apples
2. Peel tomatoes
3. Cook and put through colander
4. Boil mustard seed in vinegar
5. Strain out seed and add vinegar
6. Add spices to pulp and boil all together
7. Stir constantly to prevent burning
8. Lastly add tumeric
9. When cold, bottle and cork
