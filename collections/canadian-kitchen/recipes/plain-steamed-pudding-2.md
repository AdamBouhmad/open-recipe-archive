## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour milk
- part of a teaspoon soda
- part of a teaspoon salt
- part of a cup sugar
- flour to make stiff batter

## Directions

1. Mix sour milk, soda, salt, sugar, and flour to make stiff batter
2. Steam 2 hours
