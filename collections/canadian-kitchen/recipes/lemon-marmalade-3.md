## Ingredients

- 12 lemons
- 5 quarts of water
- 6 pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Slice 12 lemons very thin, and take out the seeds
2. Pour over them 5 quarts of water and let them stand all night
3. The next morning put them on in the same water, and let them boil until tender enough to mash with a spoon
4. Add six pounds of sugar and boil 30 minutes
5. Do not cover until cold
