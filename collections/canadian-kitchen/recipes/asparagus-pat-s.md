## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- asparagus tips
- white sauce
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut rounds of stale bread an inch and a half thick
2. Press a small cutter an inch deep into each, and dig out the inside, leaving a round saucerlike cavity
3. Butter these well and set upon the grating of a hot oven to crisp and to color lightly
4. Fill them with asparagus tips prepared as in the last recipe
5. Serve hot
