## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 3/4 cup butter or dripping
- 3/4 cup hot water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- other spices if desired

## Directions

1. Combine sugar and molasses
2. Add butter or dripping
3. Dissolve soda in hot water and add
4. Add enough flour to make a thick batter
5. Beat up and add egg
6. Add ginger and other spices if desired
