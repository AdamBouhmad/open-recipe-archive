## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk (boiled)
- 1 small teacup rice flour
- A little cold milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Five eggs well beaten
- Sugar to taste
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix 1 small teacup rice flour in a little cold milk
2. Add to the boiling milk
3. Add 2 tablespoons butter
4. When cold add five eggs well beaten
5. Sweeten to taste
6. Flavor with vanilla and bake
