## Ingredients

- 1 lb. salt cod soaked, boiled and picked up very fine
- 1 small cup milk or cream
- 1 teaspoonful flour
- 2 eggs beaten light
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- Little chopped parsley
- Half as much mashed potato as fish
- Pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Heat the milk
2. Thicken with the flour, then the potato rubbed very fine
3. Add the butter, the eggs, and parsley
4. Last, add the fish
5. Stir and toss until smoking hot all through
6. Pour into a deep dish
7. Alternatively: make a sauce of all the ingredients except the fish and potato
8. Mix the fish and potato well together with a little melted butter
9. Heat in a saucepan, stirring all the while
10. Heap in the centre of a dish and pour the sauce over all
