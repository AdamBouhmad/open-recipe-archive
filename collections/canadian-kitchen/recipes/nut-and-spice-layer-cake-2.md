## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter (scant)
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- flour
- 1 cup raisins chopped
- 1 cup sugar
- a few chopped walnuts
- white of 1 egg
- water

## Directions

1. Put 1 small teaspoon soda in 1/2 cup molasses, stir till light
2. Make enough flour to make a little stiffer than an ordinary layer cake
3. For filling, moisten 1 cup sugar with a few chopped walnuts and a little water and boil until it strings
