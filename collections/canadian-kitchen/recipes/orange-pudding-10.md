## Ingredients

- 3 oranges
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 pint milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- whites of 2 eggs
- a little granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut 3 oranges into small pieces, put into pudding dish, cover with 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar, let stand 1 hour
2. Make a custard of 1 pint milk, yolks of 2 eggs, 2 tablespoons cornstarch
3. Let milk come to a boil, stir in starch and egg, dissolved in cold water, cook a minute, pour over the oranges
4. Beat the whites of the 2 eggs, add a little granulated sugar, spread on the top, set in the oven to brown, let cool and serve
