## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound flour
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 3 pounds blue raisins
- 2 pounds currants
- 3/4 pound orange peel
- 3/4 pound almonds
- 1 ounce ground ginger
- 1/2 ounce cinnamon
- 1/4 ounce Jamaica pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 small cup sweet milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3/4 pound butter
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- cold water

## Directions

1. Prepare the fruit carefully
2. Mix fruit and all the dry ingredients together carefully
3. Moisten with the milk; only sufficient milk should be used to bind ingredients together
4. Mix butter into flour, add baking powder and enough cold water to make a firm paste
5. Roll out rather thin and cut out a piece for bottom of tin, also one for top
6. Roll out the scraps into a long-shaped piece
7. Have a cake tin well greased, put the paste in the bottom, then line sides and pack in mixture of fruit tightly as possible
8. Press into corners firmly, turn in an edge of paste, wet it and put on top of paste, put on loosely to avoid cracks
9. When mixture begins to cook, brush top with white of eggs, prick over with fork, and bake in moderate oven 4 hours
10. This will keep a year and improves with age
