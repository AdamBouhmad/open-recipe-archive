## Ingredients

- Juice and rind of an orange
- 1 cup sugar
- Yolk of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teacup milk
- Paste
- Whites of 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Line the dish with paste
2. Pour in the custards
3. Bake till done
4. Beat the whites of eggs to a stiff froth
5. Add 4 tablespoons of pulverized sugar
6. Spread it on the pie and brown lightly
