## Ingredients

- butter
- muslin cloth
- salt
- egg
- white sugar
- saltpetre

## Directions

1. Work your butter into small rolls, wrapping each one carefully in a clean muslin cloth, tying them up with a string
2. Make a brine, say three gallons, having it strong enough of salt to bear up an egg
3. Add half a teacupful of pure, white sugar, and one tablespoonful of saltpetre
4. Boil the brine, and when cold strain it carefully
5. Pour it over the rolls so as to more than cover them, as this excludes the air
6. Place a weight over all to keep the rolls under the surface
