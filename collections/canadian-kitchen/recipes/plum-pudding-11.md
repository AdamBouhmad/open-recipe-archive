## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 pound bread crumbs
- 1/4 pound suet
- 3/4 pound candied peel
- 1/4 pound raisins
- 1/4 pound currants
- 1 1/4 cups sweet milk
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Boil 6 hours
