## Ingredients

- 8 lbs. sickle pears
- 8 lbs. sugar
- 1/4 lb. candied ginger
- 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Chip or slice the pears, slice the ginger root and let them boil together with the sugar for one hour, slowly.
2. Boil the lemons whole in clear water until tender, then cut up in small bits, removing the seeds.
3. Add to the pears and boil an hour longer.
4. Put in glasses.
5. Use candied or crystallized ginger in preference to the green root.
