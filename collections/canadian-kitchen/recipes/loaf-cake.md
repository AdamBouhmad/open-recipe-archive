## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 1/4 cups sugar
- 1 cup apple sauce
- 1 cup dates chopped
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together and bake
