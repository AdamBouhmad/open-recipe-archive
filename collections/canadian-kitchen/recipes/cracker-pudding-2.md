## Ingredients

- 10 crackers rolled
- A piece of butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups stoned raisins
- 1 cup currants
- Citron, cinnamon, nutmeg and little flour (1 tablespoon)
- 1 quart milk

## Directions

1. Soak crackers in one quart milk over night
2. In the morning add well-beaten eggs, sugar, raisins, currants, citron, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour and salt
3. Steam four or five hours
