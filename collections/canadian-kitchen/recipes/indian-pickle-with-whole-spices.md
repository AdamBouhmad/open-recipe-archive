## Ingredients

- 2 quarts best vinegar
- 1 ounce whole ginger
- 1 ounce whole allspice
- 1 ounce tumeric powder
- ½ ounce chillies
- ½ pound mustard
- ½ pound salt
- ½ pound sugar
- cucumbers
- onions
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Bruise 1 ounce whole ginger
2. Mix 2 quarts best vinegar, 1 ounce whole ginger, 1 ounce whole allspice, 1 ounce tumeric powder, ½ ounce chillies, ½ pound mustard, ½ pound salt, ½ pound sugar
3. Boil all together 15 minutes
4. Put in a crock
5. Add cucumbers, onions, cauliflower, as gathered
6. Stir occasionally
7. In 3 weeks' time after last pickles are added, rescald liquid and pour over when cool
