## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 4 quarts cold water
- 1/2 cupful rice
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the fowl carefully and wash it with a wet cloth
2. Cut it into pieces and remove the fat
3. Place the joints in a saucepan with a quart of water to each pound of fowl
4. Let it simmer until the meat is tender
5. Remove the breast after four hours and strain through a sieve
6. Let the soup stand until the grease rises and carefully remove it
7. Put the soup again in the saucepan
8. Add the breast of the chicken cut into dice and half cupful of rice
9. Salt and pepper to taste and cook until the rice is tender
