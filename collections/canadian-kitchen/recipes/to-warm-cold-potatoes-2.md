## Ingredients

- milk
- egg
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix 3/4 cup milk, 1 egg well beaten, salt and pepper to taste, 2 cups fine chopped potatoes all together
2. Heat deep baking dish hot, and butter well
3. Pour in the mixture
4. Sprinkle with flour and bits of butter
5. Bake in a hot oven until nicely browned
