## Ingredients

- slippery-elm bark
- boiling water
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Break the bark into bits
2. Pour boiling water over it, cover, and let it infuse until cold
3. Sweeten, ice, and take for summer disorders, or add lemon juice and drink for a bad cold
