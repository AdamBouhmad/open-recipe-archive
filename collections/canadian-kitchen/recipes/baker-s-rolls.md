## Ingredients

- 1 pint sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- white of 1 egg
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald 1 pint sweet milk
2. While hot add tablespoon butter
3. When milk is lukewarm add the well-beaten white of 1 egg, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
4. Stir in flour and mould into a loaf
5. Let rise over night and mould three times
6. Roll out to about an inch in thickness
7. Cut into biscuits
8. Butter on half and fold over
9. Let rise and bake in a quick oven
