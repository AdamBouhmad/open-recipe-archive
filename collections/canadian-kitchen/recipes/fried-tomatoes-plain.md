## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- paprika
- salt
- sugar
- onion-juice
- fine corn-meal
- cottolene

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe, but do not peel, the tomatoes.
2. Slice, dust each piece with paprica, salt, and sugar, sprinkle with a few drops of onion-juice.
3. Dip in fine corn-meal, and fry in deep, hot cottolene, as you would fritters.
4. Serve dry with fish or with chops.
