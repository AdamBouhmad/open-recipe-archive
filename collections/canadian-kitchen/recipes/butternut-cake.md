## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 1 cup butternut meats
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake until done
