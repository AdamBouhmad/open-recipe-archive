## Ingredients

- same ingredients as dressing or stuffing for fowls
- half a can of oysters drained

## Directions

1. Make with the same ingredients as the basic dressing or stuffing for fowls, with the exception of half a can of oysters drained and slightly chopped and added to the rest.
2. This is used mostly with boiled turkey and chicken.
3. Use the remainder of the can of oysters to make an oyster sauce to be poured over the turkey when served, and also served in a separate dish.
