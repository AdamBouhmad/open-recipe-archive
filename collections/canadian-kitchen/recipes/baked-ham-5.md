## Ingredients

- fresh ham
- dressing

## Directions

1. Get a fresh ham and make a pocket in it by cutting the fat from between the rind and lean part
2. Make a dressing the same as for a turkey for it, and fill the pocket
3. Skewer the rind and bake for 4 hours
