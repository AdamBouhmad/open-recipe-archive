## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 oz. flour
- 1/2 oz. cornstarch
- 3 oz. powdered sugar
- vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Put milk to boil
2. Break eggs into a basin and add flour, cornstarch, powdered sugar, beating all well together
3. Add the boiling milk gradually, stirring well
4. Put all in the saucepan and stir till it comes to a boil
5. Remove and add vanilla
