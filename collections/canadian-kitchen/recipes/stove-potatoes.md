## Ingredients

- scraps of meat or bones
- vegetables
- potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Put meat or bones in a stewpan with as many different kinds of vegetables as can be got and cut into convenient size for cooking, along with as many potatoes wished
2. Gently cook in just enough water to keep from scorching till done
3. Season with pepper and salt at the start
