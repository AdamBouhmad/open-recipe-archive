## Ingredients

- 1 lb. under-cooked roast beef
- Salt and pepper
- 1/2 lb. of ham or bacon
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoonful of sweet herbs
- white of egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mince the beef and ham, add herbs
2. Mix with the egg, which must be previously well beaten
3. Brush each cake over with a little white of egg
4. Cover with breadcrumbs
5. Fry quickly for five minutes
