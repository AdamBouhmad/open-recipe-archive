## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 can lobster
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup sherry
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch
- 1 egg
- Salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Melt butter in chafing dish, add lobster
2. When hot add one small cup of milk, sherry (very slowly), salt and cayenne to taste
3. Thicken with cornstarch dissolved in a little milk
4. Just before serving stir in egg beaten very light
