## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 1/2 pounds flour
- 1 pound castor sugar
- grated rind of 1 lemon
- 2 ounces candied peel
- 5 or 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the butter and sugar into a clean pan
2. Add the grated rind of 1 lemon and with the hand beat it up to a very light cream
3. Add in the eggs 1 at a time, and beat well after each addition of eggs
4. When all are in and the batter is very light and creamy, add the flour
5. Stir it in lightly with the hand
6. When well mixed, divide the mixture equally into 3 or 4 round tins, papered at the bottoms and sides with buttered white paper
7. Dust sugar over from a sifter
