## Ingredients

- one cup boiling water
- two tablespoonfuls of brown sugar
- lump of butter size of an egg
- one egg

## Directions

1. Add brown sugar, butter, and beaten egg to boiling water
2. Let all come to a boil
3. Take care not to scorch
