## Ingredients

- 2 cups cornmeal
- 1 1/2 cups sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Steam 3 1/2 hours
