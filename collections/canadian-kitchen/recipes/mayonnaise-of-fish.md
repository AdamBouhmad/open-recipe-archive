## Ingredients

- One pound of fish, cooked
- Three hard-boiled eggs
- Two tablespoonfuls of best oil
- Six tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- Two teaspoonfuls of sugar
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One-half teaspoonful mustard
- One teaspoonful pepper
- Two heads blanched lettuce

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of the eggs smooth with the oil, add the sugar, salt, mustard and pepper, and when these are well mixed, the vinegar, a few drops at a time
2. Set it by, covered, while you cut the fish into strips about an inch long, and shred the lettuce
3. Mix these in a salad bowl, pour over the dressing and garnish with rings of the whites of the eggs
4. Serve as soon as it is ready or the lettuce will become limp
