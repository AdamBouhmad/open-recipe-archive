## Ingredients

- 1 colander sliced green tomatoes
- 1 quart onions
- 1 colander pared and sliced cucumbers
- 2 handfuls salt
- celery seed
- allspice
- ½ ounce celery seed
- ½ ounce allspice
- 1 teaspoon tumeric
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 1 gallon vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 colander of sliced green tomatoes, 1 quart of onions, 1 colander pared and sliced cucumbers
2. Add 2 handfuls salt
3. Stand 24 hours
4. Drain
5. Add celery seed, allspice each ½ ounce, 1 teaspoon tumeric, 1 pound brown sugar, 2 tablespoons mustard, 1 gallon vinegar
