## Ingredients

- corn
- hot water
- butter (1 tablespoonful per pint of corn)
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Husk and clean the corn, and leave it in cold water for fifteen minutes.
2. With a sharp knife split each row of grains all the way down from stem to tip of the ear; then shave, rather than cut, them off down to the cob.
3. Cover with hot water in a sauce-pan, and stew slowly for twenty minutes.
4. Stir in a tablespoonful of butter for a pint of corn; pepper and salt and serve.
