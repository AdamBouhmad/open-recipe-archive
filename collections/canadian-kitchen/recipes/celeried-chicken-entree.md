## Ingredients

- 2 cups cold cooked chicken, minced
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 heaping tablespoonful flour
- 1 teaspoonful celery salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 4 dashes pepper
- 3 slices of toast, cut lengthwise

## Directions

1. Put butter and flour in saucepan, stir till butter is melted and smooth
2. Add milk, salt and pepper, stir till it comes to a boil
3. Add parsley and celery salt, which have been mixed with a little of the butter mixture
4. Add chicken
5. Arrange toast in strips (log-cabin style)
6. Place the chicken in centre and serve
