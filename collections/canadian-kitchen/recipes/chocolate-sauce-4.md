## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1½ teaspoons cornstarch
- 2 squares Cowan's Chocolate
- 2 tablespoons hot water
- 2 eggs
- 7 to 8 cups powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Blend cornstarch with 1 pint milk
2. Add rest scalded
3. Cook in double boiler with chocolate
4. Add 4 tablespoons sugar and hot water
5. Stir until smooth
6. Add to cooked mixture
7. Beat whites until stiff
8. Add rest of sugar and unbeaten yolks
9. Stir into cooked part
10. Cook 1 minute
11. Add vanilla
