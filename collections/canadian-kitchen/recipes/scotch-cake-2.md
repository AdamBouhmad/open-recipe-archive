## Ingredients

- 0.5 lb. butter
- 2 oz. sugar
- 0.5 lb. flour
- A pinch of soda

## Directions

1. Put the butter in a large bowl, rubbing a few times with the hand
2. then put the flour, sugar and soda in a sifter, and as these are sifted into the butter mix with the hand, the warmth being sufficient to make all into a dough
3. Have a pan ready with buttered paper, and put the ball of dough on this, pressing to the desired thinness with the hands
4. Prick with fork on top, and bake twenty minutes or so in a moderate oven
5. Cut in squares while in the pan and hot, directly upon being taken out of the oven
