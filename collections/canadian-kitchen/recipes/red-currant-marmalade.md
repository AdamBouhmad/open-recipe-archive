## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. currants
- 1/4 lb. raisins
- 6 lbs. sugar
- 6 oranges
- 1/8 teaspoonful mace
- 1/8 teaspoonful cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil the currants and press through a sieve.
2. Boil the yellow rind of the oranges in a little water and chop finely.
3. Chop raisins and pulp of oranges.
4. Boil all together till thick, half or three-quarters of an hour.
5. Half yellow rind after boil is sufficient.
