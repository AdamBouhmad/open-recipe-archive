## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 3 eggs
- 6 rolled soda biscuits
- 1 pint sweet milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix 1 can salmon, 3 eggs, 6 rolled soda biscuits, 1 pint sweet milk, salt and pepper to taste together
2. Bake
