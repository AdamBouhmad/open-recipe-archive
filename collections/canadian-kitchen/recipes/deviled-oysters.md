## Ingredients

- 20 oysters
- 1 gill oyster-liquor
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful curry powder
- 1 teaspoonful Worcestershire sauce
- 10 drops tabasco sauce
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in the blazer, stir in the flour, and when this is blended, the oyster-liquor and all the seasoning except the lemon-juice
2. As soon as the sauce is boiling hot, drop in the oysters and cook three minutes or until they plump
3. Add the lemon-juice and serve them at once on Graham toast
