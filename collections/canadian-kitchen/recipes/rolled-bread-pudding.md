## Ingredients

- 1 quart bread crumbs
- 1 cup hot milk
- 1 cupful molasses
- 1 cup fruit or chopped raisins
- 1 teaspoon each of spices
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup sifted flour

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs soft in hot milk
2. Add molasses, fruit or chopped raisins, spices, butter, salt, soda, and flour
3. Boil or steam 3 hours
4. Serve with sweet sauce
