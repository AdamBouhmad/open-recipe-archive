## Ingredients

- black-eyed peas
- fat bacon
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. They are boiled always with a bit of fat bacon, to give them richness.
2. Drain well, pepper, salt, and serve with the bacon on the top of the peas; or, after they are boiled they are drained and turned into a frying-pan in which slices of fat bacon have been cooked and then taken out, leaving the fat in the pan.
3. Saute the peas in this until dry, hot, and well seasoned by the fat.
4. Serve dry, and lay the fried bacon on or about the peas.
5. Dried black-eyed peas must be soaked over night.
