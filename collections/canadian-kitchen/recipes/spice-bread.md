## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup currants
- 4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 4 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Let rise in baker 1/2 hour
3. Bake 1 hour and let rise in warm place
