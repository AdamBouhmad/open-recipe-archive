## Ingredients

- 4 1/2 cups sugar
- 4 cups sour buttermilk
- 6 teaspoons soda
- 4 tablespoons or more of lard
- flour enough to make a soft dough to roll out

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out with doughnut cutter
3. Fry in hot lard
