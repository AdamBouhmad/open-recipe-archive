## Ingredients

- 1 lemon, grated
- 1 apple, grated
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 grated lemon, 1 grated apple, 1 egg, and 1 cup sugar
2. Boil 4 minutes
