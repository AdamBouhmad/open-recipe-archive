## Ingredients

- ½ peck sliced green tomatoes
- ⅝ peck white onions
- 25 small cucumbers
- 2 heads cauliflower
- salt
- ½ cup grated horseradish
- ½ ounce tumeric
- ½ ounce ground cinnamon
- ½ ounce ground cloves
- 1 ounce pepper
- 1 pint ground mustard
- 1 pint brown sugar
- 2 bunches celery
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut up sliced green tomatoes, white onions, small cucumbers, and cauliflower
2. Sprinkle with salt
3. Let stand over night
4. Drain
5. Mix ½ cup grated horseradish, ½ ounce tumeric, ½ ounce ground cinnamon and cloves, 1 ounce pepper, 1 pint ground mustard, 1 pint brown sugar, 2 bunches chopped celery
6. Cover with vinegar
7. Boil slowly 2 hours
