## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup currants
- butter size of an egg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Wet with sweet milk
2. Mix soft and bake in a hot oven
