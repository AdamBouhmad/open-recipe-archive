## Ingredients

- 1 small nutmeg
- 2 lbs. apples
- 2 lemons, juice of one and rind of two
- 1 pt. brandy
- 2 lbs. raisins
- 3 lbs. currants
- 4 lbs. lean, chopped beef
- 3 lbs. beef suet, chopped
- 2 lbs. brown sugar
- 6 oz. mixed candied peel

## Directions

1. Stone the raisins and chop
2. Wash the currants and dry
3. Slice the peel thinly, grate the nutmeg, pare, core, and mince the apples, peel lemon and strain the juice
4. Get all your dry ingredients, including the beef (uncooked) and suet, well mixed
5. Add the lemon-juice and brandy last
6. Press the mixture into a jar that will exclude the air
7. Set away for a fortnight before using
