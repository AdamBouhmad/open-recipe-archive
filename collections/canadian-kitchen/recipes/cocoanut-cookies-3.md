## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup cocoanut
- milk just enough to wet soda
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients and roll
