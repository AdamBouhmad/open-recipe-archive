## Ingredients

- 14 pounds green tomatoes
- 6 large onions
- 1 cupful salt
- 2 quarts water
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 quarts fresh vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 ounce cloves
- 1 ounce whole ginger
- 1 ounce allspice
- 1 ounce mustard
- 1 ounce cinnamon
- 1 saltspoon cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Slice green tomatoes and onions
2. Sprinkle with salt and allow to stand over night
3. In the morning drain
4. Add 2 quarts water and 1 quart vinegar; boil 15 minutes, then drain again, throwing away the water and vinegar
5. Add 2 quarts fresh vinegar, 2 pounds sugar, 1 ounce each of cloves, whole ginger, allspice, mustard and cinnamon, 1 saltspoon cayenne pepper
6. Tie the spice in a muslin bag
7. Boil for 15 minutes
8. Pour in earthenware or glass jars and tie down when cold
