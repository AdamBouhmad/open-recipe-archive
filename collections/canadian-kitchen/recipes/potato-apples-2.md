## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot riced potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons thick cream
- 2 yolks of eggs
- cayenne pepper
- nutmeg
- flour
- egg crumbs
- deep fat for frying
- clove

## Directions

1. Mix in the order given: potatoes, butter, cheese, salt, cream, egg yolks, cayenne pepper and nutmeg
2. Shape in the form of small apples
3. Roll in flour and egg crumbs
4. Fry in deep fat
5. Drain and insert clove at stem and blossom end
