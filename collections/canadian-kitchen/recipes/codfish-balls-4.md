## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled potatoes and rice
- 1 cup soaked and shredded codfish
- 1 tablespoon butter
- curry powder
- minced onion
- paprika
- beaten egg
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Make into balls
2. Roll in beaten egg
3. Roll in cracker crumbs
4. Leave in a cool place
5. Fry quickly
6. Serve
