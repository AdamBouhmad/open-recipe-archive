## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- a piece of butter the size of a large egg
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 cups flour (scant)
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream together sugar and butter
2. Add sweet milk
3. Add baking powder and flour
4. Use any kind of flavoring desired
5. Bake as a layer cake
