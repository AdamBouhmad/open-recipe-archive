## Ingredients

- 1 cup yellow cornmeal
- 1 egg
- butter size of English walnut
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 full teaspoon baking powder
- 1 pinch salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Sift meal, flour, sugar and baking powder and salt together in a large mixing bowl
2. Add enough milk to make a thick batter, the consistency between pancake and cake batter
3. Add 1 egg well beaten
4. Lastly add the butter melted hot
5. Bake in a hot oven
