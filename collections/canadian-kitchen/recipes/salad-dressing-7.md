## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoonfuls sugar
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful mustard
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 2 eggs
- Butter size of egg
- 1/4 cup milk

## Directions

1. Take all ingredients and mix together (without vinegar)
2. Put in double boiler and let it come to boil, stirring constantly
3. Then add vinegar and come to boil again
4. If too thick when cold add cream to thin it
5. Will keep for a long time
