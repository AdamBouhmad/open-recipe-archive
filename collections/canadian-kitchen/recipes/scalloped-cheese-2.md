## Ingredients

- 3 slices bread
- 1/4 lb. cheese
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take three slices of bread, well buttered, first cutting off the brown outside crust.
2. Grate fine any kind of good cheese; lay the bread in layers in a buttered baking-dish, sprinkle over each the grated cheese, some salt and pepper to taste.
3. Mix well-beaten eggs with milk; pour it over the bread and cheese.
4. Bake it in a hot oven as you would cook a bread pudding. This makes an ample dish for four people.
