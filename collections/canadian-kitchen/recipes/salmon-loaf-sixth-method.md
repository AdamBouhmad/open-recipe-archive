## Ingredients

- salmon
- 2 eggs beaten
- 1 cup milk
- six rolled biscuits
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Open salmon
2. Pick out skins and bones
3. Beat eggs and add milk, rolled biscuits, salt and pepper
4. Mix all together
5. Put in a buttered dish
6. Cook 3/4 hour in oven
7. Nice for cold meat for supper or Sunday
