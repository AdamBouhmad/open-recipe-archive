## Ingredients

- onion
- salt
- hot water
- salt pork
- butter
- flour
- bread
- lemon
- parsley
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Put an onion, salt and hot water into a pan, and baste for 10 or 15 minutes
2. Change the pan, put in a slice of salt pork and baste with butter, and pork drippings very often
3. Just before serving dredge lightly with flour and baste
4. Ducks take from 25 to 35 minutes
5. Do not draw or take off the heads of either, garnish with fried or toasted bread, lemon, parsley and currant jelly
