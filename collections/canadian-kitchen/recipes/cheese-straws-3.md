## Ingredients

- 3 oz. flour
- 4 oz. grated cheese (Parmesan is best)
- 1/4 tablespoonful salt
- Dash of cayenne
- 1/4 lb. butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour with grated cheese, add salt, cayenne, and butter.
2. Work this to a smooth paste sufficiently stiff to roll; add a very little water, if necessary.
3. Roll out in very thin strips and cut into straws, place on a greased tin and bake ten minutes in a moderate oven.
4. They must be straw color and very crisp.
