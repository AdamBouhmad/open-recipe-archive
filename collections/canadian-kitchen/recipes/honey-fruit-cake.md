## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups honey
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups raisins
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake
