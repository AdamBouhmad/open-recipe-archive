## Ingredients

- 1 quart grated large cucumbers without seeds or peel
- 1 pint chopped onions
- 1 green pepper chopped
- 6 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons black pepper
- 1.5 pints vinegar
- 4 or 5 small red peppers

## Directions

1. Drain grated cucumbers
2. Add onions, pepper, salt, black pepper, and vinegar
3. Mix all together and bottle
4. Do not drain too dry
