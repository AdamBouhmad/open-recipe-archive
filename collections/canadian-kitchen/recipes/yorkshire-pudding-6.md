## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 eggs
- 6 ounces flour
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. add little milk, all flour and salt, then rest of milk
3. Bake in greased pan and serve with roast beef
