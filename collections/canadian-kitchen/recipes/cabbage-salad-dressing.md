## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup vinegar
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- about 1 teaspoon mustard
- about 1 teaspoon flour
- 2 tablespoons cream
- butter size of an egg

## Directions

1. Mix flour fine with 2 tablespoons cream
2. Stir until it boils
