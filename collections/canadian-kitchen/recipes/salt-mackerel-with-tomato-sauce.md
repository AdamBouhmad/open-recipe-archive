## Ingredients

- Salt mackerel
- Tomatoes, stewed and strained
- Onion-juice
- Pepper
- Salt
- Sugar
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Proceed as with boiled mackerel
2. When dished, pour over it a tomato sauce made of stewed, strained tomatoes seasoned with onion-juice, pepper, salt, and sugar, and thickened with a brown roux of butter and flour
3. Let the fish lie in this for ten minutes and serve
