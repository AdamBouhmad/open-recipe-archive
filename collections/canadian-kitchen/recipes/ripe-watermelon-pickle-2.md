## Ingredients

- 7 pounds watermelon
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons whole cloves
- allspice
- cinnamon
- 4 cups vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil watermelon until it can be pierced with a fork
2. Strain, put in sealers
3. Boil together the other ingredients a few minutes
4. Pour over fruit while hot, and set away in a cool, dark place
