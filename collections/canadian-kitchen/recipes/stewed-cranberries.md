## Ingredients

- 1 quart cranberries
- 1 quart water
- 1 pint sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 1 quart cranberries in 1 quart water for 10 minutes
2. Add 1 pint sugar
3. Cook 10 minutes
4. Remove from stove
