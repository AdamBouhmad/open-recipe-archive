## Ingredients

- any cold boiled fish, free from bones
- a cup or more of cream or milk
- a little flour
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- Cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Take any cold boiled fish, free from bones (canned salmon will do)
2. Pour over it a cup or more of cream or milk mixed with a little flour, butter, pepper, and salt
3. Put small bits of butter on top
4. Bake one-half hour
5. Cracker crumbs are an improvement
