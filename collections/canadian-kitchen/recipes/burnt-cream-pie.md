## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Brown 1 cup sugar and 3 tablespoons butter together
2. Dissolve 2 tablespoons cornstarch in part of the milk
3. Mix well together, add to the sugar and butter
4. Set on stove again and cook until thick as filling for cream pie
5. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth
6. Add 1 teaspoon sugar spread over the pie and brown lightly
