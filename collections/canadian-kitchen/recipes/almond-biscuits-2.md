## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, whites beaten
- 1 pound icing sugar
- 1/2 pound almonds, chopped

## Directions

1. Beat whites of 2 eggs
2. Add 1 pound icing sugar and beat together
3. Reserve 1/6 cup mixture to ice the cake
4. Chop 1/2 pound almonds and add to the mixture
5. Cut in narrow strips and brown slightly
6. Bake on inverted tins in a slow oven
