## Ingredients

- 1 pt. cream
- 2 doz. almonds
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Sugar to taste
- 3 glasses sherry

## Directions

1. Blanch and chop the almonds and put them into a jug with the cream.
2. In another jug put the sherry, lemon-juice and enough sugar to sweeten the whole nicely.
3. Pour rapidly from one jug to the other till the mixture is well frothed.
4. Then pour it into individual glasses.
