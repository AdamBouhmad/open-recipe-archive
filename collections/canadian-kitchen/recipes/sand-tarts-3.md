## Ingredients

- ½ pound butter
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 3 eggs
- granulated sugar
- cinnamon
- raisin or almond

## Directions

1. Roll out, cut like cookies
2. Brush with white of egg
3. Sprinkle with granulated sugar and cinnamon melted
4. Put raisin or almond in centre of each
5. Bake in quick oven
