## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/4 pints milk

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, sugar, salt and powder
2. Rub in the butter
3. Add beaten eggs and milk
4. Mix into smooth batter
5. Fill greased muffin pans about
6. Bake in hot oven 15 minutes
