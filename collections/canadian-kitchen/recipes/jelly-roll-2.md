## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- a pinch of salt
- jelly

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs until light
2. Add the sugar and salt, and stir until dissolved
3. Sift the flour and baking powder, add to the above and beat until light
4. Put in a flat pan, bake 10 or 15 minutes
5. Take out on a napkin, spread under side with jelly, and roll up
