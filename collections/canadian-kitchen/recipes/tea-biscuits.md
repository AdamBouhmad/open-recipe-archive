## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 4 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon shortening
- 2 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift 1 teaspoon soda, 2 teaspoons cream tartar, with 4 cups flour
2. Rub in 1 tablespoon shortening
3. Add 2 cups sweet milk
4. Knead as little as possible
5. Bake in a hot oven
