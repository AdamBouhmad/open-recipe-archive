## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 large tablespoon shortening
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- salt
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in boiling water
