## Ingredients

- a plump chicken weighing about 4 or 5 pounds when dressed
- salt and pepper
- 1 small loaf of baker's bread
- a lump of butter, cut fine, size of an egg
- a little sage
- 2 beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Put chicken on and boil till tender and well done
2. Remove all the bones and grisly parts and chop fine, with a little salt and pepper
3. Boil down the water it was cooked in to a pint, and it jellies, and stir it in with the chicken
4. Put bread through the meat grinder with a lump of butter, cut fine, size of an egg, a little sage, salt and pepper
5. Stir in 2 beaten eggs to hold it together
6. Put half of the chicken chopped in a deep earthen or granite dish, spread level
7. Put on the dressing, spread level, then the rest of the minced chicken
8. Set in the oven till hot, then set in a cool place over night
9. When cold slice and serve
