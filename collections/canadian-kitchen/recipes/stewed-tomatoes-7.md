## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- hot water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put ripe tomatoes into hot water and skin them
2. Throw them into an earthen stewpan, cut up
3. Let tomatoes cook gently for a few minutes
4. Season with butter, pepper and salt
5. Serve
