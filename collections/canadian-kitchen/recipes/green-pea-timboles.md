## Ingredients

- 1 can peas
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 2 eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Pass the peas through a colander
2. Mix with the butter, sugar, salt, pepper, eggs, and milk to soften
3. Pour into buttered timbole moulds
4. Bake in hot oven until firm
5. Turn out and serve with white sauce
