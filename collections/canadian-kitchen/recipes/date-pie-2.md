## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound dates
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Stone and boil 1/2 pound dates
2. Put through colander
3. Add 2 eggs well beaten, 1 cup milk, 2 teaspoons cornstarch, and 2 tablespoons sugar
4. Boil in double boiler till thick
5. Have a nice rich pie shell baked and put filling in
6. Before using, cover with nicely flavored whipped cream
