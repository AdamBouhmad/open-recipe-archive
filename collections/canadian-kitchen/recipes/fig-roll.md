## Ingredients

- light suet pastry
- self-raising flour
- figs
- golden syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare a light suet pastry using self-raising flour
2. Slice and soak the figs, then drain from the water
3. Lay upon the rolled-out sheet of paste
4. Spread with golden syrup
5. Roll up carefully, fastening the ends
6. Tie loosely in cloth
7. Boil or steam for 2 hours
