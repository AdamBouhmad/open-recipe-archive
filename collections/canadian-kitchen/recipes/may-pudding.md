## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour equal to weight of 2 eggs
- butter equal to weight of 1 egg
- sugar equal to weight of 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons jam
- 1/2 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well
2. Add flour, butter and sugar to weight
3. Add 2 tablespoons jam
4. Add 1/2 teaspoon soda
5. Steam 3/4 of an hour
