## Ingredients

- Whites of 4 eggs beaten to a stiff froth
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1/2 cup butter beaten to a cream with the sugar
- 1 3/4 cups sifted flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- chopped raisins
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts
- vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites to a stiff froth
2. Cream butter with sugar
3. Add egg whites and sugar to butter and sugar mixture
4. Add milk and sifted flour with baking powder
5. Add vanilla flavoring
6. Put 1/3 of the batter in a long tin
7. Add a thin layer of chopped raisins and chopped nuts
8. Top with remaining batter
