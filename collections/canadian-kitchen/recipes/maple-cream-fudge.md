## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1/2 cup cream or milk
- 1 cup hickorynut meats or 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Let boil without stirring for 20 minutes, or until it threads when held up with the spoon
2. Take off and beat for a few minutes, until it begins to harden
3. Add 1 cup hickorynut meats, or 1 teaspoon vanilla, to flavor
4. Pour out on a buttered pie tin or platter
