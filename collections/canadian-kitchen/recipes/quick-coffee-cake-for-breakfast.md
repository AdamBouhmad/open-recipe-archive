## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- ¾ cup sugar
- 3 level teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
- 4 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 well-beaten egg
- ½ cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift together twice flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and cinnamon
2. Mix in a soft dough with melted butter, egg and sweet milk
3. Spread in a shallow pan and sprinkle thickly with sugar and cinnamon
4. Bake in a quick oven and serve fresh and hot for breakfast
