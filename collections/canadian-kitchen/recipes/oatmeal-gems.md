## Ingredients

- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup flour
- 2/3 tablespoonfuls soda
- 1/2 tablespoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. Soak oatmeal over night in one cupful of water
2. In the morning, add sour milk, flour, soda, salt, butter, and sugar
3. Mix, and bake in hot, well-buttered gem-pans
4. If too moist, add a little more flour
