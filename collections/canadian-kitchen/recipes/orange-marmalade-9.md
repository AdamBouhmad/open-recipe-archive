## Ingredients

- 1 dozen bitter oranges
- 1/2 dozen lemons
- 1 cup water to one cup fruit
- 1 3/4 pounds sugar to 1 pint of fruit

## Directions

1. Cut up lemons fine
2. Cook each separate until tender in 1 cup water to one cup fruit
3. Put together and add 1 3/4 pounds sugar to 1 pint of fruit
4. Boil quickly for 40 minutes
