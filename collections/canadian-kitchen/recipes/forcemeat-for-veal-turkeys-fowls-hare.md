## Ingredients

- 1 liver
- 2 oz. ham or bacon
- 1 lb. suet
- rind of 0.5 lemon
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful minced parsley
- 1 teaspoonful minced sweet herbs
- salt
- cayenne
- mace
- 6 oz. breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Shred the ham or bacon and liver
2. Chop the suet, lemon peel and herbs very fine
3. Add the seasoning to taste, salt, cayenne and mace
4. Blend all thoroughly together with the breadcrumbs before wetting
5. Beat and strain the eggs
6. Work them up with the other ingredients and the forcemeat will be ready for use
