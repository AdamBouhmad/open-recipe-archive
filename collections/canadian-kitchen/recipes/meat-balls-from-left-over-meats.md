## Ingredients

- cold meat of any kind
- cooked potatoes
- a little onion
- dry-bread
- hot water
- butter or drippings

## Directions

1. Chop cold meat of any kind with the same bulk of cooked potatoes
2. add a little onion if liked
3. take dry-bread, moisten with hot water
4. use enough bread to make the meat and potatoes adhere
5. fry in cakes in butter or drippings
