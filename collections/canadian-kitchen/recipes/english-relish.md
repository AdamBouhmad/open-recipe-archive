## Ingredients

- 4 large green cucumbers
- 1 cauliflower
- 6 large onions
- 2 green peppers
- salt water
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1.5 cups sugar
- 1 dessertspoon tumeric
- 1 dessertspoon salt
- 1 dessertspoon celery seed
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon mustard

## Directions

1. Cut all vegetables fine and let stand in salt water 5 hours
2. Take out and drain and put in a kettle with vinegar and sugar
3. Cook briskly for 15 or 20 minutes
4. Add tumeric, salt, celery seed, pepper, flour, and mustard for dressing
