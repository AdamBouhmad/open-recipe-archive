## Ingredients

- 3 eggs beaten separately
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons cream or 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup pastry flour
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- jelly

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately very light, then beat them together
2. Add sugar, cream or butter
3. Add pastry flour, soda and cream tartar
4. Bake in moderate oven in long shallow tins in very thin layer
5. While warm, spread with jelly
6. Trim edges and roll
