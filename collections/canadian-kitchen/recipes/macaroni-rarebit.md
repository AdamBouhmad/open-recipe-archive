## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled macaroni
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 eggs
- mustard
- paprika
- 3/4 cup cream
- toast

## Directions

1. Melt butter
2. Add cheese and seasoning
3. Cook over hot water
4. When melted add macaroni, beaten egg diluted with cream
5. Cook until slightly thickened
6. Serve on toast
