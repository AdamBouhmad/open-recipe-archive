## Ingredients

- eight apples, chopped fine
- eight peppers (four red and four green)
- twelve tomatoes, peeled
- eight large onions
- 1/4 pound salt
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1/2 pound raisins, chopped
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 ounce ginger

## Directions

1. Chop all fine.
2. Boil till thick.
