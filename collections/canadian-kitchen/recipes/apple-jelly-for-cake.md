## Ingredients

- 1 large or 2 small apples grated
- rind and juice of 1 lemon
- one cup sugar

## Directions

1. Grate 1 large or 2 small apples
2. Add the rind and juice of 1 lemon
3. Add one cup sugar
4. Boil 3 minutes
