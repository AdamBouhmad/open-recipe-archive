## Ingredients

- 1 box gelatine
- 1 qt. milk
- 3/4 cups sugar
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cup of milk
2. Add to rest of milk, heated
3. Add yolks of eggs and sugar
4. Cook until smooth
5. Remove from stove and add whites of eggs
6. Pour in mould to cool
