## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- Their weight in flour, butter and sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful baking-powder
- A little vanilla
- 1 tablespoonful milk

## Directions

1. Cream the butter
2. Then add the sugar
3. then the eggs one at a time
4. flavor and beat in the flour and baking powder
5. If too stiff add one tablespoonful milk
6. Bake in small moulds, half full, for fifteen or twenty minutes
7. For sauce: One and one-half tablespoonfuls butter, four tablespoonfuls sugar, one egg; beat all together until light
8. Then add boiling water just before serving, and flavor
