## Ingredients

- pork tenderloin
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Split the tenderloin through lengthwise
2. Put on a wire broiler and cook over a clear fire
3. When done, put in a pan with a lump of butter and let the juice of the meat and butter come to a boil
4. This makes a delicious gravy
5. Season with salt and pepper
