## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons chocolate or good cocoa
- 1 scant teaspoon salt
- cold water
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Blend cornstarch, chocolate or cocoa, and salt with cold water
2. Add 1 quart boiling water
3. Boil 2 minutes
4. Add white sugar, butter, and vanilla
5. Serve warm or cold with cream
