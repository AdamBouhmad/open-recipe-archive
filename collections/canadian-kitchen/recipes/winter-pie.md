## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons buttermilk
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon juice of canned cherries
- pie crusts
- canned cherries

## Directions

1. Cream buttermilk and sugar
2. Add the yolks of 2 eggs beaten
3. Mix in flour with baking powder
4. Flavor with juice of canned cherries
5. Fill crusts and bake
6. Put in fruit from which the juice has been drained
7. Make a frosting of the whites of eggs
