## Ingredients

- 2 cups bread sponge
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 beaten egg
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- flour
- butter size of walnut
- 1 tablespoon cream

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups bread sponge, 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup sweet milk, 1 beaten egg, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon with flour enough to mix stiff
2. Let rise a second time
3. Roll out over 1 inch thick
4. Cream butter size of walnut, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 tablespoon cream and mix
5. Spread over top
6. Let rise and bake
