## Ingredients

- 2 cups any fruit syrup
- 2 teaspoons Knox gelatine or 1 ounce sheet gelatine
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 2 cups cream

## Directions

1. Put gelatine to soak in cold water
2. When thoroughly softened, dissolve over hot water
3. Stir into the fruit syrup
4. Stand the syrup bowl in a pan of cold water
5. Stir occasionally while whipping the cream
6. When syrup begins to thicken, fold in whipped cream
7. Turn into a wet mould
8. Set away to get perfectly cold before serving
