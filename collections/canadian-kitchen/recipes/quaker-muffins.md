## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup oats (Quaker)
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Mix and bake as muffins
