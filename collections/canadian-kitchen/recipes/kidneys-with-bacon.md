## Ingredients

- Lamb kidneys
- Thin slices of fat breakfast bacon
- Toast
- 1 teaspoonful of Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Split lamb kidneys in half and fasten open with toothpicks
2. Cook in a frying-pan thin slices of fat breakfast bacon until clear, but not crisped
3. Take up and keep hot while you cook the kidneys in the bacon-fat, turning them frequently
4. Six minutes should make them tender
5. Arrange upon thin slices of toast in a dish, garnish with the bacon
6. Add a teaspoonful of Worcestershire sauce to the gravy and pour over the kidneys
