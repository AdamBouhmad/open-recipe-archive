## Ingredients

- squab
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- buttered toast
- hot stock or gravy

## Directions

1. Split down the back
2. Rub all over with butter, salt and pepper them
3. Broil over red coals
4. Serve upon buttered toast wet with a little hot stock or gravy
