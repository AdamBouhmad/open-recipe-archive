## Ingredients

- Yolks of 2 eggs
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup sour cream
- ¾ teaspoon soda
- 1 cup currants or raisins
- teaspoon cinnamon
- a pinch of cloves

## Directions

1. Cook this mixture on the stove until the froth leaves it
2. Fill pie crust which has been previously baked
3. Put into oven until well set
4. Cover with whites beaten stiff and return to oven to brown
