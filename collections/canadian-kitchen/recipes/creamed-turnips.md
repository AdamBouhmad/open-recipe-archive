## Ingredients

- 1 large or 2 small turnips
- water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup sweet cream or milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pare and slice 1 large or 2 small turnips
2. Cover with water to which has been added 1 tablespoon salt
3. Cook until easily pierced with a fork, then drain and mash
4. Add 1 tablespoon butter and 1 cup sweet cream or milk
5. Beat the turnips until all are smooth
6. Add a few dashes of pepper
