## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 heaping teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Add the eggs and rest of ingredients
3. Roll thin and bake in a quick oven
