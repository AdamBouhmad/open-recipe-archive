## Ingredients

- 1 pk. tomatoes, peeled and sliced
- 8 large onions, sliced
- 1 cup salt
- 2 qts. vinegar
- 2 lbs. sugar
- 1/2 lb. mustard seed
- 1 tablespoonful ground cloves
- 1 tablespoonful ground mustard
- 1 tablespoonful ground allspice
- 1 tablespoonful ground ginger
- 1 teaspoonful cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes and onions in alternate layers, cover with salt and let stand twenty-four hours.
2. Drain off all the liquor.
3. Add vinegar, ground mustard, ground ginger, ground cloves, ground allspice, cayenne pepper.
4. Stew slowly two or three hours.
5. When nearly done add brown sugar and mustard seed.
