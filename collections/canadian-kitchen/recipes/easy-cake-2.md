## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 eggs
- milk
- 4 tablespoons soft butter
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, flour and baking powder together
2. Break 2 eggs into a cup (not beaten) and fill up with milk
3. Put in eggs and milk to the dry ingredients
4. Last of all add soft butter and flavoring
5. Bake in loaf or layer
