## Ingredients

- currants (2 quarts)
- sugar (2 quarts)
- vinegar
- cinnamon (1 tablespoon)
- cloves (0.75 tablespoon)
- allspice (0.75 tablespoon)
- cayenne (0.75 tablespoon)

## Directions

1. Cover currants and sugar with vinegar
2. Add cinnamon, cloves, allspice, and cayenne
3. Boil slowly for 40 minutes
