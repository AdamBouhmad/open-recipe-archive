## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour enough to stiffen
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix soft and flavor with vanilla.
