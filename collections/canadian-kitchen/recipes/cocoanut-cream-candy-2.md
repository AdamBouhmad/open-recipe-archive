## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls shredded cocoanut
- A little water

## Directions

1. Sugar and a little water boiled without stirring until it will spin a thread
2. Take from the fire
3. Add a large tablespoonful of shredded cocoanut and stir until creamy
4. Drop size of a penny on buttered paper
5. Slip off when cool and pack in boxes
