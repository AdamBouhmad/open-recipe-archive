## Ingredients

- One can salmon
- Two eggs well beaten
- One cup breadcrumbs
- A small piece of butter
- Pepper and salt
- Two hard-boiled eggs
- Drawn-butter sauce

## Directions

1. Put in a bowl and steam two hours
2. Serve with a drawn-butter sauce, in which can be added two hard-boiled eggs, chopped fine
