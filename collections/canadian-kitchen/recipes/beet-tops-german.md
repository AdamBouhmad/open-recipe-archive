## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound cold boiled ham
- salad oil
- 1/2 grated onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls hot vinegar
- beet greens
- salt
- water
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of cold boiled ham into dice and fry in a little salad oil with half a grated onion.
2. Add two tablespoonfuls of hot vinegar, and set in hot water while you wash, pick over, and boil the greens in hot, salted water.
3. Fifteen minutes should make them tender.
4. Chop fine, drain well, and mix with the fried ham and vinegar.
5. Dish hot with poached eggs on top of the greens.
