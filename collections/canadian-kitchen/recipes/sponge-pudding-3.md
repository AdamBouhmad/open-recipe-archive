## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 1 cup butter
- 3 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 5 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Wet the flour with part of the milk
2. Cook it all in the milk ten minutes
3. Add butter and sugar while hot
4. When cool add the yolks of eggs well beaten
5. Add the whisked whites and stir thoroughly
6. Bake in a two-quart basin (first set in a pan of hot water) half an hour
7. Serve with cream for sauce
