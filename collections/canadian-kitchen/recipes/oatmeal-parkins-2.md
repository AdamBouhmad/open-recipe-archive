## Ingredients

- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon allspice
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Just before final mixing break 1 egg in the mixture and stir whole together and drop in buttered tins
