## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup currants
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks of eggs separately
2. Mix with butter, brown sugar, currants and nutmeg
3. Fill and bake
