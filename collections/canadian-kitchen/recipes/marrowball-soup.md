## Ingredients

- 1 piece marrow, size of egg
- 1 cup grated bread crumbs
- yolk of 1 egg
- salt
- nutmeg
- cold water
- boiling stock

## Directions

1. Let piece of marrow melt slowly
2. When slightly cooled add grated bread crumbs, egg yolk, salt, and nutmeg
3. Mix well with little cold water
4. Take a teaspoonful and drop into boiling stock
5. Do not cover the kettle
6. If the ball falls apart add more bread crumbs
7. Put the mixture in by the spoonful and let boil slowly three minutes
