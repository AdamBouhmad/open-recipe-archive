## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound macaroni
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups cooked macaroni
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup stock
- 1 cup tomato pulp
- 1 cup grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of flour, butter, tomato and stock
2. Add macaroni cooked until tender
3. Add cheese
