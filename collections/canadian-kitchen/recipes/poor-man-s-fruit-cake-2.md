## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 lemon peel
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour enough to make a batter

## Directions

1. Mix butter and brown sugar together
2. Add sour milk
3. Add lemon peel and raisins
4. Add flour and soda to make a batter that will soon rise in the centre and not fall over
5. Bake in a slow oven 1 hour
