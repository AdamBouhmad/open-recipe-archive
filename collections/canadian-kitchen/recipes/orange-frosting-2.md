## Ingredients

- 1 yolk of egg
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 teaspoon orange extract
- confectioner's sugar

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 yolk of egg, 1 tablespoon water and 1 teaspoon orange extract
2. Add enough confectioner's sugar to make thick enough to spread
