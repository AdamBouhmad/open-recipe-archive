## Ingredients

- one bunch of salsify
- one cup milk
- one tablespoonful butter
- three tablespoonfuls cracker crumbs
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Scrape a bunch of salsify and drop into cold water as you cut into inch lengths
2. Boil in hot, salted water until tender
3. Drain and pour into the saucepan with the salsify a cupful of hot milk
4. Simmer five minutes, and stir in cracker-dust, with pepper and salt
5. Stew gently for three minutes
