## Ingredients

- 1 pint left-over meat (pork preferred)
- 1 quart small, thinly cut slices of turnip
- water
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a pinch of salt
- flour
- fat pork (uncooked)

## Directions

1. Cut meat fine
2. Put meat and turnip slices in a large-size pudding dish or baking tin
3. Pour over sufficient water to nicely cook it, about 20 minutes
4. Season to taste
5. Make a batter consisting of 1 cup buttermilk, 1 teaspoon soda, a pinch of salt and enough flour for batter
6. Spread over turnip and meat
7. Sprinkle over batter real small pieces of fat pork, uncooked
8. Bake in a moderately hot oven
