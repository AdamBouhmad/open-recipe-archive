## Ingredients

- 4 cups of flour
- 1 teaspoon of soda
- 1 tablespoon of butter or lard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- buttermilk

## Directions

1. Sift flour and soda together three times
2. Mix 1 tablespoon of butter or lard and 1 teaspoon salt well with flour
3. Add enough buttermilk to make a soft dough
4. Roll and bake in one large cake, or cut like biscuits
5. Bake in very quick oven
