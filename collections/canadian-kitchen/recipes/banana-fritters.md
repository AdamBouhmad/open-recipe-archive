## Ingredients

- 2 ounces flour
- 1 ounce cornstarch
- 1 ounce sugar
- little milk
- bananas

## Directions

1. Make a very thin paste with flour, cornstarch, sugar and milk
2. Cut bananas into small pieces
3. Dip pieces in batter
4. Fry in plenty of smoking fat, very hot
5. Drain on paper
6. Serve hot on a napkin with sugar dusted over
