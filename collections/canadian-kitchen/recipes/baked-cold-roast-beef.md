## Ingredients

- slices of roast beef
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- cold gravy or butter
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Place a layer of the slices in the bottom of a shallow pudding dish
2. Put pepper and salt, and a small slice of onion, on each slice
3. Add cold gravy or little pieces of butter
4. Put in another layer of meat until all is used
5. Cover the top with a layer of mashed potatoes
6. Bake for half an hour, or until the top is nicely browned
