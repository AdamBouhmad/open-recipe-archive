## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 1 cup currants
- butter size of egg
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon all kinds spices
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- pie crust
- jam or jelly
- pink icing

## Directions

1. Line dish with pie crust
2. Spread bottom with jam or jelly
3. Pour in the cake
4. Bake and ice with pink icing
