## Ingredients

- 1 cup thick sour cream
- 1 cup sugar
- teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon soda sifted into flour 3 times
- a little grated nutmeg or seasoning to taste
- cocoanut optional for sprinkling on top
- flour to handle nicely without sticking

## Directions

1. Mix sour cream and sugar together
2. Add salt and soda sifted with flour
3. Add nutmeg or other seasoning to taste
4. Add flour to handle nicely without sticking
5. Optional: sprinkle cocoanut on top and roll in just before cutting
6. Bake in a quick oven
