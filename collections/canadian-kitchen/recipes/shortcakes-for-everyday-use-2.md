## Ingredients

- 9½ pounds flour
- 4 ounces cream tartar
- 2 ounces baking soda
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Sift flour, cream tartar, and baking soda twice together and keep on hand
2. As required, take the amount you wish, add a little salt and a little lard
3. Mix with either buttermilk or sweet milk, buttermilk preferred
4. Roll out about 3½ or ¼ inch thick
5. Cut with cake cutter
6. Bake in very hot oven
