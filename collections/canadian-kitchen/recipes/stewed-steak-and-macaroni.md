## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs. of steak or other lean beef
- ¼ lb. of macaroni
- Butter for frying
- 1 tablespoonful of catsup
- A little flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the beef in small pieces, roll it in flour, and fry slightly in a little butter
2. put it into a stew-pan, cover with hot water, and allow it to simmer slowly for an hour and a half
3. then add the macaroni and simmer again for three-quarters of an hour
4. season with pepper, salt, and catsup, and stew for ten minutes after the seasoning is added
5. Serve on a hot dish, the beef in the centre, and the macaroni round
