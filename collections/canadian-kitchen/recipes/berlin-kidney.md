## Ingredients

- 6 lamb's kidneys
- 1 cup green peas
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoonfuls Worcestershire
- 1 cup gravy with stock
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut lamb kidneys, skin and fry in butter for a few minutes
2. Mix all together until thoroughly heated
