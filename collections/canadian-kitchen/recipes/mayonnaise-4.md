## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup malt vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoonful cayenne
- 2 teaspoonfuls mixed mustard
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls salt
- 2 cups cream
- 4 tablespoonfuls tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs, vinegar and dry ingredients together in a double boiler till they form a thick custard.
2. When cold add two cups cream and four tablespoons tarragon vinegar added drop by drop.
3. Beat for ten minutes.
4. Will keep a fortnight or longer in a cool place.
