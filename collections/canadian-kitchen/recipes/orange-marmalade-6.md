## Ingredients

- 2 dozen bitter oranges
- sugar equal in weight to the oranges
- water

## Directions

1. Weigh the oranges, cut the skin and take it off in quarters
2. Put in a cheese-cloth bag and cook in water nearly two hours, until you can pierce easily with a straw
3. Cut in thin pieces about an inch long
4. Cut the oranges into halves and scrape out the pulp and juice, throwing away the pith
5. Put sugar equal in weight to the oranges into the water you cooked the rinds in, and boil ten minutes
6. Skim and add the rinds and pulp
7. Cook half an hour
8. Dip in tumblers, and set away to cool before sealing
