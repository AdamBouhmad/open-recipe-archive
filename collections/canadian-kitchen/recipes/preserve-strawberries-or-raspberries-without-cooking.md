## Ingredients

- berries
- 3/4 pound sugar to 1 pound berries

## Directions

1. After the berries are picked over put them in a stone jar, or granite kettle
2. Mash with a potato masher
3. Add 3/4 pound sugar to 1 pound berries
4. Stir well until sugar has dissolved
5. Let stand over night and put into sealers in the morning
