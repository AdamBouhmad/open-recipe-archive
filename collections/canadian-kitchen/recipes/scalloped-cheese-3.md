## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 lb. grated cheese
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Roll bread crumbs, soak until soft in milk; mix with eggs beaten light, add grated cheese and butter in small pieces.
2. Salt and pepper to taste.
3. Put all in baking dish and cover the top with sifted bread crumbs which have been buttered, peppered and salted.
4. Bake fifteen minutes.
