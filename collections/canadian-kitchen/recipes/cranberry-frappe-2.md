## Ingredients

- 1 quart cranberries
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups water
- juice 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook cranberries and water 8 minutes
2. Force through a sieve
3. Add sugar and lemon juice
4. Freeze to a mush using equal portions of ice and salt
