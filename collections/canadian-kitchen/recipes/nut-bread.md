## Ingredients

- 4 scant cups flour
- 4 heaping teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1½ cups sweet milk
- generous pinch salt
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Sift flour and powder 3 times
2. Mix well with sugar, nuts and salt
3. Add milk, stirring all together quickly
4. Put in a well-greased pan and let rise 20 minutes
5. Bake in a moderate oven ¾ hr
6. This bread is very nice with a cup of chopped raisins in place of walnuts, or with ¾ cup of each
