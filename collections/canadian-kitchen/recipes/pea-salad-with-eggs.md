## Ingredients

- cold hard-boiled eggs
- green peas
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut cold hard-boiled eggs in the centre
2. Carefully remove the yolks and cut into small bits
3. Mix with green peas, strained fairly dry, and heated and seasoned
4. Pour carefully into the halves of the eggs
5. Serve on lettuce leaves with mayonnaise dressing
