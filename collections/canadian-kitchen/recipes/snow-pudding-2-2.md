## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 pint boiling water
- cold water
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- whites of 2 eggs
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 white of egg
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Moisten 3 tablespoons starch with cold water
2. Stir over boiling water until cooked
3. Add juice of 2 lemons
4. Add 1 cup granulated sugar
5. Set away to cool
6. Add whites of 2 eggs
7. Make sauce with yolks of 2 eggs, 1 white of egg and 1 cup sugar
8. Stir till cooked
9. Use when cold over snow pudding
