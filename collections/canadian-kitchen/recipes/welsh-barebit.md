## Ingredients

- Butter size of egg
- 2 or 3 cups grated cheese
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 pt. beer
- A little onion juice
- Salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add cheese and seasoning, then beer and beaten eggs
