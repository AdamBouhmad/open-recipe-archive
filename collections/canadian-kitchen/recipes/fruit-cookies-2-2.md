## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup shortening (butter and lard mixed)
- 1 beaten egg
- 4 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- flour
- grated nutmeg
- cinnamon
- ground cloves

## Directions

1. Seed and chop finely 1 cup raisins; flour these and set them aside
2. Beat together 1 1/2 cups brown sugar and 1 cup shortening (butter and lard mixed)
3. When creamy, add 1 beaten egg, 4 tablespoons sweet milk, 1 teaspoon baking soda and enough flour to make a stiff batter
4. Add floured raisins with grated nutmeg, cinnamon and ground cloves to taste
5. Add enough more flour to make a stiff dough
6. Roll rather thin and bake in quick oven
