## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 cup raisins
- butter size of an egg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour to make a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients to make a stiff batter
2. Steam 1 hour
