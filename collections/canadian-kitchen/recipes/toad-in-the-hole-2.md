## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound beef, mutton or sausage
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- 1 egg
- 1/4 pound flour
- 1/2 pint milk

## Directions

1. If beef or mutton is used, cut it into pieces about an inch square
2. If sausage is used, prick them and boil them 2 or 3 minutes and then remove the skin
3. Grease a pie dish
4. Put the meat or sausage at the bottom
5. Sprinkle seasoning over it
6. Make the batter as for pancakes
7. Pour it over the meat
8. Bake in hot oven for 3/4 hour
