## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes sliced
- 6 large onions sliced
- 1 cup salt
- 2 gallons vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- 2 ounces white mustard seed
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons ginger
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Slice green tomatoes and onions
2. Sprinkle on salt and let remain over night
3. In the morning drain through a colander
4. Take vinegar, sugar, mustard seed, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and cayenne pepper
5. Boil all gently 15 minutes and pour over tomatoes and onions
