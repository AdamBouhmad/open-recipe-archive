## Ingredients

- 2 cups yellow sugar
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 3/4 pounds currants
- 1 3/4 pounds raisins
- 1 pound dates
- 5 cents' worth shelled almonds or walnuts
- 3 cents' worth lemon peel
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 3/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon allspice
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- nutmeg grated
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. This cake must bake very slowly
