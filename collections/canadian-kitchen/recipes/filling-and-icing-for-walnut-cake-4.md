## Ingredients

- yolks of 5 eggs
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Stir together egg yolks, sour cream, and sugar
2. Cook till it thickens, stirring all the time as it burns very quickly
3. When cold, add chopped walnuts
