## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons of gelatine
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 1 cup of boiling water
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- 1 large cup of shelled walnuts

## Directions

1. Rub tomatoes through sieve
2. Soften 3 tablespoons of gelatine in 2 tablespoons cold water
3. Add 1 cup of boiling water and stir
4. When gelatine is dissolved, strain and mix with tomatoes
5. Season with pepper, salt and sugar to taste
6. Have ready 1 large cup of shelled walnuts
7. Mix into the mixture when it begins to thicken
8. Pour into mould
