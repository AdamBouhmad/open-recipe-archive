## Ingredients

- 1 cooked chicken
- mushroom
- ham
- truffles
- white sauce
- macaroni, cooked

## Directions

1. Cut a cooked chicken into small pieces
2. Chop up mushroom, ham, and truffles, and stir into white sauce
3. Line a mould with pieces of macaroni, cooked, cut in even lengths
4. Fill in with the volaille
5. Steam one hour
6. Turn out very carefully
7. Serve with white or brown sauce
