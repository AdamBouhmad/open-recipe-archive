## Ingredients

- salsify
- pepper
- salt
- one tablespoonful butter
- half a cup milk
- bread-crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the salsify until perfectly tender, then mash through a strainer
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Add a tablespoonful of butter and half a cup of milk
4. Put in a bake-dish, cover with bread-crumbs and bits of butter, and bake fifteen minutes
