## Ingredients

- white tender celery
- two eggs
- mayonnaise dressing
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. With a sharp knife cut white tender celery into bits a quarter of an inch long until you have a cupful.
2. Mix with it two minced eggs that have been boiled twenty-five minutes, then left in cold water until they have cooled to the heart.
3. Chop them fine and rub through a coarse sieve.
4. Work up well with the celery and beat in two tablespoonfuls of mayonnaise dressing.
5. Spread between thin slices of buttered bread.
