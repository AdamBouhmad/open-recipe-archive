## Ingredients

- 1 orange or lemon grated
- 1 egg beaten well
- 1/2 to 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Put the grated lemon or orange in a dish and set in a pot of boiling water
2. When nearly boiling, pour in the beaten egg and sugar
3. Let it boil until nicely thickened
