## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups butter and lard mixed
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 pint molasses
- 3/4 cup warm water
- 3 teaspoons soda
- 3 teaspoons ginger
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons cloves or other spice
- 2 eggs
- flour to thicken

## Directions

1. Boil mixture
2. When cool add 2 eggs and flour to thicken
