## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 2 large potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- 1 quart of milk

## Directions

1. Chop carrot, onion, and potatoes fine
2. Boil and put through a colander
3. Add pepper and salt to taste
4. Add a good-sized piece of butter and milk
5. Let come to a boil and serve
