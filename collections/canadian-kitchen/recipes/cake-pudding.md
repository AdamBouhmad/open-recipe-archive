## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1/2 cup of sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking-powder
- a little preserve

## Directions

1. Butter the mould
2. Put in a little preserve and then your batter
3. Steam one hour
