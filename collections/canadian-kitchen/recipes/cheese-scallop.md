## Ingredients

- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- fresh milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3½ pound grated cheese
- sifted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Soak 1 cup dry bread crumbs in fresh milk
2. Beat into this 3 eggs
3. Add 1 tablespoon butter and 3½ pound grated cheese
4. Strew sifted bread crumbs upon the top
5. Bake in the oven a delicate brown
