## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup flour
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
- preserves

## Directions

1. Butter your cups
2. Pour in small quantity of preserves
3. Fill with batter half-way
4. Steam 20 minutes
5. This will make 8 medium-size cups
