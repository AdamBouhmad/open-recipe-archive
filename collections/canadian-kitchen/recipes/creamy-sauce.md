## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls wine
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. Cream butter, add sugar slowly, then wine and cream.
2. Beat well and just before using, place bowl over hot water and stir till creamy, but not enough to melt the butter.
3. When the wine and cream are added, the sauce has a curdled appearance. This is removed by beating and heating enough to blend materials.
4. Sauce should be cold when served.
