## Ingredients

- 1 basket pears
- Juice of 6 lemons and rind of 4
- ½ lb. ginger root, well pounded
- ½ lb. sugar to each pound of fruit
- cloves

## Directions

1. Peel pears, leaving the stem
2. Put on with as little water as possible and let them stew a short time
3. Take them out, leaving the juice in the kettle
4. Add sugar, lemon rind and juice, and if desired any spice can be added in a muslin bag
5. Let all boil together for ten minutes
6. Add the pears with a clove or two stuck in each pear
7. Boil till the pears are tender
