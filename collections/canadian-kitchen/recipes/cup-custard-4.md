## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- a little salt
- 1 quart milk
- nutmeg for flavor

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs to a light froth
2. Add 1 cup granulated sugar, a little salt, and 1 quart milk
3. Flavor with nutmeg
4. Put the custard in 5 cups
5. Place cups in a pan of boiling water
6. Cook them until firm in the centre
7. When done stand away to cool
8. Serve it in the cups
