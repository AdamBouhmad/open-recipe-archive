## Ingredients

- chicken bones
- slice of carrot
- slice of onion
- parsley
- one bay leaf
- three cloves
- twelve peppercorns
- celery
- knuckle of veal
- water
- cheesecloth

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken and roll it lightly in a piece of cheesecloth, tying the ends well.
2. Put in a saucepan the bones of the chicken, a slice of carrot and onion, a bouquet containing parsley, one bay leaf, three cloves, twelve peppercorns, celery if convenient, and a knuckle of veal.
3. Add enough water to cover the bed of vegetables and bones.
4. Lay in the chicken and cover the pot.
5. Let it simmer for four hours.
