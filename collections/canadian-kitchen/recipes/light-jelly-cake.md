## Ingredients

- Whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 1 pint salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Bake in jelly tins
