## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups water
- 1/4 teaspoon cream tartar

## Directions

1. Stir sugar, water, and cream tartar until dissolved
2. Let boil until it reaches soft ball stage
3. Stand at once in pan of cold water
4. When cooled enough that you can stand your finger in it stir vigorously with stick
5. Never jar saucepan after it starts to boil
6. Do not attempt on wet day
7. Keep crystals wiped off sides
