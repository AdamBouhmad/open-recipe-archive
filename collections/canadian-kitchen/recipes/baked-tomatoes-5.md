## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe tomatoes, then cut in two
2. Place them in a baking dish with the skin side down
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Place in a hot oven
5. Take up carefully when done and put bits of butter on each piece of tomato
