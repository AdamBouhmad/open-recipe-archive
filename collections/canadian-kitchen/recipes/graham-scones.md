## Ingredients

- 1 cup white flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- a little salt
- 1 cup Graham flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup shortening
- 1 egg
- cold water

## Directions

1. Sift together 1 cup white flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder and a little salt.
2. Add 1 cup Graham flour, 1/2 cup sugar and 1/4 cup shortening.
3. Beat up 1 egg in a saucer and leave awhile.
4. Pour out 3/4 egg in a cup and fill up with cold water and mix to a soft dough.
5. Roll out in squares and brush with egg left in saucer.
