## Ingredients

- 12 large cucumbers
- 12 onions
- salt
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 teaspoons tumeric
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- white pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice cucumbers and onions
2. Sprinkle with salt and let stand over night
3. In the morning drain
4. Cover with vinegar
5. Add sugar, tumeric, mustard, and white pepper
6. Cook 1/2 hour
