## Ingredients

- one quart of lukewarm milk
- one Fleischmann's yeast cake
- flour enough to make stiff sponge
- cinnamon and sugar
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. sift flour twice before using
2. knead till batter shows large bubbles, mix in evening
3. next morning knead again
4. put batter about three-quarters high in tins, let raise till twice this size
5. glaze with melted butter, and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar over top (or chopped almonds instead of cinnamon)
6. Bake in medium hot oven
7. Serve with coffee
