## Ingredients

- 1 pt. water
- 3 tablespoonfuls grated cocoa
- 1 pt. hot milk
- a bit of soda

## Directions

1. Boil a pint of water
2. Rub three tablespoonfuls of grated cocoa to a smooth paste with cold water
3. Stir into the hot water
4. Boil ten minutes, hard
5. Pour upon it a pint of hot milk with a bit of soda in it
6. Boil for ten minutes longer, stirring and beating well
7. Sweeten in the cups
