## Ingredients

- large Bermuda or Spanish onions
- force-meat of minced meat, veal, ham, or chicken
- fine crumbs
- salt
- cayenne
- butter
- crumbs
- weak stock

## Directions

1. Peel large Bermuda or Spanish onions, and parboil them for ten minutes.
2. Drain, and let them get perfectly cold.
3. With a sharp knife dig out the centre from each and fill with a force-meat of minced meat, veal, ham, or chicken, well seasoned, and mixed with one-third as much fine crumbs.
4. Season with salt and cayenne and a little butter.
5. Set the stuffed onions close together in a dish, fill the interstices with crumbs, and scatter more over the top.
6. Pour about them enough weak stock to keep them from burning — about an inch in the bottom of the dish will do — and cook, covered, half an hour.
7. Uncover and brown lightly.
