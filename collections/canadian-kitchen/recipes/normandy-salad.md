## Ingredients

- One pint of French peas
- One pint of English walnuts
- Half a head lettuce
- Four nasturtiums
- Salt
- Mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Use the small French peas. Pour them into a colander, rinse in cold water, drain and dry on a towel.
2. Blanch the walnuts by letting them remain in boiling water for a few minutes, then cut into small pieces the size of the peas.
3. Sprinkle them with salt, and mix together, with enough mayonnaise to hold together.
4. Arrange on lettuce leaves, with bright yellow nasturtium here and there between the leaves.
5. Then cover the nuts and peas with the remainder of the mayonnaise.
