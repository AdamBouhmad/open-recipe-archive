## Ingredients

- stale cake
- 1 large sweet orange
- 2 cups sweet milk
- 1 egg
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 teaspoons vanilla

## Directions

1. Break up cake in small pieces in salad dish
2. Cut orange in small pieces and arrange over the cake
3. Make a cream of sweet milk, egg, sugar, and flour enough to make a thin cream
4. Let come to boil and take from stove
5. Add vanilla
6. Pour over the whole and let cool before serving
