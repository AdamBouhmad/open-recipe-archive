## Ingredients

- Juice of four lemons
- two tablespoonfuls onion juice
- six tablespoonfuls tomato catsup
- four tablespoonfuls grated horseradish
- ten drops of tobasco
- one small teaspoonful salt
- sufficient vinegar to make a thin sauce

## Directions

1. Mix juice of four lemons, onion juice, tomato catsup, horseradish, tobasco, and salt
2. Add sufficient vinegar to make a thin sauce
