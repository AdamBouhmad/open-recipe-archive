## Ingredients

- sardines in oil
- lemon juice
- cracker dust
- pepper
- butter
- crackers

## Directions

1. Lift each fish carefully from the oil in which it was put up
2. Hold suspended for a moment to let most of the oil drip from it
3. Squeeze a few drops of lemon-juice upon it and roll in very fine, peppered cracker-dust
4. Lay upon a buttered tin, or stoneware plate
5. Brown lightly upon the upper grating of a quick oven
6. Pass crackers, heated and buttered, and sliced lemon with them
