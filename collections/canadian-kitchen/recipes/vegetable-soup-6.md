## Ingredients

- One quart of common stock
- One pint of parboiled mixed vegetables
- Salt
- Pepper
- One teaspoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. To one quart of common stock add one pint of parboiled mixed vegetables cut into small dice
2. Simmer until the vegetables are tender but not pasty
3. Season with salt, pepper, and one teaspoonful of sugar
4. Serve without straining
