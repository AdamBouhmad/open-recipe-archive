## Ingredients

- one cup pearl tapioca
- one cup sugar
- one teaspoonful vanilla
- one pint grated or finely chopped pineapple
- plain or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca over night
2. Boil in the morning until clear
3. Add one cup sugar, one teaspoonful vanilla, one pint grated or finely chopped pineapple
4. Set away to cool
5. Serve with plain or whipped cream
