## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon milk to every egg used
- 1 extra tablespoon
- 5 eggs (for 3 people)
- 6 tablespoons milk (for 3 people)
- salt and pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well, add milk, pinch salt and pepper.
2. Have pan hot, well greased with butter, and pour mixture in.
3. After it begins to set, put in hot oven for few minutes.
4. Loosen with knife and turn half over.
5. Serve to be eaten immediately.
