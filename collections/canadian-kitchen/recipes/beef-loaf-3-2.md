## Ingredients

- 3 pounds beef
- 3/4 pound lean pork
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- water
- 1 small onion
- pepper
- salt
- sage

## Directions

1. Put beef through grinder
2. Put pork through grinder
3. Soak bread crumbs in water
4. Mix beef, pork, eggs, bread crumbs, onion, pepper, salt, and sage
5. Bake 1 1/2 hours
