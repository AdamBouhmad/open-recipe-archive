## Ingredients

- 1 quart stock
- 1 can corn, chopped (or 8 ears)
- 1 tablespoonful butter mixed with 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1 tablespoonful minced parsley
- 1 tablespoonful green onion tops
- 1 cup boiling milk
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Clear stock of fat after it is cold
2. Boil corn and liquor slowly together one hour after they begin to bubble
3. Rub thoroughly through a colander
4. Season and add herbs
5. Heat to boiling and stir in the floured butter
6. Simmer five minutes
7. Pour into the tureen and add the boiling milk
