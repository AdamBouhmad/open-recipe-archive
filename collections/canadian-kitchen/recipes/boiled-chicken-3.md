## Ingredients

- chicken
- cheesecloth
- boiled rice
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- celery
- white sauce
- chopped parsley
- oyster sauce or Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Truss the chicken firmly.
2. Tie it in a piece of cheesecloth, to keep it in good shape.
3. It may be stuffed or not.
4. Put the chicken into boiling salted water and simmer, allowing twenty minutes to the pound.
5. When done, remove the cloth and cords carefully.
6. Spread a little white sauce over the breast, and sprinkle it with chopped parsley.
7. Garnish with parsley, and serve with it oyster, or Bearnaise sauce.
