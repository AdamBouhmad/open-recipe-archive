## Ingredients

- 50 oysters
- 2 tablespoonfuls chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoonfuls chopped celery
- 2 eggs
- 1 pint bread-crumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- oyster liquid

## Directions

1. Drain oysters
2. Beat two eggs and add to them a tablespoonful of oyster liquid
3. Put on a board a pint of bread-crumbs
4. Have at your left side an ordinary baking-dish
5. Lift the oysters by the muscular part
6. Dip them in egg, then in bread-crumbs
7. Put them at once in the bottom of the baking-dish
8. Sprinkle over half a teaspoonful of salt, a dash of pepper, a sprinkling of parsley and celery
9. Dip and put in another layer of oysters, etc., until all are used
10. Cut small pieces of butter over the top
11. Bake in a quick oven for fifteen or twenty minutes
12. Serve in the dish in which they were baked
