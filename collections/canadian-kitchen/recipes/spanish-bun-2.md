## Ingredients

- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 0.25 cup butter
- a pinch of salt
- 0.25 cup milk
- flour to make batter
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar

## Directions

1. Bake in moderate oven
2. Beat icing (whites of 3 eggs and 1 cup brown sugar) five minutes
3. Spread icing on the cake
4. Put in the oven and brown
