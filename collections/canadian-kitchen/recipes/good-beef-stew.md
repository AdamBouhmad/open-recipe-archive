## Ingredients

- a good round steak, two or three pounds
- butter
- 1 pint of water
- pepper
- salt
- minced onion
- stoned olives
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Brown steak well on both sides in butter
2. Add a pint of water
3. Cook very slowly well covered two hours
4. Add pepper, salt and minced onion if liked
5. Cook half an hour longer
6. Add stoned olives and mushrooms
