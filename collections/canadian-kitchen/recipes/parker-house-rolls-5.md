## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- 3/4 cup lukewarm water
- flour
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 White Swan Yeast Cake in 3/4 cup lukewarm water
2. Add 3 tablespoons butter, 2 tablespoons sugar and 1 teaspoon salt to milk when lukewarm
3. Add dissolved yeast cake and three cups of flour
4. Beat well, cover and let rise until light
5. Cut down and add enough flour to knead (it will take about two and a half cups)
6. Let rise again
7. Toss on floured board and knead
8. Roll out to one-third inch thickness
9. Shape with biscuit cutter first dipped in flour
10. Dip the handle of a knife in flour and with it make a crease through the middle of each piece
11. Brush over one half of each piece with melted butter
12. Fold and press edges together
13. Place in a greased pan
14. Let rise and bake in a hot oven fifteen minutes
