## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup white flour
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- enough Graham flour to make stiff batter

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar with the eggs
2. Add the sour cream and salt
3. Sift in the white flour with baking powder in
4. Mix thoroughly and then add enough Graham flour to make a stiff batter
