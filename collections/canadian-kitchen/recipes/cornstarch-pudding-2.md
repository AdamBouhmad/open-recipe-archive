## Ingredients

- 1 pint sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- salt
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil milk
2. Add sugar and cornstarch
3. Boil until thick
4. Beat the whites of 3 eggs to a stiff froth and add just before removing from stove
5. Flavor with vanilla
