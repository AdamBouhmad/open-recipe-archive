## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 teaspoon arrowroot
- 1 tablespoon kirsch
- 2 tablespoons shredded almonds
- candied cherries

## Directions

1. Put sugar into saucepan with boiling water
2. let it boil 5 minutes
3. add arrowroot moistened with a little water and cook till clear
4. Remove from fire
5. flavor with kirsch
6. add shredded almonds and candied cherries. Cut into small pieces
