## Ingredients

- 2 cups lard
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 2 eggs
- ¾ teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 cups sugar
- flour to roll

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out
3. Bake
