## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1/2 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon each of nutmeg, cloves, cinnamon
- 1 cup currants

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll and bake
