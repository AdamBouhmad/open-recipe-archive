## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup cornstarch
- 1/4 cup butter
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 teaspoonful flavoring
- 1 cup flour
- Whites of 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar together, add milk and flavoring, sift in flour, cornstarch, and baking powder mixed together, lastly add the whites of eggs beaten to a stiff froth.
2. Bake in a square pan and ice with white icing.
