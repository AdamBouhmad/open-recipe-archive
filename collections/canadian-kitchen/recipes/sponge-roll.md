## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Jelly or jam

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and sugar together
2. slowly pour over them the boiling water
3. stir in the sifted flour and lastly the beaten whites of the eggs, retaining a little of the flour mixed with the baking powder until the very last
4. Bake in thin layers and while hot spread with jelly or jam and roll
