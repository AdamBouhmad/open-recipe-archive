## Ingredients

- Vermicelli or spaghetti
- Salt
- 1 quart cleared boiling stock

## Directions

1. Break the vermicelli or spaghetti into inch lengths, and cook tender and clear in boiling salted water.
2. Drain this off; spread the vermicelli upon a dish and allow it to get almost cold, when drop into a quart of cleared boiling stock.
3. Let it just boil again, and serve.
4. The pipe macaroni may be used in like manner, cut into quarter-inch lengths after it is cooked.
