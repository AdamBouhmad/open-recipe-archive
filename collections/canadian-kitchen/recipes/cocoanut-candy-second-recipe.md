## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup water
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cocoanut

## Directions

1. Cook sugar, water and vinegar to soft ball stage
2. Add cocoanut and beat until creamy
3. Pour out on buttered plate
