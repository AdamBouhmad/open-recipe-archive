## Ingredients

- two turkey livers
- legs of two partridges
- forcemeat
- one and one half cups bread-crumbs
- one half teaspoonful cloves
- one half teaspoonful cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- water
- paste

## Directions

1. Chop up the livers very fine, also the meat of the partridge legs
2. Add the bread-crumbs after having put them through a sifter
3. Add pepper and salt to taste
4. Mix all together and moisten with water
5. Put it in a pan and cook for half an hour, keeping it moist and turning over often
6. Put in a pie dish and cover with paste and bake
7. To be eaten cold
