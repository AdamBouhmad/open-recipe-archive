## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 oz. chocolate, melted
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat butter to a cream and gradually beat into this one cupful of sugar, chocolate melted, also two unbeaten eggs
2. Beat vigorously for five minutes
3. then stir in milk, flour, with a generous teaspoonful of baking powder, flavor with vanilla
4. pour into a buttered shallow cake-pan and bake for half an hour in a moderate oven
5. Any frosting can be used
