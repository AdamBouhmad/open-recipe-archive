## Ingredients

- prairie chicken or grouse
- butter
- lemon-juice
- salt
- pepper
- fat salt pork
- currant jelly
- bread sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and wipe the birds
2. Test them, and if they are tough, put them—trussed as for roasting—into a steamer and set over hard-boiling water for half an hour
3. While still hot rub them well with butter and lemon-juice, salt and pepper, inside and out
4. Put a small bit of fat salt pork in each
5. Roast, covered, in a quick oven for half an hour
6. Baste three times with butter and hot water, and, just before taking them up, with butter alone
7. Serve with currant jelly and bread sauce
