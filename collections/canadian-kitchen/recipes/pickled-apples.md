## Ingredients

- 1 peck sweet apples
- 3 pounds sugar
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1/2 ounce cinnamon
- 1/2 ounce cloves

## Directions

1. Pare the apples, leaving them whole
2. Boil them in part of the vinegar and sugar until you can put a fork through them
3. Take them out
4. Heat the remainder of the vinegar and sugar
5. Pour over them
6. Be careful not to boil them too long or they will break
