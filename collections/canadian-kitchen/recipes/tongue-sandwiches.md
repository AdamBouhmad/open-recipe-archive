## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely chopped tongue
- 1/4 cup chopped boiled ham
- 3 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls salad oil
- 1/4 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1/4 teaspoonful paprica
- Yolk of 1 egg
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Mix tongue with ham
2. Stir in melted butter beaten light with salad oil, mustard, and paprica
3. When the mixture is smooth and light set in a saucepan of boiling water over the fire and cook until it is thoroughly heated
4. Beat in the yolk of a whipped egg
5. Take from the fire and set by until perfectly cold
6. Spread between thin slices of bread
