## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- vanilla
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon allspice
- 1/2 nutmeg
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- jelly
- white icing

## Directions

1. To half the cake add molasses, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, and nutmeg
2. Bake in jelly tins, two layers light and two layers dark
3. Place the layers alternately, having a light one on top
4. Spread jelly between the layers and over all spread white icing
