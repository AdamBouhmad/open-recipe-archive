## Ingredients

- Whites of 4 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream tartar
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat butter in sugar, add milk, flour, nuts, eggs last
2. then bake slowly
3. Frost with chocolate frosting, with half walnuts on top
