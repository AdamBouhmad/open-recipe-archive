## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- ½ pound shortening
- Ice-cold water

## Directions

1. If the fingers are cool, rub the shortening into the flour; otherwise, cut it into it with two knives until thoroughly mixed with the flour
2. Mix into a dough with the ice water, using only enough to hold it together
3. Turn out on the floured board, and knead only enough to make the ball smooth
4. Cut into pieces for rolling out to line pie-plates, etc.
