## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1.5 cups brown sugar
- 4 yolks of eggs
- butter size of an egg
- 10 cents' worth chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Boil until thick
2. Add 10 cents' worth chopped walnuts
