## Ingredients

- 2 doz. Seville oranges
- 3 lemons
- 16 lbs. sugar
- 8½ quarts cold water

## Directions

1. Cut fruit into thin slices and take out seeds
2. Put in an earthenware pan and cover with eight and one-half quarts of cold water
3. Let it stand for twenty-four hours
4. Boil quickly for two hours, then add sugar and boil again one and one-half hours
5. The seeds should be soaked for twenty-four hours in one pint of cold water; when ready to put in the sugar pour the liquid off them through a strainer and add to the oranges
