## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups turnips
- 1 1/2 cups carrots
- 1 3/4 quarts water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 4 nice-sized parsnips
- 3 large onions
- 1 teaspoon summer savory
- A little pepper
- Butter
- 1 heaping tablespoon sifted flour

## Directions

1. Boil turnips and carrots in water with salt for 1 1/2 hours
2. Add parsnips, onions, summer savory, pepper and butter and boil 1/2 hour longer
3. Add sifted flour to thicken
