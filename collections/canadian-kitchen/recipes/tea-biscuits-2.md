## Ingredients

- 5 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup cream
- sour milk

## Directions

1. Add 1 teaspoon soda, 2 teaspoons cream tartar, 1 teaspoon salt to 5 cups flour
2. Mix together well
3. Add 1 cup cream and enough sour milk to make a nice soft dough
4. Bake in a good hot oven
