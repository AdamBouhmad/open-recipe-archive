## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 3/4 cups sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- yolk of another egg
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, shortening, milk, flour and baking powder
2. Bake in 3 layers
3. For Lemon Honey Filling: Mix lemon juice, grated rind, 1 cup granulated sugar, 1 egg and yolk of another egg with 1 tablespoon butter
4. Beat well and cook in double boiler until transparent
5. When cool spread between layers of white cake
