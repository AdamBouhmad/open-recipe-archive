## Ingredients

- 4 cups white flour
- 1 egg
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- pinch salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Pour in baking pan
3. Let stand 1/4 hour
4. Bake
