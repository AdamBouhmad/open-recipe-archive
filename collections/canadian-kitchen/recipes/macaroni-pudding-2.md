## Ingredients

- eight or ten long sticks of macaroni
- boiling water
- a quart of milk
- a cup of sugar
- a slice of butter
- three eggs
- vanilla
- oil of cloves
- oil of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Break macaroni into pieces an inch long or less.
2. Throw into a saucepan with plenty of boiling water and cook for twenty minutes.
3. Drain away the water and boil again more slowly for another twenty minutes in a quart of milk with a cup of sugar and a slice of butter.
4. Turn into a pudding dish and allow to cool.
5. Beat in three eggs, flavor with vanilla, oil of cloves or oil of cinnamon, and bake slowly.
6. Or use four eggs, keeping two whites to be beaten stiffly for the top.
7. Brown in the oven for a minute or two.
