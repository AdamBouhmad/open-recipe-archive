## Ingredients

- 1 oz. kidney
- 1 qt. second stock
- 1 tablespoonful mushroom catsup
- 1 tablespoonful Harvey sauce
- 1 oz. butter
- 1 oz. flour of rice
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Wash and pepper, and roll kidney into it, then put in pan with butter and brown quickly
2. Add part of the stock and let it come to the boil and cook a few minutes
3. Put in saucepan with the rest of the stock, and leave to simmer two hours
