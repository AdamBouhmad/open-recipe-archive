## Ingredients

- 1 large handful of hops
- 1 gallon of water
- 5 medium-sized potatoes
- a little salt
- sugar
- ginger
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake

## Directions

1. Boil 1 large handful of hops in a gallon of water for half an hour
2. Grate 5 medium-sized potatoes and strain the boiling liquid over it, stirring well
3. Add a little salt, sugar and ginger
4. Let cool, then add 1 White Swan Yeast Cake and put in a warm place to rise
5. Stir frequently
6. It is best made in a large dish and afterwards put into a jar and corked tight
