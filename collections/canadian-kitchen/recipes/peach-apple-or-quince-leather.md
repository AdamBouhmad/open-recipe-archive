## Ingredients

- One pound of the fruit (apples and quince of equal quantities, or of either separately)
- One pound sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut up the fruit in small pieces
2. Put on to boil with as little water as possible
3. While boiling mash fine with a spoon
4. Add one pound of granulated sugar and boil about fifteen or twenty minutes longer
5. Spread the mass very thin on a flat tin or platter to dry
6. In the south it is put out of doors in the sun under netting and brought in at night
7. It can be done equally well by using a cool oven, leaving the door open, so that it may stay there at night as well
8. It must not cool, only dry
9. Several days are required in drying
10. Cut in strips, roll in sugar and roll up or leave in plain lengths
