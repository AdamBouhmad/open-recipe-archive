## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1½ dozen soda biscuits
- 1 cup sweet milk
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Roll soda biscuits fine
2. Work salmon, biscuits, milk, pepper and salt all together
3. Form in lumps the size of eggs or larger
4. Cook in a hot buttered pan till a nice brown
5. Turn once
