## Ingredients

- Leftover fish
- Mashed potatoes
- A lump of butter
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 tablespoon cream
- Pepper and salt
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mince the fish fine
2. Add a few more mashed potatoes than you have fish
3. Mix thoroughly, add a lump of butter, 2 beaten eggs, 1 tablespoon cream, pepper and salt
4. Fry in lard
