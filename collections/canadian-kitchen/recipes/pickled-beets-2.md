## Ingredients

- young beets
- vinegar
- sugar
- spices as liked

## Directions

1. Cook beets till tender
2. Peel beets
3. Place in bottles whole
4. Pour hot vinegar over beets
5. Seal bottles
6. Will keep 2 years
7. If too large, may be sliced or quartered
