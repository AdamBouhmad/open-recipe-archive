## Ingredients

- 1 qt. firm tomatoes
- 2 raw eggs, beaten light
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1 teaspoonful powdered sugar
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- Pinch of cayenne

## Directions

1. Pound the boiled yolks; rub in the butter and seasoning.
2. Beat light, add the vinegar, and heat almost to a boil.
3. Stir in the beaten egg until the mixture begins to thicken.
4. Set in hot water while you cut the tomatoes in slices nearly half an inch thick.
5. Broil over a clear fire upon a wire oyster-broiler.
6. Lay on a hot-water dish, and pour the hot sauce over them.
