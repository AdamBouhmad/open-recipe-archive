## Ingredients

- 6 or 7 fine apples
- 1 cup fine breadcrumbs
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- Nutmeg and a little grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and slice the apples
2. Stew in a covered double saucepan, without a drop of water, until they are tender
3. Mash to a smooth pulp
4. While hot, stir in the butter and sugar
5. Let it get quite cold
6. Whip in first the yolks of the eggs, then the whites beaten very stiff, alternately with the breadcrumbs
7. Flavor and beat quickly three minutes, until all the ingredients are reduced to a creamy batter
8. Bake in a buttered dish, in a moderate oven
9. It will take about an hour to cook it properly
10. Keep it covered until ten minutes before you take it out
