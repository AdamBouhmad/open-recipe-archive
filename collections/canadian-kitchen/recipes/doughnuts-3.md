## Ingredients

- 3 well-beaten eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a pinch salt
- 1 nutmeg
- flour to roll out

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well
2. Mix in sugar, sour cream, soda, salt and nutmeg
3. Add enough flour to roll out
4. Cut with doughnut cutter
5. Fry in hot lard
