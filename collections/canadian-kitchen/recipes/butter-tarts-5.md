## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon water
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 2 teaspoons flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Stir well
2. Put in tart shells
3. Bake in oven
