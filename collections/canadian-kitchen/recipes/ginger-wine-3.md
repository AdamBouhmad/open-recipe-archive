## Ingredients

- tartaric acid (1 ounce)
- essence of ginger (0.5 ounce)
- essence cayenne (0.75 ounce)
- burnt sugar (1 ounce)
- lemons (3)
- white sugar (3 pounds)
- boiling water (6 quarts)
- fruit coloring

## Directions

1. Slice 3 lemons or squeeze out the juice
2. Pour over all six quarts boiling water
3. Let stand until cold
4. Bottle ready for use
5. Use a little fruit coloring if desired a darker color
