## Ingredients

- 1 cup icing sugar
- 2 tablespoons thick sweet cream
- 3 or 4 drops rose water
- 1/2 teaspoon cocoa

## Directions

1. Stir till smooth
2. Spread on cake
3. Sprinkle with chopped nuts
