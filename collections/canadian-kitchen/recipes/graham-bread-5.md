## Ingredients

- 2 cups white flour
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- 1 3/4 quarts warm water
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 breadspoons baking syrup
- about 1 3/4 sifters white flour
- Graham flour
- 2 handfuls salt

## Directions

1. At noon scald 2 cups white flour and let it stand until lukewarm
2. Add 1 soaked White Swan Yeast Cake
3. Set in a warm place to rise
4. At night put 1 3/4 quarts warm water, 1 cup sugar, 4 breadspoons baking syrup and about 1 3/4 sifters white flour with the sponge
5. Finish setting with Graham flour
6. In the morning put in 2 handfuls salt and stiffen with white flour
7. Set to rise
8. Put in pan and let rise again
9. This will make 5 or 6 loaves
