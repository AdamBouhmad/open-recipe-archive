## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 egg beaten
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1/4 cup white flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix 3/4 cup butter and 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
2. Add 1 cup raisins and 1 egg beaten
3. Add 1 cup sour milk with 1 teaspoon soda
4. Add 2 cups Graham flour, 1/4 cup white flour, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
5. Bake
