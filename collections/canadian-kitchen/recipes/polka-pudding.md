## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls arrow root
- 4 eggs
- 3 oz. fresh butter
- 1 teacup white sugar
- 1 dessertspoonful rose water
- a few drops essence of lemon
- 3 pts. of milk

## Directions

1. Mix arrowroot in a pint of cold milk
2. Beat eggs well and add butter (cut into small bits), rose water, essence of lemon or ratafia, sugar
3. Boil two pints of milk in a double boiler
4. When boiling stir in the other ingredients, without taking the boiler off the stove
5. Let it boil till thick, then pour into a mould to cool
6. Turn it out and serve cold
