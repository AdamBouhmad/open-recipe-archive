## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 3/4 cup cold water
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
- a little cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Put on the stove in a granite pan just to simmer 1/2 cup vinegar, 3/4 cup cold water, 1 cup white sugar, and 1 teaspoon butter
2. Beat up 2 eggs; take in another dish, 1 teaspoon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 3/4 teaspoon black pepper, mix with a little cold vinegar, add this to the eggs and stir into the pan
3. Stand over it and stir till it boils up, then let get cold and pour over your cabbage, having it chopped up fine
