## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- butter size of an egg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon lemon
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 scant cups flour

## Directions

1. Cream together 1 cup sugar and butter size of an egg
2. Add 1 cup milk and 1 teaspoon lemon
3. Add 3 teaspoons baking powder sifted in 3 scant cups flour
4. Mix all together
