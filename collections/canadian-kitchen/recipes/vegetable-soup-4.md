## Ingredients

- 1 quart common stock
- 1 pint parboiled mixed vegetables cut into small dice
- salt
- pepper
- 1 teaspoonful sugar

## Directions

1. Add parboiled mixed vegetables to stock
2. Simmer until vegetables are tender but not pasty
3. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar
4. Serve without straining
