## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups sour milk
- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 2 teaspoons baking soda

## Directions

1. moisten with hot water
2. Bake in gem rings
