## Ingredients

- 1 pound stoned raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound stale bread crumbs
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 3/2 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon mixed spice
- 1/8 a nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 ounces lemon peel
- 2 ounces orange peel
- 2 ounces citron peel
- 2 cups milk
- eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all the dry ingredients together
2. Beat eggs separately and mix into the dry mixture
3. If not soft enough, use 1 or 2 more eggs
4. Grease mold, put in the mixture into the steamer, cover closely, and steam 6 hours the first day and 2 hours before using
