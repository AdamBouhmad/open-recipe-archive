## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup shortening
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ginger enough to suit taste
- cornmeal enough to make batter

## Directions

1. Mix well and roll like round loaf bread
2. Cut off about 1 inch thick and lay in cookie pan to bake
