## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- bacon
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cream potatoes in the usual way by cutting in cubes and warming in milk sauce
2. When they have simmered about five minutes, add some bacon which has been cut in tiny cubes and fried until nearly brown
3. Bacon should be well drained before it is put with the potatoes
4. Let bake a few minutes and serve
5. Alternatively, place a layer of potatoes, then a layer of grated cheese, and so on until a medium-sized basin is filled
6. Always have a layer of the grated cheese on top
7. Bake in the same way as the former
