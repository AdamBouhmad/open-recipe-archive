## Ingredients

- Light Part: 1 cup white sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- whites of 3 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 2 small spoons baking powder
- 1 cup chopped nuts, or 5 cents' worth of preserved cherries
- Dark Part: 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 2 3/4 cups flour
- 1 cup seeded raisins
- spices and vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Pour the dark part in a pan and the light part over it
