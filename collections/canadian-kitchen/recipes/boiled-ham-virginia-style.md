## Ingredients

- A twelve-pound ham
- 1 cup of molasses
- 1 cup of vinegar
- A few pieces of stick cinnamon
- Water
- Breadcrumbs
- 1 cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a cup of molasses, one cup of vinegar and a few pieces of stick cinnamon, and stir these ingredients into the water in which the ham is to be boiled
2. Put in the ham and boil slowly three hours
3. Leave the ham in the water until it is lukewarm
4. Take it out and skin
5. Cover with breadcrumbs and put in a pan in the oven, with one cup of vinegar
6. Bake one hour
