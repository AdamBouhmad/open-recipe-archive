## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 1 cup currants
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Roll dough in a roll about as large as a fifty-cent piece
2. Cut into pieces about ½ inch thick and bake
