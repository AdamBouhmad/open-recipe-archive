## Ingredients

- stale pieces of crust of bread
- the end pieces of the loaf
- boiling water
- ice
- cream and sugar

## Directions

1. Take stale pieces of crust of bread, the end pieces of the loaf
2. Toast them a nice, dark brown, care to be taken that they do not burn in the least, as that affects the flavor
3. Put the browned crusts into a large milk pitcher, and pour enough boiling water over to cover them
4. Cover the pitcher closely, and let steep until cold
5. Strain, and sweeten to taste
6. Put a piece of ice in each glass
7. This is also good, drank warm with cream and sugar, similar to coffee
