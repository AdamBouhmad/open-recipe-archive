## Ingredients

- 1 cup mashed potato
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup milk
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter or good dripping

## Directions

1. Add 3/4 cup milk and seasoning to 1 cup mashed potato
2. Beat in the well-beaten eggs
3. Melt a tablespoon of butter or good dripping in a frying pan
4. When quite hot, turn in the mixture and cook until nicely browned on the bottom
5. Set the pan in a moderately hot oven and cook until puffed and set on top
6. Fold and serve on a hot platter
