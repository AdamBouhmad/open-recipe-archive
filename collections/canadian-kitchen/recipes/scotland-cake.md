## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- a scant cup butter
- 3 well-beaten eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- lemon or vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat butter to a froth
2. Dissolve 1 teaspoon soda in the milk
3. Sift 2 teaspoons cream tartar into the flour
4. Mix ingredients together
5. Flavor with lemon or vanilla and bake
