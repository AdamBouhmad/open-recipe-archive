## Ingredients

- 1 cup lard and butter mixed
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 7 teaspoons sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 cups flour
- a little salt
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Do not bake too quickly.
