## Ingredients

- 2 cups rich sweet milk
- 1 egg
- 2/4 cup sugar
- 1 large tablespoon flour
- cocoanut
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Heat milk in double boiler
2. Beat egg, sugar, and flour together
3. Cook mixture in milk
4. Have a rich pie crust previously baked
5. Fill with layer of filling, then cocoanut with whipped cream on top
