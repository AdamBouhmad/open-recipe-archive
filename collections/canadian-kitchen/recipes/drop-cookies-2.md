## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- whites 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 a nutmeg
- flour enough to stir very stiff
- currants

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop in spoonfuls on a buttered tin
3. Sprinkle the top with currants
4. Bake quickly
