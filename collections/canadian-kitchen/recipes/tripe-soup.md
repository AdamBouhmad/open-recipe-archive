## Ingredients

- Tripe
- Water
- A little vinegar
- 10 eggs
- Salt
- Lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Cleanse the tripe thoroughly, slice in small pieces and plunge in boiling water
2. Remove carefully, wash again in hot water, and if there are any spots black or red left, scrape with a knife
3. Put in a sauce-pan with plenty of water and a little vinegar and boil until tender
4. Break ten eggs in another dish, add salt and lemon-juice, beat thoroughly and stir into it a little of the boiling broth until danger of curdling is past
5. Then add to the tripe and water, cook a moment longer and serve
