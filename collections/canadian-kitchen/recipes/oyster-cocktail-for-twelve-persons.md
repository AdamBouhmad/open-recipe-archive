## Ingredients

- 60 small oysters
- 3 tablespoonfuls of fine grated horseradish
- 1 teaspoonful of tobasco sauce
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 5 tablespoonfuls of Worcestershire sauce
- 3 tablespoonfuls of tomato catsup
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt

## Directions

1. Clean and chill oysters
2. Mix with sauce
3. Serve in sherry glasses, in grape-fruit or lemon shells, or in tomato cups
4. If fresh tomatoes are not at hand cups may be shaped from tomato jelly
