## Ingredients

- 13/4 dozen corn cut off cob
- 1 head cabbage
- 3 heads celery
- 2 red peppers (take out seeds)
- 5 large onions
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 4 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 5 cents' worth of turmeric
- 1/2 cup salt

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage, celery, and peppers
2. Put vinegar on to boil, add sugar and salt
3. Mix flour and mustard together, then add turmeric
4. Add corn, onions, peppers, cabbage and celery
5. Boil slowly until tender
