## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons dripping
- little nutmeg
- 3 tablespoons currants
- 1 egg beaten in 1/2 cup cold water
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour, baking powder, brown sugar, dripping, nutmeg and salt very lightly
2. Add currants
3. Add egg beaten in cold water
