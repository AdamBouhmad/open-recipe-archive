## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Heat butter, stir in the flour, add the hot milk.
2. Cook two minutes.
3. Set away to cool; when cold stir in the cheese and yolks of eggs.
4. Last the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth.
5. Turn into a buttered dish and bake from twenty to twenty-five minutes.
6. Serve in the same dish.
