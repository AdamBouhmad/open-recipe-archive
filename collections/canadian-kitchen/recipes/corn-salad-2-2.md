## Ingredients

- 12 ears of corn
- 1 head cabbage
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup dry mustard
- sweet pepper to suit taste
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut tops of the corn off, then scrape all off the cob
2. Put in enough vinegar to cover it and cook for 20 minutes
