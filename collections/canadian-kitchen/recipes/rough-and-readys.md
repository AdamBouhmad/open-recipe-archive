## Ingredients

- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup shortening
- 2 teaspoons soda dissolved in 1 cup boiling water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- flour

## Directions

1. Add enough flour to roll
2. Brush tops in milk and dip in white sugar
