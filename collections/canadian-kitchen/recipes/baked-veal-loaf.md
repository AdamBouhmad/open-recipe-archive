## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds cold roast veal
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 pepper
- nutmeg
- 6 rolled crackers
- 3 eggs
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Chop the veal fine, add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and rolled crackers.
2. If veal is thin, add a piece of butter half as large as an egg, and a tablespoon of cream.
3. Form all into a large roll and spot the roll over with bits of butter, then strew over it the pounded crackers remaining after mixing meat to proper stiffness.
4. Put in the oven and from time to time add a little water.
5. Cook slowly 2 hours and slice when cold.
