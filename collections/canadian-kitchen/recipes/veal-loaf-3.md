## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds raw veal
- 0.25 pound raw or cooked bacon or ham
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- savory or other herbs
- 0.5 cup of breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons good stock
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Mince veal and bacon or ham thoroughly
2. Season with pepper and very little salt, as the bacon or ham salts it
3. Add a little nutmeg, savory or other herbs
4. Add 0.5 cup of breadcrumbs and two eggs well beaten, keeping out enough to brush over the top
5. Add 2 tablespoons good stock
6. Mix thoroughly and press into a square pan to shape it
7. Turn out and brush over with the beaten egg
8. Bake 1.5 hours in a slow oven, basting occasionally with a teaspoonful of butter melted in 0.5 cup of water
9. Serve with brown gravy thickened slightly
