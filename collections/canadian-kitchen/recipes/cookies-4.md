## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour enough to roll
- nutmeg or lemon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake in a hot oven
