## Ingredients

- ¾ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 1¾ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- whites of 4 eggs
- 5 tablespoons of grated Cowan's Chocolate
- milk
- vanilla
- pulverized sugar
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add milk and flour
3. Add whites of 4 eggs last
4. Take ½ of mixture and add 5 tablespoons of grated Cowan's Chocolate, wet with milk and flavored with vanilla
5. Drop in a pan in alternate spoonfuls
6. Bake and ice with chocolate icing made with pulverized sugar and grated chocolate
