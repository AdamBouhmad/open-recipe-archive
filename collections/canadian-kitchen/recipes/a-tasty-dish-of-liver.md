## Ingredients

- liver of any kind
- onions
- sage
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- water
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut liver in thin slices, dip in flour, place in shallow baking dish.
2. Slice onions on top, add sage, pepper and salt, and 3/4 pint water.
3. Bake in moderate oven 1 hour.
4. Tomatoes sliced improves this dish.
