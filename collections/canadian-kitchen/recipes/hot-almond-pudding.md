## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound ground almonds
- one pound sugar
- one-quarter pound suet
- two eggs
- one-quarter pound flour
- one teacupful water
- two ounces currants
- two ounces raisins
- one ounce preserved cherries
- a little angelica
- a little ground mace
- one-quarter nutmeg, grated
- one-quarter teaspoonful ground ginger
- a few drops almond extract

## Directions

1. Chop the suet fine
2. Add all the other ingredients and work them together for ten minutes, so that all is well mixed
3. Boil for three hours or more
