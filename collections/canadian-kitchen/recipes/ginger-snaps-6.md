## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup golden molasses
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon soda
- 2 tablespoons ginger
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 nutmeg
- flour to roll

## Directions

1. Take a portion the size of a walnut, roll in a ball instead of cutting with cutter, and place on buttered pan and bake.
