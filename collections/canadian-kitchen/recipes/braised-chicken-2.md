## Ingredients

- fowl
- carrot
- turnip
- onion
- celery
- salt pork
- parsley
- bay-leaf
- cloves
- peppercorns
- salt
- hot water
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the fowl as for roasting, trussing it into good shape.
2. Cut into dice a carrot, turnip, onion, and stalk of celery.
3. Put them in a pot with a few slices of salt pork, and on them place the fowl, with a few pieces of salt pork laid over the breast.
4. Add a bouquet of parsley, one bay-leaf, three cloves, six peppercorns, also a teaspoonful of salt, and a pint of hot water.
5. Cover the pot closely and let simmer for three hours.
6. If any steam escapes, a little more water may have to be added.
7. When done, rub a little butter over the breast, dredge with flour, and place in the oven a few minutes to brown.
8. Strain the liquor from the braising pot, season to taste, and if necessary thicken with a little butter and flour browned.
9. Serve it with the chicken as sauce.
