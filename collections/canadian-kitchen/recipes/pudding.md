## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 4 beaten eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. make a batter of milk, beaten eggs, flour, and salt
2. Be careful not to get batter too stiff
3. Pour batter into pan under the meat
4. let the juice from the meat drip on it while baking
5. It should be yellow-brown when done
