## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped cooked raisins
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 cup light brown sugar
- juice of 1 small lemon
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1 crust
- egg white

## Directions

1. mix raisins, milk, egg yolk, brown sugar, lemon juice, and cornstarch smoothly
2. add raisins, and bake in 1 crust
3. Make frosting of egg white
