## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 teaspoons butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- apples, peeled and cut in sixths
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sweet milk, 1 cup sugar, 3 teaspoons butter, 2 teaspoons baking powder, and 2 cups flour together
2. Pour dough into jelly tins
3. Press apples peeled and cut in sixths into the dough as close together as possible, covering the top of the cake with them
4. Cover with sugar and a little cinnamon and bake
