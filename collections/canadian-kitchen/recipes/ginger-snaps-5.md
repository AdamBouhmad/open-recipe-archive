## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup lard
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3 teaspoons ginger
- flour to make a stiff dough

## Directions

1. Boil brown sugar, molasses, and lard for a few minutes
2. Stir in soda and ginger
3. Add flour to make a stiff dough
4. Roll out thin and cut as desired
5. Bake in a moderate oven till a dark brown
