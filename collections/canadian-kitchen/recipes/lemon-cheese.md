## Ingredients

- 1 lb. fresh butter
- 3 lemons, juice
- 3 eggs well beaten
- 1 lb. sugar
- Peel of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Make in double boiler, boiling to the consistency of thick honey — stirring all the while.
