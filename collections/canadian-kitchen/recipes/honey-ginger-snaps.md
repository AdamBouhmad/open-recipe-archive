## Ingredients

- 1 pint honey
- 3/4 pound butter
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil honey, butter and ginger together a few minutes
2. When nearly cold, put in flour until it is stiff
3. Roll out thin and bake quickly
