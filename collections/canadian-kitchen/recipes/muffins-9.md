## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- butter size of an egg
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix well and add flour enough to drop from spoon
