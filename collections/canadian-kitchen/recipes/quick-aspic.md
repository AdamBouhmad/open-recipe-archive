## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups cold water
- 1 tablespoon chopped celery
- 1 tablespoon chopped carrot
- 1 teaspoon beef extract
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 slice onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 cloves
- 1 sprig parsley
- 1 ounce gelatine
- 3/4 cup water for soaking
- 1 tablespoon sherry or tarragon vinegar
- kitchen bouquet or caramel

## Directions

1. Put 1 3/4 cups cold water, celery, carrot, beef extract dissolved in 1 cup hot water, onion, bay leaf, cloves, and parsley in a saucepan
2. Cover and let simmer 3/4 hour
3. Soak 1 ounce gelatine in 3/4 cup water for an hour
4. Add gelatine to saucepan and stir until dissolved
5. Season with tablespoon sherry or tarragon vinegar
6. If deeper color is desired, add a few drops kitchen bouquet or caramel
7. Clear if necessary and strain through double cloth
8. If for moulding use at once; if for garnishing, turn into a shallow pan to set
9. Can be stamped or cut into fancy shapes more easily if cooked in layers of right thickness
10. Observe always the proportion of 2 ounces gelatine to 6 cups liquid
