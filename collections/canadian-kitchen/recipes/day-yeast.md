## Ingredients

- 1 dozen large potatoes
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 3 1/2 cup salt
- 1/8 cup sugar
- a couple of quarts of cold water
- 4 yeast cakes

## Directions

1. Take 1 dozen large potatoes and boil till well done
2. Drain water and mash fine
3. Drain the potatoes on the following ingredients: 2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon ginger, 3 1/2 cup salt, 1/8 cup sugar
4. Drain the water on while hot and if too thick put a couple of quarts of cold water on to thin it down to the consistency of cream
5. When lukewarm, add 4 yeast cakes
6. Let rise for 24 hours
7. Set away in a cool cellar. This will last a month
