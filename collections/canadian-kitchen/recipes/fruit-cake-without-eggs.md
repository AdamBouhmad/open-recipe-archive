## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups raisins
- 2 cups currants
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 1/2 teaspoons soda
- 4 cups flour
- spice to suit the taste

## Directions

1. Combine 1 cup butter, 2 cups sugar, 2 cups raisins, 2 cups currants, 2 cups buttermilk, 1 1/2 teaspoons soda, 4 cups flour and spice to suit the taste.
