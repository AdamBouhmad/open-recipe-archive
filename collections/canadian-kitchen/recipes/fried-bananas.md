## Ingredients

- bananas
- flour
- butter
- eggs
- cracker dust
- cottolene

## Directions

1. Strip off the skins; cut each banana into three slices, and flour well.
2. Saute in hot butter in a frying-pan, or fry in deep fat.
3. Drain dry and serve hot.
4. Or, roll in egg, then in cracker dust; set on ice for one hour and fry in hot, deep cottolene.
