## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoonfuls grated chocolate
- 1 pt. boiling water
- 1 pt. milk
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Rub the chocolate to a paste with a little cold water
2. Stir into the hot water
3. Boil twenty minutes
4. Add the milk and boil ten minutes longer, stirring often
5. Sweeten in the cups
6. It is improved by laying upon the surface of each cup a teaspoonful of cream
