## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs, add the other ingredients, and mix thoroughly.
2. Melt the butter in a frying pan.
3. Pour in the mixture, stir constantly till cooked, and serve at once.
