## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 1/4 pound salt
- 1 pound cooking sugar
- 3.5 pound mustard
- 1 ounce whole black pepper
- 1 ounce allspice (whole)
- 1 ounce cloves (whole)
- 1 ounce ginger root pounded
- 1 onion chopped fine
- 1 teaspoon curry
- 1 quart vinegar

## Directions

1. Strain the tomatoes after boiling
2. Tie all the spices and the onion in a muslin cloth
3. Boil these together 1.5 hours
4. Rub mustard perfectly smooth in the vinegar
5. Add the vinegar with the mustard to the boiling mixture
6. Boil 20 minutes longer
