## Ingredients

- 7 cups flour
- 1 pint boiled milk
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake

## Directions

1. When milk is cold, add butter, sugar, egg and White Swan Yeast Cake
2. Set to rise at 8 a.m.
3. At 3 p.m. roll out about 1 inch in thickness
4. Cut with large cutter
5. Dip in melted butter
6. Fold over and rise till time to bake
7. Bake in quick oven
