## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped meat (fresh)
- 2 cups chopped cold potatoes
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1 small onion or little sage
- pepper and salt
- 1 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. season with pepper and salt
2. pour over 1 cup sweet milk
3. cover and bake slowly 1 hour
