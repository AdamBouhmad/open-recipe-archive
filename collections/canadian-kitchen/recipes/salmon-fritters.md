## Ingredients

- 1/2 can salmon
- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 1 slice of bread squeezed out of cold water
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper to taste
- flour for rolling
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Mould into round flat balls
2. Roll in flour
3. Fry until brown
