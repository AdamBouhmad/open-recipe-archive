## Ingredients

- 20 oysters
- 1 gill oysters liquor
- 1/2 cup celery minced
- 1 gill of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 gill sherry or Madeira
- 1 teaspoonful each of salt and paprica

## Directions

1. Put the oyster-liquor, celery, and paprica in the chafing-dish over hot water, and when it comes to a boil simmer three or four minutes
2. Add the butter and the cream, and when these are boiling hot put in the oysters
3. Cook until the edges curl, stir in the wine and salt, and serve at once on toast
