## Ingredients

- large apples
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter and core large apples
2. Pack in an earthen jar with brown sugar
3. Cover closely
4. Bake slowly in a moderate oven until the contents have been shrunken to about half their original bulk and are rich, red and luscious
