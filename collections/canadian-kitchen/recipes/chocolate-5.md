## Ingredients

- chocolate
- cold water
- yolks of eggs
- milk
- water

## Directions

1. Scrape chocolate fine
2. Mix with a little cold water and the yolks of eggs well beaten
3. Add this to equal parts of milk and water
4. Boil well, being careful that it does not burn
5. Sweeten to taste
6. Serve hot
