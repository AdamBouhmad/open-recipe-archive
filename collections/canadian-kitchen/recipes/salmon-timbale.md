## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1 cup crumbs
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1/16 teaspoon pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix salmon, crumbs, parsley, salt, pepper, eggs and lemon juice thoroughly
2. Add milk enough to moisten
3. Put into buttered mould
4. Place in the oven and poach
5. Turn and serve with parsley sauce
