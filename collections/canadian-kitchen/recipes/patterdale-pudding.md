## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one-quarter pound sifted sugar
- one-quarter pound butter
- one-quarter pound flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Beat the eggs in one by one
3. Add the sugar and flour
4. Put into cups
5. Bake in a moderately heated oven twenty minutes
