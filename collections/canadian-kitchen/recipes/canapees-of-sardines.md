## Ingredients

- Sardines
- yolks of three or four hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- mustard
- pepper
- vinegar
- slices of toast

## Directions

1. Take some slices of roll and cut them neatly into oval or octagon shapes
2. Toast them slightly, or fry them in oil or butter till they are of a nice yellow color
3. Take some sardines and strip them from the bones
4. Lay one-half of them aside, and pound the other to a smooth paste with the eggs and butter
5. Add the mustard, pepper and vinegar
6. When these ingredients are well mixed, spread the paste over the prepared slices of toast
7. On the top lay the other half of the sardines, cut into small strips
8. Stand them in a Dutch oven before the fire and serve very hot
