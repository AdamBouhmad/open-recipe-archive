## Ingredients

- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1/2 cup stoned raisins
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda stirred in the cream
- 1 cup or more flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Make as a layer cake
