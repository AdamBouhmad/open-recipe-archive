## Ingredients

- 1 cup currants
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon flour
- yolks of 2 eggs
- beaten whites of 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Bake in 1 crust with currants, brown sugar, water, flour, and egg yolks
2. When baked cover with the beaten whites and 3/4 cup granulated sugar and brown
