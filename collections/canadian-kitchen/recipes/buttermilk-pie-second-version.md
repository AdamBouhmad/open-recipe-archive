## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- a small piece of butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar, 2 cups buttermilk, 2 eggs, 2 tablespoons flour, a small piece of butter
2. flavor with lemon
3. This makes 2 pies
