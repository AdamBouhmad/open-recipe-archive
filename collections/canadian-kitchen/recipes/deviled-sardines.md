## Ingredients

- 1 box boneless sardines
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful lemon juice
- 1 teaspoonful paprica
- 1/2 saltspoonful cayenne
- 1 saltspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in the blazer and when hissing hot lay in the sardines
2. Cook until heated through, turning once, sprinkle with salt and paprica, add the lemon-juice, and serve on toast
