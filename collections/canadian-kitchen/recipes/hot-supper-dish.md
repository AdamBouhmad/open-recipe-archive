## Ingredients

- bits of left-over vegetables
- small onion (if liked)
- salt and pepper to taste
- cream
- loaf bread (optional)
- milk with butter (optional)
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons water (optional)

## Directions

1. Save all the bits left in the vegetable dishes at noon
2. Mince finely and add a small onion if liked
3. Add salt and pepper to taste
4. Moisten with cream
5. Put in a hot oven and serve as soon as browned
6. If there are not enough vegetables, crumb up a piece of loaf and add
7. If cream is not obtainable, use milk with a few bits of butter scattered over the top of the dish
8. Alternatively, take 1 egg, beat until light, add 2 tablespoons water and pour over the mixture, using the bits of butter as with the milk
