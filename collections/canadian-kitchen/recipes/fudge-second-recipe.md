## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup cream
- butter size of an egg
- 1/2 cup grated Cowan's Perfection Chocolate or Cocoa
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and chocolate before adding cream
2. Boil until it balls in water
3. Remove from stove and add flavoring
4. Beat until cool enough to turn in buttered pan
