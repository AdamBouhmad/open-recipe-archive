## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup black molasses
- 1 cup lard (scant)
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls ginger
- 1 heaping teaspoonful soda dissolved in 2 of hot water
- flour to roll

## Directions

1. Bake in hot oven.
