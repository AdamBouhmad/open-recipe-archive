## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 small cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup cream
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- lard or goose grease

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Fry in lard or goose grease
