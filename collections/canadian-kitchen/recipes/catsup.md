## Ingredients

- 1/2 peck ripe tomatoes
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- 1 small cup salt
- 1 cup mustard seed
- 2 tablespoons black pepper
- 2 red peppers
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon mace
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 quart best cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Skin and chop tomatoes fine
2. Cut red peppers fine without seeds
3. Mix all well together without boiling
4. Put away in small jars
5. Can be used at once
