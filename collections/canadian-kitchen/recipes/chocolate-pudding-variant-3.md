## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons Cowan's Perfection Chocolate (grated)
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- pinch of salt
- vanilla to flavor

## Directions

1. Let milk, bread crumbs, chocolate, and sugar come to a boil, stirring the while
2. When cool add eggs, pinch of salt, and vanilla to flavor
3. Pour into buttered dish
4. Bake 1/2 hour
5. Add the whipped whites of eggs
