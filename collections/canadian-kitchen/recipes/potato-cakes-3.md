## Ingredients

- 1 doz potatoes
- a little salt
- 2 cups milk
- 0.25 cup butter (melted)
- 1 cup yeast or 0.5 cake Fleischmann's
- flour to make stiff enough to roll

## Directions

1. Boil and mash potatoes
2. Add milk, salt, melted butter
3. When cool enough add yeast (home made, or one-half cake Fleischmann's)
4. Add flour to make stiff enough to roll out and cut in squares
5. If wanted for tea make up in the morning and leave to rise
6. Bake about like biscuits
