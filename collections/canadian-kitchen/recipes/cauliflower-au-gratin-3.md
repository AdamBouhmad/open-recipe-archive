## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- pepper
- grated cheese
- dried crumbs
- milk

## Directions

1. Cook cauliflower; drain well and remove the flowerettes
2. Tear the rest to pieces with a fork, lay it in a deep dish and sprinkle over it a little salt, pepper and grated cheese and a few dried crumbs moistened with milk
3. Then add the top layer of the flowerettes and sprinkle with the salt, pepper and cheese, and bake until slightly brown
