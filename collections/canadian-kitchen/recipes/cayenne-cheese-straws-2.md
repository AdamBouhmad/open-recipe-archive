## Ingredients

- 2 oz. butter
- 2 oz. Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 lb. flour
- a pinch of salt
- a little cayenne
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Mix into a paste with the yolk of an egg.
2. Roll out to the thickness of a silver quarter, about four or five inches long; cut into strips about a third of an inch wide, twist them as you would a paper spill, and lay them on a baking-sheet slightly floured.
3. Bake in a moderate oven until crisp, but they must not be the least brown.
4. If put away in a tin, these cheese straws will keep a long time.
5. Serve cold, piled tastefully on a glass dish. You can make the straws of remnants of puff-pastry, rolling in the grated cheese.
