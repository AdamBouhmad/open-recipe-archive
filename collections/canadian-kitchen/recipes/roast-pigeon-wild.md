## Ingredients

- pigeon
- salt
- pepper
- cotton string
- fat bacon
- hot water or weak stock
- butter
- lemon-juice
- currant jelly
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Draw and wash them thoroughly
2. Wipe dry, salt and pepper the insides
3. Truss and bind them into shape with cotton string
4. Cover the breasts with thin slices of fat bacon tied in place
5. Lay them, breasts upward, in your roaster, and pour in half a cupful of hot water or weak stock
6. Cook, covered, fifteen minutes
7. Remove the pork, rub all over with butter and lemon-juice, and brown
8. Keep the pigeons hot while you stir into the gravy a tablespoonful of butter cut up in one of browned flour and another of currant jelly
9. Boil up once and pour over the pigeons
