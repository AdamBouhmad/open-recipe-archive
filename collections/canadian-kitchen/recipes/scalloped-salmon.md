## Ingredients

- 1 tin salmon
- 1 pint milk
- butter about size of an egg
- pepper
- salt
- about 6 soda biscuits
- lemon

## Directions

1. Pick all bones out of salmon and flake it up finely with a fork
2. Roll soda biscuits out fine
3. Add all ingredients and heat to boiling point in a double boiler
4. Put it in a baker
5. Sprinkle a few more cracker crumbs over the top
6. Brown in the oven
7. Just before going to the table cut up a lemon in about eight pieces and put over the top, a piece being allowed for each person
8. Each one may then squeeze the juice over their portion as they desire
