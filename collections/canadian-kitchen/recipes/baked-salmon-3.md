## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon vinegar or juice of 1 lemon
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Drain the salmon and pick to pieces
2. Work in the melted butter, egg, seasoning and crumbs
3. Moisten with liquid drained from salmon
4. Turn into a well-buttered pan in boiling water
5. Cover closely
6. Bake 1 hour
