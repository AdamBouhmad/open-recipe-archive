## Ingredients

- currants (5 pounds)
- granulated sugar (5 pounds)
- raisins (2 pounds)
- oranges (4)

## Directions

1. Stem currants and seed and chop raisins
2. Cook currants for 15 minutes
3. Add sugar warmed as for jelly, just set it in the oven while you cook currants
4. After you add sugar cook 5 minutes more
5. Then add raisins and cook 20 minutes
6. Then add the orange pulp and grated rind and cook 5 minutes
7. Put in pint cans
