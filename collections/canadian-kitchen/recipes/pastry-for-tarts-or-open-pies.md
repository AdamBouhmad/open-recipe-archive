## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1/4 cupful butter
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- Water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, salt, and sugar together
2. Cut in the butter as directed above
3. Mix in the beaten yolks, then enough water to make a paste which is not very stiff
4. Roll it two or three times, then wrap it in a cloth, or cover it closely, and put it in the ice-box for an hour
5. This gives enough paste for four small tarts
