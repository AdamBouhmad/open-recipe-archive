## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 2 level teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat first 3 ingredients together
2. mix cornmeal and flour
3. add a little at a time, beating well for at least five minutes
4. add the stiffly beaten white of egg
5. bake in gem pans in moderate oven
