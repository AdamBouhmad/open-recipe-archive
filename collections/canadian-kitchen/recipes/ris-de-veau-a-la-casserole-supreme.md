## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Onion
- Celery
- Stock or white sauce
- Fried bread
- Truffles and mushrooms or peas

## Directions

1. Steep the sweetbreads in water for an hour
2. Blanch them and press between two dishes
3. When cold, cut away any sinews or fat
4. Place them in a stew-pan with a little onion, celery, and stock of white sauce
5. Braise for twenty or thirty minutes
6. Take out of saucepan and put into the oven to brown, basting with its own liquor
7. Serve on fried bread with sauce around with truffles and mushrooms or peas
