## Ingredients

- 5 fresh eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil 5 fresh eggs 15 or 20 minutes
2. Dip in cold water just an instant and shell
3. Cut lengthwise in 4 slices on a hot, shallow vegetable dish set in range closet to keep warm
4. Make a sauce: cook 1 tablespoon butter with 1 onion chopped fine
5. Add 1 tablespoon flour and rub smooth, may be browned if desired
6. Add 1 pint of sweet milk and 1/2 teaspoon salt, little pepper
7. Cook till like gravy and pour over the eggs and serve hot
