## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 pounds flour
- 4 ounces sugar
- 5 ounces butter
- 1 pound syrup
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 egg
- milk

## Directions

1. Warm butter and syrup together
2. Stir in flour, sugar, baking powder, ginger, and egg
3. Add little milk if required
4. Bake in a buttered tin
5. Cut into squares when done
