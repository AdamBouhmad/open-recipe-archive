## Ingredients

- 4 pounds rhubarb
- 4 lemons
- 4 pounds sugar
- 3/4 pound broken walnuts

## Directions

1. Cut rhubarb and lemons small
2. Cover with water
3. Boil 1 1/2 hours
4. Add 4 pounds sugar and boil 15 minutes
5. Just before removing from the fire, add 3/4 pound broken walnuts
