## Ingredients

- clams
- hot water
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour
- toast

## Directions

1. Lay clams on a gridiron over hot coals
2. Take out of shells as soon as open
3. Save the juice
4. Add a little hot water, pepper, and a very little salt
5. Add butter rolled in flour sufficient for seasoning
6. Cook for 5 minutes
7. Pour over toast
