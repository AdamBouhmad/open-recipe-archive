## Ingredients

- salt pork
- 1 large or 2 small onions
- 2 quarts water
- 1 quart clams
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 pint milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- Worcester sauce

## Directions

1. Cut salt pork into very small squares and fry a light brown
2. Add 1 large or 2 small onions cut very fine, and cook about ten minutes
3. Add 2 quarts water and 1 quart clams
4. Mix 1 tablespoon flour with the water, put it with 1 pint milk and pour into the soup
5. Let it boil about five minutes
6. Add butter, pepper and salt, Worcester sauce to taste
