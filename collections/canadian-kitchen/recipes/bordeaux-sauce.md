## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 2 small heads cabbage
- 10 large onions
- 5 hot peppers
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1/2 ounce mustard seed
- 1 ounce tumeric powder
- 3 pounds granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup salt
- 3 quarts vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop tomatoes and cabbage fine
2. Let stand in salt over night
3. Add onions, hot peppers, celery seed, mustard seed, tumeric powder, sugar, and vinegar
4. Boil slowly 2 or 3 hours
