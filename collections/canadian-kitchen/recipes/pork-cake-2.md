## Ingredients

- 1 pound fat salt pork, free from lean or rind, chopped fine
- 1/2 pint boiling water
- 1 pound seeded and chopped raisins
- 1/2 pound citron shaved into shreds
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon saleratus
- salt
- sifted flour
- 1 ounce nutmeg finely ground
- 1 ounce cloves finely ground
- 2 ounces cinnamon finely ground

## Directions

1. Chop fat salt pork fine as almost like lard
2. Pour 1/2 pint of boiling water on it
3. Add seeded and chopped raisins and citron shaved into shreds
4. Add sugar, molasses, saleratus rubbed fine and put into the molasses, and a little salt
5. Mix all together
6. Stir in sifted flour to make the consistency of common cake mixture
7. Add nutmeg, cloves, and cinnamon all finely ground
8. Bake slowly, testing with a sliver; when nothing adheres, it is done
