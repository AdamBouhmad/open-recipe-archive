## Ingredients

- 9 bitter oranges
- 3 sweet oranges
- 2 lemons
- 8 lbs. sugar
- 1 glass whisky

## Directions

1. Slice the fruit across the grain, as thin as possible, being careful to remove all seeds
2. Place in a deep dish with four quarts of water
3. Allow it to stand for twenty-four hours
4. Add sugar and boil for one hour, or until it will jelly
5. Before taking off the fire add one glass of whisky to clarify
