## Ingredients

- 1 pound raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound suet
- 1 pound bread crumbs
- 7 eggs
- lemon peel to suit taste
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- sour milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 teaspoon soda in just enough sour milk to moisten it
2. Add flour to make stiff enough
3. Steam 3 hours
