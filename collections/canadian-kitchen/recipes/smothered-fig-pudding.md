## Ingredients

- 3 cups rich milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- Butter size of egg
- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- Figs
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Make custard in double boiler.
2. Place a layer of finely cut up figs, then a layer of custard, and so on till dish is full.
3. Set away till cool.
4. Put whipped cream on top and serve. Preserved ginger used instead of the figs is also good.
