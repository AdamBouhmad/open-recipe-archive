## Ingredients

- One quart of water
- Pared rind of two lemons
- Two and one-half cups sugar or more
- One tablespoon cornstarch
- Juice of five lemons

## Directions

1. Boil water with pared rind of two lemons (avoiding white part)
2. Add two and one-half cups sugar or more (very sweet)
3. Add one tablespoon (not heaping) of cornstarch
4. Add juice of five lemons
5. Strain and freeze
6. Should stand one hour before using
