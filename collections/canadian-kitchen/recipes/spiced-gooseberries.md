## Ingredients

- 6 pounds ripe gooseberries
- 5 pounds sugar
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 1 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook 3 hours
2. When half done add 1 cup vinegar
