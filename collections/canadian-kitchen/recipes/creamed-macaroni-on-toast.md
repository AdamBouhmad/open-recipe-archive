## Ingredients

- 1 cup thin white sauce
- 1 cup boiled macaroni
- 1/2 cup grated cheese
- 6 slices toast (buttered)

## Directions

1. Add macaroni to sauce
2. Cook for some minutes
3. Pour over toast and place on baking pan
4. Dust with cheese
5. Set on top shelf of oven for some time
