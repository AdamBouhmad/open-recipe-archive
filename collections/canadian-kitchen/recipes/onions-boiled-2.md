## Ingredients

- onions
- milk
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Select onions of uniform size
2. Remove the outer skin
3. Boil until tender in a large quantity of milk and water
4. Drain them when tender
5. Season with butter, salt and pepper
