## Ingredients

- one large whitefish
- salt
- pepper
- one pint milk
- two tablespoonfuls flour
- two eggs
- one-fourth pound butter
- onion-juice
- bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Steam till tender one large whitefish
2. remove bones and sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. heat one pint of milk thickened with two tablespoonfuls of flour
4. when cold add two eggs and one-fourth of a pound of butter
5. put into a baking-dish a layer of the fish and a layer of dressing
6. season with one-half teaspoonful of onion-juice
7. cover top with bread-crumbs, and bake one-half hour
