## Ingredients

- 1 pt. cream
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- 1/4 teaspoonful pepper
- 3 1/2 tablespoonfuls white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix beaten yolks with other ingredients
2. Pour the cream in last
3. Put on the stove, stir all the time until it is well scalded
4. Pour into a jug
5. Nice to eat with sliced tomatoes, salmon or lettuce
