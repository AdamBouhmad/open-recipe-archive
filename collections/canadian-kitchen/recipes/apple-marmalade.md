## Ingredients

- 1 pound apples
- 1 pound sugar
- 3/4 a lemon
- 1/8 ounce whole ginger

## Directions

1. Peel and core apples
2. Slice into eighths
3. Put in a basin of water
4. Lift apples dripping into the preserving pan
5. Boil with sugar, ginger and rind of lemon until quite clear
6. Add lemon juice about 20 minutes before the preserve is done
