## Ingredients

- Large 3/4 cup corn meal
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- A little salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoonful cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoonful soda

## Directions

1. Sift soda and cream tartar into flour
2. Bake twenty minutes
