## Ingredients

- One coffee-cup butter melted
- one cup sweet milk
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- three and one-half cups flour
- three eggs
- three teaspoons baking powder
- one small cup sugar
- one tablespoon cornstarch
- two cups cocoanut
- one teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Bake in a shallow meat tin
2. Beat whites of three eggs to a stiff froth
3. add slowly one small cup sugar and one tablespoon cornstarch
4. Place mixture in double boiler and cook fifteen minutes, stirring constantly
5. Then add two cups cocoanut and one teaspoon vanilla and drop on buttered tins in teaspoonfuls and bake a delicate brown
