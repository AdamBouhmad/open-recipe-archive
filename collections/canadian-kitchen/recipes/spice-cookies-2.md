## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 1 cup lard or dripping
- 3½ cups brown sugar
- 3½ cups molasses
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- buttermilk or sour milk

## Directions

1. Rub flour and lard well together
2. Add brown sugar and molasses
3. Add currants and soda
4. Mix with buttermilk or sour milk till stiff enough to roll ¼ inch
5. Cut as desired
6. Bake in a moderate oven till a dark brown
