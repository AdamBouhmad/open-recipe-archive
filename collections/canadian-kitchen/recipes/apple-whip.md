## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 pint stewed and sweetened apple
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 pint candied cherries

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eggs until stiff and perfectly dry
2. Fold lightly into the stewed apple
3. Add chopped nuts and candied cherries
4. Beat until firm
5. Chill through
