## Ingredients

- 1 head red cabbage
- 1 onion
- 2 apples
- salt
- pepper
- allspice
- 1 wine glass sherry
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- butter size of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Shave cabbage very fine, put slowly into quart of boiling water.
2. Add onion cut fine, apples, salt, pepper and allspice to taste, sherry, vinegar, sugar, butter, let simmer one and one-half or two hours and serve hot.
