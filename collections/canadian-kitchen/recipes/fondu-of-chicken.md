## Ingredients

- 1 cupful minced chicken (turkey or veal)
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 cup of boiling milk
- 1/2 onion boiled in the milk and strained out
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 slice cold boiled ham, minced
- 2 eggs beaten
- Pinch of soda
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Soak the crumbs in the milk
2. Stir in the butter and beat very light
3. Let the mixture cool while you mince the meat and beat the eggs
4. Stir in the meat when the bread and milk are nearly cold
5. Season, lastly adding the eggs
6. Beat well up
7. Put it into a well-greased baking dish
8. Set in a brisk oven
9. When the fondu is a light delicate brown puff send at once to the table in the same dish in which it has been baked
