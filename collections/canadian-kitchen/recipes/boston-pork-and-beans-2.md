## Ingredients

- beans
- cold water
- warm water
- hot water
- boiling salted water
- pork
- boiling water
- dry mustard
- extract of celery or celery salt
- molasses

## Directions

1. Soak the beans over night in cold water, changing this in the morning for warm, an hour later for hot.
2. Put over the fire half an hour afterwards, in boiling salted water, and cook until tender, but not broken.
3. Drain them then, and put into a deep dish or bean-pot, bury a piece of pork (parboiled) in the centre.
4. Stir into a large cupful of boiling water half a teaspoonful of dry mustard, half as much extract of celery or celery salt, and a tablespoonful of molasses.
5. Pour this over the pork and beans.
6. Cover closely, set in the oven and bake slowly from four to six hours according to size of the pot.
7. Serve Boston brown bread with it always.
