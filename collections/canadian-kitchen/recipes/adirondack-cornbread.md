## Ingredients

- 5 eggs beaten separately
- 1 pt. milk
- ½ pt. cornmeal
- 2 oz. butter, melted
- ½ pt. flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful sugar

## Directions

1. Eggs beaten separately.
2. Add to milk, flour and cornmeal well mixed, the beaten yolks and melted butter; let it stand ten minutes.
3. Then add whites, salt and sugar, baking powder.
4. Mix thoroughly and bake in long biscuit tins.
