## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup seeded raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Combine 1 1/2 cups brown sugar, 3 eggs, 1/2 cup molasses, 1 cup butter, 1 cup seeded raisins, 1 cup currants, 1 teaspoon allspice, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 teaspoon ginger, 2 teaspoons baking powder and 1 teaspoon soda.
2. Add flour to mix soft dough.
