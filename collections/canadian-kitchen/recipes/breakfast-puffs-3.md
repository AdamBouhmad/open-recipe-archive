## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup milk

## Directions

1. mix the liquid and add the flour gradually
2. beat until light
3. pour into hot buttered gems
4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in hot oven
