## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound chocolate
- one pound pounded almonds
- one-quarter pound sugar
- yolks of seven eggs
- bread or cracker crumbs
- unsalted butter

## Directions

1. Beat yolks very light with sugar, add chocolate, melted, almonds pounded, and last the whites of eggs beaten very stiff.
2. Butter a flat shallow pan with unsalted butter, and sift in finely rolled cracker or bread crumbs.
3. Pour in the mixture and cook half an hour in a moderate oven.
4. Must be cut while hot into cubes.
