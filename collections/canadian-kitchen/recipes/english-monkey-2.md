## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of stale breadcrumbs
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 heaping teaspoonful of butter
- 3/4 of a cup of cheese cut fine
- 1 beaten egg

## Directions

1. Soak breadcrumbs in milk fifteen minutes
2. Put butter into chafing-dish
3. Add cheese cut fine; stir until melted
4. Add crumbs with beaten egg
