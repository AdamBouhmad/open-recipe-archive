## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons soda
- 3 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 egg
- a few chopped raisins
- 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Knead with spoon
2. Let rise 1/2 hour
3. Bake in moderate oven 3/4 hour
