## Ingredients

- 2 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 3/4 cups yellow sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Mix molasses, sugar and butter
2. Dissolve soda in 1 tablespoon water
3. Add all together
4. Roll thin and bake slowly
