## Ingredients

- 10 onions
- 2 pints cold cooked beans
- 1 teaspoonful pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls salad oil
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 1 scant cup vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Olives
- Two hard-boiled eggs
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Peel and slice onions very fine. Pour cold water over them and press hard to remove the strong taste. Drain well.
2. Have ready in the salad bowl cold boiled or baked beans, preferably the latter.
3. Mix with them the minced onion, salt, pepper, salad oil, sugar and vinegar.
4. Toss and mix with a fork lightly, but thoroughly, and garnish with olives, two hard-boiled eggs sliced and lettuce leaves.
