## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 large green cucumbers
- 1 onion minced fine
- 2 tablespoons lard
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Pare and slice cucumbers
2. put 2 tablespoons lard in a frying pan over the fire
3. when hot put cucumbers and onion in and simmer till tender
4. add salt and pepper to taste, a tablespoon flour, 1 cup sour cream, and cup vinegar
5. serve hot
