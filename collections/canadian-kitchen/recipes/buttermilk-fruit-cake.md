## Ingredients

- 2 cups buttermilk
- 2 cups raisins
- 2 cups currants
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 4 cups flour
- spice to suit taste

## Directions

1. Beat all together, put it in your pan ready for the oven, then let it rise over night
