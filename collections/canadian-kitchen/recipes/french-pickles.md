## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 6 onions
- 4 green peppers
- 1 teacup salt
- 1 cup vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop green tomatoes, onions, and green peppers fine together
2. Add 1 teacup of salt
3. Let stand over night
4. Drain off all water
5. Add 1 cup vinegar, 2 pounds sugar, 1 teaspoon each of mustard, ginger, cloves, and caraway if desired, and 2 tablespoons cinnamon
6. Boil 1 hour
