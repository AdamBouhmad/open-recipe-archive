## Ingredients

- 1 cup suet
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt
- 3 large tablespoonfuls brown sugar
- 3 large tablespoonfuls molasses
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 large teaspoonful ground ginger
- A small grating of nutmeg
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1/4 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Line a melon mould with raisins, pour in the batter, and steam for two and one-half hours.
