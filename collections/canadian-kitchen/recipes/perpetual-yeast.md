## Ingredients

- 3 cups potato water
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- 3 cups warm water
- 3 cups of flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Add 3 cups potato water, 1 cup granulated sugar and 1 White Swan Yeast Cake together
2. Let stand 48 hours where temperature is moderate, then seal in 2 quart can and set in cool place
3. At noon, save 3 cups potato water and add 3/4 cup granulated sugar
4. Pour in the yeast
5. At night take 3 cups of this liquid and to it add 3 cups warm water (always cup for cup)
6. Allow 3 cups of flour to 1 of liquid
7. Add 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon salt
8. Knead well and put in pan to rise
9. In morning make into loaves
10. If yeast is put with potato water at night you may knead stiff in morning
11. Grease loaves with melted butter just before placing in oven
