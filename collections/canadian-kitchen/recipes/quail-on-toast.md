## Ingredients

- salt
- pepper
- flour
- water
- butter
- baker's bread
- browned flour

## Directions

1. After the birds are nicely cleaned cut them open down the back
2. Salt and pepper them, and dredge with flour
3. Break down the breast and back bones so they will lie flat
4. Place them in a pan with very little water and butter in a hot oven, covering them up tightly until nearly done
5. Place in a spider in hot butter, and fry a moment till a nice brown
6. Have ready slices of baker's bread toasted, and slightly buttered upon a platter
7. The toast should be broken down with a carving knife so that it will be tender
8. On this place the quail
9. Make a sauce of the gravy in the pan, thicken lightly with browned flour and pour over each quail and the toast
