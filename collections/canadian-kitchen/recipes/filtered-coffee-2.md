## Ingredients

- 2 ounces coffee
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Place coffee in strainer, place strainer in pot
2. Add boiling water to coffee, 0.5 cup at a time, at intervals of 3 to 5 minutes
3. If desired stronger, re-filter
4. Serve at once
5. Coffee pot may be placed in a kettle of boiling water
