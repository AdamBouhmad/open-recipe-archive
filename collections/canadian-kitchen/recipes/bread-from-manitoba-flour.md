## Ingredients

- flour
- 3 quarts warm water
- 1 quart yeast
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons salt

## Directions

1. In the morning sift flour into your bread pan
2. Take 3 quarts of warm water to 1 quart of yeast, 2 tablespoons sugar, 3 tablespoons salt
3. Mix to consistency of a nice soft dough
4. Punch for half an hour, gradually sifting flour till you have a nice, smooth, spongy dough
5. Let rise and mix down twice
6. Put in the pans and let rise for an hour
7. Bake in oven
