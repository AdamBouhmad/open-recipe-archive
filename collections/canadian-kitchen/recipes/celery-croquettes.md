## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup chopped celery
- 1 large potato
- 2 tablespoons chopped nuts
- butter
- salt and pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pare and boil potato and celery together until tender
2. Remove potato, mash, drain the celery
3. Mix together chopped nuts, butter, salt and pepper
4. Cool, form into croquette shapes
5. Dip in egg and bread crumbs and fry
