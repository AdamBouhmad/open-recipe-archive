## Ingredients

- 12 large cucumbers
- 6 large onions
- salt
- vinegar
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon tumeric
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Peel and slice cucumbers and onions
2. Sprinkle with salt
3. Let stand over night
4. Drain
5. Put in kettle and cover with vinegar
6. Let boil until tender
7. Add sugar, mustard, and tumeric
8. Mix flour with cold vinegar
9. Stir into pickle
