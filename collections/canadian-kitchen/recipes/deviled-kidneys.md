## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- One quart of water or stock
- One clove
- Four teaspoonfuls of onion-juice
- Salt and pepper
- One tablespoonful of butter
- One tablespoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys in thin round slices
2. Cover them with cold water and let stand half an hour
3. Wash them clean and put in stew-pan with one quart of water or stock, a clove, four teaspoonfuls of onion-juice, salt and pepper
4. Simmer two hours or longer if not tender
5. Set away, and for breakfast put one tablespoonful of butter in frying-pan
6. When hot add one tablespoonful of flour
7. Stir till brown and smooth
