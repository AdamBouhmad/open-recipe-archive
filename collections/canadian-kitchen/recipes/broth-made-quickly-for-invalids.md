## Ingredients

- One pound of lean meat, chopped to a fine mince
- One pint of cold water
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop lean meat to a fine mince
2. To a pound of meat add one pint of cold water
3. Let soak for fifteen minutes
4. Let slowly boil for half an hour
5. Season and strain
