## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons mashed potato
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- cold water
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- warm water
- flour

## Directions

1. Take 5 tablespoons mashed potato, 2 tablespoons salt, 1 tablespoon sugar and cold water enough to make mixture lukewarm; beat thoroughly together and add 1 White Swan Yeast Cake.
2. Let stand until night, then measure the yeast into a quart measure and add enough warm water to make four quarts of liquid.
3. Into this stir the flour gradually until too stiff for the spoon, then mix with hands to a medium stiff dough.
4. Put in warm place till morning, then work down in the pan, let stand until light again.
5. Mould into loaves.
6. Let rise one hour, put into medium hot oven, and bake from 1 to 1 3/4 hours.
