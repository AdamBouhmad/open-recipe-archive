## Ingredients

- butter
- fresh or canned raspberries
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1.5 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg if desired
- pinch of salt
- sweet milk
- 1 cup yellow sugar
- 1/2 cup butter

## Directions

1. Butter cups and fill about full of fresh or canned raspberries
2. Make a batter by sifting 2 teaspoons baking powder in 1.5 cups flour
3. Add 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 egg if desired, and pinch of salt
4. Add enough sweet milk to make rather stiff
5. Fill cups 3/4 full of batter
6. Place in steamer over boiling water and steam from 20 to 30 minutes
7. Turn out on plates and serve hot with 1 cup yellow sugar creamed with 1/2 cup butter
