## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 0.75 cup water
- 0.75 cup vinegar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- previously popped corn

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, water, vinegar, and butter together without stirring until it stands in cold water, as for white sugar syrup
2. Stir this over the previously popped corn
3. This colors the corn a golden brown
