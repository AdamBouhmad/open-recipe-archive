## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1/4 lb. butter
- 1/4 lb. sugar
- Yolks 5 eggs
- Almonds crushed

## Directions

1. Mix well together and put on ice to cool; then form into S's; put on ice again till quite hard, then dip into egg and sugar, or almonds.
2. Bake till brown.
