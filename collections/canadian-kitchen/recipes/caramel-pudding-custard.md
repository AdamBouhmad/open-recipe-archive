## Ingredients

- 6 eggs (yolks)
- 2 oz. sugar
- 2 oz. castor sugar
- 1/2 pt. cream
- Enough water to moisten sugar

## Directions

1. Put sugar and water into a mould and cook until sugar is of a dark color.
2. Allow it to run all over the mould.
3. Dip in cold water to set it.
4. Pour in your custard.
5. Put into a saucepan with boiling water half way up the mould.
6. Cook about twenty minutes, cover mould with a buttered paper, and put cover on saucepan while the custard is steaming.
