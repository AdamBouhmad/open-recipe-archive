## Ingredients

- One-half pound flour
- One-half pound butter
- One-half pound sugar
- Two eggs
- A teaspoon mace

## Directions

1. Well mix flour, butter and sugar together, add two eggs and a teaspoon mace, roll out thin and cut any size you like; bake in a slow oven.
