## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 large nutmeg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 2 tablespoons hot water

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add well-beaten eggs
3. Add sour milk
4. Add molasses and spices
5. Add flour
6. Stir all together
7. Just before putting into pan, stir in soda dissolved in hot water
