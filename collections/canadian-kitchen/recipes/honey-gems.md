## Ingredients

- 2 quarts flour
- 3 tablespoons melted lard
- 3/4 pint honey
- 1/4 pint molasses
- four heaping teaspoons brown sugar
- 13/4 level tablespoons soda
- 1 level teaspoon salt
- 1 pint water
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Mix flour, melted lard, honey, molasses, brown sugar, soda, salt, water and vanilla extract together
