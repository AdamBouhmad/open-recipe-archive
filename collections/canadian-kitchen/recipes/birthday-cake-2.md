## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 2 cups flour
- whites of 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- small teaspoon salt
- nutmeg
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup cocoanut
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch

## Directions

1. Beat 1 cup sugar and 1 cup sweet cream together first
2. Add 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder next
3. Add small teaspoon salt and a little nutmeg
4. Add whites of 3 eggs well beaten and mix quickly
5. Bake in 3 layers
6. For the filling: boil 1 cup sweet milk, 1/4 cup sugar, and 3/4 cup cocoanut together
7. When boiling, stir in 2 teaspoons cornstarch wet with cold milk
