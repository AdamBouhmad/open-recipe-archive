## Ingredients

- 1 cup blackstrap
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sour milk (sweet will do)
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- a little over 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Bake in layers
