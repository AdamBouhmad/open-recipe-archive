## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 3 eggs
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. boil sugar and water 5 minutes
2. beat whites until stiff
3. add yolks to whites, beat until mixed
4. add syrup gradually
5. add lemon juice
6. chill
