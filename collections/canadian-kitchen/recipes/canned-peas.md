## Ingredients

- canned peas
- cold water
- salted boiling water
- white sugar (a lump)
- mint (a small sprig)
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Drain and leave in cold water for ten minutes, put on in salted boiling water, cook fifteen minutes; drop in a lump of white sugar and a small sprig of mint, and cook five minutes longer.
2. Drain, butter, pepper and salt, and serve.
