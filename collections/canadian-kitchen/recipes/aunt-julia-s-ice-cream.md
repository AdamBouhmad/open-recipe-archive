## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup cream
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Scald milk
2. Beat eggs, sugar and flour together
3. Add to milk when hot, stirring constantly
4. Let cool, add cream and flavoring
5. For the freezing, use 3 of ice to 1 of salt; have ice fine and mix thoroughly with salt in a pan before packing
