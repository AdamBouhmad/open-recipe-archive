## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful rice
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 5 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 qt. cream
- 1 glass wine
- 5 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of ground nutmeg
- salt
- 1 lb. raisins, cut in halves
- 1 lb. Zante currants
- 1 lb. citron cut in strips

## Directions

1. Wash rice, and boil it in two teacupfuls of water
2. Then add, while the rice is hot, butter, sugar, eggs well beaten, nutmeg, a little salt, one glass of wine, raisins, currants, citron, and cream
3. Mix well, pour into buttered dish and bake an hour in a moderate oven
