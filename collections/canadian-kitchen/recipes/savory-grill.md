## Ingredients

- cold meat or fowl
- flour
- 1 tablespoonful of Worcestershire sauce
- 1 dessertspoonful of chutney sauce
- 1 dessertspoonful of Yorkshire relish
- 1 teaspoonful of anchovy sauce
- butter the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Cut up any cold meat or fowl, and sprinkle with flour on both sides
2. Place the mixture in any dish that will stand the heat of the top of the stove
3. Mix well
4. Allow it to heat slowly and thoroughly
