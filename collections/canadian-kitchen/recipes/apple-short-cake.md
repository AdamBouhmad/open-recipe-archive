## Ingredients

- Apple sauce seasoned with a little butter, and pinch of salt
- One pint of flour sifted with two teaspoonfuls baking powder
- a little salt
- butter half size of an egg
- one coffee-cup sweet milk
- melted butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Rub butter half size of an egg into the flour, and mix into a soft dough with one coffee-cup sweet milk
2. Divide dough into two parts, roll out one-half, put in pan
3. brush surface with melted butter
4. Roll out the rest and put on top
5. Bake in very hot oven
6. Divide, butter, and spread with sauce
7. Serve with cream
