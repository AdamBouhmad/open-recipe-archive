## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 cups graham flour
- 2 cups warm milk
- 1 cake compressed yeast
- ½ cup molasses
- 2 teaspoonfuls salt
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Make as stiff as can be stirred with a spoon
2. Let it rise over night
3. In the morning beat it a little, form in one or two loaves, put in pans
4. When it rises again, bake one hour in a moderate oven
