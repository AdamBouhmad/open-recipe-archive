## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 pinch salt
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- flour to make a soft dough
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar until very light
2. Add sour milk, soda and salt
3. Add melted butter
4. Add enough flour to make a soft dough
5. Roll out thin
6. Cut with doughnut cutter
7. Fry in hot lard
