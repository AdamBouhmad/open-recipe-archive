## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- white pepper
- salt
- butter
- egg yolks
- grated cheese
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Pare and boil sufficient quantity of potatoes till tender
2. Drain and mash and add a little milk, white pepper and salt to suit the taste
3. Beat till smooth
4. Pile on plate or dish and mould the shape of volcano
5. Make a little hollow in top of mound for crater and fill with yolks of two eggs, 4 tablespoons grated cheese and 1/2 cup butter
6. Spread a little butter over the mound and sprinkle with cracker crumbs
7. Brown in a hot oven
8. Serve hot
