## Ingredients

- 1 pint of thick cream
- 3 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- one-third of a cup of very strong coffee
- ice and salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix well together one pint of thick cream, three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar, one teaspoonful of vanilla and one-third of a cup of very strong coffee.
2. Chill thoroughly, then whip, setting the bowl in a pan of ice water.
3. Take off the froth as it rises and lay on a sieve.
4. When no more froth will rise turn the drowned whip carefully into a mould.
5. Cover lightly, binding the edges with a strip of muslin dipped in melted butter.
6. Bury in ice and salt as for freezing, let stand for two or three hours.
7. Wipe off the mould and turn out on a serving dish.
