## Ingredients

- 1 pound raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound suet
- 1 pound sugar
- 3/4 pound flour
- 1/2 pound bread crumbs
- 1/2 pound mixed peel
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 nutmeg grated
- 1/2 teaspoon spice
- 8 eggs
- 3/4 pint milk

## Directions

1. Stone raisins if not the dressed ones
2. Wash, dry and pick currants
3. Mince suet fine
4. Cut into thin slices the mixed peel
5. Grate fine the bread crumbs
6. Sift the flour
7. Add spices, sugar and salt
8. Beat the eggs and add the milk
9. Stir well that everything may be thoroughly blended
10. Press into buttered moulds or basins
11. Tie down tightly with a floured cloth
12. Boil from 6 to 8 hours
13. Serve hot with a good sweet sauce
