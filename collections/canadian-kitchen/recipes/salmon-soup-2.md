## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked salmon
- 1 pt. milk
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig parsley
- 1 slice onion

## Directions

1. Put milk in double boiler, and bay leaf, onion and parsley
2. Let it come to scalding point
3. Rub butter and flour together, put into milk, stir till it thickens
4. Remove flavorings, add salmon, which has been rubbed through a colander
5. Stir until it becomes smooth, add salt and cayenne
6. Serve
