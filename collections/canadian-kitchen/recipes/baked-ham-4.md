## Ingredients

- ham
- onion
- carrot
- fresh tomato
- hot water
- flour
- Madeira or sherry or other pale wine
- browned cracker-crumbs

## Directions

1. Soak, wash, and parboil the ham, twelve minutes to the pound.
2. Skin as soon as you can handle it, and staunch the flow of juices by rubbing flour into it.
3. Put into a good oven; slice an onion, mince a carrot and a fresh tomato, and lay about the meat, pour in half a cupful of hot water to prevent burning, cover closely, and bake twelve minutes to the pound.
4. During this time baste the ham four times with Madeira or sherry or other pale wine, using two glasses in all, and four times with the pan-gravy.
5. Have ready some browned cracker-crumbs and sift them thickly over the ham when done.
6. Leave it in the oven until firm and evenly colored.
