## Ingredients

- pigeon
- crumbs
- chopped fat pork
- hard-boiled eggs
- celery
- onion-juice
- pepper
- salt
- boiling water
- browned flour
- curry-powder
- boiled rice
- ice-cold bananas

## Directions

1. Cook as directed in English Jugged Pigeon recipe
2. Dish and add to the gravy two teaspoonfuls of curry-powder
3. Boil one minute before pouring over the birds
4. Serve with boiled rice
5. Serve ice-cold bananas with this dish
