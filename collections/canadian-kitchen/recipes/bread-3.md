## Ingredients

- Three pints of lukewarm water
- flour
- one cup of yeast
- salt

## Directions

1. Add flour enough to lukewarm water to make a thick batter
2. Add one cup of yeast, let rise till morning
3. Add salt to taste
4. Mix in flour enough to make into loaves
5. Let rise, then put in pans
