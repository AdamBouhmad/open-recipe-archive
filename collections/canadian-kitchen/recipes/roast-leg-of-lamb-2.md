## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- cupful of boiling water
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- mint sauce

## Directions

1. Put into the covered roaster, dash a cupful of boiling water over it, cover and cook about fifteen minutes to the pound.
2. Twenty minutes before taking it up, take off the cover, rub all over with butter, dredge with pepper, salt, and flour, and brown.
3. Serve with mint sauce.
