## Ingredients

- 2 cups minced meat
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 2 teaspoons flour
- 2 cups stock or milk
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire or onion juice
- 6 slices toast
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a white or brown sauce
2. Reheat meat in it
3. Serve on toast
4. Garnish with parsley
