## Ingredients

- twelve oysters
- twelve round croutons
- twelve pieces of bacon two inches long and one-half inch wide
- lemon juice
- cayenne
- watercress

## Directions

1. Beard and trim each oyster and put one on each piece of bacon
2. Squeeze on each a drop of lemon juice and a very little cayenne
3. Roll it up in the bacon
4. Cook it in a brisk oven long enough to cook the bacon
5. Serve very hot, dished on watercress
