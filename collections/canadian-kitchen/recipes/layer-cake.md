## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- Whites of 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 0.5 nutmeg
- Flour to make very stiff

## Directions

1. Drop in small spoonfuls on a buttered tin
2. Sprinkle the top with English currants and sugar
3. Bake quickly
