## Ingredients

- 1/2 dozen cooking apples
- grated bread crumbs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and slice 1/2 dozen cooking apples
2. Grease a pudding dish
3. Put a thick layer of grated bread crumbs
4. Then a layer of apples, with sugar and nutmeg
5. Repeat until dish is full
6. Bake thirty minutes
7. Serve with cream and sugar
