## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar until light
2. Add the cream and soda and cream tartar sifted in the flour by taking good measure
3. Bake (this will make three good cakes)
