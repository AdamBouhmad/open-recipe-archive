## Ingredients

- flour
- baking powder
- salt
- sausage or sausage meat

## Directions

1. Make a dough as for biscuits
2. Roll out quite thin and cut in large rounds
3. Lay sausage or sausage meat on half of each piece
4. Turn other half over and pinch together
5. Put in baking tin and bake 1/2 hour
6. These are good either hot or cold
