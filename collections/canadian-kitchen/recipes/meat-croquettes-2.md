## Ingredients

- left-over boiled or roasted beef or pork
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup stale bread crumbs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop up left-over boiled or roasted beef or pork
2. use 3 eggs, 1 cup stale bread crumbs, salt and pepper to taste
3. fry till light brown
