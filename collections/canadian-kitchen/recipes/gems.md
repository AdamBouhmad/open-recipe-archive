## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, yolks and whites, separate beaten
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to roll real soft

## Directions

1. Bake in a quick oven
