## Ingredients

- large solid tomatoes
- egg
- rolled cracker crumbs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in halves large solid tomatoes
2. Dip in egg, then in rolled cracker crumbs
3. Fry in butter with a dash of pepper and salt
4. Place on buttered toast
