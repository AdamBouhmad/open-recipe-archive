## Ingredients

- good sized piece of boiling venison
- 1 small onion
- cayenne
- a few cloves
- 1 dessertspoonful ground cinnamon
- 1 wineglass good port
- gelatine

## Directions

1. Soak the venison in salted water one-half hour.
2. Put on to boil with onion, cayenne pepper, a few cloves, and ground cinnamon.
3. When done take the venison out carefully so as not to break; put in mould.
4. Take the liquor or stock, add a wineglass of good port, strain all and pour over venison.
5. Add a little gelatine if liquor is not sufficient to harden.
