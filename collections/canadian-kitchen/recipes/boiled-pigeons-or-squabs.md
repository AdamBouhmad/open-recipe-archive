## Ingredients

- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Young pigeons, or squabs, are cleaned, washed and dried carefully with a clean cloth
2. Split down the back, and broil like chickens
3. Season with pepper and salt and butter liberally in dishing them
