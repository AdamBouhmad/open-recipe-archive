## Ingredients

- whites of 4 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- ¾ cup butter
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or lemon
- 2¾ cups sifted flour
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup molasses
- ¾ cup butter
- ¾ cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon mace
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1¾ cups sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put it in the cake dish alternately, first one part and then the other
