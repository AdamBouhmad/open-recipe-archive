## Ingredients

- 4 ripe bananas
- 3 oranges
- 1 apple thin sliced
- 1 cup water
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- ½ cup sugar

## Directions

1. Pass pulp of bananas through a colander, then a sieve
2. Add apple and water
3. When boiling, add cornstarch and sugar
4. Simmer 10 minutes
5. Add orange juices
6. Strain
