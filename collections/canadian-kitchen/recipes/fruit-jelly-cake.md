## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 small cup chopped raisins
- 1/2 teaspoon allspice
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir together sugar, butter, milk, flour, eggs, and baking powder
2. Divide into 3 equal parts
3. Into 1 part stir chopped raisins, allspice, cinnamon, and nutmeg
