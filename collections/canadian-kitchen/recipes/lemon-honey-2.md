## Ingredients

- 3 ounces of fresh butter
- 1 cup of sugar
- 3 eggs, yolks beaten
- Grated peel of a large lemon
- Juice of the lemon

## Directions

1. Melt butter and sugar together
2. Stir the beaten yolks of 3 eggs into which have been mixed the grated peel of a large lemon
3. Stir over the fire till it begins to thicken
4. Add juice of the lemon
5. Continue stirring till as thick as honey
6. Pour into jelly tumblers and cover
