## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 cup oatmeal
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 cup raisins
- a pinch salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. mix as thick as possible
2. drop in greased tins far apart the size of walnuts
