## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. chops
- Pepper and salt
- 1 slice onion
- 1 cup hot water
- 2 carrots
- browned flour
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Brown chops
2. Brown carefully a sliced onion
3. Add carrots cut in pieces, pepper, salt, and a cup of hot water
4. Cook slowly two hours
5. Add more water if necessary
6. Thicken slightly with browned flour when cooked
7. Add a little minced parsley
