## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 3 teaspoonfuls soda dissolved in 3 tablespoonfuls boiling water
- Flour to make stiff dough
- any kind of jelly

## Directions

1. Roll thin
2. cut with cookie-cutter
3. when cooked and while hot spread with any kind of jelly and stick two together
