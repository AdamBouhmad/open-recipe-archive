## Ingredients

- 4 pounds peaches stewed and pared
- 2 1/2 pounds sugar
- juice 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Boil peaches for 3/4 hour
2. Add sugar and lemons
3. Boil 20 minutes
4. Skim while boiling
