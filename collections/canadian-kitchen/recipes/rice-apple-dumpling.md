## Ingredients

- half pound of rice
- butter
- salt
- two well-beaten eggs
- squares of white muslin
- flour
- apples, peeled and cored
- currant jelly
- sugar
- spice
- cream or vanilla sauce

## Directions

1. Boil rice and season it with butter and salt.
2. After removing it from the fire stir in two well-beaten eggs.
3. Have ready squares of white muslin. Dip them one by one in hot water, sprinkle with flour and put in each two tablespoonfuls of the rice.
4. Spread the rice and in the centre place a peeled and cored apple.
5. In the cavity left by the core, put some currant jelly and sugar or spice and sugar.
6. Draw the corners of the cloth together and fasten closely at the top with pins.
7. Boil or steam for a half hour.
8. Serve with cream or vanilla sauce.
