## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 small teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 small nutmeg
- ¾ cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to thicken

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in buttermilk
2. Mix all ingredients together and bake
