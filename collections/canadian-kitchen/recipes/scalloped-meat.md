## Ingredients

- 2 cups meat
- 3 teaspoons fat
- 3 teaspoons flour
- 1 1/2 cups hot milk or stock
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- onion juice or parsley
- 2 cups buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 cup buttered crumbs in a baking dish
2. Mix the meat in the sauce
3. Pour this into the dish
4. Cover the top with the remainder of the crumbs
5. Brown in a hot oven 20 minutes
