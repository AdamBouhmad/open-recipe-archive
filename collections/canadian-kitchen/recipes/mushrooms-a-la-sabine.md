## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- melted butter
- stock
- grated cheese
- toast

## Directions

1. Sprinkle with salt, pepper
2. roll in flour
3. saute in melted butter 3 minutes
4. add stock, cook 5 minutes
5. sprinkle with grated cheese
6. Serve on toast
