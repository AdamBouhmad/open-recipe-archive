## Ingredients

- bananas
- boiled dressing
- finely chipped walnuts
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Peel bananas and cut in two lengthwise
2. Cut across in quarters
3. Roll each quarter in boiled dressing
4. Roll in finely chipped walnuts
5. Serve on lettuce leaves
