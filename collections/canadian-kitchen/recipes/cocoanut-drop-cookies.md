## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 3/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sour cream
- 3/2 teaspoon soda
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 tablespoons desiccated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs
2. Add sugar, butter, sour cream, soda, flour, vanilla and cocoanut
3. Drop on buttered tins
4. Bake a delicate brown
