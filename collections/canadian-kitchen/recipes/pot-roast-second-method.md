## Ingredients

- meat
- flour
- dripping
- water

## Directions

1. Rub meat well with flour
2. Have pot hot with a little dripping in it
3. Put in meat and let brown nicely
4. Keep turning till browned all around
5. Put in a little water
6. Cook slowly till tender
7. Take out and make nice brown gravy
