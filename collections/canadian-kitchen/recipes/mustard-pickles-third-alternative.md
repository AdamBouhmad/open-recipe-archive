## Ingredients

- 2 cups vinegar
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup sugar
- pepper
- cayenne or ginger
- small green cucumbers

## Directions

1. Combine 2 cups vinegar, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 tablespoon flour, 1 cup sugar, a little pepper, and cayenne or ginger
2. Thicken over fire
3. Have ready small green cucumbers
4. Put in as many as pickle will cover
5. Let boil up a few minutes on back of range
6. This may be made in winter with cucumbers taken from brine and freshened, or from sliced cucumbers
