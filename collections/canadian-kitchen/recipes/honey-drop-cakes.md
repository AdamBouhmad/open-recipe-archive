## Ingredients

- 1 cup honey
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter or lard
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tablespoon soda
- 4 cups sifted flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake
