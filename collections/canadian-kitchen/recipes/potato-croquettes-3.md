## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 1 cup chopped meat
- 1 even tablespoon chopped onions
- 1 even tablespoon chopped parsley
- salt
- flour
- beaten egg
- biscuit crumbs

## Directions

1. Take 2 cups of mashed potatoes (left from dinner) and 1 cup chopped meat (any kind you have left over)
2. Add 1 even tablespoon each chopped onions and parsley and a little salt
3. Mix all together and with the hands mould into oblongs the size of a finger
4. Dust with flour
5. Roll in beaten egg and then in biscuit crumbs
6. Fry, turning frequently, so they will be round when finished
7. Fry a golden brown and serve hot
