## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 beaten eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 level teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix 1 level teaspoon soda with cream
2. Add to eggs and sugar
3. Add 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves and 1 teaspoon cinnamon
4. Bake 20 minutes in a loaf pan
