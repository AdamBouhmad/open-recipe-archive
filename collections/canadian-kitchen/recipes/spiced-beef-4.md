## Ingredients

- shank
- onion
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil a shank until tender
2. Lift out meat and chop very fine
3. Add onion, pepper, salt or other seasoning if you choose
4. Return to kettle containing the liquid and boil to a jelly
5. Put in bowls to cool
