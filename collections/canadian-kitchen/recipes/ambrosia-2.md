## Ingredients

- one pineapple chopped quite fine
- one-half box strawberries
- six bananas sliced and the slices quartered
- six oranges sliced and the slices quartered
- one lemon cut fine
- sugar
- one wineglassful sherry

## Directions

1. Chop pineapple quite fine
2. Slice bananas and quarter the slices
3. Slice oranges and quarter the slices
4. Cut lemon fine
5. sweeten to taste
6. Add one wineglassful sherry and set away until cold
