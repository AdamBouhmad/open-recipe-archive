## Ingredients

- 6 oz. chocolate, grated
- 6 oz. sugar
- 1 pt. cream
- Yolks of 6 eggs
- 2 oz. gelatine

## Directions

1. Grate finely six ounces chocolate; put chocolate into a saucepan with cream, sugar, and yolks of eggs.
2. Stir over fire until it thickens, run through sieve; add dissolved gelatine.
3. Mix and fill mould and place on ice.
