## Ingredients

- 4 cups bread crumbs
- 4 cups milk
- 4 eggs yolks
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 lemon grated rind only
- 1 cup sugar
- jelly
- 4 eggs whites
- 1 lemon juice
- 3/4 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk until soft
2. Beat the eggs, sugar and a little salt together
3. Add to the bread and milk with the butter and lemon
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Bake in a quick oven, but do not let it get watery
6. After it is cooked spread with jelly
7. Frost with whites of eggs, lemon juice and sugar mixed together and whipped until light
