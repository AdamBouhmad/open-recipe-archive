## Ingredients

- one ounce butter
- one ounce sugar
- one ounce flour
- one gill milk
- vanilla
- three eggs
- four ounces mixed fruits: pears, peaches, glace cherries, angelica, apricots, pineapple
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Dissolve butter in a sauce-pan, mix in sugar, flour, milk
2. Stir all over the fire until boiling
3. Cover it with a lid, and let it stand on a warm part of the stove for five minutes
4. Remove it to the table and stir in the yolks of the eggs
5. Whip the whites to a stiff froth and stir them in lightly and thoroughly
6. Add a few drops of vanilla essence
7. Add about four ounces of different sorts of fruit, cut into small pieces, such as pears, peaches, glace cherries, angelica, apricots, pineapple, etc.
8. Put the pudding into a plain round mould, which must be previously buttered, covered with a greased paper
9. Steam for three-quarters of an hour
10. Turn on to a dish and pour wine sauce round
