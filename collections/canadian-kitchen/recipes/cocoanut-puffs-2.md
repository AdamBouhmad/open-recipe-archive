## Ingredients

- Whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup ground sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 cups shredded cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat the whites well
2. Add the sugar and beat over steam until a crust forms on the bottom and sides of dish
