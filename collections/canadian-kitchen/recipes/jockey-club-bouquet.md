## Ingredients

- Extract of rose, one pint
- Extract of tuberose, one pint
- Extract of cassia, half a pint
- Extract of jasmine, four ounces
- Tincture of civet, three ounces

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Filter the mixture
