## Ingredients

- 4 cups vinegar
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons whole cloves
- allspice and cinnamon
- 7 pounds fruit (melon, apples, large-sized cucumbers)

## Directions

1. Peel and seed cucumbers
2. Boil fruit until it can be pierced with a straw
3. Put in glass sealers
4. Boil syrup a few minutes
5. Pour over fruit while hot
6. Set away to cool
7. Seal tight
