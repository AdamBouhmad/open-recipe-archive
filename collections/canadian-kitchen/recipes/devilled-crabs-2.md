## Ingredients

- 2 cups crab meat (8-10 crabs)
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon mixed parsley
- 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
- 2 yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Make white sauce, add seasoning, meat and yolk of egg
2. Cook to thicken
3. Cool slightly, put into shells
4. Cover with buttered crumbs and brown in oven
