## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- flour

## Directions

1. stir in enough flour to make stiff enough to drop from a fork
2. Drop in with meat and gravy, boiling hot
3. let boil slowly 20 minutes
