## Ingredients

- 1 pound shin beef
- 2 sausages
- 1 cup tomatoes
- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut beef in small pieces and season with salt and pepper
2. Cut sausages in 1-inch pieces and roll in flour
3. Put sausages and beef in earthen dish
4. Add tomatoes, oatmeal, and Worcestershire sauce
5. Cook 5 hours in aladdin or slow oven
