## Ingredients

- One quart of chicken, veal, or calf's-head broth
- One small onion, minced
- A pinch of mace
- Half a cupful of soaked rice
- Juice of a lemon
- One generous tablespoonful of brown roux
- One teaspoonful of curry powder
- Salt to taste
- One teacupful of strained tomato-juice

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a pot and simmer until rice is cooked through
