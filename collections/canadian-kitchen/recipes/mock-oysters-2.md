## Ingredients

- Pulp of 1 can corn
- sifted crumbs to hold mixture together
- egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Add enough sifted crumbs to hold mixture together
2. Moisten with egg, add baking powder, and seasoning
3. Drop by spoonfuls into hot fat
