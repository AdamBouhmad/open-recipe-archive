## Ingredients

- 1 bundle of asparagus
- salted water
- 1 quart of good stock
- 2 ounces of butter
- 2 ounces of flour
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 gill of cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean a bundle of asparagus, cut off tips and boil in salted water till soft
2. Boil the stalks twenty minutes in a quart of good stock
3. Put two ounces of butter in a stew-pan with two ounces of flour, mix smoothly and pour in the hot stock, having previously pulped the asparagus through a sieve
4. Add one pint of milk
5. Boil up and skim
6. Put the tips in a tureen with a gill of cream
7. Pour in the boiling soup
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve
