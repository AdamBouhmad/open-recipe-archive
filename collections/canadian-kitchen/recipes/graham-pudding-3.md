## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup Graham flour
- 3/4 cup golden syrup
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 scant teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup figs or raisins

## Directions

1. Melt butter.
2. Add milk and egg well beaten.
3. Add fruit last.
4. Sift flour on fruit.
5. Steam 1 1/2 hours.
