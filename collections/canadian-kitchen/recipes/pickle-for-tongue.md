## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls salt
- 1 tablespoon saltpetre
- 1/8 teaspoon allspice
- 1/8 teaspoon cloves
- tongue

## Directions

1. Rub the tongue well, and leave in pickle for 10 days.
2. Boil gently until tender, press and jelly.
