## Ingredients

- 3 large potatoes
- 4 biscuits
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup sweet milk or cream
- pepper
- salt
- 1 can salmon
- butter

## Directions

1. Mash 3 large potatoes very fine
2. Roll 4 biscuits smooth
3. Mix together potatoes, biscuits, 1 egg, 3/4 cup sweet milk or cream, little pepper, salt, and 1 can salmon
4. Fry in buttered pan
