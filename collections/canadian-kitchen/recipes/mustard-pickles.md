## Ingredients

- 1.5 gallons white wine vinegar
- 1 pound mustard
- 4 ounces ginger, bruised
- 1 ounce garlic
- 2 ounces allspice
- 2 pounds salt
- 0.5 ounce chillies, bruised
- 2 onions, cut in pieces
- 2 ounces turmeric

## Directions

1. Mix the mustard and turmeric well with some of the vinegar before putting on to boil
2. Boil for a quarter of an hour, the onions and garlic only three minutes
3. When done put in a crock
4. When cold put in the vegetables
