## Ingredients

- 5 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup golden syrup
- 1 cup water
- 4 egg whites
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Put sugar, water and syrup on to boil and stir until it melts
2. Beat the egg whites
3. After the mixture has boiled a few minutes, pour 1 cup over the egg whites
4. Let the remainder boil until it hairs
5. Add nuts and pour over the eggs
6. Beat until stiff
7. When partially cool, beat in the flavoring
8. Pour on buttered plates and cut in squares as used
