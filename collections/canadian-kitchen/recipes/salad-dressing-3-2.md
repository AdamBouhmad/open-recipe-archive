## Ingredients

- 1 cup vinegar
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/6 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 cup cream

## Directions

1. Heat 1 cup vinegar
2. Beat 2 eggs well
3. Pour vinegar over eggs
4. Add sugar, salt, pepper and cream
