## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 quart milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- flavor to taste

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 tins and split while hot and fill in with cream filling
2. For cream filling: heat 1 quart milk
3. Mix 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1 egg, 1/4 cup white sugar, 2 tablespoons butter
4. Add to milk and flavor to taste
