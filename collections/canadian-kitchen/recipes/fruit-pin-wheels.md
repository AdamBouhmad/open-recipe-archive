## Ingredients

- 1 pt flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 0.5 pt milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 cup currants
- a little nutmeg
- a large spoonful of slightly melted butter
- one cupful of Demerara sugar

## Directions

1. Mix sifted flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder
2. Rub in butter, and mix into a moderately stiff dough with half a pint of milk
3. Sprinkle the pastry board with flour, turn out the dough and roll it to a large square about half an inch thick
4. Spread a large spoonful of slightly melted butter on this
5. Add one cupful of Demerara sugar, and one cupful of well-cleaned currants
6. Grate a little nutmeg over the sugar and currants
7. Roll up just like a jelly roll or poly-poly
8. Cut the roll into slices about three-quarters of an inch thick
9. Place them upon a well-buttered baking shell or tin, but do not let the slices touch
