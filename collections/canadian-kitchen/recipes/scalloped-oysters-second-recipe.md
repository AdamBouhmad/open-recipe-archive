## Ingredients

- one quart of oysters
- oyster liquor
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one cup of hot milk
- one cup of oyster liquor
- two teaspoons of butter
- six tablespoons of bread crumbs
- three tablespoons of chopped celery
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scald a quart of oysters in their own liquor till plump
2. Drain and place to keep warm
3. Make sauce of one tablespoon each of butter and flour
4. Heat in a pan two teaspoons of butter, and brown six tablespoons of bread crumbs
5. Put three tablespoons of chopped celery in the bottom of a bake dish
6. Add the white sauce flavored with salt and pepper to taste
7. Top with the browned crumbs
8. Place in oven and heat thoroughly
9. Serve very hot
