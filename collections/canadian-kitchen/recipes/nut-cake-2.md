## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup nuts

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups sugar, 1 cup butter, 1 cup milk, 3 eggs, 3 cups flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder, and 1 cup nuts
2. Bake
