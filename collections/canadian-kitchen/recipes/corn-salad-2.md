## Ingredients

- 1 dozen ears corn
- 1 head cabbage
- 4 onions
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 large sweet pepper
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 small teaspoon turmeric

## Directions

1. Boil corn and cut off
2. Chop all together: corn, cabbage, onions, and pepper
3. Cook, adding 1 pint vinegar
4. Mix mustard, turmeric with water and add to the mixture
5. Cook a few minutes
