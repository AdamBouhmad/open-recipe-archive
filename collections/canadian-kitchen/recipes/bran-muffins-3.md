## Ingredients

- 1 egg beaten to stiff froth
- 3/4 cup bran
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 4 tablespoons sweet cream
- the remainder of the cup filled with buttermilk
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 even teaspoon of soda

## Directions

1. Add last of all the beaten whites and soda
