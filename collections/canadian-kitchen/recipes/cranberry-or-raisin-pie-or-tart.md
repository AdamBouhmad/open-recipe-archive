## Ingredients

- 1½ cups cranberries
- ¾ cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons cracker crumbs
- ¾ cup seeded raisins
- ½ cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Chop cranberries and raisins together
2. Add water and cook slightly
3. Add sugar and crumbs
4. Cook until thoroughly mixed
5. When cool, add vanilla
6. Just before serving, put between an upper and under crust, previously baked or in a shell and cover with a lattice of baked crust
