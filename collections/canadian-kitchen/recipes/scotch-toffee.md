## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. yellow sugar
- 6 oz. fresh butter
- Cream to make thin batter

## Directions

1. Melt the butter slowly and when fully melted add the sugar
2. Mix thoroughly and stir in sufficient cream to make the whole of the consistency of a thin batter
3. Now place on the stove and stir until the boiling point is reached
4. After this it must not be stirred on any account
5. Boil very slowly until it feels tough, but not brittle, when tested by cooling a little in cold water
6. Now pour into buttered trays and cool slowly, then cut into squares
