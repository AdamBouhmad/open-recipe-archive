## Ingredients

- 1 ounce melted butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- pinch pepper
- 1 cup milk
- ¾ cup grated cheese
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix together in a saucepan 1 ounce melted butter and 1 tablespoon flour
2. When quite smooth, add 1 teaspoon salt and a pinch pepper and 1 cup milk
3. Cook and stir this till thick as good cream
4. Remove from stove and beat in ¾ cup grated cheese and yolks of 2 eggs
5. Beat in lightly the stiffly beaten whites of the eggs
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered pudding dish
7. Bake in a very hot oven for about 25 minutes
8. Serve as soon as taken from oven
