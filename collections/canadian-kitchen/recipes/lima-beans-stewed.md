## Ingredients

- a quart of beans
- hot, salted water
- four tablespoonfuls of hot milk
- a tablespoonful of butter
- a teaspoonful of flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Shell a quart of beans, and boil tender in hot, salted water
2. Drain, add four tablespoonfuls of hot milk, in which has been melted a tablespoonful of butter rolled in a teaspoonful of flour
3. Simmer for five minutes
4. Season with pepper and salt, and serve
