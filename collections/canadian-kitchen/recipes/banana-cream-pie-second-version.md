## Ingredients

- 2 cups sweet milk (large cups)
- 2 large tablespoons cornstarch
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- little piece of butter
- little salt
- few drops oil of lemon or vanilla
- bananas

## Directions

1. cook until it thickens in double boiler
2. add few drops oil of lemon or vanilla, also a little in the icing
3. slice bananas in bottom of pie dish, and cover with the custard, with icing on top
4. let get perfectly cool before using
