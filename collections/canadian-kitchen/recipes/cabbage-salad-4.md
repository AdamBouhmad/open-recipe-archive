## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1½ cups sweet cream
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 3½ cup vinegar
- chopped cabbage

## Directions

1. Cook in a double boiler
2. Stir until thick
3. When cold or ready to use, add vinegar
4. Serve on chopped cabbage
