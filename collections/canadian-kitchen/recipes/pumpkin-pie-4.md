## Ingredients

- 6 cups cooked pumpkin
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 2 finely rolled crackers
- 1 teaspoonful ginger
- 1/4 nutmeg
- Cinnamon and salt to taste
- rich milk

## Directions

1. Put pumpkin in a pan with eggs, sugar, molasses (maple preferred), crackers, ginger, nutmeg, cinnamon and salt.
2. Stir well, add enough rich milk to thin.
3. Bake in pastry shape.
