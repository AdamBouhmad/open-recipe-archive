## Ingredients

- 2 pounds lobster
- 2 cups cold water
- 4 cups milk
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 3/4 teaspoons salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Remove meat from lobster shell
2. Add cold water to body bones and tough end of claws
3. Cut in pieces
4. Bring slowly to the boiling point and cook 20 minutes
5. Drain and reserve liquor
6. Thicken with butter and flour
7. Scald milk with tail meat of lobster finely chopped
8. Strain and add to liquor
9. Season
10. Add tender claw meat
11. When coral is found in lobster, wash and wipe it
12. Force through strainer
13. Put in mortar with butter
14. Work until blended
15. Add flour and stir into soup
