## Ingredients

- small cup and a half of milk
- one cup of flour
- one egg
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- one-half teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt
2. Grease iron (special iron for waffles) well and have hot
3. Cook until done
