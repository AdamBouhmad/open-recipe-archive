## Ingredients

- large apples
- 1 pint water
- large cup sugar
- heaping dessertspoon gelatine
- cold water
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel some large apples and core, but do not quarter
2. Boil a pint of water with a large cup of sugar
3. Put in the apples
4. Cook till they are transparent, but do not let them break
5. Arrange these in a deep dish
6. Measure the syrup, and to the pint add a heaping dessertspoon of gelatine dissolved in a little cold water
7. Strain
8. When cool pour a little at a time over the apples till they are covered with jelly
9. Turn out and invert so that they will be right side up on a glass dish
10. Serve with whipped cream
