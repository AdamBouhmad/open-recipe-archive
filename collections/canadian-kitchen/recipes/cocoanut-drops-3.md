## Ingredients

- 1 grated cocoanut
- half its weight of sugar
- the white of one egg

## Directions

1. Add sugar to grated cocoanut and the white of one egg
2. Beat to a froth and mix thoroughly
3. Drop on buttered white paper or tin sheets
4. Bake 15 minutes
