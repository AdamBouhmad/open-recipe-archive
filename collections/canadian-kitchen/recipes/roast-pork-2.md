## Ingredients

- pork leg, loin, shoulder, or chine
- boiling water
- force-meat
- bread crumbs
- sage
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- grated lemon-peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The skin is scored in squares, or in parallel lines, the knife just cutting through to the flesh.
2. Put into the roaster, dash a cup of boiling water over it; heat gradually until the fat begins to run, when quicken the fire.
3. Baste often and abundantly, that the skin may be tender, even when crisp.
4. Allow at least twenty minutes to the pound.
5. Rub well into the deep lines made by the knife in the rind a force-meat of crumbs, sage and onions, seasoned with pepper, salt, a little grated lemon-peel, and the juice of a lemon.
6. This was done before the meat went into the oven and the cracks were well filled.
7. Serve apple sauce with roast pork, or Chili sauce, or catsup, or a good bread sauce.
