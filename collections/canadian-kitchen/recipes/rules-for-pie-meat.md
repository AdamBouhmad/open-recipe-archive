## Ingredients

- 3 pints chopped meat
- 6 pints chopped apple
- 1 pint boiled cider
- 1 pint molasses
- 2 pints brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 3 tablespoons cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon nutmeg
- 3 tablespoons salt
- currants
- raisins
- citron
- wine or brandy

## Directions

1. Cook slowly
