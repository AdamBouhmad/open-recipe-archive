## Ingredients

- 1 pound prunes
- 1 quart cold water
- 3 1/2 cup gelatine dissolved in cold water
- 1 lemon sliced thin, rind and all
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak prunes over night in 1 quart cold water
2. Boil in the morning in the same water till soft
3. Remove from stove, take out stones
4. Have ready 3 1/2 cup gelatine which has been dissolved in cold water
5. Add 1 lemon sliced thin, rind and all, to the prunes
6. Boil a few minutes longer and turn into mould
7. Serve with whipped cream
