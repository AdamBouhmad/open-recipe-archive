## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Flavoring as desired

## Directions

1. Bake in a moderate oven.
2. Ice with the following: The whites of three eggs well beaten with one and one-half cups of sugar.
