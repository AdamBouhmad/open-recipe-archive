## Ingredients

- carrots
- water
- onions
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil as many carrots as are required in water until done
2. Drain the carrots
3. Fry as many onions as there are carrots in butter
4. Put carrots into the pan with the onions
5. Serve hot
6. Season with salt and pepper
