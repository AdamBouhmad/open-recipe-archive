## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1/16 teaspoon mustard
- ⅜ teaspoon salt
- cayenne pepper
- ½ pound cheese cut fine
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Make white sauce
2. Add yolks
3. Add cheese
4. Fold in beaten whites
5. Cover in blazer and let stand 25 minutes over hot water without lifting the lid
