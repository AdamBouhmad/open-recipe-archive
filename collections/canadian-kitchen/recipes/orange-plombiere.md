## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 1 cup cold water
- cups sugar
- 1 cup cream

## Directions

1. Strain juice of oranges
2. Rub rind from 1 orange into a cup cold water, add sugar, turn into freezer
3. When mixture begins to thicken, add cream
4. Freeze until very stiff
5. Remove dasher, pack firm
