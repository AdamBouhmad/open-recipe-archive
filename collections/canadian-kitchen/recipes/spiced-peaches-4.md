## Ingredients

- 1 quart vinegar
- 6 cups brown sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 1 cup mixed spices
- whole cloves
- peaches

## Directions

1. Make syrup of 1 quart vinegar, 6 cups brown sugar, 1 stick cinnamon, and 1 cup mixed spices
2. Put whole cloves in the peaches
3. Add peaches to syrup
