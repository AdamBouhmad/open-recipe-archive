## Ingredients

- 1 teacup soft boiled rice
- yolk of 1 egg
- pinch salt
- 2 tablespoons sifted flour
- sweet milk
- beaten white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Beat together rice, yolk of egg, salt, and flour
2. Add sweet milk until about the consistency of sponge cake or thick cream
3. Just before baking stir in lightly the beaten white of an egg
