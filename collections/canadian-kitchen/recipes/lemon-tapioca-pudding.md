## Ingredients

- one small cup tapioca
- two cups of water
- another cup of water
- juice of one and a half large lemons
- rind of one lemon
- one cup sugar
- yolks of two eggs beaten
- whites of eggs
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca over night in two cups of water
2. Add another cup of water in the morning
3. Add juice of one and a half large lemons, rind of one lemon, one cup sugar, yolks of two eggs beaten
4. Cook till the tapioca is clear—about an hour
5. Whip whites of eggs with a little sugar and put on the top
