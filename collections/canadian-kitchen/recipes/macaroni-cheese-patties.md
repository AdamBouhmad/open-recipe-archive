## Ingredients

- 1 ounce macaroni
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- salt and pepper
- pie crust
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Boil macaroni in quickly boiling water until tender
2. Drain and cut into small pieces
3. Put into a pan and mix with cream, grated cheese, salt and pepper
4. Line patty pans with pie crust and fill with macaroni mixture
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and grated cheese
6. Bake until lightly browned
