## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 2 eggs
- 2 soda biscuits rolled finely
- pepper and salt
- little lemon juice
- butter
- hot water

## Directions

1. Wet this mixture with hot water
2. Press in a dish
3. Steam for 3/4 hour
4. Slice and eat cold
