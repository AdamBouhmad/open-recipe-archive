## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups sugar, scant
- Butter size of egg
- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- 1 1/2 cups water

## Directions

1. Grate the rind and squeeze the juice of the lemons
2. Beat eggs light and melt the butter
3. Mix all together
4. Make a puff paste and line the tins with the pastry
5. Brush over with the white of an egg and fill with the lemon mixture and bake
