## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe peeled tomatoes
- 8 onions
- 3 ripe peppers
- 1¾ cups white sugar or 1½ cups horseradish
- ½ cup salt
- 2 tablespoons mustard seeds
- 2 tablespoons pepper
- 1 stalk of celery
- 1 quart vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onions
2. Do not cook
3. Can right up
