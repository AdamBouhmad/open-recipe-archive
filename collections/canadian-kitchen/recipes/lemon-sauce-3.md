## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 1/2 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Beat together until light the yolks of eggs, sugar, butter, cornstarch, and lemon juice
2. Stir this into boiling water until sufficiently thick for the table
