## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour equal in weight to 2 eggs
- sugar equal in weight to 2 eggs
- butter equal in weight to 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons marmalade
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons marmalade

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar together.
2. Lightly stir in other ingredients.
3. Set in a buttered basin and steam 2 hours.
4. Turn out to serve and pour marmalade sauce round.
5. For sauce: boil milk with cornstarch, sugar and marmalade.
