## Ingredients

- 1 large tablespoonful flax-seed
- 1 tumbler and a half cold water
- 1 quarter pound pulverized sugar
- 1 ounce pulverized gum arable
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Boil flax-seed and cold water together till the liquid becomes very sticky
2. Strain it hot over the pulverized sugar and gum arable
3. Stir it till quite dissolved
4. Squeeze into it the juice of a lemon
5. Take a wine-glass of it as often as the cough is troublesome
