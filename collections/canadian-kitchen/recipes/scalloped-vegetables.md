## Ingredients

- cooked vegetable
- cream sauce made with milk substituted for vegetable water
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make a cream sauce, using the same ingredients as for vegetable sauce, substituting milk for the vegetable water
2. To each cup of cooked vegetable use one cup of cream sauce
3. Place in alternate layers in a baking dish, having the top layer sauce
4. Sprinkle with crumbs that have been mixed with a small quantity of melted butter
5. Sprinkle grated cheese over each layer
6. Brown in the oven
