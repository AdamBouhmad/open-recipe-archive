## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 scant teaspoon soda
- 2 scant teaspoons cream tartar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Sift flour, soda, cream tartar, and salt twice
2. Add 3/4 cup sweet milk and mix light, handling as little as possible
3. Roll 1/4 inch thick and cut into small squares
4. Sift a little flour over them
5. When chicken is nearly cooked, lay dumplings on top
6. Cover tightly and let boil for fifteen minutes without moving the cover
