## Ingredients

- 1 cup yeast
- 1 pt. warm water
- 2 pts. rye flour
- 1 pt. wheat flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls lard
- 2 tablespoonfuls brown sugar
- 1 qt. warm milk
- 1 cup Indian meal
- Enough rye flour to make into dough

## Directions

1. Mix together yeast, warm water, rye flour, flour, lard and brown sugar
2. Beat together, and let rise over night
3. In the morning mix with this: One quart of warm milk, one cup of Indian meal, enough rye flour to make into dough
4. Knead; cover, set in a warm place to rise two or three hours
5. Knead again, and make into loaves
6. If there is the least tendency to sourness, add a teaspoonful of soda, dissolved in warm water
7. It is best to always add this in warm weather
8. Rub soda smooth with a knife blade before measuring
