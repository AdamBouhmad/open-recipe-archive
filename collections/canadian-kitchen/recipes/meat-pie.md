## Ingredients

- left-over meat
- salt and pepper
- flour
- meat gravy
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- 1 saltspoon salt
- 1 pint flour
- 1 tablespoon lard
- 1 scant cup milk

## Directions

1. Take left-over meat and place in baking dish
2. Add salt and pepper and dredge with flour
3. Pour over this meat gravy and heat
4. While this is heating, make a rich biscuit dough by sifting 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder and 1 saltspoon salt into 1 pint flour
5. Rub in 1 tablespoon lard
6. Wet with 1 scant cup milk
7. Drop this by the spoonful on the hot meat from the oven and spread to cover the pie
8. Bake 3/4 hour
