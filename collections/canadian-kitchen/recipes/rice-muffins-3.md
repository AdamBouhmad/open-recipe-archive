## Ingredients

- 1 scant cup boiled rice
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- A little salt
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter

## Directions

1. Combine boiled rice, egg, sugar, salt, and milk
2. Mix in flour and baking powder
3. Stir in melted butter
4. Bake in muffin tins
