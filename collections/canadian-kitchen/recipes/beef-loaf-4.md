## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 2 tablespoons dripping

## Directions

1. Chop beef fine
2. Put egg into a bowl
3. Beat egg just enough to bring yolk and white together
4. Add dripping
5. Mix together and pour it over the meat
6. Mix the whole well together
7. Wet the hands and form the meat into a roll
8. Bake in a hot oven 30 minutes
9. Baste every 10 minutes
