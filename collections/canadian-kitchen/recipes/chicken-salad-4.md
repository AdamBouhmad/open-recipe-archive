## Ingredients

- 2 qts. cooked chicken (cut in dice)
- 1 qt. celery (cut in 1/2-inch pieces)
- 4 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls oil
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful pepper

## Directions

1. Place chicken in an earthen bowl, and season with vinegar, oil, salt and pepper
2. Set away in a cold place for two or three hours
3. Put minced celery in the ice-box until serving time
4. Make the mayonnaise dressing
5. Mix the chicken and celery together, and add half the dressing
6. Arrange in a salad bowl or on a flat dish, and pour the remainder of the dressing over it
7. Garnish with white celery leaves
