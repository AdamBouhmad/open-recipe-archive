## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped meat
- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 1 egg
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Take 1 cup chopped meat, 1 cup mashed potatoes, 1 egg well beaten, and a little seasoning
2. Make into patty cakes and fry
