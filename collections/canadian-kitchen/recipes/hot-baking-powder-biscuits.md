## Ingredients

- Two cups flour sifted well
- Four teaspoons baking-powder
- One scant teaspoon salt
- One tablespoon lard
- One tablespoon butter
- Three-quarters cup milk (or water, or both)

## Directions

1. Mix to a soft dough; drop on pan, or roll out.
