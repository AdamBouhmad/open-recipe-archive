## Ingredients

- 6 eggs hard boiled
- 3/4 cup finely chopped ham or chicken
- 1 or 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 teaspoon mustard
- cayenne
- 3/4 teaspoon salt (omit with ham)
- 1 teaspoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the eggs in half transversely, remove the yolk.
2. Press yolk through strainer, add ham and seasonings, moistening the mustard in vinegar.
3. Mould into balls, fit into whites and serve on a thick bed parsley or lettuce.
