## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 pounds fine red raspberries (not too ripe)
- equal quantity of the finest quality white sugar
- 1 quart currant juice

## Directions

1. Mash the whole well in a preserving kettle
2. Add 1 quart currant juice and boil gently until it jellies upon a cold plate
3. Put into small jars, cover with brandied paper, and tie a thick white paper over them
4. Keep in a dark, dry and cool place
