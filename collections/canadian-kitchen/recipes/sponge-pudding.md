## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 4 tablespoonfuls flour
- 5 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 6 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- 1 pt. sweet milk

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar, flour and one pint of sweet milk together, then take off the fire and stir in butter.
2. When cool add the eggs well beaten.
3. Bake one hour in a pan of water.
