## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 3/4 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in warm water
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- cinnamon
- cloves
- nutmeg
- 3/4 cup nut meats
- 3/4 cup chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Drop 1 teaspoon of mixture in a place on a buttered tin and bake in quick oven.
