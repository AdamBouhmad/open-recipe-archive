## Ingredients

- 1 oz. arrowroot
- 3 oz. powdered sugar
- 1 egg, yolk only
- 1 qt. milk
- A little lemon peel and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix the arrowroot with a little of the milk to a smooth paste
2. Add to it the egg
3. Boil the remainder of the milk with the sugar and flavoring
4. Pour it boiling hot on the arrowroot
5. Keep it well stirred till nearly cold
