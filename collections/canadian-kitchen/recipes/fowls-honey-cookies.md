## Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons soda
- 2 cups warm honey
- 1 cup shortening containing salt
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 1 cup hot water
- flour sufficient to roll

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in warm honey
2. Add shortening containing salt and ginger
3. Add hot water
4. Mix with flour sufficient to roll
