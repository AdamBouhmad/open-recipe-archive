## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg well beaten
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 4 level teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Bake in buttered tins in quick oven
