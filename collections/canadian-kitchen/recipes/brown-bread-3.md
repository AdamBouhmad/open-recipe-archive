## Ingredients

- 2 cups white flour
- 3 cups Graham flour
- almost 3 cups milk
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Bake in a moderate oven
