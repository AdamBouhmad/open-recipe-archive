## Ingredients

- 3 large lemons
- 3 large apples
- 1 pound stoned raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound suet
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 ounce sliced candied citron
- 1 ounce sliced candied orange peel
- 1 ounce sliced candied lemon peel
- 1 teacup brandy
- 2 tablespoons orange marmalade

## Directions

1. Bake apples and remove cores
2. Add citron, orange peel, and lemon peel to the lemons, lemon juice, and sugar
3. Put in the remaining ingredients one by one and mix everything very thoroughly together
4. Put the mincemeat into a stone jar with a closely fitting lid
5. In a fortnight it will be ready for use
