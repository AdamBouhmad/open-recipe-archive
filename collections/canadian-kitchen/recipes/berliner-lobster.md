## Ingredients

- 1 fresh lobster
- Butter size of egg
- Yolks 3 eggs
- 1/2 pt. cream
- 1 small glass sherry
- Salt and paprica to taste

## Directions

1. Cut lobster into small pieces, put in butter, melt quickly in double boiler
2. Beat up yolks eggs with cream
3. Beat slowly while standing in another dish of hot water till creamy, then take off the fire
4. Add salt and paprica to taste and small glass of best sherry
5. Pour over lobster and serve quickly and hot
