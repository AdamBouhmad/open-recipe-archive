## Ingredients

- cherries (5 pounds)
- vinegar (1 quart)
- sugar (2 pounds)
- cinnamon (0.75 ounce)
- cloves (0.5 ounce)
- mace (1 ounce)

## Directions

1. Grind spices and tie in a muslin bag
2. Boil sugar, vinegar and spices together
3. Pour hot over the cherries
