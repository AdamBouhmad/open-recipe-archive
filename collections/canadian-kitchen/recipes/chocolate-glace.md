## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 square chocolate
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cupful milk
- 1.5 cupfuls flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat butter to a cream
2. Gradually work into this 1 cup sugar
3. Add chocolate, melted, and unbeaten eggs
4. Beat vigorously five minutes
5. Stir in milk
6. Stir in flour with which has been mixed 2 teaspoons of baking powder
7. Bake in a buttered, shallow cake-pan for half an hour in a moderate oven
8. Ice first with white icing, flavored with orange juice
9. When this is set, ice with thick chocolate icing
