## Ingredients

- pears
- 4 pounds granulated sugar to each 8 pounds pears
- 2 cups best vinegar
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon whole cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon whole cloves

## Directions

1. Peel pears
2. Put all together in granite kettle and let stand for an hour
3. Place on fire and bring slowly to boil
4. Cook slowly, and when pears are tender remove carefully and place on plates to cool
5. Boil the liquid to a syrup
6. Fill jars with fruit and cover with the boiling syrup and seal while hot
