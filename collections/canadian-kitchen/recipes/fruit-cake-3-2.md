## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 4 tablespoons molasses
- 1/2 teaspoon soda dissolved in it
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup citron cut fine
- 1/2 pound seeded raisins
- 1/2 pound well-washed currants
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cloves and cinnamon
- 2 cups sifted flour
- essence of lemon
- whites of the eggs

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add eggs gradually, then spices, fruit and flour
3. Beat well, add peel and almonds
4. Add the whites of the eggs at the very last
5. Bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour
