## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. flour
- 3.5 lbs. butter
- 3.5 lbs. sugar
- 36 eggs, leaving out twelve whites
- 2 lbs. citron
- 2 lbs. orange
- 2 lbs. lemon
- 4 lbs. almonds mixed with as much sugar
- 4 lbs. raisins
- 3 lbs. currants
- 3 oz. mixed spice
- 1 pt. each brandy, white wine and rose water

## Directions

1. Pound almonds fine, and mix with as much sugar
2. Put in an iron pot and stir constantly over the fire until quite dry
3. Stone raisins, and mince half of them fine
4. Mix batter and add fruits and spices
5. Add wine and egg whites last
6. Bake four hours in a well-buttered and papered pan
7. Sift plenty of flour on top before putting the cake in the oven
