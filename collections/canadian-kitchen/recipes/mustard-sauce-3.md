## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix mustard in vinegar
2. Add eggs, white sugar, butter, and salt
3. Cook in double boiler
