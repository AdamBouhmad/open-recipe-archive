## Ingredients

- 3 cups sugar
- 1 1/2 cups butter
- 6 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups sour cream
- 2 teaspoons saleratus or soda
- 1 pound currants
- 1 1/2 pounds raisins
- 3/4 pound citron peel
- 1 nutmeg
- flour to make stiff

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs thoroughly, then add sugar and butter
2. Beat till smooth
3. Dissolve the soda in a little warm water, put in the cream
4. Make the cake quite thick with flour
5. Cut the raisins in halves and remove seeds
6. When put in pan one layer of cake and one of citron peel
