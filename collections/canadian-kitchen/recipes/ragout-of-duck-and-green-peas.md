## Ingredients

- remnants of a pair of roast ducks
- a quart of green peas

## Directions

1. Cook the remnants of a pair of roast ducks as directed in recipe for Salmi of Duck
2. When done pile the meat in the centre of the dish
3. Put a quart of green peas, well boiled and drained, about them like a green fence
4. Pour the gravy over all
