## Ingredients

- 1 cup melted butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons buttermilk
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 1/2 cups Graham flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- fruit

## Directions

1. Roll thin and cut in squares
2. put pieces together with fruit between
