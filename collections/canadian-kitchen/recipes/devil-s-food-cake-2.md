## Ingredients

- 1 cup chocolate
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ⅜ cup butter
- 3 eggs 1 at a time
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon soda in a little warm water
- 2½ cups flour

## Directions

1. boil and cool
2. Add first part to second part, stirring thoroughly
