## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oats
- 2.5 cups flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 0.25 cup lard
- 0.75 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 lb. dates
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup hot water

## Directions

1. Roll paste out quite thin
2. Spread date filling between two layers
3. Cook together
4. Let cool
