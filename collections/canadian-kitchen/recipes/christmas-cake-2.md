## Ingredients

- 13 1/2 pounds flour
- 1 pound butter
- 12 ounces moist brown sugar
- 3 pounds currants
- 1/2 pound citron peel
- 5 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vinegar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 4 ounces ground almonds

## Directions

1. Warm the butter slightly to make it beat easily, but not oily
2. Put it into a bowl with the sugar and beat both to a cream
3. Sift the flour and mix it in, a tablespoon at a time
4. Add the currants washed and dried, the peel chopped fine, the ground almonds and the soda
5. When all these are mixed, add the yolks beaten to a froth
6. Mix for five minutes
7. Add the whites of the eggs beaten to a froth
8. Add lastly the vinegar
