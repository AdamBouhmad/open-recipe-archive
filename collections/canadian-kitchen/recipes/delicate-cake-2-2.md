## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- ½ cup butter and lard mixed
- ½ cup cornstarch mixed in ½ cup sweet milk
- 1 cup sifted flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- whites of 3 eggs
- flavor with almond

## Directions

1. beat stiff and added last
2. bake in a loaf
