## Ingredients

- 3 cups sifted flour
- 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- 1.5 cups brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sweet milk
- a handful currants
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Sift 3 cups sifted flour with 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
2. Melt a piece of butter the size of an egg
3. Mix 1.5 cups brown sugar, 1 egg (not beaten), 1 cup sweet milk, a handful currants, a little grated nutmeg together
4. Stir into flour
