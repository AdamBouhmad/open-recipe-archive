## Ingredients

- butter
- onions
- soda crackers
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- fine biscuit crumbs

## Directions

1. Butter baking dish of desired size
2. Put in a layer of sliced onions
3. Add a layer of soda crackers broken in small pieces
4. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper and dots of butter
5. Continue this until dish is full
6. Cover with milk
7. Sprinkle with fine biscuit crumbs
8. Bake in a moderate oven 1 hour
