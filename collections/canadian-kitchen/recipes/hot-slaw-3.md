## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon of sugar
- salt and pepper
- 1 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop and cook in water with a little salt some cabbage
2. When done, drain, leaving a little water to prevent scorching
3. Pour over the cabbage this dressing: the yolk of 1 egg, 1 teaspoon of sugar, salt and pepper, and 1 cup vinegar, well beaten together
