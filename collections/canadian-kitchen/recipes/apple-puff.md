## Ingredients

- 6 apples
- yolks of 2 eggs
- tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 pint milk
- vanilla
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- jelly

## Directions

1. Bake 6 apples
2. Let cool
3. Take yolks of 2 eggs, tablespoon cornstarch, 1 pint milk
4. Cook till thick
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Cool
7. Scrape white part of apple into beaten whites of eggs
8. Add 1 cup sugar
9. Beat hour
10. Put custard in dish with a little jelly
11. Cover with beaten apple
