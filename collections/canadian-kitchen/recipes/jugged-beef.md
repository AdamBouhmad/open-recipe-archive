## Ingredients

- 2 pounds thin beef or stewing steak
- 2 onions
- 1 medium-sized carrot
- 1 teaspoon mixed herbs
- 1 small onion stuck with dozen cloves
- Yorkshire relish
- 1 tablespoon flour
- pepper and salt
- water or stock

## Directions

1. Put beef cut into convenient sized pieces into a brown stewing jar
2. Add onions cut in slices
3. Add carrot cut in cubes
4. Add mixed herbs
5. Add small onion stuck with cloves
6. Add a little Yorkshire relish
7. Dust all with flour
8. Season with pepper and salt to taste
9. Do not quite cover with water or stock
10. Put on the lid and set jar in the oven
11. Let it cook for 2 hours
12. Serve with boiled potatoes
