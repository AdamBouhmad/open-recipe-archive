## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 cups flour
- 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4 cup milk

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add beaten egg
3. Add milk and flour and baking powder alternately
4. Bake in buttered gem pans
