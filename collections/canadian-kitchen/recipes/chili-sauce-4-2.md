## Ingredients

- 12 large ripe tomatoes
- 4 apples
- 2 onions
- 2 red peppers
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 3 cups vinegar
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- ginger
- cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop fine
2. Boil till soft
