## Ingredients

- sturgeon
- flour
- egg
- crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- thyme
- Indian pickle
- tomato sauce or piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Cut in slices quarter of an inch thick
2. dry, flour, and egg them
3. dip in crumbs, seasoned with pepper, salt, parsley, and thyme
4. fry them
5. serve with Indian pickle, tomato, or piquant sauce
