## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- a little salt
- 3/4 cup oatmeal mixed in flour
- figs or other fruit

## Directions

1. Make into dough
2. Take half the dough and roll out and put on bottom of pan
3. Spread with figs or other fruit
4. Roll remainder of dough and put on top
5. Cook until done
