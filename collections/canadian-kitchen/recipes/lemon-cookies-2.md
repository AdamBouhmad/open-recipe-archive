## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup lard
- 5c. oil lemon
- 5c. cooking ammonia
- 1 pint sweet milk
- flour to make a stiff dough

## Directions

1. Roll out and cut in medium size.
