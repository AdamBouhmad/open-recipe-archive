## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- ½ cup butter
- milk
- 1 quart strawberries
- 1 pint sugar

## Directions

1. Sift cream tartar and soda into flour
2. Rub in butter
3. Add milk enough to make it roll out about 1 inch thick
4. Bake in oven
5. When baked, cut in square pieces
6. Split the squares and spread with butter
7. Make a filling with strawberries and sugar mashed together
8. Spread the squares with the filling and put together again
