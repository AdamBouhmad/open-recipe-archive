## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups rolled oats
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 cup granulated coconut
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/8 teaspoon soda
- 1 egg
- enough milk to wet nicely

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Drop off spoon in greased bake pan
3. Bake in moderate oven
