## Ingredients

- 10 eggs (yolks)
- a large cupful of maple syrup
- 1 quart of cream

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of ten eggs very light, and add a large cupful of maple syrup.
2. Put it over the fire, stir till it thickens, then take off and beat till cold.
3. Add quickly a quart of cream beaten till it is perfectly stiff.
4. Pour into a two-quart melon mould.
5. Pack in ice and salt for five or six hours.
