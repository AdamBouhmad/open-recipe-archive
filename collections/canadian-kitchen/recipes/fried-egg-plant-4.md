## Ingredients

- one egg-plant
- one egg
- one cup milk
- one-half cup flour
- pepper and salt
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Slice the egg-plant about half an inch thick, peeling the slices
2. Lay them in salt and water for an hour, placing a plate on them to keep them down
3. Wipe each slice dry
4. Dip into batter made of egg, milk, flour, pepper and salt
5. Fry in boiling dripping
6. Drain off all the grease
