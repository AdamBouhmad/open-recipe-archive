## Ingredients

- 2 quarts stewed mash pumpkins
- 1 quart rich milk
- 6 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups light brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- Nutmeg
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. To make 4 large pies, combine all ingredients and bake
