## Ingredients

- 6 ounces sweet almonds blanched and pounded fine
- whites of 2 eggs
- 12 ounces powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound fine 6 ounces sweet almonds
2. Mix in a large bowl the whites of 2 eggs and 12 ounces powdered sugar
3. Beat with a wooden spoon for 5 minutes
4. Drop through pastry tube in pieces about the size of a quarter on sheets of buttered paper, in baking pans
5. Bake in a slow oven until cakes are golden brown
