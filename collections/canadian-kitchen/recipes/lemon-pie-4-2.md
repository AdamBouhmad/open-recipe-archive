## Ingredients

- juice and grated rind of one lemon
- 2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 3 cups boiling water
- whites of 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Cook juice, rind, cornstarch, sugar, and egg yolks with 3 cups boiling water till thick, stirring all the time
2. This makes filling for 2 pies
3. Beat stiff the whites of the 4 eggs with 1 tablespoon sugar
4. Spread evenly over the tops and return to the oven to brown slightly
