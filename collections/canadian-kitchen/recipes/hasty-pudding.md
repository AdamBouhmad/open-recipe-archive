## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 dessertspoon butter
- 2 small teaspoons baking powder
- fruit of any kind

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients with flavoring.
2. Steam with fruit of any kind, either put in the bottom or put on after.
3. Use with cream and sugar.
