## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- butter the size of an egg
- yolk of 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 8 tablespoons milk
- 1 tablespoon heated vinegar
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, mustard, salt, egg yolk, flour and milk
2. Cook until thick
3. Add 1 tablespoon heated vinegar
4. When cold fold in the beaten white of egg
