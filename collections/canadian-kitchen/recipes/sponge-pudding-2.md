## Ingredients

- 1 oz. butter
- 1 oz. sugar
- 1 oz. flour
- 1/2 pt. milk
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Scald the milk, and put the butter, sugar, flour, and yolks of eggs into it.
2. Beat the whites stiff and stir in.
3. Bake in a pan set in another pan of water, for about half an hour.
