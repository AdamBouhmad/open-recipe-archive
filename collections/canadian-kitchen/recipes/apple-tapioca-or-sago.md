## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup pearl or minute tapioca
- cold water to cover
- 4 cups water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 square inches lemon rind
- 7 sour apples
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. soak tapioca 1 hour, drain and add water and salt and lemon rind
2. cook in double boiler until transparent
3. Core and pare apples, arrange in buttered baking dish
4. fill cavities with sugar and bake in moderate oven until apples are soft
5. serve with sugar and cream
