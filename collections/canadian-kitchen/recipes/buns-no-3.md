## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/8 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 pint hot water
- 1 pint sweet milk
- flour
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- warm water
- currants if desired

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over butter, sugar, and salt
2. When butter and sugar are melted, cool by adding 1 pint of sweet milk
3. Add flour enough to make a rather stiff batter
4. Soak White Swan Yeast Cake for 10 minutes in warm water
5. Stir in yeast cake
6. Let it stand in warm place over night
7. In the morning add flour enough to knead into a stiff dough
8. Let it rise and when light roll out an inch thick
9. Spread lightly over the top with butter
10. Cut with small cake cutter
11. When light, bake in a moderate oven
12. A cup of currants may be added if desired
