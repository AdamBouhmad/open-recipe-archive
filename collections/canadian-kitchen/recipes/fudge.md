## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1/4 lb. chocolate
- 1 cup milk
- Butter size of walnut

## Directions

1. Scald the milk, then add the sugar, butter and chocolate broken into small pieces
2. Boil until it sets when tried in cold water
3. Take off the fire, and beat until nearly cold
4. Then turn out on plates and mark into squares
