## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- 1 teaspoon Magic baking powder
- a pinch of salt
- milk
- berries

## Directions

1. Make into a soft batter with milk
2. Put into well-buttered cups a spoonful of batter, then one of berries, then another of batter
3. Steam
