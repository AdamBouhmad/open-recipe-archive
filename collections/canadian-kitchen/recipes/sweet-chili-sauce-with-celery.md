## Ingredients

- 1 pk. ripe tomatoes
- 4 bunches celery
- 3 cups vinegar
- 3 cups brown sugar
- ½ cup salt (small)
- ½ lb. whole mixed spice
- 1 teaspoonful pepper
- 1 teaspoonful mustard

## Directions

1. Peel tomatoes and slice
2. Chop celery fine
3. Add vinegar, brown sugar, salt, mixed spices tied in a bag, pepper and mustard
4. Boil all together one hour or longer
