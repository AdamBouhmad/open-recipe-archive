## Ingredients

- 2 cups cold mashed potatoes
- 4 eggs
- pepper and salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat together until light.
2. Cook in hot buttered frying-pan, stirring constantly.
3. Season with pepper and salt before removing from fire.
