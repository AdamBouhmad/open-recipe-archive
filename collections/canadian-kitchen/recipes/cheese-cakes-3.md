## Ingredients

- 2 cups of curd of sour milk
- 1 cup of currants
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1/2 a nutmeg grated
- cinnamon and cloves, 1/2 teaspoon each
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 2 ounces of lemon peel
- good paste

## Directions

1. Add to curd 1 cup of currants, 1 cup of sugar, 1/2 a nutmeg grated, cinnamon and cloves, 1/2 teaspoon each, 2 eggs well beaten, and 2 ounces of lemon peel
2. Mix all together
3. Line some patty pans with good paste and fill with the curd
4. Bake in a slow oven
