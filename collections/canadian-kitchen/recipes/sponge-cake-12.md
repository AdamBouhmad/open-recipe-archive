## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- Weight of 10 eggs in sugar
- Weight of 9 in flour
- Juice and rind of 1 lemon
- 1 heaping teaspoonful baking powder
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat yolks until very light
2. Add sugar, grated rind and juice of lemon, and well-beaten whites, beating all together fully five minutes
3. Add flour as quickly as possible
4. Pour into large dripping pan lined with buttered paper about two inches in depth
5. Put in well-heated oven at once
6. Don't open oven door for fifteen minutes at least
7. Try cake with a straw and when it comes out clean cake is done
