## Ingredients

- 1 cup extracted honey of best flavor
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3 tablespoons sweet cream or milk
- 1 teaspoon extract of vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil honey, sugar, and cream or milk to soft crack, or until it hardens when dropped into cold water but not too brittle, just so it will form into a soft ball when taken in the fingers
2. Pour into a greased dish, stirring in vanilla just as removed from the stove
