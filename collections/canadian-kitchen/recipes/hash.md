## Ingredients

- any kind of left-over meat
- 1 large onion
- cold potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter or dripping

## Directions

1. Mince meat fine
2. Add large onion
3. Add as many cold potatoes as the meat and onions together
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Fry in either butter or dripping
