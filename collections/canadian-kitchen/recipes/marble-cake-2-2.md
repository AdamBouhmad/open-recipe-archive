## Ingredients

- ¾ cup butter
- 1 cup white sugar
- whites of 3 eggs
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups of flour
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup butter
- yolks of 3 eggs
- ¾ cup molasses
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1¾ teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- red sugar

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar together for light part
2. Cream brown sugar and butter together for dark part
3. By putting red sugar in ½ the light part, 3 colors may be had
