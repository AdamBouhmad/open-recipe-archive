## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cooked cold beef
- 3 cups mashed potatoes
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Pack in granite pudding dish
3. Cook in the oven for 3 1/2 hours
