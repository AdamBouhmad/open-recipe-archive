## Ingredients

- 6 large baking pears
- ½ lb. sugar
- ½ pt. wine
- 8 whole cloves
- ½ lemon
- ½ oz. gelatine

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and cut them into quarters
2. Put them into a shallow dish with sugar, cloves, and water enough to cover them
3. Stew until tender, not broken
4. Take the pears from the liquor and put them in a mould
5. To half a pint of liquor add the gelatine juice, grated rind of lemon and the wine
6. Let these ingredients boil quickly five minutes
7. Strain the warm liquid over the pears and set in a cool place
