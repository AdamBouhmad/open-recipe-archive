## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 1/2 cups buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3 cups Graham flour
- white flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1/2 cup sugar and 1 egg together
2. Add 2 1/2 cups buttermilk, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon soda, 3 cups Graham flour and enough white flour to make a stiff batter
3. Have ready 4 one-pound baking powder cans, well greased, lids and all
4. Fill the cans half-full of Graham batter
5. Put on lids and steam 2 1/2 hours
6. Remove from steamer and set in oven for 15 minutes with lids off
7. Turn upside down and with a little shaking turn out on a board
