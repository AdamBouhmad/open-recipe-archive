## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1 small tablespoon butter
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk or cream
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat butter and egg together
2. Add sugar
3. Add milk or cream
4. Mix in soda, cream of tartar, and salt
5. Sift with flour enough to make cake stiff
6. Add any flavoring desired or none at all
7. Bake until done
