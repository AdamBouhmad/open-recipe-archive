## Ingredients

- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 3/2 head of cabbage
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Bring vinegar and butter to a boil
2. Mix mustard, brown sugar, egg, and sweet milk thoroughly, and stir these in with vinegar and butter until it boils
3. Chop 3/2 head of cabbage, season with pepper and salt, put it into the dressing and let it come to a boil
