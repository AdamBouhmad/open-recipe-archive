## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. grated breadcrumbs
- 1 lb. currants
- 1/2 lb. suet
- 1/2 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. sultanas
- 1 large tablespoonful treacle
- Grated rind of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix well together with a little milk, and boil in a basin, or mould, for an hour and a half.
