## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup milk
- Whites of 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Rind of 1 orange, grated

## Directions

1. Flavor the cake with the rind of an orange grated.
2. Bake in shallow long pan, pour icing over cake without removing from the oven; when the cake is quite done let it harden.
3. Use yolks for icing with one cup sugar and a teaspoon lemon extract.
