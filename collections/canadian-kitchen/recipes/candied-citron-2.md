## Ingredients

- citron
- alum
- sugar (3/4 pound to 1 pound citron)
- ginger root
- lemon (1)
- citrons (2 medium-sized)

## Directions

1. Boil citron in water with a small piece of alum until tender and clear
2. Make a syrup, allowing 3/4 pound sugar to 1 pound citron
3. Boil a piece of ginger root in the syrup
4. Put the citron in and boil for a few minutes
5. Put in 1 lemon with 2 medium-sized citrons
