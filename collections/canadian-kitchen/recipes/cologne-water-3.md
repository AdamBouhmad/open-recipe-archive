## Ingredients

- Oil of lavender, two drachms
- Oil of rosemary, one drachm and a half
- Oil of orange, one drachm
- Oil of lemon, one drachm
- Oil of bergamot, one drachm
- Essence of musk, two drachms
- Attar of rose, ten drops
- Proof spirit, one pint

## Directions

1. Shake all together thoroughly three times a day for a week
