## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- bread

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes and cut a piece off the top, and remove a little of the pulp.
2. Put a piece of butter or a few drops of oil in each one; dust with salt and pepper, replace the top, sprinkle it with crumbs, pepper, and salt.
3. Put a small piece of butter or a little oil on each one, and place on a slice of bread.
4. Bake in oven fifteen to twenty minutes.
