## Ingredients

- parsnips
- butter
- fresh drippings
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape, splitting largest ones
2. Cut crosswise in ¼-inch slices
3. Have spider ready with butter or fresh drippings
4. Put parsnips in
5. Add salt and pepper to taste
6. Cover and fry slowly 25 or 30 minutes
7. Turn with cake turner or broad knife to prevent sticking
