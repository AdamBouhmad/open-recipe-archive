## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs (save white of one)
- 1/2 cup syrup
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon spice

## Directions

1. Mix butter and brown sugar
2. Add eggs and syrup
3. Add buttermilk with soda dissolved in it
4. Add flour and spice
5. Bake in jelly tins
6. Cool and frost with icing
