## Ingredients

- grapes
- 1 pint granulated sugar per quart of juice
- 1 cup vinegar per quart of juice
- 2 teaspoons salt per quart of juice
- 1 tablespoon whole cloves per quart of juice
- 1 grated nutmeg per quart of juice
- 1 teaspoon ground allspice per quart of juice
- 1 tablespoon powdered cinnamon per quart of juice

## Directions

1. Cook grapes in a double boiler until the skins burst
2. Carefully strain off every particle of juice and return to the fire
3. Add to each quart of juice: 1 pint granulated sugar, 1 cup vinegar, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 tablespoon whole cloves, a grated nutmeg, 1 teaspoon ground allspice, 1 tablespoon powdered cinnamon
4. Boil slowly about 1 hour, and seal
