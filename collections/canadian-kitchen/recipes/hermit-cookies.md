## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 large cup sugar
- 3/4 cup shortening
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 cups oatmeal
- 1 cup chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Drop in tins and bake.
