## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 2 pounds raisins chopped fine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup butter, 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup molasses, 1 cup sweet milk, 3 cups flour, 4 eggs, 2 pounds raisins chopped fine, and nutmeg
2. Bake in a slow oven
