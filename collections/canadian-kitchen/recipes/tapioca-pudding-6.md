## Ingredients

- 1 quart hot milk
- 2 heaping tablespoons tapioca
- a little salt
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- whites of 2 eggs
- any flavoring

## Directions

1. Cook in double boiler for 15 minutes, hot milk, tapioca, and salt, stirring often
2. Beat yolks of eggs and sugar
3. At the end of 15 minutes stir into milk and tapioca
4. Let cook till it thickens like custard
5. Pour into dish
6. Whip in the whites, beaten till no whites are seen
7. Add any flavoring
8. This is delicious if poured over, when cold, any fresh fruit such as strawberries, raspberries, peaches or oranges
