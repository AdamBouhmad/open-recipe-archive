## Ingredients

- currants (4 quarts)
- vinegar (1 pint)
- sugar (3 pounds)
- cinnamon (1 tablespoon)
- allspice (1 tablespoon)
- cloves (1 tablespoon)
- nutmeg (1 tablespoon)

## Directions

1. Cook for 1 hour
2. Keep in a cool place tightly covered
