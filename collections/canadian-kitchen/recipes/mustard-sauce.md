## Ingredients

- 12 ripe tomatoes
- 12 onions
- 12 apples
- salt
- 3/4 pound best mustard
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 cups

## Directions

1. Cook well
2. Put through colander
3. Add mustard and vinegar
