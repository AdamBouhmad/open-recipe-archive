## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 2 large potatoes
- Pepper and salt
- A good sized piece of butter
- 1 quart of milk

## Directions

1. Chop carrot, onion and potatoes fine
2. Boil, and put through a colander
3. Add pepper and salt to taste
4. Add a good sized piece of butter, and one quart of milk
5. Let come to a boil and serve
