## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of cold salmon
- 2 eggs beaten light
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter (melted but not hot)
- 1 tablespoonful of fine bread crumbs
- Seasoning of salt
- Pepper and minced parsley

## Directions

1. Chop the fish fine
2. Rub it in a mortar or bowl with the back of a silver spoon, adding the butter until it is a smooth paste
3. Beat the breadcrumbs into the eggs
4. Season before working all together
5. Put it into a buttered pudding-mould
6. Steam or boil for half an hour
