## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup washed currants
- white icing sugar

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar, butter, milk, flour, and baking powder
2. Dredge currants with flour and add
3. Bake
4. When cooked, ice over the top with white icing sugar
