## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup cocoanut
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat sugar to a cream with the yolks
2. Add sweet milk, 3/4 cup sugar, cocoanut, cornstarch, and salt
3. Bake in crust
