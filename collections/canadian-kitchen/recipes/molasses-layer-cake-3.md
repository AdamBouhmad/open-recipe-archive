## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- scant 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup seeded and chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Save whites of 2 eggs for frosting
2. Put 1 cup sugar on the stove to boil
3. Beat the whites of eggs and stir with the raisins in the syrup for filling
