## Ingredients

- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup currants and raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour
- 1 nutmeg grated

## Directions

1. Mix fruit and soda with flour, 1 nutmeg grated
2. This makes a nice pudding if baked first, then steamed
3. Serve with sauce
