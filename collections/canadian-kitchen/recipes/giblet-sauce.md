## Ingredients

- giblets
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the giblets until tender.
2. Chop them, but not very fine.
3. Add a tablespoonful of flour to the pan in which the chicken was roasted.
4. Let it brown, stirring constantly.
5. Add slowly a cupful of water in which the giblets were boiled.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Strain and add the chopped giblets.
8. Serve in a sauceboat.
9. The liver is a tidbit, and should be roasted and served with the chicken, instead of being used in the sauce.
