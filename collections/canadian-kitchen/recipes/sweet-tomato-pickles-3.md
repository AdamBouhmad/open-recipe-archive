## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 6 large onions
- salt
- 3 1/2 gallon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 3 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Slice green tomatoes and onions, sprinkle with salt and let stand over night
2. Drain
3. Cook in 3 1/2 gallon vinegar, 2 tablespoons cinnamon, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 tablespoon cloves, 1 tablespoon ginger, 3 pounds sugar
