## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened apple sauce
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup melted butter and lard
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ginger or cinnamon to taste

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 teaspoon soda in apple sauce
2. Mix all ingredients together
3. Bake in slow oven
