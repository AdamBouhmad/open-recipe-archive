## Ingredients

- celery
- 1 sweet apple
- white Malaga grapes
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon mustard
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut celery in dice
2. Pare 1 sweet apple, cut in dice
3. Take a bunch of white Malaga grapes cut in two
4. Make a dressing of 2 eggs, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup vinegar, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1/2 tablespoon mustard, salt and a piece of butter
5. Boil until thick, pour over the above
