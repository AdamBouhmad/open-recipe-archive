## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- a little salt
- a little milk or water
- chicken broth
- pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour, eggs and salt all together and make very stiff with flour
2. If 4 eggs does not wet the flour enough, add a little milk (sweet) or water, so as to mix like pie dough, only very stiff
3. Roll in a sheet or two, let lay 1 or 2 hours
4. Cut in long strips 2 inches wide, put 2 or 3 on top of each other and use a very sharp knife to cut in thin strips about 1/8 inch or less wide
5. Have chicken broth boiling hot, drop by handsful in kettle and stir with other hand
6. Let it boil 1/2 hour, add a little pepper and 1 tablespoon butter
