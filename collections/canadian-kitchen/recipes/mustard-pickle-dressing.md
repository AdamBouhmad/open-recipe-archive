## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- 1 small teaspoon turmeric
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- 1.5 cups brown sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 quarts of pickles

## Directions

1. Mix flour, mustard, turmeric, celery seed, brown sugar, and vinegar together to a smooth paste
2. Stir in pickles
3. Let boil for 10 minutes
4. Bottle while hot
