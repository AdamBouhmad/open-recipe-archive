## Ingredients

- 2 ounces fresh butter
- 2 ounces sifted sugar
- 2 ounces flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 2 tablespoons raspberry jam

## Directions

1. Beat butter to a cream.
2. Add sifted sugar.
3. Gradually stir in flour and baking powder.
4. Add well-beaten eggs and raspberry jam.
5. Beat the whole thoroughly together.
6. Put in a buttered mould and steam 2 hours.
