## Ingredients

- 1 small cabbage
- pepper
- salt
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 3 well-beaten eggs
- ¼ cup thin cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil a small cabbage until tender
2. Drain and let stand until quite cold
3. Chop fine and season with pepper and salt
4. Add melted butter, eggs and cream
5. Melt butter in a frying-pan
6. Turn in the mixture
7. Stir until nicely browned
8. Turn out on a hot dish
9. Garnish with parsley
