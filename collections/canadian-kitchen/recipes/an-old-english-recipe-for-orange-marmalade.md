## Ingredients

- 12 bitter or Seville oranges
- 4 lemons
- 12 pounds granulated sugar
- 6 quarts water

## Directions

1. On a platter cut oranges and lemons into quarters
2. Take out pith and pips into bowl and cover with water
3. Slice or shred thin as possible oranges and lemons
4. Put into a vessel with remainder of water
5. Allow it to stand 24 or 36 hours
6. Boil 2 hours, but first strain water off the pips into the whole
7. Have sugar warmed before adding
8. Boil 1/4 an hour, or until it jellies
