## Ingredients

- milk
- eggs
- bread
- butter
- flour
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Put as much milk in a saucepan as will just cover the number of eggs needed.
2. When on the point of boiling, drop in the eggs and let cook about 5 minutes.
3. Take out and put each egg on a slice of freshly made toast, lightly buttered.
4. Thicken the milk with a little flour, add pepper and salt.
5. Pour over toast and serve hot.
