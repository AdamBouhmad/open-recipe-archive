## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cocoanut
- 1/2 cup crackers
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- whites of 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Combine 1 pint milk, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 2 tablespoons cocoanut, 1/2 cup crackers, and 1 teaspoon lemon extract
2. Bake 1.5 hours
3. For frosting, beat whites of 2 eggs with 3/4 cup sugar, put in oven and brown
