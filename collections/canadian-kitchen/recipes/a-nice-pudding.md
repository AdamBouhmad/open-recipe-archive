## Ingredients

- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 2 cups suet
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- sufficient milk to make all nice and soft
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. mix bread crumbs, suet, sugar, eggs, and sufficient milk to make all nice and soft
2. season with cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg
3. Bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour or more
