## Ingredients

- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 pound granulated sugar
- 1 ounce tartaric acid
- 2 eggs
- soda

## Directions

1. Put 1 quart boiling water, 1 pound granulated sugar, 1 ounce tartaric acid together and boil 20 minutes
2. Remove from stove and pour over the beaten whites of 2 eggs
3. Let cool and flavor
4. Put in jar and keep in cool place
5. 2 tablespoons to 3/4 cup of water and a pinch of soda makes a refreshing drink
