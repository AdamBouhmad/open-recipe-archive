## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of grape fruit or oranges
- apples
- celery
- bananas
- cream dressing
- tender lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Peel the grape fruit or oranges, carefully removing all the bitter white skin
2. Cut the pulp with bananas and apples into small dice
3. Cut the celery fine as for other salads
4. Put the orange and apple together; the latter will absorb the juice of the orange
5. Set all on ice
6. When it is time to serve mix the fruit and celery together
7. Put into a salad bowl
8. Cover with cream dressing into which has been stirred a third as much cream as there is dressing
9. Add a little more salt to it in mixing
10. Serve in a bed of tender lettuce leaves
