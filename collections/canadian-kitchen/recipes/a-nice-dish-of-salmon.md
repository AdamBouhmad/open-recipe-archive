## Ingredients

- 2 cans red salmon
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 1 large spoonful flour
- pepper
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 or 4 cold boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Drop unopened cans of salmon in boiling water and boil 20 minutes
2. Put a pint of sweet milk in a double boiler
3. Mix a large spoonful of flour in a little cold milk and season with pepper and salt
4. When the milk boils add a tablespoon of butter, then the flour
5. Cook till thick and smooth
6. Peel cold boiled eggs and cut in thin slices
7. Turn out the fish on a large dish and spread out
8. Lay the sliced eggs over the fish
9. Put the sauce over all
