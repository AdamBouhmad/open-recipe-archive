## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 6 large tomatoes
- 3 large potatoes
- 1 pint sweet corn
- 1/2 pint shell beans, green
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put the chicken over and cook till nearly done
2. Add tomatoes and potatoes and beans
3. Let cook until about 15 minutes before taking up
4. Then add corn, salt and pepper to taste
5. The stew should cook 3 hours altogether
