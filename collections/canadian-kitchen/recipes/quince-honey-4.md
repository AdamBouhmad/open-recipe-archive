## Ingredients

- 5 quinces
- 2 apples
- 1 quart of water
- 5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Grate them or put through your finest meat chopper
2. Have on the stove 1 quart of water and 5 pounds sugar melted
3. Add quinces and apples and boil 30 minutes, stirring that it does not burn
