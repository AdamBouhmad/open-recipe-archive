## Ingredients

- cooked vegetables
- capers
- pickled gherkins
- olives
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut all sorts of cooked vegetables neatly in small dice, balls, cubes, in equal proportion
2. Add some capers and pickled gherkins cut into shreds
3. Use olives for garnish
4. Mix three parts of oil to one of vinegar, salt and pepper as desired
5. Toss vegetables in this dressing
6. Pile on a china stand and garnish with shreds of any cold game, fowl, smoked salmon, lobster coral, anchovy, olives, hard-boiled eggs, parsley, lettuce, or celery tops, with fancy stars of beetroot
