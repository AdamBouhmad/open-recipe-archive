## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup cornstarch
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup sour cream
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in warm water
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 1 tablespoon cloves ground
- 1 tablespoon nutmeg
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 2 cups flour or enough to make a thick batter

## Directions

1. For white part: mix whites of 3 eggs, cornstarch, sweet milk, butter, granulated sugar, flour, and baking powder
2. Bake in 2 layers
3. For dark part: mix yolks of 3 eggs, brown sugar, sour cream, butter, soda dissolved in warm water, molasses, cloves, nutmeg, and raisins
4. Add flour to make a thick batter
5. Bake in 2 layers
