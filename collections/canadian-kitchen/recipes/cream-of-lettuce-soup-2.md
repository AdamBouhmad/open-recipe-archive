## Ingredients

- 2 heads of lettuce
- 1 quart of good stock
- 2 tablespoonfuls of white roux
- 1 tablespoonful of cold boiled onion, minced fine
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- 1 cup of milk
- Pepper and salt
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Shred finely two heads of lettuce — the greener the better.
2. Cook for half an hour in a quart of good stock, rub through a colander.
3. Return to the fire; stir into a cup of this two tablespoonfuls of white roux and a tablespoonful of cold boiled onion, minced fine, and one of minced parsley.
4. Heat a cup of milk in another vessel, season with pepper and salt, stir in a well-whipped egg, and pour this mixture into the tureen, adding finally the lettuce soup.
