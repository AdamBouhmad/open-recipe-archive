## Ingredients

- Whites of 3 eggs beaten stiff
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2½ cups grated cocoanut
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Boil whites of eggs and sugar 3 minutes in double boiler
2. Add grated cocoanut and cornstarch mixed together
3. Drop on buttered paper and brown in oven
