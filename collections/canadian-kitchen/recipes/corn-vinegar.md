## Ingredients

- 1 pint of corn
- 1 pint of brown sugar or molasses
- 1 gallon of rain water

## Directions

1. Cut off the cob 1 pint of corn
2. Take 1 pint of brown sugar or molasses to a gallon of rain water
3. Add corn
4. Put into a jar and cover with cloth
5. Set in sun
6. In 3 weeks will be good vinegar
