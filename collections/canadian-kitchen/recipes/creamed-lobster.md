## Ingredients

- Meat of 1 lobster chopped fine
- 1/2 pt. whipped cream
- 1/2 oz. gelatine
- A little anchovy paste
- cayenne and salt

## Directions

1. Take the meat lobster, chop it fine, add whipped cream, gelatine which has been soaked in water, a little anchovy sauce, salt and cayenne
2. Stir it gently till nearly set, and pour into a slightly oiled border mould
3. When turned out, fill the centre with mayonnaise, garnish with mashed green peas put through a forcepipe, and the claws round the outside
