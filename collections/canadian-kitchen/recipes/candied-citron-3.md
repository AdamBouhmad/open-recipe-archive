## Ingredients

- citron
- white sugar (1 pound to each pound of citron)
- sugar (10 pounds to 1 pint water)
- lemons (2)
- root ginger (2 ounces)

## Directions

1. Pare the citron and cut into slices about 1.5 inches thick, then into strips
2. Take out the seeds and weigh
3. Make a syrup by allowing 10 pounds of sugar to 1 pint water
4. Simmer gently for 20 minutes
5. Put in citron and boil for 1 hour till tender
6. Shortly before taking off the fire put in 2 lemons sliced thin, and 2 ounces root ginger
7. Don't stir while boiling
