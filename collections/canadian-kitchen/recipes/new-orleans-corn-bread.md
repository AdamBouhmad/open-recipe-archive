## Ingredients

- 1½ pts. cornmeal
- ½ pt. flour
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1½ pts. milk
- 1 tablespoonful lard

## Directions

1. Sift together cornmeal, flour, sugar, salt and powder
2. Rub in lard, cold
3. Add eggs (beaten), and the milk
4. Mix into a moderately stiff batter
5. Pour from bowl into a shallow cake-pan
6. Bake in rather hot oven thirty minutes
