## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 1 cup salt
- 1 tablespoon pepper
- cayenne pepper
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 quart good vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice tomatoes
2. Strain in salt in alternate layers of tomatoes
3. Let stand over night
4. Drain off all juice
5. Chop fine
6. Add pepper, cayenne pepper, sugar, and vinegar
7. Bottle for use
8. Strain if desired
