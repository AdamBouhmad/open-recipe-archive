## Ingredients

- 12 clams
- 1 pint milk
- 3 eggs
- clam liquor
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Chop or leave clams whole
2. Add clam liquor, salt and pepper to milk and eggs
3. Add flour enough for a thin batter
4. Fry in hot lard
