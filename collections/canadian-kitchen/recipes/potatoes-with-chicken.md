## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot mashed potatoes
- butter, size of an egg
- 1.5 cups hot milk
- beaten yolks of 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- minced celery
- beaten whites of 2 eggs
- stewed chicken

## Directions

1. Use 2 cups hot mashed potatoes, butter size of an egg, 1.5 cups hot milk, beaten yolks of 2 eggs, salt and pepper to season, and a little minced celery
2. Beat till all are thoroughly mixed
3. Fold in the beaten whites of eggs
4. Pour in a border mould
5. Bake in a hot oven
6. Turn out on a hot dish, and fill centre with stewed chicken
