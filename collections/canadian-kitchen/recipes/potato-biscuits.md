## Ingredients

- 6 tumblers flour
- 2.5 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tumblers mashed potatoes
- little salt
- butter or lard
- milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, 2.5 teaspoons baking powder, 2 tumblers mashed potatoes, little salt, butter or lard same as for biscuits
2. Mix with milk
3. Bake in hot oven
4. Eat warm
