## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup cold water
- 3/4 cup lard
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon, cloves, allspice

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop in buttered pan
3. Bake
