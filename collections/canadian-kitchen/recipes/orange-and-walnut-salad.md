## Ingredients

- Four peeled oranges
- Three or four tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- One tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- Lettuce leaves
- One cupful of sliced nut meats
- One tablespoonful of oil
- A dash of salt
- Half a teaspoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Slice oranges lengthwise, dress with three or four tablespoonfuls of olive oil and one tablespoonful of lemon-juice.
2. Arrange slices in a mound upon a layer of lettuce leaves.
3. Dress one cupful of sliced nut meats with one tablespoonful of oil, a dash of salt and half a teaspoonful of lemon juice, and dispose upon the centre of mound.
4. Mix together before serving.
