## Ingredients

- 12 ounces shredded almonds
- 4 ounces ground almonds
- 4 ounces cornstarch
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- grated rind of 2 oranges

## Directions

1. Pass sugar and cornstarch through sieve twice
2. Add almonds, rind and eggs beaten light, without separating
3. With buttered hands shape mixture into balls the size of walnuts
4. Bake in slow oven on tins covered with waxed or oiled paper
