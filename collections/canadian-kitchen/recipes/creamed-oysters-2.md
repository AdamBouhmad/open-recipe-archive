## Ingredients

- 1 cup thick cream sauce
- salt
- black pepper
- cayenne
- celery salt
- 1 pint oysters
- toast

## Directions

1. Make 1 cup of thick cream sauce and season with salt, black pepper, cayenne and celery salt
2. Wash and pick over 1 pint oysters
3. Parboil until plump
4. Skim carefully, drain and add them to sauce
5. Serve on toast and garnish dish with points of toast
