## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 cups finely chopped potatoes
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir milk, egg, salt, pepper, and potatoes together
2. Heat deep baking dish hot and butter well
3. Pour in the mixture
4. Sprinkle with flour and bits of butter
5. Bake in a hot oven until nicely browned
