## Ingredients

- veal or leaf steak
- pepper
- salt
- bacon
- string
- flour
- butter
- dripping
- water

## Directions

1. Cut up veal, or leaf steak, in pieces about 2 inches square, and pound each piece until it is quite flat.
2. Dust all with pepper and salt, and lay on each piece a strip of bacon, roll it up and tie with string.
3. Dip each piece in flour and brown in a mixture of butter and dripping.
4. When birds have become brown pour on enough water to cover them.
5. Let them simmer gently about 2 hours.
6. Thicken gravy with a little flour and serve hot.
