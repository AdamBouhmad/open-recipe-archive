## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- pinch salt
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat the egg just enough to mix it thoroughly
2. Stir in the sugar and salt
3. Add milk and flavoring
4. Stir until the sugar is dissolved
5. Turn into a baking dish
6. Set the baking dish in a pan and pour boiling water around it
7. Bake in a hot oven until the point of a knife will come out of the centre with nothing on it
