## Ingredients

- 4 eggs, keep out 1 white
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sifted flour
- 1 teaspoonful vinegar
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking-powder
- 2 tablespoonfuls cold water
- 1 cup icing sugar
- juice of lemon or essence

## Directions

1. Mix eggs and sugar together
2. put baking powder in flour
3. beat well
4. make icing with white of one egg; one cup icing sugar, juice of lemon or essence
