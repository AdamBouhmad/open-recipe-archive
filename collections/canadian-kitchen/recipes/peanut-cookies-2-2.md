## Ingredients

- 1 pint peanuts chopped fine
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 tablespoons milk
- a pinch of salt
- flour to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar, add the beaten eggs, nuts and other ingredients, and enough flour to make a soft dough.
2. Roll and cut in circles.
