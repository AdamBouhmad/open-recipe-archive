## Ingredients

- 1 lb. raw chicken
- 3 oz. butter
- 4 eggs
- 1.5 pts. double cream
- pepper and salt to taste
- good white sauce made with butter, flour, milk, good chicken stock and a little cream

## Directions

1. Pound meat in a mortar (or pass twice through fine mincer), add gradually three ounces butter, four yolks and two whites of eggs.
2. Season to taste and then pass through a hair sieve.
3. Whip the remaining two whites of eggs to a stiff froth.
4. Half whip the cream and stir these in very gently to the chicken mixture.
5. Steam very gently in mould for three-quarters of an hour.
6. Turn out and serve with good white sauce made with butter, flour, milk, good chicken stock and a little cream.
