## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- one gill of best Lucca oil
- two tablespoons vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Stir yolks and salt in bowl with wooden or silver spoon
2. Drop in oil and vinegar alternately in small quantities
3. Always stir vigorously
4. Take half an hour to it
