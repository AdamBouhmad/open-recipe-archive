## Ingredients

- Walnut bark, one ounce
- Water, one pint
- Alum, a lump the size of a filbert

## Directions

1. Boil an ounce of walnut bark in a pint of water for an hour
2. Add a lump of alum the size of a filbert
3. When cold apply with a camel's hair brush
