## Ingredients

- 1/2 box gelatine
- 6 small bananas
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 small cupful preserved ginger, chopped
- 1 cupful walnuts, chopped fine
- 1 pt. milk
- 1/2 pt. whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine for fifteen minutes in a cupful of cold milk.
2. Mash the bananas till smooth.
3. Mix in the chopped ginger and walnuts, add the sugar.
4. Pour the milk (boiling) on the gelatine, stir till dissolved, to which add the bananas, ginger, and walnuts.
5. Set in a bowl in a can of crushed ice or cold water.
6. Stir occasionally till the mixture begins to grow firm, then very gently fold in the whipped cream and pour into a mould which has been rubbed inside with sweet oil.
7. When firm turn out and serve with whipped cream.
