## Ingredients

- 1 dozen bitter oranges
- About 20 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. First day: Chip off the yellow rind of the oranges, using a slicer
2. Peel off the tough white skin with the fingers, and put this with skin through a mincer or chop it up very finely and mix with yellow rind
3. Measure this into pints, packing loosely, and for every pint add 2 3/4 pints cold water
4. Put into preserving kettle and let stand at least 12 hours, or until next morning
5. If there is much juice in the pulp of the oranges, strain it out and add without measuring
6. Second day: Boil 1 hour after it reaches the boiling point and set aside for another 12 hours, or until next day
7. Third day: Measure again into pints, and for every pint add pounds sugar
8. Boil for 1 hour, put in jars and wax or seal up
