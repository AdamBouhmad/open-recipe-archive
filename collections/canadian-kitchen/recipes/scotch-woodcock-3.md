## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- toast or crackers

## Directions

1. Melt butter and add flour, cook till smooth and thick.
2. Add 1 cup milk, 3/4 teaspoon salt, pepper to suit taste.
3. Add 4 hard-boiled eggs chopped fine.
4. Reheat and serve on toast or crackers.
5. If after eggs are boiled hard they are put in cold water, they will not turn dark.
