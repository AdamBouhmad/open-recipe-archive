## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon lard
- pinch of salt
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, 3 teaspoons baking powder, 1 tablespoon lard, and pinch of salt well
2. Add sweet milk enough to make a soft dough
3. Bake in a hot oven
