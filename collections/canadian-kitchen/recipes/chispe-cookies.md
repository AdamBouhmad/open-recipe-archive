## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 small teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup sweet cream
- butter size of an egg
- flour enough to mix in a soft dough
- granulated sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in a soft dough
2. Roll to whatever thickness you want and sprinkle with granulated sugar
3. Roll once and cut out
4. Bake in a hot oven
