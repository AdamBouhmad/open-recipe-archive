## Ingredients

- 1 cup maple syrup
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- nuts

## Directions

1. Cook until a little dropped in cold water will thicken
2. Remove from fire and when nearly cold, stir in a few nuts
3. Pour into a square pan and cut in squares
