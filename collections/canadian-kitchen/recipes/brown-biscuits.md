## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 generous tablespoons butter or lard
- 1 cup of fine bran
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift 2 cups flour, 3 tablespoons baking powder and 1 teaspoon salt into a mixing bowl
2. Rub in thoroughly 2 generous tablespoons butter or lard
3. Add 1 cup of fine bran
4. Wet with sweet milk
5. Roll to 1/2 inch in thickness
6. Cut as tea biscuits
7. Bake in a quick oven
