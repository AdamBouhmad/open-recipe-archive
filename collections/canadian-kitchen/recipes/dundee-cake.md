## Ingredients

- 5 ounces butter
- 5 ounces white sugar
- 10 ounces flour
- 2 ounces almond nuts
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 5 ounces sultanas
- 5 ounces currants

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add yolks of eggs not beaten
3. Add flour and milk alternately
4. Add fruit, baking powder and lastly whites of eggs beaten stiff
5. Bake 1 1/2 hours
