## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1/4 cup corn syrup
- 1/4 cup cold water
- the beaten whites of 2 eggs
- vanilla
- nut meats

## Directions

1. Put sugar, corn syrup, and cold water into a saucepan and boil until it will harden in water
2. Have ready the beaten whites of 2 eggs
3. When the syrup has boiled sufficiently beat it into the whites of the eggs and add vanilla
4. Add nut meats just before turning out in the pan
5. This candy is better when a couple of days old than when first made
