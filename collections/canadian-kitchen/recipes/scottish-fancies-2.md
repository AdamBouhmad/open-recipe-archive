## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/2 cups rolled oats

## Directions

1. Beat 1 egg till very light and lemon colored using egg beater
2. Add 1 cup granulated sugar, 1/2 tablespoon melted butter, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 3/4 teaspoon vanilla, 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
3. Stir until well mixed together and drop from spoon and shape like macaroons, on a buttered pan
4. Bake in a moderate oven till delicately browned
