## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 2 cups scalded milk
- 3 yolks eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 5 lady fingers
- 6 macaroons
- angelica
- candied cherries
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Make custard with scalded milk, egg yolks, sugar, and salt
2. Add gelatine soaked in cold water
3. Strain and let thicken slightly
4. Add brandy and vanilla flavoring
5. Place mould in ice water and decorate bottom with angelica and cherries
6. Cover with mixture
7. When firm, add layer of lady fingers soaked in the gelatine mixture, then layer of macaroons
8. Repeat until mould is full
9. Serve with cream sauce and garnish with candied cherries
