## Ingredients

- 1 peck tomatoes
- 3/4 cup salt
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 6 onions
- 3 green peppers
- 5 cups vinegar
- 1 teaspoon cassia
- cloves
- allspice

## Directions

1. Chop all ingredients fine
2. Cook well
