## Ingredients

- one cupful of breadcrumbs
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- chopped parsley
- onion-juice
- one minced onion
- one cupful of soaked bread
- one-half cupful of stock
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one-half teaspoonful of pepper
- one-half teaspoonful of thyme
- one-half cupful of celery

## Directions

1. Moisten a cupful of breadcrumbs with a tablespoonful of melted butter.
2. Season highly with salt, pepper, thyme, chopped parsley, and onion-juice.
3. Alternatively, put in a saucepan a tablespoonful of butter and fry in it one minced onion.
4. Add one cupful of soaked bread, the water being pressed out, one-half cupful of stock, one teaspoonful of salt, one-half teaspoonful each of pepper and thyme, and one-half cupful of celery cut into small pieces.
5. Stir it until it leaves the sides of the pan.
