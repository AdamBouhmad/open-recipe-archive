## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely chopped suet
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 3/4 teaspoon each cloves, cinnamon and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix finely chopped suet, molasses, sour milk, soda, chopped raisins, flour, cloves, cinnamon and nutmeg
2. Steam 2 hours
