## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 cup sugar
- a little salt
- 1 quart of milk
- vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Save the whites of 3 eggs for top
2. Beat together 2 tablespoons cornstarch and 1 cup sugar
3. Add yolks of eggs and a little salt
4. Take 1 quart of milk and let come to a boil
5. Stir in sugar, cornstarch, and other ingredients
6. Cool
7. Put in vanilla to taste
8. Beat the whites of eggs
9. Cook the beaten whites on boiling water
