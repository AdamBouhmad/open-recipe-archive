## Ingredients

- 1 qt. milk
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 teaspoonful flour
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Slices toasted bread

## Directions

1. Put milk over the fire, put into it cold butter
2. Stir a heaping teaspoonful of flour into half a gill of milk
3. As soon as the milk on the fire boils, stir in the flour, add salt
4. Let all boil up once, remove from the fire, and dip in thin slices of toasted bread
5. When all are used up, pour what is left of the scalded milk over the toast
6. Cover, and send to the table hot
