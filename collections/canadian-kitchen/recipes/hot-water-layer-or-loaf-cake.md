## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- heaping teaspoon butter
- 3/4 cup boiling water
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Stir sugar, 1 cup flour, and heaping teaspoon butter all together
2. Add 3/4 cup boiling water
3. Add 2 beaten eggs
4. Add 1/4 cup flour and 1 teaspoon baking powder
5. Bake
