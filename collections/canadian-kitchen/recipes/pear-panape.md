## Ingredients

- 7 pounds pears
- 6 pounds sugar
- 2 oranges
- 2 lemons
- 2 ounces preserved ginger

## Directions

1. Cut pears in 8 pieces
2. Cut rinds of orange and lemon fine
3. Squeeze pulp
4. Put altogether over slow fire, using no water
5. Cook until well preserved
