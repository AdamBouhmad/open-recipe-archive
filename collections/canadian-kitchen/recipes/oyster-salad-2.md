## Ingredients

- 50 small oysters
- 3 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoonful pepper
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt
- 1 pint chopped celery
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- Olives
- Celery tips

## Directions

1. Let oysters come to a boil (no more) in their liquor. Skim and strain.
2. Season the oysters with vinegar, oil, pepper, salt, and place on ice for two hours.
3. Cut finely the tender part of celery.
4. When ready to serve mix this with the oysters and mayonnaise dressing.
5. Arrange in bowl, garnish with olives and celery tips.
