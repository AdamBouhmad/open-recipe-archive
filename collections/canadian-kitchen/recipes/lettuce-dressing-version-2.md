## Ingredients

- 1 small cup vinegar
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- pinch cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. place 1 small cup vinegar on stove to boil
2. when boiling add following mixture: 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1 teaspoon mustard, 1 tablespoon flour, teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon butter, pinch cayenne pepper
