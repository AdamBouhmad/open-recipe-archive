## Ingredients

- pastry
- cooked bacon or ham
- 1 or more eggs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put a layer of pastry in a soup plate
2. Put small pieces of cooked bacon or ham in
3. Beat 1 or more eggs, season with pepper
4. Pour over the bacon
5. Cover with pastry and bake
