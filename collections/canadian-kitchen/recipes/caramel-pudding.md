## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 pt. milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Put sugar on stove and stir until brown
2. Put on stove again to simmer one pint milk, two tablespoonfuls corn starch, and vanilla flavoring
3. When all are ready mix together and stir until thick
4. Put in a mould to cool
