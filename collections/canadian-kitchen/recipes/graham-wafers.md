## Ingredients

- 3/2 cup butter
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- unbeaten white of 1 egg
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 8 tablespoons warm water
- 1 quart Graham flour

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream 3/2 cup butter.
2. Add gradually 1 cup granulated sugar and the unbeaten white of 1 egg.
3. Beat vigorously for 2 minutes.
4. Dissolve 3/4 teaspoon soda in 8 tablespoons warm water and add.
5. Stir in slowly 1 quart Graham flour.
6. Knead until smooth.
7. Roll out a little at a time into a sheet as thin as a wafer.
8. Cut in squares and bake in a slow oven until crisp and brown.
9. Rye meal may be used.
