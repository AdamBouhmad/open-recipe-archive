## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 2 teaspoonfuls curry powder or paste
- Butter and lard
- 1 teacup hot water
- 4 onions

## Directions

1. Cut a fowl into pieces the size of a walnut, put into a stewpan with a little lard and stir until nearly cooked
2. Cut onions into thin slices, place them in another stewpan with a little lard or butter, grill until nicely browned
3. When browned, add a little more butter or lard, with curry; stir a few minutes
4. Add the contents of the two saucepans together and stir until the meat is sufficiently cooked
5. After adding a teacup of water, allow to stand a short time before serving
