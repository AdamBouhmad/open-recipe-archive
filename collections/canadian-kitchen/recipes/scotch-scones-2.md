## Ingredients

- 1 sifter flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 cups buttermilk

## Directions

1. Sift the cream tartar 2 or 3 times with flour
2. Mix with knife
