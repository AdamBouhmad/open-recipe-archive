## Ingredients

- 1.5 gal. white wine vinegar
- 4 oz. ginger, bruised
- 2 oz. allspice
- 1/2 oz. chillies, bruised
- 2 oz. turmeric
- 1 lb. mustard
- 1 oz. garlic
- 2 lbs. salt
- 2 onions, cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Boil for a quarter of an hour, the onions and garlic only three minutes.
2. The mustard and turmeric should be mixed well with some of the vinegar before it is put on to boil.
