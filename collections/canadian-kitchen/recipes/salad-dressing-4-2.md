## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon paprika or red pepper
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt butter
2. Stir in flour
3. Add 1 cup milk and boil
4. Mix sugar, salt, mustard, paprika or red pepper and egg
5. Add to first mixture and cook until thick
6. Add 1/2 cup vinegar slowly
