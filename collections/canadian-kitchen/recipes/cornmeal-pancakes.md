## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour milk
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 egg well beaten
- equal parts flour and cornmeal to make a soft batter

## Directions

1. Mix sour milk, soda, and well beaten egg. Add equal parts flour and cornmeal to make a soft batter. Fry on a hot buttered pan.
