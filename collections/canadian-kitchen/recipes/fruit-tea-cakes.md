## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup chopped raisins (seeded)
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients with flour enough to make a stiff dough to roll out
2. Cut in shapes
3. Bake in a hot oven
