## Ingredients

- 4 cups raisins
- 3 cups sugar
- 4 cups currants
- 1/2 pound lemon peel
- 1 cup molasses
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 pound butter
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- a saltspoon salt
- butter size of an egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Combine 4 cups raisins, 3 cups sugar, 4 cups currants, 1/2 pound lemon peel, 1 cup molasses, 2 cups buttermilk, 1 pound butter, 1 teaspoon soda, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 2 teaspoons cinnamon, 1 teaspoon cloves and 1 teaspoon nutmeg.
2. Bake with slow fire.
3. For White Part: beat 1 cup granulated sugar, a saltspoon salt, butter size of an egg, and 1/2 cup milk together.
4. Fold in 1 1/2 cups flour and the whites of 3 eggs beaten light with 1 teaspoon baking powder.
5. Bake in layers.
6. Put together with fruit part and ice with caramel frosting.
