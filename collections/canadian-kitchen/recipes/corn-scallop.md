## Ingredients

- 1 can of corn
- 1 pint milk
- 1 beaten egg
- 2 slices bread crumbled fine
- bits of butter
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Empty a can of corn into a baker
2. add to it 1 pint milk, and a beaten egg
3. add 2 slices bread crumbled fine
4. dot the top with bits of butter, pepper and salt
5. brown in a hot oven
