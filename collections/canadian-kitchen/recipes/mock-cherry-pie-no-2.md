## Ingredients

- 1 cup cranberries
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon almond essence
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Sprinkle with flour
3. Bake in pie crust
