## Ingredients

- 1 pound soft maple sugar
- 1/4 to 1/2 cup boiling water
- whites of eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Add 1/4 to 1/2 cup boiling water to 1 pound soft maple sugar and stir occasionally until sugar is dissolved
2. Boil without stirring until syrup will thread when dropped from tin spoon
3. Pour gradually on beaten whites, beating mixture continually
4. Continue beating until of right consistency to spread
