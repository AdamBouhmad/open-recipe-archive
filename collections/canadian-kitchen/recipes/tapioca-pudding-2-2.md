## Ingredients

- 1 cup tapioca
- a pint of water
- nearly a quart of milk
- beaten yolk of 4 eggs
- 1 small cup sugar
- salt
- vanilla
- whites of 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca over night in a pint of water
2. In the morning add nearly a quart of milk
3. Boil in double boiler until clear
4. Add the beaten yolk of 4 eggs, sugar, salt and vanilla
5. Pour into a dish
6. Beat the whites of 4 eggs
7. Season with sugar and vanilla
8. Spread on top and brown in the oven
