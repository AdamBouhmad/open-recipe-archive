## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 pt. milk
- 1/2 oz. gelatine
- 3 oz. sugar
- 1/2 pt. double cream
- pineapple

## Directions

1. Take the pineapple and trim well, cut in half, cut one-half in dice, the other half pound in a mortar and pass through a hair sieve.
2. Whip the cream, add the juice of pine, also the dice of pine.
3. Dissolve the gelatine in a gill of the pineapple syrup, add sugar.
4. When cold add cream, and pour it into a decorated mould and set on ice.
