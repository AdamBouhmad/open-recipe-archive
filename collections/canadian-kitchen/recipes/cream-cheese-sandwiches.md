## Ingredients

- half a Philadelphia cream-cheese
- a tablespoonful of butter
- powdered yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- paprica
- crackers

## Directions

1. Rub together half a Philadelphia cream-cheese, a tablespoonful of butter, and the powdered yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
2. Season with salt and paprica
3. Spread this between crackers — saltines, or water-thin biscuits, or sea foams
4. Home-made cottage cheese can be substituted for the Philadelphia
