## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup currants
- ½ cup molasses
- ½ cup cold coffee
- ½ teaspoon each of cinnamon and cloves
- 1 teaspoon soda in the coffee

## Directions

1. Mix as thick as a jelly cake
2. Bake in layers
