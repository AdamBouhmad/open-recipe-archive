## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1/8 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- whites of 6 eggs
- strawberry extract
- lemon extract
- 1 pint milk
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream 2 cups white sugar with 1 cup butter
2. Add 1 cup sweet milk
3. Sift together 3 cups flour, 1/8 teaspoon soda, and 1 teaspoon cream tartar
4. Combine ingredients
5. Divide into two halves - flavor one half with strawberry extract and the other with lemon extract
6. For filling: combine 1 pint milk, yolks of 4 eggs, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, flavor with vanilla, sweeten to taste
