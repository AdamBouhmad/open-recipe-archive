## Ingredients

- raw potatoes to make 2 quarts (peeled and chopped)
- fresh suet to make 2½ cups (chopped)
- salt
- flour as much as will stick them together

## Directions

1. Salt the potatoes and suet and mix all together with as much flour as will stick them together
2. Put in a pudding bag and boil for 2 hours
