## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Boil 2 chickens in as little water as possible to cook them without burning, until the meat will drop from the bones
2. Remove the meat from the bone and cut it up fine
3. Put it back in the kettle with plenty of water, pepper and salt and sage
4. Heat it thoroughly
5. Slice hard-boiled eggs in the bottom of a dish, then pour the chicken over it while hot
6. Place a weight upon it, and set in a cool place
7. When cold it will come out in a loaf and may be sliced
