## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 1/2 cups sweet milk
- butter
- pound of the best flavored honey

## Directions

1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, shortening, and sweet milk
2. Roll quickly, and bake in a hot oven
3. When done, split the cake and spread the lower half thinly with butter, and the upper half with pound of the best flavored honey
4. Let it stand a few minutes, and the honey will melt gradually, and the flavor will permeate all through the cake
5. To be eaten with milk
