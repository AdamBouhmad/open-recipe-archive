## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- Butter size of a large egg
- Sugar
- Sweet cream, sweetened
- A little vanilla
- Chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar and mix in the usual way
2. Bake in a large flat tin
3. When cold, cut in small squares
4. For icing: Cream butter size of a large egg
5. Add as much sugar as will cream in well
6. Add sweet cream, sweetened and a little vanilla, until proper stiffness has been obtained
7. Ice the squares and roll in chopped walnuts
