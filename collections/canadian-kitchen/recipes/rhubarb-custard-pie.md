## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups rhubarb
- 1 3/4 cups sugar
- 2 yolks
- About 1 tablespoon water
- Meringue

## Directions

1. Cook rhubarb with about 1 tablespoon water
2. Add sugar just as remove from fire
3. Add beaten yolks of eggs and stir vigorously
4. Put in shell
5. Cover with meringue
