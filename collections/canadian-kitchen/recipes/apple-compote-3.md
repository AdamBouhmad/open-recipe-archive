## Ingredients

- apples
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- apple jelly
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Core and peel as many apples as wanted and cook slowly in a syrup made by boiling 1 cup sugar to 1 cup water
2. When done lift to a dish and fill the spaces where the cores were with apple jelly and sprinkle with granulated sugar
3. Pour the syrup around them
4. Nice red apples can be quartered and cored and the skins left on them and cooked slowly in the same way, turning them in order that both sides may be cooked alike
