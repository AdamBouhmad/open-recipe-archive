## Ingredients

- 4 sour apples
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 1/2 cups cornmeal
- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful soda

## Directions

1. Chop apples very fine
2. Stir into them beaten egg, molasses, cornmeal and sifted flour
3. Dissolve soda in warm water and add it, using enough water to thin batter
4. Bake in buttered gem-pans in a moderate oven
