## Ingredients

- A heaping tablespoonful of butter
- A medium-sized onion
- Two pounds of finely chopped lean raw beef
- One quart cold water
- The white and shell of an egg, well beaten, with a little cold water
- Four peppercorns
- Teaspoonful of salt
- Two cloves
- A blade of mace

## Directions

1. Heat a heaping tablespoonful of butter in a covered saucepan
2. Slice into it a medium-sized onion; stir until the onion is browned
3. Add two pounds of finely chopped lean raw beef, one quart cold water; cover closely and let it simmer three hours
4. Strain the soup, return to the kettle
5. Add the white and shell of an egg, well beaten, with a little cold water; add also four peppercorns, teaspoonful of salt, two cloves and a blade of mace
6. Boil five minutes, then drain and serve from a hot tureen
