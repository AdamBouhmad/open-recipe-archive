## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. Mix with brown sugar, butter, currants and vanilla
3. Fill tart shells
4. Bake
