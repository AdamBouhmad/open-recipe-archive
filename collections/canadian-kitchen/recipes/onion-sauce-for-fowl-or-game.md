## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1 pt. milk
- 4 pt. cream or milk
- 1 cup chopped onions
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour and salt with cream
2. Bring milk to a boil and slowly add the first mixture, stirring gently
3. When thick, stir in onions cut very fine and boiled very soft
