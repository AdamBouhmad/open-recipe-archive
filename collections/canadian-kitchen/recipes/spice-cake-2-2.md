## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 small teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in milk
- 1 cup raisins chopped
- 2 cups sifted flour

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter
2. Add cinnamon, allspice, and salt
3. Add sour milk with dissolved soda
4. Add chopped raisins
5. Add sifted flour
6. Bake in a loaf in a slow oven
7. Can be used as a pudding
