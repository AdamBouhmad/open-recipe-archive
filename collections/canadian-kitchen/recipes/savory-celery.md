## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- stock
- onion-juice
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- roux

## Directions

1. Select the whitest and tenderest stalks and lay aside in ice-water
2. Cut the outer, coarser stalks into three-inch lengths
3. Stew in a cupful of stock, seasoned with half teaspoonful of onion-juice, salt, pepper, and parsley
4. Cook, covered, for an hour, slowly
5. Drain and press in a colander
6. Return the stock to the fire
7. When it boils put the reserved stalks, also cut into short lengths, into it
8. Cook gently until tender
9. Thicken with a good spoonful of roux
10. Boil up and serve
