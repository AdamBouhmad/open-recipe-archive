## Ingredients

- one ounce of tincture of benzoin
- two wine-glasses of whisky
- one cup of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix one ounce of tincture of benzoin, two wine-glasses of whisky, and one cup of molasses well
2. One teaspoonful whenever cough is troublesome
