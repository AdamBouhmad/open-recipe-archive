## Ingredients

- 2 ounces Cowan's Perfection Chocolate (grated)
- 1 pint milk
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants

## Directions

1. Steam 1 hour
2. Serve hot
