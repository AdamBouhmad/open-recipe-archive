## Ingredients

- 2 quarts raw cabbage
- 2 quarts boiled beets
- 2 cups grated horseradish
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 teaspoons black pepper
- cayenne pepper
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage fine
2. Chop boiled beets fine
3. It is best to put the cabbage, beets and horseradish through a food chopper
4. Combine cabbage, beets, horseradish, 4 cups brown sugar, 2 tablespoons salt, 2 teaspoons black pepper, and a little cayenne pepper
5. Cover with cold vinegar
6. Put in jars, airtight
