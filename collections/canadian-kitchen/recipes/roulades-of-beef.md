## Ingredients

- Slices of undercooked roast beef
- slices of boiled ham
- one egg
- pepper and mustard
- a little thick gravy
- fine crumbs
- butter or dripping for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into thin, even, oblong slices, the ham rather thinner and smaller
2. spread one side of the beef with mustard, and pepper the ham
3. Lay the ham upon the beef and roll up together as lightly as possible
4. brush over with the egg, roll each in the crumbs, and pierce through with a slender skewer, in such a manner as to keep the roll pinned together
5. Put several on each skewer, but do not let them touch one another
6. fry brown, lay on a hot dish, and gently withdraw the skewers, then pour the gravy boiling hot over them, and serve
