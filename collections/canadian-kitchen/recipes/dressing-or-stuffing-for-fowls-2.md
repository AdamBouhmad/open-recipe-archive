## Ingredients

- brown crust from slices or pieces of stale bread
- tepid water
- pepper
- salt
- powdered summer savory
- sage
- melted butter
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cut the brown crust from slices or pieces of stale bread until you have as much as the inside of a pound loaf.
2. Put it into a suitable dish, and pour tepid water over it.
3. Let it stand one minute, as it soaks very quickly.
4. Take up a handful at a time and squeeze it hard and dry with both hands, placing it in another dish.
5. Toss it all up lightly through your fingers.
6. Add pepper, salt (about a teaspoonful), also a teaspoonful of powdered summer savory, the same amount of sage or the green herb minced fine.
7. Add a little melted butter, and a beaten egg.
8. Work thoroughly all together, and it is ready for dressing either fowls, fish or meats.
