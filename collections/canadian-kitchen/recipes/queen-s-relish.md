## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 2 heads cabbage
- 3 or 4 green cucumbers
- 8 or 10 large onions
- 5 or 6 green peppers
- salt
- vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 2 tablespoons tumeric
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- celery seed
- pickling spice

## Directions

1. Sprinkle with salt and let stand over night
2. Next morning drain
3. Chop all fine and let stand over night
4. Drain next morning, cover with vinegar
5. Add sugar, mustard, curry powder, tumeric, cinnamon, and cloves
6. Use celery seed and pickling spice to suit taste
7. Tie in a cloth and cook tender
