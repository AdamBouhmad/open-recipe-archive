## Ingredients

- 3 eggs beaten separately
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup butter creamed
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 10 cents' worth shelled walnuts

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Add eggs beaten separately
3. Sift cream tartar and soda with flour
4. Mix in flour
5. Chop half the walnuts
6. Put into well-buttered pan
7. Spread thin layer of mixture
8. Add layer of chopped nuts
9. Cover with rest of batter
10. Ice and put rest of nuts on top
