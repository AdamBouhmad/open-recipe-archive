## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1½ cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup dates
- 1 cups chopped walnut meats
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Drop in small teaspoons on buttered tins and bake
