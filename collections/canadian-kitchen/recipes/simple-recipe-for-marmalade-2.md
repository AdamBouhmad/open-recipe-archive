## Ingredients

- 12 bitter oranges
- 1 lemon
- 6 qts. water
- 7 or 8 lbs. sugar

## Directions

1. Cut bitter oranges and one lemon very thin, and put into water
2. Let stand over night or thirty-six hours
3. Boil rather fast two and one-half hours
4. Add seven or eight pounds of white sugar and boil one hour longer
5. Take out all seeds and hard ends
6. If sweet oranges are used put in only the pulp
7. The addition of the pulp of two sweet oranges sometimes improves the marmalade
