## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes
- 2 quarts water
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- good biscuit dough

## Directions

1. Peel and cut potatoes in thick slices
2. Put in 2 quarts water and boil until done
3. Add salt and pepper and 1 tablespoon butter
4. Make good biscuit dough, drop in and let boil 10 minutes without cover
