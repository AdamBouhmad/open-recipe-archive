## Ingredients

- 1 cup suet, chopped very fine
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup water
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking-powder
- A pinch of salt
- Plum jam
- Two eggs
- one cup of sugar
- half cup of butter
- one cup of boiling wine

## Directions

1. Roll on a board and spread thickly with plum jam
2. Place in a tin and steam one and a half or two hours
3. For sauce: Mix two eggs, one cup of sugar, and half cup of butter well together
4. Pour into the mixture one cup of boiling wine (sherry is best)
