## Ingredients

- Orris root, four ounces
- Oil of cloves or bruised cloves, three ounces
- Gum benzoin, two ounces
- Calamus root, four ounces
- Angelica root, six ounces
- Oil of cinnamon (true), ten drops
- Oil of bitter almonds, forty drops
- Essence of bergamot, one drachm
- English oil of lavender, forty drops
- Oil of verbena, thirty drops
- Fresh rose-leaves to nearly fill the jar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Gather fresh rose-leaves to nearly fill the jar
2. Sprinkle some salt through them and leave to stand for a few days
3. Pour over them the mixture of oils, roots, and essences
4. It will perfume the air for years
