## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound flour
- 1/4 pound bread crumbs
- 3/4 pound suet
- 2 ounces sugar
- 2 ounces sultanas
- 3 1/2 nutmeg
- a pinch of salt
- 2 tablespoons treacle
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- rind and juice 1 lemon
- or a few drops essence lemon

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Steam 2 hours
