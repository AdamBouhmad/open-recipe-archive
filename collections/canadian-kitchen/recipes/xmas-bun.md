## Ingredients

- 2 pounds best layer raisins (stoned)
- 4 pounds best currants
- 3 1/2 pounds best peel (mixed)
- 1 pound almonds (blanched)
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon essence lemon
- 3 pounds bread dough, ready for loaves
- 1/2 pound butter

## Directions

1. Take 3 pounds bread dough, ready for loaves, divide in 2 parts
2. Take 1 part and mix with all the fruit
3. Take 1/2 pound butter and mix into other half
4. Line your pan bottom and sides with this
5. Pack in the fruit mixture
6. Wet the edges and put on top crust
7. Prick all over with a fork
8. Bake in a slow oven from 1 1/2 to 2 hours
