## Ingredients

- 1 raw egg
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- ½ cup vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 hard-boiled egg chopped fine
- lettuce also chopped fine

## Directions

1. boil until thick like honey
2. remove from stove and add chopped egg and lettuce
