## Ingredients

- 2½ cups rolled oats
- 3½ cup flour
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons lard
- 1 cup molasses
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon soda
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix all well together and drop on buttered pan
2. Bake in a moderate oven
