## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup fruit juice
- 3/8 teaspoon mace
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. put over hot water
3. stir until liquid
4. add beaten yolks, mace and fruit juice
5. stir until it thickens
6. Serve at once
