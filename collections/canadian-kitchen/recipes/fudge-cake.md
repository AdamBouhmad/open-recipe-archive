## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup chocolate melted
- 1/2 cup walnuts broken up
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 3/4 teaspoons butter
- 3/4 cup cocoa
- a few grains salt
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream 3/4 cup butter and 1 cup sugar together.
2. Add 1 cup sweet milk.
3. Stir in lightly 2 1/2 cups flour sifted with 1 teaspoon baking powder.
4. Stir in 1/2 cup chocolate melted and 1/2 cup walnuts broken up.
5. Add lastly 3 eggs, yolk and whites beaten separately.
6. For Fudge Frosting: Melt 1 3/4 teaspoons butter.
7. Add 3/4 cup cocoa, 1 1/2 cups sugar, a few grains salt and 3/4 cup milk.
8. Boil 8 minutes and add 1 teaspoon vanilla.
