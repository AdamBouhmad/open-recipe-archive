## Ingredients

- 4 large green peppers
- 2 quarts or 4 large cucumbers
- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 quarts small onions
- 4 cauliflowers
- 2 heads celery
- 1 cup mustard seed
- 2 tablespoonfuls mustard
- 2.5 cups flour
- 3.5 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls turmeric powder
- 1 gallon cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop finely peppers, slice cucumbers, green tomatoes, and onions
2. Break in pieces four cauliflowers and chop two heads of celery
3. Cover with salt and let stand twenty-four hours
4. Drain and add mustard-seed
5. Mix mustard, flour, sugar, turmeric powder, one pint cider vinegar for dressing
6. Put on to scald three and a half quarts cider vinegar
7. When scalding add mixture and vegetables
8. Leave on stove to scald and get soft
9. Do not boil
