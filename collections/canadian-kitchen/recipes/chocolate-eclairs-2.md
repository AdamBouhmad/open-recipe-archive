## Ingredients

- paste
- plain tube
- buttered sheet
- cream filling
- chocolate frosting

## Directions

1. Press paste from bag with plain tube, 3/8 inch in diameter on buttered sheet, in strips 3 1/2 inches long
2. When baked or cold, fill with cream filling
3. Spread smooth on under side with chocolate frosting
