## Ingredients

- 1 pound treacle
- 1 pound moist sugar
- 1 pound butter

## Directions

1. Put butter first and rub well over bottom of saucepan
2. Add treacle and sugar, stirring gently with a knife
3. Boil over a clear fire in a saucepan large enough to allow of fast boiling
4. After it has boiled about 10 minutes, ascertain if it is done by dropping a little into cold water from point of knife
5. If sufficiently done it will be quite crisp when taken from the water
6. Butter a shallow tin, pan or dish and pour toffee from saucepan into dish to cool
