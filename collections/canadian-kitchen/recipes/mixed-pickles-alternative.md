## Ingredients

- 1 peck vegetables (onions, cucumbers and cauliflower)
- 1 cup salt
- vinegar
- 1 pound whole mixed spice
- 2 tablespoons turmeric powder
- 3 cups white sugar

## Directions

1. Soak vegetables in brine made with 1 cup salt over night
2. In morning wash in cold water
3. Put in kettle and almost cover with vinegar
4. Add 1 pound whole mixed spice, 2 tablespoons turmeric powder, and 3 cups white sugar
5. Bring to boiling point
6. Bottle
