## Ingredients

- pieces of meat
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take pieces of meat and put through grinder
2. Cook for a few minutes with just enough water to wet nicely
3. Season well with salt and pepper
4. Press in bowl to slice when cold
