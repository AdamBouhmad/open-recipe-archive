## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1.5 pts. milk
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- Bread-and-butter marmalade
- 1 pt. boiled custard
- Vanilla to taste
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of bread-and-butter
2. Spread them with the marmalade
3. Fit them neatly into a buttered pie-dish until it is half full
4. Pour over them gradually a hot custard made of the milk heated almost to boiling, then taken off the fire, and the beaten eggs and sugar stirred in with the flavoring
5. Place a small plate on the top to prevent the bread from rising
6. Let it soak for half an hour
7. Grate a little nutmeg on the top
8. Bake
9. When done turn it out of the dish
10. Pour over it a pint of boiled custard
