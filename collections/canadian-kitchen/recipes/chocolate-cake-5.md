## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1/4 pound grated Cowan's Perfection Chocolate
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 5 tablespoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons boiling water

## Directions

1. To the grated chocolate add 5 tablespoons sugar, 3 tablespoons boiling water and stir over fire until smooth and glossy
2. Cream butter and sugar together
3. Stir chocolate mixture into the creamed butter and sugar
4. Add well beaten eggs
5. Add milk
6. Mix flour with soda and cream tartar
7. Add flour mixture to other ingredients
8. Bake 20 minutes in a moderate oven
