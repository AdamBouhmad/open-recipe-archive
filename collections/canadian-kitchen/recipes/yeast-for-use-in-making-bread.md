## Ingredients

- 12 medium-sized potatoes
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup lukewarm water
- 3 White Swan Yeast Cakes
- 1 quart cold water
- 3 parts hot water

## Directions

1. Pare and boil potatoes, mash well
2. There should be 1 quart water on them when done
3. Put the flour in a crock, sugar and salt, scald with 1 pint boiling water
4. Add the quart of hot water from the potatoes, with 1 quart cold water
5. Soak yeast cakes in lukewarm water, then add to the mixture when about cold
6. Let it rise all night
7. Keep in a cool place
8. It will keep from 3 weeks to a month
