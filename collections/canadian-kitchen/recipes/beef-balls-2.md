## Ingredients

- 2 pounds raw beef
- suet
- salt
- pepper
- a handful of flour
- hot drippings

## Directions

1. Chop very fine the beef with some suet
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Mix in a handful of flour
4. Mould into balls
5. Fry in hot drippings to a nice brown on both sides
