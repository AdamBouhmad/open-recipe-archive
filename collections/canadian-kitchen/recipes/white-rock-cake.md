## Ingredients

- Whites 3 eggs
- 0.5 cup melted butter
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2.5 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 0.75 cup flour
- 0.75 cup milk
- 1 cup cornstarch

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs to a stiff froth
2. Sugar and butter to a cream
3. Mix flour and cornstarch with two and one-half teaspoonfuls baking powder
4. Mix all together
