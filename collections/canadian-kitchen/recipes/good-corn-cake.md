## Ingredients

- 1 large cup cornmeal
- 1 small cup flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 2 teaspoons heaped baking powder
- 3 tablespoons heaped of butter
- a pinch of salt
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix 1 large cup cornmeal, 1 small cup flour, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 eggs well beaten, 2 teaspoons heaped baking powder, 3 tablespoons heaped of butter, a pinch of salt
2. Add sweet milk to make a thin batter
3. Bake in biscuit tin in moderate oven
