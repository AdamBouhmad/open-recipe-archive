## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes
- cold lean meat
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- melted butter or milk

## Directions

1. From cold mashed potatoes make balls
2. With a teaspoon press a hollow in the top
3. Chop fine some cold lean meat and season with pepper and salt
4. Moisten meat with a little gravy
5. Put a spoon of meat in the hollow of potatoes with a little melted butter or milk
6. Place in the oven and brown
