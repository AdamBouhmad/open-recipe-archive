## Ingredients

- 2 cups lobster meat
- 1 cupful celery, minced
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- Juice 1 lemon
- 1/2 pt. milk
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 saltspoonful cayenne
- 1 dessertspoonful flour

## Directions

1. Cook together the butter and flour over hot water, add the milk, stir until smooth, put in the celery and cook three minutes, add the lobster, seasoning, and yolk of egg
2. stir until thick, and serve
