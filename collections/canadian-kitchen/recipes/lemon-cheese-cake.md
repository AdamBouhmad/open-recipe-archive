## Ingredients

- 1 lb. lump sugar
- Juice of 3 lemons
- 6 eggs (yolks)
- Rind of 2 lemons, grated
- Whites of 2 eggs
- 1/4 lb. butter

## Directions

1. Mix all together in double boiler and stir gently over a fire until the mixture becomes thick
2. put away in a bowl or crock and it will keep for weeks
3. Make a nice light paste and line little patty pans with it, and put a teaspoonful of the mixture into each
4. bake in a hot oven
