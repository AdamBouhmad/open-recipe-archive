## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1/2 glass jam or jelly
- 4 tablespoons sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in a little hot water
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream together sugar and butter
2. Beat the yolks of eggs
3. Mix in jam or jelly
4. Mix in sour milk and soda dissolved in a little hot water
5. Mix in sifted flour and baking powder
6. Beat the whites of the eggs and add at the last
7. Bake in jelly cake tins
