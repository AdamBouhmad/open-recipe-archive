## Ingredients

- 20 ears of corn
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup salt
- 1/2 teaspoon celery seed
- 3/4 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 4 onions chopped fine
- 1 large head cabbage
- 2 ounces mustard
- 2 quarts vinegar (strong)
- 2 green peppers chopped fine

## Directions

1. Boil corn until tender, then cut off cob
2. Mix corn, peppers, cabbage and onions together and pour 1 quart vinegar over this and let come to boil
3. Mix other ingredients together with the other quart of vinegar
4. Mix all well, and boil mixture 1 hour
5. Seal in jars
