## Ingredients

- 1 peck tomatoes
- 12 apples
- 6 onions
- 4 peppers chopped fine
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Chop tomatoes, apples, onions, and peppers fine
2. Add 1 pint vinegar and 2 cups sugar
3. Boil until thick
