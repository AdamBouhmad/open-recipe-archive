## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 even teaspoonful of mustard
- A pinch of paprica or cayenne
- Salt
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- Cracker-dust

## Directions

1. Slice and take out hard centres and fat
2. Have ready, beaten to a cream, a tablespoonful of butter, an even teaspoonful of mustard, a pinch of paprica or cayenne, a little salt, and a teaspoonful of lemon juice
3. Melt, without really heating the mixture
4. Coat each slice with it
5. Roll in cracker-dust
6. Broil, turning often
7. They should be done in eight minutes
8. Put a few drops of the deviled sauce upon each and send to table
