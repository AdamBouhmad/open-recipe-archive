## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup boiled ham
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls milk
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- Pepper
- Buttered toast

## Directions

1. Put milk and butter in a saucepan, let them come to a boil
2. Add the ham, pepper and eggs beaten light
3. Stir constantly till it thickens
4. Serve on squares of buttered toast
