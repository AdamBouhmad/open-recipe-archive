## Ingredients

- 10 cups of corn cut from the cob
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup salt

## Directions

1. Mix together corn, sugar, and salt
2. Put in a kettle at the back of the stove until enough of the milk of the corn has exuded to cook it without adding water
3. Cook for 1 hour, watching carefully that it does not scorch
4. Can and seal
5. When you wish to use the corn take the required amount and put in cold water for 3 1/2 hours
6. Turn off the salty water and prepare with milk and butter
