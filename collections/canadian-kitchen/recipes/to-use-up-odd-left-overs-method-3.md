## Ingredients

- roast leftover
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- bread
- flour

## Directions

1. Take what is left from a roast and cut in small pieces
2. Put in a stewpan and cover with plenty of water
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Stew from 20 to 30 minutes
5. Flavor with onion cut in small pieces if desired
6. Toast a couple of slices of bread and cut in small squares and put on platter
7. Thicken the stew with flour and pour over toast
8. Serve hot
