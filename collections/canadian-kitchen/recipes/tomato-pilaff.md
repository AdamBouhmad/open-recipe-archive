## Ingredients

- 1 cup stewed and strained tomato
- 1 minced onion
- 1 cup stock or gravy
- salt
- pepper
- 1 cupful of rice
- 1/2 cupful of butter

## Directions

1. Cook together one cup of stewed and strained tomato, one minced onion, one cup stock or gravy, with seasoning of salt and pepper.
2. When boiling add one cupful of rice and toss lightly until the liquor is absorbed.
3. Melt one-half cupful of butter, pour over the rice and set on back of stove to steam.
4. After about twenty minutes remove the cover, shake well, that the kernels may be distinct, and cover with a towel until ready to serve.
