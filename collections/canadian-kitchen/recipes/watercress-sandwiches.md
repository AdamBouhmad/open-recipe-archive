## Ingredients

- watercress
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt and pepper
- stale loaf
- fresh butter
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Wash well some watercress and dry them in a cloth, pressing out every atom of moisture
2. Mix with the cresses hard-boiled eggs chopped fine, and seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Have a stale loaf and some fresh butter
4. Cut as many thin slices as will be required for two dozen sandwiches with a sharp knife
5. Cut the cress into small pieces, removing the stems
6. Place it between each slice of bread and butter, with a slight sprinkling of lemon-juice
7. Press down the slices hard and cut them sharply on a board into small squares, leaving no crust
