## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- whites 8 eggs
- 1 pound citron sliced fine
- 1 pound almonds blanched and chopped fine
- 1 small cocoanut grated

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar thoroughly together
2. add the milk and flour and baking powder sifted 3 times
3. stir all well together
4. add the citron and nuts, stirring only enough to mix thoroughly
5. Last of all add the whites of eggs beaten to a stiff froth
6. Fold in lightly and bake in a moderate oven
7. When cold, ice with confectioner's sugar mixed with white of egg
