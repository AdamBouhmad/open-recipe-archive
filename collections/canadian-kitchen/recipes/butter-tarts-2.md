## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup currants
- nutmeg or flavor of choice

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. This quantity will fill a dozen shells
3. Fill into uncooked shells
4. Brown well
