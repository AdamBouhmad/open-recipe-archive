## Ingredients

- ripe gooseberries (8 pounds)
- sugar (4 pounds)
- good vinegar (1 pint)
- cloves (2 ounces)
- cinnamon (2 ounces)

## Directions

1. Boil berries and sugar for 3 or 4 hours
2. Add spices and boil a little more
3. Pour through a sieve and bottle
