## Ingredients

- 2 heads cabbage
- 2 heads cauliflower
- 2 qts. onions
- 2 qts. green tomatoes
- 1 small cup sugar
- 2 cucumbers
- 6 roots of celery
- 2 green peppers
- 2 qts. vinegar
- 1/2 lb. mustard
- 1 oz. turmeric
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Chop fine.
2. Boil in just enough water to cover until moderately soft.
3. Strain.
4. Take vinegar, mustard, turmeric, sugar and salt to taste.
5. Let this come to a boil and pour it over the mixture.
