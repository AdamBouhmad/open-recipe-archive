## Ingredients

- Several oranges
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls olive oil
- 1 tablespoonful lemon juice or vinegar
- crisp lettuce leaf

## Directions

1. Peel oranges, remove the inside skin, leaving the pulp as unbroken as possible
2. Make a very smooth French dressing by mixing sugar, olive oil, vinegar or lemon juice in the order given, adding oil and vinegar slowly
3. Place a little of the orange on a crisp lettuce leaf
4. Pour over a little of the dressing and serve
