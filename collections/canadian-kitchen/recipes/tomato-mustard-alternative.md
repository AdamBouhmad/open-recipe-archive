## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 12 onions
- 12 tomatoes
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 3/4 cup salt
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Boil apples, onions, and tomatoes together till soft
2. Put through a sieve
3. Add 1 pint vinegar, 1 tablespoon mustard, 3/4 cup salt, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon cloves, and 2 cups sugar
4. Set on stove
5. Heat up and bottle
