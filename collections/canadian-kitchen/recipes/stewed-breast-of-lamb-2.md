## Ingredients

- One breast of lamb
- pepper and salt to taste
- sufficient stock to cover it
- thickening of butter and flour
- a pint and a half of green peas
- A few stewed mushrooms

## Directions

1. Skin the lamb and cut into pieces, and season them with pepper and salt
2. lay these in a stew-pan with sufficient stock of gravy to cover them, and stew gently for an hour and a half
3. Just before serving, thicken the gravy with a little butter and flour, give one boil, and pour it over the meat
4. Have ready a pint and a half of green peas and lay them over and around the meat
5. A few stewed mushrooms will be found an improvement if they can be obtained, but they are not necessary for this dish
