## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 large tablespoons butter
- about 2 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, cream tartar, soda and salt
2. Work into the flour 2 large tablespoons of butter
3. Add about 2 cups of sweet milk (mixing with a knife) until you have a soft dough
4. Turn out on the mixing board but do not knead
5. Pat into shape, roll out, cut into large biscuits
6. Bake in a very hot oven
