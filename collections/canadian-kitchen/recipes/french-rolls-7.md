## Ingredients

- 1 pint scalded milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 White Swan Yeast Cake
- 3/2 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald 1 pint milk
2. Add 1 teaspoon salt, 3/4 White Swan Yeast Cake, 3/2 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon butter
3. Rub in flour to make a sponge
4. Let rise over night
5. Next morning mix stiff and set to rise again
6. Mix down twice
7. Cut with cake cutter and double over
8. Cook in a moderately hot oven
