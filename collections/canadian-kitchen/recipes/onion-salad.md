## Ingredients

- onions
- boiling water
- cream
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel onions and slice them very fine
2. Cover with boiling water and let stand till cool
3. Take out the onions, squeezing out all the water possible
4. Cover with a dressing made of 2 parts of cream and 1 of vinegar
5. Use pepper and salt to taste
6. Add more vinegar if desired
