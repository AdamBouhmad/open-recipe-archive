## Ingredients

- pork chops
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- powdered sage
- finely minced onion
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Cut off the skin, trim neatly and dip in beaten egg.
2. Dip in cracker-crumbs seasoned with salt, pepper, powdered sage, and finely minced onion.
3. Set in a cold place for an hour or more.
4. Fry in hot fat, turning often, for at least twenty minutes.
5. Send in dry and hot, and serve with apple sauce.
