## Ingredients

- Grated rind and juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks separately
2. Combine grated rind and juice of 1 lemon, 1 tablespoon butter, 3/4 cup sugar, 1 egg
3. Boil till it drips like honey
