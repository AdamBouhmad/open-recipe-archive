## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds beef
- 1 quart flour
- 0.5 pound suet
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- salt and pepper
- flour
- water
- chopped onion
- sage herbs

## Directions

1. Cut beef into small pieces
2. Chop suet very fine and add to flour and salt
3. Mix into a stiff paste with a knife
4. Roll out and line a bowl with the paste
5. Fill in the beef with salt and pepper to taste
6. Dust with flour and add a little water
7. Cover the top with paste and join the edges well
8. Tie down with a cloth and plunge into boiling water
9. Keep boiling for 4 hours
10. Optional: put in a chopped onion and a little sage herbs if liked
