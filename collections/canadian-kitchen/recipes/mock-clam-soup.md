## Ingredients

- 1 pint of marrowfat beans
- Water enough to cover them
- 1 small onion
- 1 gallon of cold water
- A little summer savory
- 2 ounces of butter
- 1 cup of cream or rich milk
- Salt and pepper
- 2 slices of toast
- 4 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook marrowfat beans over night in water enough to cover them
2. In the morning drain and put them on the fire with a small onion and a gallon of cold water
3. Boil until tender and strain
4. Add to the stock a little summer savory, two ounces of butter and a cup of cream or rich milk, season with salt and pepper
5. When the soup comes to a boil, cut two slices of toast into dice, and four hard-boiled eggs in slices
6. Put these in the tureen and pour the soup over and serve
