## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup rice
- salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 4 teaspoons butter
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Let 3 cups milk come to scald in double boiler
2. Add 1 cup rice and some salt
3. Boil till soft
4. Add 3/4 cup sugar and 4 teaspoons butter
5. Put in a dish
6. Sprinkle with remainder of sugar, butter and cinnamon
