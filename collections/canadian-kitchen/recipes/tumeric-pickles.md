## Ingredients

- 4 quarts large green cucumbers peeled and sliced
- brine
- vinegar
- water
- 4 large onions sliced
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 teaspoon tumeric
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Slice cucumbers about 1 inch thick
2. Soak in brine over night
3. Drain and scald well in about equal parts of vinegar and water until they look a little cooked, then drain
4. Put on clear vinegar, add onions sliced, cook until tender
5. Stir up mustard, flour, tumeric, and sugar in a little water
6. Pour in the boiling pickles, stirring constantly until it thickens
7. Seal while hot
