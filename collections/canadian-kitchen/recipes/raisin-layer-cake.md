## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 1/4 teaspoonful soda
- 1/4 teaspoonful each cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice

## Directions

1. Bake in layers in a moderate oven.
