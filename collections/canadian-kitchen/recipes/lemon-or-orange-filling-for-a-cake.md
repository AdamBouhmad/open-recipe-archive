## Ingredients

- 1 orange or lemon grated
- 1 egg beaten well
- 3/2 to 1 cup sugar according to taste

## Directions

1. Put the lemon in dish, set in a pot of boiling water
2. When nearly boiling, pour in the beaten egg and sugar
3. Let it boil until nicely thickened
4. Spread between layers of cake
