## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- pinch soda
- 1/2 cake grated chocolate
- tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Add sugar, butter, soda, and grated chocolate to milk and water
2. When this boils add tablespoons cornstarch dissolved in a little water
3. Let this boil until thickened
4. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla
5. Place in tart shells and put icing on top
