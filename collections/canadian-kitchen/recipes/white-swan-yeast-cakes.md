## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 1 tablespoon soda dissolved in 2 tablespoons sour milk
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- flour to make stiff dough

## Directions

1. Mix brown sugar, molasses, and butter
2. Dissolve soda in sour milk and add to mixture
3. Add ginger
4. Mix with flour to make stiff dough
