## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, butter, eggs and milk
2. Combine baking powder with flour
3. Mix all together
4. Bake
