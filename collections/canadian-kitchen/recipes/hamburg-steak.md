## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds raw meat from the round or poorer portions
- 2 cups bread crumbs soaked in milk
- 1 onion, minced
- 2 eggs
- salt and pepper to taste
- warm water
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop meat very finely
2. Add bread crumbs soaked in milk
3. Add minced onion
4. Season to taste
5. Add eggs
6. Make into a roll
7. Put in a baking pan
8. Pour over it warm water in which a piece of butter has been melted
9. Bake, basting frequently
10. Alternatively, may be made into small cakes and fried
