## Ingredients

- 1 pk. green tomatoes
- 1 cup salt
- 3 qts. vinegar
- 2 qts. water
- 2½ lbs. brown sugar
- 2 small tablespoonfuls ground allspice
- 2 small tablespoonfuls mustard seed
- 2 small tablespoonfuls ginger
- 2 small tablespoonfuls mustard
- 2 small tablespoonfuls cloves
- 2 large tablespoonfuls cinnamon

## Directions

1. Slice green tomatoes, sprinkle with one cup of salt, and leave over night
2. Drain well and add two quarts of water and one of vinegar
3. Boil fifteen minutes
4. Drain again and add two quarts of vinegar, brown sugar, mustard seed, allspice, ginger, mustard, cloves, two large tablespoonfuls of cinnamon
5. Mix, and boil fifteen minutes
