## Ingredients

- oranges
- pineapple
- bananas
- malmaga grapes
- chopped nuts
- 1 cup cream
- 1 teaspoon flour
- whites of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- small saltspoon salt
- a pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon mustard

## Directions

1. Cut and dice oranges, pineapple, bananas
2. shred thin malmaga grapes and chopped nuts
3. beat the cream to boiling point, stir in flour previously wet with cold milk
4. When half cold beat in the whipped eggs, set aside to cool
5. when quite cold whip in melted butter, pepper, salt and mustard
6. When salad is ready to serve, whip in lemon juice
7. Fill in shells of oranges
