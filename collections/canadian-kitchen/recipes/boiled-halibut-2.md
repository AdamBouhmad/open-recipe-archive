## Ingredients

- halibut
- salt
- saltpetre
- horseradish
- parsley
- egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a small halibut, or what you require from a large fish.
2. Put it into the fish-kettle, with the back of the fish undermost, cover it with cold water, in which a handful of salt, and a bit of saltpetre the size of a hazelnut, have been dissolved.
3. When it begins to boil, skim it carefully, and then let it just simmer till it is done.
4. Four pounds of fish will require nearly thirty minutes to boil it.
5. Drain it, garnish with horseradish or parsley — egg sauce or plain melted butter are served with it.
