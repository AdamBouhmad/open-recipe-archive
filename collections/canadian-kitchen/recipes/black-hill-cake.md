## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- white of egg
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour
- yolk of egg
- 1 tablespoon blackstrap
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon flour

## Directions

1. Cream together 1 cup sugar and 1 tablespoon butter
2. Add white of egg beaten to a froth
3. Add 1/4 cup sweet milk, 2 teaspoons baking powder and enough flour to stiffen
4. Leave 3 tablespoons of light part, add yolk of egg, 1 tablespoon blackstrap, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1 teaspoon flour
5. Bake in hot oven
