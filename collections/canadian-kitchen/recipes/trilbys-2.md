## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups yellow sugar
- 1 cup butter and lard mixed
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 2 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 2 eggs (if you have plenty)
- 1 teaspoon soda
- little salt

## Directions

1. Mix butter and lard with sugar
2. Add buttermilk
3. Mix oatmeal, flour, soda, and salt
4. Add eggs if desired
5. Combine all ingredients
6. Bake as cookies
7. Spread filling when cool
