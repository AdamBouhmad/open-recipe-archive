## Ingredients

- 2 quarts small onions
- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 quarts cucumbers
- 2 heads cauliflower
- 4 heads celery
- 1 dozen green peppers
- salt and water

## Directions

1. Cook all together in salt and water until tender, not soft
