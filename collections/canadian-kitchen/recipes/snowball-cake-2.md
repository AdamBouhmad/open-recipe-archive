## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1 snowball a little larger than an egg

## Directions

1. Stir ingredients quickly
2. Put in a very hot oven before snow melts
