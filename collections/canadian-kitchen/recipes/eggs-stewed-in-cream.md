## Ingredients

- one pint cream
- ten whole black peppers
- six eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil one pint cream
2. Add ten whole black peppers
3. When boiling add six eggs
4. Let cook on top of stove five minutes
5. Bake in oven ten minutes
6. Add pinch of salt
7. Serve from same dish
