## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3 cups oatmeal
- 1 cup brown sugar
- a pinch of salt
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 1/4 cup butter or lard
- cold water

## Directions

1. Mix to a dough with cold water, cut in squares with a knife
2. Bake in a hot oven
