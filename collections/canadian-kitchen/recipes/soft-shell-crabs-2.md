## Ingredients

- crabs
- boiling water
- cracker crumbs
- egg
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Wash crabs
2. Drop in boiling water for 1/2 minute to kill them
3. Take off apron (tail pieces folded on under shell)
4. Lift points at each side and cut out gills
5. Cut off a triangular piece at head including eyes and stomach
6. Wipe dry
7. Crumb
8. Egg crumb
9. Fry in deep fat until brown
