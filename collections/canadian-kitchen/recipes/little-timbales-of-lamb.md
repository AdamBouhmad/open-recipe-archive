## Ingredients

- cold cooked mutton
- butter
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- gravy or water
- capers

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup of cold cooked mutton, 3/4 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon onion minced fine, 3/4 teaspoon salt, pepper, 1/2 cup crumbs, gravy or water to moisten and 1 teaspoon capers
2. Cook onions and butter together without burning
3. Butter little moulds and place capers in moulds
4. Rub crumbs into onion and butter
5. Moisten, season and heat on stove till it begins to steam
6. Put in moulds and cook 15 or 20 minutes in moderate oven
