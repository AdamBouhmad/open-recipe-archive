## Ingredients

- slices of sponge cake or ladies' fingers
- sliced bananas
- a quart of blackberries
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Line a glass bowl with slices of sponge cake or ladies' fingers
2. fill the bowl with sliced bananas
3. squeeze the juice and pulp from a quart of blackberries, sweeten it well
4. pour it over the bananas
5. stand on ice until ice-cold
6. cover with a deep layer of whipped cream and serve
