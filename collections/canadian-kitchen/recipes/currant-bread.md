## Ingredients

- 1 cup new milk
- 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes
- flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup currants
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scald 1 cup new milk and let it cool
2. Add 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes and flour enough to make soft batter
3. Set in a warm place to rise until light
4. Add 1 cup brown sugar, half a cup butter, 1 cup currants, 2 well-beaten eggs, and a little nutmeg
5. Add flour enough to knead soft
6. Let rise again
7. Tuck into tins and set in a warm place
8. Let rise about 2 hours
9. Bake in a moderate oven
