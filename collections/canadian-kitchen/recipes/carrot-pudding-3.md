## Ingredients

- one cup grated carrots
- one large cup currants
- one cup grated potato
- one egg
- one cup brown sugar
- a little chopped peel
- one cup suet
- one teaspoonful soda
- one large cup raisins
- salt and spice to taste
- brandy sauce

## Directions

1. Steam in mould three hours
2. Serve with brandy sauce
