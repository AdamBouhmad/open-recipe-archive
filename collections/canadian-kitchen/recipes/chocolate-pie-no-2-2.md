## Ingredients

- ½ teacup Cowan's Perfection Chocolate (grated)
- 1½ teacups hot water
- ½ teacup sugar
- butter the size of an egg
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- beaten yolk of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well
2. Cook on top of stove until thick
3. Stir constantly
4. Pour into the shell
5. Let it cool
6. Beat the whites and spread on top
7. Brown in oven
