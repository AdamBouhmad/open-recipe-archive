## Ingredients

- kidney beans
- salt
- clarified dripping
- half a small onion
- chopped parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak over night and boil tender, but not until they break
2. Drain perfectly dry, throw in a little salt, and leave over an empty pot in the colander at the side of the range to 'dry off'
3. Have ready in a frying-pan a great spoonful of clarified dripping with half a small onion, grated, and a little chopped parsley
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, and when hissing hot put in the beans
5. Shake over the fire about two minutes, until the contents of the pan are well mixed, and as hot as may be without scorching, then serve
