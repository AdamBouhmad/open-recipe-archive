## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 pound suet chopped fine
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 2 3/4 pounds flour
- 1 ounce candied lemon peel
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon allspice
- 1 tablespoon salt

1. Combine all ingredients
