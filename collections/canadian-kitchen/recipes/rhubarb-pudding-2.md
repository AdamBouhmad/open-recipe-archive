## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 2 cups rhubarb cut in dices
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix together sugar, eggs, cream, rhubarb, flour and baking powder
2. Bake in oven
