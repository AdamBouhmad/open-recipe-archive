## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful brown sugar
- 2 teacups cornmeal
- 1 heaping tablespoonful flour
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very light, add melted butter, brown sugar, cornmeal, and flour, to which add baking powder and a cupful of milk.
2. Mix thoroughly, pour into greased muffin tins and bake in a quick oven.
