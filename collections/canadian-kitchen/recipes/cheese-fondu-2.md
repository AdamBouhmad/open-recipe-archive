## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- Gill hot cream
- 3 eggs
- 6 tablespoonfuls grated cheese
- Saltspoonful salt
- Pinch cayenne

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan butter and when melted stir in flour and hot cream.
2. Stir constantly and when thick and smooth stir in grated cheese, salt and cayenne.
3. Turn out into a bowl and beat in the beaten yolks of two eggs.
4. Beat the whites of three eggs as stiff as possible; have the baking dish heated and buttered, and just fifteen minutes before the fondu is wanted mix in the whites very quickly and lightly and bake.
5. The oven should be hot, but not over hot, and the fondu should rise to twice its original height.
