## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 small cup milk
- 1 tablespoonful lard or lard and butter
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- enough flour to make stiff dough

## Directions

1. Roll out, cut desired size, bake in a hot oven.
2. If liked, add two tablespoonfuls of white sugar.
