## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 2 cups sweet milk
- 1 cup lard
- 3/4 of 5c. worth oil of lemon
- 1/2 of 5c. worth baking ammonia
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, milk, lard, oil of lemon and baking ammonia
2. Roll a little thicker than pie crust on bottom of tin
3. Cross with a fork
4. Sprinkle with sugar
5. Bake
