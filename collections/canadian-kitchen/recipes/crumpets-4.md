## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 small cups flour
- 2 tablespoons sour milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/4 teaspoon of all kinds of spices

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
