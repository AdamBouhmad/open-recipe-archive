## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 cups flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful granulated sugar
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1 cup of chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 1/4 cup molasses, 1/4 cup sour milk, 1 teaspoon soda, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 2 cups flour, and 3 eggs, saving whites of 2 for filling
2. Bake in 2 layers
3. For filling: Put 1 cupful granulated sugar on to boil and let boil to a thick syrup
4. Add the beaten whites of 2 eggs with 1 cup of chopped raisins
5. Stir until right thickness to spread on cake
6. Put between layers as well as on top
