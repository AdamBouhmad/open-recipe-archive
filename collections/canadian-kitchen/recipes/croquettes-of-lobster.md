## Ingredients

- Meat of 1 fine lobster
- 2 tablespoons of butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 good teaspoonful of lemon juice
- 1/4 cup of fine breadcrumbs
- Pinch of mace and lemon peel
- 1 teaspoonful of anchovy sauce
- Yolks of 2 raw eggs beaten very light
- Yolks of 2 eggs, boiled
- Salt and cayenne pepper
- Cottolene for frying

## Directions

1. Mince the meat, work in the butter, melted, but not hot
2. Add the seasoning, the raw eggs, and lastly the breadcrumbs
3. Make into oblong balls, set on the ice for two hours
4. Fry quickly in deep cottolene
5. Drain them of every drop of fat by rolling each, for an instant, very lightly upon a hot, clean cloth
6. Be sure your dish is well heated
