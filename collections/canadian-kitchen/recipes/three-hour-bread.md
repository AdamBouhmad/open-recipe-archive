## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 cup flour
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 3/4 cup salt
- potato water
- 1 quart of mashed potatoes
- 3 quarts of boiling water
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake

## Directions

1. Scald flour, white sugar and salt with the water of the potatoes, add 1 quart of mashed potatoes and 3 quarts of boiling water and when cool enough add 1 White Swan Yeast Cake.
2. Set in a warm place to rise over night.
3. In the morning put this yeast into your flour (have the flour warm), mix stiff, let rise 1 hour, mix down, let rise 1 hour, then put in pans.
4. Let rise and bake.
