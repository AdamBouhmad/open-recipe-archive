## Ingredients

- 1 quart beans (Lima or white beans)
- 1/4 pound salt pork
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon mustard
- 1/8 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and soak beans over night
2. In the morning pour off the water and rinse well
3. Cover with fresh water
4. Bring to boiling point and simmer until skins will burst; this is best determined by taking a few in the hand and blowing on them; if skins burst, beans are cooked
5. Drain beans and throw away the water
6. Scald rind of pork, scrape and cut in two
7. Half of it put in bottom of bean crock, the other half reserve for the top
8. Put beans in the crock and over them pour the seasoning mixed with 1 cup boiling water
9. Add enough more boiling water to cover beans
10. Bake in covered bean pot in a slow oven 6 or 8 hours
11. Uncover the pot the last hour that pork rind may become brown and crisp
