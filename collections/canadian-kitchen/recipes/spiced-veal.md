## Ingredients

- 4 pounds veal
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- 4 small crackers
- 1 egg
- a lump of butter nearly the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Chop veal fine and season highly with salt, pepper, cloves and cinnamon
2. Add 4 small crackers rolled out, 1 egg, and a lump of butter nearly the size of an egg
3. Mix thoroughly together
4. Press in a baking tin and bake
5. Slice when cold
