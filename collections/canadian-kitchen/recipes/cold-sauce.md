## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 6 large onions
- 3 red peppers
- 5 cups grated horseradish
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 4 cups vinegar
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- ginger
- mustard
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Peel tomatoes
2. Chop onions
3. Don't cook this sauce
4. Keep in open jars in a cool cellar
