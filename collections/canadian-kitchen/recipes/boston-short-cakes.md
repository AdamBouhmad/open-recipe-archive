## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- a little shortening
- handful white sugar
- 1 egg
- sweet milk or cream
- a little salt
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1 egg well and fill the cup with sweet milk or cream
2. For filling: beat 1 egg, 1 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup flour
3. Roll dough out and spread with filling
4. Roll up and cut in pieces the same as jelly cake and bake
