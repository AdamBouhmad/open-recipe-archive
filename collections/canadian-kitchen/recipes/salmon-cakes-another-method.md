## Ingredients

- 1 box salmon
- 4 biscuits rolled
- 3 eggs
- salt
- butter for frying
- stale bread crumbs (optional, may be used instead of biscuits)

## Directions

1. Make in small flat balls
2. Salt and fry in butter till light brown
3. Note: any fish left over from roasting or frying may be used the same way
