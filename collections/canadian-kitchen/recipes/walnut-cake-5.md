## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup walnuts
- 3/4 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and add the salt, sugar and nuts
2. Stir well together
3. Add the milk and flour
4. Butter tins and set in warm place for 20 minutes
5. Bake in a moderate oven for 20 or 30 minutes
