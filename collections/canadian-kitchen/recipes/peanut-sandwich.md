## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 8 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup of sweet cream
- 10 cents of peanuts
- fresh slices of bread

## Directions

1. Cook eggs, vinegar, sugar, mustard, and salt until thick. Stir in sweet cream. Shell peanuts and put through a meat chopper. Stir in dressing and spread on fresh slices of bread.
