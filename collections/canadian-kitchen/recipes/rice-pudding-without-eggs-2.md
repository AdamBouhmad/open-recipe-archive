## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice
- 1 1/2 quarts milk
- grated nutmeg
- brown sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Wash 3/4 cup rice
2. Add 1 1/2 quarts milk
3. Add grated nutmeg
4. Sweeten to taste with brown sugar
5. Bake very slowly and stir well once in a while
