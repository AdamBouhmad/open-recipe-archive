## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and nutmeg
- flour to mix and roll

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out
3. Bake
