## Ingredients

- peas
- slightly salted boiling water
- butter (a lump the size of an egg)
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Shell and wash; put them in slightly salted boiling water, and cook them in this for twenty-five minutes.
2. Drain well, turn into a hot dish, put a lump of butter the size of an egg upon them and a little pepper and salt.
