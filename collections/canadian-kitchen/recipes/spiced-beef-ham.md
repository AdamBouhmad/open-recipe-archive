## Ingredients

- 24 pounds beef ham
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 pound salt
- 3/4 pound pepper
- 3/4 pound allspice
- 1 ounce saltpetre

## Directions

1. Mix together salt, sugar, pepper, allspice and saltpetre
2. Rub over meat
3. Pack in a jar very closely
4. Turn to rub every day for ten days
5. Then hang up to dry
