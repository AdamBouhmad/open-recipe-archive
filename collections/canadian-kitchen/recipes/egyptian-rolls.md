## Ingredients

- cabbage outer green leaves
- 1 tablespoon chopped meat
- salted water
- cream sauce or English drawn butter

## Directions

1. Scald the outside green leaves, remove the midrbs
2. Put a tablespoon chopped meat in each and roll it up
3. Cook these in salted water and serve with a cream sauce or English drawn butter
