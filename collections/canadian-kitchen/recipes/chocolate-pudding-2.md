## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup grated Cowan's Perfection Chocolate
- 1 1/2 pints sweet milk
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- butter size of walnut
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve chocolate in milk
2. Add cornstarch and eggs
3. Boil for 8 or 10 minutes
4. Put in buttered cups to cool
5. Eat with cream and sugar
