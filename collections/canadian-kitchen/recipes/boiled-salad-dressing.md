## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1/4 tablespoonful flour
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls lemon juice
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups vinegar
- Dash of cayenne

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients and add to yolks of eggs slightly beaten
2. Beat butter and flour to a paste and put on stove till it bubbles
3. Add vinegar, stir in egg, take from stove
4. Beat in as much whipped cream as is necessary to make desired consistency
5. Add lemon last, slowly
