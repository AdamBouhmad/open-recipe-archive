## Ingredients

- 2 qts flour
- 1 yeast cake
- 1 pt milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls lard
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix at 9 o'clock with half of the flour
2. At 12 stir in rest of flour
3. At 2.30 knead well
4. At 3.30 roll about a quarter of an inch thick, cut and spread lightly with melted butter, and double over
5. Let them rise until 5.40
6. Bake twenty minutes in quick oven
