## Ingredients

- turkey
- oyster stuffing
- salted boiling water
- pickled salt pork
- oyster or celery sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare as you would for baking or roasting
2. Fill with an oyster stuffing, made as the above
3. Tie the legs and wings close to the body
4. Place in salted boiling water with the breast downward
5. Skim it off and boil about two hours, but not till the skin breaks
6. Serve with oyster or celery sauce
7. Boil a nicely pickled piece of salt pork, and serve at table a thin slice to each plate
