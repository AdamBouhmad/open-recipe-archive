## Ingredients

- 6 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons lard or butter
- a pinch of salt
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 egg beaten well

## Directions

1. Mix 6 cups flour, 3 teaspoons baking powder, 2 tablespoons lard or butter, a pinch of salt well to make a soft dough
2. Roll out thin
3. Spread with filling of 1 cup white sugar and 1 egg beaten well
4. Roll like a jelly roll
5. Cut in slices 1 inch thick
6. Flour the pans well and bake in a hot oven
