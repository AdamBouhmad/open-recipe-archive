## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two teaspoons cornstarch
- one-quarter teaspoon baking powder
- three tablespoons of milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks separately.
2. Stir in whites last with the cornstarch, baking powder, and milk, seasoning to taste.
