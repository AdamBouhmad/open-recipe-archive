## Ingredients

- 1 pt. new milk
- 1 box gelatine
- 1/4 lb. white sugar
- 4 eggs (whites and yolks beaten separately)
- 2 teaspoonfuls vanilla
- a little almond extract

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in a little water till very soft.
2. Boil the milk, stir in gelatine, then sugar, then the well-beaten yolks.
3. Stir well, and let the mixture thicken.
4. Put in flavoring and boil till the mixture separates.
5. Take from the fire, beat in the whites (previously beaten stiff).
6. Stir lightly together and pour into a mould.
