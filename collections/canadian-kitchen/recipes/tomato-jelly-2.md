## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1 onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoonful gelatine

## Directions

1. Cook till tomatoes are soft, then strain
2. Dissolve one tablespoonful gelatine in half a cup of cold water
3. Pour the tomatoes over the gelatine while hot
4. Pour into moulds and cool
5. For sauce: One tablespoonful grated horseradish, four tablespoonfuls whipped cream, two tablespoonfuls of vinegar
6. Salt and cayenne to taste
