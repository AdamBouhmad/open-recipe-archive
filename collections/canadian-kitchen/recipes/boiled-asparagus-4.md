## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the stalks and lay them in cold water for half an hour.
2. Tie into rather loose bundles with soft string, and cook in hot, salted water for half an hour.
3. If desired, undo the strings and arrange the stalks upon a hot dish.
