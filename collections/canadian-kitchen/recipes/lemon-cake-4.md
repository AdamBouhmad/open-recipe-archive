## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon lard
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1 egg
- grated rind of lemon
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup currants
- flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix butter, lard and sugar
2. Add egg
3. Add grated rind of lemon
4. Add sweet milk
5. Add currants
6. Add flour enough to make a stiff batter and baking powder
7. Mix and bake
