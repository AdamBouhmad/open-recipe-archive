## Ingredients

- sturgeon
- force-meat
- bread
- butter
- brown gravy
- anchovy
- Seville orange
- lemon

## Directions

1. Put a good-sized piece in a large cradle-spit (five or six pounds will make a handsome dish for the head of the table).
2. Stuff it with force-meat.
3. Keep it at the fire for two or three hours, but remove the skin.
4. Cover it with crumbs of bread, and brown it with the salamander.
5. Baste it constantly with butter, and serve with a good brown gravy, an anchovy, a squeeze of Seville orange or lemon, and a glass of wine.
