## Ingredients

- White of 1 egg
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 5 tablespoons wheat
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat white until stiff
2. Add gradually the sugar
3. Add wheat, then vanilla
4. Beat constantly
5. Drop on buttered sheet
6. Bake 12 to 15 minutes in slow oven
