## Ingredients

- 1 cup noodles
- boiling water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup hot milk
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- 1 cup noodle stock

## Directions

1. Cook noodles in boiling salted water 20 minutes or until soft
2. Drain, reserving 1 cup stock
3. For sauce: put butter, flour, salt and pepper into a saucepan
4. Stir over the fire until it froths
5. Add the milk and stir constantly until it thickens and boils
6. Add grated cheese and stir until well mixed
7. Serve with noodles
