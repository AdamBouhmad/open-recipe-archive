## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- ¼ cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- ½ teaspoon soda
- whites of 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar thoroughly
2. Add milk
3. Sift flour with cream tartar and soda
4. Add the stiffly beaten whites of eggs, folding them in lightly
5. Bake in a moderate oven 30 minutes or more
