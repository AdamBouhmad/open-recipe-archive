## Ingredients

- 1 cup tapioca
- 4 cups cold water
- 1 pound dates stoned and chopped
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 dessertspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Soak 1 cup tapioca in 4 cups cold water over night
2. Add 1 pound dates stoned and chopped, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1 teaspoon salt
3. Steam 1 hour
4. After taking from stove add 1 dessertspoon vanilla
5. Serve with cream
