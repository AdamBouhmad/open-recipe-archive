## Ingredients

- tender chicken
- water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- lard or butter
- milk or cream
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a tender chicken in pieces.
2. Dip the pieces in water.
3. Sprinkle them with salt and pepper, and roll them in flour.
4. Saute them in a tablespoonful of lard or butter, browning both sides.
5. Remove and add to the pan a tablespoonful of flour.
6. Cook it for a minute without browning, stirring all the time, and add a cupful of milk or cream.
7. Stir until it is a little thickened.
8. Strain.
9. Mix into it a tablespoonful of chopped parsley.
10. Place the sauce on the serving-dish and arrange the pieces of chicken on it.
