## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- a dash of cayenne pepper
- butter size of a walnut
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 cup vinegar and water mixed (more water than vinegar)

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Cook in double boiler till thick
