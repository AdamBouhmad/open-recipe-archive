## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 4 quarts water
- 1 cupful rice
- 2 sticks celery
- 1 sprig parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Place the fowl cut into pieces in a saucepan with four quarts of cold water
2. When it comes to the boiling-point, draw it aside and let it simmer for three hours
3. Add one thick slice of onion, two sticks of celery, one sprig of parsley, and one cupful of rice
4. Simmer for another hour
5. Strain and let the soup stand until the grease can be taken off the top
6. Remove the meat, bones, and vegetables from the strainer and press the rice through the sieve
7. Stir this into the soup
8. Season with salt and pepper and heat again before serving
