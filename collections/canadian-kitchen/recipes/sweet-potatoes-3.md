## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Wash and scrub the potatoes and put them in boiling water.
2. Cook until they can be pierced with a fork, then pour off the water.
3. Cover the pot with a cloth, and draw it to the side of the range to let the potatoes steam for ten minutes.
4. Peel them before serving.
