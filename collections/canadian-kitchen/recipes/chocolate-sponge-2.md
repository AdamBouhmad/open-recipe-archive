## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls milk
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- 1/4 cup flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the sugar
2. add the grated chocolate and milk by turns
3. put the baking powder into the flour and add them to the mixture
4. and lastly the beaten whites of the eggs
5. Steam an hour and a quarter
6. Serve with whipped cream
