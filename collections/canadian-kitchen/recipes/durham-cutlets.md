## Ingredients

- 3 cups cold left-over beef
- 1 small onion
- 1 cup milk
- 0.5 cup gravy
- 0.5 tablespoon flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Grind beef fine with onion
2. Heat milk and gravy
3. Thicken with flour
4. When cold, mix with the meat
5. Set away to cool in a mould
6. Cut in slices and fry
