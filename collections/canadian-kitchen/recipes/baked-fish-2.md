## Ingredients

- fish of three or four pounds
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- lemon
- pork
- melted butter
- lard
- brown paper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Procure a fish of three or four pounds.
2. Season with one heaping tablespoonful of salt and one teaspoonful of pepper.
3. Rub the seasoning well in and outside the fish.
4. Place the fish with two sliced onions on a large dish.
5. Sprinkle over the juice of one large lemon.
6. Cover and set aside for one hour.
7. Lay the fish in a baking-pan with four thin slices of pork under it and three slices of pork on top.
8. Pour one tablespoonful of melted butter over.
9. Bake forty-five minutes.
10. Serve in a hot dish garnished with lemon cut into quarters and parsley.
