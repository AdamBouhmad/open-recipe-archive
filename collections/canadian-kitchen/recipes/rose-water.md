## Ingredients

- Attar of rose, twelve drops
- White sugar, half an ounce
- Carbonate magnesia, two drachms
- Water, one quart
- Proof spirit, two ounces

## Directions

1. Rub attar of rose with half an ounce of white sugar and two drachms carbonate magnesia
2. Add gradually one quart of water and two ounces of proof spirit
3. Filter through paper
