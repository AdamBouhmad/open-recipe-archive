## Ingredients

- cold boiled corned beef
- cold boiled potatoes
- onion
- butter
- pepper and salt
- rich milk or cream

## Directions

1. Chop fine cold boiled corned beef; to one pint meat add one pint and a little more of cold boiled potatoes, chopped, though not too fine.
2. A little onion can be used if liked.
3. Have ready a pan with a good piece of butter in it, put in hash, season with pepper and salt, then add rich milk or cream, enough to moisten.
4. Cover and make hot.
