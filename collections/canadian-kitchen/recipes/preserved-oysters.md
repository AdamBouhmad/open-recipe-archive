## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt
- water
- whole peppers
- mace
- cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put oysters in salt and water to cover them
2. Let them come to a boil, then skim them into cold water
3. Scald whole peppers, mace and cloves in a little vinegar, the quantity of these to be determined by the number of oysters
4. When oysters are cold put into a stone jar with layers of spice between them
5. Cover with liquor in which they are cooked
6. Season to taste
