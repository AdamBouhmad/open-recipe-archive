## Ingredients

- scraps of meat
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 pint water
- 1 or 2 carrots

## Directions

1. Cut scraps of meat into small pieces
2. Fry a nice brown in a buttered pan
3. Fry an onion or two with it
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Rub into it 1 tablespoon of flour
6. Add 1 pint of water and 1 or 2 carrots sliced fine
7. Simmer 2 hours
