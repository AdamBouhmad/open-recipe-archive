## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 3/4 pound butter well beaten
- whites and yolks of 8 eggs beaten separately to a stiff froth
- 1 pound flour
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- nutmeg or mace
- 4 ounces finely sliced citron
- 4 ounces almonds blanched

## Directions

1. Beat butter well
2. Cream butter and sugar together
3. Beat whites and yolks of 8 eggs separately to a stiff froth
4. Mix flour dry with baking powder
5. Combine all ingredients
6. Add nutmeg or mace for flavor
7. Stir in citron and almonds before baking
