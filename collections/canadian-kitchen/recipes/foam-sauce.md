## Ingredients

- Half cup butter
- one cup white sugar
- one egg
- six tablespoonfuls of milk
- one wineglass of brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a froth; add yolk of egg and milk.
2. Set on a slow fire; add brandy and white of egg well beaten just before serving.
