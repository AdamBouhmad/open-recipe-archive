## Ingredients

- 1 basket grapes
- water
- 2 pounds sugar for each basket
- paraffine wax

## Directions

1. Take the contents of 1 basket grapes, wash and stem
2. Put on stove with just enough water to cover
3. Let come to a boil, remove from fire and pour into cheese cloth bag
4. When cool enough, press out the juice with the hands
5. Put juice back on stove with 2 pounds sugar for each basket
6. Let come to a boil
7. Bottle while hot, cork and seal with paraffine wax, to exclude the air
