## Ingredients

- ⅜ cup butter
- 1⅓ cup flour
- ¾ cup grated cheese
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon mustard
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt the butter
2. Add the flour and stir over the fire until frothy
3. Add the milk and stir constantly until it thickens and boils
4. Stir in the seasonings and cheese
5. Stir in the beaten egg-yolks and take from the fire at once
6. Stand the saucepan aside and let the mixture cool
7. Beat the egg whites stiff
8. Add a little to the cheese mixture and stir it in
9. Add remainder and fold it in lightly but thoroughly
10. Turn the whole into a buttered baking dish
11. Bake in a moderate oven until golden (usually about an hour)
12. If the oven is too hot, stand the baking dish in a pan of boiling water while baking
