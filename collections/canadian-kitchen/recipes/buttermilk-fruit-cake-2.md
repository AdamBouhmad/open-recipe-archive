## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 1/2 cups buttermilk
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 2 teaspoons spices according to taste
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Bake in a moderate oven
