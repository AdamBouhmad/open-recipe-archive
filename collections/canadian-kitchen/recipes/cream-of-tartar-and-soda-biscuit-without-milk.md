## Ingredients

- 1 qt. flour
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls butter
- 2 cups cold water
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream tartar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- ½ teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Mix cream tartar with flour and dissolve soda in water
2. Stir the dissolved soda in the cold water
3. Mix the dough very quickly, having it just stiff enough to handle and roll
4. Bake in a quick oven
