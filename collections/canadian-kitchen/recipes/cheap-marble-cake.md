## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar and milk
- 2¾ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup lard or butter
- 2 tablespoons dark molasses
- 2 tablespoons chocolate
- spice

## Directions

1. Sift flour with baking powder
2. Take ½ the mixture, add 2 tablespoons dark molasses and 2 tablespoons chocolate, spice to taste
3. Pour light and dark at one time in cake pan
4. Bake in moderate oven
