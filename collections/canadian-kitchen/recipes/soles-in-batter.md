## Ingredients

- 1 pair of soles
- 1/4 lb. flour
- Pepper and salt
- 2 oz. butter
- Lard or drippings for frying
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 2 eggs
- Milk to mix it

## Directions

1. Fillet the soles, and cut each fillet in two pieces
2. Sprinkle them with pepper and salt
3. Make a light batter with the above ingredients, taking care that it is not very thin
4. Dip each piece of fish into this, and fry quickly in boiling fat to a golden brown
5. Arrange them in a circle, one overlapping the other, on a hot dish
6. Garnish with fresh or fried parsley
7. Serve as soon as cooked
8. Melted butter may be sent to table with them in a tureen, if liked
