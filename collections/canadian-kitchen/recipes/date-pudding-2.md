## Ingredients

- 1 pound dates
- 1/2 pound chopped suet
- a little salt
- 1.5 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- cold water

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pound dates, 1/2 pound chopped suet, a little salt, 1.5 cups flour, and 2 teaspoons baking powder
2. Mix with cold water very stiff
3. Steam 2.5 hours
