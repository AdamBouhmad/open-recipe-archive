## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar
- peanuts, shelled and broken up

## Directions

1. Put sugar in granite dish on a hot fire and stir till all is a golden brown
2. Have peanuts shelled and broken up on a buttered platter
3. Pour the hot melted sugar over
4. Set away to cool
5. Break up into small pieces
