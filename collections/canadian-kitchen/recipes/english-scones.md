## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins
- 4 cups flour
- 1 cup lard
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix with sweet milk to pack all together and cut with three-cornered cake cutter
