## Ingredients

- 1 head cabbage cut fine
- 1 dozen ears of corn cut off
- salt
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- a little pepper
- vinegar enough to cover

## Directions

1. Mix cabbage and corn with a little salt and let stand a few hours
2. Add brown sugar, mustard, and pepper, and vinegar enough to cover
3. Cook until done and put in sealers
