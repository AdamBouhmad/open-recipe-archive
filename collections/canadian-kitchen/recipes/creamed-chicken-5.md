## Ingredients

- 1 chicken of 4 1/2 pounds, or 2 of 6 pounds
- 4 sweetbreads
- 1 can of mushrooms
- 4 coffee cups cream
- 4 large tablespoons butter
- 5 even tablespoons flour
- a small half of grated onion
- a very little grated nutmeg
- black and red pepper
- bread crumbs
- pieces of butter

## Directions

1. Boil chicken and sweetbreads, and when cold cut up as for salad
2. In a saucepan put 4 coffee cups cream
3. In another put 4 large tablespoons butter and 5 even tablespoons of flour
4. Stir until melted, then pour on the hot cream, stirring until it thickens
5. Flavor with a small half of grated onion and a very little grated nutmeg
6. Season highly with black and red pepper
7. Put chicken and ingredients together with sweetbreads and mushrooms (which if large should be cut in 4 pieces) in a baking dish
8. Cover with bread crumbs and pieces of butter, and bake 20 minutes
