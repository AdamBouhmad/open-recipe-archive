## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 1/4 cups sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- butter rubbed in to make like pie-crust
- currants
- chopped lemon peel
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, flour and baking powder together
2. Rub in butter to make like pie-crust
3. Add currants and chopped lemon peel
4. Mix to a very stiff batter with a little milk
5. Drop by spoonfuls on greased paper and bake
