## Ingredients

- beef dripping
- onion
- liver
- boiling water
- brown gravy
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Place in a stewpan enough beef dripping to fry either a large onion or several small ones
2. Cut your liver in nice squares, not too thin
3. Brown them nicely in the stewpan
4. Add a little boiling water and a little brown gravy if you have it
5. Stew till tender and season to taste
6. Before dishing, thicken the gravy with cornstarch
