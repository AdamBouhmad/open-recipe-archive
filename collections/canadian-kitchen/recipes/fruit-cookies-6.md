## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups white sugar
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon syrup
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1/2 cup raisins
- some lemon peel
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour enough to roll
- cocoanut

## Directions

1. Mix and roll
2. Sprinkle with cocoanut
