## Ingredients

- 7 lbs. grapes
- 3½ lbs. sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- good tablespoonful ground cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Squeeze out the pulp from grapes
2. Heat up pulp without any water
3. Let it come to a boil, then put through a sieve
4. Take skins and pulp, and mix with sugar, vinegar, ground cloves and cinnamon
5. Cook till thick
6. Watch it constantly
