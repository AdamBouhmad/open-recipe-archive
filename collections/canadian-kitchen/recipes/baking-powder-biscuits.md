## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 dessertspoons butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- cold water or milk

## Directions

1. Sift together 2 cups flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 2 dessertspoons butter, 1 tablespoon sugar
2. Mix with cold water or milk to a stiff dough with a spoon
3. Do not work much with the hands, just enough to roll out
4. Roll quickly and bake in a hot oven
