## Ingredients

- oysters
- fine cracker-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- oyster liquor
- cream
- sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a greased bake-dish with oysters
2. Cover the oysters with fine cracker-crumbs
3. Sprinkle these with pepper, salt, and bits of butter
4. Lay in more oysters and go on in this order until all are in
5. The top layer should be of crumbs and well buttered
6. Pour over each layer of oysters as it goes in, a few spoonfuls of oyster liquor
7. Upon the crumbs pour the same quantity of cream
8. Bake, covered, in a quick oven until hot all through
9. Uncover and brown lightly
10. Serve with sliced lemon
