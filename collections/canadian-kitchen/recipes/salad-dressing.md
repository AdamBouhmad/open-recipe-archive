## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1-1/2 tablespoonful mixed mustard
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful pepper
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 cup vinegar
- butter size of egg

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs together
2. Add sugar, mixed mustard, water, vinegar, butter melted, salt and pepper
3. Cook over steam until it thickens
