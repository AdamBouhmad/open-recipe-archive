## Ingredients

- 3 cups finely mashed potatoes
- 1 cup cold meat
- 1 small onion
- 1 beaten egg
- salt
- pepper
- powdered sage
- flour
- butter, roast or sausage dripping

## Directions

1. Put 3 cups of finely mashed potatoes into a mixing bowl
2. Add 1 cup of cold meat previously run through the mincer
3. Add 1 small onion cut fine
4. Add one beaten egg
5. Add salt, pepper and powdered sage to taste
6. Mix all thoroughly
7. Form with the hands well floured into small, round cakes about the size of a cake cutter, and about 1 inch thick
8. Fry to a delicate brown on either side in butter, roast or sausage dripping
