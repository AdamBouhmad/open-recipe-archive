## Ingredients

- 2 gallons green tomatoes
- 12 onions
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop fine all ingredients
2. Cook 1 hour
