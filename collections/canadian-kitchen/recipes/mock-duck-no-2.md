## Ingredients

- dressing as for turkey
- 1 round of steak
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare dressing as for turkey
2. Pound a round of steak and spread dressing over it
3. Lap over the ends
4. Roll steak tightly and tie closely
5. Spread butter over steak after tying
6. Lay on a rack in bake pan
7. Bake as a turkey or duck, basting often
8. Bake for 3.5 hours in a brisk oven
9. Pour gravy over it and serve hot
