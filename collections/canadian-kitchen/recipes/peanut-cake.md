## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1¾ cups flour
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 cup of finely chopped peanuts

## Directions

1. Cream together the butter and sugar
2. Add the beaten yolk of the egg
3. Put in half the flour, then the milk, then the rest of the flour
4. The flour and baking powder should be sifted together twice
5. Lastly fold in 1 cup of finely chopped peanuts and the stiffly beaten white of the egg
6. Bake in a loaf
