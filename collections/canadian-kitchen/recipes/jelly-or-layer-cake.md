## Ingredients

- Whites of 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 2 cups milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1½ cups sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 4 yolks
- ½ cup milk
- 2½ cups flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the whites stiff and add last (after the flour)
2. flavor with a few drops of lemon or vanilla
3. By adding fruit will make a very good fruit cake
