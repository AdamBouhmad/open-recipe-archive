## Ingredients

- slices of sponge cake
- halves of apricots
- whites of two or three eggs
- sugar
- whipped cream
- apricot juice

## Directions

1. Upon some slices of sponge cake place halves of apricots (the round sides uppermost)
2. whip the whites of two or three eggs to a snow frost with sugar
3. Place this around the apricot halves so as to make them resemble poached eggs
4. Add a little of the apricot juice as flavoring
5. Whipped cream, if obtainable, is even better than the meringue
