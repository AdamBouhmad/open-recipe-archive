## Ingredients

- one pint of bread sponge
- beaten whites of 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter

## Directions

1. Add beaten whites of 2 eggs, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup butter to one pint of bread sponge
2. Mix well and set in a warm place to rise
3. Roll out to a thickness of 1/4 inch
4. Cut out with cake cutter
5. Butter top of one and place another on top
6. Set aside to rise
7. Bake for one-half hour
8. Currants may be added if desired
