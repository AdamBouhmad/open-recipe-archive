## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound walnuts
- 1/2 pound dates
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 cups sweet milk
- 4 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons cream tartar
- 2 teaspoons soda
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Let rise 1/2 hour
3. Cook 3/4 hour in oven hot enough to brown
