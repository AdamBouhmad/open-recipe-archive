## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour for a thin batter

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake in a shallow dish in a moderate oven
3. Ice this cake with whipped cream, sugar and vanilla
