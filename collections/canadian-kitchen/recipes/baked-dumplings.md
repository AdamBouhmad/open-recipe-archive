## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- milk
- apple, peach or any desired fruit
- 1 cup sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Wet with milk and roll out into 8 dumplings, putting apple, peach or any desired fruit inside
2. Lay in bake dish and sprinkle 1 cup sugar and a little cinnamon over top
3. Add little bits of butter
4. Cover with boiling water and bake
