## Ingredients

- Three-quarters cupful of pumpkin (well mashed)
- one and a half cupfuls of milk
- half cupful of sugar
- one egg
- half a teaspoonful of ginger
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Mix together three-quarters cupful of pumpkin (well mashed), one and a half cupfuls of milk, half cupful of sugar, one egg, half a teaspoonful of ginger, nutmeg, cinnamon and cloves.
