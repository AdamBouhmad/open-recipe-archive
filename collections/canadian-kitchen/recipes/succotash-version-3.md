## Ingredients

- 1 pint of beans
- 1 quart of green corn
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and boil beans for nearly an hour
2. add a quart of green corn, salt and pepper to taste, a large tablespoon butter, a tablespoon sugar
3. cook until done
4. Dried corn may be used by soaking for a while
