## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a cup cornmeal
- two cupfuls flour
- half a cupful sugar
- half a cupful butter
- two eggs
- one cupful milk
- three tablespoonfuls baking powder
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Bake in gem-pans
