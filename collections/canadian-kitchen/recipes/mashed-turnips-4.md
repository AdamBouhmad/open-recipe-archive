## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, cut in slices
2. Cook in boiling water salted until soft (30-45 minutes)
3. Drain
4. Mash
5. Season with butter, salt and pepper
