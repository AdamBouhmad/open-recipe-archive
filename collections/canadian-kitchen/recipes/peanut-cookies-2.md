## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup chopped peanuts

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together and bake.
