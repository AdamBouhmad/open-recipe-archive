## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Put 2 quarts water and 2 tablespoons salt in kettle, let come to a boil
2. Peel tomatoes and put the whole tomatoes in liquid, cook 20 minutes
3. Skim out tomatoes and fill jars, seal, leaving liquid in kettle
4. The same liquid may be used for another kettle of tomatoes by adding a little more salt, but no more water
