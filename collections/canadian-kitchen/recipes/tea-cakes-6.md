## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 teaspoon soda sifted
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup currants
- 1/2 orange peel cut fine
- sour milk

## Directions

1. Rub butter into flour with soda sifted in
2. Mix in sugar, currants, and orange peel
3. Add enough sour milk to make a soft dough
4. Roll to 1/2 inch thick
5. Cut any shape desired
6. Bake in a moderate oven for 20 minutes
7. Eat warm with maple syrup
