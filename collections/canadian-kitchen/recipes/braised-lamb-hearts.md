## Ingredients

- lamb hearts
- salt
- pepper
- Worcestershire sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe each cut of extra fat and take out partition walls
2. Stuff using recipe and tie up and sew
3. Make 3/4 cup brown sauce in dish in which heart was seared
4. Put in double roasting pan and cook in aladdin or slow oven 3 to 5 hours
5. Season with Worcestershire or tomato sauce and serve
