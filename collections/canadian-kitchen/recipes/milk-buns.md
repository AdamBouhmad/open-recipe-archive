## Ingredients

- 2 cups new milk
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 heaping tablespoon lard
- 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake or 1/4 cup hop yeast
- flour
- 1 egg
- salt

## Directions

1. Let milk come to a boil
2. Stir in sugar and lard
3. When lukewarm put in White Swan Yeast Cake or hop yeast
4. Add enough flour to make a batter as thick as for sponge cake
5. Let rise till morning and keep very warm
6. In morning put in egg and a little salt
7. Mix as stiff as soft bread
8. Let rise again till real light
9. When very light take out on bakeboard and roll out 3/8 inch thick
10. Cut in round cakes
11. Butter and turn over double
12. Pinch ends
13. Let rise 1 hour
14. Bake in quick oven
