## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls grated cheese
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 2 oz. bread
- 2 teaspoonful mustard
- 1 gill milk
- pepper and salt to taste
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the bread and milk on to boil, stir and boil until smooth, add the cheese and butter, stir over the fire for one minute.
2. Take off, add seasoning and the yolks of two eggs.
3. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth and stir them in carefully.
4. Pour into a greased baking-dish and bake fifteen minutes in a quick oven.
