## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold meat
- 1/2 cup mashed potatoes
- 1/2 teaspoon made mustard
- gravy or stock
- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Chop meat fine
2. Mix 1 cup cold meat chopped fine, 1/2 cup mashed potatoes, 1/2 teaspoon made mustard, and enough gravy or stock to moisten
3. Put in buttered baking dish
4. Mix 1 cup mashed potatoes, 2 teaspoons butter, 1 cup milk, and one egg well beaten
5. Cover this over the top of the meat
6. Bake in the oven until a nice brown
