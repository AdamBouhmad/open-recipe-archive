## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out and cut with doughnut cutter
3. Fry in hot lard
