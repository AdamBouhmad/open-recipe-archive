## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 2 cups cream
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, cream, eggs, and baking powder
2. Mix in enough flour to roll out soft
3. Fry in very hot lard
