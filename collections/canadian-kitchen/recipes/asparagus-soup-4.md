## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 pounds of veal cut fine
- a little salt pork
- 2 or 3 bunches of asparagus
- 3 quarts of water
- 1 pint of milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil one-half of the asparagus with the meat and salt pork
2. Leave the rest of the asparagus in water until about 20 minutes before serving
3. The soup should boil about 3 hours before adding the last half of the asparagus
4. Add the rest of the asparagus and boil just before serving
5. Add 1 pint of milk
6. Thicken with a little flour and season
