## Ingredients

- 1 oxtail
- 5 pints of water
- 1 onion
- 1 ounce of butter
- piece of celery or pinch of celery seed
- 1 ounce of flour
- pinch herbs
- 1 carrot
- 1 teaspoon peppercorns
- piece turnip
- 2 cloves
- 1 dessertspoon catsup
