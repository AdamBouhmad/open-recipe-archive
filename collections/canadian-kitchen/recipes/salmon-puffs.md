## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat salmon lightly
2. Season with salt, pepper and celery salt
3. Mix in bread crumbs and melted butter
4. Beat eggs lightly and add
5. Put in a buttered mould
6. Cook over boiling water until done
7. Turn out and serve with cream sauce
