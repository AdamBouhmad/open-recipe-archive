## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- sweet cream
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flavoring
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Break 2 eggs in cup, fill the cup with sweet cream and beat till light
2. Mix in 1 cup sugar
3. Add 1 3/4 cups flour
4. Add 2 teaspoons baking powder, flavoring and a pinch of salt
