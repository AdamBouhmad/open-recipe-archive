## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups honey
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sour milk
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix butter, honey, eggs and sour milk
2. Add soda, ginger and cinnamon
3. Mix with flour and bake
