## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- pinch salt

## Directions

1. boil till it thickens
2. cool, add 1 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup sweet milk, 1 teaspoon soda, 1 teaspoon cream tartar, 1 1/2 cups flour, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla, pinch salt
