## Ingredients

- 1 head celery
- 4 potatoes
- 1 carrot
- 1 Spanish onion
- 2 quarts water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the onion small and fry in butter until brown
2. Add to the water and boil for 15 minutes, then strain out the onion
3. Add the vegetables, and boil 1 hour, seasoning with pepper and salt
