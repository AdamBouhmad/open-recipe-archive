## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 6 peppers
- 6 large onions
- 4 cups celery
- 1 cup horseradish
- 1 quart vinegar
- 3/4 cup salt
- 2 ounces celery seed
- 2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Chop peppers and onions fine
2. Let drain for several hours
3. Mix with celery, horseradish, vinegar, salt, celery seed, and sugar
4. This is not cooked
5. Bottle tight
