## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 1 pt. cream

## Directions

1. Whip the cream very stiff
2. Beat the eggs and place them in a double boiler
3. Pour in the maple syrup and stir constantly until the mixture gathers on the spoon
4. Take off the stove and beat till cold
5. Stir into the whipped cream
6. Put in a freezer that has been previously packed in ice and salt
7. Let stand four hours
