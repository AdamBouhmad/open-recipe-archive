## Ingredients

- 2 quarts cabbage chopped fine
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup vinegar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- ½ teaspoons mustard

## Directions

1. Boil until thick as cream
2. Pour over cabbage while hot
3. Mix well
