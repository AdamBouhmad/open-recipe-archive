## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup grated cheese
- ⅜ teaspoon salt
- pinch of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Mix butter and flour
2. Add salt, pepper and milk
3. Cook and stir until thick as good cream
4. Remove from stove and beat in 1 cup grated cheese and yolks of 3 eggs
5. Beat in lightly the stiffly beaten whites of the eggs
6. Steam for 1 hour
