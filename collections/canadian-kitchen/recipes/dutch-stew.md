## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- A little onion
- laurel leaves
- 4 or 5 cloves
- 1 cupful of gravy or bouillon
- 1 beaten egg
- squares of cooked meat

## Directions

1. Fry together butter, vinegar, sugar, salt, onion, laurel leaves, and cloves.
2. Add a cupful of gravy or bouillon and a beaten egg.
3. When hot add squares of cooked meat.
4. A great improvement is a little red wine, about two tablespoons, in which case a little more sugar is needed.
