## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup chopped raisins
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1/4 teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful ground cloves

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. drop the eggs in (not beaten)
3. add soda to the milk
4. then part of the flour, then the fruit and the rest of the flour
