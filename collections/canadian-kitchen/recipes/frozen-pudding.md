## Ingredients

- 1 large pt. milk
- 1/2 cupful flour, scant
- 2 cupfuls sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls gelatine
- brandy
- 1 qt. cream
- 1 lb. preserved fruit
- 4 tablespoonfuls sherry wine

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in water two hours
2. Soak fruit in brandy
3. Cook milk, flour, eggs, gelatine and one cupful of sugar
4. When cool add cream, wine, and the other cup of sugar, and freeze
5. Then add fruit, and pack in bricks
