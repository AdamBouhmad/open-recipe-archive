## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds chopped fine
- 1/2 cup butter slightly warmed
- 8 tablespoons icing sugar
- 1 tablespoon brandy or cold coffee

## Directions

1. Chop blanched almonds fine and put in the oven to dry
2. Mix butter and sugar to paste
3. Add nuts and brandy or cold coffee
