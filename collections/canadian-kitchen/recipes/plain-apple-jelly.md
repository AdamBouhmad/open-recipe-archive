## Ingredients

- 1/2 a bushel of sound, firm greening apples
- cold water
- 1 fresh lemon
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Cut apples from core, skin left on
2. Wash clean
3. Put in a granite kettle with water to not quite cover
4. Cook till all are broken up
5. Empty in a jelly bag made with thin cotton
6. Let drip over night
7. Return to the fire
8. Cut up one fresh lemon and tie loosely in a thin cloth
9. Put in the juice and let boil briskly half an hour
10. Skim and take out the lemon
11. Add pound for pound granulated sugar
12. Heat sugar in the oven before adding
13. Let boil 10 or 15 minutes
14. Pour up in glasses
