## Ingredients

- 4 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup lard
- 1 egg well beaten
- milk
- 1/2 water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup flour

## Directions

1. Mix paste ingredients: flour, salt, sugar, butter, lard, egg with milk and water to a dough
2. Add soda and cream tartar
3. Make filling: mix 1 egg, 1 cup brown sugar, 3/4 cup flour
4. Roll paste out
5. Spread with filling
6. Roll up like rolly-polly pudding
7. Cut in slices crosswise
8. Leave softest part up in pan
9. Bake in quick oven
