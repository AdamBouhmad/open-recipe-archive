## Ingredients

- 2 quarts onions
- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 quarts green cucumbers
- 4 bunches celery
- 2 small or 1 large cabbage cut fine
- salt
- 1 gallon vinegar
- 3/4 pound mustard
- 1 cup flour
- 1 ounce turmeric
- 2 3/4 cups brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon celery seed

## Directions

1. Salt vegetables over night and drain well in morning
2. Put in kettle with 1 gallon vinegar
3. Cook 15 minutes
4. Mix mustard, turmeric and flour with cold vinegar and add to mixture
5. Cook 15 minutes longer
