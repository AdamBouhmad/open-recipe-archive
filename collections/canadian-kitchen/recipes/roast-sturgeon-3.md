## Ingredients

- five or six pounds sturgeon
- force-meat
- crumbs of bread
- butter
- brown gravy
- anchovy
- Seville orange or lemon
- sherry

## Directions

1. Put a good-sized piece in a large cradle-spit
2. stuff it with force-meat
3. keep it at the fire for two or three hours, but remove the skin
4. cover it with crumbs of bread, and brown it with the salamander
5. baste it constantly with butter
6. serve with a good brown gravy, an anchovy, a squeeze of Seville orange or lemon, and a glass of sherry boiled up, and poured into the dish
