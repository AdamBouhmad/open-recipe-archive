## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups strong coffee
- 1 cup milk
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon gelatine
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix coffee, milk, sugar, salt, and gelatine in double boiler
2. Reserve 1/4 cup sugar and beat with egg yolks
3. When ingredients are heating, stir in boiling mixture with egg yolks
4. Return to boiler until it begins to thicken
5. Remove from fire
6. Stir in stiffened egg whites and vanilla
7. Turn into a mould dipped in cold water
