## Ingredients

- 1 quart of stock
- 12 or 13 ounces of pulped carrot
- salt
- cayenne
- 4 good-sized carrots per quart
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Boil as many carrots as required (about four good-sized ones to each quart) till quite tender
2. Cut up the red part and rub it through a sieve
3. Weigh it and add gravy soup or good stock in the proportions of twelve or thirteen ounces of pulped carrot to each quart of stock
4. Mix it gradually and season with salt and a little cayenne
5. Let it boil up, and serve very hot, with a dish of fried bread cut into small squares
