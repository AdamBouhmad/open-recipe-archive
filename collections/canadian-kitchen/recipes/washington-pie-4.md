## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 2 eggs
- A piece of melted butter the size of an egg
- Milk
- Jam or jelly

## Directions

1. Break into a cup 2 eggs
2. Add to them a piece of melted butter the size of an egg
3. Fill the cup with milk
4. Bake in 2 large layers
5. Place jam or jelly between
