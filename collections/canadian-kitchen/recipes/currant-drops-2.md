## Ingredients

- currant-juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Use currant-juice, instead of water, to moisten a quantity of sugar
2. Put it in a pan and heat, stirring constantly
3. Be sure not to let it boil
4. Then mix a very little more sugar, let it warm with the rest a moment
5. Then, with a smooth stick, drop on paper
