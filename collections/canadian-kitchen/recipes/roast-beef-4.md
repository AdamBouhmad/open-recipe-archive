## Ingredients

- a rib piece or loin roast of seven or eight pounds
- butter or suet fat
- salt
- pepper
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Wipe the roast thoroughly all over with a clean wet towel
2. Lay it in a dripping-pan, and baste it well with butter or suet fat
3. Set it in the oven
4. Baste it frequently with its own drippings, which will make it brown and tender
5. When partly done season with salt and pepper
6. Dredge with sifted flour to give it a frothy appearance
7. Roast for about two hours' time to be properly done, leaving the inside a little rare or red — half an hour less would make the inside quite rare
8. Remove the beef to a heated dish, set where it will keep hot
