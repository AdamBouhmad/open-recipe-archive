## Ingredients

- 1 tin of tomatoes
- 1 cup of grated cheese
- dash of cayenne pepper
- salt
- lump of butter
- 4 or 5 soda biscuits

## Directions

1. Crumb four or five soda biscuits.
2. Mix one tin of tomatoes, one cup of grated cheese, dash of cayenne pepper and salt in cheese, lump of butter on top, and crumbed soda biscuits.
3. Bake in a pudding-dish in oven.
