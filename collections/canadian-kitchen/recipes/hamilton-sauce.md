## Ingredients

- 30 ripe tomatoes
- 12 large apples
- 10 large onions
- 3 green peppers
- 12 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 1 pint vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop fine
2. Cook 1 hour
