## Ingredients

- parsnips
- eggs
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mash boiled parsnips
2. Add beaten egg
3. Pile on buttered dish
4. Cover with buttered crumbs
5. Bake in the oven until the crumbs are browned
