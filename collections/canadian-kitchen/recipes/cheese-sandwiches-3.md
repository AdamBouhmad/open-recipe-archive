## Ingredients

- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1/4 lb. cheese, grated
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful pepper
- 1/4 teaspoonful mustard
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful vinegar
- biscuits or oat-cake

## Directions

1. Take the yolk of the egg and put it into a small bowl and crumble it down
2. Put into it the butter and mix it smooth with a spoon
3. Add the salt, pepper, mustard, and the cheese, mixing each well
4. Put in the tablespoonful of vinegar, which will make it the proper thickness
5. If the vinegar is not relished, then use cold water instead
6. Spread this between two biscuits or pieces of oat-cake
