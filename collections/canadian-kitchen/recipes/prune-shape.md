## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of best prunes (or figs)
- sugar
- one six-ounce packet of gelatine
- one pint of cold water
- one-half pint of boiling water
- juice of prunes
- one cup sugar
- a few drops of ratafia
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Stew prunes with a little sugar till quite tender
2. Strain the liquid from them and take out the stones
3. Dissolve gelatine in one pint of cold water
4. Add a small half pint of boiling water mixed with juice of prunes
5. add one cup sugar and a few drops of ratafia
6. Place the prunes round the mould and pour liquid over
7. Let it stand till quite cold
8. Blanched almonds are an improvement
