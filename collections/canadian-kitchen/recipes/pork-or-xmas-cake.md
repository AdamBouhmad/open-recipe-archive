## Ingredients

- 1 pound raw pork off back
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 3/4 pound citron peel
- 1/2 pound lemon peel
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup buttermilk or sour milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 heaping teaspoons cream of tartar
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 2 cups black strap
- 1 cup nut meats if desired
- flour enough to bake in loaf cake

## Directions

1. Grate the pork and beat to a cream with brown sugar
2. Add eggs well beaten and beat well together
3. Add sour cream, molasses and sour milk in which dissolve soda
4. Sift cream of tartar with flour and spices
5. Mix all well together
6. Try a little in a gem tin before baking the whole cake
7. Will make 4 or 5 cakes according to size of pans
