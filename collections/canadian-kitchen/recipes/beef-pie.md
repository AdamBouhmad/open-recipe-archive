## Ingredients

- 1 dessertspoon dripping
- 1 onion sliced
- 3/4 pound beefsteak
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 pints water
- salt and pepper
- potato and carrot
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Put dripping in a pan and heat until smoking hot
2. Add sliced onion
3. Cut beefsteak into bits and brown
4. Shake in flour and brown
5. Add water and stir slowly
6. Season and cover
7. Simmer gently for 2 hours
8. Add bits of potato and carrot if liked
9. For biscuits: sift 3 cups flour with 2 teaspoons baking powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt
10. Rub in 1 tablespoon butter and make into dough with sweet milk
11. Divide dough in two and pat out (do not knead or roll)
12. Cut as you would pie and bake
13. Break open biscuits on a platter and pour meat mixture over
14. Serve
