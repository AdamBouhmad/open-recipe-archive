## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup lard
- 4 cups flour
- 4 1/2 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups raisins
- 10 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in the milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix well
2. Drop a teaspoonful for each cookie, leaving plenty of room to spread
3. Bake
