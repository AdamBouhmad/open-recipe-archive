## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 cup cracker crumbs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cup molasses
- 1/6 cup lemon juice or vinegar
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 3/4 cup butter or 3 1/2 cup suet
- 1 egg
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Mix in order given
2. Bake between crusts or cook, add eggs at last and serve in baked crusts
