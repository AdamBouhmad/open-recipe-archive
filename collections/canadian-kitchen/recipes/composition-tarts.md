## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 cups table molasses
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Fill tart shells
3. Bake in medium hot oven
