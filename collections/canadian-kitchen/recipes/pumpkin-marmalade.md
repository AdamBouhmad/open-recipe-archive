## Ingredients

- 6 pounds pumpkins cut very fine
- 7 pounds sugar
- 3 lemons
- 6 oranges with rind

## Directions

1. Put the sugar on the pumpkins over night
2. Put lemons and oranges in and boil 2 3/4 hours
3. Stir while boiling
