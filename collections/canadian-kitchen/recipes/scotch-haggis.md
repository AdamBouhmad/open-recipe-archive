## Ingredients

- bag and pluck of a sheep
- heart, lights and liver
- 1 pound of fresh suet
- 2 handfuls of oatmeal
- few onions
- black pepper
- allspice
- salt

## Directions

1. Procure the bag and pluck of a sheep
2. Clean the bag very carefully
3. Parboil the heart, lights and liver for an hour and a half
4. Let them cool, and then mince very fine
5. Mince also a pound of fresh suet and grate the parboiled liver
6. Mix this along with two handfuls of oatmeal (previously browned in the oven), a few onions, black pepper, allspice and salt to taste
7. Take the bag and wash it first with cold water, then with boiling water
8. When quite clean fill in the mince, but do not let it be more than half full, else the bag will burst
9. Add a little of the liquid in which the meat was parboiled, and sew up the bag
10. Put it in boiling water and prick it frequently with a large needle to let the air escape
11. Boil it for three hours with a plate in the bottom of the pot
