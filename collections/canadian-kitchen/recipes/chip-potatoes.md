## Ingredients

- long-shaped potatoes
- boiling fat

## Directions

1. Peel and boil some long-shaped potatoes till nearly done
2. Strain
3. Cut each potato lengthwise into 4 pieces
4. Put into a baking dish in boiling fat
5. Bake in oven till nicely browned
