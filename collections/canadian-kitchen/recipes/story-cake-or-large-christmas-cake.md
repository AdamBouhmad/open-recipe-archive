## Ingredients

- 9 eggs
- 5 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups blackstrap
- 1 1/2 pounds butter
- 2 1/2 cups buttermilk
- 3 cups sour cream
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cloves
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 1/2 grated nutmegs
- 3 lemon peels chopped fine
- 7 cups washed currants
- 7 cups stoned raisins
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients with flour to make a very stiff batter
2. Bake in slow oven
3. Can be iced with the beaten yolks of the eggs and white sugar, or with icing sugar and decorated with candies and other ornaments
