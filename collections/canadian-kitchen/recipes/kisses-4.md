## Ingredients

- Whites of 2 eggs
- 1 cup powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat whites of 2 eggs to a stiff froth
2. Add 1 cup powdered sugar
3. Beat this together 15 minutes
4. Drop in teaspoonfuls on brown paper
5. Put on a dripping pan turned bottom side up in the oven
6. Bake until a crust forms on them
