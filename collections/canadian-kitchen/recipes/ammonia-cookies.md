## Ingredients

- 1 pint granulated sugar
- 1 pint good sweet cream
- 1 ounce baking ammonia
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- lemon essence or vanilla
- flour enough to roll out and cut

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients and roll out and cut
