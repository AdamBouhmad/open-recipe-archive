## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup walnuts
- vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Crumble together 2 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup butter
2. Take out 1/3 of a cup and keep aside
3. To the balance add 1 egg, 1 cup sour milk, 1 teaspoon soda, 1 cup raisins, 1 cup walnuts, vanilla to taste
4. Put in pan and sprinkle the part kept out on top
