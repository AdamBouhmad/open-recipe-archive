## Ingredients

- 1 pt. Indian meal
- 1 qt. milk or water
- 1 pt. Graham flour
- 1 cup wheat flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- ½ cup yeast, or 1 cake

## Directions

1. Scald Indian meal with milk or water; boiling milk and water can be used, half and half
2. When cool add: Graham flour, wheat flour, butter (melted), salt, yeast
3. Dissolve yeast cake in a cup half full of warm water
4. Mix at night and let rise
5. In the morning stir down; put in a well-buttered pan, letting it rise first for half an hour
6. Bake slowly
