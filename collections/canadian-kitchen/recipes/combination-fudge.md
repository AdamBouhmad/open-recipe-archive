## Ingredients

- 3 cups coffee sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup broken English walnuts
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 square unsweetened chocolate
- butter size of a small egg

## Directions

1. In one pan put 3 cups coffee sugar and 1 cup milk, boil until it forms a soft ball in water
2. Add 1 teaspoon butter and let boil up
3. Take from the fire and allow it to cool so the hand may rest upon it
4. Beat with a spoon, adding after it begins to thicken 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1 cup broken English walnuts
5. In another pan put 2 cups granulated sugar, 3/4 cup milk, 1 square unsweetened chocolate
6. Cook 8 minutes, then add butter size of a small egg
7. When it makes a firm ball in water take from the fire and add a few drops of vanilla
8. Beat until like very cold molasses, then pour into the pan that contains the other fudge
9. It will harden over the other and can be cut in squares
