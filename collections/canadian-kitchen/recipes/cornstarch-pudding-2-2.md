## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- a little milk
- whites of 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons white powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart milk
2. Beat the yolks of 4 eggs with 4 tablespoons cornstarch and a little milk
3. Stir into the boiling milk and let it boil up once
4. Turn into a pudding dish
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a froth and add 4 tablespoons white powdered sugar
6. Cover the pudding with the mixture, set in the oven and let brown lightly
7. Flavor with vanilla
