## Ingredients

- Rump steak, about an inch thick
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Butter a sheet of white paper and twist the four corners so as to form a little tray
2. Lay the steak in this and broil quickly from five to ten minutes, turning it once in the paper while cooking
3. When done lay it on a hot dish, season with pepper and salt
4. Add a little bit of butter, and serve at once
