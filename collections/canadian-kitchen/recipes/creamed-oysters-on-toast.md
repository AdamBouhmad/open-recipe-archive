## Ingredients

- Slices of hot toast
- 1 pint boiling milk
- 1 pint oysters
- 2 dessertspoonfuls cornstarch or flour
- Salt
- cayenne
- lump of butter size of small egg

## Directions

1. Cover large hot platter with slices of hot toast
2. Turn in oysters to boiling milk, then add 2 dessertspoonfuls cornstarch or flour stirred smooth in a little milk
3. Add salt, cayenne, and lump of butter size of small egg
4. Pour over toast and serve instantly
