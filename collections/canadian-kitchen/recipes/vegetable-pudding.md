## Ingredients

- 1 cup apples
- 1 cup potatoes grated
- 1 cup carrots grated
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 cup flour
- 2 cups raisins
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Steam 3 hours
