## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 2 spoons baking powder
- dash of salt

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll and bake
