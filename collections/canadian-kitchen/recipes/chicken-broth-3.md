## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 4 quarts cold water
- salt and pepper
- 1 small cupful rice
- 1 cup milk (optional)
- 1 or 2 beaten eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut up the fowl and put it into the pot with four quarts of cold water
2. Stew until there are but three quarts left
3. Take out the chicken
4. Season the liquor and add a small cupful of rice
5. Cook rice tender
6. May add a cup of milk and one or two beaten eggs just before serving
