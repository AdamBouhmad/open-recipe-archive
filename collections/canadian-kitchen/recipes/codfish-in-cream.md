## Ingredients

- half a cup of salted codfish
- cold water
- one cup of rich milk
- one spoonful of flour
- one spoonful of butter
- one hard-boiled egg
- pepper
- one teaspoonful of minced parsley

## Directions

1. Shred and soak half a cup of salted codfish over night
2. In the morning drain, place in a stew-pan, cover with cold water
3. When it boils, drain
4. Cover again with water and simmer gently for fifteen minutes
5. Add one cup of rich milk
6. Rub one spoonful of flour smooth in one spoonful of butter
7. Add to the codfish
8. Mince one hard-boiled egg, stir into the mixture
9. Add a pinch of pepper and a teaspoonful of minced parsley
10. Boil up once
