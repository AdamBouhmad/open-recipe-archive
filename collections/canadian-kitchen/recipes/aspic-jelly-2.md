## Ingredients

- 1 qt. good stock
- whites and shells of 2 eggs
- 1 cup lean raw beef
- 1 oz. gelatine
- 1 tablespoonful vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls sherry
- 20 peppercorns
- 10 cloves
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 0.5 saltspoonful ground mace
- sprig of parsley

## Directions

1. Mix the beef and eggs thoroughly together and add to the stock before it gets hot
2. When hot add gelatine (previously soaked in cold water)
3. Boil for about five minutes
4. Strain and add vinegar, sherry, twenty peppercorns, ten cloves, two or three stalks of celery, one teaspoon salt, one-half saltspoon of ground mace, sprig of parsley
