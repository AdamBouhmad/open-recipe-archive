## Ingredients

- 1 pound raisins well chopped
- 1 pound currants
- 3/4 pound suet finely chopped
- pinch salt
- 3/4 pound butter
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 3/4 pound mixed peel (lemon, orange and citron) grated
- 1/4 small nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon each ginger, cinnamon, allspice
- a very little ground cloves
- 6 eggs well beaten
- 3/4 pound blanched almonds cut fine
- 1 pound bread crumbs
- 1 cup flour

## Directions

1. Be sure and flavor your fruit well before adding
2. Place in a bag, tie tightly, and boil for not less than 6 hours
3. Add a little salt to water in which it is boiled
4. Serve with sauce seasoned with grated lemon, and a little of the juice
