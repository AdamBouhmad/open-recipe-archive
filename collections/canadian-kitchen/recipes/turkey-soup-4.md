## Ingredients

- Turkey bones
- Water enough to cover them
- A little dressing
- 1 beaten egg
- A little butter
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey bones for 1 hour in water enough to cover them
2. Stir in a little dressing and a beaten egg
3. Take from the fire, and when the water has ceased boiling add a little butter with pepper and salt
