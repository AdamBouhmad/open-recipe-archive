## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds finely chopped beef or Hamburg steak
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 2 cups milk
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped fat pork
- salt
- pepper
- sage

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients to taste
2. Roast like duck
