## Ingredients

- 1 can of salmon
- 4 soda biscuits rolled very fine
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Steam an hour and a half
2. Serve cold and garnished with a little parsley
