## Ingredients

- Windfall apples
- Sugar
- Water
- Thin rind of two lemons

## Directions

1. Wash apples and boil whole in a preserving kettle until quite pulpy
2. Strain through a jelly bag without squeezing
3. Pass through a silver wire sieve first, will run easier
4. Put sugar into a kettle with a little water, enough to melt
5. Boil quickly for ten minutes
6. Add strained apple juice; to every five pints, four pounds of sugar is allowed
7. Add thin rind of two lemons
8. Boil all together one hour
9. Measure apple juice carefully before putting on sugar and gauge sugar exactly
