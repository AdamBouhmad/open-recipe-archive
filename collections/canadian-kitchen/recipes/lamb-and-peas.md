## Ingredients

- chopped lamb
- onion
- cold potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- stock
- buttered crumbs
- green peas

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups chopped lamb (cold), 1 small onion, 1 cup cold potatoes, pepper and salt, 3 tablespoons stock, 3/4 cup buttered crumbs, 1 cup green peas lightly
2. Heap in mould or shallow butter dish
3. Cover with crumbs and bake until browned
4. Before serving, surround with seasoning and hot peas
