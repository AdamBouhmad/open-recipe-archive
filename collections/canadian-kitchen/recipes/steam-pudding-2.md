## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a pinch of salt
- flour to make stiff

## Directions

1. Mix shortening, brown sugar, currants, raisins, buttermilk, eggs, soda, salt, and flour to make stiff
2. Steam 2 1/2 or 3 hours
3. Serve with sauce
