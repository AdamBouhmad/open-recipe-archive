## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- whites of 5 eggs
- 4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup nuts
- 1/2 cup chopped raisins
- 1/2 cup grated cocoanut
- grated rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Beat egg whites to a stiff froth
3. Divide batter into four parts
4. Leave one part plain
5. Add 1 cup nuts to one part
6. Add 1/2 cup chopped raisins to another part
7. Add 1/2 cup grated cocoanut and grated rind of a lemon to the fourth part
8. Bake all parts
9. Put together with white icing, placing the layers in any order you prefer
