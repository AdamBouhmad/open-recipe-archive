## Ingredients

- 1 quart vinegar
- 3/4 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- sugar to taste
- red pepper to taste
- celery seed to taste
- cucumbers
- onions
- small tender corn cobs
- salt and water

## Directions

1. Peel and slice cucumbers, onions, and corn cobs the night before in not too strong salt and water
2. Drain in the morning
3. Put vinegar on to boil
4. Mix mustard, flour and sugar to a paste
5. Add to hot vinegar and let boil up
6. Add onions, cucumbers, and corn cobs
7. Stir till the whole comes to a boil
