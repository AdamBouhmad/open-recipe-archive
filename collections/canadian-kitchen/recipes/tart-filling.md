## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1 tablespoon butter
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix altogether
2. Put in tart shell
3. Bake in a moderate oven
