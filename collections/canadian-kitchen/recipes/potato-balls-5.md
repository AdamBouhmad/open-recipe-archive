## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt water
- 2 cups potato balls
- 1 cup white sauce
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, soak potatoes
2. Cut with French cutter
3. Cook in boiling salt water until tender
4. Drain
5. Add 1 cup white sauce to 2 cups potato balls
6. May be sprinkled with parsley
