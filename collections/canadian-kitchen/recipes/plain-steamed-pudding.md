## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour milk or cream
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix molasses and butter together
2. Beat till light
3. Stir in milk and salt
4. Then stir in the flour and soda
5. Steam 1 1/2 hours
