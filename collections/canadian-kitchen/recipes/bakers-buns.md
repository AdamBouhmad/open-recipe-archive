## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread dough
- 1/2 cup sugar
- lard the size of an egg
- 1 cup lukewarm water
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix all together and add flour to make a stiff dough, but not as stiff as for bread
2. Take the cup of dough out when putting bread on the pans
3. Put it on a plate and set it away to rise for about 4 or 5 hours
4. Then add these ingredients and let rise about 3 hours more
5. Make into buns, and let it rise 9 hours more
6. Bake in a slow oven
