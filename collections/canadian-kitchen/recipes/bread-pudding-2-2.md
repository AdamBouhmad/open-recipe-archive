## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread crumbs
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup seeded and chopped raisins
- 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon cloves
- 3/4 teaspoon allspice
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 4 tablespoons cold water
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- grated rind and piece of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir all together
2. Steam 1/2 hour
3. For sauce: stir flour and cold water until smooth
4. Add 1 cup boiling water
5. Sweeten to taste
6. Add 1 tablespoon butter
7. Add grated rind and piece of 1 lemon
