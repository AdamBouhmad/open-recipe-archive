## Ingredients

- one gill of milk
- one ounce of sugar
- yolks of three eggs
- one gill of syrup from preserved ginger jar
- two ounces of preserved ginger
- three-quarters ounce gelatine
- water
- one-half pint whipped cream

## Directions

1. Make a custard of a gill of milk, one ounce of sugar, the beaten yolks of three eggs.
2. Stir in a double boiler until thick, let it cool.
3. Add one gill of the syrup from the jar of preserved ginger, and two ounces of the ginger cut up.
4. Add three-quarters ounce, full weight, of gelatine melted in as little water as possible.
5. Last of all add one-half pint of whipped cream.
6. Mix gently until well blended, pour into a mould and set on ice.
