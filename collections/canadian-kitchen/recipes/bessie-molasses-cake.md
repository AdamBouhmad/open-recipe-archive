## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup dark molasses
- 3/4 cup shortening
- 1 cup raisins chopped fine
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup dark molasses, and 3/4 cup shortening
2. Add 1 cup raisins chopped fine
3. Dissolve 1 teaspoon soda in 1 cup hot water and add to mixture
4. Add 4 cups flour
5. Bake in long pan
6. Ice with icing
