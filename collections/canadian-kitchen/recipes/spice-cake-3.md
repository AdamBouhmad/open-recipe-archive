## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoonful cloves
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients and bake. This is nice for either a loaf or a layer cake.
