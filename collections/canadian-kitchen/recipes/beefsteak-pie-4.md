## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound steak
- 3 or 4 medium-sized potatoes
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- butter
- 1 quart flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 0.75 cup lard
- salt
- 1 cup sour milk

## Directions

1. Cut steak in small pieces and stew till tender
2. Cut potatoes in small pieces and add to steak
3. Cook together until done and remove
4. Make a thickening for broth of 1 cup milk and 1 tablespoon flour, a little butter and season to taste
5. Make a crust of 1 quart flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1 teaspoon soda, 0.75 cup lard, a pinch of salt and 1 cup sour milk
6. Mix and line a dish with crust
7. Fill with mixture, having plenty of broth to cover beef and potatoes
8. Cover the top with a crust and cut a hole in top crust
9. Bake to a golden brown in a moderate oven
