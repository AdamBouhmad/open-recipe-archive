## Ingredients

- 1 peck butter beans
- weak salt water
- 3 pints cider vinegar
- 3 pounds white sugar
- 1 large cup flour
- 1 cup mustard
- 2 tablespoons celery seed
- 2 teaspoons turmeric powder
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut beans small, wash and put on to boil in weak salt water
2. boil 3 pints cider vinegar and 3 pounds white sugar together
3. prepare 1 large cup of flour, 1 cup mustard, 2 tablespoons celery seed, 2 teaspoons turmeric powder
4. mix to a paste with cold vinegar
5. stir into your boiling vinegar and sugar
6. pour over cooked beans
7. bottle and seal
