## Ingredients

- fine large oysters
- cracker-dust
- pepper
- salt
- beaten egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Drain and wipe fine large oysters
2. Dip each first in cracker-dust (peppered and salted)
3. Then in beaten egg
4. And again in the cracker
5. Arrange upon a large cold platter
6. Set upon ice for half an hour
7. Fry in butter that has been gradually brought to a boil
8. Cook a few at a time
9. If the crumbs come off in the fat, strain them out before the next instalment goes in
