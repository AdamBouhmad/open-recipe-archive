## Ingredients

- 3 lemons
- 1 oz. tartaric acid
- 3 lbs. sugar
- 2 bottles ginger ale

## Directions

1. Pare rind of lemons as thin as possible, put rinds, white sugar and tartaric into a jar and cover with quart boiling water, and stir till sugar is melted.
2. When cold add the juice of the lemons and two quarts of cold water.
3. When about to serve add two bottles of ginger ale.
