## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice
- salted water
- 1/2 cup pitted dates
- cream for serving

## Directions

1. Wash 3/4 cup rice
2. Cook in large quantity of salted water until nearly tender
3. Drain thoroughly
4. Put into double boiler with 1/2 cup pitted dates
5. Cook until tender
6. Serve with cream
