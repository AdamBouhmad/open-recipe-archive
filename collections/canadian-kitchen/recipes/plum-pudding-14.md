## Ingredients

- 1 cup beef suet
- 1 heaping cup sugar
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup chopped almonds
- 1/8 cup citron peel
- a teaspoon soda
- 1 pint flour
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoon each cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and salt

## Directions

1. Boil 4 hours
