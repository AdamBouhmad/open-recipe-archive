## Ingredients

- a fowl
- butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1/2 ounce of gelatine

## Directions

1. Boil a fowl until it will slip easily from the bones
2. Let the water be reduced to about 1 pint in boiling
3. Pick the meat from the bones and place in a wet mould
4. Skim the fat from the liquor
5. Add a little butter, pepper and salt to the taste, and 1/2 ounce of gelatine
6. When this dissolves pour it hot over the chicken
7. Season the liquor pretty high, for the chicken absorbs
