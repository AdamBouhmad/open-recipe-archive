## Ingredients

- young fowl
- boiling water
- carrot
- onion
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Cover a young fowl, cut in joints, with boiling water
2. Let simmer until tender, with a few slices of carrot, 1/4 an onion and a stalk of celery
3. Remove skin and bones and return with broth to fire and let simmer until reduced to 1 cup
4. Strain broth and set aside
5. When the flesh is nearly cold, chop fine, remove fat from broth, reheat
6. Stir the chicken into it, adding salt and pepper
7. Pack in pot, cover with buttered paper, weight it down
8. Let stand until set, slice thin and serve with salad
