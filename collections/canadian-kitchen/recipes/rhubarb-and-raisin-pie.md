## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins
- 1 1/2 cups rhubarb
- 1 cup sugar
- Lemon (grated rind only)
- 2 yolks

## Directions

1. Stone raisins
2. Chop fine with rhubarb
3. Add sugar and rind
4. Cook
5. As removed from fire add yolks
6. Stir vigorously
