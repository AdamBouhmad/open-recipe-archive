## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sweet cream
- 2 cups flour
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- a pinch of salt
- flavor to taste
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup rich sweet cream

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs in cup and fill cup with sweet cream
2. Sift flour 3 times with baking powder, salt, and flavor
3. Mix together
4. For nut cake, add a cup of chopped nuts, or for loaf cake use 1 cup chopped raisins
5. For icing: boil 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar with 1/2 cup rich sweet cream till it hairs
6. Beat till cool, then add flavoring and spread between layers and on top
