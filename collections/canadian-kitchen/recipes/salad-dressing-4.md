## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- Pepper to taste
- 2 cups milk or cream
- Vinegar to thicken

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard and take the yolks
2. Rub smooth with melted butter, salt, mustard, sugar, and pepper to taste
3. Add to this mixture milk or cream, stirring it in gradually
4. Add enough vinegar to thicken it
5. Boil all together, stirring briskly
6. Do not mind if it curdles, go on stirring till it gets quite smooth
7. If no cream is used a tablespoonful of cornstarch dissolved in the milk will help to thicken it to the consistency of batter
8. Use whites of eggs to garnish salad
