## Ingredients

- 2 pounds maple sugar
- 1 quart rich milk
- part cream

## Directions

1. Break maple sugar into small pieces
2. Put it in a saucepan with a quart of rich milk (part cream is better)
3. Let this boil until it reaches the stage where it hardens in cold water
4. Pour it into pans
5. Mark it in squares as you would taffy or caramels
