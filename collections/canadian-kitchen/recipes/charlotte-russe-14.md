## Ingredients

- 2 cups cream
- 3/4 cup sugar
- lady fingers or stale sponge cake
- flavoring to taste
- 1 teaspoon Knox gelatine, or 1 ounce sheet gelatine
- 3/4 cup cold water or milk

## Directions

1. Line a mould or dish holding about 6 cups with lady fingers or strips of sponge cake
2. Trim the cake to fit into the dish
3. Stick lady fingers together with white of egg or dissolved gelatine to keep them in place
4. Put the gelatine to soak in cold water or milk
5. Whip the cream
6. Beat in the sugar and the flavoring
7. Stand the gelatine over hot water and dissolve it
8. Rapidly mix the dissolved gelatine with the whipped cream
9. Turn it into the cake-lined dish
10. Set away to get perfectly cold before turning out to serve
