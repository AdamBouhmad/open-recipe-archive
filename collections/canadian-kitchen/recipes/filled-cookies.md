## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup lard or butter
- 2 eggs
- 3 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to roll out soft
- 1 cup raisins chopped fine
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 large cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Make dough and roll very thin and cut out round or square
2. For filling: Put 1 cup chopped raisins, 1 cup boiling water, and 1 large cup sugar on the stove
3. When boiling stir in 2 tablespoons of flour
4. When cold, spread between the cookies
5. Bake
