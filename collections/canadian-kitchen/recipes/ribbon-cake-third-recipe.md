## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs beaten
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- melted chocolate
- pink cake coloring

## Directions

1. Beat together butter and sugar
2. Add 3 beaten eggs and beat again
3. Add 1 cup milk, beating well after each addition
4. Beat in 3 cups flour in which 1½ teaspoons baking powder have been sifted
5. Divide the batter into four parts, coloring 1 with melted chocolate and another with pink cake coloring
6. Bake in jelly tins, and put together with a filling
