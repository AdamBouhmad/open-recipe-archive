## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoonful of butter
- one tablespoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Pare off the outer shell
2. Take out the seeds
3. Cut into small pieces
4. Boil in hot, salted water until tender (twenty minutes if young, longer for full-grown squash)
5. Drain well
6. Rub through a vegetable-press
7. Return to the saucepan
8. Mix with salt, pepper, and a tablespoonful of butter made into a roux with a tablespoonful of flour
9. Stir and beat for a whole minute, until you have a creamy, smoking mass
10. Pour out
