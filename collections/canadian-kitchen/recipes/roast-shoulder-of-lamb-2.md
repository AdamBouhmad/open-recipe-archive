## Ingredients

- 1 shoulder of lamb
- water
- gravy
- butter
- lemon-juice
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- dressing

## Directions

1. Cook with more water in the pan and more slowly than the leg.
2. When nearly done, baste plentifully with the gravy, and, five minutes later, with butter into which a little lemon-juice has been beaten.
3. Brown lightly, after dredging with salt, pepper, and flour.
4. Your object should be to make every part of the shoulder eatable, the muscles soft, and the skin gelatinous.
5. You can vary the dish by having the bone of the shoulder taken out, filling the cavity with a dressing of breadcrumbs and butter, seasoned with pepper and salt.
