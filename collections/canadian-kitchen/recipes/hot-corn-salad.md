## Ingredients

- 1 can corn
- 1 egg
- 1 cupful sweet milk
- 5 sodas rolled
- salt and pepper to taste
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Mix corn, egg, milk, rolled sodas, salt, pepper and butter
2. Bake in oven until brown
