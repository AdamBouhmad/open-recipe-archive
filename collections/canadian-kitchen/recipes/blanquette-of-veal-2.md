## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls chopped cooked veal
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 cupful of stock
- 1 cupful of cream
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 12 button mushrooms
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, add flour, stir until smooth, add the liquid, and when the sauce thickens add meat and mushrooms.
2. Cook all together for a few minutes.
3. When ready to serve add yolks of eggs and parsley, cooking for a minute; garnish with whole mushrooms.
