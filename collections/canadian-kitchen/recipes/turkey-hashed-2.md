## Ingredients

- turkey remnants
- turkey bones
- water
- turkey gravy
- white stock
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the remnants of turkey from a previous dinner into pieces of equal size
2. Boil the bones in a quart of water, until the quart is reduced to a pint
3. Take out the bones, and to the liquor in which they were boiled add turkey gravy, or white stock, or a small piece of butter with salt and pepper
4. Let the liquor thus prepared boil up once
5. Put in the pieces of turkey, dredge in a little flour, give it one
