## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 even tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 yolks of eggs
- juice and grated rinds of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Bake in 3 layers
2. For icing: Cook sugar, water, cornstarch, yolks of eggs, juice and grated rinds of lemons all together until thickened
3. Cool and spread on layers of the cake
