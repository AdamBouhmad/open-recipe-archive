## Ingredients

- Half a hard crisp cabbage
- Butter
- Vinegar
- Water
- Boiled pickled pigs' knuckle

## Directions

1. Slice as fine as possible half a hard crisp cabbage. Should be in shreds; put in frying-pan with butter and cook slightly, turning carefully.
2. Pour over it vinegar and water and cover closely until cooked.
3. Serve hot with boiled pickled pigs' knuckle that has been carefully and slowly cooked until almost jellied.
