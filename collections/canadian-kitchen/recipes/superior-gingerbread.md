## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup molasses
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 3/4 tablespoon ginger
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- water
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups raisins
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Combine brown sugar and butter
2. Add sour milk and molasses
3. Add sifted flour
4. Add ginger
5. Dissolve soda in a little water
6. Add eggs
7. Add raisins and walnuts
8. Put soda in last, when well mixed
