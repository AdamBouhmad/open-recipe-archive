## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- flour enough to make stiff

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together with flour enough to make stiff.
