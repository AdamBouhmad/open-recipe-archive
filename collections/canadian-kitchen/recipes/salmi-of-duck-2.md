## Ingredients

- 1 roasted or braised duck
- salad oil
- lemon-juice
- cold water
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- browned flour
- butter
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 can of champignons or fresh mushrooms
- sippets of fried bread

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat of a roasted or braised duck into neat dice
2. Cut the bones, stuffing, and skin into small pieces
3. Cover the meat-dice with a marinade of salad oil and lemon-juice, and leave in a cold place
4. Cover the bones, etc., well with cold water
5. Add parsley, pepper, and salt, and simmer, after this reaches the boil, for two hours
6. Strain, thicken the gravy with browned flour rubbed up with a spoonful of butter
7. Add the juice of half an onion, boil up and put in the meat
8. Draw to the side of the range and let it almost, but not quite, boil
9. Take out the meat and arrange neatly upon a flat dish
10. Add to the gravy half a can of champignons (or, if you can get them, fresh mushrooms are far better)
11. Simmer three minutes and pour over the meat
12. Garnish with sippets of fried bread
