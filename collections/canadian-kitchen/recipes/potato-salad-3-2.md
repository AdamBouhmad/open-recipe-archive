## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- ¾ teaspoon mustard
- small teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg beaten
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- cold potatoes and onions sliced thin
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Bring 1 cup sour cream to boiling point
2. Add ¾ teaspoon mustard, small teaspoon sugar, 1 egg beaten
3. Remove from range and add 1 tablespoon vinegar
4. Pour over cold potatoes and onions sliced thin, with a little salt
