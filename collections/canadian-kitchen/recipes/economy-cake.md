## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- a pinch of salt
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder and salt together four times
2. Scald milk and pour it while still hot on to the sifted ingredients
3. Stir thoroughly
4. Stir in the beaten whites of 2 eggs
5. Bake in a shallow, oblong tin and use an icing
