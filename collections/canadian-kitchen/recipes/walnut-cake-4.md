## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs
3. Add milk
4. Mix flour, cream tartar, and soda
5. Add flour mixture to batter
6. Season with lemon or vanilla
7. Bake in 2 sheets
8. Fill and frost
