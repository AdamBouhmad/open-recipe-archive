## Ingredients

- 1 pt. flour
- 1/2 pt. milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the dry ingredients and sift them well.
2. Beat the eggs light and add the milk to them.
3. Add this to the dry ingredients and add the melted butter.
4. Beat the batter vigorously for a few seconds.
5. Put on buttered muffin pans and bake in a hot oven about twenty minutes.
