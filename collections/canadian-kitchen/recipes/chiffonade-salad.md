## Ingredients

- 2 large green peppers
- 1 grape fruit
- 3 large tomatoes
- 1 head lettuce
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Cut open peppers, remove the seeds and veins and cut into shreds; drop into boiling water for one minute; drain and cool.
2. Cut a grape fruit. Take out the pulp with a spoon and cut into bits.
3. Peel tomatoes, and cut into small pieces.
4. Shred lettuce finely.
5. Marinade each article separately with a French dressing.
6. Put the grape fruit pulp in the centre of the salad dish and arrange around it the prepared peppers, tomatoes and lettuce in such a way as to display the different colors to the best advantage.
