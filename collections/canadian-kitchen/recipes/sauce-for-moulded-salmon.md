## Ingredients

- One cup of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of cornflour
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- 1 raw egg
- 1 teaspoonful of anchovy, mushroom or tomato catsup
- 1 small pinch of mace
- 1 pinch of cayenne

## Directions

1. Heat the milk to a boil
2. Thicken with a tablespoonful of cornflour
3. Add one large spoonful of butter
4. Put the egg in last
5. Very carefully boil one minute to cook it
6. When the pudding is turned from the mould, pour over it and serve
