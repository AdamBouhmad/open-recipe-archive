## Ingredients

- fowls
- flour
- salt and pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the fowls open and lay them flat in a pan
2. Break down the breast and the back bones
3. Dredge with flour and season well with salt and pepper and bits of butter
4. Put in a very hot oven until done, basting frequently with melted butter
5. Or when half done take out the chicken and finish by broiling it upon a gridiron over bright coals
6. Pour over it melted butter and the juices in the pan in which it was baked
