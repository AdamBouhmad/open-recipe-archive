## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 2 1/4 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup chopped raisins or currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- mixed spice

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup molasses, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 2 1/4 cups flour, 2 tablespoons milk, 2 eggs, 1 cup chopped raisins or currants, 1 teaspoon soda, and mixed spice
2. Drop and bake
