## Ingredients

- blackberries
- ½ lb. sliced apple per pound of berries
- 1 lemon per 6 pounds of berries, very thinly sliced
- equal weight of fruit and sugar

## Directions

1. Pick over your blackberries and weigh them
2. To each pound of berries put half a pound of sliced apple
3. To every six pounds of berries add one lemon, very thinly sliced
4. Put the fruit on and let it boil steadily nearly half an hour
5. Add the sugar by degrees, and boil about twenty minutes
