## Ingredients

- 1½ cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- ¾ cup currants
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- very small teaspoon soda
- cloves and nutmeg to taste
- flour to roll

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients and roll
