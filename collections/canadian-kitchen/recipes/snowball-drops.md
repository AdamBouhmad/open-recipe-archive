## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 5 cups flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar together
2. Add milk, salt and nutmeg
3. Add baking powder sifted in the flour last
4. Drop off spoon in hot fat and cook a light brown
5. When nearly cold roll in sugar
