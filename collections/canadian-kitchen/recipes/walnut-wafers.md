## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- about 8 or 10 tablespoons flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and eggs together
2. Add nuts and butter
3. Add flour last
4. Spread on pan very thin
5. When baked, cut in squares while warm
