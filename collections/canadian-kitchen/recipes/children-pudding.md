## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 3/4 cup raisins
- 3/4 cup currants
- 1 teacup buttermilk
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Boil in a cloth for 2 hours
2. Serve with sauce
