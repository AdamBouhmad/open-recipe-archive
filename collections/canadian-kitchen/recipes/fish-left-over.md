## Ingredients

- one teaspoonful of minced onion
- one teaspoonful of butter
- one level tablespoonful of flour
- one cup of hot stewed tomatoes
- one part cold boiled fish
- one part macaroni
- one-quarter cup of cheese
- one cold egg
- one-quarter cup of fine cracker crumbs
- one-third cup of melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook minced onion in butter until yellow
2. Add flour and when well mixed and bubbling, stir it into a cup of hot stewed tomatoes
3. When it has cooked a little and is thick, season to taste
4. Take one part each of cold boiled fish and macaroni with one-quarter cup of cheese
5. Cut into small bits one cold egg
6. Strain the tomato sauce over them and one-quarter cup of fine cracker crumbs, moistened in one-third cup of melted butter on the top
7. Bake till brown
