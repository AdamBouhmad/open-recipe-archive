## Ingredients

- 6 pounds green, unripe gooseberries
- 6 pints water
- 1 pound sugar per pint of juice
- 1 sprig of vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil 6 pounds green, unripe gooseberries in 6 pints water
2. Pour into a basin, and let stand covered with a cloth 24 hours
3. Strain through a jelly bag
4. To every pint of juice add 1 pound sugar
5. Boil it for an hour, then skim and boil for an hour longer with a sprig of vanilla
