## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter or lard
- 1 cup baking syrup
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 2 teaspoons soda
- flour enough to roll out easily

## Directions

1. Mix and roll out
