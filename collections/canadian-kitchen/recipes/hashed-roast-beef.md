## Ingredients

- 2 cups roast beef
- 1 large onion
- 3 tablespoons flour
- butter size of an egg
- 1 cup water
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut beef fine with knife
2. Cut onion fine
3. Put butter and flour in pan and brown
4. Put in cut beef and onion
5. Stir well
6. Add cup of water, a little more if too thick
7. Season with pepper and salt to taste
8. Let boil 15 minutes
