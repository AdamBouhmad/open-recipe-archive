## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Dress young chickens, wash, and let them stand in water half an hour to make them white
2. Put into a baking pan (first cutting them open at the back)
3. Sprinkle salt and pepper over them, and put a lump of butter here and there
4. Cover tightly with another pan the same size and bake one hour
5. Baste often with butter
