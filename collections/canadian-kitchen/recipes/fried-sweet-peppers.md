## Ingredients

- sweet peppers
- salt
- flour
- cottolene

## Directions

1. Cut open crosswise, extract the seeds, cut the peppers into slices
2. Lay in cold water for fifteen minutes
3. Salt slightly
4. Dust with flour
5. Fry in hot cottolene for five or six minutes
