## Ingredients

- 2 chickens chopped coarsely
- 3 bunches celery chopped
- 2 heads of lettuce
- 2 hard-boiled eggs chopped fine
- a few pickled cucumbers
- salt to taste
- mayonnaise dressing
- lemons for garnish

## Directions

1. Chop chickens coarsely
2. Chop celery and lettuce
3. Chop hard-boiled eggs fine
4. Mix with mayonnaise dressing
5. Garnish with lemons just before sending to table
