## Ingredients

- whites of 5 eggs
- 1 scant cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Beat egg whites
3. Mix cream tartar and soda with flour
4. Add flour mixture and milk alternately to batter
5. Bake
6. Fill and ice
