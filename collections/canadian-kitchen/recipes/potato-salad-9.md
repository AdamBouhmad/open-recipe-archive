## Ingredients

- five or six potatoes
- fresh onion or cucumber
- one cup vinegar
- two tablespoonfuls sugar
- two eggs
- one teaspoonful dry mustard
- pepper and salt
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut up potatoes in small pieces
2. If you have fresh onion or cucumber mix a few pieces up with them, cut very finely
3. For the dressing take one cup vinegar, two tablespoonfuls sugar, two eggs, one teaspoonful dry mustard, a little pepper and salt
4. Put on the stove and stir all the time till thick
5. If too thick add a little cream when cold
6. Pour over the potatoes an hour or two before using
