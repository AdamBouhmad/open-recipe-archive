## Ingredients

- good puff paste
- crusts of bread
- oysters
- butter

## Directions

1. Line a shallow pudding-dish with good puff paste
2. Fill with crusts of bread
3. Cover with a top of the paste and bake
4. Butter the edges of the dish well so that the crust would not adhere to them too closely
5. When the pastry is baked lift off the top carefully
6. Remove the bread crusts
7. Put in oysters, prepared just as for oyster scallop, except that the oysters are allowed to cook in the sauce for three minutes after they go in
8. Lay the crust back on the dish and send to table
