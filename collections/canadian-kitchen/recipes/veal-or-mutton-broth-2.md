## Ingredients

- scrag-end of mutton (two pounds)
- two quarts of cold water
- an ounce of pearl barley or rice
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- one turnip
- one carrot
- one onion

## Directions

1. Put scrag-end of mutton in a saucepan, with two quarts of cold water, and an ounce of pearl barley or rice
2. When it is coming to a boil, skim it well
3. Then add half a teaspoonful of salt
4. Let it boil until half reduced, then strain it, and take off all the fat
5. If vegetables are liked in this broth, cut turnip, carrot, and onion in shreds, and boil them in the broth half an hour
6. The barley may be served with the vegetables in broth
