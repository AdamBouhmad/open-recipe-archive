## Ingredients

- ¾ cup macaroni broken in inch pieces
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Boil 20 minutes in 2 quarts boiling water with 1 tablespoon salt
2. Boil until soft
3. Rinse with cold water
