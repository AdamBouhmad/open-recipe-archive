## Ingredients

- thin stale bread
- cream
- thin slices of buttered bread
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Toast very carefully thin stale bread
2. Spread with cream while hot
3. Place between thin slices of buttered bread
4. Some add a dash of nutmeg to the cream and sweeten it a very little
5. Cut in oblong fingers
