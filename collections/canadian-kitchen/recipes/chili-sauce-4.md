## Ingredients

- 12 ripe tomatoes
- 2 large onions
- 4 green peppers
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 4 teaspoonfuls salt
- 1 teacup vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onions and peppers very fine.
2. Boil one hour and a half.
3. Add one teacup of vinegar half an hour before taking from the fire.
4. Bottle, cork closely.
5. Keep in the coolest part of the cellar.
