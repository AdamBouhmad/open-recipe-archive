## Ingredients

- 4 cups sweet cream
- 1 cup milk
- a little salt
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup currants
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir 4 cups sweet cream, 1 cup milk, a little salt, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup currants, 2 teaspoons baking powder, and 1 teaspoon soda with a spoon
2. Add flour enough to make a stiff dough
3. Roll out on board
4. Bake in a quick oven
