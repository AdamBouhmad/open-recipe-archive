## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup thick white sauce
- 2 yolks
- 1 3/4 cups cheese
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Make the sauce
2. Add yolks and cheese
3. When fairly beaten, add seasoning
4. Spread in shallow saucepan
5. Cool
6. Cut in shapes
7. Roll in crumbs
8. Fry
