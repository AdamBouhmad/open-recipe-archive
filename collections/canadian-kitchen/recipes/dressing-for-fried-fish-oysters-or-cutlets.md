## Ingredients

- Soda biscuits
- seasoning
- eggs
- pepper and salt for fish and oysters
- thyme, sweet marjoram and summer savory for cutlets
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Roll biscuits (if a bottle is used for this it will be found to roll the biscuits as fine as flour)
2. Add seasoning, pepper and salt for fish and oysters; for cutlets, thyme, sweet marjoram and summer savory
3. Beat eggs
4. Dip oysters or any fry in them
5. Roll in seasoned cracker crumbs
6. Fry in butter or lard
