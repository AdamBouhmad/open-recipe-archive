## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup boiling water
- 1 cup flour
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. mix as cream puffs
2. drop by spoonfuls and fry in deep fat
3. Drain
4. fill with preserves or cream filling
5. sprinkle with powdered sugar
