## Ingredients

- 1½ cups flour
- A little salt
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- Butter size of walnut

## Directions

1. Sift flour, salt and baking powder together.
2. Yolks of eggs well beaten, add milk, and melted butter.
3. Then stir in flour; beat up whites of eggs and stir in, have tins well buttered.
4. Bake in quick oven twenty minutes to one-half hour.
