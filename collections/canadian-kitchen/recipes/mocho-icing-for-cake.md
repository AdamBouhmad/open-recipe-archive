## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup blanched almonds, chopped fine
- 5 tablespoons icing sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 cup blanched almonds chopped fine, 5 tablespoons icing sugar, 2 tablespoons butter, 1 tablespoon hot water, and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla together
