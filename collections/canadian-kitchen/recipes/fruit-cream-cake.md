## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- butter the size of an egg
- 1 cup rich sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- nutmeg to taste
- 1 1/2 cups seeded raisins

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake 1 hour
