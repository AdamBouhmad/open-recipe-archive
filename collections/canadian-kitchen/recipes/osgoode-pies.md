## Ingredients

- 3 eggs (whites of 2 for top)
- 1 cup raisins
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon mixed spice
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 cup water
- Crust

## Directions

1. Cook all together
2. Put in crust that has been previously made
3. This will make 2 pies
