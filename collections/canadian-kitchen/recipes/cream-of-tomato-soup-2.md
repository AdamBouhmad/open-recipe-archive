## Ingredients

- 1 can of tomatoes (1 quart)
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 quart milk

## Directions

1. Stew the tomatoes slowly 1/2 hour
2. Strain, and add soda while hot
3. Melt butter, add flour, mix fine
4. Add milk gradually, then the seasoning
5. Add the tomatoes to this mixture and serve at once
