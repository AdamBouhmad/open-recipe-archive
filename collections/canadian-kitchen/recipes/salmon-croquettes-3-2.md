## Ingredients

- 1 box salmon
- 4 biscuits
- 3 eggs
- salt
- butter
- stale bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Roll 4 biscuits
2. Make in small flat balls with 1 box salmon, rolled biscuits, 3 eggs, and salt
3. Fry in butter till light brown
4. Stale bread crumbs may be used instead of biscuits
5. Any fish left over from roasting or frying may be used the same way
