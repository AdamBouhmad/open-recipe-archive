## Ingredients

- 1 pint new milk (warm)
- 1/2 cup sugar
- butter size of an egg
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 pint new milk (warm), 1/2 cup sugar, butter size of an egg, 1 White Swan Yeast Cake and flour enough to make a thick paste with 1/2 teaspoon salt
2. Let rise over night
3. Knead in the morning the same as bread
4. Keep in a warm place
5. When ready to rise the last time, roll out lightly on the breadboard quite thick
6. Dip in melted butter and lard
7. Let rise till very light
8. Bake 3/4 of an hour
