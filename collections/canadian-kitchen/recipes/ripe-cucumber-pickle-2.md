## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe tomatoes, chopped
- 1 quart chopped ripe cucumbers
- 1 pint onions
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon tumeric powder
- 2 tablespoons mustard

## Directions

1. Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, sugar, and vinegar
2. Boil 1 hour
3. Mix flour, salt, tumeric powder, and mustard in a little vinegar
4. Add to the bulk
5. Boil for a few minutes
