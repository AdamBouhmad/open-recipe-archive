## Ingredients

- 1 quart stock
- 3/4 cup carrot cut in thin strips 1½ inches long
- 3/4 cup turnip cut in thin strips 1½ inches long
- 2 tablespoons cooked peas and string beans cut in strips

## Directions

1. Cook carrot and turnip until tender
2. Add the stock, add peas and beans
