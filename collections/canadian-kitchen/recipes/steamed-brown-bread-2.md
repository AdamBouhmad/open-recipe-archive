## Ingredients

- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup milk
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1 cup raisins
- little salt
- good teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Steam 3 hours
2. Put in the oven half an hour. (Lovely with beans)
