## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Indian meal
- 1 1/2 cups rye meal
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Pour in a dish and steam 3 hours
3. When taken off put in the oven for 15 minutes
