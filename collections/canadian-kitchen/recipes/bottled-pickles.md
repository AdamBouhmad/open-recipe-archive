## Ingredients

- small cucumbers
- boiling water
- 1 gallon vinegar
- 1 cup salt
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon alum
- spices to suit taste

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over cucumbers
2. Let stand for four hours
3. Mix vinegar, salt, brown sugar, alum, and spices
4. Let come to a boil
5. Put cucumbers in bottles
6. Pour boiling vinegar over them
7. Seal
