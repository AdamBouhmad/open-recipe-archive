## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. cook till waxy
2. then add vanilla and beat till creamy
3. then spread between layers and on top of cake
