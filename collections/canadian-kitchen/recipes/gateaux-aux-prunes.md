## Ingredients

- 1 lb. prunes
- 3 oz. sugar
- 1/2 oz. gelatine
- 1 glass brandy
- 1 pt. water
- A few drops cochineal
- Juice and rind of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the prunes in the water and sugar until quite soft.
2. Take them out and take the stones out, crack the kernels.
3. Add kernels with the brandy, lemon juice and peel, a little cochineal and the gelatine dissolved in a little water.
4. Boil all for twenty minutes.
5. Pour into a border mould.
6. When set, turn out, and serve with whipped cream in the centre.
