## Ingredients

- 1 package granulated gelatine
- 12 tablespoons cold water
- 4 cups granulated sugar
- 16 tablespoons water
- 3 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 1 cornstarch

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 package granulated gelatine in 12 tablespoons cold water, letting stand while sugar boils
2. Boil 4 cups granulated sugar, moistened with 16 tablespoons water, until it threads
3. Pour hot liquid over gelatine and beat 20 to 25 minutes, having a pan sprinkled with 3 teaspoons powdered sugar and 1 cornstarch
4. Let stand for 1 day and night
5. Cut in strips 3 inches long 1 inch wide
