## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- a little pepper

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together and stir until they froth
2. Add the milk and stir constantly until it thickens and boils
3. Add the salt and pepper
4. If it must stand any time before serving, it should be closely covered and stirred occasionally to prevent a skin forming on the top
5. When a large quantity is made, 3/4 of the milk may be scalded and added after the first third is stirred in
