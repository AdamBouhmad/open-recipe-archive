## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- three hard-boiled eggs
- sharp green pickle
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut bread very thin, buttering it lightly on the loaf
2. Upon each slice spread a filling made by mixing three hard-boiled eggs, minced extremely fine, with half their bulk of sharp green pickle chopped equally small
3. Season this compound with salt and pepper to taste
4. Work in a little butter
5. Lay another thin slice of bread, buttered side down, over this
6. Cut them into square and triangular sandwiches
