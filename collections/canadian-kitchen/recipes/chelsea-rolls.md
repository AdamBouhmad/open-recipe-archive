## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 2 large teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons lard
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 large teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup currants
- milk and sugar for glaze

## Directions

1. Make biscuit dough with 1 quart flour, 2 large teaspoons baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 4 tablespoons lard and 1 cup sweet milk
2. Roll out to about 1/4 inch thick
3. Spread with 2 tablespoons butter, 1 cup sugar, mixed with 1 large teaspoon cinnamon, 1 cup currants
4. Give a light roll with rolling-pin to keep the currants from dropping out
5. Roll as you would a jelly roll
6. Cut in slices about 1 inch thick
7. Place in pan and bake a light brown
8. Glaze with a spoonful of milk and sugar
