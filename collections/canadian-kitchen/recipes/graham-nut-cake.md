## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- sour milk or hot water
- 1 cup walnuts cut up
- 2 cups Graham flour
- 2 cups white flour
- 1 pinch salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 heaping teaspoon soda in sour milk or hot water.
2. Mix 1 cup molasses, 3/4 cup brown sugar, the soda mixture, 1 cup walnuts cut up, 2 cups Graham flour, 2 cups white flour and 1 pinch salt like cake batter.
