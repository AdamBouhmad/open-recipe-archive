## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated potato
- 1 cup grated carrot
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a little salt
- 2 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix grated potato, grated carrot, sugar, suet, raisins, currants, soda, salt, and flour
2. Steam for 3 hours
