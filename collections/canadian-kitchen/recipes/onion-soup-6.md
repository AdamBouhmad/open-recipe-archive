## Ingredients

- One tablespoonful of butter
- One finely chopped onion
- One quart of soup stock
- One tablespoonful of flour
- A little cold water
- One-half a teaspoonful of salt
- One-quarter teaspoonful of pepper
- Two slices of stale bread

## Directions

1. Put one tablespoonful of butter in the frying-pan
2. When hot add one finely chopped onion; fry it until nicely browned, being careful not to burn
3. Put one quart of soup stock into a stew-pan; add the fried onion and cook for fifteen minutes
4. Strain; return to the fire, add one tablespoonful of flour wet in a little cold water, to thicken, and boil for five minutes longer
5. Season with one-half a teaspoonful of salt and one-quarter teaspoonful of pepper
6. Cut two slices of stale bread into dice; brown the dice in the oven, put them in the soup tureen, pour the soup over them and serve at once before they become soft
