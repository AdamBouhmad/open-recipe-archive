## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very light, the whites to stiff froth, the yolks to a smooth, thick batter
2. Add to yolks a small cupful of milk, pepper and salt
3. Lastly pour in whites lightly
4. Have ready in hot frying-pan a good lump of butter
5. When it sizzles pour in lightly your mixture, setting over clear fire
6. Do not stir, but as eggs set, slip broad-bladed knife under the omelet to guard against burning
7. If your oven is hot you may put your frying-pan into it as soon as the middle is set
8. Serve with brown side of omelet up
