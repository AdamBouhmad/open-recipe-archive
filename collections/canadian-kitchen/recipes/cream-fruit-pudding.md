## Ingredients

- whites of 2 eggs
- 1 cup berry jam
- 1/2 cup sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Beat whites of 2 eggs, 1 cup berry jam, and 1/2 cup sugar together for 1/2 hour
2. Serve with sweet cream
