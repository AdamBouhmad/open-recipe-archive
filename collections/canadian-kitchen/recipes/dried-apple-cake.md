## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried apples
- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 4 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 good spoon soda
- 1 cup sour milk
- spice of all kinds

## Directions

1. Soak dried apples over night in water
2. Chop fine and simmer in 2 cups of molasses
3. When nearly cold, add 1 cup sugar, 3/4 cup butter, 4 cups flour, 2 eggs, 1 good spoon soda, 1 cup sour milk, and spice of all kinds
