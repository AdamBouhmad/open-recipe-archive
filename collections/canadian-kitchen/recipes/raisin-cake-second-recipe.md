## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup sour milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- little spice if wished

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together and bake
