## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- butter
- 1 or 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Slice cold boiled potatoes and fry in butter till nicely brown
2. In meantime beat 1 or 2 eggs and stir into the potatoes nicely
3. Take up at once so as not to harden the egg, but merely to cook slightly
