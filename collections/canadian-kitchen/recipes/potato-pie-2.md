## Ingredients

- mashed potato
- cold minced meat
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a deep pie and cover the bottom with mashed potato
2. Layer with cold minced meat, with pepper and salt to taste
3. Sprinkle with flour and moisten with a little water
4. Repeat till dish is full, the last layer being potatoes with small pieces of butter placed on top
5. Bake till a light brown
