## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add molasses
3. Add eggs 1 by 1 and beat thoroughly
4. Put soda in sour milk and mix well
5. Sift flour and spices
6. Add flour and spice mixture to other mixture, alternating with milk
7. Beat well
