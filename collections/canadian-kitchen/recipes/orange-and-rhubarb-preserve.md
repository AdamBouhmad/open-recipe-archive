## Ingredients

- 1 pound of rhubarb
- 3 or 4 Seville oranges
- 1 1/2 pounds white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges thinly and cut them into strips as for marmalade
2. Take off the white pith
3. Remove the pips from the fruit and cut into slices the yellow part of the peel also
4. Place oranges, rhubarb and sugar in preserving pan and boil gently until sufficiently cooked and the scum has ceased to rise
5. Put the preserves in small pots and cover while hot with paper brushed over with white of egg
