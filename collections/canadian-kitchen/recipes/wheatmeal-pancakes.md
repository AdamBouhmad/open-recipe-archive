## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 egg (yolk and white beaten separately)
- 1 cup wheatmeal
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Combine sour cream, buttermilk, sweet cream, melted butter, and egg yolks and whites beaten separately. Add wheatmeal, flour, soda, and baking powder.
