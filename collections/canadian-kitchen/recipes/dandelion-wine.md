## Ingredients

- Four quarts of dandelion flowers
- One gallon of water
- Six lemons
- Four pounds of white sugar
- Half royal yeast-cake

## Directions

1. Cover dandelion flowers with one gallon of water and boil
2. Strain, and when luke-warm add six lemons, four pounds of white sugar and half royal yeast-cake
3. Let it stand about ten days, or until done working
4. Then strain, bottle and seal
