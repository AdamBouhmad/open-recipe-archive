## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 ounces Cowan's Perfection Chocolate
- 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Heat sugar, milk and butter to boiling point
2. Add chocolate, stirring constantly until it is melted
3. Boil until mixture forms a soft ball in cold water
4. Remove from fire and cool slightly
5. Add vanilla and beat until creamy and until mixture begins to sugar slightly around edge of pan
6. Pour on buttered plate and when cool enough mark in squares
