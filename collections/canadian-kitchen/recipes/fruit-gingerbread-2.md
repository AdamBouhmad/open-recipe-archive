## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 cups raisins
- a generous quantity lemon peel
- 1 heaping tablespoon ginger
- 1 1/2 teaspoons soda
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. Add brown sugar
3. Add molasses and butter
4. Add sour cream
5. Add raisins and lemon peel
6. Add ginger
7. Dissolve soda in a little water and add
8. Add flour to make rather stiff
9. Bake in large dripping pan
