## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- cream
- pepper and salt
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage fine, put it on with just enough water to cook it
2. When done add a little cream, season with pepper and salt, then add two tablespoons vinegar
3. Stir until vinegar is all through the cabbage
