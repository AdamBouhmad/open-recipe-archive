## Ingredients

- 8 cups sugar
- 2 cups honey
- 4 cups milk or water
- 1 pound almonds
- 1 pound English walnuts
- 3 cents' worth candied lemon peel
- 3 cents' worth candied orange peel
- 5 cents' worth citron
- 2 large tablespoons soda
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons ground cloves
- flour

## Directions

1. Put the milk, sugar and honey on the stove to boil 15 minutes
2. Skim off the scum, and take from the stove
3. Put in the nuts, spices and candied fruit (cut fine)
4. Stir in as much flour as can be done with a spoon
5. Set away to cool, then mix in the soda (don't make the dough too stiff)
6. Cover up and let stand over night
7. Work in flour enough to make a stiff dough
8. Bake when you get ready
9. Roll out a little thicker than a common cookie, cut in any shape you like
