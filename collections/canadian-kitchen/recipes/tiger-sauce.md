## Ingredients

- 12 ripe tomatoes
- 13 large sour apples
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 2 large cups onions chopped fine
- 1 small cup salt
- 1 teaspoon cayenne
- 1 1/4 teaspoons turmeric
- 4 tablespoons mustard
- 1 pint vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes and apples separately
2. Put through colander
3. Add 1 pound brown sugar, 2 large cups onions chopped fine, 1 small cup salt, and 1 teaspoon cayenne
4. Add 1 1/4 teaspoons turmeric
5. Mix 4 tablespoons mustard with water and add
6. Add 1 pint vinegar
