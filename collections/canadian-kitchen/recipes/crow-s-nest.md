## Ingredients

- butter
- sliced apples
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- flour for a nice stiff batter
- soft sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Butter a pie plate and fill with sliced apples
2. Make a batter with 2 eggs, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon butter, 1/2 cup sweet milk, 1 teaspoon baking powder, and flour for a nice stiff batter
3. Pour batter over apples and bake slowly so that apples will cook well
4. Turn out of the plate upside down
5. Sprinkle with soft sugar and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon and a little butter in spots
6. Turn a plate over it and let steam about ten minutes
7. Serve with or without milk
