## Ingredients

- several kinds of left-over vegetables
- salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter or dripping

## Directions

1. Chop fine and mix several kinds of left-over vegetables
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Put a tablespoon of butter or dripping in a spider
4. Add the mince, cover and cook slowly until hot
