## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- onion-juice
- salad oil
- paprika
- Graham-bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel with a sharp knife.
2. Cut a piece from the top and gouge out most of the pulp, leaving the walls intact.
3. Season what you have removed with pepper, salt, sugar, a few drops of onion-juice, and twice as much salad oil when you have chopped the pulp rather coarsely.
4. Put it back into the tomatoes, replace the top, sprinkle with oil, paprika, and salt, and arrange upon a baking-pan.
5. Bake, covered, for twenty minutes, and uncovered for five, and serve upon buttered Graham-bread toast.
