## Ingredients

- one and a half cups of grated whole wheat bread-crumbs
- half a cupful of butter
- half a cupful of sugar
- one cupful of chopped figs
- one egg well beaten
- a good half cup of milk

## Directions

1. Rub butter into bread-crumbs.
2. Add sugar, figs, egg and milk.
3. Steam three hours.
