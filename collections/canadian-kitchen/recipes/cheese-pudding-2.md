## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- 1 egg
- 1 pint sweet milk

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in inch squares
2. Butter evenly
3. Put 1 layer in pudding dish
4. Add a little salt and pepper and 3/4 cup grated cheese
5. Repeat layers, but for top layer use 3/4 cup cheese
6. Beat 1 egg and add 1 pint sweet milk
7. Pour over bread
8. Bake 3/4 hour
9. Serve hot in place of potatoes
