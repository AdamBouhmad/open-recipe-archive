## Ingredients

- apples
- eggs

## Directions

1. Make the old-fashioned apple sauce by stewing the apples until soft
2. Sweeten and beat
3. Add the beaten whites of eggs
4. Pile on nice white dish
5. Serve with a soft custard made from the yolks of the eggs
