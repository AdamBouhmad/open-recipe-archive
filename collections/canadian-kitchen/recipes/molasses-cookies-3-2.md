## Ingredients

- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup yellow sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 large teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- salt
- flour to mix as soft as can be rolled

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together.
