## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 pint milk
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- vanilla flavoring
- butter

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add 3 eggs beaten in 1 cup milk
3. Add 3 cups flour, 2 teaspoons cream tartar, 1 teaspoon soda
4. For middle cream: let 1 pint milk come to a boil
5. Add 1/4 cup flour, 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs
6. Flavor with vanilla and a lump of butter
