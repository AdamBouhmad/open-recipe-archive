## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes
- 2 qts. water
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup salt
- 1 yeast cake
- A handful of hops

## Directions

1. Tie the hops in a piece of cheesecloth and cook in with potatoes.
2. When done put through the colander; add salt and sugar.
3. When luke warm add the dissolved yeast cake.
4. Let stand in a warm place, when it will be ready for use; after keep in a cool place.
