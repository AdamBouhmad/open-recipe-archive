## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoonful gelatine
- juice of 4 lemons
- grated rind 1 lemon
- 1 cup sherry
- 2 eggs (whites)
- 2 tablespoonfuls powdered sugar
- green coloring

## Directions

1. Dissolve one cupful of sugar in one pint of boiling water.
2. Take from the fire and add gelatine which has been soaked until soft in half a cupful of water.
3. Add the juice of lemons and the grated rind.
4. Let stand for half an hour, then strain.
5. Add sherry and three cupfuls of cold water and color green.
6. Turn into the freezer.
7. When half frozen, add meringue made by beating together the whites of eggs and powdered sugar until stiff and glassy.
8. Finish the freezing, pack and set away until serving time.
