## Ingredients

- 1 quart chopped cabbage
- 1 quart boiled beets
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 quart chopped cabbage, 1 quart boiled beets, 2 cups brown sugar, 1 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon pepper, 1 cup grated horseradish
2. cover with cold vinegar, and keep from the air
