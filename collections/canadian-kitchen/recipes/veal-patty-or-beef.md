## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. raw leg of veal
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 8 tablespoonfuls of rolled crackers
- 3 tablespoonfuls cream
- butter size of egg
- pepper
- water
- cracker crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Chop veal very fine
2. Mould into a loaf
3. Put into pan with a little water
4. Sprinkle with cracker crumbs and small bits of butter on the top
5. An egg may be added
6. Bake two hours and eat cold
