## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 lemons
- 2 bottles rum
- 1 bottle brandy
- 1 pt. lime juice
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 bottle fresh milk
- 2 lbs. sugar
- one and one-half bottles water

## Directions

1. Steep rinds cut from lemons in brandy for three days with a grated nutmeg; shake frequently.
2. Dissolve sugar in one and one-half bottles water, add rum, lime juice and the above lemon brandy.
3. Bring one bottle of fresh milk to a boil, and that instant pour it into the other ingredients, stirring constantly.
4. Let stand twenty-four hours and strain through double flannel; cork well. Will keep any time.
