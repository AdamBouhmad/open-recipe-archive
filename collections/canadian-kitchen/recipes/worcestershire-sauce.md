## Ingredients

- 1/2 bushel ripe tomatoes
- 1 quart cider vinegar
- 10 lemons cut in pieces
- 1 pound salt
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 3/4 pound allspice
- 1 ounce ground cloves

## Directions

1. Cook slowly 2 hours
2. Put through sieve
3. Add juice of 1 lemon
4. Bottle and seal hot
