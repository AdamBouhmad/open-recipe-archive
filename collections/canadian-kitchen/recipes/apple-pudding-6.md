## Ingredients

- sliced apples
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fill a buttered baking dish with sliced apples
2. Pour over the top a batter made of 1 tablespoon of butter, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 egg, 1/2 cup sweet milk and 1 cup flour, in which has been sifted 1 teaspoon baking powder
3. Bake in a moderate oven from 30 to 45 minutes
4. Serve with cream and sugar or pudding sauce
