## Ingredients

- 6 bottles claret
- 6 bottles soda water
- 4 lemons
- 6 wine glasses curagao
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Peel lemons quite thin, throw the rind in bowl and then squeeze in the lemons; put a lump of ice in bowl first.
2. Then pour claret on top of it, putting claret and soda in last. Sugar to taste. For about fifty people.
