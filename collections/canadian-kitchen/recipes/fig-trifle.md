## Ingredients

- 6 figs
- 3 oz. shelled walnuts
- 1 glass liquor
- 1 dessertspoonful sugar
- 1 pt. cream

## Directions

1. Cut the figs with a sharp knife into fine dice
2. Chop the walnuts coarsely
3. Whip the cream
4. Sweeten with sugar to taste
5. Lightly stir in the figs and walnuts
6. Flavor with the liqueur
7. Serve in custard glasses
