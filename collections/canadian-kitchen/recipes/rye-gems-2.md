## Ingredients

- 1 pint of warm milk
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- A pinch of salt
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- Rye flour enough to make a thin batter

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in warm milk
2. Add a pinch of salt and two eggs, well beaten
3. Add rye flour enough to make a thin batter
4. Bake in gem-pans
