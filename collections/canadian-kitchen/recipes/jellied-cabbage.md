## Ingredients

- 1 head white cabbage
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup vinegar
- a little salt
- 1 package gelatine

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage
2. Mix with lemon juice, sugar, vinegar and salt
3. Dissolve gelatine in water
4. Mix with cabbage
5. Put in a mould when jellied
6. Serve with any salad dressing
