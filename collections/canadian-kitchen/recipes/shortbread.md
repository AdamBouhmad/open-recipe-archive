## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 2 lbs. flour
- 1/2 lb. brown sugar
- Sweet almonds
- Caraway comfits

## Directions

1. Put butter into a basin, squeeze till quite soft, squeeze into it flour and sugar and almonds, chopped fine.
2. Mix all well together, cut into cakes one-half an inch thick.
3. Bake in a slow oven.
