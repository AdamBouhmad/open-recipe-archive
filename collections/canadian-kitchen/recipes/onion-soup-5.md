## Ingredients

- 1 qt. milk
- 6 large onions
- Yolks of four eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cup of cream
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Put butter in frying-pan, cut onion in thin slices and drop in butter
2. Stir until they begin to cook, then cover tight and set back where they will simmer and not burn for one-half hour
3. Put milk on to boil; add the dry flour to onions, stir constantly over fire for three minutes
4. Pour mixture into milk and cook for fifteen minutes; strain
5. Season with salt and pepper and return soup to fire
6. Beat yolks thoroughly, add them to the cream and stir into soup
7. Cook three minutes, stirring constantly
8. Pour over fried croutons in tureen and serve
