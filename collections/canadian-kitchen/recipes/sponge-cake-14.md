## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- Juice and rind of 1 lemon
- 1 scant cup flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately
2. when whites are very stiff beat into them one-half a cup of sugar
3. beat the yolks and add the other half cup of sugar
4. beat for five minutes by the clock
5. add to yolks rind and juice of lemon
6. now beat yolks and whites together and scant cup of flour stirred in quickly
7. Sprinkle top of cake with sugar when in cake-tin before putting in the oven
8. Bake one-half an hour
