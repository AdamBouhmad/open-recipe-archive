## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- salt
- sliced beet root
- vinegar
- mace
- bruised ginger
- whole pepper
- cloves

## Directions

1. Slice cabbage into a sieve
2. Sprinkle each layer with salt
3. Let the whole drain 3 days
4. Add some sliced beet root
5. Place the whole in a jar
6. Pour boiling vinegar over
7. Mace, bruised ginger, whole pepper and cloves may be boiled in the vinegar for improvement
