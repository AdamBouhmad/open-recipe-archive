## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ cup sweet milk
- ⅜ teaspoon soda
- 1½ teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 2 good cups flour

## Directions

1. Stir eggs and butter first of all to a cream and sugar too along with it
2. Add flour, soda, and cream tartar
3. Bake in a medium hot oven, not too fast
4. Ice if you wish when cool
5. Flavor with lemon
