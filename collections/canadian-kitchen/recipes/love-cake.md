## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Make base with 1 cup sugar, 3/4 cup butter, 2 eggs, 1 cup sweet milk, 3 teaspoons baking powder, and 2 1/2 cups flour
2. Take out for two layers
3. Add to third layer 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 3/4 teaspoon cloves, and 1 cup raisins
4. Put third layer between an icing
