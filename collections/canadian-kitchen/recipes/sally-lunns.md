## Ingredients

- 1 qt flour
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- butter size of egg
- little salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 cups milk
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Stir well together
2. Add two eggs not beaten, two cups milk
3. Mix all
4. Bake in muffin rings
