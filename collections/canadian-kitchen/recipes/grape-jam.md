## Ingredients

- grapes
- water
- 3/4 pound sugar per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Separate skin from pulp of grapes, keeping them in separate dishes
2. Put pulp in preserving kettle with a teacup of water
3. When thoroughly heated, run them through a colander to separate the seeds
4. Put your skins with them and weigh
5. To each pound of fruit put 3/4 of a pound of sugar, add merely enough to keep from burning
6. Cook slowly 3/4 hour
