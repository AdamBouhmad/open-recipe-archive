## Ingredients

- cold roast beef
- cold potatoes and onions
- 1 cup rich gravy
- 1 cup boiling water
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Slice meat in small pieces and also potatoes
2. Put a layer of meat, then a layer of potatoes, then a little onion, pepper and salt
3. Continue until dish is full
4. Add one cup rich gravy and 1 cup boiling water
5. Make a crust as you would for biscuits, only a little richer
6. Cover pie and bake in medium oven for 3/4 hour
