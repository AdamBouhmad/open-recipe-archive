## Ingredients

- potatoes
- green cucumbers sliced
- onions
- salad dressing

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes with jackets on, let cool
2. Peel and slice 2 layers in bottom of dish
3. Add a layer of green cucumbers (sliced), and layer of onions
4. Pour on salad dressing and repeat until dish is full
5. Can be made night before if required
