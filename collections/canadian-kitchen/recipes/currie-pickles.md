## Ingredients

- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 tablespoon currie powder
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon tumeric
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 dozen large cucumbers
- 1 dozen onions
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice cucumbers and onions
2. Sprinkle with salt
3. Let stand over night
4. Drain thoroughly in the morning
5. Add the sugar and spices
6. Let it cook until tender
