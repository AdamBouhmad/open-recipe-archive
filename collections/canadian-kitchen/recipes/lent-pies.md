## Ingredients

- Two tablespoons of ground rice
- one pint of new milk
- five eggs
- sugar to taste
- two bay leaves
- pie crust

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoons of ground rice with one pint of new milk.
2. Add five eggs and sweeten to taste, flavoring with two bay leaves.
3. Cover pie plates with a good crust.
4. Pour in mixture and bake.
