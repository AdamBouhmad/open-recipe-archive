## Ingredients

- very short paste
- currants
- brown sugar
- syrup

## Directions

1. Make a very short paste and roll lightly (always in one direction, never back and forth), and cut into squares about five inches.
2. Place on each square currants which have been heated in a syrup of brown sugar and fold over the sides so as to make a square cake with round opening.
3. Put a little syrup in each cake before baking in a very hot oven.
