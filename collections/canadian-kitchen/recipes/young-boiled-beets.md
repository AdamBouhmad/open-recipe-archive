## Ingredients

- young beets
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. After washing them, boil three-quarters of an hour, scrape, slice, and pour over them a tablespoonful of butter, two of vinegar, and a little pepper and salt.
