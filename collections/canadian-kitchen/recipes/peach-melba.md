## Ingredients

- one pint milk
- yolks of five eggs
- vanilla flavoring
- peaches or pears
- raspberry syrup

## Directions

1. Make the custard with milk and eggs, vanilla essence to taste
2. make this custard into an ice
3. When ready for the melba, take the ice out of the freezing machine and place in rather a deep dish
4. put peaches or pears on the top of the ice
5. pour raspberry syrup over the whole and serve
6. To make the raspberry syrup, take a small jar of raspberry jam and pass through a fine sieve
7. take a very small bottle of raspberry syrup and well mix with the jam as the syrup alone is not thick enough
