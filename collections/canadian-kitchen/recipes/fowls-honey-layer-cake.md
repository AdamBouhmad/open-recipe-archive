## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup honey
- 3 eggs beaten
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1.5 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream the honey and butter together
2. Add the beaten eggs and milk
3. Add 2 cups flour containing 1.5 teaspoons baking powder previously stirred in
4. Stir in flour to make a stiff batter
5. Bake in jelly-tins
6. When the cakes are cold, spread finely flavored candied honey between layers
