## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs beaten separately
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 1/2 cups sifted flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups raisins
- 1 ounce peel

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake in slow oven
