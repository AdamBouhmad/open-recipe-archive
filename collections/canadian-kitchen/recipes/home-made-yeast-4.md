## Ingredients

- 4 medium-sized potatoes
- 1/4 cup hops
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 2 level tablespoons salt
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes, drain away the water, and mash the potatoes until free from lumps.
2. Pour the boiling water, which may contain the water drained from the potatoes, over the hops and simmer 15 minutes.
3. Measure into a three-quart bowl, or crock, the flour, sugar and salt, and mix them thoroughly.
4. Strain the hop water, and add at once to the crock, stirring rapidly all the time.
5. Add the mashed potatoes and give the whole a thorough beating.
6. Cool to 70° or 80° F., and add one White Swan Yeast Cake or one dry yeast cake soaked in 1/4 cup of warm water for half an hour.
7. Keep at a temperature of 70° F. for 3 or 4 hours, stirring down as often as it comes to the top.
8. Bottle and keep in a cool place. Do not cork it tightly at first.
