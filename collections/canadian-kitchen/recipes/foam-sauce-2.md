## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- yolks of two eggs
- one wineglass of sherry
- beaten whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the yolks of two eggs and a wineglass of sherry
3. Add the beaten whites
4. Stand in a bowl of hot water
5. Stir for one minute
