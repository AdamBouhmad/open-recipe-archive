## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup chocolate
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 tins and ice with white icing
