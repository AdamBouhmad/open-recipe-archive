## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 3/4 cup stock
- 1/2 can tomatoes
- 1 sprig parsley
- 1 sprig thyme
- 2 cloves
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper-corn
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Wash rice
2. Steam with stock until stock is boiled away
3. Add tomatoes which have been immersed 20 minutes, with seasoning and sugar, then rubbed through a sieve
4. Add other materials
5. Cool and shape
