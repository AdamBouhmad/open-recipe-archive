## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1/4 pound butter
- 3/4 cup sour milk
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour
- 1 cup seeded raisins
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Save whites of 2 eggs for icing
2. Mix eggs, sugar, butter, sour milk and syrup
3. Add nutmeg, cinnamon, allspice and soda mixed in flour enough to stiffen
4. Add seeded raisins
5. Beat well and bake in two layers
6. For icing: Boil 1 cup white sugar in 2 tablespoons water till stringy
7. Beat whites of 2 eggs well
8. Add sugar and eggs together and beat well
9. Flavor with vanilla
