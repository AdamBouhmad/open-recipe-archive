## Ingredients

- beets
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean nicely, but do not pare them, leaving on a short piece of its stalk
2. put over, boil in hot water
3. Young beets will cook tender in an hour, old beets require several hours boiling
4. When done, skin quickly while hot
5. slice thin into your vegetable dish
6. put on salt, pepper and a little butter
7. put over a little vinegar and serve hot or cold
