## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- butter the size of an egg
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 small teaspoons baking powder
- flour to make a stiff batter
- vanilla for flavoring
- chocolate for icing

## Directions

1. Mix egg, sugar, butter, sweet milk, baking powder and flour to make a stiff batter
2. Flavor with vanilla
3. Bake in 1 large tin or in 2 small ones
4. Ice with chocolate
