## Ingredients

- 1 slice dry toast
- butter
- 6 oysters
- 1 half teacupful oyster liquor
- 1 half cupful milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a nice slice of dry toast, butter it and lay it on a hot dish
2. Put six oysters, half a teacupful of their own liquor, and half a cupful of milk, into a tin cup or basin, and boil one minute
3. Season with a little butter, pepper and salt
4. Pour over the toast and serve
