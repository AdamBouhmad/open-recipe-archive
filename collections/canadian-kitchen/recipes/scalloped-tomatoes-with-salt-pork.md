## Ingredients

- large ripe tomatoes
- 2 slices fat salt pork
- 1 small onion
- breadcrumbs
- sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and slice tomatoes.
2. Chop salt pork and onion.
3. Place a layer of tomatoes in a pudding-dish, pepper and salt lightly, sprinkle with a very little sugar and with the pork and onion.
4. Cover with crumbs and continue using the ingredients in this order until the dish is full.
5. Have the top layer crumbs.
6. Bake, covered, half an hour, then uncover and brown ten minutes.
7. Serve in the dish in which they were baked.
