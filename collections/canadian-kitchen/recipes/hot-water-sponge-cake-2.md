## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3/4 cup hot water

## Directions

1. Sift flour and cream tartar together
2. Whip sugar and egg to a cream
3. Add sifted flour
4. Add soda in hot water last
5. Bake in a hot oven
