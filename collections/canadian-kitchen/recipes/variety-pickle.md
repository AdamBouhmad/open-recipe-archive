## Ingredients

- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 4 quarts cabbage
- 1 quart onions
- 4 green peppers
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1 ounce tumeric powder
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 2 tablespoons allspice
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 gallon vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop green tomatoes, cabbage, onions, and green peppers fine
2. Add celery seed, tumeric powder, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, salt, sugar, and vinegar
3. Cook until tender
