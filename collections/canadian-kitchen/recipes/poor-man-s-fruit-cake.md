## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 lemon peel
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour enough to make a batter which will rise in the centre and not fall over

## Directions

1. Bake in a slow oven 1 hour
