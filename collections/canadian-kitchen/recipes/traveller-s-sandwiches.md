## Ingredients

- sardines
- ham
- pickles
- mustard
- pepper
- catsup
- Worcestershire sauce
- salt
- vinegar
- buttered bread

## Directions

1. Chop sardines, ham and a few pickles finely together
2. Season with mustard, pepper, catsup, Worcestershire sauce, salt, vinegar, etc., to taste
3. Spread between nicely buttered bread
