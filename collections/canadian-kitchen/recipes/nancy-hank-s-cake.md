## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/4 cup milk and 3 eggs mixed together
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 4 tablespoons chocolate
- 4 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Mix 1 1/2 cups white sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 1/4 cup flour
2. Mix 1/4 cup milk and 3 eggs together and add
3. Add 1 1/2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder
4. Boil 4 tablespoons chocolate with 4 tablespoons milk
5. Cool chocolate mixture before adding to cake
6. Bake
