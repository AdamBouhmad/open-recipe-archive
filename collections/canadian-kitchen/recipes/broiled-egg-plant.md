## Ingredients

- egg-plant
- three tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- juice of half a lemon
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into rather thin slices
2. Lay in salted ice-water for an hour
3. Spread upon a soft towel and cover with another, patting and pressing the slices until they are entirely dry
4. Leave them for ten minutes in a mixture of three tablespoonfuls of olive oil and the juice of half a lemon
5. Sprinkle them with salt and pepper
6. Broil quickly upon a wire broiler for twelve minutes to cook both sides
