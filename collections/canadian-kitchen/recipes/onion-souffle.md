## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sauce
- 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 cup cooked onions (chopped)
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/4 teaspoonful white pepper
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- whites beaten dry
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Soak the crumbs in cold milk, add white sauce, onions, yolks, pepper, salt, and last beaten whites.
2. Turn into buttered mould and bake forty-five minutes.
