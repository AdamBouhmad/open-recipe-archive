## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- drawn butter or sauce piquante

## Directions

1. Pare off the stems and the lower and coarser leaves
2. With a sharp knife trim the tops evenly, and take out the hard core
3. Wash and lay in cold water ten minutes
4. Shake off the wet and cook in boiling, salted water for thirty-five minutes, or until the bottoms are tender
5. If large, cut into halves; if of moderate size, serve whole with drawn butter or sauce piquante poured over them
