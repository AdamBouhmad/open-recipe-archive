## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe plums
- 1 dozen white onions
- 1 pint vinegar
- 4 lbs. white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful cayenne
- 1 teaspoonful black pepper
- 2 oz. stick cinnamon broken in pieces
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Stone the plums.
2. Chop onions and plums very fine.
3. Put the plums on to cook in a saucepan, the onions on in the vinegar.
4. Cook until done.
5. Then add the plums, also sugar and seasoning.
6. It will take several hours to cook, doing slowly at the back of the stove.
7. When cool cork tight in bottles.
