## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- cold water
- 1 small onion
- boiling water
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon allspice

## Directions

1. Skin and draw the hare, retaining only the heart
2. Wash in cold water
3. Separate the legs from the body, cut the body into sections and put the whole into cold water to cover, over a slow fire
4. Bring to a boil, skim off any scum that may gather and let boil slowly for 1 1/2 hours, adding boiling water if necessary from time to time
5. Then add 1 small onion, finely minced, and boil 3/4 hour longer
6. At the end of the 2 hours, lift the meat on to the platter
7. Add boiling water to the liquor to make 1 1/2 pints gravy approximately
8. Stir in thickening made with 1 tablespoon flour mixed with cold water to a thin paste
9. Add salt and pepper to taste, and 3/4 teaspoon allspice
10. Let boil up well, pour over the meat and serve at once
