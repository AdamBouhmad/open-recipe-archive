## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
- 4 eggs
- 1 pint cream
- 1 scant cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix butter, sugar, mustard, salt and cornstarch to a cream
2. Add beaten eggs
3. Add cream
4. Add vinegar a little at a time
5. Cook in a double boiler until thick like cream
