## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 eggs
- 3 1/2 cups oatmeal or rolled oats
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs and beat
3. Sift in flour, salt and baking powder and beat
4. Stir in rolled oats
5. Drop from teaspoon on greased pan
6. Bake in hot oven till brown
