## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound of cheese
- a small piece of butter
- 1 tablespoon milk
- pepper to taste
- 2 slices of bread
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Melt in the oven 3/4 pound of cheese, a small piece of butter, with 1 tablespoon milk, and pepper to taste
2. While the cheese is melting, toast 2 slices of bread and poach two eggs
3. Butter the toast
4. When cheese is in a thick cream, pour the melted cheese over it and place an egg on each slice
5. Serve very hot
