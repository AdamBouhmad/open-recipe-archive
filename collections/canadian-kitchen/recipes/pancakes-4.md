## Ingredients

- nine eggs
- flour enough to make very stiff
- two tablespoonfuls of ginger
- a little salt
- milk enough to make a nice thin batter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very light
2. Mix in flour enough to make very stiff
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of ginger and a little salt
4. Add milk enough to make a nice thin batter
