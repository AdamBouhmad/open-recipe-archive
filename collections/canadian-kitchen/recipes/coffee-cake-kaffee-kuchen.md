## Ingredients

- bread dough
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Spread bread dough 3/4 inches thick upon buttered pans
2. Let rise
3. Brush with melted butter
4. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
5. Bake
