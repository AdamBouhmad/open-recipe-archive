## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup blackstrap
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Combine eggs, brown sugar, and blackstrap
2. Add melted butter
3. Add flour, ginger, cinnamon, and allspice
4. Put soda in boiling water and add last
