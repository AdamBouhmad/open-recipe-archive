## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. muscatel raisins
- 1 1/2 lbs. currants
- 1 lb. sultana raisins
- 2 lbs. moist sugar
- 2 lbs. breadcrumbs
- 2 lbs. chopped suet
- 6 oz. mixed peel
- rind of 2 lemons
- 1 oz. ground nutmeg
- 1 oz. ground cinnamon
- 2 oz. chopped almonds
- 1/2 pt. brandy
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all the dry ingredients together and moisten with the brandy and well-beaten eggs.
2. Boil about six hours.
