## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup shortening
- 3 teaspoons sour cream
- 3 teaspoons syrup
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- flour to roll out

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients and roll out
