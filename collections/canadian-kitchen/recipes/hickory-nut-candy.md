## Ingredients

- 1 cup nut meats
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 cups sugar
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water without stirring until thick enough to spin a thread
2. Set off to cool
3. Stir until white, and add nut meats
4. Turn into a flat tin and when cold cut in squares
