## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound suet
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- a little salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- cold water

## Directions

1. Mix stiff with cold water
2. Tie in a floured cloth
3. Boil 2 hours
4. Serve with all kinds of hot meat dinners
