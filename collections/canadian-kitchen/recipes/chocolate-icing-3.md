## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 ounce unsweetened chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar, milk and cream of tartar together for 6 minutes
2. Remove from stove and add butter, chocolate and vanilla
3. Beat until proper consistency to spread on cake
