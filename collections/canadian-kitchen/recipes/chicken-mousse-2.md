## Ingredients

- 1 lb. cooked ham
- 1 pt. cream
- 1 pt. aspic jelly (liquid)
- A very little glaze
- 1 liqueur of brandy
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Have ready a saucepan with the aspic just warm, mince the ham and pass through a wire sieve, add it to the aspic with the glaze brandy and pepper.
2. Whip the cream a little thick and add half a pint.
3. Whisk over a slow fire until the ingredients are well mixed (do not allow it to get too hot).
4. Take it off the fire and slowly stir in the remaining half pint of cream.
5. Put a little cochineal to make the mousse a nice pink (the color of ham).
6. Pour the whole into a souffle dish and let it stand to get quite cold.
7. When it is quite set, pour over the top a little aspic; let this also set, then ornament with truffles or white of egg.
