## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon flour
- ½ cup raisins cut and seeded
- 1 cup sour cream
- a little salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir all ingredients together
2. Bake with 2 crusts
3. Bake very slowly
