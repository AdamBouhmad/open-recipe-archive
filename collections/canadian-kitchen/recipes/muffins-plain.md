## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup water
- 1 1/2 cups sweet milk
- 3 teaspoonfuls butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and shortening to a cream
2. Add the liquid
3. Sift the flour and baking powder into it
4. Beat well
5. Heat gem irons hot, grease, fill nearly full, and bake in hot oven twenty minutes
