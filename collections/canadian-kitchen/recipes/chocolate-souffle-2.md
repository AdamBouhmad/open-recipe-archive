## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 3 tablespoons Cowan's Perfection Chocolate (grated)
- Whites of 6 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and powdered sugar with grated chocolate for 10 minutes
2. Beat whites of eggs with salt very stiff
3. Fold in whites
4. Turn into a buttered pan
5. Bake 10 minutes in a hot oven
6. Serve immediately
