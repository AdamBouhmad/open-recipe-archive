## Ingredients

- Two potatoes
- Hot water
- Two tomatoes
- Two ears of corn
- One slice of onion
- Two quarts of cold water
- A level tablespoonful butter
- One teaspoon salt
- One-half teaspoon pepper
- One of minced parsley
- One teaspoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Peel and slice two potatoes, parboil them in enough hot water to cover them
2. While they are cooking, chop two tomatoes, slice the corn off two ears of corn, and add one slice of onion
3. Drain the potatoes, and put all on to cook in two quarts of cold water
4. When done, rub all through a colander, return the soup to the pot
5. Add a level tablespoonful butter, one teaspoon salt, one-half teaspoon pepper and one of minced parsley
6. If not thick enough, moisten a teaspoonful of flour with cold water, thin with the soup and stir in; let boil up once, and it is ready for the table
