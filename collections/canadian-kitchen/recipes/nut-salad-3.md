## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped celery
- 2 cups chopped apples
- 1 pound English walnuts
- sweet salad dressing

## Directions

1. Chop celery
2. Chop apples
3. Chop English walnuts
4. Mix together
5. Add sweet salad dressing
6. This may be varied by using canned peas instead of celery
