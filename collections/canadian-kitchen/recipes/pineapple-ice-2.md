## Ingredients

- 1 pint pineapple juice
- juice 2 lemons
- 1 3/4 pints water
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Mix together pineapple juice and lemon juice
2. Add water and sugar boiled together 10 minutes
3. Strain and freeze
4. When frozen remove the dasher
5. Pack mixture in a mould, return to ice and salt; let stand 1 hour
