## Ingredients

- 1 quart molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 level teaspoon soda
- 1 quart shelled peanuts

## Directions

1. Boil molasses, sugar, and butter rapidly until it will snap when dropped in cold water
2. Add a level teaspoon soda, and stir a moment
3. Add 1 quart shelled peanuts
4. Stir enough to mix well
5. Pour in greased tin
6. When partly cold, pull
