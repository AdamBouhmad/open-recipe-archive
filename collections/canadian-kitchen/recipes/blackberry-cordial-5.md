## Ingredients

- 1 quart blackberry juice
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls grated nutmeg
- 2 teaspoonfuls cinnamon
- 2 teaspoonfuls allspice
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 pint good brandy

## Directions

1. Pound and squeeze enough blackberries through a coarse muslin bag to make a quart of juice
2. Put this into an agate-iron or porcelain-lined kettle, with a pound of sugar
3. Tie the spices up in little thin muslin bags
4. Stir the sugar until dissolved
5. Set over the fire and cook together, after the boil begins, fifteen minutes
6. Take off the scum
7. Turn into a jar, and cover closely while it cools
8. When perfectly cold strain out the spices
9. Add a pint of good brandy
10. Bottle and seal
