## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- just enough flour to roll

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out
3. Bake quickly
