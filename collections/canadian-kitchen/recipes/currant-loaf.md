## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 5 teaspoons baking powder or 1 teaspoon soda and 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- butter or shortening size of 3/2 egg
- a little nutmeg or spice to taste
- 1 cup currants or raisins
- 3/2 teaspoon ratafia
- cold water

## Directions

1. Sift flour with baking powder or soda and cream of tartar
2. Mix in sugar, salt, butter or shortening, nutmeg or spice, currants or raisins, and ratafia
3. Wet with cold water to make like a loaf of bread
4. Put in a greased pan
5. Let stand an hour to raise in a warm place
6. Bake an hour
