## Ingredients

- 6 dozen small cucumbers
- 1 quart onions, sliced
- 5 cents mustard seed
- 5 cents celery seed
- 2 ounces olive oil
- 1 teaspoon tumeric
- 1 teaspoon cayenne
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon curry powder
- 3 cups brown sugar
- 3 pints vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice cucumbers and onions fine
2. Sprinkle 1 cup salt over and let stand over night
3. Drain
4. Add vinegar and all ingredients
5. Boil 10 minutes and bottle
