## Ingredients

- 1 dozen ears corn
- 1 head cabbage
- 1½ quarts vinegar
- salt
- 3 cups sugar
- 3 red peppers
- ¾ tablespoon mustard
- 4 or 6 onions chopped fine

## Directions

1. Cook gently until tender
