## Ingredients

- 2 oz. flour
- 2 oz. butter
- 3 oz. cheese
- 1 egg
- A little salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Grate the cheese and mix all together; roll out and cut into thin strips with a pastry cutter and bake in a flat tin.
