## Ingredients

- One pound sifted flour
- Two cups sour or buttermilk
- One teaspoon soda
- A little salt in the milk
- If sweet milk is used, to one teaspoonful of soda put two of cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Sift the soda well through the flour with salt in the milk.
