## Ingredients

- 8 pounds grapes
- 4 pounds sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 2 tablespoons ground cloves

## Directions

1. Take 8 pounds grapes and cook until very soft
2. Put through a sieve, return to kettle
3. Add 4 pounds sugar, 1 quart vinegar, 1 tablespoon cinnamon and allspice, and 2 tablespoons ground cloves
4. Boil till thick
