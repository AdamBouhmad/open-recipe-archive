## Ingredients

- 20 oysters
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 teaspoonful, large, of anchovy paste
- A little cayenne
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and the anchovy together in the blazer, put in the oysters, cook three minutes
2. Add the cayenne and lemon-juice and serve on buttered toast or breakfast-biscuit
