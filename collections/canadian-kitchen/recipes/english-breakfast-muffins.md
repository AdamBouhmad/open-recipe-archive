## Ingredients

- 3 level teaspoonfuls butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 3 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 pt milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Soften the butter, add to it the yolks of two eggs and beat
2. Add milk and mix
3. Add flour, salt and baking powder and beat well
4. Stir in well-beaten whites
5. Bake in quick oven twenty-five minutes in well-greased rings
