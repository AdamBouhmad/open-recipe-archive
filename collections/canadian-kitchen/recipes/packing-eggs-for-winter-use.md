## Ingredients

- eggs
- barley

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a wooden box with barley which has been heated in the oven to thoroughly dry it, but not enough to cook or overheat it and destroy the life of the grain
2. Stand the eggs, small end down, in the barley, allowing none to touch each other
3. Cover with barley and continue thus until box is filled
4. Cover well with barley and put in a dry place
5. Do not place paper over or layers of paper between
