## Ingredients

- sardines
- mashed yolks of hard-boiled eggs
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Mix sardines with equal quantity of mashed yolks of hard-boiled eggs
2. Arrange in nests of lettuce leaves
3. Serve with mayonnaise
