## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- a small tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
- bananas
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Slice bananas thin
2. Sprinkle granulated sugar over them and put between layers
