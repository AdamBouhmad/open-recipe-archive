## Ingredients

- one cupful of freshly ground coffee
- three large cupfuls of freshly boiled water

## Directions

1. Make as directed in last recipe, running through the filter three times.
2. Serve in small cups, and give the drinkers their choice of sugar or no sugar. Black coffee is a good digestive agent and is far more wholesome than coffee mixed with cream or milk.
