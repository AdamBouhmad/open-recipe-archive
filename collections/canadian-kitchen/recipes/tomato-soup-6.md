## Ingredients

- 1 quart stock
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1/2 teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 small bay leaf
- 3 cloves
- 1/2 each onion, celery, carrot and raw ham cut in dice
- Salt and pepper
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 4 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cook vegetables and ham in butter 5 minutes
2. Add flour and seasonings, cook 3 minutes
3. Add tomatoes, cover and simmer 1/2 hour
4. Rub through strainer, add hot stock
