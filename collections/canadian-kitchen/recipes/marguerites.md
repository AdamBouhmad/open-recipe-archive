## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- marshmallows
- whites of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons shredded cocoanut
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- saltines

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until it threads
2. Remove to back of range and drop in marshmallows cut in pieces
3. Let stand to dissolve, then gradually pour on to the whites of 2 eggs beaten until foamy
4. Add 2 tablespoons shredded cocoanut and 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
5. When partially cool add 1 cup chopped walnuts
6. Spread on saltines and brown slightly in hot oven
