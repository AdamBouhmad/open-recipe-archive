## Ingredients

- milk
- prepared rennet
- cloth
- cool water
- fresh cream
- powdered loaf-sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. One gallon of milk will make a moderate dish
2. Put one spoonful of prepared rennet to each quart of milk
3. When you find that it has become curd, tie it loosely in a thin cloth and hang it to drain
4. Do not wring or press the cloth
5. When drained, put the curd into a mug and set in cool water, which must be frequently changed
6. When you dish it, if there is whey in the mug, ladle it gently out without pressing the curd
7. Lay it on a deep dish, and pour fresh cream over it
8. Have powdered loaf-sugar to eat with it; also hand the nutmeg grater
