## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup cold water
- 3 cups flour
- 3 tablespoons boiling water
- flavor to taste

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to cream
2. Add the eggs well beaten
3. Take 3 tablespoons out of the cup of cold water and just before putting in pan to bake, add 3 tablespoons boiling water
4. Flavor to taste
