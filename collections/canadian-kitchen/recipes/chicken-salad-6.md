## Ingredients

- white meat of cold boiled or roasted chicken or turkey
- 3/4 same bulk celery (chopped)
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 raw egg
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 3 teaspoons salad oil
- 2 teaspoons white sugar
- 3/4 teacup vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince the meat, removing all fat gristle and skin
2. Mix with celery
3. Pour dressing over when cold
