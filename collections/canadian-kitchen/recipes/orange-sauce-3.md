## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 pint boiling water
- juice and rind of 2 oranges and lemons
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. let simmer 10 minutes
2. add juice and rind of oranges and lemons
3. add butter
