## Ingredients

- 2.5 pounds sugar
- 1.75 pints water
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon tartaric acid
- 1 teaspoon extract of lemon

## Directions

1. Put into a kettle sugar, water, and cream tartar
2. Let boil until it becomes brittle when dropped into cold water
3. When sufficiently done take off fire and pour into a shallow dish which has been greased with a little butter
4. When this has been cooled so that it can be handled, add tartaric acid and extract of lemon
5. Work them into the mass until evenly distributed
