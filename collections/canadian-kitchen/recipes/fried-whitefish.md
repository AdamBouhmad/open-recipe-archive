## Ingredients

- fish
- flour
- dripping

## Directions

1. Scale and draw the fish
2. Wash in cold water
3. Cut off the head and fins
4. Split down both sides of the back
5. Cut the lengths into sections of the desired size
6. Roll in flour
7. Cook in plenty of very hot dripping
8. Do not let burn
9. It is done when well browned on both sides
