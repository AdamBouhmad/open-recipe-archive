## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs beaten lightly
- a little salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4 cup scalded milk
- 1 teaspoon butter melted in the milk
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1/4 cup butter creamed
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons cocoa
- 2 teaspoons strong coffee

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs beaten lightly, a little salt, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1 cup flour, and 1 teaspoon baking powder
2. Last of all add 3/4 cup scalded milk with 1 teaspoon butter melted in the milk
3. Bake
4. For filling: Cream 1 cup powdered sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 2 teaspoons cocoa, and 2 teaspoons strong coffee all together
5. Spread filling between layers
