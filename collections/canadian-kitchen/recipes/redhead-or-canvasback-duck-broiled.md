## Ingredients

- ducks
- salad oil
- lemon-juice
- currant or grape jelly
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and wipe with a soft, damp cloth within and without
2. Split down the back and flatten the protuberant breast-bone with the broadside of a hatchet
3. Leave them in a marinade of salad oil and lemon-juice for one hour, setting them in cold place
4. Without wiping them, broil over red, clear coals for twenty minutes, if they are plump and large; less time will do for small birds
5. Turn them twice
6. Serve with currant or grape jelly
7. When dishing put upon each breast a teaspoonful of butter beaten to a cream with lemon-juice and finely chopped parsley
