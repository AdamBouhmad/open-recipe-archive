## Ingredients

- 1 stalk celery to 6 beets
- beets
- celery
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Worcester sauce
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup milk
- butter
- 1 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Boil beets
2. chop celery and beets both fine
3. prepare dressing: 2 teaspoons mustard, 1 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup vinegar, 1 tablespoon Worcester sauce, 3 eggs, 3/4 cup milk, a little butter, 1 teaspoon pepper
4. mix dressing with beets and celery
