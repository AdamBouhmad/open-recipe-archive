## Ingredients

- One can of salmon minced
- Four eggs
- Four tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- One-half cup of breadcrumb
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Pick fish fine; rub butter smooth
2. Beat the crumbs into the eggs, and season before mixing
3. Steam one hour in a buttered mold
4. For the sauce: One cup of milk thickened with one tablespoonful of cornstarch, one tablespoonful butter
5. Add the liquor off the fish and one raw egg, then pour over the loaf
