## Ingredients

- sour milk
- boiling water
- sweet cream
- celery salt

## Directions

1. Take sour milk, cream and all, and scald with boiling water poured over it until the milk separates from the whey
2. Strain off the water and let the milk drain in a cheesecloth bag for 10 hours
3. Remove and run through a grinder to make it fine and creamy
4. Mix with sweet cream and season to taste
5. Celery salt gives a tasty flavor to cottage cheese
