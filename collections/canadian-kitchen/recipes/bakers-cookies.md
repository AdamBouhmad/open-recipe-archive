## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- flour to make stiff

## Directions

1. Scald molasses and when cold add sugar, egg, cold water, vinegar, soda, and ginger
2. Add flour to make stiff
3. Roll thin and bake in a quick oven
