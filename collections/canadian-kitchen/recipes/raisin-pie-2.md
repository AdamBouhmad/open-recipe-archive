## Ingredients

- Yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sweet cream
- Pinch of salt
- 1 cup seeded raisins chopped fine
- Flour
- Vanilla flavoring
- Baked crust
- Whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook till it thickens
2. Flavor with vanilla
3. If not quite thick enough add a little flour
4. Put into a baked crust
5. Beat the whites of the 2 eggs
6. Add a little sugar and vanilla and spread over the top
7. Set in the oven to harden a little
