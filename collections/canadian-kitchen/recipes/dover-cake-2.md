## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 3/4 pound butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- flavoring to taste

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Bake 1 hour slowly
