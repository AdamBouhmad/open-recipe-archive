## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/2 pound lard
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup good cream

## Directions

1. Stir butter, lard, and brown sugar well together
2. Add 1 egg, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1 cup good cream
3. Roll out 1/2 an inch thick and cut in squares
