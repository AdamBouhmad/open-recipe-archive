## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 12 chopped apples
- 6 onions
- 4 peppers
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 cups sugar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients
2. Cook until thick
