## Ingredients

- 2 cups apple sauce
- white of 1 egg
- whipped cream or boiled custard

## Directions

1. Cook apple sauce as dry as possible
2. Press through sieve
3. Sweeten
4. Add the white of 1 egg, beaten stiff
5. Beat briskly
6. Heap in a dish
7. Pour around it whipped cream or boiled custard
