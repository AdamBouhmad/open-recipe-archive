## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped suet
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3/4 teaspoon each of cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt
- 3/4 cup currants
- 3/4 cup seeded raisins
- flour to make middling stiff

## Directions

1. Mix chopped suet, sweet milk, sugar, molasses, soda, cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, currants, and seeded raisins
2. Add flour to make middling stiff
3. Steam 3 hours or longer. Extra cooking does not injure
