## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/4 teaspoon spice (cloves and nutmeg)
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream together 1 1/2 cups brown sugar and 3/4 cup butter
2. Add 2 eggs, 1 cup sweet milk, 1 teaspoon soda, and 1/4 teaspoon spice (cloves and nutmeg)
3. Add flour enough to make a thick batter
4. Bake in a long pan
