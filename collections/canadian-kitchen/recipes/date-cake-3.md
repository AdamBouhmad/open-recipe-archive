## Ingredients

- 1 large cup brown sugar
- butter size of an egg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- pinch salt
- spice to taste
- 2½ cups flour
- 1 pound dates

## Directions

1. Bake in slow oven
