## Ingredients

- 1 pound almonds blanched
- yolk of 1 egg
- whites of 3 eggs beaten stiff
- 3 cups icing sugar

## Directions

1. Roll the nuts to a fine powder
2. Add the yolk of 1 egg and stir well
3. Add the whites of 3 eggs beaten stiff
4. Add 3 cups icing sugar
