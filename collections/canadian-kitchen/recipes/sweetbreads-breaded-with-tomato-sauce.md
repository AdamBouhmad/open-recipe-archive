## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Eggs
- Flour
- Pepper and salt
- Cracker dust or dry breadcrumbs
- Hot boiling lard
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare a batter of eggs, flour, and pepper and salt, beat well together
2. Turn the sweetbreads in this batter until they are all covered with it
3. Turn them afterwards in cracker dust or dry breadcrumbs
4. Fry in hot boiling lard to a fine light brown color
5. Serve with tomato sauce
