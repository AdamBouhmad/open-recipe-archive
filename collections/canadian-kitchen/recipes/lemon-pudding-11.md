## Ingredients

- one quart of water
- two tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- the juice and grated rind of two lemons
- a large cupful of white sugar
- the whites of three eggs
- the yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Put one quart of water to boil
2. When boiling mix in two tablespoonfuls cornstarch, previously dissolved in a little water
3. Add the juice and grated rind of two lemons and a large cupful of white sugar
4. When quite thick beat in the whites of three eggs, beaten stiff
5. Set aside to cool
6. Serve with a thin custard made with the yolks of eggs, flavored with vanilla
