## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of stock
- 1 pint of haricot beans
- pepper
- salt
- cold water
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Soak the beans all night in cold water, drain them and boil in cold water, slightly salted, till quite tender, about one and a half hours
2. Press them through a sieve with a spoon, and add them (leaving out the husks) to the stock, which should be warm
3. Add pepper and salt to taste
4. Boil up once more, and serve with a dish of fried bread cut into small squares
