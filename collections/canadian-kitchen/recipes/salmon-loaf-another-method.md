## Ingredients

- 1 cup salmon
- 3/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon parsley
- 3/4 teaspoon lemon juice
- pepper and onion juice

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients first
2. Beat egg
3. Add milk
4. Add to dry mixture
5. Add lemon and onion juice last
6. Bake in oven till done
7. Serve with white sauce
