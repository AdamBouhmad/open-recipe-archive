## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 cup grated chocolate (3 ounces)
- 1/2 cup milk
- 5 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Cook butter, flour, chocolate and milk over fire, stirring constantly
2. Set it to cool
3. Beat yolks with sugar until creamy
4. Add it to cold mixture, a spoonful at a time
5. Mix thoroughly
6. Fold in beaten whites
7. Turn into a buttered mould
8. Steam 2 to 3 hours
9. Serve with hard sauce
