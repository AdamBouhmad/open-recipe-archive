## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon well blended
- Yolks of two eggs
- 2 pinches of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of cayenne
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- Small piece of onion
- 8 tablespoonfuls milk or cream
- Small piece of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mix well
2. Put in mould and steam
3. Serve with butter sauce
