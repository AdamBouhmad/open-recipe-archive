## Ingredients

- 1 pound fat salt pork chopped fine and free from lean and rind
- 1 pint boiling water
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon nutmeg
- 2 ounces cloves
- 2 pounds raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teacup sifted flour

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the salt pork chopped fine and free from lean and rind
2. Let it stand until nearly cold
3. Add brown sugar, molasses, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves
4. Add raisins and currants
5. Add cream tartar and soda
6. Stir in sifted flour
7. Bake slowly 1 1/2 hours
