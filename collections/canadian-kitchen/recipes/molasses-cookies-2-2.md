## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- 1 egg
- 6 tablespoons boiling water
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- salt according to shortening used
- ginger to taste
- flour to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Add flour to make a soft dough and bake in oven as hot as may be without scorching.
