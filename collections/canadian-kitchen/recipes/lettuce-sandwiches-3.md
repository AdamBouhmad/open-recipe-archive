## Ingredients

- Graham bread
- butter
- mayonnaise dressing
- crisp heart lettuce

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices from the end of a loaf of Graham bread, buttering before slicing.
2. Cut these into rounds with a cake-cutter.
3. Spread each slice with mayonnaise dressing and enclose between every two a leaf of crisp heart lettuce.
4. Trim off the projecting edges of the leaves.
