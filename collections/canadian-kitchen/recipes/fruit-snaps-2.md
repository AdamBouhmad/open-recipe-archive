## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Roll as soft as can be cut
3. Bake
