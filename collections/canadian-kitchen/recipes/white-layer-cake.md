## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup milk
- 2 1/4 cups flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix flour, salt and baking powder
2. Cream butter and add 1/2 of sugar and beat
3. Add beaten egg and remainder of sugar and beat 4 minutes
4. Add flour and milk alternately, beating well
5. Add vanilla
6. Bake in quick oven
