## Ingredients

- chicken prepared as above
- cheesecloth
- bones of the chicken
- slice of carrot
- slice of onion
- parsley
- bay leaf
- three cloves
- twelve peppercorns
- celery
- knuckle of veal
- water

## Directions

1. Roll the chicken lightly in a piece of cheesecloth, tying the ends well.
2. Put in a sauce-pan the bones of the chicken, a slice of carrot and onion, a bouquet containing parsley, one bay leaf, three cloves, twelve peppercorns, celery if convenient, and a knuckle of veal.
3. Add enough water to cover the bed of vegetables and bones.
4. Lay in the chicken.
5. Cover the pot, and let it simmer for four hours.
