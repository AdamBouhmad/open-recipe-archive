## Ingredients

- 1 oz. arrowroot
- 1 qt. milk
- 3 oz. powdered sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- A little lemon peel and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix the arrowroot with a little of the milk to a smooth paste and add to it the egg.
2. Boil the remainder of the milk with the sugar and flavoring, and pour it boiling hot on the arrowroot, keeping it well stirred till nearly cold, when it may be set aside in custard glasses.
