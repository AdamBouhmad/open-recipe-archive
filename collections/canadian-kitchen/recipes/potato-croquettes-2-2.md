## Ingredients

- 3 cups hot potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mash 3 cups hot potatoes
2. Mix with 1 tablespoon butter, 3/4 teaspoon pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon salt
3. When slightly cool, add beaten yolk of egg
4. Mould or shape in balls
5. Dip in beaten white of egg and roll in crumbs
6. Fry until a light brown
