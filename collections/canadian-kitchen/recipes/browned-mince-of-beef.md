## Ingredients

- Remains of cold roast beef
- Breadcrumbs
- 1/2 as much potato, mashed
- 1 cup of gravy
- Seasoning of salt, pepper, mustard and catsup
- butter

## Directions

1. Mince the meat very fine, mix with it the potato, and season well
2. Add the cup of gravy, work all together and make very hot in a saucepan
3. Pile upon a dish, cover with fine breadcrumbs
4. Put bits of butter over the top
5. Brown quickly in the oven
6. Serve in the dish it is baked in
