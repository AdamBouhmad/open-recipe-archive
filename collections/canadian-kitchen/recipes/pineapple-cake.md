## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1 1/8 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 grated pineapple
- 1 grated lemon
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/8 teaspoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, butter, sugar, sweet milk, flour, baking powder, and vanilla
2. Bake in layers
3. For the jelly take 1/4 grated pineapple, 1 grated lemon, 3/4 cup milk, 1/8 teaspoon cornstarch
4. Let it come to a boil, or until it thickens
