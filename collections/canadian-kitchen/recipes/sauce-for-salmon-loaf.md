## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch or flour
- liquid from the salmon
- 1 large spoon butter
- 1 teaspoon tomato catsup
- red pepper and salt to taste
- 1 egg beaten

## Directions

1. Thicken milk with cornstarch or flour
2. Add the liquid from the salmon
3. Add butter and tomato catsup
4. Add red pepper and salt to taste
5. Beat in egg very carefully
6. Can be eaten hot or cold
