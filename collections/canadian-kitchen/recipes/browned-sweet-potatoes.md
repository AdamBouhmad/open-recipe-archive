## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled potatoes into slices one-quarter of an inch thick.
2. Sprinkle them with salt and pepper.
3. Spread with butter, and sprinkle with sugar.
4. Place them in a hot oven to brown.
