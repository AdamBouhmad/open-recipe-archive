## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 pint flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake in a moderate oven
