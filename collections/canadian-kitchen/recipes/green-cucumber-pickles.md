## Ingredients

- 1 gal. vinegar
- 1 oz. ginger root
- 1/2 lb. mustard
- 1 lb. salt
- 2 oz. mustard seed
- 2 oz. peppercorns
- a few red peppers
- cucumbers
- beans

## Directions

1. Drop in as many cucumbers, beans, etc., as the vinegar will cover.
2. Stir frequently.
3. Good to use in a month.
