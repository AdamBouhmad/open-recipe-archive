## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cabbage
- 1 cup chopped boiled beets
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1/2 teaspoonful dry mustard
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 tablespoonful horseradish
- 1 tablespoonful brown sugar
- vinegar
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Break an egg in a sauce-pan
3. Add half cup of vinegar and butter size of walnut
4. Stir over fire till thick, do not boil
5. Pour over salad
6. Toss lightly and serve cold
