## Ingredients

- rice, a cupful
- cold water
- water, two quarts
- salt, a teaspoonful

## Directions

1. Pick over the rice, a cupful.
2. Wash it thoroughly in two or three cold waters; then leave it about twenty minutes in cold water.
3. Put into a stewpan two quarts of water with a teaspoonful of salt in it, and when it boils, sprinkle in the rice.
4. Boil it briskly for twenty minutes, keeping the pan covered.
5. Take it from the fire, and drain off the water.
6. Afterwards set the saucepan on the back of the stove, with the lid off, to allow the rice to dry and the grains to separate.
7. Serve it hot in a separate dish or laid round the chicken curry.
