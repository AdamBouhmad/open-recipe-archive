## Ingredients

- 2 qts. little onions
- 3 qts. cucumbers
- 3 heads celery
- 3 heads cauliflower
- 4 green peppers (chopped)
- 3 qts. vinegar
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup flour
- 2 cups mustard
- ½ oz. turmeric
- curry powder

## Directions

1. Cut all up and put in brine twenty-four hours
2. Put on the stove and let come to a boil in the brine
3. Remove and drain perfectly dry
4. To boiling vinegar add sugar, flour, mustard, turmeric, with a little curry powder mixed in
5. Pour over vegetables
