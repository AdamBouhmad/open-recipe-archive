## Ingredients

- 4 pounds ripe tomatoes
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 pint cider vinegar
- 0.5 ounce stick cinnamon
- 0.5 ounce cloves

## Directions

1. Cook all together till tomatoes are done
2. Take out tomatoes
3. Boil juice down to a syrup and pour over tomatoes
4. Store in an open jar
