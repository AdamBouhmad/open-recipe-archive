## Ingredients

- bread sponge
- 1 cup walnut meats
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 tablespoon caraway seed
- flour

## Directions

1. Make sponge same as for white bread
2. To each loaf add 1 cup walnut meats broken into four or five pieces, 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons melted butter, and 1 tablespoon caraway seed
3. Mix in flour same as for white bread
4. Turn out on board and knead until the dough does not stick to hands
5. Let rise
6. Bake in a moderate oven
