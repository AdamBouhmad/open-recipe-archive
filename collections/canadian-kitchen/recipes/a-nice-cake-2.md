## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1½ cups sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ teaspoon soda
- 3 cups flour
- lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Flavor with lemon
