## Ingredients

- beets
- salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and cook in hot, salted water from two to three hours, according to age and size.
2. Throw at once into cold water when done, to loosen the skins.
3. Peel quickly, slice thin, dish, and pour over them a sauce made of three tablespoonfuls of scalding vinegar, a tablespoonful of butter, and a little pepper and salt.
4. Serve hot.
