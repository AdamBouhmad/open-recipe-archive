## Ingredients

- Strong sage tea, one pint
- Bay rum, one pint
- Tincture of cantharides, quarter of an ounce
- Castor oil, one ounce
- Rose or other perfume, one teaspoonful

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Shake well before applying to the hair, as the oil will not mix
