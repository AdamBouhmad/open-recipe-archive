## Ingredients

- 4 pounds meat
- 4 1/2 pounds apples
- 3/4 pound suet
- 3 pounds raisins
- 1 pound figs
- 4 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 3 nutmegs
- 1 cup stock
- 1 cup sweet pickle vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil raisins and figs
2. Chop meat, apples and suet fine
3. Put on stove with sugar, salt, etc.
4. Add cup stock (liquor in which raisins and figs were boiled)
5. Add 1 cup sweet pickle vinegar
6. Cook till all is boiling hot
7. Put in sealers or crock and cover tight
