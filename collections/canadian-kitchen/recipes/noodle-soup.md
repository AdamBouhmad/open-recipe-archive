## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour
- salt
- boiling water
- cold water
- 3 pints hot stock

## Directions

1. Beat yokes of 2 eggs, add flour and salt, mould stiff
2. Roll thin and cut in strips
3. Put in boiling water ten minutes, then in cold water for three
4. Add 3 pints hot stock
