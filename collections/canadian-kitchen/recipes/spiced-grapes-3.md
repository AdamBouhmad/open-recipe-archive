## Ingredients

- 7 lbs. grapes
- 5 lbs. sugar
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful allspice
- 1 teaspoonful cloves

## Directions

1. Squeeze pulp from skin
2. Boil pulp and strain out seeds
3. Boil again with skins and other ingredients until it jellies
