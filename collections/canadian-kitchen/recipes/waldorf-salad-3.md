## Ingredients

- 1/2 dozen rather tart apples
- 1 cup or more diced celery
- 1 cup chopped English walnuts

## Directions

1. Select 1/2 dozen rather tart apples, pare and slice evenly in salad dish
2. Mix with 1 cup or more diced celery and 1 cup chopped English walnuts
3. May also be served in cups made of hollowed apples
