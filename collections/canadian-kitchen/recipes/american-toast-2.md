## Ingredients

- one egg
- one cup of sweet milk
- a little salt
- Light bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Beat one egg thoroughly
2. Put one cup of sweet milk to the beaten egg, and a little salt
3. Slice light bread and dip into the mixture, allowing each slice to absorb some of the milk
4. Brown on a hot, buttered griddle or thick-bottom frying-pan
5. Spread with butter, and serve hot
