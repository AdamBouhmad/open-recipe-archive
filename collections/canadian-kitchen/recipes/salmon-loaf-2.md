## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 4 eggs well beaten
- 4 tablespoons soft butter
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs or crackers
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon minced parsley

## Directions

1. Pick carefully the skin and bones of salmon
2. Add eggs, butter, bread crumbs or crackers, pepper, salt, and parsley
3. Mix well
4. Pack in buttered mould
5. Steam 1 hour
