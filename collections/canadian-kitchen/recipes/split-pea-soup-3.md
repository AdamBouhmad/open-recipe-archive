## Ingredients

- 1 cup split peas
- 2.5 quarts water
- 2 teaspoons chopped onion
- 3 teaspoons butter
- 3 teaspoons flour
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 0.75 teaspoon pepper
- 1 pint milk

## Directions

1. Wash the peas and soak them over night in cold water
2. Drain and rinse thoroughly
3. Add 2.5 quarts cold water and the onion
4. Cook slowly until soft
5. Rub through a strainer and add the remainder of the liquid
6. Make a white sauce and cook until it is thick like thick cream
7. Cooking a ham bone with the soup improves the flavor
