## Ingredients

- 1 lb. stoned raisins
- 1 lb. currants
- 1 lb. beef suet
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- 2 oz. lemon peel
- 2 oz. citron peel
- 2 oz. orange peel
- 6 eggs
- 6 oz. flour
- 1/2 lb. breadcrumbs
- Little nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves and a little salt
- A little milk

## Directions

1. Mix all together with eggs and milk
2. Dip cloth in boiling water and flour
3. Put in mixture and tie loosely
4. Boil fast for four hours and a half
