## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 large tablespoon molasses
- 1 large cup seeded raisins
- 1/2 cup very sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 small cups flour
- walnuts chopped fine

## Directions

1. Mix butter, brown sugar, eggs, cinnamon, nutmeg, molasses, raisins, sour milk, soda and flour
2. Make as a layer cake
3. Put chopped walnuts between cake in layers and in the icing
