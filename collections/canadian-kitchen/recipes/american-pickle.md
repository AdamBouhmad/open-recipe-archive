## Ingredients

- 1 quart sliced cucumbers (not too large)
- 1 large onion
- 1 green pepper
- salt
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 tablespoon grated horseradish
- 1 tablespoon mustard seed
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice cucumbers
2. Chop onion and green pepper
3. Sprinkle with salt and let stand 3 hours
4. Rinse in cold water
5. Add brown sugar, turmeric, grated horseradish, mustard seed and celery seed
6. Cover with vinegar
7. Bring to a scald
