## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup soft yeast or 1/2 cake compressed yeast dissolved in 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 nutmeg
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- flour to make soft dough

## Directions

1. Mix milk, yeast and add flour to make a thick batter.
2. Set as a sponge over night.
3. In the morning add melted butter, sugar, nutmeg, grated, salt.
4. Add sufficient flour to make a soft dough.
5. Form into balls, flatten out with the hand, and mark deeply in the form of a cross with the back of a knife.
6. Lay on buttered tins, and set to rise, and bake when light.
7. Some cooks add a teaspoonful of coriander seeds.
