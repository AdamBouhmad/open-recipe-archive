## Ingredients

- Bay rum, two pints
- Alcohol, one pint
- Castor oil, one ounce
- Carb. ammonia, half an ounce
- Tincture of cantharides, one ounce

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well
2. This compound will promote the growth of the hair and prevent it from falling out
