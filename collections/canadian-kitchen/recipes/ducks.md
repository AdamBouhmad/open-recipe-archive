## Ingredients

- duck
- dressing as for turkey
- slices of onion
- dripping from pan
- chopped giblets
- brown flour
- water
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. When roasted, use dressing as for turkey, with the addition of a few slices of onion
2. Many cooks lay over the game slice of onions, which take away the fishy flavor, removing the onion before serving
3. Make a sauce with the dripping in the pan in which the game is roasted, and to which are put the chopped giblets, which are previously well cooked
4. Thicken the gravy with brown flour moistened with water
5. Serve with currant jelly
