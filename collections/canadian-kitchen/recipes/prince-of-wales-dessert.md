## Ingredients

- sponge cakes, stale preferred
- sherry or syrup
- three-quarters pound cornflour
- one ounce arrowroot
- one pint boiling milk
- one teaspoon vanilla
- red currant jelly
- chopped pistachio nuts
- whipped cream
- cherries
- chopped citron peel

## Directions

1. Take sponge cakes, stale preferred; dip in sherry or syrup and line a mould with them.
2. Take three-quarters pound cornflour, one ounce arrowroot, and mix together.
3. Add one pint boiling milk and cook a few minutes.
4. Add one teaspoon vanilla and pour this into the lined basin.
5. When cold turn out.
6. Put a large spoonful of red currant jelly on the top, and sprinkle well with chopped pistachio nuts.
7. Alternatively, put the cakes soaked in raspberry juice or wine in a glass dish; fill as above over the top of this.
8. When cold spread whipped cream; decorate with cherries and chopped citron peel.
9. Serve very cold and in the glass dish in which it was made.
