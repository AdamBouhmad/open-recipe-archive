## Ingredients

- porous, leathery Swiss cheese
- French mustard
- thin buttered bread

## Directions

1. Procure porous, leathery Swiss cheese and shave into thinnest slices
2. Spread with a little French mustard
3. Place between thin buttered bread
4. Cut a much larger size than others
