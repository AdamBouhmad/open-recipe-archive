## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons soda
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and egg together
2. Warm the molasses and pour into the mixture
3. Add ginger and salt, stir well
4. Add soda and vinegar, beat and mix with flour
5. Roll rather thin, cut in shapes, and bake in hot oven
