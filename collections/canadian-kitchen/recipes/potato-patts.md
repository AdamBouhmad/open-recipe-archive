## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- flour
- dripping

## Directions

1. Take mashed potatoes and sprinkle with pepper and salt
2. Add sufficient milk to make a little soft
3. Add flour so as to be able to roll on bakeboard
4. Cut with cake cutter and fry with dripping
