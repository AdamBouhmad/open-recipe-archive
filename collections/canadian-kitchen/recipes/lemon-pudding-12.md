## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound suet
- 3/4 pound bread crumbs
- 3/4 pound flour
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- grated rind and juice of 2 small lemons or one large one
- 2 eggs
- about 1/2 cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients with milk.
2. Boil 4 hours.
