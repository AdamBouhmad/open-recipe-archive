## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked and flaked fish
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1-16 teaspoon pepper
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 tablespoon mixed parsley

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Pack into buttered bowl
3. Surround with hot water
4. Cover
5. Poach
6. Unmould
7. Garnish with parsley
