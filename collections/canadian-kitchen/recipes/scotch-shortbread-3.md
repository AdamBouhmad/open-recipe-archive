## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 pound flour
- 8 ounces butter
- 4 ounces coffee sugar
- yolk of one egg

## Directions

1. Measure out the ingredients, put the butter and sugar in a basin, beat to a cream
2. Make a hole in the centre of the mixture and add the yolk of the egg
3. Mix flour and baking powder well together and add gradually to the butter and sugar, mixing well
4. Turn out on a floured pastry board and work the mixture into flat, round shapes about as large as saucers
5. Notch the edges or place on a wooden shape or earthenware mould and bake in a slow oven for twenty minutes till a nice brown color
