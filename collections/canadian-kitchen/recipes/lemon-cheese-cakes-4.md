## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- butter the size of an egg
- 1 well-beaten egg
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- 1 cup currants
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- rich paste

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar with butter the size of an egg
2. Add 1 well-beaten egg, juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
3. Add 1 cup currants and 3/4 cup sweet milk
4. Line tart pans with rich paste
5. Put a spoonful of mixture in each and bake
