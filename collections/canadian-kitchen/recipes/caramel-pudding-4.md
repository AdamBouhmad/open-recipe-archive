## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 pint milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- vanilla
- almonds

## Directions

1. Scald milk
2. Brown 1 tablespoon butter and 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
3. When melted, add to milk
4. Add cornstarch
5. Add vanilla
6. Add almonds if desired
7. Put in delf mould
8. Serve with cream and sugar when cold
