## Ingredients

- 1 quart raw cabbage
- 1 quart boiled beets
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon white or black pepper
- ½ teaspoon red pepper
- cold vinegar
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage fine
2. Chop beets fine
3. Add 2 cups sugar, 1 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon white or black pepper, ½ teaspoon red pepper
4. Cover with cold vinegar and keep from the air
5. Slice horseradish and place on top of pickles
6. Keep covered and serve as cold pickle
