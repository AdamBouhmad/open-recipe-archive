## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup maple syrup
- White of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Bake in layers
2. Maple syrup boiled to a soft wax and poured over the white of egg beaten to a stiff froth
3. Beat thoroughly
