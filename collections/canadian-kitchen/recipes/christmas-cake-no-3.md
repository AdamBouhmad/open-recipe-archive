## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 3 pounds raisins
- 3 pounds currants
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 1 cup blackstrap
- 1 dozen eggs
- 1 tablespoon soda
- 2 ounces lemon peel
- 2 ounces citron peel
- 2 ounces orange peel
- a little salt
- 4 cups sugar
- flour to make stiff

## Directions

1. This will make a 4-story cake
