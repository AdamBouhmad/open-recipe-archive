## Ingredients

- 2 heads celery
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 cup whipped cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut 2 heads celery into finger lengths, simmer in a quart of milk for 3½ an hour
2. Remove celery
3. Thicken with tablespoon cornstarch blended with milk and cup whipped cream
4. Season and serve
