## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- whites of 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 1/4 cup cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup flour
- sliced bananas

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Separate eggs and beat yolk, add to butter
3. Sift flour, baking powder and cornstarch, add to butter, mix and add milk
4. Add whites of eggs well beaten
5. Bake in layers
6. Just before serving put a layer of sliced bananas between the layers
