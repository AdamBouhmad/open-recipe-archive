## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Cook until thick
2. Fill tart shells already cooked
3. Cover with whipped cream or the meringue of whites of eggs
