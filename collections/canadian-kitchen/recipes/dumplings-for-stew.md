## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix and sift flour, baking powder, and salt
2. Mix to a soft dough with milk
3. Turn on board and roll out 1 inch thick
4. Cut in small circles
5. Roll each in flour
6. Drop in simmering stew
7. Cover and cook 20 minutes
