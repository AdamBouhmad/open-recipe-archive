## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter (scant)
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream eggs, sugar and butter
2. Mix milk and baking powder in flour
3. Add chopped nuts
4. For filling: Chop 1 cup raisins and 1 cup sugar with a few chopped walnuts
5. Beat white of 1 egg
6. Moisten sugar with a little water and boil until it strings
7. Add nuts and raisins
8. Add beaten white of egg last
9. Beat until creamy and spread between layers and on top
