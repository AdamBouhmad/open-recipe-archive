## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground almonds
- 1 pound fine sifted white sugar
- whites of 2 eggs
- few drops essence almonds

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and ground almonds well together
2. Add little by little whites of eggs beaten to a froth, until the mixture is a thick paste
3. Add essence of almonds
4. Mix all thoroughly
