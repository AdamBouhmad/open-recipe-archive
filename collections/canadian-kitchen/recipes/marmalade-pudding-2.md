## Ingredients

- 1 lb. suet
- 1 lb. breadcrumbs
- 1 lb. sugar
- 2 oz. flour or ground rice
- 1 tablespoonful milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls orange marmalade
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful baking-powder

## Directions

1. Finely chop the suet and put it in a bowl with the flour, sugar, breadcrumbs and baking powder
2. Mix well together
3. Beat the eggs until light, then beat into it the milk and marmalade
4. Mix all together
5. Pour into a well-greased mould
6. Twist over a sheet of paper and steam four hours
