## Ingredients

- cold turkey
- bread crumbs
- milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- turkey stuffing
- hot water
- turkey gravy
- eggs
- melted butter
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Pick the meat from the bones of cold turkey, and chop it fine
2. Put a layer of bread crumbs on the bottom of a buttered dish, moisten them with a little milk
3. Put in a layer of turkey with some of the stuffing, and cut small pieces of butter over the top
4. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
5. Add another layer of bread crumbs, and so until the dish is nearly full
6. Add a little hot water to the gravy left from the turkey and pour over it
7. Take two eggs, two tablespoonfuls of milk, one of melted butter, a little salt and cracker crumbs as much as will make it thick enough to spread on with a knife
8. Put bits of butter over it, and cover with a plate
9. Bake three-quarters of an hour
10. Ten minutes before serving, remove the plate and let it brown
