## Ingredients

- a dozen oysters
- eggs
- butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Parboil a dozen oysters in their own liquor
2. Skim them out and let them cool
3. Add them to the beaten eggs, either whole or minced
4. Cook the same as a plain omelet
5. Thicken the liquid with butter rolled in flour
6. Season with salt, cayenne pepper and a teaspoonful of chopped parsley
7. Chop up the oysters and add to the sauce
8. Put a few spoonfuls in the centre of the omelet before folding
9. When dished, pour the remainder of the sauce around it
