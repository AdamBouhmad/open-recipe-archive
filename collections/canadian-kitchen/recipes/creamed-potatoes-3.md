## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- parsley
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Take some cold boiled potatoes and cut in dice
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Add a little butter and a sprig of parsley, cut up fine
4. Put milk into frying-pan and add potatoes
5. Add enough milk to make it creamy
6. Add a little thickening of flour
7. Add enough milk to make a nice gravy
