## Ingredients

- 1/2 can tomatoes
- 1 quart milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons butter
- pinch of soda
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes until soft enough to strain
2. Scald milk in double boiler
3. Add cornstarch and butter
4. Boil 5 minutes
5. Add pinch of soda
6. When ready to serve, add strained tomatoes with salt and pepper to taste
