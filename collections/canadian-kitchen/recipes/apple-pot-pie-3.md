## Ingredients

- 8 apples
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup rich milk
- a pinch of salt
- enough flour to make a stiff paste
- butter the size of a small egg
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Select 8 apples, cut in 1/8's, remove core but not skin
2. take 1 egg, 3/4 cup rich milk, a pinch of salt, enough flour to make a stiff paste
3. roll out very thin, and cut into 2-inch squares
4. put butter the size of a small egg into a granite kettle, allow it to become a delicate brown, remove to back of stove
5. put in a layer of apples, a layer of the rolled paste, a little brown sugar, a pinch of cinnamon, repeat until your apples and paste are all used
6. Over this pour boiling water and cook for 20 minutes
7. Serve hot
