## Ingredients

- 1 quart Graham flour
- 1/2 pint fine white flour
- enough milk or water, a little warm, to make a thick batter

## Directions

1. no salt or baking powder
2. have your oven hotter than for biscuit
3. have your gems pans standing in the oven until you get ready
4. beat batter thoroughly
5. grease your pans
6. drop in while the irons are smoking hot
7. Bake quickly a nice brown
