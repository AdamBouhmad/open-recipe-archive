## Ingredients

- odds and ends of boiled ham
- eggs
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Take the odds and ends of boiled ham and chop fine
2. Add eggs and a little flour
3. Mix and make in flat cakes
4. Fry in a little butter until brown
