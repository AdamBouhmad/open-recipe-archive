## Ingredients

- puff paste
- veal cutlet
- tender steak
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- mace
- pigeons
- butter
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs
- veal broth

## Directions

1. Make a fine puff paste, lay a border of it around a large dish
2. Cover the bottom with a veal cutlet, or a very tender steak free from fat and bone
3. Season with salt, cayenne pepper and mace
4. Prepare as many pigeons as can be put in one layer of the dish
5. Put in each pigeon a small lump of butter, and season with pepper and salt
6. Lay them in the dish breast downwards
7. Cut in slices a dozen hard-boiled eggs and lay in with the birds
8. Put in more butter, some veal broth, and cover the whole with crust
9. Bake slowly for an hour and a half
