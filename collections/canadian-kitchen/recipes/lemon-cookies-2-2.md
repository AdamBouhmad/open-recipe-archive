## Ingredients

- 3 cups granulated sugar
- 2 cups sweet milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup lard
- 5 cents baking ammonia
- 5 cents oil of lemon
- 3/4 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together and bake.
