## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 8 eggs well beaten
- 3 pounds currants
- 3 pounds raisins
- 1 pound mixed peel cut fine
- 1/2 pound blanched almonds chopped fine
- 5 cups sifted flour
- 2 nutmegs
- 2 teaspoons mixed spice
- vanilla and lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Weigh your butter and cut in pieces, soften, but do not melt
2. Add the sugar
3. Beat the eggs and put into the sugar and butter
4. Sift the flour over your fruit, mixed well
5. Add to the other ingredients
6. Last of all, put in the mixed peel and add nuts
