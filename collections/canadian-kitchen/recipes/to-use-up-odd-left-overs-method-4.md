## Ingredients

- rich pie dough
- meat
- raw potatoes
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- water or gravy

## Directions

1. Line sides of pudding dish with rich pie dough
2. Cut meat in small pieces on bottom of dish
3. Add a layer of thinly sliced raw potatoes, a little onion, salt and pepper
4. Continue with a layer of meat and potatoes
5. End with meat and cover with a rich pie dough
6. Put a little water or gravy on so it will not burn
7. Bake 3/4 hour
