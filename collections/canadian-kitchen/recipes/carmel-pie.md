## Ingredients

- Butter the size of an egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 spoon vanilla
- yolks of 2 eggs
- crust
- whites of eggs

## Directions

1. put Butter the size of an egg and 1 cup brown sugar in pan and let brown
2. thin with 1 cup hot water
3. mix 1 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 spoon vanilla, yolks of 2 eggs, with water, and stir in
4. have crust ready and pour filling in, using the white of eggs on top
