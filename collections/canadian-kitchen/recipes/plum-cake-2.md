## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds flour
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 3/4 pound currants
- 3/4 pound sultanas
- 10 eggs
- 1/2 pound candied peel
- 3/4 pound rice flour
- 3/4 pound split almonds
- a little wine
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix the rice flour with the sugar
2. Mix a teaspoon baking powder with the flour
3. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
4. Add the wine and eggs
5. Gently stir in the flour
6. Add the rest of the ingredients
7. Bake in tins lined with buttered paper 2 hours
