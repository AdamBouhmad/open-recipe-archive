## Ingredients

- Grated rind and juice of 12 oranges
- 4 ounces citric acid
- 7 pounds granulated sugar
- 12 glasses water

## Directions

1. Let stand 24 hours
2. Strain through a cloth
3. When serving, use 2 tablespoons of liquid to a glass of water
