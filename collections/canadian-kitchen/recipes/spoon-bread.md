## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- Salt
- Cornmeal to make thin batter
- Butter

## Directions

1. Stir into milk enough white cornmeal to make a thin batter.
2. Add baking powder, salt and eggs, the whites and yolks of which have been beaten separately.
3. Pour the mixture into a baking dish, smooth the top with a broad, flat knife, and dot with pieces of butter.
4. Bake and serve in the dish with a spoon.
