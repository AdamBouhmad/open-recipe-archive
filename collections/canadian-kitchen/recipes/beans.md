## Ingredients

- 2 cups common beans
- melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- liquid from a can of tomatoes
- ground peanuts
- bread or cracker crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil 2 cups common beans until they crack
2. Drain
3. Add melted butter, pepper and salt
4. Mash with potato masher
5. Put in your pudding pan or baker
6. Add the liquid from a can of tomatoes
7. Cover thickly with ground peanuts
8. Then a layer of bread or cracker crumbs with dots of butter
9. Bake in oven until brown
10. Good either hot or cold
