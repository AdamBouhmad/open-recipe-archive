## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup cornmeal
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 cup cooked rice
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup scalded milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 3 tablespoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Turn milk on meal, let it stand 5 minutes
2. Add rice and dry material
3. Add beaten yolk, butter and white of egg, beaten stiff
