## Ingredients

- 1 cup of broken cheese
- 1 teaspoonful of made mustard
- a tiny drop of cream
- salt
- pepper
- thin buttered slices

## Directions

1. Pound and mix together cheese and mustard
2. Add a tiny drop of cream if not soft enough
3. Add salt and a dash of pepper
4. Spread on thin buttered slices
