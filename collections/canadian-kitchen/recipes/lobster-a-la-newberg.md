## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups diced lobster
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sherry
- 2/5 teaspoon salt
- paprika or cayenne
- 2 yolks
- 1 cup cream

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add lobster and heat
2. Add wine and cook 1/4 minute
3. Add cream, yolks and seasonings
4. Cook until it thickens, but does not curdle
5. Serve immediately
6. One finely chopped trifle may be added
