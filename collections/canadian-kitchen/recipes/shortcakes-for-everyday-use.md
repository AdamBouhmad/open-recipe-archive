## Ingredients

- 9 1/2 pounds flour
- 4 ounces cream tartar
- 2 ounces baking soda
- salt
- lard
- buttermilk or sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift flour, cream tartar, and baking soda twice together
2. Keep this on hand
3. Then as required, take the amount you wish, add a little salt, also a little lard not much
4. Mix with either buttermilk or sweet milk, buttermilk preferred
5. Roll out about 3/4 or 1 inch thick, cut with cake cutter and bake in very hot oven
