## Ingredients

- large cucumbers
- salt
- butter or lard
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take large cucumbers, peal and slice lengthwise
2. sprinkle with salt
3. have the pan ready with butter or lard and butter
4. have it hot
5. dip the cucumbers in flour
6. sprinkle with pepper and fry to a nice brown
