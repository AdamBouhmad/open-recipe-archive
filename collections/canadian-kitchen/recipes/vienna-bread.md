## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk, scalded
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 yeast cake
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Flour to knead (about 4 cups)

## Directions

1. In three tablespoons milk and water dissolve the yeast thoroughly and add to remaining milk and water.
2. Add salt and sufficient flour (warmed) to make a light dough (about four cups).
3. Turn out and knead until thoroughly smooth and does not stick to the hands.
4. Put in a greased basin, cover, and stand in a warm place to rise, for three hours.
5. When risen, turn out and cut into small rolls, knead each roll a little and put in a greased tin.
6. Grease over tops with melted butter, cover and stand in a warm place till twice their original size.
7. Grease again and bake in a hot oven.
