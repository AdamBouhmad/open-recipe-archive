## Ingredients

- 2 cups of lobster dice
- 2 cups weak stock
- 1 teaspoonful minced onion
- 2 teaspoonfuls of curry powder
- Saltspoon of salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Fry the onion in the butter, add the salt, the stock, the curry, and cook gently for five minutes, before putting in the lobster
2. Serve as soon as this is thoroughly heated
3. Pass plain boiled rice with this dish
