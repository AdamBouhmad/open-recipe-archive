## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- white of 1 egg
- 1/2 cup raisins seeded and halved

## Directions

1. Boil sugar till it threads from a fork
2. Beat the white of the egg stiff
3. Beat in the hot syrup
4. Mix in 1/2 cup raisins seeded and halved
5. Spread on and between cake
