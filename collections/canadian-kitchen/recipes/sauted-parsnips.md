## Ingredients

- parsnips
- cold water
- salted boiling water
- butter
- sweet dripping
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the parsnips in cold water
2. Scrape them and cut in sections or thick slices
3. Boil in salted boiling water until tender
4. Drain them
5. Put a little butter or sweet dripping in a hot frying pan
6. Saute the parsnips until nicely browned
7. Alternatively, mash the boiled parsnips until very smooth
8. Season with salt, pepper and butter, or a little cream
9. Form into cakes and saute them
