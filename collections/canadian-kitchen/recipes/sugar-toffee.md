## Ingredients

- 3 pounds best brown sugar
- 1 pound butter
- water

## Directions

1. Add enough water to moisten sugar
2. Boil until crisp when dropped into cold water
3. Usually requires 0.75 hour boiling fast without stirring
4. Pour into pans or upon platters as thin as possible
