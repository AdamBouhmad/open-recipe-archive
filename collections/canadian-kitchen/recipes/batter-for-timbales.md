## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- 1 pt. milk
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Put the flour into a basin; drop in yolk of egg.
2. Stir in the milk by degrees.
3. Whip the white to a stiff froth and add lightly.
4. Dip your hot iron into this and fry in deep boiling lard.
