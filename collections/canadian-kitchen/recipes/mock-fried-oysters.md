## Ingredients

- fresh veal
- salt and pepper
- beaten egg
- crackers
- hot butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut fresh veal in small pieces 3 or 4 inches square
2. Dry with a towel and season to taste
3. Have a beaten egg and crackers rolled fine on 2 different dishes
4. Dip pieces of cutlet in the egg, then in the rolled crackers
5. Drop in hot butter or lard to cover and fry
6. Make a gravy after meat is removed
