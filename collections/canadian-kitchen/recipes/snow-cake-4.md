## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1½ cups flour
- ¼ cup milk
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- chocolate filling

## Directions

1. Cream butter, adding gradually sugar and vanilla
2. Beat eggs to a stiff froth
3. Add milk
4. Add flour and the beaten whites last
5. Bake
6. Use chocolate filling
