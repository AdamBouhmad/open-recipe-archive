## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 5 tablespoons lard or butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- flavoring to taste

## Directions

1. Sift baking powder in flour
2. Mix in all ingredients
3. Bake
