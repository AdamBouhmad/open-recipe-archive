## Ingredients

- asparagus tips
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- white sauce
- milk

## Directions

1. Use only the delicate tips of asparagus, less than two inches long
2. Boil in hot, salted water until tender
3. Drain, turn into a deep dish, pepper, salt, butter
4. Pour a good white sauce over them — half a cupful to one cupful of the tips
