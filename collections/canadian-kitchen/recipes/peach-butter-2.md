## Ingredients

- pound for pound peaches and sugar
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook peaches alone until they become soft
2. Put in the sugar, and stir 1/2 hour
3. Add the remainder of the sugar, and stir an hour and a half
4. Season with cloves and cinnamon
