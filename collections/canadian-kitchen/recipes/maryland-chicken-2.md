## Ingredients

- two tender spring chickens
- salt
- pepper
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- clarified butter
- cream sauce
- thin boiled bacon
- small corn fritters

## Directions

1. Split chicken in half, detach the legs and wings
2. Lay all on a plate, and season with salt and pepper
3. Dip the pieces in beaten egg, and afterwards in bread crumbs
4. Place them in a buttered pan, pour an ounce of clarified butter over, and roast in the oven about twenty minutes
5. Pour half a pint of cream sauce on a serving dish, and arrange the fowl on it
6. Alternate with slices of thin boiled bacon, and small corn fritters
