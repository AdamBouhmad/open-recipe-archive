## Ingredients

- 5 medium-sized onions
- hot water
- 2 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Peel onions and quarter
2. Place in pot with hot water and boil 20 minutes
3. Take off and strain
4. When ready to serve, cover with white sauce made by heating 2 cups milk
5. Add 3 tablespoons flour that has been creamed with 2 tablespoons of butter
6. Stir constantly till it thickens
