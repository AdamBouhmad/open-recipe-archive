## Ingredients

- 3 cups finely mashed potatoes
- 1 egg
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup chopped ham
- salted water
- lard

## Directions

1. Mould 3 cups finely mashed potatoes, 1 egg, 1 cup flour, and 1 cup chopped ham into round balls
2. Boil for 1 hour in salted water
3. Let them cool and cut in thin slices
4. Fry in lard
