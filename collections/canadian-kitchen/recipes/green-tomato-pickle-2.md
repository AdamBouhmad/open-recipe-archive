## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes sliced
- 6 onions sliced
- 1 cup salt
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons mustard seed
- 2 tablespoons ginger
- 6 green peppers chopped fine

## Directions

1. Slice green tomatoes and onions
2. Sprinkle with salt, let stand over night
3. Drain in the morning
4. Add vinegar, sugar, cloves, cinnamon, mustard seed, ginger, and green peppers
5. Boil 2 hours and put in stone jars
