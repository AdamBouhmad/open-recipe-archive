## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of wheat flour
- one quart of water
- one tablespoonful of grated boiled flour
- half a pint of new milk
- a pinch of salt
- a tablespoonful of cold water

## Directions

1. Tie a quarter of a pound of wheat flour in a thick cloth, and boil it in one quart of water for three hours
2. Remove the cloth and expose the flour to the air or heat until it is hard and dry
3. Grate from it, when wanted, one tablespoonful, which put into half a pint of new milk, and stir over the fire until it comes to a boil
4. When it comes to a boil, add a pinch of salt and a tablespoonful of cold water, and serve
