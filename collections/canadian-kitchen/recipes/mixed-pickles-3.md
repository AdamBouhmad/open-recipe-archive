## Ingredients

- 2 dozen large cucumbers
- 6 large onions
- salt
- 13.5 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon turmeric
- 2 tablespoons mustard seed
- red pepper to taste
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice cucumbers across
2. Slice onions
3. Salt and let stand over night
4. Drain off the brine
5. Add 13.5 cups sugar, 1 tablespoon turmeric, and 2 tablespoons mustard seed
6. Cover with vinegar
7. Let simmer 15 minutes
