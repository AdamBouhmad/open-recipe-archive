## Ingredients

- berries
- water
- sugar (granulated)

## Directions

1. To each pound of berries add one pint of water, and boil half an hour, stirring gently and skimming
2. Strain well
3. to each pint of juice add one pound of sugar (granulated)
4. Boil again until it jellies
