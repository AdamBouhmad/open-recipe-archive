## Ingredients

- 1 pint bottle rhubarb
- 3 oranges
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, removing as much as possible of the white pith
2. divide into sections
3. put all the ingredients together into a preserving kettle and simmer gently for about an hour
