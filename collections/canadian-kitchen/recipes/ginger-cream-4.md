## Ingredients

- Four ounces of prepared ginger
- One ounce of gelatine
- One pint of milk
- Four tablespoonfuls of sugar
- One pint of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Cut four ounces of prepared ginger in dice
2. Put one ounce of gelatine into a saucepan with a pint of milk and four tablespoonfuls of sugar
3. Let it boil slowly, stirring all the time till the gelatine is dissolved
4. Add ginger
5. When cool add one pint of whipped cream
6. Pour in dampened mould to form
