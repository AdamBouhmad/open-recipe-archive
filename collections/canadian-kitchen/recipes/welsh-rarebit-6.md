## Ingredients

- 1/2 glass old ale
- 1/2 lb. old cheese
- Pinch red pepper
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt
- Dash of Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Grate cheese fine, place in a chafing-dish or small saucepan on fire, rub well with back of spoon until thoroughly dissolved
2. Mix pepper, mustard and Worcester sauce thoroughly with ale, and pour into cheese
3. Thoroughly mix until smooth
4. Serve on buttered toast, cut diamond shape on red-hot dish
