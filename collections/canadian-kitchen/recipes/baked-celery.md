## Ingredients

- 3 cups celery cut in 1-inch pieces
- 1 cup stale bread crumbs buttered
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Cook celery in boiling water until tender
2. Drain and save the liquid
3. Make a sauce
