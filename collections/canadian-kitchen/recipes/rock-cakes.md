## Ingredients

- 0.5 lb. butter
- 0.5 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful cream tartar
- 0.5 teaspoonful soda
- 1 cup currants
- Flavor to taste

## Directions

1. Drop on buttered tins
2. Bake in moderate oven
