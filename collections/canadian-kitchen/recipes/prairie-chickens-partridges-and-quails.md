## Ingredients

- soda
- water
- dressing
- butter
- parsley
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Clean nicely, using a little soda in the water in which they are washed
2. Rinse and dry
3. Fill with dressing, sewing up nicely, binding down the legs and wings with cords
4. Put in a steamer over hot water, and let them cook until just done
5. Then place in a pan with a little butter until of a nice brown
6. They ought to brown nicely in about 5 minutes
7. Serve on a platter with sprigs of parsley alternating with currant jelly
