## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon molasses if sugar is light
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients with flour enough to roll
2. Bake
