## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cranberries
- 1 quart of water
- 1 pint of sugar
- juice of 2 lemons
- ice and salt

## Directions

1. Boil cranberries and water for five or six minutes.
2. Strain through a coarse cheese cloth.
3. Add one pint of sugar and stir and boil until sugar is dissolved.
4. When cold add juice of two lemons strained.
5. Freeze to a mush, using equal parts of ice and salt.
6. Serve in glass cups.
