## Ingredients

- 1 qt. white stock
- 1 gill cream
- Yolks of two eggs
- 1/4 teaspoonful pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls sago or round tapioca

## Directions

1. When stock is boiling put in the sago (soaked for half an hour)
2. Beat yolks with cream
3. When sago boils for ten minutes, add a cup of soup gradually to cream and eggs, stirring all the time
4. Put back on the stove and cook a few minutes, stirring all the time
