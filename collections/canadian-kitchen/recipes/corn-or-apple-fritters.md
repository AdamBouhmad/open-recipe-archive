## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Make into a thick batter and drop in spoonfuls into boiling lard.
2. May use chopped apples instead of corn.
