## Ingredients

- one level tablespoonful flour
- one tablespoonful butter
- half a cup hot milk
- pepper
- half-cupful shredded codfish
- two cupfuls cold boiled potatoes
- one tablespoonful butter

## Directions

1. Cook together one level tablespoonful of flour and one of butter
2. add gradually half a cup of hot milk and a little pepper
3. pour boiling water on a half-cupful of shredded codfish, drain and mix with the thickened milk
4. add two cupfuls of cold boiled potatoes chopped fine
5. melt a tablespoonful of butter in a spider
6. when hot turn in fish and cook slowly until a thick crust has formed
7. fold over and serve on hot platter
