## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 tablespoons apple butter
- 1 small teaspoon allspice
- Butter size of an egg
- Bottom crust

## Directions

1. Bake with bottom crust
2. This makes 2 pies
