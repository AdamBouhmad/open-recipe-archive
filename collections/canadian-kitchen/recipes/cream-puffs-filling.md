## Ingredients

- One cup of milk
- one egg
- half a cup of sugar
- two small tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- vanilla
- a small lump of butter

## Directions

1. Thicken one cup of milk and one egg mixed with half a cup of sugar using two small tablespoonfuls of cornstarch.
2. Flavor with vanilla and add a small lump of butter.
