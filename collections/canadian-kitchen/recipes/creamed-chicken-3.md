## Ingredients

- 2.5 lbs. chicken, raw
- 0.5 lb. cooked sweetbreads
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 pint cream
- 0.5 can mushrooms
- 1.5 tablespoonfuls flour

## Directions

1. When chicken and sweet-breads have been cooked tender allow them to cool, chopping sweet-breads fine.
2. Put butter in a saucepan; when melted add flour, and when dissolved, cream, which has been heated.
3. When thick add seasoning and chicken.
4. Mix all well together; pour into a baking-dish, spread bread-crumbs and butter on top and bake twenty minutes.
