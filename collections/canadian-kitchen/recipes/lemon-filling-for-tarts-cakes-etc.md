## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 egg
- 1 dessertspoonful cornstarch
- Grated rind and juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and egg to a cream
2. Add rind and juice of lemon, previously prepared, also cornstarch, and blend thoroughly
3. Pour in slowly cup of water, which must be boiling
4. Put on the fire in a double saucepan and allow it to simmer for ten minutes
