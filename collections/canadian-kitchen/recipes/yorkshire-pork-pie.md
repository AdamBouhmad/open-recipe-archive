## Ingredients

- lean pork
- butter
- paste
- minced onion
- hard-boiled eggs
- pepper
- salt
- powdered sage
- flour
- puff-paste
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Chop lean pork somewhat coarsely; butter a pudding-dish and line with a good paste.
2. Put in the pork interspersed with minced onion and hard-boiled eggs, cut into bits.
3. Sprinkle with pepper, salt, and powdered sage.
4. Now and then dust with flour and drop in a bit of butter.
5. When all the meat is in, dredge with flour and stick small pieces of butter quite thickly all over it.
6. Cover with puff-paste, cut a slit in the middle of the crust.
7. Bake half an hour for each pound of meat.
8. When it begins to brown, wash the crust with the white of an egg. It will give a fine gloss to it as well as preserve the juices.
