## Ingredients

- rich paste for shell
- 1 pint of sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons of flour
- a little milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons of sugar
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 2 whites of eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Make a shell of rich paste
2. Put 1 pint of sweet milk on in double boiler
3. Wet 2 tablespoons of flour with a little milk
4. Pour on it the boiling milk slowly
5. Add to this the yolks of 2 eggs beaten with 3 tablespoons of sugar and 1 of butter
6. Cook 5 minutes
7. Pour into the crust which has been already baked
8. Make meringue of the 2 whites whipped light with 2 tablespoons sugar
9. Brown slightly
