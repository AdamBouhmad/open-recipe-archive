## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 grated lemon
- 1 grated sour apple
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, butter, milk, eggs and baking powder
2. Bake in layer cake tins
3. For filling: stir together grated lemon, grated sour apple and sugar
4. Boil 4 minutes
5. Use as filling between layers
