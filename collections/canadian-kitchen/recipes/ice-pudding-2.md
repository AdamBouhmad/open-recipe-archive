## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 pt. milk
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls sherry
- 1 pt. cream
- white cherries
- plums
- citron
- almonds, pounded
- angelica
- pineapple
- 1/2 glass curagao
- macaroons, crushed
- candied ginger
- red cherries

## Directions

1. Make custard of milk and eggs and flavoring, the night before, as it is better.
2. The fruit must be cut fine, having been soaked the night before in curagao.
3. Whip cream a little, mix cold custard with it, half freeze in freezer.
4. Do this very slowly, then mix in macaroons, then fruit.
5. Let freeze a while longer.
6. When frozen put in shape.
