## Ingredients

- nice crust
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1¼ cups sweet milk
- a little piece butter
- 2 level tablespoons cornstarch
- vanilla
- a pinch of salt
- white of an egg or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Line a pie dish with nice crust and bake
2. Take 2 eggs, 1 cup sugar, 1¼ cups sweet milk, a little piece butter, 2 level tablespoons cornstarch, flavor with vanilla, a pinch of salt
3. Put on the stove and bring to a boil
4. Put this mixture into the crust
5. Top with either the white of an egg beaten for frosting, or whipped cream
