## Ingredients

- 1 quart of peas
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- pepper
- paprica or a dash of cayenne
- 1/2 teaspoonful of sugar
- 3 mint leaves, finely minced
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Boil and rub a quart of peas through a colander, or pass them through a vegetable-press.
2. Heat a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan with pepper, paprica, or a dash of cayenne, half a teaspoonful of sugar, and three mint leaves, finely minced.
3. Stir in the pulped peas and toss and stir with a silver fork until they are very hot.
4. Pile upon a hot platter and lay triangles of fried bread about the base.
