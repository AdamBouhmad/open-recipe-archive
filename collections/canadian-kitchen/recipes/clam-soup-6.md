## Ingredients

- Clams
- One cupful of hot milk per quart of juice
- One tablespoonful of butter
- One tablespoonful of flour
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Remove the clams from the shells as soon as they have opened
2. Put them in a warm place, until the juice is prepared
3. Add a cupful of hot milk to a quart of juice
4. Thicken it with a roux made of one tablespoonful of butter and one tablespoonful of flour
5. Add the clams, chopped fine, season, and bring the soup again to the boiling-point and serve
6. Two spoonfuls of whipped cream served on each plateful of soup is an improvement to the dish
