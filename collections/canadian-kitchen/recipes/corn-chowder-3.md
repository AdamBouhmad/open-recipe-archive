## Ingredients

- 1 can corn
- 4 cups potatoes cut in 1/2-inch slices
- 1 1/2-inch cube fat salt pork or butter
- 1 sliced onion
- 4 cups scalded milk
- 8 common crackers
- 3 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Try out pork, add onion and cook, stirring often that onion may not burn
2. Parboil potatoes 5 minutes in boiling water to cover, drain and add to onion and strained fat, then add 2 cups boiling water
3. Cook till potatoes are soft, add corn and milk, and heat to boiling point
4. Season with salt and pepper, add butter and crackers split and soaked in enough cold milk to moisten
5. Remove crackers, turn chowder into a tureen and put crackers on top
