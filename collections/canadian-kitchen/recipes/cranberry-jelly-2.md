## Ingredients

- one quart of cranberries
- one scant cupful of water
- two cupfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. To one quart of cranberries add one scant cupful of water, and cook until the berries are tender
2. Remove from the fire and strain through a fine sieve
3. Return the juice to the saucepan, add two cupfuls of sugar, and cook just long enough to thoroughly melt the sugar
4. When cool, put in a mould and pack in ice for an hour, and serve in sherbet glasses
