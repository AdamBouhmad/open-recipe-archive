## Ingredients

- 1 cup macaroni
- 1 cup grated cheese
- ⅜ cup dry bread crumbs
- 2 cups cream sauce
- 6 cups water
- 1½ teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Put 6 cups water and 1½ teaspoons salt on to boil
2. Break macaroni into inch pieces
3. When water boils hard, drop in macaroni
4. Keep boiling hard till macaroni is tender
5. Grate cheese and make white sauce
6. When macaroni is tender, drain through a colander and put into a baking dish
7. Put cheese and sauce with the macaroni
8. Cover with crumbs
9. Bake for about 15 minutes
