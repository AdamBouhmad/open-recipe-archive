## Ingredients

- One-half teaspoon mustard
- Two teaspoons granulated sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- One-quarter cup vinegar
- One-quarter cup grated horseradish
- Three-quarters cup whipped cream

## Directions

1. Mix mustard and sugar, vinegar, etc.; add radish; add three-quarters cup whipped cream.
