## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- water
- sugar
- cornstarch
- sweetened cream

## Directions

1. Prepare rhubarb as for sauce
2. Boil with plenty water
3. Add sugar to make quite sweet
4. When well cooked add enough cornstarch previously dissolved in cold water to make quite thick
5. Stir briskly to keep smooth
6. Serve when quite cold with sweetened cream
