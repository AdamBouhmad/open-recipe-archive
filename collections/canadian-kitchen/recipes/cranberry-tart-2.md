## Ingredients

- 2 3/4 cups cranberries
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup water

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash cranberries
2. Add water, cover and cook over moderate fire until all the berries have burst
3. Add sugar and cook to thoroughly combine
4. Cool and put in previously baked lower crust just before serving
5. Cover with lattice of baked crust, or bake mixture in crust without previously cooking
