## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup raisins
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and butter to a cream
2. Add milk
3. Add flour in which cream tartar and baking soda have been sifted
4. Add raisins and spice to taste
5. Bake
