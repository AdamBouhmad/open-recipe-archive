## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sweet milk
- lump butter size of an egg
- nutmeg
- pumpkin

## Directions

1. Cut a fair-sized pumpkin in halves
2. Remove seeds and waste matter
3. Cut out the meat from one half, leaving only the rind whole
4. Place in other half and cover with hollow half
5. Set in kettle of boiling water to cook
6. When done, remove from water and add the eggs beaten well
7. Add sugar, milk, butter and flavor with nutmeg to taste
8. Stir together thoroughly until the whole assumes the form of a custard
9. Replace the pumpkin covering and bake in a moderate oven
