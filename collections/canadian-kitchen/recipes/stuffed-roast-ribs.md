## Ingredients

- whole side of spare ribs
- stale bread
- salt
- pepper
- sage
- onion
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Take a whole side of spare ribs, hack down the centre, but do not separate the pieces, wash and thoroughly dry
2. Prepare a stuffing as you would for turkey, using about half a loaf of rather stale bread, slice and rub until finely crumbled
3. Season with salt, pepper and sage, also an onion cut fine
4. Rub on a tablespoon of butter and moisten the whole with a little cold water
5. Lay the stuffing in the hollow ribs next to the backbone, pack firmly, then turn over the other half
6. Take a cord, and beginning at one end wind smoothly around, covering all the stuffing
7. Tie firmly, put in pan with about cup of hot water
8. Roast in a rather hot oven for an hour and a half
9. When done take out of the pan, lay on a platter, remove the cord
10. For the gravy pour off some of the fat in the pan, add a tablespoon flour, rub smooth
11. Pour in boiling water till of the right consistency, stirring all the time
12. Let boil for a minute, season with salt and pepper, and serve with the roast
