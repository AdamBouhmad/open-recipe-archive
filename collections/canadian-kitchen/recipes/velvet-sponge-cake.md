## Ingredients

- 2 eggs beaten light
- 1 cup granulated or powdered sugar
- 3/4 cup sifted flour
- 1 cup flour sifted with 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4 (scant) cup boiling water
- butter for tin

## Directions

1. Beat eggs light
2. Beat in sugar
3. Add 3/4 cup sifted flour
4. Add next cup flour sifted with baking powder
5. Add boiling water very gradually
6. Butter the tin and fill
7. Bake immediately in a well-heated oven
