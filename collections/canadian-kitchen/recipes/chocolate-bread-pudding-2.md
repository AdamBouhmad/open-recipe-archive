## Ingredients

- 1 cup stale bread crumbs
- 2 cups milk
- a heaping tablespoon butter
- grated Cowan's Chocolate or Cocoa
- 1/2 cup sugar
- a teaspoon vanilla
- yolks of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Put bread crumbs and milk on the fire
2. Heat to the boiling point
3. Add a heaping tablespoon butter
4. Add grated Cowan's Chocolate or Cocoa
5. Add 1/2 cup sugar and a teaspoon vanilla
6. Take from the fire and cool
7. Stir in the beaten yolks of 3 eggs
8. Last, fold in the stiff whites
9. Put in a buttered dish and bake
10. Serve with cream
