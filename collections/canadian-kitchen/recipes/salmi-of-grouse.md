## Ingredients

- pair of underdone grouse
- cupful of good stock or consomme
- salt
- minced onion
- chopped carrot
- stalk of celery
- minced parsley
- brown roux
- mushrooms
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut neatly into joints a pair of underdone grouse and divide the breasts into two pieces each
2. Put a cupful of good stock or consomme in a saucepan, season well
3. Add a minced onion, a chopped carrot, and a stalk of celery, with a little minced parsley
4. Cook slowly one hour
5. Rub through a colander
6. Stir in a tablespoonful of brown roux
7. Bring to a boil and put in the grouse
8. After this it must not boil, but set it in a saucepan of boiling water just where it will keep at the scalding-point for half an hour
9. At the last put in half a cupful of mushrooms, heated in their own liquor
10. Serve
