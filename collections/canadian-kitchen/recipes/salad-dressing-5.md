## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 3 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Dash of cayenne
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- Salt to taste
- 1/4 cup rich cream

## Directions

1. Mix the vinegar, sugar, mustard, pepper, and stir in the egg well beaten; will take nearly five minutes
2. Cook all in a double boiler, stirring constantly
3. When cold add the cream and salt
4. This makes a pint and will keep for weeks
