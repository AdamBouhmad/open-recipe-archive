## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- three eggs
- three cups of milk
- half a cup of flour
- a pinch of salt
- baking powder

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of rice
2. Beat together three eggs, adding three cups of milk with the rice
3. Sift into this half a cup of flour to which a pinch of salt and baking powder has been added
4. Fry and serve immediately
