## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sour milk
- 3 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- honey or jam

## Directions

1. Cut in small cookies
2. Put together with honey or jam
