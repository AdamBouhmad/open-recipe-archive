## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 cups flour
- salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon orange extract

## Directions

1. Cream 3/4 cup butter
2. Add slowly 2 cups sugar and cream together
3. Sift together 2 teaspoons baking powder with 3 cups flour and a little salt; sift four times
4. Add 1 tablespoon of the sifted flour to the creamed butter and sugar
5. Add 4 eggs, 1 at a time unbeaten, adding a tablespoon flour before each egg
6. Continue adding the flour alternately with 1 cup milk
7. Flavor with 1 teaspoon orange extract
8. Bake in a moderate oven until cake shrinks from edge of pan and feels firm
9. When cool, frost with orange frosting
