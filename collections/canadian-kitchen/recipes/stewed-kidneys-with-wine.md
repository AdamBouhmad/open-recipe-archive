## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- Cold water
- Flour
- Butter
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 cupful of boiling water or heated stock
- 1 tablespoonful of catsup
- A dash of paprica or cayenne
- Salt
- 1 small tablespoonful of butter
- Browned flour
- A generous glass of sherry or claret

## Directions

1. Slice the kidneys, after they have been soaked in cold water
2. Wipe dry and roll in flour
3. Have ready in a saucepan a little butter in which has been fried a slice of onion
4. Lay in the kidneys and roll them over and over, coating them with the butter, for two minutes
5. Pour in a cupful of boiling water or heated stock
6. Simmer not longer than ten or twelve minutes
7. Take them up and lay upon a hot dish
8. Add to the gravy a tablespoonful of catsup, a dash of paprica or cayenne, and salt, a small tablespoonful of butter that has been rolled in browned flour
9. When it has boiled up, add a generous glass of sherry or claret
10. Pour over the kidneys and serve
