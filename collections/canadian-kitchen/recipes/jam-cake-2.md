## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup jam
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 small cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake
