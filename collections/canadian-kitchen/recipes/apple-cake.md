## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3 teaspoons butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- apples peeled and cut in sixths
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pour dough into jelly tins
2. Have ready some apples peeled and cut in sixths
3. Press them into the dough as close together as possible, covering the top of the cake with them
4. Cover with sugar and a little cinnamon and bake
