## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 teaspoons shortening
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- salt
- milk
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour, baking powder, shortening, cinnamon, and salt with milk
2. Roll out a little over 3/4 of an inch thick
3. Spread over mixture of granulated sugar and butter beaten to a cream
4. Roll up
5. Cut off in lengths for buns
6. Set on ends and bake in quick oven
