## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- egg
- boiling water
- crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes into slices and drain well
2. Dust each with pepper and salt
3. Beat an egg in a saucer until light, and add to it a tablespoon of boiling water
4. Dip each slice first in the egg mixture, then in crumbs
5. Fry brown on both sides
6. Take up carefully, and serve very hot
