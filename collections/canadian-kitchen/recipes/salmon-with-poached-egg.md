## Ingredients

- bread rounds, one inch thick
- melted butter
- canned salmon
- cream sauce
- poached egg
- parsley
- sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Stamp out the required number of rounds of bread an inch thick
2. Cut the centres out of these, leaving a case with a narrow rim
3. Brush over the outsides of cases with melted butter
4. Brown in oven
5. Fill the space in the centre with canned salmon flaked and heated in a cup of cream sauce
6. Lay a poached egg above the salmon
7. Serve garnished with parsley and sliced lemon
