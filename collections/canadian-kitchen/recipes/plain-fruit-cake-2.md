## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups sifted flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 pounds raisins chopped fine
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Bake in a slow oven
