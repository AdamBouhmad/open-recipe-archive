## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup cream
- ½ cup shredded cocoanut
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs
2. Add the sugar gradually
3. Add the cocoanut, cream and flour with baking powder and salt
