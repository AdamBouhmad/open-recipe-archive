## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ¼ cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon soda in 1 cup boiling water
- 1 cup flour
- 2 cups oatmeal
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bake in a slow oven
