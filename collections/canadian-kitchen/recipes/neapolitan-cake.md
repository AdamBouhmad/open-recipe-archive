## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Whites of 5 eggs
- Cochineal to color half
- nuts

## Directions

1. Cream butter, sugar and milk together, then part of flour, whites of eggs with the rest of flour
2. Divide and color one portion
3. Fill bake-pan with alternate layers of white and pink
4. Must not be moved in the oven until cooked
