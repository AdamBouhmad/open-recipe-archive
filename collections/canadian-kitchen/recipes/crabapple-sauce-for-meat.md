## Ingredients

- 3 pounds crabapples
- 1½ quarts vinegar
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 1 dessertspoon cinnamon
- 1 dessertspoon cloves
- 1 dessertspoon pepper
- 1 dessertspoon salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil 3 pounds crabapples in 1½ quarts vinegar
2. Strain through a colander
3. Add 3 pounds brown sugar, 1 dessertspoon each cinnamon, cloves, pepper and salt
4. Let boil about 5 minutes
5. Bottle hot
6. Add more vinegar if too thick
