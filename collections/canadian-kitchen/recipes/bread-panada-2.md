## Ingredients

- 3 gills water
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls crumbs of stale white bread
- 1 glass white wine
- 1 grated lemon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put three gills of water and one tablespoonful of white sugar on the fire
2. Just before it boils add two tablespoonfuls of the crumbs of stale white bread
3. Stir it well, and let it boil three or four minutes
4. Add one glass of white wine, a grated lemon and a little nutmeg
5. Let it boil up once
6. Remove it from the fire, and keep it closely covered
