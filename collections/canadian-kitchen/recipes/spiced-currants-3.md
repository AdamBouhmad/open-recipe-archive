## Ingredients

- 4 pounds currants
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1.5 cups vinegar
- 1.5 pounds raisins
- 1 ounce stick cinnamon
- 1 ounce mace
- 0.5 ounce whole cloves
- Teaspoonful white mustard seed

## Directions

1. To currants add sugar, cinnamon, mace, cloves, mustard-seed tied in a muslin bag
2. Cook slowly one hour, then add vinegar
3. Stir constantly for fifteen minutes
4. Add one and one-quarter pounds of raisins chopped fine
