## Ingredients

- wormwood
- arnica
- half a pint of cold water
- bran or rye-meal

## Directions

1. Steep the herbs in half a pint of cold water, and when all their virtue is extracted stir in a little bran or rye-meal to thicken the liquid
2. The herbs must not be removed from the liquid
3. This is a useful application for sprains and bruises
