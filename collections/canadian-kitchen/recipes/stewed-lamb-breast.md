## Ingredients

- One breast of lamb
- pepper and salt to taste
- sufficient stock to cover it
- thickening of butter and flour
- 1 pint and a half of green peas
- stewed mushrooms

## Directions

1. Skin the lamb and cut into pieces, and season them with pepper and salt
2. Lay these in a stew-pan with sufficient stock of gravy to cover them
3. Stew gently for an hour and a half
4. Just before serving, thicken the gravy with a little butter and flour
5. Give one boil, and pour it over the meat
6. Have ready a pint and a half of green peas and lay them over and around the meat
7. Add a few stewed mushrooms
