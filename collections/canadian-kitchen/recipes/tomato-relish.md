## Ingredients

- 1 dozen large ripe tomatoes
- 3 large onions cut up fine
- salt to taste
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil 2 hours
