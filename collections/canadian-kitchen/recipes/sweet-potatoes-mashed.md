## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot riced potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- hot milk

## Directions

1. Beat potatoes, butter, and salt together
2. Add enough hot milk to make thin
3. Beat until light
