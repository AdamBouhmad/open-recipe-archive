## Ingredients

- 1 small teacup vinegar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- butter half the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Beat the egg
2. Put other ingredients over the fire
3. Put in the egg
4. Stir until it boils
5. Cool and pour over chopped or shaved cabbage
