## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1½ cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 pound dates chopped
- 1 pound almond nuts chopped, not too fine
- 1 small dessertspoon soda dissolved in a little warm water
- 2¾ teaspoons cream tartar
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Drop on a buttered pan in pieces larger than a walnut
2. Do not allow them to touch in pan or they will spread out rather than rise high
3. Batter should be quite stiff
4. It is best to try one to see, as often more flour is needed than is mentioned in recipe
