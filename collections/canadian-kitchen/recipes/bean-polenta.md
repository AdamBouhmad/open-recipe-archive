## Ingredients

- beans
- 3 tablespoons molasses for every 2 quarts
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- egg and flour

## Directions

1. Wash beans, cook 3 hours until soft and mealy
2. for every 2 quarts add 3 tablespoons molasses, 1 teaspoon salt, 3/4 teaspoon pepper, 1 teaspoon mustard, 1 tablespoon butter, 2 tablespoons vinegar
3. cook 10 minutes
4. stir good, do not scorch, should be dry and mealy
5. The above is nice mashed, mixed with egg and flour and fried
