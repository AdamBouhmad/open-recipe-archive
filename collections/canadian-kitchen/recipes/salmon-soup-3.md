## Ingredients

- 1 quart sweet milk
- 1/2 a can of salmon
- three or four rolled soda biscuits
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put sweet milk in a granite kettle and set it on the stove until the milk comes to a boil
2. Add salmon and rolled soda biscuits
3. Let it all boil for three minutes
4. Add pepper and salt
5. Serve
