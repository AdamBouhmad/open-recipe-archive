## Ingredients

- 1 quart onions
- 1 quart green tomatoes
- 1 quart cucumbers
- 1 head cauliflower
- 3 red peppers
- 3 heads celery chopped fine
- 1 cup salt
- 3/4 cup mustard
- 1/8 cup tumeric powder
- 1/2 ounce butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup coffee sugar
- 3 quarts vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix onions, green tomatoes, cucumbers, cauliflower, red peppers, and celery chopped fine together
2. Add 1 cup salt, let stand over night
3. In the morning cook in the brine
4. Make dressing with 3/4 cup mustard, 1/8 cup tumeric powder, 1/2 ounce butter, 1 egg, 1 cup coffee sugar, 3 quarts vinegar; cook and stir in other
