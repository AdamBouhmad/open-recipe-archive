## Ingredients

- 1 ounce of butter
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoon of cream
- 2 ounces of boiled macaroni
- 2 ounces of Parmesan cheese grated
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Melt an ounce of butter in a stew-pan over a gentle fire
2. Beat it up with a dessertspoonful of flour and a tablespoon of cream, so as to make a thick paste
3. Add two ounces of boiled macaroni, two ounces of Parmesan cheese grated, a little salt, pepper, and a grate of nutmeg
4. Beat the mixture over the fire till smooth and firm and leaves the sides of the saucepan with the spoon
5. Mould it into quenelles with a teaspoon dipped in hot water
6. Poach them in boiling gravy till they are done thoroughly
7. Lift them out with a skimmer, and put them into the tureen with the soup
