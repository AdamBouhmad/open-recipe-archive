## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter
2. Add eggs well beaten
3. Add last, milk and flour sifted with the baking powder
4. Bake in 3 layers and put together with lemon filling or icing
5. Ice top and side
