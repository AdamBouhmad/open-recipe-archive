## Ingredients

- Smoked herring, alewives, bloaters, or similar
- Clean, wet manilla paper
- Sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Wash thoroughly, wipe dry, wrap them in clean, wet manilla paper
2. Leave in a quick oven for fifteen minutes
3. Serve with sliced lemon
