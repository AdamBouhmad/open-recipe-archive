## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup oatmeal
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 pint of boiling water
- 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake
- flour

## Directions

1. Put 1 pint of boiling water over the oatmeal
2. Let stand till warm
3. Mix all together with 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake, and flour enough to make a stiff batter with a spoon
4. Let rise over night
5. In the morning stir down and put in bake pan
6. When light bake
