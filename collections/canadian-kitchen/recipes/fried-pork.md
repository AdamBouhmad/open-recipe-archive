## Ingredients

- pork slices
- sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Have the pan hot greased
2. Cut the slices less than a quarter of an inch thick, take off the rind, place on the pan
3. If fresh, add a little sugar, salt and pepper, will brown quicker and a nicer flavor
4. Have a fork and spoon; cook thoroughly, press down all white places with a spoon until they are brown
5. When nicely browned both sides, lay on a platter
6. With the spoon pour a little gravy over each piece, and serve
