## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 whites eggs
- 1 cup fruit

## Directions

1. Cream butter, add sugar
2. Add flour and baking powder and milk
3. Fold in the whites of eggs
4. Put fruit in bottom of cups, add batter
5. Cover with oiled paper and steam 1 1/2 hour
