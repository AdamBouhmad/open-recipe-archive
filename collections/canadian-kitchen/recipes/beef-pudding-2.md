## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound beef
- 1 quart flour
- 1/2 pound suet
- 1 teaspoon salt
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- a little water

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into small pieces
2. Chop suet very fine, and add to flour, and salt and mix into a stiff paste with a knife
3. Roll out and line a bowl with the paste
4. Fill in the beef, with salt and pepper to taste
5. Dust with flour
6. Put in a little water
7. Cover the top with paste, and join the edges well
8. Tie down into a cloth and plunge into boiling water
9. Keep boiling for 4 hours
