## Ingredients

- 7 pounds green tomatoes
- 3 pounds green apples
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 large onions
- 1 3/4 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon red pepper
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop fine and put all together and boil well
