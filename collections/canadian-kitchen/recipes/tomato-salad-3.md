## Ingredients

- 1 qt. can tomatoes
- 2 bay leaves
- Juice 1 small onion
- A pinch of cayenne
- 1 teaspoonful celery salt or celery tops and salt
- 1 tablespoonful lemon juice
- 1 box gelatine

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes, bay leaves, salt, celery, onion and pepper in pan on stove
2. Stew gently for three hours
3. Soak gelatine in one cup of cold water for half an hour
4. Add it and one tablespoonful lemon juice to tomatoes, and strain into moulds
5. If tomatoes are very thick when opened, add a little water to them
