## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup rich milk

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes and leave in cold water 1/2 hour
2. Put in a steamer and cook until tender
3. Have ready a granite dish and put your potatoes in it
4. Mash well and fine
5. Season with salt, 1 tablespoon butter, and 1 cup rich milk
6. Mix together well
7. If liked, put in the oven to brown
