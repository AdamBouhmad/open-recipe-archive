## Ingredients

- 32 tomatoes chopped fine
- 6 cups brown sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1 teaspoon mace
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil 32 tomatoes chopped fine with 6 cups brown sugar for 1.5 hours
2. Add 1 quart vinegar, 1 teaspoon each mace, cloves and cinnamon
3. Boil 15 minutes
4. Cool, place in jars
