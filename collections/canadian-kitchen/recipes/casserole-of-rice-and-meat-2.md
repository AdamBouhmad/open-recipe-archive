## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped meat
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon onion juice
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup fine bread crumbs
- 4 cups boiled rice
- stock
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Season the meat and mix with the crumbs and beaten egg
2. Add enough stock to make it pack easily
3. Line a buttered dish with 3 cups rice
4. Fill with the meat
5. Cover with the remainder of rice
6. Cover tightly and steam 45 minutes
7. Serve with tomato sauce
