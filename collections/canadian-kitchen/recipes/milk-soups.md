## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup vegetable water or other liquid
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ½ cup vegetable pulp or chopped fish
- 1 tablespoon fat

## Directions

1. Put the vegetable water, fat and ¾ of the milk over the fire to heat
2. Mix the flour smoothly with the remaining milk
3. Stir it into the scalding liquid and stir constantly until it thickens
4. Stir in the vegetable pulp, season to taste with salt and pepper
5. Serve hot
