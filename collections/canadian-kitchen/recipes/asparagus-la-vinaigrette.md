## Ingredients

- asparagus stalks
- salt
- three tablespoonfuls salad oil
- one tablespoonful vinegar
- one teaspoonful French mustard
- salt
- cayenne
- one saltspoonful sugar

## Directions

1. Boil asparagus as directed
2. While the stalks are hot pour over them a dressing made of three tablespoonfuls of salad oil to one of vinegar, a teaspoonful of French mustard, a little salt and cayenne, and a saltspoonful of sugar
3. Set away in a closely covered dish
4. When cold put upon the ice for some hours before serving
