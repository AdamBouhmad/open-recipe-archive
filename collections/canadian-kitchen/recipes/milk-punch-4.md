## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 6 lemons
- 4 qts. water
- 1 qt. rum
- 1 pt. brandy
- 2 lbs. sugar
- 1 qt. new milk

## Directions

1. Pare oranges and lemons as thin as possible.
2. Steep the peels in rum and brandy in a close crock for twenty-four hours.
3. Squeeze the fruit on sugar; add water and boiling hot new milk; then stir the rum and brandy into the above and run it through a jelly bag until perfectly clear.
4. Bottle and cork at once. It will keep good for some weeks.
