## Ingredients

- 1 quart of tomatoes
- 1 quart water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 quart milk
- small piece of butter
- few rolled biscuits
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring tomatoes and water to a boil
2. Add soda
3. Add milk and butter
4. Add rolled biscuits
5. Add pepper and salt to taste
