## Ingredients

- thin fingers of nice pastry
- rich jam or jelly

## Directions

1. Bake very thin fingers of nice pastry
2. When cold spread daintily on the top of each finger a very little rich jam or jelly
3. Serve laid in star fashion on a large flat plate
