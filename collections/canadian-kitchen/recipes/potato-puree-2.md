## Ingredients

- 1 pound potatoes
- 1 Bermuda onion
- 2 leaves celery
- 1½ pints stock
- 1 gill single cream
- 1 ounce butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Put potato, celery, onion and butter in saucepan over fire, stir for five minutes
2. Add 1 pint stock and boil until potatoes are done, pass through sieve, adding the pint of stock, hot, to prevent clogging sieve
3. Return to saucepan, add cream, pepper and salt, and stir until it boils
4. Serve at once
