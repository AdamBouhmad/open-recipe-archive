## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 0.75 cup cornstarch
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs, butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add whites, starch, flour, baking powder and milk last
