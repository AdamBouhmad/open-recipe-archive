## Ingredients

- pieces of cold turkey or chicken
- butter
- small thick slices of bread or biscuit
- hot salted water
- a large spoonful of butter
- one or two cupfuls of milk
- any gravy that may be left over
- flour
- cold milk or water
- salt
- pepper
- dark meat

## Directions

1. Warm up pieces of cold turkey or chicken with a little butter in a frying-pan.
2. Place it on a warm platter and surround it with small thick slices of bread or biscuit halved, first dipping them in hot salted water.
3. Place the platter in a warm oven with the door open.
4. Into the frying-pan put a large spoonful of butter, one or two cupfuls of milk, and any gravy that may be left over.
5. Bring it to a boil.
6. Add sufficient flour, wet in a little cold milk or water, to make it the consistency of cream.
7. Season with salt, pepper and add a little of the dark meat chopped very fine.
8. Let the sauce cook a few moments.
9. Pour over the biscuit and fowl.
