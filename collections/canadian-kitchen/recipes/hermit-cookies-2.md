## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 large cup raisins stoned and chopped
- 1 teaspoon mixed spice
- 3/4 nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour to roll out

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients and roll out and bake.
