## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. beef
- 3 lbs. suet
- 3 lbs. raisins
- 3 lbs. currants
- 1/2 pk. apples
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup beef liquor
- 1 pt. brandy
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 2 lbs. sugar
- 1 dessertspoonful each cinnamon, cloves, ginger, allspice

## Directions

1. Chop beef, suet, apples, raisins, add spices and mix
