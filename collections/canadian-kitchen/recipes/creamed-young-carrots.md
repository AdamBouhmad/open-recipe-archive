## Ingredients

- young carrots
- one tablespoonful butter
- hot water
- salt and pepper
- four tablespoonfuls cream
- one teaspoonful chopped parsley
- beaten yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Scald for five minutes and rub off the skins with a rough cloth
2. Slice crosswise and thin
3. Heat in a saucepan butter, two tablespoonfuls of hot water, salt and pepper to taste
4. Put in the sliced carrots
5. Cook gently, covered, for half an hour
6. In another saucepan heat cream and chopped parsley
7. When the mixture boils take from the fire and pour upon the beaten yolks of eggs
8. Stir up well
9. Pour over the carrots
10. Cook one scant minute
