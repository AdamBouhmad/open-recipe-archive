## Ingredients

- 5 cups rhubarb chopped fine
- 3 large oranges
- 5 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 3/4 cups sugar to each cup rhubarb
- 1 cup chopped English walnuts

## Directions

1. Boil until thick
