## Ingredients

- 20 large tomatoes
- 2 bunches celery
- 4 large onions
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- red pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon mustard

## Directions

1. Skin and slice tomatoes
2. Chop celery and onions fine
3. Drain off juice
4. Add sugar, vinegar, cinnamon, red pepper, and mustard
5. Boil about 2 hours
6. Bottle
