## Ingredients

- 1 cup white or yellow sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons sweet cream
- 3 eggs well beaten
- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon fruit color
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- cold milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- yolk of 1 egg
- white of 1 egg
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- water
- 1/2 cup cocoanut

## Directions

1. Make into 3 layers and put 3/4 teaspoon fruit color in centre layer
2. For filling, boil 1/2 cup sweet milk
3. Thicken with 2 tablespoons cornstarch dissolved in cold milk
4. Add 1/2 cup sugar
5. When cool, add yolk of 1 egg
6. For top of cake, beat white of 1 egg
7. Boil 1/2 cup white sugar dissolved in a little water a few minutes
8. Stir these two together
9. Add 1/2 cup cocoanut
