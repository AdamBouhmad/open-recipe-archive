## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 cup boiling sweet milk

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, sugar, flour and baking powder together well
2. Add 3/4 cup boiling sweet milk
