## Ingredients

- shank of beef
- cold water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Procure a nice shank of beef chopped into about four pieces
2. Cover well with cold water and boil till tender
3. Boil until there is enough juice to cover the meat when taken from the bones
4. Put loosely in any convenient mould or basin
5. Let stand till cold in some cold place
6. Season to taste
