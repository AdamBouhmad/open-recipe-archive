## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. beef collops
- 1/2 lb. sausage
- 1 egg
- 1 small onion
- salt
- pepper
- cabbage leaves
- lard

## Directions

1. Form beef collops and sausage into croquettes.
2. Have some half-boiled leaves of cabbage, wrap each croquette in one large leaf, completely covering the meat.
3. Tie firmly and fry slowly in lard.
