## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/4 tablespoons flour
- onion juice
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 1/2 cups chopped meat (chicken, lamb, tongue or beef)
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Stir in 1 1/2 cups chopped meat (chicken, lamb, tongue or beef)
2. While cooking, add beaten yolks of 2 eggs
3. Cook 1 minute and set away to cool
4. When cool add beaten whites (stiff)
5. Bake 20 minutes in buttered pudding dish
