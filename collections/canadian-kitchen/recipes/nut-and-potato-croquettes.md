## Ingredients

- 1 cup black walnut meats
- 1 cup mashed and seasoned potatoes
- 1 cup soft bread crumbs
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper
- onion-juice
- lemon-juice
- 3 tablespoons beef-stock

## Directions

1. Coarsely chop black walnut meats to measure 1 cup
2. Mix with 1 cup of mashed and seasoned potatoes and 1 cup of soft bread crumbs
3. Stir in 2 well-beaten eggs
4. Add a high seasoning of salt, pepper and onion-juice, a few drops of lemon-juice and 3 tablespoons of beef-stock
5. When cold, mould into croquettes
6. Dip each into beaten egg
7. Roll in fine crumbs
8. Fry in deep, smoking-hot fat
