## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- 2 ounces butter
- seasoning
- 1/2 onion
- parsley
- 2 ounces flour
- 1 pint of broth
- grated nutmeg
- mixed herbs
- bacon

## Directions

1. Cut rabbit into small joints
2. Place them in a saucepan with 2 ounces butter, some seasoning, 1/2 onion, and some parsley chopped very fine
3. Fry very slowly until cooked golden brown
4. Dust over 2 ounces of flour
5. Add 1 pint of broth, a little grated nutmeg and mixed herbs
6. Let simmer 3/4 hour
7. Pile the rabbit in a heap on hot platter and pour sauce over
8. Garnish with small pieces of bacon
