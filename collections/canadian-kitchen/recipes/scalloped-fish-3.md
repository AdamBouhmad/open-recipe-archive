## Ingredients

- 1 cup flaked fish
- 1 cup stale crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1-16 teaspoon pepper
- 1 1/16 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix
2. Put in buttered dish
3. Cover with buttered crumbs
4. Bake in a moderate oven 30 to 40 minutes
