## Ingredients

- carrots
- weak stock
- two tablespoonfuls of milk
- one tablespoonful of butter
- one tablespoonful of flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape and boil whole three-quarters of an hour
2. Drain
3. Cut into cubes half an inch square
4. Have ready in a saucepan enough weak stock to cover the carrot-dice
5. Put them on in it and cook twenty minutes, or until tender
6. Add two tablespoonfuls of milk, a tablespoonful of butter cut up in one of flour, salt and pepper to taste
7. Simmer five minutes
8. Serve
