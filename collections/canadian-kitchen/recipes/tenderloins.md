## Ingredients

- tenderloins
- butter
- water
- salt
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take tenderloins and put in a saucepan with 2 tablespoons butter
2. Keep turning till all sides are browned nicely
3. Cover with water and a pinch of salt and let boil till tender
4. Let the water boil down
5. Thicken with 2 teaspoons flour and a little water
6. Season with pepper to taste
