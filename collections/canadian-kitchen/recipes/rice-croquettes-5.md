## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons cooked rice
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon minced parsley
- few grains of cayenne
- beaten egg
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Form rice mixture into balls
2. Roll in beaten egg and cracker crumbs
3. Fry in deep fat
