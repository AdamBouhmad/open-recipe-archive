## Ingredients

- 1 qt. boiling water
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 4 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- 2 eggs, beaten separately
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch with a little cold water, add the lemonjuice.
2. Have the water boiling on the fire; put in the sugar and stir in the cornstarch.
3. When it has well thickened add the whites of the eggs beaten very stiff.
4. Stir well and pour into a mould.
5. The yolks of the eggs can be used to make a custard, with one pint of milk and one small tablespoonful of cornstarch; sugar to taste.
6. Serve cold.
