## Ingredients

- 24 pears
- 10 ripe quinces
- Juice 3 lemons
- 1 1/2 lbs. sugar to each 1 lb. fruit
- 1 pt. water to each lb. sugar

## Directions

1. Weigh fruit after it is ready to cook
2. Pare and core the fruit and throw into cold water
3. Stew parings and cores in a little cold water to make syrup
4. When the parings are well boiled strain off liquid
5. When cold put in fruit and bring quickly to a boil, boiling until smooth
6. Add the sugar and lemon juice, and cook steadily for an hour
7. Work with a spoon to a rich jelly
