## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 1 pt. boiling water
- 4 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 scant cup grated chocolate
- 1 pt. strong coffee
- 10 drops vanilla
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Scald one pint of milk and add the same amount of boiling water.
2. Mix together sugar and grated chocolate.
3. Add sufficient of the hot liquid to mix to a smooth paste, gradually dilute and turn into the milk and water, then cook gently for five minutes.
4. Add a pinch of salt, coffee, and vanilla, and take from the fire.
5. Serve with whipped cream for luncheon or 5 o'clock tea.
