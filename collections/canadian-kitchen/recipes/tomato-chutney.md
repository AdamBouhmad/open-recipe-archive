## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. ripe tomatoes
- 4 lbs. sour apples
- 2 lbs. raisins
- 2 lbs. brown sugar
- 4 small onions
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 8 oz. salt
- 3 oz. ginger
- 2 oz. allspice
- 1 oz. cayenne
- 1 nutmeg grated
- 2 qts. vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop tomatoes, apples, onions and raisins very fine; add everything else and boil one hour slowly; bottle when cold.
