## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of calf's liver
- 3 oz. bacon
- 1 lb. mushrooms
- 1 oz. flour
- 1/2 pint of stock
- Fried bread

## Directions

1. Fry the liver and bacon and the mushrooms separately
2. Put all into a stew-pan with half a pint of stock and simmer for one hour
3. Serve with fried bread
