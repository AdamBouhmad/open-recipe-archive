## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- sweet milk
- 5 or 6 slices of pork

## Directions

1. Pare raw potatoes and slice very thinly enough to fill a 2 quart pudding dish
2. Season with salt and pepper over the top
3. Pour over sweet milk 3/4 full
4. Cut 5 or 6 slices of pork and lay over the top
5. Bake about 2 hours
6. If liked, the pork may be left off and a little more milk added
