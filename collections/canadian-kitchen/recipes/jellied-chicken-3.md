## Ingredients

- fowl
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- 1/2 ounce gelatine

## Directions

1. Boil a fowl until it will slip easily from the bones; let the water be reduced to about 1 pint in boiling
2. Pick the meat from the bones, place in a wet mould
3. Skim the fat from the liquor, add a little butter, pepper and salt to the taste, and 1/2 ounce of gelatine
4. When this dissolves pour it hot over the chicken
5. Season the liquor pretty high, for the chicken absorbs
