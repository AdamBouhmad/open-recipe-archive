## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- butter
- rich boiling milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread thin and toast quickly to a light brown. Butter while hot and pile them in a deep dish. Cover them with rich boiling milk. Let it stand a few minutes and serve. A little salt may be added if necessary.
