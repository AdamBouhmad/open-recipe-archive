## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- whites of 4 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- cloves
- cinnamon
- spices
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup flour
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk

## Directions

1. Take 1/2 this mixture, add to it 1/2 cup molasses, cloves, cinnamon and spices to taste
2. Add 1/2 cup raisins
3. Add 1/2 cup flour and 3 tablespoons sweet milk
