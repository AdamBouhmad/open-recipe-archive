## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the butter, sugar and egg until very light
2. Add vanilla and milk
3. Add flour sifted with baking powder
