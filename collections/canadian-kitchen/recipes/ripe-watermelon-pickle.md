## Ingredients

- watermelon
- powdered alum
- water
- 2 pounds white sugar
- 2 pints white wine vinegar
- whole cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut in 4-inch pieces
2. Cover with alum water, allowing 2 teaspoons powdered alum to each quart water
3. Heat to boiling point
4. Let stand on back of stove for 2 hours
5. Remove and chill in water
6. Make syrup of 2 pounds white sugar, 2 pints white wine vinegar, 2 tablespoons whole cloves and cinnamon (1 of each), tied in bag
7. Add your pickles
8. Cook 10 minutes, put in jar
9. Heat syrup 3 mornings, pour over
10. The last, pour fruit and all, scald up
11. Put in jars and seal
