## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- butter size of an egg
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 egg
- 1 orange
- little piece butter
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, eggs, butter size of an egg, milk, flour and baking powder
2. Bake in layers
3. For filling: Mix 1 cup sugar, 1 cup boiling water, 1 egg, 1 orange, little piece butter and cornstarch to thicken
