## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup water
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat 3 eggs 1 minute
2. Add 1 1/2 cups white sugar and beat 5 minutes
3. Add 1 cup flour and beat 1 minute
4. Add 3/4 cup water and flavor to taste
5. Mix 1 cup flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder together and add
6. Bake in a cake pan
7. Ice with icing sugar moistened with milk and flavored to suit
8. Lay on shelled walnuts so you can cut in square pieces
