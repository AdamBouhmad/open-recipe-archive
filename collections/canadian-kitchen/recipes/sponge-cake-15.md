## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks separate
2. Add sugar to whites, then yolks, flour, and baking powder
3. Mix quickly
