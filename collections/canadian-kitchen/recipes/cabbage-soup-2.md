## Ingredients

- a piece of meat, either pork or beef
- water
- cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- 1 cupful of good sour cream
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Add enough water to cover meat and bring to a boil
2. Skim it
3. Cut cabbage fine, cover with water, bring to a boil, and drain and add it to the boiling meat
4. Season with salt, pepper and onion
5. When meat and cabbage are done, add a cupful of good sour cream, bring to boiling again
6. Take out the meat and serve the soup
7. Tomatoes may be added and it will be all the nicer
