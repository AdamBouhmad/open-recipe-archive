## Ingredients

- 1 part ice-cold water
- 1 part thick sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 quart flour
- chicken
- rolled crackers
- broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Line a deep biscuit pan with pastry made with 1 part ice-cold water and one part of thick sour cream, with 1 teaspoon soda and 2 of cream of tartar to 1 quart of flour
2. Bone the chicken and distribute white and dark meat evenly over crust
3. Sprinkle rolled crackers 1/2 inch in depth over meat when pan was half filled
4. Simmer broth to good strength, thicken and season as for gravy
5. Pour in pan enough to nearly cover second layer of meat
6. Leave opening in top crust for escape of steam
7. Bake 40 minutes
