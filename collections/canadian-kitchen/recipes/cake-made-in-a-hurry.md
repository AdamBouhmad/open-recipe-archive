## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup milk or water
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Put all in a bowl together and beat until light
