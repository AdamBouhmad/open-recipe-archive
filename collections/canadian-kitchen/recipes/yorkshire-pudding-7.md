## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- 6 ounces flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- beef dripping

## Directions

1. Beat eggs light
2. add part of milk, all flour and salt, then the rest of the milk
3. When ready to cook take plenty of the beef dripping in which the meat was roasted, place in another pan
4. When boiling hot add the pudding and cook in hot oven
5. When a nice brown remove, and after it cools a minute cut in squares and serve
