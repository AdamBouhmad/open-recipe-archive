## Ingredients

- oysters
- cayenne
- lemon juice
- tomato sauce
- tobasco sauce

## Directions

1. Put three or four oysters in a glass (small lemonade glass)
2. Mix cayenne, lemon-juice and tomato sauce, also put a few drops of tobasco sauce (very little)
3. Let all stand in glasses, on ice, for about three hours
4. Serve glasses on small plate and watercress around the glasses
