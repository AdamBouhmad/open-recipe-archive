## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiling water
- 2 squares in lemon rind
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook flour until somewhat thick
2. Add butter and lemon juice just before removing from the stove
3. Remove rind
