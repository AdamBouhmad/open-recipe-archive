## Ingredients

- 1 gallon hot water
- juice of 5 lemons
- juice of 4 oranges
- 2 pounds granulated sugar
- whites of 10 eggs
- 2 cans pineapple
- gelatine

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the water
2. Add whites of 10 eggs well beaten
3. Add 2 cans pineapple grated
4. Add a little gelatine dissolved in hot water
5. Put the whites of eggs in last
6. Freeze the same as ice cream
