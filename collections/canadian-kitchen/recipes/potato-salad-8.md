## Ingredients

- One pint sifted hot potatoes
- butter size of an egg
- one-quarter teaspoon mustard
- one-half teaspoon salt
- shake of pepper
- one egg lightly beaten
- vinegar or lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Cream butter, mustard, salt, and pepper together
2. Add lightly beaten egg
3. Mix in sifted hot potatoes
4. Add a little vinegar or lemon-juice
