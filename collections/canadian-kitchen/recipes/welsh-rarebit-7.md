## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 lb. soft cheese
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- Saltspoonful cayenne
- 1/2 cup milk or cream

## Directions

1. Break eggs into the upper pan of the chafing-dish, beat them well, then add cheese, broken into small bits; butter, salt, mustard, cayenne and cream or milk
2. stir this mixture well, until cheese is melted
3. Serve on crisp toast or on toasted thin water-crackers
