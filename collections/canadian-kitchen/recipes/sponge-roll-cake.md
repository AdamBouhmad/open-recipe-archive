## Ingredients

- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 1 cup light brown sugar
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup flour
- raspberry preserves

## Directions

1. Mix baking powder with flour
2. Beat all ingredients together
3. Bake in a quick oven
4. When cooked, spread a little raspberry preserves over the top
5. Roll while warm
