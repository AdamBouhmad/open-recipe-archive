## Ingredients

- large fruits
- water
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Cut large fruits in pieces
2. Place in a granite or aluminum kettle with scarcely enough water to cover
3. Cover tightly and boil slowly until soft
4. Turn into jelly bag which has been soaking in hot water
5. Drain in a place free from draughts
6. Do not squeeze the fruit
7. Measure the juice and to each pint allow a pound of granulated sugar
8. Place juice on stove and sugar in a pan in the oven to heat
9. Stir sugar occasionally
10. Boil juice steadily and not too rapidly for 20 minutes
11. Turn the sugar into the juice and boil all together for 3 minutes
12. Pour into hot glasses
