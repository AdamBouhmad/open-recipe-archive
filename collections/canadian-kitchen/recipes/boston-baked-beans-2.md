## Ingredients

- 1 quart white beans
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup cream
- 2 cups sweet milk
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- salt and pepper to taste
- a few slices of fresh pork

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash 1 quart white beans
2. put on with 2 quarts boiling water and 1 teaspoon soda
3. cook until soft, adding water from time to time while cooking
4. drain and add 1 cup cream, 2 cups sweet milk, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 2 tablespoons molasses, salt and pepper to taste
5. put in a dripping pan and place on top of the beans a few slices of fresh pork
6. bake until a rich brown
