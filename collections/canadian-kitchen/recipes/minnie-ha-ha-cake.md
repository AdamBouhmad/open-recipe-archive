## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk or water
- piece butter size of an egg
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar mixed in flour
- 1 teaspoon soda mixed in milk
- a little salt
- 1 3/4 cups sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar, 1 egg, 1 cup milk or water, piece butter size of an egg, 2 cups flour with 2 teaspoons cream tartar mixed in flour, 1 teaspoon soda mixed in milk, and a little salt
2. Bake cake
3. For icing: Beat 1 3/4 cups sugar, 1 cup raisins, and white of an egg to a stiff froth
4. Spread icing on cake
