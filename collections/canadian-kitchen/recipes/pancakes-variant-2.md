## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup sour milk or buttermilk
- pinch of salt
- 2 cups sifted flour
- a little brown sugar
- milk or flour to thin or thicken the batter

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in sour milk or buttermilk. Add salt and sifted flour with a little brown sugar. Add milk or flour to thin or thicken the batter.
