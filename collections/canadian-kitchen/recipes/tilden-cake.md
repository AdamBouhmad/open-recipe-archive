## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons lemon extract

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake
