## Ingredients

- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 oz. butter
- 1 teaspoonful anchovy paste
- cayenne
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the eggs in half and take out the yolks very carefully.
2. Cut a small slice from the end of the whites.
3. Pound together the yolks, butter and anchovy paste.
4. Add seasoning and fill the whites with the mixture.
