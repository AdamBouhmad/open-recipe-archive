## Ingredients

- 1 pt. mashed sweet potato
- 1 teacup sweet milk
- Yolks of 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- A little nutmeg or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter together
2. Mix with potatoes, flavor with nutmeg or cinnamon
3. Add beaten yolks and milk
4. Beat the whites to a stiff froth and stir in
5. Pour in pie-pan lined with crust and bake quickly
