## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cranberries
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon butter
- ½ cup water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake between 2 crusts
