## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated oatmeal
- 1 cup flour
- 1 level teaspoon of black pepper
- 1 level tablespoon salt
- 1 cup suet
- 2 medium-sized onions

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly and put in cloth and tie up
2. Leave a little room to swell
3. Boil 2 hours
