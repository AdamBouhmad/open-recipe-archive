## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rice
- 1/4 cup scalded milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- grated rind of 1 1/2 lemons
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- eggs and crumbs for rolling
- jelly cubes

## Directions

1. Wash rice and cook until tender
2. Drain
3. Add water or milk, yolks of eggs and butter
4. Cool
5. Shape
6. Roll in eggs and crumbs
7. Fry
8. Put cubes of jelly on each
