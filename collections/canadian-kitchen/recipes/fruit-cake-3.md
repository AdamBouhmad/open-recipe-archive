## Ingredients

- 1 lb. melted butter
- 1/2 lb. brown sugar or white sugar
- 3/4 cup scalding milk
- 1 lb. flour
- 1/2 nutmeg, grated
- 1 lb. currants
- 1/2 lb. raisins and candied peel mixed
- 1 small teaspoonful soda

## Directions

1. Mix well-melted butter with sugar
2. Add scalding milk
3. Grate 1/2 nutmeg
4. Stir in well the sifted flour, currants, raisins and candied peel
5. Add soda wet with boiling water
6. Butter the pans and put a piece of buttered paper in bottom of pans
7. Bake for 1 and 1/2 hours in a moderately hot oven
