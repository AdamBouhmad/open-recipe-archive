## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- vinegar
- whole allspice
- cloves
- mustard
- bread crumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in cold water for 12 hours
2. Put it on to boil, allow 15 minutes for each pound
3. Pour the water off, fill the pot with fresh water (boiling)
4. Put in a pint of vinegar, a tablespoon of whole allspice, teaspoon cloves
5. Allow the ham to cook in this, 15 minutes for each pound
6. Let it remain in this liquor till cold, skim it
7. Make a paste of vinegar and mustard and cover the top with it
8. Put bread crumbs and a little sugar on, then return to oven for a short time
