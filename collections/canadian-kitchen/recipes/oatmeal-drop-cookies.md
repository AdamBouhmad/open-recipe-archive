## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup butter
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 4 cups oatmeal
- enough flour to drop on the bottom of pan

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop on the bottom of pan
3. Do not roll
4. Bake
