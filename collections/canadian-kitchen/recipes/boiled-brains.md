## Ingredients

- Calf's or bullock's brains
- pepper and salt
- marjoram or sage
- sippets of toasted bread
- melted butter sauce

## Directions

1. Steep the brains in lukewarm water for two hours to draw the blood
2. then tie in muslin, put into boiling water and boil for twenty minutes
3. take them up, drain in a colander, then turn into a basin and beat thoroughly with a fork
4. season with plenty of pepper and salt and a little marjoram or sage — sage is best
5. Put on a hot dish, pour over a good melted butter sauce, and garnish with sippets of toast
