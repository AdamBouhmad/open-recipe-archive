## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, flour, sugar, melted butter, and sweet milk
2. Add raisins and baking powder
3. Steam 1 1/2 hour in cups
4. Serve with maple syrup
