## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- 1 cupful of stock
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Cook tender in two waters — the second salted and boiling.
2. Drain well, pressing each onion in a coarse cloth, gently, not to break it, and when they are dry, lay all together, side by side, in a bake-pan.
3. Pepper, salt, and butter, and add a cupful of stock.
4. Brown in a quick oven; take out the onions and keep them hot in a deep dish while you thicken the gravy left in the pan with browned flour.
5. Pour over the onions, set in the oven for two minutes, and serve.
