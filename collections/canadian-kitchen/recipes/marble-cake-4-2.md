## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups white sugar
- ¾ cup butter
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- ¾ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- whites of 4 eggs
- 2¾ cups flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup molasses
- ¾ cup butter
- ¾ cup sour milk
- ¾ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 2¾ cups flour
- yolks of 4 eggs
- ½ tablespoon each of cloves
- ½ tablespoon allspice
- ½ tablespoon cinnamon
- ½ tablespoon ground nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat and mix light part
2. Beat and mix dark part
3. When each part is ready, drop a spoon of dark, then a spoon of light, over the bottom of the pan, in which it is to be baked
4. Proceed to fill up the pan, dropping the light upon the dark as you continue with the different layers
