## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup fruit pulp either peach, apricot, quince or prunes
- 3 whites of eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook fruit, rub through a sieve, sweeten and add perhaps lemon juice
2. If canned fruit is used, first drain from syrup
3. Beat whites of eggs until stiff
4. Add fruit pulp gradually and continue beating
5. Turn into buttered moulds, having them 3/4 full
6. Set moulds in pan of hot water and bake in slow oven until firm
7. Serve with custard
