## Ingredients

- 4 pounds sugar
- 8 tumblers hot water
- Juice from 4 oranges
- 1 ounce citric acid
- Whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Let stand 24 hours
2. Strain and bottle
3. Use like raspberry vinegar, in water
