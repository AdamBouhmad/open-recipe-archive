## Ingredients

- one cup of fish
- cold water
- one cupful of good cream
- one cupful of milk
- two tablespoonfuls of flour
- one tablespoonful of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick one cup of fish fine, then freshen in cold water
2. Bring just to a boil, then drain
3. Take one cupful of good cream and one cupful of milk
4. Mix two tablespoonfuls of flour smooth with some of the milk
5. Add one tablespoonful of butter and more salt if necessary
