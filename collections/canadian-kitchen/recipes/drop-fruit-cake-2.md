## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup each chopped English walnuts and dates or raisins
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Drop on a greased pan
