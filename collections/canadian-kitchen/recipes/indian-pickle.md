## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 12 tomatoes
- 9 onions
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2½ cups brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- ½ teaspoon red pepper
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Peel and core 12 apples
2. Peel 12 tomatoes
3. Peel 9 onions
4. Boil into 1 quart vinegar, 2½ cups brown sugar, 1 teaspoon mustard, 1 teaspoon ginger, 1 teaspoon cloves, ½ teaspoon red pepper
5. Add salt to taste
