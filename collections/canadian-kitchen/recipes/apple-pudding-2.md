## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 level teaspoons baking powder
- 1 egg
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- a little salt
- 3 large apples, peeled and sliced

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar, then egg, and beat well
2. Add flour and milk alternately, stirring apples
