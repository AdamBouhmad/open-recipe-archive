## Ingredients

- hot boiled corn from cob
- equal quantity of hot boiled shelled beans
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut hot boiled corn from cob
2. add an equal quantity of hot boiled shelled beans
3. season with butter and salt
4. re-heat before serving
