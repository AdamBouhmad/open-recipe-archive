## Ingredients

- 4 pounds stew beef
- small piece of suet
- water
- 3 medium carrots
- large onion
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put beef and suet in pot and brown
2. Cover with water and let come to a boil
3. Keep at a slow simmer for four hours
4. While stewing, add 3 medium carrots (put through the chopper) and a large onion
5. Add pepper and salt to flavor
6. Let stew down until there is just enough liquid for gravy
