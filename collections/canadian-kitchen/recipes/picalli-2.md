## Ingredients

- Green tomatoes cut fine
- cabbage
- onions
- celery
- vinegar
- sugar
- spices
- pepper
- 1 cup of horseradish

## Directions

1. Put on stove with vinegar, sugar, spices, pepper, and horseradish
2. Boil until cooked
