## Ingredients

- fresh fruit (berries preferred)
- a little sugar
- slices of bread

## Directions

1. Boil some fresh fruit and a little sugar for 10 minutes
2. Line a wet bowl with slices of bread
3. Pour fruit into bowl
4. Cover with slices of bread
5. Place a weighted cover on bowl
6. Put to cool in a pan or basin of cold water or pack round with ice
7. Best eaten next day
