## Ingredients

- fresh bacon
- potatoes
- sage
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Take a fresh piece of bacon, cut in small pieces, put in a kettle and stew
2. Add potatoes cut in small pieces, a little sage, pepper and salt and flour and water
3. Serve hot
