## Ingredients

- 2 cups pineapple
- 1 cup rhubarb
- 2 parts sugar and 1 part water

## Directions

1. Cut pineapple and rhubarb in dice
2. Put in sterilized jar
3. Pour over them a heavy syrup made of 2 parts sugar and 1 water
4. Place in water and let cook for some time
