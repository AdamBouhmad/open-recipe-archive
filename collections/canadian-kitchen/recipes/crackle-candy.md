## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar
- butter size of small egg
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, butter, water, and lemon juice together
2. When it hardens in cold water, remove from fire and flavor with vanilla
3. As soon as cool enough to handle, flour the hands and pull
