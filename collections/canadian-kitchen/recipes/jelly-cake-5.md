## Ingredients

- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup butter
- lemon filling

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar, water, flour, baking powder, salt, and butter
2. Bake
3. Lemon filling is nice for this cake
