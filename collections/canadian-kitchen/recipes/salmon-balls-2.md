## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 12 soda biscuits
- 2 eggs
- milk
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Roll 12 soda biscuits fine
2. Mix 1 can salmon, rolled biscuits, 2 eggs, and enough milk to moisten
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Roll in balls
5. Fry in butter
