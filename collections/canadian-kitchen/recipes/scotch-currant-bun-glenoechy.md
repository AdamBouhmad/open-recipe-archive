## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1 lb. sugar
- 2 lbs. currants
- 2 lbs. raisins
- 1 lb. orange peel
- ½ lb. almonds
- 1 large cup of buttermilk
- 2 teaspoonfuls ginger
- 2 teaspoonfuls cinnamon
- 2 teaspoonfuls Jamaica pepper
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream tartar
- ½ teaspoonful black pepper
- 1½ lbs. flour for paste
- ½ lb. butter for paste
- ½ teaspoonful baking powder for paste

## Directions

1. Stone raisins, clean currants, blanch almonds
2. Cut up orange-peel fine
3. Put all fruit, flour, sugar, and spices into a big basin; set aside
4. Make paste by rubbing butter into flour with baking powder, making into a stiff dough with water
5. Roll out thin
6. Grease tin, line it with paste; keep piece for top of bun
7. Pour milk into flour and mix also fruit all well together with the hand
8. It must be just moist, not too thin
9. Pour all into the tin and lay on top sheet
10. Dot with fork
11. Bake for three hours in moderate oven
