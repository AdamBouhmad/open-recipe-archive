## Ingredients

- Yolks 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 teaspoonful each salt, pepper and mustard
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1/4 cup cream
- 1/4 teaspoonful cayenne
- 1/2 pt. of vinegar

## Directions

1. To the well-beaten yolks add sugar, salt, mustard and black pepper, cream and cayenne pepper
2. Beat all these thoroughly
3. Take vinegar and heat; when hot add butter
4. Pour this over the mixture, boil up and when cold put into large-mouthed bottles
5. This will keep many weeks in a cool place
