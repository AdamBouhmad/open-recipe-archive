## Ingredients

- pig's liver
- fat pork
- onion
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a good-sized pig's liver and 1 pound of fat pork
2. Boil together until tender
3. Take out and mince fine with one large onion
4. Add pepper and salt to suit taste
5. Boil the liquor down to a small quantity and pour over the minced liver
6. Let cool
7. Can be served either warm or cold
