## Ingredients

- stiff mashed potatoes
- two teaspoonfuls of breadcrumbs
- a chopped tomato
- parsley or herb seasoning
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing with two teaspoonfuls of breadcrumbs, a chopped tomato, a little parsley or herb seasoning, and moisten with beaten egg.
2. Shape two rounds of potato, make a little hollow in one, fill with stuffing and press the other over it.
3. Roll in egg and breadcrumbs and then fry.
