## Ingredients

- 6 large apples (grated)
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1/4 lb. sugar
- 2 eggs (whites and yolks beaten separately)
- Juice of 1 lemon and half the rind grated
- Pastry

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar into a cream
2. Stir in the yolks, the lemon, the grated apple, and lastly the whites of the eggs
3. Line a dish with pastry
4. Pour in the mixture
5. Bake till nicely browned
6. This is best cold
