## Ingredients

- sweet milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- potatoes
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put 1 pint of sweet milk to heat
2. Add a small piece of butter, a little pepper and salt
3. Moisten 3 teaspoons flour in a little milk and add
4. Keep well stirred
5. Have 3 or 4 potatoes peeled, slice them in and cover with bread crumbs
6. Bake until brown (about 1½ hours)
