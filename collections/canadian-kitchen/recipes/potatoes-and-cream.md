## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- rich milk or cream

## Directions

1. Mince cold boiled potatoes fine
2. Put them into a spider with some melted butter
3. Cover them and let them fry a little
4. Put in another piece of butter, and pepper and salt
5. Pour over them some rich milk or cream
6. Boil up once and serve
