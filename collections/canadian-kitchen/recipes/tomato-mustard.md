## Ingredients

- 1 pk. tomatoes
- 3 red peppers
- 5 onions
- 1 lb. salt
- 1 pt. vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful black pepper
- 1 oz. ginger
- 1 oz. cloves
- 1 oz. mace
- 1 lb. mustard

## Directions

1. Boil tomatoes with peppers and onions for an hour.
2. Then strain through a colander and add salt, black pepper, ginger, cloves, mace (ground).
3. Boil for one hour; when cold add mustard and vinegar.
