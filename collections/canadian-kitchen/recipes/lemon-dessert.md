## Ingredients

- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon butter
- pinch of salt
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. When water comes to boil, add beaten yolks, cornstarch and lemon juice
2. beat the whites stiff, and add to mixture when it begins to bubble
3. Beat all briskly
4. Serve cold
