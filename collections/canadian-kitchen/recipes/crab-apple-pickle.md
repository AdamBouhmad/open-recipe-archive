## Ingredients

- 1 pk. apples
- 3 lbs. sugar
- 2 qts. vinegar
- 1 oz. cinnamon
- 1 oz. cloves

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, vinegar, spices, tying spices in muslin bag; prepare apples by sticking three or four cloves in each apple; steam until soft; then put them in syrup and let simmer for fifteen minutes; then take apples out and let syrup boil for ten minutes longer.
