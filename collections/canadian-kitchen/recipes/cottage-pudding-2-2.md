## Ingredients

- 7/8 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Combine 7/8 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 egg, 1/2 cup sweet milk, 1 cup flour, and 1 teaspoon baking powder
2. Put in a round buttered tin and bake in a quick oven
