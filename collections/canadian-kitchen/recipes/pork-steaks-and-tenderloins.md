## Ingredients

- pork steaks and tenderloins
- pepper
- salt
- powdered sage
- onion-juice
- lemon-juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Broil over a clear fire, turning every two minutes for twenty or twenty-five minutes.
2. Lay upon a hot dish and dust with pepper and salt and powdered sage.
3. Sprinkle with onion-juice and with lemon-juice, and drop bits of butter here and there.
4. Cover closely over hot water for ten minutes before sending to table.
