## Ingredients

- lemon jelly
- strawberries
- half a pint of cream
- one-quarter of an ounce of best leaf gelatine
- water or milk
- strawberry pulp

## Directions

1. Line a mould with nicely flavored lemon jelly
2. Put in a layer of strawberries, freed from their stalks and cut in halves
3. Set this layer with a little more jelly, have a smaller mould the same shape as the first
4. stand it in the centre of the larger mould
5. fill up the outer circumference with alternate layers of the strawberries and lemon jelly
6. Place the mould on ice to set
7. whip half a pint of cream to a stiff froth, adding about one-quarter of an ounce of best leaf gelatine, dissolved in a very little water or milk
8. very gradually add to this a cupful of strawberry pulp, sweetened to taste
9. When the jelly is set, remove the inner mould by pouring a little warm water into it and lifting out as quickly as possible
10. fill up the space thus left with the whipped cream and strawberry pulp
11. put the mould back on the ice for an hour or two
12. turn out and serve with cream
