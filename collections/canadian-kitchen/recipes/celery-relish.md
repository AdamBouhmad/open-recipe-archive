## Ingredients

- 1 dozen bunches celery cut fine
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 small tin mustard (1/2 pound)
- 2 ounces curry powder
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 red peppers
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Let vinegar come to the boil and keep simmering
2. Mix mustard, curry powder, and flour separately in a little vinegar
3. Be sure they are very smooth
4. Put the flour in the hot vinegar first
5. Then add the mustard and curry powder and chopped peppers
6. Add the celery and boil all for 10 minutes
7. Be sure it is not lumpy before adding the celery
8. Put away in jars
