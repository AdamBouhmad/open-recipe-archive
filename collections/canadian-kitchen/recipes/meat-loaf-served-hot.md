## Ingredients

- 2 cups finely chopped meat (cold)
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- 1 small onion (chopped)
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- gravy

## Directions

1. Salt and pepper, mix thoroughly
2. Place in well-buttered basin
3. Cover with buttered paper
4. Set in a large basin of hot water
5. Bake 1 hour
6. Turn on a hot platter and serve with gravy
