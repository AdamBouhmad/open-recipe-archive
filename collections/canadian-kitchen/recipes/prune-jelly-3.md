## Ingredients

- One pound of stewing prunes
- Half a package of gelatine
- About half a cupful of sugar
- Water
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Wash prunes well and leave them to soak about an hour
2. Put half a package of gelatine to soak
3. Put the prunes in a saucepan on the fire, well covered with water and about half a cupful of sugar
4. Boil for about an hour
5. Strain the juice from the prunes
6. Add the gelatine to the juice and put on the fire to boil up
7. Cover the prunes with the juice and gelatine mixed
8. Put in hot in a mould and leave till cold
9. Serve with whipped cream
