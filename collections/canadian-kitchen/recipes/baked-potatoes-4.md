## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup cream or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat potatoes and butter
2. Add whipped eggs, cream or milk, and salt to taste
3. Beat well
4. Pour into a buttered dish
5. Bake in a quick oven until well browned
