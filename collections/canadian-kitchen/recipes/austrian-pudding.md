## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- A pinch of salt
- 1 large teaspoonful baking-powder
- 1/2 lb. suet, chopped
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 breakfast cup of milk
- 1 breakfast cup of treacle

## Directions

1. Mix the dry ingredients together, then warm the milk, stir it into the treacle, and add it to the pudding.
2. Mix well and boil slowly but continuously in a well-buttered basin for three hours.
