## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 beaten egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Graham flour for good batter

## Directions

1. Mix butter, egg, milk, sugar, and baking powder into graham flour batter
2. Bake in gem-pans
3. Tear gems open with a fork, and butter
