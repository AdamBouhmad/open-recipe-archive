## Ingredients

- white of one egg
- one teaspoon vanilla
- one tablespoon water
- icing sugar
- one-sixth of a package of Baker's chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat white of one egg stiff
2. add one teaspoon of vanilla and one tablespoon of water, and enough icing sugar to make stiff
3. Melt one-sixth of a package of Baker's chocolate
4. beat into egg and sugar and spread it on the cake
