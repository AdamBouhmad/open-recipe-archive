## Ingredients

- 2 scant tablespoonfuls butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. Rub butter to a cream
2. Add flour
3. Pour on gradually one cup of hot milk
4. Cook eight minutes in double boiler, stirring often
5. Separate yolks and whites of eggs
6. Put whites on ice
7. Beat yolks
8. Add sugar
9. Add to the milk
10. Set away to cool
11. Half an hour before serving beat the whites stiff
12. Cut them in lightly
13. Bake in buttered pudding dish in moderate oven thirty minutes
14. Serve at once with creamy sauce
