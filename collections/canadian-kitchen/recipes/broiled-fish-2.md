## Ingredients

- fish
- suet
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and split the fish.
2. Rub a double broiler with suet.
3. Lay the fish, flesh side down, on the broiler and set over the fire.
4. Turn until both sides are brown.
5. When done take up carefully on a heated dish.
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
7. Spread with butter and serve.
