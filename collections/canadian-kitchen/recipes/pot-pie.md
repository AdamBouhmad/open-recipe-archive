## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 heaping teaspoons baking powder
- butter size of small egg
- a little salt
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. mix flour, baking powder and salt together
2. rub in butter as for pie crust
3. wet with sweet milk as thick as can be stirred
4. drop in pot in small spoonfuls
