## Ingredients

- 1 dozen ears of corn
- 1 large head cabbage
- 1 quart white wine vinegar
- 3 or 4 cups brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 6 peppers (3 green and 3 red)
- 1 tablespoon mustard seed
- 1 tablespoon celery seed
- 5 cents turmeric and curry

## Directions

1. Boil 15 or 20 minutes
2. Add more vinegar if you think it too thick
