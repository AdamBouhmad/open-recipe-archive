## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of shin of beef, or 6 lbs. of knuckle of veal
- Bones, trimmings of poultry, or fresh meat
- 1 lb. of lean bacon or ham
- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 large onions, each stuck with cloves
- 1 turnip
- 3 carrots
- 1 head of celery
- 2 oz. of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of whole pepper
- 1 large blade of mace
- 1 bunch of savory herbs except sage
- 4 quarts and 1/2 pt. of cold water

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat and bacon, or ham, into pieces of about three inches square
2. Break the bones into small pieces
3. Rub the butter on the bottom of the stewpan
4. Put in one-half a pint of water, the broken bones, then meat and all other ingredients
5. Cover the stew-pan, and place it on a sharp fire, occasionally stirring its contents
6. When the bottom of the pan becomes covered with a pale, jelly-like substance, add the four quarts of cold water, and simmer very gently for five or six hours
7. Do not let it boil quickly
8. When nearly cooked, throw in a tablespoonful of salt to assist the scum to rise
9. Remove every particle of scum whilst it is doing, and strain it through a fine hair sieve
10. When cool remove all grease
