## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 7 tablespoons vinegar
- 12 tablespoons cream
- small piece of butter
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Take 1 spoonful vinegar and rub the mustard well so as not to leave any lumps
2. Add all together and cook
3. When it begins to thicken it is cooked
4. Let cool
5. Chop your cabbage
6. Mix together
