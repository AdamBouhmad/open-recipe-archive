## Ingredients

- 1 soup bone
- 1 quart turtle beans
- 1 large spoonful of powdered cloves
- salt
- pepper
- nearly 6 quarts of water
- hard-boiled eggs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Soak the beans over night
2. Put them on with a soup bone in nearly 6 quarts of water and cook 5 or 6 hours
3. When half done, add the cloves, salt and pepper
4. When done, strain through a colander, pressing the pulp of the beans through to make the soup the desired thickness
5. Serve with a few slices of hard-boiled eggs and lemon sliced very thin
