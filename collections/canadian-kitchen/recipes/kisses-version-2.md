## Ingredients

- Whites of 3 eggs
- 1 pound icing sugar
- 1 pound grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat whites of 3 eggs until stiff
2. Add 1 pound icing sugar and 1 pound grated cocoanut
3. Beat all together until stiff
4. Drop in a buttered pan and cook until brown
