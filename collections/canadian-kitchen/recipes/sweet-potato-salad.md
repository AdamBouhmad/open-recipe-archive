## Ingredients

- Three large sweet potatoes
- Two stalks celery
- French dressing
- Olives
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes and cut into squares; add the celery cut small.
2. Mix, and pour over French dressing.
3. Garnish with olives and parsley.
