## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- butter
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut boiled potatoes in 1/4-inch slices
2. Put layer in baking dish
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and brown sugar
4. Dot with butter
5. Repeat layers
6. Cover with buttered crumbs
7. Bake until browned
