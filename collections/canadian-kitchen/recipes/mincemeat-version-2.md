## Ingredients

- 4 pounds beef tenderloin
- 2 pounds suet
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 3 pounds seeded raisins
- 3 pounds currants
- 1 ounce mace
- 1 ounce nutmegs
- 1 ounce cinnamon
- 1 ounce cloves
- 15 large apples
- 2 pounds citron
- grated rind and juice of 4 lemons
- grated rind and juice of 4 oranges
- 1 quart brandy
- 1 pint Madeira wine
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Boil the beef until well done, when cold chop it fine
2. Chop suet and apples and add to the beef
3. Mix the sugar and spices and add to them the wine, brandy, lemon and orange juice
4. Mix the raisins, currants, citron, lemon and orange rinds
5. Combine gradually the three sets of ingredients, after having added the salt to the liquid part, using a small portion of each until all are used
6. Pack in stone jars, cover closely, and keep in a dry, cool closet
