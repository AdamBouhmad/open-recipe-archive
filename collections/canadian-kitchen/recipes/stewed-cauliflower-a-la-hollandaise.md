## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- weak stock or bouillon
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- drawn-butter
- yolks of two raw eggs

## Directions

1. Cut into large clusters of uniform size and stew tender in weak stock or bouillon
2. Drain, butter, salt, and pepper, and pass with it drawn-butter, into which have been whipped the yolks of two raw eggs
