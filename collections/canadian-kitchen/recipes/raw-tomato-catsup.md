## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 1 cup salt
- 1 tablespoon pepper
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 quart good vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice tomatoes
2. Sprinkle with salt alternately
3. Let stand over night
4. Drain
5. Put through colander
6. Add pepper, brown sugar, and vinegar
7. Bottle for use
