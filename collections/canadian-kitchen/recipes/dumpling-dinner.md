## Ingredients

- broth from soup bone
- a dozen or more potatoes
- 1 pint water or milk
- a pinch of salt
- 1 egg
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Set about half the broth away in a cool place
2. Pare potatoes and drop in cold water, then in the boiling broth
3. Mix water or milk, salt, beaten egg, and baking powder
4. Sift in flour to make a very stiff batter
5. Drop from the spoon in round lumps into boiling broth
6. Let boil about fifteen minutes
7. Serve with some of the broth
