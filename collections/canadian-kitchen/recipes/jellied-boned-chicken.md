## Ingredients

- braised boned chicken
- gelatine
- cold water
- jelly
- truffles
- ham
- capers
- gherkins
- lettuce
- Mayonnaise sauce
- Bearnaise sauce
- Tartare sauce

## Directions

1. Strain the water in which the chicken was braised, and let it cool.
2. Remove the grease and clarify the liquor and season it highly.
3. If veal has been used and the liquor jellies, it may be used as it is.
4. If veal has not been used, add gelatine soaked in cold water, observing the proportion of one box of gelatine to one and a half quarts of liquor.
5. Mask a mould with jelly.
6. When the jelly is set, put in the chicken, and add enough liquid jelly to entirely cover it.
7. Alternatively, on the bottom of the mould, make a decoration of either truffles, ham, capers, gherkins, or any combination suitable.
8. Fix it with a thin layer of jelly.
9. When hardened, add enough more to make a layer of jelly one-quarter of an inch thick.
10. When that is hardened lay in the chicken, and surround it with the liquid jelly.
11. Garnish the dish on which the jellied chicken is served with lettuce, and serve with it a Mayonnaise, Bearnaise, or Tartare sauce.
