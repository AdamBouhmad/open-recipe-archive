## Ingredients

- 1 pk. spinach
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream
- 1 teaspoonful powdered sugar
- salt
- nutmeg
- black pepper
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil and chop spinach, and while hot stir in butter and beaten egg, salt, and nutmeg
2. Season with a little sugar and pepper, and set away to get cold
3. When you are ready for it, whip into the cold spinach the cream and the stiffened whites of the eggs
4. Pour into a handsome bake-dish, sift a small teaspoonful of powdered sugar on top, and bake in a hot oven ten minutes, covered, five minutes when you have uncovered it
5. Send immediately to table, as it soon falls
6. Each portion should be helped out upon a square of fried bread
