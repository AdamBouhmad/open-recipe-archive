## Ingredients

- rhubarb cut in pieces
- boiling water
- 4 pounds sugar to every gallon of wine
- 1 ounce whole ginger
- juice of 1 lemon
- 7 cayenne pods
- 1/2 ounce isinglass dissolved in a little hot water
- 1 gill of rum to every gallon of wine

## Directions

1. To every pint of rhubarb cut in pieces add 1 pint of boiling water
2. Let it stand a week, stirring it once every day
3. Strain and add 4 pounds sugar to every gallon of wine, 1 ounce whole ginger, the juice of 1 lemon, 7 cayenne pods, 1/2 ounce isinglass dissolved in a little hot water
4. Let it stand 2 weeks to ferment
5. Strain again and add 1 gill of rum to every gallon of wine
6. Bottle
