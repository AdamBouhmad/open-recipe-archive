## Ingredients

- juice and rind of 1 orange
- 1 cup sugar
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 cup milk
- pie paste

## Directions

1. Line pie tin with pie paste
2. Pour in mixture and bake till cooked
3. Save white of eggs for frosting
