## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 cake yeast
- 1 egg
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Add butter, sugar and salt to the scalded milk and water.
2. When lukewarm add the yeast, and when that is dissolved add the egg and flour and beat all well together.
3. Place the crock of this mixture in a warm room for over night.
4. The next day fill buttered rings two-thirds full of this batter, and set them aside till risen to the top of the rings.
5. Bake half an hour.
