## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of veal or beef chopped fine
- 5 tablespoonfuls of cracker-crumbs
- 1 tablespoonful of melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1/4 cup milk or cream
- 2 eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Form into a loaf and cover with cracker-crumbs
3. Put in the oven with a little water in the pan for two hours
4. Baste occasionally
