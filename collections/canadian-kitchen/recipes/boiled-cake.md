## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup water
- 1/4 cup lard
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon cloves
- salt
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Boil it and then cool
2. add 2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 3/4 teaspoon cloves, salt, 1 teaspoon soda
