## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes chopped fine
- 2 dozen large onions peeled and chopped
- large red pepper chopped fine
- salt
- pepper
- spice to suit taste
- 1 cup brown sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop green tomatoes, onions, and red pepper fine
2. Season with salt, pepper and spice to suit taste
3. Add 1 cup brown sugar
4. Pour boiling hot vinegar over all
5. Bottle for winter use
