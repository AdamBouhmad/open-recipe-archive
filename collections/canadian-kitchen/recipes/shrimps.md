## Ingredients

- 2 cups shrimps
- 2 cups white sauce
- 2 tablespoons mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon chopped celery
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- crackers

## Directions

1. Break shrimps once or twice
2. Make 2 cups white sauce
3. When cooked, add 2 tablespoons mushrooms chopped, 1 teaspoon chopped celery, 1 teaspoon onion juice, 1 teaspoon lemon juice
4. Heat shrimps in sauce
5. Serve on crackers
