## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons wine, or 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter
2. add sugar gradually
3. add egg well beaten and wine
4. beat while heating over hot water
