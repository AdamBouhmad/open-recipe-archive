## Ingredients

- 1 large cup new milk
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 teaspoonful arrowroot
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put the sugar into the milk, the salt into the boiling water, which should be poured into a farina-kettle
2. Add the arrowroot, make into paste with cold water, and boil, stirring constantly until it is clear
3. Put in the milk, and cook ten minutes, stirring often
4. Give while warm, adding hot milk should it be thicker than gruel
