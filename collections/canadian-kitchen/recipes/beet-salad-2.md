## Ingredients

- half a dozen young beets
- warm vinegar
- a little sugar
- a couple of bunches of chives
- three stalks of celery
- celery leaves
- salad dressing

## Directions

1. Boil half a dozen young beets, chop fine and pour over them warm vinegar with a little sugar in it.
2. Slice very thin a couple of bunches of chives, and chop fine three stalks of celery.
3. In serving make a foundation of celery leaves, and on it place first the thin slices of chives, then a layer of beets, then chives, and sprinkle over them the chopped celery.
4. Pour over all a salad dressing.
