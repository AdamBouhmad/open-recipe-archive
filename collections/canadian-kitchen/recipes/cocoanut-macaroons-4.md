## Ingredients

- Whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat stiff the whites of 3 eggs
2. Mix with 1 cup granulated sugar and 2 teaspoons cornstarch
3. Cook in a double boiler
4. Stir in cocoanut till very thick
5. Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla
6. Drop in spoonfuls on a pan
7. Bake in a moderate oven
