## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- Juice of half lemon
- 1 saltspoon salt
- A pinch cayenne pepper
- 1/2 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Stir butter to a cream
2. Add yolks of 2 eggs, 1 at a time, beat well
3. Stir in the juice of half lemon, salt and cayenne pepper
4. When ready to serve, add 1/2 cup boiling water
5. Place the bowl in a pan of boiling water and cook until thick, stirring all the while
