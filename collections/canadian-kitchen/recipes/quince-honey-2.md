## Ingredients

- 4 large quinces
- 2 large Spitzenburg apples grated
- 4 pounds white sugar
- 2 quarts water

## Directions

1. Grate the quinces and apples and put on to boil with the water for 15 minutes
2. Have your sugar warm and add
3. Let boil till thick
