## Ingredients

- 12 pounds green tomatoes
- 4 pounds sugar
- 3 pints vinegar
- 1 ounce cloves
- 1 ounce cinnamon
- 1 ounce ginger
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice tomatoes and sprinkle a little salt over them, let stand over night
2. Put all ingredients together except tomatoes, let come to a boil
3. Add tomatoes and boil until tender
