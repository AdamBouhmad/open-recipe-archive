## Ingredients

- 1 pound pork
- 1 pound lean veal
- 1 pound beef suet
- 7/8 pound bread crumbs
- 6 sage leaves
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 2 teaspoons salt
- thyme
- marjoram
- savory

## Directions

1. Chop pork, veal, and beef suet all very fine together
2. Mix with bread crumbs, 6 sage leaves, 1 teaspoon pepper and 2 of salt, thyme, marjoram and savory shred fine
