## Ingredients

- One new-laid egg
- One gill turpentine
- One gill vinegar
- One-half ounce spirits of camphor

## Directions

1. Beat egg well
2. Add turpentine and vinegar by degrees, alternately putting in one-half ounce spirits of camphor
3. For rheumatism, lumbago, for sore throat, cold in chest, etc., rub in well with hand, night and morning
4. A flannel may also be soaked in embrocation and put on, covered with a cloth or flannel
5. Can be used also as a substitute for mustard plaster
