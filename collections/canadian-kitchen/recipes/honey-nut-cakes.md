## Ingredients

- 8 cups sugar
- 2 cups honey
- 4 cups milk or water
- 1 pound almonds
- 1 pound English walnuts
- 3 cents' worth candied lemon peel
- 3 cents' worth candied orange peel
- 5 cents' worth citron
- 2 large tablespoons soda
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon

## Directions

1. Combine sugar, honey and milk or water
2. Cut candied lemon peel, orange peel and citron fine
3. Add almonds and walnuts
4. Add soda and cinnamon
5. Mix together
