## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup raspberries
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix 2 eggs, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 1 cup raspberries, 1 teaspoon soda, and 1 1/2 cups flour
2. Bake in layers
