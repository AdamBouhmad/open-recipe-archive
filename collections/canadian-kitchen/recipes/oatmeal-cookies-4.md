## Ingredients

- 3 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful soda dissolved in 1/4 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Bake in oven until a light brown.
