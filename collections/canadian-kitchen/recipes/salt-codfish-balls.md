## Ingredients

- six pared or quartered potatoes
- boiling water
- one cupful of picked codfish
- a beaten egg
- beef fat

## Directions

1. Cover six pared or quartered potatoes with boiling water
2. Put a cupful of picked codfish above the potatoes and cook until tender
3. Drain, mash and season to taste
4. Add a beaten egg and beat the mixture until light
5. Shape into smooth, light balls
6. Fry in beef fat, smoking hot
7. Drain carefully and serve at once
