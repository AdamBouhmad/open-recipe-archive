## Ingredients

- nice plump young chicken
- flour
- good butter
- half clove of garlic
- quarter pod green peppers
- strong chicken broth
- carrots
- turnips
- salt
- Saratoga chips
- French peas

## Directions

1. Secure a nice plump young chicken, clean and cut into pieces, not too small.
2. Flour them and saute in good butter until of a nice golden brown color.
3. Mince half clove of garlic very fine, also quarter pod green peppers and cook in butter for five minutes.
4. Mix sufficient flour to absorb butter, and moisten with half a pint of strong chicken broth.
5. Stew with a few carrots and turnips cut into small diamond shapes until tender.
6. Season with salt, and serve on platter with Saratoga chip border sprinkled with French peas.
