## Ingredients

- One can of lobster
- One head of lettuce
- 1/2 cup of dressing

## Directions

1. Cut lobster quite fine (not chop)
2. Break the leaves from a head of lettuce, let stand in a pan of ice cold water till crisp
3. Lay the leaves on a board and shave fine
4. Just before serving mix the lettuce, lobster and half a cup of dressing together lightly with a fork
5. Arrange some lettuce leaves on a dish and put the mixture in the centre
