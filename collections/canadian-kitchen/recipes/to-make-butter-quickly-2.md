## Ingredients

- milk
- cream
- wooden spoon
- cold water
- fine salt
- fine white sugar

## Directions

1. Immediately after the cow is milked, strain milk into clean pans, and set it over a moderate fire until it is scalding hot; do not let it boil
2. Set it aside; when it is cold, skim off the cream; the milk will still be fit for any ordinary use
3. When you have enough cream, put it into a clean earthen basin
4. Beat it with a wooden spoon until the butter is made, which will not be long
5. Take it from the milk and work it with a little cold water, until it is free from milk
6. Drain off the water, put a small tablespoonful of fine salt to each pound of butter, and work it in
7. Work in a small teaspoonful of fine white sugar with the salt
8. Make the butter in a roll; cover it with a bit of muslin, and keep it in a cool place
