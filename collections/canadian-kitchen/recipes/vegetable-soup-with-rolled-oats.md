## Ingredients

- 4 large potatoes
- 3 carrots
- 1 good-sized turnip or swede
- 6 onions
- Water
- Pepper and salt
- 1 cup rolled oats
- A lump of butter
- 1 cup cream
- A little flour

## Directions

1. Slice all vegetables, cover with water, with pepper and salt
2. Put on to boil
3. When nearly cooked add a cup of rolled oats, with a lump of butter
4. Add a cup of cream
5. Thicken with a little flour
