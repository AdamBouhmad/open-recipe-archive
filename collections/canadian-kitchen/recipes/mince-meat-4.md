## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. raw beef, chopped
- 6 lbs. suet, chopped
- 1 pk. sour apples
- 6 lbs. brown sugar
- 4 lbs. seeded raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1/2 lb. citron
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 6 lemons, juice
- Pulp and peel of 2 lemons
- Spices to taste

## Directions

1. Can be kept a year
