## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup nuts (walnuts preferred)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and cream the sugar with it. Add the beaten egg. Mix baking powder and flour and sift. Lastly, mix in chopped nuts. Drop a very small quantity on pan and bake in a moderate oven.
