## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 cups hot milk
- 1/2 cup chopped almonds
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- juice and rind of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Soak crumbs in milk
2. Beat yolks with sugar
3. Add to milk
4. Add nuts and lemon
5. Fold in beaten whites
6. Bake in a small mould 20 minutes
7. Serve with orange sauce
