## Ingredients

- chicken
- 2 medium-sized potatoes
- 2 large onions
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken until tender, having first put in two medium-sized potatoes and 2 large onions sliced very thin, pepper and salt, or any seasoning one may wish
2. Thicken gravy nicely with little flour and water, having enough to cover chicken
3. Pour all into granite pan until crust is prepared
4. Prepare crust for top: Same as for pie crust, but should be at least half an inch thick, and not too rich
5. Bake in quick oven from 20 minutes to 1/2 an hour
