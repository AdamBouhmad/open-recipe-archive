## Ingredients

- 2 cups white flour
- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1 egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- buttermilk to make a stiff batter, or about 1 1/2 cups
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- a pinch salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. When well mixed, set on the back of the stove for twenty minutes
3. Bake for about half an hour
