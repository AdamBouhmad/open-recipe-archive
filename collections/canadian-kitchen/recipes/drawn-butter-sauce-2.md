## Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons of butter
- 2 teaspoons flour
- boiling water
- 1 chopped hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Let butter get soft
2. Mix with flour
3. Pour on boiling water until it is the right consistency
4. Do not boil it
5. Add a chopped hard-boiled egg
