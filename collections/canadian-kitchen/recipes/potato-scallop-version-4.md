## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- cream sauce
- parsley
- bread crusts
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut cooked potatoes in square or thin slices
2. Put into dish a layer of cream sauce, then potatoes, alternately, ending with a layer of sauce
3. Sprinkle over top a little chipped parsley, then cup buttered crumbs
4. Dry and roll crusts till fine
5. For 1 cup crumbs use 2 tablespoons butter, teaspoon salt and a little pepper
6. Bake about 30 or 40 minutes
