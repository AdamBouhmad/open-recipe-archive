## Ingredients

- steak, cut 1 inch thick
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Have steak cut 1 inch thick
2. Cut off the tail-ends of 2 medium-sized steaks
3. Trim off all excess fat, leaving the portions containing the T bone for broiling
4. Place on broiler and broil over hot coals for about 7 minutes
5. Turn and broil the other side
6. If no broiler is at hand, heat thick frying pan very hot, smear with some of the fat and put steak in, turning the same as when broiler is used
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. When cooked put on hot platter, putting bits of butter over meat
9. Add a very little water to pan, and put in platter, but do not pour it over meat as that toughens it
10. Serve at once
