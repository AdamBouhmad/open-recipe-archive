## Ingredients

- 30 large cucumbers, firm part only
- 15 onions
- 2 large heads of cabbage
- salt water
- 1 gallon white wine vinegar
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1/2 pound mustard
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1/4 cup white pepper
- 1/4 cup cinnamon
- 3/4 cup tumeric

## Directions

1. Chop cucumbers, onions, and cabbage as fine as mincemeat
2. Put into salt water over night
3. In the morning drain off the water
4. Put the mixture on the stove with vinegar, sugar, mustard, celery seed, white pepper, cinnamon, and tumeric
5. Bring to a boil
6. Pack in jars
