## Ingredients

- 12 ripe tomatoes
- 12 sour apples
- 12 large onions
- 1 quart good vinegar
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- salt
- 1 ten cent can of mustard

## Directions

1. Bring tomatoes to a boil and then put through a sieve to take out seeds
2. Boil apples and onions till soft and put through sieve
3. Add all together
4. Add 1 quart good vinegar, 2 cups sugar, 1 teaspoon pepper, salt to taste, and 1 ten cent can of mustard
5. Dissolve mustard in a little cold vinegar before adding to the mixture
6. Boil till real thick, then bottle
