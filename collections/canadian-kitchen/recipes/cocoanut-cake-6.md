## Ingredients

- 3 eggs (the whites of 2 to be used for frosting)
- ¾ cup sugar
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 1⅜ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- ½ teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Bake in layers
2. Beat the whites of 2 eggs well with 4 dessertspoons sugar
3. Spread frosting on cake and sprinkle with cocoanut
