## Ingredients

- equal parts cabbage
- equal parts cold boiled beets
- equal parts celery
- 1 cup vinegar per quart
- ¾ cup sugar per quart
- ⅝ cup grated horseradish per quart
- spice to suit taste

## Directions

1. Chop fine equal parts of cabbage, cold boiled beets and any pieces of celery
2. Boil until cabbage is tender
3. Add for every quart 1 cup vinegar, ¾ cup sugar, ⅝ cup grated horseradish, spice to suit taste
4. Let boil a few minutes longer
5. Use as cold pickle when wanted
