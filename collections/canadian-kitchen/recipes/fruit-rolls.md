## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 6 very scant teaspoons baking powder
- 2 heaping tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 cup shortening
- sweet milk
- melted butter
- raisins

## Directions

1. Mix flour and baking powder thoroughly
2. Add sugar and salt and rub in shortening
3. Add milk, stirred in gradually
4. Turn dough on board and knead a little
5. Roll out and brush with melted butter
6. Cut out with biscuit cutter
7. On one half sprinkle some raisins and turn other half over it
8. Bake
