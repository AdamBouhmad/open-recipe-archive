## Ingredients

- 8 pounds pears
- 8 pounds sugar
- 4 lemons
- 1/2 pound crystallized ginger
- water

## Directions

1. Boil lemons whole in water until soft
2. Remove and chop fine, taking out seeds
3. Peel fruit and slice thinly
4. Put all in kettle, using water lemons were boiled in
5. Simmer gently until cooked, stirring almost constantly
