## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup walnut meats

## Directions

1. Cream 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup butter together.
2. Add yolks of 2 eggs and beat till well mixed.
3. Add 1/2 cup milk.
4. Sift in 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder.
5. When well mixed, beat with an even stroke so that air will get into it to make it light.
6. Add beaten whites of 2 eggs, 1/2 cup raisins, and 1/2 cup walnut meats.
7. Bake in bread tin.
