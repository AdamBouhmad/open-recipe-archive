## Ingredients

- 2 ounces coffee
- 1 pint water
- 1 raw egg
- 4 tablespoons cold water

## Directions

1. Beat dry coffee into 1 raw egg mixed with 4 tablespoons of cold water
2. Add boiling water
3. Set coffee-pot on trivet in kettle of boiling water
4. Steam 20 minutes
