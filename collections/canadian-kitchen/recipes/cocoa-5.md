## Ingredients

- 2 level teaspoons cocoa
- 2 level teaspoons sugar
- water
- 0.5 cup milk

## Directions

1. Stir cocoa and sugar together
2. Add water drop by drop, stirring constantly until a smooth, even paste has been secured
3. Place 0.5 cup milk over fire and allow it to heat until it begins to simmer around the sides of the pan
4. Add the cocoa and allow it to come to the boiling point
5. Remove from the fire and beat vigorously for 2 or 3 minutes
