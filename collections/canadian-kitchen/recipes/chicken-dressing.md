## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon summer savory
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix butter well through bread crumbs
2. Add 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon pepper, 1 tablespoon summer savory
3. Mix all together with 2 eggs
4. Put in chicken and roast
