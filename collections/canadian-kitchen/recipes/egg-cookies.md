## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- just enough flour to roll

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, 3 eggs together
2. Sift 2 teaspoons baking powder and 1 teaspoon cream tartar with just enough flour to roll
3. Bake in a moderate oven
