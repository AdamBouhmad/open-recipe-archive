## Ingredients

- whites of 11 eggs, beaten stiff
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar, sifted once
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cream tartar
- lemon flavoring
- vanilla flavoring
- pineapple flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat whites of 11 eggs stiff on a platter
2. Add 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar, sifted once
3. Take 1 cup flour with 1/2 teaspoon cream tartar and sift 4 times
4. Gradually add to the cake
5. Mix lemon, vanilla and pineapple flavoring in equal quantities and add 1/2 teaspoon of mixture
6. Use a pan with tube up the centre and do not grease it
7. Bake 40 minutes
8. When baked, invert the pan on 2 cups and let it stand until cool
