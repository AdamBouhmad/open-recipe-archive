## Ingredients

- 1 cup beans
- 2 eggs
- flour
- salt
- milk
- biscuits

## Directions

1. Wash a cup of beans, soak in lukewarm water all night
2. Drain water off in morning, boil until tender
3. Take 2 eggs, beat, add flour to make a thin paste
4. Drop from spoon, let come to a boil
5. Then add salt, milk and biscuits to taste
