## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar (scant)
- 3/4 cup lard (scant)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup rich milk (cream is best)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup of any desired fruit (apples are nice)
- enough flour to make a nice batter

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar, lard, and salt thoroughly
2. Add milk, baking powder, fruit, and enough flour to make a nice batter
