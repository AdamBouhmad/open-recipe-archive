## Ingredients

- 3 cups brown sugar
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
- pinch salt

## Directions

1. Boil gently without stirring until it reaches the soft ball stage in cold water
2. When nearly done add butter
3. When done set away for 10 minutes
4. Beat until creamy
5. Just before cold pour into a buttered dish
