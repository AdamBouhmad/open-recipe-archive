## Ingredients

- 5 cups stock
- 3/4 cup rice
- 1 1/2 cups strained tomatoes
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 slices onion
- 1 ounce peppercorns
- 3/4 teaspoon celery salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Add seasonings to tomatoes, cover, simmer 30 minutes
2. Rub through sieve into hot stock
3. Brush with butter and flour
4. Add cooked rice
