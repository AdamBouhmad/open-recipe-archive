## Ingredients

- Frogs' legs
- Milk
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Hot butter
- Hot water
- Cream
- Butter
- Flour
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Skin, lay in milk for fifteen minutes
2. Roll in peppered and salted flour, and saute in hot butter for three minutes
3. Cover (barely) with hot water, and stew tender
4. Twenty minutes should suffice
5. Heat half a cupful of cream to boiling, stir in a tablespoonful of butter rolled in flour, boil up
6. Turn into the saucepan where the frogs' legs are simmering
7. Season with pepper, salt, and a little chopped parsley
8. Cook gently for three minutes and serve
