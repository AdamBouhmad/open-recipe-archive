## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- just enough water to moisten well
- white of 1 egg beaten

## Directions

1. Boil 1 cup white sugar with just enough water to moisten well, until it hairs from the spoon
2. Pour into the well-beaten white of 1 egg
3. Beat until cool or stiff enough to spread
