## Ingredients

- Three eggs
- Three ounces sugar
- Half pint of whipped cream
- Half glass of chartreuse

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar over boiling water for ten minutes
2. Remove and whip until cold
3. Add cream and chartreuse
