## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful cream tartar
- A pinch of salt
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoonful soda dissolved in tablespoonfuls cold water
- Juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix together flour, sugar, cream tartar, and a little salt.
2. Break eggs and beat thoroughly; soda dissolved in cold water, juice of lemon.
3. Bake in moderate oven.
