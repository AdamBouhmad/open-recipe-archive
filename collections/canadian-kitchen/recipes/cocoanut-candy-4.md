## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- shredded cocoanut
- 3 squares chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water to a syrup, or till it forms when dropped in water
2. Mix in cocoanut and chocolate and beat till it gets thick and creamy
3. Add vanilla
