## Ingredients

- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1½ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- ⅜ cup molasses
- ¼ teaspoon each of cinnamon, allspice and nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Just before final mixing break 1 egg in the mixture and stir whole together
2. Drop in buttered tins
