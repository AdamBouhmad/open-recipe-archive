## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups chopped raisins
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix butter soft and bake carefully
