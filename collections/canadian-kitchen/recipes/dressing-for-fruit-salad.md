## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- Juice 2 lemons
- wine

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water five minutes
2. Pour on to the beaten yolks of eggs
3. Return to the fire and cook over hot water, stirring constantly until thickened
4. Cool and add juice of two lemons
5. One-half cup wine may be used in place of the lemon-juice, retaining one tablespoonful of lemon-juice
6. Pour over the fruit salad and serve after being thoroughly chilled
