## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 1 pint cream
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Put oysters in double boiler and cook till milk juice begins to flow out
2. Drain the oysters in a colander
3. Put the cream in the double boiler
4. When it comes to a boil, thicken with flour, wet with milk to the consistency of cornstarch, ready to mould
5. Put in oysters and cook five minutes
