## Ingredients

- 13.5 cups brown sugar
- butter size of walnut
- 3 tablespoons sweet cream
- 5 cent cake of chocolate grated or about 1/2 cup cocoa

## Directions

1. Boil till it threads
2. Beat in a 5 cent cake of chocolate that has been grated, or about 1/2 cup cocoa
