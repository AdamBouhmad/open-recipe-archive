## Ingredients

- 1 can of salmon
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 5 soda crackers rolled
- butter the size of an egg melted
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Carefully remove all bones and skin from salmon
2. Add beaten eggs, rolled crackers, melted butter, salt and pepper
3. Put in a well-greased mould
4. Steam for 2 hours
5. Serve with drawn butter
