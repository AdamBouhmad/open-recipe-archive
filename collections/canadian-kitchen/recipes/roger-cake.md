## Ingredients

- ⅜ cup syrup
- ⅜ cup brown sugar
- ⅜ cup sour milk
- yolk of 1 egg
- butter size of egg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- pinch salt
- flour to thicken (not too thick)
- 1 cup white sugar
- 5 teaspoons water
- 1 cup raisins stoned and chopped fine
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix syrup, brown sugar, sour milk, egg yolk, butter, soda, salt and flour
2. For icing: boil water and sugar until it strings
3. Add 1 cup raisins stoned and chopped fine, and cook a few minutes
4. Cool a little and add the stiff white of the egg
5. Put between layers and on top
