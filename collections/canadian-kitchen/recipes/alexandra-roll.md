## Ingredients

- Butter
- Cold game or chicken, pounded
- Fresh butter, about a third of its bulk of the chicken
- A few capers
- A washed and boned anchovy for every ounce or so of chicken
- Cayenne pepper
- Thin brown bread
- Finely minced parsley
- Lobster coral or coralline pepper

## Directions

1. Butter sparingly some thin brown bread.
2. Have ready some cold game or chicken pounded, about a third of its bulk of fresh butter, a few capers, a washed and boned anchovy for every ounce or so of chicken, and a good seasoning of cayenne pepper.
3. Spread the bread and butter with this mixture, roll up cigar fashion.
4. Butter the top very lightly and roll half of these (cigars) in finely minced parsley and the other half in lobster coral or coralline pepper.
