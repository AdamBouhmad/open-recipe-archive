## Ingredients

- hot sliced potatoes
- butter
- salt
- cream
- cayenne pepper
- egg yolks
- creamed peas

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups hot sliced potatoes, 2 tablespoons butter, 3/8 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons cream, a little cayenne pepper, yolks of 2 eggs together
2. Shape in small nests
3. Fill with creamed peas
4. Cover and roll into croquette shapes
5. Dip in egg and crumbs
6. Fry in deep fat
7. Drain
