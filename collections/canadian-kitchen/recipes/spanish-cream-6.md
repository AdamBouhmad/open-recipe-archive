## Ingredients

- 3 pts. milk
- 1 box gelatine
- 8 tablespoonful sugar
- Whites 6 eggs beaten stiff
- Yolks 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in half of the milk for an hour.
2. Scald the rest of the milk, then stir in the gelatine.
3. Add the yolks of beaten eggs and eight tablespoons of sugar.
4. Take from the fire and allow to cool, when put in the beaten whites and turn into a mould.
