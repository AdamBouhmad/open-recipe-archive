## Ingredients

- 12 tomatoes
- 12 apples
- 8 large onions
- 2 pounds raisins
- 3/4 gallon vinegar
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 3/4 pound mustard
- 2 teaspoons allspice
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 2 teaspoons cloves
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons tumeric
- 1 cup salt

## Directions

1. Chop fine
2. Boil 1 hour
3. Rub through colander
4. Add vinegar, brown sugar, mustard, allspice, ginger, cloves, cinnamon, tumeric, and salt
5. Boil 3/4 hour and stir occasionally
