## Ingredients

- four sweet oranges
- three pints of cold water
- half a pound of loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Peel oranges, being careful not to get any of the white skin in
2. Put the yellow skin in cold water and loaf sugar
3. Cook together into a syrup
