## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- 1 cup stale bread crumbs
- 1 can salmon
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a white sauce of milk, flour and butter with salt, pepper and onion juice
2. Cook the sauce
3. Add stale bread crumbs
4. Add salmon picked free from bones and rubbed smooth with a silver fork
5. Add beaten yolks of eggs and beat all
6. Fold in whites beaten very stiff
7. Turn into buttered dish
8. Bake in pan standing in water in oven for 25 minutes
