## Ingredients

- 1/4 chicken, minced
- 2 oz. ham
- 6 mushrooms, minced
- A gill cream
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Truffle

## Directions

1. Make some caisses of puff paste, or line little moulds with the paste.
2. Put into a stewpan white sauce, add the chicken, ham, etc., when it is hot add the cream and lastly stir in the yolks of eggs.
3. Fill the caisses and serve.
