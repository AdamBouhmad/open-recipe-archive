## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. shin of beef or other meat and bones
- 4 carrots
- 1 turnip
- 4 onions
- 1 small head of celery
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 4 teaspoonful of peppercorns
- 6 cloves
- 5 pints cold water

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat and bones and place in the stock pot
2. Pour over the water and skim when boiling
3. Prepare the vegetables and add
4. Cover closely and simmer four hours
5. The spices should be added with the vegetables
