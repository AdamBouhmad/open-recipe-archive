## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- salt

## Directions

1. Take off outside leaves
2. Cut in quarters
3. Remove tough stalk
4. Soak in cold salt water
5. Cook in uncovered vessel in boiling water
6. Change water every 10 minutes
7. Drain
