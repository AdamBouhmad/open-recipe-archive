## Ingredients

- 8 cups of vegetables, peas, beans or corn
- 3/4 cup salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 cups water

## Directions

1. Boil for 20 minutes
2. Then fill jars and seal
3. On opening jar put on in cold water and let them come to a boil
4. Then drain it off
