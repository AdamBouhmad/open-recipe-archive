## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 4 cups scalded milk
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 3/4 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Clean oysters by placing in colander and pouring over them 3/4 cup cold water
2. Carefully pick over oysters and remove liquor
3. Heat liquor to boiling point and strain through double cheesecloth
4. Add oysters and cook till they are plump and edges begin to curl
5. Remove oysters with skimmer and put in tureen with butter, salt and pepper
6. Add oyster liquor and milk
