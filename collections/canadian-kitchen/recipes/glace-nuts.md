## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3/4 cup white wine vinegar
- nuts

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water without stirring until thick
2. Add vinegar until, when dropped in cold water, it cracks
3. Set the saucepan on warm place, but not where it can boil
4. Stick nuts on ends of hat pins and, taking 2 at a time, dip into the taffy
5. Hold in cool place until hard enough to handle them, then drop on tin plate
