## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 small teaspoons soda
- 1 saltspoon salt

## Directions

1. Steam 2 1/2 hours.
