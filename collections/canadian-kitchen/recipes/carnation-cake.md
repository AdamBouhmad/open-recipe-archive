## Ingredients

- Yolk of 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 3/4 cup chopped almonds
- 8 dessertspoons icing sugar

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks, sugar, and butter
2. Add flour, raisins, sour milk, soda, nutmeg, and allspice
3. Bake in layers
4. For icing: Mix sweet cream, chopped almonds, and icing sugar
5. Spread between layers
