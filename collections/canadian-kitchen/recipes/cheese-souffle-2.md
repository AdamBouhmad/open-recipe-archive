## Ingredients

- 1 large cup milk
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- salt and cayenne
- 4 tablespoonfuls grated cheese
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Set milk and breadcrumbs over boiling water, stir till smooth.
2. Take from fire and add butter, grated cheese, salt, cayenne, yolks eggs beaten well.
3. Fold in the whites of eggs well beaten.
4. Bake in buttered dish for twenty minutes in a pan of hot water.
5. Serve at once.
