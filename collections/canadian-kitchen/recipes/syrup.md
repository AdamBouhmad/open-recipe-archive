## Ingredients

- 2 quarts potatoes
- 1 quart boiling water
- 3 pounds white sugar
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 1/2 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes with skins on and drain
2. Add 1 quart boiling water, 3 pounds white sugar, 2 pounds brown sugar, 3/4 cup sweet milk, 1/2 teaspoon soda
3. Boil 1 hour
