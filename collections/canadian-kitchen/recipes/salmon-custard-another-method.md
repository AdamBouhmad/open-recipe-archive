## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- salt and pepper to taste
- rolled soda biscuit crumbs
- 1 well-beaten egg
- 1 3/2 cups milk
- bits of butter

## Directions

1. Take a deep bake dish
2. Put alternate layers of salmon and rolled soda biscuit crumbs
3. Pour milk and egg mixture over salmon
4. Put bits of butter on top of the dish
5. Bake until custard is set
6. Let get thoroughly cold and slice
