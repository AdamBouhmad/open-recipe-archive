## Ingredients

- 1 lb. suet
- 1 lb. moist sugar
- 1 lb. currants
- 1 lb. raisins
- 1 lb. sultana raisins
- 1 lb. mixed candied peel
- 1/2 lb. breadcrumbs
- 1/2 lb. flour
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful mixed spice
- 8 eggs
- 1/2 pt. brandy

## Directions

1. Mix all in the following order. Flour, salt, spice, sugar, raisins, peel, breadcrumbs, sultanas, and currants
2. Beat egg and strain them for ten minutes, add brandy to them and pour over the mass, stir until all are thoroughly mixed
3. Butter four small bowls and fill, scald cloth and flour it, tie down and boil five and one-half hours (or more), if one large pudding boil thirteen hours
