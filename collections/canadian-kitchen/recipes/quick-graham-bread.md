## Ingredients

- One pint sour milk
- One teaspoonful of soda
- Half a cup of molasses
- Half teaspoon salt
- Graham flour to make a stiff dough
- A little shortening

## Directions

1. Stir in graham flour to make a stiff dough
2. Bake in a quick oven
