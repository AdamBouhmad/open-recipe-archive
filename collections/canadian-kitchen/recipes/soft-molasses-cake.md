## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 1 cup molasses
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 egg
- pinch salt
- juice of 1 lemon and grated rind

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup warm water, 1 cup molasses, 2 1/2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon soda, 1 egg, pinch salt, and juice of 1 lemon and grated rind
2. Bake in a loaf
3. The lemon may be omitted and a good tablespoon vinegar used instead
