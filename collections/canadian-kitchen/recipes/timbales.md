## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg, well beaten
- lard for frying
- creamed chicken
- parsley or cress

## Directions

1. Beat all together; let stand one hour.
2. Have ready a saucepan of boiling lard.
3. Put timbale-iron first in lard, then in batter and then in lard again for one-half minute, when the shells will fall off the iron readily.
4. Fill with creamed chicken and garnish with parsley or cress after heating a few moments in the oven.
5. The shells can be made the day before if desired and heated when required.
