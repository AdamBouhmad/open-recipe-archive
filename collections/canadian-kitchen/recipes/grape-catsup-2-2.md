## Ingredients

- 5 pounds grapes
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Boil 5 pounds grapes to a pulp
2. Strain
3. Add 2 pounds sugar, 1 pint vinegar, 1 tablespoon each cinnamon, cloves, allspice, and 1 teaspoon pepper
4. Boil until thick enough for catsup
