## Ingredients

- One cupful of tomato sauce
- One can of shrimps
- Salt to taste
- one saltspoonful of cayenne

## Directions

1. Stir the shrimps into the tomato sauce, bring to a boil, season, and serve on toast or in scallop-shells, or nappies
