## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 tablespoon lard
- 1 cup sour cream
- flour
- currants

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks of 3 eggs separately
2. Mix sugar, cream tartar, and baking soda
3. Add lard and sour cream
4. Beat to a stiff batter with flour
5. Put into well-greased tart tins
6. Shake a few currants on top of each
