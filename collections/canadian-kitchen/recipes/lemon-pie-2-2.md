## Ingredients

- pie crust
- yolk of 1 egg
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- 2 heaping teaspoons cornstarch
- 1 cup boiling water
- white of 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- lemon drops

## Directions

1. Line a pie plate with crust and bake and set aside to cool
2. Mix yolk of egg and 1 cup granulated sugar together
3. Mix juice of lemon and 2 heaping teaspoons cornstarch
4. Mix all together and stir into 1 cup boiling water
5. Cook until thick and pour into pie shell
6. Frost with the white of egg beaten to a stiff froth with a teaspoon sugar and a few drops lemon
7. Set in oven until top is browned
