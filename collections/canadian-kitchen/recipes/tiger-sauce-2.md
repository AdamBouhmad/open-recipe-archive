## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound raisins chopped fine
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1/2 pound salt
- 1 tablespoon ground red pepper
- 1 1/4 ounces ground mustard
- 7/8 ounce turmeric
- 2 cups onions chopped
- 13 large sour apples
- 12 large ripe tomatoes
- 3 pints white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel apples and boil in 1 pint vinegar
2. Peel and boil tomatoes in another pint of vinegar
3. Put apples and tomatoes through a colander
4. When cool mix with 3/4 pound raisins chopped fine, 1 pound brown sugar, 1/2 pound salt, and 2 cups onions chopped
5. Mix mustard, turmeric and pepper with a little vinegar and add
6. Seal in jars
