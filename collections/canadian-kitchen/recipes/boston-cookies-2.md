## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- 3 eggs well beaten
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in 1½ tablespoons hot water
- 3¾ cups flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Cream butter
2. Add gradually sugar and eggs well beaten
3. Add soda dissolved in hot water
4. Sift together flour, salt, and cinnamon
5. Add ¾ of flour mixture to butter mixture
6. Add chopped walnuts and chopped raisins
7. Put in the rest of flour and stir well
8. Drop by teaspoons 1 inch apart on buttered pans
9. Bake in moderate oven
