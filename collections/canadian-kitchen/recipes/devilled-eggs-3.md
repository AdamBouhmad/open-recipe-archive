## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled eggs in halves.
2. Remove yolks and mix to a soft paste with mayonnaise dressing.
3. Replace in whites and serve on lettuce leaves.
