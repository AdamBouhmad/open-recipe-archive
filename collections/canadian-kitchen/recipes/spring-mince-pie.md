## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups chopped raisins
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup warm water
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 5 crackers
- spices to taste
- rich crust

## Directions

1. Pound crackers fine
2. Stir all together and season with spices as other mince pies
3. Bake with rich crust
4. For the top crust roll, then cut in narrow strips and twist and lay across
5. This will make 2 pies
