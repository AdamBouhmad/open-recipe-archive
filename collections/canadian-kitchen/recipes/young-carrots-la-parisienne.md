## Ingredients

- young carrots
- butter
- parsley
- white sugar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil for five minutes
2. Take up and rub off the skins with a coarse cloth
3. Return to the fire and cook until tender
4. Slice lengthwise, making three pieces of a medium-sized carrot, two of a small
5. Have hot in a frying-pan a tablespoonful of butter for each cupful of the carrots
6. When it bubbles lay in the slices
7. Sauté on both sides, quickly
8. Just before taking them up sprinkle with chopped parsley
9. Dish dry
10. Stew over them a little white sugar, pepper, and salt
11. Serve very hot
