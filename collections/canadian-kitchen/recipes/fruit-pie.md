## Ingredients

- pie crust
- chopped sour apples
- chopped raisins
- sugar
- butter
- cloves
- nutmeg
- 1 pound of raisins
- 10 or 12 good-sized apples
- 2 large cups sugar

## Directions

1. Make a nice pie crust and put a layer of it in the bottom of a 2-quart tin basin
2. Add a layer of chopped sour apples 2 inches thick
3. Add a layer of chopped raisins and sprinkle sugar over this
4. Add pieces of butter and sprinkle cloves and nutmeg
5. Alternate layers of crust and fruit until dish is full
6. Put a crust on the top
7. Bake slowly for 2 hours
8. When done turn bottom upwards on a plate
9. Before putting it on the table sprinkle fine sugar over it
