## Ingredients

- 4 quarts cabbage chopped fine
- 4 quarts green tomatoes seeded and chopped
- 10 onions chopped fine
- 1 teacup salt
- 4 green peppers
- 2 pounds sugar
- 3/4 ounce turmeric powder
- 3/4 ounce celery seed
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix cabbage, green tomatoes, onions, and salt
2. Let stand 3/4 hour
3. Drain well
4. Put in a kettle with 4 green peppers, 2 pounds sugar, 3/4 ounce turmeric powder, and 3/4 ounce celery seed
5. Cover with vinegar and boil 1 hour
