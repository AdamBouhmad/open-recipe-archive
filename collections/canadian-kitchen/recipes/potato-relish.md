## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cold boiled potatoes
- salt and pepper
- 4 tablespoons sweet cream
- ¾ cup chopped pickled beets
- 1 hard-boiled egg chopped

## Directions

1. Season potatoes with salt and pepper
2. Mix all together
3. Garnish with slices of hard-boiled eggs
