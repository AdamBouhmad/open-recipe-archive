## Ingredients

- left-over mashed potatoes
- egg
- bread crumbs
- cream
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Take left-over mashed potatoes, 1 egg, 1 cup bread crumbs, 1/2 cup cream, 1 onion chopped and season with pepper, salt and sage
2. Shape into a loaf and put where cool
3. Cut in slices, dip in flour and fry in a little butter
