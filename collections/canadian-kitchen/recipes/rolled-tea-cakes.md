## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with flour
2. Add butter, eggs, cream tartar, and baking soda
3. Mix well
4. Roll thin
5. Bake
