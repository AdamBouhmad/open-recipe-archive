## Ingredients

- 3 carrots
- 1 pint of water
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 quart milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Grate 3 carrots, cover with a pint of water and add a slice of onion and a bay leaf
2. Simmer gently for 30 minutes
3. Remove onion and bay leaf and add 1 quart milk
4. Moisten 1 tablespoon cornstarch in a little cold water and add it to soup
5. Stir till smooth, add salt and pepper
6. Just at serving time add 2 tablespoons butter
