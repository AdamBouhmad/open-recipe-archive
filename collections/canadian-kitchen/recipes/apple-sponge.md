## Ingredients

- 1 lb. apples
- 0.5 oz. gelatine
- 1 lb. sugar
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Rind of 1 lemon
- 0.25 pt. boiling water
- Whites of three eggs
- Custard

## Directions

1. Boil the water and sugar in a saucepan until dissolved
2. Peel and slice the apples thinly, and add to the syrup
3. Stew until tender
4. Add gelatine, and strain all through a sieve
5. Add lemon juice and rind and beat until cool
6. Beat the whites of three eggs stiffly and add to the mixture
7. Beat all until cold
8. Put into a mould
9. Serve with custard
