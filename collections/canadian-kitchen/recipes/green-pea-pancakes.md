## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of green peas
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 1/2 cupful of flour

## Directions

1. Boil the peas expressly for this dish, mashed while hot, and rubbed through a colander.
2. Season with pepper, salt, and butter to taste; let them get cold; then add two beaten eggs and a cupful of milk.
3. Sift half a teaspoonful of baking powder twice through half a cupful of flour, and beat in lightly at the last.
4. Mix well and bake as you would griddle-cakes.
5. Eat hot.
