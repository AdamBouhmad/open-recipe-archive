## Ingredients

- ½ pint cream, or rich milk
- ⅛ pint vinegar
- 1 small cup sugar
- 3 eggs well beaten
- lump of butter size of egg
- 1 teaspoon ground mustard
- 1 teaspoon pepper and salt

## Directions

1. mix all together cold and cook until thick
