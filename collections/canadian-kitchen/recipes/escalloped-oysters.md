## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 4 tablespoons oyster liquor
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 1 beaten egg
- 1/2 cup stale crumbs
- 1/8 cup cracker crumbs
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix crumbs in butter
2. Arrange alternate layers in buttered baking dish of crumbs, oyster seasonings, wetting
3. Bake 35 minutes
