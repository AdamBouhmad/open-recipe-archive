## Ingredients

- one gill of boiling water
- one tablespoonful of Indian meal
- one teaspoonful of mustard flour
- muslin

## Directions

1. Into one gill of boiling water stir one tablespoonful of Indian meal
2. Spread the paste thus made upon a cloth, and spread over the paste one teaspoonful of mustard flour
3. If you wish a mild poultice, use a teaspoonful of mustard as it is prepared for the table, instead of the mustard flour
4. Equal parts of ground mustard and flour made into a paste with warm water, and spread between two pieces of muslin, form the indispensable mustard plaster
