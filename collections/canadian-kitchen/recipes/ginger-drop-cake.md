## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 rounding teaspoon soda
- flour enough to make a thick batter

## Directions

1. Mix shortening, sour milk, brown sugar, molasses, eggs, ginger and soda with flour to make a thick batter
2. Drop from a spoon in drops as large as an egg in a bread pan, far enough apart not to touch
3. Bake and eat warm
