## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 1/2 teacupfuls flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoonful baking powder
- 1/2 cup milk (large)
- 1 cup jam
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Butter a mould, cover the bottom with jam, fill with batter, and steam one hour and a quarter.
2. Serve with sauce.
