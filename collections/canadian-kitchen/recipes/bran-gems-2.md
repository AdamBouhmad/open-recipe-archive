## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups bran
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. beat butter, sugar, and eggs together
2. add the sour milk and bran
3. add the soda sifted in the flour
