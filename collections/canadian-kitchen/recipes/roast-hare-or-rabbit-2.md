## Ingredients

- hare or rabbit
- livers
- 1 slice fat pork
- bread-crumbs
- champignons or mushrooms
- pepper
- salt
- onion-juice
- salad oil
- lemon-juice
- 1 cupful weak stock, consomme, or butter and water
- bacon
- butter
- browned flour
- 1 teaspoonful catsup
- 1/2 glass claret

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the hare or rabbit thoroughly.
2. Chop the livers fine, also a slice of fat pork, and mix with bread-crumbs.
3. Add a few champignons or mushrooms if you like.
4. Season with pepper, salt, and onion-juice.
5. Stuff the rabbits with this, sew them up, and anoint well with salad oil and lemon-juice, leaving them in this marinade for an hour.
6. Put into the roaster, pour a cupful of weak stock, or consomme, or butter and water under them; cover and cook for an hour.
7. Take off the bacon, wash over with butter, and brown.
8. Dish the hares, and keep hot, while you thicken the gravy with browned flour, boil up, add a teaspoonful of catsup and half a glass of claret, pour a few spoonfuls over the rabbits, the rest into a boat.
