## Ingredients

- 1 pint fruit
- 3/4 pint sugar

## Directions

1. Wash, drain grapes, remove from stems
2. Heat to boiling point
3. Wash and cook until seeds come out
4. Strain out seeds and skins
5. Cook 10 minutes, measure, add sugar and boil until liquid thickens
6. Put in sterilized jars
