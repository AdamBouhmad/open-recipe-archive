## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 cup currants
- nutmeg
- 3 1/2 lemon peel
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 pound suet
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 cups flour
- 2 heaping teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Steam 3 hours
