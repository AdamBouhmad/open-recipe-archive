## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound well-washed currants
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 cup butter beaten to a cream
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 eggs
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Paper a dish and bake 1 hour
