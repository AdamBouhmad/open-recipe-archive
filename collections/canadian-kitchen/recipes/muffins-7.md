## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
- 6 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients
2. Add the milk gradually and the egg well
