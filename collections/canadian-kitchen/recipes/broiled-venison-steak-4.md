## Ingredients

- venison steak
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls currant jelly
- butter

## Directions

1. Broil venison steak over a clear fire, turning often.
2. It requires more cooking than beef.
3. When sufficiently done, season with salt and pepper, pour over two tablespoonfuls of currant jelly, melted with a piece of butter.
4. Serve hot on plates.
