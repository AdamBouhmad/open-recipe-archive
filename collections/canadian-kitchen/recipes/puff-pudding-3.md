## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- pinch salt
- heaping teaspoon baking powder
- sweet milk
- whites of 2 eggs
- preserved fruit
- 2 yolks
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- flavoring to suit

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pint flour, pinch salt and heaping teaspoon baking powder into a soft batter with sweet milk
2. Add whites of 2 eggs beaten stiff
3. Have ready well-greased cups, this quantity requires 6 or 7
4. Into each put a couple teaspoons batter, then a little preserved fruit, then more batter, leaving plenty room to rise
5. Steam 20 or 30 minutes
6. Serve hot with sauce made of the 2 yolks, a cup milk, 2 tablespoons sugar, flavoring to suit
