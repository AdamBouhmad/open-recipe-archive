## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 3/4 cup lard and butter mixed
- 3 cups heaped flour
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- warm water

## Directions

1. Combine brown sugar, molasses, sour milk, and lard and butter mixed
2. Add flour
3. Add eggs
4. Add ginger and cinnamon
5. Dissolve soda in warm water
6. Bake in deep pans
7. Will keep a long time
