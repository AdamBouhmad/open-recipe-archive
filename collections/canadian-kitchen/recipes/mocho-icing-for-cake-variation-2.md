## Ingredients

- 1 cup icing sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- blanched almonds
- flavor to taste

## Directions

1. Beat 1 cup icing sugar, 1/2 cup butter, and 1 tablespoon boiling water to a cream
2. Flavor to taste
3. Spread on cake
4. Blanch almonds by covering in warm water for a while, then take peel off
5. Brown in oven and roll while warm
6. Sprinkle on icing
7. This will cover an ordinary-sized cake in a pan
