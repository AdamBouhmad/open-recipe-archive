## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup lard or butter
- ¾ cup sour milk
- ½ teaspoon soda
- just enough flour to roll
- any flavoring desired

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out thin
3. Bake quickly
