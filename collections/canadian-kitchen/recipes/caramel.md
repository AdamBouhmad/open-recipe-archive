## Ingredients

- two cups white sugar
- one-half cup water
- one-half large cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Put on a hot fire in a frying-pan, and stir constantly until it burns a dark brown color and becomes liquid.
2. Remove from the fire and add one-half large cup of boiling water.
3. Set away when cool in a jar for use.
