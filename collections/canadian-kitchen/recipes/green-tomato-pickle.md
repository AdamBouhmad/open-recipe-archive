## Ingredients

- 4 quarts green tomatoes
- 1 head cabbage
- 1 head cauliflower
- 1 bunch celery
- 6 large cucumbers
- 6 onions
- 6 teaspoons mustard
- 1 teaspoon tumeric powder
- 1 cup flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 quarts vinegar

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients
2. Cook together
