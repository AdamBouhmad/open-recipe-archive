## Ingredients

- slices of home-made bread
- butter
- currants
- three eggs
- a pint of milk
- sugar
- grated lemon-peel or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut bread about half an inch thick, butter and lay in a pudding-dish, sprinkle with currants.
2. Put in another layer of buttered bread and currants.
3. Beat three eggs light and stir into a pint of milk, sweeten to taste.
4. Flavor with a little grated lemon-peel or cinnamon.
5. Pour over the bread and butter and bake in a moderate oven until the custard is set.
6. Test with a knife; if the knife comes out clean the pudding is done.
7. Serve cold in the dish in which it is baked, with a simple sauce.
