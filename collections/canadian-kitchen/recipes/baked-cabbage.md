## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- pepper
- salt
- a little cream

## Directions

1. Boil a cabbage
2. Put in a colander and drain until perfectly dry
3. Chop fine
4. Add pepper, salt, and a little cream
5. Put in an earthen baking pan
6. Bake 1 hour
