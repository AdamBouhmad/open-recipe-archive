## Ingredients

- One can of pineapple (grated)
- Three ounces of loaf sugar
- Half a pint of water
- Three-quarters of an ounce of gelatine
- One and a half pints of cream

## Directions

1. Drain syrup from pineapple
2. Put in half a pint of water and sugar in sauce-pan
3. When dissolved add fruit
4. Boil ten minutes
5. Add gelatine and boil ten minutes longer
6. When entirely cold add the cream, well whipped
7. Pour in moulds
