## Ingredients

- 20 large cucumbers
- 8 large onions
- 1 tablespoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon tumeric
- 2 cups brown sugar
- vinegar to cover

## Directions

1. The night before, peel cucumbers and onions
2. Take out all seeds from cucumbers
3. Chop fine and let stand in an earthen or granite vessel in weak vinegar and water and salt over night
4. Simmer for 30 minutes
