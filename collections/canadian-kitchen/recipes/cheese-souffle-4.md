## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- pinch of cayenne
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/4 cup old cheese
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Make same as white sauce; add yolks and cheese, set to cool, then add whites beaten stiff.
2. Put into a greased pudding dish in medium oven, bake fifteen or twenty minutes.
