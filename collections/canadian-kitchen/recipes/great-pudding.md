## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound macaroons
- six eggs
- two and one-half pounds ladies' fingers soaked in wine
- two and one-half cups sugar
- two tablespoonfuls gelatine
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat macaroons, ladyfingers, eggs and sugar for one-half an hour
2. Dissolve gelatine and mix with eggs after they are beaten
3. Whip quickly for a minute or two
4. Have whipped cream on top
