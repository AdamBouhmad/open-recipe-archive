## Ingredients

- cherries, stoned
- sugar (0.5 pound to a pound of stoned cherries)
- cherry stones

## Directions

1. Take 0.5 pound sugar to a pound of stoned cherries
2. Make syrup and put in the fruit
3. Let it scald, not boil hard, for 10 or 15 minutes
4. Can and seal
5. Optionally put some of cherry stones in a muslin bag and put into the syrup to scald, but they should not be put into the jars with the fruit
