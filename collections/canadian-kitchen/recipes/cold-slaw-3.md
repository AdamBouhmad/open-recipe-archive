## Ingredients

- 1 small cup vinegar
- 1 well-beaten egg
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a small lump butter
- pepper
- salt
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Add egg, mustard, sugar, butter to vinegar
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Let come to a boil
4. Pour over nicely chopped cabbage while hot
