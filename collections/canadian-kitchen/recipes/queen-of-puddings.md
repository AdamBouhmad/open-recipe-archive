## Ingredients

- 1 pint bread crumbs
- 1 quart milk
- 5 tablespoons sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- jelly
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar together, leaving out whites of 2 for meringue
2. Stir in 1/2 pint milk
3. Put bread crumbs into a buttered pudding dish
4. Pour the other pint milk over them, then the eggs
5. Cook in a moderate oven 1 hour
6. Spread over top jelly and whites of eggs sweetened with 1 tablespoon brown sugar
