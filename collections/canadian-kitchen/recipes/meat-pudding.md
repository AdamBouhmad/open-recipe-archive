## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound lean meat, bacon or ham
- 1 chopped parboiled onion
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- suet paste
- water
- salt
- potatoes
- gravy

## Directions

1. Prepare suet paste, roll it to an oblong square, wet the edges with water
2. Spread the beef, sprinkled over with onion and pepper over the paste
3. Roll it up, keeping it back from the edges, and pressing them well together
4. Dip a pudding-cloth in boiling water, flour it well, roll the pudding in it
5. Tie the ends of the pudding not too close, as it swells a little
6. Have ready a large pan of boiling water with teaspoon of salt in it
7. Place pudding in this and boil 2 hours
8. Place a plate in the bottom of saucepan to prevent pudding from sticking
9. Serve with potatoes and nice gravy
