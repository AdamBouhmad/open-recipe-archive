## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- drawn-butter
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley
- lemons
- tomato catsup

## Directions

1. Weigh the fish.
2. Carefully bind up the fish in thin muslin or coarse cheese-cloth.
3. Use a large kettle that will accommodate the fish easily.
4. Salt the water well first.
5. Heat the water to boiling point, but not boiling when the fish is put in.
6. Ensure sufficient water to fully cover the fish, but not in excess.
7. For a large fish, add three tablespoonfuls of vinegar to the water.
8. Keep the water boiling and allow six minutes to each pound; if the fish is large, add six minutes to the computation.
9. Never stab the fish with a fork or skewer to find if it is done.
10. Serve with drawn-butter and hard-boiled eggs sliced.
11. Garnish with parsley and sliced lemons.
12. Some like tomato catsup poured over the fish, without the eggs and lemons.
