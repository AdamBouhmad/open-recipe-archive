## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 12 tablespoons milk
- 3 tablespoons baking powder
- 2 quarts flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll thin
3. Bake quick to a light brown
