## Ingredients

- breakfast bacon or salt pork
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook either breakfast bacon or salt pork in frying pan till nearly done
2. Pour over it a batter made of 1 cup milk, 1 egg, and flour to thicken
3. Cook till set, then put in oven to brown
4. Turn over on flat dish
5. Serve on a cold winter night with hot biscuits
