## Ingredients

- 4 quarts tomatoes
- 2 pounds sugar
- 3/4 pound raisins
- 2 cups stewed apples
- 2 tablespoons allspice
- 1/2 tablespoon cloves
- 3/4 tablespoon cinnamon
- 4 tablespoons ginger
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop raisins
2. Boil 1 hour
