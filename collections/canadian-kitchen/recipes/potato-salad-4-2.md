## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped potatoes
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- 1 very small onion chopped fine
- 1 bunch celery cut small
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- pepper to taste
- 2 large tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients
2. Garnish either with small cabbage leaves or celery tops
