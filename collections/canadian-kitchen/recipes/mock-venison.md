## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- strips of salt pork
- pepper
- cloves
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Lard a leg of mutton with strips of salt pork inserted in deep slits in the meat, which has been previously rolled in pepper and cloves.
2. Bake 2 hours, or according to size of roast.
3. About an hour before serving, spread over it currant jelly, return to the oven and let it brown.
