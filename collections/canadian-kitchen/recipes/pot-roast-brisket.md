## Ingredients

- 8 pound brisket
- 1/2 cup water
- brown flour

## Directions

1. Put brisket in a pot with 1/2 cup water
2. Cover close and cook 4 hours gently
3. Brown it in its own fat
4. Thicken gravy with brown flour
5. Season to suit
