## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup tapioca
- 7 sour apples
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- cold water
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 1/2 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca 1 hour in cold water to cover
2. Add boiling water and salt
3. Cook in double boiler until transparent
4. Pare and slice apples
5. Place in a buttered pudding dish
6. Sprinkle sugar over apples
7. Pour tapioca over apples
8. Bake in a moderate oven until apples are soft
