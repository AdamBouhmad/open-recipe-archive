## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1.5 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup butter or half lard
- 2 cups raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- 2.5 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, brown sugar, butter, raisins, currants, walnuts, soda, flour and cinnamon
2. Drop mixture in teaspoonfuls in buttered pan and bake, or roll out thin like tea cakes
