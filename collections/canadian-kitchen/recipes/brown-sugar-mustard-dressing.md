## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup of vinegar
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 1 tablespoon of mustard
- 1 of brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1/2 head of cabbage cut up fine
- pepper and salt
- a little chopped onion

## Directions

1. Let 1/4 cup of vinegar and a tablespoon of butter come to a boil
2. After thoroughly mixing a tablespoon of mustard, 1 of brown sugar, an egg and a cup sweet milk, stir in slowly with the vinegar and keep stirring till it boils
3. Cut up fine half head of cabbage, season with pepper and salt, and put into the dressing; a little chopped onion could be added if wished
