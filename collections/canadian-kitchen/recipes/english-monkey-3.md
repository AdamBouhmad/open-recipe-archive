## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup soft bread crumbs
- ¾ cup cheese

## Directions

1. Melt butter
2. Add cheese and stir until melted
3. Add crumbs soaked in the milk
4. Add egg lightly beaten
5. Cook until thickened considerable
