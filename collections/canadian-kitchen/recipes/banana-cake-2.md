## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon banana flavoring
- sliced banana

## Directions

1. Mix 1 egg, 1 cup granulated sugar, 1 tablespoon butter, 3/4 cup sweet milk, 2 cups flour, and 2 teaspoons baking powder
2. Bake in 3 layers
3. For filling: Mix 1/4 cup granulated sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, yolk of 1 egg, 1 cup sweet milk, and 1 teaspoon banana flavoring
4. Cook filling till thick, put between layers of cake
5. Beat the white of the egg and add sugar enough to make icing
6. Cover the cake with the icing and slice banana over the top
7. Best while fresh
