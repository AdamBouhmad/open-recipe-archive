## Ingredients

- 1 quart finely-powdered bread crumbs
- water
- 3/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- a little nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 pint boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix bread crumbs, cloves, molasses, flour, raisins, and soda well
2. Steam 1 hour
3. For pudding sauce: mix sugar with 2 tablespoons flour
4. Add a little nutmeg and 1 tablespoon butter
5. Pour over this 1 pint boiling water
6. Stir till smooth
