## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 lb. flour
- Bread and jam

## Directions

1. Make a batter with the milk, egg, and flour.
2. Cut some slices of bread rather thin, in squares or three-cornered pieces, spread half of them with jam and cover with the other slices.
3. Dip them into the batter, and fry in boiling lard till of a light brown color.
4. Serve very hot, piled on a dish, and sprinkled with white sugar.
