## Ingredients

- 1 solid pt. chopped chicken
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful pepper
- 1 cup cream or chicken stock
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of onion juice
- 1 tablespoonful lemon juice
- 1 pt. bread-crumbs
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter

## Directions

1. Put the cream or stock on to boil
2. Mix the flour and butter together and stir into the boiling cream
3. Add the chicken and seasoning
4. Boil for two minutes, and add two of the eggs well beaten
5. Take from the fire immediately and set away to cool
6. When cool, shape, roll in egg and crumbs and fry
