## Ingredients

- eggs
- chopped ham
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- cream
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil some eggs quite hard
2. Shell them and cut in halves
3. Take out the yolks
4. Pass through a sieve
5. Mix with chopped ham, parsley, a little bit of onion, pepper and salt and a little cream
6. Put back in the whites
7. Place on little rounds of bread and butter
8. Serve cold
