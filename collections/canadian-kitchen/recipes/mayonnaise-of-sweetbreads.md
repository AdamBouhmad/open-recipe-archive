## Ingredients

- A pair of sweetbreads
- Good veal stock
- Pepper, salt, mace
- A very small piece of garlic
- A half a pint or a little more of stock
- A quarter of an ounce of leaf gelatine
- Mayonnaise
- Aspic or savory jelly
- Beet-root
- Hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Blanch the sweetbreads
2. Boil them for a few minutes in some good veal stock
3. Put them to get cold and cut into small round pieces
4. Place them in a stewpan with pepper, salt, mace, a very small piece of garlic, a half a pint or a little more of the stock they were first cooked in, and a quarter of an ounce of leaf gelatine
5. Simmer very steadily for fifteen minutes or so
6. Place the pieces separately in a shallow dish
7. Pour the gravy over them
8. When they have set quite firmly cover thickly with mayonnaise
9. When quite cold, ornament according to taste with aspic or savory jelly, with beet-root, hard-boiled egg cut into fancy shapes and placed over the dish
