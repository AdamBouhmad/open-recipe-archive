## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- ½ cup sweet cream
- 1 cup raisins seeded and chopped
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- a little nutmeg
- flour enough to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Bake
