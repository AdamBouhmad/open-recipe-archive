## Ingredients

- 2 cups stale bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups sliced apples
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- grated rind and juice of 1/2 a lemon

## Directions

1. Butter pudding dish
2. Cover with bread crumbs
3. Put in a layer of apples
4. Sprinkle with sugar, lemon rind and juice
5. Dot with butter
6. Repeat till dish is full, finishing with bread crumbs
7. Cover when first put in the oven to prevent crumbs browning too rapidly
