## Ingredients

- Whites of 5 eggs
- 1/4 cup butter
- 3 cups granulated sugar
- 2 cups white flour
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- vanilla flavoring
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- spices to taste

## Directions

1. For White Part: Mix whites of 5 eggs, 1/4 cup butter, 3 cups granulated sugar, 2 cups white flour, 1 cup sweet milk, 2 teaspoons baking powder, and vanilla flavoring
2. For Dark Part: Mix 1 cup brown sugar, 3/4 cup butter, 1 cup sweet milk, 3/4 cup molasses, 2 cups flour, 1 egg, 3/4 teaspoon soda, and spices to taste
3. Put in the pan by spoonfuls
