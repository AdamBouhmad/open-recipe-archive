## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 3/8 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon melted butter

## Directions

1. mix dry
2. add liquid gradually
3. add butter
4. Do not beat too much
5. Have pan hot, rubbed with pork fat, butter burns very easily
6. Drop by spoonfuls
7. When puffed and full of bubbles, cooked on edge, turn and cook other side
