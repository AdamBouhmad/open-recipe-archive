## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- salted water
- cold tender meat
- salt
- pepper
- 1 beaten egg
- 2 tablespoons cracker crumbs
- hot water or stock
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Boil 1 cup rice until tender in salted water
2. Line a mould half an inch deep
3. Chop fine any kind of cold tender meat
4. Season to taste
5. Add 1 beaten egg
6. Add 2 tablespoons cracker crumbs
7. Moisten with hot water or stock
8. Pack in the mould or cover with rice
9. Steam 45 minutes
10. Turn out on platter
11. Pour tomato sauce over
