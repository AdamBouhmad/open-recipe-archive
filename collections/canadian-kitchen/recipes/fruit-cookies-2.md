## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon grated nutmeg
- 3/4 cup currants
- flour
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix soft and roll out, using just enough flour to stiffen sufficiently
2. Cut out with a large cutter
3. Wet the tops with milk and sprinkle sugar over them
4. Bake on buttered tins in quick oven
