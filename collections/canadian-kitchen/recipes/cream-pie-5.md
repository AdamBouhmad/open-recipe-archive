## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 small cup sugar
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- Butter size of egg
- 1 tablespoonful flour

## Directions

1. Boil until it thickens, stirring constantly
2. Fill the shell (which has previously been baked) with the mixture
3. Ice with the whites of eggs
4. Place in oven a few minutes until icing begins to brown
