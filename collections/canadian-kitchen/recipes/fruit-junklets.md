## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups white sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 pound chopped dates
- 3/4 pound walnuts
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon warm water

## Directions

1. Stir 1 1/4 cups white sugar and 1/2 cup butter together.
2. Add beaten 3 eggs, then 3 cups flour.
3. Add 1 pound chopped dates and 3/4 pound walnuts and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
4. Dissolve 1 teaspoon soda in 1 tablespoon warm water and stir in last of all.
5. Drop mixture, size of a walnut, in buttered pans and spread with a wet knife.
6. Bake in a hot oven.
