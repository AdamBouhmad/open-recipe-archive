## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup cream
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup chopped nuts
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- a little salt
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix 2 eggs, 1/4 cup cream, 1/4 cup white sugar, 1 cup flour, 3/4 cup chopped nuts, 2 teaspoons baking powder, a little salt, and flavoring
2. Bake
