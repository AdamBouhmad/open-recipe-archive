## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup blanched almonds ground very fine
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 egg
- butter size of an egg
- rose water
- white icing

## Directions

1. Beat sugar, butter, egg and milk to a cream
2. Stir in the nuts
3. Boil 8 minutes in a double boiler
4. Flavor with rose water
5. Spread on cake
6. After it sets, spread white icing over
