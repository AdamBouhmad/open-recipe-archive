## Ingredients

- One ounce of Balm of Gilead buds
- One quart of water
- One pint of Bourbon whisky
- One pound of loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Put buds in a quart of water and boil down to a pint
2. Add one pint of Bourbon whisky and one pound of loaf sugar
