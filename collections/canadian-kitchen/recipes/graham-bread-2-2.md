## Ingredients

- 1 cup Graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Steam three hours
