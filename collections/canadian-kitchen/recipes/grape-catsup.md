## Ingredients

- 5 pounds grapes
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls cinnamon
- 2 tablespoonfuls allspice
- 0.5 teaspoonful red pepper
- A little ground cloves
- 1 teaspoonful grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stew grapes over a slow fire until soft, then strain through a sieve
2. Add sugar, vinegar, salt, cinnamon and allspice, red pepper, cloves (ground), and grated nutmeg
3. Mix all together and boil till quite thick
