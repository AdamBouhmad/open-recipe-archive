## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls baking powder
- 1 qt. flour
- 1 tablespoonful cold butter
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 pt. cold milk

## Directions

1. Sift baking powder with flour; put in cold butter; add well-beaten egg, sugar, and salt; rub well together, and make into a dough, with a pint of cold milk.
2. Roll out less than half-inch thick.
3. Cut with a large biscuit-cutter.
4. Spread soft butter over the top of each, fold them together, and lay a little apart on greased tins.
5. Brush over the tops with sweet milk and set immediately in a hot oven.
