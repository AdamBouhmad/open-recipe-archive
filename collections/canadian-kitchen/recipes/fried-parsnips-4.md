## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salted water
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- hot butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Treat parsnips in the usual manner
2. Cut in slices lengthwise about ½ inch thick
3. Boil in slightly salted water until tender
4. Remove from water
5. Dip in flour seasoned with salt and pepper
6. Lay in pan with some hot butter
7. Brown
8. Serve at once
9. May also be dipped in beaten egg
