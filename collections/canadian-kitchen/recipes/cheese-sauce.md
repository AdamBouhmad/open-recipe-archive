## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- a little pepper
- 3/4 cup grated cheese

## Directions

1. Put the butter, flour, mustard, salt and pepper into a saucepan
2. Stir over the fire until it froths
3. Add the milk and stir constantly until it thickens and boils
4. Stand over hot water, or on a cooler part of the stove
5. Add the cheese and stir until it is well mixed in
