## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 egg
- 3 teaspoons of melted butter
- a little salt
- 2 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, cream of tartar, and soda with egg, melted butter, salt and sweet milk
2. Bake in gem pans
