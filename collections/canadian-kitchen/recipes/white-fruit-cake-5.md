## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar (1 cup white and 1/2 cup brown)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 3/4 cup milk
- small teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 nutmeg
- grated rind of orange
- 1/2 pound blanched almonds cut fine
- 1/2 pound figs cut fine
- 1/2 pound citron peel cut fine
- 1 pound sultana raisins
- 1 large cup currants
- 1 pound cocoanut
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream together 1 cup butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar.
2. Add 4 eggs, 1/2 cup orange juice, 3/4 cup milk, and small teaspoon vanilla.
3. Add grated rind of orange and 1/2 nutmeg.
4. Stir in 1/2 pound blanched almonds cut fine, 1/2 pound figs cut fine, and 1/2 pound citron peel cut fine.
5. Add 1 pound sultana raisins and 1 large cup currants.
6. Sift together 2 1/2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder and stir in.
7. Put in 1 pound cocoanut at the last.
8. Bake in tin lined with paper for 2 hours.
