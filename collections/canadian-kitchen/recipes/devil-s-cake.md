## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter creamed
- ½ cup grated chocolate
- ½ cup sour milk
- ½ cup boiling water
- 2½ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon cream tartar
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda in 1 tablespoon boiling water
