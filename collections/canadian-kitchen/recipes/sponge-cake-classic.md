## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- one cupful of fine white sugar
- one cupful of flour
- lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and whites separately
2. add half the sugar to yolks and half to whites and beat well
3. then beat both together
4. stir in flour
5. bake in slow oven
