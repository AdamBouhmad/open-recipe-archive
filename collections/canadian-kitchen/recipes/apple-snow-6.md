## Ingredients

- 4 large apples
- granulated or powdered sugar
- salt
- whites of 3 eggs
- custard made with yolks of eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 cups milk
- jelly

## Directions

1. Wipe, pare and core 4 large apples
2. Steam until soft
3. Press through sieve
4. Sweeten with granulated or powdered sugar
5. Add a few grains of salt
6. Beat very stiff the whites of 3 eggs
7. Pour in the apples, beating constantly
8. Chill
9. Serve with custard made with yolks of eggs, 3/4 cup sugar and 2 cups milk
10. If wished to look especially nice, add a few dots of bright jelly
