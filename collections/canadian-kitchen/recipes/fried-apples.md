## Ingredients

- fair-sized apples
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take as many fair-sized apples as required
2. Cut them in two crosswise
3. Remove core
4. Fry the cut side until brown in butter
5. Turn over
6. Fill cavity with sugar
7. Brown the other side
8. Set pan in oven until thoroughly cooked
