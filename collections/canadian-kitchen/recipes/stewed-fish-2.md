## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- fish
- 6 onions
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Put 2 tablespoons butter in the frying pan
2. When hot lay the fish in with the outside down
3. Slice 6 onions and put on top of fish
4. Sprinkle well with salt and pepper
5. Cover tight and allow to cook 20 minutes or 1/4 hour
6. If it should cook dry, add a very little water
7. Serve hot
