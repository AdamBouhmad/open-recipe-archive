## Ingredients

- ¾ pound macaroni broken in small pieces
- 1 quart of salt water
- cheese broken in small pieces
- rolled bread or cracker crumbs
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Cook ¾ pound macaroni broken in small pieces about 20 minutes in a quart of salt water
2. Turn into colander and pour cold water over it
3. Let drain
4. Put layer of cheese (broken in small pieces) in bottom of baking dish
5. Put a layer of macaroni on top
6. Repeat until dish is full
7. Cover the top with rolled bread or cracker crumbs and little pieces of butter
8. Pour over the whole enough heated milk (about 2 cups) to moisten
9. Bake until brown
