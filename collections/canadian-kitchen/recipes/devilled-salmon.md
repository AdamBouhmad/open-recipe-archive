## Ingredients

- canned or cold boiled salmon
- Yolks of 3 eggs boiled hard
- 1 pound salad oil or melted butter
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- salt and cayenne to taste
- vinegar
- lettuce leaves
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Arrange fish in neat flakes
2. Rub egg yolks with salad oil or melted butter to a smooth paste
3. Add sugar and mustard, salt and cayenne to taste
4. Add a little vinegar
5. Arrange lettuce leaves around the plate
6. Pour the dressing over the fish
7. Garnish with the whites of eggs cut in rings
8. Note: This is nice prepared with shredded (not chopped) cabbage
