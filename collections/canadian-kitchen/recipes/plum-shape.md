## Ingredients

- one quart of plums
- boiling water
- one-half box gelatine
- one cup cold water
- one cup water
- whipped cream
- lemon juice or wine

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over a quart of plums, let them stand long enough to soften the skins, but not to break them open.
2. Pour off the water and when cool peel and remove the stones, taking care to save all the juice.
3. Soak half a box of gelatine in a cup of cold water.
4. Stew the plums until tender after adding a cup of water.
5. Sweeten to taste.
6. Stir the whole while hot into the gelatine.
7. Serve with whipped cream.
8. Lemon juice or wine may be used with the water, if liked.
