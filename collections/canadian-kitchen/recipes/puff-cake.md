## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix white sugar, eggs, butter, and milk
2. Add flour and baking powder
3. Bake quickly in loaf
