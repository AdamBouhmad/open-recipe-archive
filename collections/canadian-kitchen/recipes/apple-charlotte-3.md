## Ingredients

- one pint stewed apples
- one cup sugar
- one-quarter box gelatine
- whites of three eggs
- juice and rind of one lemon
- lady fingers

## Directions

1. Mix apples with one cup sugar, the grated rind and juice of lemon
2. Soak gelatine in one-third of a cup of cold water twenty minutes
3. add one-third of a cup of boiling water to dissolve the gelatine
4. when cool add it to the apples
5. When beginning to stiffen, add the beaten whites of three eggs
6. pour into moulds lined with lady fingers
7. Serve with soft custard poured round the base of the charlotte
