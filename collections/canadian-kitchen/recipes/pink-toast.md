## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 3/4 cups stewed tomato strained
- 3/4 teaspoon soda in tomatoes
- 1 cup scalded milk
- slices of unbuttered toast

## Directions

1. Heat butter and add flour and salt. Add stewed tomato strained with soda. Add scalded milk. Pour over slices of unbuttered toast.
