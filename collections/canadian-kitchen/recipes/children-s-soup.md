## Ingredients

- 6 or more good-size potatoes
- 2 onions
- water
- 1 cup of cream
- 2 cups milk
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare potatoes, slice in thin slices
2. Cut 2 onions fine, add water enough to cook
3. When done add a cup of cream and 2 cups milk
4. Let come to a boil, season with pepper and salt, and butter
