## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- Butter size of egg
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1 cup rich cream
- Slices of toast

## Directions

1. Heat to boiling, and add butter
2. Stir flour smoothly into cream, and add some of the boiling milk to this
3. Heat it gradually and prevent the flour from lumping
4. Stir into the boiling milk, and let it cook a few moments
5. Salt to taste
6. After taking from the fire stir in a beaten egg
7. Strain the mixture on to toast lightly buttered
