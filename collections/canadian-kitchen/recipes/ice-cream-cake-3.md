## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 2 cups sifted flour
- yolks of 3 eggs
- icing sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix whites of 3 eggs, brown sugar, butter, sweet milk, soda, cream tartar, and sifted flour
2. Bake
3. For icing: thicken yolks of 3 eggs with icing sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla
