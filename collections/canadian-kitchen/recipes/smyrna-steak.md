## Ingredients

- beef or mutton
- juice of onion
- salt
- pepper
- fine breadcrumbs
- several beaten eggs
- hot butter
- melted butter
- tomato juice

## Directions

1. Chop beef or mutton very fine, and season with juice of onion, salt and pepper
2. Add fine breadcrumbs and several beaten eggs
3. Mix well
4. Make into rolls and brown in hot butter in a frying-pan
5. Put in a kettle, cover with melted butter and a little tomato juice
6. Simmer gently until tender
