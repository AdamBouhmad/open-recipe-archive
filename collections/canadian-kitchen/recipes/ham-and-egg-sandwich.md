## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons finely chopped ham
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- 3/4 teaspoon dry mustard
- a pinch of salt
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- buttered bread

## Directions

1. Put ham, eggs, parsley, dry mustard, salt, and melted butter into a mortar and pound them until a smooth paste
2. Spread mixture on buttered bread, cover with another slice, and cut into neat shapes
