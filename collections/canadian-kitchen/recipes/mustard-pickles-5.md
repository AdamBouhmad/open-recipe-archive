## Ingredients

- 4 quarts cucumbers
- 1/2 peck pickling onions
- 4 heads cauliflower
- 4 green peppers
- 7 cups brown sugar
- 1 gallon cider vinegar
- 3.5 pound mustard
- 1 cup flour
- 1 ounce curry powder
- 1 ounce turmeric

## Directions

1. Mix 3.5 pound mustard, 1 cup flour, 1 ounce curry powder, and 1 ounce turmeric with cold vinegar
2. Stir into the boiling vinegar
3. When it thickens put in the vegetables
4. Cook slowly 1/2 hour
