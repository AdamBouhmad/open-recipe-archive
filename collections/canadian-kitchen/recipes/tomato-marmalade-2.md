## Ingredients

- 5 pounds tomatoes
- 5 pounds granulated sugar
- 1 good-sized lemon
- 2 good-sized oranges

## Directions

1. Cut lemon and oranges very small
2. Boil tomatoes and fruits together until a little thick, and fruit is tender
3. Add sugar and just let boil up
4. Can
