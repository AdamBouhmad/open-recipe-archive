## Ingredients

- One-half pound flour
- one-half pound butter
- one-quarter pint cold water
- yolk of one egg
- juice of one-half lemon
- salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour thoroughly, then add a pinch of salt and two ounces of butter.
2. Mix the yolk of egg and lemon juice with the water and mix the flour into a paste with this, stirring with a silver fork.
3. Turn it on a board, roll it out one-half inch thick, put three ounces of butter on, fold it over and roll out three times.
4. Line the patty tins thinly with this and put in a cool place till wanted.
5. Roll out the remainder of the paste, put the last three ounces of butter on it, and roll out five times.
6. Let it stand one-half an hour.
7. Fill the patty tins with mincemeat, roll the paste out as thin as possible, wet the edges of the paste in pans and cover each pie with three folds of paste.
8. Bake in a quick oven for about twenty minutes.
