## Ingredients

- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 beaten egg
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 onion
- pepper
- salt
- 1 cucumber minced
- 1 tablespoon cream

## Directions

1. Put 1 cup vinegar to boil
2. Add 1 beaten egg, 1 tablespoon ginger, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 onion, pepper, salt, 1 cucumber minced
3. Let boil
4. When cold, add 1 tablespoon cream
