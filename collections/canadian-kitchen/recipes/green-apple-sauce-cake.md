## Ingredients

- 2 cups unsweetened apple sauce
- 4 teaspoons soda
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- half butter and lard
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- nutmeg
- 5 cents' worth lemon peel
- 2 cups chopped raisins
- 3 tablespoons molasses
- 3 1/2 cups sifted flour

## Directions

1. Stir 4 teaspoons soda in the apple sauce
2. Mix in 2 cups granulated sugar, 1 cup shortening (half butter and lard), 2 teaspoons cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon cloves, 1 teaspoon allspice and a little nutmeg
3. Add 5 cents' worth lemon peel, 2 cups chopped raisins, 3 tablespoons molasses and 3 1/2 cups sifted flour
4. Bake in a 4-quart pan
