## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 cup cream
- ½ teaspoon soda dissolved in teaspoon water
- 1 egg
- 1 cup raisins
- ½ cup cornstarch
- 2 cups flour
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon cloves

## Directions

1. Bake in moderate oven
