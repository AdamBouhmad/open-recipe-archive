## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 2½ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1½ cream tartar
- ⅜ cup raisins
- ½ cup nuts chopped fine

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Ice and put nuts on top
