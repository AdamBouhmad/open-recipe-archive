## Ingredients

- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup lard
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon butter
- a few drops vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups molasses, 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup lard, 1/2 cup sour cream, 1 teaspoon soda, and 2 teaspoons ginger
2. Stir thick and roll out a sheet long enough to fill a pan
3. Bake quickly
4. For icing: Mix 1 cup brown sugar, 3 tablespoons water, 1 teaspoon butter, and a few drops vanilla
5. Ice the whole sheet and cut in squares
