## Ingredients

- 2 cups Graham flour
- 2 1/2 cups white flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup lard
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 pound dates
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup hot water

## Directions

1. Mix Graham flour, white flour, brown sugar, lard, milk and baking powder
2. Roll and bake
3. Cook 1 pound dates, 1 cup brown sugar and 1 cup hot water well
4. Put the date mixture between cookies
