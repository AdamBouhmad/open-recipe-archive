## Ingredients

- Whites of 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
- 1 1/2 cups cocoanut
- 1/2 teaspoon pineapple flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat whites of 2 eggs stiff
2. Beat in 1 cup sugar with whites
3. Set in boiling water
4. Add 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
5. Stir briskly until crust begins to form
6. Take out, add 1 1/2 cups cocoanut and 1/2 teaspoon pineapple flavoring
7. Have pan with buttered paper and drop in a teaspoon of the mixture
8. Bake 15 minutes in a moderate oven
