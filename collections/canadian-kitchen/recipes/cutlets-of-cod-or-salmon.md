## Ingredients

- Three pounds of fish cut in slices three-quarters of an inch thick
- a handful of fine breadcrumbs
- pepper and salt
- a little mixed parsley
- one egg, beaten light
- Enough butter, lard, or dripping to fry the cutlets

## Directions

1. Cut each slice of fish into strips, as wide as your two fingers
2. Dry them with a clean cloth
3. Rub lightly with salt and pepper
4. Dip in the egg, then the breadcrumbs
5. Fry in enough fat to cover them well
6. Drain away every drop of fat, and lay upon hot white paper in a heated dish
