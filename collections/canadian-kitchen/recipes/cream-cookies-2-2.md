## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup thin sweet cream
- 1 egg
- 3 level teaspoons baking powder
- flour to roll soft

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll soft and bake
