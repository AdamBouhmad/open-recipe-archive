## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces cornstarch
- 3 ounces butter
- 3 ounces fine white sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 1 pint milk

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan
2. Add to it the cornstarch and sugar and mix them together
3. Add milk heated to nearly boiling
4. Stir well with a wooden spoon until it boils and thickens
5. Let it cool and beat well into it the yolks of the eggs
6. Beat the whites to a stiff froth and stir gently into the mixture
7. Add any flavoring you like
8. Slightly butter a dish, pour in the mixture and bake in a slow oven
9. Serve quickly as Souffles will soon fall
