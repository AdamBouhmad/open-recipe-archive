## Ingredients

- stale pieces of bread
- sweet milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- little salt
- flavoring
- jelly or fresh fruit

## Directions

1. Pour milk over bread to cover nicely
2. Put a small plate on top to keep bread under milk
3. Let stand a couple of hours
4. Beat up 2 eggs separate
5. Stir in sugar, little salt and flavoring
6. Stir into soaked bread
7. Bake in oven
8. When done spread a few spoonfuls of jelly or fresh fruit on top
9. Cover with beaten whites of eggs
10. Return to oven to brown
11. Serve with a good sauce or cream
