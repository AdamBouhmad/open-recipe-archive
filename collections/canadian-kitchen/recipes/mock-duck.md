## Ingredients

- 1 round of well-pounded beefsteak
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs dressing as prepared for goose or duck
- flour

## Directions

1. Dust beefsteak with pepper and salt
2. Spread on it a thick layer of bread crumbs dressing as prepared for goose or duck
3. Roll up and tie with cord
4. Dust with flour
5. Roast in a covered meat pan till cooked, about 1.5 hours
