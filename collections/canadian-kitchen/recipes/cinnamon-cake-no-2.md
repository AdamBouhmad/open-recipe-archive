## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- butter the size of an egg
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1/2 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Take sugar, butter, eggs, milk, flour, and baking powder together
2. For the icing, mix butter the size of an egg, cinnamon, and sugar
3. Mix well and spread on each layer
