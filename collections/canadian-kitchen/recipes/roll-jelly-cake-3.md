## Ingredients

- 3 eggs well beaten
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup flour
- lemon flavoring
- jelly

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sugar
2. Add sweet milk
3. Sift baking powder with flour and add to mixture
4. Flavor with lemon
5. Bake at once in a moderately hot oven
6. Turn out on a cloth wet with cold water
7. Spread with jelly and roll quickly
