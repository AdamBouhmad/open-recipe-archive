## Ingredients

- One pound of prunes, well washed
- Water
- Sugar
- Gelatine soaked in one cup of water
- Juice of lemon
- Juice of orange

## Directions

1. Cover prunes with water and allow to soak for six hours
2. Put on to boil in same water until tender
3. Add sugar, and boil ten minutes
4. Strain and remove stones
5. Put on stove juice from prunes equal to two and a half cupfuls
6. Add juice of lemon and orange
7. When this commences to boil add gelatine and prunes
8. Turn into mould and serve when cold with whipped cream
