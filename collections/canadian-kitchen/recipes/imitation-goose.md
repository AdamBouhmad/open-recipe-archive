## Ingredients

- sage and onion dressing
- cold meat
- gravy

## Directions

1. Make a good quantity of sage and onion dressing
2. Grease a pie dish
3. Place alternate layers of dressing and small pieces of meat till the dish is full, having dressing for the top layer
4. Have ready enough gravy thickened and flavored to taste, to moisten the whole
5. Bake about 1 hour
