## Ingredients

- 12 ears of corn
- 1 small head cabbage
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon tumeric
- 4 onions
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 red peppers
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 quart vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut grain from cob of corn
2. Add cabbage, sugar, tumeric, onions, salt, red peppers, mustard, flour, and vinegar
3. Chop all fine and cook 1 hour
4. Seal while hot
