## Ingredients

- 1 large cup milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- vanilla to taste
- 1/2 cup shredded cocoanut

## Directions

1. Stir over fire until it thickens
2. Just before taking it off the stove add 1/2 cup of shredded cocoanut
3. Keep the white of egg for frosting pie
