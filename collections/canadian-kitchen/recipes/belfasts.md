## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1/4 nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup seeded raisins or 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 7 1/2 cups Graham flour

## Directions

1. Mix 1 egg, 1 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup buttermilk, 1/4 nutmeg, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 cup seeded raisins or 1 cup currants, 1 teaspoon soda, and 7 1/2 cups Graham flour
2. Bake in gem rings
