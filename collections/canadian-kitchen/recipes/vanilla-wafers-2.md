## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoonfuls milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls vanilla
- 1.5 teaspoonful cream tartar
- 0.5 teaspoonful soda
- Flour to make stiff enough to roll

## Directions

1. Cut in pieces three inches by one inch.
