## Ingredients

- ¼ pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ pound currants
- ½ pound sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon carbonate soda

## Directions

1. Rub butter into the flour
2. Add currants and sugar and mix well
3. Whisk eggs well and stir into mixture
4. Dissolve soda in milk
5. Add milk and soda to mixture and stir well
6. Put in greased cake tin and bake at once for 1 hour
