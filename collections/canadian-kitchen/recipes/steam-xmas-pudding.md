## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to make not too stiff
- salt and spices to taste

## Directions

1. Combine 1 egg, 1/2 cup sugar, 3/4 cup molasses, 1 cup sour milk, 1 cup suet, 1 cup raisins, and 1 cup currants
2. Season to taste
3. Add 1 teaspoon soda
4. Add flour to make not too stiff
5. Steam
