## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ⅜ cup butter
- ⅜ cup sugar
- 1 cup canned raspberries
- 2 small cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake in 2 layers, or in loaf
3. For icing: Make boiled icing with 1 cup granulated sugar boiled with ½ cup water until it hairs, beaten white of 1 egg, and chopped raisins
