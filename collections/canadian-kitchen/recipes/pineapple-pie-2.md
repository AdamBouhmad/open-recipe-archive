## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup fine white sugar
- Well-beaten yolks of 5 eggs
- 1 small cup sweet cream
- 1 small pineapple grated
- Stiffly beaten whites of the eggs
- 1 crust

## Directions

1. Beat 1/2 cup butter and 1 cup fine white sugar to a cream
2. Add the well-beaten yolks of 5 eggs
3. Add 1 small cup sweet cream
4. Add 1 small pineapple grated
5. Lastly add the stiffly beaten whites of the eggs
6. Bake in 1 crust
