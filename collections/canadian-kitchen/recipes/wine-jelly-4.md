## Ingredients

- 1 box gelatine
- 1 pt. cold water
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 lemons
- 2 eggs
- 1 pt. hot water
- 0.5 cup wine
- Wine glass whisky
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine for several hours in pint of cold water
2. Grate the rinds of lemons
3. Add with the juice of the lemons the whites and shells of two eggs, hot water (not boiling), wine, whisky, some cinnamon and sugar
4. Let all boil
5. Skim and strain through flannel bag
6. Add large cup sugar and remainder of wine
7. Put in mould to cool
