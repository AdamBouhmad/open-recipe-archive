## Ingredients

- raw parsnips
- fine bread crumbs
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Grate raw parsnips to make a cupful
2. Add 1 cup fine bread crumbs
3. Add 2 beaten eggs and a piece of butter
4. Add salt and pepper
5. Moisten with sweet milk
6. Form into cakes
7. Fry brown in hot butter
