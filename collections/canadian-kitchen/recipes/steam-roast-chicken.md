## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- bouillon for basting
- bread soaked in water and squeezed out
- onions chopped and browned in lard or butter
- chopped celery
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- 2 eggs
- minced boiled salt pork if desired
- tomatoes (1 gallon)
- 2 gallons water
- onions
- garlic
- celery
- parsley
- bones of roast beef or chicken bones
- marrow or butter
- flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Roast a chicken in a covered roasting pan or in a Dutch oven from 3.5 to 3 hours, according to size of chicken
2. Baste with bouillon
3. For dressing: Mix bread that has been soaked in water and squeezed out, with onions that have been chopped and browned in lard or butter
4. To dressing add chopped celery, parsley, pepper, salt, butter and 2 eggs, also a little minced boiled salt pork if desired
5. For gravy: Boil tomatoes (if 1 gallon, then with 2 gallons of water) with onions, garlic, celery, parsley and bones of roast beef or chicken bones
6. Marrow or butter can be used instead of the bones
7. Boil 3 hours and then add some bouillon
8. Steam and boil again with flour to thicken ever so slightly, and a little salt to suit taste
