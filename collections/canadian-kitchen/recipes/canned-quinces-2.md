## Ingredients

- quinces
- water
- 1/2 pound sugar to pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Peel, core and quarter, or cut in 8 pieces if large
2. Weigh your quinces
3. Put them in the kettle, cover with water and cook till soft
4. Drain off the water all but a very little
5. Add 1/2 pound sugar to pound of fruit
6. Cook till sugar is melted
