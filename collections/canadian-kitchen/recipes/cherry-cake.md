## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup cherry preserves without pits
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 heaping cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix brown sugar and butter
2. Add cherry preserves
3. Add eggs, sour milk, soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg
4. Add flour
5. Bake until done
