## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 6 tablespoons grated cheese
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup thin cream
- 1/4 teaspoon mustard
- a few grains cayenne
- bread

## Directions

1. Make a cream sauce with the starch, butter and cream
2. Mix in with the starch the mustard and cayenne before adding these to the butter
3. When the sauce is made, stir in the cheese
4. Do not cook any longer unless necessary to melt cheese
5. Pour over the untoasted side of bread
