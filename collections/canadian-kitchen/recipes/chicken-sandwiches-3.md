## Ingredients

- cold boiled or roasted chicken
- gravy, water or cream
- butter
- pepper
- buttered bread slices

## Directions

1. Mince up fine any cold boiled or roasted chicken
2. Put it into a saucepan with gravy, water or cream enough to soften it
3. Add a good piece of butter and a pinch of pepper
4. Work it very smooth while it is heating until it looks almost like a paste
5. Spread it on a plate to cool
6. Spread it between slices of buttered bread
