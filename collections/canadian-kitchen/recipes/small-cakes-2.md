## Ingredients

- ¾ cup butter
- 1½ cups granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- flour enough to make it roll
- granulated sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Roll thin and sprinkle top with granulated sugar
2. Cut out with hole in centre and bake in a moderate oven
