## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 pound dates
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon warm water

## Directions

1. Cream together 1 1/2 cups brown sugar and 1/2 cup butter.
2. Add 3 eggs, 1 pound dates, 1/2 cup chopped walnuts and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
3. Mix in 3 cups flour.
4. Dissolve 1 teaspoon soda into 1 tablespoon warm water and stir in.
5. Put in teaspoonfuls in a greased pan and bake quickly.
