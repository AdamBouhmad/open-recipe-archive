## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon (scant)
- 1 cup chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Add sour milk
3. Add baking soda
4. Add flour and cinnamon
5. Add chopped raisins
