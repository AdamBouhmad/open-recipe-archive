## Ingredients

- three pounds of lean mutton
- two tablespoonfuls of butter or beef dripping
- large sliced onion
- cold water
- four potatoes
- carrot
- tomato
- stalk of celery
- tablespoonful of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut three pounds of mutton, which must be lean, into pieces of uniform size, and not more than an inch square.
2. Heat two tablespoonfuls of butter or beef dripping in a saucepan, brown a large sliced onion in it and put in the meat.
3. Turn it over and over until coated with the fat, and slightly browned, add enough cold water to cover the meat an inch deep, put on a tightly fitting top, and stew two hours, or until the meat is very tender.
4. Have ready in another vessel four potatoes, sliced thin, a carrot cut into dice, a tomato cut into bits, a stalk of celery minced, and a tablespoonful of chopped parsley.
