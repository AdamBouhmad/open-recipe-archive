## Ingredients

- 3 whites
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 2 yolks
- 3 cups flour
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
- powdered sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat whites until stiff and dry
2. Add sugar gradually
3. Add yolks beaten until thick and lemon colored
4. Add flavoring
5. Cut and fold in flour mixed and sifted with salt
6. Drop from tip of spoon on unbuttered paper
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
8. Bake about 8 minutes in a moderate oven
