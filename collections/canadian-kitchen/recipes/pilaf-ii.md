## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown stock
- 3 tablespoons butter
- seasonings
- 1/2 cup rice (washed)
- 3/4 cup tomatoes, sliced and strained

## Directions

1. Add tomato to stock
2. Heat to boiling point
3. Add rice and steam until soft
4. Stir in butter and seasoning
