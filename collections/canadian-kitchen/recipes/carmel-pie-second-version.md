## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 large tablespoon flour
- 1 pint milk (not skimmed)
- 2 tablespoons of white sugar

## Directions

1. take yolk of egg, stir it, add a little of the milk, in which stir the flour until thoroughly smooth, then add rest of milk
2. Place frying pan on stove; when hot, put on cup of sugar, stirring so as not to burn
3. when nicely boiling, pour on the mixture, stirring it, and let boil until sugar is all dissolved
4. pour in pie shell, which has previously been baked in oven
5. Beat white of egg to a stiff froth, and gradually add 2 tablespoons of white sugar, or a little more if required, put on pie and nicely brown
