## Ingredients

- stemmed berries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place quantity of stemmed berries, which will fill preserve kettle, on a platter and add 3/4 pound sugar to each pound of fruit
2. Let them stand 2 or 3 hours, till the juice is drawn from them
3. Pour it in the kettle and let come to a boil, and remove the scum which rises
4. Then put in the berries very carefully
5. As soon as they come thoroughly to a boil, put them in warm jars and seal while boiling hot
6. Be sure cans are airtight
