## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 3/4 cups sour milk
- 1/4 cup dark molasses
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1/4 cup citron peel
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3 large cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
