## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salt
- hot water
- pepper
- flour
- dripping

## Directions

1. Boil tender in salted, hot water
2. Let them get cold
3. Scrape off the skin and slice lengthwise
4. Pepper and salt
5. Dredge with flour
6. Fry in hot dripping to a light brown
7. Drain and serve
