## Ingredients

- 1/2 peck green tomatoes
- 10 onions
- 1 head cabbage
- 1 cup salt
- 4 green peppers
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1/2 ounce celery seed
- 1/2 ounce tumeric powder
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Let stand 1/2 hour and drain well
2. Add green peppers, brown sugar, celery seed, and tumeric powder
3. Boil one hour in vinegar enough to cover
