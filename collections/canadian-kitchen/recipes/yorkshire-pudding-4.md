## Ingredients

- Pint new milk
- Two eggs
- Five tablespoons flour
- Pinch of salt
- Lots of melted dripping

## Directions

1. Stir milk and flour to batter, put in salt, add well-beaten eggs.
2. Have a shallow tin pan with lots of melted dripping boiling hot.
3. Pour in batter, bake half an hour in hot oven.
4. English cooks set the cooked pudding under the roast and allow it to catch some of the dripping just before serving.
