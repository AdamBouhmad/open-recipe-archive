## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon spice
- 1 egg
- Flour to make a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup butter, 2 cups molasses, 1 cup sour milk, 1 teaspoon soda, 1 tablespoon spice, and 1 egg
2. Add flour to make a stiff batter
3. Bake
