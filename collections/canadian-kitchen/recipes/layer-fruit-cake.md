## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 2 cups chopped seeded raisins or 1 pound chopped dates
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- a little nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- flour to make a soft batter

## Directions

1. Bake in layers and use any kind of nice filling
