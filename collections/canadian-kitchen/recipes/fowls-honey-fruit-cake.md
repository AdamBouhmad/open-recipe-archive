## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup honey
- 3/4 cup apple jelly or boiled cider
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teacup raisins
- 1 teacup dried currants
- flour enough to make a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Warm the butter, honey and apple jelly slightly
2. Add the beaten eggs
3. Add the soda dissolved in a little warm water
4. Add spices and flour enough to make a stiff batter
5. Stir in the fruit and bake in a slow oven
6. Keep in a covered jar several weeks before using
