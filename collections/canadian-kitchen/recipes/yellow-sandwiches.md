## Ingredients

- yolks of eggs
- annatto
- pepper
- salt
- thin buttered bread
- fringed yellow tissue paper mat

## Directions

1. Pound yolks of eggs well
2. Color deeply with a trifle of annatto
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Spread on thin buttered bread
5. Serve on plate covered with a fringed yellow tissue paper mat
