## Ingredients

- 2 cups honey
- 1 cup butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 good quart flour
- 1 level teaspoon soda or saleratus

## Directions

1. Mix honey, butter and eggs well together
2. Add buttermilk and mix
3. Stir in flour and soda, adding a little more flour if too thin to make proper consistency
4. Add flavoring if desired
5. Bake in a rather slow oven, as it burns very easily
6. To make cookies, use a little more flour so they will roll out well without sticking to the board
