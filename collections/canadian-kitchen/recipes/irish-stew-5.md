## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds of stewing meat
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- 3 medium-sized potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Season with salt and pepper
2. Cut up meat in small pieces
3. Put on to boil with carrots and turnips, cut in small squares
4. When nearly done, put in potatoes
5. A little parsley will give it a nice flavor
