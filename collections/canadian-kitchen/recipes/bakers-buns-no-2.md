## Ingredients

- 3 mashed potatoes
- 1 teaspoon coffee sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3/4 White Swan Yeast Cake
- 3/4 cup warm water
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 1 cup sugar
- butter size of an egg
- flour

## Directions

1. At noon mix together mashed potatoes, coffee sugar, and flour
2. Scald with 1 cup boiling water
3. When cool add White Swan Yeast Cake which has been dissolved in 3/4 cup warm water
4. Let stand till night
5. Take 1 pint sweet milk, 1 cup sugar, butter size of an egg, scald together and let cool
6. Stir in yeast and flour enough to make a stiff batter
7. Let rise until morning
8. In the morning mix down as for bread
9. Let stand until light
10. Mould into buns and let rise
11. Bake 20 minutes
