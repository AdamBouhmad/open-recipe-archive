## Ingredients

- tenderest cuts of beefsteak and mutton chops
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Select the tenderest cuts, and broil over a clear, hot fire
2. Let the steak be rare, the chops well done
3. Salt and pepper
4. Lay between two hot plates three minutes, and serve to your patient
5. If he is very weak, do not let him swallow anything except the juice, when he has chewed the meat well
