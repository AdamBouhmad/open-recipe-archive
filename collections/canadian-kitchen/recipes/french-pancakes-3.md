## Ingredients

- several slices of stale bread
- thick, sour milk
- 3 1/2 teaspoon soda scalded
- a little flour
- a little sweet milk
- an egg or two well beaten
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cover several slices of stale bread with thick, sour milk and let stand over night. Stir well and add soda scalded, a little flour, and if needed, a little sweet milk. Add an egg or two well beaten and butter. Bake on buttered pans.
