## Ingredients

- 1 large sifter full flour
- 1 large cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- a little salt
- good rich cream

## Directions

1. Sift all well together
2. Add a little salt and good rich cream to make into a dough
3. Roll out and bake in a hot oven
