## Ingredients

- 1 peck butter beans
- slightly salted water
- 1 cup mustard
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons turmeric
- cold vinegar
- 2 3/4 pints boiling vinegar
- 2 1/2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Take ends off beans
2. cook 1 hour in slightly salted water
3. drain
4. prepare dressing: 1 cup mustard, 1 cup flour, 2 tablespoons turmeric, mix with a little cold vinegar to a batter
5. add 2 3/4 pints boiling vinegar in which has been dissolved 2 1/2 pounds sugar
6. mix all together, mix 5 minutes
7. put in glass jars and seal
8. Pick nice yellow beans when tender
