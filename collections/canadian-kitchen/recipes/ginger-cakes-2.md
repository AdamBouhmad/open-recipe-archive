## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour, enough to roll out nicely

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar, molasses and butter
2. Let it cool
3. Add 1 egg, 1 teaspoon ginger, 1 teaspoon soda and flour, enough to roll out nicely
