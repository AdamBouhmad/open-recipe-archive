## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- butter size of an egg
- 1 heaping tablespoon flour
- 1 beaten egg
- 2 cups milk

## Directions

1. Cream together 3/4 cup sugar, butter size of an egg, and 1 heaping tablespoon flour
2. Add 1 beaten egg and 2 cups milk, and flavor to taste
3. Turn into an under crust and bake till firm
