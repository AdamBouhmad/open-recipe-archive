## Ingredients

- plain suet pudding
- 4 tablespoons molasses
- 2 teaspoons ground ginger

## Directions

1. Add to plain suet pudding 4 tablespoons molasses and 2 teaspoons ground ginger.
2. Serve with lemon sauce.
