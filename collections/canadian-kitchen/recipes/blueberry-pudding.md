## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 2 large tablespoonfuls sugar
- A little salt
- 1 cup milk (sour)
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- Flour to make stiff batter
- 1 large pint blueberries

## Directions

1. Stir in a large pint blueberries
2. Mix and put in buttered basin
3. Steam one hour and fifteen minutes
4. Serve hot with whipped cream
