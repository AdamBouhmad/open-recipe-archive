## Ingredients

- Three cups of brown sugar
- Half a cup of cream or milk
- A dessertspoonful of butter
- A tablespoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. When it comes to the boil add a dessertspoonful of butter
2. Let it boil for fifteen minutes, stirring just before taking off the fire
3. Put in tablespoonful of vanilla
4. Remove from the fire
5. Beat briskly for five minutes
6. Butter your plates
