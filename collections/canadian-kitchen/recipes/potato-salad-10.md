## Ingredients

- a salad bowl nearly full of hot mashed potatoes
- butter size of an egg
- milk
- 1 small onion chopped fine
- 1 teaspoon of mustard
- vinegar to taste
- salt and pepper
- 2 hard-boiled eggs chopped

## Directions

1. Add butter to mashed potatoes
2. Add milk to make it mix smoothly
3. Add 1 small onion chopped fine, 1 teaspoon of mustard, vinegar enough to taste
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add 2 hard-boiled eggs chopped
