## Ingredients

- allspice
- cinnamon
- 4 cups vinegar
- 7 pounds watermelon

## Directions

1. Boil watermelon until it can be pierced with a fork
2. Strain
3. Put in sealers
4. Boil together the other ingredients a few minutes
5. Pour over fruit while hot
6. Set away in a cool, dark place
