## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 cup syrup
- 1/2 cup lard
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in a little warm water
- flour to roll out thin

## Directions

1. Put all this together and let come to a boil, then add to this just before removing from the stove 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in a little warm water.
2. Let this mix, cool and then add enough flour to roll out thin.
