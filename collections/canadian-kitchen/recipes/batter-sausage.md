## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk
- salt
- baking powder
- flour
- sausages

## Directions

1. Mix together 3 eggs, 1 1/2 pints milk, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon baking powder
2. Add 1 cup flour and eggs, then add other ingredients
3. Put in pan and spread sausages on top of batter
4. Cook 1 hour
