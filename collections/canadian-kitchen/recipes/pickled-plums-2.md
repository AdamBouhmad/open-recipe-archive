## Ingredients

- 7 pounds plums
- 4 pounds sugar
- 2 ounces stick cinnamon
- 2 ounces cloves
- 1 quart vinegar
- a little mace

## Directions

1. Put in the jar, first a layer of plums, then add a layer of spices, alternately
2. Scald the vinegar and sugar together
3. Pour it over the plums
4. Repeat 3 times for plums (only once for cut fruit, as apples or pears)
5. The fourth time scald all together
6. Put in glass jars and seal for winter use
