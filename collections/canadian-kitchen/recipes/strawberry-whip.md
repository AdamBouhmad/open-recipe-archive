## Ingredients

- 1 cup strawberries
- 1 cup fine granulated sugar
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Crush strawberries in fine granulated sugar
2. Beat the white of egg stiff
3. mix together and beat 20 minutes
4. Sprinkle with strawberries
