## Ingredients

- 2 dozen large ripe tomatoes
- 6 large sour apples
- 6 onions
- 1 large head of celery
- 1 pint cider vinegar
- 2 cups brown sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scald and skin tomatoes and cut into quarters
2. Cut apples, onions and celery into thin slices
3. Put all into a kettle together with cider vinegar and brown sugar
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Cook until tender (about 1 1/2 hours)
6. Seal hot
