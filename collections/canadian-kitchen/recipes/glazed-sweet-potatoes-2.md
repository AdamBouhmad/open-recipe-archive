## Ingredients

- medium-sized potatoes
- sugar
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes and cut in halves
2. Lay evenly in a baking pan
3. Bake in a hot oven until tender and nicely browned
4. Baste while baking with a syrup made by boiling sugar, butter and water together for a few minutes
