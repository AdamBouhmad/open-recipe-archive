## Ingredients

- two cups fresh sweet corn or canned corn
- one tablespoonful salt
- one teaspoonful pepper
- two eggs
- one-quarter pound sifted flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Season corn with one tablespoonful of salt and a teaspoonful of pepper, add two eggs and a quarter of a pound of sifted flour
2. Do not beat the mixture, but stir vigorously with a wooden spoon for five minutes, till firm
3. Butter a frying pan and put in the preparation about a gill at a time
4. Be careful that the fritters do not touch one another, and fry a golden color
5. Serve hot in a folded napkin
