## Ingredients

- crabapples
- cold water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fill preserving kettle with crabs picked fresh from the tree
2. Pour in enough cold water to just come to the top of the apples
3. Set on the stove to boil until the apples are quite soft
4. Take a cup and dip the apples into the bag and then tie very tightly with a strong string
5. Carry the bag to the pantry or cellar where there is no dust or flies
6. Hang up the bag on a strong nail and leave to drip into the bowl all night
7. Next day boil the juice for about 20 minutes
8. Add the sugar, allowing one pound sugar to one pint juice
9. Boil until it will jelly when a little is cooled in a saucer
10. Pour into large-mouthed quart glass jars which have been heating in the oven
