## Ingredients

- chicken
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Prepare chicken as for stewing
2. Roll each piece in flour
3. Place in a small dripping pan
4. Sprinkle with pepper and salt and some butter
5. Fill the pan half full of sweet milk
6. Cook in the oven for 1 hour
7. The milk makes a nice gravy
