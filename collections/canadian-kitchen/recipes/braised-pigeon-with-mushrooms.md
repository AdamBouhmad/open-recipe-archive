## Ingredients

- pigeon
- crumbs
- chopped fat pork
- onion-juice
- salt
- pepper
- minced carrot
- onion
- celery
- parsley
- stock
- butter
- water
- fresh mushrooms
- brown roux

## Directions

1. Drain, wash, and stuff with a force-meat of crumbs and chopped fat pork, seasoned with onion-juice, salt, and pepper
2. Prepare the usual bed of vegetables — minced carrot, onion, celery, and parsley
3. Lay the pigeons upon it
4. Add a cupful of stock, or of butter and water
5. Cover and cook gently one hour, or until tender
6. Dish the birds and keep hot
7. Rub the gravy through a colander into a sauce-pan, season to taste
8. Add a dozen fresh mushrooms cut into small pieces, simmer five minutes
9. Thicken with a tablespoonful of brown roux, boil up and pour over the pigeons
