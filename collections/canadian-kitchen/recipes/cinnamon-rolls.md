## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- butter size of an egg
- brown sugar
- cinnamon
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Mix as little as possible
2. Roll thin
3. Spread with brown sugar, cinnamon and a little butter
4. Roll up and slice into 1/2-inch slices
5. Bake in medium oven
