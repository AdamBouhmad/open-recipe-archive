## Ingredients

- 1 quart sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- flour

## Directions

1. Bring milk, sugar, and butter to a boil
2. Allow the mixture to cool to lukewarm
3. Add 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
4. Stir in flour enough to make a thin batter
5. Cover warm and let stand over night
6. In the morning mix the same as bread (not very stiff)
7. Let rise
8. Roll out like biscuits
9. Spread with melted butter
10. Cut like biscuits and fold one side over on other and press down once
11. Put in tin
12. Let rise
13. Bake in moderate oven
