## Ingredients

- 1 cup maple syrup
- 1 pint whipped cream
- yolks of 4 eggs or 2 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the syrup and pour it over the well-beaten eggs
2. When cold, add to it the whipped cream
3. Pour into a covered dish and pack in ice and salt for 3 or 4 hours, using plenty salt
