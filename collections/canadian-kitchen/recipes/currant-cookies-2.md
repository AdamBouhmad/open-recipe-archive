## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to roll out

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out and bake
