## Ingredients

- lard or butter
- apples
- 1 cup cream
- 1 egg
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Grease a deep pie plate with lard or preferably butter
2. cut apples in small pieces, not thin slices, and spread over the bottom of plate
3. make a batter of following: 1 cup cream, 1 egg, 1 cup flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder, a pinch of salt
4. pour this over the apple and bake in a moderate oven till nicely browned
5. Serve with a dressing after the Jonathon has been turned upside down on a plate
