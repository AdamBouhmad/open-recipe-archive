## Ingredients

- 1 cup tapioca
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3 pints milk
- vanilla

## Directions

1. At night wash 1 cup tapioca and put it into a small tin pail, with 3 pints good milk
2. Stir and set away in cold place until morning
3. Then place pail with mixture in a pot of boiling water
4. Boil until tapioca is clear, stirring several times
5. Add sugar and last of all the eggs, which have been well beaten
6. Stir the eggs and flavoring in well, but do not boil after adding them
7. Pour into serving dish
8. Serve cold with cream and sugar
