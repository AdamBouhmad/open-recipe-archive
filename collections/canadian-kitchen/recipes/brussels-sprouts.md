## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- salt
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Pick over and remove wilted leaves
2. Soak in cold water 15 minutes
3. Cook in boiling water 20 minutes, add salt when partly cooked
4. Drain and add to white sauce
