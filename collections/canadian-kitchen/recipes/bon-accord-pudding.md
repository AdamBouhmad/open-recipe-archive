## Ingredients

- 4 good sized apples
- 1/4 lb. breadcrumbs
- 4 oz. sugar
- 4 oz. raisins
- a little salt and nutmeg
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Pare, core and mince apples quite fine.
2. Beat up and add eggs to the other ingredients, beating all well.
3. Put into a buttered mould, tie down with a cloth, and boil for an hour and a half.
4. Serve with sweet sauce.
