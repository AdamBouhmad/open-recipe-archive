## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sour milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in warm water
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup cornstarch
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup white sugar
- water
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. For dark part: mix brown sugar, butter, and sour milk
2. Add flour, raisins, soda dissolved in warm water, molasses, yolks of 3 eggs, cloves, and nutmeg
3. For white part: mix flour, cornstarch, sweet milk, butter, white sugar, and baking powder
4. Bake all in four layers
5. For boiled icing: boil 1 cup white sugar in water enough to dissolve it till it will hair
6. Beat the white of 1 egg to a stiff froth
7. Pour the hot sugar on the froth
8. Flavor to suit the taste and beat till cool enough to stay on the cake
9. Put layers together with boiled icing
