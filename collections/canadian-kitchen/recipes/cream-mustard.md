## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon dry mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 cup vinegar on the stove to boil
2. Mix 1 tablespoon dry mustard, 1 teaspoon salt, 2 eggs, and 3/4 cup sweet cream
3. Beat this well
4. Pour into boiling vinegar
5. Stir whole mixture over boiling water until like thick cream
