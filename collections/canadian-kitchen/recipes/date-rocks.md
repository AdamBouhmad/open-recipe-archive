## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter and lard mixed
- 1½ cups brown sugar
- 1 pound chopped dates
- ¾ pound chopped walnuts
- 3 eggs
- 1 level teaspoon soda dissolved in 1 tablespoon water
- 1 tablespoon cassia
- ¾ grated nutmeg
- flour to make a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Drop on buttered tin in large teaspoons
