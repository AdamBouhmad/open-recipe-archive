## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef
- 0.5 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 cup boiling water
- 1 small onion, chopped fine
- 1 egg
- 0.5 cup flour
- 0.5 cup drippings

## Directions

1. Chop meat fine
2. Add seasonings
3. Beat egg and mix with meat
4. Shape in round cakes about 1 inch thick
5. Dredge with flour
6. Fry in dripping until browned
7. Add 0.5 cup boiling water
8. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes
