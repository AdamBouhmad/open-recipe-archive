## Ingredients

- pie paste
- tart quartered apples
- 3 1/2 cup flour
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 sour cream

## Directions

1. Line a pie pan with pie paste
2. lay in side by side tart quartered apples
3. sprinkle over this 3 1/2 cup flour, 3/4 cup sugar, and 3/4 sour cream
4. Bake a rich brown without upper crust
