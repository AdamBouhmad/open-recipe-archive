## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of rhubarb (the red kind)
- 4 pounds of loaf sugar
- 5 ounces whole ginger

## Directions

1. Peel and cut up the rhubarb into small pieces
2. Add the sugar and ginger
3. Boil until clear
4. Pot and tie down as for other preserves
