## Ingredients

- 1 pk. ripe tomatoes
- 6 red peppers
- 1/2 lb. salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls black pepper
- 1/2 pt. vinegar
- 1/2 lb. mustard
- 1 oz. ginger
- 1 oz. allspice
- 1 oz. mace
- 1 oz. cloves
- 2 onions
- a few cloves of garlic
- 1 tablespoonful red pepper

## Directions

1. Take out stalks of tomatoes and boil for one hour with red peppers.
2. Strain through a colander and add salt, black pepper, ginger, allspice, mace, cloves, and garlic, two onions.
3. Boil for one hour.
4. When cold add vinegar, red pepper, and mustard.
