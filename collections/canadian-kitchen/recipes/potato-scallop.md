## Ingredients

- potatoes peeled and sliced very thin
- onions thin sliced
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Grease a deep pudding dish and place in the bottom a layer of potatoes, peeled, and sliced very thin
2. Add a layer of thin sliced onions, then a layer of bread crumbs
3. Sprinkle some pepper over this and a little pinch of salt
4. Repeat with layer of potatoes until dish is full, having the potatoes on top
5. Cover with sweet milk and bake in a hot oven for an hour, or until the potatoes can be pierced with a fork
