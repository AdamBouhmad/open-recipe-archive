## Ingredients

- 1 lb. apples
- 1 teaspoonful red pepper
- 8 oz. tomatoes
- 2 oz. ginger
- 1 oz. salt (or 1 oz.)
- 2 oz. garlic
- 8 oz. sugar
- 2 oz. onions
- 8 oz. raisins
- 3 pts. vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop apples, onions, tomatoes, and raisins; mix spices and vinegar with them; put in a warm place and stir twice a day for a fortnight.
