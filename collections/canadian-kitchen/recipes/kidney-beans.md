## Ingredients

- fresh or dried kidney beans
- salted boiling water
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. If fresh, cook them as you would Lima beans
2. If dried, soak over night, and put over the fire in the morning in salted boiling water, and cook gently one hour, or until soft, but not broken
3. Drain, stir in pepper, salt, and a lump of butter, and serve
