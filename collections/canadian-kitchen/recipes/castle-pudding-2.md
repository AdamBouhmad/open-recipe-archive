## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- their weight in butter
- their weight in sugar
- their weight in flour
- 3/4 teaspoon baking powder (Royal)

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Add the eggs well beaten
3. Stir in the flour, having the powder well mixed with it
4. Bake in buttered darioles (long-shaped tins) for 20 minutes
5. Turn out and serve with jam and jee poured round
