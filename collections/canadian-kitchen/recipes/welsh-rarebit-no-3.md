## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- thin layers of cheese

## Directions

1. Take stale bread, cut in slices, butter it
2. Put in a baking pan butter side down
3. Cut thin layers of cheese and lay on your slices of bread
4. Put into the oven and bake until the cheese begins to spread
5. Have your oven hot and serve at once when removed from the oven
