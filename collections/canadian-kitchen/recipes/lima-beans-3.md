## Ingredients

- Lima beans
- boiling water
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. After shelling, cook about half an hour in boiling water with a little salt
2. Drain dry
3. After dishing stir in a lump of butter half the size of an egg and pepper and salt to taste
