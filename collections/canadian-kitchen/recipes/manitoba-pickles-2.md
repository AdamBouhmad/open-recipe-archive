## Ingredients

- 1 dozen cucumbers (table size)
- 1 dozen onions or equal quantity
- weak brine
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 dessertspoon turmeric
- 1 dessertspoon curry powder

## Directions

1. Pare and slice cucumbers in 1/4-inch rings
2. Let cucumbers and onions stand for 24 hours in weak brine
3. Dissolve 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 dessertspoon turmeric, and 1 dessertspoon curry powder in cold vinegar
4. Heat 1 pint vinegar and 2 cups brown sugar together until boiling and well blended
5. Add the spice mixture to the vinegar and sugar
6. Well drain the pickles and add them to the boiling dressing
7. Allow to come to a scald
8. Bottle hot
