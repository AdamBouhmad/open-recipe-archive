## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls sherry wine
- 4 tablespoonfuls brandy
- strawberry or raspberry jam
- 1 qt. cream
- 1/2 lb. macaroons
- 1 doz. lady fingers
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- 1/2 teacup seeded raisins
- 1/2 cupful chopped, blanched almonds
- any kind of stale cake, sponge or pound cake preferred

## Directions

1. In a dish put first a layer of cake, then a little brandy, then jam, then cake, wine, raisins and nuts, also a little cream whipped.
2. Begin again as before, then add a few macaroons and make a wall of lady fingers.
3. After all the cake, wine and raisins are used, over the top put plenty of whipped cream (stiff).
