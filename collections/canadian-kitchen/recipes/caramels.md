## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- Butter size of egg
- 1 cup brown sugar
- Vanilla flavoring
- 1 cup cream or milk

## Directions

1. Beat all together
2. Boil until it thickens in water
3. Turn into flat tins
4. When nearly cold cut into small squares
