## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 1 cup salt
- 2 quarts water
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 2½ pounds brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons mustard seed
- 2 tablespoons allspice
- 2 tablespoons ginger
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 2 large tablespoons cinnamon

## Directions

1. Slice 1 peck green tomatoes
2. Sprinkle 1 cup salt
3. Leave over night
4. Drain well
5. Add 2 quarts water and 1 pint vinegar
6. Boil 15 minutes
7. Drain again
8. Add 2 quarts vinegar, 2½ pounds brown sugar, 2 tablespoons each of mustard seed, allspice, ginger, mustard, cloves, and 2 large tablespoons cinnamon
9. Mix and boil 15 minutes
