## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes
- butter
- eggs
- sweet cream or milk

## Directions

1. Take 2 cups cold mashed potatoes
2. Stir in 2 tablespoons melted butter
3. Beat to a fluffy cream
4. Add 2 well-beaten eggs, 1 cup sweet cream or milk
5. Beat all well together
6. Pour into deep pudding dish
7. Bake in a quick oven till nicely browned
