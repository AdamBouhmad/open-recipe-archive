## Ingredients

- 3 oz. cheese
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 4 oz. grated bread
- 1 dessertspoonful mustard
- 3 oz. butter
- A little salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grate cheese, and mix it with the yolks of eggs, bread, and butter
2. Beat the whole together in a mortar with made mustard, a little salt and some pepper
3. Toast some slices of bread, cut off the outside crust, cut it in shapes and spread the paste thick upon them
4. Put them in the oven, let them become hot and slightly browned
5. Serve hot as possible
