## Ingredients

- 15 onions
- 2 large head of cabbage
- salt
- 1 gallon white wine vinegar
- 3 pounds sugar
- ½ pound mustard
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1 cup white pepper
- ½ cup cinnamon
- 3½ cup tumeric

## Directions

1. Chop onions and cabbage as fine as mincemeat
2. Put into salt water over night
3. In the morning drain off the water
4. Put the mixture on the stove with 1 gallon white wine vinegar, 3 pounds sugar, ½ pound mustard, 1 ounce celery seed, 1 cup white pepper, ½ cup cinnamon, 3½ cup tumeric
5. Bring to a boil
6. Pack in jars
