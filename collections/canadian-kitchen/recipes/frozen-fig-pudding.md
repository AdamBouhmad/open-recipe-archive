## Ingredients

- 1 quart sweet milk
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 1 pound English walnuts
- 1 pound figs

## Directions

1. Scald the milk
2. Beat eggs, add sugar
3. Pour hot milk into the eggs and cook until it coats the spoon
4. Add the other ingredients
5. Freeze
