## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup boiled rice
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 pint cream
- 3/4 pound raisins
- 1/4 pound peel
- sugar to suit taste

## Directions

1. Combine boiled rice, eggs, nutmeg, cream, raisins and peel
2. Add sugar to taste
3. Bake until done
