## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes (grated or ground in a food chopper)
- 1 egg well beaten
- a little salt
- grease

## Directions

1. Grate or grind potatoes in a food chopper, and add 1 egg well beaten, and a little salt
2. Fry like griddle cakes, only use a little more grease and have it very hot
3. Use 1 large spoonful for 1 pancake
