## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound breadcrumbs
- one-quarter pound brown sugar
- one pound apples
- two ounces suet, chopped
- grated rind of one lemon

## Directions

1. Butter a pie dish and sprinkle it with sugar.
2. Mix suet and bread-crumbs together.
3. Put a layer of apple in small pieces, sugar and rind of lemon, then suet and crumbs.
4. Repeat until dish is full.
5. Bake thirty or forty minutes.
6. Turn out.
