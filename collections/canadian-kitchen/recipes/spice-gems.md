## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoonful ginger
- ½ teaspoonful each of other spices
- 1 egg
- ½ cup molasses
- ½ cup milk
- Flour to make stiff batter that will cling to spoon
- Small teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put in ingredients in order mentioned.
2. Dissolve small teaspoonful of carbonate of soda in a very little boiling water; add to the batter a pinch of salt.
3. Mix thoroughly, and bake in buttered gem-pans in moderate oven.
