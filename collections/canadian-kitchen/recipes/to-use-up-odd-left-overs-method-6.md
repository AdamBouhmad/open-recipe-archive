## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- meat cooked
- gravy or stewed tomatoes
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Put a small piece of butter in a frying pan and slice an onion in
2. When cooked add slices of meat cooked
3. Cover closely a few minutes until meat is thoroughly heated
4. If you have left over gravy, add it and season to taste; if not add stewed tomatoes
5. Let come to boil and serve hot
