## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. brown sugar
- 1/4 lb. chocolate
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 small cup cold water
- 2 teaspoonfuls vanilla

## Directions

1. Put on the fire brown sugar, chocolate broken into small pieces, and a small cupful of cold water
2. Boil this until a little of it hardens in water
3. Stir into it butter and vanilla
4. Turn into buttered pans and cut into squares
