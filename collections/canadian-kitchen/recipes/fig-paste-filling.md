## Ingredients

- 1 lb. figs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Put figs into a bowl and pour boiling water over them, letting stand till soft, then cutting into small pieces with scissors; first cut off all the small hard pieces, and then chop figs until they become a thick paste
2. add sugar and water, and cook till thick and clear
