## Ingredients

- 1 gallon green tomatoes
- 1 large cabbage
- 10 onions
- 4 green peppers
- 2 heads celery
- 1 teacup salt
- 1 ounce tumeric
- 3/4 pound mustard seed
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 gallon vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop all fine and mix with salt
2. Let stand 1 hour
3. Drain and put in a kettle
4. Add tumeric, mustard seed, brown sugar, and vinegar
5. Boil and seal
