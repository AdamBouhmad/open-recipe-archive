## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Peel and slice in medium slices as many potatoes as required
2. Add a few onions, peeled and cut up
3. Add pepper and salt and a few bits of butter
4. Put all in a dish, fill up with water and either bake in the oven or set on top of the stove and cover closely
