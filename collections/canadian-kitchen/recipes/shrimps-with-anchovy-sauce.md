## Ingredients

- 1 can shrimps
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 gill cream
- 1 teaspoonful anchovy paste
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 saltspoonful cayenne

## Directions

1. Melt together the butter and anchovy, lay in the shrimps, pepper them, and saute until they are hot through
2. Break the eggs in a bowl, beat the cream into them, and pour into the chafing-dish
3. Stir two or three minutes, until the sauce thickens, and serve at once on toast. This dish should be prepared over hot water
