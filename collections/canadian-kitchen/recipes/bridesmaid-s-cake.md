## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. butter
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- Whites of 14 eggs
- 1/2 lb. almonds
- 1 glass sherry
- A little rose water and mace
- 3/4 lb. flour

## Directions

1. Butter and sugar beaten to a cream, the whites of eggs beaten to a stiff froth, almonds pounded fine, with rose water and sherry, and a little mace, are made into a paste; rub this into three-quarter pounds of flour, and add lightly and quickly to the other ingredients already mixed.
2. The almonds, paste, and flour should be prepared first.
3. Bake in moderate oven.
