## Ingredients

- 2 pounds currants
- 2 pounds stoned raisins
- 2 pounds suet
- 2 pounds flour (dry it)
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1/2 pound bread crumbs
- 1 pound candied fruit
- 2 grated nutmegs
- 1/2 ounce mixed spice
- 15 eggs
- juice of 2 lemons
- pinch of salt
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Mix stiff with a little milk
2. Boil 8 hours
3. Boil again when wanted
4. Will keep for months
