## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 3/4 cups sour milk
- 3 teaspoons ginger
- 2 teaspoons cloves
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons nutmeg
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound raisins
- 2 3/4 teaspoons soda
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Combine sugar and butter
2. Add eggs
3. Add molasses and sour milk
4. Add ginger, cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg
5. Add flour
6. Add fruit last
7. Bake in a slow oven
