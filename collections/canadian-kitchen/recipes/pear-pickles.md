## Ingredients

- pears
- white wine vinegar
- white sugar
- whole spice
- cloves

## Directions

1. Peel pears and stick a clove in blossom end
2. Have white wine vinegar scalding with enough white sugar to make pretty sweet
3. Have mixed whole spice in a bag
4. Put in pears and cook till tender
5. Take out pears in bottles and pour the boiling vinegar over and seal
