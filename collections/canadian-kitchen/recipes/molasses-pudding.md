## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup Barbados molasses
- 2 cups flour
- a little salt
- spices to suit taste

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Steam 3 hours.
3. Serve with sauce.
