## Ingredients

- 1/2 dozen cooking apples
- butter
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- 1 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Pare and slice fine cooking apples
2. Butter a pudding dish
3. Put in a layer 1/2 inch thick of bread crumbs
4. Add bits of butter
5. Put in a layer of chopped apples, sugar and nutmeg
6. Repeat till the dish is full
7. Pour over the whole a cup of cold water
8. Bake for 30 minutes
9. Serve with cream
