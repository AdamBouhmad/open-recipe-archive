## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. flour
- 1 lb. butter
- 1½ lbs. brown sugar
- 8 eggs, beaten separately
- 2 teacups milk
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 3 lbs. raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1 lb. mixed peel
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls ground cinnamon
- 4 teaspoonfuls lemon extract
- ½ nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Bake between three and four hours
