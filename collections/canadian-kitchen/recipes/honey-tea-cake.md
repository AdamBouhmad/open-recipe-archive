## Ingredients

- 1 cup honey
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 cups flour
- scant 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon cream tartar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake 30 minutes in a moderate oven
