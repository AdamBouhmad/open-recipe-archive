## Ingredients

- Medium-sized cucumbers
- Salted water
- French dressing
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Peel medium-sized cucumbers. Slice across as thin as possible into salted water, in which leave for several hours.
2. Take out and drain, place in towel and wring gently until dry.
3. Turn into salad dish and toss lightly in French dressing (oil, vinegar, salt and pepper), and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
