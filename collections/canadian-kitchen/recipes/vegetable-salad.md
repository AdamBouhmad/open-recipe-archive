## Ingredients

- 2 boiled beets
- 1 cup celery
- 1 parsnip
- 1 carrot
- salt
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Chop the beets fine
2. Chop the celery fine
3. Boil and chop fine the parsnip and carrot
4. Boil another parsnip and carrot and cut oblong
5. Lay the oblong pieces alternately for garnishing
6. Season with salt and whipped cream for dressing
7. Cool and serve at once
