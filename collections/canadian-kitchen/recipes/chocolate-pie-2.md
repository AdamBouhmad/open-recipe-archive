## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cowan's Perfection Chocolate
- a little boiling water
- 2 small cups of new milk
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 egg
- a little cold milk
- 2½ teaspoons of sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Grate Cowan's Perfection Chocolate
2. Dissolve in a cup with a little boiling water
3. Have in your dish 2 small cups of new milk, then add the melted chocolate
4. When this boils add 3 tablespoons flour, ½ cup white sugar, yolk of 1 egg
5. Make smooth with a little of the cold milk
6. Beat white of the egg for the top with 2½ teaspoons of sugar and ½ teaspoon vanilla
7. Place on pie and brown
