## Ingredients

- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 quart water

## Directions

1. To every 3 pounds sugar allow 1 quart water, and boil for 1/4 hour
2. Draw kettle to back of stove, where mixture will not boil
3. Let it remain till you have jars filled with fruit
4. After jars are filled stand on a wet cloth and fill with the hot syrup
5. Seal and put a sack or cloth in a tub
6. Put jars in and pour boiling water in until jars are covered
7. Let stand until cool
