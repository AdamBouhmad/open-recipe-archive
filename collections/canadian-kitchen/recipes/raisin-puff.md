## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup raisins, chopped
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder sifted in 2 cups flour
- 1 teacupful milk

## Directions

1. Steam one hour in an ordinary brown bread steamer.
2. Serve with whipped cream or cold sauce.
