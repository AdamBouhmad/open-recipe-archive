## Ingredients

- one pint milk
- butter size of walnut
- one heaping tablespoonful flour
- one cup white sugar
- one lemon
- one cup blanched almonds
- four eggs, beaten separately

## Directions

1. Boil milk; keep a wineglass milk cold and mix with flour
2. Pour hot milk on this and stir thoroughly until thick
3. Let cool
4. Add grated rind of lemon, butter, and white sugar, blanched almonds cut in about eight pieces, the yolks of eggs well beaten, and whites of two eggs
5. Mix all these ingredients well and pour into a buttered pudding dish
6. Bake until a light gold color
7. Ice with two whites left, juice of a lemon and a little white sugar, well beaten
8. Return to oven until icing is stiff
9. Allow pudding to become quite cold
