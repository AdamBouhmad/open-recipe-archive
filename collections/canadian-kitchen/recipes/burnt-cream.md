## Ingredients

- one quart milk
- four tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- one pound brown sugar
- one-half pint cream, whipped
- a few walnut meats

## Directions

1. Boil milk, stir in cornstarch, add a few broken walnuts.
2. Put in a saucepan one pound of brown sugar, let it brown as dark as possible, then add the milk.
3. After the milk is thick beat well together and turn into a mould.
4. Decorate with half walnuts around, or solitaire moulds with a half walnut on top, whipped cream around dish.
