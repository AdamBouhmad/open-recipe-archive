## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 lemon peel
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 2 teaspoons nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons cassia
- 3 teaspoons syrup
- 1 teaspoon soda in 2 teaspoons warm water
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 layers and put together with lemon filling
