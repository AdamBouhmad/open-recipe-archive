## Ingredients

- rich crust
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- butter size of egg
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Line tins with rich crust
2. Add well-beaten eggs mixed with sugar, butter, currants and vanilla
3. Bake light brown
