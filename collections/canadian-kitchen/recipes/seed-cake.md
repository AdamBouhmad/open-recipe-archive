## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar (scant)
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Whites 6 eggs
- A heaping teaspoonful caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar, add milk, half the stiffly beaten whites and half the flour and baking powder, then the rest of the whites and flour and the seeds.
2. Beat well and bake in two small loaves or one large one.
3. Ice while warm.
