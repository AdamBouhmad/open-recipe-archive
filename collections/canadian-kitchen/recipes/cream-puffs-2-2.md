## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup flour
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Put butter and water on the stove
2. While boiling add the flour, stirring all the time till cooked
3. When cool, add 4 eggs
4. Drop in small spoonfuls on a buttered pan
5. Bake 25 minutes in a hot oven
6. When cool, split 1 side
7. Fill with cream filling or whipped cream
