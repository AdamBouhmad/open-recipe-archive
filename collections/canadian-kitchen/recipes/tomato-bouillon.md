## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 2 qts. stock
- 1 tablespoonful chopped onion
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 whole cloves
- 1 level teaspoonful celery seed
- Whites of 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes and stock over fire, add onion, bay leaves, cloves, celery and pepper
2. Cover and cook twenty minutes
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Beat whites of eggs till partly light, add these to tomato, bring to boil and boil rapidly five minutes
5. Strain through two thicknesses of cheesecloth
6. Re-heat, season with salt and serve with tiny cubes of toasted bread
