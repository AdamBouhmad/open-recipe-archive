## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- cream
- oysters
- eggs

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing for the craw of chopped bread and butter, cream, oysters and the yolks of eggs
2. Sew it in and dredge flour over the turkey
3. Put it to boil in cold water, with a spoonful of salt and enough water to cover them well
4. Let it simmer for 2 hours, or, if small, less time
5. Skim it while boiling
6. It looks nicer if wrapped in a cloth dredged with flour
7. Serve it with drawn butter, in which put some oysters
