## Ingredients

- 3 large cauliflowers
- 60 small cucumbers
- 1 qt. string beans
- 4 green peppers
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup flour
- 6 tablespoonfuls mustard
- 3 qts. cider vinegar
- 1 oz. turmeric
- 2 qts. white onions (pickles)
- 3 qts. green tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut up tomatoes, cutting out all imperfections
2. Cut up with them the four peppers, throwing away the seeds
3. Put these in a strong brine for an hour, then drain and put on to cook till tender in fresh, hot water
4. In another kettle put the cauliflowers, broken into small pieces
5. In another kettle put the peeled onions
6. When all are well done drain
7. Put in the vinegar in a large kettle
8. Take the mustard, flour and turmeric, and stir into a smooth paste, with a little of the vinegar
9. Stir into the rest of the vinegar and bring it to a boil
10. Put all the ingredients into this liquid
11. Cook slowly an hour or more, stirring often
12. It should be thick and of an even consistency
