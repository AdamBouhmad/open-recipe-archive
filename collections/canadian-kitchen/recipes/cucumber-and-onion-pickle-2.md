## Ingredients

- 12 ripe cucumbers
- 8 large onions
- salt
- vinegar
- 1 tablespoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon tumeric powder
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3 cups white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers and cut lengthwise, taking out all seeds
2. Slice very thin across each piece
3. Cut onions in slices
4. Put a layer of each and sprinkle with salt
5. Let stand 2 hours
6. Drain through a colander
7. Put in a kettle with barely enough vinegar to cover
8. Add 1 tablespoon white pepper, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 tablespoon tumeric powder, 1 tablespoon flour, 3 cups white sugar
9. Mix all with a little vinegar
10. Boil slowly 1 hour
