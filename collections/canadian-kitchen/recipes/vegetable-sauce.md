## Ingredients

- 1 cup vegetable water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- sprinkle pepper

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour and stir over the fire until frothy
2. Add vegetable water and stir constantly until it thickens and boils a minute
3. Pour over the vegetable, allowing 1 cup of sauce to 2 cups of vegetable pulp
