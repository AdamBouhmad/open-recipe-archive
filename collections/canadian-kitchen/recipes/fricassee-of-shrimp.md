## Ingredients

- one quart tomatoes
- one quart water
- onion
- butter
- flour
- two cans shrimps
- rice

## Directions

1. stew one quart of tomatoes, one quart of water, and onion to suit the taste together until the tomatoes can be passed through a sieve
2. after steaming stew with seasoning (season highly), and a tablespoonful of butter creamed with a little flour, for fifteen minutes
3. add two cans of shrimps, carefully washed
4. heat thoroughly and serve with rice
