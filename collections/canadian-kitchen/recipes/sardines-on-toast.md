## Ingredients

- Sardines
- cayenne
- lemon-juice
- hot-buttered toast
- anchovy paste

## Directions

1. Scrape and bone the sardines
2. Lay them on a plate
3. Sprinkle them with lemon-juice and a little cayenne pepper
4. Stand them in the oven until thoroughly hot
5. Have ready some neat slices of hot-buttered toast
6. Lay the sardines on these and serve at once
7. This dish may be varied by spreading the toast with anchovy paste before laying on the sardines
