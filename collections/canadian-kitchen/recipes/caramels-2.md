## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. brown sugar
- Butter size of egg
- 1 cake chocolate
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate and dissolve
2. Then add the sugar and butter and boil until they will harden in cold water
3. Put into a buttered pan
4. Work off in squares when half cold
