## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- butter size of a nut
- 3/4 cup sugar
- pepper to taste
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. Add mustard, salt, butter, sugar and pepper
3. Cook until thick
4. Thin with milk or cream for chicken
