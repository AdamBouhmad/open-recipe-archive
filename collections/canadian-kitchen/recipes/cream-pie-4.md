## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful sugar
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- 1 cupful flour
- 2.5 cups milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 0.5 cupful sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and baking powder together.
2. Beat the yolks and sugar together.
3. Add the flour and lastly the whipped whites of the eggs.
4. Bake this cake mixture in two layers.
5. For the cream filling, scald the milk and turn it on to the beaten egg.
6. Return it to the fire and add the flour moistened with a little milk, and the sugar, and stir until thickened.
7. Let it cool before adding it to the cake.
8. Place between the cake layers when cold, and just before serving, a thick layer of whipped cream or the cream filling.
9. Have the top piece covered with a boiled icing.
