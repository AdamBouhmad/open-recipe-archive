## Ingredients

- 2 gallons green tomatoes sliced without peeling
- 12 good-sized onions sliced
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 2 quarts brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons salt
- ground mustard
- ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon allspice

## Directions

1. Slice green tomatoes without peeling
2. Slice 12 good-sized onions
3. Mix all together with vinegar, brown sugar, salt, ground mustard, ground black pepper, cloves, and allspice
4. Stew until tender, stirring often lest they scorch
5. Put in glass jars
