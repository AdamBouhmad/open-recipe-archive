## Ingredients

- four eggs
- quarter pound of ground almonds
- quarter pound of powdered sugar
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs until very light
2. Add the sugar and almonds gently
3. Beat till it bubbles
4. Put in a greased dish
5. Put blanched almonds on top
6. Bake in rather a moderate oven
