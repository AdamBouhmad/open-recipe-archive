## Ingredients

- quail
- salad oil
- lemon-juice
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- buttered toast
- hot stock

## Directions

1. Draw, wipe, and split down the back
2. Leave them in a marinade of salad oil and lemon-juice for half an hour
3. Without wiping, broil on a wire bird-broiler for ten minutes, turning twice
4. Butter, salt, and pepper them
5. Serve on squares of buttered toast, upon each of which has been poured a teaspoonful of hot stock
