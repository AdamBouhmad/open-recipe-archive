## Ingredients

- bacon
- chicken
- flour
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place a layer of bacon in bottom of stew kettle, then a layer of chicken, season and sprinkle a little flour, and so on until you have sufficient
2. Fill kettle with water until within 1 inch of top of meat
3. Cover tightly and cook about 2 hours
4. Do not stir, only turn in round in the pot
5. Skim off grease
6. Make gravy with butter and a little flour
