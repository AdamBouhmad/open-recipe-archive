## Ingredients

- 1 dozen ears sweet corn
- 1 cabbage head
- 4 onions
- 2 red peppers
- 3 pints of vinegar
- 3/4 pound mustard
- 2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Remove corn from cob
2. Chop together corn, cabbage
