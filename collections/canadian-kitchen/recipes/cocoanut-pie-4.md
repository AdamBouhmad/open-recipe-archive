## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cocoanut
- 2 tablespoons flour
- about 1 1/2 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar, cocoanut, flour, and milk together
2. Bake with an under crust
