## Ingredients

- 2 cups warmed milk
- 1/4 cup old-fashioned yeast
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoonful cloves, cinnamon and allspice
- 3 eggs
- 1 lb. seeded raisins
- Flour to make smooth, soft dough
- 1 cake of compressed yeast can be used

## Directions

1. Mix warm milk, flour to make a soft dough, a quarter of a cup of old-fashioned yeast; let it stand in a warm place till light.
2. Then add butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, cloves and allspice, eggs, raisins and bread flour enough to make it smooth and soft.
3. Dough kneaded well; put in a warm place to rise.
4. When light mould in a loaf; let rise again.
5. When it has risen to the top of the pan bake in a moderate oven for one hour.
