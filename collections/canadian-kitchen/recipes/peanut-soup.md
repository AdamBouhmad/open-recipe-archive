## Ingredients

- One quart of rich milk
- One large cupful of peanuts, shelled and skinned
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Red pepper
- One cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Put milk on to cook in a double boiler; add salt to taste, and season highly with black and red pepper
2. Add the peanuts, which have been put through a meat chopper two or three times until they are ground fine
3. Cook twenty or thirty minutes
4. Just before taking from the fire add a cupful of cream
5. Strain
