## Ingredients

- whites of 2 eggs
- 3/4 pound granulated sugar
- pinch of salt
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- butter
- Devonshire or whipped cream
- strawberries or raspberries, or glace cherries, chopped pistachio nuts or cocoanut

## Directions

1. Whip the whites of eggs until very stiff
2. add granulated sugar, pinch of salt, lemon juice
3. mould with dessert spoon on buttered paper
4. dry in a slow oven
5. When dry, scrape the inside part out and fill with Devonshire or whipped cream
6. When strawberries or raspberries are in season, garnish with these; when not in season, use glace cherries, chopped pistachio nuts or cocoanut
