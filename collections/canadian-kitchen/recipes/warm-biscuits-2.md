## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon soda
- buttermilk enough to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Sift baking powder and soda into flour
2. Mix butter into flour
3. Add the buttermilk
4. Bake in a quick oven
