## Ingredients

- ½ pound of macaroni broken an inch in length
- water
- salt
- grated cheese
- pepper
- butter
- 1 cup sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan about ½ pound of macaroni broken an inch in length with water sufficient to boil tender
2. Add a little salt
3. When done, drain and put into a well-buttered dish in layers
4. Sprinkle grated cheese very liberally over each layer
5. Use pepper, salt and butter
6. When the dish is full pour over one cup sweet cream
7. Bake in a moderate oven about 25 minutes
8. Serve hot
