## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Drain salmon and remove bones and skin
2. Heat milk and butter together
3. Thicken with flour smoothed in part of the cold milk
4. Mix this with the salmon and part of the bread crumbs
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
6. Put remainder of bread crumbs on top
7. Dot with butter
8. Bake 20 minutes
