## Ingredients

- sweet peppers
- force-meat of tongue, chicken, ham, or veal
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Make an incision in one side
2. Extract the seeds through this with a bit of stick
3. Stuff with a force-meat of tongue, chicken, ham, or veal, mixed up with boiled rice
