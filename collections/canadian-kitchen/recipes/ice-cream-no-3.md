## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 pint cream
- 1 quart milk
- 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites and yolks separately
2. Beat 2 cups granulated sugar with yolks
3. Mix all well together: beaten eggs, sugar, 1 pint cream, 1 quart milk, and 1 tablespoon vanilla
4. Freeze
5. This makes 1 gallon ice cream
