## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup cocoa
- a pinch of salt
- 1 cup milk
- a lump of butter the size of an egg
- 1/2 teaspoon cream tartar
- vanilla
- nuts

## Directions

1. Stir sugar, cocoa, and salt well together
2. Add milk a little at a time, stirring thoroughly
3. When well blended, place on stove over a brisk flame and stir constantly
4. When candy will not separate upon being dropped into cold water but will adhere together like gum on the bottom, just before removing from stove add butter
5. When butter has melted add cream tartar
6. When candy foams up in kettle remove from fire at once and add vanilla and beat briskly until stiff
7. Nuts may be added after it has been beaten for a few moments
8. Pour into a buttered plate and mark in squares when cold
