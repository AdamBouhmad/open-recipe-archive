## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt well together
2. Beat 1 egg and 1 tablespoon butter light, add 3/4 cup sweet milk
3. Stir all together, roll out, cut and put on hot, well-greased griddle
4. Bake 20 minutes, turn
