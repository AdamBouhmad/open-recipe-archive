## Ingredients

- 1 pint stewed apples sweetened and cooled
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 4 eggs beaten well
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix the apples, milk and eggs
2. Put in baking dish
3. Grate a little nutmeg over the top
4. Bake 3/4 an hour
