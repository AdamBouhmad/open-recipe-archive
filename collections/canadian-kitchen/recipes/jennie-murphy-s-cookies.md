## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup lard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together and bake.
