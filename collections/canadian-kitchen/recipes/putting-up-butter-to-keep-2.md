## Ingredients

- pure common salt
- white sugar
- saltpetre
- butter
- brine
- egg

## Directions

1. Take of the best pure, common salt two quarts, one ounce of white sugar and one of saltpetre
2. Pulverize them together completely
3. Work the butter well, then thoroughly work in an ounce of this mixture to every pound of butter
4. Make the butter into half-pound rolls
5. Put into a brine made of three gallons of brine strong enough to bear an egg, with a quarter of a pound of white sugar added
