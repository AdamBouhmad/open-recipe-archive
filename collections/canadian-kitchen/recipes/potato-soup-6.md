## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 2 stalks of celery
- 1 small onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 cup of mashed potatoes
- A piece of butter the size of an egg
- Salt and pepper
- A little cornstarch

## Directions

1. To one quart of milk add two stalks of celery, one small onion and one bay leaf
2. Put in a double saucepan and let come to the boil
3. Strain, add a cup of mashed potatoes and a piece of butter the size of an egg
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
5. Thicken with a little cornstarch and serve very hot
