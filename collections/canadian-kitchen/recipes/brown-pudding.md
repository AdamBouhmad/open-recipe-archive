## Ingredients

- Weight of 2 eggs in flour
- Weight of 2 eggs in butter
- Weight of 1 egg in sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls raspberry jam
- 1 teaspoonful baking soda

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and eggs to a cream
2. Beat jam till all bubbles
3. Add flour and half a teaspoonful carbonate of soda
4. Steam one hour and three-quarters
5. For sauce: Whip yolks of three eggs with two tablespoonfuls castor sugar and one cup cooking sherry in a double saucepan, just a minute
