## Ingredients

- 1 quart sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 1 dozen eggs
- 1 3/4 pounds flour
- 1 3/4 pounds mixed peel
- 1 pound blanched almonds
- 3 pounds currants
- 2 pounds raisins
- 2 pounds sultanas
- 1 ounce rose water
- 1/20 ounce mixed spice
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 pint sherry
- 3/4 pint brandy
- juice of 2 oranges
- juice of 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Bake very, very slow for 6 hours
