## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed pumpkin
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 2 soda biscuits rolled fine
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon ginger
- A little grated nutmeg
- 3/4 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. This makes enough for 2 pies
