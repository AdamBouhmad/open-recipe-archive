## Ingredients

- parsnips
- water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel parsnips and wash
2. Cut into cubes
3. Boil in sufficient water to cover them until tender
4. Place in the oven a dish in which put a good-sized lump of butter
5. Let gradually melt
6. When parsnips are cooked, drain
7. Season with pepper and salt
8. Place in a vegetable dish
9. Pour over them the melted butter
10. Serve hot
