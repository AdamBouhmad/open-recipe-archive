## Ingredients

- 1 doz. French chops
- Salt and pepper
- 3 cepes (large mushrooms)
- Cracker dust
- 1 egg, beaten
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. Flatten and trim the chops, divide each cepe into four strips
2. Make a hole with the point of a knife in the thickest part of each chop and thrust through it a slice of the mushroom
3. Pepper and salt
4. Dip in raw beaten egg
5. Coat with cracker-crumbs and set in a cold place for one hour
6. Fry them in deep fat to a fine brown
