## Ingredients

- 1 firm cabbage
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 3 tablespoons cream or milk
- pepper and salt
- fine crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil a firm cabbage in two waters, drain and press and let it get perfectly cold, then mince fine
2. Add 2 tablespoons melted butter, 2 eggs well beaten, 3 tablespoons cream or milk, pepper and salt to your taste
3. Put into a buttered bake dish, sift fine crumbs on top, and bake, covered, half an hour, then brown
