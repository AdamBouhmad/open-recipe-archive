## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 2 pounds meat
- 2 pounds apples
- 3 pounds raisins
- 2 pounds currants
- 1 1/2 pounds suet
- 1/2 pound lemon peel
- 1/4 pound citron peel
- 1 pint brandy
- juice and grated rind of 3 lemons
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 2 nutmegs
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 pint cider

## Directions

1. Chop meat, apples, and suet fine
2. Mix together all ingredients
