## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- ½ cake chocolate grated and stirred in ½ cup boiling water
- ½ cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda settled in 1 tablespoon hot water
- ½ teaspoon cream tartar
- 2½ cups flour

## Directions

1. cream butter with sugar
2. mix with sour milk
3. sift cream tartar with flour
