## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cocoanut
- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 3 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Stir butter and sugar together well
2. Add egg, then milk
3. Sift flour and baking powder together and add them, beating vigorously
4. When all is smooth, add the cocoanut
5. Bake in a quick oven
