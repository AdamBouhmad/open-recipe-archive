## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup lard and butter
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 cup figs or raisins (chopped)
- 3 eggs
- 4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

## Directions

1. Drop on buttered baking pans with spoon, according to size desired
