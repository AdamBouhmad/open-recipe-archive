## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. boiled beef (chopped)
- 2 lbs. beef suet
- 4 lbs. apples
- 2 lbs. raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 oranges, large
- 2 lbs. powdered sugar
- 1 qt. whisky
- 1 wineglass rose water
- 2 grated nutmegs
- 1/2 oz. cinnamon
- 1/2 oz. each cloves and mace
- 1/2 lb. citron, sliced

## Directions

1. Chop beef, suet, oranges, apples
2. Add spices, then liquids
3. Cook 3 or 4 hours, slowly
4. Keep in jars tightly covered and set in a dry, cool place
