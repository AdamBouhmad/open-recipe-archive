## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups of brown sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sour cream
- pinch of salt
- 1 small teaspoon cinnamon
- a good 1/2 cup of currants
- 3 cups Graham flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar together
2. add the sour cream, salt, cinnamon and currants
3. beat well together
4. add the flour in which the soda has been thoroughly mixed
5. Bake in well-buttered gem rings
