## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful olive oil
- 1 teaspoonful dry mustard
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. Beat all together
2. Let come to a boil until of the consistency of thick cream
3. Serve when cold
