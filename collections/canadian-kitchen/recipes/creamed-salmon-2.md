## Ingredients

- 1 tin salmon
- 1 pint milk
- butter size of an egg
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- 1 egg beaten
- biscuit crumbs

## Directions

1. Turn out salmon on a dish and flake with a fork
2. Make sauce in double boiler from 1 pint milk, butter size of an egg, and a tablespoon of flour, salt and cayenne pepper to taste
3. Melt butter and flour together until smooth
4. Add to milk slowly, also 1 egg beaten, and put in just like boiled custard
5. Put in a baking dish alternate layers of salmon, biscuit crumbs, and sauce until all is used
6. Put layer of crumbs on top
7. Bake 15 minutes and serve hot
