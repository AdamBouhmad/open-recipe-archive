## Ingredients

- 1 gallon green tomatoes
- 10 large onions
- 1 head cabbage
- 1 cup salt
- 1 teaspoon tumeric powder
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- 2 teaspoons mustard seed
- 13/4 pounds brown sugar
- 3 pints vinegar
- 1 cup horseradish grated

## Directions

1. Peel and slice tomatoes
2. Cut onions fine
3. Sprinkle 1 cup salt and let stand over night
4. Drain off water
5. Add onions, cabbage cut fine, tumeric powder, celery seed, mustard seed, brown sugar, and vinegar
6. Mix well and boil 1 hour
7. Just before bottling add grated horseradish
