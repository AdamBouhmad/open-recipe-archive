## Ingredients

- 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake
- water
- flour
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter and lard mixed
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Soak 1/2 White Swan Yeast Cake at noon
2. Stir in a thin batter about 3 o'clock
3. In the evening add a quart of water and flour enough to make a thin batter
4. Set in a warm place to rise over night
5. At 4 in the morning add eggs, butter and lard mixed, salt, and sugar
6. Mix in a stiff dough the same as bread
7. Let rise again, then knead
8. When it rises make into loaves and put in pans
9. Let rise and bake in hot oven
