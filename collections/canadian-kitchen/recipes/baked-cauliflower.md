## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- boiling, salted water
- white sauce
- grated cheese
- paprica

## Directions

1. Cut into clusters and stew tender in boiling, salted water
2. Or, if you have a couple of small cauliflowers, boil them whole and dish together
3. Drain and lay in a bake-dish
4. Pour over it a good white sauce (hot), sprinkle with grated cheese and paprica, and bake, covered, twenty minutes
