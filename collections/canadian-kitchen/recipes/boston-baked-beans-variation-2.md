## Ingredients

- 1 pound pork sliced
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1/2 cup sugar
- pepper and salt
- 1 quart beans
- water

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart beans until skins break open
2. place 2 or 3 slices of meat in bottom of bean pot
3. add a layer of beans, a little mustard, sugar, pepper and salt if pork is fresh
4. continue until bean pot is full, leaving a little pork for top
5. fill bean pot with water
6. bake all day in a moderate oven, adding water occasionally
7. Serve hot
