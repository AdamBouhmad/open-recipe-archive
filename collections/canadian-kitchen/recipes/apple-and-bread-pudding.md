## Ingredients

- 1/2 dozen apples
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Chop apples
2. put a layer in a baking dish, then a layer of bread crumbs, sugar and nutmeg sprinkled on
3. continue until the dish is full
4. take egg beaten in sweet milk, and pour over it
5. bake 1/4 hour
