## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoons sweet cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- a pinch salt
- flour to knead very hard

## Directions

1. Mix egg, cream, sugar, and salt with flour to knead very hard
2. Roll out, cut in strips
3. Tie each one in two knots
4. Lay in hot lard
5. Sprinkle with white sugar while hot
