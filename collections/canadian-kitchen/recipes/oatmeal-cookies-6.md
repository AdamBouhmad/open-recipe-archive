## Ingredients

- 2 cups oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup butter and lard
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 1 pound of dates cooked with 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Put all in pan but sour milk and soda, mix well, then add milk and soda; roll out thin.
2. Have 1 pound of dates cooked with 1 cup sugar, put a few dates in centre of cake, then place another thin rolled cake on top of dates, press down the top cake good around edges, then bake.
