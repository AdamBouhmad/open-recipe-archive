## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound butter
- 5 eggs
- 1/4 milk
- 3/4 pound sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 6 ounces currants

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the eggs 1 by 1
3. Add the flour by spoonfuls
4. Beat in the currants
5. Stir thoroughly and add the milk
6. Line 2 cake tins with buttered paper
7. Pour in the mixture and bake in moderate oven 1 3/4 hours
