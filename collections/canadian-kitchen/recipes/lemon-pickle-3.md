## Ingredients

- 6 lemons
- 0.5 pound salt
- 0.5 ounce cloves
- 0.5 ounce mace
- 0.5 ounce turmeric
- 3 pints vinegar
- Quarter ounce cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the lemons in halves
2. Squeeze out the juice
3. Cut each half into four pieces
4. Rub each piece with salt
5. Put them in a slow oven till they are quite brown and hard
6. Rub them with the turmeric and put them in a jar
7. Put the juice, spice and vinegar and quarter ounce of cayenne pepper over them
8. Cover them up and set the jar on a hot hearth or on the back of the stove till the pickles are soft
