## Ingredients

- 1 pt. flour
- 0.5 teaspoonful salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 generous tablespoonful shortening
- 1 large tablespoonful mashed potato
- 1 small cup raisins or currants

## Directions

1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt twice
2. cut in shortening
3. moisten with milk, adding potato and currants
4. Roll gently to thickness of one inch
5. cut in round cakes the size of small tea-plate
6. Bake twenty minutes in hot oven
7. cut open, butter and replace, cutting each cake in four
8. Serve hot
