## Ingredients

- Eight good-sized potatoes
- salt and pepper to taste
- four onions

## Directions

1. Boil and mash potatoes, salt and pepper to taste.
2. Chop four onions very finely and mix with hot potato.
3. Stuff body of goose as full as possible.
