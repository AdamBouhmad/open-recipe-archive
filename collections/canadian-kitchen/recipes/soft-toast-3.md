## Ingredients

- 2 thin slices stale bread
- salt
- boiling water
- butter

## Directions

1. Toast well, but not too brown, two thin slices of stale bread
2. Put them on a warm plate
3. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pour upon them some boiling water
4. Quickly cover with another dish of the same size, and drain off the water
5. Put a very small bit of butter on the toast and serve at once while hot
