## Ingredients

- 4 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups figs
- 1 cup almonds
- 2 tablespoons honey
- spices to taste
- 6 tablespoons eggs
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Follow Solomon's prescription for making a good boy and you will have a good cake
2. Bake in a slow oven
3. This cake will keep a year and is better to be made some time before wanted
