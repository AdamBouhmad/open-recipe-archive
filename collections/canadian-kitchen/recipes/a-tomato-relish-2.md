## Ingredients

- 30 ripe tomatoes
- 12 sour apples
- 10 onions
- 6 peppers
- 5 tablespoons salt
- 1 pint vinegar
- sugar and cinnamon to taste

## Directions

1. Scald and skin tomatoes
2. Pare and core apples
3. Peel onions
4. Remove seeds of peppers
5. Chop all fine, mix thoroughly and stew slowly 1.5 hours
