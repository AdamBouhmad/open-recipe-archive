## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup soft butter
- 2 cups flour
- 4 tablespoons sweet milk
- 2 level teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 pounds dates
- 1 1/2 cups water
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs and other ingredients
3. Roll thin and bake
4. For filling: boil dates, water, sugar and vanilla for 7 minutes
5. Cool filling and spread between cakes
