## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Bake in 3 layers
3. Put together with icing made from 1 cup sugar, 1 cup water, 1 even tablespoon cornstarch, 2 yolks of eggs, juice and grated rinds
