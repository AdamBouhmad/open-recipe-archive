## Ingredients

- 1 firm, medium cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter or salt fat pork
- 1 egg
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage in ½-inch strips using a very sharp knife or cabbage cutter
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Put butter or salt fat pork in a kettle
4. Put in cabbage and cook 10 minutes, stirring constantly
5. Remove to back of stove to steam until done
6. Break in an egg and beat well
7. Add vinegar to taste
8. Serve very hot
