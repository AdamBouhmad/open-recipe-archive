## Ingredients

- 1 quart large onions
- 1 quart small onions
- 1 quart cucumbers
- 1 quart small cucumbers
- 1 large cauliflower
- 3 green peppers
- 3 cups of sugar
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 pound white mustard seed
- 3/4 ounce turmeric (powdered)
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 3/4 cup of flour
- 2 ounces mustard

## Directions

1. Chop large onions and cucumbers
2. Leave small onions and small cucumbers whole
3. Put all vegetables in separate dishes and let stand over night
4. In morning add sugar and vinegar
5. Add white mustard seed, turmeric and celery seed
6. Put all in kettle and scald
7. Make a paste of flour and mustard with a little vinegar
8. Turn in slowly, stir briskly and let boil up and seal
