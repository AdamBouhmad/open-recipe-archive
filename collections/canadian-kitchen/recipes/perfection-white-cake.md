## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- whites 4 eggs beaten very stiff

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 layers and put together with boiled icing
