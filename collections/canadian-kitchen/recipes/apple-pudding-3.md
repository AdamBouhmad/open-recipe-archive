## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- good cream
- 2 cups chopped apples
- 1/2 cup sugar
- any ordinary spice or flavoring
- 1 cup sugar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly
2. Moisten with good cream after mixing dry ingredients
3. Have the dough quite soft, as for biscuit
4. Roll to the size of a large pie pan
5. Place in a large, well-buttered pudding dish
6. Spread on the dough 2 cups chopped apples, 1/2 cup sugar and any ordinary spice or flavoring
7. Moisten the edge of dough and press together to form a covering for the filling
8. Over the top spread a cup of sugar
9. Pour into the dish enough cold water to completely cover the pudding
10. Place in a moderately hot oven and bake 1 3/4 hour
