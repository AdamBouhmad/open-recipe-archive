## Ingredients

- Whites of 4 eggs
- ¾ pound sugar
- 1 cup butter
- ¾ cup cornstarch
- 1 cup flour
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- vanilla or lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar together
2. Mix cornstarch with milk and add
3. Add eggs beaten stiff
4. Add flour in which the baking powder has been sifted
5. Add flavoring
6. Put in well-greased mould and bake
