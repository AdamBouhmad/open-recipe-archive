## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 0.75 cup milk
- 0.5 cup butter
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat eggs well
2. Add sugar
3. Add the softened butter
4. Stir in part of milk
5. Add half of the flour, through which baking powder has been sifted
6. Add remainder of milk and flour
7. Bake in square tin
8. Cover with chocolate icing if desired or sifted sugar
