## Ingredients

- 3 cups grated bread
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup coffee sugar
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves

## Directions

1. Steam from 1 to 2 hours
