## Ingredients

- sweet green peppers
- two peppers
- one small onion
- one large tomato
- stale breadcrumbs
- one teaspoonful salt
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the tops off the sweet green peppers and carefully remove the seeds
2. Chop together very fine two peppers, one small onion and one large tomato (peeled)
3. Add an equal amount of stale breadcrumbs, one teaspoonful of salt and sufficient melted butter to moisten the mixture
4. Fill the peppers with the mixture, replace the tops and bake for half an hour in a moderate oven
