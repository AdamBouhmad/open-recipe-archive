## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 heaping tablespoon flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon sharp vinegar
- 1 cup cold water
- nutmeg
- 2 pie crusts

## Directions

1. Beat egg, flour, and sugar well together
2. Add vinegar and cold water
3. Flavor with nutmeg
4. Bake with 2 crusts
