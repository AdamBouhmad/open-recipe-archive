## Ingredients

- 1 gallon tomatoes
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 3 tablespoons red peppers
- 3 tablespoons mustard
- 1 ounce whole cloves
- 1 ounce whole allspice
- 2 ounces ginger

## Directions

1. Boil 2 hours
2. Strain
