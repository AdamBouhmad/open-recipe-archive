## Ingredients

- cold meat
- potatoes
- two slices of bread
- cold water or milk
- salt and pepper
- a little sliced onion
- butter size of an egg

## Directions

1. Chop cold meat and potatoes, twice as much potatoes as meat
2. dip two slices of bread in cold water or milk
3. add salt and pepper with a little sliced onion, and butter size of an egg
4. Put in baking pan and cover tightly when cooking
5. Bake 3/4 hour and turn out in a cake on a hot dish
