## Ingredients

- 1 pk. tomatoes
- 1 lb. brown sugar
- ½ lb. mustard
- 3 red peppers
- 1 pt. cider vinegar
- ½ lb. salt
- 1 oz. ginger
- ½ oz. cloves
- ½ oz. mace
- ½ teaspoonful cayenne
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes in oven until hot, and squeeze through a strainer
2. Add other ingredients
3. Put spices, which must be whole, in a bag
4. Add a tablespoonful of cornstarch to mustard, which makes it thicken
5. Cook for an hour, counting from time it begins to boil
