## Ingredients

- fruit
- water
- sugar
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash fruit
2. Remove stems and decayed part
3. Cook with enough water to cover until tender
4. Drain
5. Add 1 cup sugar for each cup juice
6. Heat sugar first
7. Boil until it jells on cool saucer
8. Add ½ tablespoon lemon juice
9. Mould with sardines and serve with mayonnaise
