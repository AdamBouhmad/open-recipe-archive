## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. green rhubarb
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1/2 oz. bitter almonds
- Rind of 1/2 lemon
- A little ginger

## Directions

1. Wipe the rhubarb quite dry, cut it into pieces about two inches long
2. Put it into a preserving-pan with the sugar, the rind of the lemon cut very fine, and the almonds blanched and divided
3. Boil the whole well together, taking care to stir and skim frequently
4. When it is nearly done stir in the ginger
5. Young rhubarb will take about three-quarters of an hour, but if old it must be boiled for an hour and a half
