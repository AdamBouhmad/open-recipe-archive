## Ingredients

- 1 quart of lamb or mutton broth
- 2 cups of turnip dice
- White, young turnips

## Directions

1. Cook turnip dice in the liquor half an hour after the boil begins.
2. When very tender, rub through a colander.
3. Return to the fire and proceed as with cream of celery soup, only putting in both white and yolk of the egg.
