## Ingredients

- 1/2 dozen peppers
- 1/2 dozen onions
- 1/2 cup salt
- 1 head cabbage
- vinegar
- 1/2 cup white mustard seed
- 1 tablespoon celery seed
- sugar to suit the taste

## Directions

1. Chop peppers and onions fine
2. Add salt and let drain over night
3. Chop cabbage fine
4. Mix the whole together and cover with vinegar
5. Add mustard seed and celery seed
6. Add sugar to suit the taste
7. Let come to a boil
