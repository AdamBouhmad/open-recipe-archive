## Ingredients

- 2 cups maple syrup
- 2 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Directions

1. Scald milk and add to hot syrup.
2. Add cornstarch.
3. Add eggs, well whipped.
4. Remove from fire and add nuts.
5. Place in mould on ice and serve with whipped cream.
