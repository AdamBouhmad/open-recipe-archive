## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon mustard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- vinegar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook so that it will not taste raw
2. Remove from stove and add egg and vinegar to taste
