## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes chopped fine
- 3 red peppers
- 6 onions
- 1 cup salt
- ground cloves
- allspice
- cinnamon
- 7/8 cup grated horseradish
- 1 cup sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop green tomatoes, red peppers, and onions fine
2. Add 1 cup salt and mix all together
3. Let drain over night
4. In morning add 1 teaspoon each of ground cloves, allspice, and cinnamon
5. Add 7/8 cup grated horseradish and 1 cup sugar
6. Cover with vinegar and boil till tender
7. Bottle
