## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup butter
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 6 tablespoons milk
- flour enough to roll easy
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Roll out
3. Bake
