## Ingredients

- 2.5 cups pumpkin, cooked
- 2 cupfuls milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful molasses
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful ginger
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. To pulp add milk, salt, butter, cinnamon, and ginger, molasses, eggs, and sugar.
2. Add the beaten eggs last and after the mixture is cold.
3. Pour it into an open crust and bake slowly forty to fifty minutes.
