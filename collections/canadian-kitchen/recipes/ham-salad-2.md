## Ingredients

- eggs
- mustard
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- sweet milk
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs light with 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 teaspoon pepper
2. Stir in 1 cup sweet milk
3. Put on stove 3 1/2 cup vinegar and let scald
4. Cook the egg mixture and then pour in vinegar
