## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons milk
- a pinch of salt
- flour
- boiling broth

## Directions

1. Break eggs, add milk and salt, mix until light
2. Add flour to make stiff
3. Roll very thin, flour well and cut in little strips
4. Drop into boiling broth
5. Cook for thirty minutes
6. Serve with broth
