## Ingredients

- 50 chestnuts
- 1 qt. milk and water
- 12 oz. sugar
- 3 oranges
- 0.5 pt. cream
- Maraschino
- Whipped cream
- Vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Remove husks and skin from the chestnuts
2. Boil gently in the milk and water until like floury potatoes, and strain them
3. Boil the sugar until it purls on the surface and flavor with vanilla bean
4. Add the chestnuts and work all together vigorously
5. Rub through a potato masher on to a dish
6. Pile up whipped cream in the centre of a dish
7. Gently strew the chestnuts on top of the cream in a conical form
8. Garnish with orange quarters soaked in maraschino at the base of the cream and nuts
