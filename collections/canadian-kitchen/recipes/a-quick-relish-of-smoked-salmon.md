## Ingredients

- Half a pound of smoked salmon cut into narrow strips
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- Juice of half a lemon
- Cayenne pepper
- Dry toast

## Directions

1. Parboil the salmon ten minutes
2. Lay in cold water for the same length of time
3. Wipe dry
4. Broil over a clear fire
5. Butter while hot
6. Season with cayenne and lemon-juice
7. Pile in a log-cabin square upon a hot plate
8. Send up with dry toast
