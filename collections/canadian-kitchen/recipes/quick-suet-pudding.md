## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup chopped suet
- cold water
- raspberries or any fruit preferred

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt
2. Add chopped suet
3. Make in a batter with cold water
4. Put raspberries or any fruit preferred in the bottom of pudding dish
5. Pour batter on
6. Steam 1 hour
