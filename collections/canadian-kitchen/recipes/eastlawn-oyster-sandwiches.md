## Ingredients

- thin glutinous brown bread
- butter
- 1 cupful of oysters
- cayenne
- salt
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Butter the thin glutinous brown bread well
2. Beard and dry oysters on a napkin
3. Cut oysters fine with knife (do not chop)
4. Season with cayenne, a little salt and squeeze lemon-juice over
5. Spread sparingly on the brown bread
6. Set on the ice till wanted
