## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 pint table syrup
- 1/2 pound currants
- 2 eggs
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Cut rind in very small pieces
2. Use good rich pastry
3. Divide the mixture in half
4. Line 3 plates with pastry
5. Put 1/2 of mixture in them
6. Cover with thin pastry without markings
7. Add remaining 1/2 of mixture and cover
8. Bake well in a moderate oven
