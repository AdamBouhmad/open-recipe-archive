## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1 1/2 dozen soda biscuits rolled fine
- 1 cup sweet milk
- pepper and salt to taste
- butter for cooking

## Directions

1. Work all together
2. Form in lumps the size of eggs or larger
3. Cook in a hot buttered pan till a nice brown
4. Turn once
