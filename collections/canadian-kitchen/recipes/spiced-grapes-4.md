## Ingredients

- 8 pounds grapes
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- scant 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare grapes the same as for preserved grapes
2. Cook vinegar, spices and fruit after it has been prepared 10 to 20 minutes
3. Add sugar and boil down until of right consistency
