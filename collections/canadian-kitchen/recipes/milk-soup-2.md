## Ingredients

- 4 potatoes
- 2 onions
- 2 ounces butter
- pepper
- ¾ ounce salt
- 1 pint milk
- 3 tablespoons tapioca
- 2 quarts water

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of water in a saucepan
2. Peel and cut potatoes in quarters, cut up onions
3. When the water boils put in potatoes and onions
4. Add the butter, salt and pepper to taste
5. Allow it to boil to a mash, then strain through a colander, working the vegetables through also
6. Return the pulp and soup to the stewpan, add one pint of milk to it and boil
7. When boiling, sprinkle in by degrees three tablespoons of tapioca, stirring all the time
8. Let boil for fifteen minutes gently
