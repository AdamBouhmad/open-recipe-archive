## Ingredients

- 1 cup currants
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup suet
- 1 cup flour or a little more if needed
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup syrup
- a pinch salt
- spice to suit the taste

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Steam 3 hours
