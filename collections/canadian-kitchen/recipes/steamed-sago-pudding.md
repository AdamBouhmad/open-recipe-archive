## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 3 oz. sago
- 2 oz. fresh butter
- 3 oz. sugar
- Grated rind of 1 lemon
- three eggs
- one-half tablespoon flour
- one and one-half teaspoonfuls sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan milk, sago, fresh butter, sugar, and the grated rind of one lemon
2. Boil all together for thirteen and a half minutes
3. Work in three eggs
4. Mix together one-half tablespoon flour and one and one-half teaspoonfuls sugar
5. Butter mould and dust flour and sugar into it well
6. Pour in the mixture and put buttered paper on top
7. Steam one hour
