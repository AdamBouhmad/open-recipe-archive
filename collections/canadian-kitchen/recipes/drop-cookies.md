## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup currants or walnuts
- salt and flavor to taste

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop by spoonful on pan and bake
