## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup water
- white of 1 egg
- 1/2 cup chopped raisins
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 tablespoons cocoanut
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water till stringy
2. Beat the white of 1 egg
3. Mix egg with the sugar
4. Add chopped raisins, walnuts, and cocoanut
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Spread between the layers
7. Frost the cake with white frosting
