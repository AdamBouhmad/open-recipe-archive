## Ingredients

- 6 medium-sized potatoes cooked
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 onions
- 1 cucumber peeled and seeded
- 3 stalks of celery

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes
2. Cut all ingredients fine and combine
