## Ingredients

- two cups cooked and creamed squash (cold)
- two cups milk
- two eggs
- saltspoonful of salt
- one cup flour
- one-half teaspoonful baking powder

## Directions

1. To squash add milk, eggs, salt, and flour in which has been sifted the baking powder
2. There should be just enough flour to hold the mixture together
3. Bake on a griddle as you would cakes
4. Send to table hot
