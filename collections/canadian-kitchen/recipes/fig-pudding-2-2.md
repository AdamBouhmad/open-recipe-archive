## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound figs chopped fine
- 1/8 pound suet
- 1/2 pound brown sugar
- 1 pound bread crumbs
- 2 ounces candied lemon
- 2 eggs well beaten
- a little nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon molasses mixed in milk

## Directions

1. Put in a mould
2. Boil 3 hours
