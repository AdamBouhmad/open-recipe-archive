## Ingredients

- 4.5 lbs. green tomatoes
- 4.5 lbs. apples, quartered
- 3 pts. vinegar
- 4 lbs. sugar
- 1 oz. allspice
- 1 oz. peppers
- stick cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Quarter apples and stick a few cloves (two) in each quarter.
2. Tie all the spices in muslin bags.
3. Let vinegar, sugar and spices come to a boil.
4. Add tomatoes and apples, and boil till tender.
