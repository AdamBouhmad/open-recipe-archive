## Ingredients

- 5 cups beans
- 3 nice sized onions
- butter size of a walnut
- heaping teaspoon salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash and soak beans over night
2. put into a crock
3. slice 3 nice sized onions and place on top of beans
4. add butter size of a walnut, heaping teaspoon salt
5. cover with water and add more from time to time as required to keep them covered
6. Place on top of the stove uncovered all day, and do not allow to boil
7. Serve as baked beans
