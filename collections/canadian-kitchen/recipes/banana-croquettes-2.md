## Ingredients

- small yellow bananas
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- cracker-crumbs
- cottolene

## Directions

1. Select small, yellow bananas; strip off the skins and cut off the ends, so as to make them look like croquettes.
2. Pepper and salt, roll in egg, then in cracker-crumbs, set on the ice for one hour to stiffen them.
3. Fry in hot, deep cottolene to a golden brown.
4. Serve dry and hot.
