## Ingredients

- 2.5 cups sugar
- 1.5 cups lard
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 2 eggs
- five cents' worth of baker's ammonia
- five cents' worth of lemon oil
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat sugar, lard, and eggs together
2. Pulverize and dissolve ammonia in sweet milk
3. Put lemon oil in last
4. Mix in flour till stiff
5. Roll about as thick as pasteboard
6. Cut in squares or round shapes
7. Add a little salt
8. Bake in a quick oven
