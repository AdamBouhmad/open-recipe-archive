## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. cheese
- 2 oz. butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls ale or Yolks 2 eggs beaten in 1/2 cup milk
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 1 saltspoonful dry mustard
- 1/2 saltspoonful pepper
- A dash of cayenne

## Directions

1. Stir in a saucepan over the fire until melted smoothly together
2. Pour on a couple of slices of toast laid on a hot dish
