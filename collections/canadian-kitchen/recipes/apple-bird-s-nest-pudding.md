## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 pint milk
- 1 egg
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Pare and core apples
2. Fill cavity with sugar
3. Pour over batter made with 1 tablespoon flour, 1/2 pint milk, 1 egg, and sugar to taste
4. Bake 1 hour
