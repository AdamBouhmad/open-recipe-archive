## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- 3 pounds beef or lean mutton

## Directions

1. Cut potatoes in halves and boil sufficient amount
2. Drain potatoes
3. Put into a baking tin
4. Add a little butter
5. Place beef or lean mutton in centre of potatoes
6. Bake 1 hour
7. Turn potatoes over occasionally until they are a golden brown
