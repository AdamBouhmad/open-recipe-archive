## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup grape-nut
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup chopped raisins
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Scald milk and pour over grape-nut.
2. Beat yolks of eggs with sugar, raisins and flavoring.
3. Add to the milk.
4. Beat the whites of eggs and add to mixture.
5. Bake in a slow oven in a pan of water for 1/2 hour.
