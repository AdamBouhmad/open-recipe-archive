## Ingredients

- hard boiled yolk of eggs
- salt
- pepper
- French mustard
- melted butter
- thin crackers
- celery
- ale or beer

## Directions

1. Pound hard boiled yolk of eggs, and if necessary pass through sieve
2. Add a seasoning of salt, pepper, French mustard and a little melted butter
3. Spread on thin crackers
4. Serve with celery and ale or beer
