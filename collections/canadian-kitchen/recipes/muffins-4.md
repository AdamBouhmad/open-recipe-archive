## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- A little salt
- 1 cake compressed yeast
- ½ pt. milk
- A little brown sugar
- Enough flour to make a thick batter

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and butter, dissolve the yeast in a little milk, beat the eggs well, add enough flour to make a thick batter.
2. Leave it to rise some hours.
3. Put into well-buttered rings.
4. Rise another hour.
5. Bake twenty minutes in a good oven.
