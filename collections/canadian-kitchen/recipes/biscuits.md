## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 dessertspoons sugar
- 1 small teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 3/4 cup currants
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar

## Directions

1. Sift flour, cream tartar, soda and salt together
2. Add sugar and currants
3. Mix with a spoon stiff enough to roll out
4. Leave for 5 minutes
5. Cut into cakes, put in the pan
6. Leave 5 minutes more
7. Bake in a hot oven; if the fire is good will cook in 5 or 10 minutes
