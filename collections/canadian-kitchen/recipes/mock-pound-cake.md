## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 6 ounces butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 small cup milk
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1 pound flour
- flavoring to suit taste

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar together
2. Add eggs
3. Add milk
4. Mix soda and cream tartar with flour
5. Combine all ingredients with flavoring to suit taste
6. Bake in moderate oven
