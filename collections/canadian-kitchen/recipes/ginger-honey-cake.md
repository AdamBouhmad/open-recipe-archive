## Ingredients

- 1 cup honey
- 1/2 cup butter or drippings
- 1 tablespoon boiled cider in 1/2 cup hot water (or 1/2 cup sour milk)
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour enough to make a soft batter

## Directions

1. Warm honey, butter (or drippings), and boiled cider in hot water together
2. Add 1 tablespoon ginger and 1 teaspoon soda sifted in with flour enough to make a soft batter
3. Bake in a flat pan
