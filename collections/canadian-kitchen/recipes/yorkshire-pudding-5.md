## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 pt. of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls flour sifted twice
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Put flour and salt into a bowl, break eggs into it.
2. Mix well, and add milk by degrees, beating till well mixed.
3. Cook twenty to thirty minutes in heat of cake oven.
