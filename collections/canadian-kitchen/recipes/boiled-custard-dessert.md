## Ingredients

- 3 pints milk
- 2 heaping tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 eggs
- 1 small cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Let milk come to a scald
2. Wet cornstarch with a little milk
3. Beat eggs very light
4. Pour in cornstarch when milk is scalding, but before it boils
5. Add sugar and beaten eggs
6. Add vanilla
7. Serve warm
