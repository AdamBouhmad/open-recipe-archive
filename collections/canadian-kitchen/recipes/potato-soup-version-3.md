## Ingredients

- 4 good-sized potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour
- milk
- onion

## Directions

1. Peel and slice potatoes
2. Put on to cook
3. When done season with pepper, salt and butter
4. Make a thickening of flour and milk
5. Pour on potatoes and boil for a few minutes
6. A little onion may be added if desired
