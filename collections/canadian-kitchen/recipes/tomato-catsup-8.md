## Ingredients

- 1/2 bushel skinned tomatoes
- 1 quart good vinegar
- 3/4 pound salt
- 3/4 pound black pepper
- 1 ounce African cayenne
- 3/4 pound allspice
- 1 ounce cloves
- 3 boxes mustard
- 20 cloves garlic
- 6 good onions
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 handful peach leaves

## Directions

1. Boil all ingredients for 3 hours
2. Constantly stir to keep from burning
3. When cool, strain through fine sieve
4. Bottle for future use
5. Will improve by age
