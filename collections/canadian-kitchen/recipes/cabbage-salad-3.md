## Ingredients

- 1 quart boiled beets chopped fine
- 2 quarts raw cabbage cut fine
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup ground horseradish
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- ¾ tablespoon red pepper
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Cover with cold vinegar
3. Use
