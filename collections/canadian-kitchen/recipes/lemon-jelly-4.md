## Ingredients

- 1 box gelatine
- 1 pt. hot water
- 0.5 pt. wine
- 1 pt. sugar
- 2 lemons, juice and rind
- Whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine for one hour in a pint of cold water
2. Add hot water, wine, sugar, lemons, juice and thinly pared rind
3. Boil for one-half a minute and strain
4. Beat whites of three eggs to a stiff froth and stir in before quite cold
5. Put in mould
