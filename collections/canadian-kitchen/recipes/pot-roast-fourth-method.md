## Ingredients

- meat
- 2 cups water
- salt
- 1 cup hot water
- 3 onions
- 2 teaspoons flour
- water

## Directions

1. Put meat in pot and add 2 cups water
2. Shake generously with salt
3. Cover tightly and let boil dry
4. Just scorch, not burn
5. Add 1 cup hot water and let boil dry
6. Repeat the same process for 1 hour
7. Add 3 onions cut fine
8. Repeat the same process for another hour
9. Take out meat
10. Mix 2 teaspoons flour in a little water
11. Pour in liquid in pot and add water to make sufficient for gravy
