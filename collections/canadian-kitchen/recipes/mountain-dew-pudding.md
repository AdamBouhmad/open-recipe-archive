## Ingredients

- 3 crackers rolled
- 1 pint milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- a small piece butter
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Bake 1/4 hour
2. Take the whites of the eggs, beat to a stiff froth, add 1 cup sugar and put it on the top and bake 15 minutes
