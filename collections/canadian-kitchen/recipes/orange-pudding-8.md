## Ingredients

- one cup sugar
- one orange
- one cup cracker crumbs
- one pint milk
- two eggs
- one teaspoonful butter

## Directions

1. Soak the crumbs in a little of the milk, butter and sugar
2. Add grated rind and eggs beaten together
3. Add orange juice and crumbs
4. Bake half an hour in moderate oven
