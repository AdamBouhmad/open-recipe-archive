## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold cooked meat chopped fine
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 tablespoon bread crumbs
- a few drops onion juice
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup stock soup

## Directions

1. stir all together over fire until warm
2. then cool
3. form in ball
4. roll in crumbs
5. then egg
6. fry in hot lard
7. serve with bechamel sauce
