## Ingredients

- potatoes
- chopped ham
- butter
- salt
- parsley
- milk

## Directions

1. Select 6 large potatoes
2. Wash and put in hot oven and bake until soft
3. Take out, remove the one end and with a teaspoon take out all the inside, being careful not to break the jacket
4. Mash thoroughly
5. Add 3/4 cup chopped ham (any other meat will do), a small piece of butter, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon chopped parsley and sufficient milk so you can cream it
6. Fill up jackets again, replace the end and put in oven for 10 or 15 minutes
7. Serve hot
