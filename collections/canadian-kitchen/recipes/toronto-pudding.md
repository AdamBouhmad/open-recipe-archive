## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 4 apples
- 1/4 pound bread crumbs
- 4 ounces sugar
- 3 ounces currants
- salt and grated nutmeg to taste
- rind of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Pare, core and mince the apples into small pieces
2. Mix them with the other dry ingredients
3. Beat up the eggs
4. Moisten the mixture with these
5. Beat it well
6. Put the pudding into a buttered mould
7. Tie it down with a cloth
8. Boil for 1 hour and a half
9. Serve with sweet sauce
