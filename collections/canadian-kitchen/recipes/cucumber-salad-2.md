## Ingredients

- 12 large green cucumbers
- 3 large onions
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 small cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- ½ teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon white mustard seed
- 1 level teaspoon tumeric

## Directions

1. Pare, slice and salt over night
2. in the morning drain
3. pour over cucumbers and onions 1 pint vinegar, 1 small cup sugar, 1 teaspoon celery seed, ½ teaspoon pepper, 1 tablespoon white mustard seed, 1 level teaspoon tumeric, and let come to a boil
4. Put away in jars
