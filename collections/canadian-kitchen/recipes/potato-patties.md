## Ingredients

- 3 cups cold mashed potatoes
- 1 cup cold meat
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper
- sage
- grease

## Directions

1. Take 3 cups cold mashed potatoes and cold meat of any kind
2. Grind 1 cup of the meat and mix with potatoes
3. Beat 1 egg and add with salt, pepper and sage to season
4. Make into little round patties and fry in a little grease
