## Ingredients

- 1 quart green tomatoes
- 1 quart cabbage
- 3 large onions
- 1/4 ounce tumeric seed
- 1/4 ounce celery seed
- 1/4 ounce small red peppers
- 1/4 ounce white mustard seed
- 1/4 ounce whole cloves
- 1/4 ounce root ginger
- 1/4 ounce allspice
- 1/4 quart strong vinegar
- 1 pound brown sugar

## Directions

1. Chop fine tomatoes, cabbage and onions
2. Put all spices in a bag made of clean muslin
3. Add to vegetables
4. Add vinegar and brown sugar
5. Boil all together for about 3/4 hour
