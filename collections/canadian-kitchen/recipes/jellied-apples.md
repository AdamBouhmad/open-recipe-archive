## Ingredients

- 1 dozen or more tart apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- 1/4 cup water
- whipped cream or boiled custard

## Directions

1. Pare and slice thin 1 dozen or more tart apples
2. Place in a pudding dish alternate layers of apples and sugar
3. Add a dust of cinnamon
4. Pour over it 1/4 cup of water
5. Lay a buttered plate over the top
6. Cook slowly for 3 hours
7. Set in a cool place
8. When ready turn out in a glass dish
9. Serve with whipped cream or boiled custard
