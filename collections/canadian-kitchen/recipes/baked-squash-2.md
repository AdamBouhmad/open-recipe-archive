## Ingredients

- squash
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut squash in halves
2. Remove seeds and stringy portion
3. Place in a dripping pan and cover
4. Bake 2 hours, or until soft, in a moderate oven
5. Remove from shell and mash
6. Season with butter, salt and pepper
