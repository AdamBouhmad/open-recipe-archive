## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. chocolate
- 1 pt. milk
- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate.
2. Heat one pint milk; add yolks of eggs, and sugar.
3. Stir until creamy; add the chocolate.
4. It must not boil.
