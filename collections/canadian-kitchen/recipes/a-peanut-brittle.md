## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup unshelled peanuts

## Directions

1. Shell peanuts and remove brown skin
2. Roll nuts until broken fine
3. Put sugar in frying-pan and heat, stirring constantly until melted
4. Add rolled nuts, mix thoroughly and turn at once on an unbuttered pan inverted
5. Roll to desired thickness and mark in squares before it hardens
