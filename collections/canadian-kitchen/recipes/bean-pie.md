## Ingredients

- 1 pint white beans
- salt and pepper to taste
- left-over meat scraps
- 1 small cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash a pint of white beans
2. boil in soft water till tender, but not done
3. take any left-over meat scraps, mince fine
4. add salt, pepper to taste, a small cup of vinegar
5. put all in a bake dish and bake 1 hour
6. serve hot
