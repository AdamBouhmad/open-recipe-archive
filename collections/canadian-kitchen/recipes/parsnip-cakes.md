## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- clarified dripping

## Directions

1. Wash, boil, and scrape the parsnips tender
2. While hot mash
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Make with floured hands into small, flat cakes
5. Flour well
6. Fry in clarified dripping
