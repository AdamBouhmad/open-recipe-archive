## Ingredients

- butter
- sliced tomatoes
- cold meat
- bread and butter
- pepper
- salt
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Butter the dish well
2. Put in a layer of sliced tomatoes, a layer of cold meat sliced thin, a layer of bread and butter, and so on until the dish is full
3. Season well with pepper and salt
4. Pour beaten eggs over the top
5. Bake brown
