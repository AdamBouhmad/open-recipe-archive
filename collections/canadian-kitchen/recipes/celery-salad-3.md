## Ingredients

- two heads of celery
- two sour apples
- seven olives
- mayonnaise dressing
- chopped walnuts
- capers
- hot-house cress

## Directions

1. Chop celery, apples, and olives together, not too fine
2. Mix a little mayonnaise dressing with it
3. Pour over the top more mayonnaise dressing
4. Put chopped walnuts and capers over top
5. Arrange hot-house cress around salad
