## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- milk and water
- salt
- drawn butter

## Directions

1. Remove leaves
2. Cut the main stock close to the flower
3. Lay it in boiling milk and water, slightly salted, with the stalk down
4. When done, take out carefully and drain in a colander
5. Place in the vegetable dish and pour over it a rich drawn butter dressing
