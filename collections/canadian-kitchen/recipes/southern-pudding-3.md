## Ingredients

- 2 cups corn pulp or whole green corn
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- salt to taste
- bread crumbs
- bits of butter

## Directions

1. Butter a shallow bake dish
2. Pour the mixture of corn pulp or whole green corn, well-beaten eggs, milk, sugar, and melted butter into the dish
3. Season to taste
4. Cover with bread crumbs and bits of butter
5. Bake in a slow oven until brown
