## Ingredients

- Minced cold beef
- 1/2 as much mashed potato
- Gravy enough to moisten them, in which an onion has been cooked
- Season with catsup, pepper, salt and a pinch of marjoram
- Fine breadcrumbs
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes, while hot, very smooth, or if cold potatoes be used, see they are free from lumps
2. Mix in the meat, gravy, and seasoning
3. Bind all together with the beaten egg and form into the desired shapes
4. Roll them in fine breadcrumbs
5. Fry quickly to a light brown
6. Drain on soft paper before the fire till free from fat
7. Serve hot
