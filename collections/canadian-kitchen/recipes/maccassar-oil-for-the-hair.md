## Ingredients

- Alkanet root chippings, quarter of an ounce
- Sweet oil, eight ounces
- Tincture of cantharides, sixty drops
- Oil of rose, ten drops
- Oil of neroli, sixty drops
- Oil of lemon, sixty drops

## Directions

1. Tie quarter of an ounce of alkanet root chippings in coarse muslin
2. Put it in a bottle containing eight ounces of sweet oil
3. Cover to keep out dust and let stand several days
4. Add sixty drops of tincture of cantharides, ten drops of oil of rose, and sixty drops each of oil of neroli and lemon
5. Let it stand one week and it will be ready to use
