## Ingredients

- 1 lb. figs
- 3 eggs, slightly beaten
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Grease a plain-sided oval mould with butter, and line it with figs, which are split through the centre lengthwise
2. Place the skin side next to the mould
3. Make a custard of milk, eggs and sugar
4. place the bowl containing this custard in a saucepan of boiling water and stir gently until it thickens to the consistency of cream (it must on no account boil)
5. add three drops vanilla and set it aside to cool
6. When cool pour it into the lined mould and steam till it is set, which will probably be in twenty minutes
7. Take care that the top of the mould is covered to prevent the steam settling
8. Turn out of the mould to serve
9. To be eaten with cream and sugar
