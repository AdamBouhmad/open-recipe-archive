## Ingredients

- 2 cups light brown sugar
- ½ cup dark brown molasses
- 2 cups sour milk
- ½ cup butter
- 4 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- ½ cup stoned raisins

## Directions

1. Mix well
2. Oven the cake quickly
3. Any cake made with soda should be promptly baked
4. Makes 2 good-sized cakes if wished
