## Ingredients

- 4 doz. or 1 pt. small oysters
- 1/4 pt. chopped celery
- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour (heaping)
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- 2 teaspoonfuls salt
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- A pinch of cayenne
- 1/4 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick over and cut oysters in half, and parboil in the liquor and add chopped celery
2. Put butter into double boiler, melt without browning, add flour, and stir till smooth, add milk
3. Beat eggs without separating, salt, mustard, sugar, vinegar and cayenne
4. Before adding vinegar, mix dry materials, add to the milk, return to fire and cook for five minutes
