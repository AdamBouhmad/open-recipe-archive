## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. dates or figs
- 1/4 lb. suet
- 1/4 teaspoonful soda
- 5 oz. brown sugar
- 1/4 lb. breadcrumbs
- a little salt and nutmeg
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients with well-beaten eggs.
2. Put into buttered mould, and boil two and a half hours.
3. Serve with brandy sauce.
4. The pudding is improved by soaking the dates beforehand in a small cupful of sherry or whiskey.
