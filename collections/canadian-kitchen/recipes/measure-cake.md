## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 1¾ cups sugar
- 1 cup of eggs, 3
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat flour and butter together to a cream with the hands
2. Beat sugar and eggs together with a few drops of flavoring
