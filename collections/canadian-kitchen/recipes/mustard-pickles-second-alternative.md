## Ingredients

- 1 quart cucumbers
- 2 quarts onions
- 1 quart green tomatoes
- 2 heads cauliflower
- 6 green peppers
- 1 pound mustard
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup salt
- 1 gallon water
- 1 gallon vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak pickles in water made with 1 cup salt to 1 gallon water over night
2. Scald in 1 gallon vinegar
3. Let boil
4. Make a paste of the mustard and flour with vinegar
5. Stir into the boiling vinegar to thicken
