## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1/4 lb. lemon peel
- 1 lb. flour
- 1/4 lb. citron peel
- 1/2 lb. almonds
- 1/4 lb. orange peel
- 1 gill brandy
- 1 lb. butter

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and whites separately; lemon, orange, and citron peel cut into small pieces, butter beaten to a cream, add sugar, almonds chopped very fine, flour, yolks of eggs and brandy.
2. Add whites of eggs last.
3. This cake will keep for months in a cool dry place.
