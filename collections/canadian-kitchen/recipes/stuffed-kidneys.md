## Ingredients

- kidneys
- melted butter
- bread crumbs
- cooked salt pork
- parsley
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- onion-juice
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Split the kidneys lengthwise, leaving enough meat and skin on one side to serve as a hinge.
2. Rub well inside with melted butter, and broil them, back downward, over a bright fire for eight minutes.
3. Have ready a stuffing of bread crumbs, cooked salt pork, parsley and butter, seasoned with pepper, salt, and onion-juice.
4. Heat in a double boiler, stir in the juice of a half a lemon.
5. Fill the kidneys with the mixture, run a toothpick through the outer edges or lips to keep in the stuffing.
6. Pepper them and serve with sauce piquante.
