## Ingredients

- 3 pounds seeded raisins
- 3 pounds cleaned currants
- 1/2 pound lemon peel
- 2 pounds light brown sugar
- 2 pounds butter
- 4 nutmegs
- 15 eggs
- 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/8 teaspoon ginger
- 1/8 teaspoon allspice
- 3 tablespoons lemon extract
- 2 tablespoons vanilla
- 2 teaspoons rose water
- 2 teaspoons soda dissolved in 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 3/4 pounds flour

## Directions

1. Bake for 3 hours slowly
