## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 1 cup stoned raisins
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Combine 1 1/2 cups brown sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 1/4 cup sour milk, 1 cup stoned raisins, 1 tablespoon cream, 1/2 teaspoon soda, 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
2. Mix in 3 cups flour.
3. Roll 1/2 inch thick.
