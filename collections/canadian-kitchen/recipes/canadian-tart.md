## Ingredients

- short paste
- raspberry jam
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Cover pie plate with short paste
2. Cover with raspberry jam
3. Beat 1 tablespoon butter with 3 tablespoons sugar
4. Add 2 eggs, 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, and flour, and make soft paste
5. Spread on jam
