## Ingredients

- Half a tin of lobster drained
- 1 ounce of butter
- 1 ounce of flour
- Half a teaspoonful of salt
- Dash of pepper, preferably cayenne
- 1 gill of lobster juice
- Milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- Lobster, chopped fine
- Macaroni
- Egg
- Biscuit-crumb
- Butter for frying
- Fried parsley

## Directions

1. Make a panada of one ounce of butter, one ounce of flour, half a teaspoonful of salt, a dash of pepper, and one gill of lobster juice; if not enough juice add milk
2. Boil well, add two tablespoonfuls cream and one of lemon-juice
3. Stir in lobster, chopped fine
4. Beat all well together and cool
5. When set divide in small portions, form cutlets; for bone stick in a small bit of macaroni
6. Egg and biscuit-crumb the cutlets and fry in butter
7. Serve in a circle round a centre of fried parsley
