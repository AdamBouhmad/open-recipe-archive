## Ingredients

- fine, dry crumbs
- sliced and peeled tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- onion-juice

## Directions

1. Butter a bake-dish and cover the bottom with fine, dry crumbs.
2. Next put a layer of sliced and peeled tomatoes; season with pepper, salt, sugar, butter, and a few drops of onion-juice.
3. More crumbs and more tomatoes until the dish is full.
4. The top layer should be crumbs, peppered, salted, and buttered.
5. Bake half an hour, covered.
6. Uncover and brown.
7. If canned tomatoes are used, drain off half the juice before you begin the scallop, or it will be too watery.
8. Season the liquor and save for sauces and soups.
