## Ingredients

- 8 cups sweet corn scraped off cob
- 3 cups water
- 1/2 cup salt
- 1 cup granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Mix well
2. Let come to a boil and boil for 20 minutes
3. Seal hot
4. The jars, new rubbers, tops and rings must be scalded before using
5. To prepare for table, put on in cold water and let come to a boil
6. Drain off, put on fresh water again, and let come to a boil
7. Drain off, fix with cream or milk, thicken with flour
