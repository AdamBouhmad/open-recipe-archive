## Ingredients

- 9 large green cucumbers peeled
- 4 large onions
- brine
- 1.5 pints vinegar
- 2.5 cups granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 tablespoon celery seed
- 2 tablespoons mustard

## Directions

1. Let cucumbers stand in brine 4 hours
2. Chop fine with onions
3. Cook together as for salad dressing
4. Add cucumbers and onions and cook for 5 minutes
