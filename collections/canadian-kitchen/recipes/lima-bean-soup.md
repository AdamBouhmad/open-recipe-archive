## Ingredients

- 2 mutton chops
- 1 quart Lima beans
- 1 onion
- 2 potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- a sprig of parsley

## Directions

1. Cut up mutton chops and cut potatoes and onion in very small pieces
2. Add Lima beans, salt, pepper and a sprig of parsley
3. Cook
