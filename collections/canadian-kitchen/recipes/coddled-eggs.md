## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Scald the milk, add egg slightly beaten.
2. Cook over hot water, stirring constantly.
3. Add seasoning and serve on hot toast.
