## Ingredients

- wings, second joints, and drumsticks of cold cooked chicken
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take the wings, second joints, and drumsticks of cold cooked chicken; dip them in melted butter, sprinkle them with salt and pepper, and broil them until they are very hot and well browned.
