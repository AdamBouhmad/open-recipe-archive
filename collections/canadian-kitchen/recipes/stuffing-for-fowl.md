## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread crumbs
- 1 cup onions finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon summer savory
- 1 tablespoon sage
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup butter
- 3 1/2 cup hot water

## Directions

1. Put butter in the frying pan and heat
2. Add the ingredients
3. When real hot add 3 1/2 cup hot water
4. Cover and let steam a few minutes
5. When it is ready to mash nicely, then it is ready for use
