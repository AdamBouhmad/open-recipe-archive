## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups orange juice
- 3/4 cup lemon juice
- grated rind of 2 oranges

## Directions

1. Make syrup by boiling water and sugar 15 to 20 minutes
2. Add fruit juice and rind
3. Cool, strain and freeze
