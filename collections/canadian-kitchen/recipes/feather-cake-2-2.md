## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup cornstarch
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon essence lemon
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and butter together
2. Add the whites of eggs and sweet milk
3. Add cornstarch and flour
4. Mix in the baking powder
