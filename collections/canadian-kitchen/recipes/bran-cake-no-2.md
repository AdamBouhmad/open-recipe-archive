## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 heaping tablespoon butter
- 2 cups bran
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- sweet milk enough to make a batter

## Directions

1. Bake in a quick oven
