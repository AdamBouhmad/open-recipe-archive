## Ingredients

- 1 whole lemon, grated
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 beaten egg

## Directions

1. Grate a whole lemon and put it in a small saucepan with 1 cup sugar
2. Let it boil a few minutes
3. Add a beaten egg
4. Spread between layers
