## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- water
- salad oil
- vinegar
- lemon-juice
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the fish.
2. Soak all night, changing the water at bed-time for tepid, and again early in the morning for almost scalding.
3. Keep this hot for an hour by setting the vessel containing the soaking fish on the side of the range.
4. Wash, now, in cold water with a stiff brush or rough cloth, wipe perfectly dry, rub all over with salad oil and vinegar, or lemon-juice, and let it lie in this marinade for a quarter of an hour before broiling it over clear coals.
5. Lay on a hot dish and spread with a mixture of butter, lemon-juice, and minced parsley.
