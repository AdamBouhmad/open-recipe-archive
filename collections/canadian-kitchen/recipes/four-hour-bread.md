## Ingredients

- potato water
- 6 potatoes
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup salt
- warm water
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake

## Directions

1. On the day before the baking is to be done, drain the water in which the potatoes for dinner have been boiled into a small pail or crock, add 6 potatoes mashed fine, and when it has cooled add 2 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, about 1/2 cup salt, and enough warm water to make three quarts.
2. Add a White Swan Yeast Cake and set away in a warm place until the next day.
3. When ready to bake set this pail or crock in a pan of warm water until it is warm (not hot), then add flour enough to knead into a stiff dough.
4. Let it rise; when light, knead again and put in tins.
5. Let it rise again and bake.
