## Ingredients

- 1 pt. cream (whipped stiff)
- 1 oz. gelatine
- 1/4 pt. milk
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1 teacupful powdered sugar
- flavor to taste
- lady fingers
- icing

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in one-quarter pint of hot milk.
2. Fasten lady fingers with icing.
3. Mix cream, whites of eggs and sugar together.
4. Add gelatine last, a little at a time.
5. Beat quickly with a spoon.
