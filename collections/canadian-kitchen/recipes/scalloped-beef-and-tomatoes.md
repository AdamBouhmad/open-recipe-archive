## Ingredients

- cold roast or boiled beef
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 cup gravy
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Drain the juice from tomatoes and set on the fire with 1 tablespoon of sugar
2. Cut the beef into slices and place a layer in a greased baking dish
3. Over this put some tomato, moisten thoroughly with the juice and gravy
4. Season highly, sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with bits of butter
5. Add a little pepper and salt
6. Bake 3 1/2 hour, uncover, brown and serve
