## Ingredients

- one-quarter ounce gelatine
- yolks of five eggs
- one lemon
- sherry
- six ounces sugar
- one-quarter pint milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in a little water
2. Beat the yolks of eggs
3. Rub sugar into them with a spoon
4. Add half a pint of milk and the gelatine
5. Stir over fire until thick like custard
6. Take off and add the juice of a lemon and a wineglass of sherry and whites of eggs which have been well beaten
7. Mix well together
8. Put in a mould and let stand until next day
9. Should be eaten with cream
