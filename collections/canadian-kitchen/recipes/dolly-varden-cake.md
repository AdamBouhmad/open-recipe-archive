## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- ⅜ cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 eggs
- ⅜ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 3 cups flour
- flavor with lemon
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- ½ cup currants
- piece citron chopped fine
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- cloves and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Bake ⅔ of this in two pans
2. to the remainder add molasses, raisins, currants, citron, and spices
3. Bake in two pans and put in sheets, alternately, with a little jelly or white of an egg beaten to a froth
