## Ingredients

- 1 lb. chopped suet
- 1 lb. chopped beef
- 1 qt. apple cider
- 1 pt. molasses
- 1 pt. preserved grapes
- 1 lb. brown sugar
- 1 teacupful brandy
- 2 lbs. sultana raisins
- 2 lbs. currants
- 1/2 lb. citron or lemon peel
- Allspice, cinnamon, and cloves to taste

## Directions

1. Simmer for half a day on the back of the stove till thoroughly cooked
2. Put in a cool place in an earthen jar
3. If a very moist mince meat is preferred, add cider to each pie when baking; have crust very short
