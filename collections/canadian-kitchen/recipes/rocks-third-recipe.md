## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 cup nuts
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 3½ cup hot water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Drop on greased pan and bake
