## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 pound marshmallows
- 2 tablespoons hot water
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Put sugar and milk in saucepan and heat slowly to boiling point without stirring
2. Boil 6 minutes
3. Break marshmallows in pieces and melt in double boiler
4. Add hot water and cook until mixture is smooth
5. Add hot syrup gradually, stirring constantly
6. Beat until cool enough to spread
7. Add vanilla
8. This may be used for frosting and filling
