## Ingredients

- cranberries (1 quart)
- water (1 pint)
- white sugar (1 pound)
- raisins (0.5 pound)
- walnuts, chopped (10 cents' worth)
- oranges (2)

## Directions

1. Boil cranberries and water together until cooked
2. Add white sugar, raisins, chopped walnuts, grated rind and juice of 2 oranges
3. Boil for 20 minutes
