## Ingredients

- juice of half an orange
- white of an egg
- tablespoonful of water
- sugar to sweeten
- lemon

## Directions

1. Mix juice of half an orange, white of an egg, tablespoonful of water, and sugar to sweeten
2. Strain through muslin
3. Lemon can be used
