## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- hot water

## Directions

1. Boil a chicken until tender
2. Take out all the bones and chop the meat very fine
3. Season with salt, pepper and plenty of butter
4. Add to the liquid the chicken was boiled in, 1 cup bread crumbs made soft with hot water, and to this the chopped chicken
5. When heated, take out and press into a basin
6. Serve cold
