## Ingredients

- buttered bread
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 1 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Line a baking dish with buttered bread
2. Whip 2 eggs, 1 cup grated cheese, 1 cup sweet milk together
3. Pour over bread
4. Bake in a very hot oven
