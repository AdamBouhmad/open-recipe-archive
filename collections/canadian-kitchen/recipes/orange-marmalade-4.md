## Ingredients

- 9 bitter oranges
- 3 sweet oranges
- 4 lemons
- 4 quarts water
- 8 pounds sugar
- A wineglass of whisky or brandy (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the oranges and lemons across the grain as fine as possible
2. Put all together in a jar and cover with four quarts of water
3. Let it stand for twenty-four hours
4. Boil one hour
5. Add sugar (white) and boil two hours
6. Pot for use
7. A wineglass of whisky or brandy may be added just before removing from fire to clarify the marmalade
