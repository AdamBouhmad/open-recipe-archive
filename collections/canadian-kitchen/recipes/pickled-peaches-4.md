## Ingredients

- peaches
- cloves
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- 4 or 6 pounds sugar to each gallon vinegar
- cinnamon stick
- mace

## Directions

1. Rub fruit with coarse cloth.
2. Stick one clove and one bit cinnamon into each peach.
3. Pack closely in a stone crock.
4. Boil vinegar enough to cover fruit with sugar.
5. Add cloves, cinnamon stick and a small quantity of mace tied in a thin muslin bag.
6. When all are boiled for five or seven minutes, pour over the peaches and cover closely.
7. Boil again the two days following and pour over fruit while boiling hot.
8. Cover close and keep in a cool cellar.
