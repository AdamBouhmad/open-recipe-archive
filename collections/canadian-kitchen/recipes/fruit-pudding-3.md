## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup suet, chopped
- 1 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup currants
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- Baking powder, salt, spices to taste

## Directions

1. Steam two hours in buttered mould.
2. Serve with hard sauce (butter and sugar creamed together and hot water sparingly added to thin to desired consistency), flavored with vanilla or lemon.
