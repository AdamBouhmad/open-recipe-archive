## Ingredients

- 2 quarts flour
- 3 tablespoons melted lard
- 1 pint honey
- 3/4 pint molasses
- level tablespoons soda
- 1 level teaspoon salt
- 3/4 pint water
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix flour, melted lard, honey, molasses, soda, salt, water and vanilla together
