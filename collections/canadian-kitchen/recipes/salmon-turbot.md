## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1 cup milk
- 4 soda biscuits
- butter half size of an egg
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Roll soda biscuits
2. Beat all ingredients well together
3. Steam 3 hours in a well-buttered dish
4. Turn out and cover with sauce made of 1 cup milk thickened with a tablespoon cornstarch, 1 egg, pepper and salt
5. Pour sauce over salmon
