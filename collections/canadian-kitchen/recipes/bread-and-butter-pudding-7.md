## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- raisins
- two eggs
- one pint and a half of milk
- sugar
- about a dessertspoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Spread your bread with a little butter and cut into slices about half an inch thick
2. Put into a flat buttered pudding dish, put one layer on the bottom of the dish and then put some raisins over it
3. Do this until the dish is full
4. Make a little custard with two eggs to a pint and a half of milk
5. Pour the custard over the bread, first sprinkling sugar over the bread
6. Then put a piece of butter, about a dessertspoonful, in the custard, so that it will float on the top
7. Bake in a moderate oven
