## Ingredients

- 1 cup cut cranberries
- 1½ cups chopped raisins
- 1 scant cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- ½ cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Bake between 2 pie crusts
