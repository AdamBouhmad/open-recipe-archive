## Ingredients

- rib-roast
- cold water

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from rib-roast and roll up neatly
2. Place in a kettle of cold water on the back of the stove
3. Let simmer slowly for five or six hours
4. When the liquid is boiled down, make gravy from it
5. Put your roast in the oven to cook 1 hour
