## Ingredients

- 1 package gelatine
- 4 lemons
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 cup pineapple juice
- 2½ cups boiling water
- ¾ cup chopped pineapple

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in one cup cold water for one-half hour
2. Add the grated rind of two lemons to this and soak for fifteen minutes
3. Add sugar, juice of four lemons, two and one-half cups boiling water and pineapple juice
4. Let come to a boil and strain into a mould which has been soaked in cold water
5. Drop three-quarters cup chopped pineapple into the mould and set away to cool
