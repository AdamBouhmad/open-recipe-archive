## Ingredients

- whitefish
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash and drain the fish
2. sprinkle with pepper and lay with the inside down upon the gridiron, and broil over fresh bright coals
3. when a nice brown, turn for a moment on the other side
4. take up and spread with butter
