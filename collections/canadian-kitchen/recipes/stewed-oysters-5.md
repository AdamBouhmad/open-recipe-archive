## Ingredients

- 2 quarts oysters
- 1 pint hot water
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 large cup boiling milk

## Directions

1. Add a pint of hot water to the liquor drained from 2 quarts of oysters
2. Put a little salt and pepper with it
3. Set over the fire and let it boil up once
4. Turn in the oysters and let them cook for not more than five minutes, or until the gills begin to crimp
5. Watch carefully for this stage, as every second beyond it means injury to the flavor of the oyster
6. Add 2 tablespoons of butter and a large cup of boiling milk
7. Take from the fire immediately and serve as soon as possible
8. Never let them stand at the back of the stove and keep warm
