## Ingredients

- 6 pounds firm pippins
- 1/4 pound green ginger root
- 4 lemons
- 5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut apples into even sized pieces while firm
2. Cut the ginger root into thin slices and the lemon peel into thin chips
3. Boil till it looks clear
4. Drain, and let stand till cold, using the water in which they are boiled to make the syrup
5. Simmer the apples in this syrup till they are tender enough to be pierced with a straw
6. Put them into fruit jars
7. Cook the lemon and ginger a little more in the syrup and then divide it among the jars of apples, filling up with a thick syrup
8. Screw the lids while the syrup is hot
