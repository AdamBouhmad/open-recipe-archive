## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Combine molasses, butter, milk, and flour
2. Add soda, ginger, and salt
3. Add raisins, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg
