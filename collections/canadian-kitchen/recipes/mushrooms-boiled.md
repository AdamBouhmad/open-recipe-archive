## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- melted butter
- buttered toast
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Wash, remove stems
2. dip caps in melted butter
3. place on broiler
4. broil 5 minutes
5. serve on pieces of buttered toast
6. sprinkle with seasonings
