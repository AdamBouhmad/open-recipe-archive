## Ingredients

- left-over meat (pork or beef)
- 1 slice bread
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- 1 egg
- sage or other herb

## Directions

1. Put left-over meat through the meat chopper
2. To 1 quart of the minced meat add 1 slice bread (also minced)
3. Add salt, pepper and onion to taste
4. Add 1 egg
5. Add a little sage or other herb if desired
6. Mix all well together
7. Form into small patties or balls
8. Fry a golden brown
