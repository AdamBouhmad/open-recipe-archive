## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 2 eggs
- 1 small cup milk
- 1/8 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup raisins
- lemon extract or grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cream 1/2 cup butter with 1 cup granulated sugar
2. Sift flour with 1 teaspoon cream tartar
3. Add 2 well-beaten eggs
4. Dissolve 1/8 teaspoon soda in milk
5. Dredge 1 cup raisins with flour
6. Combine all ingredients and flavor with lemon extract or grated nutmeg
