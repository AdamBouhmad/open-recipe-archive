## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream sugar with butter
2. Add the yolks of 2 eggs and nearly a cup of milk
3. Stir in 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons baking powder
4. Add the beaten whites of the 2 eggs
5. Bake in cups or muffin tins
