## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- yolk 1 egg
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/2 cups flour in which has been sifted 1 teaspoon soda
- white of egg
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3 tablespoons water
- chopped raisins or chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Mix 1 tablespoon butter, 3/4 cup brown sugar, yolk 1 egg, 1/4 cup molasses, 1/4 cup cold water, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and 1 1/2 cups flour in which has been sifted 1 teaspoon soda
2. Bake in two layers in a moderate oven
3. For icing: Beat white of egg to a stiff froth
4. Take 1 cup granulated sugar, add 3 tablespoons water, and boil till it threads
5. Pour into the beaten white of the egg and stir till it is cool enough to spread
6. Put between layers and on top
7. Chopped raisins or chopped nuts may be added to icing if desired
