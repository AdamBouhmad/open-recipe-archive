## Ingredients

- 2 cups of mashed potatoes cold
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1 cup sweet milk or cream

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients
2. Pour in deep dish and bake in quick oven
