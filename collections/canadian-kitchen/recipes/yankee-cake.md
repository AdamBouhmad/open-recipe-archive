## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 2 cups chopped raisins
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- icing sugar

1. Mix all ingredients
