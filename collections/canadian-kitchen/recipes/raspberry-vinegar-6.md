## Ingredients

- 9 quarts raspberries
- good strong vinegar
- 1 pint sugar to each pint of juice

## Directions

1. Take 9 quarts raspberries and cover with good strong vinegar
2. Let stand for 24 hours
3. Put all into thin muslin bag and let juice drain out
4. To each pint of juice add 1 pint sugar
5. Boil for 15 minutes
