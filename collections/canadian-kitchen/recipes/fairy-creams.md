## Ingredients

- good pie paste
- red raspberries (fresh or preserved)
- sugar
- flour
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Line patty pans with good pie paste
2. Fill half full with red raspberries
3. Sprinkle on each a pinch of sugar and flour
4. Bake and cool
5. Before serving heap them full of whipped cream
