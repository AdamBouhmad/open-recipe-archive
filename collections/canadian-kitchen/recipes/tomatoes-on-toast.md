## Ingredients

- firm tomatoes
- bread
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash and slice in half-inch rounds good firm tomatoes.
2. Place a layer of tomatoes on slices of bread half-inch thick.
3. Sprinkle with pepper and salt, and on each round of tomato put a small piece of butter.
4. Bake in shallow meat-pan in rather quick oven until bread turns golden brown.
5. Serve at once.
