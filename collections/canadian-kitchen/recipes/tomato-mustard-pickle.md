## Ingredients

- 4 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 quarts green cucumbers
- 1 quart onions
- 1 quart cauliflower
- 2 bunches celery
- 3 green peppers
- salt
- 1/2 gallon vinegar
- 4 tablespoons mustard
- 1 tablespoon tumeric
- 1 cup flour
- 4 cups white sugar

## Directions

1. Slice all very fine, sprinkle with salt; let stand over night
2. In the morning scald in the same water and then drain well
3. Boil 1/2 gallon vinegar, 4 tablespoons mustard, 1 tablespoon tumeric, 1 cup flour, 4 cups white sugar
4. Add the pickles; let all come to a boil, then seal airtight
