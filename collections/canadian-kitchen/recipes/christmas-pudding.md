## Ingredients

- 1 lb. suet
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 lb. currants
- 1 lb. grated breadcrumbs
- 1 lb. raisins
- 1/2 lb. citron
- 1 lb. sultanas
- 1/4 lb. orange peel
- 1 lb. moist sugar
- 1/4 lb. lemon peel
- 1/2 pt. milk
- 1/4 lb. flour (browned)

## Directions

1. Mix well and boil five hours.
