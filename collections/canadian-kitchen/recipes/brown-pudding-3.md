## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup suet
- 2 eggs
- 1 small teaspoon soda
- milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, cornmeal, molasses, suet, eggs, soda with enough milk to make a nice batter
2. Steam 2 hours
3. Serve with sweet sauce
