## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 White Swan Yeast Cake
- warm water or new milk
- candied lemon peel, caraway seeds, or raisins

## Directions

1. Beat eggs in a quart basin
2. Add butter, sugar, and White Swan Yeast Cake
3. Fill basin with warm water or new milk
4. Make into batter
5. In morning knead stiff
6. Add candied lemon peel, caraway seeds, or raisins
7. When risen, knead into buns
8. Bake in a quick oven
9. When nearly done wash with sugar and water
