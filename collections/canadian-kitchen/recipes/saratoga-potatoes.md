## Ingredients

- potatoes
- ice
- smoking-hot fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the potatoes with a plane into slices as thin as paper if possible.
2. Let them soak in cold water for a little time to wash out the starch.
3. Put them into fresh water with a piece of ice to thoroughly chill them.
4. Drain a few of the slices at a time, dry them on a napkin.
5. Put them in a frying-basket and immerse them in smoking-hot fat.
6. Keep them separated, and remove as soon as slightly colored.
7. Turn them into a colander to drain, and sprinkle them with salt.
