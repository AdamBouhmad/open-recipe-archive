## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Cool
3. Shape
4. Fry
5. Drain
6. Serve with sauce
