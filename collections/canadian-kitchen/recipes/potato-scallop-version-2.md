## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- rolled biscuit
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- egg

## Directions

1. Peel and slice thin raw potatoes
2. Butter a dish
3. Put in a layer of potatoes, a layer of rolled biscuit
4. Season with salt, pepper and butter
5. Continue in this way until the dish is filled
6. Just before putting in the oven cover with milk to which has been added a beaten egg
7. Bake slowly 1 hour
