## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 10 onions
- 4 heads celery
- 3 red peppers
- 1 cup salt
- 10 cups coffee sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 1 tablespoon allspice
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 1 quart vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onions and tomatoes fine
2. Put in a cup of salt and let stand all night
3. Drain as dry as possible
4. Chop celery and peppers together
5. Add sugar, spices, and vinegar
6. Mix and bottle
