## Ingredients

- 2 oz. chocolate
- 1 cup butter
- 1.5 cups sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 4 eggs
- 0.75 cup milk
- 1.5 cups flour
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve chocolate in five tablespoonfuls boiling water
2. Beat butter to a cream
3. Add gradually sugar, beating all while
4. Add yolks of eggs, beat again
5. Add milk, then the melted chocolate, and flour
6. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
7. Add to the mixture one teaspoon vanilla and baking powder sifted with flour
