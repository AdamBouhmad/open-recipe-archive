## Ingredients

- 1 ounce lean beef from shoulder or shin
- 2 ounces sausage meat
- crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- 1 slice onion
- teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons catsup
- flour
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix sausage meat with equal quantity of crumbs
2. Cut meat in slices 1/4 inch thick and spread on sausage
3. Roll up and tie firmly
4. Salt, pepper and dredge thickly with flour
5. Put into earthenware dish with other ingredients
6. Cover with boiling water and cook 5 hours in aladdin pail
