## Ingredients

- milk
- prepared rennet
- water
- cloth
- thread
- fine salt
- sweet grass

## Directions

1. Scald the quantity of milk desired; let it cool a little, then add the rennet according to directions on the packages of Prepared Rennet
2. When the curd is formed, take it out on a ladle without breaking it
3. Lay it on a thin cloth held by two persons
4. Dash a ladleful of water over each ladleful of curd, to separate the curd
5. Hang it up to drain the water off, and then put it under a light press for one hour
6. Cut the curd with a thread into small pieces
7. Lay a cloth between each two, and press for an hour
8. Take them out, rub them with fine salt, let them lie on a board for an hour, and wash them in cold water
9. Let them lie to drain, and in a day or two the skin will look dry
10. Put some sweet grass under and over them, and they will soon ripen
