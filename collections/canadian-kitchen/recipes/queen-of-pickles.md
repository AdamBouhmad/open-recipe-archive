## Ingredients

- 2 quarts white onions
- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 quarts green cucumbers
- 2 large cauliflowers
- 1 bunch celery
- 1 dozen green peppers
- cayenne
- salt
- vinegar
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 3.5 cup mustard
- 1 ounce tumeric
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cut up vegetables and sprinkle salt on over night
2. Drain off brine and cook all the above in vinegar until tender, not soft
3. Strain off vinegar and add to it 4 cups brown sugar, 1/2 cup flour, 3.5 cup mustard, 1 ounce tumeric, 1 egg, 1 tablespoon butter
4. Boil egg, vinegar, butter, mustard and flour together, being careful not to burn
5. Turn on vegetable and bottle
