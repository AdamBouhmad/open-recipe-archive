## Ingredients

- cold beef
- cracker crumbs
- 1 egg
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- hot water or meat gravy

## Directions

1. Chop the bits of cold beef very fine
2. To 1 cup of chopped meat add 1 cup of cracker crumbs
3. Add 1 egg, pepper and salt
4. Add a little chopped onion if desired
5. Add a little hot water or meat gravy if needed to make crumbs stick together
6. Make into little cakes
7. Fry in hot buttered pan
