## Ingredients

- 1 quart of medium-sized onions
- 1 teaspoonful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of hot milk
- whole pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel one quart of medium-sized onions, place them in a saucepan, cover with boiling water; add one teaspoonful of sugar, and boil until nearly done; add one teaspoonful salt; boil a few minutes longer, then drain in a colander.
2. Melt two tablespoonfuls of butter, add half a tablespoonful of flour.
3. Stir and cook two minutes; add one cupful of hot milk and cook two minutes longer, and season with whole pepper and salt.
4. Put the onions in a hot dish and pour the sauce over them.
