## Ingredients

- 2 heads cabbage
- 1/2 peck onions
- 3 1/2 peck tomatoes (green)
- 3 heads celery
- 1 ounce turmeric powder
- 3/4 pound mustard
- 2 pounds sugar
- 3 pints vinegar
- 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
