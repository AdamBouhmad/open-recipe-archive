## Ingredients

- 12 large potatoes
- 2 3/4 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 quart boiling water
- potato water
- 1 quart of cold water
- 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes
- warm water

## Directions

1. Take 12 large potatoes, peel boil and mash fine.
2. Take 2 3/4 cups flour and 2 tablespoons brown sugar and 1 tablespoon salt, and scald this with 1 quart boiling water.
3. Mix well, then add the potato water, then 1 quart of cold water.
4. Put 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes to soak in a little warm water and when the yeast is lukewarm, put the yeast cakes in and let rise.
5. One cup of yeast and 1 of water makes 1 loaf.
