## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- pepper and salt
- butter
- 6 tablespoons nice cream

## Directions

1. Beat to a froth the whites of 6 eggs, a little pepper and salt.
2. Pour into a buttered baking tin.
3. Dip upon it 6 tablespoons nice cream, 1 only in a place.
4. Upon each spoonful of cream drop 1 of the yolks whole (being careful not to break them).
5. Place in moderately hot oven to cook, and serve hot as omelet.
