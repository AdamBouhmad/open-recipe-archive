## Ingredients

- Slices of undercooked roast beef or mutton
- one-half pound flour
- one large or two small eggs
- salt
- milli
- lard or dripping for frying
- fried apples for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into moderately thick slices, and as neat a shape as possible, pepper and salt each piece
2. then make a batter in the above proportions, taking care that it is not very thin
3. Have ready a pan of boiling lard or dripping, dip each piece of meat into the batter, and fry quickly to a light brown
4. Serve on a hot dish, and garnish with a thick border of fried apples
