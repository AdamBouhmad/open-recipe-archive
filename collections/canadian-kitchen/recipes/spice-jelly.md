## Ingredients

- 1 peck crabapples
- 7 cups vinegar
- 5 cups water
- 1 ounce whole cinnamon
- 1 ounce whole allspice
- 3 1/2 ounce whole cloves
- 1 pound sugar to 1 pint of juice

## Directions

1. Boil and strain
2. Add 1 pound sugar to 1 pint of juice
3. Boil until it jells
