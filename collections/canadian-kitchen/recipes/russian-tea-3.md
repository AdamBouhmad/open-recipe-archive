## Ingredients

- one quart of boiling water
- two tablespoonfuls of black tea
- large cup or three parts of sugar
- juice of four lemons
- large piece of ice

## Directions

1. One quart of boiling water poured on two tablespoonfuls of black tea; add large cup or three parts of sugar and the juice of four lemons.
2. Let stand two hours, then strain through a cloth; add large piece of ice. A delicious drink for hot weather.
