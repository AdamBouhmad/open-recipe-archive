## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- any kind of fruit
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put any kind of fruit in bottom of dish
2. Pour over it batter made with 1 tablespoon butter, 1/2 cup white sugar, 1 egg, 3/4 cup sweet milk, 2 cups flour, 2 teaspoons cream tartar and 1 teaspoon soda
3. Add sugar on fruit as desired
4. Steam 1 1/2 hours
5. Serve with cream or with any sauce, as you please
