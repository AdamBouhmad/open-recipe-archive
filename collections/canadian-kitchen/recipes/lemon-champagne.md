## Ingredients

- 4 ounces citric acid
- 2 quarts warm water
- 5 pounds white sugar
- 1 dram oil of lemon
- soda

## Directions

1. Dissolve 4 ounces citric acid in a little warm water
2. Pour 2 quarts warm water on 5 pounds white sugar
3. When dissolved add acid and 1 dram oil of lemon
4. Stir all well and bottle for use
5. Use 1 tablespoon to glass of water, and a pinch of soda
