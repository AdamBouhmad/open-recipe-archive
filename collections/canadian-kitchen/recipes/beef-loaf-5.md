## Ingredients

- 3 pounds beef
- 4 biscuits
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- butter size of egg
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon pepper

## Directions

1. Chop beef fine and uncooked
2. Crush biscuits fine
3. Mix all ingredients
4. Shape into loaf
5. Bake in slow oven 2 1/2 hours
6. Baste often
7. Serve cold for lunch
