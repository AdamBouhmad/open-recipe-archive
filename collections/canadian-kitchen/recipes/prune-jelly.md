## Ingredients

- One pint of prunes
- Half a box of gelatine
- Sugar to taste
- A pinch of salt
- Essence of vanilla or juice and rind of a lemon
- Juice of two oranges

## Directions

1. Wash the prunes
2. Boil slowly till soft in sufficient water to cover
3. Take out the stones
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Add essence of vanilla, or if preferred the juice and rind of a lemon, and the juice of two oranges
6. Soak the gelatine till soft
7. Add the above, stir all well together
8. Pour into a mould and let stand till it is very cold
