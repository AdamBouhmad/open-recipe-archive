## Ingredients

- 1/2 package macaroni
- salt
- boiling water
- 1/2 can tomatoes
- salt and pepper
- butter
- 1 teacup grated cheese

## Directions

1. Put macaroni in salt boiling water
2. Boil for 3 1/2 hours
3. Drain and pour cold water over it
4. Drain again
5. Add 1/2 can tomatoes
6. Season with salt and pepper and butter
7. Boil all together
8. Remove from stove and add grated cheese
