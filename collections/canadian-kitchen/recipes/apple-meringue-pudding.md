## Ingredients

- 1 pt. stewed apples
- 3 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- Little cinnamon
- 1 teacup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- 1 teaspoonful essence of almond
- two tablespoonfuls of white sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the apples as for a pie, and stew till almost a pulp
2. Sweeten and spice
3. While hot stir in the yolks of the eggs gradually
4. Beat very light
5. Pour into a buttered dish and bake for ten minutes
6. Cover, without drawing it from the oven, with a meringue made of the beaten whites, two tablespoonfuls of white sugar, and the almond flavoring
7. Spread it over with a tablespoon, evenly and quickly
8. Close the oven again and brown very slightly
9. Serve either hot or cold, as preferred
