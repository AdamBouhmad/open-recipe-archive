## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup molasses
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup water
- 3 teaspoons vinegar
- 3/4 teaspoon cream tartar to 3 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- speck soda

## Directions

1. Bring molasses, sugar, water, and vinegar to a boil
2. Add cream tartar
3. Cook to hard ball stage
4. Add butter and soda
5. Pour out on buttered plate
6. Pull
