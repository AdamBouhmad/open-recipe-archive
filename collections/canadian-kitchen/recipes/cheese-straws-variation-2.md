## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 saltspoon salt
- 1 saltspoon paprika
- 1 saltspoon baking powder
- 1 cup grated cheese
- ¾ cup butter
- ice-water

## Directions

1. Sift into a bowl 1 cup flour, 1 saltspoon each of salt, paprika and baking powder
2. Add 1 cup grated cheese
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Add ¾ cup of butter and chop the ingredients together until of the consistency of meal
5. Mix to a paste with ice-water (use as little as possible)
6. Turn on to the pastry board and roll into a sheet about a quarter of an inch thick
7. Cut with a sharp knife into narrow strips about six inches long
8. Bake to a pale yellow
9. Arrange cob-house fashion on a doiley-covered plate
