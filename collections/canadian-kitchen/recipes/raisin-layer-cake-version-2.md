## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped raisins
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Mix raisins, sugar, butter, sour milk and eggs
2. Add flour and soda
3. Add spice to taste
4. Bake in layers
