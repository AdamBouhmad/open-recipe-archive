## Ingredients

- 1 white of egg
- 2.5 cups brown sugar
- 0.75 cup water
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water just past soft ball stage
2. Pour into beaten white
3. Add nuts and vanilla
4. Drop by spoonfuls on to buttered paper
