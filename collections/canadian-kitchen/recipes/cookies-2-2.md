## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups lard
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 1 small nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons cream tartar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Bake in quick oven
