## Ingredients

- A fresh fish of any kind
- A piece of butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Cut fish in pieces about 2 inches square
2. Place these in a bean crock with a piece of butter the size of an egg
3. Cover and place in the oven
4. The fish will cook in its own liquor
