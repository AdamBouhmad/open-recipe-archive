## Ingredients

- 1 cup light brown sugar
- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup walnuts, chopped fine
- butter size of a walnut
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and cream in water till it makes a ball in water
2. Put in the butter, vanilla and nuts
3. Beat creamy
4. Spread on a platter
