## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 eggs well-beaten
- 1/2 cup sour milk
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 3 1/2 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/4 cup cocoa
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 4 tablespoons boiling water

## Directions

1. Cream brown sugar and butter
2. Add well-beaten eggs and sour milk in which soda has been dissolved
3. Beat in gradually flour, baking powder and cocoa thoroughly mixed
4. Bake in 3 layers
5. For Caramel Filling: boil together milk, butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar until thickening slightly
6. Remove from fire and stir until thick enough to spread
7. For icing: boil together granulated sugar and boiling water until syrup hairs
8. Beat into frosting
