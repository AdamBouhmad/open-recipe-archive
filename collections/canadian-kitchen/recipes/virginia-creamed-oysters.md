## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 oysters per nappy
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cupful oyster liquor
- 1 cup cream
- baking soda
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cook butter and flour together until they bubble
2. Pour on them a cupful of oyster liquor and 1 of cream
3. Add a tiny pinch of baking soda to the cream
4. Stir over the fire in a double boiler until you have a thick, smooth sauce
5. Season to taste with salt and white pepper
6. Beat up hard once and pour over the oysters in the shells or the nappies
7. Set in the oven and after the shells or dishes are hot, bake for about 5 minutes
