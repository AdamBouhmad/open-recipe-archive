## Ingredients

- Yolks of two eggs
- one-quarter teaspoon salt
- dash of cayenne
- one cup of cold salad oil
- one tablespoon vinegar
- one tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let oil and egg be thoroughly chilled before beginning
2. Have the yolk entirely free from any white of egg
3. Add oil drop by drop
4. Success depends on adding oil slowly at first, afterwards it can be added in large quantities
5. When it has become a little thick alternate with a few drops of lemon juice
6. If it curdles add it to another yolk
7. If mustard is liked add a quarter of a teaspoon
