## Ingredients

- 1 pound prunes
- 1 teacupful sugar
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Stew one pound prunes and sweeten with one teacupful of sugar
2. Beat to a very stiff froth the whites of four eggs and stir lightly into the prunes when prunes are quite cold
3. Bake twenty minutes
4. Serve cold with cream
