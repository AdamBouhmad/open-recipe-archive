## Ingredients

- whites of two eggs
- one cup sugar
- one cupful of hot syrup of preserved pears, apricots, peaches or anything of that sort, or a cupful of hot milk

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs and sugar together well
2. This may be done an hour or more before serving
3. Add last thing before sending to table one cupful of hot syrup or hot milk
4. Beat all together and serve
