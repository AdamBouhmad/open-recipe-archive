## Ingredients

- One and one-half cups of minced fish
- Two-thirds of a tablespoonful of butter
- Two-thirds of a cup of milk
- One and one-half tablespoonfuls of flour
- One tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- One-half teaspoonful of celery salt
- Grating of nutmeg
- Salt and pepper
- Puree of peas and potatoes

## Directions

1. Butter and flour put in saucepan stirred until well blended
2. Add milk, cook, stirring constantly till it leaves bottom and sides of pan
3. Add flavorings, seasoning and fish
4. Mix well together
5. Form into balls without using flour
6. Arrange down the centre of a dish which has been garnished with a puree of peas and potatoes using pastry bag
7. Garnish with parsley
