## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon golden syrup
- 3/4 cup butter
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- spices to suit taste
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 cup nut meats
- 3 small cups oatmeal (rolled oats)
- 2 good cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Drop on baking sheet
3. Bake
