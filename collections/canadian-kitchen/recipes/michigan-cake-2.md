## Ingredients

- ¾ cup black molasses
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1½ cups flour
- 1 cup hot water
- 1½ cups white sugar
- water
- ¾ cup raisins
- egg white

## Directions

1. Bake in two layers
2. For filling: boil 1½ cups white sugar and a little water till it hairs
3. Chop ¾ cup raisins fine
4. Beat the white while syrup boils
5. Put the raisins in the syrup and stir well before putting in the egg
6. Spread between and on top of the cake
