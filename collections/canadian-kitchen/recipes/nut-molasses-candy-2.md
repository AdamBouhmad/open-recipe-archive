## Ingredients

- molasses
- nuts
- peanuts

## Directions

1. When making molasses candy, add any kind of nuts you fancy
2. Put them in after the syrup has thickened, and is ready to take from the fire
3. Pour out on buttered tins
4. Mark it off in squares before it gets too cool
5. Peanuts should be fresh roasted and then tossed in a sieve, to free them of their inner skins
