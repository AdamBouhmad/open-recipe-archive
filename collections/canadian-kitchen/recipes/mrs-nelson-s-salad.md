## Ingredients

- 10 large potatoes
- 2 onions
- 1 bunch celery
- 10c. worth of nuts
- dressing

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes and cut into dice
2. Chop onions
3. Chop celery
4. Mix together
5. Add dressing
