## Ingredients

- one bunch salsify
- two eggs
- one-quarter cup milk
- flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape and grate the roots, and stir into a batter made of the beaten eggs, the milk, and flour
2. Grate the salsify directly into this, that it may not blacken by exposure to the air
3. Salt, and drop a spoonful into the hot fat to see if it is of the right consistency
4. As fast as you fry the fritters, throw into a hot colander to drain
5. One great spoonful of batter should make a fritter
