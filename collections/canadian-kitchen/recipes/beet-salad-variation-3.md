## Ingredients

- 1 quart chopped cabbage
- 1 quart boiled beets chopped
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup grated horseradish
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon red pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 quart chopped cabbage, 1 quart boiled beets chopped, 2 cups sugar, 1 cup grated horseradish, 1 teaspoon black pepper, 3/4 teaspoon red pepper, 1 tablespoon salt
2. cover with cold vinegar, put in a crock and cover
3. Will keep all winter
