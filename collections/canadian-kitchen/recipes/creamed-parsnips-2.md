## Ingredients

- parsnips
- butter
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil, scrape, and slice crosswise
2. Heat a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan
3. Put in the parsnips and shake and turn until all are coated with the butter and very hot
4. Turn them into a deep dish
5. Pour over them a sauce made by adding to the butter left in the saucepan a teaspoonful of flour and thinning it with three or four tablespoonfuls of hot cream
6. Boil up once, and when you have covered the parsnips with it, serve
