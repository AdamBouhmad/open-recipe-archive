## Ingredients

- 2 dozen oysters
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of curry
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 2 ounces of butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop the onion up quite fine
2. Mix the curry-powder, flour and butter together
3. Put all these ingredients into a stew-pan
4. Simmer till of a nice brown, stirring all the time
5. Add the liquor of the oysters and the lemon-juice
6. Boil together for five minutes
7. Put in the oysters
8. Boil up once
9. Serve with a dish of rice
