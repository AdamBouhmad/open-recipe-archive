## Ingredients

- 3 cups potato water
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- yeast
- 3 cups warm water
- 3 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Save 3 cups potato water and add 1/2 cup granulated sugar; pour in the yeast.
2. At night take 3 cups of this liquid and to it add 3 cups warm water (always cup for cup).
3. Allow 3 cups of flour to 1 of liquid; add 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon salt.
4. Knead well and put in pan to rise.
5. In morning make into loaves.
6. Grease loaves with melted butter just before placing in oven.
