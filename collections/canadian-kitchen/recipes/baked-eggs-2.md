## Ingredients

- 6 or 7 eggs
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Break 6 or 7 eggs into a buttered dish, taking care that each is whole and does not encroach upon the others so much as to mix or disturb the yolks
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
3. Put a bit of butter upon each
4. Put into an oven and bake until whites are set
5. Nice for breakfast served on toast or alone
