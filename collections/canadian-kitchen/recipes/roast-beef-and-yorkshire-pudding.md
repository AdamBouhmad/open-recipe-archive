## Ingredients

- roast
- flour
- salt
- hot water

## Directions

1. Place grate in roasting pan, put roast in this
2. Dredge meat with flour and salt
3. When the flour is browned put in 1/2 pint hot water
4. Watch carefully that the water does not boil away and the gravy become burned
5. Add little water at a time and baste often
