## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 4 tablespoons blackstrap
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- a pinch of salt
- 2 cups flour (scant)
- dates for filling

## Directions

1. Bake in 2 layers
2. Cook the dates and spread between layers
