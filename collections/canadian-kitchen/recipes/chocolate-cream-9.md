## Ingredients

- six ounces chocolate, grated
- six ounces sugar
- one pint cream
- yolks of six eggs
- two ounces gelatine

## Directions

1. Grate finely six ounces chocolate
2. put chocolate into a saucepan with cream, sugar, and yolks of eggs
3. Stir over fire until it thickens, run through sieve
4. add dissolved gelatine
5. Mix and fill mould and place on ice
