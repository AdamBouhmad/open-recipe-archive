## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup cocoanut
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- butter size of an egg
- yolk of 2 eggs
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Beat the whites for the top
