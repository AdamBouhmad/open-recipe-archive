## Ingredients

- chopped cold roast meat
- stewed or canned tomatoes
- roast gravy or stock
- mashed potatoes
- rolled biscuit
- butter

## Directions

1. place in a granite pan and cover with stewed or canned tomatoes and a little of the roast gravy or stock
2. cover with mashed potatoes and rolled biscuit and dot with butter
3. Bake 3/4 of an hour in oven
