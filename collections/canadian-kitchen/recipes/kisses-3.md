## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch
- 2 cups cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs stiff
2. Remove the lid of the kettle, set the bowl in the kettle and stir fifteen minutes
3. Add 2 cups of cocoanut
4. Drop on buttered paper
5. Bake in slow oven
