## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking syrup
- 1 cup chopped figs
- 1 3/4 cups buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- flour to make a good stiff batter

## Directions

1. Mix egg, butter, sugar, baking syrup, chopped figs, and buttermilk together
2. Add soda and flour to make a good stiff batter
3. Steam 1 hour
