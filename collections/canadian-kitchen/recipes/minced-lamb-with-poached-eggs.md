## Ingredients

- Remains of cold roast lamb
- one good cup of gravy
- pepper
- salt
- seasoning of mint
- poached eggs
- buttered toast
- a little brown flour

## Directions

1. Trim the meat and mince it finely, well seasoned with pepper, salt, and a little mint
2. Put the gravy into a saucepan (make it from the bones if you have no other), and let it get hot
3. then stir in the mince and let all become very hot, but do not let it boil, thicken with a little brown flour, and pile on a flat dish
4. Have ready a few slices of buttered toast, cut into neat squares, lay a poached egg on each, place these around or upon the mince, and serve
