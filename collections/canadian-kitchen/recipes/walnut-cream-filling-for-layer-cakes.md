## Ingredients

- Quarter pound of butter
- quarter pound icing sugar
- half pound walnuts
- vanilla
- a little sherry

## Directions

1. Beat butter with sugar to a cream
2. chop the walnuts fine and beat them well into the sugar and butter
3. flavor with vanilla and a little sherry
