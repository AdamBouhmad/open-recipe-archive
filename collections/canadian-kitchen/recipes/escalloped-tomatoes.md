## Ingredients

- cracker crumbs
- butter
- tomatoes
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Put into an earthen baking dish a layer of cracker crumbs and small bits of butter
2. Then a layer of tomatoes with a little sugar sprinkled over them
3. Add another layer of cracker crumbs, seasoned with butter and a layer of tomatoes until your dish is full, with cracker crumbs on top
4. Pour over a little water to moisten
5. Bake for half hour
