## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- flour enough to make stiff batter

## Directions

1. Drop by spoonfuls into hot fat and fry
2. Serve hot with syrup
