## Ingredients

- one ounce butter
- two ounces flour
- one yolk of an egg
- water
- salt
- two ounces grated cheese
- yolks of two eggs
- pepper
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Make a paste of one ounce butter, two ounces flour, one yolk of an egg, a little water and salt.
2. Line some patty pans with the paste.
3. Beat up two ounces grated cheese in a basin with yolks of two eggs.
4. Add pepper, salt, etc.
5. Work in a little cream or milk.
6. Fill each patty with mixture and bake in moderate oven.
