## Ingredients

- 1 lb. garlic, chopped
- 1 lb. onions, chopped
- 1 lb. raisins, chopped
- 1 lb. brown sugar
- 1 lb. salt
- 1 lb. ground mustard
- 1 lb. ground ginger
- 1 oz. cayenne pepper
- 3 pts. vinegar
- 13 large sour apples peeled and cored
- 13 large tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cook apples and tomatoes together and mash.
2. Mix all together.
3. Cook till thoroughly soft, bottle.
