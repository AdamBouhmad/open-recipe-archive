## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- 1 pt. milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Let milk boil, then add the cornstarch moistened with milk
2. boil sugar and scorch on a tin pie-dish
3. Then pour into the cornstarch, stirring very quickly
4. Pour into mould
