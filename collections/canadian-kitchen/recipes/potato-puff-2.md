## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1 cup of milk
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Add 2 tablespoons melted butter to 2 cups mashed potatoes and beat until light
2. Mix in 2 eggs well beaten, a cup of milk and salt to taste
3. Beat together thoroughly
4. Bake in a buttered pudding dish in a quick oven until nicely browned
