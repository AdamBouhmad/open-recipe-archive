## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 1 quart onions
- 5 green peppers
- 1/2 cup salt
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground cloves
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 4 cups vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop fine and press out water
2. Put in kettle
3. Add salt, sugar, cloves, and cinnamon
4. Boil 1 hour
