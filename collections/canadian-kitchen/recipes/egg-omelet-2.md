## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk
- salt and pepper
- tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Separate whites and yolks.
2. Beat whites to very stiff froth.
3. Beat yolks and 4 tablespoons milk with a sprinkle of salt and pepper.
4. Have ready a frying-pan with tablespoon butter in it.
5. When hot, stir both mixtures together quickly, turn into pan.
6. When a nice brown on bottom, turn 1/2 over on the other.
7. Let cook a minute longer, serve at once.
