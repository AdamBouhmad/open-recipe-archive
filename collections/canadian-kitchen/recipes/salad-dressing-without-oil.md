## Ingredients

- Yolks of 11 eggs
- 3 teaspoonfuls salt
- 3 teaspoonfuls mustard
- 1 1/2 cups vinegar
- 9 teaspoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. In a double boiler put the yolks of eggs, salt, mustard, and sugar; stir well
2. Add vinegar (not too strong)
3. Cook till quite thick, stirring all the time
4. When sufficiently cooked take from the fire and beat with an egg-beater until cool
5. This will keep for weeks
6. When required to use add cream to thin
