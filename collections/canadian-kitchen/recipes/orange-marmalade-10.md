## Ingredients

- 1 dozen bitter oranges
- 5 quarts cold water
- 7 pounds granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Peel off the rind as thin as possible in long strips with a sharp knife
2. Remove the white part
3. Cut the yellow part in very thin shreds about 1 inch long
4. Cut the white part into bits the size of small beans
5. Cut up the pulp rather fine, saving the pips
6. Put pips in a bowl with 1 pint water poured over them
7. Pour the 5 quarts water on the cut-up rind and pulp
8. Let stand over night
9. Next day add the liquid from the pips, and boil 3 hours
10. Set away until next day, bring to a boil
11. Add the sugar (which has been heated)
12. Boil an hour or less if it jellies
13. Put in glasses
