## Ingredients

- 1 peck ripe tomatoes
- 1/2 cup cooking sugar
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- salt to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 3 tablespoons mixed spice

## Directions

1. Extract juice from tomatoes
2. Use 2 quarts juice
3. Add sugar, vinegar, salt, ginger, and cayenne pepper
4. Tie mixed spice in a bag and add
5. Boil 3 hours
