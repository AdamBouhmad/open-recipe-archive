## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- good dripping
- 1 sliced onion
- 1 cupful of stock or consomme
- 1 tablespoonful of tomato catsup
- 1 dash of lemon-juice
- 1 tablespoonful of brown roux
- 1 glass of sherry

## Directions

1. Joint as for fricassee
2. Pepper, salt, and flour the duck
3. Heat good dripping in a frying-pan and fry a sliced onion to a light brown
4. Take out the onion, put in the duck, and cook ten minutes, turning two or three times
5. Put into a sauce-pan a cupful of stock or consomme, and while it is still cold lay in the jointed duck
6. Cover and stew slowly until tender
7. Season with pepper and salt, a tablespoonful of tomato catsup, and a dash of lemon-juice
8. Simmer three minutes
9. Stir in a tablespoonful of brown roux
10. Cook a minute to thicken it
11. Add a glass of sherry, and serve
