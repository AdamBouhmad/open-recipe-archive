## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 even tablespoon cornstarch
- yolks of 2 eggs
- juice and grated rind of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Bake in 3 layers
2. For icing: Cook sugar, water, cornstarch, yolks of eggs, juice and grated rind of lemons all together until it thickens
3. When cool spread on the layers of cake
