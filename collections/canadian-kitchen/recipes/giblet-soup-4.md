## Ingredients

- One quart of chicken stock
- Giblets of two fowls
- A little chopped parsley
- Two tablespoonfuls of brown roux
- Hard-boiled yolks
- A little butter
- A raw egg
- Browned flour

## Directions

1. Heat one quart of chicken stock
2. When it boils, stir in the finely minced giblets of two fowls with a little chopped parsley, cook half an hour and thicken with two tablespoonfuls of brown roux
3. Season judiciously
4. Make force-meat balls of hard-boiled yolks, rubbed to a paste with a little butter, bound with a raw egg and rolled in browned flour
5. Drop force-meat balls in one minute before the soup leaves the fire
