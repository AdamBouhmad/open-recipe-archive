## Ingredients

- 1 head cabbage
- 1 bunch celery
- 3 large green cucumbers
- 12 medium green tomatoes
- vinegar
- 1 pound mustard
- tumeric
- salt
- sugar to taste
- black and red pepper

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage, celery, cucumbers, and tomatoes fine
2. Cover with vinegar
3. Put on stove and simmer well
4. Add mustard, tumeric, salt, sugar to taste, and black and red pepper
5. Simmer 20 minutes
