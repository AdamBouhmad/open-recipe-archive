## Ingredients

- young turnips
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter.
2. Cook half an hour, or until tender, but not broken, in boiling, salted water.
3. Drain, still without breaking, and dish.
4. Sprinkle with pepper and salt, then butter plentifully and serve.
