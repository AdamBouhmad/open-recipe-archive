## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- chopped salt pork
- bacon
- water
- sliced onions
- sweet herbs
- mace

## Directions

1. Wash and stuff them with bread crumbs seasoned with pepper, salt, butter or chopped salt pork, and fasten them tight
2. Line a stewpan with slices of bacon
3. Add a quart of water and a bit of butter the size of a goose egg, or else four slices of salt pork
4. Add, if you like, sliced onions, sweet herbs and mace
5. Stew till tender
6. Take them up and strain the gravy over them
7. Add boiling water if the liquor is too much reduced
