## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- six hard-boiled eggs
- seasonings
- 2 cups thin white sauce
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut up cold boiled potatoes and six hard-boiled eggs into slices
2. Arrange alternate layers with seasonings in buttered baking dish
3. Pour over it 2 cups thin white sauce
4. Cover with buttered crumbs
5. Bake until browned
