## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. add the egg well beaten, then the milk and vanilla
3. add the flour
4. Bake in quick oven
