## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Bake in long tin
2. when done ice with chocolate and sprinkle with chopped walnuts cut in squares
