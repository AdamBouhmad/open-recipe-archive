## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon gelatine
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1/2 cup boiling water
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 1/2 cup orange juice
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- English walnuts

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water 10 minutes
2. make a syrup of cold water until slightly yellow, pour syrup over gelatine to dissolve it
3. add lemon juice
4. Cover bottom of shallow pan with half of the mixture
5. When firm, place on it, 1 inch apart, halves of English walnuts, cover with remaining mixture
6. Chill, cut in squares for serving
