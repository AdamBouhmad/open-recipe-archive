## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- white milk sauce
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Take boiled potatoes left over from dinner and cut in dice
2. Put in pudding dish
3. Pour over this a white milk sauce
4. Cover the top with a layer of grated cheese
5. Bake for 1/2 hour
6. Serve hot
