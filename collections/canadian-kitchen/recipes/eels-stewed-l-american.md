## Ingredients

- Three pounds eels, skinned, cleaned, and fat removed from inside
- One young onion chopped
- Four tablespoonfuls of butter
- Pepper and salt
- Little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the eels in pieces, about two inches in length
2. Season and lay in a saucepan containing the melted butter
3. Strew the onion and parsley over all
4. Cover the saucepan closely, and set in a pot of cold water
5. Bring this gradually to a boil, then cook very gently for an hour and a half, or until the eels are tender
6. Turn out into a deep dish
