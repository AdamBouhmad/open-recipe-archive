## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded pineapple
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 sweet red pepper
- mayonnaise cream dressing
- lettuce hearts
- nut meats

## Directions

1. Add 1 cup chopped celery and 1 sweet red pepper, cut into dice to 2 cups shredded pineapple
2. Use mayonnaise cream dressing
3. Serve very cold on lettuce hearts
4. Garnish with nut meats
