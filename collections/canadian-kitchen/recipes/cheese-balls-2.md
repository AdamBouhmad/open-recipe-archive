## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- speck cayenne
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Fold in 3 egg whites beaten stiff
2. Shape in small balls
3. Roll in fine crumbs
4. Fry
5. Drain
