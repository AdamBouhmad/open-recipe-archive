## Ingredients

- 8 lbs. ripe tomatoes
- 1 lb. onions
- 1 lb. apples
- 1 lb. raisins
- 2 lemons
- 1 lb. brown sugar
- 1 cup suet
- 1/2 oz. red pepper
- 1 qt. vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop all fine together.
2. Add sugar, suet, pepper and vinegar.
3. Boil two hours.
