## Ingredients

- 1 pint cold cooked beef
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- a dash of cayenne
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- a grating of nutmeg
- 1/2 pint milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Chop sufficient cold cooked beef to make 1 pint
2. Add to it 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon onion juice, a dash of cayenne, 3/4 teaspoon pepper, and a grating of nutmeg
3. Put half a pint of milk over the fire
4. Rub together 1 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons flour
5. Add to the hot milk and stir until you have a smooth, thick paste
6. Take from the fire and mix with it the meat
7. Let cool
8. When cool, form in croquettes
9. Beat 1 egg and add to it
