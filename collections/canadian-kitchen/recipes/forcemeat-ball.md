## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked meat
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 3/4 teaspoon thyme
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- pepper
- yolk of 1 egg
- egg

## Directions

1. Mix in order given, adding enough egg to moisten meat
2. Form in balls size of a marble
3. Roll in flour and brown in hot fat
