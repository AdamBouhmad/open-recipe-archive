## Ingredients

- 2 oz. butter
- 2 oz. flour
- 2 oz. breadcrumbs
- 2 oz. cheese
- salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Grate the cheese and mix the ingredients into a paste; season with the pepper and salt; roll out very thin and cut into strips quarter of an inch wide and six inches long, then twist several times and lay on a buttered tin dish.
2. Bake about five minutes.
