## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 teacup melted butter
- Milk enough to make batter
- 2 teacups flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- A pinch of salt
- 1 cake chocolate and 1 teacup water
- Confectioner's sugar

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks separately first, and then together; beat into this sugar, and then melted butter.
2. Sift flour into which you have stirred two teaspoons baking powder and a pinch of salt.
3. Beat it gradually into the eggs, etc., and then add enough milk to make a moderately stiff batter.
4. Bake in two layer cake tins in quick oven.
5. Melt chocolate in teacup boiling water on stove; add sugar enough to stiffen it into a thick paste; put this between layers while they are hot and ice top of cake.
