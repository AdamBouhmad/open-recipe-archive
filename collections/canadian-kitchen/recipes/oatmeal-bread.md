## Ingredients

- ½ pt. oatmeal
- 2½ pts. flour
- ½ teaspoonful salt
- 1½ pts. salted water
- 3 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- ⅔ pts. milk

## Directions

1. Boil the oatmeal in water for one hour
2. Add milk; set aside until cold
3. Then place in bowl, sift together flour, salt, and powder, and add
4. Mix together smoothly and deftly
5. Bake in greased tin forty-five minutes, protected with paper twenty minutes
