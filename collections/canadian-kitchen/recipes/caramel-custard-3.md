## Ingredients

- One-half cup sugar
- Two tablespoonfuls water
- One quart milk
- Six eggs
- One-half teaspoonful salt
- One teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Put sugar in pan
2. Stir until it melts and is light brown
3. Add the water
4. Stir into the warm milk
5. Beat eggs lightly
6. Add salt and vanilla and part of the milk
7. Strain into the remainder of the milk
8. Pour into a buttered two-quart mould
9. Set the mould in a pan of warm water
10. Bake from thirty to forty minutes, or till firm
11. Serve with cream and fruit sugar
