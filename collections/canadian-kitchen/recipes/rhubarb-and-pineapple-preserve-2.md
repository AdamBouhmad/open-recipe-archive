## Ingredients

- 8 pounds rhubarb
- 1 pineapple sliced and cut in small pieces
- 5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Wash rhubarb but do not peel
2. Cut in pieces about 1 inch long and dry it
3. Slice and cut in small pieces 1 pineapple and boil it separate 1/2 hour
4. Mix with rhubarb
5. Add 5 pounds sugar and let simmer till cooked
