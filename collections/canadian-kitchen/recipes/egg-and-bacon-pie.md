## Ingredients

- short paste
- bacon
- 2 eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Make a short paste and line pie plate
2. Cover with bacon cut into fine strips
3. Beat 2 eggs with a little milk, season to taste
4. Pour over bacon
5. Cover with crust and bake in moderate oven
