## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon each of cloves and allspice
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, and butter together
2. Take 1/4 cup out to sprinkle on top of cake
3. Add spices to the remainder (1 teaspoon each of cloves and allspice)
4. Add 1 teaspoon soda and 1 cup sour milk to the remainder
5. Add 1 cup raisins
6. Sprinkle reserved portion on top
