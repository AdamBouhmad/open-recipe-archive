## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated potatoes
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 lemon
- butter size of an egg
- pie crust

## Directions

1. Boil 1 cup grated potatoes, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup boiling water, 1 lemon (juice and rind grated), and butter size of an egg together until thick
2. Put into the pie crust and cover just as any other pie would be covered
