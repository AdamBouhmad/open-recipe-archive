## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of pearl tapioca
- 1 large cup of cold water
- 1 quart of well-seasoned boiling clear stock

## Directions

1. Soak two tablespoonfuls of pearl tapioca in a large cup of cold water four hours.
2. Stir into a quart of well-seasoned boiling clear stock, and simmer ten minutes.
