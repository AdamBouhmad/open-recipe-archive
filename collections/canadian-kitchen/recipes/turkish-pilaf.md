## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 1/2 cups stewed tomatoes
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and drain rice
2. Cook in butter until brown
3. Add water and steam until absorbed
4. Add tomatoes
5. Cook until soft
6. Season
