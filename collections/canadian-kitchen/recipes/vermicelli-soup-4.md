## Ingredients

- A knuckle of lamb
- A small piece of veal
- Water to cover well
- Salt and pepper
- Herbs to taste
- A small onion
- About 1 tablespoon Halford or Worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 pound vermicelli

## Directions

1. Cook lamb and veal in water until well cooked
2. Season with salt and pepper, herbs to taste, onion and Halford or Worcestershire sauce
3. Boil vermicelli until tender
4. Strain soup from the meat, add the vermicelli
5. Let it boil well and serve
