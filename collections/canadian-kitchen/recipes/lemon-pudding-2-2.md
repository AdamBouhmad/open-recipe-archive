## Ingredients

- 1 lemon (juice and rind)
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 1/2 cups boiling water
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook lemon, sugar, cornstarch and water till thick.
2. Take from stove and when nearly cool, stir in the whites of 2 eggs beaten to a stiff froth.
3. Serve with sauce.
