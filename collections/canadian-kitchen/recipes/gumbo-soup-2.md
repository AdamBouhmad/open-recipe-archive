## Ingredients

- 1 slice bacon
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 2 quarts fresh tomatoes, peeled and cut up
- 1 quart okra, cut thin
- 1 teaspoonful chopped parsley
- 3 quarts hot broth
- 1 scant tablespoonful salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful pepper

## Directions

1. Try out the fat of bacon and drain it off
2. Fry the slices of onion brown in the bacon fat
3. Put tomatoes, okra, and chopped parsley together in a stew kettle with hot broth
4. Let it cook slowly for three hours
5. Season with salt and pepper
