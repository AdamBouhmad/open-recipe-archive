## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 5 eggs
- scant teaspoonful cream of tartar
- scant 1/2 teaspoonful soda
- 2/3 cup milk

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients and bake. This recipe was used before baking powder was known. A heaping teaspoonful of baking powder could be used instead.
