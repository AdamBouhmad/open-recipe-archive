## Ingredients

- 1 pint or more of beans
- water
- celery tops
- parsley
- onion
- celery salt or table salt

## Directions

1. Cover beans with water
2. Heat and boil from 10 to 30 minutes
3. Drain after this parboiling
4. Now cover well with water
5. Flavor with celery tops, parsley or onion, using one or all to suit taste
6. Season with celery salt (table salt may be used)
7. If desired for soup, boil till soft
8. If for baking, when the skins curl back they are ready for the oven
