## Ingredients

- 8 lbs. tomatoes
- 3 lbs. onions
- 3 pts. vinegar
- 2 doz. small peppers
- 1 lb. prunes, stoned
- 1 lb. raisins
- 2 lbs. sugar
- 2 oz. ground ginger
- 1 lb. mustard seed
- 1 cup salt

## Directions

1. Chop well, cook slowly, stir often and seal well.
