## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1/2 cup sour cream

## Directions

1. Let come to a boil
2. When cold add sour cream
3. For 2 cans salmon
