## Ingredients

- oysters
- one egg
- one tablespoonful of warm water
- fine bread-crumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- one tablespoonful of chopped celery
- one-half cupful of thin cream

## Directions

1. Pick over, rinse, drain, and dry the oysters, which should be of fair size
2. Break an egg into a saucer and add a tablespoonful of warm water
3. Beat just enough to mix
4. Have ready in a bowl a quantity of fine bread-crumbs
5. Drop each oyster in the beaten egg, then into the crumbs, and lay in a buttered dish
6. When the bottom of the dish is covered with the oysters sprinkle over them a little salt and pepper
7. Add a few drops of onion-juice and a tablespoonful of chopped celery
8. Fill the dish in the same order
9. Put over the top one tablespoonful of butter cut into pieces
10. Pour over one-half of a cupful of thin cream
11. Bake about twenty-five minutes in a hot oven
