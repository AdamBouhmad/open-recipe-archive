## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup lard or other shortening
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda dissolved in vinegar
- flour sufficient to make a stiff dough

## Directions

1. Put molasses, sugar and lard on the stove and boil up, but do not burn
2. Add 1 teaspoon ginger and a heaping teaspoon soda dissolved in a little vinegar
3. Stir well, remove from fire
4. Add flour sufficient to make a stiff dough
