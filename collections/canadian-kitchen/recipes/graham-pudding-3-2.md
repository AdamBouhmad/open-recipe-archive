## Ingredients

- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1/2 cup yellow sugar
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 cup chopped dates, raisins, figs or currants (optional)

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in the water.
2. Combine all ingredients.
3. Steam 2 or 3 hours.
