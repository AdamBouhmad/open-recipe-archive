## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 rounding teaspoon soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Combine shortening, sour milk, brown sugar, and molasses
2. Add eggs and ginger
3. Add soda
4. Add flour enough to make a thick batter
5. Drop from a spoon in drops as large as an egg in a bread pan, far enough apart not to touch
6. To be eaten warm
