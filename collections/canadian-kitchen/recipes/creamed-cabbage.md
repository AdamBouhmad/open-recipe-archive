## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- milk
- flour
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil cabbage in salted water until tender
2. Drain and pour milk enough to almost cover
3. Mix flour and cold milk and add just as milk and cabbage boils
4. Cook five minutes
5. Add butter and nutmeg to taste
