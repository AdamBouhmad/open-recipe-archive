## Ingredients

- 1 quart of peeled tomatoes
- A pinch of soda
- 1 quart of hot boiled milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- A small piece of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of rolled crackers

## Directions

1. Add a pinch of soda to one quart of peeled tomatoes, boil for one hour, or longer
2. Strain and return to the fire and add one quart of hot boiled milk
3. Season with salt, pepper, and a small piece of butter
4. Add three tablespoonfuls of rolled crackers and serve hot
