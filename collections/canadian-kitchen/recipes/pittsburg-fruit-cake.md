## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup New Orleans molasses
- 1 cup chopped apples
- 1 cup sour milk
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- flour enough to make a batter

## Directions

1. Bake in a square pan with a slow fire about 45 minutes
2. When baked, it is moist
3. Frost if you like
