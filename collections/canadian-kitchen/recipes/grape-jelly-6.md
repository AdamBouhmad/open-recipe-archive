## Ingredients

- Small cupful of water
- Grapes to fill a four-quart kettle
- One pound of sugar to a pint of juice

## Directions

1. Put small cupful of water in the bottom of a four-quart kettle, filled with grapes
2. Let fruit boil until all the juice is extracted
3. Strain and add a pound of sugar to a pint of juice
4. Boil until it jellies
