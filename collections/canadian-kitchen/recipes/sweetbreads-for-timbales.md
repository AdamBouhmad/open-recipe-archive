## Ingredients

- 2 pairs sweetbreads
- 1 can mushrooms
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1.5 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1 small onion
- 1 blade mace
- Salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Boil sweetbreads twenty minutes in salted water, throw in cold water to harden, free from skin and cut in dice, cut mushrooms in dice.
2. Put onion and mace in milk till flavored, then take out, melt butter, add flour, then the milk, let boil a minute.
3. Add sweetbreads and mushrooms.
