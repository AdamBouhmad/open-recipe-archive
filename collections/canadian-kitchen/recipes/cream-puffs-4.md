## Ingredients

- 1 cup hot water
- 1 1/2 cups butter
- 1 cup sifted flour
- 3 unbeaten eggs

## Directions

1. Boil water and butter together
2. While boiling stir in sifted flour
3. Take from stove and stir to a thin paste
4. After this cools stir in 3 unbeaten eggs
5. Stir 5 minutes
6. Drop in tablespoonfuls on buttered tins
7. Bake in quick oven 25 minutes, opening door not oftener than necessary
8. Be careful that they do not touch each other
9. Fill when cold
