## Ingredients

- butter
- apples
- egg
- sugar
- water
- fine bread crumbs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Butter a deep tin pan
2. Cover the bottom with a layer of apples
3. Beat egg, put in enough sugar to sweeten apple, add a little water
4. Thicken with fine bread crumbs
5. Flavor lightly with lemon
6. Pour this mixture over apples and bake until the apples are done, which will take about an hour
