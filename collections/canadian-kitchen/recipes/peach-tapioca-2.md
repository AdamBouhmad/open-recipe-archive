## Ingredients

- 1 cup tapioca
- syrup from a can of peaches
- water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- a little salt
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. soak tapioca 1 hour in cold water and drain
2. add enough water to the syrup poured from a can of peaches to make in all 3 cups
3. add the soaked tapioca and sugar and a little salt to this liquid
4. Cook till thoroughly clear
5. line a mould with the peaches, dust with sugar, and fill with the tapioca
6. serve with whipped cream
