## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 hard-boiled eggs
- Butter size of egg
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoonful flour
- 1 cup milk
- Hot buttered toast
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut eggs into slices
2. Put butter into a sauce-pan, and when it begins to bubble add a fine chopped onion
3. Let the onion cook a little without taking color, then stir in flour
4. Add milk, and stir until it becomes smooth
5. Put in the slices of eggs and let them get hot
6. Pour over neatly trimmed slices of hot buttered toast
7. Season to taste with pepper and salt
