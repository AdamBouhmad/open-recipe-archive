## Ingredients

- cold meat
- salt and pepper
- allspice and nutmeg
- bread
- cold milk
- 1 egg to every three persons
- a lump of butter as large as a walnut

## Directions

1. bone and chop it fine
2. season with salt and pepper, also allspice and nutmeg if liked
3. soak about 1/8 as much bread in cold milk
4. press out and mix with the meat
5. allow one egg to every three persons, and a lump of butter as large as a walnut
6. mix thoroughly
7. roll into balls and fry in hot fat
