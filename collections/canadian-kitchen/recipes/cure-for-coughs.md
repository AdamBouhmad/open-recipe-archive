## Ingredients

- three newly-laid eggs
- the juice of six lemons
- one-half pound of rock candy
- one pint of Jamaica brandy

## Directions

1. Pour the juice of six lemons over three newly-laid eggs, unbroken, and allow to stand for forty-eight hours
2. Pick out any bits of eggshell which are not dissolved
3. Add one-half pound of rock candy, and one pint of Jamaica brandy
4. Mix well and bottle
5. Dose: 1 tablespoonful three or four times a day
