## Ingredients

- round of beef weighing about 20 pounds
- 2 pounds clear fat fresh pork
- 2 ounces ground pepper
- 1 ounce ground allspice
- 1 ounce ground ginger
- 1 nutmeg grated
- 1/2 ounce powdered saltpetre
- 1 pound dark brown sugar
- 1 pound fine salt
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take half the quantity of salt and rub the beef thoroughly, and let stand over night
2. Add the other ingredients, which have been well mixed together
3. Rub well, and turn meat every day for a fortnight, basting well at the same time
4. Have the pork lying in the dish with the meat
5. When it is ready to cook, put the pork in the hole where the bone was, strew and tie up well
6. Put in a pot with a close cover, and cover well with cold water
7. When it comes to a boil, boil hard for 1 hour
8. Remove from stove, keeping it well covered
9. Stand on floor and cover with rugs and blankets and let stand for 24 hours
10. Take it out of the water and put it away to get thoroughly cold before using
11. Cut in very thin slices
