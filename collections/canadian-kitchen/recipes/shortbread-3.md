## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. flour
- 1/2 lb. butter
- 1/4 lb. sugar

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar, beating until light.
2. Add the flour.
3. Mould and roll into cakes about an inch thick.
4. Pinch them neatly round the edges, and prick them on the top with a fork.
5. Bake slowly till a light golden brown.
