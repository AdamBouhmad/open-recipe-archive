## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup ground coffee
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 1/2 cups fresh milk

## Directions

1. Make the coffee in the usual way
2. Strain into a coffee-pot or pitcher
3. Add the milk, scalding hot
4. Set for five minutes, closely covered, in boiling water
5. When allowed to cool and then iced this is a favorite beverage at hot-weather luncheons and picnics
