## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup rich milk or cream
- piece butter size of a walnut
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup chopped walnut meats

## Directions

1. Boil 5 minutes
2. Add piece butter size of a walnut, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and chopped walnut meats
3. Beat mixture till thick and creamy
4. Turn in buttered tins and cool
