## Ingredients

- equal parts fine cut celery
- shredded cabbage
- one cup salad dressing
- half tablespoon salt
- half tablespoon mustard
- one cup sugar
- one egg slightly beaten
- two and a half tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of fine cut celery and shredded cabbage together with one cup salad dressing
2. Mix half tablespoon each of salt and mustard and one cup sugar
3. Add one egg slightly beaten and two and a half tablespoons butter
