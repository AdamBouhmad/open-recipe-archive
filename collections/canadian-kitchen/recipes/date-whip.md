## Ingredients

- 1 pound dates
- water
- whites of 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- whipped cream for serving

## Directions

1. Stone dates and take ends off
2. Boil in water, watching not to burn
3. Stir until it gets smooth and let cool
4. Beat whites of 4 eggs well
5. Add 1/2 cup white sugar
6. Stir all into dates
7. Put in oven until light brown
8. Serve with whipped cream
