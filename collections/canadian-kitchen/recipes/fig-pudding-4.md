## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 pound suet
- 1 pound figs cut fine
- 1 pound bread crumbs
- 1 pound brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 nutmeg
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup flour
- sour milk enough to moisten

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Steam 3 hours
