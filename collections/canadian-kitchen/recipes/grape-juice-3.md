## Ingredients

- grapes
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash grapes, pick from stems
2. Put in preserving pan and crush slightly
3. Heat slowly and boil gently for 3/4 an hour
4. Crush fruit with wooden spoon, strain through cheese cloth over sieve or colander
5. Drain well, then draw edges of cheese cloth together and twist hard to press out all juice and put in clean preserving kettle on fire
6. When it boils up, draw back and skim
7. Let it boil up again and skim
8. Add 1 gill of sugar to each quart of juice, stir until dissolved, boil 5 minutes, skimming carefully
9. Fill hot sterilized jars or bottles, put jars in moderate oven for 10 minutes in pans of boiling water
10. Fill jars with more boiling juice and seal
