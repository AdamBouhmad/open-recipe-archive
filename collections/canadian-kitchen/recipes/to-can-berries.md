## Ingredients

- berries (raspberries or blackberries)
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Fill jars with berries
2. Make a thin syrup and pour boiling over berries
3. Seal jars and place in the reservoir of hot water for 20 minutes or less
4. If water is boiling, take out and turn upside down to be sure that they are airtight
