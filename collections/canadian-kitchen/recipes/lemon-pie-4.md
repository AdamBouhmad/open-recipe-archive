## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs
- 2 cups warm water
- 2 dessertspoons cornstarch
- 1 cup sugar
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of 2 eggs
2. Mix with 2 cups warm water, 2 dessertspoons cornstarch, 1 cup sugar, juice and grated rind of 1 lemon, and pinch of salt
3. Beat whites of eggs for top
