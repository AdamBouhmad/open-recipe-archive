## Ingredients

- Three cups of cream
- one cup of milk
- one box of gelatine
- sugar to taste
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Whip cream and milk stiff.
2. Put the sugar with the gelatine when it is hot.
3. Flavor with vanilla.
4. Stir till it thickens, then mould.
