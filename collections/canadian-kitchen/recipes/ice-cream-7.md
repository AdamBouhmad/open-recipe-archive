## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 cups milk
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 4 cups sweet cream
- vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Make a custard of the yolks of 3 eggs and 3 cups milk, add 1/2 cup white sugar
2. Let the custard get cold
3. Beat the 3 whites of eggs stiff and beat them into 4 cups sweet cream
4. Add the custard, sweeten to taste, flavor with vanilla and freeze
