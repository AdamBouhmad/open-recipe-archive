## Ingredients

- parsnips
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil parsnips until tender
2. Drain and mash
3. Add a generous lump of butter, pepper and salt to taste
4. Butter a baking dish
5. Cover the bottom with a layer of bread crumbs
6. Put in parsnips
7. Cover with a layer of crumbs
8. Brown in oven
