## Ingredients

- 2 cups sour milk
- 4 cups flour
- 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 yolks or 2 whites beaten

## Directions

1. Set to rise 2 cups sour milk, 4 cups flour, 2 White Swan Yeast Cakes
2. Add 4 tablespoons butter, 4 tablespoons sugar, 3/4 teaspoon salt, creamed together
3. Add 2 yolks or 2 whites beaten
4. Let rise
5. Add flour to foam
