## Ingredients

- yeast
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Allow 2 cups of yeast for every loaf of bread.
2. Warm the yeast and flour, add a little salt, mix stiff and let rise.
3. Knead and let rise again.
4. Put in pans and rise again and bake.
5. Keep at an even temperature while rising.
