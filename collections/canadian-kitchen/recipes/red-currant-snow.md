## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiling water
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 cup sugar
- juice of 2 cups red currants
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook boiling water, cornstarch, and sugar until thick
2. Remove from fire
3. Add juice of red currants crushed and pressed through colander
4. Beat the egg whites
5. Add a little sugar to egg whites
6. Pour over all
