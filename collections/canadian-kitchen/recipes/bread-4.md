## Ingredients

- 1 pint of yeast per loaf
- flour
- salt
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a pint of the yeast for every loaf required
2. Set on the stove and stir with the hand until warm (not hot)
3. Warm a quantity of flour, add a little salt, sugar and butter (about a tablespoon of each for three loaves)
4. Make into a dough, let rise about 1 hour
5. Then knead, when light again mould into loaves, let rise
6. Bake in a hot oven about 1 hour or maybe more, according to size
7. Sugar and butter may be omitted
8. Do not put salt in upon the yeast, as it kills yeast germs
