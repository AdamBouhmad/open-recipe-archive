## Ingredients

- 6 good tart apples
- 1 onion
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- butter size of an egg

## Directions

1. Chop 6 good tart apples fine
2. Chop 1 onion fine
3. Put together
4. Boil vinegar, mustard, pepper, salt, cornstarch, sugar, and butter size of an egg on the stove till thick
5. Put by till cool
6. Put sauce on apples when cold
