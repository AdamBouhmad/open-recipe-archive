## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- lemon or vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs one minute.
2. Add one and one-half cups white sugar and beat five minutes.
3. Add one cup flour and beat one minute more.
4. Add one-half cup water, one cup flour, two teaspoons baking powder; flavor to taste with lemon or vanilla.
5. Bake in a moderate oven twenty-five or thirty minutes.
