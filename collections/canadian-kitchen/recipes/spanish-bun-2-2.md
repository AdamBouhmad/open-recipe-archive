## Ingredients

- 3 eggs yolks and 1 whole egg
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 heaping dessertspoon mixed spices
- 2 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- Whites of 3 eggs for icing
- 1/2 cup white sugar for icing

## Directions

1. Beat sugar, butter, spices, and eggs together
2. Add milk and flour
3. Put in oven
4. For icing: beat whites of 3 eggs and mix with 1/2 cup white sugar
5. Put icing on cake and let brown a little
