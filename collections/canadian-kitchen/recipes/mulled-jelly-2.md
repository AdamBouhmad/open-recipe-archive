## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful currant or grape jelly
- white of 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 1 teacupful boiling water
- 1 slice dry toast or 2 crackers

## Directions

1. Take one tablespoonful of currant or grape jelly
2. Beat with it the white of one egg and a teaspoonful of sugar
3. Pour on it a teacupful of boiling water
4. Break in a slice of dry toast or two crackers
