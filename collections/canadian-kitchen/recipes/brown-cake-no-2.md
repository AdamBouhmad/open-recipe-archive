## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, save white of 1 for frosting
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon mixed pastry spice
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Bake in layers in moderate oven
