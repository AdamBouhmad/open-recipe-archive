## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- Butter the size of an egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup flour
- Strawberry or raspberry jam
- Whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a stiff batter as for cake, using 1 teaspoon baking powder to 1 cup flour
2. Bake in jelly pan
3. Slip it off and spread with strawberry or raspberry jam
4. Over this spread the whites of 2 eggs beaten to a stiff
