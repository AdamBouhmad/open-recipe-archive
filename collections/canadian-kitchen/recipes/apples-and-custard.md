## Ingredients

- 6 large apples
- sugar
- butter
- water
- 1 pint milk
- yolks 2 eggs
- 1 level teaspoon cornstarch
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Pare and core 6 large apples, filling the cavities with sugar and a bit of butter
2. Put a little water in a dish, place in the apples and bake
3. When nearly done, pour a custard around made of 1 pint milk, yolks 2 eggs, 1 level teaspoon cornstarch, sugar and flavor to taste
4. Put back in the oven and bake
5. Make a meringue of the 2 whites, and put a tablespoon over each
6. Brown lightly and eat cold
