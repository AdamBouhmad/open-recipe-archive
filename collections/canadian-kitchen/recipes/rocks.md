## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ⅜ cup butter
- 2 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 pound each chopped English walnuts and raisins
- 1 teaspoon each cinnamon, cloves and soda

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in a little hot water
2. Drop by spoonfuls on buttered tins
3. Bake
