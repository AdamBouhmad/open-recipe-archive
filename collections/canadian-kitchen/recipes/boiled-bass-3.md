## Ingredients

- Bass
- Water
- Half a cupful of vinegar
- Teaspoonful of salt
- An onion
- A dozen black peppers
- A blade of mace
- Clean mosquito-netting
- Drawn-butter

## Directions

1. Put enough water in the pot for the fish to swim in easily
2. Add half a cupful of vinegar, a teaspoonful of salt, an onion, a dozen black peppers, and a blade of mace
3. Sew up the fish in a piece of clean mosquito-netting, fitted to its shape
4. Heat slowly for the first half hour, then boil twelve minutes to the pound, quite fast
5. Unwrap, and pour over it a cup of drawn-butter, based upon the liquor in which the fish was boiled
