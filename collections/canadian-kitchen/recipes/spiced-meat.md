## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of good steak or veal
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup cracker crumbs
- butter size of an egg
- 1/2 nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- 3 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Chop meat
2. Add eggs, cracker crumbs, butter, nutmeg, pepper and salt to taste
3. Add 3 tablespoons cream
4. Stir together
5. Bake in a loaf for 1 hour
