## Ingredients

- codfish
- eggs
- butter
- butter and lard mixed

## Directions

1. Properly soak and pick the codfish in bits
2. To each cup add 2 eggs beaten well together
3. Drop from a spoon in hot butter or butter and lard mixed
4. Fry to a nice brown on both sides
