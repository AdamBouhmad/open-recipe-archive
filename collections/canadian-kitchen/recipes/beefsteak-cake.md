## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup baking syrup
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 1 good teaspoon soda
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 cup butter and 3/4 cup brown sugar
2. Add 2 eggs, 1/4 cup baking syrup, 1/2 cup currants, 1 teaspoon cloves, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/2 cup buttermilk, 1 good teaspoon soda, a little salt
3. Add flour enough to make not too stiff a batter
4. Bake
