## Ingredients

- 1 pt. milk
- 1 small onion
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- A little mace
- 1 cup of whipped cream
- Long stalks of 3 heads of celery
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil onion, celery and mace from thirty to forty minutes
2. Mix flour with two tablespoonfuls of cold milk and add to boiling milk; cook ten minutes
3. Mash the celery in the water it was boiled in and stir in boiling milk
4. Add butter and season with salt and pepper to taste
5. Strain, and serve immediately
6. Whip a cup of cream and add to soup after it is in the tureen
