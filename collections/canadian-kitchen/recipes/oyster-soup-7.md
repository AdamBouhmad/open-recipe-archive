## Ingredients

- One knuckle veal
- One small onion
- Two blades mace
- Two or three celery leaves
- Two small red peppers
- One half pint cream
- One half pint milk
- One quart oysters

## Directions

1. Boil veal with onion, mace, peppers, celery leaves and salt to taste, in four quarts of water, adding more water till the meat is boiled to shreds
2. Strain and set the liquor to cool
3. When cold skim off every particle of fat, and leave behind any grounds that may be at the bottom of the jelly, which should be firm
4. Put the jelly over the fire; when boiling add cream and milk
5. Thicken with flour previously blended to the consistency of cream, or oyster crackers powdered
6. Stir till the soup is thickened, then add the oysters, stirring constantly for three or four minutes
