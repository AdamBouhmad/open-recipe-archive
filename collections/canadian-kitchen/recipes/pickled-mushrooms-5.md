## Ingredients

- small button mushrooms
- vinegar
- salt
- mace
- cloves
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Take the small buttons and rub them very clean with a flannel and some vinegar
2. Put them in a dish with a little salt over them to draw the liquor from them
3. When this is done put them into a kettle with their own liquor and sufficient good vinegar to cover them, also some mace, cloves and black pepper to suit taste
4. Boil gently for a few minutes
5. Bottle and cork tightly
