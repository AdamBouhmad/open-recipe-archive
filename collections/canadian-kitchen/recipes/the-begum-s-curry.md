## Ingredients

- meat or chicken
- 1 tablespoonful curry powder
- 1 tablespoonful vinegar
- 0.5 tablespoonful Harvey sauce
- salt
- cocoanut juice
- ground rice
- butter
- onions
- 0.5 cup gravy

## Directions

1. Cut some meat into small dice, and put butter in frying-pan.
2. Fry the meat a nice brown.
3. While cooking, add all ingredients, which must be previously all mixed together: one tablespoon curry powder, one of vinegar, one-half of Harvey sauce, a little salt, the juice of a cocoanut, ground rice and butter.
4. Add half a cup of gravy.
5. Stir all together.
6. Let it cook a little while, and then turn the curry into a brown earthenware jar.
7. Fry some onions in butter, and add to the curry.
8. Let the jar stand on the hob and simmer until required.
