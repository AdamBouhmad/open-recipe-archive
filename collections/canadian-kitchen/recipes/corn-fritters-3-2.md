## Ingredients

- 1 can corn
- 3 eggs well beaten
- a little salt
- flour enough to make a rather stiff batter

## Directions

1. Mix corn, eggs, salt, and flour to make a rather stiff batter
2. Cook in deep hot lard same as doughnuts
