## Ingredients

- one cupful of cranberries
- a half cupful of seeded raisins
- a cupful of sugar
- a half cupful of water
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a teaspoonful of vanilla
- upper and under crust

## Directions

1. Chop one cupful of cranberries and a half cupful of seeded raisins together into small pieces.
2. Add to them a cupful of sugar, a half cupful of water, a tablespoonful of flour, and a teaspoonful of vanilla.
3. Bake with an upper and under crust.
