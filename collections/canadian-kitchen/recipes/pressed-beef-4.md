## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold roast beef
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- salt and pepper to taste
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put through food chopper 1 cup cold roast beef and 1 hard-boiled egg
2. Add 1 teaspoon mustard and season with salt and pepper to taste
3. Moisten the whole with vinegar and press
