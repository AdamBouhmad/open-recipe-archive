## Ingredients

- 4 pounds round beef (uncooked) chopped fine
- 6 eggs beaten together
- 6 soda crackers rolled fine
- little butter and suet
- pepper
- salt
- sage

## Directions

1. Mix chopped beef, eggs, crackers, butter, suet, pepper, salt, and sage
2. Make 2 loaves
3. Roll in crackers
4. Bake about an hour
5. Slice when cold
