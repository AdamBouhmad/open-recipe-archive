## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- 1 teaspoonful baking powder
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately.
2. Add to beaten yolks the flour, baking powder, milk, salt.
3. Put the whites in last.
4. Have frying-pan hot, and put in butter.
5. When melted cook batter as quickly as possible after the whites are added.
6. Do not turn, but place in hot oven to cook top.
