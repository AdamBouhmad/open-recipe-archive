## Ingredients

- 1 can chopped corn
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ¾ tablespoon melted butter
- 1 pint scalded milk

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. turn into buttered baking dish
3. Bake in slow oven until firm
