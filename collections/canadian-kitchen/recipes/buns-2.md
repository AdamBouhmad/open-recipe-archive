## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread dough
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- a few raisins and currants if desired

## Directions

1. Mix into a stiff dough
2. Set into a cool place to rise until bed-time
3. Make into buns and place in a cool place until morning
4. Bake in oven when ready
