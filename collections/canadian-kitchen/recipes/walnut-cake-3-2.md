## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups sifted flour
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon powdered mace
- a coffee cup walnuts chopped small
- raisins

## Directions

1. Cream sugar with butter
2. Add eggs
3. Mix baking powder with flour and sift
4. Add flour mixture and milk alternately
5. Add powdered mace
6. Add walnuts
7. Fill cake pans with layer of cake
8. Add layer of raisins
9. Add layer of nuts
10. Repeat until pan is two-thirds full
11. Line tins with well-buttered paper
12. Bake in steady, but not quick, oven
