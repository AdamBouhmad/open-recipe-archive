## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs. veal
- Pepper and salt
- 1 lemon
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 slice of ham

## Directions

1. Stew the meat, with the thin rind of the lemon, in a very little water till quite tender
2. When done, cut up both veal and ham into small pieces, mince the lemon rind finely, and set these aside to cool
3. Strain the stock, add the lemon-juice and seasonings, and let this also cool
4. Cut the eggs into slices, and arrange them in a plain mould or dish, pour in the cool stock and meat, and set aside till quite cold, when it should turn out whole
