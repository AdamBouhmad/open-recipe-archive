## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. raw or rare mutton
- 1 sour apple
- sufficient water to stew
- curry
- pepper
- salt
- 1 gill of cream
- 1 cupful of Patna rice
- 2 quarts of boiling water
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil one sour apple with meat in sufficient water to stew
2. Add curry and pepper and salt to taste
3. When serving add a gill of cream
4. Boil a cupful of Patna rice in two quarts of boiling water twenty minutes
5. Strain through colander and shake well
6. Make a wall of the rice around the serving dish
7. Pour in the mixture and garnish with parsley
8. Serve very hot
