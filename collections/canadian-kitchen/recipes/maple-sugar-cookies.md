## Ingredients

- 2 large cups maple sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 2 tablespoons sweet milk
- 1 teaspoon cream tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- flour enough to roll soft

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together and roll soft and bake.
