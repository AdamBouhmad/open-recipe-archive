## Ingredients

- 6 fine tomatoes, pared and sliced thick
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 2 raw eggs, beaten light
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 1/4 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- pinch of cayenne

## Directions

1. Rub butter, pounded yolks, pepper, salt, mustard, and sugar together.
2. Beat hard, add vinegar, and heat to a boil.
3. Put this gradually upon the beaten eggs and whip to a smooth cream.
4. Set in hot water while you broil the tomatoes in an oyster-broiler over clear coals.
5. Lay this upon a hot-water dish and pour the scalding dressing upon them.
