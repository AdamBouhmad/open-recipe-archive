## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons molasses
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 saltspoon soda
- 1 small teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, molasses, water, and butter together without stirring till it hardens in water
2. Add vanilla and pour at once on a buttered platter
3. When hard break into squares
