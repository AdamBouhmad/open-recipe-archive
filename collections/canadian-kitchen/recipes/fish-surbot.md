## Ingredients

- 1 whitefish (medium size)
- 1 pint milk
- 1/2 onion
- flour
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 pound butter
- cracker crumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Steam whitefish 20 minutes
2. Pick fine
3. Boil milk with 1/2 onion in it
4. Thicken with flour
5. When cold, add 2 eggs and 3/4 pound butter
6. Put in a dish a layer of fish and then a layer of dressing, till all is used
7. Cover with cracker crumbs
8. Add bits of butter and juice of a lemon
