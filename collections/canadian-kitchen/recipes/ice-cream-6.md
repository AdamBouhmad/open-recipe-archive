## Ingredients

- 1 qt. milk
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch
- vanilla flavoring to taste

## Directions

1. Put milk on to boil.
2. Dissolve cornstarch in two tablespoons of cold milk.
3. Stir into boiling milk.
4. Cook five minutes, strain, add sugar, flavor to taste.
5. When perfectly cold, freeze.
