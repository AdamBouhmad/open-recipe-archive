## Ingredients

- 4 pounds rhubarb
- 5 pounds sugar
- 1 pound figs
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Cut the rhubarb into small pieces
2. Peel the lemons very finely and cut into small pieces, also the figs
3. Put all together into an earthen vessel
4. Put half the sugar on and let it remain all night
5. Next day add the other sugar and boil it well for 3 hours
