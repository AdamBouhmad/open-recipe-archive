## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon vinegar
- 3/8 ounce mustard seed
- 3 cups sugar
- 3/8 ounce celery seed
- 3/4 cup flour
- 1/8 pound mustard
- 1 quart cucumbers (whole)
- 1 quart onions
- 1 cauliflower broken up
- 1 bunch celery broken up
- 1 quart cucumbers chopped
- 1 quart onions chopped
- 1 cauliflower chopped
- 1 bunch celery chopped
- salt water

## Directions

1. Make paste of 3/4 cup flour and 1/8 pound mustard
2. Mix with cucumbers, onions, cauliflower, and celery
3. Scald in salt water
4. Let cook up good before sealing
