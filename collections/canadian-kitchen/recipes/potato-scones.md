## Ingredients

- Half a dozen large boiled potatoes
- A little flour
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mash potatoes carefully and salt
2. Knead potato with a little flour
3. Form into scones an inch thick
4. Bake in moderate oven and prick to prevent blistering
5. Split and butter plentifully, and serve at once, piping hot
