## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes
- 4 pounds sugar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 2 tablespoons allspice
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 3/4 cups vinegar
- 2 pounds raisins
- 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Cut green tomatoes fine
2. Sprinkle tomatoes with salt, let stand over night, then drain
3. Cook all together
4. Add 2 pounds of raisins and 3 lemons
5. Use for pie or as a sauce with cold meats
