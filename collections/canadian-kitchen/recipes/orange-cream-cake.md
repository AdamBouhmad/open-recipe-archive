## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1/4 cup sweet milk
- 1/4 teaspoon cream tartar
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon orange extract

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
