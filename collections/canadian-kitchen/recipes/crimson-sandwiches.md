## Ingredients

- dark-colored, cold boiled beet-root
- tabasco sauce
- very white buttered slices

## Directions

1. Take dark-colored, cold boiled beet-root and pass through a fine sieve
2. Flavor with a little tabasco sauce
3. Spread on very white buttered slices, so that the color shows well at edges
