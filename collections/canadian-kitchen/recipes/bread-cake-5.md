## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- 4 cups flour
- 2 yeast cakes
- 6 to 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs beaten

## Directions

1. Set to rise 2 cups scalded milk, 4 cups flour, 2 yeast cakes
2. When light, add 6 to 8 tablespoons butter, 8 tablespoons sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, creamed together
3. add 2 eggs beaten and flour to form
4. let rise again
