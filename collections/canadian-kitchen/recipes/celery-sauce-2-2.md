## Ingredients

- 4 pounds tomatoes
- 2 1/2 pounds celery
- 9 teaspoons salt
- 6 teaspoons ground pepper
- 2 1/4 teaspoons cinnamon
- 6 teaspoons mustard
- 3/8 pound white onions
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 quart vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop celery and onions
2. Peel tomatoes
3. Cook until tender
4. Strain and add all the other ingredients
5. Cook until celery is tender
