## Ingredients

- A small lobster or 1 can passed through a sieve
- 1/2 pt. of cream
- 1 gill tomato juice
- 1 gill aspic jelly
- 1 tablespoonful of mayonnaise
- 1/2 oz. or little more of gelatine
- Small pieces of lobster
- Small leaves of parsley

## Directions

1. Put a little aspic jelly in the bottom of a border mould, decorate with small pieces of lobster and small leaves of parsley
2. Beat gelatine in tomato juice, whip the cream; also whip the aspic
3. Mix these together
4. Stir in the mayonnaise and the melted gelatine and tomato, also your lobster
5. Fill the mould carefully and set
6. When cold turn out and fill the centre with small salad
