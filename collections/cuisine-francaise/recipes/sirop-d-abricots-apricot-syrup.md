## Ingredients

- one pound of very ripe apricots
- two pounds of sugar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the apricots after having peeled the almond
2. Cut them into small pieces
3. Put two pounds of sugar in a pan with one glass of water and cook as the previous cherry syrup
4. Add the apricots with the pits
5. Cook together over medium heat until taking syrup with one finger and touching it to another, it forms a thread when opened that holds somewhat without breaking
6. Pass them through a sieve
