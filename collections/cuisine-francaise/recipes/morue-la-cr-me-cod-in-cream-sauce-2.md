## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- butter
- flour
- garlic
- coarse pepper
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Cook the cod in water
2. Drain and remove the flesh by flakes
3. In a casserole, put a good piece of butter and half a spoonful of flour
4. Add a touch of chopped garlic and coarse pepper
5. Moisten with cream or milk
6. Thicken the sauce on the fire
7. Add the cod fillets
8. Heat and serve
9. If you wish to coat it, add a little more butter and three egg yolks, arrange in the serving dish, coat the top, and brown it
