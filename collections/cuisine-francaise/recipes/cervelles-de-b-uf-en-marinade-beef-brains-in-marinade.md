## Ingredients

- beef brains
- marinade
- frying batter
- fried parsley
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains by cleaning and blanching them
2. Cook them in a marinade
3. Make a frying batter
4. At serving time, fry them
5. Ensure your frying oil is not too hot
6. Put fried parsley on top
7. If the brains are fully cooked, cut them into pieces
8. Season with salt, pepper, and enough vinegar so they are submerged in it
9. Drain them
10. Put them in your batter and fry them
