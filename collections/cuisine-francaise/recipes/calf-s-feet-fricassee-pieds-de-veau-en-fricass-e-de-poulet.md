## Ingredients

- Calf's feet
- butter
- mushrooms
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- shallots
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- cloves
- flour
- white wine
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked calf's feet into pieces and place them in a casserole with a good piece of butter, mushrooms, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, two shallots, a bay leaf, thyme, basil, and two cloves
2. Pass them over the fire and add a pinch of flour, moisten with a glass of white wine and as much bouillon
3. Season with salt and coarse pepper and bring to boil for half an hour over low heat
4. When the sauce is reduced by half, remove the bouquet and add three egg yolks mixed with a spoonful of vinegar and as much bouillon
5. Make the sauce bind without boiling and serve
