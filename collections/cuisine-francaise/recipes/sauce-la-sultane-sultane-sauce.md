## Ingredients

- one chopine of bouillon
- one glass of white wine
- two slices of lemon with skin removed
- two cloves
- one clove of garlic
- half a bay leaf
- parsley
- chives
- one onion and one carrot root
- salt
- coarse pepper
- one hard egg yolk
- a pinch of blanched and finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in a saucepan
2. Boil for one and a half hours over low heat and reduce to the consistency of a sauce
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Add a little salt, coarse pepper, one hard egg yolk chopped, and a pinch of finely chopped blanched parsley
