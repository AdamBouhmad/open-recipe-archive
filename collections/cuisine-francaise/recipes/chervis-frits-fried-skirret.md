## Ingredients

- Skirret
- Water
- Salt
- Flour
- White wine
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook the skirret with water and salt (quarter hour cooking time is sufficient)
2. Drain and let cool
3. Make a batter with flour, white wine, a spoonful of oil and salt, not too thin (it should fall in streams when held in the air on a spoon)
4. Dip the skirret in this batter
5. Fry the skirret after dipping in the batter
6. Serve as an entremetс
