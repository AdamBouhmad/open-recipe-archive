## Ingredients

- thirty carrots
- thirty turnips
- thirty small onions
- twenty leeks
- ten celery heads
- six whole lettuces
- broth

## Directions

1. Take vegetables all of the same size, an inch high
2. Blanch them
3. Cook them in broth
4. Cook the lettuces separately
5. Pour everything into a tureen without adding bread
