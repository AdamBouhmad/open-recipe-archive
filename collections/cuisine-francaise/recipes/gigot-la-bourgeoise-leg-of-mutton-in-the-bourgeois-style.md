## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- twelve carrots
- twelve onions
- two bay leaves
- two cloves
- twelve potatoes
- parsley and chives bouquet
- one spoonful of broth or water
- salt
- one pound of small lard cut into six pieces

## Directions

1. Prepare the leg of mutton as for the leg in water
2. Place it in a braising pot
3. Add twelve carrots, twelve onions, two bay leaves, two cloves, twelve potatoes, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and one spoonful of broth or water
4. Add salt and one pound of small lard cut into six pieces, removing the top and bottom from each piece
5. Braise gently for three and a half hours, turning it from time to time
6. Ensure the fire continues steadily
7. When ready to serve, untie the leg, arrange it on the platter with the vegetables around it
8. If the cooking liquid is too thin, reduce it and place it under the leg
9. Ensure the leg has a good color, or glaze it
