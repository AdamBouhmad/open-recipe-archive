## Ingredients

- Sheep tails
- Purée of lentils, peas, green beans, or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tails as described for braised tails
2. At the time of serving, drain them and arrange them on the plate
3. Cover them with a purée of lentils, green peas, beans, or a tomato sauce
4. If you have neither meat nor lard to cook your tails, cook them with the seasoning explained for braised tails
5. Season them well with salt
6. These tails may be served in a terrine; instead of seven, you will need twelve
