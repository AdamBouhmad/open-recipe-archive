## Ingredients

- salmon trout or common trout
- red wine
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the trout in a court-bouillon with red wine.
2. Serve on a napkin garnished with green parsley.
3. If serving as an entrée, serve with a sauce on top as for other fish.
4. Alternatively, soak in oil and cook on a grill as previously explained for other fish, and serve with a lean ragout.
