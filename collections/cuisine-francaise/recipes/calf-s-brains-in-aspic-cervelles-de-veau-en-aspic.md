## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Aspic

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your brains as you would for the panfried version
2. Pour lukewarm aspic into the bottom of a mold to a depth of seven or eight lines
3. When your aspic is cold and set, cut your brains into four pieces and arrange them so that they form a beautiful pattern and are positioned on your jelly to create a design
4. Pour nearly cold aspic over them so that your brains are well enclosed in the jelly
5. Place your mold on ice to allow the aspic to congeal
6. When it is well set, at the moment of serving, heat a cloth well and rub the mold with it until you see your aspic begin to release, or dip the mold in lukewarm water without leaving it in too long for fear the jelly will melt too much
7. Place your platter over your mold and invert it
8. If your aspic does not release, place a warm cloth on your mold and rub it around with this same warm cloth
9. Lift your mold very gently, taking care not to damage your aspic
10. If there is melted jelly on the platter, use a straw to suck it up
11. Place your platter briefly on ice
