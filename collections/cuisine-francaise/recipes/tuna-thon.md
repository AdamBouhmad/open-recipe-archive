## Ingredients

- Marinated tuna from Provence
- Good butter
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboules), chopped
- Breadcrumbs (mie de pain)

## Directions

1. Arrange the tuna on the serving dish
2. Serve with good butter, parsley, and chopped chives
3. Coat with breadcrumbs
4. Brown in the oven or under a pastry dome cover
