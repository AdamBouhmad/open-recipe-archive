## Ingredients

- veal tendons
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- coarse pepper
- mushrooms
- bouquet garni
- small onions

## Directions

1. Cut your tendons either into oyster pieces or another way; blanch them and put them in cold water
2. When they are cold, trim them
3. Mix a quarteron of butter in a pan; sauté your tendons in it
4. When they are well browned in your butter, pour over them a spoonful and a half of flour; sauté everything in your pan so the flour mixes with your butter
5. Moisten your tendons abundantly with bouillon; add some coarse pepper, mushrooms, and a bouquet garni
6. When your tendons have boiled for two hours, add small onions that are well peeled and all the same size
7. When your onions are cooked, if the sauce is too thin, sauté your tendons, your small onions, your mushrooms, put them in another pan, and reduce your sauce until there is enough left to sauce your dish
