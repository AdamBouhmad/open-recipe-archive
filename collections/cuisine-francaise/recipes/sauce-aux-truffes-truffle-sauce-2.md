## Ingredients

- Two or three truffles
- Oil or butter
- Four or five spoonfuls of velouté
- One spoonful of consommé
- Or alternatively: a little flour
- Five or six spoonfuls of bouillon
- A little coarse pepper
- A little quatre épices (four spices)

## Directions

1. Chop the truffles very finely
2. Brown them lightly in oil or butter
3. Add four or five spoonfuls of velouté and one spoonful of consommé
4. Boil your sauce for a quarter hour over low heat
5. Degrease it, then place it in a small saucepan and keep it in a bain-marie
6. If you do not have velouté, use a little flour with your truffles
7. Add five or six spoonfuls of bouillon, a little coarse pepper, and a little quatre épices
8. Simmer your sauce and serve it for the indicated dishes
