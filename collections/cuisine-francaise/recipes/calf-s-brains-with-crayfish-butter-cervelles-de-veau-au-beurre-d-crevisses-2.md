## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Four spoonfuls of reduced velouté
- Crayfish butter, about the size of a small egg
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your brains as you would for the panfried version
2. At the moment of serving, drain them and arrange them on the platter
3. Have ready four spoonfuls of reduced velouté in which you will place crayfish butter about the size of a small egg
4. Stir your sauce well so that the butter melts and blends with your sauce, without allowing it to boil
5. When the butter is incorporated, add a little coarse pepper and pour your sauce over your brains
