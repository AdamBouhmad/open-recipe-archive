## Ingredients

- Three half-setiers of milk
- A bit of green lemon peel
- A pinch of coriander
- A small piece of cinnamon
- A bit more than half a quarter pound of sugar
- Two grains of salt
- A pinch of flour
- Six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put three half-setiers of milk in a saucepan and bring it to boil.
2. Add a bit of green lemon peel, a pinch of coriander, a small piece of cinnamon, a bit more than half a quarter pound of sugar, and two grains of salt.
3. Make it boil and reduce to half.
4. Let it cool a little.
5. In another saucepan, have a pinch of flour mixed with six egg yolks.
6. Pour your cream into it little by little, stirring as you go.
7. Pass it through a tamis and pour it into the serving dish.
8. Set it in a bain-marie, and before serving, pass a hot poker over it to color it.
