## Ingredients

- cooked larks
- toasted bread slices
- rocambole
- verjuice
- fried croutons for garnish

## Directions

1. Use larks that have been served from the table and were cooked on the spit
2. Remove the heads and what they have in the body
3. Discard the gizzards and use the rest with the toasted bread slices
4. Pound everything in a mortar
5. Dilute what you have pounded with a little crushed rocambole and a dash of verjuice
6. Heat the larks in this sauce without letting it boil
7. Serve garnished with fried croutons
