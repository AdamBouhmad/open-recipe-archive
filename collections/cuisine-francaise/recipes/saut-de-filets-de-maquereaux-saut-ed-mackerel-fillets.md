## Ingredients

- Mackerel fillets
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Parsley
- Chives, finely chopped
- Butter
- Velouté
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Ravigote, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Lift the fillets from your mackerel in their entirety
2. Slide your knife between the skin and flesh to remove the skin
3. Trim your fillets and place them in a sauté pan with salt, coarse pepper, parsley, and finely chopped chives
4. Warm a good piece of butter and pour it over them
5. At the moment of service, place them on the fire and stir them to prevent sticking
6. When they are slightly heated on one side by a few bubbles of the butter, carefully turn them to avoid breaking them
7. Ensure they are cooked and arrange on the plate
8. In a saucepan, place a good piece of butter, a spoonful of velouté, three egg yolks, the juice of two lemons, salt, coarse pepper, and finely chopped ravigote
9. Turn your sauce constantly until it is bound; do not let it boil as it would curdle
10. Check the seasoning and pour it over your fillets
