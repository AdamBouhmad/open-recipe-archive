## Ingredients

- Sheep cutlets
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Bread crumbs
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Trim your cutlets as described for sautéed cutlets
2. Make a piece of butter lukewarm
3. Put them in it after seasoning them with salt and coarse pepper
4. When they are covered with butter, put them in bread crumbs, ensuring they take it exactly
5. Put them on the table and give them an agreeable shape
6. Place them on a saucepan cover, sprinkling bread crumbs over and under them
7. A quarter hour before serving, put them on the grill over a moderately hot fire, taking care that your cutlets do not cook too much and your bread crumbs do not burn
8. Arrange them in a crown and put a clear jus underneath
