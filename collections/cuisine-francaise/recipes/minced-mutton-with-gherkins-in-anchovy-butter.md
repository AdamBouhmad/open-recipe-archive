## Ingredients

- Minced mutton
- Spanish sauce (Espagnole)
- Anchovy butter
- Gherkins
- Flour
- Fond or bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Mince the meat as in previous minced preparations
2. Reduce six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce until two-thirds remains
3. Add a piece of anchovy butter about half the size of an egg
4. Stir the sauce so the butter melts
5. Pour it over the minced mutton, keeping it warm without boiling
6. Add eight or ten gherkins cut into quarters and mix with the minced mutton
7. At the moment of serving, arrange on the plate with croutons around it
8. If you do not have sauce, make a light roux and moisten it with cooking stock or bouillon
9. When the sauce is well reduced, add salt and coarse pepper
10. When ready, add the anchovy butter and gherkins and pour the sauce over the minced mutton
11. Keep warm without boiling
12. At the moment of serving, toss well in the pan so the butter does not turn to oil, then arrange on the plate with croutons around it
