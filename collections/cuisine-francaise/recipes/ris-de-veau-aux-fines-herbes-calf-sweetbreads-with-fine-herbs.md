## Ingredients

- Fennel
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- shallots
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- Three or four calf sweetbreads

## Directions

1. Chop very finely a little fennel, parsley, chives, a small amount of garlic, and two shallots
2. Mix all these fine herbs with a piece of good butter the size of half an egg, fine salt, and coarse pepper
3. Blanch three or four calf sweetbreads and prick them in several places on top to allow the butter and fine herbs to penetrate
