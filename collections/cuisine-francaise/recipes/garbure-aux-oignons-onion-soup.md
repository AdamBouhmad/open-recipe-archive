## Ingredients

- forty large onions
- half pound of butter or more
- bread cut into very thin slices
- coarse pepper
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the onions in half from the top to the other end
2. Cut half of them into four or five parts until they form half a circle
3. Be careful not to include the head or the tail
4. When all your onions are cut this way, take half a pound of butter or more according to the quantity of your onions
5. Fry them in the butter enough so they are well browned
6. Take bread cut into very thin slices
7. Make a layer of bread and a layer of onions
8. Put on each layer a little coarse pepper until your dish is full
9. Drizzle with broth and simmer enough so the gratin forms without letting it burn, which would give it acidity
10. Your soup must be almost dry
11. Put broth in a vessel and serve it so people can add it to their plate
12. Pay attention to salt because of the reduction
