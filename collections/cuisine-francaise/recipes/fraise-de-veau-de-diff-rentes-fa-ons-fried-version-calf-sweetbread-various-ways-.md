## Ingredients

- one calf sweetbread
- water
- white flour sauce
- flour
- oil
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook and prepare the calf sweetbread as previously explained in white flour sauce
2. Degrease it and cut it into small bouquets
3. Dip it in a batter and fry it
4. Serve garnished with fried parsley
5. Make the batter by putting two handfuls of flour in a saucepan with one spoonful of oil and fine salt; thin your batter until it flows from the spoon without being too thin
