## Ingredients

- veal slices
- wings
- lard strips
- bouquet garni
- salt, coarse pepper
- one glass of Champagne wine
- one half-glass of good bouillon
- two spoonfuls of coulis

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with veal slices
2. Place wings on top and cover with lard strips
3. Add bouquet garni, salt, coarse pepper, Champagne wine, and bouillon
4. Cook gently until done
5. Add coulis to the sauce, degrease it, and pass through a sieve
6. Serve over the wings, wiped of excess fat
