## Ingredients

- Chestnuts or large chestnuts
- Four or five sausages
- Goose liver
- Lard fat or butter
- One shallot
- A hint of garlic
- Parsley
- Chives
- One young, tender goose

## Directions

1. Peel chestnuts, removing both the first and second skins by roasting in a perforated pan and stirring until the second skin can be removed
2. Reserve some chestnuts for a ragout
3. Chop the remaining chestnuts and place in a casserole with the meat of four or five sausages, the chopped goose liver, two spoonfuls of lard fat or a good piece of butter, one shallot, a hint of garlic, and chopped parsley and chives
4. Pass everything together over the fire for a quarter hour
5. Let cool
6. Empty, singe, and pluck a young tender goose
7. Fill the goose with this stuffing
8. Sew it closed so nothing escapes
9. Roast on a spit
10. Serve with a ragout of chestnuts as found in the ragout section
