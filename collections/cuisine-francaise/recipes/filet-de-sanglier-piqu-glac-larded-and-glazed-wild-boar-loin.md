## Ingredients

- Wild boar loin
- Lard
- Wild boar slices
- Thyme
- Sage
- Bay leaves
- Carrots
- Onions
- Buttered paper
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Peppercorns
- Roux

## Directions

1. Trim your wild boar loin as you would trim a beef sirloin
2. Marinate for forty-eight hours or longer if desired
3. Shape the loin as desired
4. Place lard in a casserole with a few slices of wild boar, thyme, a little sage, four bay leaves, carrots, and onions
5. Place your loin on this seasoning
6. Cover it with buttered paper
7. Add one glass of white wine, one glass of bouillon, and a little salt
8. Cook over heat above and below for two hours
9. At the moment of serving, drain and glaze it
10. Dress it on the platter
11. Serve a piquant sauce underneath; if you have no sauce, make a light roux, add the cooking liquid from your loin, reduce your sauce by half, check that it is properly salted, and place it under your loin
