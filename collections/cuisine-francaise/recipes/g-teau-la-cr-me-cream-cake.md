## Ingredients

- one litron of flour
- half a setier of double cream
- a good pinch of salt
- good half a pound of butter
- egg for glazing

## Directions

1. Put one litron of flour on a table and make a hole in the middle
2. Put in half a setier of double cream and a good pinch of salt
3. Knead the dough lightly and let it rest for half an hour
4. Then work a good half pound of butter into the dough
5. Beat it five times like puff pastry dough
6. Form into one large cake or several small ones
7. Glaze with beaten egg and bake in the oven
8. You can use this proportion to make as many cakes as you wish
