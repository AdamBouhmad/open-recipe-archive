## Ingredients

- chard
- water
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the chard
2. Cook them in water, stirring from time to time so the top does not blacken
3. When cooked, place them to drain
4. Make a white sauce with a pinch of flour, water, butter, salt, pepper, and a dash of vinegar
5. Make it thicken over the fire and put the chard to boil a little moment over low heat so they take flavor
6. If the butter turned to oil, it would be a sign that the sauce is too thick; put in a spoonful of water and stir until the sauce returns as before
