## Ingredients

- one hen cooked on a spit
- a chopine of cream or half a setier of milk
- butter the size of half an egg
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- two shallots
- half a clove of garlic
- parsley
- chives
- one egg yolk
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Ordinarily one takes a hen cooked on a spit that has been removed from the table; cut it into pieces, or better, when the hen is almost whole and large, lift all the meat and cut it into fillets
2. Put in a pan a chopine of cream or half a setier of milk; when it boils, put into it butter the size of half an egg worked with a pinch of flour, with salt, pepper, two shallots, half a clove of garlic, parsley, chives
3. Boil on low heat for half an hour
4. When it is reduced to the consistency of a sauce, pass it through a fine sieve, put the hen in it to warm without boiling
5. If the sauce is not quite bound, put an egg yolk in it, make it thicken without boiling, serving two or three drops of vinegar
