## Ingredients

- Rabbit quenelle forcemeat
- Butter
- Truffles
- Cockscombs
- Calf kidneys
- Veal sweetbreads
- Lamb sweetbreads
- Truffles

## Directions

1. Make your forcemeat slightly firmer than before by putting one-sixth less butter in the forcemeat
2. Poach them with a spoon as before
3. Drain them
4. Cut eight or ten truffles into small lardons
5. Place them in your quenelles at equal distances and of the same thickness
6. Place four rows on each quenelle
7. When all are studded, place them in a casserole with kidneys and cockscombs, small veal sweetbreads, lamb sweetbreads, and truffles
