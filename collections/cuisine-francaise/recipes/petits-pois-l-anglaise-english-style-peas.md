## Ingredients

- Water
- Salt
- Peas
- Butter

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil in a large saucepan three-quarters of an hour before serving.
2. When the water boils, add salt and your peas.
3. Let them boil until the moment of serving.
4. Drain them in a strainer.
5. Put them on the plate with six small pieces of butter on top, or only a quarter pound and a half of butter, without mixing it with the peas.
