## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- small turnips
- butter
- velouté or flour
- bouillon
- coarse pepper
- bay leaf
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare your wings as previously described
2. Cook them in a skillet as instructed
3. Turn turnips into small sticks, olives or otherwise, but all of the same size and thickness
4. Sauté them in white butter, that is to say do not let them take on color
5. After a quarter hour, pour in eight spoonfuls of velouté; if you do not have any, add a tablespoon of flour that you will mix with your butter and turnips
6. Add a pot spoonful of bouillon, some coarse pepper, a bay leaf
7. Cook them at high heat; skim and degrease them
8. When your turnips are cooked, drain your wings; arrange them in crowns
9. Add a liaison of two egg yolks into your turnips, and place them in the middle of your wings
