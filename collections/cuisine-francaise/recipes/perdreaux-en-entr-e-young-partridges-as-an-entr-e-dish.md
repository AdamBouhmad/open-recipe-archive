## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Their livers
- Lard, grated
- Salt
- Parsley, chopped
- Chives, chopped
- Butter
- Lard for wrapping
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the partridges, then gut them
2. Make a small forcemeat from their livers mixed with grated lard, a little salt, chopped parsley and chives
3. Place this forcemeat inside the body and sew it closed so nothing escapes
4. Truss the legs over the breast
5. Reheat them in a casserole over the fire with a little butter
6. Cook them on the spit wrapped in lard and paper
7. When cooked, serve them with the sauce or ragout of your choice
