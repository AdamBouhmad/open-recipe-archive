## Ingredients

- 4 carrots
- 4 turnips
- 6 leeks
- 6 onions
- a little parsley root
- a good piece of butter
- water
- a quarter pound of rice
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut these roots into small dice
2. Pass everything with a good piece of butter
3. When your vegetables are well passed, moisten them with water, as much as needed for your soup
4. Put a quarter pound of rice, or more if your soup is large
5. Make your soup boil for an hour and a half
6. When your rice and vegetables are cooked, add salt and coarse pepper for seasoning
7. Be careful that your soup does not have too much butter and never any scum
