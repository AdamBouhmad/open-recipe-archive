## Ingredients

- three spoonfuls of coulis
- the same amount of jus
- six eggs
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Dilute three spoonfuls of coulis and the same amount of jus with six eggs, whites and yolks
2. Season with salt and coarse pepper
3. Pass through a sieve and put on the serving plate
4. Cook in a bain-marie
5. When set, cut with several knife strokes while serving
6. Put clear jus on top
