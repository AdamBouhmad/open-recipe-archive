## Ingredients

- Half a setier of white wine
- As much broth
- One shallot
- A little garlic
- A pinch of salad greens, finely chopped
- Butter about the size of a walnut
- A small pinch of flour
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Boil half a setier of white wine with as much broth
2. When reduced by half, add one shallot, a little garlic, a pinch of finely chopped salad greens
3. Boil for a moment and then add butter about the size of a walnut mixed with a small pinch of flour, salt and coarse pepper
4. Bind on the fire
