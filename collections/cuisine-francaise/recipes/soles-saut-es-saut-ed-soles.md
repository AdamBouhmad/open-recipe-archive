## Ingredients

- Soles
- Chopped and washed parsley
- Chopped chives
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the head and tail from your soles
2. After cleaning them, put them in your sauté pan
3. Sprinkle them with a little chopped and washed parsley, a little chopped chives, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
