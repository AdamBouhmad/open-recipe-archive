## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- two pounds of raspberries

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of sugar in the same manner as for strawberries. When it reaches the proper cooking point, add the raspberries prepared as follows: peel two pounds of raspberries and pass them through a sieve to make jam. Put this jam on the fire to dry it until it is nearly sticking to the pan. Then put it in the sugar and let it boil a few times while stirring constantly, and then put it in your pots.
