## Ingredients

- one whole leg of mutton
- large lardons seasoned with salt, pepper and quatre épices (four spices)
- lard for barding
- four carrots
- six onions, one studded with four cloves
- four bay leaves
- thyme
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Debone the leg of mutton at the rump down to the thighbone
2. Lard the interior of the leg with large lardons seasoned with salt, pepper and quatre épices, ensuring they do not protrude on one side
3. Tie the leg with twine
4. Place lard strips on top
5. Add four carrots, six onions (one studded with four cloves), four bay leaves, and a little thyme
6. Moisten the leg with water and add salt so that it is completely submerged
7. Boil for three hours
8. When ready to serve, untie the leg and serve with a little of the cooking liquid passed through a sieve
9. Taste to ensure it has good seasoning and flavor
10. Optionally serve with turned potatoes cooked in plain water around the leg, or with tomato sauce
