## Ingredients

- Rice
- Parsnip
- Carrot
- Onions
- Parsley root
- Cabbage
- Celery
- Turnips
- Pea water
- Butter
- Onion juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the rice three or four times in warm water, rubbing it well in your hands
2. Cook it in a good lean bouillon made with parsnip, carrot, onions, parsley root, cabbage, celery, and turnips in moderate quantities, with no one vegetable dominating the others, especially restraining the celery and parsley root
3. Add a piece of butter and onion juice to the bouillon
4. Cook over low heat for three hours
5. Season to taste
6. Serve neither too clear nor too thick
