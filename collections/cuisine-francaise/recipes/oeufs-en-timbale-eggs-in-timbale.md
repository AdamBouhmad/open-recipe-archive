## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs, six
- Coulis, three or four spoonfuls
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter to coat the inside of six copper goblets or small timbales
2. Take six eggs, whites and yolks, and thin them with three or four spoonfuls of coulis
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Pass through a sieve and put into the goblets, being careful not to fill them completely
5. Cook in a bain-marie with gently boiling water
6. When they are firm, run a knife gently around the edge to detach them from the goblet and flip them into the serving dish
7. Serve with clear jus
