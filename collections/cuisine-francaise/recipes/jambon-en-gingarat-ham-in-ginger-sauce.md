## Ingredients

- ham
- ham fat or lard
- water
- vinegar
- crushed pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the ham into very thin slices and put them in a saucepan or skillet with a little ham fat or lard
2. Cook on low heat
3. When cooked, arrange the ham on a platter
4. In the same saucepan, add a little water, a dash of vinegar, and crushed pepper
5. Detach what remains in the saucepan by stirring the sauce with a spoon
6. Serve the sauce over the ham
