## Ingredients

- carp
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the carp into sections and place in a casserole with butter, parsley, chives, and mushrooms, all chopped, and one chopine of white wine.
2. When cooked, serve with good taste and short sauce.
