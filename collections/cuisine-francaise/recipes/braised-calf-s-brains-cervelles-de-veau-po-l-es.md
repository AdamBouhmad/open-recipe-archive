## Ingredients

- Three calf's brains
- Lard
- Salted water
- Vinegar
- Lemon slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Chives
- Bouillon or salted water

## Directions

1. Remove the thin skin enveloping the brain
2. Remove fibers from the interior
3. After thoroughly cleaning, soak for two hours
4. Prepare a pot of boiling water with a small handful of salt and half a glass of vinegar
5. Blanch the brains for a quarter hour in boiling water
6. Remove and place in cold water
7. Line the bottom of a pot with lard, place brains on top
8. Cover with lard
9. Pour braising liquid over sufficiently for them to cook
10. If you lack braising liquid, cook the brains between lard, lemon slices, half a glass of vinegar, carrots, onions, two cloves, two bay leaves, thyme, parsley and chives
11. Moisten with bouillon or salted water
