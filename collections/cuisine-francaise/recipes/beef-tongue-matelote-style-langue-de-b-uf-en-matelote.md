## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- three cloves
- water
- salt
- bottle of white wine
- small onions
- butter
- flour
- coarse pepper
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue as for the gherkin version, except do not lard it
2. Cook it in the same seasoning with a bottle of white wine
3. When the tongue is cooked, pass the cooking liquid through a silk sieve
4. Have small onions that you sauté in butter until they are brown
5. Dust them with a spoonful of flour and moisten with the degreased cooking liquid
6. Add a little coarse pepper and mushrooms
7. When the onions are cooked, remove them
8. If your sauce is too thin, reduce it; cut the tongue into pieces
9. Once the sauce is reduced, place it on top with the onions and mushrooms, and simmer the ragout for a quarter hour
10. Take care to degrease it and ensure it is well-salted
