## Ingredients

- Four eggplants
- Lard
- Butter
- Oil
- Mushrooms
- Shallots
- Parsley
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Spices
- Quenelle forcemeat
- Anchovies
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut four eggplants in half lengthwise without hollowing out too much so they retain some flesh
2. Grate lard twice the size of an egg and the same amount of butter
3. Heat four large spoonfuls of oil in a saucepan
4. Add two large spoonfuls of finely chopped mushrooms, a small handful of shallots and one of parsley
5. Cook these fine herbs with the butter, lard and oil
6. When well browned, add salt, coarse pepper, a little spice and let cool
7. Add as much cooked quenelle forcemeat as you have fine herbs
8. Chop three anchovies and mix with the forcemeat and herbs
9. Fill the eight eggplant halves with this mixture
10. Smooth the top of the forcemeat with egg and bread
11. Half an hour before serving, place on a baking sheet in the oven or over moderate heat with a camp oven on top
