## Ingredients

- six eggs
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- coarse pepper
- one beaten egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make three very thin omelets of three eggs each
2. Season with parsley, chives, salt, and coarse pepper
3. As you make them, spread them on a saucepan lid and roll them tightly
4. Cut each omelet in half to make six pieces from three
5. Dip them in beaten egg and coat with breadcrumbs
6. Fry them to a beautiful color
7. Serve garnished with parsley
