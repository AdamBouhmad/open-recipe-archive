## Ingredients

- one good aged leg of mutton
- celery, half-cooked in embers or broth
- cornichons
- tarragon branches, blanched
- lard
- anchovy fillets

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the leg without detaching it from the shank
2. Lard all the meat with celery that has been half-cooked in embers or broth, cornichons cut into thick strips, some blanched tarragon branches, lard, and anchovy fillets, all lightly seasoned
3. Replace the skin over the top so that nothing shows
4. Secure it with string to prevent it from shrinking during cooking
5. Cook your leg at the hearth as usual
6. Serve with a sauce in which you add a little shallot
