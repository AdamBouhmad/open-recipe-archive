## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- mushrooms
- a small point of garlic
- flour
- bouillon
- a glass of white wine
- salt
- coarse pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pass over the fire with butter, onion, and mushrooms cut into fillets, with a small point of garlic
2. When the onion begins to color, add a good pinch of flour
3. Moisten with bouillon and a glass of white wine, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Boil for half an hour and reduce to sauce consistency
5. Add hard-boiled eggs with the whites cut into fillets and the yolks whole
6. Boil for a moment and serve
