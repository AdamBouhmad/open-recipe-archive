## Ingredients

- 1 quarter pound sugar
- 1 half-glass water
- 1 pound muscat grapes

## Directions

1. Put a quarter pound of sugar with a half-glass of water in a pan
2. Bring to boil, skim and reduce to a strong syrup
3. Add 1 pound of muscat grapes, seeded and with pips removed
4. Let boil two or three times and arrange in the compote dish
5. If there is foam, remove it with white paper
