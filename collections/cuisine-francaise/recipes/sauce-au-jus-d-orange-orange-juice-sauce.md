## Ingredients

- Half a glass of good broth
- As much juice
- A few zests of bitter orange peel
- Good butter about half the size of an egg, mixed with a small pinch of flour
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Juice of one bitter orange

## Directions

1. Put half a glass of good broth in a saucepan with as much juice, a few zests of bitter orange peel, good butter about half the size of an egg mixed with a small pinch of flour, salt and coarse pepper
2. Bind on the fire and then squeeze in the juice of one bitter orange
