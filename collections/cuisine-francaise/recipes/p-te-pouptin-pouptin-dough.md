## Ingredients

- One pint of water
- One half-quarter pound of butter
- One lemon zest
- A little salt
- One liter of flour, sifted through silk
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one pint of water, one half-quarter pound of butter, one lemon zest, and a little salt
2. Place the saucepan over the fire
3. When the mixture is ready to boil, sift one liter of flour through silk and put into your saucepan as much as the water can absorb
4. When the dough is very thick, cook it by stirring constantly with a wooden spoon
5. Let it cool
6. Then break an egg into it and mix it with the dough
7. Keep adding eggs until your dough is soft
8. Butter a large saucepan to contain the dough
9. Put it to cook in an oven hotter than for biscuit
10. Your pouptin must be somewhat dry
11. When it reaches one-quarter of the saucepan height, it will be full when your dough is cooked
12. Remove it from the vessel and spread some preserve inside, coating the interior
