## Ingredients

- Seven mutton tails
- Lard
- Mutton slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Laurel leaves
- Thyme
- Bouillon
- Spanish sauce (Espagnole) reduced

## Directions

1. Choose seven mutton tails all of the same size and length
2. Put in a pan bacon strips, a few slices of mutton, four carrots, four onions (one studded with two cloves), two laurel leaves, and a little thyme
3. Cover the tails with bacon strips
4. Pour one spoonfull of bouillon
5. Braise them for four hours
6. At the moment of serving, carefully remove them from the braising liquid
7. Drain them
8. Glaze them
9. Serve reduced Spanish sauce on top
