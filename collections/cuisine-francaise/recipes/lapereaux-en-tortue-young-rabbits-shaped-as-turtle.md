## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- white wine
- bouillon
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- coulis

## Directions

1. Empty a rabbit, leave the liver and remove the bitter part
2. Debone the rabbit only in the middle of the belly
3. Make a hole in the skin to pass the front half of the rabbit through it so it becomes inverted and takes the shape of a turtle
4. Tie it and cook with a glass of white wine, a little bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a garlic clove, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, salt, and pepper
5. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve, degrease it, and add a little coulis to bind it
6. Reduce to sauce consistency and serve over the rabbit
