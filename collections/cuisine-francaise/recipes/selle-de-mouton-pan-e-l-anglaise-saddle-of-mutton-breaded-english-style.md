## Ingredients

- saddle of mutton prepared and cooked as in the braised recipe
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the saddle as for the braised version
2. When cooked, season with salt and coarse pepper
3. Dip it in warm butter
4. Coat it with breadcrumbs
5. Warm butter the size of half an egg
6. Break three eggs and mix them with the butter
7. Add a pinch of salt
8. Dip the saddle in the eggs and coat it all over
9. Place it in the breadcrumbs and give the saddle a fine shape
10. Three quarters of an hour before serving, put it on the grill with a very hot campfire oven above it to give it color
11. At the moment of serving, dress it on your platter
12. Put clear juice underneath; if your saddle was cold, you would have more ease in breading it
