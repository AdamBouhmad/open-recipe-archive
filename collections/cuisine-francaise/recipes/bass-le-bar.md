## Ingredients

- Bass
- White wine
- Butter
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Roots
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboule)
- Oil

## Directions

1. Gut and wash the bass
2. Cook in court-bouillon with white wine, butter, water, salt, pepper, onions, roots, parsley, and chives
3. When cooked and well drained, serve on a napkin garnished with fresh parsley
