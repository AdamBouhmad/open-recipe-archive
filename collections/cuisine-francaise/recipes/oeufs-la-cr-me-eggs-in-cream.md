## Ingredients

- 12 hard eggs
- a good piece of butter
- a spoonful of flour
- parsley and chives, finely chopped
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- a glass of cream

## Directions

1. Hard boil twelve eggs
2. Cut them into slices
3. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with a spoonful of flour, a little finely chopped parsley and chives, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Mix everything together
5. Add a glass of cream
6. Turn the sauce over the fire
7. At the first boil, pour it over your eggs
8. Toss them
9. When they are well heated, serve them
