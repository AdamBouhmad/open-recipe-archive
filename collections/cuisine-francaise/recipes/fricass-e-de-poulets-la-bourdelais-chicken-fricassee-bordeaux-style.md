## Ingredients

- Chicken fricassee as in the previous recipe
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make the fricassee in the same way as the previous one, with this difference: when it is arranged on its plate, bread it with breadcrumbs; put on the breadcrumbs small pieces of butter the size of a pea, brown it under a pie dish cover or in an oven; serve hot. This method is good for masking a fricassee that has been removed from the table.
