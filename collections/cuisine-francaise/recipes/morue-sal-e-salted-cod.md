## Ingredients

- salted cod
- flour
- butter
- pepper
- milk
- verjus or parsley and chives

## Directions

1. Choose cod with white flesh, black skin, and large flakes
2. Wash it after scaling
3. Cook it briefly in a pot with river water
4. Drain and remove the flesh by flakes or leave it whole
5. In a casserole, put a little flour and a piece of butter with a little pepper
6. Dilute with a little milk
7. Add verjus in grains and make the sauce thicken on the fire
8. Add the cod to impart flavor
9. Serve
10. In another season, use chopped parsley and chives instead of verjus
