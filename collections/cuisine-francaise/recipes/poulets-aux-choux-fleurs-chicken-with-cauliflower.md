## Ingredients

- Two medium chickens
- Chicken livers
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboule)
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lard
- Buttered paper
- Cauliflower
- Water
- Coulis
- Egg-sized piece of butter

## Directions

1. Singe, pluck, and gut the chickens; remove the bitter part from the livers, chop them and mix with a piece of butter, chopped parsley, chopped chives, salt, and black pepper
2. Put this small forcemeat in the body, leave the legs and truss them like a young turkey
3. Sear them on the fire in a casserole with a little butter or lard, then wrap them with lard and buttered paper to roast on the spit
4. When cooked, arrange them on the serving plate, arrange cauliflower well drained around them, which you will have cooked with water, a little butter and salt
5. Pour over a sauce made with a little coulis, butter the size of half an egg, salt, and black pepper, thicken it over heat without boiling
