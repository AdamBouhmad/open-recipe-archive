## Ingredients

- turkey
- meat forcemeat
- lard slices
- one glass of white wine
- good bouillon
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- two cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- two onions
- carrot
- parsnip
- coulis

## Directions

1. Singe a turkey and cut it in half
2. Bone each half completely
3. Spread a good meat forcemeat on each half
4. Roll each half and tie with string
5. Cook covered with lard slices, one glass of white wine, and equal amount of good bouillon
6. Add a bouquet of parsley, chives, garlic, cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, salt, pepper, onion slices, carrot, and parsnip
7. When cooked, degrease the sauce and strain through a sieve
8. Add a little coulis to bind the sauce
9. Serve over the meat
