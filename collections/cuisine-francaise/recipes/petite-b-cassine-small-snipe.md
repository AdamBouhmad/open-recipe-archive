## Ingredients

- Snipes
- A good piece of butter
- Chopped shallots
- A little grated nutmeg
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Juice of two lemons
- Half a glass of white wine
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Truss your snipes, singe them and pluck them
2. Put a good piece of butter in a casserole with your snipes, chopped shallots, a little grated nutmeg, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Cook them on a hot fire; when you have sautéed them for seven or eight minutes, add the juice of two lemons, half a glass of white wine, and a little bread crumbs
4. Leave them on the fire until your sauce has come to a boil; then remove them from the fire and serve
