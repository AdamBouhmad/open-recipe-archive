## Ingredients

- 15 pounds of cherries
- 2 pounds of currants
- two-thirds of the pits, crushed
- 1 quarteron of sugar per pint of juice

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries and currants together well
2. Crush two-thirds of the pits and mix them together
3. Put everything in a barrel with a quarteron of sugar per pint of juice
4. Ensure the barrel is full and cover it only with a grape leaf and sand around it
5. Let it ferment for about three weeks until it stops bubbling
6. Keep the barrel always full by adding cherry juice as needed
7. Once it stops bubbling, cork it with a good stopper
8. After two months, draw it off clear and put it in bottles
