## Ingredients

- sheep's feet
- cucumbers
- vinegar, one spoonful
- salt
- butter, one piece
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- garlic, one clove
- bay leaf, half a leaf
- cloves, two
- flour, one pinch
- bouillon
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut each foot into three pieces after cooking in water and thoroughly cleaning
2. Cut cucumbers into large pieces and marinate for one hour with one spoonful of vinegar and a little salt
3. Press them well in your hands to extract all the water
4. Put them in the saucepan with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, half a bay leaf, two cloves
5. Pass everything together on the fire
6. Add a pinch of flour, moistened with bouillon
7. Let boil on a small fire until the cucumbers are cooked and there is almost no sauce remaining
8. Add a liaison of egg yolks diluted with cream
9. Thicken the sauce on the fire without letting it boil, for fear it turns
10. Before serving, taste if there is enough salt and vinegar, add a little coarse pepper
