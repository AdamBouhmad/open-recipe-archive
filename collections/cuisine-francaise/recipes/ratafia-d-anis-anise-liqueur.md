## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1/2 setier water
- 1/2 setier water
- 3 ounces anise
- 3 chopines brandy

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of sugar in a pan with 1/2 setier of water, boil them together until the sugar is well skimmed and clear
2. Then boil 1/2 setier of water separately
3. Put 3 ounces of anise in it, remove from fire without letting it boil, let it infuse for a quarter hour and put it in the sugar with 3 chopines of brandy
4. Stir everything together before putting it in a jug, seal the jug well and put it in the sun
5. Let the liqueur infuse for three weeks
6. Before putting it in bottles, strain it through a cloth or filter
7. This makes two pints
