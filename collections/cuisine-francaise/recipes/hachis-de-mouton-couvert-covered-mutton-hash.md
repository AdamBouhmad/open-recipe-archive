## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- onions
- salt
- flour
- raw juice
- bouillon
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook a shoulder of mutton on the spit.
2. Mince very finely three or four onions.
3. When they begin to take color, add a pinch of salt and a small pinch of flour.
4. Mix the raw juice into this.
5. When it is a beautiful golden color, add two ladles of good bouillon.
6. When the shoulder is cooked on the spit, remove the meat without touching the skin and to the bone.
7. Since it must appear whole, take the meat you have removed, mince it very finely with parsley, chives, and mushrooms.
8. Heat it without boiling and season with nutmeg.
9. Sprinkle it over the top of the shoulder and cover the plat.
10. Then cover it with the shoulder.
