## Ingredients

- a pinch of chopped green lemon
- two ounces of sugar
- a little salt
- butter about half an egg's worth
- one good glass of water
- four or five spoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a pinch of chopped green lemon, two ounces of sugar, a little salt, and butter about the size of half an egg
2. Add one good glass of water and bring to a boil for a moment
3. Add four or five spoonfuls of flour
4. Dry it on the fire while stirring constantly
