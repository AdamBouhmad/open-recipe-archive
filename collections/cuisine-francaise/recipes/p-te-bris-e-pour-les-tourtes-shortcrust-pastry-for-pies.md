## Ingredients

- one quarter of flour
- five quarterons of good butter
- approximately one ounce of salt
- water

## Directions

1. Put your flour on a very clean table
2. Make a hole in the middle to put the salt
3. Add the butter in small pieces
4. Add water carefully, as too much will make the dough too loose and without structure
5. Mix the butter well with the water, and gradually with the flour
6. When the flour has absorbed all the water, knead with force of arm; your dough cannot be too thick, provided it is well-bound and has no lumps in it
7. Make this dough at least two hours before using it, so it has time to rest
