## Ingredients

- One glass of good broth
- A dash of vinegar
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Three hard-boiled egg yolks, chopped
- Good butter, about half the size of an egg
- A small pinch of flour

## Directions

1. Put one glass of good broth in a saucepan with a dash of vinegar, salt, coarse pepper, three chopped hard-boiled egg yolks, and good butter about half the size of an egg mixed with a small pinch of flour
2. Bind on the fire and serve as desired
