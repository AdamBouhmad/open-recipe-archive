## Ingredients

- butter the size of a walnut
- a pinch of flour
- one glass of bouillon
- one chopped anchovy
- fine capers
- salt
- coarse pepper
- two whole chives

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan and mix with a pinch of flour
2. Dilute with one glass of bouillon
3. Add chopped anchovy, fine capers, salt, coarse pepper, and two whole chives
4. Thicken the sauce over the fire
5. Remove the chives before serving
