## Ingredients

- Four rabbits
- Four partridges
- Two calf necks
- Two pounds of sliced beef
- One bottle of dry white wine
- Half great broth and half consommé
- Eight carrots
- Ten onions
- Three cloves
- A little thyme
- A little basil
- A little wild thyme

## Directions

1. Have four rabbits, four partridges, two calf necks, two pounds of sliced beef
2. Put these meats in a pot
3. Throw a bottle of dry white wine into it
4. Boil everything until it has reduced to glaze
5. When you see there is no more jus in your pot, do not wait for the bottom to take color to fill it
6. Put half great broth and half consommé
7. Try not to have too much liquid
8. Garnish your pot with eight carrots, ten onions, three cloves, a little thyme, a little basil, a little wild thyme
9. Let your pot boil gently
10. When your meats are cooked, pass your game essence through fine cloth
11. Be careful to skim your pot well and do not add salt since your great broth is seasoned
