## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- parsley branches
- lard
- hot lard or fat
- shallots
- juice
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take two handfuls of parsley branches without removing the stems and well green.
2. Lard the top of the shoulder with parsley so it is well covered.
3. Put it on the spit.
4. When the parsley is heated, pour hot lard gently over the parsley with a spoon and baste with it from time to time until the shoulder is cooked.
5. To serve, mince two shallots and put them in a little juice with salt and coarse pepper.
6. Heat the sauce and pour it under the shoulder.
