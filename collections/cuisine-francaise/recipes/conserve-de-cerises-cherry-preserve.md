## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 glass water
- 1 pound fresh cherries

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of sugar in the same way as for redcurrant preserve
2. Take 1 pound of fine cherries, remove the stems and pits, put them on the fire to release their liquid
3. Pass them through a sieve, pressing firmly so only the skins remain in the sieve
4. Put on the fire everything you have passed through to dry it
5. Finish your preserve as with the redcurrant preserve
