## Ingredients

- cod
- butter
- flour
- garlic
- coarse pepper
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Cook the cod in water
2. Once cooked, drain it and remove the flesh in flakes
3. In a saucepan, melt a good piece of butter with half a spoonful of flour and a minced point of garlic
4. Add coarse pepper and moisten with cream or milk
5. Make the sauce bind over the fire and add the cod fillets
6. Heat and serve
