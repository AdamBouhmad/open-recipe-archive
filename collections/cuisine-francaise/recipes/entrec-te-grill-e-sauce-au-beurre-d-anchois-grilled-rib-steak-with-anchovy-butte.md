## Ingredients

- Rib of beef
- Oil or butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Espagnole sauce
- Anchovy butter, the size of an egg
- Roux
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your rib as the one with jus
2. Have a prepared espagnole sauce that is somewhat clear
3. At the moment of saucing, put into your sauce, which will be very hot, anchovy butter the size of an egg, which you will stir well into your sauce until it is well melted, without putting your sauce on the fire, or at least without boiling it
4. Pour it over your rib (see Prepared Espagnole and Anchovy Butter)
5. If you lack sauce, make a light roux which you moisten with seasoned bouillon, and add your anchovy butter to it
