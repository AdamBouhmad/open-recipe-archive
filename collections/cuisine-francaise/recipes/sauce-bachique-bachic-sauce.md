## Ingredients

- One spoonful of fine oil
- Half-setier of good bouillon
- One chopine of white wine
- Shallot
- Watercress
- Tarragon
- Chervil
- Parsley
- Chives
- A little garlic
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A little coulis (optional)

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one spoonful of fine oil, half-setier of good bouillon, and one chopine of white wine
2. Boil everything together and reduce to more than half
3. Add minced shallot, watercress, tarragon, chervil, parsley, chives, a little garlic, salt, and coarse pepper, all chopped very fine
4. Boil everything together for a moment and serve
5. If you have a little coulis to add to the sauce, it will only be better
