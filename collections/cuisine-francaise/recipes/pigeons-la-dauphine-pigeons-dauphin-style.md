## Ingredients

- scalded pigeons
- lard strips
- bouillon
- lemon slice
- bouquet garni
- glazed calf sweetbreads

## Directions

1. Cook the scalded pigeons between strips of lard with a little bouillon and a slice of lemon and a bouquet garni
2. Serve them with glazed calf sweetbreads prepared like fricandeau
