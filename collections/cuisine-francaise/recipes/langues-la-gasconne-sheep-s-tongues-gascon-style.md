## Ingredients

- three sheep's tongues cooked in water
- coulis
- parsley
- chives
- half a clove of garlic
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut three cooked sheep's tongues into thin fillets
2. Have a heat-proof dish and put at the bottom a little coulis with parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, mushrooms, all chopped very fine, salt, and pepper
3. Arrange the tongue fillets on top and season them as well on top as underneath
4. Cover the entire top with breadcrumbs, and scatter small pieces of butter all over the breadcrumbs, about the size of peas, which will nourish the ragout and prevent the breadcrumbs from blackening in the heat
5. Put the dish on a small fire, cover it with a pie-dish lid and put fire on top
6. When it is a beautiful color, serve with light sauce
