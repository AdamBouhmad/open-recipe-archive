## Ingredients

- scraps of roasted suckling pig
- butter
- mushrooms
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- shallots
- cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- flour
- white wine
- broth
- salt
- coarse pepper
- egg yolks
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Take the scraps of a roasted suckling pig and cut them into thin strips
2. Put in a saucepan a piece of good butter the size of half an egg with mushrooms cut into thin strips, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a garlic clove, two shallots, two cloves, half a bay leaf, thyme, and basil
3. Put them on the fire, add a pinch of flour, moisten with a glass of white wine and as much broth, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Boil on low heat and reduce by half
5. Remove the bouquet and add the meat strips, heat without boiling
6. Add a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with two spoonfuls of verjuice and as much broth, thicken on the fire without boiling
7. Serve hot
