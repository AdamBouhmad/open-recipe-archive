## Ingredients

- calf's head
- godiveau (veal forcemeat)
- velouté sauce, reduced
- four spices (more than usual)
- parsley, minced
- shallots, minced
- lemon
- fine white linen
- lard bards

## Directions

1. Debone your entire calf's head; be careful not to puncture it, that is, avoid any holes caused by the knife
2. Be careful that the eyes remain attached to the head
3. When deboned, let it soak in cold water to disgorge
4. Blanch it so your head retains the same shape as if it had bones
5. Put it in cold water, dry it, then singe it
6. Make godiveau; when it is three-quarters moistened, add two spoonfuls of reduced velouté
7. Add somewhat more than the usual amount of four spices to this farce, along with minced parsley and shallots
8. Fill your head with this godiveau, reshaping it to its original form with a trussing needle and string
9. Sew it so it maintains its shape
10. When your head is fully prepared, rub it with lemon
11. Have a fine white linen cloth on which you place lard bards
12. Place your head on it and cover it with bards
13. Wrap it and tie both ends of your linen cloth; then truss your head
