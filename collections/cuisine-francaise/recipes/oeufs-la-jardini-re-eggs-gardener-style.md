## Ingredients

- four or five large onions
- butter
- flour
- a chopine of milk
- salt
- coarse pepper
- ten eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan four or five large onions cut into fillets with a piece of butter
2. Pass them over the fire until almost cooked
3. Then add a good pinch of flour
4. Moisten with a chopine of milk
5. Season with salt and coarse pepper
6. Boil until the sauce is thick
7. Remove from the fire to add ten eggs which you beat together
8. Put everything in the serving plate to cook over a small fire covered with a pie-dish lid
