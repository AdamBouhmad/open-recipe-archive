## Ingredients

- One pound of grated lard
- One pound of fat
- Half a pound of butter
- Two lemons, cut in slices without the white
- Two bay leaves
- Two cloves
- Four carrots, cut in dice
- Four onions
- One pot spoonful of water

## Directions

1. Put one pound of grated lard, one pound of fat, half a pound of butter, two lemons cut in slices with the white removed, two bay leaves, two cloves, four carrots cut in dice, four onions, and one pot spoonful of water together
2. Boil until reduced
3. Be careful to stir your blanc and not let it stick
4. When there is no more moisture and your fat is melted, moisten it with water
5. Add clarified salt
6. Boil it
7. Skim it and use as needed
