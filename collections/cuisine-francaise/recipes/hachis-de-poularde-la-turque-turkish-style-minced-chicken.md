## Ingredients

- two hens
- four skimming spoons of béchamel
- one spoon of cream
- butter the size of an egg
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- poached eggs
- glazed filets mignons or croûtons

## Directions

1. Spit roast two hens
2. When cooled, remove the meat and mince it finely
3. Place it in a casserole
4. Add four skimming spoons of béchamel and one spoon of cream in another casserole
5. When it has boiled a few times, put it in your mince with butter the size of an egg, a little salt, a little pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
6. Keep it hot without letting it boil
7. At the moment of serving, put it in your dish with poached eggs on top; between each egg, a glazed filet mignon or croûton
