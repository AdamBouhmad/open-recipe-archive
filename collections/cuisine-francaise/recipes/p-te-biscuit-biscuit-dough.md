## Ingredients

- Fifteen eggs
- One pound of fine powdered sugar
- A little orange flower water or a little finely chopped lemon zest, or such seasoning as you judge fit
- One pound of flour for large biscuits, or three-quarters pound for small biscuits
- Powdered sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Break the fifteen eggs; put the whites in one bowl and the yolks in another
2. With the yolks employ one pound of fine powdered sugar
3. Add a little orange flower water or a little finely chopped lemon zest, or such seasoning as you judge convenient
4. Beat your yolks and sugar well with a spatula or wooden spoon
5. When the yolks are somewhat lightened, beat the whites with a willow whisk
6. As soon as they are firm and hold upright, join your yolks to them
7. If for a large biscuit, put one pound of flour; if for small biscuits, use only three-quarters pound
8. Mix it lightly with the yolks and whites
9. Your dough well mixed, arrange it in a buttered mold or in your pans
10. Dust the exterior with powdered sugar
11. Place your biscuit in a gentle oven
12. You can also spoon it into small molds
