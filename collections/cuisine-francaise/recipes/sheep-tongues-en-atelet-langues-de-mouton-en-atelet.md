## Ingredients

- Sheep tongues
- Hashed sauce
- Raw egg yolk
- Veal teat or small lard
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Beaten egg
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tongues as those described as braised
2. Cut them into square pieces, two lines thick
3. Make a hashed sauce to thicken it
4. When reduced, put a raw egg yolk that you will mix with your sauce which will be almost boiling
5. Put your pieces of tongue with your sauce
6. Stir everything well so that your pieces take up the sauce
7. Place them on a plate to cool
8. Have a cooked veal teat or small lard that you will cut into squares of the same size as those of your tongues
9. Put a piece of tongue, a piece of teat, and so on to the end of the skewer
10. When it is full, pour sauce over it
11. With the knife, smooth it on the four sides
12. Dip your skewer in butter
13. Put it in the bread crumb
14. After dip it in beaten egg
15. Bread it once more
16. Take care that your four sides are well united, and your four corners well formed
17. Your skewers finished, a quarter hour and a half before serving, put them on the grill over gentle fire
18. Turn them on three sides, and on the fourth make them take color in the country oven or with a hot red palette for glazing
19. At the moment of serving, put them on the plate
20. Pour Italian sauce underneath
