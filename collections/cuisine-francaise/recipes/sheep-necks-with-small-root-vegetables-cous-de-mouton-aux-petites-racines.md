## Ingredients

- Two sheep necks
- Lard strips
- Meat trimmings
- Four carrots
- Five onions, one of which studded with three cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Thyme
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Small root vegetables

## Directions

1. You will have two sheep necks
2. Cut off the bloody end
3. Tie them up
4. Put lard strips in a casserole, meat trimmings, four carrots, five onions, one of which studded with three cloves, two bay leaves, a little thyme, a bouquet of parsley and chives
5. Put your necks with this seasoning
6. Cover them with lard strips
7. Moisten them with bouillon or water
8. Then add salt
9. When your necks boil, put them over a gentle fire for four hours
10. At the moment of serving, drain them
11. Arrange them on your plate
12. Pour your small root vegetables over them (See Small Root Vegetables)
13. You can also instead of root vegetables put turnips cut into small sticks (See Turnips for entrée)
14. If you do not have sauce for your root vegetables, make a light roux that you moisten with the cooking liquid in which your necks have cooked
15. Prepare your small root vegetables as those described
