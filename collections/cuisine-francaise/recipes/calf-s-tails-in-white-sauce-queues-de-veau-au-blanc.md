## Ingredients

- Calf's tails
- Butter
- Flour
- Water
- Parsley and chives
- Bay leaves
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Small onions
- Egg yolks, four or more depending on the strength of the ragout

## Directions

1. Prepare your tails as the previous recipe; sauté them in butter and add flour in the same manner
2. Moisten with water
3. Add a bouquet of parsley and chives, two bay leaves, mushrooms, salt and a little coarse pepper
4. Skim it; when it is three-quarters cooked, degrease it
5. Add small, well-trimmed onions of uniform size; do not let them cook too much lest they become mushy
6. At the moment of serving, blend in a liaison of four egg yolks, more or less according to how strong your ragout is
7. Ensure it is well seasoned with salt. This ragout can be made brown or white; you would make a roux
