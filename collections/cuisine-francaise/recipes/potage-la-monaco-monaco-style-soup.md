## Ingredients

- Soft bread crumb
- Fine sugar
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut bread crumb into small rectangular squares, three lines thick, two and a half inches long, and one and a half inches wide
2. Dust your bread pieces with very fine sugar and toast them over a gentle fire so they do not take too much color
3. Put enough of them in your soup tureen so that your soup is not too thick
4. At the moment of serving, pour your bound milk over your bread
5. This soup can also be made without being bound
