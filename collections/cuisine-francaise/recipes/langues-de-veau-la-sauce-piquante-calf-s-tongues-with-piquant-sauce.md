## Ingredients

- eight calf's tongues
- medium lardons, well seasoned
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- one pot-spoon of bouillon
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the tongues
2. Blanch them for about a quarter hour
3. Refresh them in cold water
4. Trim them
5. Lard them with well-seasoned medium lardons
6. Cook the tongues in a braise or cooking stock, or with a few carrots, onions, cloves, thyme, bay leaf in moderate amounts, and one pot-spoon of bouillon for three hours
7. Remove the skin from the tongues
8. Glaze them
9. Arrange them around the edge of the platter with an oval crouton in the center
10. Pour piquant sauce over the tongues
