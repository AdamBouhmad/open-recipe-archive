## Ingredients

- Two calf's brains
- bouillon
- two or three tablespoons of white vinegar
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- one garlic clove
- three cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- two handfuls of flour
- one tablespoon of oil
- half a septier of white wine
- fine salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Soak two calf's brains in warm water to disgorge
2. Cook them with a little bouillon, two or three tablespoons of white vinegar, a bouquet of parsley, chives, one garlic clove, three cloves, thyme, bay leaf and basil
3. When cooked, cut each piece of brain in two and dip them in a batter made with two handfuls of flour diluted with one tablespoon of oil, half a septier of white wine and fine salt
4. Fry them in lard until they are a beautiful golden color and the batter is crispy
5. Serve hot
