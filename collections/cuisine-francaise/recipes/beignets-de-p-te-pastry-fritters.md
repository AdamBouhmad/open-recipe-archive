## Ingredients

- half a litron of flour
- butter the size of an egg
- a good pinch of salt
- about half a glass of water
- frangipane cream
- sugar
- red food coloring

## Directions

1. Put on a table half a litron of flour, butter the size of an egg, a good pinch of salt, about half a glass of water
2. Knead the dough, then beat it very thin and cut it with a small pastry cutter
3. Put a little frangipane cream on each piece, cover with a top like the bottom, moisten the edges and glue them together by pinching all around
4. Fry until golden color, glaze the top with sugar and red food coloring
