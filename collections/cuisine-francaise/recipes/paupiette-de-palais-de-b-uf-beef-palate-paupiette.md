## Ingredients

- beef palate
- sauce à atelet
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the palate as in the previous recipe
2. Cut bands wider so that your roll will be somewhat long, about like a croquette
3. Coat with sauce à atelet around it (to coat your paupiette)
4. Bread it with egg and fry it like croquettes
5. Place parsley around and on top; serve without sauce underneath
