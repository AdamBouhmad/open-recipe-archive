## Ingredients

- braised oxtail
- turnips
- cooking liquid

## Directions

1. Cook the oxtail braised
2. Have turnips cut into small batons
3. Pour the turnips over the oxtail so it acquires the taste of turnip
4. Put the trimmings of the turnip batons in the cooking liquid
5. If you have no sauce for the turnips, use the cooking liquid to cook them
