## Ingredients

- Three eggs
- Half a spoonful of flour
- Sugar the size of a walnut
- A little salt
- Three portions of milk

## Directions

1. Mix the three eggs with half a spoonful of flour, sugar the size of a walnut, and a little salt.
2. Add three portions of milk and mix all together.
3. Put into the serving dish.
4. Cook over heat for a quarter-hour.
5. Pass a hot shovel over them and serve as soon as they are cooked.
