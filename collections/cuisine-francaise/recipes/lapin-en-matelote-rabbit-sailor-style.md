## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- one small spoonful of flour
- butter
- rabbit liver
- one glass of red wine
- two glasses of water
- bouillon
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- one garlic clove
- two cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a dozen small onions
- one eel cut into sections (optional)
- whole capers
- one anchovy
- croutons fried in butter

## Directions

1. Cut a rabbit into pieces
2. Make a small roux with one small spoonful of flour and a piece of butter
3. Put the rabbit pieces with the liver in it, pass them over the fire and moisten with one glass of red wine, two glasses of water and bouillon
4. Add a bouquet of parsley, chives, one garlic clove, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, salt, coarse pepper
5. Cook over low heat; half an hour later add a dozen blanched small onions
6. If you wish to add an eel cut into sections, add it only when the rabbit is three-quarters cooked
7. Before serving, remove the bouquet, degrease the sauce and add a good handful of whole capers and one chopped anchovy
8. Serve with croutons fried in butter; pour the sauce over all
