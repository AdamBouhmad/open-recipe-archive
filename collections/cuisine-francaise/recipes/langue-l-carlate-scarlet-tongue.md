## Ingredients

- one beef tongue
- two ounces of saltpeter
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- peppercorns
- two large handfuls of salt
- two carrots
- two onions
- three cloves
- two pints of water

## Directions

1. Pound two ounces of saltpeter
2. Rub the beef tongue thoroughly all over with the pounded saltpeter
3. Place the tongue in a terrine with thyme, bay leaf, basil, and peppercorns
4. Dissolve two large handfuls of salt in boiling water
5. When the salt is dissolved and the water is cold, pour it over the tongue
6. Leave the tongue in the brine for five or six days or longer
7. If you have time, soak the tongue for two hours before cooking
8. Blanch the tongue
9. Place it in a braise pan with a quarter of the brine, thyme, bay leaf, basil, peppercorns, two carrots, two onions, three cloves, two pints of water, and salt
10. Simmer gently for two hours
11. Remove from heat and allow to cool in its seasoning
