## Ingredients

- six fat goose thighs
- salt
- coarse pepper
- lard, finely chopped
- lard for barding
- three carrots
- four onions
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- two cloves
- one spoonful of bouillon

## Directions

1. Lift six fat goose thighs
2. Debone them to the interior joint
3. Season with salt and coarse pepper
4. Place some chopped lard where the bone was
5. Reassemble the meat and sew them, giving them a nice shape
6. Place lard rinds in a casserole
7. Place the thighs there and cover them with lard rinds
8. Add three carrots, four onions, two bay leaves, a little thyme, two cloves, and one spoonful of bouillon
9. Braise for two hours
10. At service time, drain them and remove the thread
11. Dress them on your dish and mask them with your puree; if you wish, you can put a puree of lentils, or of green peas, of onions, and Robert sauce, tomato sauce
