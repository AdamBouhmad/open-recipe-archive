## Ingredients

- lamprey
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Scald the lamprey as explained in the article on tench, then cut it into sections and flour it before cooking.
2. Cook on the grill like other fish and serve with a sauce of capers or a bourgeoise remoulade sauce.
3. For the sauce, put oil, vinegar, salt, coarse pepper, and mustard in a casserole, all mixed together, and serve in a sauceboat.
