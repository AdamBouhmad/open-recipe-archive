## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Good bouillon, fat or lean as needed

## Directions

1. Wash and pick through the lentils
2. Cook them with good bouillon, either fat or lean depending on your intended use
3. When cooked, pass through a sieve, moistening with their cooking bouillon
4. Season with good taste
5. Serve as you judge appropriate, either as soup or in a terrine
