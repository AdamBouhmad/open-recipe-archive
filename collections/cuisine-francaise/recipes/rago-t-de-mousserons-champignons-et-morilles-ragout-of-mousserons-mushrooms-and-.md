## Ingredients

- Mousserons or mushrooms or morels
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Flour
- One glass of bouillon
- Half a glass of white wine
- As much jus
- Coulis
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put mousserons in a saucepan with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley and chives
2. Pass them over the fire and add a pinch of flour
3. Moisten with a glass of bouillon, a half-glass of white wine, and as much jus
4. Cook for a good hour
5. Skim the fat and add a little coulis, or if you do not have any, add a little more flour
6. Season with salt and coarse pepper
7. For mushrooms and morels, proceed the same way, except that morels must be well washed and beaten in several waters to remove the sand
