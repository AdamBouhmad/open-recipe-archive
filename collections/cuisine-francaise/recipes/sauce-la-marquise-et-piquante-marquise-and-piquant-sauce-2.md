## Ingredients

- Two good pinches of fine bread crumbs
- Good butter the size of a small coin
- One spoonful of fine oil
- Minced shallot
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Verjuice, sufficient to thin the sauce

## Directions

1. Mix in a saucepan two good pinches of fine bread crumbs, butter the size of a small coin, one spoonful of fine oil, minced shallot, salt, coarse pepper, and verjuice sufficient to thin the sauce
2. Heat the sauce and thicken it by stirring with a spoon
