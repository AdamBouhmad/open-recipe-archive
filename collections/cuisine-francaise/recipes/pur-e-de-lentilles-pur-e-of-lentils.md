## Ingredients

- one and a half litron of queen lentils or other varieties
- vegetables for cooking
- lard
- beef
- three or four degreasing spoonfuls of espagnole
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Have one and a half litron of queen lentils or other varieties
2. Prepare them as the peas described above
3. When they are cooked, remove the vegetables, lard and beef
4. Pass them through a sieve
5. Once you have expressed the bouillon, place your purée in a pot with three or four degreasing spoonfuls of espagnole
6. Pour more liquid into this purée than into pea purée, as it must boil for a long time so it becomes red
7. Be careful to skim and degrease it well
8. Be careful it is not too salty, because in reducing it would become acrid
9. After it is reduced, place it in another pot for serving at the moment and for the indicated uses
