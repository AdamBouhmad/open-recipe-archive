## Ingredients

- Twelve chicken thighs from fine fat chickens
- Eight truffles
- Half a pound of lard
- Three spoonfuls of good oil
- Four spices
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Lard for barding
- Truffle scraps

## Directions

1. Prepare twelve chicken thighs as you would for a bigarrure.
2. Chop eight peeled truffles.
3. Grate half a pound of lard and put it in a casserole with three spoonfuls of good oil.
4. Add your chopped truffles, a little four spices, a little salt and coarse pepper.
5. Pass them over heat briefly, then let them cool.
6. Stuff your chicken thighs with this mixture.
7. Sew them with needle and thread so the truffles do not escape.
8. To give them a pleasant shape, insert a leg bone through the thin end of the thigh.
9. Put lards of bacon in a casserole, arrange your thighs on top, and cover them with lard.
10. Add your truffle scraps and cook covered.
11. Three-quarters of an hour before serving, place your thighs over heat.
12. At that moment, drain them.
13. Remove the thread and arrange them in a crown shape.
14. Place a sauté of truffles in the center.
