## Ingredients

- Onions
- Pork fat, pounded
- Bread crumbs, dried in milk
- Meat from a roasted fowl, cooked
- Milk
- One chopine of good cream
- Six egg yolks
- Salt
- Spices
- Intestines, cleaned

## Directions

1. Cut onions into very small dice; cook them as the previous ones; add pounded pork fat that you mix with your onions
2. Add bread crumbs that you have made by drying them in milk; take the meat from a roasted fowl that you chop, and that you pound with your bread crumbs, using equal amounts of bread crumbs, fowl, and fat
3. Add one chopine of good cream; thin your fowl mixture; add six egg yolks, salt, and your spices; mix everything together
4. Pour it into your intestines: cook them in milk cut with water; do not let it boil, to avoid your boudin bursting
5. When it is cold, prick it; grill it on an oiled sheet of paper that you place on the grill over a gentle fire: serve this boudin as an appetizer
6. With this method of making boudin, you may use hare, young rabbits, pheasants, partridge, veal, or pork
