## Ingredients

- Soles
- Butter
- Parsley
- Shallots
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg
- Fine herbs
- White wine
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the soles and dry them
2. Make an incision with the knife blade on the back ridge on the dark side
3. Melt butter on a plate
4. Add chopped parsley, finely chopped shallots, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
5. Put your soles on the plate and sprinkle fine herbs, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg on top
6. Add a glass of good white wine
7. Mask them with bread crumbs, sprinkled with drops of butter
8. A quarter hour before serving, put your soles in the oven or on a gentle heat with a very hot country tile to cover them
9. If you do not use an oven, use bread crumbs instead of bread
