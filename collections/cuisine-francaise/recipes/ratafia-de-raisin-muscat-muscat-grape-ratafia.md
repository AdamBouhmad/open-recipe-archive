## Ingredients

- Ripe muscat grapes
- Brandy, equal measure to the juice
- Sugar, one quarter pound per pint
- Mace
- Cinnamon
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Take ripe muscat grapes and put them in a terrine to crush them and extract as much juice as you can
2. Pass it through a sieve
3. Measure this juice to put with as much brandy, one quarter pound of sugar per pint, according to the quantity you have
4. Add to it in proportion a little mace, cinnamon and cloves
5. Put everything together in a well-stoppered jug to infuse five or six days, stirring it every day
6. Then pass it through a filter
7. When it is clear, put it in well-stoppered bottles
