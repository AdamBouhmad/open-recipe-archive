## Ingredients

- Young rabbits
- A half glass of white wine
- Broth
- A bouquet garni
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Beaten egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Fat

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbits into pieces and cook them with a half glass of white wine, broth, a bouquet garni, salt, and coarse pepper
2. When they are cooked, reduce the sauce so that it attaches to the meat
3. Let cool
4. Thread them onto small skewers
5. Dip them in beaten egg
6. Bread them and dip them in fat again to bread them a second time
7. Grill them until they are a beautiful color
8. Serve dry with the skewers.
