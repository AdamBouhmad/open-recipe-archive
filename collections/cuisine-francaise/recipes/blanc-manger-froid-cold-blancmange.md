## Ingredients

- 1 pound of blanched almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- cream to fill 12 small pots
- 6 ounces of sugar
- 1.5 sticks of isinglass
- water

## Directions

1. Pound 1 pound of blanched almonds with 8 bitter almonds until very fine
2. Boil cream to fill 12 small pots with 6 ounces of sugar or less if pots are small
3. Mix the almonds with the warm cream
4. Take a fine cloth and pass the mixture through it by twisting only
5. Dissolve 1.5 sticks of isinglass that has been beaten well and broken into small pieces in half a setier of water
6. Let simmer for two hours
7. Pass through cheesecloth and pour with the warm almond preparation
8. Fill the small pots and place on ice or in a cold place if you have time
9. When frozen, serve
