## Ingredients

- one large chicken or two, depending on the size of your dish
- chicken liver
- lard
- paper
- fine oil
- bread croutons
- two onions
- parsley
- chives
- shallot
- poultry liver
- one glass of white wine
- one spoonful of coulis
- salt
- crushed pepper

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing from the chicken liver and cook the chickens on the spit wrapped in lard and paper
2. When cooked, prepare the sauce by heating fine oil and frying two bread croutons quickly
3. Drain the croutons and in the same oil fry two onions cut into strips
4. When the onions are three-quarters cooked, add chopped parsley, chives, shallot, and poultry liver
5. Moisten with one glass of white wine and one spoonful of coulis
6. Degrease the sauce and season with salt and crushed pepper
7. Cook on low heat for a quarter hour
8. Serve the chickens and croutons on the side
