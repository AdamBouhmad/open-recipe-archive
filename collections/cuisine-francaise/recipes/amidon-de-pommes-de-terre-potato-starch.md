## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water

## Directions

1. Have a fine hair sieve
2. Wash the potatoes well
3. Grate them on your sieve placed over a terrine
4. Pour a large quantity of water over your grated potatoes
5. Let the water rest: one hour later, pour it out, and you will find the starch at the bottom of the vessel
6. Dry it if you wish
