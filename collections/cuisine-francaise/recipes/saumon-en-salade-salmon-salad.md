## Ingredients

- Cooked salmon
- Four tablespoons of good vinegar
- Two spoonfuls of melted aspic
- Ten spoonfuls of oil
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Chopped ravigote
- Lettuce hearts
- Croutons
- Gherkins
- Capers
- Anchovies

## Directions

1. When your salmon is cooked, separate it into pieces
2. Put it in your salad sauce
3. Pour into a saucepan four tablespoons of good vinegar, two spoonfuls of melted aspic, ten spoonfuls of oil, salt, coarse pepper, and chopped ravigote
4. Toss your salmon in this sauce and arrange it on your plate
5. Pour your sauce over it
6. Put lettuce hearts cut in quarters around it
7. Decorate your salad with croutons, gherkins, capers, and anchovies
