## Ingredients

- six pigeons
- cock crests and kidneys
- foie gras
- truffles

## Directions

1. Take six pigeons which you prepare and cook like the preceding ones
2. At the moment of serving, drain them and arrange around the edge of the plate
3. Put in the middle of your pigeons a ragout of cock crests and kidneys, foie gras and truffles (see Mixed Ragout)
