## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- mushrooms
- butter
- lemon juice
- espagnole sauce
- consommé
- cooking liquid from the tongue

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue as for the gherkin version
2. Turn the mushrooms into a decorative shape
3. Sauté them in butter and lemon juice
4. Add four spoons of espagnole and three or four spoonfuls of consommé
5. Reduce the sauce in which the mushrooms are cooking
6. Cut the tongue in half and place it on your plate
7. Pour the mushroom ragout on top; or moisten the mushrooms with the cooking liquid from the tongue
8. Pass it through a silk sieve and take care to degrease it
9. If the sauce is thin, reduce it
10. If it were too salty, add a splash of lemon juice at the time of serving
