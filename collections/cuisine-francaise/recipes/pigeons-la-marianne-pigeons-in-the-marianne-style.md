## Ingredients

- Three pigeons
- Two spoonfuls of lard
- One glass of bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Two bay leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare three pigeons as for the previous recipes
2. Flatten them slightly with the meat cleaver
3. Put them in a casserole with two spoonfuls of lard, one glass of bouillon, salt, coarse pepper, and two bay leaves
4. Cook them over hot cinders so they boil very gently
5. When they yield to finger pressure, dress them in the platter you will serve
