## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked
- good broth
- a handful of fennel

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked potatoes into slices like coins
2. Bring good broth to a boil in which you put a handful of bundled fennel and let everything boil for a quarter hour
3. Pour your broth over the potatoes that you will have put in your soup tureen
4. Be sure there are enough potatoes to take the place of bread
