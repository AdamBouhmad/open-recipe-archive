## Ingredients

- 1 pound of almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- cream with one quarter more
- sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch 1 pound of almonds with 8 bitter almonds
2. Pound them very finely
3. Arrange them in a casserole
4. Fill another casserole with cream increased by one quarter
5. Boil with sugar
6. Mix the almonds with the boiling cream
7. Pass everything through fine cheesecloth by straining
8. A quarter hour before serving, put the preparation on the fire and turn it like a porridge
9. Reduce it by continuing to stir until it binds and coats a spoon
10. Pour into a serving casserole
