## Ingredients

- three chopines of milk
- small strip of green lemon peel
- pinch of coriander
- small piece of cinnamon
- salt, two or three grains
- about three ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil three chopines of milk with a small strip of green lemon peel, a pinch of coriander, a small piece of cinnamon, two or three grains of salt, and about three ounces of sugar
2. Allow it to boil and reduce to half
3. Pass it through a sieve
4. Finish as for the previous milk soup
