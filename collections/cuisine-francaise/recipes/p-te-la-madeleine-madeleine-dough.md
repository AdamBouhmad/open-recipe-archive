## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 1 pound of sugar
- 1/2 pound of butter
- orange blossom water or lemon zest, finely chopped
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of flour, 1 pound of sugar, and 1/2 pound of butter (warmed) in a casserole
2. Add a little orange blossom water or a little finely chopped lemon zest
3. Add 6 eggs
4. Mix everything together
5. Add more eggs if your dough is too thick
6. Butter a mold or several small ones and arrange the dough in it
7. Bake in a gentle oven
