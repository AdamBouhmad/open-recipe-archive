## Ingredients

- Blancmange (as prepared for small pots)
- Fish glue (at least ten sticks, according to the size of the mold)
- Hart's horn or calf's feet jelly (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the blancmange as you would for small pots
2. Fill the mold you wish to unmold with it
3. Add fish glue in proportion; at least ten sticks are needed according to the size of the mold
4. Place it on ice and let it freeze well so it can hold together when turned out of the mold
5. You may make it with hart's horn or calf's feet jelly instead
