## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls of powdered sugar
- a little orange flower powder
- 1 pint of milk
- 6 ounces of sugar
- a little orange flower powder or other flavoring

## Directions

1. Break ten eggs and separate the whites and yolks
2. Beat the whites as you would for a biscuit; when they are well set, add two spoonfuls of powdered sugar and a little orange flower powder
3. Pour a pint of milk into a saucepan with six ounces of sugar and a little orange flower powder or other flavoring
4. When the milk boils, take a spoonful of the beaten whites and put it in the milk; poach the whites
5. Let them drain on a sieve; when all are poached, remove half the milk or only a quarter of it
6. Blend the yolks and put them in the milk; stir with a wooden spoon
7. When you see them binding together, remove from heat and strain through cheesecloth into another saucepan
8. Arrange the eggs on the dish and mask them with your sauce
