## Ingredients

- sheep's tongues cooked in water
- half a septier of milk
- piece of butter
- parsley
- chives
- one clove of garlic
- two shallots
- two cloves
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- roots and vegetables
- half a bay leaf
- thyme
- basilic
- half a clove of garlic
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooking the tongues in water, peel and split them in two without separating them
2. Put them to take flavor by boiling for half an hour with half a septier of milk, a piece of butter, parsley, chives, one whole clove of garlic, two whole shallots, two cloves, salt, and pepper
3. Remove the fine herbs and take the fattiest part of the cooking liquid to dip the tongues in it, then bread them with breadcrumbs
4. Grill them to a beautiful color
5. Prepare the piquant sauce: put in a saucepan vegetable scraps, onions, half a bay leaf, thyme, basilic, half a clove of garlic, a piece of butter
6. Pass them on the fire until they begin to take on a little color
7. Add a pinch of flour moistened with a little bouillon, one spoonful of vinegar, salt, and pepper
8. Bring the sauce to boil for a quarter hour, degrease it and pass through a sieve
9. Serve the grilled tongues with this piquant sauce
