## Ingredients

- 6 large reinette apples
- 1 large glass of water
- juice of half a lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the apples in half, remove the skin and seeds, and immediately place them in fresh water
2. Cook with a large glass of water, the juice of half a lemon, and a piece of sugar
3. When the apples are cooked, arrange them in a serving dish
4. Reduce the syrup until it is sticky to the touch
5. Pour the syrup over the apples
