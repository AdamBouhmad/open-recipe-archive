## Ingredients

- Pheasant thighs
- Small lard
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Aromatic herbs
- Lard bacon
- Carrots
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- Veal
- Bouillon
- Lentil purée
- Glazed croutons

## Directions

1. After removing the fillets from a pheasant, employ the thighs; remove them from the loins; take as much skin as possible; debone them to the joint
2. Put inside a piece of pounded small lard about the size of half an egg, seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, a little pounded aromatic herbs
3. Gather the meat together with a needle and thread; sew them so your thighs form a round at the thick end
4. Put lard bacon in a casserole; place your thighs in it; put lard bacon on top; add two carrots, four onions cut in slices, two bay leaves, two cloves, some slices of veal, one pot spoon full of bouillon
5. Simmer your thighs for an hour and a half
6. At serving time, drain them; unbind them, and arrange them in crowns, one thigh, one glazed crouton approximately the size of the thigh; put your purée in the middle
7. If you wish to arrange them otherwise, mask them with your purée
