## Ingredients

- sirloin fillet
- cucumbers
- roux
- cooking liquid from the fillet
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Trim your sirloin fillet
2. Cook it as you would cook it braised
3. Drain it and glaze it
4. Place cucumbers underneath
5. If you do not have sauce for your cucumbers, use the cooking liquid from your sirloin
6. Make a light roux which you will moisten with the juice in which your fillet has cooked
7. Pass it through a silk sieve; take care to make your sauce somewhat thin so you can reduce it
8. Skim the fat and pass it through cheesecloth and pour it under your fillet, or place your cucumbers to cook in it; make sure your sauce is well salted and add a little coarse pepper
