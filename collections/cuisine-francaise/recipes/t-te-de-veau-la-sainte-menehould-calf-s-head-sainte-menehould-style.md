## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- water
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- two garlic cloves
- three cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- one egg
- two pinches of nutmeg
- three egg yolks
- two tablespoons of vinegar
- half a glass of bouillon
- breadcrumbs
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the jaws and cut the muzzle near the eyes
2. Place it in a pot with water and blanch like a pot-au-feu
3. Add a bouquet of parsley, chives, two garlic cloves, three cloves, a bay leaf, thyme and basil
4. Season with salt and pepper and boil until tender
5. Remove the bones that are on the brain, dress it on the serving platter
6. Make a sauce by placing butter and a piece of meat in a small pot with a beaten egg, two pinches of nutmeg, salt and pepper, three egg yolks and two tablespoons of vinegar
7. Blend the egg together and add half a glass of bouillon
8. Thicken the sauce on the fire so it is nicely thick
9. Spread sauce all over the head, coat with breadcrumbs
10. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs with a little butter
11. Brown in the oven or under an oven cover elevated enough so it does not touch the breadcrumbs
12. When it is a beautiful golden color, tilt the plat to drain the fat, wipe the edges
13. Serve a piquant sauce in the bottom
