## Ingredients

- larks
- lard
- butter
- parsley
- scallions
- mushrooms
- garlic

## Directions

1. Clean the larks and remove the gizzard
2. Put the rest with grated lard in the bottom of the pie
3. Place the larks on top after removing their feet and head and having passed them in a saucepan over the fire with a little good butter, parsley, scallions, mushrooms and a point of garlic, all chopped
4. Let cool
5. Finish the pie as will be explained in the general article on pies
