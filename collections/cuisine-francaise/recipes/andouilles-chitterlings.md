## Ingredients

- The meatiest intestines of pork
- Salt
- Pepper
- Pounded aromatic herbs
- Spices
- Intestines for stuffing
- Broth
- Vegetable roots
- Parsley and chives
- Thyme
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Have the meatiest intestines of pork cleaned and washed; when they are very clean, let them disgorge for twelve hours; drain them; dry them well; place them in a terrine
2. Season them with salt, pepper, pounded aromatic herbs, spices; leave them with this seasoning for twelve hours
3. Stuff them into intestines that you tie at the end, and place them in the bottom of the salt cellar
4. When you wish to eat them, cook them in broth with vegetable roots, a bouquet of parsley and chives, a little thyme, bay leaf; let them cool in their cooking liquid
5. Slice them and place them on the grill; serve them as an appetizer
