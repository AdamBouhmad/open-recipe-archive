## Ingredients

- 2 pints brandy
- 1 good handful juniper berries
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 1 chopine water

## Directions

1. Put 2 pints of brandy in a jug with 1 good handful of juniper berries
2. Boil 1.5 pounds of sugar with 1 chopine of water until it is well skimmed and clear
3. Put the sugar mixture in the jug with the brandy and juniper
4. Seal the jug well and keep it in a warm place for about five weeks
5. Before straining it through a filter or cloth, wait until it is clear
6. When clear, put it in bottles and seal them well
7. This liqueur is stomachic and good when kept for a long time
8. This makes three pints
