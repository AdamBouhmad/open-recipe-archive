## Ingredients

- Pheasant, prepared as for stewed pheasant
- Lard strips (bardes)
- One pound of lardons
- One medium-sized cervelas sausage
- Four carrots
- Four onions
- Two cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Several slices of veal
- Cabbages, blanched
- Bouillon
- Coarse pepper
- Game essence sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare your pheasant as for the preceding recipe
2. Place lard strips in a saucepan with your pheasant, one pound of lardons, one medium cervelas sausage, four carrots, four onions, two cloves, two bay leaves, and several slices of veal
3. Blanch your cabbages; tie and cook them with your pheasant
4. Moisten with bouillon; add a little coarse pepper, and no salt because of the bouillon and lard
5. Simmer for two hours
6. At the moment of serving, drain your cabbages
7. Place your pheasant in the middle of your dish, the cabbages, your lardons, and your cervelas sausage around it
8. Pour a game-flavored sauce over and around it
