## Ingredients

- Oxtail
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley, minced
- Chives, minced
- Shallots, minced
- Garlic, minced
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into three pieces: cut it first in the middle, and split the thick end in two with the cleaver
2. Cook it in the pot like a piece of beef
3. When cooked, let it cool
4. Marinate it for one hour with a bit of oil, salt, coarse pepper, minced parsley, chives, two minced shallots, and a pinch of minced garlic
5. Make the marinade adhere to the oxtail by coating it with breadcrumbs
6. Grill it to a nice color, basting it with the rest of its marinade while it is on the fire
7. Serve without sauce
