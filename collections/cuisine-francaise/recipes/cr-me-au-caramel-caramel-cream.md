## Ingredients

- one chopine of milk
- one half-setier of cream
- a small piece of cinnamon
- a good pinch of coriander
- the zest of green lemon
- one quarteron of sugar
- one half-glass of water
- five egg yolks
- a pinch of flour

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one chopine of milk, one half-setier of cream, with a small piece of cinnamon, a good pinch of coriander, the zest of green lemon, and bring to boil for a quarter-hour
2. Remove from the fire, and put in a small office pan one quarteron of sugar with one half-glass of water
3. Make it boil on a stove until it reaches caramel, that is to say, the color of dark cinnamon
4. Remove from the fire and add the cream to it; return to the fire until the sugar is dissolved with the cream
5. Then dilute five egg yolks with a pinch of flour, add the cream to it, pass it through a sieve to cook it in a bain-marie as the previous ones
