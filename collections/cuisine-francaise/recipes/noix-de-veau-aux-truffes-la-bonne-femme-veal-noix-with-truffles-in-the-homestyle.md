## Ingredients

- three veal noix (round cuts)
- lard for larding
- truffles
- salt
- parsley
- chives
- good bouillon
- coulis

## Directions

1. Take three veal noix which you trim by lightly removing the meat that prevents a good appearance.
2. You must lard them throughout with pieces of lard and truffles, the two combined together with fine salt, parsley, chives, and chopped truffles.
3. Cook them with good bouillon.
4. When they are cooked and the sauce is well degreased, put in two spoonfuls of coulis.
5. Reduce the sauce so that it is neither too short nor too long, and serve it over the veal noix.
