## Ingredients

- Suckling pig, scalded and boned
- Good meat farce seasoned to taste
- Ham lardons
- Lard lardons
- Truffle lardons
- Hard egg yolks
- Lard barding
- Cheesecloth
- Half bouillon and half white wine
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Turnips
- Onions
- Large bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Shallots
- Garlic
- Cloves
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Basil

## Directions

1. Once well scalded, bone the suckling pig completely and extend it on a white cloth
2. Spread a good meat farce seasoned to taste over it, approximately the thickness of a large coin
3. On this farce place a row of ham lardons, then a row of lard, then a row of truffles, then a row of hard egg yolks
4. Cover all these lardons with a bit more farce
5. Roll up the suckling pig, being careful not to disturb the lardons
6. Wrap it in lard barding and cheesecloth
7. Tie it tightly with string
8. Cook for three hours with half bouillon and half white wine, salt, coarse pepper, turnips, onions, a large bouquet of parsley, chives, shallots, garlic, cloves, thyme, bay leaf, and basil
9. Allow it to cool in its cooking liquid
10. Serve cold as an entremets
