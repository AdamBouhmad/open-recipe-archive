## Ingredients

- Pike
- Court-bouillon
- Portuguese sauce

## Directions

1. Cook your fish in court-bouillon
2. When it is cooked, carefully remove the scales
3. Put it hot on your platter and mask it with Portuguese sauce; you may also use butter sauce, tomato sauce, or Indian sauce depending on taste
