## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- chives
- cream
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Put a good piece of butter in a casserole with one tablespoon of flour, salt, coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg, parsley, and well-chopped chives
2. Mix everything together
3. Add one glass of cream
4. Place the sauce on the fire and stir until it boils
5. Cut the potatoes into slices and put them in the sauce
6. Serve hot
