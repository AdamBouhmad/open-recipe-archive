## Ingredients

- very white mushrooms
- water
- juice of a lemon
- a piece of butter
- six spoonfuls of large velouté
- as much consommé
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Have very white mushrooms
2. Cut off the earthy part of the stem
3. Wash them
4. Put a little water in a pot and squeeze in the juice of a lemon
5. Sauté them inside, drain them, mince them as finely as possible
6. Place them in a white cloth and press them well
7. Take a piece of butter and place it in a pot
8. Pour in lemon juice
9. Add your minced mushrooms
10. Cook them until your butter turns to oil
11. Add six spoonfuls of large velouté and as much consommé
12. Reduce until your purée is thick enough
13. Add a little coarse pepper
14. Transfer your purée to another pot
15. In the absence of velouté, use a full spoon of flour and pour in bouillon
