## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- flour
- white wine
- lean bouillon
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Take fifty oysters in a basket that you put in hot water; when it is ready to boil, you remove it to put them in fresh water, drain them, and then take only the tender parts.
2. If you take all of it, let the hard part be chopped very fine.
3. Put the rest with it to chop as well.
4. If you wish to mix with carp meat, that will increase your hash and give it good taste.
5. Put in a pot a good piece of butter, with parsley, chive, chopped mushrooms, pass them over the fire and add a pinch of flour; moisten them afterwards with half a setier of white wine and as much lean bouillon.
6. Put in to cook your hash, until there is no more sauce, and season it to good taste.
7. When you are ready to serve, add a liaison of three egg yolks and cream; serve for an entrée.
