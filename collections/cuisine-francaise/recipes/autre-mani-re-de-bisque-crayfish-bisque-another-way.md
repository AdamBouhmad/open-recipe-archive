## Ingredients

- crayfish, fifty
- butter, a quarter pound and a half
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- bread crumb, tender, three times as large as an egg
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. Have fifty crayfish; when they are well washed, you must pound them raw; when they are well ground, have a quarter pound and a half of butter that you will put in a saucepan with your crayfish; use salt, coarse pepper, a little nutmeg, a piece of tender bread three times as large as an egg: put your crayfish on a fairly hot fire so that they can cook for half an hour; then you will pass your crayfish in purée through a fine sieve; when your purée is passed, you will put it in a saucepan on gentle heat; try that it does not boil, and that it is not too thick: you then prepare your crusts as for the previous soup. This soup is made with fat as well as lean. (See Lean Broth.)
