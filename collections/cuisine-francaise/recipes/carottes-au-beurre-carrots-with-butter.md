## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 carrots of the same size
- water
- salt
- butter, the size of a walnut
- coarse pepper
- grated nutmeg
- velouté or water
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Peel 10 or 12 carrots of the same size
2. Cut them into rounds, one and a half lines thick
3. Blanch them in water with salt and a walnut-sized piece of butter
4. When they are cooked but still firm, drain them in a colander
5. Put them in a casserole with a good piece of butter, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
6. Sauté them on the fire
7. Pour a spoonful of velouté or water only so the butter does not turn to oil
8. Do not let them boil; they should only be very hot
9. You can add fine herbs
