## Ingredients

- Salmon steak
- Oil
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter sauce
- Capers

## Directions

1. Have a salmon steak; marinate it with oil, parsley, chives, salt, and pepper.
2. If the steak is thick, it will take an hour to cook.
3. Arrange it on your plate; add a butter sauce on top with capers that you sprinkle over the salmon.
