## Ingredients

- Shallot
- A little garlic
- One spoonful of bouillon
- Two spoonfuls of coulis
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Put minced shallot and a little garlic in a saucepan
2. Crush the garlic and shallot together
3. Add one spoonful of bouillon to dilute them
4. Add two spoonfuls of coulis, salt, and pepper
5. Make a good bouillon with this sauce
6. Pass it through a sieve into another saucepan
7. Before serving, heat it for a moment
