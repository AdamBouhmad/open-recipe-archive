## Ingredients

- good bouillon, passed through silk sieve
- vermicelli from Italy

## Directions

1. Ensure your vermicelli does not smell old and has no off taste; Italian vermicelli is the best
2. Have good bouillon that you have passed through a silk sieve, sufficient for your soup
3. Bring it to a boil; when it boils, add your vermicelli so that it does not clump together
4. When it has boiled for a good half hour, remove it from the fire so it does not become too mushy and your soup is clear
5. A half pound suffices for eight or ten persons; it should not be thick
