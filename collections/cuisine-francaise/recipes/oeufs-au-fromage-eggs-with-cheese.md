## Ingredients

- a quarter pound of grated Gruyère cheese
- butter the size of half an egg
- parsley
- chives
- a little nutmeg
- half a glass of white wine
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a quarter pound of grated Gruyère cheese, butter the size of half an egg, chopped parsley, chopped chives, a little nutmeg, and half a glass of white wine
2. Boil over a small fire, stirring until the cheese is melted
3. Then add six eggs to scramble and cook over a small fire
4. Serve garnished with breadcrumbs on the edges of the plate
