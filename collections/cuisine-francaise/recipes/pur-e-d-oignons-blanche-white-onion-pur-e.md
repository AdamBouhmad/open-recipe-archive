## Ingredients

- onions (prepared as for the previous purée)
- four tablespoons of velouté
- one pint of cream
- a piece of sugar the size of a walnut
- flour (if no velouté available)
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare your onion as for the previous purée
2. Cook your onion over a gentle fire so it does not take color
3. When it is well melted, add four tablespoons of velouté and one pint of cream, and a piece of sugar the size of a walnut
4. Reduce your purée over high heat while stirring constantly
5. When it is thickened, pass it through cheesecloth
6. Ensure it has good seasoning
7. If you have no velouté, put one spoonful of flour, cream, salt, and pepper
8. Finish your purée as above
9. Put it in a bain-marie or over a gentle fire so it does not boil
