## Ingredients

- verjuice, not quite ripe
- fine sugar, equal weight to the marmalade

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the verjuice and place it in water that is on the fire ready to boil
2. When the verjuice begins to pale and has risen to the surface of the water, add a little fresh water and remove from the fire
3. Cover until it has returned to green color, and if it does not become green enough, leave it in the same water and heat it until it does
4. Drain and pass through a sieve, pressing firmly with a wooden spoon
5. Weigh the marmalade and put it in a pan with an equal weight of fine sugar
6. Boil together until, when you dip a finger in the marmalade and press it against another finger, the two fingers stick together without much resistance
7. Put into jars after it has cooled slightly
