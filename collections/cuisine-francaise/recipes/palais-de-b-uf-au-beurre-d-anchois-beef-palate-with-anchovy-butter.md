## Ingredients

- cooked beef palate
- Spanish sauce
- anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Your palates cooked, drain and cut them into not very large rounds
2. Reduce a Spanish sauce
3. When your sauce is ready, pass it through a sieve
4. Let your sauce be boiling at the moment of serving
5. Put in an amount of anchovy butter the size of half an egg
6. Be careful to stir it well in your sauce
7. Put your palates in and toss them
8. Do not put your ragout on the fire so that your sauce does not boil
9. However serve very hot
