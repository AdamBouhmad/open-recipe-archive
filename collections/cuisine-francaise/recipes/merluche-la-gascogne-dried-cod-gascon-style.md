## Ingredients

- white dried cod
- river water
- fine oil
- good butter

## Directions

1. Before soaking, beat the dried cod well all over with a hammer to tenderize it
2. Soak for several days, changing the water
3. Cook briefly with river water
4. Remove and cut into pieces by flakes
5. Put the dried cod in a saucepan with fine oil and an equal amount of good butter
6. Serve with Gascon sauce
