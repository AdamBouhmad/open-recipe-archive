## Ingredients

- wings, scalded and plucked
- lard strips
- two spoonfuls of oil
- one glass of white wine
- one glass of good bouillon
- parsley, chives, two cloves of garlic, thyme, bay leaf, basil, two cloves, salt, coarse pepper, two pinches of coriander
- coulis

## Directions

1. Place wings in a casserole over lard strips with oil, white wine, bouillon, and herbs
2. Cook until done
3. Pass the sauce through a sieve and degrease it
4. Add a little coulis to bind the sauce
5. Wipe the wings with a white cloth
6. Serve the sauce over the wings
