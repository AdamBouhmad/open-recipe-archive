## Ingredients

- five or six well-ripe peaches
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Take five or six beautiful ripe peaches, peel them properly, remove the pits and cut them into slices, to arrange in the compote dish
2. Serve by putting fine sugar underneath and over the peaches
