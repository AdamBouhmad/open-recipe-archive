## Ingredients

- Young, tender lamb
- Lamb leg meat
- Salt and coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Take a young, tender lamb, except for the backbone
2. Remove the bones from the shoulders and ribs
3. Fill it with tender leg meat that you season with salt and coarse pepper
4. Return it to its original shape
5. Pass the spit from one end to the other with skewers and string to secure the meat
6. Make sure it has an even color
7. You can prepare this large piece in an oven
