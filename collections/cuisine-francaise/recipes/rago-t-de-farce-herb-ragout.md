## Ingredients

- Sorrel
- Lettuce
- Chervil
- Parsley
- Chives
- Purslane
- Good butter
- Flour
- Jus
- Coulis
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolks
- Cream or milk

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole sorrel, lettuce, chervil, parsley, chives, and purslane, all well washed, chopped and well pressed, with a good piece of butter
2. Pass them over a good fire until there is no more water
3. Add a good pinch of flour, moisten with jus and coulis, salt, and pepper
4. Cook and serve at short sauce
5. If it is meatless, after adding the flour, moisten with lean bouillon, let it boil until the herbs are cooked and no sauce remains
6. Add a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with cream or milk, let it bind over the fire without boiling
