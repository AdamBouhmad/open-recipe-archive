## Ingredients

- Trout fillets
- Finely chopped and washed parsley
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Good butter
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Lift the fillets from the trouts and trim them
2. Remove the skin from the scale side
3. Cut your fillets into small slices at least the size of a six-franc coin
4. Trim your pieces so they are of equal size and thickness
5. Arrange them in your sauté pan
6. Sprinkle with finely chopped and washed parsley, salt, coarse pepper, and grated nutmeg
7. Warm a good piece of butter and pour it over the pieces
8. At the moment of service, put your sauté pan on a hot fire
9. When the sauté is stiffened on one side, turn it
10. Leave it only an instant on the fire
11. Arrange them in a miroton around the plate and place the rest in the middle
12. Add a lean or rich Italian sauce
