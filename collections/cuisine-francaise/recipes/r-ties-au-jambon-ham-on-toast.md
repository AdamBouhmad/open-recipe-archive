## Ingredients

- six or seven slices of bread
- butter
- ham slices
- water
- flour
- bouillon without salt
- vinegar
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut six or seven slices of bread the width of two good fingers
2. Pass them in butter until they are a beautiful golden color
3. Cut as many slices of ham of the same size, which you desalt for one hour in water if it is not fresh
4. Put them in a saucepan over a small fire for one hour
5. When it is cooked, remove it from the saucepan, and put in the same saucepan a pinch of flour to make a small roux, and moisten it with bouillon without salt and a good splash of vinegar
6. Boil for a good quarter hour
7. After having defatted it, pass this sauce through a sieve
8. Dress the ham on the toast with the sauce over it, with a few grains of coarse pepper
