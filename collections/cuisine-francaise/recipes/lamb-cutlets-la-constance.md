## Ingredients

- Eighteen lamb cutlets
- Coarse pepper
- Ice
- Espagnole sauce
- Consommé
- Lamb crests
- Foie gras
- Lamb kidneys
- Béchamel sauce
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut and trim eighteen lamb cutlets as the previous ones
2. Season them only with coarse pepper
3. Place them in your sautoir with a piece of ice the size of an egg, one deglazing spoonful of espagnole, and two similar spoonfuls of consommé
4. Half an hour before serving, place your cutlets on a moderately high heat
5. Stir them carefully and ensure they do not stick
6. When the cooking liquid is reduced so as to form a glaze, at the moment of serving, arrange your cutlets in a crown
7. Ensure they have the glaze in which they cooked
8. In the center of your crown, place a ragout of crests and foie gras
9. Cook your crests in a blanc and your foie gras and kidneys
10. Make a perfect béchamel
11. Add your garnish to it
12. Add a few turned mushrooms
13. Sauté everything well in your béchamel
14. Place your ragout in the middle of your cutlets
15. Ensure your garnishes are well drained and do not bring any water into your béchamel
