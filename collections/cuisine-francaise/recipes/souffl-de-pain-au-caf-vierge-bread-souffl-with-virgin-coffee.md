## Ingredients

- Three half-setiers of cream
- Six ounces of sugar
- Four ounces of coffee, lightly roasted

## Directions

1. Boil three half-setiers of cream
2. Add six ounces of sugar
3. Lightly roast four ounces of coffee
4. Remove from heat and throw it into the cream
5. Cover the saucepan
6. Pass it through a strainer onto the bread crumb
7. Let it cool and make the soufflé as the previous one
