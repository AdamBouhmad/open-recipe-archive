## Ingredients

- merluche (stockfish)
- fine oil
- good butter
- coarse pepper
- garlic
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the whitest stockfish well all over with a hammer to tenderize it
2. Soak it for several days, changing the water
3. Cook it briefly with river water
4. Remove and break into pieces by layers
5. Put the stockfish in a casserole with fine oil and as much good butter
6. Add coarse pepper, a little garlic and salt if it is too mild
7. Place the casserole on a stove, stirring constantly until the butter is combined with the oil
8. Eat it immediately as this sauce turns as it cools
