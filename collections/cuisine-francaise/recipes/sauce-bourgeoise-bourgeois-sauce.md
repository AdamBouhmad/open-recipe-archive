## Ingredients

- one glass of white wine
- as much meat juice
- two good pinches of very fine breadcrumbs
- butter the size of a walnut
- two shallots
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a dash of verjuice

## Directions

1. Boil over low heat for half an hour with wine, meat juice, breadcrumbs, butter, shallots, parsley, and chives
2. Season with salt and coarse pepper
3. Add a dash of verjuice when serving
