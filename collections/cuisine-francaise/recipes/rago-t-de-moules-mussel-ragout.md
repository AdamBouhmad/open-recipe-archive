## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Mushrooms
- Bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- Butter
- Onion in slices
- Root vegetable
- Flour
- White wine
- Mussel water
- Jus
- Coulis
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. For the meatless preparation, refer to page 224
2. For the meat version: put in a casserole a few mushrooms, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, two cloves, a small piece of butter, an onion in slices with a root
3. Pass them over the fire until they are colored, add a pinch of flour, moisten with a glass of white wine, mussel water, and jus
4. Let boil for a good half hour, degrease, add a little coulis; if you have none, use a little flour and jus
5. Reduce to the consistency of a sauce, pass it through a sieve, add the mussels without shells after you have had them open over the fire, a little pepper and salt, if the mussel water has not salted the sauce enough
