## Ingredients

- Beef tail
- Turnips
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook your braised beef tail
2. Have turnips cut into small sticks (see Small Turnips)
3. Pour the turnip sticks over your beef tail so it takes on the taste of turnip
4. Put the surface of your small turnip sticks into the cooking liquid
5. If you do not have sauce for your turnips, use the cooking liquid from your beef tail to cook your turnips
