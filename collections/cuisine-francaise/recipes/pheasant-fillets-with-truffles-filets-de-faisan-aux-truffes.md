## Ingredients

- Pheasant fillets
- Truffles
- Lard
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Spanish sauce
- Game stock

## Directions

1. Remove, trim, and lard your fillets; cook them as previously described
2. Remove the nerve from the tenderloin; cut a thin round of truffle and divide it in half
3. Make six incisions in your small fillet at equal distance, and place your half-circle of truffles in them
4. Once your small fillets are all garnished, give them a half-round shape
5. Place them on a tart pan between two lards of bacon, with a little salt and coarse pepper
6. At serving time, place them on a stove, and a hot country oven on top, or sauté them in butter
7. Drain your large fillets; glaze them; put a sauté of truffles on your plate and your fillets on top; in the middle, arrange your small fillets garnished with truffles
