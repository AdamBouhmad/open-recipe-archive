## Ingredients

- Quail
- Quail livers
- Cooked forcemeat
- Pepper
- Salt
- Aromatic spices
- Velouté
- Lard fat
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Bone ten fresh quail and set the livers aside.
2. Pound all the livers with the back of a knife.
3. Have a cooked forcemeat in which you put your pounded livers with a little coarse pepper, very little salt, and a little pounded aromatics.
4. Mix everything together and fill your quail with this forcemeat.
5. Give them their original shape and secure them with a needle and thread.
6. Pour a spoonful of velouté into the rest of your forcemeat and mix well.
7. Spread this on your plat and place it briefly on the fire.
8. Arrange your ten quail on it and cover them with lard fat.
9. A good half hour before serving, put them on a grate with a hot fire underneath.
10. Cover them with a hot field oven.
11. At the moment of serving, remove the fat and sauce them with an Italian sauce.
12. If you have a large hot oven, they will cook even better.
13. You can also put them au gratin without boning them.
