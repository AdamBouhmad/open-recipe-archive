## Ingredients

- Turkey legs from roasted turkey
- Salt
- Pepper
- Robert sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the legs from a turkey cooked on the spit
2. Score them with your knife
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Place them on the grill over a gentle fire
5. When they are grilled, place Robert sauce underneath
