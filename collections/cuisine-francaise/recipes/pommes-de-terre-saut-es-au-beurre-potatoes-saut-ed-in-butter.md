## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from raw potatoes and turn them to equal size
2. Cut them into round slices the width of a small coin, one and a half lines thick
3. Put a good piece of butter in a casserole and place on a hot fire
4. Add the potatoes and sauté constantly until they are golden
5. Drain them in a strainer
6. Sprinkle with fine salt and arrange on the platter with no other seasoning
