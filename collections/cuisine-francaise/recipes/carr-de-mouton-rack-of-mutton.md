## Ingredients

- rack of mutton
- fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut into cutlets and grill
2. Dip the cutlets in fresh melted butter, salt, pepper, finely chopped parsley, chives, and mushrooms
3. Bread the cutlets with bread crumbs
4. Cook on the grill
5. While cooking, baste with a little butter so they do not become too dry
6. When cooked, serve dry
