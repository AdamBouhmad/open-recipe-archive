## Ingredients

- red mullet or mackerel
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- sauce with capers and anchovies

## Directions

1. For red mullet: scale, clean, wash well, and cut slightly on both sides
2. For mackerel: only clean, wash well, and slit along the back
3. Dry both well in a cloth
4. Soak them for half an hour with salt, pepper, and oil
5. Grill them, basting with the marinade while cooking
6. When cooked, serve with a white sauce with capers and anchovies
