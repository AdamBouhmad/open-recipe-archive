## Ingredients

- perch
- white wine
- capers or other sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the gills and empty the perch, removing only half of its roe.
2. Cook in a court-bouillon with white wine.
3. When cooked, remove the scales, arrange on the serving plate, and cover with a sauce of capers or another sauce as you judge appropriate, or some lean ragout.
4. If serving in fat, the sauce or ragout will make the difference.
