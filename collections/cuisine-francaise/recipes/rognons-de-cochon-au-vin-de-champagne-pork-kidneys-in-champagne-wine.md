## Ingredients

- pork kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- shallots
- flour
- Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Slice your kidneys
2. Put a piece of butter in a casserole that you will place on a brisk fire
3. Put your sliced kidney in it, salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg, parsley and shallot, all chopped very fine
4. Sauté your sliced meat constantly, so that it does not stick
5. When your kidney is stiffened, add a spoonful of flour that you will stir with your sliced meat
6. Pour in a glass of Champagne wine
7. Turn your ragout without letting it boil
8. This ragout can be moistened with water, bouillon, or other white wine; see if it is well seasoned
