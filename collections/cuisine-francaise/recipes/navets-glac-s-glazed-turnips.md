## Ingredients

- 15 or 18 large turnips
- butter
- velouté
- bouillon
- coarse pepper
- sugar, the size of half an egg
- meat juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Peel and turn the turnips into the shape of a pear or other form
2. Put a little butter in a casserole and heat
3. Add the turnips and let them take color
4. Drain them and transfer to a clean casserole
5. Add 4 spoonfuls of velouté, as much bouillon, coarse pepper, sugar the size of half an egg, and a spoonful of meat juice
6. Simmer the turnips until cooked, then reduce the sauce
7. Arrange the turnips and pour the sauce over them
8. Alternatively, once the turnips are browned in butter, add a spoonful of flour, sugar, and bouillon, cook the same way, and serve with the sauce
