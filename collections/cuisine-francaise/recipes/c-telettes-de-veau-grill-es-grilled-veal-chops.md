## Ingredients

- veal rack
- salt
- coarse pepper
- mushrooms
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Cut a veal rack into chops and pare them properly without being too long
2. Marinate for an hour with salt, coarse pepper, mushrooms, parsley, chives, a point of garlic, and a little warm butter
3. Hold the marinade on the chops by coating with breadcrumbs
4. Grill on a small fire, basting with the remaining marinade
5. When cooked to a nice color, serve under a sauce of clear juice, two spoonfuls of verjuice, salt, coarse pepper
6. You can also serve them without sauce
