## Ingredients

- lamb feet
- blanc (white cooking liquid)
- velouté travaillé (worked velouté)
- parsley
- egg
- lemon juice
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- salt

## Directions

1. When your feet are scalded, debone them, that is remove the large bone up to the joint
2. Be careful not to cut or tear the skin
3. Put them to disgorge
4. Blanch them
5. Refresh them
6. Drain them
7. Dry them
8. Singe them
9. Remove the hair found in the cleft of the foot
10. Put them to cook in a blanc
11. Let them simmer for two hours
12. Feel them; if they are not cooked enough, then boil them for another half hour
13. Drain them
14. Trim them
15. Put them in a saucepan
16. Pour in four spoonfuls of worked velouté
17. Add some finely chopped parsley
18. Add to your sauce a liaison of one egg
19. When your sauce is bound, pour it over your lamb feet, stirring them well in your sauce
20. At the moment of serving, add lemon juice, a little coarse pepper, and dress your feet on your platter
21. Check that they are properly seasoned
