## Ingredients

- wings
- one glass of bouillon
- one half-glass of white wine
- bouquet garni
- salt, coarse pepper
- oil
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook wings gently with bouillon, white wine, bouquet garni, salt, and coarse pepper
2. When cooked, reduce all the sauce onto the wings as if glazing them
3. Cool and dip in a little oil
4. Coat with bread crumbs
5. Drizzle with a little oil and grill to a beautiful color
6. Serve without sauce or with a clear well-seasoned sauce
