## Ingredients

- Sheep cutlets
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Glaze
- Bound sauce
- Clear jus
- Small turned roots

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets from the third rib near the neck to the last, about one inch thick
2. Trim them, that is to say remove the skins and bones, except the rib bone
3. Give it a round shape on the side of the fillet, from which you have removed the bones to the end of the fillet rib
4. Trim the end of the bone on the side of the breast so that one can take the cutlet with the fingers without touching the meat
5. Beat the fillet of your cutlet with a cleaver
6. Trim it once more to remove the flesh that exceeds the others
7. Put them in your sauté pan, season them, pour warm butter over them
8. At the time of serving, put your sauté pan over a hot fire
9. Let your cutlets stiffen on one side, turn them over on the other
10. Five minutes are enough to cook them
11. Put your finger on them: if your cutlets are firm, remove them from the fire
12. Arrange them in a crown
13. You may, an instant before they are cooked, put some glaze with your cutlets
14. Put a bound sauce over them in which you put a piece of glaze
15. You may serve a clear jus underneath, and in the middle of your cutlets some small turned roots
