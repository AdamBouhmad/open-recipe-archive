## Ingredients

- one liter of lentils
- broth
- two carrots
- two onions
- two cloves
- crusts

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils with broth, two carrots, two onions, and two cloves
2. When the lentils are cooked, pass them through a sieve
3. Make a clear purée
4. Boil it to degrease it
5. Pour over your crusts a moment before serving so the bread has time to soak
