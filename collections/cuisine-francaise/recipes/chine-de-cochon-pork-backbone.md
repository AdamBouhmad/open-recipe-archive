## Ingredients

- pork backbone
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut your piece very squarely
2. Leave everywhere the thickness of a finger of fat; your square must be well covered
3. Score the top, that is to say the fat that covers it
4. Spit it for two hours
5. Before serving, put it on the fire
6. Serve it as a roast or as an entrée, with a Sauce Robert, a tomato sauce or a piquant sauce
