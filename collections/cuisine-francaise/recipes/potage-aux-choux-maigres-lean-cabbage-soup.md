## Ingredients

- half a cabbage
- a good piece of butter
- water
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Slice half a cabbage; avoid putting in the large white cores
2. Pass it with a good piece of butter according to the quantity of cabbage
3. When it is well passed and beginning to turn blond, moisten your cabbage with water
4. Add salt, coarse pepper, and let your soup boil for three quarters of an hour or an hour, until your cabbage is cooked
5. At the moment of serving, pour your soup over your bread
