## Ingredients

- beef palate cooked in water
- salt
- pepper
- garlic clove
- vinegar
- bouillon
- butter
- flour
- bay leaf
- three cloves

## Directions

1. Peel the cooked palate and cut it the length and width of a finger
2. Make a marinade with salt, pepper, a garlic clove, vinegar, a little bouillon, a small piece of butter mixed with flour, a bay leaf, and three cloves
3. Warm the marinade and place the beef palate in it for two or three hours
4. Remove and drain the palate, dry it, flour it
5. Fry and serve with fried parsley
