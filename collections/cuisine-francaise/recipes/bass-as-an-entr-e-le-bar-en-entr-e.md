## Ingredients

- Bass
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Marinate for half an hour with a little oil, salt, and pepper
2. Cook on the grill
3. Baste from time to time with the oil remaining in the dish
4. When cooked, serve with a sauce of your choice
