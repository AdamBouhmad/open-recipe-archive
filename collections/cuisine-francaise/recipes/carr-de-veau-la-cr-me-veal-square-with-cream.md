## Ingredients

- One veal square
- Salt
- Nutmeg, grated
- False Béchamel

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and nerves that are under the fillet
2. Soak it in milk for twenty-four hours
3. At the moment of putting on the spit, remove it from the milk and wipe it dry
4. Dust it with fine salt in which you have mixed a little grated nutmeg
5. Spit it with a large lardoon and place it on the spit for two and a half hours
6. Before serving, place it near the fire
7. Instead of basting it with the fat from the dripping pan, have a false Béchamel ready and baste it with this
8. At the moment of serving, remove it from the spit
9. Reserve some false Béchamel in which you put a piece of glace about the size of a walnut, a little coarse pepper, a little nutmeg
10. Pour this sauce underneath; it should not be too thick
