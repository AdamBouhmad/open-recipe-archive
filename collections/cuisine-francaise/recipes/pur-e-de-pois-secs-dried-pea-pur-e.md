## Ingredients

- Dried peas
- Lean bouillon or water
- Three carrots
- Three onions (one studded with two cloves)
- Two celery stalks
- A quarter pound of butter
- Salt
- Parsley leaves
- Chive tops
- Sorrel (optional, for color)

## Directions

1. Wash your peas in several waters
2. Put them in a pot and moisten with lean bouillon or water
3. If using water, add three carrots, three onions (one studded with two cloves), two celery stalks, a quarter pound of butter, and salt to taste
4. Cook everything together
5. Once the peas are cooked, remove the vegetables and pass the peas through a sieve
6. For fresh peas: sauté them with a piece of butter, a handful of parsley leaves, and a bit of chive tops with salt
7. Cover the pan and let them sweat for half an hour, stirring from time to time
8. When the peas yield under your finger, pound them in a mortar and pass the purée through a sieve
9. If the purée is not green enough when added to your soup, add a spoonful of sorrel juice
