## Ingredients

- one duck, prepared as for braised duck
- lard for barding
- slices of veal shank
- two carrots
- three onions
- two cloves
- one bay leaf
- thyme
- a bouquet of parsley and chives
- one spoonful of bouillon

## Directions

1. Prepare your duck as the braised duck
2. Place lard rinds in the bottom of a casserole and put the duck on top
3. Add a few slices of veal shank, two carrots, three onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme, a bouquet of parsley and chives
4. Cover your duck with lard rinds and add one spoonful of bouillon
5. If your duck is tender, three quarters of an hour is sufficient to cook it; if it is tough, leave it longer on the fire
6. At service time, drain, unbind and dress it on your dish
7. Mask it, if you wish, with a lentil puree. If your duck is very white and well fleshed, put the puree underneath
8. You can also serve it with turnip or pea puree
