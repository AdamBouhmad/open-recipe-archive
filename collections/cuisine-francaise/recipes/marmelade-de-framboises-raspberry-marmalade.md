## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 2 pounds raspberries

## Directions

1. Cook a pound of sugar the same way as for strawberries
2. When it is at its cooking point, add the raspberries prepared as follows: clean two pounds of raspberries and pass them through a sieve to make marmalade
3. Put this marmalade on the fire to dry it until it is ready to stick to the pan
