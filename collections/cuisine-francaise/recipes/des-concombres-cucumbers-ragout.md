## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- vinegar
- salt
- butter
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- coulis

## Directions

1. Peel and remove the inside, cut into pieces
2. Soak with a half spoonful of vinegar and a little salt for two hours, turning from time to time, by this means it will release its cold water to the stomach, and press it again before putting it in the saucepan
3. Cook with a piece of butter and bouillon, a bouquet garni
4. When cooked, add a little coulis, remove the fat from the ragout before serving
