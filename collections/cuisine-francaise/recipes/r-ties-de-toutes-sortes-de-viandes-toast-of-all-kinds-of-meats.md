## Ingredients

- Leftover meat from the table
- Bread crumbs
- Two raw egg yolks
- Bread crumbs for breading

## Directions

1. Take any leftover meat from the table
2. Cut it into small dice to make a well-bound ragout
3. When cold, add two raw egg yolks
4. Dress the meat on bread crumbs
5. Smooth the top with a knife dipped in beaten egg
6. Bread with bread crumbs
7. Fry until golden
8. Serve with clear sauce
