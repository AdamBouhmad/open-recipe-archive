## Ingredients

- Gurnard
- Onion slices
- Parsley
- Two bay leaves
- Two cloves
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- One or two bottles white wine
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the gurnard and tie the head
2. Put them in a casserole with onion slices, parsley, two bay leaves, two cloves, salt, coarse pepper, and one or two bottles of white wine
3. The fish must be covered; simmer for a good quarter hour
4. Drain them and arrange them on the platter; put Italian sauce underneath
