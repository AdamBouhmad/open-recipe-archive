## Ingredients

- a lamb leg
- mushrooms
- velvet sauce
- coarse pepper
- egg yolks
- croutons
- bay leaf
- parsley
- chives
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Spit-roast a lamb leg; when cooked and cooled, remove the meat
2. Remove nerves and skin; mince the meat
3. Beat with the knife handle until pieces are of equal thickness and size
4. Cut and round the angles of the minced pieces as much as possible
5. Place the prepared meat in a saucepan
6. Sauté minced mushrooms and add to the meat
7. Pour in four spoonfuls of prepared velvet sauce and a little coarse pepper
8. Just before serving, place the blanquette on fire with a liaison of two egg yolks
9. Serve with croutons around it
10. If you do not have sauce, pass minced mushrooms in butter
11. When it begins to turn to oil, add a spoonful of flour or more according to the quantity of sauce needed
12. Mix with mushrooms and pour in a spoonful of bouillon, a bay leaf, a small bouquet of parsley and chives
13. Reduce the sauce until thick enough for the blanquette
14. Remove mushrooms and place with the minced meat, pass sauce through a sieve over the meat
15. Just before serving, heat the blanquette and add a liaison of two egg yolks
16. Stir well so it thickens and does not break
17. Check seasoning and add lemon juice if desired
