## Ingredients

- two lamb breasts
- barbs
- a few slices of veal
- two calves' heads
- two onions
- two cloves
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- bouillon
- fine salt
- coarse pepper
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Place the lamb breasts with barbs, veal slices, calves' heads, onions, cloves, and bay leaves
2. Add thyme and a spoonful of bouillon
3. Simmer for two hours
4. Remove, untie, and drain them
5. Sprinkle with fine salt and coarse pepper
6. Place the breasts between two lids to cool
7. Remove the bones and trim them
8. Dip in warm butter, then in breadcrumbs, giving them a nice shape
9. Place on a tile or lid and brush with a feather dipped in butter
10. Drain the butter over the breasts and sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, blowing away excess
11. A quarter hour before serving, place them on the grill over gentle fire
12. Cover with a heated country oven to give color to the breasts
13. Dress on the platter with clear juice
