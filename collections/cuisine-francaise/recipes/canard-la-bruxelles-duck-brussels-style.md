## Ingredients

- one duck
- veal sweetbread cut in dice
- small lard well interlarded
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- two shallots
- salt
- pepper
- lard bard
- one glass of white wine
- bouillon
- two onions
- one carrot
- half a parsnip
- bouquet garni
- coulis

## Directions

1. Singe and empty the duck.
2. Make a salpicon as follows: cut veal sweetbread in dice with small lard well interlarded; mix everything with parsley, chives, mushrooms, two shallots all chopped, little salt, and coarse pepper.
3. Place this in the body of the duck; sew it up so nothing escapes.
4. Cook it with a bard of lard on the breast, one glass of white wine, as much bouillon, two onions, one carrot, half a parsnip, and a bouquet garni.
5. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve, degrease it, add a little coulis to bind it; reduce to the consistency of a sauce.
6. Serve over the duck.
