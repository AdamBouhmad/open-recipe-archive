## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Lard strips
- Veal slices
- Beef slices
- Carrots, fourteen
- Onions, five large
- Cloves, three
- Bay leaves, two
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Chives
- Bouillon, two spoonfuls
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four spices

## Directions

1. Trim your rib well
2. Clear the end of the bone
3. Lard it with large pieces of lard seasoned with four spices, salt, and pepper
4. Put strips of lard in the bottom of your casserole
5. Over your lard strips, put slices of veal and beef, fourteen carrots, five large onions, one of which is studded with three cloves, two bay leaves, a little thyme, a bouquet of parsley and chives
6. Tie with string, arrange your rib in this braise
7. Cover it with lard and put your vegetable garnishes on top
8. Add two spoonfuls of bouillon
9. Bring your braised rib to a boil, then put it on a low fire so it simmers gently for three hours; less if your rib is tender
10. When it is cooked, drain it and remove the string
11. Pass a little of the cooking liquid through a silk sieve
12. Degrease it and reduce it
13. Glaze your rib and pour your reduced liquid underneath
