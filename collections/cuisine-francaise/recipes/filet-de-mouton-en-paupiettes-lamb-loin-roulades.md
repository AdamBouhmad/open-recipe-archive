## Ingredients

- one whole lamb loin
- cooked chicken breast
- blanched beef fat
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- four egg yolks
- caul fat

## Directions

1. Cut the whole loin into slices of its full width
2. Flatten the slices and place on each a good forcemeat made with cooked chicken breast, blanched beef fat, parsley, chives, chopped mushrooms, salt, pepper, and four egg yolks
3. Roll the paupiettes and cook them on the spit, wrapped in caul fat
4. Serve au naturel or with a ragout of chicory or lettuce
