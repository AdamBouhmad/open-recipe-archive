## Ingredients

- calf's ears
- cooked forcemeat
- egg
- oil or lard for frying
- clear jus

## Directions

1. Prepare the ears and cook them as in the previous recipe
2. Stuff them with cooked forcemeat
3. Mix the forcemeat in well
4. Bread the ears in egg
5. Fry them
6. Put clear jus underneath
