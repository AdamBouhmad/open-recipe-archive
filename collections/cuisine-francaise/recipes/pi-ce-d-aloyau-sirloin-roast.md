## Ingredients

- Meat
- Skewers

## Directions

1. Put skewers to hold your meat from the flank near the bones of the filet
2. Pass your spit from the thick to the thin part of the filet
3. In case it turns, put a strong skewer on top of the sirloin to hold it
4. Attach both ends firmly to the spit
5. Serve with a piquant sauce underneath as a relief course after the soup
