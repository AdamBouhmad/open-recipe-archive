## Ingredients

- Calf's ears
- good bouillon
- half a septier of white wine
- half a lemon
- or verjus in grain
- bouquet garni
- salt
- carrots
- lard
- sauce

## Directions

1. Take well-blanched ears and blanch them, then pluck them so no hair remains
2. Make a braise this way: put good bouillon in a small pot, half a septier of white wine, half a lemon cut in slices with skin removed, or verjus in grain if in season
3. Add a bouquet garni, salt and some carrots
4. Cook the ears in this, cover them with slices of lard
5. When cooked, serve with piquant sauce
6. You can also make them into smaller dishes; you can stuff them, dip them in beaten eggs to bread them and serve them fried
7. Always cook them in the braise first before preparing them any other way
