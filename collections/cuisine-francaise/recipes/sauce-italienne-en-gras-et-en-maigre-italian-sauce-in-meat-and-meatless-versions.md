## Ingredients

- two good spoonfuls of fine oil
- chopped mushrooms
- a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a bay leaf, one clove of garlic, two cloves
- a pinch of flour
- white wine
- good bouillon
- a little coulis
- salt
- coarse pepper
- For meatless version: lean bouillon, more flour, two or three spoonfuls of onion juice

## Directions

1. Put oil and chopped mushrooms in a saucepan with the herbs and spices
2. Heat over the fire and add a pinch of flour
3. Moisten with white wine, good bouillon, and a little coulis
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Boil for half an hour
6. Skim off fat and remove the bouquet
7. For meatless version, use lean bouillon and more flour with onion juice instead of coulis
