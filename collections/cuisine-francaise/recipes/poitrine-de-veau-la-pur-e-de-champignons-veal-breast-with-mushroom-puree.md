## Ingredients

- veal breast
- mushroom puree
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare, truss, and cook your veal breast as for the glazed version
2. Place a mushroom puree underneath
3. At the moment of serving, mix an egg yolk into the puree, stirring it together with your puree
