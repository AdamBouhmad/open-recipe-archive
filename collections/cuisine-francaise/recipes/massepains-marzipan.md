## Ingredients

- One pound of sweet almonds, blanched and peeled
- Three egg whites for pounding
- Apricot marmalade or other non-liquid preserves
- Candied and pounded orange flowers
- Sugar powder
- Fine sugar
- Six egg whites
- Chopped green citron

## Directions

1. Take one pound of sweet almonds that you have blanched and peeled, pound them
2. While pounding, sprinkle them with three egg whites
3. Mix them then with apricot marmalade or other non-liquid preserves, candied and pounded orange flowers
4. When all is well mixed, place your almonds in a saucepan with sugar powder and dry them over the fire
5. Place them then on a table and work them with fine sugar
6. Add sugar until the paste no longer sticks to your hands
7. Roll it then to form marzipans of whatever shape you wish
8. Take six egg whites that you whip half-way and mix with chopped green citron
9. Dip the marzipans in this mixture and place them after in fine sugar as much as they can take
10. Arrange them on sheets of white paper, which you place on copper sheets, and bake them in the oven with gentle heat
11. To be sure of the oven temperature, put a little paste on a card; if the card takes on color, it is a sign that the oven is too hot
