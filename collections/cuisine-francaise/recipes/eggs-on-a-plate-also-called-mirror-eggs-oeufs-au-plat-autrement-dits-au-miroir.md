## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Milk

## Directions

1. Take a pan suitable for fire and spread a little butter on the bottom.
2. Place eggs on top and season with salt and pepper.
3. Add two or three spoonfuls of milk.
4. Cook over low heat on the stove.
5. Pass a hot shovel over them and serve.
