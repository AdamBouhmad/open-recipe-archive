## Ingredients

- pheasants or young pheasants
- pheasant livers
- lard
- parsley
- scallions
- salt
- pepper
- lard strips
- paper
- Provençal sauce or other small sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and lard the pheasants
2. Cook them on the spit and serve them with beautiful color
3. For a spit entrée, cook them on the spit with a small farce of their livers made by chopping them with grated lard, chopped parsley, chopped scallions, salt and coarse pepper
4. Wrap them in strips of lard and paper
5. Serve with a Provençal sauce or other small sauce in the modern taste
