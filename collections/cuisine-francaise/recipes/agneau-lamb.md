## Ingredients

- Lamb
- Lamb legs for filling the flanks
- Fine lard
- Atelets
- Twine
- Clear stock

## Directions

1. Remove the rib and shoulder bones, leaving only the backbone and thigh bones
2. Fill the flanks with lamb legs
3. When well trussed, lard it finely from the neck to the end of the thighs
4. Make a small separation from the beginning of the thighs to the loins
5. Spit it from the thighs to the neck
6. Secure it with atelets and twine
7. It can also be roasted in the oven
8. If not larded, it must be barded along the entire length
9. If serving as a relevé, place clear stock underneath
