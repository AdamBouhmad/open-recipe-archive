## Ingredients

- bread crusts
- bouillon with fat not removed
- lentil coulis

## Directions

1. Take a silver platter with bread crusts
2. Moisten them with bouillon that has not been defatted
3. Simmer your crusts until a small gratin forms at the bottom of the platter
4. Drain off the remaining fat from the platter
5. Serve the lentil coulis on top
