## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Butter
- Black pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Flour
- Cream
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Wash the cabbage and slice it finely.
2. Blanch it in salted water.
3. When the cabbage wilts under your fingers, refresh it and press it like chicory.
4. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan.
5. After cutting the cabbage with a knife several times, combine it with the butter.
6. Add salt, black pepper, and a little grated nutmeg; mix well.
7. Stir in a spoonful of flour and mix it with the cabbage.
8. Moisten with cream; if it is too liquid, reduce it.
9. Prepare it in the same manner and sprinkle with bouillon using the same seasoning.
