## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 large glass water
- 4 ounces orange blossom petals
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of sugar in a pan with 1 large glass of water, bring to boil and skim
2. Continue boiling until dipping a skimmer in the sugar and shaking it with a backhand motion, sparks fly that hold together
3. Remove from heat and add orange blossoms prepared as follows: take 4 ounces of white orange blossom petals, cut them with a few knife strokes, and moisten them with the juice of half a lemon
4. Put them in the sugar and stir without being on the fire until the sugar becomes white around the edges of the pan
5. Pour immediately into a paper mold as with the previous preserves
