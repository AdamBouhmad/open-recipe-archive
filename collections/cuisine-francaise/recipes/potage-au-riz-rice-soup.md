## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of rice (more or less according to the size of your soup, one quarter pound for four bowls)
- good broth
- veal juice

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in lukewarm water three or four times, rubbing it with your hands.
2. Cook it on low heat for three hours with good broth and veal juice.
3. When cooked, degrease it.
4. Taste to check if it has good salt.
5. Serve neither too thick nor too liquid.
