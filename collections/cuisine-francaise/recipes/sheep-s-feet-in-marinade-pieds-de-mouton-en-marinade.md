## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet, prepared and cooked as in the white sauce recipe
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your sheep's feet as those made in the white sauce manner
2. When they are cooked, drain and trim them
3. Put them in a marinade
4. If you have no marinade, season them with a little salt and pepper
5. Coat them with vinegar so that they receive it all
6. At the moment of serving, drain them
