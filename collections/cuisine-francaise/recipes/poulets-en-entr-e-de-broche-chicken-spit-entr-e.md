## Ingredients

- Two fat white chickens of equal size
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. You take two fat white chickens of equal size; singe and gut them through the cavity; remove the breastbone; put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with salt, coarse pepper
