## Ingredients

- one chopine of good milk
- a grain of good rennet the size of a pea

## Directions

1. Heat the milk slightly on the fire
2. While stirring, add a grain of good rennet the size of a pea that you will have diluted with the same milk
3. Set the curd on some ashes by covering it and putting hot ashes on the lid
4. When it is set, put the curd in a small wicker basket made for these small cheeses
5. When well drained, press it in the compotier and serve it with good cream and fine sugar on top
