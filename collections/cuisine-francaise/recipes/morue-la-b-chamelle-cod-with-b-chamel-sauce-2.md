## Ingredients

- one desalted cod
- butter
- flour
- salt
- coarse pepper
- grated nutmeg
- parsley
- chives
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cod as in the previous recipes
2. Drain it and remove the bones
3. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with one spoonful of flour, a little salt, coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg, finely chopped parsley, and finely chopped chives
4. Mix this seasoning with the butter
5. Add one glass of cream
6. Put the sauce on the fire and stir constantly until it boils; if it is too thick, thin it with cream
