## Ingredients

- Cherries
- One pound of sugar for every pound of cherries

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and pits from your cherries
2. Mix one pound of sugar for every pound of cherries
3. When the sugar reaches its cooking degree, be careful to skim the jam well
4. Let it cool slightly in your basin before putting it in pots
