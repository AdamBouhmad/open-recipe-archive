## Ingredients

- Orange jelly (prepared as for small pots, in larger quantity)
- Fish glue (more than for blancmange)

## Directions

1. Prepare your orange jelly as you would for small pots, but in larger quantity
2. Use more glue than for the blancmange
3. Place it on ice so it freezes well
4. At the moment of service, unmold onto the plate
5. With a well-heated cloth, rub it, and the jelly will detach
6. If there is any liquid, draw it up with a straw
