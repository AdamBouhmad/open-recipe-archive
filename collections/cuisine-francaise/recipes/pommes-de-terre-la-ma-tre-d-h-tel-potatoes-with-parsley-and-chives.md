## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in salted water
2. Cut them into slices
3. Place them in a casserole with a good piece of butter, chopped parsley, chopped chives, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Place on the fire and sauté with the butter and herbs
5. If the butter turns to oil, pour in a spoonful of water
6. At the moment of serving, add lemon juice
