## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes well and cook them in salted water
2. Peel them when cooked
3. Warm a good piece of butter in a casserole
4. Cut the potatoes into slices and place them in the butter
5. Add salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
6. Sauté the potato slices in the butter, being careful not to let it turn to oil
7. Serve on a platter
