## Ingredients

- One veal knuckle, well trimmed
- Lard for larding
- Fine salt
- Pepper
- Four spices
- Parsley
- Chive, finely minced
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Butter
- Four large carrots, turned into large batons
- Four large onions
- Two laurel leaves
- Two glasses of bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove your knuckle of veal well trimmed
2. Place it in a white cloth
3. Beat it with a cleaver
4. Lard it with large lard
5. Season your lardoons with fine salt, pepper, four spices, parsley and very finely minced chive, a little thyme and laurel also minced, which you mix well with your lardoons
6. Your knuckle must be well larded
7. Keep your knuckle covered with its skins, taking care that the lardoons do not pierce the skin on top
8. Tie it with a trussing needle and string that you put around, so that the skins covering your knuckle do not curl back, and when cooked they are well covered
9. Butter the bottom of your casserole
10. Put your veal knuckle in it
11. Also put around four carrots which you turn into large batons, four large onions, two laurel leaves, two full glasses of bouillon
12. Cover your knuckle with a round of buttered paper
13. When your knuckle boils, put it on a gentle fire for two hours
14. Put a little fire on the cover of your casserole
15. At the moment of serving, drain your veal knuckle
16. Untie it
17. Put underneath the stock which you reduce by half, and the vegetables that cooked with your knuckle
18. Glaze it if you have glaze; you can serve with this knuckle, while glazing it, a glaze sauce, a tomato sauce, chicory, an oseille purée, lettuces, a mushroom purée, etc.
