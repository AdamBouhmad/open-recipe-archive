## Ingredients

- white chicory
- butter
- bouillon
- coulis
- flour
- salt
- shallot

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the chicory
2. Boil it for half an hour in water, then remove it to fresh water and press it well
3. Put it to cook with a little butter, bouillon and coulis, if you have it; if not, make a small roux of flour to bind the sauce
4. When it is cooked, season to taste and degrease it
5. Add a little shallot for those who like it
