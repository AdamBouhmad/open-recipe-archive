## Ingredients

- one pound of fine cherries
- half pound of sugar, or thereabouts
- water
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Take one pound of fine cherries; cut half the stem away, and pass them through fresh water
2. Put half a pound of sugar or thereabouts in a pan, with water to dissolve it; make it boil until it is almost a syrup, and throw your cherries into it, after having made them drain on a sieve
3. Make them take a high heat for about a dozen boils or so; then remove them from the fire; skim them, and with a spoon or with paper you will remove the foam from them; you will let them cool, and you will serve them in a compote dish
