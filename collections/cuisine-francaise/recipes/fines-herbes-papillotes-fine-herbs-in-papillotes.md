## Ingredients

- one quarteron of lard, grated
- six tablespoons of oil
- one quarteron of butter
- four tablespoons of mushrooms, well chopped
- two tablespoons of shallots, well chopped
- two full tablespoons of parsley, well chopped
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate one quarteron of lard
2. Put six tablespoons of oil and one quarteron of butter in a saucepan
3. Pour in four tablespoons of well-chopped mushrooms and pass them in your saucepan over the fire
4. When your mushrooms are well browned, add two tablespoons of well-chopped shallots
5. Pass them with your mushrooms, then add two full tablespoons of well-chopped parsley
6. Pass everything together, then add salt, coarse pepper, and a little nutmeg
7. Transfer your fine herbs to a bowl and use them as needed
