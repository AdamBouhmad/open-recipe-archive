## Ingredients

- whole pears
- a small piece of tin
- water
- a quarter pound of sugar or more if the pot is large and there are many pears
- a small piece of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the pears if you wish (though they are more often served unpeeled)
2. Remove the bottoms and trim the sides of the stems
3. Place them in a small earthenware pot
4. Add a small piece of tin to make them red
5. Add water, a quarter pound of sugar or more if the pot is large, and a small piece of cinnamon
6. Cook them in front of the fire
7. When cooked and the syrup is not too thin, serve them hot
