## Ingredients

- Medium truffles
- Good butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Half a clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- Flour
- One glass of bouillon
- One glass of white wine
- Coulis
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Peel medium truffles and cut them into slices
2. Put them in a saucepan with a small piece of good butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, and two cloves
3. Pass them over the fire and add a pinch of flour
4. Moisten with a glass of bouillon and as much white wine
5. Cook on a small fire for half an hour
6. Skim the fat and add a little coulis, salt, and coarse pepper
