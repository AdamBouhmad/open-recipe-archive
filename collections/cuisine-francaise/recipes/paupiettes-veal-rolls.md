## Ingredients

- Veal slices, two fingers wide and at least three fingers long
- Forcemeat of any meat, or godiveau made with a little veal round, as much beef fat, a little parsley, chives, one shallot
- Two egg yolks
- Half a tablespoon of brandy
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lard barding

## Directions

1. Cut veal slices and flatten them with a cleaver so they are no thicker than a small coin
2. Spread forcemeat or godiveau on each slice
3. To make godiveau: chop the veal round, beef fat, parsley, chives, and shallot very finely; mix in two egg yolks, half a tablespoon of brandy, salt, and pepper
4. Roll the slices and tie them
5. Place a lard barding on each paupiette and tie with string
6. Cook on a spit wrapped in paper
7. When cooked, bread the top of the barding and give it a golden color over clear heat
8. Serve with a sauce of clear jus, well seasoned
