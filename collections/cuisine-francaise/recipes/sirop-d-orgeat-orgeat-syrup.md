## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds
- two ounces of seeds of the four cold seeds
- half an ounce of bitter almonds
- half a setier of warm water
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Put the almonds in boiling water and remove from the fire
2. Remove them when the skin comes off easily
3. As you remove the skins, throw them in fresh water
4. Let them drain and put them in a mortar with the cold seeds
5. Pound everything together until very fine
6. To prevent them from turning to oil, add from time to time a half-spoonful of water
7. Then dilute them in a good half-setier of warm water
8. Put them on hot ashes to infuse for three hours
9. Pass them through a cloth, pressing with a wooden spoon to express all the almond juice
10. Take one pound of sugar and cook it as for maidenhair fern syrup
11. Finish it in the same way on hot ashes
