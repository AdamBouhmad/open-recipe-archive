## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- chopped sauce or small roux
- shallots or chives
- parsley
- cooking liquid from the tongue
- vinegar
- coarse pepper
- capers
- pickled gherkins
- anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue as in the first recipe and prepare it the same way
2. Mask it with chopped sauce, or if you do not have any, make a small roux
3. Put your shallots or chives and chopped parsley into the roux
4. Moisten the roux with the cooking liquid from the tongue and a splash of vinegar
5. Add a little coarse pepper and ensure the sauce is somewhat thin so it may be reduced
6. Add chopped capers to the sauce
7. When ready to pour over the tongue, add two or three pickled gherkins cut small, about the size of a quarter of an egg's worth of anchovy butter, which you will let melt in the sauce
8. Mask the tongue with this sauce and ensure it is well-salted
