## Ingredients

- Rabbit fillets
- Cucumbers à la crème
- Chicory, purée of cardoons, purée of mushrooms, sautéed mushrooms, or tomato sauce (optional additions)

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your fillets as the previous ones
2. Drain and glaze them
3. Arrange them in crowns
4. Put your cucumbers à la crème in the middle
5. You may also add chicory, a purée of cardoons, a purée of mushrooms, a sauté of mushrooms, or a tomato sauce
