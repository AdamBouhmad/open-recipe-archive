## Ingredients

- A rack of aged lamb
- Good butter
- Shallots, chopped
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take a rack of aged lamb, cut it by ribs, and put them in a saucepan with a piece of good butter
2. Cook them over a small fire, turning them from time to time, until they are completely cooked
3. Remove them from the saucepan to drain of their fat
4. Leave about half a spoonful of fat in the same saucepan and add a glass of bouillon, chopped shallots, salt, and coarse pepper
5. Bring to a boil to loosen what sticks to the saucepan
6. Put the chops back in with three egg yolks
7. Bind the sauce over the fire without boiling
8. When serving, add a little nutmeg and a dash of vinegar
