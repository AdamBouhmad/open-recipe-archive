## Ingredients

- Beef tail
- Mushrooms
- Butter, the size of an egg
- Flour, one spoonful
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. When your beef tail is cooked in a braise, arrange it on a platter
2. Mask it with a mushroom ragout (see Mushrooms for an Entrée)
3. If you lack sauce, sauté your mushrooms in a piece of butter the size of an egg
4. When you see your butter turning to oil, add a spoonful of flour
5. Mix it with your butter and mushrooms
6. Moisten them with the cooking liquid in which your beef tail cooked
7. Make sure your ragout is well seasoned with salt
8. Add a little pepper
