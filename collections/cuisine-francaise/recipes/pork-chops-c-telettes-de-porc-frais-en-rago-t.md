## Ingredients

- Fresh pork loin
- Bouillon
- Bouquet garni
- Salt
- Pepper
- Veal sweetbread (ris de veau)
- Mushrooms
- Poultry (sometimes)
- Butter
- Flour
- White wine
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Cut a fresh pork loin into chops
2. Cook with a little bouillon, bouquet garni, little salt and pepper
3. Blanch a veal sweetbread, cut it into large dice, and place in a saucepan with mushrooms, sometimes poultry, and a little butter
4. Heat on the fire and sprinkle with a good pinch of flour
5. Moisten with half bouillon and a glass of white wine, adding enough juice to color the ragout
6. Season with salt, coarse pepper, bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, and two cloves
7. Let cook and reduce to a short sauce
8. Serve over the chops
