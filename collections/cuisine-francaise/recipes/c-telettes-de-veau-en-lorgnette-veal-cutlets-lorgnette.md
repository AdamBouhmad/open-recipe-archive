## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Medium lard strips seasoned with fine salt, coarse pepper, and four spices
- Scarlet tongue
- Large onions
- Butter

## Directions

1. Have cutlets which you trim
2. Flatten them
3. Lard them with medium lard strips seasoned with fine salt, coarse pepper, and four spices; lard them through and through
4. Stiffen them in a casserole with butter
5. Trim them
6. Have a scarlet tongue; take a pastry cutter the size of a small shield; cut seven rounds of tongue that are thick, three lines deep
7. Have large well-cleaned onions; cut them so they are as thick as your rounds of tongue
8. Remove the interior of your onions or put your round of tongue in it
9. Do as much as you have cutlets and put it on top so that it holds and forms lorgnette
10. When they are prepared, cook them as those called à la Drue
11. At the moment of serving, drain them; glaze around the cutlet and the piece of tongue without touching the onion
12. Arrange them on your plate
