## Ingredients

- a thick piece of veal rouelle
- thick lard for larding
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- a pinch of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- one onion
- two roots

## Directions

1. Take a piece of veal rouelle, as thick as you can to make a good dish, and lard it with thick lard, with parsley, chives, mushrooms, a pinch of garlic, all chopped, salt, and pepper.
2. Put the veal in a well-covered casserole and cook it in its own juice with an onion and two roots.
3. When it is cooked on very low heat, skim the fat from the sauce it has rendered, and serve the sauce over your piece of veal.
4. If you have coulis, you can put some in your sauce, it will only be better.
