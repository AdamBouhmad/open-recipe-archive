## Ingredients

- one calf's liver
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- half a clove of garlic
- two shallots
- half a bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- fine oil
- butter
- flour
- half a spoonful of verjuice or vinegar
- juice

## Directions

1. Cut a calf's liver into very thin fillets
2. Chop finely parsley, chives, mushrooms, half a clove of garlic, two shallots, half a bay leaf, thyme, and basil chopped like powder
3. Take a medium saucepan and put a layer of calf's liver fillets on the bottom
4. Season on top with salt, coarse pepper, fine oil, and a little of all your fine herbs
5. Continue in this manner until you have used all the liver, seasoning each layer as you did the first
6. Cook over low heat for one hour
7. Remove the liver from the saucepan with a skimmer
8. Degrease the sauce and put in a very small piece of beurre manié made with flour and half a spoonful of verjuice or a drop of vinegar
9. Thicken the sauce over the fire turning it with a spoon; if it was too thin, add a little juice
10. Put the liver in the sauce to heat it and arrange on the serving dish
