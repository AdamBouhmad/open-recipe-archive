## Ingredients

- egg whites
- grated lemon zest
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take egg whites according to the quantity you want to make (five are sufficient to make a small entremet dish)
2. Whip them to stiff peaks in a terrine
3. When well risen, add grated lemon zest and powdered sugar
4. Whip the egg whites a little more, mixing in the sugar and lemon zest
5. Pipe your meringues on a sheet of white paper in small mounds the size of half an egg, without them touching
6. Place a hot cover on top with a little ember on top to cook them
7. When cooked and golden colored, remove from the paper and take out the inside that is not cooked
8. Put a little preserve in its place
9. Glue two together one against the other
10. Serve them as dry as possible
