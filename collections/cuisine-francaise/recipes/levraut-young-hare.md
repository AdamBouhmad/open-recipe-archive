## Ingredients

- Young hare
- Hare's blood
- Lard
- Hare's liver
- Butter
- Poivrade

## Directions

1. When skinned, break the thigh bones and gut it
2. Flatten the stomach by striking it on the back near the shoulders
3. Sear it on the coals
4. Rub it with its blood, lard or bard it
5. Pass the spit from the tail to the head
6. Chop the raw liver, pass it through butter, add poivrade to it
7. Strain through cheesecloth pressing with a wooden spoon
8. Pour this sauce in a sauce boat or underneath your hare
