## Ingredients

- Currants
- Sugar, clarified, equal measure to the juice

## Directions

1. Clarify your sugar as explained previously on page 540
2. String the currants and put them in the pan
3. Make them melt on the fire by letting them boil once or twice
4. Put them afterwards to drain on a sieve
5. Measure your currant juice and put as many pints of clarified sugar in another clean pan that you will cook to the crack stage
6. You will know its cooking stage by means of a goblet full of water that you will hold in your left hand, wet the upper fingers of your right hand that you will quickly dip into your sugar, and then into the goblet, and if the sugar that stuck to your fingers dries and is brittle, you will put your currant juice into it
7. Make two boils covered and skim well
8. Put your jelly into your pots
