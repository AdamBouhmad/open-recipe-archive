## Ingredients

- Three pounds of beef slice
- One veal knuckle
- One chicken
- Thirty or forty carrots
- The same amount of turnips
- The same amount of onions
- Five or six celery stalks
- Two blanched lettuces
- One bouquet of chervil
- Four cloves
- Grand bouillon (for moistening)

## Directions

1. Put three pounds of beef slice in your pot with a veal knuckle and a chicken
2. Add thirty or forty carrots, the same amount of turnips and onions, five or six celery stalks, two blanched lettuces, and a bouquet of chervil
3. Add four cloves
4. Moisten your vegetables and meat with grand bouillon
5. Make the pot foam so that your essence becomes very clear
6. When your meats are cooked, pass your essence through a fine cloth
7. Ensure it is very clear and not in great quantity
8. Do not add salt, since your grand bouillon is already seasoned
