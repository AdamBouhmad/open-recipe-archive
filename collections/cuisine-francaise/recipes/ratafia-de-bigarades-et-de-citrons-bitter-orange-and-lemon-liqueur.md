## Ingredients

- 8 bitter oranges or 8 lemons
- 3 chopines brandy
- 1 pound sugar
- 1/2 setier water

## Directions

1. Take 8 bitter oranges or 8 lemons, peel them lightly without cutting into the white pith, cut the peel into small zests and put them in a jug with 3 chopines of brandy
2. Let them infuse together for three weeks
3. Then put 1 pound of sugar in a pan with 1/2 setier of water, boil them together and skim well
4. Put this in the jug with the brandy and let it infuse another twelve to fifteen days
5. Strain it through a cloth to put in bottles
6. This liqueur will keep for several years
