## Ingredients

- filet (beef filet)
- sel (salt)
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- beurre tiède (warm butter)

## Directions

1. Cut your filet across its length so that your piece, thick with six lines, forms a round
2. Beat it and remove the edges, leaving no skin
3. Ensure your steak is somewhat fatty
4. When your piece is trimmed, season it with salt and coarse pepper
5. Dip it in warm butter
6. Grill it at the moment of serving
