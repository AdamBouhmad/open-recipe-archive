## Ingredients

- artichokes
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the artichokes as previously described
2. Instead of velouté, add Spanish sauce
3. Cook them in the same manner
4. At the moment of serving, arrange the artichokes on a plate and pour the sauce underneath
