## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- two small spoonfuls of flour
- a little green lemon, chopped very finely
- a little orange flower water
- two half-setiers of cream
- a piece of sugar
- a quarter-pound of marrow

## Directions

1. Dilute eight egg yolks with two small spoonfuls of flour, a little finely chopped green lemon, a little orange flower water, two half-setiers of cream, and a piece of sugar
2. Take a quarter-pound of marrow which you melt on the fire and pass through a sieve, and put this marrow into the cream
3. Cook this cream on the fire for half an hour, then remove it
4. Whip the eight egg whites that you set aside and mix them well into the cream, and arrange it in the dish you must serve
5. Cook it in the oven or under a covered pie dish as the previous one; when it is cooked, take a glazer or some clean feather which you dip in good hot butter, and pass it lightly over the cream, put nonpareille immediately on top (these are small dragées of all colors), and serve
