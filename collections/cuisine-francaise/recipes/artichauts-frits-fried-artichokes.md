## Ingredients

- 2 artichokes
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 glass beer

## Directions

1. Peel the heart of two artichokes and cut each into twelve pieces
2. Place them in water to wash; let them drain
3. Place them in a terrine or pot with salt, pepper, 2 tablespoons of oil, one whole egg, two egg yolks, three tablespoons of vinegar, eight tablespoons of flour, and half a glass of beer
4. Mix everything together until the flour is well dissolved
5. You may prepare the batter separately, then place the artichokes in it
6. Stir them so they are coated with batter all over
