## Ingredients

- pigeons
- bouillon
- bouquet garni of all sorts of mushrooms
- artichoke hearts, cut in quarters and cooked halfway
- salt
- pepper
- coulis or liaison of three egg yolks diluted with bouillon and chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Blanch the pigeons briefly and remove them to fresh water, pluck them, and place them in a casserole with bouillon, a bouquet garni of all sorts of mushrooms, two artichoke hearts cut in quarters and cooked halfway, salt, and pepper.
2. When cooked, add a little coulis and serve with short sauce.
3. If you have no coulis, add a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with bouillon and a little chopped parsley.
