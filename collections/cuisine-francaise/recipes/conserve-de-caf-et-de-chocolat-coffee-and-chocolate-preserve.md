## Ingredients

- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 glass of water
- 1 ounce of ground coffee
- ½ ounce of finely grated chocolate (for chocolate version)

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of sugar in a pan with a glass of water
2. Boil and skim
3. Continue boiling until, when dipping the skimming spoon in the sugar and blowing through the holes, small sugar sparks come out
4. Remove from the fire and let it cool a little
5. Add 1 ounce of ground coffee and stir together
6. When well mixed, pour the preserve into the mold
7. For chocolate preserve, use the same method but with only ½ ounce of finely grated chocolate per pound of sugar
