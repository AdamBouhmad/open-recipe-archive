## Ingredients

- Two lamb heads with neck attached
- Bouillon
- Large bouquet garni
- Salt
- Pepper
- Roots
- Onions
- Verjuice berries or half a lemon
- Cooking liquid
- Three egg yolks
- Pinch of parsley

## Directions

1. Take two lamb heads with neck attached; remove the jaws and muzzle
2. Blanch and cook in a white braise as with veal ears (page 85)
3. Place in a pot with bouillon, a large bouquet garni, salt, pepper, roots, onions, verjuice berries or half a lemon cut in slices
4. Remove the skin and cook over low heat
5. When cooked, uncover the brains and arrange in the serving dish
6. Serve with sauce of choice, such as Spanish sauce, ravigote sauce, poivrade sauce, or green herb sauce
7. For a simpler preparation: take the cooking bouillon, being careful it is not too salty, dilute with three egg yolks and a pinch of chopped parsley, thicken over heat, and serve over the heads
8. Alternatively, serve with a ragout of ribs, a salpicon, or a ragout of truffles
9. Potages with lamb head are also made, prepared in white
