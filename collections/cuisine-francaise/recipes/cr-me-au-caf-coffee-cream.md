## Ingredients

- Three half-setiers of water
- Two ounces of coffee
- One chopine of milk
- A piece of sugar
- Five egg yolks
- A pinch of flour

## Directions

1. Put three half-setiers of water in a coffee pot.
2. When it boils, add two ounces of coffee, stir with a spoon and return it to the fire to boil until it rises four or five times.
3. Let it settle to draw it off clearly and put it in a saucepan with one chopine of milk and a piece of sugar.
4. Make it boil until only the amount you need for the size of your dish remains.
5. Mix five egg yolks with a pinch of flour, then add the cream.
6. Pass it through a tamis to pour into the serving dish, which should be on a saucepan with water ready to boil.
7. Cover with a saucepan lid with a little fire on top.
8. Let it boil until the cream is set.
9. Serve hot.
