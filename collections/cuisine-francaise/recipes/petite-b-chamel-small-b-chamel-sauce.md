## Ingredients

- approximately one pound veal cut into dice
- one half-pound ham cut into dice
- a few carrots
- a few small onions
- three cloves
- two bay leaves
- a little basil
- one half-pound butter
- 5 tablespoons wheat flour
- 2 pints milk

## Directions

1. Place the veal, ham, carrots, onions, cloves, bay leaves, basil, and butter in a saucepan
2. Brown the mixture over heat, stirring with a spoon and being careful that the meat does not take on color
3. When the meat is well browned, add the wheat flour and stir
4. When the flour is well mixed with the butter and meat, add the milk
5. Stir constantly so the sauce does not stick
6. Simmer over moderate heat for one hour and a half
7. If the sauce reduces too much, add more milk or broth
8. The sauce should have the consistency of porridge when served
9. If it is too thick, add a little milk, cream, or broth
10. Do not oversalt this sauce because of the ham and because it must be reduced
11. Strain through a sieve
