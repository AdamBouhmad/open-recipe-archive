## Ingredients

- Cucumber ragout
- Three egg yolks
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a cucumber ragout as marked on page 259
2. When finished and well-bound, add three egg yolks
3. Dress on bread crumbs
4. Finish as spinach toast
