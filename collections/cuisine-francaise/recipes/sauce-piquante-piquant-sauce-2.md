## Ingredients

- a dash of vinegar
- two cloves of hot pepper
- a pinch of fine pepper
- one bay leaf
- a little thyme
- three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- two spoonfuls of bouillon
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put into your saucepan a dash of vinegar, two cloves of hot pepper, a pinch of fine pepper, one bay leaf, and a little thyme
2. Reduce what is in your saucepan to half
3. Pour in three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and two spoonfuls of bouillon
4. Reduce your sauce enough so it is like a clear pap
5. Add the necessary salt for it to be of good taste
