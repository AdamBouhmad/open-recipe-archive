## Ingredients

- two lamb tongues, cooked in water
- three egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- forcemeat

## Directions

1. Cut the two cooked lamb tongues into five or six pieces of equal size and in their length
2. Take them and mix them with three egg yolks, let them drain on a cloth, and dry them
3. Add a little of this forcemeat and roll them in a strip of bread
4. Dip them in egg and breadcrumbs, cook them to a beautiful golden color, and serve them with a sauce
