## Ingredients

- a quarter pound of butter
- two raw egg yolks
- one spoonful of lemon juice
- coarse pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put into your saucepan a quarter pound of butter and two raw egg yolks
2. Add one spoonful of lemon juice, coarse pepper, and salt to taste
3. Place it on a fire that is not too ardent
4. Be careful to stir it and not leave it, because your sauce would curdle
5. When it becomes a little warm, whisk it by taking sauce in your spoon and letting it fall back into the saucepan
6. Stir with force so that your butter binds with the yolks
7. Make this sauce only at the moment of serving
8. If it is too thick, add a little water
