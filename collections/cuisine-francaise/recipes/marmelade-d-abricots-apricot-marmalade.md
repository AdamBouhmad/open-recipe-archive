## Ingredients

- Forty pounds of apricots
- Thirty pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Clarify thirty pounds of sugar
2. When it reaches the hard ball stage, add the apricots cut into small pieces
3. Cook the marmalade until it falls in a bound state when placed on a skimmer or between the fingers
4. If desired, remove the skin from the apricots for clearer preserves
5. Let the marmalade boil in the sugar for a quarter hour
6. Pass it through a large horsehair sieve
7. Return it to the pot and give it the degree of cooking explained above
8. When the preserves are in pots and have cooled, cut rounds of paper the size of the pot opening
9. Soak them in brandy and apply them over the preserves
10. Cover with paper and tie with string
