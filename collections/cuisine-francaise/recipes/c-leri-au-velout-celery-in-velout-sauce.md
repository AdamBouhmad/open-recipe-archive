## Ingredients

- celery
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- grated nutmeg
- velouté
- bouillon
- flour
- croutons

## Directions

1. Peel, wash, and cut the celery into small sprigs, leaving the tender leaves
2. Blanch it in plenty of water
3. When you see it bend under the finger, refresh it
4. Drain it well as you would for chicory
5. Chop it as you would chicory
6. Put a piece of butter in a casserole with the celery, a little salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
7. Pour 3 spoonfuls of velouté and as much bouillon over it
8. Reduce it until it is thick enough to serve
9. Place croutons around it
10. If you do not use velouté, when the celery is drained, add a small spoonful of flour mixed with the celery, moisten with bouillon as you would for chicory
