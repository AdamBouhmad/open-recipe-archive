## Ingredients

- Sole fish
- Parsley, chopped and washed
- Chives, chopped
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and tail of the sole; after emptying and cleaning them, place them in your sauté pan
2. Sprinkle with a little chopped and washed parsley, a little chopped chives, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
3. Warm a good piece of butter and pour it over them
4. At the moment of service, place them over a hot fire; stir them so they do not stick
5. Once one side is cooked, turn them over to the other side
6. Arrange them on your plate and drizzle with an Italian sauce (see Italian Sauce)
