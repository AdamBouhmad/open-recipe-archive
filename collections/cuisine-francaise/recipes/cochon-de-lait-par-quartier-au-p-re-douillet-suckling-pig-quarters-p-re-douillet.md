## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- veal knuckle and two feet
- parsley
- chives
- garlic cloves
- cloves
- nutmeg
- onions
- roots
- suckling pig
- four large crayfish
- white wine
- salt
- coarse pepper
- lemon
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a good broth with beef brisket, veal knuckle and two feet, a bouquet of parsley, chives, two garlic cloves, three cloves, half a nutmeg, onions, and roots
2. Once the meat is cooked, strain the broth through a sieve
3. Put the suckling pig in a vessel proportioned to its size, with four large crayfish and the strained broth
4. Add one chopine of white wine, salt, and coarse pepper
5. Cook for one hour and a half
6. Strain the cooking liquid through a sieve and degrease it, then put it on a stove to clarify it like a jelly by adding half a lemon with skin removed and six egg whites beaten with the shells
7. When clear and has enough body, strain through a cloth
8. Put the suckling pig in a vessel just its size, with the four crayfish underneath and branches of parsley
9. Pour the jelly over the pig to chill it
10. When the jelly is set, dip the bottom of the vessel in hot water and quickly invert it onto the platter for serving, with a cloth underneath
