## Ingredients

- Three pounds of liver
- Two pounds of lard
- Half pound of panne (fat)
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Aromates
- Four spices
- Pork caul or thin lard slices
- Bacon strips
- Lard slices for covering
- Lard for decoration
- Aspic

## Directions

1. For three pounds of liver, put two pounds of lard and half a pound of panne.
2. Chop everything together.
3. Add chopped parsley and chives, salt, pepper, pounded aromates, and four spices.
4. When everything is well chopped, spread a pork caul or thin lard slices in your casserole or mold so that your liver does not stick to it.
5. Put in three finger-widths of your forcemeat and seasoned bacon strips.
6. Put in another three finger-widths of forcemeat, then bacon strips, and so on until your casserole or mold is full.
7. Cover it with lard slices and put it in the oven.
8. Three hours are sufficient to cook it.
9. Let it cool in your mold before removing it.
10. You can heat it; after it has come out, you can decorate it with lard and aspic.
