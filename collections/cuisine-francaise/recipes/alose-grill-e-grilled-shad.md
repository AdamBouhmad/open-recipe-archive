## Ingredients

- one shad
- salt
- pepper
- parsley branches
- chives
- one glass of oil
- butter sauce
- capers or sorrel purée

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the shad and remove the scales
2. Dry it well and let it drain between linens
3. Place it on a platter with salt, pepper, parsley branches, chives, and one glass of oil
4. Turn it in this seasoning one hour before serving, more or less depending on its size
5. Place it on the grill at a gentle fire
6. At serving time, arrange it on the platter and coat it with butter sauce
7. Sprinkle capers over it or place it on a sorrel purée
