## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- salt
- pepper
- warm butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook your whole breast in the same manner as your carbonnade, except you will not add ham
2. When the breast is cooked, remove the rib bones
3. Add a little salt and a little pepper on top
4. Dip it in warm butter
5. Bread it as well as possible
6. Then put it on the grill over a gentle fire
7. With a brush, drain butter over the entire breast
8. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on it
9. Have a well-heated camp oven to give your breast color
10. When it is well blonde, put it on the platter with a clear jus underneath
