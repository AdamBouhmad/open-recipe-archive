## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- water
- salt
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers into small squares after peeling them
2. Round off the corners to give them a round or oval shape, making sure the pieces are uniform in size and thickness
3. Put water and salt in a pot; when it boils, throw in the cucumbers
4. As soon as they soften under the finger, remove them from boiling water and plunge them in cold water; let them drain on a cloth
5. Make a cream sauce that is somewhat thickened
6. Place the cucumbers in the sauce and serve on a platter or in an entrée dish
