## Ingredients

- Two pounds of cherries
- One pound of currants
- One pound of black guignes (wild cherries)
- One pound of raspberries
- One pound of blackberries
- Pits from half the cherries
- One quarter pound of sugar per pint of ratafia
- Eau-de-vie in equal quantity to the fruit juice
- One stick of candy

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of cherries from which you remove the stems and pits, one pound of currants, one pound of black guignes, one pound of raspberries, one pound of blackberries that you add later if you do not have them at the same time
2. Crush all these fruits together to put them in a jug with their juice and the pits of half the cherries that you have crushed
3. Let everything ferment together for three days
4. Then pass the juice through a sieve to return it to the jug with an equal amount of eau-de-vie to the fruit juice, one quarter pound of sugar per pint of ratafia, one stick of candy
5. Let it infuse for two months, then draw off the ratafia clear to put it in bottles
