## Ingredients

- three sheep's tongues cooked in water
- good butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- coulis
- flour
- bouillon
- two egg yolks
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut three cooked sheep's tongues into square pieces of the same size
2. Pass them on the fire in a saucepan with a piece of good butter, salt, pepper, parsley, chives, mushrooms, all chopped
3. Moisten with coulis if you have it, otherwise put a good pinch of flour and moisten with bouillon
4. Let the ragout boil until the sauce is very thick
5. Add two egg yolks, thicken the eggs with the sauce on the fire without letting it boil
6. Let the ragout cool and skewer all the small pieces of tongues on small wooden skewers, making the sauce adhere to them
7. Bread them with breadcrumbs and grill them, basting them from time to time with a little butter
8. When they are grilled to a beautiful color, serve on the skewers
