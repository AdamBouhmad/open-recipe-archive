## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- Half a bottle of white wine
- Bay leaf
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Clove

## Directions

1. Place partridges on a moderately hot fire
2. Brown them slowly so they do not take on color
3. When well browned, add six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, half a bottle of white wine, a bay leaf, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and a clove
4. Simmer the partridges for three quarters of an hour
5. Remove them when cooked and place in a casserole
6. Skim the sauce and reduce it by half
7. Strain it through a sieve over the partridges
8. At the moment of serving, remove the trussing strings and arrange on the platter
9. Serve the sauce underneath
10. If you do not have Spanish sauce, leave the partridges in their butter, make a light roux which you will moisten with white wine and half a bottle of bouillon, pour this over the partridges with the seasoning
11. When you reduce your sauce, skim it as much as possible and reduce it until it is thick enough to serve as sauce
12. Check that it is properly salted
