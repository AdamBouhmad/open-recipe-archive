## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet, prepared and cooked as in the white sauce recipe
- Warm onion purée, somewhat thin

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your sheep's feet as those made in the white sauce manner
2. Trim twelve of them and put them in a casserole
3. Have a warm onion purée that is somewhat thin and pour it over your feet
4. Keep them warm without letting them boil
5. At the moment of service, arrange them on the plate and coat them with your purée
