## Ingredients

- Cooked cauliflower
- Gruyère or parmesan cheese, grated
- White sauce, somewhat thickened
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. When the cauliflowers are cooked, drain them and place them on a plate; sprinkle them with grated Gruyère or parmesan cheese.
2. Then dress them on the serving plate; make a white sauce that is somewhat thickened, in which you will put grated cheese; mask your cauliflowers with it and cover them with your sauce as much as possible.
3. Sprinkle grated cheese on the cauliflowers.
4. Then put breadcrumbs on top with a brush dipped in lukewarm butter; baste the breadcrumbs with butter; you will use half breadcrumbs and half grated cheese, and bread your cauliflowers a second time so that it forms a crust.
5. Place them on a gentle fire twenty minutes before serving; put a field oven over the top; let them take on a nice color.
6. At the moment of serving, sponge away the butter that is on your plate with a piece of soft bread; clean the edges of the plate.
