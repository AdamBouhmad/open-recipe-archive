## Ingredients

- Wild boar quarter
- Piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the wild boar
2. Marinate it for five or six days as you would venison
3. Place it on the spit
4. Serve a piquant sauce underneath or in a sauce boat
5. It can be served as a relevé
