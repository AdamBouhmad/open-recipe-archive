## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Lard strips, seasoned with salt, pepper, and spices
- Ham strips
- Butter
- Lard
- Slices of veal
- Vegetables cut into lances
- Two cloves
- Laurel leaf
- Parsley and chives bouquet
- Consommé

## Directions

1. Trim your cutlets as with the previous recipe
2. Have lard strips seasoned with salt, pepper, and spices; also make ham strips
3. Lard your cutlets with one strip of lard and one of ham
4. Pay attention that your lard strips are placed symmetrically
5. Stiffen your cutlets in a casserole with butter, then remove them
6. Trim them as neatly as possible so they have a pleasing shape
7. Put lard rinds in the bottom of a casserole, a few slices of veal, vegetables cut into lances, two cloves, a laurel leaf, a bouquet of parsley and chives
8. Put your cutlets in your casserole and cover them with lard and a buttered paper lid, fill one spoon-full with consommé
9. Let them simmer for an hour and a half
10. At the moment of serving, drain them, glaze them with a beautiful glaze, and arrange them on your plate
11. You can serve underneath an espagnole sauce, cucumbers, a purée of white onions, a tomato sauce, etc.
12. Alternatively, you can cook these cutlets by putting in two or three carrots, three or four onions, a laurel leaf
13. Butter the bottom of your casserole; put your cutlets in it with this seasoning
14. Pour in about one spoon-full of jelly, consommé or bouillon
15. Cook on low heat
16. A quarter hour before your cutlets are cooked, cook them on high heat so they fall to glaze, that is so the liquid reduces and they glaze themselves
17. Put them on warm cinders at the moment of serving; remove them to arrange them on the plate
18. If you have no sauce to put with your glaze, use a little flour which you stir and moisten with a little bouillon to detach your glaze where you have put flour; make it boil and sauce your cutlets
