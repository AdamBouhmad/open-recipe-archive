## Ingredients

- cardoons cooked in blanc
- three degreasing spoonfuls of velouté
- six spoonfuls of consommé
- cream reduced
- a piece of glace the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Cook your cardoons in a blanc
2. Cut them into small pieces
3. Have three degreasing spoonfuls of velouté and six spoonfuls of consommé
4. Place your cardoons with this sauce
5. Reduce it with your cardoons
6. When they are reduced to a paste, pass them through a sieve
7. Keep your purée as thick as possible
8. If it is too thick, thin it with reduced cream
9. Add a piece of glace the size of a walnut
10. Do not boil your purée; keep it hot in a bain-marie
