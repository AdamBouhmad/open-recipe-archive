## Ingredients

- Sheep tongues
- Fine herbs for papillotes
- Oiled paper squares
- Lard strips
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tongues as those described as braised
2. When they are cooked, cut them in half lengthwise
3. Put them on a plate
4. Pour fine herbs for papillotes over them (See Fine Herbs for papillotes)
5. Have squares of oiled paper
6. When your tongues, seasoned with fine herbs, are cold, put on your paper a small piece of lard strip, your half tongue on top
7. Put also a little fine herbs with a small strip of lard
8. Fold your paper so that your tongue is enclosed
9. Cut the corners of your paper, and pleat it so that your fine herbs cannot escape
10. Tie the end of your papillote
11. A quarter hour before serving, put them on the grill over gentle fire, so that your paper does not burn
12. At the moment of serving, arrange them in crowns, and put clear jus underneath
