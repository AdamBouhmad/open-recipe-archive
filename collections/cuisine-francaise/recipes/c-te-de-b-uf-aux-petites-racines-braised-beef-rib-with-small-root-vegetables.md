## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Small root vegetables
- Carrots
- Roux
- Stock from the beef rib
- Sugar
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Trim and braise your beef rib
2. Drain it, glaze it and place it on your dish
3. Arrange small root vegetables around it; you can blanch carrots cut into small sticks
4. Make a light roux that you will moisten with the stock in which your beef rib has cooked
5. When your sauce has boiled, add your small vegetables
6. Add a small piece of sugar and a little black pepper
7. Make your sauce somewhat thin so you can reduce it to develop flavor
8. Degrease your small vegetables and serve them around the meat
