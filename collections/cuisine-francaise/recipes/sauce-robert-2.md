## Ingredients

- Twelve onions
- A good piece of butter
- One spoonful of flour, if no sauce is available
- One pot spoonful of bouillon
- One spoonful of vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two spoonfuls of mustard

## Directions

1. Cut twelve onions in half, removing the head and tail, and cut them into small dice
2. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with your onions and place the saucepan on a hot fire
3. When your onions are browned, if you do not have sauce, take one spoonful of flour and put it with your onions
4. Pour one pot spoonful of bouillon and one spoonful of vinegar, add salt and pepper
5. Make your sauce reduce until it is thick enough to coat
6. At the moment of serving, add two spoonfuls of mustard and use as needed
