## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Black butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the brains as directed for braised
2. When cooked, at moment of serving, drain and arrange on plate
3. Sauce with black butter
4. Place a bouquet of fried parsley in the middle of the brains
