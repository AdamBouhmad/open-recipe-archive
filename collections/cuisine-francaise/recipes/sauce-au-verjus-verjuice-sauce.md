## Ingredients

- Two spoonfuls of verjuice
- As much coulis
- Coarse salt
- Pepper
- Shallot, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Put two spoonfuls of verjuice in a saucepan with as much coulis, coarse salt and pepper, and finely chopped shallot
2. Make this sauce quite clear
3. Heat it and serve for grilled meats
