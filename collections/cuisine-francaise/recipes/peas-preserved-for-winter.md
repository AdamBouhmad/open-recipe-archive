## Ingredients

- Small peas
- Good corks
- String
- Water
- Pitch or tar

## Directions

1. Fill bottles with small peas
2. Cork them well with very good corks and tie them with string
3. Place the bottles in a large pot with cold water
4. Put it on the fire; when the water has boiled for half an hour, remove the pot from the fire
5. Let the water cool and remove the bottles
6. Pitch the corks and the bottle opening
7. To use, cook in cold water and use the same way as other fresh peas
