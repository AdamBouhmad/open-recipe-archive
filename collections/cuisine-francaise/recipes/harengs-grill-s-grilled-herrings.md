## Ingredients

- Herrings
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Butter
- Mustard
- Haricot purée or tomato sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Empty and scrape your herrings
2. Wipe them well
3. Add salt and pepper and drizzle with oil
4. A quarter hour before serving, place them on the grill over a hot fire, being careful to turn them
5. When cooked, arrange them on your plate
6. Make a butter sauce in which you place a spoonful of mustard
7. You may use a purée of haricots or a tomato sauce
