## Ingredients

- one litron of dried peas
- one pound of small lard blanched
- one and a half pounds of beef slices
- two carrots
- three onions, one studded with two cloves
- bouillon
- three or four degreasing spoonfuls of velouté
- spinach green

## Directions

1. Take one litron of dried peas that you will wash
2. Place them in a small pot, adding one pound of blanched small lard, one and a half pounds of beef slices, two carrots, three onions of which one is studded with two cloves
3. Fill your pot almost entirely with bouillon
4. When your peas are cooked, strain them through a sieve
5. Dry them and remove the lard, vegetables and beef
6. Pass your peas through a sieve
7. Moisten them little by little with the bouillon in which they cooked
8. Ensure your purée is thick, as it is easier to thin than to thicken
9. Pour it into a pot
10. Add three or four degreasing spoonfuls of velouté
11. If it is too thick, add a little of the cooking liquid
12. Boil it, skim and degrease it
13. When your purée is sufficiently thickened, transfer it to another pot
14. Check that it is well seasoned and salted
15. At the moment of serving, green it with spinach green
