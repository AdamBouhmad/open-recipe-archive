## Ingredients

- Carp
- Red wine
- Onions
- Carrots
- Parsley
- Six bay leaves
- Thyme
- Three cloves
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean your carp and make a very small opening; be very careful to remove the slime from your carp
2. Tie the head
3. Put it in your fish kettle
4. Boil a pint of red wine and pour it boiling hot over your carp; let it be completely bathed in the red wine
5. Take seven or eight onions cut into slices, four carrots, a handful of parsley, six bay leaves, a branch of thyme, three cloves, salt and pepper
6. Simmer it for an hour, or longer if your carp is large; then let it cool
7. Arrange a napkin on a platter, put your carp on top, and parsley around it
