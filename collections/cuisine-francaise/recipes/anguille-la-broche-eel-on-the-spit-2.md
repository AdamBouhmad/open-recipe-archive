## Ingredients

- Eel
- Butter
- Carrots
- Onions
- Parsley
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Place the eel over a very hot stove
2. Let it grill superficially and with a cloth draw the grilled skin from head to tail
3. Once the eel is stripped and its oil removed, cut off the head
4. Clean it and roll it like a hoop
5. Secure it with small skewers or small spits and string
6. Place it on a baking sheet
7. Put a piece of butter in a casserole with carrots cut in slices, onions cut the same way, parsley and bay leaf
