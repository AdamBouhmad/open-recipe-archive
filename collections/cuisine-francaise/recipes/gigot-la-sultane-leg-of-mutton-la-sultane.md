## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- veal scallop
- beef fat
- parsley
- chives
- egg yolk
- eau-de-vie
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- good bouillon
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- garlic
- cloves
- carrot
- parsnip
- hard egg
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a farce with roughly the size of an egg of veal scallop and twice as much beef fat, chopped together
2. Add finely chopped parsley and chives, one raw egg yolk, one spoonful of eau-de-vie, salt, and pepper
3. Make holes over the entire top of the leg of mutton and stuff with this farce
4. Cook on the spit wrapped in paper
5. When cooked, serve with a sauce made as follows: put in a saucepan half a setier of white wine, as much good bouillon, parsley, chives, half a bay leaf, thyme, basil, one clove of garlic, two cloves, a carrot, half a parsnip, salt, and coarse pepper
6. Boil for one hour on low heat until the sauce is reduced by half
7. Pass through a sieve, then add a finely chopped hard egg and a pinch of blanched parsley, finely chopped, as much as a nut of butter kneaded with flour
8. Thicken over fire and serve over the leg of mutton
