## Ingredients

- pears for cooking that are not very ripe
- a small piece of cinnamon
- a quarter pound of sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Place pears in a well-lit good oven until the entire skin is well burnt, turning them carefully so they grill equally
2. Continue until the skin comes off by rubbing them in water
3. Remove the skin this way, cut them in half and remove the seeds
4. Rinse them again in several waters
5. Put them in a pot to cook with a chopine of water, a small piece of cinnamon, and a quarter pound of sugar
6. Cover the pot and cook them until they soften greatly under the fingers
7. Reduce the syrup and serve them hot if you wish
