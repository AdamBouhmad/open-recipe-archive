## Ingredients

- eight or ten partridges
- velouté sauce made with game essence
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Roast eight or ten partridges on a spit according to the amount of purée you need
2. Let them cool
3. When cooked, remove the meat from the breast
4. Pound them in a mortar
5. Have a velouté sauce made with game essence; put six to eight spoonfuls of defatted sauce in your mortar to make your purée liquid
6. Pass it through a sieve without putting it on the fire
7. Press it well with a wooden spoon
8. When your purée is well passed, keep it warm without boiling it
9. Add salt, pepper, and a piece of butter the size of an egg
10. Use it as you wish, either in soup, in croutons, on bread, etc.
