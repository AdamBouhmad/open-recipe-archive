## Ingredients

- Half a quarter pound of butter
- One teaspoonful of Indian saffron powder, turmeric, or terra-mérita
- Five small cloves of enraged chili pepper
- Five spoonfuls of velouté
- A little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put half a quarter pound of butter in a saucepan with one teaspoonful of Indian saffron powder, turmeric, or terra-mérita, and five small cloves of chili pepper, chopped or crushed
2. Heat your butter until it trembles
3. When well hot, add five spoonfuls of velouté
4. Stir your sauce well and serve it without removing the fat and without straining it through a sieve
5. Add a little grated nutmeg
6. Your sauce should be very hot
