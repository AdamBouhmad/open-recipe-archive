## Ingredients

- Chickens
- One liter of peas
- Butter
- Bunch of parsley
- Chives
- Flour
- Juice
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into pieces and put them in a saucepan with one liter of peas, a piece of butter, a bunch of parsley, chives
2. Pass them over the fire and add a good pinch of flour
3. Moisten with half juice and half bouillon
4. Cook and reduce to a short sauce
5. Add salt only a moment before serving, and a little sugar if desired
