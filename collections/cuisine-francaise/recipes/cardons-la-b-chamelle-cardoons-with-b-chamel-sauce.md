## Ingredients

- cardoons
- consommé
- four spoonfuls of béchamel sauce
- two egg yolks
- butter, the size of half an egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the cardoons as previously described
2. Let them finish cooking in consommé
3. Transfer them to another pot
4. Reduce the consommé in which the cardoons cooked almost to a glaze
5. Add four spoonfuls of béchamel sauce
6. Let it come to a boil a few times
7. Sauce the cardoons with this mixture
8. When the sauce is reduced, bind it with two egg yolks and butter the size of half an egg, stirring into the sauce
9. Pass the sauce through a sieve over the cardoons
