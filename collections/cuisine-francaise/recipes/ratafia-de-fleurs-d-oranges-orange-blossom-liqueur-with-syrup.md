## Ingredients

- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 glass of water
- half a pound of orange blossom leaves
- 1 pint of eau-de-vie

## Directions

1. Put the sugar in a pan with a glass of water and bring to a boil, skimming
2. Continue boiling until, dipping a skimming spoon in it and blowing through the holes, large sparks of sugar come out
3. Remove from the fire and add half a pound of orange blossom leaves
4. Let them boil two or three times with the sugar
5. Remove from the fire and cover well
6. Leave them in the sugar for five or six hours
7. Return them to a low fire with a pint of eau-de-vie
8. Keep on low heat just long enough for the sugar to mix well with the eau-de-vie
9. Pass your liqueur through a cloth and put it in bottles
10. Adjust the quantities according to how much you wish to make
