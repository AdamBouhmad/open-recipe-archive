## Ingredients

- Sheep cutlets
- Medium lardons
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Butter
- Lard strips
- Veal slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets somewhat thick so they are not too thin after beating them
2. Stud them with medium lardons seasoned with salt, pepper, and a little spices
3. Put a piece of butter to melt in a saucepan
4. Put your cutlets to stiffen in it
5. Finish trimming them
6. Put strips of lard in a saucepan with several slices of veal, two carrots cut in slices, three onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, and a little thyme
7. Place the cutlets on this seasoning, cover them with lard, pour a good spoonful of bouillon over them
