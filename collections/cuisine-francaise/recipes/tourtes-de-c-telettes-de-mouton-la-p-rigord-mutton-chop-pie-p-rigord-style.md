## Ingredients

- a rack of mutton, cut into short chops with only the bone marking the chop
- medium truffles, peeled
- fine salt
- a little mixed spices
- lard strips
- butter the thickness of an ecu
- good coulis
- a good glass of Champagne wine, or good white wine if you do not have Champagne

## Directions

1. Line the pie dish with dough as described before
2. Arrange the chops on top
3. Place the peeled truffles between the chops
4. Season on top with fine salt and a little mixed spices
5. Cover with lard strips
6. Put butter the thickness of an ecu all over the lard strips
7. Finish the pie as described before
8. Bake for at least three hours
9. When cooked, add a good coulis to which you have added a good glass of Champagne wine, or good white wine if you do not have Champagne
