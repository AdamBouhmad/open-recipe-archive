## Ingredients

- Butter about half the size of an egg
- Two or three onions, sliced
- Carrots and parsnips cut into zests
- One clove of garlic
- Two shallots
- Two cloves
- One bay leaf
- Thyme, basil
- A good pinch of flour
- One glass of red wine
- One glass of water
- One spoonful of vinegar
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter about half the size of an egg in a saucepan with two or three onions in slices, carrots and parsnips cut into zests, one clove of garlic, two shallots, two cloves, one bay leaf, thyme, basil
2. Pass everything over the fire until it begins to color
3. Add a good pinch of flour and moisten with one glass of red wine, one glass of water, one spoonful of vinegar
4. Boil for half an hour, skim and pass through a sieve
5. Add salt and coarse pepper
6. Use for everything that needs to be enhanced
