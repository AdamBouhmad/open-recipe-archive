## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- Fine sugar
- Jelly (redcurrant, raspberry, apple, quince, or cherry)

## Directions

1. Place puff pastry in the bottom of your tart pan
2. Put a border of pastry as with the others, and bake it without further preparation
3. When your pastry is cooked, put fine sugar on the border and glaze it with the hot spatula
4. As soon as the tart is cooled, cover the entire bottom of the tart up to the edge with the jelly you wish to put in it
5. Serve cold as an entremets
