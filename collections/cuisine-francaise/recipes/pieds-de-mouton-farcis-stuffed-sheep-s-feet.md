## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Laurel leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Garlic
- Cooked meat
- Beef fat
- Bread crumbs
- Milk
- Parsley
- Chives
- Egg yolks
- Egg (beaten)

## Directions

1. Cook the feet in water with salt, pepper, laurel leaf, thyme, basil, and garlic for half an hour.
2. Remove and debone as much as possible.
3. Prepare the stuffing by chopping a small piece of cooked meat and mixing with beef fat, bread crumbs softened with milk, seasoned with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley and chives.
4. Bind with three egg yolks and stuff the feet.
5. To fry: dip in beaten egg, coat with bread crumbs, and fry until golden.
6. To grill: dip in hot butter, coat with bread crumbs, and either grill or bake with a pie cover and coals on top.
7. Drain off any fat and serve with clean platter edges. Optionally serve with a clear sauce.
