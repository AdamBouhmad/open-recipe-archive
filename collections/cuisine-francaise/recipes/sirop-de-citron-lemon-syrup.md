## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- a small glass of water
- juice of one small lemon

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of sugar in a pan with a small glass of water
2. Make it boil and skim
3. Continue boiling until, taking sugar with a finger and pressing it against the other, you open both fingers and a thread forms that breaks and makes a drop on the finger
4. Then add the juice of one small lemon
5. Make it boil a few more times and serve
