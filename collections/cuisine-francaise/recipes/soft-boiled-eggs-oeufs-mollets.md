## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water

## Directions

1. Put water in a pan and bring to a boil.
2. Add as many eggs as desired and boil for exactly five minutes.
3. Quickly remove the eggs and place them in fresh water.
4. Peel them gently so as not to break them.
5. The whites will be cooked and the yolks will remain soft and flexible under your fingers.
6. Serve whole.
