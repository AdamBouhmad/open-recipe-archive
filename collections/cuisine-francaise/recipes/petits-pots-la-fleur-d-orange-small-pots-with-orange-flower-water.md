## Ingredients

- Seven measures of cream or good milk
- Three ounces of sugar
- One spoonful of candied orange flower water

## Directions

1. Measure seven times one small pot of cream or good milk and put in three ounces of sugar and one spoonful of candied orange flower water
2. Bring your milk to a boil and let it cool
3. If your pots are large, put seven egg yolks; if small, five are sufficient
4. Mix them with your milk and strain the composition four or five times through cloth
5. Check that the cream tastes good and pour it into your small pots
6. Put water in a large saucepan and bring it to boil and place the small pots in it
7. They must be placed in the boiling water only up to three-quarters height so water does not enter; place the lid of the saucepan and put fire on top
8. Let your water bath go very gently
9. After ten minutes, check if your small pots are set; remove them, dry them, and serve them cold
10. You can give these small pots any flavor you wish: orange flower, lemon, tea, vanilla, violet, etc.
