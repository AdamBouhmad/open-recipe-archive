## Ingredients

- Foie gras (livers)
- Two spoonfuls of coulis
- Half a glass of white wine
- Good bouillon in equal measure
- Bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Half a clove of garlic
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the bitter part from the livers and leave them whole
2. Blanch them for an instant in boiling water
3. Place them in a casserole with two spoonfuls of coulis, half a glass of white wine, as much good bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, salt, and pepper
4. Let them boil for half an hour, taking care to degrease well
5. Serve with whatever meat you wish, or alone as an entremets
