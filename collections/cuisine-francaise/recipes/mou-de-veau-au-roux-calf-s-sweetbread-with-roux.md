## Ingredients

- One calf's sweetbread
- Butter
- Broth or water
- Onions
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut and prepare the sweetbread as in the blanc recipe
2. Make a roux with your butter and brown your sweetbread in it
3. Moisten with broth or water
4. Add the same seasoning as in the previous recipe
5. When it is three-quarters cooked, add your onions and a little grated nutmeg
6. When nearly cooked, degrease it
7. At the moment of serving, check that your ragout is well seasoned
