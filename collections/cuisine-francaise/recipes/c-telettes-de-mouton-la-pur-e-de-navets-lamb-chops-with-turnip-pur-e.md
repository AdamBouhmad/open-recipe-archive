## Ingredients

- Eight to twelve turnips
- Butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shallots
- Bouillon
- Lamb rack cut into chops
- Fat or oil for marinating

## Directions

1. Have eight to twelve turnips, tender if possible
2. After scraping and washing them, cut them into small pieces
3. Blanch them for half a quarter hour in boiling water
4. When drained, put them in a saucepan with a piece of butter and pass over the fire, stirring often, until they are colored
5. Add a good pinch of flour, salt, pepper, and two chopped shallots
6. Moisten with bouillon and simmer until the turnips are thick like jam
7. Pass them through a sieve
8. While they cook, cut a lamb rack into chops and marinate them with salt, pepper, and a little fat or oil
9. Grill them, basting with the remaining marinade
10. Serve on the turnip purée
