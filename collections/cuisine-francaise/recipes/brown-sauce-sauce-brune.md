## Ingredients

- One or two pounds of beef slice (according to the size of the sauce)
- Two or three pounds of veal
- Poultry trimmings
- Some carrots
- Some onions
- Water

## Directions

1. When you do not have grand bouillon of consommé but you have a little meat, mark a great sauce as you can
2. Have one or two pounds of beef slice (according to the size of the sauce) and two or three pounds of veal
3. Add poultry trimmings, whatever you have
4. Put some carrots and some onions
5. Put everything in a casserole
6. Put a full pot-spoonful of water in it
7. Put your meats on a somewhat hot fire
8. When there is almost no more moistening in your casserole, put it on a gentle fire so the glaze at the bottom of your casserole colors without burning
9. When it is a dark blonde and your glaze is brown, moisten it with water if you do not have bouillon
10. Then put in a strong bouquet of parsley and chives and two bay leaves
