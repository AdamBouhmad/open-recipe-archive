## Ingredients

- croutons
- butter
- pea purée
- salt

## Directions

1. Pass croutons in butter, that is to say fry them until they are blonde
2. They are nice if they are round or oval shaped, or large dice
3. They must be made with bread crumb
4. You can give them any shape you wish
5. Make a green purée (see Purée de Pois Nouveaux)
6. Bring it to the consistency that is clear enough for your soup
7. Melt a piece of good butter at the moment of serving
8. Pour it over your croutons at the same time
9. Make sure your purée is properly salted
