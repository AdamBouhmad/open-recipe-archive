## Ingredients

- one or two young rabbits
- their livers
- scraped lard
- butter or beef marrow
- parsley
- chives
- savory
- salt
- coarse pepper
- three egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- small lard strips
- buttered paper
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Skin and empty one or two young rabbits
2. Chop the livers to make a forcemeat with scraped lard, a little butter or beef marrow, chopped parsley, chives, and savory, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Bind this forcemeat with three egg yolks and put it inside the rabbit body, sewing it closed
4. Cook them on the coals
5. Cut strips of breadcrumbs the length of the rabbits with as many strips of small lard, well interlarded
6. Cover the entire body of the rabbits with these strips, alternating bread and lard so only the head of the rabbits shows
7. Wrap them with two sheets of well-buttered paper and cook slowly on the spit
8. When cooked and the bread slightly browned, remove the paper and serve with the bread strips, small lard, and the cooking juices to which you add a dash of verjuice
