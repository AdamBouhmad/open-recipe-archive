## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Butter, a good piece
- Flour, one tablespoon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg, grated, a little
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Chives, chopped and rinsed
- Cream, one glass

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cod as in the preceding recipes; drain it and remove the bones.
2. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with one tablespoon of flour, a little salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg, finely chopped parsley, and chopped and rinsed chives; mix this seasoning with the butter.
3. Add one glass of cream to the mixture.
4. Place the sauce on the fire, stirring constantly until it comes to a boil; if it is too thick, thin it with cream; it should be thick as porridge.
5. Pour the sauce over the cod and keep it warm without boiling.
6. At the moment of serving, arrange it in a mound shape, that is to say forming a dome on your plate.
