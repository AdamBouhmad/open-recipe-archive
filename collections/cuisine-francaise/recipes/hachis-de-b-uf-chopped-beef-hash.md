## Ingredients

- Three or four onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Half a glass of wine
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Chopped beef
- Mustard or vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince three or four onions very fine and put them in a casserole with a little butter
2. Cook over the fire until they are almost cooked
3. Add a good pinch of flour and stir until it is golden in color
4. Moisten with bouillon and half a glass of wine, season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Let boil until the onion is cooked and there is no more sauce
6. Add chopped beef and boil it so it takes on the taste of the onion
7. When serving, add a spoonful of mustard or a dash of vinegar
