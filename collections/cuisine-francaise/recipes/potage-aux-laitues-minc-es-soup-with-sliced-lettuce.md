## Ingredients

- lettuce
- butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Peel your lettuces down to the yellow part
2. Wash them
3. Cut them very thin, forming thin strips
4. Pass your lettuce through butter until well melted
5. Moisten with your bouillon
6. Cook for one hour
7. Dip your bread soup as customary
