## Ingredients

- three large young partridges
- lard
- medium truffles
- salt
- coarse pepper
- quatre épices (four spices)
- barding lard
- butter
- espagnole sauce
- game stock
- flour

## Directions

1. Take three large young partridges and clean through the breast
2. Singe lightly, taking care not to damage them
3. Pound or mince a pound of lard and place in a casserole
4. Add medium truffles cut into quarters
5. Round the pieces, chop them, and pass through heat
6. Add a little salt, coarse pepper, and four spices
7. When you simmer them with the lard for ten minutes, add the chopped truffle pieces
8. Remove from heat and let cool
9. Stuff the partridges with this mixture
10. Tie them, giving them a well-rounded shape
11. Arrange bards of lard in a casserole with the partridges on top
12. Cover with bards
13. Make a braising pan (poêle) without lemon and place on top
14. Simmer for an hour and a half
15. At service, drain, untie, and dress on a platter
16. Chop two truffles and pass through a little butter
17. Add three tablespoons of espagnole and three tablespoons of game stock
18. Reduce sauce by half, degrease, and serve with the partridges
19. If you do not have espagnole sauce, pass the chopped truffles with a half spoonful of flour and the cooking liquid through a silk sieve
20. Reduce the sauce by half, degrease, and serve under the partridges
21. They may also be roasted on a spit
