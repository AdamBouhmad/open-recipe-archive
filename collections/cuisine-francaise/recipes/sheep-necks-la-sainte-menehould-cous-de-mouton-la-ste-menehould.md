## Ingredients

- Two sheep necks
- Lard strips
- Three carrots
- Four onions
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Two bay leaves
- Thyme
- Two cloves
- Mutton or veal trimmings
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Solfia
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Two necks suffice to make an entrée
2. Cut off the bleeding end
3. Tie them up
4. Cook them between lard strips, adding three carrots, four onions, a bouquet of parsley and chives, two bay leaves, a little thyme, two cloves, some mutton or veal trimmings
5. Moisten them with bouillon or water
6. Add salt
7. Let your necks simmer for four hours
8. When they are cooked, season them with a little solfia and coarse pepper
9. Dip them in warm butter
10. Mix them in the bread crumbs
11. Take care that there is some everywhere
12. An hour before serving, put them on the grill over gentle fire
13. Turn them on three sides
14. Make them take color on top with the country oven
15. At the moment of serving, arrange them on the plate
16. Put clear jus underneath
