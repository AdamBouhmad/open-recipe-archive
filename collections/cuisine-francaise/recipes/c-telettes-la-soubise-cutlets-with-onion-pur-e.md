## Ingredients

- cutlets
- white onion purée

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets
2. Trim them
3. Cook them as the cutlets with cucumbers
4. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, arrange them in crowns
5. Put white onion purée in the middle
