## Ingredients

- Marsh beans
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Savory
- Flour
- Sugar
- Bouillon
- Three egg yolks
- Milk

## Directions

1. If eating with the skin, cook the beans in water for half a quarter hour to remove their acidity
2. Place the beans in a saucepan with butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, and a little savory
3. Cook over the fire and add a pinch of flour and a little sugar the size of a walnut
4. Moisten with bouillon
5. When cooked, add a liaison made from three egg yolks and a little milk
6. Serve as a side dish
