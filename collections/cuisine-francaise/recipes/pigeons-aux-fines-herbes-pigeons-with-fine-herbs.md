## Ingredients

- four somewhat large pigeons
- egg yolks
- butter
- flour
- salt
- coarse pepper
- mushrooms
- shallots
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- white wine
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Blanch the pigeons briefly in boiling water, pluck them, and split them halfway down the back to flatten them a little.
2. Place them in a casserole with the chopped livers, a piece of butter mixed with a good pinch of flour, salt, coarse pepper, mushrooms, shallots, parsley, chives, half a garlic clove, all chopped very fine, half a bay leaf, thyme, and basil chopped like powder.
3. Let simmer for half an hour over a low fire.
4. Then add half a glass of white wine and an equal amount of bouillon and finish cooking over a low fire.
