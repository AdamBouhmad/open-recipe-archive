## Ingredients

- pigeon ragoût
- butter
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Use a pigeon ragoût that has been removed from the table
2. Put a piece of butter in the ragoût and heat it, and for better results add two or three egg yolks
3. Then skewer everything on small hâtelets
4. Bind the sauce afterward, bread them, and grill until golden
5. Serve without sauce
