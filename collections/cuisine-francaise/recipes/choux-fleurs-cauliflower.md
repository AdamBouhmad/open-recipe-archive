## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Water
- Flour
- Butter
- Salt
- Sauce or coulis with butter (for meat days) or white sauce (for lean days)

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the cauliflower
2. Cook it briefly in water
3. Remove it and finish cooking in another white water made with a spoonful of flour diluted with water, a little butter, and salt
4. When cooked, arrange it on the serving plate and cover with sauce or coulis with butter (for meat days) or white sauce (for lean days)
