## Ingredients

- Verjuice, one pound
- Sugar, one pound

## Directions

1. Remove all the seeds from your verjuice
2. Take one part of verjuice that you will put in the pan and one part of sugar on top, which must be in powder form
3. Put your verjuice and sugar layer by layer until finished
4. Then put your verjuice on a small fire to make it boil gently until it becomes well green, which is how you will know it is at perfect cooking stage
5. Put immediately into pots
