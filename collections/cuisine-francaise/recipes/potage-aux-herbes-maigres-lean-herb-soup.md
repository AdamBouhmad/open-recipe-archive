## Ingredients

- a handful of sorrel
- 2 lettuces
- a strong pinch of chervil
- a piece of butter
- water
- salt
- coarse pepper
- 3 eggs for the liaison

## Directions

1. Take a handful of sorrel that you will cut into strips, 2 lettuces cut the same way, a strong pinch of chervil also cut
2. Pass everything with a piece of butter
3. When everything is well melted, moisten it with water, salt, coarse pepper
4. Make everything boil for half an hour
5. Add a liaison of 3 eggs at the moment of serving
