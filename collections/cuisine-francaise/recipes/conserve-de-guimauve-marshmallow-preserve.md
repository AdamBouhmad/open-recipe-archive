## Ingredients

- 1 pound marshmallow
- 1 glass water
- 1 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into very small pieces about 1 pound of marshmallow after scraping and washing it
2. Cook it in a pot with 1 glass of water until it becomes a marmalade
3. Pass it through a sieve, pressing firmly
4. Put back on the fire what you have passed through, stirring constantly until it is very thick
5. Cook 1 pound of sugar in the same way as the redcurrant preserve
6. Add the marmalade to it and stir until the sugar begins to whiten on the edges of the pan
7. Pour into a mold as with the previous preserves
