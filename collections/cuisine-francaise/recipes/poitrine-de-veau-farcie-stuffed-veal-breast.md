## Ingredients

- veal breast with rib bones
- three quarterons of veal round
- one pound of veal udder
- parsley
- shallots
- salt
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg
- three raw egg yolks
- lard
- bouillon
- Spanish sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the end of the rib bones in your veal breast
2. Make an incision between the top meat and that of the ribs
3. Introduce the following stuffing: mince three quarterons of veal round
4. Add one pound of veal udder, minced also with your veal that is already minced
5. Mix everything together
6. Add finely minced parsley, finely minced shallots, salt, coarse pepper
7. Grate a little nutmeg on top
8. Add three raw egg yolks
9. Mince the mixture again to amalgamate everything together
10. Take this stuffing and place it inside your veal breast as much as it can hold
11. Sew with a larding needle and string the meats that contain your stuffing so that it does not escape while cooking
12. When your veal breast is trussed, place lard in your braising pan
13. Put your veal breast there, having taken care to tie it
14. Cover it with lard
15. Place a cooking utensil to cook it; if there is not enough liquid, add a little bouillon
16. When your veal breast has cooked for three hours, at the moment of serving, drain it
17. Remove the strings that contain it and glaze it
18. Place it on the platter
19. Use for sauce a reduced Spanish sauce in which you put a piece the size of a walnut of glaze, and the value of half an egg of fine butter that you will melt in your boiling sauce
20. When you put it in with the spoon, stir your sauce well and pour it underneath
21. If you do not have veal udder for your stuffing, use lard, fat, veal kidneys, or beef kidneys
22. If you do not have a cooking utensil, put carrots, onions, a bunch of parsley and chives, two bay leaves, thyme, three cloves
23. Cover your veal breast with lard
24. Moisten with bouillon; if you do not have any, put water and salt to replace the sauce
25. Make a light roux that you will sprinkle with the liquid in which your veal breast has cooked
26. Make your sauce long so that you can reduce it and so that it takes on flavor
27. Add a little coarse pepper
