## Ingredients

- Two large sweetbreads or three small ones
- Oil or melted bacon
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- One shallot
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Paper for making cases

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in warm water to disgorge them
2. Blanch them by boiling in water for half a quarter-hour
3. Remove to fresh water and remove the cornet
4. Cut the sweetbreads and throat into small slices
5. Marinate them with oil or melted bacon, chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, shallot, salt, and coarse pepper
6. Make seven or eight small paper cases about three fingers long
7. Rub the bottom with oil
8. Put the sweetbreads with all seasonings into the cases
9. Place the cases on a grid with an oiled paper beneath
10. Cook on very low heat from hot ashes for half an hour, watching that the fire does not catch the paper
11. When cooked, add a squeeze of lemon juice or a dash of white vinegar
