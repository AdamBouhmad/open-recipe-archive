## Ingredients

- partridge purée
- ten to twelve egg yolks
- butter
- blonde sauce made with game essence

## Directions

1. Make a purée as above
2. Add ten to twelve raw egg yolks to the mortar
3. Pass your purée through a sieve
4. When it is passed, have a mold that is hollow in the middle
5. If you want to put a small ragout in it, butter it and put your purée inside
6. Place your mold in a water bath
7. Let it simmer for three-quarters of an hour
8. At the moment of serving, turn your bread out onto your plate
9. Moisten it with a blonde sauce made with game essence, well flavored
