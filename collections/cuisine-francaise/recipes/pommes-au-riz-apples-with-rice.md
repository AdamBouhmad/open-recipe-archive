## Ingredients

- Several apples
- Sugar syrup
- Lemon juice
- Apricot marmalade
- Rice
- Cream
- Powdered sugar
- Lemon peel
- Basil, citron peel, candied cherries, verjuice (for decoration)

## Directions

1. Remove the peel and core from several apples with an apple corer
2. Make a thin syrup in which you press lemon juice
3. Put the apples in it
4. When they have boiled, test them with a fork: if it enters easily, remove them and let them drain on a silk sieve
5. With some other apples, make a little marmalade or use apricot marmalade
6. Arrange some in the bottom of the plate
7. Put the apples on top
8. Pour apricot marmalade inside
9. Mask them with rice prepared as for rice cake, made creamy with cream, sugar, and a little minced lemon peel
10. Mask the apples giving them the shape you wish
11. Decorate them with basil, citron peel, candied cherries, verjuice, etc.
