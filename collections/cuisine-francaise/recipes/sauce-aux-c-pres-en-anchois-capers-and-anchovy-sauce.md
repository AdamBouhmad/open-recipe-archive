## Ingredients

- Good butter
- A good pinch of flour
- One anchovy, chopped
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Fine whole capers
- Two whole chives
- Jus

## Directions

1. Put good butter in a saucepan and dilute with a good pinch of flour, one chopped anchovy, salt, coarse pepper, fine whole capers, two whole chives
2. Moisten little by little with jus until there is enough to give your sauce a brown color
3. Bind the sauce on the fire
4. If the sauce is too thick, add a little broth
5. Remove the chives before serving
