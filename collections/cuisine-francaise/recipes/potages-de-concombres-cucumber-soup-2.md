## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- good bouillon
- veal juice for coloring

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers neatly
2. Put them to cook in a small pot with good bouillon and veal juice for coloring
3. When they are cooked, simmer the soup with their bouillon
