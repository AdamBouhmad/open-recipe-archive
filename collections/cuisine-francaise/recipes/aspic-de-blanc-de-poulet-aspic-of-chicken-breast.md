## Ingredients

- Eight chicken breasts
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Four degrease-spoonfuls of velouté
- Four spoonfuls of jelly
- One egg yolk
- Parsley
- Butter
- Aspic

## Directions

1. Sauté eight chicken breasts.
2. Season them with salt and coarse pepper.
3. Let them cool on a platter.
4. Pour into a casserole four degrease-spoonfuls of velouté and four spoonfuls of jelly.
5. Reduce everything to half its volume.
6. Add a liaison of one egg yolk, a little chopped and washed parsley, and an amount of butter the size of half an egg, added after your sauce is bound.
7. Blend it well with your sauce.
8. Pour it over your fillets.
9. Let it cool thoroughly until well congealed.
10. Arrange aspic in your mold to a thickness of about half an inch.
11. Put it in cold storage.
12. When well congealed, decorate it.
13. Arrange your fillets symmetrically with correct form and smooth top due to the sauce that masked them.
14. On top of your fillets arrange a small cold ragout of crests and kidneys with the fillets mignons placed in a similar sauce.
15. Then pour melted aspic to fill your mold and add it when cold.
16. Let your aspic congeal well.
17. At serving time, invert it and detach it as directed for aspic.
