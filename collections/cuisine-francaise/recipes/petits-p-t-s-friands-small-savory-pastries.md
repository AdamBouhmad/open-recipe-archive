## Ingredients

- small piece of veal round
- beef marrow equal in amount to the veal
- parsley, chives, and mushrooms, all chopped
- two whole eggs
- salt and pepper
- half a setier of cream
- puff pastry
- egg for glazing
- cooked chicken breast from the spit
- one chopine of good bouillon
- small bouquet of mixed herbs
- butter
- three egg yolks
- cream for liaison
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make small ordinary pastries with puff pastry
2. Chop together a piece of veal round and beef marrow
3. Add chopped parsley, chives, and mushrooms
4. Mix in two whole eggs, salt, and pepper
5. Dilute this mixture with half a setier of cream and taste to correct seasoning
6. Use small pastry molds and line with thin layers of puff pastry about the thickness of a small coin
7. Spread some of this forcemeat on the pastry and cover with another layer of pastry
8. Glaze with egg and bake in the oven
9. While they bake, chop cooked chicken breast very finely
10. Put about one chopine of good bouillon in a saucepan with a small bouquet of mixed herbs and a little butter
11. Reduce the bouillon to a quarter of its volume and remove the bouquet
12. Add the chopped chicken breast and a little salt
13. Heat without boiling and add a liaison of three egg yolks mixed with cream
14. Thicken on the fire and add lemon juice
15. When the pastries are removed from the oven, remove the top of each to take out the meat
16. Replace the meat with a spoonful of the chicken ragout
17. Replace the cover on each small pastry and serve as hot as possible
