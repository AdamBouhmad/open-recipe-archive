## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- frangipane cream
- egg for gilding

## Directions

1. Make puff pastry dough as described previously
2. Roll out to the size of a pie and thickness of a small coin
3. Place on a pie pan and spread frangipane cream on top
4. Cover with another rolled-out base that is cut out and openwork
5. Glue together by pressing on the edges
6. Gild with beaten egg
7. Bake in the oven for one hour
8. Small ones can be made slightly larger than tartlets using the same method
