## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Chives
- One clove of garlic
- Mushrooms
- Oil
- Half a setier of white wine
- A little broth
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Chop parsley, chives, one clove of garlic and mushrooms
2. Put everything in a saucepan with a little oil and pass over the fire
3. Moisten with half a setier of white wine and a little broth, salt and coarse pepper
4. Reduce to the consistency of a sauce
5. Skim before serving
