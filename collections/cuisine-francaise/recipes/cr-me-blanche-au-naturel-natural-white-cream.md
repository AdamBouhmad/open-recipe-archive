## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- one chopine of cream
- one piece of sugar
- a little rennet
- water

## Directions

1. Take a pint of milk, a chopine of cream, and a piece of sugar which you boil together and reduce to a third
2. Let it cool until you can put your finger in it without burning yourself
3. Take a little rennet which you dilute with water in a tablespoon, mix it well into the cream, and pass the whole thing through a sieve
4. Take the dish you must serve from and put it on hot ash, pour your cream into it and cover it with a lid on which you also put hot ash
5. Leave it until the cream is set, then carry it to a cool place to serve it cold
