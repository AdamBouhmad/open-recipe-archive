## Ingredients

- Sheep tongues
- Small root vegetables
- Suet

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tongues as those described as braised
2. At the moment of serving, drain them
3. Arrange them around the edge of the plate
4. Pour your small root vegetables in butter in the middle
5. If you do not have sauce for your small root vegetables, make a light roux that you will moisten with the cooking liquid in which your tongues have cooked
6. Put your root vegetables to cook in it
7. Throw in a small piece of suet
