## Ingredients

- scoter
- butter
- lean bouillon
- red wine
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- cloves
- savory
- turnips

## Directions

1. Pluck a scoter and clean it, brown it over coals, as a fowl that you want to put on the spit; after having cut it in four, put it in a pot to pass over the fire with a little butter for an hour, next put it in a small pot with lean bouillon, a glass of red wine, salt, pepper, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, two cloves, a little savory; cook over low heat for four or five hours, have turnips that you cut neatly.
