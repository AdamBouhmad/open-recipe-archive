## Ingredients

- Skinned and gutted rabbit
- Butter
- Flour
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Mushrooms
- Bacon
- Parsley
- Chives
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eels (optional)
- Small onions (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut your rabbit into pieces of equal size so they cook evenly
2. Put a quarter pound of butter in a casserole with two tablespoons of flour
3. Make a roux; when well browned, add the rabbit pieces to brown
4. Add half a bottle of white wine and about a bottle of bouillon
5. Stir your stew well until it boils
6. Add mushrooms, bacon that you have browned separately in a casserole, a bouquet of parsley and chives with some thyme and a bay leaf
7. Cook your stew over high heat so the braising liquid reduces
8. Add very little salt and some pepper
9. When three-quarters cooked, if desired, add eel sections and thirty small peeled onions that you have sautéed in butter over moderately high heat until well browned
10. Add the onions and eel at the same time
11. Take care to degrease your stew
12. Your sauce should be neither too thick nor too thin
13. Check that the seasoning is good
14. Remove the bouquet and serve your stew
