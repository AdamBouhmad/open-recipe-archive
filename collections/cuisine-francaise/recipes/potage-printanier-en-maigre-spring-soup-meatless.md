## Ingredients

- one liter of fresh peas
- chervil
- purslane
- lettuce
- sorrel
- three or four onions
- a pinch of parsley
- a piece of butter
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put in a pot one liter of fresh peas, chervil, purslane, lettuce, sorrel, three or four onions, a pinch of parsley, and a piece of butter.
2. Boil everything together.
3. Pass it afterward as a light purée.
4. Simmer the soup with three quarters of this broth.
5. With the remaining broth, mix six egg yolks that you bind over the fire.
6. Put them in your soup when you are ready to serve, after tasting to check if it has good salt.
