## Ingredients

- Suckling pig, scalded and eviscerated
- One pint of good milk
- Approximately half a pound of breadcrumbs
- Approximately one pound of veal round
- Beef fat, equal to the veal
- Breadcrumbs
- Parsley
- Chives
- Two shallots
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four eggs
- Suckling pig liver
- Raw ham
- Lard barding
- Napkin

## Directions

1. After well scalding and cleaning, bone the suckling pig completely, except for the head and feet
2. Prepare the farce: heat one pint of good milk in a saucepan; when it boils, add approximately half a pound of breadcrumbs and boil until it absorbs all the milk and becomes very thick, stirring often near the end to prevent sticking
3. Allow to cool
4. Chop together approximately one pound of veal round and an equal amount of beef fat
5. Mix in the cooled breadcrumb mixture
6. Finely chop and add parsley, chives, two shallots, and mushrooms
7. Add salt and pepper
8. Incorporate four eggs, white and yolk
9. Cut the suckling pig liver into large dice and add approximately the same amount of raw ham, mixing into the farce
10. Fill the body of the suckling pig with this mixture and sew it closed
11. Truss as if for roasting
12. Cover with lard barding
13. Wrap in a napkin and tie with string
14. Cook as the previous recipe
15. Serve cold on a napkin as a cold entremets
