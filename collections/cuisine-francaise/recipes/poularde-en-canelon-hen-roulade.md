## Ingredients

- One hen (poularde)
- Good poultry forcemeat
- Lard strip
- One half-glass of white wine
- Good broth
- Bouquet garni
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two spoonfuls of coulis

## Directions

1. Debone it completely after cutting it in half
2. Place a good poultry forcemeat on each half
3. Roll them and cover the top with a lard strip
4. Tie with string and cook for one hour with a half-glass of white wine, good broth, a bouquet garni, salt, and pepper
5. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve
6. Degrease it and add two spoonfuls of coulis
7. Reduce on the fire to the consistency of a sauce
8. Remove the lard strips and strings
9. Serve the sauce over the hen roulades
