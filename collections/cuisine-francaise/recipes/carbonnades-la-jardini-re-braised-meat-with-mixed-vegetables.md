## Ingredients

- carbonnades
- small roots turned into batons
- glazed lettuce
- light roux
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your carbonnades as the previous ones
2. At serving time, drain them, remove the skin covering the fat, glaze them, and arrange them around the platter: one glazed lettuce, one carbonnade, and so on
3. Put small roots turned into batons in the middle
4. If you do not have sauce for your small roots, make a light roux that you will moisten with the liquid in which your carbonnades cooked, and put your roots in it with a small piece of sugar
