## Ingredients

- Veal round, sliced as thinly as possible, one finger wide
- Parsley, chives, shallots, chopped very finely
- Oil or butter
- Fine herbs, chopped
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Lard barding or white paper
- Half a glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Cut veal round as thinly as possible, one finger wide, enough to garnish the serving dish
2. In a casserole, put a little oil or butter at the bottom with chopped fine herbs, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Arrange a layer of thin veal round on top
4. Repeat, alternating layers of fine herbs, butter or oil, salt, coarse pepper, and veal round until full
5. Cover the top with lard barding or white paper
6. Cover the casserole and cook over very low heat on hot ashes for an hour and a half
7. At the halfway point, add half a glass of white wine
8. When cooked, serve with the degreased cooking sauce
