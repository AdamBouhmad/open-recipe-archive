## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- lard
- ham
- mushrooms
- gherkins
- salt
- fine spices
- parsley
- chives
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- bouillon
- white wine
- onion
- carrot
- parsnip
- coulis

## Directions

1. Bone the leg of mutton, reserving the handle
2. Make holes throughout the inside without piercing the skin
3. Prepare a salpicon: cut lard, some ham, mushrooms, and gherkins all into dice, season with salt, mixed fine spices, finely chopped parsley and chives, thyme, bay leaf, and basil powder
4. Mix everything together and stuff throughout the leg of mutton
5. Tie it and place in a saucepan with a glass of bouillon and as much white wine, an onion, a carrot, and a parsnip
6. Cook on low heat, well covered
7. When cooked, skim the fat from the sauce and pass through a sieve, reduce over fire if too thin, add a little coulis to bind if needed
8. Serve over the leg of mutton
