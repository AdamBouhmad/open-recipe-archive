## Ingredients

- one square of veal cut into cutlets
- salt
- coarse pepper
- mushrooms
- parsley
- chives
- a hint of garlic
- warm butter
- breadcrumbs
- clear stock
- two spoonfuls of verjuice

## Directions

1. Trim the cutlets properly without making them too long
2. Marinate them for one hour with salt, coarse pepper, mushrooms, parsley, chives, a hint of garlic, and warm butter
3. Remove them from the marinade and coat with breadcrumbs
4. Cook them over low heat, basting with the remaining marinade
5. When they are cooked to a beautiful color, serve with a sauce made from clear stock, two spoonfuls of verjuice, salt, and coarse pepper, or serve without sauce
