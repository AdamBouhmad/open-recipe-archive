## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- Jam or marmalade of your choice (apricot marmalade, cherry jam, verjuice jam, apple marmalade, etc.)
- Fine sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Take puff pastry as described above, place it in the bottom of a tart pan
2. Put the jam you intend for the tart on the pastry, leaving a border of one inch that you will moisten with brushes dipped in water
3. Place small strips of pastry arranged in a design over the top, which will cover all the jam, and make a border of pastry for your tart
4. Bake in the oven; it should take at most one hour for cooking
5. When it is cooked, sprinkle fine sugar over it and pass the hot spatula over it to glaze it
