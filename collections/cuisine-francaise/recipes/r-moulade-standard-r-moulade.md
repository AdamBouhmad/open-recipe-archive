## Ingredients

- One glass of mustard
- A little shallots
- A little ravigote
- Six or seven spoonfuls of oil
- Three or four spoonfuls of vinegar
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Two raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put one glass of mustard in a bowl so you can thin it
2. Chop a little shallots and ravigote and add them to your mustard
3. Add six or seven spoonfuls of oil and three or four of vinegar, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Mix everything together
5. Add two raw egg yolks and stir with your rémoulade
6. Mix well so that your sauce is well bound
7. It should be somewhat thick
