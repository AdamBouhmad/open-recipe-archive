## Ingredients

- small pigeons
- good butter
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- a hint of garlic
- salt
- coarse pepper
- sliced veal
- half glass of white wine
- lard
- white paper
- coulis

## Directions

1. Pluck and empty small pigeons, leaving them their feet
2. Lightly brown them on the fire
3. Pass them into a saucepan with a little good butter, parsley, chives, mushrooms, a hint of garlic, all chopped, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Then put them with all their seasoning in another saucepan with sliced veal that you have blanched briefly in boiling water
5. Add half a glass of white wine, cover with lard and a sheet of white paper
6. Put a lid on the saucepan and cook over low heat so they only simmer
7. Then degrease the cooking liquid, add a little coulis to bind it
8. Serve over the pigeons
