## Ingredients

- two good pinches of fine breadcrumbs
- butter the size of a small coin
- one full spoonful of fine oil
- chopped shallot
- salt
- coarse pepper
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Put breadcrumbs, butter, oil, and chopped shallot in a saucepan
2. Season with salt and coarse pepper
3. Add enough verjuice to clarify
