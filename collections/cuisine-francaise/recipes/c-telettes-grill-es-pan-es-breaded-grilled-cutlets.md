## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets and trim them as the previous ones.
2. Season them with a little salt and coarse pepper.
3. Warm a good piece of butter.
4. Dip each cutlet in the butter coming out of the pan.
5. Place it in a vessel containing your breadcrumbs.
6. Stir it inside.
7. Remove it and place it on the table where you have put breadcrumbs.
8. After this, give it shape with your hand.
9. Make it round at the thick end as much as possible.
10. Place it on a board.
11. A half hour before serving, place it on the grill that you position on a gentle fire so your breadcrumbs do not take too much color.
12. When they are cooked, arrange them in crowns.
13. Put clear jus underneath.
