## Ingredients

- small pigeons
- basilic
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. After gutting and trussing the legs inside, cook the pigeons in a braise as you would beef tongue, page 29, using a little more basil.
2. When cooked, remove them from the braise to cool.
3. Dip them in two beaten eggs as for an omelet, coat them as you go with bread crumbs, fry them, and serve garnished with fried parsley.
