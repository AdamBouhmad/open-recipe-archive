## Ingredients

- lamb square
- parsley branches
- green herbs
- lard fat
- jus
- shallots
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a lamb square neatly, removing the skins found on the filets.
2. Lard the entire square with parsley branches and green herbs.
3. Cook it on the spit.
4. When the parsley is well dried, have hot lard fat and baste it with this fat.
5. Continue to baste it from time to time until the square is cooked.
6. Put a little jus in a saucepan with some chopped shallots, salt, and coarse pepper.
7. Heat and serve under the lamb square.
