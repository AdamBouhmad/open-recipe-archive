## Ingredients

- ten chicken thighs
- salt
- pepper
- cooked farce the size of fresh eggs
- mushroom purée
- lard
- mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Remove and bone ten chicken thighs down to the inner joint
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Take cooked forcemeat the size of fresh eggs with an equal amount of mushroom purée
4. Fill the inside of your thighs with this mixture and sew them so the farce does not come out
5. Cut the thick end at the paw side
6. Place lard strips in the bottom of a casserole with your thighs on top
7. Cover them with lard strips and add a lid to cook them
8. Put them over the fire for one hour, keeping them always simmering
9. At the moment of serving, drain them, remove the lard, dry them around the edge of the dish, and put the mushroom sauce in the middle
