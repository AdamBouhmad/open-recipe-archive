## Ingredients

- fish and vegetables (as for lean brown sauce)
- two handfuls of mushrooms
- white roux
- silk sieve

## Directions

1. Prepare your fish and vegetables as for the brown sauce
2. Put them in your saucepan
3. Make the velouté, but do not let it take color
4. When you see there is no more liquid in your saucepan, use the same procedure as the previous sauce
5. When your fish has boiled for an hour and a half, pass your liquid through a silk sieve
6. Make a white roux
7. When it reaches the right point, put two handfuls of mushrooms into it and stir them in your white roux
8. Pour the fish juice into it and stir well to avoid lumps
9. Let the velouté boil for an hour, skim and degrease it
10. Pass it through cheesecloth, ensuring it is neither too thick nor too thin, and that your sauce has good seasoning
