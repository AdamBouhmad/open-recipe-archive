## Ingredients

- Legs of poulardes or turkeys, or turkey or poularde wings
- Six small sausages the length of a finger
- Small bacon cut into slices
- Small white onions, blanched
- Bouillon
- Bacon bards
- Two slices of lemon
- Bouquet of fine herbs
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Take the legs of poulardes or turkeys, or preferably wings of turkeys or poulardes depending on the season
2. Have six small sausages the length of a finger
3. Cut small bacon into slices and blanch small white onions
4. Cook everything together in a casserole with bouillon, wrapped with bacon bards above and below
5. Add two slices of lemon and a bouquet of fine herbs
6. When everything is cooked, remove it quickly to drain it
7. Arrange it in a serving dish
8. Take the sauce remaining in the casserole, deglaze it and pass it through a sieve
9. Add one spoonful of coulis to give it a little consistency
10. Taste to ensure the sauce is well flavored
11. Serve the sauce over the dish
12. Poularde wings are prepared the same way as turkey wings; you can also prepare a whole chicken the same way
