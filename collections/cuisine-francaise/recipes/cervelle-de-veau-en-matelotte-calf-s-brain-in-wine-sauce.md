## Ingredients

- Two calf's brains
- white wine
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet garni
- small onions
- carrots
- coulis
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take two calf's brains and soak them in water to disgorge
2. Cook them with white wine, bouillon, salt, pepper and a bouquet garni
3. Make a ragout of small onions and carrots which you cook with bouillon and a bouquet garni, seasoned well and bound with coulis
4. Serve the ragout around the brains
5. Alternatively serve them in the same way with different ragouts as an entrée, or serve them as a cold side dish when marinated
6. Fry them and serve with fried parsley
