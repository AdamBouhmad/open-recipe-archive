## Ingredients

- one duck, plucked, cleaned and singed
- string for trussing
- lard for barding
- lemon juice
- espagnole sauce, worked
- coarse pepper
- bigarade juice
- bigarade zest

## Directions

1. Truss the duck's legs inside the thighs and tie with string
2. Tuck the rump into the body to shorten it and secure with trussing needle and string
3. Give your duck a round and shortened shape
4. Rub the breast with lemon juice
5. Place lard rinds in your casserole and put the duck on top
6. Cover it with lard rinds and place a cover for cooking
7. One hour before serving, put it on the fire and braise until service time
8. Drain, unbind, and dress it on the dish
9. Put three spoonfuls of worked espagnole, a little coarse pepper, the juice of a bigarade with a little of its zest in a sauce
10. Put this sauce on the fire; at the first boil pour it under your duck. If you lack bigarade, use lemon.
