## Ingredients

- Several apples
- Sugar
- Cinnamon powder
- Breadcrumbs (mie de pain)
- Butter
- Apricot marmalade

## Directions

1. Remove the peel and core from several apples
2. Cut them into small pieces
3. Add sugar in proportion and a little cinnamon powder
4. When they become marmalade, reduce them well so no water remains
5. Let it cool
6. Cut breadcrumbs into very thin and equal thickness slices
7. Dip them in lukewarm butter and arrange them symmetrically in a mold
8. Add the apple marmalade to it
9. Leave a hole in the middle to put apricot marmalade
10. Cover the charlotte with breadcrumbs dipped in butter
11. Place it in a hot oven or in red embers with a pot lid and fire on top
12. Alternatively, mince the apples and put them in a saucepan with powdered sugar and cinnamon powder
13. Let them sit on the fire for a quarter hour
14. When they are slightly melted, let them drain on a sieve until slightly cold
15. Put a layer of apples, then a layer of apricot marmalade, and continue until the mold is full
16. Cook the charlotte the same way and unmold it onto the plate at the moment of serving
