## Ingredients

- One poularde
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Garlic
- Slices of veal
- Bacon bards
- Coulis
- Verjus

## Directions

1. Flame and clean the poularde, truss the legs into the body
2. Prepare it in a casserole over the fire with a piece of butter, salt, pepper, chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, and a hint of garlic
3. Place slices of veal in the bottom of a casserole
4. Place the poularde on top with its seasoning
5. Cover it with bacon bards
6. Cook this way over hot ashes
7. When cooked, deglaze the sauce and pass it through a sieve
8. Add one spoonful of coulis and a dash of verjus
9. Taste the sauce to ensure it is well flavored
10. Serve the sauce over the poularde
