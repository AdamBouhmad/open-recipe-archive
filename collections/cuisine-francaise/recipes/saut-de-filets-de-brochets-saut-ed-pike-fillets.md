## Ingredients

- Fillets from three medium pike
- Parsley
- Scallion
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the fillets from three medium pike
2. Cut them into somewhat long squares and trim them; be sure they are all the same size
3. Arrange them in your sauté pan; add finely chopped parsley and scallion, a little grated nutmeg, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Warm a good piece of butter and pour it over your fillets
5. At the moment of serving, put them on the fire; as soon as the butter boils, turn them over
6. It takes only an instant to cook them; lean your sauté pan to the side and your fillets to the height so that your butter drains off
7. Arrange them on your platter and put Italian sauce mixed with liaison on top
