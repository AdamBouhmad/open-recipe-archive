## Ingredients

- coulis
- one good glass of white wine
- as much good bouillon
- a bouquet of parsley and chives
- two cloves of garlic
- two cloves
- half a bay leaf
- a pinch of coriander
- two spoonfuls of oil
- one onion in slices
- one carrot root and half a parsnip
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Heat the sauce over very low heat for nearly two hours
2. Skim off the fat
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Season with a little salt and coarse pepper
5. Use as desired
