## Ingredients

- Sole fillets
- Fish aspic or gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare your sole fillets as those in Mayonnaise; if you do not have fish gelatin, use aspic
2. Ensure that your pieces are equal in size
3. Pour melted aspic into your mold and let it congeal
4. Decorate your gelatin before placing your fillets in it
5. Arrange your pieces regularly
6. Fill your mold with melted but cold aspic and place it on ice or in a cold place for it to set
7. At the moment of service, turn your mold over onto the plate, using the same procedure to detach it as for aspic of white poultry meat
