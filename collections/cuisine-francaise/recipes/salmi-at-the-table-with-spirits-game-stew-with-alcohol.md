## Ingredients

- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- One spoonful of chopped shallots
- Three-quarters of a glass of white wine
- Juice of three lemons
- Butter the size of half an egg
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Place the roasted birds on the table, cut them up and leave them on the plate that you place on a spirit-alcohol burner
2. Add salt, coarse pepper, one spoonful of chopped shallots, three-quarters of a glass of white wine, the juice of three lemons, and butter the size of half an egg
3. Sprinkle your salmi with bread crumbs
4. Let it simmer for ten minutes, turning it from time to time
5. Check that your sauce is well-seasoned and distribute your pieces
