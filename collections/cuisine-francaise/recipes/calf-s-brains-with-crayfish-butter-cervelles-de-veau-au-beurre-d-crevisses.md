## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Reduced velouté
- Crayfish butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the brains as directed for braised
2. At moment of serving, drain and arrange on plate
3. Have four skimming-spoonfuls of reduced velouté into which you put the size of a small egg of crayfish butter
4. Stir the sauce well so the butter melts and binds with the sauce
