## Ingredients

- Beans
- Butter
- Onion purée

## Directions

1. When the beans are cooked, drain them.
2. Put them in a saucepan with butter about the size of two eggs and about eight spoonfuls of onion purée.
3. Sauté them over the fire without letting them boil.
4. Serve them very hot; check that they are well seasoned.
