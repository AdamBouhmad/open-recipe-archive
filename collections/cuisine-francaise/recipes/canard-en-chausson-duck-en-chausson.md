## Ingredients

- one duck
- farce de volaille or godiveau
- white wine
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- salt
- pepper
- coulis

## Directions

1. Bone and stuff the duck as for the stuffed duck (page 164).
2. Cook it with one glass of white wine and as much bouillon, a bouquet garni, salt, and coarse pepper.
3. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve, degrease it, and add a little coulis to bind it.
4. Reduce to the consistency of a sauce.
5. Serve over the duck.
