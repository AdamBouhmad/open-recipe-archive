## Ingredients

- one large red cabbage
- water
- one pound of Narbonne honey
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Cut and wash one large red cabbage
2. Put it in a pot with water and cook for three or four hours until only about a chopine of water remains
3. Pass the cabbage through a sieve, pressing strongly to extract all the juice
4. Let it rest and draw it off clear
5. Put one pound of Narbonne honey in a pan with a glass of water
6. Make it boil, skimming often
7. When the honey is completely clear, add your cabbage water and boil together until it reaches the consistency of the previous syrup
