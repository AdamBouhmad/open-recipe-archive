## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Brine
- Clarified butter
- Paper or parchment

## Directions

1. Trim the green beans
2. Blanch them in plenty of salted water
3. When they have boiled for ten minutes, cool them with plenty of water
4. When cold, drain them
5. Place them in jars or pots
6. Add brine poured over them
7. Clarify butter and put a one-inch layer on top of the beans; when cold, cover the pots with paper or parchment
8. Alternatively, when trimmed, make layers of beans and salt in pots or a tub, alternating until full, then place a wooden round that fits inside the vessel and put a stone on top
9. To cook, soak for two hours and cook in cold water
