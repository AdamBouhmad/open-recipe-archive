## Ingredients

- a quarter pound of sugar
- a glass of water
- beautiful strawberries that are not too ripe

## Directions

1. Cook a quarter pound of sugar with a glass of water until the syrup is well strong
2. Be sure to skim it well
3. Use beautiful strawberries, not too ripe, cleaned, washed and well drained
4. Put them in the syrup and remove from the fire to let them rest a moment in the syrup
5. Let them come to a boil and remove them promptly so they remain whole
