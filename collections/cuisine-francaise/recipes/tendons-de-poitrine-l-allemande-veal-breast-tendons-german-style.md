## Ingredients

- veal breast tendons
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the tendons into pieces
2. Blanch briefly in boiling water
3. Place in a pot and cook the same way as a chicken fricassée
4. When ready to serve and the liaison is made, add a pinch of blanched finely chopped parsley
