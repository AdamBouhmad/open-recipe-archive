## Ingredients

- mutton (ribs, shoulder or breast)
- butter
- flour
- warm water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- scallions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- onion
- cloves
- turnips
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut your mutton into pieces, either ribs, shoulder, or breast
2. Make a roux with butter and flour
3. When it is blonde, brown your meat in it for a quarter hour
4. Moisten it with warm water
5. Stir your meat well until your ragout boils
6. Skim it
7. Add salt, pepper, a bouquet of parsley and scallions to which you add a bay leaf, a little thyme, and an onion stuck with two cloves
8. Boil your ragout for two hours
9. Turn your turnips and brown them in butter in a casserole or pan; be careful that your turnips do not blacken
10. When they are blonde, drain them and put them in your ragout
11. When it is three-quarters cooked, add a small piece of sugar
12. Degrease your ragout well before serving
13. Remove the bouquet and the onion
14. Check that it is properly seasoned
