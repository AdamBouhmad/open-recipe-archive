## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- coarse pepper
- oil
- 2 tablespoons of Spanish sauce
- juice of 1.5 lemons
- lemon zest
- or: fine bread crumbs (brown crusts), 7 or 8 shallots, pepper, salt, 1/2 glass of water, 6 tablespoons of vinegar, 4 tablespoons of oil

## Directions

1. Clean and singe your partridges; cut off the legs and truss the thighs inside without making a large hole near the croupion
2. Split the partridge down the back from neck to croupion
3. Spread open the partridge and flatten it on the breast so it has a greater surface area
4. Dust with salt and coarse pepper
5. Place the breast side in a saucepan with oil and brown it over heat
6. Turn to the other side to brown equally
7. Half an hour before serving, place on a grill over moderate heat
8. Take 2 tablespoons of Spanish sauce, juice of 1.5 lemons, and zest it slightly to put in your sauce with salt and coarse pepper; bring the sauce to a boil once only
9. Place the partridge on the platter and sauce over it
10. If you have neither sauce nor lemon, finely bread some brown bread crusts; chop 7 or 8 shallots with a little pepper and salt, 1/2 glass of water, 6 tablespoons of vinegar, and 4 tablespoons of oil
11. Bring this sauce to a boil 2 or 3 times and place it over your partridge
