## Ingredients

- partridge
- butter
- fine herbs for papillotes
- paper
- oil
- thin bacon strips
- clear jus

## Directions

1. When your partridge is cleaned and singed, cut it in half from the neck to the rump and separate the pieces
2. Put butter in a saucepan and let the partridge pieces finish cooking for seven or eight minutes until almost cooked
3. Let them cool on a plate
4. Add fine herbs for papillotes on top
5. When the partridges are cold, cut a square of paper large enough to contain half of your partridge
6. Spread oil on your paper
7. Place a thin strip of bacon on it, your partridge half on top with your fine herbs, and another thin strip of bacon to cover it
8. Fold your paper over it and pleat it into papillote form
9. Half an hour before serving, place your papillotes on the grill over a very gentle fire
10. Arrange them in crowns on your plate with clear jus underneath
