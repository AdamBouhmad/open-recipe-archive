## Ingredients

- skate
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- onions
- vegetable root zests
- cloves
- flour
- parsley for frying

## Directions

1. Skin the skate and cut it into pieces as for the preceding recipe
2. Marinate for two or three hours with a little water, vinegar, salt, pepper, parsley, chives, garlic, onions in slices, vegetable root zests, and cloves
3. Drain and dry thoroughly
4. Flour and fry
5. Serve with fried parsley
