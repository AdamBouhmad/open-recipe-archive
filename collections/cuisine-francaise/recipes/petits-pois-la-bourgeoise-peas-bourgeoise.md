## Ingredients

- Light white roux
- Peas
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Parsley
- Chives
- Romaine lettuce
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a light white roux.
2. Add the peas; when they are well browned, moisten with boiling water.
3. Add salt, pepper, four onions, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and sliced romaine lettuce.
4. Let them reduce while cooking.
5. When the peas are cooked and at the moment of serving, add a liaison of three egg yolks.
6. Do not let them boil with the liaison for fear it will break; make sure they are well seasoned.
