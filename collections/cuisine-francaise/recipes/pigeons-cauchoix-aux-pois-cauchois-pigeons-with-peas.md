## Ingredients

- four large Cauchois pigeons
- one litron of fine peas
- good butter
- a bouquet
- flour
- bouillon
- jus
- coulis
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Scald and clean the pigeons and cut them in four
2. Place them in a casserole with one litron of fine peas, one litron for four pigeons
3. Add a piece of good butter and a bouquet
4. Pass them over the fire, sprinkle with a pinch of flour, and moisten with bouillon and a little jus
5. When they are cooked, add a spoonful of coulis, a little sugar the size of a hazelnut, and fine salt
6. If you have neither jus nor coulis, add a liaison of three egg yolks thinned with a little bouillon
