## Ingredients

- A good pinch of salad greens
- Half a clove of garlic
- Two shallots
- A little mustard
- Oil
- A dash of vinegar
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Pound very finely a good pinch of salad greens with half a clove of garlic and two shallots
2. Dilute everything with a little mustard, oil, a dash of vinegar, salt and coarse pepper
