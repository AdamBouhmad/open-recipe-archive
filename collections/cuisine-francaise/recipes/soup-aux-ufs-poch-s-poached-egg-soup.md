## Ingredients

- poached eggs
- very good broth
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Poach eggs; when they are poached, refreshed and trimmed so that they are clean to put in your soup tureen
2. Ten minutes before serving, pour your very good broth over the eggs that will be in your soup tureen
3. Add a little coarse pepper to your broth
