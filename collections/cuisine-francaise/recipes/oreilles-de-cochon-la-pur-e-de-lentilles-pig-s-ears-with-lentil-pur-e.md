## Ingredients

- Pig's ears, well flamed and cleaned
- One liter of lentils
- Two carrots
- Three onions, one studded with two cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Salt
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Place one liter of lentils in your casserole and put your pig's ears in it.
2. Add two carrots, three onions (one studded with two cloves), and two bay leaves with salt.
3. If your ears have not come out of the brine, cook everything together.
4. When the ears are cooked, remove them and place them in a casserole with a little bouillon to keep them warm.
5. Put your lentils in a cloth strainer, remove the bouillon, and press them through with a wooden spoon.
6. Pass your purée through the cloth strainer.
7. Add a little bouillon if it is too dry.
8. Put it on the fire and reduce it if it is too thin.
9. Check that it is properly seasoned.
10. At the moment of serving, drain your ears, arrange them on the plate, and mask them with your purée.
11. You can also serve them with purée of peas, beans, onions, tomato sauce, etc., and serve them with the lentils whole if you wish.
