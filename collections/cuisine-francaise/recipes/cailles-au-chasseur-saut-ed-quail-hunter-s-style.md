## Ingredients

- Quail
- Butter
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Flour
- White wine
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Clean and singe your quail.
2. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan with your quail inside, a bay leaf, salt, pepper, and a little fine herbs if you have them.
3. Place your quail over a hot fire and sauté them constantly.
4. When they resist under your finger, take a spoonful of flour which you mix with your quail, a half-glass of white wine, and a little bouillon.
5. When your sauce is thickened, remove your quail from the fire; they must not boil.
6. Arrange them on the plate and check that they are well seasoned.
