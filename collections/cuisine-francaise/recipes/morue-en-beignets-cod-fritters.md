## Ingredients

- cod
- flour
- wine
- oil
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take cooked and well-drained cod and select the largest flakes
2. Dip them in a batter made with flour, wine, a little oil and very little salt
3. Fry them
4. Serve garnished with fried parsley
