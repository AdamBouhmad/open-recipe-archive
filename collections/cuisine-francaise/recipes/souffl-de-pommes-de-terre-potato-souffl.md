## Ingredients

- one chopine of cream
- six ounces of sugar
- six tablespoons of potato starch
- four egg yolks
- butter the size of an egg
- a little grated lemon zest
- six egg yolks
- one or two whole eggs
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil one chopine of cream with six ounces of sugar and six tablespoons of potato starch; dilute the starch with the egg yolks, cream, butter the size of an egg, and a little chopped lemon zest
2. Place the mixture on the fire and stir until it comes to a boil
3. Let it cool
4. Add six egg yolks mixed together; if the soufflé is too thick, add one or two whole eggs
5. Beat four egg whites as for biscuit and fold them gently into the soufflé
6. Arrange in a small silver casserole and cook as other soufflés
