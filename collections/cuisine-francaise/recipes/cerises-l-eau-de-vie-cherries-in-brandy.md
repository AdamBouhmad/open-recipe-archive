## Ingredients

- Beautiful, healthy cherries, not too ripe
- A quarter pound of sugar for every pound of cherries
- One pint of brandy
- A few cloves
- A little cinnamon wood
- Brandy at 22 degrees

## Directions

1. Take beautiful, healthy cherries that are not too ripe and cut the stems halfway off
2. Put them in a jar with a few cloves and a little cinnamon wood
3. Clarify a quarter pound of sugar for every pound of cherries and one pint of brandy
4. When the sugar reaches the hard-crack stage, pour brandy at 22 degrees into it
5. Mix the syrup with the brandy; when it is cold, pour it over the cherries
6. Seal with a cork disk and wet parchment that you tie well
