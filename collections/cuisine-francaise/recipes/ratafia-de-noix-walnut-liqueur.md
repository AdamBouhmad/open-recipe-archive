## Ingredients

- 12 whole walnuts
- 3 chopines brandy
- 1 pound sugar
- 1/2 setier water
- 1 small piece rock candy
- 1 pinch coriander

## Directions

1. Take a dozen whole walnuts that are just formed, split them in half and put them in a jug with 3 chopines of brandy
2. Seal the jug well and keep it in a cool place for six weeks
3. Be careful to shake the jug from time to time
4. Then put 1 pound of sugar in a pan with 1/2 setier of water, boil and skim
5. After straining the brandy through a cloth, add the sugar to it with a small piece of rock candy and a pinch of coriander
6. Let it infuse for about another month
7. Strain it clear and put it in bottles
