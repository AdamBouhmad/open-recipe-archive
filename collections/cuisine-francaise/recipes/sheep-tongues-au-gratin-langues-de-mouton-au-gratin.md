## Ingredients

- Sheep tongues
- Forcemeat of poultry or godiveau quenelles
- Velouté
- Lard
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tongues as those described as braised
2. Prepare a forcemeat à quenelle of poultry or godiveau in which you will put a little velouté in the bottom of your plate
3. Place your tongues so that they rest on the gratin
4. You can arrange them in different ways
5. Cover them with lard strips
6. Put your plate over a fire that is not too hot, so that your gratin does not burn
7. Place the country oven on top
8. Take care that it is not too hot
9. When you see that your gratin is cooked, at the moment of serving, put an Italian sauce on it
10. Take care to remove the fat that is in your plate and the lard strips that cover your tongues
