## Ingredients

- butter
- 12 eggs
- bread crumb
- sauce

## Directions

1. Warm a little butter on your dish
2. Break twelve eggs and put them on the heated butter
3. Cut bread crumb into small dice and let it cook in the butter
4. When it is well golden, scrape and scatter it over your eggs
5. Put a hot field oven on top
6. When the eggs are cooked, pour a sauce made of reduced Spanish sauce over them
