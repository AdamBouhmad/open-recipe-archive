## Ingredients

- four eggs, whites and yolks
- a pinch of flour
- grated green lemon zest
- a very small piece of pounded cinnamon
- a few crushed bitter almond biscuits
- one half-spoonful of orange blossom water
- one chopine of cream
- one half-quarteron of sugar
- salt the size of a pea

## Directions

1. Dilute four eggs, whites and yolks, with a pinch of flour, a little grated green lemon zest, a very small piece of pounded cinnamon, a few crushed bitter almond biscuits, one half-spoonful of orange blossom water, one chopine of cream, and one half-quarteron of sugar, salt the size of a pea
2. Put your pan on a medium fire, pour the cream into it
3. When it is cooked, throw a little sugar on it to glaze it with the red spatula
