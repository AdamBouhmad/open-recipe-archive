## Ingredients

- Bread slices, two fingers wide and of equal size
- Small bacon, cut into small dice
- One raw egg
- Parsley
- Chives
- One shallot
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut bread slices two fingers wide and of equal size
2. Mix small bacon cut into small dice with one raw egg, parsley, chives, one minced shallot, and coarse pepper
3. Place sufficient bacon mixture on each bread slice
4. Fry at low heat
5. Serve with clear sauce and a dash of vinegar
