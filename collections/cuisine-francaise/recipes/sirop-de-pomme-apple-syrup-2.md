## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of reinette apples, healthy
- half a setier of water
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the apples into the thinnest slices you can
2. Cook them with half a setier of water
3. When they are reduced to marmalade, put them in a cloth and wring them strongly to express all the juice
4. Let the juice rest and draw it off clear
5. Cook one pound of sugar in the same manner as for cherry syrup
6. When the sugar reaches the proper cooking point, add your apple juice and boil them together until, taking syrup on a finger and pressing it against the other finger and opening both, a thread forms that does not break easily
