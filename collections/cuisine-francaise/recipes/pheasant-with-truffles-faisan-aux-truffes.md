## Ingredients

- truffles
- lard (bacon)
- coarse pepper
- quatre épices (four spices)
- pheasant trimmings
- barding lard
- espagnole sauce
- game stock or cooking liquid
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the truffles in lard for five minutes
2. Add finely chopped pheasant trimmings and let cool
3. Using a piping bag, fill the pheasant body with the mixture
4. Arrange a thin bard of lard over the opening where the truffles were inserted and cover with skin
5. Truss the legs as for a braised hen and tie securely
6. Secure the pocket skin so truffles do not escape during cooking
7. Place bards of lard in a casserole with the pheasant on top
8. Cover well with bards and place a braising pan (poêle) without lemon on top
9. Cover with a round of buttered paper and simmer for one hour
10. At service, drain the pheasant and untie carefully without tearing
11. Dress on a platter
12. Chop two truffles and pass through butter
13. Add three tablespoons of espagnole and three tablespoons of game stock or cooking liquid
14. Reduce sauce by half, degrease, and pour under the pheasant
