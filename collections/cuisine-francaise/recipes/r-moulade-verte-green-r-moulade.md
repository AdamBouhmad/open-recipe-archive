## Ingredients

- A small handful of chervil
- Half as much salad burnet
- Tarragon
- Small chives
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- One glass of mustard
- Half a glass of oil
- Two or three raw egg yolks
- Four or five spoonfuls of vinegar
- A little spinach green (optional, if not green enough)

## Directions

1. Blanch the chervil, salad burnet, tarragon, and chives (called ravigote)
2. When well pressed, pound them
3. Add salt, coarse pepper, and one glass of mustard and pound all together
4. Add half a glass of oil and mix well with the ravigote and mustard
5. When well blended, add two or three raw egg yolks and four or five spoonfuls of vinegar
6. Mix everything and strain it through a sieve as if it were a purée
7. Your rémoulade should be somewhat thick
8. If it is not green enough, add a little spinach green
