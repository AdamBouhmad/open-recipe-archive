## Ingredients

- Two calf necks and two calf knuckles
- Four carrots and four onions
- Two spoonfuls of great broth

## Directions

1. Put in a round saucepan two calf necks and two calf knuckles, four carrots and four onions
2. Moisten them with two spoonfuls of great broth
3. Place your saucepan on a good stove
4. When the broth in your saucepan is reduced, put it on a gentle fire so that your veal has time to sweat and the glaze at the bottom of your saucepan does not stick too quickly
5. When the glaze at the bottom of your saucepan is of beautiful color, fill it with great broth
6. Be careful to skim it so that your blond is not cloudy
7. Do not add salt, since your great broth is seasoned
