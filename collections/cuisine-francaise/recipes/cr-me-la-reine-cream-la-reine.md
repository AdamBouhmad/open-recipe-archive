## Ingredients

- one pint of cream
- two ounces of sugar
- half a teaspoon of orange blossom water
- two beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Put the cream on the fire with two ounces of sugar and boil until it is reduced by half
2. Remove from the fire and add half a teaspoon of orange blossom water and two beaten egg whites
3. Stir it a moment on the fire with the whisk
4. As soon as the egg whites are cooked, dress it in the compotier and let it cool before serving
