## Ingredients

- turbot or barbue
- good slices of veal
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet garni of all sorts of fine herbs
- lard
- glass of Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Put it in a turbot pan with good slices of veal, salt, pepper, and a bouquet garni of all sorts of fine herbs
2. Cover it everywhere with lard slices
3. Sweat on gentle fire
4. Add a glass of Champagne wine
5. When cooked, serve with different rich sauces or ragouts
