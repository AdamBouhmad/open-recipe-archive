## Ingredients

- cardoons
- five spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- twice as much consommé
- roux (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the cardoons as previously described
2. Add five spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and twice as much consommé
3. Reduce by more than half
4. Arrange the cardoons on the plate
5. If the sauce is not reduced enough, place it on high heat and pour it under the cardoons
6. If using only roux, moisten it as described previously and add a little juice
