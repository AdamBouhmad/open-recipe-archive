## Ingredients

- 1 glass of oil
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon zest
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Put a glass of oil in a small pan and heat it well
2. Break an egg into a small vessel, add salt and pepper, and pour it into the oil
3. Spread the boiling white with a spoon
4. Turn it and when it has a nice color on both sides, drain it on a hair sieve
5. Twelve are sufficient for an entremets
6. The eggs must be fresh
7. Arrange them in crowns; after trimming them, put a glazed crouton between each egg
8. Use a reduced Spanish sauce to which you add the zest of half a lemon and pour it underneath
