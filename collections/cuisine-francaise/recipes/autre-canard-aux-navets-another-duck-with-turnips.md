## Ingredients

- one duck, cleaned, singed and trussed
- butter for making a roux
- two spoonfuls of bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- one bay leaf
- a bouquet of parsley and chives
- turnips of uniform size
- butter for sautéing
- a small piece of sugar

## Directions

1. When your duck is cleaned, singed and trussed, make a roux
2. When it is well blonde, put your duck in it and let it brown
3. When the meat is firm everywhere, pour in two spoonfuls of bouillon; if you lack bouillon, add water, salt, pepper, and a bay leaf
4. Turn your duck with its liquid until it boils
5. Add a bouquet of parsley and chives and let it cook on high heat
6. When your duck is three-quarters cooked, add turnips that you have shaped to uniform size
7. Sauté them in butter; when they are well blonde, let them drain
8. Put them with your duck and cook on low heat
9. Degrease the duck and add a small piece of sugar
10. At service time, unbind it and pour your ragout of turnips over it. Check that it is properly seasoned
