## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Giblets
- Mushrooms
- Bunch of parsley
- Chives
- One clove of garlic
- Half a bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Flour
- One glass of white wine
- Bouillon
- Juice
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into pieces and put them in a saucepan with the giblets, mushrooms, a bunch of parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, half a bay leaf, thyme, basil
2. Add a good pinch of flour
3. Moisten with one glass of white wine, bouillon, and enough juice to color the stew
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Cook and reduce to a short sauce
