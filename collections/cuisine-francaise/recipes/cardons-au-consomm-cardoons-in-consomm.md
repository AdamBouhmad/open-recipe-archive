## Ingredients

- cardoons
- blanc
- consommé

## Directions

1. Cook the cardoons in blanc
2. Drain them, trim, and pare them
3. Ensure they are all the same size and length
4. Place them in a casserole and pour consommé over just enough to cover them
5. Boil on high heat
6. Reduce the liquid to three-quarters
7. Arrange the cardoons on the plate and use the reduced consommé as sauce
