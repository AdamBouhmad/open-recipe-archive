## Ingredients

- one rabbit, cut into pieces
- butter
- mushrooms
- blanched artichoke hearts
- flour
- bouillon
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- three egg yolks
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into pieces and put in a saucepan with a piece of butter, a bouquet garni of mushrooms and blanched artichoke hearts
2. Pass everything over the fire, add a pinch of flour, moisten with bouillon and a glass of white wine, season with salt and pepper
3. When cooked and sauce has reduced, add a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with bouillon
4. Add a little chopped parsley and serve seasoned to taste
