## Ingredients

- lamb feet
- blanc (white cooking liquid)
- marinade
- salt
- poivre (pepper)
- vinaigre (vinegar)
- pâte à frire (frying batter)
- parsley

## Directions

1. When your feet are cooked in a blanc, put a marinade over them
2. If you do not have one, put a little fine salt, pepper, and half a glass of vinegar
3. A moment before serving, drain them
4. Put them in a frying batter
5. When your feet have taken on batter all over, put them in frying fat that is not too hot
6. When they have a nice color, remove them from your frying fat
7. Let them drain on white linen
8. Dress them on your platter and put a handful of fried parsley on top
