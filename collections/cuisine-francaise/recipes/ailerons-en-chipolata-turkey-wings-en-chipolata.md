## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- bay leaf
- coarse pepper
- salt pork
- mushrooms
- parsley and chives bouquet
- twenty-four small onions
- chestnuts
- sausages
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scald, singe and debone your wings; pare them
2. Place a piece of butter in a saucepan with your wings; sauté them over high heat
3. When they are browned in your butter, add a tablespoon of flour, two pot spoonfuls of bouillon, a bay leaf, coarse pepper
4. Cook your wings at high heat; take care to skim
5. Blanch salt pork that you cut into flat squares to add to your wings
6. Also add the whitest possible mushrooms, a bouquet of parsley and chives
7. When your ragout is three-quarters cooked, add twenty-four small onions, well peeled and of the same size, chestnuts that you have blanched, long sausages that you tie in the middle, and that you blanch; degrease your ragout, and add these ingredients to it
8. When it is cooked and at the moment of serving, arrange your wings on your platter; remove the bouquet and bay leaf from your ragout
9. Check that it is properly seasoned, and add a liaison of two egg yolks; pour it over your wings
