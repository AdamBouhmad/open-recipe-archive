## Ingredients

- Smelts
- Water
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean your smelts
2. Using a small skewer, thread them through the eyes, eight or ten at a time
3. Place them in a flat but somewhat deep vessel
4. Pour good water over them
5. Break parsley leaves into four or five pieces
6. Take a little of your broth and bring to a boil
7. A quarter hour before serving, place your smelts on the fire
8. Remove them from the broth and arrange on the plate
9. Pour the broth with parsley over them
