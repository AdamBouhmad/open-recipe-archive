## Ingredients

- Carp, eel, pike, or barbel
- Small onions
- Butter
- Bay leaves
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Thyme
- Quatre épices
- Salt
- Pepper
- Red wine
- Flour
- Bread crusts

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish
2. Cut into sections
3. Place in a casserole or cooking pot with small onions sautéed in butter, two bay leaves, a bouquet of parsley and chives, mushrooms, a little thyme, a little quatre épices, salt and pepper
4. Add enough red wine so the fish is submerged
5. Place over high heat
6. When the cooking liquid is reduced by a third, take a good piece of butter
7. Mix one or two spoonfuls of flour with the butter and add in small balls to the stew
8. Stir well so the butter and flour bind the sauce
9. Arrange the fish on a plat with bread crusts around and sauce on top
