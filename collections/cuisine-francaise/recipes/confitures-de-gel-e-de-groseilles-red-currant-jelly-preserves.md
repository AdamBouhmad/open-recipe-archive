## Ingredients

- red currants
- 1 pound of sugar per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Clarify your sugar as explained previously
2. Put one pound of sugar for one pound of fruit
3. Cook your sugar to the coffee stage, which you will know by dipping your wet finger in a goblet full of water that you hold in your left hand, then dipping your fingers in your sugar and immediately putting them back in the goblet
