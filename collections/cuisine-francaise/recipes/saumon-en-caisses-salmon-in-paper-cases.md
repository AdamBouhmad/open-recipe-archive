## Ingredients

- Two slices of fresh salmon, about half a finger thick
- Fine oil
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Half a clove of garlic
- One shallot
- Half a bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- White paper
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon juice
- Spanish sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Marinate the salmon slices for one hour with fine oil, parsley, chives, finely chopped mushrooms, half a clove of garlic, one shallot, half a bay leaf, thyme, and basil chopped like powder, salt, and coarse pepper.
2. Make a paper case of white paper the size of the two salmon slices.
3. Rub the undersides of the paper with oil and place them on a plate.
4. Put the salmon in the case with all its seasoning.
5. Coat the top with breadcrumbs.
6. Cook in the oven, or alternatively place the plate on a small stove with a pie dish cover and fire on top.
7. When the salmon is cooked and the top is a beautiful golden color, add lemon juice when serving.
8. If you wish to add Spanish sauce, degrease the salmon cooking liquid before putting it on.
