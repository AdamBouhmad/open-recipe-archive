## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives, chopped
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils and drain them.
2. Put them in a saucepan with a good piece of butter, parsley, finely chopped chives, salt, and black pepper.
3. Sauté everything together.
4. Serve them very hot.
