## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Braises or bacon
- Lard for barding
- Veal slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Clove
- Parsley and chives
- Thyme
- Bouillon
- Glazed lettuce
- Small root vegetables

## Directions

1. Empty your pigeons and singe them.
2. Truss and bard them.
3. Place braises in a casserole with your pigeons.
4. Cover with bards and arrange some slices of veal around them with two carrots, three onions, two bay leaves, one clove, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and a little thyme.
5. Add one spoonful of bouillon.
6. Let simmer for an hour.
7. At the moment of service, drain, unbard, and arrange on your platter.
8. Surround with a border of glazed lettuce and place small root vegetables in the middle.
