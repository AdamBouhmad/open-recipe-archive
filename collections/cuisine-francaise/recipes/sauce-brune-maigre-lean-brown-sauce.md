## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- four or five large carrots, cut into slices
- five or six large onions, sliced
- two or three parsley roots
- three bay leaves
- a pinch of thyme
- three cloves
- three carp, cut into pieces
- two pike of medium size, also cut into pieces
- salt and pepper
- half a bottle of white wine
- a little lean broth
- a large bouquet of parsley and chives
- two or three handfuls of mushrooms
- a blonde roux
- silk sieve (for straining)

## Directions

1. Put butter in the bottom of your saucepan
2. Add the sliced carrots, onions, parsley roots, bay leaves, thyme, and cloves
3. Add the carp and pike pieces, salt and pepper
4. Moisten with half a bottle of white wine and a little lean broth
5. Let the reduction attach to the bottom of the pan until it has enough color, meaning the bottom of your pan is brown
6. If you have lean broth, moisten with it; otherwise, add half a bottle of white wine to deglaze, and fill with water
7. Add a large bouquet of parsley and chives, and two or three handfuls of mushrooms
8. Let boil for an hour and a half
9. Pass the liquid through a silk sieve
10. Make a blonde roux
11. When it reaches the right point, pour the fish juice over it
12. Be careful to properly thin the roux when moistening to avoid lumps
13. Let the sauce boil for an hour, skimming and defatting it
14. Pass it through cheesecloth
