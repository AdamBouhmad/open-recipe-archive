## Ingredients

- Pork meat, very tender
- One pound of lard for every pound of meat
- Parsley
- Chives
- Aromatic herbs, pounded
- Spices
- Salt
- Pepper
- Intestines
- Wine such as Champagne, Rhine wine, Madeira, Malvoisie, or Constance (optional)

## Directions

1. Take the tender meat of pork; put one pound of lard for every pound of meat that you will chop fairly fine
2. Add parsley, chives, a little pounded aromatic herbs and a little spices, salt, and pepper; mix everything well together, and put your meat in the intestines
3. One can add wines inside, such as a glass of Champagne, Rhine wine, Madeira, Malvoisie, Constance; this is a matter of taste
