## Ingredients

- Green almonds or green apricots
- Sugar (equal weight to cooked fruit)
- 1 half-setier water

## Directions

1. Remove the fuzz the same way as explained on page 545
2. Cook them in water until they are quite tender
3. Remove them in fresh water and let them drain
4. Crush them and pass them through a sieve
5. Put this marmalade on the fire to dry it, stirring constantly until it is ready to stick to the pan
6. Weigh this marmalade and put an equal weight of sugar on the fire with a half-setier of water
7. Bring to boil and skim
8. Continue boiling until, dipping two fingers in fresh water, you put them in the sugar and dip them again in water, and the sugar that remains on your fingers breaks clean
9. Add the marmalade at once and dilute it with the sugar without boiling on a gentle fire
10. Put it in pots afterwards
