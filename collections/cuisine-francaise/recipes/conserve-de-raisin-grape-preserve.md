## Ingredients

- 1½ pounds of grapes (preferably muscat)
- 1 pound of sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove the grapes from their stems
2. Place them on the fire to make them burst
3. Pass them forcefully through a sieve until only the skins and seeds remain in the sieve
4. Put everything that passed through the sieve on the fire and dry it until the marmalade is very thick
5. Cook 1 pound of sugar the same way as for currant preserve
6. When the sugar reaches the proper cooking point, add the marmalade to it and finish in the same manner
