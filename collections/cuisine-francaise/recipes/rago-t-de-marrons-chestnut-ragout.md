## Ingredients

- Half a hundred chestnuts
- Half a glass of white wine
- Two spoonfuls of coulis
- A little bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove the first skin from half a hundred chestnuts and put them in a perforated pan to stir over the fire until you can remove the second skin
2. Put them in a saucepan with half a glass of white wine, two spoonfuls of coulis, and a little bouillon
3. Cook and reduce to a short sauce
4. Pay attention that they are well cooked and whole
