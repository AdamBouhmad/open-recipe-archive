## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- cauliflowers
- butter
- salt
- coulis
- coarse pepper
- grated parmesan

## Directions

1. Cook the leg and cauliflowers in the same manner as above, with less salt
2. When everything is cooked, take the serving platter and put a little of the same sauce on it
3. Cover the sauce with grated parmesan, sprinkle the entire top with the rest of the same sauce, and over the sauce add parmesan
4. Place your plat on a mild stove, cover it with a pastry lid and cover until it is a beautiful golden color with reduced sauce
5. Before serving, wipe the edges of the platter and drain off the fat found on top of the sauce
