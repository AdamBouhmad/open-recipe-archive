## Ingredients

- sugar the size of half an egg
- water
- citron zest
- 8 small pots of water
- 1 quarteron of sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put sugar the size of half an egg in a casserole with a little water and citron zest
2. Boil until the caramel is made and has a slightly brown color
3. Measure 8 small pots of water and add 1 quarteron of sugar and a little citron zest
4. Bring to a boil and allow to cool
5. Break 6 egg yolks and mix them with the liquid
6. Pass four times through cheesecloth
7. When the water boils, fill your small pots and place them in a bain-marie
