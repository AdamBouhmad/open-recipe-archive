## Ingredients

- Velvet sauce, worked as needed for saucing
- Garlic butter, amount of half a walnut
- Fine butter, amount of half an egg

## Directions

1. Take velvet sauce worked as needed for saucing
2. At the moment of serving, add garlic butter the size of half a walnut and stir it into your sauce
3. Add fine butter the size of half an egg to give it smoothness
4. If you lack velvet sauce, you can make a little sauce with veal scraps or with a cooking stock, adding garlic butter at the moment of serving according to the quantity of sauce, but the garlic must dominate much
5. You can also make it with brown sauce or in a meatless version
