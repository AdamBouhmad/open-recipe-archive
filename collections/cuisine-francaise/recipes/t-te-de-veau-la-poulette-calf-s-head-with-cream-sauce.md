## Ingredients

- calf's head pieces
- fine herbs
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- two or three eggs for liaison
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown fine herbs in butter
2. Add a little flour
3. Moisten with bouillon; add a little salt and coarse pepper
4. Boil your sauce for a quarter hour
5. Put your pieces of head in it
6. Simmer gently for a moment so it is hot
7. At the moment of serving, blend in a liaison of two or three eggs, according to the size of your ragout
8. Turn your ragout until it is thickened; do not let it boil with the liaison in it, because it would curdle
9. At the moment of serving, pour in a squeeze of lemon juice or a dash of vinegar
