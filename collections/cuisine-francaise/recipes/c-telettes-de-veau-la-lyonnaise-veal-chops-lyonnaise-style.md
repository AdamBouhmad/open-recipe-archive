## Ingredients

- veal rack
- anchovies
- bacon
- gherkins
- salt
- coarse pepper
- oil
- parsley
- chives
- shallots
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut a veal rack into chops
2. Lard them with anchovies, bacon, and gherkins
3. Season with salt and coarse pepper
4. Marinate with oil, little salt, coarse pepper, parsley, chives, and shallots
5. Cook slowly in the marinade between two larded bacon strips
6. When cooked, serve with a sauce made as follows: in a pot place chopped parsley, chives, and shallots, salt, coarse pepper, two pieces of amber butter kneaded with a pinch of flour, a spoonful of fine oil, two spoonfuls of good bouillon
7. Thicken the sauce on the fire, and when serving add a squeeze of lemon juice
