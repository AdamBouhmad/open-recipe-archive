## Ingredients

- two litrons of flour
- half an ounce of salt
- butter the size of an egg
- two egg whites
- two glasses of water
- one pound of butter

## Directions

1. Put two litrons of flour on your pastry board
2. Make a hole in the middle
3. Use half an ounce of salt, butter the size of an egg, two egg whites, and two glasses of water
4. Assemble your dough; it should be as firm as butter
5. Let it rest for half an hour
6. Take one pound of butter; if it is too firm, work it
7. Flatten your puff pastry dough and place the butter on it
8. Fold the dough over the butter so it is well wrapped
9. Let it rest a moment
10. Give it two turns, that is to say with a rolling pin press on the dough, rolling it always until your puff pastry is only half an inch thick everywhere
11. Then fold it in three, that is the long piece is folded one part to two-thirds
