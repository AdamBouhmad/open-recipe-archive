## Ingredients

- cooked cod
- shallot
- garlic
- parsley
- chives
- lemon
- coarse pepper
- oil
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take cooked and well-drained cod
2. In the serving dish, place at the bottom a shallot, a little garlic, parsley, chives, lemon slices with skin removed, coarse pepper, two spoonfuls of oil, and butter the size of half an egg
3. Arrange the cod on top
4. Put the same seasoning on top as below
5. Coat with breadcrumbs
6. Place the dish on a small fire so it simmers gently
7. Brown the top with a hot shovel or a country stove cover
