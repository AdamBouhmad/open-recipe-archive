## Ingredients

- beef palate cooked in blanc
- béchamel sauce
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Have beef palates cooked in blanc that you cut into small squares, that is to say the size of a small coin
2. Have a béchamel sauce
3. Toss them in your sauce
4. Make sure it is neither too thin nor too thick
5. Add a little coarse pepper
6. Serve it well hot
