## Ingredients

- one young rabbit, cut into pieces
- veal sweetbread
- mushrooms
- beef lard for the poupeton mold

## Directions

1. Make a well-bound stew from a young rabbit cut into pieces with veal sweetbread and mushrooms
2. When finished and cold, prepare a forcemeat similar to that for veal loaf (see page 105)
3. Prepare your rabbit poupeton in the same manner as the veal loaf
