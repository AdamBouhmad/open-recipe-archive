## Ingredients

- Six or seven quails
- Butter
- Bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- Half a bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Mushrooms
- Flour
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Jus

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the quails, then gut them
2. Pass them through a casserole over the fire with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, two cloves, half a bay leaf, thyme, basil, and mushrooms
3. Sprinkle with a good pinch of flour
4. Moisten with white wine, bouillon, and jus as needed
