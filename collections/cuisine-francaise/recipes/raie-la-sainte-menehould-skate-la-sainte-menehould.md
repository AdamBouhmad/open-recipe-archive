## Ingredients

- skate
- butter
- flour
- milk (chopine)
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet of parsley, chives, garlic clove, two shallots
- three cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- pine nuts in slices
- vegetable roots in zests
- rémoulade sauce

## Directions

1. Skin the skate and cut it into pieces two fingers wide
2. Cook over very low heat for half an hour
3. In a saucepan, make a roux with butter and a spoonful of flour
4. Gradually moisten with a chopine of milk, seasoning with salt, pepper, a bouquet of parsley, chives, garlic, two shallots, three cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, sliced pine nuts, and vegetable root zests
5. Boil for a good quarter hour, then add the skate to cook
6. Once cooked, dip the skate in the richest part of the sauce to bread it, then grill it basting with a little butter
7. Serve dry with rémoulade sauce in a sauceboat
