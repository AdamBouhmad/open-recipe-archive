## Ingredients

- 1 pound green gooseberries
- 1 glass cold water
- Vinegar (a little)
- Salt (a little)
- 1 half-pound sugar
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Split the green gooseberries on the sides with a toothpick to make the seeds come out
2. Put them in hot water on the fire and leave them until they rise to the surface
3. Remove from heat and add a glass of cold water, a little vinegar and a little salt
4. Leave them in this water until they are cold so they have time to become green again
5. Remove them to put in fresh water
6. While they are cooling, put a half-pound of sugar in a pan with a glass of water
7. Bring to boil and skim until it is clear
8. Add the gooseberries well drained
9. Bring to a gentle boil without letting them do more than simmer
10. Remove them with a skimmer and put them in the compote dish
11. Finish cooking the sugar until it has the consistency of syrup
12. Put it over the gooseberries
