## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 curly chicories
- a piece of butter
- water
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a little nutmeg
- 3 eggs for the liaison
- bread

## Directions

1. Slice 4 or 5 curly chicories; be careful to put in as little of the white cores as possible
2. Pass your sliced chicory in a piece of butter
3. When your chicory is well passed, do not let it brown, moisten your chicory with water
4. When your chicory has boiled for three quarters of an hour, at the moment of serving your soup (to which you will have added salt, coarse pepper and a little nutmeg), bind it with a liaison of 3 eggs, and pour it over your bread at the moment of serving
