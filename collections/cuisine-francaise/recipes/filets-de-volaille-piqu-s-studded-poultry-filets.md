## Ingredients

- three hens
- butter
- jellied stock (three skimming spoons)
- buttered paper
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the filets with the end of the wing from three hens
2. Pare and stud them
3. Secure the wing end under your filet to make it bulge and give it grace
4. Butter your casserole
5. Put in jellied stock the size of three skimming spoons
6. Arrange your filets on top with a round of buttered paper
7. One hour before serving, put them over the fire
8. They must boil neither too fast nor too slowly
9. Place good heat on the lid
10. Watch over your filets
11. After three-quarters of an hour, if your jellied stock was not sufficiently reduced, put your casserole over more ardent heat and let it reduce to glaze
12. At the moment of serving, dip the studded side into your glaze
13. Arrange your filets on your dish and put underneath a worked Spanish sauce to which you will add the glaze from your filets
