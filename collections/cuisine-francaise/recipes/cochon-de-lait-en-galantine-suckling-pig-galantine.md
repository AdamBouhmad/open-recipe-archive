## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- forcemeat
- ham lardons
- lard lardons
- truffle lardons
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- lard slices

## Directions

1. Once well scalded, bone it completely, spread it on a white cloth, and put a good seasoned forcemeat on top, spread to the thickness of a large coin
2. Put on this forcemeat a row of ham lardons, one of lard, one of truffles, one of hard-boiled egg yolks
3. Cover all these lardons with a little forcemeat
4. Roll up the suckling pig, being careful not to disturb the lardons
5. Wrap it in slices of lard
