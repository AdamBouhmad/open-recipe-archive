## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Butter
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Garlic cloves
- Shallots
- Estragon leaves
- Cloves
- Laurel leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Flour
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter mixed with flour
- Parsley blanched and chopped
- Verjuice

## Directions

1. Prepare lamb chops as in previous recipes
2. Put them in a saucepan with a little butter, a bouquet of parsley and chives, garlic cloves, two shallots, estragon leaves, two cloves, half a laurel leaf, thyme and basil
3. Pass over the fire and add a small pinch of flour
4. Moisten with a glass of white wine and a little bouillon, season with salt and pepper
5. Simmer until the sauce is reduced and the chops are cooked
6. Dress the chops on the serving plate
7. Pass the sauce through a tamis and degrease it
8. Return to the fire with butter kneaded with a pinch of flour
9. Add a good pinch of parsley blanched in boiling water, well pressed and chopped very fine, with half a spoonful of verjuice
10. Turn until it is bound like a white sauce and serve over the chops
