## Ingredients

- small onions
- sugar
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Peel your small onions carefully, taking care not to cut the head or tail too much and not to damage the skin
2. Butter the bottom of your saucepan and place the head of your small onions upside down
3. Add sugar the size of a walnut and broth or water up to the tail of the small onions
4. Put them over a hot fire
5. When the liquid is reduced three-quarters, reduce the heat to low and let them glaze
6. Use them when necessary
