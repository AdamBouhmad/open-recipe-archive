## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds of sugar
- 1 pound of barberries, destemmed
- 1 glass of water

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar the same way as for pear marmalade
2. Prepare the barberries by placing them in a saucepan with a glass of water and boil until they become jam-like
3. Pass the mixture through a sieve and press it firmly until only the skins remain in the sieve
4. Return what has passed through the sieve to the fire and boil, stirring constantly until the marmalade is ready to stick to the pan
5. Mix the marmalade with the cooked sugar and stir together until ready to boil
6. Put into jars
