## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked hen (poularde)
- Sauce, thickened with roux or white sauce like béchamel, or sauce à la reine

## Directions

1. Take hens that have been removed from the table and cut them into fillets
2. Heat them in a good thickened sauce made with roux or white sauce like béchamel, or sauce à la reine
