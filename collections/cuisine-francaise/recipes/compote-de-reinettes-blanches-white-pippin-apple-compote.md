## Ingredients

- white pippin apples
- water
- lemon juice
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. You peel some apples and you cut them in half, after having removed the skin
2. You put them in an earthenware vessel where there is water and a lemon juice, so that they keep their whiteness
3. You clarify a half pound of sugar; as soon as the syrup is well clarified, you throw the apple halves in the sugar; take care to turn the apples; when you feel the fork sink in them, you will remove them from the syrup
4. Pass the syrup through a silk sieve, and make it reduce; you will boil it again; then you will pour it on the apples; serve them cold or hot
5. If you want to decorate them, take the skin of a red calville apple, and make a design on the halves
