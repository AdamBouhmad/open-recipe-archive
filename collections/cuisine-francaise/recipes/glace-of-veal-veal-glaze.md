## Ingredients

- One veal round, cut into four pieces
- Three chickens
- Many whole vegetables
- Consommé
- Scraps or meat debris (alternative)
- Bouillon or water (alternative)
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Cut a veal round into four pieces and add three chickens
2. Add many whole vegetables
3. Fill a casserole with consommé and make everything foam
4. Place the casserole on a gentle fire to simmer for three or four hours until the meat is cooked
5. Pass your glaze through a fine cloth so it becomes clear
6. Alternatively, you can make it with meat scraps or debris that you put in a casserole with many vegetables, bouillon or water
7. Make the meat foam and simmer until the meats are cooked
8. When the liquid is passed through cloth, put your glaze in a casserole on a hot stove and reduce it until it becomes thick like a sauce
9. Avoid dark meats such as game, mutton, or beef, because your glaze would become brown
10. Do not add salt to the glaze, as the reduction alone provides the seasoning
