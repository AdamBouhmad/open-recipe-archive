## Ingredients

- Onion
- Butter
- Cooked tripe, cut into squares
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Bouillon
- Mustard

## Directions

1. Cut onion into dice and pass it over the fire with a bit of butter
2. When it is half cooked, add the cooked tripe that has been cut into squares and seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Add a little vinegar and a bit of bouillon
4. Let boil for half an hour
5. When serving, add a bit of mustard
