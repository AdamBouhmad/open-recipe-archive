## Ingredients

- Soft bread crumb (soft-boiled is best)
- Water
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter, about the size of half an egg (more or less, depending on how strong the panade should be)
- Two or three eggs for binding (depending on the quantity of panade)

## Directions

1. Put the bread crumb in a small earthenware pot or other vessel with water, salt, and a bit of coarse pepper
2. Add the butter
3. Simmer everything together for one hour
4. At the moment of serving, add a binding of two or three eggs
5. Take care that your panade does not boil when the binding is in it
