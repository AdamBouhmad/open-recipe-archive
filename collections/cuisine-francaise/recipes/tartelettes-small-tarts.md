## Ingredients

- pâte feuilletée (puff pastry)
- crème de frangipane or jam
- egg for glazing
- sugar and cochineal for glazing

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry as indicated previously
2. Roll it out to the thickness of a small coin and cut small bases with a pastry cutter
3. Place them on small pie molds and on the paste put a small spoonful of frangipane cream or jams of your choice, provided it is not jelly
4. Cover with a few bands of paste
5. Bake for half an hour in the oven
6. Glaze with sugar and cochineal
