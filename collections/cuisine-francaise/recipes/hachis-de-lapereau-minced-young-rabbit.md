## Ingredients

- Four young rabbits
- Béchamelle, two or three spoonfuls
- Croutons
- Poached eggs
- Small larded fillets, glazed

## Directions

1. Roast four young rabbits on the spit
2. When cooked and cold, remove the fillets and the largest part of the thighs
3. Remove the nerves and skin
4. Mince all the meat, neither too fine nor too coarse
5. Place the minced meat in a casserole with two or three spoonfuls of hot béchamelle
6. Mix the béchamelle with the minced meat; it should not be too thin
7. Keep it warm in a bain-marie
8. Arrange croutons around the dish
9. At the moment of serving, place the minced meat in the center
10. You may place poached eggs around it and small larded, glazed fillets between the eggs
11. If you do not have béchamelle, reduce velouté into which you put a liaison of three egg yolks
