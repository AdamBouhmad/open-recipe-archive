## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, sliced
- twelve large onions
- butter (two spoonfuls)
- Spanish sauce (3 spoonfuls)
- consommé (3 spoonfuls)
- coarse pepper
- salt
- croûtons
- flour (if sauce is not available)
- broth or water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the meat from the leg of mutton as previously described
2. Cut twelve large onions in half, remove the head and tail
3. Cut them into slices forming half-circles, removing the core
4. Put a large piece of butter (about the size of an egg) in a pan
5. Pass the onions over a fairly hot fire
6. When the onions are golden, add three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and three spoonfuls of consommé, a little coarse pepper and salt
7. Reduce the onion until slightly thick
8. Pour it over the sliced mutton, sauté to mix with the onion, and keep warm without boiling
9. Arrange when ready to serve with croûtons like the preceding ones
10. If sauce is not available, mix one half-spoonful of flour with the butter and onion, moisten with broth or water, add salt and coarse pepper, and when ready to combine with the sliced mutton, add a dash of vinegar
