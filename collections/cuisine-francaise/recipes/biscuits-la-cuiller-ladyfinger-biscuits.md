## Ingredients

- Four eggs (weight as measure)
- Sugar (weight equal to eggs)
- Flour (weight equal to eggs)
- Fine sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Make the batter the same way as the previous recipe, with the weight of four eggs being sufficient
2. Do not put them in molds
3. When the batter is made, take one spoonful for each biscuit and lay them lengthwise on sheets of white paper
4. Sprinkle fine sugar over them
5. Bake in a slightly cooler oven than the previous biscuits
6. When cooked, remove them immediately from the paper with a knife
