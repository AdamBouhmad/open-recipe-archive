## Ingredients

- Salmon steak
- Cold mayonnaise
- Aspic for glazing
- Croutons
- Capers
- Gherkins

## Directions

1. When your salmon steak is cooked, you can serve it whole or in pieces
2. Arrange the pieces on your plate
3. Mask them with cold mayonnaise
4. Take care to add aspic so it will glaze
5. Decorate the edge of the plate with croutons, capers, and gherkins
