## Ingredients

- apples
- about a quarter pound of sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut apples in half and remove the cores
2. Arrange them in a pan with the skin side up
3. Add approximately a quarter pound of sugar and enough water for them to cook
4. When cooked on one side, turn them over
5. When fully cooked and the syrup is sufficiently reduced, arrange them in a serving dish with the syrup on top
6. Serve hot or cold as you prefer
