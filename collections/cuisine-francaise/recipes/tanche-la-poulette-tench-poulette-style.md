## Ingredients

- tench
- butter
- flour
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- parsley
- chives
- small onions
- mushrooms
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put your tench for a minute in a pot of almost boiling water; remove it with your knife
2. Remove its slime and scales; cut it into pieces and let it disgorge
3. Put butter in a saucepan; warm it with your pieces of tench
4. Sauté them in the butter; add a spoonful of flour that you mix together
5. Moisten your stew with a bottle of white wine, salt, coarse pepper, a bay leaf, a bouquet of parsley and chives, small onions, and mushrooms
6. Cook your stew fairly quickly; when it is cooked, add a liaison of three egg yolks
7. Remove the bay leaf and the bouquet; check that it tastes good
