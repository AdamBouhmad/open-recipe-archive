## Ingredients

- butter
- six eggs
- three or four spoonfuls of coulis
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter to butter the inside of six copper goblets or small ramekins
2. Take six eggs, whites and yolks, and dilute them with three or four spoonfuls of coulis
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Pass through a sieve and put into the goblets (do not fill them completely)
5. Cook in a bain-marie with the water boiling gently
6. When they are firm, gently pass a knife around the edges to detach them from the goblet and turn them out onto the plate
7. Serve with clear jus
