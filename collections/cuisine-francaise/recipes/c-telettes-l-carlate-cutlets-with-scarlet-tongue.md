## Ingredients

- cutlets
- scarlet tongue lardons
- lard lardons
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- lard strips
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- bouillon
- buttered paper
- scarlet tongue
- reduced espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets and trim them as the previous ones
2. Lard them with a lardon of scarlet tongue and a lardon of lard, that is to say several lardons in the same cutlet, half one, half the other
3. Make sure your lardons are seasoned with salt, pepper, spices
4. When your cutlets are well larded, put lard strips in a saucepan with a few veal slices
5. Place them on top
6. Add two carrots, two onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme
7. Cover your cutlets with lard strips and a round of buttered paper
8. Fill a small spoon with bouillon
9. Simmer them for two hours with heat above and below
10. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, and arrange them in crowns, putting a piece of glazed scarlet tongue between each cutlet
11. Cut your pieces of tongue like a salmis croûton
12. Your piece of tongue must be hot and cover the meat of your cutlet
13. Use reduced espagnole sauce
