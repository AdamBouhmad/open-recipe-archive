## Ingredients

- sheep's kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- sauce à l'échalotte

## Directions

1. Cook them on the grill
2. Open them through the middle and pass a small skewer through them
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. When cooked, put a sauce à l'échalotte underneath
