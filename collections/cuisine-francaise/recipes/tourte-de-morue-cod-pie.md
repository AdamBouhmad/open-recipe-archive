## Ingredients

- cod
- pastry
- butter
- coarse pepper
- bouquet garni
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Cook cod in water, drain and let cool
2. Place the cod in flakes into the pastry with butter, coarse pepper, and a bouquet garni
3. When the pie is fully cooked, remove the bouquet and add a cream sauce like the previous recipe
