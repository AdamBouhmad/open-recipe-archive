## Ingredients

- Pheasant
- Lardons
- Cooking liquid from the pheasant
- Lentil purée

## Directions

1. Empty, truss, and lard your pheasant as you would for the stewed pheasant
2. Cook it the same way
3. At the moment of serving, drain it, untruss it, and arrange it on your dish
4. Mask it with a lentil purée
5. Pass the cooking liquid through a strainer and put it in your purée
6. Reduce it so that it has the taste of pheasant smoke
