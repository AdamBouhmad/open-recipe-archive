## Ingredients

- Lamb cutlets
- Butter
- Ice
- Espagnole sauce, worked
- Consommé

## Directions

1. Cut and trim your lamb cutlets as you would mutton cutlets
2. Season them after trimming well
3. Place them in a sautoir or shallow pan
4. Add enough melted butter for them to bathe in it
5. At the moment of serving, place them on high heat
6. When the butter has well heated them on one side, turn them to the other
7. When you feel they are firm to the touch, remove the butter and add a good piece of ice
8. Toss them in your melted ice
9. Arrange them in a crown
10. Pour two deglazing spoonfuls of worked espagnole sauce into your sautoir, and one spoonful of consommé
11. Stir your sauce to detach the ice
12. Strain it through tamis cloth and pour it over your cutlets
