## Ingredients

- a little more than half a litron of flour
- a quarteron of butter
- a pinch of salt
- a glass of water
- half a setier of cream
- two eggs
- two spoonfuls of flour
- very little salt
- one ounce of sugar

## Directions

1. Put on a table a little more than half a litron of flour with a quarteron of butter, a pinch of salt, and a glass of water
2. Knead everything together to make a firm paste
3. Roll out this paste with a roller to the thickness of a half-finger
4. Cut it into pieces the size of small pie molds
5. Press your fingers on each piece and raise them on the edges by pinching all around to make a border a finger's height and the thickness of a twenty-four sous coin
6. Put them in the oven at a moderate heat for a quarter hour
7. Then add half a setier of cream diluted with two eggs and two spoonfuls of flour, very little salt, and one ounce of sugar
8. When they are cooked, sprinkle fine sugar over them
