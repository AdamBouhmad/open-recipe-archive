## Ingredients

- one pound and a half of sugar
- one pound of barberries
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Cook one pound and a half of sugar in the same manner as for pear jam. Then add barberry prepared as follows: have one pound of barberries completely stripped of stems, which you put in a pan with one glass of water and bring to a boil until it turns to jam. Pass it through a sieve and press firmly until only the skins remain in the sieve. Put back on the fire what has passed through the sieve.
