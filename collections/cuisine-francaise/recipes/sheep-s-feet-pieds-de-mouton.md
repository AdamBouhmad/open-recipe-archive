## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Butter
- Bouquet garni
- Egg yolks
- Milk or cream
- Verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. After boiling them well in water, peel off any remaining hair and remove the large bones.
2. Place them in a saucepan with a good piece of butter and a bouquet garni, and let them cook a few turns on the fire.
3. When cooked and the sauce reduced, do not degrease them.
4. Mix three egg yolks with milk or cream, and add to the sauce.
5. Let it bind on the fire, and when serving, add a dash of verjuice or vinegar.
