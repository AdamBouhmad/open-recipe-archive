## Ingredients

- Fifteen to twenty tomatoes
- A little bouillon
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Velouté
- Butter, amount of an egg

## Directions

1. Put fifteen to twenty tomatoes in a saucepan with a little bouillon and salt and ground pepper
2. Cook and reduce them
3. When your tomatoes are thick, pass them like a purée through muslin
4. If your sauce was too thin, put it in a saucepan and reduce it
5. Pour four or five tablespoons into a little velouté
6. At the moment of serving, add butter the size of an egg and let it melt in your sauce
7. Before serving, check if it is well seasoned and of good flavor
