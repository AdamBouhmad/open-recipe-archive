## Ingredients

- Pheasant, plucked and cleaned
- Medium lardons, seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, and quatre épices
- Lard strips (bardes)
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Game essence

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean your pheasant, then singe it
2. Tuck the thighs inside; truss it
3. Lard it with medium lardons seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, and a little quatre épices, larder the breast and thighs
4. Cover it with a lard strip and tie it
5. Place lard strips in your casserole with the pheasant on top
6. Cover a cooking vessel which you will moisten with half white wine, half bouillon
7. Simmer it for two hours
8. At the moment of serving, drain it, untruss it, and arrange it on your dish
9. Serve underneath a game essence
