## Ingredients

- mussels
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- flour
- bouillon
- egg yolks
- cream
- verjus

## Directions

1. After washing well, scrape their shells and drain them and put them dry in a pot on good heat from the stove; the heat will make them open, you will shell them afterwards one by one; take care to remove the crabs if you find any.
2. Put your mussels, after having removed them from their shells, in a pot with a piece of good butter, parsley, chopped chives, pass them over the fire; add a small pinch of flour, moisten with a little bouillon; when there is no more sauce, add a liaison of three egg yolks with cream, bind your sauce and add after a dash of verjus.
