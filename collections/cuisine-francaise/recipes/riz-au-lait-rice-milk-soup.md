## Ingredients

- rice, one quarter pound or more
- milk, boiling
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. After having washed a quarter pound of rice, or more, depending on how large your soup is, you have your milk boiling; you put your rice in it; you let it boil on low heat for an hour and a half, and you try that there is enough milk so that your rice bursts at ease, and that it is not in paste: your rice burst ready to serve, you will put the sugar needed so that your soup is good; you will put a little salt in it, that is to say five or six grains: be careful not to cover the vessel in which your rice cooks entirely, because the milk would turn.
