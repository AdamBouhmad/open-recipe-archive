## Ingredients

- Leg of mutton
- Truffles
- Lard
- Salt
- Fine spices
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Veal slices
- Lard strips

## Directions

1. Cut truffles into small lardons and cut lard the same way
2. Mix them together with salt, fine spices, parsley, chives, and a hint of garlic, all minced
3. Lard the leg of mutton all over with the truffles and lard
4. Wrap it in paper for two days so that it does not get air
5. Cook it slowly in a casserole in its own juice, wrapped in slices of veal and lard
6. When cooked, degrease the sauce, add a spoonful of coulis, and serve
