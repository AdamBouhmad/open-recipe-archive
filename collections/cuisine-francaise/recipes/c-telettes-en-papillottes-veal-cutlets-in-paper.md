## Ingredients

- white papers
- veal cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- shallots
- oil or butter

## Directions

1. Wrap veal cutlets in white papers with salt, pepper, parsley, chives, mushrooms, and shallots, all chopped
2. Rub the papers with oil or butter and fold the paper around the cutlet, sealing at the end
3. Butter the paper
4. Cook over low heat on the grill over a sheet of buttered paper
5. Serve with the sauce that is inside the paper
