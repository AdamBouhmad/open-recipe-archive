## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Lard
- Atelet
- Clear Robert sauce

## Directions

1. Gut it and singe it somewhat firmly
2. Clean it well
3. Break the breastbone to break the stomach
4. Bard it and spit it
5. Tie the legs onto the spit
6. If it turns, place an atelet on the back that you attach at each end to the spit
7. This meat must be well cooked but not overdone
8. If serving as a relevé, pour clear Robert sauce underneath or in a sauce boat
