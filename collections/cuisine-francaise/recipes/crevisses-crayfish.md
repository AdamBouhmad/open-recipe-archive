## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salt
- pepper
- two bay leaves
- onion slices
- parsley sprigs
- chives, cut
- white wine or water and vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash your crayfish well
2. Place them in a saucepan with salt, pepper, two bay leaves, onion slices, parsley sprigs, and cut chives
3. Add a bottle of white wine, or water and vinegar
4. Place over a hot fire and sauté them from time to time
5. When they have boiled for a quarter hour, remove from the fire and leave them in their seasoning
6. Reheat them when you wish to serve
