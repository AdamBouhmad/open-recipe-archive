## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the suckling pig on a spit
2. Once well scalded and trussed, cut the skin slightly at the head, shoulders, and thighs so the skin does not tear
3. While cooking, frequently baste it with oil so the skin is crispy
4. Eat it fresh from the spit, otherwise the skin becomes soft and loses its flavor
