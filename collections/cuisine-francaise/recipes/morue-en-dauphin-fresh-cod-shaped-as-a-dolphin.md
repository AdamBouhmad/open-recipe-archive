## Ingredients

- fresh cod
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives, whole
- garlic clove
- bay leaf
- butter
- carp roe (laitances)
- asparagus tips
- mushrooms
- bouquet of parsley and chives
- flour
- white wine
- good lean broth
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Season the cod with salt, pepper, parsley, chives, garlic, and bay leaf
2. Take an iron skewer called a hatelet and pass it through the fresh cod starting at the eyes, through the middle of the body, and finishing at the tail, shaping it to look like a dolphin
3. Place it on a baking dish and baste it with its marinade
4. Cook it in the oven
5. When cooked, remove the skewer and arrange it on the serving platter
6. For the ragout: boil three carp roe briefly in water; also boil asparagus tips briefly in water
7. Put everything in a saucepan with a good piece of butter, mushrooms, and a bouquet of parsley and chives
8. Pass over the fire and add a pinch of flour, then moisten with a glass of white wine and good lean broth
9. When the ragout is cooked and the sauce is reduced and seasoned to taste, add a liaison of three egg yolks and cream
10. Bind the sauce over the fire and serve over the cod
11. For best results, add the asparagus only when the ragout is nearly finished
