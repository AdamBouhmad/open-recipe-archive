## Ingredients

- oysters
- bouillon
- anchovies
- salt

## Directions

1. Blanch them in their water over very low heat; be careful they do not boil, as that would shrivel them.
2. Put them afterwards in fresh water, remove them next to drain well on a sieve, you then have a good fatty bouillon without salt; put two chopped anchovies and the oysters; heat them without letting them boil, and serve as you judge appropriate.
