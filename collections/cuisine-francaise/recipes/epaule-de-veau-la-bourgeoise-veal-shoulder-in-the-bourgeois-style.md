## Ingredients

- one veal shoulder
- half a setier of water
- two spoonfuls of vinegar
- salt
- coarse pepper
- parsley
- chives
- two cloves of garlic
- one bay leaf
- two onions
- two roots
- three cloves
- one piece of butter

## Directions

1. Put a veal shoulder in a terrine with half a setier of water, two spoonfuls of vinegar, salt, coarse pepper, parsley, chives, two cloves of garlic, one bay leaf, two onions and two roots cut in slices, three cloves, one piece of butter.
2. Cover the terrine with a lid and seal the edges with flour mixed with a little water.
3. Cook in the oven for three hours.
4. Degrease the sauce and pass it through a tamis.
5. Serve over the shoulder.
