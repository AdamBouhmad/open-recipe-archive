## Ingredients

- Three pounds of beef brisket
- Four old partridges
- Two pounds of veal knuckle
- One pheasant
- Carrots
- Onions
- Four stalks of celery
- Three cloves
- A small bouquet of fennel
- Three young partridges for roasting
- Breadcrumbs, three times the size of an egg
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Put the beef brisket, old partridges, veal knuckle, pheasant, carrots, onions, celery, cloves, and fennel in a medium-sized pot
2. Take the bouillon from this for your soup
3. Roast three young partridges on a spit
4. Pound them cold in a mortar with breadcrumbs that have been soaked in bouillon
5. Moisten your pounded partridges with bouillon
6. When well pounded, pass them through a sieve
7. When your purée is passed, add bouillon so it is neither too thick nor too thin
8. Place on a gentle fire; it must not boil
9. Soak croutons with bouillon as for bread soup
