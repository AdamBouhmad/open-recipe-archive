## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- coarse pepper
- 1 glass of white wine
- juice of 2 lemons
- zest from lemon peel
- shallots, minced
- garlic, pounded
- bread crust, grated

## Directions

1. When ready to eat a salmi at table, piece the partridges and place them on a silver platter set over a spirit lamp
2. Add salt, coarse pepper, 1 glass of white wine, juice of 2 lemons with a little zest of the peel
3. Add minced shallots brought from the kitchen and a little pounded garlic if desired
4. Sprinkle grated bread crust over the salmi
5. Let it simmer for 10 minutes and serve
6. The salt and coarse pepper should dominate the flavors
