## Ingredients

- a little lard
- a few slices of veal
- three or four pieces of carp
- one onion
- two shallots
- one carrot root
- one glass of white wine
- two good spoonfuls of coulis
- as much bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Place lard, veal slices, carp pieces, onion, shallots, and carrot in the bottom of a saucepan
2. Sweat over low heat for half an hour
3. When it begins to stick to the pan, moisten with one glass of white wine and two good spoonfuls of coulis
4. Add as much bouillon
5. Boil and reduce over low heat to the consistency of a sauce
6. Skim off fat and pass through a sieve
7. Season with salt and coarse pepper
