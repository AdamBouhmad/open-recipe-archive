## Ingredients

- Roux
- Herbs or onion, diced
- Bouillon or water
- Lentils
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Make a light roux.
2. Add fine herbs or onion cut into small dice.
3. Pass them through the roux and moisten with a little bouillon or water.
4. When it is diluted, add the lentils with salt and black pepper.
5. Serve them very hot.
