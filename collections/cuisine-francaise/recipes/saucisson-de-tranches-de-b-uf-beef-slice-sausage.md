## Ingredients

- one piece of beef the width of two hands and thickness of two fingers
- beef fat
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- two shallots
- basil leaves

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef cut the width of two hands and thick as two fingers
2. Cut it in two while keeping it the same width
3. Pound it to flatten it further
4. Trim the edges by cutting them slightly to make them even
5. Chop the trimmed edges with beef fat, parsley, chives, mushrooms, two shallots, and a few basil leaves, all chopped very fine
