## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lard
- Veal slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove the tough outer leaves from the lettuce.
2. Wash and blanch the lettuce in plenty of salted water.
3. When it has boiled for twenty minutes, refresh it and press it so that no water remains.
4. Season the heart of the lettuce with salt and black pepper and tie it with string.
5. Arrange strips of lard in the bottom of a saucepan with a few slices of veal, two carrots sliced, three onions, two cloves, and a bay leaf.
6. Place the lettuces on top and cover them with lard.
7. Moisten with bouillon and a little meat drippings from the pot.
8. Simmer for one hour.
9. When the lettuce is cooked, drain it on a white cloth and press it in the same cloth to extract the moisture.
10. Trim it, glaze it, and arrange it in crowns with a glazed croûton approximately the size of the lettuce.
11. Serve with Spanish sauce underneath.
