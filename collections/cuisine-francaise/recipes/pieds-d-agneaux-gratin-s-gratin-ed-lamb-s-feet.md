## Ingredients

- dozen lamb feet
- eighteen or twenty small white onions
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese
- butter
- egg yolks
- veal stock

## Directions

1. Cook a dozen lamb feet and eighteen or twenty small white onions in a good braise
2. Make a small gratin with bread crumbs, a little grated cheese, a piece of butter, and three egg yolks, mix all together
3. Spread it on the bottom of the serving dish
4. Put this dish on hot ashes to cook and slightly brown the gratin
5. Arrange the lamb feet on top, interspersed with small onions
6. Let it simmer a little more over the fire
7. Skim off the fat and serve with a good veal stock sauce on top
