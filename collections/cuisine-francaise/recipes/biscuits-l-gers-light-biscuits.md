## Ingredients

- Ten eggs
- Three quarterons of fine sugar
- Orange blossoms, pralined
- Green lemon zest, finely chopped
- Six ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of five eggs in a bowl with a little pralined orange blossoms and finely chopped green lemon zest
2. Add three quarterons of very fine sugar and beat everything together until the sugar is well mixed with the yolks and slightly liquid
3. Whip the whites of ten eggs until stiff peaks form and mix with the sugar
4. Add six ounces of flour gradually and gently, stirring as you go with a whisk
5. Dress them in buttered molds, sprinkle with fine sugar, and bake in a gentle oven
