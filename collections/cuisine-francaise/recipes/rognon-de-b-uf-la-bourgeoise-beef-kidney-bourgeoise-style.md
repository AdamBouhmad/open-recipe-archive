## Ingredients

- Beef kidney
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chives, minced
- Garlic, minced
- Vinegar
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Cut the kidney into thin slices
2. Pass it over the fire with a piece of butter, salt, pepper, minced chives and a pinch of minced garlic
3. When cooked, add a dash of vinegar and a bit of coulis
4. Do not let it boil anymore, for fear it will shrivel
