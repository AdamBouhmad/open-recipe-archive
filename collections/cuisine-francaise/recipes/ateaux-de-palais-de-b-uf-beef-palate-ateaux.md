## Ingredients

- beef palate cooked in blanc
- atelet sauce
- veal breast

## Directions

1. When your beef palates are cooked in blanc, cut them into squares measuring ten lines in all directions
2. Put them in an atelet sauce
3. Toss them in it and let them cool
4. Have a veal breast that you will have cooked in the pot and which will be cold
5. Cut small thin squares from it the same size as your palates
