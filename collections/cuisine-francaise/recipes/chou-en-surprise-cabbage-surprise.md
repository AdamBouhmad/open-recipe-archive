## Ingredients

- one whole cabbage
- water
- chestnuts
- sausages
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- root vegetables
- onions
- bouquet garni
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a good whole cabbage and cook it for a quarter hour in boiling water, then remove it to fresh water
2. Press it well in your hands without breaking the leaves
3. Place the leaves on a table and separate all of them to remove the core
4. In place of the core, put chestnuts and sausages
5. Put all the leaves back as they were, so that it does not appear that there is anything inside
6. Tie up the cabbage well and cook it in a light bed made with bouillon, a little salt, pepper, root vegetables, onions, and a bouquet garni
7. When it is cooked, put it to drain and serve with a good sauce containing butter
