## Ingredients

- Half a pound of well-cleaned rice
- Warm water
- Bouillon at a rolling boil
- Two spoonfuls of veal blonde sauce, or one spoonful of jus
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of well-cleaned rice
2. Wash it four or five times in warm water while rubbing well
3. Then wash in cold water
4. Moisten it with bouillon at a rolling boil so the rice does not turn to mush
5. Boil for two hours on low heat
6. Ensure your bouillon is not too salty due to reduction so it acquires a beautiful color
7. Add two spoonfuls of veal blonde sauce or one spoonful of jus
8. Carolina rice is the best for cooking
