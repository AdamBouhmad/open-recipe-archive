## Ingredients

- 8 to 12 head lettuces, depending on size
- meat forcemeat seasoned to taste
- flour
- white wine
- oil
- salt
- egg, beaten
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the lettuces for half an hour in water, then remove them to fresh water and press them well with your hands
2. Spread out on a table the leaves of each lettuce without separating them
3. Put in the middle a forcemeat of meat seasoned to taste, as you will find below for small pâtés
4. Wrap the forcemeat with the lettuce leaves, tie them and cook them in a small braising pan
5. When they are cooked, drain and press them in a cloth
6. Dip them in a batter made with flour, white wine and a spoonful of oil, a little salt, and fry them to a nice color
7. You can also dip them in beaten egg, coat them with bread crumbs; being stuffed in this manner and cooked in the braising pan, they serve to garnish meat entrées
