## Ingredients

- butter
- breadcrumbs
- Gruyère cheese
- eight or ten eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Spread butter as thick as a knife blade in the bottom of the serving plate
2. Scatter very thin slices of breadcrumbs and small slices of Gruyère cheese all over it
3. Then add eight or ten eggs
4. Season with a little salt, nutmeg, and coarse pepper
5. Cook over a small fire on a stove
