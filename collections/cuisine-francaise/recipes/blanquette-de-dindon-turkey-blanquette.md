## Ingredients

- Roasted turkey
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Velouté
- Consommé
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. When the turkey is cooked on the spit and cooled, remove the meat from the breast
2. Slice and flatten the meat with the knife blade, trim the corners, and prepare the white meat as neatly as possible
3. Arrange the meat in a saucepan
4. Turn the mushrooms and cut them into pieces, place them in water and lemon juice to keep them white
5. When you have sufficient mushrooms for your blanquette, put a small piece of butter in a saucepan with a little lemon juice and your mushrooms
6. Sauté them on the fire; when they no longer have water and the butter is in oil, add six spoonfuls of velouté and as much consommé
7. Reduce the whole by half, taking care to skim and degrease your sauce, and place it over the white meat of your turkey in a small saucepan
8. At the moment of serving, place your blanquette on the fire with a liaison of one or two egg yolks, according to how rich your blanquette is, and a piece of good butter the size of a walnut
9. Check that it is well seasoned
