## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- milk
- salt
- bread

## Directions

1. Use fewer onions than in the preceding recipe
2. Cook them gently over a low fire with butter until they are cooked without being colored
3. Bring milk to a boil and add it with the onion seasoned with a little salt
4. Put sliced bread thinly in the serving dish with a part of your broth
5. Cover it and place it on a little hot ashes
6. When your bread is well soaked, add the rest of the broth
7. Serve
