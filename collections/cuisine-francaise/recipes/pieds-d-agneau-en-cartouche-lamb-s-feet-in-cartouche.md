## Ingredients

- lamb feet
- blanc (white cooking liquid)
- fines herbes à papillotes (fine herbs in papillotes)
- lemon
- paper
- oil
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. When your feet are cooked in your blanc, trim them
2. Arrange fine herbs in papillotes
3. Put your lamb feet in your hot fine herbs
4. Let them take two or three turns over the fire
5. Squeeze a whole lemon over them
6. Put them to cool on a platter
7. After that, cut long squares of paper large enough to well envelope your foot
8. Oil your paper
9. When your feet are cold, introduce some of your fine herbs into the empty space and around the foot
10. Wrap it in a very thin bard of lard
11. Roll it in your paper so it forms a cartouche
12. Be careful to close the ends so that being on the grill the seasoning does not escape
13. Half an hour before serving, put them on the grill over a gentle fire
14. The paper should have a nice color
15. Serve them dry or with a clear juice beneath
