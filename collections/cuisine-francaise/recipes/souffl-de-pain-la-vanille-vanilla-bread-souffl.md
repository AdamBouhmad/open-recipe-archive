## Ingredients

- One pint of cream (more if the soufflé is large)
- One stick of vanilla
- Six ounces of sugar
- One pound soft bread
- Butter (size of two eggs)
- Two whole eggs
- Four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the pint of cream; add the vanilla stick and six ounces of sugar, bringing it to a boil three or four times
2. Take a one-pound soft bread; remove the crumb and place it in the hot cream
3. Let it soak until the mixture is cold
4. Put the crumb in a white cloth and press it to extract the cream
5. Place it in a mortar with the vanilla and pound well
6. Add butter (size of two eggs), two whole eggs, and four egg yolks
7. When well combined, pass it through a sieve or quenelle tamis, pressing with a wooden spoon
8. Place the bread purée in a saucepan
9. Whip four egg whites as for biscuit and mix them with the bread crumb
10. Place the soufflé in a silver saucepan and put it in a gentle oven
11. You may also place it on red ash with a camp oven over it
12. Once cooked, serve immediately
