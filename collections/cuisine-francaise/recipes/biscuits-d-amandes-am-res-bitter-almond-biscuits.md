## Ingredients

- Two ounces of bitter almonds
- One ounce of sweet almonds
- Fine sugar
- Flour
- Egg yolks and whites (proportional amounts)

## Directions

1. Make the same way as sweet almond biscuits, but for two ounces of bitter almonds, use one ounce of sweet almonds
2. Regulate the dose to make the quantity you wish, putting everything else in proportion as stated for sweet almonds
