## Ingredients

- Two medium chickens
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- A pinch of garlic
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Juice
- Three spoonfuls of verjuice
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Empty two medium chickens, heat the legs to pluck them
2. Cut off the spurs and push the legs into the body of the chickens and brown them on the fire, pluck them, and cut each chicken in two
3. Flatten them a little with the cleaver
4. Marinate for one hour with hot butter, parsley, chives, a pinch of garlic, mushrooms, all chopped very finely, salt, and pepper
5. Keep as much marinade on the chickens as possible and coat them with breadcrumbs
6. Grill over low heat, basting them with the remaining marinade
7. When they are cooked a beautiful color, serve with a sauce made with a little juice, three spoonfuls of verjuice, salt, pepper, a little chopped parsley, and two egg yolks
8. Thicken without boiling
