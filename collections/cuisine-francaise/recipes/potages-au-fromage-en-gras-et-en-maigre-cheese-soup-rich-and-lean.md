## Ingredients

- broth
- cabbage
- other vegetables
- salt
- half a pound or three quarters of a pound of Gruyère cheese
- bread
- butter
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Make a broth as explained for lean soups
2. Use more cabbage than other vegetables
3. When finished and passed through a sieve, add very little salt
4. Take the serving dish that should go on the fire
5. Grate half of the Gruyère cheese and cut the other half into thin slices
6. Put a little grated cheese in the bottom of the dish with some small pieces of butter
7. Cover with very thinly sliced bread
8. Then put a layer of sliced cheese, then a layer of bread covered with grated cheese, put another layer of bread, and finish with sliced cheese and small pieces of butter
9. Moisten with part of your broth
10. Simmer until a small crust forms at the bottom of the dish and no broth remains
11. Before serving, add more broth and a little coarse pepper
12. This soup should be served somewhat thick
13. For the rich version, make it the same way using good rich cabbage broth, do not degrease the broth too much, and add no butter
