## Ingredients

- Spinach, blanched
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Velouté sauce
- Consommé
- Croutons
- Flour
- Bouillon or cream

## Directions

1. After the spinach is blanched (see English-Style Spinach), chop it and put it in a saucepan with a good piece of butter; add salt and coarse pepper.
2. Place the spinach on the fire; when well cooked, pour over it about five tablespoons of velouté and as much consommé.
3. If the spinach is thin, reduce it and dress it on the plate with croutons around it.
4. If you prefer it with bouillon or cream, when well cooked, add about two teaspoons of flour; mix it with the spinach and moisten it with bouillon or cream; if too wet, reduce it.
