## Ingredients

- 1 half-pound strawberries
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Clean and wash a half-pound of strawberries
2. Let them drain and pass them through a sieve to make marmalade
3. Put on the fire a pound of sugar with a glass of water
4. Bring to boil and skim well
5. Continue boiling until, dipping the skimmer in it and shaking it, long sparks come out
6. Add your strawberry marmalade to dilute it with the sugar, stirring constantly on a medium fire without letting it boil
7. Put it in pots
8. You may regulate the quantity based on how much you wish to make
