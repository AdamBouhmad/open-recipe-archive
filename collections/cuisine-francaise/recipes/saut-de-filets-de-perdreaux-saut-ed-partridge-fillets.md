## Ingredients

- Six partridges of the same size
- Three-quarters of a pound of butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Spanish sauce with game essence
- Bouillon
- White wine
- Flour
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take six partridges of the same size
2. Remove the fillets and trim them
3. Remove the tough skin
4. Place them skin-side down on the table
5. Slide your knife blade between the skin and the flesh from one end to the other, always keeping the blade inclined toward the skin so as not to remove too much flesh
6. Put your fillets in your sauté pan
7. Season with salt and coarse pepper
8. Heat three-quarters of a pound of butter and pour it over them
9. Ten minutes before serving, put them over a hot fire
10. When they are stiffened on one side, turn them over to the other side
11. Leave them only an instant
12. Tilt your pan so that the butter separates from your fillets
13. Arrange them in a crown on your platter with a glazed crouton between each fillet
14. For sauce, use Spanish sauce worked with game essence
15. If you have no sauce or game essence, attach the juice that your fillets have released into your pan
16. When the base is blonde, remove half the butter
17. Add one spoonful of flour and mix it in
18. Add a glass of bouillon or a little juice and half a glass of white wine
19. Make your sauce bubble a few times
20. Strain through a fine sieve
21. Add a little coarse pepper and lemon juice
22. Check that it is properly salted
23. Put it under your fillets
