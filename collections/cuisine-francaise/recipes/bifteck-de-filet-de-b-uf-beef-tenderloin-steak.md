## Ingredients

- beef filet
- salt
- coarse pepper
- warm butter
- maître-d'hôtel sauce or piquant sauce or reduced espagnole sauce or anchovy butter sauce or tomato sauce or clear jus
- cornichons or potatoes
- butter for sautéing potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut your filet on the full, that is to say your piece, thick of six lines, forms the round.
2. Beat it and remove the outer edges, and do not leave any skin.
3. Ensure your steak is somewhat fatty.
4. Once your piece is prepared, season it with salt and coarse pepper.
5. Dip it in warm butter.
6. Grill it at the moment of serving.
7. Place underneath a maître-d'hôtel, a piquant sauce, a reduced espagnole, an anchovy butter sauce, a tomato sauce, or a clear jus, according to taste.
8. Serve with these sauces either cornichons or potatoes that you peel and sauté in butter until they are golden.
9. Powder them with salt and arrange them around your steak.
10. Ensure your steak goes over a great fire; it must be cooked rare, that is to say it should be bleeding.
