## Ingredients

- geese as desired
- goose fat from cooking
- lard
- bay leaves
- salt

## Directions

1. Scald and clean the geese and place them on the spit to cook only three-quarters of the way through
2. Set aside the fat that renders during cooking
3. Let the geese cool, cut them in four by removing the thighs and keeping the breast with the wings
4. Arrange them tightly packed in a pot of fat, placing three or four bay leaves and salt between each layer
5. Melt the goose fat that was set aside with much lard; there must be enough to cover the wings and thighs completely
6. Place this fat in the pot and cover with parchment only twenty-four hours after everything is completely cold
7. Store them in a dry place
8. When you wish to use them, remove them from the pot and their fat as needed, wash them in hot water before using them as desired
