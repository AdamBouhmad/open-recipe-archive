## Ingredients

- Butter, amount of half an egg
- Three cloves of hot small pepper, well crushed
- Powder of saffron from India or turmeric, amount of a thimble
- Four tablespoons of the preceding velvet sauce, without binding
- Two tablespoons of bouillon
- Butter, amount of an egg

## Directions

1. Heat the butter in a saucepan until it is slightly fried
2. Add the crushed hot pepper and turmeric powder
3. Add four tablespoons of the preceding velvet sauce without binding and two tablespoons of bouillon
4. Reduce and degrease your sauce
5. Transfer it to another saucepan and keep it warm in a bain-marie
6. At the moment of serving, add butter the size of an egg and stir well with your sauce
7. You can also bind it; it will be good with many things
