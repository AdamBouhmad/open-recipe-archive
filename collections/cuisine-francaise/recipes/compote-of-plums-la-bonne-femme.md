## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of sugar
- one glass of water
- one pound of nearly ripe plums

## Directions

1. Boil for a quarter hour a quarter pound of sugar with a glass of water, be careful to skim; when it becomes syrup, put one pound of nearly ripe plums in it
2. Make a few boils until the plums are cooked, remove the scum and arrange them in the compote dish
3. If the syrup is too thin, reduce it before pouring it over the plums
