## Ingredients

- Four calf's feet
- verjuice
- butter mixed with flour
- salt
- pepper
- shallot
- bouillon
- anchovy
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook four calf's feet in water, then when cooked and well drained, place them in a casserole
2. Add two spoonfuls of verjuice, a piece of butter mixed with a pinch of flour, salt, coarse pepper, chopped shallot, and a glass of bouillon
3. Let them simmer for half an hour over low heat
4. Before serving, add a chopped anchovy which you mix well into the sauce and a pinch of blanched parsley, finely chopped
5. If the sauce lacks acidity, add a bit more verjuice and serve with a short sauce
