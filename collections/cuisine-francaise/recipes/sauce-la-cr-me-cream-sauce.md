## Ingredients

- a little butter
- parsley, chives, shallot, all chopped
- one whole clove of garlic
- a good pinch of flour
- cream or milk
- good butter
- a pinch of blanched and finely chopped parsley
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter, chopped herbs, and whole garlic clove in a saucepan
2. Heat over the fire and add a good pinch of flour
3. Moisten with cream or milk
4. Boil for a quarter hour
5. Pass the sauce through a sieve into another saucepan
6. When ready to serve, add a little good butter, a pinch of finely chopped blanched parsley, and coarse pepper
7. Thicken the sauce over the fire
8. Use for all kinds of white entries
