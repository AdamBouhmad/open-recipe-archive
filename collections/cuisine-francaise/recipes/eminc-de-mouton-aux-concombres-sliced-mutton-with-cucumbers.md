## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, sliced
- cucumbers, cut in rounds the size of a small coin, eight lines thick
- butter
- Spanish sauce (3 spoonfuls)
- salt
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- croûtons
- flour (if sauce is not available)

## Directions

1. Slice the meat from the leg of mutton as previously described
2. Cut cucumbers in rounds the size of a small coin, eight lines thick
3. Place them in cloth and press to remove water
4. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan over a hot fire
5. When the butter is hot, add the cucumbers and sauté constantly so they take on color
6. When they are golden, drain them
7. Add three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, mix the cucumbers in it
8. Reduce them
9. When slightly thick, place them on the sliced mutton with a little salt, coarse pepper, and grated nutmeg
10. Keep warm
11. When ready to serve, arrange the sliced mutton on the platter with croûtons around it as in the preceding recipes
12. If sauce is not available, leave a little butter with the cucumbers, add almost one spoonful of flour
