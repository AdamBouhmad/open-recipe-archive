## Ingredients

- six eggs
- powdered sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Break six eggs and separate the whites and yolks.
2. Add four spoonfuls of powdered sugar.
3. Chop very finely half the zest of a lemon skin and put it with the yolks.
4. Mix them with the sugar and lemon.
5. At the moment of serving the dish, whip your egg whites as for a biscuit.
6. Mix the yolks well with the whites.
7. Then put a quarter pound of butter in the pan over a fire not too hot.
8. As soon as the butter is melted, add the eggs.
9. Stir the omelet so the bottom comes to the top.
10. When you see that the omelet has absorbed the butter, pour it as a turnover onto a buttered dish and place it on a bed of red coals.
11. Throw powdered sugar over the omelet.
12. Place a very hot camp oven over it.
13. Take care lest your omelet take too much color.
