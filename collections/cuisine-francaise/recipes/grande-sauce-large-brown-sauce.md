## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 pounds of beef shoulder (according to the size of the sauce)
- 2 or 3 pounds of veal
- poultry trimmings, whatever you have
- a few carrots
- a few onions
- water, one pot spoon full
- a large bouquet of parsley and chives
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 cloves
- mushrooms, if you have them

## Directions

1. Place all the meat and vegetables into a casserole
2. Add one pot spoon full of water
3. Place your meats on a fairly hot fire
4. When there is almost no moisture left in your casserole, move it to a gentle fire so that the glaze at the bottom of your casserole colors without burning
5. When it is a dark blonde and your glaze is brown, moisten it with water if you do not have broth
6. Add a large bouquet of parsley and chives, 2 bay leaves, 2 cloves, and mushrooms if you have them
7. Leave your meat to cook well skimmed and salted for three hours
8. After, pass the juice of your meat through a silk sieve
9. Make a roux and dilute it with your moistening liquid, and let your sauce boil for one hour on low heat
10. Degrease it, then pass it through cheesecloth or a horsehair sieve if you lack cheesecloth
11. Ensure your sauce is neither too pale nor too brown, neither too clear nor too thick, and that it is flavorful and well-salted
