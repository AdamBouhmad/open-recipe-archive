## Ingredients

- Oxtail, cut into pieces
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Onions, whole
- White wine
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Cloves
- Salt
- Pepper
- Anchovy
- Capers
- Bread
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into pieces and blanch it in boiling water
2. Remove it to fresh water, then cook it halfway in bouillon with no seasoning
3. When half cooked, make a roux with a bit of butter and a spoonful of flour
4. Moisten this roux with the bouillon in which you cooked the oxtail
5. Add the pieces of oxtail with a dozen large whole onions that you have blanched beforehand to remove their outer skin
6. Add a half-setier of white wine, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, a bay leaf, a bit of thyme, basil, two cloves, salt, and pepper
7. Cook over a low fire until the oxtail and onions are cooked
8. Take care to decrease well
9. Add a minced anchovy and two pinches of whole capers to the sauce
10. Arrange the pieces of oxtail in the middle of the platter, the onions around, and on top place seven or eight pieces of bread cut to the size of a small coin, which you will pass through butter
11. When ready to serve, pour over everything the sauce, which should be thick
