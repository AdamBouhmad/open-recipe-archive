## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- coarse lard
- salt
- fine spices
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the leg of mutton across into three or four pieces
2. Lard each piece with coarse lard seasoned with salt, fine spices, and chopped fine herbs
3. Cook them in the same manner as beef à la royale found on page 54
4. Serve hot as an entrée or cold as a side dish
