## Ingredients

- Two lamb shoulders
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Aromatic herbs, ground
- Lard for larding
- Lard strips for laying in pan
- Veal slices
- Four carrots
- Four onions
- Two bay leaves
- Thyme
- Bouillon
- Truffles
- Butter

## Directions

1. Completely debone two lamb shoulders without leaving any bone
2. Season the insides with salt, coarse pepper, and a little ground aromatic herbs
3. Gather the meat together with a trussing needle and string, forming a small balloon shape
4. Lard the top of the shoulders with fine lard, leaving about two finger widths around the edge unlarded
5. Place lard strips and veal slices in a casserole
6. Place the two shoulders on top
7. Add four carrots, four onions, two bay leaves, and a little thyme
8. Add a full pot-spoon of bouillon and place a round of paper on top
9. Braise for two hours with heat above and below so the lard takes on a beautiful color
10. At the moment of serving, drain the shoulders, remove the trussing, and glaze them
11. Cut the truffles into small lardons with a fork
12. Make small holes where the larding was and insert the truffle lardons around
13. Chop truffles and pass them through a small piece of butter
14. Add four deglazing spoonfuls of worked velouté
15. Arrange the shoulders on your plate and pour this sauce underneath
