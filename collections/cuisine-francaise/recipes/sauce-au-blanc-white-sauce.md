## Ingredients

- Good bouillon (fat or lean depending on preparation)
- A good crumb of bread
- A bouquet of parsley, chives, garlic, shallot, thyme, bay leaf, basil, cloves, a little nutmeg
- Some whole mushrooms
- One glass of white wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Three egg yolks
- Cream

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan fat or lean bouillon, a good crumb of bread, a bouquet garni of parsley, chives, garlic, shallot, thyme, bay leaf, basil, cloves, a little nutmeg, some whole mushrooms, one glass of white wine, salt, and pepper
2. Boil everything together and reduce to half
3. Pass this sauce through muslin to make a small coulis
4. When ready to use it, add a liaison of three egg yolks with cream
5. Thicken it on the fire and be careful not to let it turn
