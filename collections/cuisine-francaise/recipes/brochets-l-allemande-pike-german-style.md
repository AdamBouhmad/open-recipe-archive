## Ingredients

- Small pike
- Onion slices
- Parsley branches
- Two bay leaves
- Three scallions
- Two cloves
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- One bottle white wine
- Butter
- Flour
- Nutmeg
- Court-bouillon
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean your small pike and cut them into sections
2. Put them in a casserole with onion slices, parsley branches, two bay leaves, three scallions, two cloves, salt, coarse pepper, and one bottle of white wine
3. When your fish has simmered for half an hour, remove it; scale it and remove the fins
4. Put your sections in a casserole and pass the court-bouillon through a silk sieve; pour it over them and keep them warm
5. At the moment of serving, drain them and arrange them on your platter
6. Put a good piece of butter in a casserole; add a small spoonful of flour, grated nutmeg, coarse pepper, and half a glass of court-bouillon
7. Put your sauce on the fire, stirring until it boils
8. Have a liaison of two egg yolks ready to pour into your sauce; stir it well and do not let it boil; pass it through a strainer
9. Check that it is well seasoned and mask your fish with it
