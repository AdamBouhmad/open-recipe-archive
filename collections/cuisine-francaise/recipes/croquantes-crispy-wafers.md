## Ingredients

- half a litre of flour
- a quarter pound of sugar
- one egg white
- half a teaspoon of orange blossom water
- butter the size of half an egg
- half a glass of water
- a small pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Place flour and sugar on a table
2. Add egg white, orange blossom water, butter, water, and salt
3. Knead everything together to make a well-bound and firm dough
4. Roll out very thin
5. Cut small bases and place them on small pastry molds
6. Bake for a quarter hour in a very gentle oven
7. When cold, lightly spread currant jelly or other preserves on top
8. For cut-out croquantes, use the same dough with more egg whites and no water
