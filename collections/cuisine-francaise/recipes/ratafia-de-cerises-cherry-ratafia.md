## Ingredients

- Good ripe cherries
- A little raspberries
- Cherry pits (kernels from the pits)
- Three pints of cherry juice (resulting from pressing)
- Two pints of eau-de-vie
- One quarter pound of sugar per pint
- Three handfuls of crushed cherry pits
- A handful of coriander
- A little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take good ripe cherries, remove the stems and pits
2. Mix with a little raspberries and crush everything together
3. Place them in a clean jug and let them sit for four or five days
4. Take care to stir the marc two or three times daily to give it flavor and a beautiful color
5. Press the marc well to extract all the juice
6. Measure the juice and for three pints of juice add two pints of eau-de-vie
7. Crush three handfuls of pits from the same cherries, with one quarter pound of sugar per pint
8. Put everything to infuse in the same jug with a handful of coriander and a little cinnamon
9. Stir it daily for seven or eight days
10. After which, pass it through a strainer until clear and put it in well-corked bottles, then store in the cellar
