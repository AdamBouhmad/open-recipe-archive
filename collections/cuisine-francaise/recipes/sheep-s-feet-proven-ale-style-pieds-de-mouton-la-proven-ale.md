## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet, prepared and cooked as in the white sauce recipe
- Twelve large onions
- Half a pound of oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Juice of three or four lemons
- Two spoonfuls of bouillon

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your sheep's feet as those made in the white sauce manner
2. Drain and trim them, and place them in a casserole
3. Cut twelve large onions in half and slice them to the thickness of a line, so that your onion forms a half-circle
4. Remove the head, tail, and heart so that only half-circles remain
5. Put half a pound of oil in a casserole and place it on a hot fire
6. When your oil is very hot, put in your onions and stir them with a wooden spoon handle
7. When your onions are golden, remove them from the fire
8. Remove a little of the oil and add salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, the juice of three or four lemons, and two spoonfuls of bouillon
9. Just let it come to a boil
10. Put this sauce over your sheep's feet and keep them warm without letting them boil
11. At the moment of serving, arrange your sheep's feet on your plate and mask them with your onions
12. If you have no lemon juice, use vinegar instead
