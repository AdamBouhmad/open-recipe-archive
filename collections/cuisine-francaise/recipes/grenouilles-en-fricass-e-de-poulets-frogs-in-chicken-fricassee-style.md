## Ingredients

- Frogs
- Boiling water
- Fresh water
- Mushrooms
- Bouquet of parsley
- Ciboule
- One clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- Butter
- Flour
- One glass of white wine
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Egg yolks
- Cream
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Put the frogs in boiling water and let them boil a little
2. Remove to fresh water and drain
3. Place them in a casserole with mushrooms, a bouquet of parsley, ciboule, one clove of garlic, two cloves, and a piece of butter
4. Pass over the fire two or three times and add a good pinch of flour
5. Moisten with a glass of white wine and a little bouillon, add salt and coarse pepper
6. Cook for a quarter hour and reduce to a short sauce
7. Add a liaison of three egg yolks mixed with a little cream and a small pinch of finely chopped parsley
8. Bind without boiling
