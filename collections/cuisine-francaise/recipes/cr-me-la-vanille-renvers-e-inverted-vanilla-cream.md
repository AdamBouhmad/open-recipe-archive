## Ingredients

- cream to fill a mold
- sugar
- vanilla
- 12 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Fill a mold or small molds with cream
2. Bring the cream to a boil
3. Add sugar and vanilla
4. Allow to boil several times and then cool
5. If the mold holds a pint, use 12 egg yolks and 3 whole eggs mixed with the cream
6. Pass five times through cheesecloth
7. Lightly butter the mold and pour the cream into it
8. Place in a bain-marie to set, ensuring the water does not boil vigorously
9. Place fire on the mold cover but not too intense
10. Test if set by placing a finger in the center of the cream
11. When ready to serve, invert the mold onto a plate
12. Heat the remaining cream and turn it like a white sauce
13. When the cream coats a wooden spoon, remove from heat
14. Turn it briefly and pour over the inverted cream
