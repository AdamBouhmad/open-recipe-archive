## Ingredients

- three half-setiers of milk
- sugar
- green lemon rind
- orange flower water
- a full teaspoon of flour
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, put three half-setiers of milk with sugar, green lemon rind, and orange flower water, boil everything together and reduce by half, remove from the fire and let it cool
2. In a saucepan, dilute a full teaspoon of flour with six egg yolks (set the whites aside in a clean basin), then put your cream with the egg yolks, diluting little by little, pass your cream through a sieve and cook it in a bain-marie
3. When it is cooked and set as it should be, remove it from the bain-marie, whip the egg whites you set aside until they are well risen, add fine sugar to them, cover your cream with the egg whites in the shape of a dome, put fine sugar on top
