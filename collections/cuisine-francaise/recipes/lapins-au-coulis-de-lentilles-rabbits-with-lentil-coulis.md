## Ingredients

- rabbits
- good bouillon
- small lard
- bouquet garni
- salt
- pepper
- one litron of lentils
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbits into pieces and cook them with good bouillon, small lard and a bouquet garni, salt, and very little pepper
2. Also cook one litron of lentils à la reine with bouillon, without salt
3. When the lentils are cooked, pass them through a sieve with their bouillon
4. Remove the bread and small lard from its cooking, then reduce it on the fire until you judge it sufficiently bound to serve
5. Heat the rabbit and small lard in it
6. Serve if it is of good flavour in a tureen
