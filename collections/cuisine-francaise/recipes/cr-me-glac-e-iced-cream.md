## Ingredients

- a small handful of flour
- green lemon, chopped very finely
- a pinch of candied orange flower, pounded
- a piece of sugar
- eight egg yolks
- a chopine of cream
- a half-setier of milk

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, put a small handful of flour, finely chopped green lemon, a pinch of pounded pralined orange flower, and a piece of sugar
2. Dilute everything with eight egg yolks (set the whites aside in a clean basin) and dilute the yolks with a chopine of cream and a half-setier of milk
3. Cook this cream on the fire for half an hour
4. When it is thick, remove it from the fire and whip the whites with a whisk
5. When they are well whipped, mix them into the cream and put this cream in the dish you must serve
6. Put sugar on top so the cream is well covered
7. Cook it in the oven, which should not be too hot, or under a covered pie dish: when it is well risen and glazed, serve
