## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- salt
- glaze
- coulis
- veal round
- beef fat
- eggs
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- cream
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Break the bones underneath with the back of the cleaver
2. Cook it with bouillon, a bouquet garni, and very little salt
3. When cooked, remove the shoulder from the cooking liquid to degrease the sauce
4. Reduce the sauce to a glaze and glaze the entire top of the shoulder and let it cool
5. Put bouillon in the same casserole to detach everything remaining in it
6. Add a little coulis and pass this sauce through a sieve into another casserole which you will heat when serving
7. Take the lamb shoulder and place it on a baking dish and cover it all over with a forcemeat as described for lamb chops in dressing gown
8. Coat with breadcrumbs
