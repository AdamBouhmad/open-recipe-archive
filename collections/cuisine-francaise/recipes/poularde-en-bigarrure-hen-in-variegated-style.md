## Ingredients

- deux belles poulardes (two fine hens)
- bardes de lard (lard lardons)
- lard fin (fine lard)
- tranches de veau (veal slices)
- oignons (onions)
- carottes (carrots)
- clous de girofle (cloves)
- feuille de laurier (bay leaf)
- thym (thyme)
- persil (parsley)
- ciboules (chives)
- papier beurrée (buttered paper)
- gelée (jelly)
- truffes (truffles)
- beurre (butter)
- sel (salt)
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- quatre épices (four spices)
- jus de citron (lemon juice)
- langues à l'écarlate (scarlet tongues)
- poêle (poele)

## Directions

1. Take two fine hens with well-fleshed stomachs
2. Flame them lightly
3. Remove the four fillets with the wings as correctly as possible
4. Remove the four thighs while preserving almost all the skin
5. Lard your four fillets with fine lard
6. Pare them and cover your wing tips so they cannot be seen, so they have appearance and your fillets are more elevated
7. Put them in a saucepan with lard lardons, veal slices, two onions cut in slices, two carrots the same, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and your fillets over this seasoning
8. Cover with a buttered paper sheet
9. Fill with a small pot spoon of jelly
10. Completely bone your four thighs
11. Chop four truffles that you will pass through butter, a little salt, a little coarse pepper, a little four spices
12. When your truffles are cold, fill the four thighs with them
13. Gather the meats with a needle and thread so that your thighs form a flat round at the thick end
14. Cut off a foot three-quarters and put it in the thigh so the claws show
15. Pinch thereafter a piece of butter, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a saucepan
16. Put it on the fire with your thighs inside
17. Make them stiffen afterwards
18. Cover them with lardons and place a poêle on top
19. Let them cook and cool
20. Lard two of them all the way through with truffles in small equal sticks and at the same distance
21. Lard the two others with scarlet tongues the same way as those with truffles
22. Put them in a saucepan with the broth passed through silk sieve in which they cooked
23. Remove the nerves from the tenderloin fillets and put truffles there as it is said for rabbit fillets
24. Put them in a silver platter with warm butter and a hint of salt
25. Three-quarters of an hour before serving, put your fowl fillets on the fire
26. Place some on the lid
27. Keep your thighs warm
28. At the moment of serving, drain your fillets and glaze them
