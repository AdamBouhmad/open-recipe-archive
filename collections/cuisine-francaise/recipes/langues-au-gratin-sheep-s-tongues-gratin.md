## Ingredients

- sheep's tongues
- bouillon
- half a glass of white wine
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- half a bay leaf
- thyme
- half a clove of garlic
- two cloves
- coulis
- breadcrumbs
- butter or lard
- two raw egg yolks
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues with a little bouillon, half a glass of white wine, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a bay leaf, a little thyme, half a clove of garlic, and two cloves
2. Bring to boil for half an hour over low heat
3. Prepare the gratin: in a dish put at the bottom breadcrumbs, a piece of butter or lard, two raw egg yolks, parsley, chives, chopped very fine
4. Add a little coulis or one spoonful of bouillon, salt, and pepper; mix everything together
5. Put the dish on some hot ashes until the farce attaches itself to the bottom
6. Then drain off the butter; wipe the edges of the dish
7. Serve the tongues on top with their sauce
