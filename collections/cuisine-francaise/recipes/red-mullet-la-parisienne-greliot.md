## Ingredients

- Red mullet (greliot)
- White wine
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Roots
- Onions

## Directions

1. Gut and wash without scaling
2. Cook with white wine, a little butter, salt, pepper, and a bouquet of roots and onions
3. Boil the court-bouillon for half an hour so it has flavor when you add them
4. When cooked, remove from court-bouillon and gently remove the scales everywhere except the head
5. Serve with the same sauces as above
