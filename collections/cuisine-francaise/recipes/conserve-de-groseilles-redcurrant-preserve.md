## Ingredients

- 1 pound redcurrants
- 1 glass water
- 1 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the currants from their stems and put them on the fire with 1 glass of water
2. Cook them until they have released their liquid
3. Pass them through a sieve, pressing firmly so that only the skins remain in the sieve
4. Put everything that has passed through onto the fire and reduce it until it forms a thick marmalade
5. Put 1 pound of sugar in a pan with 1 glass of water, bring to boil and skim
6. Continue boiling until dipping your fingers in water, then in the sugar, and stirring them in water, the sugar remaining on your fingers breaks cleanly
7. Remove from heat and add your currant marmalade
8. Stir together until you see a small glaze forming on top
9. Pour into a paper mold as with the violet preserve
