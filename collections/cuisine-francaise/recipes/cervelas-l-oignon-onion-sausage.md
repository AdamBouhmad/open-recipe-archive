## Ingredients

- Pork meat, the tenderest and most interlarded, or veal, hare, or rabbit well supplied with lard
- Onions according to the quantity of meat
- Melted lard or rendered pork belly fat (sain-doux)
- Parsley, chopped
- Ciboules (chives), chopped
- Salt
- Mixed spices
- Intestines (boyaux)

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and mix with parsley, chopped chives, salt, and mixed spices
2. Take onions according to the quantity of meat you have, chop them and cook with melted lard or rendered pork belly fat
3. When cooked three-quarters, mix them with the meat and finish your sausages as described above
4. Take intestines of appropriate thickness, fill them with the mixture, and tie them at both ends
5. Put them to smoke in the chimney for two days
6. Cook them for two or three hours according to their thickness in broth without salt
