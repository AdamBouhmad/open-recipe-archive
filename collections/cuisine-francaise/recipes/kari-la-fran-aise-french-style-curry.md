## Ingredients

- Veal tendons
- All ingredients as in Indian-style curry
- Egg yolks (5 or 6)

## Directions

1. Prepare your tendons and make the curry the same as the previous recipe
2. At the moment of serving, remove the meat and garnishes from your curry
3. Make sure it is very rich and the sauce is well bound and abundant
4. Make a liaison of five or six egg yolks according to the quantity of sauce
5. Bind your sauce on the heat, constantly stirring without letting it boil
6. When you see the sauce coat your spoon, pass it through a sieve over your curry
7. The meat and garnishes must bathe in the sauce, and the pepper must dominate
8. Season well with salt
9. If your sauce is too thin, add some velouté
