## Ingredients

- lamb neck
- lentil purée

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your lamb necks as in the previous recipe
2. At serving time, drain them and remove the string
3. Arrange them on the platter
4. Cover them with a lentil purée
5. You may also serve them with pea purée, turnip purée, bean purée, etc.
