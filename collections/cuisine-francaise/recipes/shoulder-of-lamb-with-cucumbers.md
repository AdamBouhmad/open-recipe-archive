## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulders
- Medium lardons, seasoned
- Four spices
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Lard strips
- Cucumbers
- Cream

## Directions

1. Debone your lamb shoulders down to the handle
2. Lard them on the inside with seasoned medium lardons, four spices, salt and coarse pepper
3. Arrange them so they have an elongated form
4. Tie them and cover with lard strips
5. Cook them as the previous shoulders
6. At the moment of serving, drain them, remove the ties, and glaze
7. Place creamed cucumbers on your plate
8. Place your glazed shoulders on top
9. You may serve them with chicory, tomato sauce, or mushroom purée
