## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- leeks
- onions
- celery
- lettuce
- sorrel
- butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut the vegetables into dice
2. Crush the sorrel and lettuce
3. Pass the roots through butter the same way
4. Then add the leeks and herbs, all together
5. Moisten with enough bouillon for your soup
6. Always dip as a bread soup
