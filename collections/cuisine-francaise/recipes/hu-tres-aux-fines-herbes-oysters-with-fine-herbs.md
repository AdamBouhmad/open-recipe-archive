## Ingredients

- oysters in their shells
- fine herbs (shallots, parsley, mushrooms)
- lard, grated
- oil and butter
- four spices
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Have your oysters opened and detached, and put them without shells in a saucepan with their water
2. Let them stiffen without allowing them to boil
3. Prepare fine herbs as for cutlets, with shallots, parsley, mushrooms, grated lard, oil and butter, and four spices
4. Mix your oysters with your fine herbs and put them in well-cleaned shells, filling them completely
5. Arrange breadcrumbs on top
6. Place them on the grill over a hot fire with a camp oven on top
