## Ingredients

- Half a glass of white wine
- As much broth
- Two or three spoonfuls of coulis
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Five or six rocamboles, crushed

## Directions

1. Put half a glass of white wine in a saucepan with as much broth and two or three spoonfuls of coulis, salt and coarse pepper
2. Boil for a quarter hour and add five or six crushed rocamboles before serving
