## Ingredients

- A quarter pound of small bacon in thin square slices
- Two sweetbreads, disgorged and blanched
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- One shallot
- A small amount of garlic
- Flour
- Broth
- Four raw egg yolks
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into small thin squares the width of a finger
2. Cook them in a casserole over low heat until half cooked
3. Cut the sweetbreads into dice
4. Add them to the casserole with the bacon, chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, shallot, and garlic
5. Pass everything together on the fire and add a good pinch of flour
6. Moisten with broth and boil for half an hour until there is no more sauce
7. Season with salt and coarse pepper if needed
8. When almost cooked, add four raw egg yolks
9. Thicken on the fire without boiling, making the sauce thick enough to cling to the meat
10. Remove from heat when half cooled
11. Thread everything onto a small silver skewer or wooden skewers
12. Coat with breadcrumbs as you go
13. Grill over low heat to a beautiful golden color
14. Serve dry for an entremets or side dish
