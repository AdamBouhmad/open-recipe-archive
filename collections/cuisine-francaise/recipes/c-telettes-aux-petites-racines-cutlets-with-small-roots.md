## Ingredients

- cutlets
- carrot slices
- small carrots
- roux
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets
2. Trim them
3. Cook them the same way as the cutlets with turnips
4. Cover them with carrot slices
5. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, and arrange them in crowns
6. Put small carrots in the middle
7. If you do not have sauce for your small roots, make a light roux that you will moisten with the liquid in which your cutlets cooked
8. Put your roots in it with a small piece of sugar
9. Reduce them
