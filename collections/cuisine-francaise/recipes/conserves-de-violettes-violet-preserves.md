## Ingredients

- one sheet of white paper
- one pound of sugar
- one glass of water
- violets, cleaned

## Directions

1. Take one sheet of white paper that you leave doubled and fold all around to make a border the height of a good inch, as if you wanted to make a case
2. Have one pound of sugar that you put in a pan with a glass of water
3. Make it boil and skim
4. Continue boiling until, dipping the skimmer in it and shaking it with a backward motion, long sparks rise in the air that hold together
5. Remove from the fire and when it is half-cold, add cleaned violets that you pound very fine in a small mortar
6. Mix them well with the sugar, stirring quickly with the skimmer
