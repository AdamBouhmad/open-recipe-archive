## Ingredients

- Preserved samphire
- Water
- Good sauce with thickened coulis

## Directions

1. Take preserved samphire, using only the leaves
2. Blanch them briefly in boiling water to remove the strength of the vinegar
3. After they are pressed to remove the water, put them in a good sauce made with thickened coulis
