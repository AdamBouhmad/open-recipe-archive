## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound ripe verjuice

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of sugar in the same way as the violet preserve
2. When it reaches its cooking point, remove from heat and stir for about 2 minutes
3. Make a verjuice marmalade: take 1 pound of ripe verjuice, remove the stems, put it on the fire to cook until it becomes a marmalade, and put it in a sieve to press firmly until only skins and seeds remain in the sieve
4. Put the marmalade back on the fire to dry it until it is very thick
5. Put it in the sugar and stir them well together until the sugar begins to whiten on the edges of the pan
6. Pour immediately into a mold as with the violet preserve
