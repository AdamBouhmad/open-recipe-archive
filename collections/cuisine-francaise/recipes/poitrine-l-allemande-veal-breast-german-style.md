## Ingredients

- veal breast
- bouillon
- white wine
- herbs
- pepper
- coulis
- capers
- anchovies
- poultry livers
- parsley
- shallot
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Blanch the breast
2. Cook it whole with a little bouillon, a half-glass of white wine, a bouquet of fine herbs, and pepper
3. When cooked, dress it on a platter and fold the skin to the sides to expose the tendons
4. Pour over a German sauce made with a little coulis, capers, anchovies, two cooked poultry livers, blanched parsley, and a shallot, all chopped very fine
5. Boil an instant and add a little coarse pepper
6. Alternatively, degrease and sieve the cooking liquid from the breast, add a walnut-sized piece of good butter kneaded with flour and a pinch of blanched chopped parsley, and thicken on the fire
