## Ingredients

- rack of mutton
- good bouillon
- salt
- bouquet garni
- lentils

## Directions

1. Cut the rack into cutlets
2. Cook them with good bouillon, very little salt, and a bouquet garni
3. Also cook a liter of lentils à la reine with bouillon
4. When the lentils are cooked, pass them through as a purée
5. Mix this lentil purée with the cooked mutton cutlets and their seasonings
6. If the coulis is too thin, reduce it over fire
