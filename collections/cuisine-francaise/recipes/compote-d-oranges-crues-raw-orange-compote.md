## Ingredients

- 6 Portuguese oranges
- Fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the top from six Portuguese oranges in such a way that you can replace it as if they were whole
2. Pierce the flesh in several places with a small knife
3. Work fine sugar into the cuts
4. Replace the lids and serve
5. Alternatively, serve them after peeling, cut them in slices and arrange in a compote dish with fine sugar above and below
