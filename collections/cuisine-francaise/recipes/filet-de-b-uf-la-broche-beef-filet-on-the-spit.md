## Ingredients

- beef filet from the sirloin piece
- fat covering
- piquant sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Remove your filet from the sirloin piece.
2. Leave it covered with fat, that is to say, pare the fat and leave it three fingers thick all along the filet.
3. Score the surface of the fat so that the heat penetrates your filet better.
4. Pass a large and long skewer along your filet.
5. Fold it somewhat upon itself so it is not too long.
6. Secure your filet on the spit.
7. One and a half hours suffice to cook it, according to the intensity of the fire and the thickness of the filet.
8. When it is cooked, serve a piquant sauce underneath or in a sauceboat; a tomato sauce also suits.
9. This filet is served as a roast.
