## Ingredients

- four or five Portuguese oranges
- sugar
- white wine
- flour
- good oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Take four or five Portuguese oranges
2. Remove the outer rind by turning them with a small knife to cut the rind the thickness of a small coin as you go
3. Cut the oranges into quarters to remove the pips
4. Cook them with a little sugar
5. Make a paste with white wine, flour, a spoonful of good oil, and a little salt
6. Mix this paste so it is neither too thin nor too thick, so it flows when poured with a spoon
7. Dip your orange quarters in it
8. Cook them in hot fat until it is a beautiful color
9. Serve them
