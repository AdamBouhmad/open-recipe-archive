## Ingredients

- Veal tendons, cut into oyster shapes
- Lard for bardes
- Clear marinade
- Batter for frying
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut your veal tendons into oyster shapes
2. Put lard bardes in the bottom of your pot, tendons on top
3. Cover them with lard
4. Moisten with clear marinade
5. Cook for two and a half hours
6. At the moment of serving, drain your tendons
7. Dip them in batter for frying
8. Take care that your oil is not too hot
9. Put a small handful of fried parsley on your fried tendons
