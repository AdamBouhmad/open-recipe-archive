## Ingredients

- Veal round, one inch thick, same size as for fricandelles
- White paper
- Butter
- Oil or butter
- Parsley, chives, shallots, mushrooms, all chopped very finely
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- White vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut veal round one inch thick, the same size as for fricandelles
2. Make a white paper case the size of the meat and butter it all over on the outside
3. Put the meat inside with oil or butter, parsley, chives, shallots, and mushrooms all chopped very finely, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Place the case on the grill with a buttered paper sheet underneath
5. Cook over very low heat or on hot ashes, being careful not to let the paper burn
6. If the fire is too strong, reduce it a little with the shovel
7. When the meat is cooked on one side, turn it over to cook the other side
8. Serve in the paper case after lightly drizzling white vinegar on top
