## Ingredients

- One large sweetbread
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Half a bay leaf
- Two cloves
- Half a clove of garlic
- Flour
- One glass of good broth
- Half a glass of white wine
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Two spoonfuls of coulis (or three egg yolks mixed with cream for entremets)
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sweetbread by disgorging and blanching
2. Cut it into five or six pieces
3. Put in a casserole with mushrooms, butter, bouquet of parsley, chives, half a bay leaf, two cloves, and half a clove of garlic
4. Pass on the fire and add a pinch of flour
5. Moisten with one glass of good broth and half a glass of white wine
6. Season with salt and coarse pepper
7. Boil on low heat for half an hour
8. Degrease and add two spoonfuls of coulis (or for entremets, use a liaison of three egg yolks mixed with cream instead of coulis)
9. Thicken on the fire without boiling to avoid curdling
10. Serve with short sauce
11. Add a small dash of vinegar if lacking acidity
