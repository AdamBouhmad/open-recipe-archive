## Ingredients

- salsify root
- water
- white vinegar
- salt
- butter, the size of an egg
- four spoonfuls of white vinegar

## Directions

1. Scrape the salsify root to remove the black surface, leaving no spots
2. As they become white, place them in a vessel with water and white vinegar
3. Pour a large amount of water into a pot and add salt
4. Add four spoonfuls of white vinegar
5. Once the water boils, add the salsify
6. Let them boil for one hour, testing if they are cooked (they should be neither too much nor too little)
7. Drain them and arrange them in white or brown sauce
