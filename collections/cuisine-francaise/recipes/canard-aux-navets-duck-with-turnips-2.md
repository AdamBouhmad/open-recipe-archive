## Ingredients

- duck
- turnips
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Truss and cook your duck as you would cook duck in a pan
2. Turn your turnips, that is to say cut them all to the same size
3. Sauté them in butter; when they are well browned, let them drain
4. Put them with your duck; cook on a low fire
5. Degrease it; add a small piece of sugar
6. At the moment of serving, remove the trussing string, and pour your ragout of turnips over it
7. Check that it is properly seasoned with salt
