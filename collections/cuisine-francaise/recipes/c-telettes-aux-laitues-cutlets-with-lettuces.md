## Ingredients

- cutlets
- glazed lettuces
- reduced espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets, trim them, and cook them as the cutlets with cucumbers
2. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, and arrange them in crowns, one lettuce between each, that is to say one cutlet, one glazed lettuce, and so on, until your crown is formed
3. Put reduced espagnole sauce
