## Ingredients

- sirloin fillet
- Spanish sauce
- cooking liquid from the fillet
- roux

## Directions

1. This fillet must be cooked the day before
2. Trim and cook your fillet as you would cook it braised
3. When it is cold, make an oval hole in it
4. Cut into dice the meat you have removed from your fillet—neither small nor large pieces
5. Reduce a Spanish sauce with a little cooking liquid from your fillet
6. When your sauce is well reduced, add your small pieces of meat to it and keep it hot in a bain-marie
7. At the moment of serving, reheat your fillet
8. Drain it and glaze it
9. Place it on the serving dish and put your little ragout inside
10. Have a clear Spanish sauce which you will put underneath
11. Lacking sauce, make a light roux which you will moisten with the cooking liquid in which your sirloin fillet has cooked; reduce your sauce well so that it takes on good flavor
