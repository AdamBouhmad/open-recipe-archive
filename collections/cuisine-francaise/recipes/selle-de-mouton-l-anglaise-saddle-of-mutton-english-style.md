## Ingredients

- saddle of mutton
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter
- ten eggs
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Have a saddle like the previous one, which you debone, fill, season, tie, and cook the same way as the previous recipe
2. It must be cooked the day before
3. To be able to bread it more easily, remove the skin from above; pare it; season it with salt and coarse pepper
4. Butter it with a brush of feathers; bread it; let it cool
5. Warm a quarter pound of butter in a vessel
6. Break ten eggs into it and season them with salt and coarse pepper; beat them with your butter
7. Daub your saddle all over with this and bread it entirely
8. When it is breaded, warm some butter; dip your brush in it and let it drip onto your saddle so that only drops fall on top
9. Sprinkle breadcrumbs so that the top is well covered
10. One hour before serving, put your saddle on a plafond in a large oven; let it not be too hot so your saddle has a fine color
11. At the moment of serving, when it is well hot, slide a casserole lid under your saddle and place it on the platter
12. Put clear juice underneath
