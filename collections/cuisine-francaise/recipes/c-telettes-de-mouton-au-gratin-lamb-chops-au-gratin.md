## Ingredients

- Lamb cut into chops
- Lard or butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Shallots
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Coulis
- Bread crumbs
- Egg yolk
- Butter

## Directions

1. Put lamb chops in a saucepan with lard or butter, parsley, chives, and two shallots, all chopped
2. Pass them over the fire with bouillon seasoned with salt and coarse pepper
3. Cook them until done
4. Degrease the liquid and add a little coulis to bind it
5. Raise the heat and add breadcrumbs mixed with egg yolk, parsley and chives chopped very fine
6. Place on a bed of ashes so that the gratin is well colored
7. Drain off the butter and serve your chops on top of the gratin
