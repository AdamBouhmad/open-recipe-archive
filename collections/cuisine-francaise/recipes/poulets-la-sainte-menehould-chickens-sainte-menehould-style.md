## Ingredients

- Two common chickens
- Butter
- One glass of white wine
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Bunch of parsley
- Chives
- One clove of garlic
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Two cloves
- Beaten egg
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Singe, empty and truss the legs in the body of two common chickens
2. Put them in a saucepan with a piece of butter, one glass of white wine, salt, coarse pepper, a bunch of parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, thyme, laurel, basil, two cloves
3. Cook gently and attach all the sauce around the chickens
4. Dip the chickens in beaten egg, bread with breadcrumbs, dip again in butter and bread again
5. Grill to a beautiful golden color
6. Serve dry or with a clear sauce that is somewhat piquant
