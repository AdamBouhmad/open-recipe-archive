## Ingredients

- one litron of flour
- butter, about half an egg's worth
- four eggs, whites and yolks
- a pinch of salt
- one quarteron of fine sugar
- six ounces of sweet almonds, pounded very fine
- sugar and red-hot iron for glazing

## Directions

1. Put one litron of flour on a table and make a hole in the middle
2. Put in butter about the size of half an egg, four eggs, a pinch of salt, and one quarteron of fine sugar
3. Add six ounces of sweet almonds pounded very fine
4. Knead everything together and form into a cake in the usual way
5. Bake it and glaze with sugar using a red-hot iron
