## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- water
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Cook the pumpkin in water
2. Put it in a saucepan with a piece of butter, parsley, chives, salt and pepper
3. When it has boiled a quarter hour and there is no sauce remaining, add a liaison of egg yolks with cream or milk
