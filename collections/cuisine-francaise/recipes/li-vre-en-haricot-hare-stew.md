## Ingredients

- Hare
- Hare liver
- Butter
- Bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Clove of garlic
- Three cloves
- Two shallots
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Flour
- White wine
- Vinegar
- Water or bouillon
- Turnips
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Skin and empty a hare, keep its liver after removing the bitter part, cut it into pieces and place everything in a saucepan with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, three cloves, two shallots, a bay leaf, thyme, and basil.
2. Pass it over the fire and add a full tablespoon of flour, moisten with half a septier of white wine, two spoonfuls of vinegar, two or three glasses of water or bouillon.
3. Cook for one hour, then have turnips cut neatly, blanch them for half a quarter hour in boiling water and put them to cook with the hare.
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper, finish cooking and serve at short sauce.
5. Remove the bouquet and serve hot. If the hare is tender, the turnips should be added at the same time.
