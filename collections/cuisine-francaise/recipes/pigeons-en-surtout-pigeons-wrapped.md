## Ingredients

- pigeons
- ragoût
- meat forcemeat
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- clear coulis

## Directions

1. Make a ragoût as for pigeons à la bourgeoise, reduced to a short sauce
2. Let it cool
3. Take the serving dish that should go to the fire and put a good meat forcemeat in the bottom
4. Arrange the pigeon ragoût on top
5. Cover it with the same forcemeat as below so the ragoût is not visible
6. Smooth it with a knife dipped in egg, bread it with breadcrumbs
7. Cook under a pastry lid cover until golden brown
8. Drain off the fat
9. Serve with a good sauce of clear coulis on top
