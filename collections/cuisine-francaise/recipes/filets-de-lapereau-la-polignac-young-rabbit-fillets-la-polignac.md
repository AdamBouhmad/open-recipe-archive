## Ingredients

- Six rabbit fillets
- Six additional rabbit fillets
- Fine lard for larding
- Truffles cut in half-crescents
- Butter
- Glaze
- Croûtons
- Sautéed truffles in game stock

## Directions

1. Wash, trim, and lard six fillets
2. Cook them as the previous ones
3. Prepare six other fillets that are well-trimmed and slice them at equal distance with the edge of a knife to create an incision
4. Place a half-crescent of truffle in each incision
5. Garnish the entire length of the fillet this way
6. Prepare your six fillets in the same manner and give them the shape of the others
7. The larded ones should be glazed
8. The ones with truffles, sauté them in butter
9. At the moment of service, drain them
10. Arrange them in crowns with a glazed croûton and a round between them
11. Place in the middle a sauté of truffles in game stock
