## Ingredients

- Lamb rack cut into chops
- Butter
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Small white onions
- Lard
- Carrot
- Parsnip
- Savory
- Parsley
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut a lamb rack into chops that are short and thick
2. Put them in a saucepan with about half the size of an egg of butter
3. Pass them over the fire until slightly browned and moisten with a glass of white wine and as much bouillon
4. Add a dozen small white onions and simmer over a small fire
5. After half an hour, add a quarter pound of small lard with a carrot and parsnip, all cut into fillets, a small branch of savory and chopped parsley, little salt, coarse pepper, and a dash of vinegar
6. When the chops are done, remove them and place in the serving dish
7. Arrange the roots and lard fillets around the chops
