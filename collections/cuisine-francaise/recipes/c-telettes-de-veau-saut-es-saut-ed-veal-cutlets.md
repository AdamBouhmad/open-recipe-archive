## Ingredients

- Seven veal cutlets
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Fine butter, half a pound
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Shallots, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets rib by rib, ensuring they are all the same thickness
2. Trim them so they are rounded at the thick end of the meat and squared off at the bone side
3. Trim the bone to the size of half an inch
4. Place salt and coarse pepper on your sauté pan or skillet
5. Put your cutlets in the pan with fine salt on top and a little coarse pepper
6. If you wish to sauté them with fine herbs, sprinkle finely chopped parsley and finely chopped shallots on top
7. Melt half a pound of fine butter and pour it over your cutlets
8. At the moment of serving, place them on a hot fire
9. When they are cooked on one side, turn them to the other side
10. Avoid letting your fire brown the cutlets
11. Press your finger on the meat; if you feel the meat is still soft, it is not cooked enough; you would need to turn them and remove them from the hot fire so they cook more gently
12. When they are cooked, arrange them in crowns on the plate
13. Remove the butter that is in your sauté pan or skillet
14. Put in about four spoonfuls of espagnole sauce and a little glaze
15. Detach the glaze produced by your cutlets and put your sauce in the plate
16. If you have no sauce, leave a little butter; put a little flour which you stir with your stock; put a little bouillon in it, a little fine herbs; make it boil; sauce your cutlets and check if they are of good taste
