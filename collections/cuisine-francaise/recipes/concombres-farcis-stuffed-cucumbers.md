## Ingredients

- 3 cucumbers
- cooked farce
- lard strips
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- bouillon
- espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of three cucumbers and detach the interior with the handle of a serving spoon
2. When well emptied, fill them with a cooked farce
3. Line a pot with lard strips and place a few veal slices, some carrots and onions, a bay leaf, and a little thyme
4. Cover the cucumbers with lard strips and sprinkle with bouillon
5. Braise them for half an hour
6. Check with your finger to see if they soften; then remove from the fire
7. At the moment of serving, let them drain and arrange them on your platter
8. Top with reduced espagnole sauce
