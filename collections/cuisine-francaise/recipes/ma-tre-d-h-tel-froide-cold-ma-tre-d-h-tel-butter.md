## Ingredients

- A quarter pound of butter
- A little parsley
- A little shallots
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put a quarter pound of butter in a saucepan
2. Add finely chopped parsley and shallots, salt, coarse pepper, and lemon juice
3. Mix everything together with a wooden spoon
4. At the moment of serving, serve your maître-d'hôtel on top, under, or inside meats or fish
