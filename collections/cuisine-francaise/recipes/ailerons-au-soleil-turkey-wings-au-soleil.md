## Ingredients

- twelve turkey wings
- butter
- velouté
- bouillon
- bay leaf
- coarse pepper
- clove
- parsley and chives bouquet
- egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Sauté your twelve wings in butter as for chipolata
2. Add eight spoonfuls of Velouté, half a pot spoonful of bouillon, a bay leaf, coarse pepper, a clove, a bouquet of parsley and chives
3. Bring your wings to a boil; skim them
4. Three-quarters cooked, degrease them
5. When they are cooked, reduce your sauce so that it is well thickened
6. Remove the bouquet, leaf and clove
7. Add a liaison of three egg yolks into your ragout, with butter the size of half an egg
8. When it is thickened, place your wings to cool on a cake pan or platter, and their sauce on top
9. After this, coat them well with their sauce, and put them in bread crumbs
10. Then place them in an omelet of five raw eggs
11. Bread them a second time equally so that the sauce does not run into the frying oil
12. Give your wings a beautiful form
13. At the moment of serving
