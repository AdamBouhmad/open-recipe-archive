## Ingredients

- one veal breast
- a small piece of butter
- one spoonful of flour
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- parsley
- chives
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- three cloves
- onions
- roots
- water
- flour for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the veal breast into pieces the width of a finger.
2. In a casserole, make a roux with a small piece of butter and one spoonful of flour.
3. Add salt, pepper, vinegar, parsley, chives, thyme, bay leaf, basil, three cloves, onions, and roots, with water.
4. Heat the marinade on the fire, stirring constantly.
5. Place the meat in it to soak for two or three hours.
6. Remove the meat, dry it, and flour it.
7. Fry it; when cooked, serve garnished with parsley.
