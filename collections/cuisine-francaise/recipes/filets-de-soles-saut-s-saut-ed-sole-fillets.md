## Ingredients

- Sole fillets
- Chopped and washed parsley
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Italian sauce
- Lemon juice
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Lift the four fillets from your soles along with the skin
2. Trim and arrange them on your sauté pan
3. Sprinkle with a little chopped and washed parsley, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Warm some butter and pour it over your fillets
5. At the moment of serving, put them on the fire
6. As soon as they are stiffened, turn them
7. A few minutes are enough for them to be cooked
8. Arrange them on your plate and add Italian sauce and lemon juice
9. You can leave your fillets whole or cut them into two or three pieces
10. Then arrange them in crowns and put croutons around the edge of the plate
