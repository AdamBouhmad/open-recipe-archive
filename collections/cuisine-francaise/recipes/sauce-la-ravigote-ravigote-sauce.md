## Ingredients

- One glass of excellent bouillon
- Half a coffee spoon of vinegar
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Good butter the size of a walnut, mixed with flour
- Two pinches of salad garnish: a little tarragon, chives, chervil, pimpinella, watercress

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one glass of excellent bouillon, half a coffee spoon of vinegar, salt, coarse pepper, and good butter the size of a walnut mixed with flour
2. With two pinches of salad garnish ingredients like tarragon, chives, chervil, pimpinella, and watercress, boil these herbs for a moment in the sauce
3. Press them well and chop them very fine
4. Put them in the sauce and thicken it on the fire to serve on whatever you wish
