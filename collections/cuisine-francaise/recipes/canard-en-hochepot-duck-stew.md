## Ingredients

- one duck
- turnips
- quarter of a cabbage
- parsnips
- carrots
- onions
- good bouillon
- piece of small lard with rind
- bouquet garni
- salt
- coulis

## Directions

1. Singe and clean the duck, and cut it into four pieces.
2. Cook properly cut and turned turnips, quarter of a cabbage, parsnips, carrots, and onions in a small pot.
3. Blanch everything for half a quarter hour.
4. Place them in the small pot with good bouillon, a piece of small lard cut in slices with rind attached and tied, a bouquet garni, and little salt.
5. When everything is cooked, arrange the duck in a serving terrine; place all the vegetables around it.
6. Degrease the bouillon from the small pot where your vegetables cooked; add a little coulis, and serve short sauce over the vegetables and duck.
7. Taste your sauce beforehand to ensure it is of good flavor.
