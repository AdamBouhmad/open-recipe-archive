## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Flour
- Hot water
- Salt
- Sugar
- Egg yolks
- Cream
- Bread crust

## Directions

1. Put the mushrooms in a casserole with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley and chives.
2. When they have heated through, add a pinch of flour and moisten with hot water, a little salt and a little sugar.
3. When they are cooked and there is no more sauce left, add a liaison of egg yolks and cream.
4. Fry a bread crust in butter and place it in the bottom of the dish you will serve.
5. Place your ragout on top of the bread crust.
