## Ingredients

- all sorts of roots
- cabbage
- turnips
- onions
- celery
- leek
- half-liter of peas
- half-liter of lentils à la reine
- one quarter pound of rice
- a piece of butter

## Directions

1. Make first a good meatless broth with all sorts of roots, cabbage, turnips, onions, celery, leek, in proportion to the strength, and a half-liter of peas.
2. Put separately in a small pot a half-liter of lentils à la reine, which you cook with this broth.
3. When cooked, pass them as a purée.
4. Take one quarter pound of rice after washing it well.
5. Cook it in a small pot with a piece of butter and your clarified meatless broth.
6. When it is cooked and seasoned as it should be, add the lentil coulis to it.
7. Take care that your soup is not too thick.
