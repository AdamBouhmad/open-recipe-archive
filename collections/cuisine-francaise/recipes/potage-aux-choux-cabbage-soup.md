## Ingredients

- half a cabbage
- small piece of bacon cut into slices with the rind attached
- bouillon
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. Blanch the cabbage with a piece of small bacon cut into slices attached to the rind, tie each separately with string
2. Cook them separately in a small pot with the bouillon described above
3. When the cabbage and bacon are cooked, simmer the soup with the same bouillon and bread crusts
4. Serve the cabbage around the soup with the bacon, or in the simple bourgeoise manner on top
5. Take care to salt the bouillon very little because of the bacon
6. Root vegetables and turnips are made the same way; celery must be blanched longer
