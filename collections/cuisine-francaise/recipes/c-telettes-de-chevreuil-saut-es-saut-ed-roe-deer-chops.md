## Ingredients

- Roe deer chops
- Salt
- Fine pepper
- Butter
- Piquant sauce
- Vinegar
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cut and trim your chops as you would mutton chops
2. Season them with salt and fine pepper
3. Place them in your sauté pan
4. Pour warm butter over them
5. At the moment of serving, place them on a hot fire
6. When they are stiffened on one side, turn them over
7. Leave them a moment on the fire; when they resist under the finger, remove them
8. Have a piquant sauce ready
9. At the moment of serving, drain your chops, dip them in your sauce, and dress them in a crown
10. Pour your sauce underneath
11. If you have no sauce, remove a little butter from your sauté pan, pour four spoonfuls of vinegar into your pan, reduce it; when it is completely reduced, add one spoonful of flour and mix it with your butter
12. Add a small spooonful of bouillon, a little salt, fine pepper, a dash of vinegar, and a bay leaf
13. Reduce your sauce a little, pass it through cheesecloth, and serve it for your chops
