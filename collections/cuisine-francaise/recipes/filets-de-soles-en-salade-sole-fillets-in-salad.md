## Ingredients

- Sole fillets
- Four tablespoons of good vinegar
- Two spoonfuls of melted aspic
- Ten spoonfuls of oil
- Chopped ravigote
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Lettuce hearts
- Gherkins
- Capers
- Anchovies
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Use the fillets as those for Mayonnaise
2. Leave them whole or cut them into pieces
3. Put them in a saucepan
4. Make a seasoning composed of four tablespoons of good vinegar, two spoonfuls of melted aspic, ten spoonfuls of oil, chopped ravigote, salt, and coarse pepper
5. Mix everything well and pour it over your fillets that you will toss in it
6. Arrange the pieces correctly and pour your sauce over
7. Put lettuce hearts cut in quarters around the plate and decorate your salad with gherkins, capers, anchovies, croutons, etc.
