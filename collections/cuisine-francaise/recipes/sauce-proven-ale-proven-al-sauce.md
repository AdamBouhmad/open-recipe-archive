## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of fine oil
- chopped shallot and mushroom
- two whole cloves of garlic
- a pinch of flour
- bouillon
- one glass of white wine
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a bouquet of parsley and chives

## Directions

1. Put oil, chopped shallot and mushroom, and whole garlic cloves in a saucepan
2. Heat over the fire and add a pinch of flour
3. Moisten with bouillon and one glass of white wine
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Add a bouquet of parsley and chives
6. Boil over low heat for half an hour
7. Skim off fat leaving only enough oil so it appears oily and light
8. Remove the bouquet and garlic cloves
9. Serve as you judge appropriate
