## Ingredients

- lamb square
- good bouillon
- salt
- bouquet garni
- one liter of lentils
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb square into cutlets and cook them with good bouillon, very little salt, and a bouquet garni.
2. Cook one liter of lentils à la reine with bouillon.
3. When the lentils are cooked, pass them through a sieve to make a purée.
4. Mix the lentil purée with the cooked lamb cutlets and their seasonings.
5. If the sauce is too thin, reduce it on the fire.
6. Take a clean terrine suitable for serving at table and capable of withstanding fire.
7. Put the cutlets inside with half of the sauce and cover with bread crumbs toasted on one side.
8. Put the terrine in the oven and let it bubble for one hour.
9. When ready to serve, add the remaining sauce inside.
