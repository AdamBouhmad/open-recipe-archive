## Ingredients

- One tender young rabbit
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- A pinch of garlic
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Fine oil
- Lard
- White paper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the young rabbit into pieces
2. Marinate it with parsley, chives, mushrooms, a pinch of garlic (all chopped), salt, coarse pepper, and fine oil
3. Wrap each piece with its seasoning and a small piece of lard in white paper
4. Butter or brown the paper on the outside
5. Cook over very low heat on the grill, placing another greased sheet of paper underneath
6. Serve with the paper.
