## Ingredients

- three quarters of a liter of flour
- six egg yolks
- two whole eggs
- a little salt
- bouillon (sufficient for the batter to be liquid enough to pass through a perforated spoon)
- one spoonful of finely chopped parsley
- one quarter nutmeg, grated
- a pinch of coarse pepper
- good bouillon

## Directions

1. Dilute three quarters of a liter of flour with six egg yolks and two whole eggs, a little salt and bouillon (sufficiently so that your mixture is liquid enough to pass through a perforated spoon)
2. Add one spoonful of very finely chopped parsley that you mix with your dough, then one quarter grated nutmeg, a pinch of coarse pepper
3. When everything is well mixed, add to three quarters of a saucepan of good bouillon
4. When it boils, take a perforated spoon into which you pour your mixture and let it fall into your bouillon
5. Ensure it boils constantly so your dough sets
6. Take care to skim your soup so it is clear
7. This soup does not need to boil more than a quarter hour
8. You can also make this soup without meat using lean bouillon
