## Ingredients

- The liquid from your cooking (the moistening it produces)
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Pass the bottom of your cooking, that is to say the moistening it has produced, through a fine cloth or silk sieve
2. Be careful that it is very clear
3. Then reduce it in a casserole on high heat
4. When your moistening becomes thick like a sauce, that is to say when your glaze clings to the spoon, put it in a small casserole
5. Expose it to a bain-marie or over hot ashes so you can use it at the moment of service
6. Add a small piece of fresh butter to correct the salt
