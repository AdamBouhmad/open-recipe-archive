## Ingredients

- one ounce of maidenhair fern leaves
- one chopine of boiling water
- one pound of sugar
- one good glass of water

## Directions

1. Put the maidenhair fern leaves in a chopine of boiling water and remove immediately
2. Put them to infuse for at least twelve hours on hot ashes
3. Pass them through a sieve
4. Put one pound of sugar in a pan with a good glass of water
5. Make it boil and skim
6. Continue boiling until, dipping two fingers in fresh water and then in the sugar and quickly dipping them back in fresh water, the sugar that remains on your fingers breaks cleanly
7. Add your maidenhair fern water without boiling
8. Remove as soon as they are mixed together and put in a terrine
9. Cover and place on hot ashes, maintaining an even heat without burning for three days
10. You will know the syrup is done when, taking some syrup on a finger and pressing it against the other, opening both, a thread forms that does not break easily
11. Put it in bottles and do not cork them until they are completely cold
