## Ingredients

- one quarter of flour
- one ounce of fine salt
- two pounds of butter
- six whole eggs
- three glasses of water

## Directions

1. Prepare a quarter of flour on your pastry board
2. Make a hollow in the middle
3. Put one ounce of fine salt, two pounds of butter, six whole eggs, and three glasses of water
4. Work your butter with the eggs, salt and water
5. Mix your flour with the butter
6. Assemble your dough without kneading and give it four turns as explained in the puff pastry
7. This dough is used to make plumb cakes and other bases
