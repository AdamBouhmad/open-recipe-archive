## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- two ounces of saltpetre
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- peppercorns
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Have a beef tongue
2. Pound two ounces of saltpetre
3. Rub the tongue well all over with the pounded saltpetre
4. Put it in a terrine with thyme, bay leaf, basil, and peppercorns
5. Put two large handfuls of salt in boiling water
6. When the salt is dissolved and the water is cold, pour it over the tongue and leave it in the brine for five or six days or more
7. If you have the time, before cooking it, soak it to disgorge for two hours
8. Blanch it before cooking
