## Ingredients

- veal neck cut into chops
- melted lard
- parsley
- ciboule (chives)
- a bit of truffles
- salt
- pepper
- ham slice

## Directions

1. Cut the neck by chops; remove the bones and leave only the chop
2. Place them in a pan with melted lard, parsley, ciboule, a bit of truffles, salt, pepper, all chopped very fine; a slice of ham
3. Cover with a bard of lard and cook over a small fire with ashes
4. When they are cooked, remove them from the pan and arrange them on the plate you must serve, remove the slice of ham that is in the pan, and put it aside
5. Degrease the sauce, put it on the fire, and serve it over the chops
