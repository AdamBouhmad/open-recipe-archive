## Ingredients

- 15 or 18 onions, all of the same size
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- 1 glass of water
- sugar, the size of half an egg
- butter
- buttered paper
- Spanish sauce or flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Peel the onions carefully, taking care not to cut the head and tail too much
2. Butter the bottom of a casserole and add the onions placed on their head side
3. Add salt, a little coarse pepper, a glass of water, sugar the size of half an egg, and as much butter
4. Place a round of buttered paper over the onions
5. Cook on a fairly hot fire
6. When the liquid is reduced by half, place on a gentle fire
7. At the moment of serving, glaze the onions
8. Arrange them on the plate and add Spanish sauce, or alternatively: take a teaspoon of flour mixed with the glaze of the onions, add half a glass of bouillon, turn the sauce, and when it is bound, pour it under the onions
9. Check that it is properly seasoned
