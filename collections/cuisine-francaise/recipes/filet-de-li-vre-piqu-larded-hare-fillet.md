## Ingredients

- hare, skinned
- larding bacon
- veal slices
- carrot slices
- onion slices
- thyme
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. When your hare is skinned, thrust your knife along the spine of the back from the shoulder to the thigh, detaching the fillet
2. Slide your fingers between the bones and the fillet and detach it so that the thick end of the fillet still holds to the thigh
3. Then place the blade of your knife on the tender side of the fillet and your thumb on the skin side, press your blade on your fillet so that it comes to meet your thumb without cutting the skin
4. Then act as if you are pulling the fillet toward you; the sinewy skin remains and your fillet is found detached and trimmed at the same time
5. If some skin still remained, you would lightly pare your fillet by turning it
6. Lard the fillet with fine bacon and give it the shape you wish, whether long, round, or otherwise
7. Put lard bacon in a casserole, a few slices of veal, a few slices of carrots and onions, a little thyme and bay leaf
8. Place your fillets on this
