## Ingredients

- Rib of beef from under the shoulder
- Oil or butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take the rib of beef found under the shoulder
2. Trim it so only the rib bone remains, which you clear at the end
3. Beat it to tenderize it
4. Dip your rib in oil or butter
5. Season it with salt and pepper
6. Grill it over gentle heat so your rib does not burn and cooks slowly
7. It requires half an hour if your rib is not very thick; if it is, it requires three-quarters of an hour or an hour so the heat can penetrate it well
8. When it is cooked, put clear jus underneath
