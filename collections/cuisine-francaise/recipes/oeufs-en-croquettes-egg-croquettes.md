## Ingredients

- 18 hard eggs
- cream sauce
- parsley and chives, finely chopped and washed
- bread crumb
- beaten egg
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Hard boil eighteen eggs
2. Cut the whites and yolks into small dice
3. Put them in a saucepan
4. Make a cream sauce in which you put a little finely chopped and washed parsley and chives
5. Pour it over your eggs; they should be somewhat bound together
6. Stir them in their sauce
7. Let the mixture cool
8. Take a spoonful and pour it onto a board
9. When the eggs are well cold, shape them into croquettes
10. Roll them in bread crumb
11. Dip in beaten egg and bread them a second time
12. At the moment of service, put them in very hot oil
13. When they have a nice color, drain them on white cloth or in a strainer
14. Arrange them on the dish
