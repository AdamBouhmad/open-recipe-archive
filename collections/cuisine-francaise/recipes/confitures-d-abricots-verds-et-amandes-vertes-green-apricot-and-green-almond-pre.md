## Ingredients

- Green apricots or green almonds
- Water
- Sugar, one pound per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. After you have removed the down from the apricots or almonds as marked previously for green apricot compotes on page 481, make them cook in water in the same manner until by pricking them with a pin it enters easily and the apricot falls by itself
2. Then clarify sugar and put one pound of sugar for one pound of fruit
3. Boil your sugar for four or five consecutive days, morning and evening, without your fruit, which you put to drain on a sieve
4. Put it back in a terrine and pour your sugar over it, which must be only slightly more than lukewarm
5. When your apricots and almonds are well green, your preserve will be made
