## Ingredients

- half a pound of lard, cut into lardons
- parsley
- chives
- a small pinch of garlic
- one bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a veal rack fillet

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of lard into lardons and mix them with parsley, chives, a small pinch of garlic, all chopped, one bay leaf, thyme, basil chopped like powder, salt, and coarse pepper.
2. Lard the fillet of a veal rack with all of this after having cut the bones that are at the base.
