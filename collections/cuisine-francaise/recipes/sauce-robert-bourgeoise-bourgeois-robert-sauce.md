## Ingredients

- butter
- one spoonful of flour
- three large finely chopped onions
- butter for cooking the onion
- bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a dash of vinegar and mustard

## Directions

1. Melt butter with one spoonful of flour in a saucepan and brown the flour over low heat
2. When it is a good color, add three large finely chopped onions and enough butter to cook the onion
3. Moisten with bouillon
4. Skim off fat and let boil for half an hour
5. When ready to serve, add salt, coarse pepper, a dash of vinegar and mustard
6. Use this sauce for fresh pork and turkey
