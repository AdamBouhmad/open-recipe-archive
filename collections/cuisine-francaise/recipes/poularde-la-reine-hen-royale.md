## Ingredients

- poularde (hen)
- bardes de lard (lard lardons)
- chevilles de bois (wooden pegs)
- velouté (velvet sauce)
- jaune d'œuf (egg yolk)
- beurre (butter)
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- sel (salt)

## Directions

1. When your hen is cooked in a pan, let it cool
2. Remove the meat from the stomach in the shape of an oval hole the length of your piece
3. With the meat make a cooked forcemeat
4. Fill your hen and give it its original form
5. Envelope the around of your fowl with lard lardons which you will secure with small wooden pegs
6. Put it on a tourtière in the oven, or place your tourtière on a low fire with the country oven a little warm above for a good hour
7. At the moment of serving, remove the lardons that surround your hen
8. Dress it on your platter
9. Add underneath a reduced velouté in which you will put a liaison of an egg yolk and butter the size of half an egg, a little coarse pepper
10. Check that your sauce is properly salted
