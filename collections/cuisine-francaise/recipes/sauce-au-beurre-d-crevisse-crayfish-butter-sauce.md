## Ingredients

- Velvet sauce, worked as needed for saucing
- Crayfish butter, amount of an egg

## Directions

1. Have the velvet sauce worked as needed for saucing ready
2. At the moment of serving, add crayfish butter the size of an egg to your very hot velouté
3. Stir your sauce well so that the butter binds with it
4. If your sauce does not have enough color, add a little more crayfish butter
5. This sauce can also be made in a meatless version using meatless velouté
