## Ingredients

- A charbonnée or beef rib
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil or butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Shallots
- Mushrooms
- Basil powder
- White paper

## Directions

1. Take a beef rib, cut it neatly and cook it on a low fire with bouillon or water, a little salt and pepper
2. When cooked, reduce the sauce so it sticks entirely to the rib
3. Then marinate it with oil or butter, parsley, chives, shallots, and mushrooms, all chopped very finely, with a little basil powder
4. Put the rib in a sheet of white paper with all its marinade
5. Fold the paper like a papillote and grease it on the outside
6. Put it on a grill with a greased sheet of paper underneath
7. Grill on a low fire on both sides
8. Serve with the paper
