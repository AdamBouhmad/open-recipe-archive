## Ingredients

- ten fillets of young rabbit
- truffles
- salt
- coarse pepper
- three quarterons of butter
- nutmeg
- game stock
- two egg yolks
- butter the size of half an egg
- croutons

## Directions

1. Trim and prepare ten fillets, removing the nerve-like skin
2. Cut the pieces one and a half inches long
3. Make an incision at equal distance in which you will place a half-circle of truffles, putting six pieces of truffle in each piece of fillet, ensuring they penetrate slightly into the flesh
4. When the ten fillets are thus prepared, put the prepared pieces in your sauté pan
5. Season with salt and coarse pepper
6. Warm three quarterons of butter in which you will grate a little nutmeg, and pour it over your fillets
7. At the moment of serving, put your sauté pan on an ardent stove; when your fillets are stiffened on one side, turn them to the other; leave them only an instant
8. Test by pressing your finger on top; if your piece resists, it is cooked
9. Tilt your sauté pan so the butter goes to one side with a spoon
10. Put your fillets on top; when they are drained, place them in a casserole
11. Pour in a velouté worked with rabbit stock
12. Bind your sauce with two egg yolks and butter the size of half an egg that you will melt while binding your sauce
13. Pass it through a sieve over your fillets
14. Toss them and dress them immediately on your dish so your sauce does not separate
15. Check that it is properly salted and put croutons around the dish
