## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- good broth
- veal juice

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers neatly.
2. Cook them in a small pot with good broth and veal juice for coloring.
3. When cooked, simmer the soup with their broth and the broth from the pot.
4. Season the soup with good salt.
5. Serve garnished with cucumbers.
