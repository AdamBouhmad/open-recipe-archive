## Ingredients

- Velouté
- Two spoonfuls of lemon juice
- Coarse pepper
- A little grated nutmeg
- Four hard-boiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, have prepared velouté with two spoonfuls of lemon juice, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
2. Pass four hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve to form a vermicelli-like consistency
3. At the moment of serving, add the yolks to your hot sauce
4. Be careful not to let the sauce boil when the yolks are in it
5. Ensure the sauce is well seasoned
