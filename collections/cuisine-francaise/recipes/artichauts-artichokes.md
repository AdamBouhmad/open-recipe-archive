## Ingredients

- three artichokes of the same size
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take three artichokes of the same size and remove what is hard found at the heart
2. Trim the leaves by removing the tips
3. Wash them well and place them in a pot with boiling salted water
4. Ensure they are submerged in water
5. After one hour, ensure they are cooked
6. Remove them from the boiling water and place them in cold water
7. Remove the choke
8. At the moment of serving, place them in boiling water
9. Drain them and place them on a plate with white or brown sauce inside or in a sauceboat
