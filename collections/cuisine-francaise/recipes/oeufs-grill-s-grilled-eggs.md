## Ingredients

- white paper
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- chives
- a point of garlic
- salt
- coarse pepper
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Take a large sheet of white paper and cut it into eight small equal squares
2. Fold each square in half to make small cases and butter them inside and out
3. Mix a good piece of butter with half a handful of breadcrumbs, parsley, chives, a point of garlic, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Put this mixture in the bottom of your cases
5. Break an egg into each case
6. Season the top with a little fine salt and coarse pepper
7. Cook them over a small fire on the grill
8. Pass a hot shovel over them
9. Serve with runny yolks in the paper cases
