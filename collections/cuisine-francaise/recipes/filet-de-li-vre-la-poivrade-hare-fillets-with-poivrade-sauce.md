## Ingredients

- one hare or young hare roasted on a spit and served from table
- sauce à la poivrade of strong flavour

## Directions

1. Take a hare or young hare that has been roasted on a spit and served from table
2. Remove the meat to cut it into fillets; if you do not have enough to garnish a dish, leave the bones and cut the pieces large and of equal size
3. Put them in a saucepan with a strong-flavoured poivrade sauce
4. Heat without boiling
5. Serve hot
6. You will find the poivrade sauce in the section on sauces
