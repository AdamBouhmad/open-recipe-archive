## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water
- Salt
- Brine
- Oil

## Directions

1. After washing artichokes well, let them drain
2. Trim the base by removing the stem and cutting off the outer surface
3. Have a large pot three-quarters full of water with two handfuls of salt
4. When the water boils, add about a dozen artichokes
5. Let them boil; after a quarter hour, remove them and place in cold water
6. Let them drain for five or six hours
7. Arrange them in a large earthenware pot or butter pot
8. Pour brine over them so they are covered; also add one or two pounds of oil depending on the width of your dish
9. To cook, place in cold water on the fire and boil until cooked, then cool and remove the fuzzy center
10. Serve with sauce or in another manner
