## Ingredients

- eight calf's ears, blanched
- lard bards
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Soak eight calf's ears that have been blanched
2. Blanch them
3. Refresh them in cold water
4. Singe them
5. Wipe them dry
6. Slit the tip of the ear, cutting to the middle
7. Put lard bards on the bottom of a casserole
8. Place the ears on them
9. Cover them with lard bards
10. Pour a poêle over them and cover the inside of the casserole with buttered paper
11. Simmer for two and a half hours
12. Drain them
13. Arrange them on the platter and sauce them with an Italian sauce. If you do not have a poêle, place them between two lard bards and prepare a short blanc, pour it over them, and cook them.
