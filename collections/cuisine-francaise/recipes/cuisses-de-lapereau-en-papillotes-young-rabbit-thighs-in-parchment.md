## Ingredients

- Young rabbit thighs
- Medium lardons seasoned with pounded aromatics, salt, and pepper
- A quarteron of butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Fine herbs prepared for papillotes
- Paper for papillotes
- Thin bards of lard

## Directions

1. Bone the thighs down to the joint of the foreleg
2. Lard them with medium lardons seasoned with pounded aromatics, salt, and pepper
3. Put ten thighs in a casserole with a quarteron of butter
4. Leave them on the fire for a good half-hour
5. Add salt, coarse pepper, and fine herbs prepared for papillotes
6. Simmer everything together for ten minutes
7. Place the thighs on a plate with their seasoning on top
8. Let them cool
9. Prepare paper for your papillotes
10. Put a thin bard of lard above and below your thigh with its seasoning
11. Fold the paper so that the butter does not escape
12. Tie the end with string
13. Half an hour before serving, put them on the grill over a very gentle fire
14. Turn them when one side has color
15. At the moment of serving, arrange them in crowns on your plate with a clear jus underneath, or without sauce
