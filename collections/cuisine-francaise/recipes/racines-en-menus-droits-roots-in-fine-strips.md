## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- carrots
- parsnips
- celery
- turnips
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- mustard

## Directions

1. Take onion that you cut into strips
2. Cook them in a small roux made with butter and flour
3. When the onion is almost cooked, moisten it with bouillon and finish cooking it
4. Take carrots, parsnips, celery, turnips that have been cooked in the pot, cut them quickly into strips, and put them in the onion ragout
5. Season with salt, pepper, and a dash of vinegar
6. When serving, add mustard
