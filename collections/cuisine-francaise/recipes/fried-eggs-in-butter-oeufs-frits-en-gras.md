## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lard or melted butter

## Directions

1. Heat lard or melted butter in a pan until very hot.
2. Add eggs one at a time and fry them.
3. Turn them in the pan to keep them round.
4. Do not allow the yolk to harden.
5. Serve.
