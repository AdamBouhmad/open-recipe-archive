## Ingredients

- cardoons
- water
- bouillon
- flour
- salt
- onions
- roots
- bouquet of fine herbs
- verjus or verjus in grain
- butter
- coulis

## Directions

1. Cut them to the length of three inches, do not use those that are hollow and green
2. Cook them half an hour in water and remove them in lukewarm water to peel them
3. Cook them with bouillon in which you have dissolved a spoonful of flour, add salt, onions, roots, a bouquet of fine herbs, a dash of verjus or verjus in grain, a little butter
4. When cooked, remove them and put them in a good coulis with a little bouillon
5. Make them boil half an hour in this sauce so they take flavor and serve them
6. The sauce should be neither too clear nor too thick, and of a beautiful blonde color
7. If you wish to serve them in the lean way, put them in a sauce as is said for chard with leeks
