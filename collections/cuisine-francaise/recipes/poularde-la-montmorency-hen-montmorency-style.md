## Ingredients

- one hen
- livers cut in dice
- small lard
- small eggs

## Directions

1. Prick the top of the hen; after flaming and cleaning it, fill it with livers cut in dice, small lard, small eggs
2. Sew the hen so nothing escapes
3. Cook it like a fricandeau and glaze it the same way
