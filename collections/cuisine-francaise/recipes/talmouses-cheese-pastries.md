## Ingredients

- a piece of water
- half a quarter pound of butter
- salt
- two tablespoons of flour
- eggs
- fresh cheese with cream, well drained and made the same day
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Put water, butter, and a little salt in a saucepan; bring to boil
2. Add two tablespoons of flour that you thin well until your dough is firm
3. Remove from fire and thin in as many eggs as the dough can absorb without becoming liquid
4. Add fresh white cheese with cream that is well drained and made the same day, mixing it with your dough
5. Take molds for small pastries
6. Put in an abaisse of puff pastry dough, rolled out very thin
7. Place on small pastry molds so it overhangs at four corners
8. Place your cheese dough about the size of a small egg on top and wrap it with the four corners of the puff pastry
9. Gild with beaten egg
10. Bake in the oven with gentle heat
11. When cooked and golden colored, serve hot as an entremet
