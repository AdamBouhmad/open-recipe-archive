## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Onion slices
- Parsley sprigs
- Cloves
- White wine
- Small onions
- Butter
- Mushrooms
- Roux

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as in the previous recipe
2. Place in a casserole with salt, coarse pepper, two bay leaves, thyme, onion slices, parsley sprigs, two cloves and enough white wine to submerge the fish
3. Place over high heat
4. Once the fish is cooked, pass the broth through a silk sieve
5. Transfer the fish to another casserole and trim it well
6. Have thirty or forty small onions, peeled and of the same size
7. Sauté them in butter until golden brown
8. Drain them on a sieve
9. Make a roux proportional to the stew
10. Dilute it with the wine in which the fish cooked
11. Add mushrooms and reduce the sauce by a third
12. Add the small onions and decrease the sauce
13. Check the taste
14. When the onions are cooked, remove the garnish with a slotted spoon
15. Place it on the fish and strain the sauce over it
16. Keep the stew hot
17. At the moment of serving, arrange on a plat with bread crusts sautéed in butter and garnish with crayfish
