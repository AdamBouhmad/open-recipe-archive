## Ingredients

- three large artichokes
- butter, half a pound
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. If the three artichokes are large, cut them into eight pieces
2. Remove the hard part found at the heart, the choke, and the leaves until only three or four remain
3. Reduce their width
4. Place them in water as you trim them
5. Wash them well and drain them
6. Put half a pound of butter in the bottom of your pot, spread it well, and place the artichoke heart on top
7. Sprinkle salt and pepper on the artichokes
8. Half an hour before serving, place them on a moderately hot fire and place fire on the pot cover as well
9. Take care that they do not burn
10. When cooked, arrange them on the plate and pour the butter over them
11. You may also sauté them in butter on a hot fire; when they are browned, they are ready
