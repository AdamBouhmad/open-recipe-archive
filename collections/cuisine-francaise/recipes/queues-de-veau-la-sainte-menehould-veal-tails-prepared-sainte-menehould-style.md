## Ingredients

- three veal tails
- rich bouillon
- parsley
- ciboule (chives)
- one clove of garlic
- three cloves
- two shallots
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- one onion
- one carrot
- one parsnip
- three egg yolks
- a good pinch of flour

## Directions

1. Take three veal tails that you cut in two
2. Blanch them briefly in boiling water
3. Place them in a small pot with rich bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, ciboule, one clove of garlic, three cloves, two shallots, a bay leaf, thyme, basil, salt, pepper, one onion, one carrot, one parsnip
4. Boil until they are cooked and very little sauce remains
5. Remove them to cool
6. Pass the sauce through a fine sieve so that the fat passes through; there should remain about half a glass
7. Put it in a pan with three egg yolks diluted with a good pinch of flour
8. Make it bind over the fire so that it is a bit thick
9. Then dip the veal tails in it
