## Ingredients

- two large onions
- butter
- one calf's liver
- three quarterons of beef fat
- bread crumb dried over fire

## Directions

1. Cut two large onions into small dice
2. Put in a casserole with a piece of butter and pass over the fire until cooked
3. Chop a calf's liver with three quarterons of beef fat
4. Put in a mortar with the cooked onion and dried bread crumb
