## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- White sauce, thickened
- Frying batter
- Oil for frying
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflowers three-quarters done.
2. Make a white sauce that is somewhat thickened; sauté the cauliflowers in it and let them cool.
3. At the moment of serving, dip the cauliflowers in frying batter and put them in moderately hot oil.
4. When golden, remove them and let them drain on white cloth.
5. Dress them on your plate.
6. Alternatively, when they are cooked and well drained, place them in a terrine with salt, pepper, and vinegar; sauté them in this seasoning; put them in the frying batter and fry them.
