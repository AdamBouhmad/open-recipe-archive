## Ingredients

- Sheep tails
- Chicory
- Butter
- Flour
- Stock from cooking the tails
- Coarse pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tails as described for braised tails
2. At the time of serving, drain them and glaze them
3. Put them on chicory
4. If you do not have sauce to cook your chicory, put a piece of butter in a saucepan
5. Chop your chicory and put it with your butter
6. Pass it over the fire for ten minutes
7. Put a good pinch of flour in it, which you blend with your chicory
8. Pass the stock in which your tails cooked, removing the fat, and moisten your chicory with it
9. Add a little coarse pepper
10. Reduce your chicory until it is thick
11. Ensure it is well salted
12. Put it on the plate with your glazed tails on top
