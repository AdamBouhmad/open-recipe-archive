## Ingredients

- two beef palates cooked in water
- lemon
- salt
- parsley
- chives
- flour
- fine oil
- beer

## Directions

1. Peel the cooked palates and cut them like matchsticks
2. Marinate with lemon, a little salt, parsley sprigs, and whole chives
3. When they have taken on flavor, drain them
4. Make a batter: put two good handfuls of flour in a saucepan with a spoonful of fine oil and a little fine salt
5. Gradually add beer until the batter has the consistency of double cream
6. Dip the beef palate pieces in the batter and fry them to a beautiful color
7. Serve as hot as possible
