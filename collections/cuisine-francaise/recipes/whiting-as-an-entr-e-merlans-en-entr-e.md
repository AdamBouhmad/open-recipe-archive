## Ingredients

- Whiting
- Flour
- Oil or fat for frying
- Butter
- Capers
- Anchovies

## Directions

1. Prepare and fry the whiting as above
2. Remove the head and center bone
3. Arrange the fillets on the serving dish with the white side up
4. Put a white sauce with capers and anchovies on top
