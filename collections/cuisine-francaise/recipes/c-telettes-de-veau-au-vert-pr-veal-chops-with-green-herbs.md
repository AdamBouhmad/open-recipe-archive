## Ingredients

- veal chops
- butter
- parsley
- ciboule (chives)
- half a clove of garlic
- two cloves
- bay leaf
- pinch of flour
- bouillon
- one glass of white wine
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter kneaded with flour
- blanched chervil
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Place veal chops in a pan with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, ciboule, half a clove of garlic, two cloves, a bay leaf
2. Pass them over the fire and add a pinch of flour, moisten with bouillon and one glass of white wine
3. Season with salt and coarse pepper, cook over a small fire and degrease
4. When cooking is done and the sauce is short, add a piece of butter kneaded with flour the size of a walnut, with a good pinch of blanched and chopped chervil
5. Make the sauce bind, serving a squeeze of lemon juice or a dash of vinegar
