## Ingredients

- Butter
- Mushrooms
- Parsley and chives, chopped
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Sliced veal
- Egg yolks
- Verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter in a casserole.
2. Add mushrooms if you have them.
3. Pass through your butter chopped parsley and chives.
4. Put a little flour in it.
5. Moisten with a little bouillon, a little salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg.
6. Put your sliced veal in your sauce.
7. Keep it warm.
8. At the moment of eating, add to it a liaison of three egg yolks, or more according to how strong your blanquette is.
9. When it is thickened, add to it a little verjuice or a dash of vinegar.
