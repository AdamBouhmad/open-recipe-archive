## Ingredients

- 2 pints of good eau-de-vie
- 2 gros of angelica seeds
- 1 ounce of coriander seeds
- 1 good pinch of fennel
- as much anise
- juice of 2 lemons with the zests of the peels
- 1 pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Coarsely crush the seeds in a mortar
2. Put the eau-de-vie in a large glass or stoneware bottle
3. Add the crushed seeds, lemon juice with zests, and sugar
4. Let infuse in the bottle for four or five days
5. Stir the bottle from time to time to dissolve the sugar
6. Pass the liqueur through cotton or gray paper to make it clearer
7. Put it in bottles and cork them well
