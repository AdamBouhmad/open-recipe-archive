## Ingredients

- croutons
- lentil purée
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Have croutons (as in the preceding article) square
2. Pour a lentil purée at the top (see Purée de Potage)
3. Clarify it and keep it clear enough
4. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg
5. Take care to melt it without putting it on the fire
6. Do not neglect good salt
7. Pour your purée only at the moment of serving
