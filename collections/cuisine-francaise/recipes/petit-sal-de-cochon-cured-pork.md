## Ingredients

- Pork (various cuts, loin preferred)
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut pork into pieces of desired size
2. Crush salt; for every 15 pounds of meat, use 1 pound of salt
3. Rub the meat thoroughly with salt
4. Place in a vessel as it is salted
5. When full, seal the vessel well to prevent air exposure
6. Can be used after 5 or 6 days
7. For longer storage, use slightly more salt
8. Note that fresher cured meat is better
9. Serve with pea purée, cabbage ragout, vegetable ragout, lentil purée, or turnip purée
10. Do not add salt to the accompanying ragout
