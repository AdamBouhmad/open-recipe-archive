## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- white wine
- good broth
- onions
- shallots
- chives
- mushrooms
- parsley
- basil powder
- coarse pepper
- three lamb tongues, cooked in water
- thin bread slices

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole a piece of good soft butter the size of an egg with a good pinch of flour
2. Moisten with a glass of white wine and good broth
3. Add chopped onions, shallots, chives, mushrooms, and parsley, with basil powder and coarse pepper
4. Make this sauce thicken on the fire, boiling until it is thick
5. Have three lamb tongues cooked in water, cut them into thin fillets
6. In the dish you must serve, put a layer of onion at the bottom, then a layer of tongues with sauce, continuing to alternate layers of tongues and sauce, finishing with onion
7. Surround all the sides of your meat with small bread slices cut neatly, panaded with sauce
8. Glaze it in the oven if not too much, wipe the edges, and serve
