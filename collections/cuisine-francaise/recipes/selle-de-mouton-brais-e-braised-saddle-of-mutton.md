## Ingredients

- half a saddle of mutton from the first rib to the leg
- salt
- pepper
- lard strips for barding
- three carrots
- four onions
- two cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley and chives bouquet
- one tablespoon of bouillon
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Debone the saddle
2. Season the interior with salt and pepper
3. Roll it to present a long square shape
4. Tie it with string
5. Place lard strips in a casserole
6. Put the saddle in the casserole with three carrots, four onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme, and a bouquet of parsley and chives
7. Cover the saddle with lard strips
8. Add the trimmings from the saddle
9. Add one tablespoon of bouillon and a round of buttered paper
10. Cook the saddle for two and a half hours over and under fire
11. At the moment of serving, drain it, remove the string, remove the skin from above, glaze it
12. Serve underneath what you wish, such as chicory, spinach, a puree of chard, a puree of mushrooms; you can put lettuce around it, sauce underneath, glazed onions, or serve it English style, that is to say, when you have removed the skin, instead of glazing it, sprinkle very fine parsley and clear juice underneath
