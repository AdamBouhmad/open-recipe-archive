## Ingredients

- Five catillard pears
- One tablespoon of syrup
- Four tablespoons of water

## Directions

1. Cut the pears in half
2. Remove the core and peel
3. Place them in a small recently tinned copper pot
4. Add one tablespoon of syrup and four tablespoons of water
5. Simmer for two hours or longer if not cooked
6. When cooked to the proper degree, arrange on a serving dish
7. Reduce the syrup if too thin and pour over the pears
