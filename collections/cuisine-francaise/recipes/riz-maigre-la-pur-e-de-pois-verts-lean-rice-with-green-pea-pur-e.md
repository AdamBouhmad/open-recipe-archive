## Ingredients

- Rice
- Lean bouillon
- Green pea purée

## Directions

1. Wash your rice in four or five lukewarm waters
2. Moisten your rice with lean bouillon
3. Boil it for one hour and a half
4. When your rice is cooked, one hour and a half before serving your soup, add your purée
5. Watch that your purée binds your soup, and that it is neither too thick nor too thin
