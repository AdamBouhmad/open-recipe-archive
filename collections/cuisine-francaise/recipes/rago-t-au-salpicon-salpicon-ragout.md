## Ingredients

- One sweetbread of veal, blanched
- Two artichoke bottoms, blanched
- Mushrooms
- A bouquet of parsley, chives
- Half a clove of garlic
- One clove
- Half a bay leaf
- A little basil
- A piece of butter
- A good pinch of flour
- Meat juice
- White wine
- A little bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one blanched veal sweetbread, two blanched artichoke bottoms, and mushrooms, all cut in dice, with a bouquet of parsley and chives, half a clove of garlic, one clove, half a bay leaf, a little basil, and a piece of butter
2. Pass it over the fire and add a good pinch of flour
3. Moisten with meat juice, white wine, a little bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Cook and reduce to a short sauce
5. Degrease before serving
