## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- spinach
- butter
- espagnole sauce or consommé
- coarse pepper
- flour
- cooking liquid from the tongue

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue as for the gherkin version
2. Cut it into slices so that you may arrange it in miroton style and glaze it
3. When the tongue is cut, make a border around the plate
4. Have spinach that you have sautéed in butter and moistened with espagnole or consommé
5. Pour the spinach into the middle of your plate or arrange the pieces of tongue on top of the spinach
6. You may also put the whole tongue; take care to glaze it
7. If lacking sauce, sauté the spinach in butter, add a little coarse pepper, and when well browned, sprinkle a good pinch of flour and mix with the spinach
8. Moisten the spinach with the cooking liquid from the tongue, passed through a silk sieve and well degreased
9. Moisten enough so that the spinach is somewhat liquid, so that you may reduce it and it becomes somewhat thick and takes on flavor
10. Do not add salt to the spinach, because the cooking liquid is already seasoned
