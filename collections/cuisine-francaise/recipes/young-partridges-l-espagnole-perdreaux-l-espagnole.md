## Ingredients

- three young partridges of equal size
- butter
- lemon juice
- coarse pepper
- ham slice
- espagnole sauce
- white wine
- bay leaf
- parsley and chives
- clove

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean three young partridges of the same size
2. Singe lightly
3. Truss and tie them as for a braised hen
4. Put a quarter pound of butter in a casserole with the juice of a lemon, a little coarse pepper, and a slice of ham
5. Place the partridges on a moderate heat
6. Make them turn gently so they do not brown
7. When they are well turned, add six tablespoons of espagnole, half a bottle of white wine, a bay leaf, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and a clove
8. Simmer for three-quarters of an hour
9. Remove when cooked and place in a casserole
10. Degrease the sauce and reduce by half
11. Pass the sauce through cheesecloth over the partridges
12. At service, untie them and dress on a platter
13. Serve the sauce under them
