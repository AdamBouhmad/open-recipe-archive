## Ingredients

- butter
- oil
- lemon zest
- parsley
- chives
- nutmeg
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put a good piece of butter in a casserole and cut it into several pieces
2. Pour three tablespoons of oil over it with the zest of half a lemon, parsley, well-chopped chives, a little grated nutmeg, a small pinch of flour, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Peel potatoes fresh from boiling water and cut them into quarters, or sixths if too large
4. Stir them on the fire in the seasoning without boiling
5. At the moment of serving, add lemon juice
