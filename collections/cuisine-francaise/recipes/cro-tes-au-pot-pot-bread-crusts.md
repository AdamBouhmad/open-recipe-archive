## Ingredients

- crusts of bread
- broth
- pot fat
- three entries of bread
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Take crusts of bread that are not too thick
2. Mince them
3. Put them in a shallow dish
4. Pour broth and pot fat over them
5. Put on the fire until they are gratinéed
6. Take three entries of bread, remove the crumb
7. Soak them in pot fat broth
8. Season with a little salt and coarse pepper
9. Place them upright on your gratin
10. At the moment of serving, drain the fat so the soup is dry
11. Put broth in a vessel so each person can pour as much as they wish on their plate with bread and gratin
