## Ingredients

- three beef palates cooked in water
- bouillon
- garlic clove
- two cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- meat forcemeat
- flour
- white wine
- fine oil

## Directions

1. Peel the cooked palates and cut them in two crosswise through their entire length
2. Let them take on flavor for half an hour by simmering over low heat with bouillon, a garlic clove, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, salt, and pepper
3. Drain and cool them
4. Place on each piece some seasoned meat forcemeat about the thickness of a small coin
5. Roll the beef palate pieces and dip them in a batter made with flour diluted with a spoonful of oil and half a setier of white wine, and fine salt
6. The batter should be thin enough to pour from a spoon without being too thin
7. Fry them and serve garnished with fried parsley
