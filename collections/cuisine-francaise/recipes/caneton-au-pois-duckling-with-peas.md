## Ingredients

- One or two ducklings
- Flour
- Butter
- Bouillon
- One litre of small peas
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scald and eviscerate one or two ducklings
2. Truss the legs so that only the claws show
3. Blanch them briefly in boiling water
4. Make a light roux with two pinches of flour and a piece of butter
5. Moisten with bouillon
6. Add the ducklings with one litre of small peas and a bouquet of parsley and chives
7. Boil gently until the ducklings are cooked
8. A moment before serving, add a little salt
9. Serve with short sauce
