## Ingredients

- cutlets
- turnip slices
- lard strips

## Directions

1. Cut and trim your cutlets
2. Cook them as the cutlets with cucumbers
3. Cover them with turnip slices and lard strips
4. Simmer them for two hours
5. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, and arrange them in crowns on the platter
6. Place your turnips in the middle
