## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- Very good oil
- Sage

## Directions

1. When your pig is scalded and trussed, spit it through the rear
2. Make the spit come out through the mouth
3. Baste it with very good oil so the skin takes on the desired crispness
4. It must be very well cooked
5. Place a bouquet of sage in its belly
