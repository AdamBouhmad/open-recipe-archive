## Ingredients

- turkey wings and breast
- parsley, chives, shallots, basil, mushrooms
- salt, coarse pepper
- lard strips
- half a glass of white wine
- coulis
- lemon juice or verjuice

## Directions

1. Using the wings and breast from a turkey whose legs have been removed for other preparations, cut them very thinly into filets
2. Arrange them in a saucepan in several layers one on top of the other
3. Season each layer with chopped parsley, chives, shallots, basil, mushrooms, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Cover with a few lard strips
5. Cook over low heat on hot ashes
6. At the midpoint of cooking, add half a glass of white wine
7. Finish cooking and degrease the sauce before serving it over the escalopes
8. If you have coulis, add two spoonfuls to bind the sauce, then add lemon juice or a dash of verjuice
