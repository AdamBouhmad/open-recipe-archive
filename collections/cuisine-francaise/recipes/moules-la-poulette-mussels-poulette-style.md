## Ingredients

- mussels
- butter
- chives
- parsley
- flour
- pepper
- nutmeg
- eggs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After scraping your mussels well and washing them thoroughly, put them dry in a saucepan over a hot fire; sauté them
2. As they open, remove the shell and put them in another saucepan
3. Pass the water from your mussels through a silk sieve
4. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan, chopped chives that you will brown in the butter; add your parsley, then brown it a little
5. Mix in a spoonful of flour that you will blend with your butter
6. Moisten your herbs with the mussel water; add a little pepper and grated nutmeg
7. Let your sauce come to a boil a few times; add a liaison of two or three eggs, depending on the quantity of mussels, and put them in your sauce
8. Sauté them and keep them hot without letting them boil
9. At the moment of service, add lemon juice and arrange them on your plate
