## Ingredients

- sirloin fillet
- large lardons
- spices
- thyme
- bay leaves
- salt
- lard strips
- veal slices
- beef slices
- vegetables such as carrots and onions
- bouquet garni
- Malaga wine

## Directions

1. Trim your fillet as in the previous recipes
2. Lard it with large lardons in which you put spices, a little thyme, two bay leaves chopped or crushed, a little fine salt
3. When your fillet is larded, tie it
4. Put in the bottom of a pot strips of lard, two slices of veal, two slices of beef, vegetables such as carrots, onions, a bouquet garni
5. Place your fillet in your pot; garnish it with strips of lard and a half-bottle of Malaga wine, or three-quarters of a bottle, according to how large your fillet is
