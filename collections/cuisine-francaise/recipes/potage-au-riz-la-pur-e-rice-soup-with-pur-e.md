## Ingredients

- Rice
- Bouillon
- Purée of choice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Use less liquid in your rice so that purée can be added
2. When your rice is well cooked, pour your purée at the moment of serving
3. Be careful to degrease your soup well
4. Season with good salt
