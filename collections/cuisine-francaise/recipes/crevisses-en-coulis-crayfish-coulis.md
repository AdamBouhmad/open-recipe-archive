## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Boiling water
- Fresh water
- Good bouillon (meat or fish depending on use)
- Vegetables for bouillon

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish briefly in boiling water
2. Remove them and place in fresh water
3. Shell out the tails and set aside along with the shells
4. Pound the shells for three hours until fine
5. Dilute the pounded shells in good bouillon and pass through a strainer
6. If making a ragout, cook the crayfish tails in a little bouillon and reduce almost dry, then add to the coulis
7. Season to taste
8. Heat without boiling
9. For a soup, keep the coulis thinner and add to the soup when half-cooked, heating without boiling
