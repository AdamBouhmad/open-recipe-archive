## Ingredients

- One chopine of double cream, or other good cream
- One half-setier of milk
- One egg yolk
- Three quarterons of sugar
- Essences such as orange flowers, bergamot, sweet lime or citron

## Directions

1. Take one chopine of double cream or other good cream, one half-setier of milk, one egg yolk, and three quarterons of sugar
2. Bring to five or six boils and remove from the fire
3. Add several essences such as orange flowers, bergamot, sweet lime or citron
4. Place the mixture in your tin mold and set it to freeze on ice
5. Place your mold in a small bucket proportioned to the mold, after having put well-pounded ice in the bottom of the bucket, a handful of salt or saltpeter
6. Continue to place ice and saltpeter around the mold up to the top
7. When your cheese is frozen and you are ready to serve, have hot water in a cauldron, dip the mold of the cheese in it to make it detach, and dress it in the dish; it must be eaten at once
