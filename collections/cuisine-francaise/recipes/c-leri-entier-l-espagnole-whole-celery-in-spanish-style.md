## Ingredients

- celery hearts
- water
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter, the size of an egg
- Spanish sauce
- consommé
- flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove the outer ribs and trim the head
2. Cut 7-inch pieces, all of the same size and thickness
3. Blanch them in plenty of salted water
4. When they have boiled 20 minutes, refresh them and drain
5. Trim again and place in a casserole with a little coarse pepper, an egg-sized piece of butter, 4 spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, and 6 spoonfuls of consommé
6. Cook on a fairly hot fire
7. Let it boil for half an hour
8. Arrange on a plate with the sauce underneath
9. Alternatively, make a light roux, moisten with bouillon, turn the sauce until it boils, then pour it over the celery and finish as described above
