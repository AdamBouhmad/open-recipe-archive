## Ingredients

- veal breast
- butter
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- two cloves
- one bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- bouillon
- sorrel

## Directions

1. Cut the tendons from the veal breast into equal pieces the width of a finger.
2. Blanch them briefly in boiling water.
3. Place them in a casserole with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, two cloves, one bay leaf, thyme, and basil.
4. Moisten with bouillon and cook on low heat until the tendons are cooked and almost no sauce remains.
5. Do not degrease, but moisten only; take two handfuls of sorrel, remove the stems, wash them well, and squeeze them dry.
