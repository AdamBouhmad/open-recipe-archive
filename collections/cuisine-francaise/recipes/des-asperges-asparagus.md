## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water
- salt
- bouillon
- butter
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut off part of the white and wash well
2. Cook with water and salt, a half quarter hour is sufficient for them to be cooked as needed, they should be somewhat crunchy
3. Arrange them on the plate you will serve
4. If in meat broth, take some coulis, add a little butter, salt, coarse pepper, bind the sauce over the fire and place it on the asparagus
