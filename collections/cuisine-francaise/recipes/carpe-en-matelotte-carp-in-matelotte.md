## Ingredients

- carp
- other fish such as pike, eel, crayfish, barbel, or river fish
- butter
- flour
- small onions

## Directions

1. Scale the carp and remove the gills, cut it into pieces
2. Put it in a saucepan with other fish such as pike, eel, crayfish, barbel, or whatever river fish you have available
3. In another saucepan make a small roux with butter and one spoonful of flour
4. When it is a nice color, add small onions cut into quarters and cook them halfway in this same roux, adding a little more butter
