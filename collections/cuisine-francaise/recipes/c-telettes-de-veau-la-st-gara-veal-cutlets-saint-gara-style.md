## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Scarlet tongue
- Lard
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Fine pepper
- Lard strips
- Parings of tongue
- Laurel
- Basil
- Slices of ham
- Carrots
- Onions
- Consommé or bouillon
- Spanish sauce
- Mushrooms, chicory, tomato sauce, or cucumbers (optional garnishes)

## Directions

1. Cut and trim the cutlets as in previous recipes.
2. Cut the scarlet tongue into medium lardons.
3. Grate some lard and warm it, then sauté the lardons in it.
4. Add grated nutmeg and fine pepper to the lardons.
5. Let the lardons cool and lard the cutlets through and through.
6. Put a piece of butter in a large casserole and firm up the cutlets to trim them more correctly.
7. In the casserole, place strips of lard, parings of tongue, a little laurel, a little basil, and a few slices of ham.
8. Place the cutlets on this seasoning, cover them with lard.
9. Add two or three carrots cut into slices, or four onions cut into slices, and fill with two glasses of consommé or bouillon.
10. Cook the cutlets on low heat for two hours.
11. Put fire on top of the lid.
12. At the moment of serving, drain and glaze the cutlets.
13. Pass the cooking liquid through a silk sieve.
14. Add three spoonfuls of large Spanish sauce to a casserole.
15. Add four spoonfuls of the cooking liquid from the cutlets.
16. Reduce the sauce by half.
17. Arrange the cutlets on the platter and add the reduced sauce.
18. If you do not have sauce, make a light roux and moisten with the strained liquid, add two spoonfuls of jus for color, and reduce by one-third.
19. Ensure the sauce is not too salty and add a little coarse pepper.
20. You may serve under these cutlets a sauté of mushrooms, chicory, tomato sauce, or cucumbers.
