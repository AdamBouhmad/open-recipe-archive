## Ingredients

- cod
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- anchovy
- garlic
- coarse pepper
- capers
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. In the serving dish, place a little butter at the bottom with chopped parsley, onion, anchovy, garlic, coarse pepper, and some whole capers
2. Cover with cod and make several layers this way until the dish is full
3. Top with breadcrumbs and simmer over a small fire with a pie cover on top to give it color
