## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- salt, two or three grains
- sugar (optional)
- sliced bread
- five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of milk with two or three grains of salt and sugar if desired
2. Slice bread and arrange it in the serving dish
3. Pour some of the milk over the bread to soak it
4. Keep it warm on hot ashes without allowing it to boil
5. Cover the dish
6. When ready to serve, mix five egg yolks with the remaining milk
7. Place on the fire, stirring constantly
8. When the milk thickens, remove promptly from the heat as this indicates the eggs are cooked; if you delay, the eggs will curdle
