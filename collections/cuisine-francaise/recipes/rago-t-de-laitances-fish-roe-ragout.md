## Ingredients

- Two carp roe
- Two spoonfuls of coulis
- Half a glass of white wine
- Bouillon in equal measure
- Bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Half a clove of garlic
- Onions in slices
- Parsnip root cut in thin slices
- Parsley
- Chives
- Clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- Half a bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Butter
- Flour
- White wine
- Lean bouillon
- Egg yolks
- Cream or milk
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil two carp roe for a moment in boiling water and place them in a casserole with two spoonfuls of coulis, half a glass of white wine, as much bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic
2. Let them boil for a quarter hour, season with salt and pepper
3. For meatless: put in a casserole onions in slices, a parsnip root cut in thin slices, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a hint of garlic, two cloves, half a bay leaf, thyme, basil, and a piece of butter
4. Pass them over the fire, add a pinch of flour, moisten with a glass of white wine and as much good lean bouillon
5. Let it boil and reduce by half, pass the sauce through a sieve, add the roe to let it boil for a quarter hour
6. Before serving, add a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with cream or milk, salt, and pepper, let it bind over the fire without boiling
