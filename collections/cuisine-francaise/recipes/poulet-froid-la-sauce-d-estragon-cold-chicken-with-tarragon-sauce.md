## Ingredients

- Two cold roasted chickens
- Velouté
- Eight spoonfuls of degreased stock
- Four spoonfuls of aspic
- Two tablespoons of tarragon vinegar
- Coarse pepper
- One egg yolk (if needed)
- Fresh parsley
- Fresh tarragon
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Aspic for decoration
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Carve two cold roasted chickens that have been cooked in a pan as correctly as possible.
2. Place the pieces in a saucepan with velouté, eight spoonfuls of degreased stock, four spoonfuls of aspic, and two tablespoons of tarragon vinegar, and a little coarse pepper.
3. Reduce the entire mixture by one third.
4. If your sauce is a little brown, add a liaison of one egg yolk.
5. When reduced to the proper consistency, add some finely chopped parsley and tarragon.
6. Add two boils of bouillon to your sauce.
7. Check the seasoning for salt and pour it over your cold chicken pieces.
8. Allow to cool.
9. Arrange on your platter and pour the sauce, which will be almost congealed, over your chickens.
10. Decorate this dish with aspic and croutons.
