## Ingredients

- Lamb head parts
- Bouillon from cooking
- Three egg yolks
- Pinch of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Take the cooking bouillon and ensure it is not too salty
2. Dilute with three egg yolks and a pinch of chopped parsley
3. Thicken the sauce over the fire
4. Serve over the cooked lamb head parts
