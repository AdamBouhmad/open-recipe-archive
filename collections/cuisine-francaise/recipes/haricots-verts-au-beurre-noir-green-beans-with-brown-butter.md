## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Blanch the beans and remove from boiling water
2. Let drain in a strainer
3. Season with salt and coarse pepper and arrange on a plate
4. Pour brown butter over them
5. Alternatively, melt a good piece of butter in a saucepan and brown it
6. Place the green beans in and sauté on a high fire
7. Season before arranging on the plate
8. Add a dash of vinegar
