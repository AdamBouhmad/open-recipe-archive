## Ingredients

- Frogs
- Water
- Vinegar
- Parsley
- Ciboule
- Sliced onions
- Two cloves of garlic
- Two shallots
- Three cloves
- Laurel leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Flour

## Directions

1. Marinate the raw frogs for one hour in equal parts water and vinegar with whole parsley, ciboule, sliced onions, two cloves of garlic, two shallots, three cloves, a laurel leaf, thyme, and basil
2. Drain and flour them for frying
