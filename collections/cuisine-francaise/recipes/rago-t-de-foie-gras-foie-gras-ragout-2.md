## Ingredients

- Foie gras livers
- Water
- Coulis
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the bitter part from the livers and leave them whole
2. Blanch them briefly in boiling water
3. Put them in a saucepan with two spoonfuls of coulis
