## Ingredients

- One chopine of cream
- One pinte of milk
- Two grains of salt
- Peel of one green citron, grated
- One pinch of coriander
- One small piece of cinnamon
- Three ounces of sugar
- A little more than a pea-sized amount of rennet, diluted with one spoonful of water

## Directions

1. Place on the fire a chopine of cream with one pinte of milk, two grains of salt, grated green citron peel, a pinch of coriander, one small piece of cinnamon, and three ounces of sugar
2. Bring to a boil together and reduce by half
3. Remove from the fire; when it becomes lukewarm, add a little more than a pea-sized amount of rennet diluted with one spoonful of water
4. Strain the cream through a sieve and return it to a warm place near the fire
5. When it has curdled, place it in a small wicker basket to drain and take the shape of a cheese
6. Then turn it out into a serving dish or onto a plate
