## Ingredients

- good quinces, pitted and peeled
- 2 pints of eau-de-vie per 3 pints of quince juice
- 1 quarteron of sugar per pint of juice
- cinnamon
- coriander
- ginger
- mace

## Directions

1. Crush the quinces after removing the pits and peel
2. Press them well in a clean cloth
3. Measure the juice obtained
4. Mix 2 pints of eau-de-vie with 3 pints of juice and add 1 quarteron of sugar per pint, cinnamon, coriander, ginger, and mace in moderate amounts
5. Infuse everything together for ten or twelve days in a well-corked jug so it does not become exposed to air
6. Pass it through a fine strainer
7. Put it in clean bottles
8. Cork it well and put it in the cellar; the older it is, the better it will be
