## Ingredients

- Lamb head, liver, heart, lungs, and feet
- Water for deglazing
- Bouillon
- Good butter
- Bouquet garni
- Salt
- Pepper
- Three egg yolks
- Milk
- Verjuice

## Directions

1. Remove the jaws and muzzle, and soak all the offal parts cut into pieces in water
2. Blanch briefly
3. Cook over low heat with bouillon, a bit of good butter, a bouquet garni, salt, and pepper
4. When cooked, dilute three egg yolks with a bit of milk and thicken the sauce over the fire
5. Add a dash of verjuice
6. Arrange the head in the serving dish, uncover the brain, place the remaining parts around it, and pour sauce over
