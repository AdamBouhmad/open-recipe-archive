## Ingredients

- One hen (poularde)
- Poultry livers, cut into dice
- Small lard
- Small eggs

## Directions

1. Lard the top of the hen
2. After singeing and gutting it, fill it with diced poultry livers, small lard, and small eggs
3. Sew up the hen so nothing escapes
4. Cook it like a fricandeau and glaze it in the same manner
