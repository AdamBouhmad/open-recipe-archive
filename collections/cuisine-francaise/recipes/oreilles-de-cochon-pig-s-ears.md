## Ingredients

- Pig's ears
- Butter
- Twelve large onions
- Flour (one tablespoon)
- Vinegar (half a glass)
- Bouillon (one glass)
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Cook the pig's ears in the same seasoning as for hure (head cheese).
2. When they are cold, cut them into small strips and place them in a casserole.
3. Cut twelve large onions in half, remove the head and tail, and cut them in semi-circles.
4. Pass the onions in a good piece of butter until they are well browned.
5. If you do not have sauce, add one tablespoon of flour which you stir with your onions.
6. Add half a glass of vinegar, one glass of bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper.
7. Let the onions come to a boil a few times.
8. Place them over your sliced pig's ears.
9. Sauté everything together and keep it warm without boiling.
10. At the moment of serving, arrange your ragout on the plate and surround it with croutons.
