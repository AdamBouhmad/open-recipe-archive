## Ingredients

- One pint of water
- More than one quarter-pound of butter
- One lemon zest
- Two ounces of sugar
- A little salt
- Flour
- Icing, or almonds and sugar, or pistachios
- Preserves

## Directions

1. For one pint of water in a saucepan, put more than one quarter-pound of butter, one lemon zest, two ounces of sugar, and a little salt
2. When the water is ready to boil, add the flour and work the dough as with the pouplin dough
3. Keep it somewhat firmer so it is more manageable
4. Give this dough whatever shape you wish
5. Glaze it, or put almonds and sugar on top, or pistachios; if there is nothing on top, put preserves inside
