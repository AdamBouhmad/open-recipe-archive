## Ingredients

- One raw or roasted poularde
- Good bouillon
- Coulis
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Parsley
- Water
- Verjus

## Directions

1. Take a raw poularde or one that has already been roasted and served; even if it is partially eaten, this does not matter
2. Cut it into pieces and cook it in a casserole with good bouillon and coulis, salt, and a little coarse pepper
3. When cooked and the sauce is sufficiently reduced, add a good pinch of very finely chopped parsley that has been briefly cooked in water beforehand
4. Press the parsley well
5. When serving, add a dash of verjus
