## Ingredients

- three small coffee rolls
- milk
- sugar
- two small spoonfuls of flour
- seven egg yolks
- three macarons, crushed
- green lemon, chopped
- a piece of sugar
- a chopine of cream

## Directions

1. Take three small coffee rolls, remove the bottom crust so you can remove the crumb without damaging the top crust, soak the top and bottom crusts with milk and sugar
2. In a saucepan, put two small spoonfuls of flour which you will dilute with seven egg yolks (set the whites aside in a basin), put with the yolks three crushed macarons, chopped green lemon, a piece of sugar, a chopine of cream; cook everything on the fire for a quarter-hour and remove it
3. Remove the small rolls from the milk to drain them
4. Put the dish you must serve on, put this cream in the bottom, the bottom of the rolls on top, take the tops of the rolls; in place of the crumb you removed, put cream in it, and put them in the dish as if they were whole, cover them on top with the same cream, finish them on top so that each roll keeps its shape
5. Cook them in the oven or under a covered pie dish for half an hour
6. Take egg whites which you whip into snow and put sugar into it
7. Remove the small rolls from the oven to cover them with these whipped egg whites while always preserving their shape, put fine sugar on top, and put them back in the oven until they are a beautiful yellow color, and serve
