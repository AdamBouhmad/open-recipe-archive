## Ingredients

- half a quarter pound of butter
- one teaspoon of saffron or turmeric powder
- five small pods of hot chili pepper, minced or crushed
- five tablespoons of velouté
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put the butter in a saucepan
2. Heat the butter until it simmers
3. When it is very hot, add five tablespoons of velouté
4. Stir the sauce well
5. Serve it without removing the fat and without straining through cheesecloth
6. Add a little grated nutmeg
7. Your sauce should be very hot
