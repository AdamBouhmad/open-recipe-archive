## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton, aged
- lard
- parsley
- chives
- mushroom
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- slices of onions
- one glass of white wine
- coulis (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the leg of mutton lengthwise into slices about two fingers thick
2. Make four pieces from the leg
3. Lard all pieces with lard seasoned with parsley, chives, mushroom, a pinch of garlic, all chopped; salt and pepper
4. Line a casserole with a few strips of lard and slices of onions
5. Place the mutton pieces on top
6. Cover the casserole well and cook over very low heat in its own juice
7. Halfway through cooking, add one glass of white wine
8. When cooked, remove the fat from the sauce
9. If you have coulis, add a little to the sauce and serve with reduced sauce
