## Ingredients

- Two fat, well-whitened chickens of equal size
- A good piece of butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Juice of a lemon
- Thin slices of lemons
- Lard slices (for barding)
- Buttered paper

## Directions

1. Flame the chickens and empty them through the vent
2. Remove the breastbone
3. In a saucepan, put a good piece of butter, salt, coarse pepper, and lemon juice; mix the seasoning with your butter
4. Fill the chickens with this mixture
5. Truss and bridle them as for an entrée
6. Arrange thin slices of lemon on the breast of your chickens
7. Cover them with slices of lard
8. Spit them and wrap them in buttered paper
9. Three quarters of an hour before serving, put them on the fire; take care that they do not take on color
10. At the moment of serving, remove them from the spit and unbridle them
11. Remove the lard and lemon that may remain
12. Circle the opening near the rump
13. Dress them on your platter and put underneath a hot aspic, a bound velouté, a tomato sauce, sautéed mushrooms in suprême, or a crayfish butter
