## Ingredients

- seven or eight peaches
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Put seven or eight peaches on a dish with fine sugar on top and underneath, cover them with a pie-dish lid and cook them on a small fire above and below
2. When they are cooked and well glazed, serve them hot
