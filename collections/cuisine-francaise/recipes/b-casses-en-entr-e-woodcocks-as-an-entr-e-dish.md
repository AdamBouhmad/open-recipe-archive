## Ingredients

- Woodcocks
- Lard, grated or butter
- Parsley, chopped
- Chives, chopped
- Salt
- Lard for wrapping
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. When plucked and singed, split them down the back to gut them
2. Use all the innards except the gizzard; chop the rest and mix it with grated lard or a piece of butter, chopped parsley and chives, and a little salt
3. Place this forcemeat in the body and sew the opening closed
4. Truss the woodcocks and cook them on the spit wrapped in lard and paper
5. When cooked, serve them with sauce or ragout as with young partridges
