## Ingredients

- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Eggs
- Black pepper
- Jus

## Directions

1. Fill three-quarters of a medium saucepan with water, salt, and a little vinegar
2. When the water boils, place it on the edge of the stove
3. Break the eggs carefully without damaging the yolk
4. Pour the egg gently into the water
5. Poach four eggs and let them set
6. Keep the water boiling throughout
7. Remove the eggs from the water with a slotted spoon and test with your finger; if they have some consistency, place them in cold water
8. Poach them again in fresh water and change the water
9. Just before serving, reheat them
10. Drain them on white linen and arrange on a plate
11. Put a little black pepper on each egg and jus underneath
