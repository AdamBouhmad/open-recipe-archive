## Ingredients

- pigeons
- bouillon
- a bouquet garni
- mushrooms of all sorts
- artichoke hearts
- salt

## Directions

1. Scald, clean, and truss the pigeons by tucking the feet inside
2. Blanch them for a moment and remove them to fresh water, then clean them
3. Place them in a casserole with bouillon, a bouquet garni with all sorts of mushrooms, two artichoke hearts cut in four and cooked halfway through, and salt
