## Ingredients

- calf's head, cooked plain or otherwise
- marinade
- batter for frying

## Directions

1. When your calf's head is cooked plain or otherwise, cut it into medium pieces
2. Put it in a vessel and pour marinade over it
3. Ensure all your pieces are soaked in the seasoning
4. Make a frying batter
5. Drain your pieces of head and put them in your batter
6. Your frying oil should not be very hot when you add your pieces of head
