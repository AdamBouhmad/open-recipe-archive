## Ingredients

- butter the size of half an egg
- three cloves of hot pepper, well crushed
- one thimble of Indian saffron powder or turmeric
- four spoonfuls of velvet sauce without being bound
- two spoonfuls of bouillon
- butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan butter the size of half an egg, three cloves of well-crushed hot pepper, and one thimble of Indian saffron powder or turmeric
2. Heat your butter until it is a little fried
3. Then add four spoonfuls of velvet sauce unbounded and two spoonfuls of bouillon
4. Reduce and degrease your sauce
5. Transfer it to another saucepan and keep it warm in a bain-marie
6. At the moment of serving, add butter the size of an egg, which you will stir well with your sauce
