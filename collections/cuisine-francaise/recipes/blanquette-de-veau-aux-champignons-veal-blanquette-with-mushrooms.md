## Ingredients

- Veal loin, underside, roasted on the spit
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Velouté or flour and bouillon
- Consommé
- Egg yolks
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the underside of veal loin on the spit; when cooked, let it cool.
2. Slice it, beat the pieces with the flat of your knife blade.
3. Cut off the angles and round your pieces as much as possible; try to make them equal.
4. Put them in a casserole as they are cut and trimmed; use only the white meat and remove everything that has the color of the spit.
5. Have mushrooms that you will turn (shape); cut them thick about one line, forming a round shape.
6. Sauté them in butter.
7. When they are sautéed, put them with the meat of your blanquette.
8. Pour into the butter where they cooked four spoonfuls to degrease of velouté, or more if your blanquette is strong, and just over three spoonfuls of consommé.
9. Degrease your sauce, reduce it, and pass it through a sieve over your blanquette.
10. Keep it warm without it boiling.
11. At the moment of serving, add to it a liaison of two egg yolks, or more if your blanquette is strong, a little coarse pepper.
12. Stir gently your blanquette on the edge of the stove.
13. When your liaison is in and when you see that your sauce is thickened, remove it, always stirring.
14. Check if it is of good salt and good taste; then arrange it in a pyramid.
15. Try to ensure there is not too much sauce.
