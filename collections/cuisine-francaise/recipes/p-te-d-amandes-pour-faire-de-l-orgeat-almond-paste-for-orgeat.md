## Ingredients

- 1 pound of sweet almonds
- ½ pound of sugar, pounded
- Water

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds by soaking them briefly in hot water, then remove their skins
2. Pound them well, adding a little water from time to time so they do not turn to oil
3. When well pounded, add ½ pound of pounded sugar
4. Make a paste of all of it for use when you see fit
5. This paste keeps for six months and nearly a year
6. When you wish to use it, take a piece as large as an egg and dissolve it in three half-septiers of water, then pass it through a cloth
