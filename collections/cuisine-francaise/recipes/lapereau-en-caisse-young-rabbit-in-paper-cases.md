## Ingredients

- Young rabbits
- Lard, grated, about the size of an egg
- Butter, twice the amount of lard
- Two spoonfuls of oil
- Mushrooms, chopped
- Shallot
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Aromatic spices, ground
- Nutmeg, grated
- Strong double paper cases
- Oil for coating
- Thin lard strips (bardes)
- Breadcrumb (mie de pain)

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean, and cut the young rabbits into equal pieces
2. Put in a saucepan lard about the size of an egg, twice as much butter, and two spoonfuls of oil
3. Wipe your rabbit pieces well to remove the blood
4. Melt the butter and put the rabbit in it
5. Brown it well
6. Add chopped mushrooms, shallot, finely chopped parsley, salt, coarse pepper, a little ground aromatic spices, and grated nutmeg
7. Stir everything together on the fire
8. When the young rabbit has stayed ten minutes on the fire, take a strong double paper case which you oil
9. Put in the bottom a very thin lard strip, and place your rabbit pieces with all their seasoning, another thin lard strip over the top
10. At the moment of serving, place your case on the grill over gentle fire and place a hot camp oven over it
11. When serving, either add a little Italian sauce inside your case, or before putting it on the fire, mask it with breadcrumb and brown it with the oven
