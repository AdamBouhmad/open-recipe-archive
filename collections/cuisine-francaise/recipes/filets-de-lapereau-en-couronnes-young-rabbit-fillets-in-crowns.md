## Ingredients

- Twelve rabbit fillets
- Fine lard for larding
- Onions
- Bards of lard
- A few slices of veal
- Two carrots cut in slices
- Three onions
- A little thyme and bay leaf
- Bouillon
- Buttered round of paper
- Glaze sauce for serving
- Alternatively: four spoonfuls of gelée

## Directions

1. Raise your fillets in the same manner as explained for hare fillets
2. Lard them with fine lard
3. When larded, fold them over to form a round so they hold their shape
4. Put an onion of the necessary size in the middle of each fillet to fill the void
5. Secure it with small skewers
6. Put bards of lard in a casserole with a few slices of veal, two carrots cut in slices, three onions, a little thyme and bay leaf
7. Place your fillets on this seasoning
8. Add a full small spoonful of bouillon
9. Cover them with a round of buttered paper
10. Simmer with fire above and below for three-quarters of an hour
11. At the moment of serving, drain and glaze them
12. Arrange them in crowns and serve a glaze sauce underneath
13. Alternatively, if you lack lard or veal, put four spoonfuls of gelée and cook your fillets with fairly strong fire above and below; let your liquid reduce to glaze and use it to glaze your fillets
