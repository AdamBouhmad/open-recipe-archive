## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- carrots
- espagnole sauce
- consommé
- sugar
- small glazed onions
- butter
- flour
- coarse pepper
- cooking liquid from the tongue

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue and cook it as for the gherkin version
2. Cut it into slices and arrange in miroton style
3. Around the plate have carrots turned into small batons, which you will blanch
4. Refresh and drain them and put them in a pan
5. Add three spoonfuls of espagnole and five of consommé and a small piece of sugar
6. Cook them; at the time of serving, arrange the tongue and glaze it, and put the small roots in the middle around the plate
7. Make a border of small glazed onions; or if you have neither sauce nor consommé, after blanching the small roots, sauté them in a pan with a small piece of butter
8. Dust them with half a spoonful of flour
9. Pass the cooking liquid from the tongue through a silk sieve and moisten the small roots with it
10. The sauce should be thin so that you may reduce it
11. When cooking the small roots, take care to degrease them well and add a little coarse pepper
