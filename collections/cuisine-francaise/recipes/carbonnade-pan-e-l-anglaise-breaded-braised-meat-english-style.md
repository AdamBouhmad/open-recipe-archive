## Ingredients

- carbonnades
- salt
- pepper
- warm butter
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- butter for basting

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your carbonnades as the previous ones
2. When they are cooked, drain them
3. Dust them with fine salt and pepper
4. Dip them in warm butter
5. Put them in breadcrumbs
6. When they are breaded, let them cool
7. Beat four eggs that you mix in the remaining butter
8. Dip your carbonnades in it, and then put them in breadcrumbs
9. Make sure there is breadcrumb coverage everywhere
10. Put them on a saucepan lid
11. Brush them with warm butter using a feather brush
12. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on them
13. Blow on the top so there is not too much
14. Half an hour before serving, put them on the grill on a gentle fire
