## Ingredients

- Five spoonfuls of flour
- Five eggs
- One pint of milk
- Butter the size of an egg
- A little salt
- Almonds
- One bitter almond per six sweet almonds
- Macarons
- A little candied orange flower
- Sugar, grated in great quantity

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan five spoonfuls of flour, which you will mix with five eggs
2. Pour in one pint of milk, butter the size of an egg, and a little salt
3. Put your mixture over the fire
4. Stir the frangipane constantly without leaving it until it has boiled for ten minutes
5. Be careful it does not brown
6. When it is cooked, let it cool in a bowl
7. Then crush some almonds; for every six, use one bitter almond; crush some macarons until they are a fine powder
8. Add a little candied orange flower and powdered sugar in large enough quantity that it is a good sugar
9. Mix all with your mixture
10. When the frangipane is well worked with a wooden spoon, if it is too thick, add one or two eggs with your puff pastry
11. Make tarts and small cakes of all sorts and in all fashions
12. If you want your cream to be pistacio flavored, when it is cold, put pistachios instead of almonds
13. As this would not give enough color, add a little spinach green; do not use macarons or orange flower, but rather three bitter almonds and sugar
