## Ingredients

- fifteen egg yolks
- one pint of good bouillon

## Directions

1. Dilute fifteen egg yolks with one pint of good bouillon, passing it several times through cheesecloth
2. Put your mixture in a mold or vessel so you can set it in a bain-marie
3. When it is well set, pour hot bouillon into your tureen
4. With a skimmer, take some of your eggs (prepared in the bain-marie) in such a way that it forms portions that you put in your tureen; your soup should be well garnished with it
