## Ingredients

- Seasoned sorrel
- Six egg yolks
- Two spoonfuls of flour
- Cream, if available
- Or three glasses of milk

## Directions

1. Prepare your sorrel and cook it like the previous recipe
2. When it is well cooked in butter, have six egg yolks in which you put two spoonfuls of flour that you mix with cream, if you have it, or three glasses of milk
3. Put this mixture in your sorrel
4. Make it reduce on a fairly hot stove, stirring continually with a wooden spoon
5. When your purée is reduced enough, pass it through a sieve and keep it hot for use
