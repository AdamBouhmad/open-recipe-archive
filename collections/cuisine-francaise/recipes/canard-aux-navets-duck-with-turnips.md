## Ingredients

- one duck
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- salt
- pepper
- turnips

## Directions

1. Singe the duck; clean, empty, and truss the legs inside.
2. Put a little butter in a pan with a spoonful of flour; brown it to a nice color and moisten with bouillon.
3. Put the duck in with a bouquet garni, a little salt, and coarse pepper.
4. Have turnips properly cut, which you cook with the duck: if they are hard, cook everything at the same time; if they are not, add them halfway through the duck's cooking.
5. When your ragout is well cooked and degreased, add a little coulis.
6. Serve short sauce. This is the way to make duck with turnips in the homestyle fashion.
7. The other way is to cook the duck separately in a white braise, and prepare the turnips turned into ovals, blanched and cooked with bouillon, veal jus and coulis; when your ragout is made, serve it over the duck.
