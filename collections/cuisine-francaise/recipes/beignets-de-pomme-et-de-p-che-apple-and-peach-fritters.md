## Ingredients

- reinette apples
- peaches
- brandy
- sugar
- green lemon rind
- orange flower water
- flour

## Directions

1. Take reinette apples that you cut into four quarters, remove the skin and pips, pare them cleanly
2. Let them marinate for two or three hours with brandy, sugar, green lemon rind, and orange flower water
3. When they have absorbed the flavor well, put them to drain
4. Then put them in a white cloth with flour and shake them well inside so they take on flour
5. Fry them to a beautiful color and glaze them with sugar using a red-hot shovel or tongs
6. Peach fritters are made the same way
7. You can also make apple fritters in batter: hollow out the apple in the middle without breaking it to remove the pit
8. Peel and cut it into slices the thickness of a coin
9. Let them marinate as the previous ones
10. Dip them in batter and fry them as for brioche fritters
11. Serve them glazed with sugar
