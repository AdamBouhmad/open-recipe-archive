## Ingredients

- Mortified leg of mutton
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Bouquet garni
- Clear coulis
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Trim and truss the mortified leg of mutton
2. Cook it with bouillon, very little salt, and a bouquet garni
3. When the leg of mutton is cooked, remove it and degrease the bouillon, then reduce it until it becomes a glaze
4. Put the leg of mutton back in the same casserole so it takes all the substance of the meat, stirring to prevent it from sticking
5. When no more sauce remains in the casserole, arrange the leg of mutton on the serving platter
6. Put clear coulis in the casserole to detach what remains
7. Take a good handful of parsley, blanch it for half a quarter hour in boiling water, refresh it in fresh water, press it, and mince it very fine
8. Put the parsley in your sauce and season it well
9. Serve it under the leg of mutton
