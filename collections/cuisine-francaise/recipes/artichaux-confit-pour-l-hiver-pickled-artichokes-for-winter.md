## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water

## Directions

1. Remove all the leaves and leave only what is good to eat on the heart
2. Throw them in water until you have prepared them all
3. Cook them afterward in water until you can easily remove the choke
4. Put them back in fresh water
5. When they are well cleaned, put them to drain
6. If it is to dry them, put them on hurdles in an oven that should not be too hot; if you can hold your hand in it without it burning you, that is enough
7. When they are dry, you will serve them to put in stews after having made them revive in lukewarm water
8. If you wish to pickle them, they will be better; put a brine as is explained for pickled green beans and follow the same method
