## Ingredients

- Bread crumbs, same size as previous slices
- One veal kidney cooked on a spit
- Its fat
- Parsley
- Chives
- One shallot
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Bread crumbs for breading

## Directions

1. Cut bread crumbs same size as previously made slices
2. Prepare a farce by mincing a veal kidney cooked on a spit with an equal amount of its fat, parsley, chives, one minced shallot, salt, and pepper
3. Bind with four egg yolks and beaten egg whites
4. Place this farce on the slices
5. Smooth the top with a knife dipped in beaten egg
6. Bread with bread crumbs
7. Cook on a baking sheet with a little heat above and below
8. Serve with a small clear sauce, somewhat elevated in flavor
