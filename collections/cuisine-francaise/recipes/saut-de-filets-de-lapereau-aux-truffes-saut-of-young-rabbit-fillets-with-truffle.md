## Ingredients

- Ten or twelve young rabbit fillets
- Truffles
- Three quarternons of butter
- Velouté sauce
- Croûtons

## Directions

1. Raise ten or twelve fillets of young rabbit
2. Remove the sinew skin
3. Cut them into round slices, all approximately equal
4. With the blade of the knife, flatten these slices
5. Cut the angles to give them a round or oval shape
6. Place each piece in your sautoir or tourtière, continuing for all your fillets
7. When all are trimmed and arranged, peel and trim truffles
8. Cut them into equal slices
9. Put them on the fillets until they are covered
10. Warm three quarternons of butter and pour it over
11. At the moment of serving, put your sauté on a strong fire
12. When your pieces are stiffened on one side, turn them lightly with a spoon
13. Position your sautoir so your butter separates from the sauté
14. When drained, put it in a velouté reduced at the moment of serving and arrange it on the plate
15. Put also a border of croûton
