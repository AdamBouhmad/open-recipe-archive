## Ingredients

- One blonde calf's liver
- Large lardons, well seasoned
- Lard
- Skewers
- One large atelet (meat skewer)
- Sauce piquante

## Directions

1. Take a blonde calf's liver and lard it with large, well-seasoned lardons in the same manner as the previous recipe
2. Ensure the liver is not damaged and lard it with thin lard
3. Pass skewers through the width of the liver
4. Spit the liver and place a large atelet beneath it to secure it
5. Tie both ends of the atelet to the spit, ensuring the liver does not move
6. Leave the liver by the fire for one and a half hours, which is sufficient to cook it
7. At the moment of serving, unspit the liver and place it on the platter with piquant sauce beneath it
