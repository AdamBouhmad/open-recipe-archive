## Ingredients

- one calf's liver
- shallots
- butter
- a small pinch of flour
- a glass of water
- a glass of white wine
- salt
- coarse pepper
- three egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of verjuice

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices
2. Put in a casserole with shallots and a piece of butter
3. Pass over the fire and add a small pinch of flour
4. Moisten with a glass of water and as much white wine
5. Add salt and coarse pepper
6. Let boil for half an hour
7. Dilute three egg yolks with two spoonfuls of verjuice
8. When the liver is cooked and little sauce remains, add the liaison
9. Thicken without boiling and serve
