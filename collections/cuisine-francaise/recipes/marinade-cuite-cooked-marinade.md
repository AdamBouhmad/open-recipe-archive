## Ingredients

- butter
- three carrots
- four onions
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- two cloves
- parsley in branches
- some chives
- two teaspoons of flour
- one glass of vinegar
- two glasses of broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan
2. Cut three carrots into slices and four onions the same way
3. Add two bay leaves, a little thyme, and two cloves
4. Put your saucepan on the fire and pass your vegetables through
5. Add parsley in branches and some chives and pass them with your vegetables
6. Add two teaspoons of flour that you will mix with your butter
7. Add one glass of vinegar and two glasses of broth, salt, and pepper
8. Let your marinade simmer for three-quarters of an hour
9. Pass it through a hair sieve and use it as needed
10. If you have sauce, you can use it in place of flour
