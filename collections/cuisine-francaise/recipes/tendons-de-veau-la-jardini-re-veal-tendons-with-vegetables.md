## Ingredients

- veal tendons
- lettuce
- small roots
- sugar
- coarse pepper
- lard
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tendons as those said à la poêle (pan-fried)
2. At the moment of serving, drain them, dress them in crowns, glaze them, and put a border of lettuce around them
3. In the middle of your tendons, place roots turned into small sticks or olives, arranged in a pyramid
4. If you do not have sauce for your small roots, make a light roux that you will moisten with the cooking liquid from your tendons
5. Turn your roots into small sticks, blanch them, and cook them in your sauce
6. Add a small piece of sugar and some coarse pepper
7. Cook your lettuce between two strips of lard with roots, and moisten with bouillon
