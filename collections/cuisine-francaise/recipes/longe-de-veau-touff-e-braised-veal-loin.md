## Ingredients

- One veal loin
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Three quarterons of butter

## Directions

1. Debone it in such a manner that you can give it a long square form
2. Season it with salt and coarse pepper inside
3. Tie it up
4. Place it in a braising pan without liquid, only with three quarterons of butter
5. Put it on the fire
6. When it is well heated, cover your braising pan and let it cook on a low fire, turning it from time to time
7. Three and a half hours are sufficient to cook a medium-sized loin
8. You can serve it as a remove for soup, glazing it and putting a glaze sauce underneath for a large piece, or letting it cool and decorating it with aspic
