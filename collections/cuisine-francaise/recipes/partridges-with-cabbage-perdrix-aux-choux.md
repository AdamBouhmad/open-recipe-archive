## Ingredients

- partridges
- barding lard
- salt pork (petit lard)
- cervelat sausage or ham
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaves
- cabbage
- buttered paper
- bouillon
- espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare partridges as for stewed partridges
2. Place bards of lard in a casserole with the partridges, a pound of blanched and cleaned salt pork, a large cervelat or a piece of ham, and a few slices of veal
3. Cover the partridges with bards of lard
4. Add four carrots, four onions, two cloves, and two bay leaves
5. Blanch the cabbage, tie with twine, and press them
6. Place the cabbage on top of the partridges
7. Cover with bards of lard and a round of buttered paper
8. Add two spoonfuls of bouillon
9. Simmer for two hours
10. At service, drain and untie them
11. Dress on a platter
12. Drain and press the cabbage to dry, and dress around the partridges
13. Cut the lard into pieces and place at intervals on the cabbage with the cervelat
14. Top with espagnole sauce
