## Ingredients

- pork fatty casings
- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- pork fat
- pork pieces
- salt
- fine spices
- anise
- milk
- lard

## Directions

1. Clean the pork casings well and cut them to the desired length for andouilles
2. Soak them in water with one-quarter vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, and basil to remove their charcuterie flavor
3. Cut some of these casings into thin strips, pork fat into thin strips, and pork pieces into thin strips
4. Season everything together with salt and fine spices, mix with a little anise
5. Fill your casings two-thirds full, for fear they burst while cooking if too full
6. Tie them at both ends
7. Cook them with equal parts water and milk, salt, thyme, bay leaf, basil, and a little fat to nourish them
8. When cooked, let them cool in their cooking liquid
9. When ready to serve, grill them and serve as an appetizer
