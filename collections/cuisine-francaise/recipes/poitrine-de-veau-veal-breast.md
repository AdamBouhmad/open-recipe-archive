## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Skewers
- Lard (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the ends of the breast bones
2. Remove the red bones that hold the tendons
3. Put your breast on a strong skewer to avoid a large spit hole
4. Secure your breast on the spit by attaching both ends of your skewer to the spit
5. Put a layer of lard on your breast if you wish
