## Ingredients

- eggs prepared as for Eggs in Cream
- croutons
- 4 egg yolks
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare eggs as you would for Eggs in Cream
2. Glue croutons around the edge of the dish and pour your eggs into it
3. Bread the top
4. Pass four yolks through a sieve
5. Mask them with it
6. Place them on red coals with a well-heated field oven on top
7. When they have a nice color, serve them
