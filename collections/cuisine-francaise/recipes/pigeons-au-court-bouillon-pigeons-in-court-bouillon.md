## Ingredients

- Three or four large pigeons
- Coarse lard for larding
- A bouquet of parsley and chives
- One clove of garlic
- Two shallots
- Two cloves
- One bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- One parsnip
- One carrot
- Two onions
- Butter the size of half an egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- One glass of white wine
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Singe and empty the pigeons
2. Truss the legs into the body
3. Lard them with coarse lard and put them in a pot just to their size
4. Add a bouquet of parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, two shallots, two cloves, one bay leaf, thyme, basil, one parsnip, one carrot, two onions, and butter the size of half an egg
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Moisten with one glass of white wine and an equal amount of bouillon
7. Cook over low heat
8. When the pigeons yield to finger pressure, pass the sauce through a sieve and reduce it
9. If it is too thin, add half a teaspoon of verjuice or a splash of vinegar
10. Serve the sauce over the pigeons
