## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Bread
- Coarse or fine pepper
- Bouillon
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make the garbure the same way as the one with lettuce
2. Some prefer fine pepper instead of coarse pepper, according to taste
3. Be careful with seasoning as cabbage is prone to becoming acrid
4. Do not degrease the cabbage too much
5. Serve a pot of bouillon for people who wish to add it to their plate
