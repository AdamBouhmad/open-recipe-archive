## Ingredients

- one hen
- white wine
- bouillon
- six large onions
- chard
- parsnips
- bouquet of parsley
- cloves
- chives
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- two slices of lemon
- salt
- pepper
- coulis

## Directions

1. Flame and clean the hen, leave the wings and legs, and truss as for cooking in a pot
2. Lard it with lard
3. Cook it with white wine, a little bouillon, six large onions, chard and parsnips properly cut; a bouquet of parsley, cloves, chives, thyme, bay leaf, basil, two slices of lemon, salt, pepper
4. Cook on low heat
5. When it is cooked, dress the hen in the serving dish with the onions and roots around it
6. Serve with its sauce well degreased; if you have coulis to put in the sauce, it will have more consistency
