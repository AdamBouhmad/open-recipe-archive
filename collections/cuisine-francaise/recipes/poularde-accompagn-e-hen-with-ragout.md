## Ingredients

- One hen (poularde)
- Ragout
- Lard
- Good sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the wishbone from the breast after gutting the hen
2. Fill it with a mixed ragout like the one found in the ragout section
3. Cook the hen on the spit, wrapped in lard and paper
4. Serve with a good sauce such as sauce à l'Espagnole or à la Sultane
