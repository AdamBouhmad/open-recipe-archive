## Ingredients

- 1 pound of orange blossoms, cleaned
- 2 pints of eau-de-vie
- 1 pint of water
- 1 pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Infuse the orange blossoms in the eau-de-vie, water, and sugar for twenty-four hours
2. Pass them through the strainer as with the others
