## Ingredients

- 1 liter of flour, divided into 4 parts
- 1 ounce of leavening
- warm water
- 1 ounce of salt
- 2 pounds of butter
- 12 eggs

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of your flour and make a well large enough to contain your water
2. Put 1 ounce of leavening in the well and dilute it with warm water
3. Assemble your dough and place it in a clean vessel
4. Place it in a warm location
5. As soon as you notice your leaven has swollen by half, make a hole in the remaining three liters of flour
6. Put 1 ounce of salt dissolved in a little water, 2 pounds of butter, and 12 eggs into the hole
7. Work the butter with the eggs and assemble your dough
8. If it is too firm, add more eggs until the dough is somewhat soft
9. When it reaches the right consistency, fold it twice and add your leaven
10. Separate your dough with your fingers by working it in your hands, changing its place by cutting it two or three times
11. Place flour in a white cloth and put your dough in it, covering it well
12. Let it rise for 10 to 12 hours
13. Shape it by flattening, folding and refolding it on a floured surface, giving it a round form
14. Press in the middle and place another small round piece of brioche dough on top to form the entire brioche
15. Place on buttered paper or in a buttered mold or casserole
16. Give various shapes to make different small cakes
17. Bake in a warm oven
