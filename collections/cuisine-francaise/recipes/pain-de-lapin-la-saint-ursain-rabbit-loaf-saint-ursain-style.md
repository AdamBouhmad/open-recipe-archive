## Ingredients

- Rabbit quenelle forcemeat
- Butter
- Rabbit brains
- Rabbit fillets
- Rabbit kidneys
- Espagnole sauce
- Game fumé
- Champagne wine
- Optional: veal sweetbreads, cockscombs

## Directions

1. Fill a hollowed out mold (well buttered) with quenelle forcemeat
2. Place it in a bain-marie and let it simmer
3. When the forcemeat in your mold is cooked, unmold it onto your dish at the moment of serving, ensuring there is no water
4. Place in the hollow of your loaf sautéed rabbit brains, fillets and kidneys
5. Prepare an espagnole sauce worked with game fumé and half a glass of Champagne wine
6. When your sauce is well reduced, pour it over the garnishings inside your loaf and glaze the outside
7. Alternative garnishings may include small veal sweetbreads, cockscombs, etc.
