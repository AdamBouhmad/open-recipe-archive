## Ingredients

- one veal breast
- a small piece of butter
- one spoonful of flour
- one chopine of water or bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- one bouquet garni
- half a spoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the veal breast into pieces or leave it whole.
2. Make a roux with a small piece of butter and one spoonful of flour; when it is roux and of a good color, add one chopine of water or bouillon.
3. Then add the veal and cook it on low heat.
4. Season with salt, pepper, one bouquet garni, and half a spoonful of vinegar.
5. When the meat is cooked, degrease the sauce and serve at short sauce.
