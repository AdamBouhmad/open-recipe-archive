## Ingredients

- a handful of flour
- three whole eggs
- six egg yolks
- four crushed macarons
- candied orange flower
- roasted and ground praline
- a little diced candied lemon
- a half-setier of cream
- a half-setier of milk
- a good piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Take a handful of flour and mix it with three whole eggs and six egg yolks
2. Add four crushed macarons, candied orange flower, roasted and ground praline, a little diced candied lemon, a half-setier of cream, a half-setier of milk, and a good piece of sugar
3. Cook everything on the fire for a quarter hour until your cream becomes very thick
4. Put it to cool on a floured board and dust more flour on top after spreading it to the thickness of an inch
5. When it is cold, cut it into small pieces and round them in your hands with flour
6. Fry them in hot fat and dust with fine sugar when serving
