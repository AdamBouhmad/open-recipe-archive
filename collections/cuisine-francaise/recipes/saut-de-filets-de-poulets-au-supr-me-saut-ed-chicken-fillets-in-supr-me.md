## Ingredients

- Fillets of five fat chickens
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Finely chopped and washed parsley
- A good piece of butter
- Velouté, reduced
- Glazed croutons
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Take the fillets of five fat chickens and trim them as described for chicken fillets
2. Arrange them in the sauté pan or on a tourte
3. Season them with salt, coarse pepper, and finely chopped washed parsley
4. Warm a good piece of butter and pour it over them
5. At the moment of serving, put them on a hot fire
6. When they are stiffened on one side, turn them on the other
7. Remove them an instant after
8. Separate them from the butter
9. Dress them in crowns on a platter with a glazed crouton between each fillet
10. Have velouté reduced into which you will dip your fillet
11. Before dressing it, put the bottom of your sauté into it
12. After having removed the butter, put in a piece about the size of half an egg of fresh butter which you will let melt in your velouté
