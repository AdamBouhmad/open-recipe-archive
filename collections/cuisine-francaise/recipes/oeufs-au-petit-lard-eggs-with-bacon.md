## Ingredients

- a good quarter pound of bacon with good marbling
- seven or eight eggs
- coarse pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into small thin slices
2. Put it in a saucepan over a small fire until cooked, turning it carefully
3. Pour the melted bacon into the serving plate with two spoonfuls of jus
4. Break seven or eight eggs over it
5. Add the bacon slices, coarse pepper, and a little salt
6. Cook over a small fire
7. Pass a hot shovel over it
8. Serve with runny yolks
