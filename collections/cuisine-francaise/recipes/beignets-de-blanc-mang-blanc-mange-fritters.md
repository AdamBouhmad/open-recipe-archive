## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of rice
- two eggs
- one chopine of milk
- two ounces of sugar
- a pinch of grated green lemon
- candied orange flower, pralined and chopped
- a little salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a quarter pound of rice that you dilute with two eggs and one chopine of milk, two ounces of sugar
2. Cook over the fire like a porridge, stirring constantly for two hours
3. When it is very thick, remove it from the fire, and add to it a pinch of grated green lemon and candied orange flower, pralined and chopped, a little salt
4. When everything is mixed, spread the cream on a floured plate, sprinkle flour over it
5. When it is cold, cut it into small pieces to roll in your hands, the size of a medium ball
6. Fry them in very hot fat, and when they are black remove them promptly to roll them in fine sugar
