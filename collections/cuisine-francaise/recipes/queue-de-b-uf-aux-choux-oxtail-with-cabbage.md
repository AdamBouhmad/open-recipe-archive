## Ingredients

- oxtail
- braised cabbage
- lard
- large carrots
- espagnole sauce
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the oxtail as in the previous recipe
2. Arrange braised cabbage around it as an entrée with a piece of lard that has cooked with the cabbage and large carrots
3. Surround your sauce with espagnole sauce
4. Alternatively, you may cook the cabbage and lard with the oxtail and some large carrots
5. Salt the cabbage well, blanch it, and tie it before adding it to the cooking pot so you can remove it whole
6. Pour a bound sauce over the oxtail or serve it with some of the cooking liquid
