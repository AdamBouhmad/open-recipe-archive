## Ingredients

- half-liter of lentils, more or less according to the size of your soup
- good bouillon

## Directions

1. Choose lentils, peel and wash them
2. Cook them with good bouillon
3. When they are cooked, strain them through a fine sieve
4. Season your coulis with good taste
5. Note that lentils à la reine are the best for all sorts of coulis
