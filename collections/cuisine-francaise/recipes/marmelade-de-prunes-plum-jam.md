## Ingredients

- plums
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the plums you wish to use, take the quantity you judge appropriate. Boil them on the fire with a little water until they turn to jam. Pass them through a sieve and put back on the fire what you have passed through, and let it boil until this jam is nearly sticking to the pan. Then weigh it and put as much sugar by weight as you have jam. Put the sugar on the fire with a good half-setier of water. Bring to a boil and skim well. You will know when it is cooked by dipping two fingers in fresh water, then in the sugar, and then in the same water. If the sugar remaining on your fingers breaks cleanly, then add your jam to blend it with the sugar by stirring them together over the fire only until it simmers. Put in pots when cold and sprinkle a little fine sugar on top.
