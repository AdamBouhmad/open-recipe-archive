## Ingredients

- Three pigeons
- Butter
- Flour
- Bacon or salt pork
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Parsley and chives
- Bay leaf
- Coarse pepper
- Mushrooms
- Small onions

## Directions

1. Empty, singe, and truss the legs inside your three pigeons.
2. Truss them to maintain a more pleasing shape.
3. Throw a piece of butter in a casserole and warm it.
4. Add one spoonful of flour and make a roux.
5. Cut slices of bacon and brown them in the roux.
6. Place your pigeons in the casserole and sear them well.
7. Pour one and a half spoonfuls of bouillon or water over the pigeons.
8. Stir well until they boil.
9. If using water, add salt, a bouquet of parsley and chives, a bay leaf, coarse pepper, and mushrooms.
10. Skim the ragout.
11. When the pigeons are three quarters cooked, add small onions, well peeled and all of the same size, first passed in butter until golden.
12. Drain them and place in the ragout, which you will degrease.
13. Before serving, arrange the pigeons on your platter with the garnish on top.
