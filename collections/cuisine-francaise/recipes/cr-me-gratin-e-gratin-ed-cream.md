## Ingredients

- six eggs (two whole and four yolks)
- a small handful of flour
- a chopine of cream
- three macarons, crushed
- a little green lemon, chopped
- a piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Dilute two whole eggs and four yolks with a small handful of flour, a chopine of cream, three crushed macarons, a little chopped green lemon, and a piece of sugar
2. Put the dish you must serve on a stove with gentle fire, put this cream in the dish after mixing it well, cook this cream for one hour, and pass the hot shovel over it to give it color
3. If you cook this cream at too high a heat, the gratin will be burned and taste bad
