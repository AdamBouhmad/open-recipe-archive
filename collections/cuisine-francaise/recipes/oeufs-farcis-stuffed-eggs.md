## Ingredients

- 10 hard eggs
- an equal portion of butter and egg yolks
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- chives and parsley, finely chopped and washed
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- 2 or 3 whole egg yolks

## Directions

1. Hard boil ten eggs
2. Cut them in half lengthwise
3. Remove the yolks and pound them in a mortar
4. Pass them through a quenelle sieve
5. Soak a bread crumb in milk and press well to extract the milk; pound it and pass it through a sieve along with the eggs
6. Pound as much butter in the mortar as you have pounded yolks
7. Mix equal portions of butter and egg yolks together
8. When your stuffing is well pounded, add a little finely chopped and washed chives and parsley, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
9. Pound the stuffing again
10. Add two or three whole egg yolks; keep the stuffing workable by adding egg as needed
11. When finished, place it in a vessel
12. Arrange it a finger's thickness in the bottom of the dish
13. Stuff your egg halves
14. Dip a knife blade in egg white to smooth the top
15. Arrange the eggs in order on the stuffing in the dish
16. Place it on red coals with a field oven on top
17. When they have a nice color, serve them
