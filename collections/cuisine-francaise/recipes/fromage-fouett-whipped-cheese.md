## Ingredients

- a little finely chopped green lemon rind
- three half-setiers of good, thick cream
- the size of a pea of gum tragacanth in powder

## Directions

1. Chop very finely a little green lemon rind and put it in a bowl with three half-setiers of good, thick cream and gum tragacanth the size of a pea in powder
2. Whip the cream, and as the foam becomes thick, remove it with a skimmer and put it in a wicker basket
3. If the basket is not tightly woven, put a piece of muslin or other clear linen in the bottom
4. When all the cream is whipped, let the cheese drain until serving
5. Turn it out into a compotier
