## Ingredients

- Bread crumbs
- Eggs
- Butter, a small piece
- Parsley
- Chives, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cream, two spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Cut as many rounds of bread crumbs as you wish to serve eggs, shaped like a small snuff box
2. Make a small hole in the middle to hold an egg lengthwise
3. Take the eggs you wish to scramble and break them quickly at one end to empty them
4. Scramble them by putting them in a saucepan with a small piece of butter, a little chopped parsley and chives, salt, coarse pepper, and two spoonfuls of cream
5. Cook on the fire while stirring constantly until they are cooked
6. Return them to their cleaned and drained shells and place them on the bread crumb bases
