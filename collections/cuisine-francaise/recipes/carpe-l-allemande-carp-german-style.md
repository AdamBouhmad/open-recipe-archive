## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Quatre épices
- Onion slices
- Beer

## Directions

1. Cut the carp into pieces after washing it, without cleaning the insides or removing the gills
2. Remove only the large intestine
3. Place the pieces in a casserole or cooking pot with salt, coarse pepper, quatre épices, onion slices and one or two bottles of beer
4. The fish must be submerged in the sauce
5. Place the casserole over high heat
6. Reduce the sauce until only about one glass remains
7. Serve the carp with its broth without thickening
