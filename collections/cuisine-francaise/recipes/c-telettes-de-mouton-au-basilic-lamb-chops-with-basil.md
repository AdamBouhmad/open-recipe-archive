## Ingredients

- lamb chops from a rack of lamb
- bouillon
- salt
- bouquet garni
- veal round
- beef fat
- three eggs
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- basil
- less cream
- breadcrumbs
- fried parsley
- slice of crumb bread

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb rack by ribs
2. Cook them the same way as lamb chops in dressing gown
3. Finish them the same way
4. Prepare the forcemeat the same but add finely chopped basil, one more egg, and less cream
5. When well wrapped in forcemeat and breadcrumbed, fry them to a nice color
6. Serve garnished with fried parsley, arranged around a slice of crumb bread placed in the dish
