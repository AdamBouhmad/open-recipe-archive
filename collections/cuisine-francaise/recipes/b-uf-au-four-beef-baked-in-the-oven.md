## Ingredients

- Beef chuck
- Beef fat
- Lean bacon, cut into small dice
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Two shallots
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A glass of brandy
- Four egg yolks
- Lard strips

## Directions

1. Take as much beef chuck as you judge appropriate and mince it with half as much beef fat
2. Put the meat in a casserole with lean bacon cut into small dice, parsley, chives, mushrooms, two shallots, all minced very fine, salt, coarse pepper, a glass of brandy, and four egg yolks; mix everything together well
3. Line a casserole or terrine of the size of your meat with lard strips
4. Put the meat on top, pressed down well
5. Cover with a lid and seal the edges with flour mixed with a little vinegar
6. Bake in the oven for three or four hours
7. If serving hot as an entrée, remove the lard strips and degrease the sauce; for a side dish, let it cool in its cooking liquid
