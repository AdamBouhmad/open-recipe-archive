## Ingredients

- apples that are not Reinette
- a glass of water
- a little more than a quarter pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the apples in half
2. Remove the seeds
3. Prick the top of the skin in several places
4. Cook them with a glass of water and a little more than a quarter pound of sugar
5. When they begin to turn to marmalade, arrange them in the compote dish
6. Reduce the syrup and pour it over them
