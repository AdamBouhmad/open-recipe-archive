## Ingredients

- Small asparagus
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Velouté or flour
- Bouillon or water
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Sugar
- Egg yolks
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Cut small asparagus into pieces the size of peas
2. Blanch asparagus tips in boiling salted water
3. After twenty boils, place in a strainer and then in cold water
4. Half an hour before serving, drain completely
5. Melt a piece of butter in a saucepan with the asparagus, salt, and coarse pepper
6. Brown the asparagus in the butter
7. Add two spoonfuls of velouté or two teaspoons of flour mixed with bouillon or water, salt, coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg, and one teaspoon of powdered sugar
8. Bring the asparagus to a boil until the sauce is reduced
9. Add a liaison of three egg yolks
10. Arrange on a plate with croutons around it
