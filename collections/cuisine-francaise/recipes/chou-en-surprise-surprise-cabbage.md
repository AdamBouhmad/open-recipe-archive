## Ingredients

- One good whole cabbage
- Water

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage for a quarter hour in boiling water
2. Remove it to fresh water and press it well in your hands without breaking the leaves
3. Place the leaves on a table and spread them all out to remove the stem
