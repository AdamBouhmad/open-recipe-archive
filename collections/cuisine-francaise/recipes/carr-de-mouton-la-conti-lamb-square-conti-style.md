## Ingredients

- lamb square
- quarter pound of well-larded bacon
- two anchovies
- coarse pepper
- two shallots
- parsley
- chives
- half bay leaf
- three or four basil leaves
- three or four tarragon leaves
- one glass of white wine
- bouillon
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare a lamb square by removing the skins found on the filet.
2. Take a quarter pound of well-larded bacon and two washed anchovies; cut them into larding strips and mix with a little coarse pepper, two shallots, parsley, and chives, all chopped, half a bay leaf, three or four basil leaves chopped like powder, and three or four tarragon leaves also chopped.
3. Lard the entire filet with the bacon and anchovies.
4. Put the square with all these fine herbs in a saucepan.
5. Moisten with one glass of white wine and an equal amount of bouillon.
6. Cook on low heat.
7. When it is cooked, degrease the sauce and add a knob of butter the size of a walnut mixed with a pinch of flour.
8. Bind the sauce on the fire and serve it over the lamb square.
