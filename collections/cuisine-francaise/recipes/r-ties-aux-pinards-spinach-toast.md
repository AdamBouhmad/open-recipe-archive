## Ingredients

- Spinach ragout, finished and thick
- Two raw egg yolks
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a finished spinach ragout that is well-flavored and thick
2. Add two raw egg yolks to the ragout
3. Arrange the spinach on bread crumbs cut as previously
4. Smooth with a knife dipped in egg
5. Bread the top of the bread crumbs
6. Fry
7. Serve without sauce
