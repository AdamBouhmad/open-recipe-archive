## Ingredients

- Half pig's feet
- Ribbon or wide thread
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Saltpeter
- Half bottle of white wine
- Bouillon or water (two tablespoons per pot)
- Meat scraps
- Butter
- Coarse pepper
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wrap your half pig's feet with wide ribbon or thread so that they do not come apart while cooking.
2. Place them in a casserole with thyme, bay leaf, carrots, onions, cloves, a bouquet of parsley and chives, a little saltpeter, half a bottle of white wine, and two tablespoons per pot of bouillon or water.
3. Add a few meat scraps if you have them.
4. Simmer for twenty-four hours without stopping.
5. Let them cool in their cooking liquid.
6. Carefully unwrap them and leave them until the next day.
7. When you want to prepare them, dip them in warm butter.
8. Season them with coarse pepper and roll them in breadcrumbs, taking as much as possible.
9. Place them on a grill over a gentle fire and serve them without sauce.
