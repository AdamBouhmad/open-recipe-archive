## Ingredients

- turtles
- white wine
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- pigeons

## Directions

1. Cut the head and feet from turtles and cook them with white wine, bouillon, and a bouquet garni
2. Remove them carefully from their shells, being careful to remove the bitter parts
3. Put the turtles in a finished pigeon ragoût of good flavor
