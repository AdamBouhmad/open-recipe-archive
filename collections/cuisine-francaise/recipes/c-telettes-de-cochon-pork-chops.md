## Ingredients

- Pork chops
- Sauce Robert, tomato sauce, or cornichon sauce

## Directions

1. Cut and trim your pork chops as if they were veal chops.
2. Leave half an inch of fat on top.
3. Flatten them to give them a nice shape.
4. Grill them.
5. Serve underneath with a sauce Robert, a tomato sauce, or a cornichon sauce.
