## Ingredients

- three old partridges
- medium lardons (bacon strips)
- salt
- coarse pepper
- aromatic herbs
- barding lard
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- parsley and chives
- bay leaf
- thyme
- buttered paper
- white wine
- bouillon
- espagnole sauce
- game stock

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean three old partridges
2. Singe lightly
3. Lard with medium lardons seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, and pounded aromatics
4. Truss the legs as for a braised hen and tie securely
5. Place bards of lard in a casserole and arrange partridges on top
6. Add a few slices of veal, two carrots, two onions, two cloves, a bouquet of parsley and chives, a bay leaf, and a little thyme
7. Cover with bards of lard and place a round of buttered paper on top
8. Add a glass of white wine and a glass of bouillon with a little salt
9. Simmer the partridges for an hour and a half, depending on how tough they are
10. At service, drain and untie them
11. Add three tablespoons of espagnole and three tablespoons of game stock
12. Reduce the sauce by half and sauce the partridges
13. If you do not have espagnole, make a light roux and moisten with the cooking liquid from the partridges
14. Pass through a silk sieve, reduce the sauce by half so it develops flavor, degrease and strain through cheesecloth
15. Pour under the partridges
