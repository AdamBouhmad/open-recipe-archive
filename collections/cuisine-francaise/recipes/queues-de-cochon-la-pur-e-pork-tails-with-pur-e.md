## Ingredients

- six pork tails
- lentils
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaf
- bouillon or water
- salt

## Directions

1. You will have six pork tails to which you leave their rind
2. Cut them eight inches long from the thickest end
3. Clean them and singe them
4. Cook them with your lentils in which you put two carrots, two onions, two cloves, one bay leaf
5. Moisten your lentils with bouillon or water
6. Then add salt
7. When your tails are cooked, put them in a casserole with a little bouillon
8. Pass your lentils through a sieve
9. Put your purée in a casserole
10. Reduce it if it is too thin
11. At the moment of serving, arrange your pork tails on the plate
12. Cover them with your purée
