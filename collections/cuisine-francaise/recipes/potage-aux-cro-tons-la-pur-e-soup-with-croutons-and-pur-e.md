## Ingredients

- Soft bread crumb
- A quarter pound of butter
- Purée (of turnips, carrots, lentils, or peas)
- A small piece of sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread crumb three or four lines thick
2. Form them into squares, ovals, or rounds slightly larger than a small coin
3. Make twenty or thirty croutons depending on the size of your soup, ensuring they all have the same thickness and size
4. Put a quarter pound of butter in your pot with your bread pieces and set it on a high heat
5. Take care to always toss your croutons until they are well browned
6. Remove them from the pot and drain them on white linen
7. Put them in your soup tureen
8. Five minutes before serving, pour a clear, boiling purée (of turnips, carrots, lentils, or peas, whichever you prefer) over your croutons
9. Take care to put a small piece of sugar in your vegetable purée to destroy its acridness
10. This soup can be made lean or with meat
