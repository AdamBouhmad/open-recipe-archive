## Ingredients

- white calf's head
- water for disgorging and blanching
- vinegar
- blanc (cooking liquid)
- espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Have a very white calf's head; bone it; soak it to disgorge as the previous one; blanch it likewise.
2. Remove the brain that remains in the skull; soak it to disgorge; remove the fibers and the first skin that covers it.
3. Blanch it in boiling water and a dash of vinegar; cook it in a small blanc; three-quarters of an hour of cooking suffices.
4. When your calf's head is well cooled, remove it from the water, dry it well, flame it as the previous one.
5. Cut it into pieces, leaving the eyes whole and the ears likewise.
6. Truss these pieces and cook them as previously described.
7. When your head is cooked, at the moment of serving, remove it from the blanc, drain it and untruss it.
8. Arrange your pieces on the platter.
9. Separate the brain and place it at both ends.
