## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- small lard for larding (optional)
- onions
- parsnip
- carrot
- garlic clove
- cloves
- bay leaf
- basil leaves
- water or bouillon
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. If desired, lard a shoulder of mutton with small lard.
2. In the bottom of a casserole proportioned to the size of the shoulder, place some sliced onions, parsnip, carrot cut into strips, a garlic clove, two cloves, a half bay leaf, and a few basil leaves.
3. Add approximately a good half-setier of water or bouillon, and pepper.
4. If the shoulder is larded with small lard, use less salt.
5. Place the shoulder on top and cook it in the oven.
6. When it is cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve and press the vegetables firmly so they make a light purée to bind the sauce.
7. Degrease this sauce and serve it over the shoulder.
