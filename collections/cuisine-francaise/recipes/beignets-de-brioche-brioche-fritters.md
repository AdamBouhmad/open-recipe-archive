## Ingredients

- small brioches of two liards
- cooked cream or preserves
- flour
- a little oil
- salt
- white wine
- sugar
- red-hot shovel or tongs

## Directions

1. Take small brioches of two liards and cut them in half
2. Remove the crumb and put in its place cooked cream or preserves
3. Rejoin the two halves together so they appear whole
4. Dip them in a paste made with flour, a little oil, salt, and mixed with white wine
5. Fry them to a beautiful color and glaze them with sugar using a red-hot shovel or tongs
