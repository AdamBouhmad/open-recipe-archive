## Ingredients

- a thick slice of veal rouelle, two fingers thick
- small lard for larding
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- coulis
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a slice of veal rouelle two fingers thick, which you pierce on top with small lard.
2. Blanch it for a moment in boiling water, and then cook it with bouillon and a bouquet garni.
3. When it is cooked, remove it to deglaze the sauce well, pass this sauce through a tamis into another casserole.
4. Reduce it on the fire until there is almost none left.
5. Put your fricandeau in to glaze it.
6. When it is well glazed on the side of the lard, arrange it on the plate you must serve.
7. Detach what is in the casserole on the fire by putting in a little coulis and very little bouillon, taste if this sauce is of good flavor, and serve it under the fricandeau.
