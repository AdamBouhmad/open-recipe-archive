## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Coarse pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the beans
2. Boil water with salt in a large pot and add the beans
3. After a quarter hour of boiling, test if they bend under your finger
4. Drain in a strainer and place in cold water
5. At the moment of serving, melt a good piece of butter in a saucepan
6. Drain the beans and add them with salt, coarse pepper, chopped parsley and chives
7. Place on a hot fire and sauté the beans
8. When well heated, serve on a plate
9. Add lemon juice if desired
