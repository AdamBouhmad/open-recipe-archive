## Ingredients

- Whiting
- Parsley root or parsley leaves
- Chives
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Empty your whiting and scrape them
2. Clean them with a damp cloth
3. Cut off the head at the trunk and the tail a little before
4. Place them in a saucepan
5. Add parsley root or parsley leaves, two or three whole chives, a bay leaf, salt, and water
6. Simmer for a good quarter hour
7. Arrange on the plate
8. Place a little of your broth in a saucepan, break several parsley leaves into it, and bring to a boil
9. Pour it over your whiting
