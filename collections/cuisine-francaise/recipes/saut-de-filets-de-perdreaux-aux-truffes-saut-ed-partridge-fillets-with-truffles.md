## Ingredients

- Partridge fillets
- Truffles
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Spanish sauce with game essence

## Directions

1. Prepare your fillets as in the previous recipe
2. When arranged in the sauté pan, clean your truffles
3. Cut them in thick round slices of one and a half lines thick
4. Arrange them on your fillets with a little salt and coarse pepper
5. At the moment of service, sauté them over a hot fire
6. Drain them and arrange in a crown with a glazed crouton between each fillet
7. Put your truffles in the same sauce as the previous recipe
8. Sauté them in it and place them in the middle of your fillets
