## Ingredients

- artichokes
- flour
- eggs
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. If you wish to fry them, cut them into pieces and remove the choke
2. Wash them and drain them
3. When you are ready to fry them, marinate them in a pan with a small handful of flour, two egg whites and yolks, a dash of vinegar, salt, pepper
4. Fry them until they are golden, serve them with fried parsley
