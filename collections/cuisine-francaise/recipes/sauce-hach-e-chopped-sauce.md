## Ingredients

- a pinch of chopped parsley
- a pinch of shallots
- 1 tablespoon mushrooms, all chopped
- one half-glass vinegar
- a little coarse pepper
- 4 tablespoons Spanish sauce
- as much broth

## Directions

1. Place the parsley, shallots, mushrooms, vinegar, and pepper in a saucepan
2. Place on heat and reduce the vinegar until almost none remains
3. Add the Spanish sauce and broth
4. Reduce and degrease the sauce
5. When it reaches the proper consistency, it is ready
