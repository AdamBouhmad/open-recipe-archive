## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of flour
- grated green lemon zest
- toasted orange blossom, chopped
- a small pinch of salt
- five eggs, whites and yolks
- one chopine of good milk
- a piece of butter
- sugar for glazing

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan two spoonfuls of flour with grated green lemon zest, toasted orange blossom, chopped, a small pinch of salt
2. Dilute with five eggs, whites and yolks, one chopine of good milk, and a piece of butter
3. Cook it, always stirring, on the fire for half an hour
4. When it is cold, use it to make a frangipane tart or small tarts; you need only put it on puff pastry
5. When it is cooked, glaze it with sugar
