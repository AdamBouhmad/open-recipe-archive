## Ingredients

- lemon, oranges, bergamots, or Chinese limes
- water
- one glass of water
- one quarter pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut them into small pieces and cook them well in water until they are very soft under your fingers, remove them with a skimming spoon and put them in fresh water
2. Make a small syrup with a glass of water and a quarter pound of sugar, put your pieces in it to let them simmer very gently on a small fire for half an hour, and serve cold
