## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds
- water
- a pint of water
- a little sugar
- very little salt
- cinnamon
- coriander
- a zest of lemon
- sliced and dried bread

## Directions

1. Put a half-pound of sweet almonds on the fire with water.
2. When it is about to boil, remove your almonds to remove their skin.
3. Throw them as they go into fresh water.
4. Then drain them to pound them well in a mortar, watering them from time to time with a spoonful of water, for fear they turn to oil.
5. Put in a pot a pint of water, a little sugar, very little salt, cinnamon, coriander, and a zest of lemon.
6. Boil this water with what you have put in it for about a quarter hour on a stove.
7. Use this composition afterward to pass your almonds through a cloth, pressing them several times with a wooden spoon.
8. Take the dish you must serve and put sliced and dried bread on it.
9. Pour the almond milk over it as hot as you can without boiling.
10. If you want your almond milk simpler, pass it simply with hot water, then add to it the sugar you judge appropriate, and very little salt.
