## Ingredients

- half a bucket of strawberries, cleaned, washed and drained
- three half-setiers of cream
- half a bucket of milk
- sugar
- a grain of rennet the size of a coffee bean

## Directions

1. Pound the strawberries in a mortar
2. Boil three half-setiers of cream with half a bucket of milk and sugar, let them boil and reduce by half
3. Let cool slightly and add the strawberries, mix together
4. Dissolve a grain of rennet the size of a coffee bean in the cream when it is no longer warm
5. Pass it immediately through a sieve
6. Put the mixture into a compotier that can be placed on hot ashes, cover with a lid and put some hot ashes on top
7. When it is set, put it in a cool place or on ice until serving
