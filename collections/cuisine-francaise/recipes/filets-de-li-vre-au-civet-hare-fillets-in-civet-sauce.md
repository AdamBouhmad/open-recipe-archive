## Ingredients

- one roasted hare
- butter, the size of half an egg
- onions
- one garlic clove
- one bay leaf
- two cloves
- a good pinch of flour
- one glass of bouillon
- two glasses of red wine
- salt
- pepper
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a roasted hare that has been served from the table, remove all the meat and cut it into fillets
2. Break the bones a little and put them with the flanks in a saucepan with butter the size of half an egg, some onions in slices, one garlic clove, one bay leaf, two cloves
3. Pass them over the fire and add a good pinch of flour
4. Moisten with one glass of bouillon and two glasses of red wine, salt, pepper
5. Let boil for half an hour and reduce by half
6. Pass the sauce through a sieve
7. Put the hare fillets in with a little vinegar
8. Heat without boiling
