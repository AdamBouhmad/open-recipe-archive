## Ingredients

- One of five pieces of beef: the rump, the sirloin piece, the brisket, the knuckle, or the under-knuckle, boned, trussed, and tied
- Water
- Salt
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Leeks
- Onions
- Four or five stalks of celery
- Four or five cloves

## Directions

1. Place one of the five beef pieces, boned, well trussed and well tied, in a large pot that you fill almost with water
2. Put it on the fireplace hook, being careful to let a crust of foam form
3. When it boils, skim it; take cold water and put it in the pot to make another foam rise, which you then remove
4. Refresh your pot three or four times
5. Season with salt, take it down from the fireplace hook, make a bed of ash, and place it on top
6. Garnish the pot, according to its size, with carrots, turnips, leeks, onions, four or five stalks of celery, four or five cloves
7. When your piece of beef is cooked, pass your broth through fine cloth or a silk sieve
8. This broth is good only for moistening cooking, consommé, and game essences
