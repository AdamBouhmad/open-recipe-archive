## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Frying batter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the brains as directed for braised
2. After cooking, cut into six pieces
3. Place in a bowl with fine salt, a little pepper, enough vinegar for the pieces to take the flavor
4. At moment of serving, drain and put in frying batter that is not too hot
5. After frying, drain on white cloth
6. Arrange the pieces on the plate
7. Arrange a bouquet of fried parsley in a pyramid
