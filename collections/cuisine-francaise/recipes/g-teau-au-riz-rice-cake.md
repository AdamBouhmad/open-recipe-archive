## Ingredients

- One pint of cream
- Half-pound of sugar
- Three quarterons of rice
- Quarter-pound of butter
- Zest of one lemon
- Four whole eggs
- Four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of cream
2. Add half-pound of sugar and three quarterons of rice
3. When the rice bursts, melt a quarter-pound of butter into it
4. Mince the zest of a lemon and add it
5. When the rice is cold, add four whole eggs and four yolks (more if the rice is thick)
6. Butter a mold and line it with breadcrumbs, then invert so only a thin coating remains
7. After half an hour, before serving, put the rice in the mold
8. Place it on red ash and surround it with a cover on top filled with fire
9. The mold must not be full lest the rice overflow as it swells
10. At the moment of service, unmold onto the plate and take care to detach the edges
11. If you wish to make a soufflé, whip six egg whites as for other soufflés and mix them with the rice, pouring into a small silver saucepan
