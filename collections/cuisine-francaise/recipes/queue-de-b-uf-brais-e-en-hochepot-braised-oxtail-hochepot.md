## Ingredients

- oxtail
- lard
- veal or beef pieces
- three or four carrots
- four or five onions
- three cloves
- thyme
- two bay leaves
- bouillon
- lettuces
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into pieces, joint by joint
2. Soak in water for two hours
3. Blanch for half an hour
4. Place in cold water and drain
5. Trim the pieces
6. Line the bottom of a casserole with lard and a few pieces of veal or beef
7. Cover similarly with lard
8. Add three or four carrots, four or five onions (one studded with three cloves), a little thyme, and two bay leaves
9. Moisten with bouillon and bring to a boil
10. Move to a gentle fire and cook slowly for two or three hours, depending on how tough the oxtail is
11. When cooked, place the pieces in the middle of the plate
12. Arrange a ring of braised lettuces around the edge
13. Arrange small carrot batons over the oxtail to cover it and glaze the lettuces
14. Alternatively, cook the oxtail in a casserole with water and seasoning as above, with salt and carrot trimmings that have been scraped before turning; you can also moisten your small vegetables with the liquid in which the oxtail has cooked
