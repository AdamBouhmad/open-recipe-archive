## Ingredients

- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Beans
- Velouté
- Black pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Bring water to boil in a pot or saucepan with salt about the size of half an egg of butter.
2. When the water boils, add your beans.
3. As soon as they are cooked, drain them.
4. Throw a good piece of butter in a saucepan with the beans.
5. Sauté them and add a spoonful of velouté, a little salt, black pepper, and a little grated nutmeg.
6. At the moment of serving, add a liaison of two egg yolks.
