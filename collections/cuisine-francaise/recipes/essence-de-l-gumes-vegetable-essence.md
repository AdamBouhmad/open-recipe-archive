## Ingredients

- Three pounds of sliced beef
- One calf knuckle
- One hen
- Thirty or forty carrots
- Thirty or forty turnips
- Thirty or forty onions
- Five or six stalks of celery
- Two blanched lettuces

## Directions

1. Put three pounds of sliced beef in your pot, one calf knuckle, one hen, thirty or forty carrots, as many turnips, as many onions, five or six stalks of celery, two blanched lettuces
