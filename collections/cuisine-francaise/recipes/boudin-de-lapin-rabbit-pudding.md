## Ingredients

- three half-setiers of milk
- three onions
- coriander
- parsley
- chives
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- several rabbit livers
- half a pound of panne (pork fat)
- fine salt
- fine spices
- ten egg yolks
- pork intestines, about eight inches long

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with three sliced onions, coriander, parsley, whole chives, thyme, bay leaf, and basil
2. Reduce this milk to one third
3. When reduced and passed through a sieve, add several chopped rabbit livers, panne cut into small squares, a little fine salt, and fine spices
4. Mix in ten egg yolks and heat everything over a small fire, stirring constantly
5. When well mixed and not too hot, stuff this mixture into pork intestines about eight inches long, filling them only two-thirds as they will swell during cooking
6. Cook them in boiling water for a quarter hour
7. Test if cooked by pricking with a pin; if fat comes out, they are cooked
8. Remove them into fresh water and then place on a plate to grill
9. Serve dry as an appetizer
