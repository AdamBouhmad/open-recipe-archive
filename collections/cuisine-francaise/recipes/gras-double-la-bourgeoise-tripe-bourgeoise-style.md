## Ingredients

- Cooked tripe, cleaned
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Thyme
- Grease from the back of the pot or fresh butter
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take cooked tripe that has been well cleaned and cut it into pieces the size of four fingers
2. Marinate it with salt, pepper, parsley, chives, a pinch of garlic, thyme, a bit of grease from the back of the pot or fresh melted butter
3. Make the seasoning adhere to the tripe by coating it with breadcrumbs
4. Fry it
5. Serve with vinegar sauce
