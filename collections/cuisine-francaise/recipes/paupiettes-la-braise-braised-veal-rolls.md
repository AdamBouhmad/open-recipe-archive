## Ingredients

- Veal slices
- Forcemeat or godiveau
- Egg yolks
- Brandy
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lard barding
- Half a glass of white wine
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Make paupiettes the same way as the previous recipe, but without lard barding on top
2. Put lard barding in the bottom of a casserole
3. Arrange paupiettes on the barding
4. Cook over very low heat with half a glass of white wine and as much bouillon, a little salt, and coarse pepper
5. When cooked, arrange them in the serving dish
6. Degrease the cooking sauce, pass it through a sieve, and serve it over the paupiettes
