## Ingredients

- Front quarter of lamb
- Lard
- Large atelet (skewer)
- Oiled paper

## Directions

1. Bard your front quarter of lamb
2. Spit it with a large atelet and place on the spit
3. Cover with oiled paper
4. Half an hour before removing from the spit, remove the paper
5. Give it a fine color
