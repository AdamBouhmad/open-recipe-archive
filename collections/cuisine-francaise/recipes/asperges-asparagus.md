## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Oil

## Directions

1. The asparagus of Rony is preferred for taste and thickness.
2. Remove the leaves down to the bouquet with the knife blade near the tip.
3. Scrape away the outer layer from the bouquet to the end of the white part.
4. Make sure they are all the same length.
5. Tie them in bundles.
6. Twenty minutes before serving, put them in boiling salted water.
7. Test them by bending; if they are tender, refresh them so they do not cook too much and do not lose their green color.
8. If you want them with oil, serve them cold in a single bundle; they must be regularly arranged on the plate.
