## Ingredients

- Roasted leg of mutton
- Butter
- Onions
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Coarse pepper
- Muscade
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. If you have leftover roasted leg of mutton, remove the meat
2. Remove nerves and skins
3. Mince the meat
4. Put it in a pan
5. Cut six onions into dice
6. Put a quarter-pound of butter in the pan
7. Add the onions
8. Fry them in the butter until they are blonde
9. Add one spoonful of flour
10. Stir it briefly on the fire with the onions
11. Pour in about two glasses of bouillon
12. Add a little coarse pepper
13. If you do not have bouillon, moisten the onions with water and salt
14. Reduce the sauce until slightly thick
15. Put it on the minced mutton
16. Grate a little nutmeg over it
17. Mix the sauce with the meat
18. Keep warm without boiling
19. Before serving, check that it has good seasoning
