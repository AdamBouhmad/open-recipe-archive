## Ingredients

- Bread pieces, two fingers wide and slightly longer, two coins thick
- Oil
- Anchovy fillets
- Shallot
- Parsley
- Chives
- A touch of garlic
- Half a bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil powder
- Coarse pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut bread pieces two fingers wide, slightly longer, and two coins thick
2. Put them in a pan with oil and pass over low heat, turning from time to time until golden
3. Dress them on a plate
4. Place anchovy fillets on top
5. In the oil used to cook the slices, add minced shallot, parsley, chives, a touch of garlic, half a bay leaf, thyme, basil powder, coarse pepper, and a little vinegar
6. Boil for a moment
7. Dress over the slices and serve
