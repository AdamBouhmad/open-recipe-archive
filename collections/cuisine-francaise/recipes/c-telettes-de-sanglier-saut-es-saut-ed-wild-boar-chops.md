## Ingredients

- wild boar chops
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter
- espagnole sauce
- white wine
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut and trim your wild boar chops as you would veal chops
2. Put them in your sauté pan or pie dish
3. Season them with salt and coarse pepper
4. Warm some butter and pour it over them
5. Place them on a brisk fire
6. When they are stiffened on one side, turn them on the other
7. When they are firm, arrange them in a crown on your plate
8. Put in a casserole four spoonfuls of espagnole sauce, a glass of white wine which you will pour into your sauté pan to detach the glaze produced by your chops
9. Put this wine in your sauce which you will reduce by half
10. Pass your sauce through a sieve and pour it over your chops; if you do not have this, put a spoonful of flour which you will mix in your sauce
