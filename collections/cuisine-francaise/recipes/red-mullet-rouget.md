## Ingredients

- Red mullet
- White wine
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Roots and onions (bouquet)

## Directions

1. Do not scale the red mullet; gut, wash and keep the livers
2. Cook on the grill like the weever and serve with the same sauces
3. Make sure to put the livers in the sauce served over it
