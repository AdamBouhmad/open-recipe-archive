## Ingredients

- Two tablets of chocolate
- One half-quarter pound of sugar
- One chopine of milk
- One half-setier of cream
- Five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grate two tablets of chocolate and put them in a saucepan with one half-quarter pound of sugar, one chopine of milk, and one half-setier of cream.
2. Make it boil until it is reduced by one third.
3. When it is half-cold, blend in five egg yolks.
4. Pass it through a tamis and set it in a bain-marie like the previous one.
