## Ingredients

- Grapes
- Peeled and quartered fruit

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from the grapes and put the seeds in a cauldron
2. Let it boil a few times, stirring well with a spatula
3. When the seeds are well burst, pass the juice through a horsehair sieve
4. Pour it back into the cauldron; when it boils, add the peeled fruit cut into quarters
5. Cook each fruit separately; as soon as they have boiled a few times, remove them and let them drain
6. Make sure the fruits are cooked firm; skim your reduction well
7. Reduce the juice to a quarter, meaning only a quarter should remain of the total
8. Put your fruits into it; stir well with a spatula without stopping
9. When you see the grape preserve will thicken, put a little on a plate; if you see it congeals, remove the cauldron from the fire
10. Let the grape preserve rest a moment, and arrange it in your pots
11. Place them in a warm oven until the next day
12. Dip a paper disk in brandy and cover them with another paper; put string around it
13. Place the grape preserve in a dry place
