## Ingredients

- three pigeons
- two spoonfuls of butter
- one glass of bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- two bay leaves
- one anchovy
- three shallots
- a pinch of capers
- nutmeg, the size of a walnut
- butter mixed with a good pinch of flour

## Directions

1. Prepare three pigeons as previously described, flatten them slightly with the cleaver, and place them in a saucepan with two spoonfuls of butter, one glass of bouillon, salt, coarse pepper, and two bay leaves
2. Cook them over hot coals so they boil very gently
3. When they yield to pressure from your finger, arrange them on the serving dish after draining and wiping away their fat
4. Remove the bay leaves from the sauce and degrease it
5. Add the chopped anchovy, three shallots, and a pinch of capers, all chopped, nutmeg the size of a walnut, and butter mixed with a good pinch of flour
6. Thicken over the fire and serve over the pigeons
