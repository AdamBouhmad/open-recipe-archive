## Ingredients

- one and a half litron of green peas
- a quarter pound of butter
- a handful of parsley leaves
- a little green from scallion tops
- salt
- half a soup spoon of consommé or bouillon
- spinach green if needed

## Directions

1. Have one and a half litron of green peas; soak them in water
2. Add a quarter pound of butter with which you will work your peas
3. Discard the water and drain your peas in a strainer
4. Place them in a pot on a fire that is not too hot
5. Add a handful of parsley leaves and a little green from scallion tops
6. Sauté your peas for a quarter hour
7. Add a little salt and half a soup spoon of consommé or bouillon
8. Boil them on a less hot fire, covering the pot with its lid
9. After your peas have been three-quarters of an hour on the fire, place them in a mortar to pound them
10. Pass them through a strainer using cold consommé or bouillon
11. Once the purée is passed, deposit it in a pot
12. If it is not green enough, add spinach green, though the purée should be green enough by itself
13. Heat it at the moment of serving so it does not turn yellow
