## Ingredients

- one desalted cod
- a good piece of butter
- coarse pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- parsley
- chives
- a pinch of flour (optional)
- water from cooking the cod
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Once the cod is properly desalted, scrape and clean it
2. Cook it in pure water and drain it
3. Put a good piece of butter cut into small pieces in a saucepan
4. Add coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg, and finely chopped parsley and chives; if desired, mix in a pinch of flour
5. Stir the seasoning together
6. Place the cod on top with about a spoonful of cod cooking water if it is not too salty
7. At serving time, place the saucepan on the fire without leaving it; stir constantly so the butter does not turn to oil
8. When the cod is well mixed with the butter and hot, if it is too thick, add a spoonful of water
9. Arrange it on your platter and add lemon juice when serving
