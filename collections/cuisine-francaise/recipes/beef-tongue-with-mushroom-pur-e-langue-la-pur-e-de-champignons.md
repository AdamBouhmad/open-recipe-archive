## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- mushroom purée

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue as for the gherkin version
2. Cut it into slices to arrange in miroton style
3. Put a mushroom purée in the middle of your border of tongue
4. Take care to glaze the tongue, whether whole or in pieces
