## Ingredients

- Several apples
- Water
- Three lemon juices
- Three quarterons of clarified sugar
- One lemon juice
- Apricot marmalade
- Candied fruits (optional)
- Saffron (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the peel from several apples
2. Put water in a terrine with three lemon juices
3. Use a small tin mold to cut your apples into small sticks
4. Put them in the water, and the rest of the apples as well, so they do not blacken
5. Clarify three quarterons of sugar in which you press the juice of one lemon
6. Put the small sticks in it
7. Let them boil once or twice, they should only be softened
8. Remove them from the sugar and drain them on a sieve
9. Cut the apple scraps into small slices and put them in the sugar
10. When softened, remove them or let them reduce to marmalade
11. Arrange symmetrically the small apple sticks around a lightly buttered mold
12. Place some in the bottom
13. Put apple marmalade on top, apricot marmalade inside
14. The mold must be full so it can be unmolded and your chartreuse holds together without the decoration falling apart
15. You can add candied cherries, verjuice, basil, citron peel, etc. for decoration
16. If you do not use a mold, put a mass of marmalade on a plate, give it a round or square shape, arrange the apple sticks and candied fruits with symmetry
17. You can reduce the syrup, put it on a plate, when it is set, you can use it to mask your chartreuse
