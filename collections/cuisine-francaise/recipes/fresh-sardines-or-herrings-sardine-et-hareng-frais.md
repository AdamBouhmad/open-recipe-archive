## Ingredients

- Fresh sardines or herrings
- Butter
- Flour
- Vinegar
- Fine mustard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Scale and wash well, dry with a cloth
2. Cook on the grill
3. When cooked, prepare sauce with butter, a little flour, a dash of vinegar, a spoonful of fine mustard, salt, pepper, and a little water
4. Thicken the sauce on the fire and serve over the sardines or herrings
