## Ingredients

- veal breast
- forcemeat

## Directions

1. The breast must be cut specially so that all the skin remains attached
2. Place your chosen forcemeat between the skin and the tendons
3. Sew the skin so the forcemeat does not escape
4. Cook it on the spit or braised
5. Serve with whatever sauce or vegetable ragout you wish, such as with the forcemeat, leeks, peas, gherkins, or roots
