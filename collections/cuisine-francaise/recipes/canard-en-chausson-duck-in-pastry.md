## Ingredients

- One duck
- One glass of white wine
- Equal amount of bouillon
- Bouquet garni
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Debone and stuff the duck as for Canard Farci (page 164)
2. Cook it with a glass of white wine and an equal amount of bouillon, a bouquet garni, salt, and coarse pepper
3. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve
4. Degrease it and add a little coulis to bind it
5. Reduce to sauce consistency
6. Serve over the duck
