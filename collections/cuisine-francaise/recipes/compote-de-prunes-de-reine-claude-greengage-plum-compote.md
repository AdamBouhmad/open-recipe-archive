## Ingredients

- Reine-Claude plums
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. You prick the plums with several pricks of a pin, and you throw them as you go into water; afterwards you make them blanch on the fire in other water; when they have risen above, you draw them and put them promptly to cool
2. You will make them return to green by putting them on a small fire and covering them: take care that they do not boil, because they would become marmalade
3. As soon as they are well green, you will cool them again, you will drain them, and you will put them in the little sugar, which you will heat and throw two boils only; you will leave them thus until the next day, or until evening if you have need of them
4. Then you will remove them from a pot, you will let them boil until they have well taken the syrup; you see then that they do not skim anymore and that they are complete
5. If you make only one batch, and you have too much syrup, you will give it apart still some boils to make it diminish; then you will strain it over the plums: you can prepare some more, that you will keep in this manner for some time
