## Ingredients

- A quarter pound of butter
- One teaspoonful of flour
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Two spoonfuls of water
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. In your saucepan, put a quarter pound of butter, one teaspoonful of flour, finely chopped parsley and chives, salt, and coarse pepper
2. Add two spoonfuls of water
3. Put your sauce on the fire at the moment of serving
4. Work it like a white sauce
5. If it is too thick, add lemon juice with a little water
6. This sauce should be as thick as a white sauce
