## Ingredients

- Carp
- Parsley
- Scallion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Caper sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning and thoroughly scaling your carp, score it and put it on a plate with parsley, scallion, salt, pepper, and oil
2. Three-quarters of an hour before serving, place it on the grill over moderately hot fire
3. When grilled, mask it with caper sauce
