## Ingredients

- One poularde
- Good butter
- Two onions
- Two roots
- Bouquet garni of fine herbs
- Salt
- Half a glass of white wine
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Flame and clean the poularde, truss the legs into the body
2. Put a little good butter in the bottom of a casserole
3. Cut two onions into slices and place them in the butter
4. Place the poularde on top, with the breast down
5. Cover the poularde with two onions in slices
6. Add two roots cut into fillets and a bouquet garni of fine herbs
7. Season with a little salt
8. Cook the poularde this way over hot ashes
9. At half the cooking time, add half a glass of white wine
10. When cooked, deglaze the sauce and pass it through a sieve
11. Add a little coulis to the sauce
12. Serve the sauce over the poularde
