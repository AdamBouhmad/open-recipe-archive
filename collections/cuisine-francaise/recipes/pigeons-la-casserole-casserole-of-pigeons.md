## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Aromatics
- Flour
- Lemon juice or vinegar
- Bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Prepare your pigeons as previously described.
2. Put a good piece of butter in your casserole and warm it.
3. Season with salt, coarse pepper, and a little crushed aromatics.
4. Place them breast-side down in the casserole over fairly brisk heat.
5. Turn them after ten minutes.
6. Half an hour is sufficient for cooking.
7. Arrange on your platter.
8. Remove three quarters of the butter remaining in the casserole.
9. Sprinkle in a pinch of flour and mix with your cooking liquid, the juice of a lemon or two spoonfuls of vinegar, and half a glass of bouillon or water.
10. Add salt as needed.
11. Bring your sauce to boil twice and coat your pigeons with it.
12. Check that it is well seasoned.
