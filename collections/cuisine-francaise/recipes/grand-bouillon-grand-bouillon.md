## Ingredients

- One of the five best cuts of beef: rump, sirloin, breast, nut, or undernut
- Water

## Directions

1. Choose one of the five best cuts desossed, well trussed and well tied
2. Put it in a large pot that you fill almost with water
3. If possible, hang it on a crémaillère, taking care to allow a crust of scum to form
4. When it boils, skim it and add cold water to the pot to cause another scum to rise, which you then remove
5. Refresh your pot three or four times
