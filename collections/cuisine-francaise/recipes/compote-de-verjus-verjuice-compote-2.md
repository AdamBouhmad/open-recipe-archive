## Ingredients

- 1 pound verjuice, not fully ripe
- 1 glass water
- 6 ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Split each grain with the tip of a small knife to remove the seeds
2. Once all seeds are removed, throw them into water ready to boil
3. When the verjuice begins to pale, remove from heat and add a half-glass of cold water
4. Leave it in the same water until it is cold so it has time to turn green again
5. Put a fish of water with six ounces of sugar in a pan to boil
6. Add all the verjuice at once and let it boil two or three times
7. Take care to skim
8. Arrange your verjuice in the compote dish before putting the syrup on the verjuice
9. Reduce the syrup if it is too clear
