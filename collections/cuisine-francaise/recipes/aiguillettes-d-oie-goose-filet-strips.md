## Ingredients

- three geese
- espagnole sauce
- orange or lemon zest
- orange or lemon juice
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put three geese on the spit
2. When they are cooked, and at service time, cut your filets into long equal pieces
3. Take the juice thrown by your geese
4. Reduce espagnole until it is very thick
5. Pour the juice of your geese into it
6. Add a little zest of orange or lemon, the juice of one or the other, a little coarse pepper
7. Heat your sauce without boiling it
8. Pour it over your aiguillettes
