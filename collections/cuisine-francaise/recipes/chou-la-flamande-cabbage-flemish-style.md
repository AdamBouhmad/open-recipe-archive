## Ingredients

- One cabbage
- Butter
- Good bouillon
- Seven or eight onions
- Bouquet garni
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sausages
- Bread crouton

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into quarters and blanch it in boiling water for a quarter hour
2. Remove it to fresh water and press it to remove the water
3. Remove the stem and tie it up
4. Cook it with butter, good bouillon, seven or eight onions, a bouquet garni, salt and pepper
5. When almost cooked, add some cooked sausages
6. Fry a bread crouton larger than the hollow of your hand with butter
7. When the ragout is cooked, place the crouton in the bottom of the serving plate
8. Arrange the cabbage, sausages, and onions around it, all well wiped of fat
9. Degrease the cabbage sauce, add a little coulis if you have it, make the sauce short and well seasoned
10. Serve over the cabbage
