## Ingredients

- Leg of mutton
- Spanish sauce (Espagnole)
- Croutons
- Poached eggs

## Directions

1. Put a leg of mutton on the spit
2. When cold, remove enough meat for your hash
3. Remove nerves and skins
4. Mince the meat very fine
5. Place in a pan
6. Take four spoonfuls of reduced Spanish sauce and reduce it further by a quarter
7. Pour it over the minced mutton
8. Mix the sauce together
9. Keep warm only
10. At the moment of serving, pour onto the plate
11. Place croutons around it
12. Serve poached eggs on top
