## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- salt
- bouquet of fine herbs
- coulis
- butter
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Commonly they are eaten after cutting the bottom and cutting in half the leaves on top
2. Cook them in water with a little salt and a bouquet of fine herbs
3. When cooked, put them to drain and remove the choke
4. If in the fat way, you will render good coulis and put a piece of butter in it, a small dash of vinegar, salt, coarse pepper; make the sauce thicken over the fire and put it in the artichokes
5. If in the lean way, put in its place a white sauce
6. These same artichokes when cooked in water and cooled are eaten in oil with salt, pepper, vinegar
