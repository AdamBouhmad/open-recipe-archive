## Ingredients

- flour, divided into fourths
- leavening made from one quarter
- 1/3 butter
- 1/6 sugar
- sliced almonds
- 1/6 currants
- salt
- 1 glass of cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of your flour for the leavening of the three remaining quarters
2. Put them in a terrine with 1/3 butter, 1/6 sugar, almonds sliced lengthwise, and 1/6 currants (for 3 pounds of flour: 1 pound butter, 1/2 pound sugar, etc.)
3. Salt your dough as you would brioche dough
4. Warm the butter over a quarter of flour
5. Pour a glass of cream and the rest of the liquid (eggs) over it
6. Dissolve everything together
7. When your dough is soft, though somewhat thick, add the leavening
8. Mix it well with the dough
9. Butter a casserole or mold and let the dough rise in a warm place like baba, for 5 or 6 hours
10. When well risen, put it in the oven at the same heat level as for baba
