## Ingredients

- small pigeons
- truffles
- mushrooms
- poultry livers
- calf's sweetbread, blanched
- bouquet of parsley, chives
- garlic clove
- cloves
- basil
- good butter
- flour
- meat juice and bouillon
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice or white vinegar

## Directions

1. Blanch the pigeons with legs trussed inside the body, remove the neck and wings, pluck them, and place them in a casserole with two or three truffles if you have them, mushrooms, some poultry livers, a blanched calf's sweetbread cut into four pieces, a bouquet of parsley and chives, a garlic clove, two cloves, basil, and a piece of good butter.
2. Pass them over the fire, add a good pinch of flour, moisten with half juice and half bouillon, a glass of white wine, salt, and coarse pepper.
3. Let cook and reduce to short sauce, taking care to degrease.
4. When serving, add a lemon juice or a dash of white vinegar; ensure everything is cooked properly and well salted.
