## Ingredients

- Two chickens
- Mushrooms
- Veal teat
- Béchamel sauce
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. You will cook two chickens as those made à la poêle; let them cool; remove the meat from the breast forming an oval hole the length of the chicken; cut the meat into small dice; add to it mushrooms cut the same way, the size of a cooked veal teat prepared the same way; put everything in a saucepan; pour in about four tablespoons of béchamel, a little coarse pepper that you will let melt; pour it over your small ragout that you will keep warm in a bain-marie; then heat your chickens in a saucepan with a little of their stock: at the moment of serving, drain them; dress them on your platter, and put this ragout inside your chickens: take care that it is not too liquid; put a somewhat light béchamel under your chickens; if you do not have any, use velouté.
