## Ingredients

- Sheep tongues
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tongues as those described as braised
2. At the moment of serving, drain them
3. Arrange them around the edge of the plate
4. Mask them with a tomato sauce (See Tomato Sauce)
