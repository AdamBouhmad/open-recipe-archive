## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white by hand with water, the amount depending on how much sugar you want to use
2. Bring the mixture to a boil, adding cold water from time to time until the sugar is clear and well skimmed
3. Remove from heat and pass through a white cloth or a sieve
4. Use as desired
