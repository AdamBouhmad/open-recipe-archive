## Ingredients

- one chopine of milk
- one chopine of cream
- one quarteron of sugar
- rennet or gizzard
- one chopine of double cream

## Directions

1. Bring to boil one chopine of milk and one chopine of cream with one quarteron of sugar and reduce by half
2. When it is lukewarm, set it with rennet or gizzard, as the previous ones
3. When it is cold, take one chopine of double cream that you will whip with a whisk
4. As it foams, remove the foam with a skimming spoon to put it on a sieve and a plate below
5. Continue to whip the cream until you have enough to cover in a dome the velvety cream
6. Serve immediately
