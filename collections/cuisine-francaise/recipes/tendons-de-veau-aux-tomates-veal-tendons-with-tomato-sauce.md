## Ingredients

- veal tendons
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare whole tendons forming a semicircle, that is to say the tendons of two breasts which you will not cut
2. Cook them as those said à la poêle (pan-fried)
3. At the moment of serving, drain them, glaze them, and dress them on the plate so it figures the edge of a tart
4. Pour a tomato sauce in the middle
