## Ingredients

- one calf sweetbread
- water
- five or six onions
- butter
- flour
- one glass of bouillon
- one spoonful of vinegar
- Gruyère cheese
- two egg yolks
- bread
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a calf sweetbread with water
2. When cooked and drained, degrease it slightly
3. Put in a saucepan five or six onions cut into dice with butter and cook them over the fire until done and beginning to color
4. Add a pinch of flour
5. Moisten with a glass of bouillon and one spoonful of vinegar
6. Put the sweetbread in the sauce and let it simmer until the sauce is reduced and of good consistency
7. Add before serving a piece of butter and two egg yolks mixed with grated Gruyère cheese
8. Put this in the bottom of the serving dish and serve the sweetbread on top; fry the fillets of the bread cut in long pieces, layer them around the gratin with butter
9. Make another sauce: take a saucepan and put butter in it as thick as half an egg with a pinch of flour
10. Add a little bouillon and one spoonful of vinegar
11. Thicken this sauce over the fire so it is somewhat thick and put it on the sweetbread
12. Put everything on top, half bread and half grated Gruyère, and brown it underneath a pastry lid cover; it must be of such a color that no sauce remains
