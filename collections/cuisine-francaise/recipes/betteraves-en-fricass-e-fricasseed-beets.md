## Ingredients

- Beets
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Flour
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the beets in water or in the oven.
2. Put them in a casserole with butter, chopped parsley and chives, a little garlic, a pinch of flour, sufficient vinegar, salt and pepper.
3. Let them boil for a quarter of an hour.
