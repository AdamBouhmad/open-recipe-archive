## Ingredients

- Slices of bread
- Oil
- Fine oil
- Vinegar
- Coarse pepper
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboule)
- Shallot
- Anchovy fillets

## Directions

1. Cut bread slices into neat pieces the width and length of a finger
2. Fry in oil
3. Arrange in an entremets dish
4. Make a sauce with fine oil, vinegar, coarse pepper, parsley, chives, and shallot, all chopped
5. Put the sauce over and cover halfway your toasts with anchovy fillets
