## Ingredients

- Sheep tongues
- Turnips

## Directions

1. Prepare your tongues and cook them as described in the braised method above
2. At the moment of serving, drain them
3. Arrange them around the edge of the plate
4. Mask them with your turnips (See Turnips for entrée)
