## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- onions
- carrots
- bay leaves
- thyme
- bouillon
- water
- salt
- turnips
- butter
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut your breast into pieces of whatever shape you judge appropriate
2. Put round slices of onions in the bottom of your casserole
3. Arrange your pieces of breast on top
4. Add two carrots, two bay leaves, a little thyme
5. Add a good glass of bouillon
6. Bring your meat to a boil until your braising liquid is reduced to a light brown glaze
7. Add two full spoons of bouillon or water
8. Then add salt
9. Simmer your breast for two hours
10. When it is cooked, pass your braising liquid through a silk sieve
11. Remove your pieces of breast and take out the rib bones
12. Turn turnips into small sticks, put them in a casserole, and sauté them in butter until they turn blonde
13. Then add a spoonful of flour which you mix with your turnips
14. Pour the braising liquid in which your breast cooked into your turnips
15. Add a small piece of sugar
16. When your turnips are cooked, if the sauce is too thin, remove them and put them on your meat
17. Then reduce your sauce to the proper consistency
18. Take care to degrease it and pass it through cheesecloth over your breast and turnips
19. Then simmer your ragout for half an hour so it takes on flavor
