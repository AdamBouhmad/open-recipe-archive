## Ingredients

- Fennel
- Parsley
- Chives
- A small amount of garlic
- Two shallots
- Butter (about half an egg's worth)
- Fine salt
- Coarse pepper
- Three or four sweetbreads (ris de veau)
- Bacon slices
- Half a glass of white wine
- As much good broth

## Directions

1. Chop very finely some fennel, parsley, chives, a small amount of garlic, and two shallots
2. Mix all these fine herbs with butter (about half an egg's worth), fine salt, and coarse pepper
3. Blanch three or four sweetbreads
4. Prick them in several places to allow the butter and herbs to enter
5. Place the sweetbreads in a casserole with bacon slices on top
6. Add half a glass of white wine and as much good broth
7. Cook them on low heat, letting them only simmer
8. When cooked, remove the grease from the sauce, which should be short
9. Serve the sauce over the sweetbreads; if you have a spoonful of coulis, add it to the sauce for improvement
