## Ingredients

- Sugar, clarified
- Currants, one pound of fruit per pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Clarify your sugar as explained previously on page 340
2. Cook your sugar to the crack stage, which you will know by dipping a wet finger into a goblet full of water that you will hold in your left hand, dip your fingers into your sugar, then immediately into the goblet, and if your sugar dries in the water and breaks when pressed between your fingers, it is time to put the fruit into your pan
3. Make it boil twice covered
4. Remove your preserve from the fire, pass it through a sieve and put it immediately into pots
5. When it is cold, cover your pots, dip your first paper in brandy so the preserve keeps better
6. Never cover the pots except when the preserves are cold
