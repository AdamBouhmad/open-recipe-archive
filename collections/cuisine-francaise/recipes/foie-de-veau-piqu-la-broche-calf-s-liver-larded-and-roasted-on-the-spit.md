## Ingredients

- Calf's liver, well-colored
- Thick lardons, well-seasoned
- Fine lard
- Brochettes
- Atelet

## Directions

1. Have a well-colored liver; lard it with thick lardons, well-seasoned, as for the previous recipe.
2. Be careful that your liver is not damaged.
3. Lard it with fine lard.
4. Pass brochettes through the width of the liver.
5. Spit it, and put a large atelet under your liver to secure it.
6. Tie the two ends of your atelet to the spit.
