## Ingredients

- a pound of beautiful currants
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Make a strong syrup as in the previous recipe
2. Have a pound of beautiful currants washed and drained; you may leave them on the bunch if you wish
3. Put them in the syrup to give them three big boils covered
4. Remove them from the fire and skim them before dressing them in the compote dish
