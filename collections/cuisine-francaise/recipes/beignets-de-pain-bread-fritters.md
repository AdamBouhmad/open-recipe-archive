## Ingredients

- half a septier of milk
- a little sugar
- a pinch of salt
- half a spoonful of orange flower water
- a pinch of chopped green lemon
- bread crumbs
- flour
- sugar
- red food coloring

## Directions

1. Boil half a septier of milk and reduce it by half with a little sugar, a pinch of salt, a half-spoonful of orange flower water, a pinch of chopped green lemon
2. Have bread crumbs cut the size of a small coin and much thicker
3. Put them in the milk to soak for a moment
4. When they are all soaked, drain them and flour them
5. Fry them, glaze with sugar and red food coloring
