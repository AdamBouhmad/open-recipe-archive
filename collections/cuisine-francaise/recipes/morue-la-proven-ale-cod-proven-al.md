## Ingredients

- one desalted cod
- a good piece of butter
- coarse pepper
- grated nutmeg
- parsley
- chives
- one spoonful of good oil
- zest of half a lemon
- one garlic clove (optional)
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cod as in the previous recipe
2. Put a good piece of butter cut into small pieces in your saucepan
3. Add coarse pepper, grated nutmeg, finely chopped parsley and chives, one spoonful of good oil, the zest of half a lemon, and one garlic clove if desired
4. Stir everything together to mix the seasoning
5. Place the cod on top
6. At serving time, put it on the fire and stir constantly so the butter does not turn to oil
7. Arrange it on your platter and add lemon juice when serving
