## Ingredients

- Hind quarter of lamb
- Small atelets
- Lard
- Trussing needle
- Twine

## Directions

1. Cut your quarter at two cutlets near the saddle as squarely as possible
2. Put small atelets to hold the flanks
3. Bard them and pass the spit from the end of the handle to the end of the fillet without piercing it
4. Secure it with a trussing needle and twine on the spit
5. Allow one hour for your quarter to cook
