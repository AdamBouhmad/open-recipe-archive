## Ingredients

- Beautiful cauliflower
- Lard bacon strips
- Veal round
- Beef fat
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Three whole eggs
- Good bouillon
- Good coulis with butter

## Directions

1. Peel the cauliflower and cook it halfway in water
2. Remove it to fresh water and drain it in a colander
3. Take a small pan the size of the bottom of the serving plate
4. Line the bottom with bacon strips and arrange the cauliflower on top with tails pointing up
5. Make a good farce with veal round, beef fat, parsley, chives, and mushrooms, all chopped and seasoned with salt and pepper, mixed with three whole eggs
6. Fill all the cavities of the cauliflower with this farce, pressing it in with your fingers
7. Cook it with good bouillon, well seasoned
8. When cooked and the sauce is reduced, gently turn it out onto the serving plate
9. Remove the bacon strips and cover with good coulis with butter
10. Serve as an entrée
