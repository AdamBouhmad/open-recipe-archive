## Ingredients

- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Leftovers of rabbits cooked on the spit
- Two anchovies
- Capers
- Small white onions cooked in the pot (optional)
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut bread crumbs as neatly as possible like large bacon lardons
2. Place them in a pot and pass them over the fire with butter until they are a beautiful golden color
3. Drain them and set aside
4. Take the leftover cooked rabbit and remove all the meat, cutting it into thick fillets
5. Take the serving plate and arrange on it the bread fillets, the rabbit fillets, and two well-washed anchovies cut into very small fillets
6. Add whole capers
7. If you have small white onions cooked in the pot, add them and mix everything together as neatly as possible in a design
8. Season with salt, coarse pepper, oil and vinegar. Ordinarily they are seasoned only at the table.
