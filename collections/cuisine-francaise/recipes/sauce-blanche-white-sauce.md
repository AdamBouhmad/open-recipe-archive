## Ingredients

- a quarter pound of butter
- half a spoonful of flour
- salt
- coarse pepper
- one spoonful of vinegar
- a little water

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan a quarter pound of butter, half a spoonful of flour, salt, and coarse pepper
2. Work everything together with a wooden spoon
3. Pour in one spoonful of vinegar and a little water
4. Place your sauce on the fire and stir until it is bound
5. Do not let it boil, so it does not smell like glue
