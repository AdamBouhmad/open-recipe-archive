## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Fine butter
- Flour
- Ravigote
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tarragon vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the brains as directed for braised
2. Drain them
3. Arrange on your plate
4. Have a quarter pound of fine butter in a pot
5. Add three-quarters of a tablespoon of flour and chopped ravigote
6. Blend everything together with a wooden spoon
7. Add salt, coarse pepper, a dash of tarragon vinegar, and a little water
8. Place the pot on the fire and turn the sauce
9. The sauce must be thick enough to coat the brains
