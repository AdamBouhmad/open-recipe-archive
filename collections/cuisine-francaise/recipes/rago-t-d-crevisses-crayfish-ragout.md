## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water
- Half a glass of good white wine
- Good bouillon
- One glass of good coulis

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish for a moment in water
2. Peel the tails and put them in a saucepan with a half-glass of good white wine, as much good bouillon, and a glass of good coulis
3. Boil them for a quarter hour and serve as you wish
4. Alternatively, if you wish to serve them with a crayfish coulis, cook them with a little bouillon and white wine, and when there is almost no sauce remaining, put them in a crayfish coulis
