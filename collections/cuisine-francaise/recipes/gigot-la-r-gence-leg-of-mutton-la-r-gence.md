## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- lard
- salt
- fine spices
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Cut a leg of mutton across into three or four pieces
2. Lard each piece with lard seasoned with salt, fine spices, and finely chopped fine herbs
3. Cook them in the same manner as beef à la royale (found on page 57)
4. Serve hot as an entrée, or cold as an entremets
