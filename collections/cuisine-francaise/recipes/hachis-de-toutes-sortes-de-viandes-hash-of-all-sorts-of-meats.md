## Ingredients

- various meats from the butcher, poultry or game roasted on a spit
- flour
- bouillon
- meat jus
- salt
- coarse pepper
- coulis or breadcrumb paste

## Directions

1. Chop the meats very fine
2. Pass them through the fire and add a pinch of flour
3. Moisten with half a glass of bouillon and an equal amount of jus
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Boil for a quarter hour
6. Add the meat to heat through without boiling, to prevent it from shriveling
7. If the meat is tough, boil for at least an hour on very low heat
8. To bind the sauce, add a little coulis, or if you do not have it, add two pinches of fine bread crumbs
9. When serving the hash, surround it with fried bread croutons for good appearance
