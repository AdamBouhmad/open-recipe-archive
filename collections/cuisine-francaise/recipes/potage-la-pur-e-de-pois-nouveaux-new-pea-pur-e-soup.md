## Ingredients

- two and a half liters of peas
- quarter pound of butter
- small handful of parsley
- a few chives
- broth
- salt
- vert if needed
- crusts

## Directions

1. Put peas in cold water
2. Add a quarter pound of butter and mix with the peas by handling them
3. Drain them in a colander
4. Put them in a saucepan and add a small handful of parsley and some chive stems
5. Put on moderate fire and stir from time to time
6. After half an hour on the fire, put them in a mortar
7. When well crushed, pass through a sieve
8. Moisten with cold broth so the purée passes easily
9. Make it clear as for a soup
10. Boil it
11. If it is not green enough, add vert and pour over your crusts ten minutes before serving so they are soaked
