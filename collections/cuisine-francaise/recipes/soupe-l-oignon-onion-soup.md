## Ingredients

- onions
- a quarter pound of butter
- water
- salt and fine pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Peel your onions, cut them in half
2. Cut off the head and tail to avoid the acrid taste of the onion
3. Before putting your onions cut into slices in your casserole, melt a quarter pound of butter in it
4. Fry or brown your onion until it is well blond
5. Put in enough water for your soup, seasoned with salt and fine pepper, and let it boil for a quarter hour
6. Pour your broth over the bread and serve your soup
