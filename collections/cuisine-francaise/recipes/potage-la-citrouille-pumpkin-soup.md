## Ingredients

- quarter of a medium pumpkin or squash
- water
- butter, the size of an egg
- salt
- one pint of milk
- sugar to taste
- sliced bread

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and seeds from the pumpkin quarter
2. Cut the pumpkin into small pieces and place in a pot with water
3. Cook until reduced to a marmalade and no water remains
4. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg and a little salt
5. Allow it to boil a few more times
6. Boil one pint of milk and add sugar to taste
7. Pour the milk into the pumpkin
8. Arrange sliced bread in the serving dish
9. Moisten the bread with some of the pumpkin broth
10. Cover the dish and place it on some hot ashes for a quarter hour to allow the bread to soak, taking care that it does not boil
11. When serving, add the remaining pumpkin broth, well heated
