## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- vinegar
- bouillon
- coarse pepper
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the cod in water and drain
2. Place it on the serving dish with half a glass of vinegar and as much bouillon
3. Add coarse pepper
4. Boil for half a quarter hour
5. Put hot butter on top with fried parsley
