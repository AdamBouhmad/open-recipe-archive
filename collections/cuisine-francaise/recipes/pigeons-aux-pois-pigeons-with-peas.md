## Ingredients

- Three or four pigeons
- A good piece of butter
- One litron of small peas
- A bouquet of parsley and chives
- A pinch of flour
- One glass of water
- Salt
- Two eggs
- Cream

## Directions

1. Scald and blanch the pigeons
2. If they are large, cut them in two after tucking the legs inside
3. Put them in a casserole with a good piece of butter, one litron of small peas, and a bouquet of parsley and chives
4. Pass them over the fire and add a pinch of flour
5. Moisten with one glass of water
6. Cook over low heat
7. When cooked and there is no more sauce, add a little fine salt and a liaison of two eggs with cream
8. Bind on the fire without boiling
9. Serve with a light sauce
