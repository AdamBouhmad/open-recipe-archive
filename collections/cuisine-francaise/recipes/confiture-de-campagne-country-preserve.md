## Ingredients

- Sweet wine called mou
- Fruit (pears, apples, or quinces)

## Directions

1. Take sweet wine called mou, you cannot make it too sweet, take as much as you need for the amount of fruit you want to make preserve with, put it in a copper pot or pan and boil it over a clear fire until the fruit is ready to be set aside.
2. Take the fruit you want to preserve (pears, apples, or quinces), cook it in water until softened, then peel it and put it in your sweet wine syrup, and let it boil until it is cooked.
3. Be sure to skim during cooking. You will know when it is done by putting some of the syrup on a plate; if you see it remain ruby-colored and does not run when you tilt the plate, that is proof you must remove your preserve.
4. Put it in pots and cover when cold. It matters not whether the sweet wine is white or red.
