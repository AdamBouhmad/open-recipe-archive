## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- veal sweetbread
- mushrooms
- small eggs
- parsley
- chives
- cloves
- garlic
- flour
- stock
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Braise a leg of mutton in the same manner as mutton with cabbage
2. Prepare a ragout: soak veal sweetbread in tepid water, blanch for half a quarter-hour in boiling water, cool in fresh water, and cut into large dice; remove the cartilage
3. Put the sweetbread in a saucepan with mushrooms cut the same way, sauté over fire with a bouquet of parsley, chives, two cloves, and half a clove of garlic
4. Add a pinch of flour, then moisten with half stock and half bouillon in equal parts
5. Cook on very low heat, season with salt and coarse pepper
6. When cooking is almost complete, skim off fat and add small eggs that have been blanched in boiling water beforehand
7. Remove eggs to fresh water to take off the small skin
8. After they have boiled half a quarter-hour in the ragout, add lemon juice or a small splash of vinegar
9. Serve under the leg of mutton
10. Alternative for small eggs: hard boil two eggs, take the yolks and place in a mortar with half a pinch of salt, pound them, and add one raw yolk; mix well, remove to a table dusted with a little flour, roll into the shape of a small sausage, cut into very small equal pieces, roll each piece in slightly floured hands to round them, and place on a plate as you go; put them in boiling water to cook for an instant, cool in fresh water, and drain on a sieve before putting in the ragout
