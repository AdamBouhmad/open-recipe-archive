## Ingredients

- ten wings from young turkeys
- lard strips
- small turnips
- parsley and chives bouquet
- bouillon
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Scald the turkey wings, singe them, pare them, and debone them
2. Place them in a saucepan with lard strips on top and below
3. Place a skillet on top and simmer for an hour and a half, or longer if the wings are old
4. At the moment of service, drain them and arrange them in crowns
5. Make a roux; sauté your wings; baste them with bouillon with great moisture to cook them at high heat
6. Add a bouquet of parsley and chives
7. Take care to skim and degrease your ragout
8. When your wings are three-quarters cooked, add your turnips that you have cut into small sticks of the same thickness
9. Have them finish cooking in butter at the moment of serving
10. Check that your ragout is properly seasoned
