## Ingredients

- Half a medium cabbage
- Good butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Jus
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil half a medium cabbage in water for half an hour
2. Remove it to fresh water, press it well, and remove the heart
3. Chop the cabbage slightly and place it in a casserole with a piece of good butter
4. Pass it over the fire, add a good pinch of flour, moisten with bouillon and jus until there is enough to give a golden color to your ragout
5. Let it boil on low heat until the cabbage is cooked and reduced to a short sauce
6. Season with salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
7. Serve beneath the meat you judge fit
