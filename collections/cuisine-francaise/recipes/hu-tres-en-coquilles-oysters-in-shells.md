## Ingredients

- oysters
- shallots
- parsley
- mushrooms
- bacon
- oil
- butter
- four spices
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Open and detach your oysters and put them without shells in a saucepan with their water
2. Heat them without letting them boil
3. Prepare herbs as for cutlets: shallots, parsley, mushrooms, grated bacon, oil and butter, and four spices
4. Mix your oysters with your herbs and put them in well-cleaned shells; fill them
5. Arrange breadcrumbs on top; then put them on the grill over a hot fire with a camp oven on top
