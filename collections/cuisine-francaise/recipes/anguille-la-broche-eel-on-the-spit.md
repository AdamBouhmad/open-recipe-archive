## Ingredients

- eel
- butter
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- bay leaf
- thyme
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- oiled paper

## Directions

1. Place the eel on a well-heated spit; let it grill superficially, and with a cloth draw the grilled skin from head to tail by pulling
2. Once the eel is stripped and its oil removed, cut off the head; empty it and roll it like a hoop; secure it with small skewers or spits and string
3. Place it on a baking dish
4. In a saucepan, put a piece of butter, carrots cut into slices, onions cut the same way, parsley, bay leaf, and thyme; brown this seasoning well
5. Moisten it with white wine, salt, and pepper; let it boil for half an hour
6. Pass it through a silk sieve over the eel and put it in the oven
7. After three-quarters of an hour, remove it with a large lid; take it off the baking dish and put it on your plate
8. Add an Italian sauce to it; pass skewers through your eel and secure it on the spit; wrap it in oiled paper
