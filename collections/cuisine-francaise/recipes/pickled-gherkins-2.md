## Ingredients

- Small gherkins
- Long pepper
- Samphire
- Tarragon
- Cloves
- Small onions
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Brush small gherkins without scraping them
2. Place them in earthenware pots with long pepper, samphire, tarragon, a few cloves, and small onions
3. Have vinegar with salt added to it
4. Boil the vinegar and pour it into the pot with the gherkins and seasonings
5. The next day, boil it again, repeating up to four times total; then your gherkins will be green and very crisp
6. When cold, cover with parchment or paper
