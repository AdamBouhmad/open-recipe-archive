## Ingredients

- one good hen
- its liver
- lard
- parsley
- chives
- shallots
- two egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- fat from the pot
- new ham fillets
- soft bread fillets

## Directions

1. Flame, pluck and clean the hen
2. Make a stuffing from its liver with scraped lard, parsley, chives, shallots, two egg yolks, salt, pepper
3. Stuff the inside of the hen, sew it closed at both ends
4. Brown it in a pan with fat from the pot, then put it on the spit
5. Garnish all around with fillets of new ham and fillets of soft bread, the length of the hen
6. Wrap everything in several leaves of white paper so that the jus and fat cannot escape
7. Cook it at very low heat without basting
8. When taking it off the spit, be careful to put something underneath to catch the jus
9. Dress the hen in the serving dish with the ham and bread slices around it, with the jus it rendered
