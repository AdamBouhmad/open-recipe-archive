## Ingredients

- Veal round, cut the same way as for paupiettes
- Parsley, chives, shallots, mushrooms, all chopped
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Oil or butter

## Directions

1. Cut veal round the same way as for paupiettes, but without forcemeat
2. Season with oil or butter, salt, coarse pepper, parsley, chives, shallots, and mushrooms, all chopped
3. Roll and thread onto a skewer
4. Cook on a spit
5. Serve with a clear sauce, well seasoned
