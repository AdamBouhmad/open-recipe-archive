## Ingredients

- veal breast
- bouillon
- bacon
- herbs
- garlic
- salt
- lentils or dried peas
- spinach

## Directions

1. Cut a veal breast into pieces the length of a finger
2. Blanch and cook with good bouillon, a half-pound of bacon cut into slices, a bouquet of fine herbs, a clove of garlic, little salt
3. While this cooks, also cook a half-litron of lentils or dried peas with water or bouillon
4. When well cooked, pass them through a sieve as a purée
5. If making pea purée, before passing cook a handful of spinach in water, press and pound it, add it to the peas so the purée is green, then pass it, moistening with the cooking liquid from the tendons to give body to the purée
6. Place the tendons and bacon in the purée
7. Reduce on the fire if the purée is too thin
8. Serve in a terrine
