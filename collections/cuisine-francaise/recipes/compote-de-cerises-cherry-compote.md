## Ingredients

- cherries
- half a glass of water
- a quarter pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the end of the cherry stems
2. Place them in a pan with half a glass of water and a quarter pound of sugar
3. Put them on the fire and let them come to a boil two or three times covered
4. Arrange them in a compote dish and neatly pour your syrup over them
5. Serve them cold
