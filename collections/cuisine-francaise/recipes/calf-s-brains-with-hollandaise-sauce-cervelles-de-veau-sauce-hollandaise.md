## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Crayfish
- Velouté
- Tarragon vinegar
- Ravigote
- Spinach green
- Bouillon
- Roux
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the brains as directed for braised
2. At moment of serving, drain them keeping them whole
3. Place in dish with small end in the middle
4. Place a fine crayfish between them
5. Have reduced velouté into which you put a dash of tarragon vinegar
6. Take blanched ravigote passed through cloth and put in sauce at moment of serving
7. If sauce is not green enough, add a little spinach green
8. If you lack velouté, make a light roux moistened with bouillon
9. Add a dash of tarragon vinegar and coarse pepper
10. Reduce the sauce
11. At moment of serving add finely chopped ravigote with a little spinach green to green the sauce
12. Ensure it is properly salted and of good flavor
