## Ingredients

- wings
- good bouillon
- bouquet garni
- salt, pepper
- small lard
- half-litre of peas cooked with bouillon
- chive stalks and parsley
- étamine cloth

## Directions

1. Cook wings with bouillon, bouquet garni, salt, pepper, and small lard
2. Cook peas with bouillon, chive stalks, and parsley
3. Pass the peas through an étamine cloth as a purée
4. Add the cooking liquid from the wings to the purée for flavor
5. Serve the purée over the wings and small lard in a terrine
6. The purée should be neither too thin nor too thick
