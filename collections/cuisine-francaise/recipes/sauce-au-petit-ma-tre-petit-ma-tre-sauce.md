## Ingredients

- One glass of white wine
- Half a lemon, sliced
- A little very fine bread crumbs
- Two spoonfuls of good oil
- A bouquet of parsley, chives
- Two cloves of garlic
- A little tarragon
- Two cloves
- A little good bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one glass of white wine, half a lemon cut in slices, a little very fine bread crumbs, two spoonfuls of good oil, a bouquet of parsley and chives, two cloves of garlic, a little tarragon, two cloves, a little good bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper
2. Boil everything together over very low heat for a quarter hour
3. Degrease and pass your sauce through a sieve
4. Serve this sauce for poultry or game
