## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of larded bacon
- a piece of butter the size of half an egg
- veal cutlets
- two shallots
- a pinch of parsley
- salt
- coarse pepper
- half a glass of white wine
- bouillon or water
- three egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of bouillon
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into slices and place in a casserole with butter
2. Brown the bacon lightly, then add the veal cutlets
3. Cook over low heat, turning frequently until the cutlets are cooked
4. Remove the cutlets and bacon to a plate
5. Remove half the fat from the casserole and add two shallots, a pinch of chopped parsley, salt, and coarse pepper
6. Add half a glass of white wine and as much bouillon or water
7. Bring to a boil and reduce by half
8. Return the cutlets and bacon to the casserole
9. Add a liaison of three egg yolks mixed with two spoonfuls of bouillon
10. Thicken over the fire without boiling
11. When serving, add a dash of vinegar
