## Ingredients

- filet (beef filet)
- sauce tomate (tomato sauce)

## Directions

1. Trim and lard your filet as the one called à la Conti
2. Cook it in the same manner
3. Place tomato sauce underneath
4. The Indian variety is the best; you may also braise it according to taste
