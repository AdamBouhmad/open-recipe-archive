## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Meat forcemeat
- Egg (beaten)
- Bread crumbs
- Clear piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the feet as above.
2. Take a flameproof serving platter and spread a layer of forcemeat in the bottom.
3. Arrange the feet on top of the sauce and cover them with the same forcemeat.
4. Smooth the top with a knife dipped in beaten egg and coat with bread crumbs.
5. Place the platter on a small fire, cover with a pie cover, and place coals on top until golden brown.
6. Drain off the fat and serve with a clear piquant sauce underneath.
