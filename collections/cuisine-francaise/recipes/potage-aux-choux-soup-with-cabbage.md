## Ingredients

- two cabbages
- veal slices
- lard
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Blanch two cabbages cut in four pieces, more or less depending on their size, for half an hour in boiling water
2. Refresh them, then drain them
3. Tie them with string
4. Place slices of veal at the bottom of your pot, cover with lard
5. Place your cabbages on top, cover with lard
6. Place two carrots, two onions, and two cloves on top
7. When your cabbages are cooked, dip your bread soup as that with bread, that is with good bouillon
8. Drain your cabbages and place them on your soup
9. Pass the cooking liquid from your cabbages, remove the fat, and pour it into your soup
10. Alternatively, after blanching the cabbages, pressing and tying them, place them in your pot with carrots and onions, moisten with the fat of bouillon, and braise for two hours or more according to the quality of the cabbage, until it is cooked
