## Ingredients

- Bread crumbs
- Butter, a good piece
- Anchovy, chopped
- Parsley
- Chives
- Shallot
- Egg yolks, three
- Eggs, seven or eight
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take a heat-safe dish and place on it a small gratin made with bread crumbs, a good piece of butter, chopped anchovy, parsley, chives, and shallot, all chopped together
2. Mix in three egg yolks and combine everything
3. Place this mixture in the bottom of the dish to the thickness of a coin and let it set on a low fire
4. Then break seven or eight eggs on top and season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Cook gently and pass a red-hot iron or shovel over the top
6. When cooked with soft yolks, serve
