## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- milk
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut your bread as for the previous soup; you will pass your crumb in butter, as is explained in the crouton soup; you will put the number of croutons so that your soup is not too thick; at the moment of serving pour your sweetened milk over your croutons; try that your milk is neither too much nor too little sweetened; put five or six grains of salt in it.
