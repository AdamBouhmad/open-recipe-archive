## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, roasted on a spit and cooled
- chicory hearts
- Spanish sauce (3 spoons)
- consommé (3 or 4 spoons)
- coarse pepper
- salt
- butter
- croûtons fried in butter
- flour (if Spanish sauce is not available)

## Directions

1. Once the leg of mutton is roasted and cooled, remove the meat and slice it thinly
2. Deposit the slices in a saucepan
3. Blanch chicory hearts and press well to remove water
4. Mince the chicory and pass it through a sieve with butter
5. Add three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and three or four spoonfuls of consommé, a little coarse pepper and salt
6. Reduce the chicory
7. When thickened slightly, pour it over the sliced mutton, mix well together, and keep warm without boiling
8. When ready to serve, arrange in a pyramid shape with croûtons cut into mushroom shapes and fried in butter around it
9. Pour a little Spanish sauce over the top, but sparingly
10. If Spanish sauce is not available, pass the chicory in butter with salt and coarse pepper; when well browned, add one spoonful of flour, mix well with the chicory, moisten with broth, reduce if too thin (but it should be more liquid than for an entrée), and pour over the sliced mutton
11. Check seasoning
