## Ingredients

- Brie cheese, well aged
- litre and a half of flour
- three quarters of a pound of butter
- very little salt
- five or six eggs

## Directions

1. Take Brie cheese that is very ripe and knead it with litre and a half of flour
2. Add three quarters of a pound of butter and very little salt
3. Add five or six eggs to thin your dough
4. When well kneaded, moisten it and let it rest for an hour
5. Form your cake in the usual way and bake
