## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Bouillon
- A quarter pound of tender bread crumb
- Coarse salt
- Butter
- Croûtes (bread slices)

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts as explained for the garbure
2. After cooking, reserve twenty-four whole
3. Pound the rest
4. Soak in bouillon a piece of tender bread crumb weighing a quarter pound
5. Pound this with the chestnuts
6. When everything is well crushed, dilute with hot bouillon
7. Pass through a cloth sieve
8. Put your purée on the fire, keeping it clear enough so the soup is not too thick
9. Pour it over croûtes passed in butter at the moment of serving
10. Add your twenty-four chestnuts on top
11. Ensure your soup is well seasoned with salt
12. This soup can also be made lean by using lean bouillon instead of meat bouillon
