## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- lard strips
- veal slices
- two carrots
- two onions
- clove
- bay leaf
- butter paper
- consommé or bouillon
- chicory

## Directions

1. Scald your wings, singe them; debone them
2. Lard them finely
3. Place lard strips in the bottom of a saucepan, some slices of veal, two carrots cut into slices, two onions, a clove, a bay leaf
4. Place your wings on top of this seasoning, a round of buttered paper, a pot spoonful of Consommé or bouillon
5. Simmer them for an hour with fire above and below
6. Take care that your wings do not take on too much color
7. At the moment of serving, drain them; glaze them, and place chicory on the platter, your wings on top, or arrange them in crowns, and your chicory in the middle
