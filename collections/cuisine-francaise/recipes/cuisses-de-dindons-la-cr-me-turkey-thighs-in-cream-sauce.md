## Ingredients

- turkey thighs
- lard
- butter, size of an egg
- half a spoonful of flour
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- two shallots
- three cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- two pinches of coriander
- half a septier of milk
- bread crumbs
- half a glass of juice
- two spoonfuls of verjuice

## Directions

1. If using raw turkey thighs, lard them crosswise with thick lard
2. In a casserole, combine butter mixed with flour, salt, pepper, parsley, chives, garlic, shallots, cloves, bay leaf, thyme, basil, and coriander
3. Add half a septier of milk and bring to a boil
4. Place the turkey thighs in the mixture and boil on very low heat
5. When the thighs become tender under the fingers, remove and drain them
6. Take the fat from the cooking liquid and dip the thighs in it
7. Bread them immediately and grill on low heat, lightly basting with the remaining fat
8. In a casserole, heat half a glass of juice with two spoonfuls of verjuice, salt, and pepper
9. Serve the sauce under the thighs
