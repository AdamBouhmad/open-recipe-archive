## Ingredients

- Two or three pounds of beef breast
- A quarter of a large cabbage
- Bouillon
- Parsley
- Chives
- A clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- A bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Four large whole onions
- Four sausages
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Take two or three pounds of beef breast, cut it neatly into three or four equal pieces
2. Blanch them briefly in boiling water
3. Also blanch half a large cabbage for a good quarter hour
4. Put the beef breast to cook with a little bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme, and basil
5. After an hour add the cabbage cut into three pieces, well pressed and tied, with four large whole onions
6. When everything is almost cooked, add four sausages, a little salt and coarse pepper
7. Finish cooking so that little sauce remains
8. Drain the meat and vegetables and dry them of their fat with a cloth
9. Arrange the beef in the middle of the plate, the cabbages and onions around it, the sausages on top
10. Pass the sauce through a sieve and remove the fat
11. Serve over the ragout
