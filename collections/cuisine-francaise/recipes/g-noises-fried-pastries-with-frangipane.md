## Ingredients

- short pastry dough
- frangipane cream

## Directions

1. Make short pastry dough
2. Roll out to the thickness of a small coin
3. Cut with a pastry cutter of the same size
4. Place a full teaspoon of frangipane cream on each piece
5. Moisten the edges slightly
6. Cover with another piece of dough
7. Seal together well by pricking all around
8. Fry until golden color
9. Drain on a cloth
10. Glaze the top with sugar and red icing
