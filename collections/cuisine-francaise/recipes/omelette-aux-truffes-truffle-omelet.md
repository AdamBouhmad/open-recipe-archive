## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- truffles sautéed in espagnole sauce
- espagnole sauce
- flour
- bouillon
- meat jus
- salt
- coarse pepper
- quatre épices

## Directions

1. Break eggs, season and beat them.
2. Put a piece of butter in a pan over a clear fire.
3. As soon as it is melted, add the eggs.
4. Stir the omelet by shaking so it does not stick, or with a fork or spoon, lift them until your omelet is set.
5. Remove the bulk of the inside with a spoon.
6. Take truffles sautéed in a reduced espagnole sauce and put them in the void you have made in the omelet.
7. Fold it so it has the shape of a turnover.
8. Place it on the dish.
9. Chop two truffes very finely.
10. Pass them in a small piece of butter.
11. Add four spoonfuls of espagnole sauce and pour your sauce hot over the omelet.
12. If you do not want to use sauce, take a teaspoonful of flour that you mix with your truffles when they are cooked, and moisten them with bouillon, jus, a little salt, coarse pepper, and very little quatre épices.
13. By this same procedure, you can make omelets with mushrooms, with poultry or game purée, etc.
