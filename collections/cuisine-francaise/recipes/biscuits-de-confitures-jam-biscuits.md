## Ingredients

- Candied lemon zest
- Orange blossoms, pralined
- Two spoonfuls of apricot marmalade
- Three ounces of fine sugar
- Four fresh egg yolks
- Four egg whites

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar candied lemon zest with a good pinch of pralined orange blossoms
2. Add two spoonfuls of apricot marmalade, three ounces of fine sugar, and four fresh egg yolks, keeping the whites aside
3. Mix everything together and pass through a sieve, pressing with a spoon until nothing remains in the sieve
4. Add the four whipped egg whites and mix with the rest
5. Dress the biscuits lengthwise on white paper like those à la cuiller
6. Sprinkle a little fine sugar over them to glaze and bake in a gentle oven
