## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of verjuice, almost ripe
- 1 pound of sugar per pound of marmalade

## Directions

1. Place the verjuice, with stems removed, in water ready to boil
2. When it is ready to boil, remove from the fire and cover to make it return to green color
3. Leave in the same water until it is cold
4. Remove and pass through a sieve, pressing firmly with a spoon to extract as much marmalade as possible
5. Put the marmalade in a pan and dry it on the fire until it is very thick
6. For each pound of marmalade, cook one pound of sugar to the large plume stage
7. Mix the marmalade into the sugar to blend well
8. Return to the fire only to heat through, stirring constantly until it is ready to boil
9. Put into jars
