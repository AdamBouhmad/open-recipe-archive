## Ingredients

- two hard egg yolks
- one anchovy
- capers
- one glass of good bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter the size of half an egg mixed with a pinch of flour

## Directions

1. Chop two hard egg yolks and put half in a saucepan with chopped anchovy and capers
2. Add one glass of good bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Add butter kneaded with a pinch of flour
4. Thicken the sauce over the fire
5. Pour over what you wish to serve and sprinkle the remaining chopped egg yolk on the meat
