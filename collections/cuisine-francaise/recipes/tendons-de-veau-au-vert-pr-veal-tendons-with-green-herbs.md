## Ingredients

- veal breast
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- clove
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- sorrel
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the tendons from a veal breast into equal pieces the width of a finger
2. Blanch them briefly in boiling water
3. Place them in a pot with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, two cloves, bay leaf, thyme, and basil
4. Simmer until the tendons are cooked and almost no sauce remains
5. Take two handfuls of sorrel with stems, wash well, press to remove water, place in a mortar and pound very fine
6. Press very hard to extract at least a half-glass of juice, pass through a sieve
7. Use this juice to dilute three egg yolks
8. Pour this mixture into the tendons and thicken on the fire without boiling, like a chicken fricassée
