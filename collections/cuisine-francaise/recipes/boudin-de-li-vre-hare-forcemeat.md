## Ingredients

- Hare fillets
- Veal teat, cooked
- Soft breadcrumbs, soaked in bouillon
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove only the fillets and remove the sinews.
2. Pound your meat, then pass it through a quenelle sieve and gather it well to form a ball.
3. Have a cooked veal teat which you will chop and pound, passing it through a quenelle sieve.
4. Soak soft breadcrumbs in bouillon and press them well in a clean cloth to extract the bouillon.
5. Pound them and pass them through a quenelle sieve.
6. Set your breadcrumbs, teat, and meat separately.
7. Take as much breadcrumb as hare meat and as much teat as these two combined.
8. If you lack teat, use butter instead of breadcrumbs and meat, that is to say twice as much as the meat.
9. Pound it all together.
