## Ingredients

- whole pears with skin
- sugar
- half a septier of water
- a slice of lemon

## Directions

1. Blanch your whole pears with their skin in boiling water
2. When they are one-third cooked, remove them to fresh water
3. Peel them whole or in halves, placing them in fresh water as you work
4. Boil your sugar in a pan with half a septier of water
5. Put your pears in with a slice of lemon so they remain white
6. When they are cooked and have a good syrup, serve them hot or cold according to the master's taste
