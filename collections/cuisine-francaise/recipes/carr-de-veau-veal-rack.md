## Ingredients

- Veal rack
- Small skewers

## Directions

1. Trim your rack so there are no bones along your filet
2. Pass small skewers from the end of the rib to the thick part of the filet
3. Spit it with a large skewer and lay it on the spit so your filet does not have a spit hole
