## Ingredients

- veal breast
- lard
- onions
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- consommé
- coarse pepper
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Pare and truss your veal breast as for the glazed version
2. Place lard in the bottom of your casserole
3. Cut onions into slices and put them in the bottom of your casserole
4. Place your veal breast there
5. Cover it with lard
6. Add more sliced onions on top, two bay leaves, a little thyme, half a pot spoon of consommé, a pinch of coarse pepper
7. Cook your veal breast with fire underneath and on top for two and a half hours
8. When it is cooked, drain it and glaze it with the glaze from your onions
9. Arrange it on the platter with glazed onions around it
10. Pour two spoonfuls of prepared Spanish sauce into your onion glaze, with a spoonful of consommé
11. Detach your glaze with your sauce and place it under your veal breast
12. Ensure everything is hot when you serve
