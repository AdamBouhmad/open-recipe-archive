## Ingredients

- casings of desired thickness
- meat
- onions (optional)
- melted lard or lard fat (for onion version)
- truffles (optional)

## Directions

1. Fill casings of desired thickness with meat and tie them at both ends
2. Smoke them in a smokehouse for two days
3. Cook them for two to three hours, depending on their thickness, in unsalted broth
4. For cervelats with onion: chop onions according to the quantity of meat you have, cook them with melted lard or lard fat, when they are three-quarters cooked, add them to the meat and finish the cervelats as described above
5. For cervelats with truffles: chop the meat and add chopped truffles without cooking them, finish the cervelats the same way
