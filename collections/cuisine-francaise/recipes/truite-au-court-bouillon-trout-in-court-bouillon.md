## Ingredients

- Trout
- Onions
- Four cloves
- Four bay leaves
- Sprigs of thyme
- Handful of parsley in branches
- Salt
- Six bottles of white wine

## Directions

1. Clean the trout without opening its belly
2. Wash and dry it well
3. Tie its head and put it in a fish kettle
4. Cut onions into slices, add four cloves, four bay leaves, and several sprigs of thyme
5. Add a handful of parsley in branches and salt
6. Add six bottles of white wine
7. Simmer for an hour, or longer if it is large
8. Serve on a napkin that you fold on a plate
9. Arrange parsley à la génoise around it
10. Moisten with red wine and add little salt so you can use its cooking liquid passed through a silk sieve
