## Ingredients

- Calf's ears
- Blanc (cooking liquid)
- Marinade

## Directions

1. Prepare the ears as directed for the Italian style
2. Cook them in a blanc
3. When cooked, drain them
4. Cut them lengthwise into two, three or four pieces as you see fit
5. Pour marinade over them a quarter hour before serving
6. Drain them
7. Put them in frying batter
8. Put them in oil that is not too hot
9. When they have a nice color, remove them
10. Drain them on white cloth
11. Arrange them on your plate
12. Fry a handful of parsley and arrange it in a pyramid on top
