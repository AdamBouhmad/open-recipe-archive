## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs
- Piquant sauce

## Directions

1. After cooking them in water, remove the large bones and leave them whole.
2. Place them in a saucepan with a good piece of butter, chopped parsley, chives, a hint of garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Cook until almost all sauce is reduced, stirring near the end to prevent sticking.
4. When cooled, dip them in the remaining sauce and coat with bread crumbs.
5. Grill them and serve dry with a piquant clear sauce.
