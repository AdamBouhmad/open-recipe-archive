## Ingredients

- One capon
- Bouillon
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- Two cloves
- Half a pound of rice, washed five or six times
- Coarse pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. After emptying and cleaning the capon, tuck the legs inside, then lard it lightly
2. Truss it with a large needle as if quilting, securing the thighs and legs so they do not spread while cooking
3. Put your capon in a casserole
4. Fill it with bouillon
5. Skim your poultry, then add two carrots, two onions, two cloves, and half a pound of washed rice
6. Boil everything on low heat for two hours
7. Untruss your poultry and put it in your tureen
8. Pour your rice over it; do not include the carrots or onions
9. Season your soup well with salt and add a little coarse pepper
