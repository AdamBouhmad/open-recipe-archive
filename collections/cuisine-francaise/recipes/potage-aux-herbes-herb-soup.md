## Ingredients

- all sorts of cleaned and well-washed herbs
- parsnip
- carrot
- sorrel
- lettuce
- chervil
- purslane
- a little celery
- good broth
- a little veal juice

## Directions

1. Put all sorts of cleaned and well-washed herbs in a small pot with a parsnip and a carrot cut into small strips.
2. The herbs are sorrel, lettuce, chervil, purslane, and a little celery cut into strips.
3. Cook everything with good broth and a little veal juice.
4. When cooked and of good salt, simmer the soup.
5. Serve your herbs in the soup naturally without making a garnish.
