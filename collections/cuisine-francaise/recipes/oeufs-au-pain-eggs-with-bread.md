## Ingredients

- half a handful of breadcrumbs
- a fish-measure of cream
- salt
- pepper
- a little nutmeg
- ten eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan half a handful of breadcrumbs with a fish-measure of cream, salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg
2. When the bread has absorbed all the cream, break ten eggs into it
3. Beat them together to make an omelet
