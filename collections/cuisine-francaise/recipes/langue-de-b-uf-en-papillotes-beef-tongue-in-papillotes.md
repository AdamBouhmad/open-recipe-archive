## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- sauce à papillotes
- thin slices of lard
- double oiled paper
- clear jus

## Directions

1. When your tongue is cooked as previously described, cut it into pieces in the shape of cutlets
2. Arrange the pieces on a plate and pour sauce à papillotes over them
3. When the sauce is cold, coat the pieces of tongue with it
4. Place a thin slice of lard on top and underneath
5. Take a double square of oiled paper and wrap the piece of tongue in it
6. Pleat all around the edges as tightly as possible so the sauce does not escape during grilling
7. Use a gentle fire to grill these papillotes
8. Arrange them around the edge of the plate and put clear jus underneath
