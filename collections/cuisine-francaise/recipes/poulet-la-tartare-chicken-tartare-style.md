## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Good fresh butter
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboule)
- Mushrooms
- A hint of garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Singe and gut the chicken, sear it on the fire and cut it in half, break its bones slightly, and marinate it with good fresh butter that you melt, mixed with parsley, chives, mushrooms, a hint of garlic, all chopped, salt, and pepper
2. Dip it in the butter and coat with breadcrumbs, grill it over low heat, and serve dry or with a good light sauce
