## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ripe healthy cherries
- 1 large glass water
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Take 2 pounds of beautiful ripe healthy cherries, remove the stems and pits, and put them on the fire with 1 large glass of water
2. Boil them eight or ten times and strain through a sieve
3. Put 2 pounds of sugar on the fire with 1 glass of water, boil it and skim well
4. Continue boiling it until the sugar reaches the proper consistency
