## Ingredients

- half a litron of flour
- a quarteron of fine sugar
- one egg white
- half a spoonful of orange flower water
- butter the size of half an egg
- half a glass of water
- a small pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put on a table half a litron of flour with a quarteron of fine sugar, one egg white, half a spoonful of orange flower water, butter the size of half an egg, half a glass of water, and a small pinch of salt
2. Knead everything together
