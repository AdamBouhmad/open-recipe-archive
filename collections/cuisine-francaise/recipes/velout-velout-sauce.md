## Ingredients

- consommé
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare the velouté as you would the Spanish sauce
2. Reduce, degrease, and skim in the same manner
3. Ensure your velouté remains very white; for this avoid using consommé that has color
4. Add mushrooms
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Keep warm in a bain-marie
