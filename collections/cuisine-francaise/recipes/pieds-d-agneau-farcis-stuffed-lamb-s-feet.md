## Ingredients

- lamb feet
- farce de quenelle de volaille (poultry quenelle forcemeat)
- nutmeg
- fines herbes (fine herbs)
- blanc (white cooking liquid)
- sauce hollandaise verte (green hollandaise sauce)

## Directions

1. Debone your lamb feet as the preceding ones
2. Before blanching them, fill them with a forcemeat of poultry quenelle in which you put a little grated nutmeg and a little fine herbs
3. When your feet are well filled, sew the end with a needle and heavy thread so your forcemeat does not come out
4. Blanch them for five minutes in boiling water
5. Refresh them and singe them, removing the hair in the cleft of the foot
6. Make a short blanc, that is with little liquid
7. Pour it over your lamb feet which are in your saucepan and simmer them for two hours
8. At the moment of serving, drain them
9. Trim them and dress them on the platter
10. Mask them with a green hollandaise sauce
