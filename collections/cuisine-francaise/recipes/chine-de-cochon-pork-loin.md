## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Sauce Robert, tomato sauce, or piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Cut your piece of meat squarely.
2. Leave everywhere the thickness of one finger of fat; your square piece should be well covered.
3. Slash the top, that is to say the fat that covers it.
4. Spit it for two hours.
5. Before serving, put it on the fire.
6. Serve it as a roast or as an entrée with a sauce Robert, a tomato sauce, or a piquant sauce.
