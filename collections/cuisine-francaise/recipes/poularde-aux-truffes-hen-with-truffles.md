## Ingredients

- poularde (hen)
- truffes (truffles)
- beurre (butter)
- sel (salt)
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- quatre épices (four spices)
- bardes de lard (lard lardons)
- rouelle de veau (veal wheel)
- oignons (onions)
- carottes (carrots)
- feuilles de laurier (bay leaves)
- clous de girofle (cloves)
- bouillon (broth)
- jus de citron (lemon juice)
- espagnole (Spanish sauce)
- consommé (consommé)

## Directions

1. Flame and gut the hen through the stomach
2. Remove the breastbone
3. Fill the hen with truffles that you have passed through a good piece of butter seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, and four spices
4. Arrange everything in the body of the hen
5. Truss the hen as for an entrée
6. Rub the stomach with lemon juice
7. Cover with lard lardons
8. Place two or three lardons in a saucepan with the hen on top
9. Cut veal wheel into large dice about a pound and a half, add four onions, three carrots cut into pieces, two bay leaves, two cloves, half a pound of butter, and all the truffle trimmings
10. Put everything in a saucepan over a hot fire and pass for ten minutes
11. Pour in a pot spoon of broth
12. Place this seasoning on your hen
13. One hour before serving, put on the fire with fire on the lid
14. At the moment of serving, drain and unbridle the hen and dress it on your platter
15. For sauce, chop three truffes and pass them through butter
16. Add six skimming spoons of espagnole and three spoons of consommé
17. Put twelve truffles in your sauce
18. Reduce by a third, degrease, and place under your hen
19. Check that it is properly salted
20. If you do not have espagnole, make a light roux that you will moisten with the broth in which your hen has cooked, reduce it and degrease it, and use it as sauce
