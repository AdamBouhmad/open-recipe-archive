## Ingredients

- six large carrots
- four large turnips
- six large onions
- three stalks of celery
- four leeks
- a piece of butter
- a piece of sugar the size of half an egg

## Directions

1. Mince the carrots, turnips, onions, celery, and leeks
2. Put a piece of butter and a piece of sugar the size of half an egg in your saucepan with your vegetables
3. Pass them blanched, that is to say do not let them take color by tossing them over the fire
4. When you see they will start to take color, moisten them with broth and let them simmer for two hours over the fire
5. Pass them through a sieve
6. Clarify your purée
7. Make sure it is not too thick
8. After, soak your bread with good broth
9. Serve the purée poured over the bread
