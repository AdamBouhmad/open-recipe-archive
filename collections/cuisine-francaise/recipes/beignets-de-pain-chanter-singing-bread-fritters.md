## Ingredients

- twenty-four large singing breads
- cream of frangipane or preserves
- water
- flour
- white wine
- one spoonful of oil
- salt
- sugar
- red food coloring

## Directions

1. Take two dozen large singing breads; take one where you will put on it a little frangipane cream or preserves
2. Moisten the edge with water, and apply another singing bread on top
3. Pinch them all around to glue them together
4. When they are all made, dip them in a batter made with flour, white wine, a spoonful of oil and salt
5. Fry them and glaze with sugar and red food coloring
