## Ingredients

- Veal tendons
- Butter
- Flour (1.5 spoonfuls)
- Bouillon
- Pepper
- Mushrooms
- Bouquet garni
- Small onions
- Egg yolks (3)

## Directions

1. When the tendons are well browned in your butter, pour over them one and a half spoonfuls of flour
2. Sauté everything in your pot: to mix the flour with your butter, moisten the tendons well with bouillon
3. Add a little pepper, mushrooms, and a bouquet garni
4. When the tendons have boiled for two hours, add small onions that are well peeled and all the same size
5. When the onions are cooked, if the sauce is too thin, remove the tendons, onions, and mushrooms and place them in another pot, and reduce the sauce until there is enough to coat the tendons
6. Pass the sauce through a sieve over the tendons; keep it hot in a bain-marie
7. At the moment of serving, bind with a liaison of three egg yolks
8. Arrange the tendons on the plate with the onions and mushrooms on top
