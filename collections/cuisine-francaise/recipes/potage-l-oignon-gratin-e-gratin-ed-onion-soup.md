## Ingredients

- Onions
- Half a pound of butter or more according to quantity of onions
- Bread cut in very thin slices
- Coarse pepper
- Bouillon
- Salt

## Directions

1. Fry the onions in butter enough so they are well golden
2. Take bread cut in very thin slices
3. Make a layer of bread and a layer of onions
4. Put a little coarse pepper on each layer until the dish is full
5. Moisten with bouillon and simmer so the crust forms without letting it burn
6. This would give it a harsh taste
7. Your soup must be almost dry
8. Put bouillon in a vessel and serve it so people can add to their plate
9. Be careful with salt due to reduction
