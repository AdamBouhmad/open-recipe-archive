## Ingredients

- beef palate cooked in blanc
- velouté
- egg yolks
- parsley

## Directions

1. When your palates are cooked in blanc, drain them
2. Cut them as for béchamel
3. Have well reduced velouté
4. Bind it with two egg yolks
5. Pass your bound sauce through a sieve and put it over your palates which are in a saucepan
6. Add finely chopped parsley that you have blanched
7. Toss your palates in your sauce
8. Serve them well hot
