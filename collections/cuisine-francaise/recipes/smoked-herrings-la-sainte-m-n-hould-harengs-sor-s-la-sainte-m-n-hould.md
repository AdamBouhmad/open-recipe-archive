## Ingredients

- A dozen smoked herrings
- Water
- Milk
- Hot butter
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Egg yolks
- Pepper
- Verjuice

## Directions

1. Cut off the end of the head or tail of each herring
2. Soak for four hours in water, then two hours in half a setier of milk
3. Drain and dry
4. Dip in hot butter mixed with half a bay leaf, thyme, basil chopped like powder, two egg yolks, and coarse pepper
5. Coat as you dip them in the butter and grill lightly
6. Put two spoonfuls of verjuice in the bottom of the serving dish and arrange the herrings on top
