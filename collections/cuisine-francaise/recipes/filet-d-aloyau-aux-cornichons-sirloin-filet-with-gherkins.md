## Ingredients

- filet (sirloin)
- lard fin (fine lard)
- cornichons (gherkins)
- grande espagnole (espagnole sauce)
- vinaigre (vinegar)
- sel (salt)
- poivre fin (fine pepper)
- consommé (consommé)
- roux (roux)
- fond ou bouillon (broth or stock)

## Directions

1. Trim your filet as you would for braising
2. Lard it with fine lard and place it on a spit, then arrange it on your serving platter in such a way that it is not too long so your dish can contain it
3. Prepare your gherkins cut into small sticks
4. Take six spoonfuls of espagnole sauce and add three spoonfuls of vinegar, a little salt, and fine pepper to dominate; add eight spoonfuls of consommé
5. Reduce everything by half, skim and degrease your sauce
6. When reduced, strain it through cheesecloth, add your gherkins, and keep this sauce hot without boiling
7. After your filet has been on the spit for an hour and a half, place it on your platter with your sauce underneath
8. If you lack espagnole, make a light roux, moisten it with stock or broth, season as above, add your gherkins cut into thin slices, salt, and fine pepper
