## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Lightly singe your chicken.
2. Cut off the legs and truss the thighs inside.
3. Remove the giblet pocket and neck.
4. Split the chicken down the back from neck to tail.
5. Empty it and flatten it without bruising it.
6. Warm a good piece of butter.
7. Season your chicken with salt and coarse pepper and dip it in the warm butter, coating it thoroughly.
8. Roll it in breadcrumbs, pressing them on as much as possible.
9. Three quarters of an hour before serving, place it on the grill over a gentle fire.
10. At the moment of serving, arrange it on the platter and serve with tarragon sauce.
