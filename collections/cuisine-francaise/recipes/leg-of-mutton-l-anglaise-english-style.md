## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- coarse lard
- broth
- parsley
- chives
- one good clove of garlic
- three cloves
- one bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- one glass of broth
- nearly as much coulis
- capers
- one anchovy
- parsley
- chives
- one shallot
- one hard egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut the shank and skin a little on the joint bone so you can bend the shank without disfiguring the leg
2. Lard the whole leg across with coarse lard
3. Tie the leg and place it in a pot just fitted to its size with broth, a bouquet of parsley, chives, one good clove of garlic, three cloves, one bay leaf, thyme, basil, salt, and pepper
4. When cooked, drain it and wipe the fat from it with a cloth
5. For the sauce: put one glass of broth and nearly as much coulis in a casserole, with capers, one anchovy, a little parsley, chives, one shallot, and one hard egg yolk, all chopped very fine
6. Boil for two or three minutes
7. Serve on the leg
