## Ingredients

- lamb tongues, cooked and peeled
- salt
- coarse pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- a pinch of garlic
- half a lemon, cut in slices
- fine oil
- lard strips

## Directions

1. After the tongues are cooked in water and cleaned of their skins, marinate them with salt, coarse pepper, parsley, chives, mushrooms, a pinch of garlic (all chopped), half a lemon cut in slices, and fine oil
2. Place each tongue half with all the seasoning in white paper rubbed with oil, with lard strips above and below
3. Fold the paper all around so nothing escapes and cook on the grill over very low heat
4. Serve with the paper
