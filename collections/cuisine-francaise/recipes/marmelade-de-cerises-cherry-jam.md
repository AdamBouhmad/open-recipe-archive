## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- four pounds of cherries

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of sugar in the same manner as for raspberry jam. Then add four pounds of cherries after removing their pits and stems. Stir them with the sugar and let them boil together until the syrup sticks to your fingers. Remove from the fire to put in pots.
