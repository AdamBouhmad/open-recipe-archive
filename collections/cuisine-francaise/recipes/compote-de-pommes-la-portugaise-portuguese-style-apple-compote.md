## Ingredients

- reinette apples as needed to fill the serving dish
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the cores from the apples using an iron tube or knife
2. Arrange them in a baking dish or on a silver plate
3. Place a small piece of sugar or powdered sugar in each apple, and some sugar at the bottom of the dish
4. Cook in the oven or under a camp oven, with heat above and below
5. Serve hot with a little powdered sugar on top
