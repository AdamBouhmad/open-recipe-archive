## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shallots
- White wine
- Lemon juice
- Butter

## Directions

1. When your woodcocks are on the table, piece them and leave them on the plat which you put on a spirit lamp.
2. Add salt, coarse pepper, a spoonful of chopped shallots, three-quarters of a glass of white wine, the juice of three lemons, and butter about the size of half an egg.
3. Sprinkle your salmi with chervil.
