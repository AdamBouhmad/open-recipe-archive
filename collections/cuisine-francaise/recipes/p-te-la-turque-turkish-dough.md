## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of blanched almonds
- 1 pound of flour
- 1/2 pound of butter
- 3/4 pound of powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon of saffron powder
- eggs

## Directions

1. Pound 1/2 pound of blanched almonds well until very fine
2. Use 1 pound of flour, 1/2 pound of butter, 3/4 pound of powdered sugar, and 1 teaspoon of saffron powder
3. Pound everything together
4. Add eggs gradually until your mixture is soft
5. Butter a surface and arrange it on top
6. Give it an even thickness and let the dough bake in a gentle oven
7. Cut it with a knife or pastry cutter
8. When you remove it from the oven, give it whatever shape you wish
9. You can substitute pistachios for almonds if you prefer
