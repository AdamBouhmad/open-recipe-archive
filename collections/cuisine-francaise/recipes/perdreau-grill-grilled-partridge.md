## Ingredients

- partridge
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- breadcrumbs
- remoulade or tartar sauce
- clear jus or piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare your partridge as in the previous recipe; melt butter in a saucepan and dip your partridge in it so that it is covered with butter everywhere
2. Season it with salt and coarse pepper
3. Roll it in breadcrumbs so it is well breaded all over
4. Half an hour before serving, place your partridge on the grill over a gentle fire
5. When serving, place remoulade or tartar sauce on your plate and your partridge on top of it
6. If desired, you can serve clear jus underneath or a piquant sauce
