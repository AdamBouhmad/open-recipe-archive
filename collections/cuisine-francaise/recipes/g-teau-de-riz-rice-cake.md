## Ingredients

- a little more than a quarteron of well-washed rice
- water
- good milk
- one litron of flour
- salt
- four eggs
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Put in a small pot a little more than a quarteron of well-washed rice
2. Make it burst over the fire with a glass of water, and then add good milk until it is well cooked and thick
3. Let it cool
4. Make a paste with one litron of flour, salt, four eggs, half a pound of butter, and the rice
5. Knead everything together and form a cake
6. Glaze with beaten egg
7. Bake in the oven for one hour, or under a pie-dish cover, taking care to butter the paper that you put under the cake
