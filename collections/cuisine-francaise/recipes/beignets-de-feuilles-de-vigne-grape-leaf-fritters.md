## Ingredients

- grape leaves
- brandy
- frangipane cream
- flour
- white wine
- oil
- salt
- sugar
- red food coloring

## Directions

1. Soak grape leaves for one hour in brandy
2. Drain them, put a little frangipane cream inside and wrap them well
3. Dip them in a batter like the previous fritters and finish them the same way
