## Ingredients

- skate
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- garlic
- butter
- flour
- capers
- anchovy
- skate liver
- salt
- pepper
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Cook the skate as described on page 211
2. For the sauce, put parsley, chives, mushrooms, and a hint of garlic, all finely chopped, with a little butter in a saucepan
3. Pass several times over the fire
4. Add a good pinch of flour and a piece of butter
5. Add finely chopped capers and anchovy, the cooked and crushed liver of the skate, salt, and coarse pepper
6. Moisten with water or broth and bind over the fire
7. Serve over the skate
