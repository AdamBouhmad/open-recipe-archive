## Ingredients

- Veal tendons
- Fine peas (size of half a walnut)
- Eggs (3 yolks)
- Roux (if browning desired)
- Bouillon or water
- Parsley and chives
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare your tendons as in the blanc method
2. When the tendons are half cooked, add your fine peas, sized like half a walnut
3. At the moment of serving, add a liaison of three eggs
4. If you want your ragoût to be brown, make a roux and pass your tendons through it
5. When they are well coated, moisten with bouillon; if you have none, use water
6. Add a bunch of parsley and chives, a bay leaf, salt, and pepper
7. When your tendons are half cooked, add your peas, sized like half a walnut
8. When your ragoût is finished, make sure to degrease it
9. Check that it has a good flavor
