## Ingredients

- lamb neck
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- carrots
- small root vegetables
- lard
- bouillon or water
- flour for roux

## Directions

1. Season the lamb necks with salt, pepper, and onions
2. Cover them with lard strips
3. Braise them with bouillon or water
4. Add salt and bring to a boil
5. Cook on low heat for four hours
6. At serving time, drain them and arrange on a platter
7. Pour the small root vegetables over the top
8. If you do not have sauce for the root vegetables, make a light roux that you moisten with the braising liquid in which your lamb necks cooked
9. Prepare the small root vegetables as indicated
