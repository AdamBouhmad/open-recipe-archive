## Ingredients

- Five eggs
- Fine sugar (weight equal to five eggs)
- Flour (weight equal to sugar)
- Green lemon zest, finely chopped
- Pralined and chopped orange blossoms
- Fine sugar for glazing

## Directions

1. Place five eggs in a balance and weigh equal amount of fine sugar on the other side
2. Remove sugar and weigh equal amount of flour
3. Put sugar in a bowl with green lemon zest and pralined chopped orange blossoms
4. Add the five egg yolks and beat everything together until sugar is well mixed with yolks
5. Add flour and beat again together
6. Whip the egg whites that you have set apart until they form stiff peaks and mix with sugar and flour
7. Prepare two sheets of white paper folded lengthwise like a channel, the width and height of a finger
8. Put two spoonfuls of biscuit mixture in each channel
9. Sprinkle fine sugar over them
10. Bake in a gentle oven
11. When they are a beautiful color, remove them from paper and place on a sieve
12. Keep them in a dry place as long as you wish
