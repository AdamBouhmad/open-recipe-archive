## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter
- Choice of: bacon, veal kidney, asparagus tips, truffles, mushrooms, morels, or other mushrooms

## Directions

1. Your filling must always be cooked and seasoned as if you were serving it on its own
2. When it is cold, chop it so it mixes well with the eggs
3. Beat everything together
4. Make this omelette in a frying pan as you would the others
5. Adjust the seasoning based on what is in the filling so as not to oversalt the omelette
