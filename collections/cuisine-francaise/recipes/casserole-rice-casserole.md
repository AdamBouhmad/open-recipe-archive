## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- bouillon
- melted bacon
- any cooked meats of your choice

## Directions

1. Cook the rice three-quarters done in a small pot with bouillon and melted bacon
2. When it is nearly cooked, thick, and very fatty, spread a layer about two coins thick in the bottom of your serving dish, which should be silver or ovenproof earthenware
3. Place your chosen cooked meat or meats on top of the rice
4. Cover completely with rice so the meat is not visible
5. Smooth with a knife
6. Place your dish on hot ashes, cover with a pie dish cover and a good fire on top
7. Leave until the rice is a beautiful golden color
8. When serving, drain off any fat and serve dry, or if you wish, add a little sauce in the bottom
