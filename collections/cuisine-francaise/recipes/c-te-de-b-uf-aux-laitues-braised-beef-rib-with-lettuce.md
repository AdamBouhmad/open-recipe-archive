## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Lettuce
- Carrots
- Onions
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Cloves
- Parsley bouquet
- Chives
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Trim and cook your beef rib as you would a braised one; drain it, glaze it, and place it on the plate with glazed lettuces around it; sauce it with a reduced Spanish sauce
2. If you do not have the means to mark a braise, cook your beef rib with carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, cloves, bouquet of parsley and chives, a little bouillon
3. If you use only water, add salt and black pepper
4. Take the stock from your beef rib to cook your lettuces that you will have prepared; blanch them and refresh them, and press them to remove the water
5. Then put them in a casserole with the liquid in which your beef rib has cooked; an hour is enough to cook your lettuces
