## Ingredients

- One hen (poularde), roasted on the spit
- Meat from the breast
- One handful of bread crumbs
- One pint of milk
- Nearly one half-pound of beef fat
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop very fine the meat from the breast of a hen that has been roasted on the spit and removed from the table
2. Mix it with a good handful of bread crumbs boiled on the fire with one pint of milk reduced until it is thick and cooled
3. Add nearly one half-pound of beef fat, parsley, chives, mushrooms, all chopped, salt, pepper, and five egg yolks
4. Put this forcemeat in the body of the hen and in place of the breast
5. Smooth the top with a knife dipped in beaten egg
6. Coat with bread crumbs
7. Put the hen in a baking dish on lard strips
8. Cover with paper and cook under a covered baking dish lid
9. Serve with a piquant sauce
