## Ingredients

- Soissons beans
- Roux
- Jus
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. The Soissons beans are the best; cook them and drain them.
2. Make a light roux moistened with jus, a little salt, and black pepper.
3. Sauté your beans in the sauce.
4. Serve them very hot. You may fricassee them in the same manner as lentils.
