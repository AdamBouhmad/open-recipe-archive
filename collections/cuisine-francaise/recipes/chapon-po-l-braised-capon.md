## Ingredients

- Capon
- Lard
- Lemon slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. When your capon is plucked and lightly singed, pluck it further and empty it
2. Lay the legs on the thighs and truss it with needle and string, passing the string across to join the other thigh, securing the legs and breast
3. Place lard strips in a saucepan with the capon on top
4. Cover it with very thin slices of lemon and cover with lard strips and a braising lid on top
5. A short hour is sufficient to cook it
6. If you do not have a braising lid, add some carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, and a bouquet of parsley, and moisten with bouillon
7. Serve under your capon with consommé, clear jus, hot aspic, crayfish butter, tomato sauce, suprême, bound velouté, sautéed mushrooms, or a ragout of crests and kidneys, as you prefer
