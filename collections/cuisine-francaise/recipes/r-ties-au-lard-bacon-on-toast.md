## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- salt pork, cut into small dice
- one raw egg
- parsley
- chives
- one shallot
- coarse pepper
- vinegar sauce

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread the width of two fingers and of equal size
2. Put on them enough salt pork cut into small dice and worked with a raw egg, parsley, chives, one shallot, all chopped, coarse pepper
3. Fry them over a small fire
4. Serve with a clear sauce and a splash of vinegar
