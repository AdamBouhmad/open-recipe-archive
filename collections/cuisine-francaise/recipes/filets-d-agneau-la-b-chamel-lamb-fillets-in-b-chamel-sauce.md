## Ingredients

- cream
- lamb fillets

## Directions

1. Make the béchamel by reducing cream until it is thick enough to make a sauce
2. When it begins to thicken, stir it constantly so it does not become lumpy
3. When ready to serve, put in the fillets cut as described for the blanquette
4. Heat them without boiling, season them to good taste and serve
