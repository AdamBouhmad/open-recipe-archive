## Ingredients

- large carp
- vinegar
- court-bouillon

## Directions

1. Gut the carp and remove the gills, do not scale it
2. Place it on a large platter
3. Boil vinegar and pour it boiling over the carp, which will make it turn blue
4. Cook it also in court-bouillon
5. When it is cooked, serve it on a napkin garnished with fresh parsley as a lean main course dish
