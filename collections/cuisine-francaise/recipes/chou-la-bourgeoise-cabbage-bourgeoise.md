## Ingredients

- One whole cabbage
- Farce (forcemeat)
- Water
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Good coulis

## Directions

1. Wash the cabbage and boil it for a quarter hour in water
2. Remove it to fresh water and let it cool
3. Press it firmly without breaking the leaves
4. Remove the leaves one by one and put a little farce on each
5. Reassemble the leaves as if the cabbage were whole and tie it up all around
6. Cook it in a braise made as for beef tongue, well seasoned
7. When cooked, gently press it in a white cloth to remove the fat
8. Cut it in half and arrange it on the serving plate
9. Cover with good coulis
