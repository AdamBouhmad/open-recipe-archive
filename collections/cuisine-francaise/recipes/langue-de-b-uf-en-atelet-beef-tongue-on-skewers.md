## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- Italian sauce
- two or three eggs
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. When your tongue is cooked as described for cornichons, allow it to cool
2. Cut it into small thin squares
3. Place the pieces on a plate
4. Reduce Italian sauce until well reduced
5. When well reduced, add a short liaison of two or three eggs
6. Stir carefully so the sauce does not break
7. When the sauce is bound, pour it over the small squares of tongue and allow the sauce to cool
8. Ensure the tongue is well covered with sauce
9. Skewer each small piece with an atelet, ensuring the pieces are thoroughly coated with sauce
10. Make sure all pieces are the same size so the atelet presents a long square with four equal angles
11. Fill any gaps with sauce, smoothing the atelet
12. Dip it in warm butter
13. Roll it in breadcrumbs, then in beaten eggs mixed with a little butter, salt and coarse pepper
14. Bread it while maintaining its square shape
15. Grill it on three sides, browning the fourth side with a camp oven or ice shovel
16. If you lack Italian sauce, use hachée sauce instead
