## Ingredients

- flour
- egg yolks
- eggs
- salt
- bouillon
- nutmeg
- good bouillon

## Directions

1. Make the same batter as for Xavier soup, except without parsley
2. Make it thick enough so that when you put three quarters of a soup spoon of it, you can make the dough flow with your finger, and when it falls into your boiling bouillon, it forms a round, long or oval ball
3. Let your soup cook for half an hour
4. Take care to degrease it and ensure it has proper seasoning
5. It can be made without meat
