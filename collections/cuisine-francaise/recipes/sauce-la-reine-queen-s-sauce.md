## Ingredients

- Butter
- Several mushrooms
- One onion
- One carrot
- One parsnip
- Half a clove of garlic
- Parsley, chives
- A pinch of flour
- One large glass of broth
- As much white wine
- Half a setier of milk
- Bread crumb, about half the size of an egg
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter in a saucepan with several mushrooms, one onion, one carrot, one parsnip, half a clove of garlic, parsley, chives
2. Pass everything over the fire and add a pinch of flour
3. Moisten with one large glass of broth and as much white wine
4. Boil for one hour, skim and pass through a sieve
5. Boil half a setier of milk with bread crumb about half the size of an egg
6. When the bread has absorbed all the milk, pass it through a sieve by pressing hard with a spoon
7. Put it into your sauce with salt and coarse pepper
