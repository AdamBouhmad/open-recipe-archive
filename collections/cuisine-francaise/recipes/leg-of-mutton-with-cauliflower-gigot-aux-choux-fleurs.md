## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- cauliflowers
- butter
- salt
- coulis
- coarse pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the leg in the same manner as the English style
2. Once dressed on the serving platter, arrange cauliflowers all around that you have blanched for a moment in boiling water, then placed in another boiling water to cook them with a piece of butter and salt
3. When the cauliflowers are cooked and well drained, arrange them neatly around the leg, flower side up
4. Cover with a good sauce made with ordinary coulis, a piece of butter, salt, and coarse pepper
5. Thicken on the fire
6. When serving, add a small splash of vinegar
