## Ingredients

- Fish
- White wine
- Small onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Mushrooms
- Egg yolks
- Bread crusts
- Crayfish

## Directions

1. Cook the fish with white wine as in the previous recipe
2. Sauté small onions to white in butter
3. Mix in two spoonfuls of flour with the butter and onions
4. Pour the wine in which the fish cooked, passed through a sieve, over the onions
5. Stir the sauce until it boils
6. Add mushrooms and reduce by a third
7. Decrease the sauce and check the taste
8. Remove the onions and mushrooms with a slotted spoon and place on the fish
9. If the sauce is too thin, reduce it
10. Add a liaison of five egg yolks, or more if the stew is strong
11. Do not boil the sauce
12. When the liaison is in, strain it through cheesecloth over the fish
13. At the moment of serving, arrange on a plat with bread crusts sautéed in butter around it
14. Garnish the stew with crayfish
