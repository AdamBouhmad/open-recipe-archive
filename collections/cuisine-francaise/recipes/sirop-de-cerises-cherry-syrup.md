## Ingredients

- two pounds of beautiful ripe and healthy cherries
- one large glass of water
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and pits from the cherries
2. Put them on the fire with one large glass of water
3. Let them boil eight or ten times and pass through a sieve
4. Put two pounds of sugar on the fire with one glass of water
5. Let it boil and skim well
6. Continue boiling until when dipping the skimming spoon into it and shaking it over the sugar, and blowing through the holes, sparks of sugar come out
7. Immediately add all the cherry juice
8. Let them boil together until they have taken on the consistency of a strong syrup
