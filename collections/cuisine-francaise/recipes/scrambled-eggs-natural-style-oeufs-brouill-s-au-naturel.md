## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Cooking juice (coulis)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Put eggs in a pan with a little butter and two spoonfuls of cooking juice.
2. Season to taste.
3. Cook over heat, stirring constantly with a small stick with two or three branches.
4. Serve immediately when cooked.
