## Ingredients

- Eggs, quantity as desired
- Fine salt
- Butter
- Parsley and chives, optional, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Take the quantity of eggs you wish to use and place them in a saucepan with fine salt
2. Beat the eggs well
3. Melt butter in a frying pan
4. Put the eggs into the pan and cook the omelette until it has a nice color on the bottom
5. Flip it into the serving dish
6. Those who like parsley and chives may add them finely chopped
