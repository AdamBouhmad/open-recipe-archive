## Ingredients

- butter
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Prepare butter and flour as for the blonde roux
2. Place on a stovetop that is not too strong
3. Stir constantly without leaving it until it is very hot
4. Do not let it take on any color; the whiter it is, the better
5. Use only wheat flour, as rye flour is not good for thickening
