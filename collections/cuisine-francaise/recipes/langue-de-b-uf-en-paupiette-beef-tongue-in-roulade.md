## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- water
- broth matching the piece of beef
- thin slices of veal or other meat farce
- thickness of a small coin
- egg
- small strips of lard for barding
- breadcrumbs
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the core from a beef tongue and blanch it for half a quarter hour in salted water
2. Put it afterwards to cook in the broth matching the beef piece, until the skin can be removed
3. Remove the skin and let it cool
4. Cut it into thin slices across its entire width and length
5. Cover each slice with a farce of veal or other meat farce, the thickness of a small coin
6. Pass a knife dipped in egg over the farce
7. Roll them up and thread them onto a skewer, after placing on each a small strip of lard
8. Cook them on the spit
9. When they are almost cooked, throw breadcrumbs on the lards, give them a golden color over clear fire
10. Serve with a piquant sauce underneath
