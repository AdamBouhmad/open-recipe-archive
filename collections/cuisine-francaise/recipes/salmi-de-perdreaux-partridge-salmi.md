## Ingredients

- 4 partridges
- 6 shallots
- 1 handful of parsley leaves
- 1 bay leaf
- coarse pepper
- salt
- 1 glass of white wine
- 2 glasses of bouillon
- 6 tablespoons of Spanish sauce (or roux and partridge scraps if Spanish sauce unavailable)
- 1 whole lemon

## Directions

1. Spit-roast four partridges and let them cool if you have time
2. Carefully remove the limbs; crush the scraps with a knife
3. Place in a saucepan 6 tablespoons of Spanish sauce, the partridge scraps, 6 shallots, 1 glass of white wine, a pinch of parsley leaves, 1 bay leaf, a good pinch of coarse pepper, very little salt, and 1 glass of bouillon
4. Bring the sauce to a high heat; when reduced to less than half, strain through fine cloth over the partridge limbs
5. Keep the limbs hot in their sauce without boiling
6. At service, dress the partridge limbs with croutons dipped in the sauce
7. If you have no Spanish sauce, make a light roux, strain the scraps through it, add shallots and parsley, pour in 1 glass of white wine and 2 glasses of bouillon with salt, coarse pepper, and 1 bay leaf; reduce the sauce to half and strain through fine cloth over the limbs
8. At service, add the juice of one whole lemon and check the seasoning
