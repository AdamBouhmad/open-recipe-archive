## Ingredients

- milk
- vermicelli
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. When your milk boils, you put your vermicelli in it; be very careful to separate your vermicelli; when it is in your milk, you must stir it from time to time, so that it does not turn into paste; that your soup be of good sugar: half an hour suffices for your vermicelli to burst.
