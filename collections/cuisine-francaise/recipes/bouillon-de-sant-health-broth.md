## Ingredients

- Three pounds of sliced beef
- Two pounds of calf knuckle
- One hen
- Four pints of water
- Five carrots
- Three turnips
- Four large onions
- Three cloves
- One white lettuce with a pinch of chervil

## Directions

1. In an earthenware pot, put three pounds of sliced beef, two pounds of calf knuckle, one hen, four pints of water, five carrots, three turnips, four large onions, three cloves, one white lettuce in which you put a pinch of chervil
2. Let everything simmer gently until the meats are cooked
3. Pass the broth through a silk sieve
4. You can make with this broth all sorts of excellent soups and potages
