## Ingredients

- pork tenderloins
- fine lard
- lard strips
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- parsley
- chives
- bay leaves
- bouillon
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove your pork tenderloins along their entire length
2. Trim them and lard them with fine lard
3. Leave them lengthwise, or form them into gimblettes, that is to say give them a round shape, and lard them on top
4. Put lard strips in a casserole, a few slices of veal, two carrots, three onions, two cloves, a bouquet of parsley and chives, two bay leaves, and your tenderloins on top of the seasoning
5. Cover them then with a double round of buttered paper
6. Put a small spoonfuls of bouillon
7. Place them on the fire one hour before serving
8. Put fire on the lid to make them glaze
9. At the moment of eating, drain them and glaze them
10. You can put underneath chicory, cucumbers, a mushroom purée, a tomato sauce or a piquant sauce
