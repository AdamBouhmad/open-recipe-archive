## Ingredients

- One hen (poularde)
- Large lard strips, well interlarded
- Good broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bouquet garni
- Butter kneaded with flour
- Milk
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Singe it, gut it, truss the legs into the body and lard it crosswise with large lard strips well interlarded
2. Cook it with a little good broth, salt, pepper, and a bouquet
3. When cooked, let all the sauce attach around it and let it cool
4. In a saucepan, put a good piece of butter kneaded with half a spoonful of flour, moisten with a little milk, salt, and pepper
5. Thicken this sauce so it is well thick
6. Spread it all over the hen and add bread crumbs gradually until it forms a crust
7. Brown it under a covered baking dish lid
8. Serve with a piquant sauce
