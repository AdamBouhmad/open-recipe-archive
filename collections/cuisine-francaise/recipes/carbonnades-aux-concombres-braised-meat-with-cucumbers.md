## Ingredients

- saddle of meat cut into pieces
- lard strips
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- buttered paper
- bouillon
- cucumbers à la crème
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. From the saddle, cut pieces four fingers long
2. Remove the bones found under the fillet
3. Season the underside with salt and coarse pepper
4. Tie it so it has a square-long shape
5. Put lard strips in the bottom of your saucepan with a few veal slices
6. Place your carbonnades on top
7. Add two carrots, two onions, a bay leaf, a little thyme
8. Cover them with lard strips and a round of buttered paper
9. Fill a small spoon with bouillon
10. Simmer them for two hours
11. At serving time, drain them, remove the first skin from the top, glaze them, and put cucumbers à la crème on the platter with your carbonnades on top
