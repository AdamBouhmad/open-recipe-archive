## Ingredients

- Lamb
- Skewers
- String
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb at two cutlets near the saddle; try to make this end cut very square
2. Secure the flanks with skewers
3. Give a beautiful form to the saddle
4. Remove the thick muscle bones from both legs; beat them well
5. Put your beef roast on the spit
6. Hold it with skewers and string that you pass through the ribs and tie to the spit
7. Put clear jus underneath as a relief course
