## Ingredients

- Previously cooked pears from an earlier compote
- Five tablespoons of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Drain the cooked pears well on white linen
2. Place five tablespoons of powdered sugar in the bottom of a pan or skillet
3. Arrange the pears on top
4. Place on the fire
5. When the bottom of the pan takes on a somewhat dark color, remove from heat
6. Place the pears on a serving dish with syrup underneath
