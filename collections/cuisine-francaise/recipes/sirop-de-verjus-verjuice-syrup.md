## Ingredients

- two pounds of brown sugar
- one half-setier of water
- two pounds of green and large verjuice

## Directions

1. Put two pounds of brown sugar on the fire with a half-setier of water
2. Let boil and skim
3. Continue boiling until dipping the skimming spoon in the brown sugar, shaking it over, you blow through the holes, sugar that flies off in sparks should come out
4. Prepare the verjuice as follows: take two pounds of green and large verjuice, remove the stems and pound them, express the juice by passing it through a fine sieve, let it rest and draw it off clear
5. Add it to the brown sugar and let them boil together until it is reduced to a strong syrup, which you will know when a thread forms in your fingers like the previous one
