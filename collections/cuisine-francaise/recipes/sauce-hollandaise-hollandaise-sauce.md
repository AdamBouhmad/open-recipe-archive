## Ingredients

- reduced velouté
- coarse pepper
- a dash of tarragon vinegar
- butter the size of half an egg
- a little spinach juice

## Directions

1. Take reduced velouté in which you will put coarse pepper and a dash of tarragon vinegar
2. Keep your sauce warm
3. At the moment of serving, add butter the size of half an egg, which you will melt in your hot sauce
4. Then take a little spinach juice, which you will dilute in your sauce at the moment of serving
