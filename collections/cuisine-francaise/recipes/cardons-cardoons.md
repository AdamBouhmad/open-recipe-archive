## Ingredients

- tender and white cardoon ribs
- water
- salt
- blanc

## Directions

1. Take the most tender and whitest ribs of the cardoons
2. They must be full and firm inside when cut
3. All ribs should be the same length
4. Cut the edges and blanch in plenty of water
5. Test from time to time to ensure the slime is coming off
6. When it comes off easily, remove from the boiling water and add cold water so you can use your hands
7. Detach the slime and place the cardoons in fresh water
8. Trim again and drain
9. Prepare a blanc and cook them three-quarters of the way
