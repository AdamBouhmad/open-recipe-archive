## Ingredients

- one brill
- salt water or court-bouillon
- butter sauce or plain water

## Directions

1. Remove the gills and entrails from the brill
2. Wash it
3. Cook it in salted water or court-bouillon for an entrée
4. Coat it with a butter sauce or serve it in plain water
