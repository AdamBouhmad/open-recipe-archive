## Ingredients

- One shallot
- Parsley
- Chives
- A point of garlic
- One anchovy
- Capers
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A little mustard
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Put one shallot, parsley, chives, a point of garlic, one anchovy and capers in a saucepan, all finely chopped, with salt and coarse pepper
2. Dilute with a little mustard, oil and vinegar
