## Ingredients

- Five or six onions
- A little thyme
- A little bay leaf
- Twelve to fifteen tomatoes or love apples
- Bouillon or butter
- Salt
- Five or six cloves of hot small pepper
- A little powder of saffron from India or turmeric
- A glass of bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut five or six onions and put them in a saucepan
2. Add a little thyme and bay leaf
3. Add twelve to fifteen tomatoes
4. Add bouillon from the back of the pot, or a good piece of butter
5. Add salt, hot pepper cloves, turmeric powder, and a glass of bouillon
6. Put your tomatoes on the fire
7. Take care to stir from time to time, because this sauce is susceptible to burning
8. When you see that what is in your saucepan becomes a little thick, pass this sauce through muslin like a purée
9. Do not make it too thin
