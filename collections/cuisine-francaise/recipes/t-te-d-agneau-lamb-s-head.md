## Ingredients

- lamb head
- lard for barding
- blanc (white cooking liquid)

## Directions

1. Debone your lamb head up to the eye
2. Remove the lower jaw
3. Cut it up to the eye
4. Let it disgorge and blanch for a good quarter hour
5. Refresh it
6. Dry it
7. Singe it
8. Cover it with a bard of lard
9. Put it to cook in a blanc
10. Two hours suffice for it to be cooked
11. At the moment of serving, drain it and remove the strings
12. Place it on your platter
13. It is eaten plain, or with a mixed ragout, or a pasqualine composed of the liver, lights, feet, rice, and mushrooms, all in blanc
