## Ingredients

- white chicory
- butter
- bouillon
- coulis
- flour for roux
- salt
- pepper
- shallot

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the chicory
2. Boil it for half an hour in water
3. Remove it and place in fresh water to press well
4. Put it to cook with a little butter, bouillon and coulis, or if you don't have coulis, make a small roux of flour to bind the sauce
5. When cooked, season with good salt and remove the fat, add a little shallot for those who like it, and serve under roasted mutton, either shoulder, loin or leg
6. If you wish to serve it white in the lean way, in place of a flour roux, put a liaison of egg yolks and cream, serve it under soft-boiled eggs
