## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- water
- sugar
- paper

## Directions

1. Take fifteen or eighteen onions, all of the same size
2. Peel them carefully, being careful not to cut the head and tail too much
3. Butter the bottom of a casserole
4. Add the onions head-side down with salt, a little coarse pepper, one glass of water, butter the size of half an egg, and a piece of buttered paper over the onions
