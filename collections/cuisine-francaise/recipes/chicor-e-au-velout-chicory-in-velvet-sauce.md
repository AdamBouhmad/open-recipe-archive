## Ingredients

- chicory
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- velouté sauce
- consommé
- croutons

## Directions

1. Remove the outer part of the chicory so that only the white remains
2. Cut the head and place the chicory split in two in water to wash
3. Have a pot of boiling water with a handful of salt; place the chicory in it
4. Push it down in the water from time to time so it does not blacken
5. When you see it mixes with the water, it is sufficiently blanched
6. Test with your fingers; if it is soft, drain it in a colander and place it in fresh water
7. When well cooled, drain it again and squeeze it in your hands to extract the water
8. Then chop it
9. Put a good piece of butter in a pot with the chicory, a little salt, and coarse pepper
10. Stir it often and pour five tablespoons of velouté sauce and the same of consommé over it
11. Let it reduce until somewhat thick
12. Then arrange it on the platter with croutons around it
