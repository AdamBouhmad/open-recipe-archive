## Ingredients

- crayfish
- cream sauce
- nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Remove the small legs from your crayfish and the shell from the tail
2. Cut the tip of the head and that of the large legs
3. Make a cream sauce in which you will grate a little nutmeg
4. Pour it over your crayfish and let them simmer a little
