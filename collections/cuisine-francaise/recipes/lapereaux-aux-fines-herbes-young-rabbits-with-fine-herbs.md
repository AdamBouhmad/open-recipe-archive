## Ingredients

- Young rabbits
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- One clove of garlic
- A piece of butter
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Basil
- A pinch of flour
- A glass of white wine
- A little broth and jus
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbits into pieces and place them in a pot with parsley, chives, mushrooms, a clove of garlic (all chopped), a piece of butter, thyme, bay leaf, and basil (powdered)
2. Pass them over the fire
3. Add a pinch of flour
4. Moisten with a glass of white wine, a little jus and broth
5. Season with salt and coarse pepper
6. Cook and reduce to the consistency of a sauce
7. When ready to serve, take the livers that have cooked with the fricassee, crush them, and add them to the sauce.
