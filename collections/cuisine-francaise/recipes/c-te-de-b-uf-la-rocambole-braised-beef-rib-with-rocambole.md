## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Garlic
- White wine
- Rocamboles
- Light roux and stock
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare your beef rib as for braising; add a little garlic; add a half-bottle of white wine to the liquid
2. When it is cooked, drain it, glaze it, and place it on your rocamboles
3. If you do not have sauce, make a light roux and moisten it with the stock from your beef rib that you have passed through fine cloth; add plenty of liquid with your roux so it can reduce and develop flavor; it must be very thick
4. Peel a glassful of rocamboles, blanch them until they crush when pressed under your fingers; they must remain somewhat firm
5. Put them in your sauce that you will keep warm in a bain-marie; add a little black pepper
