## Ingredients

- three sheep's tongues
- good bouillon
- two spoonfuls of coulis
- breadcrumbs
- one glass of white wine
- parsley
- chives
- one clove of garlic
- mushrooms
- a small piece of butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch three sheep's tongues; after having them cooked in water, split them in half without separating them
2. Put them in a saucepan with good bouillon and two spoonfuls of coulis; if you have no coulis, put about two spoonfuls of breadcrumbs in a little bouillon, bring to boil briefly and pass through a sieve pressing with a spoon
3. Add one glass of white wine, parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, mushrooms, all chopped very fine, a small piece of butter, salt, and pepper
4. Bring to boil for half an hour until the sauce is neither too thick nor too thin
5. Serve
