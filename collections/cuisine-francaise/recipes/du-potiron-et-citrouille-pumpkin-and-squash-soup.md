## Ingredients

- pumpkin or squash
- water
- milk
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook the pumpkin or squash with water
2. When it is cooked and there is no longer much water, add milk, a piece of butter, salt and sugar if you wish
3. Soak bread in it and do not let it simmer at all
