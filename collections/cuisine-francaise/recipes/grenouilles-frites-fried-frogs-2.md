## Ingredients

- Frogs
- Equal parts water and vinegar
- Parsley
- Ciboule (whole)
- Onion slices
- Two garlic cloves
- Two shallots
- Three cloves
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Flour
- Oil
- White wine

## Directions

1. Place the raw frogs in a marinade of equal parts water and vinegar with parsley, whole ciboule, onion slices, two garlic cloves, two shallots, three cloves, bay leaf, thyme, and basil for one hour
2. Remove the frogs from the marinade and drain them
3. Coat them with flour and fry them, or alternatively dip them in a batter made with flour mixed with a spoonful of oil and a large glass of white wine, ensuring the batter is not too thin and flows thickly when poured with a spoon
4. Serve garnished with fried parsley
