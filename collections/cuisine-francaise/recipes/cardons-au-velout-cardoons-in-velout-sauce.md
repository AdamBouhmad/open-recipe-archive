## Ingredients

- cardoons
- blanc
- coarse pepper
- velouté
- consommé
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- bay leaf
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the cardoons in blanc as described in the first article
2. Drain and trim them, ensuring they are all the same size
3. Place them in a casserole with a little coarse pepper, 5 spoonfuls of velouté, and twice as much consommé
4. Cook on high heat
5. When the sauce is reduced to more than half, arrange the cardoons on the plate and pour the sauce over
6. Alternatively, simply blanch the cardoons, remove the slime, trim, and place in a casserole
7. In another vessel, make a white roux with a quarter pound of butter and a spoonful of flour
8. Moisten with bouillon, keeping the sauce thin
9. Pour it over the cardoons
10. Add a little coarse pepper, a bay leaf, and the juice of a lemon
11. Cook them in their sauce
