## Ingredients

- twelve lamb ears
- blanc (white cooking liquid)
- farce cuite (cooked forcemeat)
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- four eggs
- salt
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Let the twelve lamb ears disgorge and blanch
2. Refresh them
3. Dry them
4. Singe them
5. Cook them in a small blanc for an hour and a half
6. Drain them
7. Fill them with cooked forcemeat
8. Dip them in tepid butter, then in breadcrumbs
9. Break four eggs into the rest of your butter
10. Add salt and coarse pepper
11. Beat it all together
12. Dip your ears in it
13. Coat them with egg all over
14. Put them in breadcrumbs so they are completely masked by breadcrumbs
15. Put them on a board
16. At the moment of serving, put your frying fat on the fire
17. When it is hot, put your ears in it
18. Be careful that your frying fat is not too hot
19. When they have a nice colour, remove them and drain them on white linen
20. Fry some parsley
21. Dress your ears and put it on top
