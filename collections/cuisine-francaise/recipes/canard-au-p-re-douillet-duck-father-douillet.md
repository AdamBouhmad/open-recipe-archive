## Ingredients

- one duck
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- one clove of garlic
- two cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- coriander
- onion slices
- one carrot
- one parsnip
- butter
- two glasses of bouillon
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Singe and clean the duck well; empty it and truss the legs in a pan just to its size.
2. Add a bouquet of parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, a good pinch of coriander, onion slices, one carrot, one parsnip, a piece of butter, two glasses of bouillon, and one glass of white wine.
3. Cook over low heat; when the duck yields under your finger, pass the sauce through a sieve and degrease it.
4. Reduce the sauce over the fire to the consistency of a sauce.
5. Serve under the duck.
6. You can also serve it the same way by cutting it into four pieces before cooking.
