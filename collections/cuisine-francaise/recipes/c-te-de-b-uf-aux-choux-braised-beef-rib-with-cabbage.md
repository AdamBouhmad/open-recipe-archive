## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Lard
- Carrots
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Cloves
- Cabbage
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare your beef rib and cook it as you would a braised one; drain it, glaze it, and arrange cabbage (see Cabbage and Spanish sauce) with small lard around it; sauce it with a reduced Spanish sauce
2. Otherwise, trim your beef rib with thick lard, tie it, put it in a casserole with four carrots, four onions, one of which is studded with three cloves, two bay leaves, a little thyme
3. Blanch one or two cabbages by quarters, refresh them, tie them, and cook them with your beef rib
4. Blanch your small lard for a quarter hour and put it in the bottom with your beef rib and cabbage on top
5. If you moisten with water, add salt and pepper
6. After that, simmer everything for two hours; it must boil constantly
7. Arrange the cabbage and lard around the beef rib on the plate
8. If you do not have sauce, take a little of the cooking liquid and pour it over your beef rib; take care not to oversalt because of the lard, and that the cabbage is not bitter
