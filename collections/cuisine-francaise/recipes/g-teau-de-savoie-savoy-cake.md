## Ingredients

- fourteen eggs
- fine sugar in weight equal to fourteen eggs
- flour in weight equal to seven eggs
- grated green lemon zest
- grilled and chopped orange flower
- refined butter

## Directions

1. Weigh fourteen eggs on a balance
2. On the other side put fine sugar equal in weight
3. Remove the sugar and put flour in its place, weighing the amount of seven eggs
4. Set the flour aside
5. Break the eggs, putting the yolks in one bowl and the whites in another
6. With the yolks put the sugar you have weighed, a little grated green lemon zest, and grilled and chopped orange flower
7. Beat everything together for half an hour
8. Then add the well-beaten egg whites and the flour you have weighed, adding it gently while stirring the batter with a whisk
9. Get a medium-sized and deep saucepan or cake mold that you first rub with refined butter, wipe it well with a cloth and coat with refined butter so it spreads everywhere
10. Put your batter in and bake in the oven with moderate heat for a good hour and a half
11. When cooked, turn it out gently onto a platter
12. If it is a beautiful golden color, serve it natural
13. If it has too much color, glaze it with white icing made with very fine sugar, one egg white, and the juice of half a lemon
14. Beat everything together in a faience dish with a wooden spoon until the icing is very white
15. Use it to cover the whole cake and serve only when the icing is dry
