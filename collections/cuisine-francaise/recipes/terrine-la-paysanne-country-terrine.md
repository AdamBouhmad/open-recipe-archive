## Ingredients

- Beef brisket, sliced thin
- Lean bacon, sliced thin
- Parsley, minced
- Chives, minced
- Fine spices
- Bay leaf
- Eau-de-vie
- Water

## Directions

1. Make a layer of beef in a terrine, then a layer of bacon, then a bit of seasoning
2. Repeat layers
3. At the end, add a spoonful of eau-de-vie and two spoonfuls of water
4. Cook over hot ashes like beef à la mode, having well sealed the terrine
5. When cooked, decrease if you judge it appropriate, and serve in the terrine
