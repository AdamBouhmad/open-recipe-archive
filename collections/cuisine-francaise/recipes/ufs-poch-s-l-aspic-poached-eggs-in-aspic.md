## Ingredients

- Aspic
- Poached eggs
- Truffles

## Directions

1. Warm the aspic slightly
2. Pour some into the bottom of a mold or several small molds
3. Let it congeal
4. Decorate the poached eggs with truffles
5. Place them on the jelly
6. Fill the molds with melted aspic
7. Place on ice or in the cold until serving
8. At the moment of serving, detach the aspic and place it on a plate
