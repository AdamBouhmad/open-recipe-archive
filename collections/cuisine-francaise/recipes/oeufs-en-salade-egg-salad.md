## Ingredients

- lettuce
- hard-boiled eggs
- salad greens
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop a little lettuce and place it in the bottom of a plate
2. Arrange hard-boiled eggs cut in half symmetrically on top
3. Surround with salad greens
4. Season with oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper
