## Ingredients

- 10 small pots of cream
- 1 quarteron of sugar
- 1 quarteron of pistachios
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon of spinach juice

## Directions

1. Fill 10 small pots with cream and pour into a casserole
2. Bring the cream to a boil
3. Add 1 quarteron of sugar
4. Blanch 1 quarteron of pistachios and pound them finely
5. Mix the pistachios with the boiling cream and bring to a boil
6. Allow to cool
7. In a casserole, beat 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks and mix with your preparation
8. Add 1 teaspoon of spinach juice
9. Pass five or six times through cheesecloth
10. When the water boils, fill the small pots and place them in a bain-marie
