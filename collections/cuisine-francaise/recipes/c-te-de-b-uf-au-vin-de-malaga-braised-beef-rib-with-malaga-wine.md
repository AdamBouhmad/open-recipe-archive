## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Larding fat
- Malaga wine
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Prepare your beef rib as for braising; season your larding fat a little more; add a half-bottle of Malaga wine and the equivalent of a half-bottle of bouillon
2. Cook your beef rib
3. After that, pass the liquid through fine cloth; take care that there is no fat
4. Reduce all your liquid so that only a glassful remains to place under your beef rib
5. Take care not to salt your cooking much, so that your reduced sauce is not bitter
