## Ingredients

- Flounder
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Breadcrumbs
- White wine or water

## Directions

1. Empty and clean your flounder
2. Melt a piece of butter on your platter
3. Add a little parsley and chives, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Arrange your flounder on your platter
5. Add seasoning
6. Drizzle with a glass of white wine or water
7. Cover with breadcrumbs
8. Place on the stove with a camp oven over it
