## Ingredients

- one whole lamb loin
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- a pinch of garlic
- melted lard
- salt
- coarse pepper
- coulis

## Directions

1. Trim the lamb loin of all sinews and cut it thin
2. Place it in a casserole, layer by layer, with chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, and a pinch of garlic, melted lard, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Cook it braised over very low heat
4. When cooked, remove the fat and detach the fillets, add a little coulis to the sauce and serve
