## Ingredients

- Three very fresh eggs
- Flour
- Chopped green citron
- Orange flower water
- Fine sugar
- A little more than one half-setier of cream
- Butter or candle wax for the waffle iron

## Directions

1. Take three of the freshest eggs you can find and dilute them with as much flour as they can absorb, chopped green citron, orange flower water, and fine sugar
2. Dilute all together and then add a little more than one half-setier of cream, until the batter is somewhat liquid
3. When your batter is ready, heat the waffle iron over a fire and rub it inside with candle wax to prevent the waffles from sticking
4. When your waffle iron is hot, put a full spoonful of this batter in it, that is enough
5. Close your waffle iron and return it to the fire
6. When it is cooked on one side, turn it over to the other side and remove it then to place it on a wooden roller
7. Bend them all while hot, and when they are all made, put them in a dry place until you serve them
8. You can keep them for several days if you keep them in a drying room where they will not soften
