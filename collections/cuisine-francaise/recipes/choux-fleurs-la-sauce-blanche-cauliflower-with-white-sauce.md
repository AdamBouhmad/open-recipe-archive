## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- White sauce

## Directions

1. Peel the cauliflowers.
2. Put a saucepan on the fire with water, a little salt, and a piece of butter the size of half an egg.
3. When the water boils, put in the cauliflowers.
4. After about a quarter hour, remove them if cooked; otherwise leave them longer; they should be removed from the fire when somewhat firm.
5. At the moment of serving, drain them and dress them on a plate or in a saucepan; turn them onto a plate and mask them with a white or brown butter sauce (see White Sauce).
