## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives, chopped
- Flour, a pinch
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut cucumbers into small pieces the size of a finger
2. Pass them through the fire with butter, parsley, and chopped chives
3. Add a pinch of flour
4. Moisten with a little water seasoned with salt and pepper
5. When they are cooked and there is no more sauce, add sliced hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
6. Add milk and bring to a gentle boil
7. Taste to ensure good flavor and serve
