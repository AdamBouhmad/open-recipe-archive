## Ingredients

- Apples
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Extract the juice from the apples by boiling them in a little water
2. Pass them afterwards through a cloth and press them a little
3. Use this juice to mix with your sugar
4. Cook in the same manner as currant jelly
5. You will know when it is cooked by putting your skimming spoon into your pan and withdrawing it, and if when you lift it and hold it slightly tilted, your jelly falls in drops, this shows it is sufficiently cooked
6. Put it immediately into pots
