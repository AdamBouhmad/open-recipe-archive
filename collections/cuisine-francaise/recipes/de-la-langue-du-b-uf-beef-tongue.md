## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet garni of parsley, chives, thyme, bay leaf, basil
- cloves
- onions
- roots
- bouillon
- small lard for larding
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in fat made with salt, pepper, a bouquet garni of parsley, chives, thyme, bay leaf, basil, cloves, onions, and roots, with just enough bouillon to wet the meat
2. Cook over very low heat
3. When cooked, remove the skin and lard it with small pieces of lard
4. Cook it afterwards on the spit
5. Serve under a sauce with a dash of vinegar
