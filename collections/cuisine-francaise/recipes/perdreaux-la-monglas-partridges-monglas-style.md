## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Two or three truffles
- About twenty mushrooms
- Six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- Six spoonfuls of game essence

## Directions

1. Empty and singe your partridges
2. Truss them as you would a braised hen
3. Put them on the spit
4. When cooked, let them cool
5. Remove the breast meat in such a way that the rest of the body forms an oval well
6. Cut the meat into small dice and put in a casserole
7. Cut two or three truffles also into small dice
8. Have about twenty mushrooms, cut them small
9. Mix them together with your truffles and your partridge meat
10. Add six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and six spoonfuls of game essence
11. Reduce everything by a third
12. Strain through a fine sieve over your small ragout
13. Keep your partridges warm in a casserole
14. At the moment of serving, remove the trussing strings
15. Put your ragout inside your partridges with Spanish sauce underneath
