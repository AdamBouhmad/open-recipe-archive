## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- oil or good butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut it into slices
2. Marinate it with a little oil or good butter, salt, and pepper
3. Grill it while basting with its marinade
4. Serve with sauces or ragouts as explained for turbot
