## Ingredients

- Two fairly large sweetbreads
- Butter mixed with flour
- Half a glass of vinegar
- One large glass of water
- Three cloves
- One clove of garlic
- Two shallots
- Three or four chives
- A pinch of parsley
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Pepper
- Parsley for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in warm water for one hour to disgorge
2. Blanch them for a quarter-hour at a rolling boil
3. Remove to fresh water and cut each piece into three parts
4. Put in a casserole butter mixed with flour, half a glass of vinegar, one large glass of water, three cloves, garlic, two shallots, three or four chives, a pinch of parsley, bay leaf, thyme, basil, and pepper
5. Heat the marinade, stirring the butter until melted
6. Add the sweetbreads and remove from heat to marinate for one and a half to two hours
7. Drain and dry them with a cloth
8. Flour them and fry to a beautiful color
9. When removed, throw parsley into the frying oil to fry it green and crispy
10. Serve the parsley around the sweetbreads
