## Ingredients

- one hen
- its liver
- a little butter
- parsley
- chives
- a pinch of chopped garlic
- salt
- coarse pepper
- two egg yolks
- hot butter
- one egg yolk
- bread crumbs
- half a glass of bouillon
- a little vinegar
- butter the size of half an egg
- flour
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Flame, pluck and clean a hen, stuff it with its liver mixed with a little butter, parsley, chives, a pinch of chopped garlic, salt, coarse pepper, two egg yolks
2. Cook it on a spit
3. When it is cooked, sprinkle the top with a little hot butter in which you have dissolved an egg yolk, bread it with bread crumbs
4. Make it take on a beautiful golden color over the fire
5. Serve it with sauce made as follows: put in a pan half a glass of bouillon, a little vinegar, butter the size of half an egg worked with a good pinch of flour, salt, coarse pepper, grated nutmeg
6. Make it thicken over the fire
