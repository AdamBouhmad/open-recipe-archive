## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Butter
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Trim your beef rib; lard it with spiced thick lardons
2. Put a piece of butter the size of two eggs in a casserole and melt it
3. Put your beef rib seasoned with salt and black pepper in the casserole
4. Place the casserole on a fairly hot fire; turn your beef rib two or three times
5. When it is hot, place it on a gentle fire and also place fire on the cover of your casserole
6. When it has been cooking for an hour and a half it will be done
7. Use the cooking liquid for sauce
