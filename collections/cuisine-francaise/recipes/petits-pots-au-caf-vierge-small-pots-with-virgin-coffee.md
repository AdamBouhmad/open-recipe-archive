## Ingredients

- Ten measures of cream
- One quarter-pound of sugar
- Two ounces of coffee
- Six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Measure ten small pots of cream and bring it to boil
2. Put in one quarter-pound of sugar and keep your cream hot
3. Roast two ounces of coffee; when it is blonde, put it fresh from the pan into the cream
4. Immediately place a saucepan lid on top
5. Let the cream cool and strain it through a sieve to remove the coffee grain
6. Then put six egg yolks in a saucepan
