## Ingredients

- verjuice
- a quarter pound of sugar
- a glass of water

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from your verjuice
2. Put it in a pan with a quarter pound of sugar and a glass of water
3. Boil it over low heat
4. When it is well green and the syrup is reduced, dress it in the compote dish and serve cold
