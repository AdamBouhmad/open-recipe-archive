## Ingredients

- Veal tendons
- Flour
- Consommé
- Velouté (1 spoonful)
- Mushrooms
- Bouquet garni
- Pepper
- Veal sweetbreads
- Rooster crests and kidneys
- Veal threads, sautéed
- Chicken quenelles (12)
- Egg yolks (5 or 6)

## Directions

1. Prepare your tendons as in the blanc method
2. After adding flour, moisten with consommé
3. Add one spoonful of velouté
4. Add mushrooms, bouquet garni, and a little pepper
5. Cook the tendons for three hours
6. The sauce must be abundant as there will be garnishes in the terrine: small veal sweetbreads, rooster crests and kidneys, sautéed veal threads, and twelve chicken quenelles, all cooked separately
7. At the moment of serving, drain the garnishes and place in the terrine
8. Place the tendons and mushrooms in the terrine
9. Bind the sauce with five or six egg yolks according to how thin the sauce is
10. Pass it through a sieve over the tendons
