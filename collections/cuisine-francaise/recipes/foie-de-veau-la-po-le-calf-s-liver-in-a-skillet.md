## Ingredients

- One calf's liver
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A good piece of butter
- Four tablespoons of flour
- Half a bottle of white wine
- Broth or water if needed

## Directions

1. Cut and season your liver as in the previous recipe
2. Put a good piece of butter in the skillet; when it is melted, add your liver
3. The fire must be high heat
4. Stir your liver in the skillet
5. When the pieces are firm to the touch, add four tablespoons of flour which you mix with your liver
6. Pour in half a bottle of white wine
7. Stir your liver well so that your sauce binds
8. If it is too thick, add a little broth or water
9. Do not let your liver boil; serve it as soon as it begins to boil
10. Taste to ensure it is properly salted before serving
