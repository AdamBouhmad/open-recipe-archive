## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Vine leaves
- Lard for barding

## Directions

1. Empty and singe your pigeons fairly well.
2. Pluck and truss them.
3. Place a vine leaf under the bard if you have one.
4. Half an hour is sufficient for cooking.
