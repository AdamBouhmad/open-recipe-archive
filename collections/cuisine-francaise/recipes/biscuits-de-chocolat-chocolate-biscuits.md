## Ingredients

- Six fresh eggs
- One and a half ounces of very finely crushed chocolate
- Six ounces of fine sugar
- Six ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Break four of the six eggs and put the yolks in one bowl and whites in another
2. Put with the yolks one and a half ounces of very finely crushed chocolate and six ounces of fine sugar
3. Beat everything together for more than a quarter hour
4. Add the whipped egg whites and mix well
5. Gradually add six ounces of flour, stirring constantly
6. Dress your biscuits the same way as those à la cuiller on white paper, or if you wish in small buttered paper molds
7. Sprinkle a little fine sugar over them to glaze and bake like the previous ones
