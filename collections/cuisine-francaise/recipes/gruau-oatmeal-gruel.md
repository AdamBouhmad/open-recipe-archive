## Ingredients

- One chopine of water or milk
- One spoonful of oatmeal
- Sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Take one chopine of water or milk and put in one spoonful of oatmeal
2. Let it boil gently for a quarter hour
3. Strain it and drink it clear
4. Add a little sugar if desired
