## Ingredients

- Fifteen rousselet pears
- Water
- Sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Blanch the pears using the same procedure as for preceding pears
2. Peel them and trim the stem slightly
3. Place them in a somewhat thin syrup
4. When cooked, arrange them on a serving dish
5. Reduce the syrup if necessary
