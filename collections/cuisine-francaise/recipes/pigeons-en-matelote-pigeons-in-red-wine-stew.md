## Ingredients

- medium-sized pigeons
- butter
- small white onions
- bacon
- bouquet garni
- flour
- bouillon
- white wine
- egg yolks
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Blanch the pigeons with legs trussed inside and pass them in a casserole with a little butter and a dozen small white onions that you have cooked for half a quarter-hour in water to peel them.
2. Add a quarter pound of well-larded bacon cut into slices and a bouquet garni.
3. Add a pinch of flour and moisten with half bouillon and half white wine.
4. When the pigeons are cooked and reduced to little sauce, add a liaison of three egg yolks with verjuice or vinegar when serving.
