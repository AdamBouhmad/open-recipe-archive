## Ingredients

- Leg of mutton with bones
- Atelets (skewers)
- Twine
- Clear stock

## Directions

1. Cut at the second cutlet near the saddle, as squarely as possible
2. Secure the flanks with atelets
3. Remove the chine bone
4. Beat your leg of mutton
5. Pass the spit near the handle through to the ribs, using a trussing needle
6. Fix your piece of square cut onto the spit
7. If serving as a relevé, place clear stock underneath
