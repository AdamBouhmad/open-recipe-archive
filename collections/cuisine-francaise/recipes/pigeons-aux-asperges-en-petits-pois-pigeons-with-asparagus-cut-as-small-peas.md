## Ingredients

- Small asparagus
- Pigeons

## Directions

1. Cut small asparagus into small peas, taking only the tender part and stopping cutting as soon as the knife meets resistance
2. When you have the equivalent of one and a half litrons, put them in fresh water and wash them several times without fear they will break
3. Blanch them for half a quarter hour in boiling water
4. Remove them to fresh water and drain them
5. Prepare them in the same manner as pigeons with small peas, with the difference that in the bouquet you will add a little savory and two cloves
