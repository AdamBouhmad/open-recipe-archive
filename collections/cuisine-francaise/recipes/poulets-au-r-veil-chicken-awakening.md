## Ingredients

- Two chickens
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Two tarragon leaves
- Two or three branches of chervil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fat from the pot
- Lard
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- One clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- One bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil

## Directions

1. Flame and pluck two chickens and empty them
2. Chop the livers which you mix with a piece of butter, parsley, chives, two tarragon leaves, two or three branches of chervil, all chopped, salt, and pepper
3. Stuff the chickens with this forcemeat and truss with the legs
4. Brown on the fire with a little butter or fat from the pot
5. Cook on the spit wrapped in lard and paper
6. Put in a saucepan the butter used to brown the chickens, with two carrots in strips, two onions in slices, one clove of garlic, two cloves, one bay leaf, thyme, and basil
