## Ingredients

- Eight or ten pounds of sliced beef
- Eight old hens
- Two calf necks
- Four calf knuckles
- Great broth
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Onions
- Three cloves

## Directions

1. Take eight or ten pounds of sliced beef, eight old hens, two calf necks, four calf knuckles
2. Put your meat in your pot
3. Fill it with great broth
4. Make it skim
5. Be careful to refresh your broth three or four times to make the foam rise well
6. After make your consommé boil gently
7. Garnish your pot with carrots, turnips, onions, three cloves
8. When your meats are cooked, pass your consommé through fine cloth or a silk sieve so that your consommé is very clear
9. Do not add salt if you moisten it with great broth
