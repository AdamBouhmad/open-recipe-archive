## Ingredients

- Four calf's feet
- bouillon
- lard
- eau-de-vie
- parsley
- chives
- garlic cloves
- cloves
- bay leaves
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- coriander
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Split four well-blanched calf's feet down the middle with a cleaver and tie them
2. Place them in a pot with rich bouillon, a spoonful of lard, a glass of eau-de-vie, a bouquet of parsley and chives, two cloves of garlic, three cloves, two bay leaves, thyme, basil, salt, pepper, and a pinch of coriander
3. Cook over low heat until done and only a small amount of sauce remains
4. Let them cool slightly, then remove and coat with bread crumbs
5. Lightly sprinkle the top with bread crumbs using the same fat, grill until a nice color, and serve as an hors-d'œuvre or side dish
