## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- egg for glazing
- sugar and red-hot iron for glazing

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry as explained previously, page 508
2. Beat it with a rolling pin to the thickness of half a finger
3. Cut it into diamond shapes two fingers wide
4. Glaze the top of the cakes with beaten egg
5. Bake for a good quarter hour in the oven
6. Then glaze with sugar using a red-hot iron
