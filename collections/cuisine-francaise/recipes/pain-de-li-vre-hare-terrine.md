## Ingredients

- Forcemeat made with hare meat
- Hare liver
- Thin lard bacon slices
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Prepare a forcemeat as described previously
2. Pound the hare liver well and pass it through a quenelle sieve
3. Collect all of the sieved liver without losing any
4. Mix the liver into the forcemeat
5. Fill a mold almost completely with the forcemeat
6. Line the bottom and sides of the mold with thin slices of bacon
7. Place the forcemeat inside the mold
8. Put boiling water in a casserole
9. Place the mold in the casserole such that the water comes up to within a finger's breadth of the top
10. Simmer the water gently
11. Place bacon slices on top of the terrine
12. Cover the casserole with a lid and place fire on top
13. Test the terrine after one hour to see if it is cooked
14. Remove from the mold at the moment of serving
15. Glaze it and serve with a game sauce
16. If desired, make a hole in the middle of the terrine to place sautéed hare kidneys with Champagne wine, or tenderloin fillets inside (this can only be done for a large game preparation)
