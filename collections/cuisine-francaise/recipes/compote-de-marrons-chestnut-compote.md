## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- 1 quarter pound sugar
- 1 half-glass water
- Lemon juice
- Fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cook chestnuts in ash the same way as if you wanted to serve them in a napkin
2. When they are peeled, put them in a pan with a quarter pound of sugar and a half-glass of water
3. Let them simmer on a small fire about half a quarter-hour
4. Before serving, squeeze a little lemon juice
5. When serving, lightly add fine sugar on top
