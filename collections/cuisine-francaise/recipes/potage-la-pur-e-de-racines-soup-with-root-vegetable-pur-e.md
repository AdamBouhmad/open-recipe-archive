## Ingredients

- thirty or forty carrots
- quarter pound of butter
- bouillon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Slice your carrots
2. Take a quarter pound of butter and pass them through, turning them from time to time so they do not stick
3. When they have been on the fire for three quarters of an hour or an hour, moisten with bouillon
4. Add a small piece of sugar about the size of half an egg
5. Let them cook for two hours over low heat
6. Pass them through a sieve
7. If there is not enough of their bouillon and your purée is too thick, moisten it with bouillon from the pot
8. Your bread crusts should be as for bread soup
9. Dip them with clear bouillon so they absorb more easily
10. Pour your purée over them
11. Ensure it is lightly bound so your soup is not too thick
12. Before putting it in, take care to add bouillon to thin it
13. Boil it to be able to skim and remove the fat
