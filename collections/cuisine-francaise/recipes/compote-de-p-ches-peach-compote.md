## Ingredients

- Six peaches
- Water
- Sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Fill a pot three-quarters full with boiling water
2. Place the peaches in it
3. Give them five or six boils
4. Test if the skin comes off easily
5. Refresh them in cold water and peel
6. Split them in two and remove the pit
7. Make syrup in the pot
8. Place the peach halves in the syrup
9. Let them reach a boil
10. Remove them and place in a serving dish
11. Reduce the syrup if too clear and pour under the peaches
