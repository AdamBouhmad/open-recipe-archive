## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- leeks
- onions
- celery
- lettuce
- sorrel
- chervil
- butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut the roots into thin strips the thickness of half a line and eight to ten lines long
2. Cut the onions in two, then into slices to form half-circles
3. Cut leeks and celery into thin strips
4. Slice the lettuce and sorrel thinly
5. Pass the roots through butter until they have softened slightly
6. Add the lettuce, herbs, and chervil, ensuring everything is well softened
7. Moisten with bouillon
8. Boil over low heat for an hour or more until cooked
9. Prepare your bread and pour your Julienne over it
