## Ingredients

- cod
- fresh water
- parsley
- chives
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- verjus

## Directions

1. Scale and wash the cod
2. Put it in fresh water in a pan or pot
3. Place on the fire; when it begins to boil, skim it
4. Remove from the fire as soon as it boils
5. Cover with a cloth for half a quarter hour
6. Drain the water
7. Place on a dish with chopped parsley and chives, coarse pepper, grated nutmeg, a good piece of butter, and a spoonful of verjus
8. Heat while turning it over
9. Serve immediately
