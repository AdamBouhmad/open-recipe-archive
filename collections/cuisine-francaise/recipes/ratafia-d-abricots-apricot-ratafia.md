## Ingredients

- One quarter pound of apricots
- Apricot kernels (from broken pits)
- Two pints of eau-de-vie
- Half a pound of sugar
- A little cinnamon
- Eight cloves
- Very little mace

## Directions

1. Cut the apricots into small pieces
2. Break the pits to extract the kernels, peel and crush them
3. Place them in a jug with the apricots, two pints of eau-de-vie, half a pound of sugar, a little cinnamon, eight cloves, and very little mace
4. Cork the jug well and let it infuse for fifteen days or three weeks
5. Take care to frequently remove the jug from storage
6. After infusing, strain it through a strainer to put it in bottles to be stored in the cellar
