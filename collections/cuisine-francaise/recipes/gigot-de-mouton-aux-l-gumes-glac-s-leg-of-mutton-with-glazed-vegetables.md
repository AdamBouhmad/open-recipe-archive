## Ingredients

- Mortified leg of mutton
- Good bouillon
- Half a head of cabbage
- A dozen root vegetables
- Six large onions
- Three celery stalks
- Six turnips

## Directions

1. Trim the leg of mutton of its fat and the end of the shank; truss it and place in a pot with good bouillon
2. Turn the half head of cabbage and the dozen root vegetables cut in rounds; add six large onions and three celery stalks
3. Blanch everything together for half a quarter hour
4. Remove and refresh in fresh water, press out any water, and truss the cabbage and celery
5. Cook all these vegetables with the leg of mutton
6. When everything is cooked, remove the leg of mutton and vegetables to a platter and wipe away any grease with a white cloth
7. Arrange the leg of mutton on the serving platter with vegetables around it
8. Take the bouillon used to cook the leg of mutton, degrease it and pass through a sieve, reduce it to two spoonfuls to make your glaze
9. Lightly glaze the leg of mutton and vegetables
10. Put clear coulis in the casserole where the glaze was reduced to detach what remains, pass this sauce through a sieve to make it clearer, season it well, and serve over the vegetables without touching the glaze
