## Ingredients

- One rabbit, boned except for the head
- Flesh from the thighs of the rabbit
- Fillets of two other rabbits
- Lard, equal amount to the meat
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Aromatic herbs, pounded
- Truffles, chopped
- Medium lardons for larding
- Lardons of scarlet tongue
- Pieces of truffles
- Lardons
- Slices of bacon for larding
- Veal knuckle, cut into pieces
- Debris from the rabbits

## Directions

1. Bone the rabbit, except for the head
2. Remove the largest part of the flesh from the thighs to make room for stuffing
3. Lard the flesh with medium-sized lardons seasoned
4. Mince the flesh from the thighs and fillets of two other rabbits
5. Add an equal amount of lard and mince everything together
6. Add salt, coarse pepper, a little pounded aromatic herbs, and chopped truffles
7. Once the forcemeat is made, lay out the rabbit and season it
8. Put a layer of forcemeat on it, then add lardons of scarlet tongue, pieces of truffles, and lardons
9. Add another layer of forcemeat, continuing in this manner
10. Once the rabbit is well filled, give it its original shape
11. Cover it with slices of bacon
12. Tie it with string and wrap it in white linen, which you will tie again
13. Place slices of bacon in a braising pan
14. Place the rabbit in it along with a veal knuckle cut into pieces and the debris from your rabbits
