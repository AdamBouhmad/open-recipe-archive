## Ingredients

- sheep's tails, five beautiful ones
- bouillon
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- cloves, two
- garlic, half a clove
- bay leaf, half a leaf
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- rice, about six ounces

## Directions

1. Put the tails to cook with bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, two cloves, half a clove of garlic, half a bay leaf, thyme, basil, salt, pepper
2. Cook them on a small fire
3. Remove them from their braise to let them drain and cool
4. Take about six ounces of well-cleaned rice, wash it several times in warm water rubbing with your hands
5. Put it in a small pot with the bouillon that served to cook the tails, passed through a sieve without degreasing it, and if there was not enough, you add more
