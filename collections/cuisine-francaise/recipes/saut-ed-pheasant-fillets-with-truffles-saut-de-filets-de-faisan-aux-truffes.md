## Ingredients

- Pheasant fillets
- Truffles
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Game stock
- Egg yolks
- Velouté sauce
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Remove your fillets and take off the nervous skin
2. Cut your fillet in two lengthwise; slice your pieces two lines thick; give them an agreeable shape
3. Put them in your sauté pan or tart pan with salt, coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg; warm some butter and pour it over them
4. Remove the nerve from the tenderloins and put them with your sauté
5. Peel your truffles; cut them into rounds one and a half lines thick; arrange your truffle rounds on your fillets
6. At serving time, put your sauté on the fire; turn your fillets; when they are stiffened on one side turn them to the other; leave them another moment on the fire
7. Tilt your sauté pan and put your sauté high so the butter separates from your sauté
8. Take velouté reduced with game stock; make a liaison with two egg yolks
9. If you wish, sauté your sauté in your sauce and dress it right away so your sauce does not break; put croutons around your plate
