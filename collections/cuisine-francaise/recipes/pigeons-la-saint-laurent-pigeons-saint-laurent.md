## Ingredients

- Four pigeons
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Clear broth or water
- Shallots
- Vinegar
- Breadcrumb coating

## Directions

1. Take four pigeons and singe them lightly.
2. Truss the legs inside the body.
3. Split them down the back from neck to tail.
4. Empty and beat them on the breast so the pigeon is flat.
5. Season with coarse pepper and salt.
6. Heat a piece of butter in a saucepan and dip them in it, then roll in breadcrumbs.
7. When well breaded, half an hour before serving, place them breast-side down on the grill over gentle heat.
8. Turn them at the appropriate time.
9. Arrange on the platter.
10. Place clear broth underneath or a shallot sauce made with: half a glass of bouillon or water, salt, fine pepper, two tablespoons of finely chopped shallots, three spoonfuls of good vinegar, and one spoonful of breadcrumb coating.
11. Bring the sauce to boil two or three times and pour under your pigeons.
12. Check that it is well seasoned.
