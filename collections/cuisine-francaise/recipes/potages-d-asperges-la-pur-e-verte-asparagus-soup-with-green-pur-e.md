## Ingredients

- roots
- water or broth
- one liter of green peas
- asparagus of medium size
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a broth of roots like the preceding one
2. When passed through a sieve, take a part to cook one liter of green peas
3. Take asparagus of medium size, as much as you need to garnish the soup
4. Cut them to the length of three fingers
5. Blanch them briefly in boiling water and remove them to fresh water
6. Drain them and tie them in several small bundles, cut off a little the end of the tip, and put them to cook with the peas
7. When the peas are cooked, pass them into a purée
8. Simmer the soup with the root broth
9. Make a garnish on the edges of the dish with the asparagus
10. When serving, add the pea coulis
11. The rich version is made the same way using good rich broth in place of the lean
