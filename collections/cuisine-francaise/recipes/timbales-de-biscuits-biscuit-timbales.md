## Ingredients

- six eggs
- sugar equal to the weight of six eggs
- flour equal to the weight of three eggs

## Directions

1. Take six eggs and as much sugar weighing the weight of three eggs of flour, which will provide you with enough to make six timbales the size of a good glass each, which will make a small entremes dish
2. Follow the same method as for the Savoy cake found previously, with the difference that it only takes half an hour for cooking and the oven should be a little gentler
