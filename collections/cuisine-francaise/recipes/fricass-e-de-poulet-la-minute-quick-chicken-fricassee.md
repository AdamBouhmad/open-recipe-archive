## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- Mushrooms
- Flour
- Bouillon or water
- Liaison (optional)
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Flame your chicken
2. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan
3. Place your chicken members with salt, pepper, a bay leaf, and your mushrooms
4. Put your saucepan on a high fire
5. Sauté your chicken
6. As soon as your members are well hit by the heat, add a little flour which you mix with your chicken
7. Add a glass of bouillon or water; be careful with the salt
8. If you put in bouillon, stir it on the fire
9. At the first boil, remove your fricassee
10. You may add a liaison if you wish, and lemon juice
