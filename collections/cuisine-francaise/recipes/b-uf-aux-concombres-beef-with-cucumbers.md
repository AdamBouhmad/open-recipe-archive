## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Cucumbers
- Spanish sauce or light roux and stock

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your beef rib as you would a braised one
2. Place pieces or quarters of cucumber underneath or around it
3. Glaze them and put a reduced Spanish sauce underneath
4. If you do not have sauce, pass the cooking stock through fine cloth, make a light roux, moisten with your stock
5. Make your sauce somewhat thin so you can reduce it
6. Put your pieces of cucumber in your sauce, which must be very reduced, because cucumber always releases water even when well drained
7. For quarters, cook them in a little stock and fat from your beef rib for about half an hour
