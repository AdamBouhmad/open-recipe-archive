## Ingredients

- A rack of lamb or lamb chops
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Egg yolks
- Ravigote herbs (salad greens, chervil, tarragon, burnet, watercress)
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Keep your rack whole or cut into chops
2. Cook in a casserole with a little butter, salt and coarse pepper over a small fire
3. When degreased, prepare the same sauce you put on a plate, thin it with three egg yolks and ravigote herbs
4. Put this binding in the saucepan where the rack or chops are
5. Bind over the fire without boiling
6. Dress your chops on the plate you will serve
7. Ravigote greens should contain all salad ingredients like chervil, tarragon, burnet, watercress, using about a handful of each herb
8. Blanch them for half a quarter hour in boiling water, then drain, press them well in your hands, and pound them very fine before adding to the binding
