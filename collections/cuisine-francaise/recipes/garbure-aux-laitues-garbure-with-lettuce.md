## Ingredients

- Thirty lettuces
- Slices of veal
- Lard
- Bread, thinly sliced
- Two or three carrots
- Three onions
- Two cloves
- Bouillon
- Coarse pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Blanch the lettuces for half an hour, keeping them whole
2. Let them cool, press them, and tie them with string
3. Put slices of veal and lard at the bottom of a saucepan
4. Add the lettuces and cover them with lard along with two or three carrots, three onions, and two cloves
5. Moisten with bouillon and simmer for an hour and a half until cooked
6. Drain and cut lengthwise into slices
7. Make a layer of thinly sliced bread in your dish, then a layer of lettuce, continuing until full
8. Pour the bouillon from the lettuce over it without degreasing, but after passing through a silk sieve
9. Put the dish on the fire to simmer until it has a golden crust
10. Spare the salt due to reduction; add a little coarse pepper to each layer
11. Before serving, degrease your garbure and serve bouillon in a separate vessel
