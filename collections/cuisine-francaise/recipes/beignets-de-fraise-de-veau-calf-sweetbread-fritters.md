## Ingredients

- one calf sweetbread
- water
- salt
- parsley
- chives
- two cloves of garlic
- one bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- three cloves
- butter
- two spoonfuls of vinegar
- shallot
- coarse pepper
- egg
- bread crumb

## Directions

1. Cook a calf sweetbread with water, salt, a bouquet of parsley, chives, two cloves of garlic, one bay leaf, thyme, basil, and three cloves
2. When cooked, let it drain and degrease it
3. Cut into small bouquets and put it to marinate for one hour with a little butter, two spoonfuls of vinegar, chopped parsley, chives, shallot, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Warm the marinade
5. Remove all the small pieces of sweetbread and roll them as you go, making sure to keep the fine herbs attached
6. When they are cold, dip them in beaten egg, coat with bread crumb, and fry to a beautiful golden color
