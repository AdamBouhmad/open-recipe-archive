## Ingredients

- almonds, half-pound sweet almonds
- bitter almonds, a few
- milk, two pints for soup
- fresh water
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. You will blanch your almonds, that is to say you will put them in a saucepan and water that you will make almost boil; you will see if the skin of the almond comes off; when your almonds are blanched, when the skin is removed, you will mix them in fresh water; a half-pound of sweet almonds, a few bitter almonds suffice for a soup of two pints of milk; you pound your almonds in a mortar; when they are well ground, you put them in a saucepan; you wet them with a half-setier of milk; then you put them in a fine cloth, and press them until all the milk has come out; you pour this milk into your soup at the moment of serving it; serve it very hot, and with good sugar and a little salt.
