## Ingredients

- One veal knuckle, whole and well covered
- Fine lard for larding
- Coarse lard for larding
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Three carrots, turned into large batons
- Four large onions
- Two cloves
- Two laurel leaves
- Parsley and chive bouquet
- One spoonful of jelly or consommé or bouillon
- Grande Espagnole sauce
- Flour and bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove your knuckle whole and well covered
2. Beat it like the previous one
3. Pare the flesh that is under the nipple, without touching the nipple
4. Lard with fine lard the flesh that appears from your knuckle
5. When your knuckle is larded with fine lard, lard it underneath with coarse lard
6. Season the lardoons with salt and coarse pepper
7. When your knuckle is larded, truss it like the previous one
8. Put it in a casserole
9. Cover it with buttered paper
10. Put in three carrots turned into large batons, four large onions, one of which is studded with two cloves, two laurel leaves, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and one spoonful of jelly; if you have no jelly, put consommé or bouillon in it
11. Cook your knuckle on a gentle fire for two hours, fire above, fire below
12. When your knuckle is three-quarters cooked, remove your vegetables with your knuckle, and make it fall to glaze, that is to say put it on a hot fire, and reduce the liquid of your knuckle until it is glazed
13. At the moment of serving, remove your knuckle from your casserole
14. Untie it
15. Glaze it with what is in your casserole
16. Put three spoonfuls of grande Espagnole sauce in your casserole, where you will have removed the fat, and loosened the glaze
17. Put this sauce under your knuckle; in case you have no sauce, you would put a little flour in it that you would loosen with a little bouillon
