## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Dark red wine
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Salt
- Butter, a good piece
- Flour, two tablespoons

## Directions

1. Cook your salmon as in the preceding recipe; moisten it with dark red wine and add the seasoning of the preceding recipe without salting too much.
2. Put a good piece of butter in your saucepan with two tablespoons of flour, which you will mix with the butter.
3. Pass the court-bouillon through a silk sieve and combine it with the butter.
4. Place it on the fire and stir until your sauce boils; reduce it by half.
5. Skim and degrease it; then strain it through fine silk; keep it warm without boiling.
6. At the moment of serving your fish, drain it and arrange it plainly on the plate; then mask it with your sauce.
