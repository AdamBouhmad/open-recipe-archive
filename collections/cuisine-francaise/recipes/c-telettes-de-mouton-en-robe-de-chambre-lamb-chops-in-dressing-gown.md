## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- bouillon
- salt
- bouquet garni
- veal round
- beef fat
- two eggs
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- cream
- breadcrumbs
- clear sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the chops with bouillon, very little salt, and a bouquet garni
2. When cooked, degrease the bouillon and pass it through a sieve
3. Reduce it to a glaze and dip the chops in it to glaze them
4. Remove the chops after glazing and let them cool
5. Make a forcemeat with veal round, beef fat, two eggs, salt, pepper, parsley, chives, mushrooms (all chopped), and moisten with cream
6. Wrap each chop with this forcemeat
7. Place them on a baking dish and coat with breadcrumbs
8. Cook in the oven; when they are lightly colored, drain them of their fat
9. Serve under a good clear sauce
