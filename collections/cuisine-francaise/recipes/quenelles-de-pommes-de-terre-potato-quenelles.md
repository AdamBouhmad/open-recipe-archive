## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- bouillon
- espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in red ashes
2. Remove everything hard from the potato and take only the floury part
3. Mix this part in a mortar and pound it well
4. Pass it through a quenelle strainer
5. Make a pile and add half butter, pounding it with the potatoes
6. When well pounded, grate in a little nutmeg, add salt, coarse pepper, parsley, and well-chopped chives
7. Add five or six egg yolks, or more according to quantity
8. Beat two or three egg whites and fold them with the mixture
9. Poach them in bouillon as with poultry quenelles
10. Drain the quenelles, arrange on the platter, and pour espagnole sauce over them; alternatively use tomato sauce, portugaise sauce, béchamel or reduced velouté
