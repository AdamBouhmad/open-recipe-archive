## Ingredients

- duck
- shallots
- veal stock (blond de veau)
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Truss, tie, and cook your duck as you would cook duck in a pan
2. When it is three-quarters cooked, at the moment of serving, drain and remove the trussing string
3. Serve it on your plate; make eight cuts, four on each side on the breast
4. Chop shallots very finely, one spoonful, which you put in a saucepan with veal stock two spoonfuls, coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg, a little salt
5. Let your shallots boil a few times; remove from the fire
6. Press the juice of two lemons into your sauce; pour it over the incisions in your duck
7. You can serve your duck as an entrée on the spit, or simply on the spit
