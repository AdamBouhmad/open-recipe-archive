## Ingredients

- Eight fresh eggs
- Cooked ham, chopped
- Cooking juice (coulis)
- Bouillon
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Poach eight fresh eggs.
2. Put chopped cooked ham in a pan with a little cooking juice and bouillon.
3. Add a dash of vinegar, coarse pepper, and a little salt.
4. Heat the sauce and serve it over the eggs.
