## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- chicken

## Directions

1. Cut them the same way and cook them in water a quarter hour
2. Put them back in fresh water
3. Prepare them afterward in a fricassee of chicken
4. When they are cooked, put in a liaison and serve them as a side dish
