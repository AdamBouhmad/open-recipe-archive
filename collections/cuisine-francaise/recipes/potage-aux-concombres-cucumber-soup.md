## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- lard strips
- carrots
- onions
- coarse pepper
- two cloves
- broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut cucumbers into small ovals
2. Blanch them for ten minutes
3. Let them cool and drain
4. Put strips of lard in the bottom of the saucepan
5. Add your cucumbers and cover them with lard
6. Add carrots, onions, coarse pepper, and two cloves
7. Cook for three quarters of an hour
8. Prepare your broth as for bread soup
9. Place cucumbers on top
10. Pass the broth through a silk sieve, degrease it, and pour over your soup
11. Alternatively, after blanching your cucumbers, cook them in broth for three quarters of an hour and pour them over the bread of your soup
