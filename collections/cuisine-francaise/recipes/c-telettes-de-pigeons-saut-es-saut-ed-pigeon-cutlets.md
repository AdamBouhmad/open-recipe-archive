## Ingredients

- six pigeons
- salt
- coarse pepper
- aromatic spices
- butter
- clear espagnole sauce
- glace (meat glaze) about the size of half a walnut

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the breast of your six pigeons and trim them
2. Pass a bone through them which you take from the tip of the wing fillet
3. Arrange them in your sauté pan
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Sprinkle lightly with ground aromatic spices
6. Warm a good piece of butter and pour it over your cutlets
7. At the moment of serving, place them on high heat
8. As soon as they are stiffened on one side, turn them to the other
9. Leave them only an instant
10. Drain them and arrange in a crown shape with a glazed crouton between each one
11. Serve with a clear espagnole sauce into which you put glace about the size of half a walnut
