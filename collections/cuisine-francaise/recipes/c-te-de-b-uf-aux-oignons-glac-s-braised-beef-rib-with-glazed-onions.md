## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Glazed onions
- Espagnole sauce
- Cooking liquid

## Directions

1. Trim and braise your beef rib
2. When it is cooked, remove the string, drain it, and arrange it on your platter
3. Make sure it looks good and is completely whole
4. Put glazed onions around it, and a clear espagnole sauce that you have prepared with a little cooking liquid from your rib
5. Make sure your sauce is well degreased and passed through cheesecloth
