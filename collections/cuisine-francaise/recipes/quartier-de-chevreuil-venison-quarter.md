## Ingredients

- Venison quarter
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Fine pepper
- Seven or eight bay leaves
- Thyme
- Onion slices
- Parsley in branches
- Whole chives
- Piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Lard the venison
2. Place it in a terrine
3. Pour in a bottle of vinegar, salt, fine pepper, seven or eight bay leaves, thyme, onion slices, parsley in branches, and whole chives
4. Leave it five or six days if you can
5. Spit it as you would a leg of mutton
6. Pour a piquant sauce underneath or in a sauce boat
7. It can be served as a relevé
