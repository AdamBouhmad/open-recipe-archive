## Ingredients

- Chicory with cream
- Poached eggs
- Cream sauce

## Directions

1. Arrange chicory with cream on a plate
2. Place poached eggs on top
3. You may put under the poached eggs: cream-dressed cabbage, sorrel purée, mushroom purée, cardoon purée, cucumbers with cream, chopped celery with cream, tomato sauce, or asparagus tips
