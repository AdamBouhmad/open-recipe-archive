## Ingredients

- Carp
- Fish roe (laitance)
- Lard
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Onion slices
- Cloves
- Lard strips
- Carp quenelles or pigeons à la Gautier
- Glazed veal sweetbreads
- Glazed veal knuckles
- Crayfish
- Foie gras
- Truffles
- Rooster crests
- Rooster kidneys
- Espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the carp carefully, avoiding damaging it
2. Fill its body with fish roe
3. Remove the scales and skin superficially on one side
4. Lard that side from the gill to the tail
5. Place it in a fish kettle
6. Moisten with white wine and add salt, pepper, parsley leaves, six bay leaves, thyme, onion slices and cloves
7. Cover the head with lard strips and place in the oven, or cover the fish kettle with parchment paper, place fire on top and simmer for a good hour
8. At the moment of serving, drain it and place on a plat
9. Arrange around it carp quenelles or pigeons à la Gautier, glazed veal sweetbreads, glazed veal knuckles, crayfish, poultry foie gras, truffles, rooster crests and kidneys
10. If the cooking liquid is not too seasoned, pass it through silk sieve
11. In a saucepan put two spoonfuls of espagnole and two spoonfuls of the carp cooking liquid
12. Reduce the sauce by half
13. Add the small garnishes and pour the sauce around the carp after glazing it
