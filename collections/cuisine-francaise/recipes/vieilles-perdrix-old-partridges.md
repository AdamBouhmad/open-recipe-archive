## Ingredients

- Old partridges
- White wine
- Lentil coulis
- Bacon (petit lard)

## Directions

1. Always cook them braised, as you would beef tongue (page 29), adding white wine
2. When cooked, place them in a terrine with lentil coulis and bacon
