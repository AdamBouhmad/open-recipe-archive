## Ingredients

- three quarterons of small bacon, cut into very thin slices
- one and a half litrons of flour
- bacon fat rendered from cooking the bacon
- half a pound of butter
- a little salt
- two eggs
- one large glass of water

## Directions

1. Cut three quarterons of small bacon into very thin slices
2. Put them in a saucepan over a low fire to cook for half an hour
3. Spread one and a half litrons of flour on a table and make a hole in the middle
4. Put in the rendered bacon fat from cooking the bacon, half a pound of butter, a little salt, and two eggs
5. Add one large glass of water and knead the dough
6. Let it rest for one hour
7. Distribute all the bacon slices throughout the dough, spacing them somewhat apart from each other
8. Form your cake in the usual way, glaze with beaten egg
9. Bake for a good hour in the oven or under a pie-dish cover
