## Ingredients

- eel
- butter
- bouquet
- romaine lettuce hearts
- salt
- three egg yolks
- cream
- verjuice or wine

## Directions

1. Cut the eel into pieces and cook it as if making a chicken fricassee
2. When it is almost cooked, have romaine lettuce hearts that are well cleaned and cooked in white water with a little salt and butter
3. Drain the lettuce and let it take on flavor with the eel
4. Add a liaison of three egg yolks mixed with cream
5. Make it bind over the fire, and when serving add a dash of verjuice if you did not put wine in your eel fricassee
