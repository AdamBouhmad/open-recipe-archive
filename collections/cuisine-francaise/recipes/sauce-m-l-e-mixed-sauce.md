## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- A little point of garlic
- A little butter
- A pinch of flour
- Good bouillon
- Two gherkins
- Three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take parsley, chives, mushrooms, and a little point of garlic, all well chopped, and pass them over the fire with a little butter
2. Add a pinch of flour and moisten with good bouillon
3. When your sauce is cooked and reduced to half, add two minced gherkins and a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with bouillon
4. Thicken your sauce and season it to good taste
5. Serve for whatever you judge appropriate
