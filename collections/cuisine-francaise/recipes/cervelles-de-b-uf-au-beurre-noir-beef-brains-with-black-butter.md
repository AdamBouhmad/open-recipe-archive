## Ingredients

- beef brains
- lard
- bay leaves
- onion slices
- carrots
- parsley and chives bouquet
- white wine
- bouillon
- black butter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the brains by removing coagulated blood, thin skin, and fibers
2. Soak in lukewarm water for two hours
3. Cook between lard, two bay leaves, onion slices, carrots, parsley and chives bouquet, white wine, and bouillon
4. Simmer for half an hour over the fire
5. Drain the brains
6. Put black butter underneath and fried parsley in the middle
7. Alternatively, the brains may be cooked with carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, chives, a dash of vinegar, salt, and water
