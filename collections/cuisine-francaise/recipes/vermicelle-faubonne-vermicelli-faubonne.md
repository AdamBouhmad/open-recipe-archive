## Ingredients

- Root vegetables
- Vermicelli
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the root vegetables into small fillets instead of dice
2. Cook your vegetables before adding the vermicelli
3. Once the vegetables are cooked, put your vermicelli into your bouillon
4. Take care to skim from time to time so that your vermicelli does not clump together
5. Season with salt and coarse pepper
