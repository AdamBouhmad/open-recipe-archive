## Ingredients

- 1 young rabbit, dressed and cleaned
- Aromatic herbs, pounded
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- 1 small spoon of flour
- 1 glass of Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your young rabbit as in the previous recipe; add the same seasonings.
2. Pour in a small spoon of flour, which you blend with your young rabbit without letting it brown on the fire.
3. Also add about one glass of Champagne wine.
4. Place your casserole on the fire and stir so that your ragout thickens without boiling.
5. When you see your sauce thickened, serve your ragout. This should only be made at the moment of serving. Check that it is properly salted.
