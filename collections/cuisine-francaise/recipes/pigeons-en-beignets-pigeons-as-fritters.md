## Ingredients

- leftover pigeons
- flour
- white wine
- oil
- salt
- seasoning
- fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Use leftover pigeons from the table and cut them in half
2. Have them take flavor in a seasoning
3. Let them cool
4. Dip them in a batter made with flour, white wine, a spoonful of oil and salt
5. Fry them
6. Serve garnished with fried parsley
