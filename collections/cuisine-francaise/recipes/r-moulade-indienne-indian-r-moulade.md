## Ingredients

- Ten hard-boiled egg yolks
- Eight spoonfuls of oil
- Ten cloves of small chili pepper
- One teaspoonful of powder of Indian saffron or turmeric
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Four or five spoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Pound ten hard-boiled egg yolks
2. When well pounded, moisten your yolks with eight spoonfuls of oil, adding them one after another while always pounding your egg yolks
3. Add ten cloves of small chili pepper, one teaspoonful of Indian saffron or turmeric powder, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Take four or five spoonfuls of vinegar
5. Mix everything together as well as possible
6. Strain this sauce through a sieve like a purée
7. It should be somewhat thick
8. Put it in a sauce boat
