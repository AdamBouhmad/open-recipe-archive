## Ingredients

- One and a half litron of small peas
- Butter
- Parsley
- Ciboule
- One head lettuce, cut in four
- Two egg yolks
- Cream

## Directions

1. Wash the small peas and place them in a saucepan with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley and ciboule, and the head lettuce cut in four
2. Cook them in their own juices over very low heat
3. When the peas are cooked and there is almost no sauce remaining, add a liaison of two egg yolks mixed with cream
4. Allow the liaison to thicken on the fire and serve
