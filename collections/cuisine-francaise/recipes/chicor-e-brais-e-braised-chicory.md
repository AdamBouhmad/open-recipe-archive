## Ingredients

- Chicory
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Velouté sauce
- Consommé
- Croutons

## Directions

1. If the chicory yields when pressed, drain it in a colander and place it in fresh water; when well chilled, drain it again and press it in your hands to extract the water.
2. Chop the chicory.
3. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with the chicory, a little salt, and coarse pepper; stir it much and pour over it about five tablespoons of velouté and as much consommé; reduce it until it becomes somewhat thick.
4. Dress it on the plate with croutons around it.
5. If you wish to make chicory with cream or bouillon, add about two teaspoons of flour and moisten the chicory with cream or bouillon; if you have added too much liquid, reduce it.
