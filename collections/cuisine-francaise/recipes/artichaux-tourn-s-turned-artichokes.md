## Ingredients

- artichokes of good size and regular shape
- water
- salt
- butter
- bouillon
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Choose artichokes of good size and regular shape, remove the largest leaves
2. After having removed the green by cutting it uniformly all around with a knife, and turning the artichoke so that it is cut equally on all sides
3. Cut off the stalk and place on white cloth, plunge them in fresh water and remove them
4. Retire them to fresh water to remove the black
5. Cook them in blanc made with water, butter, salt, flour, the juice of half a lemon, and grain of salt
6. When all are cooked, serve them with sauce au blanc made with water, salt, and verjuice
