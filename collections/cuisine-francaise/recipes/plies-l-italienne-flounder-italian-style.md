## Ingredients

- Flounder
- Italian sauce
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the flounder
2. Cook them in good water or court-bouillon
3. Arrange them on your platter and put Italian sauce on top; that is, add a liaison of an egg yolk
