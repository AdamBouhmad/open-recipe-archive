## Ingredients

- apricots
- water
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. In their first freshness they are employed without peeling them; but afterwards, you cut them and you remove the pit; pass them through water on the fire, as for those that one wants to preserve: when they rise above and are soft, you draw them and make them cool; afterwards you let them drain, then you put them in the little clarified sugar, and you let them throw three or four boils: take care to skim them well, and if the syrup is not cooked enough, you will give them apart still four or five boils, and you will pour it over the fruit; when the apricots are cold, you will arrange them in a compote dish to serve them
