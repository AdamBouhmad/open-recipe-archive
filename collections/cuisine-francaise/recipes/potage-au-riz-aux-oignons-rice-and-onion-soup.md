## Ingredients

- onions
- a good piece of butter
- water
- salt
- fine pepper
- a quarter pound of rice

## Directions

1. Cut onions into dice, without putting in either the head or tail
2. Pass your onion in a good piece of butter
3. Let it fry until it is more than blond
4. Then moisten it with water, as much as needed for your soup
5. Season it with salt, fine pepper
6. Put a quarter pound of rice (or more according to the size of your soup) in your broth where your onion is, and let it boil for an hour and a half
7. If one wanted to avoid finding the onion, you let it cook; after half an hour you pass your broth through a sieve, and you put your rice in it to finish cooking
