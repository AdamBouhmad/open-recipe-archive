## Ingredients

- Two medium chickens
- Butter
- A hint of garlic
- Two shallots
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboule)
- Flour
- One glass of white wine
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Singe and pluck two medium chickens, split them in half down the middle of the breast, gut them and pass them in a casserole with a piece of butter, a hint of garlic, two shallots, mushrooms, parsley, chives, all chopped
2. Add a pinch of flour, moisten with a glass of white wine and as much bouillon, season with salt and black pepper, cook and reduce to short sauce; degrease before serving
