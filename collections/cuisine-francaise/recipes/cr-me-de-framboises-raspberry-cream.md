## Ingredients

- raspberries
- cream
- sugar
- two fresh egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make it the same way as strawberry cream, with the difference that when removing the cream from the fire after it is sufficiently reduced, add two fresh egg yolks that you will have previously diluted with two spoonfuls of cream
2. Put back on the fire briefly while stirring the cream only to cook the eggs without boiling, for fear it will turn
3. Finish as with the previous recipe
