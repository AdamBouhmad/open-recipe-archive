## Ingredients

- ordinary broth, about one chopine of the fattiest part
- bread crumb the size of an egg
- white meat of roasted poultry
- sweet almonds
- six hard-boiled egg yolks
- half-setier of cream or a spoonful of milk
- salt to taste
- bread crusts, finely breadcrumbed

## Directions

1. Boil the fattiest part of ordinary broth (about one chopine) for a few boils on a stovetop
2. Pound together the roasted poultry white meat, sweet almonds, and six hard-boiled egg yolks very finely in a mortar
3. Mix this pounded mixture with the broth where you have placed the bread crumb
4. Pass everything through a fine sieve, adding half-setier of cream or a spoonful of milk
5. Season to taste
6. Keep it warm in a bain-marie
7. Simmer your soup gently with well-breadcrumbed bread crusts and a little broth
8. When ready to serve, add your hot coulis to the soup without boiling it, as it would curdle
