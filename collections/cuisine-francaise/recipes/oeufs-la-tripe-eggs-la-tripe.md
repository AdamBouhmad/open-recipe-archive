## Ingredients

- 12 hard eggs
- a good piece of butter
- 12 onions
- a spoonful of flour
- 2 glasses of cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Hard boil twelve eggs
2. Cut them into slices and put them in a saucepan
3. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan
4. Cut twelve onions into slices and cook them in white in the butter
5. When they are softened, put in a spoonful of flour that you mix with the onions
6. Add two glasses of cream, salt, and pepper
7. Let the onions simmer and reduce
8. Pour them over your eggs
9. Toss them and serve well hot
