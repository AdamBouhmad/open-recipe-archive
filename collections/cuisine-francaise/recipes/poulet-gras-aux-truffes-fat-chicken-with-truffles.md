## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Truffles
- Lard for barding
- Oiled paper

## Directions

1. Lightly singe your chicken.
2. Pluck and clean it by the stomach.
3. Arrange your chicken as you would a fattened hen with truffles.
4. Place a small slice of lard between the skin of the pocket and the truffles, to prevent the skin from bursting.
5. Attach the legs to the spit and wrap it in oiled paper.
