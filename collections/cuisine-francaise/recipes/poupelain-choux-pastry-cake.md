## Ingredients

- three glasses of water
- a good pinch of salt
- butter the size of half an egg
- half a litre of flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put water, salt, and butter in a saucepan and bring to boil
2. Remove from fire and add half a litre of flour that has been thinned
3. Put back on fire, stirring constantly until the dough is thick and begins to stick to the pan
4. Transfer the dough to another pan
5. Add one egg at a time and mix in as you go
6. Continue adding eggs one by one until the dough is soft but not liquid
7. Butter a pan of the size you want to make the poupelain
8. Put your dough in to bake in the oven for an hour and a half
9. Remove from the pan and cut in half by turning the knife all around to remove the top
10. Remove all the dough inside that is not cooked
11. Fill with hot butter, rubbing with feathers or a brush
12. Sprinkle fine sugar everywhere and very finely chopped lemon zest
13. Replace the top of the poupelain on the other half
14. Rub everywhere with butter and fine sugar on top
15. Glaze with red icing
