## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons Spanish sauce
- 3 tablespoons consommé
- a handful of mushrooms
- veal stock if needed for color

## Directions

1. If your Spanish sauce is not sufficiently colored, add veal stock
2. Boil the sauce over moderate heat
3. Skim and degrease carefully so the sauce is of good color and not cloudy
4. When the sauce is reduced and thickened like clear porridge, strain through a sieve
5. Place in a saucepan and keep warm in a bain-marie for use as needed
