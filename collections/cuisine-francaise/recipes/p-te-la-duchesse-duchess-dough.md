## Ingredients

- One pint of cream
- One spoonful of orange flower water
- Two ounces of sugar
- One quarter-pound of butter
- A little salt
- Flour
- Eggs
- Icing

## Directions

1. Pour one pint of cream into a saucepan with one spoonful of orange flower water, two ounces of sugar, one quarter-pound of butter, and a little salt
2. When the cream begins to boil, add flour as described for the pouptin dough
3. Work it the same way, that is to say add eggs little by little while always kneading the dough with a wooden spoon
4. Keep it as firm as the choux dough
5. Give it whatever shape you wish by putting flour on the pastry board and rolling it to make small duchess breads
6. You can spoon them onto a baking sheet
7. Cook them after the puff pastry and glaze them
