## Ingredients

- Veal tongue
- Small skewers

## Directions

1. If it is to make a large relief course or other large piece, cut it at the second cutlet down to the joint of the knuckle
2. Remove the knuckle and the thick muscle so your piece cooks about evenly throughout
3. Secure it with small skewers that go from the flank to the filets
4. Pass the spit from the rib to the thick end near the knuckle
