## Ingredients

- rousselet pears
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook in water until tender under the fingers, the quantity of rousselet pears you judge appropriate. Remove the skin and take only the flesh which you pass through a sieve. Put it on the fire and stir constantly until it is nearly sticking to the pan. Then weigh it and put as much sugar in a pan with half a setier of water. Bring to a boil and skim. Continue boiling until dipping the skimming spoon in it and shaking it, long sparks of sugar rise up that hold together. Add the jam to blend it with the sugar on the fire. When it begins to simmer, put it in pots, and when cold, put a little fine sugar on top.
