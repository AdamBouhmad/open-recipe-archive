## Ingredients

- breadcrumbs the size of half an egg
- grated Parmesan cheese
- butter
- two raw egg yolks
- a little nutmeg
- coarse pepper
- ten eggs

## Directions

1. Put in the bottom of the serving plate breadcrumbs the size of half an egg with a little grated Parmesan cheese, butter, two raw egg yolks, a little nutmeg and coarse pepper
2. Mix everything together and spread it on the bottom of the plate
3. Brown it over a small fire, then break ten eggs over it
4. Dust the top of the eggs with grated Parmesan
5. Cook and pass a hot shovel over it
6. Serve with the yolks runny
