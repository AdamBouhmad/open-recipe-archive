## Ingredients

- one good tender hen
- its liver
- lard
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- small white onions
- bouillon
- coulis

## Directions

1. Flame, pluck and clean the hen; take its liver, chop it and mix with lard scraped with a knife, parsley, chives and mushrooms; chop everything together, season with salt and pepper, mix well this stuffing and stuff your hen with it
2. Sew it closed so the stuffing does not escape
3. Cook it on a spit, wrapped in lard and paper
4. When it is cooked, serve it with a ragout of small white onions made as follows: take small white onions and cut a little off the tip of the head and tail
5. Cook them for a quarter hour in boiling water, remove them in fresh water to remove the first skin
6. Cook them afterwards in bouillon; when they are cooked and drained, put them to take flavor in good well-seasoned coulis, making them boil a few times over a stove
7. Serve around the hen
