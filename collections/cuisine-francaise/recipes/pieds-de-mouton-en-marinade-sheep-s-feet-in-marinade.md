## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Flour
- Oil or fat for frying
- Fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your sheep's feet as those prepared in white sauce; when they are cooked, drain them, trim them, and then place them in a marinade
2. If you do not have marinade, season them with a little salt and pepper; drench them with vinegar so that they are thoroughly covered
3. At the moment of serving, drain them and place them in a frying batter; place them in hot oil: pay attention that they have a nice color; drain them; arrange them on the plate; sprinkle fried parsley on top
