## Ingredients

- white beans
- broth
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare your soup the same way as the lentil purée
2. Keep it clear and do not boil too much
3. At the moment of pouring over your crusts, add a piece of good butter which you will melt by stirring your purée as much as necessary
4. Before serving your soup, check if it is seasoned enough
