## Ingredients

- 1 pound of apricot kernels, choosing the best
- 2 pints of eau-de-vie
- 1 pint of water
- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 handful of coriander
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Infuse the apricot kernels in the eau-de-vie and water with sugar, coriander, and cinnamon for eight days
2. Pass it through the strainer until it is very clear
3. Put it in bottles
