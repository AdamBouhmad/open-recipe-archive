## Ingredients

- 8 small pots of coffee with water
- 3 ounces of sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Measure 8 small pots of coffee made with water
2. Add 3 ounces of sugar, or more if the pots are large
3. Bring to a boil and allow to cool
4. Break 6 egg yolks into a casserole and mix them with the coffee
5. Pass the mixture four times through cheesecloth
6. When the water boils, fill your small pots and place them in a bain-marie
