## Ingredients

- velouté
- two or three egg yolks
- butter the size of half an egg
- coarse pepper
- salt
- veal cut into dice
- a little butter
- carrot pieces
- three or four small onions
- one bay leaf
- two cloves
- one spoonful of flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take worked velouté and add two or three egg yolks according to the quantity of sauce you need
2. After binding it, add butter the size of half an egg after your butter has melted, stirring your sauce
3. Strain through a sieve to avoid finding any particles
4. Keep your sauce in a bain-marie
5. Add a little coarse pepper and ensure it is well salted
6. Alternatively, pass a little veal cut into dice in a saucepan with a little butter, a few pieces of carrot, three or four small onions, one bay leaf, and two cloves
7. When everything is browned, add one spoonful of flour, which you stir with what is in your saucepan
8. Then moisten with bouillon and stir your sauce until it boils
9. Once your meat is cooked, strain your sauce through a sieve and bind it as above
