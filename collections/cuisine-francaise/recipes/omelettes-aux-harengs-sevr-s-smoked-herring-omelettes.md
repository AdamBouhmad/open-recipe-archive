## Ingredients

- Smoked herrings
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Open the herrings along the back and grill them
2. Chop them and put them in the omelette as if you were adding ham
3. Do not add salt to the eggs
4. Finish this omelette as you would the others
