## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Liver and interior of the woodcock
- Chopped chives or shallots
- Two glasses of white wine
- Salt
- Fine pepper
- A few bread crusts

## Directions

1. Put your woodcock on the spit and cut it up
2. Place the pieces in a casserole
3. Chop the liver and inside of the woodcock and put it with your pieces, along with chopped chives or shallots, two glasses of white wine, salt, fine pepper, and a few bread crusts
4. Bring your salmi to a boil two or three times and serve at table
