## Ingredients

- Gudgeons
- Good butter
- Parsley
- Ciboule (scallions)
- Mushrooms
- Two shallots
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- One glass of red wine

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the gudgeons, then dry without washing
2. In the serving dish, place good butter at the bottom with finely chopped parsley, ciboule, mushrooms, two shallots, thyme, laurel, and basil
3. Add salt and coarse pepper
4. Arrange the gudgeons on top and season them above and below
5. Moisten with a glass of red wine
6. Cover the dish and boil on good heat until only a little sauce remains
7. Cook for about fifteen minutes
