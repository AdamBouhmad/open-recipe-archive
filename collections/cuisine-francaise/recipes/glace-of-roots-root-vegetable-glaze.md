## Ingredients

- Vegetables: carrots, turnips, onions (more of the latter two than carrots)
- Four or five cloves (according to quantity)
- Veal (optional)
- Bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Fill three-quarters of a casserole with vegetables such as carrots, turnips, and onions
2. Use four or five cloves according to quantity, using more of the last two vegetables than carrots
3. Add veal if you wish
4. Moisten everything with bouillon or water
5. Cook your vegetables on a low fire
6. Use the same procedure as for the glaze above
