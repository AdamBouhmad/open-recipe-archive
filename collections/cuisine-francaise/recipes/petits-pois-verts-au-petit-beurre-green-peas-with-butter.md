## Ingredients

- Peas
- Butter
- Water
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Sugar
- Parsley
- Chives

## Directions

1. Take two liters of peas.
2. Put a quarter pound of butter in them.
3. Sprinkle with water and knead them together with your hands.
4. Let them drain in a strainer.
5. Put them in a saucepan over a hot fire.
6. Sauté the peas when they have felt the heat well.
7. Moisten them with boiling water.
8. Add salt, black pepper, the equivalent of half a walnut of sugar, a bouquet of parsley and chives.
9. Reduce the liquid until there is almost none left.
10. At the moment of serving, when the peas are boiling, add three small pieces of butter, or about the size of two eggs of butter.
11. Sauté them without putting them on the fire until they are well bound.
12. Arrange them in a bush shape; check that they have good salt.
