## Ingredients

- apricots
- 3 quarterons of sugar per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Peel the apricots if you wish and remove the pits
2. Clarify the sugar as explained previously
3. Cook the sugar to the large ball stage, which you will know by putting your skimming spoon in the sugar and removing it, then blowing on it - you will see the sugar fly, which marks that it is at the right cooking point
4. Add the apricots and boil, stirring constantly with a wooden spatula, until the marmalade is sticky to your fingers
5. This is a sign that you can put it immediately into jars
