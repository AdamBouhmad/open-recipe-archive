## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- lard and anchovy fillets (optional for larding)
- one chopine of water
- one chopine of broth
- parsley
- chives
- half a clove of garlic
- three shallots
- two cloves
- two onions
- one carrot
- one parsnip
- coulis (optional)

## Directions

1. Trim the shank of the leg by cutting the end so it is not so long
2. Lard the meat if desired with lard and some anchovy fillets; if you leave it without, tie it with string
3. Place it in a pot just fitted to its size with one chopine of water and as much broth
4. Bring to a boil and skim
5. Add a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, three shallots, two cloves, two onions, one carrot, and one parsnip
6. When the leg is cooked, pass the broth through a sieve and remove the fat
7. Place it on the fire to reduce it until it becomes a glaze like a fricandeau
8. Put this glaze on top of the leg
9. Then put a few spoonfuls of broth in the pot to detach what remains
10. If you have some coulis, use it in place of the broth
11. Pass it through a sieve and serve under the leg
