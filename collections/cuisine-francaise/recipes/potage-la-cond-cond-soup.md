## Ingredients

- one liter of red beans
- broth
- two carrots
- two onions
- pot fat
- two cloves
- crusts passed in butter

## Directions

1. Cook the beans with broth
2. Add two carrots, two onions, a little pot fat, and two cloves
3. When cooked, pass through a sieve
4. Make a clear purée
5. Pour over crusts that have been passed in butter, that is to say fried
