## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Sirup (made as for plum preserves)
- One pint of eau-de-vie per hundred apricots

## Directions

1. Preserve the apricots in the same manner as explained for plum preserves
2. Place them on the fire with the sirup
3. When they boil, throw in one pint of eau-de-vie to make a broth and remove them, placing them in pots
4. Observe that only one pint per hundred is needed
5. When adding the eau-de-vie to the apricots, pull the pan off the fire because fire could ignite it; if this happens, have a white cloth ready, wet it and cover your pan, and the fire will extinguish, but try to prevent this from happening
