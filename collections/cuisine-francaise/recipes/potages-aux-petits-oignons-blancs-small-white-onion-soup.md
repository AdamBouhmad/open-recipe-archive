## Ingredients

- small white onions
- bread soaked in egg white

## Directions

1. Blanch the onions, remove their first skin
2. Cook them separately in a small pot
3. When they are cooked, arrange them in a border around the edge of the platter where you will serve your soup
4. To make them hold, place on the edges of the platter a strip of bread dipped in egg white
5. Heat the platter for a moment over a stove so the bread attaches
6. Use these strips to help hold all sorts of soup garnishes in place
