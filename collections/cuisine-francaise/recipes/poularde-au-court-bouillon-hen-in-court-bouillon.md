## Ingredients

- one good hen
- butter
- two onions
- one root
- one parsnip
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- one clove of garlic
- three cloves
- two shallots
- salt
- coarse pepper
- two glasses of bouillon
- one glass of white wine
- one spoonful of verjus

## Directions

1. Flame the hen, heat the legs to remove the skin, cut the claws in half, clean it and truss the legs by making them enter into the body
2. Tie the hen and put it in a pot just its size with a piece of butter, two onions in slices, a root, a parsnip, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, three cloves, two shallots, salt, coarse pepper
3. Moisten with two glasses of bouillon, one glass of white wine, one spoonful of verjus
4. Cook on low heat
5. When your hen bends under the finger, pass all the court-bouillon through a sieve, reduce it over the fire to the consistency of a sauce
6. Serve it over the hen
