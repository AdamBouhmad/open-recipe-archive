## Ingredients

- six large carrots
- four large turnips
- six large onions
- three celery stalks
- four leeks
- butter
- sugar
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Slice six large carrots, four large turnips, six large onions, three celery stalks, four leeks (more if your soup is strong)
2. Put a piece of butter and a piece of sugar about the size of half an egg in your pot with your vegetables
3. Pass them white, that is do not let them take color by softening them on the fire
4. When you see they want to take color, moisten them with bouillon and braise for two hours on the fire
5. Pass them through cheesecloth
6. Then clarify your purée
7. Try to ensure it is not too thick
8. Afterward dip your bread with good bouillon
