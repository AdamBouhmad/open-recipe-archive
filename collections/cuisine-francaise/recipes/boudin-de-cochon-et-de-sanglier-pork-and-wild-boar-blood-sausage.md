## Ingredients

- Onion
- Water
- Pork belly fat (panne)
- Blood
- Cream, one-quarter part
- Salt
- Mixed spices
- Intestines (boyaux)

## Directions

1. Chop the onion and cook it with a little water and pork belly fat
2. When well cooked and only fat remains, cut pork belly fat into dice and put it in the pan with the onion
3. Add blood and one-quarter cream, season with fine salt and mixed spices
4. Mix everything together well and stuff into intestines that you have cut beforehand to the length you wish to make the sausages
5. Do not fill them too full for fear they burst while cooking
6. Tie the two ends of each intestine
7. Cook them in boiling water for a quarter hour to cook them
8. To see if they are cooked, draw one out with a skimmer and prick it with a pin; if no more blood comes out, only fat, this is proof they are cooked
9. Set them to cool to grill them when you wish to serve them
10. The same method is practiced for wild boar sausage
