## Ingredients

- ten to twelve turkey wings or fifteen hen wings
- small lardons
- water

## Directions

1. Scald and pluck the wings
2. Lard them with small lardons
3. Blanch them briefly in boiling water
4. Cook them as fricandeaux à la bourgeoise, as described on page 118
