## Ingredients

- 3 quarterons of orange blossoms
- 3 chopines of water
- 2 pints of eau-de-vie
- 1 pound and a half of sugar

## Directions

1. Put the orange blossoms, water, eau-de-vie, and sugar in a well-corked jug
2. Put this jug in a pot full of water and bring to a boil over the fire for ten hours
3. Remove from the fire and let it cool in the jug before passing it clear
