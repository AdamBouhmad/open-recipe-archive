## Ingredients

- artichokes
- pepper
- five spoonfuls of velouté sauce
- six spoonfuls of consommé
- butter, the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Trim the heart of the artichokes; if they are large, cut them into eight pieces
2. Trim the leaves and remove the choke
3. Place the pieces in water, drain them, and arrange them in a pot
4. Add pepper, five spoonfuls of velouté sauce, six spoonfuls of consommé, and butter the size of an egg
5. Half an hour before serving, place them on the fire to boil vigorously
6. Place fire on the pot cover
7. Arrange the artichokes on the plate when cooked
8. If you have no sauce, make a light roux, moisten it with broth, and pour it over the artichokes
