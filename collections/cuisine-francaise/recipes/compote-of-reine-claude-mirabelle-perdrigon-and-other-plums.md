## Ingredients

- plums
- water
- clarified sugar or sugar

## Directions

1. Boil water and throw your plums in it to blanch them; when they are very soft under your fingers, remove them with a skimming spoon and put them in fresh water
2. Put them then in a pan with a little clarified sugar, or not, if you have none
3. Put them on a small fire so they can shimmer and become well green, and serve cold
