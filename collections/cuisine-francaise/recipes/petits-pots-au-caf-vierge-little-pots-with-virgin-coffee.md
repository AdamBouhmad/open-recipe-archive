## Ingredients

- 10 small pots of cream
- 1 quarteron of sugar
- 2 ounces of coffee
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Measure out 10 small pots of cream and bring to a boil
2. Add 1 quarteron of sugar and keep the cream hot
3. Toast 2 ounces of coffee until blonde
4. Place the coffee directly from the pan into the cream and immediately cover with a casserole lid
5. Allow the cream to cool
6. Pass the cream through a sieve to remove the coffee grounds
7. In a casserole, beat 6 egg yolks and mix them with your cream
8. Pass the entire mixture four times through cheesecloth
9. Bring water to a boil and pour the cream into 7 small pots
10. Place them in a bain-marie to set
