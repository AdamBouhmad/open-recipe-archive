## Ingredients

- beef brains
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet garni
- small onions
- root vegetables
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- garlic
- parsley
- chives
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil

## Directions

1. Braise the brains with white wine, salt, pepper, and a bouquet garni
2. When cooked, remove from the braise and serve with an appetizing small sauce from chapter XV, or with a ragout of small onions and root vegetables
3. Alternatively, marinate with salt, pepper, vinegar, a piece of butter mixed with flour, garlic, parsley, chives, thyme, bay leaf, and basil
4. Drain and flour the brains, then fry them
5. Serve garnished with fried parsley
