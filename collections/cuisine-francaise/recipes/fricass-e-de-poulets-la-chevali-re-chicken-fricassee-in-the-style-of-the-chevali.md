## Ingredients

- Two fat, well-fleshed chickens
- Butter
- Flour
- Hot bouillon
- Velouté (four degreasings spoons)
- Bay leaf
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Coarse pepper
- Small onions
- Three egg yolks (for the liaison)
- Lard

## Directions

1. Flame the chickens lightly
2. Lift the breasts entirely, lard them finely, and cook them separately in a saucepan to glaze them
3. Lift the other members as correctly as possible
4. Break the thigh bones to the joint and cut the large end at the side of the leg
5. Trim well the kidneys and wings; in a word, cut your pieces neatly
6. Put a piece of softened butter in a saucepan
7. Arrange the members so they take a beautiful form
8. Place your saucepan on the fire
9. Leave your fricassee an instant without sautéing so your thighs take a form they can maintain while cooking
10. Sauté them until they are stiffened
11. Add a little flour which you mix with your chickens and butter
12. Moisten your fricassee with hot bouillon
13. Add four degreasings spoons of velouté, a bay leaf, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and coarse pepper
14. Let it go to a high fire
15. Three-quarters cooked, add your small onions, all equal and well cleaned
16. Degrease your fricassee
17. When it is cooked, at the moment of serving, remove the bouquet and bay leaf
18. Change it to another saucepan
19. Pass the sauce through cheesecloth over it
20. Add a liaison of three egg yolks
21. Dress it on your platter between your four thighs
22. Put the four larded glazed fillets and the garnish in the middle
