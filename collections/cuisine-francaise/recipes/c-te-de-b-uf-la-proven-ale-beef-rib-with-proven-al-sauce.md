## Ingredients

- beef rib
- oil
- twenty large onions
- one and a half quarteron of oil
- vinegar
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Trim and cook your rib as you would cook it for a good wife preparation, but use oil instead of butter for cooking
2. When it is cooked, place it on your serving dish
3. Take twenty large onions and cut them in half from the top of the head to the tail
4. Cut your onion halves into slices so that they form semicircles
5. Put one and a half quarteron of oil in your pot and heat it well
6. Place your onions in to fry; when they are well browned, pour in a glass of vinegar, a little bouillon, salt, and pepper
7. Cover your rib with your onions; do not skim the fat
