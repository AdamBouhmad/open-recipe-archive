## Ingredients

- Lamb
- Skewers
- String or needle for trussing
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Cut at the second cutlet near the saddle, as square as possible
2. Secure the flanks with skewers
3. Remove the thick muscle bone
4. Beat your leg
5. Pass the spit near the handle down to the ribs with a trussing needle
6. Fix your piece of rack on the spit
7. If you serve it as a relief course, put clear jus underneath
