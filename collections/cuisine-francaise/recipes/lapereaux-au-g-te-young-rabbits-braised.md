## Ingredients

- Two young rabbits
- Their livers
- A piece of butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A glass of white wine
- Broth
- A bouquet garni
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Stuff the two young rabbits with their livers, a piece of butter, parsley, chives, and mushrooms (all chopped), salt, and coarse pepper
2. Sew them and truss the hind legs under the belly and the front legs under the nose
3. Insert skewers to hold them in place
4. Cook them with a glass of white wine, broth, a bouquet garni, salt, and coarse pepper
5. When they are cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve and degrease it
6. Add a little coulis and reduce to the consistency of a sauce
7. Dress the sauce over the rabbits as if they were au gîte.
