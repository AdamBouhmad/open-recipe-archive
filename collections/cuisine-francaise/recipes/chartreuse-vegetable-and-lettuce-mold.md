## Ingredients

- roots for vegetables
- lettuce
- cooking liquid from roots
- root bouillon
- demi-glace
- meat glaze

## Directions

1. Prepare roots and cook them as described for entrées.
2. Dress them the same way, or you can change the design.
3. Instead of putting meat inside, use lettuces that you have carefully drained and pressed to extract all liquid.
4. When your mold is full, keep it hot in a bain-marie.
5. Take the cooking liquid in which your roots have cooked.
6. Add root bouillon to it.
7. When it boils, pass it through a fine cloth so it is clear.
8. Reduce it to a demi-glace and pour it over the chartreuse that you have placed on your dish.
9. If your demi-glace is not sufficiently thick, add a little well-blonde meat glaze to it.
