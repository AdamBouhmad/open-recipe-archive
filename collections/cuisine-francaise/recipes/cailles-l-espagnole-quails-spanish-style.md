## Ingredients

- eight fresh quails
- butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bacon strips
- needle and coarse thread
- white wine
- bouillon
- croutons
- espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Have eight very fresh quails that you clean through the cavity to avoid a large incision near the rump
2. Work a piece of butter in which you put lemon juice, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Put as much as will fit in your quails
4. Fasten the thighs with a needle and coarse thread
5. Leave the legs free
6. Give them an agreeable shape by trussing them
7. Put strips of bacon in a saucepan, your quails on top of them
8. Cover them also with strips of bacon and put a pan moistened with half white wine and half bouillon on top
9. A good half hour before serving, put them over heat
10. When removing them, drain them and remove the trussing strings
11. Have croutons the size of your quails and place them on top
12. Put an espagnole sauce
