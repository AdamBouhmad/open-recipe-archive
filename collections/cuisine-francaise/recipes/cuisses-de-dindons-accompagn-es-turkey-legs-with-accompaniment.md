## Ingredients

- calf's sweetbreads
- many mushrooms
- lard, parsley, chives, basil, shallots
- salt, coarse pepper
- two egg yolks
- two raw turkey legs
- one glass of white wine
- good broth
- a bouquet of parsley, chives
- lard strips
- coulis or beurre manié with flour
- fresh parsley
- lemon juice or verjuice

## Directions

1. Blanch the calf's sweetbreads in boiling water and cut them into large cubes with many mushrooms also cut into cubes
2. Combine them with grated lard, chopped parsley, chives, basil, shallots, salt, coarse pepper, and two egg yolks
3. Take two raw, well-cleaned turkey legs and bone them completely except for the end bone attached to the leg
4. Fill the legs with the sweetbread mixture and sew them shut so nothing escapes
5. Cook them in a braise made with one glass of white wine and an equal amount of good broth, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and a little salt
6. Cover with lard strips and cook over low heat
7. When cooked and little sauce remains, degrease it, remove the lard strips and bouquet
8. Add two spoonfuls of coulis to bind the sauce, or if you have none, add a piece of butter worked with a pinch of flour and a little blanched chopped parsley
9. Thicken over the fire and serve on the legs with lemon juice or verjuice
