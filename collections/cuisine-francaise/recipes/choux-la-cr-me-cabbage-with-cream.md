## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. After washing the cabbage, slice it thinly and blanch it.
2. Put a handful of salt in water; when the cabbage yields under the fingers, refresh it and press it as you would chicory.
3. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan.
