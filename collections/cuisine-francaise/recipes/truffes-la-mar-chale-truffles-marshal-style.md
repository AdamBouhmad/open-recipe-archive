## Ingredients

- Beautiful truffles, well washed and brushed
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Paper

## Directions

1. Take beautiful truffles well washed and brushed
2. Season four truffles with salt and coarse pepper
3. Wrap them in several pieces of paper
4. Place in a small pot without any liquid
5. Cook in hot ashes for about one hour
6. Serve them hot in their natural state
