## Ingredients

- One chopine of cream
- Two ounces of sugar
- One half coffee spoon of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Place on the fire one chopine of cream with two ounces of sugar
2. When it boils, remove it to cool
3. Then add one half coffee spoon of orange flower water
4. Whip the cream with a wooden whisk, and as the foam becomes somewhat thick, remove it with a skimming spoon and place it in a basket lined with fine cloth
5. Continue whipping until you have no more cream
6. Let the cheese drain and serve in a serving dish or on a plate
