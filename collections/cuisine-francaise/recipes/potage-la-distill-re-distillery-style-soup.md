## Ingredients

- Bread
- Butter
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut your bread as for the Monaco soup
2. Pass your bread pieces through butter as explained in the crouton soup
3. Put the number of croutons needed so that your soup is not too thick
4. At the moment of serving, pour your bound milk over your croutons
5. Take care that your milk is neither too much nor too little sweetened; add five or six grains of salt
