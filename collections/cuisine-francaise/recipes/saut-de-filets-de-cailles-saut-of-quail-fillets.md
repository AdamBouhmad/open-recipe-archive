## Ingredients

- Quail fillets
- Butter
- Croutons
- Spanish sauce
- Meat glaze

## Directions

1. Take the fillets of twelve quail, pare them, and arrange them in a sauté pan.
2. Make a piece of butter lukewarm and pour it over your fillets.
3. At the moment of serving, put them over a hot fire; four or five minutes are sufficient to cook them.
4. Drain them and arrange them in crowns on your plat with a glazed crouton between each fillet.
5. Make a circle around the plat, then in the interior.
6. If you serve them as an appetizer, six quail are sufficient.
7. Sauce them with a clear Spanish sauce in which you put a glace about the size of half a walnut.
