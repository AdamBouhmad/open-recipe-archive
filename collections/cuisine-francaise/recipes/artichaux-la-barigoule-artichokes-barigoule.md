## Ingredients

- artichokes
- bouillon or water
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- roots
- bouquet garni
- vinegar
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Take three or four artichokes according to their size or the size of your side dish plate
2. Cut the green from below and half the leaves
3. Put them in a pan with bouillon or water, two spoonfuls of good oil, a little salt and pepper, an onion, two roots, a bouquet garni
4. Cook them and reduce the sauce entirely
5. When they are cooked and there is no more sauce, let them fry a moment in the oil to brown
6. Put them after on a pie dish with the oil that remains in the pan
7. Empty them of their choke and put on top a hot pie dish cover
8. Put fire on the cover to make the leaves grill
9. If you have a hot oven, they will only be more beautiful
10. When they are grilled a beautiful color, serve them with a sauce of oil, vinegar, salt and coarse pepper
