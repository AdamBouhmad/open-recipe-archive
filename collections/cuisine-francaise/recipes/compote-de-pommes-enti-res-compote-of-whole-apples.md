## Ingredients

- Seven fine reinette apples
- Water
- Lemon juice
- One half-pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Using an apple corer, remove the core and peel from the apples
2. Place them in water and lemon juice
3. Clarify one half-pound of sugar and make a syrup that is somewhat thin
4. Add the apples to the syrup
5. Cook them at a gentle boil
6. Stir them often
7. When a fork easily pierces them, remove them and place on a serving dish
8. Reduce the syrup and pour it under the apples
