## Ingredients

- good bouillon
- half pound of lazagnes (more or less depending on the size of your soup)
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. When your bouillon is in your saucepan and boiling, add half pound of lazagnes
2. Boil for three quarters of an hour, or a bit more, until your lazagnes are sufficiently swollen but not too much, as your soup would not be distinguished and would turn to paste
3. At the moment of pouring them into your tureen, add a little coarse pepper
4. Alternatively, you can blanch them in salted water without letting them cook too long; then put them in the bouillon
