## Ingredients

- One very fresh cod
- Fine oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Whole chives
- One clove of garlic
- One bay leaf
- An iron skewer called a hatelet
- Carp roes

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fresh cod.
2. Cisele and dry it.
3. Marinate it for two hours with fine oil, salt, pepper, parsley and whole chives, one clove of garlic, and one bay leaf.
4. Take an iron skewer called a hatelet and pass it through the cod starting at the eyes, through the middle of the body, and finishing at the tail, making it take the shape of a dolphin.
5. Put it on a pie dish and baste it with its marinade.
6. Cook it in the oven.
7. When it is cooked, remove the hatelet and arrange it on the plate you will serve it on.
8. Serve with a ragoût made as follows: boil three carp roes for a moment in water.
