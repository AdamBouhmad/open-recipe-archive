## Ingredients

- one litron of flour
- six egg yolks
- two tablespoons of oil
- one piece of butter the size of an egg
- salt
- pepper
- one glass of beer
- two egg whites

## Directions

1. Put one litron of flour in a bowl
2. Add six egg yolks, two tablespoons of oil, and one piece of butter the size of an egg that you will warm so it mixes with the batter
3. Add salt, pepper, and one glass of beer
4. Dilute your batter so there are no lumps
5. Add more butter if your batter is too thick, but take care that it is not too thin
6. It should run from the spoon when you let it fall
7. Beat two egg whites as for biscuit and mix them with your batter
8. Use it as needed
9. You can moisten your batter with white wine or milk
