## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- sauce à papillotes
- thin slices of lard
- oiled paper

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue as you would for cornichons
2. When the tongue is cooked and cold, cut it into small long squares of four inches by eight or ten lines
3. Arrange the pieces on a plate and cover with sauce à papillotes
4. When the sauce is cooled, take a piece of tongue, surround it with sauce and cover it with a very thin slice of lard
5. Take a square of oiled paper and wrap the piece of tongue in it so that it has the shape of a cartouche
6. Grill it over a gentle fire
7. Arrange on the plate in a roof shape or in a pile
8. Do not use sauce; be careful to seal the paper hermetically so the sauce does not escape
