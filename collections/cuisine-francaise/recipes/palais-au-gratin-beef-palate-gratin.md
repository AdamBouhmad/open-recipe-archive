## Ingredients

- beef palate, cooked in blanc
- cooked farce
- veal teat, well cooked and thin
- lard
- italienne sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked beef palate into long bands fifteen lines wide
2. Spread cooked farce on the underside of the palate (the side without a raised edge)
3. Place a thin band of well-cooked veal teat on the farce, add a little more farce on top
4. Roll the band and arrange it around the inside of the dish where you have placed farce
5. Form a cordon of farce in the bottom that makes a turban shape
6. Cover the palate with lard so that the heat from the oven or field oven does not roast the palate
7. After approximately fifteen minutes to a half hour in the oven, remove the fat
8. Pour italienne sauce into the middle
9. Optionally, place scarlet tongue between each piece of beef palate to form a crest; these would be called palais à la Saint-Garat
