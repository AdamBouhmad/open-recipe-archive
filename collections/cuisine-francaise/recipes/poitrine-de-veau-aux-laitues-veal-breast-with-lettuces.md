## Ingredients

- veal breast
- needle for trussing
- lard bardes
- carrots, sliced
- onions
- two bay leaves
- parsley and chives bouquet
- veal breast trimmings
- lard for covering
- bouillon
- salt
- lettuces
- espagnole sauce or light roux

## Directions

1. Prepare your breast as the previous one
2. Truss it the same way
3. Put lard bardes in the bottom of your pan, sliced carrots, onions, two bay leaves, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and the trimmings from your breast
4. Cover it with lard
5. Moisten it with bouillon and a little salt
6. Cook it over a gentle fire for two and a half hours
7. At the moment of serving, drain it, untie it, glaze it, and place it on your plat
8. Also arrange a cordon of lettuces around it, which you will glaze
9. Have a prepared espagnole sauce which you will place under it
10. If you do not have sauce, make a light roux which you will moisten with the cooking liquid from your breast
11. Make the sauce loose so you can make it proper for serving
