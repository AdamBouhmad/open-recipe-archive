## Ingredients

- About three spoonfuls of flour
- Six eggs, whites and yolks
- A bit of grated lemon peel
- Pralined orange flower, chopped
- One chopine of milk
- A piece of sugar
- A small pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put about three spoonfuls of flour in a saucepan and dilute it little by little with six eggs, whites and yolks, a bit of grated lemon peel, pralined and chopped orange flower, one chopine of milk, a piece of sugar, and a small pinch of salt.
2. Cook it on a low fire for half an hour, stirring constantly.
3. When it is very thick, spread it to the thickness of half a finger on a floured dish and sprinkle flour over it.
4. When it is cold, cut it as you wish to fry it in very hot oil.
5. Then glaze it with sugar and a hot poker.
