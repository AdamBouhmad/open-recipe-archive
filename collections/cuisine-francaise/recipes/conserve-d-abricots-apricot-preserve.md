## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 15 to 18 apricots
- water

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of sugar in the same way as the violet preserve
2. Make apricot marmalade: take 15 to 18 apricots not quite ripe, remove the pits and skins, cut them into pieces, and cook them with a little water until they become a well-dried and thick marmalade
3. When the sugar is at its point, add a quarter pound of apricot marmalade
4. Put the marmalade in the sugar and finish the preserve as with the redcurrant preserve
