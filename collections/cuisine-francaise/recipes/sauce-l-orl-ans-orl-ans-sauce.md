## Ingredients

- Three or four small spoonfuls of vinegar
- A little fine pepper
- A little shallots
- Butter, amount of half an egg
- Brown sauce, worked
- Four or five gherkins
- Three hard-boiled egg whites
- Four or five anchovies
- One cooked calf's head or sweetbread
- One spoonful of whole capers

## Directions

1. Put vinegar, fine pepper, shallots, and butter in a saucepan
2. Reduce everything together
3. Pour in four or five tablespoons of worked brown sauce
4. At the moment of serving, cut four or five gherkins into dice
5. Cut three hard-boiled egg whites in the same manner
6. Divide four or five anchovies in half to remove the bone, then cut into small squares
7. Cut a cooked calf's head or sweetbread into dice the same size as the gherkins
8. Add one spoonful of whole capers
9. At the moment of serving, put all this in your sauce and place it on the fire for an instant; it must not boil
10. You can also make this sauce with a small roux, which you moisten with a cooking stock or bouillon; season your sauce as before and add the same ingredients
