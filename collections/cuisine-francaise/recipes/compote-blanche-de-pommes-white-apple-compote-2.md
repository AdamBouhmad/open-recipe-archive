## Ingredients

- six large Reinette apples
- fresh water
- juice of half a lemon
- a piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut six large Reinette apples in half
2. Remove the skin and seeds, throwing them into fresh water as you work
3. Cook them with a large glass of water, the juice of half a lemon, and a piece of sugar
4. When the apples are cooked, arrange them in a compote dish
5. Reduce the syrup until it sticks to your fingers
6. Dress the syrup over the apples
