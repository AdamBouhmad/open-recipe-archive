## Ingredients

- one blonde calf's liver
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- shallot
- a hint of garlic
- a pinch of flour
- half a septier of wine
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a blonde liver, remove the nerves, and cut it into slices the thickness of a finger
2. Melt butter in a pan and cook the liver pieces seasoned with salt and pepper
3. When cooked on one side, turn them to cook on the other
4. Remove from the pan
5. In the same butter, cook parsley, chives, shallot, a hint of garlic, all chopped together
6. Add a pinch of flour and moisten with half a septier of wine
7. Bring the sauce to a boil briefly
8. Serve with a hint of vinegar
