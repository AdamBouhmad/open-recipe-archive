## Ingredients

- 3 large quinces (or more if small)
- 1 quarter pound sugar
- 1 half-glass water

## Directions

1. Put quinces in boiling water to cook until tender under the fingers
2. Put them after in cold water
3. Cut them in four
4. Remove the cores and peel them cleanly
5. Put a quarter pound of sugar in a pan with a half-glass of water
6. Bring to boil and skim
7. Add the quinces to finish cooking them
8. Serve hot with light syrup
