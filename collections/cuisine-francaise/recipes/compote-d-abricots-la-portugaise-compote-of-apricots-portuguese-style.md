## Ingredients

- apricots that are almost ripe
- sugar
- half a glass of water

## Directions

1. Take the quantity of apricots you wish
2. Split them in half and remove the pits
3. Put sugar in the bottom of a dish with half a glass of water
4. Arrange the apricots on top
5. Place them on a moderate fire to boil until they are almost cooked underneath and there is almost no syrup remaining
6. Remove them from the fire and sprinkle fine sugar over them
7. Cover them with a tourtière lid and put fire on top until they are cooked and of a beautiful glazed color
8. Dress them in the compote dish while they are hot
