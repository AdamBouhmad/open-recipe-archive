## Ingredients

- Asparagus tips
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut asparagus tips and blanch them
2. Add them to the eggs
3. Let them set
4. Arrange on a plate and arrange asparagus tips around it
5. You may also add peas, small cucumbers, chopped cauliflower, or other vegetables
