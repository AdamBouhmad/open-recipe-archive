## Ingredients

- Cabbage, cut into quarters
- A piece of small bacon with rind
- Meat (tendons of veal, beef breast, beef rump, pork andouille, boned and rounded lamb shoulder, or capon)
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- Cloves
- A little nutmeg
- Two or three roots
- Good coulis sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into quarters and wash them
2. Boil them for a quarter hour in water with a piece of small bacon cut into pieces with rind attached
3. Remove them into fresh water, press them well and tie them up
4. Boil the meat for two minutes in water to remove its scum
5. Cook the meat and cabbage together in a braise made of bouillon, salt, pepper, a bouquet of parsley, chives, cloves, a little nutmeg, and two or three roots
6. When the meat and cabbage are cooked, remove them and wipe them well of their fat
7. Arrange them on the serving plate with the bacon on top
8. Cover with a sauce made of good coulis and well seasoned
