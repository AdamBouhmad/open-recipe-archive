## Ingredients

- Chervil
- Chives
- Salad burnet
- Tarragon
- Velouté
- Two spoonfuls of vinegar
- A little coarse pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Chop the chervil, chives, salad burnet, and tarragon finely, with the tarragon dominating
2. In velouté, add two spoonfuls of vinegar and a little coarse pepper
3. Keep your sauce hot
4. At the moment of serving, add your chopped ravigote with a small piece of fine butter
5. Stir your sauce to melt the butter and mix the herbs together
6. Ensure the sauce is well seasoned
