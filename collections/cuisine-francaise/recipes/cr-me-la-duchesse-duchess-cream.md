## Ingredients

- one chopine of milk
- one half-setier of cream
- a small piece of cinnamon
- the zest of green lemon
- one half-quarteron of sugar
- six egg yolks
- a pinch of flour
- a few bitter almond biscuits
- one half-tablet of chocolate
- a little pralined orange blossom

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one chopine of milk with one half-setier of cream, a small piece of cinnamon, the zest of green lemon, one half-quarteron of sugar
2. Bring to boil for half an hour, and reduce by a third
3. Pass it through a sieve and then dilute it with six egg yolks and a pinch of flour
4. Add a few bitter almond biscuits, one half-tablet of chocolate, a little pralined orange blossom, all chopped very fine
5. Cook it in a bain-marie as the coffee one
