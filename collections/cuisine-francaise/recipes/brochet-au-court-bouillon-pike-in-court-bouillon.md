## Ingredients

- Pike
- Court-bouillon

## Directions

1. Clean the pike without making an opening
2. Tie the head
3. Put it in the fish kettle
4. Pour court-bouillon over it
5. Simmer for an hour or longer if your fish is large
6. If you serve it as a roast, let it cool; arrange a napkin on a platter and place your pike on it, with parsley around it
