## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- broth
- water
- parsley
- chives
- one clove of garlic
- three cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- two onions
- two carrots
- one parsnip
- salt
- pepper
- cornichons

## Directions

1. Place a leg of mutton to cook in a pot just fitted to its size with a little broth and water, a bunch of parsley, chives, one clove of garlic, three cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, two onions, two carrots, one parsnip, salt, and pepper
2. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve and remove the fat
3. Reduce the sauce to a glaze
4. Put it all over the leg
5. Serve underneath a ragout of cornichons made by cutting cornichons into two or three pieces according to their size, scrape them a little and blanch them for a moment in boiling water to remove the strength of the vinegar, and put them in a bound sauce
