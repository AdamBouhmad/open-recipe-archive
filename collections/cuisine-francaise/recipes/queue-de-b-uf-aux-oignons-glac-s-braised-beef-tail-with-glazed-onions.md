## Ingredients

- Beef tail
- Glazed onions
- Espagnole sauce
- Roux
- Cooking liquid from beef tail
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook your beef tail in a braise
2. Drain it, arrange it on your platter in a pyramid shape, and arrange glazed onions around it (see Glazed Onions)
3. Add a prepared espagnole sauce
4. If you lack espagnole, make a light roux and moisten it with the cooking liquid, passed through a silk sieve, in which your beef tail cooked
5. Make your sauce clear so you can reduce it
6. Season it with good flavor and salt
7. Add a little pepper, and sauce your beef tail with it
8. Take care to degrease it
