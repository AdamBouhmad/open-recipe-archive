## Ingredients

- 8 partridges
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 clove
- 3 shallots
- sage
- 2 large glasses of bouillon
- eggs or poached eggs
- croutons fried in oil

## Directions

1. Spit-roast 8 partridges; when cooked, let them cool
2. Remove the fillets and chop them; place in a saucepan
3. Make a light roux and add the livers and lungs of your partridges, 1 bay leaf, 1 clove, 3 shallots, and a little sage
4. Brown the whole with your roux; add 2 large glasses of bouillon
5. Reduce the sauce to half; strain it through fine cloth without pressing
6. Add sauce to your hash until it is thick and bound; keep it warm without boiling
7. At service, arrange croutons around your platter and put your hash in the center
8. Add around it soft-boiled eggs or poached eggs, placing a round crouton fried in oil between each egg
