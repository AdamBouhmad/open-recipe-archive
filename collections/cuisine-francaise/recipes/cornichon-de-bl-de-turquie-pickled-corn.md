## Ingredients

- Corn still in the kernel and green
- Water
- Vinegar (one third the amount of water)
- Cloves
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take corn that is still in the kernel and completely green.
2. Cook it halfway in water.
3. Remove it and refresh it in fresh water.
4. Then put it to pickle: boil water with a third vinegar, some cloves and salt.
5. Pour the boiling brine over the corn and place it on hot ashes.
6. The next day, make the same brine again and pour it over the corn.
7. Continue this way until it is very green.
8. Then cover your pots and store them to use the same way as gherkins.
