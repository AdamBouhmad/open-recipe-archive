## Ingredients

- salsify, cooked and drained
- white sauce, somewhat enhanced
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- frying batter

## Directions

1. Once the salsify is cooked, drain it
2. Make a white sauce that is somewhat enhanced
3. Sauté the salsify in it and let it cool on a plate
4. At the moment of serving, dip them in frying batter and fry them
5. Alternatively, when drained, place them in a terrine with a little salt, pepper, and vinegar and let them marinate briefly before putting them in the batter
