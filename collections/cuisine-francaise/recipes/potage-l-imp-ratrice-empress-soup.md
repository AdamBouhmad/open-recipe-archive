## Ingredients

- Three or four chickens
- Two large spoonfuls of rice cooked for a quarter hour in boiling water
- Good consommé

## Directions

1. Roast three or four chickens on a spit
2. When cooked, let them cool
3. Remove the meat and pound it with rice that has cooked for a quarter hour in boiling water
4. When the chicken breast and rice are well pounded together, thin your purée with good consommé and pass through a sieve
5. When your purée is passed, moisten it with consommé so it is neither too thin nor too thick
6. Prepare crusts as for bread soup and moisten with boiling consommé a quarter hour before serving
7. In your consommé put your poultry scraps and let them simmer on a low fire for two hours
8. Pass your bouillon through a fine cloth or silk sieve
9. Ensure your bouillon is not too amber-colored, that is, not too dark in color
10. Pour your purée at the moment of serving; ensure your soup is very hot and well salted
