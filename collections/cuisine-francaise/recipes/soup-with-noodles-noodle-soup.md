## Ingredients

- half a liter of flour
- four egg yolks
- a little salt
- a little water
- good bouillon
- coarse pepper
- optional: nutmeg and egg whites

## Directions

1. Have half a liter of flour; add four egg yolks, a little salt and a little water
2. Mix everything together and knead until your dough is well combined and you can flatten it with a rolling pin
3. When your dough is very thin, cut it into thin strips, taking care to dust them with flour so they do not stick together
4. Have good bouillon and drop your shredded dough into it when it is boiling
5. Half an hour is sufficient for your dough to be sufficiently cooked
6. Degrease your soup well; add a little coarse pepper just before putting it in the tureen
7. You can also make this dough with egg whites alone; add a little nutmeg and coarse pepper according to taste
