## Ingredients

- lettuce
- veal slices
- lard
- onions
- carrots
- bouillon
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the lettuce carefully, ensuring the leaves hold well with the heart
2. Wash them thoroughly
3. Blanch them by throwing into boiling water with a handful of salt according to the quantity of water
4. When they have boiled for half an hour, refresh them in a bucket of fresh water
5. Press them well
6. Tie them with string so they do not separate
7. Place slices of veal at the bottom of your pot, then lard strips
8. Place your lettuces on top, cover with lard
9. Cut slices of onions and carrots and garnish your lettuces
10. Moisten with bouillon
11. Cook over low heat for an hour until well cooked
12. Alternatively, after blanching, place them in your pot and cook with bouillon and fat from the pot
13. When cooked, dip your bread with good bouillon, then drain your lettuces and arrange them on your soup
