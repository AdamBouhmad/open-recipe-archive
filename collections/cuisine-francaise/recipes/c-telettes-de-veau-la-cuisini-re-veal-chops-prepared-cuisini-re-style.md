## Ingredients

- veal square cut into chops
- a quarter pound of small lard cut in slices
- a bit of butter
- three egg yolks
- bouillon
- blanched parsley
- shallot
- vinegar
- coarse pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut a veal square into chops and trim them
2. Put in the bottom of a pan a quarter pound of small lard cut in slices, a bit of butter, and the chops on top
3. Cook them over a small fire, browning them by turning them often
4. When they are cooked, arrange them on the plate you must serve with the pieces of small lard on top
5. In the pan in which they cooked, put a liaison of three egg yolks with bouillon, blanched and chopped parsley, a chopped shallot
6. Detach everything that can stick to the pan, make it bind over the fire, and then add a dash of vinegar and a bit of coarse pepper
7. Serve over the chops; add a bit of salt if needed, if the small lard is not salty enough
