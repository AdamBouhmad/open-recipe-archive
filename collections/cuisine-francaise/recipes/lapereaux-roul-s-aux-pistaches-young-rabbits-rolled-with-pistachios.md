## Ingredients

- one or two young rabbits
- their livers
- other cooked meat
- breadcrumbs
- milk
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- four egg yolks
- white wine
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- coulis
- about twenty pistachios, blanched

## Directions

1. Debone one or two young rabbits
2. Make a forcemeat from their livers with some other cooked meat, breadcrumbs passed through milk, parsley, chives, mushrooms, salt, and pepper
3. Bind the forcemeat with four egg yolks
4. Spread this forcemeat on the rabbits, roll them, and tie with string
5. Cook them with a little white wine, bouillon, and bouquet garni
6. When cooked, degrease the sauce and pass through a sieve
7. Add a little coulis to bind it and reduce to sauce consistency
8. When serving on the rabbits, add about twenty blanched pistachios
