## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons velouté
- 3 tablespoons consommé
- 3 pints cream

## Directions

1. Place the velouté in a saucepan
2. Add the consommé
3. Reduce over high heat, stirring constantly, from eleven spoonfuls to five
4. In a separate pot, place the cream on high heat and reduce by half
5. Stir the cream constantly with a wooden spoon and scrape the bottom so it does not stick and does not take on a burnt taste
6. Mix the reduced velouté and cream together
7. Bring to a boil over high heat, stirring constantly so the sauce thickens and does not stick
8. Stir the béchamel for three-quarters of an hour to one hour
9. When the sauce is sufficiently thickened, strain through a sieve
10. The sauce should be a yellowish white
