## Ingredients

- a pinch of chopped green lemon
- two ounces of sugar
- a little salt
- butter the size of half an egg
- a good glass of water
- four or five spoonfuls of flour
- eggs
- a little candied orange flowers
- two bitter almond biscuits

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a pinch of chopped green lemon, two ounces of sugar, a little salt, butter the size of half an egg, and a good glass of water
2. Bring to a boil for a moment and add four or five spoonfuls of flour
3. Dry out over the fire, stirring constantly until the paste is very thick and begins to stick to the saucepan
4. Remove from the fire and add one egg at a time, stirring vigorously with the spoon until it is well mixed with the paste
5. Continue adding eggs one by one in this manner until the paste is soft without being liquid
6. Add a little candied orange flowers and two bitter almond biscuits, finely ground, to the paste
7. Form small cakes the size of half an egg on buttered paper
8. Glaze the top with beaten egg
9. Bake for half an hour in the oven at a gentle heat
