## Ingredients

- 10 oranges
- 3 lemons
- half pound of sugar
- 3 sticks of isinglass
- water

## Directions

1. Take 10 oranges and 3 lemons
2. Zest 3 oranges very lightly, removing the peel in small portions with a knife
3. Place the zest in a casserole
4. Press the 10 oranges and 3 lemons over the zests
5. Clarify half a pound of sugar
6. Place the orange juice and zests with the clarified sugar brought almost to hard crack stage
7. Heat with the sugar and juice of oranges
8. Pass through fine cloth
9. Add a decoction of 3 sticks of isinglass made by beating well and placing in small pieces in a casserole with 3 spoons of water, simmering for two hours, then passing through cheesecloth without straining or pressing
10. Mix the isinglass with the warm preparation
11. Amalgamate well the isinglass and juice
12. Pour the jelly into small pots and place on ice or in a cool place
13. When the jelly is completely set, remove from ice
14. 7 small pots suffice for an entremets
