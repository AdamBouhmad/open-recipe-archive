## Ingredients

- two large spoonfuls of flour
- four eggs, whites and yolks
- one half-spoonful of orange blossom water
- finely chopped green lemon zest
- one half-setier of cream
- one half-setier of milk
- salt the size of a pea
- two ounces of sugar
- one piece of sugar
- one half-glass of water

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan two large spoonfuls of flour that you dilute little by little with four eggs, whites and yolks, one half-spoonful of orange blossom water and a pinch of finely chopped green lemon zest
2. Moisten with one half-setier of cream and one half-setier of milk, add salt the size of a pea, and two ounces of sugar
3. Cook on a small fire for half an hour, always stirring
4. Then put a piece of sugar with one half-glass of water in the dish you must serve
5. Make them boil on a good stove until it is a beautiful cinnamon color, and quickly pour the cream into it
6. Have a large knife ready to spread the sugar that is on the edge of the dish by bringing it onto the cream
