## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Quarter pound of ham
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Prepare scrambled eggs as usual
2. Cut a quarter pound of ham into small dice
3. Finish preparing the eggs as customary
4. Arrange croutons around them
