## Ingredients

- one pound of brown sugar
- three quarterons of marshmallow root, chopped
- one chopine of water

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of brown sugar in the same way as that for verjuice syrup
2. Prepare a marshmallow water as follows: cook three quarterons of chopped marshmallow root in one chopine of water after having scraped and washed it
3. Let it boil until the water sticks to the fingers
4. Then put it in a cloth and wring it with force of arms
5. Let it rest and draw it off clear
6. Take the clearest part and put it in the brown sugar
7. Let them boil together until they have the consistency of a strong syrup like the previous ones
