## Ingredients

- one chopine of good cream
- half a teaspoon of orange blossom water
- a good half-quarteron of sugar
- two eggs, yolks and whites

## Directions

1. Whip together the cream, orange blossom water, sugar, and eggs until the cream is thick
2. Put everything in a silver compotier and set the cream on hot ashes, covered with a pie-dish lid with a little fire on top
3. When it is set, put it to cool on ice or in a cool place until serving
