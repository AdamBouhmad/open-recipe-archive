## Ingredients

- turkey
- butter or melted lard
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- lard slices
- one glass of white wine
- equal amount of bouillon
- coulis

## Directions

1. Singe and clean a turkey, flatten it slightly on the breast
2. Place in a casserole with melted butter or lard, finely chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, and garlic
3. Brown the turkey and transfer to another casserole with seasoning, salt, and pepper
4. Cover the breast with lard slices
5. Moisten with one glass of white wine and equal amount of bouillon
6. Cook on low heat
7. Degrease and add a little coulis to the sauce to bind it
