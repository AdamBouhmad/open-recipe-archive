## Ingredients

- A ravigote (mixed herbs)
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- One spoonful of cold velouté
- Two spoonfuls of oil
- Four or five spoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop a ravigote and put it in a saucepan with salt, coarse pepper, and one spoonful of cold velouté
2. Stir your sauce with the ravigote
3. Add two spoonfuls of oil and mix well with your sauce so that it is bound
4. Add four or five spoonfuls of vinegar
5. Your sauce must be well stirred at the moment of putting it on your salad of meat or fish
