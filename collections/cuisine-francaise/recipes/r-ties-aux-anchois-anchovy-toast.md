## Ingredients

- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Half a dozen anchovies, well-washed
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Pass bread crumbs in butter
2. Arrange on top half a dozen anchovies that have been well-washed and cut into thin fillets lengthwise
3. Season the slices with oil, vinegar, and coarse pepper
