## Ingredients

- Whiting
- Flour
- Oil or fat for frying

## Directions

1. Scale, gut, wash and dry the whiting
2. Keep the livers in the body
3. Cut lightly in five or six places on each side
4. Dredge in flour
5. Fry at high heat
6. Serve on a napkin as a roast dish
