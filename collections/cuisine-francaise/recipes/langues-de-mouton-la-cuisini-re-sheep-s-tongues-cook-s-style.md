## Ingredients

- sheep's tongues grilled
- good butter about the size of a small egg
- two raw egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of verjuice
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. After grilling the tongues, put in a saucepan a piece of good butter about the size of a small egg, two raw egg yolks, two spoonfuls of verjuice, a little bouillon, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
2. Turn it on the fire until it is thickened like a white sauce
3. Serve under the tongues
