## Ingredients

- White beans
- Butter
- Flour
- Onion
- Parsley, chopped
- Chives, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in water until done
2. In a saucepan, make a roux with butter and a little flour
3. Add chopped onion to the roux and cook it
4. Add the cooked beans to the saucepan with the parsley and chives, chopped, salt, pepper, and a dash of vinegar
5. Simmer everything together for a quarter hour
6. Serve
