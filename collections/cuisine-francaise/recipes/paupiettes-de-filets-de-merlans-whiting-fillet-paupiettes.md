## Ingredients

- Whiting fillets
- Fish forcemeat
- Lard strips or buttered paper
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the fillets as for sautéing
2. Trim them evenly
3. On the inside, spread a little fish forcemeat equally
4. Roll the fillet over it so it forms a barrel shape
5. Put some of the same forcemeat on your platter and arrange your fillets around and in the middle
6. Cover them with lard strips or double buttered paper
7. Put them in the oven or on a stove with a camp oven on top; half an hour is sufficient to cook them
8. Mask them with Italian sauce
