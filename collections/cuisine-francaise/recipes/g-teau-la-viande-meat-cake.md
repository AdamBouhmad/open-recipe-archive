## Ingredients

- meat (such as hare, rabbit, beef, or mutton)
- beef fat
- one pound of lard cut into dice
- ten egg yolks
- fine salt
- fine spices
- half a glass of brandy
- mushrooms
- a little shallots
- parsley
- chives
- lard slices for lining

## Directions

1. Choose the meat that will give the name to your cake, such as hare cake, rabbit cake, beef cake, etc. All are made the same way, except that game is mixed with an equal amount of butcher's meat
2. For a mutton cake, take a leg, remove all the meat after removing the skin, and chop it with a little beef fat
3. Mix this meat with one pound of lard cut into dice, ten egg yolks, fine salt, fine spices, half a glass of brandy, mushrooms, a little shallots, parsley, and chives, all chopped
4. Put lard slices at the bottom of a saucepan and your meat farce on top after mixing it well with all its seasoning
5. Cook in the oven for at least three hours
6. When cooked, let it cool in the saucepan, then remove it to turn it out onto a plate
7. Leave the lard slices that are around the cake and rasp them lightly with a knife
8. Serve on a plate garnished with a napkin
