## Ingredients

- 1 young rabbit, dressed and cleaned
- 1/4 pound of butter
- Aromatic herbs, pounded
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A little grated nutmeg
- Parsley and shallots, finely chopped

## Directions

1. After dressing and cleaning the young rabbit, cut it into pieces and remove the lung. Dry the pieces well so no blood remains.
2. Put approximately 1/4 pound of butter in your casserole; when it is a little warm, put your young rabbit in it with a little pounded aromatic herbs, salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg.
3. Cook it over high heat; when your pieces are well stiffened, add a little finely chopped parsley and shallots.
4. Leave it another three or four minutes on the fire.
5. You can serve it coming straight from the casserole. Check that it is properly salted: ten minutes or at most a quarter hour is sufficient to cook your young rabbit.
