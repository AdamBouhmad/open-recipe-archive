## Ingredients

- a little coulis
- as much bouillon
- a pinch of blanched and chopped parsley
- two cooked poultry livers
- one anchovy and capers, all finely chopped
- butter the size of half an egg
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in a saucepan
2. Thicken the sauce over the fire
3. Serve as you judge appropriate
