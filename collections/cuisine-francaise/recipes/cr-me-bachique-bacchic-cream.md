## Ingredients

- three half-setiers of white wine
- two green lemon rinds
- a pinch of coriander
- a small piece of cinnamon
- three ounces of sugar
- a half-teaspoonful of flour
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Bring in a saucepan three half-setiers of white wine with two green lemon rinds, a pinch of coriander, a small piece of cinnamon, and three ounces of sugar
2. Boil at a gentle fire for a good quarter hour
3. In another saucepan, mix a half-teaspoonful of flour with six egg yolks
4. Gradually add the boiled wine mixture to the flour and eggs
5. When it is half-cooled, pass everything through a sieve
6. Cook your cream in a bain-marie
7. When it is set, remove it from the bain-marie and put it to cool until you serve it
