## Ingredients

- Young rabbit thighs
- Fine lard for larding
- Small piece of lard to replace the bone
- Bards of lard
- A few slices of veal
- Two carrots
- Three onions
- Two bay leaves
- A little thyme
- Buttered round of paper
- Bouillon
- Chicory
- Cucumbers à la crème, sautéed mushrooms, tomato sauce, game stock, or glaze sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Bone the thighs down to the joint as with the previous ones
2. Lard them with fine lard
3. Season them on the inside
4. Put a piece of small lard in place of the bone
5. With a needle and thread, bring the flesh together and secure it
6. Put bards of lard in a casserole with a few slices of veal, two carrots, three onions, two bay leaves, and a little thyme
7. Place your thighs on top with seasoning
8. Cover them with a round of buttered paper
9. Pour in a full spoonful of bouillon
10. Simmer for an hour and a half, or more depending on the quality of your rabbit
11. Be careful to put fire on your lid
12. At the moment of serving, drain your thighs
13. Remove the trussing strings and glaze them
14. Place your chicory on your plate and your thighs on top
15. You may serve underneath cucumbers à la crème, a sauté of mushrooms, tomato sauce, game stock, or a glaze sauce
