## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Oil
- Onions

## Directions

1. Trim and cook your beef rib as you would a country-style one; in place of butter use oil for cooking it
2. When it is cooked, place it on your plate
3. Take twenty large onions that you cut in half from the head to the root
4. Then cut your onion halves in slices so that they form semicircles
5. Put a quarter pound and a half of oil in your casserole, that you heat well
6. Add the onions
