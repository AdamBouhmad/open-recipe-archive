## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal cut into dice
- one half-pound ham cut into dice
- 2 chicken thighs
- 3 or 4 carrots
- 4 onions
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 cloves
- a little basil
- one half-pound butter
- a little salt
- 12 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 pint cream

## Directions

1. Place the veal, ham, chicken thighs, carrots, onions, bay leaves, cloves, basil, butter, and salt in a saucepan over moderate heat
2. When the meat is slightly browned, crush the hard-boiled egg yolks finely
3. Add the crushed egg yolks to the saucepan with the meat
4. Stir the yolks with the meat when the butter is hot
5. Only put the saucepan on the heat after adding liquid
6. Stir the yolks well, then pour in the cream little by little so the yolks dissolve properly
7. Boil the entire mixture over moderate heat
8. Stir constantly so the sauce does not become lumpy
9. If the sauce is too thick, add cream or milk
10. When it has boiled for one hour and a half, strain through a sieve and use as indicated
