## Ingredients

- One hundred heads of chicory
- Salt
- Water
- Clarified butter

## Directions

1. Toward the end of September, take one hundred heads of chicory or more
2. Remove the hard green ribs that envelop the white part, keeping only the good parts
3. Rinse the head without cutting too close
4. Have a large cauldron three-quarters full of boiling water
5. Put two large handfuls of salt in the water
6. When the water boils, add your chicory, well-washed and drained
7. Be careful to submerge it in the boiling water so it does not blacken
8. After it has boiled for ten minutes, remove it and put it in cold water
9. When well refreshed, remove it and press it in your hands to extract the water
10. Arrange it in jars or pots
11. Make a brine
12. When it is cold and clear, pour it over your chicory
13. Cover it with clarified butter
14. When it is congealed, close your container
