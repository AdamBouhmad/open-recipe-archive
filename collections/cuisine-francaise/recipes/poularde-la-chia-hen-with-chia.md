## Ingredients

- One hen (poularde), roasted on the spit
- Chia (a type of gherkin from the Indies)
- Sauce with coulis

## Directions

1. Cut the chia into slices
2. Soak them for a quarter hour in nearly boiling water
3. Drain them and put them in a sauce with coulis
4. Serve over a hen cooked on the spit
