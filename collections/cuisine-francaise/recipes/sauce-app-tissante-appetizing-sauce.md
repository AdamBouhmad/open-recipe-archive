## Ingredients

- All sorts of small salad herbs
- Three shallots
- One spoonful of bouillon
- Two spoonfuls of coulis
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- A little butter
- One spoonful of mustard

## Directions

1. Take all sorts of small salad herbs, each according to its strength, and after peeling and washing them, put them with three shallots
2. Boil everything a little in a small pan and refresh it in fresh water
3. After pressing it, pound it well fine in a mortar
4. Put it in a saucepan with one spoonful of bouillon and two spoonfuls of coulis
5. Dilute everything and pass it through a sieve into another saucepan
6. Add salt, coarse pepper, a little butter, and one spoonful of mustard
7. Thicken the sauce without letting it boil
