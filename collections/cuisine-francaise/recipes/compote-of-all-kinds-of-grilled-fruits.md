## Ingredients

- fruit
- syrup

## Directions

1. Let your syrup reduce until your fruit begins to stick to the pan, then you must not leave it, you must move your compote around in it until you see it has a beautiful color
2. Then put a plate on your compote that you hold with your left hand, and turn it over by sliding it properly into your compote dish
3. Serve them hot or cold, they are better hot
4. You can make compotes with those that have already been used to make a change
