## Ingredients

- A piece of butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Shallots
- A pinch of flour
- A half-setier of milk
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Seven hard-boiled eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Butter, hot

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a piece of butter with parsley, chives, mushrooms, and shallots, all chopped
2. Pass it over the fire and add a pinch of flour
3. Moisten with a half-setier of milk
4. Season with salt and black pepper
5. Boil until the sauce is bound
6. Add seven hard-boiled eggs cut in four
7. Let them boil and arrange them on the dish that you will put on top so that one does not see the ragout of eggs underneath
8. Rub the top of the omelet with hot butter
9. Bread it with bread crumbs and pour butter over it again
10. Brown it underneath a pie-dish lid
