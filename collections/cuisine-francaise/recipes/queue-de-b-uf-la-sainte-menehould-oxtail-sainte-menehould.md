## Ingredients

- cooked oxtail
- salt
- coarse pepper
- warm butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. When the oxtail is cooked as in the hochepot recipe, season it with a little salt and coarse pepper
2. Dip it in warm butter and roll it in breadcrumbs
3. Bread it twice
4. Brown it in the oven or under the grill
