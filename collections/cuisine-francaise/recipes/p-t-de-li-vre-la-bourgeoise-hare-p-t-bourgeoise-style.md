## Ingredients

- Hare
- Hare blood
- Large lardons
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Half a glass of brandy
- Piece of butter

## Directions

1. Skin the hare and keep its blood; after emptying it, cut it into pieces and lard throughout with large lardons rolled in salt, pepper, chopped parsley, chives, and garlic.
2. Place it in a small pot with half a glass of brandy and a piece of butter; cook over a low fire.
3. When it is cooked and there is almost no sauce, add the blood, heat it without letting it boil.
4. Arrange the hare in the serving dish; serve everything together so that it appears to be a single piece.
5. Serve this pâté cold as an entremets.
