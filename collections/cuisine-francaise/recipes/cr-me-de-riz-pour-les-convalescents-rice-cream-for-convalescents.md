## Ingredients

- one quarteron of cleaned and washed rice
- good fatty broth

## Directions

1. Have one quarteron of rice cleaned and washed in three or four lukewarm waters
2. Put it to cook with good fatty broth; when it is cooked and thick, crush it with a spoon and then put it in a strainer to pass it, pressing hard with a wooden spoon
3. Add from time to time a little hot broth to help it pass through
4. Serve it the thickness of a double cream
