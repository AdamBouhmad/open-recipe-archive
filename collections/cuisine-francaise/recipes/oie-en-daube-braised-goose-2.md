## Ingredients

- one goose, plucked, cleaned, singed and plucked
- lardons seasoned with salt, pepper, and quatre épices
- lard for barding
- four carrots
- four onions
- three cloves
- a large bouquet of parsley and chives
- one veal shank, cut into several pieces
- salt
- three spoonfuls of bouillon

## Directions

1. Truss the goose's legs inside
2. Lard the goose with seasoned lardons
3. Place lard rinds in the bottom of a braising pan and put the goose on top
4. Add four carrots, four onions, three cloves, a large bouquet of parsley and chives, and one veal shank cut into several pieces
5. Cover the goose with lard rinds
6. Add salt and three spoonfuls of bouillon
7. Braise for two hours, longer if the goose is old
8. At service time, drain and unbind the goose
9. Strain the cooking liquid through a silk sieve
10. Reduce by half
11. Degrease and glaze the goose
12. Pour the reduction underneath
13. You may serve around it glazed onions, tomato sauce, glazed cabbage or glazed turnips
