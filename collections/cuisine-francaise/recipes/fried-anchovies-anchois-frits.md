## Ingredients

- Fresh anchovies
- Flour
- Oil
- Egg
- White wine

## Directions

1. Desalt the anchovies
2. Prepare a paste with flour, a spoonful of oil, and dilute with white wine, making sure the paste is not too liquid
3. Dip the anchovies in the paste and fry
4. Serve with a nice color as an entremets
