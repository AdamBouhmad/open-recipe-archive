## Ingredients

- Apricots not quite ripe
- Water
- Three pounds of sugar per twelve pounds of fruit

## Directions

1. Choose apricots that are not fully ripe
2. Place them in a pot with cold water
3. Place on the fire
4. When the water trembles and the apricots rise to the top, carefully remove them with a skimmer
5. Plunge them into cold water and let it come to a boil once more
6. Cool them in the water
7. Drain them on a sieve
8. For twelve pounds of fruit, clarify three pounds of sugar
9. When it reaches the pearl stage, add the apricots
10. Let them reach five or six boils
11. Remove them from the syrup and remove from the heat
12. Let the apricots drain
