## Ingredients

- one skate
- court-bouillon
- one glass of vinegar
- white sauce
- capers or cornichons cut into dice

## Directions

1. Cook the skate in court-bouillon with a glass of vinegar
2. When cooked, remove the slime or skin from both sides
3. Trim it and place it on the platter
4. Coat it with white sauce and top with capers or cornichons cut into dice
