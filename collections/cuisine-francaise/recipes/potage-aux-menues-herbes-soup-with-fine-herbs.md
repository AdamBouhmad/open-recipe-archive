## Ingredients

- two lettuces
- handful of sorrel
- chervil
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Peel and wash everything
2. Crush your lettuce leaves, rejecting the cotton parts
3. Also crush the sorrel and chervil
4. Take bouillon from the back of the pot and pass through a sieve
5. Make the herbs melt
6. Moisten them with bouillon so they can cook
7. When cooked, dip your bread soup with good bouillon and arrange your herbs on your soup
