## Ingredients

- Verjuice, one pound
- Sugar in powder, one pound

## Directions

1. Put everything into a pan and cook on a good fire
2. Make it boil three or four times covered
3. If your verjuice is not well green, you must let it cook at a gentle boil until it is well green
4. Put it immediately into pots
