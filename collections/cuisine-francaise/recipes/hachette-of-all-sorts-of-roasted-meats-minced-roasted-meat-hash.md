## Ingredients

- roasted meat such as butcher's meat, poultry, or game
- parsley
- chives
- shallots
- mushrooms
- good bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- breadcrumbs
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted meat into very thin slices
2. Put it in a saucepan with a little parsley, chives, shallots, mushrooms (all chopped), a little good bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Simmer everything on the fire for a quarter hour
4. Take the dish you wish to serve and put a little of the meat's sauce mixed with breadcrumbs on it
5. Arrange your meat on the breadcrumbs and put a little more breadcrumbs on top of the meat
6. Attach it on a gentle fire until a small gratin forms on the bottom of the dish
7. Put the rest of the sauce with a dash of verjuice on top
