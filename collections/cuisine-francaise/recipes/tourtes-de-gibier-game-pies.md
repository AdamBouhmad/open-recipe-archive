## Ingredients

- Rabbit, hare, woodcock, or larks
- Lard
- Bouquet of fine herbs
- Salt
- Fine spices
- Butter
- Pastry dough
- Good coulis
- Juice of oranges
- Optional: veal sweetbreads with mushrooms or sliced truffles

## Directions

1. For rabbit: cut into pieces and crack the bones slightly with the back of a cleaver.
2. For hare: remove all bones and use only the meat; reserve bones for civet.
3. For woodcock: take two birds, cut each into four pieces, and lard the inside with lard before placing at the bottom of the pie.
4. For larks: remove the legs and head, empty the inside, and make a stuffing as you would for woodcock.
5. Place the game in the pie dish with a bouquet of fine herbs, salt, and fine spices.
6. Cover with lard and butter.
7. Cover with a layer of pastry dough and finish as for other pies.
8. When cooked and degreased, fill with a good sauce made from coulis.
9. When serving, press the juice of two oranges into the sauce.
10. Alternatively, use a good ragout of veal sweetbreads with mushrooms or sliced truffles.
11. Always add the juice of one orange when serving, as game benefits from a slightly tart flavor.
