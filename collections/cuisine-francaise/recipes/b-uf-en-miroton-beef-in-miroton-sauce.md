## Ingredients

- Cooked beef breast
- Onions
- Pickles or gherkins
- Parsley
- Chives
- Capers
- Anchovies
- A small pinch of garlic
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Take beef breast cooked in a pot, drain it when it is cool and tender
2. Cut it into very thin slices
3. In the serving dish, put in the bottom a layer of sliced onions, pickles or gherkins, parsley, chives, capers, anchovies, and a small pinch of garlic, all chopped
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Arrange your beef slices on top and season them the same way as the bottom layer
6. Cover the dish and put it to boil gently on a stove for half an hour
7. Serve with a light sauce
