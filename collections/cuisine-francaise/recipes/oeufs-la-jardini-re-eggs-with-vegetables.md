## Ingredients

- Four or five large onions, cut into strips
- A piece of butter
- A good pinch of flour
- One chopine of milk
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan four or five large onions cut into strips with a piece of butter
2. Pass them over the fire until they are almost cooked
3. Then add a good pinch of flour
4. Moisten with one chopine of milk
5. Season with salt and black pepper
6. Boil until the sauce is thick
7. Remove from the fire to add ten eggs that you beat together
8. Put everything in the dish you must serve
9. Cook it over a small fire covered with a pie-dish lid
