## Ingredients

- one leg of mutton
- veal sweetbread
- mushrooms
- small eggs
- rice

## Directions

1. Braise one leg of mutton in the same manner as the one with cauliflowers
2. Serve on top a ragout mixed with veal sweetbread, mushrooms, and small eggs
3. For the ragout, have veal sweetbread soak in tepid water
