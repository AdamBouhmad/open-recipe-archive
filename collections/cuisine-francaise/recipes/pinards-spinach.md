## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Butter
- Salt
- Flour
- Milk or cream
- Veal coulis and jus (for the rich version)

## Directions

1. After cleaning and washing the spinach, cook it in water.
2. Remove the spinach and place it in fresh water to press it well.
3. Put it in a casserole with a piece of good butter and let it boil on a low fire for a quarter of an hour.
4. Add a pinch of salt, a pinch of flour, and moisten with milk or cream.
5. For the rich version, replace the cream with good veal coulis and jus.
6. When prepared this way, you can serve it with meat roasted on a spit.
