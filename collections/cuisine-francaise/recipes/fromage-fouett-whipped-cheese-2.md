## Ingredients

- A little peel of green citron, finely chopped
- Three half-setiers of good thick cream
- About the size of a pea of gum arabic powder

## Directions

1. Chop the green citron peel very finely and place it in a terrine with three half-setiers of good thick cream and gum arabic powder about the size of a pea
2. Whip your cream, and as the foam becomes thick, remove it with a skimming spoon to place it in a wicker basket
3. If the basket is not tightly woven, place a piece of muslin or other fine cloth in the bottom
4. When all the cream is whipped, let the cheese drain until you are ready to serve
5. Turn it out into a serving dish and sprinkle fine sugar on top
