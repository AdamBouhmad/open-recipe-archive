## Ingredients

- Weever fish
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Capers
- Anchovies
- Flour
- Water

## Directions

1. Scale, gut, wash and dry the weever thoroughly
2. Cut lightly in five or six places on each side
3. Soak with a little oil, salt, and pepper
4. Grill and baste from time to time with the remaining oil
5. Prepare sauce with butter, capers, anchovies, a little flour and water, salt and pepper
6. Thicken the sauce on the fire and serve over the weever
