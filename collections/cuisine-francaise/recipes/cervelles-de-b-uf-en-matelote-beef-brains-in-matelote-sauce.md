## Ingredients

- beef brains
- red or white wine
- small onions
- butter
- flour
- mushrooms
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains as in the previous recipe
2. Instead of water and vinegar, moisten them with red or white wine
3. When cooked, strain the cooking liquid through silk sieve
4. Clean and peel small onions, sauté them in butter until golden
5. Dust with flour, about one spoonful
6. Moisten with the wine in which the brains cooked
7. Add some mushrooms
8. When the ragout is cooked, drain the brains and arrange on plate
9. Pour the ragout over them; ensure good seasoning
