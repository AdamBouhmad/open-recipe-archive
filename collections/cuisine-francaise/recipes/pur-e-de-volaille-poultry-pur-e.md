## Ingredients

- three hens
- veal teat the size of an egg
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- two skimming spoons of white jellied stock or consommé
- three spoonfuls of béchamel or velouté
- croûtons

## Directions

1. Spit roast three hens
2. When cooked and cooled, remove the whites
3. Pound them well in a mortar
4. Add veal teat the size of an egg, a little salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
5. Pound again so everything mixes well together
6. Melt two skimming spoons of white jellied stock or consommé and three spoonfuls of béchamel or velouté
7. Put these five spoonfuls in a casserole over the fire and add your pounded whites that you will thin out
8. Do not let them boil
9. Pass everything through a sieve
10. When your purée is in a casserole, keep it hot in a bain-marie
11. At the moment of serving, put croûtons in it
