## Ingredients

- water
- two handfuls of soda
- green apricots or green almonds
- fresh water
- boiling water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil water in a pan with two handfuls of soda; when it has boiled twice, throw your apricots or almonds into the pan
2. When they have boiled once, remove them with a skimming spoon and rub them well in your hands to remove the down, and as you do so throw them into fresh water
3. Have fresh boiling water ready in another pan to cook your apricots
4. You will see when they are cooked enough by pricking them with a pin; if it enters easily and the apricot falls by itself, it is a sign they are cooked enough
5. Remove them in fresh water, then boil sugar in your pan and put your apricots or almonds in it
6. Let them boil gently on a small fire until they are well green, and serve
7. Alternatively, some do not make lye for their apricots or almonds, they merely rub them with salt to remove the down, and then blanch and cook as described above
