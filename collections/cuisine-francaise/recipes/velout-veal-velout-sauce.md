## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 pieces of veal knuckle from the leg
- 2 chickens
- 4 carrots
- 4 onions, one studded with 2 cloves
- a large bouquet of parsley and chives
- consommé, one pot spoon full
- white roux
- about 20 mushrooms, sautéed cold in water and lemon

## Directions

1. Place all the meat and vegetables into a casserole
2. Add one pot spoon full of consommé
3. Place your casserole on a fairly hot fire
4. Be careful to skim your meats well and wipe the interior of your casserole so your sauce is not cloudy
5. When you see your moisture is diminished and forms large bubbles when boiling, moisten your sauce with consommé
6. Ensure it is very clear and has no brown color
7. When you have filled your casserole with consommé, skim it well
8. When it boils, place it on the corner of the stove
9. Make a white roux in which you place about 20 mushrooms that you have sautéed cold in water and lemon, which you will stir in your hot roux
10. Dilute your white roux with the moisture from your velouté
11. Pour it over your meats
12. Make your sauce boil on the corner of the stove
13. Skim it well
14. After an hour and a half, degrease it
15. When your meat is cooked, pass your sauce through cheesecloth
16. Ensure your velouté is as white as possible
