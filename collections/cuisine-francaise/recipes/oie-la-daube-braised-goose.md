## Ingredients

- One goose not tender enough for the spit
- Bacon lardons
- Parsley
- Chives
- Two shallots
- Half clove of garlic
- Laurel leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg
- Two glasses of water
- Two glasses of white wine
- Half glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Empty the goose and truss its legs into the body
2. Singe and pluck it
3. Lard it all over with bacon lardons seasoned and mixed with chopped parsley, chives, two shallots, half a clove of garlic, a laurel leaf, thyme and basil (chopped like powder), salt, coarse pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Tie the goose with string and place it in a pot just fitting its size
5. Add two glasses of water, two glasses of white wine, and half a glass of brandy, plus a little more salt and coarse pepper
6. Seal the pot well and cook over very low heat for three or four hours until the sauce is very short
