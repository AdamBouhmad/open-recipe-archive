## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- one half tablespoon shallots
- finely chopped mushrooms in the same amount as shallots
- one half-bottle white wine
- butter the size of an egg
- 2 tablespoons velouté
- 1 tablespoon consommé

## Directions

1. Place the parsley, shallots, mushrooms, and white wine in a saucepan with the butter
2. Boil until well reduced
3. When there is almost no liquid remaining in the saucepan, add the velouté and consommé
4. Boil the sauce over moderate heat
5. Skim and degrease carefully
6. When the sauce is reduced to the proper consistency, meaning it is thick like clear porridge, remove from heat
7. Place in another saucepan and keep warm in a bain-marie
