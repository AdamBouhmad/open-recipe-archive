## Ingredients

- Eight or ten soles
- Mayonnaise sauce
- Aspic
- Croutons
- Gherkins
- Anchovies

## Directions

1. Fry eight or ten soles
2. When they are cold, put them on the grill over a very low fire without heating them completely, only to make it easy to lift the skin
3. Detach the fillets from your soles
4. As soon as they are lukewarm, remove the skins
5. Then trim and cut your fillets into long squares of two inches
6. Arrange them correctly in a mold or on your plate
7. If in a mold, dip your sole fillets in your mayonnaise sauce and arrange them until your mold is full of fillets
8. Warm your sauce and fill your mold with it
9. Put it on ice or in the cold so your sauce congeals
10. An hour before service, pour it on the plate and decorate with croutons, gherkins, anchovies, etc.
11. If you arrange your pieces on your plate, also pour your sauce over it and decorate it
