## Ingredients

- 4 partridges
- 6 shallots
- handful of parsley leaves
- coarse pepper
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 tablespoons of Spanish sauce
- 1/2 glass of white wine
- grated nutmeg
- 1/2 glass of bouillon
- 1 clove

## Directions

1. Spit-roast 4 partridges and piece them as for partridge salmi
2. Pound the scraps in a mortar with 6 shallots, a handful of parsley leaves, coarse pepper, and 1 bay leaf
3. Pour 4 tablespoons of Spanish sauce into a saucepan along with 1/2 glass of white wine, the pounded scraps, a little grated nutmeg, and 1/2 glass of bouillon
4. Reduce the whole to half; strain your sauce through fine cloth, pressing it through the cloth
5. Place it over your hot partridge limbs, keeping them warm without boiling
6. If you have no Spanish sauce, make a roux; add your pounded scraps, 1 glass of white wine, 2 glasses of bouillon, and 1 clove
7. Reduce the sauce, degrease it, and strain through fine cloth
8. Check the seasoning
