## Ingredients

- veal breast
- lard
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- chives
- bay leaves
- cloves
- thyme
- bouillon or water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a veal breast
2. Remove the meats that cover your tendons
3. When your tendons are uncovered, cut them very close to the rib bones
4. When they are separated from the ribs, cut the tender red bones that hold to your tendons
5. After separating the tendons from your veal breast, cut them into oysters, that is to say in flat and long form, or in thick pieces forming a square, or leave them whole
6. Have your tendons disgorge
7. Blanch them
8. Pare them so they have a neat and even form
9. Place lard in a casserole
10. Arrange your tendons on top and cover them with lard
11. Pour a cooking liquid over it
12. Your tendons must cook for three hours
13. Before removing them from cooking, pay attention to whether the larding needle enters easily into your tendons
14. If you do not have a cooking utensil, put lard in the bottom of your casserole
15. Place your tendons on top and cover them with lard
16. Then place the trimmings and rib bones from your veal breast on top, with two or three carrots, four onions, a bunch of parsley and chives, two bay leaves, two cloves, a little thyme
17. Moisten your tendons with bouillon or water
18. Add the appropriate amount of salt
