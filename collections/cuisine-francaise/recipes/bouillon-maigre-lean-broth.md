## Ingredients

- 20 carrots, cut into slices
- 20 turnips
- 20 onions
- 4 or 5 celery stalks
- 4 whole lettuces
- a handful of chervil tied with twine
- 1 cabbage cut into strips
- a few parsnips, also cut
- 1 pound of butter
- 1 pint of water
- 2 liters of peas
- 4 cloves
- salt and coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a copper pot or casserole the carrots cut into slices, as many turnips, onions, celery stalks, whole lettuces, a handful of chervil tied with twine, cabbage cut into strips, and some parsnips also cut
2. Put in 1 pound of butter
3. Pour 1 pint of water over your vegetables and bring to a boil until they are reduced to glazing, that is, until there is no more water in your pot and your vegetables are just simmering a little with the butter
4. Then fill your pot with water in which you put 2 liters of peas, 4 cloves, salt and coarse pepper enough so that your broth is well seasoned
5. When your pot has boiled for 3 or 4 hours, pass your broth through a silk sieve
