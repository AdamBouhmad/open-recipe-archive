## Ingredients

- One hare, plucked and cleaned
- Medium lardons, well seasoned with crushed aromatics, salt and pepper
- Lard strips
- One veal shank, cut into pieces
- Bouquet of parsley and chives
- Three laurel leaves
- Bouquet of thyme
- Three carrots
- Four onions
- Three cloves
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. When your hare is plucked and cleaned, lard it with well-seasoned medium lardons flavored with crushed aromatics, salt, and pepper.
2. When the thighs and fillets are well larded, place some lard strips in a braising pan or daubiére and the hare on top.
3. Place a veal shank cut into pieces around it.
4. Cover it with lard strips.
5. Add a bouquet of parsley and chives, three laurel leaves, a bouquet of thyme, three carrots, four onions, and three cloves.
6. Moisten it with bouillon; if you have none, use water, salt, and pepper.
7. Simmer for two hours according to the quality of the hare.
8. At the moment of serving, drain it, glaze it, and reduce the braising liquid to three quarters.
9. Pass it through a silk sieve and serve it underneath the hare.
