## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar, equal weight to the marmalade

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces in water until they are tender
2. Cool them in fresh water until completely cold
3. Cut them into quarters and remove the cores and skins
4. Crush them and pass through a sieve
5. Put what has passed through on the fire and stir constantly until the marmalade is thick
6. Weigh the marmalade and add an equal weight of sugar, cooking the same way as for pear marmalade
7. Mix the marmalade with the sugar together on the fire and remove when it begins to simmer
8. Put into jars
