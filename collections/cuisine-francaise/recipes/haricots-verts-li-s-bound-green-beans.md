## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Butter
- Fine herbs
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch the green beans and let cool and drain
2. Add butter the size of two eggs to a saucepan with finely chopped fine herbs and cook slightly
3. When the butter is warm and herbs slightly fried, add two teaspoons of flour mixed with the butter
4. Pour over one glass of bouillon, a little salt, and coarse pepper
5. When the sauce boils, add the beans and sauté them
6. At the moment of serving, add a liaison of two egg yolks
7. Check the beans for good taste
8. You may add lemon juice
