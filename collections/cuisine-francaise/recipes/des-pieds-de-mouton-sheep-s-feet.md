## Ingredients

- sheep's feet
- butter
- bouquet garni
- egg yolks
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. After having the feet well cooked in water, peel off what remains of the hair and remove the large bones
2. Put them in a saucepan with a good piece of butter and a bouquet garni
3. Let them have a few turns on the fire
4. When they are cooked and the sauce reduced, do not degrease it
5. Add three egg yolks diluted with milk or cream if you have it
