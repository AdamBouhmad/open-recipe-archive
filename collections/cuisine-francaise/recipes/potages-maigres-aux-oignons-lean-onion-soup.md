## Ingredients

- approximately a dozen medium onions
- a piece of butter
- water or lean broth
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut the onions into strips
2. Put them in a saucepan with a piece of butter
3. Cook them over the fire, turning them from time to time, until they are cooked and lightly colored
4. Moisten them with water or lean broth, if you have it
5. Add salt and coarse pepper
6. Bring to a boil a few times
7. Then add bread to make your soup simmer in the ordinary way
