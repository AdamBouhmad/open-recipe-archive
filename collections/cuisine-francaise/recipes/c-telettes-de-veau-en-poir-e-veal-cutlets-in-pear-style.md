## Ingredients

- very thick veal cutlets
- calf's sweetbread
- bacon cut into pieces
- parsley
- shallots
- mushrooms
- pine nuts
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bacon strips for larding
- a little bouillon
- white wine
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut thick veal cutlets and make a pocket in the side with your finger
2. Prepare a stuffing with calf's sweetbread cut into pieces, bacon, parsley, shallots, mushrooms, and pine nuts, all chopped together with salt and coarse pepper
3. Fill the pockets with this stuffing
4. Lard the cutlets and place them in a casserole with bacon strips, a little bouillon, and white wine
5. Cook until done and arrange on the platter
6. Strain the sauce, skim the fat, add a little coulis to bind it, and serve over the cutlets
