## Ingredients

- Rib of beef
- Oil or butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Piquant sauce
- Gherkins
- Water
- Fine pepper
- Shallots
- Breadcrumbs
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare your rib as the one with jus
2. When it is cooked, have a piquant sauce in which you put your gherkins
3. At the moment of saucing, if you do not have gherkins, you can make a sauce with water, salt, pepper, minced shallots, a little breadcrumbs, and a little vinegar
4. Boil everything together
5. At the moment of pouring your sauce, add your gherkins
