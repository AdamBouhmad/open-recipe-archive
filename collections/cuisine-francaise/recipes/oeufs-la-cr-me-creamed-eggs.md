## Ingredients

- half a setier of cream
- eight eggs
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put half a setier of cream in the serving plate
2. Boil and reduce by half
3. Add eight eggs, salt, and coarse pepper
4. Cook them
5. Pass a hot shovel over the top
6. Serve with runny yolks
