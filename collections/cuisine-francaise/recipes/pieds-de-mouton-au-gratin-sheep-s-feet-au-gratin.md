## Ingredients

- sheep's feet
- white wine, one glass
- bouillon, three spoonfuls
- coulis, three spoonfuls
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- garlic, half a clove
- cloves, two
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the feet in water
2. Put them in a saucepan with a glass of white wine, three spoonfuls of bouillon and as much coulis, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, two cloves, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Boil them on a small fire and reduce to a short sauce
4. Remove the bouquet
5. Serve them on a gratin, as that of sheep's tongues, previously on page 69
