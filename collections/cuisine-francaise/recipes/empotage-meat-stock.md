## Ingredients

- Three or four pounds of sliced beef
- Two calf necks and one calf knuckle
- Four old hens
- Two spoonfuls of broth
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Onions

## Directions

1. Put in a pot or saucepan (according to the quantity of empotage) three or four pounds of sliced beef, two calf necks and one calf knuckle, four old hens
2. Moisten this quantity of meat with two spoonfuls of broth
3. Put your pot on a stove and boil everything until it is reduced
4. Be careful that your empotage is not too colored; for this you must be careful that your sweating does not stick too much; make it a beautiful blond color
5. Then fill your pot with great broth and garnish it with vegetables such as carrots, turnips, onions
6. Do not add salt since your great broth is seasoned
7. Let your pot remain three and a half hours on the fire
8. When the meats are cooked, pass the broth of your empotage through fine cloth or a sieve
9. Try to make your broth very clear; you will use it to moisten all your soups
