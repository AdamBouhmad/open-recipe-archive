## Ingredients

- Several apples
- Powdered sugar
- Brandy (eau-de-vie)
- Cinnamon powder
- Batter for frying (pâte à frire)
- Oil or fat for frying

## Directions

1. Remove the peel and core from several apples with an apple corer
2. Cut them into slices of equal size and thickness
3. Put them in a terrine with powdered sugar, brandy, and cinnamon powder
4. At the moment of serving, drain them
5. Put them in a frying batter
6. Place them in hot oil that is not too hot
7. When they have a nice color, if they were not cooked enough, remove the frying pan from the fire: it will finish cooking them
8. Drain them and put powdered sugar on top
9. With a hot shovel, glaze them and then arrange them on a plate
