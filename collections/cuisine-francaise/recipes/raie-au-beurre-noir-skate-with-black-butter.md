## Ingredients

- one skate
- court-bouillon
- parsley leaves
- black butter sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the skate as in the previous recipe
2. Clean and trim it in the same way
3. Fry parsley leaves and place them around the skate
4. Coat it with black butter sauce
