## Ingredients

- Six tablespoonfuls of flour
- One whole egg
- Four egg yolks
- One pint of cream
- Butter (size of one egg)
- Six tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- Two bitter macarons
- Three sweet macarons
- One dried biscuit
- One tablespoonfull of candied orange flower in powder

## Directions

1. Put six tablespoonfuls of flour in a saucepan and mix with one whole egg and four yolks
2. Add one pint of cream and butter (size of one egg)
3. Place over fire and stir constantly
4. When cooked, let it cool
5. Add six tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar, two bitter macarons and three sweet ones, and one dried biscuit, all finely crushed
6. Add one tablespoonfull of candied orange flower powder
7. Mix everything together
8. Add four egg yolks, more if the mixture is thick
9. Whip five egg whites as for biscuit
10. Add them to the mixture
11. Place the soufflé in a silver saucepan and cook as the previous ones
