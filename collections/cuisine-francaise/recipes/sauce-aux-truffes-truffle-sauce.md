## Ingredients

- Two or three truffes, finely chopped
- Oil or butter
- Four or five tablespoons of velouté
- One spoonful of consommé

## Directions

1. Chop two or three truffles very fine
2. Have them lightly brown in oil or butter, according to taste
3. Add four or five tablespoons of velouté and one spoonful of consommé to your truffles
4. Boil your sauce a quarter hour over low heat
5. Degrease it and put it in a small saucepan
6. Keep it in a bain-marie
