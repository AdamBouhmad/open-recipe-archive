## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- Sugar equal in weight to the 8 eggs
- Flour equal in weight to 4 eggs
- A little finely chopped green lemon zest
- Butter for greasing the molds
- Fine sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Weigh 8 eggs on a balance and put equal weight of sugar on the other side
2. Weigh the flour using the weight of 4 eggs and set it aside on a plate
3. Crack the 8 eggs, separating the whites into one bowl and the yolks into another bowl with the sugar and a little finely chopped green lemon zest
4. Beat the yolks well with the sugar for half an hour
5. Whip the whites until well risen
6. Mix the whites with the yolks and sugar, then gradually and lightly mix in the flour, stirring your biscuit mixture constantly
7. Have tin or paper molds ready and well buttered inside with refined butter
8. Fill them slightly more than halfway
9. Sprinkle fine sugar on top
10. Bake in a gentle oven for half an hour
11. When they are a beautiful golden color, remove them from the molds when they are half-cold
