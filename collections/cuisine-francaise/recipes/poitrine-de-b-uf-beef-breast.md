## Ingredients

- beef breast
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a beef breast of the desired size
2. Bone it almost entirely up to the tendon
3. Give your piece a rectangular shape
4. Tie it with string and make it well rounded
5. Cook it
6. For serving à la Sainte-Menehould: cook it somewhat less than if serving plain
7. Prepare it the day before so you can better trim and finish it
8. Season with fine salt and coarse pepper
9. Dip it in butter or with a brush
10. Butter it, making sure your butter is not too hot
11. Apply bread crumbs on top as much as possible with your brush dipped in butter and let it drip over your piece of beef
12. Sprinkle bread crumbs so that it forms a crust
13. Before serving, let your piece of beef take color over the fire and heat it sufficiently
14. Place a worked and clear Spanish sauce underneath your piece with just a little coarse pepper
