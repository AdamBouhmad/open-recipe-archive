## Ingredients

- Calf's ears
- Mushroom ragout
- Butter
- Velouté
- Consommé
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the ears as directed for the Italian style
2. When cooked, drain them and arrange on the plate
3. Top with mushroom ragout
4. Turn and sauté them in butter
5. Add four skimming-spoonfuls of velouté and as much consommé
6. Reduce the sauce by half
7. At moment of serving, bind the ragout with two egg yolks and pour over the ears on the plate
