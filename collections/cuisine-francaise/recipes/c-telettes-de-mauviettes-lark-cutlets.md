## Ingredients

- Larks
- Lark breast meat
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Espagnole sauce
- Meat glaze

## Directions

1. Remove the breast meat from your larks; pass a piece of leg through it, cutting off a large piece as if it were a cutlet; take care to flatten each piece of breast so it takes on more surface
2. Arrange your cutlets on a silver platter; season them with salt and coarse pepper; warm a piece of butter and pour it over them
3. At the moment of service, place them on a hot fire; as soon as they have received a little heat, turn them; after they have stayed a moment on the fire, remove them
4. Tilt your platter and place your cutlets on the edge so that your butter separates from your cutlets; arrange them in a crown; sauce them with a little espagnole in which you put about half a walnut-sized piece of meat glaze
