## Ingredients

- one turkey
- foie gras
- mushrooms
- small lard
- salt, fine spices
- parsley, chives
- lard strip

## Directions

1. Singe the turkey over a well-lit stove
2. Debone it carefully
3. Make a raw ragout of foie gras, mushrooms, and small lard cut into small dice, mixed with salt, fine spices, minced parsley and chives
4. Put this ragout inside the body of the turkey
5. Sew up the turkey and shape it like a loaf of bread
6. Place a lard strip over the breast
7. Wrap it in a piece of étamine cloth
8. Place it in a pot to cook
