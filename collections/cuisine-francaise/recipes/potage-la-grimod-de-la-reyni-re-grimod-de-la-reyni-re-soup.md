## Ingredients

- One well-trussed capon
- Two pigeons
- A piece of beef brisket weighing three pounds
- Good bouillon
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Onions
- Celery
- Leeks
- Two cloves
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. Put a well-trussed capon, two pigeons, and a three-pound piece of beef brisket, all well tied for good appearance, in a medium pot
2. Fill this pot with good bouillon
3. Skim it
4. Garnish with carrots, turnips, onions, celery, leeks, and two cloves
5. At the moment of serving, put your capon and two pigeons in a shallow platter with whole lettuces around the platter, small onions, carrots cut into large dice, and turnips the same way
6. Arrange these three kinds of vegetables in large quantity, cooked as for the Hameau soup
7. When your vegetables are cooked, arrange them around your capon so they form a mound
8. Pass the bouillon from your pot through a fine cloth or silk sieve
9. Serve beside your platter a pot full of hot bouillon, well seasoned with salt
