## Ingredients

- One duck
- Half glass of white wine
- Equal amount of bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Two spoonfuls of oil
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- One clove of garlic
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cook a duck with half glass of white wine and an equal amount of bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper
2. In a casserole, place two spoonfuls of oil with chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, and one clove of garlic
3. Pass them over the fire
4. Add a pinch of flour
5. Moisten with the duck's cooking liquid, which should be degreased and passed through a sieve
6. Reduce to sauce consistency
7. Degrease and serve over the duck
