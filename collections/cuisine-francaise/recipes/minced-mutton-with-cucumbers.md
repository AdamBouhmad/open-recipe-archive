## Ingredients

- Minced mutton
- Butter
- Cucumbers
- Spanish sauce (Espagnole)
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Croutons
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a pan
2. Add cucumbers and sauté constantly until they turn blonde in color
3. Drain the cucumbers
4. Mix in three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
5. Cook until slightly thick
6. Place on the minced mutton with a little salt, coarse pepper, and grated nutmeg, keeping it warm
7. At the moment of serving, arrange the minced mutton on the plate and put croutons around it
8. If you do not have sauce, leave a little butter with the cucumbers and add nearly one spoonful of flour which you stir with them
9. Moisten with bouillon, salt, coarse pepper, and a dash of vinegar
10. When the cucumbers are sufficiently reduced, place them with the minced mutton
