## Ingredients

- Whiting
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Oil
- Butter sauce
- Capers
- Tomato sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Empty and clean your whiting
2. Score them on both sides
3. Add salt, coarse pepper, and oil
4. Half an hour before serving, place them on the grill over a moderately hot fire
5. When grilled, mask them with a butter sauce and sprinkle capers over them
6. You may also use a tomato sauce
