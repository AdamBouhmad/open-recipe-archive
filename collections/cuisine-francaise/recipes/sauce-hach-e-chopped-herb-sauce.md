## Ingredients

- a pinch of chopped parsley
- a pinch of shallots
- one spoonful of mushrooms, all chopped
- half a glass of vinegar
- a little coarse pepper
- four spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- as much bouillon
- one spoonful of chopped capers
- two or three chopped cornichons
- one or two anchovies
- butter

## Directions

1. Throw the chopped parsley, shallots, and mushrooms into a saucepan along with half a glass of vinegar and a little coarse pepper
2. Place the saucepan on the fire and reduce the vinegar until almost none remains
3. Add four spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and as much bouillon
4. Reduce and degrease the sauce
5. When it reaches the proper point, add one spoonful of chopped capers and two or three chopped cornichons
6. Transfer the sauce to another saucepan and place it in a bain-marie
7. At the moment of serving, add one or two anchovies pounded and worked with butter, stirring well into the sauce
