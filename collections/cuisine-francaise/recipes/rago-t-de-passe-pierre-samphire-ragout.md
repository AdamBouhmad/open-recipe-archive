## Ingredients

- Pickled samphire leaves
- Good coulis
- Water for blanching

## Directions

1. Take the pickled samphire, using only the leaves
2. Blanch them for a moment in boiling water to remove the strength of the vinegar
3. Press them to remove the water
4. Place them in a good sauce made with bound coulis
