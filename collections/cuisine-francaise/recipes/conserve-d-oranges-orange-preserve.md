## Ingredients

- ½ pound or three quarterons of sugar
- ½ glass of water
- Zest of 1 sweet orange, grated very finely

## Directions

1. Put the sugar in a pan with half a glass of water
2. Boil it without skimming until, when dipping the skimming spoon in the sugar and blowing through the holes, large sugar sparks come out
3. Remove from the fire
4. When it is half cold, add the finely grated orange zest that you have ready
5. Stir it with the sugar until it begins to thicken
6. Pour the preserve into the mold
7. Lemon and bigarade preserves are made the same way
