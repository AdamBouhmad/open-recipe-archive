## Ingredients

- pork meat with more fat than lean
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- fine spices
- veal or pork casings
- truffles (optional)
- shallots (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop the pork meat and add chopped parsley and chives
2. Season with salt and fine spices
3. Stuff everything into veal or pork casings
4. Tie the sausages to the desired length
5. Grill them, giving them the flavor you wish, such as with truffles or shallots
6. For truffles: chop them with the meat according to the quantity you wish
7. For shallots: use very little so the flavor does not dominate
8. For flat sausages: make them the same way, but put the meat in pork caul and grill them the same way
