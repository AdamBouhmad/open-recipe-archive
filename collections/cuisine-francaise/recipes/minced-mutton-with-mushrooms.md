## Ingredients

- Minced mutton
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Spanish sauce (Espagnole) reduced
- Consommé
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Laurel leaf
- Croutons
- Poached eggs

## Directions

1. When your meat is minced as described above, mince finely about twenty mushrooms
2. Press them in a cloth
3. Put a quarter-pound of butter in a pan with the mushrooms
4. Pass them on the fire until the butter turns to oil
5. Then add four spoonfuls of reduced Spanish sauce and three spoonfuls of consommé
6. Reduce your sauce to half
7. Pour it over your meat and mix
8. Keep your hash warm without boiling
9. If you do not have sauce, add one spoonful of flour which you stir briefly on the fire with the mushrooms
10. Add three glasses of bouillon, a little salt, a little coarse pepper, and a laurel leaf
11. Reduce everything by half
12. Pour the sauce over the meat
13. Check if your hash has good seasoning
14. Serve with croutons around it
15. You can also serve poached eggs on top
