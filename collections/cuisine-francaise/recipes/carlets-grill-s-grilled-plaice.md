## Ingredients

- plaice
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- straw tubes
- lean Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the plaice
2. Dry them
3. Oil them and add salt and pepper
4. Place straw tubes on the grill and plaice over them
5. Grill over a small fire
6. Coat with a lean Italian sauce
