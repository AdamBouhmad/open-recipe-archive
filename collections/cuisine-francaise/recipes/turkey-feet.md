## Ingredients

- turkey feet
- salt, seasoning

## Directions

1. Cook the feet in the coals like beef tongue, as described on page 29, with good seasoning
2. When cooked and cooled, dip them in the fat from their cooking
3. Coat them with bread crumbs and grill to a beautiful color
4. Serve dry as an entrée
