## Ingredients

- same ingredients as the previous sauce
- same moistening as the other sauces

## Directions

1. Prepare the juice as in the previous sauce
2. Let it attach until it is almost black
3. Use the same ingredients as for the other sauces, and the same moistening
4. When your juice has boiled for an hour and a half, pass it through a silk sieve
5. Use it as needed
