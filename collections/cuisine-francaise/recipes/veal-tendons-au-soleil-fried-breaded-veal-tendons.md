## Ingredients

- Veal tendons
- Sauce from previous preparation
- Breadcrumbs
- Eggs
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tendons as in the blanc method
2. Bind them and place on a plate
3. Cover with their sauce and let cool
4. The sauce must be well seasoned
5. When cooled, bread them with breadcrumbs, then dip in egg
6. Pass them a second time through the coating
7. At the moment of serving, fry them; take care that your oil is not too hot
8. Put fried parsley on top
