## Ingredients

- pears for cooking
- a glass of water
- a small piece of cinnamon
- two cloves
- half a quarter pound of sugar
- a glass of red wine

## Directions

1. Place whole pears in a pot with a glass of water, a small piece of cinnamon, two cloves, and half a quarter pound of sugar
2. Cook them well covered over some hot ashes
3. At half the cooking time, add a glass of red wine
4. When well cooked, reduce the syrup as very little is needed
5. Serve hot over the pears
