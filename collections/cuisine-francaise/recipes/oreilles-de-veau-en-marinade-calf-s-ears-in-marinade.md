## Ingredients

- calf's ears
- blanc
- marinade
- batter for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the ears as for the Italian style version
2. Cook them in a blanc
3. When cooked, drain them
4. Cut them lengthwise into two, three or four pieces as desired
5. Pour marinade over them a quarter hour before serving
6. Drain them
7. Put them in frying batter
