## Ingredients

- one beef rump
- one pint good white wine
- good beef bouillon
- veal slice
- lard strip
- large bouquet garni
- salt
- pepper
- approximately thirty Dutch onions, or large red onions if unavailable

## Directions

1. Take a fine rump and bone it, tie it with string
2. Cook it in a good braise made with one pint of good white wine, beef bouillon, veal slice, strip of lard, large bouquet garni, salt, and pepper
3. When it is half-cooked, add approximately thirty Dutch onions, or large red onions if Dutch onions are unavailable
4. When the beef is cooked, remove it to drain it well of its fat
5. Arrange it in the serving dish with the onions around it
6. Serve with a good sauce of fine color on top
