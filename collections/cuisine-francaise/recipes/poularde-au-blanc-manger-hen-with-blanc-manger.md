## Ingredients

- One pint of good milk
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Coriander
- One handful of bread crumbs
- One quarter pound of panne (lard), cut into small pieces
- One dozen sweet almonds, ground very fine
- Salt
- Nutmeg, grated
- Five raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the milk in a saucepan with thyme, laurel, basil, and coriander until reduced to half
2. Pass it through a sieve and add a handful of bread crumbs
3. Return to the fire until the bread has absorbed the milk
4. Remove from fire and add the panne cut into small pieces, the ground almonds, salt, grated nutmeg, and five raw egg yolks
5. Put all this into the body of the hen, which should be singed, gutted and well cleaned
6. Sew it up so nothing escapes
7. Cook it between lard strips
8. Moisten with milk, season with salt and a little coriander
9. When cooked and well wiped of its fat, serve with a sauce à la reine
