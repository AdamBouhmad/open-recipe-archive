## Ingredients

- one cod
- salted water

## Directions

1. Clean out your cod and wash it
2. Make well salted water, because the fish only takes up what is needed
3. When the water is clear, tie the head of your fish
4. Put it in the fish cooker with the salted water over it
5. Cook it over a very small fire
6. If serving it as a main course, add a cream sauce; if it is for a roast, serve it plain on a platter with a napkin and parsley leaves around it
7. You can also cook it in a court-bouillon like the turbot
