## Ingredients

- Flounder
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Butter
- Capers
- Anchovy butter or Spanish sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the flounder
2. Score them
3. Season with salt, pepper, and oil
4. Half an hour before serving, place them on the grill over moderately hot fire
5. When cooked, arrange them on the platter and mask them with butter sauce; sprinkle capers over them
6. You may also use Spanish sauce with the amount of anchovy butter the size of half an egg, or use Italian sauce
