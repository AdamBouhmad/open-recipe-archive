## Ingredients

- Milk
- Sugar
- Salt
- Four eggs (for one pint of milk)
- Bread

## Directions

1. When your milk has boiled, add sugar and a small pinch of salt
2. At the moment of serving, add a binding of four eggs to your hot milk
3. Put it on the fire and stir well with a wooden spoon
4. When you see your milk thicken and stick to your spoon, remove it from the fire (take care that it does not boil, as it would curdle)
5. Pour it over your bread
6. Choose well-sliced crust from the top of the loaf; soft bread is best for this soup
