## Ingredients

- Sea eel
- Water
- Salt
- Parsley root or parsley
- Bay leaves, three or four

## Directions

1. Cook your eel in water with salt, parsley root or parsley, and three or four bay leaves.
2. Mask it with a cream sauce or a brown sauce in which you put butter the size of half an egg mixed with anchovy butter, or with a tomato sauce. (Choose the sauce you prefer.)
