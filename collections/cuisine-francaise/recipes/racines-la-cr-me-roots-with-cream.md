## Ingredients

- large tender roots
- butter
- bouquet of parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- shallots
- cloves
- basil
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- bouillon
- egg yolks
- cream
- white vinegar

## Directions

1. Take large tender roots, scrape and wash them, blanch them for half an hour in boiling water
2. Cut them into thick strips and put them in a casserole with a piece of good butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a garlic clove, two shallots, two cloves, basil
3. Pass them over the fire, add a pinch of flour, salt, pepper, and good bouillon
4. Cook and reduce to a short sauce
5. Remove the bouquet and add a liaison of three egg yolks with cream, thicken without boiling
6. When serving add a large dash of white vinegar
