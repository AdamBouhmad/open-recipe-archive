## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Bouquet of herbs
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Always cook sheep's feet in water for whatever ragout you wish to make.
2. When well cooked, remove the leg bone and leave the foot whole.
3. Peel off any remaining hair and place in a saucepan with butter, bouillon, a bouquet of all sorts of fine herbs, salt, and pepper.
4. Boil gently for half an hour.
5. When they have taken on enough flavor, remove to a clean cloth to dry them of their fat.
6. Arrange on the serving platter and cover with whatever sauce you judge appropriate, such as à la Hollande, à l'Espagnole, or others found in the sauce section.
