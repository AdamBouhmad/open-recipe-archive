## Ingredients

- partridge
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- espagnole sauce
- lemon
- flour
- white wine
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Prepare your partridge as in the tartar recipe
2. Place a good piece of butter in a saucepan
3. Place your partridge in it stomach-side down
4. Twenty minutes before serving, cook it over a high heat
5. Add salt and pepper
6. Turn it when you feel it is firm under your finger
7. Remove it from heat and place it on the plate with espagnole sauce reduced with the juice of half a lemon
8. If you have neither sauce nor lemon, remove three-quarters of the butter from your saucepan
9. Add a little flour, a half-glass of white wine, as much bouillon, salt, and pepper
10. Let your sauce boil a few times and place it under your partridge
