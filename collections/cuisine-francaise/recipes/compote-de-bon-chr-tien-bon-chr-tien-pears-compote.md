## Ingredients

- Five fine bon-chrétien pears
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Cut the pears in half
2. Remove the cores
3. Plunge them in cold water
4. Blanch them
5. Peel them and place in water and lemon juice to keep them white
6. Cook them in the syrup
7. Add lemon juice
8. Arrange them on a serving dish
9. Reduce the syrup if it is too thin
