## Ingredients

- 1 partridge
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bread crumbs
- rémoulade or tartare sauce

## Directions

1. Clean, singe, truss, and split your partridge as in the previous recipe
2. Warm butter in a saucepan and dip your partridge in it so it is well coated
3. Dust with salt and coarse pepper
4. Roll it in bread crumbs so it is well breaded all over
5. Half an hour before serving, place your partridge on a grill over gentle heat
6. When serving, place rémoulade or tartare sauce on your platter with the partridge on top
7. If desired, you can serve clear jus underneath or a sharp sauce
