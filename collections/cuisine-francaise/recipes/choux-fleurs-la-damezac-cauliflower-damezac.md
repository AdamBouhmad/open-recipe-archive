## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- White sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflowers until somewhat firm.
2. Drain them.
3. Have ready a white sauce that is somewhat thickened.
4. Put the cauliflowers in it and sauté them in the sauce.
5. Dress them on the plate (see White Sauce).
