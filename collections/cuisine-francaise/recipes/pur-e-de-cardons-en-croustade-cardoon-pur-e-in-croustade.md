## Ingredients

- crumb from a large loaf of bread
- butter
- cardoon purée
- butter, the size of half an egg

## Directions

1. Make croûtons from the crumb of a large loaf of bread, leaving them one and a half inches thick
2. Make an incision on the edge before passing them in butter
3. Pass them in butter until well browned
4. Drain them and remove the interior crumb
5. Fill them with purée to which you add butter the size of half an egg
6. Mix the butter into the purée when it is very hot
