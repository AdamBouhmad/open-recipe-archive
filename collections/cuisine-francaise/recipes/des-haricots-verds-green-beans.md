## Ingredients

- Young tender green beans
- Butter
- Parsley
- Ciboule
- Flour
- Good bouillon
- Salt
- Three egg yolks
- Milk
- Verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Select very tender green beans and break off the small ends, then wash them and cook them in water
2. When cooked, drain the beans
3. In a saucepan, melt butter with chopped parsley and ciboule
4. Add the drained beans and toss them two or three times over the fire
5. Add a pinch of flour and a little good bouillon with salt, and allow them to boil until there is no more sauce
6. When ready to serve, add a liaison of three egg yolks mixed with milk, then a dash of verjuice or vinegar
7. Once the liaison has set on the fire, serve as an entremets
8. Alternatively, you may serve them in gravy by using coulis and veal juice instead of the liaison
