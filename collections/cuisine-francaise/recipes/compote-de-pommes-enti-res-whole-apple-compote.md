## Ingredients

- seven fine reinette apples
- water
- lemon juice
- half pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Take seven fine reinette apples; with a corer, you will remove the heart and skin from them; you put them in water and lemon juice
2. Clarify half a pound of sugar; leave your syrup a little thin; put your apples in it; make them cook on small boils; taste them often; as soon as the fork teeth enter into them, remove them and place them on your compote dish
3. Make the syrup reduce, and pour it under the apples
