## Ingredients

- Three small chickens à la reine
- Butter

## Directions

1. Flame the chickens
2. Empty them through the vent, truss them, and garnish them with butter
3. Cook them as you would cook braised chickens
4. At the moment of serving, drain them and unbridle them
5. Dress them on your platter with a hollandaise sauce and a large crayfish in each chicken
6. You may serve underneath a hot aspic, a tomato sauce, a crayfish butter, or a mixed ragout
