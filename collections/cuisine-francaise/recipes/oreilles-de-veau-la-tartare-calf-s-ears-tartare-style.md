## Ingredients

- four calf's ears
- a skewer for each ear
- butter
- bread crumb
- a little broth
- verjuice
- shallots
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch four calf's ears in boiling water
2. Split them at the thick end without separating them completely
3. Pass a skewer through each one to keep them open
4. Put them in a small pot to cook the same way as the previous recipe
5. When cooked and well drained, dip them in warm butter and bread them with bread crumb
6. Grill them, lightly basting with the remaining butter
7. When they have a nice color, serve them with a clear sauce made of a little broth, verjuice, chopped shallots, salt, and coarse pepper
