## Ingredients

- Three half-measures of cream
- Sugar
- Orange flowers
- Praline
- Candied citron
- Marzipan
- Eight eggs

## Directions

1. Boil three half-measures of cream with sugar, orange flowers, praline, candied citron, and marzipan, all chopped very finely.
2. Take eight eggs and beat the whites separately, setting the yolks aside.
3. Using a spoon, poach two or three spoonfuls of the beaten whites at a time in the cream, forming poached eggs without yolks.
4. Drain them and stack them one on top of another until you have formed eight poached eggs.
5. Arrange them on the serving dish.
6. Put the cream on the fire to reduce it to the consistency of a sauce.
7. When ready to serve, add the eight yolks and thicken over the fire without boiling, to prevent them from curdling.
8. Arrange the sauce over the egg whites.
