## Ingredients

- apricots
- boiling water
- fresh water
- one quarter pound of sugar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Blanch your apricots in boiling water; when they are very soft, remove them with a skimming spoon and put them in fresh water
2. Boil a quarter pound of sugar with a glass of water in a pan, put your apricots in it
3. Make two or three boils, skim them well, and remove them to arrange in a compote dish
4. Put your syrup over them to serve cold or hot, as you wish
