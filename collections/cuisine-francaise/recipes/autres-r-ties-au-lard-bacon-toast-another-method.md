## Ingredients

- One pound soft bread, long and stale
- Small bacon, cut into lardons

## Directions

1. Take a one-pound soft bread, long and stale
2. Cut off both ends
3. Lard the entire middle with small bacon cut into lardons
4. Cut the bread into slices the thickness of two coins
5. Dip these slices in beaten egg
6. Fry in oil that is not too hot at low heat until golden brown
7. Serve with clear sauce, a dash of vinegar, and some coarse pepper
