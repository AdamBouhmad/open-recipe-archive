## Ingredients

- sirloin fillet
- Spanish sauce
- lettuces

## Directions

1. Trim your sirloin fillet as you would trim it braised
2. Drain it and glaze it
3. Add a Spanish sauce to it and arrange a border of lettuces around it
4. If you do not have what you need to make your lettuces, cook your sirloin fillet in advance; use the stock for your lettuces; keep your fillet warm; one hour is enough to cook your lettuces
5. Put a bound sauce on your fillet
