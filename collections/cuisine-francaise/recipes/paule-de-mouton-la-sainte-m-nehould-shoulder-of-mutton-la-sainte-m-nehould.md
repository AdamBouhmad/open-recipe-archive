## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- bouillon
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- onions
- lardons
- salt
- pepper
- coulis
- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- shallot sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a shoulder of mutton with a little bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a garlic clove, three cloves, a bay leaf, thyme, basil, onions, lardons, salt, and pepper.
2. When it is cooked, remove it from the cooking liquid and drain it.
3. Dress it on the serving plate.
4. Make a well-bound sauce by taking two spoonfuls of coulis, putting it in a casserole with a piece of butter mixed with flour and three egg yolks.
5. Bind it over the fire and pour it over the shoulder.
6. Coat it with bread crumbs and gently moisten the bread crumbs with the degreased cooking liquid from the shoulder.
7. Brown it under a covered tourte dish with a little fire on top.
8. Drain the fat from the plate, wipe the edges, and serve under a clear shallot sauce or simply a little juice with salt and coarse pepper.
