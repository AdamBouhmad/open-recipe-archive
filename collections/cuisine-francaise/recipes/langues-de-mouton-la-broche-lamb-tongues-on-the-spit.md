## Ingredients

- four lamb tongues
- salt
- one onion studded with two cloves
- one carrot
- one parsnip
- coarse lard
- small lard
- paper
- three tablespoons of peas
- two tablespoons of verjuice
- a small piece of butter
- flour
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the four tongues in water with salt, an onion studded with two cloves, a carrot, and a parsnip
2. When almost cooked, remove the skin and lard them across with coarse lard, or better, cover all the top with small lard
3. Place them in a lardoir and attach to the spit, wrapped in paper
4. When cooked to a beautiful color, serve them with a sauce made as follows: put in a casserole three tablespoons of peas, two tablespoons of verjuice, a small piece of butter mixed with flour, salt, and coarse pepper, make it thicken on the fire and serve under the tongues
