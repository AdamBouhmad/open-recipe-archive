## Ingredients

- beef palate cooked in blanc
- velouté
- egg yolks
- butter
- bread crumbs
- salt
- coarse pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Your palates cooked in blanc, drain and cut them into small dice
2. Have a velouté much more reduced than for a sauce, which you bind with two or three egg yolks
3. Add a small piece of butter to give it softness
4. Throw your diced palates into your sauce and stir everything together
5. Make sure there is not too much sauce
6. Take some with a spoon and make thirty small portions on a board
7. Let them cool so they are more manageable
8. Then with these small portions, shape them with your hands into whatever form you want, whether pear-shaped, round, oval, etc.
9. First put them in bread crumbs
10. Then pass them à l'œuf, that is to say after the first breading, put them in beaten eggs seasoned with a little salt and coarse pepper
11. When your croquettes are soaked, put them in bread crumbs
12. At the moment of serving fry them
13. Your frying oil must be very hot
14. Put a little fried parsley on top
