## Ingredients

- sole, dab, plaice or flounder
- good butter
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- verjus

## Directions

1. Scale the fish
2. Melt good butter and put it in the serving dish with chopped parsley, onion, and mushrooms, salt and pepper
3. Arrange the fish on top
4. Make the same seasoning on top of the fish as underneath
5. Cover the dish well and cook over a small fire on a stove
6. When cooked, serve with a short sauce and add a dash of verjus on top
7. Alternatively, after preparing as above, you can cover the top with breadcrumbs and cook it in the oven or under a pie cover
