## Ingredients

- two lamb shoulders
- salt
- gros poivre (coarse pepper)
- aromates piles (ground aromatics)

## Directions

1. Debone two lamb shoulders entirely without leaving any bone
2. Season them inside with salt, coarse pepper, and a little ground aromatics
3. Gather the flesh together with a needle
