## Ingredients

- eight or ten nearly ripe peaches
- one quarter pound of sugar
- one glass of water
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Take eight or ten nearly ripe peaches that you leave whole, put them on a well-lit stove, burn all the skin evenly by turning them as you go, throw them in fresh water
2. When you have removed all the skin and washed in several waters, put them to cook with a quarter pound of sugar in a glass of water until they give way under your fingers
3. Arrange them in the compote dish and the syrup over them
