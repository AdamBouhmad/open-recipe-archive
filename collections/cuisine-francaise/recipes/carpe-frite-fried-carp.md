## Ingredients

- carp
- flour
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Scale and gut a carp; split it down the back so that the head is also separated; your carp should only hold together at the belly
2. Give several knife blows to the large bone so that your carp has a firm, flat appearance
3. Dust it with flour, as well as its milt or eggs, and put it in well-heated oil for frying
