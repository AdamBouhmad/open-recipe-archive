## Ingredients

- onion
- butter, one piece
- sheep's feet
- bouillon
- coulis, a little
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- vinegar, a thread of

## Directions

1. Cut the onion into threads
2. Put it in a saucepan with a piece of butter
3. Cook it halfway
4. Put the sheep's feet cut into three and thoroughly cleaned, moistened with bouillon and a little coulis seasoned with salt and pepper
5. When your ragout is cooked, add mustard, a thread of vinegar, and serve at a short sauce
