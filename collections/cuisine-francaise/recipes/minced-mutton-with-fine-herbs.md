## Ingredients

- Minced mutton
- Butter
- Shallots
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Coarse pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Salt
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Prepare the hash as previously described
2. Take a piece of butter twice the size of an egg and one spoonful of finely chopped shallots
3. Pass them in the butter without letting them brown
4. Add four spoonfuls of finely chopped mushrooms
5. Pass them with the shallots
6. Add one spoonful of finely chopped parsley
7. Stir everything on the fire
8. If you do not have sauce, add one spoonful of flour and mix well with the fine herbs
9. Pour in about two glasses of bouillon
10. If the sauce is too thin, reduce it until slightly thick
11. Pour the sauce over the minced mutton
12. Add a little coarse pepper and grated nutmeg
13. Mix the sauce well with the minced meat
14. At the moment of serving, check the seasoning and put croutons around it
