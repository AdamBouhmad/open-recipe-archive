## Ingredients

- Fillets from woodcocks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four spices
- Oil
- Garlic
- Woodcock trimmings
- Parsley
- Cloves
- Shallots
- Bay leaf
- Flour
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Lemon juice
- Lemon zest

## Directions

1. Prepare your fillets as the previous ones; season them with salt, pepper, and four spices; cover them with oil and add a clove of pounded garlic
2. Pour oil in a casserole; add your woodcock trimmings and let them brown with a pinch of parsley leaves, a clove of garlic, two cloves, six shallots, and a bay leaf
3. When your trimmings are well browned, add about a spoonful of flour; moisten them with a glass of white wine and three glasses of bouillon
4. Reduce your sauce by half, skim it, and strain it through cloth
5. When ready to serve, sauté your fillets and arrange them in crowns with a glazed crouton between each
6. Add the juice of one lemon and a little zest to your sauce and pour it under your fillets
