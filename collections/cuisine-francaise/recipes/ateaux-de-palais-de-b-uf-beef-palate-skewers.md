## Ingredients

- beef palate, cooked in blanc
- veal teat, cooked in marmite and cold
- sauce à atelet
- breadcrumbs
- anglaise for breading
- clear jus or clear espagnole or italienne sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked beef palate into squares of ten lines on all sides
2. Put them in sauce à atelet, sauté them in it, and let them cool
3. Cut the cold cooked veal teat into small thin squares the same size as the palate pieces
4. Alternate pieces of palate and teat on a skewer so that the atelet is well-formed
5. If it is not regular, fill gaps with the same sauce
6. First coat the atelets in breadcrumbs, then finish with anglaise breading
7. Ensure the atelets have their four square sides well united
8. Place them on the grill with gentle heat; you may grill only three sides and color the fourth with a field oven or glazing peel
9. Serve the atelets with clear jus, clear espagnole, italienne, or without sauce
