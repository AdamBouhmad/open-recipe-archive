## Ingredients

- 1 pound of butter
- 1 liter of flour, more if your butter can absorb more
- wheat flour (old flour is best; rye flour does not thicken well)

## Directions

1. Let 1 pound of butter melt in a casserole
2. Add 1 liter of flour, more if your butter can absorb it; that is to say, your flour mixed with the butter should be thicker than if it were a thin porridge; if it is too thin, add more flour
3. Place your butter and flour on a fairly hot stove
4. Stir it until your roux is somewhat blonde
5. You will have a gentle fire on which you place some ash; place your roux on it and let it go on low heat until it is a beautiful blonde
6. Be careful to use wheat flour; old flour is best; rye flour does not thicken well
7. You use this roux to thicken all the sauces mentioned
