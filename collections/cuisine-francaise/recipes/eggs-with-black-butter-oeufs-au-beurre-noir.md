## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter in a pan and melt it over the fire.
2. When it stops crackling, add eggs that have been broken into the pan and seasoned with salt and pepper.
3. Cook the eggs.
4. Pass a hot shovel over them to cook the yolk.
5. Serve with a dash of vinegar on top.
