## Ingredients

- One turkey
- Foie gras
- Mushrooms
- Small bacon
- Salt
- Fine spices
- Parsley
- Chives
- Bacon bard for the breast
- Cheesecloth
- Bouillon
- One glass of white wine
- Bouquet of fine herbs
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Flame the turkey over a well-lit stove
2. Bone the turkey completely
3. Mix together raw foie gras, mushrooms, and small bacon cut into small dice, seasoned with salt, fine spices, chopped parsley and chives
4. Place this mixture inside the body of the turkey
5. Sew the turkey closed and shape it like a loaf of bread
6. Place a bacon bard on the breast
7. Wrap the turkey in cheesecloth
8. Place it in a pot that is not too large, with bouillon, one glass of white wine, and a bouquet of fine herbs
9. Cook until done
10. Remove the turkey from the pot and keep it warm
11. Deglaze the bouillon in a saucepan and reduce it to a small sauce
12. Add two spoonfuls of coulis to the sauce
13. Unwrap the turkey from the cheesecloth, remove the string and bacon bards
14. Wipe the turkey clean of fat by pressing with white linen
15. Serve with the sauce poured over it
