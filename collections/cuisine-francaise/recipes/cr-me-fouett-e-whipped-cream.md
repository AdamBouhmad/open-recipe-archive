## Ingredients

- one pint of good cream
- a little candied orange blossom, finely chopped
- half a quarteron of fine sugar
- the size of a hazelnut of powdered gum tragacanth

## Directions

1. Put the cream in a bowl with the candied orange blossom, finely chopped, half a quarteron of fine sugar, and gum tragacanth the size of a hazelnut, powdered
2. Whip the cream and as it foams, remove it with a skimmer and place it on a sieve with a dish underneath to catch what drips
3. Continue whipping the cream until none remains in the bowl
4. If you do not have enough, take what dripped from the sieve and whip it again
5. Dress the cream in a compotier
6. It can be garnished with candied citron cut into strips, or served plain
7. Those who like lemon can add a little finely chopped green lemon to the cream before whipping
8. Dress it in the compotier in the form of a dome, or for variety, in the form of several small rocks
