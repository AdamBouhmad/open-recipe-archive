## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- godiveau farce
- lardons
- ham
- parsley
- chives
- garlic clove
- cloves
- bay leaf
- lardons
- parsnip
- salt
- pepper
- bouillon
- coulis

## Directions

1. Bone a shoulder of mutton and fill it with a godiveau farce the thickness of a small finger.
2. Cover the farce with slices of lardons and ham cut into fillets.
3. Put a little farce on top, just to hold them in place.
4. Roll the shoulder and wrap it tightly in linen.
5. Cook it in a little bouillon with a bouquet of parsley, chives, a garlic clove, three cloves, a bay leaf, lardons, parsnip, salt, and pepper.
6. When cooking is done, degrease the sauce and pass it through a sieve.
7. Reduce it if it is too thin.
8. Put it on ice with coulis to bind it.
9. Serve the shoulder glazed.
