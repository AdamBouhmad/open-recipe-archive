## Ingredients

- Several apples
- Powdered sugar
- Half a setier of brandy (eau-de-vie)
- Lemon juice
- Cinnamon powder
- Apricot marmalade

## Directions

1. Remove the peel and core from several apples with an apple corer
2. Cut them into slices of the same size and thickness
3. Put them in a terrine with powdered sugar, half a setier of brandy, lemon juice, and a little cinnamon powder
4. When the apples have infused for three or four hours, let them drain on a sieve
5. Arrange the apple slices in a miroton pattern around the plate with the scraps, and apricot marmalade in the middle
6. Add several rows of miroton so they form a dome
7. Half an hour before serving, put your plate in the oven or on red embers with the field oven on top
8. Make sure it has a nice color
9. You can do the same with pears
