## Ingredients

- Pork meat, the tenderest and most interlarded, or veal, hare, or rabbit well supplied with lard according to desired quantity
- Parsley, chopped
- Ciboules (chives), chopped
- Salt
- Mixed spices
- Intestines (boyaux) of appropriate thickness

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and mix with parsley, chopped chives, salt, and mixed spices
2. Take intestines of whatever thickness you judge appropriate, fill them with meat, and tie them at both ends
3. Put them to smoke in the chimney for two days
4. Cook them for two or three hours, according to their thickness, in broth without salt
