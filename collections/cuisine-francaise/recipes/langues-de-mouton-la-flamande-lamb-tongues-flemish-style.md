## Ingredients

- two or three onions, cut in slices
- butter
- flour
- one glass of white wine
- half a glass of juice
- mushrooms
- two shallots
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- coarse pepper
- a dash of vinegar
- three lamb tongues, cooked in water

## Directions

1. Cut two or three onions in slices and pass them on the fire with butter until they begin to brown
2. Add a pinch of flour and moisten with a glass of white wine and half a glass of juice
3. Add mushrooms, two shallots, parsley, and chives (all chopped very fine), salt, coarse pepper, and a dash of vinegar
4. Boil everything together for half a quarter hour
5. Have three lamb tongues cooked in water, peel them, and split them in two without separating them
6. Put them in the sauce to boil together until they have taken on flavor and little sauce remains
7. Serve
