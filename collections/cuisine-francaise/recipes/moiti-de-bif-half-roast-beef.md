## Ingredients

- Half lamb cut at two cutlets near the saddle
- Small atelets
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb at two cutlets near the saddle as squarely as possible
2. Secure the flanks with small atelets
3. Ensure your piece has a fine shape
4. Lard it to the end of both thighs
5. Leave a small distance from the thighs to the loins
6. Place your piece on the spit
7. Make more fire on the side of the thighs
8. If not larding your roast, bard it
9. If serving as a relevé, place clear stock underneath
