## Ingredients

- Three large pigeons
- Butter the size of an egg mixed with two pinches of flour
- Parsley in branches
- Whole chives
- Two onions in slices
- Zest of carrots and parsnips
- One whole clove of garlic
- Three cloves
- Salt
- Pepper
- One bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Three portions of milk
- Breadcrumbs for breading
- Fat from the sainte-Menèhoult
- Sauce rémoulade

## Directions

1. Empty the pigeons, keeping the livers
2. Truss the legs into the body
3. Singe and pluck them
4. Put in a casserole butter the size of an egg mixed with two pinches of flour, parsley in branches, whole chives, two onions in slices, zest of carrots and parsnips, one whole clove of garlic, three cloves, salt, pepper, one bay leaf, thyme, and basil
5. Moisten with three portions of milk
6. Bring to a boil and then put in the pigeons to cook over very low heat for one hour
7. When cooked, remove them to drain
8. Remove the fat from the sainte-Menèhoult and put it on a plate
9. Dip the pigeons in it and bread them as you go
10. Grill to a beautiful color, basting them with the remaining fat in which you dipped them
11. Serve dry with sauce rémoulade in the sauce boat
