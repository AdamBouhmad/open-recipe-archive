## Ingredients

- reinette apples
- sugar, equal weight to the marmalade

## Directions

1. Boil whole apples in water until they begin to soften under the fingers
2. Remove them to fresh water and peel them
3. Take the flesh and pass it through a sieve, pressing firmly
4. Put what has passed through in a pan and dry it on the fire until it is thick
5. Cook an equal weight of sugar to the large plume stage
6. Mix the marmalade and sugar together, stirring with a spatula or wooden spoon
7. Return to the fire only to heat through, stirring constantly
8. When it begins to boil, remove from the fire and put into jars when slightly cooled
9. Cover the jars only when they are completely cold
