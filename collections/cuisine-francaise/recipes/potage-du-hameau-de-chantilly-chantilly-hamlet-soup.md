## Ingredients

- Three pounds of beef brisket
- One whole veal knuckle
- Two partridges
- Two domestic pigeons
- Good bouillon or consommé
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Onions
- Leeks
- Two stalks of celery
- Two cloves
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Put the beef brisket, veal knuckle, partridges, and pigeons in a medium pot, ensuring the meats are well tied to remain whole
2. Fill your pot with good bouillon or consommé
3. Skim your pot
4. Garnish with carrots, turnips, onions, leeks, celery, and cloves
5. When your meats are well cooked, at the moment of serving, arrange them on a large shallow platter
6. Place around your meats carrots, turnips, onions, leeks by compartment, so the vegetables are not mixed together
7. Turn forty or fifty carrots into rounds two inches long, somewhat thick, all of the same length and thickness; prepare the same amount of turnips, onions, and medium leeks of the same thickness and well cleaned
8. Cook them in a separate bouillon, adding a small piece of sugar to each cooking to remove bitterness
9. When your vegetables are cooked, arrange them around your meats
10. Serve on the side a pot of bouillon from your pot, passed through a fine cloth or silk sieve so your bouillon is very clear
11. Season with good salt; do not serve the beef with this soup
