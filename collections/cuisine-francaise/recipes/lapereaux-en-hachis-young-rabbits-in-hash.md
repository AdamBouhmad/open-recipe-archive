## Ingredients

- leftover roasted young rabbits from the table
- some roasted mutton
- bones from the young rabbits
- butter
- shallots
- one half-garlic clove
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- two good pinches of flour
- one glass of red wine
- one glass of bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Take leftover roasted young rabbits that have been served from the table, remove all the meat
2. Mix with a little roasted mutton and hash everything together
3. Take the bones from the young rabbits, cut them into small pieces, put them in a saucepan with a little butter, some shallots, one half-garlic clove, thyme, bay leaf, basil
4. Pass them over the fire and add two good pinches of flour
5. Moisten with one glass of red wine and as much bouillon
6. Let boil for half an hour over low heat
7. Pass the sauce through a sieve and put the hashed meat in it with salt, coarse pepper
8. Heat without boiling
9. Serve hot; if you wish, garnish around the hash with fried croutons like those for spinach
