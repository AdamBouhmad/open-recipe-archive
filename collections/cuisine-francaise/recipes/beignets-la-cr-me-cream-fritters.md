## Ingredients

- half a septier of cream
- half a septier of milk
- a little salt
- a pinch of finely chopped green lemon
- three large spoonfuls of flour
- cream
- sugar
- red food coloring

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan half a septier of cream, half a septier of milk, a little salt, a pinch of finely chopped green lemon
2. Boil and reduce by half
3. Then add three large spoonfuls of flour that you dilute on the fire with cream, and stir until it is very thick
4. Remove from the fire to put on the table
5. Beat it with the roller until it is thin like a small coin
6. Cut it into diamonds
7. Fry and glaze with sugar and red food coloring
