## Ingredients

- butter
- mushrooms
- bouquet garni
- flour
- bouillon
- lamb fillets cooked on the spit
- egg yolks
- milk
- verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan with mushrooms cut into fillets and a bouquet garni, pass them over the fire, and add a pinch of flour
2. Moisten with bouillon and cook the mushrooms until there is scarcely any sauce remaining
3. Put in the lamb fillets cooked on the spit, cut into small thin pieces, with a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with milk
4. Make the sauce bind over the fire without boiling, season it to good taste
5. Add a dash of verjuice or vinegar when serving
