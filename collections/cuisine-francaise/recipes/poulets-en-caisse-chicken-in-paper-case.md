## Ingredients

- Two chickens
- Parsley
- Chives (ciboule)
- Shallots
- Garlic
- Fine oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lard bacon slices
- Paper
- Verjuice (optional)

## Directions

1. Singe and gut the chickens, truss the legs in the body, leave the wings and flatten the chickens slightly
2. Marinate them with whole parsley, chives, shallots, garlic, fine oil, salt, and black pepper
3. Make a paper case, put the chickens in it with all their seasoning, and cover them with lard slices and paper
4. Cook over low heat on the grill or under a pie dish cover; when cooked, remove the fine herbs and lard slices; serve in the case, putting a few drops of verjuice on the chickens; you can also remove them from the case and serve with the sauce of your choice
