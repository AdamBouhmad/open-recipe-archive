## Ingredients

- one or two turkey giblets (wings, legs, neck, liver, gizzard)
- a piece of butter
- a bouquet of parsley, chives, garlic clove
- two cloves
- thyme, bay leaf, basil
- mushrooms
- a good pinch of flour
- water or broth
- salt, coarse pepper
- three egg yolks
- cream
- vinegar or verjuice
- broth and jus (for the brown variation)
- small peas (optional)
- a good piece of butter (for the peas variation)

## Directions

1. Blanch the turkey giblets and clean them thoroughly
2. Place them in a saucepan with a piece of butter, a bouquet of parsley, chives, garlic clove, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, and mushrooms
3. Pass everything over the fire and add a good pinch of flour
4. Moisten with water or broth and season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Cook and reduce to a short sauce
6. When ready to serve, remove the bouquet
7. Add a liaison of three egg yolks with cream and thicken without boiling
8. Serve with a dash of vinegar or verjuice
9. For the brown sauce version: after flouring, moisten with equal parts broth and jus, let reduce to a short sauce
10. For the version with small peas: add them to the saucepan to cook together with a good piece of butter, flour, and moisten with equal parts broth and jus, then cook and reduce to a short sauce
