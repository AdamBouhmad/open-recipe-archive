## Ingredients

- Whiting fish
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Oil
- Butter
- Capers
- Tomato sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the whiting
2. Score them on both sides
3. Season with salt, coarse pepper, and oil
4. Half an hour before serving, place them on the grill over moderately hot fire
5. When grilled, mask them with butter sauce and sprinkle capers over them; you may also use tomato sauce
