## Ingredients

- a large pinch of parsley leaves
- a few chives
- two bay leaves
- a little thyme
- a strong pinch of fine pepper
- one glass of vinegar
- a little butter
- two large spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- one spoonful of bouillon

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan a large pinch of parsley leaves, a few chives, two bay leaves, a little thyme, a strong pinch of fine pepper, one glass of vinegar, and a little butter
2. Reduce the vinegar and seasoning until little remains in the saucepan
3. Pour in two large spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and one spoonful of bouillon
4. Reduce the sauce to its proper point and strain through a sieve without pressing
5. The vinegar and fine pepper should dominate the flavor
