## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Glazed onions
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Trim and braise your beef rib
2. When it is cooked, remove the string, drain it and arrange it on your dish; ensure it looks good and is completely intact
3. Arrange glazed onions around it and a clear Spanish sauce that you will have worked with a little of the marrow fat from your beef rib
4. Ensure your sauce is well degreased and passed through fine cloth
