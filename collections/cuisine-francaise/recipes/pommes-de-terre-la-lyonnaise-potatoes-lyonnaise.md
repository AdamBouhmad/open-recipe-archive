## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- onions
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- bouillon or water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in water and cut them into slices
2. Place them in a casserole
3. Make a clear onion purée and pour it over the potatoes; keep warm without boiling
4. Alternatively, put a good piece of butter in a casserole and cut eight onions into slices
5. Place on the fire and when well browned, add one teaspoon of flour, mixing well with the onions
6. Add salt, coarse pepper, one small pot spoon of bouillon or water, and a dash of vinegar
7. Simmer the onions for a quarter hour
8. Place them on the potatoes and keep warm
