## Ingredients

- one quarteron of healthy reinette apples
- one half-setier of water
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Take one quarteron of healthy reinette apples, cut them into slices as thinly as you can
2. Cook them with one half-setier of water
3. When they are in marmalade, put them in a cloth to wring them strongly and express all the juice
4. Let this juice rest and draw it off clear
5. On one half-setier, cook one pound of sugar in the same way as for the cherry syrup
6. When it is at its cooking point, add your juice
