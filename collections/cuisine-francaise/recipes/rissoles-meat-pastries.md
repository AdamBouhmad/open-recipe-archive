## Ingredients

- leftover roasted meat
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- flour
- bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- pastry dough made with flour, butter, water, and salt

## Directions

1. Cut leftover roasted meat into small pieces the size of peas
2. Pass the pieces in a pan with good butter, chopped parsley, and chopped chives
3. Add a pinch of flour and moisten with a little bouillon
4. Season with salt and coarse pepper
5. Reduce to a short sauce so it sticks to the meat
6. Let cool
7. Make a pastry dough with flour, a little butter, water and fine salt, knead and roll with a roller as thin as a coin
8. Cut circles from the dough, fill each with a small amount of meat
9. Fold and seal well together
10. Cut with a small wheel or knife
11. Fry until they are a beautiful golden color
12. Serve as an appetizer
