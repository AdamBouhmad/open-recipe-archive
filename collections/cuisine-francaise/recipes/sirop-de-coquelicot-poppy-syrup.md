## Ingredients

- half a pound of poppies
- one chopine of boiling water
- one pound of sugar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of poppies in a terrine and pour a chopine of boiling water over them
2. Let infuse for twenty-four hours on hot ashes
3. Boil for two boils and pass through a sieve, pressing strongly to extract all the juice
4. Put one pound of sugar in a pan with a glass of water
5. Make it boil and skim well
6. Add your poppy water and boil together until the consistency of syrup
7. You will know it is done when, taking it on the finger and pressing it against the other, a thread forms that does not break easily
