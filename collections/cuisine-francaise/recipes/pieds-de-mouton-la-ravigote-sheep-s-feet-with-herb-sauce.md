## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Butter
- Bouquet garni
- Bouillon
- Coulis
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chervil
- Burnet
- Purslane
- Staghorn
- Balm
- Tarragon
- Chives

## Directions

1. After cooking them in water, remove the large bones.
2. Place them in a saucepan with butter, a bouquet garni, bouillon, coulis, salt, and pepper.
3. Boil until the sauce is almost reduced.
4. Prepare the ravigote by blanching chervil, burnet, purslane, staghorn, a little balm, a little tarragon, and chives for about fifteen minutes at most.
5. Remove from water, press, and chop very fine.
6. Serve the herbs in the ragout, ensuring the sauce is neither too clear nor too thick, and season to good taste.
