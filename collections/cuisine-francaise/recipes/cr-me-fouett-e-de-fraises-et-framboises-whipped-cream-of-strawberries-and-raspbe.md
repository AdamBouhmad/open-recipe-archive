## Ingredients

- three half-setiers of double cream
- two handfuls of strawberries or raspberries, well cleaned
- two ounces of fine sugar

## Directions

1. Whip three half-setiers of double cream, and as it foams, remove it with a skimmer and place it in a sieve with a dish underneath to catch what drips
2. When you have whipped it all, take two handfuls of strawberries or raspberries that are well cleaned and pass them through a sieve by pressing them hard
3. Mix in two ounces of fine sugar with the strawberries or raspberries
4. Mix everything together by whipping it with the cream a moment before serving, then dress it in the compotier
