## Ingredients

- calf's mesentery
- water
- two carrots
- three onions
- two cloves
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- bouquet of parsley and chives
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the mesentery well in cold water
2. Put it in a pot of boiling water and boil for about a quarter hour
3. Put it in cold water until completely cooled
4. Tie it up and cook it in a blanc, or alternatively cook it with water, two carrots, three onions, two cloves, two bay leaves, a little thyme, a bouquet of parsley and chives, plus a glass of vinegar, and salt for two hours
5. For the sauce, boil vinegar with salt and fine pepper, making sure the seasoning is strong since the mesentery is very bland by itself
6. Serve with the vinegar sauce
