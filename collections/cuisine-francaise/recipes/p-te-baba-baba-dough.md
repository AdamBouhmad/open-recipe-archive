## Ingredients

- leavening made as for brioche dough
- 1 pound of raisins in a box
- 1/4 pound of currants
- 1 teaspoon of saffron powder

## Directions

1. Make the leavening as for brioche dough
2. In the dough mixture, add 1 pound of raisins in a box, 1/4 pound of currants, and 1 teaspoon of saffron powder for a quarter batch
3. Follow the same process as for brioche dough, but keep the dough much softer
4. When the dough is finished, butter a large casserole and put your dough in it
5. Place it in a warm location and let it rise for 6 hours
6. Your dough will be very swollen
7. Put it in a hot oven as for brioche
