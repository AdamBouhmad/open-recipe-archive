## Ingredients

- six pigeons
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- grated nutmeg
- juice of one lemon
- bay leaf
- flour
- consommé
- mushrooms
- one onion
- clove
- two eggs
- breadcrumbs
- beaten eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Lightly singe six spoon-style pigeons
2. Put a piece of butter in a casserole with salt, coarse pepper, a little grated nutmeg, the juice of one lemon, a bay leaf
3. Put your pigeons in it and place over the fire
4. When they are well browned, take half a tablespoon of flour which you mix with your butter
5. Add a full tablespoon of consommé and let them boil on high heat
6. Add mushrooms and an onion studded with a clove
7. After a quarter hour remove your pigeons from the sauce
8. If the sauce is not sufficiently reduced, leave it on the fire until only about four tablespoons remain
9. Add a liaison of two eggs
10. Remove the bay leaf and onion
11. Pour your sauce over your pigeons and let them cool
12. Chop the mushrooms and mix them with the sauce
13. Use this to stuff your small pigeons
14. Coat them well with sauce and put them in breadcrumbs, then in beaten seasoned eggs
15. Coat a second time
16. At the moment of serving, fry them
17. When they have a nice color, arrange them on your plate with fried parsley in the middle
18. Once your pigeons are cooked and cold, you can cut them in half, stuff them and bread them the same way, then fry them
