## Ingredients

- Small root vegetables and vegetables such as carrots
- Turnips
- Small onions
- Peas
- Asparagus
- White beans
- Green beans
- Artichoke hearts
- Eggplants
- Cauliflower
- Cucumbers
- Small broad beans

## Directions

1. Prepare many small roots and vegetables as described for macédoine in entrées
2. Cut carrots, turnips, small onions, peas, asparagus, white beans, green beans, artichoke hearts, eggplants, cauliflower, cucumbers, and small broad beans
3. Prepare in portions of approximately equal and uniform size
4. Cook the vegetables in appropriate seasoning
5. Drain the vegetables thoroughly until well drained
