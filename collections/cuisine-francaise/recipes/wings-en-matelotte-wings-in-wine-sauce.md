## Ingredients

- one spoonful of flour and butter
- one half-septier of white wine
- one half-septier of good bouillon
- wings
- parsley, chives, two cloves of garlic, thyme, basil, two cloves, salt, coarse pepper
- at least a dozen small white onions, blanched
- bread crumbs the size of a small coin
- butter
- a pinch of fine capers

## Directions

1. Make a roux with flour and butter, moisten with white wine and bouillon
2. Put in the wings with herbs and seasonings
3. At half cooking time, add the small onions that have been blanched for a quarter hour and cleaned
4. Cut bread into small pieces and brown them in butter
5. When the ragout is finished with reduced sauce, add the capers
6. Arrange the wings and croutons on a platter and pour sauce over
