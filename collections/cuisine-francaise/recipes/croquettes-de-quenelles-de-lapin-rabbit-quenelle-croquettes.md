## Ingredients

- Rabbit quenelles
- Velouté sauce
- Game fumé
- Egg yolks
- Eggs
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Oil or fat for frying
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Make your quenelles and poach them as directed in the rabbit quenelles article
2. Place them on a dish and let them drain
3. Reduce velouté with game fumé
4. When well reduced, add a liaison of three egg yolks
5. Pour your sauce over your quenelles
6. Let them cool and coat well with sauce
7. Place in breadcrumbs
8. Break four eggs in which you put a little salt and coarse pepper and beat as for an omelet
9. Dip your quenelles in the beaten egg ensuring it covers everywhere
10. Place them in breadcrumbs and give them an attractive shape
11. Half an hour before serving, put your frying oil on the fire
12. When well hot, place your croquettes in it
13. When they have a good color, remove them
14. Drain them on white linen
15. Fry parsley leaves and place on top of your croquettes
