## Ingredients

- One duck
- Half-litre of dried peas or one litre of fresh peas
- Bouillon
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bouquet of parsley, chives, thyme, laurel, basil
- Half clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- Optional: small piece of bacon with rind

## Directions

1. Cook half-litre of dried peas with a little bouillon, parsley, and chive tops; if using fresh peas, use one litre and omit the parsley and chives
2. Pass the peas through a sieve to make a thick purée
3. Cook a duck with bouillon, salt, pepper, a bouquet of parsley, chives, thyme, laurel, basil, half a clove of garlic, and two cloves
4. When the duck is cooked, pass its sauce through a sieve and add it to the purée to give it body
5. Reduce the purée until it is neither too thin nor too thick
6. Optionally, cook a piece of small bacon cut in slices with the rind attached and serve it around the duck
7. Serve the duck
