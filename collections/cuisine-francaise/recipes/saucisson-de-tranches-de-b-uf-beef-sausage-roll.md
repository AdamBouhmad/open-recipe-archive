## Ingredients

- A piece of beef cut the width of two hands and thick as two fingers
- Beef fat
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Two shallots
- Basil leaves
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four egg yolks
- Bouillon
- A glass of white wine
- An onion
- Two cloves
- A carrot
- A parsnip

## Directions

1. Cut the beef in half lengthwise while keeping the same width
2. Flatten it by beating
3. Trim the edges to make them even
4. Mince the trimmings with beef fat, parsley, chives, mushrooms, two shallots, and basil leaves, all chopped very finely
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Bind the filling with four egg yolks and spread it on the beef slices
7. Roll them in the shape of a sausage and tie with string
8. Cook in a casserole with a little bouillon, a glass of white wine, salt, pepper, an onion, two cloves, a carrot, and a parsnip
9. Cook on a low fire
10. Pass the cooking liquid through a sieve and remove the fat
11. Reduce it to the consistency of a sauce without strong flavor
12. Serve the sauce over the sausages
13. You can also serve them with vegetable ragout as you see fit; if you wish to serve them cold as a side dish, reduce the sauce by letting it boil with the sausages until almost no fat remains, let cool with what remains, and serve on a napkin
