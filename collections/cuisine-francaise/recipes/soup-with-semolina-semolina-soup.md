## Ingredients

- bouillon, passed through silk sieve
- semolina
- veal stock or coloring as needed

## Directions

1. Pass bouillon through a silk sieve into a saucepan
2. When it boils, take semolina and pour it into your boiling bouillon
3. Stir carefully with a large spoon so that the semolina does not stick or form lumps
4. After half an hour, remove it from the fire; it will be sufficiently cooked
5. Be careful to degrease your soup; if it is too pale, color it with veal stock or whatever you have to give it a beautiful color
6. Ensure it has good flavor and proper seasoning
