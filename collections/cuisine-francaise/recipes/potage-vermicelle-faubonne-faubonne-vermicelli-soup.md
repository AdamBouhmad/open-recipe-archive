## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- leeks
- onions
- parsley root
- a good piece of butter
- water
- vermicelli
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the roots into small strips instead of dice
2. Cook your vegetables before putting in your vermicelli
3. Then, your vegetables cooked, put your vermicelli in your broth, which you take care to skim from time to time so that your vermicelli does not clump together
4. Salt as needed and coarse pepper, that is its seasoning
