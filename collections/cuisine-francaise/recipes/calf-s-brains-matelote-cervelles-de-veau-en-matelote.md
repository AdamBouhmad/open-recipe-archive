## Ingredients

- Calf's brains
- Lard
- Carrots
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Parsley
- Chives
- White wine
- Small onions
- Butter
- Roux
- Jus
- Mushrooms
- Bouquet garni
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains as directed for braised
2. Cook between lard, two carrots, three onions cut in slices, two bay leaves, parsley and chives bouquet
3. Moisten with white wine
4. Cook for three quarters of an hour
5. When cooked, at moment of serving, drain and arrange on a plate
6. Have small onions well cleaned
7. Sauté them in butter until blonde
8. Transfer to another pot
9. Make a light roux and moisten with white wine and a little jus
10. Pour the sauce over the onions
11. Add mushrooms and bouquet garni
12. Cook everything together
13. Skim and degrease the ragout
14. When cooked, pour over the brains
15. The sauce should be neither too thick nor too thin and properly salted
