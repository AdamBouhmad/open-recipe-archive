## Ingredients

- One duck
- Bouillon
- Half glass of white wine
- Bouquet of parsley, chives, thyme, laurel, basil
- Two cloves
- Seven or eight large onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the duck with a little bouillon and half glass of white wine, and a bouquet of parsley, chives, thyme, laurel, basil, and two cloves
2. In a casserole, place seven or eight large onions cut into slices with a piece of butter
3. Pass them over the fire, turning often until they are colored
4. Add a good pinch of flour
5. Moisten with the duck's cooking liquid
6. Cook the onions and reduce to short sauce
7. Degrease it and add a dash of vinegar
8. Serve over the duck
