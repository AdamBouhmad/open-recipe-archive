## Ingredients

- Carp
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Caper sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the carp well
2. Cut it into pieces and place on a plat with parsley, chives, salt, pepper and oil
3. Three-quarters of an hour before serving, place it on the grill over a fairly hot fire
4. When grilled, coat it with caper sauce
