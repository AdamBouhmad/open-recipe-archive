## Ingredients

- Butter
- Two carrots
- One parsnip
- Two or three onions
- One large clove of garlic
- Two cloves
- One bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- One glass of white wine
- Bouillon
- Good butter
- Flour
- Two pinches of chervil

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a little butter with two carrots, one parsnip cut in strips, two or three onions in slices, one large clove of garlic, two cloves, one bay leaf, thyme, and basil
2. Cook over medium heat until slightly colored
3. Moisten with one glass of white wine and as much bouillon
4. Cook over low heat and reduce by half
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Add a piece of good butter the size of half an egg worked with a good pinch of flour and two pinches of chervil chopped very finely
7. Thicken this sauce over the fire
8. Serve over chickens cooked on the spit
