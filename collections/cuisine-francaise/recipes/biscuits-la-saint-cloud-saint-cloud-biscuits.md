## Ingredients

- Two ounces of rice flour sifted fine
- Half pound of fine sugar
- Four egg yolks
- Green lemon zest, finely chopped
- Eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of rice flour sifted fine in a bowl with half pound of fine sugar, four egg yolks, and a pinch of finely chopped green lemon zest
2. Beat everything together for a quarter hour
3. Add eight whipped egg whites
4. Dress your biscuits in small buttered paper molds
5. Bake in a gentle oven
6. When they are a beautiful golden color, remove them from the molds while hot
7. Cover the entire top with a glaze made as follows: put on an earthenware or faience plate (recipe continues on next page)
