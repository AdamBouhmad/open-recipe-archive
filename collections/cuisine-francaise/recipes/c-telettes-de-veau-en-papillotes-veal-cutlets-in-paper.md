## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Fine herbs for papillotes
- Paper
- Oil
- Thin strips of lard
- String
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Cut and trim the cutlets as in sautéed cutlets.
2. Take a good piece of butter and melt it in a casserole.
3. Place the cutlets on top.
4. Season them with salt and a little coarse pepper.
5. Cook them almost through in the butter.
6. After, place them on a platter and pour the butter in which they have cooked over them.
7. Add fine herbs for papillotes.
8. Let the cutlets cool.
9. Cut a square of paper large enough to easily contain your cutlets.
10. Oil it, that is to say put a little oil on top and rub it.
11. When the paper is prepared, place a thin strip of lard on top.
12. Then place your cutlet with fine herbs on each side.
13. Cover it with a thin strip of lard.
14. Fold the paper over your cutlet.
15. Trim the angles of the paper.
16. Pleat it so that the seasoning cannot escape when on the grill.
17. The pleats of your paper must be as close as possible to the flesh of your cutlet.
18. Tie the end where you finish the pleats of your paper, which must be found at the end of the bone, so that neither the jus nor the butter can escape.
19. A quarter hour before serving, place your cutlets on the grill.
20. Be careful that the fire is gentle so the paper does not burn and your cutlets cook.
21. Arrange them in crowns.
22. Put clear jus underneath.
