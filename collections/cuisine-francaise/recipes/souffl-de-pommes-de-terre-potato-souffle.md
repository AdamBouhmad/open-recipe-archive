## Ingredients

- One chopine of cream
- Six ounces of sugar
- Six tablespoons of potato starch
- Four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil one chopine of cream with six ounces of sugar
2. Mix six tablespoons of potato starch with the eggs and cream
