## Ingredients

- Roasted chickens
- Egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or oil

## Directions

1. Take roasted chickens that have been served from the table
2. Cut them into pieces and skewer each piece on silver skewers or small wooden skewers
3. Dip them in beaten egg, season with salt, pepper, chopped parsley, chives
4. Bread them and dip in butter or oil, bread them again
5. Grill over gentle fire, lightly basting with a little oil
6. Serve dry or with a clear sauce
