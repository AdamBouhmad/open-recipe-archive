## Ingredients

- Pork head
- Lard
- Salt
- Mixed spices
- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Garlic
- Bouillon
- Red wine
- Bouquet garni
- Onions
- Roots
- Pepper
- White cloth

## Directions

1. Sear the head over a clear fire on a well-heated stove
2. Scrub it vigorously with a brick, then with a knife
3. Once clean, debone it halfway without removing the skin
4. Lard it on the inside with thick lard
5. Season with salt, mixed spices, parsley, chives, mushrooms, and garlic, all chopped
6. Wrap in white cloth, tie it, and cook in a good braising liquid made with bouillon, red wine, a large bouquet garni, onions, roots, salt and pepper
7. Once cooked, let it cool in the braising liquid
8. Drain it on a napkin to remove excess liquid
9. Serve cold as a main course
