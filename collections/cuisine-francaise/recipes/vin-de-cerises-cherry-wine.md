## Ingredients

- Fifteen pounds of cherries
- Two pounds of currants
- Two-thirds of the pits from the cherries

## Directions

1. To make five pints of cherry wine, take fifteen pounds of cherries with two pounds of currants that you crush well together
2. Crush two-thirds of the pits and mix them together
3. Put everything in a barrel
