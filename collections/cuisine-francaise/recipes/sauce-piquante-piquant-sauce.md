## Ingredients

- Butter
- Two large onions, sliced
- One carrot
- One parsnip
- Thyme, bay leaf, basil
- Two cloves
- Two shallots
- One clove of garlic
- Parsley, chives
- A good pinch of flour
- Broth
- One spoonful of vinegar
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter in a saucepan with two large onions in slices, one carrot, one parsnip, a little thyme, bay leaf, basil, two cloves, two shallots, one clove of garlic, parsley, chives
2. Pass everything together over the fire until it is well colored
3. Add a good pinch of flour and moisten with broth and one spoonful of vinegar
4. Boil over very low heat, skim and pass through a sieve
5. Season with salt and coarse pepper
6. Use this sauce for everything that needs to be enhanced
