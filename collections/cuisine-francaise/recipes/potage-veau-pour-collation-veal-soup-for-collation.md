## Ingredients

- a quarter of cabbage
- four roots
- two parsnips
- six onions
- a foot of celery
- a small parsley root
- three or four turnips
- sorrel
- beet greens
- chervil
- half-liter of peas

## Directions

1. Take a pot of about three pints.
2. Put in it a quarter of cabbage, four roots, two parsnips, six onions, a foot of celery, a small parsley root, and three or four turnips.
3. Make a bundle with sorrel, beet greens, and chervil, which you tie well together.
4. Tie a half-liter of peas in white linen.
5. Boil all these vegetables with water for three hours.
6. Pass this broth through a sieve.
7. Simmer your soup after having added to the broth the salt it needs.
8. You will garnish the soup with the vegetables that are in the pot.
9. If you wish, you can regulate putting more or less vegetables according to the quantity of broth you want to make.
