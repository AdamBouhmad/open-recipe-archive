## Ingredients

- Fifteen martin sec pears
- One half-pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Hollow out the head of each pear slightly
2. Shorten the stem by detaching it a little from the pear
3. Clarify one half-pound of sugar and extend the syrup with water
4. Place the pears in the syrup
5. Simmer them for a good hour
6. When they are almost cooked, let them reach a full boil
7. Remove them from the heat and arrange on a serving dish
8. When the syrup is sufficiently reduced, pour it over the pears
