## Ingredients

- calf's fressure (lung, heart, and spleen)
- good butter
- a bouquet garni
- a pinch of flour
- broth
- three egg yolks
- a little milk
- verjuice

## Directions

1. Cut the fressure into pieces
2. Soak in cold water to disgorge, then blanch briefly in boiling water
3. Put in a casserole with a piece of good butter and a bouquet garni
4. Pass over the fire and add a pinch of flour
5. Moisten with broth
6. When the ragout is cooked and well seasoned, add a liaison of three egg yolks diluted with a little milk
7. Thicken over the fire
8. Before serving, add a hint of verjuice
