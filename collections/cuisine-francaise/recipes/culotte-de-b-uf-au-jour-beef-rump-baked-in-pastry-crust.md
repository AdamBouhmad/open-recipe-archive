## Ingredients

- one beef rump of desired size
- coarse lard
- salt
- fine spices
- one chopine white wine
- pastry dough

## Directions

1. Take a beef rump of the size you judge appropriate
2. Bone it if you wish and lard it with coarse lard seasoned with salt and fine spices
3. Put it in a vessel just fitting its size with one chopine of white wine
4. Cover with a lid and seal the edges with pastry dough
5. Bake in the oven for five or six hours depending on its size
6. Serve it with its sauce well defatted
