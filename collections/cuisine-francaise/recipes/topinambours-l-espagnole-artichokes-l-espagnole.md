## Ingredients

- topinambours
- pepper
- espagnole sauce
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Turn the topinambours, that is, remove the skin by cutting into the vegetable
2. Wash them well
3. Place them in a casserole with a little coarse pepper, four tablespoons of espagnole sauce, and one pot spoon of bouillon
4. Place on the fire and let them boil for three quarters of an hour
5. If the sauce is not reduced enough, remove the topinambours and reduce the sauce further
6. Arrange the topinambours on a platter or in an entremets casserole and pour the sauce over them
