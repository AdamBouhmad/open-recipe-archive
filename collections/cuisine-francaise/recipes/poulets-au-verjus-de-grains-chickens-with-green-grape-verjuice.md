## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Chicken liver
- Butter
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Two onions
- One clove of garlic
- One carrot
- One parsnip
- Two cloves
- Flour
- One glass of bouillon
- A good handful of green grape verjuice
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Singe, pluck and empty the chickens
2. Stuff the inside with the liver mixed with butter, parsley, chives, salt, coarse pepper
3. Cook on the spit
4. Put in a saucepan a little butter with two onions, one clove of garlic, parsley, chives, one carrot, one parsnip, two cloves
5. Pass all together over the fire until colored
6. Add a good pinch of flour
7. Moisten with one glass of bouillon
8. Let cook and reduce to half
9. Strain through a sieve
10. Take a good handful of green grape verjuice, remove the seeds and blanch them an instant in boiling water
11. Remove them to drain
12. Put them in the sauce with two egg yolks, bind over the fire without boiling, stirring always
13. As soon as it reaches a boil, remove from the fire and serve over the chickens
