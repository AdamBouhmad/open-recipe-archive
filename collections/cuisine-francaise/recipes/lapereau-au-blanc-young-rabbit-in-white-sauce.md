## Ingredients

- Skinned and gutted young rabbit
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Mushrooms
- Lemon
- Water
- Parsley
- Chives
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Pepper
- Bacon

## Directions

1. Skin your young rabbit and gut it
2. Cut it into pieces like the previous recipe
3. Remove the liver and lungs
4. Wipe the pieces well so there is no blood, otherwise you would need to blanch it and that causes it to lose flavor
5. Put a quarter pound of butter in a casserole
6. Warm it and put your young rabbit inside
7. Brown it over high heat
8. When you see that all your pieces are stiffened, pour in two tablespoons of flour that you mix with your butter and rabbit
9. Add four tablespoons of bouillon
10. Stir your stew well until it boils
11. Add mushrooms that you have sautéed in water and lemon, a bouquet of parsley and chives, a bay leaf, a little thyme, a little pepper, and about half a pound of bacon that you have cut into small pieces
