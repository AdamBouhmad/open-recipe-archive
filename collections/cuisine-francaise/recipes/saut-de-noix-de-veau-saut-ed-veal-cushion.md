## Ingredients

- One veal cushion
- Three quarter-pounds of butter
- Fine salt and coarse pepper
- Parsley and chives, finely chopped
- Velouté sauce
- Two eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the veal cushion
2. Cut the cushion into long pieces, then into thin slices slightly larger than a small coin and one and a half lines thick
3. Beat each small piece with the back of your knife and arrange them in your sauté pan
4. When they are well rounded and the cushion is cut, trimmed, and arranged, warm three quarter-pounds of butter and pour it over the veal pieces seasoned with fine salt, coarse pepper, parsley and chives finely chopped
5. Just before serving, place the sauté pan on high heat
6. When the pieces are slightly heated on one side, turn them; they should barely see the fire
7. When the sauté is cooked, remove it from the butter and put it in a casserole
8. Make a reduction of slightly more velouté than usual
9. Add the juice thrown off by the sauté
10. Mix in a liaison of two eggs
11. When the sauce is bound, strain it through a cloth over the sauté
12. Toss it so it takes the sauce and dress it on the plate
