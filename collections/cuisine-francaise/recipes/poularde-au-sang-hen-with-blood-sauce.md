## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Chives
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- One good half-setier of pork blood
- Four egg yolks
- One quarter pound of panne (lard), chopped
- A small pinch of ground coriander
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan chopped parsley, chives, mushrooms, and a small piece of butter
2. Cook on the fire, then add a good half-setier of pork blood with four egg yolks, chopped panne, a small pinch of ground coriander, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Thicken all together on the fire without letting it boil, stirring well
4. When cold, put it in the body of a hen
5. Cook the hen on the spit wrapped in lard and paper
6. Serve with a piquant sauce
