## Ingredients

- dozen larks
- butter
- bouquet garni
- mushrooms
- calf sweetbread
- flour
- white wine
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pluck, singe and clean a dozen larks
2. Truss the feet to pass through the beak as for roasting
3. Pass them in a saucepan over the fire with a piece of butter, a bouquet garni, mushrooms and a calf sweetbread
4. Add a good pinch of flour
5. Moisten with a glass of white wine, bouillon and juice as needed to give color
6. Boil and reduce to the point of a bound sauce
7. Degrease and season with salt and coarse pepper
