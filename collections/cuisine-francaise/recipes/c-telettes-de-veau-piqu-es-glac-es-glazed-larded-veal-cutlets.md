## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets from near the kidney
- Fine lard
- Slices of veal
- Carrots
- Onions
- Laurel leaf
- Two cloves
- Consommé or bouillon
- Paper

## Directions

1. Take the cutlets closest to the kidney
2. Trim them as well as possible
3. Do not flatten them too much so they keep their thickness
4. Lard them with fine lard
5. Put lard in the bottom of a casserole, a few slices of veal, some carrots and onions, a laurel leaf, two cloves
6. Put your cutlets on this seasoning
7. Ensure that the larding does not become hidden so that when cooking they can glaze
8. Put a round of buttered paper over the top and fill about one spoon-full with consommé or bouillon
9. Place your casserole on the fire; when the inside boils, put its lid on it and put a little hot fire on top
10. Then place your casserole on gentle fire, but one that always keeps boiling
11. It takes an hour and a half to cook your cutlets
12. At the moment of serving, drain them on a casserole lid, glaze them and serve underneath cucumbers, chicory, tomato sauce, sorrel purée, mushroom purée, spinach, espagnole sauce, sautéed mushrooms, etc.
13. You can also put these cutlets in a buttered casserole moistened with jelly, consommé, or bouillon, and the above seasoning
