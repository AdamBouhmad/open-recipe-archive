## Ingredients

- one dozen very ripe quinces
- one pound of brown sugar
- one half-setier of liquid

## Directions

1. Take a dozen very ripe quinces, remove their cores and skins
2. Pound the flesh and put it in a large cloth to wring it with force of arms, by this process you will draw out all the juice
3. Let this juice rest and draw it off clear
4. On one half-setier you will take one pound of brown sugar which you will cook in the same way as that of verjuice syrup
5. When your brown sugar has its degree of cooking add the quince juice and let them boil together until the syrup has the same consistency as the previous one
