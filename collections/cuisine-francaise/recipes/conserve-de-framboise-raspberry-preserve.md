## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 glass water
- 1 pound raspberries
- 2 ounces redcurrants

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of sugar in the same way as for redcurrant preserve
2. Crush and pass through a sieve 1 pound of raspberries with 2 ounces of redcurrants, all cleaned
3. Put what has passed through the sieve into a pan on the fire to dry it
4. Put it into the sugar and finish your preserve as with the redcurrant preserve
