## Ingredients

- Two cold chickens cooked in a poêle
- Velouté
- Jelly
- Tarragon vinegar
- Coarse pepper
- One egg yolk
- Parsley
- Tarragon

## Directions

1. Cut up two cold chickens that have been cooked in a poêle as correctly as possible.
2. Put the pieces in a casserole with velouté, eight degrease-spoonfuls, four spoonfuls of jelly, and two spoonfuls of tarragon vinegar and coarse pepper.
3. Reduce everything by one-third.
4. If your sauce is somewhat brown, add a liaison of one egg.
5. When reduced to the proper point, add some finely chopped parsley and tarragon.
6. Bring two boils to your sauce.
7. Check the seasoning and put the sauce on your cold chicken pieces.
8. Let it cool.
9. Dress it on your platter.
