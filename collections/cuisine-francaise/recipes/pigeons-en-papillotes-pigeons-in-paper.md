## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- coarse pepper
- aromatic spices
- butter
- one quarter pound of minced fat pork
- four tablespoons of oil
- three tablespoons of finely chopped mushrooms
- one tablespoon of shallots
- parsley
- salt and spices
- paper
- oil

## Directions

1. After emptying and singeing your pigeons, cut off their legs and tuck the thighs inside
2. Cut them down the back like a chicken à la tartare
3. Flatten them
4. Season with salt, coarse pepper, and a little ground aromatic spices
5. Put a good piece of butter in a casserole with one quarter pound of minced fat pork and four tablespoons of oil
6. Place the casserole on the fire with your pigeons inside
7. Sauté them for a good quarter hour
8. Place them on a plate
9. In your butter, pass three tablespoons of finely chopped mushrooms, one tablespoon of shallots, an equal amount of parsley, all finely chopped, a little salt and spices
10. When they have returned in the butter, place them on your pigeons
11. Let them cool
12. Prepare squares of paper which you oil
13. Wrap your pigeons as instructed in papillotes articles
14. Half an hour before serving, place your pigeons on the grill at a gentle fire
15. At the moment of service, arrange them on the plate with clear broth underneath
