## Ingredients

- six pigeons
- butter
- juice of one lemon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bacon rashers
- poêle (braising liquid)
- crayfish
- green hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Lightly singe six Gautier-style pigeons
2. When they are plucked and trimmed, put a good piece of butter in a casserole with the juice of one lemon, a little salt and coarse pepper
3. Stiffen your pigeons in this seasoning
4. Put them in a casserole between bacon rashers and the butter in which you seared them
5. Add poêle to cook them (see Poêle)
6. A good quarter hour before serving, put them on the fire
7. At the moment of service, drain them and arrange on your plate
8. Place a crayfish between each pigeon
9. Pour green hollandaise sauce underneath
