## Ingredients

- Fruit of choice (cherries, currants, strawberries, raspberries, or blackberries)
- One pint of water per pound of fruit
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Take such fruit as you judge fitting to make your refreshing water
2. For one pound of fruit put one pint of water
3. Crush your fruit and dilute it with water
4. Pass it through a white cloth and add a little sugar
5. Pass it again through a strainer so your water is clear
6. Keep it cool until the moment you serve it
7. If you wish to make ices from it, add a little more sugar and put your water in tin molds, then freeze it with ice and salt or saltpeter
8. When they begin to freeze, take care to stir them from time to time with a spoon until they are set, because the edges would be too frozen and the middle would not be; when they are set properly, serve them in small cups and drink them immediately
