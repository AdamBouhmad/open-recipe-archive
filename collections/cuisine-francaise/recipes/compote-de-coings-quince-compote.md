## Ingredients

- Six quinces
- Water
- Sugar syrup
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the quinces in half
2. Remove the cores
3. Blanch them like bon-chrétien pears
4. Remove the peel
5. Place clear syrup in a pot with lemon juice
6. Allow the quinces to finish cooking
7. Arrange them in the serving dish and pour the syrup somewhat thick underneath
