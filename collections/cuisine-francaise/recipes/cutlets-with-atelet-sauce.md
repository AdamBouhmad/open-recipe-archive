## Ingredients

- Lamb cutlets
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Warm butter
- Atelet sauce
- Breadcrumbs
- Warm butter for coating
- Italienne garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare your lamb cutlets as the previous ones
2. Season them with salt and coarse pepper
3. Place them in your sautoir with warm butter poured over them
4. Place them on ordinary heat
5. When your cutlets are stiffened on both sides, drain them and let them cool
6. Trim them again
7. Coat them with atelet sauce
8. Put it in breadcrumbs, then in warm butter
9. Bread them once more
10. Place on a table to take on an agreeable form by rounding the end and smoothing it
11. Place on a plafond
12. A quarter hour before serving, place them on a grill over very low heat
13. When they have color on one side, turn them to the other
14. At the moment of serving, arrange them in a crown and add an italienne garnish
