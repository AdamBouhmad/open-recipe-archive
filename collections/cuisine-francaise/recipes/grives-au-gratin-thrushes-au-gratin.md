## Ingredients

- Thrushes
- Forcemeat
- Velouté
- Butter
- Lard strips
- Buttered paper
- Salt
- Coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare your thrushes as in the previous recipe; have enough forcemeat to place on the bottom of the dish; pour into your forcemeat about one skimming-spoon of velouté and mix well
2. Place this on your dish set over gentle heat to set your forcemeat; then arrange your thrushes that you have stiffened in butter without adding any paste
3. Once you have removed the string holding the flesh, place them on your forcemeat; cover them with strips of lard and a round of buttered paper; arrange them in the gratin
4. A quarter hour before serving, if you do not have an oven, place them on a gentle stove with a hot camp oven; at the moment of serving, remove all the grease found in your dish with a piece of soft bread; sauce them with an italienne sauce
