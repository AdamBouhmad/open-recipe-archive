## Ingredients

- 3 artichokes
- 12 onions
- 1/2 pound oil
- 3 anchovies
- butter
- espagnole sauce or roux
- lard strips

## Directions

1. Prepare three artichokes as for artichokes barigoule
2. Cut twelve onions into four pieces, about one line thick, each piece forming a quarter circle
3. Put half a pound of oil in a pot and place it on the fire with the onions inside
4. When the onions are well browned, pound three anchovies and mix them with butter and the onions
5. Put the mixture inside the artichokes
6. Cook them in a pot as described for artichokes barigoule
7. When serving, pour espagnole sauce inside, or make a light roux that you moisten with the braising liquid from the artichokes
8. You may also cook them simply on a lard strip with heat above and below
