## Ingredients

- three or four artichokes
- a piece of butter
- a pinch of flour
- two egg yolks
- verjuice
- salt
- coarse pepper
- verjuice in grain

## Directions

1. Remove the green from underneath and cut the leaves in half
2. Cook them in a small bed of coals, season lightly
3. Let them drain and remove the choke
4. In a saucepan, put a piece of butter, a pinch of flour, two egg yolks and verjuice, salt, coarse pepper
5. Bind the sauce over the fire
6. Add verjuice in grain that you have boiled briefly in water
7. Serve the artichokes with this sauce
