## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Foie gras
- Two or three artichoke hearts
- Bouquet of parsley
- Chives
- Half a clove of garlic
- Butter
- Flour
- Half a glass of white wine
- Coulis
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Small eggs
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole mushrooms cut into four, foie gras, two or three artichoke hearts cooked halfway in water and cut into pieces, a bouquet of parsley, chives, half a clove of garlic, a little butter
2. Pass everything over the fire, add a pinch of flour, moisten with half a glass of white wine, a little coulis and bouillon
3. Cook for half an hour, degrease, season with salt and pepper
4. If you have small eggs, boil them for an instant in water, remove the small skin and put them in the ragout
5. To make artificial eggs: take two hard egg yolks that you pound and mix with a raw egg yolk and a pinch of fine salt, place them on a floured table, roll them like a small sausage and cut into small pieces of equal size, roll each piece in your hands with a little flour to round them, and place them as you go on a floured plate
6. When they are all made, put them for a moment in boiling water, after they have boiled twice, remove them to fresh water, let them drain before putting them in the ragout
7. If you wish to put this ragout in white sauce, do not put coulis in it; before serving, add a liaison of three egg yolks with cream
