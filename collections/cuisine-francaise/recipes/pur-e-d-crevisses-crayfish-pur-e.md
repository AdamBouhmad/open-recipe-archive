## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Nutmeg
- Tender breadcrumbs, three times the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Place your crayfish on a fairly hot fire so they can cook for half an hour
2. Pass your cooked crayfish through a sieve (étamine) to make a purée
3. When your purée is passed, put it in a saucepan on a gentle fire
4. Ensure it does not boil and is not too thick
5. Prepare crusts as for the previous soup and serve with the purée
