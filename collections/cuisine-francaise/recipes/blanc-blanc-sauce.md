## Ingredients

- fourteen ounces of beef fat, cut into dice
- one pound of grated lard
- half a pound of butter
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- three or four bay leaves
- two or three lemons, with the peel, seeds, and white removed
- water
- clarified salt water

## Directions

1. Cut the beef fat into dice, or larger depending on the size of your blanc
2. Cut the carrots, onions, and sliced lemons, and put them with your fat
3. Fill a large cooking spoon with water
4. Bring to a boil until the contents of your pot froth a little and your fat is melted
5. Stir your blanc with a wooden spoon
6. Do not let it take color
7. Moisten it with water
8. Add clarified salt water to season it
9. Let your blanc boil, skim it, and use it for the purposes indicated
