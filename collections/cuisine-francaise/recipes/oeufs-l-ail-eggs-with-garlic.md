## Ingredients

- ten cloves of garlic
- two anchovies
- a good pinch of capers
- oil
- a thread of vinegar
- salt
- coarse pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook ten cloves of garlic for half a quarter hour in water
2. Pound them with two anchovies and a good pinch of capers
3. Dilute them with oil, a thread of vinegar, a little salt, and coarse pepper
4. Put this sauce in the bottom of the serving plate
5. Arrange hard-boiled eggs neatly on top
