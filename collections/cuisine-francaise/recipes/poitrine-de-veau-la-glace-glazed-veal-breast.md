## Ingredients

- veal breast
- sliced carrots
- onions
- two bay leaves
- bunch of parsley and chives
- lard
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and truss your veal breast
2. Cover it with lard
3. Moisten it with bouillon and a little salt
4. Cook over low heat for two and a half hours
5. At the moment of serving, drain it, remove the trussing strings, glaze it, and arrange it on your platter
6. Place a border of glazed lettuces around it
7. Make a Spanish sauce that you will place underneath; if you do not have sauce, make a light roux that you will moisten with the cooking liquid in which your breast has cooked
8. Make the sauce long so that you can reduce it
9. Add a little coarse pepper
