## Ingredients

- Pear cider made without water
- Fruit

## Directions

1. Take pear cider made without water for this preserve; apple cider is not sweet enough
2. Reduce the cider to two-thirds before adding your fruit
3. Finish your preserves in the same manner as those made with sweet wine
