## Ingredients

- artichokes
- light roux
- broth
- juice
- finely chopped herbs
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Cut and prepare the artichokes into pieces as previously described
2. Make a light roux and moisten it with broth and a little juice
3. Put many finely chopped herbs on the artichokes, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Pour the sauce over them
5. Cook them as the artichokes with velouté
