## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- parsley
- chives
- five or six tarragon leaves
- three shallots
- capers
- anchovies
- half a handful of breadcrumbs
- butter, size of half an egg

## Directions

1. Cook the beef tongue in a pot after blanching it
2. When cooked, remove the skin and let it cool
3. Cut it into slices
4. Chop parsley, chives, five or six tarragon leaves, three shallots, capers and one anchovy
5. Take half a handful of breadcrumbs, which you mix with butter the size of half an egg, and part of what you have chopped, to put everything together in the bottom of the dish
6. Arrange half of the tongue
