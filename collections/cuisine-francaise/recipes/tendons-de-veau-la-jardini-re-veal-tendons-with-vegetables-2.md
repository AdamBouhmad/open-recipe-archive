## Ingredients

- veal tendons
- lettuces
- small root vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your tendons as for the braised version
2. At the moment of serving, drain them
3. Arrange them in crowns and glaze them
4. Place a border of lettuces around them
5. In the middle of your tendons, place root vegetables turned into small batons or olives, arranged in a pyramid
