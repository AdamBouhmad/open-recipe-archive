## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour, one spoonful
- Onions, a handful, cut into small squares
- Bouillon
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Vinegar, a dash
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take a little butter and one spoonful of flour and brown them on the fire
2. Add a handful of onions cut into small squares and cook them in this roux, adding a bit more butter
3. Moisten with bouillon
4. When the onions are cooked, add sliced hard-boiled eggs
5. Bring to a gentle boil
6. Add a dash of vinegar, salt, and pepper
7. Serve with reduced sauce
