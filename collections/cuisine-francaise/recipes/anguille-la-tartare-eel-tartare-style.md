## Ingredients

- eel
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- tartare sauce

## Directions

1. Strip your eel as for the eel on the spit; cut it into sections of five or six inches
2. Prepare a marinade as described for the previous eel; before moistening it, add a little flour
3. When your sauce is cooked, pass it through cheesecloth over the pieces of eel and let them cook
4. When they are cold, put them in breadcrumbs, then dip them in seasoned and beaten eggs
5. Bread them a second time
6. A good quarter hour before serving, put your sections on the grill over a gentle fire; place a well-heated camp oven on top
7. Add a tartare sauce on your plate and put the sections on it
