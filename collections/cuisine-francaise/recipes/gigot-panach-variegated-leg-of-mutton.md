## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- gherkins
- ham
- lard
- bouillon
- white wine
- ham slice
- parsley
- chives
- cloves
- garlic
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- hard egg yolks
- capers
- anchovies
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Lard throughout with gherkins, ham, and lard, all cut into lardons
2. Tie and place in a pot just fitting its size with half a setier of bouillon, a glass of white wine, a slice of ham, a bouquet of parsley, chives, three cloves, one clove of garlic, thyme, bay leaf, and basil
3. Cook on low heat for three or four hours
4. Pass part of the sauce through a sieve and skim the fat
5. Add three finely chopped hard egg yolks, capers, anchovies, and blanched parsley
6. Add the slice of ham that cooked with the leg of mutton
7. Chop everything very finely
8. Add a small piece of butter kneaded with flour, thicken the sauce over fire and serve over the leg of mutton
