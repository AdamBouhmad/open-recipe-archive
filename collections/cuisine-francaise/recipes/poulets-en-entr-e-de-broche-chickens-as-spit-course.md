## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Lard, grated
- Chicken liver
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Fat from the cooking pot
- Paper

## Directions

1. Singe at the flame of a furnace, empty the chickens and put in the body a little grated lard and the chopped chicken liver, a little chopped parsley and chives, very little salt
2. Sew them so nothing comes out
3. Brown them in a saucepan with fat from the pot
4. Cook on the spit, wrapped in lard and paper
5. Do not place over too ardent a fire, for fear they color, because chickens as a spit course should be served white
6. When cooked, arrange them on the serving plat and add whatever sauce or stew you judge appropriate
