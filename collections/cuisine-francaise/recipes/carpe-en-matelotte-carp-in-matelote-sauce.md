## Ingredients

- carp
- pike
- eel
- crayfish
- barbel
- river fish
- butter
- flour
- small onions
- red wine
- lean broth
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet garni
- fine herbs
- bread croutons

## Directions

1. Scale and remove the gills from the carp, then cut it into sections and place in a casserole with other fish such as pike, eel, crayfish, barbel, or other river fish as available.
2. In another casserole, make a small roux with butter and a spoonful of flour.
3. When it is a nice color, add small onions cut into quarters and cook them halfway in the same roux, adding a little more butter.
4. Moisten with equal parts red wine and lean broth.
5. Pour the onions and their sauce into the casserole with the prepared fish and season with salt, pepper, and a bouquet garni of fine herbs.
6. Cook the matelote over high heat for half an hour.
7. When ready to serve, add some bread croutons to the sauce and serve with the matelote.
