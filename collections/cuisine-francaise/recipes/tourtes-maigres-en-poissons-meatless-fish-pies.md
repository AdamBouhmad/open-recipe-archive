## Ingredients

- Fish such as eel, pike, salmon, or tench
- Bouquet of fine herbs
- Fine salt
- Fine spices
- Good butter
- Pastry dough
- Flour
- Butter
- White wine
- Lean broth or warm water
- Mushrooms
- Fish roe
- Capers

## Directions

1. Choose any fish you prefer.
2. Scale and cut the fish into sections.
3. Line a pie dish with pastry dough as described for other pies.
4. Place the fish on top with a bouquet of fine herbs, fine salt, and fine spices.
5. Cover all the fish with good butter.
6. Cover with a layer of pastry dough and finish the pie as explained for previous recipes.
7. Bake for one and a half hours.
8. When cooked and degreased as for others, prepare a lean ragout.
9. Make a roux by browning a pinch of flour with a little butter.
10. When the roux is made, moisten with half a septier of white wine, lean broth or warm water.
11. Add mushrooms, a bouquet of fine herbs, and a little salt.
12. Boil the ragout for half an hour and degrease it.
13. If you have fish roe, boil briefly in water and cool in fresh water, then add to the ragout and boil for half a quarter-hour.
14. When finished and of good taste, pour into the pie.
15. You may also use various lean ragouts such as truffles, morels, mushrooms, or asparagus tips, depending on the season.
