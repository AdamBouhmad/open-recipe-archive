## Ingredients

- Well-reduced Spanish sauce
- Anchovy butter, amount of half an egg
- Lemon juice
- Or alternatively: small brown sauce and anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Have the well-reduced Spanish sauce ready
2. At the moment of serving, add anchovy butter the size of half an egg and lemon juice to destroy the salt that the butter might produce
3. When adding the anchovy butter to your hot sauce, stir well with a spoon so that the butter binds well with the sauce
4. If you lack Spanish sauce, make a small brown sauce and add your anchovy butter
5. This sauce can be made as a meatless version
