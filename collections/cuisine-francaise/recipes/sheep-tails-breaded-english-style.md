## Ingredients

- Sheep tails
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Butter
- Eggs (four)
- Bread crumbs
- Clear jus

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook your sheep tails as described for braised tails
2. When they are cooked, drain them and season them with salt and coarse pepper
3. Make a piece of butter lukewarm
4. Dip your tails in it
5. Put them in bread crumbs
6. Break four eggs which you put with your butter
7. Beat everything together
8. Dip your tails in your eggs, making sure the tail takes it everywhere
9. Put it in bread crumbs so that your tails are exactly breaded, well united, and retain their shape well
10. Half an hour before serving, put them on the grill over a very gentle fire
11. Cover them with a very hot camp oven to make them take color
12. At the time of serving, arrange them on the plate and put a clear jus underneath
