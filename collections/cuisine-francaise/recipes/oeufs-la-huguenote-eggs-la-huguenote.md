## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Jus
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take the serving dish and place it on a moderate fire with a little jus
2. Gently break eggs so that the yolks remain whole
3. Season with salt and coarse pepper
4. Cook the tops with a red-hot iron or shovel
5. Serve semi-soft
