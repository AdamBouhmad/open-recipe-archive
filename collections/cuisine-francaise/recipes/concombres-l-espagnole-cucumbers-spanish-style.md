## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- espagnole sauce
- consommé

## Directions

1. Cut cucumbers in four lengthwise
2. Peel them and give them an attractive shape
3. Blanch them and let them drain on a cloth
4. Arrange them in a pot
5. Pour in five tablespoons of worked espagnole sauce and two tablespoons of consommé
6. Place the cucumbers on the fire half an hour before serving and let them simmer
7. Then arrange them; if the sauce is too thin, reduce it and pour it over
