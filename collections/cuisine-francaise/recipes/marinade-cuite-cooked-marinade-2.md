## Ingredients

- A piece of butter
- Three carrots, cut in slices
- Four onions, cut the same way
- Two bay leaves
- A little thyme
- Two cloves

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan
2. Cut three carrots in slices and four onions cut the same way, add two bay leaves, a little thyme, and two cloves
3. Put your saucepan on the fire and cook your ingredients
