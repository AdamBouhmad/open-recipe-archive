## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- lard strips
- ham slices
- carrots
- onions
- bay leaves
- thyme
- bouillon
- buttered paper
- chicory or spinach, sorrel, tomato sauce, or white onion purée

## Directions

1. Cut off the red bone that is attached to the tendons
2. Divide your breast into pieces forming the matelote crouton, that is to say a pointed oval
3. Put lard strips in a casserole
4. Add a few slices of ham and your carbonnade pieces on top
5. Cover them with lard
6. Add two carrots cut into slices, four onions also cut, two bay leaves, a little thyme
7. Pour in one full spoon of bouillon
8. Add a round of buttered paper
9. Simmer them with heat above and below for three hours
10. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, and put chicory or spinach, sorrel, tomato sauce, white onion purée, etc. on top
