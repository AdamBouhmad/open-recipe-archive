## Ingredients

- cutlets
- larding bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, and spices
- butter
- lard strips
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- bouillon
- cucumbers à la crème

## Directions

1. Cut your cutlets somewhat thick so they are not too thin after beating them
2. Lard them with medium lardons seasoned with salt, pepper, and a little spices
3. Put a piece of melted butter in a saucepan
4. Place your cutlets in it to stiffen
5. Finish trimming them
6. Put lard strips in a saucepan with a few veal slices, two carrots cut into slices, three onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme
7. Place the cutlets on this seasoning
8. Cover them with lard strips
9. Pour in just under a spoonful of bouillon
10. Simmer your cutlets for two hours
11. At serving time, drain them, glaze them, and arrange them in crowns on the platter
12. Put cucumbers à la crème in the middle, or you can place them plain in the saucepan where you had them stiffen
13. Coat them with a little aspic
14. Preserve the vegetables from the cooking liquid such as carrots, onions, etc.
15. Moisten them with bouillon
