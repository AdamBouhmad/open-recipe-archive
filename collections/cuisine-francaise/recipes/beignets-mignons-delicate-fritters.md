## Ingredients

- two good spoonfuls of flour
- four eggs, whites and yolks
- a little salt
- two ounces of sugar
- one green lemon, grated
- half a spoonful of orange flower water
- half a septier of milk and half a septier of cream
- two spoonfuls of flour for the batter
- one spoonful of brandy
- a pinch of salt
- two eggs for the batter
- sugar
- red food coloring

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan two good spoonfuls of flour that you dilute with four eggs, whites and yolks, a little salt, two ounces of sugar, one grated green lemon, a half-spoonful of orange flower water, a half-septier of milk and half-septier of cream
2. Cook over a small fire stirring constantly
3. When it is cooked and very thick, spread it on a floured plate, dust the top with flour
4. When it is cold, cut it into pieces with a small pastry cutter
5. Dip each piece in a batter made as follows: put in a saucepan two spoonfuls of flour, one spoonful of brandy, a pinch of salt, dilute with two eggs
6. Fry and serve glazed with sugar and red food coloring
