## Ingredients

- Roasted hare
- Hare bones
- Butter
- Onions
- Clove of garlic
- Bay leaf
- Two cloves
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Red wine
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take a roasted hare that has been removed from the table, remove all the meat and cut it into fillets, lightly crush the bones and place them with the flanks in a saucepan with butter about the size of half an egg, some sliced onions, a clove of garlic, a bay leaf, and two cloves.
2. Pass them over the fire and add a good pinch of flour, moisten with a glass of bouillon and two glasses of red wine, salt, and pepper.
3. Boil for half an hour.
