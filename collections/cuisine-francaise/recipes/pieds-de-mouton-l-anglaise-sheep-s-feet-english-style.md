## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- Bouillon
- Verjuice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Garlic
- Carrot
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a dozen sheep's feet in water.
2. Place them in a saucepan with bouillon, a spoonful of verjuice, salt, pepper, some onion slices, a garlic clove, and a carrot cut into zest.
3. Boil for half an hour to develop flavor.
4. When well cooked, drain and remove the bones.
5. Replace each bone with a piece of bread crumb of the same length and thickness.
6. Fry by passing them on the fire with butter until they achieve a beautiful golden color.
7. Place a piece in each foot to imitate the removed bone.
8. Arrange on the serving platter and cover with piquant sauce.
