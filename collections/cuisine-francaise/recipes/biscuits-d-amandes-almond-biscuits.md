## Ingredients

- One quarteron of sweet almonds
- Fine sugar
- One ounce of flour
- Three egg yolks
- Four ounces of fine sugar
- Four egg whites
- Fine sugar mixed with half flour for topping

## Directions

1. Shell and pound the sweet almonds very finely in a mortar
2. To prevent them from turning to oil, add a pinch of fine sugar from time to time
3. Beat them for a quarter hour with one ounce of flour, three egg yolks, and four ounces of fine sugar
4. Whip four egg whites and mix with the rest of the mixture
5. You have paper molds made like boxes, square, two fingers in size
6. Lightly butter them inside and dress your biscuits there
7. Sprinkle over them fine sugar mixed with half flour
8. Bake in a very gentle oven
9. When they are a beautiful color, remove them from the paper while hot
