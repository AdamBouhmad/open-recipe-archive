## Ingredients

- beef slice
- coarse lard
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- eau-de-vie

## Directions

1. Lard the beef slice with coarse lard mixed with parsley, chives, mushrooms, and a touch of garlic, all chopped, salt, and pepper
2. Cook it at low fire in its own juice
3. Add about one spoonful of eau-de-vie when it is cooked
4. Serve it cold
