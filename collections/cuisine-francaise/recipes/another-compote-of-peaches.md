## Ingredients

- seven or eight nearly ripe peaches
- boiling water
- one quarter pound of sugar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Take seven or eight nearly ripe peaches, split them in half; after removing the pit, put them for a moment in boiling water and remove them as soon as you can remove the skin
2. Boil a quarter pound of sugar with a glass of water, be careful to skim, and then put the peaches in it to cook
3. Reduce the syrup if it is too thin before arranging it over the peaches
