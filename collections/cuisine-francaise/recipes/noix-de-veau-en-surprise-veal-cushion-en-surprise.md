## Ingredients

- One beautiful veal cushion
- Thick and thin larding fat
- Lard bards
- Veal slices
- Two carrots
- Four onions, one studded with two cloves
- One bay leaf
- A bouquet of parsley and chives
- Salt
- Butter
- Buttered paper
- Mushrooms
- Béchamel sauce
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the cushion as for veal à la Conty: lard it with thick and thin larding fat
2. Place lard bards in the bottom of a casserole or braising pan
3. Add veal slices, two carrots, four onions (one studded with two cloves), one bay leaf, a bouquet of parsley and chives, and a little salt
4. Place the veal cushion in the casserole
5. Cover only the teat with lard bards
6. Butter a round double sheet of paper and cook the cushion with fire on top and below
7. When the cushion is cooked, let it cool
8. Make an oval hole and remove the flesh, leaving enough at the bottom so the cushion can hold a ragout without leaking
9. Cut the meat removed from the cushion into small dice, except for the top, which you preserve to mask the ragout so one cannot see the cushion has been opened
10. Also cut mushrooms into small dice and add them with the meat to a béchamel sauce
11. Heat the veal cushion
12. At serving time, drain it well and glaze it
13. Pour the small ragout into the hollow of the cushion
14. Cover it with the top you have preserved so one cannot see the cushion has been opened
15. It must be glazed, and you will put Spanish sauce underneath
