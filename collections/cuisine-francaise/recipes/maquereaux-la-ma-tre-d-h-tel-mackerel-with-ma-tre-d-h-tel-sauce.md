## Ingredients

- Three mackerel
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Chives
- Parsley in branches
- Oil
- Butter
- Flour
- Water
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Empty the mackerel and remove the intestines by inserting the knife tip in the hole in the middle of the body
2. Wipe them with a damp cloth
3. Split them from the back side from head to tail
4. Place them on an earthenware platter
5. Add salt, coarse pepper, chives, and parsley in branches
6. Drizzle the mackerel with oil
7. Half an hour before serving, place them on the grill at a very gentle fire, being careful to turn them
8. At the moment of serving, arrange them on the plate
9. Make a maître-d'hôtel butter by placing a piece of butter in a saucepan with a spoonful of flour, parsley, finely chopped chives, salt, and pepper
10. Mix the flour with the seasoning
11. Add half a glass of water and lemon juice
12. Place your sauce on the fire and stir constantly
13. At the first boil, if it tastes good, pour it over your mackerel
