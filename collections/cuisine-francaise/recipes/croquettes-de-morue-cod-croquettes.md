## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Butter, size of two eggs
- Flour, one small tablespoon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg, grated, a little
- Cream, half a glass
- Eggs, four or five
- Breadcrumbs for coating

## Directions

1. When your cod is cooked, cut it into dice and put it in a saucepan.
2. Add butter the size of two eggs, one small tablespoon of flour, a little salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg, and half a glass of cream.
3. Place your sauce on the fire and stir until it comes to a boil.
4. Pour it over your cod, mix it well with the sauce, and let it cool.
5. Divide it into fifteen or twenty portions and shape your croquettes into an agreeable and uniform form.
6. Coat them with breadcrumbs.
7. Break four or five eggs, season them, beat them, and dip your croquettes in them; coat a second time with breadcrumbs.
8. An instant before serving, place your frying oil on the fire; when it is very hot, put your croquettes in it, and as soon as they have a nice golden color, remove them.
9. Place them on a white napkin, arrange them in pyramids, and put fried parsley on top.
