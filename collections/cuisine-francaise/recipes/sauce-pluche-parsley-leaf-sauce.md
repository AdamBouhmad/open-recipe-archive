## Ingredients

- Four or five spoonfuls of velouté
- Half a glass of white wine
- Coarse pepper
- A little parsley root
- Parsley leaves

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, put four or five spoonfuls of velouté and half a glass of white wine, coarse pepper, and a little parsley root cut into small strips
2. Cook the parsley root in your sauce
3. When it is sufficiently reduced, have parsley leaves broken into four or five pieces each
4. Blanch these parsley pieces in salted water
5. When the parsley is blanched, refresh it in cold water
6. At the moment of serving, add the blanched parsley to your sauce
