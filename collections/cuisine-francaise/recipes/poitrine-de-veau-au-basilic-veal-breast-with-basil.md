## Ingredients

- veal breast
- bouillon
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- cloves
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- parsley for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the veal breast into pieces one inch wide
2. Blanch briefly in boiling water
3. Cook with bouillon, a bouquet of parsley, chives, a clove of garlic, thyme, bay leaf, basil, two cloves, salt, and pepper
4. When cooked, reduce the sauce until it adheres to the meat
5. Remove the meat to a plate to cool
6. Dip each piece in beaten egg like an omelet
7. Coat with breadcrumbs as you proceed
8. Fry to a nice color and serve garnished with fried parsley
