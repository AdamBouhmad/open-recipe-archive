## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- large lardons seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, four spices, parsley and chives
- lard
- veal and beef slices
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cloves
- bouillon
- piquant sauce
- gherkins

## Directions

1. Have a beef tongue that you will soak to degorge
2. Blanch it for half an hour
3. Let it cool
4. When cold, trim it
5. Prepare large lardons seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, four spices, finely chopped parsley and chives
6. Lard the tongue with these seasoned lardons
7. Cook it in a casserole with some lard, slices of veal and beef, carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, and three cloves
8. Moisten the cooking with bouillon
9. Simmer the tongue on low heat for four to five hours or more, depending on how tough the tongue is
10. At serving time, trim it and remove the skin
11. Cut it lengthwise down the middle, but not completely, so the cut tongue forms a heart on the plate
12. Prepare a piquant sauce with gherkins cut into thin slices, or arrange sliced gherkins in a miroton pattern on the edges of the tongue or otherwise
13. Pour the sauce over the tongue and serve
14. Alternatively, prepare the tongue as described above, cook it with lard, carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, three cloves, and moisten with water
15. Season the cooking with salt, but not too much so you can use the cooking liquid in which the tongue was cooked
16. To make the sauce, make a small brown roux, moisten it with the jus in which the tongue cooked, add a dash of vinegar
