## Ingredients

- Sheep's feet
- Water
- White wine
- Bouillon
- Coulis
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic
- Cloves
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook them in water.
2. Place them in a saucepan with a glass of white wine, three spoonfuls of bouillon and as much coulis, a bouquet of parsley and chives, half a garlic clove, two cloves, salt, and coarse pepper.
3. Boil gently and reduce to a short sauce.
4. Remove the bouquet and serve on a gratin like that of sheep's tongues.
