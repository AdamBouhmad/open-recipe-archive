## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- Jam or marmalade
- Egg for gilding
- Butter
- Nonpareille sprinkles or fine sugar

## Directions

1. Take puff pastry and form two equal cakes the size of your entremets dish, each the thickness of two coins
2. Put jam on the first one, leaving a finger's width of border that you will moisten with a brush dipped in water
3. Place the second cake on the first and glue them well together with your fingers, pressing all around
4. After shaping them a bit, gild them with beaten egg and bake in the oven
5. When they are cooked and coming out of the oven, pass a brush dipped in butter over them and sprinkle nonpareille sprinkles all over
6. Alternatively, instead of nonpareille, you may put fine sugar and pass the hot spatula over it to glaze it
