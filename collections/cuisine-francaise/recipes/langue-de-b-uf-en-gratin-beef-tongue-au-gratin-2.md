## Ingredients

- one beef tongue
- parsley
- chives
- five or six tarragon leaves
- three shallots
- capers
- one anchovy
- half a handful of breadcrumbs
- butter the size of half an egg
- salt
- coarse pepper
- three or four spoonfuls of bouillon
- half a glass of wine

## Directions

1. Blanch the beef tongue and cook it in the pot
2. When cooked, remove the skin and let it cool
3. Slice the tongue
4. Chop together parsley, chives, tarragon, shallots, capers, and anchovy
5. Mix the breadcrumbs with butter and part of the chopped herbs
6. Place this mixture in the bottom of the dish
7. Arrange half of the tongue slices on top
8. Season with salt and coarse pepper, and add the remaining herbs on top
9. Arrange a second layer of the remaining tongue slices
10. Season with salt and coarse pepper
11. Moisten with three or four spoonfuls of bouillon and half a glass of wine
12. Boil until a gratin forms on the bottom of the dish
13. When serving, add a little bouillon so it shows a small sauce
