## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard
- bouillon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Blanch the tongue for a quarter hour in boiling water
2. Lard it with fat lard
3. Cook it in the pot with beef
4. When cooked, remove the skin
5. Split it slightly more than halfway down its length to open it in two without separating it
6. Serve with bouillon, salt, coarse pepper, a dash of vinegar if desired, and chopped parsley
