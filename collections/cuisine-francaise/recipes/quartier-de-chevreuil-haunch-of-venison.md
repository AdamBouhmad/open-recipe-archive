## Ingredients

- venison loin and leg
- lard fin (fine lard)
- marinade

## Directions

1. Bone your venison loin and leg
2. Lard it with fine lard
3. Place it in marinade like venison loins; it can remain eight days in the marinade
4. When you wish to serve it, remove it from the marinade and put it on the spit
5. Two hours suffice to cook it
6. At the moment of serving, remove the skewers and strings
7. Prepare the handle by wrapping it in a piece of paper
8. Place it on a roasting platter
9. Serve a sauceboat with a piquant sauce
