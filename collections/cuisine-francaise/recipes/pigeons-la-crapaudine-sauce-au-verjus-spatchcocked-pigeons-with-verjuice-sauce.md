## Ingredients

- good pigeons
- fine oil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- bread crumbs
- onion
- verjuice
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Truss the legs inside; if large, cut them in half, otherwise only split them down the back and flatten them without breaking the bones too much.
2. Marinate them with fine oil, salt, coarse pepper, parsley, chives, and chopped mushrooms; let them absorb the seasoning as much as possible and coat with bread crumbs.
3. Place them on the grill and baste with the rest of their marinade; grill them over a low fire until a beautiful golden color.
4. For the sauce: put a chopped onion in a mortar with verjuice, pound everything together well, and extract as much juice as you can; mix with bouillon, salt, and coarse pepper, heat, and serve under the pigeons.
5. These same pigeons can be served without verjuice using another clear sauce that is slightly piquant; you can use butter, lard, or good pot fat instead of oil.
