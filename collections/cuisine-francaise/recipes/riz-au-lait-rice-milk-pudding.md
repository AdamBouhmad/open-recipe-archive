## Ingredients

- Rice
- Water
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well
2. Cook it for half an hour over low heat with a little water to make it burst
3. Gradually add hot milk little by little until it is cooked
4. Season with salt and sugar
