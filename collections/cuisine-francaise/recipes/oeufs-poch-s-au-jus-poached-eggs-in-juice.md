## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put water three-quarters full in a medium saucepan with salt and a little vinegar
2. When it boils, place it at the edge of the stove and break the egg
3. Be careful not to damage the yolk when opening the shells
4. Pour the egg gently into the water
5. Put in four of them and let them set
6. Keep the water boiling
7. Remove them from the water with a perforated spoon and put your finger on top; if it has a little consistency, put it in cold water
