## Ingredients

- Twelve thrushes or more
- Fine forcemeat
- Ground aromatic herbs
- Coarse pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take twelve thrushes or more, very fresh
2. Pluck them carefully to preserve their skin whole
3. Singe them lightly
4. Bone them starting from the back
5. When they are well boned, take the livers and pound them with the back of the knife
6. Mix them with fine forcemeat, a bit of ground aromatic herbs, coarse pepper
7. Mix it all together
8. Fill your thrush with this, but first pass a leg through the middle of the body, of which you have cut off a large piece, so that it represents the tail of a cherry
9. When the body is filled with forcemeat, bring the flesh together with a needle and fasten them
10. Put your thrushes in a saucepan with salt and coarse pepper
