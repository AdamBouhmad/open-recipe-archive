## Ingredients

- sirloin fillet
- glazed onions
- Spanish sauce
- roux
- cooking liquid from the fillet

## Directions

1. Trim your sirloin fillet
2. Cook it as you would cook it braised
3. Drain it and glaze it
4. Place it on your serving dish with glazed onions
5. Put a Spanish sauce worked around it
6. Lacking sauce, make a light roux which you will moisten with the juice in which your sirloin fillet has cooked; add glazed onions to it
