## Ingredients

- leeks
- a piece of butter
- water
- a little cinnamon
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut your leeks to one inch long, then cut them into strips
2. Pass them with a piece of butter
3. When your leeks are fried so that they are somewhat blond, moisten your soup with water
4. Add a little cinnamon, salt, coarse pepper, and let everything boil for half an hour
5. At the moment of serving, pour your broth over your bread
