## Ingredients

- turkey
- coarse lard
- salt, pepper, parsley, chives, garlic, shallots
- one chopine of white wine
- broth
- roots
- onions
- a bouquet garni

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean, and truss the turkey with legs inside the body
2. Finish singeing it over the coals
3. Lard it with coarse lard seasoned with salt, pepper, parsley, chives, garlic, and shallots, all chopped together
4. Place it to cook in a pot just fitting its size
5. Add one chopine of white wine, broth, roots, onions, a bouquet garni, salt, and pepper
6. Cook over low heat
7. When cooked, strain the broth through a sieve and reduce it to a glaze
8. Let the glaze cool, then spread it over the turkey, or place any remaining glaze inside the turkey
9. Serve the turkey on a platter on a napkin garnished with fresh parsley
