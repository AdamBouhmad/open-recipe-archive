## Ingredients

- wings
- bouillon
- bouquet garni
- salt, coarse pepper
- small onions ragout

## Directions

1. Make a small onion ragout as explained elsewhere
2. Cook the wings in a casserole with bouillon, bouquet garni, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Degrease the cooking liquid and pass it through a sieve
4. Add it to the onion ragout to strengthen it
5. Serve with reduced sauce over the onions
