## Ingredients

- beef palate
- onion
- butter
- good bouillon
- coulis
- bouquet garni
- mustard

## Directions

1. Clean the palate well and cook it in water
2. Peel off the skins and cut into slices
3. Sauté onion in butter over the fire
4. When half cooked, add the palate slices
5. Moisten the ragout with good bouillon, a little coulis, and a bouquet garni
6. Season to taste
7. When well degreased and the sauce sufficiently reduced, add a little mustard when serving
