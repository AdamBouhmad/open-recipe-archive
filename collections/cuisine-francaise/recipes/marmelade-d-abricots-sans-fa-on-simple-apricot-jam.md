## Ingredients

- six pounds of apricots
- four and a half pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut as thinly as you can six pounds of not-too-ripe apricots and put them as you go into a clean pot. Crack the pits, remove their skin and cut them very fine to add with the apricots. Pound four and a half pounds of sugar to put with the apricots. Put your pot on a clear fire and stir constantly with a skimming spoon for fear the jam sticks to the bottom. When the apricots are well advanced in cooking, remove the pot from time to time to crush the pieces of apricots that do not turn to jam. Cook until it sticks to your fingers without much resistance, testing by taking some jam in your fingers and pressing them together. Then put it in pots. This method, though simple, is very good.
