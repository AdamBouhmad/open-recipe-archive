## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- salt
- coarse pepper
- fine oil
- white wine
- coulis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue slightly more than halfway in water
2. Remove the skin and cut it into thin slices
3. Arrange the slices in a saucepan with finely chopped parsley, chives, and mushrooms
4. Season with salt, coarse pepper, and fine oil
5. Cook over very low heat
6. When it begins to boil, add a glass of white wine
7. When cooked, degrease it and add a little coulis
8. When serving, if it is not sufficiently sharp, add lemon juice
