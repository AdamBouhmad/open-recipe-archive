## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- lardons
- salt
- coarse pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lard strips
- ham slices
- mutton bones
- mutton slices
- four carrots
- six onions
- three bay leaves
- thyme
- three cloves
- parsley and chives bouquet

## Directions

1. Have a leg of mutton which you debone halfway down the handle
2. Season it with lardons, salt, coarse pepper, pounded thyme and bay leaf, and lard the inside of your leg: do not let your lardons stick out on top
3. When it is well larded, make it take its original form; tie it so that it does not appear that it has been deboned
4. Put lard strips in the bottom of your braisière, a few slices of ham, crushed bones, a few slices of mutton, four carrots, six onions, three bay leaves, a little thyme, three cloves, a bouquet of parsley and chives
