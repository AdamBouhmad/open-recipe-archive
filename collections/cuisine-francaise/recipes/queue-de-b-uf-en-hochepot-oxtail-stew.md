## Ingredients

- Oxtail, cut into pieces
- Good bouillon
- Bouquet garni
- Salt
- Onions
- Carrots
- Parsnips
- Turnips
- Cabbage
- Coulis

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into pieces and blanch it
2. Cook with good bouillon, a bouquet garni, and little salt for five hours
3. At the midway point of cooking, add onions, carrots, parsnips, turnips, and a bit of cabbage, all blanched and cut neatly
4. When everything is cooked, remove it onto a cloth and wipe it so no grease remains
5. Arrange the vegetables with the meat in a proper terrine for serving at table
6. Decrease the sauce in which the meat cooked, add a bit of coulis, and reduce over the fire if the sauce is too thin
7. Take care that there is not too much salt, pass it through a sieve, and pour it over the meat and vegetables
8. You may also serve the oxtail the same way using only one type of vegetable at a time, or without vegetables and with different sauces instead
