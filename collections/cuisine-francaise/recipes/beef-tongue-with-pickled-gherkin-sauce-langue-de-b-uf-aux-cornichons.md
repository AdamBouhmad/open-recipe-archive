## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- three cloves
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- coarse pepper
- pickled gherkins

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue by larding it
2. Cook with lard, carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, and three cloves, moistening with water
3. Season the cooking liquid with salt, but not too much so it may be used for the sauce
4. Make a small brown roux and moisten it with the cooking liquid from the tongue
5. Add a splash of vinegar and coarse pepper to the sauce
6. Make the sauce somewhat thin so it may be reduced and take on good flavor
7. At the time of serving, place the tongue on the plate and mask it with the sauce in which you have put pickled gherkins
8. Ensure the sauce is well-salted and flavorful
