## Ingredients

- one half pound of seaweed jelly
- three quarters pound of sugar
- two big, tart apples
- two tablespoonfuls of kudzu starch
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. After washing half a pound of seaweed jelly soak it in water for about three hours, then squeeze it dry and break in small pieces into a pan with one and a half quarts of water.
2. Boil over medium heat.
3. Add three quarters of a pound of sugar, stirring the while, and a pinch of salt.
4. Prepare two big, tart apples by peeling and removing the cores, then rub them on a nutmeg grater until they are reduced to powder.
5. Transfer jelly to another saucepan, strain it, and mix with powdered apple.
6. When thoroughly boiled, add two tablespoonfuls of kudzu starch, dissolved in cold water.
7. Stir well with wooden chopsticks or spoon.
8. Remove from fire, and pour into lacquered box or molds.
9. Two or three hours after it has hardened, cut in desired shapes and sizes.
