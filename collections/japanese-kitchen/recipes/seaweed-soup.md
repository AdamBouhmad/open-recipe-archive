## Ingredients

- two yolks of hard-boiled eggs
- one can of seaweed
- three chicken giblets
- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- one and one half teaspoonfuls of Quong Sang Chong (water chestnut flour)
- salt to taste
- spiced vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil one can of seaweed until it is like thin jelly
2. Have ready three chicken giblets, chopped very fine, having first boiled them one hour in a quart of water
3. Add the seaweed, and boil all together for half an hour
4. Strain, then crumble in the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
5. Stir in two tablespoonfuls of syou, and salt to taste
6. Rub smooth one and a half teaspoonfuls of Quong Sang Chong in a little cold water, then add to the soup and stir until it thickens slightly
7. Serve with a small piece of seaweed on top that has been soaking in spiced vinegar
