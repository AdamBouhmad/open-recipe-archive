## Ingredients

- one half cup of shrimp meat
- five eggs
- one cupful of mushrooms
- two tablespoonfuls of sweet lard
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put the sweet lard in the pan, and when hot throw in the mushrooms, which have been first washed and cut small
2. Cook for five minutes, then add the shrimp meat, which has previously been cooked and flaked
3. Cook for two minutes more
4. Beat five eggs and throw in the pan, and cook for five minutes, taking care not to burn, but do not stir or shake the pan
