## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- one half cupful of shrimp meat
- one half cupful of syou sauce
- goma-seed oil

## Directions

1. Beat up the eggs with half a cupful of syou.
2. Have ready about four or five small frying pans.
3. Wipe pans when hot with paper soaked in goma-seed oil.
4. Pour into each pan an equal quantity of the egg, and quickly divide and scatter in the shrimp meat, which has been finely chopped.
5. Fry all slowly for four minutes.
