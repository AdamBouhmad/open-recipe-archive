## Ingredients

- one quart of soup stock
- one quarter pound of noodles
- one and one half teaspoonfuls of salt
- one quarter pound of minced ham
- one tablespoonful of syou
- one small onion
- one stalk of celery
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the noodles until tender—about ten minutes
2. Remove from the hot water, and put in cold water to soak
3. To one quart of good soup stock add one onion and one stalk of celery, chopped fine, pepper and salt, and one tablespoonful of syou
4. Boil ten minutes
5. Remove noodles from the cold water, add them to the soup, and boil for five minutes more
6. Shred the ham
7. Just before serving sprinkle it on top
