## Ingredients

- about two quarts of boiling peanut oil
- noodles

## Directions

1. Have about two quarts of boiling peanut oil.
2. Throw in the noodles and fry a golden brown.
3. Remove from oil, and drain off all fat by laying noodles on bit of paper.
