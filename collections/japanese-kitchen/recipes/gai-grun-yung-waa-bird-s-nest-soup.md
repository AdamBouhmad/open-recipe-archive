## Ingredients

- one half pound of bird's nest
- one pint of chicken stock
- one quarter pound of cooked breast of chicken
- one boiled egg
- one quarter pound of minced ham
- one teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Boil the bird's nest an hour, then drain and put into cold water
2. Pound the cooked chicken meat well so as not to be in large or hard pieces
3. Add a cupful of the cold stock to the chicken meat
4. Take the bird's nest from the cold water and well drain it
5. Add the bird's nest to the soup stock
6. Boil for half an hour
7. Add the chicken meat, and also the egg, the latter having previously been finely crumbled
8. Take the soup off the fire as it begins to boil again after the last addition
9. Before serving, sprinkle the minced ham on top
