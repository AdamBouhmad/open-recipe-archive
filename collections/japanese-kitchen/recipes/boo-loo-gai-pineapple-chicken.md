## Ingredients

- One young chicken
- one tablespoonful of sweet lard
- one tablespoonful of syou
- one can of preserved pineapple
- one teaspoonful of Quong Sang Chong

## Directions

1. Wash and singe a young, fresh chicken and cut off all the flesh.
2. Slice it, put a tablespoonful of sweet lard in the pan, and fry.
3. Do not let it burn.
4. Add the chicken, and fry brown.
5. Add a tablespoonful of syou and a can of preserved pineapple, and cook slowly for fifteen minutes.
6. Thicken the pineapple juice with a teaspoonful of Quong Sang Chong, and serve hot with rice.
