## Ingredients

- six char quars
- one teaspoonful of olive oil
- one tablespoonful of Quong Sang Chong
- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- two tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley
- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. First wash thoroughly in cold water, then turn them into boiling salted water and boil about twenty minutes.
2. Meanwhile prepare the following sauce: Mix one teaspoonful of olive oil with one tablespoonful of Quong Sang Chong (water chestnut flour) until very smooth, then pour enough boiling water over it to make a thick cream.
3. Add one teaspoonful of syou, and let it just come to a boil, stirring in the chopped parsley gently.
4. Arrange the artichokes on a small platter with green lining, or place a green leaf under each artichoke.
5. Fill the center of each with the yolks of hard-boiled egg, whipped smooth with a little olive oil and salt.
6. Lastly, pour the sauce over, and serve.
7. This should look like a water lily.
