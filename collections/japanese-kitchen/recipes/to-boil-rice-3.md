## Ingredients

- iron pot
- rice
- one cupful of rice
- one and one quarter cupfuls of cold water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the rice thoroughly in many waters, until the water is as clear as for drinking
2. To one cupful of rice add one and one quarter cupfuls of cold water
3. Cover tight, and boil slowly half an hour—no more
4. On no account stir
5. Set on back of range, covered with a clean cloth or napkin, until ready to serve
6. Rice should be served in individual bowls, replenished as soon as empty throughout the meal
7. Salt is not added until it is served
