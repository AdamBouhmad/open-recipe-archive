## Ingredients

- One plump squab
- Two chicken livers
- One dozen fresh mushrooms
- One tablespoonful of Chinese almonds
- One tablespoonful of chicken fat
- Two tablespoonfuls of syou
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One quarter small onion
- One teaspoonful of cornstarch

## Directions

1. Clean and singe a plump squab.
2. Rub inside and out with salt.
3. Stuff it with the following: A dozen mushrooms, peeled and cut small, a tablespoonful of almonds which have been blanched and chopped fine, and a little minced onion.
4. Mix together, and fill the squab.
5. Now sew or skew tight, and melt the tablespoonful of chicken fat and pour it over the squab.
6. Place in a hot oven and roast for half an hour, basting and turning frequently.
7. Make the gravy, meanwhile, by chopping fine two chicken livers and frying them in a teaspoonful of chicken fat.
8. When very brown, stir in a teaspoonful of cornstarch until brown.
9. Now add two tablespoonfuls of syou, and serve with the squab.
