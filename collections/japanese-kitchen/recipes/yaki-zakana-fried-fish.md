## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- peanut or goma-seed oil, or olive oil
- oil-fried Tofu
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Wash the fish and scale it if necessary, removing all fins.
2. Rub well with salt.
3. Have ready a deep iron pot and use peanut or goma-seed oil, or, if preferred, olive oil.
4. Do not have too much oil in pan, but only enough to prevent burning.
5. Lay the fish in the pan, but do not crowd one on top of another; fry a light brown, being careful in turning not to break the fish.
6. Have ready oil-fried Tofu, and place each fish on a cake of about the length of the fish.
7. Sprinkle with grated lemon peel, and serve with rice, very hot.
