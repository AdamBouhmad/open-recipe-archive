## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one half a minced onion
- four sticks of celery
- four ounces of pork
- one dessert-spoonful of syou
- one half teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs well; have ready half a minced onion and four sticks of celery, chopped very fine.
2. Put in the frying pan four ounces of pork, chopped fine, and fry until brown.
3. Now add the herbs, with a dessert-spoonful of syou, and finally the beaten eggs.
4. Let the whole cook for five minutes, without touching, but be careful to keep it from burning.
5. Fold one half over the other, and slip on the platter.
6. Serve at once, with rice.
