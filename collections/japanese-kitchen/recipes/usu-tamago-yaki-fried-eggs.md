## Ingredients

- One half dozen eggs
- one cupful of cooking juice
- two tablespoonfuls of syou sauce

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with chopsticks, adding the cooking sauce and syou
2. Mix all well
3. Wipe a frying pan with paper soaked with goma-seed oil
4. As soon as pan is well heated, pour in the above mixture and fry over a slow fire
