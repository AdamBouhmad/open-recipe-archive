## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- one half pound of white mushrooms
- one dozen water chestnuts
- one quarter pound of bean sprouts
- one stalk of celery
- two small onions
- three eggs
- bit of garlic
- three pints of boiling, salted water

## Directions

1. Have ready three pints of boiling, salted water, with the two tablespoonfuls of syou in it
2. Add all vegetables (which have been previously washed in cold water, and cut in small pieces) except the mushrooms and celery
3. Boil for fifteen minutes, then add the mushrooms and celery
4. Whip up the three eggs in a little cold water, and drop slowly into the boiling soup, stirring the soup as it drops
5. Remove at once, and serve
