## Ingredients

- squash or vegetable marrows
- salt
- syou sauce
- chopped meat or fish
- miso
- pepper
- spices
- lemon

## Directions

1. Peel and boil squash in salted water for twenty minutes
2. Remove and allow to simmer in boiling syou sauce for five minutes longer
3. To stuff one, take cold meat, chop it very fine, then mix with it two tablespoonfuls of miso and salt, pepper, and spices, and two tablespoonfuls of syou, and fill the center
4. Fish can be used instead of meat; but if fish is used, add the juice of a lemon
