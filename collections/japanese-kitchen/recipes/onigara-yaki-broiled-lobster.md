## Ingredients

- One lobster
- syou sauce
- salt and pepper
- powdered sanshi spice

## Directions

1. Wash the lobster carefully in cold water.
2. Place in boiling, salted water, and boil very slowly for one hour.
3. Remove from the saucepan, and immediately throw into cold water.
4. Cut in two pieces lengthwise, and dip each half in syou sauce.
5. Place upon gridiron, and toast well both back and front, taking minute care not to blacken skin and fins.
6. Once again cover with syou sauce, sprinkle with sanshi spice and pepper, and serve.
