## Ingredients

- two pounds chicken flesh
- one half pound seaweed jelly or gelatine
- one tablespoonful sugar
- salt
- pepper
- one quarter pound dried mushrooms
- one cup mirin sauce
- water

## Directions

1. Cook in boiling water a young, tender chicken until it is tender.
2. Take from the liquid, remove all the bone and cut in small pieces.
3. Pick off stalks from mushrooms and soak in lukewarm water for twenty minutes.
4. Cut in small pieces and boil for twenty-five minutes in about two cupfuls of water.
5. Add the mirin sauce and boil again about five minutes.
6. Wash the dry seaweed jelly and soak for about two hours, then take from water and squeeze dry.
7. Break into small pieces and boil in one quart of water until it dissolves.
8. Strain into another saucepan, with a pinch of salt and a tablespoonful of sugar.
9. To this add the mushrooms and mirin sauce, and mix all together, with the chicken meat, salt, pepper, spices, etc.
10. Have ready a lacquered box or smooth wooden box or mold, and pour in the mixture.
11. Let it set, and, when serving, cut it in slices.
