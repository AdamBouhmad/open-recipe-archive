## Ingredients

- Two and one half pounds of chicken
- Two tablespoonfuls of sweet lard
- One tablespoonful of syou
- One half onion
- One teaspoonful of cornstarch or Quong Sang Chong
- One teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Clean a young spring chicken, and cut it into small pieces.
2. Fry to a golden brown in hot, sweet lard.
3. Serve with a brown gravy prepared as follows: Boil the giblets until tender, then chop very fine and return to the liquor in which they were boiled.
4. Add a tablespoonful of syou and half an onion chopped fine; boil for ten minutes.
5. Thicken with a teaspoonful of cornstarch or rice flour, and pour over the chicken.
6. Serve hot, with rice.
