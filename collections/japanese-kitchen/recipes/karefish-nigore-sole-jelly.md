## Ingredients

- one medium-sized sole
- one cupful mirin sauce
- two carrots
- one quarter pound dried mushrooms
- one half pound Kanton (dried seaweed jelly)
- salt
- water
- sugar
- syou sauce

## Directions

1. First prepare the sole by cutting away the head and thoroughly cleaning and scaling.
2. Cut in fillets or slices, removing the small bones.
3. Take one cupful of mirin sauce and a pinch of salt.
4. Place in pot, and boil only a few minutes.
5. Chop into small pieces two carrots and about a quarter pound of dried mushrooms (first washed and soaked).
6. Boil in a small quantity of water, and when tender, strain.
7. Add the mirin sauce, previously boiled for fifteen minutes.
8. Wash half a pound of dry seaweed jelly, and soak in water for two hours.
9. Take out, and squeeze dry.
10. Break in small pieces, and throw in pan with a quart of water.
11. Boil and dissolve.
12. Strain into another saucepan.
13. Add half a cupful of sugar and a tablespoonful of syou sauce.
14. With this mix the fish and vegetables, and stir thoroughly.
15. Transfer to lacquered or smooth wooden box, and let it set.
16. When it is firm, cut in desired small shapes.
