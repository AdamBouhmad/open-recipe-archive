## Ingredients

- One good-sized hard cabbage
- one cupful of syou sauce
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- one pound of salt pork
- one onion
- salt
- pepper
- goma seeds

## Directions

1. First boil the salt pork for one hour, then chop it in small pieces
2. Select a good hard cabbage without blemishes and wash it thoroughly in cold water
3. Soak it in water with a handful of salt to kill all possible vermin
4. Remove from salted water, and rinse in fresh water, removing all salt
5. Carefully cut out the heart and center leaves, making a good-sized pocket, and fill this space with the chopped pork and one onion chopped very fine, salt, pepper, and a teaspoonful of goma seeds
6. Press the cabbage leaves back into place, and tie tight in cheesecloth
7. Have ready a deep pot of boiling salted water, and in this place the cabbage in the bag
8. Cover, and boil slowly for two hours
9. Remove to a platter, and pour over it the syou sauce, which has first been boiled
10. Serve with rice
