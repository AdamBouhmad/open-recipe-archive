## Ingredients

- five eggs
- small pink snapper (fish)
- miso
- pepper
- salt
- udo shoots
- lettuce head

## Directions

1. Boil five eggs hard
2. Place in cold water
3. Remove shells carefully, so as not to blemish whites
4. Carefully cut off top with thread, one end between teeth, the other between fingers, drawing thread through egg
5. Remove the yolks
6. Boil a small pink snapper (fish) in hot water for ten minutes, or steam for thirty
7. Remove all bones and fins, and chop together until fine
8. Mix with finely mashed miso, pepper, and salt
9. Chop yolks daintily and fluffily, and mix with fish meat
10. Fill the whites with this mixture
11. Place the filled whites in center of a lettuce head and arrange fine strips of udo shoots round it
12. Carefully adjust and separate all the leaves of the lettuce head, wash, and then put back into shape again
13. Hold together with toothpicks
