## Ingredients

- water chestnuts
- two tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- one onion
- one tablespoonful of fine vinegar
- two teaspoonfuls of syou
- one teaspoonful of salt
- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- one half teaspoonful of sugar
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel and wash these little nuts, which are about the size of an ordinary chestnut.
2. Slice very thin.
3. Make a dressing of the following: To two tablespoonfuls of olive oil add the juice of one onion, one tablespoonful of fine vinegar, two teaspoonfuls of syou, one teaspoonful of salt, the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs crushed smooth, and half a teaspoonful of sugar.
4. Mix all until smooth, then pour it over the water chestnuts.
5. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
