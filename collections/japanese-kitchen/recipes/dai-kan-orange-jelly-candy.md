## Ingredients

- one half pound of Kanton (seaweed jelly)
- three quarters pound of fine white sugar
- three tablespoonfuls of kudzu starch
- two oranges

## Directions

1. Wash half a pound of dry seaweed jelly, and soak it for two and a half hours in cold water.
2. Then squeeze out all the water and break in small pieces.
3. Boil in about a quart of water, until well melted, then mix three quarters of a pound of sugar with this, stirring with chopsticks or wooden spoon.
4. Strain into another pan, and boil over medium fire.
5. Take three tablespoonfuls of kudzu starch and dissolve in a little cold water.
6. Then, drop by drop, gradually drop this into the jelly, while stirring with wooden spoon.
7. When it becomes transparent, squeeze in the juice of two oranges and immediately remove from fire.
8. Pour into a wooden box, and set in cool place for two hours to harden.
