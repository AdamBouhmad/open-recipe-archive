## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- water
- one tablespoonful of olive oil
- one teaspoonful of vinegar
- a dash of cayenne
- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, and place in salted boiling water.
2. Boil slowly until the cucumbers become transparent.
3. Drain off all water and serve with a sauce made of one tablespoonful of olive oil and one teaspoonful of vinegar, beaten well with a dash of cayenne and the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs.
