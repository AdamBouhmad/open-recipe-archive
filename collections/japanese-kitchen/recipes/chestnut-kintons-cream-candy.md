## Ingredients

- one quart of chestnuts
- one pint of sweet potatoes
- one pound of sugar
- three quarters pint of mirin sauce
- one tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Blanch the chestnuts and place them in lukewarm water.
2. Boil, changing water several times, until you can pierce the nuts with a needle.
3. Strain off water, then add one pound of sugar, three quarters of a pint of mirin sauce, and boil slowly.
4. Have ready sweet potatoes which have been peeled and boiled for twenty minutes.
5. Add to them the vinegar, and strain and press through sieve.
6. Gradually mix with the chestnuts, and slowly boil, stirring constantly, until dry; then mold into desired shapes.
7. Roll in powdered sugar.
