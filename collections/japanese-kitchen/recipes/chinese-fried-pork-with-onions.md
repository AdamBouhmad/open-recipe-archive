## Ingredients

- One pound of pork
- two large onions
- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- one teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cut about a pound of fresh pork (not too fat) in tubes, and fry a light brown.
2. Cut up two large onions and fry with the pork until all is brown, then add the salt and two tablespoonfuls of syou, and fry very hot.
3. This is very tasty, and good served with boiled rice.
