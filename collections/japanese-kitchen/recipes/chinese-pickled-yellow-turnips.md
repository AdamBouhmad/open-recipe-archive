## Ingredients

- turnip
- two cupfuls of vinegar
- a dash of cayenne
- one quarter of a clove of garlic
- two teaspoonfuls of salt
- one teaspoonful of ginger

## Directions

1. Cut the turnip in thin slices, then cut each slice in narrow strips until it looks like strands.
2. Place in bowl and over it pour two cupfuls of vinegar, a dash of cayenne, a quarter of a clove of garlic, two teaspoonfuls of salt, and one teaspoonful of ginger, and let stand twenty-four hours before serving.
