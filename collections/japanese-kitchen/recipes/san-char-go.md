## Ingredients

- red Chinese dates
- powdered sugar
- gelatine

## Directions

1. The fruit is first mashed to a pulp, then mixed with powdered sugar and gelatine, beaten into a paste, rolled in sheets as thin as paper, and spread in the sun to dry.
2. After it has dried crisp, it is cut into squares of six by four inches, wrapped in waxed paper, and packed in bundles.
