## Ingredients

- long plump bananas
- shredded coconut
- cream
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bake long, plump bananas for ten minutes in medium oven.
2. Remove and split skin down one side.
3. Remove the inside, and whip it up with shredded coconut, a little cream and sugar, and a little lemon juice.
4. Return to shell, and bake for another ten minutes.
