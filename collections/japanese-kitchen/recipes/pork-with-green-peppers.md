## Ingredients

- One and one half pounds of pork
- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- six green peppers
- one onion
- one teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cut the pork in strips about two inches long and half an inch thick.
2. Wash the peppers, remove the seeds, and cut into small pieces; then add to the meat with two tablespoonfuls of syou, one onion chopped fine, and a tablespoonful of salt.
3. Cover, and cook for ten minutes.
4. Serve with rice.
