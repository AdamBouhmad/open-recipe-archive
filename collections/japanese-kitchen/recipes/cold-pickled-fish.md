## Ingredients

- two pounds of fish
- one pint of vinegar
- one pint of water
- four red peppers
- one tablespoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the fish, then wipe dry and lay it in an earthen dish
2. Mix together one pint of vinegar and one pint of water, and when boiling, pour it over the fish
3. Cut up four red peppers, taking out the seeds
4. Sprinkle the salt and sugar over the fish, and then lay in the peppers
5. Cover tight, and put away for two hours
6. Drain off all vinegar and water
