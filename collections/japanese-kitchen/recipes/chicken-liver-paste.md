## Ingredients

- chicken livers
- fresh or dried mushrooms
- onion juice
- lemon juice
- salted crackers

## Directions

1. Fry chicken livers over slow fire, or boil if preferred.
2. Drain from water or fat when cooked, and crush through sieve.
3. Fry a cupful of fresh or dried mushrooms.
4. Crush these also through sieve, and add it to the livers.
5. Add the juice of an onion and some lemon juice.
6. Serve on salted crackers.
