## Ingredients

- hard crisp cabbage
- goma-seed or olive oil
- stoned prunes
- lemon juice
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and shred fine a hard, crisp cabbage.
2. In deep pan put goma-seed or olive oil.
3. Add the cabbage, with half a dozen stoned prunes and the juice of a lemon, pepper, and salt.
4. Let this slowly stew in its own juices for an hour or so.
