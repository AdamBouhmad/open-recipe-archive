## Ingredients

- duck
- salt
- kudzu starch
- eggs
- oil
- giblets
- one cupful of water
- one cupful of syou sauce
- pepper
- one teaspoonful of sugar
- orange or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the duck and wash in lukewarm water, then wipe dry with a clean cloth.
2. Rub well with salt.
3. Cut in pieces, and dip each piece of the duck in a batter made of kudzu starch and eggs.
4. Make the batter of about the thickness of fritters.
5. Lay these in a pan that has been oiled, and bake in a slow oven for two hours.
6. Make a gravy by chopping the giblets very fine and putting them in a pot with a cupful of water, a cupful of syou sauce, salt and pepper, and a teaspoonful of sugar.
7. Boil slowly while the duck is baking.
8. Place the duck, which should be well browned, on a platter, then pour over it the gravy and garnish with orange or lemon peel, grated.
