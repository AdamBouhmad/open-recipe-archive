## Ingredients

- Two pound chicken
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one quart of lychee nuts
- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- one half small onion
- two tablespoonfuls of sweet lard

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces a fresh young chicken of about two pounds and rub well with salt.
2. Fry two tablespoonfuls of sweet lard a very light brown.
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of syou, and cover tight.
4. Simmer for half an hour.
5. Have ready a quart of lychee nuts, peeled and stoned.
6. Add these to the chicken, with the minced onion, and cook slowly for twenty-six minutes.
7. Serve with rice.
