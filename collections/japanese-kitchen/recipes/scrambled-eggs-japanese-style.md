## Ingredients

- Eggs
- green pepper
- cold boiled rice
- mushrooms
- onion

## Directions

1. Break into frying pan half a dozen eggs, stirring lightly with knife
2. Add quickly, before egg begins to cook, a tablespoonful of chopped green pepper, the juice of an onion, half a cupful of chopped mushrooms, and half a cupful of boiled rice
3. Toss all quickly together
4. Cook over a very slow fire, and serve hot
