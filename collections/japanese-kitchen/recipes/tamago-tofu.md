## Ingredients

- Eggs
- cooking juice
- syou sauce
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Break any required number of eggs and beat with chopsticks
2. Mix with twice the amount of cooking juice and one third of syou sauce
3. Pour the mixture into a thin china bowl or a tin mold, and steam over boiling water for thirty or forty minutes
4. Transfer a soupladleful to each individual bowl, and over each pour a small quantity of cooking juice and a few drops of syou sauce
5. Sprinkle with ground horseradish, and serve very hot
