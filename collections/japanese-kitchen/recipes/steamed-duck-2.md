## Ingredients

- Three and one half pound duck
- One quarter pound of pork
- One quarter pound of mushrooms
- One quarter pound of water chestnuts
- One quarter cup of barley
- One and one half tablespoonfuls of syou
- One teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. After the duck has been well cleaned, inside and out, cut down the breast, dry well, and rub it thoroughly with salt.
2. Lay in a bowl, and then prepare the following filling: Take one quarter pound of pork, chopped fine; one quarter pound of mushrooms, cut small, and one quarter pound of water chestnuts, sliced thin.
3. Boil one quarter pound of barley for ten minutes, adding half a tablespoonful of syou and a teaspoonful of salt.
4. Mix together all of the above ingredients, fill the duck, and sew or skew it up.
5. Steam for three hours, and serve with rice.
