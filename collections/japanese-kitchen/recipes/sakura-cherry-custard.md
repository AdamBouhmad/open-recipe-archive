## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- one cupful of milk
- sugar
- three tablespoonfuls of cherry sirup
- ripe, plump red cherries

## Directions

1. Beat up lightly and frothily six fresh eggs
2. Add one cupful of milk, sugar, and three tablespoonfuls of cherry sirup
3. Throw a handful of ripe, plump red cherries into the baking pan, and over this pour the above mixture
4. Bake for about half an hour in a medium oven
5. Remove, and at intervals over top of dish set more cherries
