## Ingredients

- eggplant
- ice water
- salt
- beaten eggs
- goma-seed oil
- one cupful of cooking sauce
- goma seeds
- rice

## Directions

1. Peel the eggplant and cut it in slices
2. Cover with ice water and a handful of salt
3. Put on a heavy weight, iron or stone, to press out the juice, and leave for about half an hour
4. Strain off all water, which will be a dark brown, and rinse with fresh cold water, wiping dry each slice
5. Dip each piece in beaten eggs, and fry in goma-seed oil
6. Drain off all oil
7. Place on a platter, covering well with one cupful of cooking sauce, boiled
8. Sprinkle with goma seeds, and serve with rice
