## Ingredients

- water
- sugar
- vinegar
- mixed spices
- plums
- mirin sauce

## Directions

1. Have a sirup made of half a cupful of water, two cupfuls of sugar, a quarter cupful of vinegar, and half a teaspoonful of mixed spices, ground.
2. When it is boiling drop in the plums, and let boil slowly for twenty minutes.
3. Add half a cupful of mirin sauce, or any other desired spirits, and let all come to a boil once.
4. Remove, and serve.
