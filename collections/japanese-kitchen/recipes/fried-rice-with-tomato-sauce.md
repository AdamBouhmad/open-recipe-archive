## Ingredients

- one teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- two cupfuls of boiled rice
- four tomatoes, or half a can
- one onion
- one tablespoonful of fat pork
- dash of cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut up the pork and fry brown.
2. Put in the onion, then the tomatoes and salt and cayenne.
3. Fry for five minutes.
4. Turn the rice, which has previously been cooked, into the pan and fry all together for five minutes.
5. Place on hot platter, and garnish with minced parsley and eggs, threaded.
6. Serve very hot.
