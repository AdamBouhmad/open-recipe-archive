## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of lard
- one quarter of an onion
- one half pound of fresh mushrooms
- one tablespoonful of syou
- four eggs
- one quarter teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Into a tablespoonful of hot, sweet lard fry quarter of an onion a light brown.
2. Cut half a pound of fresh mushrooms very fine, and cook for five minutes.
3. Beat four eggs with one tablespoonful of syou, and turn into pan.
4. Cook for five minutes without touching, but keep from burning by gently shaking the pan.
5. Fold over, or slip from pan into dish which is kept warm over a dish of hot water.
