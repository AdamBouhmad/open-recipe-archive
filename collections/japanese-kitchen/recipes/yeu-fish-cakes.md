## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one cupful of fish meat
- two cupfuls of rice flour
- two tablespoonfuls of peanut oil
- one and one half teaspoonfuls of salt
- one quarter pound of almonds
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take any dried fish, and remove all bones and skin
2. Pound the fish to a powder, and mix one cup of the fish with a quarter pound of blanched almonds, also pounded
3. Add two cupfuls of dried and powdered rice flour, and half a teaspoonful of salt
4. Beat the whites of two eggs with a little cold water, enough to make a thin paste
5. Mix well all together
6. Roll very thin, cut into cakes, and bake in a hot oven in hot peanut oil for twenty minutes
7. Remove the cakes from the oil, and drain on paper in a slow oven until cakes are dry and crisp
