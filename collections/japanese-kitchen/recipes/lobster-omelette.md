## Ingredients

- one teaspoonful of peanut oil
- two eggs
- one tablespoonful of cold water
- one tablespoonful of chopped lobster meat
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Use small frying pan and put into it a teaspoonful of peanut oil
2. Heat it
3. Beat two eggs with a tablespoonful of cold water
4. Pour half in the pan
5. Have ready the cooked lobster, broken into small pieces
6. Quickly pour in the other half of beaten eggs, and cook slowly for five minutes
7. Slip off pan without breaking, and make two or three more omelettes in exactly the same way, or have several small frying pans and cook all at once
8. Serve one omelette on top of another in a hot water-heated platter
