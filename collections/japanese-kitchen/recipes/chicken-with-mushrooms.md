## Ingredients

- One tablespoonful of sweet lard
- two pounds of chicken
- one onion
- one pound of fresh mushrooms
- one tablespoonful of syou
- two teaspoonfuls of salt

## Directions

1. Put the lard in the pan, and have it boiling hot.
2. Cut up a young chicken, and remove all the bones; then lay it in the fat, and cook to a golden brown.
3. Mince the onion and add.
4. Wash and cut up the mushrooms, and add with a tablespoonful of syou.
5. Cook all slowly for fifteen minutes.
