## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- a tiny piece of washing soda
- endive
- one tablespoonful of olive oil
- one tablespoonful of vinegar
- a dash of cayenne
- one half teaspoonful of sugar
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one clove of garlic

## Directions

1. Have water boiling hot and salted.
2. Now put in a tiny piece of washing soda.
3. Wash the endive, throw it into boiling water, and boil slowly for ten minutes.
4. While boiling, take one tablespoonful of olive oil and heat it.
5. Add one tablespoonful of vinegar, a dash of cayenne, half a teaspoonful of sugar, a teaspoonful of salt, and one clove of garlic, chopped very fine.
6. Let this all simmer for five minutes, then drain off the water, and pour out the endive, and serve.
