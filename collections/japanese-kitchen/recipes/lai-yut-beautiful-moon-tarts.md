## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of rice flour
- one heaping tablespoonful of clarified goose fat
- two eggs
- ice-cold water
- two cups of lychee nuts, stoned and mashed to a pulp
- one and one half cupfuls of sugar
- quarter of a cupful of crystallized limes
- one teaspoonful of mixed spices
- candied orange peel or fruit coloring

## Directions

1. Work a heaping tablespoonful of clarified goose fat into two cups of rice flour.
2. Add ice-cold water slowly to make a stiff paste.
3. Roll out, brush with the whipped whites of eggs, and fold over several times, each time brushing with egg.
4. Roll about quarter of an inch thick and cut into rounds about the size of a small saucer.
5. Put in the filling, and place another round of paste on top.
6. Press together at the edge, forming a rounded edge, and brush all again with white of egg.
7. Bake for fifty minutes in a moderate oven, then remove the outer flake, which will leave the tart snow white.
8. Decorate with a yellow moon in the center, cut from candied orange peel or painted with fruit coloring.
