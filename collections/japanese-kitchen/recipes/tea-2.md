## Ingredients

- tea leaves
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Rinse the pot with boiling water before putting in the tea
2. The water should just come to a boil—no more
3. Pour on the tea leaves
4. Let it stand for two or three minutes, but on no account must it be allowed to boil
5. Strain tea from leaves, and serve pure, if Chinese tea is used
