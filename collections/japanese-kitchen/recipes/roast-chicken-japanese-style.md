## Ingredients

- young, tender chicken
- rice
- chicken gravy
- chopped onion
- mixed spices
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- roasted sweet potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the chicken.
2. Put the feet to boil until the scabs scale off, then place with the giblets to boil slowly.
3. Make a stuffing of rice boiled dry in chicken gravy, add chopped onion and a pinch of mixed spices, salt, pepper, and a dash of cayenne.
4. Put this in body of chicken and sew up.
5. Roast for an hour, or longer, according to age of chicken.
6. Make a gravy from the stock of the giblets.
7. Chop the giblets up fine and add to the gravy.
8. Serve with roasted sweet potatoes.
