## Ingredients

- Two and one half pound chicken
- two tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- one cupful of Chinese almonds
- two stalks of celery
- one teaspoonful of onion juice
- two tablespoonfuls of syou
- one quarter pound of white mushrooms
- one cupful of almonds or walnuts, blanched and chopped
- Quong Sang Chong

## Directions

1. Use only the breast of a young chicken, and cut it in small tubes.
2. Fry to a golden brown in two tablespoonfuls of olive oil.
3. Take a cupful of almonds or walnuts, blanched and chopped, and half a pound of small white mushrooms cut up small; to this add a teaspoonful of onion juice and two tablespoonfuls of syou.
4. Turn all in with the chicken and simmer half an hour.
5. Thicken with Quong Sang Chong, and garnish with a border of nuts.
