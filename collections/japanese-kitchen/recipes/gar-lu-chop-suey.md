## Ingredients

- One half pound of pork
- One half pound of beef
- One quarter clove of garlic
- One onion
- One half bunch of celery
- One dozen lotus seeds
- One half can of bamboo shoots
- One and one half pounds of bean sprouts
- Three tablespoonfuls of syou
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Dash cayenne pepper
- Chinese dried mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of pork (not too fat) into small pieces, and fry to a nice golden brown.
2. Chop into small pieces half a bunch of celery, and wash and soak Chinese dried mushrooms, discarding the stalks.
3. Chop up one onion, and a very small piece of garlic, chopped very fine.
4. Season with three tablespoonfuls of syou, salt, and add a dash of cayenne.
5. Fry for ten minutes, then add half a can of bamboo shoots and fry for five minutes longer.
6. Lastly, add one and one half pounds of bean sprouts, and cook all together for ten minutes.
7. Serve with rice.
