## Ingredients

- two and one half pounds of chicken
- one quarter pound of lean pork
- one quarter pound of dried mushrooms
- one dozen lotus seeds
- three stalks of celery
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful of syou
- one onion
- three pints of cold water

## Directions

1. Clean a fresh young chicken of about two and one half pounds
2. Cut off all the flesh; put the bones, with liver and gizzard, into three pints of cold water, and boil for two hours, adding water if it boils away
3. While the stock is boiling, cut up the chicken meat into small pieces
4. Cut up very fine one quarter pound of lean pork
5. Wash and soak in lukewarm water one quarter pound of dried mushrooms
6. Pull off all the stalks
7. Peel and cut a dozen lotus seeds or water chestnuts
8. Chop three stalks of celery very fine, and also one onion
9. Pour off the liquor from the bones, and strain
10. Cut up liver and gizzard fine and return to stock
11. Add all the ingredients, with one teaspoonful of salt and one tablespoonful of syou
12. Boil all together for one hour
