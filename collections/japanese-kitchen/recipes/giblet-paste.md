## Ingredients

- giblets
- onion juice
- lemon juice
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Boil giblets until they are in shreds.
2. Crush through sieve, then add the juice of an onion and lemon juice.
3. Whip lightly with the hard-boiled yolk of eggs.
4. Serve on crackers, with crumbled yolks of eggs sprinkled on top.
