## Ingredients

- Two ounces of Kanton (Japanese gelatine)
- six eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of cherry sirup
- one ounce of goma seeds

## Directions

1. To make the pink custards, whip the whites of five eggs into the dissolved two ounces of gelatine.
2. Add, while stirring over a slow fire, three tablespoonfuls of cherry sirup.
3. Let it come to a boil, then pour quickly into flower-shaped molds, and let it set.
4. Yellow custards can be made by using the yolks instead of the whites of eggs, and half a pint of coconut milk.
5. When the custards are solid, turn them from the molds on to large leaves on a plate, or decorate the flowered custard with leaves suitable to the flower represented.
6. If the flower has a dark center, use goma seeds, and if variegated colors are liked, pour half the yellow and half the cherry custards into the molds.
7. The pouring should be done very quickly and lightly, and the molds, of course, should be of flower designs.
