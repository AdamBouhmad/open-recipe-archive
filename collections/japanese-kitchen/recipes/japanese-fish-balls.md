## Ingredients

- boiled fish, cold
- rice
- flour
- goma-seed oil

## Directions

1. Take any boiled fish, cold, and mix with rice, boiled to a paste.
2. Roll in flour to balls the size of large marbles.
3. Toss into boiling goma-seed oil, and fry a golden brown.
