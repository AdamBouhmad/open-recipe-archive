## Ingredients

- one half teaspoon of sweet lard
- one onion
- five eggs
- one teaspoonful of salt
- dash of cayenne pepper or black pepper

## Directions

1. Put half a tablespoonful of lard in frying pan and when it is very hot toss in one onion, chopped fine, and fry to a golden brown.
2. Break five eggs in a bowl, beat well, and turn into the pan; then keep stirring until it is done.
3. It should all be a light golden brown.
4. This is very good with rice.
