## Ingredients

- eggs, cooked and hard
- cold water
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- fresh butter
- grated cheese
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, ground
- egg yolks
- butter for the pan
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook eggs hard and place in cold water, shell them, cut lengthwise in two halves, remove the yolks and pound them in a mortar with bread soaked in milk, a piece of fresh butter, grated cheese, chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and ground nutmeg
2. Bind with five or six egg yolks, butter the bottom of a plate and put a little of the mixture on it, arrange the hard egg whites filled with the same mixture in a circle and covered as if they were whole, brush with butter on top, cover with fine breadcrumbs, and place to cook in the oven
3. When browned, serve hot as an entrée
