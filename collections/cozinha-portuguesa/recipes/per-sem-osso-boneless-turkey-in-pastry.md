## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Fatback lard, salted and peppered
- Quarter measure of flour
- Turkey feet
- Turkey head and beak

## Directions

1. Prepare the boneless turkey in the same manner as boneless chicken was prepared, larding it very well with salted and peppered fatback lard
2. Knead together a quarter measure of flour, making a tender dough
3. Place the turkey in the dough, seasoning it on the outside, and seal the pastry very well
4. Place the turkey's feet in one bowl and the head with beak in another, so that it appears alive
5. Cook the pastry pie in gentle heat and serve at the table
