## Ingredients

- hare
- lard
- blood of the hare
- liver
- penned
- blood
- picante sauce or sweet sauce

## Directions

1. Skin the hare, open it by the belly, remove the tripe and bile, keep the fresadura and blood
2. Break the bones of the thighs with the flat of the knife, and putting the legs one into the other, place it to stiffen over some coals and rub it with its own blood
3. When cold, lard it with fine lard and put it to roast on the spit
4. After roasted, serve it with picante sauce or with sweet sauce
5. Also after pounding the liver and serving in peverada sauce, pass it through a sieve, put it on the fire, bind it with the rest of the blood, and serve it separately
