## Ingredients

- Plovers
- Bacon, scraped
- Onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Egg yolks
- Lard
- Bacon sheets
- Paper leaves
- Olive ragout

## Directions

1. Pluck the plovers, open them along the sides, remove the entrails and liver
2. Mince everything with scraped bacon, onion, and seasoning with salt, pepper, and half a garlic clove
3. Bind it with two egg yolks
4. Stuff the plovers with this mixture
5. Sew them closed, straighten them, and lard them in pork lard
6. Cover with bacon sheets and paper leaves
7. Roast them
8. When roasted, serve with olive ragout on top
