## Ingredients

- sour cherries
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the sour cherries and weigh one and a quarter arratel of them
2. Place water in a pan on the fire and bring to a boil
3. Add the cherries and give them a good boil for a short time
4. Remove the pan from the fire and with a skimming spoon or ladle remove the cherries, placing them in a bowl with cold water
5. Leave them in the water for a quarter of an hour, then drain the water and add fresh cold water
6. After another quarter of an hour, remove them and place on a plate to drain until well drained
7. Clean one arratel of sugar and place it in the pan with the cherries inside, leaving them overnight
8. The next day place on the fire to thicken, and when a drop of sugar placed on a plate no longer runs, they are done
9. Place in small pans
