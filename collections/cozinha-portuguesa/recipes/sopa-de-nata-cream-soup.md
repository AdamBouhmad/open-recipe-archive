## Ingredients

- butter
- slices of toasted bread
- powdered sugar
- four bowls of cream
- eight egg yolks
- a small amount of milk

## Directions

1. Grease a plate with butter
2. Line it with slices of toasted bread and sprinkle powdered sugar on top
3. Beat four bowls of cream with eight egg yolks and a small amount of milk
4. Pour this mixture over the bread slices with more powdered sugar on top
5. Bake in the oven over low heat
6. If the cream soaks into the bread, add more cream with eggs and sugar
