## Ingredients

- lobsters
- butter
- sauce
- chives
- truffles
- half a quartilho of white wine
- half a spoon of culí
- one clove of garlic
- a drop of oil
- lime juice

## Directions

1. After the lobsters are cooked, let them cool
2. Open them and remove the intestine from the tail
3. Cut them into equal fillets
4. Brown them in butter with sauce, minced chives, and truffles
5. After browning, add half a quartilho of white wine, half a spoon of culí, one clove of garlic, and a drop of oil
6. Let everything simmer little by little
7. When the sauce is reduced and of good taste, serve the lobsters with lime juice
