## Ingredients

- veal small intestines
- pork fat
- veal udder
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- fine herbs
- two shallots, chopped
- four or five egg yolks
- handful of grated bread
- one quartilho of milk

## Directions

1. Fill the small veal intestines with a piece of blanched pork fat and a veal udder, both cut into thin strips or slices
2. Place in a casserole seasoned with salt, pepper, fine spices, fine herbs, and two chopped shallots
3. Add four or five egg yolks, a handful of grated bread, and one quartilho of milk
4. Heat on the fire while stirring constantly
5. Once thickened and having good taste, fill the intestines
6. Tie them as instructed
7. Cook them in the same way
8. Once cooked, cooled, and dried, cover them with fine grated bread
9. Fry them on a gentle fire on both sides
10. Serve hot
