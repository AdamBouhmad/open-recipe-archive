## Ingredients

- clams
- flour
- olive oil
- vinegar
- garlic
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the clams very well from the sand
2. Place them in a pot to open
3. Once opened, wash them again in clean water
4. Dry them in a clean cloth
5. Flour them with some flour powder
6. Send them to the oven in an earthenware pot with olive oil, vinegar, garlic, and lemon juice
7. Once cooked, serve at table in the same pot
