## Ingredients

- young, large, and fat pigeons
- bacon
- ham
- veal sweetbreads
- truffles
- cucumbers
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Take young, large, and fat pigeons and remove all their bones
2. Fill them with a forcemeat made of bacon, ham, veal sweetbreads, truffles, and cucumbers, seasoned with little salt, pepper, and fine herbs, and mixed with three beaten egg whites
3. After stuffing and sewing closed, wrap in bacon slices and paper leaves
4. Roast them until done, then remove the bacon slices and paper
5. Serve with Italian sauce of good taste or any other sauce that seems appropriate
