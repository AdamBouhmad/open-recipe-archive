## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- citron peel
- orange flowers
- almond biscuits
- fine sugar
- salt
- milk
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites with rod and raise them
2. Add citron peel, orange flowers, almond biscuits, all chopped
3. Mix in fine sugar and salt
4. Add a drop of milk
5. Mix everything well
6. Cook as the previous recipe in clarified butter, well heated
7. Once cooked, place on plate and serve glazed with fine sugar and hot spatula as an entrée
