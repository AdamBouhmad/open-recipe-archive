## Ingredients

- Repolhos (cabbage)
- Coaves lombardas (Lombard cabbage)
- Barbante (twine)
- Qualquer caça ou peça de carne (any game or meat)
- Essência (essence)
- Picados (minced ingredients)
- Várias guarnições (various garnishes)
- Peças de carne de porco (pork meat)
- Chouriços (chorizo)
- Salhichas (sausages)
- Presunto (ham)
- Molho claro (clear sauce)

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbages into quarters
2. Blanch them in water
3. Drain and tie them with twine
4. Cook them in a light braize
5. When cooked, drain them
6. Arrange them around any game or meat
7. Serve with essence on top
8. They can also be served stuffed with minced ingredients and various garnishes
9. They can also be cooked with pieces of pork, chorizo, sausages, and ham with clear sauce on top
