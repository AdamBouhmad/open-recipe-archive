## Ingredients

- two sole, whole and fried
- half arratel of butter, washed
- a small amount of mustard
- onion, finely chopped
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take two sole after frying them whole, split them down the middle from top to bottom, and place them on plates
2. Take half an arratel of washed butter with a small amount of mustard and onion very finely chopped, and put it to fry with pepper, clove, and nutmeg
3. When it boils, pour this sauce over the sole
