## Ingredients

- Fish, cleaned
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Shallot
- Garlic, a small piece
- Salt
- pepper
- Fine herbs, pounded
- Butter
- Onion slices
- Bread, finely grated
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and open it on either side, remove the insides entirely, leaving only the head attached to the loins; if they cannot be left whole, they can be cut into fillets without separating them from the head
2. Season and marinate with olive oil, salt, shallot, a small piece of garlic, salt, pepper, all chopped, and pounded fine herbs
3. Line the bottom of a baking dish with butter and some onion slices
4. Straighten the fish on top with its fillets rolled around the head, which should remain in the middle
5. Cover with finely grated bread and put to cook in the oven
6. When cooked, pass to the serving plate carefully and serve with little sauce underneath and lemon juice
