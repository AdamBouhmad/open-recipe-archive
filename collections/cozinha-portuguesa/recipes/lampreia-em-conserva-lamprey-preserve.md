## Ingredients

- one or many lampreys
- blood
- olive oil
- pepper
- salt
- half a quartilho of olive oil
- half a quartilho of white wine
- a little vinegar
- pepper
- nutmeg
- three or four dried bay leaves

## Directions

1. Clean lampreys as described
2. Roast them in the oven in a pan with their blood, a splash of olive oil, pepper, and salt on top
3. When roasted, remove them from the sauce
4. Boil this same sauce with half a quartilho of olive oil, half a quartilho of white wine, and a little vinegar so the sauce is not too strong
5. While boiling, season with pepper, nutmeg, and three or four dried bay leaves
6. Let it cool
7. Put the lampreys in a barrel
8. Pour the cold sauce over them, covering them well with the sauce
9. Seal the barrel
