## Ingredients

- three arrátels of peaches
- three arrátels of sugar in thread point

## Directions

1. After drained, place three arrátels of peaches in three arrátels of sugar at thread point
2. Cook until it forms small bubbles
3. Remove from heat
4. Add scent if desired
5. Pour into small baskets
