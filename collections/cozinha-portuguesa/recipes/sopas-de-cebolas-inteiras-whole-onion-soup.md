## Ingredients

- Two or three dozen small round onions
- Broth
- Substance (broth essence)
- Bread crusts boiled in broth
- A capon or chicken cooked

## Directions

1. Boil two or three dozen small round onions in water
2. When half-cooked, place in cold water
3. Peel and prepare them
4. Place them to cook in broth with one spoon of substance
5. Let them simmer little by little
6. When cooked, serve over bread crusts boiled in broth as mentioned, with a capon or cooked chicken in the middle
