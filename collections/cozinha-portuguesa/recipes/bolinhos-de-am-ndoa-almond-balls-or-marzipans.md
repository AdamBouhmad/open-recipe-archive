## Ingredients

- One arratél of almonds
- Up to three egg whites
- A little marmalade or apricot preserve or any other
- A little orange flower covered and reduced to powder
- Fine sugar for drying
- More fine sugar for preventing sticking
- Egg whites, half beaten
- Fine sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Blanched, cleaned and dried one arratél of almonds, grind them in a stone mortar, adding from time to time up to three egg whites
2. When ground, add a little marmalade or apricot preserve or any other, a little orange flower covered and reduced to powder
3. Mix everything and dry it with fine sugar on the fire
4. Once dried, pour it on a table with another little sugar until the paste stops sticking to the hands
5. Cut afterwards into pieces, make them into little rolls, form the marzipans in the shape desired
6. Pass them through half-beaten egg whites, place them on fine sugar and pulverize them on all sides with the same sugar
7. When well covered with it, place them on sheets of white paper and these on sheets of copper
8. Put them to bake in a mild oven and serve them when necessary
