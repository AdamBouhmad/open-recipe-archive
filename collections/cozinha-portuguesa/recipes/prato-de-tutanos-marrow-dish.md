## Ingredients

- bone marrow from three or four bones
- two spoons of broth
- half arratel of sugar
- white wine
- salt
- chopped herbs
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- saffron
- spices
- two dozen beaten egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- cinnamon and sugar (mixed)

## Directions

1. Boil the bone marrow cut into pieces the size of a thumb in a pan with two spoons of broth
2. Add half arratel of sugar, white wine, a pinch of salt, chopped herbs, lemon juice, cinnamon, saffron, and all spices
3. When it has boiled two or three times, pour over two dozen beaten egg yolks mixed with breadcrumbs, cinnamon, and sugar
4. Brown in a frying pan with low heat underneath and on top so it cooks slowly and takes on good color
5. Detach from the pan with a spatula and arrange upright on a plate over pastry dough or toasted slices, with three or four egg pasta tubes around the plate, or without them, send hot to the table (for they are not good fried)
