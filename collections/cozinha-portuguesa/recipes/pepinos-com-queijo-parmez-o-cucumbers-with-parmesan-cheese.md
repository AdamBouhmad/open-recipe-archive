## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Broth
- Butter
- Ham essence
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Stuff and cook the cucumbers in broth
2. Arrange on a plate with a little butter underneath
3. Pour over them a little ham essence mixed with butter
4. Cover with grated cheese and place in the oven
5. After browning, clean the plate edges and serve as the preceding dish
