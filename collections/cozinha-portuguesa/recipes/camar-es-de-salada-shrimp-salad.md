## Ingredients

- Shrimp tails
- Salt
- Pepper
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Chives in slices
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook and clean the shrimp tails
2. Season with salt, pepper, olive oil, vinegar, sliced chives, and a little chopped parsley
3. Place in the plate with its sauce
4. Arrange neatly and serve garnished with parsley in the middle
5. They may also be served with remoulade sauce and a bit of mustard if desired
