## Ingredients

- Six or eight arratels of beef brisket
- broth
- a bundle of sausage
- chives
- two cloves of garlic
- thyme
- one bay leaf
- six or eight quarters of cabbage
- seven or eight whole onions
- salt
- whole pepper
- a dozen whole sausages

## Directions

1. Take six or eight arratels of beef brisket and after cutting into seven or eight equal parts, well trimmed, and soaked, blanch in boiling water
2. Place to cook in broth with a bundle of sausage, chives, two cloves of garlic, thyme, and one bay leaf
3. When half-cooked, add six or eight quarters of cabbage, which are also first blanched and squeezed, well tied, with seven or eight whole onions, everything seasoned with little salt and whole pepper
4. When everything is almost cooked, add a dozen whole sausages
5. When everything is finished cooking, remove the meat and vegetables and place to drain
