## Ingredients

- ham
- strong vinegar
- vine ash, sifted
- carqueja

## Directions

1. After the hams are well dried and hung, pass them through strong vinegar and powder them, and cover them with sifted vine ash; and hang them again until they dry
2. Place them then in a box with the same sifted ash on top and below, and thus arrange layers of hams and ash until the box is full, and between the layers a little carqueja
3. Having done this, nail the box lid in such a way that air cannot enter it, and when embarking, take care to place it in the coolest and driest part of the ship
4. By this method certainly the ham can be eaten with all its perfection, even if the voyage is of two or three years, and circumnavigate the globe
