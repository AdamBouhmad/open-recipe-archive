## Ingredients

- chickens
- caul
- chicken breasts or veal
- cream
- bacon slices

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the chickens as in the preceding recipe
2. Spread pieces of caul on a cloth, as many as there are chickens, placing on these and around them a hash made of chicken or veal breasts in cream
3. So that the hash cannot fall out, brush the edges of the caul pieces with a brush, and wrap each one around itself with its chicken in the middle
4. Place them in the oven to cook in a baking dish with bacon slices underneath
5. When cooked, serve with sauce of ham essence or any other that seems best
