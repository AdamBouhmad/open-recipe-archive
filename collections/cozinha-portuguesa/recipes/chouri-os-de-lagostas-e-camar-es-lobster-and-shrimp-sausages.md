## Ingredients

- three or four onions, roasted in the oven or under coals
- pork lard, cut into small dice
- tails from fifty shrimp, chopped
- eight or ten egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of the meat of the same shrimp
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- fine herbs
- fine spices
- intestines for filling
- white wine

## Directions

1. Roast three or four onions in the oven or under coals
2. Chop them and pound them
3. Mix with pork lard cut into small dice
4. Add the chopped tails from fifty shrimp
5. Add eight or ten egg yolks and two spoonfuls of the meat of the same shrimp
6. Place on the heat and stir always
7. Season with salt, pepper, chopped parsley, fine herbs, and fine spices
8. Remove when it begins to thicken and before it boils, when it has good taste
9. Fill the intestines as instructed
10. Cook them in white wine, being careful to prick them with a needle so they do not burst
11. Once cooled, store them on grills with paper underneath
12. Serve hot in small plates with slices of bread crumbs interleaved and sour oranges
