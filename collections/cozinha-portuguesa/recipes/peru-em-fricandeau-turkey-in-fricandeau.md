## Ingredients

- Young turkey (peru novo)
- Bacon plank (prancha de toucinho)
- Fine bacon (toucinho fino)
- Broth without salt (caldo sem sal)
- Veal planks (pranchas de vitella)
- Ham (presunto)
- Onion studded with cloves (cebola cravejada)
- Bundle of saffron (capella de salsa)

## Directions

1. After a young turkey is cleaned and cleaned with the legs tucked inside according to custom, singe it over a lit furnace and rub it with a plank of bacon.
2. After being well dried, lard it with fine bacon and put it to soak, and put it to cook in broth without salt, with planks of veal, ham, onion studded with cloves, a bundle of saffron, and planks of fine herbs.
