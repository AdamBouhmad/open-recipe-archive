## Ingredients

- hare
- salt
- pepper
- hare livers
- bacon, scraped
- sage
- chives
- truffles
- shallot
- garlic clove
- paper
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Clean the hares and cut into four or five pieces
2. Break the larger bones with the flat of the knife and season with little salt and pepper
3. Make a forcemeat with the livers of the same hares, scraped bacon, sage, chives, truffles, and shallot, with one garlic clove finely chopped, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Take sheets of paper and cut into quarters
5. On each one place a small strip of bacon with a little of the forcemeat, and the piece of hare in the middle with the same amount of forcemeat and another strip of bacon
6. Wrap the paper carefully in the shape of a papillote
7. Do the same with the remaining pieces
8. Place to roast on a grill over gentle heat and over a sheet of paper brushed with olive oil
9. After roasting both sides, serve dry in their papillotes for an appetizer
