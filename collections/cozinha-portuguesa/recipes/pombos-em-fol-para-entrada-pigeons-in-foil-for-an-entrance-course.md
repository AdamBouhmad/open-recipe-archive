## Ingredients

- five or six young pigeons
- bacon slices
- fine herbs
- milk
- salt
- whole pepper
- cloves
- dry coriander
- flour
- white wine
- clarified butter
- egg whites
- pork lard

## Directions

1. Take five or six young pigeons and after emptying, singeing, and cleaning them well, leaving their wings, feet, and heads, stuff them with a fine forcemeat made à la crème
2. Cook them between bacon slices and fine herbs, moistened with a half can of boiled cow's milk, seasoned with salt, whole pepper, cloves, a little dry coriander, cooking them slowly and taking care not to cook them too much
3. When cooked, make a sauce with flour, white wine, a bit of clarified butter, and two beaten egg whites, moisten the pigeons in it and fry them in pork lard
4. When fried and well-colored, serve garnished with fried sauce
