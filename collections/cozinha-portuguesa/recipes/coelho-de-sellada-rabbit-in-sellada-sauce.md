## Ingredients

- roasted rabbits
- salt
- pepper
- olive oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Roast the rabbits and cut into gigote pieces
2. Arrange on a plate of well-prepared sellada sauce with salt and pepper
3. When ready to serve, pour hot olive oil, vinegar, and pepper on top
4. Send to table
