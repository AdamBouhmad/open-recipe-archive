## Ingredients

- wheat flour, finest and most pure
- necessary quantity of leaven
- sufficient water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take the finest and most pure wheat flour and mix it with the necessary quantity of leaven
2. Add sufficient water for kneading, observing the temperature of the water, which should be tempered with salt
3. Work it with force for some time, and after it is very well worked and mixed with the leaven
4. Leave it in a place that is neither cold nor excessively hot, for it to ferment and rise
5. When it is leavened, remove it from the kneading trough and place it on a table
6. Cut it into equal pieces and form the loaves to the desired size
7. After shaping, put them to bake in the oven, carefully observing the degree of heat
