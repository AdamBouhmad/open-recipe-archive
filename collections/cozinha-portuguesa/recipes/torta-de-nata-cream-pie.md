## Ingredients

- Six bowls of cream
- One dozen egg yolks
- Half arratel of grated bread of ló or five réis of grated bread
- One arratel of sugar in thread stage

## Directions

1. Place in a pot six bowls of cream, one dozen egg yolks, half arratel of grated bread of ló or five réis of grated bread, and one arratel of sugar in thread stage.
2. Beat everything very well.
3. Allow it to boil, and when it is thick allow it to cool.
4. Make the pie with puff pastry, fill it with the cream mixture, bake it, and send to the table.
5. The same method is used for curd cheese and milk pies, but milk requires more eggs.
6. Fritters, almojavenas, and small pastries shaped like half-moons can also be made this way.
7. Breads of ten réis can also be hollowed out, filled with cream, browned, and sent to the table.
