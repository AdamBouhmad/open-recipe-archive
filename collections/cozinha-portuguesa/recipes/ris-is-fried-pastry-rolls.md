## Ingredients

- bread dough or puff pastry
- forcemeat or herb filling
- beaten eggs
- clarified butter or pork butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out with the rolling pin a piece of soft dough or folded puff pastry
2. Cut into strips the width of a hand breadth
3. Place on top at intervals small pieces of forcemeat à crème or other herb filling, well bound
4. Wet the dough all around with beaten eggs
5. Fold the dough one over the other lengthwise
6. When sealed, cut with a pastry wheel
7. Fry in clarified or pork butter
8. After frying, serve with grated sugar on top
