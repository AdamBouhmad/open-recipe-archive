## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton, cut very small
- half arratel of bacon
- vinegar
- all spices
- one quart of almonds
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut two arratels of mutton into very small pieces
2. Braise with half arratel of bacon and its vinegar
3. When cooked, season with all spices
4. Pound one quart of almonds and toast until brown
5. Add to the mutton to make it black
6. Add sugar and lemon juice to the sauce so it becomes sour and sweet
7. Place on plate over bread slices and serve
