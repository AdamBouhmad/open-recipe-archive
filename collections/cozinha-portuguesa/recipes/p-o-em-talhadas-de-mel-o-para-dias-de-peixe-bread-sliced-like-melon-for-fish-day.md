## Ingredients

- bread
- butter, browned
- minced fish
- roux
- four small red mullets, cooked
- cooked chives or fillets of sole
- butter
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- roux
- marisco of the best taste

## Directions

1. Remove the crust and crumb from bread and pass it for an instant through browned butter
2. Stuff it with minced fish and place it on its dish with a little roux underneath
3. Cover it with a little of the same minced fish
4. Arrange on top four small red mullets already cooked
5. Garnish the intervals with cooked chives or fillets of sole
6. Sprinkle with butter and egg yolks
7. Cover with breadcrumbs and put it to brown in the oven
8. Once browned, drain off the butter, clean the dish around the edges
9. Serve with roux sauce and garnished with marisco of the best taste, or as seems best
