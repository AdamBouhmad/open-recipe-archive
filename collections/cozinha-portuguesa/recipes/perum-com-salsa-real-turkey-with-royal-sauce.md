## Ingredients

- one roasted turkey
- the cooking liquid
- six quarters of quince
- four quarters of medlar
- vinegar
- all seasonings

## Directions

1. Roast the turkey as in the previous dish
2. Strain the sauce into a bowl set on the fire
3. Cook six quarters of quince and four quarters of medlar in this liquid
4. Season with vinegar and all seasonings so it becomes piquant
5. Cut the turkey into pieces and place on slices
6. Pour the sauce over with the fruit quarters and lemon
7. Serve to the table
