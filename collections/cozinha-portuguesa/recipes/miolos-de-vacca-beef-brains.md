## Ingredients

- beef brains
- water
- broth
- white wine
- pork fat
- salt
- pepper
- herbs

## Directions

1. Immediately place the brains in water, being well washed and cleaned of the membranes that cover them outside
2. Blanch them in boiling water
3. Then cook them in a half broth made with broth, white wine, pork fat, salt, pepper, and a bundle of dried herbs
4. When cooked, remove them from the broth and serve with a spicy sauce of good flavor, or with a ragout of whole small onions
