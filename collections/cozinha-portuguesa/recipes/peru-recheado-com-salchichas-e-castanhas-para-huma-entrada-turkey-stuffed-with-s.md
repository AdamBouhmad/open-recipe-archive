## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Turkey livers
- Chicken livers
- Scraped bacon
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sauce
- Onion
- Fine herbs
- Sausages
- Pork lard
- Broth
- Ham essence
- Almond kernel from chestnuts
- Cured ham essence
- Cullis

## Directions

1. Toast the chestnuts by the fire so their skin can be removed, and rub them very well
2. Mix the whole chestnuts with a hash made from the livers of the same turkey and chickens, scraped bacon, butter, salt, pepper, sauce, onion, and fine herbs
3. Stuff the turkey with this mixture after it has been mortified
4. Add some sausages that have already been browned
5. Close and tie with string
6. Brown in a casserole with pork lard
7. After browning, place in a covered roasting pan with bacon slabs and paper leaves
8. Roast on low heat for two or three hours
9. Remove from the roasting pan and untie the string
10. Serve with a ragú made from the almond kernels of the chestnuts and pieces of sausages cooked in broth, bound with ham essence and cullis
