## Ingredients

- cheese, fatty and grated
- white wine
- butter
- anchovies
- sauce
- pepper
- nutmeg
- egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole over fire half a pound of grated fatty cheese, half a quart of white wine, a piece of washed butter, two anchovies and one of chopped sauce, pepper, and nutmeg
2. Mix everything with a spoon until the cheese melts, add four egg yolks and their beaten whites
