## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- bacon, fine slices
- shallot sauce or sweet sauce
- vinegar
- sugar
- cloves
- cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in a light braise as above
2. Remove the skin and let it cool
3. Lard it with fine bacon
4. Place it on a spit and roast until the bacon is colored
5. Serve with shallot sauce or sweet sauce made with vinegar, sugar, cloves, and whole cinnamon
