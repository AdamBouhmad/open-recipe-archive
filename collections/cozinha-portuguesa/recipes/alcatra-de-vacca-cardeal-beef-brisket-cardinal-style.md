## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- thick bacon for larding
- two ounces of pounded saltpeter
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- a little juniper seed
- a glazed earthenware pot with lid
- bacon slices
- cloth and twine
- one canada of red wine
- two of water
- cloves studded onions
- one head of garlic
- carrots
- parsnips
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- thyme
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove the eyes from the underside of a piece of beef and lard it with thick bacon
2. Season without piercing the surface part, then rub with two ounces of pounded saltpeter
3. Season with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, and a little juniper seed
4. Place in a glazed earthenware pot with lid where it shall remain for four or five days
5. Cover with some bacon slices and wrap in cloth, tie with twine
6. Place to cook in a pot with one canada of red wine and two of water, with some cloves studded onions, one head of garlic, carrots, parsnips, bay leaf, marjoram, thyme, and nutmeg
7. When cooked, let it cool in the same broth
8. When cold, unwrap, trim, and serve for fiambre on a plate with its napkin
