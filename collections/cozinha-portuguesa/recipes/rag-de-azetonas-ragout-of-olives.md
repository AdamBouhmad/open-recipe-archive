## Ingredients

- Um cento de azetonas (one hundred olives)
- Água (water)
- Culis de vitela (veal extract)
- Enxova picada (minced anchovies)
- Capella (bouquet garni)
- Vinho branco (white wine)

## Directions

1. Take one hundred olives
2. Remove the pits so they appear whole
3. Blanch them in water
4. Drain them
5. Simmer for an instant in veal extract with minced anchovies, bouquet garni, and white wine
6. After simmering sufficiently, remove the bouquet garni
7. Season to taste
8. Serve for entrees of thrushes, woodcock, or any game
