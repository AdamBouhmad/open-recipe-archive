## Ingredients

- large pieces of veal
- large pieces of ham
- two roots
- one onion
- melted lard
- a piece of butter dusted with flour
- beef broth
- beef milk reduced
- salt
- pepper
- one clove of garlic
- one onion studded with cloves
- fresh herbs

## Directions

1. Cut into large pieces some pieces of veal and ham, two roots, and one onion
2. Pass everything through the fire in a saucepan with melted lard and a piece of butter dusted with flour
3. Then add broth and reduced beef milk, salt, pepper, one clove of garlic, one onion studded with cloves, fresh herbs
4. Let it boil gently and slowly
5. When cooked and reduced, pass it through a sieve
6. This sauce is used for roasted game fillets, vegetables, and other garnishes
