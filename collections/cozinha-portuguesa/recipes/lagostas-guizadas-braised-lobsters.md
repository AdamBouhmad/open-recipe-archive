## Ingredients

- lobster
- water
- salt
- butter
- sage
- chives
- truffles
- white wine
- cullis
- garlic
- olive oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Catch the lobsters, tie them with twine, and place in boiling salted water
2. Cook for half an hour, then remove and let cool
3. Open them, remove the intestine from the tail, and cut into equal fillets
4. Brown in butter with sage, chives, and chopped truffles
5. Add half a quartilho of white wine, half a tablespoon of cullis, a clove of garlic, and a drop of olive oil
6. Let everything simmer little by little
7. When the sauce is reduced and of good flavor, serve hot with lemon juice
