## Ingredients

- a piece of veal
- two stones of salt
- a handful of wild chicory, well washed

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of veal with two stones of salt in the manner described
2. Add a handful of wild chicory, well washed
3. Let it boil little by little
4. When the meat is cooked and the broth is reduced, pass through a sieve
5. Take it hot in the morning and afternoon
