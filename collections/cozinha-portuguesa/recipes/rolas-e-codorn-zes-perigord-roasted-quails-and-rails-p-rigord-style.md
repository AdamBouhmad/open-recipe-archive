## Ingredients

- Roasted quails or rails
- Hen livers
- Scraped bacon
- Fine herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Melted bacon
- Veal slices
- Ham slices
- Seven or eight truffles
- Sausage
- Laurel leaf
- Marjoram
- White wine
- Substance (meat extract)
- Stock (culí)
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. After singeing and emptying, stuff with a mixture of hen livers, scraped bacon, fine herbs, salt, and pepper
2. Stiffen them in melted bacon
3. Place in a pan lined at the bottom with veal and ham slices
4. Arrange the stuffed birds on top, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs, with a whole peeled truffle, a sausage made with laurel leaf and marjoram
5. Cover with slices like those at the bottom
6. When half cooked, add a glass of white wine
7. When done, remove the birds and truffles, add to the remaining broth half a spoon of substance and some stock, reduce
8. When reduced and of good flavor, remove the grease, pass through a sieve, pour over the birds, and serve hot with lemon juice
