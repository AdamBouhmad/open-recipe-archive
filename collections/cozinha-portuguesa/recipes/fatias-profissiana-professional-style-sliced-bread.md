## Ingredients

- bread crumb
- spirits
- eggs
- clarified butter
- sugar
- white wine
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the crumb into slices two fingers thick
2. When cut into rounds the size of an apple
3. Soak in spirits and drain
4. Pass through eggs beaten with only half the whites
5. Fry in hot clarified butter, frying little by little
6. When half fried, pass through eggs again
7. Fry and repeat the same until well covered and browned
8. Remove to a strainer, let drain, and serve with sugar and white wine sauce at point, and powdered cinnamon on top
