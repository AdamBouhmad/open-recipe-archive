## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the peaches in a strainer, making sure they are not small or soft, and very dry
2. Remove the pits with an iron tool that runs in a circle to eject the pit
3. Put it back in the peach once the pit is removed
4. After doing this to all, keep them four days in sugar at esparto point, giving them one boiling each day
5. On the last boiling remove them from the sugar to remove the pits and cover with the same
