## Ingredients

- clarified butter
- flour
- four onions cut in thin fillets
- white wine
- meat stock
- salt
- pepper
- egg whites cut in fillets
- egg yolks
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté in clarified butter with flour four onions cut in thin fillets
2. After sautéing add a glass of white wine and half a spoon of meat stock, season with salt and pepper and leave to simmer little by little
3. Add the egg whites cut in fillets, keeping the yolks whole, and when the sauce is reduced and of good taste, serve it over the yolks with a splash of vinegar or lemon juice
