## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Brandy
- Sugar
- Water
- White wine
- Salt
- Clarified butter

## Directions

1. Pit the apricots and open them in half
2. Marinate them as described above
3. Pass them through a batter made with water, white wine, salt and clarified butter
4. Fry them in very hot oil
5. When fried and with good color, serve dusted with fine sugar
