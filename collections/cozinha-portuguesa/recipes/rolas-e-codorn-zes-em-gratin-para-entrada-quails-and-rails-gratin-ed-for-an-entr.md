## Ingredients

- Quails or rails
- Fat livers
- Fennel (funcho)
- Braise

## Directions

1. After singeing, cleaning, and folding the legs of the codornices or quails, open them somewhat along the sides and fill them with a mixture of fat livers in which fennel (also called funcho) should dominate
2. Put them to cook in a good dry braise
3. When cooked, spread on the bottom
