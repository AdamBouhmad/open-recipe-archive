## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- broth
- Champagne wine or other generous white wine
- reduction
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- clear broth

## Directions

1. After preparing and skinning the tongue, cook it in broth
2. Cut into slices and arrange in a casserole with broth, half a quarter-liter of Champagne wine or other generous white wine, and half a spoonful of reduction
3. Let it boil until little sauce remains
4. Pour half of this sauce into the bottom of the serving dish, sprinkling Parmesan cheese on top
5. Arrange the tongue slices on top of this, pour the remaining sauce over them, and cover everything with grated Parmesan
6. Place to brown in the oven or under the lid of a covered pan
7. When well browned, serve, first pouring a little clear broth over it
