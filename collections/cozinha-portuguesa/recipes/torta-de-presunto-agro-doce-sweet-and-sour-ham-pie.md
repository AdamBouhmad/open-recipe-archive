## Ingredients

- two arratels of ham
- one arratel of sugar
- one cup of white wine
- cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg grated
- flour for pastry dough
- butter for pastry dough

## Directions

1. Cut the ham into small pieces and soak for twenty-four hours
2. Cook in one arratel of sugar at the point of soft-ball stage, with one cup of white wine, cinnamon, and clove all whole, and grated nutmeg, and let cool
3. Make the dough in the form of common pastries
4. Make two crusts; place the ham with some syrup on one crust, and cover with the other
5. Seal the pie well, cook on gentle heat, pour the syrup in which the ham was cooked through two holes made in the middle, and serve hot to the table
