## Ingredients

- 1 spoonful of flour dissolved in milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 ounces of sugar
- salt
- 1 cinnamon stick
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Dissolve a spoonful of flour in milk with 8 egg yolks and 6 ounces of sugar
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Season with salt
4. Add a cinnamon stick and lemon peel
5. Place over heat, stirring constantly until it cooks little by little
6. When thickened, remove the lemon and cinnamon
7. Pour onto a plate
8. Sprinkle with fine sugar
9. Pass a hot spatula over the top
10. Serve as an entremets dish
