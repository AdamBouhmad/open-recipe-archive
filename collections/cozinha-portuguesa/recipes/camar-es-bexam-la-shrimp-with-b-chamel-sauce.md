## Ingredients

- Shrimp tails
- White wine
- Butter
- Fine herbs
- Béchamel sauce
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and blanch the shrimp tails
2. Cook them in an adobo made of white wine, butter, and fine herbs
3. Once cooked, transfer them to a béchamel sauce made in the customary manner and of good taste
4. Place over heat to warm
5. Bind with two or three beaten egg yolks and lemon juice
6. Serve as previously described
