## Ingredients

- two arratels of lean ham
- half arratel of fat
- wine
- vinegar
- one clove of garlic, ground
- cow intestines
- pepper
- cardamom
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop the lean ham and fat very finely
2. Keep in a brine of wine, vinegar, and ground garlic for twelve hours
3. Make salpicões in cow intestines the size of sausages
4. Season well with pepper and cardamom
5. Pass through hot water and hang to dry
6. Fry in butter and send to the table
