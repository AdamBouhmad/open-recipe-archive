## Ingredients

- trout
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- saffron
- garlic
- chives
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and prepare the trout with incisions on the sides
2. Marinate in a quarter quart of good olive oil with salt, pepper, saffron, finely chopped garlic and chives
3. Place on a baking sheet or plate as is customary, with all its marinade
4. Cover with fine breadcrumbs
5. Place to cook in the oven
6. When cooked, serve with anchovy sauce
