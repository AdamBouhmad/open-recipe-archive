## Ingredients

- Alfaces (lettuce)
- Aipos (celery)
- Bréza (braize)
- Óleo de basilica (basil oil)

## Directions

1. Make ragoûts of lettuce or celery the same way as chicory ragoûts
2. Serve for garnishing entrees and side dishes
3. They can also be served stuffed, cooked in braize, and fried with basil
