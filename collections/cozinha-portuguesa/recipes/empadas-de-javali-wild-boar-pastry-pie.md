## Ingredients

- One wild boar leg
- Water
- Salt
- Wine
- Sage
- Marjoram
- Fatback lard, salted and peppered
- Half alqueire of rye or wheat flour
- Pork butter
- Thyme
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Cook one wild boar leg in water and salt
2. Then cook it in wine with sage and marjoram
3. When cooked, lard it with salted and peppered fatback lard very well
4. Knead half alqueire of rye or wheat flour with pork butter and make a large cake that fits the entire leg
5. Add thyme and marjoram, seal the pastry pie, place it in the oven to cook slowly, and send it to the table
