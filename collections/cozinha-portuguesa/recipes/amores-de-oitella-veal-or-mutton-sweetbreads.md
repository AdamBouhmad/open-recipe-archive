## Ingredients

- veal or mutton sweetbreads
- onion slices
- sage sprigs
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- lemon slices
- water
- vinegar
- flour

## Directions

1. Take the quantity of sweetbreads needed
2. Cut into pieces five or six inches long
3. Marinate with onion slices, sage sprigs, marjoram, bay leaf, salt, pepper, lemon slices, half a cup of water, and a little vinegar
4. After marinating two or three hours, place on cloth
5. Dry, coat in flour, fry in hot frying oil
6. Fried and of good color, serve as an entrée
7. Can also be passed through a batter if desired
