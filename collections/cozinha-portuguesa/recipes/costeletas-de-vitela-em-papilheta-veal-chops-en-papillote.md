## Ingredients

- veal rib section
- melted bacon
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- chives

## Directions

1. Take a veal rib section and cut the chops as described above and beat them slightly with the flat of the knife
2. Marinate in melted bacon with salt, pepper, fine herbs, and chives
