## Ingredients

- two or three cans of water
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- half a handful of dried coriander
- two quarts of well pounded almonds, passed through a cloth two or three times
- bread crumb
- bread slices dried in the oven
- biscuits

## Directions

1. Bring two or three cans of water to boil, seasoned with salt, sugar, cinnamon, lemon peel, and half a handful of dried coriander
2. When this water has boiled for three or four minutes, mix in two quarts of well pounded almonds and passed through a cloth two or three times
3. Mix well with a piece of bread
4. Squeeze very well
5. Pour this water into a small pan, place it near the heat, and when hot and of good flavor, moisten some bread slices dried in the oven with it
6. Serve garnished all around with fine biscuits or almond biscuits
