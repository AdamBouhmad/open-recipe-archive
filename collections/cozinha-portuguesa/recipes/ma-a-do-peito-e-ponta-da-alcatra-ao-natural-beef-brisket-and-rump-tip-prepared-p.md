## Ingredients

- A piece of beef brisket or rump tip
- Salt
- One onion studded with cloves
- Various roots or vegetables such as carrots, leeks, parsnips, and celery
- Water

## Directions

1. Soak the meat in water, then wash it in two or three waters
2. Tie it with twine
3. Place it in a pot over heat with sufficient water and scum it very well, adding a little cold water from time to time
4. Once well skimmed, let it boil on one side only, seasoned with salt, one onion studded with cloves, and some roots or vegetables such as carrots, leeks, parsnips, and celery
5. Remove the piece of beef from the pot, trim it, and place it on a plate
6. Serve with sauce poured around it or with the garnishes and sauces that seem best
