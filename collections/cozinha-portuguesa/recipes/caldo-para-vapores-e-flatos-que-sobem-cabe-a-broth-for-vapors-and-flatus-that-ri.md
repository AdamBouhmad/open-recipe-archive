## Ingredients

- one handful each of mallow leaves, betony, mercury plant, artemisia, marsh mallow, and sorrel
- six grains of castor in powder

## Directions

1. Take leaves of mallows, betony, mercury plant, artemisia, marsh mallow, and sorrel, one handful of each, wash all very well and clean them
2. Cut them fine and place to simmer in one canada of water
3. After simmering and when the water is reduced to half, strain through a sieve
4. Divide into two parts and take one in the morning on an empty stomach and another at night
5. In the portion taken in the morning, add six grains of castor in powder
6. Use this broth for the course of fifteen days, purging at the beginning of them, in the middle, and at the end
