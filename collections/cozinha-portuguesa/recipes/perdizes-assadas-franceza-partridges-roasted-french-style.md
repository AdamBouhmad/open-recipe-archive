## Ingredients

- two whole partridges with heads and feet
- melted bacon fat
- two slices of bread
- bread (from pounding)
- sauce from the partridges
- olive oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- toasted bread slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. First roast the two whole partridges with their heads and feet in a frying pan with melted bacon fat
2. Toast two slices of bread and pound them in a mortar
3. Mix with the sauce from the partridges (as one makes mustard)
4. Place this mixture in a cooking vessel with a splash of olive oil and two of vinegar, pepper, clove, and nutmeg
5. Place over hot coals until it comes to a boil
6. Pour over the partridges which should be on a plate over toasted bread slices
7. Add lemon juice on top
8. Send to table
