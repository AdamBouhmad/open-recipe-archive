## Ingredients

- one eighth of flour
- two fresh eggs
- half a pound of butter
- a few grains of salt
- milk
- a bit of yeast

## Directions

1. Take one eighth of flour, two fresh eggs, half a pound of butter, a few grains of salt
2. Knead everything with as much milk as necessary
3. Add to it a bit of yeast
4. When the dough is bound and worked, place it on a sheet of paper
5. Cover it with a warm towel
6. Let it ferment by the fire for five quarters of an hour
7. Then form the cake as usual
8. Place it on buttered paper
9. Put it to cook in the oven
10. When baked, eat it when desired
