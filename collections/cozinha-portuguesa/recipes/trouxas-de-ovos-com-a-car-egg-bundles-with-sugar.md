## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites until stiff like snow
2. Cook on both sides
3. When cooked, place carefully on plate
4. Dust with fine sugar
5. Brown with hot spatula
6. Serve as an entrée
