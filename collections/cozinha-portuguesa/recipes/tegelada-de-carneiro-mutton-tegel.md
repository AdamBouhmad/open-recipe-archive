## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton, chopped
- half arratel of bacon with herbs
- caldo
- vinegar
- all spices
- two eggs
- bread slices
- four eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Chop two arratels of mutton with half arratel of bacon and herbs
2. Braise in a tegel with one spoonful of broth
3. When cooked, season with vinegar and all spices
4. When dry, thicken with two eggs like pastry curds
5. Place the meat in a frying pan over bread slices
6. Over the meat place other slices dipped in four beaten eggs
7. Drizzle remaining egg mixed with lemon juice over the slices
8. When fried golden, serve at table
