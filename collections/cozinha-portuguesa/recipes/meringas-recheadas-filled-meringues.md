## Ingredients

- Dozen egg whites
- Finely chopped lemon zest
- Powdered and sieved sugar

## Directions

1. Beaten with rods and well raised, take a dozen egg whites, add a little finely chopped lemon zest and powdered sugar that is well sieved
2. Mix everything
3. Place in small pieces the size of walnuts on sheets of paper sprinkled with fine sugar
4. Put to cook on the same table, under the lid of a dome-shaped oven with heat on top
5. When cooked and of good color, remove from the middle with a small spoon the white
