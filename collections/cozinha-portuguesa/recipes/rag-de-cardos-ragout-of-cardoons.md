## Ingredients

- Pés de cardos (cardoon stalks)
- Vinagre (vinegar)
- Água fria (cold water)
- Branco com manteiga (white stock with butter)
- Gordura de vacca (beef fat)
- Rodas de limão (lemon slices)
- Essência (essence)
- Manteiga (butter)
- Vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Cut cardoon stalks into equal pieces
2. Boil them in water with a drop of vinegar
3. Place them in cold water
4. Remove the strings
5. Cook them in white stock with butter or beef fat and lemon slices
6. When cooked, drain them
7. Simmer for an instant in good essence
8. Add a small piece of butter and a splash of vinegar
9. Serve for entrees
