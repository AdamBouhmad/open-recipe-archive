## Ingredients

- rabbit
- broth
- white wine
- sage bundle
- fine herbs
- bacon strips
- onions
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- curry

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbits and cut into quarters
2. Cook in a braise with broth, white wine, a bundle of sage, and fine herbs, covered with bacon strips
3. Cut a dozen onions into slices and cook slowly in a pot with a little olive oil
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. When half cooked, move to a stronger flame
6. As they begin to brown, moisten with a little vinegar, broth from the braise, and a little curry
7. Reduce so the onion rings remain whole
8. When the sauce has little liquid and good flavor, remove the oil
9. Take the rabbit quarters from the braise and place on a plate with the onion ragú on top
10. Serve hot for an appetizer
