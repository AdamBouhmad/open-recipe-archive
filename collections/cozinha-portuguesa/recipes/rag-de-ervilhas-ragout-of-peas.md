## Ingredients

- Peas
- Melted salt pork or butter
- Broth or stock
- Parsley bouquet
- Small onion
- Cloves
- Veal cullis
- Ham cullis
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté the peas in melted salt pork or butter
2. After sautéing, add a little broth, a parsley bouquet, and a small onion with two cloves
3. Allow to cook
4. When cooked, remove the fat, bind with veal cullis and ham cullis
5. When it has good flavor, serve as garnishes for first courses with lemon juice
