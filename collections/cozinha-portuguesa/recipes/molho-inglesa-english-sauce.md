## Ingredients

- clear essence
- one chopped anchovy
- fine capers
- two whole small onions
- a piece of fresh butter
- two hard egg yolks, chopped
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put clear essence in a pan with one chopped anchovy, fine capers, two whole small onions, and a piece of fresh butter
2. Place on heat and stir constantly until it is bound
3. Add two hard egg yolks chopped over a cloth and lemon juice
4. This sauce serves for cooked game, roasted, or as it seems fit
