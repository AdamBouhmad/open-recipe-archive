## Ingredients

- two partridges
- two guinea fowls
- four pigeons
- two rabbits
- six quails or doves
- some pieces of beef loin
- four lamb loins
- pork belly lard
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- parsley
- marjoram
- bay leaves
- sheets of beef and veal
- sheets of pork belly
- ragù of truffles, mushrooms, sweetbreads, and combs

## Directions

1. Trim and prepare the following meats and birds
2. Lard them with seasoned pork belly: two partridges, two guinea fowls, four pigeons, two rabbits, six quails or doves, some pieces of beef loin, and four lamb loins
3. Season everything with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
4. Line the bottom of a pot with sheets of pork belly
5. Arrange all the meats on top, interspersed
6. Add a layer of parsley, marjoram, and bay leaves
7. Cover everything with sheets of beaten beef and veal
8. Top with sheets of pork belly
9. Cook slowly and gently between hot coals for five or six hours
10. When cooked, remove the pieces of loin and all the birds
11. Place them in the terrine that is to be served
12. Pour over them a ragù of truffles, mushrooms, sweetbreads, and combs well finished
13. Add to it the braising liquid
14. Serve as an entrance course
