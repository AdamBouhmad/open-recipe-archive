## Ingredients

- One spoonful of Roman sauce
- Good amount of fresh butter
- Flour
- Parsley
- Tarragon
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole in place of culls one spoonful of Roman sauce, add a good amount of fresh butter sprinkled with flour, parsley, and chopped tarragon, put on the fire, and stirring always with a spoon until it is bound and of good taste
2. Serve for chickens, capons, and for everything else that is necessary
