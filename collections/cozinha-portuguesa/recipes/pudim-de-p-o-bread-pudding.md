## Ingredients

- the crumb of three or four small portions of bread
- half a pint of boiling milk
- fifteen egg yolks
- a quarter pound of clarified butter or beef marrow
- lemon or orange peel, chopped
- grated sugar
- some Corinth raisins
- salt
- fine spices
- half a pint of Madeira wine
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Place the bread crumb in a casserole and pour boiling milk over it
2. When soaked, add fifteen egg yolks, a quarter pound of clarified butter or beef marrow, chopped lemon or orange peel, grated sugar, some Corinth raisins
3. Season with salt and fine spices, add half a pint of Madeira wine
4. Mix everything with beaten egg whites
5. Tie in a cloth greased inside with butter and floured
6. Place in a pan with boiling water and continue until cooked
7. Drain, place on its plate, and serve with sauce like the previous puddings
