## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- a little milk
- half a dozen eggs with their whites
- half a pound of melted beef fat
- an equal amount of chopped Alicante raisins
- a drop of brandy
- salt
- a little ground coriander seed

## Directions

1. Break the flour in a little milk, and after being well dissolved, add to it the eggs with their whites, melted beef fat, chopped raisins, brandy, salt, and ground coriander seed
2. After everything is well mixed, pour into a buttered casserole dish
3. Put in the oven to bake for an hour and a half
4. When baked and well coloured, serve hot, dusted with fine sugar
