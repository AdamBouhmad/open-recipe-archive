## Ingredients

- one chicken breast, partially cooked and shredded
- one and a half canada of milk
- two arratels of sugar
- one arratel of rice flour
- half canada of milk (for cooling)
- egg yolks, about a dozen
- flower water

## Directions

1. Once a chicken breast is more than halfway cooked, shred it and break it down with a spoon in a pan
2. Mix with one and a half canada of milk, two arratels of sugar, and one arratel of rice flour
3. Put on to cook; while cooking, add half canada of milk little by little
4. When almost cooked, add soft eggs, about a dozen yolks
5. After cooking, add flower water
6. Remove from heat and serve on a plate
