## Ingredients

- two arratels of very finely ground almonds
- two arratels of sifted stone sugar
- rose water
- two dozen egg yolks
- cinnamon
- flour

## Directions

1. Knead very well two arratels of very finely ground almonds with two arratels of sifted stone sugar and rose water
2. Place in a pot with two dozen egg yolks, stirring constantly so they do not stick, until the paste is firm
3. Remove the pot from the heat and cool over a basin of water
4. Form small balls, place in a basin with flour and cinnamon, and bake well in the oven with cinnamon on top
