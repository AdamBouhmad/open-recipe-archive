## Ingredients

- twelve or fifteen egg yolks
- half of their egg whites
- a spoonful of béchamel sauce well seasoned and finished
- butter
- lemon juice
- pieces of bread crumb fried

## Directions

1. Beat twelve or fifteen egg yolks with half of their egg whites
2. Add a spoonful of well-seasoned and finished béchamel sauce
3. Mix everything
4. Stir in a little melted butter over heat
5. When cooked and slightly soft, serve with lemon juice, garnished with pieces of fried bread crumb
