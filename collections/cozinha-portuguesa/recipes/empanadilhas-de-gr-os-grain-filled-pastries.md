## Ingredients

- one arratel of sugar
- half arratel of finely ground almonds
- grains as needed
- butter or oil for frying
- pastry dough made with butter and sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take one arratel of sugar and half arratel of finely ground almonds and grains as seem necessary
2. Strain and filter the sugar, bring to spun sugar point, remove from heat
3. Add a handful of almonds, then grains, all broken up until all grains and almonds are added
4. The mixture should be thick like fartes
5. Mix all and remove the pan from heat
6. Spread on a plate to cool
7. Take pastry dough made with butter and sugar, knead and stretch, fill with the mixture like making fartes
8. Fry in butter or oil if desired and pass through sugar at point
9. Or bake in oven and pass through sugar at point and cover with cinnamon
