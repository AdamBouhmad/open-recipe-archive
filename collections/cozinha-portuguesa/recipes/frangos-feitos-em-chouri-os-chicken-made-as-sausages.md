## Ingredients

- five or six chickens
- chicken breasts
- pork fat
- milk
- thyme
- bay leaf
- coriander seed
- bread crumb
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- bacon slices
- onion slices
- fine herbs
- cloves
- veal broth
- cullis à Rainha

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean five or six chickens thoroughly and stuff them with a hash made of roasted chicken breasts, pork fat, milk boiled with a little thyme, bay leaf, and dried coriander seed passed through a sieve, to which is added bread crumb, all bound with egg yolks and seasoned with salt and pepper
2. After the chickens are stuffed and sewn closed, blanch them in broth
3. When blanched, dry them and place them to cook in a casserole with bacon slices, onion slices, fine herbs, salt, cloves, and whole pepper, and veal broth, so that they boil and cook slowly
4. When cooked and dry, serve with cullis à Rainha sauce
