## Ingredients

- clarified butter
- a dozen or dozen and a half fresh eggs
- salt
- ground cinnamon
- fine sugar
- pistachios
- almonds in slivers
- orange flower
- six bitter almond biscuits
- all chopped and in powder

## Directions

1. Beat in a casserole with melted and hot clarified butter a dozen or dozen and a half fresh eggs, seasoned with salt, ground cinnamon, fine sugar, pistachios, almonds in slivers, orange flower, six bitter almond biscuits, all chopped and in powder
2. Mix everything well with a spoon
3. Place in the plate in which it is to be served
4. Cook in the same plate with heat from below and a lid with heat on top
5. When cooked, dust with fine sugar
6. Brown with a hot spatula
7. Serve as an entrée
