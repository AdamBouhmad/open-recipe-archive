## Ingredients

- two arratels of lamb, halved
- half arratel of bacon
- aromatics
- seasonings
- half arratel of grated cheese
- bread slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Braise two arratels of lamb halved with half arratel of bacon and aromatics
2. Season with all seasonings
3. When nearly cooked, strain the broth into a hollow plate or frying pan
4. Place over slices of bread covered with half arratel of grated cheese
5. Toast it over some coals
6. Place the lamb on top with lemon juice and take to the table
