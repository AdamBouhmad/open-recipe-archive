## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Butter
- Peel of citron, orange and lemon
- A spoonful of minced parsley
- Salt, sugar and two egg yolks
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach in water, mince it and squeeze it out
2. Sauté it for a moment with butter
3. Place it in a mortar with minced peel of citron, orange and lemon
4. Add a spoonful of minced parsley, salt, sugar and two egg yolks
5. Make the pie with puff pastry dough as is customary
6. When cooked and glazed with sugar and hot, serve between courses
