## Ingredients

- four onions
- lard or melted butter
- substance (meat essence)
- calf meat jelly
- salt
- pepper
- two small spoonfuls of mustard

## Directions

1. Peel four onions, cut them into slices or dice
2. Sauté them in lard or melted butter
3. When beginning to brown, add substance and calf meat jelly
4. Season with salt and pepper, and let them boil gently
5. When cooked, remove the fat, add two small spoonfuls of mustard, and serve for whatever is necessary
