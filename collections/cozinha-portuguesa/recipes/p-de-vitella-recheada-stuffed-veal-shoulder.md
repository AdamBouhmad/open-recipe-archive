## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- ground veal meat
- veal sweetbreads
- truffles
- chicken livers
- ham
- herbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Once the shoulder is boned, remove all the meat from inside and make a ground preparation of it as is customary
2. After washing the shoulder very well, dry it in a clean cloth
3. Put a little of the ground mixture with a salpicon on top, made of veal sweetbreads, truffles, chicken livers, ham, all cut into dice and seasoned with herbs
4. Cover with the rest of the ground meat and tie it all around the shoulder, seal it closed with a needle as if it were a pouch
5. Place it to cook in a braise
6. When cooked, remove the twine and serve with Spanish sauce or any other sauce that seems good
7. You can also put a ragout of squabs or small birds in the middle of the ground mixture instead of the salpicon, or stuff the shoulder with a ground mixture Italian style made with brains, herbs, grated bread, cheese, cream, and egg yolks; finally it can be stuffed with whatever seems best according to the taste of those you serve
