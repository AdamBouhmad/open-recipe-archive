## Ingredients

- one breast of capon
- one breast of chicken or partridge, roasted
- a piece of bread crumb soaked in broth
- one hard egg yolk
- veal broth
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Pound in a stone mortar one breast of capon and one breast of roasted chicken or partridge
2. Add a piece of bread crumb soaked in broth and one hard egg yolk
3. After everything is pounded, put it in an earthenware pot and dissolve with warm veal and chicken broth
4. Pass through a sieve and heat, stirring always with a spoon
5. Before it boils, use it as desired
