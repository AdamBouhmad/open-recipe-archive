## Ingredients

- Capons or chickens
- Chicken livers
- Bacon
- Fine herbs
- String
- Pork butter or melted bacon fat
- Ham pieces
- Bacon sheets
- Paper
- Ham slices
- Spicy sauce
- Cullis
- White wine
- Demi-glace
- Fine herbs
- Garlic cloves
- Studded onion
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stuff inside with a mixture made from their own livers, bacon, and fine herbs, straighten and tie with string
2. Sear in pork butter or melted bacon fat, and after dried and cooled, lard the entire upper part of the breast with small pieces of ham so that only the tips appear like sea urchins
3. Cover with bacon sheets and paper, and after roasted and placed on its plate, serve garnished with ham slices and spicy sauce made with a little cullis, white wine, a demi-glace, fine herbs, garlic cloves, studded onion, passed through a sieve, and lemon juice
