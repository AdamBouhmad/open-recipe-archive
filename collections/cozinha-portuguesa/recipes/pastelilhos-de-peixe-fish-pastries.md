## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Fillets of eels, bream, and hake
- Butter
- Salt, pepper, chives
- Parsley
- Bread cooked in milk
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Line the forms with puff pastry as is customary
2. Put inside a small amount of chopped mixture made with fillets of eels, bream, hake
3. Nourish with sufficient butter
4. Season with salt, pepper, chives, and chopped parsley
5. Bind with bread cooked in milk, some egg yolks, and lemon juice
6. Fill the forms
7. Cover with a small piece of dough
8. Gild, cook in the oven
9. Serve hot
