## Ingredients

- 4 chickens
- 2 arratels of chopped mutton
- seasoning
- bread loaf
- butter
- chicory, lettuce, or cardoon
- 6 egg yolks
- lemon
- broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning four chickens well with their beaks and feet, place them in a pot; when half-cooked, remove them
2. Stuff with two arratels of chopped mutton seasoned as for pastries and a bread loaf; open the chickens along the back
3. Place the stuffed bread in the middle of a buttered plate and arrange the chickens around it, with four portions of chicory, lettuce, or cardoon between them
4. When the plate is ready, place six egg yolks beaten with lemon and one spoonful of broth from the pot on top
5. Brown the plate and send to the table
