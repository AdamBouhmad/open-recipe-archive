## Ingredients

- trout
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- sage
- garlic
- chives
- anchovies
- one tablespoon of cullis
- fresh butter
- lemon juice or bitter orange juice
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and prepare the trout with incisions along the sides
2. Marinate in half a quartilho of good olive oil with salt, pepper, sage, garlic, and chopped chives
3. Place in a baking dish or plate with all its marinade
4. Cover with finely grated bread crumbs
5. Bake in the oven until cooked
6. Make the anchovy sauce with two chopped anchovies, one tablespoon of cullis, and a good piece of fresh butter, then bind it and finish with lemon juice
7. Serve the trout hot with the anchovy sauce underneath
