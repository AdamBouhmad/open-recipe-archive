## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs, beaten
- cream of milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter in a pot, add a few beaten eggs with a little cream of milk, seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Stirring always with a spoon until cooked, serve hot
