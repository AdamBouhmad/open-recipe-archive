## Ingredients

- artichokes
- cold water
- vinegar
- eggs
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- pork lard or beef butter or olive oil

## Directions

1. Trim the artichokes, cut into quarters, remove the hay and first leaves
2. Soak in cold water and a little vinegar, then drain
3. At serving time, put them in a bowl with eggs, flour, salt, pepper, and a dash of vinegar
4. Mix everything very well and fry little by little in pork lard that is not too hot
5. When fried, serve garnished with parsley for an entre-mets course
6. For meatless days, fry in beef butter or olive oil
