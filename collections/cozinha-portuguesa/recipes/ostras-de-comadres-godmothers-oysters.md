## Ingredients

- oysters
- crumb of bread boiled in milk
- sauce
- brains
- chives
- washed butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Blanch the oysters without letting them boil
2. Drain them in a sieve
3. Chop them finely and place in a large stone bowl
4. Pound with crumb of bread boiled in milk, sauce, brains, and chives, all previously chopped
5. Add a piece of washed butter, little salt, pepper, and nutmeg
6. Bind with some egg yolks and fill the same shells with this mixture
7. Cover with fine breadcrumbs and bake in the oven
8. When golden, serve as an entrée
