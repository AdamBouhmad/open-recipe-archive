## Ingredients

- veal tenderloins
- breadcrumbs
- parsley, chopped and ground
- three hard egg yolks
- butter
- French soup

## Directions

1. After the veal tenderloins have been roasted on the spit
2. Make a mixture of breadcrumbs, chopped and ground parsley, and three hard egg yolks
3. Coat the tenderloins with butter first
4. Spread the mixture on them
5. Brown them and place them on French soup
6. Send to the table
