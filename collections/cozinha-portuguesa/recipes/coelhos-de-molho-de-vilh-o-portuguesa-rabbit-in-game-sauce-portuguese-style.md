## Ingredients

- vinegar
- beef substance or broth or water
- garlic cloves
- onion
- lemon
- sage in sprigs
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- rabbit livers
- rabbit

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil in a pot a quart of vinegar with another quart of beef substance or broth or water, two garlic cloves, an onion, and lemon in slices, sage in sprigs, marjoram, a bay leaf, little olive oil, salt, and pepper
2. When well boiled and the sauce reduced to half, bind it with the rabbit livers
3. Strain everything through a sieve
4. Cut the rabbits into quarters after they are roasted
5. Warm them in the sauce without boiling
6. Serve for an appetizer
