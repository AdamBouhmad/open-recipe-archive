## Ingredients

- tart apples
- brandy
- sugar
- lemon peel
- orange flower water
- flour
- pork lard
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Take some tart apples, peel them, cut into quarters, and remove the pits
2. Marinate for two or three hours in brandy with sugar, a piece of lemon peel, and orange flower water
3. Drain in a cloth, sprinkle with flour, and fry in pork lard
4. When fried and well-browned, serve glazed with fine sugar
