## Ingredients

- royal dough
- milk or water
- eggs
- pork butter or clarified beef fat
- sugar in thread

## Directions

1. Make the royal dough with milk or water and break it up with eggs as the preceding one, pass it through a syringe or any other mold that may be, fry in pork butter or clarified beef fat
2. After it is fried and passed through sugar in thread, serve between meals
