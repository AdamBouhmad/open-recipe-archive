## Ingredients

- Plums
- Clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Put plums in boiling water and when soft and almost cooked, pass them to cold water.
2. Remove them, drain them, put them in clarified sugar, place on heat, and without boiling they will stay this way until they turn green.
3. Arrange them on a plate, reduce the sugar to its necessary point, pour on top, and serve like the preceding ones.
