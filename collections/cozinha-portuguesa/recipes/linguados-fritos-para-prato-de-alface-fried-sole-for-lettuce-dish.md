## Ingredients

- sole
- finely ground salt
- flour
- clarified butter or hot oil

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and wash the sole thoroughly
2. Season with finely ground salt
3. Open along the sides with the tip of a knife, from head to tail
4. Dry very well in cloth
5. Coat with flour
6. Fry in clarified butter or very hot oil
7. When fried and golden in color, serve garnished with fried parsley
