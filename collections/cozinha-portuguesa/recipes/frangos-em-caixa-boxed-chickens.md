## Ingredients

- roasted chickens
- truffles
- ham
- fat livers
- veal sweetbreads
- scraped bacon
- fine herbs
- shallots
- salt
- pepper
- good oil
- laurel
- marjoram
- onion slices
- lemon slices
- essence sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Singe and stuff the chickens with a mixture made of truffles, ham, fat livers, veal sweetbreads, scraped bacon, fine herbs, shallots, salt, and pepper
2. Tie them with twine and marinate them in good oil with salt, pepper, laurel, marjoram, onion slices, and lemon slices
3. After they have taken flavor in this marinade, make paper boxes and place each chicken in its box, covered with bacon slices
4. Place them on grills to roast over gentle heat
5. When roasted, drain the fat from them and serve with essence sauce and lemon juice
