## Ingredients

- One eighth measure of lentils, picked and cleaned
- Broth
- A piece of lean bacon
- Two carrots
- One bunch of celery
- A roasted partridge
- Substance made of a partridge and slices of veal

## Directions

1. Pick and clean one eighth measure of lentils and place to cook in a small pan with broth, adding a piece of lean bacon, two carrots, one bunch of celery, all first passed through boiling water and tied with twine to a roasted partridge
2. Let it simmer little by little
3. When the lentils are cooked, pass them through a sieve with substance made of a partridge and slices of veal
4. Then place to boil, skim, and when of good taste, pour over bread crusts boiled and dried as mentioned, and serve with some partridges in the middle
5. From this lentil broth one can make soups of ducks, teals, wild pigeons, and tame pigeons
