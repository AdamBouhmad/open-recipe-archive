## Ingredients

- Turkey breasts with thighs removed
- bacon lardons
- bacon strips
- veal
- ham slices
- bouquet of parsley, bay leaf, marjoram, and clove-studded onion
- water

## Directions

1. Trim the turkey breasts from which the thighs have already been removed, and beat them between two cloths with the back of a knife
2. Remove the skin and lard them very well with minced bacon
3. Remove them from soaking, then blanch them in water
4. Once this is done, put them to cook covered with bacon strips, veal, ham slices, a bouquet of parsley, bay leaf, marjoram, and a clove-studded onion
5. Once cooked, remove the fat from the broth and let it boil until it reaches the consistency of a glaze, pour it over the breasts, and once it has set and has a good color, remove them and serve with chicory sauce à béchemel or with any other vegetable sauce that seems fitting
6. Note however that one must detach what remains stuck to the bottom of the casserole with a single drop of broth
7. They can also be served with clear essence, strained through what remained of the glaze, and with lemon juice
