## Ingredients

- Chicórias (chicory)
- Bréza (braize)
- Molho à bexamela (béchamel sauce)
- Gemas de ovos (egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Blanch the chicory
2. Cook them in a braize
3. Drain them
4. Cut them into equal pieces
5. Let them simmer for an instant in béchamel sauce
6. Thicken with egg yolks
7. Season to taste
8. Serve for various entrees and also for side dishes with poached eggs on top
