## Ingredients

- six slices of ham of the same size and well equal
- water
- lard melted
- white wine
- cullis
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut six slices of ham of the same size and well equal
2. Place two hours to soak in water
3. Dry very well
4. Brown in melted lard on both sides
5. Remove from heat
6. Add to what remained stuck to the bottom of the saucepan a little white wine and a little cullis
7. Give it four boilings
8. Add pepper and a drop of vinegar
9. Remove the fat, pass through a sieve
10. Serves as garnish for first course
