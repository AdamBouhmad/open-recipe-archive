## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Garlic clove
- Shallot
- Laurel
- Marjoram
- Good olive oil
- Butter
- Flour
- Verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. A veal liver cut into very thin slices, is seasoned with salt, pepper, fine herbs, a clove of garlic, and shallot, all finely chopped, laurel, marjoram, and good olive oil; once seasoned, arrange the slices very straight, and curled in a pot, and place them to cook on gentle heat for the space of an hour; when cooked, remove them with a skimmer, strain the olive oil, and pour in its place a bit of well washed butter, pulverized with flour, with verjuice or vinegar, make it bind on the heat, pour it over the liver slices, and serve them very hot
