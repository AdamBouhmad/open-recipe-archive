## Ingredients

- Two or three dozen small onions of equal size
- Water
- Vegetable broth with herbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the small onions in water until half cooked
2. Pass them through cold water and drain them
3. Finish cooking them in well-seasoned and good-tasting vegetable broth with herbs
