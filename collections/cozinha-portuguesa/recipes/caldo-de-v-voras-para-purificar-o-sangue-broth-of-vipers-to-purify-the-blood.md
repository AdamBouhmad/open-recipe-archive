## Ingredients

- one handful each of chicory, pimpinella, chervil, and lettuce
- one chicken
- one viper, skinned alive

## Directions

1. Take one handful each of chicory, pimpinella, chervil, and lettuce, wash them and cut them fine
2. Add to them one chicken and one viper, skinned alive, to which you will remove the head, tail, and entrails, reserving the livers and the heart
3. Place everything to simmer in one canada of water
4. After simmering and when the water is reduced to half, strain through a cloth
5. Divide into two parts and take one every day on an empty stomach
6. This broth can be used for fifteen or twenty days, purging first and at the end of this time
