## Ingredients

- presunto (ham)
- água morna (warm water)
- vinho da Madeira (Madeira wine)

## Directions

1. After the ham is cleaned and the skin is removed, soak it in warm water until the salt is removed
2. Dry it in a cloth
3. Then soak it again in Madeira wine for ten or twelve hours
4. Place it on the spit and roast it, basting from time to time with the same Madeira wine in which it was soaked, so that it drips into the drip pan
5. When finished roasting, remove it and serve it with pepper sauce or any other sauce that seems appropriate
