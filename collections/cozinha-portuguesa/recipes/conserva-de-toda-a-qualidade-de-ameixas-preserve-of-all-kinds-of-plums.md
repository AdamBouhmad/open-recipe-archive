## Ingredients

- a hundred plums
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Put a hundred plums in boiling water, allow them to boil until they become soft, remove them with a skimming spoon, and place them in cold water
2. Drain them afterward through a sieve, arrange them in small vessels with care so they are not bruised, pour over them hot clarified sugar, and allow them to sit until night, when the sugar will drain off
3. The drained sugar is taken to heat again, and boiling, skimmed and hot, is poured over the plums; this is repeated twice a day for four or five days
4. Advising that in the last cooking the sugar must be freshened, giving it a quarter measure of water, and placing it to boil, skimmed and reduced to its necessary point, pour it boiling over the plums
5. In the same manner are made almonds and green apricots as preserve
