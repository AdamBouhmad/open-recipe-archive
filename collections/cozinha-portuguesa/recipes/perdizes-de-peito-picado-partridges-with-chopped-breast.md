## Ingredients

- two partridges
- one chicken breast
- one quarter pound of bacon
- four eggs
- all the seasonings
- vinegar
- lemon
- bacon lard
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts from two plucked partridges
2. Chop the breasts with one chicken breast and one quarter pound of bacon
3. Season in the mortar with four eggs, all the seasonings, vinegar, and lemon
4. Fill the partridges with this mixture, larding them with bacon and egg yolk as done with lamb neck
5. Cook for three hours on gentle heat in a frying pan with one spoonful of broth
6. Place the partridges on a plate and thicken the substance with two egg yolks
7. Pour over the top with prosciutto bits
8. Send to table
