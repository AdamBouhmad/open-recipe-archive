## Ingredients

- young pigeons
- bacon slices
- paper leaves
- chicken livers
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- shallot

## Directions

1. Clean and singe young pigeons and cover them with bacon slices
2. They serve as a roasted dish and for various appetizers on the spit
3. After singing and drawing them, mince the livers with a little bacon seasoned with little salt, pepper, parsley, and shallot
4. Stuff them with this mixture and cover them with a slice of bacon and paper leaves
