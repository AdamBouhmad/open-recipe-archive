## Ingredients

- Quails or rails
- Good olive oil
- Laurel leaves
- Whole marjoram
- Ham slices, thin and square-cut
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic clove
- White wine
- Substance (meat extract)
- Stock (culí)

## Directions

1. After cleaning, open the quails or rails along the sides and marinate them in good olive oil, whole laurel leaves, whole marjoram, thin square-cut ham slices, lemon juice, salt, and pepper
2. A quarter hour before serving, pass them through the fire in a casserole with the same marinade
3. After thus passed, remove them and arrange on their plate
4. To the casserole add a garlic clove, a drop of white wine, substance, and some stock, reduce
5. When reduced and of good flavor, pass through a sieve, pour over the codornices or quails, and serve with lemon juice
