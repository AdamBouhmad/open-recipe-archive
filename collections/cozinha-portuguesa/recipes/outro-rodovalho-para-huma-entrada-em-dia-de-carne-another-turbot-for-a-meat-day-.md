## Ingredients

- one turbot of appropriate size
- pork lard
- bacon strips
- salt
- onion slices
- two or three cloves
- herbs
- ham slices
- Rhine wine or white wine
- Spanish sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Choose a turbot of appropriate size for the plate it is to serve on
2. Clean and trim it, then lard it with bacon; if it is excessively large, remove the loins and lard them
3. Line the bottom of a baking dish with bacon strips
4. Place the fish on top
5. Season with a little salt, onion slices, two or three cloves, herbs, and ham slices
6. Cover with more bacon strips and place in the oven
7. After some time in the oven, pour in a quarter-pot of Rhine or white wine and let it finish cooking slowly
8. When cooked and well-colored, serve with Spanish sauce and lemon juice
