## Ingredients

- cherries
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the cherries and weigh out one arratel and a quarter
2. Place in a pot over fire with boiling water
3. Add the cherries to the water, giving them a good boil for a little while
4. Remove the pot from the fire
