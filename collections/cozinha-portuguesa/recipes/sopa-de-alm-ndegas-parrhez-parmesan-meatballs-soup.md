## Ingredients

- breast of partridge
- breast of hen
- four loins of rabbit
- crumb of bread moistened in milk
- beef suet
- butter
- Parmesan cheese grated
- four egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg grated
- shallots
- veal stock base

## Directions

1. Take the breast of partridge, breast of hen, and four loins of rabbit, all raw, and make a minced mixture
2. Pound it in a mortar and add a small piece of bread crumb moistened in milk but well squeezed
3. Add melted and strained beef suet, a small piece of well-washed butter, and a handful of grated Parmesan cheese
4. Add four egg yolks and season with little salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and some shallots
5. Pound everything very well
6. After pounding, form meatballs the size of partridge eggs
7. Cook them in broth
8. When cooked, remove them with a skimmer and place in a soup bowl
9. Serve with another broth and veal substance
