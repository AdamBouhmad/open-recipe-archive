## Ingredients

- one shallot, very finely minced
- chives, very finely minced
- parsley, very finely minced
- two cloves of garlic
- olive oil
- vinegar
- half a spoonful of cullis
- salt
- pepper
- a bundle of parsley
- bay leaf
- two cloves
- mustard

## Directions

1. Mince very finely one shallot, chives, parsley, two cloves of garlic; pass them in a little bit of olive oil in a pan on the fire; then add to it a little bit of vinegar and half a spoonful of cullis, salt, pepper, a bundle of parsley, bay leaf, two cloves, and let everything simmer for an instant; remove from the fire, remove the bundle, the olive oil, and the bay leaf, add mustard, and tasting good, serve it for roasted meats on the grill and on the spit, as needed.
