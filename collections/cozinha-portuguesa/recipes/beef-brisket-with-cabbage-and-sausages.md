## Ingredients

- eight equal pieces of beef brisket, well trimmed
- broth
- shallot
- two cloves of garlic
- thyme
- one bay leaf
- six or eight quarters of cabbage
- seven or eight whole onions
- salt
- whole pepper
- one dozen whole sausages

## Directions

1. Soak the pieces of beef brisket and then blanch them in boiling water
2. Cook them in broth with a shallot, two cloves of garlic, thyme, and a bay leaf
3. When they are half-cooked, add six or eight quarters of cabbage that have been blanched and pressed, well tied, along with seven or eight whole onions, seasoned with a little salt and whole pepper
4. When everything is almost cooked, add one dozen whole sausages
5. When everything is cooked, remove the meat and vegetables, drain them well on a sieve, and once they are well dried of their fat, place the pieces of beef in the middle of the plate and garnish completely with the cabbage, onions, and the sausages on top
6. Pass the broth through a sieve, remove the fat from it, pour it over, and serve everything well hot
