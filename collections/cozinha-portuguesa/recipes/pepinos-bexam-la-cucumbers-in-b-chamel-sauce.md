## Ingredients

- Three or four cucumbers
- Béchamel sauce
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut into equal pieces three or four cucumbers
2. Blanch and strain them
3. Place in a good-tasting béchamel sauce
4. Let simmer a little, and when cooked, bind with egg yolks
5. Serve for a course between meals
