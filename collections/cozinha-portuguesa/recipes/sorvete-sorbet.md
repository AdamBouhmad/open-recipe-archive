## Ingredients

- seven arrátels of stone sugar
- juice of six lemons
- one cruzado of powdered pearl
- one cruzado of powdered coral
- one cruzado of powdered gold
- six tostões of musk
- twelve tostões of amber
- stone basar as desired

## Directions

1. Place in a pot seven arrátels of stone sugar with the juice of six lemons
2. Beat very well until it reaches good point
3. Add one cruzado of powdered pearl, one cruzado of powdered coral, one cruzado of powdered gold, six tostões of musk, and twelve tostões of amber and stone basar as desired
4. When all this is well beaten pour it into small bowls
