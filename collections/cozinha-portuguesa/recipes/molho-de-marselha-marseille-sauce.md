## Ingredients

- pieces of veal and ham
- melted bacon fat
- one cup of white wine
- two spoons of broth
- two cloves of garlic
- a bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- two cloves
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumb interior soaked in milk
- four egg yolks, pounded
- a little bit of milk, reduced

## Directions

1. Fry for an instant in melted bacon fat pieces of veal and ham; then add to it one cup of white wine, two spoons of broth, two cloves of garlic, a bundle of parsley and fine herbs, two cloves, salt, pepper, and let it simmer little by little; when cooked, pass it through a sieve with breadcrumb interior soaked in milk, four pounded egg yolks, and a little bit of reduced milk; and having heated it, and without letting it boil, it will serve for chickens, little squabs, and for everything else that is necessary.
