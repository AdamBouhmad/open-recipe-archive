## Ingredients

- um presunto bom (a good ham)
- pranchas de vitela (veal slices)
- toucinho (bacon)
- salsa (parsley)
- hervas finas (fine herbs)
- cebola cravejada (clove-studded onion)
- raizes (roots)
- uma cabeça de alhos (one head of garlic)
- ragú de espinafres à crema (spinach ragout with cream)
- caldo (broth)

## Directions

1. Take a good ham, trim it, remove the bones from the inside through the bottom
2. Soak it for two or three days, and after removing the salt, put it to boil for half an hour in water
3. After it is set, line the bottom of a pan with slices of veal and bacon, a layer of parsley, fine herbs, a clove-studded onion, some roots, and one head of garlic
4. Cover it with other slices of veal and bacon, and put it to cook gently between two fires for ten or twelve hours
5. When cooked, serve it with spinach ragout with cream well made
6. Alternatively, after removing the ham, the veal slices can be moistened with a spoonful of broth, and after boiling a little, this broth can be strained through a sieve, the fat removed, and reduced to a glaze consistency; and when the ham is browned in it, serve it with the same sauce or with vegetable ragout, whichever seems better
