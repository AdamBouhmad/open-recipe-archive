## Ingredients

- carrots
- flour powder
- butter
- parsley
- cilantro
- parsley
- onion
- oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the carrots, then cut them in quarters and pass them through flour powder
2. Place them to fry, arrange them in a frying pan and pour a potage on top made by sautéing in cow butter a small amount of parsley, cilantro, parsley, onion, and oregano, seasoning with vinegar
