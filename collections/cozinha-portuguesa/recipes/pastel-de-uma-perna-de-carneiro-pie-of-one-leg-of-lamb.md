## Ingredients

- One leg of lamb
- Half an arratel of bacon
- Two ounces of butter
- Two onions
- A splash of vinegar
- Whole seasonings
- A bundle of all aromatics
- Water
- About half a dozen beaten eggs
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put into a pot one leg of lamb, half an arratel of bacon, two ounces of butter, two onions, a splash of vinegar, whole seasonings, and a bundle of all aromatics.
2. Cook in little water until the lamb is more than half cooked.
3. Remove it and mince all the lamb separately, and likewise mince the aromatics separately.
4. In a shallow bowl buttered, layer lamb, layer of bacon, and layer of aromatics until all is finished.
5. Pour about half a dozen beaten eggs over the top and put it to brown on a low flame.
6. When done, make some sops of the said substance outside and after they are well soaked, turn the bowl onto the plate, and break the bowl so that the sop remains whole.
7. Place the pie on top, pour lemon juice over it, and send it to the table.
8. This can also be made with loins and veal, or whatever meat you wish.
