## Ingredients

- vinegar
- water
- olive oil
- salt
- bay leaves
- lemon juice
- lime juice
- ginger, pounded
- black spices

## Directions

1. Boil a little vinegar diluted with water in a pan
2. Add olive oil, salt, and bay leaves
3. Add lemon juice, lime juice, and pounded ginger with the other black spices
4. When boiling, adjust seasonings by tasting to ensure the escabeche is not too strong
5. Adjust the quantity according to the amount of fish it will serve
