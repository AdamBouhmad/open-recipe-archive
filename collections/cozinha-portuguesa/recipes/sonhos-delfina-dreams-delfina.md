## Ingredients

- sugar
- flour
- orange blossom
- a piece of lemon peel
- egg whites
- clarified butter
- grated sugar
- hot spatula

## Directions

1. Make a dough, half of sugar, half flour, orange blossom, a piece of lemon peel, and egg whites
2. After everything is kneaded and worked, extend it with bread, cut it, and make into equal sketches, and as you please, fry in clarified butter
3. And after they are fried serve glazed with grated sugar and a hot spatula
