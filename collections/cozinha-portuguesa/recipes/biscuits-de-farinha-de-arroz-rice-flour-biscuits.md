## Ingredients

- Four ounces of rice flour, sifted through fine silk sieve
- One arratél of fine sugar
- Eight egg yolks
- A little finely chopped lime peel
- Fifteen or sixteen egg whites, beaten and well raised

## Directions

1. Sift through a very fine silk sieve four ounces of rice flour, put it in a bowl with one arratél of fine sugar, eight egg yolks, a little finely chopped lime peel
2. Beat everything with two spoons for the time of a quarter hour
3. Carefully mix fifteen or sixteen well-beaten and raised egg whites
4. Form afterwards some paper boxes greased with butter inside, put the composition in them
5. Put them to bake in a very mild oven
6. When cooked, remove them from the boxes, cover them with white glaze made with one egg white, fine sugar and some drops of lemon juice
7. Put them in the oven again and when the glaze is dry, remove them and serve them when it is necessary
