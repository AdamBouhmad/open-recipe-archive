## Ingredients

- one ounce of peas
- butter or melted lard
- lettuce
- chervil
- sorrel
- three or four small onions
- a little broth
- substance of veal

## Directions

1. Cook one ounce of peas with butter or melted lard
2. Combine with lettuce, chervil, sorrel, and three or four small onions, all finely chopped
3. Add a little broth and veal substance
4. Let it boil little by little
5. When everything is cooked and of good taste, remove the fat
