## Ingredients

- truffles
- a slice of ham
- melted lard
- broth
- half cup of white wine
- one head of garlic
- one onion with two cloves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cut truffles into thin slices
2. Brown with a slice of ham and melted lard
3. Then add broth, half cup of white wine, one head of garlic, and one onion with two cloves and pepper
4. Let it boil slowly and gently
5. When cooked and without fat, add one spoonful of cullis
6. Serves as garnish for any first course
