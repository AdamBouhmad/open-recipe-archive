## Ingredients

- one arratel of clean sugar
- fifteen eggs
- three quarters of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Take one arratel of clean sugar and place in a pot, add fifteen eggs on top and beat with a beater or spoon very well until thick
2. Once thick, check if the oven is prepared, then add three quarters of sifted flour and beat very well so the flour does not settle at the bottom
3. Once well beaten, pour into a basin or baking vessel, beating constantly at the bottom until it goes into the oven so the flour does not settle
