## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- eight whole eggs
- three quarters of fine sugar
- one pound of butter
- lime peel
- candied orange peel, chopped
- water

## Directions

1. After everything is mixed and kneaded, stretch it so it is the thickness of a thumb
2. Place on buttered paper
3. Bake in the oven
4. After baking, serve glazed with sugar and a hot spatula
