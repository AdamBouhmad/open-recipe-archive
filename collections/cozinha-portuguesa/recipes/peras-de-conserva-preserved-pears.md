## Ingredients

- pears
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select pears that are not very ripe
2. Pierce them with a borer in a cross pattern
3. Place them to cook in a pan of boiling water
4. Once cooked, remove with a skimming spoon and clean the bottoms by placing in cold water
5. Wash them in four waters
6. Place them in sugar at hair point in a quantity that covers the pears
7. Give them nine boilings over nine days for this preserve to thicken
8. If you wish to coat the pears, remove them from the preserve and place to drain on a sieve
9. Once drained, place them in boiling sugar until it reaches pearl point
10. Remove the pan from the fire and place on the ground
11. Remove the pears with small tongs and spoon sugar over them with a spoon
12. Place on a sieve or grill, wrap in paper, and do not place in a humid location because they will spoil
