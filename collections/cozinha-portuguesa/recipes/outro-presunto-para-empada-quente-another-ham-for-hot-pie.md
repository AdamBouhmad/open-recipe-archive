## Ingredients

- presunto (ham)
- pranchas de vacca (beef slices)
- vitela (veal)
- hervas finas (fine herbs)
- raizes (roots)
- cebola cravejada (clove-studded onion)
- um quartilho de vinho branco (a quarter of white wine)
- toucinho pizado (pounded bacon)
- manteiga lavada (washed butter)
- pranchas de toucinho (bacon slices)

## Directions

1. After the ham is roasted, trimmed, and without salt, put it to cook in a braise with slices of beef, veal, fine herbs, roots, clove-studded onion, and a quarter of white wine
2. When almost cooked, remove it and let it cool
3. Meanwhile, line the bottom of the pie with pounded bacon or washed butter
4. Place the ham inside with the same braise sauce and covered with bacon slices, close the pie with the dough, and put it in the oven to cook for three or four hours
5. After it is cooked, drain the fat and serve it with sauce like the preceding one
