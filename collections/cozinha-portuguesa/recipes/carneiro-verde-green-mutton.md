## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton, cut in pieces
- one quart of bacon
- vinegar
- herbs
- all spices
- parsley sauce with cilantro
- butter
- bread slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut two arratels of mutton into pieces
2. Braise with one quart of bacon, a splash of vinegar, and herbs
3. When cooked, season with all spices
4. Make a small parsley sauce with cilantro, minced
5. Spread a buttered plate with bread slices
6. Put the mutton on top with lemon juice
7. Pour the sauce on the mutton so it becomes green when sending to table
