## Ingredients

- bread
- oil, hot
- anchovy fillets
- olive oil
- coarse pepper
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Remove the crusts from bread and cut into equal slices
2. Fry in hot oil
3. When golden, drain in a sieve
4. Once drained, arrange anchovy fillets on top
5. Serve with oil, coarse pepper, and sour orange juice
