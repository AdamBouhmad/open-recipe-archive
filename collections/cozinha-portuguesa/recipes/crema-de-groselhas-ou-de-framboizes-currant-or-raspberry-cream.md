## Ingredients

- currants or raspberries
- sugar
- milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the currants in a saucepan with sugar and crush them with a spoon
2. Let them boil a little
3. Then remove them and pour into milk already boiled and seasoned as in the strawberry one, and its egg yolks
4. Strain through a napkin
5. Pour into a plate or Indian cups
6. Cook as stated and serve as the others
