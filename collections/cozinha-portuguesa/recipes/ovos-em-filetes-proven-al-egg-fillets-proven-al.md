## Ingredients

- four or five onions cut in thin slices
- olive oil
- flour
- broth or hot water
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- one dozen hard eggs cut in fillets
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cut four or five onions in thin slices, sauté in olive oil dusted with flour
2. After sautéing add half a spoon of broth or hot water, season with salt, pepper, and a bay leaf
3. Add one dozen hard eggs cut in fillets, and when the sauce is reduced and of good taste, thicken with egg yolks mixed in a little good vinegar and chopped parsley
4. Serve as an entrée like the above
