## Ingredients

- veal kidney with its fat
- butter
- half a quarter of milk
- chopped lemon peel
- a dozen egg yolks
- fine sugar
- salt
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Roast with all its fat a veal kidney as described above
2. Sauté in cow butter
3. When nearly cold, add half a quarter of milk, chopped lemon peel, a dozen egg yolks, fine sugar, and salt
4. When everything is well mixed, add the beaten egg whites to snow peaks
5. Cook on both sides
6. When cooked, place carefully on its plate
7. Dust with fine sugar, brown with a hot spatula, and serve as an entrée
