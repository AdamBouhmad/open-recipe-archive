## Ingredients

- flounders
- butter
- onion slices
- finely chopped forcemeat à crème
- clarified butter
- egg yolks
- sour orange juice or lime juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the flounders as stated, cut off the heads and tips of the tails
2. Open them through the middle
3. Remove the backbone
4. Place in a baking dish with butter and onion slices underneath
5. Stuff with a finely chopped and well-bound forcemeat à crème
6. Brush with clarified butter and egg yolks, all mixed together
7. Place in the oven or under the cover of a baking dish to cook
8. When cooked and well-colored, serve with anchovy sauce and cullis, and with sour orange juice
