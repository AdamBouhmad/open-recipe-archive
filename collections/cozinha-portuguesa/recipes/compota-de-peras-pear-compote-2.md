## Ingredients

- Whole pears
- Clarified sugar
- A slice of lemon

## Directions

1. Take whole pears and put them in boiling water until softened and almost cooked.
2. Transfer to cold water and peel them, keeping them whole or cutting them in half or quarters.
3. Place in another water.
4. Cook in clarified sugar with a slice of lemon to keep them whiter.
5. When cooked, arrange on a plate, reduce the sugar to the necessary point, pour over the top, and serve hot or cold as preferred.
