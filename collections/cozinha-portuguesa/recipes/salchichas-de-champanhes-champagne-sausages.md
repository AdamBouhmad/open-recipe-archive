## Ingredients

- Meat from a pork leg or loin
- An equal amount of fat, cut into dice
- Salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
- Half a bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Pound the meat from a pork leg or loin
2. Cut an equal amount of fat into dice
3. Season with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
4. Add half a bottle of white wine
5. Mix everything well and let it marinate
6. Stuff the intestines as is customary
7. Let them smoke for a couple of days
8. Once dried, serve them as desired
