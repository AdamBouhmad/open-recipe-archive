## Ingredients

- Porco montez (wild boar)
- Toucinho grosso (thick bacon)
- Sal (salt)
- Pimenta (pepper)
- Especias finas (fine spices)
- Vinagre (vinegar)
- Alhos (garlic)
- Hervas finas (fine herbs)
- Molho picante (spicy sauce)

## Directions

1. Skin the boar and quarter it
2. Lard the hind quarters with thick bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine spices
3. Marinate in salt, pepper, vinegar, garlic, and all types of fine herbs
4. After marinating, roast on a spit and serve with spicy sauce
5. Alternatively, they may be served cooked in an adobo or as stewed meat
