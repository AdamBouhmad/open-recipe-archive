## Ingredients

- Salmon, in steaks two fingers thick
- Bacon, sliced
- Veal, sliced
- Ham, sliced
- Roots
- Onions
- Garlic, a few cloves
- Fine herbs
- Salt
- pepper
- Cloves
- White wine
- Butter
- Cullis

## Directions

1. Cut the steaks two fingers thick and arrange in a proportioned pot or casserole with slices of bacon, veal, ham, roots, onions, a few cloves of garlic, fine herbs, salt, pepper, and cloves
2. Add a little white wine and braise very well between two fires of coals
3. When cooked, serve with broth from the bottom of the coals, bound with a small piece of washed butter and a little cullis
