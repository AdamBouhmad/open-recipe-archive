## Ingredients

- Veal chops
- Melted lard
- Lard
- Fine herbs
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard for frying
- Gravy

## Directions

1. Marinated and prepared chops, pass them through the fire with melted lard, wrap each one in its papillote garnished with chopped lard, and seasoned with fine herbs, so that the chops remain in the middle; and placed to roast as the preceding, serve them also in the same manner
2. Sometimes a small slice of lard can be placed under each one, with a little chopped on each side
3. Also after being cooked in a braise, and when cold, they can be covered with chopped well bound, passed twice through beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs; and then fried in pork lard, served with fried gravy all around
4. They can also be made of chopped only with a pinch in the middle, applying the clean chop bone, and passing them twice through beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs, and browned in the oven with slices of lard underneath; and when they have good color, serve them with sauce of ham essence
