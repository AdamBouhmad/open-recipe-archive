## Ingredients

- all the meat of a fish
- other good fish
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- a little dry coriander pounded
- false pepper
- onion
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- egg yolks
- sheets of buttered paper
- ragù of sole loins and shrimp tails
- cullis of shrimp or other sauce

## Directions

1. Take all the meat of a fish, of other good fish
2. Season with salt, pepper, spices, and a little dry coriander pounded, false pepper, and onion
3. Mince it very well on a board
4. Then pound it with bread crumb soaked in milk and bind it with egg yolks
5. Line a forcemeat mold with sheets of buttered paper
6. Place the forcemeat at the bottom and around
7. Put in the middle a ragù of sole loins and shrimp tails
8. Cover with the rest of the same forcemeat and on top a sheet of buttered paper
9. Put to cook in the oven
10. When cooked, remove the paper, turn it out onto the plate, remove the other paper
11. Serve with cullis of shrimp or with any other sauce that seems good
