## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Fine lard
- Thick lard
- Peverada sauce

## Directions

1. Commonly the veal livers are eaten roasted on a spit, being first larded with fine lard, and served with peverada sauce underneath
2. Also they can be larded with thick lard, and cooked in a braise; and after being cooked serve them with the same sauce, or with any other that seems good
