## Ingredients

- fruit (strawberries, currants, blackberries, or raspberries)
- two arratels of sugar per arratel of fruit

## Directions

1. For each arratel of fruit such as strawberries, currants, blackberries, or raspberries, reduce two arratels of sugar to the cracking point
2. When at this stage, add the fruit and give it one boil
3. Remove from heat and pour into a sieve
4. Strain the fruit through it and when the syrup is cold, pour into bottles
5. Cork and use this syrup to make drinks, chilled or otherwise
