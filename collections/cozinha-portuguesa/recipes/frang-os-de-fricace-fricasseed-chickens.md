## Ingredients

- bread, onion, parsley, and bacon finely chopped
- butter
- water
- chickens (or hens or squabs)
- saffron
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- bread crumbs
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put finely chopped bread, onion, parsley, and bacon in a pot with butter and enough water for what you wish to make, and bring to a boil
2. Meanwhile, wash the chickens well and keep them in water
3. Once the onion, parsley, and bacon mixture is well cooked, add the cut chickens
4. When cooked with the seasonings (saffron, little clove, pepper, cinnamon, and vinegar), add grated bread crumbs to thicken the sauce
5. When the bread appears to be cooked, beat egg yolks in a bowl and add them, stirring constantly so they do not curdle
6. Place on slices and serve with lemon juice on top
