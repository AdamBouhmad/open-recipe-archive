## Ingredients

- young hare
- lard slices
- salt
- pepper
- twine

## Directions

1. Skin the young hare in fresh water, removing only the down
2. After cleaning and washing it well, stuff it between the skin and the meat with some slices of lard seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Tie it well with twine and place on the spit to braise
4. Once roasted, remove the twine and send to the table
