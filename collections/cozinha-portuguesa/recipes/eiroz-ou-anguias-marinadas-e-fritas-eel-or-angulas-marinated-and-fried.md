## Ingredients

- eels
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- onions
- lemon juice
- flour
- clarified butter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the eels as in preceding recipes
2. Cut into equal pieces
3. Marinate with vinegar, salt, pepper, bay leaf, onions, and lemon juice
4. After marinating two or three hours, drain
5. Flour and fry in clarified butter
6. Serve garnished with fried parsley around it
