## Ingredients

- milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- pistachio nuts

## Directions

1. Heat and season half a handful of milk with salt, sugar, cinnamon, and a bit of lemon zest
2. Let it cool almost completely
3. Add to it a few well-ground pistachios
4. Pass forcefully twice through a napkin
5. Pour afterwards into the serving plate
6. Cook as above and serve garnished with pistachios around the edge
