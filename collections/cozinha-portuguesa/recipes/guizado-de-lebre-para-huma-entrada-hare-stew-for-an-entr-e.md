## Ingredients

- hare
- bacon planks
- broth
- quarter measure of wine
- salt
- pepper
- bundle of parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- onion studded with cloves
- head of garlic
- raw liver
- broth
- blood
- fine capers

## Directions

1. Prepare the hare as in the preceding recipe and set the blood aside, cut it into pieces
2. Put it to simmer in a small pan with bacon planks, a measure of broth, a quarter measure of wine, salt, pepper, a bundle of parsley, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, an onion studded with cloves, and a head of garlic
3. After it is well cooked, remove the bacon planks, the bundle, the onion, and the garlic
4. Pound the raw liver and pass it with broth through a sieve, add it with the blood
5. Put the hare on the fire with little sauce, bind it gradually without letting it boil
6. Serve with fine capers on top
