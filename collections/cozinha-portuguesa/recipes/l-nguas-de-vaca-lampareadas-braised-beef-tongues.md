## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- bacon
- wine
- vinegar
- seasonings
- toasted flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. When a tongue is cooked and cut into slices, place it to braise in a pot with half an arratel of melted bacon, cleaned of scraps
2. Add a splash of wine and another of vinegar, and all the seasonings
3. After braising, add a little toasted flour to make the sauce thick and brown, and sugar if desired
