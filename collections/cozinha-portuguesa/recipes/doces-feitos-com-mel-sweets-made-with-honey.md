## Ingredients

- honey
- egg

## Directions

1. Place honey in a pot over heat
2. Once it begins to boil, remove all the foam and allow it to cook very well
3. This will be known by removing it from the heat and placing inside it a hen's egg; if this egg goes to the bottom the cooking is imperfect; if the egg stays on top, the cooking is perfect
4. Add any type of fruit, place it over heat, allow it to boil slowly and carefully so it does not burn
5. Honey serves in place of sugar
