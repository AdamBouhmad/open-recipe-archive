## Ingredients

- turkey thighs
- thick bacon
- milk
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- allspice grains
- coriander seeds dried and crushed
- rosemary
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion
- garlic

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey thighs and lard them with thick bacon
2. Put them to cook in an adobo made with half a Canadian measure of milk, a piece of butter mixed with flour, seasoned with salt, pepper, two grains of allspice, some crushed dried coriander seeds, rosemary, bay leaf, marjoram, parsley, onion, and two cloves of garlic
3. Let them cook slowly
4. When cooked, remove them, let them drain, then pass them through the fat of the same adobo
5. Cover them with fine grated bread, sprinkle them with butter, and roast them on the grill
6. When roasted, serve with clear essence, verjuice juice, salt, pepper, or another sauce that suits best
7. The same thighs of turkey after roasting can also be served passed through the grill with Robert sauce
