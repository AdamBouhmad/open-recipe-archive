## Ingredients

- Two sweetbreads
- Truffles
- Cucumber
- Chives
- Parsley
- Olive oil
- White wine, one cup
- Two cloves of garlic
- Pepper
- Half a bay leaf
- Two lemon slices
- Veal culls
- Pork culls
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Finely chop two sweetbreads, truffles, cucumber, chives, and parsley, and sauté everything in olive oil
2. When sautéed, add one cup of white wine, two cloves of garlic, pepper, half a bay leaf, and two lemon slices
3. While cooking, add one spoonful of veal culls and pork culls; when it has good taste, remove the fat, strain through a sieve, and serve with lemon juice in everything that is necessary
