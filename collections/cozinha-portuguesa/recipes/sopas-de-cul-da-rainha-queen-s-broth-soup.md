## Ingredients

- One handful of sweet almonds
- Breast of a roasted capon
- Four hard egg yolks
- A piece of bread soaked in milk
- Broth of veal and ham

## Directions

1. Pound in a stone mortar, after shelling, one handful of sweet almonds
2. When pounded, add the breast of a roasted capon, chopped, four hard egg yolks, a piece of bread soaked in milk, and continue pounding everything very well
3. When pounded, pass to a casserole, dissolve with broth of veal and ham
4. Place to heat, and without boiling, pass through fine cloth, heat again, and when hot pour over bread crusts boiled in broth, with their chickens in the middle
5. This broth serves for all types of white soups, and also for various entries, noting that when for soups, it should be more liquid and less strong
