## Ingredients

- One arratel of rice
- One pint of milk
- One arratel of sugar
- Flower water
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak one arratel of rice in water.
2. Cook it with one pint of milk and one arratel of sugar and flower water.
3. When it is cooked, send to the table with cinnamon on top.
