## Ingredients

- sweetbreads cut in pieces
- ham cut in pieces
- mushroom caps cut in pieces
- artichoke hearts cut in pieces
- cucumber pieces
- flour
- butter
- substance
- white wine
- a bunch of parsley
- fresh herbs
- one clove of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- liver cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces sweetbreads, ham, mushroom caps, artichoke hearts, and cucumbers
2. Dust with flour and brown in butter
3. After browning everything, add a little substance and white wine, a bunch of parsley and fresh herbs, one clove of garlic, salt, and pepper
4. Let it cook
5. When cooked, add some livers cut into pieces and blanched
6. When the sauce is reduced, remove the fat
7. Serves for everything
