## Ingredients

- veal slices
- ham slices
- parsley sauce
- fine herbs
- bacon rendered
- turkey wings
- white wine
- cullis
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a casserole with slices of veal and some slices of ham, a bed of parsley sauce, fine herbs, and rendered bacon
2. Place on top a dozen well-trimmed turkey wings
3. Put them to cook on gentle heat moistened with a little white wine
4. When cooked, remove them and add to what remains in the bottom of the casserole a spoonful of cullis and put to boil
5. Once reduced, pass this sauce through a sieve, remove the fat, and put a little in the bottom of the serving plate with a little grated cheese
6. Arrange the wings on top, pour the rest of the sauce over them, cover with grated cheese
7. Put it to color in the oven or over a stove, color it on top with a hot spatula, and serve immediately for an entrée
