## Ingredients

- one round loaf of bread
- chopped partridge breasts
- good essence sauce
- lemon zest
- milk
- flour
- pork butter
- larded and glazed gizzards
- sliced ham
- essence sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the top crust of one round prepared loaf of bread
2. Stuff it with chopped partridge breasts placed in a good well-bound essence sauce
3. Place under it the piece of crust that was removed
4. Tie it in a cross with string
5. Dip it in milk for a few moments
6. Dust it with flour
7. Fry it in pork butter
8. After fried and well colored, remove the string
9. Place it on its dish
10. Garnish it with larded and glazed gizzards and sliced ham
11. Serve it with essence sauce of good taste
