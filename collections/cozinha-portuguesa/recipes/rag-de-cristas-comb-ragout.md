## Ingredients

- two dozen combs
- white broth
- ham essence
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch and skin two dozen combs
2. Place to cook in white broth
3. When cooked, place in ham essence
4. Give it four boilings in it
5. Remove it
6. Add the juice of one lemon
7. Serve as the preceding
