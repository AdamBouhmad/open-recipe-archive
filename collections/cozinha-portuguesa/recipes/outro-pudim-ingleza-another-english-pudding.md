## Ingredients

- one pound of chopped beef fat
- strong cream made of rice flour or wheat flour
- eight egg yolks
- lemon peel
- preserved orange blossom
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg flower
- sugar
- a drop of brandy
- butter
- butter sauce
- wine of the Kingdom
- sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of chopped beef fat with a strong cream made of rice flour or wheat flour, eight egg yolks, lemon peel, preserved orange blossom, seasoned with salt, cinnamon, nutmeg flower, sugar, and a drop of brandy
2. After everything is incorporated, put it in a pot with the bottom lined inside with butter, and bake in an oven for the space of two hours
3. Remove it, turn it over onto its plate, coat with sugar and a hot spatula, and serve with butter sauce, wine of the Kingdom, sugar, and lemon peel
