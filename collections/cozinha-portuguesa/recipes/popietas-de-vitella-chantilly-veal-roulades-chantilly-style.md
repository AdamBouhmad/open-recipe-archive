## Ingredients

- tender pieces from the leg of veal
- good oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- truffles
- cucumbers
- lemon juice
- shallot sauce

## Directions

1. Obtain the tenderest pieces from the leg of veal as if for roulades, and after making them very equal, beat them between two cloths with the back of a knife.
2. Marinate them in a little good oil, salt, pepper, parsley, chives, truffles, cucumbers, all finely chopped, and a little lemon juice.
3. After they take the taste of the marinade, roll them as roulades, thread them on a small spit, tie them on a spit, and put them to roast.
4. Once roasted, serve them with shallot sauce of good taste.
