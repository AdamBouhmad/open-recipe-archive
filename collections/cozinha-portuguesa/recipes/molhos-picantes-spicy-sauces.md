## Ingredients

- Half cup of vinegar
- Two cloves of garlic
- Onion rings
- Dried marjoram
- One bay leaf
- Whole parsley
- Two lemon slices without skin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Substance
- Half culls

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole half a cup of vinegar, two cloves of garlic, onion rings, dried marjoram, one bay leaf, whole parsley, two lemon slices without skin, salt, and pepper, and bring to a boil
2. When boiling for an instant, add one spoonful of substance and half culls, and let it cook little by little
3. When reduced and of good taste, strain through a sieve, and serve for hares, venison, and all the wild game you wish
