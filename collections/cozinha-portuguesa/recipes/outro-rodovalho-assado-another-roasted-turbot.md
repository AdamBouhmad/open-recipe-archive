## Ingredients

- turbot
- broth
- white wine
- butter
- onion slices
- salt
- whole pepper
- cloves
- fine herbs
- four egg yolks
- clarified butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the turbot, either in portions or whole
2. Cook it in an adobo made with broth, white wine, a piece of butter, onion slices, salt, whole pepper, cloves, fine herbs, letting it simmer slowly
3. When cooked, place it in a baking dish with butter on the bottom
4. Dissolve four egg yolks in clarified butter and a little heat, pour this over the fish
5. Cover with finely grated bread crumbs and put in the oven to brown, or brown it under a cover, and serve afterward with one of the preceding sauces
