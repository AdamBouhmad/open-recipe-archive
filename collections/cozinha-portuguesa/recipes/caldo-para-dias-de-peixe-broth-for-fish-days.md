## Ingredients

- onions
- carrots
- parsnips
- butter
- clear pea broth
- fish heads, tails and other fragments
- salt
- parsley made into a sauce
- chives
- fine herbs
- some cloves of the Indies

## Directions

1. Line the bottom and sides of a pot with sliced onions, carrots, and parsnips
2. Add a piece of butter and put on heat to sweat slowly
3. When it begins to brown, add a little clear pea broth
4. When it comes to a boil, add fish heads, tails and other fragments judged most convenient
5. Season with salt
6. Add parsley made into a sauce, chives, fine herbs, and some cloves of the Indies
7. Let boil for about one hour
8. Pass this broth through a sieve and use it to moisten various soups
