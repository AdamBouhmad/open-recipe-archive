## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- onions
- butter
- milk
- egg yolks
- caul fat
- bread crumbs
- essence
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the chops and cover them with their glaze as described above
2. Cover them with a ragout of onions cut into slices
3. Pass the chops through butter and cook them in milk thickened with egg yolks
4. Wrap them in a piece of caul fat and pass them through beaten eggs
5. Place them in a baking dish covered with bread crumbs
6. Cook them in the oven
7. When cooked and well-colored, serve with clear essence sauce as in the preceding recipes
