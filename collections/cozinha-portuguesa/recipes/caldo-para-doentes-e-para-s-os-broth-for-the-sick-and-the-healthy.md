## Ingredients

- two measures of veal meat
- one hen
- water
- salt
- one onion
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Put two measures of veal meat and one hen in an earthenware pot
2. Add one and a half measures of water
3. Skim when appropriate
4. Season with salt
5. Add one onion with two cloves
6. Let boil slowly
7. When the meat and hen are cooked and the broth is reduced to half, pass through a sieve
8. Remove the fat and use as needed
