## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- one arratel of sugar
- one vintém of bread
- quarter of washed butter
- cinnamon
- flower water

## Directions

1. Make in the same manner as the Soup of Any Kind of Roast
2. Uses twelve egg yolks, one arratel of sugar, one vintém of bread, quarter of washed butter, cinnamon, and flower water
