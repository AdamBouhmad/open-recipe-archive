## Ingredients

- Two or three ounces of citron or lemon peel
- A little orange flower in powder, covered
- Two small spoonfuls of marmalade, of apricots or any other
- Four ounces of fine sugar
- Four or five egg yolks
- Egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Grind in a stone mortar two or three ounces of citron or lemon peel, add a little orange flower powder covered, two small spoonfuls of marmalade of apricots or any other, four ounces of fine sugar, four or five egg yolks
2. After everything is ground and well mixed, pass through a sieve with force until everything dries
3. Add afterwards the well-beaten egg whites, mix everything well
4. Form the biscuits with a spoon on sheets of paper, like the preceding ones
5. Pulverize with fine sugar on top, bake in a mild oven and serve them like the preceding ones
