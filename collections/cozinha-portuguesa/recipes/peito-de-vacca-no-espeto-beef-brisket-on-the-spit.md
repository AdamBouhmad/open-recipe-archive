## Ingredients

- Beef brisket
- bacon for larding
- white wine
- vinegar
- onions
- garlic
- sausage
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- lemon slices
- butter
- spicy sauce or anchovy and caper sauce or other sauce as preferred
- paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. It is necessary that it be first half-cooked in a good braise
2. After it is cold and larded with bacon as is done for fricandeau, marinate for three or four hours in white wine, vinegar, onions, garlic, sausage, salt, pepper, fine herbs, and lemon slices
3. After, place on the spit and wrap very well in paper greased with butter
4. When well browned and colored, serve with spicy sauce or anchovy and caper sauce or as preferred
