## Ingredients

- fifteen eggs with five whites removed
- one arratel of powdered stone sugar
- one arratel of flour
- powdered sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat fifteen eggs (removing the whites from five) with one arratel of stone sugar in powder for the space of three hours
2. When beaten, add one arratel of flour, stirring until it incorporates with the eggs
3. Cut onto paper to the length of a palm and height of a finger
4. Put in the oven with powdered sugar on top
5. When half-cooked, remove and detach from the paper with a knife
6. Put back in the oven to bake through
