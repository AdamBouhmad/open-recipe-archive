## Ingredients

- eels
- butter
- flour
- broth
- fish substance or stock
- salt
- pepper
- sauce
- fine herbs
- white wine
- orange juice or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the eels as mentioned
2. Remove the spines from the sides
3. Cut into pieces
4. Pass through butter and colored flour
5. Moisten with broth and fish substance or stock
6. Season with salt, pepper, sauce, and fine herbs
7. Simmer slowly, adding half a quartilho of wine
8. When cooked and sauce reduced and of good taste, serve with orange juice or lemon juice
