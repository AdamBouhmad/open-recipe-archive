## Ingredients

- two arratels of flour
- half an arratel of butter
- one ounce of ground salt
- eighteen eggs
- three or four ounces of ferment in small pieces

## Directions

1. Place on the table two arratels of flour, half an arratel of butter, one ounce of ground salt, eighteen fresh eggs, and knead everything two or three times with the palm of the hand
2. Then add to it three or four ounces of ferment made into small pieces, knead everything again with the palm of the hand five or six times
3. Then add salt to the dough, sprinkle with flour, and wrap it in a towel until the next day
4. Take it out, place it on the table, make it into a roll, cut it into small pieces, shape it on one and the other side by putting it in boiling water, stirred from time to time with the skimmer so that it does not boil over
5. When the small cakes rise above the water, remove them
6. Place them in cold water, then place them in a sieve to drain
7. When necessary, put them to bake in the oven, and eat them hot or cold, as you wish
