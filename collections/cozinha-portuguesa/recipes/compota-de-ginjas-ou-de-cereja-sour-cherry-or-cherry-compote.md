## Ingredients

- Sour cherries or cherries
- Sugar at high point

## Directions

1. Cut equally at the stems of the sour cherries or cherries, put them in sugar at high point, and place on heat.
2. After four boils, arrange on a plate, reduce the sugar, skim it, pour on top, and serve cold.
