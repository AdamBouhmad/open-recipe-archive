## Ingredients

- eight eggs (two with whites and yolks, six with yolks only)
- one arratel of powdered sugar
- one arratel of flour

## Directions

1. Put eight eggs (two complete, six yolks only) on top of one arratel of powdered sugar in a pan or small basin
2. Beat everything very well until white and thick
3. Add one arratel of flour gradually, always stirring without stopping
4. Take in small pieces rolling in the hand, wrapping in something with flour so they do not stick
5. Place in basins that are greased with butter and send to the oven
