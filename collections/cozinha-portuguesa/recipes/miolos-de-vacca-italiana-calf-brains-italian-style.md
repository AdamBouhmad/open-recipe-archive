## Ingredients

- Calf brains
- Water
- Butter
- Onion
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Bread
- Essence sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the brains and firm them in hot water as described above, then cut them into dice
2. Place them in a casserole with butter, onion, parsley, seasoned with salt and pepper, and half a dozen beaten eggs for each brain
3. Place on heat and mix everything with a spoon, then place in a small buttered timbale garnished with slices of very thin bread
4. Finish cooking in the oven and when well browned, serve with clear essence sauce on top and lemon juice
