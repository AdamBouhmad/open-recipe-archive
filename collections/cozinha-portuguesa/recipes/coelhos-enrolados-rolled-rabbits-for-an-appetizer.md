## Ingredients

- rabbit
- cloth
- raw chopped forcemeat
- ham strips
- hard egg yolks
- truffles
- pistachios
- egg whites, beaten
- twine
- broth
- white wine
- sage bundle

## Directions

1. Bone the rabbits entirely except for the head and neck, which should remain attached
2. Lay them flat on a clean cloth with the loins facing down
3. Cover with a raw chopped forcemeat bound well with ham strips, hard egg yolks, truffles, and pistachios if available
4. Moisten the top with beaten egg whites
5. Roll them up carefully so the head points outward
6. Tie with twine and secure
7. Cook as the previous recipe
8. Serve with the same sauce or with anchovy and caper sauce, or Spanish-style sauce
