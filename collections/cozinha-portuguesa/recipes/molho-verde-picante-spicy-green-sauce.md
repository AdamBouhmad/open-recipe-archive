## Ingredients

- melted bacon fat
- pieces of veal and ham
- powdered flour
- one cup of white wine
- two spoons of broth
- parsley
- chervil
- tarragon
- mint
- chive stems
- lemon juice
- four egg yolks
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry in melted bacon fat pieces of veal and ham, powdered flour; after frying, add to it one cup of white wine, two spoons of broth, and let it simmer little by little; then pound parsley, chervil, tarragon, mint, chive stems, and squeeze everything with lemon juice, extract the green juice, dissolve it with four egg yolks, bind the sauce with this, pass it through a sieve; and tasting good, serve it for chickens, veal, lamb, kid, and for everything that is tender.
