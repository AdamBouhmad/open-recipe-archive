## Ingredients

- lamb chop squares
- parsley in thin strips
- pork butter
- shallot sauce
- lemon juice
- bacon lard

## Directions

1. Take two squares of lamb chops, clean them, and prepare them as stated
2. Lard them with bocal-sized pieces of green parsley
3. Tie them to a spit and place them to roast
4. When the parsley is dry, baste them with hot pork butter and continue until roasted
5. Serve with shallot sauce and lemon juice, or they can be larded with fine bacon and after being marinated, roasted, and served with the same sauce
