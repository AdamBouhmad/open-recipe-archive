## Ingredients

- two arratels of lamb from the leg
- a quarter of bacon
- aromatics
- vinegar
- seasonings
- a quarter of grated bread
- six eggs
- butter
- bread slices
- lemon
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut two arratels of lamb from the leg into small pieces without bone
2. Braise with a quarter of bacon and aromatics
3. When well cooked, season with vinegar and seasonings
4. Give it another boil
5. After it has boiled, add a quarter of grated bread
6. Bind with six eggs
7. Place on a plate greased with butter over slices of bread
8. Top with lemon slices and cinnamon
