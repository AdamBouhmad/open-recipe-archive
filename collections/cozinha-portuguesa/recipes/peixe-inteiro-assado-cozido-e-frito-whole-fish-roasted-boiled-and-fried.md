## Ingredients

- one whole fish
- water

## Directions

1. Divide the fish into three parts with two cuts, leaving the head as one part, the middle as another, and the tail as another
2. Hold it by the head and tail, fry the middle part, and turning it in the same manner, fry it on the other side
3. After the middle is fried, roast the tail on a grill on both sides
4. When roasted, wrap the cold and roasted part in paper, and holding it with your hands, boil the head in a pot of boiling water
5. Place the fish on a plate on top of a pastry without a lid, and make three small boxes inside, putting hot sauce for roasted in one, cold sauce for boiled in another, and escabeche for the cold part in the third
6. When done, send it to the table
