## Ingredients

- one or two veal rib sections
- fine bacon for larding
- water
- broth
- aspic or juice
- lemon juice
- chives ragú
- sorrel ragú
- spinach ragú
- celery ragú
- chicory ragú
- lettuce ragú
- green peas ragú

## Directions

1. Take one or two veal rib sections and cut the chops to good thickness, well trimmed of all skin
2. Cut the bones so they are not too long and lard with fine bacon
3. Soak in water for a short time and braise
4. Cook, season, and finish in the same manner as a fricandeau
5. Deglaze with a little broth on the glaze that stuck, or a little aspic
6. Remove the fat and pass through a sieve to serve under the chops with lemon juice
7. Alternatively, place under the glazed chops a ragú of well-blanched chives, and other times of sorrel, spinach, celery, chicory, lettuce, or green peas
8. Finally, serve with the sauce or ragú that seems most convenient, giving the dish the name of the garnish it has
