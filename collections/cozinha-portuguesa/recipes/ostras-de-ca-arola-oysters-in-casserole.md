## Ingredients

- oysters
- their own water
- washed butter
- half a clove of garlic
- minced sauce
- pepper
- one or two small spoonfuls of white wine
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Open oysters and put them with their own water in a casserole
2. Place on heat and remove before boiling
3. Trim them and arrange on a plate with washed butter on the bottom
4. Add half a clove of garlic and well minced sauce, pepper, and one or two small spoonfuls of white wine
5. Cover with another piece of butter and fine breadcrumbs
6. Cook in the oven or under a cover
7. When cooked and golden, serve as an entrée
