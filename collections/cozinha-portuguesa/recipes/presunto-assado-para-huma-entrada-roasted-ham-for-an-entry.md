## Ingredients

- ham
- warm water
- Madeira wine
- peverada sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the ham and remove its skin, soak it in warm water until the salt is removed, dry it in cloth, and soak it again in Madeira wine for ten or twelve hours
2. After this time, place it on the spit, and being half roasted, baste it from time to time with the same wine in which it was soaked, in such a way that it falls in the dripping pan
3. Having finished roasting thus, remove it and serve with thickened peverada sauce or with other suitable sauce
