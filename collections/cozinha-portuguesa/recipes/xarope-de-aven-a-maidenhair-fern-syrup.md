## Ingredients

- maidenhair fern leaves
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Steep one ounce of maidenhair fern leaves in a quart of boiling water.
2. Let it infuse over gentle heat for twelve hours.
3. Pass through a sieve.
4. Pour this juice into one pound of sugar in the hard crack stage.
5. Put it in a bowl with gentle and equal heat for two or three days.
6. When the sugar is reduced and forms a thread that does not break between the fingers, guard it in bottles after it cools, like the preceding ones.
