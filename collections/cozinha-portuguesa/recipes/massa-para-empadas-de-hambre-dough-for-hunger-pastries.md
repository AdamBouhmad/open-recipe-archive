## Ingredients

- half an alqueire or one quarter of flour
- two or three arratels of butter
- some pinches of salt
- four egg yolks
- necessary water

## Directions

1. Place on a table half an alqueire or one quarter of flour with two or three arratels of butter, some pinches of salt, and four egg yolks
2. Mix everything very well, adding the necessary water little by little until it is well bound and consistent
3. Afterwards divide it in two halves, knead each one very well
4. After being well kneaded, use for tarts, pastries, or whatever is necessary
