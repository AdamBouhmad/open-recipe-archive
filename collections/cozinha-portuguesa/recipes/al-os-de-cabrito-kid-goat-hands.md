## Ingredients

- kid goat hands, skinned
- eggs
- flour
- citron slices
- bread slices
- chicken or mutton broth
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. After skinning the kid goat hands, cook them first, then place them in conserve for four hours
2. Pass them through a batter of eggs and flour and place on a silver plate or frying pan over slices of bread also passed through the same batter, with thin citron slices between hand and hand
3. Make a sauce of rich chicken broth or sour and sweet mutton broth and pour it over the hands
4. Send to the table after browning with cinnamon on top
