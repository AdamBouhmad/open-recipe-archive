## Ingredients

- one chicken breast, lightly cooked and shredded
- one and a half canada of milk
- one and a half arratels of rice flour
- three arratels of sugar in pieces

## Directions

1. Shred a lightly cooked chicken breast
2. Put to cook in a pan with one and a half canada of milk and one and a half arratels of rice flour
3. While cooking, add three arratels of sugar in pieces little by little, piece by piece
4. When cooked, place in a basin
5. Shape into small balls the shape of Zaragoza plums
6. Put in the oven to brown and cover with sugar
