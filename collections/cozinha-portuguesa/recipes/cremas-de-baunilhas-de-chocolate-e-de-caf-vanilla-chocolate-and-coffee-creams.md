## Ingredients

- three quartilhos of milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- vanilla or chocolate or coffee
- eight egg yolks
- two or three egg whites

## Directions

1. Put to boil three quartilhos of milk seasoned with salt, sugar, cinnamon, and lemon zest
2. When reduced to half a handful, grind the necessary quantity of vanilla, pour it into the milk with eight egg yolks and two or three egg whites
3. Pass everything through a sieve
4. Pour into its plate and cook as the others, and serve in the same way
5. For chocolate cream, proceed in the same manner
6. For coffee cream, brew the coffee in milk, then let it settle and clarify; mix this milk with another seasoned one and with egg yolks, pass through a sieve, pour into the plate, cook and serve as the others
