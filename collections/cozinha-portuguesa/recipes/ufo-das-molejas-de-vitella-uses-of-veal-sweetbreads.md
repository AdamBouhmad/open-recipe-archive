## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Water

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads for a little time in warm water and clean them well
2. Blanch them in boiling water
3. Cut them into pieces and add them to stews and pies where needed
4. They can also be served roasted after being larded with fine bacon
5. Other times they can be cooked like fricandeau
