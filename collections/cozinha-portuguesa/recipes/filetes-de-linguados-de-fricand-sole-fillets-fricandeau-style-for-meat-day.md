## Ingredients

- sole loins
- fine bacon
- white wine
- water
- salt
- fine herbs
- reduced broth

## Directions

1. Lard the sole loins with fine bacon
2. Cook in white wine, water, salt, and fine herbs
3. After cooking, pour reduced broth into another pan
4. Place the fillets with the bacon underneath
5. Let simmer gently
6. When the broth reaches a glaze consistency and clings to the bacon with good color, serve with essence sauce and lemon juice
