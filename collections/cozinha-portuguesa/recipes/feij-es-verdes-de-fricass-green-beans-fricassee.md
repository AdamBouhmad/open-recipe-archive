## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- egg yolks
- milk
- parsley
- garlic
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in plain water, then stir-fry them with butter dusted with flour and seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Add then a spoon of broth, give them a boil, and bind with egg yolks mixed in milk with a little chopped parsley, a touch of garlic, and a dash of vinegar
3. Having good taste, serve for an entre-mets
