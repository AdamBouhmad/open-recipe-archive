## Ingredients

- a chicken breast very well cooked, shredded and broken apart
- two canadadas of milk
- two arratels of sugar
- one and a quarter arratels of rice flour
- half a canadada of milk for cooling
- one arratel of sugar
- flower water

## Directions

1. Once a chicken breast is very well cooked, shred it and break it apart with a spoon in a pot.
2. Add two canadadas of milk, two arratels of sugar, and one and a quarter arratels of rice flour and mix while cooking.
3. While cooking, add little by little half a canadada of milk for cooling and one arratel of sugar.
4. When cooked (you can tell by inserting the point of a knife and it comes away clean), add flower water.
5. Remove from heat and cut into pieces.
6. In the same way it can be made with fish or lobster instead of chicken.
