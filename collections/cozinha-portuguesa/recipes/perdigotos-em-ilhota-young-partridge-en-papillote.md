## Ingredients

- Three or four young partridges, eviscerated and singed
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chopped chives
- Truffles
- Garlic
- Parchment paper

## Directions

1. After eviscerated and singed three or four young partridges with legs tucked inside the body, flatten them between two cloths with the flat of a knife
2. Marinate them in good olive oil, salt, pepper, parsley, chopped chives, truffles and half a garlic clove, all finely chopped
3. After marinating for two or three hours to take flavor, wrap each one in half a sheet of paper with the same seasonings and place to roast on grills on a gentle fire in such a way that the paper does not burn
4. Once roasted on both sides, serve in the same paper
