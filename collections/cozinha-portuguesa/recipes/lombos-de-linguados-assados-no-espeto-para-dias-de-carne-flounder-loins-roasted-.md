## Ingredients

- flounder loins
- raw chopped forcemeat made of chicken breasts
- scraped bacon
- sauce
- shallots
- egg yolks
- bread soaked in milk
- bacon strip
- buttered paper
- breadcrumbs
- piquant sauce or clear essence
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Remove the loins from the flounders and cut them into equal fillets
2. Cover them with a little raw chopped forcemeat made of chicken breasts, scraped bacon, sauce, and shallots, seasoned and bound with egg yolks and bread soaked in milk and well squeezed
3. Roll them like patties and cover with a strip of bacon
4. Put in a small skewer, tie to another larger skewer, cover with a sheet of buttered paper
5. Place to roast
6. After roasting, remove the paper, cover with fine breadcrumbs, and place to brown
7. When browned, serve with piquant sauce or clear essence and lime juice
