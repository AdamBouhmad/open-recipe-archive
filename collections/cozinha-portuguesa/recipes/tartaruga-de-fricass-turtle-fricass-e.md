## Ingredients

- turtle
- water
- onion slices
- fine herbs
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- sage
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place a pot on the fire with water, onion slices, and fine herbs
2. When boiling, put the turtles inside with heads and feet cut off
3. When they are stiffened and can easily have their shells separated, remove the meat
4. Clean the meat, cut into quarters, and brown in butter mixed with a little flour
5. Add a drop of broth and season with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
6. When cooked and bound with eggs and chopped sage, finish with lemon juice
7. Serve hot as an entrée
