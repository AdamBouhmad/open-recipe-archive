## Ingredients

- ray
- water
- vinegar
- butter
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- onion in slices
- saffron
- cloves
- lemon in slices
- clarified butter
- flour
- fried saffron

## Directions

1. Cut the ray into equal pieces
2. Blanch, skin, and marinate for two or three hours in warm water, vinegar, butter, fine herbs, salt, pepper, garlic, onion slices, saffron, cloves, and lemon slices
3. After marinating, drain in a sieve
4. Dry in a cloth
5. Flour the pieces
6. Fry in clarified butter
7. Serve with fried saffron slices arranged around
