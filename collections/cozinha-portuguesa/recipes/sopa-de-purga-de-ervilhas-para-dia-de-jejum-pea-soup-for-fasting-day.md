## Ingredients

- one eighth of peas
- green onion tails
- marjoram leaves
- legurella herb
- butter
- root substance

## Directions

1. Boil in water one eighth of peas, green onion tails, some marjoram leaves, and a sprig of legurella
2. When everything is softened, strain through a sieve
3. Pour into a pot and cook in butter
4. When almost cooked, pound in a stone mortar with already-pounded green onion tails
5. After pounding, transfer to a pot, dissolve with root substance, and bring to boil
6. When boiled enough, pass through a fine strainer, and when hot and good flavor, serve over dry bread crusts cooked in root broth
