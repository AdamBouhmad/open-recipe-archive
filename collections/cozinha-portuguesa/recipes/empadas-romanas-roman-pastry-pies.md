## Ingredients

- Half alqueire of flour
- Water
- Salt
- Two or three dozen live birds

## Directions

1. Make a large pastry from half alqueire of flour kneaded in water and salt, covering it like a pastry pie
2. When fried, blow air into it through a small hole so that it becomes well filled with air
3. After it is very well cooked, open a round hole in the bottom and insert two or three dozen live birds into it
4. Seal the hole with the same dough and send it to the table
