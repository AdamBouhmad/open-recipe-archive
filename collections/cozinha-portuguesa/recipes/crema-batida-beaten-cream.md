## Ingredients

- one pan of good cow's milk
- half a pound of sifted and fine sugar
- one small spoonful of orange flower water
- a piece of gum tragacanth the size of a hazelnut, in powder

## Directions

1. Pour one pan of good cow's milk into a bowl with half a pound of sifted and fine sugar
2. Add one small spoonful of orange flower water
3. Beat on the edge with a bundle of rods, and add as much gum tragacanth powder as a hazelnut
4. Continue always beating and lifting the foam, remove it with a skimmer into a sieve with a plate underneath
5. Continue thus until all the milk is finished
6. Arrange neatly with the skimmer and serve with fruit as the final course
