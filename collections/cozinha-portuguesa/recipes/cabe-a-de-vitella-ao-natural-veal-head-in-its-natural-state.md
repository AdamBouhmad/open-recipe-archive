## Ingredients

- one veal head, skinned and blanched
- flour
- water
- salt
- whole pepper
- one bundle of herbs (fines herbes)
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- onion studded with cloves
- pieces of parsnip
- carrots
- some slices of lemon without peel
- one glass of white wine
- broth
- vinegar
- fines herbes, chopped finely

## Directions

1. After the head is skinned and well blanched, and singed over a well-lit hearth, remove the jaws and the bone of the nose up to the eyes, lifting the skin very carefully without damaging it
2. Soak it in water for a whole night
3. Blanch it in fresh water
4. After giving it a few boilings, remove it and put it in cold water
5. Scrape the inside of the jaws very well, wash them very well, tie it with twine
6. Put it to cook in a white broth made with a little flour dissolved in a pan of water, seasoned with salt, whole pepper, one bundle of herbs, bay leaf, marjoram, onion studded with cloves, pieces of parsnip, carrots, some slices of lemon without peel, and one glass of white wine
7. When cooked, remove it and let it drain
8. After removing the bones and uncovering the brains, place it in a good shape on the serving plate with its spicy sauce on top made with a little broth, vinegar, salt, pepper, and finely chopped fines herbes
9. It can also be served with different sauces, such as ravigota, peverada, or Italian style
10. Note that it can also be made without fines herbes or roots, in case they are not available
