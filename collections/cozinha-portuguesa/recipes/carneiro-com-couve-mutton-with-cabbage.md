## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton, cut in pieces
- half arratel of bacon
- herbs
- cabbage
- two heads of garlic
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place two arratels of mutton, cut in pieces, to braise with half arratel of bacon and herbs
2. When boiling, add the cabbage and two heads of garlic with some pepper
3. When cooked, season with all spices and serve
