## Ingredients

- butter
- truffles
- ham
- hard egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- chives
- fresh egg
- water

## Directions

1. Grease molds inside with butter, like for pastries
2. Cover the bottoms and sides with truffles, ham, cooked hard egg yolks, all chopped fine with herbs, chives, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. Break a fresh egg into each mold
4. Dust on top with the same chopped garnishes that were placed below
5. Put to cook inside a casserole with a little water at the bottom, covered with its lid and coals on top
6. When cooked, remove from the casserole, turn the molds onto their plate, and serve with glace or clear essence sauce, or as preferred
