## Ingredients

- Venison scrapings
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- Cinnamon
- White wine
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Having scraped the venison, cook for five or six hours
2. When cooked and the broth reduced to a sufficiently strong consistency, strain everything through cloth and press
3. Season with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, and a little white wine if it seems good
4. Bring back to the heat with beaten egg whites
5. When it comes to a boil, strain two or three times through a cloth
6. When clear and set, serve as the others
