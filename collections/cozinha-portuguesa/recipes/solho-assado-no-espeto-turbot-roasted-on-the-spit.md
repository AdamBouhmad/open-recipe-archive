## Ingredients

- one turbot portion the size of the serving plate
- lard
- anchovy fillets
- fish broth
- vinegar
- onion wheels
- lemon wheels
- bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cullis sauce
- washed butter

## Directions

1. Cut one turbot portion the size of the serving plate
2. Lard it with anchovies and anchovy fillets in pieces and seasoned
3. Tie it to a spit and place to roast
4. Drip with a marinade made of fish broth, vinegar, onion wheels, lemon wheels, bay leaves, salt, pepper, and a piece of butter
5. When roasted, serve as an entree with the sauce from the remaining marinade, bound with a spoon of cullis and a piece of washed butter
