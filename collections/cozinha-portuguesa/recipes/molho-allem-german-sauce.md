## Ingredients

- Shallot
- Turnip greens
- Sorrel
- Toasted bread soaked in vinegar
- Rhine wine, half a bottle
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pound shallot tails and turnip greens in a stone mortar, but one thing against the already made
2. Add a handful of sorrel, a slice of toasted bread soaked in vinegar; when everything is well pounded, dissolve in a casserole with half a bottle of Rhine wine
3. Season with sugar and salt, put on the fire, and stirring always with a spoon, let it boil for an instant
4. Strain through a sieve, and it will serve for game as the preceding one
