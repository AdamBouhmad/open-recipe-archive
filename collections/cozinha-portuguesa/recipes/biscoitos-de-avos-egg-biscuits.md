## Ingredients

- a dozen eggs with whites
- one arratel of sugar
- one arratel of flour
- a small amount of fennel, chosen
- rose water

## Directions

1. Beat a dozen eggs with whites and one arratel of sugar
2. Add one arratel of flour, a small amount of fennel, and rose water
3. After beating this mixture very well, place with a spoon in basins on baking sheets at the oven door with flour underneath the sheets
4. When half-cooked, remove, cut with a knife to desired size, and turning them, finish cooking in the oven
