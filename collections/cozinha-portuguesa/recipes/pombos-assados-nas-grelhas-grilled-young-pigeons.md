## Ingredients

- four large fat young pigeons
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- good olive oil
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- fine breadcrumbs
- marinade

## Directions

1. Take four large fat young pigeons, clean and singe them, tuck the legs inside, split them down the back or breast
2. Pound them between cloths with the knife blade
3. Season with salt, pepper, parsley, minced onion, good olive oil, bay leaf, and marjoram
4. Let sit for two or three hours to absorb the flavors
5. Dust with fine breadcrumbs and grill over coals, basting with the marinade
6. When grilled and of good color, serve with shallot sauce or spicy sauce
