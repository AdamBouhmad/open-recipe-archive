## Ingredients

- sweet almonds
- bitter almonds
- peaches, apricots, or other sweets
- lemon peel
- beef marrow
- egg yolks
- fine sugar
- salt
- milk
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound sweet almonds and bitter almonds as is customary
2. Add some peaches, apricots, or other sweets, a little grated lemon peel, and as much beef marrow as a handful
3. Pound everything well
4. Add egg yolks
5. Season with fine sugar and salt
6. Dissolve in a little milk
7. Mix with beaten egg whites
8. Line a pan with butter
9. Add the mixture
10. Cook in the oven
11. Once cooked, serve glazed with hot spatula and sugar on top
