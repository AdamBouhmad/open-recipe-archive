## Ingredients

- one chicken breast (or liver and giblets of chicken, soaked)
- one quarter pound of sugar, finely ground
- ground cinnamon
- four egg yolks
- bread slices
- flour
- butter or oil for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the chicken breast (or liver and giblets) finely with the finely ground sugar, ground cinnamon, and four egg yolks
2. Place bread slices in a pan or frying vessel with flour underneath
3. Put the chopped mixture on top of the bread
4. Place over coals, and after browning with coals on top, remove and send to the table
