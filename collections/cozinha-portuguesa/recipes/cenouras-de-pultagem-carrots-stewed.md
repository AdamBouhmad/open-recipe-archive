## Ingredients

- carrots
- cow butter
- olive oil
- onion
- salt
- vinegar or lemon
- seasonings
- honey or sugar
- flour fried and well toasted
- broth

## Directions

1. After cooking the carrots, cut them in quarters and sauté them in cow butter with olive oil and onion
2. Once sautéed, place them to cook in a pot in hot water
3. Season with salt, vinegar or lemon, all the seasonings, honey or sugar, well toasted fried flour, and a small amount of broth in which they will cook
4. Once cooked, serve at table
