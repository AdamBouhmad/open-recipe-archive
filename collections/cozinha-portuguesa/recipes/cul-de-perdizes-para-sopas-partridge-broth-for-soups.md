## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- Two partridges
- Pieces of bread crusts
- Substance
- Stock

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef and two partridges, remove the breasts and pound the rest with pieces of bread crusts
2. Dissolve with good substance and stock
3. Season and give it four boilings
4. Pass through a strainer or sieve
5. Use for various soups
