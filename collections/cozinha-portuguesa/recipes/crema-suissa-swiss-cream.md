## Ingredients

- one bottle of white wine or Rhine wine
- one quarter of sugar
- a piece of green lemon peel
- whole cinnamon
- twenty-four grains of coriander seed
- six egg yolks
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Put one bottle of white wine or Rhine wine in a pan with one quarter sugar, a piece of green lemon peel, whole cinnamon, and twenty-four grains of coriander seed
2. Let it boil gently for four or five minutes
3. When reduced to two-thirds, pass it through a sieve with six egg yolks and a little flour dissolved
4. Pour into a dish and place it to cook over a pan of boiling water
5. Put a little heat on top
6. When it is set, let it cool and serve as an intermediate course
7. The same can be made with water, Madeira wine, Malaga wine, or any other wine desired
