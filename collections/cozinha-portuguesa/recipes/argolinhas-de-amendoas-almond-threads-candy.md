## Ingredients

- one arratel of sugar
- one arratel of almonds, finely ground
- some flour

## Directions

1. Put one arratel of sugar in a pan and bring to thread point
2. When at thread point, remove from heat and add one arratel of finely ground almonds and some flour that is sufficient to cook well until they clean the pan well
3. See if they can be drawn into threads, and when drawn, let cool
4. Send to the oven; when they return, cover with one arratel of sugar put at low thread point
