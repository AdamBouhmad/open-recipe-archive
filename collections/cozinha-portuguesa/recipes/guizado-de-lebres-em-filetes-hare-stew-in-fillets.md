## Ingredients

- hare
- bacon planks
- butter or melted lard
- meat essence
- green onion
- pepper
- limes

## Directions

1. Stiffen the hare over coals and rub it with its own blood
2. Cover it with bacon planks, put it on the spit, and put it to roast, basting with butter or melted lard
3. After roasted, remove the meat and cut it into very thin fillets
4. Put them in meat essence, adding a little finely chopped green onion mixed with pepper, and when hot without boiling, serve with lime juice
