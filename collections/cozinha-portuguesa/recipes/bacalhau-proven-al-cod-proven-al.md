## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- olive oil
- garlic
- fresh butter
- water or milk

## Directions

1. Choose the clearest cod possible and soak it for one or two days, changing the water two or three times
2. Drain and place it on the fire in sufficient water until it comes to a boil
3. Remove from heat and let it rest half an hour
4. Break it into pieces, removing the bones
5. Cook it on the fire with olive oil and chopped garlic
6. Add a good piece of fresh butter and a drop of water or milk
7. Stir constantly over gentle heat until it binds
8. Serve hot
