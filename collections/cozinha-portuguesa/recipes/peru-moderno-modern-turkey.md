## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- half cup of white wine
- good oil
- garlic
- pair of anchovies
- pair of mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- two lemon slices peeled
- paper
- bacon

## Directions

1. In a casserole with four egg yolks, half a cup of white wine, a little good oil
2. Add finely minced anchovy, mushroom (the note indicates this is garlic), salt, and pepper
3. Put on the heat with two lemon slices without skin and stir constantly around with a wooden spoon, being careful that it does not break
4. When bound and of good taste, and the turkey is roasted, remove the paper and bacon, place and arrange it on its plate with the aforementioned sauce on top, and serve hot
