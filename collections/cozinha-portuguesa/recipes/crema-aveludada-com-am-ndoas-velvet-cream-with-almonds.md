## Ingredients

- one quarter of almonds, blanched
- half a dozen bitter almonds
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- chicken marrow

## Directions

1. Grind and moisten at the same time with a drop of milk one quarter of blanched almonds and half a dozen bitter almonds
2. After well ground and made into a paste, dissolve in boiled, seasoned and reduced milk as in the preceding one
3. Add to it two or three chicken marrow fillets
4. Pass two or three times through a napkin
5. After well squeezed, pour into its plate
6. Place over moderate heat or over a saucepan full of very hot water
7. When curdled, serve it
