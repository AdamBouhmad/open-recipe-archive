## Ingredients

- One chicken, cut into pieces
- One quarter of bacon
- Aromatics
- Salt
- Vinegar
- All seasonings
- Four eggs
- Parsley
- Bread slices
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut one chicken into pieces and put them to stew with one quarter of bacon, aromatics, salt, and vinegar.
2. Season with all seasonings and thicken at the end with four eggs (or if you do not wish eggs, make it a parsley potage).
3. Place on slices of bread, lemon juice on top, and send it to the table.
4. The same is made of turkey, pigeons, young chickens, and kid goats.
