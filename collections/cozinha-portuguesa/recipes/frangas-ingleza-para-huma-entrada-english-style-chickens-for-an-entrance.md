## Ingredients

- two chickens
- water
- salt
- flour
- pork fat
- melted butter
- broth
- parsley
- cauliflower or cabbage

## Directions

1. Clean two chickens and secure with pins
2. Blanch in water, then flour them
3. Cover with a slice of pork fat and cook in a pan with water and salt
4. When cooked, serve with melted butter thickened with a drop of broth, adding finely chopped and blanched parsley
5. Can also be garnished with cooked cauliflower, cabbage, or other vegetables
