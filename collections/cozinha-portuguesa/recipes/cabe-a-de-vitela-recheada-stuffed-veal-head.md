## Ingredients

- veal head
- ground meat
- pheasants or squabs with livers
- ham
- veal tongue
- string
- beef or mutton broth
- vinegar
- whole spices
- bay leaves
- butter or lard
- veal feet, larded

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the veal head, keeping it whole
2. Make a very fine ground meat mixture and spread it inside
3. Make a layer of pheasants or squabs with their livers and a piece of ham, placing it inside between the ground meat
4. Put the veal tongue in the mouth, pointing halfway out
5. Sew the corners of the mouth closed with string so it does not fall
6. Sew closed in the same manner along the neck side
7. Place the head in a pot where beef is cooking and partially cook it
8. Then place it in a pan with a bit of broth and vinegar, whole spices, and bay leaves
9. Coat the head with butter or lard on top and put it in the oven
10. Finish cooking in this sauce, turning it occasionally from top to bottom and bottom to top
11. Once the head is cooked, place it on a plate and garnish with a layer of liver or ham mixed with eggs and lemon water, surrounded by larded veal feet
12. Send to the table
