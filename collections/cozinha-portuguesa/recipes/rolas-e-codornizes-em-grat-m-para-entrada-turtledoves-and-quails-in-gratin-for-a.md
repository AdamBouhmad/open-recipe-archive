## Ingredients

- Turtledoves or quails
- Fat livers
- Fennel (fiolho or funcho)
- Bread crumbs
- Fat from the pan
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean, and lard the quails or turtledoves
2. Open them somewhat along the sides and fill with a mixture of chopped fat livers in which fennel should dominate
3. Cook them in a good dry braise
4. When cooked, spread some of the same mixture on the bottom of the serving plate and place the birds on top
5. When the liver is set, skim the fat from it
6. Place the turtledoves or quails on top with Spanish sauce over them
