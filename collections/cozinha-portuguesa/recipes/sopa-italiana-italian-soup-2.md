## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton
- half arratel of bacon
- one and a half canada of water
- half quartilho of wine
- one splash of vinegar
- herbs and onions
- cloves
- cinnamon stick
- quarter of butter
- pepper
- two handfuls of flour and butter in small pieces
- six egg yolks
- lemon
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook two arratels of mutton and half arratel of bacon in a pot with one and a half canada of water, half quartilho of wine, one splash of vinegar, herbs and onions
2. Stud the onions with cloves and a whole cinnamon stick
3. When cooked, season with all the seasonings and let it boil
4. Remove the bacon and mutton so that the broth remains clear
5. Reduce to three quartilhos
6. Add a quarter of butter and pepper
7. Let the broth boil, and when it is boiling, add two handfuls of flour and butter in small pieces
8. Boil until it thickens, stirring constantly
9. Add six beaten egg yolks with lemon juice and cinnamon
10. When well thickened, pour into a plate
11. Place the mutton on top, which should remain hot
12. Garnish with lemon slices and cover with cinnamon
13. Serve at table
