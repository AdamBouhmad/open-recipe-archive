## Ingredients

- One or two turkeys, depending on the size of the platter
- bacon lardons
- paper sheets
- veal, bacon, and beef fat
- salt
- pepper
- shallot
- fine herbs
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- six egg yolks
- egg whites
- turkey gizzards, truffles, livers, or other choice ingredients

## Directions

1. Prepare and pluck the turkeys, place them on a spit covered with bacon strips and paper sheets on top
2. Roast them
3. Meanwhile, make a fine mince of veal pieces, bacon, and beef fat, all chopped and cut into small pieces, and pass them over moderate heat seasoned with salt, pepper, shallot, and fine herbs
4. Once the turkeys are roasted and cooled, remove the breast meat, mix it with the mince, chop it on the table, and pound it very well in a mortar
5. Add to it a bread crumb soaked in milk but cooled, and bind it with six egg yolks and egg whites beaten until stiff
6. Place the turkeys in a baking dish with bacon strips underneath, fill the place where the breasts were removed with the prepared mixture, placing in the middle a ragout of turkey gizzards, truffles, livers, or any other choice ingredient, as long as it is cold and well bound
7. Cover it with the rest of the mince, seal it with beaten eggs and the back of a knife blade so it appears whole, cover it afterwards with finely grated bread crumb, and put it to cook in the oven
8. Once cooked and browned, serve with good essence or Spanish sauce
