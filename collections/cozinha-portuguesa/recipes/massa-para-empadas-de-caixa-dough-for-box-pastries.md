## Ingredients

- One quarter of flour
- Butter
- Hot water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take one quarter of flour and knead it on a table with butter and hot water, but make it stiffer than the preceding dough
2. After being very well worked, make two balls
3. From one, raise the box or boxes with skill on sheets of paper and put inside whatever seems good to you, after being seasoned and finished
4. Cover with part of the other dough
