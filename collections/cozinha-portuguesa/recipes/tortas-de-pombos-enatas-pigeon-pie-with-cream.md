## Ingredients

- Four roasted pigeons, meat picked
- Six bowls of cream
- Twelve egg yolks
- Half an arratel of sugar
- Clove
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop the meat of four roasted pigeons and mix with six bowls of cream, twelve egg yolks, and half an arratel of sugar
2. Add clove and cinnamon
3. Make the pie, cook, and serve
