## Ingredients

- parsley
- garlic
- olive oil
- green beans
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make two bundles of parsley and a clove of garlic and dissolve everything in olive oil
2. Dissolve it, pour it over the beans already cooked in water
3. Season with ground salt and pepper, mix and stir everything, and serve as you see fit
