## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- Cream à franchipana or fruit preserve
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Stretch on the table a piece of puff pastry that is a little thicker than a new silver cruzado
2. Cut it into rounds, place in small forms for pastries
3. Garnish the middle with cream à franchipana or any fruit made into preserve
4. Cover with some strips of dough with relief around the edge
5. Put to cook in the oven for half an hour
6. Glaze with sugar with a hot spatula, and serve for a side dish
