## Ingredients

- a dozen eggs
- cooked and finely chopped ham
- herbs
- half a cup of milk
- salt
- a little pepper
- butter or bacon

## Directions

1. Beat in a casserole a dozen eggs
2. Add cooked and finely chopped ham, herbs, half a cup of milk, salt, and a little pepper
3. Beat everything with a spoon
4. Cook in melted bacon or butter, following the same method as the previous ones
5. After cooked, place on its plate and serve with a little broth and chopped ham on top
