## Ingredients

- veal breasts
- lettuce
- peas
- rendered pork belly
- piece of ham
- substance broth
- cullis

## Directions

1. Cut equal pieces of veal breasts
2. Parboil them, trim them, and put them to cook in a braise with some lettuces already boiled in water and tied
3. Take some peas, stew them in rendered pork belly and a piece of ham
4. Once stewed, add to them a little substance broth and a little cullis
5. When well seasoned, cooked and of good taste, arrange the pieces of veal in the terrine after cooking with the lettuce cut into pieces
6. Pour the ragù of peas over the top
7. Serve hot as an entrance course
