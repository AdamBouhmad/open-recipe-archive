## Ingredients

- half an arratel of powdered sugar, sifted and of the finest
- one or two egg whites
- some drops of orange flower water
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Place in a glazed bowl half an arratel of powdered sugar, sifted and of the finest.
2. Add to it one or two egg whites, some drops of orange flower water, and the juice of one lemon, and dissolving everything with a wooden spoon and well beaten, when it reaches the necessary point and is white as snow, place it carefully on all the dishes where it is necessary.
3. Put these in a very mild oven, and as soon as the glaze is dry, serve for side dishes.
