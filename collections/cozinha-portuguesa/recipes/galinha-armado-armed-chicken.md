## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Two arratels of lamb
- Half arratel of bacon
- All aromatics
- Eggs from the chicken
- Vinegar
- Four eggs
- One quarter of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mince one chicken with two arratels of lamb, half arratel of bacon, and all aromatics, being careful to leave the eggs from the chicken whole.
2. When half of all this meat is stewed, return it to the chopping block to be minced together with the other half and season with all seasonings, vinegar, four eggs, and one quarter of bread crumbs.
3. After the meat is seasoned, assemble the chicken with the bones.
