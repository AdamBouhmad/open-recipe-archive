## Ingredients

- herbs as desired
- eggs, beaten
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Take any herbs and scald them in boiling water and braise them
2. When they are well scalded, fry them, and then pass them through beaten eggs, and in the manner of bundles layer them in a plate or frying pan until it is full with all the seasonings, braise gently and send them to the table
