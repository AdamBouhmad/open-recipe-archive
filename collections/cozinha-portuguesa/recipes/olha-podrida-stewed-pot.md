## Ingredients

- piece of very fat beef
- chicken
- game bird
- partridge
- pigeons
- rabbit
- hare
- pork ear or shoulder if in season
- piece of bacon
- sausages
- sausage links
- pork loin
- turnips or radishes
- three heads of large garlic
- grains
- two or three dozen chestnuts
- salt
- aromatics

## Directions

1. Place in a pot to cook a piece of very fat beef, a chicken, a game bird, a partridge or pigeons, a rabbit, a hare, a pork ear or shoulder if in season, a piece of bacon, sausages, sausage links, and pork loin, all mixed with turnips if available or radishes, three heads of large garlic, grains, two or three dozen chestnuts, salt, and aromatics
2. When cooked, serve on a plate over bread slices
