## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs, fresh and whole
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Butter the bottom of a plate
2. Place it over heat
3. Break eggs one by one into it, keeping them whole
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Cook over gentle heat
6. Pass a hot spatula over the top
7. When cooked and the yolk is not hard, serve hot
