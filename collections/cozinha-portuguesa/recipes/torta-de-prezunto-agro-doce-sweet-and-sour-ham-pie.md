## Ingredients

- Two arratels of ham, sliced
- One arratel of sugar in thread stage
- One cup of wine

## Directions

1. Soak ham slices for twenty-four hours, then cut into small slices
2. Cook in one arratel of sugar in thread stage with one cup of wine
