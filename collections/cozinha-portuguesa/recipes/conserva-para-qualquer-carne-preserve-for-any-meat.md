## Ingredients

- one quartilho of wine
- half quartilho of vinegar
- one head of garlic
- one branch of oregano
- salt

## Directions

1. Put wine, half vinegar, a head of garlic, a branch of oregano, and salt to taste in a bowl
2. Keep meat in this preserve cut in pieces for three or four hours
3. Then fry the meat in butter and pass through a bit of the preserve brine, brought to a boil
4. Send hot to the table
