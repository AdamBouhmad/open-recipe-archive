## Ingredients

- flounders
- clarified butter or fine oil
- salt
- pepper
- sauce
- shallot
- garlic clove
- bay leaf leaves
- lime juice
- buttered paper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and prepare the flounders
2. Cut off the heads and tails
3. Remove the four fillets
4. Marinate them in a little clarified butter or fine oil, seasoned with salt, pepper, sauce, shallot, a clove of garlic, all chopped, bay leaf leaves, and lime juice
5. Make little boxes of paper buttered inside
6. Put the fillets inside without pressing, with the same marinade
7. Cover with breadcrumbs
8. Brush very well with butter
9. Place in the oven to cook
10. When cooked, serve with lime juice
