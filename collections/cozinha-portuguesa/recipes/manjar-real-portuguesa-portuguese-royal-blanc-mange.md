## Ingredients

- One hen
- Salt
- Bread crumbs
- One arratel of sweet almonds
- Four arratels of sugar
- Chicken breast

## Directions

1. Pluck a hen in hot water, clean it, and wash it, and put it to cook in water with a little salt
2. When almost cooked, strain the broth through a sieve and remove the fat
3. Soak the crumb of two vintém pieces of bread in it, and when well soaked, squeeze through cloth
4. Pour it into a mortar in which there is one arratel of well-ground sweet almonds
5. Grinding everything again, strain through a sieve, removing the chicken breast
6. Pour everything into four arratels of sugar at the point of hardening
7. Bring to gentle heat, stirring always with a spoon until it incorporates to proper consistency
8. Pour into dishes or small bowls, and serve when it seems good
