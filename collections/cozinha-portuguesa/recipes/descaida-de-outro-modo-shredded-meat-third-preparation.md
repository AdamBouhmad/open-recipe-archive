## Ingredients

- Descaida meat, shredded
- chicken meatballs (almonds)
- yellow broth
- cinnamon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Shred the descaida meat
2. Make small meatballs the size of buttons, seasoned with chicken meatballs
3. Cook in yellow broth as described above
4. Place on slices of bread and serve with cinnamon and lemon juice on top
