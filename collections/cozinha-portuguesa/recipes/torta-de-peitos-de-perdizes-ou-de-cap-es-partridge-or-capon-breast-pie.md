## Ingredients

- Partridges or capons
- Quarter pound of sweet almonds, ground
- Peel of citron, orange and lemon
- Two spoonfuls of well-bound cream
- One handful of sugar
- Four egg yolks
- Beef marrow, minced
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Pluck the partridges or capons and remove the breasts, clean them after plucking
2. Grind the breasts in a mortar in which you have already placed a quarter pound of ground sweet almonds
3. Add peels of citron, orange and lemon, two spoonfuls of well-bound cream, one handful of sugar, four egg yolks, and minced beef marrow
4. Spread a piece of puff pastry dough on a baking dish, place all the mixture on top
5. Cover with strips of the same dough and seal the edges all around as is customary
6. Bake in the oven and under a lid; when cooked, serve glazed with sugar and hot
