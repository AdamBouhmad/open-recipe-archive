## Ingredients

- Azedas (sorrel)
- Manteiga (butter)
- Cebola picada (minced onion)
- Essência (essence)
- Gemas de ovos (egg yolks)
- Leite (milk)

## Directions

1. Wash and blanch the sorrel in water
2. Drain through a sieve
3. Pound them
4. Sauté minced onion in butter
5. Add the sorrel to this sauté with a little essence
6. Let them simmer for an instant
7. When of good taste, thicken with egg yolks dissolved in milk
8. Serve under any entree or for side dishes with eggs on top
