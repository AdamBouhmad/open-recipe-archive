## Ingredients

- half a measure of strawberries, cleaned and washed
- three or four ounces of fine sugar
- foam from beaten milk

## Directions

1. Take half a measure of cleaned and washed strawberries and put them in a pan with three or four ounces of fine sugar
2. Mash very well with a wooden spoon
3. Pass this pulp through a sieve
4. Beat it a second time with the foam of milk
5. When everything is incorporated, serve with the fruit as the preceding cream
