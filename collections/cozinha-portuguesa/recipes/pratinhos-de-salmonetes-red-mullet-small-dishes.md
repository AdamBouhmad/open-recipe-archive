## Ingredients

- red mullets
- butter
- small amount of chopped onion
- parsley
- capers
- small amount of prepared mustard

## Directions

1. After the red mullets are roasted, make a sauce by frying in butter a small amount of chopped onion, parsley, and capers
2. When fried, add on top a small amount of prepared mustard, and giving it two more turns in the pan, cover the red mullets with this sauce on top and send it to the table
