## Ingredients

- sheep tongues
- water
- salt
- butter
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues in plain water with little salt or in broth
2. Cut them into equal dice and place in a casserole
3. Sauté them in melted butter with chopped chives and salt, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Once sautéed, sprinkle with flour and a few drops of broth
5. Once cooked and the sauce is thick and tasty, bind with some egg yolks
6. When cold, thread the pieces onto skewers, ensuring all the sauce clings to them
7. Cover with breadcrumbs and roast on the grill, basting from time to time with melted butter
8. When roasted, serve them dry or with a little broth, chopped shallot, salt, pepper, and lime juice
