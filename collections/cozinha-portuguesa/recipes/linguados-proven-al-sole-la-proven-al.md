## Ingredients

- soles
- salt
- oil
- flour
- butter
- flour
- small onion
- mushrooms
- parsley
- garlic clove
- wine
- substance or broth
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- onion
- cloves
- capers
- sour orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the soles, open them along the back with the point of a knife from head to tail
2. Sprinkle with a little salt, dry them, flour them, and fry in oil
3. When fried, in a pan melt a piece of butter with a handful of flour and stir with a wooden spoon over the fire
4. When browned, sauté in it a small onion, some mushrooms, parsley, a garlic clove, all finely chopped
5. After everything is sautéed, add a glass of wine and a little substance or broth, season with salt, pepper, a bay leaf, and an onion with two cloves
6. Let it simmer slowly; when this sauce is reduced, cut the heads and tails from the fried soles and put them to simmer two or three minutes in this sauce with finely chopped capers on top, and serve afterward with sour orange or lemon juice
