## Ingredients

- one veal leg
- veal from the leg
- bacon
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- one clove of garlic
- four egg yolks
- bacon slices
- pistachios
- sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Cook a veal leg and reserve the best part
2. Remove the nerves and skin well from the remainder and make a forcemeat with bacon and ham, seasoned with salt, pepper, fine herbs, and one clove of garlic
3. Take the best part of the veal, a piece of ham, and bacon
4. Cut everything in dice and combine with the forcemeat
5. Add four egg yolks
6. Line a casserole with bacon slices, arrange everything inside, and cook in a moderate oven
7. Once cooked, let it cool to serve sliced
8. Other times you may add pistachios and sweet almonds cut in small dice and serve with a napkin underneath
9. It can also be served hot with Spanish sauce
