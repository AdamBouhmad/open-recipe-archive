## Ingredients

- eight arratels of sugar at low thread point
- four arratels of finely ground almonds
- one arratel of citron in small thin pieces
- clove
- cinnamon
- a little fennel
- one quarter of grated bread by measure
- pastry dough made with butter and sugar

## Directions

1. Put eight arratels of sugar at low thread point in a pan
2. Add four arratels of finely ground almonds and one arratel of citron in small thin pieces
3. Add clove, cinnamon, and a little fennel and boil briefly
4. Remove pan from heat and add one quarter of grated bread by measure
5. The sign that this mixture is done is to drop some grated bread on top and place a finger to see if it stays dry
6. Let cool on a plate
7. Take pastry dough made with butter and sugar and shape the fartes
8. When made, send to the oven
