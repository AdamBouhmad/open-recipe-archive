## Ingredients

- five or six shallots
- beef or veal substance
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop five or six shallots
2. Place in a saucepan with beef or veal substance, salt, pepper, and lemon juice
3. Put over heat to warm, but do not let it boil
4. Serves for roasted veal, beef, and mutton, and chops grilled on the grill
