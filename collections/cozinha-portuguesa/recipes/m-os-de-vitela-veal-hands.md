## Ingredients

- veal hands
- eggs
- six raw egg yolks
- one arratel of butter
- two spoonfuls of broth
- quarter pound of sugar
- vinegar
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Cook and fry the veal hands
2. Bread them with eggs and place them on a soup called Joldresi
3. To make this soup, beat six raw egg yolks in a pan with one arratel of butter, two spoonfuls of broth, quarter pound of sugar, a bit of vinegar, pepper, nutmeg, and salt
4. Put all this over the fire and when it begins to boil, which is the sign it is done, put the breaded veal hands inside
5. Place them in a small pot on coals so it does not break and on bread slices send to the table
