## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one arratel of lean ham
- whole seasonings
- one quince in quarters, or lacking quince, sour apples
- half arratel of sugar
- one dash of good vinegar

## Directions

1. Put one chicken in a pan with one arratel of lean ham, whole seasonings, one quince in quarters, or lacking quince sour apples, half arratel of sugar, one dash of good vinegar
2. Place to cook
3. When cooked, place it in a plate upon slices with one lemon, and send it to the table in white broth
