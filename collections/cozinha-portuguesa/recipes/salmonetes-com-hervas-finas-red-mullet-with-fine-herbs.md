## Ingredients

- red mullet
- melted butter
- parsley
- minced scallion
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- breadcrumbs
- remoulade sauce
- mustard

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and wash the red mullet
2. Cut off the heads and tail tips
3. Marinate in melted butter with parsley, minced scallion, salt, pepper, and fine herbs
4. After one hour of marinating, place the pan on fire
5. When the butter is melted, cover with fine breadcrumbs
6. Place on grill to roast
7. When roasted on both sides, serve with hot remoulade sauce and mustard
