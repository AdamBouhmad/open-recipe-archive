## Ingredients

- Fish, cut into pieces three fingers thick
- Tuna or needlefish if thicker
- Bacon strips
- Ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Fine herbs
- Butter
- Dough
- Eggs
- Clear broth

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces three fingers thick, or thicker if tuna or needlefish
2. Pass them through boiling water until they are firm, then lard with seasoned thick bacon and ham
3. Line the bottom of the pastry box with a layer of minced bacon, seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
4. Place the fish inside, seasoned on top as it was seasoned below
5. Cover with minced bacon, a piece of butter, and bacon strips
6. Place its dough lid, decorate it, gild it, and put in the oven to cook
7. When half-cooked, add a quarter of clear broth with the spoon and finish cooking
8. When cooked, let cool and serve cold as an appetizer
