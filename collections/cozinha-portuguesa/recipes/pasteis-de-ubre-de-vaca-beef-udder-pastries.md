## Ingredients

- Beef udder, cooked and sliced
- Half an arratel of chopped bacon
- One drop of water
- All seasonings
- Beaten eggs
- Lemon juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the beef udder and cut into slices, brown in half an arratel of bacon with one drop of water
2. Season with all seasonings and let cool
3. Take the dough as described above and make boxes without lids the size of an orange
4. After cooking, fill with udder and beaten eggs on top to serve as a lid, lemon juice, and sugar
5. Brown and serve
