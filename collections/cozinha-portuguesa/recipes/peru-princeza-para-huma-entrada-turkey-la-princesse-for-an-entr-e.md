## Ingredients

- one turkey
- breadcrumbs
- lard
- egg yolks
- capers
- parsley
- other condiments

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean the turkey, cut it in half, beat it between two cloths
2. Remove the larger bones and put it to cook in a good broth
3. Once cooked and cold, trim it, cover it with fine grated bread as is customary, and fry it in lard
4. When fried and colored, serve hot with remoulade sauce made with egg yolks, capers, parsley, and other condiments as described in the section on sauces
5. Alternatively, it can be grilled and served with the same sauce or with another spicy sauce
