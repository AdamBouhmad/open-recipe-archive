## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- sauce
- chives
- half a clove of garlic
- flour
- egg yolks
- broth
- lime juice
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Open and blanch the oysters in their own water
2. Remove before boiling, clean, and drain in a sieve
3. Brown briefly in butter with sauce, chives, and minced half clove of garlic
4. Add a little flour and bind with egg yolks diluted in broth and lime juice
5. Let cool and thread on silver skewers
6. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs, place on grills
7. When golden, serve without sauce as a hors d'oeuvre
