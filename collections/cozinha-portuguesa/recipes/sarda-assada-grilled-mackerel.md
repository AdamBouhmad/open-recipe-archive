## Ingredients

- mackerel
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- capers

## Directions

1. Remove entrails and wash the mackerel very well
2. Cut off the head and tail tip
3. Open along the sides
4. Marinate in oil, salt, and pepper
5. Place on grill to roast
6. When well roasted, place on serving plate, open, remove the backbone
7. Serve with butter and capers sauce
8. Alternatively, serve with burned butter sauce and fried parsley
