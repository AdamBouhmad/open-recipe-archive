## Ingredients

- one handful of milk
- salt
- coriander seed
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- one quarter of sugar
- seven egg yolks
- two or three egg whites

## Directions

1. Put to boil in a saucepan one handful of milk, seasoned with salt, coriander seed, cinnamon, lemon zest, and one quarter of sugar
2. When boiling and reduced to half, and almost cold, strain through a napkin with seven egg yolks and two or three egg whites
3. After straining, pour into a plate or porcelain cups
4. Cook in a vessel with boiling water
5. After cooked, sprinkle with fine sugar
6. Pass a hot spatula over the top
7. Serve cold if desired
