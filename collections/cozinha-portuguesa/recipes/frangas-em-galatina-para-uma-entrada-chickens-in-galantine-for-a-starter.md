## Ingredients

- Two or three chickens
- Minced filling, well bound
- Truffle fillets
- Bacon fillets
- Pistachio fillets
- Hard egg yolk fillets
- Bacon slices
- Linen cloth
- Broth
- White wine
- Ham essence sauce or other sauce

## Directions

1. Take two or three chickens, cut them in half and completely bone them as is done with hens in cannelloni.
2. Cover them with a fine minced filling, well bound, and over this make a cord of truffle fillets, one of bacon fillets, another of pistachios, another of hard egg yolk fillets, and continue thus to the end.
3. Cover all these fillets with a little of the minced filling and bind with beaten eggs.
4. Roll the chicken with the skin outward, cover each roll with its slice of bacon, wrap each one in its piece of linen cloth, tie by the ends and around.
5. Place to cook in a braise moistened with a little broth and white wine.
6. When cooked, remove, untie, dry, trim, and serve with ham essence sauce or any other sauce that seems better. They can also be served cold as an entrée dish.
