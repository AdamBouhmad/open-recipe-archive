## Ingredients

- pork head
- sage
- thyme
- marjoram
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- ginger
- strong vinegar
- whole spices

## Directions

1. Cook a pork head very well with sage, thyme, marjoram, cinnamon, clove, pepper and ginger, all whole
2. After cooking, remove the bones while still hot and wrap it in thick cloth in the shape of cheese
3. Keep it in a very tight press for twenty-four hours
4. Cut into long slices and place them in very strong vinegar with whole spices
5. Keep in this pickle for as long as desired
