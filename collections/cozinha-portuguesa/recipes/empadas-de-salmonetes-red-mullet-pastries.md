## Ingredients

- red mullets
- olive oil
- vinegar
- seasonings, ground
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the red mullets in a sauce of olive oil and vinegar with all the seasonings ground, for the space of two hours
2. While this happens, work the dough and make the pastries the size of the red mullets with pepper underneath and on top
3. After cooked, send them to the table
