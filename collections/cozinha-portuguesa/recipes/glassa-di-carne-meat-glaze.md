## Ingredients

- three or four arratels of veal
- a piece of ham
- simple broth, as much as needed
- a pinch of salt
- fine herbs
- one onion
- two or three cloves

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into three or four pieces and place in a bowl with a piece of ham, sear it, add as much simple broth as needed.
2. Season only with a pinch of salt and fine herbs, and one onion with two or three cloves, and let it simmer little by little.
3. When the meat is cooked, pass the broth through a sieve.
4. Return to heat until it reduces, taking care that it does not burn.
5. When it begins to stick, add half a spoon of broth and stir with a wooden spoon.
6. When it is dissolved and thickened to the necessary point and with good color, serve for all game dishes, gratins, fillets, and everything desired.
