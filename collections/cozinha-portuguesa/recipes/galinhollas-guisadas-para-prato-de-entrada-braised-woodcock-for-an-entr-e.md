## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Bacon
- Egg yolks
- Parsley
- Chopped scallion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thread for tying
- Bacon slices
- Buttered paper

## Directions

1. After plucking and singeing the woodcock, open it along the back to remove the entrails and livers
2. Chop the livers and add scraped bacon, two egg yolks, parsley, chopped scallion, season with salt and pepper
3. Stuff the woodcock with this mixture
4. After stuffing and sewing with thread so the chopped filling does not come out
5. Turn it upside down with its own beak
6. Cover with bacon slices and buttered paper
7. Place to roast
8. After roasting, remove the paper and bacon
9. Serve with the sauce or garnish that seems appropriate, such as olives, anchovies, capers, etc.
