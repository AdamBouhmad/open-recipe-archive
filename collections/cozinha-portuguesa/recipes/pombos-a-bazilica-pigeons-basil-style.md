## Ingredients

- half a dozen pigeons
- egg
- fine breadcrumbs
- pork lard
- fried sauce

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen pigeons and after scalding and cleaning them, straighten them with legs inside
2. Stuff them with fine forcemeat and cook in a white braise
3. When cooked and cold, pass through beaten egg, cover twice with fine breadcrumbs
4. Fry in pork lard and serve garnished with fried sauce
