## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 flounder fillets
- salt
- pepper
- wine
- white vinegar
- onion slices
- garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- marjoram
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- pork butter
- fried sauce

## Directions

1. Marinate 10 or 12 flounder fillets in salt, pepper, wine, white vinegar, onion slices, garlic, bay leaf, thyme, and marjoram
2. After these have taken on the flavor of the marinade, place on a cloth and dry them
3. Pass 2 or 3 times through beaten eggs covered with breadcrumbs
4. Fry in pork butter
5. When fried, serve garnished with fried sauce
