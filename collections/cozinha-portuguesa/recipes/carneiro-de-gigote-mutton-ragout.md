## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton
- one frying pan
- wine
- vinegar
- pepper
- clove
- ginger
- nutmeg
- half arratel of bacon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place two arratels of mutton to roast with a frying pan underneath to catch the drippings
2. Add to the drippings a splash of wine, splash of vinegar, pepper, clove, ginger, and nutmeg
3. When the mutton is roasted, squeeze it over the drippings so all substance falls in
4. Chop the mutton with a knife
5. Melt half arratel of bacon and remove the cracklings
6. Mix with the mutton drippings
7. Put everything together to boil with the mutton and season again as necessary
8. Place on plate and send to table with lemon juice on top
9. Optional: poach some eggs to serve with it
