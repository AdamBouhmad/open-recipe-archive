## Ingredients

- fifteen egg yolks
- one arratel of sugar at high thread stage
- tender pastry dough the size of a cruzado
- sugar at thread stage

## Directions

1. Pour fifteen egg yolks into one arratel of sugar at high thread stage and mix very well until they become thick.
2. Make small boxes of tender dough the size of a cruzado and fill them with the eggs.
3. Bake them and pass through sugar at thread stage.
4. Send to the table.
