## Ingredients

- shoulder of kid goat quarter
- water
- butter
- herbs bundle
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot water or broth
- white truffles
- artichoke stems
- egg yolks
- milk
- parsley, chopped
- garlic
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- peas (optional)
- cullis (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the shoulder blade from a quarter of kid goat and cut the meat into pieces the width of three fingers, as well as the breast and ribs
2. Place everything in cold water and then blanch in hot water
3. When blanched and trimmed, place the pieces in a casserole with butter, a bundle of herbs, salt, and pepper
4. Place on the fire and dust with flour
5. Moisten with hot water or broth and add white truffles and half a dozen artichoke stems
6. When everything is cooked and the sauce is reduced, remove the fat and bind with five or six egg yolks mixed in milk, chopped parsley, a bit of garlic, and nutmeg
7. When bound and of good taste, squeeze the juice of a lemon and serve hot
8. It may also be made brown by moistening with substance and binding with a little cullis
9. If desired, green peas may be added, bound with egg yolks or a little cullis
