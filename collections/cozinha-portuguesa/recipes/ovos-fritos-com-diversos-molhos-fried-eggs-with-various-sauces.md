## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter for meat days
- cow butter for fish days

## Directions

1. For meat days, fry in butter; for fish days fry in cow butter, both melted and well hot
2. Fry the eggs so they remain round and cooked in such a way that the yolk is not hard
3. Serve with the same sauces and ragús mentioned for the previously mentioned eggs
