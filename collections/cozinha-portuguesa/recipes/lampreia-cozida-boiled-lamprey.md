## Ingredients

- one lamprey
- wine
- olive oil
- vinegar
- juice of two sour oranges
- garlic leeks
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- water

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare a lamprey as described above
2. Put a splash of wine and another of olive oil, vinegar and juice of two sour oranges in a pan
3. Let this mixture stay while six garlic leeks are sautéed with a splash of olive oil
4. When the leeks are half sautéed, put the whole lamprey inside
5. Cover it with water and cook
6. When almost cooked, add the sauce in which it was in with pepper, clove, and nutmeg
7. When cooked, serve it on slices
