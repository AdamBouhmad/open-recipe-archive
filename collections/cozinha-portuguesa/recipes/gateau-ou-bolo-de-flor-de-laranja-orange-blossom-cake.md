## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar clarified and reduced to hair point
- half a pound of orange blossoms
- sugar powder
- egg white

## Directions

1. Clarified and strained two pounds of sugar reduced to hair point so that these stick to one another, add to it half a pound of orange blossom and allow it to boil until the sugar returns to the same point
2. Then remove from heat, stir very well with a wooden spoon, both in the middle and on the sides, until the sugar begins to rise
3. Then add at the same instant a spoonful of fine sugar dissolved in an egg white, and stirring always with haste, pour into paper boxes the height of three fingers, sustaining them on the bottom of the basin so that the heat does not make it fall
4. Once set and cool, remove them from the molds and serve as desired
5. Cakes of violet blossoms are also made in the same manner
