## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- flour
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Clean the small fish well and wash them thoroughly
2. Season with finely ground salt, dry them, flour them, and fry them in very hot olive oil over a brisk fire
3. Once fried, serve with fried parsley in a circle
4. In the same way all small fish are served, both from the sea and from the rivers
