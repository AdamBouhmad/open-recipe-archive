## Ingredients

- Quails or rails
- Olive oil
- Melted bacon
- Fine herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine breadcrumbs
- Shallot sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the quails or rails very well with legs folded inside the body, open along the sides, beat with the flat of a knife
2. Pass them in a casserole for an instant over the fire with olive oil, melted bacon, and all kinds of fine herbs, salt, and pepper
3. Let them cool, and after cooling cover with fine breadcrumbs, baste with what remains in the casserole
4. Put them to roast on the grill, and after roasting serve with shallot sauce and lemon juice
