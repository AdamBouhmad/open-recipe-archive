## Ingredients

- Rice
- Vegetable broth
- Vegetables
- Celery
- Salsify root
- A piece of butter
- Two spoonfuls of onion substance

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in three or four waters
2. Cook it in a vegetable broth with legumes, arranging so that one vegetable does not cover another, especially the celery and salsify root
3. When half cooked, add a piece of butter and two spoonfuls of onion substance
4. Let it simmer little by little until the rice takes on enough color
5. When cooked and tasting good, serve with broth that is neither very thick nor very thin
