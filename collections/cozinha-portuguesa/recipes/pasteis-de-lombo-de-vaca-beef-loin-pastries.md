## Ingredients

- One preserved beef loin, half-roasted
- Half an arratel of chopped bacon
- Pepper
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Lemon

## Directions

1. When the beef loin is more than half roasted, brown it in half an arratel of bacon in slices
2. When browned, season with pepper, clove, nutmeg, and lemon
3. Make pastry boxes as for common pastries, cook with the meat inside, and serve
