## Ingredients

- one or two loins of veal
- raw minced meat made of veal or chicken or chicken livers with bacon
- five or six small boned birds

## Directions

1. Take one or two loins of veal, and after cutting and trimming them, marinate them in the same manner as the above, placing after marinating a little raw minced meat made of veal, or chicken, or also chicken livers with bacon.
2. On top of the minced meat five or six small boned birds that have also been in the marinade for some time.
3. Once the balls are finished being filled with the same minced meat, close them in such form that they remain very round, and put them to cook in the same manner as the above, and serve them with their own sauce or with another whatever seems better.
