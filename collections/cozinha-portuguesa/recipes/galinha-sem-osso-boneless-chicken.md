## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Two arratels of lamb
- Half arratel of bacon
- Aromatics, finely minced
- Butter
- All seasonings
- Eight cooked eggs
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Pluck one chicken dry and while still hot, open it along the back and remove all the meat and bones, leaving only the wing tips for when it is to be assembled.
2. Mince the chicken meat with two arratels of lamb, half of bacon, finely minced aromatics fried in butter.
3. Take it to the chopping block to season with all seasonings.
4. Fill the chicken with the minced mixture and eight cooked eggs.
5. Put it to cook in the bowl on a low flame.
6. When cooked, season it once more with all seasonings.
7. Place it in a plate or skillet in which a cheese soup has already been made.
8. The same is made of young chickens, pigeons, or turkey.
