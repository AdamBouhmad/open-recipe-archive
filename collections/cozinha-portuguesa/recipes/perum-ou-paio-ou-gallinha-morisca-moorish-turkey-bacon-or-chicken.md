## Ingredients

- one turkey
- one arratel of bacon
- mutton broth
- half a dozen walnuts, crushed
- a small amount of mustard
- a slice of grated bread
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Take one turkey and braise it in quarters in one arratel of bacon
2. Once braised, place it in a frying pan over slices with the same bacon
3. Make a sauce outside of mutton broth with half a dozen crushed walnuts and a small amount of mustard made with a slice of grated bread
4. When this sauce boils, season it with all the seasonings and pour it over the turkey quarters placed in the frying pan, thus brown it and send it to the table
