## Ingredients

- ordinary dough
- butter
- a dozen apples, sliced
- grated sugar
- eggs, beaten
- string or cloth
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Spread a piece of ordinary dough on a buttered cloth
2. Arrange a dozen apples sliced in layers with grated sugar
3. Fold the edges of the dough and unite with beaten eggs
4. Tie with string or in a cloth and place in boiling water
5. Continue boiling for two hours
6. Drain and serve sprinkled with sugar and butter sauce on top
7. Note: puddings of quinces, pears, peaches, apricots, cherries, and all kinds of fruits are made the same way
