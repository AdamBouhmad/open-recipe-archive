## Ingredients

- flour
- fine sugar
- a piece of butter
- two eggs
- a piece of lemon peel, grated

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of flour and fine sugar, a piece of butter, two eggs, and a piece of grated lemon peel, knead with hot water so it is not too soft
2. Let it rest, and it serves for whatever is needed
