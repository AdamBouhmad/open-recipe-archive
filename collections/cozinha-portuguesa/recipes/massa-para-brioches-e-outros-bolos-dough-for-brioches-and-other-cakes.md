## Ingredients

- half an ounce of bread ferment or yeast
- half an eighth of flour
- one eighth of flour
- eight eggs
- one arratel of butter
- half an ounce of salt
- a little water

## Directions

1. Knead with hot water half an ounce of bread ferment or yeast and half an eighth of flour
2. When well bound, wrap it in a cloth, place in a warm place, and let it ferment a quarter of an hour if it is in summer, and one hour if in winter
3. Then place it on a table with one eighth of flour, eight eggs, one arratel of butter, half an ounce of salt, and a little water, and knead everything together
4. With the palm of the hand two or three times, sprinkle with flour, wrap it again in the cloth, and let it ferment nine or ten hours
5. Take out the dough, cut it into pieces, mold them with the hand, flatten them, brush them with beaten eggs, put them in the oven to bake
6. If they are small, only for half an hour, and if they are large, an hour and a half
7. These cakes are eaten hot or however you wish
