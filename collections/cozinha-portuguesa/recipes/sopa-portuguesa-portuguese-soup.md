## Ingredients

- parsley, cilantro, and mint bundle
- bread
- quarter of potatoes
- beef broth made from beef, bacon, ham, partridge, handful of grains, two cabbage sprouts, two turnips, head of garlic, and onion studded with cloves
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. Place in the bottom of a pot a bundle made of parsley, cilantro, and mint
2. Cut a loaf of bread in half and cut into pieces
3. Arrange in the pot with crusts facing down and a quarter of potatoes
4. Cover with broth made from beef, bacon, ham, a partridge, a handful of grains, two cabbage sprouts, two turnips, head of garlic, and an onion studded with cloves
5. Bring to boil for a moment over some coals with the pot covered
6. When cooked, serve from top to bottom onto a deep plate or in a soup tureen
7. Serve garnished around with the bacon and ham and in the middle the cabbage sprouts with six poached eggs
