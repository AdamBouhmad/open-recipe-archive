## Ingredients

- piece of beef from the point of the roast
- bread crusts fried
- carcasses of partridges, capons, or other game birds
- veal stock base
- salt
- marjoram
- piece of lime peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast a piece of beef from the point of the roast in the spit
2. When well roasted and browned, while still hot, pound it in a mortar with some fried bread crusts and carcasses of partridges, capons, or any other available game birds
3. Moisten from time to time with a little broth
4. After everything is well pounded, dissolve it in veal stock base and good broth
5. Season with salt if necessary
6. Add a little marjoram and a piece of lime peel
7. Give it four boils
8. Strain through a sieve
9. Heat again and serve as stated, over bread crusts with lemon juice
