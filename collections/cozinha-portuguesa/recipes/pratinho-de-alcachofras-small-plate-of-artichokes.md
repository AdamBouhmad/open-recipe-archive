## Ingredients

- artichokes
- butter
- herbs
- vinegar
- seasonings
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- lemon

## Directions

1. Scald the artichokes and cut the tips and feet of them
2. Sauté them in butter with herbs, vinegar, and all the seasonings
3. Bind them with egg yolks
4. Serve at table with cinnamon and lemon on top
