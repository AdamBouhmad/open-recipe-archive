## Ingredients

- ragout or cream
- beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- butter or melted lard

## Directions

1. Make bundles from various ragouts, white or brown, or also from creams such as ragouts of truffles, sweetbreads, mushrooms, peas, asparagus tips, broad beans, spinach, sorrel, and various shellfish such as oysters, mussels, and shrimp
2. When well finished and of good taste, mix with beaten eggs
3. Season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
4. Form bundles with well-heated butter or melted lard
5. After cooking on both sides, serve with the same ragout they were made with or with any other that is more convenient
