## Ingredients

- prepared pigeons
- bacon
- ham
- parsley
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- clove-studded onion
- broth
- veal glaze

## Directions

1. Prepare the pigeons by singing, tucking legs inside, flattening between cloths with the knife blade, then turning over and cleaning
2. Lard with fine bacon and soak briefly in cold water, then retighten in hot water
3. Place between bacon sheets, a slice of ham, a sprig of parsley, bay leaf, marjoram, clove-studded onion, and a little broth
4. Simmer slowly and gently until cooked, strain the broth through a sieve, remove the fat, add to a veal glaze
5. After reducing and achieving good color, glaze the pigeons and serve with the sauce or vegetable garnish desired
