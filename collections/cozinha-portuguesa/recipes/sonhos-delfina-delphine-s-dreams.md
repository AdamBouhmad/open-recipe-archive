## Ingredients

- half sugar
- half flour
- orange flower
- a piece of lemon rind
- egg whites
- clarified butter
- sugar for glazing

## Directions

1. Make a dough with half sugar and half flour, orange flower, a piece of lemon rind, and egg whites
2. After everything is mixed and kneaded, stretch it with bread, cut it, and make it into equal designs as you prefer
3. Fry in clarified butter
4. After frying, glaze with grated sugar and serve hot with a bread paddle
