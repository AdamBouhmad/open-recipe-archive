## Ingredients

- one arratel of almonds cut in half
- one arratel of finely ground almonds
- two and a half arratels of stone sugar

## Directions

1. Add one arratel of almonds cut in half and one arratel of finely ground almonds to two and a half arratels of stone sugar
2. After two turns over the fire, remove and beat very well until it rises
3. Place on stone or in a buttered basin, stirring with it so it loses its gloss
4. Cut into slices and serve at table
