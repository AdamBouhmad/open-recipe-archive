## Ingredients

- Flour
- Beer or white wine
- A drop of olive oil or a piece of clarified butter
- Salt
- Egg whites
- Strawberries
- Pork lard
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a batter of flour mixed with beer or white wine, a drop of olive oil or a piece of clarified butter, and salt
2. When about to fry, add two or three well-beaten egg whites
3. Pass some strawberries through it and fry in pork lard
4. When fried, serve hot dusted with sugar
