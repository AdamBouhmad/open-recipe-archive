## Ingredients

- soles
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- garlic clove
- bay leaves
- thyme
- marjoram
- red wine
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Clean, trim, and wash the soles, cut them into equal portions
2. Put in a pan with a piece of washed butter dusted with flour
3. Season with salt, pepper, a garlic clove, a bundle of bay leaf with sprigs of thyme, a bay leaf, and sprigs of marjoram
4. Add a quarter-pot of good red wine and put to simmer slowly
5. When cooked and the sauce is reduced, remove the herb bundle and serve with sour orange sauce
