## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the quinces without seeds or core and weigh four arratels of them
2. Place to cook in two arratels of sugar until thick
3. Once thick, place everything in a cloth and squeeze into the same pan, pressing with a press
4. Place the pan with the strained liquid back on the fire to cook until thicker, at which point the jelly is done
5. Remove from the fire and add fragrances if desired
6. Place in glasses if it is fine
