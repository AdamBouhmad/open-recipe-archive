## Ingredients

- pigeons
- milk
- fine herbs
- fine breadcrumbs
- remoulade sauce
- egg
- fried sauce

## Directions

1. Cut pigeons in half, flatten them like chickens in a marinade with milk and fine herbs
2. After cooking, cover with fine breadcrumbs, place on grills and serve with hot remoulade sauce
3. If you want to serve them fried, pass them through beaten egg, cover with breadcrumbs, and after frying, serve on a folded napkin, garnished with fried sauce
