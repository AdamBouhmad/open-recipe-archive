## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- streaky pork belly
- substance broth
- cullis of veal and ham

## Directions

1. Serve the cabbage in water with streaky pork belly
2. After parboiling, pass it to cold water, squeeze it, and tie it with twine
3. Leave it in a pot with substance broth and a spoonful of braising liquid with some fat
4. Let it simmer slowly
5. When cooked, arrange the partridges at the bottom of the terrine with the cabbage and streaky pork belly interspersed and in pieces on top
6. Add a cullis of veal and ham well finished
7. Serve hot
