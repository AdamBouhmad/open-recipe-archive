## Ingredients

- One leg of lamb
- Minced lamb
- Seasonings

## Directions

1. Prepare the leg of lamb as for armed leg, as described above, with the only difference being that to stuff it, the meat must be carefully removed from one part of the leg, and through the same opening it must be stuffed with the same minced and seasoned meat.
