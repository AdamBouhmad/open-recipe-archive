## Ingredients

- milk
- egg yolks
- almond biscuits
- orange flower
- two ounces of chocolate

## Directions

1. Heat the milk, seasoned, and strain with egg yolks as mentioned in the preceding article
2. Add to it almond biscuits, orange flower, two ounces of chocolate, all ground, reduced to powder, and well mixed
3. Pour into deep porcelain cups
4. Cook carefully as the preceding one
5. Serve as the others
