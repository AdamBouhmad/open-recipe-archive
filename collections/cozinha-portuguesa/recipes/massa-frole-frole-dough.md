## Ingredients

- flour
- fine sugar
- egg yolks
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Take equal amounts of flour and fine sugar, knead them with egg yolks and fresh butter
2. When formed, stretch it with the dough roller, cut it into pieces as seems fit, and put it to bake in a mild oven
3. When baked, glaze it with sugar and a hot roller, and serve for various side dishes
