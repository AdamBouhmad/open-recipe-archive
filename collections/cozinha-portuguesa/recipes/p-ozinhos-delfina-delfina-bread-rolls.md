## Ingredients

- a dozen rolls the size of an apple
- milk
- sugar
- cream
- egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon zest
- conserved orange flower, finely chopped
- clarified butter
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the top crust from a dozen rolls the size of an apple
2. Open them from the bottom, remove all the crumb from them
3. Dip them for a few moments in milk and sugar
4. Make a filling of cream, egg yolks, sugar, lemon zest, and finely chopped conserved orange flower
5. Stuff the rolls with this
6. Place in each one the piece of crust that was removed
7. Tie them in a cross with string
8. Fry them in clarified butter
9. When fried, remove the string
10. Dust them with fine sugar
11. Pass a hot spatula over them
12. After being glazed and well colored, serve them hot as a side dish
