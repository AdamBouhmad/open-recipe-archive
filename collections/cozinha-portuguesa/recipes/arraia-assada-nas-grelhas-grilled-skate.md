## Ingredients

- skate halves
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. After parboiling, skinning, and cleaning two skate halves
2. In a pan, melt a piece of butter
3. Place the skate inside, season with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
4. Remove, cover with fine breadcrumbs
5. Place on grill to roast, basting occasionally
6. When roasted on both sides and golden in color, serve
