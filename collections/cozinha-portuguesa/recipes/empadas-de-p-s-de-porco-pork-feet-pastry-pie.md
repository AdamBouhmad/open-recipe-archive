## Ingredients

- Pork feet, well cooked
- Sugar (optional, for sweet preparation)
- Spices

## Directions

1. Cook the pork feet very well, remove the bones
2. If sweet is desired, pass them through sugar at the right consistency
3. Season with spices and after cooling, place each foot in its own pastry pie, divided in half, and send to the table
