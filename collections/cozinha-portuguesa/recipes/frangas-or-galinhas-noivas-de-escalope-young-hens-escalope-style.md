## Ingredients

- 4 young hens
- olive oil
- melted pork fat
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- shallots
- garlic clove
- ham slices
- bacon slices
- a little substance (broth)
- a drop of white wine
- a little cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take 4 young hens and remove the breasts
2. Cut the breasts into thin and equal fillets
3. Marinate them in olive oil and melted pork fat with little salt, pepper, parsley, shallots, and a garlic clove, all finely chopped
4. Cover the bottom of a cassole with thin slices of ham
5. Arrange the breast fillets with their marinade on top
6. Cover with bacon slices and cook between two fires for a little while
7. Transfer the fillets to another cassole and add to the braising liquid a little substance, a drop of white wine, and a little cullis
8. Let it boil for a little time
9. When reduced, pass through a sieve and remove all the fat
10. Put the hen fillets inside, only to heat through
11. Serve with lemon juice
