## Ingredients

- fresh eggs
- beaten eggs
- bread
- grated Parmesan cheese
- butter or pork fat, very hot
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Poach eggs in water and keep them fresh
2. Trim them and let them cool
3. Pass twice through beaten eggs, bread, and grated Parmesan
4. Fry in very hot butter or pork fat
5. Remove before they harden
6. Serve garnished with fried parsley
