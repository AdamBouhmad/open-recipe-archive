## Ingredients

- lamb quarter
- veal mince
- butter
- salt
- onion
- pepper
- beef fat
- bacon
- water
- bread crumb
- milk
- egg yolks
- spinach ragú, cucumber ragú, peas ragú, sorrel ragú, or other ragú of choice

## Directions

1. Place the lamb quarter on a clean cloth and remove the meat in pieces from the inside
2. Pass the meat pieces through the fire with a piece of butter, salt, onion, and pepper
3. Add some beef fat and some pieces of bacon soaked in water
4. After everything is cooked, make a mince and grind it in a stone mortar
5. Add a piece of bone marrow mixed with milk and bind everything with five or six egg yolks
6. Stuff the lamb quarter with this mixture, shaping it as desired
7. Tie it with twine or string and place it to cook in a braise
8. When cooked and well drained of its fat, serve with a ragú of spinach, cucumbers, peas, sorrels, or another ragú of choice
