## Ingredients

- one eighth of flour
- hot water
- pork butter
- salt
- two or three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Knead one eighth of flour with hot water, pork butter, pounded salt, and two or three egg yolks, but do not let it be soft
2. When kneaded, let it rest a little while
3. Stretch it with the roller until it becomes thin
4. Cut it in two or three equal pieces, spread pork butter on top while hot, make some well-closed rolls, and after being cut across and rounded, it will serve for tarts, pastries, and whatever is necessary
