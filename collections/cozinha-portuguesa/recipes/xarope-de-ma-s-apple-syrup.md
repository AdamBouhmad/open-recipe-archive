## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut a dozen apples into thin pieces.
2. Boil them in a little water.
3. When cooked, pour into a strong cloth and squeeze very well.
4. Let the juice settle, then take the clear part.
5. For each quart of it, add one pound of sugar in the hair stage.
6. Let it boil until it takes the consistency of a syrup.
7. Then let it cool and guard as stated.
