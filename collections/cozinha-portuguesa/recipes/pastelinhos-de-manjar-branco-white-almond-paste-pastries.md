## Ingredients

- a dozen portions of white almond paste
- six egg yolks
- flower water
- pastille sugar

## Directions

1. Beat a dozen portions of white almond paste with six egg yolks and flower water.
2. Once well beaten, make small boxes (as mentioned above) covered with pastille sugar on top and brown them.
3. Send to the table.
4. They can also be made from cold dough in the shape of a half moon.
5. In the same way they can be made with royal almond paste or cooked cream.
