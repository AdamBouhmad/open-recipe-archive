## Ingredients

- Artichokes, cut into quarters
- Ham essence or ham broth
- Cullis
- Flour
- Broth
- Egg yolks
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut artichokes into quarters, trim them, blanch them, and cook them in a light braise
2. When cooked, place them in a ham essence
3. Give them four boilings
4. Bind them with a little cullis and serve for garnishes of first courses
5. They can also be made white, dusted with flour, cooked in broth, and bound with egg yolks and milk, serving in this way for side dishes
