## Ingredients

- 1 young hen
- water
- vinegar
- a piece of butter dusted with flour
- salt
- pepper
- onion rings
- parsley
- 2 or 3 cloves
- 2 or 3 garlic cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- marjoram
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Take a young hen that is very tender, remove its skin and cut it into pieces
2. Heat water and vinegar with a piece of butter dusted with flour, salt, pepper, onion rings, parsley, cloves, garlic cloves, bay leaves, and marjoram over the stove, stirring until the butter melts
3. Once the butter is melted, add the cut hen pieces and let them take on the flavor of the marinade
4. Remove from the marinade, dry in a white cloth, dust each piece with flour
5. Fry in pork fat and serve with fried parsley rings. Alternatively, they can be passed through egg batter, fried, and served the same way
