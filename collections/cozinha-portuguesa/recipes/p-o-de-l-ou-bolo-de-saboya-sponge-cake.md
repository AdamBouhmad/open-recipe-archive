## Ingredients

- Twenty egg yolks
- Fine sugar, weight equal to the eggs
- Grated lemon peel
- Conserved orange flower
- Egg whites
- Flour, weight equal to half the eggs
- Clarified butter

## Directions

1. Place twenty egg yolks and fine sugar equal to the weight of the eggs in a bowl, along with grated lemon peel and conserved orange flower
2. Beat this for half an hour with two spoons
3. Then add the well-beaten egg whites, and after mixing well with the yolks, gradually add, carefully, flour equal to half the weight of the eggs
4. Pour everything into a mold of any desired shape, first greased inside with clarified butter, and bake in a moderately heated oven for an hour and a half to two hours
5. When baked and with good color, serve as is, or if desired, cover with a glaze made of fine sugar, egg white, and lemon juice
