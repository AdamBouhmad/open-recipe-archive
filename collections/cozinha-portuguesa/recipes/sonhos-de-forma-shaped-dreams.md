## Ingredients

- a little flour
- milk
- two eggs
- a little Madeira wine
- clarified butter
- fine sugar
- grated lemon peel
- salt
- ground cinnamon
- oil or melted butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve a little flour in milk, and add to it two eggs, a little Madeira wine, clarified butter, fine sugar, grated lemon peel, salt, and ground cinnamon
2. After everything is mixed, and in the form of a thumb heat the mold which should be of iron in hot oil or butter, in which they are to be fried, dip it in the dough, and put it immediately in the frying pan, and when it slips off return to the dough, and continue thus to the end
3. After they are fried and glazed with sugar and a hot spatula, serve as the preceding ones
