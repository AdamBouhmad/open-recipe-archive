## Ingredients

- One duck
- Thick bacon for larding
- Melted bacon
- A piece of ham
- Two arratels of cow meat
- Broth and hot water
- Salt
- One onion studded with cloves
- One head of garlic
- One cabbage

## Directions

1. Clean one duck, lard it with thick bacon, sauté it in a casserole with melted bacon, a piece of ham, and two arratels of cow meat
2. When sautéed, add a little broth and a splash of hot water
3. Season with salt, one clove-studded onion, and one head of garlic
4. Let it simmer little by little
5. When half-cooked, blanch a cabbage in boiling water, squeeze it, tie it with twine, and finish cooking everything together
6. When everything is cooked, pass the broth through a sieve
7. Add some bread crusts and a little substance, and place to boil a little
8. Then remove the fat, garnish with the cabbage and the duck in the middle, and serve
9. One can also make soups of cabbage or Lombard cabbage with partridges, pigeons, ducks, or teals
