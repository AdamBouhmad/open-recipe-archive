## Ingredients

- Five or six cucumbers
- Minced meat with cream
- Broth
- Salt pork slices
- Onion
- Fine herbs
- Ham essence
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Halve and remove the seeds from five or six cucumbers
2. Blanch them and stuff with minced meat with cream, well bound
3. Place to cook in broth with salt pork slices, onion in rounds, and fine herbs
4. When cooked, place to drain in a sieve
5. Serve with ham essence and lemon juice for a course between meals
