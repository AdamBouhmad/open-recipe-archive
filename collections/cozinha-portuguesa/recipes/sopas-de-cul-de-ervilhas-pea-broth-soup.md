## Ingredients

- One or two eighths measure of green peas
- Tails of small onions
- Some sprigs of parsley
- Broth
- Melted bacon
- Substance made of slices of veal, a piece of ham, and two roots

## Directions

1. Pick one or two eighths measure of green peas, tails of small onions, some sprigs of parsley and clean them all in hot water
2. Sauté them afterwards in melted bacon
3. Add a little broth and let them cook
4. When cooked and mashed, dissolve them in a casserole with two spoonfuls of substance made of slices of veal, a piece of ham, and two roots
5. Then pass through a fine sieve
6. When of good taste and hot, serve over bread crusts boiled as mentioned, with small young chickens or ducks in the middle
