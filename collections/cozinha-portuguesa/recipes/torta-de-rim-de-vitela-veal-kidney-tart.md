## Ingredients

- one thumb-sized piece of veal
- veal kidney with all its fat
- bound cream
- biscuits of almonds
- spoonfuls of orange zest
- spoonfuls of citron zest
- spoonfuls of lime zest
- six egg yolks
- sugar
- egg whites beaten to snow

## Directions

1. Take a thumb-sized piece of veal, remove the kidney with all its fat, allow it to cool, and then chop it very finely
2. Once chopped, pound it with an equal amount of bound cream, biscuits of almonds, spoonfuls of orange zest, citron zest, and lime zest, all chopped finely
3. After everything is pounded, add six egg yolks, sugar, and the egg whites beaten to snow
4. Place a piece of puff pastry on a tart pan, form its border the width of two fingers with the same pastry, place the composition in the middle, and put it in the oven to bake
5. When baked and browned, pour a little sugar on top and serve hot
