## Ingredients

- a little bit of restored sauce or clear essence
- pickled shallot
- capers, minced
- six rocamboles
- minced pimiento
- nutmeg, grated
- a piece of butter
- powdered flour
- lemon juice or verjuice

## Directions

1. Put on the fire a pan with a little bit of restored sauce or clear essence, pickled shallot, minced capers, six rocamboles, minced pimiento, grated nutmeg, a piece of butter, and powdered flour, and stir until it is bound; and tasting good, serve it for whatever is desired with lemon juice or verjuice.
