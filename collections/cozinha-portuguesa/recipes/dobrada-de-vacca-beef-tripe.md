## Ingredients

- Beef tripe
- Water
- Pork belly
- Parsley
- Herbs
- Lemon
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef tripe that is tender and very white, and after scalding it well, place it to cook in plain water with a slice of pork belly, seasoned with salt, a bunch of parsley, fine herbs, and slices of peeled lemon
2. When cooked and almost cold, cut it into equal fillets and serve as white fricassee with egg yolks and lemon juice
3. It can also be served à l'Ancienne by placing the fillets in a little sauce with a piece of washed butter
4. Pies, tarts, and timbales can also be made from it
5. It is eaten well in its natural state after cooking, hot, with a sauce made of broth, vinegar, finely chopped parsley, seasoned with salt and pepper
6. It can also be eaten roasted on grills with piquant sauce after being passed through butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs; however in whatever way it is made, it is always cut into fillets as said at the beginning
