## Ingredients

- Two twenty-reis loaves of bread
- Broth and substance
- Five or two breasts of partridges, chopped
- Parmesan cheese
- Alternatively: breasts of capons, hens, peacocks, partridges, or any other birds

## Directions

1. After toasting two twenty-reis loaves of bread, cut them in two parts
2. Remove all the crumb, dry them at the fire
3. Moisten them with broth and substance, and place them to boil for an instant
4. When almost dried, place on top five or two chopped partridge breasts covered with Parmesan cheese
5. Place in the oven to brown, or brown with a hot spatula
6. After browning, serve with two or three spoonfuls of flavorful, good-colored broth
7. In place of chopped partridge, one can use breasts of capons, hens, peacocks, partridges, or any other birds one wishes
