## Ingredients

- bread with crumb
- clarified butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the crumb of bread into thin slices
2. Toast on both sides on grills
3. When browned, arrange on a plate
4. Pass briefly through boiling sugar
5. Add fresh whole egg yolks
6. When cooked, place on the slices with sugar in point and powdered cinnamon on top
7. Serve hot
