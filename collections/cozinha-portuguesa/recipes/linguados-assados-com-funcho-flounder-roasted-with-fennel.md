## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 flounders
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- fennel branches
- anchovies
- browned butter
- a drop of wine
- half a spoon of cullis

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and wash 2 or 3 flounders
2. Cut off the heads and the tips of the tails
3. Open them along the back
4. Season with a little salt and pepper
5. Pass them through melted butter
6. Place them to roast slowly on grills with fennel branches underneath
7. When roasted on both sides, serve with a sauce of anchovies dissolved in browned butter, a drop of wine, and half a spoon of cullis
