## Ingredients

- one eighth of flour
- water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Place on the table one eighth of flour, knead it with water and a little salt, in such a way that after being bound it be neither soft nor hard
2. Knead and let it rest half an hour
3. Afterwards stretch it with a roller until it reaches the thickness of a finger
4. Place on top of it an equal amount of butter as there is dough, and roll it very well so that the butter remains enclosed inside
5. Stretch it with the roller, continue doing the same three or four times
6. Finally reduce it to the thickness that seems necessary, and it will serve for meat tarts, sweet tarts, small pastries, timbales, fried dishes, and other oven pieces
