## Ingredients

- tender veal meat
- veal lard
- pork lard
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Make them in the same way as the preceding sausages
2. With the difference that instead of pork meat, use tender veal meat with an equal amount of pork fat and a piece of pork lard
3. Season and finish as pork sausages
4. Serve them fried in the same way
5. They can also be made with breasts of capons, partridges, turkeys, and any kind of meat or game you wish
