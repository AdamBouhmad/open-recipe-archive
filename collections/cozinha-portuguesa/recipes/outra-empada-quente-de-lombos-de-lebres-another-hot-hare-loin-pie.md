## Ingredients

- All the skins and meat from hare loins
- Half an arratel of scraped bacon, seasoned with little salt, pepper, some spices, sausage, shallots, truffles, all finely chopped
- Half an arratel of bacon, cut into small cubes
- Another half arratel of ham, cut into small cubes
- A quartilho of milk
- Six egg yolks
- A spoonful of breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove all skins and meat from the hare loins and make a chopped mixture with half an arratel of scraped bacon seasoned with little salt, pepper, some spices, sausage, shallots, truffles, all finely chopped
2. Put in a bowl with half an arratel of bacon and another half arratel of ham, both cut into small cubes, a quartilho of milk, and six egg yolks, and mix all with a spoonful of breadcrumbs
3. After mixing well, fill the pie box, cover with bacon slices, and finish the pie
4. Put in the oven to cook for four or five hours
5. When cooked, open it, drain the fat
6. Serve with good sauce and lime juice
7. If making for a larger dish, you can add veal loin, mutton leg, or veal, etc.
