## Ingredients

- one large sole
- broth or court-bouillon
- sauce à la Hollandaise or oil, vinegar, pepper, and salt

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and trim a large sole
2. Make an incision along the back from head to tail
3. Put it to cook in a broth or court-bouillon like other fish, letting it simmer slowly
4. When cooked, serve with sauce à la Hollandaise, or when that is not available, eat with sauce of good oil, vinegar, pepper, and salt
