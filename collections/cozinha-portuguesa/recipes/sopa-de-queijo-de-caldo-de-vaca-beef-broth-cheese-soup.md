## Ingredients

- beef
- duck
- bread slices
- butter
- beef marrow
- eggs
- herbs

## Directions

1. Cook a duck with beef
2. When cooked, let it cool
3. Slice the bread thickly
4. Place bread slices on a buttered plate
5. Place the duck cut into slices on top of the bread
6. Place other bread slices on top of these slices
7. On top place beef marrow, and continue filling the plate this way
8. When full, pour the broth over it, first poaching six eggs in it
9. Place the eggs to cook
10. Finally, place a layer of all the herbs on top
11. Pour the fat over them so that it takes on the flavor of the herbs
12. This becomes a good plate of this sort
13. Send to table
