## Ingredients

- turkey
- turkey livers
- parsley
- chives
- truffles
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- scraped bacon
- egg yolks
- butter colored with shrimp shells
- shrimp cullis

## Directions

1. Take a mortified young turkey or hen turkey for two or three days
2. Stuff it with a forcemeat made from its own livers, parsley, chives, truffles, mushrooms, seasoned with salt, pepper, and scraped bacon, bound with two egg yolks
3. Tie the turkey leaving the legs extended
4. Gently separate the skin on the breast without breaking it
5. Fill it with butter colored with crushed shrimp shells to give it color
6. Secure the skin at the wing tips
7. Roast on a spit covered with bacon strips and paper leaves
8. When roasted, remove the paper and string, and serve with shrimp cullis sauce and other sauces
