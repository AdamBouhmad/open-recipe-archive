## Ingredients

- fresh hare, not washed
- hare offal
- water
- melted lard, cleaned of cracklings
- herbs, minced
- onion
- toasted bread crumbs the size of an egg
- vinegar
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- good wine
- bread slices
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Skin a fresh hare without washing it and remove the offal, which after cleaning of hairs should be washed of blood
2. Place the hare to braise in a pot with the same water in which the offal was washed of blood, with melted lard cleaned of cracklings, minced herbs, and onion
3. Once cooked, add a piece of toasted bread crumbs (the size of an egg) crumbled in vinegar, with spices, clove, pepper, a little saffron, cumin, and good wine
4. Place on a plate over slices of bread and send to the table with slices and juice of sour lemon
