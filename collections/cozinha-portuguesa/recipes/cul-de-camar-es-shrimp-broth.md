## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onions, roots, and pieces of fish
- Stock
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- Two or three cloves
- Some shrimps
- Some almonds
- Four hard egg yolks
- Two pieces of bread soaked

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with butter, onions, roots, and pieces of fish as for lean broth
2. After it has sweated and begins to stick, moisten with stock and season with one bundle of parsley and fine herbs and two or three cloves
3. Let it simmer for one hour
4. Pound some shrimps with some almonds and four hard egg yolks, and two pieces of soaked bread
5. Dissolve everything in the stock, pass through a sieve
6. Heat it and without letting it boil, use for soups, entrees, terrines, tarts, and for everything that needs shrimp broth
