## Ingredients

- One eighth of flour
- Water
- Salt
- Butter, equal amount to the dough

## Directions

1. Spread on a table one eighth of flour, knead with water and salt, little by little, in such a way that after being bound it is neither soft nor hard
2. Work it and let it rest for half an hour
3. Then spread with a rolling pin until it is the thickness of a finger
4. Spread on top an equal amount of butter as there is dough, roll up very well so that the butter remains enclosed inside
5. Spread with the rolling pin and continue doing the same three or four times
6. Finally reduce to the thickness that seems necessary, and it will serve for meat tarts, sweet tarts, small pastries, timbales, fried pastries, and other puff pastry pieces
