## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Put quinces in boiling water and let them boil until they become soft.
2. Put them in a bowl with cold water, cut them in quarters, clean them inside, remove the skin with care, and put them in sugar.
3. Place on heat and skim very well, letting them simmer little by little.
4. When cooked and the sugar reduced, put them on a plate and serve hot with the syrup poured over.
