## Ingredients

- wild boar loins
- fine lard
- salt
- pepper
- peverada sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the loins along their entire length and trim all the thin membranes that cover them
2. Lard them with fine lard
3. After marinating and roasting, serve with peverada sauce thickened
4. Alternatively, lard them with thick lard well seasoned, as with the leg meat, and make them into pies lined with chopped lard underneath and strips of fine lard on top; serve cold as an intermediate course
