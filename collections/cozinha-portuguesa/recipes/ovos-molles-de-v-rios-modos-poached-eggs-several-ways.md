## Ingredients

- fresh eggs
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- various sauces: meat sauce, ham essence, Robert sauce, roe and capers sauce, white sauce, green sauce
- vegetable ragús: asparagus, lettuce, chicory, celery, spinach, sorrel, truffle ragú, sweetbreads, cucumbers

## Directions

1. Heat a half pot of water with salt and a little vinegar on the heat
2. When boiling, cook fresh eggs in it, breaking them so they remain separate from each other
3. Remove before they harden and pass them to warm water
4. Trim them and serve with various sauces such as meat sauce, ham essence, Robert sauce, roe and capers sauce, white sauce, green sauce, vegetable ragús like asparagus, lettuce, chicory, celery, spinach, sorrel, truffle ragú, sweetbreads, cucumbers, or any other that seems good
