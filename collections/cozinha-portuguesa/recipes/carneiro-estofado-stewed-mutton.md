## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton from the shoulder near the leg
- half arratel of bacon
- two heads of garlic
- one quince, quartered
- two apples
- cinnamon
- pepper
- ginger
- clove
- nutmeg
- two bay leaves
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take two arratels of mutton from the shoulder near the leg, cut in pieces with half arratel of bacon, two heads of garlic, one quince quartered, two apples, cinnamon, pepper, ginger, clove, nutmeg all whole, two bay leaves, a splash of wine, a splash of vinegar, a little water, and necessary salt
2. Put everything in a pot and seal well on the outside so the steam does not escape
3. Place on gentle heat for three hours
4. When done, serve hot at table on silver plates or frying pans with lemon juice on top
