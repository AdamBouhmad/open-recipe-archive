## Ingredients

- Lombos de porco montez (wild boar loins)
- Toucinho fino (fine bacon)
- Molho de peverada ligada (bound pepper sauce)

## Directions

1. Cut the loins along their entire length and trim of all small skins that cover them
2. Lard with fine bacon
3. After marinating and roasting, serve with bound pepper sauce
