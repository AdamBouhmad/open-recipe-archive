## Ingredients

- A piece of butter
- Onions in slices
- Parsnips
- Carrots
- Pieces of fish
- Stock
- One spoon of substance
- Flour browned in butter
- Half a quarter of white wine
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- Some cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter in a casserole, add onions in slices, parsnips, carrots, and pieces of fish
2. Let it sweat little by little
3. When it sticks, add stock and one spoon of substance, and thicken with flour browned in butter
4. Season with half a quarter of white wine, one bundle of parsley and fine herbs, and some cloves of garlic
5. When cooked and of good taste, remove the fat, pass through a sieve, and use for all sauces where necessary
