## Ingredients

- two lettuces
- green parsley
- water
- two cloves of garlic
- one spoonful of broth
- broth
- butter
- three or four egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pound two lettuces and green parsley in a stone mortar
2. Boil in water and pass into a saucepan with two cloves of garlic, one spoonful of broth, and broth
3. Let it boil slowly and gently
4. When reduced, pass through a sieve
5. Add a piece of butter and three or four chopped egg yolks
6. When bound and good tasting, serve with lemon juice in the quantity that seems best
