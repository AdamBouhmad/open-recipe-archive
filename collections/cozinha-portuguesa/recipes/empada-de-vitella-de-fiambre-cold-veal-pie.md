## Ingredients

- Veal loin, choice pieces
- Thick bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Fine herbs
- Lemon juice
- White wine, small amount
- Salsa
- Pastry dough
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Lard the best meat pieces from a veal leg with thick bacon
2. Marinate for three or four hours with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, juice of two lemons, and a little white wine
3. Meanwhile, chop two arráteis of bacon with salsa, and after grinding and seasoning, spread half of the dough
4. Place half the bacon on top of this half of dough, and place the arranged veal pieces on top, well fitted together
5. Cover with the rest of the bacon, and on top place planks of the same
6. Place the pastry lid, ornament it in the best possible way
7. Place in the oven to bake for four or five hours
8. After cooking, let it cool
9. Serve it cold with a napkin underneath
