## Ingredients

- one fish steak of solho weighing two arratels
- one quartilho of wine
- one quartilho of vinegar
- one quartilho of water
- one head of garlic, pounded
- a bunch of oregano
- two bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- olive oil
- lemon juice
- butter or olive oil
- pastry dough

## Directions

1. Cook one fish steak of solho weighing two arratels with one quartilho of wine, one quartilho of vinegar, one quartilho of water, one pounded head of garlic, a bunch of oregano, two bay leaves, salt, and pepper
2. When half cooked, add a splash of olive oil
3. When cooked, let it cool in a pan in which you put a small amount of the broth in which it was cooked, seasoned with all seasonings, lemon juice, and a good splash of olive oil or butter
4. Make a pastry box of one eighth of a loaf
5. Put the molho inside it with the fish
6. Cook it over low heat and serve it
