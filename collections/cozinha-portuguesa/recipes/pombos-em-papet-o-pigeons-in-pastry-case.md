## Ingredients

- bacon slices
- forcemeat
- pigeon ragout with garnishes
- ham essence
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of the pastry case with bacon slices, cover the bottom with a layer of forcemeat, put in the middle a ragout of pigeons with their garnishes
2. Cover with the same forcemeat and bacon slices and cook in the oven
3. When cooked, turn over onto a plate, remove the bacon slices, drain all the fat, arrange on the plate and serve hot with ham essence and lemon juice
