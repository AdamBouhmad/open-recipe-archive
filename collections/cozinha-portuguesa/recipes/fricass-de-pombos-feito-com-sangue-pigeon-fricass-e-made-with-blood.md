## Ingredients

- five or six young pigeons
- pigeon blood
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- a slice of ham
- a sprig of parsley
- an onion studded with cloves
- veal stock
- veal coulis

## Directions

1. Take five or six young pigeons and save their blood, stirring it as it cools so it does not coagulate
2. Prepare the pigeons the same way as those in compote
3. After passing them through the fire in a casserole with melted bacon, salt, pepper, a slice of ham, a sprig of parsley, and an onion studded with cloves, moisten them with stock and thicken with a little veal coulis
4. When cooked, skim off the fat, pass the blood through a sieve, and add it to the fricassée, taking care that it does not boil
5. When bound and of good taste, arrange on a plate and serve hot as an entrance course
