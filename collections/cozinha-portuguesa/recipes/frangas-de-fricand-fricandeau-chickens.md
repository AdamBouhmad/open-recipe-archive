## Ingredients

- one or two young good quality chickens
- pork fat for blanching
- fine pork fat for larding

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare one or two good young chickens and remove the breast meat
2. Season and blanch in pork fat, or if not possible, blanch in a furnace with boiling water
3. Lard with fine pork fat and cook
4. Finish in the same manner as veal fricandeau
5. Serve with a ragout of cucumbers, chicory, or any other ragout that seems appropriate
