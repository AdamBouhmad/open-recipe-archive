## Ingredients

- One chicken, plucked and cleaned
- One strip of bacon, about a palm's length and four fingers' width
- Butter
- Paper
- Twine

## Directions

1. After one chicken is plucked, cleaned, and spitted, take one strip of bacon about a palm's length and four fingers' width, cut it into strips, and place it on the chicken on top and underneath.
2. Wrap the chicken in four or five sheets of paper well buttered and tie it very well with twine in many turns so that it does not come undone.
3. Put the chicken to roast, first moistening the paper on top with water so that it does not burn.
4. After the chicken is roasted, place it on the plate with the same strips of bacon, slices of bread underneath, and send it to the table.
5. In this manner one roasts capon, turkey, guinea fowl, and young chickens.
