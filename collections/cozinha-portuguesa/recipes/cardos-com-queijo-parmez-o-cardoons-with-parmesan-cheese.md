## Ingredients

- Cardoons
- White broth
- Ham essence or béchamel sauce
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Cut the cardoons to desired size
2. Blanch and cook in white broth
3. When cooked, place in sauce of essence or béchamel sauce
4. Arrange on a plate with a little butter and grated cheese underneath
5. Pour the rest of the sauce on top
6. Cover with grated cheese, place to brown in the oven
7. After browning, clean the plate edges and serve for a course between meals
