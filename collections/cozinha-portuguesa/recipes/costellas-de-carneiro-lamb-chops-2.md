## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Butter
- Pork belly
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion
- Parsley
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the chops equally and after being well trimmed and beaten between two cloths with the flat of the knife, moisten them with butter or melted pork belly and season with salt, pepper, onion, finely chopped parsley, and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
2. Place them on grills to roast, basting them from time to time, and serve them hot
