## Ingredients

- one eighth of flour
- hot water
- pork butter
- pounded salt
- two or three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix one eighth of flour with hot water, pork butter, pounded salt, and two or three egg yolks, but do not make it too soft
2. When kneaded, let it rest a short time, extend with the rolling pin until thin, cut into two or three equal pieces
3. Brush the top with hot pork butter, make well-closed rolls, and after cutting lengthwise and rounding, it will serve for tarts, pastries, and other uses
