## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton
- half arratel of bacon
- two heads of garlic
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- ginger
- wine
- vinegar
- herbs
- saffron
- rice

## Directions

1. Place two arratels of mutton to braise with half arratel of bacon, two heads of garlic, cinnamon, pepper, clove, ginger, all whole, a splash of wine, a splash of vinegar, and herbs
2. When more than half cooked, season with all spices and plenty of saffron
3. Place in a frying pan, add the rice
4. Cover with a lid and place on coals
5. Cook slowly until dry
