## Ingredients

- Half a pound of rice
- A little vegetable broth
- A piece of butter
- Seven or eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook half a pound of rice in a little vegetable broth as described in the preceding recipe, without the substance
2. Let it simmer little by little
3. Season with a piece of butter
4. When cooked, dissolve seven or eight egg yolks in broth and place on fire, stirring constantly with a spoon until they are set and without boiling
5. Season to good taste and serve mixed with the rice, everything well hot
