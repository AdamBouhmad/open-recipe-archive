## Ingredients

- veal breasts
- chicken or partridge breasts
- bacon, half-cooked
- veal livers
- bread cooked in milk
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- shallots
- twine
- hot water
- fine bacon for larding
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal breasts and open them with a knife between the skin and ribs
2. Soak them in water for some time and clean them well
3. Stuff with a mixture made from minced chicken or partridge breasts, half-cooked bacon, veal livers, bread cooked in milk, and egg yolks, all well seasoned with salt, pepper, parsley, and shallots
4. Tie the opening with twine
5. Braise in hot water until cooked through and cooled
6. Lard with fine bacon and cook as for fricandeau, finishing in the same manner
7. Serve hot covered with buttered paper, or if not larded, covered with bacon slices with spicy sauce underneath
