## Ingredients

- gingias or cherries
- sugar at high point

## Directions

1. Dip the gingias or cherries in sugar at high point and place over heat
2. Once it reaches four boilings, arrange them on the plate
3. Reduce the sugar, remove the foam from it, and pour it over the top
4. Serve cold
