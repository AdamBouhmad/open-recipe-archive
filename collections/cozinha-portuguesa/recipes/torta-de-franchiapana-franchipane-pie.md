## Ingredients

- Bound cream
- Biscuits of sweet and bitter almonds
- Peel of citron, orange flower and saffron
- A little sugar
- Five or six egg yolks
- A piece of clarified butter
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Make a bound cream as for frying
2. Add sweet and bitter almond biscuits, peel of citron, orange flower and saffron, all minced
3. Add a little sugar, five or six egg yolks, and a piece of clarified butter
4. Mix everything well and place in a baking dish with puff pastry dough underneath and finished as before
5. Serve in the same manner
