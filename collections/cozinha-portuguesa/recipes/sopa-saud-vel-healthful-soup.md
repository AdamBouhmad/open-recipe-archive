## Ingredients

- lettuce cut into thin fillets
- celery
- sorrel
- chervil
- roots such as turnips, carrots, and parsnips
- two or three spoons of broth
- one spoon of substance, without fat

## Directions

1. Cut lettuce, celery, sorrel, chervil and some roots like turnips, carrots, and parsnips into thin fillets
2. Wash very well
3. Put in boiling water
4. Remove and place on a sieve to drain
5. Put to cook in a pot with two or three spoons of broth and one of substance
6. When everything is cooked, of good taste, and without fat
7. Serve over a layer of bread soaked in broth
