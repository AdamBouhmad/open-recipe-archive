## Ingredients

- six young pigeons
- fresh butter
- sweetbreads
- truffles
- ham
- stock
- veal coulis
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Take six young pigeons and after preparing and straightening them, place in a casserole with fresh butter, sweetbreads, truffles, and ham, all cut into small dice
2. Pass through the fire, then moisten with stock and thicken with a little veal coulis
3. When cooked and the sauce is reduced, put a little grated Parmesan cheese in the bottom of the shell
4. Place the pigeon on top with its garnish and a little sauce, with grated cheese on top
5. When finished this way, put in the oven to glaze and serve hot
