## Ingredients

- two fat chickens
- two pieces of ham
- four arratels of beef from the leg
- one onion studded with cloves
- one sprig of sage
- hot water or broth
- vinegar
- a sprig of mint
- coriander
- rice
- pieces of ham

## Directions

1. Pluck two fat chickens, clean them, truss them, and put them to sweat in a pot with two pieces of ham, four arratels of beef from the leg cut into three or four pieces, one onion studded with cloves, and a sprig of sage
2. After everything is browned and beginning to stick, add a drop of hot water or broth, and let it boil little by little
3. When cooked and of good taste, pass the broth through a sieve, add a little vinegar, a sprig of mint and coriander
4. After boiling, add the quantity of rice necessary so that it remains whole and dry
5. When thus cooked, arrange the chickens on the plates with the rice on top, garnished with the pieces of ham cut into slices, and serve hot
