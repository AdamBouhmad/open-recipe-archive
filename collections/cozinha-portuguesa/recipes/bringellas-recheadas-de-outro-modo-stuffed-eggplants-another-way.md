## Ingredients

- eggplants
- onion
- butter
- olive oil
- oregano
- raw eggs
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- cheese
- sugar
- cinnamon
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Cook eggplants in halves and squeeze them
2. Remove all the flesh from inside some of them, and chop others
3. After chopping, mix them with a small amount of onion that must be already fried in butter and olive oil on the heat
4. Add oregano and raw eggs, and cook them on the same heat, stirring until they are very dry
5. Place them on a cutting board or stump, chop them with eggs, breadcrumbs, and cheese, and season with sugar, cinnamon, and all the seasonings
6. Stuff the eggplants with this mixture, bread them with eggs, fry them with cinnamon and sugar
7. Serve at table
