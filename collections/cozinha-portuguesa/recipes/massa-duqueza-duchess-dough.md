## Ingredients

- half an eighth of flour
- two fresh eggs
- a piece of butter
- a little salt
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Take half an eighth of flour, two fresh eggs, a piece of butter, and a little salt, and knead together with Madeira wine, but do not let it become soft
2. Let it rest, and afterwards use it for various intermediate dishes
