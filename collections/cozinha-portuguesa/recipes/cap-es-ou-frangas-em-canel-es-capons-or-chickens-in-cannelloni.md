## Ingredients

- Capons or chickens
- Ground mixture
- Bacon sheets
- White cloth or linen
- String
- Ham essence
- Pistachios
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the capons or chickens, cut in half, debone each piece entirely, beat between two cloths with the back of the knife
2. Cover with a well-bound ground mixture the thickness of a new crown coin, cover each one with its bacon sheet and its piece of white cloth or linen, tie very well around with string
3. Place to cook in a well-nourished braise
4. Once cooked, remove the cloth and bacon, and serve with ham essence sauce with two dozen pistachios and lemon juice
