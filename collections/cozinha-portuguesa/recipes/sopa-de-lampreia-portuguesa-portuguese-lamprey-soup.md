## Ingredients

- one lamprey, cleaned and bled
- half a quartilho of red wine
- butter
- leeks cut in wheels
- half a spoon of substance
- some wine
- salt
- one onion with two cloves
- a small sprig of sage and fresh herbs
- bread crusts fried and boiled in broth and fish substance or vegetable substance of good flavor

## Directions

1. After the lamprey is cleaned, limped, and bled, set the blood aside mixed with half a quartilho of red wine
2. Cut the lamprey into pieces and brown them in butter with the leeks cut in wheels
3. Add half a spoon of substance and some wine
4. Season with salt, one onion with two cloves, a small sprig of sage and fresh herbs
5. Let it simmer little by little
6. When cooked, bind the broth with the blood and serve over fried bread crusts boiled in broth and fish substance or vegetable substance of good flavor
