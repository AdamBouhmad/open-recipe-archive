## Ingredients

- one round leg of veal, well-fattened and well-white
- thick bacon, well-seasoned
- veal slices
- beef slices
- roots
- parsley
- fine herbs
- one studded onion
- cloves of garlic
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- broth
- spinach ragout or sorrel ragout

## Directions

1. Take the round leg of a veal, well-fattened and well-white, and clean it of all skin and bone
2. Lard it with thick bacon well-seasoned
3. Line the bottom of a pot with slices of beef and veal
4. Place the leg on top, tied with twine, and add some roots, a bunch of parsley, fine herbs, one studded onion, cloves of garlic, a bay leaf, salt, and pepper
5. Cover with bacon slices
6. Cover well and braise on gentle heat
7. After one hour of sweating, add one quarter of white wine and two spoonfuls of broth and continue cooking
8. Once the leg is cooked, remove the twine and serve with a well-finished spinach ragout or sorrel ragout, or any other vegetable ragout or sauce as preferred
9. May also be served as a cold dish with the braising liquid clarified and made into aspic
