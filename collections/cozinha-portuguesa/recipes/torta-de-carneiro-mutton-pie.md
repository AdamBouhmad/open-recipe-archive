## Ingredients

- Three arratels of finely chopped mutton
- Half an arratel of chopped bacon
- Six eggs
- Aromatics
- One spoon of broth
- One splash of vinegar
- All seasonings
- Four eggs for binding
- Water
- Salt
- French puff pastry

## Directions

1. Brown the finely chopped mutton with half an arratel of bacon, six eggs, and aromatics, adding one spoon of broth and one splash of vinegar
2. When browned, season with all seasonings
3. Thicken with four eggs so the meat becomes very dry, and let cool
4. Make dough with water and salt, knead very well, make French puff pastry or the desired type, arranging six layers below and six above
5. When the meat is in the pie with two beaten egg yolks on top and finished, cook on gentle heat and serve
