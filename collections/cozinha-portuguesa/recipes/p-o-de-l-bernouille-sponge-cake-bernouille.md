## Ingredients

- Half an eighth of flour
- Four eggs with their whites
- A piece of chopped lemon peel
- A drop of flower water
- Water

## Directions

1. Take half an eighth of flour, four eggs with their whites, a piece of chopped lemon peel, and a drop of flower water
2. Knead all with plain water
3. After kneading well, let it rest for an hour
4. Roll it out
