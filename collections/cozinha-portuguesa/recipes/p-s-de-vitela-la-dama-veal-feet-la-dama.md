## Ingredients

- veal feet
- eggs
- flour
- sugar
- bay leaf
- cinnamon
- fine herbs, parsley, savory
- two cloves
- pan broth
- vinegar
- egg yolks
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cook the veal feet and cut them into small pieces
2. Remove the bones
3. Pass them through eggs and bread them by frying, passing them through eggs one or two more times
4. Place them in a pan with small pieces of flour and sugar, bay leaf, cinnamon, fine herbs, parsley, savory, and two cloves
5. Add the broth from the pan with a bit of good vinegar and bring to a gentle simmer
6. Have half a dozen or one dozen egg yolks with lemon juice, depending on the portion
7. When the pan simmers, taste it, as it should be sour and sweet according to the taste of those eating
8. Thicken this broth with the egg yolks so it becomes thick and place it on the plate and send to the table
