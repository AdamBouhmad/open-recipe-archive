## Ingredients

- a chicken breast half cooked and shredded
- the crumb of a vintem of bread, grated
- one quarter of well pounded almonds
- two and a half arratels of sugar at espadana stage

## Directions

1. Put a chicken breast half cooked and shredded in a pot and break it up very well with a spoon.
2. Mix with the crumb of a vintem of bread grated, one quarter of well pounded almonds, and two and a half arratels of sugar at espadana stage.
3. After beating everything together, cook over a gentle flame until it thickens.
4. Once thick, put on plates and send to the table.
