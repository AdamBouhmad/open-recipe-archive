## Ingredients

- flour
- fine sugar
- egg whites
- a little orange flower water

## Directions

1. Place on a table an equal quantity of flour as sugar, knead it with egg whites and a little orange flower water
2. When the dough is bound and formed, but not too soft, sprinkle the table with flour, stretch the dough with the roller, and it will serve for croquants, tarts, small pastries, and other small side dishes
