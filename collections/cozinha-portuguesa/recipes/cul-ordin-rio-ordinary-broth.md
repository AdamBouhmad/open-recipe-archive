## Ingredients

- Sheets of veal
- Two slices of ham
- Some roots and onions
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- One onion with two or three cloves
- Stock
- Flour browned in butter, or flour dissolved in substance

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a casserole with sheets of veal and two slices of ham, some roots and onions
2. Let it sweat on gentle heat, adding a drop of stock from time to time
3. Skim and season with one bundle of parsley and fine herbs, one onion with two or three cloves
4. Thicken with browned flour or flour dissolved in substance
5. Let it simmer little by little
6. When the meat is cooked, remove the fat, pass through a sieve, and use for ragus, sauces, and whatever else is necessary
