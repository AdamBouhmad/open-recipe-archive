## Ingredients

- Folhas de perrexil (parsley leaves)
- Água fervendo (boiling water)
- Substância (stock)
- Culis de vitela (veal extract)
- Culis de presunto (ham extract)

## Directions

1. Soak the parsley leaves
2. Pass them through boiling water to remove salt and vinegar
3. Drain and squeeze them
4. Simmer for an instant in a little stock
5. When cooked, thicken with veal and ham extract
6. Serve for garnishing entrees
7. Cucumbers, green beans, and other preserved vegetables are served the same way
