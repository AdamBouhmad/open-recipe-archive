## Ingredients

- Gannets
- Bread crumbs
- Fresh butter
- Flour
- Red wine
- Parsley
- Garlic, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Nutmeg, grated
- Bay leaf
- Thyme in powder
- String
- White wine
- Bread crust, grated
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Singe, clean, and remove the entrails from the gannets
2. Prepare a stuffing of bread crumbs, fresh butter, flour, two spoonfuls of red wine, parsley, chopped garlic, seasoned with salt, pepper, cloves, grated nutmeg, bay leaf, and powdered thyme
3. Mix all together and stuff inside the gannets
4. Sew the opening with thread, place on the spit and roast, basting with white wine, salt, and melted butter
5. Once roasted, serve with its own sauce, sprinkled with grated bread crust and lemon juice
