## Ingredients

- veal breast, preserved for three days
- beef breast, yellow
- lard
- clove
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon
- dozen marrow bones
- toasted bread slices
- preservation broth

## Directions

1. After a veal breast has been in preservation for three days, cook a yellow beef breast in the pot until half cooked
2. Mince it with a piece of lard and season with clove, pepper, nutmeg, and lemon
3. Use this meat to stuff the veal breast
4. Place the stuffed veal breast in a pan with a dozen marrow bones on top
5. Roast it in the oven with the preservation broth
6. Once roasted, place it on toasted bread slices and send to the table
