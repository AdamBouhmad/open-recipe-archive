## Ingredients

- eggs
- ground pepper
- grated cheese
- clarified butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and season with ground pepper and grated cheese
2. Make three or four bundles with clarified butter
3. Place on a tortilla pan, dusted with grated cheese
4. Once browned like meatballs, arrange on a plate
5. Sprinkle with butter
6. Cover with breadcrumbs
7. Bake in the oven
8. Once cooked, serve hot
