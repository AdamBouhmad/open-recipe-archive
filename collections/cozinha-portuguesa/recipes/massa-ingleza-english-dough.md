## Ingredients

- half a pot of milk
- half an arratel of butter
- a little salt
- lemon peel
- a loaf of hair
- flour
- clarified butter
- grated sugar

## Directions

1. Put in a pot half a pot of milk and half an arratel of butter, a little salt, lemon peel, and a loaf of hair
2. When it boils, add to it the necessary flour, as for fritters, and after it has been dried on the fire and cooled, extend it with bread dough, and when placed to the necessary thickness, cut into equal squares
3. Fry in clarified butter, serve with grated sugar on top, and glaze with a hot spatula
