## Ingredients

- half canada of milk
- eight egg yolks
- half arratel of sugar
- four ounces of finely ground almonds
- quarter of butter

## Directions

1. Pour into half canada of milk eight egg yolks, half arratel of sugar, four ounces of finely ground almonds, and a quarter of butter
2. Beat everything together very well
3. Place in a pot to cook over low heat
4. As it thickens, pour into a plate or skillet
5. Brown it in the pastry oven or in the oven
