## Ingredients

- a bit of royal dough
- eggs
- half a dozen almond biscuits
- orange flower
- grated lemon zest
- white icing

## Directions

1. Take a bit of royal dough
2. Dissolve it in eggs
3. Add to it half a dozen almond biscuits, orange flower and grated lemon zest, all in powder
4. Form the cakes in the size desired
5. Place them on a buttered Flanders sheet
6. Put them to cook in the oven with moderate heat
7. When baked, cover them with white icing
8. When this is dry, serve them like the others
