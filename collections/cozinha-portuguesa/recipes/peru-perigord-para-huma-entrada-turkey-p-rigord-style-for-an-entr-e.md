## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Two or three arratels of truffles
- Scraped or pounded bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- String
- Bacon slabs
- Paper leaves
- Clear ham essence
- Truffles
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take two or three arratels of truffles and after washing and peeling them very well, mix them with scraped or pounded bacon, seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Stuff the turkey with this mixture right after it has been killed and straightened
3. Tie with string
4. Allow to mortify and take on the taste of the truffles for three or four days
5. Wrap in bacon slabs and paper leaves
6. After roasting, serve with a sauce of clear ham essence, chopped truffles and egg yolks, and lemon juice
