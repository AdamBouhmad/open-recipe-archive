## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- roots
- cloves
- whole pepper
- fine herbs
- wine

## Directions

1. Prepare the ham and soak it for two or three days, wrap it in a cloth and tie it with string, and place it to cook in a pot or a braising pan of the length of the ham, with four canadas of water, some roots, cloves, whole pepper, and a bundle of fine herbs
2. When half cooked, add two canadas of wine, and cooking slowly for five or six hours, when well cooked, let it cool in its broth; then place it to drain, remove the skin carefully without damaging the fat, cover it all with fine grated bread mixed with a little pepper, brown it in the oven or with a hot pan, and serve it on a napkin for an intermediate course
