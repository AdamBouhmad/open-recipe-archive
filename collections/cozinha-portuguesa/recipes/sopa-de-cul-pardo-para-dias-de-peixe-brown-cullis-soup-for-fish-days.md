## Ingredients

- Two onions, sliced
- One carrot
- One parsnip
- Butter
- Three or four pieces of bread slices
- A little flour
- Fish broth
- One bunch of parsley
- One bunch of marjoram
- Two small onions
- Two cloves
- Some pieces of fried or roasted fish

## Directions

1. Cut the onions, carrot, and parsnip into slices and sauté everything in butter
2. Add three or four pieces of bread slices and a little flour
3. When well browned, add a quantity of fish broth and substance
4. Season with one bunch of parsley, one bunch of marjoram, two small onions, and two cloves
5. Let it simmer slowly
6. While it simmers, pound some pieces of fried or roasted fish
7. Pass it with the softened bread slices and broth through a sieve
8. Serve hot and well-flavored with other fried and placed bread slices at the bottom of the plate with stuffed bread in the middle
