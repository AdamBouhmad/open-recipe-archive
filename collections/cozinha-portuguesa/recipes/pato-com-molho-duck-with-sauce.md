## Ingredients

- vinegar
- one roasted duck
- a small amount of butter
- ground pepper
- some saffron
- ripe grapes (if available)

## Directions

1. Place a little diluted vinegar in a cooking vessel under the duck to be roasted so that it collects the drippings
2. Add a small amount of butter, ground pepper, and some saffron to the drippings
3. If you have ripe grapes, squeeze a bunch of them into the sauce
4. When the duck is roasted, brown it and give it a boil in the sauce vessel
5. When ready to serve, place the duck with the sauce on a plate over bread slices
6. Send to table
