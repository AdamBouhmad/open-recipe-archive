## Ingredients

- Egg yolks, quantity necessary for the biscuits to be made
- Fine sugar, equal weight to the yolks
- Flour, weight equal to half the eggs removed from the balance
- A little lime peel, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Take the necessary quantity of eggs for the biscuits to be made and weigh them on a balance
2. Weigh with the same amount of fine sugar
3. Remove half of the eggs from the balance and weigh the same amount of flour with the remaining eggs on the other side
4. Put the yolks in a bowl or basin with the sugar and a little finely chopped lime peel
5. Beat everything very well for the time of half an hour
6. Add the well-beaten and raised egg whites afterwards
7. Mix everything carefully, adding the flour little by little and gently mixing it with the composition after it is very well prepared
8. Form some paper boxes, grease them with butter inside, fill them to the middle with this mixture
9. Sprinkle a little fine sugar on top and put them to bake in an oven that does not have too much heat for the time of half an hour
10. When cooked and with a golden color, remove from the oven and before they cool remove them from the boxes to serve them when it is necessary
