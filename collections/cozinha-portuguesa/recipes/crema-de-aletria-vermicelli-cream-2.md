## Ingredients

- one arratel of vermicelli
- milk
- egg yolks
- clarified butter
- fine sugar
- lemon zest
- orange flower
- candied fruit
- almond biscuits
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Boil one arratel of vermicelli in boiling water
2. When half cooked, strain through a sieve
3. Finish cooking in milk
4. When cooked, thickened, and dry, add egg yolks, clarified butter, fine sugar, lemon zest, orange flower and candied fruit
5. Chop the almond biscuits and mix everything well with beaten egg whites
6. Pour into the serving dish
7. Sprinkle with sugar
8. Put to cook in the oven
9. When it has a good color, serve hot as an entremet
