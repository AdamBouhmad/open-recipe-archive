## Ingredients

- one canada of water
- one arratel of veal meat from the thigh
- roots of wild chicories
- chervil leaves
- escalopcndra leaves
- pimpinella leaves

## Directions

1. Place to simmer in one canada of water one arratel of veal meat from the thigh, and when simmering, add roots of wild chicories, leaves of chervil, escalopcndra, and pimpinella
