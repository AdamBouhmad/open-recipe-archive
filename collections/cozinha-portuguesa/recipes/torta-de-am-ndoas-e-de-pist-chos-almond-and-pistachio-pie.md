## Ingredients

- Quarter pound of almonds and pistachios, ground
- Milk drops
- A little well-bound cream
- Citron peel, lemon peel and orange flower
- All minced
- Beef marrow, melted
- Some sugar
- Four egg yolks with well-beaten egg whites
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Grind a quarter pound of almonds and pistachios, moistening with drops of milk to prevent them from releasing their oil
2. Mix after grinding with a little well-bound cream, minced citron peel, lemon peel and orange flower
3. Add melted beef marrow and some sugar
4. Strain everything and bind with four egg yolks with well-beaten and lifted egg whites
5. Place all this mixture on puff pastry dough with only its edges around it
6. Bake in the oven with moderate heat
7. When cooked and with good color, serve hot as a between course dish; but for fish days, add butter instead of marrow
