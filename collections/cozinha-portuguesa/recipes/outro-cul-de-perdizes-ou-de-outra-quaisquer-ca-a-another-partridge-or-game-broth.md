## Ingredients

- Three or four arratels of veal
- Half an arratel of ham
- Two onions in slices
- Two roots
- A little melted bacon fat
- A little flour
- Three or four spoons of stock
- Three or four spoons of substance
- Salt, pepper, two cloves
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- Some slices of truffles
- Some mushrooms
- Pieces of bread
- Two dressed and pounded partridges

## Directions

1. Put three or four arratels of veal, half an arratel of ham, two onions in slices, two roots in a casserole
2. When it sticks, add a little melted bacon fat and a little flour, and stirring always until it takes color, add three or four spoons of stock and three or four spoons of substance
3. Season with salt, pepper, two cloves, one bundle of parsley and fine herbs, some slices of truffles, and some mushrooms
4. Let it simmer little by little
5. When everything is cooked, add some pieces of bread and two dressed and pounded partridges
6. Pass through a strainer and when warm and without boiling, use for everything that is necessary
7. In place of partridges you may use chickens, capons, woodcocks, ducks, wild pigeons, quails, and rabbits, whatever you prefer
