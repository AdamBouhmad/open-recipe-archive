## Ingredients

- round bread
- sweetbreads
- artichoke hearts
- ham
- roux
- grated cheese
- five or six lamb tails
- braising liquid
- minced meat à crème with chives, cooked
- roux with melted butter
- grated Parmesan cheese
- essence sauce, clear

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from a round bread
2. Open it from the bottom and remove the crumb
3. Stuff it with a forcemeat of sweetbreads, artichoke hearts, and ham, all cooked and of good taste
4. Place it on its dish with a little roux underneath
5. Arrange around it five or six lamb tails cooked in braising liquid with their points upward
6. Fill the intervals with minced meat à crème with cooked chives
7. Pour over all a little roux with melted butter
8. Dust with grated Parmesan cheese
9. Cook covered under a lid
10. Drain off the fat afterward
11. Serve with clear essence sauce
