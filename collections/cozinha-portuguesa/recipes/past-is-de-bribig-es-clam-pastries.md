## Ingredients

- clams
- parsley
- onion
- egg yolks
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. After shelling the clams, sauté them with parsley and onion
2. Season them with four egg yolks (as one does with minced fish)
3. Make pastries of puff pastry dough the size you wish
4. Serve at table hot
