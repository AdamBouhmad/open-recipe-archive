## Ingredients

- Young partridges or young partridge
- Bacon for larding or covering
- Parchment paper
- Lard
- Lemon slices without skin

## Directions

1. To distinguish young partridges from old ones, they should have the first feather of the wings sharp and with a white mesh at the end
2. After mortifying for two or three days, clean them and lard them or cover them with bacon slices placed in the breast wrapped in parchment paper
3. Place to roast
4. After roasted and of good color, they serve as a roasted dish
