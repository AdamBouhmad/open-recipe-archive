## Ingredients

- half a saucepan of salted warm water
- a little sugar
- two ounces of butter
- flour
- a bit of grated lemon rind
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan half a saucepan of water seasoned with salt, a little bit of sugar, and two ounces of butter, and place on the fire
2. When it boils for an instant, add flour to it and mix with the spoon until the dough binds and becomes of firm consistency
3. Working always with the spoon and over the fire until it detaches from the bottom
4. Add to it a little bit of finely grated lemon rind, and add eggs one by one
5. When the dough is soft and without lumps, it serves for cream puffs, small pastries, and other different side dishes
