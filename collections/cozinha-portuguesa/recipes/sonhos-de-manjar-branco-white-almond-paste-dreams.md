## Ingredients

- chicken or partridge breasts, cleaned and pounded
- strong cream made from rice flour, eggs, and milk
- salt
- fine sugar
- ground cinnamon
- almond biscuits
- lemon peel
- covered orange flower
- flour
- oil or hot butter

## Directions

1. Clean, pound and mince chicken or partridge breasts
2. Mix with a strong cream made from rice flour, eggs, and milk
3. Season with salt, fine sugar, ground cinnamon, almond biscuits, lemon peel, and covered orange flower
4. After mixing everything, cut into small pieces the size of walnuts
5. Sprinkle with flour and fry in hot oil or butter
6. Serve sprinkled with fine sugar
