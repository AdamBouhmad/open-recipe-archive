## Ingredients

- Fish, grilled
- Butter
- Bread crumb, thin slices
- Minced fish mixture with more fish
- Bread crumb, soaked in milk
- Salt
- pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Salt
- Scallion
- Shallots
- Egg yolks
- Egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. After grilling any of these fish on a griddle, arrange it with all its length on a plafond with butter and thin slices of bread underneath
2. Remove the meat from the upper part, leaving the meat from the lower part with the spine, head, and tail attached
3. From what was removed, make a chopped mixture with some additional piece of cod or any other fish
4. Put in a mortar about the size of a twenty-real piece of bread soaked in milk, and pounding it very well, season with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, salt, scallion, and shallots
5. Bind with seven or eight egg yolks and some beaten egg whites
6. Straighten this chopped mixture on the breast as if it were whole, cover with grated bread crumb, and put to cook in the oven
7. When cooked and with good color, pass carefully to the serving plate, where it should be served with sauce and spicy sauce underneath
