## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- water
- vinegar
- onions
- garlic clove
- salt
- pepper
- laurel leaves
- marjoram
- chickens
- pork butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a marinade with a piece of butter powdered with little flour, two spoonfuls of water, vinegar, onion slices, one garlic clove, salt, pepper, laurel leaves, and marjoram
2. Put it on heat until it boils, remove it, and put the chickens cut into quarters into it
3. Leave them for two or three hours
4. After they have taken flavor, remove them, dry them, dip them in flour, and fry them in pork butter
5. When fried and browned, serve them garnished with parsley
6. Alternatively, they can be cooked in the same marinade, and after cooking, pass them through a batter made with flour, white wine, salt, pepper, and a little melted butter, then fry them and serve them as above
