## Ingredients

- 2 squabs
- half an arratel of bacon
- one quartilho of broth or hot water
- spices
- bread of ló
- half a canada of milk
- 6 egg yolks
- flour
- 2 cardoons
- spices

## Directions

1. After cleaning, cut the squabs in half and braise them in bacon and broth or hot water
2. When half-cooked, season with all the seasonings
3. Remove from the broth and place on slices of bread of ló moistened in half a canada of milk that has been cooked with six egg yolks and a little flour until thick
4. Meanwhile, cook two cardoons cut into small pieces; when half-cooked, remove from the first water and finish cooking in the squabs' broth, seasoning with all the seasonings
5. Arrange the squabs on the plate, surrounding them with cardoon, and pour the cooked milk on top
6. Brown in the oven with heat from below and above, and send to the table browned
