## Ingredients

- half a canada of veal polp cut into pieces
- leaves of betony
- leaves of lemon balm
- eyes of elder
- a small bundle of wild chicory
- the feet or tails of ten or twelve crawfish of fresh water, bruised

## Directions

1. Boil in half a canada with the veal polp cut into pieces
2. Add leaves of betony, lemon balm, and eyes of elder, a good handful of each
3. Add a small bundle of wild chicory and the feet or tails of ten or twelve crawfish of fresh water, bruised
4. After everything has boiled well and the water is reduced to half, pass through a napkin
5. Take until you are free from pain
