## Ingredients

- onions
- two cloves of garlic
- shallots
- water
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- sour orange juice
- cloth

## Directions

1. Chop onions, two cloves of garlic, and shallots
2. Dissolve everything in a drop of water and white wine
3. Put to boil tempered with salt and pepper
4. Add sour orange juice
5. Strain through a cloth
6. Serve as seems best
