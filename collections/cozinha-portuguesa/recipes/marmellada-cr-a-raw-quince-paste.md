## Ingredients

- sugar
- quince

## Directions

1. Take two arratels of clean sugar and bring to ball point
2. Add one arratel of cooked quince that has been trimmed, cut into slices, and weighed
3. As soon as the sugar reaches ball point, break apart the quince slices by hand into small pieces and add to the sugar pan away from the fire
4. Mix the quince in the sugar very well with a spoon
5. Return to the fire, stirring so the quince does not stick to the bottom of the pan
6. Once it boils a little and makes bubbles, remove from the fire and place in small paper cones
7. If desired, add fragrances when the sugar is at the right point and before adding the quince
