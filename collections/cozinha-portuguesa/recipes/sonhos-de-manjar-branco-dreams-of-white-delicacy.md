## Ingredients

- breasts of chicken or roasted partridges, chopped and pounded
- strong cream made of rice flour, eggs, and milk
- salt
- fine sugar
- ground cinnamon
- almond biscuits
- lemon peel
- covered orange blossom
- flour
- oil or hot butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Chop and pound the breasts of chicken or roasted partridges, and mix them with a strong cream made of rice flour, eggs, and milk
2. Season with salt, fine sugar, ground cinnamon, almond biscuits, lemon peel, and covered orange blossom
3. After everything is together and kneaded, cut into little mouthfuls the size of nuts, dust with flour, and made in hot oil or butter, serve dusted with sugar
