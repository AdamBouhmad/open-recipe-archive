## Ingredients

- two arratels of verjuice berries
- boiling water
- cold water
- two arratels of sugar at espadana stage
- cinnamon
- clove
- five réis of grated bread for thickening
- musk
- French puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Scald two arratels of verjuice berries in boiling water and pass through cold water.
2. Cook in two arratels of sugar at espadana stage with cinnamon, clove, five réis of grated bread for thickening, and musk.
3. Let cool while making the French puff pastry tart.
4. Once cooked, send to the table.
