## Ingredients

- Wheat flour, the finest and purest
- Yeast, necessary quantity
- Water, sufficient amount
- Salt, to temperament

## Directions

1. Take the finest and purest wheat flour and mix it with the necessary amount of yeast
2. Add sufficient water to knead, observing the temperature of the water, which should be tempered with salt
3. Work forcefully for some time, and after it is very well worked and mixed with the yeast, let it rest in a place that is neither cold nor excessively hot so it may ferment and rise
4. When risen, remove from the kneading trough and place on a table
5. Cut into equal pieces and form loaves of the desired size
6. After shaping, put to bake in the oven, carefully observing the degree of heat
