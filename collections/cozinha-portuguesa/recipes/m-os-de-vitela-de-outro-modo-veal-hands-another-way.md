## Ingredients

- veal hands
- eggs
- butter
- egg yolk
- hen or lamb broth
- sugar
- white wine
- vinegar
- ginger
- cinnamon
- pepper
- nutmeg
- clove

## Directions

1. Peel the veal hands as one would peel a suckling pig
2. Cook them well in water and salt until the bones loosen
3. Pass them through beaten eggs and fry them in butter
4. Make a sweet and sour broth with egg yolk, hen or lamb broth, sugar, white wine, and vinegar, seasoned with ginger, cinnamon, pepper, nutmeg, and clove
5. Send to the table
