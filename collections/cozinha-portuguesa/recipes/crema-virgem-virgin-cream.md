## Ingredients

- four or five egg whites, well beaten with snow
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Beat four or five egg whites with snow
2. Mix them with milk seasoned with salt, sugar, and orange flower water
3. Pour into a dish and place it to cook over a pan full of water
4. Cover with its lid
5. Put heat on top
6. When it is set and golden with sugar and a hot spatula, serve it cold or hot if desired
