## Ingredients

- two partridges
- bacon lardons
- one half arratel of bacon
- pepper
- salt
- garlic cloves
- one half arratel of butter
- flour
- two bay leaves

## Directions

1. After soaking two partridges in water, lard them very well with bacon lardons, and what remains of one half arratel, after removing the lardons, very well seasoned with salt and pepper, chop with some garlic cloves
2. Make the dough with one half arratel of butter, making one large ball, place the partridges in it with chopped bacon below and above, and two bay leaves
3. Close the pie with thick dough so that when it bakes it does not burst, and when baked, serve to the table
