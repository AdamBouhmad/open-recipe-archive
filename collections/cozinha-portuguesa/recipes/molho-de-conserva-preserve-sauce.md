## Ingredients

- solho in preserve of wine, vinegar, and garlic
- olive oil
- black seasonings
- dried bay leaves

## Directions

1. When the solho is in a preserve of wine, vinegar, and garlic (more wine than vinegar), roast it
2. After roasting, let it cool
3. Boil half the broth it was in with the same amount of olive oil, all black seasonings, and some dried bay leaves
4. When it has boiled, season it as needed and let it cool
5. Put the cold solho in this preserve to be eaten immediately; however, if it is to be kept or sent away, add less vinegar
