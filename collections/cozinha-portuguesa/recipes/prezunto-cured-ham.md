## Ingredients

- half an arratel of lean cured ham
- pork fat (toucinho)
- wine
- bay leaves
- capers
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry half an arratel of lean cured ham cut into small pieces in pork fat
2. In the same pork fat make a small sauce with a splash of wine and two bay leaves
3. Add capers and serve
