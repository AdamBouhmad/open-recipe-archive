## Ingredients

- one or two veal rib sections
- cucumber fillets
- black truffles
- ham
- hard egg yolks
- freshwater crayfish tails
- good olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- lemon juice
- bacon slices
- paper
- good essence
- or minced raw veal
- parsley
- hard egg yolks
- cooked ham
- red omelette
- black truffles from the earth
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take one or two veal rib sections and trim the skin from the loins very well
2. Make small incisions close together and insert fillets of cucumber, black truffles, ham, hard egg yolks, and freshwater crayfish tails into these cuts
3. Marinate with good olive oil, salt, pepper, fine herbs, and lemon juice
4. Braise in a dry braise covered with bacon slices and paper
5. Once cooked, remove the fat and serve hot with good essence and lemon
6. Alternatively, fill the incisions with minced raw veal powdered with different colors: one green with parsley cooked and minced well, another with hard egg yolks, another with cooked ham, another with red omelette, another with black truffles from the earth, and another with breadcrumbs, all well covered with bacon slices and paper with low heat on top
7. Once cooked, serve hot with the sauce that seems best
