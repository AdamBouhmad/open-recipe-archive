## Ingredients

- one and a quarter arratels of sugar at low thread point
- one arratel of finely chopped almonds
- eight eggs (six with yolks, two with whites)
- cinnamon
- pastry dough made with butter and a little sugar

## Directions

1. Put one and a quarter arratels of sugar at low thread point in a pan
2. When at this point, remove from heat and add one arratel of finely chopped almonds, breaking apart with a spoon as it is added
3. Return to heat while it rises in boiling and add eight eggs (six with yolks, two with whites)
4. When it boils a bit, it is done
5. Sprinkle cinnamon on top and remove from fire
6. Take pastry dough made with butter and a little sugar and shape the little cheeses
7. After the mixture cools, shape and send to the oven
