## Ingredients

- citron, lemon, orange, or bergamot fruit
- sugar, clarified

## Directions

1. Peel the fruits carefully with delicacy, clean them inside, leaving them whole or cutting into quarters
2. Place in cold water and cook in boiling water until soft and cooked through
3. Place in cold water again, remove from it, and drain
4. Put in clarified sugar and let simmer little by little over mild heat
5. When the sugar is reduced and at the proper point, remove and store
6. Serve for compotes, for covering, and for whatever else is necessary
