## Ingredients

- thistles
- herbs
- butter
- egg

## Directions

1. Chop and sauté the thistles (as mentioned above)
2. Fry it in a beaten egg in the manner of a flattened fried pastry
3. Place the chopped thistle in this pastry
4. Folding it, place them on the plate; in this manner you make the bundles until you fill the plate
