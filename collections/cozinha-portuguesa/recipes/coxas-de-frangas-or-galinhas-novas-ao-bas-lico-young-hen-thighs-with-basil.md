## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 hen thighs
- cream
- cooked hen breast
- fat livers
- veal sweetbreads
- truffles
- béchamel
- egg yolks
- twine and needle
- egg wash
- breadcrumbs
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Remove bones from 10 or 12 hen thighs in the same manner as the previous recipe
2. Stuff them with a fine cream mixture containing a little hash of cooked hen breasts, fat livers, veal sweetbreads, and truffles, all passed in a well-bound béchamel with egg yolks
3. Sew the thighs very well around with twine and needle
4. Cook them in a well-maintained white braising liquid
5. When cooked, pass through egg wash, coat twice with fine breadcrumbs
6. Fry in hot pork fat and serve with fried parsley rings
