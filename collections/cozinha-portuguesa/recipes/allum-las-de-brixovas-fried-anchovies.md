## Ingredients

- thirty anchovies
- olive oil
- salt
- flour
- white wine

## Directions

1. Open the anchovies in two parts and remove the spines
2. Soak in a quart of olive oil
3. Remove the salt, place on cloth, and dry
4. Pass through a batter made with flour, white wine, and a little olive oil
5. Fry
6. Fried and of good color, serve hot as an entrée
