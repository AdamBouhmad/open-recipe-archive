## Ingredients

- one quarter of flour
- butter
- hot water

## Directions

1. Take one quarter of flour and knead it on a table with butter and hot water, but let it be stiffer than the preceding one
2. After being very well kneaded, make two cakes, from one lift the box or boxes skillfully, and on sheets of paper, and put inside to cook everything that seems good to you, after being seasoned, finished, and covered with part of the other dough
