## Ingredients

- Nabos (turnips)
- Água (water)
- Manteiga (butter)
- Toucinho derretido (melted bacon)
- Bocado de presunto (piece of ham)
- Farinha (flour)
- Substância (stock)
- Sal (salt)
- Pimenta (pepper)
- Capella (bouquet garni)
- Cebola com dois cravos (onion with two cloves)

## Directions

1. Cut turnips into equal pieces
2. Boil them in water for an instant
3. Drain them
4. Brown them over heat in a pan with butter or melted bacon and a piece of ham
5. Dust with flour
6. Add a little stock
7. Season with salt and pepper, bouquet garni, and onion with cloves
8. Let simmer slowly
9. When cooked, remove the bouquet garni and fat
10. Thicken with extract
11. When of good taste, serve for terrines and garnishing entrees
