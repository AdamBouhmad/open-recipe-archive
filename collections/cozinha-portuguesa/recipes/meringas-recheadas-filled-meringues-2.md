## Ingredients

- egg whites
- lemon zest, chopped finely
- powdered sugar, well sifted
- fruit jelly

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites with rods until well risen
2. Add a little finely chopped lemon zest and well-sifted powdered sugar, mixing everything together
3. Place in small pieces the size of walnuts on paper leaves, dusted with fine sugar
4. Bake over the same table, under the lid of a dome oven, with fire on top
5. When cooked and of good color, remove the unattached egg white from the middle with a small spoon
6. Replace the egg white with fruit jelly, and join some together
7. Serve immediately before they soften
