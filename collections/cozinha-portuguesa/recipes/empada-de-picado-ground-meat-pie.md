## Ingredients

- Veal, ground and seared in bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Culí sauce or reduced milk
- Bacon planks
- Pastry box with lid
- Sauce essence
- Lemon juice
- White sauce
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a ground mixture of veal pieces seared in bacon, season it
2. Finish it as is customary
3. Mix in a little culí or reduced milk
4. Put everything in the pastry box
5. Cover with bacon planks
6. Place the box lid
7. Put in the oven to bake for two hours
8. When cooked, open it, remove all the fat
9. Serve with essence sauce and lemon juice, or with white sauce made with egg yolks dissolved in milk and lemon juice
