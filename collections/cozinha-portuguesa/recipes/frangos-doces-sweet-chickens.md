## Ingredients

- 2 large chickens
- drippings from the chickens
- 1 arratel of sugar
- half an arratel of finely ground almonds
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. After roasting two large chickens and taking their drippings, place them in one arratel of sugar at half-point consistency with half an arratel of finely ground almonds and the said drippings
2. When the chickens are cooked and the sauce is thick, place on slices of bread with sauce and cinnamon on top
