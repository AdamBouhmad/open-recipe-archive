## Ingredients

- two fresh tuna steaks
- wine
- vinegar
- garlic
- olive oil
- black seasonings
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. After two fresh tuna steaks have been in a preserve of wine, vinegar, and garlic for two hours, roast them in the oven in a pan with a small amount of the broth they were in and a splash of olive oil
2. When half cooked, season with all black seasonings and lemon juice
3. Make a pastry box from one eighth of flour
4. Put the tuna with the sauce inside it
5. Cook it and serve it
