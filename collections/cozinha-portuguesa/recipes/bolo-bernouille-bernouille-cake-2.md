## Ingredients

- half an ounce of flour
- four eggs with two extra whites
- a piece of chopped lemon rind
- a drop of water

## Directions

1. Take half an ounce of flour, four eggs with two extra whites, a piece of chopped lemon rind, a drop of water
2. After being well kneaded, let rest for an hour
3. Stretch it out, cover with butter, fold or roll, stretch again, and finish like puff pastry
