## Ingredients

- ten egg yolks
- half an arratel of powdered sugar from the Island of Madeira

## Directions

1. Put ten egg yolks in a pot with half an arratel of powdered sugar from the Island of Madeira
2. When this mixture becomes thick, form the melindres
3. Place with a spoon onto papers, making them the size of pastilles
4. Bake on low heat so they do not burn
5. When baked, serve at the table
