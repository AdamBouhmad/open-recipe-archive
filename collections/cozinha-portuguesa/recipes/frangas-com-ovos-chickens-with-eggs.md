## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Chicken livers
- Scraped bacon
- Fine herbs
- Egg yolks
- Bacon sheets
- Paper
- Hard egg yolks
- Ham essence
- Butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stuff the chickens with their livers and scraped bacon, seasoned with fine herbs and bound with two egg yolks
2. Cover with bacon sheets and paper as in preceding recipes
3. Roast on the spit
4. Make a sauce with four hard-boiled egg yolks, chopped on cloth and placed in ham essence bound with butter, but do not boil
5. Serve with lemon juice
