## Ingredients

- one mutton leg
- half arratel of bacon, chopped
- herbs
- all spices
- three eggs
- grated bread
- vinegar
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop all the meat of a mutton leg with half arratel of bacon and herbs, keeping the leg bone whole
2. Season the meat on the chopping block with all spices, three eggs, grated bread, vinegar, and lemon juice
3. In a buttered frying pan that fits the leg, begin to arm the leg
