## Ingredients

- poppy leaves
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour half a pound of poppy leaves into a glazed bowl.
2. Pour a quart of boiling water over them.
3. Let them infuse for twenty-four hours over hot ashes.
4. Give it two boils.
5. Squeeze through a cloth.
6. Add to the juice one pound of sugar in the hair stage.
7. Boil it all together until it takes the consistency of a syrup, like the others stated.
8. Guard it in the same manner.
