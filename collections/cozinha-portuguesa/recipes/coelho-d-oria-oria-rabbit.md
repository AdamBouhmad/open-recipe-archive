## Ingredients

- young rabbits, well larded
- onion rounds
- parsley, finely minced
- olive oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- capers

## Directions

1. After larding the young rabbits well, roast them
2. Once roasted, carve them into a plate
3. Place some onion rounds on top (which should be in water) and finely minced green parsley
4. Pour over hot olive oil and vinegar mixed with pepper and capers
