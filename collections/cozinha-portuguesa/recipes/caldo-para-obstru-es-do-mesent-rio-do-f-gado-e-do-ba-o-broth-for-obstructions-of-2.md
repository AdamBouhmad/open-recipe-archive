## Ingredients

- one arratel of veal knuckle meat
- one canada of water
- roots of wild chicory
- leaves of chervil
- leaves of scolopendra
- leaves of pimpinella
- leaves of agrimony
- leaves of sorrel
- one ounce of iron filings well washed in hot water
- one dram of wormwood salt
- another dram of bruised rhubarb

## Directions

1. Bring one canada of water to boil with one arratel of veal knuckle meat
2. When boiling, add roots of wild chicory, leaves of chervil, scolopendra, pimpinella, agrimony, and sorrel, a little of each thing, all well washed and finely chopped
3. Add one ounce of iron filings well washed in hot water and tied in a cloth
4. Add one dram of wormwood salt and another dram of bruised rhubarb
5. After everything has boiled and the broth is reduced to half, remove the cloth with the filings
6. Strain through a napkin and press gently
7. Divide the broth into two doses, taking one in the morning on an empty stomach and the other four hours after dinner
8. Purge at the beginning, middle, and end of the time that you continue to take it
