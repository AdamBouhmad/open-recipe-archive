## Ingredients

- turbot cut in portions the thickness of a finger
- butter
- pepper
- fine herbs
- salt
- chopped chives
- bread crumb
- remoulade or hot garlic sauce

## Directions

1. Cut turbot into portions the thickness of a finger
2. Marinate for two or three hours in melted butter, pepper, fine herbs, salt, and chopped chives
3. Cover with bread crumb and roast on the grill on both sides, dripping with melted butter from time to time
4. When roasted and with good color, serve with hot remoulade or hot garlic sauce
