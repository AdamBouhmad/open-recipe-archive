## Ingredients

- Some sour apples
- Brandy
- Sugar
- A piece of lemon peel
- Flower water
- Flour
- Pork lard

## Directions

1. Take some sour apples, peel them, cut them into quarters and remove the seeds
2. Marinate for two or three hours in brandy with sugar, a piece of lemon peel, and flower water
3. Drain them on cloth, dust with flour, and fry in pork lard
4. When fried and with good color, serve glazed with fine sugar, as with the preceding recipe
5. Alternatively, cut the apples into rounds, pass them through a batter, fry them, and serve in the same manner
