## Ingredients

- lamb chitterlings
- pork fat (toucinho)
- aromatics (cheiros)
- egg yolks
- yellow broth
- cinnamon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Braise the chitterlings in a small pot cut into slices with pork fat and aromatics
2. Bind with egg yolks and yellow broth
3. Place on slices of bread and serve with cinnamon and lemon juice on top
