## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- half dozen egg whites
- six egg yolks
- chopped sorrel
- capers
- salt
- chives, finely chopped
- pepper

## Directions

1. Break eggs into a plate with butter at the bottom
2. Beat half dozen egg whites separately until stiff
3. Fold in six yolks with chopped sorrel, capers, salt, and finely chopped chives
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Beat and mix everything together
6. Cover the eggs already in the plate with this mixture
7. Bake in the oven or under a cover
8. When cooked and without hardening them, serve hot for an entrée
