## Ingredients

- Tripe or intestines
- Veal feet and hands
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Broth
- Vinegar
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. The tripe, or intestines, the hands, and the feet of veal, cleaned and blanched in hot water, can be cooked in a white braise, and served natural with salt, pepper, chopped parsley, little broth, and good vinegar
2. The same hands and feet after being cooked, and cut into pieces, are served as a fricassee; they are also served as a marinade and fried
3. Also the tripe after being cut into pieces, and placed in a good flavored gravy sauce, serve
