## Ingredients

- kid goat or lamb, whole or in quarters
- paper sheets buttered with butter
- bacon, finely cut
- shallot sauce
- enxova
- green onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take kid goat or lamb, either whole or in quarters
2. Almost always roast them in a spit covered with buttered paper sheets
3. Alternatively, after soaking in water, lard them with finely cut bacon and covered with buttered paper, place them to roast in the spit
4. When roasted and of good color, serve with a little shallot sauce as a roast dish
5. They can also be served with a sauce made with an enxova and chopped green onion mixed in substance, with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
