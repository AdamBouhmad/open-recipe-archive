## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Oysters
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Line the forms with puff pastry
2. Put a little chopped mixture in them
3. On top of this place an oyster that has been first passed through a little parsley and chopped garlic
4. Season with pepper
5. Cover with another piece of chopped mixture and this with washed butter
6. Put its pastry cover
7. Gild, cook in the oven
8. Once cooked, serve hot
