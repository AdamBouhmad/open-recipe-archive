## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- beef fat, chopped
- eight or ten eggs
- Alicante pears
- sugar
- a small amount of brandy
- salt
- nutmeg flower, grated
- cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with chopped beef fat, eight or ten eggs, Alicante pears, sugar, and a small amount of brandy
2. Season with salt, nutmeg flower, and ground cinnamon
3. Mix everything very well
4. Wrap in a cloth, tie it, and place in boiling water
5. Continue boiling for two to three hours
6. When cooked, drain and remove from the cloth
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with butter sauce
