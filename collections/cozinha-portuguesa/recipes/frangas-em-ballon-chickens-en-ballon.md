## Ingredients

- Two or three chickens
- Chicken breasts
- Partridge
- Turkey
- Game meat
- Ham
- Truffles
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Pork fat

## Directions

1. Take two or three chickens, debone them as above, and stuff with a ground mixture made of chicken breasts, partridge, turkey, or any other tender game, ham, truffles, all cut into small dice, mixed with scraped bacon, and seasoned with salt, pepper, fine herbs
2. After filling the chickens, tie around and close them like a purse so they remain round like a buoy
3. Place to sear in pork fat
