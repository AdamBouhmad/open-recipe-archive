## Ingredients

- veal, ham, game, and roots (for the restorative)
- herbs such as spinach, sorrel, parsley, tarragon

## Directions

1. Make a restorative with veal, ham, game, and roots
2. Clarify it
3. Pound the desired quality of herbs such as spinach, sorrel, parsley, tarragon
4. After pounding, squeeze through a cloth
5. Pour into the restorative and serve as the previous preparation
6. With poached eggs on top
