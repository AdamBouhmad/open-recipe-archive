## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- chives
- parsley
- butter
- cooked game birds
- clarified butter
- breadcrumbs
- grated cheese
- essence sauce

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and season with salt, pepper, chopped chives and parsley
2. Make three or four bundles with butter
3. Cover each one with chopped cooked meat from game birds such as partridges, chickens, turkeys, etc., of good taste and well finished
4. Roll as other meatballs
5. Arrange on a tortilla pan
6. Sprinkle with clarified butter and egg yolks
7. Cover with breadcrumbs and fine grated cheese
8. Brown in the oven
9. Once browned and cooked, cut into pieces and serve with clear essence sauce as an entrée
