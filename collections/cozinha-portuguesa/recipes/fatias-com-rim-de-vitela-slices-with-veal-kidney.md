## Ingredients

- veal kidney, roasted with all its fat
- lemon zest, grated
- four egg yolks
- egg whites, beaten
- powdered sugar
- slices of bread crumb, two fingers wide
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a roasted veal kidney with all its fat, pound it in a mortar with a little grated lemon zest, four egg yolks and their beaten whites, and powdered sugar
2. Cut slices of bread crumb two fingers wide and place them in a buttered baking dish
3. Garnish the slices with the pounded kidney mixture
4. Bake in the oven
5. When cooked, cover with fine sugar, glaze with a hot spatula, and serve hot as an entrée
