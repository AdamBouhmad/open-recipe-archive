## Ingredients

- Half arratel of sweet almonds
- Half dozen bitter almonds
- Milk
- Aspic broth
- Sugar
- Two cloves
- One stick of cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Salt
- Orange flower water

## Directions

1. Grind in a stone mortar half arratel of sweet almonds and half dozen bitter almonds, moistening from time to time with a little milk
2. After ground and dissolved in two or three spoonfuls of aspic broth, season with sugar, two cloves, one stick of cinnamon, lemon zest, and salt
3. Place on the heat, and when hot, strain with force two or three times through a cloth
4. Add a little milk to it
5. Pour into its dish and add some drops of orange flower water
6. Pass a sheet of white paper over it, and when cold and set, serve for a side dish
