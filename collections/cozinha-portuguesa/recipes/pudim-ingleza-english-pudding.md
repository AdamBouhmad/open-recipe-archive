## Ingredients

- one or two pounds of flour
- water or milk
- chopped beef fat
- eight or ten eggs
- Alicante raisins
- some sugar
- a brandy coin's worth of brandy
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Dissolve one or two pounds of flour in water or milk, then add to it chopped beef fat, eight or ten eggs, Alicante raisins, some sugar, a brandy coin's worth of brandy
2. Season with salt, nutmeg flower, and ground cinnamon
3. Mix everything together very well, put it in a cloth napkin, and put it into boiling water, and continue to boil for two to three hours
4. When cooked, let it drain, then remove from the cloth napkin, dust with sugar, and finish with butter sauce
