## Ingredients

- paio (Portuguese cured sausage)
- four arratels of ham
- wine
- bacon
- pepper
- clove
- thyme
- marjoram

## Directions

1. After soaking a paio and four arratels of ham for twelve hours in good wine, mince everything very well
2. Braise it in melted bacon with good pepper, clove, thyme, marjoram, and a little wine in which it was soaked
3. When everything has simmered, pour it into thick cloth and make cheese in the form described above
4. This cheese is cold food
