## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- salt
- pepper
- 4 beaten eggs
- pork butter
- bread slices
- lemon

## Directions

1. After half-roasting two chickens, cut them in half and salt and pepper them
2. Pass them through four beaten eggs; once well coated, fry them in pork butter until well browned
3. Place on slices of bread, garnished with slices of lemon on top
