## Ingredients

- Gannets
- Eel fillets
- Fish roe
- Gannet livers
- Butter
- Fine herbs
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- Sour orange peel, chopped
- Egg yolks
- Fish stock
- Red wine
- Parsley
- Fine herbs
- Pennyroyal sprigs
- Onion studded with cloves
- Garlic cloves
- Turnips
- Bread crumbs, fried

## Directions

1. After cleaning the gannets, blanch and lard them with eel fillets and fish roe
2. Prepare a stuffing of gannet livers, butter, fine herbs, parsley, half a garlic clove, seasoned with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, small pieces of chopped sour orange peel, bound with two egg yolks
3. Stuff the gannets and sew the opening with thread, tie with string
4. Place in a pan with fish stock, a glass of red wine, seasoned with salt and pepper, parsley, fine herbs, sprigs of pennyroyal, an onion studded with cloves, and two garlic cloves
5. Allow to simmer little by little for four or five hours
6. Once cooked, remove from the pan, prepare a ragout of turnips cooked in the same broth, and after binding and removing the fat, add pieces of fried bread crumbs
7. Serve with sour orange or lemon juice
8. They may also be served with ragouts and different garnishes such as oysters, mussels, and others
