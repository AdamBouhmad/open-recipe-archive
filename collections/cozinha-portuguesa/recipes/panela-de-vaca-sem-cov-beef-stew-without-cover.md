## Ingredients

- beef
- bacon
- six large onions
- sage
- marjoram
- oregano
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Wash the beef and place it to cook in a pot with a piece of bacon
2. When cooked, add six large onions with some cuts in the middle of each so they do not break, and whole spices: a bundle of herbs, sage, marjoram, oregano, and cilantro
3. When very well seasoned, remove it from the fire, and after half an hour send it to the table
4. This dish is suitable for the months of May, June, July, August
