## Ingredients

- pork head
- ground spices
- saffron

## Directions

1. Cook the pork head as described above
2. After cooking, mince it very well at the chopping block and season with ground spices without saffron
3. Place in a bread mold or form and press in a press
4. This cheese is not placed in vinegar but is kept until it pastes
5. This can also be made from wild boar head
