## Ingredients

- peas
- water
- salt
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the peas in boiling water with salt and a piece of sugar
2. When cooked, drain them through a sieve, pass them to a casserole with butter, give them two turns on the heat, and serve for an entre-mets
