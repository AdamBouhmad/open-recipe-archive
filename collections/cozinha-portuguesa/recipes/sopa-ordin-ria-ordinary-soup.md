## Ingredients

- bread cut into thin slices
- broth from the pot
- one spoon of veal or beef substance, with the fat removed

## Directions

1. Cut bread into thin slices and soak with broth from the pot
2. Add one spoon of veal or beef substance, with the fat removed
3. Let it boil for a moment and serve hot
