## Ingredients

- pieces of veal and ham
- sufficient broth
- one quarter of white wine
- a bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- a sprig of mint
- coriander seed
- two cloves
- nutmeg
- ginger
- cloves of garlic
- four shallots
- salt
- pepper
- slices of lemon without peel
- two small spoons of fine olive oil
- good white vinegar

## Directions

1. Put in a pan pieces of veal and ham, sufficient broth, one quarter of white wine, a bundle of parsley and fine herbs, a sprig of mint, coriander seed, two cloves, nutmeg, ginger, cloves of garlic, four shallots, salt, pepper, slices of lemon without peel, and two small spoons of fine olive oil, and let it simmer little by little; when reduced, add to it half a cup of good white vinegar, and let it simmer; when simmering, remove the olive oil from it, bind it with half a spoonful of cullis, pass it through a sieve, and tasting good, serve it for whatever is desired.
