## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Whether whole or cut in half, put apricots in boiling water, give them two boils, and pass them to cold water.
2. Drain them through a sieve, place on heat in sugar at point, give them three or four boils, and skim very well.
3. Arrange on a plate, reduce the sugar, pour on top, and serve.
