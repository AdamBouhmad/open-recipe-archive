## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Butter
- Chives
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic clove
- Bay leaf
- Cullis
- Orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the mussels and remove them from the shells, cleaning them as done previously
2. Pass them through the fire in a saucepan with butter, chives, and chopped parsley
3. Add two small spoonfuls of their own water
4. Season with a little pepper, one clove of garlic, and a bay leaf
5. Bind with a little cullis
6. Leave for a moment over gentle heat without allowing to boil
7. Serve with orange or lemon juice
