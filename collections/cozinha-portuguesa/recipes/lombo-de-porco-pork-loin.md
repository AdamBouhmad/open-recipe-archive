## Ingredients

- pork loin
- fat and bones removed
- bacon lards
- salt
- pepper
- bacon
- water
- onion
- cloves
- wine
- vinegar
- toasted flour
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. After removing the fat and bones from the loin, cut it into pieces of three quarters each
2. Season with salt and pepper, and lard the pieces of loin lengthwise with thick bacon lards
3. Fry them in a pot with melted bacon, cleaned of scraps, so they become well browned
4. Cook them in water in a pot, and when they are half cooked, add an onion studded with cloves, a little wine, a splash of vinegar
5. After cooking, add a little toasted flour dissolved in the same broth of the loin to make the sauce thick
6. Send to the table on toasted bread slices
