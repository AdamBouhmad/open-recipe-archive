## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- six egg yolks
- broth
- half a spoon of culinary salt
- ground pepper
- breadcrumbs
- slices of bread toasted with eggs
- fried

## Directions

1. After cooking in a pot in the ordinary manner, remove to a platter
2. After trimming, take six egg yolks mixed in a little broth, half a spoon of salt, and ground pepper
3. Thickening all this over the fire, spread it over the piece of beef, which is covered with breadcrumbs
4. After browning, serve it garnished with slices of bread toasted with eggs and fried
