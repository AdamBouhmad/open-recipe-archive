## Ingredients

- Half a dozen veal ears
- Broth
- White wine
- Onion slices without skin
- Parsley
- Laurel
- Marjoram
- Onion studded with cloves
- Whole pepper
- Lard slices

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen well-scalded veal ears, and very white, place them to cook in a pot with broth a quarter of white wine, slices of an onion without skin, a pinch of salt, laurel, and marjoram, an onion studded with cloves, whole pepper, and slices of lard; after cooking slowly little by little, serve them with spicy sauce
2. They can also be stuffed with chopped à la crème, well bound, and passed through eggs, and breadcrumbs two or three times, and served fried garnished with gravy
