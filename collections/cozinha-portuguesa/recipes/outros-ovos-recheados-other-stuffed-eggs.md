## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- sorrel leaves
- borage leaves
- chervil
- parsley
- butter
- hard egg yolks, one dozen
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- salt
- pepper
- garlic, half clove
- raw egg yolks
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil in water lettuce leaves, sorrel leaves, borage leaves, a little chervil, and a little parsley
2. After chopping all and squeezing out, sauté in a pot with butter
3. After sautéing, pound with one dozen hard egg yolks, bread soaked in milk, seasoned with salt, pepper, and half a clove of garlic, bind with raw egg yolks
4. Stuff the eggs as said above, cover with fine breadcrumbs, brown them, and serve hot
5. Also after being stuffed, they can be served à Basilica, that is, fried and garnished with fried parsley
