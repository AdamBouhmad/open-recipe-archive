## Ingredients

- Spinach leaves, boiled and chopped
- Butter or melted salt pork
- Ham essence or good broth
- Béchamel sauce
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Blanch and cook the spinach leaves in plain water, chop them, and press them well
2. Sauté them in butter or melted salt pork
3. After sautéing, add a good ham essence or broth, or if not available, moisten with béchamel sauce
4. Bind with egg yolks and serve underneath first courses, larded with ham and other suitable garnishes
