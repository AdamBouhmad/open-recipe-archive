## Ingredients

- wine
- vinegar
- garlic
- oregano
- salt
- fish steaks
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Preserve two fish steaks in a mixture of wine, vinegar, garlic, oregano, and salt for two hours
2. Remove the fish from the preserve and roast it
3. Place the fish on a plate
4. Make a sauce from some of the preserve in which it was kept with a splash of olive oil
5. Pour the sauce over the fish and serve
