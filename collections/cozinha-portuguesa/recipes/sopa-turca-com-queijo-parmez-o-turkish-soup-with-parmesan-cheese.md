## Ingredients

- One twenty-reis loaf of bread
- Clarified cow butter
- Broth and substance
- Grated cheese
- A cooked hen

## Directions

1. Cut a twenty-reis loaf of bread into two halves and make it into very thin slices
2. Fry them in clarified cow butter
3. When fried and well-colored, place to drain over a sieve
4. After draining, moisten with broth and a little substance
5. Place to boil for an instant
6. After boiling, serve with grated cheese on top and a cooked hen in the middle
