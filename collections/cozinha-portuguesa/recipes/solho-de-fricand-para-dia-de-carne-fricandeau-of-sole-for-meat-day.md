## Ingredients

- Fine lard on one side only
- A portion of sole the width of two fingers
- Broth
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chives
- A little thyme
- Bay leaf
- Marjoram
- Three cloves of India
- Two garlic cloves
- Two slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Lard with fine lard on one side only a portion of sole the width of two fingers
2. Place it to cook in a broth made of broth, white wine, salt, pepper, a bunch of parsley, chives, a little thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, all tied in a bundle, three cloves of India, two garlic cloves, and two slices of lemon
3. When cooked, remove it
4. Place it, larded side up, on a veal aspic
5. When of good color, serve with the sauce or with a garnish of herbs or vegetables as you prefer
