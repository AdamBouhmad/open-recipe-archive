## Ingredients

- cream cooked with flour and sugar
- 7 or 8 egg whites, beaten until stiff
- thin slices of Savoy biscuits

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cream with flour and sugar as in Crema Queimada
2. Mix in 7 or 8 stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Place in a plate to a depth of one finger's height
4. Arrange thin slices of Savoy biscuits on top
5. Cover with another layer of cream
6. Add another layer of biscuit slices on top of that
7. Add another layer of cream, and continue this way until the plate is full
8. Sprinkle sugar on top
9. Place in the oven to cook
10. When cooked and browned, serve hot as an entremets
