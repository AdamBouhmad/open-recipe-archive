## Ingredients

- half a canada of milk
- a quarter of butter
- a quarter of sugar
- an eighth of flour
- a dozen eggs
- oil or melted butter for frying
- sugar in thread stage
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put half a canada of milk in a pot to boil
2. When it boils, add a quarter of butter, a quarter of sugar, and an eighth of flour
3. Cook this mixture, stirring until it becomes very hard
4. Remove the pot from the fire and add a dozen eggs, two by two, beating the mixture very well until it becomes soft
5. Fry in boiling oil or butter using a piping bag greased with butter or oil
6. When fried, pass through sugar in thread stage with cinnamon on top and serve at the table
