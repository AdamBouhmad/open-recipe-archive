## Ingredients

- slices of bread fried in butter
- fricassee of hard eggs, well-bound
- other cooked eggs
- clarified butter
- egg yolks
- bread and grated cheese, mixed

## Directions

1. Put in the serving plate slices of bread fried in butter
2. On top place a well-bound and well-flavored fricassee of hard eggs
3. Cover with another well-made bundle of cooked eggs
4. Sprinkle with clarified butter and some egg yolks
5. Cover with bread and grated cheese mixed together
6. Bake in the oven or under a cover until it takes color
7. Serve hot for an entrée
