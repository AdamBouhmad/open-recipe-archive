## Ingredients

- a piece of ordinary dough
- butter
- a dozen apples
- grated sugar
- eggs
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Extend a piece of ordinary dough on a buttered cloth napkin, arrange on it a dozen apples cut into slices and grated sugar
2. Fold the ends of the dough and unite them with beaten eggs, tie with string or in a cloth napkin, and put into boiling water, in which it will continue for two hours
3. Then let it drain, and serve dusted with sugar with butter sauce on top
