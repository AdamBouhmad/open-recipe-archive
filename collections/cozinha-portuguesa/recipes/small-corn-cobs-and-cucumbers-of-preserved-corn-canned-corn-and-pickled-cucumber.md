## Ingredients

- corn cobs, still green
- water
- vinegar
- cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Take the quantity of corn cobs that seems right, which should still be green and tender like marrow, and boil them in water for half a quarter of an hour
2. Pass them afterwards to cold water and drain them in a bowl
3. Pour over them a hot brine made with water and a third part vinegar, some cloves and salt, and place them on hot ashes until the next day
4. On that day pour over them another equal quantity of brine, which should be done until they become well preserved
5. Afterwards put them in bottles, seal them and keep them in a cool place to use them when needed
6. The same shall be done to cucumbers, which the French call cornichons
