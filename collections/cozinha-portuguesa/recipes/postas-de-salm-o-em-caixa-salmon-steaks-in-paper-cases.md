## Ingredients

- two or three salmon steaks, two fingers thick
- good olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- small onions
- truffles
- cucumbers
- garlic clove
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Marinate two or three salmon steaks, two fingers thick, in good olive oil, salt, pepper, parsley, small onions, truffles, cucumbers, and a garlic clove, all finely chopped
2. Make paper boxes lined inside with butter and place in each one its salmon steak with all the marinade
3. Grill on a slow fire on both sides
4. Once grilled, squeeze lemon juice on top and serve in the same boxes
