## Ingredients

- chickens
- beef fat
- aromatic herbs
- beef broth
- slices of paio

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens in beef fat with a head of aromatic herbs
2. Once cooked, make a soup of the same broth; place the chickens on top
3. Arrange slices of paio around the plate and send to the table
