## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the quinces into quarters, clean and trim them without seeds or core
2. Cook until soft, then pass through a fine sieve
3. Place two arratels of sugar at thread point with one arratel of the quince mass
4. Once the mass is well dissolved in the sugar, place the pan on the fire just until it comes to a boil
5. Remove from the fire and add amber or musk if desired
6. Place in small paper cones and set to dry in the sun
