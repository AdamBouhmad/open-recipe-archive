## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter or melted bacon fat
- ham
- herbs
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot water
- broth
- white wine
- egg yolks
- pickled parsley
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Cut and prepare in the same manner as the preceding recipes
2. The difference is that after soaking, add a glass of white wine that has been boiled, and thicken with egg yolks mixed in broth, pickled parsley, and lime juice
