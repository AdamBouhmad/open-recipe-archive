## Ingredients

- good butter
- flour
- broth
- one chopped anchovy
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pulverize flour with a good piece of fresh butter
2. Put it in a pan with a spoonful of broth
3. Add one chopped anchovy that has been rinsed with salt, season with salt and pepper
4. Put on heat, stirring constantly until it is bound
5. Then add lemon juice
6. This sauce serves for any game or fish
