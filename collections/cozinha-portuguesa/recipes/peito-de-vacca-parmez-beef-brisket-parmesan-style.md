## Ingredients

- A whole beef brisket
- thick bacon for larding
- twine
- beef slices
- veal slices
- bacon slices
- onions cut in rounds
- carrots
- parsnips
- white wine
- broth
- salt
- grated cheese
- ham essence or spicy sauce or other sauce as preferred

## Directions

1. Take a whole beef brisket, remove the larger bones, and lard it with thick bacon through the middle as possible
2. Tie it very well with twine
3. Place to cook in a proportioned pot so it fits snugly, on the bottom of which have first been placed some slices of beef, veal, and bacon
4. On top of the beef brisket place onions cut in rounds, carrots, parsnips, with other slices of beef and bacon
5. Cover the pot very well and place between moderate fire below and above to braise for about an hour
6. Dampen with white wine and broth; let simmer gently to cook slowly
7. When cooked, place on the plate in which it is to be served with a little salt-free broth and grated cheese on top
8. Place in the oven in this form and when well colored, serve with ham essence or spicy sauce or any other sauce as preferred
