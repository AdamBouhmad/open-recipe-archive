## Ingredients

- pigeons, opened along the back, breast, and legs
- finely minced and seasoned mutton

## Directions

1. Open the pigeons along the back, breast, and legs
2. Stuff them with finely minced and seasoned mutton (prepared as in the mutton stuffing recipe)
3. Cook with gentle heat in a shallow bowl with simmering broth
4. Season with all spices and thicken so the broth becomes thick in the plates with egg yolks
5. Place on slices with lemon juice and cinnamon on top
