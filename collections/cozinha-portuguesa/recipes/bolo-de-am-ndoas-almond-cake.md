## Ingredients

- half an ounce of flour
- four eggs with their whites
- a little bit of butter
- a quarter of fine sugar
- some grains of salt
- six ounces of ground almonds

## Directions

1. Mix everything together, and when it is bound and of good consistency, form the cake
2. Place on buttered paper
3. Put in the oven to bake with moderate heat
4. After baking, when browned and glazed, serve when desired
