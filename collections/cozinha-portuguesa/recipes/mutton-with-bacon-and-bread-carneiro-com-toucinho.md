## Ingredients

- mutton, split in half
- half arratel of bacon
- herbs and seasonings
- all spices
- cheese, half arratel, grated
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Split the mutton in half with half arratel of bacon and its herbs
2. Season with all spices
3. When almost cooked, strain the broth into a hollow dish or frying pan over slices of bread covered with half arratel of grated cheese
4. Place over hot coals
5. Put the mutton on top with lemon juice and serve
6. It can also be made with roasted mutton
