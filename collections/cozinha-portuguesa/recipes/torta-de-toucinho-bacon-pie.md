## Ingredients

- One arratel of finely chopped bacon
- One arratel of finely ground almonds
- One arratel of sugar in thread stage
- Ambergris or amber
- Cinnamon
- Indian clove
- Eight egg yolks
- One eighth of flour
- Half an arratel of butter
- Four egg yolks
- Half an arratel of sugar
- One cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Cook in a hollow pot one arratel of finely chopped bacon with one arratel of finely ground almonds and one arratel of sugar in thread stage
2. When cooked, add ambergris or amber, cinnamon, and Indian clove
3. Thicken with eight egg yolks and let cool
4. Make dough with one eighth of flour, half an arratel of butter, four egg yolks, half an arratel of sugar, and one cup of white wine
5. After kneading, make two cakes, place the aforementioned bacon mixture on one, cover with the other cake, work the pie well, cook on gentle heat, and serve
