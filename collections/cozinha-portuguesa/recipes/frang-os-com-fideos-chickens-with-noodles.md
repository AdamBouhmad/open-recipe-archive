## Ingredients

- 2 chickens in pieces
- one quarter of bacon
- aromatic herbs
- vinegar
- spices
- fideos pasta
- lemon

## Directions

1. Braise two chickens in pieces with one quarter of bacon, aromatic herbs, and vinegar
2. When half-cooked, season with all the seasonings
3. Add fideos pasta (which are like toasted pine nuts) to the stew
4. Remove from the heat and place in embers while awaiting service
5. When serving, add lemon juice on top
