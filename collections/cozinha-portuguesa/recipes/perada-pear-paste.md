## Ingredients

- pears
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the pears and place three arratels of them to cook in water
2. Once cooked, grate or pound them and add to four arratels of sugar at esparto point
3. Stir until dry
4. Once dry, remove from the fire and add fragrance if desired
5. Place in small paper cones
