## Ingredients

- two halves of ray
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- breadcrumbs
- remoulade sauce or anchovy coulis
- ray liver

## Directions

1. After the ray is cleaned, skinned, and trimmed, cut it into two halves
2. Melt a piece of butter in a casserole
3. Place the ray inside, season with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
4. Remove and cover with fine breadcrumbs
5. Place on grills to roast, basting from time to time
6. When roasted on both sides and of good color, serve with remoulade sauce or, if not available, with anchovy coulis and the ray liver pounded, making sure it is hot and spicy
