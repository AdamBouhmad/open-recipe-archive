## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar
- milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put the strawberries on the heat in a saucepan with sugar and crush them with a spoon
2. Add to milk and egg yolks
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Pour into the plate
5. Cook as the preceding one
6. Serve garnished with whole strawberries around the edge
