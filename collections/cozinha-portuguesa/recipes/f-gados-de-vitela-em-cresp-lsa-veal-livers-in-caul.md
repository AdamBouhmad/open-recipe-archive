## Ingredients

- Veal livers
- Beef fat
- Bone marrow from bread cooked in milk
- Two large onions
- Fine herbs
- Cucumbers
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Lard slices
- Pork caul
- Egg whites beaten
- Ham essence
- Verjuice or lemon juice

## Directions

1. The veal livers, with an equal amount of beef fat, are pounded in a mortar, adding to it a bone marrow of bread cooked in milk, two large onions cut into cubes, and passed through butter, fine herbs, cucumbers, salt, pepper, egg yolks; pounded, add beaten egg whites to it; line a pot with slices of lard, and place on it a pork caul, or well washed and well dried caul, pour over it the pounded liver, cover it with the same caul well folded and moistened with beaten eggs, so that it remains stuck; cover it with paper, and place it to cook on a gentle fire above and below; when cooked, turn it over on a plate, and after drying and in fat, serve it with sauce of clear ham essence, and verjuice or lemon juice
