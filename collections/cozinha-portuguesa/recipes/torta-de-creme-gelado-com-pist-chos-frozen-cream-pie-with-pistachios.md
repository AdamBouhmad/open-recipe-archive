## Ingredients

- Half pound of pistachios
- Three quarter-liters of milk, boiled
- Half pound of sugar
- A piece of cinnamon stick
- Lemon peel
- Twelve egg yolks
- Snow
- Puff pastry or almond dough or hazelnut dough or sugar, flour and eggs dough

## Directions

1. Shell the pistachios, remove the skin and grind them in a stone mortar, wetting them occasionally with a drop of milk
2. Dissolve the ground pistachios in three quarter-liters of boiled milk with half pound of sugar, a piece of cinnamon stick and lemon peel
3. When somewhat warm, strain everything through a sieve with twelve egg yolks and return to the fire, stirring constantly with a spoon
4. When bound and before it boils, let it cool
5. Place in a freezer with snow around it and stir while freezing as is customary
6. Serve on bases of almond dough, hazelnut dough or sugar, flour and eggs dough
7. For frozen fruit pies, when the milk is tempered with salt, sugar, cinnamon and lemon peel with beaten egg yolks, bind it on the fire, and after binding, strain it through a sieve with any fruit cooked first in sugar, freeze as above and serve in the same manner
