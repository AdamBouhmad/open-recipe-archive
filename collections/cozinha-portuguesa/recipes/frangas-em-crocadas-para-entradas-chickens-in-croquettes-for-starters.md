## Ingredients

- Two or three fat chickens
- Bacon slices
- Ham
- Bacon strips
- Bay leaf
- Bundle of parsley
- Coriander seeds
- Cow's milk
- Butter
- Flour
- Two egg yolks
- Bread crumbs
- Pepper sauce or other sauce

## Directions

1. Take two or three fat chickens, prepare them as is customary, and after cleaning and cooking, lard them with thick bacon and ham.
2. Line the bottom of a casserole with bacon slices, place the chickens on top, season with salt, whole pepper, one bay leaf, one bundle of parsley, a little dried coriander seed.
3. Cover with other bacon slices and place to cook slowly over a low heat, then moisten with boiling cow's milk and let cook on low heat, simmering gently.
4. When cooked, let cool, then cover with a thickened sauce made with a piece of butter dusted with flour, two egg yolks, and a drop of milk.
5. Cover with fine breadcrumbs, place in the oven to brown, and serve browned with pepper sauce or any other sauce that seems better.
