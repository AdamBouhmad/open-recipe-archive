## Ingredients

- chickens
- wine
- vinegar
- garlic
- oregano
- pork butter
- slices of bread
- bacon
- spices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Keep the chickens for the space of two hours in a preserve of wine, vinegar, garlic, and oregano
2. Fry them in pork butter with slices of bread
3. When done, melt a little chopped bacon; when melted, add a little of the preserve from the same chickens
4. Season with all the spices and bring the chickens to a boil in this sauce
5. After passing through the boil, send to the table with lemon juice and sliced lemon on top
