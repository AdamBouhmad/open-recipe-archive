## Ingredients

- Cabbages
- Half a dozen onions
- Two carrots
- One parsnip
- Salsify roots
- Vegetable broth and substance
- Salt
- Two cloves
- A piece of melted and browned butter
- Slices of bread

## Directions

1. Trim and tie the cabbages as in the Italian soup
2. Place them in a pot with half a dozen onions, two carrots, one parsnip, and salsify roots
3. Add enough vegetable broth and substance
4. Season with salt, two cloves, and a piece of melted and browned butter
5. When cooked, soak some slices of bread in the same broth from the cabbages
6. Simmer and serve surrounded with the same cabbages on top
