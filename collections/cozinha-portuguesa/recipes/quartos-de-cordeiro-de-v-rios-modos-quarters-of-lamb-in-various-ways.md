## Ingredients

- roasted lamb or kid goat quarters
- butter
- ham pieces
- onions
- carrots
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- cloves
- egg yolks
- verjuice or white vinegar or lemon juice
- milk
- velouté sauce

## Directions

1. After the lamb or kid goat quarters are roasted and cooled, remove all the meat and cut it into fillets
2. Serve with white fricassée sauce or béchamel
3. For white fricassée: place butter, ham pieces, onions, carrots (all dusted with flour) in a casserole and pass through the fire
4. Moisten with broth and season with salt, pepper, a bundle of fine herbs, and two cloves
5. When cooked and reduced, pass everything through a sieve and then place the fillets inside to warm without boiling
6. When warm, bind with three egg yolks, adding a little verjuice, or if not available, white vinegar or lemon juice
7. They may also be served à béchamel: cut the ham, veal, and carrot pieces into dice and instead of broth moisten with milk and a little velouté, finishing as the white fricassée described above
