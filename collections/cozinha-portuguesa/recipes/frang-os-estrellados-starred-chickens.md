## Ingredients

- 4 chickens
- salt
- pepper
- melted bacon
- toasted slices
- bacon
- lemon
- sausages
- bacon strips of ham or loin

## Directions

1. Cook four chickens in a pot; when cooked, remove whole
2. Salt and pepper well, then fry in melted bacon until browned
3. Place on toasted slices with bacon on top and lemon juice
4. Garnish this plate with sausages and bacon strips of ham or loin, and send to the table
