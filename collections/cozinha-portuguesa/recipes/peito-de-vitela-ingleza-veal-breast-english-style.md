## Ingredients

- large veal breast
- thick bacon for larding
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- nutmeg
- parsley
- shallots
- thyme
- marjoram
- cloth
- twine
- broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take a large veal breast and remove the bones
2. Open it in the middle and lard with thick bacon and ham
3. Season with salt, pepper, fine spices, nutmeg, minced parsley, shallots, thyme, and marjoram
4. Let it rest seasoned for one day to the next
5. Roll it tightly and wrap in cloth, tie with twine
6. Braise in a wet broth with broth and white wine
7. Once cooked, let it cool in its own braise
8. After cooling and trimming, serve whole on a napkin
