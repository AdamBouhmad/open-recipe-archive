## Ingredients

- Turkey (singed and cleaned)
- Gizzards (molejas)
- Fat livers (figados gordos)
- Truffles (trufas)
- Ham planks (pranchas de presunto)
- Scraped bacon (toucinho raspado)
- Saffron (salsa)
- Shallot (chalota)
- Fine herbs (hervas finas)
- Garlic clove (dente de alho)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Pork lard (manteiga de porco)
- Bacon planks (pranchas de toucinho)
- Veal pieces (bocados de vitella)
- Onions
- Fine herbs
- Broth (caldo)
- Half a canada of white wine (meia canada de vinho branco)

## Directions

1. After the turkey is singed and cleaned, open it along the ribs, remove all the bones.
2. Take some gizzards, fat livers, truffles, and ham planks, cut everything into small cubes, and mix them with scraped bacon, saffron, shallot, fine herbs, a clove of garlic, little salt, and pepper.
3. Fill the turkey with this stuffing, sew it all around with a needle and twine, and close it in such a way that it becomes entirely round, like a ball.
4. After preparing it in pork lard, put it to cook in a braze with bacon planks, pieces of veal, onions, seasonings, fine herbs, a little broth, and half a canada of white wine.
5. When cooked, remove the twine and serve it with a good sauce or ragout, or with the sauce that seems best.
