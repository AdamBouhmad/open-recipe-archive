## Ingredients

- Half a dozen pears with skin, or six or eight
- Clarified sugar
- A piece of cinnamon
- Two or three cloves
- Half a cup of red wine

## Directions

1. Prepare half a dozen pears with skin, or six or eight, and place them to cook in a bowl or small pan with clarified sugar, a piece of cinnamon, and two or three cloves.
2. Let them simmer little by little.
3. When half cooked, add half a cup of red wine, cover with a lid, and finish cooking slowly.
4. When cooked, place on a plate, reduce the sugar to a high point, pour over hot, and serve.
