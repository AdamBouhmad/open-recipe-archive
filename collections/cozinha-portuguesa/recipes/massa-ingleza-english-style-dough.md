## Ingredients

- half a canada of milk
- half a pound of butter
- a little salt
- lemon peel
- a stick of cinnamon
- flour
- grated sugar

## Directions

1. Place in a pot half a canada of milk, half a pound of butter, a little salt, lemon peel, and a stick of cinnamon
2. When boiling, add the necessary flour as for fitters, and after dried on the fire and cooled, extend with the rolling pin and cut into equal squares
3. Fry in butter, and serve with grated sugar on top and glazed with a hot spatula
