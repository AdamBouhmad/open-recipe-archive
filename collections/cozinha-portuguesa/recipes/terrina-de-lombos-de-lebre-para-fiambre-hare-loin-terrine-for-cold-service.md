## Ingredients

- Two or three hares
- Medium bacon for larding, seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, sausage, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, and marjoram
- Good butter
- Half a quartilho of brandy
- A bundle of sausage
- Veal and bacon slices for covering

## Directions

1. Remove the loins and meat from two or three hares
2. After trimming and equalizing them, lard with medium bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, sausage, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, and marjoram, all in powder
3. Arrange in a small pan with good butter, half a quartilho of brandy, and a bundle of sausage
4. Cover with veal and bacon slices
5. Cook over gentle heat for five or six hours
6. When cooked and with little sauce, arrange the loins in their terrine and pour the sauce over them after passing through a sieve
7. Cover with the same bacon slices, let cool, and serve for cold service
