## Ingredients

- young pigeons with down still on
- bacon sheets
- lemon slices without peel
- parsley
- veal sweetbreads
- essence sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The pigeons should still have down on them; after bleeding, plucking, and cleaning, turn them over with feet inside the body
2. Stiffen in hot water, then cook between bacon sheets, two lemon slices without peel, and a sprig of parsley until done
3. Serve garnished with veal sweetbreads, flanked by fine bacon, glazed like fricandeau, with essence sauce and lemon juice
