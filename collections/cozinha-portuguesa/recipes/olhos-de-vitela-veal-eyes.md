## Ingredients

- Veal eyes
- Broth
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shallot
- Verjuice or ham essence

## Directions

1. The veal eyes after being cut all around, and well trimmed, are cooked in a braise, and served in the same manner as the ears mentioned in the preceding Article
