## Ingredients

- one quarter of veal
- thick bacon
- three or four canadian measures of milk
- one arratel of butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- dried coriander
- one clove of garlic
- onion slices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Lard a quarter of veal with thick bacon
2. Make a marinade with three or four canadian measures of milk, one arratel of pulverized butter mixed with flour, salt, pepper, cloves from India, dried coriander, one clove of garlic, onion slices, and parsley
3. Heat the marinade and let it cool
4. Once cold, immerse the quarter of veal in the marinade for ten or twelve hours
5. Remove the veal when it has absorbed the flavor of the marinade
6. Dry it in a clean cloth
7. Place it in a roasting pan covered with bacon slices and paper leaves
8. When ready, serve with spicy sauce or covered with grated bread with pepper sauce
