## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Hot water
- Truffles
- Salsa
- Chives, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Veal, ground (picado)
- Onion studded with cloves
- Fine herbs
- Butter
- Bacon planks
- Pastry box with lid
- Eggs
- Ham sauce
- Veal sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scald the necessary pigeons in hot water
2. Pluck them, cook them, place the feet inside the body
3. Singe, clean, and remove the entrails
4. Sear them in melted bacon with truffles, salsa, chopped chives, and season with salt and pepper
5. Let them cool
6. Meanwhile, garnish the bottom of the pastry box with ground veal
7. Place the pigeons on top with a studded onion, a piece of salsa, and fine herbs
8. Cover with the same ground mixture, butter, and bacon planks
9. Cover with its lid
10. Finish as the previous pies
11. When cooked, remove the salsa, onion, and planks, drain the fat
12. Serve with ham and veal sauce and lemon juice
