## Ingredients

- one arratel of sugar
- two ounces of crushed violet flowers

## Directions

1. Reduce one arratel of sugar to the thread point
2. Add two ounces of crushed violet flowers and stir constantly with a spoon until well mixed with the sugar
3. Pour into a form made of a sheet of paper about half a finger high
4. Mark the pieces with the point of a knife
5. When half cooled, store in a dry place
