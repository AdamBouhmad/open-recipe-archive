## Ingredients

- half a dozen turnips
- one and a half canada of water
- four ounces or six ounces of sugar in thread, clarified

## Directions

1. Peel half a dozen turnips and cut into slices
2. Simmer in a pan with one and a half canada of water
3. When cooked and the broth reduced to half
4. Pour everything into a cloth and press very well
5. Then add four ounces or six ounces of sugar in thread, clarified
6. Take it hot before serving
7. Can also be taken in the morning and for as long as needed
