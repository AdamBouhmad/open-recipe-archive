## Ingredients

- thick bacon
- one large turbot portion
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- cloves of India
- chives
- garlic
- sage
- fine herbs
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Lard a large turbot portion with thick bacon, seasoned as is customary
2. Marinate for two or three hours in warm water, vinegar, salt, pepper, some cloves of India, chives, garlic, sage, fine herbs, and a piece of powdered butter with flour
3. Tie it to a spit, place to roast, dripping with the fattiest part of its marinade
4. When roasted, serve with any ragout or sauce
