## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- salt
- butter
- ham essence
- butter
- coarse pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Trim evenly the tips of the artichoke leaves
2. Leave them to simmer in water and salt with a piece of butter
3. When cooked, place them to drain, remove the hay with a spoon, arrange them on the plate, and serve with sauce of essence bound with butter, coarse pepper, and a dash of vinegar
