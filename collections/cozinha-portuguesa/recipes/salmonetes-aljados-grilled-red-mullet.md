## Ingredients

- red mullet
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- red mullet livers
- good oil
- lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. Remove gills and entrails from red mullet and wash very well
2. Cut off the tail tip
3. Dry in clean cloth
4. Season with salt
5. Dip in melted butter
6. Place on grill to roast with moderate fire until golden on both sides
7. Serve with a sauce made from the fish's own livers, good oil, salt, pepper, and lemon or bitter orange juice
8. Alternatively, after grilling remove the scales and serve with butter sauce containing anchovies, to which half a spoon of shrimp paste or any other preferred sauce may be added
