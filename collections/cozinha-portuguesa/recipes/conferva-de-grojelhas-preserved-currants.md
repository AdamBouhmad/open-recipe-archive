## Ingredients

- one arratel of currants
- two cups of water
- sugar at thread point

## Directions

1. Boil one arratel of currants for an instant in two cups of water
2. Pour with a skimmer into a sieve and strain with force like the sour cherries
3. Take to the heat to dry
4. When dried and dry, add sugar at the thread point and stir for an instant over the heat
5. Pour into a paper mold, let cool, form into pieces, and store like the others
