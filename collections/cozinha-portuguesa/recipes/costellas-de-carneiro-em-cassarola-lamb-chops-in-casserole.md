## Ingredients

- lamb chops, well aged
- salt
- pepper
- butter or rendered pork fat
- white wine
- stock
- shallot
- egg yolks
- green lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take lamb chops that are well mortified and cut them into equal smaller chops
2. Trim them and season with salt and pepper
3. Sauté them in butter or rendered pork fat, turning them from time to time until cooked
4. When browned, remove them and drain the fat from the casserole
5. Moisten what remains with a little white wine and half a spoon of stock, adding a chopped shallot
6. When the sauce is reduced, put the chops back in and before it boils, add three or four egg yolks mixed in green lemon juice
7. When bound and of good taste, serve for an entrance course, or with a spicy sauce or another as preferred
