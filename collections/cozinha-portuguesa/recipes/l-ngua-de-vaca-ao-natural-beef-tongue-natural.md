## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- bacon, thick slices
- parsley
- bay leaf
- thyme
- marjoram
- onion studded with cloves
- celery
- carrots
- parsnips
- white wine
- one cup
- salt
- pepper
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Blanch the beef tongue in boiling water
2. Remove the skin and let it cool
3. Lard it with thick bacon and place it to cook in a light braise
4. Add a bouquet of parsley, bay leaf, thyme, marjoram, an onion studded with cloves, vegetables such as celery, carrots, and parsnips, one cup of white wine, a bacon slice, salt, and pepper
5. Moisten with water or broth
6. When cooked, split it lengthwise along the middle without separating it
7. Serve with a sauce of chopped parsley, a little broth, salt, coarse pepper, and a splash of vinegar
