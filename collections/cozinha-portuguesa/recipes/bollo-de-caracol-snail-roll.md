## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- cream or franchipane cream
- eggs
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Stretch out some puff pastry the length of the table, cut into two strips six fingers wide
2. Put cream or well-bound franchipane cream in the middle, brush the pastry edges with beaten eggs, and roll like a sausage
3. Roll into a snail shape on a greased paper sheet, brush with egg, and bake in the oven
4. When cooked and golden, serve dusted with fine sugar as a between-course dish
5. It can also be filled with marmalade or any fruit preserve
