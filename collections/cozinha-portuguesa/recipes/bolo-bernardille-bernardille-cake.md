## Ingredients

- half an ounce of flour
- four eggs with their whites
- a bit of lime peel, chopped
- a drop of flower water
- simple water

## Directions

1. Mix all together with simple water, and after being well kneaded, let it rest for an hour
2. Then stretch it out, cover with butter, fold it or roll it, stretch it again, finish it like puff pastry, and place in a round shape the thickness of a thumb
3. Put it in the oven to bake
4. Serve glazed with sugar and a hot spatula
