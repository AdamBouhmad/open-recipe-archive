## Ingredients

- ripe quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and clean a dozen ripe quinces inside.
2. Chop and pound them very well.
3. Put them in a cloth and squeeze all the juice forcefully into a bowl.
4. Let it settle, then take the clearest part.
5. For each quart of juice, have one pound of sugar in the hair stage.
6. Adding this juice to the sugar, let it boil until it takes on the consistency of a syrup.
7. When cold, pour into bottles and guard as stated.
