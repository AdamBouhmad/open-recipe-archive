## Ingredients

- lamb sweetbreads
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Skin the sweetbreads, cut into quarters
2. Sprinkle with ground salt and flour
3. At the moment of eating, fry in hot oil
4. After fried and browned, serve garnished with sauce
