## Ingredients

- water
- roots
- onions
- garlic
- salt
- whole pepper
- cloves
- fine herbs
- butter
- turbot
- milk
- béchamel sauce
- shrimp coulis
- curry

## Directions

1. Let half a portion of water simmer little by little for an hour, seasoned with roots, onions, garlic, salt, whole pepper, cloves, and fine herbs, and a piece of butter
2. After it has simmered, strain it through a sieve, put the fish in a large pot with a napkin underneath, pour it the aforementioned broth with as much hot milk, and put it to cook little by little over a gentle fire without raising a boil
3. Once cooked, serve with various sauces, such as Hollandaise sauce, béchamel, shrimp coulis, or any other that seems fitting
