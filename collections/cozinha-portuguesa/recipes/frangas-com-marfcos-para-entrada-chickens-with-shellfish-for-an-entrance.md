## Ingredients

- two or three chickens
- three or four dozen oysters
- ham essence
- butter
- flour
- shallots
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare two or three chickens in the same manner as chickens with fine herbs and roast them
2. For the ragout, blanch three or four dozen oysters in their own water, taking care not to let them boil
3. When cleaned, steep them in ham essence with a piece of butter dusted with flour and finely chopped shallots
4. When the sauce is well thickened, pour it over the chickens and serve with lemon juice
5. These same chickens or capons can also be larded with thick pork fat, cooked in a braising pan, and served with the shellfish ragout that seems best
6. They can also be served with shrimp coulis instead of ham essence
