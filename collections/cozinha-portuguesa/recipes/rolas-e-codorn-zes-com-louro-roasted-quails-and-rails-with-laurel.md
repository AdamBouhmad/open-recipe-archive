## Ingredients

- Roasted quails or rails
- Their own livers
- Scraped bacon
- Sausage
- Chopped chives
- Two egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon slices
- Paper leaves
- Ham stock
- Six laurel leaves
- Orange juice

## Directions

1. After singeing and emptying, stuff with a mixture made from their own livers, scraped bacon, sausage, chopped chives, two egg yolks, seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Put them on a spit covered with bacon slices and paper leaves
3. When roasted, serve with ham stock and six laurel leaves previously boiled in water with orange juice
4. Alternatively, open them along the sides and pass them through the fire in a pan with melted bacon, salt, pepper, sausage, and chopped chives
5. After thus sautéed, remove them, cover with fine breadcrumbs, roast on the grill on both sides, and serve with shallot sauce and orange or lemon juice
