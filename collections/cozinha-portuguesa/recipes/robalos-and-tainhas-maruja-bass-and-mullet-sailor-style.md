## Ingredients

- Bass or mullet, cleaned and prepared
- Equal portions
- One or two dozen small onions, half-cooked in water and drained
- Salt
- pepper
- salted capers
- fine herbs
- One clove-studded onion
- Butter
- flour, browned
- Wine
- Water or broth

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned and prepared fish into equal pieces
2. Arrange in a proportioned casserole with one or two dozen small onions, half-cooked in water and drained
3. Season with salt, pepper, a spoonful of salted capers, fine herbs, and a clove-studded onion
4. Add a broth made of butter and browned flour, wine, and a drop of water or broth on top
5. Let simmer slowly
6. When the fish is cooked and the sauce reduced, remove the capers and clove-studded onion
7. Serve with orange or lemon juice
