## Ingredients

- thistles
- onion
- butter
- milk
- almonds
- egg yolks
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the thistles
2. Cook them in water
3. Once cooked, sauté a small amount of chopped onion with herbs in a quarter of cow butter
4. When sautéed, add the cooked thistles inside
5. Season with salt, half a measure of milk made from half an arroba of almonds, and all the seasonings
6. Bind with six beaten egg yolks
7. Serve at table on slices with lemon juice on top
