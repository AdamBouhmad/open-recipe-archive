## Ingredients

- one chicken
- two arratels of mutton
- half arratel of pork
- all aromatics
- chicken eggs
- vinegar
- four eggs
- one quarter of grated bread
- lard of pork
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop one chicken with two arratels of mutton, half arratel of pork, and all aromatics, leaving the chicken eggs whole
2. Once half of all this meat is braised, return it to the chopping block to be chopped together with the other half
3. Season with all seasonings, vinegar, four eggs, and one quarter of grated bread
4. After the meat is seasoned, arm the chicken with the bones in a buttered frying pan
5. When it reaches the desired size, arrange strips of pork lard on top
6. Place it to cook on low heat from below and on top
7. When cooked, place a cream broth made in the form of a gilt-head bream, and send it to the table
