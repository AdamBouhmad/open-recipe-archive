## Ingredients

- hot water
- chicken testicles and eggs
- cold water
- ham essence

## Directions

1. Blanch in hot water a small plateful of chicken testicles and eggs
2. Then place the blanched ones in cold water
3. Remove the skins
4. Place in ham essence
5. Let boil in it for a moment
6. When good tasting, remove from heat
7. Add the juice of one lemon
8. Serve as the preceding for garnish
