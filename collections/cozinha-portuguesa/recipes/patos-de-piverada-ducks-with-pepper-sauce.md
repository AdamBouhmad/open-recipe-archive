## Ingredients

- one duck
- white wine
- garlic clove
- nutmeg
- pepper
- bay leaf
- duck liver, half-roasted
- sauce

## Directions

1. While roasting the duck, collect the drippings
2. Add a splash of white wine to these drippings with a clove of garlic, nutmeg, pepper, and a bay leaf
3. Allow to boil
4. Pound a half-roasted duck liver and mix with a little sauce until soft
5. Add to the boiling liquid
6. Place the duck on toasted bread slices and pour the sauce on top
7. Send to table
