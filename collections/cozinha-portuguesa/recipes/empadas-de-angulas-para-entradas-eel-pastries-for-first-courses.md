## Ingredients

- Eels, cut into pieces and deboned
- Soft dough
- Minced meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Mustard
- Onion
- Truffles
- Cucumbers, minced
- Fine herbs
- Fresh butter
- Eggs
- Truffle ragú

## Directions

1. Cut the eels into pieces without bones and make a box of soft dough
2. Line the bottom with good minced meat
3. Place the eel pieces on top, season with salt, pepper, spices, mustard, onion, minced truffles and cucumbers, and a bouquet of fine herbs
4. Add fresh butter on top, place its soft dough lid with its decorations
5. Gild with beaten eggs as is customary and cook in the oven for two hours
6. When cooked, open it, remove the bouquet, drain all the fat, and serve with truffle ragú or any other ragú or suitable sauce
