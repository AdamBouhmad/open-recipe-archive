## Ingredients

- ham, chopped
- butter, washed
- grated bread
- two egg yolks
- flour
- chives
- cucumbers
- salt
- pepper
- rabbit
- thread
- bacon strips
- twine
- broth
- white wine
- bundle of sage
- bay leaf
- onion studded with cloves
- garlic cloves
- lemon juice or orange juice

## Directions

1. Chop ham with a bit of washed butter, grated bread, two egg yolks, flour, chives, cucumbers, salt, and pepper, and use this mixture to stuff the rabbits
2. After filling their bellies, tie them with thread
3. Pierce the legs downward under the belly and the front legs downward toward the snout, as if they were crouching, and secure them in this position with skewers and tied with twine
4. Place them to cook in a pot with a little broth, white wine, a bundle of sage, bay leaf, onion studded with cloves, some garlic cloves, salt, pepper, and bacon strips
5. Cook slowly and gradually
6. Pass the sauce through a sieve and remove the fat
7. Add a little curry and bring to a boil, reduce to the proper consistency
8. Serve the sauce over the rabbits with lemon juice or sour orange juice
