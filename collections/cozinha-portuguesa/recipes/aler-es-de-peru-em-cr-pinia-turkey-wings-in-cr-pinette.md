## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- chicken breast forcemeat à crème
- caul
- eggs
- fine breadcrumbs
- lard
- parsley sauce fried

## Directions

1. After trimming, soaking, and blanching a dozen turkey wings, put them to cook in a good white broth
2. When cooked and cold, cover all around carefully with a forcemeat of chicken breasts à crème well bound
3. Wrap them in a piece of caul moistened in eggs so it adheres
4. Dust them with fine grated bread and fry them in lard
5. When fried and of good color, serve garnished with fried parsley sauce
6. They can also be colored in the oven or on the grill and served with clear essence sauce and lemon juice
