## Ingredients

- one cleaned and plucked turkey
- bread from the mass
- lard, thick and seasoned with salt and pepper
- diced bacon
- six sour apples
- all seasonings whole and ground (except saffron)
- a measure of wine
- little vinegar
- one quartilho of water

## Directions

1. Grind the turkey with the bread
2. Blanch it in a pot with water and salt
3. Lard it first with thick, seasoned lard
4. Place in a pot with diced bacon, six sour apples, all seasonings whole and ground (except saffron), a measure of wine, little vinegar, and one quartilho of water
5. Seal the pot so steam does not escape
6. Place in the oven for three hours, or surround with embers around the fire for four hours, turning the pot frequently
7. When done, place on a plate over slices with lemon juice on top, or verjuice if available
