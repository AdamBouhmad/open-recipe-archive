## Ingredients

- two young rabbits or hares
- bacon, medium cut
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- rabbit livers
- scraped bacon
- veal slices
- bacon slices
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean two young rabbits or hares and cut them into quarters
2. Lard them with medium-cut bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
3. Line the bottom of the pie with a mixture made from the rabbit livers and scraped bacon
4. Place the rabbit quarters on top, covered with the same mixture, then veal slices and bacon slices
5. Close up the pie as is customary with necessary decorations
6. Put it in the oven to cook for two or three hours
7. When cooked, remove the veal and bacon slices
8. Serve with Spanish sauce or with a sauce made from the livers of the rabbits
