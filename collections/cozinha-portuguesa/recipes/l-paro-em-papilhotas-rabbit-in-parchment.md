## Ingredients

- garlic clove, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- paper sheets
- thin slices of bacon
- rabbit quarters

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into quarters
2. Season the finely chopped garlic with salt and pepper
3. On each piece of paper cut into quarters, place a thin slice of bacon with some of the seasoned garlic mixture and a rabbit quarter in the middle with another portion of the mixture, then another slice of bacon
4. Wrap the paper around the rabbit pieces tightly to form parchment packages
5. Do the same with the remaining pieces
6. Place them on a grill with a low flame over a sheet of paper greased with olive oil
7. Once roasted on both sides, serve them dry in their parchment packages as an appetizer
