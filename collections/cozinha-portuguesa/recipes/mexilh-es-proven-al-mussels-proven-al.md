## Ingredients

- mussels
- oil
- sauce
- garlic
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the mussels
2. Put them in a casserole and heat to open them
3. Remove one half of the shell, keeping the other half with the mussel inside
4. Sauté in oil with minced sauce, garlic, and pepper
5. After sautéing, add half a spoon of their own water
6. Without boiling, serve hot as an entrée
