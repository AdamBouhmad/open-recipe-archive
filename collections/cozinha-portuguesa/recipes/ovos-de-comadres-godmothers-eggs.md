## Ingredients

- six onions cut into pieces
- butter
- flour
- half spoonful of beef reduction
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- dozen beaten eggs
- six egg whites, stiffly beaten
- essence sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Braise six onions cut into pieces in butter
2. When braised and cooked, dust with flour
3. Add half spoonful of beef reduction
4. Season with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
5. Let simmer slowly
6. When thickened and well-flavored, add a dozen beaten eggs and six stiffly beaten whites
7. Mix everything with the onions
8. Transfer to another buttered saucepan
9. Bake in the oven
10. When cooked, turn it over onto its plate
11. Serve with essence sauce bound with a bit of butter
