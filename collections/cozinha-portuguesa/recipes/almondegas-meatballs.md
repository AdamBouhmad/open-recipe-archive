## Ingredients

- two arratels of lamb from the leg
- a quarter of bacon
- aromatics
- three eggs
- a crumb of grated bread
- seasonings
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean two arratels of lamb from the leg, removing nerves and skin
2. Finely chop with a quarter of bacon and aromatics
3. Add three eggs, grated bread crumb, seasonings, vinegar, and salt
4. Finely chop everything again very well
5. Heat a bowl over the fire
6. Cook the meatballs in it over low heat, making them the size desired
7. Season again with all seasonings
8. Bind with three egg yolks and lemon juice
9. Place on a plate greased with one ounce of cow butter with slices of bread
10. Place the meatballs on top with thick broth
11. Top with cinnamon and lemon, and take to the table
