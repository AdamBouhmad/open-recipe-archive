## Ingredients

- veal shoulder or palm cut
- butter
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- parsley
- garlic
- bay leaves
- cloves

## Directions

1. Take a veal shoulder or palm cut, or any other piece of it, cover it with butter
2. Place it in an earthenware pan with half a quartilho of water, a little vinegar, salt, pepper, two onions in slices, some sprigs of parsley, some cloves of garlic, two bay leaves, and two cloves
3. Cover it with a sheet of paper greased with butter
4. Place it in the oven for two or three hours
5. When browned, remove it, strain the sauce through a sieve, and serve hot over the veal
