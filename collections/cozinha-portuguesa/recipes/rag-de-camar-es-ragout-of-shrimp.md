## Ingredients

- Shrimp, boiled and tails removed
- Melted salt pork
- Truffles, sliced
- Mushrooms
- Ham
- Broth or stock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shrimp cullis

## Directions

1. Boil the shrimp and remove the tails
2. Sauté in melted salt pork with slices of truffles, mushrooms, and a piece of ham
3. When everything is sautéed, add a spoon of broth and season
4. Allow to cook
5. When cooked, bind with a little shrimp cullis
6. Finish and ensure good flavor
7. Serve for whatever purpose desired
