## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place two arratels of quinces in quarters in three arratels of sugar at light point so they cook in it
2. As they cook, break them apart with a spoon
3. Once well cooked and dry, add fragrance if desired
4. Place in small paper cones and set to dry in the sun
