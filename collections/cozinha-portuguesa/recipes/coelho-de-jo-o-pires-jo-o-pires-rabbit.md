## Ingredients

- half arratel of lard, minced
- onion
- parsley, finely minced
- rabbits, cut into pieces
- cumin
- lemon
- olive oil
- verjuice or vinegar
- bread slices
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Sauté the minced lard with onion and finely minced parsley
2. Once sautéed, add the rabbit pieces cut into pieces
3. Once cooked, season with all the seasonings, cumin, lemon, olive oil, verjuice (if you have it) or vinegar
4. Place on slices of bread with lemon slices on top and send to the table
