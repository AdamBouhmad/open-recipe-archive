## Ingredients

- Breasts of capons or roasted chickens
- Bound essence of good taste

## Directions

1. Cut into dice or thin fillets the breasts of capons or roasted chickens
2. Place them in a bound essence of good taste
3. Let it cool
4. Once cold, place a small spoonful in each form lined with puff pastry
5. Finish and cook like the previous ones
6. Serve in the same manner
7. They can also be made from chopped chicken breast with cream
