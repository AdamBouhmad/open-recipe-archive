## Ingredients

- good broth
- fish substance
- half pound of vermicelli or Italian pasta shapes

## Directions

1. Pass through a sieve and into a pot two spoonfuls of good broth
2. Add one spoonful of substance and bring to boil
3. When boiling, add half a pound of vermicelli or any of the Italian pasta shapes that come in the shape of hearts, melon seeds, etc.
4. Let simmer by the fire for about an hour
5. When cooked, remove the fat
6. Serve neither very thick nor very thin; if it comes out too thick, add the broth and substance needed to make it more liquid
