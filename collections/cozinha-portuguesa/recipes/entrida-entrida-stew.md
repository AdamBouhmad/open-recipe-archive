## Ingredients

- two chickens
- two guinea hens
- two partridges
- rabbit
- two arratels of pork rib meat
- water
- vinegar
- seasonings
- broth
- sugar
- butter
- eggs
- lemon juice
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook in a pot two chickens, two guinea hens, two partridges, a rabbit, and two arratels of pork rib meat with water as for lamb
2. Season with vinegar and all the seasonings
3. When cooked, remove the broth and dissolve three or four sugar balls and butter in it, then bring to a boil
4. When thick, finish cooking with eight eggs and juice of four lemons
5. When well reduced, place on a plate with cinnamon on top and the meat on this entrida, then serve
