## Ingredients

- Broad beans
- Lettuce eyes
- Water
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chives
- Coriander, chopped
- Hot water
- Sugar
- Eggs, fried

## Directions

1. Cook the broad beans with lettuce eyes in plain water
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Place in a casserole with oil, seasoned with salt, pepper, chopped chives, and chopped coriander
4. Sauté slowly
5. After sautéing, add a drop of hot water and let simmer slowly
6. When cooked and with little sauce, place on a plate with sugar and fried eggs on top, and serve as desired
