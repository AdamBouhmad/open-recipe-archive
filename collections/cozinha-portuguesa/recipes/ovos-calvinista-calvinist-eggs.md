## Ingredients

- half spoonful of beef or veal substance, braised
- eggs, fresh and whole
- pepper
- butter (in the cooking plate)

## Directions

1. Put half spoonful of braised beef or veal substance in a plate
2. Break eggs one by one into it, keeping them whole
3. Season with a pinch of pepper
4. Place over gentle heat to cook slowly
5. Pass a hot spatula over the top
6. Do not let the yolks harden
7. Serve immediately for an entrée course
