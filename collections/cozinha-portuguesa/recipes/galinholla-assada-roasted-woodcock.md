## Ingredients

- Woodcock, snipe, or curlew
- Bacon lard
- Bacon slices
- Bread slices
- Sour orange

## Directions

1. After plucking and turning the bird over with its own beak, roast it over coals
2. After roasting and browning, lard it with fine bacon or cover it with bacon slices
3. Without removing the entrails, roast it on a small spit tied to a larger spit
4. Place slices of bread underneath to catch what drips from the body
5. After roasting and achieving good color, serve garnished around or on top of the same slices with sour oranges
