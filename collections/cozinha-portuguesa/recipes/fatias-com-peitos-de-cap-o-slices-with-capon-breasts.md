## Ingredients

- chopped capon or partridge breasts, roasted
- egg yolks
- bread crumb cooked with cream
- salt
- sugar
- lemon zest, grated

## Directions

1. Take a chopped mixture of roasted capon or partridge breasts, pound it and bind it with egg yolks and bread crumb cooked with cream
2. Season with salt, sugar, and grated lemon zest
3. Place on cooked slices, glazed as the previous recipe
4. Serve the same way
