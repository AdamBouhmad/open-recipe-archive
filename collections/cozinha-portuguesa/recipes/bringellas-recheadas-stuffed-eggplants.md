## Ingredients

- eggplants
- eggplant flesh
- herbs
- cow butter
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- Alentejo cheese
- bread slices
- egg yolks
- broth
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the eggplants in halves
2. Remove the flesh from inside, chop it along with part of the eggplants and herbs sautéed in cow butter, breadcrumbs, eggs, and Alentejo cheese
3. Stuff the eggplants with this mixture
4. Place in a hollow plate or frying pan over already-moistened bread slices with a thick sauce made of two egg yolks dissolved in a little broth in which the herbs will be sautéed
5. Cook on gentle heat below and above, first pouring the sauce that remained in the sauté, and with cinnamon on top and grated cheese
6. Serve at table
