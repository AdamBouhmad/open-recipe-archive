## Ingredients

- Young partridges, cleaned and singed
- Young partridge livers
- Scraped bacon
- Bacon slices
- Veal slices
- Ham slices
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chopped chives
- Spirits
- Substance broth
- Cullis
- Good butter
- Orange juice
- Whole pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the young partridges with legs tucked inside the body or when they have the feet inside the breast, open them from the back and stuff them with a small mixture made from their own livers and scraped bacon
2. Place to cook with bacon slices, veal slices and ham slices and season as is customary
3. When almost cooked, add a small glass of spirits
4. After finished cooking, remove them and moisten what remains at the bottom of the pot with a spoon of substance and half a spoon of cullis, reduce it, and once reduced, pass it through a sieve
5. Remove all the fat, add a piece of good butter, and once finished binding over the fire, serve hot with orange juice and whole pepper
