## Ingredients

- A piece of butter
- Sliced onions
- Pieces of good fish
- Four crushed juniper berries
- Roots
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- One head of garlic
- Two or three cloves
- One glass of white wine
- One glass of thin stock

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter in a pot
2. Add sliced onions, pieces of good fish, four crushed juniper berries, roots, one bundle of parsley and fine herbs, one head of garlic, and two or three cloves
3. Let it cook on gentle heat for half an hour
4. Move to a stronger heat, and when it begins to stick, add one glass of white wine and one glass of thin stock
5. Let it simmer little by little, and when reduced and of good taste, pass through a sieve
6. Use for all sauces and ragus
