## Ingredients

- lean meat from the cow's thigh
- half of it combined with fat meat, well cleaned of skins
- bacon cut into small cubes
- some small cubes of ham
- salt
- pepper
- shallots
- parsley
- chives
- half a pint of brandy
- four egg yolks
- bread

## Directions

1. Choose the necessary amount of lean meat from the cow's thigh and make a mince with half of what you have of meat with fat, well cleaned of skins
2. Once everything is minced, place it in a bowl or casserole with enough bacon cut into small cubes and some small cubes of ham
3. Season with salt, pepper, shallots, parsley, chives, half a pint of brandy, and four egg yolks
4. After everything is well mixed with a spoon of bread, line a casserole with strips of bacon, arrange the mince well in it, cover it with its lid well sealed around with flour mixed in vinegar
5. Cook in its own steam for three or four hours
6. Once well cooked, let it cool, and served cold it is used as a middle course dish. It can also be served hot with whatever sauce seems appropriate
