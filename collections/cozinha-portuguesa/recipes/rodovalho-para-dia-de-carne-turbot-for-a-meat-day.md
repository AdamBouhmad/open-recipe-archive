## Ingredients

- bacon slices
- veal slices
- turbot
- salt
- whole pepper
- onion with cloves
- fine herbs
- ham slices
- white wine
- coulis

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a large pot with bacon slices and thin, beaten veal slices, place the turbot on top, season with little salt, whole pepper, onion studded with cloves, a handful of fine herbs, and two slices of ham
2. Cover everything with bacon slices and let it sweat between two fires
3. After that, pour it a quarter of white wine and finish cooking it little by little
4. Once cooked, place it carefully on the plate and serve with the sauce from the bottom of the same braising pot, strained with a little coulis
