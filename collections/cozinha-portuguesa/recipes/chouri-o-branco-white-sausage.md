## Ingredients

- Milk
- Salt
- Onion rounds
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Coriander seed
- Bay leaves
- Almonds, ground
- Lard, in small pieces
- Chicken or capon meat
- Egg yolks
- Egg whites, beaten
- Salt, ground
- Pepper
- Fine spices
- Pork casings

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil in a casserole a layer of milk tempered with salt, onion rounds, parsley, thyme, marjoram, coriander seed, and bay leaves
2. After all has boiled and reduced to half, remove from heat and pass through a sieve
3. Add a quarter of ground almonds, pass through a cloth with force
4. After passing, add lard cut in small pieces, three parts of chicken or capon meat, a dozen egg yolks, five or six beaten egg whites, and finish seasoning with ground salt, pepper, and fine spices
5. On heat and stirring always with the spoon, when beginning to bind and with good taste, without letting it cool, fill pork casings, which will be tied as is customary with all equality
6. After filling, let boil a quarter hour in water
7. When cooked, place to drain on a cloth, and after cooling, lay on oiled paper and serve hot
