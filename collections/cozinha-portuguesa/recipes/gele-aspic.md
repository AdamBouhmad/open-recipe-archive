## Ingredients

- Veal feet
- Veal shank
- Chicken or old hens
- Turkey feet
- All glutinous parts
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- Cinnamon
- Half quartilho of white wine
- Ten or twelve egg whites

## Directions

1. Take veal feet, shanks, chicken or old hens, turkey feet, and all glutinous parts according to the quantity of aspic desired
2. Soak, wash, and blanch them
3. Cook for three or four hours
4. When cooked and the broth reduced to the proper consistency, strain through a sieve
5. Remove all the fat
6. Season with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, and half quartilho of white wine
7. Bring back to the heat with ten or twelve beaten egg whites
8. Stir everything, and when it comes to a boil two or three times, add a little cold water
9. Strain through a cloth until clear
10. Pour into molds or glasses
11. Place in a cold place, and when set, serve as is customary
