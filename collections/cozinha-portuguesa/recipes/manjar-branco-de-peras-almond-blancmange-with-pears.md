## Ingredients

- half canada of milk
- half dozen large pears or one dozen small pears, cleaned, peeled, and without seeds
- two canadas of milk
- two arratels of sugar
- one arratel of rice flour
- half canada of milk (for cooling)
- flower water

## Directions

1. Put half canada of milk on to cook with half dozen large pears or one dozen small pears, cleaned, peeled, and without seeds
2. When halfway cooked, place in a pan and break down with a spoon
3. Add two canadas of milk, two arratels of sugar, and one arratel of rice flour
4. Put on to cook; while cooking, cool with half canada of milk added little by little
5. When cooked, add flower water
6. Remove from heat and serve on plates
