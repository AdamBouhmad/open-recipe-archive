## Ingredients

- one large bowl of cream
- four eggs with whites
- half a quartilho of olive oil
- one arratel of cheese
- one arratel of bread slices
- one arratel of sugar
- some washed butter

## Directions

1. Grease a plate or frying pan
2. Make a layer of bread slices
3. Beat some of the cream and egg mixture and sprinkle powdered sugar on top
4. Add a layer of cheese slices with more cream mixture and sugar
5. Add small pieces of washed butter
6. Continue layering until all ingredients are used, ending with cheese
7. The soup should be well soaked
8. Place in the oven
9. When half cooked, pour two bowls of cream on top without eggs or sugar
10. Finish cooking and send to the table
