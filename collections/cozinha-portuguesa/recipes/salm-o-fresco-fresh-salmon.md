## Ingredients

- salmon
- vinegar
- wine
- water
- a large bunch of parsley
- pepper, pounded

## Directions

1. Clean the salmon
2. Make a broth of two parts vinegar, one part wine, and one part water
3. Add a large bunch of parsley and well-pounded pepper
4. Cook
5. When boiling, add the salmon
6. When cooked, remove it and eat it cold
7. In this same form it sits at the table the whole time it lasts
