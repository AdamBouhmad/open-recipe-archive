## Ingredients

- one and a half dozen apricots
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel a dozen and a half apricots and remove the pits
2. Cut into pieces and cook in water until it becomes like marmalade
3. When dried and desiccated, add sugar and make like the preceding recipes
