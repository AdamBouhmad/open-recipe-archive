## Ingredients

- half a quarter of flour
- cold water
- salt
- two egg yolks
- little butter
- rose water
- one arratel of almonds pounded
- one arratel of sugar in point
- a dozen strands of white blancmange
- six egg yolks
- a quarter of sugar
- half a dozen cooked egg yolks
- a little lemon
- half a dozen royal eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Knead half a quarter of flour with cold water seasoned with salt
2. Add two egg yolks, little butter, and rose water
3. When very well kneaded, cut it into strands
4. Spread into thin leaves the size of the basin
5. In a basin well greased with butter, put in a leaf
6. Over it put one arratel of almonds pounded, made into marzipan in one arratel of sugar in point
7. Cover with another leaf
8. Over it put a dozen strands of white blancmange beaten with six egg yolks and a quarter of sugar
9. Cover with another leaf greased with butter
10. Over it put some soft eggs with cinnamon on top
11. Cover with another greased leaf
12. Put over it half a dozen cooked egg yolks, a little lemon around, half a dozen royal eggs in the middle
13. Send to the oven
14. When cooked, if desired, cut into slices of desired size
15. Pass through sugar in point and serve at the table sprinkled with cinnamon
