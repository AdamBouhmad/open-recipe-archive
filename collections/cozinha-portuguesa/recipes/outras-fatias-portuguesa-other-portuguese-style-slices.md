## Ingredients

- bread crumb
- sugar, boiling
- fresh egg yolks
- powdered sugar
- pounded cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the bread crumb into thin slices
2. Toast on both sides on grills
3. When browned, arrange on a plate, pass briefly through boiling sugar, add fresh whole egg yolks
4. When cooked, place on the slices with powdered sugar and pounded cinnamon
