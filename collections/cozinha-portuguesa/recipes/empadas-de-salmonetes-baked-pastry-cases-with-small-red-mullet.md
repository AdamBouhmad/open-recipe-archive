## Ingredients

- Small red mullet
- Livers
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sauce
- Truffles
- Tarragon
- Fine herbs
- Nutmeg, grated
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the small red mullet, removing heads and tails; set aside the livers
2. Take some of the livers and make a forcemeat with butter, salt, pepper, sauce, truffles, tarragon, fine herbs, grated nutmeg, and bind with two egg yolks
3. Make a pastry case according to custom
4. Line the bottom with fresh butter and a little forcemeat
5. Arrange the small red mullet inside, placing some forcemeat between each one
6. Season lightly with salt and pepper
7. Cover with the remaining forcemeat and washed butter
8. Close the pastry case with its pastry lid
9. Finish and gild it, then place in the oven to bake
10. When cooked, remove the lid, drain the fat, and serve with a piquant coulis made from the remaining livers and roe
