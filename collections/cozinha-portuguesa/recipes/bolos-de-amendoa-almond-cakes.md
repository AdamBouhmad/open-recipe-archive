## Ingredients

- four arratels of almonds, very well beaten
- four arratels of sugar in wire point
- two egg whites
- eight egg yolks
- one good spoon of butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat four arratels of almonds very well with four arratels of sugar in wire point
2. Add two egg whites and beat again
3. Put on the fire
4. When the pot shows the bottom, add eight egg yolks and one good spoon of butter
5. After giving it a couple of turns, place where desired
6. Spread with flour on the cakes, putting them in a basin away from each other
7. Send to cook
8. After cooked, pass through sugar and serve at the table with cinnamon on top
