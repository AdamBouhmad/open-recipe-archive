## Ingredients

- Turtledoves or quails
- Ham slices
- Good quality butter
- Garlic cloves
- Bay leaf
- Vinegar
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cooking stock

## Directions

1. Take the necessary fat turtledoves or quails, clean, prepare, and lard them
2. Roast them covered with sheets of bacon and paper leaves
3. When roasted, sweat half a dozen slices of ham with good quality butter
4. Brown the ham on both sides and remove it
5. In what remains in the pan, add two crushed garlic cloves, one bay leaf, one drop of vinegar, one drop of white wine, and a little cooking stock
6. Season with pepper and salt if needed
7. Let it simmer briefly
8. Strain through a sieve
9. Pour over the turtledoves or quails and serve
