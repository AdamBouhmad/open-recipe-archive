## Ingredients

- slices of ham
- white wine
- one spoonful of substance (meat essence)
- pepper
- fine herbs
- one small onion with two cloves
- half a spoonful of calf meat jelly

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a pan with slices of ham
2. Put it on the heat to render its fat on both sides
3. When it begins to stick, add a little white wine and one spoonful of substance
4. Season with pepper, fine herbs, and one small onion stuck with two cloves
5. When reduced to half, add half a spoonful of calf meat jelly and let it boil a little longer
6. Pass through a sieve and serve for pies, tarts, stews, and whatever else is necessary
