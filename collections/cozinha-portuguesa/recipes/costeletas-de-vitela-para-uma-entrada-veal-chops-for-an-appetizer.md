## Ingredients

- one or two veal rib sections
- fine bacon for larding
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- fine herbs
- lemon slices
- onion slices
- melted bacon
- shallot sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take one or two veal rib sections, the piece that runs from the kidney to the neck
2. Trim the top of the loin of all its skin
3. Lard with minced bacon
4. Marinate and season with salt, pepper, bay leaves, whole fine herbs, lemon slices, onion slices, and melted bacon
5. After marinating three or four hours, roast on a spit covered with buttered paper
6. Once roasted, remove the paper and serve with shallot sauce and lemon juice
