## Ingredients

- sheep tails
- water
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter or fine oil
- shallot sauce or lime juice

## Directions

1. Take the quantity of tails judged necessary and soften them in hot water
2. Trim them and tie them two by two
3. Cook them in a half broth made with broth, two onions, two roots, a bundle of parsley, and fine herbs, seasoned with little salt and pepper
4. When cooked, they can be served in different ways
5. To roast them on the grill, pass them first through beaten eggs and cover with fine grated breadcrumbs
6. Drizzle with melted butter or fine oil and cover a second time with breadcrumbs
7. Return to roasting, basting from time to time with a little butter
8. When well colored, serve with shallot sauce, lime juice, or with a hot parsley sauce
9. They can also be fried in pork butter and garnished with fried parsley
