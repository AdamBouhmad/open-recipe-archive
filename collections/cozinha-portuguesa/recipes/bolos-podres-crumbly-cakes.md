## Ingredients

- half alqueire of sifted flour
- four arratels of sugar
- two canadas of oil
- one arratel of very finely ground almonds
- one vintem of clove, sweet herb, cinnamon, and salt
- yeast in small pieces

## Directions

1. Take four arratels of sugar, clean and brought to a thick paste consistency, add two canadas of oil and one arratel of very finely ground almonds, and one vintem of clove, sweet herb, cinnamon, and salt
2. Allow this to boil very well, then gradually add the half alqueire of sifted flour, stirring with a spoon until well cooked
3. Once cooked, place in a bowl and stir until it can be kneaded
4. While kneading, gradually add yeast in small pieces, continuing to knead
5. Once fully kneaded, cover until risen
6. Form the cakes, pressing well with the hands, place in basins, and send to the oven
