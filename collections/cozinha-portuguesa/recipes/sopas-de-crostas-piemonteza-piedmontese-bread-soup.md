## Ingredients

- bread
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- fat
- beef broth
- veal substance

## Directions

1. Cut a loaf of bread in half and remove the crumb, keeping the crusts
2. Serve the crusts as in the previous recipe; after cooking, transfer them to a baking dish
3. Cover with grated Parmesan cheese
4. Dot with a little fat and place in the oven to brown
5. Cut a piece of bread into thin slices
6. Arrange the slices in a serving plate with grated cheese on a heat source
7. Pour a little broth over them
8. When stuck to the bottom of the plate, place the well-browned crusts in the middle
9. Serve with broth from the potage, lightly salted, and with veal substance
10. They may also be served with different broths and substances such as partridge, chicken, capons, ducks, pigeons, etc., noting that neither the broths nor the substances destined for soups or stews that contain cheese should be salty
