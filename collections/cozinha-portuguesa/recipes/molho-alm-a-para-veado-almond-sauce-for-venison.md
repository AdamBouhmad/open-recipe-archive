## Ingredients

- One bottle of wine
- Same amount of vinegar
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Two lemon slices
- Cherries or plums cooked in sugar

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole one bottle of wine and the same amount of vinegar, add sugar, cinnamon, two lemon slices, and bring to a boil until reduced to half
2. Having cooked some cherries or plums in a little sugar, mix in the sauce, strain through a sieve, and it will serve for venison, roebuck, hare, and all the wild game
