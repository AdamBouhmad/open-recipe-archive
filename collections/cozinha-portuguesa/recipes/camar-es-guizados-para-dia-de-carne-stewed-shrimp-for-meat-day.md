## Ingredients

- Shrimp
- Rendered bacon
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Veal substance
- Cullis of shrimp
- Veal substance
- Ham

## Directions

1. Clean the shrimp as previously described
2. Pass the tails through the fire in rendered bacon with truffles and mushrooms
3. Add a little veal substance
4. Allow to simmer little by little
5. Once the sauce is reduced, remove the fat
6. Bind with a cullis of shrimp made with veal substance and ham
7. Keep over gentle heat without boiling
8. Serve well hot
