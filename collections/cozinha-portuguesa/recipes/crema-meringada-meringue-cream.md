## Ingredients

- cream prepared as in Crema Queimada
- egg whites
- 2 or 3 handfuls of fine sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cream as in the previous recipe
2. Place it on its plate
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff like snow, as for biscuits
4. Add 2 or 3 handfuls of fine sugar and mix very well
5. Spread over the cream
6. Sprinkle again with sugar
7. Place in the oven with mild heat
8. When browned, serve hot
