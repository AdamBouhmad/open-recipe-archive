## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water
- herbs
- butter
- seasonings
- lemon juice
- egg yolks
- pastry dough
- butter
- sugar
- eggs
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook the asparagus with three waters
2. Sauté them in herbs that must already be sautéed on the heat
3. Once cooked and seasoned with all the seasonings and plenty of lemon juice, bind them with six egg yolks and let the sauce be thick
4. Let them cool
5. Make the tart dough very well kneaded with butter, sugar, eggs, and white wine
6. Once made with asparagus inside, cook it
7. Serve at table
