## Ingredients

- One dozen thrushes
- Melted bacon
- Flour
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Capelet with onion
- Herbs
- Cloves
- Lemon juice
- Olives or juniper berries

## Directions

1. Take one dozen thrushes, pluck and singe them
2. Straighten them by putting their legs inside the body
3. Rub them with melted bacon
4. Dust them with flour
5. Place them in a pan with white wine, salt, pepper, one capelet with onion, herbs, and two cloves
6. Simmer gently
7. When cooked and bound with a little essence, serve with lemon juice
8. They can also be served with olives or juniper berries cooked in water
