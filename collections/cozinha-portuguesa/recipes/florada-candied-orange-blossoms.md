## Ingredients

- orange blossoms or blossoms of sweet or china orange (the largest and best)
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place in a bowl with cold water the large and best orange blossoms or blossoms of sweet or china orange
2. Wash them very well so as not to break them
3. Place them in other water in another bowl
4. Bring them to boil briefly in a pot that should already be boiling
5. After boiling place them in cold water with sugar, passing them two or three more times through other waters
6. When sweet so it is not bitter, place them to drain on a sieve
7. After being dry and very well squeezed, cook in sugar at point
8. When cooked place them to cool, fanning to cool quickly
9. Add scent if desired
10. Pour into glazed pans
