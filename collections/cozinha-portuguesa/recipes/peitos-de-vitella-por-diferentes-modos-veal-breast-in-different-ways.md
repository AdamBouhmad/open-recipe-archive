## Ingredients

- veal breast
- butter
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- hot water
- egg yolks
- milk
- verjuice or apple juice
- green peas
- asparagus tips
- sorrel
- other vegetables

## Directions

1. Parboil the veal breasts and soak them in water for some time, blanch them, and cut them into equal pieces
2. Pass them through the fire with a piece of butter, a slice of ham, a bouquet garnish with little salt and pepper, and dust with flour
3. Moisten with broth and hot water
4. When cooked, remove the bouquet and fat, thicken with four egg yolks mixed in milk, and serve with verjuice or apple juice
5. They can also be made with green peas and thickened with egg yolks as above
6. If not, after being sautéed, moisten them with stock and a little broth
7. They can also be larded as one does with fricandeau and finished the same way, and served with a ragout of asparagus tips, peas, sorrel, or any other vegetable garnish
8. These pieces of veal breast after being cooked in a braise can be served in terrines garnished with cabbage and pieces of intermingled lardoon, or with any other ragout that seems best
