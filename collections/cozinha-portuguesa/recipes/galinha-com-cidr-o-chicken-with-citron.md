## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one quarter of pork
- aromatics
- all seasonings
- bread in slices
- sugar
- lemon
- half arratel of citron
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook one chicken with one quarter of pork and aromatics
2. Once cooked, season with all seasonings
3. Make bread in slices, and cut them in the manner of dice, place them in a plate where you will pour all the chicken broth so that they become white
4. Add sugar and lemon to make this broth sour and sweet, and it must be browned with heat from below and on top
5. Then place the chicken on top, cut in quarters and passed through beaten eggs with good lemon
6. Cut half arratel of citron into thin small pieces, and after passed through the same eggs, place them around the plate between the chicken, thus it goes to the table
