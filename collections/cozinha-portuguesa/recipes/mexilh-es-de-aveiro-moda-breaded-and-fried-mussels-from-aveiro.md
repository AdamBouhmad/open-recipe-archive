## Ingredients

- mussels from Aveiro or other
- butter
- vinegar
- garlic
- pepper
- orange
- butter

## Directions

1. Bread the mussels
2. Fry them in butter
3. Pass them through a sauce made of vinegar, garlic, pepper, orange, and butter
4. Serve at table
