## Ingredients

- large eel
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- sauce
- onion
- shallot
- garlic
- string
- white wine
- fine herbs
- broth
- butter
- ragú of truffles, morels, mushrooms, or other

## Directions

1. Prepare a large eel
2. Cut off the head
3. Open along the belly
4. Remove all the spine
5. Cut in two pieces of equal length
6. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, sauce, onion, shallot, and garlic clove, all very finely chopped
7. Make two rolls
8. Tie each with string
9. Poach in white wine with fine herbs and a little broth and a piece of butter
10. When cooked, remove the string
11. Serve with ragú of truffles, morels, mushrooms, or any other that suits
