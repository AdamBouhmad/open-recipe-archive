## Ingredients

- half an eighth of flour
- four eggs with two egg whites
- a bit of lemon zest, chopped
- a drop of plain water

## Directions

1. After being well kneaded, let it rest for one hour
2. Then extend it, cover it with butter, fold or roll it
3. Extend it again, finish it like puff pastry
4. Place it in round form the thickness of one inch
5. Put it to cook in the oven
6. Serve it glazed with sugar and pass a hot spatula over it
