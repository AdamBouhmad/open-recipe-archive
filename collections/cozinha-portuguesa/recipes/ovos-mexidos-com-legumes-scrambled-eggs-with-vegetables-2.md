## Ingredients

- one dozen egg yolks
- half dozen egg whites
- butter
- two or three spoonfuls of asparagus tips ragú, well finished

## Directions

1. Take one dozen egg yolks and half a dozen egg whites, beat very well and place in a pot with melted butter, to which add two or three spoonfuls of a well-finished ragú of asparagus tips
2. Stirring everything together until cooked and of good taste, serve as the above
