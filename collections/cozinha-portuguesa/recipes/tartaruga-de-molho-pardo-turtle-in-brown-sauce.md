## Ingredients

- cooked turtle
- butter
- flour
- onion
- wine
- cullis
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- capers
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cooking and cutting into quarters, transfer to a pan with butter and browned flour
2. Brown them with chopped onion
3. Add a glass of wine and a tablespoon of cullis
4. Season with salt, pepper, and the previous herbs
5. Let simmer little by little
6. When cooked and the sauce is reduced and of good flavor, add some capers
7. Serve with lemon juice
