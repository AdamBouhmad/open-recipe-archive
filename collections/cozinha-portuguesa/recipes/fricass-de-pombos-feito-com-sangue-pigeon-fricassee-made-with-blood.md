## Ingredients

- five or six young pigeons
- pigeon blood
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- ham
- parsley
- clove-studded onion
- substance

## Directions

1. Take five or six young pigeons and save their blood, stirring it until it cools so it does not coagulate
2. Straighten the pigeons the same way as those in compote, then brown them in a casserole with rendered bacon, salt, pepper, a slice of ham, a sprig of parsley, and a clove-studded onion
3. Moisten with substance and thicken
