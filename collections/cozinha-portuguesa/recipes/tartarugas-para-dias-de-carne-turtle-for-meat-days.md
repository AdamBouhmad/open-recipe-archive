## Ingredients

- sea turtle
- water
- salt
- bacon
- pepper
- sage
- shallots
- veal
- fine herbs
- white wine
- spicy sauce or vegetable ragú

## Directions

1. Cook the turtle in water and salt
2. Cut into quarters and clean
3. Lard with bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, sage, and chopped shallots
4. Cook in a braise between bacon slabs, veal slabs, fine herbs, and a drop of white wine
5. When cooked and dried, serve with spicy sauce or vegetable ragú that seems best
