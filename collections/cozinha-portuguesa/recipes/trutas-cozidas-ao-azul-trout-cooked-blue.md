## Ingredients

- trout
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion slices
- roots and sprigs of saffron
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- garlic cloves
- red wine
- water

## Directions

1. Boil half a gallon of the most vinegary vinegar possible with salt, pepper, onion slices, roots, and sprigs of saffron, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, and some garlic cloves
2. After it has boiled for some time, pour this hot liquid over the trout, which should be prepared with only the innards and gills removed but with the scales intact in a casserole
3. After a few minutes, add red wine and water so that the trout are covered
4. Let them boil for some minutes
5. When cooked, serve on a folded napkin with sprigs of saffron around
