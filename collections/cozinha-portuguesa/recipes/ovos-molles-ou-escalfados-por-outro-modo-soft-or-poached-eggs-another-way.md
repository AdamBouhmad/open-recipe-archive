## Ingredients

- fresh eggs, two to three days old
- water

## Directions

1. Place fresh eggs with their shell in boiling water and leave them to boil five or six minutes
2. Immediately pass them to cold water and peel gently so they don't break and remain whole
3. After shelling, place them to heat in warm water and when hot, serve them whole with any of the sauces or ragús mentioned
