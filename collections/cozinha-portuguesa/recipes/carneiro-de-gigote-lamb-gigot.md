## Ingredients

- two arratels of lamb
- wine
- vinegar
- pepper
- clove
- ginger
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Roast two arratels of lamb with a frying pan underneath to catch the drippings
2. Pour a splash of wine into the pan with vinegar, pepper, clove, ginger, and nutmeg
