## Ingredients

- two dozen egg yolks
- small basin of flour
- two arratels of sugar at espadana point
- soft eggs

## Directions

1. Beat two dozen egg yolks in a small basin of flour with two arratels of sugar at espadana point
2. Once beaten, place in a pot and set over fire to boil
3. Once curdled, cut and turn to cook on the other side
4. Once cooked, form bundles, putting soft eggs inside if desired, pass through sugar at point, and serve with cinnamon on top
