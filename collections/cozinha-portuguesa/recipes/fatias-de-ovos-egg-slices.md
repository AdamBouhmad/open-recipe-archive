## Ingredients

- one dozen eggs
- twelve ounces of flour
- one arratel of very finely ground almonds
- one arratel of sifted stone sugar
- one spoon of cow butter
- flower water mixed with rose water

## Directions

1. Knead one dozen eggs with twelve ounces of flour, one arratel of very finely ground almonds, one arratel of sifted stone sugar, one spoon of cow butter, and flower water mixed with rose water
2. Once all is very well kneaded, place some flour in a basin and set wafers on top sprinkled with flower water
3. On top of these, pour the thin paste and bake in the oven
4. Once baked, cut the slices and turn them, placing to bake on the other side on other wafers
5. Once cooked, serve at table
