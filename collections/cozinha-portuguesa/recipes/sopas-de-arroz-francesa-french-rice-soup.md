## Ingredients

- half pound of rice
- broth
- ham
- onion
- cloves
- veal substance

## Directions

1. Wash the rice three or four times in warm water and drain it in a sieve
2. Put it to cook in a small pot with a little broth, a piece of ham, an onion with two cloves
3. Let it simmer gently for an hour and a half or two hours
4. After cooking, finish moistening it with good broth and two spoonfuls of veal substance
5. Remove the ham, onion, and fat
6. When it has good flavor, serve it neither very thick nor very thin
