## Ingredients

- lentils
- water
- broth
- piece of streaky pork belly or ham
- roasted partridge
- onion studded with cloves
- celery
- head of garlic
- veal substance
- cullis

## Directions

1. Wash and parboil the lentils in water that comes to a boil
2. Cook them in broth with a piece of streaky pork belly or ham, adding a roasted partridge, an onion studded with cloves, a piece of celery, and a head of garlic
3. Once cooked, remove the fat
4. Pass through a sieve with veal substance and a little cullis
5. Place over heat and arrange the partridges that are to be cooked as above
6. Serve, pouring the cullis of the lentils over the top
