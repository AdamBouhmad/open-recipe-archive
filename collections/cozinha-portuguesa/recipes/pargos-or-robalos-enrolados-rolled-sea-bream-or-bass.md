## Ingredients

- Sea bream or bass, scaled and cleaned
- Salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- butter, melted
- egg yolks
- bread, finely sliced
- shrimp tail sauce or other sauce of choice

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, remove the bones as in preceding recipes, but leave the heads attached at both ends; if you do not wish to leave the heads, leave a piece of the tail
2. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice, straighten it on a plate if it is silver, cover the fillets with bacon
3. Roll the fish around the head or tail, which should remain in the middle
4. Pour melted butter mixed with egg yolks over top
5. Cover with finely sliced bread
6. Brush with oil as is customary and put to cook in the oven or under a lid
7. When cooked, drain the fat and serve with shrimp tail sauce or any other sauce desired
