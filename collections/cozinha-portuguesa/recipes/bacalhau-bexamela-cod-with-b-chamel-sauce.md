## Ingredients

- cooked cod
- béchamel sauce
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the cod and shred it very finely
2. Mix it into a well-made béchamel sauce of good flavor
3. If making a single dish, add half a dozen egg yolks and a quarter of a loaf before serving
4. Mix in the beaten egg whites
5. Grease the bottom of a dish with butter
6. Place the cod mixture inside
7. Bake in the oven or under a baking dish lid
8. When it rises and looks good, serve hot in the same dish
