## Ingredients

- equal parts of leaves of blessed thistle
- equal parts of centaury
- equal parts of chamomile
- equal parts of wormwood
- equal parts of veronica
- equal parts of scolopendra
- equal parts of germander
- equal parts of sour orange peel
- equal parts of gentian root
- one ounce weight
- one canada of water
- one arratel of veal
- one bruised chicken

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of leaves of blessed thistle, centaury, chamomile, wormwood, veronica, scolopendra, germander, sour orange peel, and gentian root, all dried in the shade and finely chopped
2. Mix with equality and guard in a small tin box
3. When needed, take the weight of one ounce and put it in a pot with one canada of water, one arratel of veal, and one bruised chicken
4. After it has boiled and the broth is reduced to half, strain through a napkin without pressing
5. Divide into two equal parts, of which take one in the morning on an empty stomach and another four hours after dinner
6. This can be used for fifteen or twenty days, purging before and after taking it
