## Ingredients

- peeled apricots
- brandy
- sugar
- lemon rind
- flower water
- batter made with water, white wine, salt, and clarified butter

## Directions

1. Peel the apricots and open them in half
2. Marinate as described above
3. Pass them through batter made with water, white wine, salt, and clarified butter
4. Fry in very hot oil
5. When fried and golden, serve dusted with fine sugar
