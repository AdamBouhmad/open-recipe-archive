## Ingredients

- Turkey with legs folded inside
- Bacon strips
- Ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Lemon juice
- Dough
- Bacon, minced
- Fine herbs
- Butter
- Eggs
- Lily flowers

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey with legs folded inside, break the thighbones
2. Singe it, then lard with thick bacon and ham
3. Season with salt, pepper, spices, and lemon juice
4. Place on dough half a finger thick, with minced bacon on top and bottom, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
5. Cover with butter and bacon strips
6. Cover with another sheet of dough sealed with eggs and properly folded
7. Decorate with lily flowers, gild it, and cook in the oven for five or six hours
8. When cooked, remove, let cool, and serve cold with a well-folded napkin underneath
