## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Bacon
- Truffle
- Tender or puff pastry
- Veal slices
- Butter
- Onion

## Directions

1. After plucking and roasting the woodcock, cut it into four parts: wings and thighs
2. From the rest of the meat, livers, and entrails make a chopped mixture
3. After chopping in a mortar with scraped bacon, a truffle, little salt, and pepper, all chopped
4. Cover the bottom of a pie dish with tender or puff pastry
5. Place the chopped mixture on top
6. Arrange the quarters of woodcock seasoned with salt, pepper, fine herbs, a little onion, and a piece of fresh butter
7. Cover everything with beaten veal slices and bacon
8. On top place a sheet of paste like the one below
9. Gild with egg
