## Ingredients

- Four young partridges
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Lemon juice
- Paper sheets
- Bacon slices
- Veal slices
- Ham slices
- Onion slices
- Bay leaf
- Marjoram
- Twine
- Water

## Directions

1. Take four young partridges and after cleaning, prepare and beat them between two cloths
2. Marinate them with salt, pepper, fine herbs, and lemon juice
3. After marinating, wrap them in paper sheets with slices of bacon, veal, and ham, some onion slices, bay leaf, and marjoram, both above and below
4. After wrapping well, tie with twine
5. Sprinkle the paper box with water and place to cook between ashes covered with coals
6. When cooked, carefully open the box
7. Remove the young partridges and serve with the sauce that seems most appropriate
