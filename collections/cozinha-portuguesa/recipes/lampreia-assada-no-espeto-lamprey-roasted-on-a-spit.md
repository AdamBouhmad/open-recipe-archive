## Ingredients

- lamprey
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- bread slices
- butter
- sweet sauce or pleasant sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the lamprey into equal pieces
2. Marinate in olive oil with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
3. Cut an equal number of bread slices
4. Thread the lamprey pieces and bread slices alternately onto a small spit
5. Tie to another larger spit
6. Roast, basting with butter
7. When roasted, serve with sweet sauce or pleasant sauce
