## Ingredients

- tender turkey hen
- raw bacon
- parsley
- chives
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- pork butter
- bacon strips
- paper leaves

## Directions

1. Take a tender mortified turkey hen
2. Separate the skin from the breast meat
3. Stuff with a forcemeat made of raw chopped bacon pounded in a mortar with parsley, chopped chives, and all manner of fine herbs, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. After stuffing the breast and placing the same forcemeat inside the body of the turkey hen, sear it in a casserole with pork butter and let it cool
5. Once cooled and cleaned, place it on a small spit and tie it to a large spit covered with bacon strips and paper leaves
6. Roast with care
7. When roasted, remove the paper and bacon, serve with sauce or ragout of all kinds of garnishes
