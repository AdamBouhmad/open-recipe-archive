## Ingredients

- One bowl full of sweet almonds
- Some pieces of prepared fish or some fillets of fried sole, without skin
- Six yolks of hard eggs
- Two spoonfuls of fish broth
- Some mushrooms
- One bunch of parsley
- One bunch of marjoram
- Some small onions
- Two cloves
- A piece of bread crumb

## Directions

1. Pound the sweet almonds in a stone mortar until crushed
2. Add some pieces of prepared fish or some fillets of fried sole without skin and six yolks of hard eggs
3. Pound everything very well
4. Pass two spoonfuls of fish broth through a sieve and add it
5. Add some mushrooms, one bunch of parsley, one bunch of marjoram, some small onions, two cloves, and a piece of bread crumb
6. Let it simmer slowly for a quarter of an hour
7. The almonds and ground fish dissolve in this broth
8. Pass it through a sieve and reheat, stirring constantly with a spoon and without letting it boil
9. Serve over slices of bread cooked in broth but dried
