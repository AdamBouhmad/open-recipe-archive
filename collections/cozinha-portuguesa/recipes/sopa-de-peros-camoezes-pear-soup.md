## Ingredients

- pear sauce
- three or four pears
- roasted meat
- toasted bread slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a new pot, pour the sauce from any kind of roast
2. Add three or four pears in separate quarters
3. Let it boil
4. When the pears are cooked, place them with the sauce on a plate over toasted bread slices
5. Place the roast on top with lemon juice
6. Serve garnished with marrow from the same sauce
