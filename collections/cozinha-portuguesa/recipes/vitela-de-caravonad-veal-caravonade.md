## Ingredients

- four arratels of veal from the leg, sliced very thin
- lard, seasoned with salt and pepper
- pork lard or lard for frying
- preservation broth
- all black seasonings
- breadcrumbs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. After being in preservation for three days, cut four arratels of veal from the leg into very thin slices
2. Lard them with pieces of seasoned lard
3. Fry them in pork lard or lard
4. Once fried, cook them for about an hour with a sauce made from the preservation broth and all black seasonings
5. Remove them from the sauce and brown them on the grill, covered with breadcrumbs and cinnamon
6. Once toasted, send to the table
7. The same is done with kid and cow loin
