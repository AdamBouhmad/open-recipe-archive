## Ingredients

- half an arratel of fat cheese, grated
- half a quartilho of white wine
- a piece of washed butter
- two scallions
- one of parsley, minced
- pepper
- nutmeg
- four egg yolks
- egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Place on heat in a saucepan half an arratel of fat cheese, grated, and half a quartilho of white wine, a piece of washed butter, two scallions, and one of parsley minced, pepper, and nutmeg, and stirring everything with a spoon until the cheese melts.
2. Add to it four egg yolks and their beaten whites, and mix everything together and cook.
3. Place on the plate garnished with pieces of fried bread crumb.
4. Serve hot as a side dish.
