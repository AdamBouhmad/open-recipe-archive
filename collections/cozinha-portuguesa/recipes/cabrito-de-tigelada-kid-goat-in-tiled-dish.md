## Ingredients

- kid goat, cut into small pieces
- lard
- bread slices
- eggs

## Directions

1. Braise the kid goat in small pieces with lard
2. Once cooked and seasoned, place it in a frying pan over slices of bread
3. Pour six beaten eggs on top and brown it
