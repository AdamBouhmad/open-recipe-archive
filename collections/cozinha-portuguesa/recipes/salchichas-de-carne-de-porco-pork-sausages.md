## Ingredients

- tender pork meat
- pork fat
- pork lard
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- parsley, chives, and shallots, chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop very finely some tender pork meat with an equal amount of its fat and a piece of lard
2. Season with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
3. If you wish, add chopped parsley, chives, and shallots
4. Fill the intestines using a wide funnel as is customary
5. Tie them
6. Make them the length you wish
7. Fry them on grills
8. Serve them
