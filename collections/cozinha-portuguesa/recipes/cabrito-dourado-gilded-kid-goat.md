## Ingredients

- roasted kid goat
- eggs
- butter
- cheese
- bread
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon

## Directions

1. Once the kid goat is roasted, cut it into pieces and pass them through beaten eggs and fry in butter
2. Place a cheese soup in a silver plate or low frying pan, consisting of a quarter of cheese in slices, bread of ten réis in slices, and a quarter of sugar
3. Butter the plate, place the bread slices as a bed, and on each bed of slices another of cheese slices with sugar and cinnamon on top
4. Finally pour over all of this the beaten egg that remained from covering the kid goat
5. Brown it and send to the table with the kid goat, cinnamon, and lemon on top
