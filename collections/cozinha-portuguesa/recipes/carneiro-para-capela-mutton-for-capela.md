## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton from the leg, finely chopped
- half arratel of bacon, chopped with herbs
- one half quart of butter
- all spices
- two eggs
- one ten-réis bread, grated
- caldo from the pot
- four eggs for thickening
- bread slices
- cinnamon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Finely chop two arratels of mutton from the leg
2. Separately chop half arratel of bacon with herbs and fry in one half quart of butter
3. When fried, mix with the chopped mutton and chop all together well
4. Season with all spices, add two eggs and grated bread
5. Make the capela in a frying pan with one spoonful of broth from the pot
6. Place on gentle heat underneath and on top
7. When ready to serve, thicken with four eggs, place bread slices on top, cinnamon and lemon juice on top
