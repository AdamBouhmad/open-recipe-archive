## Ingredients

- Two partridges
- Bacon for larding
- Two sides of cow meat
- A piece of ham
- Two roots (vegetables)
- One onion with two cloves
- Broth
- Some turnips
- Butter
- Flour
- Bread crusts

## Directions

1. Clean two partridges, clean them, lard them with bacon, and sauté with two sides of cow meat, a piece of ham, two roots, and one onion with two cloves
2. After everything is sautéed, add the necessary broth
3. Pass everything to a pot and place to boil
4. Meanwhile, cut some turnips into equal pieces, turn them, pass them through the fire in a casserole with melted butter, dust them with flour, and when they are somewhat browned, add a little broth from the partridges
5. After cooking and without fat, boil some bread crusts in the same broth, garnish with the turnips, place the partridges in the middle, and serve
6. In the same manner, one makes ducks, ducks, teals, and pigeons
