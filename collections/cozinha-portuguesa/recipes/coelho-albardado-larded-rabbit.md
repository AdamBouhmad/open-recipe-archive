## Ingredients

- rabbit, roasted
- bread slices
- lard, melted and cleaned of cracklings
- butter
- onion, cut into pieces
- herbs
- parsley
- pepper
- clove
- ginger
- saffron
- lemon

## Directions

1. Once roasted, place the rabbit on a plate over slices of bread with many cuts along the loin and legs
2. Make a sauce with a quarter of melted lard, cleaned of cracklings, a spoonful of butter, an onion cut into pieces, plenty of herbs and parsley, all finely minced
3. Season the sauce with pepper, clove, ginger, and a little saffron
4. Pour the sauce over the rabbit, add lemon on top and send to the table
