## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- finely chopped onion
- two anchovies
- one cup of white or red wine
- half spoon of meat broth
- half spoon of substance
- salt
- pepper
- small pinch of saffron
- bay leaf
- sprig of marjoram
- two halves of fried ray with whole capers
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Brown the flour in butter with finely chopped onion
2. Add two anchovies
3. Pour in one cup of white or red wine, half a spoon of meat broth, and half a spoon of substance
4. Season with salt, pepper, a small pinch of saffron, a bay leaf, and a sprig of marjoram
5. Let it boil for a moment
6. Add the two halves of fried ray with whole capers
7. Serve hot with a splash of vinegar or lemon juice
