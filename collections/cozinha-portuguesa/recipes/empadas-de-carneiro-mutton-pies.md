## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton from the leg without bones
- bacon for larding
- bacon chopped
- all seasonings
- wine
- vinegar
- two dried bay leaves
- one eighth of flour
- one half arratel of butter
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Sauté in chopped bacon two arratels of mutton from the leg without bones, larded with bacon lardons
2. When half-cooked, season with all the seasonings, a touch of wine, a touch of vinegar, and two dried bay leaves, and after seasoning without finishing cooking, let cool
3. Knead one eighth of flour with one half arratel of butter and four eggs, make a box, in which place the mutton with broth
4. When the pie is made and baked, serve to the table
