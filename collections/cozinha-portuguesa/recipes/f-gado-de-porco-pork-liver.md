## Ingredients

- pork liver, well roasted
- pork caul fat
- onion
- salt
- vinegar
- spices
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- garlic
- orange juice
- bread slices

## Directions

1. When you have a very well roasted pork liver, melt pork caul fat in a pot
2. Braise a chopped onion in it, and after braising, season it with salt and vinegar with spices, clove, pepper, a little saffron, and garlic
3. Add the liver and when it has simmered, remove it from the fire and add orange juice
4. Serve on bread slices and send to the table
