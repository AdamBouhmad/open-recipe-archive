## Ingredients

- one duck, half-roasted
- salt and pepper
- slices of prosciutto
- loin or bacon, also salted and peppered
- butter
- thin dough leaves

## Directions

1. After the duck is half-roasted and well salted and peppered, insert slices of prosciutto, loin, or bacon (also salted and peppered) inside
2. Coat the duck very well with butter
3. Cover the top with thin dough leaves
4. Wrap in paper greased with butter and return to the fire
5. Finish roasting, then remove the paper and place on a plate
6. Serve at table
7. Optionally, you may also make a crust of beaten eggs and sugar or breadcrumbs on top
