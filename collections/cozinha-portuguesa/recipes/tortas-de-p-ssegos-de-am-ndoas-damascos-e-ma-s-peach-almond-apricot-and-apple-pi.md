## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Fine sugar
- Peaches, almonds, apricots or apples, peeled and halved
- More fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a baking dish with a piece of puff pastry dough
2. Cover the center with fine sugar, place the peaches, almonds, apricots or apples halved and pitted on top
3. Cover with fine sugar as underneath
4. Enclose the pie with strips of dough, make a border around it of two fingers width
5. Place in the oven to bake; when cooked, glaze with sugar and serve hot between courses
6. If the peaches are not ripe, cook them in sugar first and then make the pies as is customary for pears, apples, marinellos, cherries, verjuice, strawberries, and other fruits
