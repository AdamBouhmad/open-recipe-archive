## Ingredients

- two bundles of sorrel leaves
- washed butter, powdered with flour
- two egg yolks
- salt
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pound two bundles of sorrel leaves in a mortar
2. Press out the juice through a cloth into a saucepan
3. Add a bit of washed butter powdered with flour
4. Add two egg yolks, little salt, and grated nutmeg
5. Place over heat and stir with a spoon until thickened and well-flavored
6. Serve with fresh poached eggs on top
