## Ingredients

- flour
- water
- salt
- little butter
- one egg yolk
- pork butter
- two egg yolks beaten with butter
- soft eggs
- sugar in point
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a dough with flour, water, salt, little butter, and one egg yolk
2. When very well kneaded, spread it
3. Put pork butter on it generously and roll it up in the breadlike dough well greased
4. Cut the cakes round and fry them
5. Open the puff pastry with a small roll
6. Put in each ball two egg yolks beaten with sufficient butter
7. Always open the puff pastry and fill with soft eggs if desired
8. After frying, pass through sugar in point with cinnamon on top and serve at the table
