## Ingredients

- ten or twelve egg yolks
- half of their egg whites
- spoonful of veal substance
- good quality coulis
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Beat ten or twelve yolks with half of their egg whites
2. Add a spoonful of veal substance and good coulis
3. Pass through a strainer or sieve
4. Pour into molds, first buttered with clarified butter
5. Place in a vessel filled with boiling water
6. Cook with gentle heat below and above
7. When cooked, turn over onto the plate
8. Serve with substance underneath for an entrée
