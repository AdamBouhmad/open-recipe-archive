## Ingredients

- one arratel of sugar in high point
- one dozen egg yolks beaten
- soft eggs or almond cakes

## Directions

1. Once one arratel of sugar is at high point, gradually pour in one dozen beaten egg yolks through an eggshell with a small hole in the bottom so the egg flows in a thin thread
2. While pouring, stir the sugar pot very well so the sugar does not soften its consistency
3. Once all egg is poured, place the threads on a plate, open with a fork, and add soft eggs or almond cakes in the middle, serve at table
