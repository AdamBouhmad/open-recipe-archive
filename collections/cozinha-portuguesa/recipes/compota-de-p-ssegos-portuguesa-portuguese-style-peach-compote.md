## Ingredients

- Seven or eight almost ripe peaches
- Finely grated sugar

## Directions

1. Cut seven or eight almost ripe peaches in half and remove the pit.
2. Arrange them on a plate with finely grated sugar underneath and on top, place over gentle heat, covered with a lid with fire on top, and let them cook slowly.
3. When cooked and glazed with good color, serve hot.
