## Ingredients

- rabbit, half-roasted
- sugar
- almonds, ground
- toasted bread crumbs
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- clove of India
- musk

## Directions

1. After singeing and half-roasting the rabbit, mince it well
2. Place it in an arratel of sugar syrup, with half an arratel of ground almonds
3. Cook slowly for the space of an hour
4. Add five réis of toasted bread crumbs and eight egg yolks to thicken it
5. Season with cinnamon, clove of India, and musk
6. Pour into small dishes and send to the table browned
