## Ingredients

- fat chickens no more than two months old
- bacon for larding
- paper spread with butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Choose fat chickens no more than two months old; after bleeding them, plucking them, and letting them rest, clean them as is customary, straighten them, and truss them over the coals
2. Lard them with bacon, put them on the spit, cover them with sheets of buttered paper, put them to roast, turning constantly with sufficient fire
3. After roasting, remove the paper, and when browned serve them hot
4. Also, instead of being larded, they can be covered with strips of bacon
5. Also after roasting, the bacon removed, cover them with fine breadcrumbs, brown them, and serve them hot as a roasted dish
