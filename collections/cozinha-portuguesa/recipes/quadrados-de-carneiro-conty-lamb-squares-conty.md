## Ingredients

- lamb chop squares
- bacon strips
- mushrooms
- pepper
- shallots
- parsley
- chives
- tarragon leaves
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- stock
- white wine
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Take two squares of lamb chops that match well and prepare them as described above
2. Cut into slices some bacon and well-washed mushrooms
3. Season with pepper, shallots, parsley, chives, four tarragon leaves, half a bay leaf, and a little pounded marjoram, mixing everything well
4. Lard the chops and place them to cook in a casserole over gentle heat with little stock and white wine
5. When cooked and the stock is reduced, remove the fat
6. Bind with equal amounts of butter, dust with flour, and serve hot over the chops
