## Ingredients

- one turkey
- one small piece of good butter
- seasonings
- one garlic clove
- a measure of vinegar
- a measure of water
- one quarter of ground almonds
- black spices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the turkey in an oven in a pan with a small piece of good butter, seasonings, one garlic clove, a measure of vinegar, and a measure of water
2. When roasted, strain the sauce into a bowl
3. Add one quarter of ground almonds and bring to a boil
4. Season with black spices
5. Carve the turkey and place on slices
6. Pour the sauce over with lemon juice
7. Serve to the table
