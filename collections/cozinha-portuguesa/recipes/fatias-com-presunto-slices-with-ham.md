## Ingredients

- bread crumb slices, two or three fingers wide and long
- ham slices, not too fatty
- rendered bacon fat
- broth
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread crumb two or three fingers wide and long
2. Cut equal slices of ham that is not too fatty
3. Fry in a casserole with rendered bacon fat
4. When browned on both sides, place each piece on its bread slice
5. Deglaze the casserole with a little broth, add a splash of vinegar and a bay leaf
6. Pour over the slices with coarse pepper and serve hot
