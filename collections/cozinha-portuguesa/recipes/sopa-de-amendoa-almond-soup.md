## Ingredients

- half canada of milk
- eight egg yolks
- half arratel of sugar
- four ounces of almonds, very well ground
- a quarter of butter

## Directions

1. Pour half canada of milk into a pan with eight egg yolks, half arratel of sugar, four ounces of very well ground almonds, and a quarter of butter
2. Beat everything together very well
3. Place in a pan to cook over low heat
4. As it thickens, pour it into a plate or frying pan
5. Brown it in the oven or on the griddle
6. Once browned, take it to the table
