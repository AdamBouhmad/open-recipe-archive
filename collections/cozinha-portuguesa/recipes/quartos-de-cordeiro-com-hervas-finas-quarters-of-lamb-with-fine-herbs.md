## Ingredients

- two hind quarters of lamb
- bacon, scraped
- salt
- onion, chopped
- pepper
- fine herbs
- fresh butter
- bacon slabs
- paper
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- meat sauce
- cullis
- shallot, chopped
- lemon or bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Take two hind quarters of lamb and skin them carefully from the kidney area to the tail, taking care not to tear the skin
2. Prepare a mixture of scraped bacon, salt, chopped onion, pepper, fine herbs, and fresh butter, all finely chopped and mixed
3. Introduce this mixture between the meat and the skin
4. Tie with string so it does not come out
5. Place on the spit covered with bacon slabs and paper leaves
6. When roasted, remove the paper and bacon, brush with butter, cover with breadcrumbs, and brown
7. When browned, serve with meat sauce made of substance with a little cullis, chopped shallot, salt, pepper, and lemon or sour orange juice
