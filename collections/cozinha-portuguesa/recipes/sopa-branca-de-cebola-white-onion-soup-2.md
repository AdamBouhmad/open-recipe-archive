## Ingredients

- two or three dozen small onions of equal size
- onion broth or vegetable broth with herbs, well seasoned and of good flavor
- bread slices or crusts
- white sauce made from some almonds
- yolks of hard boiled eggs
- bread crumb soaked in broth, passed through a sieve, and heated
- or egg yolks mixed and bound with broth

## Directions

1. Boil the onions in water until half cooked, then pass them through cold water and drain them
2. Finish cooking them in vegetable broth and herb broth, well seasoned and of good flavor
3. Soak some bread crusts or slices in the same broth in which the onions were cooked
4. Serve the soup with white sauce made from some almonds, yolks of hard boiled eggs, and bread crumb soaked in broth, passed through a sieve, and heated
5. In place of the white sauce, one may serve with egg yolks mixed and bound with broth
