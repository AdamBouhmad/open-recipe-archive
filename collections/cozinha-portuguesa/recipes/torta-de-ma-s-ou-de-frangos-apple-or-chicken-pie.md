## Ingredients

- Apples or chickens
- Bacon or melted butter
- Truffles
- Salt and pepper
- Puff pastry dough
- Minced meat
- Bay leaf studded with cloves
- A little minced parsley and fresh butter
- Bacon strips
- Egg yolks
- Orange juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the apples or chickens with feet tucked inside and once stiffened, fry in bacon or melted butter
2. Mince truffles and season with salt and pepper
3. Spread a piece of puff pastry dough on a baking dish
4. Add a layer of minced meat, arrange the apples or chickens on top with parsley sprigs
5. Add a layer of fried bread, a bay leaf studded with cloves, a little minced parsley and fresh butter
6. Cover with bacon strips and another piece of puff pastry dough that is sealed and browned
7. Bake in the oven for about two hours
8. Once cooked, open it, remove the bacon strips and bay leaf, add a sauce made from egg yolks, truffles, mushrooms, and serve hot with orange juice
