## Ingredients

- Cauliflower, divided into florets
- Water
- Salt
- Flour
- Butter or salt pork
- Meat essence
- Butter

## Directions

1. Divide the cauliflower into pieces and remove the skin
2. Cook in water with salt, flour, and a piece of salt pork or butter
3. When cooked, arrange around any piece of meat with essence and a piece of butter bound on top
