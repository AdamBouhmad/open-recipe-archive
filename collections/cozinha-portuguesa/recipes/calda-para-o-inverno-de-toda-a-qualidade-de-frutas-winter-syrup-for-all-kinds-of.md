## Ingredients

- fruit (strawberries, currants, blackberries, or raspberries)
- sugar

## Directions

1. For each pound of fruit, reduce two pounds of sugar to the hard crack stage.
2. Add the fruit to the syrup.
3. Give it one boil.
4. Remove from heat.
5. Strain through a sieve.
6. Let the fruit drain and the syrup cool.
7. Pour the syrup into bottles.
8. Cork it.
9. From this syrup make cold or unfrozen drinking waters as desired.
