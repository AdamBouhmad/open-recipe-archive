## Ingredients

- two or three chickens
- half a canada of pork, veal, or chicken blood
- one arratel of pork fat cut into dice
- parsley
- chives
- herbs
- eight egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- pounded coriander

## Directions

1. Clean and tenderize two or three chickens
2. Debone them as with previous recipes
3. Take half a canada of pork, veal, or chicken blood and one arratel of pork fat cut into dice
4. Add parsley, chives, herbs, and eight egg yolks
5. Season with salt, pepper, and pounded coriander
6. Thicken over low heat
7. When incorporated and good tasting, cool and stuff the chickens with this mixture
8. Sew with needle and cook in a white marinade with fine herbs and dried coriander, simmering gently
9. When cooked, remove and serve with ham essence
