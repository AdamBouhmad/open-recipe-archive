## Ingredients

- flour
- beer or white wine
- a drop of oil or a piece of clarified butter
- salt
- egg whites
- strawberries
- pork lard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a batter of flour mixed with beer or white wine, a drop of oil or a piece of clarified butter, and salt
2. At serving time, add two or three well-beaten egg whites
3. Pass some strawberries through it and fry in pork lard
4. After frying, serve hot, dusted with sugar
