## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 spoonfuls of finest wheat flour
- some milk
- 1.5 dozen eggs, beaten
- 1.5 to 2 Canadian measures of milk
- salt
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- 0.5 arratel of clarified butter

## Directions

1. Pour the spoonfuls of finest wheat flour into a saucepan
2. Dissolve it with a little milk
3. Add the beaten eggs and mix everything together
4. Then add 1.5 to 2 Canadian measures of milk
5. Season with salt, cinnamon, and lemon peel
6. Add half an arratel of clarified butter
7. Place over heat, stirring constantly until it is thickened and cooked, taking care that it does not stick
8. Remove from heat, let it cool, and use it for tarts, fritters, and other small dishes as needed
