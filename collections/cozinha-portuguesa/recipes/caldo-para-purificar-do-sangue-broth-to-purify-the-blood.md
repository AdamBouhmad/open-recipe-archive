## Ingredients

- one arratel of veal meat from the loin
- three quartilhos of water
- salt
- seven or eight live freshwater crayfish

## Directions

1. After skimming and when almost cooked one arratel of veal meat from the loin in three quartilhos of water, seasoned with salt
2. Crush seven or eight live freshwater crayfish and add them to simmer for half a quarter hour with the veal
3. Strain the broth through a sieve
4. Use it as with the previous broth
