## Ingredients

- Mussels, opened and shelled
- Mussel cooking liquid
- Truffles
- Cucumbers
- Butter or melted salt pork
- Pepper
- Parsley bouquet
- Fine herbs
- White wine
- Broth or stock
- Veal cullis
- Ham cullis

## Directions

1. Open the mussels over heat and shell them, reserving a little of their own liquid
2. Separately, sauté truffles and cucumbers in melted butter or salt pork, seasoned with pepper, a parsley bouquet, and fine herbs
3. After sautéing, add a drop of white wine and broth
4. Allow to simmer gently and slowly
5. When reduced, bind the sauce with veal cullis and ham cullis
6. Add the mussels with their liquid
7. Ensure good flavor and that it does not boil
8. Serve this ragout for all first courses with mussels
