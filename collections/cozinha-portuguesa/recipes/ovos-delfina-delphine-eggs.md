## Ingredients

- half pound clarified and hot butter
- dozen to dozen and a half fresh eggs
- salt
- ground cinnamon
- fine sugar
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Beat fresh eggs in a saucepan with half pound of hot clarified butter
2. Season with salt, ground cinnamon, fine sugar, and pistachios
