## Ingredients

- Roasted partridges
- Red wine
- Broth substance
- Cullis
- Shallot
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sour orange peel
- Sour orange juice
- Bread marrow
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. After roasting the partridges, remove the breasts and thighs, and from the rest, except the gizzard, pound in a mortar
2. Dissolve it with a spoon of bread in a saucepan with red wine and a little broth substance
3. Bring to a boil and add a little cullis, a chopped shallot, salt, pepper, a small piece of sour orange peel
4. When it has good flavor and the sauce is reduced, strain through a sieve
5. Place the quarters of partridge inside to heat
6. After becoming well heated without boiling, serve with sour orange juice, garnished with pieces of fried bread marrow
