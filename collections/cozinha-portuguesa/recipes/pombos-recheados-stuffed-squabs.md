## Ingredients

- 4 squabs
- mutton, finely chopped
- spices

## Directions

1. After opening the squabs along the back, breasts, and legs, stuff them with finely chopped mutton, seasoned according to the dish's style
2. Cook them over gentle heat in a low pot with boiling broth
3. Season with all the spices and thicken the broth with egg yolks so it becomes thick in the plates
4. Place on slices with lemon juice and cinnamon on top
