## Ingredients

- milk
- rice flour
- egg yolks
- clarified butter
- citron
- orange flowers
- almond biscuits
- salt
- ground cinnamon
- grated lemon peel
- fine sugar
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a thick, well-bound cream with milk and rice flour
2. Once cooled, add egg yolks
3. Add clarified butter, citron, orange flowers, almond biscuits, all chopped
4. Season with salt, ground cinnamon, and grated lemon peel
5. Add fine sugar
6. Mix everything together
7. Add beaten egg whites
8. Put all in a pan as the previous recipe
9. Once cooked, serve in the same manner and hot
