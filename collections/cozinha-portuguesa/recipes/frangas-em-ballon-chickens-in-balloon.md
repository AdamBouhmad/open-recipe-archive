## Ingredients

- two or three chickens
- chicken breasts
- partridge meat
- turkey meat or other tender game
- ham
- truffles
- pork fat, scraped
- salt
- pepper
- herbs

## Directions

1. Clean and bone two or three chickens as above
2. Prepare a filling made from diced chicken breasts, partridge, turkey or other tender game meat, ham, and truffles, all cut into small dice
3. Mix with scraped pork fat and season with salt, pepper, and herbs
4. Stuff the chickens and sew them around, closing them like a bag so they become round like a buoy
5. Blanch them in melted pork fat, then remove and drain them
6. Dry them very well and cover with a slice of pork fat
7. Cook in a braising pan until done
8. Serve with Italian sauce, or alternatively with Spanish sauce or clear essence and lemon juice
