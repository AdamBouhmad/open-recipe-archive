## Ingredients

- shredded pork fat (toucinho)
- four arratels of beef chuck
- four arratels of pork loin
- two chickens
- two partridges
- four pigeons
- half a dozen thrushes
- two rabbits
- half a dozen pork tongues
- turnips
- cabbages
- garlic
- grains
- whole spices
- flour

## Directions

1. Braise in shredded pork fat the following things, each separately: four arratels of beef chuck, four arratels of pork loin, two chickens, two partridges, four pigeons, half a dozen thrushes, two rabbits, half a dozen pork tongues, turnips, cabbages, garlic, grains, and all the whole spices
2. When everything is braised, which is halfway cooked in pork fat, knead a bushel of flour
