## Ingredients

- one arratel of sugar at thread point
- half an ounce of chocolate

## Directions

1. Reduce one arratel of sugar to the thread point
2. Add half an ounce of chocolate only and stir until well mixed
3. Pour into a paper mold, let cool, break, and store as described
