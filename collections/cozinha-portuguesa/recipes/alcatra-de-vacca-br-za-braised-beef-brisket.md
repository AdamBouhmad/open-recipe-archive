## Ingredients

- A piece of beef weighing fifteen or twenty pounds
- thick bacon for larding
- twine
- bacon slices for the bottom and sides of the pot
- beef slices
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- onions
- carrots
- two heads of garlic
- a bundle of fine herbs
- half a spoon of broth
- half a quartilho of white wine

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef weighing fifteen or twenty pounds and lard it with thick bacon
2. Tie it with twine and place it in a pot with a bottom lined with bacon slices and beef slices, seasoned with salt, pepper, cloves, onions, carrots, two heads of garlic, and a bundle of fine herbs
3. Cover with beef and bacon slices as below
4. Cook slowly between coals
5. Pour half a spoon of broth and half a quartilho of white wine and let it simmer slowly
6. When cooked, remove the twine, drain of its fat, place on a plate with the ragú of vegetables or other sauce as preferred
