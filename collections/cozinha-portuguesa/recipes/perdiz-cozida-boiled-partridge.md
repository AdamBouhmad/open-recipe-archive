## Ingredients

- one partridge
- one quarter pound of bacon
- two ounces of butter
- six grains (peppercorns)
- all the seasonings and herbs
- vinegar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook one partridge with one quarter pound of bacon, two ounces of butter, six grains, all the seasonings and herbs, and vinegar
2. Cook until done with enough broth to fill a vessel
3. Send to table with lemon juice
