## Ingredients

- breasts of capons, hens, and turkey thighs
- bacon scraped
- sage
- two shallots minced
- bread crumb moistened in milk
- four or five egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg grated
- pasta dough

## Directions

1. Take raw breasts of capons, hens, and turkey thighs, scrape them with a knife and remove all skin and mince them
2. Pass the minced meat to a stone mortar and pound with scraped bacon, some sage, and two finely minced shallots
3. When well pounded, add a piece of bread crumb moistened in milk but well squeezed and four or five egg yolks
4. Season with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
5. Pound again and place on a plate
6. Make a piece of thin pasta, stretch it so it is very thin
7. Cut it into strips and place small portions of the minced mixture at intervals, brushed around with beaten eggs
8. Cover with another piece of pasta
9. Cut with a mold and blanch in boiling water
10. Then cook in another broth for six minutes
11. Transfer to a sieve, place in a soup tureen, and serve with broth and veal substance of good taste and color
