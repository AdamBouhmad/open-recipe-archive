## Ingredients

- two roasted partridges, quartered
- two cloves of garlic
- olive oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- two bay leaves

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil in a vessel two roasted partridges cut in quarters with two cloves of garlic, olive oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and two bay leaves
2. When they finish boiling, send to table
