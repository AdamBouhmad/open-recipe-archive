## Ingredients

- Half pound of sweet almonds
- Milk
- Breast of capon
- Rice flour
- Sugar
- Salt
- Cinnamon
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak half pound of sweet almonds in hot water, shell them, and grind them, moistening from time to time with a little milk
2. Add to this a capon breast
3. Grind everything and make into a mass, dissolve with rice flour and one canful of milk
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Season with sugar and a little salt, whole cinnamon, and lemon juice
6. Place on the heat and stir always with a spoon, let it thicken little by little
7. When cooked and with good taste, serve hot or cold, as preferred
