## Ingredients

- dozen egg yolks
- half dozen egg whites
- melted butter
- two or three spoonfuls of ragout of asparagus tips, well finished

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and whites very well
2. Put them in a saucepan with melted butter
3. Add two or three spoonfuls of well-finished asparagus tip ragout
4. Stir everything together until cooked and well-flavored
5. Serve as above with lemon juice
