## Ingredients

- veal kidney with all its fat
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- chopped chives
- half a quarter of milk
- eggs
- essence sauce

## Directions

1. Roast with all its fat a veal kidney, let it cool, and after cold chop very finely
2. When chopped, sauté in melted butter, seasoned with salt, pepper, herbs, and chopped chives
3. Add half a quarter of milk
4. Break the necessary eggs in another casserole, then mix, beat everything together
5. Cook as above on both sides
6. Serve with hot essence sauce
