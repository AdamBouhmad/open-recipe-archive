## Ingredients

- one pound of beef fat
- strong cream made from rice flour or wheat flour
- eight egg yolks
- lemon peel
- preserved orange flower
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg flower
- sugar
- one drop of brandy
- butter
- Rhine wine
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take one pound of beef fat and mix with a strong cream made from rice flour or wheat flour, eight egg yolks, lemon peel, and preserved orange flower
2. Season with salt, cinnamon, nutmeg flower, sugar, and one drop of brandy
3. After incorporating everything, place in a casserole with the bottom greased inside with butter
4. Put in an oven and bake for two hours
5. Remove, turn onto its plate, glaze with sugar using a hot spatula, and serve with butter sauce, Rhine wine, sugar, and lemon peel
