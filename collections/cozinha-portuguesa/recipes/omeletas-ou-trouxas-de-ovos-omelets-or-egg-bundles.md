## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- chopped chives
- bacon or butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs in a casserole, season with salt, pepper, herbs, and chopped chives
2. Pour into a certain heat of bacon or very hot butter
3. When the eggs are set and browned on one side, turn the bundle over
4. When cooked and browned, turn onto its plate with care and serve hot
