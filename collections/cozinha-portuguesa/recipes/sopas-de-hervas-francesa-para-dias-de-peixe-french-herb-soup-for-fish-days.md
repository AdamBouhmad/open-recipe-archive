## Ingredients

- five or six lettuce sprouts
- half dozen green onions
- half ounce of peas
- some asparagus tips
- butter
- root broth
- root substance

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces five or six lettuce sprouts, half a dozen green onions, half ounce of peas, and some asparagus tips
2. Sauté everything in butter; after sautéing, add one spoonful of root broth and another of the same substance
3. Let simmer gently
