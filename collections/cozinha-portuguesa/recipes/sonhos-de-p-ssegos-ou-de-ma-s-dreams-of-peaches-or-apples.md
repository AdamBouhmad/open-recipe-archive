## Ingredients

- a few ripe apples
- brandy
- sugar
- a piece of lemon rind
- flower water
- flour
- pork lard

## Directions

1. Take a few ripe apples, peel them, cut into quarters, remove the cores, and marinate for two or three hours in brandy with sugar, a piece of lemon rind, and flower water
2. Drain them in a cloth, dust with flour, and fry in pork lard
3. When fried and golden, serve glazed with fine sugar like the previous ones
4. Alternatively, cut the apples into rounds, pass through batter, fry, and serve the same way
