## Ingredients

- a quarter of bacon with onion, finely chopped
- two arratels of lamb
- three eggs
- toasted bread
- lemon
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Finely chop a quarter of bacon with onion
2. Finely chop two arratels of lamb separately
3. Brown the bacon with half of the lamb
4. When half browned, return to the cutting board
5. Mix with the other half of lamb
6. Season with three eggs, toasted bread, lemon, and all seasonings so it becomes well soured
