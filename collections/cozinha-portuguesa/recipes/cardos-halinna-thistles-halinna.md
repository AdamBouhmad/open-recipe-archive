## Ingredients

- thistles
- water
- salt
- onion
- butter
- dark seasonings
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Place thistles to cook in water and salt after cleaning them very well and cutting them to the size of a finger, about half a dozen
2. Once cooked, sauté a small amount of chopped onion in half an arroba of butter
3. When sautéed, add the thistles inside as they finish cooking
4. Season with dark seasonings
5. Serve at table with plenty of orange juice on top
