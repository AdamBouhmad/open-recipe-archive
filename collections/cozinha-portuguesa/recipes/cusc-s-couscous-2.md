## Ingredients

- flour
- a piece of wheat
- water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and mix with a piece of wheat.
2. Place the couscous in a couscous maker lined very well with papers inside.
3. Put an iron or clay pot with water on a trivet over the fire and bring to a boil.
4. Place the couscous maker on top of the pot opening.
5. When the couscous is cooked (it should taste like cooked bread), remove it and return it to the bowl to break it apart with great force in only one direction.
6. Once broken apart, put it in the sun to dry and harden.
7. When ready to eat, prepare the broth separately in a bowl as desired, either meat broth, sweet broth, or broth made like for vermicelli.
8. Once the broth is made and boiled, remove from heat and pour the couscous on top.
9. Let it dry and serve on plates with cinnamon on top.
