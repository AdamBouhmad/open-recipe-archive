## Ingredients

- Two dozen small birds
- Half an arratel of chopped bacon
- Small slices of pork loin
- All ground seasonings

## Directions

1. Brown two dozen small birds in half an arratel of bacon with small slices of pork loin, seasoning with all ground seasonings
2. Cook and let cool
3. Shape pastry boxes as described for common pastries, filling with the birds
4. Cook and serve
