## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- half a pound of pistachios
- egg whites
- a bit of citron
- lime peel
- one pound of grated sugar
- fifteen fresh egg yolks
- egg whites, beaten and raised

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of almonds and half a pound of pistachios in hot water, then place them in cold water, drain them in a cloth, and dry them
2. Pound them in a stone mortar with a few egg whites
3. When pounded and reduced to a paste, add a bit of chopped citron and lime peel, one pound of grated sugar, and fifteen fresh egg yolks
4. Mix everything very well, add the beaten and raised egg whites
5. Butter a dish, put everything in it, butter it around the edges, dust with fine sugar
6. Put in the oven to bake with moderate heat
7. After baking, serve glazed
