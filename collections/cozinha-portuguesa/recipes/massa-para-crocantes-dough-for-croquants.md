## Ingredients

- flour
- fine sugar
- egg whites
- a little orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Place on a table equal quantities of flour and fine sugar, moisten with egg whites and a little orange blossom water
2. When the dough is bound and formed, but not too soft, dust with flour, extend the dough with a rolling pin, and it will serve for croquants, tarts, small pastries, and other small inter-course dishes
