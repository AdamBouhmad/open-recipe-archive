## Ingredients

- pieces of veal and ham, in large dice
- two roots
- one onion
- melted bacon fat
- a piece of butter
- powdered flour
- restored sauce
- reduced cow's milk
- salt
- pepper
- one clove of garlic
- one onion studded with cloves
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Cut in large dice pieces of veal and ham, two roots, and one onion; pass everything through the fire in a pan with melted bacon fat and a piece of butter powdered with flour; then add restored sauce and reduced cow's milk, salt, pepper, one clove of garlic, one onion studded with cloves, fine herbs, and let it simmer little by little; when cooked and reduced, pass it through a sieve, and serve this sauce for slices of roasted game, vegetables, and other garnishes.
