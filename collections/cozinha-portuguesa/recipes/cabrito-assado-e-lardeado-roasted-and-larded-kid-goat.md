## Ingredients

- quarter of kid goat
- thin lard slices

## Directions

1. Begin roasting a quarter of kid goat on the spit
2. When almost roasted, lard it with thin slices of lard half a finger in length
3. Finish roasting and send to the table over the soup of choice
