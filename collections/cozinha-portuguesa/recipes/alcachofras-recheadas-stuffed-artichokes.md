## Ingredients

- artichokes
- ham or bacon slices
- ground meat, fish, or herbs for filling
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Trim and clean the artichokes, remove the hay from the middle, and stuff them with any ground meat, fish, or herbs
2. Place them to cook between slices of bacon, with half a spoon of broth, salt, pepper, two cloves, and a bundle of fine herbs
3. When cooked, place them to drain on a sieve and serve afterwards with ham essence
