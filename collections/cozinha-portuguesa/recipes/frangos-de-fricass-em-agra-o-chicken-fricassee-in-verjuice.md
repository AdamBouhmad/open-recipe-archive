## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter or melted bacon fat
- ham
- herbs
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot water
- broth
- egg yolks
- verjuice
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Prepare, brown, and cook as in the preceding recipe
2. The difference is that the egg yolks should be mixed in verjuice instead, of which some whole berries blanched in water should be left
