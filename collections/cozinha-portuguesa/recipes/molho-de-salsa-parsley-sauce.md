## Ingredients

- half spoonful of broth
- a little cullis
- one egg yolk
- tender chopped parsley
- a drop of vinegar
- a piece of butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place in a saucepan half spoonful of broth, a little cullis, one egg yolk, tender chopped parsley, a drop of vinegar, a piece of butter, and pepper
2. Put over heat, stirring always until it is bound
3. Serves for all kinds of game or fish, as preferred
