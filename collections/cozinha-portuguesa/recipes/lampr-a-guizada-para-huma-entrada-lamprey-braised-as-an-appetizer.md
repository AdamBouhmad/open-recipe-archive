## Ingredients

- One lamprey, bled
- The blood of the lamprey
- Hot water
- Butter
- Flour
- Browned flour
- A little wine
- A spoon of broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- A bunch of fine herbs
- Onion with three cloves
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bleed and set aside the blood of one lamprey
2. Place the lamprey in hot water
3. After being scalded and cleaned with the back of a knife, remove the tripe
4. Cut into equal pieces
5. Braise for an instant in butter with browned flour
6. Add a little wine and a spoon of broth
7. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, a bunch of fine herbs, and onion with three cloves
8. Let simmer little by little
9. When cooked and the sauce reduced, add the blood
10. After binding, serve hot with lemon juice
