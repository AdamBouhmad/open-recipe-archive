## Ingredients

- small amount of parsley
- onion
- mussels with vinegar
- eggs
- pastry dough

## Directions

1. Chop a small amount of parsley and onion very well and put it to stew
2. After stewed, add the mussels with their vinegar
3. When boiling, season with eggs and set to cool
4. Make the pie and after cooking send it to the table
