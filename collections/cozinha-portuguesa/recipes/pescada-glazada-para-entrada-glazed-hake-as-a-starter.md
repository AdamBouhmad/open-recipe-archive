## Ingredients

- hake
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- fine herbs
- flour
- butter or olive oil
- butter
- flour
- fish substance
- white wine
- fish roe
- capers
- bay leaf
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Braise the hake along the sides, from the head to the tail; remove the loins and marinate for the space of an hour in vinegar, salt, pepper, onion slices, and some fine herbs
2. Dry them in a cloth, flour them, and fry them in clarified butter or in very hot olive oil over a brisk fire
3. Once fried, put them in a butter, flour, and meat sauce with some fish substance, white wine, fish roe, and capers, seasoned with salt, pepper, and a bay leaf
4. Once it has simmered and the sauce is reduced, serve with lemon juice
