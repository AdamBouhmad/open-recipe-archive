## Ingredients

- half a pound of bacon, chopped
- onion
- parsley, well chopped
- rabbits, cut into pieces
- all the seasonings
- cumin
- lemon
- olive oil
- verjuice or vinegar
- bread slices
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Brown half a pound of chopped bacon with onion and well-chopped parsley
2. When browned, add the rabbits cut into pieces
3. When cooked, season with all the seasonings, cumin, lemon, olive oil, and verjuice (if available) or vinegar
4. Place on bread slices with lemon slices on top
5. Send to table
