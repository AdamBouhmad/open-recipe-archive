## Ingredients

- turbot
- water
- salt
- napkin
- milk

## Directions

1. After cleaning the turbot at the gills and without opening it, wash it very well and serve it in a round pot with a napkin underneath and a half portion of water and salt
2. Let it simmer for half an hour little by little, and when it is almost cooked, remove the pot from the fire, place it over a few brazas, pour it a pint of hot milk, let it sit for half an hour without boiling
3. Once the fish is cooked, suspend the napkin so it does not break, and place it on the plate in which it will be served, send it to the table garnished with parsley
