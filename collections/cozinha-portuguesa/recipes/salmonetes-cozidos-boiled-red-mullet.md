## Ingredients

- red mullet
- white wine
- butter
- salt
- roots
- onion slices
- cloves
- fine herbs
- parsley sauce

## Directions

1. Remove gills and entrails from red mullet without opening or scaling them
2. Cut off the tail tips
3. Cook in a broth made with white wine, a piece of butter, salt, roots, onion slices, cloves, and fine herbs
4. When cooked, serve garnished with parsley sauce around it
5. Alternatively, after cooking remove the scales and serve with Dutch sauce made with butter, anchovies, and capers, as is customary
