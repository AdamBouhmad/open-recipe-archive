## Ingredients

- 2 whole squabs
- bacon
- seasonings
- Portuguese puff pastry
- paper
- string

## Directions

1. Wrap two whole squabs in bacon and season with seasonings, then roast them on a spit
2. When they are browned, cover each one with a piece of Portuguese puff pastry
3. Wrap them in a sheet of paper and tie with string, then roast again on the spit until the pastry is cooked
4. Remove the paper and send to the table hot
