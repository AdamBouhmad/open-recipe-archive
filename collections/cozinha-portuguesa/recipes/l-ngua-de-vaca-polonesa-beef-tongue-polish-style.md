## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- sugar
- orange peel candied
- cinnamon pieces
- sugar
- wine
- half pound
- one quarter measure
- substance

## Directions

1. Prepare the beef tongue and place it in boiling water to remove the skin
2. Once peeled, place it to cook in a braise
3. When cooked, remove it and split it in the middle without separating it
4. Lard it with candied orange peel that has been dusted with sugar and with small pieces of fine cinnamon
5. Put half a pound of sugar, one quarter measure of wine, and a little substance in a pan and bring to a boil
6. Place the tongue inside to cook gently
7. When the sauce is reduced and made, remove it to its plate and pour the sauce over it
