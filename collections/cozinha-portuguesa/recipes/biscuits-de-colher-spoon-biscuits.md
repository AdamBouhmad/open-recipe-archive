## Ingredients

- Composition same as the preceding biscuits
- One additional egg white according to the quantity made

## Directions

1. Prepare the composition the same as the preceding biscuits, adding one additional egg white according to the quantity being made
2. Fill a small silver spoon with the mixture and place gracefully and lengthwise the small pieces on sheets of white paper
3. Sprinkle a little fine sugar on top and put them to bake in a very mild oven
4. When cooked, remove them, detach them quickly from the paper with a knife and serve them as fresh as possible
