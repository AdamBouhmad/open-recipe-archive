## Ingredients

- A piece of beef brisket weighing fifteen or twenty pounds
- salt
- whole sausage for larding
- water
- cabbage
- English potatoes
- turnips
- carrots
- other garnishes as preferred
- good melted butter

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef brisket weighing fifteen or twenty pounds and rub it well all over with salt
2. After the salt is absorbed, lard it with whole sausage
3. Place to cook in a large pot with water only
4. After boiling two hours, prick with a larding needle: if blood comes out, it is not cooked; but when it begins to exude substance, remove from the fire
5. Serve garnished with cabbage, English potatoes, turnips, carrots, or any other garnish as preferred, with good melted butter
6. It can also be eaten afterwards cold
