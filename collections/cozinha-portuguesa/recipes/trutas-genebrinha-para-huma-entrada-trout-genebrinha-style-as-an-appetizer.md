## Ingredients

- trout
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- white or red wine
- meat substance
- saffron
- onion with cloves
- bread crust
- fresh butter
- lemon or sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Scale the trout and remove the gills and innards without opening them
2. Fill the cavity with a piece of butter mixed with a little salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. Place in a casserole
4. Pour one or two bottles of white or red wine so the trout are covered
5. Season with salt, pepper, a bundle of saffron, fine herbs, and a onion studded with cloves, and a piece of bread crust
6. Let it boil over a strong flame until the fire catches it, when the flame begins to die down, add half a pound of fresh butter to the sauce, not all at once but bit by bit
7. Remove the trout
8. When the sauce is reduced, remove the onion and the bundle
9. Serve over the trout with lemon or sour orange juice
