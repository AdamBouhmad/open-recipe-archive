## Ingredients

- Half an alqueire or one quarter of flour
- Two or three arratels of butter
- Some pieces of salt
- Four egg yolks
- Water, as necessary

## Directions

1. Spread on a table half an alqueire or one quarter of flour with two or three arratels of butter, some pieces of salt, and four egg yolks
2. Knead everything very well, adding the necessary water little by little until it is well bound and consistent
3. Divide into two portions
4. Work each one very well
5. After being well worked, use for tarts, empadas, or whatever is necessary
