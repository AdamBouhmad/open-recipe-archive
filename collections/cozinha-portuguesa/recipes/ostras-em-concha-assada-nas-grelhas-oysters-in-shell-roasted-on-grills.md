## Ingredients

- oysters
- pepper
- sauce
- salt
- oil or melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Open the oysters and trim them around the edges
2. Season each one with pepper, minced sauce, oil or melted butter
3. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs
4. Roast on grills, turning with a hot pan
5. Serve with lime juice
