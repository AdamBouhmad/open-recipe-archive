## Ingredients

- two or three dozen egg yolks, well beaten
- sugar in espada point

## Directions

1. Put two or three dozen very well beaten egg yolks in a tall pot that must be over heat with sugar in espada point
2. As they begin to curdle, stir with a spoon so they do not separate
3. When already curdled, drain the sugar
4. Let it take color in the same pot
5. Turn onto a plate and color it on the other side, putting a little sugar that was drained into the pot
6. After coloring, arrange on the plate and serve at the table with cinnamon on top
