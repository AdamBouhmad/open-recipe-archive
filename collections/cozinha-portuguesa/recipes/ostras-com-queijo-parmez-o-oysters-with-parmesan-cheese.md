## Ingredients

- butter
- oysters
- pounded pepper
- minced sauce
- half a cup of white wine
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Garnish the bottom of a plate with washed butter
2. Arrange oysters on top sprinkled with pounded pepper and minced sauce
3. Add half a cup of white wine and cover with another piece of butter and grated Parmesan cheese
4. Bake in the oven and when golden, serve as an entrée like the preceding ones
