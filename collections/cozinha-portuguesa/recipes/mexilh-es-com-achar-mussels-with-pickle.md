## Ingredients

- strong vinegar
- cinnamon stick
- pepper
- vinegar
- mustard
- nutmeg
- salt
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Boil a little strong vinegar, and when boiling, add whole cinnamon and pepper, vinegar, mustard, and nutmeg, all poorly ground
2. After seasoning the vinegar with these seasonings, pass the mussels through it
3. After passing through, place them in layers in a jar or pot, and in each layer sprinkle some of the said seasonings
4. When the jar is full, pour the boiling vinegar on it, and if it is not enough to cover the mussels, add cold vinegar, and on top salt and olive oil to form a layer so they do not spoil
