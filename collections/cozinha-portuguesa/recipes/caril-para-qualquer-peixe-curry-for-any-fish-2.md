## Ingredients

- two onions, finely chopped
- one quarter of beef butter
- a few shrimp or clams
- milk
- one quarter of almonds
- seasonings
- half an arratel of rice
- water
- salt
- fish steaks

## Directions

1. Sauté the two finely chopped onions in a quarter of beef butter
2. Add a few shrimp or clams with milk and one quarter of almonds
3. Cook everything until it becomes somewhat thick
4. Season with seasonings
5. Cook half an arratel of rice in water and salt
6. Place the rice on the plate
7. Put some fish steaks cooked in water and salt on top
8. Pour the curry over the fish
