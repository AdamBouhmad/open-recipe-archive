## Ingredients

- rabbit, skinned and washed
- lard
- ham
- herbs
- onion
- black seasonings
- salt
- partridges, pigeons, or doves (optional)

## Directions

1. After skinning and washing the rabbit well, place it to braise in a pot with a portion of lard, another of ham, herbs, an onion cut in half, black seasonings, and salt (if desired, you may also add partridges, pigeons, or doves)
2. Once cooked, send to the table very sour
