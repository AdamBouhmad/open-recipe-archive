## Ingredients

- One arratel of bacon, cut into dice
- One half-roasted chicken
- A splash of white wine
- Little vinegar
- Water
- Seasonings
- Two bay leaves
- Two egg yolks
- Bread slices
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Fry one arratel of bacon cut into dice and pour the clean fat from the cracklings into a bowl with the fat from a half-roasted chicken, a splash of white wine, little vinegar, and enough water.
2. Put it to stew, season with all seasonings with two bay leaves, and thicken with two egg yolks.
3. Place slices of bread underneath and lemon on top.
4. In this manner one makes turkeys, pigeons, kid goats, and loins.
