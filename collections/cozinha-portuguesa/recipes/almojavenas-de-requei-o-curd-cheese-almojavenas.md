## Ingredients

- Curd cheese
- Six egg yolks
- Half arratel of grated bread of ló
- Half arratel of sugar
- One quarter of cow butter
- Flower water

## Directions

1. Put curd cheese in a pot with six egg yolks, half arratel of grated bread of ló, half arratel of sugar, one quarter of cow butter, and flower water.
2. Beat this mixture.
3. Pour it into a baking pan and bake it in the pan.
4. Shape into almojavenas, pass them through sugar with cinnamon on top, and send to the table.
