## Ingredients

- brains
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the brains into slices with salt and pepper on top
2. Pass through beaten egg and fry in good butter
3. When fried, make a tangy sauce of lemon and pepper
4. Place over the brains on a plate and serve
