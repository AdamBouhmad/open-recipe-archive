## Ingredients

- pigeons, cleaned
- ham
- choice meats, cut into dice-sized pieces
- broth from the pan
- one egg yolk
- golden toast

## Directions

1. Clean the pigeons and open them along the back
2. Stuff with ham and choice meats cut into dice-sized pieces
3. Close the pigeons again and place in a pan
4. Add one spoon of broth from the pan
5. Cook slowly over heat with a cover of coals on top
6. When cooked, brush the top with one egg yolk
7. Place on golden toast and serve to the table
