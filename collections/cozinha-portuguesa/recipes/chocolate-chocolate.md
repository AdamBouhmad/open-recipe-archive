## Ingredients

- five arrátels of cocoa
- three arrátels of stone sugar
- three ounces of fine cinnamon penned
- eight vanilla pods pounded and penned

## Directions

1. Toast five arrátels of cocoa
2. After toasted clean it and remove the husk
3. Pound very well
4. Mix with three arrátels of stone sugar and three ounces of fine cinnamon penned
5. As soon as all this is very well mixed, move it on a stone like one grinds paints
6. Grind second and third time
7. When it is in paste form add eight vanilla pods pounded and penned
8. Make the cakes in the form desired
