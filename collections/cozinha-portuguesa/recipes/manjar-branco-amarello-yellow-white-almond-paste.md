## Ingredients

- a chicken breast shredded and broken apart
- one and a half canadadas of milk
- two arratels of sugar
- one arratel of rice flour
- half a canadada of milk added little by little
- a dozen egg yolks
- flower water

## Directions

1. Once a chicken breast is more than half cooked, shred it and break it apart with a spoon in a pot.
2. Cook with one and a half canadadas of milk, two arratels of sugar, and one arratel of rice flour.
3. While cooking, add little by little half a canadada of milk.
4. When almost cooked, add soft eggs, a quantity of a dozen egg yolks.
5. After they are cooked, add flower water, remove from heat, and put on the plate.
