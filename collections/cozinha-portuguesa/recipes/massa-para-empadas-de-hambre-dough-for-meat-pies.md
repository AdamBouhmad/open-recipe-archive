## Ingredients

- half an alqueire or one quarter of wheat flour
- two or three arratels of butter
- some stones of salt
- four egg yolks
- necessary water

## Directions

1. Place on a table half an alqueire or one quarter of wheat flour with two or three arratels of butter
2. Add some stones of salt and four egg yolks
3. Mix everything very well
4. Add the necessary water little by little until it is well bound and consistent
5. Divide it into two equal parts
