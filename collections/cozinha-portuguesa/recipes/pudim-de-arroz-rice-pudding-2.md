## Ingredients

- rice cooked in milk
- butter
- ten or twelve egg yolks
- grated sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- grated lemon peel
- a drop of Malmsey wine
- beaten egg whites
- dough border

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk and make it dry, pound it in a stone mortar with butter, ten or twelve egg yolks, grated sugar, seasoned with salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, grated lemon peel, and a drop of Malmsey wine
2. Add to it afterwards the beaten egg whites
3. And putting everything on a plate with a border of dough around the edge, bake in the oven for the space of one hour
4. When browned, serve with powdered sugar on top
