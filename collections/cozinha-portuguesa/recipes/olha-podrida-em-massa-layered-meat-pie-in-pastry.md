## Ingredients

- Four arratels of beef brisket
- Four arratels of pork loin
- Two chickens
- Two partridges
- Four pigeons
- Half a dozen thrushes
- Two rabbits
- Half a dozen pork tongues
- Turnips
- Cabbage
- Garlic
- Grains
- All whole spices
- Chopped bacon
- One alqueire of flour
- Butter
- Eggs
- Two loaves of ten réis bread
- Beef breast slices

## Directions

1. Brown all the meats and vegetables in chopped bacon, each item separately
2. When all are browned and half-cooked in bacon, knead one alqueire of flour with butter and eggs, removing two eggs' worth of dough
3. Place one portion of dough with paper underneath on a baking sheet
4. Arrange two loaves of ten réis bread as a base, topped with sliced beef breast, then arrange the aforementioned meats and vegetables in good order on top
5. Cover with another layer of dough, extending it to cover everything, and make the rim very thick so it does not burst
6. Make an opening on top to pour in the liquid left from browning the meats
7. Make two dough handles as on a panela and a worked top crust
8. Place in an oven to roast slowly
9. When serving, drain it and remove the sauce
