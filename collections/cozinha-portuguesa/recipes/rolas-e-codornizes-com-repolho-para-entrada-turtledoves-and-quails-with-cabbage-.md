## Ingredients

- One cabbage
- Lean bacon
- One dozen quails
- Cooking stock
- Braising liquid
- Capelet
- One onion studded with cloves
- Ham essence

## Directions

1. Take one cabbage and blanch it in hot water, squeeze it well
2. Cook it in a well-tinned pan with a piece of lean bacon also rolled, one dozen well-cleaned quails larded in melted bacon
3. Add some cooking stock and a little of the braising liquid, one capelet, and one onion studded with cloves
4. When everything is cooked, skim the fat
5. Arrange in its dish and serve with good quality ham essence on top
