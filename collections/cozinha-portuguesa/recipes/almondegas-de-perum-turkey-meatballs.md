## Ingredients

- one turkey
- half an arratel of bacon
- aromatics
- all seasonings
- grated bread crumb
- vinegar
- broth from the pan
- three egg yolks
- cinnamon
- lemon

## Directions

1. Mince one turkey with half an arratel of bacon and aromatics
2. Add all seasonings, grated bread crumb, and vinegar
3. Let simmer on low heat
4. Heat a bowl with broth from the pan
5. Add the meatballs to the broth as they are formed
6. At the end, thicken with three egg yolks
7. Place on slices with cinnamon and lemon on top
