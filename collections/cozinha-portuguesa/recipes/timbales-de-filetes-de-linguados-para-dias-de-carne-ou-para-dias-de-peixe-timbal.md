## Ingredients

- Sole fillets
- Bacon strips or buttered paper sheets
- Eggs, beaten
- Meat or fish forcemeat
- Truffles
- Sole fillets
- Prawn tails
- Sauce á l'Espagnole or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Line the bottom and sides of a timbale or casserole with bacon strips if for a meat day, or with buttered paper sheets if for a fish day
2. Arrange the fillets inside around the edge, neatly aligned and stuck together with beaten eggs
3. Cover the bottom of the center and all around with forcemeat of meat or fish according to the day
4. Place in the middle a ragout of truffles, sole fillets, and prawn tails
5. Finish covering with the same forcemeat
6. Top with bacon strips or buttered paper sheets
7. Bake in the oven
8. When cooked, remove the bacon or paper, carefully turn it onto its plate, remove the bacon or paper, drain all the fat
9. Serve with Spanish sauce or lemon juice
