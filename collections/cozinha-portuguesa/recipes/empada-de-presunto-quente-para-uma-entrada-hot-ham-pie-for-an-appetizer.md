## Ingredients

- presunto (ham)
- massa grossa (thick dough)
- toucinho picado (chopped bacon)
- pimenta (pepper)
- salsa (parsley)
- especias (spices)
- hervas finas (fine herbs)
- pranchas de toucinho (bacon slices)
- molho à Hespanhola or molho picante (Spanish sauce or spicy sauce)

## Directions

1. Take a good ham, remove all the skin and bones from the middle, soak it for two days
2. Then make a thick dough and make the pie box
3. Put plenty of chopped and pounded bacon with a little pepper, parsley, some spices, and fine herbs in the bottom
4. Place the ham on top, cover it as below, and with some slices of bacon
5. After covering it completely with the dough, put it to bake in the oven for seven or eight hours
6. When cooked, open it, drain the fat, and serve it with Spanish sauce or spicy sauce
