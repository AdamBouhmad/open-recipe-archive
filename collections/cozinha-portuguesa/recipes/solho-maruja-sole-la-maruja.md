## Ingredients

- A portion of sole
- Butter
- Shallot
- Chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Red wine
- Broth or stock
- Bay leaf
- Bread crumb
- Oil for frying

## Directions

1. Cut a portion of sole into thin strips
2. Arrange them at the bottom of a casserole first buttered
3. Add on top shallot, chives, minced shallot, little salt, and pepper
4. When the strips are browned on one side, turn them on the other
5. When cooked, remove them
6. In the butter add a little flour, a drop of red wine, a drop of broth or stock, a bay leaf, and let it simmer little by little for a quarter hour
7. When reduced and of good taste, warm the strips of sole in it
8. Serve without boiling, garnished with pieces of fried bread crumb and passed through the same sauce
