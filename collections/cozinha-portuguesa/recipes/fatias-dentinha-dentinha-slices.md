## Ingredients

- ham
- anchovy fillets
- bread crumb
- rendered bacon fat
- good oil
- coarse pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Use the bread crumb of one whole bread cut afterwards into ordinary slices
2. Fry little by little in rendered bacon fat
3. When fried and well-colored, serve with good oil, coarse pepper, and lemon juice
