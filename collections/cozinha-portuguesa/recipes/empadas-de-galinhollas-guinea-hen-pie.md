## Ingredients

- Guinea hens
- Bacon, medium thickness
- Guinea hen tripe
- Guinea hen livers
- Scraped bacon
- Truffles
- Fine herbs
- Pastry box with lid
- Fresh butter
- Bacon planks
- Eggs
- Orange juice, sour

## Directions

1. Pluck, prepare, and singe the guinea hens
2. Lard them with medium bacon
3. Make a ground mixture from the guinea hens' own tripe, livers, scraped bacon, truffles, and fine herbs
4. Stuff the guinea hens with this mixture
5. Garnish the bottom of the pastry box with chopped bacon and a little salsa
6. Place the guinea hens inside, season them
7. Cover with fresh butter and bacon planks
8. Cover the box with its lid
9. Seal with eggs, ornament, and gild it
10. Place in the oven to bake for four hours
11. When cooked, open it, drain the fat
12. Serve with sour orange juice
