## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- forcemeat of veal or chicken
- bacon slice
- breadcrumbs
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue until nearly done as above
2. Remove the skin and let it cool
3. Cut into thin, equal slices lengthwise
4. Cover each slice with raw forcemeat of veal or chicken about the size of a new crown coin in silver
5. Roll each slice over itself and cover with a bacon slice
6. Spit them on a small spit covered with paper
7. When nearly roasted, remove the paper
8. Cover with breadcrumbs and when colored, serve with spicy sauce or as preferred
