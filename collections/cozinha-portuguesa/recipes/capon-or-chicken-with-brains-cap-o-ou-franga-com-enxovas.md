## Ingredients

- capon or chicken
- scraped bacon
- two brains
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- melted bacon
- reed skewers
- paper
- bacon broth
- orange juice or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the capon or chicken as above, carefully peel back the skin from the top of the breast, and stuff it with a mixture made from scraped bacon, two brains, parsley, chives, little salt, and pepper
2. Pass the skin flap through the wing tips, straighten it, tie it with twine, lard it in melted bacon, pass some reed skewers through it, tie them to the spit, cover with paper sheets, and roast it
3. When roasted, serve it with its broth, chopped brains, orange juice or lemon juice
