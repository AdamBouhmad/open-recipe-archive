## Ingredients

- six thrushes
- one half arratel of bacon chopped
- all seasonings
- lemon juice
- one eighth of flour
- one half arratel of butter
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Sauté six thrushes in one half arratel of chopped bacon; when half-cooked, season with all the seasonings, and let cool
2. Knead one eighth of flour with one half arratel of butter and four eggs, make a box in which place the thrushes with the broth from the sautéing, seasoning well with lemon
3. When baked, serve to the table
