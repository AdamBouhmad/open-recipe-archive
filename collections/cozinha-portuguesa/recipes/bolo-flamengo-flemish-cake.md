## Ingredients

- one ounce of flour
- two fresh eggs
- half a pound of butter
- some grains of salt
- milk as needed
- a little bit of yeast

## Directions

1. Take one ounce of flour, two fresh eggs, half a pound of butter, some grains of salt, and mix everything with as much milk as necessary, adding a little bit of yeast
2. When the dough is bound and kneaded, place it on a sheet of paper, cover with a warm towel, and let it ferment by the fire for five quarters of an hour
3. Then form the cake as usual, place on buttered paper
4. Put in the oven to bake
5. When baked, eat it when desired
