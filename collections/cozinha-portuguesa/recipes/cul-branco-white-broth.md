## Ingredients

- Sheets of veal
- Slices of ham
- Onions and roots
- Stock
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- One onion studded with cloves
- Chicken breasts
- Capon breasts, or partridge breasts
- Hard egg yolks
- Bread soaked in milk

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a casserole with sheets of veal, slices of ham, onions, and roots as for ordinary broth
2. After it has sweated and before it sticks, moisten with stock and season with one bundle of parsley and fine herbs and one onion studded with cloves
3. Let it simmer little by little
4. When the meat is cooked and of good taste, pound chicken breasts, capon or partridge breasts, hard egg yolks, and bread soaked in milk
5. Dissolve everything in the stock, pass through a sieve
6. When warm but not boiling, use for white soups, sauces, and ragus
