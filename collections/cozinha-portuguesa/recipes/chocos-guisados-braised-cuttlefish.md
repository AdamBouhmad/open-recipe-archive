## Ingredients

- cuttlefish
- water
- flour
- butter
- onion
- two anchovies
- red wine
- meat substance
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the cuttlefish, cut into equal fillets
2. Blanch in boiling water
3. Brown flour in melted butter
4. Add finely chopped onion and two finely chopped anchovies, breaking them down with a spoon
5. Pour in one cup of red wine and one spoon of meat substance
6. Season with salt, pepper, and a bundle of fine herbs
7. Add the fillets and let them cook very well
8. When the cuttlefish is cooked and the sauce is reduced and of good taste, remove the herb bundle
9. Serve hot with lemon juice
