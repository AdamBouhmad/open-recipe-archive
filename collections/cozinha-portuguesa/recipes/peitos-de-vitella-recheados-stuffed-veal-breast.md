## Ingredients

- veal breast
- ground veal breast
- ham
- veal sweetbreads
- bread cooked in milk
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Parboil the veal breasts, open them with a knife between the skin and ribs, soak them for some time, and clean them very well
2. Stuff with a ground mixture in cream, made of breasts of turkey or chicken, half-cooked bacon, veal udders, bread cooked in milk, and egg yolks, all well seasoned with salt, pepper, and parsley
