## Ingredients

- citrons
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the citrons of their skin so the preserve is whiter
2. Wash in two or three cold waters
3. Scald in two or three boiling waters, squeezing in a thick cloth each time
4. Once sweetened, place in three cold waters, always squeezing with the cloth
5. Grate or pound the citrons
6. Add one arratel of citron to two of sugar at hair point and proceed with the quantity desired
7. Stir while it does not dry
8. Once the bottom shows and it is done, remove from the fire and add fragrance if desired
9. Place in small paper cones
