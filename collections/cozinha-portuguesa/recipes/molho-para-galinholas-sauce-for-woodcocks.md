## Ingredients

- two or three woodcocks
- red wine
- a little bit of restored sauce
- salt
- pepper
- one bay leaf
- quarters of woodcocks with fillets
- juice of sour oranges
- breadcrumb interior, fried

## Directions

1. Having roasted two or three woodcocks, remove their breasts and thighs; set aside the livers, and pound the bones, entrails, and livers, after pounding very well, dissolve in red wine and a little bit of restored sauce, and let it simmer seasoned with salt, pepper, and one bay leaf; when reduced, pass it through a sieve, put inside the quarters of woodcocks with fillets and juice of sour oranges; when hot, but not boiling, serve it for ducks, ducklings, snipes, curlews, teals, and other birds, garnished with pieces of fried breadcrumb interior.
