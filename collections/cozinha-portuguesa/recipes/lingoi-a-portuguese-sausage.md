## Ingredients

- pork meat from the legs and loins (or all lean pork meat)
- some bacon
- pepper
- cloves
- white wine
- garlic
- salt
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Put pork meat from the legs and loins, cut very finely, into a glazed bowl with some bacon, pepper, and cloves on top
2. Keep in a preserve of white wine, garlic, salt, and a little vinegar for two or three days, in quantity equal to the meat
3. When ready to make the sausages, place a clean board across the bowl and put the meat on it to drain the brine
4. Make the sausages
5. Hang them to dry in the chimney
