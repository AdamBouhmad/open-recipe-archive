## Ingredients

- One piece of beef
- Old bacon, cut thick
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Fine herbs
- Two cloves of garlic
- Half a quartilho of wine
- Bacon slices

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef and lard it with thick old bacon, seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, two cloves of garlic, and half a quartilho of wine
2. Let it marinate seasoned and prepared in this way for three or four hours
3. Place everything in a pot with slices of bacon underneath and on top
