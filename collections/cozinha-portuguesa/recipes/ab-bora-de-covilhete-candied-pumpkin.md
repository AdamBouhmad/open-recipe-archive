## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- sugar equal in weight to the cooked pumpkin
- musk

## Directions

1. Once the pumpkin is split, weigh it raw on a scale, and take as many arratels of sugar as there are of pumpkin
2. Place the raw pumpkin in a pot to cook
3. Once cooked, grind in a stone mortar and pass through a sieve so the water drains, then place in cloth and wring well so no water remains
4. Place the arratels of sugar in a pot over fire, and once the sugar is clean, add the pumpkin, cooking it in the sugar
5. When it no longer falls from the spoon when it is turned with it, it is done
6. Add the musk desired, place in covilhetes, and dry in the sun
