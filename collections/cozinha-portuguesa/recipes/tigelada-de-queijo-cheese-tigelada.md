## Ingredients

- Cow butter
- Flemish cheese
- Breadcrumbs
- Eggs
- Clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Place a small amount of melted cow butter at the bottom of a frying pan, and a layer of sliced Flemish cheese (if salted, soak it in water first).
2. Over the cheese place another layer of breadcrumbs with small pieces of cheese and butter, and a spoonful of beaten eggs on top.
3. Fill the frying pan with these layers.
4. Using a knife, make openings down the middle and along the sides.
5. Pour clarified sugar over it and send to the oven.
