## Ingredients

- wings, necks, and other poultry innards (except beaks and feet)
- hot water
- salt
- bacon
- whole onions
- herbs
- pig's lard or butter
- flour
- vinegar
- saffron
- hard egg yolks
- roasted or fried poultry livers

## Directions

1. Pluck the wings, necks, and other poultry innards in hot water, making a cut through the middle of each neck and each head so the brains can cook
2. Cook everything together in water and salt with bacon, whole onions, and herbs
3. When cooked, remove the bacon, onions, and herbs
4. Chop while hot and return to the pan, seasoning with spices, and let cook further
5. In a separate pan, heat some pig's lard or butter until very hot
6. Add flour so that it forms a dough in the pan, making white bubbles that remain very white
7. Pour this with the same pan into the main pan with vinegar and saffron
8. When the pepitoria is ready, pour into a dish to be covered with hard egg yolks and garnished with roasted or fried poultry livers
