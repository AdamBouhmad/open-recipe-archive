## Ingredients

- veal head
- string
- broth
- lard, diced
- whole pepper
- ginger
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the veal head at the neck joint
2. Peel it as one would peel a suckling pig
3. While it cooks, place it in cold water
4. Tie the snout tightly with string so that the tip of the tongue sticks out
5. Place it to cook in a new pot with lard diced, whole pepper, and some ginger
6. Once cooked, remove the lower jaw
7. When you wish to put it on the plate, open the upper jaw with a knife along the brains so that vinegar and ground pepper can enter inside
8. Also sprinkle vinegar and ground pepper on top of the whole head, putting vinegar first so the pepper sticks
9. Send to the table with fresh parsley on top
