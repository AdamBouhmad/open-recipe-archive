## Ingredients

- bread crumb
- Madeira white wine or other white wine
- eggs
- Rhine wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the bread crumb into round or square pieces two or three fingers thick
2. Soak briefly in white wine from Madeira or any other white wine
3. After draining, pass through beaten eggs
4. Fry and repeat the same two or three times as described above
5. After frying and achieving good color, serve with Rhine wine and sugar sauce at point
