## Ingredients

- rice, cooked in milk and dried
- butter
- ten or twelve egg yolks
- grated sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- grated lemon peel
- one drop of brandy
- egg whites, beaten
- dough border

## Directions

1. Cook rice in milk and dry it
2. Pound in a stone mortar with butter, ten or twelve egg yolks, grated sugar, seasoned with salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, grated lemon peel, and one drop of brandy
3. Add the beaten egg whites
4. Place everything in a dish with a dough border around it and bake in the oven for about one hour
5. When golden, serve with powdered sugar on top
6. Note: puddings of vermicelli or any other pasta are seasoned and served the same way
