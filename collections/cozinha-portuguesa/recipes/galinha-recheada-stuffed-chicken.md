## Ingredients

- one and a half arratels of mutton
- one quarter of pork
- aromatics
- three eggs
- all seasonings
- vinegar
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Chop in one part one and a half arratels of mutton, and in another part one quarter of pork with aromatics, all very well chopped
2. Place the pork and aromatics to braise with half of the mutton, and when half braised beat it on the block mixing it with the other half of the mutton
3. Chop everything together with three eggs, all seasonings, vinegar, and grated bread
4. Once this is done, stuff the chicken, and place it to roast in a frying pan, or to cook if you wish
5. Place it upon gilded broth with lemon juice on top, and send it to the table
