## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- two eggs
- a little Madeira wine
- clarified butter
- fine sugar
- grated lemon peel
- salt
- cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Mix flour in milk
2. Add two eggs, a little Madeira wine, clarified butter, fine sugar, grated lemon peel, salt, and ground cinnamon
3. After mixing everything, shape like an apple and heat an iron mold in hot oil or butter where it will be fried
4. Dip the mold in the dough and immediately place in the fryer
5. When it releases, dip back in the dough and continue this way until finished
6. After frying and glazing with sugar and a hot spatula, serve as the previous ones
