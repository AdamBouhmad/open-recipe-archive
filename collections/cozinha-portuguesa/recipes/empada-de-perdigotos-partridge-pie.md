## Ingredients

- Pastry dough
- Ground meat (picado)
- Young partridges
- Partridge livers
- Bacon, scraped
- Fine herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh truffles, chopped
- Salsa
- Bay leaf
- Marjoram
- Bacon planks

## Directions

1. Make the pastry box of tender dough
2. Garnish the bottom with a little ground meat
3. Place the stuffed partridges on top, stuffed with a mixture made from their own livers, scraped bacon, fine herbs, salt, pepper, fresh chopped truffles, a piece of salsa, bay leaf, and marjoram
4. Cover with scraped bacon and bacon planks
5. Seal with the pastry lid
6. Ornament and gild it
7. Place in the oven to bake for two hours
8. When cooked, open it, remove the planks, onions, salsa, and all fat
9. Serve with perdiz sauce or Spanish sauce
