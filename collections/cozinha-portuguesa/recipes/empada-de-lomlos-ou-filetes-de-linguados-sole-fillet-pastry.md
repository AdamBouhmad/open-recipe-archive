## Ingredients

- Minced meat
- Sole fillets, fried
- Mustard
- Shallot
- Truffles
- Cucumbers, minced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Onion, clove-studded
- Butter
- Dough
- Eggs
- Truffle ragú
- Shrimp tails

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a pastry box with minced meat
2. Place fried sole fillets on top with mustard, shallot, minced truffles and cucumbers, seasoned with salt and pepper, a bouquet of fine herbs, and a clove-studded onion
3. Cover everything with another little bit of minced meat, a piece of fresh butter, and close the pastry with its dough lid
4. Finish as is customary and put in the oven to cook
5. When cooked, open it, remove the bouquet and onion, drain the fat, and add a well-finished truffle ragú and shrimp tails of good flavor
6. Serve hot
