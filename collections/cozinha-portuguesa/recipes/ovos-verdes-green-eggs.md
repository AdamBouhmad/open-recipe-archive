## Ingredients

- large handful of spinach leaves
- water
- fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Wash and grind a large handful of spinach leaves
2. Dissolve in half spoonful of water
3. Squeeze and pass through a strainer
4. When the water is green, bring it to a boil
5. Poach fresh eggs in it
6. When cooked and trimmed, serve with aspic sauce or clear essence sauce
