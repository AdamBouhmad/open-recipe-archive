## Ingredients

- Two portions of hake without skin or bones
- Two onions
- Parsley
- Two eggs
- Seasonings
- Three more raw eggs
- Five réis of grated bread
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Chop two portions of hake without skin or bones
2. Fry two chopped onions with parsley, and add two beaten eggs with seasonings in the same frying pan, stirring until the eggs curdle
3. Mix everything with the hake that is chopped on the cutting board, and chop it again with three more raw eggs and five réis of grated bread
4. Season with all the seasonings, salt, vinegar, and lemon
5. This mixture can be used to stuff any fish desired, or to fill eggs, eggplants, onions, chicories, lettuces, cabbages, turnips, and artichokes
6. It can also be made into patties, meatballs, rolls, and everything else desired, adding or diminishing according to the quantity
