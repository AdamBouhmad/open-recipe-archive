## Ingredients

- rabbit
- twine
- broth
- wine
- sage bundle

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the rabbits
2. Remove only the bones around the loins from underneath
3. Remove the skin in such a way that it passes through the opening along half of the front quarters of the rabbits
4. Turn it and shape it like a turtle, securing it with twine
5. Place to cook in a braise as above
6. After cooking, serve with a well-flavored sauce of your choice
