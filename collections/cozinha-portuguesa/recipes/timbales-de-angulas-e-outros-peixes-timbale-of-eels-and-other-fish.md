## Ingredients

- Eels (angulas)
- Butter
- Flour
- Substance (broth)
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion studded with cloves
- Bundle of fine herbs and sauce
- Prawn tails and coulis
- Tender pastry or puff pastry
- Fish forcemeat
- Fish
- Pastry lid
- Eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Clean the eels and cut them into pieces
2. Sauté them in butter and browned flour
3. After sautéing, add some broth and white wine
4. Season with salt, pepper, studded onion, and a bundle of fine herbs and sauce
5. Let them cook; when cooked, add prawn tails and coulis
6. Meanwhile, line a casserole on the inside with tender or puff pastry
7. Place a fish forcemeat on top
8. Put the eels with all their sauce inside, but cold
9. Cover with the same forcemeat
10. Seal with a pastry lid, sealed around the edge with beaten eggs
11. Place in the oven to bake
12. When cooked, open it and add a little piquant coulis inside
13. Serve hot as an entrance course
