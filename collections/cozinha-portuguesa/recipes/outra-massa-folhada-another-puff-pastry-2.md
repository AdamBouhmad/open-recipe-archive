## Ingredients

- two or three arratels of beef fat
- rolled dough

## Directions

1. Remove very well all the skins from a piece of beef fat weighing two or three arratels
2. After being very well chopped, pound it until it becomes very soft and smooth like butter
3. Spread it over the rolled dough and stretch it like the preceding one four or five times
4. It will serve for tarts, small pastries, and for everything that is meat and eaten hot
