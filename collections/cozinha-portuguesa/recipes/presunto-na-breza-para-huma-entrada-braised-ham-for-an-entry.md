## Ingredients

- ham
- veal or calf meat slices
- cloves
- whole pepper
- salt
- fine herbs
- garlic head
- broth
- white wine
- vegetable ragout or spinach ragout with cream
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Soak the ham for three days until desalted, remove its skin, tie it with string and place it to cook in a braise with slices of veal or calf meat, seasoned with cloves, whole pepper, a type of sauce, fine herbs, and a head of garlic, place it thus to sweat two hours on low heat; then pour broth and white wine to it, and let it simmer slowly for five or six hours
2. When cooked, remove it and serve with a well-finished vegetable ragout or spinach ragout with cream for an entry
3. If you wish to serve this same ham for cold cuts, let it cool in its own braise, and covered with grated bread browned, serve it on a napkin garnished with sauce, slices of bread, or whatever else seems appropriate
