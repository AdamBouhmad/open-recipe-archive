## Ingredients

- one roasted chicken
- its liver
- half arratel of pork
- two onions
- mustard
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast one chicken and its liver
2. After the liver is chopped, put it in a small mustard already prepared
3. Once half arratel of pork is melted and cleaned of cracklings, fry two chopped onions in it
4. In the same pan add the mustard mixed with the liver so that everything together becomes fried again
5. Season with pepper, clove, nutmeg, cardamom, so that it becomes well spicy
6. If you wish to add aromatics, do not add mustard
7. Once this pottage is made, place the roasted chicken in pieces on the plate with this pottage and lemon juice on top
