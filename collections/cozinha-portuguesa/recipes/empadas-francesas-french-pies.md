## Ingredients

- two arratels of bacon chopped
- one head of garlic
- one chicken whole
- two pigeons whole
- four arratels of loin in slices
- all seasonings
- two partridges whole
- one rabbit in pieces
- one quarter of flour
- water
- salt
- one arratel of cow butter

## Directions

1. Melt two arratels of chopped bacon with one head of garlic, soak in it one whole chicken and two whole pigeons, all whole, and four arratels of loin in slices; season with all the seasonings, and place in a frying pan to cool
2. Sauté in that same bacon two whole partridges and one rabbit in pieces, and let cool
3. Knead one quarter of flour in water and salt, and knead very well, make French puff pastry with one arratel of cow butter, divide the dough into two crusts
4. On one place all the meat in layers with plenty of pepper and lemon, cover with the other, decorating the pie around as desired, bake it, and when half-baked, opening a hole on top, pour through it the broth that remained from the sautéing
5. Finish baking and serve to the table
