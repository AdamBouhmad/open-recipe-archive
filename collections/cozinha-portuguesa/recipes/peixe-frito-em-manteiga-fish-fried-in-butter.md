## Ingredients

- Small piece of butter
- Fish
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Parsley
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Take a small piece of butter in a bowl and melt it in gentle heat
2. Once melted, remove the scum, and the salt will remain at the bottom
3. Pour the butter into a frying pan where the fish will be fried
4. After the fish is fried, make a sauce by taking a new bowl and drawing off the butter with a spoon so that it does not break
5. Add two or three egg yolks according to the quantity of fish, lemon juice, and pour everything over the fish
6. Serve with sprigs of parsley and lemon slices on top
