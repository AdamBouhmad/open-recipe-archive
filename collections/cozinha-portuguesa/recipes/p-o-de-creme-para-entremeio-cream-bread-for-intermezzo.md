## Ingredients

- one round loaf of thirty reis bread
- milk
- sugar
- franchipane cream
- lime peel

## Directions

1. Remove the outer crust of a round loaf, open it from the bottom, take out all the crumb, and dip it in milk and sugar for a few minutes
2. Then place it on a sieve and let it drain
3. Make a well-bound franchipane cream, fill the bread with it, put underneath the piece of crust that was removed
4. Arrange it on the plate where it is to be served with a little of the cream underneath and on top, its slices of lime peel in symmetry
5. Dust with fine grated sugar, put in the oven to bake
6. When baked, glazed, and well coloured, serve hot
