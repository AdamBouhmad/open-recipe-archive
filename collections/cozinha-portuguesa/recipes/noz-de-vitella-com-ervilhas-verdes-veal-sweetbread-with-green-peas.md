## Ingredients

- one or two veal sweetbreads
- green peas
- bacon or melted butter
- parsley
- chives
- cloves
- stock
- cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take one or two veal sweetbreads very well larded as the first ones, and place them to cook, finishing them well as the fricandeau ones
2. Take some green peas and pass them through the fire with bacon or melted butter, a sprig of parsley, chives, and cloves
3. After giving it four turns, moisten them with stock, and once cooked, finish binding with a little cullis
4. Then pour a little stock into the casserole of the glaze to scrape off what remained stuck to the bottom, pass it through a sieve, and pour it into the pea ragout
5. Once cooked and of good taste, serve by placing the veal sweetbread on top, and you may also give it a ragout of spinach à la crème, noting that all these fricandeau entries are very rich and should be made with all possible care
