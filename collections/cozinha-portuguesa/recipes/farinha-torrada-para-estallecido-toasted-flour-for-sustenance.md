## Ingredients

- one arratel of sifted flour
- two and a half arratels of sugar
- ten reis of powdered sandalwood
- ten reis of flower water

## Directions

1. Mix one arratel of sifted flour, two and a half arratels of sugar, ten reis of powdered sandalwood, and ten reis of flower water
2. Put everything mixed in a new pan, sealed with dough, sent to the oven overnight
3. Then take a small amount and soak it in water as necessary for a bowl of broth, and in it place an egg yolk, it is very good, taken in the morning
