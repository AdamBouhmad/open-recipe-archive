## Ingredients

- three or four chickens
- butter or melted bacon fat
- a slice of ham
- a bundle of herbs
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot water
- broth
- egg yolks
- pickled parsley
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Scald and pluck three or four chickens in hot water
2. Cut them into pieces and soak them for some time
3. Blanch them in hot water, pare them, and pass them through a casserole with butter or melted bacon fat, a slice of ham, a bundle of herbs, chopped onion, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Once browned, dust with flour, add a spoonful of hot water and another of broth, and let them simmer slowly
5. When cooked, remove the fat, bundle of herbs, and onion
6. Thicken with egg yolks mixed in broth, pickled parsley, and lime juice, and serve hot
