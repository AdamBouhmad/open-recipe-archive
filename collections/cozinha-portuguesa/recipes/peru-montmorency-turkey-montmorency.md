## Ingredients

- one young turkey
- bacon slices
- paper leaves
- bacon finely minced
- forcemeat
- ragú of small birds or calf sweetbreads
- truffles
- mushrooms
- twine
- paper leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean a young turkey, open it along the back
2. Remove the carcass up to the croup and the large breast bones
3. Tuck the legs inside and fill with a slice of bacon and some paper leaves
4. Sew it back along the back as if it were whole, tie it, and put it to blanch in lard
5. After blanching, lard it with finely minced bacon
6. Remove the paper and the bacon inside, and stuff it with good forcemeat and a ragú of small birds or calf sweetbreads, truffles, and mushrooms in the middle
7. Sew it closed with twine, pass it with skewers, and put it to roast on the spit covered with paper leaves
8. When roasted and of good color, serve with clear essence of good taste and lemon juice
