## Ingredients

- peas
- butter
- lettuce hearts
- parsley bundle
- small onion
- cloves
- salt
- sugar
- egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. Put the peas in a casserole with butter, lettuce hearts, a bundle of parsley, small onion, two cloves of India, and over gentle heat, leave to sweat and cook little by little
2. When cooked, season with salt, a piece of sugar, and bind with egg yolks mixed in milk, and serve like the others for an entre-mets
