## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads
- ham
- truffles
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- five or six chickens
- string
- white wine
- cullis

## Directions

1. Make a hash of blanched veal sweetbreads, ham, truffles, all mixed with scraped bacon, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
2. Remove all the bones from five or six chickens, leaving only one foot attached
3. Stuff with the hash, shape them like pears, using the chicken foot as the pear stem
4. Tie with string, wrap each one in a bacon slice, and place them to cook in a braise nourished with some pieces of veal and moistened with a glass of white wine
5. When cooked, remove them, place them to drain, and when dry and properly arranged, place a spoonful of cullis in the bottom of the same braise
6. Strain through a sieve, remove the fat, and serve the chickens hot with this sauce and lime juice
