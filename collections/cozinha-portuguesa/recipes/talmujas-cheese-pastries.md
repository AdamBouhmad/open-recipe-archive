## Ingredients

- Piece of Alenquejo cheese, well fatty
- A little washed butter
- A pinch of pepper
- Handful of fine flour
- Two or three eggs
- A little milk
- Fine dough

## Directions

1. Grind in a stone mortar a piece of fatty Alenquejo cheese, a little washed butter, a pinch of pepper, a handful of fine flour, two or three eggs, and a little milk, but in such a way that it is not too thin
2. Stretch afterwards a dough cake, cut it into square pieces the width of a hand
3. Put a piece of this mixture in the middle, lifting the corners of the dough upwards
4. Brush with beaten eggs
5. Put to cook in the oven
6. When browned, serve for a side dish
