## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one dozen sheep feet
- water
- white wine
- half arratel of sugar
- ten egg whites
- lemon juice from two lemons

## Directions

1. Cook a chicken and a dozen sheep feet in enough water so that when well cooked they release the bones
2. Add half a quart of white wine
3. When the broth reaches a quantity of one and a half quarts, remove the feet and chicken
4. Shred them in a cloth and press very well into a pan
5. Add half arratel of sugar and ten beaten egg whites with the juice of two lemons
6. Bring to a boil
7. When boiled, strain through cloth three or four times until the jelly comes out clear (if it is cold, strain between two braziers)
8. Pour into dishes and when set, send to the table
