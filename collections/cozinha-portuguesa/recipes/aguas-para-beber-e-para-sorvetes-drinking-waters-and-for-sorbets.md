## Ingredients

- water
- ripe fruit (such as cherries, strawberries, blackberries, currants, or other)
- sugar

## Directions

1. In each three quarts of water, pour one pound of ripe fruit such as cherries, strawberries, blackberries, currants, or any other.
2. Crush with a spoon and mix very well in the water.
3. Strain through a cloth and sweeten with sugar.
4. Strain again through a cloth filter.
5. Put in a cool place and serve this water when occasion arises.
6. If it is for sorbets, add more sugar, put it in an ice cream maker of Flanders sheets, surround it with snow and salt, and as it begins to freeze, scrape off what sticks to the sides with a spoon.
7. Cover the ice cream maker with its lid and stir constantly with it until it is equally frozen, then fill cups with it at the moment you wish to drink it, and serve it without delay.
