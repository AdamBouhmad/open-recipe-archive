## Ingredients

- Partridges, plucked and eviscerated with legs tucked inside the body
- Thick bacon for larding, tempered with salt, pepper, fine herbs and fine spices
- Bacon slices
- Veal slices
- Some root vegetables
- Onion rings
- Whole salt
- Whole pepper
- Garlic cloves
- Bay leaves
- Whole cloves
- Vegetable ragú and root vegetables
- Or lentil coulis
- Or cabbage
- Or chestnut ragú
- Or olive ragú
- Or truffle ragú
- Or thistle shoot ragú

## Directions

1. After the partridges are plucked and eviscerated with legs tucked inside the body and trussed, lard them with thick bacon tempered with salt, pepper, fine herbs and fine spices
2. Line the bottom of a pot with bacon slices and veal slices, some root vegetables and onion rings
3. Place the partridges inside, well arranged with breasts facing down, and season with whole salt, whole pepper, some garlic cloves, bay leaves and whole cloves
4. Cover with veal slices and bacon slices
5. Cover the pot with its lid and cook slowly, placing coals above and below
6. When cooked, remove them and serve with the vegetable and root ragú that suits best, or with lentil coulis, or with cabbage, or with chestnut ragú, olive ragú, truffle ragú or thistle shoot ragú
7. They can also be served in hot and cold pies and cooked in the pot they can be used to garnish soups
