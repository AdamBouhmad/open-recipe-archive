## Ingredients

- a dozen small round breads the size of an apple
- milk
- sugar
- cream
- egg yolks
- sugar
- lime peel
- candied orange peel
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Remove the outer crust from a dozen small round breads the size of an apple, open them from the bottom, take out all the crumb, dip for a few moments in milk and sugar
2. Make a filling of cream, egg yolks, sugar, lime peel, and well-chopped candied orange peel
3. Fill the rolls with this, place in each one the piece of crust that was removed, tie them in a cross with a string
4. Fry them in clarified butter, and when fried, remove the string, dust with fine sugar, pass a hot spatula over them
5. After glazed and well coloured, serve hot as an intermezzo dish
