## Ingredients

- Cardos (cardoons)
- Caldo branco (white stock)
- Bexamela (béchamel sauce)
- Gemas de ovos (egg yolks)
- Noz-moscada (nutmeg)
- Vinagre branco (white vinegar)

## Directions

1. Cook the cardoons in white stock like the previous recipes
2. Drain them
3. Place them in béchamel sauce of good taste
4. Give them four simmers in it
5. Thicken with egg yolks
6. Add nutmeg and a splash of white vinegar
7. Serve for side dishes
