## Ingredients

- chickens or capons
- bacon strips
- buttered paper
- melted bacon
- veal sweetbreads
- fat livers cut in dice
- flour
- substance broth
- white wine
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley and fine herbs
- two or three dozen pitted olives
- water
- breadcrumbs
- orange juice or lemon juice

## Directions

1. After preparing the chickens or capons as mentioned, cover them with bacon strips and buttered paper, and roast them
2. Pass them through melted bacon in a pan with veal sweetbreads, fat livers cut in dice, dusted with a little flour, moisten with a spoonful of broth, a cup of white wine, a small spoon of olive oil, salt, pepper, and a small amount of parsley and fine herbs, and let it boil slowly
3. When cooked and of good taste, remove the fat and all the oil, add two or three dozen pitted olives boiled in a little water
4. When roasted, remove the paper and bacon, cover with fine breadcrumbs, brown it well, remove the twine, give it some strikes with the flat of the knife on the breast and at the joints of the thighs, press it between two plates, and place it for a moment with its juice in the sauce, serve it hot with orange juice or lemon juice
