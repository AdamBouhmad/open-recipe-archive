## Ingredients

- one cuttlefish, cooked
- olive oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a cuttlefish
2. Chop it finely
3. Put it to boil in a pan with olive oil, vinegar, and pepper
4. As soon as it finishes boiling, serve it
