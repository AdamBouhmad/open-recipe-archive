## Ingredients

- Chicken breast
- Beef fat
- Lard
- Veal udders
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt, pepper
- Bread soaked in milk
- Four egg yolks
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Make a chopped mixture of chicken breast, beef fat, lard, and cooked veal udders
2. After chopping, pound in a mortar
3. Season with salt, pepper, parsley, chopped chives
4. Bind with a piece of bread soaked in milk and four egg yolks
5. Form small boxes of dough the size of a new silver crusado and the height of two fingers
6. Fill with the chopped mixture
7. Cover with another small piece of dough
8. Cook in the oven
9. Once cooked, remove all the chopped mixture from inside
10. Dissolve in a little broth, egg yolks, milk, and lemon juice
11. Once bound, place back in the forms
12. Serve hot
