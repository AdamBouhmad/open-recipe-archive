## Ingredients

- half pound grated Parmesan cheese
- quarter pound fresh butter
- parsley
- chopped chives
- pepper
- nutmeg
- quarter liter white wine
- egg whites and yolks from ten or twelve eggs
- bread marrow, fried

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan the grated Parmesan, fresh butter, chopped parsley and chives, pepper, nutmeg, and white wine
2. Let it boil for an instant
3. When the cheese is melted, beat the whites of ten or twelve eggs to stiff peaks
4. Mix in the yolks
5. Pour everything into the saucepan and stir together over heat
6. When cooked and well-flavored, serve garnished with pieces of fried bread marrow
