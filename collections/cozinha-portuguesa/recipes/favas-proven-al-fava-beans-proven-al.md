## Ingredients

- fava beans
- olive oil
- artichokes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley bundle
- savory
- onion
- flour
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take very tender fava beans, stir-fry them in olive oil with half a dozen also tender artichokes cut into quarters, seasoned with salt, pepper, a bundle of parsley and savory, and an onion, dusted with flour, and leave them to cook in their own substance
2. When cooked, bind them with egg yolks mixed in vinegar with grated nutmeg
3. After binding and with good taste, serve for an entre-mets
