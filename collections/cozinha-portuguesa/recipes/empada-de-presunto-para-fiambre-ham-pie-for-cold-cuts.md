## Ingredients

- uma massa composta de farinha, agua, ovos, e manteiga (dough made of flour, water, eggs, and butter)
- toucinho picado e temperado (seasoned chopped bacon)
- presunto defóslado (boned ham)
- folhas de louro (bay leaves)
- pranchas de toucinho (bacon slices)

## Directions

1. Make the pie box with dough composed of flour, water, eggs, and butter
2. Line the bottom with plenty of chopped and seasoned bacon
3. Put the boned and unsalted ham inside, cover it with the remaining pounded bacon, bay leaves, and bacon slices
4. After completely covering it with dough and well sealed, put it to bake in the oven for ten or twelve hours
5. Remove it, and after it is cold, serve it on a napkin as a cold cuts dish
