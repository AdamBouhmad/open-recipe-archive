## Ingredients

- two pigeons
- two partridges
- one duck
- one rabbit
- one chicken
- one sausage
- one piece of pork
- one arratel of diced bacon
- herbs
- six lettuce or chicory or Murcia cabbage hearts
- all spices whole except pepper
- a large bundle of herbs
- bread
- pan bread or pelangana
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a large pot, braise two pigeons, two partridges, one duck, one rabbit cut in halves, and one chicken in quarters
2. Add one sausage, one piece of pork, and one arratel of diced bacon
3. Add herbs, six lettuce or chicory or Murcia cabbage hearts
4. Add all spices whole, only the pepper ground
5. Add a large bundle of herbs
6. When everything is cooked and seasoned with salt and other seasonings it needs, place two breads in a plate or pelangana made into soups and moistened with broth
7. Let them soak over the same pot
8. When ready to serve, place all the meat piece by piece on top of the soups
9. Garnish with lettuce or chicory hearts (everything very well arranged)
10. Top with lemon juice
11. Serve at table
