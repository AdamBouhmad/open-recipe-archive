## Ingredients

- 1 wild or tame bird
- white wine
- 2 ounces of butter
- 1 quince in quarters
- spices

## Directions

1. While roasting a wild or tame bird, collect the drippings until it is half-roasted
2. Add a splash of white wine to the drippings and place in a cooking pot
3. Braise the whole bird in this with two ounces of butter, a quince in quarters, and all the seasonings
