## Ingredients

- one plump hen
- one partridge, or two good ones
- one calf knuckle
- water
- one handful of pearl barley
- salt
- two or three egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Blanch the partridges and calf knuckle in hot water first
2. Place all together in a pot nearly full of water up to the rim
3. Add one handful of pearl barley with appropriate salt stones
4. Put on the fire to simmer gently
5. When the birds are cooked, remove them with a skimmer and place on a plate
6. Strain the broth through a strainer, stirring always with a bread spoon so the substance and liquid falls into the broth
7. Pour into a bowl and serve at table
8. For greater substance, two or three egg yolks may be added
