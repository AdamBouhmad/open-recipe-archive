## Ingredients

- Veal feet, knuckles, calves feet, or old chickens
- Turkey feet
- All things that are gelatinous

## Directions

1. According to the quantity of jelly desired, take veal feet, knuckles, calves feet, or old chickens
2. Take turkey feet and everything that is gelatinous
3. Soak, wash, and blanch
4. Then put to cook for three or four hours
5. When cooked and the broth reduced and at the proper consistency
6. Pass through a sieve
7. Remove all the fat
8. Season with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, and half a bottle of white wine
