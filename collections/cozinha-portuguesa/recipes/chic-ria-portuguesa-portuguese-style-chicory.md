## Ingredients

- Oil
- Garlic cloves, chopped
- Hot water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Chicory, chopped, cooked and squeezed
- Fried eggs

## Directions

1. Heat oil with chopped garlic cloves
2. Add a drop of hot water, a drop of vinegar, salt, and pepper
3. Bind with flour
4. Add the chopped, cooked, and squeezed chicory inside
5. Let simmer slowly
6. With good taste, serve with fried eggs for a course between meals
