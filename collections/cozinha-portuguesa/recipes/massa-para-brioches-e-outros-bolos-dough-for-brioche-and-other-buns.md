## Ingredients

- hot water
- half an ounce of bread or beer yeast
- half an eighth of flour
- one eighth of flour
- eight eggs
- one pound of butter
- half an ounce of salt
- a little water

## Directions

1. Mix the dough with hot water, half an ounce of bread or beer yeast, and half an eighth of flour; when well combined, wrap in cloth and place in a warm location, let it ferment a quarter hour if in summer, or one hour if in winter
2. Then place on a table with one eighth of flour, eight eggs, one pound of butter, half an ounce of salt, and a little water, and knead everything together with the palms of the hands two or three times
3. Dust with flour, wrap again in cloth, and let ferment nine or ten hours
4. Remove the dough, cut into pieces, shape with the hand, flatten, brush with beaten eggs, place in the oven to bake; if small, bake for half an hour, and if large, one and a half hours
5. These buns are eaten hot, cold, or as desired
