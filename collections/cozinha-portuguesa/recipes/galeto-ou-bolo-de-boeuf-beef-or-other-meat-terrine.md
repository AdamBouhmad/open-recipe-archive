## Ingredients

- meat fillets and sinews from the loins and thighs of wild boar
- loin of beef
- leg of mutton
- ham
- parsley
- small onions
- garlic clove
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- bacon cut into dice
- six egg yolks
- a quarter liter of white wine

## Directions

1. Take the meat fillets and sinews from the loins and thighs of wild boar, a loin of beef, and a leg of mutton, and add a forcemeat of ham
2. Chop everything together on a board with parsley, small onions, and a clove of garlic, seasoned with salt, pepper, some spices, and fine herbs
3. Place it in a terrine with three or four handfuls of bacon cut into dice, six egg yolks, and a quarter liter of white wine
4. Mix and blend together very well
5. Line a casserole with bacon slices and arrange the forcemeat inside so that it is well packed and united
6. Cover with other bacon slices, paper leaves, and its lid, and place in a hot oven for four or five hours
7. Allow it to cool in the same casserole; once cooked and cold, unmold it and serve on a napkin
