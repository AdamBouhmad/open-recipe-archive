## Ingredients

- one dozen egg yolks
- half dozen egg whites
- butter
- anchovies, chopped
- garlic, a little bit
- nutmeg, grated
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Beat one dozen egg yolks with half a dozen egg whites, place in melted butter with chopped anchovies and a bit of garlic, grated nutmeg, to which you can also add grated Parmesan cheese
2. Stirring always until cooked, serve hot
