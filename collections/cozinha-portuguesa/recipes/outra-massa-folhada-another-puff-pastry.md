## Ingredients

- Beef fat, weighing two or three arratels
- Dough

## Directions

1. Pound very well all the membranes in a piece of beef fat weighing two or three arratels, and after being very finely minced, pound until it becomes very soft and creamy like butter
2. Spread it on the dough, roll it up, and spread it as in the preceding recipe four or five times
3. It will serve for tarts, small pastries, and for everything that is made of meat and eaten hot
