## Ingredients

- Partridges or young partridges
- Medium bacon for larding, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
- Ordinary paste
- Butter
- Bacon slices
- Veal slices
- Chopped meat
- Whole truffles
- Scraped and seasoned bacon
- Eggs
- Napkin
- Broth
- Water
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- White wine
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges or young partridges, tender and stuffed as above
2. Lard them with medium bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. Spread a piece of ordinary paste on a buttered and floured napkin
4. Arrange slices of bacon, veal, and some chopped meat on the paste
5. Arrange the partridges on top with their breasts facing down, and place whole truffles in the spaces between
6. Cover with scraped and seasoned bacon
7. Coat the paste around the edges with eggs so it can stick together
8. Fold carefully in the shape of a pouch
9. Fold the napkin in the same way
10. Tie with twine and place to cook in a pot with broth, water, salt, whole pepper, and white wine, simmering
11. When cooked, remove from heat and let drain
12. Open the napkin, turn onto the serving plate, open the pouch carefully
13. Remove the bacon and veal slices and place a Spanish sauce inside
14. Serve hot
