## Ingredients

- turkey hen
- fat livers
- veal sweetbreads
- melted bacon
- flour
- white wine
- broth
- herb bundle
- salt
- pepper
- roe
- capers
- olives without pits
- cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare and mortify a tender turkey hen
2. Place it on a spit covered with bacon strips and paper leaves
3. While it roasts, make a stew of fat livers and veal sweetbreads that are passed through the fire in melted bacon with a little flour
4. Moisten with a quart of white wine, a little broth, and a herb bundle, seasoned with little salt and pepper
5. When reduced and of good taste, add chopped roe, capers, and pitted olives
6. Finish binding with a little cullis and remove the fat
7. Remove the turkey hen from the spit, place on its plate, and serve with its ragout on top and lemon juice
