## Ingredients

- one lamprey
- hot water
- wine
- orange juice
- nutmeg
- all seasonings

## Directions

1. Wash and clean a lamprey in hot water
2. Remove the teeth, bile, and gills
3. Make one cut in the middle of the gills and another at the navel so the intestines can be removed without dirtying the inside of the lamprey
4. Remove the intestines
5. Place the lamprey on a pan to drain the blood
6. Roast it on a spit
7. Collect the drippings in the same pan
8. When roasted, season the pan sauce with a splash of wine, juice of one orange, nutmeg, and all seasonings
9. Serve the sauce apart from the lamprey
