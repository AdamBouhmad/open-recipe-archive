## Ingredients

- two arratels of quinces in quarters
- one and a half arratels of sugar
- clove
- cinnamon
- a dozen egg yolks
- one arratel of sugar

## Directions

1. Scald two arratels of quinces in quarters and cook in one and a half arratels of sugar with clove and cinnamon.
2. When the syrup is thick, let cool.
3. Make beaten eggs from a dozen egg yolks in one arratel of sugar and let cool.
4. Put the quinces in the tart with the syrup, cinnamon, and eggs on top.
5. Close it and after baking send to the table.
6. In this way tarts of camasses and any other fruit are made.
