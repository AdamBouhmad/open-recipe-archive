## Ingredients

- eels
- creamed minced mixture
- eggs
- fine bread crumbs
- clarified butter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the eels, leaving some unpeeled
2. Cut into equal fillets
3. Place on a small amount of creamed minced mixture, well bound
4. Roll like veal patties
5. Secure with string
6. Poach for six minutes in a mixture with butter, white wine, fine herbs, and salt
7. After poaching, remove and pass twice through beaten eggs and fine bread crumbs
8. Fry in clarified butter
9. When fried and of good color, serve with fried parsley around it
