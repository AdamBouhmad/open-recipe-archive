## Ingredients

- venison meat
- thick lard
- melted lard
- broth or hot water
- quarter measure of red or white wine
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- lemon
- onion studded with cloves
- bundle of fine herbs
- cullis
- vinegar
- limes

## Directions

1. Take a piece of venison meat from any part, cut it into equal pieces
2. Lard it with thick lard well tempered, then pass it through melted lard on the fire
3. When browned, moisten it with broth or hot water and a quarter measure of red or white wine
4. Season with salt, pepper, bay leaf, a piece of whole lemon, an onion studded with cloves, and a bundle of fine herbs
5. Let it simmer slowly; when cooked and the sauce reduced, bind it with a little cullis and little vinegar, and serve with lime juice
