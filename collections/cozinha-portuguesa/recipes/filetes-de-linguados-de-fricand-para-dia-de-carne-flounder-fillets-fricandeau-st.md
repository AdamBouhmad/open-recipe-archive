## Ingredients

- flounder loins
- fine bacon
- white wine
- water
- salt
- fine herbs
- reduced broth
- essence sauce
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Lard the flounder loins with fine bacon
2. Soak them in white wine, water, salt, and fine herbs
3. After being soaked, pour reduced broth into another casserole
4. Put the fillets inside with the bacon facing down
5. Let simmer slowly
6. When the broth is at the glaze point and sticking to the bacon with good color, serve with essence sauce and lime juice
