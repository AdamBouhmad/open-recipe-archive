## Ingredients

- one arratel of clean sugar
- ten eggs
- one arratel of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Place one arratel of clean sugar in a pot, add ten eggs on top and beat very well until thick
2. Check with the oven if the flour can be added, which should be one arratel sifted
3. Once approved, add it while beating very well for some time so the flour does not settle, pour into a basin, and beat constantly at the bottom until it enters the oven
4. When it comes out, slice it and return the slices to a basin in the oven to toast lightly, ensuring it does not burn
