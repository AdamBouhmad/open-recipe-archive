## Ingredients

- rice flour
- ingredients for blancmange dreams
- paper boxes
- clarified butter
- oil
- or lard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a cream with rice flour, prepared and seasoned like blancmange dreams
2. Fill small paper boxes with it, the length of a finger and width of two fingers
3. Deep fry them in very hot clarified butter, oil, or lard
4. When fried and the paper removed, glaze the bottom part with sugar using a hot bread paddle
