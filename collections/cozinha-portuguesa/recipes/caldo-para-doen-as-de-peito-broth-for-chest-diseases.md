## Ingredients

- one beef lung, cleaned and washed
- half a canada of water
- apples of Analfe by the ounce
- sebasts by the ounce
- tamarinds by the ounce
- dried figs by the ounce
- Alicante raisins by the ounce
- half an ounce of the five capillaries
- one dram of rock sugar in each dose

## Directions

1. Clean and wash well in much water one beef lung
2. Place to cook in half a canada of water with apples of Analfe, sebasts, tamarinds, dried figs, Alicante raisins, each thing one ounce, adding also another half ounce of the five capillaries
3. Let everything cook very well and reduce the broth to two-thirds
4. Strain and press through a cloth
5. Divide afterwards into three or four doses
6. Dissolve in each one one dram of rock sugar
7. Take it in the morning and in the afternoon for as long as necessary
