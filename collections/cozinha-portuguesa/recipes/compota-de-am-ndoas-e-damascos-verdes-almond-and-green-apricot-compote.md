## Ingredients

- Four handfuls of ash
- Almonds
- Green apricots

## Directions

1. Boil four handfuls of ash in water to make a lye, put the almonds and apricots in it, and give them one boil.
2. Remove from the lye and rub them between your hands to remove the skin, putting them in cold water.
3. Pass them to boiling water, let them cook, and when cooked, which you will know by pricking them with a needle - if it enters easily - pass them to other cold water.
4. Put them in clarified sugar, place on heat, and let them simmer in it until they turn a good green color.
5. When finished and the sugar reduced to its point, serve as desired.
