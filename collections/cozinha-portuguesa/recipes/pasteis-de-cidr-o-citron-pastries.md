## Ingredients

- two slices of citron
- a dozen egg yolks
- one arratel of sugar in thread stage
- flower water
- musk
- tender pastry dough the size of a cruzado

## Directions

1. Pound very well two slices of citron in a mortar.
2. Cook with a dozen egg yolks in one arratel of sugar at thread stage, flower water, and musk.
3. Once well cooked, make small boxes of tender dough the size of a cruzado and bake them empty.
4. Fill them with the citron paste and bake with pastille sugar on top in place of a cover.
