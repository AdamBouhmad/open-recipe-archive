## Ingredients

- water
- butter
- salt
- flour
- soft cheese
- eggs
- anchovies
- pepper
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole a quart of water, a quarter pound of butter, and some salt stones, and place on fire
2. When boiling, add two handfuls of flour
3. When in dough and well dried, place in a stone mortar
4. Add half a pound of soft and fatty cheese, five or six eggs, two chopped anchovies, and some pepper
5. Mix all thoroughly and garnish some thin copper molds with puff pastry
6. Place on each one a piece of this forcemeat the size of a nut
7. Bake in a moderate oven
8. After baking, serve as an entrée
9. Can also be baked in buttered paper and served without dough
