## Ingredients

- old pigeons
- thick bacon
- veal or beef slices
- roots
- onion studded with cloves
- bouquet of fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- beef broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the pigeons and lard them with thick bacon
2. Place them to cook in a braise with slices of veal or beef, roots, onion studded with cloves, and a bouquet of fine herbs, all seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Cover them all with other slices of bacon, veal, or beef, and place them to braise between two fires
4. After a while, add a spoonful of broth and a quarter bottle of white wine
5. Let them simmer gently
6. When cooked, serve them with sauces and vegetable garnishes that seem best, such as Lombard cabbages, small whole onions, turnips, and peas
7. They can also be used for terrines and hot pies
