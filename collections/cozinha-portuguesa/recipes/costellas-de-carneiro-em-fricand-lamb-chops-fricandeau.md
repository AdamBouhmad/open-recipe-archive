## Ingredients

- lamb chops in two whole square pieces
- bacon
- cold water
- bacon strips
- ham slice
- parsley bundle
- onion
- stock
- sorrel sauce

## Directions

1. Take two whole square pieces of lamb chops and trim them very well
2. Remove the membranes over the fillets
3. Lard them with fine bacon and put them to soak in cold water
4. Then blanch them in fresh water
5. Transfer to a small casserole, cover with bacon strips
6. Add a slice of ham, a bundle of parsley, and an onion studded with cloves
7. Moisten with stock without salt
8. When cooked, strain the stock through a sieve, remove the fat, and reduce it
9. Apply the glaze to the chops and when sufficiently colored, serve with sorrel sauce or any other sauce that seems appropriate
