## Ingredients

- bread, prepared as described above
- chicken breasts, roasted
- morels
- water
- good olive oil
- two slices of ham
- flour
- half a cup of white wine
- one spoon of substance
- pepper
- salt
- bunch of fine herbs
- one chive studded with cloves
- a little roux
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the bread as described above and stuff it with roasted chicken breasts and fry it as the preceding recipe
2. Cut the morels in two halves
3. Wash them in two or three waters
4. Sauté them in a little good olive oil and two slices of ham
5. After sautéing, dust them with flour
6. Add half a cup of white wine and one spoon of substance
7. Season with pepper, salt, a bunch of fine herbs, and one chive studded with cloves
8. Let them simmer little by little
9. Once cooked and the fat removed, thicken with a little roux
10. Place them in their dish with fried bread in the middle and lemon juice
11. Serve
