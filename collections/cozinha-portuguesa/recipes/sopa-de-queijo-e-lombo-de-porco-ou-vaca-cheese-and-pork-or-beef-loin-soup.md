## Ingredients

- pork or beef loin
- wine
- garlic
- butter
- bread slices
- thin cheese slices
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take a pork or beef loin that has been marinating four days in wine and garlic
2. Roast it
3. When roasted, cut it into thin slices
4. Take a buttered skillet
5. Layer bread slices on the bottom
6. Place thin cheese slices over the bread
7. Place the loin slices on top of the cheese
8. Sprinkle with grated bread crumbs and sugar
9. Continue filling the skillet this way to the top
10. When full, pour six beaten eggs over the top
11. Bake in the oven until golden
12. When golden, make holes with a fork
13. Sprinkle sugar on top
14. Send to table with pounded cinnamon
