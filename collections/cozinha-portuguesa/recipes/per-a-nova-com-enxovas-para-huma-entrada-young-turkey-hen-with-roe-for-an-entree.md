## Ingredients

- turkey hen
- turkey livers
- scraped bacon
- roe
- parsley
- chives
- truffles
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- pork butter
- bacon strips
- paper leaves
- ham essence
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After preparing the turkey hen, stuff it with a forcemeat made from its own livers, scraped bacon, three roe, parsley, chives, truffles, all finely chopped and pounded in a mortar, bound with two egg yolks, and seasoned with little salt and pepper
2. Straighten the leg after stuffing and close it so nothing escapes
3. Sear it in pork butter and after cooling and cleaning well, cover it with bacon strips and paper leaves
4. Place to roast as is customary
5. After roasting, remove the bacon strips and paper leaves, place on its plate, and serve with ham essence sauce, one roe dissolved inside, juice and lemon fillets
