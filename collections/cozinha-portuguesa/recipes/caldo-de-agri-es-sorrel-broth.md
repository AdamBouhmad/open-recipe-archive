## Ingredients

- one arratel of veal
- one canada of water
- a little salt
- three or four bundles of sorrel, well washed and chopped

## Directions

1. Put one arratel of veal to cook in a pot with one canada of water, seasoned with a little salt
2. After skimming, let it boil little by little
3. When the broth is reduced to half, add three or four bundles of well washed and chopped sorrel
4. Let them boil for a while
5. Strain through a silk sieve and serve hot in the morning and afternoon to whoever wishes to refresh themselves
