## Ingredients

- beef loin
- bacon, chopped
- parsley
- small onions
- mushrooms
- garlic
- shallots
- salt
- pepper
- twine
- bacon slices
- paper

## Directions

1. Take a beef loin and after preparing it, pierce it with a stick from one end to the other
2. With a knife enlarge the hole and fill it with chopped bacon, parsley, small onions, mushrooms, garlic, shallots, salt, and pepper
3. Tie both ends together with twine
4. Place it to roast covered with bacon slices and paper on top
5. When roasted, serve with spicy shallot sauce
