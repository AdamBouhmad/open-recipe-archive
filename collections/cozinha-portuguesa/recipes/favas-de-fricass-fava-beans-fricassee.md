## Ingredients

- fava beans
- butter
- parsley bundle
- savory
- small onion
- cloves
- flour
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- milk
- parsley
- nutmeg
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Trim and clean the fava beans, stir-fry them in butter with a bundle of parsley and savory, a small onion with two cloves, and dusted with flour
2. Then add a drop of hot water, season with salt and pepper, and when cooked with little sauce, bind with egg yolks mixed in milk, chopped parsley, nutmeg, and a dash of vinegar
3. Being of good taste, serve for an entre-mets
