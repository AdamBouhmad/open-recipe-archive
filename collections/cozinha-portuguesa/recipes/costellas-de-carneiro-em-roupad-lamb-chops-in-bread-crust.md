## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- stock
- salt
- parsley
- fine herbs
- raw forcemeat
- bread crumbs
- bacon strips
- lemon juice
- pork butter

## Directions

1. Trim the chops and cook them in stock with little salt, a bundle of parsley, and fine herbs
2. When cooked, strain the stock through a sieve, reduce it, and glaze the chops
3. When cold, cover them with raw forcemeat and dust with bread crumbs
4. Place them in a baking dish with bacon strips underneath
5. Cook them in the oven
6. When cooked, serve them with essence and lemon juice, or fry them in pork butter and serve garnished with fried parsley
