## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Trim the peaches and cut into slices
2. Place to cook in water
3. Once cooked, place on a sieve and put a pressing board on top with a heavy weight so they drain
4. Once drained, add three arratels of peaches to three arratels of sugar at wire point
5. Cook until it makes small bubbles
6. Remove from the fire and add fragrance if desired
7. Place in small paper cones
