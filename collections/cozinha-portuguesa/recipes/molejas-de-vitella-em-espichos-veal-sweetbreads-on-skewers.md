## Ingredients

- Bacon
- Veal sweetbreads
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Shallot
- Garlic
- Flour
- Broth
- Egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of lard bacon into small thin and square pieces and put them in a casserole, heat them on the fire
2. When half cooked, add the sweetbreads also cut into dice and seasoned with salt, pepper, parsley, shallot, little garlic, all well chopped
3. Turn them several times on the fire, dust with flour, moisten with a spoon of broth, and let it boil
4. When reduced and with little sauce, thicken it with four egg yolks so it becomes thick and the sauce sticks to the meat
5. Then put the pieces of sweetbreads on skewers interspersed with pieces of bacon, pass them through the sauce, cover with breadcrumbs, roast them on grills, and serve them dry as an entrance dish
