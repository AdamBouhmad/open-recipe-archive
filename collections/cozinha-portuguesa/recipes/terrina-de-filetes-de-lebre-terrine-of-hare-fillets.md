## Ingredients

- one or two hares
- meat from thighs and loins
- medium lard
- bacon planks
- hare fillets
- salt
- pepper
- bundle of fine herbs
- truffles
- veal or calf planks

## Directions

1. Skin one or two hares, lightly remove the meat from the thighs and loins
2. Lard it with medium lard well seasoned
3. Line the bottom and around a terrine with bacon planks, arrange inside the hare fillets seasoned with salt, pepper, a bundle of fine herbs, and some truffles at intervals
4. Cover with veal or calf planks beaten, garnish with bacon planks, put on the lid
