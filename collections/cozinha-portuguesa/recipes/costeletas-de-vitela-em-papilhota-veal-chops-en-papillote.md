## Ingredients

- Veal chops
- Melted lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Chives
- Paper

## Directions

1. Take a square of veal and cut the chops as described above, and beat them somewhat with the back of a knife; then marinate them in melted lard, with salt, pepper, fine herbs, and chives
2. Take a quarter of paper, make a small hole in it so that the point of the chop bone passes through, anointing the paper on the outside, wrap them in it with their marinade; and folded neatly all around, place them to roast on the grill over another sheet of anointed paper
3. When roasted, serve them in their papillote, and without sauce
