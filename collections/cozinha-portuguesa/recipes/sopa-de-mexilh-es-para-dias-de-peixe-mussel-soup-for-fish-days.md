## Ingredients

- Mussels
- Water
- A piece of butter
- A few sprigs of parsley
- One onion with two cloves
- Some white truffle fillets
- Cucumber pieces
- Fish broth
- One dozen almonds
- Two dozens of mussels
- Four yolks of hard eggs
- One piece of bread crumb

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mussels in three or four waters
2. Place them in a pot with a little water and put on fire to open
3. If there is no fish broth to moisten the soup, add more water and season with a piece of butter, a few sprigs of parsley, and one onion with two cloves
4. When they open and come to a boil, remove from heat
5. Strain the broth through a sieve and remove the mussel shells
6. Place the mussels in a pot with a piece of butter, some white truffle fillets, cucumber pieces, and fish broth
7. When it comes to a boil, remove the butter and place in a white cullis made from one dozen almonds, two dozens of mussels, four yolks of hard eggs, and one piece of bread crumb, all pounded and dissolved in two spoonfuls of fish broth
8. Season first and to good taste
9. Pass through a sieve and place on low heat without boiling
10. Pour over soups cooked in the same mussel broth with stuffed bread in the middle and the white cullis on top
