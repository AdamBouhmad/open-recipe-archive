## Ingredients

- Ten to twelve arrobas of pork meat
- Half a bottle of white wine
- An equal amount of fat and lard, cut into small dice
- A little saltpetre, salt, pepper, and fine spices

## Directions

1. Take ten to twelve arrobas of pork meat and pour half a bottle of white wine over it
2. Wrap in a white cloth
3. Press it in a press or between two boards loaded with stones
4. Leave to sit for two days
5. After that, chop coarsely on a table
6. Add an equal amount of fat and lard cut into small dice
7. Season with a little saltpetre, salt, pepper, and fine spices
8. Add half a bottle of white wine
9. Mix everything in a bowl and cover for ten or twelve hours
10. Then fill the larger intestines, tie them equally, and let them drain
11. After that, smoke them for several days
12. Once dried, serve them as desired
13. In the same manner, salchichões of wild boar are made
