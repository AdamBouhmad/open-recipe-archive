## Ingredients

- fish fillets such as mullet, cherne, bass, or cod
- salt
- mustard
- lemon zest
- fine sugar
- sweet and bitter almond biscuits
- butter
- egg yolks
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Leave on a table fish fillets such as mullet, cherne, bass, or cod
2. Pound in a stone mortar and season with salt, mustard, lemon zest, fine sugar, and some sweet and bitter almond biscuits, all pounded and made into a paste
3. Add a piece of butter bound with egg yolks and a piece of bread crumb soaked in milk
4. Cut bread into thin slices, place in a buttered baking dish
5. Garnish the slices with the chopped mixture
6. Bake in the oven
7. When cooked, glaze with fine sugar and hot spatula, and serve as an entrée
