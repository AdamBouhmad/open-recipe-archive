## Ingredients

- half a dozen onions
- dozen and a half chicken livers, chopped
- pork lard, cut into small dice
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs, chopped
- one quartilho of milk
- half a dozen egg yolks
- intestines for filling
- salt
- lime
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Cook half a dozen onions in broth
2. Once cooked, drain and pound them in a stone mortar
3. Add dozen and a half chopped chicken livers
4. Once everything is chopped, place in a casserole with pork lard cut into small dice
5. Season with salt, pepper, spices, and fine chopped herbs
6. Add one quartilho of milk and half a dozen egg yolks
7. Mix everything and when it has good taste and texture, fill the intestines
8. Tie them as instructed
9. Cook them in milk with salt, lime, and bay leaves
10. Once cooked, let them cool
11. Store them as the preceding ones
12. Serve hot with orange juice
