## Ingredients

- slices of veal
- slices of ham
- one partridge
- two roots
- broth
- half a carafe of white wine, already boiled
- one spoonful of meat jelly
- pepper
- coriander seed
- garlic cloves
- parsley
- fine herbs
- onion studded with cloves
- good olive oil
- two lemon slices

## Directions

1. Line a pan with slices of veal and slices of ham, add one partridge and two roots
2. Put on the heat to render its fat gently
3. When it sticks, add a drop of broth and half a carafe of white wine already boiled
4. Add one spoonful of meat jelly, season with pepper, coriander seed, garlic cloves, parsley, fine herbs, onion studded with cloves, and good olive oil
5. Let it boil gently for the space of two hours
6. Then add two lemon slices and when reduced, remove the fat, pass through a sieve, and it will serve for whatever is necessary
