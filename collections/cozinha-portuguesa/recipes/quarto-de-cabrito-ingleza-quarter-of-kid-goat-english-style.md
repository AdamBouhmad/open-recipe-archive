## Ingredients

- quarter of kid goat
- water
- salt
- bacon slab
- cauliflower
- butter
- spinach or other greens (optional)

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of kid goat, either hind or front
2. Place it to soak and then dry very well in a cloth
3. Dust with flour and place to cook in a white broth made with water, salt, and a slab of bacon
4. When cooked, garnish with cauliflower cooked in water alone and serve with melted butter sauce
5. Spinach or other greens may also be added, observing that the kid goat quarter should not be overcooked
