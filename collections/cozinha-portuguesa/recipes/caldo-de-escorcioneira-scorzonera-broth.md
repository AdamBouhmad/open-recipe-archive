## Ingredients

- a piece of veal
- a dozen roots of scorzonera, well washed

## Directions

1. Make it the same way as chicory broth
2. Instead of chicory, take a dozen roots of scorzonera, well washed
3. Cook with the veal
4. When everything is cooked and the broth is reduced to half, pass through a sieve
5. Take it hot in the morning and afternoon, using it for as long as you wish, and it is excellent for the chest
