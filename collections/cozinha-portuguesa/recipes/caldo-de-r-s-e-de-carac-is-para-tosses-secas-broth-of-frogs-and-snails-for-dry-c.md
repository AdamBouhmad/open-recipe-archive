## Ingredients

- one dozen snails
- two dozen frog thighs
- cold water
- one canada of water
- two heads of leeks only, cut very fine
- or two or three turnips cut the same way
- one handful of pearl barley
- seven or eight grains of saffron in powder

## Directions

1. Let simmer for an instant one dozen snails and two dozen frog thighs
2. After simmering, place in cold water and then grind in a stone mortar
3. Ground, place to simmer one canada of water with two heads of leeks only, cut very fine, or when not available, two or three turnips cut the same way, and one handful of pearl barley
4. After simmering and when the broth is reduced, strain through a cloth but do not press
5. Before taking it add seven or eight grains of saffron in powder
6. Take it in the morning on an empty stomach and three hours after supper, which can be continued over the course of one month or six weeks, purging oneself during this time if necessary
