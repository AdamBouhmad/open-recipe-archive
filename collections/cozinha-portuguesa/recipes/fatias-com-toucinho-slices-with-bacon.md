## Ingredients

- long bread
- bacon interlarded with meat
- beaten eggs
- vinegar
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the two ends from a long bread
2. Lard with bacon interlarded with meat
3. Cut into slices the thickness of a finger, without crust
4. Pass through beaten eggs
5. Fry on low heat, little by little
6. When golden-brown and fried, serve with clear sauce, a splash of vinegar, and coarse pepper
