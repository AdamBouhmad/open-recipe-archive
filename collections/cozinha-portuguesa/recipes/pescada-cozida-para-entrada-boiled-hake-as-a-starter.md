## Ingredients

- hake
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- parsley
- onion
- fish roe
- capers
- broth
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Large hake are ordinarily boiled or fried: the large ones are served whole or in steaks, and are served in water, salt, and a little vinegar, served plain garnished with parsley; or with a sauce made of washed butter, some flour powder, two egg yolks, parsley, chopped onion, fish roe, capers, and half a spoon of the same broth in which the hake was cooked, and after seasoning with pepper and nutmeg, bind on the fire and serve with lemon juice
