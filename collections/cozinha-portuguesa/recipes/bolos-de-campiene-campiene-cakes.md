## Ingredients

- thick paper mold shaped like a manguito box
- clarified butter
- flour
- beer yeast
- eggs
- butter
- grated lemon rind
- sugar in thread stage
- colored nonpareils

## Directions

1. Make a paper mold in the shape of a manguito box, grease inside with butter, and fill halfway with dough made of flour, beer yeast, eggs, butter, and grated lemon rind, like brioche dough
2. Bake in the oven for two or three hours
3. When cooked, remove the paper, spread with clarified butter and sugar in thread stage, and serve with colored nonpareils on top
