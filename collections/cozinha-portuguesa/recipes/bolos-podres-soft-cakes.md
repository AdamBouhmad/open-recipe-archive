## Ingredients

- half an alqueire of sifted flour
- four arratels of clean sugar in pastille point
- two canadas of oil
- one arratel of very well pounded almonds
- one vintem of cloves
- fennel
- cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. For half an alqueire of sifted flour, take four arratels of clean sugar in pastille point
2. Add two canadas of oil and one arratel of very well pounded almonds
3. Add one vintem of cloves, fennel, cinnamon, and salt
4. When this boils very well, gradually add the half alqueire of sifted flour, stirring
