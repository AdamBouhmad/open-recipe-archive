## Ingredients

- Two portions of fresh tuna
- wine
- vinegar
- garlic
- a small amount of the pickling liquid
- a splash of olive oil
- black seasonings (clove, cinnamon, and pepper)
- lemon juice
- one ounce of flour

## Directions

1. After the two portions of fresh tuna have been in a preserve of wine, vinegar, and garlic for two hours, place them to roast in the oven in a frying pan with a small amount of the pickling liquid in which they were kept and a splash of olive oil
2. When half-cooked, season with all the black seasonings and lemon juice
3. Make a pastry crust of one ounce of flour, placing the tuna with its sauce inside
4. Put it to cook and send it to the table
