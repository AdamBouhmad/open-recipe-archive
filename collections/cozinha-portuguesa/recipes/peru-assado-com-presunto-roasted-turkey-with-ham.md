## Ingredients

- one young turkey
- ham cut finely like bacon
- bacon slices
- lard

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare a young turkey, then lard it
2. Cut ham finely like bacon, taking care to cut it lengthwise
3. After well larded, place it in the roasting spit well covered with bacon slices and paper leaves
4. When roasted, serve with clear ham essence, shallot, and lemon juice
