## Ingredients

- two slices of ham
- two roots
- one onion
- two cloves of garlic
- melted bacon fat
- one spoonful of broth
- one spoonful of cullis
- one drop of tarragon vinegar
- cooked and reduced broth

## Directions

1. Cut into dice two slices of ham, two roots, and one onion, and two cloves of garlic, and fry everything in melted bacon fat; after frying, add to it one spoonful of broth, another of cullis, one drop of tarragon vinegar, and let it simmer little by little in cooked and reduced broth, remove the fat from it, pass it through a sieve, and serve it for whatever is desired.
