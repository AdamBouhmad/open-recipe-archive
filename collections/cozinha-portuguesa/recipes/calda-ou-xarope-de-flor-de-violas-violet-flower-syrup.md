## Ingredients

- violet flowers
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place four ounces of violet flowers in a bowl.
2. Pour a quart of boiling water over them.
3. Cover the bowl with its lid.
4. Let it infuse for two hours over gentle heat.
5. Strain through a cloth and squeeze well.
6. If it produces half a pint, pour it into two and a half pounds of sugar in the hard crack stage, which is known by dipping two fingers in cold water, then in the sugar, and dipping again in cold water—if the sugar sticks to the fingers and breaks between them.
7. Add the half pint of violet water, and incorporate it into the sugar without boiling.
8. Pour into a bowl.
9. Cover it.
10. Place it over gentle heat for three days.
11. At the end of this time, dip your fingers in it, wet them in the syrup, then open them and if they form a thread that does not break, pour it into bottles and keep for when needed.
