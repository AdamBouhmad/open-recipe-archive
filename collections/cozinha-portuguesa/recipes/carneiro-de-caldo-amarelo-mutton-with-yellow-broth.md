## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton, cut in pieces
- one quart of bacon
- herbs
- vinegar
- all spices
- four egg yolks
- bread slices
- lemon juice
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place two arratels of mutton, cut in pieces, to braise with one quart of bacon and herbs
2. When half cooked, add a splash of vinegar
3. When cooked, season with all spices
4. Thicken with four egg yolks
5. Place on plate over bread slices with lemon juice and cinnamon on top and serve
