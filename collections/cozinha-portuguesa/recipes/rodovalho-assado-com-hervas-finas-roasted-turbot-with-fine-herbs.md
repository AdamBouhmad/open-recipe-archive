## Ingredients

- turbot
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- onion slices
- lemon slices
- garlic cloves
- fine herbs
- washed butter
- bread crumbs
- beet sauce or other piquant sauce

## Directions

1. If the turbot is large, after cleaning, cut it in two or four portions
2. Marinate in oil with salt, pepper, onion slices, lemon slices, garlic cloves, and all manner of fine herbs for two or three hours until it has taken on the flavor of the marinade
3. Line the bottom of a baking dish with washed butter
4. Place the fish on top and add its marinade
5. Cover with finely grated bread crumbs and put in a moderately hot oven to cook
6. When roasted and well-colored, serve with beet sauce or any other piquant sauce
7. If you do not have the convenience of an oven, roast the fish on grills and serve in the same manner
