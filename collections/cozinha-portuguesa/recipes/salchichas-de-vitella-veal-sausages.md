## Ingredients

- Veal, the most tender
- Lard, equal amount to veal
- A piece of fat

## Directions

1. Make them in the same form as the preceding ones, with the difference that instead of pork meat, use tender veal with an equal amount of lard and a piece of fat
2. Season and finish as pork sausages
3. Serve roasted in the same manner
4. They may also be made from breasts of capons, partridges, turkeys, and any quality of meat and game desired
5. If you do not want to eat them roasted, they can also be served cooked with white wine sauce, with Parmesan cheese, with cabbage, and with any vegetable ragout
