## Ingredients

- Three or four dozen small round onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Fish broth and substance
- A bunch of parsley
- One bunch of marjoram
- Two small onions
- Two cloves
- One spoonful of vinegar
- Bread slices, fried and cooked in the same broth

## Directions

1. Clean three or four dozen small round onions
2. Pass them through fire in a pot with melted butter and dust with flour
3. When well browned, place in a small pot with fish broth and substance
4. Let it simmer little by little
5. When cooked, add one spoonful of vinegar and serve over fried bread slices cooked in the same broth
6. If the onions are large, cut them into slices and after frying and browning in butter, moisten with vegetable broth or water and serve the soup with a splash of vinegar
