## Ingredients

- four arratels of beef brisket
- two beef tongues
- one dozen beef marrow bones
- two marrow bones
- one eighth of grains
- six heads of garlic
- clove
- pepper
- nutmeg
- savory
- oregano

## Directions

1. Place in a pot and cook for twenty-four hours in cork charcoal ash four arratels of beef brisket, two beef tongues, one dozen marrow bones, two marrow bones, one eighth of grains, six heads of garlic, clove, pepper, nutmeg, savory, and oregano
2. When cooked, serve on bread slices
