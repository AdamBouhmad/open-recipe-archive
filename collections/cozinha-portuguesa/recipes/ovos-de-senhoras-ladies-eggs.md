## Ingredients

- a handful of sweet almonds
- two bitter almonds
- milk
- two roasted and chopped partridge breasts
- salt
- coriander seed
- sugar
- lemon peel
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grind in a stone mortar a handful of sweet almonds and two bitter almonds, adding a few drops of milk at the same time
2. After well ground, add two roasted and chopped partridge breasts
3. When everything is well ground, dissolve with reduced milk
4. Season with salt, coriander seed, sugar, and lemon peel
5. Heat over the flame
6. Add the necessary egg yolks
7. Pass through a strainer
8. Cook over water in its own plate
9. Serve hot as an entrée
