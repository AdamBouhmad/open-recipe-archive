## Ingredients

- five or six small young pigeons
- reed tube
- melted bacon
- whole sauce
- onion slices
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon slices
- bacon slices
- paper leaves
- aspic sauce or remoulade sauce
- béchamel or white coulis or shrimp coulis or green parsley or Dutch green sauce

## Directions

1. Take five or six small young pigeons and after bleeding, scalding, and straightening them with legs inside the body, pass a reed tube through them to keep them in the given shape
2. Marinate them in melted bacon, whole sauce, onion slices, fine herbs, salt, pepper, and lemon slices
3. After they take on the taste of the marinade, wrap in bacon slices and paper leaves and roast them so they stay white
4. When roasted with the paper and bacon removed, serve with aspic sauce, remoulade sauce, or Italian sauce, or they can be cooked in a white braise between bacon slices and served with béchamel, white coulis, shrimp coulis, green parsley, or Dutch green sauce, or whatever sauce seems best
