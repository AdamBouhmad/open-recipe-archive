## Ingredients

- one veal sweetbread
- two chicories
- two onions
- melted butter
- flour
- stock
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- cream
- milk
- cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take one veal sweetbread, prepare it, trim it, lard it, and finish it with its glaze as described in the previous recipes
2. Take two chicories, blanch them in hot water
3. Cut two onions into fillets and pass them through a casserole with melted butter
4. After passing them, dust them with flour and add a spoonful of stock
5. Cut the chicories into pieces and add them to the onion fillets
6. Season with a little salt and pepper, and once cooked and of good taste, remove the fat and bind with egg yolks dissolved in cream and milk
7. Alternatively, after the chicories are blanched, squeeze them and tie them with string and cook them, then cut into equal pieces
8. Place them in a casserole with a little cullis, add what remained stuck to the bottom of the casserole in which the veal cooked, give it four boils, and when of good taste, serve hot with lemon juice
