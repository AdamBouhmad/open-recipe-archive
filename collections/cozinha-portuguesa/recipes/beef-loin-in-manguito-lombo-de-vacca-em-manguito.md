## Ingredients

- beef loin
- bacon, minced
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- garlic
- shallots
- salt
- pepper
- bacon strips

## Directions

1. Take a beef loin and after preparing it, bore a hole through it from end to end with a stick
2. With a knife, fill the hole with minced bacon, parsley, chives, mushrooms, garlic, shallots, salt, and pepper
3. Tie the two ends with string and roast it covered with strips of bacon
