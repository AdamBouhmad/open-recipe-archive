## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Flour
- Fine sugar

## Directions

1. Take six eggs, flour, and an amount of fine sugar equal to half the flour
2. Observe the same method as in the Onion Cake
3. Place in small copper molds greased with clarified butter
4. Bake in the oven with moderate heat
5. When cooked, serve as is and well covered with white icing
