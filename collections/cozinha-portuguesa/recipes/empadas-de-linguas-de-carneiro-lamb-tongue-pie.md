## Ingredients

- Lamb tongues
- Ground mixture made with blood
- Pepper
- Salsa
- Chives, chopped
- Shallots, chopped
- Butter
- Bacon planks
- Pastry box with lid
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the tongues
2. Place them to cook in a braise
3. When cooked, garnish the bottom of a pastry box with a ground mixture made with blood as described earlier
4. Place the tongues on top, split in half, seasoned with pepper, salsa, chopped chives, and shallots, all well chopped
5. Cover with ground mixture as below, a piece of butter, and bacon planks
6. Cover with its pastry lid sealed with eggs and gilded
