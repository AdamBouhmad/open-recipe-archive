## Ingredients

- Half a dozen onions, sliced in rings
- Four parsnips
- Carrots
- Butter
- Fish stock or vegetable stock
- Roux
- Fish fragments or pieces of other fish

## Directions

1. Slice half a dozen onions in rings, cut four parsnips and carrots into pieces
2. Stew them in butter until the onions are browned as appropriate
3. Add fish stock or vegetable stock and some roux
4. Once everything is cooked, seasoned, and of good taste, pass through a sieve
5. Let it rest and use for soups, ragus, and sauces
