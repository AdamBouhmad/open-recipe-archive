## Ingredients

- Turkey (singed and cleaned)
- Minced veal or chicken (picado de vitella ou de galinha)
- Cream (creme)
- Stuffing (salpicaõ)
- Twine (barbante)
- Fine bacon (toucinho fino)

## Directions

1. After the turkey is singed and cleaned, debone it through the neck, and fill it with a minced veal or chicken with cream, or if not, with a stuffing like the one above.
2. Leave it lengthwise in its natural form, and after closing and sewing with twine, put it to cook in a braze.
3. When cooked and dry, remove the twine and serve it with good sauce. It can also be larded with fine bacon and finished like the one above, and served in the same way.
