## Ingredients

- bread
- broth strained
- fat
- heat source

## Directions

1. Cut a bread in half as it comes from the oven
2. Remove all the crumb
3. Place the crusts in a casserole with one spoon of broth strained through a sieve with all the fat
4. Let it boil an instant
5. Transfer to a hollow plate and place over a small stove to boil
6. When attached and gratineed, drain the fat from it
7. Serve with the broths, substances, and broth that is most convenient and to the taste of those who will eat it
