## Ingredients

- two partridges
- olive oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- parsley, finely chopped
- onion, thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Take two partridges, and after roasting them, carve them and place them in a new bowl with olive oil and vinegar boiling with pepper
2. Let them cool, then make a parsley sauce that is very finely chopped, and onion sliced very thin all around, and pour it over the partridges already placed on the plate, and thus send them to the table
