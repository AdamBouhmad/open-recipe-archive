## Ingredients

- Milk
- Six egg yolks
- Three quarters sugar
- Bread of ló or flour, grated
- Flower water
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat one pint of milk with six egg yolks, three quarters of sugar, grated bread of ló or flour, and a small amount of flower water.
2. Pour this into a frying pan and brown it.
3. Once browned, place cinnamon on top and send to the table.
