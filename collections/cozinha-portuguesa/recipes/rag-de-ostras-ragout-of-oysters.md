## Ingredients

- Oyster beards
- Butter or melted salt pork
- White wine
- Broth or stock
- Pepper
- Parsley bouquet
- Small onion
- Cloves
- Cullis
- Oysters, blanched and trimmed

## Directions

1. Sauté the oyster beards in butter or melted salt pork
2. After sautéing, add half a cup of white wine and half a spoon of broth
3. Season with pepper, a parsley bouquet, and a small onion with two cloves
4. Allow to reduce
5. Bind with a little cullis
6. Add two or three dozen oysters, blanched and trimmed
7. Place over heat and allow to cook without boiling until it has good flavor
8. Serve for various garnishes
