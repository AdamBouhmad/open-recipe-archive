## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- milk
- butter
- pistachio cream
- sweet cherries
- apricots
- strawberries
- almond paste

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with salt and milk
2. Make three or four bundles with butter
3. Place on a clean cloth
4. Arrange in alternating rows: one of pistachio cream, another of sweet cherries, another of apricots, and another of strawberries
5. Roll up as meatballs
6. Cut into pieces
7. Place each piece on its own base of cooked almond paste
8. Dust with fine sugar
9. Place under a cover with fire on top
10. Once browned, serve as an entrée
