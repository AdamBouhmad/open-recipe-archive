## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- braising liquid or butter
- stock
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- veal forcemeat
- bread crumb forcemeat
- butter
- egg yolks
- chives
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the chops in a braise, or if not, pass them through butter
2. Moisten with stock and white wine, seasoning with salt and pepper
3. Let them cook slowly
4. When cooked, bind the sauce with a little cullis
5. Line the bottom of the serving plate with veal forcemeat
6. Cover it with forcemeat made of bread crumbs, butter, egg yolks, chives, and parsley, all finely chopped and seasoned with little salt and pepper
7. Place the prepared plate on a gentle heat so the forcemeat sets
8. Drain the fat from it
9. Arrange the chops with their sauce on top
10. Serve hot with lemon juice
