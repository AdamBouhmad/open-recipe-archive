## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- old thick bacon for larding
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- two cloves of garlic
- half a quartilho of wine
- bacon slices for the bottom and top of the pan

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef and lard it with old thick bacon, seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, two cloves of garlic, and half a quartilho of wine
2. Season and marinate for three or four hours
3. Place everything in a pot with bacon slices below and above
4. Cook slowly between coals for five or six hours
5. When cooked and with little sauce, serve hot or cold as desired
