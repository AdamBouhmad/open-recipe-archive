## Ingredients

- bread crumb, one or two large slices
- beaten eggs
- clarified beef butter
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon, pounded

## Directions

1. Cut into slices the bread crumb of one or two large pieces of bread
2. Pass through beaten eggs
3. Fry in clarified beef butter
4. Pass a second time through eggs and fry again
5. When well-colored, trim and drain
6. Arrange on a plate and serve with powdered sugar on top, dusted with pounded cinnamon
