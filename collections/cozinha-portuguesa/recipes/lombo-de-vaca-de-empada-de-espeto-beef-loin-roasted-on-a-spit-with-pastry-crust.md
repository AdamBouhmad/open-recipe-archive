## Ingredients

- beef loin from preserved stock
- thin pastry dough
- butter

## Directions

1. After the beef loin has been preserved, roast it on a spit
2. When roasted, cover it with a thin sheet of dough
3. Wrap it in buttered paper and return it to the fire until the dough is cooked
4. When cooked, remove the paper, place it on a plate, and send to the table
5. This same method can be done with any roast
