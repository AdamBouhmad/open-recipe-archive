## Ingredients

- rabbit
- butter or rendered bacon
- salt
- pepper
- sage bundle
- fine herbs
- onion studded with cloves
- flour paste
- broth
- white wine
- truffles or cucumbers or braised artichokes
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbits and cut into quarters
2. Brown them in a pot with butter or rendered bacon, salt, pepper, a bundle of sage, and fine herbs, and an onion studded with cloves
3. When browned, dust with flour paste
4. Moisten with broth and a little white wine
5. Skim and let simmer gently
6. When almost cooked, add truffles, cucumbers, or braised artichokes if desired
7. When everything is cooked with little broth and good flavor, remove the fat and the sage bundle
8. Bind the fricassée with egg yolks mixed with a little broth and lemon juice
9. Serve hot
