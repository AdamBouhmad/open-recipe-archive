## Ingredients

- royal dough made with milk or water
- eggs
- pork or clarified beef lard
- sugar cooked to hardball stage

## Directions

1. Prepare royal dough with milk or water and break it up with eggs, as in the previous recipe
2. Pass through a syringe or other mold
3. Fry in pork lard or clarified beef lard
4. When fried and coated with sugar cooked to hardball stage, serve between meals
