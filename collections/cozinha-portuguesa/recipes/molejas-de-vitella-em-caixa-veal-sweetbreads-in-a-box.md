## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Oil or melted bacon with salt
- Onions
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Shallot
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Bacon sheet
- Lime juice

## Directions

1. After the sweetbreads are blanched, cut them into thin slices
2. Marinate them with little oil or melted bacon with salt, finely chopped onions, truffles, mushrooms, shallot, salt, pepper, and all well chopped
3. Make seven or eight paper boxes, grease the bottoms with oil, fill them with the sliced sweetbreads arranged properly with fine herbs
4. Cover with a sheet of bacon and roast them in the oven or under a lid or on grills with a sheet of paper greased underneath, and serve with lime juice
5. They can also be made with fine herbs cooked in a little broth and white wine, covered with a sheet of bacon, and after cooked, remove the fat and serve with the same sauce, adding a little broth
