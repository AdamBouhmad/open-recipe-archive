## Ingredients

- Half arratel of ground almonds
- One arratel of sugar in thread stage
- Five réis of grated bread
- A small amount of white honey
- Musk
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Half arratel of cow butter

## Directions

1. Cook in a pot half arratel of ground almonds with one arratel of sugar in thread stage, five réis of grated bread, a small amount of white honey, musk, cinnamon, cloves, and half arratel of cow butter.
2. When this becomes hard, shape the melícias in pig intestine.
3. Pass them through hot water and dry them in a reed basket.
4. They are eaten on fish days, roasted in a frying pan.
