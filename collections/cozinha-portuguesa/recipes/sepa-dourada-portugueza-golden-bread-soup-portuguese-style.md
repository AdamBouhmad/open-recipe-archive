## Ingredients

- bread crumb
- clarified butter
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the crumb into dice
2. Fry in clarified butter
3. Let drain after frying
4. Dip briefly in clarified sugar, nearly at point
5. When beginning to soften, let boil in the same sugar
6. When thickened, add eighteen egg yolks
7. Mix always with the spoon and in a gentle fire
8. After everything is mixed and set, serve with powdered cinnamon on top
