## Ingredients

- Lamb leg
- Pork belly lard
- Broth
- Parsley
- Marjoram
- Bay leaf
- Garlic
- Onion
- Cloves
- Salt
- Sauce
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese
- Cauliflower

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb leg with thick pork belly, place it to cook with broth, a bunch of parsley and marjoram, a bay leaf, a head of garlic, an onion with two cloves, and little salt
2. When cooked, strain the broth through a sieve and remove the fat from it
3. Add a spoonful of sauce and a piece of washed butter, tie everything together, and pour a little sauce in the bottom of the serving plate
4. Arrange the lamb leg on top garnished with pieces of cauliflower cooked separately, pour the remaining sauce over it, cover everything with grated cheese, and place it in the oven
5. When browned, clean the edge of the plate and serve as an appetizer
