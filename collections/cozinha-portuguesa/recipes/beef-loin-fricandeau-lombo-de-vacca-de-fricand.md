## Ingredients

- one or two beef loins
- bacon
- good broth
- a strip of bacon
- a piece of ham
- a bundle of herbs
- one onion with two cloves

## Directions

1. Take one or two beef loins, remove the skins, and beat them between two cloths with the back of a knife
2. Lard them with fine bacon, soak them, and then pass them quickly through boiling water
3. Remove them from the water and plunge them in cold water
4. Cook them in good broth with a strip of bacon, a piece of ham, a bundle of herbs, and an onion with two cloves
5. Once cooked, pass the broth through a sieve, remove the fat, and reduce it until thick
6. Then add the fricandeau with the bacon side facing down, and when it has a good color, remove it carefully
7. Loosen what remains stuck to the bottom of the casserole with a little broth
8. Add a little culinary salt, and once it tastes good, pass it through the sieve again and serve well hot with lemon juice
