## Ingredients

- One shad, scaled, cleaned, washed, and dried
- Butter or oil
- Onion slices
- Lemon slices
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, wash, and dry the shad
2. Make an incision along the sides from the head to the tail
3. Marinate it with butter or oil, slices of onion and lemon, salt, pepper, and fine herbs
4. After marinating a short time, place it on the grill on both sides
5. Baste occasionally with the same marinade
6. When roasted and of good color, serve with well-finished azeitão sauce or with crayfish and caper sauce
7. It can also be cut into portions and after being fried, served dry or in an escabeche
