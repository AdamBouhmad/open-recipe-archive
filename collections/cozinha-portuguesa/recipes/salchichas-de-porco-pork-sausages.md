## Ingredients

- two arratels of lean pork from the leg
- one arratel of fat pork
- salt
- fennel seed, finely ground
- one splash of white wine
- clean pork intestines

## Directions

1. Chop the lean pork from the leg and fat pork very finely together on a chopping block
2. Season with salt, finely ground fennel seed, and a splash of white wine
3. Fill clean pork intestines with the mixture
4. Pass the sausages through hot water first
5. Hang them in the chimney in a house to dry
6. Fry them in a pan and send to the table
