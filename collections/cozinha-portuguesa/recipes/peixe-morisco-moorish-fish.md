## Ingredients

- two fish steaks
- half an arratel of butter
- one quartilho of white wine
- two heads of garlic
- all seasonings whole
- lemon

## Directions

1. Put two fish steaks in a pan with half an arratel of butter, one quartilho of white wine, two heads of garlic, and all the seasonings whole
2. Cover the pan and seal it on the outside with dough so the steam does not escape
3. Cook over low heat
4. When cooked, place the fish on slices
5. Put lemon on top and serve
