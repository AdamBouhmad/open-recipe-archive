## Ingredients

- minced veal
- bacon scraped
- bread crumb moistened in milk
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg grated
- shallot
- sage minced
- Parmesan cheese grated
- cream filling
- brains filling
- herb fillings
- nata
- grated bread

## Directions

1. In place of chicken breasts, make a minced veal that will be pounded with scraped bacon, a bread crumb moistened in milk but squeezed, some egg yolks, seasoned with salt, pepper, nutmeg, shallot, and minced sage
2. After everything is well pounded, form dumplings of the same size as the preceding ones
3. Cook them and serve them over bread crusts fried in broth with the necessary broth and substances
4. They can also be served over bread crusts boiled in broth, covered with grated Parmesan cheese, and browned in the oven
5. The dumplings made with pasta can be made with various fillings such as creamed minced meat, brain filling Italian style, fillings with various herbs made with cream, cheese, grated bread and egg yolks as for ravioli
6. After being served in broth, they are served in soup bowls with succulent and good-tasting broths
