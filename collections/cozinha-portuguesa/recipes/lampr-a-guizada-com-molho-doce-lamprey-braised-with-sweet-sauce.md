## Ingredients

- One lamprey, bled and cleaned
- Red wine
- A piece of browned butter
- Sugar in stone form
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bleed, clean, and prepare the lamprey as in the previous recipe
2. Cut it into pieces
3. Simmer it in red wine with a piece of browned butter, sugar in stone form, cinnamon, and salt
4. When cooked, add the blood
5. Stir so that it does not curdle
6. Give it a simmer
7. When bound, serve with lemon juice
