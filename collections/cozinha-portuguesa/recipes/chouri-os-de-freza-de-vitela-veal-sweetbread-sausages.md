## Ingredients

- one veal intestine
- one half veal sweetbread
- three quartilhos of pork blood
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- parsley
- chives
- one clove of garlic
- intestines for filling
- broth and white wine

## Directions

1. Clean and blanch one veal intestine in water
2. Cook it in one half veal sweetbread
3. Once cooked and half-cooled, chop it
4. Place it in a casserole with three quartilhos of pork blood
5. Season with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, parsley, chives, and one clove of garlic
6. When it has good taste, fill the intestines, passing this through a funnel
7. Tie them as instructed
8. Cook them in a sauce with broth and white wine
9. Once cooked and cooled, store them
10. Serve as the preceding ones
