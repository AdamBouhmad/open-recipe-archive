## Ingredients

- two front quarters of lamb or kid goat
- veal mince
- chicken breast pieces
- ham
- string
- Italian mince made of different herbs, egg yolks, cream, cheese, breadcrumbs, and fine herbs

## Directions

1. Split two front quarters of lamb or kid goat and remove all the bones from the inside
2. Stuff with a mince of veal, in which you may mix some pieces of chicken breast and ham
3. Tie with string after stuffing, giving it the shape you wish, either round or long, but leave the bone of the tip of the shoulder
4. Place to cook in a white braise
5. When cooked, remove the string and serve with chopped parsley of good taste
6. You may also stuff with an Italian mince made of different herbs, egg yolks, cream, cheese, breadcrumbs, and fine herbs
7. The same can be done to front quarters of veal and serve with Italian sauce of good taste
