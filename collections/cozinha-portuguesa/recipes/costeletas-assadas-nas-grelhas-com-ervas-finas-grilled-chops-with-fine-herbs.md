## Ingredients

- one or two veal rib sections
- melted bacon
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- fine breadcrumbs
- shallot sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take one or two veal rib sections and cut into thin chops, slightly thinner than those described above
2. After preparation, soak in a casserole with melted bacon
3. Season with salt, pepper, minced parsley and chives
4. Pass through the fire and after passing, wrap in fine dry breadcrumbs
5. Grease and grill them on the grilling grate
6. Serve with shallot sauce and lemon juice
