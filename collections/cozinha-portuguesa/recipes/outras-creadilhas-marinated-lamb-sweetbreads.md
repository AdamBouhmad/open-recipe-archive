## Ingredients

- lamb sweetbreads
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion slices
- sauce
- fresh herbs
- beaten eggs
- fine grated bread

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and cut into quarters, marinate in vinegar, water, salt, pepper, onion slices, sauce, and fresh herbs
2. After marinating, dry, pass through beaten eggs and fine grated bread
3. Like the preceding ones, when browned, serve garnished with sauce as an entrée
