## Ingredients

- Capons or chickens
- Chopped liver and bacon
- Mushrooms
- Bacon sheets
- Paper
- Mushroom or morello ragú
- Bacon lard
- Cucumber ragú
- Artichoke hearts ragú
- Whole scallions ragú

## Directions

1. Mix chopped liver, scraped bacon, and chopped seasoned mushrooms
2. Stuff the capons and chickens
3. Place to roast covered with bacon sheets and paper
4. Once roasted, remove the sheets and paper
5. Serve with mushroom ragú or morello ragú, or after larding with thick bacon and cooking in a braise, serve with cucumber ragú, artichoke heart ragú, whole scallions ragú, or any other ragú desired
