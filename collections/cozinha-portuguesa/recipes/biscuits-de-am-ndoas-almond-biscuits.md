## Ingredients

- Half an arratél of almonds
- Fine sugar for grinding
- Six egg yolks
- Two ounces of flour
- Half a pound of fine sugar
- Eight well-beaten and raised egg whites
- Fine sugar mixed with flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and dry half an arratél of almonds, grind them in a stone mortar until they become very fine powder, adding from time to time a little fine sugar
2. When ground, pass them to a bowl, add six egg yolks, two ounces of flour, half a pound of fine sugar and beat for a quarter hour
3. After beating, add eight well-beaten and raised egg whites, mix everything very well
4. Pour into square paper forms, two fingers wide, and greased with butter inside
5. Pulverize with fine sugar mixed with flour and put them in a very mild oven
6. When cooked, remove the paper while hot and serve as necessary
