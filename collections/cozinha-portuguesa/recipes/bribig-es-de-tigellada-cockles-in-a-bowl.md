## Ingredients

- cockles
- water
- flour
- olive oil
- vinegar
- garlic
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the cockles very well from the sand, place them to open in a pot
2. When open, wash them again in clean water and dry them in a clean cloth
3. Coat them with flour, place them in the oven in a bowl with olive oil, vinegar, garlic, and lemon juice
4. After cooked, send them to the table in the same bowl
