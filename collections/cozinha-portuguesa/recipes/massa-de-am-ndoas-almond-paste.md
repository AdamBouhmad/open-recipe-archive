## Ingredients

- almonds
- hot water
- egg white
- a little orange blossom water
- very fine sugar

## Directions

1. Steep the almonds in hot water, peel them, pound them in a stone mortar, adding from time to time a little beaten egg white and a small spoonful of orange blossom water
2. When pounded and reduced to a paste, dry at the fire with very fine sugar, stirring constantly with a spoon until it separates from the pot
3. Spread on the table with powdered sugar, and it serves for tarts, tartlets, bases for croquants, boxes, small baskets, flowers, and other different works
4. The scraps pounded with egg whites to soften them serve to fry, to bake in the oven, and for various garnishes
