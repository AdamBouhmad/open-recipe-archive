## Ingredients

- royal dough
- eggs
- flour
- pork butter
- orange blossom water
- fine grated sugar
- sugar in thread

## Directions

1. Make the royal dough and break it up with eggs as I said in the chapter on doughs, put it on a plate dusted with flour, and fry in pork butter, cutting with the tip of the handle of the skimmer
2. When fried and of the same size, serve with orange blossom water and fine grated sugar, or sugar in thread
