## Ingredients

- five or six young pigeons
- green peas
- broth
- eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. After cleaning and straightening the pigeons, cook them in a casserole as described for the white sauce preparation
2. Add two arratels of green peas and a little broth
3. When cooked and of good flavor, thicken with eggs and milk, and serve hot
