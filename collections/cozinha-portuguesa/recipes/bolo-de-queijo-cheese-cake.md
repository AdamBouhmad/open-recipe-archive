## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh Alentejo cheese, well softened
- an equal amount of fresh butter
- one ounce of flour
- five or six eggs

## Directions

1. Pound in a stone mortar one pound of fresh Alentejo cheese, well softened, with an equal amount of fresh butter
2. After pounding, place on the table and add to it one ounce of flour and five or six eggs
3. Mix everything together
4. When the dough is bound, wrap it in a damp cloth for an hour
5. Remove it, form the cake, pour it out, bake in the oven, and serve plain
