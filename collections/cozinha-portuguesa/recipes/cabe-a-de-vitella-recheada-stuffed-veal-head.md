## Ingredients

- one well-blanched veal head, singed
- veal brains
- pieces of veal
- beef fat
- egg yolks
- veal tongue
- veal eyes
- twine
- broth
- one quart of white wine
- roots
- one bundle with fines herbes
- onion studded with cloves
- salt
- whole pepper

## Directions

1. Take a well-blanched and singed veal head, place it on a table with cloth underneath
2. Open it between the jaws, remove all the bones without damaging the skin of the upper part
3. Make a forcemeat with the brains, pieces of veal, and beef fat, well seasoned and bound with egg yolks, mixing in the tongue and eyes cut into dice
4. Fill the head with this mixture, giving it its same shape, tying it very well with twine on all sides so the forcemeat does not escape
5. Put it to cook in a pan with broth, one quart of white wine, some roots, one bundle with fines herbes, onion studded with cloves, salt, and whole pepper
6. When cooked, remove it and let it drain
7. When dry, remove the twine and serve with spicy sauce as above
8. One can also put inside and in the middle of the forcemeat a ragout of sweetbreads, truffles, ham, chicken breasts, or squab breasts, or any other that seems appropriate
