## Ingredients

- cow butter
- one onion, chopped and powdered with flour
- hot water or milk
- pepper
- eggs, cooked and hard, sliced in rounds
- egg yolks
- milk
- nutmeg, grated
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté in cow butter a chopped onion, dust with flour after sautéing
2. Add a little hot water or milk if available, season with pepper
3. After it has boiled and the sauce is reduced, cut the hard cooked eggs into rounds and place them to heat inside the sauce
4. Give it a boil and thicken with egg yolks mixed in milk, with grated nutmeg and lemon juice
5. Serve hot as an entrée
