## Ingredients

- Capons or chickens
- Three or four fresh walnuts
- Scraped bacon
- Salt
- Scallion
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Melted bacon fat
- Bacon sheets
- Ham essence
- Two dozen blanched walnuts
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take three or four fresh walnuts, peel and well crush them
2. Mix with scraped bacon, salt, scallion, salt, pepper, and few fine herbs
3. Stuff the chickens; close and secure in melted bacon fat
4. Cover with bacon sheets
5. Serve with ham essence sauce heated at the moment of serving, or within two dozen blanched walnuts cooked in water with lemon juice
