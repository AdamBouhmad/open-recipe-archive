## Ingredients

- veal cut into pieces
- bacon lardons
- parsley chopped
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- lemon juice
- garlic
- flour
- butter
- eggs
- clove

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into pieces, each piece larded with small pieces of bacon, seasoned as follows: chopped parsley, pepper, salt, a touch of vinegar, and place in a conserve of wine and garlic, or vinegar of garlic and bay leaves, for one or two days
2. Take some bacon and chop it with parsley, lemon juice with all the seasonings, pepper, and clove
3. Make a dough of good flour, kneading it with eggs, cow butter, and salt; the butter should be washed first
4. In the pie box place small pieces of bacon chopped with parsley and lemon, separately at the bottom, and above them the meat, and above the meat another layer of small pieces of bacon like those below
5. Cover with the top of the pie and bake
