## Ingredients

- a little veal meat
- beef fat
- pork belly
- salt
- false pepper
- minced shallot
- herbs
- fine herbs
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- four egg yolks
- a little grated nutmeg
- sheets of pork belly
- ragù of pigeons
- essence sauce
- cullis

## Directions

1. Take a little veal meat, beef fat, and pork belly, seasoned all with salt, false pepper, minced shallot, herbs, and fine herbs, and mince it all very well
2. Once minced, pound it in a mortar with a bread crumb soaked in milk, four egg yolks, and a little grated nutmeg
3. Line a forcemeat mold with sheets of pork belly
4. Place part of the forcemeat inside
5. Put in the middle a ragù of pigeons
6. Cover with the rest of the forcemeat and sheets of pork belly as below
7. Put to cook in the oven
8. When cooked, turn it out onto its plate
9. Remove the sheets of pork belly and all the fat
10. Serve with essence sauce and a little cullis as an entrance course
