## Ingredients

- chickens
- bacon
- ham
- veal slices
- beef slices
- paper leaves
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After blanching and tying the chickens with legs tucked inside, lard them with bacon and ham, seasoned as is customary
2. Line a casserole with bacon and veal slices beaten thin and thin
3. Place the chickens on top with breasts facing down, season them, and cover with other slices of beef and bacon, and paper leaves
4. Close the casserole with its lid and cook between two fires, but let it simmer gently
5. When cooked, remove them, place them to drain on a sieve, and serve with the ragout that seems best
