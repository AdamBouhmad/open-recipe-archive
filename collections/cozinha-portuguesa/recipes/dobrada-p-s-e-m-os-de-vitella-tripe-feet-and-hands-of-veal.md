## Ingredients

- Tripe or trips
- Veal feet
- Veal hands
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chopped parsley
- Broth
- Good vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe, hands, and feet of veal
2. Blanch them in hot water
3. Cook them in a white broth and serve naturally with salt, pepper, chopped parsley, little broth, and good vinegar
4. The same hands and feet, after being cooked and cut into pieces, can be served as fricassee
5. They can also be served as marinade and fried
6. The tripe after being cut into pieces and placed in a good-tasting sauce is served with bread and grated cheese, browned in the oven
