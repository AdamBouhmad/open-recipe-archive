## Ingredients

- one or two salmon steaks
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Season one or two salmon steaks with olive oil, salt, pepper, and fine herbs
2. Grill them on both sides on a grill
3. Once grilled, place on a plate over low heat with a piece of washed butter, onion, chopped parsley, and a drop of broth
4. Let it simmer, and once the butter is incorporated, serve with lemon juice
