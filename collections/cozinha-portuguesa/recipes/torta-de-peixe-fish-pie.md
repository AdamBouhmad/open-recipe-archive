## Ingredients

- small amount of parsley
- onion
- two portions of any fish
- vinegar
- black seasonings
- shrimp or clams or mussels
- four egg yolks
- pastry dough

## Directions

1. Chop a small amount of parsley and onion very well and put them to stew
2. After stewed, chop two portions of any fish very well and put them to stew in the same herbs with vinegar
3. When cooked, season with all the seasonings, add shrimp or clams or mussels, bind with four egg yolks, and set to cool
4. Make the dough as desired, cook the pie, and send it to the table
