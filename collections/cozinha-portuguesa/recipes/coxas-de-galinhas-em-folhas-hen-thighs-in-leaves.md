## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 hens
- partridge breasts
- ham
- veal sweetbreads
- truffles
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- shallots
- fine herbs
- scraped bacon
- pork fat
- anchovy sauce
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean 3 or 4 hens, singe and scald them, remove the legs leaving plenty of skin all around
2. Remove all bones except the tip of the leg bone
3. Make a hash of partridge breasts, ham, some veal sweetbreads, and truffles, all cut into small dice and seasoned with salt, pepper, parsley, shallots, fine herbs, and mixed with scraped bacon
4. Open the thighs on a napkin and stuff them with this mixture
5. Sew them around with fine twine
6. Sear them in melted pork fat and then cook in a well-maintained braising liquid
7. When cooked, drain the fat and serve with anchovy and caper sauce, or with Italian sauce, or any other good sauce
