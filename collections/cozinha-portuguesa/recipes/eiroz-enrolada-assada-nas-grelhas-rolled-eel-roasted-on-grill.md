## Ingredients

- rolled cooked eels
- melted butter
- bread crumbs
- rémoulade sauce

## Directions

1. Take the rolled eels after cooking
2. Dip in melted butter
3. Cover with bread crumbs
4. Roast on grill
5. Serve with rémoulade sauce
