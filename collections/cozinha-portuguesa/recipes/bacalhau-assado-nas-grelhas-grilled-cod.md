## Ingredients

- cod
- olive oil or melted butter
- bread crumbs
- orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. If the cod pieces are large, cut them in half; otherwise cut into various pieces
2. Cook them, and when cooked, transfer to a plate
3. Drain them and pass through olive oil or melted butter
4. Cover with grated bread
5. Grill on the grates
6. Serve with olive oil and orange or lemon juice
