## Ingredients

- One arratel of rice
- Half pint of water
- One arratel of sugar in thread stage
- Six egg yolks
- Flower water
- Cinnamon
- Puff pastry

## Directions

1. Soak one arratel of rice in half pint of water.
2. Cook it in one arratel of sugar in thread stage.
3. Thicken it with six egg yolks.
4. Add flower water and cinnamon and allow to cool.
5. Make the pie with puff pastry, bake it, and send to the table.
6. If made with milk, it is better.
