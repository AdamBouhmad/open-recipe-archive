## Ingredients

- one canada of cow's milk
- one arratel of sugar
- some egg yolks
- essence such as orange flower, lemon, or bergamot

## Directions

1. Pour one canada of cow's milk into a pot and let it boil for five or six minutes
2. Remove from heat and season with one arratel of sugar
3. Beat in some egg yolks
4. Pass through a sieve
5. Place on heat a second time to warm, and when it begins to thicken, remove from heat
6. Add a few drops of essence, such as orange flower, lemon, or bergamot
7. When cold, pour into a mold of tin foil
8. Place this mold inside a bucket with pounded snow, salt, or saltpetre underneath, on top, and all around
9. When frozen and ready to serve, pass the mold quickly through hot water, and detaching what is inside, turn it carefully onto the plate on which it is to be served, so it may be eaten at that instant
