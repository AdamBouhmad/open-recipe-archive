## Ingredients

- clarified butter
- brittle dough
- fine sugar
- almond paste
- fruit jellies

## Directions

1. Grease the outside of a round casserole with butter, cover with a sheet of brittle dough, cut as desired, sprinkle fine sugar in the gaps, and bake in the oven
2. When cooked and glazed, let cool
3. When serving, place the casserole over a small flame so it releases
4. Make a base of almond paste with fruit jellies the size of the plate, place the brittle on top, and serve
