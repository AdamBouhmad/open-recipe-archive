## Ingredients

- One and a half arratel of rye bread or grated bread
- One and a half arratel of fried cow butter
- One arratel of ground almonds
- Two and a half arratel of clarified sugar
- One and a half dozen eggs, beaten with half egg whites
- Seven eighths of pinched clove
- A little pepper
- Two fingers of cooked salt
- One eighth of pinched cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put one and a half arratel of rye bread or grated bread in a mortar and pass through a sieve.
2. Mix with one and a half arratel of fried cow butter and mix very well with a spoon.
3. Cover it for a bit.
4. Add one arratel of ground almonds and mix everything together.
5. Cook with two and a half arratel of clarified sugar, one and a half dozen eggs beaten with half egg whites, seven eighths of pinched clove, a little pepper, and two fingers of cooked salt.
6. Remove from heat and sprinkle one eighth of pinched cinnamon on top.
7. Shape the morcellas in straight pig intestine, not too full, and dry them in a reed basket in a place where they do not get smoke.
