## Ingredients

- one spoon of cullis of veal and ham
- a piece of fresh butter
- some fillets of orange peel
- juice of oranges squeezed
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a pan one spoon of cullis of veal and ham, add to it a piece of fresh butter, some fillets of orange peel, and the juice of squeezed oranges; season with pepper, and when bound, serve it for ducks and ducklings roasted, etc.
