## Ingredients

- fresh eggs from the same day
- water

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil
2. Place fresh eggs in boiling water
3. Leave on the heat for two minutes
4. Remove the pot from heat, cover with its lid, and leave the eggs inside for another minute
5. Remove the eggs and serve hot on a folded napkin
