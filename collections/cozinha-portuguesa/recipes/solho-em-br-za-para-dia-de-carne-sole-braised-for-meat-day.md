## Ingredients

- A portion of sole
- Lard of bacon
- Slices of bacon
- Veal
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs

## Directions

1. Take a portion of sole, larded with bacon
2. Line the bottom of a pan or casserole with slices of bacon and veal
3. Place the sole on top
4. Season with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
5. Cover with slices like those below
6. Cover with its lid
7. Place between two fires to braise
8. After it has braised, add a quarter litre of white wine and a little broth
9. Let it simmer little by little
10. When cooked, serve with Spanish sauce or sauce made with truffles, crayfish, capers, or as you prefer
