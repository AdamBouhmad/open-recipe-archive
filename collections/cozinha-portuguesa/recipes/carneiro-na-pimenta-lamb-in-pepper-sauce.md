## Ingredients

- one or two arratels of lamb from the leg
- a quarter of bacon
- three egg yolks
- aromatics
- vinegar
- spices
- butter from a cow

## Directions

1. Finely chop one or two arratels of cleaned lamb from the leg with a quarter of bacon, three egg yolks, and aromatics
2. Braise in a bowl
3. When nearly braised, add the necessary vinegar
4. When well cooked with little broth, season with ground spices
5. Bind with three egg yolks
6. Place on a plate greased with butter
7. Top with lemon juice and cinnamon
8. If the dish is to have egg yolks, poach them separately in a pan and place on top of the chopped meat
