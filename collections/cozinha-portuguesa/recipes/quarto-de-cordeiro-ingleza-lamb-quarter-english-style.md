## Ingredients

- front quarter of lamb or kid goat, roasted
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- green onion
- shallot
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- broth
- orange juice or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Once a front quarter of lamb or kid goat is roasted, spread a good amount of butter on it
2. Then dust it with a little flour and mix in parsley, green onion, and shallot, all chopped and seasoned with little salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
3. Place the kid goat quarter, taken from the spit, on the serving plate with a little broth
4. Lift the blade with the point of a knife from one side
5. Spread the butter on top and put the blade back in its place
6. Once the butter is melted and mixed with the broth so it does not break, serve hot with sour orange or lemon juice
