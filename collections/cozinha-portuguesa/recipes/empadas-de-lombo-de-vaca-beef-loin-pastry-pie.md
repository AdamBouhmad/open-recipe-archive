## Ingredients

- Beef loin, partially roasted and sliced, larded with salted and peppered fatback
- One eighth measure of flour
- Half arratel of butter
- Four eggs
- Half arratel of chopped fatback
- Pepper
- Clove
- Lemon juice
- Four dried bay leaves

## Directions

1. After the beef loin has been partially roasted, cut it into slices and lard it with salted and peppered fatback
2. Make a pastry crust from one eighth measure of flour kneaded with half arratel of butter and four eggs
3. Place the loin inside with half arratel of chopped fatback, pepper, clove, lemon juice, and four dried bay leaves
4. Cook the pastry pie and send it to the table when done
