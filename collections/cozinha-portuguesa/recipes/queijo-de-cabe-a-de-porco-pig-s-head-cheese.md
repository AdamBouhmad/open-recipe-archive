## Ingredients

- Pig's head
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Marjoram
- Cloves of India
- Coriander seeds
- Nutmeg
- Garlic
- Ham slices
- Parsley
- Water
- Broth
- White wine
- Onion
- Roots
- Tomato

## Directions

1. Take a pig's head, cut it, singe it, and clean it as described above
2. Open it from the bottom and remove all the bones
3. Soak it for a while to remove the blood and when dry, lift all the meat and bacon without damaging the skin
4. Cut the lean meat into thin sheets, do the same with the fat, cut the ears into fillets, season everything with salt, pepper, thyme, bay leaves, marjoram, cloves of India, coriander seeds, nutmeg, all well crushed, and add two cloves of garlic
5. When seasoned, place the skin of the head in a casserole, arrange the sheets of meat on top interspersed with the fat, some slices of ham, and pieces of parsley, with the meat colored and almost filling the skin
6. Prick all around with a needle, and after closing, wrap it in a cloth and tie it very well with twine
7. Cook it for six hours in water, broth, white wine with whole salt, pepper, cloves, rounds of onion, roots, garlic, thyme, bay leaves, marjoram
8. After cooked and almost cold, remove it, drain it a little, press it in a press or place it under a heavy weight, and when well pressed, remove it, trim it, and serve it on a napkin garnished with parsley and rounds of ham or ham slices
