## Ingredients

- eels or angulas
- water
- butter
- spice
- fine herbs
- studded onion
- flour
- broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- milk
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the eels or angulas
2. Remove the spines from the sides
3. Cut into equal pieces
4. Blanch in water
5. Stew in butter with a spice, fine herbs, and a studded onion
6. Dust with flour
7. After stewing, add a spoonful of broth and half a quartilho of white wine
8. Skim, simmer slowly
9. Season with salt and pepper
10. When cooked and sauce reduced, bind with egg yolks beaten in milk
11. Serve hot with lemon juice
