## Ingredients

- Poultry (galeteirões)
- Liver from the poultry
- Scraped lard (toucinho)
- Parsley (salsa)
- Small onions (cebozinhas)
- Truffles
- Salt
- Pepper
- Three or four egg yolks
- Slices of lard
- Slices of veal or beef
- One spoonful of broth
- One spoonful of white wine
- One spoonful of ham coulis (culi de presunto)
- One splash of vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the innards from the cleaned poultry, keeping the legs inside the body
2. Stuff with a finely chopped mixture made from the poultry's livers, scraped lard, parsley, small onions, and truffles, all finely minced and seasoned with salt and pepper, bound together with three or four egg yolks
3. Close the opening with thread
4. Place in a braise with slices of lard and slices of veal or beef, seasoned and of good flavor
5. Cook between two fires
6. Add one spoonful of broth and one spoonful of white wine
7. Let simmer for four or five hours
8. When cooked, add one spoonful of ham coulis and one splash of vinegar to the bottom of the braise
9. Strain this sauce through a sieve
10. Serve hot as an entrée
