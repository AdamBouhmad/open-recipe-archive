## Ingredients

- Capons or chickens
- Bacon sheets
- Paper
- Fine herbs
- Butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open, clean, and prepare the capons or chickens, place on the spit covered with bacon sheets and paper, and roast
2. Once roasted and paper removed, placed on its plate and the breast chiseled as is customary to do to ducks, serve with hot remoulade sauce made with all kinds of fine herbs, bound with a piece of fresh butter and lemon juice
