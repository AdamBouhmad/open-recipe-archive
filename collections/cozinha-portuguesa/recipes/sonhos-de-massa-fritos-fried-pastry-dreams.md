## Ingredients

- royal dough made with eggs
- flour
- pork lard
- orange flower water
- grated sugar or sugar cooked to hardball stage

## Directions

1. Prepare royal dough and break it up with eggs, as described in the Chapter on Doughs
2. Place in a dish and sprinkle with flour
3. Fry in pork lard, cutting with the tip of a skimmer handle
4. When fried and of the same size, serve with orange flower water and finely grated sugar or sugar cooked to hardball stage
