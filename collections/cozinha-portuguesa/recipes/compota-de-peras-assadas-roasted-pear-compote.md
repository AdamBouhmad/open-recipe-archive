## Ingredients

- Six or eight pears for cooking, before ripening
- Clarified sugar
- A piece of cinnamon
- Three or four cloves

## Directions

1. Take six or eight pears for cooking, before they are ripe, and place them one after another in a well-heated oven, turning them from time to time so they roast evenly.
2. When placed in water and rubbed with a cloth, remove all the skin.
3. Cut them in half, clean them inside, wash them in three or four waters, and put them to cook in clarified sugar with a piece of cinnamon and three or four cloves.
4. Cover very well with a lid and let them simmer little by little until cooked.
5. Arrange them on their plate, reduce the sugar, pour the reduced sugar over the pears, and serve hot if desired.
