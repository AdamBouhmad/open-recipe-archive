## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Water
- White broth
- Butter
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Blanch in water and cook in white broth
2. Strain over a sieve
3. Arrange on a plate and serve with butter sauce bound with pepper and vinegar
4. After cooking and arranging on the plate with butter and grated cheese underneath, cover with sauce and grated cheese, brown in the oven, and serve for a course between meals
