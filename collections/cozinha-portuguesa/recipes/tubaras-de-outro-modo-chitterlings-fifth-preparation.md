## Ingredients

- lamb chitterlings
- pork fat (toucinho)
- pepper
- eggs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the chitterlings into finger-sized pieces
2. Fry in pork fat
3. Top with pepper and two eggs
4. Form into a cartouche like a filhós
5. Serve with lemon on top
