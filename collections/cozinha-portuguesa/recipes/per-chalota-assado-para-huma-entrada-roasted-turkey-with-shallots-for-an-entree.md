## Ingredients

- young tender turkey
- turkey liver
- bacon
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- shallots
- fresh butter
- white bacon
- paper leaves

## Directions

1. Begin with a young, tender turkey
2. After cleaning very well, make a forcemeat from its own liver and other livers with scraped bacon, parsley, chives, salt, pepper, fine herbs, shallots, and fresh butter, all finely chopped
3. Stuff this mixture inside the turkey
4. Sear it in a casserole with melted white bacon that is very clean
5. When cooled, tie it to a spit covered with bacon strips and paper leaves
6. When roasted, place on a plate with essence sauce, finely chopped shallots, and lemon juice
