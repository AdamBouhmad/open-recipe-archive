## Ingredients

- one chicken
- half arratel of pork
- two ounces of butter
- vinegar
- aromatics
- all seasonings
- one quartilho of water
- grated bread crumbs
- twelve egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place one chicken to cook with half arratel of pork, two ounces of butter, vinegar, aromatics, all seasonings, and one quartilho of water
2. When the water is spent and the substance remains, carve the chicken
3. Without chopping the giblets, chop more meats on the block with pork and aromatics
4. Once very well chopped, put it in a bowl and season again with all ground seasonings, the crumb of ten réis of grated bread, and the chicken giblet separated into thick strips, and twelve egg yolks
5. Once everything is beaten so that it becomes well fine, put it in a frying pan with melting butter by spoonfuls, frying each spoonful on itself, and placing it on a plate upon sliced larded bread
6. After the breast is full, place lemon juice on top and send it to the table
