## Ingredients

- oysters
- white wine
- garlic
- onions
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- lime
- flour
- eggs
- white wine
- clarified butter
- fried sauce

## Directions

1. Open and blanch the oysters as in the preceding recipe
2. Clean them and marinate in white wine with garlic, onions, fine herbs, salt, pepper, and lime slices
3. After marinating the necessary time, drain and dry in a cloth
4. Pass through a batter made with flour, eggs, and white wine
5. Fry in clarified butter until golden
6. Serve as an entrée garnished with fried sauce in a circle around them
