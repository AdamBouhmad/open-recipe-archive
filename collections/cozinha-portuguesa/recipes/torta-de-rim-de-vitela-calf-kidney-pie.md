## Ingredients

- One calf liver
- Bound cream
- Biscuits of almond paste
- Peel of orange, citron and lemon
- Six egg yolks with well-beaten egg whites
- Sugar
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Roast a calf liver, remove the kidney with all its fat, let it cool
2. Mince it well finely; when minced, grind it with the same amount of bound cream, almond biscuits, minced peels of orange, citron and lemon
3. After everything is ground, add six egg yolks, sugar and well-beaten egg whites
4. Spread a piece of puff pastry dough on a baking dish, form its border of two fingers width of the same dough, place the mixture in the middle
5. Put in the oven to bake
