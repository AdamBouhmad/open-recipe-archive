## Ingredients

- one turkey without bones
- bacon lardons
- salt
- pepper
- one quarter of flour

## Directions

1. Make a turkey without bones in the same manner as chicken without bones by larding them very well with seasoned bacon lardons
2. Knead one quarter of flour, making the dough quite tender, place the turkey in it, seasoning it on the outside, close the pie very well
3. Place the turkey's feet in one pot and the head with beak in another, so that it appears alive, and after the pie is baked on gentle heat, serve to the table
