## Ingredients

- Two dozen marrow bones
- One arratel of sugar in thread stage
- One dozen egg yolks
- Bread crumbs
- One eighth of flour
- One quarter of sugar
- Four eggs
- Pork butter
- One splash of white wine
- Pork butter for frying
- One arratel of sugar for coating
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pass two dozen marrow bones through hot water and cut into small pieces
2. Cook in one arratel of sugar in thread stage
3. When cooked, add one dozen egg yolks beaten with a little bread crumb to thicken, and cook
4. Let cool
5. Meanwhile, knead one eighth of flour with one quarter of sugar, four eggs, pork butter, and one splash of white wine, kneading very well
6. Make small pastries, cut with a pastry wheel, fry in pork butter, pass through one arratel of sugar and cover with cinnamon
7. Serve
