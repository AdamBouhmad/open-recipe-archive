## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- eight whole eggs
- three quarters of fine sugar
- one pound of butter
- lemon zest
- orange flower, chopped
- water

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of flour, eight whole eggs, three quarters of fine sugar, one pound of butter, lemon zest, and chopped orange flower with water
2. After everything is kneaded and worked, extend it so it becomes the thickness of one inch
3. Place it on buttered paper
4. Bake it in the oven
5. After it is baked, serve it glazed with sugar and pass a hot spatula over it
