## Ingredients

- Cream
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Butter
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat a small amount of cream with eggs and sugar until the mixture is well beaten.
2. Pour it into a low baking pan, greased with butter, no more than a finger's height.
3. Cook it over low heat, with heat from below and above.
4. Allow it to cool.
5. Once cold, make three cuts lengthwise and three crosswise so that the fritters are square.
6. Coat them with beaten eggs and egg whites.
7. Fry them in good butter and pass them through sugar in thread stage.
8. Place them over slices of bread of ló, with cinnamon on top, and send hot to the table.
9. This can also be made with grated bread or flour, and similarly tortijas can be made with curd cheese.
