## Ingredients

- eggplants
- water
- salt
- flour
- frying pan
- melted butter
- vinegar
- honey
- clove

## Directions

1. Cook the eggplants in quarters and scald them inside
2. Stew them a little, cook them in water and salt, and after squeezing and flouring with flour
3. Place them in a frying pan (not one on top of another)
4. Pour melted butter on top to coat them all, and a sauce of vinegar tempered with honey, as much as is needed to make it sweet, and ground clove
5. Cook in the oven and serve at table with cinnamon on top
