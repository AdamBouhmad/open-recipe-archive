## Ingredients

- one lamprey
- wine
- vinegar
- orange
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- pastry dough

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the lamprey
2. Put it in a shallow pan to drain the blood, striking it five or six times so it can curl
3. Season it with one cup of wine, half a cup of vinegar, orange, salt, pepper, clove, and nutmeg
4. Make a firm pastry box
5. Put the lamprey with sauce inside it
6. Cook it
7. When it begins to boil, remove the sauce through a hidden hole
8. When it finishes cooking, serve the pie and the sauce in a small bowl
