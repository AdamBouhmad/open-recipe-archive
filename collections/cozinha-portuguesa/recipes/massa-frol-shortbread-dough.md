## Ingredients

- flour
- fine sugar
- egg yolks
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of flour and fine sugar, knead with egg yolks and fresh butter
2. When formed, extend with the rolling pin, cut into pieces as desired, and bake in a gentle oven
3. When baked, glaze with sugar using a hot spatula, and serve for various inter-course dishes
