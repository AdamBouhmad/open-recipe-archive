## Ingredients

- Gannets
- Bacon or lard with pieces of eels and fish roe
- Water
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeat
- Fine herbs bouquet
- Onions studded with cloves
- Butter
- Butter sauce
- Parsley
- Fish roe, chopped
- Vinegar
- Capers

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the gannets, remove the entrails, position the feet behind the thighs, tie with string
2. Clean and wipe them
3. Lard them with pieces of eels and fish roe
4. Place them to cook in an adobo made of water, white wine, salt, pepper, nutmeat, a bouquet of fine herbs, onions studded with cloves, and butter
5. Allow to simmer little by little
6. Once cooked, remove from the adobo and serve with a sauce of butter, parsley, and chopped fish roe, vinegar, and its capers
