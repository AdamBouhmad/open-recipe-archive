## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- clarified butter
- fine sugar
- lemon or orange rind

## Directions

1. Make a royal dough with a pound of flour mixed with eggs as for dreams
2. Line a casserole inside with clarified butter, add the dough, and bake in the oven for two hours
3. When cooked, cut in half with a knife point
4. Remove all the raw dough from inside, line with clarified butter, dust with fine sugar and finely chopped lemon or orange rind
5. Place one piece on top of the other, spread butter on the outside, cover with sugar, brown with a hot bread paddle, and serve as a between-course dish
