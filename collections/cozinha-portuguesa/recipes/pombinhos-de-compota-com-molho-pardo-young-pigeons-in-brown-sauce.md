## Ingredients

- young pigeons
- bacon
- butter
- ham
- parsley
- herbs
- clove-studded onion
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- beef substance
- white wine
- cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning the pigeons and tucking the feet inside, brown them over the fire with rendered bacon or butter, a slice of ham, a sprig of parsley, and fine herbs
2. Dust with a little flour after browning, add a little broth, a spoonful of beef substance, and a little white wine
3. Let simmer slowly and gently until cooked, reduce the sauce, remove all fat, finish thickening with a little cullis
4. Serve hot with lemon juice
