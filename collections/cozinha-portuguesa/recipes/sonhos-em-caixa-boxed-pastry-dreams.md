## Ingredients

- cream made from rice flour
- flour from rice flour
- eggs
- milk
- salt
- fine sugar
- ground cinnamon
- almond biscuits
- lemon peel
- covered orange flower
- butter, oil, or lard, very hot
- sugar
- paper boxes

## Directions

1. Make a cream with rice flour, prepared the same way and seasoned like the white almond paste dreams
2. Fill small paper boxes the length of a finger and width of two fingers with this cream
3. Fry in very hot butter, oil, or lard
4. When fried and the paper removed, serve glazed on the bottom with sugar and a hot spatula
