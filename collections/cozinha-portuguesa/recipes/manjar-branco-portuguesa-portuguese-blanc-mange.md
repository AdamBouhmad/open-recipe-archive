## Ingredients

- One canful of milk
- Five quarters of rice flour
- Breast of a half-cooked hen
- Salt
- One and a half pounds of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve in a pot with one canful of milk five quarters of rice flour, to which add the breast of a half-cooked hen
2. Having well minced it, season with salt and one and a half pounds of powdered sugar
3. After everything is mixed and strained through a sieve, place on high heat, stirring always with great speed with a copper spatula
4. As it thickens, add gradually another canful of milk
5. When the flour is cooked and at the proper consistency, before it cools, pour into small dishes
6. Put in the oven to brown, and serve hot or cold, as desired
