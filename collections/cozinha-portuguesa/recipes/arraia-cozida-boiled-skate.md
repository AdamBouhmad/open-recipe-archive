## Ingredients

- skate
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- butter
- capers
- anchovies
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the skate in two halves
2. Remove the liver and entrails
3. Wash very well
4. Simmer five or six minutes in water, salt, and a little vinegar
5. Remove the skin from both sides with the back of a knife
6. Clean up, place on plate
7. Serve with bound sauce made with butter, capers, anchovies, pepper, and a drop of vinegar
