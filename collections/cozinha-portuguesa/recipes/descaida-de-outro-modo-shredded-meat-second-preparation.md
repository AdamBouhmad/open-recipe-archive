## Ingredients

- Descaida meat, shredded
- egg yolks
- chicken ovary
- broth from the pan
- cinnamon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the shredded descaida as described above
2. Fill the chicken ovary with it, making small sausages
3. Place them in a small pot with a drop of broth from the pan
4. Season as with almonds
5. Bind with an egg yolk
6. Place on slices of bread and serve with cinnamon and lemon juice on top
