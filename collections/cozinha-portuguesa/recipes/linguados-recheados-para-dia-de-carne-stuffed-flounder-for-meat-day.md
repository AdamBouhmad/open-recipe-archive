## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 flounders
- bacon strips
- onion slices
- ham slices
- chicken breasts
- fish loins
- calf udders, cooked
- bacon
- beef fat
- bread soaked in milk
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- Spanish sauce or clear essence sauce
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Clean, prepare, and remove the backbones from 2 or 3 flounders as described above
2. Line the bottom of a baking dish with bacon strips, onion slices, and some ham slices
3. Place the flounders over all this
4. Stuff them with a forcemeat made of chicken breasts, fish loins fried, cooked calf udders, bacon, and beef fat, all seasoned and bound with breadcrumbs soaked in milk and egg yolks
5. When stuffed, cover with grated bread
6. Place in the oven to cook
7. After cooked and browned, serve with Spanish sauce or clear essence sauce with lime juice
