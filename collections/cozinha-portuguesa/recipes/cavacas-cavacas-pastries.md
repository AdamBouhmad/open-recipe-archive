## Ingredients

- half alqueire of sifted flour
- one arratel of butter, melted and desalted
- salt dissolved in flower water
- one dozen eggs

## Directions

1. Sift half alqueire of flour and place in a bowl
2. Pour around the flour one arratel of butter that is melted on the fire to remove the salt, and work the flour in the hands
3. When well worked, make a well where you will pour salt dissolved in flower water and one dozen eggs
4. Beat with the hand and gradually work in the flour, adding eggs little by little, and kneading
5. The dough should not be stiff but soft and dry
6. Cut the dough into pieces the size that the cavacas are to be made
7. Place on a cloth and when stretching do not knead, but stretch lightly with light fingers on basins
8. Well pricked, send to the oven
