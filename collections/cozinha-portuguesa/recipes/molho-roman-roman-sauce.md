## Ingredients

- Veal substance and pork essence
- Roots
- Onions in rings
- White wine, one cup
- Two spoonfuls of culls
- Capers
- Four shallots
- Two cloves of garlic
- Coriander seed
- Two lemon rings without skin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two cloves

## Directions

1. Guard the substance of a casserole with veal and pork, roots, and onions in rings, and put on the fire to brown little by little
2. When the substance is browning, add one cup of white wine and two spoonfuls of culls, add capers, four shallots, two cloves of garlic, coriander seed, two lemon rings without skin, season with salt, pepper, and two cloves, and let it cook little by little
3. When reduced, bind with a little culls, and of good taste, remove the fat, strain through a sieve, and serve for what is necessary
