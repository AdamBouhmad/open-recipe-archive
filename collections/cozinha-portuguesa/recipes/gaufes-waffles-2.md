## Ingredients

- Three or four fresh eggs
- Equal amount of flour to the eggs
- A little orange flower water
- A little finely chopped lime peel
- Half a quart of good milk

## Directions

1. Break three or four fresh eggs into a bowl, dissolve them with the same amount of flour, a little orange flower water and a little finely chopped lime peel
2. After dissolving, pour half a quart of good milk, mix everything with a spoon until the mixture is well liquid and at its proper point
3. When ready, heat the iron in a well-lit furnace, pass it with wax on the part
