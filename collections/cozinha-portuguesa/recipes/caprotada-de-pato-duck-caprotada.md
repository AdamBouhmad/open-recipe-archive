## Ingredients

- one roasted duck, made into pieces
- two spoonfuls of fat broth from the pot
- quarter wheel of Além-Tejo cheese, grated
- parsley, chopped
- one more spoonful of broth

## Directions

1. After the duck is roasted and cut into pieces, place a plate or frying pan on bread slices
2. Pour two spoonfuls of fat broth from the pot on top
3. Cover with a quarter of grated Além-Tejo cheese mixed with chopped parsley
4. Moisten with another spoonful of broth on top
5. Place to bubble over hot coals
6. When dried, send to table
