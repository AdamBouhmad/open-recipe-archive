## Ingredients

- dough
- sugar, half the amount
- flour, half the amount
- orange flower
- lemon peel
- egg whites
- clarified butter
- grated sugar or sugar cooked to hardball stage

## Directions

1. Make a dough with half sugar and half flour, orange flower, a little lemon peel, and egg whites
2. After mixing and kneading everything, stretch with a rolling pin
3. Cut into equal patterns as you prefer
4. Fry in clarified butter and serve glazed with grated sugar or sugar cooked to hardball stage with a hot spatula
