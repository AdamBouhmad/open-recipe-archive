## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Salt
- Vinegar or lemon
- Honey or sugar
- Roasted wheat flour
- Broth
- Fried eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Once the carrots are braised, place them to cook in a pot with hot water.
2. Season with salt, vinegar or lemon, all the seasonings, honey or sugar, well-roasted wheat flour, and a small amount of broth in which they will cook.
3. Once cooked, send to the table.
4. Fried eggs can also be added if desired.
