## Ingredients

- thistles
- herbs
- butter
- egg yolks
- eggs

## Directions

1. After cooking the thistles in water, chop them very well
2. Sauté them with herbs and butter, seasoning with all the seasonings
3. Bind them with egg yolks (like minced mutton)
4. Put them in a frying pan, and with beaten eggs on top, place them to brown
5. Serve at table
