## Ingredients

- young pigeons
- bacon, melted
- a small piece of onion
- parsley, chopped
- made parsley sauce, a small piece
- all black seasonings
- a measure of white wine
- a measure of vinegar
- a small piece of well-browned flour

## Directions

1. Half-fry young pigeons in melted bacon
2. Remove the pigeons from the bacon
3. In the same bacon, fry a small piece of onion and chopped parsley
4. When fried, add it to a bowl with a small piece of made parsley sauce, all black seasonings, a measure of white wine, and a measure of vinegar
5. Place the pigeons on top and finish cooking them in this sauce
6. Make the sauce thicker with a small piece of well-browned flour
7. Place on slices and serve to the table
