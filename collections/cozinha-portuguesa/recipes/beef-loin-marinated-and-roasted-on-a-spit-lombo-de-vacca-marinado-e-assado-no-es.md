## Ingredients

- beef loin, tender
- fine bacon
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- onion slices
- garlic cloves
- shallot sauce

## Directions

1. Take a tender beef loin and after trimming, lard it with fine bacon in the form of fricandeau
2. Marinate it in a little vinegar, salt, pepper, bay leaf, onion slices, and some garlic cloves
3. After it has been in the marinade for two or three hours, remove it, place it on a spit covered with sheets of paper, and roast it
4. Once well roasted but not too dry, serve it with a piquant shallot sauce
