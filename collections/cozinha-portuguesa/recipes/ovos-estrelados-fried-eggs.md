## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a small amount of butter and melt it in a frying pan.
2. Pour the eggs into it, being careful not to break the yolks.
3. Using a spoon, pour the egg whites over the yolks until they finish setting.
4. Sprinkle pepper on top and add lemon juice.
5. Send to the table.
