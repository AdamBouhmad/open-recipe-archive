## Ingredients

- fish fillets
- salt
- pepper
- shallot
- nutmeg, grated
- butter, washed
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- bread crumb cooked in milk
- fine breadcrumb

## Directions

1. Take fish fillets, pound in a stone mortar and season with salt, pepper, shallot, grated nutmeg, a piece of washed butter bound with egg yolks and beaten egg whites, and bread crumb cooked in milk
2. After everything is pounded and mixed, place the slices in a buttered baking dish
3. Garnish with the chopped mixture, cover with fine breadcrumb
4. Bake in the oven or under a lid
5. When cooked and browned, serve with clear sauce on top and lemon juice as an entrée dish
