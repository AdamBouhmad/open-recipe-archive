## Ingredients

- two arratels of mutton chopped and seasoned
- vinegar
- all seasonings chopped
- one cooked cow tongue
- one arratel of ham
- one quarter of bacon
- lemon juice
- one quarter of flour
- one arratel of butter
- one half arratel of sugar
- six eggs
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Sauté two arratels of mutton very well chopped and seasoned with vinegar and all the seasonings chopped
2. Cook one cow tongue, one arratel of ham in a pot, sautéed in one quarter of bacon, seasoned with all the seasonings and plenty of lemon, let all cool
3. Knead one quarter of flour with one arratel of butter, one half arratel of sugar, six eggs, and one cup of white wine, when very well kneaded, make two crusts
4. On one place the chopped meat, above it the pigeons, then the tongue, and above the tongue the ham, and perfect the pie by covering with another crust, making a border around it, bake it
5. When baked, brush with beaten egg, cover with powdered sugar, let brown, and serve to the table
