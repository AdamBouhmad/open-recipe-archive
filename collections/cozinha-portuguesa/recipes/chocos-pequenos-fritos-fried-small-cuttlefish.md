## Ingredients

- small cuttlefish the size of walnuts
- salt
- flour
- olive oil or butter
- fried saffron

## Directions

1. Select small cuttlefish the size of walnuts
2. Remove only the tripe and ink sac
3. Wash very well
4. Sprinkle with finely ground salt
5. Dry well in a cloth
6. Flour them
7. Fry in very hot olive oil or butter over a lively flame
8. Serve garnished with fried saffron
