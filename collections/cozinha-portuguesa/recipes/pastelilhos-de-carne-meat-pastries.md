## Ingredients

- A piece of veal
- Beef fat
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt, pepper, nutmeg grated
- Three eggs
- A little milk
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Make a chopped mixture with a piece of veal, beef fat, parsley, chives
2. Season with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
3. Bind with three eggs and a little milk
4. Take as much as a walnut and place in the middle of forms lined with puff pastry underneath and on top sealed with beaten eggs
5. After gilding, cook in the oven
6. Serve hot
