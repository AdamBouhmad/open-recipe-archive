## Ingredients

- whole pears
- boiling water
- cold water
- clarified sugar
- slice of lemon

## Directions

1. Take the pears whole, place them in boiling water, and when they are firm and almost cooked, place them in cold water, peel them, and arrange them whole or cut in half or in quarters in another water
2. Then put them to cook in clarified sugar with a slice of lemon so they remain whiter
3. When cooked, arrange them on their plate, reduce the sugar to the necessary point, pour it over them and serve hot or cold, as preferred
