## Ingredients

- pigeons
- marinade
- egg whites
- flour
- pork lard
- fried sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the pigeons in half, flatten them, and marinate them as mentioned in marinated chickens and serve the same way
2. You can also cook them in the same marinade, and after cooking, pass through egg whites, flour, fry them, and serve garnished with fried sauce
