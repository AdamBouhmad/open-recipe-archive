## Ingredients

- one silver or other heat-proof plate
- two handfuls of grated breadcrumb interior
- one piece of butter or scraped bacon
- four raw egg yolks
- salt
- chopped chives
- a little broth tempered with salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take a silver plate or other heat-proof plate and place at the bottom a little forcemeat made with two handfuls of grated breadcrumb interior, one piece of butter or scraped bacon, four raw egg yolks, salt, chopped chives, and a little broth tempered with salt and pepper
2. Mix everything well and line the bottom of the plate
3. Place over gentle heat until it sets
4. Drain off the fat and arrange the cooked sheep tongues on top with their sauce from above or any other sauce that seems better
5. They can also be served after cooking on a silver plate with a little broth, grated cheese, and browned in the oven
