## Ingredients

- Five or six artichoke hearts, cut into pieces
- Half a dozen small green onions
- Half an eighth of peas
- Some asparagus tips
- Butter
- One spoonful of vegetable broth
- One spoonful of vegetable substance
- Slices of bread fried in butter
- A few leaves
- One bunch of chopped and pressed chervil

## Directions

1. Cut the artichoke hearts into pieces and chop the small green onions
2. Sauté everything in butter
3. After sautéing, add one spoonful of vegetable broth and another of vegetable substance
4. Let it simmer little by little
5. Soak slices of bread fried in butter with a few leaves and one bunch of chopped and pressed chervil in the mentioned broth and substance
6. After the bread is cooked and dried, place the herbs and peas on top
7. Remove the fat
8. If it tastes good, serve hot
