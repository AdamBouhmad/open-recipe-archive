## Ingredients

- a quarter of finely chopped bacon
- aromatics
- two arratels of half-roasted lamb cut into small pieces
- seasonings
- four egg yolks
- butter
- cinnamon
- lemon

## Directions

1. Braise a quarter of finely chopped bacon with aromatics
2. When braised, add two arratels of half-roasted lamb cut into small pieces
3. Cook very well
4. When cooked, season with all seasonings
5. Bind with four egg yolks
6. Place on a plate greased with butter
7. Top with cinnamon and lemon
