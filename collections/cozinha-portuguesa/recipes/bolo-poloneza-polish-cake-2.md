## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- some milk
- half a dozen eggs with their egg whites
- half a pound of melted beef fat
- another half pound of chopped Alicante raisins
- a drop of spirits
- salt
- a small amount of chopped coriander seed

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a pound of flour in some milk
2. After being well dissolved, add to it half a dozen eggs with their egg whites, half a pound of melted beef fat, another half pound of chopped Alicante raisins, a drop of spirits
3. Season it with salt and a small amount of chopped coriander seed
4. After everything is well mixed, pour it into a casserole greased inside with butter
5. Put it to cook in the oven for an hour and a half
6. When cooked and well colored, serve it hot, dusted with fine sugar
