## Ingredients

- Hare loins
- Butter or melted bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sausage
- Shallots
- Truffles, all chopped
- Bacon, pounded with hare liver
- Fine herbs
- Whole onion studded with cloves
- Bay leaf
- Ham essence
- Bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Pass hare loins through fire in a pan with butter or melted bacon, salt, pepper, sausage, shallots, and truffles, all chopped, and let cool
2. Line the bottom of the pie with bacon pounded with hare liver, seasoned with fine herbs
3. Place the loins on top with a whole onion studded with cloves and a bay leaf
4. Cover with the remaining chopped mixture and some bacon slices
5. Finish the pie according to custom
6. When cooked, open it to drain the fat
7. Add a sauce of ham essence with bitter orange juice
8. Serve hot
