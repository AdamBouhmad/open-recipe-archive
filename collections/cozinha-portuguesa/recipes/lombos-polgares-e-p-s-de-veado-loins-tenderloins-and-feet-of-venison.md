## Ingredients

- loins of venison
- fine lard
- paper with butter
- meat essence sauce
- soft roe
- capers
- vinegar
- vinegar
- sugar
- little salt
- four cloves
- cinnamon
- limes

## Directions

1. Lift the loins from one end to the other and after trimming all the skins, lard them with fine lard
2. Put them on a skewer, wrap them in buttered paper, tie them to a large spit, and put them to roast
3. When roasted, remove the paper and having taken on good colour, serve them with meat essence sauce with soft roe, capers, and a touch of vinegar
4. They can also be made with a sweet sauce made with vinegar, sugar, little salt, four cloves, cinnamon, and a little limes
