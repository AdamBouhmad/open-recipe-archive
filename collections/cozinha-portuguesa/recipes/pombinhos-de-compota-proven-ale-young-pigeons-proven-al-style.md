## Ingredients

- half a dozen young pigeons
- olive oil
- parsley
- green onions
- marjoram
- cloves
- garlic
- flour
- broth
- white wine
- truffle slices
- mushrooms
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen young pigeons, prepare them the same way as the preceding recipes, but using olive oil instead of butter
2. Add a bouquet of parsley, green onions, marjoram, a few cloves, and a clove of garlic
3. After cooking and seasoning, dust with a little flour, add a splash of broth and a glass of white wine, along with slices of truffle and mushrooms
4. When cooked and of good flavor, remove the bouquet and oil, thicken with egg yolks dissolved in broth, add minced parsley, a bit of garlic, and lemon juice
5. Serve hot as an entrée
