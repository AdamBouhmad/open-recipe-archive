## Ingredients

- One lamprey cut into equal pieces
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Fine herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the lamprey into equal pieces
2. Marinate in oil with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
3. Cut the same amount of other ingredients to match
