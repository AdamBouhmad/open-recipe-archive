## Ingredients

- one duck, quartered
- melted bacon fat
- beaten eggs
- cream soup without sugar

## Directions

1. After the duck is well cooked, immerse the quarters in melted bacon fat
2. When well immersed so the quarters remain whole, pass them through beaten eggs
3. Place them in cream soup without sugar
4. Send to brown
5. Serve at table
