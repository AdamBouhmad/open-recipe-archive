## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- béchamel sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat fifteen or eighteen egg whites with rod
2. Once raised, mix with all the yolks in a well-bound béchamel of good taste
3. Pour everything into a pan lined and first rubbed inside with butter
4. Bake in the oven
5. Once risen and cooked, serve hot as the previous recipes
