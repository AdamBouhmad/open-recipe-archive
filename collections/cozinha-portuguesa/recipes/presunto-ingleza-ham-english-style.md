## Ingredients

- presunto (ham)
- agua simples (simple water)
- nabos (turnips)
- cenouras (carrots)
- molho de manteiga (butter sauce)
- batatas (potatoes)
- repolhos (cabbages)
- fabas (beans)
- ervilhas (peas)
- espinafres (spinach)
- caldo (broth)
- farinha (flour)
- azeite (oil)

## Directions

1. After the ham has been soaked for two days and trimmed, cook it in simple water and serve it garnished with turnips, carrots, and butter sauce, or with potatoes and the same sauce; with cabbages, or with any other vegetables such as beans, peas, spinach cooked in the same broth, with butter sauce thickened with a little flour and a drop of broth or water, so that it is not too thick and is made with oil
2. One should note that the ham should not be allowed to cook too much, and that the vegetables in the garnish should be naturally green and each one of its natural color
