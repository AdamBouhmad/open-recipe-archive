## Ingredients

- half a hundred pears
- three canadas of water
- two arratels of sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the pears very well, cut off the tip of the stem, and scrape the rest
2. Put them in water and cook in boiling water until they are soft to the touch when pressed between the fingers
3. Remove with a skimmer and place in cold water, then drain
4. Soak for two hours in water with dissolved sugar
5. Arrange on perforated trays with the stem end facing up
6. Place in a mild oven and leave overnight
7. Remove in the morning and soak again in sugar water
8. Return to the oven in the same way and continue this process for four or five days
9. The last time they should be completely dry, then store in a dry and airy place for as long as necessary
