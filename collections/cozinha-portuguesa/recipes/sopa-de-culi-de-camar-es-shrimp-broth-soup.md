## Ingredients

- half a hundred shrimp
- one ounce of sweet almonds
- shrimp heads and shells
- breast of a roasted hen
- four hard egg yolks
- veal stock base
- ham
- onion in quarters
- half a spoon of broth
- salt
- two cloves
- dried marjoram
- sage
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. Wash and cook half a hundred shrimp, remove their heads
2. Take one ounce of sweet almonds and pound them with the shrimp heads and shells, the breast of a roasted hen, and four hard egg yolks
3. Line the bottom of a casserole with veal stock base, a piece of ham, and an onion in quarters
4. Cover the casserole with its lid and let it sweat gently over low heat
5. When it begins to stick to the bottom of the casserole, add one spoon of broth
6. Season with salt, two cloves, dried marjoram, and sage
7. Let it simmer gently with some bread crusts
8. When the veal is cooked, strain the broth through a sieve
9. Pound the bread crusts with what remains in the mortar and dissolve everything in two spoons of the same broth
10. Heat, strain through a sieve, put everything together over heat but do not boil
11. Serve over moistened bread crusts as is customary
