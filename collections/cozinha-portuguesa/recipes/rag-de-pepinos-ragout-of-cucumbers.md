## Ingredients

- Cucumbers, cut into dice
- Onions
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Browned butter
- Ham slice
- Broth or stock
- Pepper
- Veal cullis

## Directions

1. Peel and cut cucumbers and onions into dice
2. Marinate them in vinegar and salt
3. Squeeze them in a cloth
4. Sauté them in browned butter with a slice of ham
5. After sautéing, moisten with broth, season, and allow to cook
6. When cooked, remove the fat and bind with veal cullis
7. Serve for all first courses of braised meat, roasted meat, or larded meat
