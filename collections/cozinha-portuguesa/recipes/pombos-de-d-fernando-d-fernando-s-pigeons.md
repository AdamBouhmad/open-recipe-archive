## Ingredients

- roasted pigeons
- a small piece of bacon
- a small piece of butter
- all spices
- aromatics
- white wine
- a measure of vinegar
- verjuice if available, or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the pigeons
2. Sauté a small piece of bacon with a small piece of butter, all spices, and aromatics
3. Add white wine, a measure of vinegar, and verjuice if available (or lemon juice) so it becomes appetizing
4. When seasoned, place the pigeons on slices of bread
5. Pour the sauce over
6. Serve to the table
