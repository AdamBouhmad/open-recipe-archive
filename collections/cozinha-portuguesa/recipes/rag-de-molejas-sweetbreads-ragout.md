## Ingredients

- eight sweetbreads of lamb or veal
- melted lard
- a slice of ham
- flour
- broth
- substance
- pepper
- a bunch of parsley
- fresh herbs
- one onion with two cloves
- mushroom caps
- artichoke hearts
- cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak eight sweetbreads of lamb or veal in water
2. Blanch and brown in melted lard and a slice of ham
3. Dust with flour
4. Add a little broth and substance, pepper, a bunch of parsley and fresh herbs, one onion with two cloves
5. Let it cook
6. When cooked, add mushroom caps and artichoke hearts
7. Bind everything with a spoonful of cullis
8. Remove the fat
9. Serve with lemon juice to garnish pies, tarts, or any first course desired
