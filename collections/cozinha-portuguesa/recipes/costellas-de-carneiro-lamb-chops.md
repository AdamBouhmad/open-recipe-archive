## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- butter or rendered pork fat
- salt
- pepper
- chives
- parsley, finely chopped
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the chops evenly and trim them well
2. Beat them between two cloths with the flat of a knife
3. Moisten them with butter or rendered pork fat and season with salt, pepper, chives, and finely chopped parsley
4. Dust them with bread crumbs
5. Place them on grills, basting them from time to time
6. Serve them dry
