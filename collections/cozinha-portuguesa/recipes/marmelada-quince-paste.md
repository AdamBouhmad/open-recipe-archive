## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the quinces in water, and when almost cooked, place them in cold water
2. Once cool, clean them inside, peel them, break them up with a wooden spoon, pass them through a sieve, and take to heat what passed through, stirring always so it does not stick
3. Once dry, add sugar at the point of thin hair, mix very well with the spoon, take it to heat again, stirring always, and remove from fire once it rises to a boil
4. Pour into small earthenware pots or covilhetes, allow to cool, cover, and keep for when necessary
5. In the same manner are made pears, apples, and peaches
