## Ingredients

- two shallots
- capers
- chives
- parsley
- half clove of garlic
- fine olive oil
- mustard
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mince two shallots, capers, and chives, parsley, and half clove of garlic, pound it afterwards very well; when pounded, dissolve it in fine olive oil, mustard, and vinegar, seasoned with salt and pepper, and serve it apart in a sauce boat. This sauce serves for partridges, chickens, and capons roasted on the spit or on the grill, as stated.
