## Ingredients

- half an arratel of sweet almonds
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- bread slices
- or fine biscuit
- beaten egg whites or meringue

## Directions

1. Scald and peel half an arratel of sweet almonds, pound them, adding a few drops of milk from time to time
2. When pounded, dissolve them in some boiled milk, seasoned as above
3. Pass through a cloth two or three times
4. Heat without boiling and serve over very thin and dry bread slices from the oven, or over fine biscuit
5. Garnish with beaten egg whites or meringue
