## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Turkey liver
- Fine herbs
- Bacon slabs
- Paper leaves
- Butter
- Egg yolks
- Grated bread
- Tarragon
- Water
- Ham essence
- Beef butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. After mortifying the turkey and preparing it for roasting, stuff it with a hash made from its liver and fine herbs
2. Cover with bacon slabs and paper leaves
3. Roast, and when almost done, remove the paper and bacon slabs
4. Coat the legs with a little butter thickened with two egg yolks
5. Dust very well with grated bread
6. Place near the fire to brown well
7. When well browned, serve with tarragon sauce
8. For the sauce: boil the tarragon in water, then squeeze and finely chop it, put in a casserole with ham essence and a piece of good well-washed beef butter
9. Place on heat, stirring and mixing with a wooden spoon so it does not break
10. When bound, serve under the turkey with lemon juice
