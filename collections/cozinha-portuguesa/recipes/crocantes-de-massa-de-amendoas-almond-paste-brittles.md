## Ingredients

- almond paste
- clarified butter
- white glaze or colored glaze
- fruit jellies

## Directions

1. Stretch out some almond paste, cut into strips, line the bottom of any round mold already greased with clarified butter
2. Add a border of the same paste around the edge so everything is joined in one piece
3. Bake in the oven at moderate heat
4. When cooked and fried, detach carefully, cover with white or colored glaze, and serve on a base of the same paste garnished with fruit jellies
5. They can also be served spread with sugar in thread stage, garnished with colored nonpareils
