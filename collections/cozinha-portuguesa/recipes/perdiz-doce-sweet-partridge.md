## Ingredients

- one roasted partridge
- four cooked and chopped egg yolks
- one quarter pound of ground almonds
- one pound of refined sugar
- butter or bacon fat
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. After roasting one partridge, insert four cooked and chopped egg yolks and one quarter pound of ground almonds mixed with one pound of refined sugar
2. Fry the partridge in butter or bacon fat
3. After frying, pass it through boiling sugar
4. Place on a spit and dry it with cinnamon and powdered sugar
