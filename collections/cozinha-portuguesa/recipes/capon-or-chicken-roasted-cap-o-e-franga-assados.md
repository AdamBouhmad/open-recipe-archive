## Ingredients

- capon or chicken
- bacon
- bacon lard
- paper with butter

## Directions

1. After the capon or chicken is plucked and aged, singe it in a well-lit furnace without smoke
2. Clean it with care, beat the breast, straighten it, and arrange it in good form
3. Lard it, rub it with bacon, clean it, singe the bristles, tie it by the thighs with twine
4. Cover the breasts with bacon strips, put it on the spit, and roast it covered with sheets of buttered paper
5. When roasted as is customary, remove the paper, and when the bacon is browned, serve it hot as a roasted dish
6. When not serving it this way, remove the bacon, cover it with breadcrumbs, brown it, and serve it hot surrounded by watercress seasoned with salt and vinegar, or if not excessively fat, lard it with fine bacon and serve it with salt, pepper, and juice of verjuice or lemon
