## Ingredients

- six tiguelas of almond blancmange
- twelve egg yolks
- one arratel of powdered sugar
- one eighth of flour
- half arratel of butter
- four egg yolks
- one quarter of sugar

## Directions

1. Put in a pan six tiguelas of almond blancmange with twelve egg yolks and one arratel of powdered sugar
2. Bring to a boil to thicken and let cool
3. Knead one eighth of flour with half arratel of butter, four egg yolks, and one quarter of sugar
4. When kneaded, make two cakes and stretch on a board until very thin
5. Place the beaten blancmange on one cake, spread it, and cover with the other cake
6. Cut with a wheel cutter in the shape of fartes
7. Fry in butter and pass through sugar
8. Send to the table
