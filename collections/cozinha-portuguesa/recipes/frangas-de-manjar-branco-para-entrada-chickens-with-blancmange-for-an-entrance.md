## Ingredients

- two or three chickens
- half a canada of good cow's milk
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- cooked coriander
- handful of sweet almonds, pounded
- chicken breasts, roasted
- one arratel of pork fat, finely cut
- eight egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- nutmeg
- milk for cooking

## Directions

1. After tenderizing two or three chickens, scale and clean them well
2. Debone them entirely through the neck, being careful not to pierce the skin
3. Take half a canada of good cow's milk and bring to boil with a bay leaf, marjoram, and a little pounded coriander
4. When reduced to one quartilho, add a handful of sweet pounded almonds and strain through a sieve
5. Add the breasts of two roasted chickens, one arratel of pork fat cut very fine, and eight egg yolks
6. Season with salt, pepper, spices, and nutmeg
7. Incorporate over low heat
8. When thickened and cooled, stuff the chickens and sew with needle so the filling does not escape
9. Blanch in boiling water, then cook in a casserole with hot milk, salt, and dried coriander
10. Cover with slices of pork fat and sheets of paper, letting them simmer gently
11. When cooked, remove and serve with white Queen's coulis
