## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one arratel of mutton
- one arratel of pork
- three onions
- one bunch of aromatics
- vinegar
- little water
- one dozen egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. Put one chicken in a pan with one arratel of mutton, one arratel of pork, three onions, one bunch of aromatics, vinegar, and little water
2. Place to cook, and when more than half cooked, remove it
3. Chop the chicken in one part, the mutton in another, and the pork with the aromatics in another
4. Also chop one dozen egg yolks
5. Once this is done, place some slices in a buttered frying pan or plate
6. After moistening them with the substance left from the chicken, spread layers upon them
7. Place a layer of chicken with chopped egg yolk on top, over this meat place another of mutton with chopped egg yolk on top, and over this another of pork and aromatics with chopped egg yolks
8. Continue the layers in this order until all is finished, shaping it like the form of a hat crown
9. Place six raw egg yolks on top, and between yolk and yolk some beef marrow
10. Cook on low heat from below and on top, and send it thus in the same frying pan or plate to the table
