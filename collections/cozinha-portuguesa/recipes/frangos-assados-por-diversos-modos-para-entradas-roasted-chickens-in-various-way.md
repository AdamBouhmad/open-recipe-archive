## Ingredients

- young chickens
- chicken livers
- scraped bacon
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- pork butter
- bacon slices
- paper leaves
- spit

## Directions

1. Choose young chickens and after they are mortified, cleaned, and singed, leave them with their legs intact, cutting only their nails
2. Fill them with a mixture made from their own livers, scraped bacon, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. After thus preparing them, tie them with twine
4. Brown them in pork butter, remove them, cover them with bacon slices and paper leaves, attach to a spit, and roast them
5. After roasting, serve them with any of the following sauces: Spanish sauce, Italian sauce, German sauce, English sauce, Ravigote sauce, Sorrel and caper sauce, Vinegar sauce, Spicy sauce, Shallot sauce, Laurel sauce, or Aspic sauce
