## Ingredients

- anchovies
- fine forcemeat filling
- white wine

## Directions

1. Prepare anchovies opened and without spines as stated
2. Soak
3. Replace the spines with a fine, bound forcemeat
4. Arrange as if they were whole
5. Pass through a batter made with white wine
6. Fry and when of good color, serve with fried sauce in a ring around them
