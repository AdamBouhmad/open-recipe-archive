## Ingredients

- one whole chicken
- one half arratel of bacon
- all seasonings
- one eighth of flour
- one half arratel of butter
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Sauté a whole chicken in one half arratel of bacon; when sautéed, giving it a few strikes at the joints, season with all the seasonings, and let cool
2. Knead one eighth of flour with one half arratel of butter and three eggs, make a box in which the chicken fits with broth
3. When baked, serve to the table
