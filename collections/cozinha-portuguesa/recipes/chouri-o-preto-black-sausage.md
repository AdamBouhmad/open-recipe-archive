## Ingredients

- Onions
- Butter or melted salt pork
- Lard
- Belly and tripe of pork, cooked
- Pork blood
- Milk or broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine spices
- Coriander
- Fennel
- Parsley, chopped
- Pork casings
- Water
- Bay leaves
- Bread crumbs
- Orange

## Directions

1. Cut onions into small pieces, sauté in butter or melted salt pork
2. Remove from heat after sautéing
3. Add two or three pounds of lard, belly and tripe of cooked pork cut into pieces, pork blood, a quarter of milk or broth, and place on heat stirring always
4. Season with salt, pepper, fine spices, coriander, fennel, and chopped parsley
5. When beginning to bind and with good taste, without cooling, fill the casings as described
6. Serve in water with little salt and bay leaves
7. When cooked and cold, lay in oiled paper boxes and serve hot with bread crumb fillets and sour orange
