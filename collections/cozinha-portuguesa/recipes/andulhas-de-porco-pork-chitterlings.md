## Ingredients

- pork intestines
- water and vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- marjoram
- pork lard
- pork meat
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- a little fennel
- broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the intestines
2. Soak them in water and vinegar with bay leaves, thyme, and marjoram
3. Cut part of them into fillets and similarly cut pork lard and pork meat
4. Season everything with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, and a little fennel
5. Season and ensure it has good taste
6. Fill the larger intestines but leave a third part empty so they do not burst
7. Tie them at both ends
8. Make them all of the same length
9. Cook them in a sauce with broth, white wine, fine herbs, and lard
10. Once cooked, let them cool in the same sauce
11. Remove them and drain on a cloth
12. Once dried, fry them on both sides
13. Serve as an appetizer
