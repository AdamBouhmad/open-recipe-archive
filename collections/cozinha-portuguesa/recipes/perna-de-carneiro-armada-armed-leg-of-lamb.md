## Ingredients

- One leg of lamb
- Half an arratel of bacon
- Aromatics (cheiros)
- Three eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Vinegar
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Bacon lards
- Egg yolk, cooked
- Blanched almonds
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mince all the meat from a leg of lamb with half an arratel of bacon and aromatics, being careful to leave the bone of the leg whole.
2. Temper the meat on the chopping block with all the seasonings, three eggs, bread crumbs, vinegar, and lemon juice.
3. In a buttered skillet large enough to hold it, begin assembling the leg so that it appears whole, composing it with its bacon lards, cooked egg yolk, and blanched almonds.
4. Put it to cook on a low flame.
5. When cooked, strain the substance with two egg yolks and place it on the plate with slices of bread underneath, lemon juice and cinnamon on top.
