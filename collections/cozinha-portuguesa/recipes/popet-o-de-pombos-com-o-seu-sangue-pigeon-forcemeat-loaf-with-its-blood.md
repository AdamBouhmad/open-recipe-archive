## Ingredients

- minced meat of one hare
- meat of two rabbits
- ham
- piece of beef loin
- salt
- pepper
- false pepper
- minced chive
- spices
- fine herbs
- pork belly cut into small pieces the size of lentils
- three or four egg yolks
- sheets of pork belly
- ragù of small pigeons
- pigeon blood
- essence sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a forcemeat of hare meat, rabbit meat, ham, and a piece of beef loin, seasoned with salt, pepper, false pepper, minced chive, spices, and fine herbs
2. Once everything is minced, mix it with pork belly cut into small pieces the size of lentils
3. Bind it with three or four egg yolks
4. Line a casserole with sheets of pork belly
5. Place part of the forcemeat inside
6. Put in the middle the ragù of small pigeons bound with their blood and egg yolks
7. Cover with the rest of the forcemeat
8. Put sheets of pork belly on top
9. Put to cook in the oven
10. When cooked, remove the pork belly, turn it out onto the plate
11. Drain the fat
12. Serve with sauce of good taste and lemon juice
