## Ingredients

- one ten-reis bread
- bread slices
- half arratel of cow butter
- half arratel of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut one ten-reis bread into slices
2. Butter the slices and coat them with powdered sugar on both sides
3. Place them in a plate or skillet until all are used
4. Pour the remaining butter on top
5. Brown over low heat
6. Serve at table
7. This plate also serves for any kind of roast if one wishes to use it
