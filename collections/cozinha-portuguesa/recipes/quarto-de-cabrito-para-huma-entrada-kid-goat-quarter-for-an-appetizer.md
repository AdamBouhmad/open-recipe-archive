## Ingredients

- fat kid goat quarter
- buttered paper
- breadcrumbs
- clear essence sauce
- enxova
- green onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place a fat kid goat quarter to roast, covered with buttered paper
2. When roasted, remove the paper, baste the quarter, and cover it with breadcrumbs and give it good color
3. When well browned, serve with a sauce of clear essence, two enxovas, chopped green onion, salt, pepper, and lemon juice
