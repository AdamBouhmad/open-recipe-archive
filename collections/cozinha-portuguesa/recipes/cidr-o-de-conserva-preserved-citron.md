## Ingredients

- citrons
- egg brine
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the citrons very well and place in a pan, or if a greater quantity, in a pot in egg brine
2. Over nine days sweeten in nine waters
3. Make the preserve with sugar and cover the same way as described for preserved pears
