## Ingredients

- beef loin
- lettuce stems
- ham essence
- lemon juice
- sorrel ragú or spinach ragú

## Directions

1. Take a beef loin, cut it into two or three pieces, and lard each one separately
2. Prepare and finish as fricandeau
3. Take the lettuce stems and cook them in a well-nourished white broth
4. Once well cooked and well dried, place them in a ham essence, adding what remained stuck to the bottom of the casserole
5. Once well flavored, place the stems around the meat and serve with lemon juice. It can also be served with a sorrel or spinach ragú
