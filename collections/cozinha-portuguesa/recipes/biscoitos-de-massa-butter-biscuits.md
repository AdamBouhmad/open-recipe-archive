## Ingredients

- a quarter of flour
- half an arratel of butter without salt
- half an arratel of sugar
- half a dozen egg yolks
- lukewarm water
- a stone of salt

## Directions

1. Knead a quarter of flour with half an arratel of butter without salt and half an arratel of sugar
2. Add half a dozen egg yolks and lukewarm water with a stone of salt dissolved in it
3. When well kneaded, form small biscuits the size of a finger or make little rings
4. Bake in a basin
