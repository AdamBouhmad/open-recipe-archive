## Ingredients

- chicken
- two pigeons
- two partridges
- rabbit
- two arratels of cured ham and sausage
- bundle of aromatics
- three onions
- four heads of garlic
- four heads of lettuce
- grains
- whole spices
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cook in a pot a chicken, two pigeons, two partridges, a rabbit, two arratels of cured ham and sausage, with a bundle of aromatics underneath, three onions, four heads of garlic, four heads of lettuce, grains, whole spices, vinegar, and water to cover everything
2. When cooked, season with all the seasonings and top with a bundle of aromatics
