## Ingredients

- hare
- two arratels of mutton
- quarter of lard
- herbs
- butter
- cheese soup
- lard slices

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from a hare's loin and legs, leaving the bones intact, and mince it with two arratels of mutton, a quarter of lard, and some herbs
2. Once all is minced, arrange it in a buttered frying pan, so the hare retains its original shape
3. Finally arrange lard slices on top and send to the table over cheese soup
