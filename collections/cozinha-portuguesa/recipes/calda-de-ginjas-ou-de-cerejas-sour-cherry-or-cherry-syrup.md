## Ingredients

- sour cherries or cherries, ripe and sound
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and pits from two pounds of ripe, sound cherries.
2. Put them in a quart of water.
3. Bring to a boil for two minutes.
4. Strain through a sieve.
5. When strained, pour into two pounds of sugar in the hair stage.
6. Let it all boil until the sugar takes on the necessary consistency, like the preceding one.
7. Guard it in the same manner.
