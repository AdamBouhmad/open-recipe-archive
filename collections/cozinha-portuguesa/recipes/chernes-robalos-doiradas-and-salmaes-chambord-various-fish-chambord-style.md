## Ingredients

- Fish, cleaned and prepared
- Bacon, fine
- Minced fish filling à créma
- Truffles
- Morels
- Bacon, sliced
- Onion, sliced
- White wine
- Salt
- pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Marjoram
- Butter
- Paper leaves
- Essence sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare any of these fish and remove the skin from one side only
2. Lard with fine bacon from head to tail
3. Stuff afterwards with a minced filling à créma, put in the middle a sprinkling of truffles and morels
4. Line the bottom of a long baking dish with bacon slices and onion slices
5. Place the fish on top with white wine, season with salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaf, marjoram, and a piece of butter
6. Cover with bacon slices and paper leaves and put to cook in the oven
7. When cooked, remove the slices, and after browning, serve with lightly bound essence sauce and lemon juice
