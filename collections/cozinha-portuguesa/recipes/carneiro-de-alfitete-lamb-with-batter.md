## Ingredients

- Two arratels of lamb from the leg
- Flour
- Eggs
- White wine
- One quarter of melted bacon fat or lard
- One quarter of sugar

## Directions

1. When two arratels of lamb from the leg are almost roasted, make a batter of flour, eggs, white wine, one quarter of melted bacon fat (or lard) and one quarter of sugar, so that the batter becomes sweet.
2. After making this batter, spread it with a spoon over the lamb as it roasts on a brisk flame, until it sets and the batter is done.
