## Ingredients

- Young partridges or young partridge, plucked and singed
- Ham
- Calf sweetbreads
- Chicken livers
- Truffles
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Chopped chives
- Scraped bacon
- Lard
- Fine bacon for larding
- Bacon slices
- Veal slices
- Ham slices
- Parsley bouquet
- Bay leaf
- Orange slices
- Onion studded with cloves
- Broth
- Aspic
- Essence sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the young partridges or young partridge and completely bone them
2. Stuff them with a mixture made of ham, calf sweetbreads, chicken livers and truffles, all cut into small dice and seasoned with little salt, pepper, parsley and chopped chives, mixed with some finely chopped or scraped bacon
3. After stuffing, sew them all around with twine so they remain round and braise them in lard
4. After braising, lard them with fine bacon with the lardoons and place in a pot to cook between bacon slices, veal slices, ham slices, a parsley bouquet, bay leaf, orange slices and an onion studded with cloves with some broth
5. When cooked, remove them, strain the broth through a sieve, remove the fat, mix it with a little aspic, reduce it, and once reduced, place the young partridge on top with the larding facing down
6. Once the aspic is set, remove the twine and serve with essence sauce and lemon juice
