## Ingredients

- one well mortified veal sweetbread
- fine bacon lard
- finely minced chicken or ham breasts
- string
- spit

## Directions

1. Take one well mortified veal sweetbread, trim it of its skins, and beat it between two cloths as for fricandeau
2. Lard it well with fine bacon and make a hole from one side only with the spit
3. Stuff it very well with a fine mince of chicken or ham breasts
4. Close the opening with a cork and tie it very well with string
5. Tie it then to a spit and place it to roast, covered with buttered paper
6. Remove the paper afterwards to give it good color, and once roasted, remove the string and serve with good veal substance if desired
