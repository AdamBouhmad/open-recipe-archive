## Ingredients

- Asparagus, cut into equal pieces
- Butter
- Ham slice
- Flour
- Broth or stock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley bouquet
- Cullis
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the asparagus into equal pieces and blanch them
2. Pass them through butter with a slice of ham and dust with flour
3. Add a little broth, season with salt, pepper, and a parsley bouquet
4. Allow to boil for an instant
5. When cooked, remove the butter, parsley bouquet, and ham
6. Bind with cullis and lemon juice
7. When it has good flavor, serve for garnishes of any first course
