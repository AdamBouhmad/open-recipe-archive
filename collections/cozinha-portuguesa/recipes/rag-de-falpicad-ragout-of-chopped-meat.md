## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads, cut into dice
- Ham
- Strips of meat
- Artichoke hearts
- Cucumbers
- Flour
- Butter
- Broth or stock
- White wine
- Parsley bouquet
- Fine herbs
- Garlic clove
- Salt
- Pepper
- Liver, cut into dice
- Egg yolks
- Milk
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut sweetbreads, ham, strips of meat, and artichoke hearts into dice
2. Dust with flour and sauté in butter until everything is sautéed
3. After sautéing, add a little broth and white wine
4. Add a parsley bouquet and fine herbs, a garlic clove, salt, and pepper
5. Allow to cook until done
6. When cooked, add liver cut into dice and blanched
7. Reduce the sauce, remove the fat, and it is ready to serve for various purposes
8. It can also be bound with egg yolks, milk, and lemon juice
