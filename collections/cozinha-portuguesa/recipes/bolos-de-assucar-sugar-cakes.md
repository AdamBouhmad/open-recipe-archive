## Ingredients

- half an alqueire of flour from Alem-Tejo
- two arratels of Madeira sugar
- one arratel of butter
- four egg yolks
- one glass of white wine
- rose water
- wheat
- salt

## Directions

1. Put into half an alqueire of flour from Alem-Tejo two arratels of Madeira sugar
2. Add one arratel of butter, four egg yolks, one glass of white wine, rose water, wheat, and salt
3. Rub this dough very well in the hands
4. Form little cakes
5. When they come from the oven, sprinkle with rose water and cover well
