## Ingredients

- beef palates
- broth
- white vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaf
- cloves
- butter
- flour
- pork fat
- beer or white wine

## Directions

1. After preparing and cooking as above, cut equal pieces about the thickness of a finger
2. Make them take flavor in a hot marinade made of broth, white vinegar, salt, pepper, a clove of garlic, a bay leaf, two cloves, and a piece of butter and little flour
3. After being in this for two or three hours, dry them well on white cloth, flour them, and fry them in pork fat
4. Serve with cold sauce
5. Alternatively, instead of flouring, pass them through a batter made of flour mixed with beer or white wine, a little olive oil, and seasoned with salt
6. Serve well hot with fried sauce
