## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Wheat flour
- Cow butter
- Parsley
- Cilantro
- Parsley
- Onion
- Oregano
- Vinegar
- Cinnamon
- Honey or sugar
- Six eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the carrots and cut them into quarters.
2. Pass them through wheat flour.
3. Fry them in a pan, layering them in a frying pan.
4. Make a sauce by wilting parsley, cilantro, parsley, onion, and oregano in cow butter.
5. Season the sauce with vinegar, cinnamon, all the seasonings, honey or sugar, so that it is both sour and sweet.
6. Pour this sauce over the carrots.
7. Place six eggs on top of this sauce and send to the table.
