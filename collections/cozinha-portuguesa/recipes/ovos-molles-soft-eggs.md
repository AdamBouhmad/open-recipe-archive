## Ingredients

- one arratel of sugar at high point
- fifteen egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pour fifteen egg yolks into one arratel of sugar at high point
2. Stir very well while cooking
3. Once cooked, pour onto a plate and serve with cinnamon on top
