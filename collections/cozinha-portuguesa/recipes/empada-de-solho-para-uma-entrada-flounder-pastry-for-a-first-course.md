## Ingredients

- Flounder fillets made from two pieces
- Anchovy fillets
- Eel fillets
- Good washed butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Fine herbs
- Dough
- Eggs
- Shrimp consomé

## Directions

1. Make two pieces of flounder from eel and anchovy fillets
2. Line the bottom of the pastry with good washed butter, season with salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs all well minced
3. Arrange the flounder pieces on top
4. Place on top the same seasoning that was placed below
5. Cover with butter, close the pastry with its lid, decorate and gild
6. Cook in the oven
7. When cooked, drain the fat and serve with piquant shrimp consomé or other suitable sauce
