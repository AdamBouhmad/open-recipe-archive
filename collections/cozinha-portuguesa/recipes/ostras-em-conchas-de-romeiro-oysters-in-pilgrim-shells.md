## Ingredients

- oysters
- fish
- pilgrim shells
- fine herbs
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make a minced mixture of oysters combined with a piece of fish, prepared the same way as the previous recipe
2. After pounding and seasoning the same way, garnish the bottom and edges of pilgrim shells with this mixture
3. Place two or three oysters seasoned with fine herbs and butter in the middle
4. Cover as before with the same minced mixture and fine breadcrumbs on top
5. Bake in the oven and when well golden, serve as an entrée
