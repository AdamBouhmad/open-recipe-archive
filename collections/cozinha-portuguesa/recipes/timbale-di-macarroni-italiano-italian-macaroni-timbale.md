## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Water
- Cow's milk
- Cinnamon, grated
- Fresh butter
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- Red jam and some strips of pastry
- Pastry sheets

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni in water for seven or eight minutes
2. Drain in a sieve
3. Put in two or three cups of hot cow's milk
4. Cover and place over moderate heat until swollen and cooked
5. Drain the milk
6. Season with grated cinnamon, fresh butter, and grated Parmesan cheese
7. Place a thin pastry sheet over the pasta strips
8. Put the macaroni inside
9. Cover with another piece of pastry
10. Seal with eggs
11. Bake in the oven for one hour
12. Turn the timbale onto its plate and serve hot
