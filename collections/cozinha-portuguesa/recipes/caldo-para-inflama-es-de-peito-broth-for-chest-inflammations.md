## Ingredients

- one stuffed chicken
- four cold seeds, ground

## Directions

1. Bring to simmer in one canada of water one chicken stuffed with the four cold seeds, ground
2. After skimming and when the broth is reduced to two-thirds
3. Strain through a cloth and press
4. Use it every day for as long as seems fit
