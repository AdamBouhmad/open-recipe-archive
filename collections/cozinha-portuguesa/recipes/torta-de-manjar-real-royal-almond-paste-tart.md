## Ingredients

- six bowlfuls of royal almond paste
- half a dozen egg yolks
- flower water
- French puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Beat six bowlfuls of royal almond paste with half a dozen egg yolks and flower water.
2. Once well beaten, make a French puff pastry tart and put the royal almond paste inside.
3. After baking, send to the table.
4. In the same way a white almond paste tart is made.
