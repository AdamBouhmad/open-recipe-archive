## Ingredients

- sardines
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- minced garlic
- fine herbs
- butter or chopped meat
- breadcrumbs
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Scale the sardines, remove entrails, cut off heads and tail tips, wash very well
2. Marinate in oil, salt, pepper, minced garlic, and fine herbs
3. After marinating, arrange in a silver box over a little butter or chopped meat
4. Cover with fine breadcrumbs
5. Place to cook in the oven
6. When cooked, serve with a little spicy sauce on top
