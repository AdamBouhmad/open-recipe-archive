## Ingredients

- mussels from Aveiro
- flour
- eggs
- butter
- vinegar
- garlic
- pepper
- orange
- butter

## Directions

1. Coat the mussels from Aveiro with flour and eggs, and fry them in butter
2. Pass them through a sauce of vinegar, garlic, pepper, orange, and butter, and send them to the table
