## Ingredients

- one eighth of flour
- half an arratel of sugar
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Knead one eighth of flour with half an arratel of sugar and three egg yolks.
2. Once well kneaded and firm, shape the pastries the size of two tostões and two fingers high.
3. Bake them empty; when half cooked, fill them with royal almond paste and brown them with a cover of coals or send them to the oven.
4. Brown them and send to the table.
