## Ingredients

- one very fat chicken
- one partridge or two good ones
- one veal knuckle
- pearl barley
- salt

## Directions

1. The partridges and knuckle must first be blanched in hot water
2. Then together with the chicken put everything in a pan almost full of water to the rim
3. Add one handful of pearl barley with its appropriate stones of salt
4. Place it on the heat so that it goes boiling
