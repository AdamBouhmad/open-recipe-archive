## Ingredients

- Mutton tongues
- Broth
- Minced meat made with blood
- Pepper
- Salt
- Shallots
- Onion
- Lard
- Butter
- Bacon strips
- Dough
- Eggs
- Caper sauce
- Anchovies

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the tongues, then cook them in broth
2. Line the bottom of a pastry box with minced meat made with blood
3. Place the tongues, split in half, on top, seasoned with pepper, salt, finely minced shallots and onion
4. Cover with minced meat as below, a piece of butter, and strips of bacon
5. Cover with a dough lid sealed with eggs and gilded
6. Cook for two hours in the oven
7. When cooked, open it, remove the bacon strips, drain the fat, and serve with caper sauce, anchovies, or other sauce
