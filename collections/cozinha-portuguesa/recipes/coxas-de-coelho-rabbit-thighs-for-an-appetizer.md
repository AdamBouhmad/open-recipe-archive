## Ingredients

- rabbit thighs
- rabbit loins
- ham
- sage
- chives
- truffles
- salt
- pepper
- thread
- veal or bacon strips
- sage bundle
- fine herbs
- broth
- white wine
- curry
- lemon juice or sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Remove the thighs from two or three rabbits after skinning and cleaning
2. Remove the larger bone from underneath and enlarge them with your fingers from inside without tearing the skin
3. Fill them with a mixture made from loins of the same rabbits cut into cubes, mixed with a little ham, sage, chives, chopped truffles, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Once filled and sewn with thread, cook on gentle heat for half an hour in a braise with veal or bacon strips, bundle of sage, and fine herbs, salt, and pepper
5. Moisten with a spoonful of broth and a quart of white wine
6. When the thighs are cooked, remove them and add half a spoonful of curry to the bottom of the braise and bring to a boil again
7. When it has good flavor, remove the fat, strain through a sieve, and serve over the rabbit thighs with lemon juice or sour orange juice
