## Ingredients

- One turkey
- twine
- bacon lardons
- ham of medium thickness
- half a Canada of hot milk
- butter
- fine herbs
- dried coriander
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. After the turkey is prepared, singed, and straightened as is customary, blanch it in a furnace, tie it with twine, and lard it with bacon and ham of medium thickness
2. After larding, soak it in a casserole with half a Canada of hot milk, a piece of butter, fine herbs, dried coriander, salt, pepper, and onion slices
3. After marinating for three or four hours, place it on the spit covered with bacon strips and paper sheets
4. When half roasted, remove the paper and bacon, baste it with its marinade
5. Then thicken this marinade with a little milk, two egg yolks, a piece of butter, flour, and salt
6. Once thickened, gradually pour it over the turkey little by little so that a crust forms over it and takes on a good golden color
7. When roasting is finished, remove it and serve with a peppery sauce underneath or with any other piquant sauce that seems fitting
