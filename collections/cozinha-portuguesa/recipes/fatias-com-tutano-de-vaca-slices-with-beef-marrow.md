## Ingredients

- bread crumb slices
- franchipane cream with marrow
- egg whites
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Toast or roast slices of bread crumb
2. Garnish with franchipane cream with marrow
3. Cover with beaten egg whites mixed with fine sugar
4. Bake in the oven
5. When cooked, glaze with sugar and hot spatula, and serve as an entrée
