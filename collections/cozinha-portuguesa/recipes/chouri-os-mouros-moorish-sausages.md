## Ingredients

- fresh pork, lean and fat
- wine
- garlic
- cumin
- onion
- pork fat
- pork blood
- pork casings

## Directions

1. Place fresh, lean and fat pork, cut very finely, in a preserve of wine, garlic, cumin, and very finely minced onion, washed in many waters, and keep in this preserve for one or two days
2. When ready to fill the sausages, transfer the meat to another bowl without the preserve and mix it with fresh lard, minced onion, and some pork blood, enough to color the meat well
3. Fill pork casings, smoke them: after smoking, roast them and take to the table
