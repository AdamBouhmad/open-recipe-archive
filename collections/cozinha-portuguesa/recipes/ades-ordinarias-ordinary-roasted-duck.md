## Ingredients

- one roasted duck, either wild or domestic
- white wine
- two ounces of butter
- one quince in separate quarters
- all the adubos (seasonings)
- toasted bread slices
- lemon

## Directions

1. As the duck roasts, collect the drippings until it is half-roasted
2. Add a splash of white wine to the drippings
3. Place the entire duck in a cooking vessel with two ounces of butter, one quince in separate quarters, and all the seasonings
4. When cooked, place on a plate with toasted bread slices, the sauce, quince quarters, and lemon on top
5. Serve at table
