## Ingredients

- three or four fresh eggs
- equal amount of flour
- a little orange flower water
- a little finely chopped lemon zest
- half a quartilho of good milk
- wax

## Directions

1. Break eggs into a bowl and mix with flour, orange flower water, and lemon zest
2. After mixing, add half a quartilho of good milk and stir everything with a spoon until the batter is well liquid
3. Heat the waffle iron on a well-lit brazier
4. Rub the inside with wax so they do not stick
5. When the mold is hot, pour a small spoonful of batter inside, close it immediately, and place over the flame
6. When cooked on one side, flip the iron to finish cooking the other side
7. Remove carefully and place around a stick of dough so they fold
8. Store afterwards in a dry place or in a stove for use when needed
