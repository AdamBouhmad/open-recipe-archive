## Ingredients

- a knob of veal
- fine bacon cut into pieces
- bacon slices
- veal slices with bacon lard
- chopped meat
- beaten eggs
- strong, well-bound forcemeat
- truffles
- artichoke hearts
- sweetbreads or young squabs, chickens, frogs, quails, or any game

## Directions

1. Cut a knob of veal into slices and beat it between two cloths with the flat of a knife
2. Lard it with fine bacon cut into pieces
3. Line a casserole with bacon slices and arrange the veal slices with the larding facing down, joined with chopped meat and beaten eggs to make them stick together
4. Cover the inside completely with a strong, well-bound forcemeat
5. Place in the middle a ragout of truffles, artichoke hearts, and sweetbreads or, if lacking those, young squabs, chickens, frogs, quails, or any game that seems good, provided they are well-finished and of good taste
6. Cover with the same forcemeat and on top place some slices of veal, ham, and bacon, and put it to cook slowly between two fires
7. When cooked, turn it out onto a plate, remove the bacon slices and fat, and serve with essence sauce and lemon juice
