## Ingredients

- a little butter
- the crumb of two or three vintens of bread
- half a canada of very hot milk
- salt
- fine sugar
- cinnamon
- covered orange flower
- a little grated lemon peel
- twelve or fifteen egg yolks
- the same number of egg whites beaten separately

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole a little butter, the crumb of two or three vintens of bread, and half a canada of very hot milk over the bread
2. When the bread is soaked, season with salt, fine sugar, cinnamon, covered orange flower, and a little grated lemon peel, all in powder and well chopped
3. Add twelve or fifteen egg yolks with the same number of egg whites beaten separately
4. Mix everything well
5. Transfer to a casserole lined inside with buttered paper
6. Put in an oven with moderate heat
7. When cooked, turn onto its plate, remove the paper, brown with sugar and a hot spatula, and serve
