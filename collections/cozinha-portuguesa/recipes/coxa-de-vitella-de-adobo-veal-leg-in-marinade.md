## Ingredients

- one white veal leg
- thick bacon, seasoned
- salt
- fine spices
- fine herbs
- shallots
- one clove of garlic
- veal slices
- beef slices
- onions
- cloves
- marjoram
- twine
- whole pepper
- good white wine
- white broth or water

## Directions

1. Prepare a white veal leg so that it is round and well-larded with thick bacon seasoned and mixed with salt, fine spices, fine herbs, shallots, and one clove of garlic
2. Line a pot with bacon and veal slices, onions, cloves, and marjoram
3. Place the veal leg on top, tied with twine
4. Season with salt and whole pepper
5. Cover with other slices of veal and bacon
6. Add one bottle of good white wine and finish moistening with good white broth or water
7. Once cooked, let it cool in its own braising liquid
8. After cooling, drain it to serve on a plate over a well-folded napkin
9. It may also be served by clarifying the broth in which it was cooked and reducing it to aspic, then placing it over the leg with various decorative patterns
