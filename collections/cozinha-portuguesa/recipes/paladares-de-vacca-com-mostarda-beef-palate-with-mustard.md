## Ingredients

- beef palates
- water
- butter
- onions
- broth
- broth reduction
- mustard

## Directions

1. They must first be well washed and cooked in water, then cleaned of all the skins
2. Cut into fillets
3. Put a piece of butter in a casserole and place it on the heat with two or three onions cut in slices
4. When the onions are half cooked, add the palates
5. When everything is well cooked through, moisten with a little broth and half a spoonful of broth reduction
6. When the sauce is reduced and of good flavor, remove the fat, add a little mustard, and serve well hot
