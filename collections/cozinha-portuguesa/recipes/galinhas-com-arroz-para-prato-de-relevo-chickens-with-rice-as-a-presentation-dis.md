## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 arratels of rice
- beef broth
- hens
- ham
- lean pork fat
- roots
- clove-studded onion
- egg yolks
- Parmesan cheese
- nutmeg
- butter
- good essence
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Wash and dry 1 or 2 arratels of rice
2. Cook it in broth made with beef, hens, ham, and a piece of lean pork fat with roots and a clove-studded onion
3. When the rice is cooked and half-cooled, put it in a cassole with egg yolks, grated Parmesan cheese, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Mix everything and when cooled, make a border two fingers high on the serving plate
5. Cover the bottom with a little of the same rice
6. Place whole or quartered hens cooked in a braising liquid with a spoonful of good essence on top
7. Cover with the rest of the rice and breadcrumbs and grated cheese on top
8. Sprinkle with butter and cook in the oven
9. When browned, drain the fat and serve hot. Ducks, young hens, and pigeons are served the same way
