## Ingredients

- mussels
- butter
- chives
- sauce
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaf
- culí

## Directions

1. Open the mussels and remove them from the shells
2. Clean them as in the previous recipes
3. Sauté briefly in a casserole with butter, chives, and minced sauce
4. Add two small spoonfuls of their own water
5. Season with a little pepper, one clove of garlic, and a bay leaf
6. Bind with a little culí
7. Keep on low heat for an instant
8. Without boiling, serve as an entrée
