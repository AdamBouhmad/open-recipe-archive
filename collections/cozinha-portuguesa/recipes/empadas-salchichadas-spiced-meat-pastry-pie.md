## Ingredients

- Four arratels of lamb from the leg, very well cleaned
- Half arratel of fatback
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Quarter measure of flour
- One arratel of pork butter
- Fatback lard, salted and peppered
- Wine for basting
- Bay leaves

## Directions

1. Chop four arratels of very clean lamb from the leg together with half arratel of fatback very well
2. After chopping, add salt, pepper, clove, nutmeg, a splash of wine, and another of vinegar
3. Transfer this meat to the pastry board and make a well-stretched cake from it, covering the top with salted and peppered fatback lard, and sprinkling it very well with wine
4. Fold it like a rolled pastry so that the fatback lard remains inside
5. Knead a quarter measure of flour with one arratel of pork butter, well worked, and make a large cake from it, placing the meat with bay leaves on top
6. Seal the pastry pie in the shape of a half-moon and cook it very slowly
7. This pastry pie is eaten cold and lasts all week on the table
