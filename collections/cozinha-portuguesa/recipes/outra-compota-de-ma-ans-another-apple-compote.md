## Ingredients

- apples without sufficient consistency
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Take all apples that do not have sufficient consistency to cook whole, cut them in half, clean them on the inside, prick them on top with a needle, and without peeling them, place them in cold water
2. Then put them to cook with the necessary sugar, and let them boil gently and slowly
3. When cooked and before they dissolve into marmalade, arrange them on the plate, then reduce the sugar to the necessary point, and pour it over the apples
4. Serve as the preceding compote
