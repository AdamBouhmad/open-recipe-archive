## Ingredients

- half a dozen apples
- clarified sugar
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen apples, cut them in half, remove the pit, and peel them
2. Place them immediately in cold water
3. Remove from the water and cook them in clarified sugar, as much as needed, and the juice of half a lemon
4. When the apples are cooked, arrange them on a plate, and when the sugar is reduced to a point where it sticks to the fingers, pour it over the apples
5. Serve hot or cold, as preferred
