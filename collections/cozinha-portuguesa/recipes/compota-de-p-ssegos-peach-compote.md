## Ingredients

- Half a dozen almost ripe peaches
- Clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen almost ripe peaches, cut them in half, and remove the pits.
2. Put them for a moment in boiling water, remove them as soon as the skin can be taken off, and place them to cook in boiling sugar that has been well skimmed.
3. Let them simmer little by little, and when cooked, reduce the sugar to the ordinary point and serve in the prescribed manner.
