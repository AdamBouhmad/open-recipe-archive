## Ingredients

- turkey, chicken, or chicken breast skins
- forcemeat à crème
- chicken or capon fillets roasted
- béchamel sauce
- eggs
- bacon slice
- bread of good taste

## Directions

1. For this you need copper molds imitating turkey wings, which will be lined inside with the skins of turkeys, chickens, or chickens from which the breasts have been removed
2. On these place a forcemeat à crème, finishing filling the molds with fillets of roasted capons or chickens placed in a béchamel sauce bound and of good taste
3. On top of this a layer of the forcemeat that was placed below, and cover with the rest of the skins, soldered with eggs, and on top of everything a slice of bacon
4. Once done, put them to cook in the oven or gentle steam for about an hour
5. Remove them, drain the fat, turn the molds carefully onto their plate, and serve with sauce of good taste as preferred
