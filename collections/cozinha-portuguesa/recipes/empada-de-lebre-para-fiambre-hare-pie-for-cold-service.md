## Ingredients

- Hares, boned
- Medium larding bacon, seasoned as customary
- Pastry dough
- Paper sheets buttered
- Chopped bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine spices
- Good butter
- Bacon slices
- Brandy

## Directions

1. Bone the hares and lard all the pieces with medium bacon seasoned as customary
2. Spread the dough on buttered paper sheets about half a finger thick
3. Place the hare pieces on top, arranging them closely, with chopped bacon, seasoned with salt, pepper, fine spices
4. Cover with good butter and bacon slices
5. Spread another piece of dough and place it on top, and finish with customary ornaments
6. Pour two small spoonfuls of brandy through the chimney hole
7. Put in the oven to cook for four or five hours
8. When colored golden, remove from the oven, seal the hole with dough
9. Let cool in a cool place and serve as a cold dish
