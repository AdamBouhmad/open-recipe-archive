## Ingredients

- butter
- cucumbers
- garlic
- lettuce
- sorrel
- chervil
- green onions
- broth made from roots
- bread
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté in butter sliced cucumbers, garlic, lettuce, sorrel, chervil, and green onions, all cut into pieces
2. Pour in broth made from roots and let simmer gently
3. When cooked, cut bread into thin slices, dry in the oven
4. Serve the bread slices in two spoonfuls of the herb broth and one spoonful of root substance
5. Serve with egg yolks dissolved in the remaining broth and lemon juice
