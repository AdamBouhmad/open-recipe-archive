## Ingredients

- beef brains
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- small onions
- sauce
- garlic
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- butter
- flour
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces and marinate with salt, pepper, vinegar, small onions, sauce, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, a small piece of butter passed through flour
2. Once well flavored with the marinade and dried very well, flour all the pieces, fry them, and serve garnished with fried sauce
