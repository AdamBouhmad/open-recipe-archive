## Ingredients

- mussels
- bacon fat
- truffles
- ham
- sauce
- pepper
- substance
- culí
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Clean and open the mussels and remove all shells
2. Remove any crabs inside and reserve the water they release
3. Pass them through melted bacon fat with slices of truffles and a piece of ham
4. Add a sprig of sauce and a pinch of pepper
5. Add a spoon of substance and let simmer little by little
6. When the truffles are cooked, bind with a little culí and remove all the fat
7. Add the mussels with a drop of their own water
8. Keep on low heat for a short time
9. Before boiling, serve with lime juice as an entrée
