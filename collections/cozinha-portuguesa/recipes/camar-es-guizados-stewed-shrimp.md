## Ingredients

- Shrimp
- Butter
- Mushrooms
- Truffles, sliced
- Parsley bouquet
- Fine herbs
- Fish stock
- Cullis of shrimp

## Directions

1. Rinse the shrimp thoroughly in two waters and clean them well
2. Pass them through the fire with butter, mushrooms, and sliced truffles
3. Add a drop of fish stock
4. Allow to simmer little by little
5. Once the sauce is reduced and of good taste, remove the butter
6. Bind with a cullis of shrimp
7. Serve hot as an entrée
