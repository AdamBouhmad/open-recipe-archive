## Ingredients

- Ham from Lamego or Montange
- Beef strips
- Veal strips
- Bacon strips
- Whole seasonings
- Peppercorns
- Fine herbs
- White wine
- Dough
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the ham, removing the skin and all bones
2. Soak as long as necessary to remove all salt
3. Cook between strips of beef, veal, and bacon, seasoned with whole seasonings, peppercorns, and fine herbs
4. Add half a bottle of white wine and let it simmer slowly over coals for five or six hours
5. Strain the substance through a sieve
6. When the ham is cold, place it in the pastry with a layer of fat below and all its substance
7. Cover with a dough lid and cook in the oven for two more hours
8. When cooked, open, drain the fat, place on its plate, and serve with Spanish sauce or any other piquant sauce
