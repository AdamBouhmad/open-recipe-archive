## Ingredients

- stuffed hen thighs
- cullis
- Parmesan cheese
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- chopped meat

## Directions

1. Prepare stuffed thighs and cook as described above
2. Put a little cullis with a handful of grated Parmesan cheese at the bottom of a plate
3. Arrange the hen thighs on top
4. Pour a little cullis thickened with butter over them
5. Cover everything with grated cheese and brown in the oven
6. When browned, drain the fat and serve hot. Alternatively, a béchamel bound with egg yolks can be added, covered with half breadcrumbs and half cheese, and served the same way after browning
7. Another way: put a little chopped meat at the bottom of the plate making borders of the same chopped meat two fingers high, arrange the hen legs in the middle with some ragout garnish, cover with chopped meat and fine breadcrumbs on top, cook in the oven. When cooked, drain the fat and serve as directed
8. The legs can also be stuffed with an Italian chopped mixture made with different herbs, hen breasts, cheese, eggs, and cream, and can be diversified in various ways according to the taste of the cook
