## Ingredients

- almond blancmange
- one or two eggs
- some flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat almond blancmange very well with one or two eggs and some flour
2. Drop spoon by spoon into a baking pan that is placed over hot coals
3. Space each bit of blancmange apart from the others, separating them with flour below
4. Cover with a lid of hot coals and roast
5. When roasted, place on a plate with scraped rock sugar on top and send to the table
