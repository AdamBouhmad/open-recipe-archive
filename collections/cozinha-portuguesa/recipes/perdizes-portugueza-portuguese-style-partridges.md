## Ingredients

- partridges
- lard strips of prosciutto
- cord
- olive oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- French capers

## Directions

1. Once the partridges are plucked, make six lengthwise cuts in each
2. Lard them inside the cuts with strips of prosciutto
3. Tie them tightly all around with cord
4. Place to roast with fire below and above in a shallow pan with olive oil, vinegar, and pepper
5. When roasted, place on a plate
6. Allow the sauce to boil with a few French capers
7. When boiled, pour over the partridges
8. Send to table
