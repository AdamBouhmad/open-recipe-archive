## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt
- smoke

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as pig tongues, with the difference that they should not be blanched, but only placed gently in cold water
2. Prepare and arrange the thick end of the tongue very well
3. Once well dried, salt it and leave it for two, three, or four days in salt water
4. Thread it through the thin point and hang it in the chimney in the smoke so that the smoke penetrates it well and it dries thoroughly
5. Prepared this way it will keep as long as desired, and cooks in the same manner as the other or pig tongues
