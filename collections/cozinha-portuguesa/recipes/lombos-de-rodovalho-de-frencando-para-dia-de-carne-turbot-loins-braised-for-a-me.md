## Ingredients

- turbot loins, larded
- white wine
- water
- salt
- onion slices
- lemon slices
- fine herbs
- veal glaze
- ham
- cullis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. With the loins larded as described above, serve them briefly in white wine and water, seasoned with salt, onion slices, lemon slices, and fine herbs
2. When they are barely cooked, finish cooking them larded-side down over a glaze made of veal and ham
3. When they are browned as they should be, remove them and break down with a little cullis what remains stuck to the bottom of the pan
4. Pass through a sieve onto the plate where they are to go on the table and serve with lemon juice
