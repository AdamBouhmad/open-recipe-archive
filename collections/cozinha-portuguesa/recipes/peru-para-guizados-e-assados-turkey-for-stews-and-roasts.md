## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Bacon (toucinho)
- Salt
- Paper leaves (folhas de papel manteigado)

## Directions

1. First it must be bled while still warm. In summer remove the entrails immediately without opening it, and after hanging for two days in a cool place and mortified, open it, empty it, and straighten it with all cleanliness.
2. Then singe it over a well-lit furnace, and rub it while hot with a piece of bacon, and after being dried and singed, tie it skillfully by the thighs, lard it with fine bacon, put it on the spit, cover it with leaves of buttered paper, and put it to roast with care.
3. When nearly roasted, remove the paper and finish roasting; when the larded part is well browned, remove it from the spit, and serve it as hot as possible.
