## Ingredients

- half a dozen young pigeons
- butter
- onion
- cloves
- parsley
- ham
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- milk
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen young pigeons, blanch them, empty them, straighten them, and tuck the feet inside the body
2. Stiffen them in hot water, then place them in a casserole with butter, clove-studded onion, a sprig of parsley, and a piece of ham
3. Pass everything over the fire for a brief moment, dust them with flour, add a little broth, season with salt and pepper
4. When cooked, remove the fat, parsley, and onion, thicken the broth with egg yolks dissolved in milk
5. When thickened, serve hot with lemon juice
