## Ingredients

- six pigeons, halved
- six young chickens, halved
- two hens, in quarters
- bacon
- slices of ham
- a dozen egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take six pigeons and six young chickens in halves, and two hens in quarters, and after everything is half-roasted in bacon, place it in a frying pan, between half and half, place slices of ham, and fill to the top
2. When it is full, add a dozen egg yolks, and brown it in the frying pan and send it to the table
