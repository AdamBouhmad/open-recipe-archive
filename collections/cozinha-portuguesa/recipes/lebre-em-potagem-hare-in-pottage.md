## Ingredients

- hare, well washed and larded
- lard
- onions
- herbs
- seasonings
- wine
- capers
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Roast a well-washed and larded hare
2. Once roasted, mince a little lard and place it to melt in a tall pot
3. Once melted, add two onions, all the herbs, and all the seasonings
4. Once braised, remove the cracklings with a skimming spoon and add half a quartilho of wine, seasoning with everything necessary
5. Pour this pottage over the hare, which should be placed on a plate, with capers and lemon slices on top
