## Ingredients

- Gannets
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Gannet livers
- Nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Clean the gannets, remove the entrails, position the feet behind the thighs, and tie with string
2. Place on the spit and begin to roast, basting with a sauce made of vinegar, butter, salt, and pepper
3. Once roasted, make a sauce with the chopped livers of the gannets, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and a piece of butter
4. Serve with sour orange juice
