## Ingredients

- beef palates
- béchamel sauce
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. After the palates are cooked, marinated, and cold, cut them into small dice
2. Add them to a well-made béchamel sauce
3. When cold, add some egg yolks with the whites well beaten, and mixed afterward carefully with a small spoonful of bread
4. Put them in a paper box greased with washed butter or in a plate dusted with breadcrumbs, and brown in the oven
5. Serve well hot
