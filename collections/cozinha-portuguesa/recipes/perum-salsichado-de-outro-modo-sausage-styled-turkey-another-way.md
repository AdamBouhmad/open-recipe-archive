## Ingredients

- one young turkey or turkey hen, well larded
- one piece of pork loin
- one piece of bacon or ham
- all black seasonings
- a measure of vinegar
- half quartilho of water

## Directions

1. Lard the young turkey or hen well
2. Place in a pot with a piece of pork loin, a piece of bacon or ham, all black seasonings, a measure of vinegar, and half quartilho of water
3. Seal the pot so steam does not escape
4. Cook slowly over gentle heat
5. When cooked, open and adjust seasoning as needed
6. Place on slices with lemon juice on top
7. Serve the turkey to the table, garnished with slices of the same larded loin
