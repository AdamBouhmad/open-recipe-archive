## Ingredients

- A point of beef brisket of desired weight
- thick well-seasoned bacon for larding
- a quartilho of white wine
- bacon slices for cover

## Directions

1. Take a point of beef brisket of desired weight and lard it with thick well-seasoned bacon
2. Place in a vessel appropriate to its size with a quartilho of white wine
3. After seasoning, cover with bacon slices and cover the vessel with its lid
4. Seal the lid with dough, place in the oven for five or six hours
5. Remove and serve with its own sauce, removing the fat first
6. The round piece of the leg can also be served in the same manner
