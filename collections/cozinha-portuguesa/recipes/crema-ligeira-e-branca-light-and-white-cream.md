## Ingredients

- one pan of milk
- salt
- sugar
- three egg whites, well beaten
- orange flower water
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Season one pan of milk with salt and sugar, and put it to boil
2. When reduced to two-thirds of its volume, remove from the heat
3. Add three well-beaten egg whites to it
4. Put the pan back over the fire, beat constantly with rods
5. After it boils three or four times, pour it into its serving dish
6. Let it cool, then sprinkle with orange flower water
7. Dust with fine sugar and send to the table
