## Ingredients

- Rabbits
- Butter
- Grated bread
- Two egg yolks
- Sausage
- Shallots
- Cucumbers
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thread
- Broth
- White wine
- A bundle of sausage, bay leaf
- Whole onion studded with cloves
- Garlic cloves
- Bacon slices

## Directions

1. Open the rabbits by the belly, clean, and eviscerate them, remove the bile from the livers
2. Make a chopped mixture with the livers, a piece of washed butter, grated bread, two egg yolks, sausage, shallots, cucumbers, salt, and pepper
3. Stuff the rabbits with this mixture, then sew the bellies shut with thread
4. Position the hind legs down under the belly and the front legs down under the snout, as if crouching
5. Secure them in this position with skewers and tied with string
6. Cook in a pan with a little broth, white wine, a bundle of sausage, bay leaf, an onion studded with cloves, some garlic cloves, salt, pepper, and bacon slices
