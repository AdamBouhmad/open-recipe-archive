## Ingredients

- Rabbits
- Butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sausage
- Shallots
- Water
- A quartilho of red wine
- A bundle of sausage, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram
- Whole onion studded with cloves
- Garlic cloves
- Whole shallots, blanched in hot water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the rabbits and cut them into pieces or quarters
2. Brown some flour in butter, then fry the rabbits in it
3. Moisten them with water and a quartilho of red wine, season with salt, pepper, and a bundle of sausage, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, a whole onion studded with cloves, and some garlic cloves
4. Bring to a boil and remove the scum, then let simmer gently
5. When almost cooked, add a dozen whole shallots blanched in hot water
6. When finished cooking and the sauce is reduced and has good flavor, remove the fat and the herbs
7. Serve with a touch of vinegar
