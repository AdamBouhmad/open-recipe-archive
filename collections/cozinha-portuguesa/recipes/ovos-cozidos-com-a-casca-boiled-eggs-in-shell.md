## Ingredients

- fresh eggs
- water

## Directions

1. The eggs should be fresh from the same day; place them in boiling water and leave them two minutes on the fire
2. Then remove the pot and cover it with its cold lid, and the eggs inside for another minute
3. Remove them and serve hot on a folded napkin
