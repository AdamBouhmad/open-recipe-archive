## Ingredients

- cooked ham
- three cucumbers
- small onions
- parsley
- one garlic clove
- broth
- pepper
- one bay leaf
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Sauté in butter cooked ham, three cucumbers, small onions, parsley, and one garlic clove, all chopped
2. After this is sautéed, add one spoonful of broth, season with pepper and one bay leaf
3. Let it boil gently
4. When the broth is reduced, bind it with two egg yolks
