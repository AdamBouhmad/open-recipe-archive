## Ingredients

- One shad, scaled, gutted, and without gills
- White or red wine
- A drop of vinegar
- Little salt
- Whole pepper
- All onions
- Some cloves of India
- Marjoram
- A piece of rind of green lemon
- A piece of butter

## Directions

1. Scale, gut, and remove gills from one shad
2. Place it to simmer little by little in a broth made with white or red wine, a drop of vinegar, little salt, whole pepper, all onions, some cloves of India, marjoram, a piece of rind of green lemon, and a piece of butter
3. When cooked, serve it on a napkin garnished with parsley
