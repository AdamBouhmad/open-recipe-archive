## Ingredients

- Chicórias (chicory)
- Barbante (twine)
- Essência (essence)
- Chaloita picada (minced shallot)
- Sumo de limão (lemon juice)

## Directions

1. Wash the chicory very well
2. Blanch them
3. Drain and tie them with twine
4. Cook them in a light braize
5. When cooked, drain them
6. Cut them into equal pieces
7. Place them in a good essence
8. Let them simmer for an instant
9. Add minced shallot
10. Then add lemon juice
11. Serve to garnish entrees
