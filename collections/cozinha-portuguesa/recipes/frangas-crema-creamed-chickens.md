## Ingredients

- two roasted chickens
- veal
- beef fat
- pork fat
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- parsley
- shallots
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take two roasted chickens and remove all the breast meat and mince it
2. Mix with a piece of veal, beef fat, and blanched pork fat
3. When all is minced, pound very well in a mortar
4. Add a bread crumb softened in milk
5. Season with salt, pepper, fine herbs, parsley, and shallots
6. Bind with egg yolks and some beaten egg whites
7. When seasoned, well blended, and pounded, fill the chicken bodies in place of the removed meat as if they were whole
8. Unite everything well with a knife blade moistened in eggs
9. Coat with breadcrumbs and put in oven to cook in a baking dish with slices of pork fat
