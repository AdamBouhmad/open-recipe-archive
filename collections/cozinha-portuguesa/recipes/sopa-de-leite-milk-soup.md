## Ingredients

- two cans of cow's milk
- salt
- a piece of sugar
- two cloves of India
- whole cinnamon
- a small piece of lemon peel
- egg yolks
- bread crumb

## Directions

1. Bring two cans of cow's milk to boil, seasoned with salt, with a piece of sugar, two cloves of India, whole cinnamon, and a small piece of lemon peel
2. After boiling for four minutes, pour in two spoons of this mixture a dozen egg yolks
3. Heat over the flame, stirring always with a spoon, and when thickened, but without boiling, pour over bread crumb cut into thin slices previously moistened in the boiled milk
