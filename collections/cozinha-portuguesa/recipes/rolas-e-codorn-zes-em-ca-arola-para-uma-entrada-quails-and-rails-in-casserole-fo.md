## Ingredients

- Quails or rails
- Fat hen livers
- Ham slices
- Salt
- Pepper
- Seasonings
- Broth
- Essence
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the quails or rails with legs folded inside the body, open slightly along the sides, and stuff with a mixture of fat hen livers
2. Cover the bottom of a casserole with ham slices, seasoned as usual, place the birds inside and cover with other slices like those at the bottom
3. Let them sweat over gentle heat for two hours
4. When cooked, remove the birds, add a drop of broth to the casserole, bring to boil for a moment, pass through a sieve, skim the grease, and reduce
5. When reduced, put the birds back in with breasts down, arrange them on their plate when of good color
6. Add a little essence to what remains in the bottom of the casserole, pour it over the birds, and serve with lemon juice
