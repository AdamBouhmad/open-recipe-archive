## Ingredients

- half a saucepan of milk
- half an arratel of butter
- a little salt
- lemon peels
- a loaf of lady fingers
- necessary flour

## Directions

1. Place in a saucepan half a saucepan of milk and half an arratel of butter, a little salt, lemon peels, and a loaf of lady fingers
2. When boiling, remove the necessary flour from it, as for buns, and after being dried on the fire and cold, stretch it with the dough roller, and placed at the necessary thickness, cut it
