## Ingredients

- must
- pears, apples, and pomegranates
- water

## Directions

1. Take an almiide of must, or as much as the quantity of sweet that is to be made, put it in a pot or kettle and place it to boil over lively heat
2. Once reduced to two-thirds and with good consistency, it serves in place of sugar for all kinds of fruits such as pears, apples, and pomegranates
3. These fruits, being first half-cooked in water, peeled and cleaned, are placed to boil in the must until they finish cooking
4. The must is reduced, which will be known by placing a drop of syrup on a plate and tilting it to one side; if it does not run, it has the right point
5. Afterward place the fruit in glazed boxes; after it cools, pour the syrup over it, cover it, and keep it in a cool place
6. The must can be made from white or black grapes equally
