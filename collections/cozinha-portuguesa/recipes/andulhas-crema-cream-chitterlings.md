## Ingredients

- three or four onions
- butter
- veal sweetbread
- pork belly
- pork lard
- one ear
- two breasts of roasted chicken
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- a little fennel or anise
- one quartilho of milk or egg yolks
- a little grated bread
- intestines for filling

## Directions

1. Cut three or four onions into thin fillets
2. Sauté them in a casserole in melted butter
3. Once sautéed, remove from the heat
4. Add veal sweetbread, pork belly, lard, and one ear, all blanched and chopped finely
5. Add two breasts of roasted chicken, also in fillets
6. Place back on the fire seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs, and a little fennel or anise
7. Sauté everything
8. Add one quartilho of milk or egg yolks and a little grated bread
9. Once thickened and having good taste, fill the intestines
10. Tie them as instructed
11. Cook as the preceding ones
12. Once cooled, cover them with fine grated bread
13. Serve hot
