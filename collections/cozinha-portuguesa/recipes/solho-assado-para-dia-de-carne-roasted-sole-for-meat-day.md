## Ingredients

- Lard of thick bacon, seasoned
- A large portion of sole
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Some cloves of India
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Fine herbs
- A piece of powdered butter with flour

## Directions

1. Cut lard of thick bacon, seasoned as is customary, in a large portion of sole
2. Marinate for two or three hours in warm water, vinegar, salt, pepper, some cloves of India, onion, garlic, parsley, fine herbs, and a piece of butter powdered with flour
3. Put it on a spit
4. Roast it, basting it with the most fatty part of its marinade
5. When roasted, serve it with any ragout or sauce of good taste
