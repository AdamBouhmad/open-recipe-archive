## Ingredients

- Three or four arratels of veal
- Fatias of ham
- One chicken quartered
- Two roots
- Two onions
- Some cloves of garlic
- One bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- Two cloves
- A little melted bacon fat
- One drop of good oil

## Directions

1. Place at the bottom of a pot three or four arratels of veal, slices of ham, one chicken in quarters, two roots, two onions, some cloves of garlic, one bundle of parsley and fine herbs, two cloves, a little melted bacon fat, and one drop of good oil
2. Cover the pot and place on high heat
3. When the meat is done and tender, add sufficient stock and thicken
4. Let it simmer little by little for four hours
5. Once reduced and of good taste, pass through a sieve and remove the fat
6. Use for various ragus and sauces
