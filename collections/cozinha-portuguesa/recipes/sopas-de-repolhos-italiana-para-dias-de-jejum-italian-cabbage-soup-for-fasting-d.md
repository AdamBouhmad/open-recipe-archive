## Ingredients

- one or two cabbages
- onions
- head of garlic
- parsley and marjoram bundle
- broth
- root substance
- salt
- cloves
- good olive oil

## Directions

1. Cut one or two cabbages into quarters and boil them
2. After cooking, place in cold water, squeeze them, tie with twine
3. Put to simmer in a small pot with whole onions, head of garlic, bundle of parsley and marjoram, a little broth and root substance, seasoned with salt, cloves, and half a spoonful of good olive oil
4. When the cabbages are cooked, moisten the soups with the same broth
5. Serve garnished with the cabbages on top and around
