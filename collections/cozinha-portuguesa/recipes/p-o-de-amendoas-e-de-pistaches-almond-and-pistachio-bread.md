## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- half a pound of pistachios
- egg whites
- a bit of citron
- lemon zest
- one pound of grated sugar
- fifteen fresh egg yolks
- beaten and raised egg whites
- butter
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch in hot water one pound of almonds and half a pound of pistachios
2. Place them in cold water, drain them in a cloth, rinse them
3. Grind them in a stone mortar with a few egg whites
4. When ground and reduced to a paste, add to it a bit of chopped citron and lemon zest, one pound of grated sugar, fifteen fresh egg yolks
5. Mix everything very well
6. Add to it the beaten and raised egg whites
7. Grease a dish with butter
8. Put everything on it
9. Join it around
10. Dust it with fine sugar
11. Put it to cook in the oven with moderate heat
12. After baked, serve it glazed and well colored
