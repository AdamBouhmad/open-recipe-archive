## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- chopped citron peel
- covered orange flower
- almond biscuits
- fine sugar
- salt
- a drop of milk
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Beat with rods and raise a dozen egg whites
2. Add the yolks, chopped citron peel, covered orange flower, all chopped, almond biscuits, fine sugar, salt, and a drop of milk
3. When everything is well mixed, cook as the previous ones in very hot clarified butter
4. After cooked, dust with fine sugar, brown with a hot spatula, and serve as an entrée
