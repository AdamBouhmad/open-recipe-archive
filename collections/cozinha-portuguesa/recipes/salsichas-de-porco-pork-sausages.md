## Ingredients

- two and a half arratels of pork from the leg
- one arratel of fat pork
- salt
- fennel seed
- white wine
- clean pork casing
- hot water

## Directions

1. Mince very well two and a half arratels of pork from the leg and one arratel of fat
2. Season with salt and very finely ground fennel seed and a splash of white wine on the same chopping block
3. Make them in clean pork casing and pass them first through hot water
4. Hang them in the chimney in a house so they dry
5. Fry them in a pot and send to the table
