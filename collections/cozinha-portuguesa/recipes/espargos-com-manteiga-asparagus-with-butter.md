## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Hot water
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Trim the asparagus and cook whole in water and salt
2. After cooking, strain them
3. Arrange neatly on a plate, pointing the tips toward the center
4. Make a sauce with butter dusted with flour, a drop of water, pepper, vinegar, and grated nutmeg
5. Place on heat, stirring constantly until it binds
6. Pour over the asparagus and serve for a course between meals
