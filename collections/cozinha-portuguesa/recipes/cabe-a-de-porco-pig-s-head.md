## Ingredients

- Pig's head
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Ginger
- Coriander seeds
- Bay leaves
- Marjoram
- Oregano
- Water
- Wine
- Onion
- Carrots
- Parsnips
- Lemon
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Rosemary

## Directions

1. Cut the pig's head at the shoulders, singe it, and clean it well
2. Open it from the bottom and remove the bones from the neck, jaws, and snout up to the eyes
3. Soak it to bleed and then dry it
4. Place it on a cloth, lard it with thick bacon, and season with enough salt, pepper, cloves, nutmeg, ginger, coriander seeds, bay leaves, marjoram, oregano, all crushed
5. Cover with sheets of bacon, wrap in a cloth, tie it very well and tightly, put it in a pan, add equal parts water and wine, rounds of onion, carrots, parsnips, rounds of lemon, parsley, bay, little thyme, and rosemary, whole salt and pepper, and let it boil slowly for five or six hours
6. When cooked, let it cool in its own broth to gain more flavor, then drain it, remove the cloth, and when well dried and trimmed, serve it on a napkin garnished with parsley as a cold dish and as an accompaniment
