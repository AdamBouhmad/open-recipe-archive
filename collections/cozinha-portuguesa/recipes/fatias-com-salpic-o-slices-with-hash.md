## Ingredients

- roasted capon or partridge breast, cut in small dice
- truffles
- sweetbreads
- egg yolks
- bread slices
- eggs
- fine breadcrumb

## Directions

1. Cut into small dice a roasted capon or partridge breast, truffles, and sweetbreads, and make a ragú of good flavor and well-bound
2. Let cool
3. Add two or three egg yolks
4. Place on bread slices, spread with a knife blade moistened in eggs
5. Cover with fine breadcrumb
6. Brown in the oven and serve with clear sauce on top
