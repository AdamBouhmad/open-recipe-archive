## Ingredients

- a pound of orange blossoms
- water
- alum stone in powder
- juice of a lemon
- sugar at point

## Directions

1. Choose and ensure they are very white, a pound of orange blossoms, boil them for a quarter hour in water, remove from heat, add a pinch of alum stone in powder
2. Then place water boiling with the juice of a lemon to boil, and with this boiling, gradually add the orange blossoms in a skimming spoon and allow it to boil again until it becomes tender and almost cooked
3. Pass it afterward to cold water and lemon juice; from there drain it and place it in sugar at point, and take it to heat repeatedly to warm, but do not allow it to boil
4. Fill small pots afterward, allow them to cool, cover them, and serve them like the aforementioned ones
