## Ingredients

- one handful of milk
- salt
- sugar
- dry coriander seed
- cinnamon stick
- green lemon zest
- chicken marrow

## Directions

1. Season one handful of milk with salt, sugar, dry coriander seed, a stick of cinnamon, and green lemon zest
2. Put on heat to boil until reduced to two-thirds
3. When reduced and almost cold, add two or three chicken marrow fillets
4. Pass two or three times through a napkin
5. Pour into the plate
6. Place over heat with coals on top
7. As soon as it is hot and curdled, remove it, let it cool, and serve cold
