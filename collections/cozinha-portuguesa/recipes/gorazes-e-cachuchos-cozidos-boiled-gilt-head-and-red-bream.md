## Ingredients

- gilt-head or red bream
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Scale, remove entrails and gills, wash well the gilt-head or red bream
2. Season with salt
3. Simmer gently in a broth made with water, half a quart of vinegar, salt, and some fine herbs
4. When cooked, serve with Dutch sauce
5. If eating with oil sauce, serve garnished with parsley sauce around it
