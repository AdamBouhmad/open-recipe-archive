## Ingredients

- one large turbot fish
- water
- one can of white wine
- salt
- whole pepper
- cloves of India
- onion wheels
- a piece of butter
- a small sprig of sage
- thyme
- bay leaf
- some parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the turbot into pieces according to the serving dish
2. Tie the pieces with twine
3. Place them in a copper pan with its leaves, cover with water and one can of white wine
4. Season with salt, whole pepper, cloves of India, onion wheels, a piece of butter, a small sprig of sage, thyme, bay leaf, and some parsley
5. Let it simmer little by little for the space of two hours
6. When cooked, serve garnished with beef
