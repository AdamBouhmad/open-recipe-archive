## Ingredients

- four ounces of orange blossoms, white and fresh
- juice of half a lemon
- one arratel of sugar at the point of thread

## Directions

1. Place the four ounces of orange blossoms on a table
2. Cut them into four pieces and moisten with the juice of half a lemon
3. Place in one arratel of sugar at the thread point, already on the heat
4. Stir constantly with a spoon and remove from heat immediately
5. Continue stirring until the sugar turns white around the edge of the pan
6. When this state is reached, pour into paper boxes about two or three fingers high
7. Support with a brick on each side and store in the boxes for whatever is needed
