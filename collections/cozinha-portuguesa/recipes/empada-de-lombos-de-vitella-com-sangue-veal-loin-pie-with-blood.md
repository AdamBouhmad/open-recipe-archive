## Ingredients

- Veal loin pieces
- Bacon
- Ham
- Pork blood or pigeon blood
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Veal, ground
- Truffle
- Garlic
- Butter, washed
- Bacon planks
- Pastry box with lid
- Eggs
- Perdiz sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Lard pieces of veal loin with bacon and ham
2. Marinate them in pork or pigeon blood, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. Make a ground mixture like the previous one, mixing in half a quartilho of blood, a truffle, and a clove of garlic, all chopped
4. Garnish the bottom of the pastry box with half of this mixture
5. Place the marinated veal pieces on top
6. Cover with the rest of the mixture, washed butter, and bacon planks
7. Finish the pie as the previous ones
8. Place in the oven to bake for six hours
9. When cooked, open it, remove the bacon, drain the fat
10. Serve with perdiz sauce and lemon juice
