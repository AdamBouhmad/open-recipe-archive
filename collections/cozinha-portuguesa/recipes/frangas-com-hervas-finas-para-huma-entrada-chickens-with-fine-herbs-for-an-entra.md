## Ingredients

- one or two chickens
- chicken livers
- scraped pork fat
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- half a clove of garlic
- a small piece of fresh butter
- two or three egg yolks
- pork fat for blanching

## Directions

1. Prepare one or two chickens and stuff with a filling made from their livers, scraped pork fat, parsley, chives, salt, pepper, half a clove of garlic, a small piece of fresh butter, and two or three egg yolks
2. Dry them very well so the filling does not fall out
3. Blanch them in melted pork fat and let cool
4. When cool, cover with slices of pork fat and sheets of paper
5. Roast according to custom
6. Serve with ravigote sauce or any other piquant sauce
7. The same method applies to turkeys, ducks, and young chickens
