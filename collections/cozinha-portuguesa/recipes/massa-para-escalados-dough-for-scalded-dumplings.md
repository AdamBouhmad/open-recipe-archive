## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half a pound of butter
- one ounce of ground salt
- twelve and a half fresh eggs
- three or four ounces of yeast

## Directions

1. Place on a table two pounds of flour, half a pound of butter, one ounce of ground salt, and twelve and a half fresh eggs, and knead everything two or three times with the palm of the hand
2. Then add three or four ounces of yeast broken into pieces, and knead everything again with the palm of the hand five or six times
3. Add the dough, dust with flour, and wrap in a towel until the next day
4. Remove, place on the table, shape into a roll, cut into small pieces, shape on both sides, place in boiling water stirred from time to time with a skimmer so it does not boil over
5. When the dumplings rise above the water, remove them, place in cold water, then place in a sieve to drain
6. When necessary, put in the oven to bake, and serve hot or cold as preferred
