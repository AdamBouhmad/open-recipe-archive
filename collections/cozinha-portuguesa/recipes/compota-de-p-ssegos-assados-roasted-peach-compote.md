## Ingredients

- Almost ripe peaches
- Clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Follow with almost ripe peaches, place them as with pears in a well-heated oven, and turn them until the skin is charred.
2. Put them immediately in cold water, peel them very thoroughly, wash them in many waters, and place them whole to cook in clarified sugar.
3. When the peaches soften between your fingers, place them on the plate, reduce the syrup as stated, and serve like the other compotes.
