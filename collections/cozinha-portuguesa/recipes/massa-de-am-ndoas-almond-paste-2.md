## Ingredients

- almonds
- hot water
- egg white
- a small spoonful of orange blossom water
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Pass the almonds through hot water, peel them, pound them in a stone mortar, adding from time to time a little beaten egg white and a small spoonful of orange blossom water
2. When pounded and reduced to paste, dry it on the fire with fine sugar, stirring constantly with a spoon until it comes away from the pot
3. Then pour it onto the table with powdered sugar, and serve for tarts, tartlets, bottoms of croquants, boxes, small baskets, flowers, and other different works
4. The trimmings pounded with egg whites to soften them serve for frying, for cooking in the oven, and for various garnishes
