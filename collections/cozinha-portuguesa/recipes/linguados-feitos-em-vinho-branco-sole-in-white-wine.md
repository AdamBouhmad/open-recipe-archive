## Ingredients

- soles
- salt
- pepper
- one onion studded with cloves
- bay leaves
- fine herbs
- six small green onions
- lemon slice
- butter
- white wine
- broth
- bread crumbs
- orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the soles, cut off heads and tails, wash and trim them
2. Arrange them in a pan, whole or in portions, with salt, pepper, one onion studded with cloves, bay leaf, fine herbs, six small green onions, a slice of lemon, a piece of butter, a quarter-pot of white wine, and a little broth
3. Put on a well-heated stove to simmer
4. When cooked, thicken with a little finely grated bread crumbs and a piece of washed butter
5. Serve after thickening with a little sauce and orange or lemon juice
