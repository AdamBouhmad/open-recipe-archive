## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar at point
- brandy

## Directions

1. Cook the fruits whole and then place them in sugar at point, as in the preserves already mentioned
2. Once boiling, if there are for example a hundred apricots, pour in a large measure of brandy and allow it to boil for an instant with the caution that the fire does not go out; if this should happen, it will be extinguished by placing a wet cloth over it
3. Allow to cool after it is made, place in bottles or flasks, cover with syrup, seal very well, and keep for when necessary
