## Ingredients

- one vintém of bread
- bread slices
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- washed butter
- saffron
- chicken or mutton broth
- twelve eggs
- roasted meat (chicken, young roosters, pigeons, or turkey)

## Directions

1. Cut one vintém of bread into slices
2. Place one slice in a large buttered skillet
3. Cover with sugar and cinnamon
4. On this layer, place another slice the same way
5. On top place a small amount of washed butter with saffron
6. Add a little chicken or mutton broth
7. Let it stew slowly over low heat
8. Remove from heat and pour a dozen eggs on top (or fewer, depending on the skillet size)
9. Season with sugar and cinnamon
10. Take a lid with flame and place it raised slightly over the soup until it takes on good color
11. Remove from skillet and place on plate
12. Carve the roast, which can be chicken, young roosters, pigeons, or turkey
13. This is an ordinary plate
