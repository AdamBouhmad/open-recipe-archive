## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh, soft cheese from Alem-Tejo
- another pound of fresh butter
- one eighth of flour
- five or six eggs

## Directions

1. Grind in a stone mortar one pound of fresh, soft cheese from Alem-Tejo with another pound of fresh butter
2. After being ground, place it on the table
3. Add to it one eighth of flour and five or six eggs
4. Knead everything together
5. When the dough is bound, wrap it in a damp cloth for one hour
6. Then remove it, form the cake, pour it into a casserole, bake it in the oven
7. Serve it plain
