## Ingredients

- a dozen egg yolks
- six egg whites
- half a quarter of milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- a little grated lemon peel
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat a dozen egg yolks and six egg whites in a casserole with half a quarter of milk
2. Season with salt, sugar, cinnamon, and a little grated lemon peel
3. Whisk and stir with butter over heat
4. Pour into the plate in which it is to be served, with lemon juice and six beaten egg whites mixed with fine sugar
5. Put in the oven or under a lid to brown
6. Serve hot
