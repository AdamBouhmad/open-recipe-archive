## Ingredients

- gilt-head or red bream
- flour
- oil
- butter
- anchovy
- minced scallion
- minced parsley
- wine
- fish stock
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- capers
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and prepare the fish
2. Make incisions on each side
3. Dry and coat with flour
4. Fry in oil
5. After frying, set aside to drain
6. In a pan, melt a piece of butter, add some flour, stir with a spoon until well browned
7. Dissolve an anchovy, minced scallion, and minced parsley in the pan
8. Add a glass of wine and half a spoon of stock
9. Place the fried fish inside, season with salt, pepper, and a bay leaf
10. Let simmer gently
11. Add capers
12. Serve with a drop of vinegar or lemon juice
