## Ingredients

- one arratel of veal meat from the leg
- one chicken
- one canada of water
- borrage
- lettuce
- ox tongue
- wild chicory
- chervil
- pimpinella

## Directions

1. Put one arratel of veal meat from the leg and one chicken in a pan
2. Add one canada of water and place over heat
3. Skim and let simmer slowly
4. When the meat is almost cooked, add the following herbs: borrage, lettuce, ox tongue, wild chicory, chervil, and pimpinella
5. After simmering and when the meat is fully cooked, strain and press everything through a cloth
6. Remove the fat
7. Use this broth over the course of fifteen days or three weeks, or as long as seems fit, drinking it in the morning and in the afternoon
