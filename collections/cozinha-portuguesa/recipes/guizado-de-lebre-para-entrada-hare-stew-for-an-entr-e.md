## Ingredients

- hare
- thick lard
- melted lard
- broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- onion studded with cloves
- bundle of parsley
- fine herbs
- liver
- blood
- juice of sour oranges

## Directions

1. Take a hare, clean it, and cut it into quarters
2. Lard these with thick lard, braise in melted lard, dust with flour, moisten with broth and white wine
3. Season with salt, pepper, an onion studded with cloves, a bundle of parsley, and fine herbs
4. When cooked and the sauce reduced and of good taste, remove the fat and serve with juice of sour oranges or also with the liver and blood as in the preceding recipe
