## Ingredients

- One chicken
- One arratel of bacon
- Aromatics
- All seasonings
- Water
- One eighth of flour
- Half an arratel of sugar
- Six eggs
- Butter
- A splash of wine
- Bay leaves
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken with one arratel of bacon and aromatics with all seasonings and little water so that it remains well-bodied.
2. When cooked, melt the bacon and pour it into a shallow bowl in which you place the chicken in quarters to brown on low flame underneath and on top.
3. When browned, make a fine batter of one eighth flour with half an arratel of sugar, six eggs, its butter, and a splash of wine.
4. From this batter make some small balls with bay leaves, frying them and placing them in layers with sugar and cinnamon on top in a plate until full.
5. Place the chicken on top.
6. If desired, you can put egg threads on this plate.
7. In the same way one makes mutton, pigeons, young chickens, and kid goat.
