## Ingredients

- tender peas
- melted butter
- flour
- hot water
- salt
- parsley bundle
- small onion
- cloves
- sugar
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Take very tender peas and pass them through melted butter dusted with flour
2. Add then a cup of hot water, season with salt, a bundle of parsley, a small onion, and two cloves, and leave to simmer little by little
3. When cooked, add a small piece of sugar, bind with egg yolks mixed with cream, and serve for an entre-mets course
