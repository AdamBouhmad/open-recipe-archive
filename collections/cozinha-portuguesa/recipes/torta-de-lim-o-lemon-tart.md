## Ingredients

- a dozen lemons sliced thinly
- one and a half arratels of sugar at thread stage
- French puff pastry dough
- slices of citron
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Blanch a dozen lemons sliced thinly with all pith removed in two boilings of water until they no longer taste bitter.
2. Finish cooking them in one and a half arratels of sugar at thread stage.
3. When the syrup is thick, let cool.
4. Once the puff pastry tart is made, put the lemon mixture inside, place a layer of citron slices on top, and close it.
5. After baking, send to the table with sugar on top.
