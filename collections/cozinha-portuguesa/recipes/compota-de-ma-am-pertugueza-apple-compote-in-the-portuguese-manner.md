## Ingredients

- a dozen apples
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Pierce a dozen apples on top and remove the seeds and pit, arrange them whole inside a baking dish or on a silver plate with a little fine sugar underneath
2. Fill the apples with the same sugar, and put them to cook in the oven
3. When cooked, serve them hot with a little sugar on top
