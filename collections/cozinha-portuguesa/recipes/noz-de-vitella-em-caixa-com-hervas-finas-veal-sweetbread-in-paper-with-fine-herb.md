## Ingredients

- one veal sweetbread or other piece of leg
- melted bacon or olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- truffles
- shallots
- cucumbers
- butter
- lemon juice
- white vinegar

## Directions

1. Trim the veal sweetbread or any other piece of leg, beat it between two cloths with the flat of a knife
2. Marinate it afterwards in melted bacon or a little olive oil with salt, pepper, parsley, truffles, shallots, and cucumbers all finely minced
3. Remove it from the marinade, place it in a paper box lined with butter, and place it to roast on top of a grill over another buttered paper with gentle fire, taking care not to burn the paper
4. Once roasted in this way on both sides, it will be served in its same box with lemon juice or with a splash of white vinegar
5. If the veal sweetbreads are small and tender, being very well trimmed and beaten between two cloths, marinate them as above, adding some bay leaf, and roasted also in a box, remove them and serve on a plate with good sauce and lemon juice
