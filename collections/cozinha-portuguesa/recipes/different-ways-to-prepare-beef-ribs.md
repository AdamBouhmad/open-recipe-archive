## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives, minced
- a drop of oil
- breadcrumbs
- shallot sauce

## Directions

1. Once the ribs are tenderized and trimmed well and somewhat thick, season them with salt, pepper, parsley, minced chives, a drop of oil, and dust them with breadcrumbs
2. Grill them and serve with shallot sauce. They can also be cooked in a braize and served with different vegetable ragús. They can also be marinated after cooking in the braize, and once cold with oil or butter, parsley, chives, and shallots. They can also be wrapped in paper with all their marinade and grilled on top of another sheet of paper and served hot in their papillote
