## Ingredients

- venison livers
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- beaten eggs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Remove skin from venison livers
2. Sprinkle and then braise with butter, parsley, and chopped chives
3. Season when braised with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs
4. Allow to cool
5. Cold, mix with beaten eggs
6. Mix and make bundles with melted butter
7. Cook on both sides over hot spatula
8. Serve with spicy sauce
