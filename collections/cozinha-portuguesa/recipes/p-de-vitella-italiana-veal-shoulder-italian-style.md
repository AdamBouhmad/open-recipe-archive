## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- veal sweetbreads
- veal tongue
- butter
- small onions
- sorrel
- lettuce
- peas
- chervil
- ham stock or broth
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Take a veal shoulder and carefully lift the skin, leaving it attached only at the tip of the bone, and remove all other bones
2. Lard the meat with lardoons of thick bacon tempered with salt, pepper, herbs, and a hint of garlic, all finely chopped and dusted with flour, and cover it with its skin
3. Tie it with twine and place it to cook in a braise
4. In a separate pan, sauté small onions, sorrel, lettuce, peas, and chervil in good butter and reduce by heat with stock or broth, or broth and braise until cooked and well-flavored
5. Remove the veal shoulder from the braise, remove the twine, lift the skin
6. Pour the herb ragout over the meat and cover it with the same skin
7. Place it on its serving platter with some of the herb ragout underneath, and brush it with clarified butter mixed with two or three egg yolks
8. Cover everything with grated Parmesan cheese, place it in the oven to brown, and serve hot, cleaning the edges of the platter very well
9. If you do not have broth or stock, after the herbs have been sautéed in butter and lightly floured, moisten them with milk and thicken with egg yolks also mixed in a little milk
