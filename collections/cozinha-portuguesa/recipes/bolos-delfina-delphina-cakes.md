## Ingredients

- a bit of royal paste
- half a dozen almond biscuits
- candied orange peel
- grated lime peel

## Directions

1. Take a bit of royal paste, dissolve it in eggs, add half a dozen almond biscuits, candied orange peel, and grated lime peel, all in powder form
2. Form the cakes to the size desired
3. Place on buttered Flanders paper
4. Put in the oven to bake with moderate heat
5. When baked, cover with white glaze, and when it is dry, serve as the others
