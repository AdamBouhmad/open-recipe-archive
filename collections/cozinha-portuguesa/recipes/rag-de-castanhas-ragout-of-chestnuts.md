## Ingredients

- Meio cento de castanhas verdes (fifty green chestnuts)
- Vinho branco (white wine)
- Caldo ou substância (stock or essence)
- Culis de presunto (ham extract)
- Culis de vitela (veal extract)
- Salhichas (sausages)

## Directions

1. Take fifty green chestnuts
2. Shell them
3. Peel them in boiling water
4. Finish cooking them in white wine and stock or essence
5. When cooked and whole, thicken with ham or veal extract
6. Season to taste
7. Serve for terrines and other entrees
8. Sausages can also be added
