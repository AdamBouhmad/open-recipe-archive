## Ingredients

- One onion in thin slices
- Olive oil
- Pork essence
- Parsley
- Chives
- Two cloves
- Fine herbs
- Two lemon slices without skin
- Four cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Cut one onion into thin slices and sauté in olive oil; after sautéing, add pork essence, a bundle of parsley, chives, two cloves, fine herbs, two lemon slices without skin, and four cloves of garlic
2. Let it cook little by little; when reduced and of good taste, remove the olive oil, strain through a sieve, and serve for what is necessary
