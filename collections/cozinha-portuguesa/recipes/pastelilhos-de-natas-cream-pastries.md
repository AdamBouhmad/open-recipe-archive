## Ingredients

- Six handfuls of cream
- Fifteen egg yolks
- Fine sugar
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Whip fine sugar with six handfuls of cream and fifteen egg yolks
2. Thicken over the fire
3. Line the forms with puff pastry afterwards
4. Place the cream inside
5. Put them to cook in the oven
6. When the dough is cooked and has good color
7. Serve hot
