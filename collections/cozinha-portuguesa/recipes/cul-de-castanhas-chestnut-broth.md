## Ingredients

- Half a hundred chestnuts
- Veal substance
- Ham substance

## Directions

1. Remove the first shell from half a hundred chestnuts and put them in the oven to remove the second skin
2. When peeled, boil them in substance or stock
3. When cooked, pound them in a stone mortar
4. Dissolve the pounded chestnuts with veal and ham substance
5. Pass through a strainer, put in a casserole, place over heat, skim it
6. When it has good color and good taste, use for soups, terrines, and other entrees
7. All broths of vegetables, roots, and purees should be finished in the same manner
