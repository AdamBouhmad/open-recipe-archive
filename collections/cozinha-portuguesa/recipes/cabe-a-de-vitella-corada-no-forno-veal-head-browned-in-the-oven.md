## Ingredients

- cooked veal head
- slices of bacon
- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- broth
- vinegar
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take the cooked veal head as described above, remove it and let it dry well
2. Place it on a torteira or platon with slices of bacon underneath and thick sauce made with a good piece of butter, flour, egg yolks, half a glass of broth, and a little vinegar
3. Put it on the fire, and when well bound, pour it over the head
4. Cover the head with breadcrumbs, put it in the oven or under the lid of a torteira with fire, and when browned, straighten it on its plate and serve with the sauces mentioned above or with whichever seems best
