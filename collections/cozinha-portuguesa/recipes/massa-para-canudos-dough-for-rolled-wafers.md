## Ingredients

- flour
- fine sugar
- a piece of butter
- two eggs
- a bit of grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Take equal amounts of flour and fine sugar, a piece of butter, two eggs, and a bit of grated lemon rind
2. Knead with hot water so that it is not soft
3. Let it rest, and use it for whatever seems best
