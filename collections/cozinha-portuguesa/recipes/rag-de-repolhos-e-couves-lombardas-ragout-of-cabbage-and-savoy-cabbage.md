## Ingredients

- Cabbage, cut into quarters
- Water
- Binding material
- Meat or game

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into quarters, blanch in water, squeeze, and tie with binding material
2. Cook in a light braise
3. When cooked, drain and arrange around any game or piece of meat
4. Serve with essence on top
5. It can also be served stuffed with minced meat and various garnishes in the middle
6. It can also be cooked with pieces of pork, sausages, and charcuterie
