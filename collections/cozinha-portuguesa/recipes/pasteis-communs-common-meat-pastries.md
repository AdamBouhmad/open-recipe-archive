## Ingredients

- Two arratels of mutton, finely chopped
- One quarter of chopped bacon
- Aromatics
- One spoon of broth
- Vinegar
- All ground seasonings
- Three egg yolks
- One eighth of flour
- One quarter of sugar
- Half an arratel of butter
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Brown the finely chopped mutton with one quarter chopped bacon and aromatics, adding one spoon of broth
2. When half-cooked, season with vinegar and cook with all ground seasonings
3. Thicken with three beaten egg yolks and let cool
4. Toast one eighth of flour with one quarter of sugar, half an arratel of butter, and two egg yolks, kneading the dough very well
5. Shape pastries in boxes of desired size and fill with the meat
6. Cook them
7. When serving, dissolve one egg yolk in one spoon of broth with lemon juice and put a small amount of this sauce through a hole in each pastry so the meat becomes more tender
