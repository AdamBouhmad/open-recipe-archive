## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- sugar equal to the weight of the eggs
- grated lemon rind
- conserved orange flower
- egg whites
- flour equal to half the weight of the eggs
- clarified butter
- fine sugar for glaze
- egg white for glaze
- lemon juice for glaze

## Directions

1. Put twenty egg yolks in a bowl with sugar equal to their weight, grated lemon rind, and conserved orange flower
2. Beat with two spoons for half an hour
3. Add the beaten egg whites
4. After well mixing, gradually add flour equal to half the weight of the eggs with care
5. Pour into a copper mold of your choice, lined first with clarified butter
6. Bake in a moderate oven for one and a half to two hours
7. When cooked and golden, serve plain, or cover with a glaze made of fine sugar, egg white, and lemon juice
