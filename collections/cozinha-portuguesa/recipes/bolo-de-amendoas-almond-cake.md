## Ingredients

- half an eighth of flour
- four eggs with their egg whites
- a little bit of butter
- one quarter of fine sugar
- a few grains of salt
- six ounces of ground almonds

## Directions

1. Take half an eighth of flour, four eggs with their egg whites, a little bit of butter, one quarter of fine sugar, a few grains of salt, six ounces of ground almonds
2. Knead everything together
3. When bound and of good consistency, form the cake
4. Place it on buttered paper
5. Put it to cook in the oven with moderate heat
6. After baked, browned, and glazed, serve it when desired
