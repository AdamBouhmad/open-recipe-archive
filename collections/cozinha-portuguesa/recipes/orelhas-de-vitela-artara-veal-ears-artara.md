## Ingredients

- Veal ears
- Broth
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shallot
- Verjuice or lemon juice
- Bone marrow
- Bread cooked in milk
- Grated cheese
- Egg yolks
- Ham essence

## Directions

1. After well scalding and cleaning the ears, open them somewhat on the underside, and pass a skewer through them so that they remain open; place them to cook in a white braise as the preceding ones; and when cooked, and dry, pass them through melted butter, cover them with breadcrumbs, place them to brown on the grill, and having good color, serve them with sauce made with broth, salt, pepper, chopped shallot, and verjuice
2. Also after the ears are cooked and cold, they can be stuffed with a chopped of bone marrow of bread cooked in milk, grated cheese, butter, and egg yolks; and passing them through melted butter, and covering them with breadcrumbs and cheese, place them to brown in the oven, or under a lid, and serve them thus dried; or when not, with sauce of clear ham essence
