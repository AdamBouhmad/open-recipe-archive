## Ingredients

- trout
- water
- vinegar
- parsley
- salt
- vinegar for the conserve
- water for the conserve
- salt for the conserve
- olive oil for the conserve

## Directions

1. Wash the trout very well in water so they do not have any sand, open them very little only to remove the gills and some of the innards
2. Place them in a bowl with a little vinegar, water, parsley, and seasoned with salt, all boiling
3. While boiling, place the trout inside, letting them take only one boiling, then remove them and place them on a plate or where they are to remain, cold
4. For the conserve: take one part strong vinegar and two parts water, or if the vinegar is mild, equal parts, in quantity enough so the fruits are covered
5. Boil all the water and vinegar with a small amount of parsley, seasoning with salt, and after boiling well, remove from heat and let cool
6. After cold, pour it over the trout so they remain fresh, and make sure they are well covered with this conserve
