## Ingredients

- sweet almonds
- bitter almonds
- melon seeds
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch in hot water one pound of sweet almonds, one ounce of bitter almonds, and four ounces of peeled melon seeds.
2. Pound it all very well, adding from time to time a small spoonful of water so that it does not turn to oil.
3. When well pounded and moist, pour into half a pint of hot water.
4. Let it infuse for two or three hours over gentle heat.
5. Force through a cloth with a spoon.
6. Pour what was extracted into two pounds of sugar in the hard crack stage.
7. Without boiling it and mixing it well, pour it into a bowl.
8. Cover it with its lid.
9. Place it over gentle heat with equal warmth, and let it sit for two or three days.
10. When the sugar is reduced to the consistency of maidenhair fern syrup, put it in bottles and guard it as has been said in the preceding ones.
