## Ingredients

- one loin of veal, pork, or venison
- bacon
- pepper
- a small amount of toasted flour
- four egg yolks
- broth, two spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Take a loin and cut it into very thin slices, and fry them in bacon half-fried
2. Then add pepper and a small amount of toasted flour, four egg yolks, so that it thickens the broth, which you are to add, which should be two spoonfuls, and then serve it over slices to the table
