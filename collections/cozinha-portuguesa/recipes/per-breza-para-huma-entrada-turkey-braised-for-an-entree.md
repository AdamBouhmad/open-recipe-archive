## Ingredients

- turkey
- thick bacon
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- veal slices
- onions
- roots
- cloves
- white wine
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the turkey, straightening it with legs inside the body
2. Sear it and lard it with thick bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. Tie it with string
4. Place it in a well-supplied braise with bacon, veal slices, onions, roots, salt, pepper, cloves, and fine herbs
5. Position between two fires to sweat
6. Moisten with broth and some white wine
7. When cooked, place to drain and remove the string
8. Serve with vegetable ragout or any other preferred ragout
