## Ingredients

- Two onions
- Quarter measure of beef butter
- Some shrimp or clams
- Milk
- Quarter measure of almonds
- Half arratel of rice
- Water
- Salt
- Fish portions
- Seasonings

## Directions

1. Stew two well-chopped onions in a quarter measure of beef butter
2. Add some shrimp or clams with milk, a quarter measure of almonds, and cook everything until it becomes somewhat thick
3. Season with seasonings
4. Cook half arratel of rice in water and salt, place it on a plate, and on top place some portions of fish that have been cooked in water
5. Pour the curry over the top
