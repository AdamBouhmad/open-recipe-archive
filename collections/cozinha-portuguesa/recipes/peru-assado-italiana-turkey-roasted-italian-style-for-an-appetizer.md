## Ingredients

- One turkey
- chicken livers
- butter
- lemon slices
- parsley
- marjoram
- shallots
- tarragon
- sorrel
- hot water
- egg yolks
- white wine
- good olive oil
- anchovies
- capers
- salt
- pepper
- cullis

## Directions

1. Clean one turkey and fill it inside with a mince made of chicken livers as previously described
2. Straighten it, tie it with twine, and put it to blanch over heat in a casserole with pork butter, lemon slices, parsley, and marjoram whole, and leave it so it remains very white
3. Once blanched, let it cool, and when cold, place it on the spit covered with bacon strips and paper, and put it to roast
4. Afterwards take a little parsley, small onions, tarragon, sorrel, and blanch everything in hot water, squeeze it very well afterwards, chop everything very fine, put what is necessary in a casserole with four egg yolks, half a cup of white wine, a little good olive oil, a pair of anchovies, a pair of capers, all well chopped, salt, pepper, and a little cullis, and put it over the heat with two lemon slices without skin, and stir always around with a wooden spoon, taking care that it does not curdle
5. Once bound and of good taste, and the turkey roasted, remove the paper and bacon, place it and arrange it on its platter with the aforementioned sauce on top, and serve hot
