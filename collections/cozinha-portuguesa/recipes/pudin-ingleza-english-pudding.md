## Ingredients

- flour
- water or milk
- beef fat
- eggs
- raisins
- sugar
- brandy
- salt
- nutmeg
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak one or two pounds of flour in water or milk
2. Add chopped beef fat, eight or ten eggs, raisins from Alicante, some sugar, a dram of brandy
3. Season with salt, nutmeg flower, and ground cinnamon
4. Mix everything very well
5. Put in a napkin
6. Tie it and drop into boiling water
7. Continue boiling for two to three hours
8. Once cooked, drain it
9. Remove from napkin
10. Dust with sugar
11. Serve with butter sauce
