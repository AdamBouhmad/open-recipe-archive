## Ingredients

- Peas, one eighth measure, stewed with butter or melted bacon
- Lettuces
- Chervil
- Sorrel
- Three or four small onions
- Broth
- Veal substance
- Bread crusts boiled in broth

## Directions

1. Stew the peas with butter or melted bacon, one eighth measure of peas, stewed with lettuces, chervil, sorrel, three or four small onions, all cut finely
2. Add a little broth and veal substance
3. Let it simmer little by little
4. When everything is cooked and of good taste, remove the fat
5. Serve over bread crusts boiled in broth and dried, as mentioned before
