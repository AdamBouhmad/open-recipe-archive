## Ingredients

- Fish, prepared
- Water
- Salt
- Butter sauce
- pickled onion
- capers
- salt
- parsley, chopped
- nutmeg, grated
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish and make a salt brine of water and salt
2. Bring to a boil; when boiling, put the fish inside and let it continue to boil slowly
3. When cooked, remove it and serve with butter sauce, a pickled onion, capers, salt, chopped parsley, grated nutmeg, and lemon juice
