## Ingredients

- dozen egg yolks
- half dozen egg whites
- melted butter
- chopped sorrel
- a bit of garlic
- grated nutmeg
- Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and whites together
2. Put them in melted butter with chopped sorrel and a bit of garlic
3. Add grated nutmeg and optionally grated Parmesan cheese
4. Stir constantly until cooked
5. Serve hot
