## Ingredients

- two dozen sweet almonds
- shells from fifty shrimp
- four hard egg yolks
- bread crusts soaked in broth
- fish substance

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar two dozen sweet almonds, moistening occasionally with water
2. When pounded, add the shells from fifty shrimp, four hard egg yolks, and pound everything well together with bread crusts soaked in broth
3. Dissolve two spoonfuls of fish substance and place over heat in a pot, but before boiling, pass through a strainer
4. When cooked, hot, and good flavor, serve over bread crusts fried in butter and cooked in the same broth
