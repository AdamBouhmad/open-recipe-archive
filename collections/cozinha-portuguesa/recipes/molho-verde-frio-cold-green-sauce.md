## Ingredients

- chervil
- pimpinella
- tarragon
- mint
- purslane
- green chives
- fine olive oil
- half clove of garlic, minced
- salt
- pepper
- one drop of water
- vinegar
- two small spoons of mustard

## Directions

1. Pound in a stone mortar chervil, pimpinella, tarragon, mint, purslane, green chives, each according to its strength; when pounded, dissolve everything in fine olive oil, half clove of garlic minced, salt, pepper, one drop of water, vinegar, two small spoons of mustard; mix everything, and tasting good, serve it apart in a sauce boat.
