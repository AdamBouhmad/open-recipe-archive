## Ingredients

- peaches (not small, not soft, very dry)
- sugar in ribbon point

## Directions

1. Clean the peaches in a sieve
2. Select those that are not small nor soft
3. Dry them very well
4. Remove the pits with an iron tool that runs around like expelling the pit
5. Replace it in the peach after doing this to all
6. Keep them four days in sugar at ribbon point, giving it one boiling each day
7. On the last boiling remove them from the sugar to take out the pits
8. Cover them with the same sugar which should be at the proper point
9. Drain them on a grate or sieve
10. Place them on a tray to dry in the sun so cold does not strike them
11. When keeping them after being well dried, place them in linen cloth so they do not dampen
