## Ingredients

- Two or three turkeys
- Partridge breasts
- Veal sweetbreads
- Cured ham
- Truffles
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- String
- Pork lard
- Braise liquid
- Grated bread
- Grilling implements
- Shallot sauce
- Lemon juice
- Eggs
- Fried sauce

## Directions

1. Take two or three turkeys, singe them lightly, remove the thighs leaving enough skin around, remove the bones leaving only the small bone at the point
2. Remove also part of the inner meat
3. Stuff them with a mixture made of partridge breasts, veal sweetbreads, cured ham, and truffles all cut into small dice, well seasoned with salt, pepper, and spices, mixed with scraped bacon
4. When stuffed, wrap them with string in a circular motion and close them like a purse
5. Brown in pork lard
6. Clean them very well, then cook in a braise
7. Serve with whatever sauce seems best
8. If desired, they can also be coated with grated bread and broiled on grills, and served with shallot sauce and lemon juice
9. Also, after cooking, they can be passed through eggs and coated with grated bread, doing this twice, then fried in pork lard and served with fried sauce around them
