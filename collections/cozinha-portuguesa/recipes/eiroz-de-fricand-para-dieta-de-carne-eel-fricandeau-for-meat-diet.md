## Ingredients

- large eel
- lard with fine bacon
- white wine
- onions
- bay leaves
- veal stock
- ragú of vegetables with béchamel or other sauce, or bound essence

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare a large eel
2. Cut off the head and tail tip
3. Split down the middle
4. Remove the loins
5. Lard with fine bacon
6. Boil briefly in white wine with sliced onions and bay leaves
7. Remove and place over reduced veal stock, almost at glaze point, with the larded side down
8. Cook slowly until golden like fricandeau
9. Serve with ragú of vegetables with béchamel, or any other sauce, or bound essence of good taste, as you prefer
