## Ingredients

- veal loin, cleaned of nerves and skin
- half arratel of lard
- quarter pound of butter
- quarter litre of white wine
- two heads of garlic
- vinegar
- water
- whole pepper
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- half dozen cloves
- two bay leaves
- some quarters of quince

## Directions

1. Once a veal loin is cleaned of nerves and skin, place it in a pan with half arratel of lard, quarter pound of butter, quarter litre of white wine
2. Add two heads of garlic, a splash of vinegar, a splash of water, whole pepper, cinnamon, nutmeg, half dozen cloves, two bay leaves, and some quarters of quince
3. Seal the pan and cook slowly over gentle heat
4. Once cooked, season as needed
5. Place the loin on slices of bread and send to the table
6. This dish can also be made larded, and in the same manner one makes venison and loin of any meat
