## Ingredients

- hare
- quarter of lard
- two heads of garlic
- herbs
- clove
- pepper
- ginger
- cumin
- vinegar
- hare blood

## Directions

1. Carefully drain the blood from a hare
2. After washing it well, place it to cook with a quarter of lard, two heads of garlic, and some herbs
3. Season with clove, pepper, ginger, cumin, and vinegar
4. Once cooked, add the blood
