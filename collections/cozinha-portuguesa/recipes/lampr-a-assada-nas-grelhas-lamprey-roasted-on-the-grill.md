## Ingredients

- One lamprey, cleaned and trimmed
- Butter, melted
- Parsley
- Minced chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Bread crumb, finely grated
- Sweet sauce or hot remoulade sauce with mustard

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the lamprey
2. Cut into four pieces or curl it
3. Pass it for an instant through melted butter with parsley, minced chives, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
4. Cover with finely grated bread crumb
5. Place on the grill
6. Roast and baste on both sides until of good color
7. Serve with sweet sauce or hot remoulade sauce with mustard
