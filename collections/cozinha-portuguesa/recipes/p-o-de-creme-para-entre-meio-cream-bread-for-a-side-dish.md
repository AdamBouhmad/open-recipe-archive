## Ingredients

- one round loaf of bread of thirty reis
- milk
- sugar
- franchipane cream
- lemon zest
- fine grated sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the top crust of one round loaf of bread of thirty reis
2. Open it from the bottom part, remove all the crumb from it, and pour it into milk and sugar for a few minutes
3. Then remove it to a sieve and let it drain
4. From this make a franchipane cream well bound
5. Stuff the bread with it
6. Place under it the piece of crust that was removed
7. Arrange it on the dish in which it is to be served with a little of the same cream underneath and on top
8. Its fillets of lemon zest in symmetry
9. Dust it with fine grated sugar
10. Put it to cook in the oven
11. When baked, glazed, and well colored, serve it hot
