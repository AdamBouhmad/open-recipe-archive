## Ingredients

- one cleaned and plucked turkey
- half an arratel of ham
- one Portuguese gold coin
- one stone of sugar weighing one quarter
- two egg yolks
- two medlars or one quince, or if not available, two sour apples
- two ounces of butter
- all seasonings whole
- vinegar
- salt
- little water

## Directions

1. Stuff the cleaned and plucked turkey with half an arratel of ham containing one Portuguese gold coin, one stone of sugar weighing one quarter, two egg yolks, two medlars or one quince (or two sour apples if these fruits are not available)
2. Place the turkey in a pot with two ounces of butter, all whole seasonings, vinegar, salt, and little water
3. After arranging everything in the pot, seal it so steam does not escape
4. Cook slowly over gentle heat
5. Serve to the table on slices with lemon juice on top
