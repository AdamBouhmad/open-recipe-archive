## Ingredients

- half dozen eggs
- three egg whites
- half arratel of flour
- one arratel of sifted stone sugar
- one arratel of very finely ground almonds
- salt
- one spoon of flower water
- two spoons of well water

## Directions

1. Place half dozen eggs with three egg whites, half arratel of flour, one arratel of sifted stone sugar, one arratel of very finely ground almonds, salt, one spoon of flower water, and two spoons of well water in a bowl
2. Crumble all this with the hand as one would crumble couscous
3. Once well crumbled, stir with a spoon until bubbles rise
4. Pour into a basin with some flour at the bottom, fill the basin and place inside the oven, baking well between bread
5. Once baked, cut into slices, and if desired, place in the oven to biscuit
