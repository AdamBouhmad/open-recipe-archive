## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 flounders
- finely chopped forcemeat
- butter
- 4 onion slices
- a drop of white wine
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- buttered paper
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- clarified butter
- fried sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare 2 or 3 flounders and remove the fillets
2. Cover them with a little finely chopped and well-bound forcemeat
3. After rolling and tying them, arrange in a small casserole with butter underneath, 4 onion slices, a drop of white wine, seasoned with salt, pepper, and fine herbs
4. Cover with buttered paper and simmer for half an hour with fire below and above
5. Remove from the casserole, pass twice through beaten eggs covered with breadcrumbs
6. Fry in clarified butter
7. Serve after frying with fried sauce around it
