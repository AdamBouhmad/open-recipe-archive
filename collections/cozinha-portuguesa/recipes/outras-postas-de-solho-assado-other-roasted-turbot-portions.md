## Ingredients

- turbot cut in portions the thickness of two fingers
- fish broth
- white wine
- bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- fine bread crumb
- ravigote sauce or hot sauce with butter

## Directions

1. Cut turbot in portions the thickness of two fingers
2. Cook in a marinade made of fish broth, white wine, two bay leaves, salt, pepper, and a piece of butter
3. Let simmer little by little
4. When cooked, cover with fine bread crumb, roast on the grill, dripping with melted butter and moderate heat
5. When roasted, serve with ravigote sauce or hot sauce with butter
