## Ingredients

- artichokes
- butter
- egg yolks
- broth
- cinnamon
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes, remove the stem from inside, and after cooking them very well
2. Stuff them inside and between leaf and leaf in such a manner that they look like a pastry with the filling you wish
3. Place in a frying pan lined with butter with egg yolks below and on top, and a drop of broth
4. Cook slowly on gentle heat below and above, bind with egg yolks
5. Serve at table over bread slices with cinnamon on top
