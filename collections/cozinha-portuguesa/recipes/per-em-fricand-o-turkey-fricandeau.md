## Ingredients

- turkey
- bacon strips
- veal slices
- ham
- onion studded with cloves
- bundle of herbs
- salt
- clear essence
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare a young turkey with legs tucked inside according to custom
2. Sear it in a hot oven and rub with a bacon strip
3. Once well dried, lard it with fine bacon
4. Place in a pot and add broth without salt, veal slices, ham, studded onion, herb bundle, and bacon strips
5. Once well skimmed and cooked, strain the broth through a sieve and reduce it
6. Finish the glaze as is done for veal fricandeau
7. Place the turkey with the larding facing down and attached to the bacon with good color
8. Remove the string and serve with clear essence passed through the liquid remaining in the casserole and lemon juice
9. May also be served with ragout of cucumbers, endive, béchamel, creamed spinach sauce, sorrel sauce, or any other preferred sauce
