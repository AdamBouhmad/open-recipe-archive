## Ingredients

- One or two hares
- Medium larding bacon, well seasoned
- Bacon slices for the bottom and around a terrine dish
- Hare fillets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Truffles
- Veal or beef slices, beaten
- Bacon slices for covering
- Ham essence
- Lime juice

## Directions

1. Skin one or two hares and remove the meat from the thighs and loins
2. Lard with medium bacon well seasoned
3. Line the bottom and around a terrine dish with bacon slices
4. Arrange the hare fillets inside, seasoned with salt, pepper, fine herbs, and truffles at intervals
5. Cover with beaten veal or beef slices, then with bacon slices
6. Put the lid on and seal with dough
7. Cook over gentle heat
8. When cooked, remove the veal, beef, and bacon slices and all the fat
9. Add a spoonful of ham essence
10. Serve hot with lime juice
