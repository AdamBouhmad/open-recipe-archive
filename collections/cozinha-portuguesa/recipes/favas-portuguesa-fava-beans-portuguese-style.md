## Ingredients

- fava beans
- lettuce hearts
- water
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- small onion
- coriander

## Directions

1. Cook the fava beans with some lettuce hearts in plain water, drain them through a sieve, put them in a casserole with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, small onion, and chopped coriander, and stir-fry little by little
2. After stir-frying, add a drop of hot water and leave to simmer little by little
