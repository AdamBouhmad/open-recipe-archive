## Ingredients

- one quart of red wine
- sugar in stone
- two cloves
- cinnamon
- a piece of green lemon peel
- a little coriander seed

## Directions

1. Let one quart of red wine and sugar in stone boil in a saucepan
2. Add two cloves, cinnamon, a piece of green lemon peel, and a little coriander seed
3. Let it cook slowly and gently
4. When reduced to two thirds, pass it through a sieve
5. Serve cold in sauce boats for roasted game tongues, hares, rabbits, or any other wild game
