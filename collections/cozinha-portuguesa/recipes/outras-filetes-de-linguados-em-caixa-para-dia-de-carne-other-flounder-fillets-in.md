## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 flounder fillets
- salt
- pepper
- whole fine herbs
- lime juice
- chopped forcemeat à crème made of fried capon breasts
- paper boxes
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the fillets from 2 or 3 flounders
2. Remove the skin
3. Season with salt, pepper, whole fine herbs, and lime juice
4. After marinating for a while, dry them on a cloth
5. Roll them like patties with a finely bound chopped forcemeat à crème made of fried capon breasts
6. Put in paper boxes with a little forcemeat underneath
7. Cover with breadcrumbs
8. Brush with butter
9. Place in a baking dish and put in the oven to cook
10. After cooked and well-colored, serve with lime juice or sour orange juice
