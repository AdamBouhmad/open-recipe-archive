## Ingredients

- Tender green beans
- Water
- Butter
- Small onion
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth or stock
- Veal cullis
- Ham cullis

## Directions

1. Select the most tender and greenest beans, break off the tips, and cook them in plain water
2. After cooking, sauté them in butter with small onion and chopped parsley, seasoned with salt and pepper
3. When sautéed, moisten them with broth
4. Bind them with veal cullis and ham cullis
5. Serve for garnishes of first courses
