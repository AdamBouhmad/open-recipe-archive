## Ingredients

- sugar
- chocolate

## Directions

1. For each pound of sugar, add half an ounce of chocolate only.
2. Reduce the sugar to the hard crack stage.
3. Add the chocolate.
4. Give it one boil.
5. Remove from heat.
6. Strain through a sieve.
7. Cool the syrup.
8. Pour into bottles.
9. Cork and store.
