## Ingredients

- beef loin, tender and tenderized
- bacon
- ham
- veal strips
- brains
- salt
- pepper
- culinary salt
- Champagne wine
- capers
- truffles
- mushrooms
- cardoons
- chicory
- celery
- green beans
- cucumbers

## Directions

1. Take a tender and tenderized beef loin, remove the skins, and lard it well with thick bacon, ham, veal strips, and brains, everything well seasoned as is customary
2. Cook it in a good well-nourished braize
3. When well cooked, serve it with a sauce made with culinary salt and Champagne wine, capers, truffles, mushrooms, and a ragú of vegetables such as cardoons, chicory, celery, green beans, and cucumbers
