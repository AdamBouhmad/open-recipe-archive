## Ingredients

- crumb of three or four small loaves of bread
- half a pot of milk
- fifteen egg yolks
- a quarter of clarified butter or beef marrow
- lemon or orange peel, chopped
- grated sugar
- some currant raisins
- salt
- fine spices
- half a quartilho of Madeira wine
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Put in a pot the crumb of three or four small loaves of bread, pour over it half a pot of boiling milk
2. When soaked, add to it fifteen egg yolks, a quarter of clarified butter or beef marrow, chopped lemon or orange peel, grated sugar, some currant raisins, and season with salt and fine spices, and half a quartilho of Madeira wine
3. Mix everything with the beaten egg whites, tie in a cloth napkin lined with butter, and put in a pot of boiling water, and continue until it is cooked
4. Then let it foam, put it on its plate, and serve with sauce like the preceding ones
