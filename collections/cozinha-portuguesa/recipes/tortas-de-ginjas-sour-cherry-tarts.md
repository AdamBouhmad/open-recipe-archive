## Ingredients

- two arratels of sour cherries
- hot water
- two arratels of sugar at thread stage
- cinnamon
- clove
- one eighth of flour for French puff pastry
- one arratel of cow butter

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from two arratels of sour cherries and pass through hot water.
2. Remove the pits and cook in two arratels of sugar at thread stage with cinnamon and clove.
3. When very thick, let cool.
4. Make French puff pastry by kneading one eighth of flour and well worked, spread this dough on the board very well extended.
5. Place one arratel of cow butter on top, fold the dough so the butter does not appear, and do the same two more times, extending it with the palm of the hand.
6. Make the tart, put the cherries inside, brush it, and send it to the oven.
7. In the same way covered box pastries can be made.
