## Ingredients

- two pears
- a dozen eggs, well beaten
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- two bowls of cream

## Directions

1. Cut two pears into slices
2. Pass them through a dozen very well beaten eggs
3. Layer them in a plate greased with butter, alternating with sugar and cinnamon
4. Brown the pear slices
5. Beat two bowls of cream with the remaining eggs, sugar, and cinnamon very well
6. Pour this mixture over the pear slices
7. Brown again
8. Sprinkle more sugar and cinnamon on top
9. Send to the table
