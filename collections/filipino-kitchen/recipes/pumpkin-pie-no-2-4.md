## Ingredients

- 1 cup pumpkin or squash
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 large teaspoon flour
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- pinch salt
- 2 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix well pumpkin or squash with sugar, molasses, beaten eggs, flour, salt, cinnamon and about two cups of milk
2. place in crust and bake
