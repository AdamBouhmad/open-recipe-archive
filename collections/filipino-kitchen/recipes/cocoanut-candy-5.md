## Ingredients

- 1 cocoanut
- 2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Put the milk of the cocoanut and sugar on fine.
2. When it begins to boil add the grated meat of cocoanut and cook until meat is tender.
3. Pour into buttered pans to cool and cut in squares before it hardens.
