## Ingredients

- juice of 3 lemons
- 8 pounds tomatoes
- 1 pound raisins
- 4 pounds onions
- 2 1/2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 pound figs
- 1/2 pound prunes
- 1 pound red peppers
- 1/2 pound salt
- 6 pounds apples
- 2 ounces ground ginger
- 5 quarts vinegar

## Directions

1. Pound these ingredients with pestle and mortar.
2. Boil in vinegar till it is the consistency of chutney.
3. Pass with curried dishes.
