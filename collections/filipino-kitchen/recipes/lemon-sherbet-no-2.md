## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 4 lemons
- ¾ cup water
- 2 oranges
- Whites of 2 eggs
- 2 quarts water

## Directions

1. Boil sugar in one half cup of water until the syrup threads
2. Beat the whites of eggs to a stiff froth
3. Stir into this the thick syrup and put in freezer with the juice of lemons and oranges and two quarts of water
4. Freeze
