## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups corn meal
- 1/3 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 cup sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix sweet milk, molasses, flour, corn meal and soda, dissolved in sour milk.
2. Pour into a greased mould (a small lard pail answers the purpose), cover and boil for two hours.
