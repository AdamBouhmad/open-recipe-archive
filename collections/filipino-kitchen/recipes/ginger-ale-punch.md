## Ingredients

- 2 dozen large lemons
- 2 dozen pints ginger ale
- 2 large cans pineapple

## Directions

1. Mix lemon juice with both pulp (chopped very fine) and juice of pineapple
2. Fifteen minutes before serving put large piece of ice in punch bowl and pour punch over it
3. Use no water
