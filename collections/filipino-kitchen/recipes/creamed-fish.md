## Ingredients

- 2 cups cold fish
- 1 cup cream sauce
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup milk or cream
- squares of buttered toast
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Shred any left over fish, taking out bones and skin
2. Mix with hot sauce
3. Stir until smooth
4. Serve on squares of buttered toast, or put in a deep dish, cover with bread crumbs, and brown
5. Sauce: Mix butter and flour into smooth paste
6. Add milk or cream
7. Cook until thick and smooth
8. Pour over fish
