## Ingredients

- English walnuts
- uncooked fondant

## Directions

1. Crack English walnuts carefully so as to preserve the halves in perfect shape.
2. With fingers slightly moistened with water or butter, form uncooked fondant into small balls and press two pieces of the nut together on either side of the balls, flattening them to the desired shape.
3. Lay on waxed or buttered paper.
