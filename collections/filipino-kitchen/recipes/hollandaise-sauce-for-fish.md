## Ingredients

- butter
- juice lemon
- cayenne pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- juice ½ lemon
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and the yolks, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
2. Place the bowl in a dish of hot water on the stove, and beat the mixture with an egg beater one minute.
3. Add the boiling water, beating all the time.
4. When of consistency of custard, it is done.
