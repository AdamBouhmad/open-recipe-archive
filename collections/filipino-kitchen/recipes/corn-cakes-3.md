## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 egg
- pinch of salt
- 1 teaspoon butter
- powdered sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix well corn, milk, flour, egg, salt and butter into thick batter
2. Fry in small cakes in very hot butter or substitute
3. Serve with plenty of butter and powdered sugar, if so desired
