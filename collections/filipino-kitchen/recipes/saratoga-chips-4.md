## Ingredients

- potatoes
- ice water
- hot lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice potatoes as evenly as possible; drop into ice water.
2. Have a kettle of hot lard; put a few at a time into a towel, shake dry and then drop into the boiling lard.
3. Stir occasionally and when a light brown remove with a skimmer and they will be crisp, not greasy.
4. Sprinkle salt over them while hot.
