## Ingredients

- lanca seeds
- salt
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the fleshy covering from the lanca seeds and boil forty or fifty minutes.
2. Put on in boiling water.
3. Season with salt and butter before serving.
4. This is a good substitute for cabbage and tastes much like it if eaten with a little vinegar.
