## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound dates
- 1/4 pound figs
- cherries or pineapple or bananas or orange
- 1 box gelatine
- 1/4 boiling water
- 1/2 pint cold water
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 lemons
- 1 orange

## Directions

1. Stone and chop dates, mix with equal amount of chopped figs; add candied cherries, or pineapple, or the pulp of orange, or sliced bananas.
2. The mixture must be sufficient to fill a quart and a pint mould.
3. Cover a box of gelatine with cold water and let it soak for half an hour.
4. Add to it the sugar and boiling water and stir until gelatine is dissolved; add lemon and orange juice.
5. Strain into the mould over the fruit and stand it aside to harden.
6. When cold and quite firm, turn it out upon a glass dish and serve with or without whipped cream.
