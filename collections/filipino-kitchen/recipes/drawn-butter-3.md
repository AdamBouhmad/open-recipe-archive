## Ingredients

- ¾ pound fresh butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- ½ cup hot water

## Directions

1. Cut up butter and mix with flour.
2. Place in saucepan and add hot water.
3. Cover the pan and place in boiling water.
4. Move pan around continually in the same direction, till butter is entirely melted and begins to simmer.
5. Then let pan rest till it boils up.
6. If set on too hot a fire, contents will be oily.
7. If not well mixed, the butter and flour will be lumpy.
8. Avoid too much water.
