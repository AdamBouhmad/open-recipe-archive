## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 1 1/2 tablespoons pimientos
- 4 tablespoons butter or lard
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup crab meat
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- a bit of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs and take the meat from the shells.
2. Boil 4 eggs hard.
3. Chop the whites and mash the yolks.
4. Mix with fine soft white bread crumbs and chopped red peppers (the canned pimientos).
5. Melt but do not brown butter or lard; stir in the eggs, add slowly milk, and lastly crab meat.
6. When hot, put in salt, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
7. Serve at once on toast or with rice.
