## Ingredients

- macaroni
- soup stock
- cheese

## Directions

1. Divide the macaroni into four-inch pieces.
2. Simmer fifteen minutes in plenty of boiling water, salted.
3. Drain.
4. Put the macaroni in a saucepan and turn over it a strong soup stock, enough to prevent it burning.
5. Strew over it some grated cheese; when the cheese is melted, dish.
6. Put alternate layers of macaroni and cheese; then turn over it the soup stock and bake half an hour.
