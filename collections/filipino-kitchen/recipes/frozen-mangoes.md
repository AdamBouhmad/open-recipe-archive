## Ingredients

- 6 large mangoes
- 4 cups distilled water
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel mangoes and cut pulp from seeds; cut in small pieces and mash
2. Make syrup of sugar lemon juice and water, add mangoes and freeze
3. If desired add well beaten whites of two eggs when almost frozen
