## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups of cold spinach
- 4 tablespoons grated cheese
- pepper, salt and butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil eggs very hard; cool and cut lengthwise.
2. Heat spinach in a few tablespoons of hot water, and rub thru a colander; mix with pepper, salt and butter; put in bottom of baking dish.
3. Lay the halves of egg about on the spinach and pour over them a sauce made from the milk thickened and seasoned with salt, pepper and cheese.
4. Cover with bread crumbs and set in oven.
5. Serve when slightly brown.
