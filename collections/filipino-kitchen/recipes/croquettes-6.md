## Ingredients

- 1 quart cold chopped meat
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1 pint hot milk
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ¾ teaspoon white pepper
- dash cayenne
- 1 saltspoon grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon onion juice
- 2 tablespoons parsley

## Directions

1. Chop sufficient cold meat to make one quart
2. rub together butter and flour
3. add them to a pint of hot milk
4. stir and cook until a smooth thick paste, then take from fire
5. Add to meat, salt and pepper, nutmeg, parsley and onion juice
6. Mix thoroughly; add cream sauce; mix again and turn out to cool
7. When cold form into croquettes
8. dip in egg, roll in bread crumbs and fry in hot fat
