## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound salt codfish
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3/8 pint of milk
- dash of pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 3/8 teaspoon salt
- plain boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Pick apart codfish
2. Wash thoroughly in two waters
3. Soak over night in cold water
4. Next morning, drain, cover with boiling water and cook below the boiling point for five minutes
5. Drain and press
6. Rub butter and flour together
7. Add milk
8. Stir until boiling
9. Add a dash of pepper and the codfish
10. Cover and stand over hot water ten minutes
11. Add the beaten yolk of an egg and salt
12. Serve with plain boiled potatoes
