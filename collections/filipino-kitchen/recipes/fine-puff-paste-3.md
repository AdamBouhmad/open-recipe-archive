## Ingredients

- 1 quart sifted flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup lard
- 1/2 cup ice water
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix baking powder and salt with sifted flour and then sift again.
2. Have ready butter and lard, hard and cold; rub the lard smoothly into the flour, and add ice water with which the white of an egg has been mixed, and make a very stiff dough.
3. Roll into a thin sheet and spread on it one fourth the butter, dusting it with flour; roll up like a scroll, double the ends over to meet, flatten and roll again.
4. Repeat this until all the butter is used.
5. Put in an earthen dish and set in a cold place for an hour or more before making the pies.
