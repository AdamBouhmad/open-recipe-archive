## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 1/2 cups uncooked Quaker Oats
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar.
2. Add yolks of eggs.
3. Add Quaker Oats, to which baking powder has been added, and add vanilla.
4. Beat whites of eggs stiff and add last.
5. Drop on buttered tins with a teaspoon, but very few on each tin as they spread.
6. Makes about sixty-five cookies.
