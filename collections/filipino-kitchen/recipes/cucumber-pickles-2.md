## Ingredients

- 1 quart sliced cucumbers
- 1 onion
- 1 green pepper
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 20 whole cloves
- 1 teaspoonful turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon mustard seed
- vinegar to cover
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice cucumbers, onion and pepper, cover with water and salt and let stand three hours
2. Drain
3. Add sugar, spices and vinegar to cover
4. Scald fifteen minutes but do not boil
