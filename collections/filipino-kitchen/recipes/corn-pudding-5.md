## Ingredients

- 1 pint corn
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Mix well together corn, well beaten eggs, milk and butter, salt and pepper to taste
2. Bake one-half hour
