## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- pinch salt
- nutmeg or vanilla
- 1 quart milk

## Directions

1. Beat together until very light egg yolks and sugar; flavor with nutmeg or vanilla.
2. Add beaten whites, salt and, lastly, a quart of milk.
3. Mix well and pour into tins lined with paste.
4. Bake until firm.
