## Ingredients

- 4 large potatoes
- 1 pint chopped meat
- 1 tablespoon grated onion
- salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon grated parsley
- 1/2 cup stock
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash potatoes and cut them in halves crosswise.
2. With a scoop take out the centers leaving a wall at least half an inch thick.
3. Bake these shells in a hot oven twenty minutes.
4. Chop sufficient meat to make a pint; add salt, pepper, onion, parsley.
5. Add sufficient stock to make the mixture moist (about 3/4 cup).
6. Remove the potatoes from the oven; fill with this mixture, rounding it up.
7. Dust with bread crumbs and bake until brown.
