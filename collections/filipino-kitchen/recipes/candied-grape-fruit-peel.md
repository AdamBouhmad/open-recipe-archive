## Ingredients

- grape fruit peel
- lemon or orange juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. After dividing the fruit into two parts, scrape pulp from skins, and cut each piece of peel into six pieces.
2. Cover with cold water and let come to a boil.
3. Pour water off.
4. Cover with cold water again and let come to a boil and boil twenty minutes; pour off water.
5. Cover again with cold water and let boil twenty minutes; repeat this once more; each time press the water out after boiling.
6. Then cook measure for measure of peel and sugar with enough water to make a syrup.
7. Boil until all the water is taken up in peel.
8. Add lemon or orange juice before taking out of kettle; then place on buttered platter or waxed paper, expose to air several hours.
9. Then cut in slices.
