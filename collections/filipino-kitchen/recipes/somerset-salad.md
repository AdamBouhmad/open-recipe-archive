## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- green peppers
- cucumber
- lettuce
- french dressing

## Directions

1. Peel medium sized tomatoes and chill.
2. Cut in halves crosswise.
3. For each portion arrange one-half tomato on a nest of lettuce leaves and garnish top of each with slice of cucumber (fluted edges) and a smaller slice of green pepper.
4. Serve with French dressing.
