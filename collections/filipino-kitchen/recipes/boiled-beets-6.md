## Ingredients

- beets
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash carefully
2. Do not cut or pierce
3. Put in boiling water, and boil until tender, from one to two hours
4. When done, put into cold water and slip off the outside skin
5. Cut into thin slices
6. While hot, serve with butter, salt, pepper and very sharp vinegar
