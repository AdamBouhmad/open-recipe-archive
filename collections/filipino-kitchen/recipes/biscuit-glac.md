## Ingredients

- 1 pint sweet cream
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- Yolks of 4 eggs
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat cream stiff; beat egg yolks, then cream with sugar and pour into tin
2. Flavor with vanilla and freeze
