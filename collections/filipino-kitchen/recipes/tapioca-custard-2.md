## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup tapioca
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 can sweet condensed milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 1/2 cups cold water

## Directions

1. Boil tapioca twenty minutes with two cups of water
2. Beat the eggs with the milk which has been dissolved in half cup of water, and add to the boiling tapioca
3. Cook together a few minutes until thick and smooth
4. Add flavoring when done
