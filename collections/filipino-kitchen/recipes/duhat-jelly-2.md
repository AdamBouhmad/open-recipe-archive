## Ingredients

- duhats, not quite ripe
- sugar, equal parts with juice
- lemon juice, 2 tablespoons to 4 cups juice of duhats

## Directions

1. Select the fruit when it is not perfectly ripe.
2. Sort and wash.
3. Add just enough water to barely cover the fruit and cook until the fruit becomes soft and the juice well started.
4. Put the juice into a thin cotton bag, letting the juice drip through it into a porcelain or enamel receptacle.
5. Let it drain for eight or twelve hours.
6. Do not squeeze the bag or the jelly will be cloudy.
7. Measure your juice and an equal amount of sugar.
8. Place the juice only on the fire and let boil thirty minutes.
9. Add sugar and lemon juice, two tablespoons lemon juice to four cups of duhat juice.
10. Stir until the sugar is thoroughly dissolved, and let boil ten minutes.
