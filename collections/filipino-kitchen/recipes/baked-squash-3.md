## Ingredients

- squash
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut open the squash; take out the seeds and without paring cut into large pieces.
2. Place in a moderately hot oven and bake about an hour.
3. When done, serve the pieces hot, to be eaten with butter like sweet potatoes.
