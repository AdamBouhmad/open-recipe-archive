## Ingredients

- 1 green papaya
- 2 small green peppers
- 2 green tomatoes
- 1 small onion
- ginger root, small piece
- garlic, small piece
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop fine, or better still, put through a meat chopper, papaya, peppers, tomatoes, onion, ginger root and garlic.
2. Boil fifteen minutes or until tender, in vinegar, salt and sugar.
3. When cooked, cover and let cool in its own steam.
