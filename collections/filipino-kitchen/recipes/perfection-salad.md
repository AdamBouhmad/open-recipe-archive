## Ingredients

- 1 envelope Knox gelatine
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 3/4 cup mild vinegar
- 1 pint boiling water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- juice 1 lemon
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup finely shredded cabbage
- 2 cups chopped celery or tomatoes
- 3/4 can sweet red peppers
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in cold water five minutes.
2. Add vinegar, lemon juice, boiling water, sugar and salt.
3. Strain and when beginning to set add remaining ingredients.
4. Put on ice for twenty-four hours, and serve with mayonnaise dressing.
