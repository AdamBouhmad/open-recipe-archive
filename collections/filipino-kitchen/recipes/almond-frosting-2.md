## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 pound almonds

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds, pound them in a mortar with a little sugar, till a fine paste.
2. Beat eggs and fine white sugar to stiff icing, then add almond paste, mixing thoroughly.
3. Cover cake with thick coating of this and afterward with plain icing.
