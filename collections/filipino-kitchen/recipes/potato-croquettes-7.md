## Ingredients

- 1 pint mashed potatoes
- salt and pepper
- 4 tablespoons milk or cream
- grating of nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 2 eggs
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put cold mashed potatoes in a saucepan; add milk or cream, salt, pepper, onion juice and nutmeg; stir and beat until smooth and hot.
2. Take from the fire; add yolks of two eggs and form into cylinder-shaped croquettes.
3. Beat the white of one egg and to it add a tablespoon water; roll the croquettes in this, then in bread crumbs and fry in hot fat.
