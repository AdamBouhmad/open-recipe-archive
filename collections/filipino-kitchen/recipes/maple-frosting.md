## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons Karo corn syrup
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil maple syrup and Karo corn syrup to 240 degrees F., or to the soft ball stage.
2. Pour in a fine stream on the whites of eggs, beating constantly meanwhile; beat occasionally until cold.
3. If the frosting does not hold its shape, set the bowl in a double boiler (over boiling water) and keep moving it from the bottom until it thickens perceptibly.
4. Leave it rough on cake.
