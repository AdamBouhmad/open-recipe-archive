## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn meal
- ¾ cup flour
- ½ teaspoon soda
- 1 cup sour milk
- ½ cup molasses
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Sift meal, flour, soda and salt together; add the sour milk and molasses.
2. Steam three hours if cooked in one loaf.
