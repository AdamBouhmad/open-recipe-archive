## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups ground peanuts

## Directions

1. Cream butter; mix well with sugar; add eggs and milk; then flour, baking powder and salt sifted together.
2. Add nuts and flavoring.
3. Bake in little drop cakes.
