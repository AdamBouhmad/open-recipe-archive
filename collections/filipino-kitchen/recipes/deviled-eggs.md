## Ingredients

- 6 boiled eggs
- 1 teaspoon melted butter
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- vinegar
- salt and pepper
- mustard to taste

## Directions

1. Boil eggs and cut in halves.
2. Remove yolks and mash them.
3. Add melted butter, celery seed, a little vinegar, salt, pepper and mustard to taste.
4. Mix well and put back in the white cup of the eggs.
