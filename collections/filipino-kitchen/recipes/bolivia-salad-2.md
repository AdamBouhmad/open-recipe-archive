## Ingredients

- 2 cups potatoes
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons red peppers
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- salad dressing

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes; when cold cut in cubes.
2. Add hard boil eggs chopped fine, chopped peppers and salt, and mix all together.
3. Serve with any dressing.
