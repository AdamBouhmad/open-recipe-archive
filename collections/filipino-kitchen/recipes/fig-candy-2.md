## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup water
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tarter
- 1 pound figs

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until a light brown color.
2. Then stir in cream of tarter and take from fire.
3. Wash and cut open figs, spread them on platter and pour over them the mixture.
4. Set to cool until covering has hardened.
