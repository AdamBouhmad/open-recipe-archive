## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard or drippings
- tomato sauce
- sliced lemons

## Directions

1. Season with salt and pepper
2. Dredge with flour
3. Brush over with beaten egg
4. Roll in bread crumbs
5. Fry in hot lard or drippings sufficient to cover
6. When browned on one side, turn it over in the fat and brown the other
7. Drain when done
8. Serve with tomato sauce, garnished with sliced lemons
