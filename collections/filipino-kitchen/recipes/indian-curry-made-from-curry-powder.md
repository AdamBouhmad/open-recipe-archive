## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon curry powder
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 pint cold meat
- 1 cup cream or
- 1 coconut
- salt
- lemon or vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onion very fine and mix with curry powder, working it up to smooth paste.
2. Brown this in butter till golden brown.
3. Add the meat cut in small cubes, and simmer slowly for a few minutes.
4. Season with salt, lemon or vinegar.
5. Add the milk or cream and bring to boil.
6. If cocoanuts are obtainable, grate coconut, pour half pint boiling water on it and press out all the milk.
7. You can use this instead of milk or cream.
