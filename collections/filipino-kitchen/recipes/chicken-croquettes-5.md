## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken
- 5/8 cups flour
- 1½ cups broth
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Melt butter
2. When hot add flour and broth and cook until very thick
3. Stir in minced chicken and make into shape
4. Dip into beaten egg and then into cracker crumbs and fry in deep fat
5. If no broth on hand, use milk
