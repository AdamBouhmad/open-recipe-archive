## Ingredients

- 1 pound of beef
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- celery
- cabbage leaf
- parsley
- 3 quarts cold water
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Cut vegetables in small pieces and let lie in cold water
2. Cut meat in cubes with no fat, wash and put on stove
3. Add to three quarts of cold water with vegetables
4. Season to taste
5. Cook ten hours
6. Strain
