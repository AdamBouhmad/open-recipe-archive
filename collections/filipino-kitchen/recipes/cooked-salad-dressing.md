## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 tablespoon water
- 3 tablespoons cream
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- dash celery salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Rub together butter, flour, salt, mustard and sugar; add the egg and mix well.
2. Add vinegar and water, cook over slow fire, stirring all the time until thick.
3. When almost cool add cream.
