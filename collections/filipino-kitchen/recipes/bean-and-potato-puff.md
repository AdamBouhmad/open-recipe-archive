## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups bean pulp
- 1 cup mashed potato
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 beaten egg
- 3/4 small onion
- salt and pepper to taste
- parsley

## Directions

1. Blend all the ingredients
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Drop by spoonfuls on a greased tin
4. Bake in a moderately quick oven until puffy and brown
5. Turn on a platter and garnish with parsley
