## Ingredients

- 2 small green papayas
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- dash of pepper
- 3 tablespoons chopped walnuts
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- pimiento
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Peel papayas, cut into cross sections, remove seeds, and boil until tender.
2. Shave enough fine to mix with nuts and cheese.
3. Place in center of papaya, decorate with pimiento, and serve with mayonnaise.
