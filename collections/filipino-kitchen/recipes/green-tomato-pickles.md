## Ingredients

- 8 pounds green tomatoes
- 4 pounds brown sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1 teaspoon mace
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop the tomatoes fine; add sugar and boil down three hours; add vinegar and spices, boil about fifteen minutes; let cool and put in jars or other vessel.
