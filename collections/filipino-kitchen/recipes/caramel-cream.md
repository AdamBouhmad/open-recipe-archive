## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 can cream
- 1½ cups nuts
- Vanilla to taste
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 2¼ cups water

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of sugar with one fourth cup water until a thick syrup
2. Brown, but be careful not to burn
3. To one half can of cream add two parts of water
4. Place on the fire and when it boils, add heaping teaspoon of cornstarch
5. When done, add the yolks of four eggs, well beaten, with one cup of sugar
6. Remove from the fire and add the boiling hot syrup; stirring until all has been dissolved
7. Flavor with vanilla, and add nuts, finely chopped
8. When half frozen, add half a can of cream and the whites of the eggs well beaten
