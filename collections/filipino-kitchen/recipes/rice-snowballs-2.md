## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- 3 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 teaspoon corn starch
- lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil rice and mould in a cup until cold
2. Make a custard of eggs, milk and cornstarch
3. Flavor with lemon
4. When cold pour over rice balls half an hour before using
