## Ingredients

- 3 cups sifted flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 nutmeg (grated)
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 cups milk
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Take sifted flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, nutmeg and cinnamon, mix these together; add milk and well beaten eggs.
2. Mix into a soft dough with floured hands; make into round balls; rub the tops with sugar and water, and sprinkle with dry sugar and cinnamon.
3. Bake immediately.
