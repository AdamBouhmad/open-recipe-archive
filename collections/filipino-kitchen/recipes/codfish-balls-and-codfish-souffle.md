## Ingredients

- 3/8 pound salt cod
- 1 pint mashed potatoes
- 1 rounding tablespoon butter
- 1 saltspoon pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pick apart salt cod, cover with cold water, bring to boiling point and drain
2. Cover with boiling water again and let stand five minutes; drain and press
3. Add hot mashed potatoes, butter, pepper and the yolk of eggs
4. Mix thoroughly and form balls
5. Dip in beaten egg, roll in bread crumbs and fry in deep, hot fat
6. Serve plain with tomato sauce
7. For souffle: add the well beaten whites of the eggs and bake the mixture a golden brown
