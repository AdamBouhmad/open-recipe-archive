## Ingredients

- 1 quart stewed tomatoes
- 1 medium size onion
- butter size of an egg
- ½ teaspoon soda
- 1 quart milk
- 1 cup cooked rice

## Directions

1. Slice onion.
2. Fry in butter until it begins to brown.
3. Add tomatoes and stir together.
4. Cook until onion is thoroughly done.
5. Strain.
6. Add soda, then milk.
7. Let it boil up and serve.
