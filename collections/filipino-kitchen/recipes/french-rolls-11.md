## Ingredients

- 1 cup yeast
- 1 quart milk
- 3 quarts flour
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 teacup hot water
- 1 mashed potato
- 2 tablespoons lard or butter
- salt and sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Mix together yeast, milk and flour.
2. When this is light add soda in hot water, potato, butter or lard, and flour enough to make a soft dough.
3. Add salt and sugar to taste.
4. Let rise four hours or until light; make it into rolls; let rise again and bake.
