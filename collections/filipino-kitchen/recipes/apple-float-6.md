## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 grated apples
- custard

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eggs until very light
2. Add powdered sugar and beat until fine and dry
3. Into this grate two apples, beating carefully until the meringue will hold no more apples
4. Spread this over a soft custard or whipped cream
5. Serve at once
