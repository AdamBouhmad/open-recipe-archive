## Ingredients

- bread
- 1 egg
- melted butter
- onions
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Use bread at least a day old
2. Crumble fine, mixing in one egg and enough melted butter to have it crumble and be moist in the hands
3. Season highly with salt and pepper
