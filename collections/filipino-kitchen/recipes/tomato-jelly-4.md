## Ingredients

- 1 slice onion
- 3 cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 box gelatine
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- mayonnaise
- 3/4 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Boil tomatoes and spices together.
2. When done strain and add gelatine which has been soaked in one half cup water.
3. Place on ice and serve with mayonnaise.
