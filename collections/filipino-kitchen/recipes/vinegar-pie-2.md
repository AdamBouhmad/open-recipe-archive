## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon sharp vinegar
- 1 heaping tablespoon flour
- 1 teacup cold water
- 1 teacup sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat well together egg, flour and sugar
2. add vinegar and water
3. flavor with nutmeg and bake with two crusts
