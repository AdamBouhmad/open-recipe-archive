## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- lemon or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. bake in small dripping pan and serve hot with creamy sauce
3. Fruit added to the above is very nice
