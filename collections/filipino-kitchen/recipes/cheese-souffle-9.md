## Ingredients

- 1 ounce butter
- 1 ounce flour
- 3/4 pint milk
- 3 ounces finely grated cheese
- 3 eggs
- pinch of salt and cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Melt butter, mix smoothly with it the flour and seasoning, add to the milk and simmer over fire, stirring continually till as thick as melted butter; add cheese, turn into bowl and add the well beaten yolks of two eggs.
2. Whisk three whites of eggs to stiff froth and just before souffle is baked put in these whites and pour into round tin.
3. Souffle will rise very high.
4. Bake twenty minutes and serve immediately.
5. Sufficient for six people.
