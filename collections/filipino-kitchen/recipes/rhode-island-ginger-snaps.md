## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon saleratus
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil the molasses, add butter, ginger and saleratus
2. Dissolve the saleratus in tablespoon hot water and put into molasses while boiling
3. Stir in the flour when it is hot
4. Roll thin
5. Bake quickly
