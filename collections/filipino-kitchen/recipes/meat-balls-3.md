## Ingredients

- beef (topside or sirloin)
- salt

## Directions

1. Select a thick piece of topside or sirloin, and with a dull knife scrape with the grain of the meat, leaving all the stringy white fibre behind.
2. Roll this pulp into balls about the size of small marbles.
3. Have a clean, smooth frying pan.
4. Sprinkle a little salt in it; shake until brown; then lay in four balls at a time, and with a spoon keep them rolling and turning for just a minute or two.
5. The salt gives them a delicious flavor, while they will be rare inside and delicately brown outside.
