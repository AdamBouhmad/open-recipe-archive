## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 bananas
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 pint milk
- vanilla
- 3 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Put milk into vessel to boil.
2. Mix egg yolks, cup of sugar, butter and flour; beat mixture well and add to boiling milk.
3. Cook until real thick.
4. Set off to cool and flavor with vanilla.
5. Make a rich crust and bake.
6. Slice bananas in the crust and pour custard over them.
7. Beat the egg whites until stiff with three tablespoons sugar.
8. Spread on the pie and brown.
