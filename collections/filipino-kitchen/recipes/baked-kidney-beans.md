## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilo kidney beans
- 2/10 kilo fat salt pork
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 small onion
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash thoroughly kidney or red beans
2. Soak over night
3. Drain and cover with boiling water and boil thirty minutes
4. Drain and season to taste with salt, molasses, and one small onion if desired
5. Add fat salt pork, and enough boiling water to come to top of beans but not to cover
6. Place in tireless with two very hot disks and bake from four to six hours
