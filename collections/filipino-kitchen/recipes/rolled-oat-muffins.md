## Ingredients

- 2/3 cups rolled oats
- 1 cup scalded milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 tablespoons shortening
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Turn scalded milk on oats, let stand five minutes; add sugar, salt and melted shortening; sift in flour and baking powder, mix thoroughly and add egg well beaten.
