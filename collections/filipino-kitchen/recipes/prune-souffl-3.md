## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound stewed prunes
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- yolks 3 eggs
- whites 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the stones from prunes, press through a sieve
2. Add to them the yolks of three eggs slightly beaten with powdered sugar
3. Fold in the beaten whites of six eggs
4. Turn into a baking dish
5. Dust the top with powdered sugar
6. Bake in a quick oven for five or six minutes, then send immediately to the table in the dish in which it was baked
7. Serve with whipped cream
