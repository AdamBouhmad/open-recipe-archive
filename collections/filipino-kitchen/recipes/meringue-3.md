## Ingredients

- whites 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons Highland cream
- 3 level tablespoons granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites until thoroughly stiff but not dry; add gradually the sugar beating the while
2. Set this on the ice until thoroughly chilled, not less than half an hour
3. Just before serving, add cream, as you would oil in mayonnaise dressing
4. This is a delicious meringue to whip into cold custard or to serve dropped into iced chocolate
