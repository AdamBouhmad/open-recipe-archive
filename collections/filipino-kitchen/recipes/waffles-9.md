## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ¾ cup water
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 cup flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients to make enough batter for two round waffles
