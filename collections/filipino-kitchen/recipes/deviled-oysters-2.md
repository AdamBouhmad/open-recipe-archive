## Ingredients

- 50 oysters
- 1 pint liquor and milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon celery
- salt and cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and drain oysters.
2. Shake them over the fire until the gills curl.
3. Drain, saving the liquor.
4. Chop the oysters fine.
5. Rub together flour and butter.
6. Add enough oyster liquor and milk to make a pint.
7. Stir until boiling.
8. Add the oysters, the yolks of eggs slightly beaten, salt, a dash of cayenne, and chopped celery.
9. Turn into a baking dish, cover thickly with soft bread crumbs, and bake in a quick oven about ten minutes.
