## Ingredients

- 1 pound stale bread
- 3/4 pound beef suet
- 2 large apples
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 3/4 cups sugar
- 1 lemon
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Take grated stale bread, suet chopped fine, apples cut fine, baking powder, sugar, the juice and grated rind of lemon, and well beaten eggs
2. mix well, adding eggs last
3. Boil four hours in a well buttered and sugared bowl
