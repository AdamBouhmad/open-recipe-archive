## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound prunes
- 3/4 cup sugar
- cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak, stew and stone prunes.
2. Sweeten and flavor with lemon juice; add a little cinnamon.
3. Line the pan with good paste, fill with prunes and bake until done.
4. Draw the pie to the mouth of the oven and spread with a meringue made with well beaten white of eggs and one spoon of sugar.
5. Brown.
