## Ingredients

- 50 oysters
- salt and pepper
- 1 quart milk
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Drain, wash and drain again, good, fat oysters.
2. Shake over the fire until the gills curl.
3. Heat milk in double boiler.
4. Add hastily to the oysters.
5. Take from the fire.
6. Add salt, pepper and butter.
7. Serve with oyster crackers.
8. If thickening is liked, rub one tablespoon of butter and one of flour together and add to the milk.
