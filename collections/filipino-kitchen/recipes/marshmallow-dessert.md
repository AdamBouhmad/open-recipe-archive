## Ingredients

- pineapple
- 1 1/2 cups whipped cream
- marsh mallows
- maraschino cherries

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of diced pineapple (no juice) and quartered marshmallows, and let stand in ice box twelve hours to swell.
2. When ready to serve add halved maraschino cherries.
3. Mix well and add whipped cream.
4. If the cream is not stiff enough add white of one egg beaten stiff.
