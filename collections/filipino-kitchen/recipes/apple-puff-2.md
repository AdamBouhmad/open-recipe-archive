## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour for stiff batter
- 1 can apples
- cinnamon or nutmeg or sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Put apples over the fire in a pudding dish, and wash, sweeten and flavor to taste
2. Cinnamon, nutmeg or sliced lemon are good
3. Let them cook while the oven heats
4. get together sugar, butter, milk, enough flour to make a nice, ordinary cake batter, and lastly the baking powder
5. Be sure the apples have quite an amount of juice
6. Spread the batter over the boiling apple sauce
7. Bake and serve with any kind of pudding sauce
