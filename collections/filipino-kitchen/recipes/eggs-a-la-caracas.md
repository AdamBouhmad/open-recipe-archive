## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound dried beef
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- eggs
- salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix shaved dried beef with thick part of canned tomatoes, grated cheese, onion juice, salt and pepper.
2. Pour into a pan in which the butter has been melted.
3. After all is well blended and thick, beaten eggs are stirred in.
4. Cook just long enough to set the eggs.
