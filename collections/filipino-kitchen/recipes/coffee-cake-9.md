## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup molasses
- 4 cups flour
- 1 cup strong cold coffee
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 pounds raisins
- 1/2 pound citron
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 cup walnuts
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add coffee and molasses, then eggs well beaten
3. Next fruit, spices and nuts, and flour
4. Bake in one pan
