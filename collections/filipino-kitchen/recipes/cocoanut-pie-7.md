## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 1 grated cocoanut
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix cocoanut with well beaten yolks and cup of sugar.
2. Stir in the milk, filling the pan even full and bake.
3. Put over top meringue made of whites of eggs and three tablespoons sugar.
4. Brown quickly.
