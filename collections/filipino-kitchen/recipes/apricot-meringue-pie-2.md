## Ingredients

- 1 can apricots
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the apricots fine and mix them with half a cup of sugar and the beaten yolk of an egg.
2. Fill the crust and bake.
3. Take from the oven; let stand a few minutes, cover with meringue made from white of egg and one tablespoon of sugar.
4. Bake in a slow oven until golden brown.
