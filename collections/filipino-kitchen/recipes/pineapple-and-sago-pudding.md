## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sago
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/3 cup boiling water
- 3/4 cup shredded pineapple

## Directions

1. Soak sago over night
2. Stir soft sago with boiling water, cook twenty minutes
3. Add sugar, and stir until dissolved
4. Shred the pineapple with a fork and pour sago over it
5. Cover with a meringue and brown slightly in the oven
