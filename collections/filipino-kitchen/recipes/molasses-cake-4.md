## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup cold tea
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 cup flour
- pinch salt

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients
2. Bake in shallow pan
