## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated chocolate
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon glycerine

## Directions

1. Put all but the chocolate in a kettle and boil fast.
2. When it will almost thread, add chocolate.
3. Pour in buttered tins.
4. Cut when cold.
