## Ingredients

- mutton
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- water
- currant jelly or other tart sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and dry with a clean cloth.
2. Lay in dripping pan and add a little water to baste it with at first.
3. Afterwards baste with its own gravy.
4. Allow in roasting about 12 minutes to a pound, if fire is strong as it should be.
5. When it begins to roast well, salt and pepper.
6. Baste often.
7. About a quarter of an hour before you think it will be done, dredge with flour and baste with butter.
8. Skim the gravy well and thicken very slightly with brown flour.
9. Serve with currant jelly or other tart sauce.
