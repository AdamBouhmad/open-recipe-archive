## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Put sugar and water in sauce pan and stir on stove until sugar melts.
2. Remove grains of sugar around edges.
3. Boil without stirring.
4. Try in water.
5. When it makes a firm but not crisp ball, set in cold water till you can bear your fingers in it.
6. Stir and beat — knead as it begins to harden.
7. Put flavor in while creamy.
8. To this can be added chopped nuts, cocoanut or candied fruits.
9. Shape fondant into rolls and dip into melted chocolate for chocolate creams.
10. Handle rolls of fondant with tongs.
