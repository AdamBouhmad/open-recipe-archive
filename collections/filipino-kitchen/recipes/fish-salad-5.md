## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- mayonnaise dressing
- 1 1/2 pint fish
- capers and anchovies

## Directions

1. Take cold cooked fish and break into flakes.
2. Pour juice of lemons over this.
3. Arrange crisp lettuce leaves to form a bed on round plate.
4. Lay fish upon it and over the top put a few capers and some fillets of anchovy.
5. Dress with mayonnaise and garnish with sliced lemon.
