## Ingredients

- 2 cups grated raw potato
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1 nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Grate potato and add soda
2. Let stand a few minutes
3. Mix other ingredients and add
4. Steam three hours
5. Serve with coconut sauce
