## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 3 grape fruit
- 3 lemons
- 10.5 pounds white sugar
- 4 quarts water

## Directions

1. Cut all fruit very fine; soak in water over night.
2. Cook two hours; add sugar, and cook until stiff.
