## Ingredients

- onions
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- butter size of an egg

## Directions

1. Cook as boiled onions; drain.
2. Add milk, butter, pepper and salt, flour stirred to a cream.
3. Let all boil up at once.
4. Serve.
