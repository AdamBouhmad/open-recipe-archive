## Ingredients

- roselle calyxes, 2 heaping liters
- water, 1 liter
- sugar, 1/2 liter per liter of juice

## Directions

1. Wash the calyxes well, add one liter of water to two heaping liters of calyxes and boil until soft.
2. Strain through a jelly bag.
3. To each liter of juice add one-half liter of sugar.
4. Dissolve the sugar in the juice, then cook over a slow fire about twenty minutes or until the syrup jellies.
5. Do not stir the syrup while boiling.
