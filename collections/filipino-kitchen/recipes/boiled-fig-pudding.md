## Ingredients

- 1 pound figs
- 1 pound bread crumbs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 pound beef suet
- 4 eggs
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Have figs and suet chopped fine and bread crumbs grated, add eggs, sugar, the juice and rind of lemon
2. mix well, adding eggs last
3. Place in a well buttered and sugared bowl and tie in a pudding bag
4. Boil four hours
5. Do not permit water to get into bowl
