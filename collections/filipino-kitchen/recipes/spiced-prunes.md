## Ingredients

- 1 pint prunes
- 1 cup cider vinegar
- 2 cups sugar
- clove and cinnamon to taste

## Directions

1. Wash prunes and soak over night in water enough to cover well.
2. Stew until the skins are soft, then pour off the water, and add vinegar, cloves and cinnamon to suit taste.
