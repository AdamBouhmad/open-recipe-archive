## Ingredients

- 1 kidney
- 1 onion
- chopped parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- flour
- water or milk
- rice

## Directions

1. Wash kidney thoroly, pulling off the thin skin and removing all membrane.
2. Cut into pieces the size of a walnut.
3. Wash again and squeeze out the blood.
4. More than cover with hot water and simmer slowly in a covered stew-pan two or three hours, until nearly done.
5. Take off the scum.
6. Add boiling water when necessary to keep the meat covered, and salt about fifteen minutes before removing from the fire.
7. Let the kidney cool in the juice and stand over night if convenient.
8. Take kidney from juice and cut into small pieces the size of lima beans.
9. Return to juice, add chopped onion, salt and pepper to taste, and boil slowly until thoroly done.
10. Thicken with a little flour mixed with water or milk.
11. Add chopped parsley and serve with rice.
