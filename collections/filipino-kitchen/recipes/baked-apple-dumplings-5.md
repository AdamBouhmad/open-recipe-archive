## Ingredients

- rich, short biscuit dough
- four quarters of an apple per dumpling
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- sugar
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Make a rich, short biscuit dough
2. Take piece of dough, roll out thin as pie crust
3. Put in each piece of dough four quarters of an apple
4. sprinkle on a spoonful of sugar, a pinch of cinnamon
5. turn in the ends of the dough over the apple and lap tight
6. Lay dumplings on dripping pan, well buttered, the smooth side up
7. When the pans are filled, put small piece of butter on top of each
8. sprinkle over a large handful of sugar
9. turn in cupful of boiling water
10. then place in moderate oven for three-quarters of an hour
11. Baste with liquor once while baking
12. Serve with hard sauce
