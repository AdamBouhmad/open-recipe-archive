## Ingredients

- 1 cup of flour
- 2 ounces butter
- 2 ounces grated cheese
- 1 egg yolk
- pinch of salt
- few grains of cayenne

## Directions

1. Mix all into paste.
2. Roll out to the thickness of silver quarters, four or five inches long; cut into strips an inch wide; twist as you would a paper squill and lay on baking sheet, slightly floured.
3. Bake in moderate oven until crisp, but not the least brown.
4. Serve cold.
