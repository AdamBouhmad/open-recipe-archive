## Ingredients

- 4 medium sweet potatoes
- 1 pint rich milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup butter
- pinch of salt
- cinnamon, cloves, or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate potatoes on a coarse grater.
2. Add milk, eggs well beaten, butter and salt.
3. Flavor with cinnamon, cloves or nutmeg.
4. Put into a hot buttered baking dish and cook until done.
