## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. grated cheese
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 tablespoon bread or cracker crumbs
- salt, pepper and paprika to taste

## Directions

1. Mix together grated cheese, flour, bread crumbs, salt, pepper and paprika to taste; then add the milk, boiling hot, butter, the yolks of eggs and the whites stiffly beaten.
2. Mix gently; pour into buttered pudding dish and bake twenty minutes, in moderate oven.
3. Serve hot.
