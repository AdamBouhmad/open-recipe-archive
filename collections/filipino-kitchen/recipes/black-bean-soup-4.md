## Ingredients

- 1 pint black beans
- 1 quart cold water
- 1 small onion
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 saltspoon pepper
- 1 saltspoon cayenne
- 1 lemon
- 2 eggs
- 1 quart beef stock

## Directions

1. Soak the beans over night in cold water
2. In the morning, drain off and put in a sauce pan with cold water
3. Add the onion and cook until soft
4. Mash through colander
5. Add the beef stock, salt and pepper
6. Slice hard-boiled eggs and lemon
7. Place one slice lemon and eggs as desired to each plate of soup
