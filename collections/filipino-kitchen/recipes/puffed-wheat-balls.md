## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons syrup
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- pinch of soda
- 1 box puffed wheat

## Directions

1. Boil syrup, sugar and butter together until brittle in water.
2. Add pinch of soda.
3. Pour over puffed wheat and quickly form into balls, like pop corn balls.
