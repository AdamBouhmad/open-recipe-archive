## Ingredients

- cheese
- sliced lemon
- lettuce
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Arrange crisp, tender lettuce for individual serving.
2. Sprinkle freely with grated cheese.
3. Dress with mayonnaise and garnish with sliced lemons.
