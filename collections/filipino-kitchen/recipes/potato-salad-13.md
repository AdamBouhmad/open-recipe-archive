## Ingredients

- 3 potatoes
- 1 onion
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- celery
- pimiento

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes in salt water.
2. When cool, peel and dice.
3. Cut onion very fine and mix with potato.
4. Sprinkle vinegar over the potato and set away to get cold.
5. Just before serving add mayonnaise. Celery and pimiento add to the salad.
