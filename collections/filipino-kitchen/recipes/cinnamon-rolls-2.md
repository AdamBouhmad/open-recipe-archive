## Ingredients

- 1 liter bread sponge
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons butter
- flour
- powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. To bread sponge add well beaten egg, sugar and butter.
2. Stir into the sponge enough sifted flour to knead.
3. Knead the dough for about ten minutes, put it into a mixing bowl, and set it in a warm place to rise.
4. When it has doubled in size, pour it out on a well-floured board, roll it to a thickness of about one centimeter, spread it with butter, sprinkle it with sugar and dust with powdered cinnamon.
5. Roll up the dough like a jelly roll, cut it into pieces about five centimeters thick and put the slices, cut-side down, into a greased pan.
6. Let them rise to double in size, and bake them in a quick oven.
