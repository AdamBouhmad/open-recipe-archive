## Ingredients

- 3 cups custard
- 6 sponge cakes
- whipped cream
- 2 tablespoons peach jelly or marmalade

## Directions

1. Make a sweet boiled custard, about three cupfuls
2. While this is hot stir into it sponge cakes broken up very small, and jelly or marmalade
3. Beat hard and when cool set on the ice to get very cold
4. When ready to serve, heap on top of each dish a tablespoon of whipped cream
5. In the center of the cream put a very little of the peach jelly
