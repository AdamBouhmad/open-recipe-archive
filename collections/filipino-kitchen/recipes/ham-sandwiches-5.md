## Ingredients

- chopped ham
- ¾ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon mixed mustard
- 1 tablespoon salad oil
- pinch red or white pepper
- pinch of salt
- yolk of one egg
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Rub the butter to a cream
2. Add salt, pepper, mustard and egg yolk
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Stir in as much chopped ham as will make it consistent
5. Spread between thin slices of bread
6. Omit salad oil and substitute melted butter if preferred
