## Ingredients

- 2 cocoanuts
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 2 cups hot water
- 1/2 cup cream

## Directions

1. Grate two cocoanuts, pour over it two cups hot water and let stand one hour
2. Strain through a cloth
3. To this cocoanut milk add sugar and boil to a thick syrup
4. Add cream, beat well and remove from the stove without boiling
