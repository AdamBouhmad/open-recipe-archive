## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons corn starch
- 1 1/2 cups boiling water
- 1 lemon
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- whites 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Dissolve cornstarch in boiling water, add lemon juice and sugar, and boil in double boiler twenty minutes
2. When perfectly cold beat in the stiffly beaten whites of eggs and continue to beat ten minutes
3. Serve with any desired sauce
