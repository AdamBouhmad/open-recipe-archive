## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 6 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup pili nuts, broken in fourths
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar; sift; add nuts.
2. Add milk and eggs beaten together.
3. Put in buttered pans two loaves, and let stand twenty minutes.
4. Bake half an hour.
