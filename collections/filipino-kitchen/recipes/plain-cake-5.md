## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream butter and eggs
2. Add the well beaten yolks and milk
3. Sift flour three times, sifting baking powder the last time, and add
4. Lastly, fold in the well beaten whites
