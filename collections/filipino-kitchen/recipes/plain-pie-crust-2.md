## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups sifted flour
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup lard
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 large teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Rub the shortening into the flour, sifted with baking powder and salt, and mix with water.
2. Mix as little as possible and handle very lightly.
