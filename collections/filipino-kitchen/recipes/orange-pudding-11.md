## Ingredients

- 3 large oranges
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 pint boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges; cut in thin slices, removing the seeds
2. Put in a pudding dish and sprinkle over one cup of sugar
3. Beat egg yolks with cornstarch and two tablespoons of sugar; add salt and stir into boiling milk
4. When it thickens remove from the fire and when cold spread over oranges
5. Beat the whites of the eggs with two heaping tablespoons of sugar and put over pudding
6. Serve cold
