## Ingredients

- 2 cups sifted flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons shortening
- 1 cup mashed camote
- liquid to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Sift all dry ingredients.
2. Cut or rub fat into dry ingredients.
3. Rub the camote into this mixture.
4. Add just enough milk or water to make the mass cling together.
5. Do not knead.
6. Place on floured board, roll to about two centimeters thickness; cut with round biscuit cutter, place on a lightly floured biscuit tin and bake fifteen to twenty minutes in a moderately hot oven.
