## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 6 lemons (juice of 6, grated rind of 1)
- 1 quart water
- Whites of 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Add grated rind of one lemon to sugar
2. Add water and let boil five minutes
3. When cold add juice of six lemons
4. When partly frozen add well beaten whites of eggs
