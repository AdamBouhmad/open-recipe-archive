## Ingredients

- 1 pint raw oysters
- 1 cup tomato ketchup
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon grated horse-radish
- juice of 1 lemon
- tobasco sauce to taste

## Directions

1. Take oysters, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce and grated horseradish, the juice of a lemon and enough tobasco sauce to make very hot.
2. Put in glasses and place on ice to cool.
