## Ingredients

- bacon slices
- potatoes, sliced
- water
- crackers
- diluted cream

## Directions

1. Into a stew pan put some slices of bacon.
2. When hot put in sliced potatoes.
3. Add enough water to cover.
4. After the water has boiled down add crackers.
5. Cover with diluted cream and simmer until done.
