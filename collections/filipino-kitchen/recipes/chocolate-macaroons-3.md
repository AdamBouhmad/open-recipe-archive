## Ingredients

- butter size of an egg
- 2 ounces Baker's chocolate
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup flour

## Directions

1. Melt butter, stir in grated chocolate until smooth
2. Add sugar, eggs not beaten, vanilla, flour, baking powder
3. Flour board and fingers
4. Form dough into little balls
5. Bake in greased pans, leaving room to spread
6. Bake slowly
