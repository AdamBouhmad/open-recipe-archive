## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- cream
- water

## Directions

1. Take flour, baking powder, salt and butter.
2. Mix up soft dough with one part cream to two of water.
3. Roll out to the desired thickness, kneading as little as possible.
