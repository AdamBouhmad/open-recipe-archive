## Ingredients

- 1 cup coffee
- 1 pint milk
- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Put strong, clear coffee with milk in double boiler
2. Beat yolks of eggs, sugar and salt
3. Mix well, then add milk
4. Turn back into boiler and stir ten minutes, till thick like cream
5. Cool quickly
