## Ingredients

- 1 medium can fish-flakes
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1/2 onion
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Pick over can of fish-flakes
2. Chop onion and eggs
3. Mix all together with rich white or cream sauce
4. Bake in ramekins
5. Dust top of each with buttered bread or cracker crumbs
