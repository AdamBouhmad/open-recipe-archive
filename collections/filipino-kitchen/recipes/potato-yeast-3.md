## Ingredients

- 2 medium sized white potatoes
- 3½ cups water
- 1 tablespoon hops
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in two and a half cups water.
2. Take the potatoes from the water, mash them very fine and return them to the water in which they were boiled, which should be about two cups.
3. While the potatoes are cooking, boil one level tablespoon of hops in a cup of water and strain the water.
4. Add one cup of the hop water to the potatoes, and stir into the mixture two teaspoons of salt and two tablespoons of sugar.
5. Put this mixture into a jar, cover it, and let it stand until the potatoes rise to the top, which will be in about two days.
