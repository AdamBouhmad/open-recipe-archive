## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn meal
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix together cornmeal, milk, flour, butter, eggs and sugar, and add baking powder just before baking.
