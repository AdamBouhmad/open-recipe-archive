## Ingredients

- Chinese crab apples
- 1 cup sugar to 1 cup juice

## Directions

1. Wash well Chinese crab apples, cover with water and cook until soft.
2. Strain through a thin cloth.
3. To one part of juice take one part of sugar.
4. Boil until it jells.
