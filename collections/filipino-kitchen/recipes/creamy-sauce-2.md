## Ingredients

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 4 tablespoons fruit juice
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 3/4 cup cream

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and butter to a cream, add fruit juice, vanilla and cream
2. Place the bowl in hot water and stir until creamy
