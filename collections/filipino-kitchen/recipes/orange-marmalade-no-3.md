## Ingredients

- 1 dozen sour oranges
- 2 lemons, juice
- 5 pounds sugar
- 1.5 pints water

## Directions

1. Pare the oranges without cutting through the white skin.
2. Prick them and boil until tender.
3. Also boil separately the yellow peel until tender.
4. Then shred peel with scissors.
5. Chop the oranges, carefully removing the seeds and hard bits of skin.
6. Boil the water and sugar to a thick syrup.
7. Put in the oranges and the juice of lemons and shredded peel.
8. Boil one half hour.
