## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean veal
- ¼ pound Swift premium bacon
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- ½ teaspoon powdered sage
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- cream
- bacon grease
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind veal and bacon in a beef chopper.
2. Add salt, pepper and sage.
3. Form into balls and fry in bacon grease.
4. Serve with cream sauce made by adding butter, flour and cream to the bacon grease. Delicious when used with flannel cakes for breakfast.
