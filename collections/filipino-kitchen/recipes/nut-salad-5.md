## Ingredients

- 1 lb. English walnuts
- celery
- mayonnaise
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Crack nuts, blanch and set aside to cool.
2. Chop and mix with celery cut into small pieces.
3. Serve on lettuce. Dress with mayonnaise.
