## Ingredients

- onions
- salt and pepper
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Peel, slice and fry onions brown in equal quantities of butter, lard, or nice drippings.
2. Cover until partly soft.
3. Remove the cover and brown them.
4. Salt and pepper.
