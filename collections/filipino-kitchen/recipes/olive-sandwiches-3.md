## Ingredients

- 1 quart olives (stoned)
- 1 dozen hard boiled eggs
- mayonnaise dressing
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Chop together olives and eggs
2. Moisten with mayonnaise
3. Spread between thin slices of bread
