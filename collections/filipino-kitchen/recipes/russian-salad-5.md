## Ingredients

- cooked vegetables such as potatoes, carrots, beets
- peas
- string beans
- mushrooms
- canned sweet pepper
- French dressing
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut all the larger cooked vegetables such as potatoes, carrots, beets, etc., into pretty forms.
2. Alternate all these with French dressing.
3. It is very pretty to put the mayonnaise in the bottom of the salad dish and arrange the vegetables, each into a section by itself, alternating the pretty colors.
4. Serve with mayonnaise.
