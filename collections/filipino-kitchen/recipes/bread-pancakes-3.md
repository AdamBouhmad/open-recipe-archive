## Ingredients

- 4 slices bread
- ½ pint of milk
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper
- prepared flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Break stale bread into small pieces
2. Pour over a little boiling water
3. When soft beat in egg
4. Add milk, a pinch of salt and a little sugar
5. Add prepared flour enough to make a batter
