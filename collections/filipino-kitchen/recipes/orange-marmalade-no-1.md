## Ingredients

- small yellow oranges, size of limes
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take small yellow oranges the size of limes. Peel and cover with water, letting them stand all night.
2. In the morning pour off the water.
3. Take out every seed if you desire sweet marmalade; if you like it bitter, leave some seeds. Be careful to save all the juice.
4. To every cup of the fruit add three cups of water.
5. Then to every cup of this mixture add one cup of sugar and boil until it jellies.
6. Put a little in a saucer on ice and don't boil beyond the jelly point, if you want it clear.
