## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 2 tablespoons cold butter
- 1 cup milk or cream
- cayenne and salt
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Heat oysters in their own liquor until they begin to ruffle.
2. Skim out the oysters into a warm dish.
3. Add to the liquor the milk or cream, butter, pinch of cayenne and salt.
4. Thicken with flour stirred to a paste.
5. Boil up and add to the oysters.
6. Served with fish, boiled turkey or white meats.
