## Ingredients

- 7 pounds fruit
- 5 pounds brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons cinnamon
- 3 tablespoons cloves
- 1 pint vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean fruit, cut stem ends, put in preserving kettle.
2. Add sugar, vinegar and spices tied in bag.
3. Heat to boiling point and cook slowly until fruit is dark reddish brown in color and the juice thick.
