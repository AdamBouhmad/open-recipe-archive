## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 cocoanuts
- one pint of cocoanut milk

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, sugar and flour.
2. To this add one pint of cocoanut milk, obtained by grating, washing and squeezing well the meat of cocoanuts.
3. Bake either in crust or in pudding dish without crust.
