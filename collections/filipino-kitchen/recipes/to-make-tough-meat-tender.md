## Ingredients

- 1 large onion
- ⅓ cup vinegar
- ½ pound bacon or pork
- ⅔ cup water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Place in the bottom of kettle, slices of onion and thin slices of bacon and pork.
2. Pound the meat well, sprinkling it with salt and plenty of pepper.
3. Lay on onions and bacon.
4. Cover closely and cook until brown on both sides.
5. Add water and vinegar.
6. Keep closely covered and cook until tender, one and a half to two hours.
7. When first placed in kettle, spread butter over the meat.
8. After removing the meat, add a little water and thicken slightly with flour.
