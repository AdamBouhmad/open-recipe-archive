## Ingredients

- 5 cups mangoes
- 4 cups sugar
- 1 cup water

## Directions

1. Carefully peel ripe mangoes and cut from seed in lengthwise strips.
2. Boil sugar and water until it threads from the spoon, then drop in the mangoes, and boil until they become waxy in appearance.
