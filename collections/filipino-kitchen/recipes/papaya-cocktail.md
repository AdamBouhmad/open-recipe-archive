## Ingredients

- papaya
- juice of one large lemon
- 2 tablespoonfuls Heinz tomato catsup
- 1 tablespoonful Worcestershire sauce
- cracked ice

## Directions

1. Cut papaya in small pieces.
2. Mix with juice of one large lemon, two tablespoonfuls of Heinz tomato catsup, and one tablespoonful of Worcestershire sauce.
3. Serve with cracked ice.
