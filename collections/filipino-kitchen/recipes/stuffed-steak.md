## Ingredients

- round steak
- 2 cups dry bread
- 1 1/2 cup boiling water
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon mixed herbs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make stuffing by pouring over bread 1/2 cup boiling water; mash; add butter, herbs, yolk of egg; beat together; salt and pepper; put in steak.
2. Put in pot to brown as for pot roast; add one cup boiling water; let it simmer one and one half hours.
3. Then roast in oven one half hour.
