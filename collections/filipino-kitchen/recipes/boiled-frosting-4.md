## Ingredients

- 1 pint granulated sugar
- 3 tablespoons water
- 2 eggs (whites only)

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until it spins threadlike when slowly dropped from tip of spoon.
2. Have the whites of eggs beaten stiff and pour hot syrup over, beating hard all the time.
3. Flavor with any preferred extract.
