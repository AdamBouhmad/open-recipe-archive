## Ingredients

- desired amount of bananas
- sufficient sugar

## Directions

1. Cut fine the desired amount of bananas, mix thoroughly with sufficient sugar to make paste that will spread well, and place between the layers.
2. Cover the cake with boiled icing, and if to be served soon, lay long pieces of banana on top.
