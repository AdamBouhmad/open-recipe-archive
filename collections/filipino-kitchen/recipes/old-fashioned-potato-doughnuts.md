## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- 5 cups flour
- 5 teaspoons baking powder
- cooked potatoes
- cinnamon or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes, mash dry and add other ingredients
2. Sift baking powder with the flour
3. Use cinnamon or nutmeg to flavor
4. Roll, fry in lard, and sprinkle with sugar
