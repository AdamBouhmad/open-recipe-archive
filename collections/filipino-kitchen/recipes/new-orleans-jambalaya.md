## Ingredients

- 36 fresh shrimps
- thyme
- 1 onion
- bay leaf
- 1 tablespoon lard
- parsley
- 1 cup rice
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Boil shrimps.
2. When cold peel.
3. Cut onion fine.
4. Fry in lard.
5. Add rice well washed to the onion.
6. Let it fry brown, stirring constantly.
7. Flavor with thyme, bay leaf, parsley, pepper and salt.
8. When brown, add enough water to cover.
9. Let boil until the rice is well cooked.
10. Mix with the shrimps and serve.
