## Ingredients

- 8 eggs, whites
- 1 pound granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites very light, add sugar
2. Mix quickly
3. Place paper (not buttered) on a pan turned upside down
4. Drop the mixture in spoonfuls, placed well apart
5. Bake from thirty to forty minutes in very moderate oven
6. When baked, slip paper off pan onto table
7. Slip knife under kisses and take at once off paper
8. These are fine filled with whipped cream, prepared as for Charlotte Russe
