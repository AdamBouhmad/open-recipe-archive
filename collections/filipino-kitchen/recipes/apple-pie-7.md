## Ingredients

- 1 can apples
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Slice apples into lower crust, sprinkle a tablespoon of sugar over each, a little cinnamon and some slices of butter, a very little juice.
2. Cover with upper crust and bake.
