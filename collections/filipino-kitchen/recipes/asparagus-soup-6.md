## Ingredients

- 2/3 can asparagus
- 1 pint scalded milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook asparagus until very tender
2. Rub through strainer
3. Add this to scalded milk
4. Rub flour and butter together and stir into the milk
5. Cook up
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Serve
