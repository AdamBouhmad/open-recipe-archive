## Ingredients

- beef
- flour
- salt and pepper
- 1 cup boiling water
- butter or suet fat

## Directions

1. Place your rib or loin roast in a dripping pan and baste it well with butter or suet fat.
2. Set in the oven.
3. Baste frequently with its own drippings.
4. When partly done, season with salt and pepper; then dredge with sifted flour to give it a frothy appearance.
5. Remove when done.
6. Skim the drippings from all fat.
7. Add a tablespoon of sifted flour, a little pepper and teacupful of boiling water.
8. Boil up once and serve hot in a gravy boat.
