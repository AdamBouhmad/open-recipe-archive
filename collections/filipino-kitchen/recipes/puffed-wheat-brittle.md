## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 box puffed wheat

## Directions

1. Put sugar in smooth granite sauce pan, place on stove, and stir constantly until melted to a syrup, keeping it away from sides of pan.
2. Spread puffed wheat on buttered tin and pour syrup over it.
