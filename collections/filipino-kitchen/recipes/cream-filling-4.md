## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup hot water
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 tablespoon grated coconut
- 3/4 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Put water on sugar, let them simmer.
2. Beat white of egg and mix with syrup.
3. When cold add nuts, raisins and coconut.
4. Place between layers.
