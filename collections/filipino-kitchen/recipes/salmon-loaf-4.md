## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 3/4 cup bread or cracker crumbs
- salt and pepper
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Drain off the liquor for the sauce, pick out the bones, mash salmon fine with a spoon, add the eggs well beaten, melted butter, grated bread crumbs, and salt and mix well.
2. Steam three-quarters of an hour either in a mould or a bag.
3. Add to the liquor from salmon milk, flour, egg, a little salt, and boil until it thickens.
4. Slice the loaf, pour the sauce over slices, and garnish with bits of parsley and slices of lemon.
