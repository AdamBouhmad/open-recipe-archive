## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups thick stewed apple
- nutmeg or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Strain thick stewed apples through a colander, add sugar.
2. Beat yolks lightly and mix with apple; flavor with nutmeg or cinnamon.
3. Beat into this the milk, and lastly the well beaten whites.
4. Bake with one crust and let that bake before putting in the filling.
