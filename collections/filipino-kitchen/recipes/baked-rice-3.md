## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked rice
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup cheese
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 pint milk
- 1/2 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix well rice, cheese in small bits, milk, eggs, butter and salt.
2. Place in baking dish with cheese and a little butter on top.
3. Bake half an hour.
