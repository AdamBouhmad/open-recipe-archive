## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- pinch of salt
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 1 cup ground hickory nuts
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar; add eggs, flour, baking powder, salt, milk and nuts; flavor with vanilla.
2. Drop a teaspoonful on buttered pans about two inches apart; if too thick, add a little more milk.
