## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter or 1/2 butter, 1/2 lard
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 cups molasses
- 1 teaspoon hot water
- 3 level teaspoons ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- flour to roll

## Directions

1. Cream the butter
2. Beat in the sugar, then the molasses
3. Stir in a little flour
4. Add the soda dissolved in the hot water with the rest of the flour
5. Keep the dough from getting too stiff
6. Make of a consistency to allow you to shape it with floured hands into small balls the size of a walnut
7. Place on greased baking sheet about two inches apart to allow for spreading
8. Flatten out slightly
9. Bake in moderate oven
