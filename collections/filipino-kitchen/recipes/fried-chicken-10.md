## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 1 teaspoon lard
- 1 teaspoon butter
- salt
- flour
- hot water

## Directions

1. Wash the chicken thoroughly before cutting.
2. Afterwards lay in a pan to cool.
3. Have a thick, iron, frying pan piping hot.
4. Put in a spoonful each of lard and butter.
5. Fry the chicken very quickly until brown, then add just enough hot water to moisten and steam.
6. Set back on the stove, cover very tightly and steam until tender an hour or more as required.
7. Add salt just in time to let it penetrate well before serving.
8. The top may be dredged with flour just before adding the water.
