## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1/2 cake Baker's chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. After milk and sugar are boiling, add chocolate.
2. Cook until it forms a soft ball in water.
3. Set out until cooled; then add vanilla and beat until creamy.
4. Pour into buttered tins.
5. When set, cut into squares.
6. Peanuts or pili nuts may be added, less butter with pili nuts.
