## Ingredients

- guavas
- water
- white sugar, 1 cup for 1 cup of juice
- lemon or lime juice to taste

## Directions

1. Take ripe guavas, pare in quarters and drop into enough cold water to just cover them.
2. Boil until the fruit is thoroughly cooked.
3. Skim and add white sugar cup for cup — i.e. one cup of sugar for each cup of juice.
4. Let this cook slowly until the jelly is clear.
5. Add lemon or lime juice to taste; simmer one half hour longer.
6. Skim frequently, pour into wide necked bottles and seal when cold.
