## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup water
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Burn syrup: One-half cup sugar stirred in a pan until it burns
2. Add one half cup boiling water and boil until thick syrup
3. Cream butter and sugar
4. Add the yolks of eggs well beaten, water and two cups of flour, sifted three times
5. Beat for five minutes
6. Then add three teaspoons of burnt syrup, baking powder stirred into one half cup of flour, whites of eggs well beaten
