## Ingredients

- 4 tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 1 cup mushrooms
- 3/4 cup celery tops
- mayonnaise
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Scald tomatoes and chill.
2. Mince onion, chop mushrooms and celery, moisten with mayonnaise.
3. Cut a slice off the top of the tomatoes, take out seeds and fill with mixture.
4. Put mayonnaise on top and serve on lettuce.
