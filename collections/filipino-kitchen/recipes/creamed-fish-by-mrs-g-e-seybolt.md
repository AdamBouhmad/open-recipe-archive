## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3/8 pint milk
- 1 level teaspoon salt
- 1 salt spoon pepper
- 1 pint cooked fish

## Directions

1. Rub together butter and flour
2. Add milk
3. Stir until boiling
4. Take from fire
5. Add salt and pepper and cold cooked fish picked into flakes
6. Stand this over hot water until thoroughly heated
7. Serve on toast, in pate shells, potato cases, or in a potato border
