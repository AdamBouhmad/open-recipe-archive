## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 2 cups rice
- 1 cup stewed tomatoes
- 1 large onion
- a few cloves of garlic if liked
- 1 small can peas
- 1 small can pimiento
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 6 tablespoons lard
- salt and pepper to taste
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Sort, wash and soak the rice in water for one hour.
2. Dress and joint chicken with salt and pepper and fry until light brown.
3. Remove the chicken from frying pan, saute minced onion and bruised garlic until clear and tender.
4. Add the tomato, fried chicken and just enough boiling water to moisten and steam the rice.
5. Add the rice, and chopped pimiento and cook slowly until the rice is done.
6. Watch closely to prevent burning.
7. Add peas ten minutes before dishing.
8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
9. Serve the mixture on a hot platter, garnished with slices of hard-boiled eggs.
