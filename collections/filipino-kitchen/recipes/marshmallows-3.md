## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 envelope gelatine
- 1 teaspoon flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until it forms a firm ball when dropped in water.
2. Let the gelatine soak in two thirds of a glass distilled water for about ten minutes, then add to boiling sugar as it is taken from the stove.
3. Add flavoring and beat until spongy and light.
4. Pour into slightly buttered platter and let stand until next morning.
5. Cut into cubes and roll in powdered sugar or cornstarch to prevent stickiness.
6. Nuts, raisins, chocolate or kind of flavoring may be used.
