## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup hot milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs five minutes by the clock, add sugar one tablespoon at a time and continue beating.
2. Add the hot milk with the butter melted in it.
3. Sift flour three times; add baking powder at last sifting and use any flavor desired.
4. This batter may seem too thin, but if recipe is followed strictly, a delicious cake will be the result.
