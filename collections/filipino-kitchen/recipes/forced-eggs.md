## Ingredients

- eggs
- bread crumbs
- Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper to taste
- white of raw egg
- hot lard
- potato straws

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs and cut in halves.
2. Remove yolks and season with salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce.
3. Replace in the holders.
4. Paste together with white of raw egg.
5. Roll in egg and bread crumbs.
6. Fry in hot lard to amber brown.
7. Serve on nest of potato straws.
