## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup water
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 6 eggs
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter thoroughly
2. Add one cup flour, stir thoroughly and add the rest of the flour and water, alternately
3. Then stir in the whites of eggs, well beaten
4. Stir all three minutes and bake in three layers
5. Filling: Boil two cups sugar and one-half cup water until it threads and pour over stiffly beaten whites of four eggs
