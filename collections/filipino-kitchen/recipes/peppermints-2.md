## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup cold water
- 1 pinch cream tartar
- peppermint to taste

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, water and cream of tartar three minutes, or until it begins to thread.
2. Remove from fire.
3. Add peppermint to taste.
4. Beat it until it begins to thicken.
5. Drop on waxed paper.
