## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very light, add sugar and beat.
2. Add water, and flour with baking powder sifted into it.
