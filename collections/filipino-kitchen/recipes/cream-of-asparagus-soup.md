## Ingredients

- 1 can asparagus
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 tablespoon flour or corn starch
- 1 cup evaporated cream
- butter, size of walnut
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut off the tender ends of the canned asparagus
2. Press the pulp from the fibrous stems by rubbing through a sieve
3. Add this pulp to the juice from the can
4. Have ready the boiling water in which the corn starch or flour has been thoroughly cooked
5. Add the cream, the juice, and the pulp
6. Let it just reach the boiling point
7. Add the tender tips, salt, pepper, and butter
8. Serve
