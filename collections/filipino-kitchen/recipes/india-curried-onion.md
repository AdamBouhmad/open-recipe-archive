## Ingredients

- onions
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 lemon
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry sliced onions in butter, salt well.
2. Add one teaspoon curry powder and stir in two raw eggs.
3. Add a few drops of lemon juice just before removing from fire.
