## Ingredients

- 1 quart tomatoes
- 1 ounce butter
- 1 slice onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 cloves
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Boil together for twenty minutes tomatoes, onions, cloves, salt and pepper.
2. Strain through a sieve.
3. Melt butter in another pan.
4. As it melts, stir in flour until mixture browns and froths a little.
5. Mix the tomato pulp with it, and it is ready for the table.
