## Ingredients

- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 1 orange
- 2 egg whites
- 3/4 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with beaten whites of eggs, the juice and grated rind of orange and juice of lemon, flavoring with orange extract.
2. Spread between layers.
3. Ice top with boiled icing and decorate with sections of mandarin oranges.
