## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 3 1/3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs until light
2. Add sugar and shortening (creamed) and milk
3. Sift the flour, add baking powder, spices and salt, and sift again
4. Add this gradually to mixture, stirring constantly
5. If necessary add more flour, enough to roll, but remember too much flour makes doughnuts tough
6. Knead one half mixture, and pat to one fourth inch thickness; cut out doughnuts with floured cutter
7. Test fat, when hot enough, put in two or three gently
8. When brown turn with a fork, once only
9. When done, pick one up with a fork (without piercing doughnut) and immerse quickly in pan of hot water, and drain
10. Reheat fat and test before using again
11. If fat is too hot, the doughnuts will sear over and not rise
12. Just before serving, roll in powdered sugar
