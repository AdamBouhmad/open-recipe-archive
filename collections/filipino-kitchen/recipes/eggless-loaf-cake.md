## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 cups milk
- lemon or vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter
2. Sift together four times flour and baking powder
3. Mix all together with milk
4. Flavor
5. When cold, ice with Eggless Icing made of 1 cup sugar and five tablespoons milk
6. Stir until it boils, then boil five minutes without stirring, and beat until smooth and creamy
