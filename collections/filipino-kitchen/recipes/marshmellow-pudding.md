## Ingredients

- 1/8 tablespoon gelatine
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in water.
2. Beat the whites of eggs stiff and dry; beat slowly into them one fourth cup of sugar and the gelatine which has been dissolved over boiling water.
3. Add flavoring that is desired and mould in small cups.
4. Stand on ice, and unmould at serving time by dipping the outside of the cup in hot water.
5. Serve with whipped cream, or a spoonful of crushed fruit, or a custard made from the yolk of two eggs, milk and one fourth cup of sugar, cooked in double boiler.
