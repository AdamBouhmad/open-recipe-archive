## Ingredients

- 1 cup cheese
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. In a saucepan melt cheese, add milk slowly.
2. When mixture begins to thicken, add eggs slightly beaten, stir until the consistency of scrambled eggs; add seasoning.
3. Take rolls cut in half, scoop out soft portion and butter surface, crisping them in oven.
4. Fill with mixture.
