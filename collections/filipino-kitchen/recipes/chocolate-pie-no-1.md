## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 heaping teaspoon cornstarch
- 1/2 cup chocolate, melted

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, cornstarch, egg yolks and chocolate and stir into boiling milk.
2. Beat white for meringue.
3. Bake in half set pie crust.
