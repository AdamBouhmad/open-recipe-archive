## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds beefsteak
- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 eggs
- salt
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Chop beefsteak.
2. Add a little salt, onion, parsley, milk, flour and eggs.
3. Bake one hour in a quick oven.
