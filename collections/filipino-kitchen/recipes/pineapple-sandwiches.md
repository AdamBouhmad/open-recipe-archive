## Ingredients

- bread
- pineapple
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut white bread into thin slices and remove the crust
2. Butter well
3. Chop pineapple very fine and drain off the juice
4. Sprinkle the minced fruit over the bread
5. Press down firmly and cut in squares
