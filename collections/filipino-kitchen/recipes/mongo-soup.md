## Ingredients

- 1 cup mongo
- 1½ quarts water
- salt, pepper and other seasoning

## Directions

1. Wash mongo.
2. Cover with water and let it stand in water all day.
3. Drain.
4. Add water, salt, pepper, and any other desired seasoning, and simmer until the mongo is tender (about two hours).
5. Press through a sieve.
6. Add onion.
7. Serve.
