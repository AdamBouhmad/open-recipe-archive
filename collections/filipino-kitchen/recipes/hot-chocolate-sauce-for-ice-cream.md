## Ingredients

- 2 ounces Baker's chocolate
- ¾ cup milk
- 2 cups light brown sugar
- Butter size of walnut
- 1 inch length cinnamon bark
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate over hot water; add sugar, cinnamon, milk and butter
2. Cook until it will form a soft ball when dropped on ice
3. Remove the cinnamon and add a little vanilla
4. Serve hot, pouring a little sauce on each dish of vanilla ice cream
