## Ingredients

- pop corn
- 1 tablespoon lard
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Into a large deep frying pan put a tablespoon of lard or butter and half a teaspoon salt.
2. Heat pan and when very hot throw in a handful of pop corn and cover.
3. Shake pan gently while corn is popping.
4. When corn is all popped the fat will be gone.
