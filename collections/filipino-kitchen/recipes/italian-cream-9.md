## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 1 ounce gelatine
- 3 eggs
- flavoring
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- dash of salt

## Directions

1. Make custard of milk, egg yolks, sugar and salt.
2. When cooked enough to coat the spoon, add ounce of gelatine, which has soaked half an hour in some of the cold milk.
3. As soon as dissolved, remove from the fire, and when it begins to stiffen fold in carefully the whites of eggs whipped to a froth.
4. Turn into a mould to set.
