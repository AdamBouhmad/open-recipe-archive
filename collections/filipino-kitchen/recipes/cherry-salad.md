## Ingredients

- maraschino cherries
- English walnuts
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon dry mustard
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 pint cream
- salt
- red pepper
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Fill maraschino cherries with English walnuts, sprinkle nuts around cherries.
2. Beat yolks of eggs, butter, mustard, salt, red and black pepper together.
3. Add vinegar and cook until thick.
4. When cold add the cream, beating it in well.
5. Pour dressing over cherries and serve on lettuce leaves.
