## Ingredients

- 2 dozen ripe tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 3 small green peppers
- ¾ cup sugar
- ⅜ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 cups vinegar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil all together until thick
2. Then add vinegar
3. Strain and set on the stove
4. Add salt and spices
5. Boil all together five minutes and remove
