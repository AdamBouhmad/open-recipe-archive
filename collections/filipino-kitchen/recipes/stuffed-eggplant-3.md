## Ingredients

- 1 eggplant
- minced ham
- 1/4 minced onion
- 2 tablespoons grated crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- water

## Directions

1. Cut eggplant in two
2. Scrape out all the inside and put it in a saucepan with a little minced ham
3. Cover with water and boil until soft
4. Drain
5. Add crumbs, butter, onion, salt and pepper
6. Stuff each half of the eggplant
