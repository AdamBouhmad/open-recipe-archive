## Ingredients

- 3 cocoanuts
- 1 pint water
- 4 pounds brown sugar

## Directions

1. Grate cocoanuts and to the grated meat add water
2. Wash thoroughly and squeeze out
3. To the liquid obtained add brown sugar and boil to the consistency of honey, skimming frequently
