## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 orange
- 1 egg
- 2 cups flour
- 4 level teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add the sugar gradually and grated rind of orange.
2. Add without separating yolk and white, one fourth cup of orange juice and flour and baking powder sifted together.
3. A little more flour may be needed to roll into a sheet.
4. Cut into squares, set in baking pan, dredge with granulated sugar and bake in a moderate oven.
