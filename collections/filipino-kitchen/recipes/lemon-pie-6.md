## Ingredients

- 1 small cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 small tablespoon butter
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 lemon
- 4 eggs
- pinch of salt
- 4 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Mix together sugar, butter, flour, juice and one half grated yellow rind of lemon, egg yolks, salt and hot water.
2. Cook until thick.
3. Put in baked crust.
4. Cover with whites of eggs beaten stiff with four tablespoons sugar.
5. Brown quickly and serve cold.
