## Ingredients

- ⅓ can grated pineapple
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Drain off the juice from half a can of grated pineapple, mix with it lemon and orange juice
2. Mash the pineapple pulp through a strainer, taking only what will go through easily
3. Mix the sifted pulp with the juice and add sugar to taste
4. Mix this with partly frozen plain ice cream and finish freezing
