## Ingredients

- lanzones
- mayonnaise
- lemon
- orange shells

## Directions

1. Carefully peel lanzones and divide into sections, removing all the inner skin.
2. Chill and dress with mayonnaise made with lemon instead of vinegar.
3. Serve in orange shells.
