## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel off the outside; cut off the ends.
2. Put into cold water and let them scald two minutes.
3. Pour off water; add cold water with a little salt.
4. Boil slowly until tender, thirty or forty minutes.
5. When done drain quite dry.
6. Pour a little melted butter over them and season to taste.
