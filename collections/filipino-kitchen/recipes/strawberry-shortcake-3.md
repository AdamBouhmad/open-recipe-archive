## Ingredients

- 1 box Baguio strawberries
- 1 pint flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3/4 tablespoon butter
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Select firm clean berries
2. Wash in boiled or distilled water
3. Place in colander and deluge with boiling water quickly
4. Turn out on platter or flat dish
5. Mash, sweeten to taste and put on ice
6. Add salt, baking powder and sugar to flour
7. Mix thoroughly
8. Rub in butter and wet to a soft dough with cold milk
9. Roll out like biscuit
10. Spread lightly with butter
11. Fold once
12. Bake
13. Unfold
14. Spread mashed berries between layers and on top
