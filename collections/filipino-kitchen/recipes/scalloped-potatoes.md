## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- salt and pepper
- hot milk
- flour
- 1 onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and slice raw potatoes thin.
2. Butter a deep dish.
3. Put in a layer of potatoes; season with salt, pepper and butter, also onion if liked.
4. Sprinkle in a little flour.
5. Add another layer of potato and seasoning, and so proceed until the dish is filled.
6. Pour over enough hot milk to cover.
7. Put into oven and bake until tender, about three-fourths of an hour.
