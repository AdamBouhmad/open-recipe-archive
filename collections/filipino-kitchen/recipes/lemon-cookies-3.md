## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter and lard mixed
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoon lemon extract
- flour (about 2 cups)

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs well beaten and milk
3. Add flour and baking powder sifted together, and extract
4. Use flour enough to make stiff
5. Roll thin, cut out and bake
