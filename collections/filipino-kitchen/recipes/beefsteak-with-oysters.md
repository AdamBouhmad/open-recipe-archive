## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- 1 quart oysters
- 3 ounces butter
- 1 tablespoon sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put oysters with very little of the juice into stew-pan.
2. When they come to a boil, remove the scum.
3. Stir in butter mixed with flour.
4. Let boil one minute until it thickens.
5. Pour over the steak.
6. Serve hot.
