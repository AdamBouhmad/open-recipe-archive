## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- 1 dozen onions
- beef drippings or butter

## Directions

1. Have ready in a frying pan a dozen onions cut in slices and fried brown in a little beef drippings or butter.
2. Dish your steak and lay the onions thickly over the top.
