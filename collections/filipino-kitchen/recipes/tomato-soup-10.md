## Ingredients

- 12 tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 4 cups gingetan
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- 3/8 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Place tomatoes in boiling water.
2. Remove skins.
3. Cook them for fifteen minutes, with sugar and soda.
4. Run through a sieve.
5. Mix the gingetan (obtained by pouring hot water through grated coconut) and flour well together.
6. Heat until thickened, stirring constantly.
7. Add butter, salt and pepper.
8. Combine the mixtures and reheat.
