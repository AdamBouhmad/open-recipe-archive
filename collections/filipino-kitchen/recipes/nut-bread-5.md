## Ingredients

- 2 cups graham flour
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1 cup white flour
- 2 cups sour milk
- 2 cups English walnut meats
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups seedless raisins
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in 2 teaspoons hot water
- 3/4 cup sugar
- large pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients, add nuts and raisins.
2. Add molasses, milk, and soda dissolved in water.
3. Bake forty five minutes in slow oven.
