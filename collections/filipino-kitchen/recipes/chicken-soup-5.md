## Ingredients

- stock from 1 chicken
- 2 tablespoons rice
- 2 tablespoons parsley
- 2 tablespoons celery

## Directions

1. Skim off grease from stock
2. Take 1 quart of stock
3. Put in rice, parsley, celery
4. Cook one hour
5. Season
