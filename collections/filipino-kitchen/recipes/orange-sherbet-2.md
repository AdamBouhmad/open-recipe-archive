## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 1 pint sugar
- 2 lemons
- 2 tablespoons gelatine
- 1 pint cold water
- 1 teaspoon orange extract

## Directions

1. Mix juice with one pint of cold water; add sugar; stir in the gelatine soaked in cold water, and flavor with extract
2. Freeze
