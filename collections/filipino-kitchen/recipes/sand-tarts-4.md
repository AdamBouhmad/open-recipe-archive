## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- blanched almonds or pine nuts
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually and egg well beaten; then flour mixed and sifted with baking powder.
2. Chill, toss on floured board and roll one eighth of an inch thick.
3. Shape with a doughnut cutter.
4. Brush over with white of egg and sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
5. Split nuts and arrange three halves on each at equal distances.
6. Place on buttered sheet and bake eight minutes in slow oven.
