## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare squash; take out the seeds; cut it in pieces and stew slowly from twenty to forty minutes until quite soft, in a very little water.
2. Drain, squeeze and press well.
3. Season, when mashed, with salt, pepper and a very little butter.
