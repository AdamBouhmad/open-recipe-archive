## Ingredients

- 4 hard boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cupful milk
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon salt
- dash pepper
- egg and bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Rub butter and flour into a ball and stir into the hot milk until dissolved.
2. Cook well, adding all other ingredients, parsley last.
3. Let stand until thoroughly stiff.
4. Form into cutlets or croquettes.
5. Dip into egg and crumbs.
6. Fry in wire basket in very hot fat.
7. For cutlets, add paper quill to end, to look like well served chop.
