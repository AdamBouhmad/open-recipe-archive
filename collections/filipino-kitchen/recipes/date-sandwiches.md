## Ingredients

- dates
- cold water
- butter
- thin sliced bread

## Directions

1. Clean dates and cover with cold water for three minutes
2. Drain and lay on a plate, and put in hot oven for five minutes
3. Remove stones, cut in slices and place between thin buttered slices of bread
