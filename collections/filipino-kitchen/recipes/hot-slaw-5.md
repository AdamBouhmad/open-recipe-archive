## Ingredients

- 3/4 head cabbage
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- salt
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage fine, cook in salt water slowly one hour.
2. Drain off water.
3. Let simmer in one cup of vinegar and water fifteen minutes, add butter and seasoning to taste, and serve.
