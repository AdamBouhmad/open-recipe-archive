## Ingredients

- string beans
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Select young, tender beans; string and cut each one in three pieces lengthwise.
2. Cover with boiling salted water; boil twenty minutes and drain.
3. Throw them into cold water for ten minutes.
4. Then put them in boiling unsalted water, and cook for fifteen minutes longer.
5. Drain and cool.
6. Arrange them neatly on a small platter; cover with French dressing.
