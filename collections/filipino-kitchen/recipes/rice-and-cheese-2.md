## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 cup cream
- dash of red pepper

## Directions

1. Cook rice for twenty minutes.
2. Make a sauce with grated cheese, salt, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper and cream.
3. Place alternate layers of rice and cheese in dish and bake.
