## Ingredients

- 1 pint or 1 can corn
- 1 pint water
- 1 quart milk
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 slice onion
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the corn in the water thirty minutes.
2. Scald the milk with the onion, and when it boils add the butter and flour, creamed.
3. Cook for a few minutes.
4. Add the corn.
5. Cook a little longer.
6. Strain and serve.
