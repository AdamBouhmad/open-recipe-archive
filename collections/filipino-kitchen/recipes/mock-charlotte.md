## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint boiling water
- 1/4 pint milk
- 1 cup sugar
- yolks 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons corn starch
- white 4 eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Take boiling water, sugar and vanilla, thicken with moistened corn starch.
2. Pour this over the beaten whites of four eggs.
3. Set on ice.
4. For the custard: Take milk, yolks of eggs, sugar and vanilla.
5. Pour the boiling milk gradually over the eggs, bring to a boil, but remove immediately from the fire to prevent curdling.
