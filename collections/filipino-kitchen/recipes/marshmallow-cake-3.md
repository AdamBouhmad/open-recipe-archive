## Ingredients

- angel cake
- 1 large cup sugar
- 2 small egg whites
- marshmallows
- vanilla
- almond

## Directions

1. Bake angel cake in two layers
2. Make boiled icing of one large cup sugar to whites of two small eggs
3. Cut marshmallows in halves and put on coating of icing, then more icing
4. Flavor with two-thirds vanilla and one-third almond
