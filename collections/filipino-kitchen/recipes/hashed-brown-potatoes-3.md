## Ingredients

- 3/4 pint boiled potatoes
- salt and pepper
- 3 tablespoons cream
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Chop cold boiled potatoes rather fine; to each half pint add three tablespoons cream, salt and pepper; mix.
2. Put a tablespoon butter into a shallow frying pan, and put in the potatoes.
3. Flatten them in a perfectly smooth layer; cook slowly until a golden brown.
4. Fold one half over the other and turn out on a heated dish.
