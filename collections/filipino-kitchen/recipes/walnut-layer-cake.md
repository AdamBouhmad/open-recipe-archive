## Ingredients

- walnuts
- cake batter
- boiled icing

## Directions

1. Chop nuts fine and mix small cupful with cake batter
2. Put boiled icing between layers, and sprinkle thickly with chopped nuts
3. Around the top place halves of nuts on icing
