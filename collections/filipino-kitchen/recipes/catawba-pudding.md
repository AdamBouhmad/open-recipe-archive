## Ingredients

- ½ package gelatine
- ½ cup boiled rice
- 1 pint whipped cream
- ¾ cup chopped figs
- 1 cup pulverized sugar
- ¾ cup chopped dates
- 1 cup grape juice
- ¾ cup cold water

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in half a cup of cold water and melt over hot water
2. Mix with other ingredients
3. Freeze
