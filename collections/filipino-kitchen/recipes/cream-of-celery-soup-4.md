## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cooked celery
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 3/4 teaspoon celery salt
- 3/4 saltspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Add stewed and sifted celery to the foundation
2. Combine milk, butter and flour creamed
3. Add onion juice, celery salt, white pepper and a dash of cayenne or little slices of sweet pepper
