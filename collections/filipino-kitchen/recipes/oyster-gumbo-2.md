## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 1 quart chicken stock
- 1 sliced onion
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1½ pints water
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 quart young okra
- dash cayenne
- 50 oysters

## Directions

1. Prepare a chicken as for fricassee.
2. Put in baking pan.
3. Add onion, half pint of water and bake until tender.
4. Wash and cut okra into thin slices.
5. Put in a saucepan, add pint of water and cook slowly for half hour.
6. Lift the chicken to a soup kettle.
7. Add chicken stock or boiling water.
8. Simmer gently for twenty minutes.
9. Add salt, cayenne, paprika.
10. Add okra and oysters.
11. Cover the saucepan, cook for five minutes, and send to the table.
