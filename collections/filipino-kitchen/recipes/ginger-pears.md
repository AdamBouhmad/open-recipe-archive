## Ingredients

- 8 pounds hard Chinese pears
- 6 pounds sugar
- juice 4 lemons
- 2 ounces native green ginger

## Directions

1. Peel, core and slice thin the pears.
2. Scrape the ginger and pass through the meat chopper.
3. Let all stand a short time to start the juice and then boil slowly about an hour.
