## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- 1 pint milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbage until tender and chop fine
2. Place in a baking pan with milk, eggs and butter, and pepper and salt to taste
3. Mix well
4. Bake one-half hour
