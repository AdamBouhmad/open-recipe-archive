## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1 cup milk or cream

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs; slice thin in rings.
2. Place in well buttered baking dish a layer of bread crumbs, then a layer of eggs, etc. Have crumbs on top.
3. Over the whole pour a large cup of cream or milk, and brown nicely in a moderately heated oven.
