## Ingredients

- 6 pounds green tomatoes
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 1.5 cups vinegar
- 0.5 pound raisins

## Directions

1. Pare the tomatoes and boil two minutes in salt and water.
2. Drain in colander.
3. Add raisins and vinegar, cook slowly until thoroughly done.
4. Add sugar when nearly done.
