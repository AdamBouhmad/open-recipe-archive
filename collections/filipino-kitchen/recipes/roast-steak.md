## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- 1 pint boiling water
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 onion
- 1 teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Put a very thick piece of steak into the baking pan in boiling water.
2. Add pepper and salt, onion and butter.
3. Roast one hour if wanted well done, or less time if preferred rare.
