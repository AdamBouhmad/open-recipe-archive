## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 cups flour
- 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 cup chopped raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix soda and flour, add other dry ingredients and sift
2. Add butter, eggs slightly beaten and milk
3. Mold in enough more flour to roll out
