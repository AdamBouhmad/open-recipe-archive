## Ingredients

- large green papayas
- sugar
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Select large green papayas in the market
2. Peel and cut in small pieces as with green apples
3. Cook until very soft
4. Remove from stove and put in a very little sugar and the juice of one lemon
5. Beat until very soft and smooth
6. Put in the ice box to cool and serve with roast pork or roast goose
