## Ingredients

- 1 can Cross and Blackwell strawberry jam
- 1 quart boiling water
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- whites 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over contents of one can of strawberry jam (any other flavor if preferred)
2. When cool, add lemon juice and freeze
3. When almost frozen add well beaten whites of two eggs
