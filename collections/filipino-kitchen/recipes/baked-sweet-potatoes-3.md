## Ingredients

- 3 sweet potatoes
- 1/2 cup boiling water
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Have boiled three large sweet potatoes with their jackets on; when done, remove the skins and slice into a baking dish.
2. Sprinkle sugar and small pieces of butter over the potatoes and pour over all one half cup boiling water.
3. Bake a light brown.
