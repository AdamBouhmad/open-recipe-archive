## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup fine bread crumbs
- 2 cups grated cheese
- 1 saltspoon dry mustard
- 2 eggs
- pinch of cayenne

## Directions

1. Melt butter and whisk it into the boiled milk; add the dissolved bread crumbs; let cool.
2. Beat the yolks of eggs; put in seasoning, add cheese, mix all together.
3. Add whipped whites of eggs; pour into deep tin lined with buttered paper.
4. Allow for rising.
5. Bake about twenty minutes and serve immediately on taking from oven.
