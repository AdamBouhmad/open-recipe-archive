## Ingredients

- 1 can grated pineapple or 1 quart fresh fruit
- 1 tablespoon gelatine
- 1 tablespoon extract lemon
- 1 pint sugar
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Mix well pineapple, sugar, water, gelatine dissolved in hot water, and lemon extract
2. Freeze
