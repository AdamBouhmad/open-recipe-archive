## Ingredients

- 1 pint fowl
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt and pepper
- ¾ pint milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Rub together butter, flour and seasoning, add milk; stir until boiling
2. Add this to the hard boiled yolks of eggs, rubbing all the while
3. When you have a smooth yellow sauce, add the cold cooked fowl, which has been cut in cubes of an inch
4. Serve very hot
