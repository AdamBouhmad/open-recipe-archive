## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons minute tapioca
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup grated cocoanut
- 1 quart milk
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 4 eggs
- maraschino cherries

## Directions

1. Put tapioca in the milk and cook in a double boiler at least ten minutes.
2. Beat the yolks of eggs and sugar together; add them to the milk and half of the cocoanut.
3. Cook five minutes longer; take from the fire, and when cold turn into a shallow glass dish.
4. Beat the whites of eggs until light; add powdered sugar and beat again; spread this over the top and sprinkle over the remaining cocoanut.
5. Place a few maraschino cherries over the top.
