## Ingredients

- 2 cocoanuts
- 4 lumps native sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Extract milk from the meat of two cocoanuts
2. Place four lumps of native brown sugar in a stew-pan, add a little water place over the fire until sugar is melted
3. Strain thru cotton cloth
4. Pour the sugar into the coconut milk
5. Cook this mixture in a deep stew-pan, stirring occasionally, until thick enough to spread when cool
