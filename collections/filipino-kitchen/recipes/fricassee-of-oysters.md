## Ingredients

- 50 oysters
- 1 pint liquor and milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- cayenne pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Drain and wash oysters.
2. Cook until the gills curl.
3. Drain, saving the liquor.
4. Add to it sufficient milk to make a pint.
5. Put butter and flour in a saucepan and mix.
6. Add liquor and milk, and stir until boiling.
7. Add salt, cayenne pepper and the oysters.
8. Heat over hot water.
9. Beat the yolks of eggs with cream.
10. Stir them quickly into the oysters.
11. Take from the fire, and serve on toast, or in a dish, garnished with triangular pieces of toast.
12. Sprinkle finely chopped parsley over the top.
