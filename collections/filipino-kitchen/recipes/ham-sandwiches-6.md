## Ingredients

- 1 can deviled ham
- butter the size of an egg
- 6 pickled cucumbers
- ⅛ teaspoon dry mustard
- little onion if desired
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Mince the pickles and onions
2. Mix all together thoroughly
3. Spread thinly on thin slices of bread
