## Ingredients

- carrots
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper
- flour
- milk
- water

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the carrots and divide them into strips
2. Place in water enough to cover them
3. Add a spoon of salt
4. Boil slowly until tender
5. Drain and replace in pan, with two tablespoons of butter rolled in flour, salt and pepper
6. Add enough milk to moisten the whole
7. Let it come to a boil and serve hot
