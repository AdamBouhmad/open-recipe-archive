## Ingredients

- 1 eggplant
- minced ham
- 3/4 minced onion
- 2 tablespoons grated crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut eggplant in two; scrape out all the inside and put it in a saucepan with a little minced ham.
2. Cover with water and boil until soft; drain.
3. Add crumbs, butter, onion, salt and pepper; stuff each half of the hull.
4. Add a small lump of butter to each and bake fifteen minutes.
