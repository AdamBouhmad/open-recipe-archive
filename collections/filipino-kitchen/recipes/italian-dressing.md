## Ingredients

- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon tomato catsup
- dash of cayenne
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Put salt, cayenne and catsup in a bowl; gradually add the oil, mixing all the time.
2. Add garlic that has been mashed to pulp and vinegar.
