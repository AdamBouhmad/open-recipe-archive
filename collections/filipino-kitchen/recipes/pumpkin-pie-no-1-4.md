## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons stewed pumpkin
- 1/2 pint rich milk
- salt
- cinnamon or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stew pumpkin, cut into small pieces, in half pint of water, and when soft mash fine with potato masher.
2. Let the water dry away, watching closely to prevent scorching or burning.
3. For each pie take one well beaten egg, half cup sugar, two tablespoons pumpkin, half pint rich milk, little salt.
4. Stir well together, and season with nutmeg or cinnamon.
5. Bake with under crust in hot oven.
