## Ingredients

- 4 ripe bananas
- 1 pint cold custard
- lemon flavoring
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Wash very ripe bananas
2. Rub through a sieve
3. Add to a pint of cold custard
4. Flavor with lemon
5. Serve in glasses with a tablespoon of whipped cream on top of each
