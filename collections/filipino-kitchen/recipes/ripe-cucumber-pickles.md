## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 large ripe cucumbers
- syrup as for crab apple pickles
- cold salted water

## Directions

1. Select large ripe cucumbers
2. Cut lengthwise and remove the seeds
3. Pare and cut in strips three inches long and one and one half inches wide
4. Put into sauce pan and cover with cold salted water, place on slow fire and bring to a boil; be careful not to cook too long
5. Remove from the water and let drain
6. When quite dry, have ready a syrup as for crab apple pickles and place cucumber pieces in a jar and pour the boiling syrup over them
7. Let stand a few days before using
