## Ingredients

- grated cheese
- English walnuts
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of grated cheese and chopped English walnut meats
2. Season with salt and cayenne
3. Spread on thin slices of bread
4. Especially good with brown bread
