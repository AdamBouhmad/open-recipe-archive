## Ingredients

- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 saltspoon pepper
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put salt and pepper in bowl, add slowly the oil, stirring all the time.
2. When salt is dissolved add vinegar.
3. When well mixed, serve.
