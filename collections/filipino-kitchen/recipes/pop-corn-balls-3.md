## Ingredients

- bowl of popped corn
- 4 tablespoons water
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Put sugar and water in saucepan and place on hot part of stove.
2. Boil until the syrup hardens when tested in cold water.
3. Place pan where syrup will keep warm but not boil.
4. Pick up pop corn one by one and dip into the syrup and stick together, adding more and more until a ball is formed.
5. Harden in a cool place.
