## Ingredients

- 3 pounds lean beef
- 1 dozen butter crackers
- 4 eggs beaten
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- butter size of an egg

## Directions

1. Chop beef finely.
2. Add crackers, eggs beaten, pepper, salt and butter.
3. Mix thoroughly, mould into two bricks, and bake like a roast.
