## Ingredients

- 1 ounce gelatine
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup lemon juice
- 2 cups orange juice
- 4 eggs
- lady fingers

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water half an hour
2. Make syrup of sugar, lemon and orange juice
3. Turn slowly into the beaten egg yolks
4. Beat all the time
5. Put into double boiler and cook until thick
6. Add dissolved gelatine
7. Strain and beat until cold
8. Add whites of eggs beaten to a stiff froth
9. Line a mould with lady fingers
10. Pour in the mixture
11. Put on the ice to harden
