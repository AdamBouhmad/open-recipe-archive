## Ingredients

- 12 green peppers
- 12 red peppers
- 12 onions
- 1.5 pints vinegar
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 tablespoons salt

## Directions

1. Remove seeds from peppers; chop peppers and onions fine; cover with boiling water and let stand a few minutes; drain.
2. Add vinegar, salt and sugar.
3. Boil up thoroughly once and it is finished.
