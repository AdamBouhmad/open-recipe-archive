## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter
2. Add milk, flour and baking powder
3. Have whites of eggs well beaten and fold in batter
4. Bake in layers or loaf. A little more flour is used for loaf
