## Ingredients

- eggplant
- cracker or bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- butter
- salt water

## Directions

1. Take fresh, purple eggplant; cut into slices a quarter of an inch thick
2. Soak for half an hour in cold salt water
3. Dry; dip in cracker or bread crumbs; then in beaten egg, more crumbs
4. Fry to a light brown
5. Do not remove from the water until ready to cook
