## Ingredients

- santols
- sugar, equal parts with juice

## Directions

1. Wash the fruit thoroughly. Cut up without paring into eighths, taking out the seeds.
2. Barely cover the fruit with water and boil until the fruit is soft.
3. Squeeze the fruit through a cloth.
4. Take equal parts of juice and sugar. When measured, put the juice on the stove to come to a boil and put the sugar in the oven to heat through, being careful not to scorch it.
5. When both are hot, put in the sugar.
6. Barely cover the seeds and boil, but be careful as the pulp burns to the bottom readily.
7. Squeeze through a cloth and treat the seed juice in the same way as the fruit juice.
8. Do not cook after it jellies.
