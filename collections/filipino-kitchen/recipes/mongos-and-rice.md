## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup mongos
- 2 tablespoons lard
- 3/4 cup rice
- 1 onion
- 1 1/2 cups stewed tomatoes
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Wash mongos, cover with cold water and bring to a boil.
2. Drain, rub off husks, cover with boiling water and cook until tender.
3. Boil rice in a separate stewpan.
4. Slice onion and saute in hot lard until clear and tender, but not brown.
5. Add boiled rice, boiled mongo and stewed tomato.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Heat thoroughly and serve while hot.
