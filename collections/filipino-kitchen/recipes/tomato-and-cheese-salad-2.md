## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- red peppers
- cream cheese
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes after scalding them and slice thickly.
2. Wipe each slice dry and cover with a layer of cream cheese mixed with chopped red pepper and garnished with small rounds of the pepper.
3. Serve with French dressing.
