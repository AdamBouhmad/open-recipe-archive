## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 quart milk
- apples
- sugar to taste
- seasoning to taste

## Directions

1. Lay a crust on your pie plates, slice apples thin, and half fill plates.
2. Pour over them a custard made from eggs and milk, sweetened and seasoned to taste.
