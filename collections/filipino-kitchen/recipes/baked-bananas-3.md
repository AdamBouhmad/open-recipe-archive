## Ingredients

- 6 bananas
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon lime or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel and cut bananas into halves lengthwise
2. Place slices in a shallow granite pan
3. Melt the butter and stir in sugar and lime or lemon juice
4. Pour one-half of this mixture over the bananas
5. Place the pan in a slow oven and bake twenty minutes
6. While baking, baste the bananas occasionally with the remainder of the syrup mixture
