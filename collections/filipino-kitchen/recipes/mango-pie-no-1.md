## Ingredients

- 3 mangoes, rather green
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon spices
- 1 teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Pare and slice fine mangoes, add sugar, flour, vinegar, spices and butter.
2. Bake with two crusts.
