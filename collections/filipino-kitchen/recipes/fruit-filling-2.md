## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons citron
- 4 tablespoons raisins
- 3/4 cup almonds
- 1/4 pound figs
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eggs to stiff froth, add sugar.
2. Then thoroughly mix into this the whole of the chopped ingredients.
3. Put between layers of cake while it is hot.
