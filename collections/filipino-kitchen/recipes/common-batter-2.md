## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

1. Mix in very smooth batter, a little thicker than batter cakes
