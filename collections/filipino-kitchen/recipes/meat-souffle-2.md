## Ingredients

- 1 cup minced meat
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley or celery
- pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 saltspoon salt
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat egg separately
2. Mix the meat, yolk of egg and seasoning and beat thoroughly
3. When ready for oven, fold in the white of egg and sprinkle with cracker crumbs
4. Bake fifteen minutes
5. Garnish with slices of lemon
