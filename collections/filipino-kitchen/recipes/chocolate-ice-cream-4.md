## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cocoa
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- Plain ice cream

## Directions

1. Place cocoa in pan to heat (without burning) with one tablespoon of hot water
2. When the consistency of paste, add to plain ice cream and freeze
