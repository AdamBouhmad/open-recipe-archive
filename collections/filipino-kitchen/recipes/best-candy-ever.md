## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cups golden color corn syrup
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cups water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3/4 cup walnuts
- 1/2 cup dates or figs

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, syrup and water until it becomes crisp when dropped in water.
2. Beat the whites of eggs very stiff.
3. Pour the syrup slowly into the eggs and beat until it begins to harden.
4. Add vanilla, nuts and dates or figs which have been cut in small pieces with sharp scissors.
5. Spread rather thick upon a buttered platter, and cut into squares.
