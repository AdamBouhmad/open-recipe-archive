## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1 cup scalded milk
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1/2 cup jelly or jam
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cream butter and flour, stir into scalded milk
2. Cook until mixture boils
3. Beat eggs and sugar lightly and when well mixed add to the first mixture
4. To this add sifted bread crumbs, and flavor with any desired extract
5. Bake in well buttered patty pans
6. This is sufficient for five persons
7. Bake until the mixture is well set and browned
8. Place on serving dish, dust with powdered sugar, and serve with royal sauce
9. For sauce: Mix cornstarch and butter, add gradually boiling water and jelly or jam, and a little lemon juice if desired
10. If you use jam and the seeds are objectionable, strain before serving
