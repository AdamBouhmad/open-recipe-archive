## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated pineapple
- 3⅜ quarts water
- 3 oranges
- 3 lemons
- 1 cup grape juice
- 2 cups sugar
- 1½ cup tea

## Directions

1. Take pineapple and one pint of water and cook for fifteen minutes
2. Strain through cheese cloth
3. Boil sugar and one pint water for ten minutes
4. Add freshly made tea, lemon juice, orange juice, grape juice and two and one-half quarts water
5. Place in punch bowl with large block of ice
