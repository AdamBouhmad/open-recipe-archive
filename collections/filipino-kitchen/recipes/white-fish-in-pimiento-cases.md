## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of white fish or halibut
- dash of cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- whites of 4 eggs
- pimientos
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 cup shrimps
- 1 saltspoon salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook white fish, or halibut, in salted water, then drain and rub through a sieve.
2. Season with a few grains of cayenne pepper and lemon juice, then add cream sauce.
3. Blend thoroughly, and fold in the beaten whites of eggs.
4. Butter individual moulds and line with pimientos.
5. Fill with the mixture, and stand moulds in pan of hot water.
6. Cover tops with buttered paper, and bake in a moderate oven for twenty minutes.
7. Unmould on a hot platter.
8. Garnish with parsley and serve with shrimp sauce.
9. Melt butter, add flour, and gradually hot water.
10. Stir and cook until smooth.
11. Season with salt, a dash of pepper, lemon juice.
12. Add the yolk of an egg and reheat.
13. Stir in shrimps, mashed and cut into pieces.
