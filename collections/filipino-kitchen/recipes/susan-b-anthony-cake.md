## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2/3 cups milk
- 2 1/3 cups flour
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter with the yolks of eggs
2. Add milk and well sifted flour
3. After the other ingredients have been well stirred, sift baking powder into batter
4. Lastly, fold in the stiffly beaten whites
5. Bake in loaf, flavor to taste
