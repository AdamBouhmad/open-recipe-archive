## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- 1 onion
- salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon minced parsley

## Directions

1. Dice potatoes.
2. Heat butter in a frying pan; fry in it one small onion until it begins to change color.
3. Put in the potatoes; sprinkle well with salt and pepper; stir and cook about five minutes, taking care not to break them.
4. They must not brown.
5. Just before taking up, stir in a tablespoon minced parsley.
6. Drain dry by shaking in a colander.
7. Serve very hot.
