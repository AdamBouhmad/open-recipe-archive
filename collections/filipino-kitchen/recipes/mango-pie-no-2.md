## Ingredients

- 8 green mangoes
- 1 1/2 cups sugar (or more)
- spice

## Directions

1. Peel and boil mangoes until tender.
2. Pour off water; slice mango from seed.
3. Mix with plenty of sugar, at least a cup and a half.
4. Add spice and fill into crust.
5. Cover and bake.
