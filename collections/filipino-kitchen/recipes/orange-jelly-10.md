## Ingredients

- 1/4 box gelatine
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 cups boiling water
- 3 oranges
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water half an hour; add sugar and boiling water and stir until dissolved.
2. Grate the peel of two oranges and squeeze the juice of three on the peel.
3. Let stand for half an hour, while gelatine is dissolving, and then strain into jelly.
4. Turn into halved orange skins as moulds.
5. Serve in skins with whipped cream on top of each.
