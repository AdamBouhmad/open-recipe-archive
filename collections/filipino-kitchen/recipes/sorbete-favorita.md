## Ingredients

- 1 can plums (juice)
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 4 cups boiling water
- 2 cups sugar
- whites 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix juice from can of plums with water, sugar, cornstarch mixed with a little water and freeze; when half frozen add egg whites stiffly beaten
