## Ingredients

- 1 pint mashed potatoes
- 2 egg whites
- 3/4 cup milk

## Directions

1. Put cold mashed potatoes in a saucepan; add milk; stir and beat until the potatoes are hot and smooth.
2. Take from fire; fold in the well-beaten whites of two eggs.
3. Heap in a baking dish and brown quickly in a hot oven.
4. Serve with roast or broiled beef.
