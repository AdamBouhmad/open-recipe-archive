## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 4 squares chocolate (unsweetened)
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Grate chocolate, mix with milk and yolk of one egg and sugar, boil until thick
2. When cool add vanilla
3. Cream butter and sugar, add the egg yolks well beaten, and milk
4. Then add flour and soda
5. Mix in Part I, and, lastly, fold in well beaten white
6. Bake in layers and put together with boiled icing
