## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. fresh Holland butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 6 eggs
- 3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon brandy

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter
2. Add the egg yolks (seven, if small) well beaten
3. Add flour sifted three times, baking powder sifted in last sifting
4. Have whites beaten very stiff and fold in
5. Lastly, add brandy
6. Bake thirty-five to forty minutes in moderate oven — if coal stove, take forty-five minutes
