## Ingredients

- 1 quart wheat bran
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 pint flour
- 1 tablespoon lard
- 3 cups sour milk
- 6 tablespoons molasses
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients; add molasses mixed with soda dissolved in little water, lard and milk.
2. Stir well and bake in gem tins.
