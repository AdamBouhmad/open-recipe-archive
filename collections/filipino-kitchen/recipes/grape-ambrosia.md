## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- Juice of 3 lemons
- 2 cups milk
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup grape juice
- Whites of 4 eggs
- ¾ can grated pineapple

## Directions

1. Mix milk with water, sugar and fruit
2. Partly freeze; add the beaten whites of eggs and continue to freeze until hard
