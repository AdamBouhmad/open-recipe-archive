## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 pint water
- 1 cup Maraschino cherries
- 2 quarts mineral water
- 1 lemon
- 2 bananas
- 1 pineapple

## Directions

1. Make syrup by boiling sugar, water and the grated rind of lemon
2. While hot, dice into it two bananas, grated pineapple and cherries
3. When ready to serve, put a block of ice in punch bowl and pour over it the mineral water, and add prepared juices
