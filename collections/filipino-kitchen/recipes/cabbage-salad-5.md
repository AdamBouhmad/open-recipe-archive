## Ingredients

- 1 head cabbage
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tomatoes
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take a firm head of cabbage, cut fine, add hard-boiled eggs, chopped fine, vinegar, sugar, mustard, and salt and pepper to taste.
2. Mix well.
3. Garnish with sliced tomatoes.
