## Ingredients

- greens
- salt water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean carefully through several washings of salt water.
2. Allow to stand in salt water a half hour or so.
3. Put in boiling water with a handful of salt; boil until the stalks are tender.
4. Cut away any tough stalks.
5. When tender, drain, chop them a little, and return to the fire long enough to season with salt, pepper and butter.
6. Vinegar may be added if it is liked.
7. Serve while hot.
