## Ingredients

- 1 pineapple
- 4 lemons
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Pare and grate pineapple
2. Add the juice of lemons and syrup
3. Mix and add one quart of water
4. When quite cold strain and ice
