## Ingredients

- 1 pint chopped lamb
- ½ pint milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon chopped mint
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- dash of cayenne
- 6 slices toast
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Rub butter and flour together and the milk and seasoning
2. Cook until boiling
3. Add the mint and lamb
4. Place over hot water while you make the toast
5. Trim toast, butter and cover each slice with a thick layer of minced lamb, leaving a hole in center
6. Poach six eggs
7. Put one in each center
