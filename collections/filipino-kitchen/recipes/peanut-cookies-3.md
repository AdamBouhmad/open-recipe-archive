## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 1/2 tablespoons milk
- 3/4 cup chopped peanuts
- 3/4 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually; add well beaten egg.
2. Mix and sift baking powder, salt and flour; then add milk, peanuts, and lemon juice.
3. Drop on buttered sheets an inch apart.
4. Bake twelve to fifteen minutes in a slow oven.
