## Ingredients

- 1 cup apricot juice
- 1 cup water
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons corn starch
- 4 eggs (whites)
- 4 eggs (yolks)
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 cups milk
- salt
- flavor to taste

## Directions

1. Place fruit juice and water on stove
2. When hot, add corn starch dissolved in cold water
3. Stir until entirely cooked
4. Add a pinch of salt
5. Add stiff froth of whites of eggs
6. Stir up well from the bottom
7. Place on back of stove and turn over and over to make the whites of the eggs a little solid
8. Put some of this mixture in the mould then a layer of the solid fruit and so on until the mixture is used
9. Place on ice
10. Serve with yellow sauce made of milk, eggs yolks, sugar and flavoring to taste
