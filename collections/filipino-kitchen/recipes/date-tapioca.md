## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons tapioca
- 4 cups scalded milk
- 12 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup chopped dates
- pinch salt
- eggs
- lemon sauce or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca in cold water; mix with sugar, salt, add hot milk; cook fifteen minutes, add yolks of eggs; cook three minutes.
2. Beat whites of eggs stiff, heap on top; place in oven to brown.
3. Serve cold with lemon sauce or whipped cream.
