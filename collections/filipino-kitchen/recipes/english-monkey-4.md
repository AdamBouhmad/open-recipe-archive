## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup minced cheese
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 pint milk
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk, add cheese, butter, egg and seasoning.
2. Stir together until well cooked.
3. Serve on hot crackers.
