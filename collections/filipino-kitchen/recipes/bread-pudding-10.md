## Ingredients

- 1 pint bread crumbs
- 1 quart milk
- butter size of egg
- whites 2 eggs
- yolks 2 eggs
- 2 lemons
- 3/4 cup cocoanut
- salt and nutmeg
- tart jelly

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs until soft, add other ingredients and bake one hour
2. Serve with tart jelly on top
3. The grated rind of lemons and cocoanut added to the above is very delicious
4. Frost top with meringue of whites of eggs, sprinkle with cocoanut and brown
