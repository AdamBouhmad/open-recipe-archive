## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 2/3 cup flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 4 apples

## Directions

1. Sift together dry ingredients, add butter and milk, and apples cut finely; stir well and bake quickly.
