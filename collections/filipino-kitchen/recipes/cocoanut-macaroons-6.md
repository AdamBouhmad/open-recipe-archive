## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3 cups shredded cocoanut (dry)
- 2 teaspoons vanilla

## Directions

1. To the unbeaten whites of the three eggs add sugar and beat until thick and light
2. Add cocoanut and vanilla
3. Drop from end of oven until firm
