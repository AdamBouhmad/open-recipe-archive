## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups water
- 1 bottle Tansan
- 3 lemons
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Boil water and sugar ten minutes
2. Add the grated rind of one lemon, juice of three
3. Allow this to cool
4. At serving time add the egg, well beaten and the Tansan, poured from height in order that the mixture may foam
5. Serve with cracked ice
