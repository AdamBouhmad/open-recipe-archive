## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 1 onion
- salt and pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Peel, cook and mash the required amount.
2. Add while hot, salt, pepper and a very little chopped onion.
3. Form into small balls.
4. Dredge with flour.
5. Place around roast and bake about twenty minutes before serving.
