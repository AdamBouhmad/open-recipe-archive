## Ingredients

- prunes
- walnuts
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Stew prunes until they are soft, without any sugar.
2. Cut carefully on the side and remove the pit.
3. Place a piece of English walnut inside and serve with mayonnaise dressing.
