## Ingredients

- 1 cocoanut
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups hot water
- 1 lime

## Directions

1. Grate cocoanut and pour over it the hot water.
2. Stir until it becomes quite creamy.
3. Strain through a piece of gauze, squeezing to get all the cream.
4. Take the cocoanut milk and sugar, put on to boil.
5. Peel the lime without breaking pulp and drop into candy.
6. Boil without stirring until it thickens in water.
7. Take out lime and pour on buttered plates.
8. As soon as cool enough pull until creamy.
