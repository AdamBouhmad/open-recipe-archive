## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 1 large green pepper
- 1 small red pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons tomato catsup
- ½ pound ham, chopped
- ½ pound sausage or ½ pound shrimp or ½ pound chicken
- 1 pint boiling water
- sprig parsley

## Directions

1. Wash rice and soak in cold water for one hour
2. Fry the other ingredients in Crisco, then add the boiling water
3. Stir in the rice and meat
4. Cover the pot and cook slowly for thirty minutes
5. Add salt
6. If it boils too low, add boiling water
