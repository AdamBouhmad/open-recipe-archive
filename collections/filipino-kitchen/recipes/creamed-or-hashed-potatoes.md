## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop cold boiled potatoes rather fine; season with salt and pepper.
2. Put them in a baking dish; pour over sufficient good milk to just cover and put in a quick oven until nicely browned.
