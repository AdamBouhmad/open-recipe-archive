## Ingredients

- 4 lemons
- Whites of 4 eggs
- 1 pint sugar
- 1 quart boiling water

## Directions

1. Shave off the peel from two lemons in thin wafer like shavings, being careful not to get the white part
2. Put parings in a bowl, add the boiling water and let stand ten minutes, closely covered
3. Add the juice and sugar to the water
4. Mix well, and when cold, strain into the can and freeze
5. The addition of beaten whites of two eggs when half frozen adds to the recipe
