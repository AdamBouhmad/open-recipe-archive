## Ingredients

- 1 hard boiled egg
- ¾ pound grated cheese
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon melted butter
- 1 tablespoon vinegar or cold water

## Directions

1. Crumble the yolk of the egg
2. Put in the butter and work smooth
3. Add other ingredients, mixing all well
4. Spread between biscuits or oat cakes
5. If too highly seasoned, season to taste
