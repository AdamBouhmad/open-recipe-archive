## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cupful cracker crumbs
- salt, pepper and parsley
- oil from can salmon
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 teaspoon catsup
- 1 teaspoon corn starch

## Directions

1. Remove bones and skin from salmon and rub butter into it.
2. Beat up eggs and mix with salmon.
3. Add cracker crumbs with salt, pepper and parsley.
4. Pack in buttered mould and steam one hour.
5. Cook until thick the oil drained from salmon, one-half cup water, the catsup and corn starch.
6. Serve hot.
