## Ingredients

- pie crust
- grated cheese
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Make patties of rich pie crust and bake.
2. Fill with layer of jelly, a layer of grated cheese, another layer of jelly and last the cheese.
3. Put in oven to brown.
4. Good served with meat or fowl.
