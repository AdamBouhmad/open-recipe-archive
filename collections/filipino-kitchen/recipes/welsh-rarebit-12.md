## Ingredients

- 3 ounces grated dry cheese
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 ounces grated bread
- 3 ounces butter
- 1 dessert spoon made mustard
- pinch of salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix cheese with the yolks of eggs
2. add the grated bread and butter and seasoning
3. Toast slices of bread cut thin into shapes
4. spread the paste thick upon them and put in oven
5. Let them become hot and slightly browned
6. Serve hot
