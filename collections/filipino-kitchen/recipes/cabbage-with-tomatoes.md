## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- 3 tomatoes
- 2 small onions
- bit of garlic
- salt

## Directions

1. Let a cabbage boil until tender, then cut into small pieces
2. Fry together until cooked, two or three sliced tomatoes, two small onions sliced and a bit of garlic
3. Add salt
4. When cooked tender, spread over the cabbage and serve
