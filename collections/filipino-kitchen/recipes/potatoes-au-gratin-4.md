## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- cream sauce
- salt and pepper
- cheese

## Directions

1. Chop cold boiled potatoes rather fine; season with salt and pepper.
2. Put them in a baking dish; pour over sufficient cream sauce to just cover.
3. Dust the top with grated plain or Parmesan cheese, and put in a hot oven until nicely browned.
