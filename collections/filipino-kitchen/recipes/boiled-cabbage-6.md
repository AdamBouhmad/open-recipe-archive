## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- water
- bacon (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage thoroughly
2. Add considerable salt to the water when boiling
3. Boil for about an hour for large drum head cabbage or about thirty minutes for green savory cabbage
4. Do not let cabbage boil too long or it will become watery
5. When done, drain and serve
6. Boil with bacon if liked
