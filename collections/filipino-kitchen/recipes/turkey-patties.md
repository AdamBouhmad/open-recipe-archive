## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold turkey
- 1 cup cream
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 pint oysters
- salt and pepper
- patty shells

## Directions

1. Mix butter and flour.
2. Add the cream, salt and pepper, and cook until thick.
3. Add turkey and let simmer five minutes.
4. Stir in oysters and cook until they are plump.
5. Fill patty shells and serve.
