## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the fish and soak over night with the skin side up.
2. Next morning dry, put in wire broiler and broil, flesh side down, until a golden brown.
3. Turn and broil the skin side quickly.
4. Put on heated platter, moisten with butter that has been rubbed with an equal quantity of lemon juice, and serve.
