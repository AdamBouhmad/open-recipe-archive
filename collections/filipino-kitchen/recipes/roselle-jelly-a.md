## Ingredients

- roselle calyxes
- water
- sugar, equal amount to juice

## Directions

1. Wash the calyxes well, put them into a saucepan of granite ware with just enough water to cover the calyxes; boil until soft.
2. Strain the mass through a jelly bag.
3. Measure the juice and add an equal amount of sugar.
4. Cook until jelly is formed which will be in ten or twenty minutes.
