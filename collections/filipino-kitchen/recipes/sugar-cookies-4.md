## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/3 cup sweet milk
- 1 scant teaspoon baking powder
- 4 eggs
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Beat eggs very light, add sugar, beat again, add butter melted and sweet milk.
2. Have flour and baking powder in bowl, pour the above mixture in the middle and mix in gradually with a spoon.
3. Roll on a well floured cloth until very thin, sprinkle with granulated sugar, pass roller over lightly and cut.
4. Bake in a moderately quick oven.
