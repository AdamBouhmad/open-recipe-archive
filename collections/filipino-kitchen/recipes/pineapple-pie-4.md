## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup cream
- 5 eggs
- 1 pineapple

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream.
2. Add beaten yolks, then grated pineapple and cream, and lastly the beaten whites whipped in lightly.
3. Bake with under crust only.
