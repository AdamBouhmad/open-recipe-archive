## Ingredients

- bananas
- peas
- chopped nuts
- chopped parsley
- mayonnaise
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Peel large bananas; scoop out centers, making them boat shaped, and fill with the following mixture.
2. Mix small peas, chopped nuts, chopped parsley and the banana which was taken from the center with stiff mayonnaise.
3. Place half kernels of walnuts and sprigs of parsley on top.
