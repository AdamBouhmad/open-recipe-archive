## Ingredients

- 8 or 10 good sized onions
- milk or cream
- bread crumbs
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take onions, slice and boil until tender.
2. Cover bottom of buttered baking dish with onions.
3. Add layer of bread crumbs with bits of butter, pepper and salt.
4. Add more onions and more bread crumbs until dish is almost full.
5. Add milk and cream until full.
6. Bake twenty minutes or half an hour.
