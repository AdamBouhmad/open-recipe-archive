## Ingredients

- 1 can apricots
- 3 or 4 cups distilled water
- 1½ cups sugar

## Directions

1. Drain apricots and cut in small pieces
2. To the syrup add enough water to make four cups, and cook with sugar five minutes
3. Strain, add apricots and freeze
