## Ingredients

- 2 ounces macaroni
- 1/2 pound boneless codfish
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 pint strained tomato
- 1/2 tablespoon grated onion
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 saltspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Break macaroni into two inch lengths
2. Throw them into boiling water and boil rapidly for thirty minutes
3. Drain
4. Blanch for fifteen minutes in cold water; then cut in pieces half inch long
5. Wash codfish, cut into dice and cover with cold water
6. Bring just to the boiling point
7. Drain
8. Cover again with boiling water, and let it stand for five minutes, then drain
9. Rub together butter with flour
10. Add strained tomato, onion, salt and pepper
11. Stir until boiling
12. Add macaroni and fish
13. Stand over hot water five minutes and serve in a heated dish
