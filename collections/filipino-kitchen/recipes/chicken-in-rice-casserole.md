## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 2½ cups milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon pepper
- 1 pint cold chicken
- 1 cup peas
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash rice; put in boiling water and cook twenty minutes, then drain
2. Add one half cup milk, one tablespoon butter, salt and pepper
3. Stir into rather smooth paste
4. Brush custard cups with butter and line to the depth of one inch with rice mixture
5. Put two tablespoons butter and two of flour together
6. add one pint milk, stir until boiling, add salt and pepper and chicken nicely seasoned and blocked
7. Fill this mixture in center of cups, cover with rice, stand in pan of boiling water and cook in oven twenty minutes
8. Turn carefully on to heated dish, garnish with nicely seasoned peas and serve
