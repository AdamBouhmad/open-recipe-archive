## Ingredients

- stale cake
- sliced bananas
- boiled custard

## Directions

1. Cut cake in thin slices and line a dish with slices
2. Cover the cake with bananas sliced very thin
3. Over this pour a cupful of thin, boiled custard
4. Make another layer of cake, banana and custard
5. Set away to cool
