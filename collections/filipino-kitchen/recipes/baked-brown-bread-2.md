## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 5 tablespoons molasses
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1½ cups bran or graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1½ cups seeded raisins

## Directions

1. Sift together bran or graham flour and white flour.
2. Add flour mixture to liquid.
3. Sift soda into raisins; add raisins to bread mixture.
4. Bake in moderate oven fifty-five minutes.
