## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- potatoes
- onions
- butter
- stock or water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Alternate layers of steak chopped fine with layers of potatoes and onions.
2. Season each layer and add a piece of butter.
3. Over all pour stock or water.
4. Bake.
