## Ingredients

- 1 can corn
- 1 pint milk
- cracker crumbs
- butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of buttered baking dish with crackers rolled fine
2. Add layer of corn with bits of butter, salt and pepper
3. Alternate layers of cracker crumbs and corn until material is used up
4. Let the last layer be cracker crumbs with plenty of butter
5. Pour over it one pint of milk
6. Bake one hour
