## Ingredients

- thin unsweetened crackers
- 1 small box marshmallows
- butter

## Directions

1. Take any variety of thin, unsweetened cracker, spread lightly with butter and set in a baking pan
2. On the center of each cracker put a marshmallow with a bit of butter above (the butter may be omitted)
3. Put the pan into a hot oven until the marshmallow is softened and browned a little
4. Serve at once
