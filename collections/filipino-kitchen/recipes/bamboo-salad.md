## Ingredients

- bamboo sprouts
- 3 potatoes, cooked
- 1 onion
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Scrape the inside of bamboo sprouts very fine.
2. Put in boiling water and cook half an hour.
3. When it is done remove and drain.
4. Have ready three or four potatoes sliced or diced and some minced onion.
5. Mix all together and add mayonnaise, if desired, or other salad dressing.
