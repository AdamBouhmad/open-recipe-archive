## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons butter or lard
- 1/3 cup plus 1 tablespoon milk
- nutmeg
- butter
- oranges
- orange sauce

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients and sift well
2. Work in shortening with fingers, egg well beaten and milk
3. Bake in layer cake pan
4. Split and spread with butter
5. Cover with oranges which have been cut in small pieces and have stood an hour with plenty of sugar
6. Serve with orange sauce
