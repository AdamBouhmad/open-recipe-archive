## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup shortening
- 1 cup figs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 6 level teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Cream the sugar, shortening and the figs, which have been put through a food chopper
2. Add the milk slowly
3. Add the nutmeg and the salt
4. Sift together baking powder and flour and add
5. Roll a quarter of an inch thick
6. Bake in a hot oven from twelve to fifteen minutes
