## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup butter and lard mixed
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites and yolks separately
2. Melt butter and lard
3. Take milk, beaten egg yolks, melted butter and lard mixed, baking powder, and flour enough to make a batter as stiff as pancake batter
4. Add baking powder and the whites of eggs the last thing before cooking
