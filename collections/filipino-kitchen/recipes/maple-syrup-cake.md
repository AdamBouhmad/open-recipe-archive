## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup shortening
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 3/4 cup hot water
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup ginger
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 3/4 cup hot water

## Directions

1. Beat shortening to a cream
2. Beat in one half cup of sugar, eggs, well beaten
3. Then add alternately, flour, baking powder, soda and ginger sifted together, and hot water
4. Bake in a pan about thirty-five minutes using a pan about ten and one half by seven inches
5. When cold, invert and cover the surface with maple icing and decorate with halves of nut meats
