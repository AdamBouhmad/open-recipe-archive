## Ingredients

- 6 large mangoes
- 2 cups cream
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 cups distilled water

## Directions

1. Peal mangoes, cut from seeds in small pieces and mash
2. Make syrup by boiling sugar and water fifteen minutes, strain, add mashed mangoes, cool and freeze
3. When partially frozen add beaten cream
