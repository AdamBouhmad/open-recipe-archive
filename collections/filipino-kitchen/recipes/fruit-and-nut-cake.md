## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup nuts
- 3/4 cup citron
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup raisins (seeded)
- 1 cup currants
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add well beaten eggs and milk
3. Chop nuts and citron; flour fruit before adding to mixture
4. Add flour enough to make thick batter
5. Bake in slow oven
