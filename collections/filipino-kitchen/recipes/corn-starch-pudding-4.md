## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- whites 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoons corn starch
- vanilla flavor
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Dissolve corn starch into a little of the milk.
2. Stir sugar into the remainder and bring to a boil, add starch; stir constantly a few minutes then add the stiffly beaten whites of eggs; and cook a little longer.
3. Flavor with vanilla and mould, or add 1/4 of a cocoanut, grated, before putting into a mould, serve with cream or a yellow sauce.
