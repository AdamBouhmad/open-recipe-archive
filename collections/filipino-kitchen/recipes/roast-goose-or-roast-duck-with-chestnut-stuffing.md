## Ingredients

- goose
- 1 onion
- 1 orange
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 tablespoon currant jelly
- 1 pint large Spanish chestnuts
- 2 teaspoons milk
- fresh butter
- salt and pepper
- giblets

## Directions

1. Select a young plump goose or duck
2. Mince the giblets with a spoonful of fresh butter and a small onion
3. Add to gravy a tablespoon of currant jelly and juice of an orange
4. Boil chestnuts
5. Peel and mash with fresh butter, pepper and salt and milk
6. Stuff the goose with this
