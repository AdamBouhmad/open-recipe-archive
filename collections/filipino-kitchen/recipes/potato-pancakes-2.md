## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 large potatoes
- 1 egg
- 1 small grated onion
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Wash, peel and grate four or five large potatoes with a small grated onion, one beaten egg and salt to taste.
2. Stir well.
3. Fry in thin pancakes in lard, covered up, as this will make them nice and crisp and prevents the fat from being all over the stove.
4. Always stir the batter again before taking out for the next panful.
