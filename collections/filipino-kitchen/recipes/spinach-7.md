## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Do not cover while cooking.
2. Pick over carefully, and wash through several waters; drain and put into boiling water.
3. Fifteen to twenty minutes are generally enough to boil spinach.
4. Be careful to remove the scum.
5. When quite tender, drain and squeeze well; chop fine and put into pan with a piece of butter, salt and pepper.
6. Stew five minutes, stirring all the time until quite dry.
7. Turn into dish, shape into a mould and serve with hard-boiled eggs over the top.
