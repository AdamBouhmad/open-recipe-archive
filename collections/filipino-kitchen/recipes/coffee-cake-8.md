## Ingredients

- butter, melted, size of an egg
- 2 cups flour (before sifting)
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and bake twenty minutes
