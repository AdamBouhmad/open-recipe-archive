## Ingredients

- 6 eggs, beaten separately
- 1 3/4 cups sugar
- 1 1/2 cups flour (after sifting)
- 1/3 teaspoon cream of tartar
- pinch of salt
- 1 lemon or orange (juice and rind for flavoring)

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately, add sugar, cream of tartar, salt, and flour. Flavor with lemon or orange juice and rind.
