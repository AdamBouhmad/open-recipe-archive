## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 can apples
- cinnamon
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Place canned apples with their juice or partly cooked fresh apples in baking pan
2. Sprinkle with cinnamon, sugar, and dot with butter
3. Make cake batter
4. pour over apples and bake half an hour
5. Serve with any desired sauce
