## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil or any melted shortening
- 2 cups raisins
- 3 cups flour
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- 1 heaping teaspoon salt
- 1 level teaspoon cloves
- 1 level teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 level teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, water, shortening and raisins together for five minutes
2. When cool add flour, salt, soda and spices, sifted together
3. Bake in sheet in moderate oven
