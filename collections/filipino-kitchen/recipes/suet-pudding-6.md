## Ingredients

- 1 cup suet, chopped
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup raisins seeded
- 2 cups graham flour
- 1 cup wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. Steam three hours
