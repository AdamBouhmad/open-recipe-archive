## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 large cup raisins (stoned and chopped)
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order named
2. Bake in moderate oven
