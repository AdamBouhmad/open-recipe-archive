## Ingredients

- 2 small chickens
- 1 cup rice
- ¾ pound fat pork
- 1 cup ripe tomatoes
- salt and pepper
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cut up chickens.
2. Cover with cold water and bring slowly to a boil.
3. Drain that water off and cover well with more.
4. When boiling, and the meat is getting tender, add washed rice and pork cut into small dice, and tomatoes pared and cut into pieces.
5. Add salt and pepper to taste and boil until the rice is well done.
6. This stew should be thick enough to dip, not to pour.
