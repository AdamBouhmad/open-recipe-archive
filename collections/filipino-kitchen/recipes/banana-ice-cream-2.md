## Ingredients

- 2 cans cream
- 5 large cups water
- 5 eggs
- Vanilla
- Bananas
- 4 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Boil flour in water
2. Beat together eggs and sugar
3. Mix with cream and when cool add vanilla and bananas cut fine
4. Freeze
