## Ingredients

- 3 lemons
- 3 cups sugar
- 3 oranges
- 3 quarts water
- 3 bananas
- Whites of 3 eggs
- Preserved cherries or pineapple

## Directions

1. Mix the juice of lemons and oranges with the fine banana
2. Put in freezer with water and sugar
3. Mix thoroughly
4. When half frozen beat the egg whites to stiff froth and stir into the sherbet
5. Finish freezing and serve with preserved cherries or pineapple
