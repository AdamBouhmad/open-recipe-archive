## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon marrow
- 3 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- part of raw egg

## Directions

1. Mix together cooked marrow, bread crumbs, parsley, salt and pepper
2. Add enough raw egg to this mixture to insure the balls not coming to pieces in cooking
3. Form into balls the size of filberts
4. Roll in raw egg and fine bread crumbs or flour
5. Simmer in soup a few minutes before serving
