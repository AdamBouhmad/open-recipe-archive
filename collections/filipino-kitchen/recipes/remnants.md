## Ingredients

- cold meat
- 1 egg
- cracker crumbs
- stock or gravy
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Take remnants of cold meat, grind or chop very fine, salt and pepper to taste.
2. Add one well beaten egg and one third as much cracker crumbs as chopped meat.
3. Add stock or gravy enough to moisten.
4. Steam in well buttered cups until thoroughly heated.
