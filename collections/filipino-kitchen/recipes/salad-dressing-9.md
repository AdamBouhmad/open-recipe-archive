## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 teaspoons oil
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon mixed mustard
- 2 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 3 teaspoons cream

## Directions

1. Boil eggs until hard; pound yolks to a smooth paste.
2. Add oil little by little, and all other ingredients, stirring until all are thoroughly incorporated.
