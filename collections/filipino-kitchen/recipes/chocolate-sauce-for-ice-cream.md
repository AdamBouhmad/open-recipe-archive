## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- Chopped nuts
- ⅛ cake chocolate
- Water

## Directions

1. Boil together sugar and chocolate with enough water to make a thick syrup
2. When cold, chopped nuts may be added
3. Pour over ice cream when serving
