## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon corn meal
- ½ cup scalding milk
- 2½ pints warm water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- pinch of soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir corn meal into scalded milk at night.
2. Set in warm place; in the morning take a pint of warm water, a pinch of soda, and make a stiff batter.
3. Stir in the mixture that has stood over night; beat well; set in a kettle of warm water at an even temperature.
4. It should be light in two hours.
5. Then add one and a half pints of warm water, salt and sufficient flour to work into loaves.
6. Knead until smooth; put into bread pans and set over warm water or in some warm place to rise.
7. Then bake.
