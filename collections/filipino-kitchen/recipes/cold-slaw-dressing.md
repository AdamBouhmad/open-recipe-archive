## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 cup vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat up eggs with sugar, add butter, mustard, pepper, and lastly vinegar.
2. Cook like a soft custard.
3. Some like to add a cup of cream; in that case use less vinegar and add cream when dressing is cold.
