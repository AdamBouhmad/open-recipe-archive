## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar (half brown)
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup molasses (black, full cup)
- 3 eggs (leave out 1 white)
- 3/4 cup sweet milk
- 3 rounding teaspoons baking powder
- 3/8 teaspoon soda
- 2 3/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/4 cup strong coffee (if wished)
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients
2. Bake
