## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- ½ cup warm water
- 1 pint boiling water

## Directions

1. Stir flour and butter together.
2. Gradually add the boiling milk, then chopped eggs.
3. Do not let sauce boil, but keep just under boiling point.
4. Pour a little over fish and serve rest separately.
