## Ingredients

- yolks 3 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- sugar
- vanilla
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and add milk sweetened to taste, and cook in a dish of hot water
2. When nearly cold add vanilla and a pinch of salt
3. Set on ice to cool
