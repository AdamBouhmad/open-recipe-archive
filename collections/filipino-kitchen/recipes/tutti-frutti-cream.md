## Ingredients

- plain unflavored ice cream
- minced fruits in syrup
- chopped walnut meats
- pineapples, bananas, oranges, peaches or berries

## Directions

1. Serve plain unflavored ice cream in deep glasses
2. Pour over each a combination of minced fruits in syrup
3. Top with a tablespoon of chopped walnut meats
