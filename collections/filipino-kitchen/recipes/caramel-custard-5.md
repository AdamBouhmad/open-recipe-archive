## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat eggs thoroughly
2. Add four tablespoons of sugar, milk, and vanilla
3. Make custard
4. Make a caramel of four tablespoons sugar browned
5. Have ready little tin moulds and put in each one teaspoon of hot caramel
6. Turn quickly in your fingers so as to coat the sides of the mould with caramel as evenly as possible
7. Fill the moulds about three-fourths full of the custard
8. Place them in a pan of hot water
9. Bake in the oven until the custard is set
10. Turn out of the moulds while warm and serve cold
