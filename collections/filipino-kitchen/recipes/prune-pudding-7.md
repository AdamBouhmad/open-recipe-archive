## Ingredients

- 1 dozen large prunes
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- whites 5 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon cream tarter
- pinch of salt
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take stewed prunes, run through a colander
2. Beat eggs and whip in powdered sugar, with salt and cream tarter
3. Bake twenty minutes in pan of hot water
4. Serve with whipped cream
