## Ingredients

- 3 pounds beef rump or topside
- 3 cups boiling water
- 1 small onion
- flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash and put beef into a pot with barely sufficient water to cover it.
2. Set over a slow fire and after it has been stewed an hour, salt and pepper.
3. Then stew slowly until tender, adding a little onion if liked.
4. Do not replenish the water at the last, but let it nearly boil away.
5. When tender, pour the gravy into a bowl; put a large lump of butter in the bottom of the pot; then dredge the meat with flour and return it to the pot to brown, turning it often to prevent burning.
6. Remove the fat from the gravy and pour it in with the meat.
7. Stir in a large spoonful of flour, wet with a little water.
8. Let it boil up 10 or 15 minutes and pour into a gravy dish.
9. Serve hot.
