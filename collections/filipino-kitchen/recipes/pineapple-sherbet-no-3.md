## Ingredients

- 1 quart sugar
- 1 quart shredded pineapple
- 1 quart hot water
- 4 lemons
- whites 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and hot water to syrup
2. Add pulp and juice of pineapple and juice of lemons
3. Add water enough to make a gallon freezer two-thirds full
4. When half frozen add the well beaten whites of eggs
5. Freeze with plenty of salt and ice
