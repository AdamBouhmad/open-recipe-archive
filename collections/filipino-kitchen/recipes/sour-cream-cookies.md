## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Sour the cream by adding one tablespoon vinegar to each cup of cream.
2. Mix soft; roll thin; sift granulated sugar over and roll gently in.
3. Cut and bake.
