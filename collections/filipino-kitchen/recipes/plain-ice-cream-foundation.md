## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 pint cream
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Scald the milk; mix the sugar, flour and eggs together and make a soft custard
2. When cold stir in the cream and freeze
