## Ingredients

- 3 bananas
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 3/4 cup flour
- salt
- sugar
- water
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Use the banana called "Saba" for frying
2. Cut bananas in slices the long way
3. Dip in a batter made of flour, milk and a little salt
4. Fry in hot fat
5. Pour over the fried bananas a syrup made of sugar and water
