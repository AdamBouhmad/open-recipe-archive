## Ingredients

- 1½ pounds veal
- 1¼ pounds pork
- 2 eggs
- ¾ cup cream
- 3 crackers
- salt to taste
- pinch of sage
- little onion

## Directions

1. Put veal and pork through food chopper.
2. Add eggs, cream, cracker crumbs, little onion, salt, and sage.
3. Mould into a loaf and bake.
