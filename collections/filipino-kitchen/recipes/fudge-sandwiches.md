## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ⅛ cup milk
- ¾ tablespoon butter
- 1 square chocolate
- thin buttered bread

## Directions

1. Make fudge as usual
2. Beat until almost stiff
3. Before it hardens spread on thin buttered bread for sandwiches
