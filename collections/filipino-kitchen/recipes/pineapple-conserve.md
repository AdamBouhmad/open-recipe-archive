## Ingredients

- 1 pint chopped pineapple
- 3 oranges, juice
- 1 orange rind, candied
- 1 teacup English walnuts, chopped

## Directions

1. Chop pineapple in meat chopper with medium knife.
2. Add sugar and orange juice.
3. Let simmer over a slow fire until the consistency of jam.
4. When nearly cold, add candied rind and nut meats, also passed through meat chopper.
