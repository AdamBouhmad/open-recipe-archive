## Ingredients

- 1 firm white cabbage
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons rich milk or cream
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Boil cabbage fifteen minutes, changing the water from the boiling tea-kettle
2. When tender set aside to cool
3. When cold, chop fine and add two beaten eggs, butter, pepper and salt, and rich milk or cream
4. Stir all well together
5. Bake in buttered pudding dish until brown
6. Serve very hot
