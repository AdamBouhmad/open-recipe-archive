## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick over the rice carefully; wash it in warm water, rubbing it between the hands and rinse in several waters; then let it remain in cold water until ready to be cooked.
2. Have saucepan of water, slightly salted; when boiling hard, sprinkle rice into water by degrees to keep particles separated.
3. Boil steadily for twenty minutes; take from fire; drain.
4. Place the pan, with lid off, on the back of the stove, where it is only moderately warm, to allow the rice to dry.
5. Each grain will be separate.
