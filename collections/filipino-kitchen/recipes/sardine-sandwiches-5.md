## Ingredients

- 2 cans sardines
- salt and pepper to taste
- tiny pieces of lettuce or few drops lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take sardines and throw into hot water, having first drained off the oil. A few minutes will free the sardines from grease.
2. Dry in a cloth; scrape off the skin and reduce to a paste, add salt, pepper and tiny pieces of lettuce, which adds to the flavor.
3. Spread between thin slices of bread.
4. Or chop sardines fine and squeeze over them a few drops of lemon juice. Spread on bread or cold biscuit.
