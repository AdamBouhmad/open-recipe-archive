## Ingredients

- 1 eggplant
- 3/4 cup nuts
- 3/4 cup bread crumbs
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 small onion
- pinch of parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the whole egg-plant into boiling water; boil twenty minutes or until tender.
2. Cut in two and scoop out the center, leaving a wall one-half inch thick.
3. Chop the center, mixing it with bread crumbs, nuts, salt, parsley and a little onion.
4. Stuff the eggplant and bake in a moderate oven for about an hour, basting a few times with butter.
