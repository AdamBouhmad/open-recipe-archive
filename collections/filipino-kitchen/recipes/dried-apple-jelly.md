## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilo dried apples
- 6 cups water
- 10 kalamansi
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash apples, soak in water until soft, cook, drain in jelly-bag.
2. To four cups of juice, use four cups of sugar and juice of kalamansi.
3. Make as any jelly.
