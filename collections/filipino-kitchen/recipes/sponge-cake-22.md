## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup hot milk
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs five minutes by the clock
2. Add sugar one tablespoon at a time and continue beating
3. Add the hot milk with the butter melted in it
4. Sift flour three times
5. Add baking powder at last sifting and use any flavor desired
6. Note: This batter may seem too thin, but if recipe is followed strictly, a delicious cake will be the result
