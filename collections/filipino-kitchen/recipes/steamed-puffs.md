## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- flour
- fruit
- 1 cup fruit juice
- 2 teaspoons corn starch
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Take sugar, eggs, butter, milk, baking powder and enough flour to make thick batter
2. Grease as many cups as you desire puffs
3. Fill each one about one quarter full
4. Add any desired fruit
5. Put in another spoonful batter
6. Steam forty-five minutes
7. Serve hot with any good pudding sauce or a sauce made from fruit juice
