## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- mayonnaise
- 1 green cucumber

## Directions

1. Drain oil from salmon, pick it apart, remove skin and bones.
2. Select a green, crisp cucumber; pare and cut in thin slices and soak in salt ice water one hour.
3. Drain dry and mix with salmon.
4. Serve with mayonnaise.
