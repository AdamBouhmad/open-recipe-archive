## Ingredients

- 1 pint grated pineapple juice
- 1 lemon
- 1 cup water
- 1½ cups sugar
- whites 2 eggs

## Directions

1. To juice and pulp of pineapple add lemon, water and sugar
2. Dissolve the sugar in water, add lemon juice, let it boil up and strain clear
3. When cold, stir in the pineapple and freeze
4. When partially frozen stir in the well whipped whites of two eggs
