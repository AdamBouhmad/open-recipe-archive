## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1 rounded teaspoon baking powder
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sweet cream
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup grated cocoanut (dry)
- grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Sift together flour and baking powder
2. Beat egg and sugar until light
3. Add grated cocoanut and a little lemon rind
4. Add alternately flour and cream
5. Drop in little pats on buttered pans, dusted with flour, allowing room to spread
6. Sprinkle a little grated cocoanut over each cake
7. Bake in a moderate oven
