## Ingredients

- 1 grape fruit
- 1 orange
- 1 lemon
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Slice the fruit very thin, and add three times as much water as fruit.
2. Let stand for twenty four hours.
3. Boil ten minutes.
4. Let stand again twenty four hours.
5. Then add sugar pound for pound (1 pint equals 1 pound).
6. Cook from one and a half to three hours.
