## Ingredients

- ⅔ cupful milk
- ½ cupful bread crumbs
- 1 cupful cooked ham
- ⅓ teaspoon dry mustard
- 1 raw egg
- 6 hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Put milk on the fire with bread crumbs until bread has absorbed milk.
2. Take from fire and add finely chopped ham, dry mustard and raw egg.
3. Put this mixture around each hard boiled egg, place in wire basket and fry.
4. Wrap in paraffine paper and take to picnic.
