## Ingredients

- California prunes
- cold water
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Take a quantity of California prunes, pour scalding water over them, then wash in cold water
2. Add cold water enough to cover them
3. Cover closely and cook slowly until tender
4. If rich syrup is desired uncover and boil briskly
5. Turn into a baking pan, sprinkle a little sugar over the top and bake a few minutes
6. Serve with cream
