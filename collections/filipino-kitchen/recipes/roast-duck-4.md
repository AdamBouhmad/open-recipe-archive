## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- butter
- flour
- 3 pints bread crumbs
- 2 onions, chopped
- 6 ounces butter or part butter and part salt pork
- 1 teaspoon sage
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- salt and water

## Directions

1. Pick, draw, clean thoroughly, and wipe dry
2. Cut the neck close to the back
3. Beat the breastbone flat with a rolling pin
4. Tie the wings and legs securely and stuff
5. Do not stuff very full
6. Sew up the openings firmly to keep the flavor in and the fat out
7. Place in a baking pan with a little water
8. Baste frequently with salt and water
9. Turn often that it may be nicely browned
10. When nearly done baste with butter and a little flour
11. Young ducks take from twenty-five to thirty minutes; full grown, an hour or more
12. Serve with jelly or tart sauce
