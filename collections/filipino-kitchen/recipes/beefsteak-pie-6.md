## Ingredients

- sirloin
- 1 onion
- mushrooms, or oysters, or kidneys
- few slices bacon
- salt and pepper
- cold water

## Directions

1. Chop the flank end of the sirloin rather coarsely.
2. Cover with cold water and simmer until the juice is extracted, adding more water if necessary.
3. Cut the tenderloin into thin slices crosswise.
4. Rub the slices with the cut side of an onion, if desired, and dust with a very little pepper.
5. Roll the slices and put a layer of them into a small buttered baking dish.
6. Add some tiny pieces of bacon and a few mushrooms.
7. Pour some of the broth around the meat.
8. Add pepper and salt; then another layer of beef rolls and the rest of the broth.
9. Cover with crust.
10. Bake 45 minutes.
