## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 cup boiling water
- 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs
2. Add sugar and beat fifteen minutes
3. Flavor with vanilla and the juice and grated rind of half a lemon, pinch of salt
4. Add two beaten whites, flour and baking powder just before the boiling water and flavoring
5. Bake in pan one foot square
6. Cover with boiled icing
7. Place half nuts on top and cut in squares
