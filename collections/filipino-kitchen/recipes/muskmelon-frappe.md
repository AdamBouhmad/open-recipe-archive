## Ingredients

- 1 small melon
- gelatine
- sugar to taste
- water

## Directions

1. Cut small melon in half
2. Remove the seeds, membrane and as much pulp as may be removed easily, cutting it into small pieces
3. Put seeds and membrane in sieve to drain and to this add sugar to taste, a little gelatine prepared in the usual way and a little water
4. Freeze and at serving time fill the melon shells, which should be ice cold, with alternate layers of the pulp and frappe
