## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1⅜ cups flour
- 4 apples or bananas
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. Add milk, flour, baking powder
3. Chop either apples or bananas
4. Add to the batter
5. The sugar may be added if desired
6. Fry in smoking hot lard
