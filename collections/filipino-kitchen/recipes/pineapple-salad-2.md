## Ingredients

- 1 pineapple
- 1 apple
- 3/4 cup mayonnaise
- 18 green grapes
- cream cheese
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut out inside of pineapple in small pieces, being careful not to break the shell, cut apple into small pieces.
2. Mix grapes, apple and pineapple with mayonnaise and replace in pineapple shell.
3. Serve on lettuce leaves and garnish with cream cheese balls size of marbles.
