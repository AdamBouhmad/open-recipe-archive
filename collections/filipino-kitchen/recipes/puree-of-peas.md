## Ingredients

- 1 can or pint of shelled peas
- 2 cups white stock (made of veal or chicken)
- 2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Proceed as with corn soup.
