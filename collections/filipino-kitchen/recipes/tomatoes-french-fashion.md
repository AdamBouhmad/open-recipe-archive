## Ingredients

- 6 small solid tomatoes
- 3/4 pint milk
- 1 rounding tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Scald and peel small solid tomatoes.
2. To each six, allow cream sauce made by rubbing together butter and flour.
3. Add milk, stir until boiling, season with salt and pepper.
4. Put a tablespoon of sauce in the bottom of a custard cup; put in a tomato and cover with another tablespoon sauce; dust top with bread crumbs.
5. Stand the cups in a pan of boiling water and bake in moderate oven for half an hour.
6. Serve in the cups.
