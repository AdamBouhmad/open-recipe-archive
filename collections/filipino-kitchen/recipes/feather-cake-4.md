## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add milk and yolks of eggs well beaten
3. Sift flour three times, add baking powder last sifting
4. Add flour and lastly, the well beaten whites
5. Bake in three layers, put together with any good frosting or jelly
