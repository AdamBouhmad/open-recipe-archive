## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 teaspoon mapleine
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Boil together until they begin to hair.
2. Cool a little.
3. Beat white of one egg stiff and add gradually to syrup.
4. Beat all together until ready to drop on waxed paper.
