## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. To the beaten egg add butter, sugar and salt, all beaten until light.
2. Then add milk, flour, and baking powder.
3. Drop on well greased patty pans and bake twenty minutes in a quick oven.
