## Ingredients

- 8 slices of bread
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 5 hard boiled eggs
- 1 teaspoon corn starch
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 saltspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Toast the bread and arrange on a platter.
2. Cut around the eggs so as to remove the yolks.
3. Butter the toast, on which lay the whites cut in rings, and upon this grate the yolks.
4. Have ready a cream sauce made with milk, butter, corn starch (dissolved in a little milk) and salt.
5. Bring this to a boil.
6. Pour over the prepared toast and sprinkle with a little pepper.
7. Serve.
