## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- Sugar
- 1 large pineapple

## Directions

1. Pare and grate the pineapple
2. Place in a deep dish and sprinkle with sugar
3. Cover and let stand three hours
4. Then press through a sieve
5. Stir into the cream and beat well
6. Freeze
