## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup cream
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup water
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 cups sifted flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Beat eggs separately
3. Add the yolks, then milk and flour, baking powder sifted in flour, and whites of eggs
4. Bake in one pan
