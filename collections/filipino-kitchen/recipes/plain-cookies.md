## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 orange
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs well beaten, juice of orange, baking powder and enough flour to roll out nicely
3. Bake in moderate oven
