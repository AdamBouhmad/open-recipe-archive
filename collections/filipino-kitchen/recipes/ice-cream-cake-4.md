## Ingredients

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 3 egg whites
- 1 cup prepared flour
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients for filling well and place on ice
2. For cake cream butter and sugar, add flour and well beaten whites and lemon juice
3. Bake twenty minutes in two layers
