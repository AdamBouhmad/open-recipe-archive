## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 slice onion
- some celery
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 saltspoon pepper
- 1 saltspoon soda

## Directions

1. Put tomato, onion and celery into a sauce pan.
2. Cover.
3. Bring to a boiling point.
4. Add flour and butter rubbed together.
5. Stir carefully until mixture boils and thickens.
6. Add sugar, salt, pepper and soda.
7. Press through a sieve.
8. Reheat and serve.
