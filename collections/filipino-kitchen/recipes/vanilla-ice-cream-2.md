## Ingredients

- 1 can cream
- 4 eggs
- 2 cans hot water
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Add hot water to cream, place in fire and when it boils, add cornstarch dissolved in a little water; after stirring briskly let it boil; then add the yolks of eggs, beaten well with the sugar
2. Place to cool and flavor with vanilla
