## Ingredients

- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3 teaspoons soda (heaping)
- 3 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 1 teaspoon cloves

## Directions

1. Cream butter, add well beaten eggs, molasses and spices
2. Dissolve soda in water and add
3. Stir in flour
4. Bake in one pan
