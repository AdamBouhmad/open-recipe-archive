## Ingredients

- chops of pork, mutton or veal
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 4 cups water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take any kind of chop; put butter in skillet, when hot lay in chops
2. When well browned place in stew kettle
3. prepare it with a sauce made from three tablespoons melted butter, flour, salt and pepper to taste, and warm water
4. cook this to a boiling point, stirring all the time
5. Turn over chops and cook slowly till tender
