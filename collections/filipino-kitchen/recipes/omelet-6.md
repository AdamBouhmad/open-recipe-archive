## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat separately yolks and whites of required number of eggs.
2. To the yolks add salt and pepper and one tablespoon of milk for each egg.
3. Mix well and add stiffly beaten whites.
4. Mix well and cook in hot well-buttered frying pan.
5. When brown, fold and serve.
