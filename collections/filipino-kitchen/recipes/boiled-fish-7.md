## Ingredients

- fish
- vinegar
- salt
- parsley
- sliced beets
- lemon
- sliced boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Put fish into cold water and set on the fire to cook gently, or the outside will break before the inner part is done
2. When boiling fish add a little vinegar and salt to the water, to season and prevent the nutriment from being drawn out
3. Garnish with parsley, sliced beets, lemon, or sliced boiled eggs
