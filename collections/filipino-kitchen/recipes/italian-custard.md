## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- sugar
- currant jelly
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs to a cream, and while beating add sugar and lemon juice to taste.
2. Beat until quite thick.
3. Pour into glass cups.
4. Beat the whites to a stiff froth, whip in enough currant jelly to give it color.
5. Heap the glasses with this and place on ice to chill.
