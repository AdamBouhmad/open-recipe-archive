## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 9 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
2. Add milk, eggs well beaten, and butter.
3. Bake in shallow buttered pan in hot oven twenty minutes.
