## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup rice
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare chicken for frying, brown in butter.
2. Cover with boiling water and simmer till tender.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Add to the curry made from Indian Curry recipe.
5. Serve with boiled rice.
