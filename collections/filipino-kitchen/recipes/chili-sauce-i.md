## Ingredients

- 8 quarts ripe tomatoes
- ⅔ cup salt
- ¾ cup celery
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1⅜ tablespoons allspice
- 1 teaspoon mace
- 1 tablespoon pepper
- 4 red peppers
- 3 large onions

## Directions

1. Peel and cut up tomatoes
2. Chop the celery, red peppers and onions fine
3. Mix all the ingredients together in the fireless cooker kettle and allow it to boil twenty minutes and place in the cooker from eight to twenty four hours
4. Remove from the cooker, bring again to a brisk boil and seal in glass bottles or earthenware jars with wax or paraffin
