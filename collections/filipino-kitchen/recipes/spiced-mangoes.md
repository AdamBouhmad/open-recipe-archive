## Ingredients

- 5 cups mangoes
- 4 cups sugar
- 2 cups vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon cloves

## Directions

1. Tie cinnamon and cloves in a thin cotton cloth.
2. Boil them in vinegar and sugar until it becomes a syrup.
3. Peel the mangoes and cut the meat from the seeds in lengthwise strips.
4. Drop into syrup and boil ten minutes.
5. Place in cooker two hours.
6. Then put in cans.
