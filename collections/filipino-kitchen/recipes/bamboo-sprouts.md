## Ingredients

- bamboo sprouts from a few inches to a foot high
- water or coconut milk
- bacon or dry meat (optional)
- butter, salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the outside of the bamboo sprouts
2. Take the inside and scrape very fine or cut into very thin slices
3. Put into boiling water or coconut milk and boil thirty or forty minutes
4. If liked, bacon or any dry meat can be boiled with it, or it can be served with butter, salt and pepper
