## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound victory steak
- 1 green mongo
- 1 small onion
- 3 small green peppers
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 cup cream
- 3/4 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon curry powder

## Directions

1. Take the steak and roll in balls size of hickory nuts.
2. Pour sufficient hot water over to cover and simmer for half hour.
3. Slice onion fine, fry in butter till rich brown.
4. Take curry powder and flour, mix and put into hot butter.
5. Take the meat balls from liquid and fry in curry for a few minutes, then add liquid.
6. A few slices of green mongo stewed in the curry is nice.
7. If you like a hotter curry, two or three small green peppers may be added.
8. After all liquid let simmer few minutes.
9. A half cup cream added just before removing from fire makes a richer curry.
