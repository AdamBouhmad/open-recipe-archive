## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 18 sticks macaroni
- 2 cans mushrooms
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove meat from bones
2. Chop fine
3. Boil the bones in water enough to cover them, for half an hour
4. Add to the chopped chicken the eggs well beaten and sufficient of the stock to make a smooth paste
5. Season with salt and pepper, add butter
6. Boil macaroni until tender, being careful not to break
7. Line a buttered porcelain bowl with the macaroni and fill with mixture
8. Set in saucepan of boiling water and cook for half hour, or until chicken is tender
9. Reverse in circular chop dish, surrounded with mushrooms and serve hot
