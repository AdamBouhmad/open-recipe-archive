## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- 5 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 1/2 cups sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 3/4 teaspoon lemon extract

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar; beat yolks and whites of eggs separately.
2. All the ingredients must be fresh and of the best.
3. Keep mixture cool until placed in oven.
