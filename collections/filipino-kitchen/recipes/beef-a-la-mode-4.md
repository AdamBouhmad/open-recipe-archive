## Ingredients

- 4 pounds round of beef
- 3/4 pound fat salt pork
- 6 small fresh carrots
- 6 peppercorns
- 3/4 pound suet
- 2 bay leaves
- 1/4 teaspoon mace
- 2 small onions
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Have butcher skewer beef and tie the roll with stout twine.
2. Make five or six incisions and thread them with salt pork, carrots, peppercorns, mace, onions and bay leaves.
3. Pour over all two tablespoons cider vinegar.
4. Cover the vessel and let stand all night in a cool place.
5. By morning the seasoning will have permeated the meat.
6. Then cook as a pot roast.
7. Pour off the juice that has drawn during the night and start roasting with small piece of suet.
8. When this is sizzling hot, put in meat with more suet, and after the meat has browned on both sides, add little by little the juice.
9. Simmer slowly until the meat is tender and let it cool in remaining juice.
10. Serve cold with hominy boiled very dry.
