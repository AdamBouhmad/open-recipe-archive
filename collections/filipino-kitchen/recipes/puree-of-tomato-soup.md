## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1½ teaspoons butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup canned cream
- 1⅜ tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Boil tomatoes twenty minutes.
2. Strain.
3. Mix together flour and butter.
4. While tomatoes are boiling stir in flour and butter.
5. Add soda and cream, diluted with half water, salt and sugar.
6. Serve almost immediately.
