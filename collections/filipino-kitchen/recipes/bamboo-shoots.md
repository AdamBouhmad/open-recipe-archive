## Ingredients

- fresh crisp shoot
- cold water

## Directions

1. Select a fresh crisp shoot
2. Cut into thin slices, then into small pieces
3. Soak in cold water four hours, changing the water twice
4. Put into cold water and allow it to come to a boil
5. Pour off the water and boil for ten minutes
6. Change the water again and boil until tender
7. Unless the water is changed many times, the bamboo is bitter
