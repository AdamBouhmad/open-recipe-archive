## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 pound dates, chopped
- 1 pound English walnuts, chopped
- 1 teaspoon soda dissolved in 3 tablespoons hot water

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add eggs
3. Add flour
4. Add dates and walnuts
5. Dissolve soda in hot water and add
6. Bake
