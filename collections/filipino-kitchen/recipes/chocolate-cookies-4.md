## Ingredients

- 1 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 squares chocolate
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the egg
2. Add the sugar and melted or soft shortening
3. Cream thoroughly
4. Add the milk
5. Sift flour and baking powder, add slowly
6. Stir in the melted chocolate, walnuts and vanilla
7. Drop for small cookies
