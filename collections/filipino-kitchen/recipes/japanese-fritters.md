## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- stale bread or cake
- powdered sugar or jelly
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Beat eggs
2. Add milk
3. Cut stale bread into fingers about one inch square and three inches long, free from crust
4. Put these slices into the milk and egg, and let soak until they have absorbed the milk
5. Drain carefully
6. Roll in bread crumbs
7. Place in wire basket and fry in hot fat
8. Serve with powdered sugar or with sauce or jelly
9. Stale cake can be used in the same way
