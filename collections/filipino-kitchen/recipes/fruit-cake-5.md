## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 4 pounds raisins (whole)
- 4 pounds currants
- 1 pound citron
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 1 pint molasses
- 1 pint brandy
- 1 pint sherry
- 1 bottle vanilla
- 1 tablespoon mace
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- 3 nutmegs
- 1 pound flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar, add spices and yolks of eggs, well beaten, and molasses
2. Stir in flour and whites of eggs, well beaten, alternately
3. Just before adding fruit and brandy, take out enough batter to cover top of cakes
4. Use extra flour to flour fruit thoroughly, before adding to batter
5. Add brandy and sherry and bake in two pans for three hours, in moderate oven
