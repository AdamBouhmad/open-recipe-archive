## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 small solid tomatoes
- 6 tablespoon cream sauce
- 6 slices toast

## Directions

1. Peel small solid tomatoes and scoop out centers.
2. Break an egg into each tomato; stand them in a baking dish, and bake slowly ten or fifteen minutes until the eggs are set.
3. Add a tablespoon of cream sauce to each and serve on slices of toast.
