## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken
- 1/2 pint pickles
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 pint green celery or white cabbage
- 1 teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Boil chicken in salt water; when cold, skin, take from bones and chop fine.
2. Hard-boil eggs, cut celery in small pieces (tender white cabbage, shredded, will answer if no celery) chop pickles, add mustard and pepper.
3. Mix thoroughly.
