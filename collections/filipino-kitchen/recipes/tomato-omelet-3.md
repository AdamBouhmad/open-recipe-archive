## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup chopped tomato
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt to taste
- dash of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Beat eggs without separating until well mixed.
2. Add tomato, salt and cayenne.
3. Put butter into omelet pan; when hot add egg mixture; shake until set; fold and turn the omelet out on a platter.
