## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt and pepper
- stuffing
- lard or butter
- giblets

## Directions

1. Stuff the breast first, but not too full, or it will burst in cooking
2. The body should be stuffed rather fuller than the breast
3. Sew the openings with strong thread
4. Rub thoroughly with salt and pepper, then lard it or butter it over the breast
5. Place in a hot oven at first, then graduate to a moderate heat until the turkey is done, allowing about twenty minutes for each pound and twenty minutes additional
6. Boil the giblets until tender in a separate vessel
7. Chop fine and add to gravy
