## Ingredients

- green mangos
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare green mangos and slice from the seed.
2. Boil in plenty of water for a few minutes; pour off the water and boil again, being careful that the fruit is not broken by boiling too long and hard.
3. After the last parboiling add a little water and sugar sufficient to sweeten.
