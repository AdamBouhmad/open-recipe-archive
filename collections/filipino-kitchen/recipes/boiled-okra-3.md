## Ingredients

- young tender okra pods
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put young tender pods in salted boiling water in granite, porcelain or tin-lined pan (iron will discolor it).
2. Boil fifteen minutes.
3. Remove stems and serve with butter, pepper, salt and vinegar if preferred.
