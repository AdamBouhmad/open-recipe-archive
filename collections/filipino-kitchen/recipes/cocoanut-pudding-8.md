## Ingredients

- 1 cocoanut
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 cup cocoanut milk
- 3/4 cup sugar
- yolks 3 eggs
- whites 2 eggs
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Fresh cocoanut, grated, cocoanut milk mixed with it to make juice.
2. Add beaten egg yolks, sugar and vanilla.
3. Mix and bake ten or fifteen minutes.
4. Boil sugar and a little water until it threads, add to the beaten whites of eggs and spread over pudding.
5. Put in oven and brown.
6. Make in morning for evening dinner.
