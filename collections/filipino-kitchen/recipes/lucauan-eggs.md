## Ingredients

- 5 hard boiled eggs
- 1 1/2 cups white sauce
- 1 cup cooked macaroni
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- salt and pepper
- onion juice
- anchovy essence
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut eggs in eighths lengthwise.
2. Add cooked macaroni, cheese, and white sauce seasoned with salt, pepper, onion juice and anchovy essence.
3. Turn into a buttered baking dish.
4. Cover with buttered crumbs.
5. Set in the oven long enough to bake crumbs.
