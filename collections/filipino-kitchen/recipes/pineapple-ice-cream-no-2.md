## Ingredients

- 1 ripe pineapple
- juice 1 lemon
- ½ cup to pint of fruit

## Directions

1. Pare a fresh, ripe pineapple, remove the eyes and core, then chop fine and sprinkle with half a cup of sugar to a pint of fruit
2. Let it stand for half an hour, then press through a strainer
3. Add lemon juice and more sugar if needed
4. Mix with half frozen ice cream (plain) and finish freezing
