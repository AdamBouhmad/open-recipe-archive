## Ingredients

- 6 crabs
- bread crumbs
- 3 hard boiled eggs
- juice of 1 lemon
- butter, size of walnut
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Pick the meat from boiled crabs and cut into fine bits.
2. Add one-third as much bread crumbs, chopped eggs, the juice of one small lemon, salt and pepper, and butter the size of a walnut.
3. Clean the shells well and fill with the mixture.
4. Place bits of butter over the top and brown in the oven.
