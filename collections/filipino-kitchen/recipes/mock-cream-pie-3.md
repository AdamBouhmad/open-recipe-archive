## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons flour
- pinch salt
- small piece butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat sugar, flour and egg yolks together.
2. After the milk has come to a boil add the mixture, salt and butter.
3. After it has cooled, place in baked crust and sprinkle over it a little nutmeg.
4. Place meringue over top and brown in oven.
