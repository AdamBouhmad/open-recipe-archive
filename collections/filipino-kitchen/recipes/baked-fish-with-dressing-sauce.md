## Ingredients

- fish
- 4 hard boiled eggs, chopped fine
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- pinch of parsley
- chopped onion
- 4 eggs, hard boiled
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Clean and dry well, and fill with dressing
2. Tie well with cord to keep the dressing in
3. Put in baking pan with a little water; keep moist while cooking by basting
4. When done put on a platter and clip the string
5. Make a sauce of eggs, vinegar, butter, pepper and salt
6. Let it cook until thick and then pour over the fish
