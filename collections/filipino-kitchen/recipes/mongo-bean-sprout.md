## Ingredients

- 1 quart mongo bean sprout
- 1 onion
- french dressing

## Directions

1. Cook mongo bean sprout in salt water only a few minutes.
2. Pour off water and set to cool.
3. When ready to serve, slice onion over top and serve with French dressing.
