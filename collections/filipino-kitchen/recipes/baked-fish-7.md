## Ingredients

- fish
- rolled cracker or stale bread crumbs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- sage

## Directions

1. Fill the fish with a nicely prepared stuffing of rolled cracker or stale bread crumbs, seasoned with butter, pepper, salt, sage, etc.
2. Sew up
3. Bake fish slowly, basting often with butter and water
