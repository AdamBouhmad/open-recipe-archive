## Ingredients

- 1 cup tapioca
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 quart water
- 1 pint canned peaches

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca in cold water two hours
2. Add sugar
3. Put peaches in a two quart pudding dish pour the tapioca over, cover securely and bake forty minutes
