## Ingredients

- 1 large eggplant
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 3 large ripe tomatoes
- 3/4 cup cooked rice
- 1 beaten egg
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Pare the eggplant, cut into slices and steam until soft
2. Scald and peel tomatoes, press them through a sieve
3. Mix all the ingredients together, season and bake in a well greased dish
