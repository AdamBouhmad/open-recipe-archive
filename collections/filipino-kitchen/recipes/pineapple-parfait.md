## Ingredients

- ⅓ box gelatine
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 cups pineapple juice and pulp
- 2 cups cream
- whites 4 eggs
- Sliced pineapple, whipped cream and maraschino cherries

## Directions

1. Soften gelatine in a little cold water, then dissolve over hot water
2. Mix sugar and pineapple and add dissolved gelatine; blend thoroughly; then fold in the beaten whites of eggs
3. Beat until mixture begins to thicken, then add cream beaten stiff
4. Turn into a mould and pack in ice
5. When ready to serve, unmold and garnish with slices of pineapple, whipped cream and cherries
