## Ingredients

- 1 cup apples
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 cup celery
- mayonnaise
- perfect red apples
- crisp bleached celery tops

## Directions

1. Cut apples, celery and nuts in small pieces.
2. Blend lightly with mayonnaise.
3. Take hollowed out shells of perfect red apples and fill with mixture.
4. Place a teaspoon of mayonnaise on top of each and serve with a border of crisp, bleached celery tops.
