## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup grated coconut
- 1/2 cup boiling water
- 2 cups flour
- 1 orange

## Directions

1. Beat together until thick and light colored, the yolks of five eggs, whites of three, and two cups of sugar
2. Add the grated rind and juice of orange
3. Then, in succession, boiling water and stiffly beaten whites of three eggs, flour mixed with baking powder, and coconut
4. Make a boiled icing of two whites of eggs, a heaping cup sugar, half cup water and juice of one orange
5. Bake in sheet about an inch thick, in moderate oven
6. When cool, spread with jelly and cover with icing
