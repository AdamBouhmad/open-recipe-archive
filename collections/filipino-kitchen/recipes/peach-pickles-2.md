## Ingredients

- peaches
- 3 pints sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves

## Directions

1. Wash peaches well, let soak over night; place over a slow fire and cook until tender.
2. Have a syrup as for crab apple pickles, and place the peaches in the syrup, let them come to a boil and bottle or put in jars.
3. If syrup is not sweet enough, add sugar to taste.
