## Ingredients

- cabbage
- cream sauce
- bread blocks
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop any bits of cabbage that are not used, or use left-over stewed cabbage
2. Make a cream sauce and mix with the cabbage
3. Put a layer of bread blocks in the bottom of a baking dish, then a layer of cream cabbage
4. Repeat, having the top layer bread
5. Place bits of butter over the top
6. Bake in a quick oven for twenty minutes
