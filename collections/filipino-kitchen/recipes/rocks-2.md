## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1 scant cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 level teaspoon soda
- 1/4 cup hot water
- 1 cup nuts

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and butter, add eggs, flour, soda dissolved in hot water, raisins and nuts; flavor.
2. Drop off the spoon onto buttered tins.
