## Ingredients

- yolks of two eggs
- olive oil
- vinegar or lemon juice
- salt
- black pepper
- cayenne pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs constantly; add olive oil drop by drop, until enough to stand alone.
2. As the mixture becomes thick drop in slowly a little vinegar or lemon juice; thicken again with oil.
3. So proceed until the required amount of dressing is obtained.
4. Season to taste with salt, black and cayenne pepper and mustard.
