## Ingredients

- 2 cans tomatoes
- 4 large onions
- 6 green peppers
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 2 cups vinegar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- ⅜ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup mustard seeds

## Directions

1. Cook slowly two and one half hours
