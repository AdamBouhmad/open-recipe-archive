## Ingredients

- 1 cup hot milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 tablespoon flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup water in which bamboo was boiled

## Directions

1. Boil until thick milk, butter, flour, salt and pepper, and one cup of water in which the bamboo was boiled
2. Beat until smooth, then pour over the bamboo and serve hot
