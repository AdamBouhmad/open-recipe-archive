## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups sweet milk
- salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon butter or lard
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and milk with sufficient flour to make a moderately stiff batter.
2. Add baking powder, melted butter or lard, and salt.
3. Bake quickly.
