## Ingredients

- Juice of 2 lemons
- 2 dl Rhine wine (or white currant wine)
- 4 eggs
- 75 g sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 2 whole eggs with 2 egg yolks and the sugar until it is completely finely ground.
2. Add the lemon juice and the wine and let the custard bind au bain-marie while beating vigorously, but ensure that it does not boil, as the risk of curdling is then great.
3. Let the custard cool and garnish with the stiffly beaten egg white, through which some sugar has been stirred.
4. Serve with biscuits.
