## Ingredients

- Savoy cabbage
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into quarters and let it cook almost done
2. Drain well and chop finely
3. Add nutmeg, a little pepper, salt and milk
4. Let it stew for half an hour
