## Ingredients

- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 1 dl maraschino
- 50 g sugar
- 10 g gelatine
- some green candied fruits

## Directions

1. Soak the sheet gelatine 10 to 15 minutes in cold water, then dissolve it with as little boiling water as possible.
2. Add the sugar, dissolve it completely and let this mass cool somewhat.
3. Beat the cream and the egg whites stiff, add the maraschino, the fruits cut in pieces and the cooled mass.
4. Stir from time to time until the mass begins to become jelly-like, then pour it into a pudding mold brushed with unbeaten egg white and let it become cold and firm.
5. Turn the pudding out onto a damp plate and serve without sauce.
