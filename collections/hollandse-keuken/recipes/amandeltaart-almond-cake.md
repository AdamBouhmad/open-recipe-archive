## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 150 g almonds
- 150 g sugar
- 50 g potato starch or cornstarch
- 25 g flour
- salt
- jam

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs with a pinch of salt and the sugar in a bain-marie until thick, white and fluffy (approximately 15 minutes).
2. Add the potato starch or cornstarch and flour through a sieve, add most of the ground almonds last and bake the cake in a greased and dusted tart pan in a fairly warm oven until light brown and done (approximately 3/4 to 1 hour).
3. Cover with a buttered piece of paper if needed, check if it is done by pricking it with a knitting needle, which should come out dry.
4. Turn it out onto a cake rack, let it cool, spread the top and sides with some jam and garnish the cake with the reserved ground almonds.
