## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- some salt
- piece of mace
- small carrots
- sprig of parsley
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 4 dl bouillon
- some lemon juice
- some salt

## Directions

1. Cut the legs of the rabbit into 2 pieces, divide the body into 5 or 6 pieces, wash them and cook them in salted water with herbs until done (1 hour).
2. Heat butter or margarine and flour, stirring, bring to the boil, add the bouillon in small amounts while stirring, in which the pieces were cooked, and let the sauce cook through while stirring (10 minutes).
3. Stew the meat in it for another 20 minutes and add lemon juice to taste.
