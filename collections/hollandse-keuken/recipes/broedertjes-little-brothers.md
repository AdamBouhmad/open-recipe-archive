## Ingredients

- 2 and a half ounces flour
- 2 eggs
- Warm sweet milk
- Raisins
- Some almond paste
- Currants
- Candied peel
- Grated lemon zest
- Just over 1 ounce butter
- A piece of yeast
- Butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Make a batter, not too thin, with flour, eggs, warm sweet milk, raisins, almond paste, currants, candied peel, lemon zest, butter and yeast
2. Fry in a broedertje pan with butter
