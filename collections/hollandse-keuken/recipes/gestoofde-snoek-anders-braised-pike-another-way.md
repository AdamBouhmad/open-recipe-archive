## Ingredients

- one pike
- wine
- water
- butter
- one spoon grated North Holland cheese
- capers
- pepper
- grated white bread
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the pike first and place it in a dish with some wine and as much water as needed
2. Add butter, one spoon grated North Holland cheese, capers, pepper, grated white bread and lemon juice
3. Let it braise well
