## Ingredients

- cooked salmon
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- wine vinegar
- consommé
- salad oil
- tarragon
- pimpernel
- head lettuce
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- gherkins
- anchovies

## Directions

1. Once the salmon is cooked, place the pieces in a sauce of oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and fine herbs.
2. Make a sauce of four tablespoons wine vinegar, two tablespoons consommé, and ten tablespoons salad oil, salt, pepper, chopped tarragon, and pimpernel.
3. Place the pieces in this sauce and arrange them on a platter.
4. Pour the sauce over them.
5. Arrange head lettuce cut into four parts, and on each piece place a quarter egg with capers, gherkins, and anchovies.
