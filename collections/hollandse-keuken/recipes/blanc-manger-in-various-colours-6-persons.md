## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 liter milk
- 20 g white gelatin or 17 1/2 g powdered gelatin
- 80-100 g sugar
- 150 g almond macaroons (10-12 pieces)
- 1/2 drop carmine
- some coffee extract

## Directions

1. Prepare the pudding as indicated for blanc-manger with almond macaroons
2. Measure the content of the rinsed pudding mold and divide the pudding mixture into 3 parts, each of which will give an equally thick layer in the mold
3. Pour 1/3 of the mass in, let this mass become cold and stiff
4. Pour then a third part of the mixture again, which is colored rose, but make sure that it is so lukewarm that the lower part cannot melt and that the 2 layers stick together
5. Let this become stiff
6. Add to the last part some coffee extract and another half sheet of dissolved gelatin and pour this, also lukewarm, onto the congealed second part
7. Let the pudding become cold and stiff, turn it out onto a damp dish and serve without sauce
8. Note: If one wishes to color the pudding with red gelatin instead of carmine, it is best to dissolve the gelatin in 3 parts separately and in one part instead of 3 1/2 sheets of white gelatin: 1 sheet of red and 2 sheets of white gelatin. In the last part more gelatin must be dissolved, as otherwise it would be too diluted by the coffee extract and thus not become stiff
