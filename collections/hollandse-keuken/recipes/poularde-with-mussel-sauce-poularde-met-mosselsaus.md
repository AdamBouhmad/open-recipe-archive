## Ingredients

- poularde
- mussels
- parsley
- small onions
- butter
- bouillon
- pepper
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the poularde
2. Remove the beard from the mussels and wash them often
3. Put them in a pan and bring to a good fire, but do not let them boil
4. Then place them dry in another pan with parsley, small onions and some butter, a little bouillon, pepper and salt
5. Then mix 3 eggs with the mussels and let them become very hot, but not boil
6. Bring the poularde hot to the plate and cover it with the sauce
