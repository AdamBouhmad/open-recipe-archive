## Ingredients

- cleaned cod
- coarse salt
- vinegar
- mustard sauce or melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the well cleaned cod into pieces and wash it well
2. Place it in water with a good amount of coarse salt that has been brought to a boil
3. Add a splash of vinegar and constantly skim
4. Let it cook fresh for a few minutes
5. Serve it with a mustard sauce or with melted butter
6. Cook the liver separately with the milt or roe
7. The liver can also be rolled in flour after being briefly boiled and cooled, browned and cooked in butter, and some mustard sauce with vinegar poured over it
