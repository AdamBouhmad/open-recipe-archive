## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 200 g macaroni
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil.
2. Break the macaroni into pieces, wash it on a sieve until the water runs completely clear and sprinkle it stirring into the boiling milk.
3. Keep stirring until the milk boils again and let the pudding cook on very low heat, stirring occasionally, until cooked and thickened (approximately 3/4 hour).
