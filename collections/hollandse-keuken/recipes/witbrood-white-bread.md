## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 10 g yeast
- 7.5 g salt
- 2.5 dl milk

## Directions

1. Put the flour in a deep bowl, make a well in the middle, crumble the yeast into it and mix with about half of the lukewarm milk.
2. Stir in enough of the surrounding flour to form a thick dough (so-called starter).
3. Let this rise covered on a lukewarm spot for 20 to 30 minutes.
4. Then add the remaining milk and salt and knead this dough until it leaves the hands.
5. Let it rise covered on a lukewarm spot (about 1 hour).
6. Shape it so that a greased bread pan is filled about 2/3.
7. Let the dough rise here for another 1/2 hour and bake the bread in a hot oven, placed approximately in the middle, brown and done (1/2 to 3/4 hour).
8. Brush the top as soon as it becomes brown with water to get a shiny crust.
9. Tap on the bread to hear if it is done and turn it out of the pan.
10. Note: Replace the milk with water if desired.
