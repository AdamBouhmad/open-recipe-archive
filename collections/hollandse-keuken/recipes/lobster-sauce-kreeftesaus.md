## Ingredients

- Lobster butter
- Flour
- Clear meat juice
- Pepper
- Mace
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Stir a couple of spoons flour through lobster butter
2. Let it brown briefly
3. Add pure meat juice, a little pepper, mace, and the juice of a lemon
4. Let it cook through
