## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- Salt
- Butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Salt a piece of beef well and let it stand for 2 to 3 days
2. Brown it in an earthenware pan with some butter on the fire, turning it constantly so it does not burn
3. The fire must be fierce, and if you see the meat becomes too dry, add water and let it braise quickly
4. Test with a knife to see if it is done and serve it with its own jus poured over
