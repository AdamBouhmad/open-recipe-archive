## Ingredients

- 100 to 150 g rice flour
- 1/2 l milk
- 1/4 vanilla pod
- 2 eggs
- some marmalade
- currants
- candied orange peel
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Cook the porridge in the same way as semolina porridge
2. Remove the vanilla and mix the dough off the heat with the egg yolks
3. Shape into pears, placing some marmalade in the middle of each
4. Coat the pears double (use the egg whites for this), stick a piece of candied orange peel at the point and a currant (or raisin) at the other end
5. Fry the pears brown in hot frying fat
6. Let them drain well on porous paper, sprinkle with powdered sugar or serve with a sauce of marmalade mixed with a little hot water
