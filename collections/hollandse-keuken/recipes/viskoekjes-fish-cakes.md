## Ingredients

- 200 g fish scraps (without bones or skin)
- 30 g old bread
- 1/2 egg
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- fine parsley
- 30 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Chop the leftover fish, after carefully removing the small bones and skins, finely.
2. Mix it with the bread, which has been soaked in water or milk and squeezed between two spoons, the egg and the seasonings.
3. Form 4 flat cakes from it and fry them in the butter or margarine on both sides until light brown.
