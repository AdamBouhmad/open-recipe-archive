## Ingredients

- Can of lobster
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg white
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Some slices of bread soaked in milk
- Biscuit crumbs or breadcrumbs
- Butter or margarine, salad oil, beef fat or frying fat

## Directions

1. Take the meat from the lobster and chop it
2. Mix it well with the spices, the soaked bread and egg yolk
3. Make small frikadels from this, which are dipped in loosely beaten egg white thinned with water
4. Roll them in biscuit crumbs or breadcrumbs and fry them in one of the aforementioned fats until light brown
