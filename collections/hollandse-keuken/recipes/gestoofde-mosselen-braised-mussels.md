## Ingredients

- mussels
- butter or margarine
- flour
- mussel water
- a splash of milk

## Directions

1. Cook the mussels as indicated in recipe 195.
2. Make a sauce from butter, flour, mussel water and a splash of milk according to recipe 351.
3. Braise the mussels, which have been removed from their shells, in this sauce.
