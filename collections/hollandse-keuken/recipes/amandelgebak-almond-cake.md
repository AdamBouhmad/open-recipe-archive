## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces coarsely ground almonds
- 6 eggs, of which 4 with the white
- 1 ounce bread sugar
- Lemon peel grated thinly
- Butter and rusks for the form

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar with the eggs well first, then stir the almonds through it well
2. Beat together very quickly so that it rises in the pot
3. Put it in the form, which has first been greased with butter and sprinkled with rusks
4. The lid, which one puts on top, must first be warm
5. Let it bake slowly
6. Watch it from time to time
