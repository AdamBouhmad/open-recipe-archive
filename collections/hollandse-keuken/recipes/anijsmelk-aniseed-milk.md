## Ingredients

- 2 l milk
- 80 g sugar
- 12 1/2 g aniseed
- 20 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. Tie the aniseed in a cheesecloth, put it in the milk and prepare it as indicated for vanilla milk (recipe 900).
