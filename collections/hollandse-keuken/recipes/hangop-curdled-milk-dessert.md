## Ingredients

- 4 l buttermilk
- 4 large rusks
- brown sugar
- some milk or cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Place a cloth rinsed in clean water in a colander, set it in a deep bowl and pour the buttermilk into it
2. Let the watery part of the buttermilk drain off
3. From time to time scoop the thick part out of the cloth and continue until the hangop is completely drained
4. Beat the hangop smooth and stir, if the hangop is too sour, some milk or cream through it
5. Serve it with brown sugar and rusks or with cornflakes
