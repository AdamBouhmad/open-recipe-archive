## Ingredients

- ½ l syrup
- 9 spoons sugar
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar and butter in the heated syrup and cook everything together until it reaches the thickness of a breast candy syrup.
2. Pour the mass onto a greased stone plate.
3. As soon as it begins to stiffen, form balls by rolling the mass into a roll, pulling out a thick ribbon and cutting it into small pieces.
