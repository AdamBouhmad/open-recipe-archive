## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg salsify
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour or 10 g cornstarch (Duryea maizena)
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the salsify with lukewarm water with a splash of vinegar.
2. Scrape them in small pieces at a time and immediately place these in cold, boiled water or water with a splash of milk or vinegar to keep the color white.
3. Wash them and cook them until tender in abundant boiling water with milk and salt (30 minutes).
4. Let them drain in a colander and stew them for 5 minutes in the sauce of butter or margarine, flour and milk prepared in the same way as cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328) or cornstarch sauce (see recipe 329).
5. Grate some nutmeg over them in the bowl.
6. Alternatively, clean the salsify by cooking them unpeeled for a few minutes, pouring cold water over them and rubbing off the black skin.
