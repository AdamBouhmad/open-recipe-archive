## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces wheat flour
- 3 eggs
- butter
- bread sugar

## Directions

1. Grease molds with butter
2. Make a not-too-thick batter of 2.5 ounces wheat flour, 3 eggs, butter and bread sugar
3. Pour into molds
4. Bake in a tart pan until done
