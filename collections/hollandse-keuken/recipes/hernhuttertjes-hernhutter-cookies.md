## Ingredients

- 125 g butter or margarine
- 80 g brown sugar
- 125 g flour
- 70 g currants
- 20 g almonds

## Directions

1. Grind the peeled almonds finely
2. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy
3. Add the sugar, sifted flour, washed currants that have been pitted, and ground almonds
4. Form small balls from this dough
5. Bake them on a greased baking sheet in the middle of a moderately warm oven until lightly brown and done (15 to 20 minutes)
6. This dough can also be used to fill the small Hernhutter molds (see recipe 770)
