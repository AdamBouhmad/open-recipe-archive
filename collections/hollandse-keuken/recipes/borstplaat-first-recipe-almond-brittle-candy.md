## Ingredients

- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Make sugar moist with as much water that it just barely dissolves.
2. Bring the sugar solution to a boil and cook it until it has reached the required thickness.
3. Test this as follows: Stick in the sugar solution a small stick (for example the handle of a butter brush), which has been in cold water for some time, stick it afterwards again in the cold water and try whether the adhering.
