## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter bean water
- 150 g smoked bacon
- 100 g onion (2 medium-sized)
- 3 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 25 g sago or potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the bacon with lukewarm water or scrape it with a knife
2. Cut it into small cubes and fry these lightly brown in the frying pan
3. Remove them from the fat and fry the chopped onions lightly brown in it
4. Add the bacon and onions to the bean water in which the spices have steeped and which has been strained
5. Thicken this with the sago or potato flour mixed with cold water
6. Serve the sauce with brown beans or capers
