## Ingredients

- Veal head, cooked and cold
- Butter
- Flour
- Onions
- Meat juices
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Lemon peel
- Cloves
- Salt
- Parsley
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. When the head is done and cold, cut it into square pieces
2. In a pan, put some butter with a couple of spoonfuls of flour, along with a good amount of finely chopped onions and some meat juices
3. Let it cook with the meat
4. Add a little pepper, a couple of bay leaves, lemon peel, some cloves and salt to taste
5. Finally add some chopped parsley and some Madeira wine
6. Let everything braise together
