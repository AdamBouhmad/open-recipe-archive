## Ingredients

- 2 raw egg yolks
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 1/4 to 2 1/2 dl salad oil
- approximately 1/2 dl vinegar
- 1 1/2 teaspoon mustard or mustard powder
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Press the cooked egg yolks through a sieve.
2. Add the raw egg yolks, mustard, pepper and salt, then drop by drop alternately oil and vinegar (see the previous recipe).
