## Ingredients

- 1 calf kidney
- 1 egg white
- pepper
- salt
- 75 g onion
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- egg
- frying fat
- 6 to 8 thin slices of casino bread

## Directions

1. Soak the kidney several hours in cold water, remove the fat and chop or grind it finely
2. Mix it with the finely chopped onion, parsley, plenty of salt and pepper and enough egg white to obtain a fairly loose mixture
3. Remove the crusts from thin slices of casino bread, divide them in half and coat them on one side with the mixture, such that it lies higher in the middle than at the edges
4. Coat only the kidney side doubly with breadcrumbs, which is done most easily by placing the bread on the tip of a broad knife, strewing breadcrumbs over it through a sieve, then pouring beaten egg or egg white mixed with water over it and again strewing breadcrumbs through the sieve
5. Heat the frying fat in an iron pot, then place it on a gentler fire and fry the toasts light brown and cooked through in it (approximately 5 minutes), ensuring the kidney side remains longest under the fat
6. Let them drip on gray paper, place them on a warm plate covered with a cloth and serve as an appetizer
7. Garnish the plate with fresh parsley or with fried parsley
