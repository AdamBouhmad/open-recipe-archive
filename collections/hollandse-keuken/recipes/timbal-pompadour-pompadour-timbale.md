## Ingredients

- 1/2 l raspberry juice
- 1/4 l berry juice
- 100 g sugar
- 20 g gelatin
- carmine as needed
- 1/4 l milk
- 1/2 vanilla stick
- 150 g sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 20 g gelatin or 18 g powdered gelatin
- 1 1/2 dl maraschino
- 150 g candied fruits
- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 1/4 l raspberry juice with some berry juice
- 60 g sugar
- 12 1/2 g sago
- maraschino or kirsch as preferred

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar and the soaked and well-squeezed gelatin in the berry and raspberry juice (do not let the mixture boil).
2. Add carmine as needed and let this mixture cool.
3. Make a lining along the bottom and sides of a timbale mold rinsed with cold water (in summer, for this purpose rotate the mold in ice), or use a so-called double mold.
4. For the filling, boil the milk with the vanilla and sugar, bind it with the egg yolks.
5. Remove the liquid from the heat and add the soaked and well-squeezed gelatin.
6. Let this mixture cool, stirring occasionally, add it to the whipped cream and stiffly beaten egg whites, to which the candied fruits have been added, and pour it into the mold when it has become gelatinous.
7. Turn out the pudding after cooling and serve with sauce prepared in the usual manner (see recipe 748) and to which maraschino or kirsch may be added as desired.
