## Ingredients

- 1 kg eel
- 70 g butter or margarine or 1 dl salad oil
- flour or breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- salt

## Directions

1. For frying eel one preferably orders thick eel
2. Clean the eel as indicated for boiled eel (recipe 190)
3. Treat the eel, which can be coated in salt and pepper, as you would fried sole, but allow 20 to 30 minutes for cooking time
4. Do not fry eel on too hot a fire
