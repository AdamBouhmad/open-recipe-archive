## Ingredients

- 1 medium Savoy cabbage
- 2.5 dl milk
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage, cut it into coarse pieces, remove the stem, wash it and cook it in salted water until tender (45 minutes).
2. Let it drain in a colander as dry as possible, chop it as finely as possible and stew it for 10 minutes with the milk and the butter or margarine.
3. Sprinkle in some flour if the mass is too moist, but make sure it cooks completely.
4. Grate some nutmeg over it in the bowl.
