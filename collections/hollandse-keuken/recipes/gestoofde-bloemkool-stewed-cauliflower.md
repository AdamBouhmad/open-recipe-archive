## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- rainwater
- butter
- flour
- mace
- salt
- bouillon or sweet milk

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned cabbage into large pieces
2. Cook it in rainwater, but especially not too much
3. Let it drain on a colander or place it in cold water to keep it white
4. Make a sauce of butter, flour and some mace, a little salt and bouillon or sweet milk
5. Stir it well together and let the cabbage stew in it
