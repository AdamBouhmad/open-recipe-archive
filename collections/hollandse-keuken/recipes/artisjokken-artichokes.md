## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- wine
- white bread
- sugar
- butter
- nutmeg
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut short near the stem
2. Remove the hard leaves and clean the rest of all roughness
3. Place in cold water
4. Cook for one and a half hours with plenty of salt
5. Remove the stringy parts
6. Serve with a sauce made from a little wine, finely grated white bread, sugar, water, butter and grated nutmeg, stirred with a couple of egg yolks
