## Ingredients

- 100 g biscuits
- 100 g almond biscuits
- 3 eggs
- 30 g sugar
- 3 dl milk
- 50 g currants
- 50 g raisins
- 25 g candied citrus peel (or snippets)
- 1/2 grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Coat a warm pudding mold with butter or margarine.
2. Cut the biscuits straight on the sides, also cut them level on top and place them against the wall of the mold with the brown side outward.
3. Beat the eggs with the sugar and the grated lemon zest, add the milk.
4. Wash the currants and raisins and cook them briefly in water, chop the candied citrus peel (or the snippets).
5. Layer the mixture and almond biscuits in the mold, but make sure the top layer consists of almond biscuits.
6. Pour the beaten eggs over the mixture. Make sure the liquid just rises above the filling.
7. Close the mold in the usual manner and cook the pudding 1 1/2 hours in a bain-marie, but make sure the water is continuously topped up to just below the rim of the mold.
8. Let the pudding dry without the lid and serve it with warm vanilla or fruit sauce.
