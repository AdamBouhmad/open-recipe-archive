## Ingredients

- half pound macaroni
- salt and water
- butter
- cooked ham, finely chopped
- nutmeg
- Parmesan cheese
- half pint milk
- 4 eggs
- half ounce butter
- a little fine lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook half pound macaroni with salt and water, not too soft so the pieces remain whole
2. Cut the macaroni into pieces as desired
3. In a warm earthen pan, place a little butter, then a layer of macaroni
4. Add a layer of cooked and finely chopped ham, then sprinkle with nutmeg and Parmesan cheese
5. Continue layering until the pan is filled a finger's width below the rim
6. Stir together half pint milk, 4 eggs, half ounce butter and a little fine lemon peel
7. Pour this over the macaroni
8. Bake in a hot oven for another quarter hour
