## Ingredients

- veal chops, cleaned of membranes and bones
- melted butter
- brown jus
- parsley
- crushed thyme
- finely chopped shallots
- Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Roll the cleaned veal chops in melted butter
2. Place them in an earthenware pan
3. Add brown jus, parsley, crushed thyme, and finely chopped shallots
4. Braise them slowly
5. When serving, add two or three glasses of Rhine wine
