## Ingredients

- 5 ounces almond paste, of which 1 ounce bitter
- 2 ounces sugar
- 20 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Stir the almond paste, of which 1 ounce bitter, together well with sugar and egg yolks
2. Bake it on a baking sheet under a cover with fire until done
