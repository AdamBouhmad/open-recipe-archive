## Ingredients

- 3 tomatoes
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the crown from the tomatoes, wash them and cut them into the thinnest possible slices with a tomato knife.
2. Sprinkle some pepper and salt over them, pour some oil and vinegar on them and let this soak in for some time.
3. Then carefully place them on the plate without the liquid and sprinkle some very finely chopped parsley over them.
