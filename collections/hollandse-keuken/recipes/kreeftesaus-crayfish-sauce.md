## Ingredients

- Crayfish butter
- Flour
- Pure calf juice
- Pepper
- Mace
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stir a couple spoons flour through crayfish butter and let it fry brown briefly
2. Add pure calf juice, a little pepper, mace and lemon juice
3. Let it cook through
