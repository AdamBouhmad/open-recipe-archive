## Ingredients

- 20 kg broad beans
- summer savory
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Shell the broad beans, wash them and cook them in plenty of boiling water for 5 minutes.
2. Then fill well-cleaned bottles with them up to 4 cm below the rim.
3. Pour on enough cooked cold water so that the beans are just covered.
4. Add salt and summer savory if desired.
5. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize for 1½ to 2 hours.
6. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
