## Ingredients

- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 eggs
- peel of 1 lemon
- 1 dl rum
- 100 g sugar
- 8 g white gelatine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar and the grated lemon peel until the sugar is completely dissolved.
2. Let this mass be bound while stirring au bain-marie.
3. Then add, away from the fire, the rum and the soaked sheet gelatine dissolved in boiling water.
4. Beat the cream stiff and stir the remaining mass through it.
5. Let the pudding become jelly-like, stirring from time to time, pour it into a mold brushed with unbeaten egg white and let it become cold and firm.
6. Turn it out onto a damp plate and serve without sauce.
