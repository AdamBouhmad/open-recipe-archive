## Ingredients

- 4 beef steaks from the loin at 100 g each (thick, round cut)
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 5 g salt
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash the chateaubriands, dry them with a clean cloth or scrape them, rub them with pepper and salt and immediately bake them in the browned butter in the manner indicated for beef steak (cooking time 4-5 minutes per piece)
2. Finish the jus with milk
