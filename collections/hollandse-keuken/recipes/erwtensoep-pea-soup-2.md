## Ingredients

- green peas
- rainwater
- salt
- salted pork
- sausage
- celery
- scorzonera
- onions

## Directions

1. Soak the needed quantity of green peas in rainwater the evening before
2. Hang the peas with the water in which they soaked and let it almost cook down
3. When the peas are cooked, mash them with a spoon into pieces and add pieces of salted pork and sausage, some celery and scorzonera; some also add onions
4. If you don't want shells in the soup, mash the peas when cooked through a strainer or use split peas
