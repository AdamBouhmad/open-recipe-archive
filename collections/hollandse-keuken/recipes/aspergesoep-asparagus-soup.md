## Ingredients

- 1 l asparagus liquid or water in which the ends of the asparagus have been cooked
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- (1 egg)
- 1 dl cream or milk
- piece of mace
- (some cooked asparagus tips)

## Directions

1. Let the mace steep in the asparagus liquid or in the water in which the hard ends of the asparagus have been cooked
2. Sieve the liquid and pour it gradually while stirring into the melted butter or margarine, mixed with the flour and from which all lumps have been carefully rubbed out
3. Let the soup cook while stirring for 10 minutes
4. Warm the asparagus tips in it and carefully pour the soup while stirring and beating into the egg, which has been beaten in the soup tureen with the milk or cream
