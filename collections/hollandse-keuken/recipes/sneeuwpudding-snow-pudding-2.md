## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 1 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- 90 g Duryea cornstarch
- 80 g sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook stiff cornstarch paste in the usual manner, but let the vanilla steep in it and add the sugar
2. Mix the paste off the fire with the very stiffly beaten egg white
3. Pour the mass into a mold rinsed with water, let it cool
4. Turn the pudding out onto a damp dish and serve with pitted cherries or fruit sauce
5. Note: Prepare this pudding only a few hours before use, as the egg white will separate with long standing
