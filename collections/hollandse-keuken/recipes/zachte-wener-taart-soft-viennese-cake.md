## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g sugar
- 100 g flour
- 100 g almonds
- 6 eggs
- 2 or 3 kinds of jam

## Directions

1. Prepare the cake like the German sand cake, but add the peeled and ground almonds to the yolks.
2. Cut the cake after cooling into 3 or more layers, spread each layer with a different kind of jam.
3. Lay the layers on top of each other again and garnish the top with cooking and egg white icing and French candied fruits.
