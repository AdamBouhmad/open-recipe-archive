## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 100 g pearl sago
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 100 g sugar
- 1/2 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook sago pudding in the usual manner (see recipe 607), but cook the vanilla or lemon zest in with it.
2. Stir in half of the sugar when the pudding is done.
3. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the remaining sugar, carefully add the hot pudding from which the vanilla or lemon zest has been removed to it, mix it as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Finish the dish as described in recipe 602.
