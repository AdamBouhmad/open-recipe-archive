## Ingredients

- 6 calf feet
- ½ ounce fish glue
- A beer glass of Rhine wine
- Half a beer glass of vinegar
- Juice of a couple of lemons
- A spoonful of brown sugar
- Liquid from the calf feet
- Egg whites from 8 eggs
- Bay leaves
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Take 6 calf feet and let them cook for four hours
2. Meanwhile melt ½ ounce fish glue in a small pot
3. Let the liquid from the calf feet cool, remove the fat and return to the fire in a pot
4. Add a beer glass of Rhine wine, half a beer glass of vinegar, juice of a couple of lemons, a spoonful of brown sugar and some liquid from the calf feet
5. Let it stand on the fire until it is brown
6. When the liquid is lukewarm, whisk it with slightly beaten egg whites from 8 eggs
7. Let it boil until it is clear and then strain it
8. Place the mold in the sun so the aspic cools quickly, pouring in a little of the strained liquid
9. When cooled, lay some bay leaves and 5 to 6 lemon slices on it, pouring a little liquid over
10. Now place the beef on top and fill the mold again with liquid
