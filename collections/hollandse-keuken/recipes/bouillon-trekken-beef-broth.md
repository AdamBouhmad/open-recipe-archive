## Ingredients

- 250 g beef shank meat (or beef knuckle) for soup
- 1 l water
- 5 to 10 g salt
- herbs (parsley, carrot, piece of mace, thyme for drinking broth)

## Directions

1. Rinse the meat, cut it into small pieces or make deep cuts on all sides.
2. Bring it in a tightly closed pan slowly to a boil and let the broth simmer on gentle heat (3 to 4 hours).
3. Pour it through a sieve lined with a rinsed flannel or muslin cloth.
4. For drinking broth, let the following herbs simmer along: parsley, carrot, a piece of mace and thyme, but use so little that the flavor does not overpower.
5. After straining the drinking broth, warm it again and serve in cups with a slice of lemon and a rusk or piece of toasted bread.
6. The leftover meat can be excellently used in various dishes, for example rice or barley with curry.
