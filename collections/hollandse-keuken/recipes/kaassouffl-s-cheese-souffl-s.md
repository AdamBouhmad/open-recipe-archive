## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 5 eggs
- 100 g old Gouda cheese
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cream the butter or margarine, add the egg yolks one by one, mix them with grated cheese, pepper and salt to taste and finally as airy as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
2. Fill soufflé dishes greased with butter or margarine halfway with this mixture, let them become light brown in a moderately warm oven and rise (approximately 15 minutes).
3. Serve them immediately.
