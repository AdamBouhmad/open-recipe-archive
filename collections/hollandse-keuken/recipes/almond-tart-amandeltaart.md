## Ingredients

- 100 g almonds
- marmalade or jam
- butter or margarine
- sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds in warm water, peel them and grind them finely.
2. Knead them with the butter or margarine, sugar and flour into a firm ball and divide it into two equal parts.
3. Press one half into a greased tart pan, making sure the layer of dough is as thin as possible. (When using a small tart pan, divide the dough into 3 parts instead.)
4. Bake it in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked through (15 to 20 minutes).
5. Let the cake cool, turn it out and bake the second half in the pan.
6. Let this also cool and place both parts on top of each other with some jam or marmalade between them.
7. Sprinkle the tart with sifted powdered sugar or tie a colored ribbon around it or garnish the tart with water glaze and candied fruits.
