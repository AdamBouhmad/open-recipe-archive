## Ingredients

- Meat, minced or ground
- Potatoes, minced
- Onions, chopped
- Lean gravy with water (or milk or bouillon)
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Mince or grind the meat finely, make the potatoes fine.
2. Fry the chopped onions light brown.
3. Mix these with the meat and potatoes, moisten everything with the lean gravy with water (milk or bouillon) until it reaches the thickness of mashed potatoes.
4. Place it in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water, smooth the top, sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, place a few pieces of butter on it, clean the edge of the dish and let the philosopher brown in a moderately warm oven.
