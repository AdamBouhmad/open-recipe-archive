## Ingredients

- Rib roast
- Salt
- Water
- Pears or dried fruit (optional)

## Directions

1. Place a rib roast in salt for 8 to 9 days.
2. Cook it with water until done.
3. You can cook pears or dried fruit in the cooking liquid.
