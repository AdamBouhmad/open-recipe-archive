## Ingredients

- One spoonful flour
- Very finely cut shavings
- Madeira wine
- A little butter
- Water or bouillon
- Butter
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with either a spoonful of Madeira wine, a little butter and water, or with bouillon
2. Let it cook for a quarter hour
3. Add more butter and sugar
4. Stir well and use with pudding
