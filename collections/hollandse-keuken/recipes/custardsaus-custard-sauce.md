## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 15 g custard powder
- 40 g sugar

## Directions

1. Bring almost all the milk to a boil
2. Mix the custard with the sugar together and blend them with a little of the cold milk into a thin paste without lumps
3. Pour this into the boiling liquid while stirring
4. Let the sauce simmer while stirring for two minutes on a gentle heat
5. Let it cool, stirring occasionally, to prevent a skin from forming
