## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces wheat flour
- 0.5 quart milk
- 2.5 ounces butter
- 2 eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- snippets (candied citrus peel)
- sukade (candied fruit)
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook 2.5 ounces wheat flour and 0.5 quart milk until very thick
2. Stir in 2.5 ounces butter, 2 eggs, sugar and cinnamon
3. Add snippets, sukade and lemon peel
4. Bake in a cookie pan greased with butter
