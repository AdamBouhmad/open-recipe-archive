## Ingredients

- 1 kg Brussels lof
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour or 15 g Duryea corn starch
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove the brown leaves from the sprouts or scrape off the brown.
2. Remove the spongy heart that is in the stalk and is very bitter, cut off a piece of the stalk and cut the sprouts into strips.
3. Wash the lof until the water runs completely clear, let it cook with as little water as possible in a well-enameled pan (to prevent discoloration) for 1/2 hour.
4. Let it drain in a colander and braise it in a sauce made of butter or margarine, flour and milk, prepared in the manner of cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328 or recipe 329), for another 10 minutes with salt.
5. Grate nutmeg over it on the plate.
