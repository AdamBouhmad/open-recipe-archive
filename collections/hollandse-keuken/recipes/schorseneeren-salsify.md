## Ingredients

- salsify
- rainwater
- a splash of vinegar
- milk
- butter
- biscuit
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook in rainwater with a splash of vinegar for two hours
2. Drain
3. Stew with a little milk, butter, biscuit and nutmeg
