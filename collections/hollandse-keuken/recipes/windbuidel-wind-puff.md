## Ingredients

- Half pound butter
- 1 pint milk
- Flour
- 8 to 10 eggs
- Half ounce sugar

## Directions

1. Let half a pound of butter boil in a pint of milk over a high fire
2. Stir in as much flour as needed until it is bound
3. Let it cool and then add 8 to 10 eggs and half an ounce sugar
