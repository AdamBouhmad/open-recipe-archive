## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Just over 2 ounces butter
- 1 ounce sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 5 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks and milk together
2. Add butter to flour
3. Knead all together well
4. Make fifteen small heaps
5. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. Bake in the oven
