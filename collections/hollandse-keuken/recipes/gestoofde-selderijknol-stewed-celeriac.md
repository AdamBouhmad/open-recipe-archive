## Ingredients

- 2 celeriac roots
- 2.5 dl milk
- 20 to 30 g flour or 15 g corn flour (Duryea)
- 20 to 30 g butter or margarine
- salt and nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the celeriac into fairly thick slices, peel them and cut them into strips.
2. Wash them and cook them until tender in plenty of boiling salted water (approximately 1/2 hour).
3. Let them drain in a colander as dry as possible and stew them for 10 minutes in the sauce made from butter, flour and milk or celeriac stock, prepared in the same way as cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328) or corn flour sauce (see recipe 329).
4. Grate some nutmeg over it in the dish or sprinkle some celery salt over it.
