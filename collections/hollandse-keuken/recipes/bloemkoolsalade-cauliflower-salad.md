## Ingredients

- cauliflower stalks, sliced
- cauliflower florets
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower with stalks cut into slices and florets laid on top.
2. Dress with salt, pepper, vinegar, and oil.
