## Ingredients

- 1 kg sauerkraut
- 60 g fat, butter or margarine
- 1 kg mashed potatoes
- 400 g sausages
- 30 g fat or margarine

## Directions

1. Cook the sauerkraut until tender in the manner described in the previous recipe.
2. Make mashed potatoes in the usual way (see recipe 409).
3. Fry the sausages in the fat, butter or margarine until light brown and cooked (see recipe 246).
4. Make a border of mashed potatoes on a heated plate, place the sauerkraut in the middle, place the sausages on top and pour the fat over it.
