## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- Salt
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 3 tablespoons ground onions
- 5 garlic cloves
- 2 lomboks
- 1 tablespoon laos
- 1 teaspoon djienten
- 2 teaspoons ketoembar
- 1 teaspoon trassi
- 1 tablespoon goela djawa
- 2 to 3 tablespoons tamarind or assem water
- 1 dl water
- 2 dl coconut milk or milk with some salt
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Roast the cleaned and salted chicken until cooked
2. Debone it
3. Pound the spices finely with the ground onions
4. Fry them, add water, tamarind water, sugar, coconut milk or milk with salt
5. Pour this sauce over the chicken pieces and let them stew in it
