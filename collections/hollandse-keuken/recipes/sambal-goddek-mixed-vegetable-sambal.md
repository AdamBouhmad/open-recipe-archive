## Ingredients

- Cabbage, beans, peas, peteh beans
- 100 g peeled cooked shrimp
- 2 tablespoons ground onions
- 2 tablespoons finely sliced fried onions
- 5 pieces removed, roasted kemiri nuts
- 4 finely sliced garlic cloves
- 2 tablespoons finely sliced roasted lomboks
- 2 teaspoons ketoembar
- 2 citrus leaves
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Three lengthwise sliced green lomboks
- 1 dl finely sliced green gooseberries
- Water
- 5 to 7 1/2 dl milk with salt or coconut milk

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned and finely sliced vegetables with the green lomboks and the gooseberries in some water with salt until cooked
2. Drain the water and add the coconut milk or milk with some salt
3. Pound the spices — except the fried onions, the leaves and the roasted lomboks — together finely
4. Add this to the mixture and cook everything through, stirring
5. Add the lomboks, the leaves and the fried onions, and make a thick mixture from this
