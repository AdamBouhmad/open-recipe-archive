## Ingredients

- 6 cucumbers
- 1½ bottles pickling vinegar
- 2½ g turmeric
- 5 g dried ginger
- 2 Spanish peppers
- 50 g mustard seed
- Some salad oil

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers thinly.
2. Taste the ends to see if they are not bitter.
3. Cut them off if necessary.
4. Cut them lengthwise in half, scoop out the seeds with a spoon and cut the cucumbers into blocks.
5. Set them aside for a night sprinkled with salt, then let them drain as dry as possible through a colander and place them in a well-cleaned Cologne pot or jam jars.
6. Arrange the spices between them.
