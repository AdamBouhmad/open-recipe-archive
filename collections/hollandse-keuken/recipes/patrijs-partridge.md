## Ingredients

- 4 small or 2 large partridges
- at least 100 to 150 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 4 thin strips of larding bacon (optional)

## Directions

1. Treat the partridges as you would pigeon (see recipe 318).
2. If desired, place a piece of larding bacon over the breast while roasting or lard the partridge with thin strips of larding bacon.
3. Serve with stewed fruit or salad.
4. Note: Partridge can also be served with chestnut puree.
