## Ingredients

- 15 kg green beans
- 1½ or 4 kg salt
- Cologne pot
- cloth
- board
- stone

## Directions

1. Remove the ends of the green beans twice on each side, wash them, dry them with a clean cloth.
2. Cut them with a green bean knife or grind them through a green bean mill.
3. Blanch them for 5 minutes in boiling water and let them cool spread out.
4. Press them firmly layer by layer with the salt in a well-cleaned Cologne pot.
5. Make sure the last layer is salt.
6. Cover them with a clean cloth, place a board and a stone on top.
7. Store them as indicated in the general instructions.
8. If using 4 kg salt, seal the pot with a board or damp parchment paper when in use.
