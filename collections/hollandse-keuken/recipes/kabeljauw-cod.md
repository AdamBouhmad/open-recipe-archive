## Ingredients

- 2 portions of cod (approximately 3/4 kg)
- water
- salt
- small splash of vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the portions well
2. Cook them in boiling water with salt and some vinegar until a fin comes off easily (15 to 20 minutes)
3. Remove them with a fish slice or a pair of skimming spoons from the water and place them on the fish plate
4. Serve with butter sauce, mustard sauce, egg sauce, melted or glazed butter or margarine
