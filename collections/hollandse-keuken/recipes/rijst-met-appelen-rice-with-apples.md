## Ingredients

- Rice
- Water
- Good sour apples
- Brown sugar
- Cinnamon
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take 5 ounces of rice and 3 cups of water per cup of rice
2. Cut good sour apples into small pieces and add them
3. Cook together until it reaches the proper thickness
4. Stir in brown sugar, cinnamon and a lump of butter
5. Serve
