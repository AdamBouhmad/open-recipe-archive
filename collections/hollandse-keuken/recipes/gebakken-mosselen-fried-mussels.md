## Ingredients

- mussels
- flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the mussels as indicated in recipe 195
2. Remove them from the shells
3. Coat them in flour mixed with salt and pepper
4. Fry the mussels brown (for example in deep frying fat) and let them drain on porous paper
