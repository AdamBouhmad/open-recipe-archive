## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef tenderloin
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 10 g salt
- pepper
- milk
- 150 g cooked rice
- 3/4 liter brown spicy sauce
- 100 g macaroni
- 30 g ham
- 30 g smoked beef tongue
- truffle
- 1/2 tin mushrooms
- 15 g butter or margarine
- 40 g grated Parmesan or aged Gouda cheese

## Directions

1. Braise the beef tenderloin in the usual manner, cut it into not too thick slices, arrange them again in the shape of the tenderloin on a base of cooked rice or mashed potato on the platter and cover the meat with brown spicy sauce
2. Arrange around it cooked macaroni cut into pieces, mixed with finely cut smoked beef tongue, finely cut ham, truffles and grated Parmesan cheese
3. Sprinkle the tenderloin with Parmesan cheese and place halved mushrooms on it
4. Serve additional thin spicy sauce separately
