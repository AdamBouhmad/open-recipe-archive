## Ingredients

- Sausages
- Flour
- Eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the sausages until done and then remove the casing
2. Make a dough from flour, eggs, and a little salt
3. Roll it out, place each sausage separately in it
4. Let them brown in the oven
