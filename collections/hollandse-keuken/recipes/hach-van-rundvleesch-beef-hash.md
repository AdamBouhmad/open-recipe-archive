## Ingredients

- Cold beef, cut into small pieces
- Onions (finely chopped)
- A spoonful of brown sugar
- Pepper
- Cloves (finely crushed)
- Fine salt
- Potato flour
- Vinegar or red wine

## Directions

1. Cut cold beef into small pieces
2. Add finely chopped onions, a spoonful of brown sugar, pepper, finely crushed cloves, fine salt, some potato flour and vinegar or red wine according to preference
3. Let it stew for three hours
