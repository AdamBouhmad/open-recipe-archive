## Ingredients

- 2 celery roots
- 3 tablespoons salad oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- optionally a dash of Maggi's Aroma

## Directions

1. Cook the celery until done in the way as indicated for 'braised celery' (recipe 452). Let it drain in a colander and prepare the salad as described in recipe 479.
