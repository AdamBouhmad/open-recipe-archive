## Ingredients

- wild rabbit
- rabbit liver
- rabbit bones
- bouillon
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- unsalted lard
- pepper
- cream
- fresh clean intestines

## Directions

1. Cook a wild rabbit and chop the meat very fine
2. Add the liver, well cleaned and cooked
3. Crush the bones first and cook bouillon from them
4. Mix the bouillon with breadcrumbs to make a dough
5. Add some butter, unsalted lard cut into cubes, pepper and some cream
6. Stuff into fresh clean intestines and cook like other sausage
