## Ingredients

- 8 large rusks
- 1/2 l milk
- 3 eggs
- 80 g sugar
- 150 g unpeeled hazelnuts
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Rub the rusks fine in the warm milk.
2. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the sugar, vigorously add the warm porridge and the peeled and grated or ground hazelnuts.
3. Beat the egg white stiff and fold it in lightly.
4. Place the mixture in a heatproof dish.
5. Smooth the top, sprinkle with a mixture of breadcrumbs, fine hazelnuts and sugar, clean the edge of the dish and let it become light brown and rise in the oven.
