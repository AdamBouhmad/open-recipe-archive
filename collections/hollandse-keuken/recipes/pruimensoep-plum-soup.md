## Ingredients

- plums, cut small and pitted
- water
- sugar
- lemon peels, cut small
- 1 whole Ceylon cinnamon stick
- Malaga raisins
- red wine
- brown toasted flour
- white bread

## Directions

1. Cook finely cut plums, without pits, with water, sugar, finely cut lemon peels, a whole Ceylon cinnamon stick and some Malaga raisins
2. Then add some red wine and some brown toasted flour
3. Let it cook through once more and serve it over small pieces of white bread
