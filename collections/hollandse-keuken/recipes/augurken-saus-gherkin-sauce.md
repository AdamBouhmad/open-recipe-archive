## Ingredients

- finely chopped shallots
- flour
- butter
- boiling water
- fresh gherkins
- bay leaf
- tarragon
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Let some finely chopped shallots with flour and butter take on a light color by stirring on the fire.
2. Make a bound sauce with boiling water.
3. Peel fresh gherkins, cut them in half, remove the middle, cut them into small cubes and cook them with bay leaf, tarragon, crushed pepper and salt in vinegar until soft.
4. Add a small amount of Liebig's Meat Extract and let cook through.
