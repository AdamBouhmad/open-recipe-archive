## Ingredients

- Dried morels
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Flour
- Salt
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from dried morels and cut them in half or thirds if very large
2. Wash them very clean with lukewarm water and let them settle to remove sand
3. Cook them, let them drain
4. Put them in a pan with some butter and lemon juice
5. Add some flour, salt and bouillon
6. Make a ragout from these
7. For a reasonable serving, one ounce is needed
