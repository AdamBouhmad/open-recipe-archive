## Ingredients

- two good fatty livers
- onions
- bouillon
- two and a half ounces melted lard in cubes
- half a can of calf's blood
- a quarter can of cream
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Take two good fatty livers and chop them fine
2. Cook a little onions with bouillon and chop them with the liver, add to this two and a half ounces melted lard in cubes, half a can of calf's blood, a quarter can of cream, salt, pepper and spices, and cook it slowly together, always stirring
3. When cool, stuff the sausage, and roast them on the grill when you want to eat them
