## Ingredients

- Juice of 3 oranges (1½ to 2 dl)
- 3 eggs
- Grated peel of 1 orange
- Juice of ½ lemon
- 7½ g Duryea cornstarch
- 50 to 60 g sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar for a long time until they become thick, white and airy.
2. Add the grated orange peel, the cornstarch and the fruit juice, which has been strained, and let the custard cook au bain-marie while stirring vigorously for 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and mix with the stiffly beaten egg white and serve immediately after cooling with biscuits.
