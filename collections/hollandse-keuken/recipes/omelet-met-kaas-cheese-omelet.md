## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons water
- pepper and salt to taste
- 50 g Parmesan or Gouda cheese
- 25 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper and salt, add the grated cheese and water and make an omelet as described in recipe 383.
2. Place on a warmed dish and serve with bread.
