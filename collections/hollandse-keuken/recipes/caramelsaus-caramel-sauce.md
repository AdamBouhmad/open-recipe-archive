## Ingredients

- 2.5 dl milk
- 2.5 dl cooking cream
- 150 g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with the cream to a boil.
2. Put 125 g sugar in a fairly large pan and let it stand on a not too fierce fire until it has become caramel and begins to foam over the entire surface.
3. Then slowly pour, all in one stream, the boiling liquid into it, off the fire.
4. Add 25 g sugar.
5. Beat the eggs, add them slowly, stirring constantly and rubbing away lumps, to the maizena.
6. Add to this mixture carefully, stirring constantly, enough of the hot liquid until the eggs are thoroughly warmed.
7. Pour it back into the pan and let the sauce, stirring vigorously with a whisk, boil for 3-4 minutes on a gentle fire.
8. Present the sauce with warm puddings.
