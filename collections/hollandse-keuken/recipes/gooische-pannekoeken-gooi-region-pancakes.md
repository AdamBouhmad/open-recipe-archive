## Ingredients

- 2 full tablespoons wheat flour
- 2 tablespoons buckwheat flour
- 3 eggs (2 with whites, 1 without white)
- sweet milk
- 1 lump butter
- boiling water
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Mix 2 tablespoons wheat flour, 2 tablespoons buckwheat flour, 3 eggs with sweet milk until it reaches the consistency of sweet cream.
2. Put a lump of butter in a small bowl, pour boiling water over it, and slowly pour this into the batter.
3. Stir together and add sugar to taste.
4. To bake, put one sauce spoon of batter into the pan and let it quickly spread. The pan should be moderately warm.
