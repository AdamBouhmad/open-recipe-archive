## Ingredients

- 1 kg gooseberries
- 100 g sugar
- some water
- some sago or 1 egg
- some chalk

## Directions

1. Clean the berries by removing the stems and sepals.
2. Wash them, let them cook slowly on gentle heat in boiling water until they become sauce (10-15 minutes).
3. Rub them through with a wooden spoon or through a horsehair sieve, mix the sauce with the chalk and sugar and bind it with a little mixed sago, which must cook briefly, or with the egg beaten in a small bowl, into which the hot sauce is stirred carefully.
