## Ingredients

- 4 pigeons
- at least 100 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the pigeons thoroughly inside and outside, rub them with salt.
2. Make the butter or margarine light brown in a pan, place the pigeons in it, baste them on all sides and roast them brown and cooked through (approximately 1/2 hour).
3. Turn them several times during this period and add some water to the butter or margarine if needed when it threatens to become too dark in color.
4. Place the pigeons on a platter, pour the gravy, which has been finished with some water, over them and tie small manchettes around the legs.
5. Serve with stewed fruit or salad.
