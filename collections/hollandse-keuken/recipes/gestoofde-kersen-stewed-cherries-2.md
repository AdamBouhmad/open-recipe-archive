## Ingredients

- 1 kg cherries
- approximately 100 g sugar
- 1 1/2 dl water
- some sago

## Directions

1. Wash the cherries, remove the stems and pit them, but make sure all the juice is caught.
2. Set them on with the water, sugar and juice and let them cook gently for 5 minutes.
3. Bind the liquid with a little mixed sago.
