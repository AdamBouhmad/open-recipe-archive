## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- 2 egg yolks
- pepper, salt and lemon juice to taste
- 3 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the milk, pepper and salt and add 25 g of the butter or margarine
2. Allow the mixture to thicken over a bain-marie
3. Gradually stir in the rest of the butter or margarine in small quantities
4. Finish with pepper, salt and lemon juice to taste and serve with turbot, salmon, sole, etc.
