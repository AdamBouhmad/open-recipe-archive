## Ingredients

- hard butter or margarine
- flour
- 1 egg
- sugar
- lemon juice
- water
- raw or stewed fruit

## Directions

1. Sift flour into a bowl, add butter or margarine and chop finely until it resembles breadcrumbs
2. Beat the egg with sugar and lemon juice and add to the mixture
3. Stir together as lightly as possible, adding water as needed to form a firm dough ball
4. Roll out the dough on a flour-dusted surface with a flour-dusted rolling pin into a round sheet, larger than the baking dish
5. Place the required fruit in the baking dish
6. Sprinkle sugar between the raw fruit and pile the fruit high in the middle of the dish, ensuring any stewed fruit is cold
7. Moisten the edge of the dish with water, lay the dough sheet over it generously, press onto the moistened edge with a fork and trim the overhanging part
8. Cut figures from the remaining dough and attach as garnish with water
9. Brush everything with egg yolk beaten with water, prick the dough in several places
10. Bake in a fairly warm oven until light brown and cooked through (3/4 hour to 1 hour)
