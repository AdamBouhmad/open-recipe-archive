## Ingredients

- 1 Savoy cabbage (medium-sized)
- 200 g veal mince
- 200 g pork mince
- 100 g old bread
- pepper, salt, nutmeg to taste
- 40 g butter or margarine
- breadcrumbs
- Maggi's Aroma

## Directions

1. Carefully remove all leaves from the cabbage, wash them and cook them for 15 to 20 minutes in plenty of boiling salted water.
2. Let them drain in a colander.
3. Meanwhile, prepare the mince in the usual way, but make sure it is not too dry, as it becomes difficult to handle.
4. Place a washed white cloth in a colander or deep bowl, carefully arrange a layer of the largest cabbage leaves in it and spread a layer of mince over it.
5. Place another layer of leaves on top, then mince again and continue in this way until everything is used.
6. Bind the cloth tightly with string around the cabbage and cook it for 1.5 hours in plenty of boiling salted water, but make sure a plate lies at the bottom of the pan to prevent burning.
7. Remove the cabbage from the cloth, place it with the smooth side up in a fireproof small dish, pour some bouillon or jus with water over it.
8. Season to taste with Maggi's Aroma, sprinkle breadcrumbs over the cabbage, place pieces of butter or margarine on top and let the cabbage become light brown in the oven.
