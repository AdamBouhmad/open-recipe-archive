## Ingredients

- 400 g stockfish
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the fish for at least 24 hours in cold water, clean it, wash it, make not too stiff rolls of it and tie them shut with string.
2. Cook the stockfish in abundant boiling water with salt until done (+ 1 hour), but ensure that the water remains at a boil.
3. Remove the strings and serve the fish with mustard sauce, rice, potatoes and fried onions.
4. Note: One can serve anchovy sauce, small red fish and fried shrimp crackers with it (see recipe 933).
