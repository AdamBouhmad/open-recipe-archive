## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 60 g Parmesan or Gouda cheese
- 30 g butter or margarine
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper and salt, add the grated cheese and milk.
2. Prepare further as indicated in recipe 395.
3. Serve at coffee time.
