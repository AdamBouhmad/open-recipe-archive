## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound semolina
- 0.25 pound bread sugar
- 2 grated lemon zests
- 1 can milk
- 4 eggs
- almond essence
- berry sauce

## Directions

1. Let 1 can milk come to a boil and stir in the semolina, bread sugar and grated lemon zests
2. let it cook with constant stirring
3. remove from heat
4. beat 4 eggs with their whites and stir through
5. some add almond essence which makes it very tasty
6. serve in small molds or bowls, cold with berry sauce
