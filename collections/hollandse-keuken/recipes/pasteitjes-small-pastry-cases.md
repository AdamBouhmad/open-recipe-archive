## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter or calf kidney fat
- 100 g flour
- salt
- water (approximately 1/2 dl)

## Directions

1. Prepare feuilletée (puff pastry) in the usual manner (see recipe 846)
2. Roll it out the last time to a thickness of approximately 3/4 cm and cut 12 circles with a ring or wine glass
3. From half the circles, cut lids with a smaller ring or smaller glass, which are slightly flattened and brushed with beaten egg yolk mixed with water
4. Seal the remaining rings with water or egg white onto the circles, brush only the top with egg yolk beaten with water and place the pastries and lids on a baking sheet
5. Bake them in a hot oven light brown and cooked through (15 to 18 minutes), do not look in the oven during the first 5 minutes as there is a risk of sinking
6. Remove them from the sheet, hollow out the undercooked inside part slightly, fill them with some ragout and place the lids on top
