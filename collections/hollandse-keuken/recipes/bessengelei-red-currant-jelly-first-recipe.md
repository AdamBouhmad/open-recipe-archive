## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg red currants
- 1 kg sugar (approximately)

## Directions

1. Wash the currants, put them with very little liquid on a low heat and let the currant juice come out.
2. Squeeze or press out the currants.
3. Measure the juice and use 1 kg sugar for every liter.
4. Cook no more than 1 liter of juice at a time in a large pan.
5. When the juice boils, sprinkle the sugar in very slowly.
6. Stir meanwhile so that the sugar dissolves quickly and the liquid hardly comes off the boil.
7. Let the jelly boil for 2 minutes and put it, still thinly liquid, in well-cleaned jam jars.
8. Treat them further as other jelly.
9. Note: Instead of fresh fruit juice, one can also use currant juice from bottles.
