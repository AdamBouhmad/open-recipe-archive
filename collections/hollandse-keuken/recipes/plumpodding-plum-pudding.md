## Ingredients

- currants
- raisins
- candied citrus peel
- cinnamon snippets
- rum
- flour
- milk
- eggs
- finely chopped suet
- yeast
- butter
- meat stock
- rum sauce

## Directions

1. The day before, soak the necessary currants, raisins, candied citrus peel and cinnamon snippets in rum
2. make a batter of flour, milk and eggs
3. stir in finely chopped suet and the aforementioned currants, raisins, etc.
4. add a small piece of yeast
5. put the batter in a bag greased with butter
6. bind it loosely to allow the batter to rise
7. cook it 3 hours in meat stock
8. serve with rum sauce
