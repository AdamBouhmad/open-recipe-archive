## Ingredients

- 2 portions of brill (approximately 1 kg)
- water
- salt
- small splash of vinegar (optional)
- 1/2 onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the portions well
2. Cook them in boiling water with salt (some vinegar and the 1/2 onion if desired) until the fin comes off easily (approximately 10 minutes)
3. Remove them with 2 skimming spoons or a fish slice from the pan and place them on the fish plate
4. Garnish with fresh sprigs of parsley
5. Serve with egg sauce, butter sauce, or caper sauce
