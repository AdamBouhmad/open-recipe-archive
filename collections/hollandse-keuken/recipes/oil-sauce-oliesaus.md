## Ingredients

- Three lemons
- Finely chopped parsley
- Onions
- Tarragon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water
- Good vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Peel the lemons and remove as much of the white pith as possible
2. Cut them into slices of equal thickness
3. Mix with finely chopped parsley, onions, tarragon, salt, pepper, and a little water
4. Add good vinegar and oil
5. This sauce is very good with fried fish
