## Ingredients

- 4 dl brown piquant sauce
- 50 g ground veal
- 1/2 can of mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in the previous recipe, but stir in the cooked ground meat balls and the mushrooms, either whole or cut in half.
2. Serve with beef tongue, beef tenderloin, etc.
