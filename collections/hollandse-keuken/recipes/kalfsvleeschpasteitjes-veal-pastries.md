## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- 2.5 ounces ground veal
- shallots
- butter
- 2.5 ounces raw ground veal
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- mace
- bouillon
- egg

## Directions

1. Line small molds with puff pastry about the thickness of a knife back
2. Cut out pieces from the pastry with a tin cutter large enough to serve as lids for the pastries
3. Take 2.5 ounces ground veal, chop some shallots through it and let it cook in a pan with a piece of butter
4. Take 2.5 ounces raw ground veal, pound it finely in a mortar
5. Stir salt, pepper, nutmeg and a little mace through it
6. Cook it over with some bouillon and fill the pastries with it when cold
7. Close the pastries with the lids, brush with diluted egg and bake on a low fire
