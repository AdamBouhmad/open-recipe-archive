## Ingredients

- 100 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 30 g sugar
- 1 egg
- fresh fruits or fruits in juice

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, add the butter or margarine and cut it fine as breadcrumbs.
2. Stir the sugar and the beaten egg through this mixture.
3. Add enough water if necessary so the dough no longer dusts and roll it out to ¼ cm thickness on a flour-dusted board.
4. Grease tartlet molds with some butter and fill them quite full with a portion of the dough.
5. Prick here and there in the bottom to prevent bubbles and bake the galettes in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked.
6. Remove them from the molds and fill them after cooling with fresh fruits (e.g., strawberries with whipped cream) or fruits in water.
