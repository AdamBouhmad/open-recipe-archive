## Ingredients

- 4 dl milk
- 2 eggs
- 100 g sugar
- 2 1/2 dl whipped cream
- 1 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 14 g gelatine or 12 g powdered gelatine

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks vigorously with a spoonful of sugar.
2. Rinse the pudding mold with unbeaten egg white and set it upside down to let the excess egg white run out and keep the mold from drying out.
3. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the split vanilla stick and the remaining sugar, pour it, stirring vigorously, into the beaten yolks, put everything back in the pan and let the mixture, beating with a whisk, be bound over the fire (but especially do not boil).
4. Dissolve the sheet gelatine, after soaking 10 to 15 minutes in cold water, in the hot liquid, which has been taken from the fire.
5. Remove the vanilla.
6. Let the mass cool briefly.
7. Beat the cream and the egg whites stiff, pour in the pudding mixture while stirring and let it stand, stirring from time to time, until it begins to become jelly-like.
8. Then pour it into the pudding mold and let the pudding become cold and firm.
