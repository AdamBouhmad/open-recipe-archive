## Ingredients

- Veal chops
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Crushed rusks
- Meat juices

## Directions

1. Pound the chops well flat, prick them with a pointed knife
2. Smear them with butter and roll them in fine salt, pepper and crushed rusks
3. Fry them quickly on the grill or in a frying pan until done
4. Pour the meat juices over them when serving
