## Ingredients

- 150 g peeled cooked shrimp
- 2 tablespoons finely ground onions
- 5 garlic cloves
- 1 teaspoon laos
- 2 teaspoons sereh powder
- 2 whole lomboks with seeds
- A bit of roasted trassi
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 citrus leaves
- 2 peteh beans
- Coconut milk or milk with some salt
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Pound the spices, without the aromatic leaves, finely with the ground onions
2. Fry them in the butter or margarine
3. Add the aromatic leaves, the coconut milk or milk with some salt and make a thickened sauce from this
4. Add the cooked and peeled shrimp and let them stew in it
