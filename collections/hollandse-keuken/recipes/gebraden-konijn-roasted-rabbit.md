## Ingredients

- young rabbit
- water
- salt
- pepper
- fine breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the rabbit, which should be especially young, on with some water
2. Sprinkle it with fine salt, pepper and ground breadcrumbs
3. Place it straight in a roasting pan with butter and baste constantly
4. Make sure it becomes nicely browned
