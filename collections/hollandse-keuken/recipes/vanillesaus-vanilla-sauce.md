## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 1/2 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- 5 g Duryea cornstarch
- 2 eggs
- 30 g sugar

## Directions

1. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the vanilla
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar, pour them slowly onto the cornstarch, always rubbing out the lumps
3. Add to this paste, stirring briskly, as much of the boiling milk until the eggs are warmed through
4. Then pour everything back into the pan and let the sauce simmer on a gentle heat, stirring with a whisk, for 3-4 minutes
5. Remove the vanilla and stir occasionally while cooling to prevent a skin from forming
6. Serve the sauce cold with chocolate pudding, lemon rice etc., warm with warm bread pudding, ginger pudding etc.
