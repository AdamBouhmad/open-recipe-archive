## Ingredients

- 150 g brown beans
- 1/2 kg cooked potatoes
- 200 g onions
- 40 g butter or margarine
- Marmite
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the washed and 24 hours soaked beans in the known manner until tender and mash them.
2. Lightly fry the chopped onions in the butter or margarine until light brown.
3. Make the cooked potatoes fine.
4. Mix beans, onions and potatoes together.
5. Moisten the mass with the bean cooking liquid.
6. Season the whole with salt and marmite (mixed with water).
7. Place the mass in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with a little water.
8. Finish the dish as indicated in recipe 85.
