## Ingredients

- Rib roast
- Salt
- Beef fat from the breast
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Jus
- Potatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Pound and salt the rib roast as needed.
2. Lard it with fat from the breast, rolled in cloves, pepper and salt, and roast the piece in an oven, on a spit or in an iron pot, continually basting with the jus.
3. If you want potatoes with it, cook them first half done, and then roast them with the rib.
4. When serving, arrange them on the dish around the meat.
