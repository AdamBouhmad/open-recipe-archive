## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 175 to 200 g old bread with or without crust
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil with the salt.
2. Cut the bread into small cubes and put it in the milk and let it cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Serve brown sugar and if desired some fine cinnamon with the pudding.
