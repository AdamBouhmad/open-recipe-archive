## Ingredients

- 1 l light bouillon or water with salt
- 200 g fresh mushrooms
- 40 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt
- lemon juice
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms as described in recipe 477
2. Braise them in 15 grams butter or margarine with salt and lemon juice (5 minutes)
3. Add the liquid and let the mushrooms cook another 15 minutes
4. Melt 35 grams butter or margarine, add the flour and bring it to a boil while stirring
5. Rub out the lumps and add the liquid
6. Let the soup cook while stirring occasionally for 10 minutes
7. Warm the mushrooms in it
8. Pour the soup while stirring vigorously into the egg yolk, which has been beaten in the soup tureen
