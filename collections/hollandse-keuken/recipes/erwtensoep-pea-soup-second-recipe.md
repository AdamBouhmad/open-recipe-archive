## Ingredients

- 1 beef tail
- 100 g ground veal
- 4 l water
- 30 g salt
- 400 to 750 g split peas or pearl peas
- 4 leeks
- 1 bunch celery greens
- 1 small celeriac

## Directions

1. Brush the tail well in lukewarm water and let it cook gently with 4 l water for 2 hours
2. Add the washed peas and cook everything together for another 3 hours, filling the liquid from time to time
3. Remove the tail from the soup and cut off the pieces of meat
4. In the meantime, clean the vegetables, wash them, and cut them fine
5. Let them cook in the soup for approximately 1/2 hour
6. Stir from time to time to prevent burning and add water to the soup if needed
7. Add the meatballs, made in the usual way, 10 minutes before serving
8. Serve with toasted or baked bread cut into dice
