## Ingredients

- piece of cold roasted lamb
- jus
- tablespoon capers
- lemon
- flour
- lump of butter
- salt
- calf bouillon

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cold roasted lamb and cut it into pieces
2. Add some of the jus to it
3. Add a tablespoon of capers, a lemon, a lump of butter rolled in flour, salt and some calf bouillon
4. Braise everything together well
