## Ingredients

- pheasant legs
- parsley
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Cut the claws from the leftover pheasant legs.
2. Prepare these with finely chopped parsley and wrap them in buttered paper.
3. Place them on the grill and eat with a lemon sauce.
