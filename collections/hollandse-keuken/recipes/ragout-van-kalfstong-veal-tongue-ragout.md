## Ingredients

- 1 veal tongue
- 1/2 l bouillon in which mace and Spanish pepper have been infused
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 1/2 dl cream or milk
- 1/4 dl white Bordeaux wine
- 75 g ground veal
- 1 small tin of mushrooms
- a couple of drops of lemon juice if desired
- 1 to 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue until done in the way as described in recipe 267, but let the spices cook along.
2. After it has been stripped of its skin, cut it into cubes.
3. Cook the meat balls separately in some boiling water or tongue bouillon until done (10 minutes).
4. Prepare the sauce in the usual way, warm in it the pieces of tongue, the mushrooms and meat balls and add these from the heat to the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the cream and wine.
5. Stir some lemon juice through and fill pastries, fried items etc. with it.
