## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 30 g butter or margarine
- pepper, salt and Maggi's Aroma to taste
- finely chopped green herbs such as parsley, tarragon, pimpernel, chives, chervil (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with pepper, salt, Maggi's Aroma and butter in the small pan, add (optionally the chopped herbs and) the milk and stir over gentle heat until they begin to set and a porridge-like mass forms.
2. Serve with salad or with bread or as garnish for some dishes.
