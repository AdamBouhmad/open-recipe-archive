## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 300 g sugar
- 50 g snippered almonds
- 2 1/2 dl whipped cream with powdered sugar and vanilla pulp

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg whites as stiff as possible, mix them with half of the sifted sugar and pipe from this through a piping bag with a smooth nozzle onto a greased and flour-dusted baking sheet, on which with a lid or bowl 4 circles are marked, 3 rings.
2. Pipe the 4th circle, starting in the middle, completely full, to serve as the base of the polonaise.
3. Let the egg white in a very lukewarm oven dry (1 1/2 to 2 hours), but see to it that it remains white in color.
4. Then beat the remaining egg white stiff, mix it with the remaining sugar and pipe a ring of it on the edge of the base.
5. Lay a hard ring on it, pipe another soft ring on it and continue thus until the last dried ring is laid on it.
6. Now spread the outside and inside of the polonaise as smoothly as possible with the remaining egg white, sprinkle the outside with snippered, light brown toasted almonds and let the polonaise in a very lukewarm oven become dry and crisp (1 1/2 to 2 hours), but see to it that it remains as light as possible in color.
7. Fill it with whipped cream, mixed with powdered sugar and vanilla pulp or with ice.
