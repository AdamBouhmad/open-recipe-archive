## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 l milk
- 90 to 120 g tapioca
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the tapioca and soak it for 3 hours in the milk with the salt.
2. Bring the porridge to a boil while stirring and then let it cook on a gentle fire, stirring occasionally, until cooked and thickened (approximately 1 1/2 hours).
