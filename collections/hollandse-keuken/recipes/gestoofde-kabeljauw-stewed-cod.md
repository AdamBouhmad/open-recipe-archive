## Ingredients

- cod
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces
2. Wash them clean
3. Let them cook in boiling water with sufficient salt, skimming well
4. When it has boiled over three times, it is done
5. Cook the liver separately
6. Serve with creamed butter with mustard
