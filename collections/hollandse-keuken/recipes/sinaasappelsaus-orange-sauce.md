## Ingredients

- juice of 4 oranges and 1 lemon, made up with water to 1/2 liter
- 20 g sago
- approximately 75 g sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the peel of 1 well-washed orange on an enamelled or glass grater
2. Bring the water with the sugar and the orange peel to a boil
3. Pour in the sago mixed with cold water and let the sauce simmer while stirring briefly
4. Add the sieved fruit juice
5. Present it with semolina or cornstarch pudding
