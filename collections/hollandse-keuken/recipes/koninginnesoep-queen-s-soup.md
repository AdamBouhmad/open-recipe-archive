## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg chicken pullet
- 1 liter water
- salt
- mace
- Spanish pepper
- carrot
- sprig of parsley
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dl cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the soup as described in recipe 75, but do not use rice and finish the soup with the yolks, which have been beaten with the cream.
