## Ingredients

- brains from three calf heads
- water
- a little vinegar
- white or brown sauce
- vinegar
- eggs
- or bouillon and vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the brains from three calf heads and clean them very well
2. Remove the thin membrane around them
3. Remove the fibers that are in them
4. Place them in boiling water with a little vinegar and let them simmer, taking care that they stay whole
5. Dish them up and pour a white or brown sauce over them, made with vinegar and eggs, or with bouillon and vinegar
