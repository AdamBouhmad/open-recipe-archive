## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- anchovy or herring
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- bay leaves
- wine vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the leg and beat it gently
2. Lard it with anchovy or herring
3. Sprinkle the leg with pepper, salt and cloves
4. Place it with some bay leaves for 6 days in wine vinegar
5. After that, braise it with the marinade in a pan until done
6. If the liquid is too sharp, thin it with some water
