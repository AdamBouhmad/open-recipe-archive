## Ingredients

- Two ounces butter
- Half pound finely pounded almonds
- Two ounces sugar
- Juice and peel of one lemon
- Ten egg yolks
- Half pound powdered chocolate
- White of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Mix together well in order: two ounces butter, half pound finely pounded almonds, two ounces sugar, juice and peel of one lemon, ten egg yolks, and half pound powdered chocolate
2. Beat the white of eight eggs
3. Add it to the mixture
