## Ingredients

- yellow endive heads
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cut endive to the size of a finger joint
2. Wash clean and let drain
3. Line the pot or barrel with a pillowcase
4. Place endive in the pot, salting it slightly less than for string beans
5. Place a cloth and fitted board with stone on top
6. Every 14 days, clean the cloth and stone
7. To eat, soak and stew as if fresh endive
