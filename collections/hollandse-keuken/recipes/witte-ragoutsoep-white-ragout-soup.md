## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon made from veal shank meat
- piece of mace
- piece of Spanish pepper
- pinch of thyme
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 50 g veal mince
- small pieces of sweetbread and calf's tongue
- 3/4 tin of mushrooms with liquid
- 1 egg yolk
- some cream
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make bouillon from the veal meat with the spices in the usual way.
2. Let it cool, skim off the fat and strain it.
3. Cut a small portion of calf's tongue and sweetbread, which have been cooked in the usual way and carefully freed of fat and skin, into small pieces.
4. Mix the mince in the usual way.
5. Form very small balls from it and cook these for 10 minutes in some water.
6. Remove them from the liquid with a skimming spoon.
7. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring it to the boil while stirring, gradually add the strained bouillon and let the soup cook while stirring for 10 minutes.
8. Then briefly warm the meat in it, carefully pour the soup to the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the cream.
9. Add the mushrooms, either whole or cut in half, the liquid from the mushrooms and if desired some lemon juice.
