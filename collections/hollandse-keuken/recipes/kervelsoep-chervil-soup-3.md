## Ingredients

- 1 l bouillon or water with salt
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped chervil
- (1 egg)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine and bring it to a boil while stirring with the flour
2. Add the liquid gradually in small portions while stirring and let the soup cook until done (10 min.)
3. Remove from heat and mix in the fine chervil and carefully add to the egg, which has been beaten in the soup tureen, stirring vigorously
