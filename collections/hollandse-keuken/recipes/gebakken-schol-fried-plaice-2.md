## Ingredients

- 4 nice frying plaice
- butter or salad oil
- flour or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, wash the fish well inside and out, score it, rub it well with salt on both sides and let it lie on a colander for half an hour so that the salt can be absorbed.
2. Make enough oil in the frying pan hot so that the bottom is well covered.
3. Ensure that blue steam rises from the fat.
4. After drying, roll the fish on both sides through flour or milk.
