## Ingredients

- Rice
- Currants
- White wine
- Good sour apples
- Ground cinnamon
- Butter
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. For every half Dutch pound of rice, take half that amount in currants and 1 bottle of white wine, along with some good sour apples
2. Let it cook slowly
3. If it becomes too thick, add a little water
4. When cooked, stir in some ground cinnamon, butter and sugar
