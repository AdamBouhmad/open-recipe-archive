## Ingredients

- 6 bananas
- 2 1/2 dl water
- juice of 1 lemon
- approximately 60 g sugar
- optionally a splash of Rhine wine
- some sago

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas, cut them lengthwise in half, then cut each half into 4 pieces.
2. Bring the water with the lemon juice to a boil, place the pieces in it and let them cook until completely soft (7-10 minutes).
3. Remove them carefully from the liquid, add the wine and bind it with some mixed sago.
4. Pour it over the banana and serve this cold.
