## Ingredients

- 2 l milk
- 100 g sugar
- 50 g Blooker's cacao

## Directions

1. Stir the cacao with the sugar together and gradually mix with a little cold milk to form a thin paste without lumps.
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil, pour the mixed cacao into it while stirring and let the milk simmer for 5 minutes while stirring.
3. Pour it into the chocolate kettle.
