## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg liver
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Slice the liver into finger-thick slices, remove the blood vessels and membranes, wash it and rub it with some pepper and salt.
2. Roll it through some flour and fry it in the brown butter in 3-4 minutes until done and only lightly colored.
