## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces chocolate powder
- 1 pint boiling milk
- 6 lod sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 2.5 ounces chocolate powder into 1 pint boiling milk until completely dissolved
2. Add 6 lod sugar
3. Let it boil a bit more
4. It should be bound
