## Ingredients

- half Dutch pound macaroni
- water
- good veal bouillon

## Directions

1. Take half a Dutch pound of macaroni and cook it almost done in water
2. Then let this macaroni cook for another half hour in good veal bouillon
3. Take care to keep the tubes as whole as possible
