## Ingredients

- Strong coffee
- Cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Take strong coffee and put it in a can of cream in which 6 egg yolks and 4 ounces sugar have been beaten
2. Pour it into forms and let it become done in the oven
