## Ingredients

- 1 kg cherries
- approximately 2 dl water
- 1 dl berry juice
- approximately 100 g sugar
- some sago

## Directions

1. Prepare it as strawberry compote, but add the berry juice to the sugar syrup after the cherries have stewed in it and thicken the sauce with mixed sago.
2. Serve the compote for example with pigeon.
