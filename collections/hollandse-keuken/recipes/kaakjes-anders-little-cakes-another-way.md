## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- 3 ounces melted butter
- ¼ pound sugar
- ¼ pound currants
- cold butter for kneading

## Directions

1. Knead 1 pound wheat flour, 3 ounces melted butter, ¼ pound sugar, and ¼ pound currants with cold butter.
2. Form into flat balls and bake thoroughly in a tart pan.
