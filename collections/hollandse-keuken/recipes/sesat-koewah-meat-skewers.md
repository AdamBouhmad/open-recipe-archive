## Ingredients

- 500 g pork cut into cubes
- 3 tablespoons ground onions
- 3 garlic cloves
- ½ teaspoon coriander
- ½ teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon laos
- 1 chili pepper
- 1 teaspoon trassi
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 spoon assem water
- 2 teaspoons fine turmeric
- 1 cup milk or thick coconut milk
- 1 citrus leaf
- butter, salad oil, beef fat or frying fat

## Directions

1. Pound the spices, except for the citrus leaf, with the trassi and finely ground onions
2. Mix them with the meat
