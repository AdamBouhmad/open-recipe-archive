## Ingredients

- 200 g veal chicken
- 1 liter boiling water
- 50 g veal ground meat
- 35 g vermicelli
- 1 Maggi's bouillon cube
- piece of mace
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the washed chicken in the boiling water with salt and mace and let it cook gently for 1½ hours.
2. Cut the meat into neat pieces and remove the mace.
3. Add the washed and broken vermicelli and let it cook for a half hour.
4. Add the meatballs prepared in the usual manner (recipe 37) ten minutes before serving and dissolve the bouillon cube in the soup.
5. Beat the egg in the soup tureen and carefully add the soup to it while stirring vigorously.
