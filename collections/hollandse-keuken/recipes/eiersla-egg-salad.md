## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- mayonnaise
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs hard (8 to 10 minutes), place them in cold water, peel them and cut them into slices with the aid of the so-called egg slicer
2. Make sure the mayonnaise tastes sharp, otherwise stir in some more mustard, pepper and salt
3. Carefully mix the egg slices with some mayonnaise, place them on the plate and sprinkle some fine parsley over them
