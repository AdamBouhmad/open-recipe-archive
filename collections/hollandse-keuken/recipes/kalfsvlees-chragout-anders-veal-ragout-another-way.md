## Ingredients

- Roasted veal
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Flour
- Chopped onions
- Wine vinegar or lemon
- Meat drippings
- Pepper
- Mace

## Directions

1. Take roasted veal and cut it with mushrooms into small pieces
2. Roast a lump of butter, stir in as much flour as needed for the ragout
3. Add a couple finely chopped onions
4. Let them braise together until brown
5. Add a couple spoons wine vinegar or some lemon, some meat drippings, pepper and mace
6. Put the meat and mushrooms in
