## Ingredients

- young chickens
- melted butter
- small onions
- parsley
- mushrooms
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- bound sauce

## Directions

1. Cut young chickens in half
2. Singe them over the fire with some melted butter, two small onions, parsley and mushrooms, all finely chopped, and pepper and salt
3. Subsequently cover it with breadcrumbs and roast it over a bright fire
4. Serve with a bound sauce (see under sauces)
