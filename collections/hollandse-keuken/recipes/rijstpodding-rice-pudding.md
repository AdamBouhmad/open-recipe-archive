## Ingredients

- 5 ounces rice
- sweet milk
- 6 eggs
- 2.5 ounces melted butter
- juice of a couple lemons
- grated lemon zest
- candied citrus peel
- cinnamon
- bread sugar
- cream or butter sauce

## Directions

1. Cook 5 ounces rice in sweet milk until tender and dry
2. make a batter with 6 eggs, 2.5 ounces melted butter, lemon juice, grated lemon zest, candied citrus peel, cinnamon and bread sugar
3. put in a mold as for bread pudding
4. let it cook vigorously for 1 hour
5. serve with cream or butter sauce
