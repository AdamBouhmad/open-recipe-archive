## Ingredients

- two pounds of sour cherries
- 3 pounds of cinnamon
- 3 pounds of cloves
- 8 pounds of pepper
- 3 pounds of cardamom
- 5 ounces of brown sugar
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries, wash and place in a large bottle
2. Take 3 pounds of cinnamon, 3 pounds of cloves, 8 pounds of pepper, 3 pounds of cardamom and 5 ounces of brown sugar, all coarsely ground
3. Pour a jug of wine vinegar over all of this, place in a tinned pan and let it cook a little; it must be well skimmed
4. When the juice has turned blue, pour it over the cherries, shaking them once or twice daily for 14 days
