## Ingredients

- Beef
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Beat the meat well and rub it in with salt and pepper
2. Put a small piece of butter in the pan and let it brown well
3. Lay the beefsteak in and prick it until it is browned enough on one side
4. Turn it over; you do not need to prick it anymore
5. The pan cannot be too hot
