## Ingredients

- vanilla ice cream
- 1 fresh, peeled and pitted peach half
- raspberry purée made from raspberries rubbed through a hair sieve and seasoned with sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Fill an ice bowl 3/4 full with vanilla ice cream.
2. Place a fresh, peeled and pitted peach half in it, and pour some raspberry purée over it, obtained by rubbing carefully selected and washed raspberries through a hair sieve and seasoning with sugar.
3. Garnish the whole with whipped cream.
