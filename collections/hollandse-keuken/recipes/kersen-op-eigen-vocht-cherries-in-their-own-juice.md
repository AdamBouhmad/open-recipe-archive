## Ingredients

- 3 kg cherries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries, wash them and remove the pits, but make sure all the juice is caught.
2. Put the cherries with the juice in well-cleaned bottles, scatter some sugar between them, close the bottle with ring, lid and clips and heat it for 20 minutes to 80° C.
3. Let the bottles cool with the clips on, remove the clips and label the bottles with name and date.
4. (For corked bottles see the rules on page 366).
