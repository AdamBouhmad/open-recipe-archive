## Ingredients

- 1 tin of lobster or 1 fresh lobster (cooked)
- 4 dessert spoons mayonnaise
- 4 dessert spoons tomato ketchup
- 1 spoon sherry
- a splash of cognac
- a few drops of lemon juice
- English sauce and pepper
- a few lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. For garnish, set aside 4 nice pieces of lobster separately
2. Cut the rest into pieces and divide them over 4 chilled coupes or glasses
3. Mix together mayonnaise, ketchup, sherry, cognac, lemon juice, English sauce and pepper
4. Adjust the mixture to taste and pour it over the lobster pieces
5. Garnish the glasses with a small lettuce leaf and nice pieces of lobster
