## Ingredients

- pheasant
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Lard the pheasant and keep the tail whole; also cut off the head.
2. Roast it on the spit, but be careful to cover it with paper smeared with butter and baste it well.
3. When done, fasten the head back on, attach the tail and present it.
