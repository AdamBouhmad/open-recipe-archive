## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- salt
- 3 to 3.5 dl warm milk
- 20 g yeast
- approximately 400 g apricots
- approximately 60 g sugar
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with enough warm milk so it no longer clouds, stir out the lumps, add the remaining milk, salt and the mixed yeast and let it rise covered (approximately 3 to 4 hours).
2. Soak the apricots, after washing them, for 24 hours in cold water, cook them in this water for 5 minutes, let them drain on a colander and dust them with the sugar, which should infuse while the apricots are still warm.
3. Treat the beignets further as indicated for apple beignets.
