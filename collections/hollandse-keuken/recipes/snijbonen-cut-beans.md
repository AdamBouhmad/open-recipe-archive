## Ingredients

- 1 kg cut beans
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 10 g sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Trim the beans on both sides twice, wash them and cut them with a bean cutter, ordinary knife or bean cutting mill into long, thin strips.
2. Set them on with enough boiling water to just cover the bottom of the pan and add salt.
3. Let them cook tender and dry, shaking occasionally (1/2 hour to 3/4 hour).
4. Add some water if necessary during cooking to prevent burning.
5. Shake them with the butter or margarine, add sugar if the beans are not sweet enough.
