## Ingredients

- 4 baking soles
- 1 dl salad oil or 75 g butter or margarine
- salt
- flour
- milk or breadcrumbs
- 1 egg white or half an egg

## Directions

1. First method: Clean the soles. If this has not been done by the fishmonger, do as follows: cut off the head, make an incision in the skin at the tail and strip it off all at once, remove the innards, cut off part of the tail and side fins, wash the fish well
2. Rub it on both sides with salt and let it lie salted on a colander for half an hour
3. Dry it, then coat it on both sides in flour or milk
4. Fry the sole in hot salad oil or browned butter on both sides until light brown and cooked through (when the fish is done, it will split along the side fins)
5. Let it drain on porous paper and serve with salad and mayonnaise
6. Second method: Clean the fish as described above. After the salt has penetrated and the fish is dried, first pass it through beaten egg or egg white mixed with water, then coat it in fine breadcrumbs
7. Fry it in the same way as above. When frying breaded fish, move it especially carefully by shaking the pan. Let the fish drain on porous paper and save the fat after straining it for another time
