## Ingredients

- 4 white cabbages
- 2 Savoy cabbages
- 60 g salt
- 12 black peppercorns
- Cologne pot
- cloth
- board
- stone

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the cabbages, cut them in half, remove the stalk and cut them with a cabbage knife or cabbage shaver into long, thin strips.
2. Knead the cabbage with the salt until brine has formed.
3. Add the peppercorns and press the cabbage as firmly as possible into a well-cleaned Cologne pot.
4. Cover it with a clean cloth, place a board and a stone on top.
5. Store it as indicated in the general instructions.
6. Use the sauerkraut only after 6 or 8 weeks.
