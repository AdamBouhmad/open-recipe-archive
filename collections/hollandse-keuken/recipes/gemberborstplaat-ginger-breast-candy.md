## Ingredients

- 500 g sugar
- 1 dl water
- 1 level teaspoon ginger powder
- 50 g candied ginger with syrup

## Directions

1. Stir the ginger powder through the sugar, add the water and let this sugar solution boil for 10 minutes.
2. Add the chopped ginger with syrup and let it cook for another 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Remove the breast candy from the heat, let it become thick and fluid while constantly stirring, and treat it further as described in the first breast candy recipe.
