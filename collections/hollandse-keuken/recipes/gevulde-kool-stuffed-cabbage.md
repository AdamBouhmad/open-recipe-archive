## Ingredients

- cabbage
- string
- veal, pork or beef mince
- butter
- nutmeg
- bouillon or jus

## Directions

1. Hollow out the cabbage and tie it with string to prevent it from falling apart
2. Cook it a couple of times
3. Place a ball of veal, pork or beef mince in the opening
4. Stew with butter, nutmeg and a little bouillon or jus
5. Cut into four pieces when serving
