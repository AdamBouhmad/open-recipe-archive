## Ingredients

- Some pieces of fish (haddock, mackerel)
- Salt
- Pepper
- 20 kemiri nuts
- 10 boiled lomboks
- 6 garlic cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon djienten
- 1 teaspoon ketoembar
- 1 teaspoon laos
- A small piece of trassi
- Lemon juice or assem water
- 1/2 l milk with salt or coconut milk
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Pepper and salt the pieces of fish and fry them in the butter or margarine
2. Pound the spices finely and fry them
3. Add the milk with salt or coconut milk to it
4. Let this mixture come to a boil and add the fish with assem water or lemon juice
5. The sauce should look red and be thickened
