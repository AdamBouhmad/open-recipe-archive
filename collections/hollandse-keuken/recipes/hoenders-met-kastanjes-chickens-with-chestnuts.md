## Ingredients

- chickens
- mace
- nutmeg
- chestnuts
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens half done.
2. Add mace, nutmeg, and peeled chestnuts cut into quarters, some of which should be grated.
3. Also add butter and let everything stew together.
