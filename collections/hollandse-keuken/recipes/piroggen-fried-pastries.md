## Ingredients

- 125 g flour
- 1/2 dl water with salt
- 1 egg
- 25 g butter or margarine
- preserves or meat or fish ragout for filling
- egg white
- frying fat or salad oil

## Directions

1. Beat the egg, add the water with salt, the sifted flour and the melted butter or margarine
2. Stir this dough over the fire until it comes away from the pan as a ball
3. Let it cool, roll it out on a flour-sprinkled board with a flour-dusted rolling pin to 1/2 cm thickness
4. Cut square pieces from it, with the edges brushed with some egg white or water
5. Place some preserves or meat or fish ragout in the middle, fold the pieces in half, seal the edges firmly together and fry them in frying fat or salad oil with light steam coming off until light brown and cooked through
6. Let them drip on gray paper, serve the savory piroggen as an appetizer, the sweet ones cold, sprinkled with powdered sugar
