## Ingredients

- beef from an ox shank
- beef bones
- water
- salt
- rice

## Directions

1. Have the meat cut from an ox shank and finely chop it
2. Crush the bones well
3. Cook them the day before for 3 hours with water and salt
4. Strain the liquid through a sieve
5. When ready to use, remove the fat
6. Set the obtained liquid to cook
7. Add rice and vegetables as desired
