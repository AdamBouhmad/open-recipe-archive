## Ingredients

- 3 kg garden beans
- salt
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour
- 25 to 30 g butter or margarine
- bean herb
- parsley

## Directions

1. Shell the beans and wash them
2. Set them on with abundant boiling water with a splash of milk (to keep the color white) and salt
3. Let them cook quickly until done (approximately 1/2 hour) and drain them in a colander
4. Prepare the sauce in the manner indicated for cauliflower sauce and let the beans stew in it (approximately 10 minutes)
5. Wash the bean herb and parsley, cut them fine and mix them with the beans just before serving
