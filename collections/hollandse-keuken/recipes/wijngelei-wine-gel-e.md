## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter red wine
- 1/2 liter water
- 8 cloves
- 1 lemon
- cinnamon stick (10 cm long)
- 150 g sugar
- 30 g sugar
- 30 g white gelatin
- 2 to 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Treat the wine gelée as described in recipe 555, after the wine has first been infused with lemon, cloves and cinnamon stick.
2. Pour the liquid through a doubled thick cloth into a pudding mold rinsed with cold water and let it cool.
3. Serve whipped cream with this pudding.
