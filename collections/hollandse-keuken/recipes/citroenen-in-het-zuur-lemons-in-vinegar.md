## Ingredients

- 10 lemons
- fine salt
- 16 bay leaves
- half a pound of ginger
- 2 bottles of best wine vinegar
- 4 Spanish peppers
- 1 pound of white mustard seed

## Directions

1. Cut 10 lemons crosswise, add fine salt on both sides and lay them on a plate for 14 days, turning daily
2. Place the lemons raw in a pot with 16 bay leaves and half a pound of ginger between them
3. Boil 2 bottles of best wine vinegar with 4 Spanish peppers and 1 pound of white mustard seed, and pour this boiling over the lemons
