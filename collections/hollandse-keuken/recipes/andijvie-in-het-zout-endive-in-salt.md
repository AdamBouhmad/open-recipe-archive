## Ingredients

- 10 kg endive
- 1 or 3 kg salt
- Cologne pot
- cloth
- board
- stone

## Directions

1. Clean the endive, cut it into thin strips, wash it until the water runs completely clear, let it drain as dry as possible on a colander.
2. Finish it as indicated in recipe 1017.
