## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- salt
- finely chopped parsley
- nutmeg
- 1 liter water
- 0.5 teaspoon Liebig's Meat Extract
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs well with a fork together, then stir in finely chopped parsley, a little nutmeg, salt, and 1 liter of water in which you have cooked 0.5 teaspoon of Liebig's Meat Extract with some salt.
2. Place this mixture in a pot lined with butter and set it in boiling water. After the course of half an hour it becomes thick; however, do not let it become hard.
3. Then cut off small dumplings and place them in prepared soup. The above is calculated for 8 persons.
