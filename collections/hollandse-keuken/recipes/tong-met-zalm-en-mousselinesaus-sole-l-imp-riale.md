## Ingredients

- 2 cooking soles
- 200 g fresh salmon
- salt
- 60 g butter or margarine
- 200 g butter or margarine for the sauce
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 1/2 dl whipped cream
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare fillets of the soles in the usual way, wash them and rub them with some salt.
2. Remove the skin from the salmon, cut it into 12 strips and wash these.
3. Roll a piece of salmon in each fillet, but ensure that it protrudes equally on both sides.
4. Bind the rolls with a thread and stew them over a gentle fire in the butter or margarine until done (approximately 15-20 minutes), but ensure the butter does not turn brown.
5. Remove the threads, place the fish on a warmed platter and cover them with the mousseline sauce, which is prepared in the usual way (see recipe 356 or 357).
6. Garnish the platter with fleurons.
