## Ingredients

- 1 l bouillon or salted water
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped chervil
- 1 egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine and bring it to a boil while stirring with the flour, add the liquid in small amounts at a time while stirring and let the soup cook until done (10 minutes).
2. Remove it from the heat and mix in the fine chervil and if desired add it to the egg that has been beaten in the soup tureen, stirring vigorously.
