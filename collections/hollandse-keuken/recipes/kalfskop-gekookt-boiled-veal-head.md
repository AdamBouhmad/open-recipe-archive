## Ingredients

- Veal head
- Salt
- Egg sauce
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Split the head in half and let it soak overnight in pump water
2. To cook it, bind it and hang it in plenty of cold pump water with a pair of handfuls of salt
3. Let it cook for 3½ hours, watching carefully for scum
4. Serve with egg sauce and grated horseradish
