## Ingredients

- Water
- Butter
- Vinegar
- One tablespoon fried flour
- Syrup or brown sugar
- A splash of Rhine wine
- Fine ginger
- Crushed cloves
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Mix water, butter, and vinegar with one tablespoon fried flour
2. Add syrup or brown sugar, a splash of Rhine wine, fine ginger, crushed cloves, and lemon slices
3. Stir everything well and let it cook until it has proper thickness
