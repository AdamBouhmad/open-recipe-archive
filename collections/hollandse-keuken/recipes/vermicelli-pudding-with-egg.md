## Ingredients

- vermicelli pudding (see recipe 603)
- vanilla stick or lemon zest
- sugar
- egg yolks
- egg white

## Directions

1. Cook vermicelli pudding in the usual manner (see recipe 603), but cook the vanilla or lemon zest in with it.
2. When the pudding is done, mix it with half of the sugar and remove the flavoring.
3. Stir the egg yolks with the remaining sugar, carefully add the hot vermicelli pudding to it and lastly mix it as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Finish the dish as indicated in recipe 602.
