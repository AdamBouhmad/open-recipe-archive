## Ingredients

- St. John's onions
- wine vinegar
- mace
- white pepper
- salt
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. After carefully removing the outer skin, lay them in wine vinegar
2. Remove them and place in a wide bottle, scattering between them a layer of mace, white pepper, salt and bay leaves
3. Boil the vinegar in which they lay and pour it over them boiling, so that they are well covered
