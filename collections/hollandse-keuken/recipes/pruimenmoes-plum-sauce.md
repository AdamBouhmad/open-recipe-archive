## Ingredients

- 1 kg plums
- 100 g sugar
- some water
- some sago

## Directions

1. Wash the plums, remove the pits, but make sure all the juice is caught.
2. Set them on with some water and let them cook gently (about 10 minutes).
3. Sieve the sauce, add the sugar and bind it with a little mixed sago.
