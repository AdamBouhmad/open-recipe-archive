## Ingredients

- 2 to 4 kg venison loin
- approximately 300 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt it and roast it brown and done in the butter or margarine, 30 to 35 minutes per 500 g.
2. Finish the gravy in the usual way.
3. Before serving, loosen the meat on both sides of the spine and cut the thin turned-back part from the thick part.
4. Turn the loin over and remove the narrow muscle pieces from the inside.
5. Cut the thin parts into pieces and place them under the bone so that it lies well and firmly.
6. Place the two thick pieces, which have been cut into thick slices, back in their original form on the bone.
7. Pour a little gravy over the meat.
