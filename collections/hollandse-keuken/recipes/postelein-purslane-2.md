## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg purslane
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 1 egg or 10 g maizena (optional)

## Directions

1. Carefully sort through the purslane, cut off the roots if necessary, and wash until the water that comes off is completely clear.
2. Cut it, place it with as little water as possible and let it cook until tender, stirring occasionally (10 to 15 minutes).
3. Let it drain as dry as possible in a colander, braise it for 5 minutes with butter or margarine and salt.
4. If necessary, bind the purslane with the mixed maizena, which must cook for 3 minutes, or with the egg, which is beaten in the dish and to which the hot purslane is carefully stirred.
