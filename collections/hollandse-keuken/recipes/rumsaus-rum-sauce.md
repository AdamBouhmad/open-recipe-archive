## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 60 g bastard sugar
- 1 dl rum
- 1 dl water
- juice of 1/2 lemon or orange
- zest of 1/2 lemon or orange

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as wine sauce (recipe 765).
2. Serve it with warm bread pudding, almond cake pudding etc.
