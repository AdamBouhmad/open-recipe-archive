## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- 8 to 10 balls of garlic
- 10 anchovies in strips
- garlic
- bouillon
- butter
- flour
- large glass brown jus
- bouillon

## Directions

1. From a leg of lamb, remove the membranes as much as possible
2. Lard it with 8 to 10 balls of garlic and 10 anchovies plucked into strips
3. Roast it on the spit until done
4. Make a sauce from 20 balls of garlic cooked in bouillon until nearly done, fried in butter with flour, a large glass of brown jus and an equal amount of bouillon in which the garlic was cooked
5. Let it all cook well together and serve the leg in it
