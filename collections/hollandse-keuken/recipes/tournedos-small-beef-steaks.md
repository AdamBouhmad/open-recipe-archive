## Ingredients

- 4 or 8 small beef steaks from the tip of the loin at 50 or 75 g per piece (somewhat elongated in shape)
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 5 g salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare tournedos as chateaubriands, but allow 3-4 minutes cooking time
