## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2½ dl milk
- some salt
- 50 g Duryea maizena
- 225 g flour
- 40 g sugar
- scraped vanilla or fine cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the salt and flavoring.
2. Add the flour, maizena and enough milk until the flour no longer dusts.
3. Stir out the lumps from this batter and add the remaining milk.
4. Treat the fritters further as sweet fritters (recipe 168).
