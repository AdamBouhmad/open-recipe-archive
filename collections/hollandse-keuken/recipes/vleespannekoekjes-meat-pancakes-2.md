## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 100 g flour
- 2 dl milk
- salt
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 200 g cold meat (preferably veal or half veal, half pork)
- milk
- meat jus with water or bouillon
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- 25 g onion
- 15 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Mince or grind the meat finely.
2. Cook the chopped onion in butter or margarine until done, ensuring it remains light in color.
3. Mix the meat with the stewed onion and fat (if desired, pressed through a sieve), the liquid, and the spices, and let this mixture stew slowly on a low heat.
4. Beat the eggs with some salt, add the flour and the smallest half of the milk.
5. Stir out the lumps from this batter and gradually add the remaining milk.
6. Bake eight small pancakes from this batter in a pancake pan on both sides until light brown and cooked through.
7. Place a little of the meat mixture on half of each pancake and fold the other half over it.
8. Arrange the pancakes in a star pattern on a round plate with some fresh parsley sprigs between them.
