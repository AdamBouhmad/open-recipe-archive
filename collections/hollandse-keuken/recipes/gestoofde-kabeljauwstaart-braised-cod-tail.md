## Ingredients

- one cod tail
- salt
- water
- pepper
- nutmeg
- crushed biscuit
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let the tail hang one day and night in open air or in a clear cellar
2. Place it in a deep tin dish
3. Sprinkle salt over it and add enough water so it is well covered
4. Cook until done
5. Pour off the water leaving two teacups worth
6. Split the tail open and remove the bone
7. Add pepper, nutmeg, crushed biscuit and lemon juice
8. Apply heat above and below and let it braise thus
