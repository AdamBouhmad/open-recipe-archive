## Ingredients

- 2 pork feet
- 500 g fresh sausage or 50 g butter or margarine
- 750 g green peas
- 4 l water
- 30 g salt
- 4 leeks
- 1 bunch celery greens
- 1 small celeriac
- 2 potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the peas, soak them overnight in water, and cook them the next day in the same water gently until tender (approximately 2 hours)
2. Pour them with the liquid through a horsehair sieve and rub the peas through until only the skins remain
3. Cook the washed feet in this liquid for one hour, the washed and pricked sausage for half an hour (this last is then removed and reheated in the soup just before serving)
4. Add the cleaned, finely cut, and very well washed vegetables and potatoes and let everything cook together gently, stirring occasionally, until the soup is evenly bound and the meat leaves the bone
5. Fill the soup from time to time with a little water
6. Serve with rye bread
