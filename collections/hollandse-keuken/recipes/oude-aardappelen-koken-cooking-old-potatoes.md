## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg potatoes
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Select potatoes of equal size, peel them as thinly as possible, remove all eyes with the point of a knife and place them immediately in cold water
2. Wash them several times, check again that all eyes are removed and set them on with little boiling water and some salt
3. Shake the potatoes occasionally
4. Let them cook until they can be easily pierced with a fork, but not too easily (20 to 25 minutes)
5. Drain them as dry as possible; if they are not approximately dry-cooked, let them dry further on the fire without a lid, shaking occasionally to make them floury
