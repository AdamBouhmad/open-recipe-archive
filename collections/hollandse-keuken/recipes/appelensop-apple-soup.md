## Ingredients

- sour apples
- butter
- slices of day-old white bread
- sugar
- cinnamon
- white wine
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the sour apples and cut them into slices.
2. Melt some butter in a pan.
3. Lay a layer of slices of day-old white bread upon it, then a layer of apple slices with sugar, cinnamon, and butter, and so on until the pan is full.
4. Pour in some white wine and water.
5. Let it stew until done with fire under and above.
6. Some add rye bread and use bouillon or the liquid from cooked salted meat instead of water and wine.
