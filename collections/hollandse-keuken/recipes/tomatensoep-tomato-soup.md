## Ingredients

- 1 l water with salt or bouillon
- 8 tomatoes or 1/2 kg small soup tomatoes
- 8 white peppercorns
- 75 g onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- (some lemon juice)

## Directions

1. Clean the tomatoes, wash them, crush them and let them cook finely in the bouillon or boiling water with the salt, peppercorns, bay leaf and chopped onion (10-15 min.)
2. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve, rub the tomatoes through as much as possible
3. Add gradually while stirring vigorously to the melted butter or margarine mixed with the flour and brought to a boil, from which the lumps have been rubbed out
4. Let the soup cook while stirring until done (+ 10 min.), add some lemon juice if necessary
