## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter haché according to recipe 89
- 200 g rice

## Directions

1. Prepare haché as indicated in recipe 89
2. Cook the rice in the usual manner
3. Line a butter-greased warm pudding mold along the bottom and sides with rice
4. Place the haché, with the herbs removed, in the middle and lay a layer of rice on top
5. Close the mold in the usual manner and cook the pudding 1 1/2 hours in a bain marie, ensuring the water is refilled to just below the rim of the mold
6. Turn the pudding out onto a warmed plate and serve warm
