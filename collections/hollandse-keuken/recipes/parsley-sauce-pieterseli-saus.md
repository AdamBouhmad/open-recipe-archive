## Ingredients

- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Fried flour and butter
- Fresh butter
- Finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Add fried flour and butter to bouillon or water with salt
2. Set it on the heat
3. While it comes to a boil, stir in a lump of fresh butter and finely chopped parsley
