## Ingredients

- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 tin pineapple with 1 1/2 to 2 dl liquid
- 75 g brown sugar
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 12 g white gelatine or 10 g powdered gelatine

## Directions

1. Cut the pineapple into pieces and remove pits if necessary.
2. Dissolve the gelatine (which has soaked 10 to 15 minutes in cold water) in the pineapple liquid.
3. Let this mixture cool somewhat and add it to the cream, which has been beaten stiff with the sugar.
4. Mix this with the stiffly beaten egg whites.
5. Stir the pineapple pieces and the scraped vanilla through it at the same time and let the mass stand, stirring from time to time, until it becomes jelly-like and the pineapple pieces no longer sink.
6. Then pour it into a mold brushed with unbeaten egg white and let the pudding become cold and firm.
7. Turn it out onto a damp plate.
8. Garnish the pudding with jelly made from pineapple liquid (see fruit jelly recipe 566) and with pineapple pieces.
