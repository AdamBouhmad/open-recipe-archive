## Ingredients

- sweet milk
- tea infusion
- saffron
- cinnamon
- cloves
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pour a decoction of good tea, saffron, cinnamon and cloves into sweet milk, let this cook together well, add sugar to taste; to bind stir a couple of egg yolks through it
2. If one uses slemp syrup, count a good spoonful of syrup for each can of milk
