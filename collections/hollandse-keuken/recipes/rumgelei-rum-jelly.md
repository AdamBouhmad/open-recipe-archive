## Ingredients

- six looten fish glue
- four ounces sugar
- juice of two lemons
- five small glasses best rum
- a glass clear water

## Directions

1. Take six looten fish glue and four ounces sugar
2. Add the juice of two lemons and five small glasses of best rum
3. Mix together with a glass of clear water
4. Strain through a fine silk sieve
5. Pour into the mold
6. Let it cool
