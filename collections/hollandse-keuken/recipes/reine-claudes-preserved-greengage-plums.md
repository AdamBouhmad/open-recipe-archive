## Ingredients

- 6 kg reine claudes
- Sugar syrup (500 g sugar per 1 liter water)

## Directions

1. Treat them entirely as plums (see recipe 972), but heat the bottles for 20 minutes to 70-80° C.
2. Let the bottles cool with the clips on, remove the clips and label the bottles with name and date.
