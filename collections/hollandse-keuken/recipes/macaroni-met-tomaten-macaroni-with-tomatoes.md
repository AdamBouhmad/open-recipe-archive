## Ingredients

- 200 g macaroni
- 0.5 kg tomatoes
- 50 g butter or margarine
- pepper and salt
- optionally 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces of 6-8 cm length, wash it and cook it until done in plenty of boiling salted water (1/2 hour).
2. Let the macaroni drain.
3. Wash the tomatoes, slice them and lay a layer in a buttered fireproof small dish.
4. Sprinkle some pepper and salt over it and place small pieces of butter or margarine here and there.
5. Lay the drained macaroni on it and along the edge the remaining tomatoes.
6. Sprinkle pepper and salt over the tomatoes, again place small pieces of butter or margarine here and there, sprinkle fine breadcrumbs over it, clean the rim of the dish and let it braise gently in a moderately warm oven for 1/2 hour, dripping occasionally with the liquid.
7. If desired, sprinkle very finely chopped parsley over it before serving.
