## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters beef bouillon
- 15 decigrammes shallots
- 30 decagrammes butter
- 30 decagrammes flour
- 1 tablespoon washed and chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped gherkins
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the shallots, wash them and chop them as well as the parsley.
2. Put them in a pan and add the butter and vinegar.
3. Set on the fire and stir with a spoon until the vinegar is completely reduced.
4. Add the flour and let it cook together for 2 minutes.
5. Add the 4 deciliters beef bouillon with some Liebig's Meat Extract, add a little pepper and let it cook gently for 15 minutes.
6. Finally add salt to taste.
