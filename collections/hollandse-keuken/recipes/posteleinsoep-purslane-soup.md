## Ingredients

- purslane leaves
- nutmeg
- bouillon
- two eggs
- potato starch

## Directions

1. Take a soup bowl of purslane leaves, wash them carefully
2. Add them with nutmeg to boiling bouillon in which two eggs and a tablespoon of potato starch have been stirred together beforehand
3. Let the whole cook a little more and the soup is ready
