## Ingredients

- 8 slices of casino bread (without crust)
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 to 2 tomatoes
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- mayonnaise with parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Toast the slices lightly brown on both sides.
2. Cut the washed tomato with a very sharp knife or tomato knife into thin slices.
3. Divide the egg using an egg slicer, a damp knife, or thread into slices.
4. Top each slice of bread with a tomato slice, sprinkle with a little fine salt, lay a slice of egg on top, sprinkle again with a little fine salt and cover with mayonnaise mixed with parsley.
5. Place the open sandwiches on a plate with a folded napkin and place a fresh sprig of curly parsley here and there.
6. Serve these open sandwiches as a starter or at coffee time.
