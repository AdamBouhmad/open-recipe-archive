## Ingredients

- 3 kg red and white berries or black berries
- 3/4 of the weight of the fruit juice in sugar
- Jam jars
- Parchment paper
- String
- Cellophane
- Elastic bands
- Labels

## Directions

1. Wash the berries well.
2. Let them steep with some water and allow the mass to drain through a horsehair sieve.
3. Weigh the juice or measure it (1/2 liter = 500 g) and cook until about a quarter has evaporated.
4. Add 3/4 of the original weight in sugar and cook until the jelly reaches the required thickness.
5. Treat further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
