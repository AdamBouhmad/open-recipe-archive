## Ingredients

- For the dough: 125 g hard butter or calf kidney fat
- 125 g flour
- salt
- water (slightly more than 1/2 dl)
- For the filling: 75 g ground veal
- 75 g ground pork
- 25 g old bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry (feuilletée) in the usual manner (see recipe 846)
2. Grind the meat once more through the meat grinder, mix it in the usual manner with the soaked bread and a generous amount of pepper, salt and nutmeg and make small rolls from it
3. Roll out the dough the last time into a fairly thin sheet, cut pieces from it with a width of approximately 10 cm and length of approximately 16 cm
4. Lay a meat roll on it, fold the dough around it and glue it from below and on the side with a little water
5. Lay the rolls with the sealed side down on a baking sheet, make shallow notches in the top with the blunt side of a knife and brush them with egg yolk beaten with a little water or with a little milk
6. Bake the rolls in a fairly warm oven until light brown and cooked through (approximately 20 minutes)
