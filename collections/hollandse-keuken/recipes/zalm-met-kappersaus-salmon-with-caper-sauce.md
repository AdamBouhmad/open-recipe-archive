## Ingredients

- one piece salmon
- oil
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onions
- butter
- flour
- capers

## Directions

1. Take a piece of salmon, place it in oil, parsley, salt, pepper and a little chopped onion
2. Let it cook afterwards, almost an hour, if it is very thick
3. Place it on the dish and pour over a sauce of butter with flour and capers
