## Ingredients

- one white cabbage
- water
- small piece of bacon
- two carrots
- two onions
- parsley
- rabbit

## Directions

1. Cut a white cabbage into four pieces and cook it in an iron pot with water and a small piece of bacon
2. Add two carrots, two onions and a little parsley
3. Take the rabbit and cut it into five pieces
4. If the rabbit is old, add it immediately; otherwise add it when the soup is half cooked
5. Let it cook for two and a half hours
6. Wild rabbits are to be preferred over tame ones
