## Ingredients

- 6 large cucumbers
- 2 spoons finely ground onions
- 1 spoon finely chopped garlic
- 1 teaspoon ginger powder
- 1 teaspoon laos powder
- 1 spoon fine turmeric
- 1 lombok without seeds
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 bottle vinegar
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut off the bitter ends, divide them into four parts lengthwise and then divide these lengthwise into eighths
2. Let the pieces stand overnight under salt with a heavy weight, such as a stone, on top
3. The next day remove the pieces, let them drain well and dry them with a clean cloth
4. Pound the spices with the onions — except the lombok — finely and fry them
5. Add the vinegar and the lombok and let this cook together briefly
6. Put the cucumber pieces in a well-cleaned stoppered bottle and pour the mass over them
7. Let the contents cool
8. Close the bottle airtight and let it stand for a few days, after which the atjar is suitable for use
