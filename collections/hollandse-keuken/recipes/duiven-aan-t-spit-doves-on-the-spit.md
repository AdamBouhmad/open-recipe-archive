## Ingredients

- doves
- bacon
- vine leaves
- butter

## Directions

1. Ensure the doves are well cleaned and singed.
2. Open them and place under each a strip of bacon with which to lard the dove, and a vine leaf.
3. Baste with butter and roast for only half an hour.
