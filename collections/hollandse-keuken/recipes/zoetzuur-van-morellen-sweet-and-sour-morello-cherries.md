## Ingredients

- 1 kg morello cherries
- 1 bottle pickling vinegar
- 1 kg sugar
- 30 cloves
- 10 g cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Wash the morello cherries, place them in a Cologne pot, pour the vinegar over which has been cooked with the spices and sugar
2. Close the pot with damp parchment paper, label it with name and date
3. Let the sweet and sour stand for at least 3 weeks before use
