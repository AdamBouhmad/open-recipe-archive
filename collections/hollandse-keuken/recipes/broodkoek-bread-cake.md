## Ingredients

- 2 ounces white bread
- 4 cups sweet milk
- eggs
- 4 cups melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook 2 ounces white bread in 4 cups sweet milk into a pulp
2. When somewhat cooled, stir in beaten eggs and 4 cups melted butter
3. Bake in a tart pan with heat below and above
