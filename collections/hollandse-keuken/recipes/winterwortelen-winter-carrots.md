## Ingredients

- 1 kg winter carrots
- 35 g butter or margarine
- salt
- finely chopped parsley
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the carrots, cut them into equal, elongated pieces, but do not place these in water as they will otherwise lose their sweet flavor.
2. Wash them quickly once they are all ready and place them in boiling water so that the bottom of the pan is well covered.
3. Let them cook until tender and dry, stirring occasionally (approximately 45 minutes).
4. Add water during cooking if needed to prevent burning.
5. Stew them for 5 minutes with the butter or margarine and shake in the fine parsley away from the heat.
