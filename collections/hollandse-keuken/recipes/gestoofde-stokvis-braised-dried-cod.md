## Ingredients

- beaten dried cod
- water
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- flour
- butter
- finely cut onions
- nutmeg
- crushed biscuit

## Directions

1. Soak the beaten dried cod one night in water
2. Cook it well and remove the bone
3. Place it in a pan with finely cut salt, pepper, mustard, flour, butter, finely cut onions, nutmeg and some crushed biscuit
4. Braise until the onions are well done
