## Ingredients

- 200 g shrimp
- 2½ dl milk or half cream, half milk
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or 18 g Duryea cornstarch
- fine parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Inspect the shrimp carefully and wash them in salted water.
2. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102 or recipe 329.
3. Mix it with the shrimp and the seasonings.
4. Fill the dampened shells with this mixture to about 3/4 full.
5. Finish them as indicated in recipe 102.
6. Note: When the shrimp have been peeled yourself, they do not need to be washed.
