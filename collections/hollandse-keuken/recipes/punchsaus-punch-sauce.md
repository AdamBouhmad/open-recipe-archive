## Ingredients

- 4 dl water and 1 dl punch, or 1 dl rum, 1 dl cognac and 3 dl water
- zest of half a lemon
- 150 g bastard sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 6 g maizena

## Directions

1. Bring the water with the very thinly pared lemon zest to a boil.
2. Mix the maizena gradually with the beaten egg yolks, add the sugar and enough of the boiling water until the egg yolks are thoroughly warmed.
3. Pour everything back into the pan and let the sauce, stirring vigorously, boil for 3-4 minutes.
4. Add the punch or cognac and rum off the fire, and present the sauce with warm semolina pudding, bread pudding etc.
