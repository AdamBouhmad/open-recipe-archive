## Ingredients

- 300 g perfect raspberries
- 4 dl water
- 20 g sago or potato flour
- 75 g sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as strawberry sauce (recipe 758).
2. Present the sauce with maizena, semolina pudding etc.
