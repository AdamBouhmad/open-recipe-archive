## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg braising lettuce
- 2 1/2 deciliters milk (or liquid from the lettuce)
- 25 g flour or 15 g Duryea maizena
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut away the withered leaves and a piece of the stem.
2. Cut the heads, starting from the stem, into thin strips.
3. Wash them repeatedly until completely clear water comes off.
4. Prepare the lettuce further exactly as endive (recipe 426).
