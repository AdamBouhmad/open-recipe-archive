## Ingredients

- 7-8 lobsters
- water
- butter
- veal bouillon
- nutmeg
- salt
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Take 7-8 lobsters, cook them with a little water done and remove the shells
2. Crush the shells very finely and fry them with butter
3. When brown, rub them through a sieve and put them with as much veal bouillon as you reckon necessary for soup in an earthen pan
4. Let them cook and add the previously cooked lobsters
5. When serving sprinkle grated nutmeg and salt to taste into it and serve it on slices of toasted bread
