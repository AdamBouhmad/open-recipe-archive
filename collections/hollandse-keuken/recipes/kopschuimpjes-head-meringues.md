## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 250 g sugar
- 1 spoon powdered sugar
- a few drops of carmine or 15 g cacao

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar (with the cacao) in the manner as described for borstplaat.
2. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, add to it, stirring briskly, the warm sugar solution and the sifted powdered sugar.
3. Let the mixture cool a little, pipe through a piping bag with fluted nozzle onto a butter-greased and flour-dusted baking sheet in tall meringues.
4. Let them in a very lukewarm oven slowly become dry and hard, but see to it that they remain as light as possible in color.
