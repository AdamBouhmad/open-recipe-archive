## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pluck the stems from the cherries and remove the pits.
2. Cook them with sugar, skimming well.
3. Remove the cherries from the liquid.
4. Rasp lemon peel into the liquid and cook it into a syrup.
5. Pour this syrup over the cherries when serving.
