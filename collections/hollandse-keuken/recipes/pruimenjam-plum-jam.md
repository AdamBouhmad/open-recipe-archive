## Ingredients

- 3.75 kg plums (+ 3 kg without pits)
- 2.25 kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- twine
- labels

## Directions

1. Wash the plums and remove the pits, put them with as much water as necessary to prevent burning during the first moments in a pan.
2. Let them cook down to a thick pulp.
3. Add the sugar.
4. Bring them to a boil again with the lid on and let the fruit cook without the lid, stirring occasionally, until the jam reaches the required thickness (see the rules on page 369).
5. Treat it further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
6. Note: For this jam, yellow, blue, or green plums can be used.
