## Ingredients

- half a bottle white currant juice or Rhine wine
- juice of 2 lemons
- grated lemon zest
- a piece of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the white currant juice or Rhine wine with the juice of 2 lemons and grated lemon zest, along with a piece of cinnamon
2. Let it cook a little longer while stirring well
3. Pour it quickly over
