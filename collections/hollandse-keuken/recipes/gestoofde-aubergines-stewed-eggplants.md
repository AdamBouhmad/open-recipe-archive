## Ingredients

- 4 small or 2 large eggplants
- 15 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- 2 small onions
- 2.5 dl tomato sauce
- 1.5 tablespoon very finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the eggplants.
2. Cut them into pieces of approximately 5 cm long.
3. Fry the chopped onions in the butter or margarine.
4. Add the tomato sauce (see recipe 340).
5. Stew the eggplant pieces in it for approximately 30 minutes.
6. Add the parsley at the end.
