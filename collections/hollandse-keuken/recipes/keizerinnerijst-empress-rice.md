## Ingredients

- 1/2 l milk
- 50 g rice
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 75 g sugar
- 2.5 dl whipped cream
- 8 g gelatin or 6 g powdered gelatin
- some maraschino

## Directions

1. Cook rice porridge in the usual way until very soft (recipe 577), but let the vanilla cook in it.
2. Mix it, just before the rice is done, with the sugar, but make sure the rice grains are not ground fine.
3. Remove the vanilla.
4. Remove from heat and dissolve the soaked and well-squeezed gelatin in it.
5. Let the mixture cool, add the maraschino.
6. Beat the cream stiff, add the rice porridge to it and let this mixture stand, stirring occasionally, until it becomes gelatinous and the rice grains no longer sink.
7. Pour it into a mold brushed with unbeaten egg white and let the pudding become cold and stiff.
