## Ingredients

- finely crushed hardtack
- water
- a few slices of lemon
- salt
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- meat broth (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook finely crushed hardtack with water until done
2. Add a few slices of lemon, a little salt and some sugar
3. When serving, stir an egg yolk through it
4. If the soup should be strengthening and the sick person may eat meat, use meat broth instead of water
