## Ingredients

- 5 ounces flour
- 1 and a half ounces butter, melted in warm milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 5 drams crushed sugar
- A little cinnamon
- Dissolved yeast
- Fine sliced almonds
- Bread sugar

## Directions

1. Warm flour dry
2. Mix with butter melted in warm milk
3. Add egg yolks, crushed sugar, cinnamon and dissolved yeast
4. Cut dough into pieces and form into cakes the thickness of a couple of guilders
5. Pour melted butter over them
6. Sprinkle with fine sliced almonds and bread sugar
7. Bake on a sheet in the oven until done
