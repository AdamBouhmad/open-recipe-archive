## Ingredients

- A reasonably fat piece of beef
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Salt a reasonably fat piece of beef well and place it in vinegar
2. When you want to use it, wash it first
3. Stud it with some cloves and put it on the spit, turning it constantly
4. Place a pan under the meat to catch the dripping jus
5. Baste the meat with the jus from time to time
