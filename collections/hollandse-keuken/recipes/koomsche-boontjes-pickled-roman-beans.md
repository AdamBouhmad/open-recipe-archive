## Ingredients

- roman beans
- butter brine

## Directions

1. Cook the beans
2. Let drain and cool
3. Place in a pillowcase or small linen bags in butter brine
4. Weight down so they remain submerged
5. To eat, soak beforehand and stew as fresh beans
