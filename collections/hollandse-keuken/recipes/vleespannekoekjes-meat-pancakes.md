## Ingredients

- 2 eieren (eggs)
- 100 g bloem (flour)
- 2 dl melk (milk)
- wat zout (salt)
- 40 g boter of margarine (butter or margarine)
- voor het vulsel: 200 g koud vlees (for the filling: cold meat, preferably veal or half veal, half pork)
- wat melk (milk)
- jus met water of bouillon (gravy with water or bouillon)
- peper (pepper)
- zout (salt)
- nootmuskaat (nutmeg)
- peterselie (parsley)
- 25 g ui (onion)
- 15 g boter of margarine (butter or margarine)

## Directions

1. Cut or grind the meat finely.
2. Make the chopped onion tender in the butter or margarine, but ensure that it remains light in color.
3. Mix the meat with the braised onion and the fat (optionally rubbed through a sieve), the liquid and the spices and let this mixture
