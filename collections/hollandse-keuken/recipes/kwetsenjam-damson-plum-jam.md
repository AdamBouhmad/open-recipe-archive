## Ingredients

- 3.75 kg damsons (+ 3 kg without pits)
- 2.25 kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- twine
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- labels

## Directions

1. Prepare the jam entirely as for plum jam (recipe 988).
