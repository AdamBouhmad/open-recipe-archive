## Ingredients

- ten to twelve lamb tongues
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaves
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take ten to twelve lamb tongues, fill them, wash them well clean and cook them for about half an hour
2. Then change the water and let them drain, dry them afterwards and cut off the stump
3. Lard the tongues, add parsley, thyme and bay leaves with bouillon and let them braise for five hours
