## Ingredients

- one calf's head
- rainwater
- salt
- onions
- butter
- half a bottle of Rhine wine
- juice of two lemons
- zest of one lemon
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Have a calf's head split in half and soak it overnight in rainwater
2. Bind it well together and cook it with some salt for three hours, skimming well
3. Remove it from the broth and cut the meat from the bones into small cubes
4. Add some finely chopped onions fried in butter
5. Stir it with a couple of spoonfuls of salt in an earthenware pan until it is brown
6. Add half a bottle of Rhine wine and the juice of two lemons
7. Grate the zest of one lemon over it, with salt, pepper and nutmeg
8. Stir everything well together and let it stew well for about half an hour
