## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 100 to 150 g Alkmaar barley
- salt
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Wash the barley and soak it overnight in 1/2 liter cold water and let it drain.
2. Bring the milk with salt to a boil.
3. Stirring, sprinkle in the soaked barley, continue stirring until the milk boils again.
4. Let the porridge cook gently on a soft fire, stirring occasionally, slowly until done (approximately 2 1/2 hours).
