## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix butter and flour in a pan
2. Bring it to a boil with water while stirring constantly
3. Remove from heat
4. Add a little salt, pepper and vinegar
