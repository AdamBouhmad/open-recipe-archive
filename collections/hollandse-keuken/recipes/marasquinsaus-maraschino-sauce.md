## Ingredients

- 2.5 dl cooking cream
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g sugar
- 6 g Duryea maizena
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 dl maraschino

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as vanilla sauce (recipe 755).
2. Add the maraschino off the fire.
3. Give the sauce with warm semolina pudding, fine bread pudding etc.
