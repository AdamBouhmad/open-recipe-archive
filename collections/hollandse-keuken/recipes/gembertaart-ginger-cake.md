## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- 200 g dark brown sugar
- 200 g flour
- 200 g wet ginger
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add alternately an egg yolk and some sugar until a completely smooth, airy mass is obtained.
2. Mix through this with a few strokes the sifted flour, the ginger cut into pieces with some syrup.
3. Beat the egg whites very stiffly and mix the batter through this.
4. Bake the ginger cake in a greased cake or tart pan.
5. For this, place the pan in a moderately warm oven and let it stand for approximately 1.5 hours.
6. This heavy and sweet cake becomes done more slowly than unfilled pastry.
7. Treat the ginger cake further as described in previous recipes.
