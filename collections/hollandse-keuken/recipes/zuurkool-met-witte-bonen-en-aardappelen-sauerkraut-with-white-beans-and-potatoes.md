## Ingredients

- 1 kg sauerkraut
- 1 kg potatoes
- 350 g white beans
- 60 g fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the washed and soaked beans in the usual manner (see recipe 493).
2. Add the washed sauerkraut to them and after 1/2 hour add the peeled, washed potatoes.
3. Add the fat and salt if needed.
4. Let everything cook together on a gentle fire until tender and almost dry (30 to 40 minutes).
5. Mash the dish lightly together.
