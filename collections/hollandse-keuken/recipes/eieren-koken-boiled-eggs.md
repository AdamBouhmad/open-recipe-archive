## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the eggs and place them carefully, either with a spoon or with an egg net, in ample boiling water.
2. Cook soft eggs 3 minutes, medium-hard eggs 5 minutes and hard eggs 8 minutes.
3. Cook duck eggs 12 minutes.
4. For very fresh eggs, cook 1 minute longer than usual.
5. Remove them from the water and present them hot with salt.
