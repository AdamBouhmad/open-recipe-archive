## Ingredients

- 2 pigeons
- 120 g veal forcemeat
- 4 truffels or some mushrooms
- some old bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 1/2 tablespoon Madeira (optional)
- 75 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Mix the forcemeat in the usual way, but stir in the chopped truffels, which may be soaked in Madeira if desired.
2. Carefully loosen the breast skin of the cleaned pigeons with your finger and fill the space between the breast meat and skin with the mixed forcemeat (not too stiff!).
3. Sew the skin closed again, salt the pigeons and roast them in the usual way (see recipe 318) until brown and cooked through.
4. Serve them with salad or compote.
