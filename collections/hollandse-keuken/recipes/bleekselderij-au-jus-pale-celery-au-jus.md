## Ingredients

- 2 bunches celery
- salt to taste
- 2 dl lean jus with water or bouillon
- 50 g butter or margarine
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the celery until tender, as described in the previous recipe.
2. Let it drain in a colander, place it in a fireproof small dish and pour the bouillon or lean jus with water over it.
3. Place small pieces of butter or margarine here and there, sprinkle some breadcrumbs over it and let the dish stew in the oven for approximately 15 minutes.
