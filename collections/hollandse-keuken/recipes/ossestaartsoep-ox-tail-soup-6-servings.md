## Ingredients

- 1 ox tail
- 1 1/2 l water
- 70 g butter or margarine
- 1 large carrot
- 3 onions
- bunch of celery greens
- 10 white peppercorns
- 3 bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- 60 g flour (for binding)
- 60 g butter or margarine (for binding)
- English sauce and soya to taste
- Madeira (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the tail, cut or chop it into pieces and brown them evenly in the butter or margarine with the chopped carrot, chopped onions and chopped celery
2. Put everything in a pan, add the herbs and water and let it cook gently for 2 hours
3. Fill up the bouillon from time to time to prevent it from reducing too much
4. Strain the bouillon, let it cool, skim off the fat and add it while stirring to the melted butter or margarine which has been brought to a boil together with the flour and rid of lumps
5. Let the soup simmer while stirring until cooked (approximately 10 minutes)
6. Add away from heat English sauce and soya or some Madeira and if desired a few fine pieces of meat from the tail
