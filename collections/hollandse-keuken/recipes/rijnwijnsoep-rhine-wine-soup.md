## Ingredients

- 1 bottle water
- 1 bottle Rhine wine
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons flour
- slices of lemon without pits
- bread sugar
- mace
- egg white
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 bottle water, 1 bottle Rhine wine, 6 egg yolks and 2 tablespoons flour
2. Add some slices of lemon without pits and sweeten the liquid with a sufficient amount of bread sugar
3. Set it on a strong fire, stirring until it comes to a boil
4. Now quickly pour it into a soup tureen in which you have previously put some mace
5. Beat some egg white into a stiff foam and lay it in flakes on the soup with a spoon
6. Sprinkle it thickly with bread sugar and cinnamon and close the tureen well, whereby the egg white becomes cooked by the rising steam of the soup
