## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower
- 1/2 liter milk or cauliflower water
- 30 to 40 g flour or 20 g Duryea maizena
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 15 g butter or margarine extra
- 60 g aged cheese

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and cook it until tender, as described in the previous recipe.
2. Remove the cauliflower from the pan with a skimming spoon, let it drain, and place it in a heat-proof dish.
3. Pour the sauce, which is prepared in the usual manner (see recipe 328 or recipe 329) and into which most of the grated cheese has been stirred, over it.
4. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over it, drizzle with the melted butter, and let it brown lightly in the oven.
