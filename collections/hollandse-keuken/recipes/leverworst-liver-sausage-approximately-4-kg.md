## Ingredients

- 2 kg pork or calf liver
- 2 kg pork meat
- 1 kg neck pork fat
- 40 g salt
- 50 g onions
- 1 tablespoon ground cloves
- 15 g pepper
- 2 dl meat bouillon
- beef intestines

## Directions

1. Cook the washed liver, washed meat and neck pork fat for 5 hours in plenty of boiling salted water
2. Grind everything as finely as possible, mix it with the spices, (the salt), the bouillon and the very finely chopped onions
3. Prepare the intestines, which have been soaked in lukewarm water for some time
