## Ingredients

- 1 kg sweet apples
- 1/2 dl berry juice
- 45 g sugar
- peel of 1/2 lemon or piece of cinnamon stick
- some sago

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters, remove the cores.
2. Wash them, set them on with enough boiling water so they are about 1/3 submerged with the flavoring agent.
3. Let them cook gently, shaking occasionally, for about 1/2 hour.
4. Add the berry juice and sugar, remove the flavoring agent and bind the liquid carefully with a little mixed sago.
