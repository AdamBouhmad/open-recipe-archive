## Ingredients

- Beef slices
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Take beef slices, place them beforehand for a couple of days in vinegar in which salt, pepper and cloves have been added.
2. Braise them in that liquid for two hours and then roast them in a dish with heat above and below.
