## Ingredients

- 300 g cold meat
- 50 g flour
- 50 g fat from gravy and 1/2 l water with 2 Maggi's Bouillon cubes, 1/2 l diluted lean gravy or 1/2 Maggi's Gravy tablet
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 cloves
- Piece of Spanish pepper (optional)
- 300 g onions
- 1/2 dl vinegar
- Maggi's Aroma

## Directions

1. Fry the chopped onions very light brown in the fat, add the flour and fry, stirring constantly, the flour and onions uniformly brown.
2. Add the liquid and the spices to this, let the sauce cook for 5 minutes while stirring and add the meat cut into small cubes and the vinegar.
3. Let the haché stew slowly on low heat for at least 1 hour, but stir occasionally to prevent burning.
4. Before serving, remove the spices and add, if desired, a little sugar and English sauce and soya or Maggi's Aroma.
5. If the sauce is too thin when serving, it can be bound with some mixed maize starch, which is allowed to cook for 3 minutes while stirring.
6. The amount of water to be added to the gravy depends entirely on how salty it is.
