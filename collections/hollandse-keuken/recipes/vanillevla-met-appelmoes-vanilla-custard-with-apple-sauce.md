## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter melk
- 40 g suiker
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 1 ei
- 1/2 stokje vanille or vanillesuiker
- 500 g appelen
- + 50 g witte suiker
- 1/2 dl water

## Directions

1. Make appelmoes in the known manner as described in recipe 531.
2. Let this cool and place the sauce in a custard dish.
3. Pour the cooled custard, prepared as in recipe 657, over it.
