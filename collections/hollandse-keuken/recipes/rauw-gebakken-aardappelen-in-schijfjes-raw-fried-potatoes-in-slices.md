## Ingredients

- 1 kg potatoes
- approximately 75 g fat or oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes in the usual way, wash them and slice them into not too thin slices.
2. Dry them in a cloth.
3. Lay the slices in the frying pan in which the fat has been warmed, sprinkle some fine salt over them and fry them on moderately warm heat until light brown and cooked (approximately 15 minutes).
4. Turn them with a knife or pancake turner from time to time.
