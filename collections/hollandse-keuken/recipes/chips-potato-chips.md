## Ingredients

- 1 kg potatoes
- fine salt
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes as thinly as possible, carefully remove the eyes and cut them into very thin slices.
2. Dry them firmly in a cloth.
3. Fry them in frying fat until cooked and golden yellow in small quantities at a time, because they stick together quickly and the fat cools down.
4. Let them drain briefly on brown paper and sprinkle them with fine salt.
