## Ingredients

- purslane
- bouillon
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After washing, cut the purslane small
2. Set it dry so that it melts and cooks in its own liquid
3. When done, pour off the liquid and add bouillon in its place, along with butter, salt and nutmeg
4. When serving, beat a couple of egg yolks through it
