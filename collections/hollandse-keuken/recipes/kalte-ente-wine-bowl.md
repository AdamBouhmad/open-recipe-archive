## Ingredients

- 1 bottle red Bordeaux
- 1 bottle Rhine wine or Mosel wine
- 40 g sugar
- peel of one lemon
- slice of lemon
- 1 bottle champagne or soda water

## Directions

1. Mix the red and white wine together.
2. Add the sugar, the peel of one lemon, a slice of lemon.
3. After a quarter hour, remove the peel and the slice.
4. Pour in the champagne or soda water and serve the bowl in crushed ice.
