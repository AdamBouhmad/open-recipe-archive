## Ingredients

- 2 pork tenderloins (approximately 250 g per piece)
- 75 g butter or margarine
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. For preparation see roastbeef. However, reckon approximately 25 minutes total as roasting time or bake them as beefsteak.
