## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork slice
- 3 dl boiling water
- 15 g salt
- 50 g lard, butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, rub it in with the salt (and pepper), roll it through the boiling water and let it simmer gently until all the water has evaporated.
2. See then whether enough fat has cooked out of the meat so that it can continue to roast in its own fat. If this is not the case, add lard, beef fat, butter or margarine and roast the meat in it.
3. Reckon 30 minutes per 500 g as roasting time. Finish the jus in the usual way (see recipe 227).
