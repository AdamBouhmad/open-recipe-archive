## Ingredients

- Chops (not too thick)
- Butter
- Lemon juice or shallot sauce

## Directions

1. Take chops that are not too thick, beat them well
2. Braise them with sufficient butter in an earthenware pan
3. When cooked, add lemon juice to the butter or eat the chops with a shallot sauce
