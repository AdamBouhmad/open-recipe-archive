## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 1 dl Rhine wine
- 1 dl fish stock
- 1 dl whipped cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with some salt, add the slightly softened butter or margarine and gradually add the Rhine wine
2. Stir the sauce with a whisk over a bain-marie until it begins to thicken
3. Then gradually add the fish stock, continue stirring until the sauce is bound and blend it lightly off the heat with
