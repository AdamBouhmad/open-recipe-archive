## Ingredients

- milk
- 1 chocolate cake per can milk
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. For each can of milk take one chocolate cake, bring the milk to a boil, and stir in the chocolate, which you have previously made into a smooth paste with some water, and let everything cook through well
2. Add sugar and cinnamon to taste
