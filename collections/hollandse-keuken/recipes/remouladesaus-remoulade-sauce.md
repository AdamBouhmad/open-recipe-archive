## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- 3½ dl salad oil
- ½ dl vinegar
- 2 teaspoons mustard or mustard powder
- 5 gherkins
- 1 shallot
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as mayonnaise (recipe 367).
2. Stir in last the very finely chopped gherkins and the shallot.
3. Serve the sauce with cold meat.
