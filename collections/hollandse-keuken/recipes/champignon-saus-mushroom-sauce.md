## Ingredients

- very young white mushrooms
- water
- lemon juice
- butter
- bouillon
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take very young white mushrooms and cut off the stems
2. Put water with lemon juice in an earthenware pan and add the mushrooms when it boils
3. Chop them very finely and press them lightly out in a linen cloth
4. Put a little butter with lemon juice in a pan
5. Add the mushrooms with twelve tablespoons of good bouillon
6. Add a little pepper
