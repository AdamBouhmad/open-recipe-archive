## Ingredients

- Whole ripe morello cherries
- Best French brandy
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Bread sugar or white candy sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the stems off three-quarters of the way
2. Add best French brandy with some cloves, cinnamon, and bread sugar or white candy sugar
