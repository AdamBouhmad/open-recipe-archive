## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower
- 1/2 l milk or cauliflower water
- 30-40 g flour or 20 g Duryea cornstarch
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 15 g butter or margarine extra
- 60 g old cheese

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and cook it tender, as described in the previous recipe.
2. Remove the cabbage with a skimming spoon from the pan, let it drain and place it in an ovenproof dish.
3. Pour the sauce prepared in the usual way (see recipe 328 or recipe 329) and through which the greater part of the grated cheese has been stirred over it.
4. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over, pour the melted butter over it and let it color very lightly brown in the oven.
