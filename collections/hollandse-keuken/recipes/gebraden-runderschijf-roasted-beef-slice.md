## Ingredients

- Beef slice
- Salt
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Handle the slice as described for the rib on the previous page, rub it well inside with salt, cloves and pepper and let it stand in vinegar for a couple of days.
2. Roast it just like the rib roast.
