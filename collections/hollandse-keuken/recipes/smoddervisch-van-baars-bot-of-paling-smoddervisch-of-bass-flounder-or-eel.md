## Ingredients

- 3 pounds fish (bass, flounder or eel, one or two kinds or all three mixed)
- 4 large handfuls cooked onions
- nutmeg
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook 3 pounds fish nearly done in a tinned kettle
2. Pour off the water cleanly without removing the fish
3. Sprinkle over the fish 4 large handfuls of cooked onions cut into small pieces
4. Add grated nutmeg and a little parsley finely chopped, and a quantity of butter
5. Hang the kettle with all this over the fire and shake until the butter is melted
6. Pour into the dish without being concerned about the order it lies in, as the fish may well break
