## Ingredients

- 375 g cold meat
- 2 eggs
- 60 g old bread
- 1/2 dl lean gravy with water or broth
- 50 g onion
- 1 spoon finely chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- Maggi's Aroma
- trimmed lemon slices
- gherkins
- capers

## Directions

1. Mince the meat, cleaned onion and washed parsley
2. Beat the egg yolks with some Maggi's Aroma, add the soaked bread squeezed between two spoons, the herbs, the broth and the meat and stir everything together vigorously
3. Mix this mass as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white and place it in a butter or margarine-greased pudding or timbale mold sprinkled with breadcrumbs
4. Close it with a lid greased with butter or margarine and cook for 45 minutes in a pan of boiling water, ensuring the water is refilled to just below the rim of the mold
5. Turn the meat onto a warm, moist plate, pour curry or fricassée sauce (see recipe 339 or 349), or optionally béchamel sauce (see recipe 346) over it and garnish with trimmed lemon slices, gherkins and capers
6. Alternatively replace meat with boneless fish
