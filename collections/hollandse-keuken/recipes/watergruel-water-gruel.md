## Ingredients

- 1.5 l water
- 100 g pearl barley
- 2.5 dl berry juice
- 100 g currants (or half raisins, half currants)
- peel of one lemon
- 175 to 200 g sugar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Soak the rinsed pearl barley for 24 hours in the cold water with salt.
2. Bring it to a boil in the same water with the lemon peel and let it cook gently for 1.5 hours.
3. Add the sugar, the well-rinsed currants without stems and the lemon juice and let the water gruel cook gently for another half hour.
4. Add the berry juice at the end.
5. Remove the lemon peel before serving.
6. Serve the water gruel warm or cold.
7. When using quick-cooking barley, omit the soaking.
