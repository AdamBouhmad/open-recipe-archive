## Ingredients

- 6 kg plums
- Sugar syrup (500 g sugar per 1 liter water)

## Directions

1. Wash the plums with cold water and arrange them in well-cleaned bottles up to 3 cm below the rim.
2. Pour enough sugar syrup over them so that the plums are just submerged.
3. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clips and heat them for 30 minutes to 80-90° C.
4. Let the bottles cool with the clips on, remove the clips and label the bottles with name and date.
5. (For corked bottles see the rules above).
