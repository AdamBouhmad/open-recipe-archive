## Ingredients

- beef piece in proportion to number of people
- pump water
- salt
- celery
- beet
- finely ground mace

## Directions

1. Hang a piece of beef with pump water and salt to cook in proportion to the number of people
2. Let it cook for two hours, paying good attention to skimming
3. Add celery, beet and finely ground mace
4. Let it cook another hour
5. Remove the meat when serving, which is served separately
