## Ingredients

- pike
- salmon
- spices as used in common sausage
- 1 cup melted butter
- 4 to 5 eggs depending on quantity
- white bread
- butter for coating paper

## Directions

1. Cut pike and salmon into small cubes
2. Add spices as in common sausage, 1 cup melted butter and 4 to 5 eggs according to quantity
3. Knead this together with some white bread
4. Stuff into the belly of a pike or roll it in butter-coated paper to the size of sausages
5. Cook or roast as desired
