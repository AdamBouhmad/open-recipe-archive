## Ingredients

- Wheat flour
- Eggs
- Water
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from 5 ounces of wheat flour and eggs
2. Form it into balls and roll them out again
3. Let them dry a little on a board
4. Then fold them in half and cut them into strips the width and thickness of a pipe stem
5. Cook them in water with a little salt
6. Once cooked, transfer the knoedels to cold water and let them drain and dry again
7. Melt butter in a pan and place the knoedels in it
8. Beat 4 eggs and pour them over, shake it a couple of times and let them brown in the butter
