## Ingredients

- Savoy cabbage
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove the outer green leaves from the cabbage
2. Wash the cabbage and let it drain thoroughly
3. In a pan, make a sauce of butter rolled in flour, some bouillon, salt and nutmeg
4. Place the cabbage in it and let everything stew together
