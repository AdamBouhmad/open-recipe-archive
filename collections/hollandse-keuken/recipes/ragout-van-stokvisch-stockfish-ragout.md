## Ingredients

- stockfish
- salt
- onions
- veal bouillon
- butter
- flour
- cream
- Cayenne pepper
- rice

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and let stand for twenty-four hours
3. Brown the slices with a couple of onions
4. Set them with veal bouillon
5. Add flour-rolled butter and four spoons of cream, as well as salt and Cayenne pepper
6. Let simmer slowly
7. Serve with rice
