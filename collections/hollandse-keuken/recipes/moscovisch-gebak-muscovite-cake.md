## Ingredients

- 100 g mixture of currants, raisins and candied peel
- 5 eggs
- powdered sugar
- 100 g flour
- 20 g cornstarch
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g sugar

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks completely smooth with sugar
2. Rinse currants and raisins, which have had stems removed, with lukewarm water and dry in a cloth
3. Chop the candied peel
4. Stir the mixture through the yolks
5. Sift the flour and cornstarch, add hard butter or margarine and cut into small, equal pieces
6. Beat egg whites as stiff as possible, then mix as lightly as possible with butter and flour, egg yolks with sugar
7. Put batter immediately into a tulband mold that is greased and dusted with powdered sugar and flour
8. Place mold in a hot oven, after 5 minutes check carefully that the cake is not browning too much, then lay a greased piece of brown paper on it
9. Lower oven temperature and bake until brown and cooked through (30-45 minutes)
10. Test for doneness by inserting a knitting needle, which should come out dry
11. Turn out onto a cooling rack, let cool and dust through a sieve with powdered sugar
