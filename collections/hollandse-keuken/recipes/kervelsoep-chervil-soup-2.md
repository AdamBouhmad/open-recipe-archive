## Ingredients

- well-chosen chervil
- flour
- water
- strong bouillon
- eel pieces
- white bread crumb
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop good chosen chervil with some flour finely
2. Stir it in boiling water until the chervil is done
3. Then add a bowl of strong bouillon, some pieces of eel and crumb of white bread, as well as a little salt
4. Let everything cook well done
