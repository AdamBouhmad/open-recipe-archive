## Ingredients

- one and a half Dutch pounds pork liver
- one Dutch pound bacon
- two and a half ounces unmelted lard
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- spices
- bacon or lard for layering

## Directions

1. Chop the pork liver, bacon and unmelted lard very finely with parsley, pepper, salt and spices
2. In a casserole, place a piece of lard or a slice of bacon at the bottom to prevent burning
3. Layer three fingerheights of the chopped mixture, then a slice of bacon, then three fingerheights of chopped mixture again, continuing until the pan is full
4. Cover with a slice of bacon
5. Roast in the oven for three hours, then let cool
6. Warm it when ready to eat
7. Can be served cold with aspic if desired
