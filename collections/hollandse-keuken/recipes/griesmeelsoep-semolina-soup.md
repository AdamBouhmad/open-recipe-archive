## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon of beef or 1 liter water and 4 bouillon cubes (recipe 35)
- 30 g semolina
- 50 g veal ground meat
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Prepare the bouillon in the usual manner, and if desired let a piece of mace cook in it.
2. Bring the strained bouillon to a boil, sprinkle the semolina into it while stirring, and add the meatballs prepared in the usual manner (recipe 37). Or: cook the semolina in water with salt until done, then add the bouillon cubes.
3. Let the soup simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, and then carefully add it to the egg beaten in the soup tureen.
