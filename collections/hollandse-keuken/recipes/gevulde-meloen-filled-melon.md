## Ingredients

- 1 melon
- 4 mandarins
- 3 slices pineapple
- 2 pears
- 10 apricots
- 20 raspberries
- 20 pitted cherries
- 3 bananas
- Approximately 100 g sugar (to taste)

## Directions

1. Cut a cap off the melon at 2/3 of its height.
2. Remove the seeds and scoop out the fruit flesh from the melon with a silver spoon.
3. Divide this into chunks.
4. Let part of the fruit flesh stand for one hour, with the mandarin segments, the pineapple pieces, pieces of pear, the apricots, raspberries, cherries, banana slices and the sugar.
5. Fill the hollowed melon with the fruit mixture before serving and place the hollowed cap on top.
6. Whipped cream can be served with this.
