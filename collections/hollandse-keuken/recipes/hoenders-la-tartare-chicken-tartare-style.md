## Ingredients

- young chickens
- melted butter
- two small onions
- parsley
- mushrooms
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut young chickens in half.
2. Singe them over the fire with melted butter, two small finely chopped onions, parsley and mushrooms, and pepper and salt.
3. Cover with breadcrumbs and roast over a hot fire.
4. Serve with a bound sauce (see sauces section).
