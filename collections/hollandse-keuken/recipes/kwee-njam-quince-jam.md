## Ingredients

- 3 kg quinces
- 2¼ kg sugar
- Jam jars
- Parchment paper
- Cellophane
- Elastic bands
- String
- Labels

## Directions

1. Wash the pears carefully.
2. Cook them for several minutes.
3. Peel the pears, cut them into pieces, remove the cores and set the pieces with a little of the water in which the pears were pre-cooked and the sugar.
4. Bring to a boil with the lid on the pan, then remove the lid and let them cook down until the mass becomes red and syrupy.
5. Treat the fruit further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
