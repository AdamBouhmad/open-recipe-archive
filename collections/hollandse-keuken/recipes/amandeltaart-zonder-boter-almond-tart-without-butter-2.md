## Ingredients

- 2 ounces almond paste, finely ground
- 1.5 ounces grated bread sugar
- 4 eggs
- Lemon peel grated

## Directions

1. Put all together in a high pot and stir continuously for a quarter hour
2. Before it stops, put it in the pan or forms and set it to bake
