## Ingredients

- 4 dl lean jus with water
- 50 g onion
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- small carrot
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- splash of Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 359.
2. Remove from heat and mix with sugar, Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce to taste.
3. Serve with beef tenderloin, lamb cutlets, etc.
