## Ingredients

- sheep feet
- bouillon
- two egg yolks
- a little pepper
- butter
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Take some sheep feet
2. Remove the bones up to the bend
3. Blanch them
4. Then cook them thoroughly
5. Make a sauce from bouillon, two egg yolks, a little pepper, some butter, salt and flour
6. Serve over the feet
