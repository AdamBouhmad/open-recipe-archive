## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 100 g brown sugar
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 dl white wine

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar in a well-enameled pan until they become thick, white and fluffy. Then gradually add the wine and lemon juice and let the mass thicken in a bain-marie with vigorous stirring, but do not let it boil. Mix this off the heat with the stiffly beaten egg white and serve with biscuits.
