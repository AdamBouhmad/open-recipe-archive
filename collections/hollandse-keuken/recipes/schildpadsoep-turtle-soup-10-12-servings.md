## Ingredients

- 1 calf's foot
- 250 g beef shank meat
- 3 large onions
- 1/2 large carrot
- 70 g butter or margarine
- 4 cloves
- 6 bay leaves
- thyme and marjoram to taste
- 20 bruised white peppercorns
- Cayenne pepper (optional)
- 90 g flour
- 90 g butter or margarine
- English sauce and soya or Madeira to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the foot and let it cook gently with 1 l water for 1 hour
2. Cut off the fine pieces of meat to serve later as garnish in the soup
3. Brown the diced shank meat, chopped onions and carrot in 70 g butter or margarine, taking care that this mixture does not burn
4. Add a little water if necessary
5. Put them in a pan, add the calf's foot and 1 l water and let everything infuse together for 3 hours
6. Strain the bouillon, add 1 l fresh water and let this broth infuse gently for another 3 hours
7. Strain it, let it cool and skim the fat from the 3 broths
8. Combine them and let them simmer gently with the herbs for a quarter hour
9. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring to a boil while stirring, rub out all lumps and gradually add the strained bouillon
10. Let the soup simmer while stirring until cooked (10 minutes)
11. Warm the meat pieces from the foot in it and add English sauce and soya or Madeira away from heat
