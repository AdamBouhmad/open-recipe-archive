## Ingredients

- Feuilleté crust
- Oyster or chicken farce, or meat farce, warmed

## Directions

1. Make an elegant small mold from feuilleté with the hand, three fingers high
2. Make a small lid and place it inside
3. Bake in the oven
4. Fill the baked pastries with oyster or chicken farce or meat farce that has been warmed beforehand
5. Place the lid on top
