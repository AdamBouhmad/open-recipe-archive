## Ingredients

- 10 olives
- 6 anchovies
- 40 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Clean the anchovies as indicated in recipe 17
2. Press them through a hair sieve
3. Mix the resulting purée with the butter or margarine beaten to cream
4. Cut the olives open far enough so that the pits are easy to remove
5. Fill them with the anchovy butter, set them, returned to their original form, in a section of the plate
6. As a variation, use some olives filled with pimento
