## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- sweetbreads
- salt
- butter
- nutmeg
- lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare as with veal pastries
2. Remove all membranes and skins from the sweetbreads
3. Cook them briefly
4. Chop them finely and stew them now with some salt, butter, nutmeg and lemon
5. When done, let them cool and fill the pastries with them
