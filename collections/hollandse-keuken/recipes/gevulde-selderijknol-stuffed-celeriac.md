## Ingredients

- 4 small celeriac roots
- 150 g veal mince
- 150 g pork mince
- 150 g old bread
- pepper, salt, nutmeg to taste
- 40 g butter or margarine
- breadcrumbs
- lean jus with water or bouillon

## Directions

1. Peel the roots, wash them and cook them until tender in plenty of boiling salted water (3/4 to 1 hour).
2. Cut off the top cap and hollow them out.
3. Meanwhile, prepare the mince in the usual way with the soaked bread and the spices.
4. Melt 20 g butter or margarine in the pan, add the mince and keep stirring until it is cooked (approximately 10 minutes), but make sure it does not brown.
5. Fill the celeriac roots with it, replace the lids and place them in a fireproof dish.
6. Add some lean jus with water or bouillon, sprinkle some breadcrumbs over it, place a piece of butter or margarine on each root, clean the edge of the dish and let a light brown crust form on it in a moderately warm oven.
