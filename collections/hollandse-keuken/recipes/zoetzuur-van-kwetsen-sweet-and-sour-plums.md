## Ingredients

- 5 kg plums
- 2 bottles pickling vinegar
- 50 g cloves
- 50 g cinnamon stick
- 2 kg sugar
- 5 g mace (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the plums, dry them, prick them all over with a silver fork, place them in a Cologne pot and pour over the vinegar, which has been cooked with the spices (optionally also mace) and the sugar
2. Let the plums stand for a week and stir them gently from time to time
3. Cook the plums in small batches together in the liquid until they are completely smooth without wrinkles
4. Carefully remove each plum that has become smooth from the liquid, place them in the Cologne pot, and when all plums are ready, pour the liquid with the spices over them
5. Close the pot with damp parchment paper and label it with name and date
6. Let them stand for at least 3 weeks before use
7. If necessary, add more sweet and sour liquid later to replace what has evaporated
