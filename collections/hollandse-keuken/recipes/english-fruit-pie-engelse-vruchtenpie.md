## Ingredients

- 150 g flour
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 25 g sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- juice of one lemon
- salt
- fruit that cooks quickly, such as fresh plums, apples, gooseberries or rhubarb or stewed fruit or preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Use hard butter or margarine for the dough (from the refrigerator in summer).
2. Sift the flour into a bowl, put the butter or margarine in it and chop it finely until it looks like breadcrumbs; beat the egg with the sugar and lemon juice and add this to the mixture.
3. Stir this together as lightly as possible, add as much water as is necessary to form a firm ball of dough.
4. Roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board with a flour-dusted rolling pin into a round sheet, larger than the heat-resistant dish.
5. Put the required fruit in the dish.
6. Sprinkle sugar between the raw fruit and fill the dish in the middle high with the fruit.
7. Make sure that if stewed fruit is used, it is cold.
