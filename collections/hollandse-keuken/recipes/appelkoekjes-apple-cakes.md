## Ingredients

- best sour apples
- yeast batter with a dash of brandy
- boiling butter or lard, or half of each

## Directions

1. Take best sour apples, core them, and cut into slices.
2. Roll the slices in a somewhat thick yeast batter to which a dash of brandy has been added.
3. Put them in a flat pan with boiling butter or lard (or half of each) and let them brown well.
