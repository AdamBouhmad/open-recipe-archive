## Ingredients

- 500 g cold potatoes
- 2.5 dl milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 80 g grated cheese
- salt
- nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- 50 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Make mashed potato purée in the usual way, but stir only 15 g of butter or margarine through it, mix it with the egg yolk, chopped parsley and grated cheese.
2. Grease the shells with some butter or margarine, fill them three-quarters full with the purée and finish them as described in recipe 102.
