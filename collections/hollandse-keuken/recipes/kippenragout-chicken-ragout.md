## Ingredients

- Young chickens
- Rainwater
- Salt
- Chicken feet, hearts and gizzards
- Veal balls
- Sweetbreads
- Asparagus
- Parsley roots
- Nutmeg
- Veal bouillon
- Egg yolks
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut well-cleaned young chickens in pieces and cook them in rainwater with salt, skimming well
2. Put them with the chicken feet, hearts and gizzards, along with some veal balls and a couple sweetbreads, in a pan
3. Add asparagus, parsley roots, fine salt and nutmeg
4. Cover all with needed veal bouillon and let it cook again
5. Remove the parsley roots and braise the ragout with some egg yolks and flour
