## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons wheat flour
- 3 eggs
- A little cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 coffee cups cold water
- Boiling butter, or half butter and half lard

## Directions

1. Beat together the wheat flour, eggs, cinnamon, and sugar with 2 coffee cups cold water
2. Pour the batter through a funnel into boiling butter, or half butter and half lard
3. Let it become nicely brown
