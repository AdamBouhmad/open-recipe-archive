## Ingredients

- one pound calf liver
- one pound bacon
- spices and salt as for pork sausage
- half pound bread crumbs
- fresh intestines

## Directions

1. Take one pound calf liver and an equal amount of bacon
2. Remove all membranes from the liver
3. Chop it together very finely
4. Add spices and salt as for pork sausage, along with half pound of bread crumbs
5. Stuff this sausage meat into fresh intestines
6. Roast it on the grill
