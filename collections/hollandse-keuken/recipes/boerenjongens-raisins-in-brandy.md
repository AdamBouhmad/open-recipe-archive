## Ingredients

- 500 g sultana raisins
- 250 g sugar
- 2½ dl water
- 6 cm cinnamon stick
- 1 liter brandy
- 200 g almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the raisins, wash them with hot water, let them drain well.
2. Melt the sugar in the water.
3. Add the cinnamon and the fruit.
4. Let the fruit come to a boil on the fire and become soft.
5. Transfer the fruit to the well-cleaned stoppered bottle (see page 369 3°); cook the sugar solution down a bit more, let it cool and pour it over the fruit.
6. Add the brandy when the mixture has cooled completely.
7. Seal the bottle hermetically.
8. Use the fruit only after 3 months.
9. Optionally, the peeled almonds can be added at the same time as the brandy.
