## Ingredients

- 1/4 dl milk
- salt
- 100 g fine vermicelli
- 3 egg yolks
- ragout of 125 g veal for the filling

## Directions

1. Cook the washed vermicelli in boiling milk with salt (1/4 hour)
2. Stir the beaten yolks through the paste and fill buttered timbale molds or cups sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs with it
3. Bake them in a moderately warm oven light brown (1/2 hour to 45 minutes), turn them out and cut off the top cap with a sharp knife
4. Carefully hollow them out, fill them with ragout, replace the cap and let them heat through well in the oven
