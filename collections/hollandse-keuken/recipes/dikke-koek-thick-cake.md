## Ingredients

- 5 ounces wheat flour
- 4 eggs
- 12 loods currants
- 12 loods butter
- 2 loods orange peel snippets
- Warm milk
- Salt
- Yeast
- Butter and ground rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Mix the wheat flour, eggs, currants, butter, and orange peel snippets
2. If there is not enough moisture, add some warm milk
3. Add salt and yeast, beat everything together and let it rise
4. When risen, put this batter in a Turk's head or tart pan that has been previously greased with butter and sprinkled with ground rusks
5. Set the pan in hot ashes or over a moderate fire, lay fire orderly on top and let it bake
6. To know if it is done, stick a knife in it; as long as flour sticks to it, it is not ready
