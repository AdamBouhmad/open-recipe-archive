## Ingredients

- Savoy cabbage
- salt
- milk
- potato flour or flour
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage with salt until done
2. Let it drain
3. When serving, pour over a sauce made of milk, potato flour or flour, a piece of butter and some nutmeg
