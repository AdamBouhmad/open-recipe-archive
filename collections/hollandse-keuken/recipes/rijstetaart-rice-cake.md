## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 100 g rice
- grated zest of half a lemon
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 75 g sugar
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 40 g almonds (optional)
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook rice pudding in the usual way, letting the lemon zest cook through with it.
2. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the egg yolks and sugar, then the rice pudding and the ground or chopped almonds.
3. Mix everything as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg whites and put it in a greased tart pan dusted with fine breadcrumbs.
4. Bake the cake in a moderately warm oven until light brown (3/4 to 1 hour).
5. Turn it out after cooling and dust it with powdered sugar.
