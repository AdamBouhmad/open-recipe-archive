## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 l milk
- 90 g cornstarch Duryea
- 100 g sugar
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 1/2 vanilla stick or 1/4 lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook cornstarch porridge in the usual way (see previous recipe), but let the vanilla or lemon peel cook along with it.
2. Mix it with half of the sugar.
3. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the other half of the sugar, carefully add the hot porridge and last of all the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Finish the dish as described in recipe 602.
