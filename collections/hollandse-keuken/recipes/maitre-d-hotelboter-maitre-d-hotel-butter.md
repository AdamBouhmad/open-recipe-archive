## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- juice of approximately 1 lemon
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons very finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy.
2. Add the lemon juice drop by drop, then the parsley and pepper.
3. Serve with veal, cooked fish, etc.
