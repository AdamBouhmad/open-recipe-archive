## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 100 g flour
- 20 g cornstarch
- 150 g sugar
- the grated zest of one lemon
- marmalade or jam
- 50 g grated coconut

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs + 20 minutes with the sugar until a foamy mass.
2. Then stir through the sifted flour and the grated lemon zest.
3. Line tall cake molds with greased paper, fill them 1/4 with the mixture and let them bake in -- 20 minutes in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done.
4. Let the cakes cool, brush them with jam, sprinkle them with grated coconut and place a small fruit on top.
