## Ingredients

- 35 decigrams rice
- Water
- 1.2 liters bone bouillon
- Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Wash 35 decigrams of rice three times in cold water.
2. Cook the rice in 1 liter water and stir carefully with a spoon.
3. Refresh it and drain it.
4. Put the rice subsequently in 1.2 liters of boiling bone bouillon, stir it with a spoon to mix the rice well with the bouillon, and add some Liebig's Meat Extract.
5. Let the soup simmer gently for 25 minutes to give the rice time to swell, with the lid three-quarters closed.
6. Skim it and put it in the tureen.
