## Ingredients

- Apples
- Wheat flour
- Eggs
- Sweet milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take thick slices of apples and let them cook gently in the pan
2. Add the aforementioned batter to the apples
3. When done, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over it
