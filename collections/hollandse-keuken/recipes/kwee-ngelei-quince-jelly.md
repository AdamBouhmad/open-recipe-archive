## Ingredients

- 3 kg quinces
- 6 dl water
- 3/4 of the weight of the liquid from the pears after cooking in sugar
- Jam jars
- Parchment paper
- Cellophane
- Elastic bands
- String
- Labels

## Directions

1. Wash the pears carefully, cook them in boiling water for several minutes, then peel them and cut them with the cores into pieces.
2. Place them with 9 tablespoons of sugar in the liquid and let them cook until they become soft.
3. Pour everything through a clean cloth, let the liquid drain carefully, weigh or measure it (1/2 liter = 500 g) and set it with 3/4 of its weight in sugar.
4. Let the jelly cook down, stirring occasionally, until it becomes red and syrupy.
5. Prepare further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
