## Ingredients

- green peas
- rainwater
- salt
- beef broth
- lean beef
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Treat the peas as above
2. When they are cooked, add broth made from lean beef, vegetables and salt according to preference, letting everything cook together until the vegetables are well done
