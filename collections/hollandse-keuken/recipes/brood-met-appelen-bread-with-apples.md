## Ingredients

- a deep plate full of old white bread without crust, cut into cubes
- one and a half ounces butter
- one quarter can hot milk
- 4 eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- currants
- a deep plate full of peeled apples, cored
- lemon sugar for garnish

## Directions

1. Take a deep plate full of old white bread without crust, cut into cubes
2. Fry them in one and a half ounces butter until golden
3. Pour one quarter can hot milk over them and let stand for half an hour
4. Add 4 eggs, sugar, cinnamon, currants and a deep plate full of peeled, cored apples
5. Bake all together in a butter-lined mold for 1.5 hours
6. Garnish with lemon sugar
