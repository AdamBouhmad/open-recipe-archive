## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- melted butter or fat
- hard biscuit
- sour sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue until done and remove the skin
2. Slice it
3. Dip the slices in melted butter or fat
4. Roll them in hard biscuit
5. Fry them in a frying pan or on a grill
6. Serve with sour sauce
