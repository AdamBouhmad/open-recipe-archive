## Ingredients

- oranges
- sugar
- rainwater

## Directions

1. First let the oranges soak and then cook them fairly tender, setting them with cold rainwater
2. Now melt sugar and cook it, continuously skimming; add the apples and cook them another half hour; now remove them from the syrup and put them in bottles, cook the syrup a bit thicker and pour it on hot
3. Seal the bottles well
