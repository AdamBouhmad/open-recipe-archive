## Ingredients

- vermicelli
- strong veal or beef bouillon
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take vermicelli according to the number of persons
2. Cook it for half an hour in strong veal or beef bouillon
3. Add salt, nutmeg and mace to taste
4. When serving, stir a few egg yolks through it
