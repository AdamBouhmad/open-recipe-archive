## Ingredients

- 4 kidney carbonades of 150 g each
- 100 g butter, margarine or veal fat
- 7 1/2 g salt

## Directions

1. Treat the carbonades like veal cutlets (recipe 261), but be careful that the kidney does not come loose.
2. Stew them in the gravy made with a little water for another 1/2 hour gently.
