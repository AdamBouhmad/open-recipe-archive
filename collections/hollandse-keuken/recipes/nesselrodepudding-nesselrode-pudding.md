## Ingredients

- ¼ l cooking cream
- 3 eggs
- 50 g sugar
- ¼ vanilla pod or vanilla sugar
- 12 g white gelatin
- 50 g sultana raisins
- 25 g currants
- 30 g candied citron
- 50 g almonds
- ½ dl maraschino
- 70 g sugar to be caramelized

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the currants and raisins, rinse them with lukewarm water, cover with boiling water and cook for 10 minutes. Let them drain well in a sieve.
2. Peel the soaked almonds, chop or grind them finely; chop the candied citron.
3. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the sugar and the vanilla pulp, add the cream and let the mass bind in a bain-marie while stirring well.
4. Make caramel from 70 g sugar, add it to the bound mass.
5. Remove from heat and combine everything with the mixed dried fruit, the almonds, the maraschino and the gelatin, which has been soaked for 10-15 minutes in cold water.
6. Let the mixture cool somewhat, add it to the beaten egg whites and let it become jelly-like, stirring occasionally.
7. Pour it into a pudding mold rinsed with egg white.
8. Let the pudding cool, turn it out onto a damp plate and serve without sauce.
