## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter or margarine
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs
- bread

## Directions

1. Strip the spinach, carefully remove the seeds, place them for some time in abundant cold water to let the sand settle.
2. Carefully scoop them out and wash them as many times as necessary until the water runs completely clear.
3. Let them cook until tender, stirring occasionally (approximately 20 minutes) with as little water as possible.
4. Let them drain in a colander, chop them as finely as possible.
5. Stew them for approximately 5 minutes with butter or margarine and salt.
6. Place them in a bowl and garnish them with pieces of hard-boiled egg and strips of bread fried light brown in butter or margarine.
