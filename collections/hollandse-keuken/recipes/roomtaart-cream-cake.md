## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 150 g sugar
- 80 g flour
- 20 g cornstarch
- 2.5 dl milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 25 g flour
- 25 to 30 g sugar
- 1.5 vanilla pod

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar until it is completely finely ground.
2. Add the sifted flour and cornstarch and mix this mass as quickly as possible with the very stiffly beaten egg white.
3. Put the batter in a greased cake pan, dusted with powdered sugar and flour, and bake the cake in a fairly warm oven until light brown and done (approximately 30 to 35 minutes).
4. Cover if necessary with a greased piece of paper.
5. Check if it is done by pricking it with a knitting needle, which should then come out dry.
6. Turn the cake onto a cake rack, let it cool and cut it into three or four layers.
7. Spread these with the cream custard (recipe 816), which is prepared in the usual way.
8. Place the layers on top of each other and garnish the cake with icing and French fruits.
