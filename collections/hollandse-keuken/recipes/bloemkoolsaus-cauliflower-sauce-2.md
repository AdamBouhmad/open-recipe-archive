## Ingredients

- ½ l milk
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring to a boil while stirring.
2. Rub out all lumps carefully.
3. Add the milk while stirring, in small amounts at a time, and let the sauce cook while stirring until done (10 minutes).
4. Add salt as needed.
5. Serve over cauliflower or use it, either thinner or thicker, for stewing various vegetables.
6. Make a thick sauce with leafy vegetables, as moisture will draw out from the vegetables.
7. Make the sauce thinner with vegetables that release no moisture, for example salsify.
