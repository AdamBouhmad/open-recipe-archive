## Ingredients

- Just over 1 kg onions
- 2.5 dl onion cooking water
- 0.5 dl vinegar
- 30 g flour or 15-20 g Duryea corn starch
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the onions, wash them and cook them in plenty of boiling water with salt (3/4 to 1 hour).
2. Let them drain in a colander.
3. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as cauliflower sauce or corn starch sauce (see recipe 328 or recipe 329), but use onion cooking water and vinegar instead of milk.
4. Braise the onions in this sauce for approximately 10 minutes.
5. Alternatively: Clean the onions in the usual way, wash them and place them on a very low heat with little water, butter or margarine, vinegar, salt and pepper. Let them cook gently (approximately 1/2 hour) and thicken the liquid with some mixed flour or Duryea corn starch.
