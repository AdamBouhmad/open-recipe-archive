## Ingredients

- ½ pound flour
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 12 eggs (6 with whites, 6 without whites)
- unsalted butter

## Directions

1. Cook a cup of sweet milk, then sprinkle in ½ pound flour.
2. Stir until done, so that it no longer sticks to the knife.
3. Add 12 eggs, half with whites and half without.
4. Beat until smooth and fry in unsalted butter.
