## Ingredients

- beef bouillon
- 1 bouquet of parsley, thyme and bay leaves
- 2 carrots
- 1 decigram salt
- 3 milligrams coarsely ground pepper
- 5 decigrams sugar
- Liebig's Meat Extract
- 4 hectogrammes clarified butter
- 4 hectogrammes flour

## Directions

1. Let the beef bouillon come to a boil, skim it, and add the bouquet of herbs, carrots, salt, pepper and sugar.
2. Let this cook together and add the necessary Liebig's Meat Extract.
3. Strain through cloth.
4. Make a roux from the clarified butter and flour, let it take on a brown color while stirring constantly on the fire.
5. When the roux is brown enough, moisten it with the broth described above.
6. Stir on the fire with a wooden spoon and as soon as it boils, place the pan on the corner of the stove and let it simmer gently for 2 hours, keeping the lid three-quarters closed.
7. Skim it and remove the fat twice during the process.
8. Strain through cloth and keep for use.
