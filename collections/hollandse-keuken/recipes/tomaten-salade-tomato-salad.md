## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- 1 onion
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- spices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the tomatoes (if the skin does not come off easily, place them for a moment in boiling water), cut the tomatoes into thin slices.
2. Make the onion very fine, add oil, vinegar, salt and spices to it.
3. Pour this mixture over the tomatoes and let them marinate for 10 minutes.
4. Serve them in a salad bowl that is lined with a few nice yellow lettuce leaves. Sprinkle the tomatoes with some finely chopped parsley.
