## Ingredients

- 200 g strawberries
- 4 egg yolks
- 125 g bastard sugar
- 4 dl white or Rhine wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Pick the strawberries, wash them and press them through a horsehair sieve.
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar, add the wine, lemon juice and strawberry puree and let the sauce be bound, stirring, on a gentle fire or in a bain-marie.
3. Present it with warm semolina, rice pudding etc.
