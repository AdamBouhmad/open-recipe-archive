## Ingredients

- 5 ounces groats
- 6 ounces wheat flour
- 2 ounces raisins
- 4 eggs
- salt
- a piece of yeast

## Directions

1. Mix 5 ounces groats and 6 ounces wheat flour with 2 ounces raisins, 4 eggs, salt and a piece of yeast into a thick batter
2. Put it in a mold or sack and let it rise
3. Put it in boiling water and let it cook
4. After an hour of cooking, turn the mold or sack over and let it finish cooking
5. Serve with melted butter or meat broth
