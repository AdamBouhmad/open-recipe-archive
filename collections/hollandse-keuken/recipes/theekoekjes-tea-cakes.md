## Ingredients

- 6 ounces flour
- 2.5 ounces sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1.5 ounces butter
- A little cinnamon
- A little grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Make a dough from the flour, sugar, eggs, butter, cinnamon, and grated lemon peel
2. Roll it out flat and use a glass to form it
3. Roll over it once more with a roller lightly and make little squares on it
4. Lay the cakes in an open tart pan with good fire underneath
5. As soon as they are done on one side, turn them over
