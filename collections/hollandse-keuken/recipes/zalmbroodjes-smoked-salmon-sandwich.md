## Ingredients

- 3 slices stale casino bread
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 25 g smoked salmon
- mayonnaise or remoulade sauce

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of stale casino bread, remove the crusts and cut them into different shapes, e.g. triangles, diamonds, pear-shaped figures, etc.
2. Fry them in the frying pan light brown and crispy, let them cool and spread them on one side with mayonnaise (recipe 368) or remoulade sauce (recipe 371)
3. Lay a properly fitting piece of smoked salmon on top and place the sandwiches on the plate
4. If desired, replace the salmon with smoked colored pollock
