## Ingredients

- 500 g green peas
- 40 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in the usual way until done. Rub them, a small amount at a time, as warm as possible through a sieve, until only the skins remain. Mix this purée with as much of the liquid in which the peas were cooked until it has reached the thickness of mashed potatoes.
2. Stir in butter or margarine, salt and nutmeg to taste, heat it well and mix it with the finely chopped parsley. Serve the purée with sausages, pork chops, etc.
