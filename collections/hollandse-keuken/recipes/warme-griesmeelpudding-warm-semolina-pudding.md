## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 100 g semolina
- 80 g sugar
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 0.5 vanilla pod or vanilla sugar or the zest of a lemon
- 50 g almonds or raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook stiff semolina porridge in the usual way
2. Stir in the butter or margarine on the heat and carefully from off the heat the beaten yolks and if desired the peeled and chopped almonds or the washed raisins
3. Mix the porridge as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white, after removing the flavoring
4. Grease a warm pudding mold with some butter or margarine (also the lid), sprinkle some fine breadcrumbs in it and add the mixture, but ensure the mold is filled only 3/4 full
5. Close the mold firmly and cook the pudding 2 hours au bain-marie, but ensure the water is always refilled to just below the rim of the mold, without it coming to a boil
6. Let the pudding dry out a little without the lid and serve with foam sauce or warm fruit juice sauce
