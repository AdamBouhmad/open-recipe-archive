## Ingredients

- 250 g cold meat
- 8 cooked potatoes
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 4 pickled onions
- 4 pickled gherkins
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes, mix them with the chopped meat, 3 chopped onions and 3 snipped or chopped gherkins.
2. Stir in enough salad oil, vinegar, pepper and salt until a cohesive, well-flavored mixture is obtained.
3. Lay the mixture on a plate, smooth it, but ensure it is slightly higher in the middle than at the edges.
4. Garnish with the remaining onions, gherkins, hard-boiled egg cut into quarters and fresh sprigs of parsley.
5. Note: Instead of oil and vinegar, one can also stir mayonnaise or salad sauce through the mixture.
