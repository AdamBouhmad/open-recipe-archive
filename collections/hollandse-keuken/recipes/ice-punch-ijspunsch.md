## Ingredients

- One can Rhine wine
- Juice of ten lemons
- Five ounces sugar
- Half a can arak

## Directions

1. Let one can Rhine wine, the juice of ten lemons, and five ounces sugar freeze in an ice bath as previously taught
2. When frozen, put it in a bottle with a wide mouth and add half a can of arak
