## Ingredients

- 2 cooking plaice
- water
- salt
- small splash of vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, make a slit along the backbone, rinse it inside and outside
2. Cook it in boiling water with salt (and a little vinegar if desired) with the light side facing up until a fin comes off easily (5 to 10 minutes)
3. Remove it with 2 skimming spoons or the fish slice from the pan and place it light side up on a fish plate
4. Serve with egg sauce, parsley sauce, or melted butter
