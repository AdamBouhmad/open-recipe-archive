## Ingredients

- 1/2 l whipped cream
- 1 dl coffee extract
- 100 g sugar
- 10 g white gelatin or 8 g powdered gelatin

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatin 10 to 15 minutes in cold water.
2. Dissolve it with the sugar in the coffee extract.
3. Beat the cream stiff, pour the slightly cooled coffee extract through it while stirring.
4. Let the mixture become gelatinous, stirring occasionally, and place it in a mold brushed with unbeaten egg white.
5. Let the pudding become cold and stiff and turn it out onto a damp dish.
