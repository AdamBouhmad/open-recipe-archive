## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 dl fruit juice (berry juice or berry and raspberry juice or lemon juice with water)
- 80 g bastard sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar until they become frothy (10 minutes).
2. Then add the fruit juice drop by drop, continuing to beat.
3. Place the sauce on a gentle fire or in a bain-marie and let it be bound with vigorous beating, but do not boil, as otherwise there is a great risk of curdling.
4. Present the sauce with warm bread pudding, semolina pudding etc.
