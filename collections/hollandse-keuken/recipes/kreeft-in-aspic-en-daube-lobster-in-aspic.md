## Ingredients

- 1 large can lobster
- 1 l white aspic
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the aspic in the usual way, pour a thin layer into a stone mould rinsed with cold water or a rice ring or into small timbale moulds and let it cool.
2. Place some pieces of lobster on it, alternated with small figures of egg white, capers and parsley.
3. Stick these with some lukewarm aspic, let the mass cool again, pour aspic over it until everything is covered and let it cool.
4. Continue until the entire mould or ring is filled.
5. Turn the daube out onto a moist plate and garnish it with lettuce and mayonnaise.
