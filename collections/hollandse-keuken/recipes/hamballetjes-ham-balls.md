## Ingredients

- 200 g ham
- 250 g cooked potatoes
- 50 g stale bread without crust
- Some milk
- Pepper
- Some salt
- 30 g butter, margarine or deep-frying fat

## Directions

1. Mince the ham finely, mix it with finely mashed potatoes and the bread, which has been soaked in some water or milk and squeezed out between two spoons.
2. Add pepper and salt to taste and stir everything together so that it is well mixed.
3. Form small balls, roll them in breadcrumbs and fry them in the frying pan, in which the butter has been made light brown, or in the hot frying fat on all sides until brown.
