## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 7.5 g salt
- 10 g yeast
- 1 egg
- 50 g butter or margarine
- some sugar
- 2 dl lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Make bread dough in the usual way and let it rise (see recipe 835).
2. Place the dough in tall cake molds that have been greased with butter or margarine, or make round pieces approximately 3 cm thick and place them on a greased baking sheet.
3. Let the batter rise for another 1/2 hour under a damp cloth.
4. Bake them in a hot oven for 20 to 30 minutes until light brown and cooked through.
5. Cut them warm in half, spread them, place the halves back together, and serve warm.
