## Ingredients

- firm white or savoy cabbage
- salt
- black peppercorns

## Directions

1. Remove outer leaves from cabbage
2. Cut in half and remove the core
3. Slice very finely by hand or with a mill
4. Line the bottom of the barrel with cabbage leaves
5. Sprinkle with coarse salt
6. Layer in cabbage, tamping down until liquid rises
7. Sprinkle with salt and black peppercorns
8. Continue layering until barrel is full
9. Place a board with a stone on top
10. After a few days, pour off some of the top liquid and add a heavier stone
