## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or fish water
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 50 g onion (1 medium-sized)
- optionally 1/2 deciliter vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the onion, rinse it, chop it and cook it in the butter or margarine (15 minutes), but make sure it stays light in color
2. Add the flour, stir out the lumps, mix in the liquid by small portions at a time, stirring continuously, and let the sauce cook, stirring, (10 minutes)
3. Serve with stockfish or white beans, in which latter case bean water is used and vinegar is added
4. Note: If vinegar is used, take 5 g more flour. A brown onion sauce is obtained by browning the flour and onions
