## Ingredients

- juice of 3 lemons
- 2.5 dl water
- 100 g sugar
- 16 g white and red gelatin or 14 g powdered gelatin
- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- optionally 100 g candied fruit pieces

## Directions

1. Soak the leaf gelatin 10 to 15 minutes in cold water.
2. Bring the water to a boil, dissolve the sugar in it.
3. Remove from heat, dissolve the well-squeezed gelatin in it and add the sifted lemon juice.
4. Let the mixture cool somewhat and add it slowly while stirring to the stiffly beaten cream and stiffly beaten egg whites.
5. If desired, mix the pudding with pieces of candied fruit.
6. Let it stand, stirring occasionally, until it becomes gelatinous and the gelatin no longer sinks.
7. Pour it into a mold brushed with unbeaten egg white and let it become cold and stiff.
8. Turn the pudding out onto a damp dish and serve it without sauce.
