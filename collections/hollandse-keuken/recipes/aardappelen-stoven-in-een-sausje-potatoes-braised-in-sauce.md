## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg cooked potatoes
- 1/2 l milk
- 10 g flour or 8 g Duryea cornstarch
- 40 g butter or margarine
- salt
- finely chopped parsley or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Slice the potatoes or leave small potatoes (e.g. new ones) whole.
2. Make a thin sauce, add some salt and carefully braise the potatoes in it, but make sure the slices do not break.
3. Mix them just before serving with very fine parsley or grate some nutmeg over the plate.
