## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 eggs
- 2 dl berry juice
- 1 piece cinnamon stick
- 75 g brown sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar for a long time until they become thick, white and airy.
2. Add the fruit juice in small portions while stirring, add the cinnamon and let the custard bind au bain-marie while stirring vigorously.
3. After cooling, mix with the stiffly beaten egg white and serve with biscuits.
