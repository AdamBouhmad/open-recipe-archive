## Ingredients

- beef tenderloin
- calf's foot
- chicken
- water

## Directions

1. Take a beef tenderloin, a calf's foot and a chicken
2. Let this cook for 4 to 5 hours with twice as much water as the weight of the meat
3. Skim carefully
4. Strain through a sieve
