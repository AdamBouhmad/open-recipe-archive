## Ingredients

- 1/2 l strong coffee
- 15 g gelatin or 12.5 g powdered gelatin
- 60 g sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatin 10 to 15 minutes in cold water, then dissolve it with the sugar in the coffee, which has been warmed slightly.
2. Stir from time to time until the mixture begins to become gelatinous.
3. Pour it into a mold rinsed with cold water.
4. Let the pudding become cold and stiff.
5. Turn it out onto a damp dish and serve with cream sauce.
