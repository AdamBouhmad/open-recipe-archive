## Ingredients

- 4 dl lean jus with water
- 50 g onion
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- small carrot
- 40 to 50 g flour
- 40 to 50 g butter or margarine
- 1 dl Madeira

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 359.
2. Remove from heat and mix with Madeira.
3. Serve with beef tenderloin, beef tongue, etc.
