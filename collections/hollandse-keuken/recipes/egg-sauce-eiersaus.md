## Ingredients

- Vinegar
- Two to three eggs
- Flour
- Butter
- Mace

## Directions

1. After the vinegar has boiled, add two to three well-beaten eggs
2. Stir well while pouring to prevent curdling
3. Add flour, a piece of butter to taste, and some mace, which greatly enhances the flavor
