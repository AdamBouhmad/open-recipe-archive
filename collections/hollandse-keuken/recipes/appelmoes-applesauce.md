## Ingredients

- 1 kg sour apples
- approximately 100 g white sugar
- some boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the apples into quarters or sixths and immediately place them in cold water to keep them pale.
2. Wash them, set them immediately on with some boiling water (the amount depends on the thickness desired) and let them cook quickly without a lid until soft (10-15 minutes).
3. Stir from time to time to prevent burning.
4. Rub them through with a wooden spoon or press the sauce through a horsehair sieve in small portions at a time, as hot as possible.
5. Stir in the sugar and optionally an egg, with the white and yolk beaten separately.
