## Ingredients

- apples
- grated stale white bread
- glass of wine
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and bore out the cores
2. Cook the peels and cores in water for half an hour
3. Strain the liquid and set it with a quarter can of Rhine wine, a quarter kilo sugar, lemon peels and cinnamon
4. As soon as it boils, add the apples and let them cook until soft, being careful not to overcook them so they remain whole
5. Remove the cooked apples with a spoon
6. Strain the syrup and bring it to boil again with half a lemon until it becomes syrup-like
7. Pour this over the prepared apples
