## Ingredients

- 12 herrings
- 20 white peppercorns
- 3 bay leaves
- 100 g onion
- 10 cloves
- 1 Spanish pepper
- a few sliced lemon slices
- 1 liter good vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings for 24 hours in water with some milk, refreshing the water as needed
2. Cut them open along the belly side, remove the entrails, but leave the head and skin attached
3. Wash them thoroughly inside and out
4. Arrange them in a well-cleaned large bottle, so that the head of one herring lies against the tail of the previous one
5. Arrange the bay leaves, pieces of Spanish pepper, sliced lemon slices and onion slices between the wall of the bottle and the herrings
6. Place the remaining spices in the middle and pour vinegar over the herrings until they are covered
7. Store for at most 4 weeks, as otherwise the bones will entirely dissolve
