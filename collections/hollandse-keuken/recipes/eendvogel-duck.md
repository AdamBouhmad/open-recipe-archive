## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- 100 g butter or margarine
- salt
- chopped sour apples or water (optional)

## Directions

1. Press out the tear glands at the tail until no more fat comes out, or cut them out.
2. Wash the bird, salt it inside and outside.
3. Make the butter or margarine light brown in a roasting pan or stew pot, place the duck in it, baste it on all sides and roast it until light brown and cooked through (approximately 1 1/2 hours).
4. Add some water to the butter or margarine if needed when it threatens to brown.
5. Test if the duck is cooked by pricking with a fork between the joints; it should pass through easily.
6. Make the gravy in the usual way with water.
7. Note: To remove the strong flavor of duck, one may sometimes fill it with chopped sour apples or with water and sew it closed.
