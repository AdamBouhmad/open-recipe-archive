## Ingredients

- veal or a couple of chickens
- water
- salt
- rice or stale white bread pieces
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Cook from veal or a couple of chickens
2. When the meat or chickens are cooked, remove them from the broth and keep them warm
3. In the broth cook rice or pieces of stale white bread until done with the addition of salt, lemon juice and nutmeg
4. The veal or chickens are eaten with egg sauce
