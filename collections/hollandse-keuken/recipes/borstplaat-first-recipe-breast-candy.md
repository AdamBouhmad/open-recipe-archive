## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- essence or cacao or Nescafe

## Directions

1. Moisten sugar with just enough water so it is barely covered.
2. Bring the sugar solution to a boil and cook until it reaches the required thickness.
3. Test the consistency by dipping a wooden stick (e.g., a butter brush handle) that has been in cold water into the sugar solution, then return it to cold water and try to form a firm ball from the adhering sugar solution with your finger.
4. Remove the pan from the heat and add cold water drop by drop until the sugar solution no longer effervesces.
5. Let the mixture become opaque and cool while stirring occasionally.
6. Add a little essence, let the breast candy become thick and fluid, and pour it as quickly as possible into rings that have been in cold water for some time, then placed on smooth white paper.
7. When the breast candy is stiff, flip it with the rings, let the underside dry, and then remove the rings.
