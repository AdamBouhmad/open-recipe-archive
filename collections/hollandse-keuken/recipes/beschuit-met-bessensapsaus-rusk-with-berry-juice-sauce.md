## Ingredients

- 8 large rusks
- 3 dl berry juice
- 3 dl water
- 75 g sugar
- 10 g sago
- piece of cinnamon stick or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare the berry juice sauce in the usual manner (see recipe 748) without sago
2. Soak the rusks one by one in the berry juice sauce
3. Lay them in a shallow dish
4. Pour the remaining sauce, bound with sago, over them
