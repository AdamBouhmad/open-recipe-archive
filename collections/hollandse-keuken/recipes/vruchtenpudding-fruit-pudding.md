## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- thin slices of the best sour apples
- sugar
- butter
- cinnamon
- snippets
- sukade

## Directions

1. Line the mold on the inside with puff pastry
2. Fill it with thin slices of the best sour apples, adding sugar, butter, cinnamon, snippets and sukade
3. Cover everything with puff pastry
4. Bind the mold in a cloth and let it cook for 3 hours
5. One can use any other fruit instead of apples
