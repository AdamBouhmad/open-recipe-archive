## Ingredients

- 1 kg sugar
- 15 g citric acid
- 6 dl water
- juice of 6 oranges
- grated peel of 2 oranges
- juice of 1 lemon
- optionally: juice of 2 mandarins

## Directions

1. Brush 2 oranges clean, grate off the peel and add this with the sugar to the water.
2. Let this mixture simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Dissolve the citric acid in it off the heat and let the liquid cool.
4. Then add the squeezed and sieved fruit juice.
5. Pour the lemonade into boiled bottles and close them with boiled corks.
