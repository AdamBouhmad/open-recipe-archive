## Ingredients

- Pearl barley
- Water
- Wine or berry juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. For every half ounce of pearl barley, add one can of water
2. Let it cook gently for two hours and strain through a cloth
3. You can then use it with the addition of some wine or berry juice, sweetened with sugar to taste
