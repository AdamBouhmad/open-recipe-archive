## Ingredients

- two onions
- flour
- butter
- water
- braising jus
- gherkins
- pimento
- salt

## Directions

1. Braise two onions and a spoon of flour in butter until golden brown
2. Add a couple of glasses of water and braising jus
3. Add gherkins cut into pieces, pimento, and salt
4. Once the gherkins are soft, place the veal roast in it
5. Let it heat through in the sauce
