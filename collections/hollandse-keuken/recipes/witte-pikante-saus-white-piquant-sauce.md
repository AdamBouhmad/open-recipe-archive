## Ingredients

- 1/2 l bouillon
- small carrot
- sprig of parsley
- pinch of thyme
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 dl Madeira or white wine

## Directions

1. Let the herbs steep in the bouillon, strain and prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Remove from heat and blend with the egg yolk beaten with the wine or Madeira and serve with calf's meat, timbal, etc.
