## Ingredients

- 26 to 27 pounds berries per anchor
- Equal amount of common baster sugar
- Rainwater

## Directions

1. Drain the berries and rub them through a sieve
2. Take a properly sulfured and aired vessel
3. Put the juice in the vessel with the sugar dissolved in rainwater
4. Press out the seeds several times and add that juice to the rest until the vessel is full
5. Lay the vessel slightly higher at the back than at the front and let it ferment
6. After fermentation, wrap a cloth around the stopper and drive it in, not too tight, gradually tightening it each day
7. Let the vessel lie in the cellar until Christmas or New Year, when the wine is clear enough to tap
8. Handle it further as with ordinary wine
