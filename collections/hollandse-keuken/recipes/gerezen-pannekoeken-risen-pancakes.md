## Ingredients

- 400 g flour or pancake flour
- approximately 3/4 liter milk mixed with hot water
- 15 g yeast
- 75 g lard, salad oil or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Add enough lukewarm liquid to the flour so it does not fly about and stir out the lumps from this batter
2. Gradually add the remaining liquid and the yeast mixed with lukewarm milk
3. Let the batter rise, covered, in a warm place for three quarters of an hour to 1 hour
4. Stir the salt through
5. Heat enough lard, salad oil or margarine in the pancake pan so the bottom is just sufficiently greased
6. Pour enough batter so the bottom is well covered and fry the pancake on a fairly brisk fire on both sides until light brown and cooked through
7. Serve with brown sugar or syrup
