## Ingredients

- 100 g almond macaroons
- 200 g old bread without crust
- 3 dl milk
- 50 g sugar
- 3 eggs
- 50 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Soak the almond macaroons and the bread in the warm milk, rub both completely fine
2. Add the butter or margarine
3. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar
4. Add the finely made mass to this and mix it as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white
5. Grease a warm pudding mold with some butter or margarine (also the lid), sprinkle some fine breadcrumbs in it and add the mixture, but ensure the mold is filled only 3/4 full
6. Close it firmly and cook the pudding 1.5 hours au bain-marie, but ensure the water is always refilled to just below the rim of the mold
7. Let the pudding dry out a little without the lid and serve with warm vanilla, foam or rum sauce
