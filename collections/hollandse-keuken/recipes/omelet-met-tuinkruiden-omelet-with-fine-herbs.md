## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons water
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley, tarragon, pimpernel, chervil, chives
- 30 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper and salt, add most of the finely chopped herbs and the water and cook as described in recipe 383.
2. Place on a warmed plate and sprinkle the remaining herbs over it.
