## Ingredients

- ½ pound best rice
- 1 quart sweet milk
- 6 egg yolks, beaten well
- 1 package almond press
- 4 tablespoons loaf sugar
- grated lemon zest
- ½ pound melted butter
- egg whites beaten to foam

## Directions

1. Cook ½ pound rice and 1 quart sweet milk until done.
2. When almost cold, beat together 6 egg yolks, almond press, 4 tablespoons loaf sugar, and grated lemon zest.
3. Stir in ½ pound melted butter.
4. Just before baking, lightly fold in the egg whites beaten to foam.
5. Bake slowly with heat on top, in hot ash.
