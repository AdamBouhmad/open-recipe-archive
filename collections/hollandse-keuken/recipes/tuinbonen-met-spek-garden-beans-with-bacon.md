## Ingredients

- 3 kg garden beans
- salt
- 200 g bacon
- parsley
- bean herb

## Directions

1. Cook the beans until done as indicated in the previous recipe
2. Mix them with the finely chopped bean herb and parsley
3. Stir the cooked or fried bacon cut into cubes or slices through them
