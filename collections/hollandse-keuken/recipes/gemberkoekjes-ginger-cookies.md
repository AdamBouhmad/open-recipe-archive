## Ingredients

- 500 g flour
- 400 g butter or margarine
- 400 g sugar
- 100 g fresh ginger
- 100 g almonds
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cream the butter until fluffy, add the sugar, the sifted flour, the peeled and chopped or ground almonds, the ginger cut into pieces and the beaten egg.
2. Roll this dough out to 1/2 cm thickness on a sheet dusted with flour.
3. Cut out cookies with cookie cutters, place them on a greased baking sheet, brush the top with some beaten egg yolk mixed with a little water.
4. Bake the cookies in the upper part of a fairly warm oven until light brown and done (10 to 15 minutes), remove them from the baking sheet and let them cool.
