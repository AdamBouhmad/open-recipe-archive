## Ingredients

- One measure flour
- One quarter pound fine salt
- One whole egg
- Water
- One ounce lard

## Directions

1. Spread flour on the work surface and make a mound with a hole in the middle
2. Place in the hole: one quarter pound fine salt, one whole egg, water, and one ounce lard
3. Knead together
4. Let the dough rest for two hours
5. Roll out very thin
6. Pour melted lard (three ounces) over the rolled dough each time
7. Continue rolling with lard under the dough, as with butter feuilleté
8. Let rest, then roll four more times
9. Use as needed
