## Ingredients

- 1 kg Brussels sprouts
- 60 g butter or margarine
- 500 g chestnuts
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the Brussels sprouts in the manner as described in the previous recipe.
2. Make a deep cross on the chestnuts on the rounded side, wash them and let them cook until tender in enough boiling water to just cover them (30 minutes to 45 minutes).
3. Drain them, let them dry briefly, peel them and shake them with the butter or margarine through the Brussels sprouts.
4. Stew everything together for 5-7 minutes more and grate some nutmeg over them in the bowl.
