## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 10 g yeast
- 7.5 g salt
- 2.5 dl milk
- 75 g currants
- 75 g raisins
- 25 g candied peel

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for white bread.
2. Knead in the well-washed currants and raisins, which have been pitted, and the chopped candied peel, as soon as the starter has been kneaded with the remaining milk and remaining flour.
3. Bake the bread as indicated in recipe 835.
