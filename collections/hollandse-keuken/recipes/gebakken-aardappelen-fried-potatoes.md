## Ingredients

- 1 kg cold cooked potatoes without crumbs
- approximately 75 g fat or oil

## Directions

1. Slice the potatoes into not too thick slices.
2. Heat the fat in the frying pan until blue smoke rises from it.
3. Lay the slices in, fry them on both sides quickly until light brown and crispy.
4. Turn them with a knife or pancake turner.
5. Shake the potatoes around if many need to be fried at once.
