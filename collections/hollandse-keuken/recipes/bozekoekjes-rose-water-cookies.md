## Ingredients

- One piece stale white bread, without crust
- Cooked rainwater for soaking
- 8 eggs with white
- 3 spoons rosewater

## Directions

1. Soak bread in cooked rainwater
2. Beat eggs with white finely
3. Mix in rosewater
4. Fry well in fat
