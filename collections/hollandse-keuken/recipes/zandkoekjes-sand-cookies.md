## Ingredients

- 300 g flour or 50 g cornstarch and 250 g flour
- 200 g butter or margarine
- 150 g powdered sugar or brown sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour or flour mixture and the powdered or brown sugar
2. Knead both with the butter or margarine together
3. Roll out the dough on a floured board with a floured rolling pin to 1/2 cm thickness
4. Cut out cookies with cookie cutters, cookie stamps, glasses or cups
5. Place them on a greased baking sheet with some distance between them
6. Bake them in the upper part of a moderately warm oven until lightly brown and done (approximately 15 to 20 minutes)
7. Remove them immediately from the baking sheet and let them cool
8. If the dough becomes too soft from kneading to roll out, let it stiffen on a cool place for some time covered
9. Alternative method: form the dough into a thick roll after kneading with the diameter of a silver guilder, let it stiffen and cut slices from it
