## Ingredients

- 3 kg thick eel
- 3/4 liter vinegar
- 2.5 deciliters water
- 35 g white gelatin or 30 g powdered gelatin
- 4 lemon slices
- a piece of mace
- a piece of Spanish pepper
- 4 bay leaves
- 15 white peppercorns
- 15 g salt
- parchment paper
- twine
- labels

## Directions

1. Cut the eel into large pieces, clean them in the usual way, wash them and cook them in the vinegar, which has been brought to a boil with the water, salt, bay leaves, mace and peppercorns, until done (6 to 8 minutes)
2. Remove them from the liquid and place them in well-cleaned jars or bottles, arrange the sliced lemon slices, Spanish pepper and a bay leaf between the glass and the eel
3. Dissolve the soaked gelatin in the cooking liquid, strain the liquid through a dense cloth rinsed in hot water, check if there is enough salt and pour it, after it has cooled slightly, over the eel
4. Make sure the eel is well covered with the aspic
5. Close the jars with damp parchment paper, store the eel for at least 3 weeks before use and no longer than about 6 weeks
