## Ingredients

- 3.75 kg peaches (+ 3 kg without pits)
- 2.25 kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- twine
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- labels

## Directions

1. Prepare this jam entirely as apricot jam (recipe 990).
