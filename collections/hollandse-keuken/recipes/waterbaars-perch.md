## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg perch
- water
- salt
- 6 slices of white bread
- 4 slices of rye bread
- parsley
- parsley roots

## Directions

1. Clean the fish by removing the entrails, scraping off the scales and washing it well (if the fish is slimy, gently rub it with a little salt)
2. Cook it until done in boiling water with salt (not as much as for other fish) and allow a few peeled parsley roots to cook along with it (approximately 4-5 minutes)
3. Immediately place the fish with as much cooking liquid as possible in the bottom part of the fish plate, surround it with a few fresh sprigs of parsley
4. Serve with very thin square slices of white bread, trimmed of crust and thickly spread, topped with an equally large and similarly thickly spread piece of rye bread
