## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter melk
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 50 g suiker
- 4 eieren
- 1/2 stokje vanille or vanillesuiker
- 75 g amandelen

## Directions

1. Mix the maizena with the egg yolks and most of the suiker.
2. Add enough cold milk to obtain a fairly thin paste.
3. Bring the remaining milk with vanilla slowly to a boil, pour enough of it to the maizena so that the yolks are well warmed.
4. Return everything to the pan and let the custard cook, stirring vigorously with an egg whisk, for 3-4 minutes.
5. Let it cool, stirring occasionally, and pour it into a custard dish.
6. Remove the vanilla.
7. Soak the almonds in boiling water for some time, peel them and slice them.
