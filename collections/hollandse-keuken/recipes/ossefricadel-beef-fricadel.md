## Ingredients

- 1 Dutch pound of beef slices
- 2½ ounces kidney fat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Water

## Directions

1. Lay the beef slices on a board and sprinkle with salt, pepper and cloves
2. Divide the fat so that you can roll a piece of it in each slice
3. Roll up the slices and tie them with string
4. Lay them closely in an earthenware pan, add some water
5. Braise them until they are brown
