## Ingredients

- 3 bunches endive or curly endive
- 1 egg
- 3 spoonfuls salad oil
- 2 spoonfuls vinegar or herb vinegar
- optionally a splash of Maggi's Aroma
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- optionally sliced leek, onion, or chives

## Directions

1. Remove the outer wilted leaves of the endive, cut off the hard green points and a piece of the stalk and then cut it into fine strips.
2. Wash them, let them drain as dry as possible in a colander or shake them in a salad spinner.
3. Prepare the endive as described in the previous recipe.
4. Note: See note under recipe 479.
5. Vary the endive salad by occasionally mixing it with very finely cut cooked beet.
