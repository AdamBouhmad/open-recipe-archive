## Ingredients

- 4 filet steaks of approximately 100 g (thick, round shape)
- Approximately 100 g butter or margarine
- 5 g salt
- Pepper
- Milk

## Directions

1. Wash the chateaubriands, dry them with a clean cloth or scrape them, rub them with pepper and salt and fry them immediately in butter or margarine.
