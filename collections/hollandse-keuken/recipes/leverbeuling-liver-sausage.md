## Ingredients

- liver
- leaf fat
- hardtack or breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- mixed spice
- pork intestines

## Directions

1. Cook the liver with the leaf fat until done
2. Chop it very finely
3. Add hardtack or breadcrumbs, along with some pepper, salt and mixed spice
4. Stuff into pork intestines
5. When stuffed, put the sausages in a kettle and bring just to a boil
