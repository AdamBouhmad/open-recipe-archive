## Ingredients

- 1 l purslane liquid with some purslane leaves in it
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- (1 egg)
- diced fried bread

## Directions

1. Fry the flour in the butter or margarine and add the purslane liquid gradually in small portions
2. Let the soup cook while stirring for 10 minutes and then carefully add to the egg, which has been beaten in the soup tureen
3. Serve with diced fried bread
4. N.B. If you want to make this soup from fresh purslane, you need 150 g purslane, which you cook until done in 1 l boiling water, after which you prepare soup from it as indicated above
