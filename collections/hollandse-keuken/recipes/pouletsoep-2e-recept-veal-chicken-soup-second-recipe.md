## Ingredients

- 300 g veal chicken
- 1 liter boiling water
- 50 g veal ground meat
- 30 g vermicelli
- piece of mace
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare according to the method for veal chicken soup, first recipe. Because more meat is used in this soup, presumably no bouillon cube needs to be added.
