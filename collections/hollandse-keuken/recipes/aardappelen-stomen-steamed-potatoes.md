## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg potatoes
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, remove the eyes, wash them, and check again that all eyes are removed.
2. Lay them on the perforated bottom of the steaming pan.
3. Let them steam until cooked (approximately 45 minutes), sprinkle them with some salt and let them dry by removing the pan with water underneath.
4. Shake them from time to time until they are crumbly.
