## Ingredients

- 2 heads of chicory
- ¾ head of endive
- ½ head of lettuce
- a few slices of tomato and cucumber
- 2 tablespoons oil
- a bit of lemon juice
- a small bit of soy or Maggi's Aroma or marmite

## Directions

1. Clean the vegetables. Cut the stalk part wedge-shaped from the chicory head. Shred the chicory, endive and some of the lettuce. Keep a few lettuce leaves for garnish. Mix the oil with the lemon juice, soy, Maggi's Aroma or marmite mixed with water. Stir the shredded vegetables lightly through the salad dressing with the salad spoon and fork. Lay the salad dressing around the mixed salad and garnish the whole with the tomato and cucumber slices.
