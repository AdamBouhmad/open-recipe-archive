## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg sorrel
- 75 g currants
- 75 g syrup or sugar
- 1 egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the sorrel until done as described in the previous recipe
2. Let it drain in a colander and mix it with the sugar or syrup and the currants, which have been rinsed with lukewarm water and briefly heated in boiling water
3. If it is still very moist, add the egg that has been beaten in a bowl
