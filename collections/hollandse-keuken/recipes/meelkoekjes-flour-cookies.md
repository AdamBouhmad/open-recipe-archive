## Ingredients

- sweet milk or cream
- fine flour
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir fine flour into sweet milk or cream very smoothly and let it cook through briefly
2. Remove from heat and add salt
3. Mix further with flour into a stiff dough
4. Roll out, form cookies with a glass
5. Bake slowly in butter until done
