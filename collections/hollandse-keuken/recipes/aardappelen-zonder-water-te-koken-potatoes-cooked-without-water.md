## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt

## Directions

1. Let the potatoes drain well on a colander
2. Place them in an iron pot with the necessary salt
3. Close the lid of the pot so tightly that no steam can escape
4. Hang them over the fire until they are cooked
5. They will cook in the steam trapped in the pot and will be very crumbly
