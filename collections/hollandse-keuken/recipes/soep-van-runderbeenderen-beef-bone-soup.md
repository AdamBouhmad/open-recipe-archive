## Ingredients

- beef bones
- water
- salt
- rice
- vegetables
- ground mace

## Directions

1. Have the bones well crushed
2. Cook them thoroughly the day before use
3. Strain the broth through a sieve
4. When cool, remove the fat
5. Proceed as with the beef shank soup above
6. The meatballs can be removed if desired
