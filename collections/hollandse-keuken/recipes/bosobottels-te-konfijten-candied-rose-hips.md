## Ingredients

- rose hips
- half kilogram sugar
- 4 ounces rose hips

## Directions

1. Cut the fruit lengthwise open, with a spoon remove the pits, wash, cook a quarter hour in plenty of water and drain
2. Cook half a kilogram sugar until it becomes syrupy, add 4 ounces rose hips, and when everything has cooled put it in bottles
