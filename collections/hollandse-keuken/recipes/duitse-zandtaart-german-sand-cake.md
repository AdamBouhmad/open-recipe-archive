## Ingredients

- 7 eggs
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g flour
- 150 g sugar
- vanilla or grated lemon zest
- icing
- French candied fruits

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks completely smooth with the sugar, add the flavoring.
2. Sift the flour, add the hard butter or margarine in small, equal pieces.
3. Whip the egg whites as stiffly as possible, stir in the yolks with a few strokes and then, as lightly as possible, the chopped butter or margarine and flour.
4. Put the batter immediately into a greased tart pan dusted with powdered sugar and flour.
5. Place the pan in a hot oven, check carefully after 5 minutes to see if the cake is not browning too much, otherwise lay a buttered piece of gray paper on it and bake it light brown and done (25 to 30 minutes).
6. Test if it is done by inserting a knitting needle, which should come out dry.
7. Turn the cake out onto a cake rack, let it cool and garnish it if desired with icing and French candied fruits.
