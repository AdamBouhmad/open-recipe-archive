## Ingredients

- 125 g sweet almonds mixed with 4 bitter almonds
- 125 g sugar
- 1 egg
- some grated lemon zest or vanilla pulp

## Directions

1. Peel the soaked almonds.
2. Grind them twice with the sugar, egg and flavoring through the almond mill.
3. Store the almond paste in well-sealed jam jars.
4. Almond paste becomes better if it stands for some time, for example at least 2 to 3 weeks.
