## Ingredients

- 120 g flour
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g sugar
- 5 eggs
- 150 g butter
- 60 g powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon Nescafé
- 3 tablespoons water
- 1 sheet gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare the cake like the German sand cake (see recipe 863).
2. Let it cool and cut it into two or three layers.
3. For the mocha, stir the butter until creamy and add the sifted powdered sugar.
