## Ingredients

- 50 pears (fine table pears)
- Sugar syrup (500 g sugar per 1 liter water)

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, leave them whole with the stem on, or cut them in half or in quarters and remove the cores.
2. Arrange them neatly in well-cleaned bottles up to 3 cm below the rim.
3. Pour enough sugar syrup over them so that the pears are well submerged.
4. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clips.
5. Heat them for 30-45 minutes to 80-90° C.
6. Let the bottles cool with the clips on, remove the clips and label the bottles with name and date.
7. (For corked bottles see the rules on page 366).
