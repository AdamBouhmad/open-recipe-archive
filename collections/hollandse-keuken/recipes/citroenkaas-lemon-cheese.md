## Ingredients

- 500 g brown sugar
- 125 g butter
- juice and peel of 3 lemons
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the lemons, grate the peel, squeeze them out.
2. Beat in a well-tinned bowl or with 2 silver forks in a clean, enameled pan the eggs with the brown sugar and the grated lemon peel until a light mass.
3. Add the butter and the strained lemon juice and let the mass, beating well, become thick in a bain-marie and pour it directly into the cleaned jars, which have been treated as indicated in the 'rules' on page 369.
