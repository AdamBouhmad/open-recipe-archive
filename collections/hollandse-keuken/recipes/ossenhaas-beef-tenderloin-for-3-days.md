## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef tenderloin
- 150 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- 10 g salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Braise the beef tenderloin, as it is a fine meat, in butter or margarine, but not in fat
2. Allow 15 minutes braising time per 500 g, but for the whole tenderloin no more than 60 minutes, as the length increases but not the thickness
3. Finish the jus with milk in the manner indicated for beef steak
