## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces flour
- 2.5 ounces suet
- 1 ounce raisins
- 4 eggs
- nutmeg
- mace
- 0.25 can milk
- flour for dusting cloth

## Directions

1. Stir 2.5 ounces flour, 2.5 ounces suet, 1 ounce raisins, 4 eggs, nutmeg and mace well together with 0.25 can milk
2. dust a cloth or napkin with flour
3. put everything in it
4. bind it up
5. let it cook for 1 hour
