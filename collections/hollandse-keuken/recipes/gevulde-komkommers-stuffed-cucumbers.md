## Ingredients

- 2 small cucumbers
- 200 g ground pork meat
- 35 g old bread
- 20 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut off the bitter ends if necessary and cut them in half lengthwise.
2. Remove the seeds with a spoon and cook them in abundant boiling salted water until done (10-20 minutes).
3. In the meantime, mix the ground meat with soaked bread, pepper, salt and nutmeg.
4. Melt the butter or margarine in the pan, add the meat and let it cook, stirring occasionally, until done but not brown.
5. Fill the cucumbers with it, place them in a fireproof dish, pour bouillon or lean jus with water over them, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, place pieces of butter or margarine here and there, clean the edges and let them brown in a moderately warm oven.
