## Ingredients

- 500 g apricots or 300 g dried apricots
- 350 g sugar
- 2½ dl water
- some lemon peel
- 1 liter brandy

## Directions

1. Wash the fruit.
2. Wash the dried fruit with hot water.
3. Let them drain.
4. Make a sugar syrup.
5. Add the lemon peel and the fruit to it.
6. Let them boil for 1-2 minutes in the hot liquid.
7. Remove the pan from the fire.
8. Let the fresh fruit stand for 24 hours in a bowl with the hot sugar syrup.
9. Let the dried apricots first boil for 10 minutes and stand for 48 hours in the sugar syrup.
10. When the fruit is soft, transfer it to the clean stoppered bottle (see page 369 3°).
11. Cook the sugar solution down a bit more on the fire and let it cool.
12. Pour it over the fruit.
13. When the mixture has cooled completely, add the brandy and seal the bottle hermetically.
14. Store the apricots for 3 months before using them.
