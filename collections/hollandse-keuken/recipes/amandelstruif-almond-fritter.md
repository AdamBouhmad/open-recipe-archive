## Ingredients

- 5 ounces ground almonds
- 5 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls flour
- some crushed cinnamon
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Add 5 eggs to 5 ounces ground almonds
2. Stir very finely with 2 spoonfuls flour
3. Add some crushed cinnamon and milk
4. Bake in the frying pan
5. Sprinkle sugar over when serving
