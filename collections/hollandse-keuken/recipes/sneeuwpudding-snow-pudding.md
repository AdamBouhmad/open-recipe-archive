## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 1 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- 90 g cornstarch (Duryea brand)
- 80 g sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook stiff cornstarch porridge in the usual way, but let the vanilla cook in it and add the sugar, mix the porridge off the heat with the very stiffly beaten egg white.
2. Pour the mixture into a mold rinsed with water, let it cool.
3. Turn the pudding out onto a wet plate and serve with pitted cherries or fruit juice sauce.
