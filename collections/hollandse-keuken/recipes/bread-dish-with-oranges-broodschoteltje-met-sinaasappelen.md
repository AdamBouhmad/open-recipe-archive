## Ingredients

- 200 g old bread without crust
- approximately 4 1/2 deciliters milk
- salt
- juice of 4 oranges
- zest of 1 orange
- 1 to 2 eggs
- approximately 100 g sugar
- 50 to 60 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Rub the bread, which has been carefully removed of crusts, completely smooth in the boiling milk to which some salt has been added.
2. Stir the butter or margarine until it has become thick, white and airy, add the egg yolks, sugar and grated orange zest to it and rub the sugar smooth.
3. Mix this mixture with the soaked bread, add the orange juice and lastly as quickly as possible the stiffly beaten egg white to it.
4. Let the mixture in a fireproof dish in a moderately warm oven become light brown and rise.
