## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 200 g butter or margarine
- 200 g flour
- 200 g sugar
- 200 g currants and raisins
- some lemon juice or rum

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter as described in the previous recipe.
2. Mix the batter, which should look thick and airy, with the currants and raisins, which have been stripped of their stems, washed and dried, and with the lemon juice or rum.
3. Put it in a greased loaf pan, which is lined on the inside along the bottom and sides with white paper (for example, grease-proof paper).
4. Bake the cake in this in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done (1.5 hours).
5. Test if it is done by inserting a knitting needle, which should then come out dry.
6. Turn it out and let it cool.
