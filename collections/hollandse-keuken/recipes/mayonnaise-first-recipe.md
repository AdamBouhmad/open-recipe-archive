## Ingredients

- 1 egg or 2 egg yolks
- 3 1/2 dl salad oil
- approximately 1/2 dl vinegar
- 1 1/2 teaspoon mustard or mustard powder
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Stir the egg or egg yolks with mustard, salt and pepper for 10 minutes with a good tinned whisk.
2. Add a small splash of vinegar and then very small amounts of oil at a time until a thick sauce is obtained.
3. If the sauce becomes too thick and can no longer be stirred well, thin it carefully with some vinegar.
4. Continue until all ingredients are used and a good-tasting, white sauce is obtained, which can be served with cold meat and cold fish.
5. Store in a jar under a layer of oil.
