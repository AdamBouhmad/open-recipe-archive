## Ingredients

- 300 g meat leftovers
- 125 g stale bread
- 1 egg
- 50 g onions
- Some parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 50 g fat from gravy, butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Prepare the minced meat as indicated in recipe 91.
2. Form it into one large or four small balls and fry them with some remaining fat from the gravy in the frying pan on all sides until brown.
3. Finish the gravy with some water.
