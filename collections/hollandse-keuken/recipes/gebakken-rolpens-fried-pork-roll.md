## Ingredients

- 8 slices pork roll
- some flour
- 50 g pork roll fat or butter
- lard, butter or margarine
- approximately 2 sour apples

## Directions

1. Slice the pork roll into slices of approximately 1 cm thickness, remove the membrane.
2. Roll the slices in flour, heat the fat in a frying pan and fry the pork roll on both sides brown.
3. Core the apples, peel them, slice them into as many slices as there are pork roll slices and fry them carefully on both sides brown in the same fat.
4. Place a slice of apple on each slice of pork roll.
5. Make the gravy in the usual manner.
