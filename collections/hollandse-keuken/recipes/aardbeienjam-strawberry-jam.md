## Ingredients

- 3 kg strawberries (cleaned)
- 2¼ kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- cellophane
- elastic bands
- string
- labels
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the strawberries carefully, wash them, let them cook without sugar, stirring occasionally
2. Add the sugar and let them continue cooking until they reach the required thickness (test by dropping a drop in cold water—it should not spread but sink whole)
3. Remove the foam with a flat spoon
4. Add some lemon juice, fill the cleaned jars or bottles as quickly as possible, close them with damp parchment paper or cellophane
5. Fasten this with string or elastic band and label the cleaned jars or bottles with name and date
