## Ingredients

- three partridges
- livers of three partridges
- hard boiled eggs
- breadcrumb
- sliced bacon
- six to eight celery tops
- good bouillon
- butter
- potato starch
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop the livers of three partridges with hard boiled eggs and breadcrumb together
2. Fill the partridges with this mixture
3. Cook these for some minutes
4. Cover them with sliced bacon and let them braise slowly
5. Meanwhile cut the white part of six to eight celery tops into pieces one inch long
6. Let them cook for one hour in good bouillon
7. When the sauce boils well, add a piece of butter, potato starch, pepper and salt
8. Strain them and add some lemon juice
9. Serve this sauce with the birds
