## Ingredients

- 1 kg veal breast (without bone)
- 500 g ground veal
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 75 g old bread
- 1/2 tin truffles or truffle peelings
- 200 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Prepare the ground meat in the usual manner, but stir in finely cut truffles
2. Cut the veal breast with a sharp knife along one length side between the membranes open, wash the meat, spread the ground meat between the layers and sew the meat closed
3. Rub it with salt and braise it as indicated for veal fricandeau (35-40 minutes per 500 g)
4. Finish the jus with water if necessary
5. Cut the meat with a very sharp knife into thin slices
