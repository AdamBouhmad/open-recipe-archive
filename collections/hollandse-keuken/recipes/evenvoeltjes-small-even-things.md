## Ingredients

- 10 beaten eggs
- 2½ ounces wheat flour
- 2 ounces melted butter
- lukewarm milk
- yeast
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a batter of 10 beaten eggs, 2½ ounces wheat flour, 2 ounces melted butter, lukewarm milk, yeast, and a little salt.
2. Let it rise for a while.
3. Bake in a poffertje pan and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar when serving.
