## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g powdered sugar
- 100 g flour
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- some salt
- 2 egg whites (approximately 1/2 dl)

## Directions

1. Cream the butter or margarine until fluffy, add the unbeaten egg white, the sifted powdered sugar, some scraped vanilla and lastly the sifted flour.
2. Put the dough in a piping bag with a smooth nozzle and pipe long strips onto a greased baking sheet, making sure they are spaced some distance apart.
3. Bake them in a moderately warm oven.
