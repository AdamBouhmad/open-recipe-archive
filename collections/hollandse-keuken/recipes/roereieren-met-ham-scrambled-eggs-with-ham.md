## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 100 g ham
- 30 g butter or margarine
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper and salt
2. Add the finely chopped ham and milk
3. Prepare as described in recipe 395
4. Serve with bread, lettuce or spinach
