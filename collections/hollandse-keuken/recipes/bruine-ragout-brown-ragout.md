## Ingredients

- 1/2 l dark bouillon or lean jus with water
- piece of Spanish pepper
- couple of bay leaves
- piece of onion
- couple of cloves
- piece of carrot
- 55 g flour
- 55 g butter or margarine
- 50 g ground veal
- 1/2 tin of mushrooms
- approximately 450 g pieces of ox tongue
- English sauce
- soya

## Directions

1. Brown the butter or margarine and flour, add the strained bouillon in which the spices have been infused and let the sauce simmer stirring until cooked (10 minutes).
2. Warm in it the cooked meat balls and pieces of ox tongue.
3. Remove the ragout from the heat and mix with the mushrooms, English sauce and soya and fill pastries, galettes etc. with it.
