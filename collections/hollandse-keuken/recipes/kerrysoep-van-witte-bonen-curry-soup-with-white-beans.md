## Ingredients

- 1 l bean cooking liquid with leftover beans
- 15 g flour
- 20 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 50 g onion
- 1 teaspoon curry
- piece of mace
- 1 bay leaf
- parsley

## Directions

1. In the bean cooking liquid, to which mace, bay leaf and parsley have been added, let the leftover beans cook until fine. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve and rub the beans through until only the skins remain.
2. Melt the butter or margarine, fry the curry, the chopped onion and the flour in it, gradually add the bean liquid while stirring and let the soup cook and thicken while stirring (10 minutes).
3. If desired, pour it through a sieve, serve with small cubes of bread fried in butter.
