## Ingredients

- 125 g almonds
- 185 g sugar
- 5 g fine cinnamon
- grated zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1/4 grated nutmeg
- 2 1/2 g crushed cloves
- 3 egg whites
- 4 3 wafer sheets

## Directions

1. Peel the soaked almonds, grind or chop them as finely as possible, mix them with the spices and lastly as airy as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
2. Lay on square pieces of wafer sheet, which are laid on a baking sheet, each time a teaspoon of this dough, bake the cookies in a moderately warm oven light brown (20 to 30 minutes).
3. Then break the wafer sheet away along the edge of the turennes.
