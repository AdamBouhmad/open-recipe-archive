## Ingredients

- 1/2 to 3/4 kg rhubarb
- 100 g sugar
- some mixed cornstarch (optional)
- 1 egg (optional)
- a pinch of chalk (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut young rhubarb stalks unpeeled, old stalks peeled, into chunks, wash them.
2. Set them on with a little water and let the rhubarb cook gently until soft (about 5 minutes), stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
3. Rub it through with a wooden spoon, mix the sauce with the sugar.
4. Bind it if necessary with some mixed cornstarch, which must cook for 3 minutes, or pour it into the egg beaten in a bowl.
5. When using chalk, stir in the chalk first, then the sugar into the sauce.
