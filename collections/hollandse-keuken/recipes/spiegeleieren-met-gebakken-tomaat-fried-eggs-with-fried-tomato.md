## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 12 slices tomato (2 tomatoes)
- 35 g butter or margarine
- parsley

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs as indicated in recipe 375 and place them on a heated plate.
2. In the browned butter or margarine, quickly fry the tomato slices on both sides until light brown.
3. Arrange the fried tomato around the fried eggs.
4. Place a sprig of parsley here and there and present the fried eggs with pepper and salt.
