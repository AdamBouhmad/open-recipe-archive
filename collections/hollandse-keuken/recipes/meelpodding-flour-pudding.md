## Ingredients

- 0.5 ounce melted butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 grated lemon zest
- cinnamon
- circa 1 ounce bread sugar
- 5 ounces fine flour

## Directions

1. Stir 0.5 ounce melted butter for a very long time
2. add 4 eggs while stirring, 1 grated lemon zest, cinnamon and circa 1 ounce bread sugar
3. add 5 ounces fine flour and stir the flour to the thickness of ordinary pudding
4. fill a mold with it
5. let it cook vigorously for 3 hours
