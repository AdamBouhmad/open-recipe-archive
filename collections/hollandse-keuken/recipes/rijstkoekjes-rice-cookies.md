## Ingredients

- 400 g cooked rice
- 80 g flour
- 80 g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 80 g butter or margarine or 3/4 dl salad oil
- scraped vanilla or grated lemon zest or fine cinnamon
- some milk

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the sugar, add the flour, the flavoring and the cooked rice.
2. Then fold in as lightly as possible the stiffly beaten egg white and if the batter is still too thick, add some milk.
3. Fry small, round cookies from this batter in the frying pan with butter, margarine or salad oil on a gentle fire until light brown and cooked through.
4. Serve sprinkled with some crushed sugar or powdered sugar.
