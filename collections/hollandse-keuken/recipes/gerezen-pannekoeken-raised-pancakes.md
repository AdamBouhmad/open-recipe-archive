## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces wheat flour
- 2.5 ounces buckwheat flour
- salt
- 3 eggs
- warm milk
- yeast
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a thick batter with 2.5 ounces wheat flour, 2.5 ounces buckwheat flour, salt, 3 eggs and warm milk
2. Add yeast and let rise
3. Fry in butter in a pan
