## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 70 g flour
- 1 dl beer or sparkling water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the egg with the pepper and salt
2. Add the flour and enough liquid until the flour no longer rises in clouds
3. Stir out any lumps from the batter and add the remaining liquid
4. Heat frying fat in a deep iron pot
5. Let the frying iron warm in the fat and dip it in the batter
6. Let the batter dry briefly and fry the fritters in the hot fat until light brown
7. Let them drain on gray paper and fill them warm with ragout
8. If necessary, preserve them completely cooled in tins
9. If they have become soft, place the fritters nested inside each other (to maintain shape) briefly in the oven
