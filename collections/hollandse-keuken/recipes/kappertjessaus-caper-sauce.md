## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or bouillon
- small carrot
- sprig of parsley
- 4 white peppercorns
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 1/2 tablespoons capers
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let the spices steep in the liquid, strain it, prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce), mix it away from the heat with the egg yolk, the capers and lemon juice to taste
2. Serve with turbot, sole, calf's tongue, eel, etc.
