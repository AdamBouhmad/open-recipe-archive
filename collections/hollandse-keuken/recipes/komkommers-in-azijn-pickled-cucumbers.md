## Ingredients

- 6 cucumbers
- 1½ bottle pickling vinegar
- 2½ g turmeric
- 5 g dried ginger
- 2 Spanish peppers
- 50 g mustard seed
- some salad oil

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers thinly. Test the ends to see if they are not bitter. Cut them off if necessary. Cut them lengthwise in half, scoop out the seeds with a spoon and cut the cucumbers into cubes.
2. Set them aside for one night sprinkled with salt, then let them drain as dry as possible in a colander and put them in a well-cleaned Cologne pot or jam jars.
3. Arrange the spices between them, but first tie the mustard seed in a piece of cheesecloth. Pour vinegar over them so that the pieces are amply covered and pour a layer of salad oil on top.
4. Close the pots with damp parchment paper. Provide them with a label with name and date.
