## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 1 egg (optional)
- 3 dl lukewarm milk
- 15 g yeast
- 75 to 100 g currants, raisins and mixed peel
- 60 g lard
- salad oil, butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the egg and add to the flour with enough lukewarm milk so the flour does not fly about
2. Stir out the lumps from this batter
3. Add the remaining lukewarm milk, well-washed currants and raisins with stems removed, the chopped mixed peel, salt and the yeast mixed with lukewarm milk
4. Let the batter rise, covered, in a warm place for half an hour to three quarters of an hour
5. Fry round cookies from the batter using lard, salad oil, butter or margarine (or half lard, half butter) until light brown and cooked through
6. Test if they are cooked by pricking with a steel fork, which should come out dry
7. Serve with syrup or brown sugar
