## Ingredients

- 1 kg rib steak
- 150 g beef fat, butter or margarine
- 15 g salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. For preparation see roastbeef (recipe 227). The roasting time is however 25 to 30 minutes per 500 g if you want the rib steak well done, and 20 minutes when it need not be completely done.
