## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 l milk
- peel of 2 lemons
- 40 g flour
- 80 g sugar
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Slowly bring 1 1/4 l milk to a boil with the thinly pared peel of a lemon.
2. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, mix it with 30 g sugar, form small balls and cook these in the boiling milk briefly.
3. Remove them with a skimming spoon.
4. Mix the flour and sugar with the remaining milk gradually until a thin paste without lumps.
5. Pour this into the boiling milk while stirring and let the soup simmer while stirring for 10 minutes.
6. Then carefully pour it with the yolks, which have been beaten in the soup tureen.
7. Place the egg white balls on top and sprinkle with some grated lemon peel.
