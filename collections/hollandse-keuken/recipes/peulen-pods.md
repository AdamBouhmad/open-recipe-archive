## Ingredients

- 1 kg pods
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 15 g sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the pods on both sides twice
2. Wash them and set them on with enough boiling water to just cover the bottom of the pan, and salt
3. Let them cook until done and dry, stirring occasionally (10 to 20 minutes)
4. Add a little water if necessary during cooking to prevent burning
5. Shake them with the butter and add the sugar if they are not sweet enough
