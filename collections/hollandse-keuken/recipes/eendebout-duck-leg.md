## Ingredients

- duck leg
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Well washed and cleaned, sprinkle inside and outside with salt and pepper as desired.
2. Place a piece of butter in the pan and let it brown well.
3. Place the duck leg in it, pouring water over from time to time, and roast until done in an hour and a half.
