## Ingredients

- 2 salt herrings
- tomato purée

## Directions

1. Soak the herring for several hours in water with some milk.
2. Make an incision along the back, cut open the belly side, remove the entrails, soft roe or hard roe, and the black membrane around the entrails.
3. Pull off the skin and cut off the head.
4. Wash the herring thoroughly, carefully remove the backbone and small bones, cut the herring into diagonal pieces, place them in a section of the plate and pour some tomato purée over them.
