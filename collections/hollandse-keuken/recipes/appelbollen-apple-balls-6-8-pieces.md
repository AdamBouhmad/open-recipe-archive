## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter or margarine
- 100 g flour
- some water
- some salt
- approximately 6 sour apples
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 80 g sugar
- 1 teaspoon fine cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846). Peel the cored apples and place them on the dough, which has been rolled out one last time into a large, thin sheet. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the sugar and fine cinnamon and fill the openings of the apples with this mixture. Wrap the dough around them, seal it underneath after making a few folds. Brush the apple balls with egg yolk beaten with water and bake them in a hot oven until light brown and cooked (approximately 15 minutes). Remove from baking sheet and let cool.
