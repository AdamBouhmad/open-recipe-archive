## Ingredients

- 1 bottle red wine
- 1 bottle berry wine
- approximately 100 g sugar
- 1 orange
- 20 cloves
- a few pieces of cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Wash the orange, stick the cloves and a few pieces of cinnamon stick into it and put it in the wine, which is poured into the wine kettle.
2. Close the spout and lid tightly and warm the liquid gradually on a low fire.
3. Let it steep for some time, add the sugar and serve as warm as possible.
