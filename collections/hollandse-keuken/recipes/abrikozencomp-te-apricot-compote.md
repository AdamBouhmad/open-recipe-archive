## Ingredients

- 12 apricots
- 100 g sugar
- approximately 2½ dl water
- ¾ dl Rhine wine (optional)
- some sago (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the apricots carefully, peel them if desired (which is easiest done by holding them in hot water for a few minutes), cut them in half and remove the pits.
2. Let the water cook down with the sugar to a thin syrup.
3. Lay no more than 8 half fruits at a time in it and cook them gently for 2 minutes, but make sure they don't break.
4. Remove them with a skimming spoon, thicken the liquid with a little mixed sago or let it cook down and add the wine.
