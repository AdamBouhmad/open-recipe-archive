## Ingredients

- roasted venison haunch
- butter
- finely chopped shallots
- nutmeg
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted venison haunch into pieces
2. Brown the pieces in butter
3. Add finely chopped shallots, nutmeg, salt and lemon juice
4. Let it stew
