## Ingredients

- 1 kg Brussels lof
- 150 g ham or bacon
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- salt to taste
- 0.5 l milk
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 40 g flour
- 60 g aged Gouda cheese
- 20 g melted butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cook the lof (see recipe 444).
2. Cut the hard-boiled eggs into slices.
3. Make a cheese sauce in the usual manner (see recipe 336), but reserve 20 g of the grated cheese to finish the dish.
4. Wrap each drained lof sprout in a slice of ham or bacon and place them in a fireproof dish.
5. Lay the egg slices on top.
6. Sprinkle with a little table salt.
7. Pour the cheese sauce over it.
8. Sprinkle with the reserved grated cheese and pour the melted butter over it.
9. Let it brown lightly in the oven.
