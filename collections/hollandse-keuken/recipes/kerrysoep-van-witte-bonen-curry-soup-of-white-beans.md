## Ingredients

- 1 l bean liquid with remaining beans
- approximately 15 g flour
- approximately 20 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- 50 g onion
- 1 teaspoon curry
- piece of mace
- 1 bay leaf
- some parsley

## Directions

1. Let the remaining beans cook fine in the bean liquid to which mace, bay leaf, and some parsley have been added
2. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve and rub the beans through until only the skins remain
3. Melt the butter or margarine, fry the curry, the chopped onion, and the flour in it
4. Gradually add the bean liquid and let the soup cook while stirring until done and bound (10 minutes)
5. If desired, pour it through a sieve before serving
