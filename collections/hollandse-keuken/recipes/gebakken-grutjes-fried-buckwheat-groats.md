## Ingredients

- leftover buttermilk with buckwheat groats
- lard, frying fat or salad oil

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover buttermilk with buckwheat groats into not-too-thick slices and fry them in lard, frying fat or salad oil in the frying pan on both sides until light brown.
2. Ensure the fat is not too hot, as the groats will stick and burn.
3. Serve with molasses or brown sugar.
