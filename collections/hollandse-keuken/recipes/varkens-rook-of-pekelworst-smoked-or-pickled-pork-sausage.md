## Ingredients

- pork meat
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- For every 5 pounds of meat: 1 ounce salt, half ounce pepper and cloves

## Directions

1. Mince the meat not too finely with salt, pepper, and cloves
2. Stuff into medium casings
3. Use the specified proportions of seasonings per 5 pounds of meat
