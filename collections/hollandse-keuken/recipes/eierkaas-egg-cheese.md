## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 dl milk
- salt
- Maggi's Aroma to taste
- 25 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with some salt, pour the milk in (for 1 small egg ¾ dl, for 1 large egg 1 dl) and pour the mixture into cups greased with butter or margarine.
2. Place these in a pan with water that is kept just below boiling, but must not boil, as the egg cheese would then become crumbly.
3. Let them stand in it until the egg has set, turn them out and cut them into cubes for garnish and filling of soup.
4. For pressure cooker users: Beat 3 eggs with ½ l liquid, salt and spices. Fill buttered molds with this. Place them on the rack in the pressure cooker and keep the egg custard under pressure for 5 minutes. Then remove the pan from the heat and let it stand for 5 minutes before opening it. In this short time, a beautifully smooth egg custard will have been made.
