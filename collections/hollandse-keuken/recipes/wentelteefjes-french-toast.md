## Ingredients

- 12 small slices of old bread without crust
- 2 eggs
- 4 dl milk
- 40 g sugar
- 75 g butter or margarine
- some fine cinnamon, grated lemon zest or scraped vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar and the flavoring.
2. Add the warm milk and soak the slices of bread, stripped of the crust and cut evenly, in this sauce.
3. Ensure the slices, although completely soaked through, do not break.
4. Let them drain on a plate for a moment and fry the wentelteefjes in the butter or margarine that has turned light brown on both sides until brown.
5. Sprinkle with some crushed sugar or powdered sugar.
