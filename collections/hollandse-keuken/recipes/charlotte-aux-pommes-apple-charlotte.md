## Ingredients

- approximately 200 g old bread without crust
- 100 g butter or margarine
- approximately 800 g sour apples
- approximately 50 g sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons apricot jam
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Cut the bread, of which the crusts have been removed, into equal thin slices, coat them with some butter or margarine, dip the sides in the egg white and line a mold coated with butter or margarine along the bottom and side walls with them, but make sure they fit snugly together.
2. Cook applesauce in the usual way, but make sure it is as dry as possible.
3. Sieve it and mix it with the jam, sugar and egg yolks.
4. Fill the mold with this mixture, close the top with a matching piece of bread, coated with butter or margarine on both sides.
5. Put the pudding in the oven and let it turn occasionally until brown on all sides (1 to 1 1/2 hours).
