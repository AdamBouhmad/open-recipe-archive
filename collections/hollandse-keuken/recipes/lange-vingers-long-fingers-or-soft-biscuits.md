## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 100 g flour
- 20 g Duryea cornstarch
- 150 g sugar
- grated zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Pipe from the batter, which has been prepared in the known way (see recipe 869), onto a greased baking sheet covered with a piece of paper, using a piping bag with a smooth nozzle, elongated cookies.
2. Bake the cookies in a hot oven until done, approximately 15 minutes, but ensure that they remain light in color.
3. If necessary, moisten the paper on the underside to easily remove the cookies from the paper.
