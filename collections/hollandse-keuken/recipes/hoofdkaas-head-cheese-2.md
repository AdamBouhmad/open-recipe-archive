## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head or calf's slice
- 1 pork head
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg
- vinegar
- water
- fat

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's head or slice and pork head until the bones fall out
2. Remove skins, membranes, and bones
3. Mince finely with salt, pepper, cloves, and nutmeg
4. Rinse molds or small dishes with cold water
5. Place the prepared meat in the molds and let cool
6. Place in an earthenware pot with vinegar and half water
7. Pour a layer of fat over the top
