## Ingredients

- 85 g butter or margarine
- 85 g flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 dl water
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Bring the water with the butter or margarine and a little salt to the boil.
2. Stirring, add all the sifted flour at once and keep stirring until the dough comes away from the pan as a ball.
3. Remove the pan immediately and stir in the whole eggs one by one.
4. Fill sand-tart molds greased with butter three-quarters full with this dough and bake the puffs in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked (20 to 30 minutes).
5. After cooling and filling, sprinkle them with powdered sugar.
