## Ingredients

- flounder
- water
- salt
- parsley root
- parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Set the fish in water for an hour
2. Cook with water and sufficient salt, but be careful of the foam
3. As water flounder, serve with parsley root and parsley leaves in bunches and dress with some fish water
