## Ingredients

- finely sliced bacon
- salt
- pepper
- haunch
- water
- brown ragout

## Directions

1. Roll finely sliced bacon in salt and pepper
2. Lard the haunch with it
3. Place it in a deep dish with some water on top
4. Apply good heat under and above
5. Roast it until done
6. Serve with brown ragout
7. Tail pieces of deer or roe are prepared in the same way
