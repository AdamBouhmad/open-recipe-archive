## Ingredients

- One pound flour or one cup
- Two eggs, egg white whipped separately to foam
- Half pound melted butter
- Half pound currants
- Half pound sugar

## Directions

1. Mix two eggs with the flour before putting it in the tart pan, along with half pound melted butter, half pound currants, and half pound sugar
2. Knead everything well together
3. Give little heat from below, much heat from above
4. Bake for just over half an hour
5. Grease the pan well with butter and sprinkle with biscuit
