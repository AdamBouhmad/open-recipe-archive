## Ingredients

- one pike
- two biscuits soaked in milk
- two eggs with white
- four anchovies
- nutmeg
- salt
- butter
- one lemon without skin
- water
- two anchovies
- oysters
- crushed biscuit

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the pike and boil briefly
2. Remove the bone and mince finely
3. Mix with two biscuits soaked in milk, two eggs with white, four anchovies, nutmeg, salt, butter and one lemon without skin, all ground together
4. Shape to the form of a pike in a dish
5. Add enough water so it is well submerged
6. Stir in two anchovies
7. Let braise for one hour
8. Sprinkle crushed biscuit over the fish and add some oysters at the end
