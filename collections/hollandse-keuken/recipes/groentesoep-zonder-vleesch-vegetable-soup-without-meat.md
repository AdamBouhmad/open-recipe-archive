## Ingredients

- fresh sorrel
- two heads of lettuce
- chopped young chervil
- water
- salt
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Take a handful of fresh sorrel, two heads of lettuce, some chopped young chervil
2. Set this to cook with a little water and salt
3. Cook this for half an hour and beat three eggs into it when it is ready
