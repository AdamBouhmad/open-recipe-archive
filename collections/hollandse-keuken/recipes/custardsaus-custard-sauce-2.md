## Ingredients

- 1/2 l milk
- 15 g custard powder
- 40 g sugar

## Directions

1. Bring almost all of the milk to a boil.
2. Stir the custard with the sugar together and mix them with a little of the cold milk until a thin paste without lumps.
3. Pour this, while stirring, into the boiling liquid.
4. Let the sauce, stirring, boil for two minutes on a gentle fire.
5. Let it, stirring occasionally, cool down to prevent a skin from forming.
