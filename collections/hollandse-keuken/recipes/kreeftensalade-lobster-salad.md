## Ingredients

- Cooked lobster (usually from tins)
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Pepper
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Tarragon (finely chopped, sometimes)
- Parsley (finely chopped)

## Directions

1. Take cooked lobster, usually from tins, cut it into pieces
2. Arrange decoratively with salad
3. Make a sauce from hard-boiled eggs, pepper, salt, oil, vinegar, sometimes finely chopped tarragon and parsley
4. Dress the salad with this sauce
