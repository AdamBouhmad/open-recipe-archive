## Ingredients

- 500 g cold cooked potatoes
- 1 dl milk
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- salt
- nutmeg
- finely chopped parsley
- egg white
- breadcrumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Make stiff mashed potatoes in the usual way, stir through the egg yolk and some finely chopped parsley and let it cool.
2. Form small balls with two spoons, roll these through fine breadcrumbs, shape them evenly with your hands by hand, but make sure there are no cracks in them.
3. Roll them through the remaining egg white, which has been loosened with 2 tablespoons of water, but make sure this touches the croquettes everywhere.
4. Roll them once more through breadcrumbs and fry 4-5 croquettes at a time in hot frying fat, brown and crispy.
5. Make sure the croquettes are completely under the fat.
6. Remove them with a skimming spoon or croquette basket and let them drain on brown paper.
7. Serve with beefsteak, tenderloin, etc.
8. Sieve the fat after use.
