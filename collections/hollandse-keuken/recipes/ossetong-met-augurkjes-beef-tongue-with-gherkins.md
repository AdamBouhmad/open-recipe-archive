## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Cloves
- Bouillon
- Flour
- Water
- Butter
- Gherkins

## Directions

1. Take a beef tongue, cook it for half an hour and skin it, then let it cool
2. Lard the tongue and place it in a pan with some crushed cloves and some bouillon
3. Let it simmer gently for 4 to 5 hours
4. Lay the tongue on a dish and split it open from the underside so that it lies on the dish like a heart
5. Make a sauce from bouillon, flour, crushed cloves, water and butter
6. When nearly done, add finely cut gherkins and pour this sauce over the tongue when serving
7. You can also leave the tongue whole according to preference
