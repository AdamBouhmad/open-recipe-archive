## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 dl cream
- 3 1/2 dl milk
- 200 g sugar
- 17 1/2 g white gelatin or 15 g powdered gelatin
- 1/2 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- some strong coffee extract
- salt

## Directions

1. Slowly bring the cream with the milk and vanilla to a boil
2. Put 175 g sugar in a large pan, let it stand on a not too fierce fire until it has become caramel and begins to foam over the entire surface
3. Then pour the boiling liquid slowly but all at once from the fire into it
4. Add 25 g sugar
5. If necessary, let the undissolved caramel dissolve briefly on the fire
6. Dissolve the gelatin, which has been soaked 10-15 minutes in cold water beforehand, in the warm mass
7. Remove the vanilla, add coffee extract and salt
8. Stir from time to time until the pudding begins to become jelly-like
9. Pour the mass into a mold rinsed with cold water and let the pudding become cold and stiff
10. Turn it out onto a damp dish and serve with vanilla sauce
