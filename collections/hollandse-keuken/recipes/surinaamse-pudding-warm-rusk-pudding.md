## Ingredients

- 4 large rusks
- 50 g flour
- 50 g raisins
- 50 g currants
- 2 large eggs
- 50 g sugar
- 35 g almonds
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- grated zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 dl milk

## Directions

1. Pound the rusks finely, wash the currants and the raisins, which have been removed from their stems, chop the peeled almonds.
2. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the egg yolks and sugar and stir until completely finely ground.
3. Add the salt, the sifted flour, the baking powder, the currants and raisins, the almonds, the grated lemon zest, the rusks and the milk and mix this mixture as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Fill a warm pudding mold, coated with butter or margarine and sprinkled with breadcrumbs, 3/4 full and cook the pudding 2 hours in a bain-marie, but make sure the water is continuously topped up to just below the rim.
5. Let the pudding dry slightly and serve it with foam sauce.
