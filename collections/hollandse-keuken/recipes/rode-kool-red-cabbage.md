## Ingredients

- 1 medium red cabbage
- 3 dl boiling water
- 5 g salt
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 0.5 dl vinegar or 2 sour apples
- 30 g sugar
- 6 cloves

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the cabbage, cut it in half, remove the stem and cut it with a cabbage knife or ordinary knife into long thin strips.
2. Place it in the boiling water, salt, cloves, vinegar or snipped sour apples.
3. Let the cabbage cook slowly on a gentle heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Once the cabbage has cooked dry, add the butter, margarine or beef fat and the sugar and stew the cabbage with these for 10 minutes more.
