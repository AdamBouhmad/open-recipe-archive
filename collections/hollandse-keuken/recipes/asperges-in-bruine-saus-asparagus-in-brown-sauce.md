## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg asparagus
- 2.5 dl lean jus with water
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the asparagus until done as described in recipe 463
2. Prepare the brown sauce in the ordinary manner and add some sugar and nutmeg
3. Gently stir the cooked asparagus through it
4. Serve it with beef tenderloin
