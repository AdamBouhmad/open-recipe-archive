## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 200 g cold meat
- finely chopped sour onions
- finely chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 60 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Chop the meat, onions and washed parsley finely
2. Mix this mixture with 2 small beaten eggs, salt and nutmeg to taste
3. Melt the butter or margarine in a frying pan, form 4 rings from the meat mixture off the heat
4. Break one egg into the opening of each ring (like a fried egg), but ensure the white does not run over the meat mixture
5. Bake the bird's nests on a gentle heat, until white and yolk are set, or (which is easier) place the pan in the oven until the white is set
6. Slide the bird's nests carefully onto a warmed dish and serve with pepper and salt alongside bread or instead of meat at the midday meal
