## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 onions
- 1 ounce butter
- parsley, finely chopped
- bouillon
- good mustard

## Directions

1. Cut 6 or 8 onions into cubes and brown them with 1 ounce butter over a strong fire
2. Add finely chopped parsley and bouillon
3. Cook it slowly together
4. After removing from heat, stir in a spoonful of good mustard
