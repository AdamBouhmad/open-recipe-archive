## Ingredients

- 8 sour apples
- 100 g sugar
- 2 teaspoons fine cinnamon
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1/2 dl water
- 50 g raisins
- some ground coconut

## Directions

1. Core the apples, peel them and place them with the hole facing up in a heat-proof dish
2. Mix the sugar with the fine cinnamon and fill the apples with this mixture and the well-washed raisins
3. Place a knob of butter or margarine on each apple
4. Pour the water into the dish and let the apples become tender in a moderately warm oven (approximately 20 to 30 minutes), but ensure they do not fall apart
5. Baste them from time to time with the liquid
6. Sprinkle with some ground coconut towards the end
