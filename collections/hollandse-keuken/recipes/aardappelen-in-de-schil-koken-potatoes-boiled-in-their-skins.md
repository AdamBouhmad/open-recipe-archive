## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Brush the potatoes clean thoroughly, remove the eyes, set them to cook with enough boiling water so that they are submerged one-third of the way.
2. Let them cook until they can be easily pierced with a fork, though not too easily.
3. Drain them as dry as possible if necessary, let them dry on the fire without a lid, shaking occasionally.
4. Serve them with unmelted butter or margarine and some fine salt, or peel them when cooked and shake them with some butter or margarine and fine salt.
