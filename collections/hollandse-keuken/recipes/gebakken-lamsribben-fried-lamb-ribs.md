## Ingredients

- lamb ribs
- bacon
- onions
- spices

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the ribs
2. Lard them with fine pieces of bacon
3. Braise them well in a pan until done
4. You can also add a couple of onions with some spices, which makes them tasty
5. They are eaten with their own jus
