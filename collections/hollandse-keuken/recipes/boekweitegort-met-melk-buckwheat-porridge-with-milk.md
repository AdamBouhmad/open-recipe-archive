## Ingredients

- 1.5 l milk
- 200 g buckwheat groats
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil.
2. Sprinkle in the buckwheat groats while stirring and let the porridge cook while stirring until done (15 to 20 minutes).
3. For stiff buckwheat porridge, use 250 g per 1.5 l.
