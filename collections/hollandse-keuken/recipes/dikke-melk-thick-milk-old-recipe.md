## Ingredients

- 3 dl thick milk
- some cream

## Directions

1. Place the thickened milk in a pillowcase, hang the pillowcase up and place a bowl underneath to catch the watery liquid
2. Remove the mass from the pillowcase as soon as all the water has run out
3. Mix it to taste with some sweet cream and serve it with sugar and cinnamon
