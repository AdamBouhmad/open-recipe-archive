## Ingredients

- 2 large or 4 small cooking soles
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the soles well, wash them and cook them in the boiling water with the salt, until a fin comes loose easily (+. 8 minutes).
2. Remove them with a fish slice or a couple of skimming spoons from the water and serve them with parsley sauce, butter sauce, shrimp, oyster, mussel, mousseline or béarnaise sauce.
3. Note: For cleaning sole see recipe 214. Nose sole and shard sole are cooked in the same way.
