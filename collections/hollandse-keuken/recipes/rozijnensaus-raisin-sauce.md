## Ingredients

- 1/2 l beef tongue bouillon
- 40 to 50 g flour
- 40 to 50 g butter or margarine
- 1 dl white wine
- 20 g sugar
- 50 g raisins
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Taste the bouillon to check if it is not too salty; if necessary add water until a good taste is obtained.
2. Prepare the sauce in the usual manner (see cauliflower sauce).
3. Wash the raisins after removing the stems with lukewarm water.
4. Soak them briefly in boiling water, let them drain in a sieve and stir them with the wine and sugar through the sauce.
5. Add lemon juice to taste, let the sauce heat through while stirring, but do not boil anymore.
6. Serve with cooked beef tongue.
