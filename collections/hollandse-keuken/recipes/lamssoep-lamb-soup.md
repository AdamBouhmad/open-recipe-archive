## Ingredients

- lamb shank
- salt
- 5 to 6 ounces rice
- shallots
- celery roots
- butter
- lime or lemon juice
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a lamb shank thoroughly for more than two hours, skimming and removing the fat
2. Add 5 to 6 ounces rice to the broth according to quantity, add salt, some shallots and some celery roots, along with a lump of butter
3. Let everything cook for one hour
4. When serving, remove the shank and eat it with egg sauce; add some lime or lemon juice to the soup
