## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon of beef or veal or 1 liter water and 4 bouillon cubes (recipe 35)
- 35 g vermicelli
- 50 g veal ground meat
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Prepare the bouillon in the usual manner, and if desired let a piece of mace cook in it. Strain the bouillon and let the washed and broken vermicelli cook gently in it until tender (10 to 20 minutes). Or: cook the vermicelli in water until done, then add the cubes.
2. Let the meatballs prepared in the usual manner (recipe 37) cook in the soup for the last ten minutes.
3. If desired, beat the egg in the soup tureen and carefully add the soup to it while stirring vigorously.
