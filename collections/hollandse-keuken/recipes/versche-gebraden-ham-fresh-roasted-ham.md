## Ingredients

- a fresh ham
- fine salt
- shallots
- pepper
- red wine
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. From a fresh ham, cut off the rind and rub it well with fine salt
2. Roll shallots in salt and pepper, lard it with them and let it stand for a few days
3. Roast it in a pan with fire underneath and above, adding a glass of red wine, half a glass of vinegar and half a glass of water
4. It should be particularly nicely browned
