## Ingredients

- 1 dl water with salt
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 60 g flour
- 2 eggs
- 50 g butter or margarine (for filling)
- 150 g grated cheese
- 1 egg yolk
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring the water with butter or margarine to a boil.
2. Stirring constantly, sprinkle in the sifted flour and continue stirring until the dough forms a ball that releases from the pan.
3. Remove from heat and add the eggs one by one.
4. Using a pastry bag, a butter syringe, or a teaspoon, place small mounds of this dough on a buttered baking sheet.
5. Bake the puffs in 10 to 15 minutes until light brown and cooked through in a hot oven.
6. For the filling: beat the butter or margarine until creamy, mix it with the egg yolk and stir in the grated cheese with some pepper and salt.
7. Cut or open the puffs and fill them with the cheese mixture.
