## Ingredients

- 600 g sauerkraut
- approximately 3/4 kg potato purée
- 400 g lean ham
- 1/2 l creamy cheese sauce
- 1 bouillon cube
- some grated cheese extra

## Directions

1. Cook the sauerkraut in 1/4 l bouillon made from the cube.
2. Let it drain dry.
3. Wrap a portion of sauerkraut in each slice of ham.
4. Spread a thin layer of potato purée in an ovenproof dish.
5. Arrange the ham and sauerkraut parcels on top.
6. Pipe or lay the rest of the purée between them.
7. Pour the cheese sauce over the sauerkraut and purée and let the dish heat through in the oven and lightly brown on top.
