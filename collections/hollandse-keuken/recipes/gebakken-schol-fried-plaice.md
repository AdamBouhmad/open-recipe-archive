## Ingredients

- 4 large plaice for frying
- butter oil or salad oil
- flour or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, wash it well inside and outside
2. Cut it in, rub it on both sides with plenty of salt and let it lie on a colander for half an hour so the salt can penetrate
3. Heat enough oil in the frying pan so the bottom is well covered
4. Ensure blue steam rises from the fat
5. After drying, coat the fish on both sides in flour or milk
6. Fry the light side of the fish on a brisk fire until light brown, then the dark side
7. Shift the fish back and forth in the pan while frying
8. When the fish is done, let it drain well on porous paper on both sides and serve as indicated in the rules for frying fish
