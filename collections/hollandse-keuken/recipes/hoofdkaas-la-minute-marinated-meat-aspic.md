## Ingredients

- 400 g meat scraps
- 2.5 dl meat jus with water or bouillon or 2.5 dl water with one Maggi's bouillon cube
- 1 dl slightly sour vinegar
- 8 g white gelatin
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- pickled onions and gherkins
- capers
- parsley
- 2 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Chop or grind the meat finely.
2. Soak the gelatin 10 minutes in cold water, squeeze it well, and then dissolve it in the warmed jus with water (or bouillon).
3. Stir through the meat, vinegar, salt, pepper and nutmeg.
4. Rinse a stone pudding mold with cold water, garnish it along the bottom and sides with hard-boiled egg cut into quarters, capers, pieces of gherkin and fresh sprigs of parsley.
5. Gently press the meat mixture into it and let it cool and set.
6. Turn out the hoofdkaas onto a meat platter and garnish the platter with onions, gherkins, parsley and the remaining egg.
7. Note: One can also stir some finely chopped onions and gherkins through the mixture.
