## Ingredients

- pickled mushrooms
- warm water
- bouillon
- cooked butter
- nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- flour
- shallots
- milk
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pickled mushrooms must first soak in warm water for a couple of hours
2. Then stew in bouillon and cooked butter
3. Sprinkle nutmeg over the mushrooms
4. When nearly cooked, add finely chopped parsley
5. Alternatively, cut into pieces and stew with bouillon, butter, pepper, chopped parsley, a little flour and some shallots
6. Make a sauce of milk with beaten egg yolks, nutmeg, flour and butter, stirred on the fire until proper thickness
7. Pour the sauce over when serving
8. Add lemon juice
