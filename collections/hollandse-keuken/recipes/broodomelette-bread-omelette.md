## Ingredients

- 200 g old bread without crust
- 50 to 60 g sugar
- 80 g butter or margarine
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 2 dl milk
- 1/4 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon peel
- apricot jam or orange marmalade

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in the boiling milk and rub it completely fine.
2. Stir in the flavoring, the egg yolks and the sugar, and last of all fold in the stiffly beaten egg white as lightly as possible.
3. Make half of the butter or margarine light brown in the frying pan, add half of the batter and fry this light brown on both sides.
4. Place the cake on a warmed plate, spread it with some apricot jam or orange marmalade.
5. Fry the other half of the batter in the same way and place this on the first cake.
6. Sprinkle the omelette with powdered sugar and serve it warm.
