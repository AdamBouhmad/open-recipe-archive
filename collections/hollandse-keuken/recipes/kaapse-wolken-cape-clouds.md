## Ingredients

- 3/4 l milk
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 60 g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 25 g Duryea maizena
- some powdered sugar
- red sugar

## Directions

1. Bring the milk slowly to the boil with the vanilla. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, stir in some powdered sugar, form little balls with 2 spoons and cook these briefly in the boiling milk. Remove them with a skimming spoon from the milk. Add the beaten yolks to the maizena, stir the lumps out of this mass and combine it carefully, stirring well, with as much boiling milk until the egg yolks are well warmed. Then pour everything back into the pan, add the sugar and let the custard, stirring with a whisk, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes. Remove the vanilla from it, stir the custard from time to time to prevent a skin from forming. Put the custard in a flat custard dish, lay the egg white balls on it and sprinkle these with some red sugar.
