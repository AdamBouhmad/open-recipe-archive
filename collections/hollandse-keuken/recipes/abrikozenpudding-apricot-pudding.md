## Ingredients

- 200 g dried apricots
- 1/2 liter water
- 150-175 g sugar
- peel of 1/2 lemon or orange
- 25 g gelatin or 20 g powdered gelatin

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots with lukewarm water, soak them for 24 hours in the water with the peel
2. Bring them to a boil and let them cook gently (5-10 minutes)
3. Dissolve the sugar and gelatin (which has been soaked 10-15 minutes in cold water and may include 1 sheet of red gelatin) in the mixture
4. Rub the mass through a horsehair sieve, let it become jelly-like while stirring occasionally and pour it into a stone mold rinsed with cold water
5. Turn the pudding out onto a damp dish after cooling and serve with vanilla sauce
