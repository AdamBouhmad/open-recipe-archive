## Ingredients

- 100 g powdered sugar
- approximately one egg white
- a few drops of maraschino, kirsch or vanilla extract
- 1/2 drop carmine if desired

## Directions

1. Sift the powdered sugar, add small amounts of egg white until a glossy mass is obtained that is so stiff that it does not run off the spoon or spatula with which you stir but stands firm.
2. Add the flavoring and if desired the food coloring.
3. Use egg white glaze for fruit cakes. Brush the shell of puff pastry, pie dough or sand-tart dough first with egg white glaze, let it dry and then add the moist filling. This prevents the baked good from becoming moist and soft.
4. Use this egg white glaze to make decorations on water glaze or cooking glaze. Egg white glaze hardens quickly and thus holds its shape better.
