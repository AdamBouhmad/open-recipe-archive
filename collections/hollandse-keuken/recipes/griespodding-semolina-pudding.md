## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces semolina
- nearly 1 can sweet milk
- 1 ounce melted butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons bread sugar
- almond essence
- 1 grated lemon zest
- cinnamon
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook 2.5 ounces semolina with nearly 1 can sweet milk to a pap
2. stir in 1 ounce melted butter, 6 egg yolks, 4 tablespoons bread sugar, almond essence, 1 grated lemon zest and cinnamon
3. add the pap and the whites of 6 eggs beaten to snow
4. pour into the mold
5. let it cook until done
