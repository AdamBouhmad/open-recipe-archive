## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 dl blackberry juice
- 1 1/2 dl water
- approximately 60 g sugar
- 20 g sago or potato starch
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as raspberry juice sauce
2. Use blackberry juice instead of lemon juice if desired
