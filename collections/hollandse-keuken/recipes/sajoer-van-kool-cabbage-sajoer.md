## Ingredients

- 200 g finely cut Savoy cabbage
- 250 g onion (5 small)
- 50 g salad oil
- salt
- piece of chili pepper
- ¾ liter milk

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into the finest possible strips
2. Finely chop the cleaned and washed onions
3. Put the salad oil in a frying pan and brown the onions lightly in it
4. Add a portion of the cabbage and fry it, stirring constantly, until light brown
5. Continue until all the cabbage is used
6. Then add a piece of chili pepper and some milk
7. Let the sajoer stew on a low heat until the milk evaporates
8. Add milk again and let the cabbage stew until it is cooked through, but make sure it remains very moist
9. Serve with rice and ground meat
