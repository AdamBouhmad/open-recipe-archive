## Ingredients

- 500 g sugar
- 1½ dl water
- essence or 20 g cacao or 1 tablespoon Nescafe

## Directions

1. Pour the water onto the sugar, dissolve it completely and let this solution boil until a drop falling from the spoon pulls a thin thread.
2. Remove from heat and stir until it becomes opaque.
3. Add the essence, let the breast candy become thick and fluid and finish it as indicated in the previous recipe.
