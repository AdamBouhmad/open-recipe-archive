## Ingredients

- 4 red or green peppers
- 150 g veal mince
- 150 g beef mince
- 1 onion
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 3 dl tomato sauce or 3 dl paprika liquid
- 1/2 Maggi's Justa tablet
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- 20 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the peppers. Cut the peppers lengthwise down the middle or cut off a cap. Remove the seeds and seed membranes (these are sharp in taste). Wash the peppers.
2. Cook them for approximately 10 minutes in boiling salted water.
3. Mix the mince with the chopped onion and the seasonings. Cook it through but not brown with some butter or margarine in a frying pan. Fill the peppers with this mixture.
4. Place them in an ovenproof dish. Pour the liquid over them; either the tomato sauce (see recipe 340) or the Maggi's Justa tablet prepared with paprika liquid, first finely grated, mixed and brought to a boil, by which the sauce is thickened.
5. Sprinkle some breadcrumbs over it.
6. Place small knobs of butter or margarine here and there and let the dish in a moderately warm oven get a brown crust.
7. Serve with rice or macaroni.
