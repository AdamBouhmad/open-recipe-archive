## Ingredients

- 1 kg cut beans
- 1½ kg potatoes
- 250 g white beans
- 400 g smoked sausage
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the cut beans as indicated for salted vegetables and let them drain in a colander.
2. Cook the soaked white beans in the usual way (recipe 493).
3. Peel the potatoes, cut them into quarters, wash them and place them in the bottom of the pan.
4. Add enough boiling water or bean broth so they are about half submerged.
5. Add the cut beans, white beans and washed smoked sausage, and let everything cook together on low heat until done and almost dry (30 to 35 minutes).
6. Add more water if needed, but ensure the stamppot does not become too moist.
7. Remove the sausage and quickly mash everything together with a wooden spoon or masher.
