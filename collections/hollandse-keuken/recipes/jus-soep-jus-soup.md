## Ingredients

- veal or beef jus
- bread crumbs
- old white bread
- eggs
- nutmeg
- lime juice
- egg yolks
- toasted white bread slices

## Directions

1. Take jus, whether from veal or beef, remove the fat and let the jus melt in water
2. Add bread meatballs with some lime juice and let it cook well
3. When serving, beat in some egg yolks according to quantity
4. Make the bread meatballs from crumbled stale white bread, some jus, eggs and a little nutmeg
5. If not using bread meatballs, pour the broth over toasted slices of white bread when serving
