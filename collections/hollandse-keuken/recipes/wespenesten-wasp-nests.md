## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- sugar
- cinnamon
- currants
- raisins
- sukade (candied fruit)
- grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Roll sugar, cinnamon, currants, raisins, sukade and grated lemon peel in puff pastry
2. Cut the dough into pieces the length of half a finger
3. Stand them upright in a tart pan
4. Bake until done
