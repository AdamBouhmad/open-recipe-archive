## Ingredients

- 7 loot potato starch
- some of the wine to be added later
- 8 egg yolks
- stick cinnamon
- 2 ounces sugar
- skins and juice of a lemon
- wine

## Directions

1. Stir the potato starch with a bit of the wine that will be added later
2. Add 8 egg yolks, stick cinnamon, 2 ounces sugar, the lemon skins and juice and the wine
3. Stir over moderate fire until it begins to boil
4. Carefully add the stiffly beaten whites of the 8 eggs
5. Place on a cool spot to cool, stirring continuously
6. Remove the lemon zest and stick cinnamon
7. Pour it over
8. Serve decorated with biscuits
