## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg pork steak
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 7 1/2 g salt
- some milk

## Directions

1. For preparation see veal steak (recipe 254).
2. Allow 15 to 20 minutes per 500 g for cooking time.
3. Finish the gravy as indicated for beef steak.
