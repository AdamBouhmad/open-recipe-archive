## Ingredients

- potatoes, not too well cooked
- 2 eggs
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter

## Directions

1. Rub cooked potatoes fine.
2. Mix with 2 eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
3. Stir well together and fry in butter over moderate heat in a cake pan.
4. Be careful when turning.
