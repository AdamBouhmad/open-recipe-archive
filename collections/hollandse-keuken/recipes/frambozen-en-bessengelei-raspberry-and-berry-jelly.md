## Ingredients

- 1½ kg raspberries
- 1½ kg red and white berries
- 3/4 of the weight of the fruit juice in sugar
- Jam jars
- Parchment paper
- Cellophane
- Elastic bands
- String
- Labels

## Directions

1. Treat the jelly as indicated for berry jelly, second recipe.
