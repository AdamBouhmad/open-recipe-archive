## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter red wine (Bordeaux)
- 1/2 liter water
- approximately 100 g sugar
- 35 g Siamese tapioca or tapioca
- peel of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Bring the water with the lemon peel to the boil, add the Siamese tapioca or tapioca while stirring and let it cook over a gentle heat until the grains become completely transparent (1/2 to 3/4 hour).
2. Then remove the lemon peel, add the wine and sugar and serve the soup as soon as it has become hot again.
