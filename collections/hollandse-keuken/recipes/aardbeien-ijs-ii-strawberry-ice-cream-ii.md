## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg strawberries
- 1/4 liter water
- 1/2 kg sugar
- juice of 1 to 2 lemons
- a few drops of red coloring or 2 sheets of red gelatin

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the water.
2. Rub the cleaned strawberries through a hair sieve and add them with the lemon juice to the sugar syrup.
3. Add some red coloring and count on the ice becoming lighter in color by freezing.
4. Treat it further as other ice cream and serve if desired garnished with whipped cream.
