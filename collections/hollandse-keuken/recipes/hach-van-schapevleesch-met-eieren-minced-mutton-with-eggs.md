## Ingredients

- meat from a roasted leg of mutton
- parsley
- a little vinegar
- shallots
- bouillon
- fried bread
- four or five oeufs de boeuf

## Directions

1. Take the meat from a roasted leg of mutton, chop it very fine and add parsley, a little vinegar and shallots
2. Let it cook in bouillon in a pan
3. When ready, place it on a dish, arrange some pieces of fried bread around it as a border, and decorate it with four or five oeufs de boeuf
