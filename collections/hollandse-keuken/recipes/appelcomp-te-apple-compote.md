## Ingredients

- 8 sour apples
- 8 candied cherries or some marmalade
- 50 g sugar
- peel of ½ lemon
- 1 leaf of white or red gelatin
- approximately 2½ dl water with some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Core the apples, peel them, halve them, wash them and cook them gently in the liquid that has been brought to a boil with the lemon juice in a flat, wide pan (for example a braising pan).
2. Just before they are done, add the sugar and gently moisten the apples with the liquid.
3. Lay each apple that is done with a flat spoon in a compote dish.
4. Dissolve the soaked gelatin in the liquid and pour it through a horsehair sieve along or over the apples.
5. Lay a candied cherry or some marmalade on the holes.
