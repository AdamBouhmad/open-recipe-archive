## Ingredients

- One pound butter
- One pound flour
- 1 whole egg and 2 yolks
- 1 tablespoon milk or cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Put butter in flour
2. Add whole egg and two yolks with milk or cream
3. Make a not too firm dough
4. Let stand 3 to 4 hours in the cellar
5. Roll out
6. Cut into small angular or round cookies
7. Sprinkle with sugar
8. Bake light brown
