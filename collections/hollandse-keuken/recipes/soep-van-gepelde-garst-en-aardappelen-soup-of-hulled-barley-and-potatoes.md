## Ingredients

- medium hulled barley
- water
- piece of beef fat
- bone bouillon
- celery
- leek
- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Let medium hulled barley cook briefly with little water and a piece of beef fat, and gradually pour in bone bouillon. After one hour of cooking, add celery and leek, let everything cook well through (for 3 hours) and stir it now and then.
2. In the last hour, cook an equivalent quantity of potatoes in water and salt until well done and add them to the soup with a small amount of Liebig's Meat Extract and some pepper.
