## Ingredients

- kale
- potatoes
- smoked bacon
- lard
- water
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Treat the kale as before
2. Add peeled potatoes and a piece of smoked bacon
3. Stew with a little lard, water, nutmeg and a little salt
4. Shake together continuously
