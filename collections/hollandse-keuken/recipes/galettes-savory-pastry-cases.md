## Ingredients

- 100 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- pepper and salt to taste
- white or brown ragout

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, add the butter or margarine to it and cut it as fine as breadcrumbs.
2. Stir pepper and salt and the beaten egg through this mixture.
3. Add enough water, if necessary, so that the dough does not crumble and roll it out to 1/2 cm thickness on a floured board or table.
4. Brush tartlet molds with some butter or margarine and line them generously with a portion of the dough.
5. Prick here and there in the bottom to prevent it from puffing up and bake the galettes in a moderately warm oven light brown and done (15 to 20 minutes).
6. Remove them from the molds and fill them as hot as possible with ragout.
