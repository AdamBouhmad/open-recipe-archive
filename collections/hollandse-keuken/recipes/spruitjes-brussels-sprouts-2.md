## Ingredients

- approximately 1 kg Brussels sprouts
- salt
- 35 g butter or margarine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean the Brussels sprouts by cutting off the withered leaves and a small piece of the stem.
2. Place them for some time in abundant cold water to let the sand settle, then carefully scoop them out and wash them as many times as necessary until the water runs completely clear.
3. Let them cook until tender in abundant boiling water with salt (without a lid) (20-25 minutes).
4. Let them drain in a colander as dry as possible and stew them with the butter or margarine for 5 minutes.
5. Grate some nutmeg over them in the bowl.
