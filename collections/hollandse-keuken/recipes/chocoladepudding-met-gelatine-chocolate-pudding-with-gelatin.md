## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 50 g Blooker's cocoa
- 120 g sugar
- 25 g white gelatin or powdered gelatin
- 1/2 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the cocoa and sugar with some cold milk thinly.
2. Bring the remaining milk with the vanilla slowly to a boil.
3. Pour in the mixed cocoa while stirring and let the milk cook through while stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Remove the vanilla.
5. Soak the gelatin for 15 minutes in plenty of cold water. (Powdered gelatin need not be soaked but can be dissolved immediately.)
6. Squeeze it out well and add it off the heat to the cooked mixture.
7. Stir from time to time, until the pudding begins to become jelly-like and then pour it into a mold rinsed with cold water.
8. Let the pudding become cold and firm.
9. Turn it out onto a wet plate and serve the pudding with vanilla sauce.
