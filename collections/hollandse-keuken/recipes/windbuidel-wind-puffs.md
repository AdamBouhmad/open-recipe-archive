## Ingredients

- Half a pound butter
- One pint milk
- Flour, as needed to bind
- 8 to 10 eggs
- Half an ounce sugar
- Sugar for garnish

## Directions

1. Heat butter in milk over high heat until boiling
2. Stir in flour until bound
3. Let cool
4. Add eggs and half an ounce sugar
5. On a buttered baking sheet, make small heaps the size of a hazelnut, not too close together
6. Sprinkle each heap with sugar for shine
7. Bake in a good oven
