## Ingredients

- 1 kg pike
- 150 g butter or margarine
- 120 g old bread without crust
- 3 eggs
- approximately 1 dl milk or cream
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon juice (optional)
- 75 to 100 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cook the pike in the usual way (see recipe 182) in plenty of boiling salted water. Remove the meat from the bones while still warm, but make sure no bones remain. Chop or grind it finely and mix it with the beaten eggs, into which the bread soaked in milk (or cream) has been stirred. Add pepper, salt and nutmeg to taste and the 150 g butter or margarine softened somewhat. Place the mixture in a buttered fish-shaped mold or shape it as much as possible like a pike in a fireproof dish. Pour lemon juice over it if desired, place 100 g butter or margarine in small lumps on top and let the stuffed pike brown lightly in a moderately warm oven (1 hour).
