## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon made from 500 g veal shank meat or veal pullet
- piece of mace
- piece of Spanish pepper
- some parsley
- 30 g tapioca
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dl cream

## Directions

1. Make fragrant bouillon from the meat with the water and spices in the usual way.
2. Strain it several times through a flannel or double-folded linen cloth until it becomes completely clear.
3. Cook the washed and soaked tapioca in it until tender (approximately 3/4 to 1 hour) and carefully pour the soup while stirring vigorously to the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the cream, in the soup tureen.
