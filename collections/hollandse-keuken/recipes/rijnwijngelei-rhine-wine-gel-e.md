## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter Rhine wine
- 1/4 liter water
- juice of 2 lemons
- 10 cm cinnamon stick
- 8 cloves
- lemon peel
- 35 g white gelatin
- 150 g sugar
- 2 to 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Treat the gelée as described in recipe 555 or make it in a flat dish as shown in the illustration.
2. This wine gelée can be used as is, it can also be filled with fruit (see recipe 565) or serve as a decorative top layer on other cold puddings.
