## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon of beef or 1 liter water and 4 bouillon cubes (recipe 35)
- 55 g rice
- 50 g veal ground meat
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Prepare the bouillon in the usual manner, and if desired let a piece of mace cook in it. Strain the bouillon and cook the washed rice in it until tender (approximately 1/2 hour). Or: cook the rice in water with salt and a piece of mace until done, then add the bouillon cubes.
2. Let the meatballs prepared in the usual manner (recipe 37) cook in the soup for the last 10 minutes.
3. If desired, beat the egg in the soup tureen and carefully add the soup to it while stirring vigorously.
4. Instead of rice, one can also use Siamese sago or pearl sago and allow 3/4 hour cooking time.
