## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 dl béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 346 and pour it onto a warmed small dish.
2. Place the hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters on it and garnish the dish with fresh sprigs of parsley.
3. Serve this dish as described in the previous recipe.
