## Ingredients

- 500 g veal soup meat
- 250 g beef soup meat
- 1/2 veal foot
- salt
- or one chicken
- salt
- or 400 g veal soup meat with bone
- 400 g beef soup meat
- salt
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and cut it into small pieces, or wash the chicken and cut it as finely as possible.
2. Place this with 2 tablespoons water and salt in a consommé pot or in a canning jar; ensure it is filled 3/4 full, close it, place it in a pan with water that reaches just below the rim of the pot and let it cook 6-8 hours in a bain marie.
3. Taste the resulting broth, add salt if necessary and strain it through a piece of cheesecloth into a bowl.
4. Keep the consommé as cool as possible, skim off the fat before use and serve it cold or melted through bouillon.
5. Melt the remaining fat to prevent spoilage and never keep the rest longer than 1 or 2 days.
