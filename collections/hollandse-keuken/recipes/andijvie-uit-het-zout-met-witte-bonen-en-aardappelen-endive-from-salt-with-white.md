## Ingredients

- 1 kg endive
- 1½ kg potatoes
- 250 g white beans
- 60 g fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the soaked beans in the usual way (recipe 493).
2. Cook the endive as indicated for salted vegetables.
3. Let it drain in a colander.
4. Peel the potatoes, cut them into quarters, wash them and place them in the bottom of the pan.
5. Add enough boiling water or bean broth so they are about half submerged.
6. Add the endive, beans, fat and salt, and let everything cook together on low heat until done and almost dry (30 to 35 minutes).
7. Add more water if needed, but ensure the stamppot does not become too moist.
8. Quickly mash everything together with a wooden spoon or masher.
