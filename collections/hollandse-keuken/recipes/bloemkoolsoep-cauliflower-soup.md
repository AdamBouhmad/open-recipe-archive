## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- bouillon
- egg
- water

## Directions

1. Cut cauliflower into small pieces, cook them briefly
2. Then let them cook a little longer in bouillon, but do not let them become too soft
3. Now pour them off and place them in the soup tureen
4. Stir an egg in a little cold water
