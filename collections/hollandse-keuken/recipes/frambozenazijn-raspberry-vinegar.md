## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 kg raspberries
- 2 l pickling vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take 1 kg raspberries, clean them, wash them carefully and let them stand for 24 hours, covered with the vinegar.
2. Then carefully pour the liquid, without stirring, onto 1 kg fresh raspberries, which have also been picked and washed, and let them stand again for 24 hours.
3. Repeat this procedure to get a very fragrant extract if desired once more.
4. Then measure the amount of juice and add 750 g sugar per liter of liquid.
5. Dissolve this completely in it, but make sure that the liquid does not boil.
6. Skim off the foam as carefully as possible and fill well-cleaned and boiled bottles with the raspberry vinegar.
7. Close them with boiled corks.
8. Serve the raspberry vinegar with water or soda water (2 spoons per glass).
