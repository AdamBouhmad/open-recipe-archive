## Ingredients

- 1/2 l red wine (Bordeaux)
- 1/2 l water
- 100 g sugar
- 35 g Siamsago or tapioca
- peel of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Bring the water with the lemon peel to a boil
2. Add the Siamsago or tapioca while stirring and let it simmer on low heat until the grains become completely transparent (1/2 to 3/4 hour)
3. Remove the lemon peel, add the wine and sugar
4. Serve the soup as soon as it becomes hot again
