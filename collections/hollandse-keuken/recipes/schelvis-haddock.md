## Ingredients

- 2 haddock (approximately 1 kg)
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, make a slit along the backbone, rinse it thoroughly
2. Cook it in boiling water with salt until a fin comes off easily (approximately 10 minutes)
3. Remove it with the fish slice or a pair of skimming spoons from the pan and place it on the fish plate
4. Serve with butter sauce, egg sauce, mustard sauce, glazed or melted butter
