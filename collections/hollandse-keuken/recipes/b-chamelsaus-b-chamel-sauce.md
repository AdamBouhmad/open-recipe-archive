## Ingredients

- 1/4 l milk, cream or light bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- piece of mace
- pinch of thyme
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Let the herbs steep in the liquid for 15 to 20 minutes
2. Strain and prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
3. If desired, blend with the beaten egg yolk and serve with hard-boiled eggs, poached eggs or stuffed cucumber
