## Ingredients

- One spoon flour
- Three spoons bread sugar
- Three egg yolks
- Half a bottle rum

## Directions

1. Stir one spoon flour, three spoons bread sugar, and three egg yolks well together
2. Pour in half a bottle of rum
3. Let it cook while stirring constantly
