## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- ½ pound butter
- ½ pound brown sugar
- 1 grated lemon
- cinnamon
- currants and candied citron peel (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together cold.
2. Place in small molds and set in a cake or tart pan.
3. Bake slowly with heat above and below.
