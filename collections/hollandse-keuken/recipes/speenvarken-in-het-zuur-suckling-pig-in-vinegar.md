## Ingredients

- one suckling pig, cut into 4 pieces and head split
- buttermilk
- water
- lemon slices
- bay leaves
- whole pepper
- nutmeg
- cooked vinegar that has cooled

## Directions

1. After cutting the suckling pig into 4 pieces and splitting the head, soak it in water for 2 days, changing the water each day
2. Cook it in half buttermilk and half water, skimming well
3. When cooled, place the pieces in an earthenware pot and place between each layer some lemon slices, some bay leaves, some whole pepper and some nutmeg
4. Pour over everything one bottle of cooked vinegar that has cooled, and enough buttermilk so that everything is submerged
5. When using the meat, take some bay leaves and lemon slices with it and let it drain well
