## Ingredients

- 8 bunches young or 4 bunches old turnip greens
- 35 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the small roots and hard ends of the leaves, then cut the turnip greens into small pieces
2. Wash them carefully until the water that comes off is completely clear
3. Set them on with as little water as possible and let them cook until done (1/2 to 3/4 hour)
4. Drain them in a colander
5. Stew them for 5 minutes with the butter or margarine and salt, or with a sauce made from 30 g flour and 30 g butter or margarine, 2.5 dl milk, prepared in the known manner according to recipe 328
6. Grate some nutmeg over the dish
