## Ingredients

- plums without pits, cut into cubes
- old white bread without crust, cut into cubes
- butter
- hot milk
- eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- currants

## Directions

1. Cut plums (without pits) into cubes
2. Prepare as for bread with apples, but using more bread
