## Ingredients

- 1 kg pike
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, rinse it
2. Cook it in boiling water with salt until the fin comes off (approximately 10 minutes)
3. Remove it with the fish slice or 2 skimming spoons from the liquid
4. Serve with egg sauce or melted butter
