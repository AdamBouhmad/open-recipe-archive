## Ingredients

- 300 g sugar
- 200 g almonds
- 10 g butter or margarine (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the soaked almonds, dry them, snip them.
2. Put the sugar (if desired with the butter or margarine) in an iron pot or frying pan.
3. Stir it over a not too strong fire, until it has become evenly brown, and then mix it with the almonds.
4. Pour the mixture out onto a greased sheet, press it out with a lemon or damp spoon, cut it quickly into diamonds and take them off the sheet.
