## Ingredients

- Stale white bread, rolls or French bread, without crust
- Cold milk
- Beaten eggs
- Fine breadcrumbs
- Fat or brown butter for frying
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut bread into finger-thick slices in round shapes
2. Soak well in cold milk
3. Roll in beaten eggs and fine breadcrumbs
4. Fry in fat or in a pan with plenty of brown butter on both sides until brown
5. Sprinkle with sugar
6. Serve warm with chocolate custard or similar
