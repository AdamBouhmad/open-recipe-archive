## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 4 lood butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 good spoon cream or sour cream

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs and 4 lood butter together into foam
2. Add 2 tablespoons flour, 1 good spoon cream or sour cream, and make it into a dough
3. Cut this dough into small pieces and throw them into boiling bouillon
4. Let them cook somewhat, then serve
