## Ingredients

- 3 slices stale casino bread
- 6 sardines

## Directions

1. Cut very thin slices of casino bread, remove the crusts and cut them in half
2. Toast them on a baking sheet light brown in the oven
3. Clean the sardines by removing the innards
4. Lay two half sardines on each slice of bread
