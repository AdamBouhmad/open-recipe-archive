## Ingredients

- 5 kg tomatoes
- Salt
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Peppercorns
- Small preserving bottles or other bottles that are sealed with cork and wax

## Directions

1. Clean the tomatoes, wash them, put them on with some water, the herbs and some salt and let them cook for 20 minutes.
2. Press them through a horsehair sieve.
3. Check if the resulting purée has reached the required thickness, otherwise cook it for some more time in a pan without a lid.
4. Fill the well-cleaned bottles with it.
5. Close these with ring, lid and clip or with a boiled cork and heat the bottles for half an hour to 80° C.
6. Let the preserving bottles cool with the clips on, press the cork firmly in the other bottles and dip the neck of the corked bottles twice in melted wax after cooling.
7. Label the bottles with name and date and store them in the dark.
