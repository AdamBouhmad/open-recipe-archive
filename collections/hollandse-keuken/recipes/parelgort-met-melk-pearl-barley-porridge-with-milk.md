## Ingredients

- 1.5 l milk
- 150 g pearl barley
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse the pearl barley and soak it overnight in cold water.
2. Let it drain well.
3. Bring the milk with salt to a boil.
4. Sprinkle in the soaked pearl barley while stirring, let the milk come to a boil again while stirring and let the porridge become done on a very gentle fire, stirring occasionally (approximately 2 hours).
