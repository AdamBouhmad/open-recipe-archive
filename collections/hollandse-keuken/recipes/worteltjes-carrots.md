## Ingredients

- 12 large bunches of carrots
- sugar
- salt (optional)

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots, wash them carefully and arrange them neatly in the well-cleaned bottles.
2. Add some sugar and salt if desired.
3. Pour enough water until the carrots are just covered.
4. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize them for 1½ hours.
5. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
