## Ingredients

- 200 g hard butter or margarine
- 200 g flour
- some water
- salt
- jam or preserves for filling

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846), roll it out one last time to a thickness of 3/4 cm and place it on a baking sheet. Roll out the remaining dough again, cut out half-moons and place them around the edge of the circle. Seal them with water to the base layer. Cut long, thin, narrow strips from the remaining dough and divide the tart into 6 or 8 sections with these. Seal the strips with water, brush everything with egg yolk beaten with water. Make small cuts in the bottom here and there to prevent it from rising too much and bake the tart in a hot oven until light brown and cooked (15-20 minutes). Fill the different sections after cooling with preserves or jam or place different types of fruits in them.
