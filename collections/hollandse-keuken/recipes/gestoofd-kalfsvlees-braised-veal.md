## Ingredients

- 300 g lean veal
- 4 dl water with salt
- small carrot
- parsley
- pieces of mace
- pinch of thyme
- 3 white peppercorns
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 3 dl bouillon
- 1 egg yolk (optional)
- 1/2 dl milk
- 1/2 tin of mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and cook it in salted water with herbs until tender (approximately 1/2 hour). Cut it into slanting slices and keep these warm in a little bouillon to prevent drying out.
2. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring it to a boil while stirring. Add while stirring 3 dl of the remaining strained bouillon and let the sauce cook while stirring until done (10 minutes).
3. If desired, mix it with the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the milk, and warm the meat slices in it.
4. Put everything on a platter and garnish the dish optionally with mushrooms and fleurons (see recipe 847).
