## Ingredients

- 6 thin slices casino bread
- 6 anchovies
- lemon juice
- pepper
- salad oil

## Directions

1. Clean the anchovies as described above.
2. Soak them for 10 minutes in salad oil mixed with some lemon juice and pepper.
3. In the meantime, cut 6 very thin slices of casino bread, remove the crust, cut them in half and place the anchovy between two slices.
4. Press the slices firmly together.
