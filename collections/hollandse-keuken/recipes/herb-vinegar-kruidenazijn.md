## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 bottles ordinary vinegar
- Four bunches tarragon without stems
- 4 handfuls poppies
- 4 handfuls fresh elderflowers
- 2 handfuls pimpernel
- 2 handfuls basil

## Directions

1. Add the tarragon without stems, poppies, elderflowers, pimpernel, and basil to 6 to 8 bottles of ordinary vinegar
2. Let it steep in the sun in a calabash
3. Strain through a woolen bag
4. Pour into ordinary bottles
