## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 3 to 3.5 dl beer or sparkling water
- salt
- 6 sour apples (Goudreinettes or Bellefleurs)
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Heat the frying fat in a deep iron pot.
2. Core and peel the apples, cut them into not-too-thick slices.
3. Mix the flour with some beer or sparkling water and salt quickly, stir out the lumps and add enough beer or sparkling water so the batter reaches the required thickness.
4. Coat some apple slices in the batter, place them in the hot fat and fry the apple fritters light brown and cooked through.
5. Remove them from the fat with a larding needle or knitting needle.
6. Let them drain thoroughly on gray paper, dust with powdered sugar and serve warm.
