## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- potatoes
- butter
- jus

## Directions

1. Roast a leg of lamb as indicated previously
2. When it is almost done, add potatoes and cook them in the jus until tender
3. Alternatively, the potatoes can be browned in butter and placed around the leg on the platter
