## Ingredients

- ½ kg liver
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt, pepper, flour

## Directions

1. Slice the liver into finger-thick slices, remove the blood vessels and membranes, rinse it and rub it with some pepper and salt
2. Roll it in some flour and fry it in the brown butter in approximately 4 minutes until cooked and only lightly coloured
