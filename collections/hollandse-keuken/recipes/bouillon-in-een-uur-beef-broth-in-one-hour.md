## Ingredients

- half Dutch pound beef
- veal if for a sick person
- lamb if for a sauce
- carrots
- onions
- vegetables as desired
- half beer glass water
- approximately one and a half cans boiling water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces
2. Place in a pot with carrots, onions, and vegetables of choice
3. Add half a beer glass of water
4. Let simmer slowly on gentle heat for about a quarter hour
5. Once it begins to stick to the pot, pour approximately one and a half cans of boiling water over it, more or less depending on desired strength
6. Add salt
7. Let boil on high heat for a quarter hour
8. Filter the broth through a linen cloth
