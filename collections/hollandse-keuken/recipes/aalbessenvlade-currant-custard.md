## Ingredients

- 6 beaten eggs
- bread sugar
- cinnamon
- 1 beer glass red currant juice

## Directions

1. Stir 6 beaten eggs, bread sugar and cinnamon through 1 beer glass red currant juice
2. Set on the fire and cook while constantly stirring until it becomes thick
3. Pour quickly onto a small dish on which it is to be served
