## Ingredients

- 5 ounces flour
- 1 and a half ounces currants
- 1 ounce raisins
- 2 drams snippets and candied peel
- 3 to 4 sour apples, cut in pieces
- Some peeled almonds
- 8 eggs
- Yeast
- One third lard and two thirds rapeseed oil

## Directions

1. Make a moderately thick batter with warm sweet milk, flour, currants, raisins, snippets, candied peel, apples, almonds, eggs and yeast
2. Pre-fry the lard and rapeseed oil with a slice of rye bread
3. Fry the cakes in this oil
