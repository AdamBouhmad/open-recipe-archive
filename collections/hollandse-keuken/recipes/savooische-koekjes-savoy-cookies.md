## Ingredients

- 9 eggs
- 9 egg yolks
- 5 ounces sugar
- 10 drams pounded almonds
- candied citrus peel (sukade)
- snippets
- 4 ounces sifted flour

## Directions

1. Stir for one hour 9 eggs and 9 egg yolks with 5 ounces sugar
2. Add 10 drams pounded almonds, candied citrus peel and snippets
3. Mix in 4 ounces sifted flour
4. Stir well together
5. Put in round molds greased with butter
6. Bake in the oven until done
