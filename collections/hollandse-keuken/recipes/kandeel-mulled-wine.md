## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Rhine wine
- half pound sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 12 eggs
- half lood cinnamon

## Directions

1. A bottle of Rhine wine, half a pound sugar, the juice of a lemon, 12 eggs and half lood cinnamon are stirred over moderate heat until it boils
