## Ingredients

- Veal head
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Cold butter
- Crushed rusks
- Water

## Directions

1. After the head has been soaked and blanched, sprinkle the inside with fine salt, nutmeg and pepper
2. Put some cold butter in the brains and bind it closed
3. Smear the head with butter all around, roll it in crushed rusks
4. Place it in a roasting pan with a sufficient amount of water and roast in the oven
5. Serve with the same sauce as the boiled head
