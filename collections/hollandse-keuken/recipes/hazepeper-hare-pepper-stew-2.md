## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- 1 l lean hare gravy with water or 1 l bouillon
- a piece of Spanish pepper
- 3 bay leaves
- 6 cloves
- 50 g onion
- 120 g flour
- 120 g butter or margarine
- some English sauce and soya or some red wine
- 20 g sugar
- 1/2 dl vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted hare into pieces; add enough water to the lean hare gravy so that it is not too salty.
2. Make a bound brown sauce in the usual way from butter or margarine, flour and lean gravy or bouillon.
3. Add the pieces of hare, the spices bound in a cheesecloth bag and the vinegar, and let the hare pepper stew on a low heat, stirring occasionally, for 1 1/2 hours.
4. Remove the spices and add the sugar, English sauce and soya or some red wine.
