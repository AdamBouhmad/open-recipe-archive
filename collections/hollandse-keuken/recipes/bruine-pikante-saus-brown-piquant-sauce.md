## Ingredients

- 4 dl lean jus with water
- 50 g onion
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- small carrot
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- splash of Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce

## Directions

1. Mix the jus with enough water so that it is not too salty.
2. Add the spices and let them steep gently for 20 minutes.
3. Strain the liquid and prepare sauce in the usual manner (see cauliflower sauce).
4. Remove from heat and mix with Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce to taste.
5. Serve with beef tenderloin, chateaubriands, etc.
