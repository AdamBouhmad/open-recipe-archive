## Ingredients

- 1 kg potatoes
- salt
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes as thinly as possible, carefully remove the eyes and cut them into long, thin strips or make different shapes with a frying knife.
2. Wash and dry with a clean cloth as many pieces as can be fried at once and lay them in warm frying fat.
3. Let them cook (approximately 10 minutes), remove them with a skimming spoon and then fry them brown in hot frying fat.
4. Lay them on brown paper to drain, sprinkle them with table salt and serve them with steak, veal cutlet, etc.
