## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons water
- pepper and salt to taste
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 1 calf's kidney
- 2 dl bouillon or lean jus with water
- 20 g flour
- 20 g butter or margarine
- onion (optional)
- Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the omelet as described in recipe 383 and fold it in thirds after inserting the kidney ragout, which has been prepared as 'braised calf's kidney' (see recipe 266) and to which Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce have been added off the heat.
2. Place on a warmed dish and serve with bread.
3. Serve the remaining ragout on the side or place the ragout inside the omelet as described in the previous recipe.
