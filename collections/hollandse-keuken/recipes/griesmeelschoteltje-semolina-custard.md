## Ingredients

- 1.25 l milk
- 100 g semolina
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 100 g sugar
- 1/4 vanilla stick or 1/4 lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare semolina porridge as described in the previous recipe, but mix it with half of the sugar.
2. Beat the egg yolks smooth with the other half of the sugar, vigorously add the hot semolina porridge from which the vanilla or lemon peel has been removed.
3. Beat the egg white stiff and fold it in as lightly as possible.
4. Put the mixture in an oven-safe dish and let it rise and brown in a moderately warm oven.
5. Serve immediately when it comes out of the oven.
6. If desired, a layer of macaroons can be placed in the middle of the dish, approximately 150 g for this quantity.
