## Ingredients

- 3/4 l milk
- 50 g sugar
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 30 g cornstarch (Duryea brand)
- 1 to 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar and stir the cornstarch through
2. Bring the milk with the vanilla slowly to a boil
3. Add warm milk, stirring well, to the mixed cornstarch until the eggs are warmed
4. Then pour everything back into the pan and let the custard simmer on a not too fierce fire, stirring vigorously with a whisk, for 3 to 4 minutes
5. Remove the vanilla pod
6. Stir occasionally while cooling to prevent a skin from forming
