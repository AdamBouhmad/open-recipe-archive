## Ingredients

- endive, preferably curly endive
- cooked beet in slices
- onions, finely cut
- warm sour sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the endive as finely as possible.
2. Place it in a salad bowl over slices of cooked beet and finely cut onions.
3. Toss it as ordinary head lettuce.
4. Eat it with warm sour sauce.
