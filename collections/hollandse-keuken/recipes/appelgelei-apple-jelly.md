## Ingredients

- 3 kg sour apples (preferably Goudreinettes)
- Juice and peel of 2 lemons
- 3/4 of the weight of the liquid from the apples after cooking in sugar
- Jam jars
- Parchment paper
- String
- Labels

## Directions

1. Wash the apples carefully, cut them into sixths.
2. Place them with the lemon peel, the juice and enough boiling water so they are just covered and let them cook until completely soft.
3. Pour the mass into a clean cloth and let it stand without stirring until all the liquid has completely drained out.
4. Weigh the liquid or measure it (1/2 liter = 500 g), set it with 3/4 of its weight in sugar and let it cook down, stirring occasionally, until the jelly reaches the required thickness.
5. Treat further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
