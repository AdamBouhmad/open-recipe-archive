## Ingredients

- 250 g berries
- 250 g raspberries
- 8 large rusks
- Approximately 100 g brown sugar
- 75 g butter or margarine
- ¼ l whipping cream
- 25 g powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the berries, drain them; select the raspberries, wash them and let them cook quickly with the berries and part of the sugar into a puree.
2. Place this in a fireproof dish.
3. Crush the rusks finely, mix them with the remaining sugar and sprinkle them over the fruit puree.
4. Place the butter or margarine in small pieces on top and let the dish brown in a moderately warm oven.
5. Let it cool afterwards.
6. Beat the cream stiff, mix it with the sifted powdered sugar and place it on the dish.
