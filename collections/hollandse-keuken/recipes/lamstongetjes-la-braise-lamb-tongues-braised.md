## Ingredients

- ten to twelve lamb tongues
- water
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaves
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take ten to twelve lamb tongues
2. Clean them and blanch them for a little less than half an hour
3. Change the water and let them drain
4. Dry them and cut off the root
5. Lard the tongues
6. Add some parsley, thyme and bay leaves with bouillon
7. Braise them for about five hours
