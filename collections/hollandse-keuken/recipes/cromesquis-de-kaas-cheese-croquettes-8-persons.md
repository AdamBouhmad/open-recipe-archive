## Ingredients

- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- approximately 2 dl milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 150 g Parmesan cheese
- 100 g Gruyère cheese
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- for the batter: 40 g flour
- 1 spoon salad oil
- 1 dl warm water
- 1 egg
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Prepare in the usual way a thick sauce of butter or margarine, flour and milk.
2. Add the egg yolks and the grated Parmesan cheese, cut the Gruyère cheese into small pieces and stir these in at the end with pepper and salt to taste.
3. Form small round balls or elongated rolls from this cheese mixture, roll them briefly through some breadcrumbs.
4. Mix for the batter the flour with the oil, add the warm water, the egg yolk and finally the stiffly beaten egg white.
5. Dip the cheese balls briefly through this batter and fry them immediately in frying fat or salad oil, from which blue steam rises, brown.
6. Let them drip on gray paper and place them on a dish on which a napkin is folded.
7. Garnish them with fresh sprigs of parsley.
