## Ingredients

- 2 bunches celery
- 2.5 dl milk or celery cooking water
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the white and tender celery stalks into pieces of 6-8 cm length, peel the broad pieces and scrape the narrow pieces.
2. Wash them and place them with little boiling water and salt.
3. Cook them gently until tender (3/4 to 1 hour) and let them drain in a colander, but if desired catch the liquid.
4. Prepare the sauce in the manner as for cauliflower sauce.
