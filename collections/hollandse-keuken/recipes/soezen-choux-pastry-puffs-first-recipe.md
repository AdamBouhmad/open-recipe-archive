## Ingredients

- 60 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 dl milk or water
- a little salt
- 2 large eggs
- some coarse sugar

## Directions

1. Bring the liquid with the butter or margarine and a little salt to the boil and let it come up once.
2. Stirring, add all the sifted flour at once and keep stirring until the dough comes away from the pan as a ball.
3. Remove it immediately.
4. Stir in the eggs one by one away from the heat and pipe this mass in oblong balls onto a buttered baking sheet.
5. Sprinkle them with some coarse sugar and bake the puffs in a fairly hot oven until light brown and cooked (about 15 minutes).
6. After cooling, fill them with cream custard or pastry cream (see recipe 816) or jam.
7. Note: The puffs can also be brushed with dark brown water glaze (see recipe 819).
