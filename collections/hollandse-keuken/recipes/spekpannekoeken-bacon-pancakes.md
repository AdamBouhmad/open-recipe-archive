## Ingredients

- 400 g flour
- 3/4 liter lukewarm milk (optionally with water)
- 15 g yeast
- 100 g fatty smoked bacon
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter in the manner indicated for risen pancakes (see previous recipe)
2. Rinse the bacon with lukewarm water or scrape it, cut away the rind as thinly as possible and cut the bacon into thin slices
3. Fry 3 to 4 slices of bacon in the pancake pan until the bottom of the pan is sufficiently greased with fat
4. Pour enough batter so a fairly thick pancake is formed and the pieces of bacon cannot fall out
5. Fry the pancake on both sides until light brown and cooked through
6. Serve the bacon pancakes with syrup or brown sugar
7. When much needs to be baked, it is better to first fry out all the bacon and keep it warm in a steel pan or enamel bowl, using as much bacon and bacon fat as needed for each pancake
