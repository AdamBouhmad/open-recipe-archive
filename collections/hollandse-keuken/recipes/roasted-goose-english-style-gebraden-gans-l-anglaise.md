## Ingredients

- young fat goose
- chopped onions
- goose liver
- butter
- bread crumbs
- jus or bouillon sauce

## Directions

1. Take a young fat goose
2. To see if the goose is young, try to break the point of the beak; if this is easy, you can be sure it is young
3. Clean and gut the goose
4. Fill it with chopped onions and its liver, fried in butter, and some bread crumbs made into dough
5. Sew it closed from behind and roast it on the spit
6. You can make a sauce from jus or bouillon to go with it
