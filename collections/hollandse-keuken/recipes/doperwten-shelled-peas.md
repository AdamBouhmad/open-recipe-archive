## Ingredients

- 20 kg shelled peas
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Shell the peas, check carefully that there are no damaged ones, wash them, fill well-cleaned bottles with them up to 3 cm below the rim, add salt if desired.
2. Pour on enough water so that the peas are just covered.
3. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize for 1 to 1½ hours.
4. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
5. Note: It is best to sterilize the shelled peas again for one hour the next day, as they spoil easily, or blanch them before they go in the bottles.
