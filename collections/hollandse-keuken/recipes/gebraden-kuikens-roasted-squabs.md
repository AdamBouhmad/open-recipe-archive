## Ingredients

- squabs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Cut the squabs in half and flatten them.
2. Brush them with butter and sprinkle with pepper, salt and crushed rusks.
3. Roast until done.
4. Serve with a lemon or shallot sauce.
