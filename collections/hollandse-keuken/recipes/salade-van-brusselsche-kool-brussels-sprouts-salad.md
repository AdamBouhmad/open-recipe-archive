## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- cooked potatoes
- celeriac pieces
- onions, finely cut
- salt
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the sprouts almost done and let them cool.
2. Cut cooked potatoes into slices.
3. Add some pieces of celeriac and finely cut onions.
4. Toss with salt, oil, and vinegar.
5. Usually served with head cheese.
