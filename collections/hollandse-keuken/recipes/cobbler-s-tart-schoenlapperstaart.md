## Ingredients

- 12 large sour apples
- 10 large rusks
- 1½ teaspoons fine cinnamon
- 80 g butter or margarine
- 75 g sugar
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook thick applesauce in the usual way (see recipe 531), sift it, mix it with grated rusks, the butter or margarine, the sugar, the egg yolks and the fine cinnamon.
2. Finally stir in the stiffly beaten egg white as lightly as possible, put the mixture in a greased and finely breadcrumbed tart pan and bake the tart in a moderately warm oven until light brown (½ hour to 45 minutes).
3. Let it steam out on a cake rack and serve it warm or cold.
