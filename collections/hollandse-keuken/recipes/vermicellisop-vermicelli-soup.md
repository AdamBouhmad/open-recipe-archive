## Ingredients

- 60 grams vermicelli
- 1 liter water
- 4 decigrams salt
- 1 liter bone bouillon
- Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Cook 60 grams vermicelli in 1 liter water in which you have added 4 decigrams salt.
2. Refresh it and pour it through a colander.
3. Put the vermicelli in 1 liter boiling bone bouillon and add some Liebig's Meat Extract.
4. Stir gently with a spoon to prevent clumping.
5. Let it simmer gently for five minutes, keeping the lid of the pot three-quarters closed.
6. Remove the foam.
7. Taste whether the soup is salty enough and put it in the tureen.
8. Usually 1 liter bouillon is calculated for four persons.
