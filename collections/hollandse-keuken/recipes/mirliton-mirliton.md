## Ingredients

- Two eggs
- One and a half ounces fine sugar
- White of three eggs, well beaten
- Orange blossom water
- Water
- A little flour
- Salt
- Feuilleté crust

## Directions

1. Put in a bowl two eggs and one and a half ounces fine sugar, mix well together
2. Add the white of three eggs, well beaten with a whisk
3. Mix in the bowl with a little orange blossom water, water, a little flour and salt
4. Make edges of feuilleté in your molds the thickness of a guilder
5. Put your mixture in the middle
6. Bring to a mild oven
