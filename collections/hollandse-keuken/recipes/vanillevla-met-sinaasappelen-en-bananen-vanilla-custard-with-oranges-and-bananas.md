## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter melk
- 40 g suiker
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 1 ei
- 1/2 stokje vanille or vanillesuiker
- 2 sinaasappelen
- 1 banaan
- + 60 g suiker

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, carefully remove all white membranes, slice them crosswise, carefully remove all seeds and layer them with the suiker and the peeled, sliced bananas in the custard dish.
2. Drizzle them from time to time with the juice.
3. Prepare the custard as described in recipe 657.
4. Remove the vanilla, let it cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin from forming.
5. Pour the custard over the fruits.
