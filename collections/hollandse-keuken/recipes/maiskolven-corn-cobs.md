## Ingredients

- 4 young corn cobs
- salt to taste
- a little sugar
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves and the fluffy threads from the cobs.
2. Then cook the cobs in plenty of boiling water with salt and a little sugar until tender (1/2 hour, then the kernels should come away easily; larger cobs must cook longer).
3. Serve them warm on a plate with fresh butter or margarine and fine salt.
