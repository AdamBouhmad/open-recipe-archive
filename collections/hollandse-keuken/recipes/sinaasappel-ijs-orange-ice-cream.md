## Ingredients

- juice of 5 oranges
- juice of 2 lemons
- mixed with as much water until 1/2 liter liquid is obtained
- 150 g sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1.5 dl cream
- a little carmine
- grated peel of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix the orange juice with the lemon juice, the grated lemon peel and as much water until 1/2 liter liquid is obtained.
2. Dissolve the sugar in it and stir this liquid after cooling to the egg yolks, which have been beaten with the cream.
3. Add as much carmine as needed to obtain a nice red color and let the mixture freeze in the usual manner.
