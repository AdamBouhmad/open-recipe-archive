## Ingredients

- 6 kg purslane
- salad oil
- cork

## Directions

1. Sort through the purslane, wash it carefully until the water runs completely clear and let it cook with the adhering water until done (20 to 30 minutes).
2. Make sure that in the meantime bottles with wide necks are cleaned and boiled for 20 minutes.
3. Let the vegetable drain.
4. Fill the bottles with the purslane, press it in as firmly as possible.
5. Pour on enough cooking liquid so that the purslane is just covered.
6. Pour some salad oil on top.
7. Close the bottle, after cleaning the rim, with a scrubbed and boiled cork, cut it level with the rim, let it cool briefly and immerse the top of the bottle twice in melted sealing wax.
8. Attach a label with name and date.
9. Note: If desired, some melted butter can be poured on and a teaspoon of salt placed on top. The cooked purslane can also be placed in preserving jars and sterilized in them for half an hour.
