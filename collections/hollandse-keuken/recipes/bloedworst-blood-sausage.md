## Ingredients

- warm pork blood
- pork tongue
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- clove powder
- rye flour
- ox intestines
- lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Stir the warm pork blood until the skin and fibers are removed
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Cut the pork tongue and some bacon into small cubes
4. Mix with the blood, adding salt, pepper, clove powder and some rye flour
5. Stuff into ox intestines
6. Hang them in lukewarm water and remove as soon as they boil to prevent bursting
