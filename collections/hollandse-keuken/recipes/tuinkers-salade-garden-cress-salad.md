## Ingredients

- garden cress
- egg yolks from hard-boiled eggs
- oil
- sugar
- mustard
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the cress fine.
2. Add some yolks from hard-boiled eggs.
3. Toss with oil, sugar, mustard, salt, and lemon juice.
