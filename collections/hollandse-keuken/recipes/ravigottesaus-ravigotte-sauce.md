## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- 3½ dl salad oil
- ½ dl vinegar
- 1½ teaspoon mustard or mustard powder
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- chervil
- tarragon
- pimpernel
- shallot

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as mayonnaise, as described in recipe 367.
2. Stir in the last the washed and very finely chopped herbs.
3. Serve with chateaubriands, beef tenderloin, cold veal.
4. Store it, if necessary, in the same manner as mayonnaise.
