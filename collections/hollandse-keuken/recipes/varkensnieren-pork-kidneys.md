## Ingredients

- kidneys
- butter
- shallots, finely chopped
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into slices and fry them in butter with finely chopped shallots and parsley, pepper and salt
2. By constantly shaking prevent it from burning
3. Now stir in some flour as needed and let it just boil; then quickly remove it from the fire
