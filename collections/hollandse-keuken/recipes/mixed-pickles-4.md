## Ingredients

- corn
- shallots
- small onions
- gherkins
- green beans
- peppercorns
- cauliflower without stem
- melon pieces
- cucumbers
- prunes
- water
- Spanish pepper
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook all ingredients in a little water and then place in glasses, well covered with vinegar
2. All the fruits mentioned are preserved only at the time of their ripening and treated this way
3. When you have everything together, divide them well mixed in glasses, put in each a good quantity of Spanish pepper, fill the glasses further with wine vinegar, stop them with a broad cork, cover with a bladder and store in a cool place
