## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- water
- salt
- veal bouillon
- roasted bread

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts in water with a little salt
2. Carefully remove them from all shells and pound them finely in a mortar
3. Rub them with some veal bouillon through a strainer
4. Add as much bouillon as needed and as desired for the consistency of the soup
5. Let it cook for a moment and add roasted bread cut into cubes
