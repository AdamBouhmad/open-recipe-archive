## Ingredients

- 1 Ned. pound cooked potatoes
- 2.5 ounces melted butter
- 5 eggs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- cinnamon snippets
- candied citrus peel
- flour
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cream

## Directions

1. Make 1 Dutch pound cooked potatoes fine
2. add 2.5 ounces melted butter, 5 eggs, sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, cinnamon snippets and candied citrus peel
3. stir well together
4. put in the mold
5. let it cook
6. serve with a sauce of flour, butter, sugar, cinnamon and cream
