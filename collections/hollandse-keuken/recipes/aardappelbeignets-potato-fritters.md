## Ingredients

- 500 g cold potatoes
- 1-2 eggs
- 100 g old Gouda cheese
- nutmeg
- salt
- 1 egg for coating
- breadcrumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Grind the potatoes, mix them with the beaten eggs, half of the grated cheese, some salt and nutmeg.
2. Form flat cakes, roll these through fine breadcrumbs mixed with the remaining cheese.
3. Treat them further exactly as potato croquettes (recipe 416).
