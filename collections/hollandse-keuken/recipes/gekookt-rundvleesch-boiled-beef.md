## Ingredients

- A loin or rump cut of beef
- Brine (salt water)
- Water
- Anchovies or butter sauce

## Directions

1. Take a loin or rump cut, wash it well and place it in brine for a couple of days, basting it daily with brine.
2. Hang the meat with cold water and let it cook steadily for 3 to 4 hours depending on size, skimming well.
3. Ensure the pot remains filled with bouillon, refilling with boiling water from time to time as it evaporates.
4. Serve this meat either with an anchovy or butter sauce.
5. The bouillon is good for making soup, or one can prepare pears, apples, carrots etc. with it.
