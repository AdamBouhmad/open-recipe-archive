## Ingredients

- 500 g flour
- 250 g butter or margarine
- 250 g bastard sugar
- 2 eggs
- 10 g fine cinnamon

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients well together.
2. Form approximately 80 balls from this dough.
3. Clean the heated iron with salt and a piece of grey paper.
4. Grease it the first time with some butter or margarine, place it in the middle of a ball of dough, pinch the iron shut and bake the cookies above a moderate warm fire or above a gas flame on both sides until light brown and done.
5. Remove them immediately from the iron and let them cool.
