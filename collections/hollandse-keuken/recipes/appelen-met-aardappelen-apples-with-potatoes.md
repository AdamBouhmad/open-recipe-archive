## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg tart apples (or half sweet, half tart)
- 1 1/2 kg potatoes
- 40 g fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, wash them, cut them into quarters and place them in enough boiling water to half cover them.
2. Add the peeled and cut apples, from which the cores have been removed, and the fat.
3. Let everything cook together on a gentle fire until tender (approximately 1/2 hour) and mash it together.
