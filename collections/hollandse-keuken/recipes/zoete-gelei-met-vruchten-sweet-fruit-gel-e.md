## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- 300 g sugar
- 55 g white gelatin or 50 g powdered gelatin
- juice of one lemon
- 2 to 3 egg whites
- 4 dl maraschino
- 200 g candied fruits or more

## Directions

1. Prepare the water mixed with the sugar, the soaked gelatin, the lemon juice and the egg white in the same manner as described for aspic.
2. Pour the liquid through a doubled thick cloth, add the maraschino and pour a thin layer into a mold rinsed with cold water.
3. Let this cool (preferably on ice), then arrange some candied fruits, alternating in shape and color, on it, glue them with a little lukewarm gelée, let the mass cool again, pour gelée over until everything is covered and let it set.
4. Continue this way until the entire mold is filled.
5. Turn the macédoine out onto a damp plate.
6. Note: If the pudding does not come out of the mold easily, it is best to dip it briefly in warm water.
