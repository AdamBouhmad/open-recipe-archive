## Ingredients

- One quarter measure flour
- One pound salt
- Two ounces sugar
- Seven and one half ounces butter
- Twelve eggs
- Milk

## Directions

1. Make a hole in the flour
2. Place in the hole: one pound salt, two ounces sugar, seven and one half ounces butter, and twelve eggs
3. Knead well together, at least three times
4. If too stiff, add a little milk
5. Let stand for half an hour
6. Add half a pound butter
7. Roll four times on the work surface
8. Form in a thick mold
9. Lift the edges slightly with ridges
10. Make some figures on top
11. Place on a tin sheet
12. Bake in the oven for one and a half hours
