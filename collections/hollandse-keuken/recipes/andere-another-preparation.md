## Ingredients

- Cooked tongue
- Pepper
- Salt
- Cloves
- Red wine
- Lemon
- Onions

## Directions

1. Take the cooked tongue and split it lengthwise open
2. Lay it in a dish and let it simmer for a good hour with the addition of pepper, salt, crushed cloves, a glass of red wine, some lemon and some finely chopped onions
