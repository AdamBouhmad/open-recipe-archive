## Ingredients

- 500 g sausages or fresh sausage
- 40 g lard or margarine
- flour

## Directions

1. First manner: Rinse the sausages with lukewarm water, dry them with a clean cloth or between a piece of brown paper. Sprinkle a little flour over them and fry them in the fat or margarine over a not too hot fire to prevent the skin from bursting, brown and cooked (approximately 15 minutes). Serve the sausages with cauliflower or sauerkraut.
2. Second manner: Make the rinsed sausages or sausage cooked in as little water as possible, brought to a boil. Let this evaporate completely, remove the lid from the pan and roast the sausages or sausage on both sides in their own fat brown (approximately 10 minutes).
3. Note: If not enough fat has come out during cooking for frying, some margarine, lard or beef fat can be added
