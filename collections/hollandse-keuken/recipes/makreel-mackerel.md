## Ingredients

- 1 kg mackerel
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Clean the mackerel, make a slit along the backbone
2. Cook it in plenty of boiling water or poach it in a little boiling water with some vinegar, butter or margarine and salt. In the latter case the cooking time is longer (approximately 20 to 30 minutes)
3. Serve the fish with vinegar or mustard sauce
4. Do not keep mackerel too long, as it becomes rancid quickly
