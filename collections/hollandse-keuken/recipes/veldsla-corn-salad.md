## Ingredients

- 350 g corn salad
- 1 egg
- 2 spoonfuls salad oil
- 2 spoonfuls vinegar or herb vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- optionally a splash of Maggi's Aroma
- sliced leek, onion, or chives

## Directions

1. Pick through the corn salad, wash it, let it drain as dry as possible in a colander or shake it in a salad spinner.
2. Prepare it as described in recipe 479.
