## Ingredients

- chickens
- water
- butter
- veal bouillon
- rusks (finely ground)
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated lemon zest
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into pieces and cook them in water until tender, skimming well.
2. Take the necessary amount of butter and brown it.
3. Stir in veal bouillon, finely ground rusks, finely sliced onions, pepper, salt, nutmeg, grated lemon zest, flour and chopped parsley.
4. Place the chicken pieces in this mixture and let them stew until done.
