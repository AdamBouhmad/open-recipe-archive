## Ingredients

- lamb or mutton meat
- cold water
- hot water
- boiling water
- onions
- some yellow carrots
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat for this purpose well in cold water
2. Broil it with hot water once more and cover it with boiling water
3. Cook it with onions, some yellow carrots and salt, skimming carefully
4. Lamb meat requires 1 hour, but mutton requires 2 to 3 hours to become tender
