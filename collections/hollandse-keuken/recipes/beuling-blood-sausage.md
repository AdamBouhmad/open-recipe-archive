## Ingredients

- onions
- unmelted lard
- pork blood
- cream
- salt
- spices
- clean intestines

## Directions

1. Chop onions very finely and cook them with unmelted lard
2. When well melted, add more unmelted lard cut into cubes, pork blood, cream, salt and spices
3. Stuff the beuling into clean intestines, tie them and cook in water for ten minutes
4. To serve, roast them, but prick the intestine so it does not burst
