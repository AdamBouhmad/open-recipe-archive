## Ingredients

- beef tenderloin
- calf's foot
- chicken
- water

## Directions

1. Take a beef tenderloin, a calf's foot and a chicken
2. Clean them well
3. Place them in a large milk can without salt or water
4. Bind the can closed with a bladder
5. Let the can cook for 5 hours in a large pot of water, ensuring the water stays at a continuous boil
6. Remove the bladder from the can
7. Pour the broth into molds
8. This is an extremely strengthening broth for the sick
