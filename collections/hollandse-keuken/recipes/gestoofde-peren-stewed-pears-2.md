## Ingredients

- pears
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- red wine or fruit juice
- cochineal

## Directions

1. Stew the peeled pears with water, sugar, cinnamon and a splash of red wine or fruit juice
2. For a nice color, one can add some cochineal
