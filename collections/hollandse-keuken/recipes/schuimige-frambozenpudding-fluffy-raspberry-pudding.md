## Ingredients

- 500 g raspberries
- juice of 1 lemon
- 100 to 125 g brown sugar
- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 17 1/2 g gelatine with 1 sheet red gelatine (9 sheets), or 12 1/2 g powdered gelatine

## Directions

1. Prepare the pudding as the fluffy strawberry pudding from the previous recipe.
