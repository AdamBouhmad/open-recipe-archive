## Ingredients

- 4 beautiful, flat, lean veal cutlets of at least 100 g
- Salt
- 1 egg for breading
- Breadcrumbs
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 6 gherkins
- 4 anchovies
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- Lemon
- Capers

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as for kalfsoesters.
2. Place them on a warmed plate and garnish with slices of hard-boiled egg and anchovy that has been cleaned in the usual way, gherkins, halved seeded lemon slices or capers.
3. Fill the space between the cutlets with scrambled eggs or pipe potato purée alongside.
