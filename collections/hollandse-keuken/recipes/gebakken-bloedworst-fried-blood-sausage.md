## Ingredients

- 600 g baking blood sausage
- 50 g butter or margarine or lard
- approximately 2 sour apples

## Directions

1. Slice the sausage into slices of approximately 3/4 cm thickness, remove the skin.
2. Heat the fat in a frying pan and fry the blood sausage on both sides quickly brown.
3. If desired, fry apple slices in the same fat as indicated in the recipe for pork roll.
