## Ingredients

- 1.25 l milk
- 200 g macaroni
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil.
2. Break the macaroni into pieces, rinse it on a sieve until the water runs completely clear and sprinkle it into the boiling milk while stirring.
3. Keep stirring until the milk boils again and then let the porridge cook on a very gentle fire, stirring occasionally.
