## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg large carrots
- 1 kg potatoes
- 250 g white beans
- 350 g onions
- 60 g fat

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in the usual manner (recipe 493) until tender.
2. Peel the carrots, shred them, wash them and add enough water (or if available: bean water) to half cover them.
3. Let them cook for a quarter hour, then place the peeled and cut potatoes and onions on top, add the fat and the beans and let everything cook together on a gentle fire until tender and almost dry (30 to 35 minutes).
4. Add more water if needed, but make sure the mash does not become too moist.
5. Mash or stir everything quickly together with a wooden spoon or with a masher.
