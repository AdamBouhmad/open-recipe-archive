## Ingredients

- 2 large or 4 small artichokes
- 2.5 dl lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the stem from the artichokes, take out the ugly leaves and cut off the withered tips from the remaining ones.
2. Wash them, halve the large ones and bind them crosswise with twine.
3. Cook them in boiling salted water until the leaves come away (approximately 1 hour or less).
4. Let them drain on a skimming spoon, remove the inner part (the still rolled flower petals) and serve them with lemon sauce or Hollandaise sauce.
