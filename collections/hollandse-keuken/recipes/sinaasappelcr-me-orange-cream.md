## Ingredients

- 1/2 l milk
- 4 oranges (+ 2 dl juice)
- 2 eggs
- 75 g sugar
- 25 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the sugar and the maizena. Add the grated peel of one orange and the milk and bring the custard, stirring well, to the boil. Let it cook through, stirring, for 3 to 4 minutes, mix it with orange juice and the stiffly beaten egg white or pipe this on as garnish after cooling. Serve with biscuits.
