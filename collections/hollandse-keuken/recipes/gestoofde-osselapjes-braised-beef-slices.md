## Ingredients

- Beef slices
- Pepper
- Salt
- Water
- Fine onions
- Cloves
- Ground rusks

## Directions

1. Beat the slices well to tenderize and rub them in with pepper and salt
2. Lay them in an earthenware pan with some water
3. Put fire above and below and braise them for a good half hour
4. Sprinkle them with fine onions, cloves and ground rusks
5. Baste them constantly with the jus and braise them for another half hour
