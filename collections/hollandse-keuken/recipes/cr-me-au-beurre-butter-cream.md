## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 50 to 100 g powdered sugar
- strong coffee extract, cocoa powder, fruit essences, rum or maraschino, plant coloring

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy and add the sifted powdered sugar.
2. Then add a little strong coffee extract, cocoa powder, some fruit essence, rum or maraschino and if necessary a drop of plant coloring.
3. Let the crème au beurre cool well before using it for piping.
4. In very warm weather, a sheet of dissolved gelatin, which has been soaked in cold water first, can be added to the mass.
5. Note: A less fatty and cheaper crème au beurre can be made by adding the same amount of pastry cream (see recipe 816) to the crème au beurre in small quantities at a time.
