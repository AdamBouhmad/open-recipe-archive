## Ingredients

- peas
- bouillon from lean beef
- vegetables
- salt

## Directions

1. Handle the peas as described above
2. When the peas are cooked, add bouillon made from lean beef, vegetables and salt to taste
3. Let everything cook together until the vegetables are well cooked
