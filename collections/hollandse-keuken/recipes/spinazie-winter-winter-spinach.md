## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg spinach
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 8 slices of bread
- 20 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Strip the spinach, carefully remove the seeds, place it in plenty of cold water for some time to let the sand settle.
2. Carefully remove it and wash it as many times as necessary until completely clear water comes off.
3. Let it cook until tender with as little water as possible, stirring occasionally (approximately 20 minutes).
4. Let it drain in a colander and chop it as finely as possible.
5. Braise it for approximately 5 minutes with butter or margarine and salt.
6. Place it in a dish and garnish it with pieces of hard-boiled egg and slices of bread fried light brown in butter or margarine.
