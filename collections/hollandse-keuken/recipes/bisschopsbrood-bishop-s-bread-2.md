## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- flour equal to the weight of 4 eggs (approximately 200 g)
- sugar equal to the weight of 4 eggs (approximately 200 g)
- 60 g raisins
- 60 g currants
- 30 g candied citrus peel
- 25 g almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the whole eggs and the sugar for 15 minutes with a whisk.
