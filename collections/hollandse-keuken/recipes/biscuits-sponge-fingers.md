## Ingredients

- 100 g sugar
- 4 egg whites
- 5 egg yolks
- grated lemon zest
- 90 g flour
- 10 g cornstarch

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks completely smooth with the sugar and the grated lemon zest.
2. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, combine it with a few light strokes with the yolks and stir through this batter as quickly as possible the sifted flour.
3. Put the batter in a piping bag with a smooth nozzle and pipe oblong cookies onto a baking sheet with paper on it.
4. Sprinkle them with a little sugar and bake the biscuits in a hot oven done (at most 15 minutes), but ensure they remain as light in color as possible.
5. Remove them from the paper and let them cool.
