## Ingredients

- 1/4 l cooking cream or 1/4 l cream and 1/4 l milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 g Duryea maizena
- 50 g sugar
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as vanilla sauce (recipe 139).
2. Present the sauce with mocha pudding (recipe 711).
