## Ingredients

- 12 large or 14 small eggs
- 400 g sugar
- 1 stick vanilla
- 1 l brandy

## Directions

1. Whip with a whisk in a well-enameled pan the eggs with the vanilla pulp and the sugar until this is dissolved as well as possible.
2. Add the brandy gradually and let the advocaat au bain-marie or on a gentle fire, always stirring with the whisk, become warm and lightly thickened, but make sure it does not curdle.
3. Pour it into well-cleaned carafes or bottles and close them tightly.
