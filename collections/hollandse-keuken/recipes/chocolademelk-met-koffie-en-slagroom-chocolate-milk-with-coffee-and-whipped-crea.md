## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 45 g Blooker's cacao
- 75 g sugar
- 1 1/2 dl coffee extract
- 1/3 l whipped cream
- 1 tablespoon Nescafé

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar with the cacao and Nescafé together and mix with some cold milk.
2. Pour this while stirring into the boiling milk and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Pour the cacao into chocolate cups and top with whipped cream.
4. Serve with dry biscuits.
