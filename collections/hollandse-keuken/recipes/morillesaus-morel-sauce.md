## Ingredients

- Morels
- Bouillon
- Mace
- 5 to 6 egg yolks
- 1 spoon flour
- Water

## Directions

1. Wash and sort the morels well and let them cook in bouillon with some mace
2. Stir 5 to 6 egg yolks and 1 spoon flour with some water well together
3. Mix the sauce with this mixture
