## Ingredients

- 300 g Californian pears
- 75 g sugar
- some sago
- peel of ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the pears several times with lukewarm water, let them soak for 24 hours in plenty of cold water.
2. Bring them to a boil with the same water and the lemon peel and let them cook gently until tender (approximately 1 hour).
3. Add the sugar, remove the lemon peel.
4. Carefully thicken the liquid with some mixed sago.
