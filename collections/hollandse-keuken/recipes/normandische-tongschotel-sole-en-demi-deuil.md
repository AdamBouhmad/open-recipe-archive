## Ingredients

- 4 small cooking soles
- 18 mussels
- 200 g shrimp
- 1 tin of mushrooms
- 1/2 tin of truffles
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 3 dl fish stock
- mace
- parsley
- thyme
- 1 dl cream
- some Rhine wine (or milk)
- 2 egg yolks
- some salt (some lemon juice)

## Directions

1. Make fillets of the soles in the usual way or order them filleted, wash them and cook them in abundant boiling water with salt until done.
2. Draw from the bones with + 4 dl water, salt and herbs in + 1/2 hour some fish stock.
3. Lay the fillets crosswise on a warm platter and keep it warm on the steam.
4. Prepare the sauce in the usual way.
5. Use the sifted fish stock for this.
6. Add the cream (and the Rhine wine) to it and heat it well.
7. Carefully blend the sauce with the beaten egg yolks, diluted with some cream, add some lemon juice if desired and pour the sauce over the fillets.
8. Garnish the platter further with the shrimp washed in salt water, the very finely chopped truffels, the mushrooms cut in half and the mussels, which have been cooked in the usual way (recipe 195) and removed from the shell.
