## Ingredients

- 3 cans sweet milk
- 12 to 13 egg yolks
- a little rose water
- roasted white bread, cut into cubes
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Stir 12 to 13 egg yolks and a little rose water into 3 cans sweet milk
2. Place on the fire and stir until it comes to a boil
3. Pour it over roasted white bread cut into cubes and add sugar to taste
