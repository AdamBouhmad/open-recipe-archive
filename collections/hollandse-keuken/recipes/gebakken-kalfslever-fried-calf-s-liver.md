## Ingredients

- calf liver
- water
- cloth
- potato flour
- grated old white bread
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the liver in clean water for a couple of hours
2. Dry it with a cloth
3. Remove the skin
4. Cut out the tendons
5. Cut into slices the thickness of two fingers
6. Roll them in potato flour and grated old white bread in equal parts
7. Fry them in butter on both sides until brown
8. Serve with salad
