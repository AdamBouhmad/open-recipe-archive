## Ingredients

- 400 g cooked potatoes
- 2.5 dl milk
- 15-20 g flour
- 15-20 g butter or margarine
- 1-2 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- 60 g butter or margarine for frying

## Directions

1. Make the potatoes fine, mix them with the milk, flour, melted butter or margarine, egg yolks and finally the stiffly beaten egg white as airy as possible.
2. Fry 8 small pancakes from this batter in a small frying pan with the butter or margarine light brown and cooked.
