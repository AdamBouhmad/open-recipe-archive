## Ingredients

- Sweet milk
- Rice flour
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Rose water

## Directions

1. In 4 cans of sweet milk, add 6 ounces of rice flour
2. Stir in 16 to 20 egg yolks with a little sugar and rose water
3. Cook until it is properly thickened
4. You can also reduce the quantities proportionally depending on the number of people
