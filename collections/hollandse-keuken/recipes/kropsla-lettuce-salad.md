## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 small heads of lettuce
- 2 eggs
- 3 spoonfuls salad oil
- 2 spoonfuls vinegar or herb vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- optionally a splash of Maggi's Aroma
- chopped tarragon and pimpernel
- optionally sliced leek, or chives, or onion

## Directions

1. Remove the lettuce of the outer leaves, the hard green leaves and the leaf ribs.
2. Pluck the whole leaves into pieces, wash them, let them drain as dry as possible in a colander or shake them in a salad spinner.
3. Make hard-boiled egg fine in the salad bowl, stir in the oil, vinegar, mustard, pepper and salt, optionally the Maggi's Aroma.
4. Mix the lettuce as quickly and lightly as possible with this sauce, add the finely cut green herbs (and the leek, onion or some cut chives).
5. Optionally place a hard-boiled egg cut into wedges as garnish on the salad.
6. Note: Instead of oil and vinegar one can also use mayonnaise or salad sauce.
