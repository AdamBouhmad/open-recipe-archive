## Ingredients

- For the dough: 475 g flour, 120 g melted butter or margarine, 2 eggs, approximately 3 dl lukewarm milk, 30 g yeast
- For the filling: 200 g butter or margarine, 100 g sugar, 1 teaspoon fine cinnamon, 75 g candied orange peel, 1 dl syrup
- 50 g flour for dusting the board or table

## Directions

1. Make a firm batter from the flour, melted butter or margarine, the eggs, the lukewarm milk and the yeast mixed with lukewarm milk.
2. Let this rise, covered, in a lukewarm place for approximately 1 hour.
3. In the meantime, for the filling, stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the sugar, the fine cinnamon, the chopped candied orange peel and the syrup.
4. Roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board to approximately 1/2 cm thickness.
5. Cut strips of 2.5 cm width.
6. Pinch the strips together on both sides so that a groove is formed, which is filled with the filling, which should protrude slightly above the dough.
7. Roll the strips with the openings facing upward in a spiral shape (starting in the middle) into a greased iron pot, fireproof saucer or cake pan.
8. Bake the cake for 1 to 1.5 hours in a moderately warm oven and drizzle it occasionally with the liquid that comes out, or, if this is not sufficient, with melted butter or margarine mixed with some syrup.
9. Turn out the cake while warm, cut it into pieces after cooling.
