## Ingredients

- 3 large pimentos or peppers (preferably 2 green and 1 red)
- salt
- 1½ tablespoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons salad oil or some salad sauce or mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Rinse the pimentos, halve them, remove the seeds and seed membranes. Cut the pimentos into narrow strips.
2. Cook these strips for about 5 minutes in some water with salt and a very small dash of vinegar. Let the pimento drain and treat them further as described in previous salad recipes.
