## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 35 g sugar
- 35 g butter or margarine
- grated lemon zest or vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the omelet as described in recipe 389, but plan to carry out all operations as quickly as possible one after another, since the omelet, prepared without flour, is much more likely to collapse than the one to which flour has been added.
