## Ingredients

- 500 g flour
- 375 g sugar
- 375 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- 10 g fine cinnamon
- grated zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients together until well mixed
2. Roll thin strips from the dough approximately 10 cm long
3. Form into rings by pressing the ends together
4. Place on a greased baking sheet with some distance between them
5. Bake in the middle of a moderately warm oven until lightly brown and done (15 minutes)
6. Remove with a pancake knife from the sheet and let them cool
