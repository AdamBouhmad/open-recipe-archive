## Ingredients

- red raspberries
- half an ounce fish glue
- bread sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Crush red raspberries finely and strain the juice through
2. Cook half an ounce fish glue with bread sugar and some water
3. Add the raspberry juice and let it cook like berry jelly, using 1 ounce sugar for every ounce of juice
