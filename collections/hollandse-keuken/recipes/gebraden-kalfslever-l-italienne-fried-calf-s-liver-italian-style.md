## Ingredients

- calf liver
- salt
- pepper
- two eggs
- a spoon of oil or butter
- a little flour
- oil or butter
- sour sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the calf liver into thin strips
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Beat two eggs with a spoon of oil or butter
4. Roll the strips in a little flour
5. Fry them in oil or butter
6. Eat them with a sour sauce, very hot
