## Ingredients

- 2½ ounces wheat flour
- ¼ quart milk
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix 2½ ounces wheat flour with ¼ quart milk and let simmer slowly with constant stirring.
2. When cooled slightly, slowly beat in 4 eggs.
3. Roll into small balls and fry in not too hot oil.
