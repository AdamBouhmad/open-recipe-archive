## Ingredients

- 4 to 6 pisangs (bananas)
- 25 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Peel the fruits, place them in the butter or margarine that has been made light brown in the frying pan and fry them carefully, turning occasionally, until light brown on all sides.
2. Serve them with roasted meat.
