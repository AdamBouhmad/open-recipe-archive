## Ingredients

- veal shank with bones
- water
- salt
- rice
- vegetables
- veal meat for meatballs
- ground mace

## Directions

1. Treat as veal shank soup
2. You need not cook it the day before as you can leave the bones in
3. This soup needs only three hours of cooking
4. Add the meatballs half an hour before serving
