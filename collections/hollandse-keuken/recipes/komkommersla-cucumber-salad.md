## Ingredients

- 2 cucumbers
- 2 to 3 spoonfuls salad oil
- approximately 2 spoonfuls vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- celery salt

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut off the bitter points, slice or shave them into thin slices or make long, thin strips with a mandoline.
2. Mix them with oil, vinegar, salt, celery salt and pepper.
3. Sprinkle some finely cut parsley over them.
