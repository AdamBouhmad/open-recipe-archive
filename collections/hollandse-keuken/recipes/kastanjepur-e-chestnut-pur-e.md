## Ingredients

- 1 kg chestnuts
- 1/2 dl cream or 40 g butter or margarine
- 2 dl bouillon
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts until tender, as described in recipe 504.
2. Peel them in small portions at a time and press them through a sieve.
3. Mix them with the cream, butter or margarine and enough bouillon to achieve the consistency of mashed potatoes.
4. Stir in pepper and salt to taste and serve the purée with pork cutlets, veal oysters, etc.
