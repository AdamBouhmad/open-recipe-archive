## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g sugar
- 100 g flour
- 100 g almonds
- 6 eggs
- 2 or 3 kinds of jam
- 150 g icing in different colors
- French candied fruits
- nutmeg
- petit four cases

## Directions

1. Make the batter in the known way (see recipe 833).
2. Spread the dough on a greased baking sheet, which is covered with a greased piece of grease-free paper so that the paper sticks out a little above the edges on all sides, and bake the sheet of dough as described in recipe 864.
3. Remove the paper and divide the pastry into oblong, square or round pieces.
4. Halve the pieces and put some jam between both halves.
5. Drizzle the pastries with thick icing; let them drip off and garnish them with French candied fruits or nutmeg and place them in the petit four cases.
