## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 80 g brown sugar
- juice of approximately 2 lemons, made up to 1/4 l with water

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks completely smooth in a well-enameled pan with the sugar. Set the pan in a bain-marie and let the mixture thicken under constant stirring. Remove this mixture from the heat at the right moment and above all do not let it come to the boil. Add the stiffly beaten egg white in small quantities at once, and let this, beating well, thicken each time. Remove the custard from the heat, let it cool briefly and mix the lemon juice through it with a wooden spoon. Serve immediately after cooling with biscuits.
