## Ingredients

- 4 red or green peppers
- 150 g ground calf meat
- 150 g ground beef meat
- 1 onion
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 3 dl tomato sauce or 3 dl pepper broth
- 1/2 Maggi's bouillon tablet
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- 20 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Remove the stem from the fruits.
2. Cut the peppers in half lengthwise or cut off a cap.
3. Remove the seeds and seed membranes (these have a sharp taste).
4. Wash the fruits.
5. Cook them for approximately 10 minutes in boiling salted water.
6. Mix the ground meat with the chopped onion and spices.
7. Cook it but do not brown it with some butter or margarine in the pan.
8. Fill the fruits with this mixture.
9. Place them in a fireproof dish.
10. Pour the liquid over them, either the tomato sauce or pepper broth.
