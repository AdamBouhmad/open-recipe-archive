## Ingredients

- 1 kg Hamburger rib (cured and lightly smoked beef rib)
- Water
- Butter or margarine
- Garden peas

## Directions

1. Brush the rib clean in warm water.
2. Place it in plenty of boiling water and cook for half an hour per 500 g.
3. Serve with melted butter or margarine and garden peas.
