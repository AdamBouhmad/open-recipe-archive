## Ingredients

- 2 tender young chickens
- salt
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 1 small onion
- 1/3 l sour cream
- 1 teaspoon paprika

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned chickens into pieces.
2. Salt them and stew them in the butter or margarine with a chopped onion (approximately 30 minutes).
3. Pour in the sour cream and add the paprika.
4. Let the chickens stew in this sauce for another quarter hour.
5. Add more water if necessary.
