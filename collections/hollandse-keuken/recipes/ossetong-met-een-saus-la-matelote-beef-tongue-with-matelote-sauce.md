## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Red or white wine (one bottle)
- Flour
- Sugar
- Bouillon
- Shallots

## Directions

1. Prepare the tongue as described previously, but do not lard it
2. Make a sauce from a bottle of red or white wine, with some flour, sugar, bouillon and shallots
