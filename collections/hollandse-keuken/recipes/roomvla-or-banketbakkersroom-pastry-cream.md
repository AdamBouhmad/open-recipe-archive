## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dl milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 25 g flour
- 25 to 50 g sugar
- 1/4 vanilla stick

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar and flour until a smooth mass without lumps.
2. Slowly bring the milk with the vanilla to the boil, add it to the yolks, stirring well, pour everything back into the pan and let the custard cook, stirring well with a whisk (about 10 minutes).
3. Stir occasionally while cooling to prevent a skin from forming.
4. Note: The custard can also be mixed with stiffly beaten egg white, but then add a couple of sheets of dissolved gelatin to prevent it from collapsing over time (especially recommended for tompouces).
