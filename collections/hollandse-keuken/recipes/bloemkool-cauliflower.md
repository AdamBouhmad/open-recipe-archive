## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower
- 1/2 l milk
- 50 g flour or 30 g Duryea cornstarch
- 50 g butter or 40 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower by cutting out the stalk, green leaves and small leaves in the cabbage.
2. Cut the cabbage into large pieces or leave the cauliflower whole.
3. Make an incision in the remaining stalk to let it cook more easily, wash the cabbage and in the caterpillar season soak it for half an hour in salted water.
4. Set it on with enough boiling water to just cover it, add salt and let it cook until a fork easily penetrates the stalk (in summer 15-20 minutes, in winter 30-40 minutes).
5. Remove the cabbage with a skimming spoon from the pan, let it drain well and place it in a warmed dish.
6. Pour the sauce prepared in the usual way (see recipe 328 or recipe 329) over it and grate some nutmeg over.
