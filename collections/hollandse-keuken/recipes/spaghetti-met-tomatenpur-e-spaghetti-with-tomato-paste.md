## Ingredients

- 100 g spaghetti
- salt
- 1/2 tin tomato paste
- 85 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Break the spaghetti into small pieces, wash it and cook it in boiling salted water for 20 minutes until tender.
2. Place the spaghetti in a colander and rinse it with cold water.
3. Warm the spaghetti with butter or margarine, tomato paste and pepper and let the mixture stew for another 10 minutes.
4. Optionally place it in a fireproof dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, place a few pieces of butter or margarine on top and let it brown in the oven.
