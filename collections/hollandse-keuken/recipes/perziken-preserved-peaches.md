## Ingredients

- 36 peaches
- Sugar syrup (500 g sugar per 1 liter water)

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches, cut them in half and remove the pits.
2. Arrange them neatly in well-cleaned bottles up to 3 cm below the rim.
3. Pour enough sugar syrup over them so that the peaches are well submerged.
4. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clips and heat them for 20 minutes to 70-80° C.
5. Let the bottles cool with the clips on, remove the clips and label the bottles with name and date.
6. (For corked bottles see the rules on page 366).
