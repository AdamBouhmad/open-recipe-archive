## Ingredients

- 2 cups oat barley
- 1 pound currants
- half pound raisins
- 1 ounce cinnamon
- half ounce cloves
- nutmeg
- salt
- 1 pound lard
- intestines
- lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Soak 2 cups oat barley overnight
2. Mix with 1 pound currants, half pound raisins, 1 ounce cinnamon, half ounce cloves, plenty of nutmeg, a little salt, and 1 pound lard
3. Loosely stuff into well-cleaned intestines
4. Hang them in lukewarm water and bring just to a boil
