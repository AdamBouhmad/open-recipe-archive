## Ingredients

- 4 dl water
- juice of 2 lemons, together approximately 3/2 liter
- peel of 1 lemon
- approximately 75 g sugar
- approximately 20 g sago or potato starch

## Directions

1. Grate the peel of one well-washed lemon on an enamelled or glass grater
2. Bring the water with the sugar and the lemon peel to a boil
3. Pour in the sago mixed with cold water and let the sauce simmer while stirring briefly
4. Add the sieved lemon juice
5. Present it with semolina or cornstarch pudding etc.
