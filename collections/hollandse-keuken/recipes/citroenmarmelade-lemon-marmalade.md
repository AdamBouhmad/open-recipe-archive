## Ingredients

- 12 lemons
- water
- sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- twine
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- labels

## Directions

1. Brush the lemons clean, cut them whole into thin slices, removing the pits.
2. Soak them in enough water to cover them completely for 24 hours.
3. Cook them in this liquid until the pieces have softened, weigh them with the liquid, add the same weight of sugar and cook them, stirring occasionally, until they reach the required thickness.
4. Treat them further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
