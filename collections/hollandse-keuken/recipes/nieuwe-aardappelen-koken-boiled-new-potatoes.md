## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg potatoes
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the potatoes in cold water for some time, then brush them clean and scrape them with a knife, as much as possible under water, until the skin is removed.
2. Wash them, set them to cook with little boiling water and some salt (10 g per litre of water).
3. Let them cook until they can be easily pierced with a fork, though not too easily (approximately 20 minutes).
4. Drain them as dry as possible, let them dry on the fire without a lid, shaking occasionally to prevent scorching.
5. Present them with melted or unmelted butter or margarine, or if desired shake some finely chopped parsley and butter or margarine through them before serving.
