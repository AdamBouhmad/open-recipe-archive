## Ingredients

- Meat, minced or ground
- Potatoes, minced
- Fat
- Onions, chopped
- Gravy with water

## Directions

1. Mince or grind the meat finely.
2. Make the potatoes fine.
3. Fry the chopped onions light brown in the fat.
4. Layer everything layer by layer in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water, making sure the last layer is potatoes.
5. Moisten with the gravy with water and finish as indicated in the first method.
