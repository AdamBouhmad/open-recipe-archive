## Ingredients

- 3 cans buttermilk
- old-baked white bread of 10 to 15 cents, cut into pieces
- some peeled good sour apples, cut into cubes
- a little aniseed
- molasses or brown sugar

## Directions

1. Take 3 cans buttermilk
2. Add old-baked white bread cut into pieces
3. Add some peeled good sour apples cut into cubes and a little aniseed
4. Let everything cook together
5. Serve with molasses or brown sugar
