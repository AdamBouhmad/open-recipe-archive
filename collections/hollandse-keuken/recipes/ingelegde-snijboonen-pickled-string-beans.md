## Ingredients

- pickled string beans
- water
- bouillon
- pepper
- smoked or pickled sausage (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as they come from the salt in a copper kettle
2. Let them drain on a colander when cooked
3. When cold, press them out
4. Set them in water to extract, changing the water a couple of times
5. Stew them with water, bouillon and pepper
6. Alternatively, add smoked or pickled sausage, omitting the bouillon
