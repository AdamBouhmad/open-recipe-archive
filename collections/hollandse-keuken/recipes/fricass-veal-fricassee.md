## Ingredients

- best part of veal breast
- bouillon
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- crushed herbs
- egg yolks (optional for binding)

## Directions

1. Take the best part of a veal breast and cut it into pieces
2. Cook it with bouillon until half done
3. Roast flour and butter, add it to the meat and let it fry together
4. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and crushed herbs
5. Pour hot bouillon over it
6. Let everything braise until done
7. When serving, strain the jus through a sieve; if too thin, bind it with a couple of egg yolks
