## Ingredients

- apricots
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apricots, cut them in half and remove the pit
2. Set them in water on the fire, using half a pound of sugar for every pound of apricots
3. Cook them carefully and slowly until done, so they do not turn to mush
4. When cooked, remove the pan from the fire
5. As soon as they are cold, serve them with the liquid, which should not be too thin
