## Ingredients

- 8 slices of old bread
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 3½ dl berry juice
- 3 dl water
- 75 g sugar
- 10 g sago
- piece of cinnamon stick or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Remove the crusts from the bread slices and cut them evenly
2. Fry the bread slices on both sides quickly in the browned butter or margarine until brown
3. Prepare the berry juice sauce in the usual manner without sago (see recipe 748)
4. Soak the fried bread slices in the sauce until they become soft
5. Stack them pyramidally on a plate
6. Pour the remaining sauce, bound with sago, over them
7. Serve this dish warm or cold, or stack the crisp fried bread slices on top after sprinkling them with sugar and cinnamon and serve the berry juice sauce separately
8. Alternatively, instead of frying, the bread slices can be toasted on both sides so the dish contains less fat
