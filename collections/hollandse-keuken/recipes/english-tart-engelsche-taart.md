## Ingredients

- Feuilleté crust
- Crust for pies
- Cherries, morello cherries or other preserves
- Egg white
- Fine pounded white candy sugar

## Directions

1. Make a crust from feuilleté and also a round crust as used for pies
2. Make the bands and standing edges from feuilleté
3. Brush with egg white
4. Lay in this open tart: cherries, morello cherries or other preserves
5. Lay diagonal strips of feuilleté over them
6. When it comes out of the oven, sprinkle with half-finely pounded white candy sugar
