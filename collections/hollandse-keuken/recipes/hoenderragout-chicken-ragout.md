## Ingredients

- chickens
- ground veal (5 oz per pair)
- poultry (5 oz per pair)
- sweetbreads
- butter
- lemon juice
- mace
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Bring the chickens to a boil, then add for each pair 5 oz of ground veal, 5 oz of poultry and some sweetbreads.
2. Let everything cook together until done.
3. Take some of the liquid, add butter, lemon juice and mace, and bind with flour.
4. Add parsley, which should be removed before serving.
5. Let everything stew for a good quarter hour.
