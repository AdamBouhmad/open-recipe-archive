## Ingredients

- one whole white cabbage
- butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take one whole white cabbage and slice only the leaves finely and cook them briefly
2. Then fry the cabbage in a little butter until it begins to turn brown
3. Cook it for half an hour with bouillon to make soup
