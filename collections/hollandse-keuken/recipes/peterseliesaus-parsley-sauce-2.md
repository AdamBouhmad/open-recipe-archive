## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Rinse the parsley, pluck off the leaves and chop or cut them as finely as possible
3. Mix them into the sauce away from the heat
4. Serve with perch, salmon, salted fish, etc.
5. Note: Instead of butter or margarine and flour, the liquid can also be thickened with 25 g mixed sago
