## Ingredients

- 4 chops
- 5 g salt
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 75 g macaroni
- 40 g ham
- 40 g Parmesan cheese
- 20 g butter or margarine
- truffles and mushrooms
- 100 g rice or mashed potato for the base
- 3 dl lean jus with water in which Spanish pepper, cloves and bay leaves have been infused
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash, salt and pepper the chops, brown them in butter or margarine and cook until done (about 20 minutes)
2. Cook the macaroni in the usual manner until done, cut into small pieces and mix with ham cut into pieces, butter, truffles and mushrooms
3. Stand the chops against the base of rice or mashed potato
4. Tie a paper frill around the point, pour the sauce prepared in the usual manner over them, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over and arrange the macaroni around them
