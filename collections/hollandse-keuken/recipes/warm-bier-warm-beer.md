## Ingredients

- 2 cans brown beer
- citrus peel
- cinnamon
- cloves
- 2 cans milk
- 4 to 6 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon potato flour
- 2 ounces sugar
- cold milk

## Directions

1. While you cook 2 cans brown beer with some citrus peel, cinnamon and cloves, and two cans milk, each separately, stir in another pan 4 to 6 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon potato flour, two ounces sugar and a large bowl of cold milk, well together
2. Now pour while continuously stirring first the boiling milk and then the boiling hot beer through a sieve into it
3. Stir the whole over a gentle fire until the eggs are cooked and you can remove it from the fire
