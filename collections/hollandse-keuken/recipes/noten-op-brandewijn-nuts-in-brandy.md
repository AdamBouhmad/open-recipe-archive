## Ingredients

- nuts
- sugar
- brandy

## Directions

1. Peel the nuts. Put them with some sugar in a well-cleaned bottle. Pour brandy over them so that the nuts are well covered. Seal the bottle airtight. Let the nuts stand for at least 3 months before using.
