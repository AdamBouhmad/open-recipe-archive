## Ingredients

- 2½ ounces rice
- juice of 6 lemons
- zest of 1 lemon, cooked
- candied citron peel
- sugar
- tart crust

## Directions

1. Cook 2½ ounces of rice until completely done, without stirring.
2. Add the juice of 6 lemons, the zest of 1 lemon that has been cooked, candied citron peel, and sugar.
3. Put the mixture into a tart crust and bake for half an hour.
4. Allow to cool in the pan afterwards.
