## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- water
- vinegar
- syrup
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- sour apples (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop it very finely
2. Add a beer glass of water, the same amount of vinegar, a spoon of syrup, a little pepper, nutmeg, and salt to taste
3. Let this stew slowly for 4 hours
4. You can also add sour apples, but then use less vinegar
