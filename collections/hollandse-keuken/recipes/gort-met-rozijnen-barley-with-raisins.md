## Ingredients

- 200 g Alkmaar barley
- 100 g raisins
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the barley until the water that runs off is completely clear.
2. Soak it for 24 hours in 5 times as much cold water as barley.
3. Add the well-washed raisins with their stems removed and the salt.
4. Cook the barley as described in the barley recipe.
