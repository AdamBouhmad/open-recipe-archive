## Ingredients

- 4 kleine broodjes (small rolls)
- 2 hardgekookte eieren (hard-boiled eggs)
- 10 g bloem (flour)
- 10 g boter of margarine (butter or margarine)
- 1 dl melk of bouillon (milk or bouillon)
- wat peterselie (parsley)
- 4 champignons (mushrooms)
- zout (salt)
- peper (pepper)
- mosterd (mustard)
- desverkiezend wat citroensap (lemon juice, optional)
- 15 g boter of margarine om te besmeren (butter or margarine for brushing)

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 102.
2. Cut the peeled eggs into small cubes and stir these with pepper, salt, mustard and optionally lemon juice through the sauce.
3. Add the chopped mushrooms and prepare the rolls further as described in the previous recipe.
