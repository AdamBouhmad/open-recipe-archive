## Ingredients

- rabbit
- unsalted butter
- milk
- egg yolks
- nutmeg
- salt
- sugar
- bread
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into pieces and fry in unsalted butter
2. When half fried, chop a portion of the pieces very fine, adding milk, two egg yolks, grated nutmeg and salt
3. Pour this over the rabbit when it is sufficiently fried
4. Add some butter and sugar, each in equal amounts, stirring continuously
5. Serve with chopped bread and arrange chopped vegetables around the edge of the plate
