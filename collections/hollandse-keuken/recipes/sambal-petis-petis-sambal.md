## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons ground onions
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 teaspoon laos
- 2 teaspoons trassi mixed with water
- 1 finely sliced red lombok without seeds
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 teaspoon goela djawa
- 2 teaspoons tamarind water
- 3 tablespoons petis (petis ikan or petis oedang)
- 2 daon djoeroek poeroet
- Some salt
- 1 dl coconut milk
- 1 dl warm water
- Butter, salad oil, beef fat or frying fat

## Directions

1. Pound the spices, except the leaves and the lombok and fry the mixture yellow in one of the aforementioned fats
2. Add the warm water; let the mixture boil
3. Stir in the tablespoons of petis and the trassi water
4. Add the leaves, the lombok, the tamarind water, some salt and the goela djawa
5. Let the mixture come to a boil again and finally add the coconut milk
6. Cook this together until the oil comes out
