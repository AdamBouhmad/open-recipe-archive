## Ingredients

- leftover cooked fish
- diluted fish water, salted water or fish bouillon
- lemon juice
- Maggi's Aroma (optional)
- lemon slices
- butter or margarine
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Use leftover cooked fish, remove the skin while the fish is still warm
2. Take the fish flesh in pieces as large as possible from the bone and lay it in a heat-proof dish
3. Add enough diluted fish water, water with salt or fish bouillon so the bottom is covered
4. Drizzle with lemon juice and if desired a little Maggi's Aroma
5. Lay a couple of sliced lemon slices and here and there a good piece of butter or margarine on top
6. Sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, clean the rim of the dish
7. Let the fish braise gently in a moderately warm oven and become light brown
8. Baste it from time to time with the liquid
