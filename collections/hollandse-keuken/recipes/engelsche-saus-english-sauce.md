## Ingredients

- 10 anchovies
- 1 ounce capers
- 3 egg yolks from hard-boiled eggs
- pepper
- bouillon
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop the anchovies, capers and egg yolks from hard-boiled eggs very finely with a little pepper
2. Add some bouillon
3. Heat it and thicken it with a little butter and flour
