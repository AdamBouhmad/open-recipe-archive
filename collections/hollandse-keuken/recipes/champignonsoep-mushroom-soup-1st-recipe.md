## Ingredients

- 1 l light veal bouillon
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 bunch mushrooms
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dl cream or milk
- nutmeg
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring it to a boil while stirring, rub out the lumps, add the bouillon while stirring
2. Let the soup cook while stirring until done (+ 10 minutes), add the mushrooms with their liquid and some lemon juice and pour the soup while stirring vigorously into the egg yolk, which has been beaten in the soup tureen with the cream or milk and nutmeg
3. N.B. The mushrooms must not cook with the soup, as they would become tough
