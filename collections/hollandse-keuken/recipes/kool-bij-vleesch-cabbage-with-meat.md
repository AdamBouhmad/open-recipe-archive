## Ingredients

- white headed cabbage
- coarse pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- mustard

## Directions

1. Cut the white headed cabbage into quarters
2. Cook until it just begins to fall apart
3. Let drain on a colander
4. While still warm, lay in clean cloths and let stand overnight
5. Rub with coarse pepper and salt
6. Pack as tightly as possible in a pot
7. Pour vinegar mixed with mustard over it, one small spoon per cabbage
8. It must be well covered
9. Rinse with vinegar before use
