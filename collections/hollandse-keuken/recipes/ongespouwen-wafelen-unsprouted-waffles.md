## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- 14 lood butter
- ½ pound sugar
- 5½ lood cinnamon
- 2 eggs
- wine and sugar

## Directions

1. Mix wheat flour, butter, sugar, cinnamon, and eggs with wine and sugar until thick enough.
