## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 20 g yeast
- 7.5 g salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 dl lukewarm milk
- 80 g butter or margarine
- 60 g candied peel
- 35 g almonds
- 75 g raisins
- 35 g orange peel strips
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 60 g sugar

## Directions

1. Make bread dough as described for white bread (recipe 835) from flour, yeast, salt, egg and milk.
2. Let it rise.
3. Then mix in the washed and dried currants and raisins with the finely cut candied peel, the snipped almonds and orange peel strips, the melted butter or margarine, the sugar, the cinnamon, the grated lemon peel.
4. Put the dough in a greased bread pan or let it rise covered in a bowl for 1 hour 15 minutes.
5. Bake the bread in the pan as described for white bread.
6. For a true Weihnachtsstolle, flatten the dough and give it the shape of a round cake.
7. Fold it double, but make sure the bottom half is larger than the top.
8. Press the dough well together.
9. Let it rise covered on a greased baking sheet for a while.
10. Bake the stollen as indicated for white bread (see recipe 835).
11. Dust it with sifted powdered sugar.
12. Tie a red ribbon around it and stick a sprig of holly between.
