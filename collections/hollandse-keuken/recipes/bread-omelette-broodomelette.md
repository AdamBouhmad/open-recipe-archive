## Ingredients

- 200 g old bread without crust
- 50 to 60 g sugar
- 80 g butter or margarine
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 2 deciliters milk
- 1/4 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon zest
- apricot jam or orange marmalade

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in boiling milk and rub it completely smooth.
2. Stir the flavoring, the egg yolks and the sugar through it and lastly as lightly as possible the stiffly beaten egg white.
3. Make an omelette of half of the mixture.
