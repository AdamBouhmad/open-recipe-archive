## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg tart apples
- 1/2 liter water
- 100-125 g sugar
- peel of 1 lemon
- 20 g red and white gelatin or 17 1/2 g powdered gelatin

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into small pieces, rinse them and cook them in the water that has been brought to a boil with the lemon peel
2. Pour everything through a horsehair sieve, rub the apples through it and mix the puree with the sugar and gelatin (which has been soaked 10-15 minutes in cold water and if necessary dissolved with some lemon juice and then with as little boiling water as possible)
3. Let the mass become jelly-like while stirring occasionally and pour it into a stone pudding mold rinsed with cold water
4. Turn the pudding out onto a damp dish after cooling, garnish it with fruit jelly and nuts and serve with vanilla sauce
