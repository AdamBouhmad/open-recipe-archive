## Ingredients

- Potato flour
- Fat or butter
- Laurel leaf
- Softly cooked onions
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Gherkins
- Meat pieces

## Directions

1. Fry potato flour brown in fat or butter
2. Let it cook for a quarter hour
3. Add a laurel leaf, softly cooked onions, vinegar, salt and sliced gherkins
4. Put the chopped meat pieces in
5. Keep it warm until serving
