## Ingredients

- 600 g mushrooms
- salt
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 onion
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- sour cream or yogurt
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms as described in recipe 477.
2. Cut them into quarters.
3. Braise a chopped onion in the butter or margarine.
4. Add paprika powder and the mushroom pieces.
5. Let them braise tender (approximately 20 minutes) with the salt, if needed with some water.
6. Add the sour cream and bind the sauce if necessary with some mixed cornstarch.
