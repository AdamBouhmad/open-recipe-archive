## Ingredients

- 2 ounces butter
- ½ pound finely ground almonds
- 2 ounces sugar
- juice and zest of 1 lemon
- 10 egg yolks
- ½ pound powdered chocolate
- 8 egg whites
- 2 ounces melted loaf sugar
- 1 thimbleful egg white
- a few drops lemon juice
- powdered chocolate for glaze

## Directions

1. Mix together the butter, ground almonds, sugar, lemon juice and zest, and egg yolks.
2. Beat the 8 egg whites until stiff and fold in.
3. Pour the mixture into a prepared mold (see cherry cake).
4. Once cooled, glaze as follows: stir 2 ounces melted loaf sugar with 1 thimbleful egg white, a few drops of lemon juice, and powdered chocolate for a quarter hour until thick, working quickly until it becomes light and light brown.
