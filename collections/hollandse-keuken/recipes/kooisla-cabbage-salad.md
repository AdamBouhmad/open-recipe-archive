## Ingredients

- 40 g red cabbage
- 40 g white cabbage
- ½ head of lettuce
- 5 red radishes
- salad dressing from recipe 952

## Directions

1. Remove the cabbage varieties from the outer hard leaves, wash them and grind or cut them fine. Mix them with the lettuce, which is treated in the usual way. Stir the mixture lightly through the salad dressing. Garnish it with slices of red radish.
