## Ingredients

- 3 pints sweet milk
- 1 beer glass buttermilk
- a little cold butter
- a little saffron
- a couple of egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook 3 pints sweet milk
2. Pour it into a pot and stir until it is lukewarm
3. Add 1 beer glass buttermilk and mix with a little cold butter, a little saffron, and a couple of egg yolks
