## Ingredients

- 4 stalks rhubarb
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 100 to 125 g sugar
- 8 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. (Peel the rhubarb), wash it, cut it into pieces. Let these cook quickly until done, stirring or shaking occasionally, and mix them with the sugar. Stir the maizena smooth with the egg yolks, add enough rhubarb so that the yolks are well warmed, pour them back into the pan and let the mass cook through for 3 to 4 minutes with vigorous stirring. Mix this after cooling with the stiffly beaten egg white and serve with biscuits.
