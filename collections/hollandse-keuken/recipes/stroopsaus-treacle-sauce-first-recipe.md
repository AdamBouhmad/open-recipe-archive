## Ingredients

- 4 dl milk
- 150 g treacle
- 30 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with a little cold milk into a thick paste
2. Stir out the lumps and add enough milk until a thin paste is obtained
3. Bring the remaining milk to a boil
4. Pour the mixed paste in while stirring and let the sauce simmer while stirring (approximately 10 minutes)
5. Remove from heat and mix in the butter or margarine and the treacle
6. Serve with, among other things, kettle cake
