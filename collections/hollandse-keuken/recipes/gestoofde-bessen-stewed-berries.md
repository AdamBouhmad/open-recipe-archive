## Ingredients

- 1 kg berries
- approximately 100 g sugar
- some sago

## Directions

1. Wash the berries, remove them and let them cook on gentle heat, shaking occasionally, for 10 minutes.
2. Add the sugar and bind the liquid with some mixed sago.
