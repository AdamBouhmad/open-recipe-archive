## Ingredients

- 250 g cold meat (preferably veal)
- 2 dl curry or béchamel sauce
- For the garnish: 3 dl aspic in two colours
- notched lemon slices
- capers
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices, place them on a platter and cover them with the sauce, which is prepared in the usual way and, being warm, tastes sharp, by which cold it is usually good in flavour.
2. Let the sauce cool down and pour a thin layer of aspic (see recipe 555) over it. Garnish the platter further with 2 colours of very finely cut aspic with fresh sprigs of parsley, capers and notched lemon slices.
