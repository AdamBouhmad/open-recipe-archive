## Ingredients

- 4 sour apples
- 4 eggs (1 with white, 3 yolks only)
- 1½ rusk
- a piece of cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- sugar
- 2 lemons with juice and zest, grated

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together thoroughly.
2. Put into a tart crust and bake.
