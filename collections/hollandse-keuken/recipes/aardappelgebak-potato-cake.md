## Ingredients

- 5 ounces cooked potatoes
- 2½ ounces melted butter
- 10 eggs with whites

## Directions

1. Rub the cooked potatoes very fine.
2. Add 2½ ounces melted butter and 10 eggs with whites, adding the butter last.
3. Bake in a pudding or other mold with heat above and below.
