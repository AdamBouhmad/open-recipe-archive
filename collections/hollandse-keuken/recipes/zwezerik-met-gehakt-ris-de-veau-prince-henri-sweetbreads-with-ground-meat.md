## Ingredients

- 2 sweetbreads
- 500 g veal
- 300 g fatted pork
- 2 egg whites
- 100 g butter or margarine
- mace
- thyme
- onion
- pieces of cayenne pepper
- a little cream
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the soaked sweetbreads in the usual way until done (see recipe 293)
2. Rinse them with cold water, peel them and slice them to a thickness of 3/4 cm
3. Grind the washed meat and pork twice through a meat grinder finely, mix it with 1 1/2 egg white, pepper, salt, nutmeg and a little cream
4. Coat the sweetbread slices on one side with egg white, spread a layer of the meat mixture on this, fry the sweetbread in the butter or margarine that has been lightly browned in the pan until brown and done (15 minutes), and arrange them in overlapping rows on a heated platter
5. Pour the gravy, finished with a little cream and sweetbread bouillon, over them and garnish the edge with young vegetables or fleurons (recipe 847)
