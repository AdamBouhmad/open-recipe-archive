## Ingredients

- 1 calf's foot per person
- cold water
- salt
- semolina or other flour
- Liebig's Meat Extract
- finely chopped chervil

## Directions

1. Take one calf's foot per person, chop it finely and put it on with cold water and salt, cooked for 2 hours, properly skimmed and strained through a sieve.
2. Then make the soup thick with semolina or other flour and stir in one teaspoon of Liebig's Meat Extract (one calculates one teaspoon of Meat Extract for 3 to 4 bowls) to give it a wholly excellent strong flavor.
3. Finally, finely chopped chervil is added and cooked through for 10 to 15 minutes.
