## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter melk
- 40 g suiker
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 1 ei
- 1/2 stokje vanille or vanillesuiker
- 250 tot 400 g aardbeien
- + 40 g suiker

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and calyces from the strawberries, wash them and let them drain as dry as possible in a sieve.
2. Set aside several strawberries, sprinkle them with some suiker.
3. Place the other fruits at the bottom of the custard dish and sprinkle them with suiker (more or less depending on the sweetness of the strawberries).
4. Prepare the vanilla custard as described in recipe 657.
5. Remove the vanilla, let it cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin from forming.
6. Pour the custard over the strawberries.
7. Garnish the custard with the reserved fruits.
