## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dl water
- 10 g cornstarch
- approximately 1/2 dl vinegar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 dl salad oil
- approximately 1 1/2 teaspoon mustard or mustard powder
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch gradually with the water to form a thin paste without lumps.
2. Let it simmer for 3 to 4 minutes while stirring vigorously with a whisk.
3. Remove from heat and add vinegar, mustard, pepper and salt.
4. Stir occasionally while cooling to prevent a film from forming.
5. Add the egg yolks and while beating vigorously, add the oil drop by drop.
