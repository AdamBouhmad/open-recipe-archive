## Ingredients

- 3 chocolate cakes
- 3 cups water
- some cinnamon
- bread sugar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook 3 chocolate cakes with 3 cups water, some cinnamon and bread sugar
2. Beat in enough eggs so that it is well bound
3. Pour quickly over
