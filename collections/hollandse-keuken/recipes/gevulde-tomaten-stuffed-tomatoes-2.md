## Ingredients

- 2 tomatoes
- 4 asparagus
- 50 g cauliflower florets
- 4 carrots
- salad dressing from recipe 952
- some mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Wash the tomatoes, remove the crown, cut off the cap, rub the fruit pulp and cap through a horsehair sieve, mix this with the grated asparagus and carrots, which are first peeled or scraped, and the grated florets of the washed cauliflower. Add the salad dressing (with a small amount of lemon juice) through it. Fill the hollowed out tomatoes with the mixture and garnish the tomatoes with some mayonnaise (recipe 954).
