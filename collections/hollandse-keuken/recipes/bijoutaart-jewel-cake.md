## Ingredients

- 120 g flour
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g sugar
- 5 eggs
- jam
- 2.5 dl milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 25 to 30 g sugar
- 25 g flour
- 1/2 vanilla stick
- chopped almonds
- icing
- candied fruits

## Directions

1. Prepare the cake like the German sand cake (see recipe 863).
2. Cut it after cooling into different layers, which are alternately covered with jam and pastry cream, prepared in the usual way (see recipe 816).
3. Spread the side of the cake with jam and roll it through the chopped almonds lightly browned in the oven.
4. Garnish the top with icing, almonds and candied fruits.
