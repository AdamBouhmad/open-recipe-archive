## Ingredients

- 2½ dl cream
- 2½ dl milk
- 5 eggs
- ¼ vanilla stick
- 125 g sugar

## Directions

1. Slowly bring the milk with the cream and the vanilla to a boil.
2. Put 100 g sugar in a fairly large pan and make caramel from it.
3. Then slowly pour the boiling milk with cream into it, all at once but away from the heat.
4. Beat the egg yolks with 25 g sugar, carefully add, while stirring constantly, enough of the hot liquid so that the egg yolks are well warmed.
5. Pour them back into the pan and let the custard bind while stirring vigorously with a whisk over the heat, but ensure that it does not boil, as otherwise the risk of curdling becomes greater.
6. Let it cool and mix with the stiffly beaten egg white just before serving.
7. Serve with biscuits.
