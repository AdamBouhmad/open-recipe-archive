## Ingredients

- approximately 1 kg molsla
- 2.5 dl milk
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Sort through the molsla, wash it until the water runs completely clear and place it with the adhering water.
2. Let it cook until tender (1/2 hour) and drain in a colander.
3. Add the salt and stew it for 10 minutes in the sauce prepared in the same way as cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328).
