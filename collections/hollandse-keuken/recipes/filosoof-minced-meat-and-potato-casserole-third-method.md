## Ingredients

- Meat, sliced
- Potatoes, sliced
- Onions, fried
- Gravy with water
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Layer the meat sliced, the potato slices and the fried onions layer by layer in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water.
2. Make sure the last layer is potatoes.
3. Moisten everything with the gravy with water and finish as indicated in the first method.
