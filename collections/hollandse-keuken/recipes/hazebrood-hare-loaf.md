## Ingredients

- meat from a cooked hare
- veal
- bread
- calf liver
- bacon strips

## Directions

1. Take the meat from a cooked hare, mix it with some veal and chop very finely
2. Make a dough with bread, add calf liver
3. Place it in a mold that is lined with bacon strips on the bottom and around the sides
4. Cook in water so the mold sits one inch above the water and water does not boil into it
5. After it has cooked somewhat, place a lid with fire on the casserole
6. When it has roasted for one hour, serve it
7. Can be served with a wine sauce like hare pepper
