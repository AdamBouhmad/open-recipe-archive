## Ingredients

- chicken
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- parsley
- truffels

## Directions

1. Roast the chicken on the spit.
2. Stuff it with breadcrumbs, butter, parsley and finely chopped truffels.
3. Cover the chicken on the spit with paper smeared with butter.
