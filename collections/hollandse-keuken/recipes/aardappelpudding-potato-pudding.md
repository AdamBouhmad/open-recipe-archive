## Ingredients

- 1 Ned. pond cooked potatoes
- 2.5 ounces melted butter
- 5 eggs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- snippets
- sukade

## Directions

1. Mash 1 pound of cooked potatoes finely
2. Add 2.5 ounces melted butter, 5 eggs, sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, snippets and sukade
3. Mix well together
4. Put it in a mold and let it cook
5. Serve with a sauce of flour, butter, sugar, cinnamon and cream
