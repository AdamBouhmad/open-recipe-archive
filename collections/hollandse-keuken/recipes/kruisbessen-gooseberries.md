## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. After properly picking the berries, wash them clean and let them stew done in a pan with some water
2. Press them through a colander
3. Add sugar and cinnamon to taste, as well as beaten eggs to bind
