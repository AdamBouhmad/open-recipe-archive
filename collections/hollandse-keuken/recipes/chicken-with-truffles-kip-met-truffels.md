## Ingredients

- chicken
- bread crumbs
- butter
- parsley
- finely chopped truffles
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Roast the chicken on the spit
2. Stuff it with bread crumbs, butter, parsley and finely chopped truffles
3. Cover the chicken on the spit with a paper smeared with butter
