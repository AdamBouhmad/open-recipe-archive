## Ingredients

- 5 ounces flour
- 5 ounces sugar
- 3 ounces ground almonds
- 3 ounces candied peel
- 1 ounce snippets
- 15 eggs, of which 5 with white
- Cinnamon and mace to taste

## Directions

1. Make a dough
2. Form into small breads
3. Bake
