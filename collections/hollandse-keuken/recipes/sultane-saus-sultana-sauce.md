## Ingredients

- 1 can bouillon
- 1 glass white wine
- 2 slices lemon without peel
- 2 cloves
- a little shallots
- 1 bay leaf
- parsley
- finely chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Mix bouillon with a glass of white wine, two slices of lemon without peel, two cloves, a little shallots, a bay leaf and parsley
2. Let it cook for an hour
3. Strain it through a sieve
4. Add a little cooked and finely chopped parsley, salt, pepper and a chopped egg yolk
