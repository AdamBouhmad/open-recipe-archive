## Ingredients

- cooked beetroot
- onions
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Slice cooked beetroot and onions
2. Stew with a little water, vinegar, pepper and a small piece of butter
3. After stewing one hour, pour the liquid into a small pan
4. Stir in a little flour and cook a bound sauce from it
5. Pour the sauce over the dish
6. If using raw onions, place them at the bottom of the pan and stew for two hours
