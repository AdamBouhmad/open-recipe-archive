## Ingredients

- ½ l milk
- 30 g cornstarch
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch with a little of the cold milk until a thin smooth paste.
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil.
3. Pour this paste into the milk while stirring vigorously, taking care the milk is not yet boiling, and let the sauce cook for 3 minutes while stirring.
4. Add salt and stir in the butter or margarine.
5. Serve this sauce over cauliflower or use it for stewing various vegetables.
