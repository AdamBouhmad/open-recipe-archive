## Ingredients

- 500 g treacle
- 100 g butter or margarine (possibly more)

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine and the treacle together in a pan over a gentle heat until well combined
2. Take care that the butter or margarine does not melt, which would make the sauce too thin
