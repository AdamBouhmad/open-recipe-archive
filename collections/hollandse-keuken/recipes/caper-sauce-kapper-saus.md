## Ingredients

- Six eggs
- One spoon flour
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Capers

## Directions

1. Let six eggs, one spoon flour, salt, and vinegar to taste cook, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
2. When it boils well, add a lump of butter and then the capers
3. Stir it thoroughly together
