## Ingredients

- 1 kg sugar pods
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Trim the beans on both sides twice, wash them, break them and set them on with so little water that they are cooked approximately dry by the end of the cooking time.
2. Add salt and let them cook tender (1/2 hour to 3/4 hour, for old beans sometimes longer).
3. Let them drain on a colander if necessary and braise them shortly with the butter or margarine.
4. Grate some nutmeg over the plate.
