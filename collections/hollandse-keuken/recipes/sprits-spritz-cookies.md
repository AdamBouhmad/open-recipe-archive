## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 250 g butter or margarine
- 125 g brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- a pinch of salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the egg, sugar, salt, grated lemon zest, fine cinnamon and gradually the sifted flour with the baking powder.
2. Pipe this mixture with a nozzle (one straight and one fluted side) onto a greased baking sheet in long strips in the shape of an S.
3. Bake the sprits in a hot oven until light brown and done (+ 15 minutes). Remove them immediately from the baking sheet and divide them into pieces.
