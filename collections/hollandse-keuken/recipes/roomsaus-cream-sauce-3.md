## Ingredients

- 4 or 2 1/2 deciliter milk
- 1 or 2 1/2 deciliter cream, together 1/2 liter
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Mix it with pepper and salt to taste and serve with warm macaroni pudding or cooked fish
3. If desired, replace the cream with condensed milk
