## Ingredients

- 2 large or 4 small cooking soles
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a deep cut in the middle of the fish down to the bone, do the same along the fins and remove the fillets with a sharp knife.
2. Wash them, roll them up, tie them shut with a thread or knot them and cook them in enough boiling water with salt until they just are covered (4 to 6 minutes).
3. Remove them with a skimming spoon from the liquid and present them with parsley, butter, shrimp, oyster or mussel sauce, poured over as hot as possible.
4. Note: The fishmonger delivers the sole also filleted upon request.
