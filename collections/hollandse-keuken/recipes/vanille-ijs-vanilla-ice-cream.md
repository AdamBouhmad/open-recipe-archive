## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 liter milk
- 200 to 240 g sugar
- 1 vanilla stick

## Directions

1. Let the vanilla slowly steep in the milk.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar until thick, white and fluffy.
3. Add the milk mixed with the cream to the egg mixture and warm the whole until thick enough to coat the wooden spoon with which it is stirred with a thin layer.
4. Let the mass cool down, stirring occasionally.
5. Remove the vanilla.
6. Fill the ice mold with this mixture and let it freeze as previously indicated.
