## Ingredients

- 1½ kg raspberries
- 1½ kg red currants (cleaned)
- 2¼ kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- cellophane
- elastic bands
- string
- labels

## Directions

1. Pick the raspberries, check carefully that there are no worms in them
2. Wash them briefly in cold water
3. Wash the currants, remove them from the stems
4. Put the fruit in a pan, bring them to a boil with the lid on
