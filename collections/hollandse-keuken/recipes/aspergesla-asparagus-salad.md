## Ingredients

- 1 kg braising asparagus
- optionally 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons salad oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the asparagus as indicated for 'braised asparagus' (recipe 463), but do not cook it too soft. Let it drain in a colander and treat it further as described in recipe 479.
