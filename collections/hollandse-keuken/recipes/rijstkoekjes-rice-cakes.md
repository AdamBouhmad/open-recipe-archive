## Ingredients

- 5 ounces rice
- 2 cans milk
- 7 to 8 rusks
- Ground cinnamon
- 6 finely beaten eggs
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook rice porridge from 5 ounces rice and 2 cans milk
2. Take the thickest part, add 7 to 8 rusks, ground cinnamon, 6 finely beaten eggs and a little sugar
3. Beat everything together
4. Bake small cakes of it in the pancake pan
