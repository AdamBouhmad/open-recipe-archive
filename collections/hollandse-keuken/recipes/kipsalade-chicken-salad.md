## Ingredients

- leftover roasted chickens (two or three)
- oil
- vinegar
- head lettuce
- hard-cooked eggs (4)
- anchovies
- capers
- tarragon
- pimpernel

## Directions

1. Take the leftovers of two or three roasted chickens and cut them into small pieces.
2. Place them in a pan and pour oil and vinegar over them, as for salad.
3. Arrange them on a platter and around the edge place head lettuce cut into quarters.
4. On each quarter place a hard-cooked egg, also divided into quarters.
5. Fill the spaces between with anchovies, capers and chopped tarragon and pimpernel.
6. Before serving, dip the lettuce quarters in a mixture of oil and vinegar, then pour some more of this mixture over the platter.
