## Ingredients

- 3 kg gooseberries
- 2.25 kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- twine
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- labels

## Directions

1. Clean the gooseberries, wash them, put them in a pan with as much water as necessary to prevent burning during the first moments.
2. Let them cook down.
3. Add the sugar, bring them to a boil with the lid on and let the jam cook without the lid, stirring occasionally, until it reaches the required thickness.
4. Treat it further entirely as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
