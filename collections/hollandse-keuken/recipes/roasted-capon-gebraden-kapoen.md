## Ingredients

- well-larded capon
- one ounce butter
- salt
- three large cups water

## Directions

1. Take a well-larded capon and roast it with one ounce butter, salt and three large cups water
2. Take care to baste the capon from time to time with the jus
3. The jus served with it can be improved by loosening the browned bits on the pan with some bouillon and cooking them together
