## Ingredients

- 1 large celery root
- 8 cooked potatoes
- 1 small tin of tomato purée
- 2 eggs
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the celery in the way as indicated for 'braised celery' (recipe 452), but ensure it does not become too soft. Let it drain in a colander and mix it with the potatoes, which are cut into slices.
2. Cook the eggs hard, peel them and chop the white and yolk separately into fine pieces.
3. Mix half of the tomato purée with some oil, vinegar, pepper and salt and stir this sauce as lightly as possible through the celery and potatoes.
4. Garnish the salad with the remaining tomato purée, egg white and yolk and some finely chopped parsley.
