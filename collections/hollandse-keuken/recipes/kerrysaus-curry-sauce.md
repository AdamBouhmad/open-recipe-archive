## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 40 to 50 g butter or margarine
- 2 teaspoons curry
- 50 g onion (1 medium-sized)

## Directions

1. Fry flour, chopped onion and curry in the butter or margarine, add the liquid by small amounts, stirring constantly, and let the sauce cook, stirring, (10 minutes)
2. Pour it through a sieve into the sauce bowl and serve with cooked chicken, stewed cold meat, calf poulette, hard-boiled eggs, meat timbale, etc.
