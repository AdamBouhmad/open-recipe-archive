## Ingredients

- 3/4 l milk
- 35 g Blooker's cocoa
- 40 g Duryea maizena
- 60 to 80 g sugar
- 1/4 stick vanilla (optional)
- 1 egg white or small meringues (optional)

## Directions

1. Bring 6 1/2 dl milk (with the vanilla) slowly to the boil. Stir the cocoa with the maizena and sugar together, mix them with the remaining milk to a thin paste without lumps. Pour this, stirring well, into the nearly boiling milk and let the custard, stirring, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes. Let the custard cool, stirring occasionally, remove the vanilla and pour the custard into a custard dish. If desired, garnish the custard with the stiffly beaten egg white, through which some sugar has been stirred, or with small meringues.
