## Ingredients

- 8 veal cutlets of 50 to 75 g each
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- 10 g salt
- Lemon juice
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Wash or scrape the veal cutlets and rub them with salt.
2. Beat the egg and mix it with 2 tablespoons water or alternatively 1½ tablespoon water and some lemon juice.
3. Dip the veal cutlets in the egg mixture, making sure it covers all sides well.
4. Roll them in fine breadcrumbs and fry them in butter or margarine that has been heated until hot, carefully until light brown and cooked through (10 minutes).
5. Move them back and forth while frying, but ensure the crust does not come loose anywhere.
6. Finish the jus with a little water.
