## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg stewing asparagus
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean the asparagus as described in the previous recipe
2. Break them into pieces of approximately 6 cm length
3. Wash them and cook them in abundant boiling water with a little salt until done (1/2 hour to 3/4 hour)
4. When the asparagus is almost done, make sauce in the ordinary manner
5. Stew the drained asparagus in it (15 minutes) and grate some nutmeg over the dish
