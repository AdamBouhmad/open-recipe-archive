## Ingredients

- 175 g Alkmaar barley
- 9 dl water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the barley until the water that runs off is completely clear.
2. Soak it for 24 hours in 5 times as much cold water as barley.
3. Bring it with the same water to a boil on a brisk fire.
4. Add some salt and let the barley cook gently without stirring until done and dry (approximately 1 1/2 to 2 hours).
