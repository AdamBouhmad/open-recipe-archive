## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- approximately 3 dl lukewarm milk
- 10 g yeast
- 100 g currants, raisins and mixed peel (optional)
- 125 g butter, margarine or 100 g lard
- salt
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with enough lukewarm milk so it does not fly about and stir out the lumps from this batter
2. Add the remaining lukewarm milk, the beaten egg and the mixed yeast, and optionally the well-washed currants and raisins with stems removed and the chopped mixed peel
3. Let the batter rise, covered, in a warm place for three quarters of an hour to 1 hour
4. Add the salt and quickly bake puffed pancakes with the lard, butter or margarine in the puffed pancake pan
5. Sprinkle them warm with ground sugar and place a knob of butter or margarine in the middle of each plate of puffed pancakes
6. Note: In original fair puffed pancakes, no currants etc. are added
