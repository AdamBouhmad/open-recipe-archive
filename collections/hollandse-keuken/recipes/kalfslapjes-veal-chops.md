## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg veal chops
- at least 75 g veal fat, butter or margarine
- 1 lemon
- 7 1/2 g salt

## Directions

1. FIRST METHOD. Wash or scrape the chops and rub them in with the salt.
2. Make the fat, butter or margarine hot in a frying pan and fry the chops brown on both sides in it. (With a small number the chops can be fried directly in the stewing pan.)
3. Place them in a stewing pan, pour the finished gravy over it, add some lemon juice and a few slices of lemon without pits and let the chops stew gently for another half hour to three quarters of an hour.
4. SECOND METHOD. Wash the chops, rub them in with the salt.
5. Roll them through some beaten egg with water, then through breadcrumbs.
6. Fry them in the hot fat, butter or margarine brown and done on both sides (± 20 minutes).
7. Slide the chops carefully back and forth while frying.
8. Remove them from the fat and finish the gravy with some water.
