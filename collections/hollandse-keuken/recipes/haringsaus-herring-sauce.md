## Ingredients

- Herring
- Sweet milk
- Onion
- Flour
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Water
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean a herring and soak it in sweet milk
2. Remove the bones and chop it finely with an onion
3. Fry some flour brown in butter
4. Add bouillon, water and pepper
5. Add the herring
6. Finally squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
7. Let it cook through briefly
