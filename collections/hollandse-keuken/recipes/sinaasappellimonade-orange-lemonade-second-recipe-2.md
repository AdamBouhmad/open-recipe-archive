## Ingredients

- 1 kg sugar
- 15 g citric acid
- 6 dl water
- juice of 6 oranges
- grated peel of 2 oranges
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 2 mandarins (optional)

## Directions

1. Brush 2 oranges clean thoroughly, grate off the peel and add this with the sugar to the water.
2. Let this mixture boil for 5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and dissolve the citric acid in it and let the liquid cool down.
4. Then add the pressed and sieved fruit juice.
5. Pour the lemonade into boiled bottles, close them with boiled corks and provide them with a label with name and date.
6. Do not use artificially colored fruit for these recipes.
