## Ingredients

- 1 small cucumber
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- finely chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumber and cut off the bitter ends.
2. Using a sambal grater, make thin strips of it.
3. Quickly mix them with some pepper, salt, oil and vinegar and place them on the plate.
4. If desired, sprinkle some parsley over it.
