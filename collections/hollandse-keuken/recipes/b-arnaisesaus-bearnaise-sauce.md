## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 140 g butter or margarine
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1/4 dl tarragon vinegar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Crush the peppercorns, add them to the vinegar and let it boil until 1/2 dl remains, then pour through a horsehair sieve
2. Beat the egg yolks with salt until thick and foamy, add the vinegar and allow the sauce to thicken over a bain-marie, stirring constantly
3. Add butter or margarine in small pieces at a time to the sauce and keep beating
4. Continue until all the butter is used and the sauce is of good consistency
5. Serve with cooked calf's or beef tongue, turbot, sole fillets
