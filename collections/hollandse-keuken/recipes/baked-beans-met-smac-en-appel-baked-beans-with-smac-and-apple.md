## Ingredients

- 400 g white or brown beans cooked in the usual way and drained
- approximately 2 onions
- 1 tin of tomato purée or less to taste
- 50 g margarine
- 2 cloves
- a pinch of thyme
- approximately 4 dl bouillon made from 2 cubes
- 1 tin smac
- 2 star apples
- 1 large sour apple

## Directions

1. Fry the chopped onion and margarine, add tomato purée and spices, the sour apple cut into chunks and after about 5 minutes the bouillon.
2. Make a fragrant sauce from all these ingredients.
3. Add the beans and let them stew for at least 1/2 hour.
4. Then put them in a deep ovenproof dish.
5. Divide the smac into two pieces.
6. Wash the apples, do not peel them but divide them into narrow wedges with the core removed.
7. Cut both pieces of smac into slices that remain connected at the bottom.
8. Insert a piece of red apple between every 2 slices.
9. Lay these 'hedgehogs' on top of the beans and let everything heat through thoroughly in the oven.
