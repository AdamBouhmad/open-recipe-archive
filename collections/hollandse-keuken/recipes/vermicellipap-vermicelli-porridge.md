## Ingredients

- 1.25 l milk
- 200 g vermicelli
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil.
2. Sprinkle in the well-rinsed and broken vermicelli while stirring, let the milk come to a boil again while stirring and then let the porridge become done and bound on a gentle fire, stirring occasionally (approximately 3 quarters of an hour).
3. To prevent the milk from curdling when cooking vermicelli, macaroni, spaghetti, or similar pastas, cook these items for some minutes in ample water first.
4. Let them drain in a colander and rinse them with some cold water.
5. Then put them in the boiling milk and stir them to prevent sticking to the bottom.
6. In this case, use 1/4 l less milk.
