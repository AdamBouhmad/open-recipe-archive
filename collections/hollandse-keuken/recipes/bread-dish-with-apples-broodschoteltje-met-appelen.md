## Ingredients

- 200 g old bread without crust
- 1/2 kg tart apples
- 80 to 100 g sugar
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 2 teaspoons fine cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook applesauce in the usual manner (see recipe 531), but ensure it is not too dry and press it through a horsehair sieve.
2. Mix it with the sugar.
3. Cut the old bread, after removing the crust from all sides, into thin slices.
4. Spread these on both sides with some butter or margarine and layer them alternately with the applesauce in a fireproof dish.
5. Ensure that the top layer consists of bread and that the slices fit well against each other.
6. Sprinkle the fine cinnamon mixed with some sugar over it and let the dish brown in a moderately warm oven (approximately 20 minutes).
