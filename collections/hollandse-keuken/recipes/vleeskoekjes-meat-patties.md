## Ingredients

- 200 g meat leftovers
- 100 g stale bread
- 1 egg
- 1 small onion or leek
- Some finely chopped parsley
- Maggi's Aroma to taste
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 30 g fat from gravy, butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Mince or grind the meat finely, soak the bread in some water or milk, squeeze it out between two spoons, mix it with the beaten egg, the meat, the finely chopped onion or leek, parsley, spices as well as a few drops of Maggi's aroma.
2. Form 8 flat patties and fry these in the frying pan with some remaining fat from the gravy on both sides until brown.
3. Alternatively, the patties can be rolled in beaten egg mixed with water and then in breadcrumbs before frying.
