## Ingredients

- a nice pork rib
- coarse salt
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Take a nice pork rib and roast it entirely on a spit
2. Bring it to the table with coarse salt sprinkled on it and make a white mustard sauce, placing part of it on the dish
