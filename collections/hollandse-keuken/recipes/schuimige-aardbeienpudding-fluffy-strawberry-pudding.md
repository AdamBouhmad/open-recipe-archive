## Ingredients

- 500 g strawberries
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 100 to 125 g brown sugar
- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 17 1/2 g gelatine with 1/2 sheet red gelatine, or 12 1/2 g powdered gelatine

## Directions

1. Pick the strawberries, wash them, select the finest ones to place whole in the pudding later and set them aside sprinkled with some sugar for a time.
2. Press the remaining strawberries through a horsehair sieve.
3. Soak the sheet gelatine 10 to 15 minutes in cold water.
4. Dissolve it with the sugar in a little boiling water (see instructions page 267, 5°).
5. Let this solution cool.
6. Stir it into the strawberry purée.
7. Add the sieved lemon juice.
8. Fold the mixture through the stiffly beaten cream and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
9. Mix with the reserved strawberries.
10. Stir the mass from time to time.
11. When the mixture becomes jelly-like, pour it into a pudding mold brushed with unbeaten egg white.
12. Let the pudding become cold and firm, turn it out onto a damp plate and serve without sauce, but garnish the pudding if desired with Rhine wine jelly and some strawberries.
