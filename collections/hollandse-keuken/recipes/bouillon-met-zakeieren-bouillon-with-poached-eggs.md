## Ingredients

- bouillon made from 500 g beef shank meat
- 4 fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Make bouillon in the usual way, see recipe 35.
2. Strain it through a flannel or double-folded linen cloth so that all fibers are removed.
3. Prepare the poached eggs as indicated in recipe 402.
4. Place one egg in each bowl of bouillon.
