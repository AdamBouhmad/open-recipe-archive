## Ingredients

- 1 jug strong beer
- 3 or 4 eggs
- a little flour
- sugar to taste
- roasted bread or hardtack
- some ground cloves

## Directions

1. Beat 3 or 4 eggs with a little flour together
2. Stir that into the strong beer until everything is well cooked through
3. Add sugar to taste
4. Pour the mixture over roasted bread or hardtack
5. Sprinkle with some ground cloves
