## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters milk
- 100 g pearl sago
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the washed sago for several hours in cold water.
2. Bring the milk and salt to a boil.
3. Sprinkle the soaked pearl sago in stirring, keep stirring until the milk boils again and let the pudding cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until done (approximately 1 1/2 hours).
