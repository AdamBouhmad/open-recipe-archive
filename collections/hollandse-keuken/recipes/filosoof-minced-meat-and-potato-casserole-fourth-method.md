## Ingredients

- Meat, minced
- Onions, fried
- Gravy with water
- Potatoes, mashed
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a ball from the minced meat with the fried onions and some gravy with water.
2. Place it in the middle of a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water.
3. Lay mashed potatoes, prepared in the usual way, around it and finish the dish as indicated in the first method.
