## Ingredients

- 1/2 l bouillon
- mace
- thyme
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 bunch of mushrooms
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Let the herbs steep in the bouillon, then strain it and prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Remove from heat and blend with the beaten egg yolk, the mushrooms, either whole or cut in half, some of the mushroom liquid and lemon juice to taste
3. Serve with calf's tongue, ox tongue, cooked calf's meat, etc.
