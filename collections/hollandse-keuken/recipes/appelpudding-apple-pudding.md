## Ingredients

- puree of the best sour apples, well firmly cooked
- crumb of stale white bread
- melted butter
- eggs
- currants
- sugar
- cinnamon
- grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take puree of the best sour apples, well firmly cooked
2. Add crumb of stale white bread, melted butter, eggs, currants, sugar, cinnamon and grated lemon peel
3. Mix well together
4. Cook it in a mold until done
