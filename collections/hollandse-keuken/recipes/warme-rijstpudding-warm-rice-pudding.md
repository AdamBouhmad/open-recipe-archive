## Ingredients

- 0.5 l milk
- 100 g rice
- 0.5 vanilla pod or vanilla sugar or the zest of a lemon
- 80 g sugar
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 50 g almonds

## Directions

1. Cook stiff rice porridge in the usual way, but let vanilla or lemon zest steep in it
2. Mix this then, after removing the flavoring, with the butter or margarine, the egg yolks, the sugar and the chopped almonds, fold in the stiffly beaten egg white as lightly as possible
3. Grease a warm pudding mold with some butter or margarine (also the lid), sprinkle some fine breadcrumbs in it and fill it 3/4 with the mixture
4. Close it firmly and cook the pudding 2 hours au bain-marie, but ensure the water is always refilled to just below the rim of the mold
5. Let the pudding dry out a little and serve with fruit or foam sauce
