## Ingredients

- 20 kg garden beans
- 500 g salt
- bottles with wide necks
- corks
- sealing wax

## Directions

1. Pod the garden beans, wash them and layer them with the salt in bottles with wide necks that have been boiled out for at least twenty minutes.
2. Pour cold boiled water over them until the beans are just covered.
3. Let the bottles stand for a few days to ferment.
4. Then seal them with clean, boiled corks and dip these twice in sealing wax.
