## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 240 g sifted sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, quickly mix it with the sifted sugar and some vanilla.
2. Put it in a piping bag with a smooth nozzle and pipe oblong strips of 8 cm length at some distance from each other onto a greased and flour-dusted baking sheet.
3. Sprinkle a little sugar over it and let the meringues in an open very lukewarm oven slowly dry and harden (1 1/2 to 2 hours), but see to it that they remain light in color.
