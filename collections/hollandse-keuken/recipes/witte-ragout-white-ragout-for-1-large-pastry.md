## Ingredients

- 1/2 l bouillon
- piece of mace
- thyme
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 45 g flour
- 45 g butter or margarine
- 50 g ground veal
- 1/2 tin of mushrooms
- approximately 450 g pieces of veal tongue and sweetbread
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 dl cream

## Directions

1. Draw bouillon in the usual way with the spices.
2. Sieve it and add it stirring to the butter or margarine, which has been melted and then brought to a boil together with the flour and from which the lumps have been rubbed out.
3. Let the sauce simmer stirring until cooked (10 minutes).
4. Warm in it the cooked meat balls, pieces of veal tongue and pieces of sweetbread and pour the ragout stirring to the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the cream.
5. Add the mushrooms and if desired some of the liquid.
6. Fill the pastries, fried items etc. with it.
