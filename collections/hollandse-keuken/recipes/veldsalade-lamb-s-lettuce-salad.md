## Ingredients

- lamb's lettuce
- cooked beet, finely cut
- onions
- oil
- vinegar or warm sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the lettuce well.
2. Eat it with cooked beet cut finely, onions, oil and vinegar or warm sauce.
