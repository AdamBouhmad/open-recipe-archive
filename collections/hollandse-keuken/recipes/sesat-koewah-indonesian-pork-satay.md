## Ingredients

- 500 g pork cut into cubes
- 3 tablespoons ground onions
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon laos
- 1 Spanish pepper
- 1 teaspoon trassi
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon assem water
- 2 teaspoons fine turmeric
- 1 cup milk or thick coconut milk
- 1 daon djeroek poeroet
- Butter, salad oil, beef fat or frying fat

## Directions

1. Pound the spices, without daon djeroek poeroet, with the trassi and the finely ground onions; mix them with the meat
2. Fry it in one of the aforementioned fats
3. Mix it with assem water and thick coconut milk or milk with some salt
4. Add daon djeroek poeroet and let the meat stew until cooked
5. Ensure that the sauce is thickened
6. Serve the meat with the jus in a dish
