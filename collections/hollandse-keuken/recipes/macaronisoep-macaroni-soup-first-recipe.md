## Ingredients

- 1 l beef bouillon or 1 l water and 4 Maggi bouillon cubes
- 40 g macaroni
- 50 g ground veal
- 25 g Parmesan or aged Gouda cheese (optional)
- 1 egg (optional)
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Cook the broken and washed macaroni in the bouillon, which is prepared in the usual way and sieved, until tender (approximately 3/4 hour), or cook it in water and later dissolve the bouillon cubes in that water
2. Make the meatballs from the ground veal in the usual way (see recipe 37)
3. Let the meatballs cook together with the soup for the last 10 minutes
4. Stir in the grated cheese just before serving if desired, or present it separately
5. Beat the egg in the soup tureen with the cream and carefully add the soup to it while stirring well
