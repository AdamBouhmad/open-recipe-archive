## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g powdered sugar
- 100 g flour
- 1/2 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- a pinch of salt
- 2 egg whites (+ 1/2 dl)

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the unbeaten egg white, the sifted powdered sugar, some scraped vanilla and lastly the sifted flour.
2. Put the dough in a piping bag with a smooth nozzle and pipe long strips onto a greased baking sheet, ensuring they are spaced apart.
3. Bake them in a moderately warm oven very quickly until light brown and done, remove them from the sheet with a thin, sharp knife and let them cool.
