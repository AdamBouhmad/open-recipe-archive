## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter or calf kidney fat
- 100 g flour
- salt
- water (approximately 1/2 dl)
- 40 g old Gouda cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry (feuilletée) in the usual manner (see recipe 846), but mix it with the grated cheese
2. Roll it out the last time to a thickness of 1/4 cm, cut strips from it 10 cm long and 2 cm wide
3. Brush these with beaten egg yolk with water, sprinkle them with a little salt and bake them in a hot oven until light brown and cooked through (10 to 15 minutes)
4. Serve them with ragout
