## Ingredients

- lettuce head
- salt
- water
- veal mince
- jus or butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook whole with a little salt
2. Let drain
3. In each head insert a small ball of cooked veal mince prepared in the frying pan
4. Stew with a little jus or butter and nutmeg
5. When serving, pour jus or melted butter over it
