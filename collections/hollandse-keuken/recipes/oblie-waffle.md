## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces wheat flour
- 2.5 ounces sugar
- 3 loods finely ground cinnamon
- 4 eggs
- 2 ounces melted butter
- A wine glass of brandy
- A little aniseed
- Warm sweet milk

## Directions

1. Beat together with warm sweet milk the wheat flour, sugar, cinnamon, eggs, melted butter, brandy, and aniseed
2. Put this batter in a waffle iron and bake it
3. When coming out of the iron, immediately roll them around a stick and when cold, take it out
