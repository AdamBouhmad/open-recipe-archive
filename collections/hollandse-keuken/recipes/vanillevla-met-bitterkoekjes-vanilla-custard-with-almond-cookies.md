## Ingredients

- 8 bitterkoekjes (+ 100 g)
- 1/2 liter melk
- 40 g suiker
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 1 ei
- 1/2 stokje vanille or 1 zakje vanillesuiker
- wat jam of marmelade

## Directions

1. Stir maizena, suiker, ei and some milk until a smooth paste.
2. Bring the milk with vanilla slowly to a boil.
3. When cooled slightly, soak the bitterkoekjes in it, ensuring they do not crumble.
4. Remove them with a skimming spoon, place them in a custard dish and coat them with some jam or marmelade.
5. Refill the milk to 1/2 liter.
6. Add hot milk, stirring vigorously, to the mixed maizena until the egg is warmed.
7. Pour everything back into the pan and let the custard cook on a not too high heat, stirring vigorously with a whisk, for 3-4 minutes.
8. Let it cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin from forming.
9. Remove the vanilla and pour the custard over the bitterkoekjes.
