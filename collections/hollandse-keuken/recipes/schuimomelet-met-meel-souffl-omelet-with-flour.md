## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 20 g flour
- 20 g sugar
- 25 g butter or margarine
- grated lemon zest or vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar and flavoring until a thick, white and airy mixture is obtained.
2. Mix with the sifted flour.
3. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible.
4. Add the remaining mass and fold in with a few quick strokes.
5. Ensure that in the meantime the butter or margarine has melted in the skillet, but especially not browned.
6. Pour the mixture in immediately, slide the pan back and forth on gentle heat during cooking and allow the omelet to become light brown on the bottom.
7. Place the pan in the oven until the omelet has dried out and is cooked (3 to 4 minutes) or cover the pan with a lid.
8. Slide it half onto a warmed dish and fold the other half over.
9. Optionally sprinkle with sifted powdered sugar and serve the omelet immediately.
