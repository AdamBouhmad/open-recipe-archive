## Ingredients

- 150 g meat leftovers
- 1 1/2 dl milk, bouillon, or jus with water
- 20 g flour
- 20 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- 1/2 egg or one egg white for breading
- 2 sheets gelatin or 4 g powdered gelatin
- bread crumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Make the meat fine.
2. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102.
3. Mix it with the meat and spices and let the mixture, spread on a board, become cold and almost stiff.
4. Take on a spoon approximately one eighth of this dough, make it as smooth as possible with another spoon, roll it through fine bread crumbs, form it into a croquette, but ensure there are no cracks in it.
5. Roll this through the egg that has been beaten with water (for one egg count 2 spoons water, for one egg white one spoon water), but ensure it reaches the croquette everywhere.
6. Roll it once more through bread crumbs and fry 3 to 4 croquettes at a time in hot frying fat, brown and crispy.
7. Ensure especially that the croquettes are completely under the fat.
8. Remove them with a skimming spoon and let them drain on gray paper for a moment.
9. Stack them pyramidically on a small dish on which a cloth napkin lies and garnish them with sprigs of fried parsley.
