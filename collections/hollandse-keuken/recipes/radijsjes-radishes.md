## Ingredients

- small radishes
- fine salt

## Directions

1. Use small, beautiful radishes
2. Cut off the roots and most of the leaves, but leave a few small leaves in the middle
3. Wash them thoroughly until all sand is removed
4. Let them drain in a colander
5. Place them on the serving dish
6. Serve with fine salt on the side
