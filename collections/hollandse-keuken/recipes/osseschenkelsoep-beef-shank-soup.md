## Ingredients

- beef shank with bones
- water
- salt
- rice
- vegetables of choice
- ground mace
- beef meat for meatballs

## Directions

1. Have the meat cut from an ox shank and finely chopped
2. Crush the bones well
3. Cook the bones the day before with water and salt for 3 hours, skimming well
4. Strain the broth through a sieve
5. When cool, remove the fat
6. Put the broth on to cook and add rice and as many varieties of vegetables as desired
7. Make meatballs from the chopped meat with a piece of mace inside
8. Add the meatballs when the vegetables are cooking and let it cook for another two hours
