## Ingredients

- 1 pound red currants
- 1 pound sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Cook this with little water, then shake the well-washed fruits into it and let both come to a boil, continuously skimming off the foam
2. Now pour off the juice, let it cook down well once more, then pour it over the berries already placed in bottles, and tie the bottles shut as soon as they are cold
