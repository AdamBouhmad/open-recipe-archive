## Ingredients

- 1 l cauliflower water (optionally with some leftover cauliflower pieces) or 3/4 l cauliflower water and 1/4 l milk
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg (optional)
- 1 dl cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine and then bring it to a boil while stirring with the flour, add the cauliflower water with the milk in small amounts while stirring and let the soup cook until done (approximately 10 minutes).
2. Warm the cauliflower pieces in it and if desired carefully pour the soup into the egg that has been beaten in the soup tureen, optionally with the cream or milk.
3. Serve small cubes of bread fried in butter or margarine with it.
