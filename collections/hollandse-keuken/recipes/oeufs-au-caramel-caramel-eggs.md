## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 to 5 dl milk
- 1 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 80 g brown sugar
- approximately 200 g sugar for coating the cups

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla pulp. Add the cold milk (for small eggs 4 dl, for large eggs 5 dl). Make caramel from the remaining sugar and let it run in 4 cups or molds so that the bottom and sides are covered with a thin layer. Then strain the egg mixture into it and place the cups in a pan with water that is kept near the boil until the egg is set (approximately 3/4 to 1 hour); a knife must then come out dry. Turn out the puddings and serve them warm or cold.
