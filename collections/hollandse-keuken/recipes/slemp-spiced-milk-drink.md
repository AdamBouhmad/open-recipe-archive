## Ingredients

- 2 l milk
- 3/4 g saffron
- 7 g tea
- 8 cloves
- 8 cm cinnamon stick
- piece of mace
- 80 g sugar
- 20 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. Tie the spices in a cheesecloth, put them in the milk and bring slowly to a boil.
2. Let them steep as long as needed until enough flavor comes into the milk (approximately 1/2 hour).
3. Squeeze the cloth gently between two spoons from time to time, add the sugar to the milk and let it simmer for another 3-4 minutes with the mixed maizena.
4. Remove the spices and pour the slemp into the warm milk kettle.
