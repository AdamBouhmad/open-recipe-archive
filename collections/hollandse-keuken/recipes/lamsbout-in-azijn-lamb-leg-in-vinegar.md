## Ingredients

- 1½ kg lamb leg
- 100 g beef fat or margarine
- 10 cloves
- 4 bay leaves
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 1 onion
- some pepper
- 20 g salt
- 1½ dl vinegar

## Directions

1. Rinse the meat, cut away some of the fat if necessary and set it for 24 hours in half vinegar, half water with the salt and spices
2. Turn the meat several times during this period and roast it in the manner indicated for roastbeef
3. Calculate roasting time as 30 minutes per 500 g
4. Finish the gravy with some of the liquid in which the meat has been standing
5. If desired, bind the gravy with some browned flour
