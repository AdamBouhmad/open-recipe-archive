## Ingredients

- medium eel
- salt
- pepper
- crushed biscuit
- lemon
- water

## Directions

1. Take medium eel, cut it into pieces the length of a finger
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and braise them with crushed biscuit, lemon cut into slices and water
