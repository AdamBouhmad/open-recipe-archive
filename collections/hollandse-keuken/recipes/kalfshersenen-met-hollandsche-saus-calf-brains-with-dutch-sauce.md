## Ingredients

- brains from three calf heads
- water
- vinegar
- white or brown sauce
- eggs
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take the brains from three calf heads and clean them very well
2. Remove the thin membrane around them and the fibers within
3. Place them in boiling water with a little vinegar and let them steam, being careful to keep them whole
4. Dish them up and pour a white or brown sauce over them, made with vinegar and eggs, or with bouillon and vinegar
