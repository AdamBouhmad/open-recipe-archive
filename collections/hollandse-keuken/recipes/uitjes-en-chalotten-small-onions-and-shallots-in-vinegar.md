## Ingredients

- small onions
- shallots
- boiling water
- hot vinegar
- salt
- cold wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash in boiling water, drain, peel and cook in hot vinegar with salt, not too hard
2. When cold, place in small glasses and pour over with cold wine vinegar that has been boiled and skimmed beforehand, so that they are completely covered
3. Tie the bottles well
