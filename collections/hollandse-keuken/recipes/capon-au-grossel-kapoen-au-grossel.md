## Ingredients

- capon
- bouillon or water
- coarse salt
- bouillon or jus sauce

## Directions

1. Take a capon, clean it properly
2. Lard it
3. Cook it in bouillon or water in an iron pot
4. When it is done, serve it
5. Lay some pieces of coarse salt on it and pour a sauce of bouillon or jus over it
