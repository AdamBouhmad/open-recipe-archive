## Ingredients

- 2 spoons flour
- 3 spoons sugar
- a piece of butter the size of half an egg
- half a grated fresh lemon peel or some orange blossom
- 4 egg yolks
- a glass of good cream

## Directions

1. Take 2 spoons flour, 3 spoons sugar and a piece of butter the size of half an egg and let this melt
2. Add half a grated fresh lemon peel or some orange blossom and 4 egg yolks
3. Mix everything together, adding a glass of good cream
4. Put this mixture in small pastry mold forms
5. Bring them briefly in the oven and serve them warm
