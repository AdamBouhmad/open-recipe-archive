## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant
- 150 g veal forcemeat
- 20 g old bread without crust
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 truffels
- 1/4 dl Madeira (optional)
- 150 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. For preparation see: truffle-stuffed turkey (recipe 324).
2. Allow 1 to 1 1/2 hours as roasting time.
