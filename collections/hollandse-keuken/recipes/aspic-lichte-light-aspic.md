## Ingredients

- For daubes: 9 dl bouillon of veal, fish or chicken, mace, carrot, parsley, onion, cayenne pepper, 35 g gelatine or 30 g powdered gelatine, 2 egg whites and eggshells, 1 dl vinegar
- For garnishing aspic: 9 dl bouillon, mace, carrot, parsley, onion, cayenne pepper, 55 g gelatine or 50 g powdered gelatine, 2 egg whites and eggshells, 1 dl vinegar

## Directions

1. Make bouillon in the usual way with the spices, strain the bouillon and let it cool.
2. Whip the egg whites briefly with the eggshells together.
3. Add them with the (soaked in cold water) gelatine to the bouillon and bring the mixture to a boil, whisking vigorously with a whisk.
4. Remove from heat and let it stand without stirring until it becomes completely clear.
5. Pour the aspic through a doubled cloth, add vinegar to taste and let it cool.
