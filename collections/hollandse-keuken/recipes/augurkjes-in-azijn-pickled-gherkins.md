## Ingredients

- 2 kg gherkins
- 2 bottles pickling vinegar
- 2 Spanish peppers
- 5 g dried ginger root
- 5 g bay leaves
- 10 g horseradish
- 2 sprigs fennel
- some salad oil

## Directions

1. Clean the gherkins by cutting off the crown and stems and further clean them by brushing or wiping with a damp cloth.
2. Set them aside for one night sprinkled with salt. Let them drain as dry as possible in a colander the next day.
3. Fill well-cleaned bottles or pots with them and arrange the spices between them.
4. Pour vinegar over them so that the gherkins are amply covered and pour a layer of salad oil on top.
5. Close the bottles or pots with damp parchment paper and provide them with a label with name and date.
