## Ingredients

- pheasant claws
- finely chopped parsley
- buttered paper
- lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Cut off the claws from the remaining pheasant legs
2. Prepare these with finely chopped parsley and wrap them in buttered paper
3. Place them on the grill
4. Serve with a lemon sauce
