## Ingredients

- small or thin slice of bread
- Liebig's Meat Extract
- fine salt

## Directions

1. Spread a small or thin slice of bread with Liebig's Meat Extract and sprinkle with fine salt.
2. This is a pleasant and light food that proves to be of excellent service for both suffering and convalescent patients.
