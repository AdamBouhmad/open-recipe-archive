## Ingredients

- 3/4 l milk
- 20 g Duryea maizena
- 50 g sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 75 g almonds

## Directions

1. Mix the maizena with the egg yolks and most of the sugar. Add enough of the cold milk to obtain a fairly thin paste. Bring the remaining milk with the vanilla slowly to the boil, pour in enough of the maizena so that the yolks are well warmed. Return everything to the pan and let the custard, stirring well with an egg beater, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes. Let it cool, stirring occasionally, and pour it into a custard dish. Remove the vanilla. Soak the almonds for some time in boiling water, peel them and cut them lengthwise in half. Beat the egg white stiff, stir in some sugar and garnish the custard with it. Insert the almonds into it.
