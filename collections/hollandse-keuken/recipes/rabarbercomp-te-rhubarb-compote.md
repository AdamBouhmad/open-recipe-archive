## Ingredients

- 300 g red rhubarb stalks
- 2 dl water
- 150 g sugar
- some sago
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the rhubarb into equal pieces; cook them gently (approximately 8 minutes) in the water that has been brought to a boil with the lemon juice and sugar, but make sure the pieces remain whole and do not boil to pieces.
2. Scoop each piece that is done with a flat spoon into a compote dish.
3. Thicken the liquid with a little mixed sago and pour it over the pieces.
