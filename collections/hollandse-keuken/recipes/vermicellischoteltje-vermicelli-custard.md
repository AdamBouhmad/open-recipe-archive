## Ingredients

- 1.25 l milk
- 200 g vermicelli
- 100 g sugar
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 1/2 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook vermicelli porridge in the usual way (see previous recipe), but let the vanilla or lemon peel cook in it.
2. When the porridge is done, mix it with half of the sugar and remove the flavoring.
3. Beat the egg yolks with the remaining sugar, carefully add the hot vermicelli porridge and lastly mix it as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Finish the dish as indicated in the semolina custard recipe.
