## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 4 dl curry sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 339.
2. Pour it through a sieve onto a warmed small dish and place the hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters on it.
3. Serve this dish at coffee time or with cooked rice at lunch.
