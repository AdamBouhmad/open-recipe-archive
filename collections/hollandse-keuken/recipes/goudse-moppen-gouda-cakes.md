## Ingredients

- 500 g flour
- 350 g sugar
- 300 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients into a firm ball
2. Press or roll it on a greased sheet to approximately 1/2 cm thickness
3. Make diamond-shaped figures in the top with a pancake knife
4. Sprinkle the top with some sugar
5. Cut the cake into pieces approximately 5 cm long and 1/2 cm wide
6. Place the pieces on a greased baking sheet at some distance from each other
7. Bake in the middle of a moderately warm oven until lightly brown and done (20-30 minutes)
8. Remove them with a pancake knife from the sheet and let them cool
