## Ingredients

- asparagus
- sorrel
- butter
- nutmeg
- egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Break off the tips of the asparagus and remove all the woody parts
2. Wash well
3. Cook with the tips
4. Let drain well
5. Stew with melted sorrel, butter and nutmeg
6. If desired, bind the liquid with egg yolks
