## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg freshly cooked potatoes
- 1-2 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 leeks or onions
- 3/4 dl salad oil
- 1/2 dl vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- mustard
- very finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Make the hard-boiled eggs fine, mix them with the oil, vinegar, pepper, salt, mustard, very finely chopped leeks or onions and parsley.
2. Carefully mix the potatoes sliced into this sauce, let the salad marinate for at least an hour and serve with fried fish, cold meat, etc.
