## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 60 g bastard sugar
- 2 dl Rhine wine or berry wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon or orange
- zest of 1/4 lemon or orange

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and sugar vigorously together.
2. Add the wine, fruit juice and zest and stir the sauce on a gentle fire or in a bain-marie until it is somewhat bound.
3. Remove the citrus zest, beat the egg white stiff and add the hot sauce to the egg white at the last moment.
4. Serve with warm bread pudding, almond cake pudding etc.
