## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 partridges
- liver
- bacon
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- pastry crust

## Directions

1. Take 3 or 4 partridges that have been well cleaned
2. Cut off the legs and wings
3. Make a farce of liver, both from partridges and from other birds, with some bacon, parsley, salt and pepper, all well mixed together
4. Fill the partridges with it and take a crust (about 3 pounds)
5. Make an opening in the lid and let them bake about 3 hours in an oven
6. When done, let them cool and close the hole in the lid
