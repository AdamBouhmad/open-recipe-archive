## Ingredients

- 200 g ground coconut
- 200 g white sugar
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 25 g flour
- 1 large egg
- vanilla sugar
- milk

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients together with enough milk to form a moist but cohesive dough.
2. Line a baking sheet with greased buttered parchment paper.
3. Spread the dough on it and bake it until very light brown (about 25 minutes).
4. After some cooling, turn the cake and remove the paper.
5. Cut the cake into pieces.
