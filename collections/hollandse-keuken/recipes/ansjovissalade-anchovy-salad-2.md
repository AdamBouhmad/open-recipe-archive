## Ingredients

- anchovies
- cold water
- milk (if needed)
- hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- lemon slices
- parsley
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the anchovies in cold water, with milk if needed, to remove excess salt.
2. Treat them as herring, but leave half whole.
3. Let them drain and lay the half fish in slanting diamonds over each other.
4. Serve the dish decorated with slices of hard eggs, lemon slices, and parsley.
5. Dress as herring salad.
