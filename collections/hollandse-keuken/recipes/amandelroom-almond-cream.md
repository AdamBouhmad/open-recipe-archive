## Ingredients

- 6 ounces sweet almonds
- 1 can good milk
- 5 ounces sugar
- 2 egg whites
- oil
- paper

## Directions

1. Peel the almonds
2. pound them finely with a little water
3. whip the milk with the egg whites and sugar
4. place the mixture on a gentle fire and let it cook
5. when nearly reduced to three-quarters, add the almonds
6. let them cook together briefly and strain through a sieve
7. let it cool
8. place it in a dish and arrange almonds around the edge that have been rolled lightly in oil and roasted on paper
