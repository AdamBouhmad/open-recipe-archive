## Ingredients

- Half a vanilla stick
- Milk
- One pint cream
- Twelve loods sugar
- Four eggs
- Cold milk
- One tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Cut the vanilla stick into small pieces and slowly cook them out in milk
2. Meanwhile, quickly boil one pint of cream
3. Pour the vanilla milk into the cream, along with twelve loods of sugar and four eggs beaten in a little cold milk with one tablespoon of flour mixed in
4. Everything must cook well so that neither the flour nor the eggs are recognizable
5. Strain the sauce and it is ready
