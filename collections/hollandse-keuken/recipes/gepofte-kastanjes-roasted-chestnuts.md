## Ingredients

- 1 kg chestnuts
- 40 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash the chestnuts, dry them and make a deep cross in them.
2. Brown the butter or margarine in an iron pot, add the chestnuts and roast them on a fairly hot fire, stirring occasionally, until they are cooked, that is until the shells burst open (25 to 30 minutes).
3. Serve them with butter or margarine as a winter evening treat.
4. Note: Instead of in an iron pot, the chestnuts can also be roasted in hot ashes or in the oven.
