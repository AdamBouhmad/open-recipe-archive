## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 bottle Rhine wine
- 1 1/2 dl water
- 20 cloves
- cinnamon stick
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 150 g brown sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Let the spices infuse for 1 hour in the water.
2. Sieve the liquid and let it cool.
3. Whip the yolks with brown sugar and salt until foamy.
4. While whisking, add wine and spice infusion and let the mass thicken au bain-marie.
5. Pour the drink into small cups.
