## Ingredients

- 125 g flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 1 dl milk
- salt
- 5-6 sour apples
- 1/2 dl rum
- 20 g sugar
- 80 g frying fat or 3/4 dl salad oil

## Directions

1. Core the apples, peel them, slice them, and if desired soak them in rum mixed with sugar.
2. Stir the flour with the baking powder, egg yolks, salt, and gradually all the milk through each other until a smooth batter without lumps is obtained.
3. Fold this as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Dip some apple slices in this batter and fry them with the frying fat or salad oil in the frying pan until light brown and cooked through.
5. Sprinkle them with sugar mixed with fine cinnamon and serve as hot as possible.
