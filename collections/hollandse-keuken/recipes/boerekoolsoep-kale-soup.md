## Ingredients

- kale
- butter
- veal or beef bouillon
- salt
- mace
- pepper
- crushed cloves

## Directions

1. Chop kale very finely and fry it with butter until soft
2. Now add it to veal or beef bouillon with salt, mace, pepper and crushed cloves
3. Let it continue cooking until it is done
