## Ingredients

- 1 kg veal fricandeau
- 150 g butter or margarine or veal fat, or half veal fat and half butter or margarine
- 15 g salt
- (a few sheets of gelatine)

## Directions

1. Rinse the meat and salt it
2. Let the butter or margarine become very light brown or the fat become very hot (especially not too hot, as the meat must remain light in colour, which is not easy due to the long roasting)
3. Roast the meat as indicated for roastbeef (see recipe 227), but calculate roasting time as 35 minutes per 500 g (for a large fricandeau or for fricandeau that will be sliced cold: 30 minutes per 500 g)
4. If necessary, lay a butter-greased paper over the meat in the oven if it becomes too dark in colour
5. Finish the gravy with some water
6. Note: large pieces of veal that roast long enough will give a gelatinous gravy. When this is desired from smaller pieces, dissolve a few sheets of gelatine soaked in cold water or some powdered gelatine in the gravy
