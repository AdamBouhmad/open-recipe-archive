## Ingredients

- 1 can half-length pointed asparagus or 2 kg fresh cooked asparagus
- 75 g old grated cheese
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- approximately 2.5 dl asparagus liquid
- 0.5 dl cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 to 4 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Let the asparagus drain well in a colander and carefully collect the liquid
2. Make a very thick sauce from this liquid with butter or margarine and flour in the ordinary manner
3. Add the cream and egg yolks and approximately 50 g old cheese
4. Peel the hard-boiled eggs and cut them into slices
5. Lay a layer of asparagus in the bottom of the heatproof dish, then the eggs, then a layer of sauce, and so on, but ensure that the last layer consists of sauce
6. Sprinkle a little salt over the canned asparagus each time, sprinkle the reserved cheese over the dish and let a brown crust form on it in the oven
