## Ingredients

- herring
- onions, finely cut
- cooked beets
- hard-boiled eggs, finely made
- sour apples
- parsley
- cold potatoes, finely grated (optional)
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, hold it by the tail and split it in two after opening it on the back.
2. Remove the backbone and cut it into long strips.
3. The dish can be made decorative and arranged by placing the following ingredients in compartments: finely cut onions, cooked beets, hard-boiled eggs finely made, sour apples, and parsley, and if desired, cold finely grated potatoes.
4. Serve the dish after garnishing the edge with the white and yolks of eggs and gherkins cut into slices.
5. Dress with oil and vinegar.
