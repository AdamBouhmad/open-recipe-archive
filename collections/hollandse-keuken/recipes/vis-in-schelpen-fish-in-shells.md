## Ingredients

- 250 g fish remains (without bones or skin)
- 2½ dl milk
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine or 18 g Duryea cornstarch
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- fine parsley
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Chop the fish, after removing the bones and skin, finely.
2. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102.
3. Mix it with the fish and the seasonings.
4. Fill the dampened shells with this mixture to about 3/8 full.
5. Finish them as indicated in recipe 102.
