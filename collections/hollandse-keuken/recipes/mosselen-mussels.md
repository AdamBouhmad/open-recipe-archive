## Ingredients

- 5 kg mussels (750 g mussel meat)
- water
- shallots
- thyme
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the mussels well clean, check them to ensure there are no mud mussels or gaping mussels (i.e. dead specimens).
2. Set them on with the adhering water and let them cook, shaking occasionally, until all shells have opened (-- 10 minutes).
3. Serve the mussels with oil and vinegar or with mustard or egg sauce.
