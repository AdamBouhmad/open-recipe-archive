## Ingredients

- egg white from 1 egg
- 1 small cut orange flower
- grated loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white until completely foamy.
2. Mix with the small cut orange flower and grated loaf sugar to make a thick paste.
3. Form into small balls by hand and bake in a tart pan with little heat.
4. Good to serve with tea.
