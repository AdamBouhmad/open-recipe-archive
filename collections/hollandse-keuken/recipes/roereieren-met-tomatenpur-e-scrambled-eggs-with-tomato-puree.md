## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 3 tablespoons tomato puree
- 30 g butter or margarine
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper and salt
2. Add the tomato puree and milk
3. Prepare as described in recipe 395
4. Serve with lettuce or spinach
