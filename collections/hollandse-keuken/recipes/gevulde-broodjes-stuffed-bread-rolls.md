## Ingredients

- 6 small bread rolls
- 150 g cold meat
- 1½ dl milk, broth or jus with water
- 15 g flour
- 15 g butter or margarine or 7 g Duryea cornstarch
- 15 g butter or margarine
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 40 g butter or margarine to brush

## Directions

1. Cut the top cap off the bread rolls, hollow them out and brush the inside with some melted butter or margarine.
2. Chop the meat and the washed parsley finely and stir this with the seasonings through the sauce, which is made like that for 'meat in shells' (recipe 102).
3. Fill the bread rolls with this mixture and place the hollowed-out and brushed lids on them.
4. Brush the entire outside with melted butter or margarine and let the bread rolls become warm and crispy on a baking sheet or in a roasting pan in a moderately warm oven, but make sure they do not become too brown.
5. Note: One can also use shrimp instead of meat. Preparation and quantity remain the same in that case.
