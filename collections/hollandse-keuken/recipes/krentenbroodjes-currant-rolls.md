## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 125 g currants
- 125 g raisins
- 50 g candied peel
- 15 g yeast
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 2.5 dl milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 7.5 g salt
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make bread dough in the known manner (see recipe 835) from flour, yeast, salt, milk, melted butter or margarine and egg yolk.
2. Let the dough rise.
3. Wash and dry the currants and raisins and chop the candied peel.
4. Mix them through the dough.
5. Form small balls from this.
6. Place them on a thinly greased baking sheet.
7. Let them rise for about 30 minutes and bake them in a hot oven in about 20 minutes until done and light brown.
8. Brush them with a thin paste of milk and sugar to make them shiny.
9. Later dust them with sifted powdered sugar.
