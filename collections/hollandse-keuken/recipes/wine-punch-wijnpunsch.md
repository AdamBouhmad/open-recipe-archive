## Ingredients

- Seven and a half ounces sugar
- One beer glass lemon juice
- Two cans red wine
- Two cans red wine
- Half a can arak

## Directions

1. Cook seven and a half ounces sugar, one beer glass lemon juice, and two cans of red wine, well skimmed
2. When it has slightly cooled, add two more cans of wine and half a can of arak
3. Stir everything well
