## Ingredients

- Peaches or apricots
- Cloves
- Best cinnamon
- Pieces of bread sugar
- French brandy

## Directions

1. Remove the stems
2. Put them in bottles
3. Add cloves and best cinnamon, along with pieces of bread sugar
4. Pour French brandy over them and seal the bottles as airtight as possible
5. Use only unblemished fruit
