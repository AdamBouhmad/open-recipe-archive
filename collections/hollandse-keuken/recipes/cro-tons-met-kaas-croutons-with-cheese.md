## Ingredients

- 50 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- 40 to 50 g grated sharp cheese
- Cayenne pepper
- 4 slices of casino bread

## Directions

1. Remove the crusts from the slices of bread and cut them in two.
2. Toast them evenly brown and let them cool.
3. Cream the butter or margarine, add the egg yolk, the grated cheese and pepper to taste and spread this mixture on the bread, so that it lies higher in the middle than at the sides (so-called bombéed).
