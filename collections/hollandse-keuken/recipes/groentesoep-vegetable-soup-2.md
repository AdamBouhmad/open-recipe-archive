## Ingredients

- 1 liter beef bouillon (recipe 35) or 4 Maggi's bouillon cubes with 1 liter water
- 35 g rice or vermicelli
- vegetables in season (approximately 300 g), for example in summer: peas, carrots, string beans, cauliflower, etc.
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the bouillon in the usual manner and strain it. Add the washed rice or vermicelli and the finely cut and washed vegetables and let everything cook together gently until the vegetables and rice or vermicelli are tender (approximately 1/2 hour).
2. If desired, thicken the soup with a little sago and stir in finely chopped parsley away from the heat.
