## Ingredients

- For the ring: 50 g butter or margarine
- 50 g flour
- 2 dl milk
- 150 g old Dutch or Parmesan cheese
- 2 eggs
- pepper and salt
- For the ragout: 35 g butter or margarine
- 30 g flour
- 2 dl bouillon
- 1 dl cream or milk
- 1 tin of mushrooms
- 50 g cheese
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Grease a rice ring mold thickly with butter or margarine, coat it. Make a thick sauce from butter or margarine, flour and milk and away from the heat mix it with 150 g cheese, pepper and salt to taste, the egg yolks and last the stiffly beaten egg white.
2. Fill the ring 3/4 with it, close it with a lid and let it cook 30 to 35 minutes in a bain-marie. Let it dry without a lid in front of the open oven door and turn it out onto a warm platter.
3. Prepare mushroom ragout in the usual way, stir 50 g cheese through it, fill the ring with it or pour the ragout over it.
