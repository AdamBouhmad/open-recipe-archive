## Ingredients

- 2 bunches leeks, or approximately 2 kg
- 2.5 dl leek cooking water
- 0.5 dl vinegar
- 30 g flour or 15-20 g Duryea corn starch
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the leeks by cutting off the outer leaves and the roots and a piece of the stalk.
2. Cut only the white and yellow parts into pieces of 4-5 cm length.
3. Rinse them several times in plenty of water and cook them in boiling water with salt (approximately 1/2 hour).
4. Let them drain in a colander, catching the liquid.
5. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328) or corn starch sauce (see recipe 329), but use the leek cooking water and vinegar instead of milk.
6. Braise the vegetables in this sauce for approximately 15 minutes, if desired add them to the egg beaten in the bowl.
7. If desired, prepare the leeks according to the second preparation method mentioned above for onions.
