## Ingredients

- 1 Dutch pound wheat flour
- Warm sweet milk
- 4 eggs
- A little fine salt
- Dry yeast
- Sugar
- Warm milk
- Peeled pear slices
- Butter

## Directions

1. Heat the sweet milk and mix gently with the wheat flour, eggs, and fine salt
2. Beat it well together and make it into a pancake batter, fairly thick, with warm milk
3. Mix dry yeast with a little sugar and warm milk, and stir it through the batter
4. Let it rise properly
5. Cut peeled pears into slices and remove the cores with an apple corer
6. Put butter in the pan and bake the slices until tender, and lay them on a plate
7. Put batter in the pan and arrange pear slices in it as close together as possible
8. Bake the pancakes, sparing no butter
