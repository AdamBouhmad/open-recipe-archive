## Ingredients

- Cream
- Sugar
- Eggs
- Rose water

## Directions

1. Take 10 measures of cream, add sugar and 6 eggs, of which 3 with the white
2. Cook together in a new pan
3. Once cooked, add a splash of rose water
4. It is eaten cold
