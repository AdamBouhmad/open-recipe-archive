## Ingredients

- 300 g meat leftovers
- 500 g cooked rice
- 40 g fat from gravy, margarine or butter
- 75 g onion
- 1 full teaspoon curry
- Some lean gravy with water or some bouillon, or 1/2 Maggi's Gravy tablet
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Mince or grind the meat finely, fry the chopped onion and the curry in the fat light brown.
2. Stir this through the cooked cold rice and the minced meat, moisten everything with the bouillon or the gravy with water.
3. Add pepper, salt and nutmeg as needed.
4. Place the mixture in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, place a few pieces of butter or margarine on it and let a brown crust form in the oven.
5. Alternatively, layer the mixture layer by layer with tomato slices in the dish, making sure the top layer is tomato.
