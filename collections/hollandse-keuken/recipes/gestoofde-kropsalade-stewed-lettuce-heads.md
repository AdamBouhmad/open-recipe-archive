## Ingredients

- lettuce heads
- bouillon
- butter
- mace
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Rinse the heads in soft water and let them drain thoroughly on a colander
2. Put them in a pan with bouillon, butter and ground mace
3. Let them stew for a quarter hour
4. When serving, take the heads out and place them on a plate
5. Pour the remaining liquid, mixed with some egg yolks, over them
