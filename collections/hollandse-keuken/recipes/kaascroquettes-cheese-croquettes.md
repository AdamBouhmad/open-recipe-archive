## Ingredients

- 200 g grated aged cheese
- 2 dl milk
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Warm the butter or margarine with the flour, stirring, until a smooth mixture, gradually add the milk and cream and let the sauce cook through, stirring (approximately 10 minutes).
2. Mix it with the grated cheese and season with pepper and salt to taste.
3. Let the mixture cool spread out, shape croquettes in the usual manner (see recipe 116), bread them double and fry them brown in hot frying fat.
4. Let them drain on gray paper and place them on a plate covered with a folded cloth.
