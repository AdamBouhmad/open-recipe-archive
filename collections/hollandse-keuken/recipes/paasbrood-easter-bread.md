## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 20 g yeast
- 7.5 g salt
- 2 dl lukewarm milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 60 g butter or margarine
- 150 g currants
- 150 g raisins
- 30 g candied peel
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Make a supple dough from flour, yeast, salt, egg, milk, melted butter or margarine and grated lemon peel.
2. Let the dough rise covered on a lukewarm spot for 1 hour.
3. Then mix in the cleaned and dried currants and raisins with the finely cut candied peel.
4. Let the dough rise covered on a lukewarm spot for 1 hour in a butter-greased bread pan in which a butter-greased piece of butter paper has been placed.
5. Handle the bread further as indicated for white bread (recipe 835).
