## Ingredients

- two and a half ounces rice
- one and a half ounces bread sugar
- one can sweet milk
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. In a new pan, cook the rice, bread sugar and sweet milk under constant and strong stirring until it is thick
2. Grate lemon zest through it
