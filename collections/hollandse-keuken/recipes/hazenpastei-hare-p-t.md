## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- 500 g veal
- 500 g fresh bacon
- 1/2 bottle or tin of truffles (optional)
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- some vinegar
- some lard
- 4 cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- 10 white peppercorns
- minced onion

## Directions

1. Remove all bones from the hare, cut the meat into pieces, wash it and place it for 24 hours in half water, half vinegar with the spices.
2. Dry the nice pieces from back and thigh, cut them into thin slices and lard these with very thin strips of bacon.
3. Grind the washed meat and bacon twice through the meat grinder, do the same with the remaining pieces of hare and mix this mass with pepper, salt and nutmeg to taste.
4. Choose a pâté pot that can be completely filled with this quantity.
5. Cover the bottom and side walls with thin slices of bacon, arrange first a layer of the slices, place a layer of meat mixture on top and continue until everything is used up.
6. Pour the marinade mixed with some bouillon over it until the mass is covered.
7. Close the pot with the lid and brush the edge with a paste of flour and water.
8. Place the pâté in a fairly warm oven and let it stand there for approximately 3 hours.
9. Loosen the lid, pour a layer of melted lard over it, close the pot again and seal the edge of lid and pot with foil.
10. Store the pâté in a cool place.
