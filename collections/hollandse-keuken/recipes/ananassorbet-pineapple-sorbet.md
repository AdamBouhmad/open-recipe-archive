## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple
- 5 to 6 apricots
- 100 g sugar
- a little lemon juice
- a little carmine
- 1 dl maraschino or Champagne

## Directions

1. Take 3 to 4 pieces of pineapple from the can, remove the pits and cut them into very small cubes.
2. Rub the remaining pineapple with the apricots through a hair sieve and mix this purée with the lemon juice, the liquid in which the sugar is dissolved and as much carmine until a nice red color is obtained.
3. Let the mass freeze in the usual manner.
4. Add some maraschino or Champagne, let the mixture freeze a little longer and present it as soon as it has become stiff, after having stirred the pineapple pieces through it.
