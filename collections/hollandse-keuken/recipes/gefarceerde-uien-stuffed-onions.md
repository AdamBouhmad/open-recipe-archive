## Ingredients

- large onions
- ground veal or pork
- bouillon
- butter
- salt
- mace
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel the onions thickly and hollow them out
2. Fill them with balls of ground veal or pork
3. Cook them until done in bouillon with butter, salt and mace
4. Make a sauce from a portion of the cooking bouillon, binding it with a couple of egg yolks
5. Pour this sauce over the onions when serving
