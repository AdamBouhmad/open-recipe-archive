## Ingredients

- 1 l light veal bouillon or 1 l water and 4 Maggi bouillon cubes
- 1/4 Savoy or white cabbage
- 1/2 tablespoon curry
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Melt the butter or margarine in a pan, add the cabbage cut into thin strips and the curry and let the cabbage braise in the butter or margarine while stirring occasionally (+ 3/4 hour), but make sure it remains light in color
2. Add the flour and gradually add the bouillon or water with the bouillon cubes
3. Let the soup cook while stirring until done (10 minutes)
