## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- 200 g flour
- 200 g sugar
- 4 eggs
- the scraped pulp of one vanilla pod, or the grated zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add alternately some sugar and one whole egg and stir until the mass has become thick, white and airy.
2. Add vanilla or lemon zest and sifted flour, but stir this in with a few strokes.
3. Grease a cake pan with butter or margarine, put the batter in it and bake the cake in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done (3/4 to 1 hour).
4. Check if the cake is done by inserting a knitting needle, which should then come out dry.
5. Turn the cake onto a cake rack, let it cool and dust it with powdered sugar or garnish it with icing after cooling.
