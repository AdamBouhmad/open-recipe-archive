## Ingredients

- 1 kg Brussels lof
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scrape the lof with a knife, remove the outer leaves if necessary and a small piece of the stalk, but make sure the sprout stays whole.
2. Wash it and let it cook in as little boiling water as possible with salt in a well-enameled pan (approximately 3/4 hour).
3. Let it drain in a colander and braise it for 10 minutes in a sauce made of butter or margarine, flour and milk, prepared in the manner of cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328).
4. Grate nutmeg over it on the plate.
