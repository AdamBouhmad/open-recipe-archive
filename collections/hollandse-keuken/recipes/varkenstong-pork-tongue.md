## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- onions, finely cut
- vinegar
- red wine
- salt
- pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue until the skin can be easily removed, cut it into slices, and place them in a pan with finely cut onions, a splash of vinegar and red wine, salt, pepper and sugar to taste, and let it braise well for about half an hour
