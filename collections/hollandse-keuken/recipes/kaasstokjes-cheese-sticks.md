## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter
- 100 g flour
- 5 g salt
- water
- egg yolk
- 50 g Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry (feuilletée) in the usual manner (see recipe 846), but mix it with almost all of the grated cheese
2. Roll it out the last time to a thickness of approximately 1/2 cm, cut strips from it 10 cm long and 2 cm wide
3. Brush these with beaten egg yolk with water, sprinkle them with the reserved grated cheese
4. Bake them in a hot oven until light brown and cooked through in approximately 15 minutes
