## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg tart apples (or half sweet, half tart)
- 1 1/2 kg potatoes
- 400 g lean smoked bacon
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the mash as described in the previous recipe, but cook the washed bacon 1/2 hour in advance.
