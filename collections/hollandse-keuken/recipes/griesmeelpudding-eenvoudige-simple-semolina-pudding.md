## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 90 g semolina
- 75 g sugar
- ¼ stick vanilla or ½ lemon peel
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook semolina porridge in the usual manner, but allow the flavoring to steep in it.
2. Remove the vanilla or lemon peel, add the sugar and pour the mixture into a mold rinsed with cold water.
3. Let the pudding cool and become firm.
4. Turn it out onto a moist plate and serve it with a fruit juice sauce or stewed fruit.
