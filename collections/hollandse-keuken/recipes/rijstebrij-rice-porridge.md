## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liter milk
- 150 g rice (for thin porridge) or 225 g (for medium) or 300 g (for thick)
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil on a brisk fire.
2. Stirring, sprinkle in the well-washed rice.
3. Continue stirring until the milk boils again.
4. Let the rice porridge cook on a very gentle fire without stirring until done and thickened (approximately 1 1/2 hours).
5. Stir the rice porridge only when it is done and taken from the fire.
