## Ingredients

- fresh sour cherries
- water
- lemon peels
- hardtack
- 3-4 cloves
- sugar
- red wine
- salt
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take fresh sour cherries and crush them in a mortar with the pits broken
2. Put everything in a pot with water, some lemon peels, hardtack and 3-4 cloves
3. Cook it until the flesh of the cherries has merged with the mass
4. Now strain it through a sieve
5. Bring it to a boil again and serve it with some sugar and red wine
6. After first stirring through some salt and cinnamon
