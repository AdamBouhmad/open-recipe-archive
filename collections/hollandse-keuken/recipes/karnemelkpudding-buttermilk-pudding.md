## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter buttermilk or yogurt
- juice of 2 lemons
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- 200 g brown sugar
- 22 g leaf gelatin with a few sheets of red gelatin or 18 g powdered gelatin and a few drops of carmine

## Directions

1. Soak the leaf gelatin 10-15 minutes in cold water, dissolve it with as little boiling water as possible
2. Mix the buttermilk in a bowl with the cooled gelatin, lemon juice, grated lemon peel and sugar
3. Let everything become jelly-like, stirring occasionally
4. Pour the mass into a stone mold rinsed with cold water and let the pudding become cold and stiff
5. Turn it out onto a damp dish and serve with vanilla sauce
6. Note: If powdered gelatin is used, it need not be soaked, only dissolved, and color the pudding with carmine. Do not prepare buttermilk pudding the day before in warm weather, as there is a great chance of fermentation
