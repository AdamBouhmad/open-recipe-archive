## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg pork chops
- 75 g lard, butter or margarine
- some pepper
- 10 g salt

## Directions

1. Wash or scrape the chops, rub them in with pepper and salt and fry them in the hot fat, butter or margarine brown on both sides.
2. Finish the gravy with some water and stew the chops in it for another twenty minutes to half an hour.
3. Check whether the gravy has not become too fatty after stewing and finish it again if that is the case.
