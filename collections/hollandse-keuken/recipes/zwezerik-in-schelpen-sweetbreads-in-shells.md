## Ingredients

- 1 sweetbread
- 1/2 can mushrooms
- 2 dl broth
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 1/2 dl cream or milk
- 1 egg yolk
- pepper and salt
- pieces of mace
- piece Spanish pepper
- pinch thyme
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbread for half an hour in cold water, then place it in fresh cold water, drain this off as it boils and let the sweetbread simmer in plenty of boiling water with salt and the seasonings until done (20 to 30 min.)
2. Pour cold water over it, peel it and cut it into small pieces.
3. Chop the mushrooms finely.
4. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102, but in this case use the strained sweetbread broth.
5. Mix it with the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the cream or milk, the sweetbread and the mushrooms.
6. Stir in pepper and salt to taste.
7. Fill the dampened shells with this mixture for 3/4.
8. Finish them as described in recipe 102.
