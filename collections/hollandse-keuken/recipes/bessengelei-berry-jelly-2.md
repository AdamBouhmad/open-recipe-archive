## Ingredients

- Berries, washed
- Sugar, 1 kg per liter of berry juice

## Directions

1. Wash the berries and place them with very little liquid on a gentle fire and let the berry juice extract.
2. Squeeze or press out the berries.
3. Measure the juice and use 1 kg sugar per liter.
4. Cook no more than 1 liter of juice at a time in a large pan.
5. As the juice boils, sprinkle the sugar in very slowly while stirring so that the sugar dissolves quickly and the liquid barely stops boiling.
6. Let the jelly boil for 2 minutes and pour it, still thin and liquid, into well-cleaned jam jars.
7. Treat further as other jelly.
