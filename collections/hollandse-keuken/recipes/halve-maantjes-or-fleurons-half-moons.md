## Ingredients

- 50 g hard butter or margarine
- 50 g flour
- some water
- some salt
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846), roll it out the last time to a thickness of 1/4 cm.
2. Cut out half moons with a cookie cutter or glass and brush with egg yolk that has been beaten with water.
3. Lay them on a baking sheet and bake them in a hot oven, placed in the lower half, light brown and cooked through (approximately 10 minutes).
4. Use them with ragout or as garnish for dishes.
