## Ingredients

- 2 beets
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 0.5 dl vinegar
- 1 onion
- 3 cloves
- sago (15 g sugar optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the beets well in lukewarm water, let them cook in plenty of boiling water until the skin can be easily removed (in winter 3-4 hours, in summer 2-2.5 hours or less).
2. Let them drain in a colander, remove the skin and cut them into thin slices or cubes or grate them on a cucumber grater.
3. Peel the onion, wash and mince it.
4. Put the minced onion in the braising pan with a spoonful of water.
5. Add the butter or margarine, vinegar, cloves and beet and braise for another 20-30 minutes.
6. Before serving, remove the cloves and thicken the liquid with some mixed sago.
7. If the beets are not sweet enough, add the sugar.
