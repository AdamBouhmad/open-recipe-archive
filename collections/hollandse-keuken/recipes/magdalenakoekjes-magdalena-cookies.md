## Ingredients

- 125 g butter
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 125 g sugar
- 150 g flour
- 50 g snipped almonds or chopped sucade

## Directions

1. Cream the butter until fluffy, add the egg and stir it through until it has completely mixed with the butter.
2. Add the egg yolk and the sugar and stir until the sugar is completely finely ground.
3. Mix the sifted flour with it and spread this batter as thinly as possible on a baking sheet greased with butter.
4. Make the top as smooth as possible and sprinkle it with the peeled and snipped almonds or chopped sucade.
5. Bake the cake in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done, cut it into pieces of 6 by 3 cm while still warm and remove these immediately from the baking sheet.
