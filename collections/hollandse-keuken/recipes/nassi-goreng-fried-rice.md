## Ingredients

- 750 g cooked rice
- 150 g ham
- 150 g meat scraps
- 3 tablespoons ground onions
- several garlic cloves
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fried onions
- some gravy
- 1 to 2 teaspoons sambal oelek
- salt
- 3 eggs
- 3 spoons milk
- salt
- pepper
- butter, margarine or oil

## Directions

1. Fry the ground onions and the finely chopped garlic cloves light brown in butter, margarine or oil
2. Add the sambal oelek and some salt
3. Let this fry for about 5 minutes
4. Add the pieces of meat and the sliced ham
5. Let them brown (about 5 minutes)
6. Stir in the dry rice and keep stirring so the grains do not become hard (10 minutes)
7. Add some gravy if needed
8. Fry a thin omelet in one of the above fats in the known manner (see recipe 383)
9. Slice this into thin strips
10. Garnish the dish with these strips laid crosswise over each other and the separately fried onions
