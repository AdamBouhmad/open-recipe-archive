## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 1/2 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- 70 g Blooker's cocoa
- 150 g sugar
- 1 stick agar-agar (of 14 g) or 2 small ones of 7 g each

## Directions

1. Rinse the agar-agar.
2. Pour enough boiling water over it so that it just covers and let it cook until it is completely dissolved (10 to 15 minutes).
3. In the meantime, prepare chocolate milk in the usual way from the quantities indicated above.
4. Pour the dissolved agar-agar through a sieve into it and place the mixture in a stone mold rinsed with cold water.
5. Let the pudding become cold and firm, turn it out onto a wet plate and serve with vanilla sauce.
