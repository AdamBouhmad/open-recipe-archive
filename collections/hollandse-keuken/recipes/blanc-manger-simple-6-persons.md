## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 liter milk
- 20 g white gelatin or 17 1/2 g powdered gelatin
- 80 g sugar
- 1 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Slowly bring the milk with the cream and the vanilla stick, which especially must not be split, to a boil
2. Add the sugar
3. Soak the gelatin for 15 minutes in plenty of cold water, then dissolve it, after wringing out well, in the liquid taken off the fire
4. Remove the vanilla stick
5. Stir from time to time until the mass begins to become jelly-like
6. Pour it into a mold rinsed with cold water and let it become cold and stiff
7. Turn the pudding out onto a damp dish
8. Note: This pudding does not need a sauce, vanilla wafers and strips are served with it. However, a fragrant raspberry sauce tastes quite good with this cream pudding
