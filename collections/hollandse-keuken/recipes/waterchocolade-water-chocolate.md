## Ingredients

- 1 and one-half lood chocolate per cup water
- water
- egg yolk
- sugar

## Directions

1. For each cup of water take one and one-half lood chocolate, make it fine and let it stew with almost a cup of water on gentle heat, stirring strongly; when the chocolate is done, stir an egg yolk into it, with sugar to taste
