## Ingredients

- 75 g spinach
- 4 carrots
- 1 onion
- 1 tomato
- ½ tablespoon parsley
- 2 tablespoons salad oil
- a bit of lemon juice
- 1 tomato

## Directions

1. Clean the vegetables. Chop the spinach, grate the carrots and the cleaned onion. Cut the tomato into slices. For the salad dressing, dip the tomato in hot water, peel it, press it out. Mix the oil and lemon juice through it. Stir the vegetable mixture lightly through the sauce. Garnish the dish with a single tomato slice and sprinkle with the chopped parsley.
