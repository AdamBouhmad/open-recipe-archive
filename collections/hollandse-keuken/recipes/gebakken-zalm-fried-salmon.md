## Ingredients

- thickly cut pieces of salmon
- salt
- pepper
- crushed biscuit

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into reasonably thick pieces
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and let sit three hours
3. Roll them in crushed biscuit and fry them slowly in a frying pan
