## Ingredients

- 100 g shrimp
- approximately 2 tablespoons mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Prepare the shrimp as indicated in the Garnalen recipe
2. Mix them with some sharp-flavored mayonnaise
3. Place on the serving dish
