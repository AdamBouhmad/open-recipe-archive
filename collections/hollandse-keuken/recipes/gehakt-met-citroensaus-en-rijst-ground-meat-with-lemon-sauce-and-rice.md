## Ingredients

- 200 g ground calf meat
- 200 g ground pork meat
- 50 g old bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 50 g onion
- 1 egg white (optional)
- 30 g butter
- 30 g flour
- 3 dl bouillon
- 1 lemon
- 1 egg yolk (optional)
- 150 g rice

## Directions

1. Mix the ground meat with soaked bread, spices, finely chopped onion and egg white.
2. Form 4 oblong balls from this mixture and roll them in flour.
3. Make the butter light brown in a pan, add the flour and stir until the flour is light brown.
4. Add the bouillon, let the sauce cook, add the grated lemon zest and braise the meat balls in it until cooked (approximately 1/2 hour).
5. Remove the meat, strain the sauce and finish it with lemon juice and egg yolk.
6. Cook the rice in the usual manner, press it in rinsed eggcups and make a ring of it on a flat meat platter.
7. Place the meat in the middle and pour the sauce over the meat.
