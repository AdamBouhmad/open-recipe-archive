## Ingredients

- 15 kg string beans
- salt (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the string beans on both sides 2 times, wash them, cut them with a string bean knife or grinder into thin strips.
2. Cook them for several minutes in boiling water.
3. Let them drain.
4. Pack them firmly into the well-cleaned bottles with the designated masher up to 4 cm below the rim.
5. Pour enough of the cooking liquid until the beans are just covered.
6. Add salt if desired.
7. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize them for 1½ hours.
8. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
