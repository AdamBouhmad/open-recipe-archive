## Ingredients

- 1 kg raspberries
- 1.5 kg red currants (cleaned)
- 2.25 kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- twine
- labels

## Directions

1. Pick the raspberries and check carefully that there are no worms in them.
2. Rinse them briefly in cold water.
3. Wash the currants and drain them.
4. Put the fruit in a pan, bring it to a boil with the lid on and let the fruit cook without the lid, stirring occasionally, until reduced.
5. Add the sugar and let the jam cook with it until it reaches the required thickness (see the rules on page 369).
6. Treat it further as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
