## Ingredients

- 25 g dried apricots
- 25 g raisins
- 4 dates
- 25 g dried prunes
- 25 g ground almonds

## Directions

1. Pit the dates and prunes. Wash the fruits well and soak apricots, prunes and raisins. Put the almonds on with cold water and let them come to a boil (stew); when the skin comes off easily, pour the almonds over with cold water, remove the skins. Grind them through the almond mill. Mix the dried fruits together, if necessary with some brown sugar. Lay the halved dates on top and sprinkle the ground almonds over them.
