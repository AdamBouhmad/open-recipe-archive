## Ingredients

- one bottle Rhine or red wine
- one and a half spoon flour
- five egg yolks
- juice of 3 to 4 lemons
- rind of one lemon
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Let one bottle of Rhine or red wine cook gently with one and a half spoon of flour and five well-beaten egg yolks
2. Now add the juice of 3 to 4 lemons, grate the rind of one lemon over it
3. Add sugar and cinnamon to taste
4. While it is on the fire, stir carefully
