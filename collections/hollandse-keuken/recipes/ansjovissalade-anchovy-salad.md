## Ingredients

- Anchovies
- Cold water
- Milk (if needed)
- Hard-boiled egg whites
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Lemon slices
- Parsley
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the anchovies in cold water, or with milk if necessary, to remove excess salt
2. Treat them as you would herring, but leave half of them whole
3. Let them drain and arrange the half-fishes in diagonal diamonds overlapping each other
4. Serve the dish garnished with slices of hard-boiled eggs, lemon slices and parsley
5. Dress as you would herring salad
