## Ingredients

- 10 hard-boiled, peeled, half-sliced eggs
- 2 tablespoons finely ground onions
- 4 sliced garlic cloves
- 2 1/2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 4 tablespoons separately fried onions
- 2 tablespoons Duryea cornstarch
- 1 1/2 dl milk with salt or coconut milk
- Bouillon
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Pound the spices finely with the ground onions
2. Fry them in the butter or margarine; add the bouillon
3. Mix the cornstarch with some coconut milk or milk with salt and stir this through the bouillon
4. Let the mixture come to a boil for 3 minutes
5. Pour the sauce over the warm sliced eggs
6. Garnish the dish with the separately fried onions
