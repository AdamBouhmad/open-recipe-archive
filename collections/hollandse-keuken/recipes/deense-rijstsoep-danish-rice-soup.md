## Ingredients

- 100 g rice
- 1/2 lemon peel
- 1 1/2 liter water
- salt
- 100 g raisins (or half raisins, half currants)
- 2 1/2 dl berry juice
- juice of one lemon
- 125 g sugar

## Directions

1. Set the washed rice on with the water, salt and lemon peel and let it cook gently for half an hour.
2. Add the sugar, berry juice, lemon juice and well-washed raisins.
3. Let the soup cook gently for another quarter hour.
4. Remove the lemon peel.
