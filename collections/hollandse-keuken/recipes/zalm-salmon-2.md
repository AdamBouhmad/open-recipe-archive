## Ingredients

- 2 pieces salmon of 400 g
- water
- 10 g salt per litre of water

## Directions

1. Scrape the scales off the salmon, wash the pieces and cook them in boiling water with not too much salt (to keep the color nice) 2 minutes per 100 grams.
2. Remove the pieces with the fish slice or a couple of skimming spoons from the pan, place them on the fish platter and set it for a moment in front of an open window, to make the color a brighter red.
3. With warm salmon serve: parsley sauce, warm mayonnaise or melted butter, with cold salmon: cold mayonnaise and salad.
4. Note: Shad can be treated as salmon, but can also be cooked in the manner of haddock.
