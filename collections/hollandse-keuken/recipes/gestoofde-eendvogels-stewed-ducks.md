## Ingredients

- ducks
- anchovies
- verjus
- morels
- spices
- lemon peel
- salt
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Cook the ducks half done.
2. Place them in a pan and add anchovies, a glass of verjus, some morels, spices, lemon peel, salt and bouillon.
3. Cover well and let them stew further. In this way all poultry can be stewed.
