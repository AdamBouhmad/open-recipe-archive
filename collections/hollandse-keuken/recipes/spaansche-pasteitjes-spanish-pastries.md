## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- breast of chicken
- bacon
- calf sweetbread
- shallots
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare as with veal pastries
2. Cook the breast of a chicken, a piece of bacon and a calf sweetbread well done
3. Chop it now with some shallots, salt and nutmeg as finely as possible and fill the pastries with it
