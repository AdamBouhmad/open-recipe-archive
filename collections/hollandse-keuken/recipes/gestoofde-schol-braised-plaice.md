## Ingredients

- one plaice
- salt
- butter
- lemon
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cut the plaice into pieces and wash them well
2. Place them on a dish, sprinkle with salt and add a good piece of butter, along with some lemon and lemon peel
3. Let them braise gently until done
