## Ingredients

- 150 g cooked, deboned fish
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Debone and skin cooked fish leftovers while still warm as much as possible.
2. Let them cool and mix them with a sauce of oil, vinegar and salt, and treat the salad as described in recipe 9 or cover them with mayonnaise and garnish them with parsley, radish, cucumbers and tomato.
