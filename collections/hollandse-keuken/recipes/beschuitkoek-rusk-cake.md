## Ingredients

- 4 large rusks
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 l milk
- 40 g sugar
- 1/2 stick vanilla
- 40 g butter or margarine
- rum (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar and vanilla pulp, add the lukewarm milk gradually.
2. Melt some butter or margarine in a large frying pan, lay the rusks in it, pour a large part of the sauce over it and fry the cake on low heat until light brown on the bottom.
3. Add some of the remaining liquid from time to time until all of it has been absorbed into the rusks.
4. Turn it onto a butter-greased lid, let it slide from the lid into the pan and fry the other side light brown.
5. Sprinkle powdered sugar over the cake and if desired pour some rum over it.
