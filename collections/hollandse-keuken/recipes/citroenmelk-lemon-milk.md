## Ingredients

- 2 l milk
- peel of 1 1/2 lemon
- 80 g sugar
- 20 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. Wash the lemon well and peel it thinly so that no white remains on the peel.
2. Put this in the milk and prepare it as indicated for vanilla milk (recipe 900).
