## Ingredients

- snipes
- red wine
- giblets
- breadcrumb
- butter
- crushed cloves
- brown sugar
- wine
- capers

## Directions

1. Gut the snipes and let them stew for a quarter hour in a little red wine
2. Fry the giblets finely and mix them with crumbled bread
3. Braise them brown in butter
4. Add crushed cloves, brown sugar and wine
5. Let everything stew well with capers until done
