## Ingredients

- Fresh butter
- Half a spoon flour
- Milk, cream or bouillon
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- A couple egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix half a spoon flour into fresh butter
2. Add milk, cream or bouillon as preferred, along with nutmeg and salt
3. Finally stir a couple egg yolks through
