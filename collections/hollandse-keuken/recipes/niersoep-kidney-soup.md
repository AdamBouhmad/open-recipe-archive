## Ingredients

- 1 l lean stock with water or 1 l water and 4-5 Maggi's bouillon cubes
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cloves
- 1 piece Spanish pepper
- 1 onion
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 1 calf kidney
- 3/4 tin of mushrooms (optional)
- English sauce and soya to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the kidney, soak it for several hours in cold water and let it cook gently in boiling water (approximately 1/2 hour)
2. Remove it and cut into small pieces
3. Let the herbs and chopped onion infuse in the lean stock with water or bouillon
4. Strain this liquid and add it gradually while stirring to the melted butter or margarine, which has been brought to a boil together with the flour and rid of lumps
5. Let the soup simmer while stirring for 10 minutes
6. Warm the kidney pieces in it
7. Before serving, add English sauce and soya to taste and if desired some mushrooms
