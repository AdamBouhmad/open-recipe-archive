## Ingredients

- 1 l beef bouillon or 1 l water and 4 Maggi's bouillon cubes
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 50 g onion
- 1 teaspoon curry
- 1 egg (optional)
- 2 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- piece of Spanish pepper

## Directions

1. Make bouillon in the usual way with the spices and sieve it. Melt the butter, fry the curry, the chopped onion and the flour in it and gradually add, while stirring, the bouillon or water with dissolved bouillon cubes.
2. Let the soup cook while stirring for 10 minutes and if desired pour it through a sieve into the egg that has been beaten in the soup tureen.
