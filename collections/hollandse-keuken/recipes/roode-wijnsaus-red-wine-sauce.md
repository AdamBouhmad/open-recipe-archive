## Ingredients

- 3 cloves
- Cinnamon
- 2 ounces sugar
- 1 pint red Bordeaux wine
- 2 teaspoons potato flour
- Water

## Directions

1. Put three cloves, cinnamon and 2 ounces sugar in 1 pint red Bordeaux wine
2. Let it cook slowly but well
3. When it boils well, add two teaspoons potato flour stirred in cold water
4. Strain the sauce through
