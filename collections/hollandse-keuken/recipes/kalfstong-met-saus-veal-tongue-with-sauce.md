## Ingredients

- veal tongues
- water
- flour
- bouillon
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Boil the tongues for half an hour and then cook them for three hours until done
2. Make a sauce with flour, bouillon, a splash of vinegar, and a little spices
3. Remove the skin from the tongues
4. Place them on a dish and pour the sauce over them
