## Ingredients

- 6 cans buttermilk
- 1 cup flour
- molasses and butter

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup flour into 6 cans buttermilk
2. Cook while stirring constantly
3. For a stiffer version as midday food, use 1 cup flour per 3 cans buttermilk
4. Serve with molasses and butter
