## Ingredients

- 5 not overly ripe tomatoes
- 4 slices garlic
- 3 green and 3 red finely chopped lomboks
- 1/2 teaspoon laos
- 1 spoon trassi water
- 1 dl coconut milk
- 100 g peeled cooked shrimp
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Slice the cleaned tomatoes into slices
2. Pound the spices with the ground onions
3. Fry them
4. Add the tomatoes with trassi water
5. Cook this briefly and add the coconut milk with the shrimp
6. Heat this together until the fat comes out
