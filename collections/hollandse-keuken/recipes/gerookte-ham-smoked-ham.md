## Ingredients

- smoked raw ham
- cold water

## Directions

1. Weigh the smoked raw ham, brush it well clean, wash it with lukewarm water and soak it for 24 hours in cold water.
2. Place it in boiling water, just enough to cover it and cook it gently (approximately 25 to 30 minutes per 500 g).
3. Test if the ham is done by seeing if the rind comes off easily.
4. Cut it off warm from the part that will be used and keep the rest of the ham under the rind.
5. Serve the ham cold or warm sliced thin, for example with garden beans and brown butter.
6. Note: When cooking only a piece of the ham, hang the remaining part in a linen or brown paper bag in a cool place, where the ham is well protected from flies. The liquid in which ham has been cooked can be used for pea soup, it is usually fatty, more or less salty, but not aromatic.
