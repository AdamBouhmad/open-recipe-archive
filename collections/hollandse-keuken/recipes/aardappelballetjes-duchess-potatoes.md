## Ingredients

- 500 g cooked potatoes
- 2 dl milk
- 100 g flour
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Press the potatoes through a ricer finely or grind them through the mill, mix them with some salt and nutmeg.
2. Bring the milk with the butter or margarine to a boil, stir in the flour while stirring and keep stirring until the dough releases as a ball from the pan.
3. Remove from heat and stir in the eggs one by one and mix the batter with the finely made potatoes.
4. Form small, round balls, place these on a flour-dusted plate to prevent sticking.
5. Fry 10-12 balls at a time in hot frying fat light brown and cooked (2-3 minutes).
6. Let them drain on brown paper and serve with tenderloin, chateaubriands, etc.
7. Sieve the fat after use.
