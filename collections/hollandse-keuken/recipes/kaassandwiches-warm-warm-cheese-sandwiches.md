## Ingredients

- 8 dunne sneetjes casinobrood zonder korst (thin slices of casino bread without crust)
- 8 plakjes ham (ham slices)
- 16 plakjes kaas (cheese slices)
- 40 g boter of margarine (butter or margarine)

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread (preferably casino bread) into equal, thin slices.
2. Cut off the crusts.
3. Place on each slice a piece of cheese that fits exactly.
4. On half of the slices place a matching slice of ham and now place the other slices, with the cheese facing down, on top.
5. Let butter or margarine turn light brown in the pan, carefully place the cheese sandwiches in it and fry them light brown on both sides.
6. Be careful when turning so that the layers do not slip apart; hold them preferably with a fork or knife.
