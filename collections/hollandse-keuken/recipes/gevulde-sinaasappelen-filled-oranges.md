## Ingredients

- juice of 5 oranges
- juice of 2 lemons, together 4 dl, optionally made up with some water
- 75 g sugar
- 15 g red gelatin, optionally 1/2 dl Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Use oranges with attractive skin for this dish, cut them in half crosswise and press them out gently without damaging the skin.
2. Mix the juice with the lemon juice, sugar and gelatin, which has been soaked in cold water for 10 minutes, and warm it until the gelatin and sugar are dissolved.
3. In the meantime, clean the inside of the orange shells, remove the white fibers, wash them inside and out and fill them with the strained liquid.
4. It is sufficient to fill 8 half shells with this gelée.
5. Let them set cold and firm.
6. Optionally garnish the edges with whipped cream mixed with some superfine sugar and place a French fruit in the middle, or cut them in half with a damp warm knife.
7. Note: The amount of sugar depends on the acidity of the fruit. Taste the juice first before adding sugar.
