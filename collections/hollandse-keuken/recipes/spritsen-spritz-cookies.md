## Ingredients

- 6 drams butter
- Water
- 6 drams wheat flour
- 3 eggs with white
- 3 eggs without white
- Boiling butter for frying
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook butter in water
2. Stir in flour well until done
3. When slightly cooled, add 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks
4. Stir until it is a dough
5. Press through a pastry bag into boiling butter that has been well skimmed
6. Fry well until brown
7. When serving warm, sprinkle with sugar
