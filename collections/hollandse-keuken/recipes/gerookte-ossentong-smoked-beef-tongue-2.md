## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Cold water
- Warm water
- Young garden beans
- Brown butter or celery
- Stewed tomatoes
- Potato purée

## Directions

1. Wash and brush the tongue thoroughly clean.
2. Soak it for 24 hours in cold water, changing the water regularly.
3. Wash it again thoroughly with lukewarm water.
4. Cook it in plenty of boiling water over a gentle fire until tender (approximately 3 to 3½ hours).
5. Test if it is done by checking if the skin comes off easily.
6. Remove the skin while the tongue is still warm and remove the fat and small bones from the throat piece.
7. Warm the tongue again in the cooking liquid if necessary and slice it.
8. Serve with young garden beans and brown butter or celery and stewed tomatoes with potato purée.
