## Ingredients

- half pound sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 6 cloves
- water
- 1 bottle red wine

## Directions

1. Cook half a pound sugar, a stick of cinnamon, 6 cloves in half a can of water for a quarter hour, then add a bottle of red wine and now heat well and drink
