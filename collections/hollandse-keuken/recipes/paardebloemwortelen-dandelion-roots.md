## Ingredients

- dandelion roots
- sorrel
- salt
- butter
- nutmeg
- bouillon (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook after removing the buds
2. Stew with a little sorrel, salt, butter and nutmeg
3. For taste, can also add a little bouillon
