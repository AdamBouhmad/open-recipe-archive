## Ingredients

- corn salad
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut off the small stems from the corn salad
2. Make sure to remove the woolly balls if the corn salad is rather old
3. Cook it more or less sour, depending on how bitter you want it
4. Then stew it slowly with butter and nutmeg
