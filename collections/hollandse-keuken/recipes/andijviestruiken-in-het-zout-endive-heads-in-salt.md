## Ingredients

- 10 kg endive
- 1 or 3 kg salt
- Cologne pot
- cloth
- board
- stone

## Directions

1. Clean the endive heads in their entirety, wash them and boil them in plenty of boiling water for 5 minutes.
2. Remove them with a skimming spoon from the pan, let them cool completely spread out.
3. Finish them as indicated in recipe 1017.
