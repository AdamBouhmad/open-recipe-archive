## Ingredients

- ½ l whipping cream
- Approximately 3 tablespoons coffee extract
- Approximately 80 g powdered sugar or brown sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the cream stiff, mix it with the sifted powdered sugar or brown sugar and the coffee extract.
2. Serve this custard with biscuits or fill sweet fritters with it.
