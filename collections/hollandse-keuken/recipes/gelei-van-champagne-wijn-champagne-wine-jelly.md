## Ingredients

- a handful fresh orange blossoms
- two ounces fish glue
- five ounces sugar
- a bottle champagne wine

## Directions

1. Take a handful fresh orange blossoms and use only the petals, let them boil briefly in boiling water
2. Take two ounces fish glue and let it dissolve well
3. Cook it with the mixture and five ounces sugar
4. When it is almost completely stiff, add a bottle champagne wine
5. Pour into a mold
