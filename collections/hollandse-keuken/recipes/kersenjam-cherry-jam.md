## Ingredients

- 3¾ kg cherries (approximately 3 kg without pits)
- 2¼ kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- cellophane
- elastic bands
- string
- labels

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries, wash them, remove the pits
2. Put them in a pan, bring them to a boil with a lid on and let them cook without the lid, stirring occasionally, but be careful to prevent burning
3. Add the sugar and let them cook quickly until the marmalade reaches the required thickness (see the rules on page 369)
4. Treat it further as strawberry jam (recipe 983)
