## Ingredients

- Fresh butter
- Half a spoon meal
- Mace
- Lemon juice
- Bouillon
- 1 ounce butter
- Cooked and peeled crayfish
- A couple egg yolks

## Directions

1. Set some fresh butter with half a spoon meal to fire briefly
2. Add mace, lemon juice and bouillon
3. When it boils, add more than 1 ounce butter and a good portion of cooked and peeled crayfish
4. Stir a couple egg yolks finely into the sauce
