## Ingredients

- string beans
- a little water
- a small piece of butter
- a little salt
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Set the beans with a little water on the heat
2. Add a small piece of butter, a little salt, and a little sugar
3. Shake continuously
4. They require two hours to be ready
