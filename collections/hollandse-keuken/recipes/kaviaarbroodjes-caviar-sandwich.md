## Ingredients

- 3 slices stale casino bread
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 50 g caviar

## Directions

1. Cut very thin slices of bread, remove the crusts, divide them into 2 pieces and toast them light brown
2. Let them cool, spread them fairly thickly and lay a layer of caviar on top
3. If desired, add some very finely scraped onion
