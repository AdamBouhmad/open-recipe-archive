## Ingredients

- 200 g onions (approximately 4 pieces)
- 30 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, wash them, cut them into the thinnest possible slices.
2. Heat the butter or margarine until light brown in the frying pan, add the onions and fry them, turning constantly, until light brown.
3. Remove them immediately from the pan, as they will otherwise become too dark.
