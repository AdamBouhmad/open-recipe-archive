## Ingredients

- brains of one calf
- 2 cloves
- 4 white peppercorns
- 2½ dl broth
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- salt and parsley
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the brains for a couple of hours in cold water, place them in plenty of boiling water with salt, cloves and peppercorns and let them cook until done (25 to 30 min.)
2. Remove the skin.
3. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102, but in this case use the strained broth.
4. Mix it with the brains cut into pieces and the chopped parsley, add salt and lemon juice to taste and fill the dampened shells with this mixture for about 3/4.
5. Finish them as described in recipe 102.
