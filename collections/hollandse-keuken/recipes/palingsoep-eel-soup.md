## Ingredients

- eel pieces
- cloves
- bay leaves
- bouillon
- parsley
- cold butter
- flour
- hardtack
- eggs
- egg yolks
- veal bouillon

## Directions

1. Cook the eel pieces with some cloves and bay leaves well done
2. Remove some pieces from the bone and chop them finely with water and parsley
3. Now cook bouillon in which you place a lump of cold butter and stir in a couple of tablespoons of flour until a thick sauce
4. Mix in the finely chopped eel and let it cool
5. Make small pieces with a spoon, roll them in fine hardtack and form balls
6. Roll them through beaten eggs and then again through crushed hardtask
7. Finally fry them done in butter
8. Now take the necessary veal bouillon, let it cook through with mace
9. Add the eel to it and finish it with egg yolks
10. Pour the soup over the balls, previously placed in the tureen
