## Ingredients

- Veal brains
- Bouillon
- Eggs
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Finely chopped parsley
- Butter
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Remove the membranes from veal brains and put them in bouillon with some beaten eggs
2. Add salt, nutmeg, finely chopped parsley, butter and lemon slices
3. Braise it with heat under and above until done
