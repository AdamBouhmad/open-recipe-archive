## Ingredients

- 12 kg spinach
- salt

## Directions

1. Sort through the spinach, wash it until the water runs completely clear and let it cook with the adhering water (without lid) until done (10 to 15 minutes).
2. Let it drain well in a colander.
3. Chop it as finely as possible and fill well-cleaned bottles with it up to 2 cm below the rim.
4. Add salt if desired.
5. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize for 1½ hours.
6. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
