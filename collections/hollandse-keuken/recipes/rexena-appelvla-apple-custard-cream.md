## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 6 level tablespoons Rexena
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 2 large baking apples

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into small pieces.
2. Place them in a bowl and sprinkle 2 tablespoons of sugar over them.
3. Mix the Rexena and remaining sugar with a portion of the cold milk.
4. Bring the rest of the milk to a boil and, while stirring, add the prepared Rexena mixture.
5. Let the custard cream boil briefly and then pour it over the apple pieces.
6. Let the custard cream stand for at least 4 hours to set.
