## Ingredients

- 1.5 ounces butter
- flour and cornstarch
- parsley, chopped
- fine onions
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 1 glass cream or milk

## Directions

1. Mix 1.5 ounces butter with flour and cornstarch, a little chopped parsley, fine onions, a little salt, pepper and nutmeg
2. Mix this together with 1 glass of cream or milk
3. Cook it with continuous stirring
4. If the sauce is too thick, add more milk or cream
5. This sauce is very good with stewed turbot, cod, salt fish and potatoes
