## Ingredients

- 1 kg Brussels lof
- 35 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the lof as described in the previous recipe.
2. Place it in a fireproof dish, add a little of the cooking liquid.
3. Sprinkle bread crumbs over it, distribute the butter or margarine in small pieces on top, clean the rim of the dish and let it brown lightly in a moderately warm oven.
