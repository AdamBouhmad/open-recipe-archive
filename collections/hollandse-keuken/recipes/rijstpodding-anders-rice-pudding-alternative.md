## Ingredients

- 5 ounces rice
- crushed rusk
- sweet milk
- 5 hard-boiled eggs
- currants
- salt
- nutmeg
- cinnamon snippets
- grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook 5 ounces rice with crushed rusk and sweet milk to a proper thickness
2. beat it with 5 hard-boiled and finely chopped eggs, currants, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon snippets and grated lemon zest
3. put in a mold
4. let it cook in water until done
