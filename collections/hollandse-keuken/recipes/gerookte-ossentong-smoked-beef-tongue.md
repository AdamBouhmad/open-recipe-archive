## Ingredients

- smoked beef tongue
- lukewarm water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Wash and brush the tongue as clean as possible, then soak it for 24 hours in cold water, changing it repeatedly.
2. Wash it well again with lukewarm water and let it cook gently with plenty of boiling water (approximately 3 to 3 1/2 hours).
3. Test if it is done by seeing if the skin comes off easily.
4. Remove the skin while the tongue is warm and remove the fat and small bones from the throat part.
5. Warm the tongue again in the cooking liquid if necessary and then slice it.
6. Serve young garden beans and brown butter or bleached celery and stewed tomatoes with mashed potatoes with smoked beef tongue.
