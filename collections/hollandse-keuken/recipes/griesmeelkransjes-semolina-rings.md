## Ingredients

- 1/2 l milk
- 135 g semolina
- 1/4 vanilla pod or 3/2 lemon zest
- 75 g sugar
- 60 g butter or margarine
- 5 egg yolks
- some candied fruits or marmalade
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Prepare semolina porridge in the usual way, but add the butter
2. Remove the flavoring, stir in the egg yolks one by one off the heat
3. Spread the dough on a greased baking sheet to 1/2 cm thickness
4. Cut out circles with a greased ring, make a round hole in the middle of each circle (e.g. with an apple corer)
5. Remove the rings from the baking sheet with a sharp, thin knife and fry them brown in hot frying fat
6. Let them drain well on porous paper
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and place a candied fruit or some marmalade on each hole
