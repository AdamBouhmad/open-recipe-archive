## Ingredients

- 1 l water
- 1 small bunch leeks
- salt
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 30 g macaroni
- + 7 1/2 g sago
- marmite

## Directions

1. Clean the leeks, cut them into small pieces, wash them
2. Set them with the liquid and salt
3. Add the macaroni broken into small pieces and washed, and let the soup cook for 1/2 hour
4. At the end add the sago mixed with cold water and the butter or margarine
5. Let the soup cook for another minute
6. Add the marmite, which has been mixed with some water
