## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- milk
- salt
- rusks (beschuiten)
- butter
- bread sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 6 eggs fine
2. Add 1 tablespoon milk and some salt per egg
3. Soak rusks in this mixture
4. Fry in butter
5. When serving, sprinkle with bread sugar
6. May also use slices of stale white bread instead of rusks
