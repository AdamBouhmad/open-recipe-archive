## Ingredients

- milk
- sago flour
- cold milk
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil, now mix some sago flour with cold milk into a paste, stir that into the milk and let it cook through
2. Add sugar and cinnamon as desired
