## Ingredients

- 125 g almonds
- 125 g sugar
- 70 g flour
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Wash the unpeeled almonds, dry them and grind them fine.
2. Mix them with the stiffly beaten egg white, the sifted flour and the sugar and shape this dough into small rolls about 1 cm thick.
3. Lay these on a greased baking sheet and bake them in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done (15-20 minutes). Remove them from the baking sheet and let them cool.
