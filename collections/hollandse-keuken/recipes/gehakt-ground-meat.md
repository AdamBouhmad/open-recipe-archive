## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg ground meat
- 1 egg
- 75 g old bread without crust, or 150 g bread and no egg
- pepper
- 7 1/2 g salt
- nutmeg
- at least 100 g butter, margarine or fat
- lean gravy leftovers

## Directions

1. Beat the egg with the salt, pepper and nutmeg.
2. Soak the bread in some water or milk, press it out well, add the spices and salt and stir this all through the egg until it is evenly mixed with it.
3. Add the ground meat and stir everything with two spoons until it has become an even mass and no bread pieces are visible.
4. Form 2 (or more) firm balls from it.
5. Make butter, margarine or fat hot in a braising pan or small roasting pan.
6. Place the ground meat in it, sprinkle it optionally with some breadcrumbs and braise it brown and done (45 minutes per 600 g, with pork ground meat in 50 minutes per 600 g).
7. Finish the gravy with water or milk and with the lean gravy leftovers.
