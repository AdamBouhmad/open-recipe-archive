## Ingredients

- kale
- butter
- bouillon
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the kale tender and let it drain
2. Chop finely
3. Press out firmly
4. Stew with a piece of butter, a little bouillon, pepper, salt and nutmeg
