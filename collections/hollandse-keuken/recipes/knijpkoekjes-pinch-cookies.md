## Ingredients

- 5 ounces flour
- 2 and a half ounces syrup
- Aniseed
- Butter or fat
- Sweet milk
- 3 to 3.5 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a firm batter with flour, syrup, aniseed and butter or fat
2. Thin with sweet milk and eggs
3. Bake in a small waffle iron until done
