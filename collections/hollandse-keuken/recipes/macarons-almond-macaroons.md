## Ingredients

- peeled soaked almonds
- sugar
- vanilla or grated lemon zest
- wafer sheets (6 sheets)
- 30 almonds for garnishing

## Directions

1. Peel the soaked almonds. Grind them, mix them with the sugar, the flavoring and as much of the egg white until a stiff mass is obtained (as thick as purée for potato croquettes).
2. Form small balls from this, make these somewhat flat from underneath and lay them at some distance from each other on a wafer sheet, which is laid on a baking sheet.
3. For almond macaroons place a halved almond in the middle.
4. Bake the cookies in a moderately warm oven light brown (20 to 30 minutes).
5. Then break the wafer sheet away along the edge of the macaroons.
