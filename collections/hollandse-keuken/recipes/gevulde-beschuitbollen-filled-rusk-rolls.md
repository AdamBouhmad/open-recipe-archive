## Ingredients

- 8 rusk rolls or other small rolls
- approximately 8 small sour apples
- 50-60 g sugar
- 25 g currants
- fine cinnamon
- 40-50 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cook applesauce in the usual way, mix it with 35-40 g of the sugar and the well-washed currants.
2. Cut the rusk rolls in half, hollow them out, fill them with applesauce and press both halves firmly together.
3. Brush the rolls on the outside with melted butter or margarine.
4. Roll them through the rest of the sugar mixed with fine cinnamon, lay them on a baking sheet and let them become warm and crispy in a moderately warm oven.
