## Ingredients

- butter
- chopped onions and parsley
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper
- a little bread crumb

## Directions

1. Make a paper case and place a piece of butter inside
2. Add chopped onions and parsley
3. Warm on the gridiron so the butter is well melted and warm
4. Break an egg into the case with a little pepper, salt and a few bread crumbs
5. Place on a moderate fire with a glowing coal on both sides of the case
6. Take a glowing ash shovel and hold it above the egg until it is cooked
7. 6, 8 or 10 make a small dish
