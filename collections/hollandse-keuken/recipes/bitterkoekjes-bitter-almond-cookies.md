## Ingredients

- 100 g bitter almonds
- 200 g sweet almonds
- 600 g sugar
- + 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Peel the blanched almonds, dry them and grind them with 150 g sugar twice through an almond mill fine.
2. Mix them with the remaining sugar and enough egg white until a fairly loose dough is obtained.
3. Pipe through the piping bag fitted with a smooth funnel onto grey paper placed on a baking sheet, small round balls.
4. Flatten them slightly with a damp cloth and bake them in a fairly hot oven until light brown and done (+ 1/2 hour).
5. Let them cool on the paper and remove them carefully.
