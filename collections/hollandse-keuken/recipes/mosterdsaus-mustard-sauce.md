## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine, or 20 g Dureya maizena plus 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 tablespoon mustard

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce or maizena sauce)
2. Mix it away from the heat with the mustard, which to prevent lumps from forming, has been mixed with some water or some sauce
3. Serve with hard-boiled eggs, stockfish, haddock, etc.
