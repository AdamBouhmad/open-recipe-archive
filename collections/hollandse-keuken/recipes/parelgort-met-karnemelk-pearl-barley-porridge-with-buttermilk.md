## Ingredients

- 1.5 l buttermilk
- 150 g pearl barley
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bring the buttermilk with salt to a boil, stirring occasionally.
2. Sprinkle in the rinsed and soaked pearl barley while stirring (see previous recipe) and prepare the porridge further, as described in the previous recipe.
