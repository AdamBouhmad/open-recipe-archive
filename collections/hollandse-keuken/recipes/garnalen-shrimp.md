## Ingredients

- 100 g shrimp
- salt water
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the shrimp with salt water
2. Let them drain well on a sieve
3. Mix with some salt and pepper, oil and vinegar
4. Place on the serving dish
5. If desired, make a border around it with very finely chopped or cut parsley, or sprinkle parsley over the top
