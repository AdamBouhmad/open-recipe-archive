## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head
- 2 ox palates
- salt
- water
- shallots or onions
- mace
- peppercorns
- bay leaves
- 2½ hectogram calf thymus
- 2½ hectogram sausages
- butter
- flour
- beef bouillon
- herbs
- lemon slices without pits
- fine pepper
- cayenne pepper
- mace
- pimento
- morels
- mushrooms
- capers
- 1½ to 2 tablespoons Liebig's Meat Extract
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Set the calf's head and ox palates in cold water with salt on the fire, skim well and cook them with shallots or onions, mace, peppercorns and bay leaves for 2 to 2½ hours until tender and cut them into proper pieces.
2. Take the calf thymus, cook it, cut it into small pieces and let them with the sausages brown in butter.
3. Make flour browned in butter, add some beef bouillon drawn with herbs and shallots, stir it until a good smooth and well-bound sauce.
4. Add lemon slices without pits, fine pepper, a pinch of cayenne pepper, some mace and pimento and cook the meat together with morels, mushrooms and capers, as well as 1½ to 2 tablespoons Liebig's Meat Extract for about a quarter hour and add salt to taste.
5. When serving, stir in Madeira wine.
