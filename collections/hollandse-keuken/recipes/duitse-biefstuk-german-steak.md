## Ingredients

- 500 g stewing beef, chopped
- 100 g butter or margarine
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 1 onion
- 4 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Wash the pieces and chop or grind them very finely.
2. Add the milk and the spices and mix the mass until it forms a coherent whole.
3. Form steaks from this and fry them brown in the butter, finish the gravy (see beef steak recipe 253).
4. Fry the chopped onion and garnish the steaks with it.
