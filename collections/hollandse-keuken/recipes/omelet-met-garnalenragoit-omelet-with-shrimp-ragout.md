## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons water
- pepper and salt to taste
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 100 g shrimp
- 1 dl milk or cream, or half milk, half cream
- pepper and salt
- 10 g flour
- 10 g butter or margarine
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare an omelet in the usual way (see recipe 383) and fold it in thirds after inserting the shrimp ragout, which has been prepared in the usual way (see recipe 163).
2. Place on a warmed dish and serve with bread.
