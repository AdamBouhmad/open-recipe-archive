## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 spoonfuls flour (approximately 45 g)
- 4 spoonfuls sugar (approximately 60 g)
- 4 spoonfuls butter or margarine (approximately 70 g)
- 1/4 l milk
- pulp from 1 stick vanilla

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the sugar and vanilla pulp.
2. Add the sifted flour, the melted butter or margarine, and the milk gradually.
3. Ensure the batter becomes completely smooth.
4. Beat the egg white very stiffly and fold it into the batter while beating.
5. Let a very small amount of butter or margarine melt in the frying pan and pour 1/4 of the batter into it.
6. Let the cake cook and brown golden on one side on very low heat.
7. Bake the remaining dough the same way and lay the first three cakes with the fried side on the unbaked (creamy) side.
8. Turn the last cake around so the brown side is on top.
9. Sprinkle the cream puff with powdered sugar and serve immediately.
