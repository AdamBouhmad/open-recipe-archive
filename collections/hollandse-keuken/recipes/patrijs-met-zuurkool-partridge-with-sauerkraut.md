## Ingredients

- 2 partridges
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 1 kg sauerkraut
- 2 pairs knockwurst
- 500 g fairly fat bacon
- some flour
- salt
- some potato puree

## Directions

1. Roast the partridges in the usual way (see recipe 320).
2. Cook the sauerkraut in the usual way (see recipe 507) until cooked and dry, but let the lukewarm water-rinsed bacon cook with it for half an hour.
3. Cook the knockwurst like sausages (see recipe 245), but allow 10 minutes for this.
4. Cut them lengthwise in half.
5. Cut the bacon into slices and the partridges into 4 pieces.
6. Stir a couple of spoonfuls of flour through the cooked sauerkraut until the sauerkraut is bound and the flour is cooked (approximately 10 minutes).
7. Line the sides of a smooth pudding mold (e.g. warm pudding mold) with the knockwurst and bacon slices.
8. Lay a layer of sauerkraut in it, place a piece of partridge on it and continue in this way until the mold is filled, but make sure the last layer consists of sauerkraut.
9. Turn out the pudding and pipe some potato puree around it with the butter syringe.
