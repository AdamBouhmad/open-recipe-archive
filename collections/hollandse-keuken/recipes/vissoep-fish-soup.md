## Ingredients

- 3/4 l fish water
- 1/2 l milk
- mace
- piece of Spanish pepper
- parsley
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- Maggi's Aroma if desired
- 1 egg (optional)
- leftovers of cooked, boned fish

## Directions

1. Let the spices steep in the fish water. Sieve this liquid and gradually add it, while stirring, to the melted butter or margarine, which has then been brought to a boil together with the flour and from which the lumps have been worked out.
2. Let the soup cook while stirring (approximately 10 minutes). Add the leftovers of cooked, boned fish. Stir in the milk and finely chopped parsley and if desired carefully pour it into the egg that has been beaten in the soup tureen, stirring vigorously.
