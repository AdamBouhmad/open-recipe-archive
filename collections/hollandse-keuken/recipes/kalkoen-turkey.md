## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- at least 250 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 500 to 750 g chestnuts (depending on the size of the bird)

## Directions

1. Wash the turkey inside and outside.
2. Cook the scored chestnuts for 10 minutes in boiling water, remove the outer shell and brown skin and fill the turkey, which has been salted inside and outside, with them.
3. Sew the rear closed.
4. Make the butter or margarine light brown in a roasting pan or iron pot, place the turkey in it, roast it until light brown and cooked through (3 to 3 1/2 hours).
5. Add a little water to the butter or margarine from time to time when it threatens to become too brown.
6. When the turkey is roasted in the oven, cover it with a piece of paper smeared with butter or margarine.
7. Turn it in the iron pot from time to time.
8. Finish the gravy if needed with water or cream.
9. Serve with compote.
