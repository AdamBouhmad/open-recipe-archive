## Ingredients

- 1 kg carp
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the carp, rinse it
2. Cook it in boiling water with salt until a fin comes off easily (approximately 10 minutes)
3. Serve it with melted butter or margarine and very finely chopped parsley
