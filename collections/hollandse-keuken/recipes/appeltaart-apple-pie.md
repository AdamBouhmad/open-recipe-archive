## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 175 g butter or margarine
- 80 g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 12 sour apples (750 g)
- 100 g sugar
- 100 g currants
- 50 g almonds
- grated zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Knead butter or margarine, flour, sugar and 1½ eggs together into a firm ball
2. If too soft, let rest for a quarter hour in a cool place
3. Roll out into a large round sheet and press into a greased springform pan so that the bottom is completely covered and the sides are covered 4 cm up
4. Slice the peeled apples and mix with sugar, well-washed currants with stems removed, chopped almonds and grated lemon zest
5. Fill the form with this mixture
6. Lay thin strips of remaining dough in a diamond pattern over the top
7. Roll the edge slightly so the strips are firmly attached, or make an extra rim of dough, flute it and finish the edge with it
8. Brush the dough with some of the remaining beaten egg
9. Bake in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked through (approximately 1 hour)
