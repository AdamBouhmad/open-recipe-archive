## Ingredients

- hare
- salt
- pepper
- water
- vinegar
- bacon
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. After skinning and gutting the hare, rub it inside and out with salt and pepper
2. Let it soak in water for a couple of hours and then lay it for 2 days in vinegar and salt
3. Lard it with thin strips of bacon that have been rolled in salt and pepper
4. Place it straight in a hare pan with meat forks against the hindquarters
5. Add necessary amount of butter and boiling water
6. Roast until done with fire above and below, basting from time to time with the cooking liquid
7. At the end, sprinkle some fine breadcrumbs over it
