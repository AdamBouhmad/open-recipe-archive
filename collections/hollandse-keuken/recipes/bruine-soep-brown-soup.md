## Ingredients

- 1.5 Dutch pounds lean beef
- salt
- rice or barley
- veal meat for meatballs

## Directions

1. Cook 1.5 Dutch pounds of lean beef with some salt for a long time and strongly, skimming well
2. Add rice or peeled barley, and half an hour before serving, add veal meatballs
