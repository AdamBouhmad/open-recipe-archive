## Ingredients

- a beer glass currant juice
- stale white bread, grated
- a cup melted butter
- 4 to 5 eggs
- sugar and cinnamon to taste
- grated lemon zest
- some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a beer glass currant juice, stale grated white bread, a cup melted butter, 4 to 5 eggs, sugar and cinnamon to taste, grated lemon zest and some lemon juice
2. Stir well together
3. Let it thicken on a baking sheet
4. Then bake it like any other fritter
