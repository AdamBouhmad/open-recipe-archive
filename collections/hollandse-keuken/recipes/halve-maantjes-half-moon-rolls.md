## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 10 g yeast
- 7.5 g salt
- 2 dl lukewarm milk
- 1 egg
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 50 g sugar

## Directions

1. Make bread dough in the known manner (see recipe 835) from flour, yeast, salt, milk, egg, melted butter or margarine and sugar and let it rise.
2. Form small half moons from the dough and let them rise for another half hour on a buttered baking sheet under a damp cloth.
3. Brush them with some beaten egg and bake them in a hot oven light yellow and done (about 15 minutes).
