## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes
- 3/4 l water
- 1/4 l milk
- salt
- 30 g butter or margarine
- celery greens
- 3 carrots
- piece of Spanish pepper
- piece of mace
- finely chopped parsley
- Maggi's Aroma if desired

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cut them into small pieces, wash them, put them on to cook with salt, the milk, the chopped and washed carrots and the celery, the mace and Spanish pepper.
2. Let them cook until they have become completely soft (approximately 3/4 hour), pour the soup through a sieve and rub the potatoes through.
3. Gradually add the water to the thick purée while stirring, make the soup warm again and add the butter and parsley. Finish the soup with a few drops of Maggi's Aroma.
