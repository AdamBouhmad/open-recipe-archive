## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- salt
- pepper
- crushed cloves
- wine vinegar
- butter
- three finely cut onions
- pickled cucumbers
- half glass red wine
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat the leg well and cut it
2. Rub it with salt, pepper and crushed cloves
3. Place it in an earthenware pan
4. Pour wine vinegar over it and let it stand for 4 days, turning and basting daily
5. Braise three finely cut onions in butter until slightly tender
6. Place the leg in with them
7. When it is half done, add some pickled cucumbers cut into pieces and half a glass of red wine
8. Let it finish cooking
9. Bind the sauce with a little flour and dish it over the leg
