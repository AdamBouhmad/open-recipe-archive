## Ingredients

- Chicken livers and gizzards
- 4 lomboks
- 5 cloves white onions
- 4 tablespoons ground onions
- 1/2 teaspoon laos
- 1 tablespoon sereh powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon assem water
- 2 daon djoeroek poeroet
- 2 dl coconut milk
- Butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Clean the livers and gizzards well, cut them into pieces and cook them
2. Pound the spices and the onions — except the leaves — finely and fry them in the butter or margarine
3. Add the pieces of liver and gizzard with some salt, the assem water, sugar and the leaves
4. Cook this with the coconut milk until the fat comes out
