## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 dl cooking cream
- 1/2 dl salad oil
- 1/2 dl vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with salt, pepper and mustard in a good enameled pan using a tinned whisk (whip).
2. Gradually add the oil and cream and let the sauce bind au bain marie while stirring vigorously.
3. Remove from heat and add vinegar.
4. Serve with warm salmon.
