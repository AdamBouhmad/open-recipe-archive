## Ingredients

- doves
- water
- vinegar
- shallots
- morels
- celery
- parsley root
- bay leaves
- juniper berries
- pepper
- cloves
- brown butter

## Directions

1. Allow two parts water and one part vinegar with shallots, morels, celery or parsley root, bay leaves, juniper berries, pepper and cloves to boil for half an hour and then cool.
2. Place the doves in this and let them soak for a whole day.
3. Lard them and roast slowly in brown butter while constantly basting.
4. Heat the liquid in which the doves soaked and pour under them during roasting as often as needed.
