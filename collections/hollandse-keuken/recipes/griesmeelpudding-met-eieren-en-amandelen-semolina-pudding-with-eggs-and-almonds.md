## Ingredients

- ¾ l milk
- ¼ stick vanilla, vanilla sugar or peel of ½ lemon
- 65 g sugar
- 75 to 85 g semolina
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 40 g almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the pudding as semolina pudding with eggs.
2. Stir in the peeled and chopped almonds together with the semolina and sugar.
3. If desired, replace the almonds with hazelnuts, or use the semolina with small almonds as it comes in commerce.
