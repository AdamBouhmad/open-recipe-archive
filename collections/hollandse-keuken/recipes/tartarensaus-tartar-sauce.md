## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- 3½ dl salad oil
- 1½ dl vinegar
- 2 teaspoons mustard or mustard powder
- some onion juice or a very finely chopped shallot
- some Cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as mayonnaise (recipe 367).
2. Stir in last the onion juice or the very finely chopped shallot and the Cayenne pepper.
3. Serve with cold meat or cold fish.
