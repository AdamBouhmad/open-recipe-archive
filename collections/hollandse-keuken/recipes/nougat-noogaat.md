## Ingredients

- Five ounces peeled sweet almonds
- Four ounces fine white sugar
- Oil
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the sweet almonds, remove the skin again, wash them and let them dry
2. Cut them into pieces (in five)
3. Dry them in an oven until they become somewhat yellow
4. Put four ounces fine white sugar in a pan and let it melt on the stove, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon
5. When the sugar is melted, add the warm almonds
6. Take the pan from the fire and stir the almonds constantly
7. Take a mold which you grease with oil
8. Put your almonds in it and work them with a lemon that you hold in your hand against the mold, so it takes the shape you desire
9. Lay them as thin as possible
10. Slide out of the mold and set as cold as possible
11. You can make all kinds of shapes
