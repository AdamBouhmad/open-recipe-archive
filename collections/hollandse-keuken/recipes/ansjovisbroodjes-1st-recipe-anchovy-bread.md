## Ingredients

- 3 slices casino bread
- 6 anchovies
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/8 teaspoon mustard
- 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon salad oil
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean the anchovies as indicated in recipe 17.
2. Cut very thin slices of casino bread, remove the crusts, cut them lengthwise in half and fry them with butter in a skillet until light brown and crispy.
3. Boil the eggs hard (8 minutes), peel them, separate the white and yolk.
4. Rub the yolks fine, mix them with the oil, vinegar, mustard and some parsley.
5. Spread this mixture on the bread pieces on one side, place 2 rolled-up half anchovies on each slice and garnish the breads with finely chopped egg white, finely chopped parsley and capers.
