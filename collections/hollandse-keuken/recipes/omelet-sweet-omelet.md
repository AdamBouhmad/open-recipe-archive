## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 20 to 25 g sugar
- 25 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar.
2. Melt the butter or margarine in the skillet, pour in the mixture and prick with a fork until the omelet begins to release from the entire surface of the pan.
3. Allow it to brown lightly on the bottom, optionally place some jam or marmalade on it, fold it in thirds and sprinkle with some sifted powdered sugar.
