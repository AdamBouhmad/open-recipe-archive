## Ingredients

- 5 ounces wheat flour
- 8 eggs (6 with whites)
- 2 ounces currants
- 1 cup melted butter
- milk, yeast, and sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 5 ounces wheat flour and 8 eggs (6 with whites) for a long time.
2. Add 2 ounces currants, 1 cup melted butter, milk, yeast, and sugar.
3. Bake in a tart pan.
