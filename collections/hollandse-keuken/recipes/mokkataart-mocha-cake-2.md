## Ingredients

- 120 g flour
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g sugar
- 5 eggs
- 150 g butter
- 60 g powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon Nescafé
- 3 spoons water
- 1 sheet gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare the cake as in the German sand cake (recipe 863).
2. Let it cool and cut it into two or three layers.
3. For the mocha: stir the butter until creamy, add the sifted powdered sugar and the dissolved Nescafé.
4. Dissolve the soaked gelatin with as little boiling water as possible, let it cool and add it to the mocha.
5. Spread the different layers with mocha, place them on top of each other, also spread the top and pipe the remaining mocha in figures on it.
6. Note: If it is cold, no gelatin needs to be stirred through the mocha.
