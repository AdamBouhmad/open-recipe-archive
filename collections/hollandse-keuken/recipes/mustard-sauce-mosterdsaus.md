## Ingredients

- A lump of butter
- One teaspoon flour
- One tablespoon sugar
- Two tablespoons mustard
- Two spoons vinegar
- Three spoons water

## Directions

1. Mix butter, flour, sugar, mustard, vinegar, and water
2. Let it boil briefly while stirring constantly
