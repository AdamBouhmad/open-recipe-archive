## Ingredients

- 150 g shrimp
- 1 1/2 dl milk, cream, or half milk, half cream
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 20 g flour
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon Maggi's aroma
- 1/2 egg or one egg white for breading
- 2 sheets gelatin or 4 g powdered gelatin
- bread crumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Wash the shrimp in water with salt.
2. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102.
3. Mix it with the gelatin, the shrimp, and the spices and let the mixture, spread on a board, become almost stiff, but ensure it is still good to handle.
4. Form the croquettes in the manner as indicated for meat croquettes (see recipe 116), bread them double, and treat them further entirely as meat croquettes.
