## Ingredients

- Two measures flour
- Half ounce salt
- Four ounces butter
- Six egg yolks
- One glass water

## Directions

1. Spread flour on the work surface, leaving a small hole in the middle
2. Place in the hole: half ounce salt, four ounces butter, six egg yolks, and one glass water
3. Mix the salt, butter and eggs together
4. Knead with the flour
5. If too dry, add a little more water, but carefully so it does not become too soft
6. Use for chicken pie or pigeon pie, or for pan fish
7. For tarts, this crust should be somewhat softer
