## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- potato
- oysters
- white wine
- bouillon
- egg yolk
- flour
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- butter

## Directions

1. Line small molds with puff pastry
2. Put a potato in each and cover them
3. Let this bake done
4. Remove the potato from them and fill them with oysters stewed as follows:
5. Take equal parts white wine and bouillon, stir this with egg yolks and flour until bound
6. Add raw oysters to it with some lemon juice, nutmeg and butter and let it boil up briefly
