## Ingredients

- 75 g macaroni
- pepper
- salt
- 50 g ham
- 60 g old Gouda cheese
- 35 g flour
- 35 g butter or margarine or 25 g Duryea cornstarch
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 3½ dl milk
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces, wash them and cook them until done in plenty of boiling water with salt or broth (1/2 hour) and stir occasionally.
2. Drain them.
3. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102.
4. Stir in the chopped ham and grated cheese and as airy as possible the macaroni cut into pieces.
5. Add pepper and salt to taste and fill the dampened shells with this mixture for about 3/4.
6. Finish them as described in recipe 102.
7. Note: Instead of this mixture, one can also use the mixture for macaroni with ham and cheese (see recipe 126).
