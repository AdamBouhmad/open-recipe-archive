## Ingredients

- Juice of 4 oranges and 1 lemon (approximately 2½ dl)
- 8 g white gelatin or powdered gelatin
- Approximately 100 to 125 g sugar
- ¼ l whipping cream

## Directions

1. Mix the strained fruit juice with the sugar and the gelatin, which has been soaked in cold water for 10 to 15 minutes.
2. Heat until the sugar and gelatin are dissolved and let it cool.
3. Beat the cream stiff, stir the mixture through it and serve the custard after cooling with biscuits.
