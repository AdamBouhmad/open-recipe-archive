## Ingredients

- half a pound macaroni
- a piece of butter
- some salt
- two ounces butter
- four ounces grated white cheese
- three ounces Parmesan cheese
- a little nutmeg
- one or two spoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni with a piece of butter and some salt for three quarters of an hour
2. Check if it is tender
3. Let it drain briefly
4. Put it in a pan with two ounces butter, four ounces grated white cheese, three ounces Parmesan cheese, a little nutmeg and one or two spoonfuls cream
5. When the cream draws in threads, place it on the dish
