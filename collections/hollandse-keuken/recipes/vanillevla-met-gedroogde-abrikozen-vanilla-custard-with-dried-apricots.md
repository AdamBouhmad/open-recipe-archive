## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter melk
- 40 g suiker
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 1 ei
- 1/2 stokje vanille or 1 zakje vanillesuiker
- 100 g gedroogde abrikozen
- 50 g suiker
- wat sago

## Directions

1. Cook the apricots according to recipe 541.
2. Place them at the bottom of a custard dish and let them cool.
3. Prepare the vanilla custard as described in recipe 657.
4. Remove the vanilla, let it cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin from forming.
5. Pour the custard over the apricots.
