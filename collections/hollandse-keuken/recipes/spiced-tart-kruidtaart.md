## Ingredients

- 125 g butter or margarine
- 125 g flour
- 125 g currants
- 125 g raisins
- 125 g dark brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons fine cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon crushed cloves
- 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the eggs one by one and stir them in until they are completely mixed with the butter or margarine.
2. Then add the sugar and stir it as finely as possible.
3. Add the sifted flour, some salt, the spices and lastly the washed and dried currants and raisins.
4. Put the dough in a greased tart pan and bake the tart in a fairly warm oven until light brown and cooked through (approximately 1 hour).
5. Test if it is cooked by pricking it with a knitting needle, which should come out dry.
6. Turn the tart out onto a cake rack, let it cool and store it in a well-closed tin.
