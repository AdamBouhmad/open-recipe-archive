## Ingredients

- 300 g sugar
- 200 g almonds
- 10 g butter or margarine (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare nougat, as indicated in recipe 802.
2. Roll it out on a greased, warmed sheet with a greased rolling pin to a thickness of about 1/4 cm, cut circles from it with a metal ring and press these as quickly as possible into small tart molds.
3. Warm the remaining nougat again, roll this equally thin and cut long, narrow strips from it, which are bent over and warm, by way of handles, attached to the baskets.
4. Fill the baskets with ice cream or whipped cream, mixed with powdered sugar.
5. N.B. If the nougat becomes too hard during rolling, which happens very quickly, it must be put back in the pan and warmed again. To prevent it from hardening, one preferably works at a warm place.
