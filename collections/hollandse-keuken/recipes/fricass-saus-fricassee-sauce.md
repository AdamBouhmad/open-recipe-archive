## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dl bouillon
- 2 1/2 dl cream
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 2 egg yolks
- lemon juice
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Remove from heat and blend with the egg yolks, add lemon juice, pepper and salt to taste and serve with meat timbal among other dishes
