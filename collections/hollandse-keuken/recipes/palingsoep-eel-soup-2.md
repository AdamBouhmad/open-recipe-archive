## Ingredients

- 1 l salted water
- 300 g thin eel
- piece of Spanish pepper
- mace
- 35 g sorrel
- 35 g chervil
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg (optional)
- Maggi's Aroma if desired

## Directions

1. Clean the eel, cut it into pieces of approximately 3 cm, wash it and cook it until done (approximately 5 minutes) in the boiling water with the salt, Spanish pepper and mace.
2. Remove the eel with a skimming spoon from the pan, remove the fish bones and cook the well-washed and very finely chopped vegetables in the liquid from which the spices have been removed until done (15 minutes).
3. Melt the butter or margarine and bring it to a boil while stirring with the flour, carefully rub out all lumps and gradually add the liquid in small amounts.
4. Let the soup cook while stirring (approximately 10 minutes), warm the eel pieces in it and if desired carefully pour the soup into the egg that has been beaten in the soup tureen, stirring vigorously. Finish the soup with a few drops of Maggi's Aroma.
