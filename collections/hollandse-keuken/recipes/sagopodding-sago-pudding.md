## Ingredients

- 1 ounce sago
- 1 cinnamon stick
- sweet milk
- 9-10 crushed rusk
- 5 eggs
- butter
- bread sugar
- 1 grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook 1 ounce sago with 1 cinnamon stick in sweet milk until it has a proper thickness
2. coat this with 9-10 finely crushed rusk, 5 eggs, the necessary butter, bread sugar and 1 grated lemon zest
3. pour into the mold
4. let it cook until done
