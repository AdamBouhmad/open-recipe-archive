## Ingredients

- three young rabbits
- vinegar
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaves
- chopped onions
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- fried eggs
- white mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbits properly
2. Let them stand for several days in a pan with vinegar, some parsley, thyme, bay leaves, chopped onions, salt and pepper
3. Before they are finished, press the meat out between a linen cloth
4. Roll it in flour and fry it with butter
5. Arrange the pieces neatly once they are fried and lay fried eggs around them
6. Serve with a white mustard sauce
