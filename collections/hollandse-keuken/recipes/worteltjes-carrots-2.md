## Ingredients

- 12 large bunches carrots
- sugar
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots, wash them carefully and arrange them neatly in well-cleaned bottles.
2. Add some sugar and if desired some salt.
3. Pour on enough water so that the carrots are just covered.
4. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize for 1½ hours.
5. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
6. Note: The carrots can also be cooked for some minutes and then placed in the bottles.
