## Ingredients

- 8 small rusks
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 60 g cheese
- cayenne pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Stir the butter until creamy, add the grated cheese and pepper and salt to taste.
2. Spread this mixture in a thick layer on the rusks and let them become light brown in a baking tin in a moderately warm oven.
