## Ingredients

- 250 g lentils
- 1 l water
- piece of winter carrot
- 25 g onion (1 small onion)
- 1 bay leaf
- some thyme
- approximately 40 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- lemon juice to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the lentils, put them on with the cold water, the chopped onion and carrot, the bay leaf, and some thyme
2. Let them cook gently (1 1/2 hours), but add liquid from time to time
3. Pour the soup through a horsehair sieve, rub the lentils through, and mix the lentil purée with the cooking liquid
4. Let the soup cook gently until it is evenly bound
5. Just before serving, add the butter or margarine, salt, and lemon juice to taste
6. Serve with bread cut into dice and fried in butter or margarine
