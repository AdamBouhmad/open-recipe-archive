## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g flour
- 100 g sugar
- 50 g almonds
- 1 teaspoon fine cinnamon
- 1 grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Grind the peeled almonds with 50 g of the sugar twice through an almond grinder.
2. Then knead them with the remaining ingredients into a firm ball, press it into a greased tart pan and bake the tart in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked through (½ hour to 45 minutes).
3. Test if it is cooked by pricking it with a knitting needle, which should come out dry.
4. Turn out the tart after it has cooled slightly and garnish it if desired with glaze and candied fruits.
