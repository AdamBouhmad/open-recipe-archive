## Ingredients

- 5 veal cutlets of 250 g each
- 500 g ground veal
- 1 egg
- 50 g old bread
- 10 g salt
- pepper and nutmeg to taste
- 1 veal foot
- 1 dl tarragon vinegar
- 12 g white gelatine or powdered gelatine
- parsley
- capers
- scalloped lemon slices

## Directions

1. Rinse the foot, place it in cold water just covering it and let it simmer gently until 2 dl of broth remains.
2. Dissolve the gelatine, which has been soaked in cold water for 10 minutes, in the broth and add the vinegar.
3. Rinse and salt the cutlets, mix the ground meat in the usual way with the egg, the soaked bread and the seasonings and layer it alternately with the meat in the designated mold.
4. Pour the broth over it, close the mold with the heavy lid that fits it and cook the mixture for 2 hours in a bain marie.
5. Let it cool, turn it out after removing the fat from it and garnish it with fresh sprigs of parsley, capers and scalloped lemon slices.
