## Ingredients

- a sufficient quantity of pork
- jus
- onions, finely cut
- pepper
- salt
- finely ground biscuit
- a splash of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut a sufficient quantity of pork into slices, add some jus, finely cut onions, pepper, salt and finely ground biscuit, as well as a splash of vinegar, and let it braise for about half an hour
