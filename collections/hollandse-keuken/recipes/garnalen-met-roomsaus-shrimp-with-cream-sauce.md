## Ingredients

- 400 g shrimp
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 4 dl milk
- 1 dl cream
- pepper and salt
- half-moons of feuilletée or toasted bread

## Directions

1. Rinse the shrimp with salted water, warm them well in a colander over a pan with hot water.
2. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see recipe 337).
3. Mix it to taste with some pepper and salt, put warm shrimp in it and place everything on a warmed dish.
4. Garnish the edge with half-moons of feuilletée (see recipe 846) or toasted bread.
