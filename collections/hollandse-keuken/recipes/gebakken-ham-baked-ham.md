## Ingredients

- cooked ham
- bread dough or Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare as for boiled ham
2. Then wrap it in a bread dough of finger thickness, or sprinkle it on all sides with Parmesan cheese, and let it brown in the hot oven
