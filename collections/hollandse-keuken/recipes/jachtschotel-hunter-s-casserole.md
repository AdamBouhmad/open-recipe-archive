## Ingredients

- 300 g cold meat or less
- 1 kg cooked potatoes
- 100 g onion
- 300 g sour apples
- 40 g fat from gravy, margarine or butter
- 4 dl lean gravy with water or bouillon or 4 dl water with 2 Maggi's Bouillon cubes, or 1/2 Maggi's Gravy tablet
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Follow the preparation for philosopher (recipe 85) third method.
2. However, add chopped sour apples to this dish.
