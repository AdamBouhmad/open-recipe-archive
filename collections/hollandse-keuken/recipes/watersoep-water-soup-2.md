## Ingredients

- parsley roots
- celery roots
- leeks
- leaf parsley
- white bread cut into cubes
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Let parsley roots, celery roots, leeks, and leaf parsley cook well done with cubes of white bread in a pan
2. Pour enough water into it
3. Stir salt and butter through
4. Let everything cook through properly once more and serve
