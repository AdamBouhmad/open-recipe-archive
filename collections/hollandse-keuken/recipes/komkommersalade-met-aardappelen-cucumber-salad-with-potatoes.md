## Ingredients

- cucumbers, peeled
- salt
- cooked potatoes, cold
- onions, finely cut
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled cucumbers into thin slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and let them drain.
3. Lay slices of cold cooked potatoes on a dish.
4. Toss everything with finely cut onions, salt, oil, vinegar, and pepper.
