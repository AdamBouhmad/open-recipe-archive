## Ingredients

- Veal knuckle
- Wine vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Shallots

## Directions

1. Take a medium-sized veal knuckle, which must be very pale
2. Place it in a large pan with 3 bottles of good wine vinegar, a little salt, some pepper, bay leaves, thyme and shallots
3. Let it marinate for four to five days, turning it regularly
4. Lard the knuckle and roast it on the spit for four hours, wrapped in paper to keep it pale
5. You may make a white or brown sauce to serve with it
