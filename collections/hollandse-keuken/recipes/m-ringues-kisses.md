## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 250 g powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, stir the sifted powdered sugar and the vanilla through it with a few strokes and pipe from this dough onto a damp wooden board small balls in the shape of a half egg.
2. Let the meringues in a lukewarm oven become dry and hard on the outside, turn them over, hollow them out and let the inside dry in the oven.
3. Fill them with whipped cream.
