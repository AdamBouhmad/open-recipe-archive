## Ingredients

- 4 beautiful, lean, oblong-shaped beef cutlets of 75 g each
- 50 g fresh bacon
- 4 gherkins
- Salt
- At least 75 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash and salt the cutlets, cut the washed bacon into 4 equal slices, place these on the cutlets, place a gherkin in the middle and roll everything together.
2. Tie the beef rolls closed with a thin string or thread and fry them in butter or margarine on all sides until brown.
3. Finish the jus with water and braise the meat in it for approximately 1 hour more.
4. Remove the strings or threads before serving.
