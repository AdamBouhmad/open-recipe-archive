## Ingredients

- ¾ l milk
- 50 to 60 g maizena Duryea
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 70 g sugar
- ½ stick vanilla, vanilla sugar or peel of ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the flavoring.
2. Beat the eggs and mix with the maizena and sugar to a thin batter without lumps.
3. Add enough of the boiling milk so that the eggs are well heated.
4. Pour everything back into the pan.
