## Ingredients

- 4 cucumbers
- 1/4 litre of liquid from the cucumbers or 2 dl liquid from the cucumbers and approximately 1/2 dl vinegar
- 20 g flour or 10 g cornstarch
- 20 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers thinly, remove the bitter ends if necessary and cut them lengthwise into quarters.
2. Remove the seeds with a silver spoon and divide the cucumbers into pieces of approximately 5 cm length.
3. Let them cook tender in as little boiling water as possible (15 to 20 minutes), place them in a colander to drain, but catch the liquid.
4. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as cauliflower sauce or cornstarch sauce (see recipe 328 or recipe 329), but use the cucumber liquid, preferably mixed with some vinegar.
5. Braise the cucumbers in it for another approximately 10 minutes.
