## Ingredients

- roasted white bread
- eggs
- ginger
- wine
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a batter of roasted white bread, eggs, ginger, wine and sugar
2. Fry cookies in a pan with butter
3. When serving, sprinkle sugar over them
