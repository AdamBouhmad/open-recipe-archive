## Ingredients

- 100 g macaroni
- 200 g veal for stewing
- 100 g ground veal
- approximately 0.5 l boiling water
- pieces of mace
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- optionally 1 egg
- some lemon juice
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash the veal, set it on with the boiling water, salt and mace and let it cook gently for one quarter hour.
2. Add the macaroni, which has been broken into pieces and washed, and let this cook along for 1/2 hour on low heat.
3. Stir from time to time to prevent scorching, but make sure the macaroni does not break.
4. Let the meatballs, which are made with some soaked old bread, pepper
