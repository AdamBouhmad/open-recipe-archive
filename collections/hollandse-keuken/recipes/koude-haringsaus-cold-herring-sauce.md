## Ingredients

- Herring
- Sweet milk
- Finely chopped onions
- 3 hard boiled eggs
- Pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak a herring in sweet milk
2. Pull out the bones
3. Stir it into a sauce with some finely chopped onions, 3 hard boiled eggs, pepper, oil and vinegar
