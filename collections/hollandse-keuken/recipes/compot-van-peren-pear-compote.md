## Ingredients

- 20 winter pears
- water
- 6 cloves
- cinnamon
- sugar
- lemon peels

## Directions

1. Peel 20 winter pears, leaving them whole with the stem
2. Set them in enough water to cover them all
3. Add 6 cloves, cinnamon, plenty of sugar and lemon peels
4. Cover the pan and let them cook for two to four hours
5. If they have reduced too much and are not yet soft, add some water
6. Place them on small dishes with the stems pointing downward and pour the syrup over them
