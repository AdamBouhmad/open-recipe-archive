## Ingredients

- 3 small tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- lettuce
- thick mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut the washed tomatoes in half and carefully hollow them out.
2. Sprinkle them with some pepper and salt.
3. Peel the eggs and cut off a small cap on both sides so they can stand firmly.
4. Fill them if desired (recipe 29).
5. Place a tomato half on each egg and cover them here and there with thick mayonnaise (recipe 368) or very small pieces of the remaining egg white.
6. Garnish the plate with lettuce leaves and mayonnaise.
