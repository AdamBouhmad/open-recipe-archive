## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 200 g flour
- 200 g sugar
- 60 g raisins
- 60 g currants
- 30 g candied citrus peel
- 25 g almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the whole eggs and sugar for 15 minutes with a whisk.
2. Add the washed and dried currants and raisins, from which the stems have been removed, the chopped candied citrus peel and the chopped almonds.
3. Stir in the sifted flour last, as lightly as possible.
4. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake in a moderately warm oven until light brown (approximately 1 hour).
5. Check if it is done by inserting a knitting needle; it should come out dry.
6. Turn the cake out onto a cake rack and let it cool.
