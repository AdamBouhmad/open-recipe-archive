## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg eel
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the eel (by cutting off the head, loosening the skin at the neck and pulling it off, removing the innards and cutting the eel into pieces).
2. Wash the fish well, pierce the thick pieces with a larding needle, cut off the fins.
3. Wash the eel and cook it in boiling water with salt, until it is done (6 to 8 minutes).
4. Remove the eel from the pan with a skimming spoon and serve it with egg sauce, capers or butter sauce.
5. Note: Use very thick eel for cooking eel.
