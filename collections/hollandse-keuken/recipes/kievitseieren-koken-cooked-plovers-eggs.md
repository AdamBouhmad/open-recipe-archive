## Ingredients

- plover eggs
- water
- watercress or similar greens
- red radish

## Directions

1. Wash the eggs, place them in cold water, bring them quickly to a boil and let them cook for 2 minutes.
2. Then immediately place them on a small dish on which there is a folded cloth and some watercress or similar greens.
3. Serve them with red radish.
