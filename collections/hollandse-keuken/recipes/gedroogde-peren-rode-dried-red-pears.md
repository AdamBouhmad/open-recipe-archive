## Ingredients

- 300 g dried red pears
- 75 g sugar
- some berry juice
- peel of ½ lemon
- some sago

## Directions

1. Cook them as the Californian pears from the previous recipe.
2. Then add berry juice and sugar, remove the lemon peel and thicken the liquid with some mixed sago.
