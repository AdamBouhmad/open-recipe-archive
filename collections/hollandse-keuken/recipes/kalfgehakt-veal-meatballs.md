## Ingredients

- 1 to 1.5 Dutch pound ground veal
- white bread or hard biscuit soaked in milk
- 2 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Knead the ground veal with milk-soaked bread or biscuit that has been pressed out, 2 eggs, salt, nutmeg, and pepper
2. Mix well together
3. Form one or two balls
4. Braise them with butter in a pan or dish
5. Baste well
6. To thicken the jus, add a little water
7. There must be heat above and below
