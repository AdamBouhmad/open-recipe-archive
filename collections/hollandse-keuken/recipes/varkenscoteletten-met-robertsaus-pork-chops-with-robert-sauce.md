## Ingredients

- 4 pork chops
- 7.5 g salt
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 3 dl Robert sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the chops, dry them, rub them with salt and brown them in the butter or margarine which has been made light brown in the pan, cook until done (about 20-25 minutes)
2. Attach a paper frill around the bone of the chops, stand the chops against a base of toasted bread, mashed potato or rice and pour the Robert sauce over them prepared in the usual manner
