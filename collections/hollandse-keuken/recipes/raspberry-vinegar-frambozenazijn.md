## Ingredients

- Four pounds raspberries
- One bottle wine vinegar
- Three-quarters pound sugar per pound of liquid

## Directions

1. Let four pounds raspberries steep in one bottle of wine vinegar for 24 hours
2. Press them out
3. Add three-quarters pound sugar per pound of liquid
4. Let it boil together until it reaches the right thickness
5. Pour into small bottles, cork them, cover with a bladder, and store in a cool place
