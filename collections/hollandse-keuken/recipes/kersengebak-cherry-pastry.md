## Ingredients

- 2 and a half ounces butter
- 2 eggs
- 6 drams sugar
- Half a pound flour
- Candied or fresh cherries

## Directions

1. Make a dough
2. Roll out in round form and make an edge
3. Cover with candied or fresh cherries
4. After one hour of baking, sprinkle with sugar
