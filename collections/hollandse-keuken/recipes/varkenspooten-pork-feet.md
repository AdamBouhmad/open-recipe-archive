## Ingredients

- pork feet
- garlic
- parsley
- leeks
- celery
- water
- beaten eggs
- ground biscuit
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Split the feet lengthwise, roll each one individually in a cloth, which you then tie shut with twine
2. Place them in a pan with some garlic, a little parsley, leeks and celery; cover with cold water, let them cook for 5 hours, and let them almost completely cool
3. Remove the feet from the cloths and roll them in beaten eggs, ground biscuit and fried parsley
4. Roast them brown on the grill or in the frying pan
