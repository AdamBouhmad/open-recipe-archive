## Ingredients

- 2 grapefruits
- 4 slices of pineapple
- 4 candied cherries
- sugar
- Rhine wine, kirsch or sherry

## Directions

1. Cut the grapefruits across and loosen the flesh completely along the edges and membranes with a grapefruit knife
2. Remove the pits
3. Loosen the flesh with the point of the knife so that the segments can be taken out of the skin
4. Divide each slice of pineapple into 6 pieces
5. Place a chilled slice of pineapple first in a cold wide champagne glass, then the chilled grapefruit pieces sprinkled with sugar
6. If desired, pour some Rhine wine, kirsch or sherry over it and garnish with a candied cherry
