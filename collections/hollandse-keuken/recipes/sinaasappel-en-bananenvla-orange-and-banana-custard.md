## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 4 bananas
- 60 g sugar
- 1/2 l vanilla custard

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, carefully remove the white pith, cut them crosswise into slices and remove the pits. Peel the bananas, cut these also into little slices and layer the fruits with the sugar in a custard dish. Prepare vanilla custard in the usual way (recipe 657). Remove the vanilla from it, let the custard cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin from forming, and pour it over the fruits.
