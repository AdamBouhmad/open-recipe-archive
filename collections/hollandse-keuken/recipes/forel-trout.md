## Ingredients

- 1 kg trout
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, wash them and cook them in boiling water with salt, until a fin comes loose easily (+ 10 minutes), or thread a strong knitting thread through the tail and the head and pull the fish somewhat round before it is cooked.
2. Serve it with butter sauce, parsley sauce or tomato sauce.
