## Ingredients

- 2 sweetbreads
- 75 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the sweetbreads as indicated in recipe 293 for 20 minutes
2. After removing the membrane and fat, press them for a quarter hour between 2 bowls or plates with a heavy weight on top
3. Slice them diagonally and fry these slices in butter or margarine until brown and done
4. Finish the gravy with a little milk
5. Arrange the sweetbreads on a platter, pour the gravy over them and garnish the platter if desired with mashed potatoes and young vegetables of contrasting color arranged in sections around them
6. Note: the sweetbread slices can also be double-breaded before frying
