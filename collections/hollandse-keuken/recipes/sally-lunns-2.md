## Ingredients

- 350 g flour
- 7.5 g salt
- 10 g yeast
- 1 egg
- 50 g butter or margarine
- some sugar
- 2 dl lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Make bread dough in the known manner and let it rise (see recipe 835).
2. Put the dough in high cake molds that have been greased with butter or margarine or make round slices of about 3 cm thickness from it and place these on a greased baking sheet.
3. Let the batter rise for another 1/4 hour under a damp cloth.
4. Then bake them in a hot oven in 20 to 30 minutes light brown and done.
5. Cut them in half while warm, spread them, place the halves on top of each other and serve them warm.
