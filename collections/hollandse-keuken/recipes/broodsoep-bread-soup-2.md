## Ingredients

- Bouillon from beef bones
- Water
- Salt
- Bread
- Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Cook bouillon from beef bones with water and salt, taking care to skim well when it comes to a boil; let it simmer gently for four to five hours.
2. Pour the bouillon through a sieve, remove the fat and let it come to a boil again.
3. Cut some strips of bread into pieces, in proportion to the amount of bouillon, or preferably cut the bread into the soup tureen and pour the bouillon over it.
4. Add some Liebig's Meat Extract according to taste.
5. You can use stale bread very well for this.
