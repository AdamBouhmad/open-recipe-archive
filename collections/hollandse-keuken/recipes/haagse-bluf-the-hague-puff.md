## Ingredients

- 1 dl berry or raspberry juice
- 100 g sugar
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Put the berry or raspberry juice, sugar and the unbeaten egg white together in a very deep bowl.
2. Beat the mixture for about 20 minutes with a well-galvanized whisk or with silver forks until it is stiff.
