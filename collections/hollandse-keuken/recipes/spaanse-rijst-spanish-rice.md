## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 225 g rice
- salt
- 120 g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 stick vanilla or the peel of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cook rice porridge in the usual manner (see rice porridge recipe), but let the vanilla or lemon peel cook with it.
2. In a fireproof dish, stir the egg yolks smooth with half the sugar.
3. Carefully add the hot rice porridge, from which the vanilla or lemon peel has been removed and in which the remaining sugar is dissolved.
4. Stir the stiffly beaten egg white through as lightly as possible.
5. Clean the rim of the dish and let it rise and brown in a moderately warm oven.
6. Serve as soon as it comes from the oven.
