## Ingredients

- 3 dl milk
- 100 g flour
- 100 g sugar
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 vanilla stick

## Directions

1. Stir butter or margarine, flour and sugar together until all lumps disappear.
2. Gradually add the milk and cook the mixture on a moderately warm fire, stirring with a whisk, until it is done and bound.
3. Let it cool somewhat, add the egg yolks and the scraped vanilla and lastly mix as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Fill a warm pudding mold coated with butter or margarine and sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs one-third full with the mixture, close it in the usual manner and cook the pudding 2 to 2 1/2 hours in a bain-marie, but make sure the water is continuously topped up to just below the rim of the mold.
5. Be especially careful with a light pudding like this to prevent the water from boiling over when topping up.
6. Let it dry slightly without the lid.
7. Turn the pudding, which sinks quickly, onto a warmed dish and place a warmed bowl over it for transport.
8. Serve with a maraschino or white wine sauce.
