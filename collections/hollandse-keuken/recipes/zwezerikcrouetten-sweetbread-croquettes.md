## Ingredients

- 1 sweetbread
- 1/2 tin of mushrooms
- 2.5 dl stock
- 0.5 dl cream
- 35 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- pepper
- salt
- 0.5 egg or an egg white for breading
- 3 sheets of gelatin
- mace
- thyme
- cayenne pepper
- breadcrumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbread for half an hour in cold water, then place it in fresh cold water, drain when it comes to a boil and cook it until done (20 to 25 minutes) in salted water with herbs.
2. Cover it with cold water, peel it and cut it into small pieces.
3. Finely chop the mushrooms.
4. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 102, but in this case use the sweetbread stock.
5. Dissolve the soaked gelatin in it if necessary, add it to the egg yolk that has been beaten with the cream, stir in the sweetbread, mushrooms, pepper and salt to taste and let the mixture, spread on a board, become almost stiff, but ensure it is still easy to handle.
6. Shape the croquettes in the manner indicated for meat croquettes (recipe 116), bread them double and treat them further as meat croquettes (recipe 116).
