## Ingredients

- 300 g almonds or ground coconut
- 250 g sugar
- + 4 egg whites
- some scraped vanilla or grated lemon zest
- wafer papers (6 sheets)
- 30 almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Peel the soaked almonds. Grind them, mix them with the sugar, the flavoring and enough egg white until a stiff mass is obtained (as thick as puree for potato croquettes).
2. Shape small balls from this, flatten them slightly on the bottom and lay them some distance apart on a wafer paper placed on a baking sheet.
3. For almond macarons place a halved almond in the middle.
4. Bake the cookies in a moderately warm oven until light brown (20-30 minutes). Break the wafer paper away along the edge of the macarons.
