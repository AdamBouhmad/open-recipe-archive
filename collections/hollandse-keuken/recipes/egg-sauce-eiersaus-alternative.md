## Ingredients

- Two tablespoons flour
- Vinegar
- Water
- Three eggs
- Lemon peel
- Onion
- One ounce butter

## Directions

1. Stir two tablespoons flour with vinegar and water
2. Add three well-beaten eggs, lemon peel, onion, and one ounce butter
3. Stir everything together well
