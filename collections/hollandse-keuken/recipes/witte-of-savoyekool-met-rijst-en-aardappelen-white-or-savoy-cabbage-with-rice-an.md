## Ingredients

- 1 medium-sized cabbage
- ¾ kg potatoes
- 200 g rice
- 60 g fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage by cutting it into pieces and removing the stalk and hard ribs.
2. Rinse it several times and place it in a pot with enough boiling water so it is submerged three-quarters of the way, let it cook gently for half an hour.
3. Add the fat and washed rice, ensuring the rice is entirely covered by water.
4. Lay the peeled potatoes, cut into quarters, on top and let everything cook together on low heat until done and almost dry (30 to 35 minutes).
5. Add more water if needed, but ensure the stamppot does not become too moist.
6. Quickly mash everything together with a wooden spoon or masher.
7. Note: The stamppot can also be prepared without rice, but then use 1½ kg potatoes.
