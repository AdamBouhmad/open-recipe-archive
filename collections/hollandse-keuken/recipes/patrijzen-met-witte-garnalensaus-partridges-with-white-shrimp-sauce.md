## Ingredients

- three partridges
- lemon slices without skin or seeds
- thin slices of bacon
- paper
- cream and shrimp sauce

## Directions

1. Take three partridges and clean them well
2. Fill them with some slices of lemon without skin or seeds
3. Lard them (wrap them with thin slices of bacon)
4. Wrap them in paper
5. Roast them three quarters on the spit
6. Serve them with a sauce of cream and shrimp
