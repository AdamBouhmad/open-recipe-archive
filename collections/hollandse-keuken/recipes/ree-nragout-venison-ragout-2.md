## Ingredients

- Roasted venison
- Butter
- Finely chopped shallots
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut pieces from a roasted venison
2. Fry them in butter
3. Add some finely chopped shallots, nutmeg, salt and lemon juice
4. Let it stew
