## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head or 1 pork head (or ½ calf's head + ½ pork head)
- 2 kg beef or pork meat (or 1 kg beef + 1 kg pork meat)
- about 40 g salt
- pepper
- 1 grated nutmeg
- about 1½ tablespoon ground cloves
- about 1 dl vinegar
- some gherkins
- ½ to 2½ l broth in which the head was cooked

## Directions

1. Wash the head and the meat, cook the head for 5 hours in plenty of boiling water with salt, let the meat cook along for 3 hours.
2. Then carefully remove all the bones from the meat and chop or grind the meat finely.
3. Mix it with the broth until a not too stiff mass is obtained, add salt, the spices, the vinegar and if desired some chopped gherkins.
4. Put the mass in bowls or molds rinsed with vinegar up to 1 cm below the rim. Let it cool and place a properly fitting piece of white paper on it, with an opening cut in the middle.
5. Pour some vinegar on top and store the head cheese in a cool, dust-free place. Pour some vinegar on top whenever it evaporates.
