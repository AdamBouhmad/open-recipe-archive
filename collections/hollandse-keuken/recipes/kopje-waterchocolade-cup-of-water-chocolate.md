## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 teaspoon cacao
- 4 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 1/2 dl boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix the cacao with the sugar in the designated cup.
2. Stir in cold water or milk until a smooth paste without lumps is obtained.
3. Pour the boiling water over it.
