## Ingredients

- pimpernel
- chervil
- sorrel
- watercress
- tarragon
- tripmadam
- purslane
- sugar loaf
- cooked beet
- salt
- oil
- herb vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Toss with salt, oil, and herb vinegar.
