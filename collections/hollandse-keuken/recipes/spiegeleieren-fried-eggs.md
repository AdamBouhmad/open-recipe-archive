## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Break 3 or 4 eggs onto a flat earthen plate
2. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper
3. Place on a not too strong fire
4. As soon as the eggs set or coagulate, it is ready
5. Loosen with a knife to transfer them
