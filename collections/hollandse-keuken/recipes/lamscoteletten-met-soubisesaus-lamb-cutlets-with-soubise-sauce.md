## Ingredients

- 4 lamb cutlets
- 5 g salt
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- breadcrumbs for coating
- 3 dl soubise sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and salt the cutlets
2. Coat them in breadcrumbs (paneer)
3. Brown and cook them in butter or margarine until done (approximately 20 minutes)
4. Arrange them on a platter in overlapping rows (dakpansgewijs)
5. Tie a paper collar (manchetje) around each bone end
6. Cover with soubise sauce (see recipe 353)
