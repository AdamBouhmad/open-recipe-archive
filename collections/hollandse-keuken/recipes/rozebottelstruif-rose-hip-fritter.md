## Ingredients

- rose hips
- cold water
- grated stale white bread
- a glass wine
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Place rose hips in some cold water
2. Let them cook into a pulp
3. Add grated stale white bread, a glass wine, butter, cinnamon and sugar to taste
4. Stir well together
5. Bake it like any other fritter
