## Ingredients

- 4 kg beef, for example pieces
- 400 g bacon
- 50 g salt
- 4 nutmegs
- 70 g ground cloves
- 20 g pepper
- tripe

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, cut it into small pieces or grind it (preferably not too fine). Cut the washed bacon into cubes, mix the meat with the salt, the bacon and the spices and let it stand overnight.
2. Rinse the tripe well on the inside and outside in lukewarm water, sew it into bags with coarse thread and fill these not too tightly with the meat.
3. Sew the tripe closed, prick it here and there to prevent bursting and cook the stuffed tripe for 2 hours in plenty of boiling water with salt and a few cloves.
4. Let it cool in a colander and store it in a Cologne pot under ¼ of the liquid in which the stuffed tripe was cooked and ¾ vinegar. Pour a little fat on top and put the rest in a Cologne pot and keep it to fry the stuffed tripe in later.
