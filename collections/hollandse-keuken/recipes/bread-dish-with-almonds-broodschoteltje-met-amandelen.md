## Ingredients

- 12 thin slices of old bread without crust (200 g)
- 4 1/2 deciliters milk
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 100 g almonds or other nuts
- 75 g sugar
- fine cinnamon

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, peel them, grind them in an almond mill until fine.
2. Soak the bread in boiling milk, rub it smooth, add the egg yolks, the sugar, the almonds and lastly lightly the stiffly beaten egg white to it.
3. Put everything in a fireproof dish, smooth the top, sprinkle some fine cinnamon and sugar over it and let the dish brown and rise in a moderately warm oven.
