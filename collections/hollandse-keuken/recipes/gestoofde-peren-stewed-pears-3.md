## Ingredients

- 1 kg stewing pears
- approximately 50 g sugar
- 3/4 dl berry juice (optional)
- peel of 1/2 lemon or piece of cinnamon stick
- some sago

## Directions

1. Peel the pears thinly, cut them in half or quarters if they are large and remove the cores.
2. Leave small pears whole, but in this case peel the stems thinly.
3. Wash them and set them on with enough boiling water so they are half submerged, with the lemon peel or cinnamon stick.
4. Let them cook gently, shaking occasionally, for 3 to 4 hours.
5. If the pears do not cook red, add berry juice and sugar half an hour before they are done.
6. If the fruits do cook red, let only some sugar cook with them for the last 1/4 hour.
7. Remove the flavoring agent and bind the liquid with some mixed sago.
