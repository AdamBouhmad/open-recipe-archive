## Ingredients

- green cucumbers
- Spanish pepper
- bay leaves
- fennel
- horseradish
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the whole green cucumbers in a butter brine for 14 days
2. Let them drain well and add Spanish pepper, bay leaves, fennel and horseradish
3. Put them in an earthenware pot and seal it well with a bladder
