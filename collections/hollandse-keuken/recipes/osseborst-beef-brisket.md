## Ingredients

- Beef brisket
- Salt
- Water
- Flour or ground rusks
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Rub the brisket well with salt and cook it in water with a little salt until done
2. When cooked, cut it into pieces and place it in a pan with some of the cooking liquid
3. Bind the liquid with flour or ground rusks
4. Add a lump of butter, some fine salt, nutmeg, pepper and a bit of lemon peel
5. Braise it for half an hour with fire above and below
