## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg potatoes
- water
- 60 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel small round potatoes and remove the eyes; cut large ones into quarters and wash them.
2. Set them to cook with the butter or margarine, salt and little water in a well-closed pan on low heat (approximately 30 minutes).
3. If the liquid has not evaporated, it can be drained and reserved for a sauce.
4. Let the potatoes dry without a lid, shaking occasionally.
