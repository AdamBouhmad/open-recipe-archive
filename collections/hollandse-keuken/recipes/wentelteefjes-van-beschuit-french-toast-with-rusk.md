## Ingredients

- 12 small, round rusks
- 2 eggs
- 4 dl milk
- 40 g sugar
- 60 g butter or margarine
- some fine cinnamon, grated lemon zest or scraped vanilla

## Directions

1. Prepare the wentelteefjes as described in the previous recipe, but soak the rusks briefly as they break easily.
