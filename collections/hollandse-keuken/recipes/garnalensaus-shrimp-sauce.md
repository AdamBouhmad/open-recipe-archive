## Ingredients

- 1/2 l fish bouillon or salted water
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- piece of mace
- piece of Spanish pepper
- small carrot
- sprig of parsley
- 1 egg
- 100 g shrimp

## Directions

1. Rinse the shrimp with salted water and let them drain well
2. Let the herbs steep in the fish bouillon
3. Pour through a sieve and prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
4. Warm the shrimp in the sauce
5. Blend to taste with pepper, salt and the beaten egg
6. Serve this sauce with sole fillets, turbot etc.
