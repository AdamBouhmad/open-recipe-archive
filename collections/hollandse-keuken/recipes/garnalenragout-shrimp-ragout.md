## Ingredients

- 250 g shrimp
- 1 1/2 dl milk
- 1/2 dl cream
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- salt
- finely chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon Maggi's Aroma
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way.
2. Rinse the shrimp with salted water, carefully search for impurities, warm them by hanging them in a sieve or colander over hot water.
3. Then stir them through the sauce.
4. Mix this to taste with some pepper, salt, parsley and Maggi's Aroma and carefully add it from the heat to the beaten egg yolk.
5. Fill the pastries with this mixture.
