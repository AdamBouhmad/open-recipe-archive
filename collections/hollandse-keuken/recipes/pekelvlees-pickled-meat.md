## Ingredients

- 3 kg meat
- 250 g salt
- 10 g saltpeter
- 50 g brown sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the meat with the mixture of salt, saltpeter and sugar and pack it firmly in a small tub or in a large bowl.
2. Turn the meat over regularly and store it for at least 3 weeks before using it.
