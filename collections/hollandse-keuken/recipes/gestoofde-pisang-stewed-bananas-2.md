## Ingredients

- 6 pisangs
- 2½ dl water
- juice of 1 lemon
- 60 g sugar
- a splash of Rhine wine (optional)
- some sago

## Directions

1. Peel the pisangs, cut them lengthwise in half, then cut each half into 4 pieces.
2. Bring the water with the lemon juice to a boil, lay the pieces in it and let them cook until completely soft (7 to 10 minutes).
3. Remove them carefully from the liquid, add the wine to the liquid and thicken it with some mixed sago.
4. Pour it over the pisang and serve this cold.
