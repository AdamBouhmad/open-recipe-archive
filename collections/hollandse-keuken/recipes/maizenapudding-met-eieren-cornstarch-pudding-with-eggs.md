## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 50 to 60 g cornstarch (Duryea brand)
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 70 g sugar
- 1/2 vanilla stick, vanilla sugar, or the zest of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the flavoring.
2. Beat the eggs and mix with the cornstarch and sugar until a thin batter without lumps.
3. Add enough of the boiling milk so that the eggs are thoroughly warmed.
4. Pour everything back into the pan and let the mixture, stirring with a whisk, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes.
5. Remove the flavoring, pour the pudding into a mold rinsed with cold water and let it become cold and firm.
6. Turn it out onto a wet plate and serve with a fruit juice sauce or stewed fruit.
