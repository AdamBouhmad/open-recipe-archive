## Ingredients

- Beef slices
- Flour
- Butter
- Onions, finely chopped
- Salt
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Pound the slices well tender, roll them in flour and roast them brown in butter.
2. Then place them in a pan with finely chopped onions, salt and cloves, and let them stew for about an hour.
