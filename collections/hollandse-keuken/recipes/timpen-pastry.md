## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces flour
- 3 beaten eggs
- A little grated lemon peel
- Ground cinnamon
- Pieces of sukade
- 1.5 ounces butter
- Equal weight water

## Directions

1. Stir the flour, beaten eggs, grated lemon peel, cinnamon, and sukade pieces into the butter and water, previously brought to a boil, until a dough
2. Form figures as desired and let them bake on a buttered plate
