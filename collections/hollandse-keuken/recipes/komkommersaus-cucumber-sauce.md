## Ingredients

- 4 cucumbers
- water
- butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take 4 cucumbers, cut them into round slices, not too thin, and press them out between linen
2. Put water and butter in a pan and bring to a boil
3. When it boils well, add the cucumbers and stir constantly so it doesn't burn
4. Add some spoonfuls of bouillon
5. Remove the cucumbers and cook the sauce a bit longer
6. Add the cucumbers back and remove from heat
