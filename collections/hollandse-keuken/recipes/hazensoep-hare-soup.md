## Ingredients

- leftover hare
- 1 l lean hare stock with water
- a couple of chopped carrots
- 1 piece Spanish pepper
- 50 g onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cloves
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- Cayenne pepper to taste
- red wine or English sauce and soya

## Directions

1. Let the hare cut into pieces infuse gently (1 to 1 1/2 hours) in the hare stock with the herbs and enough water added so that it is not too salty
2. Strain this and add it to the melted butter or margarine which has been brought to a boil together with the flour while stirring
3. Let the soup simmer while stirring for 10 minutes
4. Add the hare meat cut into small equal pieces and if desired some Cayenne pepper
5. Stir in the wine or English sauce and soya away from heat
