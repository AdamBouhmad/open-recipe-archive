## Ingredients

- 200 g almonds
- 200 g powdered sugar
- 1 small egg white
- a few drops of lemon juice
- optionally some rose or orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Peel the soaked almonds, wash and dry them and grind them in the almond mill.
2. Mix them with the sifted powdered sugar, the unbeaten egg white, the drops of lemon juice and the rose or orange blossom water.
3. Mill this mass through a mill (with the finest blade) and catch the first portion separately, because it may have a less beautiful white color.
4. Knead the coming fine, white dough thoroughly together and let it rest 24 hours in a damp cloth.
5. Then the marzipan can be processed in various ways. For example, add some orange essence through the mass, if small root vegetables are to be made.
6. To make small potatoes, roll round balls of the dough through some cinnamon and prick it here and there.
7. Color some portions for example with cacao powder or with plant colorants.
8. Roll each portion into a sheet on a table dusted with some powdered sugar and stick them together with some water.
9. Roll them together once more and cut or stamp cookies from them.
10. Garnish them with a half hazelnut, almond or walnut.
