## Ingredients

- Two and a half ounces rice
- Juice of 6 lemons
- Peel of one lemon, cooked until soft
- Strips of citron peel
- Sugar
- Tart crust

## Directions

1. Cook two and a half ounces rice very well, without stirring
2. Add the juice of 6 lemons, the peel of one lemon cooked until soft
3. Add strips, citron peel and sugar
4. Put it in a tart crust
5. Bake for half an hour
6. Let it cool in the pan
