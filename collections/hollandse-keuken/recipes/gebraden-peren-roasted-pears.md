## Ingredients

- pears
- cinnamon
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Take pears, peeled or unpeeled as desired
2. Set them upright and tightly in a dish
3. Sprinkle them with cinnamon
4. Let them roast in the oven with some butter and water added
5. Or roast them with charcoal fire from above and below
