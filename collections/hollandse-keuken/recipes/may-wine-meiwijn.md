## Ingredients

- A bunch of blooming and washed Lady's Bedstraw
- One and a half ounces sugar
- One bottle Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Put the Lady's Bedstraw and sugar in a terrine
2. Pour a bottle of Rhine wine over it
3. Let it steep for one hour
4. Taste to see if the herbs have steeped enough
5. Serve the drink in glasses with the green leaves in it
6. You can also add slices of Chinese orange, one in each glass
7. If you want the wine to have a stronger herb flavor, let the herbs steep longer
8. The wine must be drunk cold
