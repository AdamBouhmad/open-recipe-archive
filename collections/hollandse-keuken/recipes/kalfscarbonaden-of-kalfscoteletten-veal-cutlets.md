## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg carbonades or cutlets (4 pieces)
- 75 g veal fat, butter or margarine
- 1 lemon
- 7 1/2 g salt

## Directions

1. See the previous recipe for preparation.
2. Note: Cutlets or loin carbonades have only small pieces of backbone attached, while rib carbonades have additionally a long bone, a rib.
