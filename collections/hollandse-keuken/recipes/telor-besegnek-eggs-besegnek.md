## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled halved eggs
- salt
- 2 tablespoons finely ground onions
- 1 1/2 spoon laos
- 1 1/2 teaspoon djienten
- 3 teaspoons ketoembar
- approximately 1 1/2 dl milk with salt or coconut milk
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Fry the onions, add the spices
2. Stir the mass well together
3. Add some milk with salt or coconut milk and let the sauce become bound
4. Pour it over the halved, hard-boiled eggs, which have been sprinkled with some salt
