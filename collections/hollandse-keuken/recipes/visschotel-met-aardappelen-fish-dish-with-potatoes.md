## Ingredients

- 350 g leftover cooked fish
- 3/4 kg cooked potatoes
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 lemon
- leftover butter sauce or milk
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. FIRST METHOD: Remove the fish (as warm as possible) from the bone, mash it finely, mix it with the finely mashed potatoes, moisten the mixture with some leftover butter sauce or milk, add salt if needed, put everything in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water, sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, place a few small pieces of butter and a few notched lemon slices on it and let the dish brown in the oven.
2. SECOND METHOD: Remove the fish (as warm as possible) from the bone, lay it in pieces in the middle of a fireproof dish, pour some water with salt and lemon juice over it, place a few small pieces of butter and a few notched lemon slices on it. Lay a border of mashed potatoes around it, made from the cooked potatoes, approximately 3 dl milk, 20 g butter or margarine, some pepper, salt, nutmeg. Sprinkle breadcrumbs over the dish and let it brown in the oven with a few small pieces of butter on it.
3. THIRD METHOD: Slice the potatoes and lay them layer by layer with the fish in a fireproof dish. Moisten each layer with some sauce or milk, drizzle some lemon juice here and there over the fish. Ensure the top layer consists of potatoes. Sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, place some butter or margarine and a few notched lemon slices on it and let the dish turn light brown in the oven.
