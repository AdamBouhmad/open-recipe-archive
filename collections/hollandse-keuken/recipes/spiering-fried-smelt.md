## Ingredients

- 40 smelt
- 1.5 dl butter oil or salad oil
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the smelt by cutting off the head and removing the innards
2. Check carefully that there are no smelt with worms
3. Wash them and coat them in flour mixed with salt
4. Lay the smelt with heads toward the outside and tails toward the middle like a cake in oil that is hot enough to produce blue steam
5. Fry the cake, shaking it occasionally, until light brown and cooked through
6. Turn the cake over using the help of a lid
7. Let the smelt drain on porous paper
