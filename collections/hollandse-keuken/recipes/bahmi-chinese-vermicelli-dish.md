## Ingredients

- 20 pieces of mie = approximately 500 g
- 4 spoons ground onions
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 teaspoon ginger powder
- 200 g pork cut into small cubes
- 100 g peeled shrimp
- 100 g sliced ham
- pepper
- 3 bunches celery
- 2 pieces leek
- 3 eggs
- 3 spoons water
- salt
- soy sauce
- lemon
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash the mie and let them soak in warm water for 3 hours and then drain in a colander
2. Slice them into pieces
3. Make broth from the meat cut into small cubes with some salt, about 4 dl
4. Remove the meat
5. Cook the cleaned and finely chopped vegetables in the broth until almost done
6. Pound the spices with the ground onions finely
7. Fry them
8. Then add the meat and the sliced ham and let this brown
9. Add this mass to the vegetables; add the mie and the shrimp and let this all stew in the broth (approximately 15 minutes)
10. Fry an omelet in the known manner
11. Slice this into strips
12. Put the bahmie in a heated dish and garnish it with omelet strips
13. Serve soy sauce or ketjap and eighth pieces of lemon with it
