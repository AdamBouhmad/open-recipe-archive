## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 200 g rice
- 100 g sugar
- 1/2 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon peel
- 200 g apricots
- 50 g sugar
- sago

## Directions

1. Cook thick rice porridge in the usual way (see recipe 577), but let the vanilla or lemon peel cook along with it.
2. When the rice is almost cooked, add the sugar, and before serving remove the vanilla or lemon peel.
3. Press the rice porridge as firmly as possible into a rice mold, which must be buttered when the mixture is to be turned out while warm, but is rinsed with water when turned out cold.
4. Add the stewed apricots, which are prepared in the usual way (see recipe 541).
