## Ingredients

- 500 g spinach
- 1 l water or bouillon
- 35 g butter or margarine
- 35 g flour
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash the spinach well and let it cook until done with little water. Rub it through a sieve and dilute it with water and salt or bouillon.
2. Melt butter or margarine, bring it to a boil while stirring with the flour, add the liquid in small amounts at a time while stirring.
3. Let the soup cook approximately 10 minutes and finish it with some milk.
