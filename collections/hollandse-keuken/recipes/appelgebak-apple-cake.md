## Ingredients

- 2 bowls thick applesauce
- 10-15 cents worth grated white bread
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 ounce melted butter
- cinnamon
- sugar
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 2 bowls thick applesauce and stir through 10-15 cents worth grated white bread, 8 egg yolks, 1 ounce melted butter, cinnamon and sugar
2. Beat the egg whites until foamy and add
3. Bake for more than 3 quarters of an hour in a mold
