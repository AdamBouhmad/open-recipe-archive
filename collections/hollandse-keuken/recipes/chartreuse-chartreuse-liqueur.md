## Ingredients

- 1 l brandy
- 400 g white sugar
- 1/2 g saffron
- peel of 2 lemons (very thinly peeled)
- 12 to 14 cardamom seeds

## Directions

1. Peel the cardamom seeds, take out the seeds from them, put them with all other required ingredients in a well-closing bottle.
2. Close it and let the liqueur stand for at least 4 weeks, stirring occasionally.
