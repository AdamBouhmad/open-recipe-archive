## Ingredients

- Veal shoulder
- Flour
- Aromatic herbs
- Mushrooms
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Let the shoulder soak in water beforehand, then roll it in flour and fry it in the pan
2. When brown, let it braise in an earthenware dish
3. When almost done, add some aromatic herbs and mushrooms
4. Finish it with some vinegar and chopped onion
5. Serve with pepper sauce
