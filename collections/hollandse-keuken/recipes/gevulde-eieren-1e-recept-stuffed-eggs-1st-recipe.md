## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 50 g butter or margarine
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs hard (8 to 10 minutes), place them in cold water, peel them, cut them crosswise in the middle and remove the yolks
2. Cut off the tips of the half whites so they can stand
3. Rub the yolks completely fine, gradually add the butter or margarine beaten to cream and as much mustard, pepper, chopped parsley and salt as needed until a well-flavored mixture is obtained
4. Fill the half whites with this so that the yolks stick up a bit and the shape of a whole egg is obtained
5. Place the eggs on the plate
6. Note: Filling is easiest by piping the mixture with a piping bag with fluted nozzle into the half whites
