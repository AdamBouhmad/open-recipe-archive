## Ingredients

- Veal knuckle
- Ground veal or pork
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut open the knuckle at the end and fill it with ground meat
2. Bind it closed again
3. Smear it with butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Brown the butter, add the knuckle with heat from above and below
5. Baste it continuously with its own juices and let it braise for about 2 hours
