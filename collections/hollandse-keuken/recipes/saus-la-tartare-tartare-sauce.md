## Ingredients

- 1 spoon parsley
- 1 shallot
- anchovy without the spine
- mixed pickles
- lemon juice
- water
- 6 spoons salad oil
- pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Chop a spoon of parsley, one shallot, anchovy without the spine and mixed pickles, each separately, very finely
2. Pour the juice of a lemon over it, as well as a spoon of water
3. Add 6 spoons of salad oil and a little pepper
4. Mix everything well together
5. Just before serving, add a spoon of mustard
