## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- bouillon
- 1 egg
- potato starch
- herbs to taste

## Directions

1. Cut cauliflower into small pieces and cook them
2. Let them cook further in bouillon, but do not let them become too soft
3. Drain them and place them in the soup tureen
4. Stir an egg in some cold water with a spoon of potato starch
5. Add this with desired herbs to the boiling bouillon
6. Stir well and pour it over the cauliflower
