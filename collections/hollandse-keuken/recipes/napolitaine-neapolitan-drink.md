## Ingredients

- juice of 8 oranges
- juice of 2 lemons
- peel of 1 lemon
- 4 dl berry juice
- 200 g sugar
- 14 dl water

## Directions

1. Let the lemon peel steep in the water and dissolve the sugar in it.
2. Let this liquid cool, then add all the fruit juice and pour it several times through a flannel cloth until it is as clear as possible.
3. Pour it into well-cleaned bottles, close them with a clean cork and keep it no longer than 8 days.
