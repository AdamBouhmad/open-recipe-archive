## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Salt
- Parsley
- Sorrel
- Butter
- Flour
- Lemon peel
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the veal with some salt in water until done
2. Place the meat on a dish and pour the following sauce over it: take equal amounts of parsley and sorrel, a lump of butter rolled in flour, some finely cut lemon peel and grated nutmeg
3. Let it cook until it becomes a bound sauce
4. Veal ribs or kidney piece can be treated the same way
