## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg potatoes
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the potatoes in cold water for some time, then brush them off
2. Scrape them with a knife, as much as possible under water, until the skin is off
3. Wash them, set them on with little boiling water and some salt (10 g per 1 water)
4. Let them cook until they can be pierced with a fork easily, but not too easily (approximately 20 minutes)
5. Drain them as dry as possible, let them dry on the fire without a lid, shaking occasionally to prevent burning
