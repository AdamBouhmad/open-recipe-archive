## Ingredients

- 12 small herrings
- 12 pickled gherkins
- 12 pickled onions
- 2 Spanish peppers
- 4 bay leaves
- 10 cloves
- 6 g mustard seed
- 3 onions
- 4 sliced lemon slices
- 6 deciliters good vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak small herrings for 24 hours in water with some milk, refreshing as needed
2. Cut off the heads and tails, remove the skin, cut open along the belly and remove the entrails
3. Remove the bones and small bones
4. Split the opened herrings lengthwise down the back in two, wash them and roll each one up with a gherkin or onion inside
5. Fasten them with a sharply pointed stick and arrange them in a well-cleaned bottle
6. Cook the vinegar with the cloves, bay leaves, one chopped onion, the Spanish pepper and the mustard seed bound in a piece of cheesecloth for a moment
7. Arrange lemon slices, onion slices and Spanish pepper alternately between the fish and the glass
8. Pour the lukewarm vinegar over and add enough vinegar so the fish is completely covered
9. Close the bottle with damp parchment paper and let it stand for at least 6 days
10. Do not store longer than 3 weeks
