## Ingredients

- parsley roots
- celery roots
- leeks
- leaf parsley
- white bread
- water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Let parsley and celery roots with leeks and leaf parsley, as well as cubes of white bread cook well done in a pan
2. Pour enough water over it now
3. Stir salt and butter through it
4. Let everything cook through thoroughly once more and serve it
