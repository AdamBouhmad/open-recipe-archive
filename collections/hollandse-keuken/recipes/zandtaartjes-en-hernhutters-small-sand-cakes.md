## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- 300 g flour or 50 g cornstarch and 250 g flour
- 150 g brown sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour or flour mixture into a bowl
2. Add the butter or margarine and brown sugar
3. Knead all ingredients together until no sugar granules are felt
4. Coat small molds (for Hernhutters use very small fluted molds) with melted butter or margarine
5. Fill them 3/4 full with the dough
6. Place on a baking sheet and bake in a moderately warm oven in the middle position until very lightly brown and done (approximately 20 minutes)
7. Test for doneness by inserting a larding needle, meat fork or knitting needle, which should come out dry
8. If the cakes brown too much during baking, cover with a piece of gray paper brushed with butter
9. Let the cakes cool and turn the molds over
10. Dust the cakes with powdered sugar after cooling
