## Ingredients

- 1 kg sauerkraut
- 40 g butter, margarine or fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the sauerkraut, place it in a pot with enough boiling water so it is about half covered.
2. Let it cook, stirring occasionally, until tender and dry (approximately 1 hour).
3. Braise it with the butter, margarine or fat (approximately 10 minutes).
4. Serve it with mashed potatoes, bacon or sausages.
5. Note: When braising the sauerkraut with lard or beef fat, it is best to let it cook together for half an hour. If you have made the sauerkraut yourself, washing it, which causes some of the sour taste to be lost, is naturally unnecessary.
