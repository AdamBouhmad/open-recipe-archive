## Ingredients

- light bouillon
- piece of mace
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 teaspoon curry
- 10 g onion
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 dl cream
- white Bordeaux wine
- 1 tin of mushrooms or small pieces of calf's head

## Directions

1. Make bouillon in the usual way with the spices, strain it, let it cool and carefully skim off the fat.
2. Melt the butter or margarine and bring flour, onion and curry to the boil while stirring, rub out all lumps, gradually add the bouillon and let the soup cook while stirring until done (10 minutes).
3. Pour it through a sieve to the egg yolk, which has been beaten with the cream and white Bordeaux wine.
4. Stir in the liquid from the mushrooms and the mushrooms, either whole or cut in half, or the pieces of calf's head.
