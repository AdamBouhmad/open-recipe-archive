## Ingredients

- 1 kg Kasseler rib (cured and lightly smoked pork rib)
- Butter or margarine
- Spanish sauce
- Cognac

## Directions

1. Method 1: Treat the rib as for Hamburger rib. Serve with melted butter or margarine.
2. Method 2: Brown the cleaned rib in butter or margarine (50 g per 500 g) and cook until done (½ hour per 500 g). Serve with Spanish sauce mixed with some cognac.
