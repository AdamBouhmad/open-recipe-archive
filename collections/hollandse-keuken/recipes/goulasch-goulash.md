## Ingredients

- 500 g meat (e.g. lamb)
- Salt
- 2 onions
- 35 g flour
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 2 to 3 tablespoons thick tomato puree
- 50 g fat, butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small cubes.
2. Shake it through the flour, which is mixed with salt.
3. Heat the fat in the roasting pan and fry the meat in it, shaking occasionally, until it begins to color.
4. Add the chopped onions and let them become light brown.
5. Add paprika and tomato puree to the meat and add enough water so that the meat is almost covered.
6. Let the goulash stew gently for 1 1/2 hours covered, stir the dish occasionally and add a little more water if necessary.
7. Serve goulash with mashed potatoes or dry rice.
