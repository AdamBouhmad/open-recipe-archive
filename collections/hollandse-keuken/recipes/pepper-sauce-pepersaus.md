## Ingredients

- Roasted bread
- Bouillon
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Ginger

## Directions

1. Soak roasted bread in bouillon
2. Add vinegar, wine, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and ginger
3. Let it all boil together briefly
