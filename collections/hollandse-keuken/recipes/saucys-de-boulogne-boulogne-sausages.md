## Ingredients

- lean beef
- lean pork
- bacon
- salt
- coarse ground pepper
- wide intestines

## Directions

1. Take equal parts lean beef and lean pork with bacon
2. Chop very finely together
3. Add salt and coarse ground pepper
4. Stuff this meat into wide intestines
5. Lay them in salt for two days
6. Smoke them for 10 days
