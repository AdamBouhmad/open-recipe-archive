## Ingredients

- 3 kg cherries
- Sugar syrup (500 g sugar per 1 liter water)

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries, wash them and fill the well-cleaned bottles with them up to 3 cm below the rim.
2. Pour enough sugar syrup over them so that the cherries are just submerged.
3. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clips and heat them for 20 minutes to 80° C.
4. Let the bottles cool with the clips on, remove the clips and label the bottles with name and date.
5. (For corked bottles see the rules on page 366).
