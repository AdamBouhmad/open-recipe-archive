## Ingredients

- stewed spinach
- cream sauce
- stuffed eggs
- potato purée

## Directions

1. Make a flavorful stewed spinach and place it in the bottom of an ovenproof dish (preferably glass).
2. Pour over it a layer (at least 1 cm thick) of cream sauce.
3. Make this dish in the oven or on the stove when it is to be used.
4. Prepare stuffed eggs beforehand, 2 halves per person.
5. When serving, place the stuffed eggs in the cream sauce and serve potato purée (which was heated in the oven at the same time) with this dish.
