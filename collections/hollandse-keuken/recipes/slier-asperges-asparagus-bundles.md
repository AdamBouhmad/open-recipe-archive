## Ingredients

- bunches of asparagus
- water
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the bottom ends of whole bunches of asparagus
2. Cook them in water with salt until done
3. Let them drain
4. When serving, sprinkle grated nutmeg over the heads and serve with melted butter
