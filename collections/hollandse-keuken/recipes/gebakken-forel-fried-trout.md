## Ingredients

- 1 kg trout
- 75 g butter or margarine or 1 dl salad oil
- breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, leave them whole but give the fish a couple of incisions
2. Treat them further as you would sole (recipe 214)
