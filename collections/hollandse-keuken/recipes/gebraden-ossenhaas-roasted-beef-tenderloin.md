## Ingredients

- Beef tenderloin (ossenhaas)
- Morels sauce

## Directions

1. Lard a beef tenderloin and roast it over a good fire.
2. Make a sauce of morels with it, as described in the article on ragouts of morels, but somewhat thinner.
