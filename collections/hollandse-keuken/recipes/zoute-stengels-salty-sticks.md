## Ingredients

- 50 g hard butter or margarine
- 50 g flour
- 5 g salt
- some water
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846), but mix it with some extra salt.
2. Roll it out the last time to 1/4 cm thickness and cut strips 10 cm in length and 1/2 cm in width.
3. Brush these strips with egg yolk that has been beaten with some water, lay them on a baking sheet and bake them in a hot oven light brown and cooked through (approximately 10 minutes).
