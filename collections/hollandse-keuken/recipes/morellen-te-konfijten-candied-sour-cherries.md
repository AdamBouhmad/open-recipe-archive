## Ingredients

- 5 ounces sour cherries
- three-quarters pound sugar
- berry juice
- water

## Directions

1. Cut off the stems at the top
2. Set the sugar with some berry juice and water on the fire, let it cook until the sugar has melted
3. Then add the sour cherries and let them candy gently
