## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 90 g ground rice
- 75 g sugar
- ¼ stick vanilla, vanilla sugar or ½ lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare the pudding as semolina pudding. Like semolina pudding, ground rice pudding can also be prepared with eggs and almonds. For quantity and preparation see the recipes of semolina pudding.
