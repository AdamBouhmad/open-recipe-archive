## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces wheat flour
- 1 cup groat flour
- 4 eggs
- Half water and half milk
- A little yeast
- 5 ounces currants

## Directions

1. Beat together the wheat flour, groat flour, and 4 eggs with half water and half milk thinly
2. Add a little yeast at the end
3. Add 5 ounces currants through it
4. They should not be baked larger than a guilder
