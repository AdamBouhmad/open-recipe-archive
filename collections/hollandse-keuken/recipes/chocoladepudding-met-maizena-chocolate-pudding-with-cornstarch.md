## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 75 g cornstarch (Duryea brand)
- 50 g Blooker's cocoa
- 100 g sugar
- half a vanilla stick or vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Stir together the cornstarch, cocoa and sugar and mix them with a portion of the cold milk until a thin batter without lumps.
2. Bring the remaining milk with the vanilla slowly to a boil, pour in the batter while stirring and let the mixture, stirring vigorously, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Remove the vanilla, pour the pudding into a mold rinsed with cold water and let it become cold and firm.
4. Turn it out onto a wet plate and serve with vanilla sauce.
