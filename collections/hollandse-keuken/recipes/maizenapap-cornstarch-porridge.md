## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 l milk
- 75 g cornstarch Duryea
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch gradually with some cold milk to a thin paste.
2. Bring the remaining milk with the salt to a boil, pour in the mixed cornstarch while stirring before the milk actually boils and let the porridge cook thoroughly while stirring (3 to 4 minutes).
