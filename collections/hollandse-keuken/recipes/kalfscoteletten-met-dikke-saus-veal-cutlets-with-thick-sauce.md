## Ingredients

- thick veal cutlet
- water
- bouillon
- flour
- butter
- morels (optional)

## Directions

1. Take a thick veal cutlet and be careful not to pound it too flat
2. Lard it very finely
3. Cook it in a pan with water and bouillon
4. Mix flour with bouillon and butter
5. Make a thick sauce with or without morels as desired
