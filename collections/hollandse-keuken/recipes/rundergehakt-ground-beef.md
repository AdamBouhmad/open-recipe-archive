## Ingredients

- Beef
- Kidney fat
- White bread without crust
- Sweet milk
- An egg
- Nutmeg
- Fine salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Mince the beef finely with some kidney fat
2. Knead in white bread without crust that has been soaked in sweet milk and squeezed dry, along with an egg
3. Add nutmeg and fine salt
4. Make one or more balls and braise them brown in butter with fire above and below
