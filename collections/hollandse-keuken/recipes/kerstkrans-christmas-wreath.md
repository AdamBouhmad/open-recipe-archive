## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter or margarine
- 100 g flour
- some water
- salt
- 125 g sweet almonds mixed with 4 bitter almonds
- 125 g sugar
- 1 egg
- some grated lemon zest or scraped vanilla
- candied fruits, orange strips and water glaze for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846), roll it out one last time to a thickness of 1/4 cm and width of 12 cm. Peel the soaked almonds, grind them twice with the sugar, egg and flavoring through the almond mill. Form small rolls from this almond paste on a table dusted with a little flour, approximately 2 cm thick. Place these against each other in the middle of the dough, wrap the dough around them and seal the ends with water. Place the dough with the sealed side down on the baking sheet, form it into a wreath and seal the ends together. Bake the wreath in a hot oven until light brown and cooked (approximately 20 minutes). Let cool, garnish with water glaze, candied fruits and orange strips.
