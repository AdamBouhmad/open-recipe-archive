## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 150 g brown sugar
- juice of 4 oranges and 1 lemon (together 2 dl liquid, otherwise make up with water)
- 10 g gelatin with 1 leaf red

## Directions

1. Prepare the pudding entirely as lemon pudding in the previous recipe.
2. Fill the hollowed-out orange peels with it as shown in the illustration.
