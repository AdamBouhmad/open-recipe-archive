## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- 150 g butter or margarine
- 10 large sour apples
- salt

## Directions

1. If not already done, remove the tear gland.
2. Wash the goose thoroughly inside and outside, salt it and fill it with chopped sour apples.
3. Close the rear, roast it as you would turkey (recipe 322).
4. Note: One can also fill a goose with chestnuts (see turkey) or with soaked dried prunes.
