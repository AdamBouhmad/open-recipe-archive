## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked potatoes
- approximately 4 dl milk
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper

## Directions

1. FIRST METHOD: Push warm cooked potatoes through a ricer or squeeze them through a potato press. If you do not have these tools, warm the potatoes in the boiling milk, for example 5 minutes, and mash them with a potato masher or make them fine with a fork.
2. Mix the purée with the milk, pepper, butter or margarine, salt and nutmeg to taste.
3. Let the purée become warm on the fire, stirring well.
4. SECOND METHOD: Make the potatoes fine as indicated above, mix them with the milk, half of the melted butter, salt, pepper and nutmeg to taste.
5. Ensure that the purée is not too dry, place it in an ovenproof dish, smooth the top, sprinkle some breadcrumbs through a sieve over it, place small pieces of the remaining butter here and there, clean the edge of the dish and let a brown crust form in a moderately warm oven.
