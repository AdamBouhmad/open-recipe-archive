## Ingredients

- Three or four partridges, cleaned
- Liver from partridges or other birds
- Bacon
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Pastry crust of approximately three pounds

## Directions

1. Cut the legs and wings off the cleaned partridges
2. Make a farce (forcemeat) from liver, bacon, parsley, salt and pepper, mixing everything well together
3. Fill the partridges with this farce
4. Take a pastry crust of approximately three pounds
5. Make an opening in the lid
6. Bake in the oven for approximately three hours
7. Allow to cool
8. Close the hole in the lid
