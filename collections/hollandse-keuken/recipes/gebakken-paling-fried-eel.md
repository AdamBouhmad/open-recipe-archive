## Ingredients

- thick eel
- salt
- butter
- crushed biscuit
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take thick eel, score it, cut it into pieces
2. Let sit in salt for some time and dry them
3. Spread the pieces with butter and sprinkle with crushed biscuit mixed with pepper
4. Fry them slowly in the pan until done
