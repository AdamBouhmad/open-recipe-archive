## Ingredients

- 12 herrings
- 20 white peppercorns
- 3 bay leaves
- 100 g onion
- 10 cloves
- 1 Spanish pepper
- a few notched slices of lemon
- about 1 l good vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings in water with some milk for 24 hours, refreshing it if necessary.
2. Cut them open on the belly side, remove the intestines, but leave the head and skin on.
3. Wash them well on the inside and outside.
