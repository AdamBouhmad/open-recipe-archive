## Ingredients

- 1/4 l salted water or fish bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 25 mussels

## Directions

1. Cook the mussels in the usual way (recipe 195) and remove them from their shells
2. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
3. Blend over gentle heat with the mussels and serve with sole
