## Ingredients

- apples
- quarter can Rhine wine
- quarter kilo sugar
- lemon skins
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and core them
2. Cook the skins and cores for half an hour in water
3. Strain and set the obtained juice with a quarter can Rhine wine, a quarter kilo sugar, lemon skins and cinnamon
4. As soon as this boils, add the apples and let them cook until soft
5. This should not take long, as they must remain whole
6. Remove the cooked ones with a spoon
