## Ingredients

- 2 and a half ounces flour
- 1 and a half ounces butter
- 5 egg yolks
- Half an ounce sugar
- Zest of one lemon, grated
- Milk
- Yeast
- Egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Make a batter with flour, butter, egg yolks, sugar, lemon zest and milk
2. Add yeast and beaten egg whites
3. Bake in mold until done
