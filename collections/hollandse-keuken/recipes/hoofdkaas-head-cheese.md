## Ingredients

- calf's head or calf's cheek
- pork head
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg
- cold water
- molds or small bowls

## Directions

1. Cook a calf's head or calf's cheek and a pork head until so done that the bones fall out
2. Remove the skin, membrane and bones
3. Chop it finely with salt, pepper, cloves and nutmeg
4. Rinse the molds or small bowls with cold water
5. Fill with the prepared mixture
