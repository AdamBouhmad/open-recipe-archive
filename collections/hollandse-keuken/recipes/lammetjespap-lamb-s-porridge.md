## Ingredients

- 1.25 l milk
- 100 g flour
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Mix the flour gradually with some cold milk into a thin paste, but ensure there are no lumps in it.
2. Bring the remaining milk with salt to a boil, pour in the mixed flour while stirring and keep stirring until the porridge is done (5 to 10 minutes).
3. Serve with brown sugar or syrup.
