## Ingredients

- Beef
- Savoy cabbage
- Salt
- Butter sauce
- Grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Handle the meat as described for boiled beef above.
2. When the meat is half cooked, add the cabbage with a little salt and let it cook until done.
3. When serving, transfer the cabbage to a colander, let it drain, and place it on a dish with butter sauce and some grated nutmeg over it.
4. Serve the meat separately.
