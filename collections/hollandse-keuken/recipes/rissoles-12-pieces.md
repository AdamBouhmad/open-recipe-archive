## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter or margarine
- 100 g flour
- some water
- salt
- ragout or jam for filling

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846). Roll it out one last time into a large, thin sheet and cut out circles with a diameter of 8-10 cm. Place some jam in the middle, fold them in half, seal the edges with a little water, brush with egg yolk beaten with a little water. Place them on a baking sheet and bake in a hot oven placed in the middle, light brown and cooked (15-20 minutes). Remove from baking sheet and let cool.
2. Alternatively: Make circles of dough as described above. Place some ragout in the middle, fold in half, seal the edges with water. Fry the rissoles in frying fat with blue vapor rising from it, until light brown and cooked (approximately 5 minutes). Let them drip briefly on gray paper, place them on a warmed dish with a finger cloth on top.
