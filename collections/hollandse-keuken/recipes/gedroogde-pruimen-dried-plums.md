## Ingredients

- 400 g dried plums
- 60 g sugar
- peel of ½ lemon
- some berry juice
- some sago

## Directions

1. Wash the plums several times with lukewarm water, let them soak for 24 hours in just enough cold water to cover them.
2. The next day, bring them with the same water and the lemon peel to a boil and let them cook gently until tender (20 to 30 minutes).
3. Add the berry juice and sugar and carefully thicken the liquid with some mixed sago.
4. If no berry juice is added, use less sugar.
