## Ingredients

- 250 g rice
- 1/2 kg rhubarb
- 6 dl water
- 150 g sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the rice until the water that runs off is completely clear.
2. Let the rice drain well.
3. Set it on with the water and some salt and bring it to a boil on a brisk fire.
4. Add the peeled rhubarb, cut into chunks and washed.
5. Let everything cook gently until done.
6. Stir the sugar through quickly with a wooden spoon, without breaking the grains.
