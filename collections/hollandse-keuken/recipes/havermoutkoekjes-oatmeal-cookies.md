## Ingredients

- 150 g oatmeal
- 3 dl water
- 50 g flour
- salt
- 50 g sugar
- 75 g butter or margarine
- fine cinnamon or grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Soak the oatmeal in water for 1 hour.
2. Add the flour, sugar, flavoring, and if necessary more water until a fairly thick batter is obtained.
3. Heat the butter or margarine in a frying pan.
4. Fry small round cookies from this batter until light brown and cooked through on low heat.
5. Serve them warm with brown sugar.
6. Note: one may optionally add 1-2 eggs and more flour.
