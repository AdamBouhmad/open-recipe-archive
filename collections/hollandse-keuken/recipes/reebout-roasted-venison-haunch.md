## Ingredients

- 1 kg venison haunch
- 50 g larding bacon
- 150 g butter or margarine
- some salt
- 1/2 dl sour cream (can also be replaced by yoghurt)

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, lard it and rub it with some salt.
2. Make the butter or margarine light brown in the roasting pan, turn the venison haunch through it and roast it 25 to 30 minutes per 500 g.
3. Baste it from time to time with the hot fat, add some water if necessary.
4. Pour the cream over it a quarter of an hour before it is done.
5. Finish the gravy with water.
6. Serve with chestnut purée and berry jelly, apple compote or lingonberry compote.
