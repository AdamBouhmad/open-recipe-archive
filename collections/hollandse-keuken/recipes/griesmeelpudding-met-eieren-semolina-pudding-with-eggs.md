## Ingredients

- ¾ l milk
- ¼ stick vanilla, vanilla sugar or peel of ½ lemon
- 65 g sugar
- 75 to 85 g semolina
- 1 to 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook semolina porridge in the usual manner with the sugar and flavoring.
2. After removing the vanilla or lemon peel, add it vigorously stirring to the beaten egg yolks.
3. Mix the mixture as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white and pour it into a mold rinsed with cold water.
4. Turn the pudding out onto a moist plate after cooling and serve fruit juice sauce or stewed fruit with it.
