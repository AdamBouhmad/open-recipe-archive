## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- flour and egg white
- chocolate cream

## Directions

1. Carefully blow out twelve eggs
2. Close one hole (with flour and egg white) and let it dry well
3. Fill the empty shell with chocolate cream as described later
4. After closing the second hole, place the eggs in boiling water to thicken for a considerable time, but do not let them boil
5. Clean them well and serve in a folded napkin
