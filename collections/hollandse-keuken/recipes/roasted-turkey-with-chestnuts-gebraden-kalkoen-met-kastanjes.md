## Ingredients

- turkey
- turkey organs
- veal
- bacon
- five ounces chestnuts
- salt

## Directions

1. Have the poulterer remove the bones and return them open
2. The stomach, heart, and red liver, all cooked, are finely chopped together with some veal and bacon to serve as stuffing
3. Remove five ounces of chestnuts from their shells and skin, and roast them in hot ashes
4. Lay the turkey on its breast
5. Sprinkle the inside with some salt and put the chestnuts and stuffing mixed under them
6. Sew the turkey closed on the back and roast it with continuous basting
