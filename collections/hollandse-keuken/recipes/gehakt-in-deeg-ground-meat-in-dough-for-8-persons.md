## Ingredients

- 400 g ground veal
- 300 g ground pork
- pepper, salt and nutmeg to taste
- 300 g flour
- 3 eggs
- 150 g butter or margarine
- 50 g yeast
- 1 1/2 dl milk
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put the flour in a bowl, make a small well in the middle, add the yeast and stir from the middle gradually the lukewarm milk, the melted butter or margarine and the eggs, which have been beaten with salt.
2. Rub out the lumps from this batter and let it rise covered (1/2 hour to 3 quarters of an hour).
3. Roll it out on a floured sheet, lay the ground meat mixed with the seasonings on it and roll it in firmly.
4. Give it the shape of a loaf or place it in a bread mold brushed with butter.
5. Bake it on a baking sheet in a fairly warm oven light brown and done (about 3 quarters to 1 hour) and serve it hot with curry sauce or cold.
