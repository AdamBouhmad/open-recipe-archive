## Ingredients

- 1 savoy cabbage
- 2 hectograms lean bacon
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take a savoy cabbage, remove the outer leaves as they are usually tough, cut the cabbage into 4 pieces.
2. Wash it in plenty of water.
3. Let it come to a boil for 10 minutes with 2 hectograms of lean bacon in boiling water.
4. Subsequently place it for one hour in plenty of cold water.
5. By this treatment you will make the cabbage tender.
