## Ingredients

- 1 kg eel
- 75 g butter or margarine
- lemon
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. METHOD 1: Clean the eel in the usual way (see cooked eel recipe 190), wash the pieces and rub them with pepper and salt. Place the butter or margarine in small lumps in the fireproof dish, sprinkle some breadcrumbs over it and lay a few thin slices of lemon on top. Let the eel in a moderately warm oven, first covered, then without a lid, cook and brown (25-30 minutes). If you do not have an oven, braise the eel in a flat braising pan over a gentle fire. Baste the eel repeatedly with a little of the liquid.
2. METHOD 2: First cook the cleaned eel in boiling salted water (approximately 5 minutes, depending on the thickness of the eel). Then place the pieces in the fireproof dish and treat them further as braised fish (recipe 219).
