## Ingredients

- leg of suckling lamb
- salt
- pepper
- crushed rusk
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare as the ordinary leg of lamb
2. It can also be roasted after rolling it in fine salt, pepper and some crushed rusk
3. Place a small lump of butter at the bottom of the pan
