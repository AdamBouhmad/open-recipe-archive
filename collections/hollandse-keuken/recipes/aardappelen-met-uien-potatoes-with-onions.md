## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- onions
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. In a dish, lay first a layer of sliced raw potatoes with salt and pepper, or nutmeg
2. Then a layer of onions cut into slices
3. Then again slices of potatoes, until the pan is full
4. Add butter and milk
5. Let it stew well with fire below and above
6. When the sauce cooks down, add more
