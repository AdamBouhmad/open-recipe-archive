## Ingredients

- 100 g hard butter or calf kidney fat
- 100 g flour
- salt
- water (approximately 1/2 dl)
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry (feuilletée) in the usual manner (see recipe 846)
2. Roll out the dough the last time to a thickness of approximately 1/2 cm
3. Cut out 3 circles using a cutter form with a diameter of 10 to 12 cm
4. Cut lids from 2 of the circles with a diameter of 8 to 10 cm
5. Flatten one circle, lay it on the baking sheet and brush it with egg yolk beaten with a little water
6. Glue the remaining rings to the largest circle with a little water or egg white
7. Prick the bottom of the formed pastry here and there with a larding needle to prevent too much rising
8. Place a few paper wads in the pastry to support the edges as it rises upward
9. Brush only the top ring with the yolk beaten with water
10. Bake the pastry and lid in a hot oven until light brown and cooked through (15 to 20 minutes)
11. Do not look at it during the first 5 minutes, as there is a risk of it sinking
12. Remove the pastry and lid from the baking sheet, hollow out the inner uncooked part somewhat, fill it with ragout and place the lid on top
