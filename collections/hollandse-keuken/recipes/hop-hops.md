## Ingredients

- hops
- cold water
- bouillon
- butter
- flour
- nutmeg
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the hops to the length of a finger
2. Cover them with cold water and cook until done
3. Let them drain
4. Stew them for another quarter hour in bouillon with butter, flour, nutmeg and lemon juice
