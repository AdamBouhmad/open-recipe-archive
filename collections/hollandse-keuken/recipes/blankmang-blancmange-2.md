## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces rice
- 1.5 ounces bread sugar
- 1 quart sweet milk
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook in a new pan with constant and vigorous stirring 2.5 ounces rice, 1.5 ounces bread sugar and 1 quart sweet milk until it is thick
2. Grate lemon peel through it
