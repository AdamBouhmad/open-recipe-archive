## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liter buttermilk
- 175 g rice or more
- salt
- 1/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Wash the rice until the water that runs off is completely clear.
2. Set it on with 1/4 liter water and salt and bring to a boil.
3. Gradually add the buttermilk and let the porridge cook until done and thickened (approximately 1 1/2 hours).
4. Serve this porridge with syrup.
