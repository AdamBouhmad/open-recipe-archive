## Ingredients

- ½ apple
- ½ banana
- ½ orange
- 4 figs
- 1 tablespoon ground hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Cut the well-cleaned apple into thin slices. Cut the peeled banana and orange into slices. Wash the figs in hot water and cut them through. Peel the hazelnuts and grind them. Mix the fruits together and sprinkle the ground nuts over them.
