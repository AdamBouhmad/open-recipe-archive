## Ingredients

- 5 ounces green plums
- three-quarters pound sugar
- pomegranate water

## Directions

1. The plums must first be heated with pomegranate water and sugar, but well skimmed, then set aside; do this about three times, until they are green
2. Then lay the plums on a colander, covered with vine leaves
3. Then cook the syrup, and when it is thick enough, pour it cold over the plums
