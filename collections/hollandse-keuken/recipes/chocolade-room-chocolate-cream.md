## Ingredients

- 1 can cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 ounces chocolate
- 5 ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the cream, sugar and eggs together
2. place on fire, stirring constantly, and let it reduce to a quarter
3. add the finely powdered chocolate
4. strain through a sieve
5. serve when cold
