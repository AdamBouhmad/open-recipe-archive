## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 0.5 l cream
- 0.5 l milk
- 200 to 240 g sugar
- 1 vanilla pod

## Directions

1. Let the vanilla pod infuse slowly in the milk.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar for a long time until they are thick, white and fluffy.
3. Add the milk, which has been mixed with the cream, to the egg mixture and heat the whole until it is thick enough to coat the wooden spoon.
