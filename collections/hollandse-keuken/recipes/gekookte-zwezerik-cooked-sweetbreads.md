## Ingredients

- 2 sweetbreads
- salt
- mace
- cayenne pepper
- piece of carrot
- parsley
- fleurons
- 4 dl egg sauce, lemon sauce, or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Place the sweetbreads in cold water for 1/2 hour
2. Blanch them: bring to a boil with cold water, drain when it boils, then cover again with cold water with mace, cayenne pepper, carrot piece, parsley sprig and salt
3. Let them cook until done (20 to 30 minutes)
4. Rinse them with cold water, remove the fat and membrane, and slice them
5. Braise the slices briefly in the sauce prepared in the usual way (see the recipes)
6. Arrange on a platter and garnish with fleurons (recipe 847)
