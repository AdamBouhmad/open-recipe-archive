## Ingredients

- 2 sweetbreads
- curry powder (optional)
- 9 tomatoes
- 400 g ground veal
- 50 g stale bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 75 g rice
- 3 dl tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the sweetbreads in the usual way (see recipe 293)
2. Peel them, press them between 2 plates and slice them diagonally
3. Arrange them in the shape of a sweetbread on a base of cooked rice, which may be mixed with curry if desired
4. Cover with tomato sauce prepared in the usual way (see recipe 340)
5. Garnish with stuffed tomatoes (recipe 131)
