## Ingredients

- 1.5 l milk
- 100 g oatmeal
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil, stirring occasionally.
2. Sprinkle the oatmeal in while stirring.
3. Let the milk come to a boil again while stirring and then cook the porridge on a gentle fire, stirring occasionally, until the oatmeal is cooked and the porridge is evenly bound (at least 20 to 25 minutes).
4. For quick-cooking oatmeal, let it cook for 5 minutes and use 1.25 l milk.
