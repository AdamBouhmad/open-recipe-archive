## Ingredients

- herring
- fresh parsley or red geranium flower

## Directions

1. Make an incision along the back of the herring, cut open the belly side, carefully remove the intestines, pull off the skin.
2. Cut the fish down the middle to the head, remove the bone and place the herring in its original form on a small plate.
3. Insert a sprig of fresh parsley or a red geranium flower in the mouth.
