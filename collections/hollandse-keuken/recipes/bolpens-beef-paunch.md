## Ingredients

- 15 pounds beef
- 4 ounces salt
- 4 drams cloves
- 1 ounce pepper
- 4 nutmegs
- water
- vinegar
- fat

## Directions

1. Soak the paunch in water overnight
2. Mince or cut the beef not too finely
3. Mix thoroughly with salt and spices
4. Cut the paunch into square pieces and sew into pouches
5. Fill pouches with meat, not too tightly, and sew closed
6. Hang in cold water and boil continuously for 3 hours, ensuring they remain submerged
7. Let drain and press under a weighted board until cold
8. Place in an earthenware pot with half vinegar and half of the cooking liquid
9. Cover with a slate or pour a layer of fat over the top
