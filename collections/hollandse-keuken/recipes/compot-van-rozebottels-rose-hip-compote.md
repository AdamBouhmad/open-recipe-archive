## Ingredients

- dried rose hips
- cold water
- raisins
- sugar
- lemon peels
- cinnamon
- Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Soak dried rose hips in cold water beforehand
2. Set them on with an equal amount of raisins, sugar, lemon peels and cinnamon, in half water and half Rhine wine
3. Cook them until soft, but only reduce them so the juice still covers them, though it should not be too thin
