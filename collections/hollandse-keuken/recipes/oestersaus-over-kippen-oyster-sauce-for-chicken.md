## Ingredients

- 3 dozen oysters
- water
- butter
- flour
- parsley, chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Take 3 dozen oysters and boil them briefly
2. Remove the oysters as soon as the water boils
3. Let them drain
4. Put them in a pan with water, butter, flour and the water in which the oysters were cooked
5. Add 1.5 ounces butter
6. If desired, add chopped parsley
