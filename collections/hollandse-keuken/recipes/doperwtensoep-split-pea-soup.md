## Ingredients

- 500 g old shelled peas or a packet of frozen peas
- 1 l water with 4 Maggi bouillon cubes or light bouillon
- some parsley and chervil
- 1 dl cream or milk
- 40 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cook the washed peas until done in the water with the cubes or the bouillon and the parsley and chervil
2. Pour the liquid through a sieve, rub the peas through and bring it to a boil while stirring
3. Let the soup cook until it is evenly bound, skim off the foam if necessary and add the butter or margarine and the cream or milk
4. Serve with diced brown fried bread
