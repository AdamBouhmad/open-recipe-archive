## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dl berry juice
- 2 1/2 dl water
- 75 g sugar
- 20 g sago or potato starch
- a piece of lemon peel or cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Bring the berry juice with the water, the very thinly pared lemon peel or the cinnamon stick to a boil
2. Mix the sago or potato starch with a little cold water into a thin paste
3. Pour this into the boiling liquid while stirring and let the sauce simmer while stirring briefly
4. Add the sugar afterwards
5. Remove from heat and adjust the flavour
6. Serve the sauce warm with cooked rice; cold with semolina or cornstarch pudding etc.
