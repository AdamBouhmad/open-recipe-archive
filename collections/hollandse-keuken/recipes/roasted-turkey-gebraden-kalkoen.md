## Ingredients

- turkey
- fine salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the turkey well
2. Remove the breastbone
3. Sprinkle the inside with fine salt, nutmeg and pepper, and the outside only with salt
4. Brown some butter in the pan, lay the turkey in it
5. Let it roast until done with heat under and above
6. Take care to baste it well continuously
