## Ingredients

- Flour
- Butter
- Calf juice
- Red wine
- Peeled truffels cooked in Madeira wine
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry some flour brown in butter
2. Add calf juice, red wine, and peeled truffles cooked in Madeira wine
3. Add the juice of a lemon
4. Let it boil once, stirring constantly
