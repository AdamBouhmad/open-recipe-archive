## Ingredients

- chopped beef ribs
- water
- salt
- carrots
- onions
- potatoes, pre-cooked

## Directions

1. Cook the chopped beef ribs until done in water with some salt
2. Add carrots and some onions and let them cook until tender
3. When serving, add the pre-cooked potatoes
