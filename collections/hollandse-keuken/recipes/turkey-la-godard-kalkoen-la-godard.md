## Ingredients

- fat turkey
- bouillon
- butter
- white sauce
- shrimp

## Directions

1. Take a fat turkey, clean it well
2. Cook it in bouillon and butter, as a fricandeau
3. When done, serve it pouring a white sauce with shrimp over it
