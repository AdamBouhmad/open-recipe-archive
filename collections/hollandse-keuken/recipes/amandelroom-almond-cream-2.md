## Ingredients

- six ounces sweet almonds
- one can good milk
- five ounces sugar
- water
- egg whites from 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Take six ounces sweet almonds and peel them
2. Pound them finely with a little water
3. Beat the milk with the egg whites from 2 eggs and the sugar
4. Place the mixture on a gentle fire and let it cook
5. When it has almost reduced to three quarters, add the almonds
6. Let everything cook together briefly and strain through a sieve
7. Let it cool
8. Place on a plate and arrange almonds around the edge
