## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 120 to 150 g powdered sugar
- 60 g flour
- 60 g Duryea cornstarch
- some vanilla pulp

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the powdered sugar until a smooth mass.
2. Add the vanilla pulp and gradually the sifted flour and cornstarch.
3. Mix this batter with a little of the very stiffly beaten egg white and then add it as lightly as possible to the remaining stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Put the batter in a greased cake pan, dusted with powdered sugar and flour, and bake the cake in a fairly warm oven until light brown and done (20 to 40 minutes).
5. Cover if necessary during baking with a greased piece of paper.
6. Test if it is done by inserting a knitting needle, which should then come out dry.
7. Let the cake cool on a cake rack and garnish it after cooling with icing and French fruits.
