## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 slices of old cheese
- 35 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Fry the slices of cheese briefly in the butter or margarine, break the eggs over it, fry them as described in recipe 375.
2. Place them on a heated plate and serve them with pepper and salt.
