## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or bouillon
- piece of mace
- bay leaf
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 35 to 45 g flour
- 35 to 45 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- juice of approximately 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Let the spices steep in the water or bouillon
2. Strain this, prepare sauce from it in the usual way
3. Carefully add the sauce to the beaten egg, mix it with the lemon juice and serve with cooked chicken, calf poulette, calf's or beef tongue, artichokes, etc.
