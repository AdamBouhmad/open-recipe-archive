## Ingredients

- 500 g cod or turbot
- 5 dl fish stock
- salt and pepper
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 2 egg yolks
- splash of cream
- breadcrumbs for coating
- 2 egg whites
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Use firm, flat fish such as turbot or cod
2. Cook the fish in the usual manner
3. Remove the skin and take the fish from the bones in large pieces
4. Make a thick sauce in the known manner but use no vinegar, using butter or margarine, flour, fish water, cream and egg yolks, ensuring the sauce is so thick that it adheres to the fish
5. Roll the fish pieces through the sauce on all sides and let them cool
6. Double coat them (breading as in recipe 116) and fry them in hot frying fat or salad oil until brown
7. Let them drain on gray paper
