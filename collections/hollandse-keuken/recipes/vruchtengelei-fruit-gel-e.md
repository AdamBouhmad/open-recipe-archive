## Ingredients

- 1 liter fruit juice (oranges with mandarins, pineapple juice, apricot juice, etc.)
- 35 g white gelatin or 30 g powdered gelatin
- sugar to taste
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare the fruit juice with sugar, soaked and squeezed gelatin and egg whites in the known manner (see recipe 555).
2. Fill small molds with the obtained gelée.
3. Turn the small puddings out after cooling and present them with custard sauce or with whipped cream and sugar or pour the gelée into a ring mold, see illustration.
