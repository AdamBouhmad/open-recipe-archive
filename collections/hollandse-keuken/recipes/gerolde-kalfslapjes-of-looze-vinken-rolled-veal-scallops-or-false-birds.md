## Ingredients

- finely cut strips of kidney fat
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- crushed hard biscuit
- thin veal scallops
- lemon peel
- onions
- finely cooked parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the kidney fat strips with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and crushed hard biscuit
2. Place these in thin veal scallops along with a piece of lemon peel, onions, and finely cooked parsley
3. Roll them up and bind them securely
4. Brown butter in a pan
5. Roll the scallops in crushed hard biscuit
6. Place them in the pan and baste them continually with their own jus
7. They will be ready in about an hour
