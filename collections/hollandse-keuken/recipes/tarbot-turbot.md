## Ingredients

- 2 portions of turbot or 1 whole turbot of approximately 1 kg
- water
- salt
- small splash of vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare according to the method for brill (recipe 174)
2. When cooking turbot whole, prepare as for plaice (recipe 173)
3. Cook for approximately 15 to 20 minutes
