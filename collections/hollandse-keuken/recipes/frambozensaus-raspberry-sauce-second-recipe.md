## Ingredients

- 200 g raspberries
- 4 egg yolks
- 125 g bastard sugar
- 4 dl white or Rhine wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the same manner as strawberry sauce (recipe 759).
2. Present it with warm semolina, rice pudding etc.
