## Ingredients

- 4 slices of casino bread without crust
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 1/2 dl milk
- 15 g butter or margarine
- 15 g flour
- chopped parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Halve the slices and fry them light brown on both sides in the butter or margarine
2. Chop the hard-boiled eggs finely
3. Mix them with plenty of salt
4. Make a sauce in the usual way from butter or margarine, flour, milk and the very finely chopped parsley
5. Stir the finely chopped eggs through
6. Spread this mixture on the slices so that it rises somewhat higher in the middle
7. Place the bread slices on a warmed dish on which a folded napkin lies
8. Garnish the dish with a tuft of curly parsley
9. Present the bread slices with spinach or serve at the coffee table
