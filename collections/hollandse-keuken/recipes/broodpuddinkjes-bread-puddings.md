## Ingredients

- 150 g old bread
- 100 g bastard sugar
- 75 g butter or margarine
- juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, add the sugar, lemon zest and juice and the grated or finely made old bread.
2. Fill sand-tart molds greased with butter and sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs with this mixture.
3. Place the puddings on a baking sheet and bake them in a moderately warm oven until light brown.
4. Turn them out and serve with lemon sauce.
