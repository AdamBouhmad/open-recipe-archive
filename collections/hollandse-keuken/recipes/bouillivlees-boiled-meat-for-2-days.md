## Ingredients

- 1 kg boiling meat for example breast
- approximately 1 1/2 liters boiling water
- pair of bay leaves
- piece of mace
- small carrot
- sprig of parsley
- some salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, place it in boiling water with the spices and some salt.
2. Let it simmer gently for 5 hours, turning it several times during cooking and serve it with an egg or mustard sauce, for which the bouillon of the meat is used.
3. Slice the meat thickly before serving.
