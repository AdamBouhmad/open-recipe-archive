## Ingredients

- 250 g meat remains
- 2½ dl milk, broth or jus with water
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine or 18 g Duryea cornstarch
- fine parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Chop or grind the meat finely.
2. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring to a boil while stirring, add the liquid in small portions while stirring and keep stirring until the sauce is well cooked and done (10 min.) or make a cornstarch sauce (see recipe 329).
3. Mix it then with the meat and the seasonings.
4. Fill the dampened shells with this mixture to about 3/4 full.
5. Sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, place a few pieces of butter or margarine on it, clean the edge of the shells with a damp cloth or brush and let them become light brown in a moderately warm oven.
