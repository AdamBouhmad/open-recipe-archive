## Ingredients

- bouillon made from 500 g beef shank meat
- small carrots
- peas
- small pieces of cauliflower
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make fragrant bouillon from the meat in the usual way.
2. Strain it through a flannel or double-folded linen cloth so that all fibers are removed.
3. Clean the vegetables, divide them into small pieces or cut the carrots with a fancy knife.
4. Wash them and cook them in some boiling salted water or in some bouillon until tender.
5. Let them drain in a colander and warm them in the bouillon.
6. Just before serving, stir in the very finely chopped parsley.
