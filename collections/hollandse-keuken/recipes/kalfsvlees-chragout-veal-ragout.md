## Ingredients

- Cooked or roasted veal
- Finely chopped onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Cloves
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut cooked or roasted veal into small cubes
2. Fry some finely chopped onions with butter and flour until done
3. Add bouillon, salt, cloves and some lemon juice
4. Let it stew well with the meat
