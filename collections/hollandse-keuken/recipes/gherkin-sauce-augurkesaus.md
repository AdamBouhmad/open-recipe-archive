## Ingredients

- Gherkins
- Bouillon
- Vinegar
- Shallots
- Fried butter
- Salt
- Bay leaves

## Directions

1. Peel the gherkins, remove the seeds, and cut them into pieces
2. Cook them until soft in bouillon with vinegar, shallots, fried butter, salt, and bay leaves
