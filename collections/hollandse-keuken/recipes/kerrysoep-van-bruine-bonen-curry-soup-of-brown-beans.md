## Ingredients

- 1 l brown bean liquid with remaining brown beans
- approximately 20 g flour
- approximately 20 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste
- 3 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 50 g onion
- 1 teaspoon curry

## Directions

1. Let the remaining beans cook fine in the bean liquid to which cloves, bay leaves, and Spanish pepper have been added
2. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve and rub the beans through until only the skins remain
3. Brown the flour and curry in the butter or margarine that has turned brown, add the chopped onion and cook everything together evenly brown
4. Add the bean liquid to this and let the soup cook for approximately 10 minutes while stirring
5. Pour it through a sieve into the soup tureen and serve with bread cut into dice and fried in butter
