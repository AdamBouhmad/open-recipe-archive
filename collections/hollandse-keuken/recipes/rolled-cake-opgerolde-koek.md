## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 50 g sugar
- 40 g flour
- 15 g cornstarch
- jam or marmalade

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar until it is completely finely ground.
2. Add the sifted flour.
3. Beat the egg white stiff and mix it as lightly as possible with the remaining dough.
4. Spread the dough onto a piece of buttered parchment paper, which is placed on a greased baking sheet and projects a little above all sides.
5. Let the cake bake in a fairly warm oven until light brown and cooked through (at least 10, at most 15 minutes).
6. Test if it is cooked by seeing if it easily comes away from the paper.
7. Meanwhile place a piece of grease-free paper on the table. Sprinkle some sugar on it and turn the cake with the brown side onto it.
8. Pull off the paper on top and then spread the light side as quickly as possible with some thin jam or marmalade, which if necessary has been warmed slightly.
9. Roll the cake as quickly as possible, cut the sides straight or diagonally and after cooling sprinkle the cake with powdered sugar or garnish it if desired with glaze and candied fruits or candied strips.
