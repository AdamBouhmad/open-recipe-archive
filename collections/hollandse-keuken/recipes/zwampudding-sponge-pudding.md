## Ingredients

- 3 dl milk
- 100 g flour
- 100 g sugar
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 vanilla pod

## Directions

1. Stir butter or margarine, flour and sugar together until all lumps are gone.
2. Add the milk gradually and cook the mixture on moderate heat, stirring with a whisk, until it is done and bound.
3. Let it cool slightly, add the egg yolks and the scraped vanilla and last as lightly as possible the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Fill a warm pudding mold coated with butter or margarine and sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs 1/3 full with the mixture, close it in the usual manner and cook the pudding 2 to 2 1/2 hours in a bain-marie, but make sure the water is continuously topped up to just below the rim of the mold.
5. Be especially careful with a light pudding like this so that the water does not boil over when topping up.
6. Let it dry slightly without the lid.
7. Turn the pudding, which sinks quickly, onto a warmed dish and place a warmed bowl over it for transport.
8. Serve with a maraschino or white wine sauce.
