## Ingredients

- six eggs
- three full tablespoons white sugar
- four bitter cookies, finely made
- one spoon potato flour
- a little salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Take six eggs and separate the yolks only
2. Add to the yolks three full tablespoons white sugar and four bitter cookies finely made
3. Add one spoon potato flour and a little salt
4. Mix everything together
5. Prepare a mold and grease it with butter
6. Beat the egg whites, and when they become stiff, mix them with the yolks
7. Place in the mold, but do not fill it completely
8. Place in a not too hot oven
9. When cooked, turn it out onto a plate
10. It should be good in color and tremble
