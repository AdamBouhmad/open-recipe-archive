## Ingredients

- kroepoek
- cooking oil or frying fat
- table salt

## Directions

1. Heat cooking oil or frying fat in a frying pan
2. Dip a small quantity of kroepoek in the hot fat, where they will expand greatly
3. Turn the kroepoek if necessary and let them fry on the other side
4. Ensure they become cooked but remain light in color
5. Let the crispy kroepoeks drain on grey paper
6. They can be kept crisp for some time in well-sealed tins
7. Sprinkle with table salt for serving
