## Ingredients

- Millet
- Milk or water
- Dried prunes
- Butter
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- White wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook millet with three times as much water or milk as millet
2. Cook some dried prunes with water, butter, sugar and cinnamon until half-cooked to use as a sauce alongside
3. For taste, you can also add a little white wine to the sauce
