## Ingredients

- 125 g hard butter or margarine
- 125 g flour
- approximately 1.5 dl water
- salt
- 125 g sweet almonds mixed with 4 bitter almonds
- 125 g sugar
- 1 egg
- some grated lemon zest or vanilla pulp
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846), roll it out the last time to a thickness of 1/4 cm and a width of 10 to 12 cm.
2. Make almond paste (see recipe 850).
3. Form small rolls from this almond paste on a lightly floured sheet approximately 2.5 cm thick.
4. Lay these next to each other in the middle of the dough, fold the dough around it and seal the ends with some water.
5. Lay the dough with the sealed side down on the baking sheet and shape it into the desired letter form.
6. Brush the letter with egg yolk that has been beaten with some water and bake it in a hot oven light brown and cooked through (approximately 20 minutes).
7. Remove it from the sheet and let it cool.
