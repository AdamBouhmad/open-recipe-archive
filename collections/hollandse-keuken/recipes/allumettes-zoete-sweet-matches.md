## Ingredients

- 50 g hard butter or margarine
- 50 g flour
- some water
- salt
- approximately 1 egg white
- 100 g powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846).
2. Roll it out to a thickness of 1.25 cm to a layer approximately 10 cm wide.
3. Brush this with egg white glaze (see recipe 820), cut cookies 1.5 cm wide from it and bake these in a warm oven light brown and cooked through (approximately 15 minutes).
4. Remove them from the baking sheet immediately.
