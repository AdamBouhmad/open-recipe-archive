## Ingredients

- Anchovies
- Water
- Water or bouillon
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the anchovies
2. Let them freshen in water
3. Chop them finely
4. Cook them with water or bouillon
5. When done, press them through a sieve
6. Add pepper, vinegar, and butter
7. Bind the sauce by beating in a couple of egg yolks
