## Ingredients

- 1 large or 2 small lobsters
- 2 lettuce heads
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Bring plenty of water to a boil in a large pan, immerse the lobsters in it, add plenty of salt and cook the lobsters according to their size for 20-30 minutes.
2. Remove them from the water, remove the green part, the gills, stomach and intestines.
3. Extract the meat from the body and claws by cutting these open.
4. Place the lobster with the head on a pedestal of toasted bread, placed in the middle of a large platter.
5. Arrange around it some cleaned, washed and well-drained lettuce leaves, place the pieces of lobster on these and pour or spray some mayonnaise between them.
6. Present lettuce and mayonnaise separately, or make a salad from it.
