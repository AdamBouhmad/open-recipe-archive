## Ingredients

- crumb of white bread
- water
- salt
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take crumb of white bread and cook it very gently for an hour in a small iron pot with water, salt and a little butter, according to the quantity desired
2. When it is ready, stir two, three or more eggs through it, but take care not to let it cook with the eggs
