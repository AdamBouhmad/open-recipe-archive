## Ingredients

- 5 hectograms green peas or split peas
- 5 liters water
- celery and leek
- salt
- Liebig's Meat Extract
- pork knuckle or salted sausage (optional)

## Directions

1. Take 5 hectograms of green peas or if you prefer split peas, let them cook in 5 liters of water until they are well done and become fine.
2. Take celery and leek, adjusting the proportion according to which vegetable you prefer more flavor in. Wash them well in cold water after cleaning them thoroughly, chop them finely and let them cook for about a good hour in water until they are well done.
3. Rub the cooked peas through a sieve and add them with the cooking liquid to the vegetables, add salt to taste and some Liebig's Meat Extract. If you want to enhance the flavor with a little pork, you can use a pork knuckle or salted sausage which you let cook together with the peas.
