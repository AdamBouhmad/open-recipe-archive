## Ingredients

- 1 kg cut beans
- 400 g large white beans
- 400 g smoked sausage
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the cut beans as indicated for salted vegetables.
2. Cook the beans in the usual way (recipe 493).
3. If desired, add the washed smoked sausage half an hour before serving, or prepare it separately in boiling water.
4. Mix the cut beans and white beans together and serve the sausage on the side.
