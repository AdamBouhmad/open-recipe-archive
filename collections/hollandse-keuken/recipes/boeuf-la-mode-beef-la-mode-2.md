## Ingredients

- Beef rump
- Kidney fat or bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Breadcrumbs, pounded
- Bacon slices
- Wine vinegar
- Water
- Bay leaves
- Lemon slices
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Lard a piece of the rump with kidney fat or bacon, rolled beforehand in salt, pepper and cloves, and dust the meat itself also with salt, pepper and pounded breadcrumbs.
2. Then place bacon slices at the bottom of a pan, put the meat on top of them, and add a glass of beer of wine vinegar with water, some bay leaves, slices of lemon and some butter.
3. Close the lid tightly and set the pan early in the morning on low heat.
4. When done, stir some fried flour into the pan to bind the sauce.
