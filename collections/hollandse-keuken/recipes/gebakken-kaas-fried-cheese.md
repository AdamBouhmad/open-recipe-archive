## Ingredients

- 4 niet te dunne plakken kaas a 50 g per stuk (4 not too thin slices of cheese, 50 g each)
- 60 g boter of margarine (butter or margarine)
- 1 eiwit (egg white)
- paneermeel (breadcrumbs)

## Directions

1. Cut the crust off the cheese.
2. Beat the egg white loose with one spoon of water.
3. Pull the slices through the egg white, let them drain well and pull them through breadcrumbs once more.
4. Fry the slices in the butter or margarine, which has been made dark brown, quickly brown on both sides.
5. Finish the jus in the usual manner.
6. Serve fried cheese as hot as possible.
7. Serve the fried cheese with cauliflower, salsify, leek.
