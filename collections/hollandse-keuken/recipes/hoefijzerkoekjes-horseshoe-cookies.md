## Ingredients

- 175 g flour
- 150 g butter or margarine
- 150 g powdered sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- some scraped vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream the butter or margarine until fluffy, add the sifted sugar and the egg yolks one by one.
2. Mix this batter with the sifted flour and make small rolls on a sheet dusted with a little flour, 1/2 cm thick and 10 cm long.
3. Arrange them in the shape of a horseshoe on a baking sheet greased with butter or margarine, making sure there is some space between them.
4. Brush the cookies with beaten egg and bake them in a fairly warm oven until light brown and done.
5. Remove them from the baking sheet and let them cool.
