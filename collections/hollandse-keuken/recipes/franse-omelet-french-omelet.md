## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons water
- pepper and salt to taste
- Maggi's Aroma to taste
- 50 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper, salt and some Maggi's Aroma.
2. Add the water and pour into a hot skillet in which the butter or margarine has melted, but especially not browned.
3. Prick continuously with a fork until the eggs begin to set across the entire surface.
4. Allow the omelet to become very light brown on the bottom and fold it in thirds.
5. Serve the omelet with salad or bread or fill it with ragout.
