## Ingredients

- 300 g flour or 50 g cornstarch and 250 g flour
- 200 g butter or margarine
- 150 g powdered sugar or brown sugar
- raspberry and berry or apricot jam
- 150 g water glaze
- candied fruit

## Directions

1. Bake thin sand cookies in the known manner (see recipe 772)
2. Let them cool
3. Spread half with jam and place the other half on top
4. Garnish the cookies with water glaze (see recipe 819) and candied fruit
