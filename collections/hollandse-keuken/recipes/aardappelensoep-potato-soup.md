## Ingredients

- 5 ounces potato starch
- beef or veal bouillon
- salt
- bread

## Directions

1. Take 5 ounces of potato starch and mix that with the necessary amount of beef or veal bouillon, as preferred
2. Add a little salt
3. When ready, add bread cut into cube-sized pieces
