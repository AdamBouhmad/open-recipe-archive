## Ingredients

- 2 kg sugar
- 2 l water
- 50 g citric acid
- 30 g lemon essence

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil and dissolve the sugar and the citric acid (off the heat) in it.
2. After cooling, stir in the essence and pour the lemonade into well-cleaned bottles.
3. Close them with a cleaned and boiled cork.
4. Dilute the lemonade with 3 parts water when using.
