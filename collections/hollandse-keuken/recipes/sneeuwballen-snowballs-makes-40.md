## Ingredients

- 4 dl water
- some salt
- 250 g flour
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g currants
- 7 large or 8 small eggs
- powdered sugar
- lard oil or frying fat for frying

## Directions

1. Bring the water with the butter or margarine and some salt to a boil.
2. Stir in the sifted flour and keep stirring until the dough releases from the pan as a ball.
3. Remove immediately from heat and stir in the whole eggs one by one.
4. Finally mix the dough with a few quick strokes with the well-washed and dried currants.
5. In the meantime, heat frying fat in a deep iron pot.
6. Place it on a lower heat, let it cool a bit and add small balls of dough.
7. Let the snowballs rise and bake light brown and cooked (7 minutes), remove them with a frying spoon from the fat, let them drain well on porous paper and sprinkle them with powdered sugar after cooling.
