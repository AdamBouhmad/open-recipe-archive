## Ingredients

- half a can sweet cream
- whites of 14 to 15 eggs
- a splash of orange water
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Heat half a can of sweet cream over fire
2. Beat the egg whites until foamy
3. Add them with a splash of orange water and sugar to taste
4. Stir until thick
5. Pour quickly over
