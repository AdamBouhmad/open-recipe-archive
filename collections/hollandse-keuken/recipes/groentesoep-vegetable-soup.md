## Ingredients

- soup vegetables
- water
- salt
- cooked rice
- Liebig's Meat Extract
- small meatballs made from calf's meat

## Directions

1. Take the soup vegetables and wash them carefully in plenty of cold water, chop them very finely and let them cook almost done in water with salt.
2. Add some cooked rice to them, let them cook together gently.
3. Add Liebig's Meat Extract to taste. To enhance the flavor, you can add some small meatballs made from calf's meat.
