## Ingredients

- oysters
- oyster liquor
- butter
- hardtack
- nutmeg
- salt
- water
- egg yolks
- lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the beards from the oysters
2. Lay them in a pan with their own liquor, some butter, hardtack, nutmeg, salt and water
3. Let them stew
4. Finish with egg yolks
5. Squeeze lemon juice over them
