## Ingredients

- 10 peaches
- 4 egg yolks
- 100 to 125 g white bastard sugar
- 4 dl white or Rhine wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce like apricot sauce (see previous recipe).
2. Present it with warm almond cake, cabinet pudding etc.
