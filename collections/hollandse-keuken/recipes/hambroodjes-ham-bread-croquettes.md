## Ingredients

- 100 g ham
- 100 g cold veal
- 20 g flour
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 1 to 1.5 dl water with salt or stock
- 2 eggs
- 8 thin slices of old bread
- breadcrumbs
- salad oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the ham and meat together.
2. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 102 and mix the meat with it.
3. Add salt and pepper if needed.
4. Cut thin slices of old bread, remove the crusts and make the pieces equal in shape.
5. Spread them with a thick layer of the mixture, so that it lies higher in the middle than at the edges.
6. Beat the eggs, soak the bread rolls in them until they are completely absorbed, drip the top repeatedly with the egg.
7. Roll them through fine breadcrumbs and fry them brown in hot salad oil.
8. Let them drain on gray paper and place them on a small plate on which a folded cloth lies.
