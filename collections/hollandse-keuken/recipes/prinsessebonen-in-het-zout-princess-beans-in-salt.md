## Ingredients

- 15 kg beans
- 1½ or 4 kg salt
- Cologne pot
- cloth
- board
- stone

## Directions

1. Remove the ends of the beans twice on each side, wash them, break them.
2. Boil them in plenty of boiling water for 2 minutes, remove them with a skimming spoon from the pan, let them cool completely spread out.
3. Finish them as described in the previous recipe.
