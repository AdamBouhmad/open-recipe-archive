## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liter buttermilk
- 90 to 100 g flour
- salt

## Directions

1. METHOD 1: In the pan in which the porridge will be cooked, mix the flour thickly with some cold buttermilk. Carefully stir out the lumps from this mixture, gradually add the remaining buttermilk, put the pan on the fire and stir continuously until the porridge is cooked through and done (5 to 10 minutes).
2. METHOD 2: Bring the greater part of the buttermilk to a boil, stirring occasionally. Mix the flour thickly with some cold buttermilk, stir out the lumps and add enough buttermilk until a thin pap is obtained. Pour this stirring into the boiling buttermilk and let the porridge cook while stirring (5 to 10 minutes).
