## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg sorrel
- 35 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 4 fried eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the sorrel until done as described in recipe 466
2. Let it drain in a colander and stew it for 5 minutes with the salt and butter or margarine
3. Place it in a dish and lay the fried eggs, which have been prepared in the ordinary manner (see recipe 375), on top
