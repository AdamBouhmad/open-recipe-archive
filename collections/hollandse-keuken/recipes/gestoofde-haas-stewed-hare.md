## Ingredients

- hare
- butter
- flour
- jus
- cloves
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- bay leaves
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cut the hare into pieces and wash thoroughly
2. Brown some butter and flour, add jus, cloves, finely chopped onions, pepper, salt, sugar, some bay leaves and lemon peel
3. Place the hare in this and let it braise thoroughly
