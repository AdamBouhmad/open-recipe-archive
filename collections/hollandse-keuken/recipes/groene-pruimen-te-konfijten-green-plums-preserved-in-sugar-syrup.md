## Ingredients

- 5 ounces of green plums
- three-quarters pound of sugar
- pomegranate water

## Directions

1. The plums must first be boiled with pomegranate water with the sugar, but well skimmed, then set aside; do this about three times, until they are green
2. Place the plums on a colander, covered with grapevine leaves; then cook the syrup and when thick enough, place it cold on the plums
