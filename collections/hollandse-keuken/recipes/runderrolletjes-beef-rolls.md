## Ingredients

- beef slices
- 50 g fresh bacon
- 4 gherkins
- salt
- at least 75 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash and salt the beef slices, cut the washed bacon into 4 equally large slices, place these on the beef slices, put a gherkin in the middle and roll everything together
2. Tie the beef rolls with thin string or thread and brown them in the butter or margarine on all sides
3. Finish the jus with water and stew the meat in it for about 1 hour
4. Remove the strings or threads before serving
