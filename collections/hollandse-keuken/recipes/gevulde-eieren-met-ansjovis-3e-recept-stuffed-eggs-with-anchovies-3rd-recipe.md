## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 8 anchovies
- pepper
- salt
- capers

## Directions

1. Treat the eggs as indicated in recipe 29
2. Rub the yolks completely fine, gradually add the butter or margarine beaten to cream and the anchovy purée, which is made by pressing 6 soaked and cleaned anchovies through a hair sieve
3. Stir in enough pepper (and salt) until a tasty mixture is obtained
4. Fill the half whites with this so that the yolks stick up a bit and the shape of a whole egg is obtained
5. Garnish them with thin strips of the remaining anchovy and capers
6. Note: See note under recipe 29
