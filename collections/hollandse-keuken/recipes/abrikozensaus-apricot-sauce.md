## Ingredients

- 10 apricots
- 4 egg yolks
- 100 to 125 g bastard sugar
- 4 dl white or Rhine wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Cook the apricots in some water until they are soft, press them through a horsehair sieve.
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar, add the wine, lemon juice and apricot puree and let the sauce be bound, stirring, on a gentle fire or in a bain-marie.
3. Present it with warm almond cake, cabinet pudding etc.
