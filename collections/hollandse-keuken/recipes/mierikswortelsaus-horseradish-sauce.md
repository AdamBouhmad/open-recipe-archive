## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter bouillon or water with salt
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1/2 tablespoon grated horseradish or more

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Mix it away from the heat with the grated horseradish
3. Serve with calf's head or cooked meat
