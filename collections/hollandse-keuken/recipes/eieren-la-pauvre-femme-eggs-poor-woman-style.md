## Ingredients

- a little butter
- twelve eggs
- bread, cut into cubes
- butter for frying
- mustard sauce or spices

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter on a plate
2. Break twelve eggs onto it and place in warm ashes
3. Cut bread into cubes and fry in butter
4. Add the bread to the eggs
5. Place fire on top
6. When well cooked, pour a sauce with mustard or spices over it
