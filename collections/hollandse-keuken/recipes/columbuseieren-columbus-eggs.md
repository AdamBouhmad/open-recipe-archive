## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- mayonnaise made from 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tablespoon capers
- fresh parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard, peel them and cut off a cap so they can stand
2. Prepare the mayonnaise in the usual way (recipe 367)
3. Stir the capers through
4. Stand the eggs upright on a small dish, pour the mayonnaise around them and garnish the dish with a tuft of parsley
