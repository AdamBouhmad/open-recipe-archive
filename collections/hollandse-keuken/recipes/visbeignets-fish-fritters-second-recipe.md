## Ingredients

- fish prepared as in first recipe
- 125 g flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 dl water
- salt
- frying fat or salad oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as indicated in the first recipe
2. Let the pieces cool after rolling them through the sauce
3. Then roll them through a batter (pate a frire) consisting of flour, eggs, water and salt
4. Fry the beignets in frying fat or salad oil from which blue vapor rises, until brown and crispy
5. Let them drain on gray paper
