## Ingredients

- 4 kg very young or 2 kg old peas
- salt
- 300 g butter
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and wash them
2. Cook them in little boiling water with salt until done (20 to 25 minutes, very young peas shorter), or steam them
3. Let them drain quickly in a colander if necessary
4. Stir them as warm as possible through the butter that has been placed in the bowl
5. Sprinkle some very fine parsley over them
