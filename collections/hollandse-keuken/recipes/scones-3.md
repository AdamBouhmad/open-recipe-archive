## Ingredients

- 350 g self-rising flour
- 50 g sultana raisins
- 50 g sugar
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg
- 1.5 dl lukewarm milk
- 7.5 g salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl; put the butter or margarine in it and cut the butter or margarine fine into the flour.
2. Add the sugar, the washed and then dried raisins, the beaten egg, the lukewarm milk, the salt, until a firm dough is obtained.
3. Roll this out on a flour-dusted table to a sheet of about 1 cm thickness.
4. Divide this into fairly large circles for large scones, into small circles for smaller ones.
5. Cut a cross in the large sheets with scissors.
6. Place the sheets of dough on a greased baking sheet.
7. Brush them with milk, sprinkle some sifted powdered sugar over them and bake them in a hot oven until done (about 15 minutes).
8. Cut them in half while warm and spread them with butter.
9. Place the halves on top of each other and serve them warm.
