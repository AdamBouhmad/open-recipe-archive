## Ingredients

- Leftover meat
- Herring
- Gherkins
- Potatoes
- Apples
- Cooked mushrooms
- Capers
- Shallots or cooked onions
- Potato flour
- Salad oil
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sugar
- Mustard
- Vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut leftover meat, herring, gherkins, potatoes, apples, and cooked mushrooms into cubes
2. Add capers, shallots or cooked onions and mix everything well
3. For the sauce: mix two spoonfuls of potato flour with two spoonfuls of salad oil over the fire, stirring well
4. Once cooled, stir in two egg yolks, a little salt, pepper, sugar, two spoonfuls of mustard and more salad oil
5. Thin it with vinegar and a little water, but ensure it is not too thin
6. Pour the sauce over the salad and let it soak through well
