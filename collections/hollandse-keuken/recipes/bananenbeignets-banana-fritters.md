## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 6 ripe bananas
- 3 to 3.5 dl beer or sparkling water
- salt
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Heat the frying fat.
2. Peel and halve the bananas, then cut into pieces.
3. Prepare the batter as for apple beignets.
4. Coat some banana pieces in the batter, place them in the hot fat and fry them light brown and cooked through.
5. Let them drain thoroughly on gray paper.
6. Dust with powdered sugar and serve warm.
7. Optionally serve with raspberry sauce.
