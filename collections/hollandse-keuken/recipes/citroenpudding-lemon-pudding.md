## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 150 g brown sugar
- juice of 2 lemons with some water or Rhine wine (together 2 dl liquid)
- 10 g white gelatin

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar in a well-enameled pan until thick, white and fluffy, add the lemon juice and let the mixture be bound in a bain-marie, stirring vigorously.
2. Remove from heat and blend with the gelatin (which has been soaked 10 to 15 minutes in cold water) and then dissolved with as little boiling water as possible.
3. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible and add the pudding mixture.
4. Let the pudding become gelatinous, stirring occasionally, and pour it into a mold brushed with unbeaten egg white.
5. Let it become cold and stiff, turn it out onto a damp dish and serve it without sauce, but with vanilla wafers or biscuits de Savoye.
