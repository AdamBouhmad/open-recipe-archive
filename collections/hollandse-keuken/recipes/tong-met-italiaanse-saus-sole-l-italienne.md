## Ingredients

- 2 large cooking soles
- 1/4 l Italian sauce, consisting of: 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- mushrooms
- 1/2 l fish stock
- parsley and onion
- 2 egg yolks
- 60 g old Gouda cheese

## Directions

1. Make fillets of the soles, as indicated in recipe 198.
2. After rolling them, cook them in boiling water with salt, but let coarsely chopped onion and a couple of sprigs of parsley cook along with them.
3. Remove the fillets with a skimming spoon from the liquid and place them in a fireproof small dish.
4. Cover them with the sauce, which has been made in the usual way and which has been blended with the egg yolks and the mushrooms cut into small pieces.
5. Sprinkle grated cheese over it and let the small dish become light brown in a moderately warm oven.
6. Note: See recipe 199 for preparing fish stock.
