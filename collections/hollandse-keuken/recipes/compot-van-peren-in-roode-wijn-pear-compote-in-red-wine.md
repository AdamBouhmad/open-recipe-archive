## Ingredients

- red wine
- pears
- sugar
- lemon peels
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Heat red wine
2. Lay in whole, halved or quartered pears
3. Add sugar, lemon peels and cinnamon
4. Cook until thick and syrup-like
5. When serving, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
