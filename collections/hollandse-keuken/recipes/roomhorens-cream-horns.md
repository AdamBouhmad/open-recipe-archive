## Ingredients

- 75 g hard butter or margarine
- 75 g flour
- some water
- salt
- 2.5 dl custard cream (recipe 816) for filling

## Directions

1. Make puff pastry in the usual way (recipe 846). Roll it out very thin one last time and cut long strips from it with a width of 2 cm, make a narrow edge damp with water. Starting at the point, wrap the strips around the greased cream horn molds. Brush the top of the cream horns with egg yolk beaten with a little water, place them on a baking sheet and bake them in a hot oven until light brown and cooked (12-15 minutes). Let cool, remove the molds and fill them with custard cream prepared in the usual way.
