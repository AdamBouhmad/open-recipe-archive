## Ingredients

- 2 pounds peeled quinces
- 1 pound good tart peeled apples
- 1 to 2 bowlfuls water
- three fourths pound best bread sugar per pound of liquid
- juice of 1 to 2 lemons
- cooked lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook 2 pounds peeled quinces with 1 pound good tart peeled apples and 1 to 2 bowlfuls of water into a pulp
2. Let it strain gently through a bag or coarse cloth overnight
3. For each pound of liquid, take three fourths pound best bread sugar and the juice of 1 to 2 lemons
4. Cook for three quarters of an hour
5. Have some cooked lemon zest cut into strips in the bottom of the cups or molds
6. Add the lemon zest and lemon juice at the very end of cooking, as otherwise the lemon loses its flavor
