## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- approximately 2 dl warm water
- table salt (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy.
2. Add the lukewarm water drop by drop until a thick, light sauce is obtained.
3. Add table salt if necessary.
4. Serve with asparagus, Brussels chicory, Hamburg rib, etc.
