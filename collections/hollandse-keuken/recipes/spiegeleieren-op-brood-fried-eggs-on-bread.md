## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 thin slices of old bread without crust
- 60 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Fry in the frying pan, in which the butter or margarine has been made light brown, the slices of bread, which have been trimmed of crust and cut evenly, on both sides until light brown.
2. Break an egg carefully on the warm side of each slice, fry them as described in recipe 375.
3. Place them on a heated plate and present them with pepper and salt.
