## Ingredients

- 2 cooking soles
- 4 dl white wine
- 30 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Fillet the soles in the usual way, wash them, rub them with some salt, tie them and place them in an ovenproof dish in which half of the butter or margarine has been melted.
2. Add the wine and let the fish stew covered in a moderately warm oven for approximately 20 minutes.
3. Place them on a warmed platter.
4. Bring the remaining butter or margarine to a boil with the flour, rub out the lumps and gradually add the liquid in which the fillets stewed.
5. Let the sauce cook while stirring for 10 minutes, blend it with very finely chopped parsley and pepper and salt to taste.
6. Pour the sauce over the fish.
