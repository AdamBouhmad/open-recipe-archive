## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter bouillon or milk
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- pepper and salt
- 60 g old Gouda cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Add the grated cheese and pepper and salt to taste and serve with cauliflower, salsify, Brussels endive
