## Ingredients

- beef tenderloin
- butter
- small carrots
- peas
- cauliflower
- asparagus cut into small pieces
- salt
- flour
- Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Take the beef tenderloin, lard it and braise it in butter.
2. Meanwhile cook small carrots, peas, cauliflower and asparagus cut into small pieces (all well washed) in salted water, almost tender.
3. Make flour and butter brown on the fire, stir it with Meat Extract until a smooth sauce.
4. Add the various vegetables to it and let them cook a little longer.
5. Just before serving add the jus from the beef tenderloin, place the beef tenderloin on a warm plate, put the vegetables around it and pour the sauce over.
