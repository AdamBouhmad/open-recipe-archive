## Ingredients

- green cucumbers
- salt
- whole white pepper
- peeled shallots
- boiling vinegar
- alum

## Directions

1. Peel the green cucumbers and remove the skin; peel them further down to the seed, roll them up and cut them finely
2. Lay them overnight in a bowl with plenty of salt and let them drain well
3. Place the cucumbers in an earthenware pot with some whole white pepper and peeled shallots, and pour boiling vinegar over them, in which a piece of alum must be dissolved
4. The cucumbers must be well covered and the pot must be sealed with a bladder
