## Ingredients

- 3 eggs or 1 egg
- salt
- 120 g or 150 g flour
- 1/2 l milk
- 80 g butter or margarine
- powdered sugar or crushed white sugar
- applesauce, rhubarb compote, jam, or whipped cream (for second method)

## Directions

1. FIRST METHOD: Beat the eggs with salt, add the flour (with 1 egg also 1 dl milk) and stir out the lumps with a wooden spoon or whisk. Add the milk gradually. Melt some butter or margarine in the frying pan or heat some salad oil, pour a little batter onto it but let it run as thin as possible. Fry the crepe on one side until light brown and cooked through on fairly high heat, roll it up with 2 forks. Lay the crepes crosswise on a plate, sprinkle powdered sugar or crushed white sugar over each layer.
2. SECOND METHOD: Prepare the batter as described above and fry the crepes in the same way. Do not roll them up, but lay them on a round plate with an inverted small plate on top, with the fried side facing down. Spread some applesauce, rhubarb compote, jam, or whipped cream over each crepe. Lay the last crepe with the fried side up, sprinkle some powdered sugar over it and cut the crepe cake thus obtained into wedges.
3. THIRD METHOD: Prepare the batter as described above, but use more flour. Fry the crepes on both sides until light brown and serve them with crushed sugar.
