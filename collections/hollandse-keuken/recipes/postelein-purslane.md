## Ingredients

- purslane
- salt
- nutmeg
- crushed rusk
- sorrel (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash well and set it on with the water that remains in it
2. When done, add salt, nutmeg and finely crushed rusk
3. You can optionally add sorrel
