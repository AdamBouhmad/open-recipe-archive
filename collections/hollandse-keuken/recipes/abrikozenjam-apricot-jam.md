## Ingredients

- 3.75 kg apricots (+ 3 kg without pits)
- 2.25 kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- twine
- cellophane
- rubber bands
- labels

## Directions

1. Peel the apricots, remove the pits, put them with as much water as necessary to prevent burning during the first moments in a pan.
2. Let them cook down to a thick pulp.
3. Add the sugar, bring them to a boil with the lid on and let the jam cook without the lid, stirring occasionally, until it reaches the required thickness (see the rules on page 369).
4. Treat it further entirely as strawberry jam (recipe 983).
