## Ingredients

- 3 pounds fish
- 4 large handfuls cooked onions, cut into small pieces
- grated nutmeg
- parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- peeled shrimp (as noted in the NB)

## Directions

1. Cook 3 pounds fish until nearly done in a tinned kettle
2. Pour off the water cleanly without removing the fish
3. Scatter 4 large handfuls of cooked onions cut into small pieces over the fish
4. Add grated nutmeg, a little finely chopped parsley, and a quantity of butter
5. Hang the kettle with all of this over the fire and shake until the butter is melted
6. Pour into the serving dish without worrying about the arrangement, as the fish may break
7. Add peeled shrimp when serving the fish
