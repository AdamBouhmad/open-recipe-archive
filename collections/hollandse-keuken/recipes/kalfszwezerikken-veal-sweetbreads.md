## Ingredients

- flour
- 2 egg yolks
- milk
- sweetbreads
- bouillon
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix flour, 2 egg yolks, and milk; set to fire and stir until thick
2. Previously cook the sweetbreads in bouillon with a little salt until well done
3. Place them on a dish and pour the above sauce over them
4. Sprinkle grated nutmeg over it
5. Lemon juice poured over is very nice
