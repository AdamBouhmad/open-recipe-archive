## Ingredients

- Sauce for morel ragout
- Cock's combs
- Finely chopped pork
- Chicken and cock feet, well cooked

## Directions

1. Make the same sauce as for the ragout of morels
2. Fill the bottom part of the cock's combs with very finely chopped pork
3. Add well-cooked chicken and cock feet
