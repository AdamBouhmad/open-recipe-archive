## Ingredients

- 1 small cucumber
- 3 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Treat the cucumber as described above.
2. Let the strips, sprinkled with salt, stand for some time.
3. Let them drain as dry as possible, place them on the plate and pour some cream over them.
