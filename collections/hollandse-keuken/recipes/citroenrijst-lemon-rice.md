## Ingredients

- 200 g rice
- 6 deciliters water
- zest of 1 lemon
- juice of 2 lemons
- 200 g sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice until completely clear water comes off.
2. Let it drain well.
3. Place the rice on with the cold water and the thinly pared or grated lemon zest and let it cook slowly on a gentle fire until done, but not completely dry.
4. Stir in the lemon juice and sugar off the heat, remove the lemon zest, place the mixture in a stone pudding mold rinsed with cold water and let it become cold and firm.
5. Serve the pudding, after turning it out onto a wet plate, with vanilla sauce or place a somewhat thinner mixture in a shallow dish and pour the sauce over it.
