## Ingredients

- 500 g chestnuts
- 2½ to 3 dl milk
- ¼ stick vanilla
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 60 g sugar
- 2½ dl whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 50 g powdered sugar or bastard sugar
- French fruits

## Directions

1. Wash the chestnuts, cut them deeply, place them with just enough boiling water to cover them and let them cook gently for 10 minutes.
2. Peel them 2 or 3 at a time and place them in the milk, which has been brought to a boil with the vanilla.
3. Let them cook gently in this until done (approximately ½ hour).
4. Press them through a horsehair sieve and mix this purée with the butter and sugar.
5. Place it on a plate and let it cool.
6. Beat the cream with the sifted powdered sugar or bastard sugar until stiff and mix this with the stiffly beaten egg whites and the vanilla pulp.
7. Garnish the purée with this mixture.
8. If desired, place a French fruit here and there on top or pour some sugar burned to caramel over it.
