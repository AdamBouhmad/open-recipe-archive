## Ingredients

- 200 g calf kidney fat
- 200 g flour
- water and salt
- 500 g beef steak
- 2 braised calf kidneys
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry (feuilletée) in the usual manner (see recipe 846)
2. Roll it out the last time into a large thin sheet that can amply cover the inside of a round fireproof dish, with the excess dough cut off and re-rolled to serve as the lid of the pastry
3. Bake the beef steak, cut it into pieces, prepare the braised calf kidney in the usual manner (see recipe 266), stir both types of meat together and let them cool
4. Lay them on the dough in the dish, moisten the edge of the dish, lay the remaining dough somewhat generously over it and glue it to the edge of the little dish
5. Make figures in it with a fork, roll out the last remaining dough thinly, cut figures or strips from it and glue these to the lid with a little water
6. Brush everything with egg yolk beaten with a little water
7. Prick the dough here and there and let the pastry bake in a fairly hot oven until light brown and cooked through (20 to 25 minutes)
