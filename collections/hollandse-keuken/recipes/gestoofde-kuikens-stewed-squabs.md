## Ingredients

- squabs
- water
- cooked parsley
- pepper
- salt
- white wine
- egg yolks (5)
- nutmeg
- vinegar
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the squabs into quarters.
2. Place them in a pan with water so they are covered.
3. Stew them with a handful of cooked parsley, pepper, salt and white wine.
4. Add 5 egg yolks, nutmeg, vinegar and a lump of butter.
5. Let everything come to a boil together.
