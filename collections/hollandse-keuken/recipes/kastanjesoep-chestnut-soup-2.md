## Ingredients

- 1 l water with salt
- + 20 chestnuts
- 25 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the chestnuts, make a cross on them, set them with enough boiling water so they just cover and let them cook for 10 minutes
2. Peel them then as quickly as possible and let them cook further in 1 l boiling water with salt (+ 1/2 hour)
3. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve, rub the chestnuts through and mix this purée with the cooking liquid
4. Melt 20 g of the butter or margarine and add the flour, bring it to a boil while stirring, add the liquid gradually in small portions while stirring and let the soup cook while stirring until done (+ 10 min.)
5. Add the remaining butter or margarine, salt and nutmeg to taste and if desired some milk
6. N.B. Do not cook the chestnuts until done in the liquid in which they are set, as this would discolor the soup
