## Ingredients

- 100 g brown sugar
- 100 g syrup
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon ginger powder
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 250 g flour

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, mix it with the syrup, the sugar, the milk, the ginger powder and lastly the sifted flour, into which the baking powder has been stirred.
2. Spread the dough thinly on a baking sheet greased with butter, bake it in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done (approximately 15 minutes), cut it into square pieces while still warm (in front of the open oven door).
3. Remove these immediately from the sheet and let them cool.
