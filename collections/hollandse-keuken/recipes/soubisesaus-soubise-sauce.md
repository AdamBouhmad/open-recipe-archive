## Ingredients

- 1/4 l cream
- 100 g onion (1 medium-sized)
- 60 g butter or margarine
- 25 g flour
- 6 mushrooms
- 3 truffles
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Braise the shredded onion in 10 g butter or margarine until cooked (15 to 20 minutes), but ensure it remains light in color
2. Press this mixture through a horsehair sieve and blend with a sauce made from 25 g flour, 25 g butter or margarine and cream
3. Stir in the remaining butter or margarine and some pepper and salt
4. Mix in the chopped mushrooms and truffles and serve the sauce with sweetbreads
