## Ingredients

- ¾ kg strawberries
- approximately 2½ dl water
- 100 g sugar
- some lemon juice
- some sago

## Directions

1. Wash the strawberries and remove the stem.
2. Let the sugar cook down with the water to a thin syrup, add the strawberries, bring them to a boil, remove them with a skimming spoon from the liquid and lay them in the compote dish.
3. Mix the liquid with lemon juice to taste, thicken it with some mixed sago and pour it over the strawberries.
