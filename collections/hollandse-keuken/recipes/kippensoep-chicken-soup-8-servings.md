## Ingredients

- 1 soup chicken
- 2 liters water with salt
- mace
- piece of Spanish pepper
- bay leaf
- 2 small carrots
- parsley
- 30 g rice
- 60 g flour
- 60 g butter or margarine
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the washed and cut-up chicken in the water with salt and spices (approximately 2.5 hours).
2. Strain the bouillon and cut off the soft, white pieces of meat from the chicken to serve as garnish in the soup.
3. Add the bouillon, after having carefully skimmed off the fat, while stirring to the melted butter or margarine and which has then been brought to the boil together with the flour and from which the lumps have been rubbed out.
4. Let the soup cook while stirring until done (10 minutes).
5. Warm the pieces of meat in it, add the rice, which has been cooked until tender in some chicken bouillon or water with salt, and carefully pour the soup to the egg yolks, which have been beaten in the soup tureen.
