## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant
- 150 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Roast the pheasant as you would chicken (see recipe 309).
2. Allow 1 to 1 1/2 hours as roasting time.
3. Serve with peach or apricot compote.
