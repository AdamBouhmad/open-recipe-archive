## Ingredients

- Livers of haddock or cod
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- slices of rye bread

## Directions

1. Clean the livers by carefully cutting out the gallbladder, rinse them
2. Cook them gently in some fish water until done (approximately 5 to 10 minutes)
3. Serve with slices of rye bread, salt, pepper and mustard
