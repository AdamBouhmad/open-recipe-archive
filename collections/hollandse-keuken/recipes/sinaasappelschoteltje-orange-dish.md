## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 100 g sugar
- ½ l milk
- 15 g Duryea cornstarch
- 2 eggs
- 30 g sugar
- ¼ vanilla stick

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, carefully remove all white skin and slice them across.
2. Place these, layer by layer sprinkled with the sugar, in a fireproof dish.
3. Prepare vanilla custard in the usual way, but use only the egg yolks in it.
4. Pour this over the fruit.
5. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, mix it with a few spoonfuls of sifted powdered sugar and spread or pipe it over the dish.
6. Let it dry in a fairly warm oven, but ensure that it remains as light in color as possible (½ hour to 3 quarters of an hour).
7. Serve the dish warm or cold.
