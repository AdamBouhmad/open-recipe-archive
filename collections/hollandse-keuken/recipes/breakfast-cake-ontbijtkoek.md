## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 150 g bastard sugar
- 1½ to 2 dl milk
- ½ teaspoon crushed cloves
- ½ teaspoon fine cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, add the spices, the baking soda and the milk and stir out the lumps from this stiff dough.
2. Check if the spice flavor is good and put the dough in a greased loaf pan.
3. Bake the cake in a moderately warm oven until brown and cooked through (approximately 1 hour).
4. Test if it is cooked by pricking it with a knitting needle, which should come out dry.
5. Let it cool in the pan and turn it out afterwards.
