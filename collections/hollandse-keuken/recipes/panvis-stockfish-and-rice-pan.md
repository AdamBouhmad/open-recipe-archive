## Ingredients

- 250 g cooked stockfish (= 100 g unsoaked, raw stockfish)
- 400 g cooked rice or barley
- 1 kg potatoes
- 100 g onion
- 50 g butter or margarine
- stockfish water or leftover mustard sauce
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Carefully remove the skins and bones from the leftover stockfish (the warmer it is done, the easier it goes).
2. Mash the stockfish, mix it with the rice, the finely mashed potatoes, the onions, which have been minced and fried light brown in butter or margarine, and some stockfish water (or leftover mustard sauce) until the mixture has reached the required thickness.
3. Add pepper, salt and nutmeg to taste.
4. Put everything in a fireproof dish that has been rinsed with water, sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, place a few small pieces of butter on it and let the dish brown in the oven.
