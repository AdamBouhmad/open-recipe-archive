## Ingredients

- milk
- flour
- egg yolks
- bread sugar
- white bread

## Directions

1. Set as much milk to the fire as you deem necessary, bring it to a boil
2. Beat some flour with a few egg yolks and bread sugar through it
3. Pour it over slices of white bread when serving
4. Be especially careful not to burn it and ensure that no burning wood lies against the pot
