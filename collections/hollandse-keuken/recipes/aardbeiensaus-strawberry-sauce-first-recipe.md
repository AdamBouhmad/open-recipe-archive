## Ingredients

- 300 g small, perfect strawberries
- 4 dl water
- 20 g sago or potato flour
- 75 g sugar
- lemon juice
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Pick the strawberries, wash them and let them drain briefly in a colander.
2. Bring them with the water, lemon zest and sugar almost to a boil, remove them with a skimming spoon from the liquid, place them in a glass dish and bind the liquid with the mixed sago.
3. Remove the lemon zest, add some lemon juice and pour the sauce over the strawberries.
4. Present the sauce with maizena, semolina pudding etc.
