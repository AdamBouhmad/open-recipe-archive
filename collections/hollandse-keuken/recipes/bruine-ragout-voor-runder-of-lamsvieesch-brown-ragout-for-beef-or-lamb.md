## Ingredients

- Two spoons flour
- Fat or butter
- Chopped onions
- Water or bouillon
- Vinegar
- 1 spoon mustard
- Sugar
- Cloves
- Laurel leaf
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Brown two spoons flour half in fat or butter with some chopped onions until dark brown
2. Cook it with water or bouillon until a liquid sauce
3. Add vinegar, 1 spoon mustard, sugar, cloves, laurel leaf, pepper and salt
