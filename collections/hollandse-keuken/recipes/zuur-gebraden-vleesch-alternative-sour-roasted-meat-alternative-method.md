## Ingredients

- Beef
- Beer or wine vinegar
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- Shallots
- Gherkins
- Lemon peel
- Fat or butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place the meat for four to eight days in beer or wine vinegar with bay leaves, cloves, shallots, gherkins and lemon peel, turning it every day.
2. Remove it from this liquid, strain it, and roast the meat with this liquid and the necessary fat or butter and salt, basting it very frequently.
