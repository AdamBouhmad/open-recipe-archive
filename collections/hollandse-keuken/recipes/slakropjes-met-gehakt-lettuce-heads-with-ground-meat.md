## Ingredients

- 8 small lettuce heads
- 150 g ground veal
- 150 g ground pork
- 100 g old bread
- pepper, salt, nutmeg
- 1 1/2 deciliters bouillon or lean gravy with water
- 30 g butter or margarine
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut away the withered leaves and a small piece of the stem.
2. Wash the heads under running water until all sand is removed and cook them in salted water until tender (approximately 1/2 hour).
3. Meanwhile, mix the ground meat with the soaked bread and the spices.
4. Form 8 small balls and one large ball from it and brown these in butter or margarine and cook through, or let them cook for half an hour together with the lettuce.
5. Place the heads in a heat-proof dish, fill them with the meat balls and place the large ball in the center.
6. Pour the finished gravy from the meat or (if it was cooked) some bouillon or lean gravy with water over it.
7. Sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, place a few pieces of butter or margarine on top, clean the edge of the dish, and let it brown in a moderately warm oven.
