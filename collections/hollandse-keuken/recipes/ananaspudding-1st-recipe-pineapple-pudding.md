## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 85 g Duryea maizena
- 75 g sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 5 slices pineapple

## Directions

1. Mix the maizena with some cold milk into a thin paste, without lumps.
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil, add the sugar and, stirring vigorously, the mixed maizena and let the pudding cook through while stirring for 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Then mix it away from the fire with the stiffly beaten egg white.
4. Make sure that in the meantime the pineapple is pitted and cut into small pieces, stir the pineapple through the pudding, but keep some pieces aside to mix through the sauce.
5. Pour the mixture into a mold rinsed with cold water and let it become cold and firm.
6. Bring the pineapple liquid to a boil, add sugar to taste and bind it with some mixed sago.
7. After cooling, stir the remaining pineapple pieces through it and serve the sauce with the pudding.
8. Color the sauce rose if desired by dissolving 1/2 sheet red gelatine in it.
