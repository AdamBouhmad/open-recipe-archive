## Ingredients

- Cold veal or beef
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Pickled shallots
- Gherkins
- Spanish pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt
- Mustard
- Soya sauce

## Directions

1. Cut cold veal or beef into cubes
2. Mix with finely made hard-boiled eggs, pickled shallots, gherkins, and Spanish pepper
3. Mix together with a sauce made from oil, vinegar, pepper, salt, mustard and soya sauce
