## Ingredients

- 1 ox kidney
- 1 hectogram butter
- salt
- pepper
- 25 decagram flour
- white wine (optional)
- 2 deciliters water
- Liebig's Meat Extract
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the ox kidney lengthwise.
2. Give each piece a cut to the depth of 5 streep.
3. Let the butter melt in the sauté pan and when well melted, put the kidney in the pan and add salt and pepper.
4. Shake it every minute to make it cook evenly.
5. After 6 minutes of stewing, drain the kidney in a colander.
6. Put the kidney back in the pan and sprinkle it with flour, let it stew for 2 minutes.
7. Add a little white wine if desired and in any case 2 deciliters water with some Liebig's Meat Extract.
8. Let it cook a little longer together and add a tablespoon of chopped parsley.
