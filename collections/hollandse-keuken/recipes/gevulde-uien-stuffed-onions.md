## Ingredients

- 4 Lisbon (flat) onions
- 100 g ground calf meat
- 100 g ground pork meat
- 25 g old bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 dl lean jus or bouillon
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Clean the onions, wash them and cook them in abundant boiling salted water until done.
2. Mix the ground meat in the usual way, melt 10 g butter or margarine in the pan and cook the meat in it, stirring constantly, until done (15-20 minutes), but ensure it does not brown.
3. Hollow out the onions, fill them with the meat and place them in a fireproof dish.
4. Place a piece of butter or margarine on each onion, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, add bouillon or lean jus with water and let them stew gently in the oven for a quarter hour.
