## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 50 g old Gruyère, Parmesan or Gouda cheese
- 50 g onion (1 medium-sized onion)

## Directions

1. Make the butter or margarine light brown in a shallow heat-resistant plate, add the finely chopped onion and fry it light brown.
2. Sprinkle half of the grated cheese over it, carefully break the eggs on it, but make sure the yolks remain whole.
3. Sprinkle the other half of the cheese over it and let the plate stand in a moderately warm oven until the white has set.
