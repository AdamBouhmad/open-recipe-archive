## Ingredients

- 1 kg rhubarb
- 1 kg sugar
- 1 double packet of Opekta
- jam jars and accessories

## Directions

1. Wash the rhubarb and cut it into pieces.
2. Put the pieces with very little water on the heat and bring the rhubarb to a boil.
3. Sprinkle in the Opekta while stirring well and let the pulp boil vigorously for 1 minute.
4. Add the sugar and stir until it is dissolved.
5. Let the mass boil vigorously for 7 minutes and fill the very well cleaned jam jars as described in the previous recipes.
6. Note: Rhubarb contains little or no natural pectin. Therefore it is recommended to prepare this jam with the help of Opekta.
