## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- crushed hard biscuit
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the sweetbreads until half done and cut them into slices
2. Roll them in beaten eggs
3. Sprinkle them with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and crushed hard biscuit
4. Fry them with butter in the pan until done
