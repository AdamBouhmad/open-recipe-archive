## Ingredients

- string beans
- salt

## Directions

1. Brush beans clean and mince by hand or with a mill
2. Line pot or barrel with a pillowcase
3. Place minced beans inside with plenty of salt
4. Place a cloth and fitted board with heavy weight on top
5. Every 14 days, clean the cloth and stone
