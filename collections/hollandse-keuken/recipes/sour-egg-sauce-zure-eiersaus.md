## Ingredients

- Two parts water
- One part butter
- One spoonful wine vinegar
- Grated nutmeg
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Cook two parts water and one part butter, stirring constantly
2. Add a spoonful of wine vinegar and some grated nutmeg
3. Beat in some eggs
