## Ingredients

- 1 piece vanilla
- 3 egg yolks
- 5 ounces sugar
- 1 can cream

## Directions

1. Cut the vanilla into small pieces
2. combine the eggs, cream and sugar together
3. blend them gently while cooking on the fire
4. when you think it is thick enough, let it cool
