## Ingredients

- beans
- rainwater
- sweet milk
- savory herb
- parsley
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in rainwater with sweet milk, skimming carefully and quickly
2. Cook uncovered
3. Meanwhile chop savory and parsley together
4. Let this cook well with sweet milk, butter and a little salt
5. Then add the beans and let them stew uncovered for a quarter hour
