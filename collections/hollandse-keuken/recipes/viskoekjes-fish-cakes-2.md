## Ingredients

- 200 g fish remains (without bones or skin)
- 30 g old bread
- 1/2 egg
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- fine parsley
- 30 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Chop the remaining fish, after carefully removing the small bones and skin, finely.
2. Mix it with the bread, which has been soaked in water or milk and squeezed out between two spoons, the egg and the seasonings.
3. Form 4 flat cakes from it and fry them in butter or margarine on both sides until light brown.
4. Note: The cakes can also be rolled in beaten egg with water and then in breadcrumbs before frying.
