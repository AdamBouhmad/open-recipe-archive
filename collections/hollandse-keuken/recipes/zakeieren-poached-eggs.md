## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- water
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Use the freshest possible eggs for this
2. Bring water to a boil in a wide pan, add some salt and a small splash of vinegar
3. Break the eggs as close as possible above the water surface, or break them first into a cup and let them slide into the water
4. Quickly wrap the white, which immediately begins to set, around the yolk using two spoons and let the eggs simmer gently for 3 minutes
5. Remove them from the pan with a skimming spoon and cut or trim off the loose flakes of white
6. Lay them on pieces of toasted bread or bread fried in butter or margarine, in a bowl of very strong broth for soup, or serve them with tomato, béchamel, curry sauce etc.
