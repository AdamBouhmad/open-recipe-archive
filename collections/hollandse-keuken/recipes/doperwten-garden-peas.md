## Ingredients

- 4 kg very young peas or 2 kg older peas
- salt
- 300 g butter
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Shell the peas, wash them and cook them in little boiling water with salt until tender (20 to 25 minutes, very young peas shorter), or steam them.
2. Drain them quickly in a colander if needed and stir them as warm as possible through the butter, which has been placed in the dish.
3. Sprinkle some very fine parsley over them.
