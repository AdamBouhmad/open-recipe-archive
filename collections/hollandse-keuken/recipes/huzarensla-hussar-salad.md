## Ingredients

- 250 g cold meat
- 2 sour apples
- 1 egg
- 8 cooked potatoes
- 1 beet
- 1 head of lettuce or 1 bunch of curly endive or 100 g field lettuce
- pickled onions and gherkins
- 3 tablespoons salad oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the hussar salad as in recipe 95, but use meat instead of herrings.
