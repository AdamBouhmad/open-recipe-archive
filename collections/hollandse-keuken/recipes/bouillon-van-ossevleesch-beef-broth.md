## Ingredients

- good piece of lean beef, preferably from the hind quarter
- water

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of lean beef, preferably from the hind quarter of the ox
2. Set it with water to cook, for instance for each Dutch pound use 4 bottles of water
3. Let it cook well for 4 to 5 hours
4. Pay special attention to skimming
