## Ingredients

- Sweet milk
- Buttermilk
- Cold butter
- Saffron
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook 3 pints of sweet milk, then pour it into a pot and stir until it is lukewarm
2. Add a beer glass of buttermilk and stir in a little cold butter, some saffron and a couple of egg yolks
