## Ingredients

- good barley groats, washed several times
- water
- salt, to taste
- Liebig's Meat Extract, to taste

## Directions

1. Let the good barley groats, which have been washed several times, cook for at least a full hour in water.
2. Pour through a sieve and if necessary dilute with water.
3. Add salt and Liebig's Meat Extract to taste and let it all cook together well.
4. This is a strengthening and refreshing drink for the sick and convalescent.
