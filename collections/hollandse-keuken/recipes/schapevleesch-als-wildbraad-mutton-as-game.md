## Ingredients

- leg of mutton or lean piece of mutton
- vinegar
- ragout

## Directions

1. Soak a leg of mutton or lean piece of mutton in vinegar for 3 to 4 days
2. Roast it in a pan or on the spit
3. Serve with a ragout as described in lams-ragout
