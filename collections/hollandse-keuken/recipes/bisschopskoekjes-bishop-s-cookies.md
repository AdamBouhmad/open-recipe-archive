## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 2.5 ounces butter
- grated lemon peel
- mace (foelie)
- 13 drams flour

## Directions

1. Beat 8 eggs with 2.5 ounces butter until foamy
2. Add grated lemon peel and mace
3. Mix in 13 drams flour
4. Put the batter in molds greased with butter
5. Bake gently until done
