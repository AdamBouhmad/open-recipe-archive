## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 60 g smoked bacon

## Directions

1. Fry the bacon, cut into thin slices, on both sides in the frying pan until the bottom has enough fat.
2. Then carefully break the eggs over it, fry them as described in recipe 375.
3. Serve them as fried eggs with ham (recipe 376).
4. Note: See note under recipe 375.
