## Ingredients

- 10 almonds
- 5 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Treat the almonds as in recipe 947. Pound the ground almonds well for 10 minutes in the stone mortar. Then mix the mass, while stirring with the pestle, with the water. If necessary, sieve the mass through a rinsed linen cloth.
