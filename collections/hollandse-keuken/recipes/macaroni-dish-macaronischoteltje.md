## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 200 g macaroni
- 100 g sugar
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni pudding in the usual manner (see previous recipe), but cook the vanilla or lemon zest in with it.
2. When the pudding is done, remove it and mix the pudding with half of the sugar.
3. Stir the egg yolks smooth with the other half of the sugar, carefully add the hot pudding to it and lastly mix the stiffly beaten egg white in as lightly as possible.
4. Put the mixture in a fireproof dish.
5. Finish the dish as described in recipe 602.
