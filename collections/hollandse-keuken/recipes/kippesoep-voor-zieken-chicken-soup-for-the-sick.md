## Ingredients

- one old rooster
- water
- salt
- vermicelli
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take an old rooster and treat it as described above for chicken soup, but use vermicelli instead of rice
2. When serving, stir egg yolks through it and remove the meat
3. This soup must cook one hour longer
