## Ingredients

- ¾ l milk
- ½ vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- 175 g sugar
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 25 to 30 g Duryea cornstarch

## Directions

1. Let the vanilla steep in the milk.
2. Put 150 g sugar in an iron pan with a tablespoon of water.
3. Let the sugar dissolve over low heat and then turn brown while stirring occasionally, but do not burn.
4. Add another spoonful of water and then the milk from which the vanilla has been removed.
5. Let the caramel dissolve completely in the milk.
6. Stir the egg yolks with the cornstarch and 25 grams sugar, if necessary also a few spoonfuls of milk into a smooth paste.
7. Pour the paste into the caramel milk and let the custard cook through while stirring with a whisk (3 to 4 minutes).
8. Let it cool while stirring occasionally, mix with the stiffly beaten egg white and serve the custard in a custard dish.
