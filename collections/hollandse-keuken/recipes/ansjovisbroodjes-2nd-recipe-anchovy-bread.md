## Ingredients

- 3 slices casino bread
- 6 anchovies
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the anchovies as indicated in recipe 17.
2. Rub 3 anchovies through a horsehair sieve, mix the obtained purée with the butter beaten until creamy.
3. Taste whether this anchovy butter is not too salty, if so stir in more butter.
4. Cut very thin slices of casino bread, remove the crust and cut them in different shapes, e.g. triangles, diamonds, pear-shaped figures, etc.
5. Fry them with butter in a skillet until light brown and crispy or toast them on a baking sheet in the oven until brown.
6. Let them cool.
7. Spread them on one side with the anchovy butter so that it lies higher in the middle than at the edges (so-called domed).
8. Cut the remaining anchovies into very thin strips, place these over the anchovy butter and further garnish the breads with finely chopped egg white, finely chopped egg yolk and parsley.
