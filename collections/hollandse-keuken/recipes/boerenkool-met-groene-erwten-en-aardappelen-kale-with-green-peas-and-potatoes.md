## Ingredients

- 3 bunches or 1½ kg kale
- 1 kg potatoes
- 250 g green peas
- 60 g fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Follow the preparation method of the previous recipe (Kale with Potatoes and Smoked Sausage), but add the cooked and drained peas together with the potatoes.
