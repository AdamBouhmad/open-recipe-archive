## Ingredients

- 2/3 red berries
- 1/3 black berries
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the fruit and add enough water so that they are half submerged.
2. Let them cook on a small fire until they have become soft (10 to 15 minutes) and then press them through a fruit press or through a folded linen cloth.
3. Put the juice in a clean pan, let it boil briefly and carefully skim off the foam.
4. Fill the well-cleaned and boiled bottles with it and close them immediately with boiled corks.
