## Ingredients

- cleaned and prepared rabbit
- water
- finely chopped shallots
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Let the cleaned and prepared rabbit soak in water
2. Cut it into pieces
3. Let them cook gently in a pan with water, finely chopped shallots, some butter, salt, pepper and nutmeg
4. When serving, drizzle lime juice over it
