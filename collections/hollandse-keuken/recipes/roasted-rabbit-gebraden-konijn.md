## Ingredients

- young rabbit
- water
- fine salt
- pepper
- ground hardtack
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the rabbit in a roasting pan with water
2. Sprinkle with fine salt, pepper, and ground hardtack
3. Lay it flat in a roasting pan with butter and baste it continuously
4. Ensure it browns well
5. Serve with a lemon or shallot sauce
