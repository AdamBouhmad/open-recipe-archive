## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef tenderloin
- 100 g butter or margarine
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- 1 small cauliflower
- 1/2 tin of peas
- 1 tin of asparagus tips
- 500 g carrots
- 400 g green beans
- 1 kg potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 150 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Braise the beef tenderloin in the usual manner
2. Cut it into not too thick slices and arrange them in the shape of the tenderloin on the platter
3. Place warm, stewed vegetables of contrasting colors (such as carrots, peas, asparagus tips, cauliflower, green beans, etc.) in compartments around it
4. If desired, separate the compartments with mashed potato piped from a pastry bag
5. Pour jus finished with milk over the meat and serve the remaining jus in sauce boats
