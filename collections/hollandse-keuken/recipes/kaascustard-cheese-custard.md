## Ingredients

- 8 thin slices of casino bread
- 4 thick slices of cheese
- some celery salt
- salt
- 3 eggs
- approximately 1/2 l bouillon made from 2 cubes
- aroma or spicy sauce
- some grated cheese
- approximately 50 g butter or margarine
- a few stuffed olives or a pickle

## Directions

1. Grease a long ovenproof dish (preferably rectangular and glass) with some butter.
2. Cut the old bread so that the sandwiches fill the dish without large gaps.
3. Make sandwiches from the slices of bread, filled with cheese and sprinkled with celery salt.
4. Fill the bottom of the dish with these and make a second layer if possible (possibly with more bread).
5. Make sure the bread stays at least 1.5 cm below the rim.
6. Beat the eggs, add bouillon and seasonings.
7. Pour this over the bread and let the dish stand for at least 1 hour so the liquid soaks into the bread.
8. Make sure the liquid still level with the bread afterwards.
9. Sprinkle the top with grated cheese, lay slices of olive or pickle on it and put the dish in a fairly hot oven to set the egg and brown the top.
