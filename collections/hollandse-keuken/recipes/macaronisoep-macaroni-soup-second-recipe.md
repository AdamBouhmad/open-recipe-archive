## Ingredients

- 1 l beef bouillon or 1 l water and 4 bouillon cubes
- 30 g macaroni
- 25 g cheese
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dl cream
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Cook the broken and washed macaroni in boiling salted water until tender (1/2 hour to 3/4 hour) and cut into small pieces
2. Heat butter or margarine with flour, stirring, and bring to a boil
3. Add the sieved bouillon or the water in which the bouillon cubes are dissolved while stirring, and let the soup cook while stirring until done (approximately 10 minutes)
4. Add the macaroni and grated cheese
5. Beat the egg yolk with the cream in the soup tureen and carefully add the soup to it while stirring
