## Ingredients

- 3 slices casino bread without crust
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 4 anchovies
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- ravigotte sauce
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the anchovies as described in recipe 17
2. Cut the slices of bread lengthwise in half and fry them light brown
3. Cut the hard-boiled egg into slices
4. Lay a slice of egg on each slice of bread; place half a rolled anchovy on top and garnish the sandwich by piping ravigotte sauce (see recipe 370) along it
5. Curled parsley can be placed along the plate
