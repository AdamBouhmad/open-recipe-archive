## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 liter milk
- 2 tablespoons Nescafé
- 240 g sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the whole as vanilla ice cream; mix the Nescafé with egg yolks and sugar.
