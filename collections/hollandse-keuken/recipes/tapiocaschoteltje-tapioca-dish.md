## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 l milk
- 120 g tapioca
- 100 g sugar
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 1/2 vanilla stick or 1/2 lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook tapioca porridge in the usual way (see previous recipe), but let the vanilla or lemon peel cook along with it.
2. Remove these when the porridge is cooked, stir half of the sugar through it and carefully add the porridge to the egg yolks, which have been stirred smooth with the remaining sugar.
3. Last of all fold in the stiffly beaten egg white as lightly as possible.
4. Finish the dish as described in recipe 602.
