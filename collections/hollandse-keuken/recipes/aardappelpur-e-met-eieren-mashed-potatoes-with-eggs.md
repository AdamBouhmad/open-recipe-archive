## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked potatoes
- 4 dl milk
- 1 egg
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make the mashed potato purée as described in the previous recipe, but always ensure that the milk used is boiled before it is added to the purée.
2. Stir the beaten egg yolk through it, mix the purée with the stiffly beaten egg white, put everything in an ovenproof dish and finish the purée as in recipe 409 (second method).
