## Ingredients

- Ox tongue
- Mushrooms
- Flour
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Handle the tongue as on the previous page; do not lard it
2. Make the sauce as with the gherkins, but use mushrooms instead
3. The mushrooms must be cooked in the sauce from the beginning
