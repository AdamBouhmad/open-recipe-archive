## Ingredients

- 750 g strawberries
- 500 g sugar
- 1/2 liter water or milk
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 liter cream
- a little carmine

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the warm water or warm milk and let the sugar syrup cool.
2. Pick the strawberries, wash them and rub them through a hair sieve.
3. Add the obtained purée to the sugar syrup, add the cream, the sieved lemon juice and as much carmine as needed to obtain a nice color.
4. Place the mixture in the rinsed ice mold and let it freeze in the manner as indicated.
