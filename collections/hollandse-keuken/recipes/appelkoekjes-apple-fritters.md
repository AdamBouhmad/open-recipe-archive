## Ingredients

- Best sour apples
- Wheat flour
- Yeast batter
- A splash of brandy
- Butter or lard for frying, or half of each

## Directions

1. Take best sour apples, core them and cut them into slices
2. Dip them in a somewhat thick yeast batter to which a splash of brandy has been added
3. Put them in a flat pan with boiling butter or lard, or half of each
4. Let them become nicely brown
