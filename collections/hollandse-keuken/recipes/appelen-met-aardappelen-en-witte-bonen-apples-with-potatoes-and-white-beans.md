## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg tart apples (or half sweet, half tart)
- 1 kg potatoes
- 250 g white beans
- 60 g fat
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in the usual manner (recipe 493) until tender.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut them into quarters, wash them, place them at the bottom of the pan and add enough water (or if available: bean water) to half cover them.
3. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters, wash them, add them together with the beans and fat to the potatoes.
4. Let everything cook together on a gentle fire until tender and almost dry and mash or stir it quickly together with a wooden spoon or with a masher.
