## Ingredients

- one shad
- butter
- flour
- oil
- capers or gherkins, or sorrel sauce

## Directions

1. After the shad is well cleaned, roll it in butter and flour
2. Roast it on a grill between buttered papers that are folded closed (oil may also be used)
3. Place it on a dish with a sauce of capers or gherkins, or sorrel sauce
