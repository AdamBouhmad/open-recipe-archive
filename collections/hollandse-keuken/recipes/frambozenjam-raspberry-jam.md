## Ingredients

- 3 kg raspberries (cleaned)
- 2¼ kg sugar
- jam jars
- parchment paper
- cellophane
- elastic bands
- string
- labels

## Directions

1. Pick the raspberries, check carefully that there are no worms in them
2. Wash them briefly in cold water, put them in a pan, bring them to a boil with the lid on and let the fruit cook without the lid, stirring occasionally
3. Add the sugar and let the jam cook until it reaches the required thickness (see the rules on page 369)
4. Treat it further as strawberry jam (recipe 983)
