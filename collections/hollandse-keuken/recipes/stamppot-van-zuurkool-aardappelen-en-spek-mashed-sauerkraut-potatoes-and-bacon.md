## Ingredients

- 1 kg sauerkraut
- 1 1/4 kg potatoes
- 400 g salted bacon
- salt as needed

## Directions

1. Scrape the bacon or wash it with warm water.
2. Cook it with approximately 1/2 litre of water for 1/2 hour.
3. Wash the sauerkraut and lay it on the bacon.
4. Let the cabbage cook for 1/2 hour and then add the peeled and washed potatoes.
5. Make sure the potatoes are at the bottom of the pan, covered by the sauerkraut.
6. Let everything cook until tender.
7. Remove the bacon from the pan, mash or stir the cabbage and potatoes together and season to taste.
8. Serve the dish with the bacon sliced separately.
