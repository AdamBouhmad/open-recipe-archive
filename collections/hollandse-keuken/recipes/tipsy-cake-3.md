## Ingredients

- 8 sponge cakes (kolombijnen)
- 75 g sugar
- 1/2 liter milk
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 15 g cornstarch (Duryea brand)
- 1/2 deciliter cognac
- some marmalade or jam

## Directions

1. Slice the sponge cakes in half, spread them with some marmalade or jam and place them in a fireproof dish.
2. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the vanilla and sugar, pour this, stirring vigorously, to the cornstarch, which has been mixed with the egg yolks, put everything back in the pan and let the custard, stirring with an egg beater, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Pour it over the sponge cakes after stirring the cognac in off the heat.
4. Beat the egg white stiff, stir some sugar through it and spread it over the custard.
5. Place the dish in a moderately warm oven and let the egg white dry, but make sure it remains as light in color as possible.
