## Ingredients

- 1/4 veal kidney
- half the weight of the kidney in veal meat
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 small onion
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 dl broth
- about 2 dl milk
- 20 g butter or margarine
- 10 thin slices of bread without crust

## Directions

1. Soak the veal kidney in cold water for several hours.
2. Then place it in fresh water, bring it to a boil, pour off the water and let it cook done with fresh water and salt (about 1/2 hour).
3. Chop it finely with the meat.
4. Stir the onion and parsley, egg yolks, broth, milk, butter or margarine, pepper and salt to taste through it.
5. Make this mixture quite hot on the fire.
6. Slice thin pieces of bread, remove the crusts and make the pieces equal in shape.
7. Fry them with some butter or margarine in the pan light brown and crispy.
8. Lay a thick layer of the mixture on them and serve the toasts immediately on a warm dish decorated with a few fresh sprigs of parsley.
