## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- 250 g veal forcemeat
- 250 g pork forcemeat
- 1 egg
- 50 g old bread without crust
- 1/2 can truffels
- 1/2 dl Madeira (optional)
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 250 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Wash the turkey thoroughly inside and outside.
2. Mix the forcemeat in the usual way, but stir in the truffels cut into small pieces, which may be soaked in Madeira if desired.
3. Carefully loosen the breast skin of the turkey, but make sure it does not tear anywhere.
4. Fill the space between the breast meat and skin with the forcemeat (not too stiff!).
5. Sew the skin closed again, salt the turkey and roast it as indicated in the previous recipe.
