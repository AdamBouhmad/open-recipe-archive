## Ingredients

- ½ l milk
- 1 vanilla pod
- 4 egg yolks
- 15 g gelatin or 12½ g powdered gelatin
- 150 g sugar
- ¼ l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 150-200 g almond cookies
- For the sauce: 1 small jar apricot jam, some maraschino or kirsch

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with the sugar and the vanilla to a boil and pour enough of it to the beaten yolks so that they become thoroughly hot.
2. Return everything to the pan and let the custard bind on low heat, beating with a whisk, but do not boil.
3. Dissolve the soaked gelatin in it.
4. Let the pudding mixture cool and combine it with the stiffly beaten cream and stiffly beaten egg whites and let this custard become fairly stiff.
5. Toast the almond cookies on a baking sheet in the oven until they become brown.
6. Let them cool and pound them finely in a mortar.
7. Take a timbale mold, smear it with unbeaten egg white.
8. Put a layer of the custard in it and sprinkle some pounded almond cookies on top.
9. Continue this way until the mold is filled, ensure that the last layer consists of custard.
10. Let the pudding set firmly, turn it out and sprinkle the remaining almond cookie crumbs over it until it looks breaded.
11. Serve with an apricot sauce, which is prepared as follows: warm the jam with some water.
