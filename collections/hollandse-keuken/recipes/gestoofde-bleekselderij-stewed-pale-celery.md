## Ingredients

- 2 bunches celery
- 2.5 dl milk or celery stock
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the blanched and tender celery stalks into pieces of 6 to 8 cm length, peel the broad pieces and scrape the narrow pieces.
2. Wash them and place them in a pot with little boiling water and salt.
3. Cook them gently until tender (3/4 to 1 hour) and let them drain in a colander, optionally catching the liquid.
4. Prepare the sauce as indicated for cauliflower sauce (recipe 328), let it cook until done (10 minutes) and stew the celery in it (approximately 10 minutes).
