## Ingredients

- 1 kg melon flesh (the hard flesh of the melon, without the inedible skin, by weight)
- 20 g cinnamon stick
- 20 g cloves
- 1 kg sugar
- 1 bottle pickling vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the melon flesh without the outer skin into equal cubes and insert a clove and a small piece of cinnamon stick in each cube
2. Weigh the pieces and take the same weight of white sugar
3. Pour enough vinegar over the pieces so they are just covered, add the sugar and cook the melon until the pieces become translucent and glossy
4. Immediately place each piece that is done in a Cologne pot
5. When all pieces are removed, pour the liquid over them
6. If necessary, cook the liquid briefly
7. Close the pot with damp parchment paper and twine and label it with name and date
8. Let the pieces stand for at least 3 weeks before use
