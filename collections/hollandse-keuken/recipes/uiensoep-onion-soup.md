## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- bouillon
- roasted bread
- potato starch

## Directions

1. Cut the onions in half so that they fall apart in half rings, lengthwise
2. Bake them in butter until they have an orange color
3. Add bouillon and let them cook for another half hour
4. Serve the soup with roasted bread
5. If you prefer the soup thick, add some potato starch
