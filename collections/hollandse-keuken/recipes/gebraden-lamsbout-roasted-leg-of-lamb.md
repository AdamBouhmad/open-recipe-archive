## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg
- crushed rusk
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Beat and cut the leg well
2. Sprinkle it with salt, pepper, cloves and nutmeg, along with crushed rusk
3. Place it in a pan with fire above and below
4. When half done, sprinkle it once more with rusk and add lemon slices
5. Take care to baste it well
