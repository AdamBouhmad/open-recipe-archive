## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 175 g flour
- 1/2 l milk
- salt
- 80-100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g currants

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter as indicated for crepes (recipe 643).
2. Stir in the currants, which have been rinsed in lukewarm water and had their stems removed.
3. Fry thin pancakes with the butter or margarine, light brown and cooked through on both sides.
4. Serve with brown sugar.
5. Note: Do not make these pancakes too thin, as the currants will burn.
