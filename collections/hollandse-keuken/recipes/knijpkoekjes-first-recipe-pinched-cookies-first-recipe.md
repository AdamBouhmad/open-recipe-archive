## Ingredients

- 500 g flour
- 300 g sugar
- 150 g butter or margarine
- 2 egg yolks
- 5 g fine cinnamon
- some water

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients well together, add some water (approximately 1/2 to 1 dl), knead it in quickly and make approximately 80 to 90 small balls from this amount.
2. Clean the heated pinching iron with some salt and a piece of grey paper.
3. Grease it the first time with some butter or margarine, place it in the middle of a ball of dough, pinch the iron shut and bake the cookies above a moderate fire or above a gas flame on both sides until light brown and done.
4. Remove them immediately from the iron and let them cool.
