## Ingredients

- Beef roll (rollade)
- Pepper
- Salt
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Unroll the rollade, rub it well with pepper and salt, scatter ground cloves over it and some bay leaves, and let it stand in good wine vinegar for three days.
2. Now roll it up again and tie it closed.
3. Place it in a pot, well covered, and let it simmer slowly for 3 to 4 hours.
4. Eat it warm with anchovies, or cold with mustard and some pickled vegetables.
