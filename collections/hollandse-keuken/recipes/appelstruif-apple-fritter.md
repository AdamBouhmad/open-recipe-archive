## Ingredients

- applesauce
- rusk
- cinnamon
- currants
- a little butter
- grated lemon zest
- 6 to 7 beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix rusk into applesauce in proportion to the sauce, with some cinnamon, currants and a little butter
2. Add grated lemon zest and 6 to 7 beaten eggs
3. Stir everything well together
4. Bake with fire above and below until done
