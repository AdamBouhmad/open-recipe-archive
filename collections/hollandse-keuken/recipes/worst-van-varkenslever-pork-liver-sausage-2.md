## Ingredients

- two good fat livers
- onions
- bouillon
- two and a half ounces melted lard
- half a can pork blood
- a quarter can cream
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Chop the livers finely
2. Cook some onions with bouillon and chop them together with the liver
3. Add two and a half ounces melted lard cut into cubes, half a can pork blood, a quarter can cream, salt, pepper and spices
4. Cook slowly together, stirring constantly
5. When cool, stuff the sausage and roast on the grill when ready to eat
