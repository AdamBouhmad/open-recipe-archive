## Ingredients

- 200 g biscuits or sponge cakes
- 1 1/2 deciliters Madeira, rum, or maraschino
- 3 deciliters milk
- 2 eggs
- 40 g sugar
- 10 g cornstarch (Duryea brand)
- 1/2 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar
- some raspberry jelly
- red sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the biscuits in the Madeira, rum or maraschino and stack them pyramidally as high as possible on the plate.
2. Brush each layer with some raspberry jelly.
3. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the vanilla, pour this carefully, stirring vigorously, to the cornstarch, which has been mixed with the egg yolks.
4. Put everything back in the pan and let the custard, stirring with an egg beater, cook through for 3 to 4 minutes.
5. Pour it slightly cooled over the biscuits, let it cool further.
6. Then beat the egg white stiff, place it on top and sprinkle it here and there with some red sugar.
