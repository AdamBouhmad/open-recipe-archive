## Ingredients

- 2 kg Utrecht or St. John's onions
- 2 bottles pickling vinegar
- 20 white peppercorns
- 1 Spanish pepper
- some bay leaves
- some mustard seed

## Directions

1. Set the onions aside sprinkled with salt for several hours, no longer than 12 hours, to prevent them from becoming soft.
2. Then peel them with a fruit knife or stainless steel potato knife, place them immediately in cold water, wash them and let them stand with salt sprinkled on them for 24 hours.
3. Let them drain as dry as possible in a colander and put them in well-cleaned bottles (or in a Cologne pot).
4. Arrange the spices neatly between the glass and the onions and pour vinegar over them so that the onions are amply covered. Pour some salad oil on top.
5. Close the bottles or pots with damp parchment paper and paste a label with name and date on them.
