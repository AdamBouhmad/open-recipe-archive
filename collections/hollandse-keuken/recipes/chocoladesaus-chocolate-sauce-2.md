## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 15 g Duryea cornstarch
- 20 g Blooker's cocoa
- 60 g sugar
- 1/4 vanilla stick or vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Bring 3 1/2 dl milk slowly to a boil with the vanilla
2. Mix the cornstarch with the cocoa and the sugar together and blend them slowly with 1 1/2 dl milk into a thin paste without lumps
3. Pour this into the boiling milk while stirring, from which the vanilla has been removed
4. Let the sauce simmer briskly while stirring for 3-4 minutes
5. Stir occasionally while cooling to prevent a skin from forming
6. Present the sauce with semolina or cornstarch pudding etc.
