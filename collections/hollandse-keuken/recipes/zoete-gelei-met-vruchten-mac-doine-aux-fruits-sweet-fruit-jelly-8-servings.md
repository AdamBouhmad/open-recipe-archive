## Ingredients

- 1 l water
- 300 g sugar
- 55 g white gelatine or 50 g powdered gelatine
- juice of one lemon
- 2 to 3 egg whites
- 4 dl maraschino
- 200 g candied fruits or more

## Directions

1. Clarify the water, which is mixed with the sugar, the soaked gelatine, the lemon juice and the egg white, in the same way as given for aspic.
2. Pour the liquid through a doubled dense cloth.
