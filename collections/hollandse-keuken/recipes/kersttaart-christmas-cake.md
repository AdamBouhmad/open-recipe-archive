## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 200 g butter or margarine
- 250 g sugar
- 5 eggs
- 250 g sultana raisins
- 100 g candied peel
- 25 g candied orange peel
- 100 g almonds
- salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- milk
- 200 g powdered sugar
- 2 egg whites
- juice of ¼ lemon
- candied peel
- red cherries
- ¼ liter whipped cream with sugar
- silver balls

## Directions

1. Cream butter or margarine until fluffy
2. Add salt, lemon zest and eggs one at a time, stirring each egg in completely
3. Alternately add sifted flour mixed with baking powder and sugar
4. Stir until batter is thick, white and fluffy
5. Add milk if necessary
6. Finally add peeled and ground almonds with chopped candied peel and orange peel, and cleaned and dried raisins
7. Butter a round cake pan, pour in batter
8. Bake in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked through (2 to 2½ hours)
9. Test for doneness by inserting a knitting needle, which should come out dry
10. Turn out onto a cooling rack and let cool
11. After cooling, spread egg white glaze over the cake
12. Pipe figures with whipped cream that has been beaten stiff with sugar
13. Place silver balls here and there
14. Cut holly leaf shapes from the dark part of candied peel and make red berries from red cherries
