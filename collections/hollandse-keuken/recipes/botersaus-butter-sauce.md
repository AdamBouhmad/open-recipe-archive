## Ingredients

- ½ l water with salt or bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 80 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Melt 40 g of the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring to a boil while stirring.
2. Rub out all lumps carefully.
3. Add the liquid while stirring, in small amounts at a time, and let the sauce cook while stirring until done.
4. Add the remaining butter or margarine in small pieces at a time, after the sauce is removed from the heat.
5. Serve with haddock, cod, potatoes, hamburger rib, etc.
