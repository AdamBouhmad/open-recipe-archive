## Ingredients

- ½ l water with salt or bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce).
2. Wash the parsley, pluck off the leaves and chop or cut them as finely as possible.
3. Mix them with the sauce off the heat.
4. Serve with
