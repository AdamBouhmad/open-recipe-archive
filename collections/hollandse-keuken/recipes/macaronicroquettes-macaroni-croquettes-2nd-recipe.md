## Ingredients

- 50 g macaroni
- 2 dl tomato sauce
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt
- Maggi's aroma
- breadcrumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Prepare the croquettes as in recipe 120.
2. If desired, use vegetable scraps or braised soup vegetables as filling.
