## Ingredients

- Juice of twelve lemons and Chinese oranges
- One kilo sugar
- Half a can water
- Three bottles champagne wine
- One bottle arak

## Directions

1. Cook the juice of twelve lemons and Chinese oranges with one kilo sugar and half a can water until it reaches a thick syrup, well skimmed
2. When it has cooled slightly, add three bottles of champagne wine and one bottle of arak
3. Stir well
