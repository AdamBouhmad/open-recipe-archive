## Ingredients

- 1 measuring spoon tea per person
- fresh boiling water
- sugar
- milk

## Directions

1. Rinse the teapot with hot water.
2. Add 1 measuring spoon of tea per person into the teapot.
3. Pour fresh boiling water over the tea, using only as much water as needed for the number of cups (average content of 4 cups of tea equals 1/2 liter water; 4 measuring spoons of tea equals 10 grams).
4. Let steep 3-5 minutes under the tea cozy.
5. Do not use a tea light.
6. Stir the tea briefly before pouring.
7. Add sugar and milk to taste.
