## Ingredients

- 10 golden reinette apples
- approximately 2 dl Rhine wine
- 100 g sugar
- 40 g butter or margarine
- some fine cinnamon and chopped snippets or some marmalade

## Directions

1. Cream the butter or margarine, add some marmalade or some fine cinnamon and chopped snippets and 25 g sugar
2. Cut the peeled apples, carefully cleaned of brown spots, in half
3. Remove the cores, make shallow diamond-shaped incisions on the surface and fill the apples with a little of the mixture
4. Place them in a heat-proof dish, pour over the wine in which the remaining sugar is dissolved and let the apples become tender covered in a moderately warm oven (20 to 30 minutes), but ensure they remain whole
