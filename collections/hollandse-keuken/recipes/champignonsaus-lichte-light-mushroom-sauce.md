## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter bouillon
- mace
- thyme
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tin of mushrooms
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Let the spices steep in the bouillon, strain it and prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Mix it away from the heat with the beaten egg yolk, the mushrooms, either whole or cut in half, and some of the liquid from the mushrooms
