## Ingredients

- 1 kg roastbeef
- 150 g beef fat or 200 g butter or margarine
- 15 g salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Weigh the meat, wash it and rub it on all sides with salt (and some pepper).
2. Heat the fat in a roasting pan (or cut fresh fat, after washing it, into small pieces and roast these first). As soon as blue vapor comes from the fat, turn the meat on all sides through it, place the lid on the pan, reduce the heat and roast the meat on top of the stove, turning it occasionally, 15 minutes per 500 g.
3. Pour some water by the fat as soon as the color becomes too dark and the meat is evenly brown on all sides.
4. Remove the meat when it has roasted for the specified time. Test by pricking it to see if it is done inside.
5. Make the fat hot again until blue vapor comes off and a crust forms on the bottom of the pan. Pour in enough water so that the crust can dissolve in it and remove the jus immediately from the heat.
6. Alternatively: Make the fat so hot in a roasting pan that blue vapor comes off. Roll the weighed, washed and salted meat in it and roast it in a hot oven 10 to 15 minutes per 500 g. Baste it every 7 minutes with the fat during roasting. Pour water by the fat as soon as the meat is brown and the color would become too dark. Finish the jus as indicated above.
