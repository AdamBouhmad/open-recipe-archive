## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg large carrots
- 1 1/2 kg potatoes
- 350 g onions
- 1/2 kg brisket
- 40 g fat from gravy, beef fat or lard
- 3/4 litre water
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the brisket, place it in boiling water with salt and let it cook for 1 1/2 hours on a gentle fire.
2. Peel the carrots, wash them, shred them and add them to the meat.
3. Peel the potatoes and onions, cut them into quarters, wash them and add them after a quarter hour to the meat and carrots.
4. Add the fat if the brisket is lean and let everything cook together on a gentle fire until tender and almost dry (30 to 35 minutes).
5. Add more water if needed, but make sure the mash does not become too moist.
6. When everything is cooked, remove the meat from the pan and mash or stir it together with a wooden spoon or with a masher.
