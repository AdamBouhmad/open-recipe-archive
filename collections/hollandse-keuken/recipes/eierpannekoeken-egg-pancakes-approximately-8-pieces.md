## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 3-4 eggs
- salt
- 1/2 l milk
- 80-100 g butter or margarine
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with salt.
2. Add the flour and enough milk until the flour no longer floats.
3. Stir out the lumps from the batter and pour in the remaining milk.
4. Melt some butter in the frying pan, pour a spoonful of batter in and turn the pancake as soon as it has dried from below.
5. Pour some melted butter or margarine underneath and fry the pancake, turning it repeatedly and adding a small knob of butter each time, until golden yellow and cooked through on both sides.
6. Serve with white sugar.
