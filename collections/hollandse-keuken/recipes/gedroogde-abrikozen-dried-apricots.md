## Ingredients

- 200 g dried apricots
- 60 g sugar
- some sago

## Directions

1. Wash the apricots several times with lukewarm water, let them soak for 24 hours with 2½ times as much water as apricots.
2. Bring them to a boil with the same water and let them cook slowly until tender (approximately 10 minutes).
3. Add the sugar and carefully thicken the liquid with a little mixed sago.
