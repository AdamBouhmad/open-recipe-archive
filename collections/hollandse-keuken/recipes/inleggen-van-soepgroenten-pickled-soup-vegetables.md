## Ingredients

- 1 basket salad
- 1.5 baskets endive
- 1 basket sorrel
- 8 small baskets purslane
- fat

## Directions

1. Cook salad and endive completely and press very dry
2. Cook purslane and sorrel in their own liquid, keeping the liquid as it helps preserve
3. When all is cooled, mix together
4. Place in bottles with a layer of fat on top
5. Seal well with corks
