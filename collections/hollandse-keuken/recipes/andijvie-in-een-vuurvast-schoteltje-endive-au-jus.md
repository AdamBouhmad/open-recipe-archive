## Ingredients

- 4 heads of endive or 1 1/2 kg
- 1 1/2 deciliters bouillon or lean gravy with water
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the heads thoroughly in the usual manner.
2. Wash them under running water and tie them closed with string.
3. Cook them in salted water until tender (approximately 3/4 hour).
4. Let them drain in a colander, remove the strings, and place the heads in a heat-proof dish.
5. Add the bouillon or gravy with water, sprinkle breadcrumbs over it.
6. Distribute the butter or margarine in pieces on top, clean the edge of the dish, and let it brown in a moderately warm oven.
