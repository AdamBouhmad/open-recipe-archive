## Ingredients

- 6 thin slices of sweet, dark rye bread without crust
- 100 g grated cheese
- 10 g flour
- 15 g butter or margarine
- 1 dl milk
- salt
- Cayenne pepper or ground Spanish pepper
- 1 truffle

## Directions

1. Cut thin, oblong slices of rye bread in the shape of large domino stones.
2. Make a sauce in the usual way from butter or margarine, flour and milk; stir in 75 g grated cheese over heat and season with pepper and salt to taste.
3. Spread this mixture on the rye bread slices and sprinkle the rest of the grated cheese over them.
4. Cut thin strips of truffle and use these to divide the slices into two equal halves.
5. Using round-cut pieces of truffle, make eyes on the stones.
