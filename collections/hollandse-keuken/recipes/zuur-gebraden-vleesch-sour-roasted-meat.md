## Ingredients

- Rib roast
- Salt
- Bacon
- Pepper
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Lemon peel
- Shallots
- Wine and beer in equal parts
- Water
- Butter
- Wooden roasting rack

## Directions

1. Pound and salt the rib roast as needed, lard it with bacon and place it in an earthen pan with bones underneath.
2. Add pepper, onions, cloves, bay leaves, lemon peel and shallots.
3. Pour equal parts wine and beer over it, so the meat is completely covered, and let it cook until a layer of fat forms.
4. Remove it and let it cool in the cellar; thus prepared it can keep well even 14 days in summer.
5. When ready to roast, place the meat in the pan on a wooden roaster, add the cooking liquid and the herbs, along with some more butter, pour water over it and let it braise in the covered pan for an hour.
6. Remove the meat and strain the liquid through a sieve.
7. Return the meat to the pan, pour some of the strained liquid over it and continue roasting as needed.
