## Ingredients

- Shallots or onions
- Wine or vinegar
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop the shallots or onions finely
2. Add wine or vinegar, pepper, nutmeg, and cinnamon
3. Let it cook together until thick
