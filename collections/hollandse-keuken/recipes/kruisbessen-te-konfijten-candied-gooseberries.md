## Ingredients

- 3 parts sugar
- 7 and one-half parts gooseberries with stems and crowns removed

## Directions

1. Cook three parts sugar until it becomes syrupy; then add seven and one-half parts of berries with stems and crowns removed and let everything come to a boil
2. Then remove them to strain them, and finally cook the juice to a thick syrup
3. As soon as this is cold, place the berries with this syrup in bottles
