## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 liter milk
- 20 g white gelatin or 17 1/2 g powdered gelatin
- 80-100 g sugar
- 150 g almond macaroons (10-12 pieces)

## Directions

1. Wrap the almond macaroons in a piece of cheesecloth and let them steep in the milk
2. Remove them from the milk
3. Dissolve the sugar in the milk and pour the mass through a sieve in which a piece of cheesecloth has been laid
4. Prepare the pudding further as described in the previous recipe
