## Ingredients

- 250 g butter or margarine
- 250 g honey
- 225 g flour
- 25 g Duryea cornstarch
- 5 eggs
- 50 g honey for garnishing
- some whipped cream

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the yolks one by one.
2. Then add in small quantities the sifted flour and cornstarch and the honey.
3. Beat the egg white stiffly and stir the mass through with a few strokes as quickly as possible.
4. Put the batter in a greased cake pan and let it slowly become light brown and done in a moderately warm oven (approximately 1 hour).
5. Turn the cake onto a cake rack and let it cool.
6. Then spread some honey over it and spray the cake with whipped cream using a piping bag with a fluted nozzle.
