## Ingredients

- 1 fresh horseradish root
- 2 full tablespoons well washed currants
- cold water
- salt
- butter
- 1 to 2 teaspoons Liebig's Meat Extract

## Directions

1. Grate the horseradish root finely.
2. Set this grating on the fire with the currants in cold water.
3. Let this cook together for 1 to 1½ hours on a gentle fire, well covered, until a soft paste.
4. Add the necessary salt, a piece of butter the size of a nut and 1 to 2 teaspoons Liebig's Meat Extract, by which the sauce becomes strong and the flavor greatly enhanced.
