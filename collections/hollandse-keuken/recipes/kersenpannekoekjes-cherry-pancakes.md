## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 175 g flour
- 1/2 l milk
- salt
- 80-100 g butter or margarine
- 50 cherries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter the same way as for crepes (recipe 643).
2. Melt some butter or margarine in the frying pan, pour a couple of spoonfuls of batter onto it, immediately place some cherries divided into quarters and pitted and destemmed on it.
3. Press them into the batter and cover them with some batter.
4. Fry the pancakes on both sides until light brown and cooked through.
5. Serve with powdered sugar.
6. Note: See the note under the previous recipe.
