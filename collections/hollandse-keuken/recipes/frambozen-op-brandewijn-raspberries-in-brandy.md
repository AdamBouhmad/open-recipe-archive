## Ingredients

- 500 g raspberries
- 200 g sugar
- 6 cm cinnamon stick
- 0.5 l brandy

## Directions

1. Sort the raspberries carefully, remove any worms if necessary and wash them. Treat the fruit further as described in recipe 1031.
