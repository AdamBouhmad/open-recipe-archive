## Ingredients

- 75 g sauerkraut
- ¼ beet
- ½ head of lettuce
- 1 egg yolk
- 2½ dl oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the sauerkraut, if one does not know who has pickled it, cut it fine. Peel the beet and cut it into small pieces. Clean the lettuce in the usual way and pluck it into small pieces. Mix the vegetables with approximately 2 tablespoons of the mayonnaise, which is prepared with the lemon juice as in recipe 367.
