## Ingredients

- 4 slices ham (approximately 80 g)
- 4 eggs
- 20 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Fry the slices of ham on both sides briefly in the frying pan.
2. (If the ham is not fatty enough, the butter or margarine should be melted in the pan first.)
3. Break an egg carefully over each slice, fry them in the manner described in recipe 375, place them on a heated plate and serve them with pepper and salt.
4. Note: See note under recipe 375.
