## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon of beef or 1 liter water and 4 Maggi's bouillon cubes (recipe 35)
- 40 g macaroni
- 50 g veal ground meat
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Cook the broken and washed macaroni in the bouillon, which is prepared in the usual manner and strained, until tender (approximately 3/4 hour), or cook it in water in which the bouillon cubes are later dissolved.
2. Let the meatballs prepared in the usual manner (recipe 37) cook in the soup for the last 10 minutes.
