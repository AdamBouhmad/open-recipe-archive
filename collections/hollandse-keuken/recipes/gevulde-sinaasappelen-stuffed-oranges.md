## Ingredients

- 1 orange
- 1 banana
- 12 cherries (50 g)
- ⅛ bottle whipped cream (for 6 oranges)

## Directions

1. Halve the cleaned orange and hollow it out. Peel the banana and beat it foamy. Pit and wash the cherries. Mix the orange flesh with the banana mash and the cherries. Carefully remove the white fibers from the orange skins and clean them well. Fill them with the mixture and garnish with whipped cream and a cherry.
