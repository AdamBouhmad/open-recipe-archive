## Ingredients

- orange or mandarin peel
- lemon peel
- sugar
- brandy

## Directions

1. Peel the well-cleaned oranges or mandarins and lemons (for 6 oranges or 8 mandarins take 1 lemon) as thinly as possible, so that no white remains on the peel. Put the peel with the sugar in the well-cleaned bottle. Pour brandy over it so that the peel is well covered. Seal the bottle airtight and let the contents stand for at least 3 months before using.
