## Ingredients

- 2 l milk
- 1 1/2 vanilla stick
- 80 g sugar
- 20 g Duryea maizena

## Directions

1. Bring the milk slowly to a boil with the vanilla and let it steep gently for a moment.
2. Remove the vanilla, add the sugar and the mixed maizena and let the milk simmer for 3-4 minutes while stirring.
