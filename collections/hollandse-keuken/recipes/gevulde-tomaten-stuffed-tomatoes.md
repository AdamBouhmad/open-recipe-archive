## Ingredients

- 4 large tomatoes
- 150 to 200 g ground calf meat
- 20 g old bread or breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- 25 g onion (optional)
- parsley
- 25 g butter or margarine
- breadcrumbs
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the tomatoes, wash them, dip them briefly in hot water, remove the skin, cut off the top cap and hollow them out carefully.
2. Mix the ground meat in the usual way with soaked old bread, pepper, salt and nutmeg, optionally mixed with finely chopped onion and parsley.
3. Melt 10 g butter or margarine in the pan and cook the meat in it, stirring constantly, until done (10-15 minutes), but ensure it does not brown.
4. Fill the tomatoes so the meat protrudes slightly above the rim.
5. Place them in a fireproof dish with a piece of butter or margarine on each tomato.
6. Press the fruit flesh and caps through a sieve and add this with water, lemon juice and salt to the tomatoes.
7. Let the tomatoes, sprinkled with breadcrumbs, stew in a mild oven for 15-20 minutes until done.
