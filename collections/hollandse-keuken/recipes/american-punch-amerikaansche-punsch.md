## Ingredients

- Juice of twelve lemons
- One can red wine
- One can rum
- Five ounces sugar
- Half a can water
- Half a can strong tea water

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar with half a can of water and skim
2. Mix together the lemon juice, red wine, rum, sugar syrup, and strong tea water
3. Stir everything well
