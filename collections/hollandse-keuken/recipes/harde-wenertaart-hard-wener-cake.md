## Ingredients

- 200 g flour
- 200 g butter or margarine
- 150 g brown sugar
- 1 egg or 2 egg yolks
- jam
- water glaze
- candied fruit

## Directions

1. Make a dough prepared according to recipe 770
2. Stir in the egg or yolks
3. Bake 3 cakes on a baking sheet or in sand cake rings (20-30 minutes)
4. Let the dough layers cool
5. Spread them with jam
6. Stack the cakes on top of each other
7. Cover the top with water glaze (see recipe 819)
8. Decorate the pastry with a border of finely cut candied fruit
