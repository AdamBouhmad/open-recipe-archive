## Ingredients

- 1 dl water with salt
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 60 g flour
- 2 eggs
- Filling: 50 g butter or margarine, 150 g grated cheese, 1 egg yolk, pepper, salt

## Directions

1. Bring the water with the butter or margarine to a boil
2. Stirring, sprinkle the sifted flour in and keep stirring until the dough comes away from the pan like a ball
3. Then add the eggs one by one away from the heat
4. Using a piping bag or butter syringe, or lay small mounds of this dough on a buttered baking sheet with a teaspoon and bake the puffs light brown and cooked through in 10 to 15 minutes in a hot oven
5. For the filling, stir the butter or margarine until creamy, mix it with the egg yolk and stir in the grated cheese with some pepper and salt
