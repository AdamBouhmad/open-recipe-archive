## Ingredients

- 4 dl lean jus with water
- 50 g onion
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 359.
2. Remove from heat and mix with mustard, which has been mixed with water or some sauce to prevent lumps, and sugar.
3. Serve with pork cutlets.
