## Ingredients

- 3 lemons
- bread sugar
- a glass Rhine wine or white currant juice
- 12 loot melted fish glue

## Directions

1. Grate 3 lemons and squeeze the juice over them
2. Add bread sugar and a glass Rhine wine or white currant juice
3. Stir this into 12 loot melted fish glue
4. Let it come to a boil briefly
5. Pour into molds or small glasses
6. When cold, turn out onto the serving dish
