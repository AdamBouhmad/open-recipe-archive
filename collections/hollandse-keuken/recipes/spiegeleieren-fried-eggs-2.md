## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 30 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Warm the butter or margarine, but not brown, in the frying pan.
2. Carefully crack the eggshells on the rim of the pan, let the eggs slide into the butter, but make sure the yolk remains whole and in the middle.
3. Fry them on a gentle heat until the white has set, place them on a heated plate and serve them with pepper and salt.
4. Note: You can also place the eggs in a flat heat-resistant plate in a moderately warm oven until the white has set.
