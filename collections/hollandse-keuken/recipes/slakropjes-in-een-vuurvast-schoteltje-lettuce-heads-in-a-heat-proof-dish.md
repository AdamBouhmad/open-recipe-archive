## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg braising lettuce
- 1 1/2 deciliters bouillon or lean gravy with water
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut away the withered leaves and a small piece of the stem.
2. Wash the heads under running water until all sand is removed and cook them in salted water until tender (approximately 1/2 hour).
3. Prepare them further as endive in recipe 427.
