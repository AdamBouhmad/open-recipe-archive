## Ingredients

- 7.5 ounces flour
- Butter and milk
- 5 eggs
- 4 spoons sugar
- 2 grated lemon zests
- 2 ounces almond paste
- A piece of yeast

## Directions

1. Make a batter with butter and milk, flour, eggs, sugar, lemon zests and almond paste
2. Add yeast and let rise
3. Put in molds
4. Bake in a tart pan
