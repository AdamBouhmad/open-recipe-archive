## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg beef steak
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 5 g salt
- some pepper
- some milk and water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat off or scrape it with a knife.
2. Rub it in on both sides with the salt (and pepper).
3. Make 1/2 of the butter or margarine brown in the frying pan, which has been heated well beforehand.
4. Place the steak in it, sear it closed and fry the steak, continuously sliding back and forth and turning over, but especially not pricking, evenly brown on both sides.
5. Allow 8 to 10 minutes per 500 g for cooking time.
6. Remove the meat from the pan, add the remaining butter or margarine and let it brown so that a crust forms at the bottom of the pan.
7. Remove the pan from the heat and let the fat cool a bit.
8. Add some milk and let it brown on the fire.
9. Finish the gravy with some water.
