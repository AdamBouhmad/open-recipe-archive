## Ingredients

- Boiled veal head
- Butter
- Onions
- Flour
- Cooking liquid from the head
- Raisins
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Salt
- Syrup
- Vinegar or red wine

## Directions

1. Cut a boiled veal head, the heart and if desired the lung, into small pieces
2. Fry a couple of finely chopped onions in butter and add a spoonful of flour, stirring until everything is brownish
3. Add some of the liquid in which the head was cooked
4. Add to the sauce some raisins, pepper, cloves and salt, a spoonful of syrup and some vinegar or red wine
5. Let everything cook well together in the sauce
