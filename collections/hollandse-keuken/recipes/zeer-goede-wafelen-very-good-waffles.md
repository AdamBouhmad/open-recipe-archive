## Ingredients

- 6¼ ounces fine flour
- 1¼ ounces buckwheat flour
- 12 eggs with whites, plus 3 more without whites
- 1½ cups butter
- 2 quarts sweet milk, lukewarm
- 1 tablespoon yeast mixed with brown sugar
- 1 good beer glass rainwater

## Directions

1. Mix flour, buckwheat flour, eggs, butter, milk, and yeast mixture to make a batter.
2. When baking, add 1 good beer glass of rainwater.
