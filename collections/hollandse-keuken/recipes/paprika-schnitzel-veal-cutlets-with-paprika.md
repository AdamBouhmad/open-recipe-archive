## Ingredients

- 4 beautiful, lean, flat veal cutlets of at least 100 g each
- Salt
- 1 egg
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 1 to 2 red or green bell peppers

## Directions

1. Fry the cutlets as indicated in the Wiener Schnitzel recipe.
2. Remove the stems from the bell peppers.
3. Cut the fruits lengthwise in half.
4. Remove the seeds and seed membranes.
5. Wash the halved fruits and cook them in a little salted water until tender (10 to 20 minutes).
6. Cut each half into thin strips.
7. Fry these briefly in the hot butter at the end.
8. Garnish the cutlets with them, or make paprika sauce and pour it over the schnitzels when serving.
