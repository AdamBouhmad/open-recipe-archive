## Ingredients

- one or two chickens
- water
- salt
- rice
- ground mace
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop one or two chickens into pieces and cook them in water, skimming well
2. When the meat is well cooked, strain the broth through a sieve and hang it with the meat again, adding rice, mace and salt; cook until the soup is bound
3. When serving, it is good and tasty to beat a couple of egg yolks through it
4. You can also add vegetables to this soup
