## Ingredients

- Cherry flesh
- Red wine
- Sugar
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the flesh of cherries with red wine and sugar until soft
2. Press it through a sieve
3. Let it boil once more
4. Bind it with egg yolks
