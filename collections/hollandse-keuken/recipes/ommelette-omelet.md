## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons wheat flour
- 4 eggs
- 4 teacups sweet milk
- A little sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Mix together the wheat flour, eggs, sweet milk, and sugar
2. Pour the batter into a pan with butter
3. Prick it with a fork until it is set
4. Add butter as desired
