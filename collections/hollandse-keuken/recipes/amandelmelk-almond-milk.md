## Ingredients

- quarter pound peeled sweet almonds
- 1 can water
- sugar

## Directions

1. A quarter pound of peeled sweet almonds is finely pounded, soaked on a can of water for an hour, subsequently pressed through a cloth, and sugar added to taste
