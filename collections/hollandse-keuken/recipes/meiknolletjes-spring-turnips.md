## Ingredients

- 20 small spring turnips
- 2 1/2 dl milk
- 20 g flour or 10 g cornstarch
- 20 g butter or margarine
- salt
- finely cut fresh greens or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips, wash them (cut them into quarters if they are large) and cook them in water with salt until tender (1/2 hour).
2. Let them stew for 10 minutes in the sauce made from butter, flour and milk, which is prepared in the same manner as cauliflower sauce (recipe 328) or cornstarch sauce (recipe 329).
3. Just before serving, stir in some of the finely cut green tops or grate some nutmeg over the dish.
