## Ingredients

- a piece of cold roasted lamb
- jus from the roast
- one tablespoon capers
- one lemon
- a knob of butter rolled in flour
- salt
- veal bouillon

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cold roasted lamb, cut it into pieces and add some of the jus
2. Add one tablespoon capers, one lemon, a knob of butter rolled in flour, salt and some veal bouillon
3. Everything must be braised together well
