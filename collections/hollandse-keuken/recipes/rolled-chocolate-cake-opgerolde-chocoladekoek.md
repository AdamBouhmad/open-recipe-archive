## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 50 g sugar
- 35 g flour
- 15 g cornstarch
- 20 g chocolate powder
- 30 g melted butter or margarine
- ½ l chocolate custard
- chocolate glaze and candied fruits or sugared violets

## Directions

1. Bake the cake as described in recipe 827, after stirring the melted butter or margarine into the dough at the end.
2. Fill and garnish the rolled chocolate cake.
3. If you want to fill the cake with a cream or custard filling that cannot be spread on the warm baked goods, then roll the cake unfilled and let it cool somewhat, hanging in a cloth.
4. Roll the cake out again, spread the filling between and roll the baked good up again.
