## Ingredients

- 1 l milk
- 150 g rice
- some salt
- 1/2 stick vanilla or vanilla sugar
- 125 g sugar
- 16 g white gelatin or 14 g powdered gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- 2.5 dl whipped cream
- 1 small can apricots or 200 g apricots cooked in the usual way

## Directions

1. Cook rice porridge in the usual way with the vanilla (recipe 577).
2. Mix it, just before the rice is done, with the sugar, but make sure the rice grains are not ground fine.
3. Soak the leaf gelatin 10 to 15 minutes in cold water, then dissolve it in the rice porridge removed from heat.
4. Let the mixture cool.
5. Remove the vanilla.
6. Beat the cream and egg whites stiff.
7. Add the rice porridge to it and let this mixture stand, stirring occasionally, until it becomes gelatinous and the rice grains no longer sink.
8. Drain the apricots, layer them alternately with the rice porridge in a mold rinsed with unbeaten egg white, but make sure the first and last layer consists of rice porridge.
9. Let the pudding become cold and stiff, turn it out onto a damp dish and serve with apricot sauce.
10. Use the apricot liquid in the sauce.
