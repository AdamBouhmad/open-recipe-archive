## Ingredients

- 12 smoked herrings
- butter oil, salad oil, butter or margarine
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut off the head of the smoked herrings
2. Cut open the back along the backbone, split the fish open
3. Remove the backbone, innards, black membrane and swim bladder, but if possible leave the soft roe or hard roe inside
4. Clean the smoked herrings or (better) wipe them with a damp cloth
5. Dry them with a dry cloth and rub them in with pepper and salt
6. Heat the oil until blue steam rises and fry first the opened side brown, then the outside
7. Let them drain on gray paper
