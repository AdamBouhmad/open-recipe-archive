## Ingredients

- fruits according to season
- sugar
- lemon juice
- vanilla or custard cream (optional)
- whipped cream with superfine sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel and pit the fruits if necessary and divide them into pieces or slices if they are large.
2. Make the pieces thin if the fruits are not soft.
3. Add lemon juice to give the dish a fresh taste.
4. Arrange the fruits sprinkled with sugar and sprinkled with lemon juice in a glass bowl and let them stand for about 1.5 hours.
5. Stir the mass from time to time.
6. Cool the fruit salad.
7. Serve the dish, if desired, with vanilla or custard cream or with whipped cream, which has been beaten with some superfine sugar.
