## Ingredients

- 1 l bouillon
- mace, Spanish pepper, carrot, onion, cloves and bay leaves
- 50 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 50 g ground veal
- 1/2 tin of mushrooms
- pieces of cooked ox tongue (optional)
- English sauce and soya or Madeira to taste

## Directions

1. Make bouillon with the herbs in the usual manner
2. Strain it and add it while stirring to the butter or margarine and flour, which have been browned together
3. Let the soup simmer while stirring until cooked (10 minutes)
4. Add the cooked meat balls, the mushrooms and if desired pieces of ox tongue
5. Remove from heat and add English sauce and soya or some Madeira to the soup
