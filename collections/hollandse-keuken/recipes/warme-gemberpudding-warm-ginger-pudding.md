## Ingredients

- 200 g old bread without crust
- 100 g candied ginger
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 3.5 dl milk
- 35 g sugar
- zest of a lemon

## Directions

1. Make the bread fine in the boiling milk
2. Add the egg yolks, the sugar, the grated lemon zest and the ginger cut into pieces, and if desired some ginger syrup
3. Mix this mass as lightly as possible with the stiffly beaten egg white and fill a warm pudding mold greased with butter or margarine and sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs 3/4 full
4. Close it with a lid greased with butter or margarine firmly and cook the pudding 1.5 hours au bain-marie, but ensure the water is always refilled to just below the rim of the mold
5. Let the pudding dry out a little without the lid and serve with warm vanilla sauce
