## Ingredients

- 1 liter bouillon of beef or 1 liter water and 4 bouillon cubes (recipe 35)
- 30 g rice
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dl cream
- piece of mace

## Directions

1. Cook the washed rice in the usual manner until tender.
2. Bring butter or margarine and flour to a boil while stirring, add the strained bouillon or the water in which the bouillon cubes have been dissolved while stirring, and let the soup simmer while stirring (approximately 10 minutes).
3. Add the cooked rice and carefully add the soup to the egg yolk beaten with the cream in the soup tureen.
