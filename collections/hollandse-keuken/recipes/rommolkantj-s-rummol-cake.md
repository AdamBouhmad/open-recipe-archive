## Ingredients

- 7.5 ounces flour
- butter and milk
- 5 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 grated lemon peels
- 2 ounces almond paste
- a piece of yeast

## Directions

1. Beat together flour, butter and milk
2. Add 5 eggs, 4 tablespoons sugar, 2 grated lemon peels and 2 ounces almond paste
3. Add a piece of yeast and let it rise
4. Place in molds and bake in a tart pan
