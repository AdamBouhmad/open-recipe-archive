## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 30 g grated cheese
- 50 g butter or margarine
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Treat the eggs as indicated in recipe 29
2. Rub the yolks completely fine, gradually add the butter or margarine beaten to cream, the grated cheese, pepper and salt to taste
3. Fill the half whites with this so that the yolks stick up a bit and the shape of a whole egg is obtained
4. Note: See note under recipe 29
