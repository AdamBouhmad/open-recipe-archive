## Ingredients

- sauerkraut
- vinegar
- butter
- pickled bacon or sausage (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse once
2. If it becomes too mild, add a splash of vinegar
3. Cook, shaking from time to time, until nearly dry
4. Let a small piece of butter melt in it
5. Can also be stewed with pickled bacon or sausage; some use smoked bacon
