## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 150 g rice
- 75 g sugar
- salt
- 1/2 vanilla stick, vanilla sugar, or 1/2 lemon zest
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cook thick rice porridge in the usual way (see recipe 577), but let the vanilla or lemon zest cook in it.
2. Mix some of the sugar into the rice porridge after the flavoring has been removed.
3. Stir the egg yolk smooth with the rest of the sugar and add a little of the mixture to warm the yolk beforehand.
4. Mix everything together and add the stiffly beaten egg white at the end.
5. Place the mixture in small molds rinsed with unbeaten egg white or water and let the mixture cool.
6. Turn out the puddings in the known way, garnish them with a fruit and serve with fruit juice sauce, for example apricot sauce or raspberry sauce.
