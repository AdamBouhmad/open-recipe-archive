## Ingredients

- pears, peeled
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- a splash of red wine or berry juice
- cochineal (optional, for color)

## Directions

1. Stew the peeled pears with water, sugar, cinnamon, and a splash of red wine or berry juice.
2. For a nice color, cochineal may be added.
