## Ingredients

- 12 bunches asparagus
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Break off the hard ends of the asparagus.
2. Scrape the part where the head sits and peel the lower part.
3. Wash the asparagus and arrange it in well-cleaned bottles.
4. Pour on enough water so that the asparagus is just covered, add salt if desired.
5. Close the bottles with ring, lid and clip(s) and sterilize for 1½ hours.
6. Let them cool with the clips on, remove the clips and attach a label with name and date.
