## Ingredients

- head lettuce
- hard-boiled eggs
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- mustard
- bread sugar
- tarragon and pimpernel (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the outermost leaves of the heads and cut the hearts in half, then wash them.
2. If the lettuce is rather hard, beat a few drops of oil into the water.
3. When serving, lay some hard-boiled eggs on top of the salad and toss them with oil, vinegar, and a little salt.
4. A little mustard and bread sugar make it very pleasant. Tarragon and pimpernel may also be added.
5. For wilted head lettuce, soak it for a couple of hours in cold water and remove the outermost leaves and the thick stems that are hard.
