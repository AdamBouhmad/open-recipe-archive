## Ingredients

- 1 pound wheat flour
- ½ cup buckwheat flour
- 6 eggs
- 1 quart sweet milk
- ½ quart water
- ½ pound melted butter
- 1 tablespoon thick yeast with little sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and add cold water so it reaches the right temperature.
2. Beat the eggs first, then add some of the milk and water.
3. Add the flour, then more milk and water, and beat well.
4. Add ½ pound melted butter and 1 tablespoon thick yeast with little sugar.
5. Let rise quickly by the fire, then set the pot aside until baking.
6. Before baking, stir in a glass of cold water.
