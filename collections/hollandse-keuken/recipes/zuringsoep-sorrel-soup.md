## Ingredients

- 1 l salted water or bouillon
- 200 g sorrel
- 1 head of lettuce
- chervil
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 35 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg (optional)
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the vegetables, wash them, put them on to cook with the boiling water or bouillon, the salt and the Spanish pepper and let them cook until done (approximately 1/4 hour).
2. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve, rub the vegetables through. Melt the butter or margarine and bring it to a boil while stirring with the flour, pour the sieved liquid in while stirring and let the soup cook until done (approximately 10 minutes).
3. Finally add the very finely chopped parsley.
