## Ingredients

- one pike
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- anchovies
- lemon peel
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the pike into pieces and salt them, not too harshly
2. Cook them in water
3. Make a sauce of butter, a little flour, some anchovies with bones removed, a lemon peel, pepper and a glass of water
4. Let these cook together, then add the pike and let everything braise well together, adding lemon juice from time to time
