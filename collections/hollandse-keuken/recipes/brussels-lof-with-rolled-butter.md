## Ingredients

- 1 kg Brussels lof
- 80 g butter or margarine
- 2 eggs
- nutmeg
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the lof as indicated in recipe 444.
2. Remove it with a skimmer from the pan, let it drain as dry as possible and place it on a warmed flat plate.
3. Serve with the halved hard-boiled eggs, rolled butter (see recipe 365) and nutmeg.
