## Ingredients

- 2 large chickens
- 100 g butter or margarine
- salt
- 100 g rice
- 3/4 l aspic
- truffles
- lettuce leaves
- For chaud-froid sauce: 4 dl bouillon, 50 g flour, 65 g butter or margarine, 3 leaves white gelatine or 1/2 spoon powdered gelatine, some cream

## Directions

1. Roast the chickens until done and cut nice pieces from breast and legs.
2. Let these cool.
3. Draw bouillon from the bones for the sauce and for the aspic.
4. Make a thick sauce from the butter or margarine and flour with the bouillon and dissolve in it the gelatine that has been soaked in cold water and well wrung out.
5. Finish the sauce with cream and let it cool, stirring from time to time.
6. Draw the chicken pieces through it.
7. Garnish the chicken pieces with small figures of truffle, parsley, egg white and tomato.
8. Glaze the entire surface with aspic, which begins to cool and thus becomes stiff as soon as it is poured over the chicken pieces.
9. Make a base of cooked rice on the plate.
10. Put some aspic on this.
11. Place the chicken pieces on it, which are entirely covered with chaud-froid sauce.
12. Chop the remaining aspic finely and garnish the plate with it.
13. Place a piece of truffle here and there and garnish the plate with fresh lettuce leaves.
