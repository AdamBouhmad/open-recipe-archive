## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liter milk
- 100 g oatmeal
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring the milk with salt to a boil, stirring sprinkle in the oatmeal.
2. Let the milk come to a boil again while stirring and let the porridge cook.
