## Ingredients

- 2 kg venison haunch
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions
- 2 bay leaves
- 10 peppercorns
- 9 cloves
- some salt
- 1/4 l water
- 1/4 l vinegar
- 100 g larding bacon
- 200 g butter or margarine
- 1/4 dl cream or yoghurt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, place it for 2 to 3 days in half water, half vinegar with the spices.
2. Turn it over from time to time in the meantime.
3. Rub the venison haunch with salt, lard it and roast it 25 to 30 minutes per 500 g in the butter or margarine.
4. Finish the gravy with cream (or yoghurt) and the liquid in which the meat was placed.
