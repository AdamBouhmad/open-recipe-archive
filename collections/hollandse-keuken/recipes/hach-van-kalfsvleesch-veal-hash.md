## Ingredients

- cold veal, cut into small pieces
- finely chopped onions
- brown sugar
- pepper
- crushed cloves
- salt
- potato starch
- vinegar or red wine

## Directions

1. To cold meat cut into small pieces, add finely chopped onions, brown sugar, pepper, finely crushed cloves, a little salt, potato starch, and vinegar or red wine as desired
2. Braise for 1.5 hours
