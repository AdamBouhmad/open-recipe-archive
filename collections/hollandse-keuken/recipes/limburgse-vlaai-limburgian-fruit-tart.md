## Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 50 g butter or margarine
- 5 g salt
- 50 g sugar
- 20 g yeast
- 1 egg
- 1.5 dl lukewarm milk
- fruit compote (e.g., apricot or cherry compote)

## Directions

1. Make bread dough in the usual way (see recipe 835) with an egg, melted butter or margarine and sugar mixed in.
2. Roll out the risen dough on a flour-dusted surface to a layer approximately 1/2 cm thick, the size of the form in which the vlaai is to be baked (usually a shallow large pastry form).
3. Grease the form with butter or margarine, cut out a circle of dough larger than the form.
4. Lay the dough layer in it and ensure the raised edge of the form is also covered with dough.
5. Prick the bottom with a fork and let it rise for another half hour under a damp cloth.
6. Bake the vlaai in a hot oven until cooked through and light brown (20 to 30 minutes).
7. Remove from the form and fill with fruit compote after cooling, or fill the vlaai before baking and sprinkle sugar over the fruit.
