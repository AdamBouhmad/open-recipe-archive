## Ingredients

- 4 crushed rusk
- 3 beaten eggs
- almond essence
- 1 grated lemon zest
- cinnamon
- sugar
- nutmeg
- sufficient butter
- 1 piece yeast
- berry juice sauce
- cinnamon
- sugar
- lemon zest
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a batter of proper thickness from 4 crushed rusk, 3 beaten eggs, almond essence, 1 grated lemon zest, cinnamon, sugar, nutmeg, sufficient butter and 1 piece of yeast
2. put in a mold and let it rise
3. when risen, close the mold well
4. set it half-way in boiling water and let it cook for a good hour
5. for this pudding use a sauce of berry juice, cinnamon, sugar and lemon zest, cooked a long time and then strained, after which butter is added and it is boiled once more
