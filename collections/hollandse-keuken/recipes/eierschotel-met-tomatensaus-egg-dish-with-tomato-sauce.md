## Ingredients

- 4 hardgekookte eieren (hard-boiled eggs)
- 3 dl tomatensaus (tomato sauce)
- 75 g rijst of gort (rice or barley)
- zout (salt)
- peterselie (parsley)

## Directions

1. Cut the hard-boiled and peeled eggs lengthwise in half.
2. Place them with the flat side on a warmed plate.
3. Place the cooked rice (see recipe 569) as quickly as possible in egg cups rinsed with cold water and pour them between the eggs.
4. Cover the eggs with the tomato sauce (see recipe 340) and present the rest separately.
5. Place a sprig of parsley here and there.
