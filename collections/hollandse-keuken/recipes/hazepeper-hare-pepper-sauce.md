## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- 1 liter lean gravy with water or 1 liter broth
- a piece of Spanish pepper
- 3 bay leaves
- 6 cloves
- 50 g onion
- 120 g flour
- 120 g butter or margarine
- some English sauce and soy or some red wine
- 20 g sugar
- approximately 1/2 dl vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted hare into pieces; add enough water to the lean gravy
