## Ingredients

- 8 dl berry juice
- 7 dl water
- 175 g tapioca
- 175 g sugar
- piece of cinnamon stick or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Rinse the tapioca and soak it for 3 hours in the water mixed with berry juice.
2. Bring it to a boil with the flavoring and let it cook on a gentle fire, stirring occasionally, until cooked (approximately 1 1/2 hours).
3. Remove the flavoring and serve the sugar separately or stir it through while the dish is still warm if it is to be served cold (approximately 175 g for this quantity).
