## Ingredients

- 1 kg lamb leg
- 100 g beef fat or margarine
- 15 g salt
- some pepper

## Directions

1. Cut away some of the fat if the fat layer is too thick
2. Rinse the meat, rub it with some pepper and salt and roast it in the same manner as roastbeef (recipe 227)
3. Calculate roasting time as 30 minutes per 500 g
4. Finish the gravy in the usual manner
