## Ingredients

- 100 g butter or margarine
- 100 g flour
- 100 g bastard sugar
- 100 g almonds
- marmalade or jam

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds in warm water, peel them and grind them finely.
2. Knead them with the butter or margarine, the sugar and the flour into a firm ball and divide it into two equal parts.
3. Press one half into a greased tart pan, but make sure the layer of dough is as thin as possible. (When using a small tart pan, divide the dough into 3 parts.)
4. Bake it in a moderately warm oven until light brown and cooked (15 to 20 minutes).
5. Let the cake cool, turn it out and bake the second half in the pan.
6. Let it cool as well and place both parts on top of each other with some jam or marmalade in between.
7. Sprinkle the tart with sifted powdered sugar or tie a colored ribbon around it.
