## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- 100 g butter or margarine
- 80 g flour
- lemon
- pepper
- salt
- 4 dl bouillon or water with salt

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit as indicated in recipe 304.
2. Brown the pieces in the butter or margarine, which has been made light brown in a pan, evenly brown.
3. Place them in a braising pan.
4. Add the flour to the butter or margarine, stir out the lumps and slowly pour the bouillon or water with the salt while stirring, let the sauce cook through while stirring (approximately 10 minutes), add some lemon juice, pour this over the rabbit, place a couple of notched lemon slices in it and stew the meat for another 45 minutes on a low heat, stirring occasionally.
