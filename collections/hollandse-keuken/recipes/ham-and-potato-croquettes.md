## Ingredients

- 200 g ham
- 250 g cooked potatoes
- 50 g old bread without crust
- milk or water
- pepper
- salt
- 30 g butter, margarine or frying fat
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Chop the ham finely, mix it with finely mashed potatoes and the bread, which has been soaked in water or milk and squeezed between two spoons.
2. Add pepper and salt to taste and stir everything together until well combined.
3. Form small balls from the mixture, roll them in breadcrumbs and fry them in a pan in which the butter has been made light brown, or in hot frying fat on all sides until brown.
