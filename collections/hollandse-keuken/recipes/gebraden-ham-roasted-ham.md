## Ingredients

- ham
- ground biscuit
- pepper
- water
- red wine

## Directions

1. Treat it as described above, but cook it for only one hour
2. Then remove the rind and sprinkle it with ground biscuit and pepper, some water and red wine, now let it roast in a roasting pan, with fire underneath and above, and finish roasting until done
