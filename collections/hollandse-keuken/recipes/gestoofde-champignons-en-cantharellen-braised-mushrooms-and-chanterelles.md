## Ingredients

- approximately 500 g mushrooms or chanterelles
- 40 g butter or margarine
- salt
- a few drops of lemon juice
- flour
- a few drops of milk

## Directions

1. Cut or break off the woody base parts of the stems. (Also brush these bits clean and use them to make some mushroom broth.)
2. Brush and scrape the mushrooms clean, cut them in half if they are large, wash and rinse them repeatedly until no sand remains.
3. Place the mushrooms on very low heat with butter or margarine, salt and a few drops of lemon juice.
4. Let them cook slowly (approximately 20 minutes).
5. Bind the liquid that has formed on the mushrooms with flour that has been mixed with milk and adjust the seasoning if needed.
