## Ingredients

- 250 g cooked lentils
- 30 g stale bread
- 50 g onion
- 1 raw egg
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 100 g butter or margarine
- juice of 1/4 lemon
- bread crumbs
- salt
- one large apple (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in the usual manner.
2. Make them fine and mix them with the soaked and finely made bread, the very finely chopped and fried onions, the finely divided hard-boiled egg, the egg yolk, and lemon juice.
3. Make 8 patties from this mixture and coat them in bread crumbs.
4. Beat the egg white with 1 spoon of water and coat the patties with it.
5. Coat them again in bread crumbs and brown them on both sides.
6. Finish the sauce with lentil water.
7. If desired, place a slice of fried apple on each patty.
