## Ingredients

- Half a pound flour
- Half a pound butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 large cups milk
- 1 pint curdled milk
- 5 drams breadcrumbs
- 4 eggs
- 1 and a half measures thick cream
- 1 ounce white sugar
- 4 drams sliced sweet almonds
- Raisins and currants
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Make a tender dough with flour, butter, eggs and milk
2. Divide into flat cakes
3. Prepare a cream with curdled milk, breadcrumbs, eggs, thick cream, white sugar, sliced sweet almonds, raisins and currants
4. Spread this cream finger-thick on the dough
5. Brush with beaten egg white
6. Bake in the oven
