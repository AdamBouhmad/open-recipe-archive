## Ingredients

- Three pigeons, cleaned
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Six hard-boiled egg yolks
- Pastry crust with lid
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Cut the legs, neck and wings off the cleaned pigeons
2. Cook everything together in a pan with bouillon, salt and pepper
3. Let them cook until nearly done
4. Place them in a pastry crust mold with a lid
5. Add six hard-boiled egg yolks beforehand
6. Stick the pigeon legs on top of the pastry lid
7. Brush everything with egg white
8. Bake in the oven until done
