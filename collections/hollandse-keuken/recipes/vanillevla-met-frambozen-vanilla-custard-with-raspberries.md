## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter melk
- 40 g suiker
- 20 g maizena Duryea
- 1 ei
- 1/2 stokje vanille or vanillesuiker
- 250 g frambozen
- 40 g suiker
- een paar droppels citroensap

## Directions

1. Sort through the raspberries, wash them and let them drain as dry as possible in a sieve.
2. Set aside several raspberries, sprinkle the others with suiker.
3. Place them at the bottom of the custard dish and sprinkle them with citroensap.
4. Prepare the custard as described in recipe 657.
5. Remove the vanilla, let it cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin from forming.
6. Pour the custard over the raspberries.
7. Garnish the custard with the reserved fruits.
