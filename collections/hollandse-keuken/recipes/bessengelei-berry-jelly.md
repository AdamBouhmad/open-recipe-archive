## Ingredients

- berries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove berries from their stems
2. Squeeze the berries
3. Add sugar, using 3 ounces sugar for every 5 ounces of juice
4. Cook, carefully watching for foam, until thick enough that it clings to a spoon
5. Pour layer by layer into jars
6. Cover the jars with paper soaked in brandy
