## Ingredients

- salted herring
- water
- milk

## Directions

1. Soak the herring for some hours in water with some milk.
2. Make an incision along the back.
3. Cut open the belly side, remove the intestines, roe or milt and the black membrane that surrounds the intestines.
4. Pull off the skin.
5. Cut off the head.
6. Wash the herring thoroughly clean and cut it into diagonal pieces.
7. Arrange these in the form of the fish on the platter.
