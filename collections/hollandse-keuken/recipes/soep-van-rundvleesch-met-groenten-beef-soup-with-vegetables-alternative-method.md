## Ingredients

- 1 Dutch pound beef
- salt
- half ounce rice
- two bunches celery
- handful sorrel
- handful chervil
- bunch endive
- some pieces cauliflower
- head of lettuce
- handful purslane
- finely ground mace

## Directions

1. Cut 1 Dutch pound beef into pieces
2. Cook it with salt, paying good attention to skimming
3. Add half ounce rice, two bunches celery, a handful of sorrel, a handful of chervil, a bunch of endive, some pieces of cauliflower, a head of lettuce, a handful of purslane and a little finely ground mace
