## Ingredients

- shrimp
- salt
- parsley
- butter
- wine

## Directions

1. Cook the shrimp
2. Put them on a colander and scatter salt over them
3. Peel them
4. Chop them together with a handful of parsley
5. Stew them gently with butter in a pan
6. Add some wine and let them stew
