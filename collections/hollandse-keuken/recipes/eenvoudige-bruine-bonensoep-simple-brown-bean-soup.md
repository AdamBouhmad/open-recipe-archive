## Ingredients

- 400 g brown beans
- 2 1/2 l water
- salt to taste
- 4 leeks
- 1 bunch celery greens
- 60 g butter, margarine, or beef fat

## Directions

1. Wash the beans and soak them for 24 hours in cold water
2. Bring them to a boil in this water and let them cook on low heat for 1 1/2 hours
3. Add the cleaned and washed vegetables and salt, and let the soup cook slowly for another 1 1/2 hours
4. Rub the beans fine so that the soup is evenly bound and stir in the butter, margarine, or fat
