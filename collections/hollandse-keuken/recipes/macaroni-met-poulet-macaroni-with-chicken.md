## Ingredients

- 100 g macaroni
- 200 g calf chicken
- 100 g ground calf meat
- 1/2 l boiling water
- pieces of mace
- 30 g flour
- 30 g butter or margarine
- 1 egg (optional)
- lemon juice
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine
- old bread soaked
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the chicken, place it in the boiling water with salt and mace and let it simmer gently for one quarter hour.
2. Break the macaroni into pieces, wash it and add it to the chicken, letting it cook together on a low heat for 1/2 hour.
3. Stir from time to time to prevent sticking, but ensure the macaroni does not break.
4. Let the meat balls, made with soaked old bread, pepper, salt and nutmeg, cook along for the last 10 minutes.
5. Melt the butter or margarine, add the flour and bring to a boil while stirring.
6. Rub out all lumps and add 3 dl of the remaining cooking liquid while stirring.
7. Let the sauce cook while stirring (10 minutes), carefully add it to the beaten egg and mix with lemon juice, the macaroni, chicken and meat.
8. Place the mixture in a fireproof dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
9. Place a few pieces of butter or margarine on top, clean the edges and let it brown in a moderately warm oven.
