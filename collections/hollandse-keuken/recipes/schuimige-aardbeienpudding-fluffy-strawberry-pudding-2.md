## Ingredients

- 500 g strawberries
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 100 to 125 g brown sugar
- 1/4 l whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 17.5 g gelatin with 1/2 leaf red, or 12.5 g powdered gelatin

## Directions

1. Pick the strawberries, wash them, select the prettiest ones to put whole in the pudding later and set them aside dusted with some sugar.
2. Rub the rest through a horsehair sieve.
3. Soak the leaf gelatin 10 to 15 minutes in cold water.
4. Dissolve it with the sugar in a little boiling water.
5. Let this solution cool.
6. Stir it through the strawberry puree.
7. Add the sifted lemon juice.
8. Stir the mixture through the stiffly beaten cream and stiffly beaten egg whites.
9. Mix it with the reserved strawberries.
10. Stir the mass occasionally.
