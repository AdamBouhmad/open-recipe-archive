## Ingredients

- 2 pounds best rum
- 2 and one-half ounces lemon juice
- 2 and one-half ounces powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Two pounds best rum, two and one-half ounces lemon juice and two and one-half ounces powdered sugar stir together until the sugar is well dissolved, and then put it in bottles, which you seal well
