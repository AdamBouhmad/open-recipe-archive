## Ingredients

- 1 l bouillon in which the liver has been cooked
- piece of onion
- 6 cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 50 g veal mince
- pieces of liver
- 40 g flour
- 40 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Fry the flour with the butter or margarine and the finely chopped onion and spices evenly brown. Add the liver bouillon and let the soup cook for 20 minutes.
2. Then pour it through a sieve, add the meatballs made in the usual way and let them cook until done. Add a few cubes of leftover liver and serve small cubes of fried bread with the soup.
