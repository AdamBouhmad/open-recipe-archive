## Ingredients

- 500 g cherries
- 150 g sugar
- 6 cm cinnamon stick
- 0.5 l brandy

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries. Wash them, let them drain. Put them in the well-cleaned bottle with the sugar, the cinnamon stick and the brandy. Seal the bottle airtight and let the contents stand for at least 3 months before using.
