## Ingredients

- 1 Dutch pound beef, cut into pieces
- salt
- 1.5 ounces rice
- two bunches celery
- a handful of sorrel
- a handful of chervil
- one head of endive
- some pieces of cauliflower
- one head of lettuce
- a handful of purslane
- a little ground mace

## Directions

1. Cut 1 Dutch pound of beef into pieces
2. Cook it with some salt, skimming well
3. Add 1.5 ounces rice, two bunches celery, a handful of sorrel, a handful of chervil, one head of endive, some pieces of cauliflower, one head of lettuce, a handful of purslane, and a little ground mace
