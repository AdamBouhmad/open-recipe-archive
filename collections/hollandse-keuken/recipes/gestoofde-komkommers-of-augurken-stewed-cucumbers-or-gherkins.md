## Ingredients

- yellow cucumbers
- butter
- vinegar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them into long strips
2. Stew them in a pan with some butter and vinegar
3. When serving, bind the liquid with a couple of egg yolks
