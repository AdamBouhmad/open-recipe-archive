## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork trotters
- 150 g ground veal
- 50 g old bread
- salt and nutmeg
- 1/2 tin of truffles or truffle peelings
- 150 g pork caul
- frying fat
- breadcrumbs for coating
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rinse the trotters with lukewarm water and let them cook in boiling salted water until tender (approximately 2 hours)
2. Carefully remove all bones and chop or grind the meat as finely as possible
3. Stir the ground veal in a saucepan over a small fire until it turns gray
4. Cut the truffles or truffle peelings, which may optionally have been soaked in Madeira, into small square pieces and mix them with the ground meat, the soaked bread, the meat from the trotters, the salt and nutmeg
5. Form this mixture into elongated rolls (2 by 8 cm)
6. Roll each one in a piece of pork caul that has first been soaked in lukewarm water
7. Double coat them and fry them in steaming frying fat or salad oil
8. Let them drain on gray paper
9. Place them against a center piece of toasted bread
10. Garnish the dish with fresh parsley sprigs and serve with salad
