## Ingredients

- beef and veal (in ratio 2 to 3)
- flour
- 4 large onions
- 2 to 3 Spanish peppers
- 1 to 2 tablespoons curry
- meatballs with chopped meat or hard egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the beef and veal into thin strips, roll them in flour and fry them in the pan
2. Cut 4 large onions into slices
3. Let them cook with 2 to 3 Spanish peppers, skim off the fat
4. Bind the soup with flour mixed with 1 to 2 tablespoons curry
5. Serve with meatballs with chopped meat or hard egg yolks
