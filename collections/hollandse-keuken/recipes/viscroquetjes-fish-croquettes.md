## Ingredients

- 150 g fish leftovers (without bones or skin)
- 1 1/2 dl milk, cream, or half milk, half cream
- 20 g flour
- 20 g butter or margarine
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- 1/2 egg or one egg white for breading
- bread crumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Make the fish, after removing the bones and skin, fine.
2. Prepare the sauce as indicated in recipe 102.
3. Mix it with the fish and spices and let the mixture, spread on a board, become stiff.
4. Form the croquettes in the manner as indicated for meat croquettes (see recipe 116), bread them double, and treat them further as meat croquettes.
