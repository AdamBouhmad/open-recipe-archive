## Ingredients

- lean veal
- bacon
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- spices
- parsley
- bay leaves
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Slice lean veal to the thickness of a finger
2. Beat 5 or 6 pieces well and lard them thickly with bacon
3. Put butter in a pan and place the fricadelles lard-side down
4. Braise them gently until cooked
5. Turn them and let them rest a bit longer
6. Remove them and rub a tablespoon of flour well through the pan
7. Add a spoon of bouillon, stirring first to prevent discoloration
8. Return the fricadelles to the pan
9. Add the necessary bouillon with spices, parsley, some bay leaves, and lemon slices
10. Braise it all well together
