## Ingredients

- 250 g almonds
- 500 g white sugar
- + 2 egg whites
- some lemon juice
- wafer papers
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Grind the peeled almonds twice with the sugar and egg white through an almond mill.
2. Add some lemon juice and if necessary more egg white until a fairly loose dough is obtained.
3. Put this in the piping bag fitted with a smooth funnel and pipe oblong strips diagonally onto a wafer paper.
4. Turn the wafer paper over onto white paper sprinkled with chopped almonds, press the cookies in slightly, turn the wafer paper back over, lay it on a baking sheet and bake the Wellingtons in a moderately warm oven until light brown (++ 20 minutes).
5. Break the wafer paper away along the side of the cookies.
