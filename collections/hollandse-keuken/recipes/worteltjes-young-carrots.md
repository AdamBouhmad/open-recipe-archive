## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch or 1 kg cleaned carrots
- 35 g butter or margarine
- salt
- finely chopped parsley
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots and make a cross in the thick top part, but do not place the scraped carrots in cold water as they will lose their sweet flavor.
2. Wash them quickly once they are all ready and place them in boiling water so that the bottom of the pan is well covered.
3. Let them cook until tender and dry, stirring occasionally (young carrots - 20 minutes, older carrots - approximately 30 minutes).
4. Add water during cooking if needed to prevent burning.
5. Stew them for 5 minutes with the butter or margarine and shake in the fine parsley away from the heat.
