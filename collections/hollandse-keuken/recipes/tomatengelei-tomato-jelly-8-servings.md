## Ingredients

- 1 l water
- 1 kg tomatoes
- 8 white peppercorns
- together 1.5 l liquid
- 1 small onion
- 1.5 bay leaf
- salt
- 60 g white gelatine
- 2 to 3 egg whites
- strips of hard-boiled egg white
- capers

## Directions

1. Make strained tomato juice in the known way.
2. Clear this liquid as described in the aspic recipe.
3. Place some strips of hard-boiled egg white and some capers on the bottom of timbale moulds rinsed with cold water and fill the moulds with the jelly.
4. Turn the jelly out onto a moist plate.
5. Serve it with some lettuce and mayonnaise or ravigote sauce.
