## Ingredients

- 150 g flour
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 2.5 g ground cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon fine cinnamon or 5 g speculaas spices
- some nutmeg
- one spoon milk
- 75 g dark brown sugar
- one pinch baking powder
- some rice flour

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients, with the sugar dissolved in the milk, into a ball
2. Press it firmly into a speculaas mold sprinkled with rice flour
