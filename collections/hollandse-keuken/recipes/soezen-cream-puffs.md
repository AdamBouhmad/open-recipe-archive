## Ingredients

- 2.5 ounces butter
- 0.5 quart water
- 5 eggs
- 12.5 drams flour

## Directions

1. Cook 2.5 ounces butter with 0.5 quart water thoroughly while stirring constantly and vigorously
2. Let it cool somewhat
3. Beat 5 eggs until they foam well
4. Mix in 12.5 drams flour
5. Add the cooked butter and beat smooth
6. Fill molds halfway and place in a tart pan with heat only from below, uncovered, and let bake until done
