## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 5 tablespoons wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- cold water
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat 5 eggs, 5 tablespoons wheat flour and 1 tablespoon sugar with cold water into a thin batter
2. Pour through a funnel into boiling butter
3. Let it brown in the butter
