## Ingredients

- 8 beautiful small and perfect conference pears
- 1/4 l water
- 100 g sugar
- the juice of 1 lemon and a piece of lemon zest
- approximately 50 g shredded roasted almonds
- 100 g sugar for caramel

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, leave the stems on
2. Stew them gently until tender in water with sugar, lemon zest and lemon juice
3. Let them drain and place them in a deep dish
4. Make caramel from the 100 g sugar (see caramel custard recipe 680) and pour it over the pears when golden brown
5. Sprinkle immediately with the shredded almonds
6. Let the pear liquid with the caramel remaining in the pan come to a boil once, strain the liquid and serve it as a sauce with the pears
