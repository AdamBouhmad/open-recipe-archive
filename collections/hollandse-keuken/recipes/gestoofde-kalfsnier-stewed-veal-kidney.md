## Ingredients

- 2 veal kidneys or 3 pork kidneys or 1 beef kidney
- 4 dl lean gravy with water or bouillon
- some flour
- bay leaf
- 2 cloves
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 100 g onion (1 large)
- 30 g flour
- 60 g butter or margarine
- parsley or English sauce and soya

## Directions

1. Soak the kidneys for several hours in cold water, remove the fat and slice them diagonally.
2. After sprinkling the slices with pepper and salt, roll them in some flour and fry them brown in the browned butter or margarine.
3. Remove them from the frying pan.
4. Add the flour and the minced onion to the fat and fry this light brown.
5. Add the warm bouillon, stirring.
6. Add the spices and let the sauce simmer briefly.
7. Stew the kidney pieces in it for half an hour.
8. Stir from time to time to prevent burning.
9. Add, if desired, at the last moment some very finely chopped parsley or English sauce and soya and remove the spices from the ragout.
