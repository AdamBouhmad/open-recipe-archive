## Ingredients

- 200 g macaroni
- 250 g ham
- 75 g Parmesan or old Gouda cheese
- 1 1/2 to 2 dl milk
- 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the macaroni and cook the tubes whole in boiling salted water (approximately 1/2 hour to 45 minutes)
2. Place enough on the bottom of a butter or margarine-greased pudding mold to just cover the bottom, lay a layer of chopped ham and grated cheese mixed together on top
3. Add more macaroni again, but ensure it covers the sides of the mold as much as possible
4. Continue until all is used
5. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper, add the milk and pour this mixture into the mold
6. Close it in the usual manner and cook the pudding 2 hours in a bain-marie, ensuring the water is refilled to just below the rim of the mold
7. Turn the pudding onto a warm, moist plate and serve it with a savory cream sauce (recipe 337)
