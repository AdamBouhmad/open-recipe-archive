## Ingredients

- Upper part of beef rump
- Beef fat
- Butter
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Cloves
- Vinegar
- Water
- Salt
- Bay leaves

## Directions

1. Lard the upper part of a beef rump with beef fat, rolled beforehand in a mixture of butter, pepper, nutmeg and cloves.
2. Then place it for a couple of days in vinegar.
3. Braise the meat in a pan with a little vinegar and water, sufficient salt and a couple of bay leaves.
