## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg fruit
- one kind of fragrant fruit, such as strawberries, or mixed, such as strawberries with peaches, or strawberries with pineapple
- various fresh fruit as desired: strawberries, raspberries, cherries, pears, grapes, pineapple, oranges, mandarins or peaches
- canned or bottled fruit
- 400 g brown sugar
- lemon juice
- 2 bottles Rhine wine or May wine or alcohol-free wine
- 1 bottle champagne or soda water

## Directions

1. Clean the fruit; remove the pits.
2. Cut them into neat small pieces if necessary.
3. Let them stand covered for several hours with the sugar.
4. Then add the wine and as much sieved lemon juice until the drink is flavored to taste.
5. Let the mixture stand in a cool place for several more hours.
6. Just before serving, add the chilled champagne or chilled soda water.
7. Present the bowl as cold as possible.
8. When using canned or bottled fruit, the liquid can also be used.
