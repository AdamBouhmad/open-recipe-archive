## Ingredients

- rear part of a sheep
- wooden pins
- paper
- jus

## Directions

1. Take the rear part of a sheep
2. Break the two leg bones and beat it strongly
3. Secure it with wooden pins and give it some shape
4. Cover it with paper
5. Baste it well
6. Serve it with the jus poured over
