## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- 3 1/2 dl salad oil
- approximately 1/2 dl vinegar
- 1 1/2 teaspoon mustard or mustard powder
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- chervil
- tarragon
- pimpernel
- shallot

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce like mayonnaise as described in recipe 367.
2. Finally stir in the washed and very finely chopped herbs.
