## Ingredients

- Three dozen oysters
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Water in which the oysters were cooked
- One and a half ounces butter
- Chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Bring the oysters to a boil and remove them as soon as the water boils
2. Let them drain
3. Put them in a pan with water, butter, and flour mixed together, along with the water in which the oysters were cooked
4. Add one and a half ounces of butter
5. Optionally add chopped parsley
