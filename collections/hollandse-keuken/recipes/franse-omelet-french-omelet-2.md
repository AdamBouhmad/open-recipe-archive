## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons water
- pepper
- salt
- Maggi's Aroma
- 50 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the pepper, salt and some Maggi's Aroma.
2. Add the water and pour them into a hot frying pan in which the butter or margarine has been melted, but especially not browned.
3. Prick continuously with a fork until the eggs begin to set over the entire surface.
4. Let the omelet become very light brown on the bottom and fold it in three.
5. Serve the omelet with salad or bread or fill it with ragout.
