## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 30 g sugar
- 30 g flour
- a piece of cinnamon stick or 1 teaspoon fine cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix the flour (with the fine cinnamon) and the sugar with a little cold milk into a thick paste
2. Stir out the lumps and add enough milk until a thin paste is obtained
3. Bring the remaining milk (with the cinnamon stick) to a boil
4. Pour the mixed paste in while stirring and let the sauce simmer while stirring (approximately 10 minutes)
5. Present the sauce with cooked rice, barley, etc.
