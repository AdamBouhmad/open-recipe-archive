## Ingredients

- livers of haddock or cod
- pepper
- salt
- 4 thin slices old bread without crust

## Directions

1. Cook the liver of haddock or cod in the usual manner until done (see recipe 179), rub it finely and mix it with some pepper and salt.
2. Place this mixture on oblong square pieces of bread, which have been stripped of the crust and lightly toasted in the oven.
