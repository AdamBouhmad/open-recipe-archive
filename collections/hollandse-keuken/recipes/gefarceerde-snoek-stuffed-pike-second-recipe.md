## Ingredients

- 1 pike with some eel, together yielding 1 kg fish meat
- 3 eggs
- 100 g old bread without crust
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs
- 100 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned fish in the usual way until done (see recipes 182 and 189). Remove the meat from the bones while still warm, chop it finely and mix it with the eggs, the soaked bread and the seasonings. Give it the shape of a pike in a fireproof dish. Sprinkle the fish with breadcrumbs, add the butter or margarine and lemon juice and let it brown in a moderately warm oven, basting it from time to time with the butter or margarine.
