## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 200 g sugar
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white as stiff as possible, quickly mix it with vanilla and approximately 175 g sugar and immediately spread it over a dish.
2. Sprinkle the remaining sugar, which has been finely crushed or ground in a mortar, over it through a sieve and let the dish become dry and crisp in a very lukewarm oven (approximately 1 hour), but ensure it stays as light in color as possible.
3. Preferably leave the oven door open.
