## Ingredients

- 2 salted herrings
- tomato purée

## Directions

1. Soak the herring for several hours in water with a little milk
2. Make an incision along the back and cut open the belly side
3. Remove the intestines, roe or milt, and the black membrane around the intestines
4. Pull off the skin and cut off the head
5. Wash the herring thoroughly clean
6. Carefully remove the backbone and small bones
7. Cut the herring into diagonal pieces
8. Place them in a section of the serving dish
