## Ingredients

- 150 g flour
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 2.5 g ground cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon fine cinnamon, or 5 g speculaas spices
- some nutmeg
- one spoon milk
- 75 g dark brown sugar
- a pinch baking powder
- some rice flour

## Directions

1. Knead all ingredients, with the sugar dissolved in the milk, into a ball and press it firmly into a speculaas mold dusted with rice flour.
2. Cut away any excess dough with a sharp, thin knife, carefully lift the cookies from the mold and place them on a baking sheet dusted with flour, spaced some distance apart.
3. Bake them in the middle of a fairly hot oven until light brown and done (approximately 15 minutes), remove them from the sheet and let them cool.
