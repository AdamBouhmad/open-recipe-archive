## Ingredients

- 500 g ground veal
- 150 g grated coconut
- 2 kemiri nuts
- 2 tablespoons brown Javanese sugar
- salt
- 1 teaspoon sereh powder
- 60 g soaked bread
- 1 egg
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Mix the ground veal with the grated coconut
2. Fry it without fat until light brown
3. Pound it fine in the mortar with the spices, salt and sugar
4. Mix it with the beaten egg and the soaked bread
5. Add milk if necessary
6. Form into balls
7. Fry these in the hot frying fat
8. Let them drain on grey paper
