## Ingredients

- Bouillon
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Mace
- Bread sugar
- Salt
- Crushed biscuit
- Grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Cook bouillon with vinegar, butter, mace, bread sugar, salt, and crushed biscuit
2. Stir in grated horseradish
3. This sauce must be very thick
