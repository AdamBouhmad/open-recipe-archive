## Ingredients

- 1 tin of salmon or lobster
- mayonnaise
- aspic in 2 colors
- some peas and small carrots from a tin

## Directions

1. Place some salmon or lobster in the shells
2. Pour enough mayonnaise over so that the fish is just covered
3. Further garnish the shells with finely chopped aspic in two colors (for example white and red), small carrots and peas
4. The mayonnaise can be lightly colored rose by carefully stirring in some melted red aspic
5. Then let the mayonnaise set, but especially not become too stiff
6. If the shells must be made some time in advance, it is best in any case to mix the mayonnaise with some white or red aspic, which will make it better cover the fish
