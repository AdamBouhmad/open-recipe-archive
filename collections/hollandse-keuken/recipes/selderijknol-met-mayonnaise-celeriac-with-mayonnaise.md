## Ingredients

- 1/2 celeriac
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the celeriac into very thin slices, peel them and cut them into long, thin strips.
2. Wash them and set them aside for some time sprinkled with salt and sprinkled with some vinegar.
3. Let them drain afterwards, mix them with some mayonnaise and place them on the plate.
4. Sprinkle some very finely chopped parsley over them.
