## Ingredients

- one suckling pig
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- a good knob of butter
- thin slices of bacon
- an orange or lemon

## Directions

1. Rub the suckling pig well inside with pepper, salt and cloves, put a good knob of butter inside and close the belly again with skewers
2. Bend the legs as if it were lying on its knees, cover it with thin slices of bacon and roast it in a roasting pan or on a spit, basting constantly
3. When serving, remove the skewers and place an orange or lemon in the pig's mouth
