## Ingredients

- 2 bottles pickling vinegar
- 40 g sugar
- 20 g kitchen salt
- 10 g ground turmeric
- 10 g ginger powder
- 60 g flour
- 120 g mustard powder
- a little over 1 kg vegetables, such as: cauliflower, carrots, princess beans, snap beans, broad beans, garden peas, onions, gherkins, cucumbers

## Directions

1. Mix the turmeric, ginger and mustard powder, salt and flour with some of the cold vinegar into a thin paste.
2. Bring the remaining vinegar to a boil, pour the mixed mixture through while stirring and let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Meanwhile, clean the vegetables, divide them into smaller pieces if necessary, wash them and cook them for 5 minutes in boiling water. Dry them in a cloth.
4. Cook them for 5 minutes in the sauce, put the mixture in well-cleaned small bottles. Make sure the vegetables are under the sauce, pour a little oil on top and close them with damp parchment paper. Provide them with a label with name and date.
