## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg veal steak
- 75 g butter or margarine
- 5 g salt
- some milk

## Directions

1. For preparation see beef steak; however use no pepper.
2. Let the butter or margarine become light brown, because the meat is light and must bake fairly long (15 minutes per 500 g).
3. Finish the gravy as indicated for beef steak.
