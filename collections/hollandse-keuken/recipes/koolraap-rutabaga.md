## Ingredients

- approximately 1 kg rutabaga
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour or 10 g cornstarch (Duryea maizena)
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the rutabaga into not too thick slices, peel them within the yellow edge and cut them into strips.
2. Wash them, place them in boiling water so that they are just covered, add salt and let them cook until tender (approximately 1 hour).
3. Place them in a colander to drain and stew them for 5 minutes in a sauce prepared in the same way as cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328) or cornstarch sauce (see recipe 329).
4. Grate some nutmeg over them in the bowl.
