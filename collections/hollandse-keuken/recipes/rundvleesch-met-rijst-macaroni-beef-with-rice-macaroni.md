## Ingredients

- Beef
- Rice-macaroni
- Beef bouillon
- Pepper
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cook rice-macaroni with the bouillon from the beef until thick.
2. When serving, add pepper, butter and parmesan cheese.
