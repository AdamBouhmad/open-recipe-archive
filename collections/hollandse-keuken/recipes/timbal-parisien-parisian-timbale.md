## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 65 g brown sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 5 g white gelatin
- 1/2 tin pineapple
- 8 g white gelatin or 7 g powdered gelatin
- 60 g brown sugar
- 1 dl maraschino or kirsch
- 1/4 l whipped cream
- some carmine
- 2 1/2 dl milk
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 vanilla stick
- 50 g sugar
- 2 1/2 dl cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a lemon bavarois for the pudding lining in the manner of lemon pudding (recipe 709).
2. Let this slide along the bottom and sides of a timbale mold rinsed with unbeaten egg white when it has almost stiffened, until it is completely covered.
3. Make the filling in the manner of pineapple pudding (recipe 723), but add some maraschino or kirsch and some carmine.
4. Put this mixture into the lemon bavarois when it has become fairly stiff.
5. Let the pudding cool, turn it out, and serve with sauce prepared in the manner of vanilla sauce, but which, as it is bound with egg yolks only, must not boil.
