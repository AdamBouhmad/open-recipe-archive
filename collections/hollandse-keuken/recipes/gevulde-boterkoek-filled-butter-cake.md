## Ingredients

- For the dough: 500 g flour, 400 g butter or margarine, 400 g sugar, 1.5 eggs
- For the filling: 125 g almonds, 125 g sugar, 1.5 eggs, finely grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Knead butter or margarine, flour, sugar and 1.5 eggs into a firm ball.
2. Divide the dough in half and press one half into a greased cake pan.
3. Grind the peeled almonds finely, mix them with sugar, the beaten egg and the grated lemon zest and pass the almond mixture through the mill once more.
4. Place the almond mixture on the dough in the pan, spread the other half of the dough over it and bake the cake in a moderately warm oven until light brown and done (1 to 1.5 hours).
5. Check if it is done by inserting a knitting needle; it should come out dry.
6. Turn the cake out onto a cake rack and let it cool.
