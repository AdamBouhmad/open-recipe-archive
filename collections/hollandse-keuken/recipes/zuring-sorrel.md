## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg sorrel
- 35 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Strip the sorrel and rinse it several times in abundant boiling water
2. Set it on with as little water as possible and let it cook until done, stirring occasionally (15 minutes)
3. Let it drain well in a colander
4. Stew it for 5 minutes with the salt and butter or margarine
