## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Blooker's cacao
- 4 teaspoons sugar
- 1 1/2 dl milk

## Directions

1. Mix the cacao with the sugar in the designated cup.
2. Stir in cold milk until a smooth paste without lumps is obtained.
3. Pour the boiling milk over it.
