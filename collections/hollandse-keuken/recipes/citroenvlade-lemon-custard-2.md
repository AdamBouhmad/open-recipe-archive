## Ingredients

- 0.5 bottle white wine or Rhine wine
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 grated lemon peel
- cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Cook 0.5 bottle white wine or Rhine wine with the juice of 2 lemons and 1 grated lemon peel, with a piece of cinnamon
2. Let it cook a bit more while stirring well
3. Pour quickly over
