## Ingredients

- rooster
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- fine salt
- fresh butter
- bouillon
- flour
- ginger
- cloves
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut off the head, tail and half the wings.
2. Wash it with half water and half vinegar.
3. Sprinkle with pepper and fine salt and place on the spit.
4. Roast slowly, constantly basting with fresh butter.
5. Make a sauce from bouillon, fresh butter, flour, ginger, cloves and pepper.
6. Let the sauce boil and pour it over the rooster.
7. When serving, place some lemon slices on top and fasten the head, tail and wings back on with pins.
