## Ingredients

- half lean beef
- half lean pork
- bacon
- salt
- coarsely ground pepper

## Directions

1. Mince the beef, pork, and bacon very finely together with salt and coarse pepper
2. Stuff this meat into wide casings
3. Leave in salt for two days
4. Smoke for 10 days
