## Ingredients

- one pork kidney
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onions, finely chopped
- red or white wine
- flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Take a pork kidney and place it in a pan with some butter, salt and pepper, parsley and a little finely chopped onions
2. Keep turning the kidney over, then remove it and make a sauce over it with red or white wine with some flour and bouillon
