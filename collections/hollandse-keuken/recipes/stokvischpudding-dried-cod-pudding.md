## Ingredients

- 1 measure milk
- 10 eggs
- 4 ounces melted butter
- 3 ounces grated bread
- cooked dried cod, cleaned of bones and skins
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat 10 eggs and stir them with 4 ounces melted butter together
2. Add 1 measure milk
3. Add 3 ounces grated bread and cooked dried cod cleaned of bones and skins
4. Add salt and nutmeg
5. Stir everything well and put it in a mold
6. Cook it for half an hour
7. Serve with an anchovy sauce
