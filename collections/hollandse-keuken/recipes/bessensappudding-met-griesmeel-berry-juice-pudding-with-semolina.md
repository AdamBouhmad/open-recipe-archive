## Ingredients

- 7½ dl berry juice
- 2½ dl water
- 90 g semolina
- 175 g sugar
- piece of cinnamon stick or peel of ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Bring the berry juice with the water and flavoring to a boil.
2. Sprinkle in the semolina and sugar slowly, stirring constantly, and let the pudding cook while stirring until done (5 to 10 minutes).
3. Remove the cinnamon stick or lemon peel, pour the mixture into a stone mold rinsed with cold water and let it cool and become firm.
4. Turn the pudding out onto a moist plate and serve it with vanilla sauce.
