## Ingredients

- 1 liter water with salt
- 35 g rice or vermicelli
- vegetables in season (approximately 300 g), for example in summer: peas, carrots, string beans, cauliflower, etc., in winter: celery, Brussels sprouts, salsify, etc.
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the vegetables, divide them into small pieces, and wash them.
2. Add them with the washed rice or vermicelli to the boiling water. Add salt to taste and let everything cook together until tender (1/2 hour).
3. If necessary, thicken the soup with a little sago and stir in very finely chopped parsley away from the heat.
