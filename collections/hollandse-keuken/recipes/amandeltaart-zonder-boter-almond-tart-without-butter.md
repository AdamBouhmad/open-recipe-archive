## Ingredients

- Two ounces almond paste, finely ground
- One and a half ounces grated bread sugar
- 4 eggs
- Zest of one lemon, grated

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients together in a tall pot
2. Stir constantly for a quarter hour
3. Before it sets, pour into a pan or molds
4. Bake until done
