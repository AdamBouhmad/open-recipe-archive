## Ingredients

- 500 g brown beans
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the beans and soak them for 24 hours in water with 20 g salt per liter. Pour away this water and cook the beans (approximately 1 hour) in fresh unsalted water until done.
2. Let the beans cook gently and ensure they always remain under the liquid.
3. Remove them with a skimming spoon from the pan and serve them with fried bacon and onions. Keep the cooking water for soup.
