## Ingredients

- veal breast (poulet)
- butter
- nutmeg
- mace
- jus
- salt
- lemon slices
- flour (optional for binding)

## Directions

1. Place the breast in a pan with a knob of butter, nutmeg, and mace
2. Braise until brown together
3. Add jus, a little salt, and several lemon slices
4. Braise thoroughly
5. To thicken, you may add a little flour
