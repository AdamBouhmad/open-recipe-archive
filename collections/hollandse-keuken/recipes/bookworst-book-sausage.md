## Ingredients

- 5 pounds beef
- 5 pounds lean pork
- 2 pounds bacon
- 2 and a half ounces coarse white pepper
- half ounce cloves
- 12 ounces salt
- wide intestines
- fine salt
- saltpeter

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and bacon very finely and work them well together
2. Stuff tightly into wide intestines
3. Hang to dry for one night
4. Take 4 ounces fine salt and saltpeter and rub them in
5. Smoke for three weeks, so they do not become too hard
