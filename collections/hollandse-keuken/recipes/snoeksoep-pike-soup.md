## Ingredients

- pike
- grated bread
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- hardtask
- butter
- veal bouillon
- mace

## Directions

1. Cook the pike done and let it cool a little
2. Remove the bones but keep some pieces of the back whole
3. Make a stiff dough from grated bread, nutmeg, pepper, salt and eggs
4. Chop the remaining fish finely through it and make balls
5. First roll them in fine hardtask, then in beaten egg yolks and then again in the hardtask
6. Fry these balls in butter and place them on a sieve to drain
7. For the soup take a sufficient amount of veal bouillon
8. Let it cook through well with some mace
9. Serve it in a tureen in which you have previously placed the balls
