## Ingredients

- One pound butter
- One pound sugar
- One pound flour
- Twelve egg yolks
- Rose water or orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Make the butter soft and mix everything very well together, especially knead well
2. Add rose water or orange blossom water
3. Whip the egg whites well together and mix thoroughly
4. Put into a buttered mold
5. Bake in the ashes with fire above
6. When the cake is cold and done, it should crumble like sand in the mouth
