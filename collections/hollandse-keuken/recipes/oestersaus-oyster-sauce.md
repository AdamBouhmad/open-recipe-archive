## Ingredients

- 1/2 l salted water or fish bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 20 oysters
- lemon juice to taste
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Heat the oysters thoroughly in as little boiling water as possible
2. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
3. Carefully blend with the beaten egg yolk, the oysters and as much of the oyster liquid as needed until the sauce reaches the desired thickness
4. Add lemon juice, pepper and salt to taste and serve the sauce with sole or turbot
