## Ingredients

- 200 g coarse vermicelli or noodles
- 250 g lean ham
- 100 g Gouda cheese
- 2 eggs
- 2 dl bouillon made from a cube
- salt as needed
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- some spicy sauce
- margarine

## Directions

1. Cook the vermicelli or noodles until done in plenty of salted water.
2. Rinse them briefly with cold water so the vermicelli does not stick together.
3. Let them drain as dry as possible.
4. Cut the ham into small pieces and grate the cheese (or buy it already grated).
5. Grease a warm pudding mold with some margarine and fill it layer by layer with vermicelli, ham, cheese and parsley.
6. Beat the eggs with bouillon and some spicy sauce.
7. Pour the liquid over the contents of the mold.
8. Close the mold and cook it for approximately 2 hours in a water bath.
9. Turn the pudding out onto a warm plate and serve with parsley sauce.
10. Young green beans, peas or lettuce are suitable vegetables to serve with this.
