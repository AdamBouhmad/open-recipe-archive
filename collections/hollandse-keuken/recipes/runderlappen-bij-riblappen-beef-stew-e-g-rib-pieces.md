## Ingredients

- 3/4 to 1 kg beef pieces
- 140 g beef fat, butter or margarine
- some pepper
- 20 g salt
- a couple of bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- piece of Spanish pepper
- 1 dl vinegar or 4 tomatoes (+ 400 g)

## Directions

1. FIRST METHOD. Wash the pieces, rub them in with the salt and some pepper.
2. Make the fat, butter or margarine hot in the frying pan and fry the pieces brown on both sides. (With a small number the pieces can be fried directly in the stewing pan.)
3. Place them in a stewing pan and pour over the gravy, which has been finished with some water.
4. Add the vinegar or the tomatoes sliced, washed, and the spices and let the beef pieces stew gently for at least 1 1/2 hours, turning them from time to time.
5. Strain the gravy and bind it if desired with some fine breadcrumbs or browned flour.
6. SECOND METHOD. Wash the pieces, rub them in with pepper and salt and place them for 24 hours in half water, half vinegar with the spices.
7. Turn them several times during that time and fry them as indicated above.
8. Use the liquid in which the pieces have been standing to finish the gravy.
