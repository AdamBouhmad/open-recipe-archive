## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liter buttermilk
- 100 to 150 g Alkmaar barley
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring the buttermilk with salt to a boil, stirring occasionally.
2. Sprinkle in the washed barley as in the previous recipe, stir the porridge back to a boil.
3. Let it cook gently on a soft fire, stirring occasionally, slowly until done (approximately 2 to 2 1/2 hours).
