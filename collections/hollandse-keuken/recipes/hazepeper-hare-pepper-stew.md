## Ingredients

- hare
- water
- butter
- red wine
- vinegar
- brown sugar
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- onions
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the hare into pieces and wash it
2. Boil in water for half an hour, then drain off most of the water
3. Add plenty of butter, a beer glass of red wine, half a beer glass of vinegar, a spoon of brown sugar, salt, pepper, ground cloves, very finely cut onions and crushed breadcrumbs
4. Let everything braise slowly together
