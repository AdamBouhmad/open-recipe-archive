## Ingredients

- 4 heads of endive or 1 1/2 kg
- 2 1/2 deciliters milk (or liquid from the endive)
- 25 g flour or 15 g Duryea maizena
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut off the withered leaves, a piece of the stem, and if necessary the hard green points of the leaves.
2. Cut the heads, starting from the stem, into thin strips.
3. Wash them repeatedly in plenty of water until the water is completely clear, then place them with the attached water and let them cook until tender (1/2 hour).
4. Let them drain in a colander and if desired, catch the liquid to make a sauce from it.
5. Prepare the sauce in the manner indicated for cauliflower sauce (see recipe 328 or recipe 329).
6. Add the endive with some salt and braise for about 5 minutes.
7. Grate some nutmeg over it on the plate.
