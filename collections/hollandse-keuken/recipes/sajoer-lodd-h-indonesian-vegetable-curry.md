## Ingredients

- 5 sprouts
- 4 small carrots
- 5 princess beans
- 5 pea pods
- piece of cauliflower
- piece of white Savoy cabbage
- 3 onions
- 6 garlic cloves
- piece of trassi
- 7 kemiri nuts
- 6 dried chilies (without seeds)
- 2 teaspoons laos
- 3 teaspoons sereh powder
- 2 teaspoons coriander
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 citrus leaves
- 100 g peeled cooked shrimp
- 1½ liters coconut milk
- 50 g beef fillet
- butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Pound the spices, except for the aromatic leaves, together with the ground onions in the mortar with the pestle until fine
2. Fry them in butter or margarine
3. Then add the finely cut vegetables and fry together
4. In a well-sealed pan, add the coconut milk or milk with salt, add the fried mixture with the meat pieces and the aromatic leaves
5. Let the sajoer steep and cook until done
6. Add the shrimp at the end
