## Ingredients

- vegetables of the season
- five cent roll
- butter
- nutmeg
- mace
- salt
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. For this soup take all the vegetables the season provides
2. Cook them with a five cent roll, cut into pieces and roasted
3. Add a lump of butter with some nutmeg, mace and salt
4. When serving, you can stir through a few egg yolks to thicken it
