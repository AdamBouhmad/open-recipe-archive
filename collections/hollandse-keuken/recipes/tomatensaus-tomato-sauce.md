## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 deciliter bouillon or water with salt
- 500 g tomatoes or 4 tablespoons tomato purée
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 white peppercorns
- 50 g onion
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- optionally the juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Let the washed and bruised or sliced tomatoes cook in the water or bouillon with the spices (approximately 10 minutes)
2. Pour the liquid through a horsehair sieve, press the tomatoes through it and add it, stirring, to the butter or margarine, which has been melted and then brought to a boil with the flour and from which all lumps have been rubbed out
3. Let the sauce cook, stirring, (10 minutes), optionally add the lemon juice and serve the sauce with fried sweetbreads, cooked chicken or hard-boiled eggs
4. Note: If tomato purée is used, use 4 deciliter bouillon or water with salt in which the spices have steeped and stir the tomato purée through when the sauce is cooked
