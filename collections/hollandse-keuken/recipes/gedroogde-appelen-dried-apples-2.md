## Ingredients

- 300 g dried sour apples
- 100 g sugar
- ½ dl berry juice
- peel of ½ lemon or piece of cinnamon
- some sago

## Directions

1. Carefully remove the cores, wash the apples several times with lukewarm water, let them soak in plenty of cold water for 24 hours.
2. The next day, bring them with the same water and the flavoring to a boil and let them cook gently until tender (approximately ¾ hour).
3. Remove the flavoring, add the sugar and berry juice and carefully thicken the liquid with a little mixed sago.
