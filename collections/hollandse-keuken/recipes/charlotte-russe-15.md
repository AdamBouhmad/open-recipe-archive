## Ingredients

- 100 g biscuits
- 2 1/2 dl whipped cream
- 2 egg whites
- 50 g brown sugar
- 10 g white gelatine or 8 g powdered gelatine
- some coffee or vanilla extract or some maraschino
- 35 g candied fruits

## Directions

1. Cut the edges of the biscuits straight and set them, with the brown side outward, against the wall of a smooth pudding mold, but make sure they stand as firmly as possible against each other.
2. Clean the bottom of the pudding mold with a damp brush and brush it with some unbeaten egg white.
3. Beat the cream stiff with the brown sugar and mix it with the stiffly beaten egg whites and the flavoring.
4. Soak the sheet gelatine 10 to 15 minutes in cold water, then dissolve it with as little water as possible, let the gelatine cool briefly and stir it through the cream.
5. Let this mixture become jelly-like, stirring from time to time, and pour it inside the biscuits.
6. Cut the biscuits level with the surface of the cream and let the pudding become cold and firm.
7. Turn it out and garnish the top with candied fruits or with fruit jelly and fruits.
8. Tie a narrow ribbon around it.
