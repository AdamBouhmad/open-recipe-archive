## Ingredients

- 150 g butter or margarine
- 125 g powdered sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 75 g flour

## Directions

1. Cream the butter or margarine until fluffy, add the sifted powdered sugar and stir it completely smooth.
2. Beat the egg white until stiff as possible and mix it with a few strokes with butter or margarine and powdered sugar.
3. Then add, as quickly as possible, the sifted flour.
4. Place a piece of soft white cardboard on a greased baking sheet, 9 cm wide and 11 cm long, with a piece cut out from the middle 6 cm wide and 8 cm long.
5. Spread a layer of the batter as thin as possible in this opening.
6. Place the cardboard next to the formed cookie, spread dough in it again and continue until the baking sheet is full.
7. Bake the oublies in a fairly warm oven as quickly as possible until light brown and done.
8. Roll them with a knife and remove them directly from the sheet.
9. Let the sheet cool before new cookies are spread on it.
