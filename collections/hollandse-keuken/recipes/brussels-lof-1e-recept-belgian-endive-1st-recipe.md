## Ingredients

- 1 kg Belgian endive
- 2.5 dl milk
- 25 g flour or 15 g cornstarch (Duryea maizena)
- 25 g butter or margarine
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the brown leaves from the plants or scrape the brown off.
2. Remove the spongy heart that sits in the stem and is very bitter, cut off a piece of the stem and cut the plants into strips.
3. Wash the endive until the water runs completely clear, let it cook with as little water as possible in a well-enameled pot (to prevent discoloration).
