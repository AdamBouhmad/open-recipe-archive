## Ingredients

- 170 g butter or margarine
- 170 g sugar
- 85 g flour
- 5 eggs
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons fine cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon crushed cloves
- finely crushed cardamom
- butter or margarine for greasing

## Directions

1. Stir the butter or margarine until creamy, add the sugar and egg yolks and stir until the sugar is well ground.
2. Add the spices and sifted flour.
3. Beat the egg white as stiffly as possible, fold in the remaining mixture.
4. Grease a cake pan with butter or margarine, spread a layer of batter as thinly as possible and bake in a moderately warm oven until light brown.
5. Brush with melted butter or margarine, spread a second layer and bake until light brown as well.
6. Continue until all the batter is baked, turn the cake out onto a cake rack and let it cool.
7. Dust with powdered sugar and cut into very thin slices.
8. Note: While baking, keep the remaining batter as cool as possible so the egg white does not collapse. If making a large cake, the spices can be stirred through one half of the dough while the other half remains white, making the separation between layers more visible. This cake can be stored for a long time in a tin.
