## Ingredients

- 1/2 l bouillon
- pinch of thyme
- Spanish pepper
- parsley
- piece of onion
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 50 g ground veal
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tin of mushrooms
- 1/2 dl cream

## Directions

1. Let the herbs steep in the bouillon, strain and prepare sauce from it in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce)
2. Warm the cooked ground meat balls in it and while beating vigorously, pour the sauce onto the egg yolk beaten with the cream
3. Stir in the mushrooms, either whole or cut in half, and add some of the mushroom liquid if desired
4. Serve with calf's tongue and light meat
