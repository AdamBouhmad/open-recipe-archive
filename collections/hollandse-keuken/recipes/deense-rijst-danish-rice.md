## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liter milk
- 175 g rice
- 75 g sugar
- 1 to 2 eggs
- 150 g bitter biscuits
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook rice porridge in the usual manner (see rice porridge recipe).
2. Mix this with the sugar, the egg yolks and the stiffly beaten egg white.
3. Layer the mixture alternately with the bitter biscuits in a fireproof dish, making sure the last layer consists of rice porridge.
4. Let the dish rise and brown in a moderately warm oven and serve as soon as it comes from the oven.
