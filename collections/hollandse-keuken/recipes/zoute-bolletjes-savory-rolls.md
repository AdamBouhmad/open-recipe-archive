## Ingredients

- 130 g flour
- 1/2 dl milk
- 10 g yeast
- 90 g butter or margarine
- 10 g salt
- 1/4 egg
- 1 egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Put the flour in a warmed bowl, make a small well in the middle, add the milk and yeast, stir in about half of the flour and let this starter rise for 15-20 minutes in a warm place.
2. Add the melted butter or margarine, the beaten egg and the salt and knead this dough until it releases from your hands.
3. Throw it on a flour-dusted board and work it back and forth for 5 minutes, let it rest in a cool place for half an hour.
4. Flatten it, divide it into 60-65 pieces and shape them into balls.
5. Lay them on a greased baking sheet, flatten them slightly with a damp cloth and brush them with egg yolk beaten with a little water.
6. Let the rolls rise for another 15-20 minutes in a warm place, bake them in a hot oven until light brown and done (15-20 minutes). Remove them from the sheet and let them cool.
