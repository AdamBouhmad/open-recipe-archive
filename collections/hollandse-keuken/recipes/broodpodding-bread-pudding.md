## Ingredients

- inside of stale white bread
- warm milk
- 6 fresh eggs
- 2 ounces currants
- 1.5 ounces raisins
- 1.5 ounces butter
- candied citrus peel
- cinnamon
- butter for greasing mold
- fine rusk for coating mold
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Crumb the inside of stale white bread
2. let it soak in warm milk
3. stir until smooth
4. beat in 6 fresh eggs
5. stir in 2 ounces currants, 1.5 ounces raisins, 1.5 ounces butter, candied citrus peel, snippets and cinnamon
6. pour the batter into a mold greased with butter and sprinkled with fine rusk
7. let the pudding cook vigorously for 1.5 hours
8. serve with wine sauce
