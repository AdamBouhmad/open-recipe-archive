## Ingredients

- 175 g Alkmaar barley
- 4 1/2 dl water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the barley until the water that runs off is completely clear.
2. Soak it for 24 hours in 2 1/2 times as much cold water as barley and some salt.
3. Set the bowl with barley in a pan with enough boiling water so that it comes just below the rim of the bowl.
4. Put the lid on the pan and let the water boil until the barley is done (approximately 2 to 2 1/2 hours), but make sure the water in the pan is refilled meanwhile.
