## Ingredients

- Wine
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put wine, sugar, and cinnamon to taste in a small pan
2. Add lemon peel and let them cook together
3. Bind this sauce with egg yolks
