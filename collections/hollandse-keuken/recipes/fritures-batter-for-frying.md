## Ingredients

- First recipe: 1 egg, 70 g flour, 1 dl beer or sparkling water, pepper and salt
- Second recipe: 1 egg, 75 g flour, 15 g cornstarch, 3/4 to 1 dl milk, pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Beat the egg with the pepper and salt, add the flour (or both types of flour) and as much liquid until the flour no longer floats.
2. Stir the lumps from this batter and add the remaining liquid.
3. Make frying fat hot in a deep iron pot.
