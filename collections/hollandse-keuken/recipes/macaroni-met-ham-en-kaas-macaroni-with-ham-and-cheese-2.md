## Ingredients

- 200 g macaroni
- 100 g ham
- 100 g aged cheese
- 3 dl macaroni cooking water
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 25 g flour
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- 10 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces, wash it and cook it until done in plenty of boiling salted water (1/2 hour).
2. Let it drain and reserve 3 dl of cooking water for the sauce.
3. Cut the ham finely, grate the cheese and stir it carefully with two spoons through the macaroni on low heat until the cheese threads (so-called cheese stringing), but especially make sure the macaroni stays whole.
4. Make a sauce from butter or margarine, flour and macaroni cooking water and pour this over the macaroni, add salt (and pepper) to taste, put everything in a fireproof small dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs over it, lay the 10 g butter or margarine in small pieces on top, clean the rim of the dish and let it brown in the oven or serve the hot dish in a covered dish.
