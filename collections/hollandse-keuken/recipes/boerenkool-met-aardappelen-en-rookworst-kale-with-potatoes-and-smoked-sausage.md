## Ingredients

- 3 bunches or 1 1/2 kg kale
- 1 1/2 kg potatoes
- 400 g smoked sausage
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Strip the kale, that is, remove the leafy greens from the hard central ribs, wash it several times in plenty of cold water and cook it in salted water until tender (approximately 1 hour).
