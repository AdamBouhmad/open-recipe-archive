## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- bouillon or jus
- wine
- onions
- cloves
- lemon juice
- doves

## Directions

1. Brown butter and flour.
2. Add bouillon or jus, a glass of wine, two sliced onions, crushed cloves and lemon juice.
3. Cut the doves into pieces, place everything in a pan and let it stew for an hour.
