## Ingredients

- 200 g butter or margarine
- 2 egg yolks
- pepper, salt and lemon juice to taste
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 1/2 dl whipped cream
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 355
2. At the last moment stir in as lightly as possible the cream beaten stiff with the egg white, finish the sauce with pepper, salt and lemon juice to taste and serve with salmon, turbot or sole
