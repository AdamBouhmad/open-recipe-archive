## Ingredients

- roasted meat
- ham
- saucis de boulogne
- onion
- anchovies
- 1.5 ounces bacon
- milk
- eggs
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Take roasted meat of any kind
2. Bake it finely with a little ham, saucis de boulogne, an onion, some anchovies and 1.5 ounces bacon
3. Mix milk, eggs and grated bread under it
4. Put it in a mold and cook it well
5. Serve with an anchovy sauce
