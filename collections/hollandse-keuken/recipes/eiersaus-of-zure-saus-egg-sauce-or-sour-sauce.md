## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water with salt or bouillon
- 30 to 40 g flour
- 30 to 40 g butter or margarine
- 2 eggs
- 3/2 deciliter vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce in the usual way (see cauliflower sauce) and mix it with the vinegar
2. Pour it, stirring well, into the beaten eggs
3. Serve with calf's tongue, beef tongue, pork tongue, cooked flatfish, white beans, etc.
