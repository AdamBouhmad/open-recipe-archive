## Ingredients

- a handful flour
- water
- 8 eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- small cut lemon peel
- currants
- apples, cut into large cubes
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir a handful flour with water as for a pancake
2. Add 8 eggs, sugar, cinnamon, small cut lemon peel, currants and apples cut into large cubes
3. Stir the mixture well together
4. Bake in a deep pan with butter for 1.5 hours
5. Serve with sugar and cinnamon
