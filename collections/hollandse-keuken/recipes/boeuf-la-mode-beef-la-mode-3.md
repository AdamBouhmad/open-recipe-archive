## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the rump
- Kidney fat or bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Bacon slices
- A beer glass of vinegar
- Water
- Bay leaves
- Lemon slices
- Butter
- Flour (fried)

## Directions

1. Lard a piece of beef from the rump with kidney fat or bacon that has been previously rolled in salt, pepper and cloves
2. Sprinkle the meat itself with salt, pepper and ground rusks
3. Lay bacon slices at the bottom of a earthenware pan, place the meat on top
4. Add a beer glass of vinegar with water, some bay leaves, lemon slices and some butter
5. Close the lid tightly and place the pan on a low fire early in the morning
6. When cooked, stir in some fried flour through the pan to bind the sauce
