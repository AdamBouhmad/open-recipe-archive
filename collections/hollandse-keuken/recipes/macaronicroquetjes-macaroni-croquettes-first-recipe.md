## Ingredients

- 50 g macaroni
- 2 dl cream or milk
- 25 g flour
- 25 g butter or margarine
- 25 g ham
- 30 g old cheese
- 2 1/2 sheets gelatin or 4 g powdered gelatin
- bread crumbs
- frying fat

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces, wash it, and cook it done in plenty of boiling water with salt, or in bouillon (1 1/2 hour) and stir from time to time.
2. Let it drain dry and cut it into small pieces.
3. Prepare the sauce as described in recipe 102.
4. Dissolve the soaked gelatin in it if necessary.
5. Mix the sauce with the grated cheese and the finely cut ham, carefully stir in the macaroni cut into small pieces, add pepper and salt to taste, and let the mixture, spread on a board, become almost stiff, but ensure it is still good to handle.
6. Form the croquettes in the manner as indicated for meat croquettes (see recipe 116).
7. Bread these double and treat them further as meat croquettes (recipe 116).
