## Ingredients

- Veal chops, cleaned of membranes and bones
- Melted butter
- Brown meat juices
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Shallots
- Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Roll the cleaned chops in melted butter and place them in an earthenware pan
2. Add brown meat juices, parsley, crushed thyme and finely chopped shallots
3. Let them braise
4. When serving, add two to three glasses of Rhine wine
