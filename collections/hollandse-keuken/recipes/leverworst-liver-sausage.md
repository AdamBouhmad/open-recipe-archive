## Ingredients

- 2 kg pork or veal liver
- 2 kg pork meat
- 1 kg neck bacon
- 40 g salt
- 50 g onions
- 1 tablespoon ground cloves
- 15 g pepper
- 2 dl meat broth
- beef casings

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned liver, cleaned meat and neck bacon for 5 hours in plenty of boiling salted water.
2. Grind everything as finely as possible, mix it with the spices, salt, broth and very finely chopped onions.
3. Clean the casings, which have been soaked in lukewarm water for some time, thoroughly and stuff the meat through a sausage horn into the casings, not too tightly.
4. Prick occasionally with a needle while stuffing and tie the sausages closed at both ends.
5. Cook them in plenty of boiling salted water until they float to the surface (approximately 15 to 20 minutes), but let them cook very gently so they do not burst.
6. Let them cool completely, lying side by side.
7. Note: If you wish to keep the sausage for a while, let it smoke very lightly.
8. The easiest way to fill the casings is to place the sausage horn on the meat grinder with the blade removed, then pass the mixture through the grinder. The casing, which is stretched over the sausage horn, fills evenly but must be supported by hand.
