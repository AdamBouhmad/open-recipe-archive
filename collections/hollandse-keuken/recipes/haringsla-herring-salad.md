## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 2 sour apples
- 1 egg
- 1 beet
- 8 cooked potatoes
- 1 head of lettuce or 1 bunch of curly endive or 100 g field lettuce
- pickled onions and gherkins
- 3 tablespoons salad oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings as indicated in recipe 5. Cut them into small pieces.
2. Boil the egg hard (8 minutes), peel it, separate the white from the yolk and mash both separately.
3. Peel and mince the apples, mash the potatoes.
4. Clean the lettuce, cut it finely, wash it and squeeze it as dry as possible.
5. Peel the cooked beet, cut it into slices of approximately 1/4 cm thickness and cut these into small cubes.
6. Mix most of all these ingredients together, but reserve a small part for garnish, except for the apples as they discolor quickly.
7. Stir in oil and vinegar or some mayonnaise to taste and some pepper and salt.
8. Lay this mixture on a plate, smooth it, but ensure it is slightly higher in the middle than at the edges.
9. Use the remainder as garnish and lay it on in sections or stripes, ensuring the underlying mixture is completely covered.
10. Further garnish the plate with onions and gherkins.
11. Note: The egg can also be cut into quarters or sixths and used only as garnish.
