## Ingredients

- Eggs, 6
- Butter, 30 grams
- Bread crumbs (midolla di pane), 20 grams
- Parmesan cheese, 2 heaping tablespoons
- Dried mushrooms, a pinch
- Parsley, several leaves
- Salt, to taste
- Milk
- Potatoes, 350 grams raw (or spinach, peas, or other vegetables)

## Directions

1. Hard-boil the eggs, cut them lengthwise, and set the yolks aside.
2. Soak the bread crumbs well in milk and squeeze them out.
3. Soften the mushrooms in tepid water.
4. Pound everything very finely to fill and even mound up the cavities of the whites.
5. Arrange these 12 filled egg halves on a platter, convex side down, on a layer of potatoes passed through a ricer (recipe N. 443), using 350 grams raw weight.
6. Instead of potatoes, you can place them on a layer of spinach, peas, or other vegetables.
7. Before serving, warm them with heat from a country oven cover.
