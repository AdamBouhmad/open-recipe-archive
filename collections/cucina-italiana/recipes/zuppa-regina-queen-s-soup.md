## Ingredients

- White meat of roasted chicken, cleaned of skin and tendons
- Five or six shelled sweet almonds
- One marrow from bread soaked in broth or milk
- Broth
- Bread cut into cubes
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese
- Optional: some cooked egg yolks softened in broth

## Directions

1. Finely chop the white meat of roasted chicken with a crescent knife, then pound it in a mortar with five or six shelled sweet almonds and with a marrow of bread soaked in broth or milk, in proportion of one fifth or one sixth of the quantity of meat
2. When the mixture is well pounded, pass it through a sieve, put it in the soup tureen and dissolve it with a ladle of hot broth
3. Cut the bread into cubes, fry it in butter and also throw this into the soup tureen
4. After, pour in the boiling broth, stir and send the soup to table with Parmesan on the side
