## Ingredients

- Ricotta, 300 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 50 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Cooked beet greens (bietola), as much as fits in a handful
- Scent of nutmeg and spices
- Salt, as needed
- Flour

## Directions

1. Pass the ricotta through a sieve, and if it is watery, squeeze it first in a cloth
2. Clean the beet greens of their stems, boil them without water, squeeze them well and chop them finely with the crescent blade
3. Make a paste of everything, take the mixture by spoonfuls and placing it on flour that you have spread on the work surface, wrap it well giving it the rounded and oblong shape of croquettes
4. With this amount you will make about two dozen ravioli
5. To cook them, throw them into unsalted water that is boiling hard and lift them out with the slotted spoon so they remain dry
6. Dress them either with sauce or with cheese and butter and serve them as a pasta course or as a side to a meat stew
7. Since cooking them is quick, it being enough that they set, cook them a few at a time so they do not break
