## Ingredients

- Lean boiled or roasted meat of beef or veal, cleaned of sinews or tenderness, 200 grams
- Chocolate, 100 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Pine nuts, 50 grams
- Sultana raisins, 50 grams
- Candied citron cut into small pieces, 25 grams
- Wheat flour, 170 grams
- Cornflour, 80 grams
- Powdered sugar, 80 grams
- Butter, 70 grams
- Virgin lard, 25 grams
- One egg
- White wine or marsala, as much as needed to blend it

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very finely with a mincing knife
2. Toast the pine nuts
3. Soak the raisins in marsala and dry them before use
4. Brown the meat in the butter, stirring continuously so it does not stick, and when it has taken on a somewhat reddish color, remove it from the fire to let it cool
5. Dissolve the chocolate, grated or in small pieces, over the fire in three spoonfuls of water, and when dissolved add the sugar and then pour it into the meat, adding the pine nuts, raisins and candied citron and mixing everything together
6. Now prepare a shortcrust pastry to enclose the cake
7. Take a proportionate baking pan where the mixture is no more than a finger high, grease it with butter or lard, and with one sheet below and another on top, the latter rolled out with a fluted roller, seal it between
8. Brush the surface with egg yolk, bake it in the oven or in a country oven and serve it cooled
