## Ingredients

- wild boar, about 1 kilogram
- marinade as for hare (recipe N. 531)
- three or four thin slices of lard, thin as paper
- salt
- pepper
- one whole onion
- a bouquet garni
- a small piece of butter
- half a glass of white wine (if boar is about 1 kilogram)
- more butter for coating paper
- broth

## Directions

1. Keep the wild boar in a marinade like that for hare (recipe N. 531) for 12 or 14 hours.
2. Remove it from the marinade, dry it with a cloth and then prepare it as follows.
3. Place three or four slices of lard, thin as paper, on the bottom of a pot, place the piece of wild boar on top of them, season with salt and pepper and add one whole onion, a bouquet garni, a small piece of butter and, if the boar is about 1 kilogram, half a glass of white wine.
4. Spread three or four more slices of the same lard on top of the meat and cover it with a sheet of paper coated with butter that adheres well.
5. Cook it with heat below and above, and when it begins to dry out, baste it with broth.
6. When cooked, strain the sauce without squeezing it, degrease it and add it to the boar when serving.
