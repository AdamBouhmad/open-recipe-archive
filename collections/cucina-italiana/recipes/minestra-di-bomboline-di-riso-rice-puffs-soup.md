## Ingredients

- Rice, 100 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 20 grams
- One egg yolk
- Nutmeg
- Salt, to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the rice very well and firm in milk (half a liter will do); before removing it from the fire add the butter and salt and when it is no longer boiling add the rest.
2. For the rest proceed as with the previous recipe.
3. These puffs turn out tastier than those made of potatoes.
4. This dose will serve six persons.
