## Ingredients

- Loin of beef weighing more than one kilogram, from a young animal
- Oil
- Broth
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put it on the fire half an hour before sending the soup course to the table
2. Cook it over an ardent and well-lit fire from the beginning so that it is seized quickly at the surface
3. Anoint it with oil, which you will then drain from the pan
4. Finally pass a ladle of broth over it, which, united with the fat fallen from the roast beef, will serve as sauce for the piece when you send it to the table
5. Salt it at half cooking, using somewhat sparingly as this quality of meat is flavorful in itself
