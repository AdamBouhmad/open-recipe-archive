## Ingredients

- Lean hare without bone, 250 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Flour, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 30 grams
- Egg yolks, 6 numbers
- Milk, half a liter

## Directions

1. Make a finely chopped battutino with about 20 grams of ham and a small piece of onion, put it on the fire with half of the butter and the hare cut into small pieces, salting it
2. When the fat is almost consumed and before the meat browns, pour good broth to cook it
3. When cooked, pound it in a mortar basting it with its juice, then pass it through a sieve
4. With the indicated flour, the rest of the butter and the milk make a béchamel and when it is cool beat the egg yolks well and mix everything together
5. Put the mixture in a smooth mold with buttered paper on the bottom and cook it in a water bath
6. Serve it cold, surrounded and covered with gelatin; but since nowadays in meals one seeks much the beauty and elegance in dishes, and also some pleasant surprise, it would be better in this case
