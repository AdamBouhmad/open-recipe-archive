## Ingredients

- Black cherries, raw, whole and stemless, 200 grams
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Rye breadcrumbs, 50 grams
- Sweet almonds, 40 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Rosolio, 2 spoonfuls
- Vanilla flavoring or lemon zest
- Butter, cold

## Directions

1. If you lack rye bread, use common bread.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them and chop them finely to reduce them to about half the size of a grain of rice.
3. Work the egg yolks first with the sugar until they become foamy, add the breadcrumbs, the rosolio, the flavoring and continue to work the mixture a little more.
4. Add the well-beaten egg whites, mixing gently and pour it into a smooth mold that you have first well greased with cold butter and sprinkled throughout, and more at the bottom, with the said almonds.
5. Finally throw in the cherries, but to prevent them from sinking to the bottom due to their weight, mix among the mixture the remaining almonds.
6. Bake it in the oven or in a country oven and serve it hot or cold to four or five people.
