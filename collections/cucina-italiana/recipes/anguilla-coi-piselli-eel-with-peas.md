## Ingredients

- Eel
- peas
- water

## Directions

1. Stew it as in the recipe for Stewed Eel, and when it is cooked remove it dry to cook the peas in its sauce.
2. Put it back among the peas to reheat it and serve it.
3. No tomato juice is used here, but water if needed.
