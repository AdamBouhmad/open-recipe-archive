## Ingredients

- salt cod
- flour
- oil
- garlic cloves, whole and slightly crushed
- pepper
- tomato sauce or tomato conserve diluted in water

## Directions

1. Cut the salt cod into pieces as wide as the palm of your hand and flour well
2. Heat a pan or baking dish with considerable oil and two or three whole, slightly crushed garlic cloves over the fire
3. When the garlic begins to color, add the salt cod and brown on both sides, stirring often so it does not stick
4. Season with little to no salt (taste first) and a pinch of pepper
5. Finally pour a few spoonfuls of tomato sauce or diluted tomato conserve over it
6. Boil a little longer and serve
