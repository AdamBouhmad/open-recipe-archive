## Ingredients

- Chicken livers
- One shallot or one small white onion bulb
- A piece of prosciutto fat
- Several parsley leaves
- Celery
- Carrot
- Oil
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Dried mushrooms, 2-3 pieces, softened
- Broth
- Fine breadcrumbs
- Lemon juice
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Remove the bile sac from the chicken livers carefully without breaking it, doing this best while working in a bowl of water
2. Place the livers on the fire together with a mixture (battutino) composed of one shallot or, if unavailable, one small white onion bulb, a piece of prosciutto fat, several parsley leaves, celery and carrot, a little oil and butter, salt and pepper; but everything in small quantity so as not to make the mixture piquant or nauseating
3. At half cooking, remove the livers and dry them; with two or three pieces of softened dried mushrooms, mince them finely with a crescent-shaped chopper
4. Return them to the fire in the sauce that remained from the half-cooking and with a little broth finish cooking them
5. Before serving, bind them with a pinch of fine breadcrumbs and add a bit of lemon juice
6. The crostini should be tender, so make the mixture somewhat liquid, or dip the bread slices briefly, barely, in the broth
