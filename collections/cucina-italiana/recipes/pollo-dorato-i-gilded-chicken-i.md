## Ingredients

- Young chicken
- Flour
- Eggs, 2
- Breadcrumbs
- Oil or pure lard
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Take a young chicken, clean it, remove the head and feet, wash it well and keep it in boiling water for one minute.
2. Cut it into pieces at its joints, coat it with flour, season with salt and pepper and pour two beaten eggs over it.
3. After at least half an hour of soaking wrap the pieces in breadcrumbs, repeating the operation twice if necessary and cook them on hot coals in this manner: take a sauté pan or a tinned copper pan, put oil or better pure lard in it, and when it begins to sizzle throw down the pieces of chicken making them brown on both sides over moderate heat so the cooking penetrates inside.
4. Serve them hot with lemon wedges.
