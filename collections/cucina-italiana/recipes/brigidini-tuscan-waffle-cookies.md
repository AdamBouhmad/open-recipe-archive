## Ingredients

- Eggs, 2
- Sugar, 120 grams
- Anise seeds, 10 grams
- Salt, a pinch
- Flour, as needed

## Directions

1. Make a rather firm paste, work it with your hands on a work surface and form small balls about the size of a small walnut.
2. Place them in the waffle iron on a waffle mold at a proper distance from one another and, turning the iron back and forth over the hot flame with wood fire, remove them when they have taken color.
