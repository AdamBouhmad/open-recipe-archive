## Ingredients

- Egg whites, 4
- Egg yolks, 4
- Fine breadcrumbs of hard bread, 4 heaping spoonfuls
- Parmigiano cheese, grated, 4 heaping spoonfuls
- Nutmeg, to taste

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites until stiff, incorporate the yolks into them, then pour four heaping spoonfuls of fine breadcrumbs of hard bread into it, as much grated parmigiano and the hint of nutmeg.
2. Mix gently so the mixture remains soft and throw it into boiling broth by spoonfuls.
3. Make it boil for seven or eight minutes and serve it.
4. This dose may be enough for six people.
