## Ingredients

- Savoiardi, 100 grams
- Chocolate, 100 grams
- Fresh butter, 100 grams
- Powdered sugar, 70 grams
- One egg yolk
- Milk, 2 tablespoonfuls
- Vanilla sugar flavoring
- Rosolio or alkermes liqueur

## Directions

1. Cut the savoiardi in half lengthwise and soak them with rosolio, or better, half with rosolio and half with alkermes, so they can be alternated to make a better appearance.
2. First work the butter with the sugar and egg yolk.
3. Place the chocolate, grated or in pieces, on the fire with the milk, and when it is well dissolved pour it hot into the worked butter, add the flavoring and form thus a paste by mixing it well.
4. Arrange on the platter a first layer of the said savoiardi and spread them lightly with the said paste; then layer another layer of savoiardi, then a third layer still, always spreading them lightly.
5. Pour all the remaining paste over and at the sides, evening it out as well as you can.
6. The next day, before serving it, smooth it all over the surface with the blade of a knife heated by fire, and at the same time, if you like, decorate it with a sprinkling of pistachios or slightly toasted hazelnuts, both finely chopped.
7. 40 grams of hazelnuts weighed with shell or 15 grams of pistachios will suffice.
8. This is a portion for six or seven people.
