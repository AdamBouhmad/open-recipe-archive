## Ingredients

- Milk, 8 deciliters
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Pistachios, 50 grams
- Egg yolks, 6

## Directions

1. Blanch the pistachios with hot water and pound them very fine with a spoonful of the said sugar, then put them in a saucepan with the egg yolks and the sugar, stirring everything well.
2. Add the milk and place the mixture on the fire stirring with a spoon, and when it is condensed like cream pour it cold into the sorbetierre. This dose will be enough for eight people.
