## Ingredients

- lean veal meat, 500 grams without bone
- one quarter of a medium-sized onion
- oil
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- parsley
- garlic
- good white wine or Marsala
- hot water
- tomato juice or tomato preserve
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Form small braised rolls with lean veal meat, and having 500 grams without bone, mince one quarter of a medium-sized onion and place it at the bottom of a saucepan with oil and a small piece of butter.
2. Lay the braised rolls on the base, one layer on top of the other, season them with salt and pepper and place them on the fire without touching them so that by sticking together they do not shrivel.
3. When the underside has taken on color, sprinkle a teaspoon of flour and after a little while a pinch of chopped parsley and half a clove of chopped garlic and two sparse fingers of a glass of good white wine, or if lacking this, Marsala.
4. Then separate the braised rolls from one another, mix, let them draw moisture, then pour hot water and a little tomato juice or preserve.
5. Let them boil slowly and not for very long to finish cooking them and serve them with abundant sauce, and with slices of toasted bread underneath, or if you prefer, with a side of rice cooked in water, drawn dry and lightly seasoned with butter, Parmesan and the same sauce. Indeed, the rice works very well with this and thus it pleases everyone.
