## Ingredients

- thick slices of smooth-hound (palombo) a finger's thickness
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- oil
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- tomato juice or tomato paste
- water
- parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Wash the smooth-hound slices and dry them in a cloth, then skin them with a sharp knife
2. Season with salt and pepper and soak in beaten egg for several hours
3. Coat with breadcrumbs, dipping twice in egg, then fry in oil
4. In a wide pan or dish, heat oil in proportion, add a piece of butter mixed well with flour (to bind the sauce), a pinch of chopped parsley, tomato juice or paste diluted with water, and a pinch of salt and pepper
5. Let the sauce fry a little on the fire
6. Add the fried smooth-hound slices to the sauce, turn them on both sides and add water so the sauce becomes liquid
7. Remove from heat, sprinkle with grated parmesan and serve
