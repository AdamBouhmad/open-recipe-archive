## Ingredients

- Usual boiled meat
- A handful of fresh shelled beans, or if dried, give them half cooking in water
- Savoy cabbage, spinach and a little beet
- 40 grams fat ham
- One clove garlic
- A pinch of parsley
- Celery
- Carrot
- One potato
- One zucchini
- Salt and pepper
- Broth
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook first a handful of fresh shelled beans in the broth, or if dried give them half cooking in water.
2. Slice thinly savoy cabbage, spinach and a little beet, keep them soaking in fresh water, then put them in a saucepan dry and when they have water over fire, drain them well by pressing with a spoon.
