## Ingredients

- cavolo cappuccio or verzotto (cabbage)
- vitella di latte (milk veal)
- fegatini (chicken livers)
- animelle (sweetbreads)
- balsamella (béchamel sauce)
- parmigiano (Parmesan cheese)
- rosso d'uovo (egg yolk)
- noce moscata (nutmeg)
- burro (butter)
- sugo dello stracotto (gravy from the braised meat)
- midolla di pane (bread crumb)
- brodo (broth)

## Directions

1. Take a large head of cabbage, clean it from the hard leaves on the surface, trim the stem evenly and give it half cooking in salted water.
2. Place it upside down to drain, then open the leaves one by one up to the center head and pour the filling on it.
3. Pull all the leaves back over it, close it completely and tie it in a cross pattern.
4. For the filling, make it with braised milk veal alone, or united with chicken livers and sweetbreads, everything minced finely.
5. To grace and make the mixture delicate, add a little béchamel, a pinch of Parmesan, an egg yolk and the flavor of nutmeg.
6. Finish cooking the cabbage in the gravy of the said braised meat, adding a piece of butter, with light fire below and above.
7. If you do not want to fill the whole cabbage, you can fill the wider leaves one by one by wrapping them over themselves like bobbins.
8. To the béchamel, a bread crumb soaked in broth or gravy can substitute.
