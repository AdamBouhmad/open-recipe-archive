## Ingredients

- Mantis shrimp
- Water
- Flour
- Egg
- Salt
- Oil

## Directions

1. Wash the mantis shrimp and boil them in a little water, covered with a cloth with a weight on top; 15 minutes of boiling should be sufficient.
2. Shell them after cooking and, exposing the flesh, cut it into two pieces.
3. Dust with flour, dip in beaten and salted egg, and fry it.
