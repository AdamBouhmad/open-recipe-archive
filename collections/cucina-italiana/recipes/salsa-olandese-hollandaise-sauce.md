## Ingredients

- Butter, 70 grams
- Egg yolks, 2
- Lemon juice, one spoonful
- Water, half an eggshell
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Dissolve the butter separately without heating it too much
2. Place the yolks with the water in a basin, and over a light fire or on the edge of the stove begin to beat them with the whisk and pour in the butter little by little
3. When the mixture has condensed add the lemon and finally the salt and pepper
4. Prepare it at the moment of serving; it is a delicate sauce for boiled fish or for something similar, and will be sufficient for a quantity of approximately 500 grams
