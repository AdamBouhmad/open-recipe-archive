## Ingredients

- Raspberries, any quantity
- White powdered sugar, equal weight to the strained raspberries

## Directions

1. Put the raspberries on the heat as they are without fermentation.
2. Boil for about twenty minutes.
3. Pass through a sieve.
4. Weigh the strained raspberries.
5. Return to the heat with an equal amount of white powdered sugar.
6. Boil until it reaches the consistency of preserve, which you will know by the methods already indicated.
