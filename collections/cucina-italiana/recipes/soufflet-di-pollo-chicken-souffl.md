## Ingredients

- Chicken meat without skin, 80 grams
- Flour, 50 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 20 grams
- Milk, 2.5 deciliters
- Eggs, 4
- Salt, a pinch

## Directions

1. Make a béchamel sauce with the butter, flour and milk.
2. When cooked and no longer boiling, add the Parmesan, salt, egg yolks and the chicken finely minced with a half-moon cutter.
3. Then whip the egg whites until stiff.
4. Gently add these to the mixture.
5. Pour it into a pan that can withstand fire.
6. Brown it lightly in a country oven and serve it hot, though it is also good cold.
