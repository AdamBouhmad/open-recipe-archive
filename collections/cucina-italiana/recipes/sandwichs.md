## Ingredients

- Very fine bread from one day old, or rye bread
- Fresh butter
- Cooked prosciutto, both fat and lean, or salted tongue

## Directions

1. Take very fine bread from one day old or rye bread, remove the crust and cut it into slices half a centimeter thick and approximately 6 centimeters long and 4 centimeters wide
2. Spread them with fresh butter on one side only and stick them together, putting between them a thin slice of either cooked prosciutto (both fat and lean) or salted tongue
