## Ingredients

- 300 grams long Neapolitan macaroni
- 70 grams butter
- 70 grams Gruyère cheese
- 40 grams parmesan cheese
- one small pot of broth

## Directions

1. Give the macaroni two-thirds cooking in not too salted water
2. Place the broth on the fire and when it boils throw in the grated Gruyère and butter to dissolve them well with a spoon
3. Once obtained, pour it immediately over the macaroni already drained of water, and I say immediately, because otherwise the Gruyère sinks to the bottom and becomes pasty
4. Keep the macaroni on the fire until completely cooked making sure some sauce remains
5. When you remove it, season it with the aforesaid parmesan and serve it with more parmesan on the side, for those who not having delicate taste love spiciness
