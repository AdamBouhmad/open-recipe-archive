## Ingredients

- salt cod
- water
- red wine
- cloth
- batter

## Directions

1. To boil it, place in cold water over the fire and remove as soon as it reaches a boil, as it is then cooked
2. Without further handling it can be eaten seasoned with oil and vinegar
3. Alternatively, after boiling, place the whole piece of salt cod in infusion in red wine and keep for several hours
4. Dry in a cloth and cut
