## Ingredients

- beef steak from the loin, thick as one or one and a half fingers
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the steak on the grill over hot charcoal, either natural as it comes from the animal or at most washed and dried.
2. Turn it several times and season with salt and pepper when it is cooked.
3. Send it to table with a small piece of butter on top.
4. It should not be too cooked because its beauty is that when you cut it, it releases abundant juice on the plate.
5. Do not salt it before cooking because the heat will dry it, and if you season it beforehand with oil or other things, as many do, it will taste rancid and be nauseating.
