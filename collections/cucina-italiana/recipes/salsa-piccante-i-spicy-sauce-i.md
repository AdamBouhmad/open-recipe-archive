## Ingredients

- Two spoonfuls of capers in vinegar
- Two anchovies
- A pinch of parsley
- An abundant amount of pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar or lemon juice, if needed

## Directions

1. Take two spoonfuls of capers in vinegar, two anchovies and a pinch of parsley
2. Mince everything together very finely and place this battuto in a sauce boat with an abundant pinch of pepper and much oil
3. If it is not sufficiently acidic, add vinegar or lemon juice, and serve it with boiled fish
