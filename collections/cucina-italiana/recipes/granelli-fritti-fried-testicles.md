## Ingredients

- Mutton testicles
- Salt
- Flour
- Egg

## Directions

1. Boil them in salted water, then make a superficial incision lengthwise to remove the outer membrane composed of the tunic and epididymis
2. Cut them into thin fillets, salt them a little more, flour them well, pass them through beaten egg and fry them
