## Ingredients

- milk-fed veal, loin or rump
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. The pieces most suitable for roasting on a spit are the loin and the rump, and they need only oil and salt for seasoning.
2. These same pieces can also be cooked in a pan, lightly larded with garlic and rosemary, with oil, butter and a small amount of minced cured meat, salt, pepper and tomato juice, cooking fresh peas in the braising liquid.
