## Ingredients

- Cardoons
- oil
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. After boiling the cardoons, dry them well, leave the ribs about a palm long, season them generously with oil, pepper and salt and brown them on the grill.
2. They can serve as a side dish to steak or grilled fish.
