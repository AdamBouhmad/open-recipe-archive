## Ingredients

- one large onion
- oil or lard
- one chicken
- salt
- pepper
- half a glass of Sangiovese or other good red wine
- some tomato juice

## Directions

1. Slice a large onion and soak it in fresh water for more than half an hour, then dry it and put it in a pan with oil or lard.
2. When cooked, set it aside.
3. Cut up a chicken and fry it in the remaining fat, and when browned, add the said onion.
4. Season with salt and pepper and pour in half a glass of Sangiovese or other good red wine and some tomato juice.
5. After five minutes of boiling, serve it.
6. Note that this is not a dish for weak stomachs.
