## Ingredients

- artichokes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- parsley, chopped
- breadcrumbs, finely ground
- lemon juice
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the hard leaves from the artichokes, trim them, and peel the stems.
2. Divide them into four parts, or at most six if they are large.
3. Place them on the fire with butter in proportion and season with salt and pepper.
4. Shake the pan to turn them, and when they have drawn a good part of their moisture, wet them with broth to cook them completely.
5. Remove them dry, and in the remaining sauce add a pinch of chopped parsley, one or two teaspoons of finely ground breadcrumbs, lemon juice, more salt and pepper if needed.
6. Bring to a boil while stirring; then remove the sauce from the fire and when it stops boiling, add one or two egg yolks according to the quantity.
7. Return it briefly to the fire with more broth to make it liquid.
8. Add the artichokes to reheat them and serve them especially as a side dish for boiled meat.
