## Ingredients

- shortcrust pastry from previous recipe
- marzipan from recipe No. 579
- fresh orange zest for flavoring (if candied orange is not available)

## Directions

1. Use the same shortcrust pastry indicated in the previous recipe
2. For the filling, instead of jam, use the marzipan described at No. 579
3. If you do not have candied orange, use fresh orange zest for flavoring, which is very pleasant
4. You may give these offelle a different shape to distinguish them from others
5. Use a mold to shape them, folding it in two to form a scalloped half-moon shape
