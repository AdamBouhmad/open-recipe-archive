## Ingredients

- open ovolo mushrooms
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. The open ovoli are most suited to this preparation
2. After cleaning and washing them, dry them in the folds of a cloth and season with oil, salt and pepper
3. They serve very well as a side dish to steak or any roast
