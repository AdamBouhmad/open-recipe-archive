## Ingredients

- Walnuts with hull, 30 number
- Alcohol, one and a half liters
- Powdered sugar, 750 grams
- Grated cinnamon, 2 grams
- Whole cloves, 10 number
- Water, 4 deciliters
- The rind of one garden lemon in small pieces

## Directions

1. Cut the walnuts into four quarters and place them in infusion with all the aforementioned ingredients in a demijohn or flask with a capacity of four or five liters
2. Close it well and keep it for forty days in a warm place, shaking the vessel from time to time
3. Strain it through cloth and then, to make it clear, pass it through cotton or paper, but taste it a few days before, because if it seems too strong to you, you can add a glass of water to it
