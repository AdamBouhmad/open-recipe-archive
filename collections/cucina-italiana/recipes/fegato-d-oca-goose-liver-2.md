## Ingredients

- goose liver with attached fat
- salt
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the fat into large pieces and place in a pot over heat
2. Add the heart cut into wedges
3. Add the liver in large slices last
4. Season with salt and pepper only
5. Serve at table, drained of excess fat, with lemon wedges
