## Ingredients

- Raw tripe, 700 grams
- Beef foot without bone, 100 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Lard, 70 grams
- Half of one large onion
- Two small cloves of garlic
- Nutmeg and spice flavoring
- Meat broth, as needed
- One handful of parmesan cheese
- Toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Wash the tripe well and cut into strips no wider than half a finger, and cut the foot the same way
2. Mince the onion finely and place on heat with butter
3. When it begins to take color, add the finely chopped lard with the crescent-shaped chopping tool together with the garlic
4. When this soffritto has taken on a hazelnut color, add the tripe and foot, seasoning with salt, pepper and the indicated aromatics in small measure
5. Let it boil until dry, then moisten with broth and finish cooking it over gentle heat to make it tender, which will take 7 to 8 hours total; if the broth runs out, help yourself with more broth
6. When ready to serve, enhance the flavor with parmesan and pour it over slices of toasted bread that should be swimming in the broth
7. Sufficient for five people
