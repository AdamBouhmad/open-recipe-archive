## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Ham, cut into strips
- Veal meat, finely minced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes not too much, or steam them, which would be better, and after slice them as thinly as possible.
2. Cut the ham crosswise into strips about one finger's width.
3. Mince the meat very finely with a knife by striking, and season it with salt, pepper and a little nutmeg, because this and herbs in general, as you already know, are suitable in windy foods. Divide this meat into twelve parts to form as many scallops, flattening them with the blade of the knife, then cook them in white, that is without browning them, with half of the said butter.
4. Take a metal plate or tray, pour into it any fat that may have remained from cooking and four scallops, covering them with a third of the ham and on top of this place a third of the potatoes which you will season with Parmesan and with pieces of remaining butter. Repeat the same operation three times, and lastly place the plate to simmer between two fires and serve it. This is a quantity that can suffice for four or five people.
