## Ingredients

- finocchi (fennel)
- burro (butter)
- brodo (broth)
- farina (flour)
- parmigiano (Parmesan cheese)
- sale (salt)

## Directions

1. After cutting them into pieces and blanching them in salted water, fry them in butter, finish cooking them with broth, bind them with a pinch of flour and when you remove them, season them with a little Parmesan.
