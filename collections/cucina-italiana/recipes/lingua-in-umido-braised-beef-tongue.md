## Ingredients

- One beef tongue, without the papilla, weighing approximately one kilogram
- Ham, fatty and lean, 50 grams
- Half of a medium-sized onion
- Celery
- Carrot
- Parsley
- Butter, 50 grams
- Salt and pepper
- Broth
- Tomato juice or conserve
- Butter, 20 grams
- Flour, one level spoonful
- Celery or other greens (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Take a beef tongue and boil it just enough to be able to peel it
2. Make a generous soffritto with 50 grams of ham (fatty and lean), half a medium onion, celery, carrot and parsley, and place on heat with 50 grams of butter together with the tongue seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Once browned, finish cooking it with broth poured little by little and tomato juice or conserve; then strain the sauce
4. Separately make a beurre manié with 20 grams of butter and one level spoonful of flour
5. When it has taken on a hazelnut color, pour the sauce into it and return the tongue to it for further cooking over low heat
6. Serve it cut into thick slices about one centimeter with a garnish of celery or other vegetables finished in the same sauce
7. A dish that will serve seven or eight people
