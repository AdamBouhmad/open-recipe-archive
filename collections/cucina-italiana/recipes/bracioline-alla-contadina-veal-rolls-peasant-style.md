## Ingredients

- Lean veal, well beaten
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Olives in brine
- Capers, squeezed of vinegar
- Anchovy
- Egg yolk (optional)
- Parmesan cheese (optional)
- Butter
- Tomato juice or conserve dissolved in water
- Onion (optional for soffritto)

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal rolls with well-beaten lean veal, oil them and season with salt and pepper.
2. Make a mixture of olives in brine, capers squeezed of vinegar and one anchovy, chopping everything finely.
3. Leave it simple, or add an egg yolk and a pinch of parmesan.
4. Fill the rolls with this mixture, tie them, and cook them with butter and tomato juice, or in an onion soffritto.
