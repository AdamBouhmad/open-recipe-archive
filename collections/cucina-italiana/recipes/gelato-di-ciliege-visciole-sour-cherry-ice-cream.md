## Ingredients

- Sour cherries, 1 kilogram
- Sugar, 250 grams
- Water, 2 deciliters
- Cinnamon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from 150 grams of the said cherries without damaging them too much and place them on the fire with 50 grams of the said sugar and with a piece of whole cinnamon, which you will then discard.
2. When they are syruped, that is when they have lost their rawness and you see them shriveled, set them aside.
3. Crush with your hands the remaining 850 grams of cherries, pound a handful of their pits in a mortar and mix them back in.
4. Pass a few at a time through a cloth, squeezing hard, these crushed cherries, to extract their juice, and the scraps that remain put them on the fire to extract their juice with the aforementioned two deciliters of water, let them boil 4 or 5 minutes, then pass them through the same cloth and unite the extracted liquid with the previous one.
5. Put all this juice on the fire with two pinches of powdered cinnamon and when it is about to come to a boil pour in the remaining 200 grams of sugar, stir, let it boil for two minutes and pass it through a sieve.
6. Put the strained juice in the sorbetierre and when it is well frozen mix in the syruped cherries so that they are spread equally, serve the ice cream in small glasses and you will see that for its goodness it will be appreciated by all.
