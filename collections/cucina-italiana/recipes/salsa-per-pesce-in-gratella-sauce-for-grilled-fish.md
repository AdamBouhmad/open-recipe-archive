## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Salted anchovies
- Fine oil
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs in their shells for 10 minutes and for each hardboiled egg yolk take one large anchovy or two small ones.
2. Remove the backbone and pass them through a sieve together with the yolks.
3. Dilute the mixture with oil and lemon juice to reduce it to the consistency of a cream.
4. Cover the fish already cooked on the grill with this sauce before sending it to table, or serve it separately in a sauce boat.
