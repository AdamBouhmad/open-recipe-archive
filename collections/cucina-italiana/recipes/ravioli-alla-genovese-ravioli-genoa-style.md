## Ingredients

- Half breast of capon or young hen
- One lamb brain with some sweetbreads
- One chicken liver
- A small piece of butter
- Fat and lean prosciutto
- Few boiled and passed spinach
- Grated Parmesan
- Nutmeg
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put these things on the fire with a small piece of butter and when they begin to take color, finish cooking them with meat broth
2. Lift them out dry and chop them very finely with the crescent blade together with a slice of fat and lean prosciutto
3. Then add few boiled and passed spinach, grated Parmesan, nutmeg and two egg yolks
4. Mix and close them like cappelletti Romagna style or in a simpler way; with this amount you will make about sixty
5. Cook them in broth for a pasta course, or dry with cheese and butter, or with sauce
