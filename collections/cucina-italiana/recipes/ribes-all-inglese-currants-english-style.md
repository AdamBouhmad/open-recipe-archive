## Ingredients

- Currants, 300 grams
- Sugar, 120 grams
- Water, 2 deciliters

## Directions

1. Clean the currants of their stems and put them on the fire with the said water
2. When it comes to a boil, add the sugar
3. Two minutes of boiling is enough, as the currants must remain whole
4. Pour it into a compote dish and serve it cold as cooked fruit
5. The seeds, if one does not wish to swallow them, can be sucked and spit out
