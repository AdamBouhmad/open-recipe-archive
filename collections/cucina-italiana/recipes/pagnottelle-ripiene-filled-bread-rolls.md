## Ingredients

- bread rolls the size of a common apple
- milk
- egg
- lard or oil
- ground meat filling made with chicken livers, chicken breasts, sweetbreads and similar meats
- meat broth
- flour
- truffles

## Directions

1. Scrape the crust lightly with a grater
2. Make a round opening in the middle of each roll the size of a 10-centesimi coin
3. Empty out the crumb leaving the walls somewhat thick
4. Wet them inside and out with boiling milk
5. When sufficiently soaked, close them with their round piece, which should also be soaked
6. Dip them in egg to gild them
7. Fry them in lard or oil, dropping them into the pan with the lid side down so it adheres
8. After cooking, detach the round piece with a knife point
9. Fill with a delicate and hot meat filling of pieces the size of chickpeas, made with chicken livers, chicken breasts, sweetbreads and similar meats drawn with meat broth and bound with a pinch of flour, seasoned with truffles
10. Close them again and serve
