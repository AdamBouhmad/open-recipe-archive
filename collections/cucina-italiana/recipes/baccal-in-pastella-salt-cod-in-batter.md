## Ingredients

- Salt cod
- red wine
- flour
- water
- oil
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the salt cod in cold water and remove it as soon as it reaches its first boil.
2. Place the whole piece of salt cod in an infusion of red wine and keep it there for several hours.
3. Dry it in a cloth and cut it into small pieces, cleaning it of bones and spines.
4. Lightly flour it and dip it into a simple batter made of water, flour, and a drop of oil without salt.
5. Fry it in oil and sprinkle it with sugar when it has lost its first boil.
6. Serve hot.
