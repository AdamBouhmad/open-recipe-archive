## Ingredients

- finest flour, 300 grams
- butter, 100 grams
- sugar, 80 grams
- sultana raisins, 80 grams
- eggs, 1 whole and 2 yolks
- salt, a pinch
- cream of tartar, 10 grams
- baking soda, 1 teaspoon or about 5 grams
- candied fruit in small pieces, 20 grams
- lemon zest
- milk, about 2 deciliters

## Directions

1. In winter soften the butter in a bain-marie and work it with the eggs; add the flour and milk little by little, then the rest except the raisins and powders which you will save for last; but, before pouring these in, work the mixture for at least half an hour and reduce it with milk to the proper consistency, that is, neither too liquid nor too stiff.
2. Pour it into a smooth mold taller than it is wide and with double walls so that as it rises it does not overflow and can take the form of a round loaf. Grease the walls with butter, dust it with powdered sugar mixed with flour and bake it in the oven. If it turns out well you will see that it rises greatly forming a cracked swelling on top.
