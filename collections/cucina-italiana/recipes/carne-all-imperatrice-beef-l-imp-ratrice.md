## Ingredients

- Lean beef from the neck, 500 grams
- Ham, fat and lean, 50 grams
- Parmesan cheese, grated, 3 heaping spoonfuls
- Eggs, 2
- Butter, 30 grams
- Oil, 2 spoonfuls
- Garlic
- Rosemary
- Tomato juice or conserve
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. If you do not have a meat grinder to reduce to paste both the meat and the ham, use a knife and a mortar. Add the Parmesan and eggs, season the mixture with salt and pepper, mix it well, and with wet hands form it into a flattened patty two fingers high.
2. Place 30 grams of butter and two spoonfuls of oil on the fire in a baking dish or pot; when they begin to boil place the said flattened patty of meat on it and on the same sprinkle a clove of garlic cut into slices and some sprigs of rosemary. Let it boil, and when it begins to dry out wet it with tomato juice or conserve dissolved in water. Send it to table surrounded by its sauce.
