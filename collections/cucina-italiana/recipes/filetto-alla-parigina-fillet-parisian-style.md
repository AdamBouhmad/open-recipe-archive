## Ingredients

- round braciole from the best part of beef fillet, about half a finger thick
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- meat gravy or similar sauce
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Have the butcher cut for you round braciole from the best part of beef fillet, about half a finger thick.
2. Sauté them in butter after it has taken color over lively fire; salt and pepper for seasoning.
3. When they have formed a crust on all sides so that they remain juicy and slightly undercooked inside, sprinkle a pinch of chopped parsley over them and remove them immediately.
4. Before taking them to the table, cover them with meat gravy or similar sauce, or, which is simpler, into the juice remaining after cooking throw a pinch of flour and with broth make a mixture and use this instead of the sauce.
