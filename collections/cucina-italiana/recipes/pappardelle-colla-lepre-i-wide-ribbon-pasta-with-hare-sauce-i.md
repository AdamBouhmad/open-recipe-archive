## Ingredients

- two hare fillets (180 to 200 grams total, including kidneys)
- butter (50 grams)
- cured meat (40 grams)
- half a medium onion
- half a carrot
- a piece of celery about a palm long
- nutmeg
- parmesan cheese
- flour (one tablespoon)
- meat sauce (6 deciliters)
- pappardelle made from three-egg pasta or 500 to 600 grams of store-bought pasta strips

## Directions

1. Skin the hare fillets of the membrane that wraps them and cut them into small dice.
2. Make a battuto with the cured meat, onion, celery and carrot.
3. Chop it very fine with a crescent-shaped knife and put it on the fire with a third of the said butter and with the hare meat, seasoning it with salt and pepper.
4. When the meat is browned, sprinkle it with flour and continue the preparation as indicated.
