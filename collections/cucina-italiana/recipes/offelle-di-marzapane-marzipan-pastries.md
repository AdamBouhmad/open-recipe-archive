## Ingredients

- Short crust pastry
- Marzipan
- Fresh orange for flavoring (if you don't have candied orange)

## Directions

1. Use the same short crust pastry indicated in the previous recipe and for filling, instead of jam, use the marzipan described in recipe 579 and, if you don't have candied orange, use fresh orange for flavoring which is very pleasant
2. You can give these offelle a different shape to distinguish them from the others
3. Use a stamp that you can fold in two forming a scalloped half moon
