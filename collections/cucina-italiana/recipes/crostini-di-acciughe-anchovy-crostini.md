## Ingredients

- Anchovies
- Butter

## Directions

1. Wash the anchovies and remove their spine and small bones
2. Mince them with a crescent-shaped chopper, add butter in proportion, and crush the mixture with the blade of a dinner knife to reduce it to a homogeneous paste
3. Spread on bread slices like an ointment
