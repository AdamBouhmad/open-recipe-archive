## Ingredients

- Cornmeal, 200 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Powdered sugar, 80 grams
- Dried flowers of common elderflower, 10 grams
- Egg yolks, 2

## Directions

1. If the paste becomes too firm when kneading, soften it with a drop of water
2. Roll it out with a rolling pin to slightly more than the thickness of a shield and cut it into small discs, as these can also be served with tea, and to make them more attractive they can be streaked on the surface in the same manner with the prongs of a fork or with a grater
