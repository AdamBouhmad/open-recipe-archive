## Ingredients

- flour
- water
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix flour with cold water and salt, adding a little lard
2. Work and knead the dough
3. Heat lard in a pan until it boils and remove from fire
4. Drop the dough into the hot lard to fry
5. The flatbread will puff up better this way
