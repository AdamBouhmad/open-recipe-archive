## Ingredients

- Large oranges, 4
- Garden lemons, 1
- Water, 6 deciliters
- Sugar, 300 grams

## Directions

1. Squeeze the oranges and lemon and strain their juice.
2. Boil the sugar in the water for 10 minutes, pour it into the juice, pass the mixture through a sieve one more time and put it in the sorbetierre.
3. Serve it in goblet glasses filled to the brim, or all in one piece.
