## Ingredients

- Fine bread from the previous day, or rye bread
- Fresh butter
- Sliced ham, fatty and lean
- or salted tongue

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from the fine bread of the previous day or rye bread and cut it into slices about half a centimeter thick and approximately 6 centimeters long and 4 centimeters wide.
2. Spread them with fresh butter on only one side and stick them together, placing between them a thin slice of either fatty and lean cooked ham or salted tongue.
