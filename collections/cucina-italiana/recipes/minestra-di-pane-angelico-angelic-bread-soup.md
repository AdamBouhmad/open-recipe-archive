## Ingredients

- Crumb of fine bread, 150 grams
- Fat and lean ham, 50 grams
- Beef marrow, 40 grams
- Parmesan, 40 grams
- Flour, as needed
- Eggs, 2 minus one white
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumb with boiling broth just enough to soak it well and squeeze it hard in a cloth.
2. Mince the ham very finely; flatten the beef marrow with the flat blade of a knife, and stir it with the blade until it becomes like a salve.
3. Mix these three things together with the Parmesan and add the eggs.
4. Spread a veil of flour on the work surface, pour the mixture onto it, cover it with more flour and let it take enough flour (something less than 100 grams will do) to form rather soft balls about the size of hazelnuts.
5. Drop them into boiling broth and after 10 minutes of cooking serve them.
6. This dose will serve ten or twelve persons.
