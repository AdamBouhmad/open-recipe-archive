## Ingredients

- Tripe, boiled and cut as in the previous recipe
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Eggs, beaten
- Lemon juice
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil and cut the tripe as in the previous recipe
2. Place on heat in a soffritto of garlic, parsley and butter
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. When cooked, bind with beaten eggs, lemon juice and parmesan cheese
