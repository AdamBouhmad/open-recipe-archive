## Ingredients

- Flour, 300 grams
- Blonde sugar, 300 grams
- Shelled walnuts, 100 grams
- Candied orange, 50 grams
- Anise seeds, 15 grams
- Spices and cinnamon powder, 5 grams
- Water

## Directions

1. Chop the walnuts to roughly the size of vetch.
2. Cut the candied orange into small cubes.
3. Place the sugar on the fire with one third of its weight in water and when reduced to thread stage, throw all the ingredients into it, stir and pour the hot mixture onto the work surface over the flour to knead it; you will need more flour to make the dough consistent.
4. Form the cavallucci, from which you will obtain over 40 with this amount, and since this dough sticks due to the sugar, dust them with flour on the surface.
5. Place them on a baking sheet and bake them in white at moderate heat.
6. Pay careful attention to the cooking of the sugar, because if it cooks too much it becomes dark. When, taking a drop between thumb and forefinger, it begins to form a thread, that is enough for this use.
