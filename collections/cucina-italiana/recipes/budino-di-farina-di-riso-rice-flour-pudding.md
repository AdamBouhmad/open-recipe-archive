## Ingredients

- Milk, 1 liter
- Rice flour, 200 grams
- Sugar, 120 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Eggs, 6
- Salt, a pinch
- Vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. First dissolve the flour with a quarter of the milk cold
2. Add some of the hot milk when it is boiling and pour it into the rest of the milk when it boils; this will prevent lumps from forming
3. When it is cooked add the sugar, butter, and salt
4. Remove it from the fire and wait until it is lukewarm to mix in the eggs and flavoring
5. Cook this pudding like the previous one
