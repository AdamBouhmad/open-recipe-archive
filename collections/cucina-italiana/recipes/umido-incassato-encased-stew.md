## Ingredients

- Flour, 150 grams
- Butter, 70 grams
- Parmesan cheese, 30 grams
- Milk, 6 deciliters
- Eggs, 3
- Salt, to taste
- Spinach, one bunch

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach, squeeze it well and pass it through a sieve.
2. Make a béchamel sauce with the flour, butter, Parmesan and milk.
3. Break the eggs when you remove the béchamel from the fire, and color half of the béchamel green with the spinach.
4. Grease a ring-shaped copper mold with a hole in the middle and fluted all around with cold butter; fill it first with the green béchamel, then with the yellow béchamel and let it set in a water bath.
5. Unmold it while hot and fill the center with a well-made sauce of chicken giblets and sweetbreads, or braised milk veal rolls flavored with mushrooms or truffles.
6. Cook the dish with butter and meat sauce or in another manner, ensuring it turns out delicate, and this dish will make a fine showing and be praised.
