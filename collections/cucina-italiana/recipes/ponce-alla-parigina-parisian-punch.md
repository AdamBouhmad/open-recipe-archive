## Ingredients

- One cup of about two deciliters
- One egg yolk
- Two teaspoons of sugar
- Two or three tablespoons of cognac, rum, or other liqueur to taste
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Take a cup of about two deciliters capacity
2. Whisk in an egg yolk with two teaspoons of sugar, beating until it becomes almost white
3. Add, seasoning to taste, two or three tablespoons of cognac, rum, or other liqueur you prefer
4. Fill the cup with boiling water poured gradually while continuing to whisk to create foam
