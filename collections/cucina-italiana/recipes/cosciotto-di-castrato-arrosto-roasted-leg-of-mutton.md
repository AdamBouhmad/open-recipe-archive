## Ingredients

- a leg of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- degreased broth

## Directions

1. Let it age for several days before cooking, more or less depending on temperature.
2. Beat it well with a wooden mallet before threading onto the spit.
3. Skin it and remove the middle bone without damaging it too much.
4. Tie it so it stays compact and apply intense heat at first, then reduce heat halfway through cooking.
5. When it begins to release juices, collect them in the dripping pan and baste with the same juices and degreased broth, nothing else.
6. Salt when cooking is almost complete; ensure it is not overcooked nor bloody.
7. Serve at table with its juices in a sauceboat and wrap the end of the leg bone in fringed white paper for better presentation.
