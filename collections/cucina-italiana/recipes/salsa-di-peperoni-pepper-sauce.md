## Ingredients

- Large green peppers
- Oil
- Butter
- Garlic, 1 clove
- Salt
- Tomato juice

## Directions

1. Take large green peppers, open them, clean them of seeds and cut them lengthwise into four or five strips.
2. Give them a small scald in a pan with a little oil so you can peel them.
3. After peeling, put on the fire a finely chopped clove of garlic with oil and butter and when it is browned throw in the peppers, salt them, let them take on flavor a little and add tomato juice.
4. Do not cook them too much because they would lose their spiciness, which is what gives them charm, and serve them with boiled meat.
