## Ingredients

- Ricotta or raviggiuolo, or both together, 200 grams
- Parmesan, 40 grams
- Whole eggs, 1 and one yolk
- Nutmeg and spice scent
- A pinch of salt
- Some chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Enclose them in a pasta sheet made like that of cappelletti and cut with a round disc somewhat larger.
2. You can leave them with the first fold as a half moon, but the form of cappelletti is to be preferred.
3. Cook them in sufficiently salted water, remove them dry and season with cheese and butter.
4. With this amount you will obtain 24 or 25 and they can suffice, being large, for three people.
