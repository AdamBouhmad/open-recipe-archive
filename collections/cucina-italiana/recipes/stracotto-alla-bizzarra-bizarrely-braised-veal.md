## Ingredients

- Lean veal without bone, 700 to 800 grams
- Lard, 100 grams
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Two sage leaves
- A bunch of rosemary
- Half a garlic clove
- Water
- One teaspoon of flour
- A piece of butter
- Beef broth, one ladle
- Marsala wine, one finger measure of a glass

## Directions

1. Lard the veal with pieces of lard the thickness of a finger, which you will have first seasoned with salt and pepper, and season the meat the same way.
2. Tie it so it stays compact and place it in a pot half covered with water with two sage leaves, a bunch of rosemary and half a garlic clove; if the meat is very tender use less water.
3. When, in boiling, it has become dry, let it take on color with a teaspoon of flour.
4. Add a piece of butter, then wet it with a ladle of broth and a finger measure of a glass of marsala.
5. Strain the sauce without squeezing it and pour it over the piece of meat when you serve it at table.
