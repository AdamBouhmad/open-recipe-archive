## Ingredients

- Rice, 300 grams
- Butter
- Water
- Meat broth
- Offal (liver, heart, etc.)
- Prosciutto slices (optional)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mushrooms or truffles (optional)
- Parmesan cheese
- Eggs, 2

## Directions

1. Make a good meat broth and use it both for the rice and the offal.
2. Sauté the offal in butter with optional prosciutto slices, season with salt and pepper, and cook through with the broth. The aroma of mushrooms or truffles enhances it.
3. Sauté the rice in butter until dry, then cook it with boiling water and finish with the broth and parmesan cheese. When the rice has cooled slightly, add the two beaten eggs.
4. Butter a smooth, round or oval mold, line the bottom with buttered paper, and pour in the rice to set it in a country oven.
5. Once unmolded, pour the offal sauce (condensed slightly with a pinch of flour) over the top and serve the offal around it, allowing it to be immersed in the sauce.
6. This serves five or six people.
