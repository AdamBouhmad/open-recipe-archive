## Ingredients

- Lamb loin
- Lard
- Eggs, 4 or 5
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a lamb loin, which is the part that best lends itself to this dish, into small pieces and fry it in pure lard; little is enough because in that place the meat is rather fatty.
2. At mid-cooking season the lamb with salt and pepper and when it is totally cooked pour in four or five beaten eggs and lightly seasoned also with salt and pepper.
3. Mix, making sure the eggs set only slightly.
