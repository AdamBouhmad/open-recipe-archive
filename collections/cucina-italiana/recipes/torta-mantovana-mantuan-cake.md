## Ingredients

- Flour, 170 grams
- Sugar, 170 grams
- Butter, 150 grams
- Sweet almonds and pine nuts, 50 grams
- Whole eggs, 1
- Egg yolks, 4
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. First work very well with a wooden spoon, inside a bowl, the eggs with the sugar; then pour into it the flour a little at a time, working it still, and finally the butter liquefied in a bain-marie
2. Put the mixture in a buttered copper baking pan dusted with powdered sugar and flour or breadcrumbs and decorate the top with the almonds and pine nuts
3. Cut the pine nuts in two lengthwise and the almonds, after blanching them with hot water and splitting them lengthwise, cut them crosswise, making four or five small pieces from each half
4. Be careful that this cake does not come out thicker than one and a half fingers or two at most so that it has a chance to dry well in the oven, which should be kept at moderate heat
5. Dust it with powdered sugar and serve it cold, which will be much appreciated
