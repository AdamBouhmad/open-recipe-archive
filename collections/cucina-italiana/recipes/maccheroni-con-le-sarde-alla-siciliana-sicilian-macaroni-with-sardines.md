## Ingredients

- Long macaroni Neapolitan style, 500 grams
- Fresh sardines, 500 grams
- Salted anchovies, 6 pieces
- Wild fennel (called young fennel), 300 grams
- Oil, as needed

## Directions

1. Remove the head, tail and spine from the sardines, dividing them in two parts, flour them, fry them, salt them somewhat and set them aside
2. Boil the fennel, squeeze out the water, mince them finely and set them aside
3. After cooking the macaroni whole in salted water, drain them well and set these aside as well
4. Put abundant oil in a pan over heat and in it dissolve the six anchovies, after having cleaned them and removed the spine
5. Pour into this sauce the fennel, season with little salt and pepper and let them boil for ten minutes with tomato juice or conserve dissolved in water
6. Now that you have everything ready, take a plate that can withstand heat or a baking dish and season the macaroni layer by layer with the sardines and with the anchovy-fennel sauce so that they form a mound
7. Brown them between two fires and serve them hot
