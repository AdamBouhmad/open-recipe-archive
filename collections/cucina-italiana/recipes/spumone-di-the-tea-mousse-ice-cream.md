## Ingredients

- Panna montata (whipped cream), as that which dairymen prepare, 250 grams
- Acqua (water), 200 grams
- Zucchero (sugar), 100 grams
- The (tea), the finest quality, 15 grams
- Rossi d'uovo (egg yolks), 3 number
- Colla di pesce (fish gelatin), 3 leaves

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the tea and keep it infused, in a state almost of ebullition, for 40 minutes
2. Then strain it through a fine cloth, squeezing hard to extract all the flavor, and you will see it appear black as coffee
3. With this liquid, with the egg yolks and with the sugar make a cream as in recipe 753, and regulating yourself in the same manner, add the gelatin, then unite the cream to the whipped cream mixing slowly and, having poured the mixture into an ice cream mold, place it among ice and salt as with biscuit
4. It should be enough for eight persons
