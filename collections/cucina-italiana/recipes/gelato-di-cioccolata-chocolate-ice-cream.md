## Ingredients

- Milk, 1 liter
- Fine chocolate, 200 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate and place it on the fire with the sugar and four deciliters of the aforementioned milk in a saucepan where it will remain confined.
2. Let it boil for a few minutes, stirring it constantly so that it refines.
3. Remove it from the fire, add the rest of the milk and pour the mixture into the sorbetierre when it is cold.
4. If you want this ice cream more substantial, increase the sugar to 120 grams and add two egg yolks when you remove the chocolate from the fire and it is no longer boiling. Mix, and return it to the stove for a few minutes and then, as has been said, add the rest of the milk.
