## Ingredients

- one large head of cauliflower or two small ones
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- water
- salt
- pepper
- tomato preserve
- hot water
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Divide the cauliflower into small florets and wash them
2. Cook the raw, unwashed florets as follows: heat a chopped mixture of garlic, parsley and oil over the fire, and when it is browned, stop the cooking with a little water
3. Add the cauliflower, season with salt and pepper, and when it has absorbed the mixture, finish cooking it using tomato preserve dissolved in hot water
4. Add grace and more flavor with parmesan cheese when serving it at table, where it can serve as a side dish to boiled meat, a stew, or a coteghino
