## Ingredients

- Rice, 150 grams
- Parmesan cheese, 30 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Milk, about 7 deciliters
- Eggs, 3
- Salt, to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk, adding butter, salt it and lastly, when it is cold, add the eggs.
2. Pour it into a smooth mold with a hole in the middle with buttered paper underneath, place it briefly in a water bath so it does not harden, unmold it hot and garnish the center with giblets.
3. This amount will serve five persons.
