## Ingredients

- 700 grams lean beef without bone from the rump, loin, or fillet
- 50 grams butter
- 2 deciliters cream
- 2 deciliters water
- 1 tablespoon strong vinegar or more if weak
- half an onion cut into four wedges
- 1 carrot cut into small pieces
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Place the meat in a pot with the butter, onion wedges, carrot pieces, salt and pepper
2. When the meat is well browned, pour in the vinegar
3. After some time, add the water
4. Then add the cream
5. Let it boil slowly for about three hours, adding more water if the sauce becomes scarce
6. Serve the meat sliced with its sauce strained through a sieve
7. This dish can serve five or six people as one of several courses
