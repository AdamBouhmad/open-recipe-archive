## Ingredients

- Capers in vinegar, 50 grams
- Powdered sugar, 50 grams
- Raisins, 30 grams
- Pine nuts, 20 grams
- Candied fruit, 20 grams
- Flour, 1 heaping teaspoon
- Sugar, 2 teaspoons
- Water, half a glass
- Vinegar, a small amount
- Bread slices, fried in good oil or lightly toasted

## Directions

1. Chop the capers coarsely
2. Clean the raisins of stems and wash them well
3. Cut the pine nuts into three parts lengthwise
4. Shape the prosciutto into very small dice and reduce the candied fruit into small pieces
5. Put a heaping teaspoon of flour and two teaspoons of sugar in a small saucepan over the fire; when this mixture has taken on a brown color, pour in half a glass of water mixed with a small amount of vinegar
6. When it has boiled until the lumps dissolve, throw all the ingredients into the saucepan at once and let them boil for ten minutes, tasting them meanwhile to feel if the sweet and strong flavor is right
7. When the mixture is still hot, spread it over thin slices of bread fried in good oil or simply lightly toasted
8. Serve these crostini cold, or in the middle of the meal to excite the appetite of your guests
