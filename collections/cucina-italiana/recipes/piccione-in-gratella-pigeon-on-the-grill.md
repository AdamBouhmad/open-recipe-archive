## Ingredients

- one large young pigeon
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- 40 grams of butter
- one egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take a large but young pigeon, divide it in two lengthwise and flatten the pieces well with your hands.
2. Then place them to fry in oil for four or five minutes, just enough to firm up the meat.
3. Season it while hot with salt and pepper, then prepare it in this manner: melt 40 grams of butter over heat without letting it boil; beat an egg and mix one with the other.
4. Dip the pigeon well in this mixture and after some time roll it entirely in breadcrumbs.
5. Cook it on the grill over slow heat and serve it with a sauce or side dish.
