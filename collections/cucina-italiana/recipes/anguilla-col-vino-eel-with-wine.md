## Ingredients

- One eel of about half a kilogram or more than one of the same total weight
- sand
- salt
- pepper
- garlic clove
- sage leaves
- lemon rind
- oil
- tomato juice or tomato preserve
- red or white dry wine
- water

## Directions

1. Rub the eels with sand to clean them of mucus, wash them, and cut them into chunks.
2. Place in a pan one garlic clove cut into thin slices, three or four sage leaves cut roughly, the rind of a quarter lemon, and not much oil.
3. Put it on the fire, and when the soffritto has taken on color, place the eels in it and season with salt and pepper.
4. When the stew begins to reduce, loosen them with the tip of a knife so they do not stick, and brown them on one side.
5. Brown them on the other side, pour in a good finger of red or white dry wine mixed with two fingers of water, cover them, and let them finish cooking over low heat.
6. Serve them at table with some of their sauce and serve to four people.
