## Ingredients

- Rice, 100 grams
- Milk, half a liter
- Butter, slightly more than a walnut
- Parmesan cheese, a good pinch
- Eggs, 1
- Chicken livers, 2
- Lamb sweetbreads, 2
- Nutmeg flavoring
- Prosciutto pieces
- Truffle or mushroom pieces

## Directions

1. Cook the rice well in the milk adding the butter and when it is cooked, salt it and wait for it to lose the strong heat to beat in the egg and add the Parmesan.
2. Cook two chicken livers and two lamb sweetbreads, making a delicate sauce, and give it the flavor of nutmeg; cut it into pieces smaller than a hazelnut and add pieces of prosciutto, truffles or mushrooms that give it much grace.
3. To give this filled rice the shape of pears have a small tin mold made and fill it half with rice, place two or three pieces of the sauce described and finish filling it with other rice.
4. Form the pear, and to extract it blow from the small tube, repeating the operation until you have used all the mixture.
5. To fry these pears you must gild them with eggs and breadcrumbs.
