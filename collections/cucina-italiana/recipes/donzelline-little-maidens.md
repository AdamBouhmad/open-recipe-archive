## Ingredients

- Flour, 100 grams
- Butter, one walnut-sized piece
- Milk, as needed
- Salt, a pinch
- Lard or oil for frying
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough neither too stiff nor too soft, work it much by hand on a work surface and stretch it into a sheet the thickness of a coin. Cut it into small almond shapes, fry it in lard or oil, and you will see it puff, becoming tender and delicate to the taste.
2. Dust the donzelline with powdered sugar when they are no longer hot.
