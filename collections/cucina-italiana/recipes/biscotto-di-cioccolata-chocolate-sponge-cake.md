## Ingredients

- eggs, 6
- powdered sugar, 200 grams
- wheat flour, 150 grams
- vanilla chocolate, 50 grams

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate and put it in a bowl with the sugar and egg yolks and beat them with a spoon; then add the flour and work the mixture for more than half an hour; finally the beaten egg whites mixing gently.
2. Bake it as the previous recipe.
