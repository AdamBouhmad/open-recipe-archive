## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Water
- Butter
- Milk
- Salt
- Béchamel
- Parmigiano

## Directions

1. Parboil it, squeeze out the water, chop it with a crescent-shaped blade, put it on the fire with butter and milk to cook it through and salt it.
2. When it is well cooked unite to it béchamel that is rather firm; make sure it incorporates well on the fire with the cabbage and add grated parmigiano.
3. Taste it to check if it has flavor and if the seasoning is right and serve it as a side with boiled meat or a meat stew.
