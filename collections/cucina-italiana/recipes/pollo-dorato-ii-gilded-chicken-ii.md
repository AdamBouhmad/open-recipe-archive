## Ingredients

- Young chicken
- Flour
- Eggs, 2
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon wedges

## Directions

1. After treating it as the previous recipe, cut it into smaller pieces, coat with flour, immerse it in two beaten and well-salted eggs; fry it in a pan, season it still a little with salt and pepper, and serve it with lemon wedges.
