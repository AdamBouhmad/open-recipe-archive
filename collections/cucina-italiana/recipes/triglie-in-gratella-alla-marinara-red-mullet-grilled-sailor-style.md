## Ingredients

- Red mullet
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Rosemary leaves
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Remove the intestines from the mullet using a knife tip at the gills, wash and dry them.
2. Place a small piece of garlic where the intestine was.
3. Season with salt, pepper, oil, and rosemary leaves and let sit.
4. When ready to cook, coat in breadcrumbs and oil with the seasoning while on the fire.
5. Alternatively, after cleaning, washing and drying, season with just salt and pepper and cook over high heat.
6. Place on a serving dish and season only then with oil, more salt and pepper.
7. Serve with lemon wedges.
