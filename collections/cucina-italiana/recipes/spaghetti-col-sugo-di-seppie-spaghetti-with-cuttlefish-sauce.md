## Ingredients

- 3 medium cuttlefish (650-700 grams total)
- 100 grams crumb of bread
- parsley
- 1 clove garlic
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- 1 onion of medium size
- tomato juice or conserve in large quantity
- water
- 500 grams spaghetti
- 30 grams Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the cuttlefish by removing the bone, mouth apparatus, eyes, digestive tube, and ink sac.
2. Make a mixture with 100 grams bread crumb, a good pinch of parsley and one clove of garlic, add the tentacles (two per cuttlefish) finely chopped, season with oil, salt and pepper, and use this to fill the cuttlefish sac, sewing the mouth closed.
3. Chop one onion of medium size, squeeze it to remove bitterness and put it in a pan with oil over heat; when it takes color, add the cuttlefish and season with salt and pepper.
4. Wait until they color, then cook them slowly with much tomato juice or conserve, adding water gradually.
5. Boil for three hours, ensuring enough sauce remains to season the pasta.
6. Season 500 grams of spaghetti with the sauce and grated Parmesan cheese.
7. Serve the cuttlefish afterwards as a fish course in sauce.
