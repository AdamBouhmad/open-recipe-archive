## Ingredients

- one whole veal tongue with its pappagorgia (soft palate), from milk-fed calf
- water for boiling
- as for wild boar sweet-strong sauce: raisins, chocolate, pine nuts, candied fruit, sugar, vinegar

## Directions

1. Skin the tongue and partially boil it. For the rest, proceed as for the wild boar (recipe N. 285), using the water in which it boiled to finish cooking it.
2. To skin the tongue, heat a pallet and place it on top, repeating the operation several times if necessary.
3. Prepare the sweet-strong sauce as indicated in recipe N. 285 and incorporate it into the cooking liquid.
