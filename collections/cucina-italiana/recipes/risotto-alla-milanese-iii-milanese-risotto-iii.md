## Ingredients

- Rice, 300 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- One quarter of a medium-sized onion
- Marsala wine, two fingers of a common glass
- Saffron, as needed
- Parmesan cheese
- Broth

## Directions

1. Brown the finely chopped onion with half the butter
2. Pour in the rice and after a few minutes the Marsala
3. Cook it with broth and when cooked add the remaining butter and the saffron dissolved in a little broth
4. Finally add a handful of Parmesan cheese
5. Serves three people
