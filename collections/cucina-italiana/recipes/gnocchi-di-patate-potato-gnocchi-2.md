## Ingredients

- Large yellow potatoes, 400 grams
- Wheat flour, 150 grams
- Salt
- Parmesan cheese
- Butter
- Tomato sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in water or, better, steamed, and while hot and boiling, peel them and pass them through a sieve
2. Then knead them with the said flour and work the dough somewhat with your hands, stretching it into a thin cylinder so you can cut it into pieces about three centimeters long
3. Lightly dust them with flour and, taking them one at a time, hollow them with your thumb on the back of a grater
4. Cook them in salted water for ten minutes, remove them dry and season them with cheese, butter and tomato sauce, if you like
5. If you want them more delicate, cook them in milk and serve them without draining; if the milk is of good quality, apart from salt, it is sufficient
