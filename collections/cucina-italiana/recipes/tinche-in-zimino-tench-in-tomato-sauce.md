## Ingredients

- Tench, whole
- onion, garlic, parsley, celery, carrot (for soffritto)
- oil
- salt and pepper
- tomato sauce or tomato preserve dissolved in water
- beet greens or spinach, for garnish
- peas (optional)

## Directions

1. Make a soffritto with onion, garlic, parsley, celery, and carrot.
2. Heat it in oil and when it takes color, add the tench heads cut into small pieces.
3. Season with salt and pepper and cook well, moistening with tomato sauce or preserve dissolved in water.
4. Pass the sauce through a sieve and set aside.
5. Clean the tench, cut off the fins and tail, and leave whole.
6. Place them in a pan with oil when it begins to sizzle.
7. Season with salt and pepper and cook them with the sauce added little by little.
8. You can serve them as they are, which is excellent.
9. To give the zimino its true character, serve with a side of greens (beet greens or spinach) that have been boiled and flavored in the cooking liquid.
10. Peas also go well with this dish.
11. Salt cod can be prepared in zimino the same way.
