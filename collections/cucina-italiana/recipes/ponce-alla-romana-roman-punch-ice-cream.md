## Ingredients

- Zucchero (sugar), 450 grams
- Acqua (water), 5 deciliters
- Aranci (oranges), 2 number
- Limoni (lemons), 2 number
- Chiare d'uovo (egg whites), 2 number
- Rhum (rum), one small glass
- Odore di vaniglia (vanilla flavoring)

## Directions

1. Boil for five or six minutes 250 grams of the said sugar in 4 deciliters of the said water, with a little peel of lemon and orange inside
2. Remove from fire, squeeze the juice of the oranges and lemons into this syrup, strain it through a cloth and pour it into the ice cream maker to freeze
3. Put on the fire the remaining 200 grams of sugar in the remaining deciliter of water, give it the vanilla flavoring, and let it boil until the point that pouring a drop onto a plate it remains round, and taken between the fingers makes threads; but first you will have beaten the two egg whites until very stiff, onto which you will pour the sugar thus boiling, beating them to form a united paste which you will throw, when cooled, into the ice cream already ready, mixing well; at the moment of serving add the rum and serve it at table in small glasses
