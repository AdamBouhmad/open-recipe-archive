## Ingredients

- Ladyfingers, 100 grams
- Malaga raisins, 70 grams
- Sultana raisins, 50 grams
- Candied citron, 30 grams
- Marsala wine, as needed
- Milk, 2 deciliters
- Whole eggs, 2
- Sugar, 50 grams
- Vanilla flavoring
- Whole eggs, 2
- Marsala wine, 1-1/2 deciliters
- Sugar, 50 grams

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from the malaga raisins
2. Cut the candied citron into small pieces
3. Take a ring mold and butter it with cold butter, then lightly dip only the surface of the ladyfingers in marsala and fill the mold with them, interspersing layers with the raisins and candied fruit
4. Make a cream with the milk, 2 whole eggs, 50 grams sugar, and vanilla flavor
5. Pour this raw cream into the mold over the ladyfingers
6. Cook it in a water bath, unmold it while hot, and before bringing it to the table, fill the hole overflowing with a zabaglione made from 2 whole eggs, 1-1/2 deciliters marsala, and 50 grams sugar
7. Whip the zabaglione mixture with a whisk in a basin over the fire
8. You can substitute the raisins with candied fruits, or a mixture of raisins and candied citron and orange
9. This will serve six people. It is a dessert that pleases
