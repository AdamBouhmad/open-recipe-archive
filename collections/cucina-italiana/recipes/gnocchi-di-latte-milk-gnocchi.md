## Ingredients

- Milk, one litre
- Sugar, 240 grams
- Starch reduced to powder, 120 grams
- Egg yolks, 8
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix everything in the same way you would for a cream and place it on the fire in a saucepan, constantly stirring with a spoon
2. When the mixture has become firm, keep it a few more minutes on the fire and then pour it into a baking dish or plate at a height of one and a half fingers and cut it into almond shapes when cooled
3. Arrange these pieces one on top of the other with symmetry on a copper or porcelain tray that can withstand the fire, interspersing them with some pieces of butter, and brown them somewhat in a country oven to serve them hot
