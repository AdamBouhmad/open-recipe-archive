## Ingredients

- Potatoes, 300 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 20 grams
- Egg yolks, 2
- Sugar, 1 teaspoon
- Nutmeg flavoring

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and if they are large cut them into four parts and put them to boil in salted water.
2. Pass them through a sieve while still hot and dry.
3. To the puree add the butter, melted if it is winter, and all the rest, mixing well.
4. Let the mixture cool well, divide it into ten or twelve parts and, over a light layer of flour, give them the shape of spools to obtain the croquettes.
5. Beat an egg and dip them one by one into it, coat them with breadcrumbs and fry them in oil or lard to serve as a side dish to fried meat or a roast.
