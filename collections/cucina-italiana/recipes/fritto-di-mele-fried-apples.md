## Ingredients

- Large apples of good quality, not too ripe
- Pastella (batter) N. 156
- Powdered sugar
- Anise seeds (optional)

## Directions

1. Take large apples of good quality, not too ripe
2. Remove the core with a metal tube made for this purpose, which leaves a hole in the middle
3. Peel them and cut them into thick slices just under one centimeter
4. Place them in batter N. 156 when you are about to fry them; if you like the flavor of anise, which goes well here, add a pinch
5. Dust them with powdered sugar and serve hot
