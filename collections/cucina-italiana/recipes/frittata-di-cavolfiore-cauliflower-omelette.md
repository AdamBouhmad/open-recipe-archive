## Ingredients

- 300 grams of boiled cauliflower, without leaves and stem
- 60 grams of butter
- 2 heaping tablespoons of grated Parmesan
- 1 tablespoon of oil
- 6 eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the cauliflower finely and put it in a pan with butter and oil, seasoning with salt and pepper
2. Beat the eggs with Parmesan, salt them, and pour them over the cauliflower when it has drawn in the fat
3. Keep the omelette thin so you do not need to flip it; if the pan is small, make two instead
