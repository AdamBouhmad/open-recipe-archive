## Ingredients

- one teal duck
- onion
- celery
- carrot
- ham
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- tomato sauce or preserve
- dried mushrooms
- spices or nutmeg
- flour
- parmesan cheese
- pasta

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the teal, place it in a pot with a battuto of onion, a piece of celery, half a carrot, 40 grams of ham and a piece of butter; season with salt and pepper.
2. When browned, finish cooking it with good broth and a little tomato sauce or preserve.
3. Then bone it and chop it together with some pieces of dried mushrooms if you had them united to the teal while it was cooking.
4. Return to the fire this mixture with the aroma of spices or nutmeg and a piece of butter kneaded with flour to bind it, and with it and parmesan season 350 grams of pasta which can be macaroni, strips, penne or other similar shapes.
5. This quantity can be enough for five people if they are not big eaters.
6. If you add 50 grams of beef fillet to the teal you will have a more substantial sauce.
