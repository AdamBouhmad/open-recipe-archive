## Ingredients

- A piece of sturgeon weighing at least 500 grams
- Lardons of cured pork fat, seasoned with salt and pepper
- Oil
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Broth
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Take a large piece of sturgeon weighing at least 500 grams, skin it and lard it with lardons of cured pork fat seasoned beforehand with salt and pepper.
2. Tie it crosswise, dust it with flour, place it on the fire with oil and butter and season it again with salt and pepper.
3. When it is browned on all sides, baste it with broth to finish cooking.
4. Before serving, squeeze lemon juice over it and serve it with its own sauce.
