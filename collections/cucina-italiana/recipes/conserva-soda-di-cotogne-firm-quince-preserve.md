## Ingredients

- Quinces, cleaned of skin and core, 800 grams
- Fine white sugar, 500 grams
- Water, half a glass and one glass

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar on the heat with half a glass of water.
2. Boil it a little and set it aside.
3. Cut the quinces into very thin slices.
4. Put them on the heat with one glass of water in a copper pan.
5. Keep them covered but stir often, trying to chop and crush them with a spoon.
6. When they become tender from cooking, add the prepared sugar syrup.
7. Stir often and let boil uncovered until the preserve is done, which you will know when it begins to fall in shreds taken up on a spoon.
