## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Savoiardi, 100 grams
- Malaga raisins, 80 grams
- Sultana raisins, 50 grams
- Apricot preserve, 50 grams
- Quince preserve, 50 grams
- Candied fruit, 20 grams
- Kirsch, half a deciliter
- Egg yolks, 6
- Egg whites, 4

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with sugar inside for half an hour
2. Remove seeds from the malaga raisins; cut the candied fruit into small dice and do the same with the preserves if they are firm, which in this case would be better
3. Flame the raisins and candied fruit with rum as in the Biscotto alla sultana No. 574
4. After the milk has boiled, let it cool and then add the beaten eggs and kirsch
5. Take a smooth cylindrical mold, grease it completely with cold butter and fill it in the following way: cover the bottom with a layer of the aforementioned fruit and over it spread a layer of savoiardi, then more fruit and preserves, then more savoiardi and so on until you run out of ingredients
6. Finally pour the milk prepared as described slowly and gradually into the mold
7. Cook the pudding in a water bath and serve it hot
