## Ingredients

- Cardoons
- butter
- salt
- milk or cream
- béchamel sauce (N. 137)
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Discard the hardest ribs, clean the others from filaments and boil them until half cooked.
2. Cut the cardoon ribs into pieces about three fingers long and cook them to taste with butter and sufficient salt, finish cooking them by adding milk, or better cream, then bind them with a little béchamel sauce N. 137.
3. Add a pinch of grated Parmesan and remove them immediately without letting them boil again.
4. This is an excellent side dish to braised meats, bracioles, stewed giblets and other similar dishes.
