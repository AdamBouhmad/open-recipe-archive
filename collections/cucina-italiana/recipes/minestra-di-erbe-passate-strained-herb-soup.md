## Ingredients

- 1 bunch beet greens
- 1 bunch spinach
- 1 head of lettuce
- 1 wedge of cabbage
- 1/4 onion
- parsley
- celery
- carrot
- basil or dill
- butter
- tomatoes
- 1 potato
- salt
- pepper
- water
- rice or bread for soup
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Remove the coarse ribs from the beet greens, cut all the greens roughly and soak them in fresh water for several hours.
2. Make a mixture with a quarter onion and all the herbs (parsley, celery, carrot and some basil or dill leaves); put it in a pan with a piece of butter and when well colored, add the herbs (drained somewhat), together with some tomatoes cut into pieces and a potato sliced.
3. Season with salt and pepper and let boil, stirring often.
4. When the greens are wilted, add hot water and cook until completely disintegrated.
5. Pass through a strainer, keeping the strained liquid for cooking rice or for wetting soup.
6. Before serving, taste it and add seasoning, especially butter, which will almost always be necessary.
7. Serve with Parmesan cheese on the side, but avoid making it too thick so it does not resemble plaster.
