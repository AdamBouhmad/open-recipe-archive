## Ingredients

- Transparent gobies, 300-350 grams
- oil
- garlic, 1-2 cloves
- sage leaves
- salt and pepper
- water
- eggs, 2
- Parmesan cheese, 2 spoonfuls
- breadcrumbs, 1 spoonful
- lemon, half

## Directions

1. Wash the gobies several times until they no longer produce foam.
2. Drain them on a sieve.
3. Heat oil with garlic cloves (slightly crushed) and sage leaves.
4. When the garlic is colored, add the gobies.
5. If still alive, cover with a lid so they do not jump away.
6. Season with salt and pepper, stir often with a spoon and moisten with a little water if they dry out too much.
7. When cooked, bind them with beaten eggs mixed separately with Parmesan, breadcrumbs, and lemon.
8. If serving in the cooking vessel, place between two fires at the end so they form a white crust on the surface.
