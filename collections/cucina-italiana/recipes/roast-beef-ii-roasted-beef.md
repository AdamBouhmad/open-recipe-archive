## Ingredients

- a piece of beef
- butter, softened
- salt
- charcoal fire

## Directions

1. Thread the beef onto a spit.
2. Wrap it in a sheet of white paper that is not too thin and well buttered with cold butter.
3. Tie it at both ends so it remains well sealed and place it over a very hot charcoal fire.
4. Turn it, and when it is almost cooked, tear away the paper.
5. Salt it and let it take on color.
6. Remove from the fire, close it between two plates and after ten minutes serve it.
