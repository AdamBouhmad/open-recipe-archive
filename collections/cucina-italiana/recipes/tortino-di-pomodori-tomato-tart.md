## Ingredients

- tomatoes, cut into pieces
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- parmesan cheese
- croutons cut into almond shapes
- butter or lard
- nepitella or oregano (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the cut tomatoes in a soffritto of garlic, parsley and oil; season with salt and pepper.
2. When they are cooked so that their juice is condensed, pass it and return it to the fire with eggs in proportion, beaten beforehand.
3. Add a pinch of parmesan, mix and when the eggs are set, pour them into a serving dish and surround with croutons cut into almond shapes and fried in butter or lard.
4. A few leaves of nepitella or a pinch of oregano, after passing the juice, gives the tart a pleasant aroma.
