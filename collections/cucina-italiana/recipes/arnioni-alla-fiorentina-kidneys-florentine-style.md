## Ingredients

- kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Open and remove fat from the kidneys as in recipe N. 291 and split them in half lengthwise.
2. Place a pan on the heat with a piece of butter proportionate to the amount and when it is about to boil, place the kidney in it, leaving it for a moment, then remove it from the heat and season with salt, pepper and a pinch of chopped parsley.
3. Coat it well in the seasoning and, after several hours, cook it in the same pan, or on a grill, coated in bread crumbs.
