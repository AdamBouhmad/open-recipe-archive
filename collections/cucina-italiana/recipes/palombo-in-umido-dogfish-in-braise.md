## Ingredients

- Dogfish
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Onion
- Oil
- Salt and pepper
- Red or dry white wine
- Tomato juice or tomato paste

## Directions

1. Cut the dogfish into fairly large pieces
2. Make a battute with garlic, parsley and very little onion
3. Put it in a pan with oil and when it has fried sufficiently, place the dogfish in it and season with salt and pepper
4. Once browned, add some red or dry white wine and tomato juice or tomato paste to complete the cooking
