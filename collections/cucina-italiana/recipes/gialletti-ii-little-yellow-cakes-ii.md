## Ingredients

- Cornmeal, 300 grams
- Wheat flour, 150 grams
- Powdered sugar, 200 grams
- Butter, 150 grams
- Lard, 70 grams
- Raisins, 100 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Holding them to the thickness of half a finger, you will make about twenty of these; but you can also give them whatever shape you prefer and instead of 20, keeping them small, make 40
2. Bake them as the previous ones and to knead them follow the directions as if making short crust pastry
