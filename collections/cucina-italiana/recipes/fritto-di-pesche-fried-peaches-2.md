## Ingredients

- Pesche burrone (white peaches), not too ripe
- Pastella (batter) N. 156
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take white peaches not too ripe and cut them into wedges not too large
2. Like the apples and fennel, wrap them in batter N. 156 and dust them with powdered sugar after cooking
3. It is not necessary to peel them
