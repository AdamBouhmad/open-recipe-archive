## Ingredients

- one young chicken breast (cleaned of organs, neck, and legs), approximately 600 grams raw
- Tuna in oil, 50 grams
- Capers squeezed of vinegar, 30 grams
- Anchovies, 3
- Parsley, one handful (enough to give green color to the sauce)
- four tablespoons of oil
- half tablespoon of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- two hard-boiled eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken breast in water for half an hour until cooked through.
2. Remove the skin from the cooked chicken, debone it completely, and cut it into pieces.
3. Season the chicken pieces with salt (sparingly), pepper, and two tablespoons of oil.
4. Let the chicken sit on a platter for several hours.
5. Clean the anchovies of scales and bones. Finely chop the parsley with a mincing knife.
6. Pound the anchovies, parsley, tuna, and capers together in a mortar until the mixture is very fine.
7. Transfer the sauce to a bowl and dilute it with four tablespoons of oil and half a tablespoon of vinegar.
8. Coat half of the sauce over the chicken pieces and cover with the remaining half.
9. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut into wedges around the chicken when serving.
10. Serves six people.
