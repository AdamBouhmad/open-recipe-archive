## Ingredients

- One kilogram of lean beef meat, including bone or joint
- 400 grams of beef cut into thin slices
- 1.5 liters of water
- Thin slices of lard and prosciutto
- Small pieces of butter
- One onion
- Salt
- One clove or nine to ten crushed peppercorns
- One teaspoon sugar
- One carrot cut into slices
- A bouquet garni composed of parsley, celery and other aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. From one kilogram of lean beef meat (including bone), take 400 grams cut into thin slices
2. With the rest, make broth as usual with 1.5 liters of water
3. Cover the bottom of a pot with slices of lard and prosciutto and some small pieces of butter
4. Slice an onion and place the thin slices of beef arranged on top
5. When the meat has taken on color over high heat from below, wet it with a ladle of the said broth, then turn it so it also colors on the other side, and after pour another ladle of broth
6. Then season with salt, one clove or nine to ten crushed peppercorns, and one teaspoon of sugar
7. Pour now all the rest of the broth, add a carrot cut into slices and a decorated bouquet that can be composed of parsley, celery and some other aromatic herb
8. Boil gently for about two hours, then remove the thin slices of meat, strain the gravy and remove the fat
9. With this you can wet soup and use it to flavor vegetables, or, thickening it with a mixture of potato flour and butter, dress dry soups
