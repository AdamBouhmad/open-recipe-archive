## Ingredients

- tomatoes, cut into pieces
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- salt and pepper
- eggs, in proportion
- Parmesan cheese, a pinch

## Directions

1. Boil the tomatoes cut into pieces in a soffritto of garlic, parsley and oil with salt and pepper for seasoning
2. When they are cooked so that their juice has condensed, pass it and return it to the fire with eggs in proportion, beaten beforehand
3. Add a pinch of Parmesan, mix and when the eggs are set, pour them out
