## Ingredients

- Beef, a piece of short and thick meat weighing not less than half a kilogram
- Cured meat
- Onion
- Celery
- Carrot
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Tomato juice or conserve
- Broth
- Dried mushrooms

## Directions

1. Sometimes to eat the boiled meat more willingly, one uses to reheat it in a stew; but then wait to have a short and thick piece of meat, weighing not less than half a kilogram. Remove it from the broth before it is cooked all the way and put it in a pot on top of a finely chopped mixture of cured meat, onion, celery, carrot and a piece of butter, seasoning it with salt, pepper and spices. When the mixture is rendered, finish cooking the meat with tomato juice or conserve dissolved in broth. Strain the sauce, degrease it and put it back on the fire with the piece of meat and with a handful of dried mushrooms softened.
