## Ingredients

- Leftover boiled fine fish
- Béchamel sauce
- Salt
- Grated Parmesan
- Truffles (or dried mushrooms if truffles are unavailable)
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into small pieces and clean it well of bones and spines.
2. Put it in the béchamel sauce and season it with sufficient salt, grated Parmesan, and finely cut truffles, or if lacking these, use a pinch of rehydrated dried mushrooms.
3. Take a heat-proof dish, butter it, and sprinkle it with breadcrumbs.
4. Pour the mixture into it and cover it with a thin layer of breadcrumbs as well.
5. Finally, place a small piece of butter on top in the center, brown it in the oven, and serve it hot.
