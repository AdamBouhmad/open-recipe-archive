## Ingredients

- lean braciuola of veal weighing 300 to 400 grams
- three or four eggs
- salt
- pepper
- Parmesan cheese
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Take a lean braciuola of veal weighing 300 to 400 grams, pound it and flatten it well.
2. Beat three or four eggs, season them with salt and pepper, a pinch of Parmesan, some chopped parsley and fry them in butter in the form of a frittata, approximately the width of the braciuola on which you will spread it, trimming where it overlaps and placing the trimmings where it is lacking.
3. After this, roll the braciuola together with the frittata tightly and tie it; then dust it with flour and place it in a pot with butter, seasoning it with salt and pepper.
4. When it has been well browned on all sides, moisten it with broth to finish cooking it and serve it with its own sauce, which because of the flour will be somewhat thick.
