## Ingredients

- Currants
- White fine sugar, 80 parts per 100 parts of juice

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from the currants through a cloth.
2. Put it on the heat without allowing it to ferment.
3. Add white fine sugar in the proportion of 80 parts sugar per 100 parts juice.
4. Boil, not too much, until it condenses into the form of a jelly.
5. Conserve in jars like preserves.
