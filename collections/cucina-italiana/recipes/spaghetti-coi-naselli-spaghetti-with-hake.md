## Ingredients

- Spaghetti, 500 grams
- Hake (cod), 300 grams
- Butter, 60 grams
- Oil, 4 spoonfuls
- Marsala, 4 spoonfuls
- Scent of nutmeg
- One onion of medium size
- Salt and pepper
- Tomato juice or conserve dissolved in water
- Parmesan

## Directions

1. Chop a medium-sized onion and squeeze it between your hands to remove its bitterness
2. Put it on the fire with the oil and when it begins to brown, throw in the hake cut into pieces and season them with salt and pepper
3. Once browned, pour in tomato juice or conserve dissolved in water to cook them, and then pass them through a wire sieve, wetting them with a little hot water if needed, to extract all the pulp
4. Put the passed mixture back on the fire with the butter, Marsala, nutmeg and when it has come to a boil, if the sauce does not need to be reduced to the right consistency, dress the spaghetti cooked in salted water with this sauce and Parmesan
5. This is a portion for five people and is a pasta course that will please because it is not a jumble as the description might suggest
