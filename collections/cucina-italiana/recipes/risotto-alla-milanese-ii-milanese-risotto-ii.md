## Ingredients

- Rice, 500 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Beef marrow, 40 grams
- Half an onion
- Good white wine, two-thirds of a glass
- Saffron, as needed
- Parmesan cheese, as needed
- Broth

## Directions

1. Chop the onion and put it on the fire with the marrow and half the butter
2. When well browned, pour in the rice and after a few minutes add the wine and cook it with broth
3. Before removing from the fire, season it with the other half of the butter and with Parmesan cheese and send it to the table with more Parmesan on the side
