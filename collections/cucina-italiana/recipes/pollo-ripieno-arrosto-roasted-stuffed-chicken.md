## Ingredients

- One medium-sized chicken
- Two sausages
- The chicken's liver, crest and wattles
- Eight or ten well-roasted chestnuts
- One ball of truffles or, in their absence, some pieces of dried mushrooms
- The aroma of nutmeg
- One egg
- Bread crumb
- Broth
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Start by giving the sausages and giblets half-cooking in butter, moistening them with a little broth if needed; season with a little salt and a little pepper because of the sausages.
2. Remove them dry and in the remaining liquid throw a bread crumb to obtain, with a little broth, two spoonfuls of thick paste.
3. Skin the sausages, mince with the mezzaluna the giblets and the softened mushrooms, and together with the burnt pieces, the egg and the paste, pound everything very finely in a mortar, except the truffles which should be cut into slices and left raw.
4. This is the mixture with which you will fill the chicken, whose filling will be cut better when cold than hot and will also be more pleasing to the taste.
5. If it were a turkey instead of a chicken, double the dose.
