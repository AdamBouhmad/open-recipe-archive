## Ingredients

- One mullet (or baldigara), weighing at least one kilogram
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Carrot
- Celery
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tomato juice
- Water
- Dried mushrooms
- Bread for toasting
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Scrape the scales from the mullet, clean it, and boil it with water in proportion.
2. Make a generous battuto with onion, garlic, parsley, carrot, and celery.
3. Put this mixture on the fire with oil, salt, and pepper.
4. When it has taken color, stop it with tomato juice and let it boil with the fish broth.
5. Strain this broth and with a little of the same broth cook a small quantity of celery, carrot, and dried mushrooms, all cut into small pieces, which serve to give flavor.
6. Toast the bread for the soup and cut it into dice.
7. Put the bread in the soup tureen and pour the hot broth over it along with the said flavoring vegetables.
8. Serve at the table with Parmesan cheese on the side.
