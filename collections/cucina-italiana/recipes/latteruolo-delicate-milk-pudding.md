## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Egg yolks, 8
- Egg whites, 2
- Vanilla or coriander seed flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with sugar for an hour and also an hour and a quarter if you are not quite sure of its quality.
2. If for flavoring you use coriander seeds, use them as indicated in the previous recipe.
3. In the milk, break the surface skin with a spoon from time to time, pass it through a strainer for extra precaution, and when it is cold, mix it well with the beaten eggs.
4. Prepare a pan lined with pasta matta No. 153, arrange it as in the polenta of Romagna No. 702, pour the mixture into it, cook it with moderate heat below and above and so that it does not brown on top, cover it with paper greased with butter.
5. Wait until it is well cold before cutting it into almond shapes with the dough underneath like the said polenta.
