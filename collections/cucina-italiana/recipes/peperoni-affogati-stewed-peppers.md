## Ingredients

- Bell peppers
- Oil
- Butter
- Garlic
- Tomato juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Briefly scald the peppers in a pan with a little oil so they can be peeled.
2. After peeling, put a finely chopped garlic clove in a pan with oil and butter and when it is browned, add the peppers.
3. Salt them, let them draw out flavor briefly, and add tomato juice.
4. Do not cook them too much as they would lose their piquancy, which is what gives them grace.
5. Serve them with boiled meat.
