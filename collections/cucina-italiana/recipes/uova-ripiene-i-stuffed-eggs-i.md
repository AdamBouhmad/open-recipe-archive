## Ingredients

- Eggs, hard-boiled
- Anchovies
- Parsley
- Onion
- Butter
- Mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. After hard-boiling the eggs, cut them in half lengthwise and extract the yolks.
2. Take one anchovy for every two eggs, wash them, remove the bone, and chop them finely with a little parsley and very little onion.
3. Mix in the yolks and enough butter so that you can knead everything together with a knife blade.
4. Fill the cavities left by the yolks with this mixture and arrange the stuffed egg halves neatly on a platter.
5. Cover them with mayonnaise sauce (recipe N. 126).
6. They can also be eaten simply seasoned with salt, pepper, oil and vinegar, and they are quite palatable; it also seems that the stomach is not offended by them.
