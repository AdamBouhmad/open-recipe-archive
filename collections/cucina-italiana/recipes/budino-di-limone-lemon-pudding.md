## Ingredients

- One large garden lemon
- Sugar, 170 grams
- Sweet almonds with three bitter almonds, 170 grams
- Eggs, 6
- One teaspoon of rum or cognac

## Directions

1. Cook the lemon in water, for which two hours will be sufficient
2. Remove it dry and pass it through a sieve
3. However, before passing it taste it, because if it tasted too bitter you would need to keep it in fresh water until it had lost that unpleasant flavor
4. Add to it the sugar, the blanched and very finely pounded almonds, the six egg yolks and the rum
5. Mix everything well, beat the six egg whites and add them to the mixture which you will pour into a mold to cook it in the stove or in the oven
6. It can be served either hot or cold
