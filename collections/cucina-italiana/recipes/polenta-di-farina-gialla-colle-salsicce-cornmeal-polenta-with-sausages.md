## Ingredients

- Corn flour
- Sausages
- Water
- Tomato sauce or tomato preserve
- Parmesan cheese
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a rather soft polenta from cornmeal, spread it on a work surface to the thickness of a finger, and cut it into almond shapes.
2. Place several whole sausages in a small pan with a drop of water and when cooked, peel them, crumble them, and add tomato sauce or tomato preserve.
3. Place the polenta in a baking dish or tray that can withstand fire.
4. Season it layer by layer with Parmesan, the sausages, and some small pieces of butter scattered here and there.
5. Then place it between two fires and when well heated, serve it, especially as a first course for a fork breakfast. The said polenta can also be made firm to cut into slices.
