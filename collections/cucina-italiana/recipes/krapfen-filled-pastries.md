## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eggs
- Butter, melted
- Sugar
- Salt
- Egg yolk, minced filling with gravy and béchamel
- Chicken livers
- Sweetbreads
- Prosciutto
- Salted tongue
- Truffles or mushrooms
- Water
- Lard or oil for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the remaining ingredients with eggs, melted butter, sugar, and salt
2. If the dough is too soft, add enough flour so it can be rolled out to the thickness of half a finger
3. Cut discs using a tin circle of the designated size
4. Make 24 discs total
5. Using an egg or similar tool, press the point in the middle of each disc to make an indentation
6. On 12 of the discs, place a teaspoon of the filling made from minced liver, sweetbreads, prosciutto, salted tongue, flavored with truffles or mushrooms, all cut into small dice, combined with gravy and béchamel
7. Wet the edges of the discs with a finger dipped in water
8. Cover each filled disc with one of the remaining 12 empty discs
9. Press a tin circle of equal size on top to form an indentation all around
10. Let them rise with gentle heat, by the fire or in a stove
11. When well puffed, fry them in lard or oil until covered by the fat
12. Serve hot as a fried dish or intermediate course
13. For a sweet version, fill with firm cream or fruit preserve and dust with powdered sugar after cooking
