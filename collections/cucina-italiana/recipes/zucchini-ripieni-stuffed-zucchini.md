## Ingredients

- Zucchini
- Lean veal, cut into pieces
- Onion, parsley, celery, carrot, finely chopped
- Salt pork, finely cut
- Oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Water
- Egg
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Bread crumb, boiled in broth or milk
- Spices
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the zucchini whole and hollow them out using a tin tube passed from bottom to top; if the hollow does not seem large enough, widen it with a thin knife.
2. For the filling, take lean veal, cut it into pieces and put it on the heat in a pan with a finely chopped mixture of onion, parsley, celery, carrot, a little salt pork cut into small pieces, a little oil, salt and pepper.
3. Turn it frequently with a spoon and when the meat has drawn in all moisture and taken color, pour in a ladleful of water; after it has drawn in this, pour in another and after a little another still, to finish cooking it, noting that some sauce remains.
4. Pass this sauce and set it aside.
5. Finely mince the dry meat with a crescent cutter, and with an egg, a little grated Parmesan cheese, a bread crumb boiled in broth or milk and the aroma of spices, make all into a mixture and use it for filling.
6. With the zucchini thus prepared, put them to brown in butter which you will first brown to a hazelnut color and lastly cook them through with the sauce already set aside.
