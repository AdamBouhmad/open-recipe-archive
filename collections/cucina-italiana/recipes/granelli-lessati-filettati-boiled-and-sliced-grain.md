## Ingredients

- Grain (from recipe N. 174)
- Egg yolk, 1
- Flour, 1/2 teaspoon
- Lemon, 1/2
- Broth, as needed

## Directions

1. Boil the grain and cut it into fillets.
2. Cook it with the egg yolk, flour and lemon in the manner described for the main preparation.
3. Add more broth if needed to make it more fluid.
4. Serve it.
