## Ingredients

- Fine white sugar, 650 grams
- Water, 360 grams
- Wine spirit at 36 degrees, 250 grams
- Saffron, a pinch
- Orange, 1

## Directions

1. Remove with a knife the superficial peel of the orange and place it in the spirit with the saffron, in a vessel covered with perforated paper, leaving it there for three days.
2. Pour the sugar into water in another vessel, stirring it from time to time so it dissolves well, and on the fourth day mix the two liquids together and leave them at rest for another eight days.
3. At the end of these, pass the liqueur through cloth, filter it through paper or cotton and bottle it.
