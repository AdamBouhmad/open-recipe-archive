## Ingredients

- Onion
- Butter
- Lean veal of milk-fed calf
- Salt
- Pepper
- Marsala wine
- Broth
- Egg yolk
- Wafers (ostie)
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Put on the fire a small mixture of chopped onion and butter, and when it is well colored cook a piece of lean veal of milk-fed calf in it, seasoning with salt and pepper
2. When the meat is browned, wet it with Marsala wine to complete the cooking
3. Pound it in a mortar and to soften it somewhat use the cooking liquid left over, and if this is not enough add a drop of broth and lastly an egg yolk; but mind that the mixture must remain rather firm
4. Now take some wafers, that is, thin pastry sheets, and cut them into small squares similar to those used by pharmacists for small lint pads
