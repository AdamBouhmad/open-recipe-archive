## Ingredients

- green beans
- soda
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- béchamel sauce made with cream, butter and flour
- bread fried and cut into almond shapes

## Directions

1. Boil the green beans with soda so they remain a nice green color
2. Sauté them lightly in butter so they do not lose their color
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Pour a flowing béchamel sauce over them, not too abundant, made with cream, butter and flour
5. Serve with a border of fried bread cut into almond shapes
6. They can be served as an intermediate course in a meal
