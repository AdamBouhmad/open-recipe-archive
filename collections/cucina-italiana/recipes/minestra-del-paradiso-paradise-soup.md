## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons fine breadcrumbs from hard bread
- 4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Whip the egg whites until stiff, then incorporate the yolks
2. Add to this mixture four tablespoons not quite heaping of fine breadcrumbs from hard bread, the same amount of grated Parmesan cheese, and the aroma of nutmeg
3. Mix gently so that the mixture remains light and drop it into boiling broth by teaspoons
4. Boil for seven or eight minutes and serve
5. This amount is sufficient for six people
