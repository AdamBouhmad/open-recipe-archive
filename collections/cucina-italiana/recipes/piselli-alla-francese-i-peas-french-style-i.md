## Ingredients

- Fresh peas, 1 liter
- Young onions, 2
- Parsley stems
- Butter, 30 grams at first, then another 30 grams
- Broth, as needed
- Lettuce, 2 whole heads
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour, 1 tablespoon not heaping
- Egg yolks, 2

## Directions

1. Take two young onions, cut them in half lengthwise, enclose some parsley stems in the middle and tie them.
2. Put them on the fire with 30 grams of butter and when browned, pour over them a good ladleful of broth.
3. Let boil and when the onions are very soft, strain them, squeezing them together with the juice, which you will return to the fire with the peas and two whole heads of lettuce.
4. Season with salt and pepper and let them boil slowly.
5. At the halfway point of cooking add another 30 grams of butter mixed with one tablespoon not heaping of flour and pour broth if needed.
6. Before serving, bind them with two egg yolks dissolved in a little broth. In this way they turn out very delicate.
