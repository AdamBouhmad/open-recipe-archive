## Ingredients

- Flour, 200 grams
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Sweet almonds, 100 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Lard, 30 grams
- One whole egg and one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the sun or by the fire, toast them to a hazelnut color and chop them to the thickness of a grain of rice
2. Mix these with the sugar into the flour
3. Make a well in the mass to place the remaining ingredients and knead it working as little as possible
4. Let it rest for a few hours in the form of a round loaf
5. Lightly flour the work surface and roll out the loaf first with a smooth rolling pin then with a ridged one, to a thickness slightly less than one centimeter
6. Cut with a disc or similar tool to obtain about 50 small pastries
7. Bake in a countryside oven after placing them on a baking pan greased lightly with cold butter
