## Ingredients

- sweetbreads, 150 grams
- onion and butter or gravy
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- béchamel, 2 tablespoons
- egg yolk, 1
- parmesan cheese, a good pinch
- breadcrumbs
- egg, 1 beaten
- optional: fat and lean ham, salted tongue, truffles

## Directions

1. Take 150 grams of sweetbreads and cook them in gravy or with a small mixture of onion and butter
2. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Cut them into small dice and mix with two tablespoons of rather firm béchamel, adding one egg yolk and a good pinch of parmesan
4. Take the mixture up with small spoons and pour it into breadcrumbs, shaping it into an oblong form like a spool
5. Dip them in beaten egg, then coat again in breadcrumbs and fry them
6. You can make them more tasty if you add fat and lean ham, salted tongue cut into small dice to the mixture, and if instead of nutmeg you flavor them with small pieces of truffles
7. With the stated quantity of sweetbreads you will get ten or twelve croquettes which you can add to other types of fried foods to make a mixed fry dish
