## Ingredients

- Flavorful and ripe apricots, weighed with the pit, 300 grams
- Fine white sugar, 200 grams
- Water, half a liter
- One garden lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in the water for 10 minutes
2. When cold, add the apricot pulp passed through a sieve and the lemon juice
3. Pass the mixture again before putting it in the sorbet maker
4. This is an abundant quantity for four people
