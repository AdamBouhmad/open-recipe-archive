## Ingredients

- Pork brains, 3 (about 400 grams total)
- Eggs, 2 and 1 yolk
- Heavy cream, 240 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 50 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Nutmeg
- Salt, to taste

## Directions

1. Heat the brains with the butter, salt them, and cook, stirring often to prevent sticking, without browning them. Pass them through a sieve.
2. Add the Parmesan, nutmeg, beaten eggs, and cream. Mix well.
3. Pour the mixture into a smooth mold buttered with cold butter and cook in a bain-marie.
4. This is almost better cold than hot and serves six people.
