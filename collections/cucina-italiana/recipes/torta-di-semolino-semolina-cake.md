## Ingredients

- Milk, 1 liter
- Fine semolina, 130 grams
- Sugar, 130 grams
- Sweet almonds with three bitter almonds, 100 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Zest of 1 lemon
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds in hot water and pound them very finely in a mortar with all the sugar, which you will add one spoonful at a time
2. Cook the semolina in the milk and before removing it from the fire add the butter and almonds, which, being mixed with the sugar, dissolve easily
3. Salt it and wait until it is lukewarm to add the eggs beaten separately
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered baking pan, sprinkled with breadcrumbs and of such size that the cake turns out one and a half fingers high or at most two
5. Bake it in the oven or in a country oven, unmold it when cold and serve it whole or cut into almond-shaped pieces
