## Ingredients

- Veal shank bones, one per person
- Onion
- Celery
- Carrot
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Water
- Tomato juice or conserve
- Lemon rind
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Put as many pieces of bone on the fire as there are people who will eat it, on top of a raw and chopped mixture of onion, celery, carrot and a piece of butter; season it with salt and pepper. When it has taken flavor add another small piece of butter dipped in flour to give it color and to bind the gravy and cook it through with water and tomato juice or conserve. Pass the gravy, degrease it and put back on the fire, give it flavor with lemon rind cut into small pieces, adding a pinch of chopped parsley before removing it from the fire.
