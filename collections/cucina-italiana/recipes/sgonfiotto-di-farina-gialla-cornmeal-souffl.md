## Ingredients

- Milk, half a liter
- Cornmeal, 170 grams
- Sugar, 30 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Egg whites, 6
- Egg yolks, 3
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Make a farinata: that is, pour the cornmeal into boiling milk, or better, if you wish to preserve it from lumps, first dilute the meal with a little cold milk and pour it into the boiling milk mixing well
2. Let it boil briefly, and when you remove it from the fire, add the butter, sugar and salt
3. When it has cooled, break the egg yolks into it and lastly pour in the well-beaten egg whites; mix gently and pour the mixture into a smooth mold or pan that you have buttered and dusted with wheat flour
4. Cook it in an oven with heat below and above and when it has risen, serve it immediately so that, if possible, it remains well fluffy and does not fall
5. It is better, in my opinion, to make this dish in a heat-resistant pan and bring it to the table without moving it
6. This dose will serve six people
