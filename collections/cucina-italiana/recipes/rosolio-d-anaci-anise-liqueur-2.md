## Ingredients

- Anise from Romagna, 50 grams
- White fine powdered sugar, 800 grams
- Rainwater or spring water, 1 liter
- Strong spirit, 8 deciliters

## Directions

1. Make it in the same manner as the preceding recipe.
2. Make the infusion with 50 grams of anise from Romagna instead of lemon peel, and I specify from Romagna.
