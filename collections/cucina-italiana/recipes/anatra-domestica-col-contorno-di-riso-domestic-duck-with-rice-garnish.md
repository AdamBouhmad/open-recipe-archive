## Ingredients

- domestic duck
- 1/4 of a large onion
- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- 50 grams fat and lean prosciutto
- 2 tablespoons oil
- salt
- pepper
- tomato sauce or tomato paste
- water
- a pinch of dried mushrooms
- 200 grams rice
- 40 grams butter
- hot water
- Parmigiano cheese

## Directions

1. Make a mixture with a quarter of a large onion and all the herbs, that is parsley, carrot and celery chopped together with 50 grams of fat and lean prosciutto.
2. Put it on the fire with two tablespoons of oil and the duck on top, seasoned with salt and pepper.
3. Once browned, bathe it with tomato sauce or tomato paste and the necessary water to bring it to cook, adding at the same time a pinch of dried mushrooms to cook in that sauce which will then be strained through a sieve and defatted, keeping the mushrooms to add to the rice.
4. Put the rice, in the amount of 200 grams, raw as is, in a pot with 40 grams of butter and when it begins to take color pour hot water little by little, giving it flavor with the duck sauce and Parmigiano cheese when you are about to remove it from the fire.
