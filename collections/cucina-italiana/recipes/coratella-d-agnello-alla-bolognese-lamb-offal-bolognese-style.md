## Ingredients

- lamb liver
- lamb lungs (pasto)
- lard
- butter
- tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water or broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into thin slices and the lungs into pieces and throw them as is into a pan with lard.
2. When the offal is almost cooked, drain it from all the fat and add a piece of butter; continue frying.
3. Shortly after, pour tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water or broth into the pan.
4. Season with salt and pepper, serve it with this sauce, and be sure it will be praised.
