## Ingredients

- Rice, 500 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Parmesan cheese, as needed
- Tomato sauce from recipe No. 125
- Hot water

## Directions

1. Pour the rice onto the melted butter and when it has absorbed it begin to add hot water, little by little
2. When it reaches half cooking, give it flavor with the tomato sauce from No. 125
3. Before removing from the fire add a good handful of grated Parmesan cheese
4. In the said sauce, to season the risotto, you can substitute cured meat for the oil if you wish, or use the tomato juice described in No. 6
