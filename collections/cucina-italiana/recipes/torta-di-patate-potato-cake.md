## Ingredients

- Large floury potatoes, 700 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Sweet almonds with three bitter ones, 70 grams
- Eggs, 5
- Butter, 30 grams
- A pinch of salt
- Lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes (better if steamed), peel them and pass them through a sieve while still hot
2. Peel and pound the almonds very finely together with the sugar
3. Pour the almonds into the potatoes with the other ingredients, working everything together with a spoon for a full hour, adding the eggs one at a time and the melted butter
4. Pour the mixture into a baking pan greased with lard or butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs
5. Bake it in the oven and serve it cooled
