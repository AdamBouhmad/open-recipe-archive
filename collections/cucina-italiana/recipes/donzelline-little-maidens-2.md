## Ingredients

- flour
- water
- salt
- lard or oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough that is neither too stiff nor too soft
2. Work it very well with your hands on the work surface
3. Draw out a sheet the thickness of a shield
4. Cut it into small almond shapes
5. Fry it in lard or oil, and you will see it puff up, becoming tender and delicate to the taste
6. Dust with powdered sugar when no longer hot
