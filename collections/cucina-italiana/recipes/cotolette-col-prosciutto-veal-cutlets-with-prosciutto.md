## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Prosciutto, a very thin slice fat and lean
- Egg, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Parmesan or gruyere cheese, sliced very thin
- Meat juice
- Lemon juice
- Tomato juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the cutlets as in the previous recipe and place them in the egg with a very thin slice of prosciutto fat and lean the size of the cutlet itself.
2. Coat them with the prosciutto stuck on top, salt lightly and brown them in butter on the side without the prosciutto.
3. Over the prosciutto, instead of truffles, spread very thin slices of parmesan or gruyere.
4. Finish cooking them with heat on top and serve with meat juice and lemon juice, or with tomato juice.
