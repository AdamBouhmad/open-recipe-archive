## Ingredients

- Mullet
- salt
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the scales and gills from the fish but do not gut them as the entrails are the best part.
2. Season them with salt and pepper only and place them on the grill over a hot fire.
3. When cooked, place them between two hot plates away from the fire for five minutes.
4. At the moment of serving, turn the plates so the one on top goes underneath and the rendered fat remains spread over the fish.
5. Serve at table with lemon to squeeze over.
