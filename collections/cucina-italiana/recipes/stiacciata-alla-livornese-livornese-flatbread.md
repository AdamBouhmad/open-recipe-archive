## Ingredients

- Twelve eggs
- Finest flour, 1.8 kilograms
- Sugar, 600 grams
- Superfine oil, 200 grams
- Butter, 70 grams
- Baker's yeast, 30 grams
- Anise, 20 grams
- Vin Santo, 1.5 deciliters
- Marsala, 0.5 deciliter
- Orange blossom water, 1 deciliter

## Directions

1. Mix the two kinds of wine and infuse the anise in a little of this liquid after washing them well
2. Late in the evening perform the first operation: dissolve the baker's yeast in half a glass of lukewarm water, add enough flour to form a dough of proper consistency, place it on top of the flour mound inside a bowl, cover it with a layer of the same flour and keep the bowl protected from air and in the kitchen
3. In the morning when the dough is well risen, place it on the work surface, flatten it and remix it with one egg, one tablespoon of oil, one of sugar, one of wine and enough flour to form a larger dough again, mixing everything well without overworking it, and replace it on the flour and cover it as before
