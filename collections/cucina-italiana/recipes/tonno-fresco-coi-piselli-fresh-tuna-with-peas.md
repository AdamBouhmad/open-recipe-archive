## Ingredients

- Fresh tuna
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tomato juice or tomato paste
- Water
- Peas

## Directions

1. Cut the tuna into thick slices about half a finger thick.
2. Put it on heat over an abundant soffritto of garlic, parsley and oil.
3. When the garlic begins to take color, season with salt and pepper.
4. Turn the slices on both sides and halfway through cooking, add tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water.
5. When cooked, remove the tuna and cook the peas in its sauce.
6. Place the tuna back on top of the peas to reheat it and serve with this side dish.
