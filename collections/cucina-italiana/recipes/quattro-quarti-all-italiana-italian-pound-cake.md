## Ingredients

- 5 eggs and equal weight of sugar and flour (including eggshell weight)
- Butter, 200 grams
- Baking soda, one teaspoon
- Lemon zest for flavoring
- Sweet almonds with a few bitter ones, 100 grams

## Directions

1. Make this cake in the same manner as the previous one except substitute candied fruit with lemon zest flavoring, and substitute 100 grams of sweet almonds with some bitter almonds for the raisins
2. After blanching the almonds, dry them in the sun or by the fire, pound them finely with two tablespoons of the sugar from the recipe and mix them with the flour before adding them to the mixture
3. This cake needs to be worked a great deal, both before and after adding the butter
4. Keep the bowl immersed in hot water while working for better results
5. Bake it in the oven
