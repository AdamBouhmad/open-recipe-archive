## Ingredients

- Lamb's head
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Oil
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Tomato sauce or tomato paste

## Directions

1. Cut the head lengthwise into two natural pieces
2. Make a soffritto with garlic, parsley and oil
3. When it has taken color, stop it with a ladle of broth
4. Add the head and season with salt and pepper
5. At half-way through cooking, add a piece of butter and a little tomato sauce or paste
6. Finish cooking with more broth if needed
