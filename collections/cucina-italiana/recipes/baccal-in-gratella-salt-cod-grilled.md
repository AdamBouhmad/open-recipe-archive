## Ingredients

- salt cod
- white paper
- oil
- pepper
- rosemary sprigs, optional

## Directions

1. To keep it less dry, cook slowly over a sheet of white paper, oiled beforehand
2. Season with oil, pepper and, if desired, a few small sprigs of rosemary
