## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds, 150 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Potato flour, 50 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Oranges, 1 and 1/2

## Directions

1. First work the egg yolks with the sugar, add the flour, then the almonds peeled and pounded fine with a spoonful of the said sugar.
2. Add the juice passed from the oranges and the superficial skin scraped from only one orange.
3. Last unite to the mixture the whites beaten until stiff, pour it into a paper box greased with butter, about the thickness of a finger and a half and cook it in the oven at very moderate heat.
4. After it is cooked, cover it with a white crust as at No. 789.
