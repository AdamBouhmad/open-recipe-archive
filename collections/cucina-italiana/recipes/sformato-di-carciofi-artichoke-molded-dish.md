## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salted water
- eggs
- béchamel sauce
- butter
- parmesan cheese
- salt
- nutmeg
- meat broth or stracotto broth

## Directions

1. Remove the hardest leaves from the artichokes, trim the tips and peel the stems, keeping them all, even if they are long
2. Cut them into four wedges and boil them in salted water for only five minutes
3. Remove them dry, pound them in a mortar and pass them through a sieve
4. Measure the pulp thus obtained with all those usual ingredients in other vegetable molded dishes, that is: eggs, not being stingy of one more to make it set, two or three spoonfuls of béchamel where butter does not lack, parmesan cheese, salt and the aroma of nutmeg, but taste the mixture several times to bring it to the right flavor
5. If you have meat broth or stracotto broth, it is not bad to add a little of this too, and if the artichokes are tender, instead of passing them you can leave them in small wedges
6. Cook it in a water bath in a perforated mold if you have a meat sauce to fill it with; if not, put it in a smooth mold and serve it as a course between other dishes
