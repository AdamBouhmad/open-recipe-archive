## Ingredients

- Butter, 30 grams
- Chocolate, 100 grams, in small pieces
- Powdered sugar, 30 grams

## Directions

1. Put the butter and chopped chocolate on the fire and when well melted add the powdered sugar and spread the mixture on the cake when it is no longer boiling
