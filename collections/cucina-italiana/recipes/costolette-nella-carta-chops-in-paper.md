## Ingredients

- Milk veal chops
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Prosciutto, fat and lean
- Parsley
- Breadcrumb marrow
- Raw truffle slices
- Thick white paper
- Oil

## Directions

1. Take milk veal chops, denude the bone of the chop by removing the meat, cook them in butter sautéed and season them with salt and pepper.
2. Make a proportioned mixture of fat and lean prosciutto and parsley, chop it fine, add butter and breadcrumb marrow to hold it together and with this spread the chops on both sides, then decorate them with slices of raw truffles.
3. Cut thick white paper to the pattern of as many chops as there are, oil it with butter or oil on both sides and with it wrap them tightly with the bone of the chop outside.
4. Now place them on a grill on light fire, being careful that the paper does not burn and send them to the table, for more neatness, with the end of the chop wrapped in scalloped white paper.
5. Lamb chops can also be used for this purpose if they are large.
