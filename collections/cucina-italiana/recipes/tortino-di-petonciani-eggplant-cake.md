## Ingredients

- Seven or eight eggplants
- salt
- flour
- oil for frying
- grated Parmesan cheese
- tomato sauce (N. 125)
- one egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Peel seven or eight eggplants, cut them into round slices and salt them so they release their water.
2. After a few hours, flour them and fry them in oil.
3. Take a pan that can withstand fire and, layer by layer, season them with grated Parmesan and tomato sauce N. 125, arranging them so they form a nice mound.
4. Beat an egg with a pinch of salt, a spoonful of said sauce, a teaspoon of Parmesan, two of breadcrumbs, and cover the surface with this mixture.
5. Place the pan under the lid of the country oven, with fire on top, and when the egg has set, serve the cake to the table.
6. It can be served alone, as an intermediate course or accompanied by a meat dish.
