## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds, 140 grams
- Powdered sugar, 140 grams
- Grated or powdered chocolate, about 40 grams
- Maraschino liqueur, 4 tablespoons

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the sun or by the fire and pound them very finely with two tablespoons of the said sugar.
2. Pour them from the mortar into a vessel, add the remaining sugar and knead the mixture with the said liqueur.
3. Spread the chocolate on a work surface and form from the aforementioned mixture as many balls slightly larger than hazelnuts, of which you will obtain more than 30; roll them on the chocolate so they remain well covered, and you will be able to keep them for a long time.
