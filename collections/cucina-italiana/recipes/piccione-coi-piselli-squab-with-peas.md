## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Peas
- Onion
- Ham
- Oil
- Butter
- Water or broth

## Directions

1. They say the best way to cook squabs is stewed with peas. Cook them in a stew with a finely chopped mixture of onion, ham, oil and butter, placing the squabs on top, wetting them with water or broth when they have taken color on all sides to finish cooking them. Pass the gravy, degrease it and in the same cook the peas with which you will garnish the squabs when serving them to table.
