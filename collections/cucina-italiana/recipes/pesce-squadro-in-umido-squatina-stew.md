## Ingredients

- Pesce squadro (squatina), about 600 grams
- Parsley
- Carrot
- Celery
- Garlic
- Onion
- Oil
- Tomato sauce or tomato conserve
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Make a finely minced mixture with a good pinch of parsley, half a carrot, a piece of celery, half a garlic clove, and onion the size of a large walnut (if the fish is about 600 grams).
2. Put the mixture on the fire with proportional oil and when it is browned, stop it with tomato sauce or conserve dissolved in half a glass of water.
3. Season with salt and pepper and place the piece of fish on top, preferably from the tail part, which is very thick.
4. Cook it slowly, and when it has reached two-thirds of cooking, add a small piece of butter well coated with flour to bind the sauce and give it a more delicate taste.
5. Finish cooking it.
