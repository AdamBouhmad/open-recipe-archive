## Ingredients

- artichokes
- tender artichoke leaves from the center
- prosciutto, more fat than lean, 50 grams
- onion, young, one quarter
- garlic, a pinch
- celery leaves
- parsley leaves
- dried mushrooms, softened
- bread marrow from day-old bread, crumbled, a handful
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the stem at the base, remove the small outer leaves, and wash the artichokes.
2. Trim them as the previous ones and open their inner leaves so that you can cut out with a small knife the central choke, and once removed the hair if there is any in the center, keep only the tender small leaves to add to the filling.
3. For six artichokes, compose the filling from the aforementioned small leaves, 50 grams of prosciutto more fat than lean, one quarter of a young onion, garlic the size of a nail tip, a few celery and parsley leaves, a pinch of softened dried mushrooms, a handful of bread marrow from day-old bread reduced to crumbles, and a pinch of pepper.
4. First chop the prosciutto with a knife, then everything together with the crescent-shaped tool, and with this mixture fill the artichokes which you will season and cook as the previous ones.
