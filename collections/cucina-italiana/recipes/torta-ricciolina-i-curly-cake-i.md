## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds with some bitter ones, blanched, 120 grams
- Powdered sugar, 170 grams
- Candied fruit, 70 grams
- Butter, 60 grams
- Lemon zest
- Two eggs
- Flour

## Directions

1. Mix two eggs with flour and make thin noodles equal to the finest ones you would cook for broth soup.
2. On one corner of the work surface make a mound with the almonds, sugar, candied fruit cut into pieces, and scraped lemon zest, and with a pastry cutter and rolling pin flatten and chop this pile of ingredients until it becomes as fine as grains of wheat.
3. Take a copper pan and without oiling it, begin to spread a layer of noodles in the middle, if it is large, and season them with the ingredients described above, spread another layer of noodles and season them again, repeating the operation until the ingredients are finished, making sure the cake comes out round and at least two fingers thick.
4. When it is thus prepared, pour the liquefied butter over it using a brush to oil it well on the surface and so the butter penetrates equally in all parts.
5. Bake it in the oven or in the country oven; in fact, to save coal, the lid of this alone may suffice.
6. Dust it abundantly with powdered sugar when it is hot and serve it cold.
