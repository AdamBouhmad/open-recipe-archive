## Ingredients

- Zucchini, small ones of the same weight as the rice to be cooked
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Broth or water
- Tomato sauce (recipe N. 125), optional
- Parmesan

## Directions

1. Take small zucchini of the weight of the rice you will cook and cut them into chunks the size of hazelnuts
2. Put them to brown in butter, season with salt and pepper, and once just browned, throw them still firm into the rice when it will have reached half cooking, so they finish cooking together
3. The rice should remain somewhat brothy and the zucchini should not fall apart
4. Instead of broth you can use water and make it dry: but then give it flavor with tomato sauce N. 125, pour it also into the rice at half cooking, and with Parmesan
