## Ingredients

- venison chops from the loin, sliced thin
- oil
- butter
- one clove of garlic, whole
- sage leaves
- salt
- pepper
- marsala wine

## Directions

1. Heat oil and butter in proportion to the quantity you have to cook, add one whole clove of garlic and several sage leaves.
2. When the garlic has browned, place the chops on top, season with salt and pepper and cook them quickly over high heat, basting them with marsala wine.
