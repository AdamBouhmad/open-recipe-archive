## Ingredients

- About 500 grams of beef suitable for making gravy
- Some chicken necks
- Kitchen scraps if available
- Meat gravy
- Vegetables in good measure: celery, carrot, cabbage, sorrel, zucchini, peas, etc., according to the season
- One potato
- Butter
- Bread slices half a finger thick
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare a good meat gravy (see N. 5)
2. Cut the potato and zucchini into chunks, all the others into strips
3. Boil all the vegetables and then sauté them in butter, wetting them with the said gravy
4. Keep the bread slices half a finger thick, toast them and cut them into cubes
5. Take a pan or, better, a similar vessel, decent enough because it must be brought to table, and in this prepare the soup in the following manner: a layer of bread, one of vegetables and over it a sprinkling of Parmesan, and so on
6. Finally pour the gravy over it and, without touching it, cover it with a plate and a towel and keep it for half an hour in warmth near the fire before serving it
7. Note that this soup should remain almost dry, so it is well to hold back a little gravy to add when you send it to table, in case it turns out too dry
