## Ingredients

- stale bread, not too spongy
- slices of prosciutto, fat and lean
- truffle slices
- Gruyère cheese slices
- cold milk
- 1 egg, beaten
- breadcrumbs
- lard or oil for frying

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from stale bread and cut it into almond shapes or squares of about four centimeters per side, all of equal size.
2. On each piece lay first a slice of prosciutto, then slices of truffle, and on top a slice of Gruyère cheese.
3. Cover the filling with matching pieces of bread, pressing them together to hold them in place. Cut all components thin so the fried pieces are not too large.
4. Lightly moisten with cold milk and once it is absorbed, dip each piece in beaten egg, then in breadcrumbs, repeating the egg and breadcrumb coating twice so the edges are also golden and sealed.
5. Fry in lard or oil and serve alone or mixed with other fried dishes.
