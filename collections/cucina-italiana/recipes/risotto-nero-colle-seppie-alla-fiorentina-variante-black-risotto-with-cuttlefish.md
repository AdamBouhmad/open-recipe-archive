## Ingredients

- Cuttlefish
- Rice, 600 grams
- Onion, two small or one medium with two garlic cloves
- Oil, fine and abundant
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water
- Tomato juice or tomato preserve
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese
- Peas, optional

## Directions

1. Peel and clean the cuttlefish to remove useless parts which are the bone, the mouth apparatus, the eyes and the digestive tube.
2. Finely chop two small onions, or better one onion and two cloves of garlic, and put them on the fire in a pot with fine oil and in abundance.
3. When the soffritto has taken on a reddish color, throw in the cuttlefish and wait until these, boiling, begin to turn yellow.
4. Pour in 600 grams of rice and cook it with hot water and tomato juice or preserve, giving it more grace and flavor with a piece of butter.
5. When almost cooked, unite parmesan cheese.
6. If desired, add peas at two-thirds of cooking time as mentioned in the risotto with tench.
