## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon powder
- béchamel sauce (number 137)
- butter
- eggs
- parmesan cheese
- raisins or currants with seeds removed, or dried raisins

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach in very little water or only with the water that drips from it when you lift it from the fresh water where it was soaking
2. Pass it through a sieve and, gauging by the quantity, season it with salt, pepper, cinnamon powder, a few spoonfuls of béchamel, butter, eggs, parmesan cheese, a handful of raisins or currants from which the seeds have been removed, or dried raisins
3. Mix well and pour the mixture into a smooth mold with a hole in the middle, cooking it in a water bath
4. Unmold it while still hot and serve it filled with a delicate sauce of chicken giblets or of sweetbreads, or of milk-fed veal, or of all these things together, mixed with pieces of dried mushrooms
