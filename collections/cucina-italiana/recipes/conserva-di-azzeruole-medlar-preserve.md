## Ingredients

- White azzeruoles, 1 kilogram
- White sugar, 800 grams
- Water, 7 deciliters

## Directions

1. Throw the azzeruoles into boiling water with their stems attached.
2. Boil for ten minutes.
3. While still hot, with the point of a small knife remove the pits from the flower end.
4. If any lose their shape, reshape with your fingers.
5. Peel them without removing the stem.
6. Dissolve the sugar in the 7 deciliters of water (which can be the water in which they boiled).
7. Add the azzeruoles and when the syrup taken with a spoon begins to fall in drops, remove them with their liquid and preserve in jars.
8. They remain like candied fruit and are very good.
