## Ingredients

- Milk veal from the loin or rump, 500 grams without bone
- Cured meat, 30 grams, sliced very thin
- Butter, 30 grams
- Lemon, less than half, sliced thin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Warm milk, 1 glass

## Directions

1. Beat the veal, tie it so it stays gathered and place it in a pot as follows
2. Cover the bottom of the pot with very thin slices of cured meat and butter, and on top of this layer place less than half a lemon cut into four thin slices from which you will remove the peel and seeds
3. On top of these things place the veal to brown it well on all sides; but be careful that it does not take on any burnt color because of the little liquid found there
4. Having done this, drain the excess fat, season with salt and pepper and shortly after moisten it with a glass of warm milk, which you will have first boiled a little separately, but do not be alarmed if it curdles, as is likely
5. Cover the pot with double paper and, on low heat, cook the piece of meat to completion; when you are about to serve it, pass the gravy
6. This amount should suffice for four persons
