## Ingredients

- eggs, 6
- powdered sugar, 250 grams
- wheat flour, 100 grams
- potato flour, 50 grams
- butter, 30 grams
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Work for at least half an hour the egg yolks with the sugar and one tablespoon of the said flours, using a spoon. Beat the egg whites until very stiff and add them; mix gently and, when they are blended together, let the two flours fall from a small sieve, which you will have dried in the sun or over heat beforehand.
2. Bake it in the oven or country oven in a pan where it comes up about three fingers high, but first grease it with cold butter and dust it with powdered sugar mixed with flour.
