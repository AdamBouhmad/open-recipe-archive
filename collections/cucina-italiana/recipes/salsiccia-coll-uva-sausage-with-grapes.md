## Ingredients

- sausages
- lard or butter
- grapes

## Directions

1. Prick the sausages with the tip of a fork and place them whole in a pan with a little lard or butter
2. When they are cooked, add the grapes, not in large quantity, as whole berries and let it boil until they are half dissolved
