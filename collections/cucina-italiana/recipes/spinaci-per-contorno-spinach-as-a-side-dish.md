## Ingredients

- Spinach, boiled and chopped
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil spinach and chop finely with a crescent blade
2. Prepare in any of these ways:
3. Method 1: Cook with only butter, salt and pepper, adding a little meat sauce if available, or a few tablespoons of broth or cream
4. Method 2: Cook with a very small soffritto of finely chopped onion drawn with butter
5. Method 3: Cook with only butter, salt and pepper as the first, adding a pinch of Parmesan
6. Method 4: Cook with butter, the merest drop of oil and tomato sauce or tomato paste
