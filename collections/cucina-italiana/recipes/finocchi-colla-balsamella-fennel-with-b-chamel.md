## Ingredients

- finocchi polputi (fleshy fennel)
- burro (butter)
- latte (milk)
- sale (salt)
- parmigiano (Parmesan cheese)
- balsamella (béchamel sauce)

## Directions

1. Take fleshy fennel, clean them from hard leaves, cut them into small pieces, wash them and blanch them in salted water.
2. Put them to fry in butter and, when they have absorbed it, finish cooking them entirely with milk.
3. Taste them to see if they are well salted, then remove them dry and place them on a tray that can withstand fire.
4. Dust them with Parmesan and cover them with béchamel.
5. Brown them with fire from above and serve them with boiled meat or stew.
