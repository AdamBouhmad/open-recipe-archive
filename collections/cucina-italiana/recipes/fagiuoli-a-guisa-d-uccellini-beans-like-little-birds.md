## Ingredients

- shelled beans
- water
- oil
- several sage leaves
- salt
- pepper
- simple tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the beans first in water and drain them well
2. Put a pan on the heat with oil in proportion and several sage leaves
3. When the oil crackles strongly, throw in the beans and season with salt and pepper
4. Sauté them until they draw in the fat, shaking the pan from time to time to mix them
5. Pour a little simple tomato sauce over them
6. When the sauce has incorporated, remove from heat
7. These beans work very well as a side dish to boiled meat, if you don't want to eat them alone
