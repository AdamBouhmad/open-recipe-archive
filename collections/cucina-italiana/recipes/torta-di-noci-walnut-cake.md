## Ingredients

- Shelled walnuts, 140 grams
- Powdered sugar, 140 grams
- Chocolate in powder or grated, 140 grams
- Candied citron, 20 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Vanilla sugar flavoring

## Directions

1. Pound the walnuts finely in a mortar together with the sugar, then pour them into a bowl to add the chocolate, the vanilla flavoring, the eggs, placing first the yolks and then the whipped egg whites, and lastly the candied fruit chopped very finely
2. Take a pan where the cake reaches no more than two fingers high, grease it with butter and sprinkle it with breadcrumbs to bake it in the oven or in a country oven at moderate heat
