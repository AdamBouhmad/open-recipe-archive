## Ingredients

- cuttlefish
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- water
- peas

## Directions

1. Make a fairly generous mixture with onion, one clove of garlic and parsley. Put it on the fire with oil, salt and pepper, and when it has taken color pass it through a colander, squeezing well. In this soffritto add the cuttlefish cut into fillets, but first clean them as indicated elsewhere, moisten them with water if needed, and when they are almost cooked add the peas drained from the fresh water in which you had soaked them.
