## Ingredients

- Sugar, 100 grams
- Potato starch flour, 80 grams
- Milk, half a liter
- Eggs, 3 whole eggs plus 2 or 3 extra whites
- Vanilla flavoring or lemon zest

## Directions

1. Place the sugar and flour in a saucepan and pour in the cold milk little by little, stirring
2. Set the mixture on the fire so it thickens, stirring constantly, without worrying about making it boil
3. Add the vanilla or lemon zest, and when it becomes lukewarm, mix in the 3 egg yolks, then whip the whites and fold them in gently
4. Pour it into a metal baking dish and place it on the stovetop, cover it with the lid of a country oven between two fires and wait for it to puff up and brown lightly
5. Then dust it with powdered sugar and send it immediately to the table, where it will be praised for its delicacy; if any remains, you will find it is also good cold
