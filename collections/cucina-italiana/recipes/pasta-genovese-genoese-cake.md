## Ingredients

- Sugar, 200 grams
- Butter, 150 grams
- Potato flour, 170 grams
- Wheat flour, 110 grams
- Egg yolks, 12
- Egg whites, 7
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Work the egg yolks, butter, and sugar together very well in a bowl
2. Add the two flours and work for approximately half an hour
3. Pour the beaten egg whites into the mixture
4. Transfer the batter to a copper baking pan prepared as usual with a light coating of butter and flour
5. Keep it about the thickness of a finger, cut into almond shapes when baked, and dust with powdered sugar
