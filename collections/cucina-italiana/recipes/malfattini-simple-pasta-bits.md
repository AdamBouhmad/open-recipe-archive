## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eggs
- Parmesan cheese (optional)
- Spices (optional)
- Breadcrumbs (optional alternative)

## Directions

1. Mix flour with eggs and work with hands on a work surface to form a firm dough
2. Cut into thick slices about half a finger thick and let them dry in the air
3. Chop finely with a half-moon chopper until reduced to tiny pieces about half the size of a grain of rice
4. Pass through a sieve to obtain uniform pieces, or grate from whole bread
5. Keep pieces very small for easier digestion
6. Can alternatively be made with breadcrumbs, optionally enriched with a pinch of Parmesan cheese and a hint of spices
7. Can be combined with peas, chopped beets, or both
8. Add to boiling broth to serve
