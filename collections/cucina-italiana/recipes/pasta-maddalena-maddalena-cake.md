## Ingredients

- Sugar, 130 grams
- Fine flour, 80 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Egg yolks, 4
- Egg whites, 3
- A pinch of baking soda
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. First work the egg yolks with the sugar, and when they have become whitish, add the flour and work again for more than a quarter hour
2. Add to the mixture the melted butter if it is winter, and last the beaten egg whites
3. Dry the flour by the fire or in the sun if it is summer
4. You can give this paste different forms, but always keep it thin and of little volume
5. It is customary to put it in worked molds, greased with butter and floured, or in a pan to the thickness of barely a finger, cutting it afterwards into almond shapes which you will dust with powdered sugar
6. You can also make it half a finger thick and stick the almonds together two by two with fruit preserves
