## Ingredients

- Sugar, 250 grams
- Flour, 125 grams
- Sweet almonds, 125 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Cream of tartar, 15 grams
- Baking soda, 5 grams
- Egg yolks, 8
- Egg whites, 5
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the sun or by the fire and pound them very finely in a mortar with one of the said egg whites
2. Work the butter alone with a spoon, softening it a little in winter in a bain-marie, add the yolks one by one, then the sugar and work these things together for at least half an hour
3. Add the almonds to the mixture and stir again, then the four whipped egg whites and the flour let fall from a small sieve, mixing slowly
4. Lastly pour the powders that serve to make the cake softer and lighter and bake it in the oven in a pan, not too full, buttered with cold butter and dusted with powdered sugar and flour
