## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds, 125 grams
- Sugar, 125 grams
- Cognac, 4 spoonfuls
- Grated rye bread crust, 3 heaping spoonfuls
- Eggs, 5
- Butter
- Powdered sugar and flour for dusting

## Directions

1. First work the sugar with two of the whole eggs, then add the blanched and finely pounded almonds with one spoonful of the said sugar; work the mixture again, then add the breadcrumbs and three yolks, lastly the cognac
2. Whip the three remaining egg whites and fold them in
3. Prepare a proportioned baking pan, butter it and dust it with powdered sugar and flour
4. Bake it in the oven or in a country oven
5. Cover it with a tender crust like that of recipe 645, or with a chocolate coating made as follows: Put 30 grams of butter and 100 grams of chopped chocolate on the fire and when well melted add 30 grams of powdered sugar and spread the mixture on the cake when it is no longer boiling
