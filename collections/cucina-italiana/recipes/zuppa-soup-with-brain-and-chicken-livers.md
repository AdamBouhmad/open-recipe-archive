## Ingredients

- Calf's brain, or lamb brain, or similar animal brain, 120 grams
- Chicken livers, 3
- Eggs, 3
- A pinch of parsley and a pinch of basil
- Juice of one quarter lemon
- Butter
- Meat broth
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the brain so you can skin it, and together with the chicken livers, sauté them in butter and cook them with meat broth; season with salt and pepper.
2. Put the eggs in a pot, add the chopped parsley and basil, lemon juice, a little salt and pepper and beat them; then with the cold broth, which should serve to dampen the soup, dilute the mixture little by little.
3. Add the brain and chicken livers cut into small pieces, and let it condense over gentle heat, stirring continuously with a spoon, but without letting it boil.
4. Once condensed, pour it into the soup tureen over bread, which you will have already cut into cubes and sautéed in butter or oil, but first sprinkle a handful of grated Parmesan cheese over the bread itself.
