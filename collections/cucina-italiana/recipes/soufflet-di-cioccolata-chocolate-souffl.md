## Ingredients

- Sugar, 120 grams
- Potato starch, 80 grams
- Chocolate, 80 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Milk, 4 decilitres
- Eggs, 3
- Rum, one spoonful

## Directions

1. Put the butter on the fire and when it is melted pour in the grated chocolate; once this is also liquefied, pour in the potato starch and then the hot milk little by little, and, stirring always with force, add the sugar.
2. Once the mixture is well combined and the starch is cooked, let it cool. Last, add the rum and the eggs, first the yolks, then the beaten whites; and if there were more than three, the dessert would turn out even better.
3. Butter a baking pan that can withstand heat, pour the mixture into it, place it in a country-style oven or simply over a burner between two fires, and when it has puffed up serve it hot.
4. This is a sufficient portion for six people.
