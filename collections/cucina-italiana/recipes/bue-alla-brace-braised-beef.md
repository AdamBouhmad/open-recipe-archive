## Ingredients

- 500 grams lean and tender beef without bone
- 50 grams lard cut into thick strips about a finger's length
- salt and pepper
- one quarter of a medium onion
- half a carrot
- one celery rib about a palm's length
- 30 grams butter
- cold water
- 2 ladles hot water
- additional butter
- vegetables for garnish such as spinach, Brussels sprouts, carrots, fennel

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with the lard strips seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Make a battuto (soffritto) with the onion, carrot, and celery, chopping it coarsely
3. Place the battuto in a pot with 30 grams of butter and put the tied and seasoned meat on top
4. When the battuto is nearly consumed, wet it twice with a bit of cold water
5. Once the meat is colored and the battuto consumed, pour in two ladles of hot water
6. Cover the pot with double paper and let it boil slowly until the meat is cooked
7. Strain the sauce, remove the fat, and return it to the fire with another piece of butter
8. Season the sauce to taste and serve with a garnish of vegetables of your choice
