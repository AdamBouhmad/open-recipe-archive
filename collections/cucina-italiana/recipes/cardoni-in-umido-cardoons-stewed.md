## Ingredients

- Cardoons
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- lemon juice
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. After boiling them as in the preceding recipe, stew them with a mixture of garlic and parsley; oil, salt and pepper.
2. If you desire them more pleasing to the taste and of better appearance, cover them with an egg and lemon sauce when they are already on the serving dish.
3. Beat some eggs with lemon juice, put the liquid on the fire in a small pan stirring the spoon, and pour it when it begins to thicken.
4. If you do not use the sauce, season them at least with a pinch of Parmesan.
