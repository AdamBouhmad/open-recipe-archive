## Ingredients

- Veal, 150 grams
- Chicken breast, about 130 grams
- Prosciutto (fat and lean), 50 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 20 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Nutmeg
- Salt, a pinch
- Bread crumb soaked in milk
- Béchamel sauce, 3 tablespoons
- Chicken liver
- Broth

## Directions

1. Mince the veal, chicken breast, and prosciutto with a crescent-shaped knife. Place them in a mortar with the butter, Parmesan, a piece of bread crumb soaked in milk, and pound thoroughly until it can be passed through a sieve.
2. Place the sieved mixture in a bowl and add three tablespoons of béchamel (made to the consistency of a paste for this dish), the eggs, and nutmeg. Mix well.
3. Butter a smooth tin mold and place a sheet of buttered paper cut to size at the bottom. Pour in the mixture and cook in a bain-marie.
4. After unmolding, remove the paper and spread on that spot a sauce made from minced chicken liver cooked in broth.
5. Serve hot. If well made, it will be praised for its delicacy.
6. This serves six people.
