## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Egg yolks, 8
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. First work the egg yolks with the sugar and then pour in the milk little by little
2. To speed up the cooking you can put the mixture on high heat, but as soon as you see it smoke reduce the heat so it does not curdle
3. If this happens, pass the cream through a sieve
4. Cooking is complete when the cream sticks to the spoon, which should be moved continuously around
5. Add the flavoring just before removing it from the fire
6. This cream, without flour or starch, in the proportions given, is excellent for cream ice cream. It can also be used for a liquid English trifle by adding, when cooled, slices of sponge cake or ladyfingers lightly moistened with rosolio; but if you wish to make it even more pleasing, add very thinly cut pieces of candied fruit
