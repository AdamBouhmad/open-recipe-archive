## Ingredients

- 300 grams spaghetti
- 3 deciliters very good milk
- 20 grams butter
- 5 grams flour
- Parmesan cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the spaghetti dry from the water and season on a platter with Parmesan cheese and butter in the usual way.
2. Pour over it a béchamel sauce made from 3 deciliters of very good milk, 20 grams of butter and 5 grams of flour (corresponding to half a tablespoon).
