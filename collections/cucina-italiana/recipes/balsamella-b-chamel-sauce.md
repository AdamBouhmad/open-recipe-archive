## Ingredients

- Flour, 1 tablespoon
- Butter, the volume of an egg
- Milk, half a liter of the best

## Directions

1. Put in a pan on the fire a tablespoon of flour and a piece of butter the volume of an egg.
2. Use a spoon to dissolve the butter and flour together and when this begins to take a hazelnut color, pour in half a liter of the best milk little by little, stirring continuously with the spoon until you see the liquid thickened like a cream of milky color.
3. This is the balsamella.
4. If it becomes too thick add milk, if too thin put it back on the fire with another small piece of butter mixed with flour.
5. The dose is abundant, but you can proportion it according to circumstances.
