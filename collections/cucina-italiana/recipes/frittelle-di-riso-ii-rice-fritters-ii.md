## Ingredients

- Milk, approximately half a liter
- Rice, 100 grams
- Butter, the size of a walnut
- Salt
- Sugar, one scant spoonful
- Lemon zest flavoring
- Rum, one spoonful
- Egg yolks, three
- Flour, 50 grams
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the rice very thoroughly in the milk, giving it flavor and grace with butter, a little salt, a scant spoonful of sugar, and lemon zest flavoring.
2. Once cooled, add a spoonful of rum, three egg yolks, and 50 grams of flour.
3. Mix well and let the mixture rest for several hours.
4. When you are about to fry it, whip the whites as much as you can, add them while mixing slowly, and drop it into the pan by spoonfuls.
5. Dust them as usual with powdered sugar and serve them hot.
