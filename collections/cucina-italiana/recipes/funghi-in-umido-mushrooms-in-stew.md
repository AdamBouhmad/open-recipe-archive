## Ingredients

- mushrooms smaller than medium size
- oil
- garlic cloves
- nepitella leaves
- salt
- pepper
- tomato juice

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms and cut them into thinner slices than the fried ones
2. Put a pan on the fire with oil, a few cloves of whole garlic, slightly crushed, and a good pinch of nepitella leaves
3. When the oil begins to crackle, throw down the mushrooms without flouring them, season with salt and pepper and, halfway through cooking, wet them with simple tomato juice; be however sparing with the seasonings because mushrooms do not absorb them well
