## Ingredients

- Semolina of fine grain, 150 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 30 grams
- Milk, 6 deciliters
- Eggs, two whole and two yolks
- Salt, nutmeg flavor and lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook the semolina in the milk, and if you see that it does not become firm enough, add another pinch
2. Salt it when it is cooked and wait for it to lose heat before adding the eggs and the rest
3. Place the mixture in a syringe with a round-hole stamp rather large, and push it into boiling broth, holding the syringe perpendicular and let it boil until the passatelli have solidified
4. This dose will be sufficient for six or seven people
