## Ingredients

- Open ovoli mushrooms
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After cleaning and washing the mushrooms, dry them between the folds of a cloth.
2. Season them with oil, salt and pepper.
3. Serve very well as a side dish to steak or any roast.
