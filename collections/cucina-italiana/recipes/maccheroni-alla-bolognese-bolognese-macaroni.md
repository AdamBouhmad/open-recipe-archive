## Ingredients

- Lean veal meat (preferably from the fillet), 150 grams
- Dried cured meat (carnesecca), 50 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- One quarter of a common onion
- Half a carrot
- Two stalks of white celery about one palm in length, or the aroma of green celery
- A pinch of flour, but very scarce
- A small pot of broth
- Very little salt or none, due to the cured meat and broth which are flavorful
- Pepper and, if desired, the aroma of nutmeg
- Parmesan cheese
- Macaroni pasta, 500 grams or more

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small dice, finely mince with a half-moon knife the cured meat, onion and aromatics, then put everything together over heat, including the butter
2. When the meat has taken on color, add the pinch of flour, moistening with broth until fully cooked
3. Drain the macaroni well from water and season it with parmesan and this sauce
4. The sauce can be made more agreeable with pieces of dried mushrooms or some slices of truffles, or with a chicken liver cooked among the meat and cut into pieces
5. Finally, when the sauce is done, if you wish to make them even more delicate, add half a glass of cream
6. Serve the macaroni not dry but swimming in a little sauce
