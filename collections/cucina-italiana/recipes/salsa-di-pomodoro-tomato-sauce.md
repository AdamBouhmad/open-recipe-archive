## Ingredients

- One quarter of an onion
- One clove of garlic
- One piece of celery as long as a finger
- Some basil leaves and parsley as needed
- Oil
- Salt and pepper
- Seven or eight tomatoes

## Directions

1. Make a battuto with a quarter of onion, one clove of garlic, a piece of celery as long as a finger, some basil leaves and parsley as needed
2. Season it with a little oil, salt and pepper
3. Break seven or eight tomatoes and put everything together on the fire
4. Stir from time to time and when you see the juice condensed like a liquid cream, pass it through a sieve and serve it
