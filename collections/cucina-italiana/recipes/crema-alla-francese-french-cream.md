## Ingredients

- Milk, half a liter
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Eggs, one whole egg and 4 egg yolks
- Isinglass, 2 sheets
- Vanilla or lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar well together with the eggs, add the milk little by little and lastly the isinglass in its natural form.
2. Place the saucepan on the fire, stirring the spoon continuously always in one direction and when the cream begins to condense, sticking to the spoon, remove it.
3. Take a smooth mold with a hole in the middle, such that the quantity of cream can fill it, grease it with butter or with liqueur, so that a thin layer remains at the bottom and pour it into the same.
4. In summer place the mold in ice and in winter in fresh water and unmold it onto a folded towel on the platter.
5. If you do not trust the milk too much, give it a boil of at least a quarter of an hour before making the cream, or increase one sheet of isinglass.
