## Ingredients

- Zucchini
- Canned tuna in oil, finely minced
- Eggs
- Parmesan cheese, a pinch
- Zucchini marrow removed during hollowing
- Spices
- Pepper
- Butter
- Tomato sauce from recipe 125

## Directions

1. Prepare them like the previous recipe and fill them with a mixture made with canned tuna in oil finely minced with a crescent cutter and moistened with eggs, a pinch of Parmesan and a little of that marrow removed from the zucchini, the aroma of spices, a pinch of pepper and no salt.
2. Put them to cook in butter when this has taken a hazelnut color and grace them with the tomato sauce from recipe 125.
3. If you make them with care they will turn out so good you will not believe it.
