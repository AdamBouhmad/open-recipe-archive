## Ingredients

- Milk, 8 deciliters
- Sugar, 80 grams
- Chocolate, 60 grams
- Ladyfinger cookies, 60 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate, put it in the milk and when this begins to boil throw in the sugar and the ladyfingers, crumbling them with your fingers
2. Stir from time to time, so that the mixture does not stick to the bottom and after half an hour of boiling pass it through a sieve
3. When it is cold add the beaten eggs and the vanilla, pour it into a smooth mold, the bottom of which you will have first covered with a layer of liquefied sugar, and cook it in a bain-marie
4. 50 grams of sugar are sufficient to cover the bottom of the mold
5. Serve cold
