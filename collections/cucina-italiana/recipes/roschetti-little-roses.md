## Ingredients

- Flour, 200 grams
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Sweet almonds, 100 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Rendered fat (strutto), 30 grams
- One whole egg and one yolk

## Directions

1. Shell the almonds, dry them well in the sun or by fire, toast them to a hazelnut color, and chop them to the thickness of half a grain of rice
2. Mix both these and the sugar among the flour
