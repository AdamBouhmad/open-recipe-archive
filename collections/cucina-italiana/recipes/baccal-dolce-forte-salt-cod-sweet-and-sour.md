## Ingredients

- salt cod
- oil
- flour
- pepper
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- pine nuts
- raisins

## Directions

1. Cook like salt cod recipe 506, without garlic, and when browned on both sides, pour the sweet-sour sauce over it
2. Boil a little longer and serve hot
3. For the sweet-sour sauce, prepare in advance in a glass: if the salt cod weighs about 500 grams, use one finger-length of strong vinegar, two finger-lengths of water, sugar to taste, pine nuts and raisins in proportion
4. It is good to boil this sauce separately a bit before pouring it over the salt cod
