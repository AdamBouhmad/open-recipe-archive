## Ingredients

- Large freestone peaches, not very ripe, 6
- Small ladyfinger biscuits, 4
- Powdered sugar, 80 grams
- Sweet almonds with three peach kernels, 50 grams
- Candied citron or orange, 10 grams
- Good white wine, just under half a glass

## Directions

1. Divide the peaches in two parts and remove the pits, enlarging the holes where they were with the tip of a knife
2. Unite the removed pulp with the peeled almonds, which you will pound very finely in a mortar with 50 grams of the sugar
3. To this mixture unite the ladyfinger biscuits made into crumbs, and lastly the candied fruit cut into very small dice
4. Fill and top the holes of the twelve peach halves with this filling, then place them evenly with the filling upward in a copper baking dish
5. Pour into it the wine and the remaining 30 grams of sugar and cook them between two fires to serve them hot or cooled as you prefer with their juice around them
