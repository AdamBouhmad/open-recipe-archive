## Ingredients

- Eggs, 3
- Flour, 30 grams
- Butter, about the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Work the three egg yolks with the flour and butter
2. Add the three egg whites beaten until stiff
3. Cook the mixture in the oven or camp oven in a smooth mold with an oiled paper on the bottom
4. When the bread is cooked and cooled, cut it into cubes or small almond shapes
5. Pour hot broth over it
6. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on the side
7. Serves six or seven people
