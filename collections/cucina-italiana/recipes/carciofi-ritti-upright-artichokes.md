## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- water

## Directions

1. Remove only the small and useless leaves near the stem, cutting the latter short.
2. Trim the top with a knife and spread apart the internal leaves somewhat.
3. Place them upright in a pan, together with the peeled and whole stems.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and oil, all in good measure.
5. Sauté them while keeping them covered, and when they are well browned, pour a little water into the pan and finish cooking them with the same.
