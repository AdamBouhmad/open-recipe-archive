## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Coffee, half a liter
- Sugar, 300 grams

## Directions

1. Put the sugar in the milk and dissolve it over heat
2. Pour the mixture into the ice cream maker, as for ice creams in general
3. Serve it, when it will be solidified, in cups or small glasses
