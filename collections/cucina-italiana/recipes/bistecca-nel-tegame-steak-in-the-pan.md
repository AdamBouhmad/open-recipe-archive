## Ingredients

- one large steak
- a piece of butter
- a little oil
- garlic
- rosemary
- broth or water (if needed)
- tomato juice or puree (optional)
- potatoes

## Directions

1. If you have a large steak that, for being from an animal not very young or not recently slaughtered, makes you doubt its tenderness, instead of cooking it on the grill, place it in a pan with a piece of butter and a little oil.
2. Proceed as indicated, give it the flavor of garlic and rosemary.
3. Add, if necessary, a little broth or water, or tomato juice and serve it at table with potatoes cut into chunks cooked in its gravy, and if that is not enough, add more broth, butter and tomato preserves.
