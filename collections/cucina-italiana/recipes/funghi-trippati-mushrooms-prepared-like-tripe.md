## Ingredients

- ovolo mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parmesan cheese
- meat broth

## Directions

1. This preparation suits ovolo mushrooms well and they are called trippati perhaps because they are treated like tripe
2. After cleaning and washing the ovoli, cut them into thin slices
3. Cook them in butter and season with salt, pepper and grated parmesan cheese
4. If you add meat broth they will turn out even better
