## Ingredients

- frogs
- oil
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The simplest way is to make them with a soffritto of oil, garlic and parsley, salt and pepper, and when they are cooked, add lemon juice. Some use tomato juice instead of lemon, but the former is to be preferred. Never remove the eggs as they are the best part.
