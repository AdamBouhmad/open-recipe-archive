## Ingredients

- 50 grams of Romagna anise
- Alcohol (as prepared in previous recipe)
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Make it in the same manner as the previous recipe
2. Make the infusion with 50 grams of Romagna anise instead of lemon zest, as these anise from Romagna, for their pleasant flavor and strong fragrance, are without exaggeration the best in the world
3. Before using them, throw the anise into water to clean them from the earth that they probably contain, as it is deliberately mixed in to adulterate the merchandise
4. Proceed as with the previous recipe
