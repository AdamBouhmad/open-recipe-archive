## Ingredients

- Sugar, 150 grams
- Sweet almonds, 85 grams
- Egg yolks, 5
- Milk, 4 deciliters

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and with a small curved knife reduce them to the size of grains of wheat approximately.
2. Put 110 grams of the said sugar on the fire and when it is completely melted, pour in the almonds and stir them continuously with a spoon until they have taken on a cinnamon color.
3. Throw them then into a pan greased with butter and, when they are cold, pound them in a mortar with the remaining 40 grams of sugar and reduce them very fine.
4. Add the egg yolks and then the milk, mix well and pour the mixture into a mold with a hole in the middle, which you will have first greased with butter.
5. Cook it in a water bath and after, if in summer, keep the mold in ice.
