## Ingredients

- Potato flour, 120 grams
- Powdered sugar, 120 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Four eggs
- Cream of tartar, 5 grams
- Baking soda, 3 grams
- Grated lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. First work the egg yolks with the sugar, then add the potato flour and continue stirring with a spoon; then pour in the melted butter and finally the beaten egg whites and the powders.
2. Use a small pan so it remains two fingers high; butter it and dust it with flour mixed with sugar.
3. You can bake it at home in the country oven.
