## Ingredients

- Dried peas, 500 milliliters
- Water, 2 liters (plus water from cooking the peas)
- Half an onion
- One carrot
- Two pieces of celery, one finger in length
- Dill stems (if available)
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Tomato juice
- Bread, cut into small cubes
- Butter for frying the bread

## Directions

1. Put the peas on the fire in 2 liters of water
2. Make a soffritto with half an onion, one carrot, two pieces of celery and some dill stems if you have them; chop everything and put it on the fire with a piece of butter and let it take on color
3. Pour the half-cooked peas and drain them from the water, season with salt and pepper and let them absorb all the soffritto, then add tomato juice and the water from the peas to finish cooking them
4. Pass everything through a strainer and if the purée is too thick, add hot water; taste it and add another small piece of butter if needed
5. Cut the bread into small cubes and fry it in butter
