## Ingredients

- lean veal or milk-fed veal meat
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- sour cream (cream that has turned)
- water or broth
- lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Take lean veal or milk-fed veal meat, cut it into thin braised rolls, beat them, flour them and place them on the fire with a proportionate piece of butter.
2. Season them with salt and pepper and let them boil slowly until they have taken on color from both sides.
3. Then bathe them with the said sour cream and lastly with a little water or broth if it is milk-fed veal, so that the sauce does not become too thick and they can cook better.
4. Serve them with lemon wedges on the side.
5. For four people: 500 grams of lean meat without bone, 70 grams of butter and two deciliters of cream.
