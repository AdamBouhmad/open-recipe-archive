## Ingredients

- Semolina, 180 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Pine nuts, 50 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Milk, 8 deciliters
- Eggs, 4
- Salt, a pinch
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Cook the semolina in milk and when it begins to thicken pour in the pine nuts pounded in a mortar together with the sugar; then the butter and the rest, except the eggs, which you will save for last when the mixture is cooled.
2. For the rest proceed as with the rice small cakes from no. 630.
3. Before serving sprinkle them with powdered sugar.
