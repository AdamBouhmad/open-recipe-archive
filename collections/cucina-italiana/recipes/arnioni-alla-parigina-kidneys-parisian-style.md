## Ingredients

- one veal kidney
- 30 grams of butter
- salt
- pepper
- one large or two small anchovies

## Directions

1. Take a veal kidney, remove the fat, open it and cover it with boiling water.
2. When the water has cooled, dry it well with a cloth and thread it lengthwise and crosswise with clean sticks so that it stays open (in Paris silver pins are used).
3. Season it with 30 grams of melted butter, salt and pepper, and leave it prepared this way for an hour or two.
4. Given that the kidney weighs 600 or 700 grams, take another 30 grams of butter and one large or two small anchovies and clean them.
