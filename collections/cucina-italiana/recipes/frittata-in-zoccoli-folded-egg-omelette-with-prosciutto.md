## Ingredients

- thin slices of prosciutto, both fatty and lean
- butter
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the prosciutto slices into pieces about the size of a 10 centesimo coin
2. Place the prosciutto pieces in a pan with butter
3. When they have browned slightly, pour in the eggs, salted very little
4. When the omelette has begun to set, fold it in half so it takes on the name of fish of eggs more properly
5. Add more butter to finish cooking it
