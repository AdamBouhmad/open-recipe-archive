## Ingredients

- Large chicken livers, 2
- Salted tongue, 40 grams
- Gruyère cheese, 40 grams
- Béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken livers in butter and season with salt and pepper.
2. After cooked cut them into 12 pieces and do the same with the Gruyère and the tongue.
3. Take 12 toothpicks and thread the aforementioned 36 pieces on them; first the tongue, in the middle the Gruyère and at the top the chicken liver at a certain distance from each other.
4. Then, using béchamel sauce, coat with the same the three pieces so that they remain well covered; then pass them in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs and fry them.
