## Ingredients

- White bread, 250 grams
- Grated Gruyère, 80 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 40 grams
- Beaten eggs, 3
- Large white onions, 2
- Broth or milk, about 1.5 liters

## Directions

1. Cut the onions into very thin slices and put them on the fire with the butter; when they begin to take color, cook them thoroughly with broth, or with milk if you make it with that, so you can strain them well through a sieve
2. Mix the strained liquid into the remaining liquid to soak the soup
3. Toast the bread cut into slices or into cubes, and place it in layers in the soup tureen, season it with the eggs, the Gruyère and the Parmesan as you go
4. Finally pour the hot broth or milk and send it to the table
5. If you make it with milk it will be good to salt the eggs abundantly
