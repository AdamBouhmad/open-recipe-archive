## Ingredients

- Flour, 350 grams
- Brown sugar, 100 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Cream of tartar, 10 grams
- Baking soda, 5 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Vanilla sugar flavoring
- Milk, as needed

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the flour, make a well in it to place the rest and knead it with the addition of a little milk to obtain a somewhat soft dough, which you will shape into a slightly flattened cylindrical form about half a meter long
2. To bake it in the oven or country oven, butter a baking pan, and since this stick must fit in it, divide it into two pieces, keeping them separate as they will puff up greatly
3. The next day, cut them into cookie shapes, from which you will get about thirty, and toast them
