## Ingredients

- 1 mutton shoulder, boned
- Lardoon strips wrapped in salt and pepper
- Salt
- 40 grams butter
- 1 half onion studded with 1 clove
- 1 glass of water or better broth
- 1 tablespoon of brandy
- 1 aromatic bunch
- Tomatoes, if in season, broken into pieces

## Directions

1. Bone the shoulder and lard it with lardoon strips wrapped in salt and pepper
2. Salt it somewhat
3. Roll it up and tie it tightly
4. Place it on the fire with 40 grams of butter and the half onion studded with a clove to take on color
5. Remove the braising pan from the fire and pour in a glass of water or better broth, 1 tablespoon of brandy, an aromatic bunch, and if it is tomato season, some tomatoes broken up
6. Boil gently for about three hours with the pan covered with double sheets of paper, turning the meat frequently
7. When cooked, discard the onion, strain the sauce, degrease it and add it to the meat when serving
8. Do not cook it too much or it will not be able to be cut into slices
