## Ingredients

- artichokes, 12
- shelled peas, 150 grams
- butter, 50 grams
- grated parmesan cheese, 50 grams
- meat sauce, as needed

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes of all hard inedible leaves, divide them in two and remove the hair from the center if they have any.
2. Give them and the peas half cooking of a few minutes in salted water, then throw them in fresh water, remove them, dry them well, and divide the artichokes again into two parts.
3. Place both them and the peas on the fire with 40 grams of the said butter, season with salt.
