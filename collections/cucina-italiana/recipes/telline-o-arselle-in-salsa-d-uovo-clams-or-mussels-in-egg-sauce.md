## Ingredients

- clams or mussels
- garlic
- oil
- parsley
- pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- flour
- broth
- juice from the clams

## Directions

1. Rinse the mussels well in fresh water. Place them in a pot with a soffritto of garlic, oil, parsley and a pinch of pepper, cover and shake the pot so they do not dry out. Remove them when they open and prepare the following sauce: one or more egg yolks depending on quantity, lemon juice, a teaspoon of flour, broth and some of the juice that came from the clams. Cook it like a cream and pour it over the clams when serving. Alternatively, serve without sauce poured over slices of bread dried by the fire.
