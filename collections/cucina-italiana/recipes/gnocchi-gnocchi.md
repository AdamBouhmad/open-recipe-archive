## Ingredients

- Potatoes, large and floury, 200 grams
- Breast of chicken, boiled and finely minced, 40 grams
- Parmigiano cheese, grated, 40 grams
- Egg yolks, 2
- Salt, to taste
- Nutmeg, to taste
- Flour, 30-40 grams

## Directions

1. Cook 200 grams of large floury potatoes in water, or better by steam, and pass them through a sieve.
2. To these add the boiled chicken breast minced very finely with a crescent cutter, 40 grams of grated parmigiano, two egg yolks, salt to taste and a hint of nutmeg.
3. Mix and pour the mixture onto a work surface over 30 or 40 grams of flour to bind it and be able to pull it into sticks as thick as the little finger.
4. Cut these into small pieces and throw them into boiling broth where a cooking of five or six minutes will be sufficient.
5. This dose may be enough for seven or eight people.
