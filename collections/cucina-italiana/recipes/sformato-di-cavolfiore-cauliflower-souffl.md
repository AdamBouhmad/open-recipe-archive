## Ingredients

- 1 head of cauliflower (approximately 350 grams net)
- 3 deciliters milk
- 3 eggs
- 60 grams butter
- 30 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Boil the head of cauliflower halfway in water and then cut it into small pieces
2. Sauté them with half of the butter, salting them, and when they have absorbed the fat, finish cooking them with a little of the milk; you can then leave them as is or pass them through a sieve
3. With the remaining butter and milk and one tablespoon of flour make a béchamel and add it to the mixture together with the eggs, first whisked, and the Parmesan
4. Cook it in a smooth mold like the green bean soufflé and serve it hot
5. This quantity is enough for six people
