## Ingredients

- Semolina, 120 grams
- Butter, 15 grams
- Potato flour, one heaping spoonful, equal to 25 grams
- Eggs, one whole and two yolks
- Sugar, one heaping teaspoon
- Lemon zest flavoring
- Milk, 4 deciliters
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the semolina well in the milk with the said sugar, adding the butter, flavoring and a pinch of salt when you remove it from the fire
2. When it is completely cold, pound it in a mortar with the eggs, first the yolks one at a time then the whole egg
3. Add the potato flour last, working the mixture very well with the pestle
4. Pour it onto a plate and drop it into a pan by spoonfuls to obtain balls somewhat larger than walnuts, which you will serve dusted with icing sugar when they have lost their strong heat
