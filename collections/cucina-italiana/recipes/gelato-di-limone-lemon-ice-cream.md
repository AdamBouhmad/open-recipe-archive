## Ingredients

- Fine white sugar, 300 grams
- Water, half a liter
- Lemons, 3

## Directions

1. Preferably use garden lemons which have a more pleasant taste and greater fragrance than foreign ones, which often taste of reheating
2. Boil the sugar in the water with some pieces of lemon zest for 10 minutes in an uncovered copper pot
3. When this syrup is cold, squeeze the lemons into it one at a time, tasting the mixture to adjust the acidity
4. Strain it and pour it into the sorbet maker
5. This quantity will serve six people
