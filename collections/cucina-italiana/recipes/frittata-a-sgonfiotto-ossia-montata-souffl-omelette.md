## Ingredients

- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Egg yolks, 3
- Egg whites, 6
- Lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat first for several minutes the yolks with the sugar
2. Then whip the whites until very stiff and unite them with the yolks together, mixing gently
3. Grease a tray that can withstand the fire with cold butter, pour the mixture into it so that it fills it and place it immediately in a hot country oven
4. After five minutes in the oven make some cuts with a knife and dust it with powdered sugar, then finish cooking it, which will require ten or twelve minutes altogether
5. Be careful that it does not burn on the surface and send it immediately to the table
6. To make it puff better, some add a little lemon juice to the mixture
