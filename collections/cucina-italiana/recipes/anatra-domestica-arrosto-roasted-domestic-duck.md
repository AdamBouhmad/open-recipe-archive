## Ingredients

- One duck
- Lard, in wide and thin slices
- Oil
- Salt
- String

## Directions

1. Salt it inside and wrap the entire breast with wide and thin slices of lard held in place with string.
2. Brush it with oil and salt it when cooking is almost complete.
