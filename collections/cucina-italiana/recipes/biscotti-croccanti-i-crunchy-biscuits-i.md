## Ingredients

- Flour, 500 grams
- Powdered sugar, 220 grams
- Sweet almonds, whole and blanched mixed with some pine nuts, 120 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Anise, a pinch
- Eggs, 5
- Salt, a pinch

## Directions

1. Set aside the almonds and pine nuts to add later.
2. Mix everything together with four eggs, keeping the fifth egg in reserve in case it is needed to form a somewhat soft dough.
3. Make four loaves the thickness of a finger and as wide as a hand.
4. Place them on a baking sheet buttered and floured, and gild them on top.
5. Do not bake them so much that they cannot be sliced, which will be better done the next day when the crust softens.
6. Put the slices back in the oven to toast them lightly on both sides, and you will have crunchy biscuits.
