## Ingredients

- lean veal meat, including bone or joint, 500 grams
- cured meat, 50 grams
- butter, 30 grams
- one quarter of a large onion
- one small carrot
- two pieces of celery
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the onion, carrot and celery into large pieces and the cured meat into small dice.
2. Put everything together on the fire and season with salt and pepper.
3. Turn (further instructions are cut off at the end of the page)
