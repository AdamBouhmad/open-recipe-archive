## Ingredients

- Egg yolks, 8
- Powdered sugar, 150 grams
- Cocoa powder, 40 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- Vanilla flavoring by means of vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Place these ingredients in a vessel and work them with a spoon for over a quarter of an hour
2. Then pour the mixture into four paper boxes, approximately eight centimeters long and six centimeters wide
3. Place them in a covered copper baking pan, set it over a burner with very little fire underneath and on top so the mixture firms up somewhat without forming a crust, as it must be taken up in spoonfuls
4. Therefore the name biscottini is quite improper
