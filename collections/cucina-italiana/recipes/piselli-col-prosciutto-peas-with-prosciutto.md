## Ingredients

- Peas
- Young onions, 1 or 2
- Oil
- Prosciutto, fatty and lean, cut into small dice
- Salt, little or none
- Pepper
- Broth
- Butter, a little

## Directions

1. Divide one or two young onions in half lengthwise, according to the quantity of peas, and put them on the fire with oil and some prosciutto, both fatty and lean, cut into small dice.
2. Sauté until the prosciutto is shriveled; then throw in the peas, season with little or no salt and a pinch of pepper; stir and finish cooking them with broth, adding a little butter.
3. Serve them, either alone as a legume dish or as a side dish; but first discard all the onion.
