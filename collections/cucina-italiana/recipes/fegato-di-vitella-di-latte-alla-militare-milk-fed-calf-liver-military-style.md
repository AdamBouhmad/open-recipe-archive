## Ingredients

- One shallot or young onion
- Oil and butter
- Salt and pepper
- Calf liver, thinly sliced
- Parsley, minced
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mince a shallot or young onion finely and brown it in oil and butter
2. When it has taken on a deep red color, add the liver cut into thin slices
3. At mid-cooking, season with salt, pepper and a pinch of minced parsley
4. Let it boil gently so it remains juicy and serve it with its juice, adding the juice of a lemon when you serve it
