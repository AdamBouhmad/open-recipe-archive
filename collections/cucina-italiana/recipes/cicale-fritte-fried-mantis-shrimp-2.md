## Ingredients

- mantis shrimp
- water
- flour
- egg
- salt
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. After washing them, boil in a little water, covered with a cloth with a weight on top; 15 minutes of boiling is sufficient.
2. After cooking, peel them and, with the flesh exposed, cut it into two pieces.
3. Dust with flour, dip in beaten and salted egg, and fry in oil.
