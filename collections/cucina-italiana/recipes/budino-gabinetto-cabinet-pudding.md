## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Ladyfinger cookies, 100 grams
- Malaga raisins, 80 grams
- Sultanina raisins, 50 grams
- Apricot preserve, 50 grams
- Quince preserve, 50 grams
- Candied fruit, 20 grams
- Kirsch, half a deciliter
- Egg yolks, 6
- Egg whites, 4

## Directions

1. Boil the milk for half an hour with the sugar in it
2. Remove the seeds from the Malaga raisins; cut the candied fruit into small cubes and likewise the preserves, if they were firm, which in this case would be better
3. Burn the grapes and candied fruit with rum as in the Biscotto alla Sultana N.
