## Ingredients

- Hungarian flour, 500 grams
- Sugar, 180 grams
- Butter, 90 grams
- Cream of tartar, 15 grams
- Baking soda, 5 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Lemon peel flavoring or anise or candied citron in small pieces

## Directions

1. Make a well in the flour to put the melted butter, eggs, and sugar in it
2. Mix the flour with these ingredients and with the milk needed to form a dough of proper consistency and work it well
3. Add the two powders and flavorings last
4. Instead of one ring cake you can make two and keep them with a wide hole, which come out quite thick
5. Make some incisions on the surface, brush them with egg yolk and bake them in the oven or country oven, greasing the pan with butter or lard
6. Also with half the doses you obtain a decent ring cake
