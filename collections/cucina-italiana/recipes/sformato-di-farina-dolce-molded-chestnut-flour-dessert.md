## Ingredients

- Sweet flour, that is chestnut flour, 200 grams
- Chocolate, 50 grams
- Sugar, 30 grams
- Butter, 25 grams
- Candied citron, 20 grams
- Sweet almonds, 12 and some pistachios
- Milk, half a liter
- Eggs, 3
- Whipped cream scented with vanilla, 150 grams

## Directions

1. First blanch the almonds and pistachios; cut the pistachios in half, the almonds into thin strips or small pieces and toast them
2. Shape the candied citron into small pieces
3. Melt the chocolate over the fire in a deciliter of the said milk, then add the sugar and butter and set it aside
4. Place the flour in a pan and gradually pour in the rest of the milk while stirring well so no lumps form; then add it to the chocolate and put the mixture on the fire to cook it
5. Once cooked, let it cool to add the eggs, first the yolks, then the beaten whites, and lastly the almonds, pistachios and candied citron
6. Now take a ring mold, grease it with cold butter and pour in the mixture to firm it in a water bath
7. Before unmolding, surround it completely with chopped ice mixed with salt to freeze it, and send it to the table with the whipped cream filling mentioned above
