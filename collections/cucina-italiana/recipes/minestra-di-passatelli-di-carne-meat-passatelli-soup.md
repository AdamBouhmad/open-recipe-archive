## Ingredients

- 150 grams beef fillet
- 50 grams breadcrumbs
- 30 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 15 grams beef marrow
- 15 grams butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt to taste
- nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Pound the fillet in a mortar and pass it through a sieve
2. Crush the marrow and butter together with the blade of a knife and unite them with the meat
3. Add the rest to make a paste that will be firm enough to press the iron over it like the passatelli from the previous recipe
4. Boil them in broth for ten minutes and serve for six people
5. Also a chicken breast or a piece of turkey breast, boiled or raw, can be used for this purpose instead of the fillet
