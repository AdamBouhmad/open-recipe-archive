## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eggs, two
- Grated Parmesan, 40 grams, fine
- Nutmeg flavor
- Broth

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with two eggs, 40 grams of finely grated Parmesan and the flavor of nutmeg
2. Pull out a sheet not too thin and cut it with the fluted wheel into so many strips wide a finger and a half
3. Then, with the same wheel, cut these strips diagonally and at the same distance of a finger and a half to make so many pieces in the shape of an almond
4. Take them one at a time and squeeze with your fingers the four points, two above and two below to form as it were two little rings attached together
5. Cook them in broth with little cooking
6. The dose of two eggs will be sufficient for five people
