## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds with three bitter almonds, 150 grams
- Powdered sugar, 150 grams
- Fish gelatin in sheets, 20 grams
- Cream or milk, half a glass
- Water, one and a half glasses
- Orange blossom water, two tablespoons

## Directions

1. First prepare the fish gelatin by pressing it with your fingers at the bottom of a glass, cover it with water and let it soften, then when you need it, discard the water and wash it
2. Shell and pound the almonds in a mortar, moistening them from time to time with a teaspoon of water, and when you have reduced them to a fine paste, dilute them with the aforementioned water and pass them through a strong, fine cloth, taking care to extract all the substance
3. At this point, prepare a mold of appropriate capacity
4. Then put on the fire in a pan the almond milk, the cream, the sugar, the gelatin, the orange blossom water; mix everything together and let it boil for a few minutes
5. Remove it from the fire and when it has cooled, pour it into the mold immersed in fresh water or ice
6. To unmold it, simply pass a cloth dampened in boiling water around the mold
7. Note: boiling is necessary so that the fish gelatin incorporates with the rest; otherwise it may precipitate to the bottom of the mold
