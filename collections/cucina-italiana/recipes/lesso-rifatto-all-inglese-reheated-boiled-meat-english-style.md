## Ingredients

- Beef for boiling, half a kilogram
- Egg
- Flour, 20 grams
- Milk, two deciliters
- Butter, 50 grams
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Take half a kilogram of beef for boiling, which can suffice for three people, and blend in a pot one egg with 20 grams of flour and two deciliters of milk. Slice the boiled meat thinly and, taking a tray that can withstand fire, melt 50 grams of butter inside and spread it over this, then season it with salt, pepper and spices. When it has browned on one side and the other sprinkle on top a heaping spoonful of Parmesan and then pour the contents of the pot over it. Let the liquid set and send it to table.
