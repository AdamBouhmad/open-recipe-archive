## Ingredients

- Pearl onions, about 300 grams
- Sugar, 40 grams
- Flour, 15 grams
- Water, two-thirds of a glass
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Choose small white onions, about the size of a walnut
2. Peel them and trim away any excess
3. Blanch them in salted boiling water
4. In a saucepan, heat the sugar over dry heat until liquefied
5. Add the flour and stir continuously with a wooden spoon
6. When the mixture turns red, gradually add two-thirds glass of water mixed with vinegar
7. Let the liquid boil until any lumps dissolve
8. Add the pearl onions and shake the saucepan frequently, being careful not to touch them with a spoon to avoid damaging them
9. Taste before serving and adjust sugar or vinegar if needed
