## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Egg yolks, 8 and two whites
- Vanilla flavoring, or coriander seeds, or coffee flavoring, which are those that suit best

## Directions

1. If you prefer the last one, grind several grains of roasted coffee; if you like the flavor of coriander seeds, which is as pleasant as that of vanilla, pound a pinch of it and, both the one and the other, put them to boil in the milk which you will then strain.
2. If the milk is not of much substance, let it boil also for an hour and a quarter.
3. Never forget the veil of melted sugar at the bottom of the mold. Follow the same procedure as the previous recipe, omitting only the burnt sugar.
