## Ingredients

- A breast of young hen or capon
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- One marrow from bread, large as a fist

## Directions

1. Take a breast of young hen or capon, cut it into pieces and put it to cook in butter over a slow fire; season it with salt and pepper
2. If the butter is not enough, wet it with broth
3. Remove the breast and in the remaining sauce throw a marrow from bread, large as a fist, and with broth make a somewhat thick mush
