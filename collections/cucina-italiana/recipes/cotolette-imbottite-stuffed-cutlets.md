## Ingredients

- 170 grams veal or chicken or turkey breast, trimmed of bone
- 70 grams flour
- 20 grams butter
- 2 decilitres milk
- salt
- 1 tablespoon parmesan cheese
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg, beaten
- breadcrumbs
- oil or lard for frying
- lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Lightly fry the thin cutlets in butter, salt them, and set aside.
2. Make a béchamel sauce with the flour, butter, and milk. Once removed from heat, salt it, add the parmesan cheese and egg yolk, mixing well.
3. Once the béchamel has cooled, spread it on both sides of the cutlets to the thickness of a coin, evening it out with a knife blade dipped in oil.
4. Dip the cutlets in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs, and fry them in oil or lard until golden.
5. Serve with lemon wedges.
