## Ingredients

- Milk veal breast in one piece, 500 grams
- Lean milk veal without bone, 170 grams
- Fat and lean prosciutto, 40 grams
- Bologna mortadella, 40 grams
- Parmesan cheese, 15 grams
- One egg
- Just one quarter of a garlic clove and 4 or 5 parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Make a mixture as follows: clean the lean veal of tendons and pellicles if any, and mince it very finely with a piece of prosciutto fat
