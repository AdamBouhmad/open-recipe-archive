## Ingredients

- Ombrina (a type of fish), 500 grams
- Pastine or capellini, 120 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Water, 1 liter
- Tomato juice
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Put the ombrina in cold water over the fire and salt it
2. When cooked, pass the broth through a strainer and cook the pasta in it
3. Season the pasta with tomato juice to hide the fish smell
4. Pour the pasta into a tureen where you have placed a piece of butter
5. Serve with parmesan cheese on the side as is customary for meat-based pasta dishes
