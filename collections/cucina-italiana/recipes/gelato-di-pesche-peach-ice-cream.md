## Ingredients

- Ripe white peaches, including the pit, 400 grams
- Sugar, 250 grams
- Water, half a liter
- One garden lemon
- Three kernels taken from the pits of the peaches

## Directions

1. Crush these kernels with the sugar and put them to boil in the water for 10 minutes
2. Pass the peach pulp, squeeze the lemon into it and, mixing everything, pass it all through a fine sieve
3. This will serve six people
