## Ingredients

- Wild boar piece, approximately one kilogram, with skin and one finger-width of fat
- Onion, half
- Carrot, half of a large one
- Celery, two white ribs, one palm-length
- Parsley, a pinch
- Prosciutto fat and lean, 30 grams
- Oil
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make a battuto with half an onion, half of a large carrot, two white celery ribs about one palm-length, a pinch of parsley, and 30 grams of prosciutto (both fat and lean).
2. Mince finely with a crescent knife (lunetta) and place in a pot with oil, salt, and pepper under the boar to cook together.
3. Brown the meat on all sides.
4. Drain off most of the fat and continue as directed in the recipe (the text is cut off at this point).
