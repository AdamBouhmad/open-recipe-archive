## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Amaretti cookies, 100 grams
- Egg yolks, 6
- Vanilla sugar flavoring

## Directions

1. Pound the amaretti very finely in a mortar and then place them in a copper pot, adding the sugar, egg yolks, and flavoring
2. Mix and gradually pour in the milk
3. Put the copper pot on the fire to condense the mixture as you would for cream, then pour it into the sorbet maker to freeze it
4. You will have a delicious ice cream that can serve, with good measure, eight people. Half the quantity can serve four or even five people
