## Ingredients

- spinach
- oil
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- sapa (grape must reduction)
- dried grapes
- or sugar and raisins
- pasta matta dough (recipe N. 153)
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Boil spinach without water according to common use
2. Squeeze it well and chop it roughly
3. Stew it with a soffritto of oil, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper
4. Season with a little sapa and dried grapes from which the seeds have been removed (or supply with sugar and raisins if sapa and dried grapes are lacking)
5. Wrap these seasoned spinach in pasta matta dough (recipe N. 153) mixed with a few drops of oil, rolled out thin
6. Cut with a disc about the size shown on the preceding page
7. Fold the discs in half to make a half-moon shape and pinch the fold tightly
8. Fry in oil
9. Serve as a course between meals
