## Ingredients

- Egg yolks, 3
- Powdered sugar, 30 grams
- Cyprus wine, Marsala, or Madeira wine, 1.5 deciliters or about 9 tablespoons

## Directions

1. Work first with a ladle the egg yolks with the sugar until they have become almost white
2. Add the liquid and mix
3. Place it over a hot fire, beating it continuously and being careful not to let it boil as it would curdle
4. Remove it as soon as it begins to rise
5. Double this dose will be sufficient for eight people
6. If you want it more spirited add a tablespoon of rum; also a teaspoon of powdered cinnamon is not bad
