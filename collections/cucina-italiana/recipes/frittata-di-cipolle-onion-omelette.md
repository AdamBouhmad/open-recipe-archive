## Ingredients

- large white onions
- lard or oil
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice the onions into strips about half a finger wide
2. Place them in fresh water for at least an hour
3. Dry them well in a cloth before putting them in a pan with lard or oil
4. When they begin to take on color, salt them somewhat
5. Salt the eggs before pouring them over the onion, taking care the onion does not turn black from overcooking
