## Ingredients

- piece of meat from the loin
- slices of fatty and lean prosciutto
- raisins
- pine nuts
- lard
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- three or four pieces of peeled tomato
- tomato sauce
- water
- tomato paste (if fresh tomatoes unavailable)

## Directions

1. Take a piece of meat from the loin and lard it with slices of fatty and lean prosciutto, raisins, pine nuts and with a small mixture of lard, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper
2. Once the meat is prepared in this manner and tied with string to keep it more united, place it on the fire with a soffritto of finely chopped lard and onion
3. Turn it often and pierce it from time to time with a larding needle
4. Once the meat is browned and the soffritto consumed, add three or four pieces of peeled tomato and when these are destroyed, gradually add tomato sauce
5. Wait for this to be somewhat reduced, then pour in enough water to cover the piece, season with salt and pepper and let boil on low heat
6. In the absence of fresh tomatoes use paste
7. Season the macaroni with the sauce and spicy cheese, as the Neapolitans do, and the meat serves as a side dish
8. Boil the macaroni in a wide vessel with plenty of water and do not cook them too much
