## Ingredients

- Dried Bosnian prunes, 500 grams
- Good red wine, 4 deciliters
- Water, 2 deciliters
- Marsala, one small glass
- White sugar, 100 grams
- One piece of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash the prunes and soak them for two hours in fresh water
2. Remove them and dry them
3. Put them on the fire with the red wine, water, Marsala, sugar, and cinnamon
4. Boil them slowly for half an hour in a covered pot, which should be sufficient, but before removing them from the fire ascertain that they have softened enough, as more or less cooking may depend on the quality of the fruit
5. Remove them dried and place them in the vessel where you wish to serve them
6. Let the remaining syrup reduce on the fire for eight or ten minutes in an uncovered pot and then pour it also into the vessel over the prunes
7. Instead of the scent of cinnamon, you may substitute vanilla or the zest of citron or orange
