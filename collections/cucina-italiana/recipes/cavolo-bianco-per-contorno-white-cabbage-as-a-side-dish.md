## Ingredients

- cavolo cappuccio or verzotto (cabbage)
- prosciutto (ham)
- cipolla (onion)
- burro (butter)
- brodo (broth)
- pepe (pepper)
- sale (salt)

## Directions

1. Take a head of cabbage, cut it crosswise from the stem to form four parts and cut each of these into small pieces.
2. Keep it soaked in fresh water, and blanch it in salted water and, removed from the fire, drain it well without squeezing it.
3. Make a beaten mixture of ham and onion and put it on the fire with a piece of butter.
4. When the onion has taken on red color, stop it with a ladle of broth, boil a little and then pass the gravy.
5. In this gravy put back the cabbage with a piece of ham, season with pepper and little salt and boil it slowly to finish cooking it.
6. Remove the ham and send it to the table as a side dish to boiled meat.
