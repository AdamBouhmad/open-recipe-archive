## Ingredients

- Sugar, 100 grams
- Fine bread, 60 grams
- Sultana raisins, 40 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Butter, as needed
- Milk, half a liter
- Lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. Slice the bread into thin slices and toast them lightly
2. Butter the hot slices on both sides
3. Place the buttered bread slices in a concave and presentable dish that can be brought to the table
4. Scatter the raisins and grated lemon zest over the bread slices
5. Beat the eggs well in a pot with the sugar, then add the milk and pour this mixture into the dish over the ingredients without touching them
6. To cook it, place the dish over a low fire on the hearth, cover it with a countryside oven lid with fire on top, and serve it hot
