## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds, 250 grams
- Bitter almonds, 50 grams
- Powdered sugar, amount not specified
- Egg whites, 2

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and dry them in the sun or by fire, then chop them very finely with a crescent-shaped knife.
2. Work the sugar and egg whites together with a wooden spoon for at least half an hour, and add the almonds to form a firm paste from which to make small balls about the size of a small walnut; if it turns out too soft add more sugar and if too hard add a little more egg white, this time whipped.
3. If you wish to give the macaroons a color tending toward brown, mix a little browned sugar into the mixture.
4. As you form the said balls, flatten them to the thickness of one centimeter, place them on wafers or on pieces of paper, or in a baking pan buttered and sprinkled with half flour and half powdered sugar; but at a reasonable distance from one another because they spread a lot and puff up, remaining hollow inside.
5. Bake them in an oven at moderate heat.
