## Ingredients

- Fish glue, 20 grams
- Water, 3 deciliters
- Raspberry syrup, 2 deciliters
- Marsala wine, 1 deciliter
- Rum, 1 tablespoon

## Directions

1. Dissolve 20 grams of fish glue in the fire in three deciliters of water
2. Mix in the raspberry syrup, Marsala wine, and rum
3. Sugar is not necessary or only very little, as the syrup is very sweet
4. Proceed as with the preceding gelatins
5. If making with fresh raspberries instead of syrup, use the doses from the strawberry gelatin recipe and follow the same procedure
