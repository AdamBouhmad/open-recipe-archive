## Ingredients

- 200 grams Hungarian flour
- 50 grams butter
- 20 grams beer yeast
- less than one deciliter milk or cream, so that the mixture is rather firm
- 3 egg yolks
- one teaspoon sugar
- one good pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put the yeast in a cup with a spoonful of the flour and, dissolving it with a little of the said warm milk, set it to rise near the fire.
2. Then in a bowl pour the butter, melted in winter in a water bath, and work it with a spoon, throwing in the egg yolks one at a time, then pour the rest of the flour, the yeast when it has doubled in size, the milk little by little, the salt and the sugar, working the mixture with a hand until it reaches the desired consistency.
