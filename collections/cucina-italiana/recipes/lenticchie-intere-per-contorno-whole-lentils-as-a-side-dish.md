## Ingredients

- lentils
- aromatic herb bouquet
- prosciutto, fat and lean, chopped
- butter
- onion
- defatted broth from cotechino or zampone (or regular broth if the cotechino is not very fresh)
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in water with an aromatic herb bouquet.
2. Once cooked and well drained from water, prepare a small mixture of fat and lean prosciutto, a piece of butter, and a little onion.
3. Brown the onion well, then add one or two ladles of defatted broth from the cotechino or zampone.
4. Let it boil a little, strain it, and use this sauce to prepare the lentils.
5. Add another piece of butter, salt, and pepper.
