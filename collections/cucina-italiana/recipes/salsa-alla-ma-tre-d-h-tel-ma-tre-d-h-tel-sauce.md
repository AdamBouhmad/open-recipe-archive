## Ingredients

- Parsley, a pinch
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mince a pinch of parsley and to remove its acidity (as some suggest) place it within the point of a napkin and squeeze it lightly in fresh water
2. Form a paste with butter, salt, pepper and lemon juice
3. Place it over the fire in a pan or plate and, without letting it boil, dip the steak when you remove it from the grill, or fried cutlets
