## Ingredients

- rabbit
- butter
- oil
- rabbit liver
- cured meat (carnesecca)
- onion
- celery
- carrot
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- water
- tomato sauce or preserve
- parmesan cheese
- pappardelle or strips of pasta

## Directions

1. After washing the rabbit, cut it into pieces larger than those for frying and put it on the fire in a casserole to release its water which you will then drain.
2. When well dried, throw in a piece of butter, a little oil and a battuto finely chopped and composed of the animal's liver, a piece of cured meat and all the aromatics, namely: onion, celery, carrot and parsley.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Stir it often and when it is browned, wet it with water and tomato sauce or preserve, to finish cooking it, adding at the end another piece of butter.
5. Use the sauce to season with this and with parmesan a pasta of pappardelle or strips, and bring to the table for the second course the rabbit with some of its sauce.
6. If you do not want to season the pasta, the cured meat is not necessary in the battuto.
