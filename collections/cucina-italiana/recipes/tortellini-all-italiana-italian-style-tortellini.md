## Ingredients

- Pork chops from the loin, about 300 grams
- One lamb brain or half of a larger animal's brain
- Beef marrow, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 50 grams
- Three egg yolks and, if needed, add one white
- Nutmeg flavor

## Directions

1. Debone and degrease the pork chops, then cook them in a small pot with butter, salt and a pinch of pepper.
