## Ingredients

- Tuna slices
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Cut the tuna into slices like the previous recipe, preferably using the belly part.
2. Season with oil, salt and pepper.
3. Coat in breadcrumbs.
4. Cook on the grill.
5. Serve with lemon wedges.
