## Ingredients

- milk-fed veal breast with bones, weighing 600 to 700 grams
- prosciutto, lean, finely sliced, 50 to 60 grams
- lard (strutto)
- salt
- potatoes

## Directions

1. For a family dish, leave the piece as it is with all its bones.
2. If it does not exceed 600 to 700 grams, you can cook it in a country oven.
3. Lard it with finely sliced lean prosciutto.
4. Tie it so it stays together.
5. Coat it all abundantly with lard (strutto) and salt it.
6. Place it in a baking pan and about ten minutes before removing it from the fire, add potatoes which in that fat become very good.
7. Instead of lard you can use butter and oil, and instead of prosciutto salt it generously.
