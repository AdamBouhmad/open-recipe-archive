## Ingredients

- 400 grams of shelled peas
- Broth
- A bunch of parsley, celery, carrot, and some basil leaves
- Two slices of bread fried in butter
- Meat gravy, if available
- Fine, day-old bread for the soup, cut into cubes and fried in butter

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in broth with a bunch composed of parsley, celery, carrot, and some basil leaves, which you will then discard
2. When the peas are cooked, throw in two slices of bread fried in butter to soak them up
3. Pass everything through a sieve
4. Dilute this mixture with the necessary broth, add a little meat gravy if you have it
5. Serve the soup with fine, day-old bread cut into cubes and fried in butter
