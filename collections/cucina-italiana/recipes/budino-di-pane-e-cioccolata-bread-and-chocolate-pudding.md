## Ingredients

- Fine common bread, 100 grams
- Sugar, 70 grams
- Chocolate, 40 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Milk, 4 deciliters
- Eggs, 3

## Directions

1. Pour hot milk over bread cut into thin slices
2. After about two hours of steeping, pass it through a sieve to make it uniform
3. Put it on the fire with the sugar, butter, and grated chocolate
4. Stir the mixture frequently, let it boil somewhat and let it cool
5. Add the eggs, first the yolks and then the whipped whites
6. Cook it in a bain-marie in a smooth buttered mold and serve it cold
7. For better appearance, it would be good to cover it after unmolding with a cream
8. This dose will be sufficient for five people
