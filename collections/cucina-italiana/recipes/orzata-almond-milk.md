## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds with 10 or 12 bitter ones, 200 grams
- Water, 600 grams
- Fine white sugar, 800 grams
- Orange flower water, two spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Shell the almonds and pound them in the mortar, moistening them from time to time with the orange flower water.
2. When they are reduced to an impalpable paste, dissolve them with one third of the said water and strain the juice through a cloth, squeezing it well.
3. Return to the mortar the dry paste remaining in the cloth, grind it with the pestle; then dissolve it with another third of the water and strain the juice.
4. Repeat the same operation for the third time, put all the obtained liquid on the fire, and when it is very hot pour in the sugar, stir it and let it boil for about twenty minutes.
5. When it is cold, bottle it and keep it in a cool place.
6. Made in this way, you will see that the orzata does not ferment and you can keep it for a long time, though not as long as fruit syrups.
7. Moreover it comes of such substance that very little, dissolved in a glass of water, is sufficient to obtain an excellent and refreshing drink.
8. Made with melon seeds, it comes out even more delicate.
