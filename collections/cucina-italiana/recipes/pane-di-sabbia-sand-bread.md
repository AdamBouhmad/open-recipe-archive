## Ingredients

- Fresh butter, 185 grams
- Powdered sugar, 185 grams
- Rice flour, 125 grams
- Starch flour, 125 grams
- Potato flour, 60 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Juice of one quarter of a lemon
- Cognac, one tablespoon
- Baking soda, one teaspoon
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Work the butter alone first, then add the egg yolks one by one, always stirring the spoon in the same direction
2. Then pour in the sugar, then the cognac and lemon juice
3. Then the flours and lastly the baking soda and whipped egg whites; but of these latter, pour in first two tablespoons to soften the mixture, and fold in the rest gently
4. Pour the mixture into a proportioned pan, greased with butter and dusted with powdered sugar and flour, and bake it in the oven or in a country oven at moderate heat. About one hour of baking should be sufficient
