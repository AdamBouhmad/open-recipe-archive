## Ingredients

- Flour, 150 grams
- Butter, 60 grams
- Powdered sugar, 60 grams
- One egg
- Grated lemon peel flavoring

## Directions

1. Make a dough without stirring it too much.
2. Roll it out thin to obtain two dozen breadsticks ten centimeters long.
3. Bake them in a country oven on a baking sheet without any preparation.
4. They can be served with tea or with bottled wine.
