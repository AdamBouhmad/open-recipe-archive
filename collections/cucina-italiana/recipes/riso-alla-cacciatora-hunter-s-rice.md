## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Lard
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Water
- Rice
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces, excluding the head and feet
2. In a pan, make a soffritto with lard, garlic, and parsley until colored
3. Add a piece of butter and season with salt and pepper
4. Brown the chicken in this mixture
5. Pour into boiling water in a pot
6. Add the rice and cook
7. Before removing from heat, flavor with a good handful of Parmesan cheese
8. The rice should not be too watery nor too dry
