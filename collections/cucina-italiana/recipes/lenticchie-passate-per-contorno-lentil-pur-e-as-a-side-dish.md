## Ingredients

- lentils
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- celery
- carrot
- broth (defatted broth from cotechino can be used)
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in water with a piece of butter until cooked but not mushy.
2. Pass them through a sieve.
3. Make a small mixture of onion (a little, as it should not be noticeable), parsley, celery, and carrot.
4. Place it on the fire with enough butter and brown it well.
5. Stop the cooking with a ladle of broth (which can also be the defatted broth from the cotechino).
6. Strain it and use that sauce to flavor the purée.
7. Do not forget salt and pepper, and note that it should remain as firm as possible.
