## Ingredients

- Bananas, 4, which when shelled amount to 240 grams
- White sugar, 200 grams
- One garden lemon
- Water, half a liter

## Directions

1. Pass the pulp of the bananas through a sieve, add to this the lemon juice, boil the sugar in the water for five minutes in an uncovered saucepan, mix everything together and pour the mixture into the sorbetierre, making no economy of ice and salt.
