## Ingredients

- Eggs, 3 numbers
- Sugar, equal weight to the eggs
- Potato flour, 125 grams
- Butter, 125 grams
- Sweet almonds with three bitter ones, 125 grams
- Grated lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the sun or by the fire and pound them very finely in a mortar with a third of the said sugar
2. Work with a wooden spoon the three egg yolks with the remaining sugar and the lemon zest until they become whitish
3. Add the potato flour, then the pounded almonds and the melted butter, working the mixture further
4. Lastly add the beaten egg whites and when everything is amalgamated together, bake it in the oven
5. Dust it with powdered sugar while still hot
6. Use a baking pan with a bottom diameter of approximately 22 centimeters for proper height
7. You can use the same butter to grease the pan, which, as you know, should be dusted with powdered sugar mixed with flour
