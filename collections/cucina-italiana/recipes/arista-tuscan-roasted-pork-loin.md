## Ingredients

- Pork loin with ribs, 3-4 kilograms
- Garlic
- Rosemary sprigs
- Cloves, a few
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with garlic, bunches of rosemary, and a few cloves of garlic, but sparingly, as these are flavors that easily become overwhelming.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Cook it roasted on a spit, which is better, or send it to the oven without further ado.
4. Use the fat that it renders for browning potatoes or for making vegetable dishes.
5. Serve cold.
