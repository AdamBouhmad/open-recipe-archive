## Ingredients

- Sweet grapes, 1/3 black and 2/3 white, or all white, 2 kilogramms
- Rose apples or reinette apples, 1 kilogramm
- Two large pears
- White wine, preferably Vin Santo, 240 grams
- Candied citron, 120 grams
- White mustard powder, 40 grams
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Crush the grapes as you would for wine
2. After one or two days, when it has risen, press out the must
3. Peel the apples and pears and cut them into thin slices
4. Put them on the fire with the said wine and when it has all evaporated, pour in the must
5. Stir frequently and when the mixture is condensed somewhat more than fruit preserve, let it cool
6. Dissolve the mustard powder in a little very hot wine and add it along with the candied citron cut into small pieces
7. Preserve it in small jars with a thin layer of cinnamon powder on top
