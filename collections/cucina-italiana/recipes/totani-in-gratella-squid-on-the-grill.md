## Ingredients

- Squid
- Tentacles of squid
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Breadcrumbs
- Oil
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the tentacles from the squid, leaving the sack and head intact.
2. Chop the tentacles finely with a knife together with parsley and very little garlic.
3. Mix this mixture with plenty of breadcrumbs, season with oil, pepper and salt.
4. Use this mixture to fill the sack of the squid.
5. Close the opening of the sack by piercing it with a small stick, which you will remove later.
6. Season with oil, pepper and salt and cook on the grill as described.
