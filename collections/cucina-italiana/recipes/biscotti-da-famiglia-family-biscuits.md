## Ingredients

- Flour, 250 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Powdered sugar, 50 grams
- Ammonium powder, 5 grams
- A pinch of salt
- Vanilla flavoring with vanilla sugar
- Lukewarm milk, about one deciliter

## Directions

1. Make a well in the mound of flour and place the ingredients listed above except the milk, which you will use to mix this dough, which should be soft and must be stirred well to refine it.
2. Roll out a sheet about the thickness of a scudo coin, dusting it with flour if necessary.
3. Finally pass a notched rolling pin over it, or use a grater or a fork to make some decoration.
4. Cut the biscuits in the shape you prefer.
