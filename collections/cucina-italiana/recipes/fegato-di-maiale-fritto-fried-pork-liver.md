## Ingredients

- Pork liver
- Pork pancreas (stomachino)
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon

## Directions

1. To feel the true taste of pork liver you must fry it natural, in thin slices, in virgin lard and mixed with pancreas in small pieces
2. Remove it from the pan with a little of its fat, season with salt and pepper and while still boiling squeeze a lemon over it, whose acid serves to temper the greasiness
3. The thin slices of liver can also be floured before frying
