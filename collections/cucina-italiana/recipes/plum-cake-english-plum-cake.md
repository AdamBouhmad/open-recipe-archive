## Ingredients

- Sugar, 250 grams
- Butter, 250 grams
- Finest flour, 250 grams
- Candied fruit, 80 grams
- Malaga raisins, 80 grams
- Sultana raisins, 80 grams
- Passolina raisins, 80 grams
- Whole eggs, 5
- Egg yolks, 4
- Rum, slightly less than one deciliter, that is five tablespoonfuls
- Flavor of lemon zest or vanilla

## Directions

1. Cut the candied fruit into thin strips and remove the seeds from the malaga raisins
2. First work the butter alone with a spoon, softening it at the heat if necessary, add the sugar and continue working it until it has become white
3. Break the eggs one at a time mixing them in, then the flour and finally the remaining ingredients
4. Pour the mixture into a smooth mold that has been previously lined with paper buttered on the inside and bake it in the oven
5. You can serve it hot sprinkled with powdered sugar, and also cold, which is equally good
6. The paper serves to prevent the raisins from sticking to the mold. This dose will be enough for twelve people
