## Ingredients

- Zucchini, cut into chunks
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parmesan cheese
- Nutmeg
- Béchamel sauce, fairly thick

## Directions

1. Cut zucchini into chunks slightly larger than hazelnuts
2. Brown them in butter and season with salt and pepper
3. Pour them into a fireproof dish
4. Sprinkle lightly with Parmesan mixed with a pinch of nutmeg
5. Cover with fairly thick béchamel sauce
6. Brown the surface with a camp oven cover
7. Serve as an intermediate course or alongside boiled meat or a meat stew
