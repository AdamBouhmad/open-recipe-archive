## Ingredients

- Mushrooms, 600 grams
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Meat stock
- Béchamel
- Eggs, 5
- Parmigiano

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms well from soil and wash them, then chop them finely to the size of a chickpea or less.
2. Put them on the fire with butter, salt and pepper and when they have softened somewhat, cook them through with meat stock.
3. Remove from fire, bind them with béchamel, eggs and parmigiano and set the mixture in a bain-marie.
4. 600 grams of mushrooms in nature with five eggs will make a mold sufficient for ten people.
5. Serve it hot and as an intermediate course.
