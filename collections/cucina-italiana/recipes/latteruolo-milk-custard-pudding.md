## Ingredients

- Milk, one litre
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Eggs, 6
- Lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. Remove a thin strip of zest from a lemon using a knife and place it in the milk, then bring the milk to a boil with the sugar inside for half an hour
2. When cooled, remove the lemon zest and mix in the beaten eggs
3. Cook it as the previous recipe, or without the pasta underneath; in that case, grease the baking dish abundantly with cold butter so it does not stick to the bottom
