## Ingredients

- Beignet paste (recipe N. 631)
- Cream, whipped cream, or fruit preserves for filling
- Chocolate, 120 grams
- Powdered sugar, 50 grams
- Water, 1 deciliter

## Directions

1. Prepare 20 to 23 small beignets following recipe N. 631.
2. Fill them with cream, whipped cream, or fruit preserves.
3. Whip the following mixture in the chocolate pot on the fire: chocolate, powdered sugar and water.
4. When well whipped, like chocolate served in a cup, pour it while boiling over the pastries placed one by one on a serving platter where they form a mound.
5. This is a dish best made the same day it is to be served, otherwise it hardens.
6. This quantity will suffice for six people.
