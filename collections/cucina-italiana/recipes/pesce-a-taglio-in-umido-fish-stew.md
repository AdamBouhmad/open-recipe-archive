## Ingredients

- Fish fillet (tuna, ombrina, dentice, or ragno), about 600 grams
- Oil
- Onion
- Celery
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Whole clove
- Tomato sauce or tomato conserve

## Directions

1. Remove the scales, wash and dry the fish, dust it with flour, and brown it slightly with a little oil.
2. Remove it, discard the remaining oil, and clean the pot.
3. Make a finely minced mixture with half a medium onion, a palm-length piece of white celery, and a good pinch of parsley.
4. Put it on the fire with sufficient oil and season with salt, pepper, and one whole clove.
5. When it has taken color, stop it with plenty of tomato sauce or conserve dissolved in water.
6. Let it boil a little and then place the fish in it to finish cooking, turning it often.
7. Serve it with plenty of its thick sauce so the fish is well-covered in it.
