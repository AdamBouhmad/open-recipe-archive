## Ingredients

- Tuna in oil, 50 grams
- Capers pressed from vinegar, 50 grams
- Anchovies, 2
- Hardboiled egg yolks, 2
- A good pinch of parsley
- Juice of half a lemon
- A pinch of pepper
- Oil, as needed

## Directions

1. Clean the anchovies and then chop them with a crescent-shaped knife together with the tuna, capers and parsley.
2. Pound them afterward in a mortar with the egg yolks and a little oil to soften the mixture and be able to pass it better through a sieve.
3. Then dilute it with plenty of oil and lemon juice, to reduce it to the consistency of a liquid cream.
