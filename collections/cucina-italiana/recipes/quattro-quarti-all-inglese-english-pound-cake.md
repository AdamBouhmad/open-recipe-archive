## Ingredients

- 5 eggs and equal weight of sugar and flour (including eggshell weight)
- Raisins, 200 grams
- Butter, 200 grams
- Candied fruit cut into pieces, 30 grams
- Baking soda, one teaspoon

## Directions

1. Work the eggs with the sugar first, add the flour and continue working with a wooden spoon for approximately half an hour
2. Let the mixture rest for one or two hours, then add to it the butter melted in a bain-marie, the baking soda, the raisins and the candied fruit
3. Pour it into a baking pan or smooth mold, greased with butter and dusted with powdered sugar mixed with flour
4. Bake it in the oven
5. First wash the raisins to clean them from the earth they ordinarily contain, and dry them
