## Ingredients

- artichokes
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- garlic
- nepitella (catnip), fresh
- tomato sauce or tomato paste dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Remove all inedible leaves from the artichokes and divide each into four wedges, or even six if they are large.
2. Flour them and place them on the fire in a copper pan with oil in proportion, seasoning with salt and pepper.
3. When you have browned them, add a small mixture composed of one clove of garlic, or half if the artichokes are few, and a good pinch of fresh nepitella.
4. When they have released their moisture, finish cooking them with tomato sauce or tomato paste dissolved in water.
5. They can serve as a side dish or be eaten alone.
