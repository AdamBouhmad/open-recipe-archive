## Ingredients

- one chicken
- sufficient quantity of lard
- salt
- pepper
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into small pieces and put it in a pan as is with sufficient quantity of lard, seasoning with salt and pepper.
2. When cooked, drain off the excess fat and add the tomatoes in small pieces after removing the seeds.
3. Stir continuously until the tomatoes have almost dissolved and serve.
