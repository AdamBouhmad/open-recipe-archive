## Ingredients

- one large thin sheet of meat for braising
- chicken breast, cut into fillets thicker than one centimeter
- two slices of mortadella
- two slices of prosciutto
- Butter, 30 grams
- salt
- pepper
- meat broth
- kitchen twine

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken breast and the mortadella and prosciutto slices into thick fillets.
2. Spread some of the prepared filling mixture on the meat sheet.
3. Arrange a third of the fillets on top, interspersing them, and spread more filling over them.
4. Repeat this layering process two more times.
5. Roll the meat sheet tightly around the filling (like a salami roll) and tie it securely with kitchen twine.
6. Heat 30 grams of butter in a pot and brown the roll, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Once browned, pour off the fat (which can be saved for other dishes).
8. Continue cooking for approximately three hours, adding broth gradually as needed.
9. Once cooled, remove the twine, slice into portions, and serve.
10. Serves 10 to 12 people, especially when garnished with meat gelatin.
