## Ingredients

- flour
- two eggs
- salt, a pinch
- cognac or spirits, three tablespoons (or fumetto)
- butter, 20 grams
- oil or lard for frying
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out a sheet of dough not too firm and as thin as possible by mixing flour with two eggs, a pinch of salt and three tablespoons of cognac or spirits, or perhaps better fumetto.
2. Once the sheet is made, grease it with 20 grams of melted butter and roll it, or fold it over itself to the width of 10 to 11 centimeters, but so that the greased part remains inside; then cut the roll in half lengthwise and then crosswise at proportionate distances to obtain many rectangles and press with your fingers the outer edge of these, that is the rib that has not been cut.
3. Fry them in a pan with plenty of fat and before serving dust them with powdered sugar.
4. If they turn out well you will see that these little books open and remain flaked.
5. This dose can be enough for four people.
