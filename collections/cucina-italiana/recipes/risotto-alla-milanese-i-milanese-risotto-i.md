## Ingredients

- Rice, 500 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Saffron, as much as needed to make it well yellow
- Half an onion of medium size
- Broth

## Directions

1. For cooking follow the method of No. 75
2. To make this risotto more substantial and more pleasing to the taste, broth is needed
3. If you have a bronze mortar at home, buy the saffron in its natural form, grind it fine and dissolve it in a few drops of hot broth before throwing it into the rice
4. Serve with Parmesan cheese
5. Saffron has an exciting action, stimulates the appetite and promotes digestion
6. This quantity can be enough for five people
