## Ingredients

- one beef tongue weighing over 1 kilogram without the pappagorgia
- 60 grams of lard cut into thin lardons, seasoned with salt and pepper
- 30 grams of butter
- salt
- pepper
- meat sauce (sugo di carne) or broth
- a small piece of butter
- less than half a tablespoon of flour
- boiled vegetables

## Directions

1. Skin the tongue as indicated in recipe N. 289 and lard it with 60 grams of lard cut into thin strips seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Tie it so it remains extended and place it on the heat with 30 grams of butter, season with salt and pepper and brown it somewhat, then finish cooking it with meat sauce or broth poured in gradually.
3. When cooked, strain the remaining sauce and reduce it on the heat with a small piece of butter and less than half a tablespoon of flour to bind it to the tongue.
4. Serve sliced and surrounded by boiled vegetables finished with butter and the sauce.
