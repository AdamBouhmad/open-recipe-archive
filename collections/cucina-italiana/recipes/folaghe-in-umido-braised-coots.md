## Ingredients

- 4 folaghe (coots)
- 1 large onion
- sedano (celery)
- carota (carrot)
- prezzemolo (parsley)
- 80 grams butter
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- tomato sauce or tomato paste
- water
- 1.5 coot breast
- 40 grams butter
- Parmigiano cheese
- 3 eggs of pappardelle or 500 grams strips
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After plucking the coots and singing them by flame to remove the down, gut them and wash them well.
2. Pierce them lengthwise through the body with a hot spit, then cut them into four parts throwing away the head, feet and wing tips; then keep them soaking in vinegar for an hour and afterward wash them several times in fresh water.
3. Do the same with the gizzards after emptying, washing and cutting them into four pieces.
4. Make a finely chopped mixture with a large onion and all the herbs in proportion, that is celery, carrot and parsley, and put it on the fire with 80 grams of butter, and at the same time the coots and gizzards seasoning them with salt, pepper and spice flavor.
5. When they are dry bathe them with tomato sauce or paste dissolved in abundant water to cook them and so that much sauce remains.
6. Once cooked, strain the sauce and into it add a coot breast and a half finely chopped and another 40 grams of butter, to season with it and with Parmigiano cheese three eggs of pappardelle or 500 grams of strips which, for their particular taste, will be praised.
7. Serve the coots with some of their sauce afterward as a main dish that will not be despised.
8. This entire preparation should be enough for five or six persons.
