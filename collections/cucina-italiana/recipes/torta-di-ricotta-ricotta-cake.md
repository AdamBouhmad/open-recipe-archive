## Ingredients

- Ricotta, 500 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Sweet almonds, 150 grams
- Bitter almonds, 4 or 5
- Whole eggs, 4; egg yolks, 4
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Prepare it as the said Budino N. 663; but the almonds, after being pounded with one egg white, it is good to pass them through a sieve
2. Grease a pan abundantly with lard and line it with a sheet of pastry
