## Ingredients

- Lean veal, 200 grams
- Lean pork, 100 grams
- Butter, 60 grams
- Cooked prosciutto cut thick, 60 grams
- Salted tongue cut thick, 50 grams
- Bread marrow, 50 grams
- Chicken breast, 1
- Chicken liver, 1
- A lark or similar bird, 1
- Truffle, 1
- Marsala, one deciliter

## Directions

1. Put on the fire with the said butter and season with salt and pepper the veal, pork, and the bird (from which you will remove the beak and feet).
