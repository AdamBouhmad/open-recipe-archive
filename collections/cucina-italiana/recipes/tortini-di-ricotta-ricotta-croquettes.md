## Ingredients

- ricotta, 200 grams
- grated parmesan cheese, 50 grams
- flour, 30 grams
- eggs, 2
- chopped parsley, a pinch
- spices, to taste
- salt, as needed
- butter
- tomato sauce or conserve dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Form a mixture with the aforementioned ingredients, pour it onto a work surface over a light layer of flour and make it take up enough flour, with floured hands, to form twelve soft croquettes which you will flatten somewhat.
2. Put a sautè or a pan on the fire with a piece of butter to brown them, and when they have taken color on both sides wet them with tomato juice or conserve dissolved in water.
3. They can serve as a course and can be brought to the table accompanied by a steak or a piece of hot roast beef.
