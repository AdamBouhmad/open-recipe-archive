## Ingredients

- cooked goose liver
- 20 grams butter
- 1 heaping teaspoon flour
- 1 ladle broth
- 3 tablespoons Marsala wine

## Directions

1. After cooking in the way indicated (in recipe 548), remove it dry and bind it with a mixture made of 20 grams of butter put on the fire with a heaping teaspoon of flour and, when this has taken a hazelnut color, dilute it with a ladle of broth and three tablespoons of Marsala wine, pour in the liver, let it boil again for a bit and serve it.
