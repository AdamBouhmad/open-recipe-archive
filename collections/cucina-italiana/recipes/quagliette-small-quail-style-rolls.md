## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets, filled according to recipe N. 307, or veal of milk
- A very thin slice of lard
- Twine
- Croutons
- Sage leaves
- Oil
- Salt
- Broth

## Directions

1. Use filled veal cutlets from recipe N. 307, or make the wrapping with milk veal; when they are filled, wrap them with a very thin slice of lard and tie them crosswise with twine.
2. Thread them onto the spit to cook as a roast, each one between two croutons and with some sage leaves, oil them, salt them, moisten them with a few spoonfuls of broth and unwrap them when you serve them.
3. You can also obtain an excellent roast with beef tenderloin cut into pieces, wrapped with lard, with the aroma of sage and between two croutons.
