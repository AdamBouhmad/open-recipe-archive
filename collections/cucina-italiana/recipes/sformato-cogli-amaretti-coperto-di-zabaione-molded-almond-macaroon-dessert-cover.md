## Ingredients

- Amaretti biscuits, 100 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Potato starch, 80 grams
- Milk, half a liter
- Eggs, 3
- Cold butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Place the sugar and potato starch in a saucepan and gradually pour in the cold milk while stirring
2. Pound the amaretti in a mortar to reduce them to powder; if this does not happen due to their quality, moisten them with a few drops of milk, pass them through a sieve and then add them to the mixture
3. Put the mixture on the fire to firm it up
4. When removed from the fire and lukewarm, pour in the eggs, first the yolks, then the beaten whites
5. Grease a ring mold with cold butter and pour in the mixture to cook in a countryside oven
6. Once cooked, fill and cover it with the zabaglione from recipe no. 684 and send it to the table
