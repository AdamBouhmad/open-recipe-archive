## Ingredients

- Milk, 4 deciliters
- Semolina, 120 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 40 grams
- Eggs, 2 pieces
- Salt, as needed

## Directions

1. Cook the semolina in milk.
2. When you are about to remove it from the fire, salt it and add half the butter and half the Parmesan.
3. When it is still very hot, add the eggs and mix.
4. Pour it onto a work surface or a tray, spread it to the thickness of a finger and a half, and let it cool to cut it into almond shapes.
5. Arrange the gnocchi one on top of the other in a nice display in a proportional tray, interspersing them with pieces of the remaining butter, and dusting them layer by layer, but not on the surface, with the remaining Parmesan.
6. Finally, brown them in a country oven and serve them hot, either alone or as an accompaniment to a plate of stewed meat or prepared in another manner.
