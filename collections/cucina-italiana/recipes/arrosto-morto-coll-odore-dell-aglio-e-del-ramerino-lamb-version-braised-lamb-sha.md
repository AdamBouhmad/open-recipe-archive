## Ingredients

- lamb shanks
- oil
- butter
- salt
- broth
- garlic
- rosemary

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb shanks between two fires following the method for braised veal with garlic and rosemary, placing whole garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs in the pot during cooking.
