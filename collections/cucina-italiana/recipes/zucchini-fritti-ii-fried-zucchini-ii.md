## Ingredients

- Large, thick zucchini
- Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Take large and thick zucchini that cannot be grasped with one hand. Peel them to make the fried dish more beautiful, open them in two parts lengthwise and remove the pulp from the part that shows the seeds. Then cut them into long and thin strips, a finger's width wide and place them with salt to make water, leaving them there for several hours. When you are about to fry them take them up with both hands and squeeze them hard to press out the water that they still contain, then throw them into flour loosening them with your fingers, then into the small sieve and throw them immediately in a pan with plenty of fat.
