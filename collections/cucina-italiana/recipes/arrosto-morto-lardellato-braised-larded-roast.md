## Ingredients

- a short, thick piece of lean veal or beef, from the thigh or rump, about one kilogram
- 30 grams of fat and lean ham cut into thin slices
- 30 grams of butter
- one quarter of an onion divided into two pieces
- three or four celery ribs less than a finger length
- the same number of carrot strips
- salt
- pepper
- water
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Stud the meat with the ham slices using a larding needle.
2. Tie it with string to keep it compact and place it in a pot with butter, onion pieces, celery ribs and carrot strips.
3. Season with salt and pepper and when the meat has browned, turning it often, moisten with two small ladles of water.
4. Cook over gentle heat, allowing much of the moisture to reduce, but ensure it does not dry out and become black.
5. When serving, strain the small amount of remaining sauce and pour it over the meat, which can be surrounded with potato wedges browned in butter or oil.
6. Alternatively, you can put the braised roast on the fire with only butter and cook it in the covered pot with a bowl full of water placed on the lid.
