## Ingredients

- flour, 500 grams
- fine powdered sugar, 200 grams
- butter, 160 grams
- cracklings, 150 grams
- lard, 60 grams
- Marsala or white wine, 4 tablespoons
- eggs, 2 whole and 2 yolks
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Once you have formed the dough, working it little, add the cracklings chopped fine, grease a copper pan with lard and pour the dough in, pressing it with your knuckles so it comes out bumpy; but do not keep it higher than one finger.
2. Before passing it to the oven, make cuts if you want to serve it in pieces when cooked, square cuts with the point of a knife, repeating them halfway through cooking because they easily close, and when it is cooked dust it with powdered sugar.
