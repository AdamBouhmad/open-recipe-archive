## Ingredients

- eggs, 6
- fine powdered sugar, 170 grams
- Hungarian flour or finest flour, 170 grams
- lemon zest, to taste

## Directions

1. Beat first the egg yolks with the sugar, then add the flour, dried in the oven or sun, and after working it for about half an hour pour in two tablespoons of the six beaten egg whites to soften the mixture, then the rest mixing gently.
2. Bake it in the oven.
