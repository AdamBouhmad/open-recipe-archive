## Ingredients

- Powdered sugar, 150 grams
- Wheat flour, 100 grams
- Potato flour, 50 grams
- Sultana raisins, 80 grams
- Candied fruit, 20 grams
- Eggs, 5
- Lemon zest flavoring
- Rum or cognac, two tablespoons

## Directions

1. First place on the fire the raisins and candied fruit cut to the size of watermelon seeds with enough cognac or rum to cover them; when this boils, ignite it and let it burn outside the fire until the liquor is consumed; then remove this mixture and let it dry among the folds of a towel
2. After this operation, work very well with a wooden spoon for half an hour the sugar and egg yolks where you have placed the lemon zest
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff with a whisk, and pour them into the mixture; then add the two flours letting them fall through a small sieve and at the same time mix slowly and gently so that everything combines well, without working it too much
4. Finally add the raisins, candied fruit and the two tablespoons of rum or cognac mentioned and pour the mixture into a smooth mold or in a copper pot that gives the dessert a tall and round shape
5. Butter the mold and dust it with powdered sugar and flour, being careful to put it in the oven immediately to prevent the raisins and candied fruit from sinking to the bottom. If this happens, another time leave out one egg white
6. Serve cold
