## Ingredients

- Rice flour, 125 grams
- Sugar, 170 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Sweet almonds, 50 grams
- Shelled hazelnuts, 50 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Peel the hazelnuts with hot water as you would almonds, and dry both the hazelnuts and almonds well in the sun or by the fire. Then, after pounding them very finely, mix them with the rice flour using two tablespoons of the sugar.
2. Work the eggs well with the remaining sugar, then pour the almond and hazelnut mixture into it and stir very much with a whisk. Finally add the melted butter and work the mixture again.
3. Pour it into a smooth, rounded mold that is somewhat narrow so that it comes to a height of four or five fingers, and bake it in an oven at moderate heat. Serve it cold.
4. This amount will serve six or seven people.
