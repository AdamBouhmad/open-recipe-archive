## Ingredients

- Butter, 100 grams
- Flour, one spoonful
- Vinegar, one spoonful
- One egg yolk
- Salt and pepper
- Broth or water, as needed

## Directions

1. First place on the fire the flour with half the butter and when it has taken on a hazelnut color pour the broth or water little by little stirring with the spoon and, without letting it boil too much, add the rest of the butter and the vinegar
2. Remove from the fire, dissolve the egg yolk in it and serve it
3. Its consistency should be equal to that of cream made without flour
4. For a common bunch of asparagus, 70 grams of butter with the flour and vinegar in proportion may be sufficient
