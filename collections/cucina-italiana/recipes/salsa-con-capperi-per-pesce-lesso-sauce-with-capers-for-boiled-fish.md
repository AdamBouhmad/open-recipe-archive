## Ingredients

- Butter, 50 grams
- Capers pressed from vinegar, 50 grams
- Flour, 1 heaping teaspoon
- Salt, pepper and vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the fish and while you keep it warm in its broth, prepare the sauce.
2. Place the flour with half the butter on the fire, stir, and when it begins to take color add the remaining butter.
3. Let it boil a little then pour in a ladle of fish broth.
4. Season abundantly with salt and pepper and remove the pan from the fire.
5. Add the capers half whole and half chopped, plus a drop of vinegar; but taste to adjust the sauce so that it has good flavor and the density of a liquid cream.
6. Place the dried and hot fish in a serving dish, pour the sauce over it, also hot, surround it with whole parsley and serve it.
