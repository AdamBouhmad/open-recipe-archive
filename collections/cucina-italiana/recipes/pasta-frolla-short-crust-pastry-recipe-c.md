## Ingredients

- Flour, 270 grams
- Sugar, 115 grams
- Butter, 90 grams
- Lard, 45 grams
- Egg yolks, 4
- Orange zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Pound the sugar very fine (or use powdered sugar) and mix it with the flour
2. If the butter is firm, make it pasty by working it first with a wet hand on a work surface
3. Make a paste of everything, handling it as little as possible so it does not burn, as cooks say; use a knife blade to help mix it
4. You may prepare this paste one day in advance; it improves when cooked because it becomes more crumbly over time
5. When using it for pastries, tarts, cakes, etc., first thin it with a smooth rolling pin and then work the top part with a ridged rolling pin for beauty, brushing it with egg yolk
6. If you have leftover small pieces, unite them with a drop of white wine or Marsala, which also makes the paste more crumbly
