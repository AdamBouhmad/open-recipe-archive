## Ingredients

- clams or mussels
- onion
- oil
- pepper
- parsley
- tomato juice or tomato paste
- bread

## Directions

1. Make a fine mixture of onion and put it on the fire with oil and a pinch of pepper. When the onion has taken color, add a pinch of finely chopped parsley and shortly after add the clams or mussels with tomato juice or tomato paste. Shake them often and when they open, pour them over slices of toasted bread prepared beforehand on a platter.
