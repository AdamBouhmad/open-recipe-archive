## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 chicken livers
- some veal sweetbreads
- chicken or turkey gizzard, if available
- stale bread
- onion
- prosciutto
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg or spices
- flour
- meat gravy or broth
- 1 egg
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. If using gizzards, boil them first until half-cooked and remove the tender parts.
2. Mince all the organ meats, put them in a pan with a battuto of onion, prosciutto, and butter, and season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg or spices.
3. When it begins to brown, sprinkle in a scant teaspoon of flour, mix to incorporate, and moisten with meat gravy or broth.
4. Boil, then gradually add beaten egg while stirring constantly until the mixture sets.
5. Remove from heat, add a pinch of parmesan cheese, and pour into a dish.
6. Cut stale bread into thick slices about one centimeter, remove the crust, and cut into cubes about the size of a 10-centimeter coin.
7. Mound the filling generously on one side of each bread cube and half an hour before frying, dust with flour and spread on a tray.
8. Generously pour beaten egg over them so the bread absorbs it and the filling remains covered and well-coated.
9. Fry in a pan, starting with the filling side down.
