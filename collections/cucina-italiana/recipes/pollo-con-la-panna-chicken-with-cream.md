## Ingredients

- one piece of young chicken breast
- oil
- salt
- one quarter of a medium-sized onion
- 50 grams of butter
- 10 grams of flour
- three decilitres of cream, or if this is lacking, the same amount of very good milk

## Directions

1. Put a piece of young chicken breast on the spit to give it two-thirds of roasting time; oil it, salt it and let it take on color.
2. Then divide it at its joints and cut the breast into two pieces to finish cooking it in the following manner.
3. Chop a quarter of a medium-sized onion and put it on the fire with 50 grams of butter; when it is well browned, throw in 10 grams of flour and then, little by little, three decilitres of cream or, if this is lacking, the same amount of very good milk.
4. When you believe the flour is cooked, pour in the pieces of chicken to finish cooking them.
