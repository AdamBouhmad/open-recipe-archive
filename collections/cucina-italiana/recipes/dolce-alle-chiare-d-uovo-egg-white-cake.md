## Ingredients

- Egg whites, 8 or 9
- Hungarian flour, 300 grams
- Powdered sugar, 150 grams
- Butter, 150 grams
- Sultana raisins, 100 grams
- Cream of tartar, 10 grams
- Baking soda, 5 grams
- Vanilla sugar flavoring

## Directions

1. Whip the egg whites and pour into them the flour and sugar
2. Mix and then add the melted butter
3. When the mixture is fully combined, add the powders and lastly the raisins
4. Pour the mixture into a baking pan greased with butter and dusted with powdered sugar and flour, where the cake reaches at least two fingers high
5. Bake it in the oven or in a country oven and serve it cold
