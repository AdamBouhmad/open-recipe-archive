## Ingredients

- Fine lamb chops
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Béchamel, thick
- Prosciutto
- Salted tongue
- Parmesan cheese
- Nutmeg
- Truffle or softened and chopped dried mushrooms
- Puff pastry
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Take fine lamb chops, denude the bone of the chop, flatten them, trim them, cook them naturally in butter sautéed, season them hot with salt and pepper and set them aside.
2. Make a thick béchamel and in it throw prosciutto and salted tongue into very small dice, a pinch of Parmesan, a pinch of nutmeg and a truffle sliced or softened and chopped dried mushrooms, and set this mixture aside so it cools well.
3. Make a puff pastry proportioned to the quantity of chops and with it wrap them one by one, leaving the bone of the chop exposed, but first spread them abundantly on one side and the other with the said mixture.
4. When you have closed them, gild them with egg yolk, place them upright around the edge of a baking dish, cook them in a country oven and serve them hot.
5. You can cut the puff pastry with a paper template, so the wrapping will be more precise; for more neatness and elegance, before sending them to the table, wrap the end of each chop with scalloped white paper.
