## Ingredients

- Cornmeal, 200 grams
- Wheat flour, 150 grams
- Powdered sugar, 150 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Lard (rendered fat), 50 grams
- Anise, 10 grams
- Eggs, 1

## Directions

1. Mix together the two flours, sugar, and anise and knead with the butter, lard, and egg, making as much dough as you can, forming it into a loaf that you set aside
2. Mix the remaining dough with a little white wine and a little water and form another loaf, then combine the two loaves and work them as little as possible, ensuring the paste is rather soft
3. Roll it out with a rolling pin to the thickness of half a finger, dusting it with mixed flour so it does not stick to the work surface, and cut it with tin cutters into different shapes and sizes
4. Grease a baking pan with lard, dust it with flour, and place the cookies on it, brush them with egg, bake them in the oven, and dust them with powdered sugar
