## Ingredients

- one wild duck (Anas boscas), about one kilogram
- 30 grams of prosciutto, fat and lean together
- celery, parsley, carrot, one quarter of a large onion
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- water
- black cabbage or whole lentils for accompaniment

## Directions

1. When you buy a wild duck in the market, open its beak to observe the tongue.
2. If you find it very dried out, you can say that the animal has been dead for a long time, and then smell it to make sure it does not stink.
3. Empty the duck, keeping the liver, heart and onion; remove its head and the skin of the neck, after having opened it to extract the vertebrae, fold it back over the animal's breast.
4. For a duck that weighs about one kilogram, finely chop 30 grams of prosciutto, fat and lean together, with all the aromatics, that is celery, parsley, carrot and a quarter of a large onion.
5. Put everything together with oil in a casserole and on top of the mixture place the duck, seasoning it with salt and pepper.
6. Let it take on color from all sides and then add water to cook it through.
7. Cook the accompaniment of black cabbage or whole lentils in water and make it over in the aforementioned sauce: taste it to add, if necessary, a small piece of butter, which will make it more agreeable and flavorful, and combine it with the duck when you send it to the table.
8. Cut the cabbage coarsely and season it as well with salt and pepper.
