## Ingredients

- Bread sticks or bread slices
- Butter
- Truffles, prepared as described in recipe 269
- Sauce from truffle preparation

## Directions

1. Take bread sticks and cut them into diagonal slices, or prepare bread slices in an elegant form
2. Lightly toast them and brush them with butter while hot
3. Spread the truffles prepared according to recipe 269 over them
4. Drizzle with the sauce that remains
