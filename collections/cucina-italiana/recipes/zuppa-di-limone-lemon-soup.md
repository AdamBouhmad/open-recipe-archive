## Ingredients

- Sugar, 135 grams
- Egg yolks, 2
- Egg whites, 5
- Juice of one large lemon
- Water, half a glass
- Flour, one scant tablespoon
- Savoiardi biscuits or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Dilute the flour with water, whisking it well, pour it into a pan and add the rest.
2. Stir everything together and place the mixture on the fire, stirring continuously with the spoon as you do for cream.
3. When it is thickened, pass it through a sieve if necessary, then pour part of it into a dish, spread savoiardi or sponge cake over it, and cover it with the rest of the mixture.
4. Serve it cold.
