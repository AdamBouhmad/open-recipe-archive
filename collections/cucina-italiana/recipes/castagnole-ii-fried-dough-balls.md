## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two tablespoons of water
- two tablespoons of meat stock
- butter, 20 grams
- sugar, 20 grams
- a pinch of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Put the egg yolks, sugar, meat stock, water and salt in a bowl.
2. Mix, beat the egg whites and with these ingredients and the butter knead enough flour on a work surface to form a dough that can be worked with your hands.
3. Work it thoroughly to refine it, then make small balls the size of a small walnut and fry them as the previous ones over low heat in plenty of fat.
