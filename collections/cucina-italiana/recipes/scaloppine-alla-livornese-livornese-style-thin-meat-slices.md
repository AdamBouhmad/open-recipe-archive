## Ingredients

- thick meat slices (bracioline)
- butter
- broth
- salt and pepper
- flour
- marsala wine
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take thick meat slices and beat them well to tenderize them
2. Throw them in a pan with a piece of butter
3. When they have absorbed the butter, wet them with a few spoonfuls of broth to complete cooking
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Bind them with a pinch of flour
6. Give them the flavor of marsala
7. Before removing from heat, make them more agreeable with a pinch of chopped parsley
