## Ingredients

- cavolo nero (black cabbage)
- prosciutto (ham)
- cipolla (onion)
- burro (butter)
- brodo (broth)
- pepe (pepper)
- sale (salt)
- olio (oil)
- aglio (garlic)
- pane (bread)

## Directions

1. Remove the hard ribs, boil it and mince it finely.
2. If you do not have meat gravy, make a small beaten mixture of ham and onion, put it on the fire with a piece of butter and when the onion is well browned, wet it with a drop of broth and pass the gravy formed.
3. In it throw the cabbage, season with pepper, little or no salt, add another piece of butter and more broth, if needed, and serve it as a side dish to boiled meat or coteghino.
4. Some use for a soup to roast slices of bread thick a finger, rub them with garlic, dip them barely in the water in which the black cabbage has boiled, placing the cabbage itself on it, still hot, and seasoning it with salt, pepper and oil. This, which is called in Florence cabbage with slices, is a dish for Carthusians or to be inflicted as penance on a glutton.
