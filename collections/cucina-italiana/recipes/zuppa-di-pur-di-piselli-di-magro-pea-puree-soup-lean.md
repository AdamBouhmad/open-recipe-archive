## Ingredients

- Fresh shelled peas, 400 grams
- Fatty and lean prosciutto, 40 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- One young onion no larger than an egg
- One small carrot
- A pinch of parsley, celery and some basil leaves
- Bread fried in butter
- Salt and pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Finely chop the prosciutto with a knife and make a battuto with this and the other ingredients.
2. Put it on the fire with butter, a little salt and a pinch of pepper.
3. When it has browned, pour the water you judge sufficient to make the soup and when it has come to a boil throw in the peas to cook them together with two slices of bread fried in butter.
4. Then pass everything through a sieve.
5. Obtained in this way a puree for six people, dampen with the same the bread that you will have already prepared as in the fat purees.
