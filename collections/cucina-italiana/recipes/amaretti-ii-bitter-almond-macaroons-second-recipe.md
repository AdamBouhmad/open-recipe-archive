## Ingredients

- Powdered white sugar, 300 grams
- Sweet almonds, 180 grams
- Bitter almonds, 20 grams
- Egg whites, 2

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and dry them in the sun or by fire; then pound them fine in a mortar with one egg white poured in several times.
2. Having done this, mix in half the sugar, working the mixture with a hand.
3. After pour it into a bowl and, always working so it incorporates, add half an egg white, then the other half of the sugar and afterwards the last half egg white.
4. You will obtain, thus worked, a homogeneous mixture of proper consistency that you can shape into a stick to cut into equal pieces.
5. Pick them up one by one with slightly dampened hands to form balls about the size of walnuts.
6. Flatten them to the thickness of one centimeter and for the rest proceed as for the previous ones, but sprinkle them lightly with powdered sugar before putting them in a hot oven, and I say oven because a country oven would not be suitable for this paste.
7. With this amount you will obtain about thirty macaroons.
