## Ingredients

- Green beans or zucchini
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Meat gravy or tomato sauce from recipe 125

## Directions

1. If it is green beans, boil them halfway; if zucchini leave them raw cut into wedges or small pieces.
2. Put them to brown in butter when this, boiling, will have taken a hazelnut color.
3. After cooked in this manner you may add a little meat gravy or a little tomato sauce from recipe 125, which will no longer follow the rules of foreign or modern cuisine; but you will feel, in my opinion, a better taste and the stomach will be more satisfied.
