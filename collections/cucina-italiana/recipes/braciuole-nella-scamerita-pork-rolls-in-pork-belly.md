## Ingredients

- Pork rolls (from scamerita)
- Oil
- Garlic cloves with skin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Red wine
- Kale, blanched
- Water

## Directions

1. Place the rolls in a pan with very little oil, two or three cloves of garlic with their skin, slightly crushed, and season with salt and pepper.
2. When they have taken color on both sides, pour two or three fingers of a glass of red wine into the pan and let it reduce by half as it boils.
3. Set the rolls aside dry, keeping them warm.
4. In that liquid, adjust the seasoning of the blanched kale, pressed of water, cut not too finely and also seasoned with salt and pepper.
5. Serve at table with the kale underneath.
