## Ingredients

- Cooked chicken breast, 50 grams
- Salted tongue, 40 grams
- Fatty and lean prosciutto, 30 grams
- Parmesan, one spoonful
- One small truffle or nutmeg flavoring
- Milk, 2 deciliters
- Butter, 30 grams
- Flour, 30 grams
- Wafers or host

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the chicken and cut it into very small dice, doing the same with the tongue and prosciutto; slice the truffle into thin strips.
2. Make a béchamel sauce with the milk, butter, and flour.
3. When the béchamel is cooked, pour in the aforementioned ingredients and let it cool well before using the wafers as in the preceding recipe.
