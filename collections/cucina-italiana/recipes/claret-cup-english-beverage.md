## Ingredients

- Red wine of excellent quality, 5 decilitres
- Water, 5 decilitres
- Lemons, 5 number
- White sugar, 500 grams

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in the water for five minutes.
2. Removed from the fire, squeeze the lemons into this syrup and pour in the wine, then strain it through a cloth.
3. Return it to the fire to boil slowly for 25 minutes and when it is cold bottle it.
4. Use it diluted with water and chilled with ice in summer.
5. If you need to keep it for a long time, keep it in the cellar.
