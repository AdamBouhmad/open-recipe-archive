## Ingredients

- broccoli di rapa (turnip greens)
- aglio (garlic)
- olio (oil)
- sale (salt)
- pepe (pepper)
- olio e aceto (oil and vinegar)
- lardo (lard)

## Directions

1. Clean the broccoli from the hardest leaves, boil them, squeeze out the water and cut them roughly.
2. Mince two or three garlic cloves or leave them whole, and once they have taken color with abundant oil in a pan, add the broccoli.
3. Season with salt and pepper, stir often and let them fry well.
4. They can be served as a side dish to boiled meat or eaten alone.
5. If you prefer, boil them and season with oil and vinegar.
6. In countries where oil is not perfect, you can substitute with lard; in fact, to my taste, fried with this they are better.
