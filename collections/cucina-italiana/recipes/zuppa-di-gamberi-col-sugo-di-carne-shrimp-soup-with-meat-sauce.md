## Ingredients

- Shrimp, 150 grams (for four people)
- Broth, two ladles
- Bone marrow, 30 grams, browned in butter
- Meat sauce (recipe N. 4) or meat for making it, 30 centesimi worth
- Bread, simply roasted or in cubes, fried in lard or oil
- Grated Parmesan

## Directions

1. Wash the shrimp and put them on the fire with two ladles of broth; once cooked, remove them dry and in the remaining liquid dissolve 30 grams of bone marrow browned in butter, to moisten them when you pass them through the sieve, after having pounded them in the mortar
2. Once all the pulp has been extracted, unite it to meat sauce like that of recipe N. 4 and if you do not have it in the kitchen you can make it with only 30 centesimi worth of meat suitable for that use
3. Mix now this mixture with the rest of the broth to moisten the soup, which can be simply roasted bread, or in cubes, fried in lard or oil
4. Serve it with grated Parmesan
