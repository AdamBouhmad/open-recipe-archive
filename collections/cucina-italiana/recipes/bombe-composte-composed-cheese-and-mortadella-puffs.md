## Ingredients

- Water, 180 grams
- Flour, 120 grams
- Gruyère cheese, 30 grams
- Butter, size of a walnut
- Mortadella from Bologna, 30 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Salt, a pinch

## Directions

1. Put water on the fire with butter and salt
2. When it begins to boil, throw in the cheese in small pieces and immediately all the flour at once, stirring vigorously
3. Keep the paste on the fire about 10 minutes, stirring always, then let it cool
4. Work it much and in stages with a spoon, adding one egg at a time, first the yolk then the beaten white
5. When about to fry it, add the mortadella in dice about one centimeter wide
6. If the mixture turns out a bit too firm due to the flour quality or because the eggs were small, add another egg
7. You will obtain enough for six people
8. If these puffs turn out well, you will see them puff up and remain empty inside
9. Serve hot as a side to fried meat or liver, or mixed with any other fried dish
