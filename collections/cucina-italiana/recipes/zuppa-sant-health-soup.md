## Ingredients

- Various vegetables, for example carrots, sorrel, celery, and white cabbage
- Salt
- Pepper
- A piece of butter
- Broth
- Fine, day-old bread (at least one day old), cut into small cubes
- Butter or virgin oil or lard for frying the bread

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage like taglierini and make it release water over the fire, squeezing it well
2. Cut the carrots and celery into strips about three centimeters long, and together with the cabbage and sorrel cleaned of stems, put them on the fire with a little salt, a pinch of pepper, and a piece of butter
3. When the vegetables have drawn out the fat, finish cooking them with broth
4. Meanwhile prepare the bread, which should be of fine quality and day-old at least; cut it into small cubes and fry it in butter or also in virgin oil or lard; but so that it absorbs little fat, keep the latter abundant and throw in the bread when it is boiling well, or roast it only as thick slices and cut it into cubes afterward
5. Put the bread in the soup tureen, pour over it the boiling broth together with the vegetables, and send the soup immediately to table
