## Ingredients

- Short crust pastry (half the recipe A of number 589, or the entire recipe B of the same number)
- Ricotta cheese, 150 grams
- Sweet almonds with three bitter ones, 70 grams
- Sugar, 50 grams
- Flour, 20 grams
- Eggs, 1 whole and 1 yolk
- Lemon zest or vanilla flavoring
- Milk, half a glass

## Directions

1. Make a cream with the milk, sugar, flour, and the whole egg mentioned, and when it is cooked and still boiling add the yolk and give it flavor
2. Add to the cream the ricotta and the blanched and finely pounded almonds
3. Mix everything and fill with this mixture the short crust pastry arranged like a tart, that is between two sheets of the same pastry decorated on top and brushed with egg yolk
4. It should be baked in the oven, served cold and dusted with powdered sugar
