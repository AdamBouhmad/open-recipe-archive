## Ingredients

- Slightly underripe small pears, 600 grams
- Sugar, 120 grams
- Water, two glasses
- Half a lemon

## Directions

1. Squeeze the lemon into the water and set it aside
2. Place the pears on the fire covered with water and boil them for four or five minutes; then throw them into fresh water, peel them, cut half of their stem off, and as you peel them drop them into the water prepared with lemon
3. Having done this, take the same water, pass it through a strainer, put it on the fire and when it begins to boil pour in the sugar and let it boil somewhat
4. Then throw in the pears to cook them, but in such a way that they do not fall apart
5. Remove them dry, place them in a compote dish, reduce the syrup and pour it over the pears, passing it through the strainer once more
6. If they are very large pears, cut them into wedges after they have undergone the first boiling
7. Serve them cold
