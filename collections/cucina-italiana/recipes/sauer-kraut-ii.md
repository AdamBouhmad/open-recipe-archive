## Ingredients

- Head of cabbage or savoy cabbage
- Onion
- Cured meat
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Take a head of cabbage or savoy cabbage, cut it into strips about one centimeter wide and keep it soaked in fresh water.
2. Remove it from the water without squeezing it and press it in a pot over the fire to release its water, which you will then drain by squeezing it with a spoon.
3. Make a mixture with a quarter of a large onion, some finely chopped cured meat, and a piece of butter; when it takes color, add the said cabbage with a piece of whole cured meat in the middle, which you will remove later, and season with salt and pepper.
4. Let it boil slowly, moistening it with broth to cook it through and finally add a little vinegar and a teaspoon of sugar, but in such a way that the vinegar is barely noticeable.
