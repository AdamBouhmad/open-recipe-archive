## Ingredients

- Currants, 500 grams
- Sugar, 300 grams
- Black cherries, 150 grams
- Water, half a liter
- One large garden lemon

## Directions

1. Crush the currants and cherries with your hands, add the lemon juice and pass everything through a sieve, pressing well.
2. Boil the sugar in the water for 10 minutes in an uncovered saucepan to obtain the syrup, and when it is cold mix it into the described mixture and pour it into the sorbetierre. It will be enough for seven or eight people, serving it in small glasses. The cherries, besides their special flavor, serve to give the ice cream a more beautiful color.
