## Ingredients

- Raspberry syrup (recipe 723), 2 deciliters
- Isinglass, 20 grams
- Water, 3 deciliters
- Marsala, 1 deciliter
- Rum, one tablespoon

## Directions

1. Dissolve 20 grams of isinglass on the fire in three deciliters of water and mix into it the raspberry syrup, Marsala and rum.
2. Sugar is not necessary or, if at all, very little, as the syrup is very sweet.
3. Proceed as in the previous gelatins.
