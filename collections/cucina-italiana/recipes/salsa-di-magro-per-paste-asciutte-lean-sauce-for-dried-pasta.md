## Ingredients

- Spaghetti, 500 grams
- Fresh mushrooms, 100 grams
- Butter, 70 grams
- Pine nuts, 60 grams
- Salted anchovies, 6
- Tomatoes, 7 or 8
- One quarter of a large onion
- Flour, one teaspoon

## Directions

1. Place half the butter in a pot and with it brown the pine nuts
2. Remove the pine nuts dried and pound them in a mortar with the indicated flour
3. Mince the onion very finely, place it in the remaining sauce and when it has taken on much color, add the tomatoes in pieces
4. Season with pepper and little salt, and when they are cooked, pass them through a sieve
5. Return the sauce to the fire with the mushrooms cut into thin slices no larger than a pumpkin seed, the pine nut paste which you can first dissolve with a little water, and the rest of the butter
6. Bring to a boil for half an hour, adding water to make the sauce more liquid, and lastly dissolve the anchovies on the fire with a little of this sauce, without letting them boil, and combine them with it
7. Remove the spaghetti dried, season it with this sauce and if you want it better add parmesan cheese
8. Serves five people
