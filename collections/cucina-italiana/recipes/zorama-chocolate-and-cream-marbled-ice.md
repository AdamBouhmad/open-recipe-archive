## Ingredients

- Colla di pesce (fish gelatin), 3 leaves
- Zucchero (sugar), 100 grams
- Cioccolata in polvere (chocolate powder), 80 grams
- Rossi d'uovo (egg yolks), 3 number
- Latte (milk), 3 deciliters
- Chiare d'uovo (egg whites), 3 number
- Panna montata (whipped cream), as that which dairymen prepare, 150 grams
- Acqua (water), small amount
- Rosolio (rosoli liqueur)

## Directions

1. First soak in fresh water three leaves of fish gelatin and meanwhile prepare a cream with sugar, chocolate powder, egg yolks, and milk
2. When cooled unite to it the three beaten egg whites and after 150 grams of whipped cream, as that which dairymen prepare, mixing in such a way that the white of this appears scattered here and there
3. Then dissolve on the fire in a small amount of water the said three leaves of fish gelatin and this liquid thus hot scatter it over the mixture mixing
4. Then pour it into the ice cream mold or another vessel moistened with rosoli and closed hermetically, keeping it for three or four hours surrounded and covered with much ice mixed with salt
5. It should be enough for eight persons
