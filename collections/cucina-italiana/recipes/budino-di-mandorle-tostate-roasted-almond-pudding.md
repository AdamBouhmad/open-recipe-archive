## Ingredients

- Milk, 8 deciliters or 800 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Ladyfinger cookies, 60 grams
- Sweet almonds, 60 grams
- Eggs, 3

## Directions

1. First prepare the almonds, that is blanch them in hot water and roast them on the fire over a stone or iron slab; then pound them reducing them almost to powder and, putting the mixture on the fire without the eggs, add the almonds to it and after a short cooking pass it through a sieve
2. Now add the beaten eggs and set it in a bain-marie with a layer of melted sugar on the bottom of the mold
3. No flavoring is needed
4. The roasting of the almonds will give this pudding a grayish color and will give it a flavor so pleasant as to deserve the praise of men and even more that of women of delicate taste
5. Both this and the chocolate pudding can be put on ice before serving them, and also, to give them a more beautiful appearance, they can be covered with a cream adorned with colored confetti, or with whipped cream
