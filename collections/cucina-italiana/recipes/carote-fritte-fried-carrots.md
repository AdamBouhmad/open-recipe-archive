## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Salt
- Flour
- Egg

## Directions

1. Without peeling them, cut them into thin fillets the length of a finger
2. Salt them and after a few hours, taking them up still moist, pass them through flour and shake them free of it
3. Place them in egg, turn them in it, and taking the fillets one by one with your fingers, throw them into the pan
4. These carrots can serve as a side dish for a fry when there are no more zucchini
