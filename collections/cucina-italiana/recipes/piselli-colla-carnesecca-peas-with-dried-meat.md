## Ingredients

- Dried meat (carnesecca)
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth or water

## Directions

1. Put on the fire a fine mixture of dried meat, garlic, parsley and oil; season with little salt and pepper, and when the garlic has taken on color, throw in the peas.
2. Once they have absorbed the fat, finish cooking them with broth or, in its absence, with water.
