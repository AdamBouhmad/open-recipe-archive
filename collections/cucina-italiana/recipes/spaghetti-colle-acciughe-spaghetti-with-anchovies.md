## Ingredients

- Medium spaghetti, 350 grams
- Five anchovies
- Good oil in abundance
- A pinch of pepper
- 50 grams of butter
- Tomato juice or conserve

## Directions

1. Wash the anchovies and clean them well of spines and bones, chop them somewhat with the crescent blade and put them on the fire with good oil in abundance and a pinch of pepper
2. Do not let them boil, but when they begin to heat, add 50 grams of butter, a little tomato juice or conserve and lift them from the fire
3. Dress the spaghetti cooked in lightly salted water with this sauce, making sure they remain firm
