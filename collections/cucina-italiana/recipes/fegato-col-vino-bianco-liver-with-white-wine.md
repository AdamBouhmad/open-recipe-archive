## Ingredients

- liver
- oil
- butter
- flour
- one tablespoon
- excellent and dry white wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into thin slices and fry it in a pan with oil and butter as is.
2. Beat a tablespoon of flour with excellent dry white wine in a small pot to form a very liquid mixture.
3. When the liver is two-thirds cooked, pour this mixture over it.
4. Finish cooking and season with salt and pepper.
