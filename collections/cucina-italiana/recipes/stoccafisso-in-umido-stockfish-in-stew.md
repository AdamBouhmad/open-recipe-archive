## Ingredients

- Stockfish, soaked, 500 grams (300 grams backbone, 200 grams belly)
- oil, in abundance
- garlic, 1 large clove or 2 small cloves
- parsley, a good pinch
- salt and pepper
- tomato sauce or fresh tomatoes without skin and seeds, 6-7 spoonfuls
- water, hot
- potato, cut into small pieces

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and all spines from the stockfish.
2. Cut the backbone part into thin slices and the belly into squares two fingers wide.
3. Make a soffritto with abundant oil, garlic, and parsley.
4. When colored, add the stockfish and season with salt and pepper.
5. Stir to allow it to absorb flavor, then add tomato sauce or fresh tomatoes without skin and seeds.
6. Boil gently for at least three hours, adding hot water little by little.
7. After two hours of boiling, add a potato cut into small pieces.
8. This quantity is sufficient for three or four people.
9. This is an appetizing dish, but not for weak stomachs.
