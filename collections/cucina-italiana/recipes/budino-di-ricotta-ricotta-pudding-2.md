## Ingredients

- Ricotta cheese, 300 grams
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Sweet almonds, 100 grams and three or four bitter almonds
- Eggs, 5
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds in hot water and pound them very finely in a mortar with one of the egg whites mentioned above. Mix them well with the ricotta, passing this first through a sieve if it was too hard or lumpy.
