## Ingredients

- Cornmeal, 300 grams
- Wheat flour, 100 grams
- Raisins, 100 grams
- Sugar, 50 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Lard, 30 grams
- Beer yeast, 20 grams
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. With half the wheat flour and the beer yeast, make a dough moistened with tepid water and set it to rise
2. Meanwhile, knead with hot water the two flours mixed together with all the above ingredients, except the raisins
3. Add the risen dough to the mixture when it is ready, work it somewhat, and finally add the raisins
4. Divide it into fifteen or sixteen parts forming as many loaves in the shape of a spool, and with the back of a knife incise on the surface of each an almond-shaped lattice
5. Set them to rise in a warm place, then bake them in the oven or a country oven at moderate heat so they remain tender
