## Ingredients

- Two young onions or three to four small white onions
- Butter
- Lamb
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Broth

## Directions

1. Slice the onions and sauté them in a piece of butter until they take on a deep red color
2. Add the lamb and season with salt and pepper
3. Wait for the meat to begin to color and add another piece of butter dusted with flour; stir and let it take on a nice color
4. Finish cooking the meat with broth added little by little
5. Serve not dry, but with a certain amount of its gravy
