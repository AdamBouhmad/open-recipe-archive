## Ingredients

- One pigeon
- One veal cutlet or milk veal cutlet
- Salt
- Pepper
- A pinch of spices
- Butter
- Truffles (optional)
- The pigeon's liver and heart
- One chicken (as alternative to pigeon)

## Directions

1. If you have a pigeon to put on the spit and want it to serve more than one person, fill it with a veal cutlet or milk veal cutlet of proportionate size.
2. Beat the cutlet well to make it thinner and more tender, season it with salt, pepper, a pinch of spices and some pieces of butter, roll it up and place it inside the pigeon, sewing the opening closed.
3. If you add slices of truffles to the seasoning mentioned above, it will be even better.
4. You can also cook separately the small onion and the pigeon's liver in sauce or butter, mash them and use them to spread on the cutlet; in this way the different aroma of the two types of meat blends together and forms a better flavor.
5. What has been said for the pigeon applies equally to a chicken.
