## Ingredients

- white celery ribs
- butter
- béchamel sauce (recipe 137)
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Use white celery ribs and cut them into pieces about two centimeters long.
2. Boil them for five minutes in salted water and then sauté them in butter.
3. Bind them with the béchamel sauce (recipe 137), kept rather firm, and flavor with parmesan cheese.
