## Ingredients

- Butter of good quality and very fresh, 180 grams
- Powdered sugar, 180 grams
- Long savoiardi or sponge cake, approximately 150 grams
- Hard-boiled egg yolks, 6
- Vanilla sugar, enough to give it the flavor
- Rosolio liqueur, as much as needed to lightly dip the savoiardi

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for only seven minutes and remove the yolks, dissolve them in the butter, then pass them through a sieve, then add the powdered sugar and the vanilla sugar, and work the mixture well with a spoon to whip it
2. Take a mold, preferably one with ribs, dampen it with rosolio, cut in half lengthwise the savoiardi, dip them lightly in the rosolio, or half in rosolio and half in alkermes and with these line the mold alternating the two colors
3. Then pour the mixture in the middle, cover it with other savoiardi dipped in the same way, leave it in the ice for at least three hours and serve it
4. The mixture, if convenient, can be made a day ahead and this quantity is enough for eight people. It is a very refined pudding
