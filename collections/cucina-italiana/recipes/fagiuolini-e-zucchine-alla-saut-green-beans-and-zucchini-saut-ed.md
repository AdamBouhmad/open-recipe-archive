## Ingredients

- Green beans or zucchini
- Butter
- Salt and pepper, in small quantity

## Directions

1. If it is green beans, boil them halfway; if zucchini leave them raw cut into wedges or small pieces.
2. Put them to brown in butter when this, boiling, will have taken a hazelnut color.
3. For seasoning put only salt and pepper in small quantity.
