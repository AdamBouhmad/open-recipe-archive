## Ingredients

- Flour, 400 grams
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Butter, 80 grams
- Almonds, 40 grams
- Sultana raisins, 30 grams
- Pine nuts, 20 grams
- Citron or candied squash, 20 grams
- Anise, a pinch
- Spirits of wine, 2 tablespoons
- Baking soda, a scant teaspoon
- One whole egg and three yolks

## Directions

1. These biscuits are finer than the previous ones.
2. Blanch the almonds, and leave both these and the pine nuts whole.
3. Cut the candied fruit into small pieces.
4. Make a well in the mound of flour and place the eggs, sugar, butter, spirits, and baking soda in it.
5. Mix the dough without working it too much, then open it and spread it to add the rest.
6. Roll out a somewhat compressed stick about one meter long, divide it into four or five parts so it fits in the baking sheet.
7. Gild it and bake it in the oven.
8. Once baked, cut it in the shape of biscuits into slices slightly thicker than one centimeter.
9. Toast them lightly on both sides.
