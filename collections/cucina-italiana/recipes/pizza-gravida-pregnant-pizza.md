## Ingredients

- Milk, one quarter liter
- Sugar, 60 grams
- Cornstarch, 30 grams
- Egg yolks, 2 numbers
- Flavoring of your preference
- Whole pine nuts, 30 grams
- Raisins, 80 grams
- Short crust pastry

## Directions

1. Prepare a cream using the milk, sugar, cornstarch, egg yolks, and flavoring
2. When you remove it from the fire, add the whole pine nuts and raisins
3. Fill a short crust pastry with this mixture as you did for pizza alla napoletana
4. Cook it as you did the previous one
