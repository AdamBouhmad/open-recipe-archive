## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Prosciutto, more fat than lean
- Parsley
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Truffles (optional)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Lemon wedges

## Directions

1. After baring the bone of the cutlet and discarding the trimmings, flatten them with the blade of a large knife to widen and thin them.
2. Then make a mixture with prosciutto more fat than lean, a little parsley, grated Parmesan cheese, the aroma of truffles if you have them, and a little salt and pepper.
3. Spread this mixture on the cutlets on one side only, dip them in egg, then bread them and cook them sauté in butter, serving them with lemon wedges.
4. For five cutlets, if they are not very large, 50 grams of prosciutto and two heaping tablespoons of Parmesan will suffice.
