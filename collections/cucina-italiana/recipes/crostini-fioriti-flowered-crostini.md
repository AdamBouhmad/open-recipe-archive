## Ingredients

- Fine marrow bread, 1 centimeter thick
- Fresh butter
- Parsley leaves
- Anchovy fillets

## Directions

1. Cut slices of the finest marrow bread to the thickness of one centimeter, giving it the shape of almonds or small squares.
2. Spread them with fresh butter and lay two or three leaves of parsley on top, surrounding them with anchovy fillets in the form of little snakes.
