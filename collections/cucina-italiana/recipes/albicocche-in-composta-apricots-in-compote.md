## Ingredients

- Slightly underripe apricots, 600 grams
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Water, one glass

## Directions

1. Make an incision in the apricots to remove the pit in such a way that they do not spoil, and place them on the fire with the aforementioned water
2. When this begins to boil, pour in the sugar and, shaking the saucepan from time to time, let them cook
3. Once they become soft and somewhat wrinkled, remove them one by one with a spoon and place them in a compote dish; drain the liquid they have brought from the saucepan and let the remaining water reduce on the fire, which you will pour into the compote dish over the apricots when it has thickened like a syrup
4. Serve them cold
