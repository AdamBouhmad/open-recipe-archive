## Ingredients

- 200 grams of sorrel
- One head of lettuce
- Salt
- 30 grams of butter
- Two egg yolks
- Broth
- Bread cut into cubes and fried

## Directions

1. After soaking these vegetables, drain them well, cut them into strips and put them on the fire
2. When they are cooked, season them with a pinch of salt and 30 grams of butter
3. Put in the soup tureen two egg yolks with a little lukewarm broth, add the said vegetables and then, little by little while stirring, add all the necessary boiling broth for the soup
4. Throw in the bread cut into cubes and fried, and serve at table with Parmesan on the side
