## Ingredients

- Raspberries
- Sugar
- Citric acid

## Directions

1. After mashing the fruit well with your hands, proceed the same way as the currant syrup recipe with the same proportions of sugar and citric acid
2. Since this fruit contains less gluten than currants, the fermentation period will be shorter
