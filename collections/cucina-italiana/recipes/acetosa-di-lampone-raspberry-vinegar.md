## Ingredients

- Raspberry syrup
- High-quality wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Form this vinegar by substituting excellent quality wine vinegar for citric acid, which is poured into the raspberry syrup when it is removed from the fire
2. Regulate the dose of vinegar by tasting, putting in a little at first and tasting the syrup in a little water, increasing the dose as needed
3. This beverage will be more refreshing than the others and equally pleasant
