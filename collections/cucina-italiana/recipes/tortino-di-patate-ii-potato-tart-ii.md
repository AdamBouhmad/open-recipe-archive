## Ingredients

- Potatoes, 500 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Flour, 30 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Parmesan cheese, two tablespoons
- Milk, as needed
- Salt, to taste

## Directions

1. Make a béchamel sauce with the flour, half the butter and necessary milk
2. Pour the already cooked and passed potatoes into it
3. Work over the fire, adding the remaining butter, salt and enough additional milk to make a not too soft paste
4. Let cool
5. Add the remaining ingredients
6. Brown the mixture as in the previous recipe
