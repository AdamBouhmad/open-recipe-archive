## Ingredients

- mantis shrimp
- fresh water
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- tomato juice or conserve
- bread
- scissors

## Directions

1. Before cooking, keep the mantis shrimp in fresh water so they don't drain and instead swell.
2. Make a battuto with garlic, parsley and oil; once browned, place the whole mantis shrimp in it and season with salt and pepper.
3. When they have taken on the seasoning, wet them with tomato juice or conserve and serve them on slices of bread dried over the fire.
4. Before bringing them to the table, make an incision with scissors along the back so they can be peeled more easily.
