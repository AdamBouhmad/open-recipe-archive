## Ingredients

- Ferrara salami
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the salami several times with tepid water to clean them of the fatty patina that covers them
2. Place them in a pot with abundant cold water
3. Boil them slowly for one and a half hours only, tied tightly in a cloth to prevent the skin from splitting
4. Serve them hot with side dishes like coteghini
