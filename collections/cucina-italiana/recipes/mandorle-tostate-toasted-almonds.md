## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds, 200 grams
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Water

## Directions

1. Rub the almonds with a cloth
2. Put the sugar in a non-tinned pan on the fire with two fingers' width of water and when it is dissolved pour in the almonds, stirring constantly
3. When you feel them crackling remove the pan to the edge of the stove and you will see the sugar thicken and become grainy
4. Lift it out and separate the almonds from the sugar
5. Put half of this sugar back on the fire with another two fingers' width of water and when it smells of caramel pour in the said almonds, stir and when they have taken on the sugar remove them
6. Put the other half of the remaining sugar on the fire with another two fingers' width of water and repeat for the third time, which will be the last
7. Pour the almonds onto a plate and separate those that have stuck together
8. They are very good without any flavoring, but if you like you can give them the fragrance of vanilla with vanillin sugar, or the taste of chocolate with 30 grams of this grated
