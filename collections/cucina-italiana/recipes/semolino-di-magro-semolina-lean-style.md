## Ingredients

- Semolina
- Water
- A piece of butter proportioned to the quantity of semolina
- Salt
- Tomato juice or conserve
- Two or three eggs mixed with grated Parmesan cheese
- For one person: one egg yolk with two tablespoons of Parmesan

## Directions

1. Cook the semolina in water and before removing it from the fire, salt it, dissolve in it a piece of butter proportioned to the quantity of semolina and flavor it with a little tomato juice or conserve.
2. Beat in the tureen two or three eggs mixed with grated Parmesan and pour the semolina into it.
3. If it is a soup for only one person, one egg yolk with two tablespoons of Parmesan may be enough.
