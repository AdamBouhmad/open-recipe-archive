## Ingredients

- A little onion
- Parsley
- Some basil leaves
- Lean prosciutto
- Capers squeezed from vinegar
- Good oil
- Broth
- One or two anchovies
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a finely minced battutino with little onion, parsley, some basil leaves, all lean prosciutto and capers squeezed from vinegar
2. Place it on the fire with good oil, let it boil slowly and when the onion is browned stop it with a little broth
3. Let it boil a few more times, then remove it adding one or two minced anchovies and lemon juice
4. This sauce can serve for poached eggs, for steaks, which in this case do not need salt, and also for cutlets
