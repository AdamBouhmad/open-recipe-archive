## Ingredients

- Eggplants
- salt
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut them in half lengthwise without peeling them, make cross-hatched incisions on the white part, season them with salt, pepper and oil, place them on the grill from the skin side; then cover them with a lid or iron pan and cook them between two fires, so they do not need to be turned; halfway through cooking give them another coating of oil.
2. They will be cooked when the flesh has become soft.
