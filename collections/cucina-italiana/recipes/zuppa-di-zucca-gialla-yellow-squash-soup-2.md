## Ingredients

- Yellow squash, peeled and cut into thin slices, 1 kilogram
- Broth, two ladles
- Butter, 60 grams
- Oil, two level spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Put the squash to cook with two ladles of broth and then pass it through a sieve.
2. Make a mixture on the fire with 60 grams of butter and two level spoonfuls of oil.
