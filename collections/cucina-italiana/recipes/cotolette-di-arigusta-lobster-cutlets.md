## Ingredients

- Lobster (about 650 grams)
- béchamel sauce
- breadcrumbs
- egg (1)
- oil

## Directions

1. Boil the lobster as indicated in the previous recipe, then shell it to extract all the internal part and mince it coarsely with a crescent-shaped blade
2. Make a béchamel sauce in the proportions and manner of recipe no. 220
3. When removing it from the fire, add the lobster inside, salt it and after mixing well the mixture, pour it onto a plate and let it cool well for some hours
4. When ready to form the cutlets, divide the mixture into ten equal parts and touching them to breadcrumbs, shape them between the palms of your hands to the thickness slightly more than half a finger
5. Dip them in beaten egg, bread them again and fry them in oil
6. Make ten pieces from the long horns of the lobster that you will insert into the cutlets when sending them to table so that they attest to the noble material of which the cutlets are composed
7. Can be enough for five people and is a very delicate dish
