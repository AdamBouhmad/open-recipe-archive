## Ingredients

- 800 grams lean beef or veal without bone from the shank
- 80 grams ham fat
- 1 large carrot or 2 medium ones
- 3 or 4 celery ribs about a palm's length
- half a glass of dry white wine, or if lacking, two fingers of marsala
- salt and pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with the ham fat cut into strips rolled in salt and pepper
2. Salt and tie the meat so it stays together
3. Cut the carrot and celery into small pieces and place them on the bottom of a rather small pot
4. Place the meat on top and cover with water
5. Let it boil slowly with the pot covered
6. When it has reabsorbed the water, strain the sauce and vegetables through a sieve
7. Return the sauce and vegetables to the fire together with the meat and wine
8. When cooked, serve it sliced with its sauce on top
9. This dish can serve six people
