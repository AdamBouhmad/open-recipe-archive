## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Slices of fat and lean prosciutto, slightly more than a finger's width wide
- Three whole garlic cloves
- Two small sprigs of fennel
- Several peppercorns
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Fill the chicken with slices of fat and lean prosciutto, add three whole garlic cloves, two small sprigs of fennel, and several peppercorns.
2. Season the outside with salt and pepper.
3. Cook it in a pot with only butter, using heat both above and below, until cooked.
