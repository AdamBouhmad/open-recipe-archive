## Ingredients

- Two large artichokes
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour, 30 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 20 grams
- Milk, 2 deciliters
- Egg yolk
- Egg for coating
- Oil or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Take two large artichokes, clean them of hard leaves and scrape the stem, then boil them, but not too much, and while still boiling cut them lengthwise into five slices each, leaving some stem, and season with salt and pepper.
2. Make a béchamel sauce: melt the butter, add the flour, and gradually add the milk, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and mix in an egg yolk, the Parmesan, and a pinch of salt.
3. Taking them up one by one by the stem, dip the artichoke slices in the béchamel, lay them on a tray and cover them with the remaining béchamel using a spoon. After several hours, when they are completely cold, coat them with a beaten egg, bread them, and fry them in oil or lard.
