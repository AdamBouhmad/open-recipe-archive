## Ingredients

- 500 grams of veal muscle or milk veal
- Oil
- Two cloves garlic, unpeeled but bruised
- Salt
- Pepper
- Half a spoonful of flour
- Tomato sauce or tomato paste
- Butter
- Water or broth
- Bread for toasting
- Fresh mushrooms or potatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the muscle into small pieces
2. Put oil on the fire with two cloves of garlic slightly bruised; let it soften and then throw in the meat, seasoning with salt and pepper
3. Once browned, sprinkle half a spoonful of flour over it, add tomato sauce or paste and a piece of butter; then water or broth, little by little, and cook to completion, but arrange it so that there remains some sauce
4. Arrange slices of toasted bread on a platter, pour the stufatino over them and serve
5. You can also serve it without bread and add fresh mushrooms cut into slices, or potatoes when the meat is almost cooked
