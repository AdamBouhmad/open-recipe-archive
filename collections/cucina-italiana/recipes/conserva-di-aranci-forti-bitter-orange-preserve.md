## Ingredients

- Bitter oranges
- Water
- Sugar, 150 grams per 100 grams of fruit (white, fine)

## Directions

1. Boil the bitter oranges in water until they can be easily passed through from one side to the other with a stick.
2. Remove them from the boiling water and place them in cold water and keep them there for two days, changing the water often.
3. Cut them as the previous recipe, cleaning them of seeds and those white filaments found inside.
4. After weighing them, put them on the fire without water with 150 grams of white fine sugar for every 100 grams of fruit.
5. Boil slowly and be careful that the syrup does not condense too much, otherwise the oranges will harden.
