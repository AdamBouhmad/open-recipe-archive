## Ingredients

- Leftover bread pieces, dried and pounded and sifted into breadcrumbs, in the same proportion as semolina
- Eggs, two or more depending on quantity
- Parmigiano cheese, one heaping spoonful for each egg

## Directions

1. Pour the breadcrumbs into boiling broth in the same proportion as semolina.
2. According to the quantity, beat two or more eggs in the soup tureen, add to it one heaping spoonful of parmigiano for each egg and pour the hot soup into it little by little.
