## Ingredients

- One guinea fowl
- A ball of butter mixed with salt
- Lard for larding
- Butter
- Salt
- Oil

## Directions

1. Place a ball of butter mixed with salt inside the guinea fowl.
2. Lard the breast with strips of lard.
3. Wrap it in a sheet smeared with cold butter dusted with salt.
4. Roast it on a spit.
5. Remove the wrapping at two-thirds of the cooking time to finish cooking it and color it over the fire, brushing it with oil and salting it again.
