## Ingredients

- Rice, 150 grams
- Sugar, 70 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Candied fruit, 30 grams
- Milk, 8 deciliters
- Eggs, 3
- Rum, 2 tablespoons
- Salt, a pinch

## Directions

1. Cook the rice very much, stirring it often with a wooden spoon so it does not stick.
2. At two-thirds of cooking pour in the sugar, butter, salt and candied fruit cut into small pieces.
3. When it is cooked and cooled add the rum, the egg yolks first and the whipped egg whites after.
4. Take brioches molds, butter them well, sprinkle them with breadcrumbs, fill them and bake them in a country oven.
5. They are better hot than cold.
6. With this amount you will make 12 or 14.
