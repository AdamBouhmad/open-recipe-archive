## Ingredients

- Sole or eel
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Sapa
- Sage leaves
- Pine nuts
- Raisins
- Garlic
- Candied fruit
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Fry sole in oil and salt, or cut eel into half-finger-length pieces and cook on grill or spit without skinning.
2. Season with salt and pepper when fat is rendered.
3. In a pot, combine vinegar, sapa, whole sage leaves, whole pine nuts, raisins, garlic cloves cut in half, and candied fruit pieces in proportion to the fish.
4. Substitute sugar for sapa if needed and adjust vinegar strength to taste.
5. Bring to boil and pour over fish arranged in earthenware dish with liquid covering all sides.
6. Bring to boil again with fish inside, then cover and set aside.
7. When serving, take needed quantity with sauce and ingredients.
8. Refresh with additional marinade if fish dries out over time.
