## Ingredients

- green beans
- butter
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the green beans in salted water and cut them into two or three parts
2. Sauté them in a pan with butter and oil, seasoning with salt and pepper
3. Beat the eggs with a pinch of Parmesan and a pinch of salt
4. When the green beans appear somewhat shriveled, pour the eggs over them
