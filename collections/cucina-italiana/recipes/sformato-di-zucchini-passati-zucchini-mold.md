## Ingredients

- Zucchini, 600 grams
- Parmigiano, 40 grams
- Eggs, 4
- One quarter onion
- Celery
- Carrot
- Parsley
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter, 60 grams
- Flour, 2 tablespoons
- Milk, 4 deciliters

## Directions

1. Make a battuto with a quarter onion, celery, carrot and parsley.
2. Put it on the fire with oil and when it takes color pour in the zucchini cut into small pieces seasoned with salt and pepper.
3. When they are browned, cook them through with water, pass them dry through the sieve and add the parmigiano and eggs.
4. Make a béchamel with 60 grams of butter, two tablespoons of flour and 4 deciliters of milk.
5. Mix everything together and, using a smooth perforated mold, cook it in a bain-marie.
6. Unmold it hot, fill the center with a delicate sauce and serve it.
7. This amount will serve eight or ten people.
