## Ingredients

- Frogs, twelve large ones for 300 grams of rice
- Rice, 300 grams
- One quarter of a large onion
- One clove of garlic
- Carrot, celery, parsley, basil
- Oil, pepper, salt
- Tomatoes, in pieces
- Hot water
- Butter, a piece
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. To make the frog meat tender, throw them in hot water just after skinning and then pass them in fresh water, but be careful—only half a minute, or you will cook them
2. Leave behind the thighs; do not use the eggs in this case
3. Make a battuto with one quarter of a large onion, one clove of garlic, carrot, celery, parsley, basil and put it on the fire with oil, pepper and salt
4. When it has taken color throw in the frogs, stir from time to time and once browned throw in pieces of tomatoes, which you will let fall apart
5. Then pour in as much hot water as may be needed
6. Boil slowly until the frogs are well cooked and then pass everything through a strainer, squeezing well
7. In a little of this broth cook the thighs set aside, bone them and unite them with the rest
8. Put the rice on the fire with a piece of butter, stir, and when the butter has been completely absorbed pour the hot frog broth one ladle at a time until completely cooked
9. Before removing the rice throw into it a handful of Parmesan cheese and serve it
