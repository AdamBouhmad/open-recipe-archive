## Ingredients

- Veal cutlet from the sotto-noce or lean parts of the thigh
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- Egg, beaten
- Breadcrumbs, fine
- Butter
- Truffles, sliced thin
- Parmesan or gruyere cheese, sliced thin
- Broth or meat juice

## Directions

1. Cut the meat thin and the size of a palm of your hand. Beat them and give them a smooth and elegant shape, like a heart for example, wide at the top and narrowing at the bottom.
2. Prepare them on a plate with lemon juice, pepper, salt and very little grated parmesan.
3. Leave them in this infusion for an hour or two.
4. Pass them in beaten egg and keep them in it for the same amount of time.
5. Coat them with fine breadcrumbs and fry them with butter in a copper pan.
6. When they are just browned on one side, turn them and on the cooked side spread first slices of truffles and over these slices of parmesan or gruyere, cutting both as thin as possible.
7. Finish cooking them with heat under and over, adding broth or meat juice.
8. Remove them and arrange them on a platter with their juice around, squeezing the juice of a lemon over them, or half a lemon if there are few.
