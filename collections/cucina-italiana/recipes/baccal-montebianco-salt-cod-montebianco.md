## Ingredients

- Fleshy soaked salt cod, 500 grams
- Superfine oil, 200 grams
- Cream or excellent milk, 1 deciliter

## Directions

1. Clean the salt cod of its bones, small bones, skin and sinews, which present themselves like threads; this will leave approximately 340 grams of cleaned cod.
2. After having prepared it thus, pound it in a mortar and then place it in a small pot, together with the cream or milk, over a fire that is not too hot, stirring continuously.
3. When it has absorbed the cream or milk, begin to pour the oil drop by drop, as you would do for mayonnaise, always working it very much with the "wielded weapon", that is with the wooden spoon, so that it becomes refined and does not separate.
4. Remove it when it seems to you cooked to the point and serve it cold with a garnish of raw truffles cut into very thin slices, or with slices of fried bread, or caviar crostini.
5. This quantity can suffice for eight persons.
