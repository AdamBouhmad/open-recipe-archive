## Ingredients

- milk-fed veal, castrated lamb, pork or similar kidneys
- parsley
- half a clove of garlic
- juice of half a lemon
- five or six slices of bread marrow, dried by the heat
- 400 or 500 grams total kidney weight
- 50 to 60 grams of butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare a fine battutino (small chopped mixture) composed of parsley, half a clove of garlic, the juice of half a lemon and five or six slices of bread marrow, dried by the heat.
2. Open the kidneys to remove the fat and cut them into thin slices crosswise.
3. For a total of 400 or 500 grams, throw them in a pan with 50 to 60 grams of butter over high heat.
4. Move them often and as soon as they begin to brown add the battutino, season with salt and pepper and, always moving them with a spoon, pour in the lemon juice and finally a ladle of broth.
5. The operation should take about five minutes total and before serving pour them over the slices of bread.
6. This will be enough for four people.
