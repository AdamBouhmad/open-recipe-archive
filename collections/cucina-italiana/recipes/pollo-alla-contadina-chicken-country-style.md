## Ingredients

- One young hen
- Sprigs of rosemary
- One garlic clove, divided into four or five pieces
- Lardoon, minced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tomatoes, in pieces
- Potatoes, raw
- Oil or lard or butter

## Directions

1. Lard the chicken with some sprigs of rosemary and with the garlic clove divided into four or five pieces.
2. Put it on the fire with minced lardoon and season it with salt and pepper inside and outside.
3. When it is browned on all sides, add tomatoes in pieces, having removed the seeds.
4. When these are broken down, moisten it with broth or water.
5. Separately in oil, lard or butter, brown some raw potatoes cut into wedges.
6. Let them take flavor in the chicken's sauce and serve them as a side dish.
7. Substitute butter for the lardoon if you want the chicken with a more delicate taste.
