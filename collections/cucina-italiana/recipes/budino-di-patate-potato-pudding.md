## Ingredients

- Large floury potatoes, 700 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- Flour, 20 grams
- Milk, 2 deciliters
- Eggs, 6
- A pinch of salt
- Cinnamon or lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in water or steamed, peel them and pass them hot through a sieve. Put them back on the fire with the butter, flour and milk, poured in little by little, working them well with a wooden spoon; then add the sugar, salt and flavoring and let them rest until all these things are well incorporated together.
2. Remove from the fire; when they are lukewarm or cold, add the yolks and then the beaten whites.
3. Cook it like all the other puddings; that is in the oven or in the stove and serve it hot.
