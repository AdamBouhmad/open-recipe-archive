## Ingredients

- romaine lettuce or lettuce
- beets
- potatoes, boiled
- anchovies, washed and cleaned of bones
- boiled fish, cut into pieces
- capers (optional)
- pitted olives (optional)
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- eggs
- mustard
- lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Take romaine lettuce or lettuce, cut it into strips one finger wide, mix with beets and boiled potatoes cut into thin slices, some washed anchovies, cleaned of the bone and cut into four or five parts, and finally boiled fish in small pieces.
2. You can add at most some capers and the flesh of two or three sweetened olives.
3. Season everything together with salt, oil and not too much vinegar, turn it over so it takes up the seasoning well and heap it all together so that it makes a mound.
4. Make a mayonnaise sauce as in recipe N. 126 which, in the dose indicated there, can be enough for seven or eight people; but instead of pepper give it the piquancy with a teaspoon of mustard and to the lemon add a drop of vinegar in which you can dissolve the mustard.
5. With this sauce spread all the salad on the surface and then decorate it with other slices of beets and potatoes alternated so that they make a fine show; if you have a suitable mold, place on top of the salad, for beauty, not to eat it, a flower made with butter.
