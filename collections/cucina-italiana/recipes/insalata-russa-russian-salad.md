## Ingredients

- Lettuce, 120 grams
- Beets, 100 grams
- Green beans, 70 grams
- Potatoes, 50 grams
- Carrots, 20 grams
- Capers in vinegar, 20 grams
- Gherkins in vinegar, 20 grams
- Salted anchovies, 3
- Hard-boiled eggs, 2
- Egg yolks, 2 raw
- Oil, 2 deciliters
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fish glue, 3 leaves
- Water

## Directions

1. The lettuce, which can be of two or three varieties, such as romaine lettuce (lattugoni), radicchio, or lettuce, cut it into thin strips.
2. The beets, green beans, potatoes and carrots weigh them after boiling and cut them into small dice smaller than a chickpea and likewise the whites and one yolk of the two hard-boiled eggs.
3. Leave the capers whole and cut the gherkins to the same size as the capers.
4. The anchovies, cleaned and with the spine removed, cut them into small pieces, and having done all this mix everything together.
5. Now prepare a mayonnaise (see N. 126) with two raw yolks and the remaining hard-boiled yolk and 2 deciliters of finest oil.
6. When it is whipped, add the juice of a lemon, season it with salt and pepper and pour it into the said mixture stirring well so that it covers everything.
7. Dissolve on the fire three leaves of fish glue in two fingers of water height in a glass after having kept it soaked for a few hours and, once dissolved, pour an amount the size of a coin on the bottom of a smooth mold and mix the rest into the mixture which you will then pour into the said mold to put it on ice.
8. To unmold it easily, wet the mold with hot water and if you wish to give it a more beautiful and elegant appearance, when you have poured the thin layer of fish glue in the mold, before adding the mixture you can make an ornament of various colors on it with the vegetables, the whites and yolk of the hard-boiled eggs mentioned above.
9. This amount will serve eight or ten people.
