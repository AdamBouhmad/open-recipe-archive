## Ingredients

- Fine white sugar, 220 grams
- Sweet almonds, 200 grams
- Bitter almonds, 20 grams
- Egg whites, 2
- Orange peel flavoring

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the sun or by the fire and pound them very finely in a mortar with two tablespoons of the sugar, poured in several times; then mix in the rest of the sugar.
2. Beat the egg whites in a bowl and pour in the almonds thus prepared and the grated orange peel. Mix again with a spoon and pour the mixture onto the work surface over a light layer of flour to take only the small amount of flour needed to lightly roll with a rolling pin into a soft sheet, half a finger thick.
3. Cut them with the form marked below and you will obtain 16 to 18 to cook in the following way:
4. Take a baking sheet, make a layer of bran as high as a shield and cover it all with wafers to place the ricciarelli on and bake them in the oven at moderate heat so they remain tender. In the absence of an oven, which would be most suitable, use a country oven.
5. After cooking, cut away the wafer that protrudes from the edges of these pastries, which turn out to be of fine quality.
