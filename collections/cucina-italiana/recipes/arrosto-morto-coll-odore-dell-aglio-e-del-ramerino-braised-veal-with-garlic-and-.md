## Ingredients

- milk-fed veal from the loin
- oil
- butter
- salt
- broth
- garlic, whole cloves
- rosemary sprigs
- potatoes or vegetables
- tomato juice or paste (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal as in the previous recipe for braising in a pot with oil and butter.
2. Instead of studding the meat with garlic and rosemary pieces, throw a whole clove of garlic and two sprigs of rosemary into the pot.
3. When you send the roast to the table, strain its reduced sauce without squeezing it and surround the meat with potatoes or vegetables cooked separately if you wish.
4. If you like, you can also enhance the meat with a very small amount of tomato juice or paste.
5. Lamb shanks are also very good prepared this way, cooked between two fires.
