## Ingredients

- Veal brain, or lamb brain, or other similar animal brain, 120 grams
- Chicken livers, 3
- Eggs, 3
- A pinch of parsley and one of basil
- The juice of a quarter of a lemon
- Butter
- Meat sauce
- Salt and pepper
- Broth, cold

## Directions

1. Blanch the brain to be able to skin it and, both this and the livers, brown them in butter and cook them with meat sauce; salt and pepper for seasoning
