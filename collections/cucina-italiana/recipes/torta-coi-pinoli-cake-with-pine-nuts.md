## Ingredients

- Milk, half a liter
- Medium-grain semolina, 100 grams
- Sugar, 65 grams
- Pine nuts, 50 grams
- Butter, 10 grams
- Two eggs
- Salt, a pinch
- Vanilla for flavoring
- Flour, 200 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- One egg
- White wine or Marsala

## Directions

1. The amount of semolina is not entirely strict, but make sure it comes out rather firm.
2. Chop the pine nuts with a pastry cutter to the thickness of half a grain of rice.
3. When the semolina is cooked in the milk add everything else and last the eggs mixing them quickly.
4. Make a short crust pastry with flour, butter, sugar, and one egg. If this is not enough to mix the flour, use a drop of white wine or Marsala.
5. Take a pan in which the sweet does not come higher than two fingers, butter it and cover the bottom with a thin sheet of the said pastry; pour the mixture into it and make the top with the same pastry.
