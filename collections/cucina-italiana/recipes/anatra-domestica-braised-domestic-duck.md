## Ingredients

- domestic duck
- onion
- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- tomato sauce or tomato paste
- water or broth
- butter
- Parmigiano cheese
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare it like the mallard duck (recipe 270) and put it on the fire with a similar mixture of finely chopped vegetables.
2. When the duck has taken color, bathe it with tomato sauce or tomato paste and bring it to cook with water or broth.
3. Strain the sauce, remove the excess fat and put it back on the fire with the duck and a small piece of butter.
4. With this sauce and Parmigiano cheese you can dress a soup of strips or homemade lasagne noodles and serve the duck with a side of vegetables cooked in a little of that same sauce.
