## Ingredients

- For each egg: Semolina, 30 grams
- Parmigiano cheese, grated, 20 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Salt, a pinch
- Nutmeg, to taste

## Directions

1. Dissolve the butter over the fire and, removed from the fire, pour the semolina and parmigiano over it, dissolving the mixture well with the eggs.
2. Then pour it into a saucepan with a buttered sheet underneath to set it between two fires, taking care that it does not brown.
3. Once unmolded and cooled, cut it into small dice or in another way, making it boil in the broth for ten minutes.
4. Three eggs will be enough for five people.
