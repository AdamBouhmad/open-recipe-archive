## Ingredients

- Ripe and sound peaches
- White powdered sugar
- Lemon
- Ice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take ripe and sound peaches, throw them two at a time into boiling water for one minute
2. Remove them from the water and peel without touching the flesh
3. Roll them thoroughly in white powdered sugar and place them in a bowl or decent deep vase
4. Take as many small squares of sugar as there are peaches, rub them on the skin of a ripe garden lemon until each square is impregnated with the lemon's essence, and hide them among the peaches
5. Let them rest thus arranged for at least two hours
6. Before bringing the vase to the table, keep everything closed and covered in a lot of ice for two or three hours
7. Serve cold
