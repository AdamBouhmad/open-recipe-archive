## Ingredients

- Flour, 240 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Powdered sugar, 20 grams
- Two eggs
- One tablespoon of brandy
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Make a fairly firm dough with these ingredients
2. Work it very thoroughly by hand and let it rest a little, floured and wrapped in a cloth
3. If it becomes too tender to work, add more flour
4. Roll out a sheet the thickness of a coin
5. Cut it with a knife or fluted wheel into strips about a palm long and two or three fingers wide
6. Make some cuts in these strips so you can fold, braid or crumple them before frying
7. Drop them into a pan where the fat, oil or lard, floats in abundance, in bizarre shapes
8. Dust with powdered sugar when they are no longer hot
