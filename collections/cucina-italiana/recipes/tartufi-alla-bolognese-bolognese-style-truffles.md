## Ingredients

- Black or white truffles
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt
- Pepper
- Best quality oil
- Lemon
- Butter (optional, in small amount)

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the truffles with a soft brush dipped in fresh water
2. Slice them very thin
3. Alternate the truffle slices with equally thin slices of Parmesan cheese
4. Arrange in layers on a tinned copper platter, starting with the truffles
5. Season with salt, pepper, and plenty of best quality oil
6. Bring just to a boil, then squeeze lemon juice over them and remove from heat immediately
7. Optionally add small pieces of butter, but use sparingly to avoid making them too heavy
8. Alternatively, serve raw by slicing very thin and seasoning with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
