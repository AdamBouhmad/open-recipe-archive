## Ingredients

- Cooked meat, left over
- Prosciutto, a slice fat and lean
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Raisins
- Pine nuts
- Breadcrumb paste, made with marrow cooked in broth or milk
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Oil or lard
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Egg yolk and lemon juice sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat with a mezzaluna and chop separately a slice of fat and lean prosciutto to mix with it.
2. Season with parmesan, salt, pepper, spices, raisins, pine nuts, and some spoonfuls of paste made with bread marrow cooked in broth or milk, binding the mixture with one or two eggs depending on quantity.
3. Form many balls the size of an egg, flattened at the poles like the globe.
4. Coat them with breadcrumbs and fry them in oil or lard.
5. Then with a small soffritto of garlic and parsley and the fat left in the pan, pass them to a pan, adorning them with an egg and lemon juice sauce.
6. Alternatively, place them in the pan with a piece of butter.
