## Ingredients

- Lobster
- onion
- carrots
- parsley
- bay leaves (2)
- vinegar (2 tablespoons)
- salt
- oil
- lemon

## Directions

1. Fold the lobster's tail under and tie it before throwing it into boiling water
2. Flavor the water with a bundle made of onion, carrots, parsley and two bay leaves, adding two tablespoons of vinegar and a pinch of salt
3. Boil for 30 to 40 minutes depending on its size
4. Let the lobster cool in its broth
5. When removed, drain the water by squeezing the tail and after drying it, rub it with a few drops of oil to make it shiny
6. Send to table with an incision from head to tail to easily extract the flesh
7. Serve simply seasoned with oil and lemon juice, or accompanied with mayonnaise sauce or other spicy sauce
8. Alternatively serve with a sauce made as follows: extract the flesh from the head and mince it finely with a hard-boiled egg yolk and some parsley leaves, place the mixture in a sauce boat, season with pepper, little or no salt and dilute with fine oil and the juice of half a lemon, or vinegar
