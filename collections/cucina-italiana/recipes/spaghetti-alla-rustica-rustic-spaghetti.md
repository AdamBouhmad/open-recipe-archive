## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic
- parsley
- basil (if desired)
- oil
- 6 or 7 tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- spaghetti or vermicelli
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Make a small mixture with two cloves of garlic and a good pinch of parsley and the aroma of basil if desired; put it in a pan with a good amount of oil.
2. As soon as the garlic begins to color, add six or seven tomatoes cut into pieces, seasoning them with salt and pepper.
3. When well cooked, pass the sauce through a strainer.
4. Dress the spaghetti or vermicelli (cooked briefly in plenty of salted water) with the sauce and grated Parmesan cheese.
5. Send immediately to table so they remain succulent.
6. Tagliatelle can also be seasoned this way.
