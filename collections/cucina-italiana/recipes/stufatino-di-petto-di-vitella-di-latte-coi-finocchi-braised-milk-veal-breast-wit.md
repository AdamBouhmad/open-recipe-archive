## Ingredients

- Milk veal breast with bones
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Celery
- Carrot
- Cured meat
- Oil
- Pepper
- Salt
- Flour
- Tomato sauce or tomato paste
- Broth or water
- Butter
- Fennel

## Directions

1. Chop the milk veal breast leaving its bones
2. Make a battuto with garlic, parsley, celery, carrot and a proportional slice of cured meat; add oil, pepper, salt and put it on the fire together with the meat
3. Turn it often, and when it is somewhat browned, sprinkle a pinch of flour over it, a little tomato sauce or paste and cook it to completion with broth or water
4. Finally add a piece of butter and fennel cut into large pieces already reduced to half-doneness in water and sautéed in butter
5. Keep the pot always covered
