## Ingredients

- 100 grams breadcrumbs
- 20 grams beef marrow
- 40 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 eggs
- nutmeg or lemon zest, or both together

## Directions

1. The marrow serves to make them more tender and is not necessary to dissolve it by heat; it suffices to crush it and break it up with the blade of a knife
2. Knead everything together to form a rather firm loaf; but leave aside some breadcrumbs to add later if needed
3. They are called passatelli because they take their special form by being forced through the holes of an iron made expressly for this purpose
4. They can also be passed through a syringe
5. Boil them in broth and serve
6. This amount is sufficient for four people
