## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder or leg (cosciotto)
- lard strips
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb entirely with a larding needle using strips of lard seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Oil it with butter and milk or with milk alone and salt it halfway through cooking.
3. Roast it on a spit until tender and delicate.
