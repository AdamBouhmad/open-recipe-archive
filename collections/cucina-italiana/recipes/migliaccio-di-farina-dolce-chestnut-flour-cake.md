## Ingredients

- chestnut flour, 500 grams
- salt
- water, 8 decilitres
- pine nuts, one handful
- walnuts
- raisins
- rosemary leaves
- oil

## Directions

1. Pass the chestnut flour through a sieve to make it soft since it clumps easily
2. Put it in a container and season with a scant pinch of salt
3. Mix it with 8 decilitres of cold water poured gradually to make a liquid batter
4. Add a handful of whole pine nuts
5. Some add walnut pieces and raisins with a few rosemary leaves on top
6. Take a baking pan where the migliaccio will be about one and a half fingers thick
7. Cover the bottom with a light layer of oil
8. Spread two spoonfuls of oil over the batter when it is in the pan
9. Bake in an oven or between two fires
10. Turn it out hot
