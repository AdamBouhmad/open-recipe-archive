## Ingredients

- Egg yolks, 6
- Powdered sugar, 70 grams
- Isinglass, 15 grams, equal to 6 or 7 sheets
- Water, three quarters of a tablespoon glass
- Flavoring, three whole cherry laurel leaves or other as you prefer

## Directions

1. Beat in a saucepan the egg yolks and the sugar, add the water and the said leaves and place it on the fire stirring the spoon, until it is cooked, which thing, as I have already told you, is known by its condensing and sticking to the spoon.
2. Then pour it into a basin and thus hot beat it firmly with a whisk until it has mounted; remove the leaves and continuing always to beat it, add, when it is mounted, the isinglass little by little.
3. Take a decorated mold, grease it with oil, surround it with ice and pour the whipped cream into it, in the middle of which, if you wish, you can put ladyfingers dipped in liqueur or smeared with fruit preserve.
4. Leave it in the ice for more than an hour, and if it does not want to unmold naturally, pass a cloth soaked in hot water around the mold.
5. The isinglass is prepared beforehand thus: it is first put in soak, then on the fire with two fingers of a glass of water, it is boiled until the water, evaporating in part, forms a somewhat dense liquid, which sticks between the fingers, and thus boiling is poured into the cream to which you can give the taste of alkermes, coffee or chocolate.
