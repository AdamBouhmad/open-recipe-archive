## Ingredients

- Water, 140 grams
- Sugar, 50 grams
- Egg yolks, 4
- Vanilla flavoring
- Fish glue (isinglass), 2 sheets
- Whipped cream, 150 grams
- Ice and salt for freezing

## Directions

1. Make a cream with water, sugar, egg yolks, and vanilla flavoring
2. Put it on the fire, stirring continuously, and when it begins to stick to the wooden spoon, remove it and whip it with a whisk
3. If it takes too long to whip, keep the basin on ice
4. Gradually add two sheets of fish glue dissolved on the fire in a little water
5. Once whipped, slowly add 150 grams of whipped cream to the mixture
6. Place the mixture in a mold made for frozen pieces or in a copper pot or vessel, completely covered
7. Let it freeze for at least three hours, surrounded by a thick layer of ice and salt
8. This quantity will serve seven or eight people and makes a very pleasing dessert
