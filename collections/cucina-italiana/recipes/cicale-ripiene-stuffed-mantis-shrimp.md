## Ingredients

- Mantis shrimp (_Squilla mantis_)
- Breadcrumbs
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Oil
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Split the mantis shrimp along the back.
2. Fill it with a mixture of breadcrumbs, parsley and garlic scent.
3. Season both the filling and the fish with oil, pepper and salt.
