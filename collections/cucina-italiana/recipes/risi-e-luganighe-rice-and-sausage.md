## Ingredients

- Rice
- Sausages (luganighe)
- Broth
- Turnip or cabbage
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Do not wash the rice, only clean it and rub with a cloth to remove dust
2. Cut the turnip into cubes or the cabbage into thin strips
3. Blanch the turnip or cabbage until half-cooked
4. Sauté the blanched vegetables in butter
5. Crumble the sausages and add to the broth as it cooks with the rice
6. Shortly before the rice is done, add a generous pinch of Parmesan cheese to bind it better and give it a more pleasant flavor
