## Ingredients

- Coteghino weighing about 300 grams, skinned raw
- Lean veal or beef slice, 200 to 300 grams, wide and thin
- Butter
- Celery
- Carrot
- One quarter onion
- Dried mushroom pieces
- Water
- Flour
- Carrots for contorno

## Directions

1. Skin the coteghino raw and beat the veal or beef slice well
2. Wrap the coteghino with the meat slice, tie it all with string and put it on the fire in a pot with a piece of butter, celery, carrot and a quarter onion, all cut coarsely
3. When the piece has taken color on all sides, pour in enough water to cover it halfway and add some pieces of dried mushrooms, letting it boil slowly until completely cooked
4. Pass the gravy, unite the mushrooms to it and use this with cheese and butter to dress pasta, serving the wrapped coteghino, freed from the string, with some of its gravy around it as accompaniment
5. To thicken the gravy for the pasta, put a pinch of flour in a pot with a piece of butter and when it begins to take color pour the gravy into it and let it boil a little
6. The contorno of carrots works well with this dish, first boiled to two-thirds done, then finished in that gravy
