## Ingredients

- Eggs and flour for pasta dough, no water
- One thick slice of prosciutto, both fat and lean
- Celery
- Carrot
- Butter
- Tomato juice or tomato paste
- Broth or water (if using tomato paste)
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a thick slice of prosciutto into small dice
2. Finely chop celery and carrot in quantities such that both together make approximately the volume of the prosciutto
3. Put these three ingredients on the fire together with a piece of butter proportionate to the amount of pasta
4. When the mixture has taken on color, add tomato juice or tomato paste, but with paste you need a ladle of broth or water
5. Make and roll out the pasta dough into a sheet somewhat thicker than thin noodles and cut into wider strips
6. Cook the tagliatelle briefly and salt them very little because of the prosciutto; remove them dry and season them with the sauce and parmesan cheese
