## Ingredients

- Woodcocks
- Woodcock innards, gizzards included but not emptied
- Parsley leaves
- Anchovy fillets, 2 per 3 woodcock innards
- Butter
- Pepper
- Meat juice
- Bread slices, elegant form, lightly toasted
- Woodcock for roasting
- Sage sprigs
- Thin slice of lard

## Directions

1. Clean the woodcocks and remove their innards, throwing away only the end of the intestine that borders the anus
2. Combine with these the gizzards without emptying them; some parsley leaves and the flesh of two anchovies for every three innards
3. No salt is needed
4. Mince everything very finely with a crescent-shaped chopper, then put it on the fire with a piece of butter and a pinch of pepper, moistening it with meat juice
5. Spread this mixture on elegant slices of bread, lightly toasted
6. Send these crostini to the table accompanied by the woodcocks that you will have cooked roasted with some small sage sprigs and wrapped with a thin slice of lard
