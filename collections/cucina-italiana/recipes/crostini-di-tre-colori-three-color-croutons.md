## Ingredients

- two chifels (elongated loaves)
- spinach, cooked with juice or butter and parmesan
- two hard-boiled eggs
- butter or oil

## Directions

1. Cut the two chifels into thick slices one centimeter wide and fry them in butter or oil.
2. Take spinach cooked with juice or butter and parmesan, finely chopped, and cover the slices of chifel with a layer of the same.
3. Take two hard-boiled eggs, shell them, cut them in half crosswise and set aside the yolks.
4. From the white cut many concentric circles and place them over the layer of spinach.
5. From the yolk make many small pieces or small dice and place them in the middle of the circles of white.
6. In this way you will form croutons that can surround a roast dish which, having as a base fried bread covered by the green of the spinach, with the white and the yellow-red of the eggs will appear in three colors.
