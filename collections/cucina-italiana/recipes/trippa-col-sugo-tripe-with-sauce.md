## Ingredients

- Tripe, boiled
- Butter
- Meat broth or tomato sauce
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Tripe, however cooked and seasoned, is always an ordinary dish. Whether boiled already in some cities or boiled at home, preferring the thick cordoned type, once boiled, cut it into strips half a finger wide and dry it between the folds of a kitchen cloth. Then put it in a pot to sauté in butter and when it has drawn the butter, add meat broth or, not having this, tomato sauce; season it with salt and pepper, cook it as much as you can and when you are about to remove it, throw in additional ingredients.
