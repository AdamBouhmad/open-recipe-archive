## Ingredients

- herring, male preferred
- milk, boiling
- oil
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the herring from the back, remove the head and flatten it
2. Place in infusion in boiling milk and leave for eight to ten hours, changing the milk once if possible
3. Dry with a cloth
4. Cook on a grill like common herring
5. Season with oil and very little vinegar, or if preferred, with oil and lemon juice
6. Alternatively, place the herring on the fire with cold water, bring to boil for three minutes, then hold briefly in fresh water, dry it, remove the head, open from the back and season as above
