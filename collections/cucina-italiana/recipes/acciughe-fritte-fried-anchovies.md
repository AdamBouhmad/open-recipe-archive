## Ingredients

- Anchovies or sardines
- Flour
- Egg
- Salt
- Oil

## Directions

1. Remove the head from the anchovies and coat them with flour.
2. Take them one by one by the tail, dip them in beaten and well-salted egg.
3. Dip them again in flour and fry them in a pan with boiling oil.
4. For a better result with larger fish, open them along the back by cutting with a fine knife and remove the spine, leaving them joined at the tail.
