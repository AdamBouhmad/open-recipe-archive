## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Fresh water
- Flour
- Egg white
- Egg yolk
- Salt
- Lemon wedges
- Oil or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Take, for example, two artichokes, clean them of tough leaves, trim the tips, clean the stem and cut them in two parts
2. Cut these half artichokes into wedges or rather into slices to get 8 or 10 per artichoke even if not very large
3. As you cut them, throw them into fresh water
4. When well refreshed, remove them and dry them roughly or simply squeeze them, throwing them immediately into flour so it sticks well
5. Beat to medium peaks the white of one egg (one is enough for two artichokes)
6. In the white mix the yolk and salt it
7. Put the artichokes in a small sieve to shake off excess flour and after pass them in the egg, mix and let them stay a little so the egg incorporates
8. Throw the pieces one by one into a pan with boiling fat and when they have taken on a fine golden color, remove them and send them to table with lemon wedges
