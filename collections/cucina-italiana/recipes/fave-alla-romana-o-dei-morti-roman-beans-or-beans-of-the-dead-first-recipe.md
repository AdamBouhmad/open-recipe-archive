## Ingredients

- Flour, 200 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Sweet almonds, 100 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Egg, 1
- Lemon peel flavoring, or cinnamon, or orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and pound them with the sugar to the size of about half a grain of rice.
2. Mix them with the flour together with the other ingredients and form a somewhat soft dough with as much rosoli or brandy as needed.
3. Shape into small pastries, in the form of a large bean, which will yield about 60 or 70 for each recipe.
4. Arrange them on a copper baking sheet first greased with lard or butter and dusted with flour; brush with egg.
5. Bake in the oven or country oven, noting that, being small, they cook quickly.
