## Ingredients

- 300 grams lean veal breast, sliced thin
- salt
- pepper
- sage leaves
- thin slices of prosciutto, fat and lean
- toothpicks
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound the veal scallops and dampen them to flatten.
2. Season each scallop lightly with salt and pepper.
3. Place half a sage leaf on each scallop and on top of the sage place a thin slice of prosciutto.
4. Secure the three components together with a toothpick.
5. Cook in butter in a sauté pan, keeping the prosciutto side on the heat only briefly so the prosciutto does not harden.
6. Serve with any side dish desired.
