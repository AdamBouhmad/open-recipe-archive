## Ingredients

- Pine nuts, 40 grams
- Capers pressed from vinegar, 15 grams
- 1 salted anchovy
- 1 hardboiled egg yolk
- The pulp of three olives in brine
- Half a clove of garlic
- A good pinch of parsley, including the stems
- A crumb of bread as large as an egg, soaked in vinegar
- A pinch of salt and a pinch of pepper
- Oil, 60 grams
- A drop of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop very finely with a crescent-shaped knife the parsley and garlic and then put these and everything else in a mortar and after having reduced the mixture very finely pass it through a sieve and dilute it with 60 grams of oil and a drop of vinegar; but taste it first to adjust it properly.
2. It is an excellent sauce and sufficient for 600 grams of fish.
