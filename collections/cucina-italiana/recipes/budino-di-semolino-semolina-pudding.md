## Ingredients

- Milk, 8 deciliters
- Semolina, 150 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Raisins, 100 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Rum, 3 tablespoons
- Salt, a pinch
- Lemon zest for flavoring

## Directions

1. Prepare the mixture and remove it from the fire
2. Cook it in a smooth or worked mold, first greased with butter and dusted with breadcrumbs
3. If a common or country oven is lacking, puddings can also be cooked well in a hearth burner
4. Serve this pudding hot
