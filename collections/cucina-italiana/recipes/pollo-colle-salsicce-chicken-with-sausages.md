## Ingredients

- half an onion, finely chopped
- a piece of butter
- four or five slices of prosciutto, one finger wide
- one whole chicken
- pepper
- salt
- broth or water
- three or four fresh whole sausages

## Directions

1. Finely chop half an onion and place it in a casserole with a piece of butter and four or five slices of prosciutto, one finger wide.
2. Place a whole chicken on top of these ingredients, season it with pepper and a little salt, and put it on the fire.
3. Let it take on color from all sides, and when the onion is completely rendered, moisten it with broth or water and add three or four fresh whole sausages.
4. Let it cook over low heat, making sure that at the end there remains some liquid.
