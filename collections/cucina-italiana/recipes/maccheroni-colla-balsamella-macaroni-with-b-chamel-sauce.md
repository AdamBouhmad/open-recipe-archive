## Ingredients

- Long Neapolitan-style macaroni
- Butter
- Salted water
- Milk
- Béchamel sauce
- Egg yolk
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Take long Neapolitan-style macaroni and cook them two-thirds through in salted water.
2. Remove them dry and return them to the fire with a piece of butter and when they have absorbed it, add enough milk to finish cooking them over moderate heat.
3. Meanwhile prepare béchamel sauce as indicated, and when it is no longer boiling bind it with an egg yolk and then pour it over the macaroni together with grated Parmesan in proportion.
4. Macaroni prepared this way are very suitable as an accompaniment to a piece of stewed beef or a piece of milk veal in fricandeau.
5. In this case you can take a tray that can withstand fire, place a metal form in the middle, and the macaroni around it.
6. Place the tray in a country oven or under an iron lid.
