## Ingredients

- Potatoes, partially boiled
- Truffles, sliced thin
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth or meat sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Slice partially boiled potatoes thinly
2. Layer potatoes in a small baking dish alternating with thin slices of truffle and grated Parmesan
3. Add small pieces of butter, salt and pepper
4. When they begin to sizzle, moisten with broth or meat sauce
5. Before removing from heat, squeeze a little lemon juice over them
6. Serve hot
