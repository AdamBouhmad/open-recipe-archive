## Ingredients

- Potatoes, 500 grams
- Capers in vinegar, 30 grams
- Pickled peppers, 2
- Pickled gherkins, 5
- Pickled small onions, 4
- Salted anchovies, cleaned, 4
- Celery rib about one palm long
- A pinch of basil
- Hard-boiled eggs, 2
- Oil, good measure
- Vinegar, small amount
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oregano, optional

## Directions

1. Boil or steam 500 grams of potatoes
2. Peel while hot and slice thinly into a salad bowl
3. Finely chop capers, pickled peppers, gherkins, pickled onions, cleaned anchovies, celery rib and basil together and place in a bowl
4. Chop hard-boiled eggs finely, then crush with a knife blade and add to the chopped mixture
5. Season the mixture with good oil, little vinegar, salt and pepper
6. Mix well until it becomes almost liquid
7. Use this mixture to dress the potatoes
8. Add oregano if desired
9. This portion serves six or seven people and can be kept for several days
