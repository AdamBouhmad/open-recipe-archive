## Ingredients

- pork loin (the elongated muscle at the sides of the spine)
- croutons
- sage
- oil

## Directions

1. The pork loin is that elongated muscle positioned at the sides of the spine, which in Florence is called loin of pork.
2. Cut it into small pieces and thread it onto a spit, interspersing it with croutons and sage as is done with birds, and oil it as you would those.
