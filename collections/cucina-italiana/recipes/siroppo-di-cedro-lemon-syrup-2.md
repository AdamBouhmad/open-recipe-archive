## Ingredients

- Garden lemons, 3
- Fine white sugar, 600 grams
- Water, 1 tablespoon glass (about 3 deciliters)

## Directions

1. Remove the internal pulp from the lemons without squeezing it, cleaning it well of peel and seeds
2. Put the water on the fire with the peel of one of the said lemons cut into thin ribbons with a knife, and when it begins to boil pour in the sugar
3. Wait for it to boil a few times, then remove the peel and pour in the lemon pulp
4. Let it boil until the syrup has reduced and cooked to the right point, which is known by the pearl it makes when boiling and by the white wine color it acquires
