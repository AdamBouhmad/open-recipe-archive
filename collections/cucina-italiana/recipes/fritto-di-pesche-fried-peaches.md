## Ingredients

- Peaches, burrone variety, not too ripe
- Batter N. 156
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the peaches into slices that are not too large
2. Like apples and fennel, wrap them in batter N. 156
3. Dust them with sugar after cooking
4. It is not necessary to peel them
