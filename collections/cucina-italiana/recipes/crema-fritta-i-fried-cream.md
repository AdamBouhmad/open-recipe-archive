## Ingredients

- cornstarch, 100 grams
- sugar, 30 grams
- butter, 20 grams
- milk, 4 deciliters
- two whole eggs
- lemon zest
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Work the eggs with the sugar, then add the cornstarch reduced to powder, the grated lemon zest, the milk poured little by little and the butter.
2. Put the mixture on the fire stirring continuously as you would for a common cream and when it is condensed so that it no longer grows, add the pinch of salt and pour it onto a tray or board, spreading it to the thickness of a finger.
3. Cut it into almond shapes when it is well cold, coat it with egg and breadcrumbs, fry it in lard or oil and serve it hot as a side dish to other fried foods.
