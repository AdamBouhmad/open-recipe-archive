## Ingredients

- Sugar, 300 grams
- Sweet almonds, 60 grams
- Egg yolks, 10
- Water, 1 deciliter
- Orange blossom water flavoring
- Cinnamon powder, as needed

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, toast them to a hazelnut color, pound them very finely and set aside.
2. Boil the sugar with the water for one or two minutes, ensuring it does not brown, and after, when it is lukewarm, begin adding the egg yolks, one or two at a time and, over very moderate heat, work it continuously stirring the spoon always in one direction.
3. When the mixture has condensed somewhat so there is no longer danger it will curdle, you can then, in order for it to puff up better, beat it with a whisk from bottom to top until the yolks have not lost their bright color and taken on the appearance of a dense cream.
4. Then give it the orange blossom water flavoring and add the almonds, mixing well.
5. Pour it into the small cups and on the surface of each one, in the middle, place a pinch of the said cinnamon, which, when mixed into the sweet by whoever must eat it, will acquire more fragrance.
