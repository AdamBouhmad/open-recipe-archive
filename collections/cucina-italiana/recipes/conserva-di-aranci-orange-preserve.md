## Ingredients

- Oranges, 12 number
- One garden lemon
- Fine white sugar, equal to the weight of the oranges
- Water, half the weight of the oranges
- Genuine rum, four tablespoons

## Directions

1. With the prongs of a fork prick the entire skin of the oranges.
2. Keep them soaked for three days, changing the water morning and evening.
3. On the fourth day cut them in half and each half into strips about half a centimeter thick, discarding the seeds.
4. Weigh them and only then regulate the sugar and water according to the proportions indicated.
5. Put them on the heat first with water alone and after ten minutes of boiling add the lemon cut like the oranges.
6. Immediately after add the sugar and stir continuously until the liquid resumes a strong boil, because otherwise the sugar precipitates to the bottom and could stick to the pan.
