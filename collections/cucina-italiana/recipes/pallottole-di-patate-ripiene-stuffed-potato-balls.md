## Ingredients

- Potatoes, 300 grams
- Parmesan cheese, 2 heaping spoonfuls
- Eggs, 2
- Nutmeg flavoring
- Flour, as needed

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes, peel them and pass them hot through a sieve over a layer of flour.
2. Make a hollow on top of the potato mound, salt them, give them the flavor of nutmeg and pour the eggs and Parmesan into it.
3. With as little flour as possible, form a soft and long paste that you will divide into 18 parts and for each one, with floured fingers, make a small hollow to fill with a meat forcemeat.
4. Pull the edges over it to cover it and, with floured hands, form round balls that you will fry in lard or oil, sending them to the table as a side dish to fried meat.
