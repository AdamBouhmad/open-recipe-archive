## Ingredients

- Pork loin chops, approximately 300 grams
- One lamb brain or half of a larger animal brain
- Beef marrow, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 50 grams
- 3 egg yolks and, if needed, add one egg white
- Nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Bone and trim the fat from the pork chops, then cook them in a saucepan with butter, salt and a pinch of pepper. In the absence of pork, you may use lean turkey breast in the proportion of 200 grams, cooked in the same manner.
2. Pound or mince very finely the meat with a lunette; then add to it the boiled and skinned brain, the raw marrow and all the other ingredients, mixing them well together.
3. The tortellini are then enclosed in a sheet of pasta as cappelletti are, and folded in the same way, except that these are made much smaller.
