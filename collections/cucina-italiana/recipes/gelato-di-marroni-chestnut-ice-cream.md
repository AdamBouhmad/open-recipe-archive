## Ingredients

- Marroni (chestnuts), 200 grams
- Zucchero (sugar), 150 grams
- Latte (milk), half liter
- Odore di vaniglia (vanilla flavoring)

## Directions

1. Put the chestnuts to boil in water as if making ballotte
2. When well cooked, clean them of both skins and pass the pulp through a strainer
3. Put this pulp on the fire with the milk and sugar and let it boil slowly in an uncovered pan for a quarter hour
4. Give the mixture the vanilla flavoring with the vanilla sugar and pour it into the ice cream maker
5. Serve it at table all in one piece; if it should serve nine or ten persons double the dose
