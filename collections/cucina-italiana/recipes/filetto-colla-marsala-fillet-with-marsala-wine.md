## Ingredients

- fillet of beef, approximately one kilogram
- onion of medium size
- slices of prosciutto
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth or water
- Marsala wine

## Directions

1. Tie the fillet and place it over the fire with a medium-sized onion sliced into thin strips, together with some slices of prosciutto and a piece of butter; season lightly with salt and pepper.
2. When it has been browned on all sides and the onion consumed, sprinkle a pinch of flour over it, let it take color and then moisten it with broth or water.
3. Cook slowly, then strain the sauce, degrease it and with this and three fingers (of a glass) of Marsala return it to the fire to boil again, but slowly; serve it with reduced sauce, but not too thick from too much flour.
4. You can also insert lard into the fillet and cook it with only butter and Marsala.
