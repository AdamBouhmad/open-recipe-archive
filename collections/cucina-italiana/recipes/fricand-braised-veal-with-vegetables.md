## Ingredients

- A piece of milk-fed veal from the thigh, all in one piece
- Fat and lean prosciutto for larding
- Salt to taste
- One onion
- Two cloves
- One carrot, cut into strips
- Celery
- Parsley
- A piece of butter
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Lard the veal with prosciutto.
2. Tie the piece and salt it sparingly or not at all because oversalting is the worst defect in food.
3. Stud an onion with two cloves and make a bouquet with carrot cut into strips, celery and parsley.
4. Put everything in a pot with a piece of butter, brown the meat and cook it through with broth.
5. When cooked, discard the onion and bouquet, strain the sauce, remove the fat and reduce it separately until it becomes a jelly that you will add to the fricandò when you serve it at table.
