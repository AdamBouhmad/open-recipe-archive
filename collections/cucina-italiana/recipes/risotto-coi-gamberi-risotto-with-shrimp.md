## Ingredients

- 300 grams shrimp
- 700 grams rice
- half an onion
- three cloves garlic
- carrot
- celery
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- tomato sauce or paste
- hot water
- butter
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Make an abundant soffritto with half an onion, three cloves of garlic, carrot, celery and parsley and place it on the fire with oil in proportion
2. When the soffritto has taken color, throw in the shrimp and season with salt and pepper
3. Turn them often and when all have become red, moisten them with tomato sauce or paste and shortly after pour in enough hot water to suffice for the rice
4. Let boil not too long, since shrimp cooks quickly, then remove them dried and set aside a quarter of the largest ones after shelling them
5. Pound the remaining shrimp in a mortar, pass them through a sieve and mix the strained pulp into the broth where they were cooked
6. Place a piece of butter on the fire in a pot and pour in the cleaned rice without washing it; stir continuously and when the rice has taken the sheen of the butter, pour in the broth hot little by little
7. At more than half cooking add the whole shelled shrimp and before serving give it grace with a handful of parmesan cheese
