## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Egg yolks, 8
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Use the recipe for cream: make a cream with milk, sugar, egg yolks, and vanilla flavoring
2. You will hear it is a delicious ice cream, well churned and very firm, if you know how to work it properly
3. This quantity will serve ten people
4. Instead of vanilla flavoring you can give the cream the flavor of coriander, burnt coffee, or toasted almond. For coriander, see Portuguese-style Milk, N. 693; for coffee, boil several crushed coffee beans separately in the milk; for toasted almond, make a brittle like that of N. 617, somewhat more cooked, with 100 grams of almonds and 80 grams of sugar; pound it fine, boil it separately in a little milk, strain it and add it to the cream
