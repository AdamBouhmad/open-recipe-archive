## Ingredients

- Clams or arkshells
- garlic
- oil
- parsley
- pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- flour
- broth
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Put the clams or arkshells on the fire with a soffritto of garlic, oil, parsley, and a pinch of pepper.
2. Shake them and keep the vessel covered so they do not dry out.
3. Remove them when they have opened.
4. For the sauce, use one or more egg yolks depending on the quantity, lemon juice, one teaspoon of flour, broth, and some of the juice that came out from the clams.
5. Cook it like a cream and pour it over the clams when you serve them at table.
