## Ingredients

- lean veal breast, sliced thin
- oil
- butter
- sage leaves
- salt
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Pound the veal scallops well with the back of a knife blade.
2. Heat oil and butter in a saucepan or sauté pan with whole sage leaves and let them soften slightly.
3. Add the veal scallops, season with salt and pepper, and boil over high heat for five or six minutes.
4. Squeeze lemon juice over them and serve.
