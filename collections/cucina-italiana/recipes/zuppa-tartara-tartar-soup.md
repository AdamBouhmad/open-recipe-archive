## Ingredients

- Ricotta, 200 grams
- Milk
- Powdered sugar, 30 grams
- Ground cinnamon, two pinches
- Savoiardi biscuits
- Rosolio or alkermes liqueur
- Fruit preserve (apricots or peaches), not too liquid
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take 200 grams of ricotta, soften it somewhat with milk and season it with 30 grams of powdered sugar and two pinches of ground cinnamon, mixing well.
2. Take a worked mold and wet the internal walls with rosolio or grease them with butter.
3. Dip savoiardi in rosolio or alkermes and, starting with these or with a not-too-liquid fruit preserve, cover the bottom of the mold.
4. Fill it, alternating in layers, with the ricotta, the savoiardi, and the preserve, which can be apricots or peaches.
5. Unmold it after a few hours and, if you have arranged it carefully, in addition to the taste the eyes of the diners will also be satisfied.
6. The ricotta can be softened with citron rosolio instead of milk, and then cinnamon is not needed.
