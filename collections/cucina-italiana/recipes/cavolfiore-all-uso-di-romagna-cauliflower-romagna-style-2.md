## Ingredients

- 1 large head of cauliflower or 2 if small
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- water
- salt
- pepper
- tomato paste
- hot water
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Divide the cauliflower into small florets and wash them
2. Place a proportionate amount of chopped garlic, parsley and oil over heat, and when it is browned, stop it with a drop of water
3. Then throw in the cauliflower, seasoning with salt and pepper
4. When it has absorbed the mixture, cook it through with tomato paste dissolved in hot water
5. Add grace and more flavor with Parmesan when you serve it
6. It can serve as a side dish to boiled meat, a stew or a coteghino
