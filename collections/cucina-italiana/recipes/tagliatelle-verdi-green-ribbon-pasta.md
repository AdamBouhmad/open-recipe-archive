## Ingredients

- Spinach, cooked and drained, a handful
- Eggs, 2
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cook spinach, squeeze it well and chop it with a curved knife
2. With 2 eggs and a handful of this chopped spinach, mix flour on a work surface to obtain a very firm pasta dough which you will work a lot by hand
3. With a rolling pin, roll it out to a thin sheet and when it begins to stick because of the herb which produces stickiness, sprinkle it lightly with flour
4. Wrap the sheet in a cloth and when it is dry cut it somewhat wider than thin noodles for broth, noting that the beauty of such pasta is its length which indicates the skill of who made it
5. As soon as the water comes to a boil remove the pasta dry and season it as with spaghetti alla rustica, or as with macaroni or tagliatelle, or simply with cheese and butter
