## Ingredients

- sole fish, at least 150 grams each
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- oil
- butter
- dry white wine
- parsley
- parmesan cheese
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take sole at least 150 grams each, remove the head and skin
2. With a sharp knife, separate the flesh from the bones to obtain four long fillets per sole, or eight if the sole are very large
3. Using the back of the knife, beat the fillets lightly and flatten them thin with the blade
4. Leave them in beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper for several hours
5. Coat in breadcrumbs and fry in oil
6. Pour a little of the remaining oil from the pan and a piece of butter into a pan or dish where the fillets can lie flat
7. Arrange the fillets and season again lightly with salt and pepper
8. When they have browned a little, wet them with dry white wine, let boil for five minutes together with a little chopped parsley
9. Serve with the sauce, sprinkling with a pinch of parmesan
10. Serve with lemon wedges
