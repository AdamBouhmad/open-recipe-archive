## Ingredients

- milk-fed lean veal without bone, cut into very thin scaloppine, 300 grams net
- lardoon cut into thin slices, 70 grams
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- spice aroma
- grated Parmesan cheese
- chopped parsley
- spinach
- butter for the spinach

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter on the fire in a proportionate saucepan and on the bottom and around it lay out the lardoon, on top of which you will place a first layer of scaloppine seasoning them with salt, pepper, the aroma of spices, grated Parmesan and chopped parsley.
2. Then another layer of scaloppine seasoned in the same manner, and so on until you have used up everything.
3. On the last layer of scaloppine scatter various small pieces of butter and cook them between two fires, more from below than above, until they remain almost dry and the lardoon is browned.
4. Turn the casserole over a layer of spinach cooked with butter and serve it to four people.
