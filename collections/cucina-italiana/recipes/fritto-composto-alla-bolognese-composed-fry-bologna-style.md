## Ingredients

- Lean veal of milk-fed calf, stewed
- Small brain, boiled or cooked in gravy
- Slice of fat and lean prosciutto
- Egg yolk or whole egg
- Béchamel sauce N. 137
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Nutmeg flavoring
- Truffles, finely chopped
- Flour
- Egg
- Fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take a piece of lean veal of milk-fed calf that has been stewed, a small brain boiled or cooked in gravy, and a slice of fat and lean prosciutto
2. Mince everything with a crescent-shaped blade and then pound it fine in a mortar
3. After add an egg yolk or a whole egg, depending on the quantity, and a little béchamel sauce N. 137
4. Put the mixture on the fire and stirring always let the egg cook
5. Add last grated Parmesan, the flavor of nutmeg, very finely chopped truffles and pour it onto a plate
6. When it is completely cold make from it as many round balls the size of a small walnut and flour them
7. Then dip them in egg and after in finest breadcrumbs, repeating the operation twice and fry them
