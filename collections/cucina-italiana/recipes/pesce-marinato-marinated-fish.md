## Ingredients

- sole or large eel
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- sapa (or sugar as substitute)
- whole sage leaves
- whole pine nuts
- raisins
- garlic cloves
- candied fruit, in small pieces

## Directions

1. If using sole, fry them first in oil and salt them; if using eel, cut it into pieces about half a finger long and, without skinning them, cook them on a grill or on a spit.
2. When they have rendered their fat, season them with salt and pepper.
3. In a pot, pour in proportion to the fish: vinegar, sapa (which goes with it like cheese on macaroni), whole sage leaves, whole pine nuts, raisins, a few cloves of garlic cut in half lengthwise and candied fruit in small pieces.
4. If you lack sapa, substitute with sugar and taste to correct the flavor of the vinegar, if it is too strong.
