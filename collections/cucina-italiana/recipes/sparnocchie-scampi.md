## Ingredients

- scampi
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- flour
- oil

## Directions

1. It is best to roast them on a grill without any seasoning, then shell them and season with oil, pepper, salt and lemon juice.
2. Small scampi can also be dusted with flour and fried as they are, or in the manner indicated for mantis shrimp.
