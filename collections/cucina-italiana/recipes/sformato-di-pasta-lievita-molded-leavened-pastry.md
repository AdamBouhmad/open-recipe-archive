## Ingredients

- Hungarian flour, 300 grams
- butter, 70 grams
- butter, 30 grams
- brewer's yeast, 30 grams
- egg yolks, 3
- cream or very good milk, 2 deciliters
- salt, to taste

## Directions

1. Note that the cream may be too much if needed.
2. With a quarter of the said flour, the brewer's yeast and a little of the said lukewarm cream form a small loaf like that of Krapfen and let it rise.
3. Mix the rest of the flour with the 70 grams of butter dissolved in winter, the egg yolks, the salt, the small loaf when it has doubled in size and enough lukewarm cream to obtain a dough of proper consistency that can be worked with a wooden spoon within a basin.
4. When with the working it shows signs of detaching from the walls of the vessel, let it rise in a warm place and, once achieved, turn it onto the work surface over a dusting of flour and with floured hands flatten it to the thickness of half a centimeter.
5. Take a smooth mold with a hole in the middle with a capacity of about a liter and a half of water, because with the dough it must only be filled halfway, grease it and flour it and, the dough cut into strips, arrange them in this manner.
6. At each layer of strips, as long as you have them, grease them with the 30 grams of butter mentioned above using a brush.
7. Cover the mold and having let the mixture rise again, when it reaches the opening, bake it in the oven or in a country oven.
8. Fill it after having turned it out and serve it at the table.
