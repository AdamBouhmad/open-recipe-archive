## Ingredients

- cornmeal flour, 300 grams
- raisins or dried grapes, 100 grams
- lard, 40 grams
- pine nuts, 30 grams
- sugar, three teaspoons

## Directions

1. Remove seeds from the raisins
2. Cut the pine nuts in half lengthwise
3. Grease the pan with lard and dust it with flour
4. Proceed as for the previous recipe
