## Ingredients

- Sponge cake, 100 grams
- Chocolate, 100 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Sugar, 30 grams
- Rosolio liqueur, as needed

## Directions

1. Cut the sponge cake into thin slices
2. Grate the chocolate
3. Melt the butter in a bain-marie and in it pour the sugar and chocolate, working the mixture with a ladle until you have reduced it very fine
4. With this and the sponge cake dipped in rosolio fill in layers a mold that you will have first moistened with the same rosolio to be able to better unmold the sweet
5. In summer keep the mold in ice so that the mixture sets
