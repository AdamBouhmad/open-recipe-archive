## Ingredients

- Chicken giblets: livers, crests, unborn eggs, bean-like organs and gizzards
- Celery
- Prosciutto, fat and lean
- Onion, small amount
- Butter
- Potato flour, a pinch
- Salt, pepper and spice flavoring
- Broth
- Tomato juice or tomato conserve, small amount

## Directions

1. Give the celery, cut into pieces about half a finger long, one third of its cooking time in salted water.
2. Make a small battue of fat and lean prosciutto and a little onion, place it on the fire with butter and when well browned, add first the gizzards cut into three pieces, then a pinch of potato flour, then the livers in two pieces and all the rest.
3. Season with salt, pepper and spice flavoring and when it has absorbed the flavors, moisten with broth and a little tomato juice or conserve.
4. Fry the celery separately in butter and when cooked add the giblets to it, let it boil a little longer, add more broth if needed and serve.
