## Ingredients

- Tomatoes, 1 kilogram
- White sugar, 300 grams
- Juice of one lemon
- Vanilla flavoring and lemon zest

## Directions

1. The tomatoes for this use must be very ripe, pulpy, and possibly round.
2. Place them in very hot water so they can be peeled easily. After peeling, cut them in half and with the handle of a teaspoon remove the seeds.
3. Dissolve the sugar in the fire in two fingers (of a glass) of water, then throw in the tomatoes, the juice of the lemon, and a little of its grated skin.
4. During boiling over low heat in an uncovered pan, stir somewhat, and if any seeds appear that have remained, remove them.
5. Finally give it flavor with vanillated sugar and remove it when it has reached the consistency of common preserves.
6. Note: It is difficult to specify the quantity of sugar in this preserve, as it depends on how watery the tomatoes are. Make double the dose as it reduces greatly.
