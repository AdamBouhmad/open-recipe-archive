## Ingredients

- Lamb sweetbreads or from larger animals
- Water for partial cooking
- Flour
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Marsala or Madeira wine
- Optional: meat broth, butter and flour for sauce

## Directions

1. Those of lamb need no preparation; those of larger animals must be given half-cooking in water, skinning them if necessary. Leave the first ones whole, cut the second ones into pieces and flour all of them well and put them to brown in butter seasoning them with salt and pepper. Then moisten them with Marsala or Madeira wine, and after make them come to a single boil. You can also make the sauce separately with a pinch of flour, a small piece of butter and the wine.
2. If you enhance them with meat sauce, from being good as they are, they will become exquisite.
