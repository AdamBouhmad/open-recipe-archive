## Ingredients

- One beef braciole
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley, chopped
- Water

## Directions

1. Beat it very well with the back of a knife.
2. Put it on the fire with a piece of butter proportioned to its size.
3. Season it with salt and pepper.
4. Turn it often so it browns on both sides.
5. When it has absorbed almost all the butter, moisten it twice with a few drops of water.
6. When cooked, sprinkle a pinch of chopped parsley over it.
7. Keep it on the fire for another moment and serve it with its sauce.
8. You can garnish it with fried potatoes if you like.
