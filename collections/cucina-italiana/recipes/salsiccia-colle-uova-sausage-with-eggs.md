## Ingredients

- sausage
- eggs
- butter or lard, if the sausage is too lean

## Directions

1. If the sausage is fresh, split it lengthwise into two parts and cook it in a pan without any fat or seasoning, as it contains its own
2. If it is aged, cut it into slices and remove the skin
3. As soon as the sausage is cooked, break the eggs into the pan and serve when they are set
4. For each common link of sausage, one egg is sufficient or at most two
5. If the sausages are too lean, it is good to cook them with a little butter or lard
6. If instead of sausage it is cured meat, add a small piece of butter and pour the eggs in after beating them separately
