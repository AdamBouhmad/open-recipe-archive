## Ingredients

- Rose apples or other firm variety
- Water, sufficient to cook them
- Cinnamon, a small piece
- Sugar, as needed
- Candied fruit, chopped

## Directions

1. Take firm apples, remove the core with a tin corer, peel them and cut them into thin round slices
2. Put them on the fire with enough water to cook them and a small piece of cinnamon
3. When half cooked, add enough sugar to make them sweet and some chopped candied fruit
4. Take a copper plate or porcelain serving dish that can withstand heat, pour them into it, cover with puff pastry, put them in the oven or in a country oven, and serve them hot as a sweet course
