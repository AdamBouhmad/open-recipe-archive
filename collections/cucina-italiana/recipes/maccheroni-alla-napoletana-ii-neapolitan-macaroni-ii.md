## Ingredients

- 300 grams long macaroni
- two large slices of onion
- 30 grams butter
- two tablespoons oil
- 500 grams tomatoes
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- 50 grams raw butter
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. In a pan or pot, brown two large slices of onion with 30 grams of butter and two tablespoons of oil
2. When the onion, which naturally breaks apart while boiling, is well browned, squeeze it with a spoon and throw it away
3. In that fat at boiling point pour 500 grams of tomatoes and a good pinch of coarsely chopped basil; season with salt and pepper
4. Prepare the tomatoes beforehand because they must be peeled, cut into pieces and cleaned of seeds as much as possible
5. Season the macaroni with the condensed sauce, with 50 grams of raw butter and parmesan and send them to the table
6. These will be especially appreciated by those who would swim in tomato sauce
7. Instead of long macaroni, penne can be used, indeed these will take the sauce better
