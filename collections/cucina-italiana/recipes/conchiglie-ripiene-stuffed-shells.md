## Ingredients

- Fine fish (cooked), 130 grams
- Parmesan cheese (grated), 20 grams
- Flour, 20 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Egg yolks, 2
- Milk, 2.5 deciliters

## Directions

1. Make a béchamel sauce with the milk, butter and flour
2. When removing it from the fire, add the Parmesan and, no longer at boiling point, the egg yolks and the minced fish, seasoning with salt and pepper
3. Pour it into shells buttered beforehand with cold butter
4. Brown it slightly in a country oven and serve it
5. Alternatively, the shells could be filled with minced cooked chicken flesh keeping the same proportions
