## Ingredients

- Milk, 1 liter
- Sugar, 250 grams
- Candied red squash, 40 grams
- Candied citron, 30 grams
- Almonds, 30 grams
- Pistachios, 20 grams
- Egg yolks, 4
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Make a cream with the milk, the sugar and the egg yolks, giving it the flavor of vanilla, and pour it into the sorbetierre.
2. When it is frozen mix into it the aforementioned ingredients. Blanch the pistachios and almonds in hot water; divide those into three parts and mince these to the size of a vetch and toast them.
3. Cut the candied fruit into thin slices and the squash into small cubes, which being red will make a more beautiful appearance.
4. If the milk is good, by boiling it for half an hour with the sugar in it, you can do without the egg yolks, but the mixture will then be of less flavor.
5. The almonds in this and similar cases come out better toasted in the following manner. When peeled and minced, put them on the fire with a spoonful of the said sugar and a drop of water, stir them continuously and when they have taken color stop them with another drop of water; pour them then into a colander over the remaining sugar and use them.
