## Ingredients

- Wheat flour, 500 grams
- Powdered sugar, 180 grams
- Butter, 180 grams
- Raisins (Zibibbo), 70 grams
- Chopped pine nuts, 50 grams
- Candied citron in small fillets, 30 grams
- Cream of tartar, 8 grams
- Baking soda, 4 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Milk, 1 deciliter

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the flour and make a mound on the work surface
2. In the well you make in it, place the butter, eggs, and milk (this lukewarm with the two powders in it), which you will already see beginning to ferment
3. Knead everything together and when the dough has become homogeneous, open it to add the pine nuts, candied citron, and raisins
4. Work it so that these things are spread evenly throughout to form two breads in a shuttle shape, barely more than a finger high
5. Brush them with egg yolk and bake immediately in the oven or in a country oven
