## Ingredients

- Rice, 100 grams
- Sugar, 80 grams
- Milk, 6 deciliters
- Eggs, 3
- A small piece of butter
- Rum, one tablespoon
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in the milk, but if you do not cook it very much you will make nothing good
2. At half cooking time add the butter and sugar, including the vanillaed sugar for the flavoring, and after it is cooked and cooled, mix in the yolks, the rum, and the whipped whites
3. For the rest, follow the same procedure as for the potato starch soufflé
