## Ingredients

- Marasca cherries, 1,500 kilograms
- Finest powdered sugar, 2 kilograms
- Cinnamon stick, one small piece

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries and keep them in the sun for five or six hours.
2. Then put them on the fire with a small piece of cinnamon and when they have released a certain amount of moisture, pour the sugar into them, being careful to stir slowly to keep the cherries whole.
3. When they have become wrinkled and have taken on a brown color remove them and use them for the intended purpose.
