## Ingredients

- Salt cod, boiled, 500 grams
- anchovies, 2
- parsley, a pinch
- pepper, a few pinches
- Parmesan cheese, grated, a handful
- bread crumb paste (made from bread marrow, water, and butter), 3-4 spoonfuls
- eggs, 2
- breadcrumbs
- lemon, cut into wedges or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the salt cod as in the preceding recipe.
2. Finely chop the anchovies, parsley, and salt cod together with a knife.
3. Add pepper, grated Parmesan, and the bread crumb paste made from bread marrow, water, and butter to make it more tender, along with two eggs.
4. Form the mixture into spoonfuls and dip each into breadcrumbs.
5. Flatten with your hands to give them the shape of cutlets.
6. Dip the cutlets into beaten egg and then coat again in breadcrumbs.
7. Fry in oil and serve with lemon wedges or tomato sauce.
8. Half this quantity will make 9 or 10 cutlets.
