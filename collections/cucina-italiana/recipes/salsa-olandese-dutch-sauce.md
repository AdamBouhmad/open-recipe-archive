## Ingredients

- Butter, 70 grams
- Egg yolks, 2
- Lemon juice, 1 tablespoon
- Water, half an eggshell
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Melt the butter separately without heating it too much.
2. Put the egg yolks with the water in a small basin, and over a light fire or on the edge of the stove begin to beat them with a whisk and gradually pour in the butter little by little.
3. When the mixture has thickened, add the lemon juice and finally the salt and pepper.
4. Prepare it at the moment of serving; it is a delicate sauce for boiled fish or similar things, and will be sufficient for approximately 500 grams of fish.
