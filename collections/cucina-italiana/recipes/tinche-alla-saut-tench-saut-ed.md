## Ingredients

- large tench fish
- flour
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- lemon
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take large tench, cut off their fins, head and tail; open them along the back, remove the backbone and small bones and divide them in two parts lengthwise. Coat them in flour, then dip them in beaten egg that you have first seasoned with salt and pepper; coat them in breadcrumbs, repeating this last operation twice. Cook them in a sauté with butter and serve at table with lemon wedges and a side of fried mushrooms in their season. To remove or diminish the muddy smell of marsh fish, throw them in boiling water, keeping them there a few minutes until the skin begins to crack, and refresh them in cold water before cooking them.
