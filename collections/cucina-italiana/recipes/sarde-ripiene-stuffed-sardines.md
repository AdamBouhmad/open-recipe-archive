## Ingredients

- Sardines, 20 to 24 large ones
- Bread crumb, 30 grams
- Salted anchovies, 3 pieces
- One egg yolk
- Half a clove of garlic
- A pinch of oregano
- Milk
- Egg white
- Breadcrumbs
- Oil
- Salt
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the sardines, remove the head, and open them from the belly using your fingers to extract the spine.
2. Soak the bread crumb in milk and then squeeze it out.
3. Clean the anchovies of their scales and spine, then chop them.
4. Mix together the bread crumb, anchovies, egg yolk, half a clove of garlic, and a pinch of oregano, using the blade of a knife to make the mixture fine.
5. Spread this mixture onto the sardines and close them.
6. Dip them one by one into the remaining beaten egg white, coat them with breadcrumbs, and fry them in oil.
7. Salt them lightly and serve with lemon wedges.
