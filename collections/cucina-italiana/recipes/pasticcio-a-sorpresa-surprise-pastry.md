## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Rice flour, 200 grams
- Sugar, 120 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Eggs, 6
- Salt, a pinch
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Take a saucepan and gradually pour into it the eggs, sugar, flour and milk, mixing as you go so that no lumps form; but reserve some of the milk to add later if needed.
2. Place the saucepan on the fire and, stirring the spoon continuously as in making cream, cook the mixture adding, before removing it from heat, the butter, vanilla and salt.
3. Let it cool completely, then pour it into a metal plate or earthenware dish that can withstand the fire, arranged so that it forms a mound.
4. Cover it with shortcrust pastry (recipe 589, recipe B or recipe C), make some decorative work on it, brush it with egg yolk, bake it in the oven and serve it hot, dusted with powdered sugar.
