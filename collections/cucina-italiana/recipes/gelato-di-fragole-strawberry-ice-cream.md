## Ingredients

- Ripe strawberries, 300 grams
- Fine white sugar, 300 grams
- Water, half a liter
- One large garden lemon
- One orange

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in the water for 10 minutes in an uncovered copper pot
2. Pass the strawberries through a sieve along with the juice of the orange and lemon
3. Add the syrup after having strained it as well
4. Mix everything together and pour the mixture into the sorbet maker
5. This quantity will serve eight people
