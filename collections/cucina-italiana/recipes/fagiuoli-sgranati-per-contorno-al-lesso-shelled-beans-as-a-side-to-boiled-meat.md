## Ingredients

- 300 grams shelled beans
- 30 grams dried meat
- 2 deciliters water
- 4 tablespoons oil
- a small bundle of 4 or 5 sage leaves
- salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Put the beans on the heat with all the above ingredients
2. Let them boil slowly and shake them often
3. Remove the sage and dried meat
4. Serve
5. This side dish is enough for four people
