## Ingredients

- chifels, 2
- milk, 2 deciliters
- sugar, 20 grams
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the tips from the chifels and cut them into thick slices one centimeter each, placing them on a tray
2. Put the milk on the fire with the sugar and when it boils pour it over them to soak them lightly
3. Once cooled, dip them in two beaten eggs, coat with breadcrumbs and fry them
4. For those easily pleased they can serve as a sweet dish if you give them vanilla flavoring, dusting them with powdered sugar after cooking
