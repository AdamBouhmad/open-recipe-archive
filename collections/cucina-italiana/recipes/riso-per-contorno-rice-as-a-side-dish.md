## Ingredients

- rice, 200 grams
- broth from boiled chicken or capon
- butter
- Parmesan cheese, small quantity
- egg or two egg yolks
- spinach, two or three spoonfuls, if making green rice variation

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice in water and finish cooking it with the broth from the boiled chicken or capon
2. Cook it firm and when almost done, flavor it with butter and Parmesan in small quantity
3. When you remove it from the fire, bind it with an egg or better with two yolks
4. If the rice is to serve as a side to braised veal or bracioline, add to the above ingredients two or three spoonfuls of cooked spinach passed through a strainer to make green rice
5. These side dishes can be given a better appearance by reducing the rice in a bain-marie within a mold, but be careful it does not become too hard, as that would be a serious defect
