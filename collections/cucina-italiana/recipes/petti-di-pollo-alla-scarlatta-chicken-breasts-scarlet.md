## Ingredients

- Chicken or large hen breast
- Butter, 20 grams
- Flour, 40 grams
- Milk, 2 deciliters
- Salted tongue, 50 grams
- Eggs, 1
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon wedges

## Directions

1. From a capon breast or a large hen you will be able to get six thin slices, which at a meal will be enough for four or five people.
2. Cook them with butter and season with salt and pepper.
3. Make a béchamel with butter, flour and milk.
4. When it is cooked add 50 grams of finely chopped salted tongue with the lunette, and when it has cooled, spread the chicken breasts with it on all sides.
5. Dip them then in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs and brown them in butter or lard in a sauté pan, and serve them with lemon wedges.
