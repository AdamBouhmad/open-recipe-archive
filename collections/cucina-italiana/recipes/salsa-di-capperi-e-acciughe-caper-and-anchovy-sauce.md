## Ingredients

- Capers, sweetened
- Anchovy, 1 piece
- Oil
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take a pinch of sweetened capers, squeeze them of vinegar and mince them with a crescent-shaped knife together with an anchovy that you have first cleaned of its scales and spine.
2. Put this mixture to heat on the fire with oil, and pour it on the steak that, just removed from the grill, you will have seasoned with salt and pepper and brushed with butter; in this case, however, brush it lightly, because otherwise the butter would fight with the vinegar of the capers in the stomach.
