## Ingredients

- pork liver pâtés
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- lard
- bay leaves or fennel seed (optional)

## Directions

1. Make pork liver pâtés seasoned with oil, pepper and salt, wrapped in netting and cooked on the grill, on a spit or in a pan.
2. After cooking, place them in a pot and fill it with rendered lard at boiling temperature.
3. Remove them as needed for use and reheat them.
4. Some use cooking the pâtés between two bay leaves, or as in Tuscany, add some fennel seed to the seasoning, but these are strong flavors that many stomachs cannot tolerate.
