## Ingredients

- Cardoon ribs
- Butter
- Salt
- Milk or cream
- Béchamel sauce (recipe N. 137)
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the cardoon ribs into pieces about three fingers long
2. Sauté them with butter and salt to taste
3. Finish cooking by adding milk or preferably cream
4. Bind them with a little béchamel sauce (recipe N. 137)
5. Add a pinch of grated Parmesan cheese and remove from heat immediately without boiling further
6. Serve as an excellent side dish to braised meats, braciole, or stewed offal
