## Ingredients

- 60 grams of chocolate for an abundant cup, or 50 grams if light is preferred, up to 80 grams if very thick is desired
- two decilitres of water
- cinnamon or vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Break the chocolate into pieces and place in a chocolate pot with the water mentioned above
2. When it begins to be hot, stir it so that it does not stick and dissolves well
3. As soon as it comes to a boil, remove it from the fire and whisk it for five minutes
4. Then bring it to a boil again and serve it
