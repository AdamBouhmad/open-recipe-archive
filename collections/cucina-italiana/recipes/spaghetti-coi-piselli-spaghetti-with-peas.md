## Ingredients

- 500 grams spaghetti
- 500 grams shelled peas
- 70 grams cured meat
- 1 small onion
- 1 clove fresh garlic
- celery ribs
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- dill stems (if available)
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Make a mixture with the cured meat, a small onion, fresh garlic and some celery ribs and parsley.
2. Put it in a pan with oil and when it begins to take color, add the peas together with some chopped dill stems if you have them.
3. Season with salt and pepper and cook.
4. Chop the spaghetti by hand to make them short, less than half a finger long, cook them in salted water, drain well, mix them with the peas and serve with Parmesan cheese on the side.
