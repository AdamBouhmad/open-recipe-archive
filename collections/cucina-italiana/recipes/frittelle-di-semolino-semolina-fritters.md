## Ingredients

- Milk, half a liter
- Semolina, 130 grams
- Butter, the size of a walnut
- Rum, one spoonful
- Lemon zest flavoring
- Salt, as needed
- Eggs, 3

## Directions

1. Cook the semolina in the milk, salt it when it is cooked and, once cooled, add the eggs and rum.
2. Fry them in oil or lard and serve them at table dusted with powdered sugar.
3. This quantity should be enough for four or five people.
