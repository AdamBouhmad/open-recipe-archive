## Ingredients

- 200 grams of raw spinach
- 40 grams of butter, divided
- 4 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- Parmesan cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove spinach from water, boil it, and while still dripping, immediately after removing from heat place it in fresh water
2. Squeeze it well and chop it coarsely
3. Throw it in a pan with a piece of butter and season with salt and pepper
4. Stir frequently and when it has drawn out its fat, pour the beaten and lightly salted eggs over it
5. Once browned on one side, flip it with a plate and return it to the pan with another small piece of butter
6. If desired, add a pinch of Parmesan to the eggs
