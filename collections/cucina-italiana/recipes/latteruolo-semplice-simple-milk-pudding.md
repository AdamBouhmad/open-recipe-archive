## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Eggs, 6
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. The fragrance of citrus fruits, being the product of a volatile oil enclosed in superficial cells, it will suffice to cut with a penknife a very thin ribbon of their rind, at least a palm long, and let it boil in the liquid that you wish to aromatize.
2. From the surface of a lemon lift with a penknife a strip of rind of a certain length and put it in the milk, which you will boil for half an hour with the sugar inside.
3. When it is cold, remove the lemon strip.
