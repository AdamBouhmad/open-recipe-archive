## Ingredients

- bread crumbs soaked in broth
- butter
- Marsala wine
- nutmeg
- spaghetti
- salted water
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Pass bread crumbs through a wire sieve, moistening them with a little hot water if needed to extract all the pulp.
2. Return the strained mixture to the heat with butter, Marsala, nutmeg and when it reaches a boil, if the sauce does not need to be reduced to the right consistency, season the spaghetti (cooked in salted water) with this sauce and Parmesan cheese.
