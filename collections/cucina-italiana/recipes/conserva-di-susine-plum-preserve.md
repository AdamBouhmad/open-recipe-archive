## Ingredients

- Plums, preferably Claudia variety, any quantity
- White powdered sugar, 60 grams per 100 grams of fresh plums

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the plums.
2. Boil for a few minutes.
3. Pass through a sieve.
4. Return to the heat with white powdered sugar in the proportion of 60 grams of sugar per 100 grams of fresh plums.
5. If mold forms after some time, it indicates insufficient cooking; remedy by returning to the heat.
