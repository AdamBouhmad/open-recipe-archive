## Ingredients

- Half a hare, cut into pieces
- One medium onion
- Garlic, 2 cloves
- Celery, one palm-length piece
- Rosemary leaves
- Butter, a small piece
- Oil, 2 tablespoons
- Prosciutto, four or five finger-width strips
- Salt, pepper, and spices
- White wine or marsala, half a glass
- Fresh or dried mushrooms, a handful
- Broth
- Tomato juice or tomato paste
- Butter for finishing

## Directions

1. Chop the hare into pieces.
2. Make a fine battuto with one medium onion, two garlic cloves, one palm-length piece of celery, and various rosemary leaves.
3. Heat a small piece of butter with two tablespoons of oil and four or five finger-width strips of prosciutto in a pot.
4. After five minutes of cooking, add the hare and season with salt, pepper, and spices.
5. Brown the meat, then moisten with half a glass of white wine or marsala.
6. Add a handful of fresh mushrooms, or dried mushrooms that have been softened.
7. Finish cooking with broth and tomato juice or paste.
8. Before serving, taste and add more butter if needed.
