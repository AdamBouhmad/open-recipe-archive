## Ingredients

- Head of white cabbage
- Salt
- Strong vinegar
- Water
- Slice of fat prosciutto or cured meat
- Butter
- Broth from sausages or boiled meat

## Directions

1. Take a head of white cabbage, clean it of green leaves and large ribs and cut it into four wedges starting from the stem.
2. Wash them well in fresh water and then with a long sharp knife cut them crosswise very thin as you would for noodles.
3. Once the cabbage is reduced this way, place it in an earthenware vessel with a pinch of salt and pour boiling water over it until covered.
4. When it is cold, remove it squeezing it well, then put it back in the dry vessel with a finger of strong vinegar mixed in a glass of fresh water. If the head of cabbage is very large, double the dose.
5. Let it infuse for several hours, squeeze it well again and put it to cook in the following manner.
6. Finely chop a proportionate slice of fat prosciutto or cured meat and put it with a piece of butter in a pot; when they have softened a little, throw in the cabbage and cook it with broth from sausages or boiled meat if these are freshly made and not too spicy, otherwise use regular broth.
7. Before serving taste it to see if it has the right amount of vinegar, which should be slightly perceptible, and salt.
