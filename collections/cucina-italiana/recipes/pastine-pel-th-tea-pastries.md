## Ingredients

- Hungarian or finest flour, 440 grams
- Potato flour, 160 grams
- Powdered sugar, 160 grams
- Butter, 160 grams
- Two egg whites
- Tepid milk, as needed

## Directions

1. Form a mound on the work surface with the two flours and sugar mixed together
2. Make a hole in the middle, place the egg whites and butter in pieces, and with the blade of a knife first and with your hands after, using the milk, knead it and work it for about half an hour to obtain a rather soft paste
3. Roll it out with a rolling pin into a sheet the thickness of a coin, cut it into round disks like those of N. 7, perforate them with the tines of a fork and bake them in the oven or in a country oven in a butter-greased pan
4. With only half the dose of the recipe, you will obtain quite a lot
