## Ingredients

- Lean milk veal without bone, 300 grams
- Potatoes, 300 grams
- Fat and lean prosciutto cut fine, 80 grams
- Butter, 70 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 30 grams
- Nutmeg flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes not too much, or cook them in steam, which would be better, and afterwards cut them into very thin slices.
2. Cut the prosciutto crosswise into strips about one finger wide.
3. Mince the meat very finely with a knife and season it with salt, pepper and a little nutmeg, as these and spices in general are appropriate for windy foods.
4. Divide this meat into twelve parts to form as many scaloppine, flatten them with the blade of the knife, then cook them in white, that is without browning, with half the said butter.
5. Take a metal plate or platter, pour the fat that may remain from cooking and place four scaloppine on it, covering them.
