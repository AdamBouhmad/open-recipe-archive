## Ingredients

- Capers
- Pitted sweet olives, equal quantity to the capers
- Finely chopped onion
- Butter
- Water
- Flour
- 1 anchovy
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a handful of capers and press them from vinegar; take as many sweetened olives as, after removing the pit, equal the quantity of capers and chop both finely with a crescent-shaped knife.
2. Put on the fire a fine mixture of chopped onion with a small piece of butter and when it has taken color, wet it little by little with water so that it breaks down.
3. Pour into it the mixture of capers and olives and let it boil for a while.
4. Finally add a drop of vinegar, a pinch of flour and another small piece of butter.
5. Last add a chopped anchovy, and without letting the sauce boil anymore, serve it.
