## Ingredients

- patate (potatoes)
- burro (butter)
- sale (salt)
- pepe (pepper)
- olio (oil)
- farina (flour)

## Directions

1. This means, in good Italian, potatoes browned in butter.
2. Peel raw potatoes and cut them into thin slices which you will put on the fire in a baking pan with butter, seasoning with salt and pepper.
3. It is very suitable to place them under the steak when it is sent to the table.
4. They can also be fried in a pan with oil in the following manner. If they are new potatoes, there is no need to peel them; just rub them with a rough cloth.
5. Cut them into very thin slices and leave them in fresh water for about an hour; then dry them well between the folds of a cloth and flour them.
6. Be careful not to roast them too much and salt them after cooking.
