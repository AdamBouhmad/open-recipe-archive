## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Oil
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sugar
- Raisins

## Directions

1. Boil spinach with only the water that drains from soaking
2. Squeeze well
3. Stew with a soffritto of oil, garlic and parsley, salt and pepper
4. Leave whole
5. Season with a pinch of sugar and some raisins from which the stems have been removed
