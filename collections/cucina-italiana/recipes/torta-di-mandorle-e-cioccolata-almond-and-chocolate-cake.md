## Ingredients

- Almonds, 150 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Chocolate, 100 grams
- Potato flour, 60 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Milk, 3 deciliters
- Eggs, 4
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the sun or by fire and pound them very finely in a mortar together with one third of the sugar.
2. Make a mixture on the fire with the butter, potato flour and milk poured in little by little.
3. When it reaches the right consistency, add the grated chocolate and the remaining sugar, and after they are well dissolved, add the pounded almonds, stirring continuously.
4. When the mixture is well combined, give it flavor with vanilla sugar and let it cool to add the beaten eggs.
5. Make a paste with 100 grams of flour following recipe N. 153 and with it, following the method as in the Ricotta Cake N. 639, pour it into a baking pan to a thickness of over a finger to cook in a country oven.
6. Cut into almond-shaped pieces when well cooled.
