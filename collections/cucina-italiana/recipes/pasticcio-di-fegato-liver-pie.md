## Ingredients

- Liver mixture from recipe 374
- Black truffles, 30 grams, cut into wedges
- Pastry from recipe 372

## Directions

1. Use the mixture from recipe 374, adding only 30 grams of black truffles cut into wedges and bringing them to a boil in the Marsala before scattering them in the pie.
2. Cover it with the pastry from recipe 372, cook it in the oven or in a country oven, and serve it cold.
3. It will be enough for twelve people.
