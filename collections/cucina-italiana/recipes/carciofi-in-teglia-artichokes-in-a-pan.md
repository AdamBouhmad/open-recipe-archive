## Ingredients

- artichokes, prepared as in recipe N. 186
- good oil, sufficient quantity
- eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the artichokes as described in N. 186 and after shaking off excess flour, spread them in a pan in which good oil has begun to sizzle in sufficient quantity
2. When the artichoke slices are browned on both sides, pour beaten eggs over them, but be careful not to cook them too much
3. Sprinkle the seasoning of salt and pepper partly on the artichokes and partly in the eggs before pouring them
