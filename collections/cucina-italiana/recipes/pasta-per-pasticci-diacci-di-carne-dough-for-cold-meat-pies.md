## Ingredients

- Flour, 250 grams
- Butter, 70 grams
- Salt, a generous pinch
- Milk as needed to blend and bring to proper consistency

## Directions

1. It does not need to be worked excessively. Form it into a loaf and let it rest for about half an hour, wrapped in a damp cloth and dusted with flour. This amount should be sufficient for a pie even larger than the game pie described in recipe no. 370.
