## Ingredients

- Fresh anchovies
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare anchovies in an oil-based stew with minced garlic, parsley, salt, pepper and oil.
2. When almost cooked, add a little water mixed with vinegar.
