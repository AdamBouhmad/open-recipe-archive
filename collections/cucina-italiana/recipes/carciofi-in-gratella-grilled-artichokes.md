## Ingredients

- Tender artichokes
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Choose tender artichokes, trim them, cut the stem at the base and leave them with all their leaves.
2. Open them out somewhat so they will take the seasoning well, which should be composed of nothing but oil, pepper and salt.
3. Place them upright on the grill, and if necessary to hold them firm, skewer them on a stick near the stem two or three together.
4. Give them another brushing of oil halfway through cooking and leave them on the fire until the outer leaves are burned.
