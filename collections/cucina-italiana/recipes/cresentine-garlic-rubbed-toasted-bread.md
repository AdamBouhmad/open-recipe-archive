## Ingredients

- Bread slices
- Garlic clove
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Toast bread slices on both sides.
2. While hot, rub them with a garlic clove.
3. Season with salt, oil, vinegar, and sugar.
