## Ingredients

- Two raw and fresh egg yolks
- Olive oil, six or seven spoonfuls or more if absorbed
- Lemon juice
- Salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Place two raw and fresh egg yolks in a bowl and after whisking them somewhat, let fall on them little by little and almost drop by drop, especially at first, six or seven spoonfuls or even more if they absorb it, of olive oil
2. Then have them absorb the juice of one lemon
3. If the sauce turns out well it should have the appearance of a dense cream; but it is necessary to work it for more than 20 minutes
4. Finally season it with salt and white pepper in good measure
5. To be more certain of the result, to the two raw egg yolks it is customary to add another hard-boiled one
