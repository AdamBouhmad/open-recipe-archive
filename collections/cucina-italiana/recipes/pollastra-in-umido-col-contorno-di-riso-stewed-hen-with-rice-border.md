## Ingredients

- One hen, empty, weighing approximately 700 grams
- Rice, 300 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Fat and lean ham, 40 grams
- One onion, more than medium size
- One piece of carrot
- One handful of dried mushrooms

## Directions

1. Tie the hen to hold its parts together.
2. Put 30 grams of the butter and the ham cut into strips in a pot.
3. Chop the onion and carrot over it.
4. Place the hen breast-side down in the pot, seasoning it with salt and pepper.
5. Keep it covered and when browned on both sides, moisten it gradually with hot water until fully cooked, leaving enough sauce to flavor the rice, but strain the sauce.
6. Put the rice on the fire as is with half of the remaining butter.
7. Draw it to completion with hot water and finally with the hen's sauce.
8. When fully cooked, add the rest of the butter and give it greater flavor with a good handful of grated Parmesan.
9. Cook the liver and gizzard together with the hen and cut into pieces, mix them into the rice.
10. The risotto thus prepared can also serve as a soup and be enough for three people, but then serve the hen separately with some of its sauce and the mushrooms as a side dish.
