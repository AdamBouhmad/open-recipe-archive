## Ingredients

- One young rooster or young chicken
- Butter or oil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take a young rooster or young chicken, remove the neck and legs, and open it completely along the front, flattening it as much as possible.
2. Wash and dry it well with a cloth, then place it on a grill.
3. When it begins to brown, turn it, brush it with melted butter or oil using a brush, and season it with salt and pepper.
4. When the opposite side begins to take color, turn it and treat it in the same manner.
5. Continue brushing it with butter or oil and seasoning it sufficiently, keeping it over the fire until it is cooked.
