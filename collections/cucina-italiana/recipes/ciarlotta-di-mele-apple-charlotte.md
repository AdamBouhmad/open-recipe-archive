## Ingredients

- Reinette apples, 500 grams
- Powdered sugar, 125 grams
- Crumb of stale bread, as much as needed
- Fresh butter of good quality, as much as needed
- A piece of whole cinnamon
- Half a lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters, remove the seeds and the compartments that contain them and place them in fresh water where the lemon has been squeezed
2. Remove these quarters of apple dry and cut them crosswise into thin slices, which you will place on the fire in a saucepan without water and with the piece of cinnamon
3. When they begin to liquefy, add the sugar and stir, moving them often until they are cooked
4. Then remove the cinnamon and use it as follows
5. Melt the butter on the fire and when it boils, that is very hot, dip as many slices of bread crumb as needed, less than one centimetre thick, which you will have prepared before
6. With these cover the bottom of a smooth round mold and line the walls with them so that no empty spaces remain
7. Pour the preserve in the middle and cover it with the same slices of bread dipped in butter
8. Cook it
