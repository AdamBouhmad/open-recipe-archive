## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Oil
- Butter
- One slice of fat and lean prosciutto, finely minced
- A few small pieces of garlic
- One small sprig of rosemary
- Tomatoes in neat pieces without seeds, or tomato paste dissolved in water
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Place the chicken over the fire with oil, butter, finely minced fat and lean prosciutto, a few small pieces of garlic, and one small sprig of rosemary.
2. When it is browned, add tomatoes in neat pieces without seeds, or tomato paste dissolved in water.
3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it and in that sauce cook potatoes cut into small pieces, then return the chicken to the fire to reheat it.
