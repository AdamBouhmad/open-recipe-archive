## Ingredients

- Lean veal with bone or joint, 500 grams
- Cured meat (carnesecca), 50 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- One quarter of a large onion
- One small carrot
- Two pieces of celery
- Salt and pepper to taste
- A pinch of flour
- Tomato juice or tomato paste
- Water
- Dried mushrooms, a few pieces (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop the onion, carrot, and celery coarsely and cut the cured meat into small dice.
2. Put everything together in a pot and season with salt and pepper.
3. Turn the meat frequently and when it is browned, sprinkle a pinch of flour over it.
4. Pour tomato juice or paste over it and cook it through with water added little by little.
5. The flour serves to bind the sauce and give it color, but be careful that it does not burn or it will give an unpleasant taste and a nearly black color which the sauce should not have.
6. Strain the sauce, and if desired, add aroma with a few pieces of dried mushrooms, first softened in hot water and boiled a little in the sauce.
7. Cook the macaroni in salted water and drain well, then keep it briefly in the sauce near the fire and season with butter and a modest amount of parmesan cheese, as this can be added at table.
