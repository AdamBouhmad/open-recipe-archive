## Ingredients

- Dogfish
- Egg, lightly salted
- Breadcrumbs
- Parmesan cheese
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the dogfish into rounds not too thick and soak them in lightly salted egg for a few hours
2. Half an hour before frying, coat them in a mixture made of breadcrumbs, Parmesan, chopped garlic and parsley, salt and pepper (a small garlic clove is sufficient per 500 grams of fish)
3. Fry and serve surrounded with lemon slices
