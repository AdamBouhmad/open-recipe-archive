## Ingredients

- hind quarters of hare
- three glasses of water
- half a glass of vinegar or less
- three or four shallots
- one or two bay leaves
- a bunch of parsley
- salt
- pepper
- lardons of fine quality lard
- cream

## Directions

1. Infuse the hare for twelve to fourteen hours in a liquid prepared as follows: put on the fire three glasses of water with half a glass of vinegar or less in proportion to the size, three or four chopped shallots, one or two bay leaves, a small bunch of parsley, a bit of salt and a pinch of pepper; boil for five or six minutes and pour cold over the hare.
2. Remove from infusion and dry it well.
3. Stud it entirely with fine lardons using a larding needle.
4. Cook over gentle heat, salt sufficiently and baste with cream and nothing else.
