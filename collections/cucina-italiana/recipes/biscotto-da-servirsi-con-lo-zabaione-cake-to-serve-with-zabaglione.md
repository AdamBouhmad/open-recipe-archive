## Ingredients

- Potato starch flour, 50 grams
- Wheat flour, 20 grams
- Powdered sugar, 90 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Lemon zest flavor

## Directions

1. First work the egg yolks with the sugar for almost half an hour, add the well-beaten egg whites and let the flour fall from a sifter, mixing everything nicely so that the mixture remains light
2. Pour it into a ring mold that you have first buttered and dusted with flour and powdered sugar
3. Put it immediately in the oven to cook, and once cooled and unmolded, pour the zabaglione from recipe 684 into the hole and send it immediately to the table
4. This is a dose that can serve five or six people
