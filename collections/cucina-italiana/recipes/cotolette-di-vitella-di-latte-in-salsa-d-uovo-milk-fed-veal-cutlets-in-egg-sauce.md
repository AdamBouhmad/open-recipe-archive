## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Butter
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Brown and cook the cutlets sauté style as in recipes 312 and 313.
2. Spread over them a sauce of egg yolks, butter and lemon juice.
3. Keep them a little longer on the fire and serve.
4. For seven or eight cutlets, three egg yolks, 30 grams of butter and half a lemon, beaten together in a small pan before pouring, will suffice.
