## Ingredients

- Long zucchini, a good quantity as they reduce much
- Oil, a good measure
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Oregano, a good pinch

## Directions

1. Cut the zucchini into round slices the thickness of a coin.
2. Put a sauté pan or copper pan with oil on the heat in good measure and when it begins to boil throw in the zucchini as they are natural and, on high heat, stir them often.
3. Season them halfway through cooking with salt and pepper and when they begin to brown sprinkle a good pinch of oregano over them and remove them immediately with a slotted spoon.
4. They can be served alone or as a side dish and will be pleasing.
