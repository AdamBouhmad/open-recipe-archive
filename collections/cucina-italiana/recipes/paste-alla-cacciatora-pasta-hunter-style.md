## Ingredients

- two teal ducks
- pasta (small shapes like nocette, paternostri, penne)
- celery
- carrot
- parsley stems
- salt
- water
- butter
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Discard the head, feet, tail and intestines of the teal to boil them with a bunch garnished with celery, carrot and parsley stems in enough salted water to cook the pasta.
2. When cooked, bone them and chop them with a crescent-shaped knife together with the livers and gizzards emptied that you will have cooked with the teal.
3. Cook the pasta in the said broth, drain it well and season it in layers with this chopped meat, butter and parmesan in good measure.
4. This makes a tasty pasta and, what matters more, of not difficult digestion.
