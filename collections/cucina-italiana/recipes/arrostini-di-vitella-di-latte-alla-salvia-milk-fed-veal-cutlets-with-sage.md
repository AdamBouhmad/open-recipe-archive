## Ingredients

- Milk-fed veal loin free of skin and with its bone attached, cut into thin scaloppine
- Sage leaves
- Butter, proportioned amount
- Salt
- Flour, one teaspoon
- Marsala wine, just under one finger (of a glass)

## Directions

1. Use a sauté pan or a copper skillet, and put it on the fire with some whole sage leaves and a proportioned piece of butter. When it has sautéed a little, throw in the scaloppine and while they brown over high heat, salt them on both sides, then sprinkle a little flour over them and finish cooking them with Marsala. They should remain with little moisture.
