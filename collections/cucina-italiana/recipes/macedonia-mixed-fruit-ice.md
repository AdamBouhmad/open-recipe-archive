## Ingredients

- Ribes (currants)
- Fragole (strawberries)
- Lampone (raspberries)
- Ciliege (cherries)
- Susine (plums)
- Albicocche (apricots)
- Una pesca (one peach)
- Una pera (one pear)
- Zucchero (sugar), 100 grams for 500 grams of fruit
- Sugo di un limone di giardino (juice of one garden lemon)

## Directions

1. Take many varieties of fruit of the season, ripe and of good quality
2. Beginning with the cherries, peel all the said fruits and cut them into thin slices like pumpkin seeds approximately, throwing away the cores and pits
3. Use very little currant because it has seeds that are too large and hard; instead it would be good to add some fragrant melon
4. Having prepared the fruit in this manner, weigh it and, assuming it is in total 500 grams, scatter over it 100 grams of powdered sugar and the juice of one garden lemon
5. Mix them and let them rest for half an hour
6. Place a sheet of paper at the bottom of the said vessel, fill it by spreading the fruit pressed somewhat, close it and place it in a bucket surrounded by ice and salt, which should remain completely covered for several hours
7. If it does not unmold naturally, wet it with hot water and serve it as you will see it makes a fine appearance as a hard frozen and marbled piece
8. This is a dose for four or five persons
