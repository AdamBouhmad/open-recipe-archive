## Ingredients

- 1 veal tongue from a calf or beef
- 20-30 grams saltpeter
- salt
- cold water
- 1 bouquet garni
- 1/2 onion studded with 2 cloves
- gelatin (recipe No. 3)

## Directions

1. Take a veal tongue and rub it thoroughly with 20-30 grams of saltpeter depending on size until it has drawn in the salt.
2. After twenty-four hours, wash it several times with cold water.
3. While still damp, rub it generously with salt and leave it for eight days, turning it each morning in the brine produced by the salt dissolving in water.
4. To cook, place it in a pot with cold water, its natural brine, a bouquet garni, and half an onion studded with two cloves.
5. Boil for three or four hours.
6. Peel it while still boiling, let it cool, and serve it.
7. It will be an excellent and refined cold dish if you surround it with gelatin (recipe No. 3).
8. It can also be served hot, alone or accompanied by potatoes or spinach.
