## Ingredients

- Zucchini of elongated form, finger-sized or larger
- Flour
- Salt
- Oil, lard, or other fat for frying

## Directions

1. Take zucchini of elongated form the size of a finger and larger; wash them and cut them into strips about one centimeter wide or less, remove some of the pulp and salt them not too much. After one or two hours from this preparation drain them of the liquid they have released and, without drying them at all, throw them into flour and from there into a small sieve, shaking them well of excess flour; immediately after throw them into a pan where the fat, oil or lard, is found in abundance and boiling. At first do not touch them so as not to break them and only when they have solidified move them with a slotted spoon and remove them when they begin to take on color.
