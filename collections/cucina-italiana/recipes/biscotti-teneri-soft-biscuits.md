## Ingredients

- Wheat flour, 40 grams
- Potato flour, 30 grams
- Sugar, 90 grams
- Sweet almonds, 40 grams
- Candied citron or orange, 20 grams
- Fruit preserve, 20 grams
- Eggs, 3

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, cut them in half crosswise and dry them in the sun or by the fire.
2. Cut the candied fruit and preserve, which may be quince or other firm fruit, into small dice.
3. Work the egg yolks with the sugar and a little of the flour for more than half an hour.
4. Add the stiffly beaten egg whites and after mixing them in, add the flour, letting it fall from a small sieve.
5. Mix gently and scatter the almonds, candied fruit, and preserve into the dough.
6. Butter a tin box and dust it with flour.
7. Cut the biscuits the next day, toasting them on both sides if you like.
