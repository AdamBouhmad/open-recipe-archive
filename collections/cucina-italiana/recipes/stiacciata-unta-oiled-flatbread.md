## Ingredients

- Bread dough, 700 grams
- Lard, 120 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Cracklings, 60 grams
- Four egg yolks
- A pinch of salt
- Scent of orange or lemon zest

## Directions

1. Work it moderately so it does not lose its strength
2. Made in the evening and left in a warm place it rises by itself; made in the morning it will need three hours in an earthen chamber
3. If you want it without cracklings add two more egg yolks and another 30 grams of lard
4. Half this amount is enough for five or six people
