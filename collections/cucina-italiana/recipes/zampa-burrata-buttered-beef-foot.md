## Ingredients

- Beef foot
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Meat broth, a little
- Parmesan cheese
- Tomato juice or tomato conserve (if meat broth is unavailable)

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef foot and boil it
2. Bone it, cut into small pieces and place on heat with butter, salt and pepper, a little meat broth and parmesan when serving
3. If meat broth is lacking, tomato juice or tomato conserve can adequately substitute
