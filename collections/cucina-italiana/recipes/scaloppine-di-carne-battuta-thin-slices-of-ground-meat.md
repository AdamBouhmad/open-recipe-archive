## Ingredients

- lean meat from large beef
- salt and pepper
- grated parmesan
- spices as desired
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- tomato sauce or tomato paste diluted in broth

## Directions

1. Clean the meat of sinews and skin, and mince it finely with a knife and then with a crescent cutter
2. Season with salt, pepper, and grated parmesan; add spices if desired
3. Mix well and form the meat into a ball
4. With breadcrumbs under and on top so it doesn't stick, roll it out with a rolling pin on a work surface, moving it often to make a thin patty slightly larger than a scudo coin
5. Cut it into square pieces the width of a hand's palm
6. Cook them in a baking pan with butter
7. When the scaloppine have taken color, sprinkle them with tomato sauce or paste diluted in broth
