## Ingredients

- potatoes
- flour
- cheese
- butter
- tomato sauce or milk

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in water or, better, steamed and, while hot, peel them and pass them through a sieve.
2. Mix them with the flour and work the dough somewhat with your hands, stretching it into a thin cylinder so that it can be cut into pieces about three centimeters long.
3. Dust them lightly with flour and, taking them one at a time, hollow them out with your thumb on the reverse side of a grater.
4. Cook them in salted water for ten minutes, remove them dry and season them with cheese, butter and tomato sauce, if you like.
5. If you want them more delicate, cook them in milk and serve them without draining; if the milk is of good quality, apart from salt, no seasoning is necessary or at most a pinch of parmesan.
