## Ingredients

- one veal kidney (a piece of veal)
- 30 grams butter, melted
- salt
- pepper
- 30 grams butter
- one large anchovy or two small anchovies

## Directions

1. Take a veal kidney, remove the fat, open it and cover it with boiling water.
2. When the water has cooled, dry it well with a cloth and skewer it lengthwise and crosswise with clean skewers so that it stays open (in Paris they use silver pins).
3. Season it with 30 grams of melted butter, salt and pepper, and let it sit prepared for one or two hours.
4. If the kidney weighs 600 or 700 grams, take another 30 grams of butter and one large anchovy or two small ones, clean them, chop them and crush them with the blade of a knife together with the butter and form them into a ball.
5. Cook the kidney on a grill, but not too much so that it remains tender, place it on a platter, spread it while hot with the ball of butter and anchovy and serve it to the table.
