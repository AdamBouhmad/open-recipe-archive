## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Rice, 200 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Sweet almonds with 4 bitter ones, 100 grams
- Candied citron, 30 grams
- Whole eggs, 3
- Egg yolks, 5
- Lemon peel flavoring
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Shell the almonds and pound them in the mortar with two tablespoons of the said sugar
2. Cut the candied fruit into very small dice
3. Cook the rice very firm in the milk, pour in the condiment after, and when it is cold, the eggs
4. Put the mixture in a pan greased with butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs, set it firm in the oven or between two fires, the next day cut the cake into almonds and only when you serve it at table dust it with powdered sugar
