## Ingredients

- Rum, 1-1/2 liters
- Alcohol, 1 liter
- Water, 1 liter
- Fine white sugar, 1 kilogram
- Juice of three oranges
- The grated rind of one garden lemon kept in infusion for three days in one deciliter of the said alcohol

## Directions

1. Put the water and sugar on the fire and let it boil for five or six minutes
2. When it is cold add the rum, the juice of the oranges and the alcohol, including that of the infusion passed through cloth
3. Filter it like the other liqueurs and bottle it
4. It is customary to serve it lit in small glasses
