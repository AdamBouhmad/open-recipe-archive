## Ingredients

- Flour, 180 grams
- Butter, 60 grams
- Parmesan, 40 grams
- Milk, 4 decilitres
- Eggs, two whole and two yolks
- Salt, to taste
- Nutmeg
- A handful of spinach

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach, squeeze it well of water and pass it through a sieve.
2. Put the butter on the fire and when dissolved throw in the flour mixing well; then pour in the hot milk little by little, salt it and while it cooks work it with a spoon to make a homogeneous paste.
3. Remove it and when it is lukewarm thin it with the eggs adding the Parmesan and nutmeg.
4. Then divide this mixture into two equal parts, in one of which you will mix the said spinach in sufficient quantity to make it take on a green color and no more.
5. Place the mixture in a syringe with the round-hole stamp and push it into boiling broth like the passatelli of No. 48; but this operation must be done in two times, first with the yellow mixture and then with the green.
6. This dose will be sufficient for eight or ten persons.
