## Ingredients

- Milk, 2 decilitres
- Sugar, 30 grams
- Flour, half a spoonful
- Eggs, 1
- Lemon zest for flavoring
- Water, 2 decilitres
- Sugar, 50 grams
- Sponge cake, 300 grams
- Fruit preserves

## Directions

1. Make a cream with the milk, sugar, flour, egg and lemon zest
2. Make a syrup by boiling the sugar in water for ten minutes; when cooled squeeze a lemon into it
3. Take 300 grams of sponge cake and with it, cut into slices, with fruit preserves and the said cream, fill a well-worked pudding mold
4. Lastly pour the said syrup into it to soak the sponge cake or ladyfingers, and after several hours unmold it and serve it
5. This dose will be enough for eight people
