## Ingredients

- mallard duck
- 30 grams butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 500 milliliters water
- salt
- pepper
- 1/4 orange peel in one piece
- 1 celery rib about a palm long
- 1 piece of carrot

## Directions

1. Put the mallard duck in a pot with 30 grams of butter and let it brown.
2. Remove it and throw a tablespoon of flour into the remaining fat, stirring with a spoon until it takes on a brown color.
3. Remove from heat and not at a boil anymore, pour half a liter of water over that mixture and put the duck back in; season with salt and pepper and let it boil covered until completely cooked with a quarter of an orange peel in one piece, a celery rib about a palm long and a piece of carrot, both cut roughly.
4. Finally strain the sauce, break apart the duck at its joints, put it back in its sauce squeezing the juice of the orange over it to boil it a few more minutes and serve it.
