## Ingredients

- Cardoons
- Butter
- Salt
- Flour
- Pastella (batter) N. 156

## Directions

1. After removing the strings from the cardoons, boil them in salted water
2. Cut them into pieces and sauté them in butter, salting them a little more
3. Flour them, place them in batter N. 156 and fry them
4. They can serve as a side dish with a meat fry or a stew
