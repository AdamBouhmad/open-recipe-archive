## Ingredients

- Mature Claudia plums
- White powdered sugar, 60 grams of sugar per 100 grams of plums

## Directions

1. Any variety may do, but mature Claudia plums are to be preferred.
2. Remove the pit and after a few minutes of boiling pass them through a strainer and return them to the fire with white powdered sugar in the proportion of 60 grams of sugar for every 100 grams of plums in their natural state, that is as they come from the plant.
