## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cheese
- Butter

## Directions

1. Break the eggs when the water boils and let them fall from a low height.
2. When the white is well set and the yolk is no longer trembling, remove them with a slotted spoon.
3. Season them with salt, pepper, cheese and butter.
4. They may be served with tomato sauce, green sauce (recipe N. 119), sauce N. 127, or a specially made sauce prepared by dissolving an anchovy in hot butter and adding capers squeezed of vinegar and chopped somewhat.
5. They can also be served on a layer about a finger-thick of potato purée, or over spinach sautéed in butter.
