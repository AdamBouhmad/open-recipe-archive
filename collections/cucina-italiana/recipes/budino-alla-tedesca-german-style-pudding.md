## Ingredients

- Fine bread crumb, 140 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Sugar, 80 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Lemon zest flavoring
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. If you can find bread in loaf form, English style, use this as it is better than any other. Crumble or slice the bread crumb and soak it with ice-cold milk. When well soaked, squeeze it out with a cloth and pass it through a sieve. In winter, melt the butter in a bain-marie and work it with a wooden spoon together with the egg yolks until they are incorporated; add the whites, the bread crumb and the sugar and stir again. Pour the mixture into a mold greased with butter and dusted with breadcrumbs and cook it like the other puddings; that is in the oven. If you do it with care it will come out looking fine and tasting delicate. Serve hot.
