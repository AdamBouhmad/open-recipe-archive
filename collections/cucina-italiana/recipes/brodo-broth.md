## Ingredients

- Lean veal or beef cut into thin slices
- Cold water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut lean veal or beef into thin slices and arrange them one on top of the other in a wide pan
2. Salt them somewhat and pour over them enough cold water so that they are submerged
3. Cover the pan with a plate that closes it securely
