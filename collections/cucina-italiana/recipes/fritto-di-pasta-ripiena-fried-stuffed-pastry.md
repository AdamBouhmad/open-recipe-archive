## Ingredients

- Pasta N. 212 or puff pastry N. 154
- Filling from the previous recipe (N. 161)

## Directions

1. Take the pasta and roll it out to the thickness of a shield
2. Cut it into scalloped discs approximately the size indicated in the illustration
3. Place the filling from recipe N. 161 on each disc
4. Cover with equal-sized discs of the same pastry, moistening the edges so they stick together
5. Fry them and serve hot
