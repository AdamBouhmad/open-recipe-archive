## Ingredients

- Chicken livers, 2 pieces
- Anchovies, 1 piece
- Butter
- Broth
- Pepper
- Fresh marrow bread slices

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken livers in butter and when they have taken color, moisten them with broth; add a pinch of pepper, but no salt.
2. When cooked, mince them finely together with the washed and cleaned anchovy; then put the mixture back in the pan where the chicken livers were cooked, add a bit more butter, heat the mixture on the fire without letting it boil, and spread it on slices of fresh marrow bread.
