## Ingredients

- Salt cod, soaked, 400 grams
- butter, 70 grams
- flour, 30 grams
- potato, about 150 grams
- milk, 3 1/2 deciliters
- parsley, chopped
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil the salt cod and clean it of skin, spines, and bones.
2. Boil the potato and cut it into small pieces.
3. Make a béchamel sauce with the milk and flour.
4. When cooked, add some chopped parsley and flavor with nutmeg.
5. Pour in the potato pieces and salt.
6. Add the salt cod in pieces, mix well, and after a brief rest serve it.
7. Garnish with hard-boiled egg wedges if desired.
8. This quantity is sufficient for four people.
