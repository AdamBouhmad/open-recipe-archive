## Ingredients

- Shelled peas, 600 grams
- Fat and lean prosciutto, 50 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Flour, 20 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Parmesan, one spoonful

## Directions

1. Make a battutino with the prosciutto, a small young onion, and a pinch of parsley. Put it on the fire with oil and when it takes color, pour in the peas, seasoning them with salt and pepper.
2. When cooked, pass a quarter of them through a sieve and unite the puree to a roux made with the indicated butter and flour and diluted on the fire with meat stock or broth.
3. Then mix everything together, including the Parmesan, and cook the mixture in a bain-marie in a smooth mold with buttered paper underneath.
