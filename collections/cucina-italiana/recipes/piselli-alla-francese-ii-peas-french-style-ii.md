## Ingredients

- Onion
- Butter
- Flour, a pinch
- Broth, 1 or 2 ladlefuls
- Peas
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lettuce, 1 or 2 whole heads
- Sugar, 1 teaspoon (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice a good amount of onion into very thin slices and put it on the fire in a pot with a piece of butter.
2. When well browned, pour in a pinch of flour, stir, and then add, depending on the quantity, one or two ladlefuls of broth and let the flour cook.
3. Pour in the peas, season with salt and pepper, and at the halfway point of cooking add one or two whole heads of lettuce.
4. Boil slowly, taking care that the liquid does not become too thick.
5. Some sweeten the peas with a teaspoon of sugar; but in this case use little, because the sweetness should seem natural and not added artificially.
6. When you serve them remove the lettuce.
