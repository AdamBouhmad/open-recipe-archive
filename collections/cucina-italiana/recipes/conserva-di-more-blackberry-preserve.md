## Ingredients

- Blackberries, 1 kilogram
- White sugar, 200 grams

## Directions

1. Crush the blackberries by hand.
2. Bring to boil for about ten minutes.
3. Pass through a sieve.
4. Return to the heat with the mentioned sugar to reduce to the consistency of fruit preserves.
