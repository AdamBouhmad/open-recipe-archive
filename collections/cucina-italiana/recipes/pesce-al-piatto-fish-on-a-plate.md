## Ingredients

- various small fish or fish cut into thin slices
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- lemon juice
- good white wine

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and dry the fish.
2. Place it in a metal or porcelain vessel that can withstand fire and season it with a mixture of minced garlic and parsley, salt and pepper, oil, lemon juice and good white wine.
3. Place half of the mixture with a little oil at the bottom, lay the fish over it, and then, pouring more oil and the rest of the ingredients, make sure the fish swims in it.
4. Cook it with fire below and above; if the dish is porcelain, place it on hot ashes.
