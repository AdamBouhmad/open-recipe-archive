## Ingredients

- Fontina cheese, trimmed of rind, 400 grams
- butter, 80 grams
- egg yolks, 4 number
- milk, as needed

## Directions

1. Cut the Fontina into small dice and keep it soaking in milk for two hours
2. Put the butter on the fire and when it has taken color, add the Fontina, but of the milk in which it has been soaking, keep only two spoonfuls
3. Work it well with a wooden spoon without letting it boil and when the cheese is completely melted, remove it from the fire to add the yolks
4. Put it back on the fire for a little while, stirring again and in winter, pour it into a warm serving dish
5. If it has turned out well it should be neither grainy nor stringy, but have the appearance of a dense cream
