## Ingredients

- Chicken livers (removing the gall bladder)
- Chicken crests
- Chicken testicles
- Butter, in proportion
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Beef broth as needed
- Egg yolks
- Flour
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the gall bladder from the livers, blanch the crests with boiling water, cut them in two or three pieces and cut the livers in half.
2. Put on the fire with butter in proportion, first the crests, then the livers and last the testicles and season with salt and pepper, then broth if needed to cook these things.
3. In a small pot, put one or two egg yolks with a teaspoon or half only of flour, lemon juice and boiling broth whisking so the egg does not curdle.
4. Pour this sauce over the giblets when they are cooked, let it boil for a while and add more broth, if necessary, to make it more liquid, and serve it.
