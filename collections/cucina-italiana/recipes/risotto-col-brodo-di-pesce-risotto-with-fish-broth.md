## Ingredients

- fish broth
- quarter of an onion
- one or two cloves garlic
- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- tomato sauce or paste
- rice
- butter
- parmesan cheese
- dried mushrooms (optional for soup)
- ladle

## Directions

1. Make a soffritto with a quarter of an onion, one or two cloves of garlic, parsley, carrot and celery and place it on the fire with oil, salt and pepper
2. When it has taken color, stop it with tomato sauce or paste dissolved in a ladle of the said broth
3. Let boil a little and then pour in the rice which you will cook to completion with the same boiling broth, poured little by little
4. At half cooking add a piece of butter, and when the rice is cooked, a small handful of parmesan cheese
5. For soup you can add a pinch of dried mushrooms and serve the parmesan separately
