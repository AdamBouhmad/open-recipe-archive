## Ingredients

- Nutmeg, 2 whole
- Ceylon cinnamon (also called queen cinnamon), 50 grams
- Clove pepper, 30 grams
- Cloves, 20 grams
- Sweet almonds, 20 grams

## Directions

1. Pound everything together in a bronze mortar
2. Pass the spices through a fine silk sieve
3. Conserve them in a glass jar with a ground glass stopper, or in a small bottle with a cork stopper, and they will keep for years with the same fragrance as the first day
