## Ingredients

- Morello cherries or sour cherries, 400 grams
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Isinglass, 20 grams
- Water, 3 deciliters
- Rum, three tablespoons
- A small piece of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries and break them apart with your hands, adding some crushed pits.
2. Leave them thus for a few hours and then strain their juice through a cloth, squeezing well; let it rest further and then strain it several more times, even through paper or cotton, so that it remains clear.
3. Boil the sugar for ten minutes in two deciliters of the said water with the cinnamon in it, strain this also through the cloth and mix it with the juice of the cherries.
4. Add the isinglass dissolved in the remaining deciliter of water and finally the rum, proceeding as for the previous gelatins.
