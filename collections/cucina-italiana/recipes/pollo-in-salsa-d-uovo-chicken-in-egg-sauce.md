## Ingredients

- one young chicken, cut into pieces
- 50 grams of butter
- salt
- pepper
- a pinch of flour
- broth
- one egg yolk
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut a young chicken into pieces and place it in a casserole with 50 grams of butter.
2. Season it with salt and pepper.
3. When it has sautéed somewhat, sprinkle a pinch of flour over it to help it take on color, then cook it through with broth.
4. Remove it to a platter, keeping it warm, and into the remaining sauce pour an egg yolk, beaten beforehand with the juice of half a lemon, to form the sauce.
5. Stir it somewhat over the fire, pour it over the chicken and serve.
