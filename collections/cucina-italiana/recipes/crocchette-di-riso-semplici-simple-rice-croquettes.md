## Ingredients

- milk, half a liter
- rice, 100 grams
- butter, 20 grams
- grated parmesan cheese, 20 grams
- eggs, 2

## Directions

1. Cook the rice very firm in milk, and at half cooking add the butter and salt it
2. Remove from the fire and pour in the parmesan
3. While still boiling, break in one egg stirring immediately to incorporate it
4. When well cooled, take it up in spoonfuls and roll it in breadcrumbs giving it a cylindrical form; with this amount you will get twelve croquettes
5. Beat the remaining egg, drop the croquettes into it one by one, roll them again in breadcrumbs and fry them
6. They can be served alone but better accompanied with other types of fried foods
