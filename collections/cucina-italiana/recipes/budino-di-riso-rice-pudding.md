## Ingredients

- Milk, one liter
- Rice, 160 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Currants (sultanina), 80 grams
- Candied fruit, 30 grams
- Eggs, two whole and two yolks
- Rum or cognac, one small glass
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Cook the rice well in the milk and at half cooking throw into it the sugar, the currants, the candied fruit cut into very small pieces, a pinch of salt, and butter about the size of an egg minus a little. When cooked, remove it from the fire and while still hot, but not boiling, add the eggs, the rum and the vanilla, mixing everything well. Then pour it into a pudding mold well greased with butter and dusted with breadcrumbs; cook it in the oven or on the stove and serve it hot.
2. Set aside a third of the milk to add as needed as the rice thickens.
3. Will be enough for eight people.
