## Ingredients

- Large chestnuts, 150 grams
- Sugar, 90 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- Eggs, 5
- Milk, 2 deciliters
- Maraschino, 2 tablespoons
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil the chestnuts in water for only five minutes, which is enough to shell them while hot and remove their inner skin
2. Then cook them in the said milk and pass them through a sieve, then blend them with the sugar, melted butter, maraschino, and vanilla
3. Finally add the eggs, first the yolks, then the well-whipped whites
4. Grease a metal baking dish with butter, pour the mixture into it, cook it in a country oven and before sending it to the table dust it with powdered sugar
