## Ingredients

- Red mullet
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Celery
- Oil
- Tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Make a finely chopped mixture with garlic, parsley and a piece of celery.
2. Put it in a pot with a good measure of oil over heat.
3. When the garlic has taken color, add tomatoes in pieces and season with salt and pepper.
4. Let the tomatoes cook well, stir frequently and pass the sauce through a sieve.
5. Place the mullet in this sauce and cook them.
6. If they are small, they do not need to be turned over.
7. If the pot they have boiled in is not presentable, remove them one by one carefully not to break them and place them on a serving dish.
8. Just before removing from heat, lightly sprinkle with chopped parsley.
