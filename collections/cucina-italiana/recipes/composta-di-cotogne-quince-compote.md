## Ingredients

- Quinces, 500 grams
- Fine sugar, 180 grams
- Water, one and a half glasses

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces and cut them into wedges not too large, removing the middle part that was part of the core
2. Place them on the fire with water and when they have boiled covered for a quarter of an hour, pour in the fine sugar
3. Once cooked, remove them dry and place them in the dish you want to serve at the table; reduce the remaining juice to make it into a syrup, then pour it over the quinces and serve them cold
