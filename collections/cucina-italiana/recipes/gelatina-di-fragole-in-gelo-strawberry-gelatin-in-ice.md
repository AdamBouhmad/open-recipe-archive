## Ingredients

- Very red and well-ripened strawberries, 300 grams
- Sugar, 200 grams
- Isinglass, 20 grams
- Water, 3 deciliters
- Rum, three tablespoons
- The juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Squeeze the strawberries in a cloth to extract all their juice.
2. Boil the sugar for ten minutes in two deciliters of the said water and unite this syrup with the juice of the strawberries; add the lemon and strain everything again through a fine cloth.
3. To the isinglass, after having soaked it as in the previous recipe, bring it to a boil in the remaining deciliter of water and pour it hot into the aforesaid mixture; add rum last, mix and pour the mixture into the mold to set it in ice.
