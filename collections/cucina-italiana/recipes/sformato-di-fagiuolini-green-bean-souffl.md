## Ingredients

- 500 grams tender green beans
- salt
- meat sauce or oil, shallot, parsley, celery
- pepper
- 30 grams butter
- 1 scant tablespoon flour
- 2 deciliters milk
- 1 handful grated Parmesan cheese
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the ends and string from the green beans
2. Throw them into boiling water with a pinch of salt and as soon as they return to a boil, drain them and throw them into fresh water
3. If you have meat sauce, season them with this and butter; if not, make a soffritto with a quarter shallot, some parsley leaves, a piece of celery and oil, and when the shallot takes color, throw in the green beans, seasoning with salt and pepper and cooking them with a little water if necessary
4. Prepare a béchamel with 30 grams of butter, one scant tablespoon of flour and two deciliters of milk
5. With this, with a handful of grated Parmesan and four whisked eggs, bind the green beans, already cooled
6. Mix and pour the mixture into a smooth mold, first buttered and whose bottom is covered with a sheet
7. Cook it over heat or in a bain-marie and serve it hot
