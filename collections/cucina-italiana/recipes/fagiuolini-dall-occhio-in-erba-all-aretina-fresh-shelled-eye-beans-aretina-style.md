## Ingredients

- fresh-shelled eye beans
- 2 cloves garlic
- raw tomato sauce
- cold water
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Trim both ends and cut into three parts
2. Put them in a pot with two whole cloves of garlic, raw tomato sauce, and enough cold water to cover
3. Season with oil, salt and pepper
4. Place over heat and boil slowly until completely cooked
5. Make sure some sauce remains concentrated to make them more pleasant
6. They can serve as an intermediate course or as a side dish to boiled meat
