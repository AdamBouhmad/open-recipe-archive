## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Basil
- Chervil
- Pimpinella (salvestrella)
- Celery leaves
- Shallots, 2 or 3 or a small onion if shallots are lacking
- Anchovy or 2 if small
- Capers, sweetened
- Raw egg yolk, 1
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Mince everything finely, or pound it and pass it through a sieve, then put it in a sauce boat with a raw egg yolk, season it with oil, vinegar, salt and pepper; mix it well and serve it.
