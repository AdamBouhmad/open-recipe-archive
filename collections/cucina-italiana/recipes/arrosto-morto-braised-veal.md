## Ingredients

- milk-fed veal, a nice piece from the loin including the stone
- oil
- butter
- salt
- broth

## Directions

1. Roll the meat and tie it with string so it stays together.
2. Place it in a pot with a small amount of fine oil and butter.
3. Brown it on all sides.
4. Salt it halfway through cooking and finish cooking it with broth so that little or no sauce remains.
5. If you do not have broth, use tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water.
6. If you like it more flavorful, add finely minced cured meat.
