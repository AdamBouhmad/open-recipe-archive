## Ingredients

- Butter, 100 grams
- White powdered sugar, 100 grams
- Hungarian flour, 100 grams
- One egg white

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a bowl just as it is and begin to stir it with a spoon
2. Then pour in the sugar, then the flour and lastly the egg white, always working the mixture to reduce it to a homogeneous paste
3. Place it in a syringe with a disc with a round or square hole about one centimeter in size, and push it into a pan lightly greased with butter in the form of pieces one finger long, keeping them spaced out because, as they melt, they spread
4. Bake them in a country oven at moderate heat. With this dose you will obtain about fifty
