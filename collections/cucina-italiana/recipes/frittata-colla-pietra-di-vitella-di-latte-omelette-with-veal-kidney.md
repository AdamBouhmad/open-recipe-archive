## Ingredients

- one veal kidney
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- parsley, chopped
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Take a veal kidney, open it lengthwise, and leave all its fat intact
2. Season it with oil, pepper, and salt; cook it on a grill and cut it into thin slices across the width
3. Beat eggs in proportion to the amount of kidney, season these also with salt and pepper, and mix in a pinch of chopped parsley and some Parmesan
4. Throw the kidney into the eggs, mix, and make an omelette cooked in butter, which when the bottom is set, fold in half so it remains tender
