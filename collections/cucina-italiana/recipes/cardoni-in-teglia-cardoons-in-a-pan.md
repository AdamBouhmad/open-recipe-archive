## Ingredients

- Cardoons
- flour
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. First remove the filaments from the cardoons carefully and give them half cooking in salted water, then throw them immediately into fresh water so they do not blacken.
2. Cut them into small pieces, flour them and when the oil begins to boil, throw them in and season with salt and pepper.
3. Beat the eggs first and wait to pour them when the cardoons are browned on both sides.
