## Ingredients

- Yellow squash, peeled and cut into thin slices, one kilogramm
- Two ladles of broth
- 60 grams of butter
- Two level tablespoons of flour
- Fried bread cubes
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Put the squash to cook with two ladles of broth, then pass it through a sieve
2. Make a roux over the fire with 60 grams of butter and two level tablespoons of flour, and when it takes on a golden color, stop the cooking with broth
3. Add the strained squash and the rest of the broth sufficient for six people
4. Pour it boiling over fried bread cubes and serve the soup at table with grated Parmesan cheese on the side
