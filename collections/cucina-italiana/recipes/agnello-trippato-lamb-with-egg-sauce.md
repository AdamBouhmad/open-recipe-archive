## Ingredients

- 500 grams of lamb from the loin
- virgin lard for frying
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two eggs
- parmesan cheese, grated
- half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut 500 grams of lamb from the loin into small pieces and fry it with virgin lard.
2. Make a soffritto in a pan with the fat remaining in the pan, garlic, and parsley, and when the garlic has taken color, throw in the already fried lamb, season with salt and pepper, turn it well, and leave it on the fire for a while so the seasoning incorporates.
3. Then bind it with the following sauce: beat in a pot two eggs with a good pinch of grated parmesan and half a lemon.
4. Pour it into the lamb, stir, and when the egg has somewhat set, serve at table.
