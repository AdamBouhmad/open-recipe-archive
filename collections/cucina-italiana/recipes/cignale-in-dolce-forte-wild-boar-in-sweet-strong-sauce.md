## Ingredients

- wild boar, about 1 kilogram with skin and a finger's width of fat
- half an onion
- half a large carrot
- two stalks of white celery about a palm's length
- a pinch of parsley
- 30 grams of fatty and lean prosciutto
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot water
- 40 grams raisins
- 30 grams chocolate
- 30 grams pine nuts
- 20 grams candied fruit in small pieces
- 50 grams sugar
- vinegar, as needed

## Directions

1. Make a battuto (finely chopped mixture) with half an onion, half a large carrot, two stalks of white celery about a palm's length, a pinch of parsley and 30 grams of fatty and lean prosciutto. Mince it finely with a crescent-shaped knife and place it in a pot with oil, salt and pepper under the wild boar to cook together.
2. When the piece has browned on all sides, drain most of the fat, sprinkle a pinch of flour over it, and finish cooking it with hot water poured in from time to time.
3. Meanwhile prepare the sweet-strong sauce in a glass with the following ingredients and add it to the pot after straining the cooking liquid: 40 grams raisins, 30 grams chocolate, 30 grams pine nuts, 20 grams candied fruit in small pieces, 50 grams sugar, and vinegar as needed (use sparingly as you can add more later).
4. Before serving, let it boil again so that the sauce incorporates well. Note that the sweet-strong sauce works better if made a day in advance.
5. If you prefer a simpler version, make the sweet-strong sauce with only sugar and vinegar.
