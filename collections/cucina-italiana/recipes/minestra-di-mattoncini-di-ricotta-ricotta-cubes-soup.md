## Ingredients

- 200 grams ricotta
- 30 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 eggs
- salt to taste
- lemon zest and nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Break up the ricotta by passing it through a sieve, add the rest and the eggs one at a time
2. Mix well and pour the mixture into a smooth mold to cook it in a bain-marie
3. Unmold it when cool, remove the paper with which you will have covered the bottom of the mold and cut it into cubes of approximately one centimeter in size
4. Place them then in the tureen, pour boiling broth over them and send them to the table
5. This amount will be enough for five or six people
