## Ingredients

- Caesar's mushrooms (ovoli), 600 grams before cleaning (about 500 grams after)
- Lard, 50 grams
- Parsley, a pinch
- Butter, 50 grams
- Oil, three spoonfuls
- Salt, to taste
- One whole egg and one yolk
- Grated Parmesan cheese, a handful
- Toasted bread dice

## Directions

1. Clean and peel the mushrooms, then wash them whole and cut them into small, thin slices or pieces.
2. Make a soffritto (sauté mixture) with 50 grams of lard and a pinch of parsley and put it on the fire with 50 grams of butter and three spoonfuls of oil.
3. When it has softened, pour in the mushrooms and salt them somewhat to give them partial cooking, then pour them into the broth with all the soffritto to let them boil another ten minutes.
4. Before removing from heat, break a whole egg and one yolk in the tureen with a handful of grated Parmesan and pour the soup over it little by little stirring, then add dice of toasted bread; but note that the soup should remain very brothy.
5. This dose will serve six or seven persons.
6. If you make half the amount, only the whole egg will suffice.
