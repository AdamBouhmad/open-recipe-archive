## Ingredients

- Guinea fowl
- butter
- salt
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Place a ball of butter mixed with salt inside the guinea fowl
2. Lard the breast with strips of lard
3. Wrap in a sheet of cold butter spread and dusted with salt
4. Remove the sheet at two-thirds of cooking time to finish cooking and browning by the fire
5. Oil and salt again during the final cooking
