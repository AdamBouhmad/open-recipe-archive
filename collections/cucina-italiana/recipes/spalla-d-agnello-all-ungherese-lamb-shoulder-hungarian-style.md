## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- two new onions or three or four small white onions
- a small piece of butter
- salt
- pepper
- another small piece of butter
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut the shoulder into thin pieces about three fingers square.
2. Slice two new onions or three or four small white onions; put them to soften with a small piece of butter, and when they have taken on a deep red color, throw in the lamb and season with salt and pepper.
3. Wait for the meat to begin to color and add another small piece of butter mixed with flour; stir and let it take on a nice color, then finish cooking it with broth poured in little by little.
4. Do not serve it dry, but with a certain quantity of its own sauce.
