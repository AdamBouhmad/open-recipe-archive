## Ingredients

- Celery with full ribs
- Butter
- Meat stock
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Prosciutto, 30 grams
- Half a medium onion
- Flour
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard or oil for frying

## Directions

1. Select celery with full ribs and use only the white ribs and tender stems
2. Cut into 10 centimeter pieces (or 5 centimeters for the fried version)
3. After peeling the stem, cut it crosswise and keep it attached to the rib
4. Boil in salted water for no more than five minutes and remove, draining well
5. For the first preparation: sauté in butter, finish cooking with meat stock, and add Parmesan cheese when serving
6. For the second preparation: place 30 grams butter and a battuto made with 30 grams of prosciutto (fat and lean mixed together) and a quarter of a medium onion in a saucepan with two cloves; bring to a boil; when the onion colors, add broth and finish cooking the soffritto; pass everything through a sieve and place the sauce in a pan with the celery pieces; season with pepper (salt is not needed) and serve with the sauce
7. For the third preparation: flour the celery, dip in batter (recipe N. 156) and fry in lard or oil; or better yet, after flouring, dip in egg, bread it, and fry; this last preparation works better as a side dish to braised meat dishes, which you can use to moisten it
