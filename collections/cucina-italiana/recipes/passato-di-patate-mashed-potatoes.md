## Ingredients

- Potatoes, large and floury, 500 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Good milk or cream, half a glass
- Salt, to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes
2. Peel them while still hot
3. Pass them through a sieve while hot
4. Put them in a pot over the fire with the butter, milk or cream and salt
5. Work them well with a spoon to refine them
6. Test if cooked by piercing with a pointed stick that should pass through freely
