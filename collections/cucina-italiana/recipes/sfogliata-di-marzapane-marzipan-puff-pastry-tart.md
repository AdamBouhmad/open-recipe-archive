## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough (quantity and proportions as in recipe N. 154)
- Marzipan mixture (as in recipe N. 579)
- Egg yolk for gilding
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry dough in the quantity and proportion of N. 154.
2. When spread out, cut two rounds the size of a common plate, with large scalloped edges.
3. Over one of them, leaving a bit of margin, spread the marzipan mixture of N. 579, which should be about one centimeter in height.
4. Lay the other round of puff pastry over it, attaching them together at the edges with a finger dipped in water.
5. Gild the surface of the tart with egg yolk.
6. Bake it in the oven or in a country oven.
7. After baking, dust it with powdered sugar.
8. This quantity will serve seven or eight people.
