## Ingredients

- Butter, 20 grams
- Flour, one heaping teaspoon
- Fish broth, two ladles
- Oil, two spoonfuls
- One egg yolk
- Lemon juice, half of one
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Place on the fire in a small pot 20 grams of butter with one heaping teaspoon of flour and after it has taken on a hazelnut color, pour over it little by little two ladles of broth from the fish itself
2. When you see that the flour, while boiling, no longer rises, remove the sauce from the fire and pour into it two spoonfuls of oil and one egg yolk, stirring well
3. Finally add the juice of half a lemon, salt and pepper in good measure
4. Let it cool and then pour it over the fish which you will send to the table surrounded by fresh parsley
5. This sauce should have the appearance of a cream not too liquid to remain attached to the fish
6. You will find it is good and delicate
7. For those who do not like cold fish serve it hot
