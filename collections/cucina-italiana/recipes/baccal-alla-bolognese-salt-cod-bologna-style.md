## Ingredients

- salt cod
- oil
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- butter pieces
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces as large as the previous recipe and place raw in a pan or baking dish oiled
2. Sprinkle with a finely chopped mixture of garlic and parsley on top and season with pepper, oil and small pieces of butter
3. Cook over high heat and turn gently because, not being floured, it breaks easily
4. When cooked, squeeze lemon over it and serve
