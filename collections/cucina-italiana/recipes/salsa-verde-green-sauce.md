## Ingredients

- Capers, squeezed of vinegar
- Anchovy, 1 piece
- Small amount of onion
- Very little garlic
- Parsley, generous amount
- Basil leaves
- Fine oil
- Lemon juice
- or bell peppers as substitute for capers

## Directions

1. Mince everything together with a crescent-shaped knife: capers squeezed of vinegar, an anchovy, a little onion and very little garlic.
2. Crush the mixture with the blade of a knife to make it fine and place it in a sauce boat.
3. Add a generous amount of parsley, minced with some basil leaves, and dissolve everything with fine oil and lemon juice.
4. Lacking capers, bell peppers can serve as a substitute.
