## Ingredients

- Mutton spleen, 120 grams
- Anchovies, 2 pieces
- Butter
- Meat juice or broth
- Small diced onion
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Bread crumbs, 1 teaspoon
- Bread slices
- Butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the spleen and cook it with butter and meat juice. In the absence of this, use a small mixture of a little onion, oil, butter, salt, pepper and spices for seasoning.
2. Then mince these ingredients together with the anchovies very finely, put them back on the fire in the sauce that remains, adding a teaspoon of bread crumbs to bind them together, and without letting them boil further, spread them on slices of bread that you have first dried by the fire without roasting and brushed with butter.
