## Ingredients

- Bruised Peruvian quinine bark, 50 grams
- Dried rind of bitter orange bruised, 5 grams
- Alcohol, 700 grams
- Water, 700 grams
- White sugar, 700 grams

## Directions

1. First mix 250 grams of the said alcohol with 150 grams of the said water, and in this mixture place in infusion the quinine and orange rind, keeping it in a tepid place for about ten days, stirring the vessel at least once a day
2. Then strain it through cloth squeezing well so that all the substance comes out, and filter it through paper
3. After this, dissolve the sugar in the fire in the remaining 550 grams of water without letting it boil and pass it through a sieve, or better through cloth, to clean it of any impurity if there were any
4. Add the remaining 450 grams of alcohol, mix everything together and the elixir will be done
5. Before filtering it taste it and if it seems too strong to you add water
