## Ingredients

- Egg whites, 6
- Sweet almonds, 170 grams
- Powdered sugar, 170 grams

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the sun or by the fire, chop them with a mincing knife and put them on the fire in a saucepan when the sugar is dissolved
2. After the mixture has taken on the color of brittle, or the color of almond skin, pour it into a mortar and, once cooled, reduce it to powder
3. Mix this powder with the six beaten egg whites, put the mixture in a mold buttered with only cold butter and cook it in a bain-marie to serve it cold
