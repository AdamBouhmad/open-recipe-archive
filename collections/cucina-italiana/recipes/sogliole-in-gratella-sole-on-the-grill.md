## Ingredients

- large sole fish
- lard, virgin and cold
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the guts, scrape off the scales, wash and dry the sole well
2. Lightly coat with cold virgin lard that does not smell rancid
3. Season with salt and pepper and roll in breadcrumbs
4. Melt a little more lard in a small pan and brush the sole with a feather as you grill it and when you turn it over on the grill
