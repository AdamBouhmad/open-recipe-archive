## Ingredients

- Red mullet (about half kilogram)
- Garlic (two cloves)
- Parsley
- Oil
- Tomato juice
- Red wine
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Make a finely chopped mixture with two cloves of garlic and a good pinch of parsley.
2. Put it in a pot or pan with a good measure of oil over heat, large enough for the mullet to lay flat.
3. When the soffritto is browned, stop it with plain tomato juice.
4. Let it boil briefly, then place the mullet in it, turning them one by one in the sauce.
5. Cover and let them boil slowly.
6. When they have absorbed a good part of the liquid, add a finger of glass of red wine diluted with two fingers of water.
7. Let them boil a little longer and serve.
