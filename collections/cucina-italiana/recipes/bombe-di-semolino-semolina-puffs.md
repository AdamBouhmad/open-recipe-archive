## Ingredients

- Milk, 3 deciliters or 300 grams
- Fine-grain semolina, 130 grams
- Butter, size of a walnut
- Sugar, one teaspoon
- Salt, as needed
- Lemon zest
- Eggs, 3

## Directions

1. Put milk on the fire with butter and sugar
2. When it begins to boil, add the semolina little by little so it does not form lumps
3. Keep it on the fire until well firm, stirring always with a spoon so it does not stick to the bottom
4. Remove from the fire, salt it and immediately break in the first egg, then when about to fry it, add the other two, one at a time, beating the whites and working it always much with the spoon
5. When you throw it in the pan, give it the shape of small balls which will puff up to become very light puffs
6. Dust with powdered sugar once they have lost their strong heat
7. Use gentle fire and shake the pan
