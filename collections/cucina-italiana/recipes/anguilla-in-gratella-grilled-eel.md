## Ingredients

- medium eel
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the medium eel on a grill with its skin.
2. Season with salt and pepper only at half cooking.
3. The lemon juice softens the skin when it is brought to the table and can offer a pleasant flavor when sucked.
4. Eat them hot.
