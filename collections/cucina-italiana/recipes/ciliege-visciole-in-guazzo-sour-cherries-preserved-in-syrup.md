## Ingredients

- Sour cherries, 1 kilogram
- White sugar, 300 grams
- A small piece of cinnamon

## Directions

1. From the sour cherries, separate 200 grams of the worst or damaged ones, extract their juice and strain it
2. The others, having removed the stem, layer them in a crystal vase: one of cherries and one of sugar, then pour the said juice over them
3. Remove the pits from some of the damaged cherries and throw these along with the cinnamon into the vase
4. Close the vase and do not move it for at least two months
5. You will see that the sugar will gradually dissolve and the cherries will first float in the liquid, then as this converts to alcohol the cherries sink to the bottom and then they are edible and good
