## Ingredients

- artichokes
- slice of fatty and lean prosciutto
- butter
- veal or beef bracioline
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- oil
- tomato juice or tomato paste
- water

## Directions

1. Remove all the tough leaves from the artichokes and cut them into four or five wedges.
2. Take a slice of fatty and lean prosciutto and chop it very finely, mix it with a little butter and spread this mixture over the artichoke wedges.
3. Pound and flatten the bracioline, which can be of veal or beef, season them with salt and pepper and place two or three of the prepared artichoke wedges in the middle of each one, then roll them up and tie them with string in a cross pattern.
4. Make a battutino with a little onion, put it in a pot with butter and oil and when the onion is well browned place the bracioline in it and season them again with salt and pepper.
5. Brown them, then finish cooking them with tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water, and when you serve them remove the string.
