## Ingredients

- Flour, 220 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Milk, as needed
- Salt, a pinch
- Salted anchovies, 4 pieces

## Directions

1. Mix flour with butter, milk, and salt to form a dough of proper consistency, working it well if you want the pasta to puff in the pan.
2. Let it rest briefly, then cut it in half and stretch both pieces open somewhat.
3. Clean the anchovies, divide them lengthwise in half, remove the bone, and cut them into small square pieces. Place these spread over one of the pasta pieces, cover with the other piece to seal them together.
4. Stretch the united pieces with a rolling pin to a thin sheet and cut into almond shapes.
5. Fry in oil. This amount is sufficient for six people and can serve as a first course at breakfast or as an accompaniment to a fish fry.
