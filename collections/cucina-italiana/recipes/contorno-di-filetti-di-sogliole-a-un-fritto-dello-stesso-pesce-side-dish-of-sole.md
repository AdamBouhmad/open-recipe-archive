## Ingredients

- sole fish, a pair of medium size or one large
- lemon juice
- milk
- flour
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Take a pair of medium sole or one large sole, remove the fillets after skinning them (there will be four), and cut them crosswise into fine strips like matches
2. If you cut them at an angle they will be somewhat longer and better
3. Put them in a bowl with the juice of one or more lemons as needed and let them marinate for two or three hours, which will make them firm, otherwise they would be limp
4. Shortly before serving, dry them with a cloth, immerse in milk, flour them, make sure they don't clump, and fry in oil
5. Salt them lightly
