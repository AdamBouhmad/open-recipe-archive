## Ingredients

- Eggs, 4
- Butter, 40 grams
- Cream, 1 deciliter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread, toasted slices
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Put the butter on the fire and when it begins to fry, pour in the beaten eggs.
2. Season with salt and pepper and, constantly stirring with a spoon, add the cream little by little.
3. Once the mixture is set, cover three slices of toasted bread, nearly a finger thick and without crust, which you will have previously arranged on a platter and buttered while hot.
4. Dust them with grated Parmesan cheese and serve.
