## Ingredients

- Fresh egg, 1
- Powdered sugar, 2-3 teaspoons
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolk well in a cup or bowl shape with 2-3 teaspoons of powdered sugar
2. Whip the egg white until stiff and fold it in gently so it does not deflate
3. Place the cup in front of the child with slices of bread to dip, with which he will make yellow whiskers
