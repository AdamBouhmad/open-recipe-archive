## Ingredients

- Flour, 250 grams
- Eggs, 6
- Water, 3 deciliters
- A pinch of salt
- Lemon zest flavoring
- Butter, oil, or lard
- Sugar, powdered

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the said water poured a little at a time and salt it.
2. Drop this mixture into a pan to cook it in white with butter, oil, or lard and when it is set on one side, turn it over with a plate on the other side, and there is your tondone.
3. Now pound it in the mortar with the said flavoring and soften it with the eggs: two at once, the other four one at a time with whipped whites, working the mixture a lot.
4. Fry it by spoonfuls to obtain fritters that, puffing greatly, take on the appearance of bombs.
5. Dust them with powdered sugar.
6. If you like, you can add 100 grams of Málaga raisins to the mixture, but in that case first soak them for twenty-four hours in fresh water and then remove the seeds.
7. Should be enough for six people, or for four if you make half the recipe.
