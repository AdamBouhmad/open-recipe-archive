## Ingredients

- Semolina of fine grain, 70 to 80 grams
- Milk, 3 deciliters
- Eggs, 1
- Sugar, three teaspoons
- Butter, the size of a walnut
- Salt, a pinch
- Lemon zest flavoring

## Directions

1. Put the milk on the fire with butter and sugar and when it begins to boil pour in the semolina little by little, stirring continuously with a spoon
2. Salt it and beat an egg into it; mix and when the egg has incorporated remove the semolina from the fire and spread it on a buttered tray or on a floured work surface to the height of a finger
3. Cut it into almond shapes and dip first in beaten egg then in fine breadcrumbs and fry it
4. Dust it with icing sugar if you desire it sweeter, and serve it alone or, better yet, as a side dish to a meat fry
