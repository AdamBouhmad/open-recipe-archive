## Ingredients

- Sugar, 150 grams
- Isinglass, 20 grams
- Water, 4 deciliters
- Alkermes, four tablespoons
- Rum, two tablespoons
- One large sweet orange
- One lemon

## Directions

1. Soak the isinglass and, changing the water once, leave it for one or two hours.
2. Boil the sugar in half of the aforementioned water for ten minutes and strain it through a cloth.
3. Squeeze the orange and lemon into this syrup, passing their juice through the same cloth.
4. Take the isinglass that has been soaked and bring it to a boil in the remaining two deciliters of water and pour this also into the said syrup.
5. Add to it the alkermes and rum, mix everything and when it begins to cool pour it into the mold held in ice in summer and in cold water in winter.
