## Ingredients

- Flour, 60 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- Parmesan, 30 grams
- Milk, 4 decilitres
- Eggs, 4
- Salt, to taste
- Nutmeg, if desired

## Directions

1. Put the butter on the fire and as soon as it is melted pour in the flour.
2. Stir, and when it begins to take color pour in the milk little by little.
3. Let it boil for a while, then remove the mixture from the fire and season it by adding the eggs last when it is cooled.
4. Cook it in a bain-marie as with semolino soup No. 15 and proceed as with the same.
5. This dose will serve eight or ten persons.
