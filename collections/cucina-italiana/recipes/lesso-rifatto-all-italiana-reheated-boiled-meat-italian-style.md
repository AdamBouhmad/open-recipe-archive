## Ingredients

- Beef for boiling
- Small onions, 150 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- Parsley
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. If onion does not bother you, this turns out better than the previous one. For the same quantity of boiled meat mince 150 grams of small onions, put them in a pan with 50 grams of butter and when they begin to brown throw in the boiled meat cut into thin slices, one clove of garlic whole, dressed and lightly crushed, which you will then remove, and season it with salt and pepper. As it begins to dry out wet it with broth and after seven or eight minutes add a pinch of chopped parsley and the juice of half a lemon, and serve it.
