## Ingredients

- Flour, approximately 180 grams
- Oil, two spoonfuls
- White wine or Marsala, two spoonfuls
- Sage, five or six leaves
- One egg
- Salt, as needed

## Directions

1. Chop the sage with a half-moon knife.
2. Mix the flour with all the ingredients, working it well and ensuring the dough remains rather soft.
3. Stretch it with a rolling pin to the thickness of a coin, dusting with flour if necessary, and cut into almond shapes.
4. Fry in oil or lard. This quantity is sufficient for four people.
5. Some people eat them together with figs and ham.
