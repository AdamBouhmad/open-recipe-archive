## Ingredients

- Flour, 220 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Salt, a pinch
- Milk, as much as needed to mix it
- Mostarda di Savignano or homemade mustard (recipe N. 788)

## Directions

1. Form a rather tender pasta with flour, butter, salt, and milk, working it well with your hands on a pastry board
2. Let it rest for half an hour
3. Roll it out with a rolling pin to the thickness of a thin shield
4. Cut many discs with the stamper from N. 162 (approximately 80 discs)
5. Place some mustard on 40 of them and cover with the other 40, first moistening their edges with a finger dipped in water to stick them together
6. Fry them and dust them with sugar before serving
