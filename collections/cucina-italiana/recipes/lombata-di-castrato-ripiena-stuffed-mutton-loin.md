## Ingredients

- 1 piece of mutton loin with its attached membrane, weighing 1 kilogram
- 150 grams lean veal from milk-fed calf
- 50 grams fat and lean prosciutto
- 40 grams grated Parmesan
- 1 egg
- Salt and pepper
- 50 grams butter
- A finger (measure) of Marsala
- 1 small onion, cut in two pieces
- 2 or 3 pieces of celery
- 2 or 3 pieces of carrot
- Parsley stems
- Water or broth

## Directions

1. Take a piece of mutton loin with its attached membrane, weighing 1 kilogram, degrease it somewhat but not completely, bone it and season with salt and pepper
2. Prepare the filling by mixing together 150 grams lean veal from milk-fed calf, 50 grams fat and lean prosciutto, 40 grams grated Parmesan, 1 egg, salt and pepper
3. Mince the filling very fine
4. Spread this filling all over the inside of the loin
5. Roll it up, pulling the membrane over it, and sew it so the filling does not burst out
6. Place it on the fire with 50 grams of butter and when browned, moisten it with a finger measure of Marsala
7. Add to the pan to braise slowly with it: half a fairly small onion cut into two pieces, 2 or 3 pieces of celery, the same amount of carrot, and parsley stems
8. Moisten with water or broth to complete the cooking
9. Finally strain the sauce and the rest, degrease it and serve it
10. This is a dish that can serve eight people and deserves to be recommended
