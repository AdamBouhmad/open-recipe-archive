## Ingredients

- Potato flour, 120 grams
- Powdered sugar, 120 grams
- Eggs, 4
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. First beat very well the egg yolks with the sugar, add the flour and lemon juice and work all of it for more than half an hour
2. Finally beat the egg whites, unite them to the rest mixing with delicacy so as not to deflate the foam
3. Pour the mixture into a smooth and round mold, or into a proportioned baking pan, buttered and dusted with powdered sugar and flour, and place it immediately in the oven
4. Unmold it while cold and dust it with vanilla powdered sugar
