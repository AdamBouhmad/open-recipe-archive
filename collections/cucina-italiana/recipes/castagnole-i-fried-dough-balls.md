## Ingredients

- flour
- two eggs
- one tablespoon of meat stock
- lemon zest
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. On a work surface, make a firm dough with flour, two eggs, a tablespoon of meat stock, lemon zest and salt to taste.
2. Work it thoroughly and strongly with your hands as you would common bread, making it gradually absorb a tablespoon of fine oil little by little.
3. Finally, shape it into small sticks, cut them into pieces the size of a walnut and immediately put them in a pan over low heat, stirring continuously.
4. When the castagnole are cooked, dust them with powdered sugar and serve them cold, as they are better cold than hot.
5. Note: if you use cognac or brandy instead of meat stock, you will not obtain the same effect and they will not puff up as much.
