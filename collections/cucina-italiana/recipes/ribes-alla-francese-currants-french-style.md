## Ingredients

- Currants in their clusters, raw
- Gum arabic powder
- Water
- Crystalline powdered sugar, not confectioners' sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a light solution of gum arabic powder dissolved in water
2. Take the raw currants in their little clusters with your fingers, dip them one at a time into the solution and dust them with crystalline powdered sugar, but not confectioners' sugar
3. Arrange them on a plate and this brilliant red base will make a fine display among the fruits of a meal and will be much appreciated by ladies
4. You may also intersperse red currants with white ones
