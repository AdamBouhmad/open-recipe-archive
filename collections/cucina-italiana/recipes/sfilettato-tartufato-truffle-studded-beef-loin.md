## Ingredients

- a large piece of beef loin (sfilettato)
- truffles, white preferred, cut into points about three centimeters long
- butter
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the entire piece of beef with pieces of truffle, adding to each piece a small piece of butter to fill the hole you have made with the knife tip to insert them.
2. Make incisions across the skin so it does not shrink.
3. Tie it and thread it onto the spit for cooking.
4. When two-thirds cooked, brush it lightly with oil and salt it sparingly, as these meats from large animals are quite flavorful and do not need much seasoning.
