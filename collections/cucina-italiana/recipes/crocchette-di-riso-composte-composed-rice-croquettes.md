## Ingredients

- rice croquette mixture (recipe from preceding recipe)
- chicken giblets cooked in butter and gravy
- or onion mixture if gravy is lacking

## Directions

1. Use the recipe for simple rice croquettes and mix into the rice when cooked and measured the giblets of a chicken cooked down with butter and gravy (or supply with an onion mixture if the gravy is lacking)
2. Cut the giblets after cooking to the size of a chickpea
