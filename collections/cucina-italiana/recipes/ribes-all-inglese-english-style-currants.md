## Ingredients

- Currants, 300 grams
- Sugar, 120 grams
- Water, 2 deciliters

## Directions

1. Clean the currants of their stems
2. Put them on the fire with the said water
3. When it comes to a boil, pour in the sugar
4. Two minutes of boiling is enough, as the currants should remain whole
5. Pour it into a compote dish and serve it cold as cooked fruit
6. The seeds, if you do not wish to swallow them, can be sucked and spit out
