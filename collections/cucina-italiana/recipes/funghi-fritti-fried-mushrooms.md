## Ingredients

- medium-sized mushrooms
- flour
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose mushrooms of medium size which are also of proper ripeness
2. Scrape the stem, clean them from earth and wash them whole without soaking them, as they would lose their pleasant aroma in the water
3. Then cut them into rather thick slices and flour them before throwing them into the pan
4. Oil is the best fat for this frying, and the seasoning consists exclusively of salt and pepper which you scatter over them when they are still boiling
