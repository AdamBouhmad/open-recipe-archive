## Ingredients

- 20 small amaretti
- rum or cognac
- batter (recipe N. 156)
- lard, butter or oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take 20 small amaretti and lightly dip them so they don't soften too much in rum or cognac
2. Roll them in the batter from recipe N. 156, which is a sufficient amount
3. Fry them in lard, butter or oil
4. Lightly dust with powdered sugar and serve them hot
