## Ingredients

- Rock red mullet (500-600 grams)
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Parsley
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Season the fish with oil, salt and pepper.
2. Cook over high heat.
3. When removing from heat, coat while hot with a mixture of butter, chopped parsley and lemon juice prepared beforehand.
4. Serve immediately.
