## Ingredients

- Garden lemons, 3 number
- Fine white sugar, 600 grams
- Water, one table glass corresponding to about 3 decilitres

## Directions

1. Remove from the lemons, without squeezing it, the internal pulp cleaning it well from the membranes and seeds.
2. Put the water on the fire with the peel of one of the said lemons cut into strips and thinly with a knife and when it begins to boil pour in the sugar.
3. Wait for it to bubble a few times, then remove the peel and pour in the pulp of the lemons.
4. Boil until the syrup has reduced and cooked to the point, which is known by the pearl that forms boiling and by the color of white wine that it acquires.
5. Preserve it in a glass vessel if possible, to take it by the spoonful and dissolve it in fresh water: thus obtaining an excellent and refreshing drink which I am amazed is lacking among the drinks of cafés, in various provinces of Italy.
