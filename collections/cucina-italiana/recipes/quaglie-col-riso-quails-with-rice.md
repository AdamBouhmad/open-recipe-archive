## Ingredients

- Quails, 4
- Ham (prosciutto)
- Onion, 1/4
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Broth
- Rice, 400 grams
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Make a battuto with ham and a quarter onion
2. Put this in a pan with butter and cook until the onion is colored
3. Place clean, gutted, whole quails in the pan
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Once browned, partially cook the quails with broth
6. Add rice and cook with enough broth until done, together with the quails
7. Season with Parmesan cheese when cooked
8. Serve either broth-rich or dry, as preferred, mixed with the quails
9. Serves four people
