## Ingredients

- Quinces, any quantity
- Water
- White powdered sugar, equal to the weight of the raw quinces

## Directions

1. Cut the quinces into segments.
2. Remove the hard part of the core.
3. Leave the skin on and after weighing them, put them on the heat covered with water.
4. When well cooked, pass them and return them to the heat with the water in which they boiled and as much white powdered sugar as was their raw weight, waiting to pour it when they are boiling.
5. Stir often and when (pouring a few drops on a plate) you do not see it running too much, remove it.
