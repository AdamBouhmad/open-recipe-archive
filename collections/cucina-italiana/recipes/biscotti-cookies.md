## Ingredients

- Flour, 250 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Powdered sugar, 50 grams
- Ammonia powder, 5 grams
- A pinch of salt
- Vanilla flavoring with vanilla sugar
- Lukewarm milk, approximately one deciliter

## Directions

1. Make a well in the mound of flour and place in it the aforementioned ingredients except the milk, which you will use to knead this dough, which should turn out soft and must be worked thoroughly to refine it
2. Then stretch out a sheet about one scudo thick, dusting it with flour if needed, and finally pass over it a ridged rolling pin, or use a grater or fork to make some decorations
3. After that, cut the cookies into whatever shape you prefer, or if you do not want to make long strips just slightly longer than a finger and two centimeters wide as I do
4. Place them directly in a copper baking pan and bake them in the oven or in a country oven
