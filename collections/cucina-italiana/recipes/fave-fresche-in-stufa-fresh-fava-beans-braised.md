## Ingredients

- Pods of large fava beans
- Young onion
- Oil
- Fat and lean prosciutto
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lettuce
- Broth

## Directions

1. Take pods of large fava beans and shell and peel them.
2. Finely chop a young onion, put it on the fire with oil, and when it begins to brown, throw into the soffritto prosciutto cut into cubes.
3. After a short time add the fava beans, season with pepper and a little salt, and when they have taken on the seasoning, add one or two heads of lettuce cut into large pieces and finish cooking them with broth if needed.
