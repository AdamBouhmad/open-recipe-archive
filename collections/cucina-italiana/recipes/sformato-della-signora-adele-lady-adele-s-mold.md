## Ingredients

- Butter, 100 grams
- Flour, 80 grams
- Gruyère cheese, 70 grams
- Milk, half a liter
- Eggs, 4
- Bread crumbs
- Chicken offal and sweetbreads for filling

## Directions

1. Make a béchamel with the flour, milk, and butter. Before removing from heat, add the grated or diced Gruyère and salt.
2. Remove from boiling heat and add the eggs, first the yolks one at a time, then the stiffly beaten whites.
3. Pour into a smooth mold with a hole in the center after buttering it and dusting with bread crumbs.
4. Bake in a country oven and serve filled with a ragout of chicken offal and sweetbreads.
5. This serves six people.
