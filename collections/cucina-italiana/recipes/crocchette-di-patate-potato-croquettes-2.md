## Ingredients

- potatoes, 300 grams
- butter, 30 grams
- grated parmesan cheese, 20 grams
- egg yolks, 2
- sugar, one teaspoon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and if they are large cut them into four pieces and put them to boil in salted water
2. Pass them through a sieve dry when they are cooked and still boiling
3. To the puree add the butter, melted in winter, and all the rest, mixing
4. Let the mixture cool well and divide it into ten or twelve parts
