## Ingredients

- Rice, 500 grams
- Butter, 100 grams
- Parmesan cheese, as needed
- One onion of medium size
- Peas from recipe No. 427, in proper proportion

## Directions

1. Clean and rub the rice in a cloth without washing it
2. Finely chop the onion with a crescent knife and put it on the fire with half the butter
3. When it has taken on a red color, pour in the rice and stir constantly with a wooden spoon until it has absorbed all the soffritto
4. Begin pouring hot water one ladle at a time, being careful that if it boils too tightly it will become hard in the center and break apart on the surface
5. Salt it and cook until dry, adding the remaining butter
6. Before removing from the fire, add the peas in proper proportion and give it flavor with a good handful of Parmesan cheese
7. This portion will be enough for five people
