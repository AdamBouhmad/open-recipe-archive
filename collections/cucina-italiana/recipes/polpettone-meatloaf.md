## Ingredients

- Cooked meat, left over
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parmesan cheese
- Egg
- Breadcrumb paste, made with bread marrow cooked in milk, broth, or water with a little butter
- Flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. Remove the fat and chop the lean meat with a mezzaluna.
2. Season and proportion it with salt, pepper, parmesan, one or two eggs, and two or three spoonfuls of paste.
3. The paste may be made from bread marrow cooked in milk, broth, or simply in water graced with a little butter.
4. Mix everything together, form it into an oval loaf and flour it.
5. Then fry it in lard.
