## Ingredients

- Eels
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- tomato juice or tomato preserve
- water
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. If the eels are large, do not peel them; cut them into short pieces.
2. Finely chop a generous amount of onion and parsley, put it on the fire with a little oil, pepper, and salt.
3. When the onion has taken on color, add the eel.
4. Wait for it to absorb the flavor of the soffritto, then finish cooking it with tomato juice or tomato preserve dissolved in water.
5. Ensure there remains plenty of sauce if you want to serve it at table over barely toasted bread slices.
