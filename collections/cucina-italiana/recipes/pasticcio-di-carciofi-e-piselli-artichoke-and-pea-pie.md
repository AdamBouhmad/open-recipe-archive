## Ingredients

- Artichokes, 12
- Shelled peas, 150 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 50 grams
- Meat broth, as needed
- Flour, 1 tablespoon
- Shortcrust pastry dough (recipe below)
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes of all hard inedible leaves, divide them in half and remove the fuzzy center if present.
2. Give the artichokes and peas a brief half-cooking of a few minutes in salted water, then transfer them to fresh water, lift them out, and dry them well; divide the artichokes in half again.
3. Place both the artichokes and peas on the fire with 40 grams of the mentioned butter, season with salt and pepper, and finish cooking them with meat broth.
4. With the remaining 10 grams of butter, 1 tablespoon of flour and the aforesaid broth, make a kind of béchamel to bind the mixture.
5. Place the mixture in a fireproof dish and season it in layers with this béchamel and Parmesan cheese.
6. Cover it with the shortcrust pastry noted below; brush it with egg yolk, bake the pie in a country oven and serve it hot because it loses much if left to cool.
7. This quantity can serve seven or eight people.
