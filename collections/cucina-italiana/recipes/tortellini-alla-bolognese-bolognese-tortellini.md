## Ingredients

- Fatty and lean prosciutto, 30 grams
- Bologna mortadella, 20 grams
- Beef marrow, 60 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 60 grams
- 1 egg
- Nutmeg to taste
- Salt and pepper, none

## Directions

1. Mince finely with a lunette the prosciutto and mortadella, mince equally the marrow without breaking it over heat, add it to the other ingredients and bind everything together with the egg, mixing well.
2. Enclose them in an egg pasta sheet as the others, cutting it with the small stamp of N. 8.
3. They keep well for days and even for a few weeks and if you wish them to retain a beautiful yellow color, place them, as soon as they are made, to dry in a warm place.
4. With this amount you will make just under 300, and you will need a pasta sheet made with three eggs.
