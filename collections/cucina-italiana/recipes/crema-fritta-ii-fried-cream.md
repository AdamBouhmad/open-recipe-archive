## Ingredients

- flour, 100 grams
- sugar, 20 grams
- two whole eggs
- milk, 5 deciliters
- vanilla or lemon zest

## Directions

1. Keep it on the fire until the flour has lost its raw taste.
2. For the rest, proceed as with the previous recipe number.
3. Half the amount, mixed with other fried foods, will be enough for four or five people.
