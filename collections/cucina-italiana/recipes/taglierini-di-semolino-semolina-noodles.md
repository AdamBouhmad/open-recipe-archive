## Ingredients

- Fine semolina
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Fine semolina is required and it needs to be soaked with eggs several hours before pulling out the sheet.
2. If when you are about to pull it out it turns out too soft, add a few pinches of dry semolina to reduce the dough to the necessary hardness so it does not stick to the rolling pin.
3. No salt or other ingredients are needed.
