## Ingredients

- Potatoes, large and floury, 500 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Good milk or cream, half a glass
- Grated Parmesan cheese, two tablespoons
- Eggs, 2
- Salt, to taste
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the mashed potatoes recipe (N. 443)
2. Let cool
3. Add Parmesan and eggs
4. Butter a copper pastry pan or proportionate baking dish
5. Dust with fine breadcrumbs
6. Pour in the mixture after mixing well
7. Shape into a flat cake about one to one and a half fingers high
8. Place under a camp oven to brown
9. Serve hot from the bottom or top side, whichever is more attractive
10. Alternatively, make small tarts or use molds for elegant shapes
