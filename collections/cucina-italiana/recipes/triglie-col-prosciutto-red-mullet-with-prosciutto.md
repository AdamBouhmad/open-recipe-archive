## Ingredients

- red mullet of medium size
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- lemon juice
- thin slices of fat and lean prosciutto
- sage leaves
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. After cleaning and washing the mullet, dry them well with a cloth
2. Place them in a serving bowl and season with salt, pepper, oil and lemon juice
3. Leave them thus for several hours
4. When ready to cook, cut thin slices of fat and lean prosciutto as wide as the mullet and in quantity equal to the number of mullet
5. Spread some whole sage leaves at the bottom of a metal serving dish or pan
6. Coat the mullet well in breadcrumbs and arrange them as follows: place them upright close together and insert the prosciutto slices between one and the other, sprinkling more sage leaves on top
7. Finally pour the remaining seasoning over them and cook them between two fires
