## Ingredients

- hind quarters of well-aged hare
- lardons of salted lard
- butter
- paper
- salt
- a sprig of rosemary

## Directions

1. Remove the thickest membranes from the external muscles.
2. Stud the entire piece with lardons of lard that you have salted beforehand.
3. Thread onto the spit, wrap it in buttered paper sprinkled with salt.
4. When cooked, remove the paper and brush with a sprig of rosemary dipped in butter, make it brown, salting it a bit more.
