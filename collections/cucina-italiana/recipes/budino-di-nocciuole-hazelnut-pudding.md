## Ingredients

- Milk, 7 deciliters
- Eggs, 6
- Shelled hazelnuts, 200 grams
- Sugar, 180 grams
- Savoiardi biscuits, 150 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Blanch the hazelnuts in hot water and dry them well in the sun or by the fire.
2. Then pound them very finely in a mortar with the sugar poured in little by little.
3. Put the milk on the fire and when it comes to a boil, crumble the savoiardi biscuits into it and let them boil for five minutes, adding the butter.
4. Strain the mixture through a sieve and put it back on the fire with the pounded hazelnuts to dissolve the sugar.
5. Let it cool, then add the eggs, first the yolks, then the whites beaten stiff.
6. Pour it into a mold buttered and dusted with breadcrumbs, not filling it completely.
7. Bake it in the oven or in a cooking stove and serve it cold.
8. This quantity will serve nine or ten people.
