## Ingredients

- artichokes, 6
- milk veal lean meat, 100 grams
- prosciutto more fat than lean, 30 grams
- one central choke from the artichokes
- onion, young, one quarter
- parsley leaves
- dried mushrooms, softened, a pinch
- bread marrow, crumbled, a pinch
- parmesan cheese, grated, a pinch
- salt
- pepper
- spice flavor

## Directions

1. For six artichokes, compose the filling as described.
2. When the artichokes have taken color with oil alone, pour a little water and cover them with a cloth dampened and held firm by the lid.
3. The steam that emanates, reaching them from all sides, cooks them better.
