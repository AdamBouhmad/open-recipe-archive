## Ingredients

- White fine powdered sugar, 800 grams
- Rainwater or spring water, 1 liter
- Strong spirit, 8 deciliters
- Somewhat greenish garden lemons, 3

## Directions

1. Pour the sugar into water and stir it every day until it is dissolved.
2. At the same time grate the peel of the lemons and keep it infused in two deciliters of the said spirit for eight days; for three or four days stir it often, and in winter keep it in a place protected from the cold.
3. After eight days pass the infusion of lemons through a damp cloth, squeeze it well and mix the extract with the remaining six deciliters of spirit and let it rest for twenty-four hours.
4. The next day mix everything together, pour the liquid into a flask, which you will shake from time to time, and after fifteen days pass it through paper or several times through cotton.
5. This is to be placed at the bottom of the funnel and in the middle of it make a broom stick with several branches in the upper part pass through it in order to give the liquid the means to pass.
