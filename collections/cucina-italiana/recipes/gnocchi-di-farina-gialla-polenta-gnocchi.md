## Ingredients

- cornmeal or semolina
- salt
- water
- cheese
- butter
- tomato sauce or tomato preserve

## Directions

1. Salt the water and, when it boils, pour the flour with your left hand little by little and with a ladle in your right hand, stir continuously.
2. It is necessary that this flour boils a lot, and when it is reduced in such a way as to hold well on the ladle, throw it with a table knife in pieces into a tray and season each layer with cheese, butter, tomato sauce or preserve dissolved in water.
3. Fill the tray and send it hot to the table.
4. If you prefer them more seasoned, you can treat them like polenta with sausages or like bolognese macaroni.
