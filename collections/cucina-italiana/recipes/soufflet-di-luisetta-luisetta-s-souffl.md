## Ingredients

- Milk, half a litre
- Sugar, 80 grams
- Flour, 70 grams
- Butter, 50 grams
- Sweet almonds, 30 grams
- Eggs, 3
- Flavour of vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and pound them very fine with a spoonful of the said sugar.
2. Make a béchamel with the butter, flour and milk poured hot. Before removing it from the fire add the almonds, the sugar and the flavour. Once it is cooled, combine with the eggs, first the yolks and then the beaten whites.
3. Butter a baking pan that can withstand heat, pour the mixture into it and finish cooking it in a country-style oven.
4. This will be sufficient for five or six people.
