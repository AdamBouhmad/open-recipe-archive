## Ingredients

- Short pastry dough (recipe C from no. 589)
- Sweet almonds with three bitter ones, blanched, 180 grams
- Sugar, 150 grams
- Butter, 25 grams
- Candied orange, 25 grams
- One egg yolk
- Several tablespoons of water

## Directions

1. Prepare short pastry dough according to recipe C from no. 589.
2. Make a marzipan according to the recipe from no. 579 with the given proportions.
3. Use brioches molds or slightly smaller ones, which would be better; butter them, line them with thin short pastry about the thickness of a coin, place the marzipan inside, fold the edges of the pastry over it, wet the rim with water, cover them with the same short pastry, brush the surface with egg, bake them in an oven or country oven and afterwards sprinkle them with powdered sugar.
4. With this amount you can make from 16 to 18.
