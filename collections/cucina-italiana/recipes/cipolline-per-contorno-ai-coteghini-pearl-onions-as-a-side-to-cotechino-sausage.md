## Ingredients

- Pearl onions, 28 or 30 pieces
- Butter, 50 grams
- Broth from cotechino sausage, half a ladle
- Vinegar, half a tablespoon
- Sugar, one teaspoon
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the onions as described in the previous recipe
2. Sauté them in butter and season with salt and pepper
3. Wet them with defatted broth from the cotechino sausage
4. Add vinegar and sugar to enhance them
