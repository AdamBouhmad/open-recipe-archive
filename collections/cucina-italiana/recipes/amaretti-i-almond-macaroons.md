## Ingredients

- White powdered sugar, 250 grams
- Sweet almonds, 100 grams
- Bitter almonds, 50 grams
- Egg whites, 2

## Directions

1. Peel the almonds and dry them in the sun or by the fire, then chop them very fine with a crescent-shaped chopper.
2. Work the sugar and the whites with a spoon for at least half an hour, and add the almonds to form a stiff paste so as to make little balls the size of a small walnut; if it turns out too soft add more sugar and if too hard another bit of white, this time beaten.
3. If you would like to give the macaroons a color tending toward brown, mix a little burnt sugar into the mixture.
4. As you form the said balls, which you will flatten to the thickness of a centimeter, place them on wafers or on pieces of paper.
