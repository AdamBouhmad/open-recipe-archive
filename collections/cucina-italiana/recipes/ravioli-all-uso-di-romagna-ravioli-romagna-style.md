## Ingredients

- Ricotta, 150 grams
- Flour, 50 grams
- Grated Parmesan, 40 grams
- One egg and one yolk
- Salt, as needed

## Directions

1. Make everything into a paste and spread it on the work surface on a veil of flour to give it a cylindrical shape
2. Cut it into fourteen or fifteen equal pieces shaping them as needed
3. Boil them then for two or three minutes in unsalted water
4. Dress them with cheese and meat broth, or serve them as a side to a pot roast or a fricandeau
