## Ingredients

- Quinces with yellow skin
- Water
- White fine sugar, equal weight to the water collected

## Directions

1. Take quinces with yellow skin, which are more mature than green ones.
2. Cut them into thick slices half a finger thick, excluding the core.
3. Put them on the heat covered with water.
4. Without ever touching them with a spoon, boil them covered until well cooked.
5. Pour them into a fine sieve over a bowl to collect all the water without squeezing.
6. Weigh the water and return it to the heat with an equal amount of fine white sugar.
7. Boil uncovered in a pan, skimming the foam, until condensed, which you will know by the small pearl that the sugar begins to form, or if a few drops poured on a plate do not run too much.
