## Ingredients

- Half an egg per person
- Flour (as many teaspoons full as there are eggs)
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place as many teaspoons full of flour as there are eggs in the bottom of a pot.
2. Add grated Parmesan, nutmeg, a pinch of salt, and lastly the eggs.
3. Beat everything together very well and pour the mixture into boiling broth, passing it through a colander with large holes while stirring the broth at the same time.
4. Let it boil for a while and serve.
