## Ingredients

- Squash, 1 kilogram
- Sweet almonds, 100 grams
- Sugar, 100 grams
- Butter, 30 grams
- Breadcrumbs, 30 grams
- Milk, half a liter
- Eggs, 3
- A pinch of salt
- Cinnamon powder for flavoring

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and clean it of surface fibers, then grate it over a cloth
2. Gather the four corners of the cloth and squeeze it to remove most of the moisture it contains; the kilogram will reduce to about 300 grams
3. Boil it in the milk until cooked, which can take from 25 to 40 minutes depending on the quality of the squash
4. Meanwhile, pound the peeled almonds together with the sugar in a mortar, reducing them to a fine powder
5. When the squash is cooked, add all the ingredients except the eggs, which you will add when the mixture is cool
6. For the rest, proceed as for the Torta di Ricotta of the previous recipe
