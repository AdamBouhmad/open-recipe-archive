## Ingredients

- 60 grams shelled walnuts
- 60 grams breadcrumbs
- 30 grams powdered white sugar
- 1 heaping teaspoon fine spices
- 400 grams spaghetti
- oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pound the walnuts together with breadcrumbs, add powdered sugar and the aroma of spices.
2. Remove the spaghetti dry from the water and season first with oil and pepper, then with this paste in good measure.
