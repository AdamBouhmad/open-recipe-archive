## Ingredients

- Pike weighing approximately 600-700 grams
- Lard strips seasoned with salt and pepper
- Onion about the size of a large walnut
- Small garlic clove
- Celery rib
- Small piece of carrot
- Pinch of parsley
- Oil
- Tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water
- Salt and pepper
- Butter
- Flour
- Marsala or wine

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, clean it, remove the head and tail, and divide it into four or five pieces
2. Stud each piece lengthwise with two lard strips seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Make a proportionate battute with onion the size of a large walnut, a small garlic clove, a celery rib, a small piece of carrot, and a pinch of parsley, all finely chopped
4. Put the battute in a pan with oil and when it takes color, stop it with tomato juice or tomato paste dissolved in water, salt and pepper to season
5. Thicken this sauce somewhat with a small piece of butter mixed with flour, stir well and place the fish in it, letting it boil slowly and turning it
6. Finally add a spoonful of Marsala or, if lacking, a splash of wine, and let it boil a little more before serving in the middle of its sauce
