## Ingredients

- 150 grams lean veal
- 25 grams fatty prosciutto
- 25 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons pap made with bread marrow, water and a small piece of butter
- 1 egg
- nutmeg to taste
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. First mince the meat and prosciutto with a knife by chopping, then with a crescent-shaped blade, then pound them in a mortar and pass through a sieve
2. Make a paste with the egg and the other ingredients
3. When the broth boils, drop it in by teaspoons or pass it through a syringe to give it a graceful shape, and after sufficient boiling to cook it, serve the soup
4. This quantity is enough for four or five people, but you can also make it serve twelve by mixing it in a soup. Take the finest bread from the day before, cut it into small dice and quickly brown it in a pan with plenty of fat. When you are about to serve, place the said bread in the tureen and pour the above-described strained meat soup over it
