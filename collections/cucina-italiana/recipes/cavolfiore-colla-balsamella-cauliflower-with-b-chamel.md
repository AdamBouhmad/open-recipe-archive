## Ingredients

- Large head of cauliflower
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parmesan
- Béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves and green ribs from a large head of cauliflower, make a deep cross-cut in the stem and cook it in salted water.
2. Cut it into small florets and season to taste with butter, salt and pepper.
3. Place it in a fireproof dish, sprinkle a small pinch of Parmesan over it, cover it entirely with béchamel sauce and brown the surface.
4. Serve this cauliflower hot as a side dish or better accompanied by a meat stew or boiled chicken.
