## Ingredients

- Healthy large chestnuts about thirty, 500 grams
- Powdered sugar, 130 grams
- Chocolate, 60 grams
- Citron rosolio, 3 tablespoons

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts in water as you would for roasted chestnuts, blanch them and pass them while hot
2. Reduce the chocolate to powder and then with all the ingredients form a paste
3. Take a large, round and presentable plate, place a small coffee cup inverted in the center and with a horsehair sieve on top pass all the mixture while rotating the plate so that it is distributed equally
4. Once the operation is complete, carefully remove and clean the coffee cup and the empty space left in the middle by filling it with 300 grams of whipped cream
