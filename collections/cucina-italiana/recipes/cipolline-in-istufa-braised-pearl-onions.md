## Ingredients

- Pearl onions
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Meat stock
- Flour
- Lard
- Mirepoix bundle
- Prosciutto slice
- Broth

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and trim the top and bottom evenly
2. Boil them in salted water for ten minutes
3. Brown a piece of butter until it turns hazelnut color
4. Add the onions, season with salt and pepper, and brown on one side
5. Turn them over and brown on the other side
6. Wet them with meat stock and bind with flour mixed with butter
7. Alternatively, if meat stock is unavailable, cook them in white: after boiling and cooling in fresh water, place them in a saucepan with a mirepoix bundle, a small slice of prosciutto, a piece of butter, and a ladle of broth
8. Season with pepper and little salt
9. Cover with very thin slices of lard and place a sheet of butter-greased paper on top
10. Finish cooking between two fires and serve as a side dish with the reduced sauce
