## Ingredients

- The breast of a young chicken, without legs, neck, and innards
- Butter
- Salt
- Pasta (from recipe No. 277)
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Take the breast of a young chicken, without legs, neck, and innards, grease it all with cold butter, dust it with salt, and add a pinch of salt inside.
2. With the wings folded, wrap it with two wide and thin slices of lean prosciutto and cover it with pastry described in recipe No. 277, rolled out with a rolling pin to approximately the thickness of a coin.
3. Brush it with egg yolk and cook the dressed chicken at moderate heat in an oven or camp oven.
4. Serve it as it is to be opened and carved at table.
