## Ingredients

- sugar, 120 grams
- candied fruit in small pieces, 30 grams
- fine breadcrumbs, 120 grams
- sultana raisins, 30 grams
- eggs, 4
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Work first the egg yolks with the sugar until they have become almost white; add the breadcrumbs, then the candied fruit and raisins, and finally the well-beaten egg whites. Mix gently so as not to deflate them and when the mixture is all combined, pour it into a buttered and floured pan or dusted with breadcrumbs, where it rises about two fingers high and bake it in the oven; this cake will take on the appearance of sponge cake which you will dust, after baking, with powdered sugar.
2. If it is to serve ten or twelve people, double the amount.
