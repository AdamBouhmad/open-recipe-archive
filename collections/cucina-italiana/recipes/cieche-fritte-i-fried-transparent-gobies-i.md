## Ingredients

- Transparent gobies
- oil
- garlic, whole
- sage
- eggs
- salt
- Parmesan cheese
- breadcrumbs
- lemon, cut into wedges

## Directions

1. Cook the gobies in oil with whole garlic and sage as described in the Cieche alla Pisana recipe.
2. Remove the garlic and finely chop the gobies.
3. Beat eggs in proportion, salt them, add Parmesan and a little breadcrumbs.
4. Mix the gobies into the egg mixture.
5. Fry by spoonfuls to make small fritters.
6. Serve with lemon wedges.
7. Few will notice it is a fish dish when eaten this way.
