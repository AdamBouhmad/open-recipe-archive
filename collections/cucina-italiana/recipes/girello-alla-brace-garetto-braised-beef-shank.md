## Ingredients

- 700 grams beef girello (rump) without bone
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Place the meat in a pot with only salt and pepper seasoning, no water or other ingredients
2. Close the lid of the pot with several layers of paper held firm by the lid
3. Let it cook very slowly
4. The meat will release a copious amount of sauce which it will gradually reabsorb
5. When it has reabsorbed all the sauce, remove it from heat and serve
6. It is almost better served cold than hot
