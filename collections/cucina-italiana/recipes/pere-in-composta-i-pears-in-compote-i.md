## Ingredients

- Pears, 600 grams
- Fine powdered sugar, 120 grams
- Water, two glasses
- Half a lemon

## Directions

1. If they are small pears, leave them whole with their stems; if they are large, cut them into wedges: as you peel them, drop them into the aforementioned water in which you have squeezed the half lemon
2. This serves to preserve the whiteness of the fruit
3. Boil them in the same water passed through a strainer, pour in the sugar when it begins to boil and for the rest follow the procedure as for the apricots
4. Serve them cold
