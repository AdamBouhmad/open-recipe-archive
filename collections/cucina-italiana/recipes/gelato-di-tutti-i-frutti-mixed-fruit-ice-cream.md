## Ingredients

- Sugar, 200 grams
- Well-ripened apricots, weighed with the pit, 100 grams
- Raspberry, 100 grams
- Currants, 100 grams
- Candied citron, 20 grams
- Water, half a liter

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in the water for ten minutes, then add the pulp of the said fruits passed through a sieve, then the candied citron cut into small pieces.
2. Instead of apricots you can use burrone peaches, and strawberries can be substituted for currants.
