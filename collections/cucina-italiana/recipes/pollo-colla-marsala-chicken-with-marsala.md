## Ingredients

- One chicken, cut into large pieces
- Onion, finely chopped, minced
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- Marsala

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into large pieces and put it in a pot with minced onion and a piece of butter.
2. Season it with salt and pepper.
3. When it is well browned, add some broth and cook it through.
4. Strain the sauce, degrease it if necessary and put the chicken back on the fire with some Marsala.
5. Remove it as soon as it returns to a boil.
