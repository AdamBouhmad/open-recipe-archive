## Ingredients

- Ricotta, 200 grams
- Flour, 40 grams
- Eggs, 2
- Sugar, 2 scant teaspoons
- Lemon zest flavoring
- Salt, a pinch
- Brandy, 2 tablespoonfuls

## Directions

1. Use any quality of ricotta provided it has not become strong; ricotta from Rome and Maremma are excellent
2. Let the mixture rest for several hours before frying
3. With the stated amounts the mixture will be somewhat firm, which is good so the fried pastry takes the shape of bombs approximately the size of a walnut
4. To give these bombs a round shape as possible, take up the mixture with a spoon moistened with the hot liquid from the pan, shaping it with the tip of a table knife, also moistened in the same hot liquid
5. Dust them with powdered sugar and serve hot as a side dish with a meat fry
6. Note: there is little sugar in the mixture because it burns and the fried pastry would not then take on a nice golden color
