## Ingredients

- liver, approximately 300 grams
- three large onions
- lard
- good red wine, less than half a glass
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice three large onions and soak them in fresh water for one or two hours.
2. Drain the water and put the onion in a pan to dry it; once dry, add the lard to fry it.
3. When it has taken on a brown color, add the liver cut into thin slices.
4. Let it soften somewhat, mixed with the onion; then pour less than half a glass of good red wine into the pan.
5. After five minutes, stirring constantly, serve it seasoned with salt and pepper.
6. Note that this is not a dish for delicate stomachs.
