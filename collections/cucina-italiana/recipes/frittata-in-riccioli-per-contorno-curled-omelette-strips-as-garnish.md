## Ingredients

- a bunch of spinach
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil a bunch of spinach and pass it through a sieve
2. Beat two eggs, season with salt and pepper, and mix in the spinach in such quantity as to make them only green
3. Put a pan on the fire with just a drop of oil to grease it, and when very hot pour in a portion of the egg mixture, turning the pan in every direction so the omelette comes out thin as paper
4. When well set and dry, remove it, flipping if necessary, and repeat or triple the operation with the remaining eggs
5. Roll these two or three omelettes together, cut them into fine strips like noodles, and lightly fry them in butter
6. Season with Parmesan and use these strips as a garnish for fricandeau or similar dishes
