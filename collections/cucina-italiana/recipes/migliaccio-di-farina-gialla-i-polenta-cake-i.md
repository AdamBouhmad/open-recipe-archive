## Ingredients

- cornmeal flour
- salt
- boiling water
- dried grapes or raisins
- lard
- rosemary sprigs

## Directions

1. Put whatever amount of cornmeal flour you wish to use in a container
2. Salt it well and mix it firmly with boiling water
3. When mixed so no dry flour remains at the bottom of the vessel, add dried grapes or raisins in proper amount
4. Take a copper pan and place it on the fire with abundant virgin lard
5. When the lard begins to crackle, pour in the mixture, which because it is thick must be spread and leveled with a spoon
6. Spread the surface with a little more lard and sprinkle with sprigs of fresh rosemary
7. Bake in an oven or between two fires until it browns somewhat
8. Turn it out
