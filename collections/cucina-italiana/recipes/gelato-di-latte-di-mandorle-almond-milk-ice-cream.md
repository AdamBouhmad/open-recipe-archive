## Ingredients

- Zucchero (sugar), 200 grams
- Mandorle dolci con 4 o 5 amare (sweet almonds with 4 or 5 bitter), 150 grams
- Acqua (water), 8 deciliters
- Panna (cream), 2 deciliters
- Odore di acqua di fior d'arancio o di coriandoli (flavoring of orange blossom water or coriander)

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in the water for ten minutes with coriander inside, as in the Portuguese Milk recipe, if you use it for the flavoring
2. Shell the almonds, pound them very fine in the mortar diluting them with a few spoonfuls of the syrup obtained and mix them to the same
3. Then pass them through a fine cloth squeezing well to extract from the almonds all the substance possible, repeating the mortar operation more times if needed
4. Unite the cream to the squeezed liquid, freeze it in the ice cream maker and when it is well firm serve it in small glasses
5. This dose should be enough for nine or ten persons
