## Ingredients

- green beans
- oil
- 1 shallot
- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- onion (if substituting shallot)
- broth
- tomato sauce
- 2 teaspoons vanilla sugar
- nepitella (if substituting vanilla)

## Directions

1. Soak the green beans in fresh water
2. If they are tender, put them whole and raw into a stew without draining them too much
3. Make a soffritto with oil, shallot, parsley, carrot and celery, all finely chopped
4. Season with salt and pepper, and when it takes color, dilute with broth or pass it through a strainer, squeezing well
5. To this strained sauce, add tomato sauce
6. Throw in the green beans to cook them
7. Before removing them from heat, season with two teaspoons of vanilla sugar
8. If this flavor is not pleasing, substitute with nepitella
