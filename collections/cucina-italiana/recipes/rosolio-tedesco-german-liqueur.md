## Ingredients

- Fine alcohol, 500 grams
- White powdered sugar, 500 grams
- Milk, half a liter
- One garden lemon
- Half a vanilla pod

## Directions

1. Mince the whole lemon, removing the seeds and including the zest that you have previously grated
2. Divide the vanilla into small pieces
3. Mix everything else together in a glass vessel and you will see that the milk immediately curdles
4. Shake the vessel once a day and after eight days strain it through cloth and filter it through paper
