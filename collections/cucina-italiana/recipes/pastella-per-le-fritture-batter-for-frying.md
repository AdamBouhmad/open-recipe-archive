## Ingredients

- Flour, 100 grams
- Fine oil, one spoonful
- Aquavite, one spoonful
- Eggs, 1
- Salt to taste
- Iced water as needed

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the egg yolk and the other ingredients, pouring in the water little by little to make a batter that is not too liquid. Work it well with a spoon to blend it thoroughly, and let it rest for several hours. When you are about to use it, add the beaten egg white. This batter can serve for many fried dishes and especially for those of fruit and vegetables.
