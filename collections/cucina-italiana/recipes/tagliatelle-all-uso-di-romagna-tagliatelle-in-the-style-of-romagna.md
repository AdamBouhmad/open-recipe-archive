## Ingredients

- Eggs and flour for pasta dough
- Ingredients for spaghetti alla rustica sauce
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make the sheet and cut it as tagliatelle
2. Cook them briefly, drain them well from the water and put them in a saucepan over the fire for a moment to absorb the seasoning
3. Season with spaghetti alla rustica sauce plus a piece of butter proportionate to the quantity of pasta
4. Mix gently and serve
