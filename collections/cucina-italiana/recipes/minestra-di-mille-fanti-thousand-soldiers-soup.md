## Ingredients

- half an egg per person
- flour equal in tablespoons to the number of eggs
- grated Parmesan cheese
- nutmeg to taste
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Take a pot and at the bottom of it place as many heaping tablespoons of flour as there are eggs; add grated Parmesan, nutmeg aroma, a pinch of salt and finally the eggs
2. Beat everything together very well and pour the mixture into the broth when it boils, passing it through a tin strainer with large holes, stirring the broth at the same time
3. Let it boil somewhat and serve
