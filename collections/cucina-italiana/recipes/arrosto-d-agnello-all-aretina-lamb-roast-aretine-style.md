## Ingredients

- a leg or quarter of lamb
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- a drop of vinegar
- a sprig of rosemary

## Directions

1. Season the lamb with salt, pepper, oil and a drop of vinegar.
2. Pierce it here and there with the point of a knife and let it sit in this mixture for several hours.
3. Thread it onto the spit and frequently baste it with this liquid using a sprig of rosemary until cooked, which serves to remove any stable smell from the lamb, if you fear it has one, and to give it a pleasant taste.
4. If you prefer a more pronounced rosemary aroma, you can stud the piece with some sprigs of the same, removing them before serving at table.
