## Ingredients

- Hungarian flour, 100 grams
- Butter, 20 grams
- Beer yeast, the size of a walnut
- Eggs, 1
- Salt, a pinch

## Directions

1. Roll out the dough to a sheet slightly less than half a finger thick, cut it with a tin tube of the marked diameter to make like so many pastilles which you will set to rise.
2. You will see them grow in the form of little balls and then fry them in oil, if you have excellent oil, otherwise in lard or butter.
3. When you are about to send to table place them in the soup tureen and pour boiling broth over them.
