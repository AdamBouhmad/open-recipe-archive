## Ingredients

- pigeons
- whole sage leaves
- slices of fat and lean prosciutto
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- broth
- lemon
- toasted bread slices
- verjuice (optional alternative to lemon)

## Directions

1. Garnish the pigeons with whole sage leaves, place them in a pan or pot over slices of fat and lean prosciutto and season them with oil, salt and pepper.
2. When they have taken color, add a piece of butter and bring them to cook with broth.
3. Before removing them from the fire squeeze a lemon over them and use their juice to serve them with slices of toasted bread placed underneath.
4. Note to salt them very little because of the prosciutto and the broth.
5. At the time of verjuice, you can use the latter instead of lemon.
