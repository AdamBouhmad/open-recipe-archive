## Ingredients

- 300 grams green beans
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon flour
- juice of 1/4 lemon
- 1 ladle of degreased cold broth

## Directions

1. Remove both ends and the string from the green beans
2. Blanch them in salted water until half-cooked
3. Drain and cut into three pieces
4. Sauté with butter, salt and pepper to taste
5. Whisk together the egg yolk, flour, and lemon juice in a pot
6. Dilute the mixture with one ladle of degreased cold broth
7. Place over heat, stirring constantly with a spoon, until it becomes a flowing cream
8. Pour the sauce over the green beans
9. Keep them on the heat a little longer so the sauce incorporates well
10. Serve as a side dish to boiled meat
