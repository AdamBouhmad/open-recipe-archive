## Ingredients

- Potatoes, 500 grams
- Butter, 40 grams
- Grated Parmesan cheese, 40 grams
- Egg yolks, 3
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in water or, better, steamed, peel them, pass them hot through a sieve and salt them.
2. Add the aforementioned ingredients and work them somewhat.
3. Spread a veil of flour on the work surface and pour the mixture onto it so you can draw it into sticks without the flour penetrating inside, and with these form balls about the size of hazelnuts.
4. Fry them in oil or lard where they can swim around and place them in a tureen pouring boiling broth over them.
5. This dose will serve eight or ten persons.
