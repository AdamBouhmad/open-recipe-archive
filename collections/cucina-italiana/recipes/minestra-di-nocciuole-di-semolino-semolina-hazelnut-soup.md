## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters milk
- 100 grams semolina
- 20 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 whole egg and 1 yolk
- butter the size of a walnut
- salt to taste
- flour to taste
- nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Put the milk on the fire with butter and when it boils pour in the semolina little by little
2. Salt it; when it is cooked and still hot, but not boiling, beat the eggs into it, add the Parmesan and the aroma and stir
3. Let it cool well and then pour it onto a board over a layer of flour
4. Roll it lightly on the same flour pulling out a small stick which you will cut into equal pieces to make as many balls the size of a hazelnut
5. Drop them into the broth when it boils and shortly after, pour them into the tureen and send them to the table
6. This amount can be sufficient for five or six people
