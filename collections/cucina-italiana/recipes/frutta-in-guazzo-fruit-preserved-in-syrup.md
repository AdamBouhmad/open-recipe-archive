## Ingredients

- Strawberries, currants, and raspberries, 50 to 100 grams each sort
- Sugar equal to half the weight of the fruit
- Brandy or cognac enough to submerge the fruit
- Cherries
- Plums
- Apricots
- Peaches
- Gooseberries
- Grapes
- Pears

## Directions

1. Begin with the first fruits that appear in spring: strawberries, currants, and raspberries, placing 50 to 100 grams of each sort in a vase
2. Cover them with sugar equal to half their weight and enough brandy or cognac to submerge them
3. Continue with cherries, plums, apricots, and peaches, all pitted and, except for the cherries, sliced into thin strips, always adding sugar and brandy in proportion
4. You may also add gooseberries, grapes, and some pears, but then taste the liquid and add sugar or brandy according to your taste
5. Once the vase is formed, leave it to rest for several months before serving
