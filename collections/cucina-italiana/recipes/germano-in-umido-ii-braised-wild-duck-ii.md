## Ingredients

- one wild duck
- 30 grams of butter
- one spoonful of flour
- half a litre of water
- salt
- pepper
- one quarter of an orange peel, in one piece
- one celery rib, one palm long
- one piece of carrot, coarsely sliced

## Directions

1. Put the duck in the casserole with 30 grams of butter and let it take on color.
2. Remove it and throw into the remaining fat one spoonful of flour to have it take on, stirring with a wooden spoon, a brown color.
3. Removed from the fire and no longer boiling, pour onto that mixture half a litre of water and put the duck back in it; season it with salt and pepper and let it boil covered until completely cooked with one quarter of an orange peel in one piece, one celery rib one palm long and a piece of carrot, sliced coarsely.
