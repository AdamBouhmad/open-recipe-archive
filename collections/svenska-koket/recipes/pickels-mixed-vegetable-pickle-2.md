## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter small Västerås cucumbers
- 4 small tomatoes
- 1/2 liter sugar peas
- 1/2 liter small green beans
- 1 cauliflower head
- 1/2 liter small carrots
- 2 deciliters mustard seeds
- 2 deciliters apple seeds
- 4 grams whole white pepper
- 3 deciliters small pickling onions
- 2 grams hot pepper
- 10 grams ginger in small pieces
- 2 grams mace
- 4 grams cloves
- 2 pieces Spanish pepper
- dill
- 3 liters good vinegar
- 3 hectograms sugar

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the cucumbers and tomatoes with 1 tablespoon salt and let stand one day.
2. Clean and rinse the vegetables, cutting peas, beans, cauliflower and carrots into neat pieces.
3. Blanch all vegetables except cucumbers and tomatoes separately in lightly salted water (1 liter water, 1 tablespoon salt), rinse with cold water and drain on cloth.
4. Dry cucumbers and tomatoes on cloth.
5. Layer the dried cucumbers and tomatoes with the blanched vegetables and spices into 1/2 liter glass jars.
6. Heat the vinegar with the sugar and fill the jars once cooled.
7. Bind the jars well and store in a cool place.
