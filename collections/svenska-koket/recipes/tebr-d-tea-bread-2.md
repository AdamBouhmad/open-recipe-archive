## Ingredients

- 100 gr. butter
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- 50 gr. bitter almond
- 10 vanilla drops
- 1 pinch ammonium carbonate
- 275 gr. wheat flour
- 2 egg whites for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 1/2 kkp pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour with ammonium carbonate on a baking board.
2. Divide the butter into flakes and place on top of the flour along with sugar, the beaten egg, the blanched and grated bitter almond, and vanilla drops.
3. Work all ingredients together into a smooth, cohesive dough.
4. Roll out quite thin and cut small cakes with a measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with almond and sugar.
6. Bake to a nice golden-brown in moderate oven heat.
