## Ingredients

- 5 gr. dried morels
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 lit.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1/2 dcl. mushroom liquid
- 3 dcl. cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. sugar
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Let the morels soak in cold water for 2 hours and blanch for 15 minutes, changing the water once.
2. Strain them in a sieve and let drain and pat dry in a cloth together with the mushrooms.
3. Slice the mushrooms and fry in butter until it becomes clear. Squeeze it out of the butter with a spoon.
4. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the mushroom liquid and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add the mushroom and let the stewing come to a boil and season. Prepare the omelet as in recipe no. 27, fill it with the stewing, turn it out, garnish with parsley and serve for the smorgasbord.
