## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. rice grains (100 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 1/4 lit. milk
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in cold water
2. Scald them by letting them lie in hot water for 10 minutes
3. Drain in a colander and rinse again
4. Melt the butter in a pot, add the milk and bring to a boil
5. Add the grains and let them cook slowly with a tight lid for about 2 hours, stirring often and thinning with more milk as needed
6. Season with salt and serve with sugar and cinnamon
