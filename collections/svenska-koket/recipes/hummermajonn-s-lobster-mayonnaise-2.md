## Ingredients

- 2 lobsters (400 g each)
- 4 liters water
- 1 1/2 hectograms coarse salt (or 1 can lobster [1 liter])
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt, cayenne
- 1/2 teaspoon French mustard
- 1 1/2 deciliters fine oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- 3 heads salad
- 10 slices fresh cucumber
- 2 tomatoes in slices
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon paprika
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster as in recipe 170 and let it cool.
2. Split it lengthwise and crush the claws.
3. Loosen the meat carefully and cut into pieces, arrange high on a round plate, setting aside some fine pieces for garnish.
4. Prepare the mayonnaise as in recipe 10, but omit the gelatin.
5. Pour it over the lobster and garnish with the reserved lobster pieces.
6. Clean the salad, rinse quickly and place on linen so the water drains well.
7. Cut it crosswise and place in a bowl together with the cucumber and tomato slices, sprinkle salt and pepper over and mix the salad well.
8. Add the oil and vinegar gradually while continuing to mix.
9. Place the salad around the lobster mayonnaise and garnish with the hard-boiled eggs cut into slices.
10. Serve as a course between meals.
