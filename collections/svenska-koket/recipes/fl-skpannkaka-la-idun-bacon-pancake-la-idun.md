## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/2 dcl. water
- approximately 80 gr. flour
- 6 dcl. thick cream
- 250 gr. lightly salted side bacon

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with a steel whisk; whisk in the water and flour, and let the batter stand for 1 hour to rise.
