## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper
- 10 fresh asparagus tips for filling
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams)
- 1/2 tablespoon flour (5 grams)
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the asparagus in salted water and let it drain.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Season with spices and carefully blend in the asparagus.
4. Prepare the omelet as in recipe no. 24 and just before the eggs are fully set, add the asparagus stewing.
5. Form the omelet with a fork, tip it onto a warm dish and garnish, if desired, with a few blanched asparagus tips, which have been browned in butter.
6. The blanched asparagus can also be mixed with a few tablespoons of hollandaise and filled into the omelet after it has been tipped onto the dish.
