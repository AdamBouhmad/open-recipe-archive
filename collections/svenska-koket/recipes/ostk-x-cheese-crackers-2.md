## Ingredients

- Shortcrust dough: 2 hg. butter, 1 egg, 30 gr. sugar, 245 gr. flour
- Cheese mixture: 1 hg. butter, 75 gr. fine grated cheese

## Directions

1. The butter is washed (the water is worked out well) and stirred until it becomes white and fluffy.
2. The egg yolk and sugar are stirred for 10 minutes, then the butter and flour are added and the dough is worked until it becomes smooth.
3. It is allowed to stand in a cold room for 2 hours, then rolled out to an oblong cake of 2 mm thickness, which is pricked closely.
4. It is cut out into slanted squares, which are placed on a well-cleaned baking sheet and baked a nice golden brown in ordinary oven heat.
5. For the cheese mixture, the butter is stirred with the cheese until it becomes white and fluffy.
6. The cheese mixture is spread on half of the crackers and the remaining crackers are placed on top with the pricked side up.
7. Served with consommé or broth.
