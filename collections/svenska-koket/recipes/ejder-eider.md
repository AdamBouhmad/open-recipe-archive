## Ingredients

- 1 eider duck for 4-5 persons
- milk

## Directions

1. Clean the bird (see page 299) and remove the oily skin.
2. Parboil the bird and let it lie in milk for 1 day before serving.
3. Treat it thereafter, roast and serve as duck.
