## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1/2 tsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp chopped capers

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in half of the butter and place on a warm platter. Brown the remaining butter lightly, season with vinegar and pour over the eggs. Mix the parsley and capers and sprinkle over. Serve at the smörgåsbord.
