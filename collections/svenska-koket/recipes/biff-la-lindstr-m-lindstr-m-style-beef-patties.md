## Ingredients

- 600g boneless beef
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped Portuguese onion
- 2.5 cups cooked potatoes, cut into cubes
- 1 cup cooked beets, cut into cubes
- 3 tablespoons beet vinegar
- 100g butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it into cubes and pass it 3 times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the egg yolk, milk, spices and the browned onion into the forcemeat and work it very well.
3. Carefully mix in the potatoes, beets and vinegar.
4. Form round patties from the forcemeat and fry them a beautiful brown in the butter.
