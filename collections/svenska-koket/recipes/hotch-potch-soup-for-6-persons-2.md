## Ingredients

- 1 kg lamb breast
- 2 liters secondary broth
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 1 leek
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 celery
- 1/4 liter canned peas (or 2 cups fresh peas)
- 12 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small head of cauliflower
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Dry the lamb breast with a cloth, wring it out in hot water, break it well and set it to cook in the broth.
2. When the meat is half-cooked, remove it and cut into small, even pieces.
3. Strain the broth, skim carefully and pour back into the pot.
4. Brown the meat in 2 tbsp of the butter together with the leek, cut into fine strips, sprinkle flour over and stir the meat well.
5. Place it in the broth, which is allowed to simmer for about 1 hour.
6. Sauté the roots, cut into fine strips, brown in the remaining butter and add together with the peas to the soup 1/2 hour before it is ready.
7. Clean the Jerusalem artichokes and cauliflower in the usual way, place in 1/2 liter boiling water, seasoned with 1/2 tsp salt, and allow to cook until tender.
8. Place them in the soup bowl and reduce the cooking liquid until it becomes about 3 dcl, then pour it over the vegetables.
9. Skim the soup, season with salt and pepper, pour into the soup bowl and serve very hot.
