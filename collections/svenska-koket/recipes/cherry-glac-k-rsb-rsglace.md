## Ingredients

- 1 litre pitted cherries
- 6 dcl sugar syrup
- 1/4 dcl kirsch
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Crush the pits and pound the kernels with the pitted cherries.
2. Let them steep in the sugar syrup mixed with kirsch for approximately 1 hour.
3. Pass the mixture through a sieve.
4. Add lemon juice.
