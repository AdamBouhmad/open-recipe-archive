## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter potatoes
- 2-3 tbsp. salad oil
- 1-2 tbsp. vinegar
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1 dcl. broth
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1-2 tbsp. chopped Portuguese onion

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the potatoes as in the previous recipe, let them cool, peel and cut into fairly thick strips.
2. In a salad bowl mix the salad oil, vinegar, spices and broth.
3. Carefully stir the potato strips into the sauce together with the parsley and onion.
4. Serve the salad with roasted cold meat and with sausage.
