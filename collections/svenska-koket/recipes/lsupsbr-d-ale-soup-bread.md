## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 1 kg wheat flour
- 1 liter drink
- 2 kg rye sifted flour
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)
- 3 dcl syrup
- 2 tbsp crushed fennel
- 1 tbsp crushed anise
- 3 tbsp finely chopped orange peel

## Directions

1. In the milk whisk as much of the wheat flour as needed to make a thick paste.
2. Boil the drink, add the paste and let the mixture come to a good boil while stirring.
3. Pour it into a baking pan and stir in enough rye sifted flour to make a loose dough.
4. When this has cooled to about 40°, add the yeast mixed with salt and enough flour to make a quite firm dough, and let it rise to double its size.
5. Boil the syrup with the spices, allow it to cool and work it into the dough along with wheat flour so that the dough becomes firm.
6. Let it rise again, then treat, shape and bake as in recipe no. 939.
