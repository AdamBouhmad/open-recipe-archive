## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 hg. salt salmon
- 1 liter skim milk
- 2 dcl. rice grains (2 hg.)
- 1 1/2 liters whole milk
- 3 tablespoons cold butter (60 gr.)
- 2-3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Prepare the salmon as in the previous recipe and cut it into cubes.
2. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, add them to the boiling milk and let them cook with stirring until they are almost soft; add a little more milk if needed.
