## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg salted lamb (breast or shoulder)
- water

## Directions

1. If the meat is too salty to boil directly, soak it the day before.
2. Break into suitable pieces which should still hang together, and place in cold water (short broth).
3. When the broth boils, skim well.
4. If it tastes too salty, pour it off and add new cold water.
5. Let the meat boil until tender or about 2 hours.
6. Serve with fried roots, root purée or potato purée.
