## Ingredients

- 2 celery roots
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 liters stock
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- salt
- cayenne
- 2–3 tablespoons sherry
- 2–3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and cut the celery roots into small pieces.
2. Set aside half a celery root and cut it into cubes; cook these in salted water and reserve to serve in the soup.
3. Prepare and serve the purée as in recipe no. 266: Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the purée and remaining stock, let the soup cook for 5 minutes, season with the spices, whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl, fold in the purée while whisking, and serve with toasted bread or cheese croutons.
