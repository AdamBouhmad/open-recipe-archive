## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 part white French wine
- 2 teaspoons potato flour
- 2 water
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The lemon is zested so that only the yellow peel comes off, and the juice is pressed out.
2. All ingredients are whisked well together in a pan and are simmered over the heat with vigorous whisking until the mixture thickens.
3. The pan is removed from the heat, and whisking is continued until the sauce has cooled.
