## Ingredients

- 8 dcl. fresh or 6 dcl. preserved gooseberries
- 8 dcl. water
- 2 1/2–3 dcl. crushed sugar
- 1 1/2 tbsp. mondamin flour (23 gr.) or 1 1/2 tbsp. potato starch (23 gr.)

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse them and let them drain.
2. Cook a thin, clear syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Add the gooseberries and let them cook until they begin to burst.
4. Mix the flour with 2 tbsp. cold water or French wine and use to thicken the cream.
5. Let the cream cook for 5 minutes if thickened with mondamin, but only 2 minutes if thickened with potato starch.
6. Taste the cream and add more sugar if needed.
7. Stir occasionally until cold, then pour into a compote dish.
8. Serve with cream or milk.
