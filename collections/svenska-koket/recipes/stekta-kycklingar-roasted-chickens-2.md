## Ingredients

- 3 chickens at ½ kg each
- 2-3 teaspoons salt
- 1 bunch parsley
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½-1 liter water or broth
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- 1½ parts thick cream
- meat dripping

## Directions

1. Pluck the chickens, singe carefully, clean out, rinse well and dry inside and outside with a towel. Rinse a few parsley sprigs, brush with 1 tablespoon of the butter and place inside the chickens, then sew them shut and truss.
2. Brown the remaining butter in a roasting pan and place the chickens in it with their backs down. When nicely browned on all sides, salt them, then add the boiling broth or water and let them braise slowly, covered.
3. Cut the chickens and serve on a warm platter, garnished with curly lettuce.
4. Strain and skim the meat dripping, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules.
