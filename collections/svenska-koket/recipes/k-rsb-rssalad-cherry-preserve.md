## Ingredients

- 2 liters cherries
- 0.75 liters wine vinegar
- 300 grams granulated sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon, 1 decimeter long
- Peel of 0.5 lemon
- 8 cloves

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries and cut the stems off halfway.
2. Place the cherries in glass bottles with wide mouths.
3. Cook the vinegar with the sugar and spices and pour it to cool.
4. When the brine is cold, pour it over the berries and cork and seal the bottles with resin.
5. Should the berries begin to ferment after some time, pour off the brine and cook it up.
6. When it has cooled, pour it over the berries and seal the bottles well again.
