## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg spareribs
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 apples
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 hectogram prunes
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- meat gravy for sauce
- 1 tablespoon flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Break the spareribs, wash with a cloth dipped in hot water and rub in the pepper and salt.
2. Rinse the apples and dry them well, remove the cores and cut into slices, dip in the sugar.
3. Spread out the spareribs with the bony side up and cover with the apple slices and rinsed prunes.
4. Roll it up and tie it in three places with cooking twine, first dipped in boiling water.
5. Brown the meat in a roasting pot, add boiling water and let it cook with a tightly fitting lid until the meat is tender, which takes about 2 hours.
6. Take it up, remove the cooking twine, and cut the meat into nice pieces, which are placed high on a warm dish and garnished with the apple slices and prunes.
7. Prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
