## Ingredients

- 1 large cabbage head
- water, salt
- 300 g boneless beef
- 200 g boneless veal or pork
- 50 g lard
- 2 tsp. salt (10 g)
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dcl. crushed rusks
- 4-5 dcl. cream or milk
- 100 g beef fat
- 100 g lard, in one large slice
- 1 liter broth

## Directions

1. Place the cabbage head in boiling salted water and let it cook until soft.
2. Remove it immediately and let it drain on a cloth.
3. Remove the outer green leaves and hollow out the head from the top.
4. Prepare the ground meat as in recipe no. 637 and it should be quite loose.
5. Fill the cabbage head with the ground meat (but not too full, as the meat will rise during cooking), cover with a few large cabbage leaves, and tie with fine twine.
6. Brown it well in hot beef fat.
7. Place it in a pot on the lard slice, together with the cabbage that was cut out from the head, add the boiling broth and let it cook slowly with the lid tightly closed for about 2 hours, or until the meat is done, which is tested with a whisk.
8. Place the cabbage head on a round platter, garnish with the carved-out cabbage and with small meatballs made from the meat that did not fit in the head.
9. Serve with strained sauce (see recipe no. 429), prepared from the cooking liquid.
