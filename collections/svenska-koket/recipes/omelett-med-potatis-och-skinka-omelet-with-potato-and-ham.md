## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 10 tablespoons thick cream
- 0.5 liter cooked potato
- 200 g smoked ham
- 0.25 Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and cream together.
2. Cut the potato into slices and the ham into oblong rectangular pieces.
3. Peel, chop, and fry the onion in butter.
4. Butter an omelet pan, layer the potato, ham, and onion in it, and pour the egg mixture over.
5. Shake the pan over the heat until the omelet has set, then turn it out first onto a lid and then onto the serving plate so the unbaked side comes up.
6. Sprinkle the omelet with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
