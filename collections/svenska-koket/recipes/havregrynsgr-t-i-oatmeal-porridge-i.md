## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters ordinary oat groats (225 grams)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams)
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Place the groats in a sieve, rinse with cold water and let drain.
2. Place the butter in a pot, pour in the water, add the rinsed groats and let them swell for about 12 hours.
3. Bring the porridge to a boil while stirring and then let it simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely soft, or about 3 hours.
4. Toward the end of cooking, thin with the cream.
5. Season with salt and serve with milk.
