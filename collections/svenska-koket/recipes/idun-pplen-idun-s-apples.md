## Ingredients

- 4 hg. simple butter dough
- 10 good, loose apples
- 75 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 hg. icing sugar
- 1 small egg white
- 2 tbsp. butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 34.
2. Peel the apples evenly and remove the cores with a melon baller.
3. Prepare the almond paste and fill the apples with it as in recipe no. 836.
4. Grease a baking pan with cold butter, place the apples in it, sprinkle with sugar, cover with a lid, and bake in the oven until half-soft and allow to cool.
5. Roll out the butter dough to approximately 1/2 cm. thickness and cut with a pastry wheel into even squares.
6. Place one apple on each square, fold the corners of the dough over the apple and press together well.
7. Brush the pastries with egg, sprinkle with sugar, place on a baking sheet, and bake in a warm oven.
