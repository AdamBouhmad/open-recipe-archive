## Ingredients

- 250 gr. powdered sugar
- 1 1/4 dcl. water
- 8 gr. glucose

## Directions

1. Cook caramel sugar to 180° from the powdered sugar, water, and glucose.
2. Cool rapidly by dipping the pan briefly in cold water.
3. Place the pan tilted in a frame of straw.
4. Lightly oil a marble slab or 2-3 baking sheets.
5. Oil a narrow stick and place it on the slab under a weight so it stands away from the slab.
6. With a broken whisk or broad fork, fling the sugar right and left over the stick until you have the needed amount.
7. Cut the threads with a heated knife and immediately wrap them around the dish to be garnished.
8. The sugar can be colored with various caramel colors.
