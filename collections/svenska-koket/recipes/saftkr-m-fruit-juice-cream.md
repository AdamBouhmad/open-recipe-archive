## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. sweet or sour fruit juice
- 6 dcl. water (if using sour juice)
- 3 dcl. sugar
- 50 gr. mondamin flour or 50 gr. potato starch

## Directions

1. The cream turns out best if two types of juice are used.
2. Mix the juice and water, and add sugar if the juice is sour.
