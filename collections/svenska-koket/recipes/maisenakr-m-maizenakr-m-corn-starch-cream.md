## Ingredients

- 10 cups skim milk
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 3 heaping tablespoons corn starch flour
- 4 to 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix one coffee cup of milk with the sugar and corn starch flour.
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil with the vanilla.
3. Stir in the mixture and let the cream cook 5 minutes, then remove from heat and add the whisked egg yolks.
