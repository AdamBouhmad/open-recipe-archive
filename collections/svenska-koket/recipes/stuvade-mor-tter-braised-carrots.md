## Ingredients

- 750 gr. carrots
- 1/2 litre water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 4 1/2 - 5 decilitres carrot broth
- 10 gr. cold butter
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse the carrots, brush clean, scrape, rinse again, dry and cut into long pieces, which are placed in boiling water, with salt and sugar added, and let boil soft.
2. Drain in a colander and save the broth.
3. Melt butter and flour in a saucepan for 2 minutes, add the carrot broth and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
4. Add the carrots, bring the braised dish to a boil and season with the cold butter, salt, sugar and parsley.
5. Serve with salted meat and fish.
6. Carrots can also be braised with milk.
