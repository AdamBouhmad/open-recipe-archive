## Ingredients

- 5 hg. prime wheat flour
- 50 gr. butter
- 3 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 15 gr. pressed yeast
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 1 tsk. sugar (5 gr.)
- 5-6 cardamom pods
- 1 egg for brushing
- 1 msk. poppy seeds

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl. Distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work it in with the fingertips, then stir in the milk and the yeast, dissolved with the salt and sugar and the finely crushed cardamom, if desired.
2. Work the dough very well and roll out into 15 round buns, in which a cross is made with a sharp knife.
3. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, allow to rise for approximately 1 1/2 hours, brush with whipped egg or milk, sprinkle with poppy seeds and bake in a warm oven for 20-25 minutes.
4. The breads can be formed as horns and treated as above.
5. The dough can also be rolled out into 30 thin sticks, which are cut diagonally, allowed to rise, brushed with milk, sprinkled with salt and baked until beautifully brown in a warm oven.
6. Instead of yeast, 2 teaspoons baking powder can be used, but the breads are then baked immediately they are rolled out.
