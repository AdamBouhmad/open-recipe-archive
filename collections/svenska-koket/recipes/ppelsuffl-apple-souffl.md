## Ingredients

- 1 kg apples
- 6 eggs
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the form

## Directions

1. Fry the apples and press them through a sieve
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy; mix in the applesauce, the grated lemon zest and lemon juice
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and gently fold in
4. Place the batter in a buttered pie form and place in the oven for approximately 30 minutes
5. Serve the soufflé immediately with whipped cream
