## Ingredients

- 1 hg flour
- 1/4 tsk salt
- 1/2 tsk sugar
- 2 dcl cream
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1/2 msk clarified butter (10 gr)
- 1/2 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add the seasonings, cream and egg yolk, and stir the batter until it becomes even and glossy.
2. Clarify the butter, let it cool and stir it in last.
3. The lard in which the crisps are to be cooked should be of the best quality. At the same time as the lard is put in the pot, place the iron in it to heat slowly.
4. The lard is warm enough when a thin blue smoke rises from the surface.
5. Cook the crisps as in the preceding recipe.
