## Ingredients

- For the pastry: 40 gr. sweet almond
- 3 bitter almonds
- 105 gr. fine butter
- 65 gr. sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 85 gr. flour
- For the shells: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- For garnish: 1 egg white
- 40 gr. icing sugar
- 15 pistachio almonds
- For filling: Compote of apples, pears or rowanberries, vanilla sauce

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, dry them and grind through an almond mill.
2. Wash the butter and work it in a mixing bowl together with the almonds, sugar, egg yolk and flour into a smooth dough, which should rest 2–3 hours in a cool place.
3. Butter small shells on the outside with a little butter.
4. Roll out the pastry to 3 mm thickness and cut with a large, round cutter into cakes, with which the shells are covered on the outside.
5. Place the shells with the pastry side up on a sheet and bake in ordinary oven heat until they are beautifully light brown, then remove them, let them cool and unmold.
6. Whip the egg white until stiff, beat in the icing sugar and spread on the cooled shells, then decorate with pistachios.
7. Fill with compote of fruit and serve with vanilla sauce.
