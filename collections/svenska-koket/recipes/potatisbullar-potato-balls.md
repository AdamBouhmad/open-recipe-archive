## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters potatoes
- water, salt
- 0.5 hectogram cold butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 tablespoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 4 tablespoons butter or cream

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, peel them and mash them with a fork or press them through a potato ricer.
2. Add the cold butter, salt, sugar and egg yolk; lastly cut in the egg white, beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Form small round balls with a floured spoon, flatten them, roll them in crushed rusks and fry them golden brown in butter or cream.
4. The cold butter and egg yolk can in simpler cases be replaced with milk.
