## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 50 grams sugar
- 2.5 parts white wine
- zest and juice of 0.5 lemon

## Directions

1. The lemon is zested so that only the yellow skin comes with it, and the juice is pressed out.
2. All ingredients are whisked well together in a pan and are allowed to simmer with vigorous whisking over high heat until the sauce has risen to double its size, at which point it is removed from heat and immediately served.
