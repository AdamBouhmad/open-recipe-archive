## Ingredients

- 1 large head of cauliflower
- 0.5 liters water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters broth
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, rinse it and place it in boiling water with salt, and let it cook until tender, after which place it on a sieve to drain.
2. Save the cauliflower cooking liquid.
3. Melt the butter and flour together, add the cauliflower liquid and broth, and let the soup simmer for 10 minutes.
4. Add the separated cauliflower, season the soup to taste and serve with toasted bread or cheese sandwiches.
5. If a simpler soup is desired, use 1.5 liters broth and 0.5 liters milk.
