## Ingredients

- 20 sprats
- 10 anchovies
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The sprats are cleaned, boned and rinsed well.
2. The anchovies are cleaned, boned and split.
3. An omelette mold is well buttered and sprinkled with crushed rusks.
4. An anchovy half is placed in each sprat, and the sprats are laid in the mold tightly together with the backs up.
5. The crushed rusks are sprinkled over, and the butter is dotted on top.
6. The mold is placed in good oven heat, and when the sprats are brown and cooked through, it is served.
