## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp. cream
- 2 tbsp. caster sugar
- 1/2 part brandy
- 1/3 tsp. grated lemon zest or 1 drop orange
- 250-300 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 kg. deep fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Work all the ingredients well together, and place the dough in a cool place to rest for at least 2 hours.
