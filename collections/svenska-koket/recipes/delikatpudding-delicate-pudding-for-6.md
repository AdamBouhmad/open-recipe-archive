## Ingredients

- 210 gr. sweet almonds
- 8 gr. bitter almonds
- 200 gr. sugar
- 160 gr. butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 15 gr. wheat flour
- 3 cups apple compote (or 2 liters apples, 1 cup crushed sugar, water)

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry in the oven and grate (or run through an almond mill) and pound very finely together with the sugar.
2. Clarify the butter and stir until it becomes white, then mix with the egg yolks, egg white and wheat flour.
3. Add the almonds and stir the mixture well.
4. Grease a pie mold with butter and line with the above mixture, then fill the mold nearly full with apple compote.
5. Place a ring of dough around the edge of the mold.
6. Bake in gentle oven heat and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
