## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 1 dcl. water or milk
- 60 gr. flour
- 25 gr. grated cheese
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 kg. broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Boil the butter and water together, add the flour while whisking steadily and let the mixture boil until it releases from the pan, then pour it out and press out the whisk well.
2. When the mixture has cooled, add the grated cheese and work in the eggs one by one.
3. Stir the mixture for 10 minutes, then form it with two teaspoons dipped in broth into small balls the size of a hazelnut.
4. Cook them golden-brown in the broth and place them on gray paper to drain.
5. The broth should not be too hot, otherwise the balls cannot puff up.
6. Serve lukewarm, placed on cake paper, to broth.
