## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. butter
- 6 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 18 asparagus tips
- 2 tbsp. tomato puree

## Directions

1. Butter six small heat-proof porcelain dishes or ordinary shells with half of the butter.
2. Carefully place one raw egg in each dish, season with salt and pepper and cook in a water bath in the oven for about 6 minutes.
