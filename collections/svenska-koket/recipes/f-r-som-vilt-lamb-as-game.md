## Ingredients

- 2 kg. lamb roast (loin) or saddle
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg. bacon
- 2 dcl. white or red wine
- 2 dcl. vinegar
- 2 dcl. water
- 2 red onions, in wedges
- 1/2 celery, in cubes
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsk. whole cloves
- 2 tsk. coarsely crushed juniper berries
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 3 dcl. broth
- 5 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung so that it is tender, is beaten with a damp mallet, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. For the marinade, blend all the ingredients in a stone crock, place the meat in it and let it lie 2-3 days, turning it 2 times a day.
3. Remove it, let it drain, wrap it in a cloth, lard it and roast as in no. 533.
4. Garnish with browned potatoes and parsley. Serve with reduced sauce (see no. 429), salads and cucumber.
