## Ingredients

- Dough: 220 gr. flour
- 1 decilitre lukewarm milk
- 25 gr. fresh yeast
- 220 gr. fine butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- 30 gr. sweet almonds or hazelnuts
- 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon or orange peel
- 5 whole eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.) for the mold
- Sugar syrup: 1/2 kg. sugar
- 5 decilitres water
- 3 decilitres maraschino liqueur or white wine or 1/2 decilitre arrack

## Directions

1. Sift the flour; work part of it well together with the milk and the yeast dissolved with 2 teaspoons sugar.
2. Work the dough until it becomes supple and glossy, and let it rise on the oven door.
3. Cream the butter well with the sugar and salt, blanched and prepared almonds or hazelnuts, and lemon or orange peel.
4. Continue with remaining steps as the recipe text is cut off.
