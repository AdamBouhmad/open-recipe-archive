## Ingredients

- Three different types of ice cream, such as vanilla, pistachio and chocolate—1/2 batch of each
- Candied fruit (optional)

## Directions

1. Once the three different ice creams are frozen in the machine, layer them alternately in an ice mold.
2. Pack the mold with ice and salt (see recipe no. 929).
3. If desired, candied fruit may be placed between the layers.
