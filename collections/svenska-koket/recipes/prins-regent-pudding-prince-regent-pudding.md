## Ingredients

- 125 gr butter
- 125 gr sugar
- 6 eggs
- 125 gr grated dark bread (pumpernickel)
- 50 gr sweet almonds
- ground cinnamon
- crushed cloves
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy.
2. Add the yolks, the grated bread, the chopped almonds and spices to taste.
3. Last, carefully fold in the beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and bake in a water bath on the stove for 2 hours.
5. Serve the pudding warm with foam sauce.
