## Ingredients

- 1 pike perch, 1.5 kg
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)
- 2 tbsp clarified butter (40 gr)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tbsp finely crushed rusks
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr) for frying
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 gr)
- 4 dcl thick cream
- mushroom caps
- butter
- mushroom sauce
- pressed potato

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and prepare as in recipe n:o 353.
2. Garnish with mushroom caps sautéed in butter and serve with mushroom sauce and pressed potato.
3. Note: If serving the fish at a dinner with multiple courses, 1 kg pike perch is sufficient for 6 persons.
