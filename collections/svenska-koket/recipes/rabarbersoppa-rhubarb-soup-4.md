## Ingredients

- 3–4 rhubarb stalks or 1/2 liter preserved rhubarb
- 2 liters water
- Zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1–1 1/2 coffee spoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 coffee spoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse it, dry it, and cut it into 2 cm. long pieces.
2. Boil the water, add the rhubarb and lemon zest, and let it cook until it falls apart.
3. Strain the soup, return it to the pot, and add the crushed sugar.
4. Bring it to boil again, thicken with the potato flour mixed in 1/2 coffee spoon water, and let it cook 2 minutes.
5. Beat the egg yolks and powdered sugar in the soup bowl to a foam, and pour the soup in with steady whisking.
6. Serve with potato flour cakes or rusks. In simpler versions, omit the egg yolks but use more potato flour.
