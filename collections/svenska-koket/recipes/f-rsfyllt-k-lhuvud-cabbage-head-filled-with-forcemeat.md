## Ingredients

- 1 medium-sized cabbage head
- 300 g boneless beef
- 200 g lard
- 3-4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 1 small tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 pork knuckle
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 liter bouillon

## Directions

1. Blanch the cabbage head whole, remove it and let it drain.
2. Hollow it out from the top and fill it with the worked meat forcemeat, which should be fairly loose.
3. Cover with a few large cabbage leaves and bind it together.
4. Brown it in smoking hot butter.
5. When the cabbage head is browned, remove it, discard the butter, place a pork knuckle on the bottom of the pot, and lay the cabbage head upon it.
6. Pour the boiling bouillon over and let the dish simmer slowly for at least 2 hours.
7. Serve on a platter with the sauce in a separate sauce bowl.
