## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless inner thigh
- 100 gr. lard, in 12 slices
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 6 dcl. boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until tender, is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into 12 slices, which are well beaten and covered with the lard slices.
2. These are sprinkled with a mixture of salt and pepper and the meat slices are rolled up tightly and bound in two places with fine twine, dipped in boiling water, or fastened together with toothpicks.
3. The rolls are well browned in the butter and sprinkled with flour; the boiling broth or water, mixed with a little meat extract or soy sauce, is poured on, after which they are allowed to simmer slowly with a tight lid, 2 1/2–3 hours.
4. During this time the pot is shaken often so that the rolls do not burn.
5. They are placed on a warm platter, the strings are cut away and the sauce is whisked well, thinned if necessary, tasted and poured over the rolls.
6. Served with boiled or pressed potatoes.
