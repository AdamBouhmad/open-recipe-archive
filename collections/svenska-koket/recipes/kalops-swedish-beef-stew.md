## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg. boneless beef (thigh, rib, or marrow pipe)
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 3–4 red onions
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 8 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 6 dcl. boiling water
- 1/2 tbsp. soy

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until tender, is prepared (see page 232), and cut across the muscle fibers into approximately 1 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are lightly pounded on both sides and turned in a mixture of the spices and flour.
2. The onion is peeled, chopped coarsely, and browned in the butter in an iron pot; the meat slices are added along with the allspice berries and bay leaves, and the water mixed with soy is added.
3. The pot is set over a fresh fire so that the liquid rapidly comes to a boil; the meat is then allowed to simmer slowly with a tight lid until it is tender, or approximately 2 1/2–3 hours.
4. Serve with the meat gravy, strained and skimmed.
