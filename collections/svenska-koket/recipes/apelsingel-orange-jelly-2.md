## Ingredients

- 375 g lump sugar
- peel and juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 12 oranges
- 1 liter water
- 45 g gelatin
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Dry the lemon, rub the yellow peel against the sugar, and press out the juice.
2. Cut the oranges in half and press out the juice.
3. Pour the sugar, lemon and orange juice, and water into a saucepan and place over heat.
4. When the mixture is warm, add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little warm water and the beaten egg whites, and while whisking, bring to a brief boil.
5. Strain the jelly through a cloth wrung out in hot water.
6. The first jelly that runs through is not clear, so pour it back into the cloth.
7. Pour the clear jelly into a jelly mold or a jelly form rinsed with cold water and set out to set.
8. Garnish with a little set and chopped jelly.
