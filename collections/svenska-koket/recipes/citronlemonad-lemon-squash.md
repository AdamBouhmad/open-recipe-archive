## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- 2 1/2 kilograms sugar
- 1 hectogram citric acid
- 2 liters boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash and dry the lemons, cut them in half and press out the juice well.
2. Remove all the white part from the peels, which are then cut into small pieces.
3. Place the sugar in a crock, add the lemon juice, peel, citric acid and water, and stir the mixture well.
4. Place a clean folded towel and a plate with weight on top and let the lemonade stand for one day, stirring occasionally.
5. Strain and fill into dry bottles, which are corked.
6. When serving, dilute 1 deciliter of lemonade with approximately 1 liter of water and serve with meals or as a refreshing drink.
