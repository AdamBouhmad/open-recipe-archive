## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized lettuce heads
- 150 gr. fresh cucumber, in slices
- 2 tomatoes, in slices
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- a little paprika or white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoon vinegar or acetic acid
- 3-4 tablespoon fine table oil

## Directions

1. The lettuce heads are treated as in the previous recipe.
2. The cucumber is peeled and cut into slices, the tomatoes are rinsed, dried and cut likewise into slices.
3. The salad is prepared just before it is served—preferably at the table.
4. In the salad bowl the seasonings, vinegar and table oil are mixed, the salad, cucumber and tomato slices are added and gently mixed with the dressing.
5. Served with roasted meat.
6. In the same way salad can be prepared from endive, chicory, watercress, tender dandelion leaves and the like.
