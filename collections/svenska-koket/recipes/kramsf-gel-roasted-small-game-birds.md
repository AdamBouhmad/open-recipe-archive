## Ingredients

- various small wild birds such as thrush, sparrow, quail, lark, etc.
- thin slices of lard
- butter
- cream
- parsley
- mushroom caps
- croutons
- applesauce

## Directions

1. Pluck, gut, rinse well, dry, truss the birds, wrap them with thin slices of lard.
2. Brown in butter, add cream and roast approximately 15 minutes, basting frequently.
3. Split the birds and arrange them on croutons.
4. Whip up the sauce with a little thick cream and cold butter, season to taste and pour over the birds.
5. Garnish with parsley and, if desired, with mushroom caps sautéed in butter, or with small triangular croutons fried in butter and topped with applesauce.
