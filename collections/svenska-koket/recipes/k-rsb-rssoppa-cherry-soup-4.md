## Ingredients

- 6 parts fresh or preserved cherries
- 2 litres water
- Sugar to taste
- 1½ tablespoon potato flour

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil, add the cherries and let them cook until soft.
2. Add sugar if needed.
3. Thicken the soup with potato flour mixed with a little cold water, bring to a good boil and let it cool.
4. Serve with whipped cream if desired.
