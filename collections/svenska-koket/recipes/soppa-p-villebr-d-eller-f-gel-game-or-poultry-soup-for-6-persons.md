## Ingredients

- Scraps and bones of hare or game bird carcass and scraps of poultry
- 2 carrots
- 1/2 root celery
- 1 small red onion
- 4 parsley stems
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 liters broth without meat
- 4 white peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- salt
- cayenne
- sugar
- 1/2 dcl sherry or madeira
- 10 gr fine butter

## Directions

1. If raw bones and carcasses are used, the soup will be finer in flavor.
2. Cut off the best meat and use it for forcemeat or meatballs, which are served in the soup.
3. Cut the scraps and bones into cubes and add, then allow the soup to cook until the vegetables are tender.
4. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, thin the milk and cream, add the soup and allow to cook with stirring for 5 minutes.
5. Season with salt, cayenne and sugar, and add the sherry or madeira.
6. Finish with fine butter and serve.
