## Ingredients

- 3 kg. of leg or shoulder
- 3 hg. lard
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 dcl. vinegar
- 3 dcl. white wine
- 2 dcl. water
- 2 tbsp. salad oil
- 6 cloves
- 1 small piece of ginger
- 1 red onion, in slices
- 4 parsley stems
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 boned anchovies
- 5 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tsp. sugar

## Directions

1. Pound the meat with a damp mallet, lard it, rub with the remaining spices and place in a deep dish.
2. Mix all the marinade ingredients and pour over the meat, which should lie in it for 2-3 days, turning it often.
3. Remove it, let it drain, dry it well, brown it in butter in a roasting pan, add the strained marinade, cover with the anchovies and simmer 1 hour.
4. Cook the cream with sugar and add it, then let the meat roast until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours.
5. Cut and garnish with browned potatoes or vegetables and serve with reduced sauce (see no. 429), added with 1 tbsp. chopped capers.
