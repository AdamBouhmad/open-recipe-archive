## Ingredients

- 2 hg. sugar
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling water
- 1 1/2 dcl. cognac

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a lukewarm frying pan and stir over the fire until it has melted and attained a light brown color.
2. Add the water; when the sauce has cooled slightly, carefully pour the cognac over it and ignite.
3. Serve flaming with marrow pudding, etc.
