## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 parts fish stock
- 2 parts thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 medium tomatoes

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour, add the fish stock and cream, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes, then season and finish with the egg yolks.
