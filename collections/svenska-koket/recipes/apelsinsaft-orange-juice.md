## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 1.5 liters water
- 36 grams citric acid
- 3 kg lump sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and dry the oranges.
2. Chop the sugar into small pieces and rub the peel against the sugar, then place it in a crock.
3. Press out the orange juice and pour it over the sugar together with the water and the finely ground citric acid.
4. Cover the crock and let the mixture stand for 4 days, stirring it occasionally.
5. Strain the juice and fill it into dry bottles, which are immediately corked, sealed with resin or paraffin, or else closed as in the previous recipe.
