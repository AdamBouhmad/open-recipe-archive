## Ingredients

- 50 g butter
- 50 g flour
- 1/4 liter unwhipped milk
- 5 eggs
- 50 g caster sugar
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- preserved fruit
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together, add the milk gradually while stirring constantly, and let the mixture come to a good boil.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy, then blend it into the cooked mixture.
3. Finally fold in the whites, beaten until stiff.
4. Place a layer of preserved fruit in a buttered pie form and pour the batter over it.
5. Bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 1/2 hour.
6. Serve warm.
