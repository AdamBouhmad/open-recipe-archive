## Ingredients

- 2½ kg. reindeer saddle
- 4 liters drink
- 1 kg. lard
- 3 tbsp. butter
- ½ tbsp. salt
- ¾–1 liter broth or water
- ½ tbsp. butter for sauce
- 1½ tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Place the meat in drink overnight, remove it, rinse well and dry with a cloth. Remove all membranes carefully, and lard the reindeer saddle in close rows with the lard cut into fine strips. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, and brown the roast well therein; thereafter salt it and dilute with broth little by little, baste often and turn a couple of times during roasting.
