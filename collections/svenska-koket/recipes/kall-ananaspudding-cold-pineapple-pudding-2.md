## Ingredients

- 8 parts cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 1.5 tablespoons sugar
- 1.5 cans pineapple
- 1 part sherry
- 6 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Whip 3 parts of the cream together with the egg yolks and sugar over heat until the cream thickens.
2. Continue whipping until the cream cools.
3. Whip the remaining cream to stiff peaks and fold in together with the pineapple, cut into small pieces, and the sherry.
4. Finally fold in the gelatin sheets, dissolved in the pineapple juice.
5. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
6. Dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with pineapple.
