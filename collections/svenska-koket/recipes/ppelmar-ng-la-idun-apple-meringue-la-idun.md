## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 120 grams sugar
- 60 grams wheat flour
- 4 bitter almonds
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed biscuits
- 200 grams sugar for the compote
- 4 decilitres water
- juice and peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1 1/2 litres apples
- 3 egg whites
- 105 grams icing sugar
- 10 grams pistachio almonds (approximately 22 pieces)
- 40 grams sweet almonds (approximately 40 pieces)

## Directions

1. A mold or baking pan of the same width as the dish on which the meringue is to be served is buttered and sprinkled with crushed biscuits.
2. The eggs and sugar are stirred together for 1/2 hour, then the flour and the blanched and grated almond are added.
3. The mixture is poured into the buttered mold, and the cake is baked in ordinary oven heat for approximately 20 minutes.
4. It is turned out onto a cooling rack, allowed to cool and then placed on a round dish, preferably silver.
