## Ingredients

- 1 slice beef fillet (1 hg.)
- 2 cooked cold potatoes
- 1 cooked beet, cut into cubes
- 2 tsk. finely chopped red onion
- 2 tsk. finely chopped capers
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Scrape the meat finely, form into a round ball, which is flattened and placed on a round dish and garnished with the potatoes, beet, onion and capers.
2. Serve a raw egg yolk alongside in an egg cup.
