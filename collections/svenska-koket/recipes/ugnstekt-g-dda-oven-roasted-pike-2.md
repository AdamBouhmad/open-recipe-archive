## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 80 gr. butter
- 3 dcl. boiling broth
- 1/2 dcl. white wine

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; keep the head but remove the gills
2. Rub with salt and let rest for 1 hour, then dry thoroughly
3. Dip in the beaten egg and coat with crushed rusks
4. Roast in the oven for about 1/2 hour as in recipe n:o 338
5. Serve with mushroom sauce and mashed potatoes
6. Alternatively, the pike can be filleted with head, backbone, and skin removed (these can be used to make broth for basting the fish), then roast in the same manner
7. Roasted pike can also be served with applesauce
