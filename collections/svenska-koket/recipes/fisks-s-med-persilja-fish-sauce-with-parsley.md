## Ingredients

- 1½ tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 6 deciliters fish stock
- 3 tablespoons chopped parsley, or 2 tablespoons chopped chives, or 2 hard-boiled

## Directions

1. Prepare the base sauce from butter and flour, add fish stock, and cook until the desired consistency is reached. Add the chosen flavoring ingredient.
