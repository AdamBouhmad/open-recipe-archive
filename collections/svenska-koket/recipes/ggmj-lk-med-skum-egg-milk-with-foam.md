## Ingredients

- 2 liters milk
- 3 eggs
- sugar
- salt
- peel of 1/8 lemon, grated

## Directions

1. Separate egg yolks and whites; place yolks in a soup bowl and whisk whites until stiff foam.
2. Bring milk to boil and drop small lumps of egg white foam into it with a spoon.
3. Do not let the milk boil during this process.
4. After a couple of minutes, remove the lumps with a slotted spoon and place on a sieve.
5. Grate the lemon peel into the milk, season, and whisk together with the egg yolks.
6. Place the lumps in the bowl, sprinkle with sugar, and serve the milk.
