## Ingredients

- 4 kg. lamb saddle
- 2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 hectogram butter
- 3/4 liter meat gravy or broth
- 1 can mushrooms (1/2 liter)
- 1 shallot
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr.)
- 3 deciliters meat gravy
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons melted butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The saddle is treated as in the preceding recipe, but the outer skin is not cut but carefully removed and the saddle is larded in even, dense rows with finely cut lard strips, rolled in a mixture of the salt and pepper. Rub the saddle with the remaining seasonings.
2. Bind it and roast as in the preceding recipe. It should not roast too long, as it will then become dry.
3. For the stewing, chop the mushrooms and shallot finely and sauté in the butter until it becomes clear, squeeze them out with a spoon and sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes.
4. Thin with the gravy and cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes, after which add the mushroom and shallot to the stewing and season with the seasonings.
5. Remove the saddle, loosen the meat neatly on both sides of the backbone; place on a cutting board and cut into thin slices.
