## Ingredients

- 3 large celeriac roots
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt for pre-cooking
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 deciliters boiling broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Wash, brush, and peel the celeriac roots. Cut into cubes and pre-cook 7-8 minutes with the lid open.
2. Drain in a colander and let drip on a cloth.
3. Prepare as creamed root vegetables, following the method for creamed parsnips.
