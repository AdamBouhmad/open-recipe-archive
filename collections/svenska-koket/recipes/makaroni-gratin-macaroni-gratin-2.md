## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hectogram good macaroni
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 80 gr. butter
- 1 finely chopped red onion
- 50 gr. lean veal (in fine cubes)
- 40 gr. flour
- 8 deciliters boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.) for the mold
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 3 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni in salted boiling water until tender, drain and set aside.
2. For the béchamel sauce: Brown the butter in a saucepan, add the finely chopped red onion and the lean veal cut into fine cubes.
3. Sprinkle the flour over and stir well, then gradually add the boiling milk while stirring constantly.
4. Season with salt, white pepper and a little grated nutmeg.
5. Butter a mold and layer the macaroni with the béchamel sauce.
6. Sprinkle grated cheese and crushed bread crumbs on top.
7. Bake in the oven until golden brown.
