## Ingredients

- 3 kg roast beef
- 1 tbsp salt
- boiling water
- 2 large onions
- 3 tbsp butter

## Directions

1. The roast beef should hang for 1-2 weeks depending on the season
2. Wash with a cloth dipped in hot water, pound lightly, and sprinkle with salt
3. Place in a hot roasting pan
4. Pour a little boiling water into the pan and place in the oven
5. The oven should not be too hot at first, as some fat on the roast beef must melt before actual browning begins
6. Gradually increase the heat in the oven and baste the roast with its juices, occasionally thinning with boiling water
7. Towards the end of roasting, cover the roast beef with butter or waxed paper to prevent burning
8. Allow 20-25 minutes per kg of meat for roasting
9. Peel and cook the onion in butter
10. Place the roast beef on a warm plate and garnish with onion
11. Strain the sauce, skim carefully, and serve as gravy
