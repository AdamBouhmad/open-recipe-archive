## Ingredients

- 2 kg. berries
- 2 kg. loaf sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Treat the berries as in recipe no. 1030 and place them in a deep bowl.
2. Prepare a syrup from the sugar and water (see previous recipe), which should be poured hot over the berries.
3. Let everything stand until the following day, when the preserve should be brought slowly to a boil and cooked with skimming and shaking until the berries are clear and sink.
4. Refer to the previous recipes for further treatment.
