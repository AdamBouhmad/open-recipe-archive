## Ingredients

- 2½ kg pork (preferably loin)
- 425 grams lard pork
- 1⅓ liters water
- 2 parts beer
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate
- 2 teaspoons ground white pepper
- 1 teaspoon ground strong pepper
- 5 meters casings (curved casing)
- For rubbing in: 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water.
