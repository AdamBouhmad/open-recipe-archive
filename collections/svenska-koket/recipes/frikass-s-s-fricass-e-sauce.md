## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tablespoon flour (50 gr.)
- 6 deciliter meat broth
- 2—3 egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2—3 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
2. Gradually add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the beaten egg yolks and let the sauce simmer with whisking.
4. Season with seasonings and lemon juice and serve over the stewed meat.
