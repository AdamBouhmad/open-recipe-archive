## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower head
- 1 hectogram young carrots
- 3 hectograms small green cucumbers
- 1 hectogram pickling onion
- 1 hectogram young turban beans
- water
- salt
- 15 grams whole white pepper
- 7 grams mace
- 2 slices Spanish pepper
- 1.5 liters vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil all vegetables and root vegetables separately in salted water until just tender.
2. Let them drain well and place them then in glass preserving jars together with the spices.
3. Boil the vinegar, let it cool, and pour over.
