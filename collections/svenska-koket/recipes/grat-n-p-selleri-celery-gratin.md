## Ingredients

- 2 medium celeriac roots
- 2 liters water
- 15 gr salt
- 60 gr butter
- 70 gr flour
- 5 dcl boiling milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel and rinse the celeriac, place in boiling salted water and cook until tender, testing with a fine whisk
2. Remove, let cool, and cut into cubes
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, then add boiling milk and cook under stirring for 5 minutes
4. Pour into a dish and stir in the seasonings
5. When most of the heat has passed, stir in the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 15 minutes
6. Finally carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
7. Butter a mold and sprinkle with crushed rusks
8. Place the egg mixture and celery cubes in alternating layers, with egg mixture as first and last layer
9. Do not fill the mold more than 3/4 full, as the mixture will rise
10. Bake in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 1/2 hours, adding boiling water as needed to maintain even heat
11. Test with a whisk and serve with melted butter immediately upon removing from the oven
