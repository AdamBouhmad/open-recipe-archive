## Ingredients

- 750 gr. fresh beans
- 7 deciliters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 3 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.) - optional

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the beans, let them drain on cloth, and cut them into 4 mm thick pieces, crosswise (somewhat on the slant).
2. Place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until they are soft; then pour off excess liquid and mix the beans with the parsley and cold butter.
3. If a thicker sauce is desired, mix the flour with half of the butter, add the cooking liquid, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes, then add the beans and mix with the parsley and the remaining butter.
4. Serve with braised ham, boiled sausage and roasted meat.
