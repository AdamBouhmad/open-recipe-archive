## Ingredients

- 350 gr. dried apricots
- 1.3 lit. water
- 125 gr. sugar
- 3 1/2 dcl. of the cooking liquid
- 3–4 leaves gelatin
- the strained apricots

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well and cook them in the water with added sugar until soft.
2. Strain the liquid from the apricots and pass the apricots through a sieve.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and place in the fruit liquid, which is then brought to a boil and whisked until cold and beginning to thicken.
4. Add the fruit purée, and pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 3–4 hours to set.
5. Loosen the pudding from the edges with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish, garnish with fruit and serve with vanilla sauce.
