## Ingredients

- 4 pork feet
- 4 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 gr.)
- baking soda, of the size of a pea

## Directions

1. The pork feet are allowed to lie in plenty of cold water for 1 day. They are then scalded with hot water and scraped well, placed in cold water with the salt and soda added, skimmed and allowed to cook until a whisk goes easily through the meat. They are immediately taken out of the broth and cooled in cold water to become white. The soda is used during cooking for the same reason. Stored in brine. Served with beets.
