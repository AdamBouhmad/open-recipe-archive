## Ingredients

- 3 hg. cooked, salted beef brisket (leftovers)
- 1/2 lit. mashed potatoes (leftovers)
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the beef brisket while warm into fine strips and mix with the warm mashed potatoes.
2. Cut the mass into slices and fry in the butter and place on a warm dish.
3. Garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish. Cooked, salted lamb brisket can also be used advantageously.
