## Ingredients

- 2 litres large, beautiful sugar peas
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 2 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the peas, rinse well and let drain on a cloth.
2. Place them in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them simmer slowly until completely tender.
3. Strain the cooking liquid and gently mix the peas with cold butter and parsley.
4. Serve with ham and roasted meat.
5. The pea pods can also be tied in bundles, cooked as above but in slightly more water, placed on a napkin on a warm platter and served with creamed butter as a side dish.
6. Sugar peas can also be braised like garden peas.
