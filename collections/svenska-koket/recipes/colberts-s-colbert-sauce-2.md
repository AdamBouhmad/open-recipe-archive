## Ingredients

- 120 gr. maitre-d'hôtel butter
- 1/2 tsp. chopped tarragon leaves
- 1/2 dcl. light meat stock

## Directions

1. Prepare the maitre-d'hôtel butter as in recipe 405 and blend with finely chopped tarragon.
2. Boil the meat stock and add it to the butter, little by little, with continuous stirring.
3. The sauce must not boil.
4. Season to taste and serve with fried fish and meat.
