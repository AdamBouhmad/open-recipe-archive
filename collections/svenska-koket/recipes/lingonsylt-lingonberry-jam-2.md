## Ingredients

- 6 lit. lingon (approx. 3 kg.)
- 1 1/2 lit. vatten
- 2 1/2 kg. toppsocker

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries and rinse them, preferably with hot water, which should drain well.
2. Boil the sugar and water for approx. 15 minutes under frequent skimming.
3. Add the berries and let the jam cook slowly with skimming until the berries appear clear.
4. Do not let the lingonberries cook too long, or the jam will become dark.
5. Ladle the jam into warm, dry jars and let cool.
6. Bind the jars with parchment paper.
7. For simpler lingonberry jam, the sugar can be reduced or replaced with syrup.
