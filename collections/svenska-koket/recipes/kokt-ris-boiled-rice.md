## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. Carolina rice (2 hg.)
- 3 lit. water
- 1 1/2 msk. salt (23 gr.)
- 75 gr. butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in cold water, add to boiling salted water and let boil vigorously for 15 min., then pour into a colander, rinse with cold water and let drain.
2. Put the rice in a saucepan, distribute the butter in flakes and blend it into the rice with a fork.
3. Place the lid on the saucepan, which should then stand in a warm oven for about 15 min., during which the rice is often stirred with a fork.
4. Serve with eggs, cold fish, boiled meat and chicken.
