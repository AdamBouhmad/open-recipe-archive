## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 part water (scarcely)
- 150 gr. fine butter
- 1 hg. blanched sweet almonds
- 4 eggs
- 2 hg. granulated sugar
- 1 part strawberry jam

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 34, roll out thinly, cut with a round mold into cakes, and line small fluted pastry molds with it.
2. For the filling, dry the almonds in a cloth and grind them.
3. Whip the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the almonds.
4. Place a little jam in each mold, then fill the molds nearly to the rim with almond mixture.
5. Bake the pastries in a warm oven until the almond mixture feels dry when tested with a whisk.
6. Turn the pastries out, let cool, and serve with coffee or tea.
