## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. thin heavy cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. sugar
- 50 gr. biscuits
- 10 cloudberries or 6 pears
- 5 dcl. extra thick heavy cream

## Directions

1. Heat the thin heavy cream with the vanilla pod and let it stand covered for 20 minutes to infuse
2. Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar in a pot for 15 minutes, add the cream while whisking, and let the mixture simmer over the fire while continuing to whisk until it is thick and frothy
3. Pour the cream out and whisk until it is cold
4. Finely pound the biscuits, drain the fruit, cut it and remove the seeds
5. Whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the cream
6. In a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold, layer the cream, pounded biscuits, and fruit alternately, with cream as the first and last layer
7. Freeze the pudding
