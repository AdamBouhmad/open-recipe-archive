## Ingredients

- 850 gr. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 lit. milk
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1 hg. butter
- 125 gr. sugar
- 20 gr. sweet almonds (20 pieces)
- 5 bitter almonds
- 1 egg
- 6-8 cardamom pods
- 1 egg for brushing
- 15 gr. sweet almonds
- 50 gr. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, set aside a small amount for rolling out. Heat the milk to approximately 40 degrees and stir it into the flour together with the yeast, dissolved with the salt and 1 tsk. of the sugar, after which the dough is worked until it is smooth and glossy.
2. The dough can be rolled out into 15 round buns, in which a cross is made with a sharp knife.
3. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, allow to rise for approximately 1 1/2 hours, brush with whipped egg or milk, sprinkle with poppy seeds and bake in a warm oven for 20-25 minutes.
4. The dough can also be rolled out into 30 thin sticks, which are cut diagonally, allowed to rise, brushed with milk, sprinkled with salt and baked until beautifully brown in a warm oven.
5. Instead of yeast, 2 teaspoons baking powder can be used, but the breads are then baked immediately they are rolled out.
