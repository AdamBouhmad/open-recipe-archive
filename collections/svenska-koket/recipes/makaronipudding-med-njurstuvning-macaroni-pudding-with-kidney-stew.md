## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms macaroni
- water
- 3 eggs
- ½ liter unskimmed milk
- 1 tablespoon butter (for the mold)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs (for the mold)
- 3 calf kidneys
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons wheat flour
- white pepper
- salt
- sugar
- ½ cup grated cheese
- stock
- pepper
- salt
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 1 decimeter long pieces and parboil well in water.
2. Place them in a buttered and breadcrumb-covered ring mold.
3. Whisk the eggs together with the milk and seasonings, and pour this mixture over the macaroni.
4. Put the pudding in the oven to bake for 1 hour.
5. Blanch the kidneys and cut them into cubes, brown them in butter, add stock and let them cook until soft.
6. Melt butter and flour together, add meat gravy and stock, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
7. Add the kidneys and season the stew with the spices and wine.
8. Turn the pudding out onto a plate, sprinkle the grated cheese over it, and place the stew in the middle.
