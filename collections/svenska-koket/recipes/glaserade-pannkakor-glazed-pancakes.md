## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 dcl. water
- approximately 135 gr. flour
- 2 dcl. melted butter (2 hg.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) for the pan
- 2 cups fine jam (raspberry or cloudberry)
- 50 gr. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with a steel whisk, add the cream, then whisk in the flour, which should preferably be sifted.
2. Dilute with the water, add the salt and butter, and let the batter rest for 2 hours.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it.
4. Stir the batter well and fry thin pancakes, placing them on top of each other on a warm plate.
5. Coat each pancake thinly with some of the jam, which has been warmed in a water bath.
6. Cut the pancakes into 12 pieces and let them cool.
7. Place the pancakes on a baking sheet, brush them with warm jam, cut them diagonally at both ends, cut them lengthwise down the middle, and arrange them with the rounded side up.
8. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, glaze in a hot oven, and serve arranged on cake paper.
