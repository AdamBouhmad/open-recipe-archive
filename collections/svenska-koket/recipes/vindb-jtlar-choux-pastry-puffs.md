## Ingredients

- 3.5 dl water
- 105 g wheat flour
- 105 g butter
- 3 eggs
- 2 tsp sugar

## Directions

1. Bring water, butter, and sugar to a boil
2. Whisk in the flour, and let the mixture cook until it pulls away from the pan
3. Once most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg yolks one at a time; lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
4. Press the mixture through a piping bag or fluted pastry cone onto a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet in the shape of "S"s or other figures
5. Bake in a warm oven
6. During baking, open the oven door as seldom as possible, as the draft will cause the pastries to collapse and become flat
7. If the oven's bottom heat is too strong, use double baking sheets
