## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. melted butter (40 gr.)
- 1 3/4 lit. milk
- 1 piece cinnamon stick (5 cm.)
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 1 tsp. salt

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs for 3 minutes, add the flour and butter, and work the dough until smooth and supple, then let it rest for 1 hour.
2. Boil the milk with the cinnamon and sugar, press the dough through a colander with not-too-small holes into the milk, keeping it at a gentle simmer.
3. Move the saucepan to the side of the stove and let it stand covered for about 10 minutes, then season the gruel with salt.
