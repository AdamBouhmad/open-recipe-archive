## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hg star pasta
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 liters stock
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 deciliter sherry or madeira
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream

## Directions

1. Place the pasta in boiling salted water and allow to cook until completely soft, then drain in a colander.
2. Rinse with cold water so it does not stick together and place in the soup bowl.
3. Bring the stock to a boil and season with the cheese, spices and wine.
4. Beat the egg yolks with the cream and whisk into the soup, then pour over the pasta.
5. Serve very hot.
