## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. extra thick cream
- 3 dcl. jam
- 3 dcl. pieces of small bread or 18 small sugar biscuits
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk (for soaking)

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks.
2. Mix in the jam and small bread or biscuits (soaked in 1 1/2 dcl. milk).
3. Place the cream in a compote bowl.
4. The small bread can be omitted, in which case the cream is piped into cones instead.
