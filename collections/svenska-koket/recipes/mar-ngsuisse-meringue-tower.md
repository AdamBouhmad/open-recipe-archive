## Ingredients

- 165 gr. icing sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 8 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Sift the sugar through a fine sieve.
2. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and carefully fold in the sugar with a fork into the egg white foam.
3. Spread the mixture onto a well-cleaned and buttered sheet in the form of round bases, which should be uniform if the meringue tower is to be made cylindrical, but larger and smaller if the tower is desired to be pyramid-shaped.
4. On the edge of each base, pipe a ring of small meringues, close together.
5. Dust the meringues with a little sugar and bake in gentle oven heat until they are almost dry and release from the sheet, approximately 1 hour.
6. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks, then add the vanilla sugar.
7. When the meringue cakes are cold, layer them together with cream between each.
8. Cover the tower externally with whipped cream, smoothing it even with a spoon.
9. Divide the remaining cream in two parts; color one part pink with a little dissolved carmine.
10. Pipe the white and pink cream through a fluted piping tube in beautiful garlands over the tower, then carefully transfer it to a flat glass dish with parchment paper.
11. Do not decorate the meringue tower until 1/2 hour before it is to be served.
12. It may be decorated with only white cream and preserved fruits if desired.
