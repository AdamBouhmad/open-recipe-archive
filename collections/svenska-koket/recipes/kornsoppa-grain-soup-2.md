## Ingredients

- 1 cup crushed groats
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 24 prunes
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- salt to taste
- 2 1/2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats, put them on in the cold water and let them cook until they are soft, then pass them through a sieve.
2. Rinse the prunes and put them on with the lemon zest and sugar in cold water to cook slowly until soft, with a closed lid.
3. Pour the strained soup back into the pot, bring to a good boil, and add the prunes with their cooking liquid.
4. Taste the soup with sugar and salt and serve with whipped cream.
