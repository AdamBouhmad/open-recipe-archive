## Ingredients

- 1.5 to 2 kg veal brisket
- 1.5 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 carrots
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 6 deciliters meat broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the meat as in recipe no. 524, place on a warm plate and cover while the sauce cooks
2. Strain the broth, skim well, and cook the sauce as in recipe no. 441, but omit the egg yolks and use parsley instead of lemon juice
3. Pour the sauce over the meat, which is served with boiled potatoes
