## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg chestnuts
- 4-5 deciliters broth
- 1 small celery stalk
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 2-3 tablespoons thick cream
- salt
- a little white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a cross 1/2 cm deep into the pointed end of the chestnuts.
2. Place them in boiling water and let cook 2 minutes so the shells loosen.
3. Remove both the outer and inner shells while the chestnuts are warm; otherwise the shells stick easily.
4. Place the chestnuts together with the celery stalk and sugar in cold broth and let cook until completely soft.
5. Press through a sieve.
6. Mix the puree with butter and cream, heat it, and season with the spices.
7. Serve preferably in pastry shells.
