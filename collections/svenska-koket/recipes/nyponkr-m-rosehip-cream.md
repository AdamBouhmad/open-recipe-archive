## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter rosehips
- 1 1/2 to 2 liters water
- 1 to 1 1/2 cups crushed sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 3 to 4 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the rosehips, place in cold water, and allow to cook slowly for approximately 2 hours.
2. During cooking, vigorously whisk them occasionally to better extract the rosehip flavor.
3. Pass the rosehips through a sieve and return the juice to heat with sugar and cinnamon.
4. When it boils, skim carefully, and thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, then bring to a short boil.
5. Cool the cream in a jelly mold and serve with whipped cream.
