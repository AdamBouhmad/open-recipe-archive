## Ingredients

- 1½ kg boneless veal loin
- 1 hg lard
- 1 tbsp butter
- ½ tbsp salt
- 1 carrot
- ½ Portuguese onion
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 1 liter broth
- 3 hg of the meat for the forcemeat
- ½ kg salted ox tongue
- 1 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 large carrots for the vegetables
- 1 can French peas
- 1 can green beans
- ½ tbsp butter for sauce
- 1½ tbsp flour for sauce
- meat gravy
- ½ cup sour milk
- 1 egg
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 cauliflower head
- asparagus
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and trim carefully.
2. Lard it densely with long strips of lard.
3. Cut open the veal loin on one side and cut out about 3 hg of meat.
4. Make a forcemeat from the cut meat, crushed breadcrumbs, milk, egg, and seasonings, which should be fairly firm.
5. Cut the ox tongue (and truffle if using) into small cubes and mix in last.
6. Fill the opening in the loin with the forcemeat and sew it shut.
7. Brown the roast in butter and salt it.
8. Add the trimmed carrot cut into slices, the onion, parsley, and broth, then let the meat roast slowly for about 2 hours.
9. Baste the roast frequently with its sauce during roasting.
10. Carve it whole and serve, if desired, on a bed of rice.
11. Garnish with cooked carrots carved decoratively, peas, beans, cauliflower, and asparagus.
12. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
