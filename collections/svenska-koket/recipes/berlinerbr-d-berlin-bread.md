## Ingredients

- 150 gr. sweet almonds
- 120 gr. butter
- 180 gr. sugar
- 3 eggs
- 335 gr. flour
- 5 tsp. baking powder (6 gr.)

## Directions

1. Dry the almonds in a cloth and chop fairly coarsely.
2. Wash the butter and stir for 10 minutes, thereafter add the sugar, the eggs one at a time, the almonds and the sifted flour, well mixed with the baking powder.
3. Place the dough with 2 spoons on a well buttered baking sheet in approximately 2 1/2 cm. wide strips, which are baked a nice golden brown in a not too hot oven.
4. Cut the strips at once diagonally into 1 cm. wide breads, which are dried well in a warming oven.
