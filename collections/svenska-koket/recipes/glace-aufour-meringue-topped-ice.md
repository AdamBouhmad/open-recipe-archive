## Ingredients

- 1 silver platter
- jam or sugar paste
- 1 cm. thick oval slice of sponge cake
- 2 egg yolks
- 250 gr. icing sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 1 batch vanilla ice cream (prepared and frozen)

## Directions

1. Fasten the sponge cake slice to the silver platter with jam or sugar paste.
2. Place the platter on ice.
3. Prepare a meringue: whisk the egg yolks and two-thirds of the sugar for 20 minutes.
4. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and continue whisking for 15 minutes, then carefully add the remaining sugar and finally the whisked yolks.
5. Arrange the frozen vanilla ice cream with a heated silver spoon, placed high and oval on the sponge cake.
6. Spread some of the meringue smoothly over the ice, then pipe the remaining meringue through a fluted piping bag in attractive patterns.
7. Place the platter immediately in a pan with crushed ice and coarse salt and put into a very hot oven for about 2-3 minutes, so the meringue gets a golden colour.
8. Sprinkle with sugar and serve immediately with hot chocolate sauce, if desired.
