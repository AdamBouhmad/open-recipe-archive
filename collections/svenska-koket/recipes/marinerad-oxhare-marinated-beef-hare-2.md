## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1 hg lard
- 2 dcl white or red wine
- 1 dcl vinegar
- 1 dcl water
- 2 tbsp table oil
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 5 cloves
- 1 tsp coarsely crushed ginger
- 1 tbsp coarsely crushed juniper berries
- 1 red onion in slices
- 1–2 bay leaves
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tsp sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 cleaned, boned anchovy
- 4 dcl cream

## Directions

1. The beef hare, which should have hung 4–8 days depending on the season, is prepared and larded (see page 232), and placed in a pot.
2. For the marinade all ingredients are mixed well, after which it is poured over the meat, which is allowed to lie in it 2–3 days and should during that time often be turned.
3. The meat is taken up, dried well, tied so that it gets a nice shape, and cooked as in no. 474.
4. It is first moistened with 2 dcl of the strained marinade, sprinkled with sugar, covered with anchovy and thereafter cream is added gradually.
5. Served with browned potatoes and strained sauce (see no. 429), mixed with 1 tbsp chopped capers or 2 tbsp currant jelly.
