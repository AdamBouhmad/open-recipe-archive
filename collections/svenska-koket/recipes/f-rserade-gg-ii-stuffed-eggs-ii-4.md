## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 hectogram boneless beef
- 1/2 hectogram boneless veal
- 30 gr. fat
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 1 teaspoon salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped pickled onion
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 1 egg for coating
- 1 cup crushed rusks for coating
- 1/2 kilogram stock for poaching

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 5 minutes, cool in cold water and peel.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth, cut into pieces together with the fat and pass 5 times through a meat grinder. Pound with a wooden spoon until the filling becomes smooth and supple.
3. Beat the egg yolks well with the cream and work in the filling tablespoon by tablespoon, along with the rusks, seasonings and pickled onion. Taste and adjust seasoning.
4. Coat the peeled eggs with the meat filling, roll in crushed rusks, then in the beaten egg, and again in crushed rusks. Flatten the filling at both ends and let rest for 1 hour.
