## Ingredients

- 35 gr. butter
- 425 gr. sweet almonds, 4 bitter almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 425 gr. sugar
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 2 liters apples for filling
- 2 dcl. sugar (170 gr.) for filling
- 1 1/2 dcl. water for filling
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the pan
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir until it is white and frothy.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, pass them through a mill and pound in a mortar to a fine mass.
3. Whip the egg yolks, egg whites and sugar for 1/2 hour with a steel whisk.
4. Mix the egg mixture with the butter and almonds, and lastly add the flour.
5. The apples should be cooked the day before. Rinse them, dry them, peel them, cut them into fine wedges, remove the cores and cook with water and sugar until they fall apart, then strain and let them cool.
