## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked fish (1/2 kg. pike)
- 80 gr. butter
- 80 gr. flour
- 5 deciliters boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoon breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Grate the cooked fish finely like rice grains using a fork.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Pour into a bowl; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time, and stir the batter for 10 minutes.
4. Mix in the fish along with the seasonings and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
5. Season the mixture, pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
6. If using a metal form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour and turn out.
7. Serve the soufflé immediately after removing from the oven as a middle course with crayfish or lobster sauce, hollandaise or lemon sauce.
8. Instead of fish, calf's sweetbread, poultry or game can be used.
