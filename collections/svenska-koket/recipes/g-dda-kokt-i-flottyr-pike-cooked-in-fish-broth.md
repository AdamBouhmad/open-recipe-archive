## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pike
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp vinegar
- Batter: 75 gr flour
- 1 part lukewarm water
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tbsp melted butter
- 1/2 kg fish broth for cooking

## Directions

1. The pike is cleaned, rinsed and dried well.
2. The skin is removed and the fish is cut into filets.
3. The filets are then cut into appropriate sized pieces and all bones are removed.
4. The filets are rubbed with salt and vinegar and left for a while.
5. The fish is dipped in batter and cooked in fish broth.
6. The batter is prepared as follows: Fine, well-dried wheat flour is sifted with salt in a bowl, and in the middle of the flour water, egg yolks and lastly butter are stirred in.
7. The batter is stirred until it is glossy and thick enough to coat the spoon.
8. Just before use, egg whites are beaten to peaks and folded in well.
