## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg lean, slightly salted pork
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 cups water
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into even slices crosswise and remove the rind.
2. Mix the flour and pepper on a plate, turn the pork slices in it and brown quickly in a hot pan.
3. Place them in a small pot, add boiling water and let them cook about 1 hour.
4. Thin the sauce with milk and thicken if needed.
