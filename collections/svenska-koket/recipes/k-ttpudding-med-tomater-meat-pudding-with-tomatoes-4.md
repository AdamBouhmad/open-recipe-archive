## Ingredients

- meat forcemeat as in recipe no. 631 or no. 632
- 6 large tomatoes
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare the forcemeat according to the aforementioned recipe.
2. Clean, rinse and slice the tomatoes, sprinkle the slices with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Butter a pudding mold and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
4. Layer the meat forcemeat and tomatoes alternately in the mold; the first and last layers should be forcemeat.
5. Cook or bake the pudding as in recipe no. 638 and serve with pan gravy (see recipe no. 429) or clarified butter and potatoes.
