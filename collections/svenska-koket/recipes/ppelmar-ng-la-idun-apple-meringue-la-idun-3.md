## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 120 g. sugar
- 60 g. wheat flour
- 4 bitter almonds
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold (10 g)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 2 hg. sugar for compote
- 4 dcl. water
- juice and peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1 1/2 liters apples
- 3 egg whites
- 105 g. powdered sugar
- 10 g. pistachio almond (about 22 pieces)
- 40 g. sweet almond (about 40 pieces)

## Directions

1. Butter a mold or baking pan with the same width as the platter on which the meringue will be served, and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar for 1/2 hour, then add the flour and the blanched and grated almond.
3. Pour the mixture into the buttered mold and bake in regular oven heat for about 20 minutes.
4. Turn the cake out onto a cooling rack, let it cool, and then place it on a round platter, preferably of silver.
