## Ingredients

- 1.5-2 kg lamb from young sheep
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 liter meat stock or bouillon

## Directions

1. The young lamb has white, fine-textured meat and requires short cooking time.
2. Use the loins and half kidney loin together to obtain sufficient weight.
3. Treat, roast and serve as in recipe 531 but should not be larded.
4. Prepare the saddle as lamb saddle (see recipe 535).
