## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb breast
- water for cooking
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp milk
- 2 tbsp crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. If the lamb breast is too salty, soak it in water the day before. Break it into appropriately sized pieces that hold together, place in cold water (short broth), bring to a boil, skim, and simmer with a tightly closed lid until the meat is tender, then remove it. Whisk the egg with the milk, brush the breast with this mixture and sprinkle with the finely crushed hardtack. Place it in a baking pan whose bottom is covered with the broth, and put it in the oven to get a beautiful light golden color.
2. Serve the breast laid on potato purée and garnished with parsley. Can also be served advantageously with browned root vegetables.
