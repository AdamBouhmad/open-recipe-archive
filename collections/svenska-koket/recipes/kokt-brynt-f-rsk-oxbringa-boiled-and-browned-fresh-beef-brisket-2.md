## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg beef brisket (forebrisket)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- water
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 piece celery
- 1 large carrot

## Directions

1. Wash the brisket with a cloth dipped in hot water, rub in the salt, and let it rest until the next day
2. Break it well and place in enough boiling water to cover the meat well
3. When the water comes to a boil, remove all floating scum, add spices and vegetables, and let the brisket simmer slowly until completely tender (2-3 hours)
4. Remove and place in a roasting pan whose bottom is covered with the broth, and place in the oven to brown
5. Cut the brisket into neat pieces and garnish with sliced carrot
6. Strain the broth and skim it
