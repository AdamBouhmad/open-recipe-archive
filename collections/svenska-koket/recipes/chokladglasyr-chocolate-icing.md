## Ingredients

- 1/2 hg unsweetened chocolate
- 1 hg powdered sugar
- 1 part water

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water in a pot and heat.
2. Melt the chocolate in another pot in a water bath, and add the hot sugar solution little by little while stirring constantly with a wooden spoon.
3. Continue stirring until the icing is smooth and shiny and of proper thickness.
4. Coat the cake with this icing, and this should be done quickly, because the icing soon hardens.
