## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef (flank)
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 6 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into slices across the grain.
2. Beat the slices lightly on both sides, sprinkle with pepper and salt, and turn them in the crushed rusks.
3. Brown the meat slices in butter, then place in a pot and add the boiling water.
4. Let them simmer slowly for approximately 1 1/2 hours.
5. Serve the roulade with its sauce.
