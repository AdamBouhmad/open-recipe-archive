## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 200 gr. yellow flour
- 1/2 tsk. ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/8 tsk. ground cardamom
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsk. ammonium carbonate
- 425 gr. wheat flour
- water for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 1/4 kkp pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with sugar and the ground spices for about 20 minutes.
2. Add the eggs along with 1/3 of the flour.
3. Mix the rest of the flour with the ammonium carbonate and work into the dough last.
4. Roll out and cut small cakes with a measure.
5. Brush with water and sprinkle with chopped almond and sugar.
6. Bake golden-brown in good oven heat.
