## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg mutton neck
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 celery root
- 4 potatoes
- 1 leek
- 1 white or red onion
- 4 tablespoons butter or lard
- 2 tablespoons small oat groats
- a little marjoram
- 3 liters water
- 15 gr. salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the mutton neck quickly, dry it well and cut it into pieces.
2. Prepare the root vegetables and onion in the usual way, cut into slices and brown well in the butter or lard together with the meat, groats and a little marjoram.
3. Place it in cold water with the salt.
4. Let the soup come to a boil, skim well and then cook slowly with a tight lid for 3-4 hours.
5. Remove the meat, separate it from the bones and cut into small pieces.
6. Strain the soup, skim and measure it — it should be 2 liters — let it come to a boil with the meat, season with the spices and serve very hot.
