## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. strösocker (128 gr.)
- 2 dcl. vatten
- 1 lit. blåbär

## Directions

1. Make a thin, clear syrup from the water and sugar.
2. Clean the blueberries, rinse in a sieve and drain well, then place in the sugar syrup.
3. Cook for 4-5 minutes.
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and place in a warm compote bowl.
5. Cook the syrup until it is syrupy and pour immediately over the berries.
6. Serve cold with whipped cream.
