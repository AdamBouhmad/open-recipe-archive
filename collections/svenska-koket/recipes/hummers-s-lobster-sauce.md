## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter fish stock
- 1 can of lobster of 1/4 liter
- salt
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are cooked together
2. Fish stock is added
3. The sauce is allowed to boil for 10 minutes
4. The lobster is cut into smaller pieces and placed in the sauce
5. The sauce is finished with the egg yolks and seasoned
6. After the egg yolks are stirred in, the sauce must not boil further
