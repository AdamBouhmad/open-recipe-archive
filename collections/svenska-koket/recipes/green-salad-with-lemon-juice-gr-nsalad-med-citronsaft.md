## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads of lettuce
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 g.)

## Directions

1. Separate the lettuce heads, remove the stems, rinse the leaves well and shake them in a cloth to dry them. Cut the largest leaves into pieces, and place the salad in a crystal bowl.
2. Press out the lemon juice, mix it with the sugar and pour it over the salad.
3. Serve with roasted meat.
