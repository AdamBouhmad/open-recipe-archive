## Ingredients

- 5 kilograms moose roast
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 liters water
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 hectograms lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2–2.5 liters broth or water
- 2.5 tablespoons butter for sauce
- 5 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The meat should be chosen from young animals.
2. Rub it well with the spices and let it lie 2–3 days in the water, diluted with the vinegar.
3. Remove it and dry well with a cloth.
4. Lard the roast with the lard cut into fine strips, brown it in the butter, season further if desired, and add the boiling broth or water.
5. Let the roast roast slowly, which takes approximately 3–4 hours, basting it often.
6. Strain and skim the meat drippings, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
