## Ingredients

- 360 gr. wheat flour
- 1 1/2 tsk. baking soda (4 1/2 gr.)
- 2 eggs
- 325 gr. brown sugar
- 1 hg. melted butter
- 3 dcl. thick sour cream
- 80 gr. coarsely chopped sweet almond
- 1 tsk. crushed cloves
- 1 tsk. ground cinnamon
- 1 tsk. ground ginger
- 1 tsk. ground cardamom
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 3 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and mix well with the baking soda.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar for 15 minutes, then add the melted but cooled butter, the cream beaten to stiff peaks, the almond and spices.
3. Stir in the flour last and pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-breadcrumbed large ring or bread pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 45 minutes.
