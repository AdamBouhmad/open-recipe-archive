## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-öre white bread
- 2 tbsp butter
- 30 sweet almonds
- 6-8 bitter almonds
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp powdered sugar
- 1/2 liter cream milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups powdered sugar for the mold
- 5-6 tbsp boiling water

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a frying pan and dilute it with the boiling water, then line a mold with this.
2. Cut the bread into slices and spread butter on both sides.
3. Layer the slices in the mold alternately with the blanched and chopped almonds and the grated lemon peel.
4. Whisk the eggs and milk together, add the sugar, and pour this custard over the bread.
5. Let the mold stand for approximately 1 hour or until the bread is thoroughly soaked, then place it in the oven to bake.
6. When it has cooled and acquired a nice light yellow color, remove it and turn it out onto a dish or bowl. Serve cold.
