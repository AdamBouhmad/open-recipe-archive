## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rhubarb
- 125 g crushed sugar
- 1/2 part water
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 5 tablespoons caster sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb, cut it into pieces and cook it quickly until soft in the water together with the crushed sugar.
2. Stir the egg yolks with the cream, caster sugar and lemon zest until the mixture is foamy.
3. Beat the whites until stiff peaks form and fold them in gently.
4. Pour half of the mixture into a buttered, heat-resistant mold.
5. Drain the cooked and cooled rhubarb through a colander and place it in the mold.
6. Pour the remaining batter over it.
7. Bake the soufflé in moderate oven heat for about 1 hour.
8. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
