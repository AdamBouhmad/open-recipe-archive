## Ingredients

- 1 pike-perch of 1 1/2 kg.
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Scale the pike-perch, gut it and cut off the fins.
2. Keep the head on, but remove the gills.
3. Rinse thoroughly, dry and rub with salt; let rest 1 hour, then dry again.
4. Place on a sieve in a fish kettle with the back up and sides spread open and cook in a little water as in recipe no. 327.
5. Arrange immediately when done on a plate with sieve or on a broken napkin, garnish with parsley and serve with melted butter and chopped eggs, hollandaise, crayfish or oyster sauce and boiled potatoes.
