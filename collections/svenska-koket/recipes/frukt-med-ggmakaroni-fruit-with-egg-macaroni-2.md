## Ingredients

- 150 grams small fine egg macaroni
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3/4 liter cooked milk
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- canned fruit: apples, pears and plums

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into even pieces, cook them soft in water and drain them on a sieve.
2. In a pot, melt butter and flour together, gradually add the milk, and let the mixture come to a good boil.
3. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
4. Add lemon peel and sugar to taste.
5. Mix the well-drained macaroni into the mixture and let it simmer over heat until it thickens.
6. Remove and let it cool.
7. Arrange the fruit high on a dish and place the cooled macaroni around it.
