## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg perch
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 2 msk wheat flour
- 1 msk coarse rye flour
- 4 msk butter (80 gr.)
- 3 msk white wine
- 1 1/2 dcl fish stock
- 1 1/2 dcl thick cream
- 2 tsk lemon juice
- 1/2 dcl white wine
- 1 tsk caramel coloring
- 1 msk finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the perch as in the previous recipe.
2. Cook fish stock from the head, bones and skin, to be used for the sauce.
3. Dip fillets in the beaten egg and turn in the flour, which has been well mixed beforehand.
4. Fry in butter as in recipe no. 330.
5. When fillets are fried on both sides, pour 1 tsp white wine over each one, then place on a warm plate.
6. Whisk the frying pan with the fish stock, cream, lemon juice, wine and caramel coloring.
7. Let the sauce come to a boil, add the parsley and pour the sauce over the fish on the plate.
8. Garnish the dish with fluted lemon slices and parsley.
