## Ingredients

- 1 liter good milk
- 3 tbsp. mondamin flour (45 gr.)
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 3-4 egg yolks
- 3 tbsp. granulated sugar (45 gr.)

## Directions

1. Stir the mondamin flour into a cup of the milk. Heat the remaining milk with the finely grated lemon zest or vanilla sugar, add the flour mixture and let the cream boil while stirring for 5 minutes. Beat the egg yolks well with the granulated sugar and add them, then let the cream simmer and taste for seasoning. Pour into a compote bowl, let it cool, garnish with jam and serve with milk or fruit sauce. Maisenacream is prepared in the same way.
