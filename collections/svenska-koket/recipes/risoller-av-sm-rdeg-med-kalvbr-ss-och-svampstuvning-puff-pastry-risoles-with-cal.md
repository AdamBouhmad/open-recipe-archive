## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scant)
- 2 hg. butter
- 5 gr. dried morels
- 150 gr. calf thymus
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1 dcl. thymus broth
- 1 dcl. asparagus broth
- 1 dcl. cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 sheets gelatin
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed bread crumbs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff pastry as in no. 33, place in a napkin and let rest a few hours.
2. Prepare the stew as in no. 47. When it has simmered with the egg yolk, immediately add the gelatin, rinsed in cold water and dissolved in 1 tbsp. hot water.
3. Season the stew with the spices, spread thinly on a flat dish and let cool.
4. Roll out the puff pastry to 2 mm. thickness and cut with a large round measure (risole mold) into cakes; place 1 dessert spoon of stew in the center of each.
5. Brush the edges with egg white and fold them together so the cake takes the form of a half moon, then crimp the folded edge.
