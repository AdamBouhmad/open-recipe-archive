## Ingredients

- 120 gr. fillet of sole or whiting
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. boiling water
- 2 tbsp. cold béchamel sauce
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- salt, white pepper
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the fillets, wrap in cloth, rub with salt and cut into small cubes.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the fish cubes, add water, let them simmer slowly with lid 8-10 minutes, then pour off and let cool.
3. Pound the fish in a mortar to a fine mass, add the sauce and pound again.
4. Prepare the royale as in the preceding recipe. Bake and serve as in recipe no. 306.
