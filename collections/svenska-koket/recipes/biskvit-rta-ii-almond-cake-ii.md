## Ingredients

- 320 gr. sweet almond
- 320 gr. powdered sugar
- 7 egg whites
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan
- 3 candied orange peels for garnish
- fruit and jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almond, dry it in the oven, grind it and mix with the sugar.
2. Carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a well-buttered and breadcrumb-strewn baking pan and bake the cake in gentle oven heat for about 1 hour.
4. Turn the cake out when it has cooled and garnish, when it is cold, with fruit and jelly and, if desired, with liqueur.
5. Serve with vanilla sauce.
