## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters potatoes
- 40 g butter
- 3 eggs
- 1-2 deciliters thick cream
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the dish
- 2 tablespoons fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes thinly, cook them and press through a puree press.
2. Stir the cold butter into the mash with a fork along with the beaten egg yolks and cream.
3. Add the seasonings and lastly fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Place the mixture in a buttered and bread crumb-lined ovenproof dish and sprinkle with fine bread crumbs.
5. Bake the soufflé in a good oven heat until it has a beautiful golden brown color, or about 40 minutes.
6. Serve immediately after removing from the oven as a sandwich course or with roasted meat in general.
