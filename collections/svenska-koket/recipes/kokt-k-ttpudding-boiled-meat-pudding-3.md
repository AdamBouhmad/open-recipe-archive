## Ingredients

- 400 g boneless, cooked or fried meat
- 6 dcl boiling milk
- 1 1/2 dcl finely crushed rusks
- 3–4 eggs
- 100 g butter (melted)
- 1 msk salt (15 g)
- 1/2 tsk white pepper
- 1 msk finely chopped salt cucumber
- 1/2 msk finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 10 g butter for the onion
- 1/2 msk butter for the mold (10 g)
- 2 msk crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very finely (do not grind) and mix with the milk, rusks, and beaten egg yolks; let the mixture stand for 1 hour to swell; if it is then too stiff, add a little more milk.
2. Add the butter, seasonings, cucumber, and onion (fried in butter), and taste the mixture carefully.
3. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn baking pan and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours.
5. Turn out onto a serving dish and garnish with slices of tomato or cucumber and parsley.
6. Serve with meat gravy, meat sauce, or melted butter. If desired, the cucumber and onion may be omitted.
