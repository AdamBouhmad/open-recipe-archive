## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2-3 cups cabbage broth
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and place it in water mixed with vinegar for about 1/2 hour.
2. Place it in salted and boiling water (just over 1 liter) and let it simmer slowly until it feels tender.
3. Remove it and let it drain.
4. Fry the butter and flour together, gradually add the cabbage broth and milk, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes.
5. Cut the cauliflower into wedges and add it, and season the stewing.
6. Arrange the stewing on a serving dish and serve.
