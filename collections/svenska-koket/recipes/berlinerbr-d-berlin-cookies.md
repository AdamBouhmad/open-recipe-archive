## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 425 g caster sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 425 g wheat flour
- 200 g coarsely cut almond

## Directions

1. Cream the eggs and sugar well; sift the flour with the baking powder and stir into the egg mixture.
2. Last, add the unpeeled and coarsely cut almond.
3. On buttered baking sheets, place approximately 3 cm wide strips from the batter, which are baked a nice golden brown in moderate oven heat.
4. While still quite hot, cut the strips into 1 cm wide pieces at an angle.
