## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 200 gr. eggs (4-5 pieces)
- 200 gr. wheat flour
- 200 gr. currants
- ½ msk. washed butter for the pan
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a foam.
2. Add egg yolks and flour alternately.
3. Rinse the currants in lukewarm water, dry well in a cloth and clean them, then rub them with some of the flour and mix into the batter.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mass into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pan and bake in good oven heat for about 1 hour.
