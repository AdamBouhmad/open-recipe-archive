## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 45 gr. sweet almond
- 45 gr. granulated sugar
- 200 gr. wheat flour
- raspberry jam for filling
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortbread dough in the usual way.
2. Roll out two-thirds of the dough into a thin cake and place on a well-cleaned baking sheet.
3. Spread raspberry jam over the cake.
4. Pipe the remaining dough in narrow strips and lay them in a lattice pattern over the jam.
5. Brush the cake with egg and bake in a hot oven.
6. Cut into diagonal squares.
