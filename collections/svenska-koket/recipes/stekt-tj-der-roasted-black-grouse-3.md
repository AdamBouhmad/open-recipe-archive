## Ingredients

- 1 black grouse
- 1 hg. lard
- 3—4 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. bouillon
- 5 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The female black grouse has the finest meat and is calculated for 6 persons. A young male black grouse also has delicious meat and serves 8—10 persons.
2. If the bird is frozen, it should be thawed in a warm kitchen, cleaned (see page 299) and allowed to lie in lukewarm milk overnight.
3. Dry it thoroughly thereafter, lard with fine lard strips and truss.
4. Roast the bird in a pot as a capercaillie, but requires 2—3 hours of roasting, depending on whether it is a female or male.
5. Carve, garnish (see page 300) and serve with poured sauce (see no. 429), mixed with thick cream and cold butter and, if desired, with 1 tbsp. red currant jelly.
