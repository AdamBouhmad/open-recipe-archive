## Ingredients

- 2 heads celeriac
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 2 hectograms beef
- 1/2 hectogram veal
- 1/2 hectogram lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 chopped pickled onions or 1/2 tablespoon chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 egg
- 2 deciliters cream
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams) for frying
- 3 deciliters broth

## Directions

1. Peel the celeriac, rinse well, place in boiling salted water and let cook about 1 hour, or until almost tender, then remove and let cool.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and grind together with the lard 3 times through a meat grinder.
3. Mix in the flour, egg, seasonings and cream, little by little, and work the forcemeat about 1/2 hour.
4. Stir in the onion and taste the forcemeat.
5. Cut a lid from the celeriac and hollow it from above. Fill with the forcemeat, place the lid on top and bind the celeriac with twine, first dipped in hot water.
6. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the celeriac and brown nicely, thin with broth and then let braise slowly with the lid tightly closed about 1 1/2 hours.
7. Place on a warm platter, cut off the twine and pour the sauce over. Serve at the smörgåsbord.
