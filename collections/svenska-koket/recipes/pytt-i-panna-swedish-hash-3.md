## Ingredients

- 2-3 red onions
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- leftovers of cooked cured ham or beef brisket and beef or veal steak (approximately 1/2 liter finely cut meat)
- 1/2 liter cooked, finely cut potato
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry well in the butter in a frying pan.
2. Add the meat and potato along with the seasonings and stir the mixture carefully so that it does not break apart and allow it to fry until it has taken on a lovely light brown color.
3. Serve with beets as a breakfast dish.
