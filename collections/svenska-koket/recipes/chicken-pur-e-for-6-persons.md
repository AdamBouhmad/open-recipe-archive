## Ingredients

- 1 old hen
- 2 liters water
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3 white peppercorns
- 1 clove
- 1 1/2 liters light stock
- 65 gr rice grains
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr)
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the hen in the usual way and let it soak in plenty of water for 12 hours.
2. Dry it, cut into 4 pieces, place in cold water with salt added, skim well and let it cook with the spices and 1 liter of the stock until the meat is tender, about 4 hours.
3. Rinse the rice grains in hot water and let them cook soft in the remaining stock.
4. When the meat is tender, separate it from the bones, which should boil again in the stock until it makes 2 liters.
5. Cut the meat into pieces, pass 4 times through a meat grinder and pound in a mortar together with 2 tbsp of the butter and the cooked rice grains.
6. Pass the forcemeat through a fine sieve.
7. Strain and skim the stock.
8. Brown the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, thin the stock little by little, add the forcemeat and let the purée cook while stirring for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
9. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl and pour in the purée while whisking vigorously.
10. Serve with cheese or butter pastry sticks.
