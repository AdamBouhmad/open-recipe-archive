## Ingredients

- 150 grams butter
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 100 grams sweet almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- 250 grams wheat flour
- 2 1/2 cups apple sauce
- granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
- 1 small tablespoon wheat flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 2 1/2 parts cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough in the usual way
2. For the egg custard, whip all ingredients together in a saucepan and, with vigorous whisking, simmer over the fire until the mixture thickens
3. Line small pleated forms with butter dough and set in the oven to half-bake
4. Fill the forms with apple sauce and place some of the custard on top, then set them back in the oven to bake through
5. Turn out while still warm
