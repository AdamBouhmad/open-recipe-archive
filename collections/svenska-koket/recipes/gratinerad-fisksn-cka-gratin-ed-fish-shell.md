## Ingredients

- leftovers of cooked fish, approximately 3 deciliters
- 1 can of lobster (1/4 liter)
- 6-8 mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter for shells (30 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Free the fish from all bones and cut into fine cubes
2. Cut the lobster into small pieces and the mushrooms into thin slices
3. Fry the mushroom in the butter until clear, then squeeze it out of the butter with a spoon
4. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes
5. Add the milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes and season with spices
6. Butter six shells well
7. Place fish, lobster and mushroom in the shells
