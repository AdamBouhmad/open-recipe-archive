## Ingredients

- 9 bananas (about 1 kg.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (50 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas and split them lengthwise.
2. Brown the butter lightly in a frying pan together with the sugar, add the bananas and fry quickly until beautifully golden brown on both sides.
3. Arrange them on a hot silver platter and serve hot.
