## Ingredients

- 1/2 tsp. finely minced red onion
- 50 gr. lean veal (in fine cubes)
- 60 gr. butter
- 40 gr. dry flour
- 7 dcl. boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- a little grated mesost (optional)

## Directions

1. Sauté the onion and meat in the butter until clear, then add the flour and sauté with the butter for 2 minutes.
2. Add the milk, season and let the sauce simmer while stirring for 15 minutes, then strain through cloth, taste and heat in a bain-marie.
3. Serve with boiled chicken, veal or lamb and may, if desired, be enriched with a couple of tbsp. minced mushrooms sautéed in butter.
4. Used for gratin and for royal garnish on fish and poultry, etc.
