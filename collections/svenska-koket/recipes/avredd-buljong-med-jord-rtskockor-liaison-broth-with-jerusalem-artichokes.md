## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- broth from the Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 liter secondary broth
- 2—3 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the Jerusalem artichokes well and scrape them, preferably under running water.
2. Shape them round and place them in vinegar-mixed water (1 tablespoon vinegar, 1 liter water) so they do not become dark.
3. After 1 hour, place them in boiling, salted water and let them cook until tender, then place them in the soup bowl; reserve the broth, which should be about 1/2 liter.
4. Cook the soup as in the previous recipe, pour it over the Jerusalem artichokes and serve with cheese croutons (see no. 312).
