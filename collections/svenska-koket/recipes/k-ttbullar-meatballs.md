## Ingredients

- 300g boneless beef
- 200g boneless veal
- 100g fat pork
- 2-3 cups milk
- 1-2 eggs
- 1/2 cup crushed rusks
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped Portuguese onion
- 4 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with the fat into cubes and pass it three times through a meat grinder.
2. Whip up the eggs and milk, add the crushed rusks and let them soak.
3. Peel the onion, chop it finely and fry it in 1/2 tablespoon of the butter.
4. Mix the soaked rusks and spices into the forcemeat.
5. Work the forcemeat until it becomes cohesive like a dough.
6. Finally add the fried onion.
7. Brown the butter in a frying pan, shape the meatballs with spoons dipped in lukewarm water, and brown them in the hot butter.
8. Add 1 cup of boiling water, cover with the lid, and let the meatballs cook through for 1.5 hours.
