## Ingredients

- 1 can of pineapple (about 750 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- 75 gr. sugar
- 50 gr. wheat flour
- 15 gr. mondamin or cornstarch
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 50 gr. butter
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 1 1/2 dcl. pineapple juice
- 1 1/2 dcl. French wine
- 2 tbsp. sugar for sauce (30 gr.)
- 1 tsp. mondamin flour (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Half of the pineapple is sufficient for this pudding. The remainder can be used for cold pudding or mixed into compote.
2. Cut the pineapple into cubes, which are wrapped in a cloth.
3. Whisk the egg yolks well with the sugar in a saucepan, add the flour and cream, and let the cream simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
4. Remove the saucepan from heat, add the butter in small flakes and whisk the cream until it is cold.
5. Then stir in the drained pineapple and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie mold and bake in regular oven heat for about 40 minutes.
7. Serve the pudding immediately after removing it from the oven.
8. For the sauce: bring the pineapple juice to a boil with the wine and sugar, and thicken with the mondamin, dissolved in 1 tbsp. cold water, after which let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
9. Taste and serve warm.
