## Ingredients

- 100 gr. butter
- 100 gr. potato flour
- 1 whole egg
- 100 gr. sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 drops almond essence
- 1 tbsp. butter for the molds
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The butter is melted, allowed to cool and is stirred with the potato flour until it becomes white and fluffy.
2. The eggs and sugar are stirred until the mixture becomes white, and the two batters are then mixed well together.
3. Small pastry molds are greased with melted butter that has been allowed to cool and stirred to a foam.
4. They are sprinkled with crushed rusks, filled halfway with the batter and placed in a not too hot oven.
5. When the pastries are baked, they are turned out immediately.
