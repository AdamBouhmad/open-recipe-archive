## Ingredients

- Leftovers of sponge cake, cut into small cubes (approximately 5 dcl.)
- 1 dcl. currants (60 gr.)
- 30 gr. candied fruit
- 4 eggs
- 50 gr. sugar
- 4 dcl. good milk
- 1 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. The sponge cake, which should be cut into small cubes, is placed in a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn timbale mold alternately with the well-rinsed currants and the chopped candied fruit.
2. The eggs are whisked well with the sugar, the milk is added and with this the mold is filled.
3. The pudding is baked in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour, turned out and served very hot with warm vanilla sauce (see no. 454).
