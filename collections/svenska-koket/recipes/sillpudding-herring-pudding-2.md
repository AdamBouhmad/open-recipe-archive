## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 1 lit. cooked, cold potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 2 tsk. flour (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. milk
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1-2 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 msk. butter for mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for mold

## Directions

1. Skin the herrings, clean them, rinse well, scale, remove the backbone and lay the fillets in water the evening before they are to be used.
2. Wrap them in a fish towel or white paper, free from all small bones and cut into strips or 3 cm. wide pieces across.
3. Prepare the pudding thereafter and serve as in recipe no. 399.
