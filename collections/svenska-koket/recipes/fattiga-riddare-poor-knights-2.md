## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 8 dcl. milk approximately
- 60 gr. flour
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 18 slices wheat bread (not freshly baked)
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)

## Directions

1. Make a pancake batter from the eggs, 4 dcl. of the milk, flour and sugar as in recipe no. 763.
2. Dip the bread slices in the remaining milk, then in the batter.
3. Fry immediately until beautifully golden brown in the butter.
4. Arrange on a warm dish and serve with jam.
