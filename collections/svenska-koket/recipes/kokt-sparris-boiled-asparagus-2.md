## Ingredients

- 50 fresh asparagus spears
- 2 liters water
- 30 grams salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus carefully with a sharp knife toward the tip without touching it; remove only the outermost skin
2. Rinse the asparagus carefully in cold water
3. Tie the asparagus into five bundles with the tips facing the same direction
4. Boil the water with the salt and skim well
5. Add the asparagus and cook for 15-20 minutes, depending on the nature of the asparagus
6. Remove immediately and let drain on a sieve or cloth
7. Place the asparagus on a platter with a folded napkin, cut the strings and remove them
8. Serve very hot with melted butter arranged as small rolls in a sauce boat, or with a sauce such as true hollandaise, mousseline or béarnaise sauce
