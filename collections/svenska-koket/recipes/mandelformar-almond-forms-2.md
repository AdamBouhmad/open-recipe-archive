## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 210 gr. powdered sugar
- 75 gr. sweet almond
- 10 gr. bitter almond
- 2 egg yolks
- 210 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with the sugar until it is white and fluffy.
2. Add the blanched and ground almond as well as the flour and work the dough until it becomes even and smooth.
3. Let it then stand in a cold place for about 2 hours.
4. Roll the dough out into a thin rope, which is cut into pieces, with which small fancy pastry forms are lined on the inside.
5. Place the forms on a baking sheet.
