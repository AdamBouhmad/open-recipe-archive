## Ingredients

- 0.25 kg lamb breast or saddle
- 1 small cabbage head
- 1.5 tablespoons salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2.5 liters cold water

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into pieces.
2. Brown it lightly together with the coarsely cut cabbage.
3. Sprinkle flour over, add salt and pepper, and mix everything well.
4. Add the cold water and let the soup cook slowly for 2.5-3 hours.
5. Skim off all the fat carefully, season and serve.
