## Ingredients

- 40 grams butter
- 60 grams flour
- 1 part water
- 40 grams grated cheese
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix butter and flour together, add water with frequent stirring, and let the batter cook until it comes away from the pan.
2. Let the mass cool, then mix in the eggs one at a time along with the cheese.
3. Form small round balls from the batter; these are cooked light brown in fat and laid on gray paper to drain.
