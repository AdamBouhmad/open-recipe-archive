## Ingredients

- 2-3 celery roots
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 50-75 gr. truffle
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- a few grains paprika
- 3-4 msk. fine cooking oil
- 1 1/2-2 msk. vinegar or wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Rinse the celery roots, brush them, scrape them, rinse again, place them in cold water with salt and let them cook until soft, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Place them on a cloth to cool and cut with a vegetable knife into oblong pieces or strips.
3. Cut the truffle into fine strips or cubes and mix with the celery strips in a salad bowl.
4. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 448 and pour it over the salad.
5. Serve with roasted meat.
