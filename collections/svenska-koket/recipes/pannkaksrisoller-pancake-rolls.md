## Ingredients

- For the thin pancakes: 3 eggs, 600 ml milk, 90-100 g flour, 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- For the pan: 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- For the stew: 300 g boneless roasted meat, 1 tin of mushrooms (250 ml), 2 tbsp butter (40 g), 4 tbsp flour (40 g), 400 ml stock, 1 egg yolk, 100 ml thick cream, salt, white pepper
- For frying: 200 ml finely crushed breadcrumbs, 3 tbsp butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. For the pancake batter, whisk the ingredients well with a wire whisk, then let the batter rest for 1 hour to rise.
2. Cut the meat into fine cubes.
3. Cut the mushrooms likewise into cubes and fry in butter until clear, then press them up with a spoon; fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Whisk the egg yolk with the cream and add to the sauce, then let it simmer.
5. Add the meat and mushrooms and season the stew with spices.
6. Heat a pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath and brush the pan with it.
7. Make thin pancakes from the batter, browning them on one side only.
8. Arrange them on a cutting board and distribute the meat mixture on them, roll them up and fry in breadcrumbs and butter until golden.
