## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple
- 1 liter water
- 200 gr. sugar
- citric acid
- 25 gr. gelatin
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Heat the pineapple syrup, water, sugar and citric acid to taste in a saucepan.
2. Add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little warm water, whisk in the egg white, then bring everything to a brief boil.
3. Strain the jelly through a cloth wrung out in hot water.
4. Pour a couple of coffee cups of the clear jelly onto the bottom of a ring form rinsed with water and set out to set.
5. Fill the form with pineapple cut into beautiful slices, and finally pour in the rest of the jelly.
6. Set it out to set again, then turn the jelly out.
