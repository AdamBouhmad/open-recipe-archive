## Ingredients

- 2 kg. berries
- 2 kg. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them quickly in a sieve and let them drain well.
2. Layer them alternately with the sugar in an enameled preserve pot, cover, and let stand for one day, by which time the sugar should be nearly dissolved in the fruit juice.
3. Let the pot stand about 10 minutes beside the heat and then move it over gentle heat.
4. When the preserve has come to a boil, lift the pot from the heat and shake it carefully so that the scum can be removed, then return the pot to the heat and let the preserve simmer gently until it is clear (about 20 minutes).
5. During this time shake the pot often and skim the preserve carefully.
6. Immediately ladle the preserve into warm jars rinsed with cognac, which should stand in a cool and airy room until the preserve is cold, then cover with parchment paper dipped in cognac.
7. Bind the jars with parchment paper as previously described.
