## Ingredients

- 212 gr. butter
- 6 eggs
- 212 gr. powdered sugar
- 212 gr. potato flour
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 1-2 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir until white and foamy.
2. Stir in alternately 1 egg yolk, 1 tbsp. sugar and 1 tbsp. flour, until all ingredients are worked in.
3. Season the batter with finely grated lemon zest or vanilla sugar and stir for about 20 minutes more.
4. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, and pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn ring mold and bake for about 45 minutes in a not too hot oven.
