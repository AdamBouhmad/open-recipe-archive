## Ingredients

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tsp lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with water, flavored with a little lemon juice, and stir the mixture until the sugar has melted.
2. With a knife spread this icing over the cake.
3. Only after the icing has dried, garnish the cake.
4. If you want white icing, use sifted sugar instead of powdered sugar.
