## Ingredients

- Leftovers of veal roast or poultry (1/2 litre finely chopped meat)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 decilitre mushroom paste
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 litre)
- 1-2 truffles
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 3 decilitres thin cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut or chop the veal or poultry finely.
2. Chop the mushrooms and, if desired, 1-2 truffles finely.
3. Melt the butter, add the mushrooms and fry until the butter is clear. Remove the mushrooms with a spoon.
4. Fry the flour with the butter 2 minutes, add the mushroom paste and cream and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
5. Mix the meat and mushrooms into the sauce and bring to a boil, then season carefully with the spices and divide into 6 small heat-proof porcelain pans or shells.
6. Fry the eggs in a frying pan, shape with a glass according to the size of the pan and place on top of the stew.
7. Serve as a breakfast dish.
