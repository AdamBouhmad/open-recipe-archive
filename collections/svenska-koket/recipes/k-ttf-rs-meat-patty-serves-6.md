## Ingredients

- 300 g. beef without bone
- 200 g. veal without bone
- 100 g. salted pork fat
- 2 eggs
- 3-4 tablespoons cream milk
- 0.5 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons chopped Portuguese onion
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- For breading: 1 egg (or 1 egg white), 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- For frying: 1.5 tablespoons butter, 0.5 liter boiling stock or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with the fat into cubes and pass through the meat grinder three times
2. Pound it thereafter in a mortar until it becomes fine and smooth
3. Whisk together the eggs and milk, add the crushed breadcrumbs and let them swell
4. Peel and finely chop the onion and fry it in 0.5 tablespoon butter
5. Mix the soaked breadcrumbs together with the seasonings into the forcemeat; work it until it becomes cohesive like dough
6. Grease a long pan with cold butter, place the forcemeat in it and shape it like an oblong loaf
7. Brush it with egg (or egg white) and sprinkle with finely crushed breadcrumbs
8. Place the forcemeat in the oven, brown it and then add a little boiling stock or water, baste it around every ten minutes and let it cook about 1 hour
9. Serve the sauce either as gravy or reduced according to general rules for sauces
