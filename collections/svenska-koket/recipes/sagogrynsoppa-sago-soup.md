## Ingredients

- sago grains
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- 3 parts red wine
- 3 tablespoons redcurrant juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grains and set to cook in water together with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon peel.
2. When the grains are clear, add the wine and redcurrant juice.
3. Heat the soup but do not let it boil, and season with sugar.
4. Serve with rusks or biscuits.
