## Ingredients

- water, 2 cm. depth
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. vinegar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Fill a well-cleaned frying pan with water to 2 cm. height; add 1/2 tsk. salt and 1/2 tsk. vinegar.
2. When the water boils, carefully break an egg into a cup and let it slide gently into the water, which should only just barely boil.
3. When the white has set, remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and plunge quickly into cold water so the scum goes away.
4. Polish them with a glass and serve as a breakfast dish, laid on fried bread slices, or as garnish to other dishes.
