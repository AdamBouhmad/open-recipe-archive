## Ingredients

- 75 gr butter
- 100 gr flour
- 3 parts unskim milk
- 80 gr sugar
- 5 eggs
- 30 sweet almonds
- 2 bitter almonds
- 1 tsp grated lemon peel
- 10 gr biscuits
- 35 gr sultana raisins
- 1 tbsp cognac
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks
- butter for the mold

## Directions

1. One-third of the butter, together with the flour and milk, are cooked until the batter releases from the pan.
2. Stir in one whole egg immediately, and let the batter cool.
3. Mix the remaining butter with the yolks and sugar until fluffy and blend into the batter together with the blanched and grated almonds, lemon peel, the crushed biscuits, the cleaned and rinsed sultana raisins, and the cognac.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and cook in a water bath in the oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
6. Serve with jam or foam sauce.
