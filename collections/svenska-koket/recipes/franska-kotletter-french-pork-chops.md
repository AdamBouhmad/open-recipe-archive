## Ingredients

- 250 grams lean, fresh pork
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 deciliter cream
- 2 deciliters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 cup crushed breadcrumbs for breading
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter for frying
- 1 1/2 deciliters broth or water

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass through a meat grinder 6 times.
2. Whisk the egg with the cream and water and add the crushed breadcrumbs to it.
3. Add the breadcrumb mixture little by little to the forcemeat, which is worked for 1 hour.
4. Season well with spices and finely chopped parsley and let stand until the next day.
5. Stir the forcemeat for 15 minutes, form into oblong round chops, turn them in crushed breadcrumbs and fry them a beautiful brown in butter.
6. Wipe the pan with a little broth and pour the sauce over the chops.
7. Serve with preserved peas or browned potatoes as the main course.
