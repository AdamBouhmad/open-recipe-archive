## Ingredients

- 3 hg. dried apricots
- 1 dcl. granulated sugar (85 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. water

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well in several portions of lukewarm water and let them drain on cloth.
2. Layer the apricots alternately with the sugar in an enameled preserving pot, add the water, and let the fruit stand in a warm place until the following day.
3. Bring the apricots to a boil and transfer with a slotted spoon to a compote bowl.
4. Add more sugar to the syrup if desired, cook until it is syrupy, and pour over the fruit.
5. Serve cold with whipped cream.
