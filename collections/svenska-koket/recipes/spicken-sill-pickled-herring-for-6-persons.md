## Ingredients

- 4 small, salted fat herrings
- 1 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 12 hours, changing the water once.
2. Fillet, clean, rinse and dry with a fish towel.
3. Rub with pepper and let rest 1 hour.
4. Dry again thoroughly, cut into 1 1/2 cm. wide pieces crosswise, arrange on a platter and garnish with parsley or dill.
5. Serve with fresh or braised potatoes or braised green peas as a breakfast or dinner course.
