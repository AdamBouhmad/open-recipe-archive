## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover porridge made from grain, oat, or rice
- 1.5 deciliters wheat flour or crumbled rusks
- 2.5 tablespoons butter (50 grams)

## Directions

1. Cut the porridge into 2 centimeter thick slices
2. Turn the slices in flour or crumbled rusks
3. Fry slowly until beautifully golden brown in butter
4. Arrange on a hot plate
5. Serve with milk
