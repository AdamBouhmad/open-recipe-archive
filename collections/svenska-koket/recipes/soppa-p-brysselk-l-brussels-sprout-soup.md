## Ingredients

- 35 brussels sprout heads
- 3 lit. water for blanching
- 1 msk. salt for blanching
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. stock

## Directions

1. Blanch the brussels sprout heads in boiling salted water.
2. In a pot, brown the butter, add the flour and mix well.
3. Pour on the boiling stock little by little and let the soup cook with stirring.
4. Add the blanched brussels sprouts and let everything cook together.
