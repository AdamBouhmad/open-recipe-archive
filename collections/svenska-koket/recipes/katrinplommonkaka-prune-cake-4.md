## Ingredients

- 250 gr. fine prunes (approximately 22 pieces)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (80 gr.)
- 3 dcl. water
- 4 eggs
- 1 hg. sugar
- 75 gr. butter
- 4 dcl. boiling cream
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes well in several lukewarm waters and place in a bowl alternately with the sugar.
2. Add the water and let the prunes soak overnight.
3. Cook them thereafter approximately 5 minutes, let them cool, remove the pits, cut into strips, which are placed in a buttered cake mold.
4. Crush the pits, place the almonds in boiling water, peel, dry in the oven, chop finely and sprinkle over the prunes.
5. Beat egg yolks, sugar and butter for 20 minutes, add the boiling cream under vigorous whisking together with the lemon zest, and continue whisking until the cream is cold, then carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the cream over the prunes, and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately 3/4 hour, or until the mass is set when tested with a whisk.
7. Serve the cake warm or cold with or without whipped cream.
