## Ingredients

- 425 grams sweet almonds
- 425 grams granulated sugar
- 3 egg whites
- wafers
- various colored fruits or jelly for filling

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them, and grind or pass them through an almond mill.
2. Pound them quite finely with the sugar in a mortar, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Cut a round cake from wafers and spread half of the dough on it.
4. Press the remaining dough through a pastry bag in squares over the cake.
5. Place the cake on a well-cleaned baking sheet and bake in weak oven heat.
6. Fill the squares with various colored fruits or jelly.
