## Ingredients

- 3 kg. dubbelbiff or högrev
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 2 hg. smoked ham in slices
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 carrots
- 3 shallots
- 1/2 liter boiling bouillon
- 1/2 liter madeira

## Directions

1. Chop the meat before weighing it.
2. Cut away the rib bones, but leave the backbone.
3. Clean away most of the fat on the outside, rub the meat with salt and lard or cover with ham slices, which are fastened with kitchen twine dipped in boiling water.
4. Oil a long pan with cold butter to the size the meat occupies, place in the prepared and thickly sliced carrots and the peeled and quartered onions, and place the meat on top with the outside up.
5. Place in a warm oven and baste often with the butter until nicely browned (the vegetables should not burn), then dilute gradually with the bouillon and wine.
6. Baste the meat every 10 minutes and let it roast until tender, which is tested with a whisk; remove and cut off the strings.
7. Slice the roast, place on a warm platter, garnish with vegetable patties or potato croquettes and kale or parsley.
8. Serve with brown sauce.
