## Ingredients

- 2 orrhönor or 4 ripor, approximately 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 hg. flour breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 3-4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the birds and treat and pound the meat as in the preceding recipe.
2. Pound the forcemeat with the breadcrumbs until the mixture becomes even and smooth, then place it in a bowl, and add the whipped eggs and egg yolks, a little at a time, with continuous vigorous stirring.
3. Pass the forcemeat through a hair sieve, place it in a mold or smaller pot, which is set in a bowl with ice (otherwise it will break when the cream is worked in) and let it stand 1 hour, or until it is completely cooled.
4. Stir it then, let it remain on the ice and add 2 dcl. of the cream, a little at a time, with even and vigorous stirring.
5. Last, stir in the rest of the cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
6. Test it as in the preceding recipe; if it is too loose, add a little egg white, in the opposite case a little cream and more spices, if needed.
7. Mix lastly with chopped truffle, if desired.
8. Cook as in the preceding recipe and serve warm or cold with truffle sauce or brown mushroom sauce, or cold, in jelly, with vegetable salad.
