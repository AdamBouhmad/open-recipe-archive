## Ingredients

- 1½ kg veal shoulder
- salt, white pepper
- ½ hg prunes
- 1 hg dried apples
- 60 gr butter
- ½-1 liter broth or boiling water
- beef stock
- 1½ tbsp wheat flour
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, remove all membranes and tendons.
2. Beat the meat well and rub it in with salt and white pepper.
3. Rinse the dried fruit and spread it on half of the meat.
4. Fold the other half over, and sew the edges together.
5. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the meat in, and when it has taken good color on all sides, dilute it with boiling broth or water.
6. Let the meat thereafter slowly cook 3-4 hours. When the meat is done, remove the twines, cut the meat into neat pieces, place on platter and garnish with the fruit.
7. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
