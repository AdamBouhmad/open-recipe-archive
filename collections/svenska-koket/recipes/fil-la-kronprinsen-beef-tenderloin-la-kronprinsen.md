## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. beef tenderloin
- 3 slices salted beef tongue
- 1 hg. calf sweetbread
- salt
- white pepper for the sweetbread
- 1 egg white
- 2 msk. crushed rusks
- 3 1/2 msk. butter (70 gr.)
- 1 dcl. rice grains
- 1 lit. water
- 2 tsk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. French mustard
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.) for sauce
- 2 1/2 msk. flour (25 gr.)
- 4 dcl. strong meat stock
- salt
- white pepper
- 1-2 truffles
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.) for molds

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, add them to boiling salted water and let them cook until soft. Pour into a colander, rinse with cold water, let it drain and mix well with the mustard.
2. Grease small brown, fireproof molds about 1 dcm. in cross-section with butter, in each one place a base of rice. The base should be 1 cm. high and 1 cm. smaller than the mold.
3. On top of the rice base place a half slice of tongue, on top of the tongue a slice of grilled, fried calf sweetbread and at the top a slice of fried or roasted beef tenderloin.
4. Place the molds in a warm oven for 2-4 minutes to heat them through.
5. Pour brown sauce, flavored with chopped truffle, over the whole dish and serve the preparation very hot as a breakfast dish.
