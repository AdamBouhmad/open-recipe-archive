## Ingredients

- 2 small cauliflower heads (3/4 kg)
- 1 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr)
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 decilitres milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr)
- 2 tablespoons dried bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Treat and boil the cauliflower heads as in no. 709, let drain in a sieve, divide into florets, which are placed in a buttered and bread-crumbed, heat-proof form.
2. For the egg custard, whisk the egg yolks with the flour, salt and sugar and milk, after which the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
3. Pour the egg custard over the cauliflower and sprinkle with parsley, if desired.
4. Place the form in regular oven heat and let stand until the egg custard is baked and has a nice colour.
5. Serve with farces, roasted meat or as an intermediate course. In the latter case, sprinkle the egg custard with 1 tablespoon grated cheese, instead of parsley, before placing it in the oven.
