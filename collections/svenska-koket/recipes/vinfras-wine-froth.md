## Ingredients

- 125 gr. lump sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- peel and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 15 gr. gelatin (approximately 9 leaves)
- 1 dcl. warm water, for the gelatin
- 1 1/2 dcl. French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl. sherry

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water to make a clear syrup, remove from heat immediately, and blend with the finely grated lemon peel and lemon juice.
2. Add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in the warm water and the wine.
3. Stir the mixture well and pour it into a stretched cheesecloth, previously wrung out in hot water, and allow it to drip through.
4. As long as the jelly drips turbidly through, pour it back into the cloth.
5. See further page 452.
6. For this jelly, blackcurrant wine can be used alone to good effect.
7. The jelly can be beaten in a large bowl placed on ice, then whipped until it resembles snow.
8. Then pour into a bowl or use as garnish for clear jellies.
