## Ingredients

- 750 grams rhubarb
- sugar to 1 kg rhubarb

## Directions

1. Rinse the stalks, cut them into pieces, weigh them and place them alternately with the sugar in an enamelled preserve pan.
2. Cook the mass thereafter with stirring until it falls in flakes from the spoon.
3. Pour into small warm jars, which are bound over when the marmalade is cold.
