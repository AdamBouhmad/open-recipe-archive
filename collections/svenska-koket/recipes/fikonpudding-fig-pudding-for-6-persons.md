## Ingredients

- 300 gr. confectionery figs
- 300 gr. sweet almonds
- 240 gr. sugar
- 2⅓ dl. cream
- 6 egg yolks
- ½ tbsp. butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Cut the figs into strips, place in a bowl, cover with a lid, and set in boiling water so that the figs swell.
2. Scald the almonds, grate or pass through an almond mill, pound with the sugar and then mix with the cream and egg yolks.
3. Finally fold in the figs, and pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and bake in mild oven heat.
4. Turn the pudding out onto a platter and serve with vanilla or foam sauce.
