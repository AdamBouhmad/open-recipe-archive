## Ingredients

- 75 grams butter
- 100 grams flour
- 3 deciliters whole milk
- 80 grams sugar
- 5 eggs
- 30 sweet almonds
- 2 bitter almonds
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
- 10 grams biscuits
- 35 grams sultana raisins
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Cook one-third of the butter with the flour and milk until the mixture leaves the pan.
2. Stir in one whole egg immediately and let the mixture cool.
3. Cream the remaining butter with the egg yolks and sugar until light and fluffy, and blend into the mixture together with the blanched and grated almonds, lemon zest, crushed biscuits, cleaned and rinsed sultana raisins, and cognac.
4. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and cook in a water bath in the oven for 1.5-2 hours.
6. Serve with jam or foam sauce.
