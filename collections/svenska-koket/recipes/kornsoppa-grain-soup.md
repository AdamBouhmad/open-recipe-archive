## Ingredients

- 1 cup crushed groats
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 24 prunes
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- salt to taste
- 2 1/2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats, add to cold water and let them cook until soft, then pass through a sieve
2. Rinse the prunes and add with the lemon peel and sugar to cold water to cook slowly soft with a tight lid
3. Pour the passed soup back into the pot, bring to a good boil, and add the prunes with their cooking liquid
4. Season the soup with sugar and salt
5. Serve with whipped cream
