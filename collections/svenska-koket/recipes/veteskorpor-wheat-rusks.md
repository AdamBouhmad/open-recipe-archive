## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. cream (40 g.)
- 1 liter unskimmed milk
- 1.7 kg. wheat flour
- 50 g. compressed yeast
- 150 g. butter
- 150 g. sugar

## Directions

1. Melt the cream in the milk — the rusks become thereby softer. — The dough is made and worked as in no. 950. When it has risen for the second time, knead the dough well on the work surface and divide into 10 equal parts. Each part is worked well and cut into 25—30 pieces. These are rolled into small rusks, which should be completely smooth and shiny when laid on the pan. They are covered, allowed to rise in a warm, draft-free place and baked in a warm oven. When the rusks are cold, they are cut in half and dried first in a not too warm oven, until they have taken on a beautiful light brown color, and then in weaker oven heat, until they are dry and soft. — The rusks can be made simpler by using skimmed milk and by reducing the butter and sugar.
