## Ingredients

- 1 score crayfish or 2 hg crayfish tails
- 5 dcl boiling milk
- 6 eggs
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp breadcrumbs for the mold
- 2 liters boiling water
- 40 gr coarse salt
- 2 dill stems

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish and place in 2 liters boiling water with 40 gr coarse salt and 2 dill stems, boil for 10 minutes.
2. If using salted crayfish tails, soak them in milk for several hours.
3. Clean the crayfish, cut the tails and claws into pieces and scrape out the fat thoroughly.
4. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
5. Add the milk while stirring and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
6. Pour into a bowl; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
7. Mix in the crayfish meat and fat along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten until stiff peaks form.
8. Taste and adjust seasonings.
9. Pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold and bake in regular oven heat for about 45 minutes.
10. If the soufflé becomes too brown on top, cover with buttered paper.
11. Serve immediately upon removal from the oven as a middle course with crayfish sauce or stirred butter.
12. If using a metal form, bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour and turn out.
