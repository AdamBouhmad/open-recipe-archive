## Ingredients

- 8 dcl fresh or 6 dcl preserved gooseberries
- 8 dcl water
- 2 1/2-3 dcl crushed sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch flour (23 g) or 1 1/2 tablespoon potato flour (23 g)

## Directions

1. Remove the tops from the gooseberries, rinse them and let them drain.
2. Make a thin, clear syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Add the gooseberries and cook.
