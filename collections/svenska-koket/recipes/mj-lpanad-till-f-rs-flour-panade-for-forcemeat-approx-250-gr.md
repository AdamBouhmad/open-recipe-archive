## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. water
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 30 gr. butter
- 75 gr. sifted flour
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the dish

## Directions

1. Boil the water with salt and butter and whisk in the flour under full boiling.
2. Stir the mixture thereafter with a ladle over fresh fire until it releases from the pan and the ladle, then transfer it to a buttered dish and brush lightly with butter on top so that it does not dry.
