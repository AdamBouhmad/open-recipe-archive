## Ingredients

- 1 liver
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 red onions, in slices
- 4 dcl. meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. The liver should lie in water for a couple of hours so the blood will be drawn out.
2. Wrap it in cloth, remove the membrane, and cut the liver into thin slices.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and coat in 2 msk. of the flour.
4. Brown three msk. of butter in a frying pan, add the liver slices, fry quickly on both sides and place on a warm plate.
5. Brown the rest of the butter with the onion in the frying pan, mix 1 msk. of flour well with the butter and onion.
6. Add the broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then taste and pour over the liver.
7. Serve with boiled potatoes.
