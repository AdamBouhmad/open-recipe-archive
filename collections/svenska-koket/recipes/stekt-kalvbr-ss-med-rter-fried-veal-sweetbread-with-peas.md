## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms veal sweetbread
- 1 1/2 liters water for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1 can peas (1/2 liter) for garnish
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.) for garnish

## Directions

1. Let the veal sweetbread soak in water, which is changed often, so that all blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch it for 10 minutes and place in a bowl with fresh water.
3. When the sweetbread is cold, clean it well, i.e., remove cartilage and membranes, and place it in a cloth under light pressure for approximately 1 hour.
4. Cut it into 8 mm thick slices, which are sprinkled with seasonings, dipped in the egg beaten with water, and turned in the crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Fry them a beautiful golden brown in the butter and arrange in a ring on a round plate.
6. Heat the peas with a spoon in a saucepan, bring to a boil and place with a wooden spoon in the middle of the plate.
7. Place the cold butter in flakes on top of the peas.
8. Garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
