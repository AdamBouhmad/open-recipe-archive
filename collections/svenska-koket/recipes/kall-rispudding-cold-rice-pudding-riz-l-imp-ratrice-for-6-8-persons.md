## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl rice grains (125 gr)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1/2 vanilla bean or grated peel of 1/4 lemon
- 60 gr granulated sugar
- 4 sheets gelatin
- 2 tbsp hot water
- 3 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, blanch them in hot water, add to boiling milk and cook with the vanilla or lemon peel, stirring, until completely soft.
2. Thin with more milk if needed.
3. The porridge, which should be thick, is poured out and allowed to cool, then the sugar is added.
4. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in the hot water and fold into the porridge together with the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Fill the batter into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled timbal mold and let it set on ice for 3-4 hours.
6. Turn out, garnish with red currant jelly and serve with apricot sauce or strawberry juice.
