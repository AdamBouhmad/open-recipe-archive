## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg pork back
- 3 litres water

## Directions

1. Soak the pork back in water for 12-24 hours, depending on how heavily it is salted.
2. Crack it, place in cold water and bring quickly to a boil, skim and boil for 15 minutes at 100°.
3. Cool the broth to 82°-83° and let the meat remain at this temperature until tender, approximately 2 hours.
4. Cut into beautiful pieces, place on a warm plate, garnish with parsley and serve with root vegetable purée, potato purée or dumplings.
