## Ingredients

- 12 lamb chops
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 hectogram rice grains
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped shallot onion
- 50 gr. butter
- 1/2 liter bouillon
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1-2 deciliters thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 50 gr. melted butter

## Directions

1. Treat and season the chops as in recipe 538, fry lightly in butter and place on a dish.
2. Rinse the rice grains and let them drain well.
3. Sauté them together with the onion in butter until clear, then add bouillon and let the grains cook with stirring until soft - add more bouillon if needed.
4. Pass the grains through a sieve, mix with egg yolks and a little thick cream, and season with salt and pepper.
5. Pipe the rice mixture beautifully through a decorating tube onto the top of the chops, sprinkle with grated cheese and pour over melted butter.
6. Place the chops in a baking pan and gratinate in a hot oven 8-10 minutes.
7. Place on a hot dish with parchment paper, garnish with parsley and serve with brown sauce (see recipe 430) and small potatoes.
8. The chops will be juicier if braised over charcoal.
