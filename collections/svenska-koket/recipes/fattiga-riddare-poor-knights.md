## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-hour wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter milk
- 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cut the wheat bread into slices.
2. Whip wheat flour, eggs and spices together with the milk.
3. Place bread slices in this batter and let them lie until they are soft.
4. Fry them beautifully brown on both sides.
5. Serve hot with jam.
