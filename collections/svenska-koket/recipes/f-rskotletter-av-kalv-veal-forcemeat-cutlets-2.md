## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal
- 2-3 cups cream milk
- 1 tablespoon potato flour
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- For breading: 1 egg, 1.5 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- For frying: 3 tablespoons butter, 2 cups bouillon

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the forcemeat with the cream milk, potato flour and spices.
3. Form round long cutlets from the forcemeat, brush them with egg and sprinkle with finely crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Heat the butter in a frying pan, add the cutlets and brown them on both sides.
5. When all cutlets are browned, pour the boiling bouillon over them, then let them simmer gently for 10-15 minutes with a tightly closing lid.
