## Ingredients

- 2 kg lamb roast (from cold storage)
- 150 gr. larding fat
- 0.5 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 0.25 teaspoon ginger
- Marinade: 2 deciliters vinegar
- 1 deciliter water
- 1 deciliter white wine
- 3 tablespoons cooking oil
- 0.5 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 small red onion, sliced

## Directions

1. The roast should have hung so that it is tender.
2. Remove the connective tissue and thin parts, and lard the roast with strips of larding fat dipped in a mixture of salt, pepper, and ginger, and place in a deep dish.
3. Mix all marinade ingredients well, then pour over the meat, which should then sit for 3-4 days, turning twice daily.
4. Bring the marinade to a boil with enough water to level with the meat.
5. Place the roast in and let it simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 3 hours.
6. Remove and let cool, cut into thin slices and garnish with stuffed eggs and green salad or parsley and serve with mayonnaise.
