## Ingredients

- 1.5 deciliters regular oat grains (112 grams)
- 1.25 liters water
- 15 almonds (3 bitter) (15 grams)
- 1 liter milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 grams)
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 1.5 hectograms prunes

## Directions

1. Cook the grains in water as in the previous recipe and strain them.
2. Blanch the almonds, grind them through a mill, and pound them finely in a mortar.
3. Bring the milk to a boil with the almonds; add the oat puree, sugar, and salt, and let the soup boil while stirring.
