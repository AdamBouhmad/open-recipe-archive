## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg burbot
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- 6 peppercorns
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 pickled onions
- 1-2 cups boiling water or milk
- 0.5 lemon
- French wine
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Skin the burbot, open it and gut it, rinse well and dry it.
2. Trim the fins, rub the fish in with a mixture of the salt and vinegar, and cut into pieces.
3. Reserve the roe and liver, rinse them and cook separately in a little salted water.
4. Grease a casserole with half the butter, lay the fish in it, and between each layer sprinkle flour.
5. On top lay the rest of the butter, divided into small flakes, the pickled onions and the spices.
6. Last add the water carefully, and let the fish simmer gently for about 20 minutes.
7. Lay it on a platter, season the sauce with lemon juice and wine and finish with an egg yolk and pour it over the fish.
8. Garnish the fish with the roe cut into slices, the liver and the lemon.
