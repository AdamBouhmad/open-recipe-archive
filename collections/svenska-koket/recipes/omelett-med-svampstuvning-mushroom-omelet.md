## Ingredients

- 5 grams dried morels
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter mushroom stock
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Let the morels soak in cold water for 2 hours and then blanch for 15 minutes, changing the water once.
2. Drain in a sieve and pat dry in a cloth together with the mushrooms.
3. Slice the mushrooms and fry in butter until it becomes clear.
4. Squeeze it out of the butter with a spoon.
5. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the mushroom stock and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
6. Add the mushrooms and let the stewing come to a boil and season.
7. Prepare the omelet as in recipe no. 27, fill with the stewing, tip it out, garnish with parsley and serve at the smorgasbord.
