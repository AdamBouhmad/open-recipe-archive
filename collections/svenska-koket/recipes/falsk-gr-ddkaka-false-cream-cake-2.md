## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 deciliters thin cream or milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the pan
- 2 cups apple sauce or jam for garnish

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil with the sugar
2. Whisk the flour carefully under full boiling, and let the mixture boil 3-4 minutes, then pour it out
3. When most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg yolks one at a time; last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
4. Pour the mixture into a well-buttered, heat-proof dish and bake until half done in the oven
5. Remove the dish and quickly garnish the cake with apple sauce or jam, sprinkle with sugar, return to the oven and finish baking
6. Serve immediately
