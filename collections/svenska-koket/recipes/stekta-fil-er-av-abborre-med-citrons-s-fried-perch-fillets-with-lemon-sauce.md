## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg perch
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 g)
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)

## Directions

1. Do not scale the perch, clean them (see page 171), cut into fillets and remove the backbone together with all small bones.
2. Place the fillets with the skin side against the cutting board and cut the meat from the skin with a pointed knife.
3. Rub the fillets with salt and let them lie for 1 hour, then dry them again with fish towel.
4. Turn them in flour and fry them beautifully golden brown in butter on top of the stove (see no. 330).
5. Place on a warm serving dish and pour the sauce — see no. 416 — over.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and grooved lemon slices and serve with mashed potatoes.
