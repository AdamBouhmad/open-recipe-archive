## Ingredients

- 150 gr. icing sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 4 dcl. vanilla ice cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in recipe no. 921 and place with a spoon on well-cleaned and buttered sheets in the form of eggs, smoothed over with the spoon and dusted with sugar.
2. Bake in gentle oven heat until they are half dry and release from the sheet.
3. Remove them, form over an egg, and let them cool.
4. Fill the meringues with vanilla ice cream and serve doubled.
