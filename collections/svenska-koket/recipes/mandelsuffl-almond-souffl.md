## Ingredients

- 75 gr. butter
- 4 dcl. milk
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 40 gr. almonds, of which 2 bitter
- 125 gr. sugar
- 1/4 tsk. finely grated lemon zest
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and let it boil up, whisk in the flour under full boiling, add the scalded and ground almonds, sugar and lemon zest, and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes.
2. Pour out and let cool, then add the egg yolks.
