## Ingredients

- 3 lobsters (about 4 hg. each)
- 5 liters water
- 2 hg. coarse salt
- 1 liter good fish bouillon or light meat bouillon
- 10 leaves gelatin
- 2 egg whites and the rinsed shells
- 1 tbsp. cold water
- 3 tbsp. good, white wine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 truffle
- parsley
- salad

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster as in no. 170.
2. Cut the meat into beautiful pieces.
3. The aspic is prepared as follows: the bouillon is poured into a pot together with the rinsed gelatin and the egg whites lightly beaten with water, mixed with the crushed eggshells.
4. The mixture is brought to a good boil while whisking, the lid is placed on the pot, which is lifted from the heat and left at the side of the stove for about 15 minutes.
5. The bouillon is then strained through a cloth or napkin wrung out in hot water.
6. If the first liquid that runs through is not clear, it is strained again.
