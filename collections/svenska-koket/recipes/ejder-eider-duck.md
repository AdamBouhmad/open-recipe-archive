## Ingredients

- 1 eider duck (for 4-5 persons)
- Milk

## Directions

1. An eider duck is usually calculated for 4-5 persons.
2. After the bird is cleaned, the fishy skin is stripped off, then it is blanched and allowed to soak in milk for 1 day before it is prepared.
3. It is then treated, roasted and served like duck.
