## Ingredients

- 8 dcl. fresh or 6 dcl. preserved gooseberries
- 8 dcl. water
- 2 1/2–3 dcl. crushed sugar
- 1 1/2 tbsp. mondamin flour (23 gr.) or 1 1/2 tbsp. potato flour (23 gr.)

## Directions

1. Top the gooseberries, rinse and drain them.
2. Boil a thin, clear syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Add the gooseberries and let them boil until they begin to burst.
4. Mix the flour with 2 tbsp. cold water or French wine and add to the cream.
5. Let the cream boil for 5 minutes if thickened with mondamin, or only 2 minutes if thickened with potato flour.
6. Taste the cream and add more sugar if needed, stirring occasionally until cool.
7. Pour into a compote bowl and serve with cream or milk.
