## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 60 gr. granulated sugar
- 4 parts spirits
- 320 gr. wheat flour
- 2 egg whites for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 1/3 kkp pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with sugar until white and fluffy.
2. Stir in a little of the sifted wheat flour and then the spirits.
3. Finally work in the remaining flour.
4. Roll out not too thin and cut small square cakes with a measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with chopped almond and sugar.
6. Bake in quite a hot oven.
