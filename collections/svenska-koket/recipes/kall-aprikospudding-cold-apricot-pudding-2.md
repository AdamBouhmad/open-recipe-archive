## Ingredients

- 350 gr. dried apricots
- 1.3 lit. water
- 125 gr. sugar
- 3 1/2 dcl. of the liquid
- 3–4 leaves gelatin
- the passed apricots

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well and cook them in the water with the added sugar until they are soft.
2. Strain off the liquid and pass the apricots through a sieve.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces and place in the fruit liquid, which should then be brought to a boil and whisked until it is cold and begins to thicken.
4. Then add the fruit purée, and pour the mixture into a water-rinsed mold dusted with sugar and let it stand on ice for 3–4 hours to set.
5. Loosen the pudding from the edge with a pointed knife, unmold onto a flat glass dish, garnish with fruit and serve with vanilla sauce.
