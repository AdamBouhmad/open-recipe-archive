## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg perch
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp wheat flour
- 1 tbsp coarse rye flour
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 3 tbsp white wine
- 1.5 dcl fish stock
- 1.5 dcl thick cream
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1/2 dcl white wine
- 1 tsp sugar coloring
- 1 tbsp finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the perch as in the previous recipe. — Cook fish stock on the head, bones and skin, which is used for the sauce.
2. Dip the fillets in the whipped egg and turn them in the flour, which has been well mixed beforehand.
3. Fry them in butter as in no. 330.
