## Ingredients

- Leftovers of cooked fish, about 3 dcl.
- 1 can lobster (1/4 lit)
- 6-8 mushrooms
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 1/2 lit. boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.) for shells
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Free the fish from all bones and cut into fine dice
2. Cut the lobster into small pieces and the mushrooms into thin slices
3. Brown the mushroom in the butter until it is clear, then press it out of the butter with a spoon
4. Brown the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes
5. Season with spices
6. Butter six shells well, then add fish, lobster and mushroom
