## Ingredients

- 60 gr. sweetened block chocolate
- 1/2 lit. cream
- 1/2 tsp. potato starch (8 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1—2 tsp. powdered sugar (15 gr.)
- cognac or liqueur (optional)

## Directions

1. Divide the chocolate finely into the cream, which is brought to a boil while whisking, so that the chocolate melts.
2. Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar and potato starch until foamy.
3. Add the boiling milk to the egg mixture under vigorous whisking.
4. Let the sauce simmer over the heat with continued whisking until it is thick and frothy.
5. Serve both warm and cold with puddings and cakes, and may be flavored with a little cognac or liqueur if desired.
6. The sauce can also be mixed with 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream, whisked until stiff peaks form.
