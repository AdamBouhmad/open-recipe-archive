## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 160 grams sugar
- peel of 1 lemon
- juice of 2 lemons
- 7 leaves gelatin
- 3 tablespoons warm water

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the finely grated lemon peel and lemon juice.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in the warm water.
3. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
4. Let the gelatin cool and gradually stir it into the egg mixture together with the egg white foam.
5. Pour the mixture into a compote bowl and let it stand on ice for 3-4 hours to set.
6. Serve with almond slivers.
