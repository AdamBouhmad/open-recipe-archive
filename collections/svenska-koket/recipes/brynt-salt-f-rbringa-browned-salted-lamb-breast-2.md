## Ingredients

- 1½ kg lamb breast
- water to boiling
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp milk
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. If the lamb breast is too salty, soak it in water the day before.
2. Break it into appropriately sized pieces that hold together.
3. Place in cold water (short broth), bring to a boil, skim, and cook with a tightly closed lid until the meat is tender, then remove.
4. Whisk the egg with the milk, brush the breast with this mixture and sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
5. Place in a long pan whose bottom is covered with the broth, and put in the oven to achieve a nice light golden color.
6. Serve the breast arranged on potato purée and garnished with parsley. Can also advantageously be served with browned vegetables.
