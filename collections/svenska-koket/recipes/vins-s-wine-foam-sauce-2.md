## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 125 gr. caster sugar
- 5 dcl. pure wine

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks carefully from the whites, place them in a well-scoured saucepan and whisk with the sugar for 15 minutes.
2. Pour in the wine and let the sauce simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and foamy, then remove from heat and serve immediately.
