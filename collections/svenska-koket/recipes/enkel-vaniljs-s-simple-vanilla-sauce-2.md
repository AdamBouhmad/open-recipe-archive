## Ingredients

- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 3 parts milk
- 1 1/2 parts cream
- 1-2 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 1/2 tbsp. potato flour
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The milk and cream are allowed to simmer with the vanilla pod and sugar until the liquid has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor.
2. The vanilla pod is removed, the potato flour is stirred out with a little cold milk and added, after which the sauce is brought to a short boil.
3. The saucepan is lifted from the heat, and the egg yolks are stirred in.
4. The saucepan is then placed over heat again, and the sauce is allowed to simmer under whisking, but not boil, until it becomes sufficiently thick.
5. It is then whisked until it becomes cold.
