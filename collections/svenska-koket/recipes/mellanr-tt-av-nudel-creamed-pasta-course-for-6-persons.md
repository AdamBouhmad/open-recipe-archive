## Ingredients

- 2 cups pasta
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 8 deciliters cream
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the water with salt in a large pot.
2. Add the pasta and let it cook about 15 minutes.
3. Pour it into a colander and let the water drain well.
4. Melt the butter in the pot, add the pasta and dilute the cream, a little at a time, while the stew cooks about 1 hour.
5. Season the dish with the spices, arrange in shells, sprinkle with cheese and serve very hot.
6. A stew of mushrooms can also be mixed in.
7. The mass can also be placed in a buttered and breadcrumbed mold, baked in a water bath in the oven and turned out onto a platter, but the stew should then be a little firmer.
