## Ingredients

- 2 hg good butter
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 parts strong stock
- 1 pinch nutmeg

## Directions

1. Work the butter well with the rinsed and coarsely chopped parsley.
2. Bring the stock to a boil and move it to the side, stir in the worked butter along with the lemon juice.
3. The sauce should not boil thereafter.
4. Add a tablespoon of cold water and serve immediately.
5. Colbert sauce is served with both meat and fish (fried, grilled, or cooked in cream).
