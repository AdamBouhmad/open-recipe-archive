## Ingredients

- 40 g tapioca pearls
- 1 1/2 cups water
- 2 1/2 liters clear consommé
- Salt and cayenne to taste
- 100 g pickled, cooked beef tongue
- 1 can truffles (1/8 liter)
- 1 can fine mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 30 g butter

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca pearls cook in water until they are clear, then strain them through muslin and add the liquid to the consommé.
2. Cut the tongue and truffles into fine strips and the mushrooms into thin slices.
3. Sauté the mushrooms in 30 g of butter until the butter is clear, then let it drain on a sieve.
4. Add the garnish to the consommé.
