## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into slices.
2. Grease a baking pan with some of the butter, lay the potatoes in rows in it and sprinkle with salt and grated cheese.
3. Place the remaining butter in flakes on top, and put the potatoes in the oven to cook through.
