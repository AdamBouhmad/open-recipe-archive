## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters weak beer or malt beverage
- 4 parts water
- 3 tablespoons syrup
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix the beverage, water, syrup, and juice of 1 lemon.
2. Cut the other lemon into slices and add.
3. To chill the soup, a little ice may be added.
4. Serve with rusks.
