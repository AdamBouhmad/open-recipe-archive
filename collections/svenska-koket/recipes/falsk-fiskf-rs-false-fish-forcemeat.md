## Ingredients

- 150 grams butter
- 150 grams flour
- 1 litre unskimmed milk
- 8 eggs
- salt, sugar
- white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoon crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are mixed together, milk is added, and the mass is allowed to boil until it leaves the pan
2. When the batter has cooled somewhat, egg yolks are stirred in one by one, and the forcemeat is well seasoned with spices
3. Last, the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in, the batter is poured into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated mold and baked in a water bath in the oven or on top of the stove for 40-50 minutes
4. Served with crayfish or mushroom sauce
