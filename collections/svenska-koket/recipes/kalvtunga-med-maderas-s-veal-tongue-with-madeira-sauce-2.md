## Ingredients

- 1 veal tongue
- 2 liters water
- 1 msk. salt
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tsk. salt
- white pepper
- 3 kkp good broth
- 1 tsk. soy
- 4 dcl. meat jus
- 1 tsk. cornstarch
- 1 msk. Madeira
- 2 msk. finely cut, browned mushrooms

## Directions

1. Brush the tongue well, rinse, place in the boiling water with added salt, and let it cook until the skin can be easily removed.
2. Brown the butter in a cast iron pot, place the tongue in and brown nicely on all sides, salt and dilute with the broth, with added soy.
3. Let it cook slowly until tender, about 1 hour, basting over every ten minutes so it does not dry out.
