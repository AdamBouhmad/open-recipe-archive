## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 105 gr. icing sugar
- peel of 1/4 lemon or 4 bitter almonds
- 55 gr. dry potato flour
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sifted icing sugar vigorously for approximately 1 hour.
2. Add the finely grated lemon peel or the blanched and ground almonds, and the potato flour.
3. Pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined ring pan or skillet and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 1/2 hour.
