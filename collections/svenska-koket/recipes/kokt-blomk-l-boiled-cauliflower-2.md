## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads (1 kg)
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 40 g melted butter or 3 deciliters hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. The cauliflower heads should be white and firm. Remove the green leaves and cut away the bottom of the stalk.
2. Rinse the cauliflower well and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water (see page 338) for 1 hour.
3. Rinse again, place in boiling salted water with the flower facing down, and let it cook 15-20 minutes, or until just tender, which is tested with a whisk.
4. Lift the cauliflower out with a slotted spoon onto a warm dish, pour the butter or sauce over it, and serve very hot as a vegetable dish or with roasted meat, salted beef brisket, or hamburger brisket.
5. Cauliflower can also be used as a garnish around meat together with other vegetables. Cauliflower becomes whiter if cooked in half milk and half water.
