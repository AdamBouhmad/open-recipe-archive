## Ingredients

- 250 gr. butter
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 4 eggs
- 100 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 3 cups apple sauce
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and let it cool.
2. Stir it together with the sugar until white and fluffy.
3. Mix in the eggs one at a time and finally the sifted wheat flour.
4. Bake the batter on a pancake iron to 3 bottoms.
5. When these are cold, layer them with apple sauce between.
6. Garnish the tart with whipped cream.
