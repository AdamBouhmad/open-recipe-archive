## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast (gödkalvbringa)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 5 parsley stems
- 2 deciliters rice grains (risgryn)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 30 g butter
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 6 deciliters of the broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter
- 3 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs (stötta skorpor)
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the veal breast with a cloth wrung out in hot water, chop it into even, beautiful pieces, which are placed in enough boiling water to cover the meat evenly; skim well and add the spices, the browned carrot, and the rinsed parsley stems.
2. Let the meat simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 1 1/2 hours.
3. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, place them in boiling salted water and let them cook 15–20 minutes, then pour them into a colander, let them drain well, and mix while hot with the cold butter.
4. For the sauce: fry butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, then taste it with the spices and finish it with the egg yolks (see page 209).
5. Grease a fireproof dish with cold butter and sprinkle with half of the crushed breadcrumbs, then layer the meat, rice, and sauce alternately.
