## Ingredients

- 2 calf shanks
- 1 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/2 liters beef broth
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 1 portion Madeira
- sugar (optional)
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the shanks, sauté them, and cook them in water for two hours, then remove them
2. Cut the meat into pieces and strain and skim the liquid
3. Fry together the butter and flour, then add 1/2 liter of the cooking liquid and the broth, as well as the cleaned and rinsed parsley, the wine, and the seasonings
4. Let the soup cook 1/2 hour over low heat, then remove the parsley bunch
5. Serve the cut meat in the soup, which should have a distinct wine flavor
