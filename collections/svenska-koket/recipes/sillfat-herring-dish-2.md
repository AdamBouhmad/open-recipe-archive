## Ingredients

- 1-2 salted herrings
- 1 onion
- 6 cooked potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Soak the herring the day before, clean and remove skin and bones.
2. Butter an omelette dish with butter.
3. Cut the potatoes into slices and lay one layer on the bottom of the dish; on top of that place the herring, then the onion, peeled and sliced, and on top again a layer of potatoes.
4. Sprinkle the crushed rusks over and place butter in flakes on top.
5. Place the dish in the oven to get a nice color.
