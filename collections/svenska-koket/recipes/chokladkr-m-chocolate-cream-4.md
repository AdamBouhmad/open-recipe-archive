## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 2 dcl. milk
- 35 gr. cocoa powder
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (128 gr.)
- 5 sheets gelatin, 3 tbsp. water
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs in a saucepan together with the milk, cocoa and sugar.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, dissolve it in 3 tbsp. hot water and add it, whereupon the cream is allowed to simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking.
3. Pour out the cream and whisk until it is cold, then carefully stir in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
5. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a glass dish.
6. Garnish with whipped cream and serve with it as well, if desired.
