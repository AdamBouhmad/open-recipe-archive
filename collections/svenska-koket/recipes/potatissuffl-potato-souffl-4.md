## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters potatoes
- 40 grams butter
- 3 eggs
- 1–2 deciliters thick cream
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the form
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare the potatoes as described, peel thinly, cook and press through a purée press.
2. Mix the cold butter into the purée with a fork along with the whipped egg yolks and cream.
3. Add the seasonings and lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Place the mixture in a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn fireproof form, sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
5. Bake the soufflé in good oven heat until it has taken on a beautiful golden-brown color, or approximately 40 minutes.
6. Serve immediately after removing from the oven to the smorgasbord or to roasted meat in general.
