## Ingredients

- 130 gr apples
- 130 gr currants
- 130 gr raisins
- 60 gr candied fruits
- 130 gr beef kidney suet
- 1 tbsp wheat flour
- 130 gr crushed rusks
- 130 gr sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 part brandy
- 1 to 1 1/2 cups water
- 1 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, core them and chop finely.
2. Clean and wash the currants, rinse the raisins, pit them and chop, cut the candied fruits into thin slices.
3. Free the kidney suet from membranes and chop finely together with the wheat flour.
4. Mix all the dry ingredients well together.
