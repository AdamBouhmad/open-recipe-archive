## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms chestnuts
- 1 liter coarse salt

## Directions

1. Cut a 1/2 cm deep cross into the pointed end of the chestnuts.
2. Place the salt in a roasting pan, embed the chestnuts in it and roast in the oven 30-40 minutes or until they easily release their shells.
3. Place them on a broken napkin and serve hot with softened butter for the smörgåsbord.
