## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoon flour (30 gr.)
- 5 deciliter meat broth
- 1—2 tablespoon finely chopped dill
- 1—2 tablespoon vinegar
- 2—3 teaspoon sugar (10—15 gr.)
- salt
- 1—2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry 1 tablespoon of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
2. Gradually add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the chopped dill.
4. Season the sauce with vinegar and seasonings.
5. Add the beaten egg yolk and let the sauce simmer with vigorous whisking but do not boil, as it breaks easily.
6. Finish the sauce with the remaining cold butter.
