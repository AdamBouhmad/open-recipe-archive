## Ingredients

- 1½ kg spare ribs or 1 kg loin
- 1 calf's foot
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 liter of cooking liquid
- 3 sheets gelatin
- 1 hard-boiled egg, beets, parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the calf's foot and pork with a cloth dipped in warm water.
2. Place them in boiling water, skim when the liquid comes to a boil, and add the spices.
3. Let the meat cook slowly until tender.
4. Remove the pork, peel off the skin, and cut the meat into nice slices or pieces.
5. Let the calf's foot continue cooking in the broth, which is then strained and returned to the heat.
6. Add the egg and dissolved gelatin, then bring the broth to a good boil while stirring.
7. Strain through a napkin or cheesecloth wrung out in cold water.
8. Pour the clear aspic onto the bottom of a rinsed aspic mold and set out to set.
9. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled eggs and parsley.
10. Place the finest pieces on the bottom and sides, fill the mold with the remaining meat, and carefully pour the rest of the aspic over.
11. Set the aspic in a cold place to set.
