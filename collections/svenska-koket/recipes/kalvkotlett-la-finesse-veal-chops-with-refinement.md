## Ingredients

- 3–4 truffles
- 150 g. cooked, smoked ham
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 6 veal chops
- 3 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp. water
- 3 tbsp. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 g.) for frying
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 g.)
- 3 dcl. good broth
- 1 tsp. tomato purée
- 6 bread slices, cooked in bouillon
- French potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the truffle, ham and mushrooms into small, even cubes. Chop the trimmings finely and mix with the crushed breadcrumbs.
2. Dry the chops with a cloth wrung out in hot water, clean them, beat well on both sides and rub with the spice mixture.
3. Brush with the beaten egg mixed with the water and turn in the ham, mushroom and breadcrumb mixture.
4. Brown the butter in a frying pan, place the chops in and fry about 5 minutes on each side.
5. Quickly fry the mushroom and ham cubes in a little of the butter and place in the center of a round serving dish. Place the bread crust around it and lay the chops on top.
6. Fry the flour well with the butter in which the chops were cooked, add the broth and tomato purée and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
7. Taste it and pour over the chops, garnish the dish with French potatoes and parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
