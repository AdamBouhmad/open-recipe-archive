## Ingredients

- 6 dcl. sugar syrup (made from 1 hg. to 5 hg. sugar per 3 1/2 dcl. water)
- 1 liter fruit purée or juice
- Lemon juice to taste

## Directions

1. Cook a clear syrup from the sugar and water.
2. The proportions of sugar and water should be calculated according to how sweet and juicy the fruit is, and can vary as stated above.
3. Crush and strain the fruit or let it drip naturally and mix with the sugar syrup.
4. If a saccharometer is available, measure the mixture. If it shows over 22 degrees, dilute with a little cooled boiled water; if it shows under, add a little more dissolved sugar.
5. Freeze the fruit ice cream as cream ice cream (see recipe no. 929), but it must stand about 1 hour longer in the mold with the salt mixture packed tightly around it before unmolding.
6. It melts quickly and should therefore be served just as it is to be served.
