## Ingredients

- 3 parts milk
- 1/2 hectogram unsweetened chocolate (cocoa powder)
- 1 1/2 coffee cups sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 6 gelatin leaves
- thick cream

## Directions

1. Pour the milk, chocolate, sugar, and eggs into a saucepan and whisk vigorously over heat until simmering. Remove from heat but continue whisking until the cream cools. Add the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the thick cream beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture into a cold water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and refrigerate until set, about 2 1/2 hours. To turn out, hold the mold briefly in lukewarm water. Serve with whipped cream.
