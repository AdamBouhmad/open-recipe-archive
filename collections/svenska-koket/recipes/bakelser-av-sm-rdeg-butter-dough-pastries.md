## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scarcely)
- 250 gr. fine butter (equal weight to the dough)

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 33.
2. After the last resting, roll out to 1 cm. thickness and cut with a pastry wheel into round or square cakes.
3. Place a little applesauce in the center of the cakes, brush the round ones around the edge with a little water, then fold in half so the cake becomes a half-moon shape, press the edges together and lightly flute with a pastry wheel if desired.
4. For the square cakes, make an incision from each corner with the wheel and fold every other of the eight flaps over the applesauce and press together.
5. Bake the pastries in a warm oven.
6. This recipe makes approximately 12 butter pastries.
