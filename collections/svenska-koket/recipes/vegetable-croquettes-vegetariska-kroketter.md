## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 deciliter rice grains
- 1 liter milk
- 2 deciliter mushroom stock
- 1 can mushrooms (1/2 liter)
- 2 1/2 tablespoon butter (50 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil
- 1 cup crushed bread crumbs
- 1 kg. cooking fat
- 1 head of lettuce for garnish
- 2-3 tomatoes for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald in hot water and cook in the milk and mushroom stock until a thick porridge forms, strain immediately.
2. Cut the mushrooms into dice and fry in butter until it becomes clear, then stir into the porridge together with the beaten egg yolks.
3. Season the mixture with salt and white pepper and treat as in no. 49.
4. Form the croquettes into oblong shapes.
5. Garnish with lettuce and tomato wedges and serve as a side course with fresh or canned peas.
