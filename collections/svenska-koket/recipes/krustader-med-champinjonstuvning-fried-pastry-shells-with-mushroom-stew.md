## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 parts cream
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1/2 kg. lard for cooking
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- mushroom stock
- 1/2 litre thin cream
- 1 can mushrooms (of 1/4 litre)
- pepper
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Sift the dry ingredients in a bowl and mix with half of the cream, whipped with the egg
2. Melt the butter, let it cool, and add it together with the rest of the cream, and stir everything well
3. Heat the lard in an iron pot, and heat the pastry mould in it
4. Dip the mould thereafter in the batter, observing that the batter does not reach over the edge of the mould
5. Cook the pastry shells in the lard, until they are golden brown
6. Fry the mushrooms in butter and take up, then mix the flour together with the butter
7. Add the mushroom stock and cream with constant stirring, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes
8. Add the fried mushrooms, and season the stew with pepper and salt
9. Finally stir in the whipped egg yolk, and fill the stew in the pastry shells
