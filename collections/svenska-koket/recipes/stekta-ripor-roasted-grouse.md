## Ingredients

- 3 grouse
- 1 hg. lard
- 1½ tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt
- ½–1 liter broth
- ½ tbsp. butter for sauce
- 1⅖ tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the grouse, singe, remove innards, rinse well and dry with a cloth. (If the grouse have been frozen, place them in milk overnight.) Lard them thereafter with the lard cut into fine strips and truss. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the grouse in with breasts up and brown well on all sides; salt them thereafter and dilute with the boiling broth. The roasting takes approximately 1 hour.
2. Strain the meat stock and skim, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
