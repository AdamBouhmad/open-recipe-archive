## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal (from the loin)
- 1 hectogram lard
- white pepper
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2-3 cups boiling water
- For the sauce: 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat broth
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into slices, which are pounded lightly.
2. Each slice is then larded with three strips of lard and seasoned.
3. Brown in butter, add the boiling water, and let the dish cook with the lid closed until the meat is tender.
4. Serve the meat on carrot with the sauce over.
5. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
