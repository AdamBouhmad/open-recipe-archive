## Ingredients

- 80 gr. butter
- 1/4 liter milk
- 150-200 gr. wheat flour
- 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Bring butter and milk to a boil together, carefully stir in the flour, and let the batter cook until it leaves the sides of the pan, then remove from heat.
2. Add the egg yolks immediately one at a time, and when the batter is cold, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Form small balls of the batter with teaspoons and cook them in hot deep fat.
4. When the pastries have turned a beautiful light brown color, place them on gray paper to drain and sprinkle with fine sugar.
5. Serve warm or cold with jam.
