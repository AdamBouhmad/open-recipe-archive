## Ingredients

- 15 liters raspberries
- 5 liters red currants
- 2 liters water
- 0.5 kg sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Clean the raspberries and rinse the currants and strip them from their stalks.
2. Place the berries with water and let them cook with stirring until they are completely broken down.
3. Pour the mass into cheesecloth and let it drain until the next day.
4. Cook the juice with the sugar with careful skimming until it is clear and glossy.
5. Bottle it hot or cold; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
