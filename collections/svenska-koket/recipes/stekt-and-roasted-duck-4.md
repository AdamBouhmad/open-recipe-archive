## Ingredients

- 2 ducks
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5–1 liter broth
- lemon juice
- soy sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck the ducks, singe, clean, rinse very well, dry and truss.
2. Rub them in with the liver until they have taken on a reddish-brown color, then brown them in the butter and salt them.
3. Add the boiling broth a little at a time, and let the ducks roast for about 1 hour, basting them often with their drippings.
4. Serve the ducks very hot.
5. Strain and skim the sauce, flavor it with lemon juice and soy sauce and serve as drippings. If a bound sauce is desired, prepare it according to general sauce rules.
