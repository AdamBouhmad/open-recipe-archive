## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters good, loose apples
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 two-öre almond breads
- 1 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 120 gr. butter
- 120 gr. sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- Grated zest of 1/2 lemon
- 25 gr. sweet almonds (about 25 pieces)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, remove the cores with a pastry tube, and cut the apples into thin slices, which are placed alternating with sugar in a bowl and allowed to stand for about 1 hour.
2. Cut the almond breads into pieces and place them in the milk.
3. Stir butter and sugar into a foam, add the whisked eggs and egg yolks together with the lemon zest, the blanched and ground almonds, and lastly stir in the soaked, squeezed-out bread.
4. Work the dough well and place it alternating with apple slices in a buttered and rusk-strewn pie pan - 3 layers of dough and 2 layers of apple slices.
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour, or until the apples are soft and the dough when tested with a whisk feels dry.
6. Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
