## Ingredients

- 24 strömmingar (Baltic herring)
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 12 ansjovisar (anchovies)
- 3 msk. smör (60 gr.) (butter)
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor (crushed rusks)
- 1/2 msk. smör (10 gr.) for the form (butter)
- 1 msk. stötta skorpor for the form (crushed rusks)

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, remove the bones, scrape away the dark skin, rinse well and wrap in a fish cloth.
2. Sprinkle with salt inside and outside and let sit for a while.
3. Clean the anchovies, remove the bones and split them in half lengthwise.
4. Butter a heatproof omelet dish with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
5. Place one anchovy half in each herring, roll the herring tightly and lay them in the dish close together.
6. Distribute butter in flakes on top; finally sprinkle with crushed rusks.
7. Place the dish in a good oven heat and let the herring fry until nicely brown, about 25 minutes.
8. Serve for the smörgåsbord or with fried potatoes as breakfast.
