## Ingredients

- 4 kilograms prunes
- 1 liter water
- 1 cup sugar
- lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums and soak them in cold water for 1 hour before use.
2. Place them in the water in which they were soaked, add the sugar and let the plums cook until soft.
3. Thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, give it a short boil, taste and pour into a jelly mold or carrot to cool.
