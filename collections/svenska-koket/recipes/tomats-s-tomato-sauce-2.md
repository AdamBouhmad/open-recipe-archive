## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 parts fish stock
- 2 parts thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 medium tomatoes

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are cooked together
2. Fish stock and cream are added
3. The sauce is allowed to boil for 10 minutes
4. It is seasoned and finished with the egg yolks
5. The tomatoes are peeled; two of them are pressed, the others are cut into small pieces, and everything is stirred into the sauce
