## Ingredients

- 5 parts water
- 0.5-1 kilogram cocoa
- 3 teaspoons potato flour
- sugar to taste
- 2 parts cream

## Directions

1. The water is boiled up, and the cocoa is whisked in.
2. Potato flour, stirred out in a little water, is added, and the sauce is brought to a boil for a short time.
3. It is seasoned with sugar, mixed with cream and poured up to cool.
