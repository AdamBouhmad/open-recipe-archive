## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped mushrooms
- 2 tomatoes cut into cubes
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike, see page 171; the head may remain but the gills and fins are removed
2. Score the fish with 2 cm between each cut, rub with salt and let rest 1 hour, then dry again
3. Beat 2 tablespoons (40 g) of the butter until white and fluffy, and mix with the parsley, mushrooms, and tomatoes; fill the pike with this mixture and sew it closed
4. Brush the pike with egg and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs
5. Grease a roasting pan with some of the butter to the size of the fish, place the pike in it with the back up, arrange the remaining butter in flakes on top
6. Place in a hot oven and roast for approximately 1/2 hour, basting often with the butter
7. Place the pike on a hot serving dish, remove the thread, pour the pan butter over the fish and garnish with parsley and fluted lemon slices
8. Serve with mushroom or tomato sauce
