## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter sour or sweet fruit juice
- 1 1/2 liters water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm.)
- 1 1/2–2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Mix the fruit juice, water, lemon peel, cinnamon, and sugar in a saucepan (omit the sugar if the juice is sweet).
2. Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Skim the soup and thicken with the potato flour mixed with 1/2 tablespoon cold water.
4. Let cook for 2 minutes.
5. Serve with whipped cream, dumplings, yeast-powder rusks, or biscuits.
