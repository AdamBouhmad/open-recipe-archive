## Ingredients

- 5 deciliters milk
- 40 grams butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 60 grams wheat flour
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Bring milk, butter and sugar to a boil.
2. Carefully whisk the flour in under full boiling and let the mass cook until it releases from the pan.
3. Pour it out and stir in the egg yolks one by one.
4. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks and pour the mass into a well-buttered omelet pan and bake in a warm oven until the omelet has set and acquired a beautiful golden brown color.
5. Turn it out and fill either with finely sliced roast in its sauce or with braised calf kidney.
