## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal shoulder
- 100 grams larding fat
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 0.75 to 1 liter boiling water
- 0.25 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 0.5 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the shoulder with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Carefully remove the shoulder blade and also the ligament.
3. Spread out the meat; cut the larding fat into thin slices and lay them over the veal.
4. Mix pepper and salt and sprinkle over the fat.
5. Roll the meat and bind it loosely with string.
6. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the roll in it and put it in the roasting oven to brown on all sides.
7. When it has taken on a nice brown color, add the water.
8. Allow the roll to roast further for about 1 hour, basting it with its gravy every tenth minute.
9. Remove the roll and cut into thin slices and garnish with parsley.
10. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
