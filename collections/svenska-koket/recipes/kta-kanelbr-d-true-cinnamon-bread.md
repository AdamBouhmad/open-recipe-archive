## Ingredients

- 212 gr. sweet almond
- 2 whole eggs
- 212 gr. granulated sugar
- 50 gr. ground cinnamon
- 40 gr. granulated sugar, for rolling out

## Directions

1. Dry the almond well in a cloth and drive it through an almond mill.
2. Then pound it in a mortar with the eggs, one at a time.
3. Add the sugar and cinnamon and pound the paste.
