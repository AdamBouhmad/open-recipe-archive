## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 root celery
- 1 small leek
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 liter boiling water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons fresh peas
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 liter milk
- 1/2 hectogram spinach
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon cold butter (20 g)

## Directions

1. Prepare the roots in the usual way and cut into small cubes; cut the leek into fine strips; clean the cauliflower, divide into florets and let soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour
2. Sauté the vegetables and leek well in half of the butter, place in the boiling water with salt, and cook until half-tender
3. Add the rinsed cauliflower and peas and cook until done
4. Remove vegetables with a slotted spoon to a soup bowl and use the broth for the soup
5. Sauté the remaining butter with flour for 2 minutes, gradually add the broth and cooked milk, bring to a boil for 5 minutes stirring constantly
6. Add the cleaned, rinsed and skimmed spinach and cook under constant stirring for 5 minutes
7. Add the parsley and season with spices
8. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl and pour the soup over them while whisking
9. Simmer and pour over the vegetables and cold butter in the soup bowl
10. In simpler cases, the eggs and cream may be omitted
