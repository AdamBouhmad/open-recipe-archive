## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/2 dcl. water
- approximately 80 gr. flour
- 6 dcl. thick cream
- 250 gr. lightly salted side pork

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with a steel whisk; whisk the water and flour into them, and let the batter stand for 1 hour to swell, after which the cream whipped to stiff peaks is added.
2. Treat the pork and bake the pancakes as in the preceding recipe.
3. This batch yields two large pancakes.
