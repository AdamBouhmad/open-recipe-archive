## Ingredients

- 1 salmon 1.75-2 kg
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 grams)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- For breading: 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons fine bread crumbs
- For cooking: 100 grams butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the salmon as in recipe 331 and score both sides with 2 cm between each cut. Rub with the seasonings and let rest in a cool place for a couple of hours.
2. Dry again, brush with the beaten egg and sprinkle with fine bread crumbs.
3. Butter a long pan with cold butter in an area as large as the fish occupies. Place the salmon with the back up and sides opened out and fry.
