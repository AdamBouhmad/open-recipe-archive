## Ingredients

- 6 medium-sized Portuguese onions
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. finely chopped red onion
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 tbsp. fresh breadcrumbs
- 1 tbsp. tomato puree
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- a little white pepper
- 1 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, cut off one quarter of the onion from the top, then blanch the onions (place them in boiling salted water and let them cook without a lid) until they are soft, but do not fall apart.
2. Place them on a cloth and let them drain, then remove the inside with a teaspoon, so that only about 8 mm. of the onion remains.
3. Chop the inside finely and sauté it in the butter together with the red onion, the mushrooms cut into thin slices, the breadcrumbs, tomato puree, the spices and the parsley. Sauté the mixture until it becomes appropriately thick, then fill it into the onions and sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
4. Brown the butter in a saucepan, place the onions in it, put the saucepan in a warm oven, and gratinée the mixture until it gets a beautiful golden brown color, or about 10-15 minutes. During this time, frequently baste the onions with the butter in the pan. Serve for the smörgåsbord or as a garnish around roasted meat. The onions can also be filled with risotto (see recipe no. 133), tomatoes or spinach soufflé (see recipe no. 728).
