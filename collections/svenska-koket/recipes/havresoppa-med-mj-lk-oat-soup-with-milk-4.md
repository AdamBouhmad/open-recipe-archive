## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. regular oat groats (112 gr.)
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 15 almonds (3 bitter) (15 gr.)
- 1 liter milk
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1 1/2 hg. prunes

## Directions

1. Boil the groats in the water as in the previous recipe and pass through a sieve.
2. Blanch the almonds, grind them through a mill and pound finely in a mortar.
3. Boil the milk with the almonds; add the oat purée, sugar and salt and let the soup boil while stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Rinse the prunes well in lukewarm water, boil them in a short broth with a lid until they are soft and swollen, strain them and add them to the soup, which is tasted carefully.
