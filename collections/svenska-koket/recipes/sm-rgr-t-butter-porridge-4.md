## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 80 gr. butter
- 115 gr. flour
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, gradually add the milk while stirring, and let the porridge cook 15 minutes.
3. Season with salt and serve with milk.
