## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg beef loin or tenderloin
- ⅓ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 2-3 parts boiling bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into approximately 3 cm thick slices; these are beaten lightly on both sides, peppered and salted.
2. A frying pan is placed on the heat to become hot, the butter is browned in it, and the steaks are placed in to fry quickly approximately one minute on each side.
3. The pan is whisked out with the boiling bouillon or water, and the sauce is poured over the steaks.
4. Finally, the peeled onion cut into slices is browned, and with it the steaks are garnished.
