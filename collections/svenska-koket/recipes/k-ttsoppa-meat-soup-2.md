## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg beef rib or marrow bone
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 small parsnip
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 2 carrots
- 1 small leek
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth, wrung out in cold water, and chop into four pieces.
2. Place it in cold water, bring quickly to a boil and skim well.
3. Add the salt and the prepared vegetables and let the soup cook with a tightly closed lid for 3-4 hours or until the meat is very tender.
4. When the vegetables and leek are soft, remove them, cut with a vegetable knife into nice pieces and place in the soup bowl.
5. Strain the soup and skim off the fat well.
6. Return it to the pot, add the parsley, let the soup come to a boil, season with salt and pepper and pour into the soup bowl.
7. Serve the meat separately with horseradish sauce or also in the soup, cut into small pieces.
