## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. karolina-risgryn (2 hg.)
- 3 msk. smör (60 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. mjölk
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 msk. socker (30 gr.)
- 20 sötmandlar
- 10 bittermandlar
- 40 gr. vanliga russin
- 3 ägg
- 1/2 msk. smör för formen (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the rice grains as in recipe no. 794.
2. Pour the porridge and immediately mix with the cold butter, spices, the scalded and ground almonds, and the well-rinsed, blanched and pitted raisins.
3. When the porridge is cold, stir in the egg yolks and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1 hour.
5. Serve warm with jam, compote or fruit sauce.
