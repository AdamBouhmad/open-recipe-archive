## Ingredients

- 80 gr. butter
- 80 gr. flour
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 5–6 eggs
- 60 gr. grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- (1/4 tsp. mustard)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried 2 minutes, the milk is added and the sauce is allowed to boil 5 minutes.
2. It is poured into a bowl; when it has cooled, the egg yolks are added, one at a time, and the mixture is stirred 10 minutes.
3. The grated cheese is mixed in together with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. The mixture is tasted, poured into a buttered and breadcrumbed pie form and baked in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
5. The soufflé is served at once as a middle course with stirred butter or to the smörgåsbord.
6. The mixture can also be baked in scallop shells or small forms and served then instead of cheese and crackers.
