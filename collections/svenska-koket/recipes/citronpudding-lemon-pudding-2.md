## Ingredients

- Zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water, for the gelatin
- 3 dcl. extra thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the lemon. Remove the zest neatly and press out the juice.
2. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the lemon zest and juice.
3. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces and dissolve in the hot water.
4. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
5. Slowly stir the gelatin into the egg mixture, then fold in the cream very carefully.
6. Fill the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted porcelain mold and let set on ice for 3-4 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edges with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass plate lined with parchment paper. Garnish with cookies.
