## Ingredients

- 3 kg apples
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 4 parts water
- juice and zest of 1 lemon
- 1 gram salicylic acid

## Directions

1. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup and pour it out to cool.
2. Peel the apples and cut them into very thin quarters; remove the cores.
3. As the apples are peeled, place them in the cold syrup to preserve their color.
4. Place on heat and allow to cook until they fall apart and form a pulp.
5. If the pulp is not sufficiently sour, add the lemon juice.
6. When the pulp is ready, stir in the salicylic acid.
7. Place the apple pulp hot into the crock.
8. To prevent the pulp from molding, once it has cooled, cover it with a thin layer of lingonberry jam or cover it with parchment paper dipped in cognac.
