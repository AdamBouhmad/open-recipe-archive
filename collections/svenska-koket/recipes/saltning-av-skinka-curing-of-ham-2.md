## Ingredients

- 1 skinka (ham)
- 4 msk. salt
- 2 msk. socker (sugar)
- 1 tsk. kalisalpeter (potassium nitrate)
- 12 lit. vatten (water)
- 1.3 kg. smörsalt (butter salt)
- 26 gr. kalisalpeter (potassium nitrate)
- 160 gr. strösocker (powdered sugar)

## Directions

1. Rub the ham with a mixture of salt, saltpeter and sugar.
2. From the water, salt, saltpeter and sugar, boil a brine, which is well skimmed and poured up to cool.
3. When the brine is cold, place the ham in it and let it remain for 14 days.
