## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1 dcl. mushroom broth
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the mushrooms into slices or dice and fry them in the butter until it is clear, then squeeze out the mushroom with a spoon and fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
2. Add the meat stock and mushroom broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, add the fried mushrooms and season the sauce with spices.
3. If desired, a little madeira or white wine may be added.
