## Ingredients

- 1 litre cooked potatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoon flour (15 gr.)
- 3-4 decilitres good milk
- salt
- 2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes with the skins on, let them cool, peel them and cut them into thin slices.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
3. Thin the milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Add the potato slices and let the stew cook slowly for about 5 minutes, after which season it with salt and the finely chopped parsley.
5. Serve with salted salmon, fried fish and meat, pickled sausage, etc.
