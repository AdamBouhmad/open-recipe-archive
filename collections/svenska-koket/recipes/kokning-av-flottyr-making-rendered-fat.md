## Ingredients

- ⅓ kg kidney tallow
- ⅓ kg pork fat
- water
- 1 potato
- 1 apple

## Directions

1. Cut the tallow and fat into fine cubes and let soak in water for about 24 hours
2. Squeeze the cubes well and put on to cook in new, cold water
3. Peel and halve a potato or apple and add to the fat, which will thereby become clearer
4. Let the fat cook until a thin, bluish smoke rises from the surface and the cubes (the "cracklings") sink to the bottom
5. Strain into a clean earthenware crock and store in a cool place
6. Note: Fat can also be made from kidney tallow alone but will be harder
