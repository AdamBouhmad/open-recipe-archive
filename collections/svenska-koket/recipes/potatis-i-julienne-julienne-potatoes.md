## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 kg fat/lard
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. The potatoes should be oblong and of even size.
2. Wash them, brush them clean and peel thinly, then cut them into thread-fine strips.
3. Place the strips in water as they are cut.
4. Dry them well in a cloth, cook them as in the previous recipe and they should be properly crisp.
5. Serve with poultry and game.
