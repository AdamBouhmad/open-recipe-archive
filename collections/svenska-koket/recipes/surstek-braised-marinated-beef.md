## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef round, inner or outer
- 2 tbsp salt (30 g)
- 2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp crushed cloves
- For the marinade: 2 1/2 dcl good vinegar
- 2 liters good weak broth
- 150 g sugar
- 15 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 red onions, sliced
- For braising: 3 hg lard
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1 liter boiling beef stock
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- 1 tbsp soy sauce

## Directions

1. The meat is well beaten with a damp meat mallet and rubbed with a mixture of salt and crushed spices.
2. It is placed in a crock and covered with a marinade of vinegar, broth, sugar, whole spices and onion, all well mixed together.
3. The meat is allowed to lie for 6-8 days and should be turned often during this time.
4. It is then removed, well rinsed, dried with a cloth, larded (see page 232), and tied so that it has a nice shape.
5. It is then braised and served as in no. 471; first diluted with the stock, then with the boiling milk mixed with soy sauce, and braised for 3 1/2-4 hours.
6. The sauce is served reduced, mixed with cream (see no. 429).
