## Ingredients

- 1 piece of salmon weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 5 dill stalks
- 1 1/2 liters of fish stock
- 10-12 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 4 tablespoons good white wine
- 5 dill stalks
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- dill

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, rinse well and dry with a fish cloth.
2. Rub it with half of the salt and let it rest for 1/2 hour, dry it again and place in the cold water with the remaining salt, spices and dill stalks, and let it simmer slowly until done or approximately 15 minutes.
3. Remove it immediately, pull off the skin and divide the fish into 2 fillets.
4. Place the bones in the stock, which should cook for another 20 minutes.
5. Cut the fish into beautiful pieces.
6. Prepare gelée from the fish stock with the dill stalks as in recipe no. 181. When it is cold, add the wine.
7. Prepare the dish and serve to the smörgåsbord or as a course with genuine mayonnaise.
