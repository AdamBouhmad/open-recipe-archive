## Ingredients

- Small fish, fish slices or small fillets
- Salted milk
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Fine bread crumbs
- Deep fat (see recipes 43 and 44)
- Parsley
- Lemons

## Directions

1. If the fish is thick, after cleaning make a few deep crosswise cuts before breading to facilitate even cooking.
2. For flatfish, partially detach the two lower fillets from the backbone instead of scoring them.
3. Before frying, dip the fish in salted milk and roll in flour, or bread them by rolling in flour, dipping in beaten egg, and rolling in fine bread crumbs.
4. Tap with a knife blade so the breading adheres well, and score crosswise with the back of a knife if desired for a neater appearance.
5. Heat the deep fat in a frying pan or long pan, place the fish in and fry until a beautiful golden brown, then place on absorbent paper so the fat drains off.
6. Arrange on a folded napkin or parchment paper and garnish with parsley fried in deep fat, and neatly cut lemon halves or fluted lemon slices.
