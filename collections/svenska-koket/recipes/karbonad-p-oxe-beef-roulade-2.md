## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef (flank)
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 cup crushed hardtack
- 3 tbsp butter
- 6 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into slices across the grain.
2. Beat the slices lightly on both sides, sprinkle with pepper and salt, and turn them in the crushed hardtack.
3. Brown the meat slices in butter, then place them in a pot and dilute with boiling water, after which let them stew slowly for about 1 1/2 hours.
4. Serve the roulade with its sauce.
