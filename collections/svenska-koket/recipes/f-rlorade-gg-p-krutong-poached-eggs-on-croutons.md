## Ingredients

- 6 poached eggs
- 6 slices of wheat bread
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1 tablespoon good meat stock
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms smoked ham
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the bread slices into elongated pieces, hollow them out and fry them a beautiful golden brown in the butter.
2. Place them on a hot plate and put one egg on each slice.
3. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the cream and meat stock, then let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Taste the sauce and season with the spices, then pour it over the eggs.
5. Cut the ham into fine strips, fry in butter and sprinkle in a ring around each egg.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve as a breakfast course.
