## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- powdered sugar, equal weight to the eggs
- wheat flour, 1/2 the weight of the eggs
- lemon zest or vanilla sugar
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs and sugar with a whisk for about 45 minutes.
2. Sift the flour and add it, then season the batter with 1 tsp. finely grated lemon zest or 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar.
3. Pour into a buttered and rusk-strewn ring mold or frying pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 40 minutes.
