## Ingredients

- 750 g boneless beef chuck
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 2-3 red onions or 1 large Portuguese onion
- 1-2 dcl beef broth

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and run through a meat grinder twice.
2. Form the ground meat into thin or thick patties depending on preference, chop them with a knife, even out the edges well and sprinkle with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Fry the patties and serve as in recipe no. 483.
