## Ingredients

- 1 calf head
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 small piece ginger
- 2 red onions
- 2 carrots
- 1 celery
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters veal and beef broth
- 1 knife tip cayenne pepper
- browned sugar
- 2 tablespoons sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Split the calf head and remove the brain
2. Rinse the head well and soak it in water for about 12 hours
3. Remove it and place it on the heat in enough cold water to cover it
4. Add the salt, and when the liquid boils, skim it well and add the seasonings and vegetables
5. Let the soup cook until the meat begins to loosen from the bones, then remove it and strain the broth
6. Separate the meat from the bones, cut it, and when it is quite cold, cut it into small pieces
7. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it in the butter, add the flour and broth, and let the soup cook 10–15 minutes
8. Season with a little cayenne pepper and wine and serve with the cut meat
