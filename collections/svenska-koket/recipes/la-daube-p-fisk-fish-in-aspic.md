## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 kg fish
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1 liter fish stock
- 4 tablespoons white French wine
- 2 tablespoons water
- 10 sheets gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the fish in a short stock in the usual way
2. Put the fish stock together with the wine and the gelatin dissolved in a little hot water
3. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the lightly beaten egg whites, and let the aspic come to a brief boil while whisking
4. Strain the stock through a cloth wrung out in hot water
5. Pour the clear aspic on the bottom of a rinsed aspic mold and set it aside to set
6. Garnish the mold with parsley and fill it with the fish, cut into beautiful pieces
7. Pour the rest of the aspic carefully over, and let the à-la-daube set in a cold place
