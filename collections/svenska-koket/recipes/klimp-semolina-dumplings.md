## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 4 cups milk
- 8 bitter almonds
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 hectogram sweet almonds for garnish
- 1 1/2 bunches chopped parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and flour together, gradually thin with the milk, and let the mixture cook for 15 minutes
2. Lift the pan from the side of the heat; add the blanched and grated bitter almond, sugar and the whisked eggs
3. Let the klimp simmer over heat until it becomes thick
4. Cool it in a water-rinsed mold and turn it out once it has cooled
5. When served with fruit soup, garnish with the blanched sweet almond cut into strips
6. When used with bouillon, garnish with chopped parsley
