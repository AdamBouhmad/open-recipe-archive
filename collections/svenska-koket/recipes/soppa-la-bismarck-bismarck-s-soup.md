## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters dark broth
- 15 g mondamin
- salt
- cayenne
- 1/2 deciliter sherry or madeira
- 1 can asparagus tips (1/4 liter)
- quenelles of chicken forcemeat

## Directions

1. Let the broth come to a boil, thicken it with the mondamin flour stirred in 2 tablespoon cold water, and let it cook with stirring for 5 minutes.
2. Season the soup with spices and wine. Add the asparagus tips, which have been heated in a water bath, and the quenelles to the soup bowl and pour the soup over. Serve very hot.
