## Ingredients

- Fresh beans, newly picked and tender
- 4-5 hg coarse rock salt per kg beans

## Directions

1. The beans should be newly picked and tender. Remove the ends, rinse them, let them drain on linen and cut them diagonally into 8-4 mm thick slices.
2. Layer the slices alternately with salt in a wooden tub or crock and press down gently with a wooden pestle.
3. Place a thick layer of salt on top.
4. Add a bottom and a light weight, cover the vessel well and store in a cellar.
5. When the beans are to be served, remove the undissolved salt crystals, then boil the beans in several changes of water until they reach the desired saltiness.
6. In the last water, add a little sugar.
