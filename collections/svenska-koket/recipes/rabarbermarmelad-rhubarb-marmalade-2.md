## Ingredients

- Rhubarb
- 750 gr. sugar per 1 kg. rhubarb

## Directions

1. The stalks are rinsed, cut in pieces, weighed and laid in layers with the sugar in an enameled jam pot.
2. The mass is cooked thereafter while stirring, until it falls in flakes from the spoon.
3. Pour into small warm jars, which are bound when the marmalade is cold.
