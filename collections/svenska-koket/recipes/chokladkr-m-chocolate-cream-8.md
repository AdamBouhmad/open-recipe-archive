## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 2 deciliters milk
- 35 grams cocoa powder
- 1.5 deciliters granulated sugar (128 grams)
- 5 leaves gelatin, 3 tablespoons water
- 3 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg in a saucepan together with the milk, cocoa and sugar.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, dissolve it in 3 tablespoons of hot water and add it, after which the cream is allowed to simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking.
3. Pour the cream out and whisk it until it is cold, then gently fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2–3 hours.
5. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a glass dish.
6. Garnish with whipped cream and serve with it as well, if desired.
