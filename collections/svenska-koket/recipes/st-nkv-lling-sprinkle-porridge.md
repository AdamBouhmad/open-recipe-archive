## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 g)
- 1 3/4 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs for 3 minutes, add the flour and butter and work the mixture until smooth and supple, then let it rest for 1 hour.
2. Bring the milk to a boil with the cinnamon and sugar.
3. Press the dough through a colander with not too small holes into the milk, which should only simmer gently.
4. Move the pot to the side of the stove and let it stand covered for about 10 minutes, then season the porridge with salt.
