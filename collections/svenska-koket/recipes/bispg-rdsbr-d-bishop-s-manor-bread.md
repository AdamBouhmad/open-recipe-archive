## Ingredients

- 210 g butter
- 210 g granulated sugar
- 125 g sweet almond
- 375 g wheat flour
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 25 g sweet almond
- 1 dcl pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and work out the water well. Stir it with the sugar for 5 minutes, then add the blanched and ground almond. Finally work in the sifted flour, except for 75 g, which is saved for rolling out. Roll out the dough to 1/2 cm thickness and cut out with a pointed mold into cookies, which are brushed with lightly beaten egg white and sprinkled with blanched and ground almond and sugar. Bake them until beautifully golden brown in not too warm an oven.
