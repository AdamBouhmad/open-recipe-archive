## Ingredients

- 3 liters red, half-ripe gooseberries
- 1/2 liter water
- sugar equal in weight to the juice

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the berries and cook them very slowly with the water until they begin to burst
2. Strain through a sieve cloth and let drain until the following day
3. Weigh the juice and dissolve the sugar in the cold juice
4. Bring to a boil and cook rapidly for 15-20 minutes, skimming carefully
5. The jelly is ready when a drop of it, poured onto a plate, sets quickly
