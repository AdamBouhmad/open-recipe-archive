## Ingredients

- 1 small white cabbage head
- 3 litres water, 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.) for blanching
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- 3 decilitres boiling broth
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1-2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer green leaves from the cabbage head and loosen the remaining leaves from the stem, freeing them from the thickest part of the main vein.
2. Blanch the cabbage (place in boiling salted water and let it cook 8-10 minutes without a lid), remove with a slotted spoon, let it drain on a cloth, and cut into cubes or strips.
3. Brown the butter and sugar in a pot, add the cabbage, pour the soy sauce over it, and brown with frequent stirring until it gets a nice color.
4. Add the broth and let the cabbage cook slowly with a lid until completely tender, or about 3 hours.
5. Season with salt and more sugar if desired.
6. Serve with boiled or roasted pork and boiled sausage.
