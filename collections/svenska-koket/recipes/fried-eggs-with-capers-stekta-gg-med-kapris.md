## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped capers

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in half of the butter and place on a warm plate.
2. Lightly brown the remaining butter, season with vinegar and pour over the eggs.
3. Mix the parsley and capers together and sprinkle over.
4. Serve to the smorgasbord.
