## Ingredients

- 2 hg. wheat flour
- 2 hg. rye sieve or coarse rye flour
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 75 gr. cold butter
- milk (approx. 2 dcl.)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with sugar and salt.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes into the flour and work it in with fingertips.
3. Add enough milk so that it becomes a reasonably firm dough, which is worked until it becomes smooth and shiny and releases from the bowl.
4. Roll out the dough very thinly, prick lightly, and cut with a pastry wheel along a ruler into oblong rectangles, which are placed on buttered baking sheets and baked light brown in a not too hot oven.
5. Suitable for open sandwiches and sandwich bread.
