## Ingredients

- 7 egg whites
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 hg. sugar
- 150-160 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg whites and egg yolk with the sugar until light foam is formed.
2. Add the flour, which should be sifted, and work the batter well.
3. Prepare the crusts as described in the preceding recipe.
4. Serve with coffee or tea.
