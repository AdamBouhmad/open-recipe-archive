## Ingredients

- 3 kilograms reindeer roast
- 4 liters drink (marinade liquid)
- 2 kilograms lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1–1.5 liters boiling broth or water
- 1.5 tablespoons butter for sauce
- 3 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 1–2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. If the roast is frozen, let it lie in the drink until completely thawed.
2. Remove it, rinse well, dry with a cloth, and carefully remove all skin.
3. Lard the roast in close rows with the lard cut into fine strips.
4. Heat the butter in a roasting pan and brown the roast well in it.
5. Salt it and add the broth a little at a time.
6. Baste often and turn a couple of times during roasting.
7. Roasting takes 3–4 hours.
8. Strain and skim the meat drippings, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
