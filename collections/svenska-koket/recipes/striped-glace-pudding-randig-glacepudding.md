## Ingredients

- Three different kinds of glace, such as vanilla, pistachio and chocolate
- 1/2 batch of each

## Directions

1. Once frozen in a machine, layer the different glace varieties alternately in a glace mold
2. Pack the mold in ice and salt
3. If desired, preserved fruit can be laid between the layers
