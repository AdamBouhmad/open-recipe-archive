## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter brown beans
- 1 carrot
- 1 small celery
- 2 cloves
- 2 liters water
- 1/4 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the beans and place them together with the prepared vegetables in soft water with salt.
2. When they are soft, strain them.
3. Fry butter and flour together, add the purée and broth, and let the soup cook for 10-15 minutes, then taste and adjust seasoning.
4. Serve with fried bread or cooked rice grains.
