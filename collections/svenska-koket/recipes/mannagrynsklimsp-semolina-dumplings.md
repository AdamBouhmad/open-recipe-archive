## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter good milk
- 0.25 cup semolina
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 0.5 cup sultana raisins
- 2 egg whites
- 2 bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil, rinse the semolina well and add it with constant stirring along with the sugar, cleaned and blanched sultana raisins, and grated almond
2. Cook until thick (approximately 0.5 hour), then remove the pot from the fire and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
3. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed porcelain mold, let it cool, and turn it out
4. Serve with fruit soup
