## Ingredients

- 1½ kg halibut
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 teaspoons white pepper
- vinegar
- 3 tablespoons butter for roasting
- 3 cups fish broth
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1½ teaspoons prepared English mustard
- 1 teaspoon soy

## Directions

1. Free the halibut from skin and bones, and cook broth on these.
2. Cut the fish into 3 cm thick slices, which are lightly pounded and placed on a serving platter.
3. Sprinkle the slices with salt, pepper and, if desired, a little finely chopped onion.
4. Pour enough vinegar over so that it is level with the fish.
5. After two hours, remove the slices and dry well with a cloth.
6. Brown them in the butter and place on a hot platter.
7. Stir the flour in the pan, add the fish broth, soy and mustard, and let the sauce come to a good boil, taste and pour over the fish.
8. Place it on the serving platter and garnish with parsley and small butter pastry squares.
