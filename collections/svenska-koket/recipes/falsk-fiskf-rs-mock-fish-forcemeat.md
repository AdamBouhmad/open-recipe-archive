## Ingredients

- 1 hg. butter
- 4 dcl. sour milk
- 1 hg. flour
- 6-7 eggs
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- For the mold: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.), 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- Lye fish (stockfish prepared with lye)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and mix with the crushed rusks. Add the lye fish together with the well-beaten eggs.
2. Bring the milk to a boil and pour it hot over the mixture, then stir well.
3. Season with the spices, pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold holding about 1 1/2 lit., cover with the lid and cook the forcemeat in a water bath in the oven or on top of the stove for 1-1 1/2 hours.
4. Turn out and serve immediately with crayfish or lobster sauce.
