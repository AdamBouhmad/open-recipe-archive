## Ingredients

- 150 gr. cooked potatoes
- 150 gr. cooked celery
- 1/2 small cooked leek
- 75 gr. salt beef tongue or salt meat
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 2 good apples
- 2 good pears
- 1/2 hectogram truffle
- Genuine mayonnaise: 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt, cayenne
- 1/4 teaspoon French mustard
- 1½ decilitres oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- For the mold: 2 decilitres thick, reduced tomato purée
- For garnishing: 2 tomatoes, in slices

## Directions

1. Cut all ingredients into fine cubes and mix in the mayonnaise, which is prepared as in no. 10.
2. Line a smooth or fluted mold with a lid with the tomato purée, add the salad, cover with butter paper and lid and freeze in ice and salt.
3. When the salad is to be turned out, dip the mold for a moment in hot water.
4. Turn the salad out onto a cake server in a crystal bowl, garnish with tomato slices and serve with cold, roasted fowl.
