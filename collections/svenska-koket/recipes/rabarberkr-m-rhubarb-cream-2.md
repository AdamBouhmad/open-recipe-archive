## Ingredients

- 5 rhubarb stalks (approximately 7 hectograms)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons mondamin flour (23 grams) or 1/2 tablespoon potato flour (23 grams)
- 1/2 litre water
- 2 1/2 decilitres crushed sugar (238 grams)

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the rhubarb, peel very thinly and cut into 2 centimetre long pieces.
2. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water, add the rhubarb and let it cook until soft but not falling apart.
3. Mix the flour with 2 tablespoons cold water and add it, then let the cream cook 5 minutes if thickened with mondamin, but 2 minutes if thickened with potato flour.
4. Pour it into a compote dish rinsed with lukewarm water and sprinkle with powdered sugar to prevent a skin from forming.
5. Serve with cream or milk.
