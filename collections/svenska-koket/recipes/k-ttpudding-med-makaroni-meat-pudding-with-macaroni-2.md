## Ingredients

- ¼ kg macaroni
- 1½ liters milk
- salt and sugar
- ½ liter cooked or roasted chopped meat and ham
- ½ medium Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 parts broth and leftover roast sauce
- 3-4 tablespoons grated strong cheese
- 3 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- ½ tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni tender in the milk, season and pour out to cool.
2. Finely chop the onion and brown it together with the meat in the butter.
3. Add broth or roast sauce, layer the macaroni and meat alternately in the buttered pie dish; the first and last layer should be macaroni.
4. Sprinkle the pudding with the grated cheese and with crushed bread crumbs and place in moderate oven heat to get a nice brown color.
5. This takes about ¾ hour.
6. Serve with clarified butter or roast sauce.
