## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- 7 egg whites
- 150 gr. caster sugar
- 150-160 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 kg. deep fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolk and four of the egg whites with the sugar until white and fluffy.
2. Sift the flour and carefully fold it into the batter.
3. Lastly, fold in the remaining egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Heat the deep fat in a pot, no more than a small teacup full.
5. Through a pastry horn or ordinary funnel, drizzle a tablespoon of the batter back and forth into the fat so that it forms a latticed cake.
6. When the pastry is golden brown on the underside, turn it with a slotted spoon, and when it has achieved an even, beautiful color, remove it, bend it over a stick, and let it drain on gray paper.
7. Sprinkle the pastries with sugar before serving.
