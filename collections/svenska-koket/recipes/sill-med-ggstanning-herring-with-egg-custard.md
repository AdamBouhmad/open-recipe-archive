## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, skin them and soak them in water the day before use.
2. Wrap them in a fish towel or white paper and remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Cut each fillet into 6 pieces.
4. Butter a low heatproof mold, lay the herring pieces in it and pour the egg custard over them.
5. Sprinkle parsley on top, place the mold in a warm oven and let it stand until the custard has taken on a beautiful light brown color.
6. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
