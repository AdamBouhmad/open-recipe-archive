## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram calf thymus
- 1 liter water for blanching
- 2 teaspoons salt for blanching
- 1 hectogram macaroni
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Blanch the thymus as in the previous recipe.
2. Cut it into slices and season.
3. Cook the macaroni in water with salt until tender.
4. Prepare according to the previous recipe's method.
5. Serve as a breakfast dish.
