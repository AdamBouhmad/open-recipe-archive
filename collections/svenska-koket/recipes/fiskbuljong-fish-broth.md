## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg fish
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 carrots
- 1 small celery
- 1 parsnip
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 6 white peppercorns
- 3 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish in the usual way, rinse well and dry with a cloth, cut into pieces and place in cold water with the salt.
2. When it comes to a boil, skim well and add the prepared vegetables, spices and butter.
3. Let the broth slowly cook with a tightly closed lid for about 2 hours.
4. Strain and use either clarified, with vegetables, or as a base for purées.
5. Using salt-water fish makes the best broth.
