## Ingredients

- 2 kg. berries
- 2 kg. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them quickly in a sieve and let them drain well.
2. Layer them alternately with the sugar in an enameled jam pot, cover, and let stand for a day, during which the sugar should be nearly dissolved in the fruit juice.
3. Place the pot about 10 minutes to the side of the fire, then move it over a gentle fire.
4. When the jam comes to a boil, lift the pot from the fire and shake gently so the foam can be removed.
5. Put the pot back on and let the jam cook slowly until it is clear (about 20 minutes).
6. During cooking, shake the pot often and skim the jam carefully.
7. Pour the jam immediately into warmed jars rinsed with brandy, and let them stand in a cool, airy room until the jam is cold.
8. Cover with parchment paper dipped in brandy.
9. Bind the jars with parchment paper as previously described.
