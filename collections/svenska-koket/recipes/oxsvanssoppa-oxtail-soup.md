## Ingredients

- 1 oxtail
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 small shallot
- 1 small carrot
- 3 parsley stems
- 10 allspice berries
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters broth
- white pepper
- salt
- 3 tablespoons sherry

## Directions

1. Wash the oxtail with hot water and dry well
2. Cut it into pieces at the joints
3. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the oxtail pieces together with the peeled onion, the rinsed and scraped carrot cut into pieces, the parsley stems, and the allspice berries
4. Let everything brown slowly with stirring for 1–2 hours, then add the flour and continue browning for another 1/2 hour
5. Pour in the broth and let the meat cook until completely tender
6. Strain and skim the soup well and season with the wine, salt, and white pepper
7. Serve the tail pieces in the soup
