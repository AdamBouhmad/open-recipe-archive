## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 1/2 decilitres milk
- 25 gr. sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
- 175 gr. flour
- 5 whole eggs
- cream filling or vanilla-flavored whipped cream

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan with a rounded bottom, pour in the milk and let it come to a boil with sugar and lemon zest.
2. Whisk in the flour during full boiling and let the mixture cook until it pulls away from the pan.
3. Remove from heat, let cool slightly, then stir in the eggs one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
4. The mixture can either be piped through a fluted pastry tube onto a well-buttered baking sheet into oblong pastries (éclairs), which are split after baking and filled with some cream, or placed with a spoon into round buns (choux); these are filled with whipped cream flavored with vanilla.
5. Bake immediately in a not-too-hot oven, taking care not to open the oven door until after 8–10 minutes.
