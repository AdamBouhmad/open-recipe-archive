## Ingredients

- Firm cucumbers with small seeds
- Pounded coarse rock salt

## Directions

1. Peel firm cucumbers with small seeds.
2. Cut them from flower to stem into thin slices.
3. Layer the slices alternately with pounded rock salt in glass jars.
4. Place a thick layer of salt on top and bind the jars well.
5. When the cucumbers are to be used, drain them and prepare as if fresh.
