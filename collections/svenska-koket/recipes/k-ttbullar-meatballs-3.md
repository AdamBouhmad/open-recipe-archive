## Ingredients

- 300 g boneless beef
- 200 g boneless veal
- 100 g lard bacon
- 2-3 cups milk
- 1-2 eggs
- 1/2 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped Portuguese onion
- 4 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with the lard into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder.
2. Whisk the eggs and milk, add the crushed breadcrumbs and let them swell.
3. Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in 1/2 tablespoon of the butter.
4. Mix the soaked breadcrumbs and spices into the forcemeat.
5. Work it until it becomes cohesive like a dough.
6. Finally add the browned onion.
7. Brown the butter in a frying pan, form the meatballs with spoons dipped in lukewarm water, and brown them in the hot butter.
8. Add 1 cup of boiling water, cover, and let the meatballs cook through for 1.5 hours.
