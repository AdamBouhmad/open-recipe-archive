## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoon flour (30 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon English mustard
- 3 deciliter water
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoon fine oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the butter, flour and mustard for 2 minutes.
2. Gradually add the water and let the sauce cook with stirring for 10 minutes.
