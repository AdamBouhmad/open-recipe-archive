## Ingredients

- 2 liters paradise apples
- 1 kg granulated or granulated sugar
- 2 parts vinegar
- 7 parts water
- 6 cloves

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, prick with a silver fork, and cut the stems off halfway.
2. From the sugar, water, vinegar and spices cook a clear brine, which is poured hot over the apples.
3. The next day pour off the brine, let it come to a good boil with skimming and pour it hot over the fruit again.
4. This is repeated several times until the brine becomes syrupy.
