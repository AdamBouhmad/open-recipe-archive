## Ingredients

- 75 grams sweet almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 3 tablespoons granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Dry the almonds on a cloth, but do not blanch them
2. Chop them coarsely and stir together with the sugar in the beaten egg whites
3. Spread the batter thinly on a well-cleaned and buttered or waxed baking sheet
4. Bake golden brown in weak oven heat
5. While the sheet is still hot, cut the cake into square pieces and bend them over a stick
