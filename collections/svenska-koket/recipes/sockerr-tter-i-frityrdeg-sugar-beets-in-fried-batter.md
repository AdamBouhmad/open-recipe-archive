## Ingredients

- 24 fine even sugar beets
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 80 grams dry flour
- 3 grams salt
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter (10 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter of the cooking liquid
- 1/2 deciliter milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 kilogram lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sugar beets as in the preceding recipe and let them drain on linen.
2. For the fried batter, whisk together all the ingredients to a thick pancake batter, which should stand and swell for about 2-3 hours.
3. Cut the sugar beets in half, take them with a fork and dip them into the batter, which must completely cover them, and cook in the lard until they are beautifully golden brown.
4. Let them drain on brown paper, arrange on a plate with a broken napkin or wax paper and serve very hot with stirred butter.
