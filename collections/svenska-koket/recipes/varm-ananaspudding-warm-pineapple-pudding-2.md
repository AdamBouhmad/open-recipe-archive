## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- 75 gr. sugar
- 50 gr. wheat flour
- 15 gr. mondamin or cornstarch
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 50 gr. butter
- For the mold: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.), 2 tbsp. crushed breadcrumbs
- For the sauce: 1 1/2 dcl. pineapple juice, 1 1/2 dcl. French wine, 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.), 1 tsp. mondamin flour (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Half of the pineapple is sufficient for this pudding. The remainder can be used for cold pudding or mixed into compote.
