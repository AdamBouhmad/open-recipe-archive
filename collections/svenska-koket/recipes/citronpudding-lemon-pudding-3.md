## Ingredients

- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water for the gelatin
- 3 dcl. extra thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the lemon. Zest it neatly and press out the juice. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the lemon zest and juice. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the hot water. Whip the cream to stiff peaks.
