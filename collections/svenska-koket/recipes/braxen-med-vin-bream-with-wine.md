## Ingredients

- 2 kg bream
- 1 kg butter
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 4 parts French wine
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, rinse well and dry it.
2. Melt the butter in a fish kettle without a strainer, place the fish in it and sprinkle with the crushed rusks; add salt and sugar, pour in the wine, and let the fish simmer slowly on low heat.
3. When the fish is done, remove it to a serving dish.
4. Strain the sauce and bind it with the egg yolks.
