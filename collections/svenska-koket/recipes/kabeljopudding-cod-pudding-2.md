## Ingredients

- 4 hg. cod
- 2 dcl. rice grains (2 hg.)
- 1 1/2 lit. milk
- 3 msk. cold butter (60 gr.)
- 2-3 eggs
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. butter for mold (10 gr.)
- 3 msk. crushed rusks for mold

## Directions

1. Cook the cod as in recipe no. 393, allow to cool and chop finely or pass twice through a meat grinder.
2. Cook the rice grains and prepare the pudding and serve as in the preceding recipe.
3. Any sort of fish, cooked or fried, can be used for this pudding.
