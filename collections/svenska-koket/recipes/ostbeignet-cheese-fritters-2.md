## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 1 dcl. water or milk
- 60 gr. flour
- 25 gr. grated cheese
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 kg. cream (for cooking)

## Directions

1. Butter and water are brought to a boil, flour is added with steady whisking and the batter is allowed to boil until it releases from the pan, then it is poured out and the whisk is squeezed out well.
2. When the mass has cooled, the grated cheese is added and the eggs are worked in one at a time.
3. The mass is stirred for 10 minutes, then formed with two teaspoons, which are dipped in cream, into small balls the size of a hazelnut.
4. These are cooked golden brown in the cream and laid out on greaseproof paper to drain.
5. The cream should not be too hot, otherwise the balls cannot swell.
6. Served lukewarm, arranged on pastry paper, with consommé or bouillon.
