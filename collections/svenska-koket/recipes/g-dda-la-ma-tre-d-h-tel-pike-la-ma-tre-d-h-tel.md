## Ingredients

- 3 pike each weighing 425 gr.
- 3 teaspoons salt (15 gr.)
- 3 1/2 tablespoons butter (70 gr.)
- 3 sheets white paper
- Maître d'hôtel butter: 1 small bunch parsley, 1 hectogram butter, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; keep the head but remove the gills
2. Rub with salt and let rest for 1 hour, then dry thoroughly
3. Cut paper sheets into long rectangles and butter them with the butter; wrap the fish well and place in a buttered long pan
4. Roast in a hot oven for about 20 minutes
5. Remove the paper only when the fish is placed on the serving platter to preserve the sauce that has formed
6. Cover the fish inside and out with slices of maître d'hôtel butter, prepared as in recipe n:o 406
7. Garnish with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes
