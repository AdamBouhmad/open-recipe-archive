## Ingredients

- 2 kg. short-chopped saddle
- 3 liters skim milk or drink
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 2 deciliters finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The saddle is prepared and cooked according to the method for the venison roast, being larded and roasted. It is served with fried potatoes.
