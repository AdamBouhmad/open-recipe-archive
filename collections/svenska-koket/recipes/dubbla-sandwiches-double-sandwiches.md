## Ingredients

- 1 25-öre loaf of wheat bread
- 2 1/2 hg. butter
- 1/2 tsp. French mustard
- 1/2 hg. cooked ham
- 1/2 hg. tongue
- 1/2 hg. veal roast
- 1 pickled cucumber
- 1 hg. smoked salmon
- 1 hg. Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. The bread should be baked the day before.
2. Cut the bread into thin slices lengthwise, trimming the edges.
3. Mix the butter with the mustard until it becomes fluffy.
4. Spread the bread slices with this mixture on both sides and lay them on top of each other with a layer of cold cuts between them.
5. On top of the veal roast, lay the pickled cucumber cut into thin slices.
6. Only spread butter on one side of the first and last bread slices.
7. Place the sandwiches under pressure between two cutting boards; place a light weight on the top board.
8. After 2 hours the bread can be cut into thin slices, these are then shaped and served as in the previous recipe.
9. Dark rye bread can also be used together with the wheat bread for these sandwiches.
