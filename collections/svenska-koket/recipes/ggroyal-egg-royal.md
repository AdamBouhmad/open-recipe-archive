## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling cream or consommé
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Whip the egg yolks and whites quickly
2. Pour in the boiling cream or consommé while whisking and skim the egg custard
3. Pour into a well-buttered, smooth timbal mold with a lid, which is placed in a saucepan of boiling water
4. Place the saucepan to the side of the heat so that the water stays hot without boiling, otherwise the custard becomes tough and cannot be used
5. When it has set, after approximately 35 minutes, it is done
6. Remove the mold carefully and let the royal cool in its mold
7. Turn out onto a napkin, wipe the top, and cut into 1/2 cm. thick slices
8. Cut the slices into beautiful shapes or small balls with fluted or smooth cutters
9. Serve as garnish in clear broth or consommé
