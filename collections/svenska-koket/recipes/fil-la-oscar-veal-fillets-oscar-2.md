## Ingredients

- 6 hg. veal fillet or boneless veal loin
- 2 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 2 1/2 msk. butter (50 gr.) for frying
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 1 dcl. asparagus puree
- 4 dcl. cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- 1 can asparagus (1/4 liter)
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. sugar
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper

## Directions

1. Dry the fillet with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into 1 cm. thick slices, which are pounded on both sides and sprinkled with pepper and salt.
2. For the sauce, fry butter and flour 2 minutes, add asparagus puree and cream, and let the sauce boil while stirring for 5 minutes.
3. Cut the lobster and asparagus into pieces (the most beautiful pieces are set aside for garnish) and place in the sauce, which is then brought to a boil and seasoned with the spices.
4. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the fillets in and fry quickly on both sides.
5. Arrange on a hot serving platter and pour the finished sauce over the meat.
6. Garnish the dish with the lobster, asparagus, and parsley and serve immediately with potatoes as the course.
