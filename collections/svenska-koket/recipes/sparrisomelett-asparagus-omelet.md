## Ingredients

- 1 can omelet asparagus (1/4 lit.)
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1/2 dcl. asparagus liquid
- 3 dcl. cream
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. sugar
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, thin the asparagus liquid and cream while stirring and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the asparagus and let the stewing come to a boil and season with the spices.
3. Prepare the omelet as in the previous recipe, fill it with the stewing, turn it out, garnish with parsley and serve for the smorgasbord.
