## Ingredients

- 5 kg veal roast
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 12 allspice berries
- 2–2 1/2 liters broth or water
- 2 tbsp butter
- 5 tbsp flour
- meat jus
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Cut off the flank and break the backbone, if the roast is so large that the loin section is included.
3. Grease a roasting pan with butter, place the roast in with the inside up and put in a hot oven to brown.
4. When browned, remove it, salt it and turn it over.
5. Add the boiling liquid, put the roast back in the oven to cook through and baste it with its jus every ten minutes. The roasting takes 3–4 hours.
6. Strain the meat jus, skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
