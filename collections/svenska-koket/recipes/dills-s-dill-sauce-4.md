## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 5 deciliters meat stock
- 1—2 tablespoons finely chopped dill
- 1—2 tablespoons vinegar
- 2—3 teaspoons sugar (10—15 gr.)
- salt
- 1—2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Melt 1 tablespoon of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes
2. Add the stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
3. Add the chopped dill, season the sauce with vinegar and spices
4. Add the beaten egg yolk and let the sauce simmer under vigorous stirring but do not boil, as it curdles easily
5. Finish the sauce with the remainder of the cold butter
