## Ingredients

- 2 lit. tender nettles
- 1 small bunch chives
- 6 msk. flour
- 2 msk. butter
- 2 lit. good broth
- white pepper
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the nettles well, rinse them and blanch them in salted boiling water for approximately 15 minutes.
2. Drain the water, rinse the nettles with fresh water, and lay them on a sieve to drain.
3. Chop the nettles finely together with the cleaned and rinsed chives and sprinkle with flour.
4. Fry them in the butter, and add the hot broth.
5. Let the cabbage now cook very slowly with a tightly closed lid for approximately 3/4 hour.
6. Skim well, season with the spices and serve with poached eggs.
