## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 salt herring
- 2 cup cooked or fried meat
- 2 cup cooked beets
- 2 1/2 cup cooked potatoes
- 1 salt cucumber
- 2 apples
- 4-6 tablespoon vinegar
- white pepper
- sugar
- 2 eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. The herring is soaked in water the day before, cleaned and freed from skin and bones, wrapped in a cloth and cut into small cubes
2. The salt cucumber and the raw, peeled apples are cut like the meat, beets and potatoes into cubes
3. Everything is mixed well, and vinegar, sugar and pepper are added
4. The herring salad is placed loosely packed on the serving platter and garnished with finely chopped beets and hard-boiled eggs
5. Served with cream or sharp sauce
