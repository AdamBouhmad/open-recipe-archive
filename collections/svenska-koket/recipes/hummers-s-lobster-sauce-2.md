## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. fish broth
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1–2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The sauce is prepared as in the previous recipe
2. Cut the lobster into small pieces; once it is added, stir the sauce very carefully so that the lobster does not break apart
3. If fresh lobster is used, allow one lobster for six people
4. From the shell, lobster butter is then prepared and the sauce is cooked from the lobster broth
