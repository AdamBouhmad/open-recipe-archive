## Ingredients

- 2 1/2-3 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 liter whole milk
- 1 tablespoon butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and mix with sugar and salt in a bowl.
2. Whisk the eggs together with the milk and stir into the flour little by little so the batter does not become lumpy.
3. Let it stand for 1 hour so the flour can swell, then give it a vigorous whisking.
4. Pour into a buttered frying pan and place in a good oven heat to bake.
