## Ingredients

- 3 kg. veal breast
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 carrot
- 1 shallot
- 1 lit. broth with 1 tsk. meat extract
- 2 hg. boneless veal
- 1 hg. fresh pork
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1—1 1/2 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the breast as in the previous recipe and rub with half of the seasonings.
2. Cook broth on the bones to dilute the roast with.
3. Prepare the stuffing as in recipe 325 and it should be quite firm.
4. Spread it on the inside of the breast, which is then treated and roasted as in the previous recipe.
5. Brown the scraped carrot, cut in slices, and the peeled onion and roast them with the meat.
6. Serve with cooked vegetables or vegetable pastries, salads and strained sauce with cream added (see recipe 429).
