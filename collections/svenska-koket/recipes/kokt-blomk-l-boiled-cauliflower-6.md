## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads (1 kg)
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 40 g melted butter or 3 deciliters hollandaise
- 1 1/4 liters water

## Directions

1. The cauliflower heads should be white and firm.
2. Remove the green leaves and cut away the lowest part of the stalk.
3. Rinse well and let soak in vinegar-mixed water (see page 338) for 1 hour.
4. Rinse again, place with flower side down in boiling salted water.
5. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until just tender, which is tested with a whisk.
6. Place the cauliflower on a warm dish with a slotted spoon, pour the butter or sauce over, and serve very hot as a vegetable course or with fried meat, salted brisket, or hamburger brisket.
7. Cauliflower is also used as garnish around meat together with other vegetables.
8. Cauliflower becomes whiter if cooked in half milk and half water.
