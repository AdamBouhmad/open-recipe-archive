## Ingredients

- 15 anchovies
- 4 boiled potatoes
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Butter an omelet dish with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks
2. In this lay slices of cooked, cold potatoes and on top of this a layer of cleaned anchovies and finally again a layer of potatoes
3. Sprinkle the whole with crushed rusks and chopped parsley
4. Place the rest of the butter on top, distributed in flakes, and put the dish in the oven to achieve a brown color
