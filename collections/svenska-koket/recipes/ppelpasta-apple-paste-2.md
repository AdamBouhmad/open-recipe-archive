## Ingredients

- 10 liters apples
- 2 liters water
- 1/2 kg sugar for each kg of apples

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into pieces, and remove the cores.
2. Place them with the water and sugar and allow them to cook under constant stirring for 4-5 hours.
3. When the apples are completely cooked down and the sauce falls in flakes from the spoon, the paste is ready.
