## Ingredients

- 2 root celeries
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 egg, 1 tablespoon water
- 1 teacup crushed bread crusts
- 150 gr. smoked ham
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.) for frying

## Directions

1. Peel the celeries thinly, rinse them well, put them in boiling salted water and let them cook with a lid until half-soft.
2. Let them cool, cut them into 1/2 cm thick slices, which are dipped in the egg whisked with water, and turned in crushed bread crusts.
3. Fry them golden brown in part of the butter and arrange them in a wreath on a warm serving dish.
4. Cut the ham into strips, fry it in the rest of the butter and sprinkle it over the celery.
5. Garnish the dish with small browned potatoes and green salad and serve as a breakfast dish.
