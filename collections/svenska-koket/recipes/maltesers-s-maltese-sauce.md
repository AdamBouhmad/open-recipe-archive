## Ingredients

- 5 dcl hollandaise
- juice of 1 blood orange
- 1/4 tsk finely grated orange zest

## Directions

1. To hollandaise sauce, prepared according to recipe no. 412, add, just before it is served, the orange juice and the finely grated orange zest.
2. This sauce is the finest one can serve with asparagus, artichoke hearts, and sugar beets.
