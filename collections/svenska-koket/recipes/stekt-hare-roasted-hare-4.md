## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- 0.5 lemon
- 1 hectogram lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 0.5–1 liter broth or water
- 1 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 1–2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The hare should have hung for a few days to become tender.
2. Skin it and carefully remove all membranes.
3. Cut off the head at the shoulders, and also the thin parts on the sides.
4. Break the back into 5–6 connected pieces, and likewise break the hind legs.
5. Insert one hind leg between the sinews of the other.
6. Rinse the hare well, dry with a cloth and rub with the lemon until the meat has a fresh and sour smell.
7. Lard it with the lard cut into fine strips in four rows on each side of the back and on the fleshiest part of the leg.
8. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, brown the hare on all sides, salt it and add the broth a little at a time.
9. Let it roast covered with a tight-fitting lid, which takes 1–1.5 hours.
10. The hare can either be served with its drippings, or the sauce can be bound according to general sauce rules.
