## Ingredients

- 5 hg. prime wheat flour
- 50 gr. butter
- 3 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 15 gr. pressed yeast
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 5-6 cardamom pods
- 1 egg for brushing
- 1 tbsp. poppy seeds for brushing

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes into the flour and work it in with fingertips, then stir in the milk and yeast, stirred with the salt and sugar, and the finely cracked cardamom pods.
3. Work the dough until it becomes smooth and shiny and quite firm.
4. Place in a warm place to rise to double size.
5. Turn the dough onto a floured work bench, work and divide, shape into buns or rolls, place on buttered baking sheets to rise again.
6. Brush with egg and sprinkle with poppy seeds.
7. Bake in a hot oven until golden brown.
