## Ingredients

- 150 grams butter
- 150 grams flour
- 1 decilitre cream
- 2 cup cooked fish
- 6 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoon crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The butter is mixed with flour and stirred with cream to form a thin custard; this is brought to a boil with whisking, then poured out to cool
2. The cooked fish is picked fine like rice grains and mixed into the cooled batter along with egg yolks one by one
3. It is seasoned with pepper and salt, and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in
4. The mass is immediately poured into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated mold and baked for about 1 1/2 hours in moderate oven heat
5. The soufflé is served immediately with fine fish sauce
