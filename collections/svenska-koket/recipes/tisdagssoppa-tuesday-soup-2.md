## Ingredients

- 30 gr. pearl barley
- 1.5 liters pork or meat broth
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 leek
- 1/2 liter potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 1/2 liter milk
- 2 deciliters thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse the barley, place it in the boiling broth and let it cook for about 1.5 hours.
2. Prepare the root vegetables and leek in the usual way, cut into strips and add to the soup.
3. Peel the potatoes
