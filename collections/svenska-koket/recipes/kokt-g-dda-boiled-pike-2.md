## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
- 3 teaspoon salt (15 gr.)
- Water
- 7 gr. coarse salt per liter of water
- Melted butter
- Grated horseradish
- Potatoes
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Do not scale the fish, clean it (see page 171)
2. Rub with salt and bind it by passing a needle with string through the head and tail so the fish lies curved when served (hence the name kromgädda)
3. Cook with the roe as in recipe 327
4. When the fins loosen easily, it is ready
5. Place on a fish platter with sieve or on a torn napkin
6. Garnish with parsley and the roe and serve with melted butter, grated horseradish, and potatoes
