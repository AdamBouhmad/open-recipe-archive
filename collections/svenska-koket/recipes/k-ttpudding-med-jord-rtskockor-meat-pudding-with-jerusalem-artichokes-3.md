## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipe 632
- 1.5 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 0.75 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 4 deciliters cooking liquid
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the ground meat as in recipe 632.
2. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes clean, scrape them, rinse them and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour to keep them white.
3. Place them in boiling water with the salt and let them cook until soft.
4. Drain them in a colander and reserve the cooking liquid.
5. Prepare and bake the stewing and pudding as in the previous recipe.
