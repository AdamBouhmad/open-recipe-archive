## Ingredients

- 1 kg cut beef steak, beef hare, boneless chuck or tenderloin
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1-2 decilitres broth
- 3-4 red onions or one large Portuguese onion

## Directions

1. Heat a frying pan and brown the butter in it.
2. Add the beef and brown it nicely on all sides.
3. Add the broth and onions, season with salt and white pepper.
4. Let simmer covered until tender.
