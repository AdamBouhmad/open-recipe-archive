## Ingredients

- 1 12-penny wheat bread
- 1 hectogram butter
- 9 anchovies
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices and spread butter on one side of each.
2. Clean the anchovies, fillet them, and remove the skin and bones.
3. Place three anchovy halves diagonally over each sandwich.
4. Brush them with the lightly beaten egg white, place on a sheet, and put in weak oven heat to get a nice golden brown color.
5. Serve mainly with vegetarian soups.
