## Ingredients

- 100 grams sweet almonds
- 4 grams bitter almonds
- 150 grams fine sugar
- 6 parts water
- 6 parts thick cream
- 12 grams gelatin
- zest of 1 orange
- juice of 2 oranges
- carmine
- spinach green

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and grate them.
2. Boil a syrup from the sugar and water, in which the almonds are allowed to cook for 10 minutes.
3. To extract more almond flavor, let the syrup stand beside the fire for an additional 20 minutes, then strain it.
4. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, add the sugar syrup, and lastly the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water.
5. Divide the mixture into three equal parts.
6. Flavor one part with orange zest and juice; color the second part with carmine and the third with spinach green.
7. Rinse a beautiful tin mold with cold water and sprinkle it with sugar.
8. On the bottom, lay the green mixture and set it out to stiffen.
9. On top of that, lay the white mixture, and when this has set, add the red.
10. When the blanc-mangé is to be served, dip the mold in lukewarm water and turn out the pudding.
