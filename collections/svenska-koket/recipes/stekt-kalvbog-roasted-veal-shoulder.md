## Ingredients

- 2 kg. veal shoulder
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 fresh or 1/2 hg. dried apples
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water. The shoulder blade is carefully removed and cut into pieces; then broth is cooked to dilute the roast with. The meat is rubbed with the seasonings and filled with the peeled and sliced fresh apples or else with the well-rinsed dried apples, and sewn together. It is then roasted in an oven or pot (see no. 498 or 500). Served with boiled potatoes and strained sauce (see no. 429), mixed with cream.
2. Veal shoulder can also be filled with forcemeat and roasted the same way.
