## Ingredients

- 1 kg. carrots
- 3 shallots
- 5 tablespoons butter (1 hectogram)
- 2 liters water
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream
- 1 deciliter rice grains
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the carrots in the usual manner, cut into cubes and brown in 2 tablespoons of butter together with the peeled shallots cut into slices.
2. Add water and let the carrots cook until completely tender, approximately 2 hours, then strain.
3. Prepare the purée as in recipe no. 266 and place 1 tablespoon of cold butter in the soup bowl.
4. Rinse the rice grains, add to boiling salted water and let cook until tender, approximately 15 minutes, then strain, rinse with hot water, drain, place on carrot and serve with the soup.
