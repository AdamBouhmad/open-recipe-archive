## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 125 gr. powdered sugar
- 5 dcl. pure wine

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks carefully from the whites, place them in a well-scoured saucepan and whisk with the sugar for 15 minutes.
2. Add the wine and let the sauce simmer over the heat with vigorous whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
3. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
