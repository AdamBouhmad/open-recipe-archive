## Ingredients

- 3 large Portuguese onions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the onion and parboil it until it is almost soft, then remove it and let it drain.
2. Brown the butter in a pot, place the onion in it and brown it, then let it slowly fry until it is completely soft.
3. Just before it is done, sprinkle it with sugar.
