## Ingredients

- 500 gr. boneless veal bottom round
- 1 deciliter crushed bread crumbs
- 2–2½ deciliters heavy cream
- ½ tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 can mushrooms (¼ liter)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 1 deciliter meat stock (preferably from game)
- 3 deciliters heavy cream
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, cut into pieces and grind 4 times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the crushed bread crumbs with the cream and let stand to swell for 1 hour.
3. Work the meat well with the bread crumb mixture, season with spices and form into 15 round patties.
4. Brown the patties quickly in butter and place in a pot.
5. Slice the mushrooms and fry in butter in the frying pan, add flour and mix well with the butter and mushrooms, add meat stock and cream and bring sauce to a good boil, season with spices.
6. Pour sauce over the patties, which then braise slowly with the lid tightly closed for 10–15 minutes.
7. Place on a warm dish, whip up the sauce and pour over the patties.
8. Serve with mashed potatoes or fried potatoes.
