## Ingredients

- Leftovers from mutton roast
- 3 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 small parsnip
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Cut the finest meat from the roast into cubes. Crush the remaining meat together with the bone and place it in cold water along with the blanched parsnip and salt to cook for 3 hours.
2. Mix butter and flour together, add the strained and skimmed broth, and let the soup cook for 10 minutes. Then add the cut meat and season with pepper and salt.
3. Whip the egg yolks and cream in a soup tureen, and pour the soup into it while whisking vigorously.
