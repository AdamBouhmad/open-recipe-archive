## Ingredients

- 2 partridges
- 80 gr butter
- 4 dcl thick cream
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr)
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 50 gr truffle (optional)
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Pluck the partridges, remove the innards, rinse well and skin them.
2. Cut all meat from the carcasses and scrape thoroughly clean from sinews and membranes.
3. Run the meat through the meat grinder 3 times, mix with the butter and grind again 3 times.
4. Place the mixture in a dish, add half the cream tablespoonful by tablespoonful while stirring vigorously.
5. Pass the mixture through a hair sieve and add the egg yolks one at a time, along with the flour and seasonings and, if desired, the finely cut truffle.
6. Stir the mixture for 1/2 hour, during which time add the remaining cream, whipped to peaks, little by little.
7. Finally fold in carefully the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
8. Taste the mixture and fill into a well-buttered and bread-crumbed mold and cook in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 1/4 hours.
9. Turn out, garnish with fine vegetables or stuffed tomatoes and serve immediately as a side course with a light sauce made from the game bones.
10. Soufflé of other game or poultry can be prepared in the same way.
