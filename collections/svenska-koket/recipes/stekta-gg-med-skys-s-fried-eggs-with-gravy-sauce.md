## Ingredients

- 6 slices wheat bread
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 6 eggs
- 6 anchovies
- 3 dcl. strong meat gravy
- 1/2 tbsp. cornstarch flour
- 1/2 glass sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. The bread slices should be 2 cm. thick, they are cut round, fried in half of the butter and placed on a warm dish.
2. Fry the eggs in the rest of the butter and place on the croutons.
3. On each egg place the fillets of a cleaned and boned anchovy crosswise.
4. Heat the meat gravy and thicken with the cornstarch flour mixed in the wine.
5. Let the sauce cook while stirring for 5 minutes and pour around the eggs on the dish.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
