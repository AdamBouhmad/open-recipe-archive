## Ingredients

- 250 gr. granulated or castor sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 12 good, uniformly sized apples
- 25 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water to make a clear syrup and let it cool.
2. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, and remove the cores with a corer.
3. Place the apples directly in the syrup and boil until they are soft and clear, then remove with a slotted spoon and arrange in a pyramid on a jelly dish.
4. If desired, fill the holes left by the cores with apple or red currant jelly.
5. Blanch the almond, cut it into fine cubes, lightly toast them in the oven, and sprinkle over the pyramid.
6. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream. If the pyramid is served cold, it can be garnished with 3 dcl. cream, beaten until stiff, if desired.
