## Ingredients

- 60 gr. sweetened block chocolate
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 tablespoon potato flour (8 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1—2 tablespoon powdered sugar (15 gr.)
- cognac or liqueur (optional)

## Directions

1. Divide the chocolate finely in the cream, which is brought to a boil while whisking, so that the chocolate melts.
2. Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar and potato flour until frothy. Add the boiling milk while whisking vigorously and let the sauce simmer over the fire, while continuing to whisk, until it is thick and foamy.
3. Serve both warm and cold with puddings and cakes and can, if desired, be flavored with a little cognac or liqueur.
4. The sauce can also be mixed with 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
