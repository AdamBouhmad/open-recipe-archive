## Ingredients

- 6 whole poached eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 kg lard for frying
- 4 large red tomatoes
- 4 dcl broth
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 g)
- 1 dcl cream
- salt
- cayenne
- 6 slices white bread
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Dip the eggs in egg whites mixed with 1 tbsp cold water, roll in crushed breadcrumbs and let rest 1 hour in a cold place.
2. Rinse the tomatoes, clean them, cut into pieces and cook until soft in the broth, then strain. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the tomato purée and cream, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes. Season with salt and cayenne.
3. Fry the eggs until golden brown in hot lard. The bread slices should be 2 cm thick, cut into oblong pieces, hollow out, fry golden brown in butter and place on a warm platter. Place the eggs on the croutons and pour the tomato sauce around them.
4. Garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
