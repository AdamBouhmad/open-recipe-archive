## Ingredients

- 2 liters water
- 9-12 teaspoons medium-strength cocoa powder
- 9-12 teaspoons sugar
- 4 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the cocoa powder with the sugar in a cup, blend in a little of the cream beaten to stiff peaks, and stir vigorously until the mixture becomes smooth. Pour boiling water on it, and place the rest of the whipped cream on top.
