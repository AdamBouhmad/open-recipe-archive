## Ingredients

- 80 fine, even sugar beets
- 1.5 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Scrape the sugar beets, rinse them well and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Bundle them together, 6 in each bunch, place in boiling salted water with the juice of 1/2 lemon and cook until tender, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon, place on an oblong platter with a folded napkin and serve very hot with creamed butter or hollandaise sauce, mixed with the juice of 1 blood orange.
