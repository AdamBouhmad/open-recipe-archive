## Ingredients

- 3 parts syrup
- 400 g brown flour
- 2 tbsp ground ginger
- 30 drops lemon oil
- 350 g butter
- 3 parts thick cream
- 1 tbsp bicarbonate of soda
- 1 1/8 kg wheat flour
- brown flour for rolling out

## Directions

1. Stir the syrup cold together with the sugar, spices, and the washed butter into a smooth mass for about 1/2 hour.
2. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and stir in gently.
3. Sift the flour with the bicarbonate and work in.
4. When the dough is firm enough to be handled, knead in the remaining flour on the work surface with a rolling pin.
5. Let the dough stand until the next day.
6. Roll out to 1 cm thickness. Now strew brown flour on the work surface under the cake, and then roll it out to about 4 mm thickness.
7. Cut small round cookies from the dough, brush with water, and bake a nice brown.
