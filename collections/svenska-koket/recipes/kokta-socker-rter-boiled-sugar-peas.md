## Ingredients

- 2 liters large, beautiful sugar peas
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the peas, rinse them well and let them drain on a cloth.
2. Place them in boiling water seasoned with salt and sugar, and let them simmer slowly until they are completely tender, then strain off the liquid and carefully blend the peas with the cold butter and parsley.
3. Serve with ham and roasted meat.
