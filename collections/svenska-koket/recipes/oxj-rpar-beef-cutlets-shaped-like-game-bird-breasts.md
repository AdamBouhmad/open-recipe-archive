## Ingredients

- 500g boneless beef
- 200g fat
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 3-4 parts milk
- 1 egg (for breading)
- 2 cups crushed rusks (for breading)
- 3 tablespoons butter (for frying)

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with 100g of the fat into cubes and pass it three times through a meat grinder.
2. Add the whipped egg, spices, potato flour and milk, and work the forcemeat until it is smooth and cohesive.
3. Form small cutlets from the forcemeat in the shape of game bird breasts, lard them with the remaining fat cut into fine strips.
4. Bread the cutlets and fry them in butter.
5. Serve them with peas or other vegetables.
