## Ingredients

- 3 sole, approximately 500 g each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- For breading: 1 1/2 decilitres milk
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons cooking oil
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 cup finely crushed rusks
- For frying: 1/2 kg butter
- 1 lemon
- parsley for garnishing

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the fish as in recipe 371
2. Rub the fillets with salt and pepper and let them lie on top of each other for 1 hour, then dry them well with a fish cloth
3. Dip them in milk, turn in flour, then dip them in the well-beaten eggs mixed with cooking oil and seasoned with a little salt and pepper, and finally turn them in crushed rusks
4. Tap them with a knife so that the breading adheres and let them lie for 1 hour to harden
5. Fry them in the butter as in recipe 329, place on a torn napkin or parchment paper, pour a little melted butter mixed with lemon juice over them, garnish with beautifully cut lemon wedges and fried parsley and serve with remoulade sauce or maitre d'hôtel butter and small boiled potatoes
