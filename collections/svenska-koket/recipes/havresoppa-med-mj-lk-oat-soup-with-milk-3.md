## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 deciliters regular oat grains (112 grams)
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 15 almonds (3 bitter) (15 grams)
- 1 liter milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 grams)
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 1 1/2 hectograms prune plums

## Directions

1. Boil the grains in water as in the previous recipe and pass them through a sieve.
2. Blanch the almonds, grind them and pound them finely in a mortar.
3. Boil the milk with the almonds; add the oat puree, sugar, and salt and let the soup boil while stirring.
