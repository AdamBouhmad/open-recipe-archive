## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 4 deciliters cream
- 2 leaves gelatin
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon anchovy sauce
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt
- 2 teaspoon vegetable oil
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 kg. cooking fat

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, cool in cold water, peel and cut into fine cubes.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Carefully stir in the diced eggs along with the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water.
4. Season the mixture with salt and white pepper and treat as in recipe no. 49.
5. Shape the croquettes into rounds.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish with stewed peas or spinach.
