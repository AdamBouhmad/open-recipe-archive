## Ingredients

- salmon
- salt
- saltpeter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Brush, clean and wash the salmon well.
2. Rub it with salt, saltpeter and sugar mixed together and let it rest for a while.
3. Place the salmon on the fire in warm, but not boiling water, and skim carefully when the broth comes to a boil.
4. Let the fish simmer slowly at the side of the fire until the fins come loose.
5. Place it immediately on the serving platter and garnish with parsley, salad, lemon, beets or hard-boiled eggs.
6. If the fish is served cold, garnish it with mayonnaise sauce, which is also served with the fish. When served warm, serve with Hollandaise sauce or another fine fish sauce.
