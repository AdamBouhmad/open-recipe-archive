## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram young suckling pig or young shoulder pork
- 1/2 kilogram calf's feet
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 carrot
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 3 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- 8 deciliters of broth
- 2-3 leaves gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 beet
- parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the calf's feet with a cloth wrung out in cold water, split them, and place in cold water, skim well when it comes to a boil.
2. Dry the pork with a cloth wrung out in hot water and add it together with the peeled and cut carrot and the spices; skim again and let simmer slowly until tender, about 1 hour.
3. Remove the meat, and when cool cut it into even pieces.
4. Let the broth cook with the calf's feet until it reduces to about 1 liter and let cool; remove all fat carefully.
5. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 181.
6. Garnish the bottom of the mold with the hard-boiled egg and beet, cut into attractive shapes, and parsley.
7. Pour a few drops of aspic over the garnish and let set so the garnish does not move when the meat is added.
8. Arrange the most attractive pieces on the bottom and sides, fill the mold with the remaining meat, and pour the remaining aspic carefully over.
9. Place the aspic in a cold place to set.
10. Unmold as in the previous recipe and garnish with beets, egg, and parsley.
11. Serve on the smorgasbord.
