## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple
- 1 liter water
- 200 g sugar
- citric acid
- 25 g gelatin
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Heat the pineapple syrup, water, sugar and citric acid to taste in a saucepan.
2. Add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little warm water, whisk in the egg white, then bring to a brief boil.
3. Strain the jelly through a cloth wrung out in hot water.
4. Pour a couple of coffee cups of the clear jelly onto the bottom of a water-rinsed ring mold and set out to set.
5. Fill the mold with pineapple sliced into nice slices, and finally pour the rest of the jelly over.
6. Set again to set, then turn the jelly out.
