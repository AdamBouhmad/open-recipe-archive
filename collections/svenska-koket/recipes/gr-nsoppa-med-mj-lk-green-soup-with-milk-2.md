## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 0.25 celery root
- 1 leek
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 0.5 liter cooked peas
- 2 small cauliflower heads
- 1.5-2 liters good milk
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 0.5 cup cream

## Directions

1. Rinse and scrape the carrots and cut them into thin slices; peel the celery and leek and cut them into fine strips.
2. Fry the root vegetables and leek in the butter, add a little boiling water, and cook them soft in a short stock.
3. Clean the cauliflower and let it soak for a while in water mixed with vinegar, then put it on with the peas to cook in a little salted water.
4. Heat the milk and thicken it with the flour mixed in a little cold milk, then let the soup cook for 10 minutes.
5. Add the finely chopped parsley together with the cooked root vegetables and greens with their liquid, and taste the soup.
6. Whip the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl and mix the soup in with vigorous whisking.
7. If using canned peas, heat them in their can and add them last to the soup.
