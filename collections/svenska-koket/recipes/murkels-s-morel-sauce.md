## Ingredients

- 15 gr. dried morels
- 1/2 liter cold water
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1 dcl. truffle liquid
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the morels and let them soak in plenty of water for several hours.
2. Place them in the cold water and let them cook until tender, or approximately 1/2 hour, then pour them into a colander and reserve the liquid.
3. Dice the mushroom into small pieces and fry them in butter until clear.
4. Prepare the sauce as in the preceding recipe.
