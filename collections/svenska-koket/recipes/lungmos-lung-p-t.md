## Ingredients

- Heart and lungs of veal
- water
- 1 red onion
- 24 allspice berries
- 4 bay leaves
- salt
- 2 tablespoons crushed grain

## Directions

1. Trim the heart and lungs, rinse well and let soak in cold water for about 12 hours to remove the blood.
2. Change the water at least three times.
3. Place the heart and lungs in cold water; when the broth comes to a boil, skim thoroughly; then add the seasonings and the halved onion, and let the meat cook slowly.
4. When it feels tender, remove it and strain the broth.
5. Cook the grain in the broth, and when it is almost done, add the finely chopped meat, and let everything cook further until the grain is soft.
6. Taste the pâté for seasoning.
