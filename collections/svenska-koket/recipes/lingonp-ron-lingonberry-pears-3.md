## Ingredients

- 2 litres lingonberries plus 1 1/2 litres water equals 1 litre lingonberry juice
- 750 gr crushed sugar
- 3 litres peeled pears

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries, rinse, place with the cold water and let simmer slowly until they fall apart, then pour them into cheesecloth to drain themselves.
2. Cook the lingonberry juice with the sugar to a clear syrup, which is skimmed well.
3. Peel the pears, halve them, remove the cores, scrape the stems, and place the pears directly in the syrup.
4. Let them simmer slowly thereafter without a lid until they appear clear.
