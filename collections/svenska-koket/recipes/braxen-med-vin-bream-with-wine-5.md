## Ingredients

- 1 braxen på 2 kg. (bream weighing 2 kg.)
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 3 msk. smör (60 gr.) (butter)
- 4 msk. mjöl (40 gr.) (flour)
- 4 dcl. vitt eller rött vin (white or red wine)
- buljong (broth)
- 5 vitpepparkorn (white peppercorns)
- 1 lagerblad (bay leaf)
- 3 persiljestjälkar (parsley stems)
- 2—3 äggulor (egg yolks)
- socker (sugar)

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it (see page 171); the head may remain but must be freed from the gills.
2. Rub it with salt and let it lie for 1 hour, then dry it again.
3. Brown the butter lightly and fry it with the flour for 2 minutes.
4. Dilute with wine and as much broth as needed to make the sauce of appropriate consistency.
5. Place the fish in a fish kettle without a strainer together with the roe or milk, pour the sauce over it and it should stand level with the fish.
6. Add the pepper, bay leaf and parsley and let the fish cook slowly until the meat is tender, about 20 minutes.
7. Shake the kettle often during this time so that the sauce does not burn.
8. Place the fish on a hot platter and garnish with parsley and sliced lemon.
9. Strain the sauce, bind it with the egg yolks, let it simmer, season with salt and sugar and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
