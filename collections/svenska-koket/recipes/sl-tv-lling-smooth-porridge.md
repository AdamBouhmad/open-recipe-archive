## Ingredients

- 5–6 tbsp. flour (50–60 gr.)
- 2 lit. milk
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir the flour with 1/4 lit. of the milk and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the remaining milk, bring to a boil, and add the flour mixture while whisking, then let the porridge boil while continuing to whisk for 8–10 minutes.
3. Season with salt and sugar and serve at once.
4. If desired, add the whisked egg yolks and let the porridge simmer under vigorous whisking for 5 minutes.
