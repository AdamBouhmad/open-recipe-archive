## Ingredients

- 15 gr. parsley
- 15 gr. chervil
- 15 gr. tarragon
- 15 gr. chives
- 30 gr. watercress
- 30 gr. spinach
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 tsk. salt
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- Real mayonnaise
- 1 tsk. Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the vegetables, place in boiling salted water and let cook 2 minutes, drain in a colander, immerse in cold water and let drain well.
2. Pound the vegetables together with the egg yolks in a mortar until a fine mass, pass through a hair sieve and mix with the mayonnaise, see recipe no. 442; which should be very thick.
3. Finish the sauce with Worcestershire sauce, taste and serve with cold meat in general and to à-la-dauber.
