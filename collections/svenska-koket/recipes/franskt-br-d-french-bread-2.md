## Ingredients

- 8 hg. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 lit. skim milk or water
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. sugar (8 gr.)
- 1 msk. melted butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and set aside a small portion for rolling out. Heat the milk or water to approximately 40 degrees and mix into the flour together with the yeast, dissolved with salt and sugar and the butter, and work the dough until it becomes smooth and glossy and quite firm.
2. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
3. Spread the dough out on a floured work surface, work it and divide it into 5 parts and divide each fifth into 6 parts; these are rolled out into French breads, which are placed on well-buttered baking sheets to rise again.
4. Brush the breads with milk and bake in a warm oven.
5. The butter and sugar can be omitted if the breads are used the same day.
6. The dough can be formed into oblong breads, which are brushed with milk. From the dough a twist is made, which is placed lengthwise along the bread. The buns are then brushed with egg, sprinkled with poppy seeds and are allowed to rise, after which they are again brushed with milk before being put in the oven.
7. The dough can also be placed in two buttered bread forms and allowed to rise 2-2 1/2 hours. Bake in a very warm oven for approximately 1 hour. Brush with hot water when removed.
