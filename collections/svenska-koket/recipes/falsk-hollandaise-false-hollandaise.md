## Ingredients

- 60 gr butter
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr)
- 2 gr salt
- 1 gr sugar
- 1/2 gr white pepper
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 dcl water
- 1 tsk lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stir the butter in a warm saucepan until it becomes white and creamy, then fold in the flour, the egg yolks one at a time, the salt, sugar, and pepper.
2. Place the saucepan over low heat, add the cold water little by little, with constant whisking, and let the sauce simmer until it is thick and glossy.
3. Remove from heat, season immediately with lemon juice, and keep warm in a water bath.
