## Ingredients

- 1 liter cooked, leftover potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 1 egg yolk
- 1–2 tablespoons thick cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a little nutmeg
- 1 part crushed rusks for breading
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.) for frying

## Directions

1. Grate the potatoes on a grater or press through a puree press.
2. Stir the butter until foamy and mix well with the puree together with the egg yolk, cream and seasonings.
3. Form into balls using two spoons.
