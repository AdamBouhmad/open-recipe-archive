## Ingredients

- 60 gr. mondamin flour
- 5 dcl. milk
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4-5 eggs
- 75 gr. sugar
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon rind
- 2 candied orange peels, cut into fine dice
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Mix the mondamin in 2 dcl. of the milk. Boil the remaining milk with the butter, add the mondamin mixture and let the porridge cook for 5 minutes under stirring. Remove the saucepan from heat and add the egg yolks one at a time, together with the lemon and orange peel. When the cream is cold, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture immediately into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated pie mold, and bake the pudding in regular oven heat for about 40 minutes. Serve immediately with compote or fruit sauce.
