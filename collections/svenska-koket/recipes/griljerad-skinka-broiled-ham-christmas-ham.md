## Ingredients

- 5 kg ham
- 4 tbsp salt
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 0.5 tbsp saltpeter
- 12 liters water
- 1.3 kg salt butter
- 25 gr saltpeter
- 160 gr granulated sugar
- water for cooking
- 24 allspice berries
- 0.5 tbsp mustard
- 0.5 tbsp sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. Rub the ham with salt, sugar, and saltpeter. From the water, salt, saltpeter, and sugar, make a brine which is well skimmed and poured up to cool. When the brine is cold, place the ham in it. It should stay in the salt brine for 14 days.
2. When the ham is to be cooked, take it out of the brine, dry it with a cloth and place in a pot with boiling water. When the broth boils up, skim it thoroughly, add the allspice berries, and let the ham simmer slowly for 4-5 hours. If the ham is not sufficiently salted, add salt during cooking.
3. When the ham is cooked, remove it from the broth, and strip off the skin immediately. Brush the ham with the above coating and sprinkle with finely crushed hardtack. Put it in the oven and let it stay until it gets a nice brown color, which takes about 1 hour.
4. When the ham is to be served, wrap the shank with creped paper and garnish the ham with greens, cooked prunes, and apple sauce.
