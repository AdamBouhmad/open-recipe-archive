## Ingredients

- 750 gr. fresh beans
- 7 dcl. water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 3 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.), optional

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse them, let them drain on a cloth, cut them into 4 mm thick pieces, crosswise (somewhat at an angle).
2. Place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until they are tender, then drain off excess liquid and blend the beans with the parsley and cold butter.
3. If a thicker sauce is desired, make a roux with the flour and half of the butter, add the liquid, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes, then add the beans and blend with the parsley and remaining butter.
4. Serve with brined ham, boiled sausage and roasted meat.
