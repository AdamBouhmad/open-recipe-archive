## Ingredients

- 1/3 liter syrup
- 635 g brown flour
- 2 tbsp powdered soda
- 3 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 tsp lemon oil
- 1/3 liter thick cream
- 1.275 kg wheat flour

## Directions

1. Stir the syrup until light.
2. Then stir in the sugar, soda, spices, and the whipped cream.
3. Last, work in the flour.
4. Roll out the dough not too thin, and cut small round cookies with a measure, which are brushed over with water and baked in even oven heat.
