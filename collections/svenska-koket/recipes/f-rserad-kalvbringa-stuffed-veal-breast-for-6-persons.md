## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 200 grams boneless meat from the shoulder
- 100 grams kidney fat
- 1 egg
- 2 parts thin cream
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 red onion
- 1 liter broth (made from the bones)
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 teaspoon soy sauce
- meat gravy

## Directions

1. Wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water, carefully remove the shoulder blade and ribs so that the meat remains as whole as possible.
2. Make broth from the bones to dilute the roast during cooking.
3. Cut 200 grams of the meat into cubes and grind through a meat grinder three times together with the broken up kidney fat.
4. Pound the meat with butter in a stone mortar and mix with the seasonings, the beaten eggs, cream and crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Work the forcemeat until it becomes smooth and cohesive.
6. Rub the breast with salt, fill with the forcemeat and sew it well shut.
7. Brown it together with the peeled onion in the browned butter, dilute with the boiling broth and allow it to roast slowly with a tightly closed lid for 1 to 1.5 hours.
8. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
