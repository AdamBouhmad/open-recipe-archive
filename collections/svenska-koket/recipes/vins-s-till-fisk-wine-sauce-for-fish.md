## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 5 dcl. good fish broth
- 1–2 egg yolks
- salt, white pepper
- 1/2 dcl. white wine

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes
2. Dilute with the fish broth and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes
3. Whisk in the whipped egg yolks and let the sauce simmer
4. Season with the seasonings and serve very hot
