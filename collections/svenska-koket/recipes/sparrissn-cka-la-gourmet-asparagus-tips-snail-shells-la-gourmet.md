## Ingredients

- 1 can asparagus tips (1/2 liter)
- 1 liter water
- 10 grams salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter asparagus cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- 6 snail shells
- Coarse salt for the pan
- Salad or parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Open the can and prepare the asparagus as in the previous recipe
2. Butter six snail shells with cold butter and place the asparagus in them
3. Melt the butter and flour together and fry for 2 minutes
4. Add the asparagus cooking liquid and cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes
5. Season the sauce with the spices and bind it with the egg yolk
6. Pour the sauce over the asparagus in the shells and sprinkle a little grated cheese over the top
7. Pour a little clarified butter over the preparation
8. Place the shells in a baking pan with coarse salt and gratinée in a warm oven for 10-15 minutes
9. Serve immediately on a round platter with a folded napkin, garnished with salad or parsley
