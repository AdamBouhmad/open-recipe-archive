## Ingredients

- 20 smelts
- 10 anchovies
- 2 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoon crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The smelts are cleaned, boned and rinsed well
2. The anchovies are cleaned, boned and split
3. An omelette mold is well greased and sprinkled with crushed rusks
4. An anchovy fillet is placed in each smelt, and the smelts are laid in the mold close together with the backs up
5. The crushed rusks are sprinkled over, and the butter is placed in flakes on top
6. The mold is placed in good oven heat, and when the smelts are brown and cooked through, it is served
