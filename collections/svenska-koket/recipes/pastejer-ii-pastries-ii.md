## Ingredients

- 120 gr. cooked, cold potato
- 120 gr. fine butter
- 120 gr. flour
- 20 gr. sweet almond
- 20 gr. caster sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp. flour
- 2 dcl. thin cream
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the forms
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Peel the potato, grate it and weigh it.
2. Wash the butter and work it quickly with the potato and flour into an even dough.
3. For the filling, blanch the almond and pass it through an almond mill, then pound it with the sugar and a little of the cream into a fine mass, which is mixed with the whisked egg, flour and the rest of the cream.
4. Butter the pastry forms.
5. Roll out the dough to 1 1/2 mm thickness, and cut cakes with a round measure, with which the forms are lined.
6. Fill them to 3/4 with cream; place a lid of dough over and press firmly against the edge.
7. Cut the rest of the dough into narrow strips, which are laid in squares over the lid.
8. Bake and serve as in the previous recipe.
9. When they are half-baked, brush them with the whisked egg to get a nice color.
