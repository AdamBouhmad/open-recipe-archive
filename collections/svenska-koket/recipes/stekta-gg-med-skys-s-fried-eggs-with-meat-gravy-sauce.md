## Ingredients

- 6 slices white bread
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 6 eggs
- 6 anchovies
- 3 dcl strong meat gravy
- 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1/2 glass sherry or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. The bread slices should be 2 cm thick. Cut them round, fry in half of the butter and place on a warm platter. Fry the eggs in the remaining butter and place on the croutons. On each egg place the fillets of a cleaned and boned anchovy crosswise.
2. Heat the meat gravy and thicken with the cornstarch dissolved in the wine. Let the sauce simmer 5 minutes while stirring and pour around the eggs on the platter. Serve as a breakfast dish.
