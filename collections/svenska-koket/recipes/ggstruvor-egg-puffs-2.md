## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 75 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 and 1/3 decilitres thick cream
- 200-225 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 kg. oil for frying

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar for about 1/2 hour.
2. Add the cream and sifted flour and stir the batter well.
3. Heat the oil in an iron pot, not more than a small teacup.
4. Through a rosette pipe or regular piping bag, squeeze a tablespoon of the batter back and forth into the oil so that it forms a perforated cake.
5. When the puff is golden brown on the underside, turn it with a slotted spoon, and when it has taken on an even colour, remove it and bend it over a stick and let it drain on grey paper.
6. Dust the puffs with sugar before serving.
