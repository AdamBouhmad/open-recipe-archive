## Ingredients

- 2 fish, 8 hg each
- 1/2 msk vinegar
- 2 msk salt (30 gr.)
- 1 msk butter (20 gr.) for the form
- 4 eggs
- 3 dcl sour milk
- 2 msk finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Handle the fish as in no. 384.
2. Cut off the head and tail and discard.
3. Rub the fish with salt, let lie 2 hours, dry well, cut into pieces 4 1/2 cm wide across the width, which are placed in a butter-greased ovenproof form, preferably with a lid, together with the roe and liver, cut into pieces.
4. Whip the eggs well with the milk, mix in the parsley and pour the egg custard over the fish, place the lid on or bind the form with butter-greased, double butter paper.
5. Cook the dish in a water bath in the oven about 40 minutes or until the fish feels soft.
6. Serve from the form with melted butter, added with lemon juice and cooked potatoes.
