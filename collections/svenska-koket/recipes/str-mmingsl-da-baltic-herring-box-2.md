## Ingredients

- 24 Baltic herrings
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 12 anchovies
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan
- 1 tablespoon crushed hardtack for the pan

## Directions

1. Clean the Baltic herrings, remove the bones, scrape away the dark skin, rinse well and wrap in a fish towel
2. Sprinkle the salt inside and outside the herrings and let them rest for a while
3. Clean the anchovies, remove the bones and split them in half lengthwise
4. Grease a heat-resistant omelet pan with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed hardtack
5. Place an anchovy half in each herring, roll the herrings tightly and arrange them close together in the pan
6. Distribute the butter in flakes on top, then sprinkle the crushed hardtack over all
7. Place the pan in a hot oven and fry the herrings until they are nicely brown, approximately 25 minutes
8. Serve at the smorgasbord or with fried potatoes as a breakfast dish
