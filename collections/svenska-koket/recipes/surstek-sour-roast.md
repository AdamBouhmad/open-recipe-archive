## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 kg inner or outer loin of beef
- 3 liters beverage
- 6 parts vinegar
- 3 tbsp butter for roasting
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3 tsp white peppercorns
- 3 parts sugar
- 2 hectograms lard
- 1 1/2-2 liters boiling water
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water, pound lightly, and place in a brine made of beverage, vinegar, sugar, and pepper mixed cold
2. Let the roast lie in this brine for 12-14 days
3. Remove, rinse quickly, and dry well
4. Lard and roast like regular pot roast
5. Strain the meat gravy and skim it; prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
