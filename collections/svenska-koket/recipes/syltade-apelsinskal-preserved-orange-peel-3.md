## Ingredients

- Orange peels
- Water
- Sugar at a ratio of 8 hg per kg of peel
- 2 dcl water per kg of peel
- 2 gr citric acid per kg of peel

## Directions

1. The orange peels are allowed to soak in water for 4 days, with the water changed daily.
2. They are then placed in boiling water and cooked slowly until they are completely soft, then placed on cloth to drain.
3. The peels are weighed. For each kg of peel, 8 hg sugar, 2 dcl water and 2 gr citric acid are taken and cooked to a clear, thick syrup.
4. The peels are added and cooked slowly about 20 minutes, then placed in a porcelain jar and the syrup is boiled until it is thick and poured hot over them.
5. The jar is covered when the peels are cold.
