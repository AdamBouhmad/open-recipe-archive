## Ingredients

- 600 g pork sirloin
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 deciliters stock
- 1 large Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Trim the sirloin steaks, dry them with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Cut them crosswise into 1 cm thick slices, which are lightly pounded on both sides and seasoned.
3. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the steaks in and fry quickly.
