## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter thick cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 75 gr. sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 10 gr. gelatin
- sponge cake (glace bread)
- 1/2 egg white

## Directions

1. Half of the cream is boiled, the vanilla pod is placed in it, and the cream is left at the side of the heat until it has taken on a good vanilla flavor, then the vanilla pod is removed.
2. The sugar and egg yolks are added, and everything is whipped over heat until the cream begins to simmer and thicken.
3. The pot is removed from heat and whipping is continued until the cream has cooled.
4. Then the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the rest of the cream, whipped to stiff peaks, are added.
5. A mold is lined with sponge cake, which on the inside is lightly brushed with slightly whipped egg white.
6. The cream is poured in and set out on ice to stiffen.
7. When the pudding is to be turned out, the mold is dipped for a moment in lukewarm water.
