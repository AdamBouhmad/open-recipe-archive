## Ingredients

- 1 can asparagus tips (1/2 liter)
- 1 liter water
- 10 grams salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoon flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter asparagus liquid
- 3 deciliter cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese

## Directions

1. Open the can and treat the asparagus as in the preceding recipe.
2. Grease six shell-shaped dishes with cold butter and place the asparagus in them.
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
4. Add the asparagus liquid and cream, and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Season the sauce with the spices and bind it with the egg yolk.
6. Pour the sauce over the asparagus in the shells and sprinkle a little grated cheese on top.
7. Drizzle the dish with a little clarified butter.
8. Place the shells in a roasting pan with coarse salt and gratinée in a warm oven for 10-15 minutes.
9. Serve immediately on a round platter with folded napkin, garnished with salad or parsley.
