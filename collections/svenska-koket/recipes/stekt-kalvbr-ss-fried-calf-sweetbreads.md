## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads from 1 calf
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Place the sweetbreads in water so that the blood runs out, then blanch them, take them up and let them drain
2. Cut them into thin slices, brush these with egg and turn them in crushed rusks
3. Fry the slices thereafter in butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Place them on the serving dish, whisk the pan with a little bouillon or water, and pour the sauce over the sweetbreads
5. Serve the dish with peas
