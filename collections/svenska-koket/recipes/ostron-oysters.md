## Ingredients

- Fresh oysters
- Ice
- Lemon wedges
- Brown or toasted bread for small sandwiches

## Directions

1. Ensure oysters are alive by checking that the shells are tightly closed.
2. Open with an oyster knife, taking care not to spill the liquid.
3. Scrape off the beard.
4. Serve in the deep shell, placed on ice and garnished with lemon wedges.
5. Serve small sandwiches on brown or toasted bread alongside the oysters.
