## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef tenderloin
- 3 tbsp preserved mushrooms
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp salt
- ½-1 liter broth or water
- ½ tbsp butter (for sauce)
- 1½ tbsp flour
- beef stock
- ½ part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, whereupon it is larded with the finely cut mushroom stems (the caps are saved for garnish).
2. Brown the beef tenderloin in 1½ tbsp of the butter, salt it, and add the boiling liquid little by little.
3. Let the fillet slowly roast with a tightly closed lid 1-1½ hours, or until the meat feels tender.
4. Slice the meat into thin slices and garnish with the mushroom caps sautéed in the remainder of the butter.
5. Strain and skim the beef stock, and prepare the sauce according to general rules.
