## Ingredients

- 6 slices wheat bread
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 6 eggs
- 6 anchovies
- 3 decilitres strong meat stock
- 1/2 tablespoon mondamin flour
- 1/2 wine glass sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. The bread slices should be 2 cm. thick, cut round, fried in half of the butter and placed on a warm plate.
2. Fry the eggs in the remaining butter and place on the crouton.
3. On each egg place the fillets of a cleaned and boned anchovy in a cross pattern.
4. Boil the meat stock and thicken with the mondamin flour, stirred into the wine. Let the sauce simmer 5 minutes with stirring and pour around the eggs on the plate.
5. Serve as a breakfast dish.
