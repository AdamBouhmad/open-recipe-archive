## Ingredients

- 60-70 gr. finely ground rusks
- 4 dcl. milk
- 1/2 liter blood
- 2 msk. syrup
- 3 msk. melted butter (60 gr.)
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 tsk. ginger
- 1/2 tsk. finely ground marjoram
- 1 finely chopped red onion (optional)
- 1/2 msk. butter for the onion
- 3 msk. butter for the griddle

## Directions

1. Place the rusks in the milk and let them stand for 1 hour to swell.
2. Strain the blood through a hair sieve, whisk it well, and mix with the rusk mixture.
3. Add the syrup, butter, spices, and the onion if desired, and stir the batter well.
4. Heat a pancake griddle slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush it over the griddle.
5. Stir the batter well and fry thin pancakes from it, arranging them on a warm serving dish.
6. Serve with melted butter and lingonberry jam.
