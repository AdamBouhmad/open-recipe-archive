## Ingredients

- 40 gr. tapioca
- 1½ kkp water
- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped chives
- forcemeat pancakes

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca grains cook in the water until clear, then strain through muslin and add the strained portion to the consommé, which then comes to a boil and is seasoned with spices
2. Cut the forcemeat pancakes with a round measure into cakes (approximately 2 cm. in diameter), which are placed in the soup bowl together with the chives, and the hot consommé is poured over
