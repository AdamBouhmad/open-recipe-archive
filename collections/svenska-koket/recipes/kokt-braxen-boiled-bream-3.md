## Ingredients

- 1 braxen på 2 kg. (bream weighing 2 kg.)
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 8 vitpepparkorn (white peppercorns)
- 5 kryddpepparkorn (allspice berries)
- 2 lagerblad (bay leaves)
- 5 dillkvistar (dill sprigs)

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it (see page 171); the head may remain but must be freed from the gills.
2. Rub it with salt and let it lie for 1 hour.
3. Boil it thereafter either whole or in pieces as in recipe no. 327; when the fins loosen easily, it is done.
4. Bream may be served either hot or cold.
5. If served hot, it should be boiled whole, placed on a napkin and garnished with parsley.
6. If served cold, it is boiled in pieces and the stock is strained over the arranged fish and allowed to set into jelly.
7. Serve it hot or cold with cold horseradish sauce.
