## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 deciliter mushroom stock
- 5 deciliters cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the mushrooms into pieces or slices and fry them in the butter until it is clear, then press them up with a spoon, and fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
2. Add the mushroom stock and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the mushroom, let the sauce come to a boil, season to taste, and if desired, add the whipped egg yolks, after which the sauce should simmer.
