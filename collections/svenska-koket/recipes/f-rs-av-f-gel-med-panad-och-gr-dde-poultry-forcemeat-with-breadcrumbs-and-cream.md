## Ingredients

- 2 wood grouse or 4 willow grouse, approximately 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 hg. breadcrumb coating
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 3-4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the birds and treat and pound the meat as in the previous recipe.
2. Pound the forcemeat with the breadcrumb coating until the mass becomes even and smooth, then place it in a bowl.
3. Add the whipped eggs and egg yolks a little at a time, with continuous vigorous stirring.
4. Pass the forcemeat through a hair sieve, place in a mold or smaller saucepan, which is set in a bowl with ice (otherwise it will break when the cream is worked in) and let it stand 1 hour, or until it is completely cooled.
5. Stir it then, let it remain on the ice and add 2 dcl. of the cream a little at a time, with even and vigorous stirring.
6. Finally stir in the rest of the cream, whipped to stiff foam.
7. Test it as in the previous recipe; if it is too loose, add a little egg white, otherwise a little cream and more seasoning, if needed.
8. Mix lastly with chopped truffle, if desired.
9. Cook as in the previous recipe and serve warm or cold with truffle sauce or brown mushroom sauce, or cold in aspic with vegetable salad.
