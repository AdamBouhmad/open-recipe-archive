## Ingredients

- Approximately 1 1/2 kg salted salmon loin
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 4 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. The salmon loin (or head, if desired) is cut into pieces, rinsed very thoroughly and left to soak in milk overnight.
2. The pieces are wrapped in cloth, dipped in the egg whisked with cream, turned in crushed rusks and fried a beautiful brown in the butter.
3. If leaner salmon is desired, place the pieces on blotting paper as they are fried.
4. Arrange on a hot platter, garnish with parsley, serve with currant sauce and potatoes, or with creamed potatoes.
