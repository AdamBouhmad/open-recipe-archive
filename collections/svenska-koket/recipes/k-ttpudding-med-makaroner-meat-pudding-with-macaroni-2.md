## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipes 632, 631, or 633
- 1.5 hectograms fine macaroni
- 1.5 liters water
- 3 tablespoons melted butter (60 grams)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 grams)
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 grams)
- 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. The pudding can be made with beef, veal, or ham meat according to the aforementioned descriptions.
2. Break the macaroni into 2 cm long pieces, rinse them quickly in cold water.
3. Place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft.
4. Drain them in a colander and let them drain.
5. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the macaroni and shake well in the butter.
6. Season with salt and pepper if needed.
7. Butter a pudding mold with cold butter and coat with breadcrumbs.
8. Alternate layers of ground meat and macaroni in the mold; the first and last layer should be meat.
9. Cook the pudding as in recipe 638, turn it out onto a plate, and serve with the same kind of sauce.
