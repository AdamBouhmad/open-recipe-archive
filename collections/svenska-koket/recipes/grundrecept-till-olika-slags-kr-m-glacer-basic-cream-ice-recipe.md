## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 litre boiling cream or milk
- 150 gr. sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar in a saucepan for about 20 minutes.
2. Add the boiling cream little by little, then let the cream simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until it is thick and foamy.
3. Remove the saucepan from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
4. Note: Unskimmed milk can be used instead of cream, but then use 10 egg yolks and 180 gr. sugar.
5. For all cream ices, the proportions of eggs, sugar and cream, as well as the preparation, are the same; they differ only through various flavoring additions.
