## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal roast
- 175 grams larding fat
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 2 carrots
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 liter broth

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, pound lightly with a damp mallet and lard densely with fine strips of larding fat.
2. Line a roasting pan with slices of larding fat and place the roast in it.
3. Add the scraped carrots cut into thick slices, the peeled and chopped onion, the seasonings and butter.
4. Dilute with half of the broth and allow it to cook down under careful supervision so that the meat does not burn.
5. When the liquid on the bottom becomes thick and cloudy, add the remaining broth, and allow the meat to roast slowly for 1.5 to 2 hours.
6. Turn the roast a couple of times during roasting.
7. Strain and skim the sauce and serve as gravy.
