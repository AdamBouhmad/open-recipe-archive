## Ingredients

- 7 egg whites
- 1 egg yolk
- 200 gr. sugar
- 150–160 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and egg yolk with the sugar until light foam forms.
2. Add the flour, which should be sifted, and work the batter well.
3. Prepare the crisps as in the previous recipe.
4. Serve with coffee or tea.
