## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 grams salt
- 3-4 truffles
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter Jerusalem artichoke cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the Jerusalem artichokes as in the previous recipe.
2. Remove with a slotted spoon, let cool and cut into 3 mm thick slices.
3. Cut the truffle into thin slices and mix with the Jerusalem artichokes.
4. Place them in 6 small shells buttered with half of the butter.
5. Pour white sauce over; sprinkle the arrangement with grated cheese and crushed rusks, melt the remaining butter and pour over.
6. Place the shells in a baking pan with coarse salt and gratinize in a warm oven 10-15 minutes.
7. Serve immediately on a round platter with a folded napkin, garnished with parsley or salad.
