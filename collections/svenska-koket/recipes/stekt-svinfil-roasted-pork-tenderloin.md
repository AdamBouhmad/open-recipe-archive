## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork tenderloin
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 2 cups broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the tenderloin with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Brown it in the butter and salt it; add the hot broth and let the tenderloin cook slowly for 20-30 minutes.
3. When it is tender, remove it, cut it into thin slices, place on a serving platter and garnish with vegetables.
4. Strain the sauce, skim it and serve as gravy.
