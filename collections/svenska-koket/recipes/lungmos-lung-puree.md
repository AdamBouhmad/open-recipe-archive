## Ingredients

- heart and lungs of calf, sheep or pork
- water
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 1 red onion
- 20 white peppercorns
- 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 0.75 liter of broth
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. The heart and lungs are cut up, rinsed and soaked in water for 12-15 hours, during which the water is changed 2 times.
2. They are placed in as much cold water as to just cover the meat, well skimmed and the seasonings are added; allowed to cook until tender, then the windpipe and air passages are cleaned away and the meat is chopped very finely.
3. Butter and flour are sautéed for 2 minutes, the well-skimmed broth is added, the meat is placed in it, the vinegar is added and the puree is allowed to cook while stirring for 10 minutes, after which it is carefully seasoned and served with boiled potatoes and vinegar.
4. If the puree is too thick, it is diluted with a little more broth; in the opposite case it is finished with a little potato flour, mixed in cold water, and then allowed to cook for 2 minutes.
