## Ingredients

- 1 hg. flour
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. sugar
- 2 dcl. cream
- 1—2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tbsp. clarified butter (10 gr.)
- 1/2 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add the seasonings, cream and egg yolk, and stir the batter until it becomes even and shiny.
2. Clarify the butter, let it cool and stir it in last.
3. The lard in which the crusts are to be cooked should be of the best quality.
4. When the lard is placed in the pot, set the iron form in it to warm slowly.
5. The lard is sufficiently warm when a thin blue smoke rises from the surface.
6. Cook the crusts as in the previous recipe.
