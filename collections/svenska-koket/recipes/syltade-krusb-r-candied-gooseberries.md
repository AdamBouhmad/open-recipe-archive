## Ingredients

- 1 kg red, half-ripe gooseberries
- 3/4 kg lump sugar
- 2 1/2 - 3 parts water

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse quickly and let drain.
2. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup, which should be thick enough that a cooled sample threads between the fingers or a drop on a plate does not spread out.
3. Pour the hot syrup over the berries, cover them and let stand until the next day.
4. Strain the syrup and pour it hot over the berries again.
5. On the third time, boil the berries with the syrup.
6. Skim the jam carefully during cooking.
7. Shake it in the pot until it has cooled somewhat, then immediately pour into prepared jars.
