## Ingredients

- 50 gr. freshly roasted and ground coffee beans
- 1 litre cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar

## Directions

1. Add the ground coffee to the boiled cream, cover well and let stand in a warm place for about 20 minutes.
2. Strain through muslin.
3. Prepare the cream as described in the basic recipe and freeze in a machine as in recipe n:o 929.
4. Serve with ice cream bread.
