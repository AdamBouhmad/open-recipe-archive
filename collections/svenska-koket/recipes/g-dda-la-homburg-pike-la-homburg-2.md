## Ingredients

- 1 pike, 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter (40 gr.)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- For frying: 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 gr.)
- 3 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. Treat the pike as in the previous recipe, rub the fillets with salt and let rest for 1 hour, then dry thoroughly with a fish towel, brush on both sides with the clarified butter mixed with the beaten egg yolk, and sprinkle with the crushed rusks.
