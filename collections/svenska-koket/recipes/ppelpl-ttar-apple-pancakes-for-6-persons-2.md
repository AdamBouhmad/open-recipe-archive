## Ingredients

- 2-3 eggs
- 3 dcl. cream
- approximately 160 gr. flour
- 7 dcl. milk
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1 1/2 msk. melted butter (30 gr.)
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.) for the pan
- 2 dcl. brown sugar
- 2 dcl. water
- 1/2 tsk. finely grated lemon zest (optional)
- 1 liter loose apples

## Directions

1. Prepare the pancake batter as in the preceding recipe and let it swell.
2. Boil a clear syrup from the sugar and water, flavored with lemon zest if desired.
3. Peel the apples and remove the cores.
4. Cut the apples into 3 mm thick slices.
5. Place the slices directly in the sugar syrup and cook until half soft.
6. Heat the pancake pan slowly.
7. Melt the butter in a water bath and brush the pan with it.
8. Stir the batter well.
9. When the batter is poured on the pan, place an apple slice in each section.
10. Cook the pancakes on both sides.
11. Place on a hot plate, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm without jam.
