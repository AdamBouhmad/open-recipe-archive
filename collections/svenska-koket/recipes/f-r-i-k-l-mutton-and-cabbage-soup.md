## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg. mutton breast or saddle
- 1 small cabbage head
- 1 1/2 msk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 1/2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. flour
- 2 1/2 lit. cold water

## Directions

1. Rinse the mutton with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut it into pieces.
2. Brown it lightly together with the coarsely cut cabbage.
3. Sprinkle flour over it, add salt and pepper, and mix everything well.
4. Dilute with the cold water, and let the soup cook slowly for 2 1/2 - 3 hours.
5. Skim off all the fat carefully, season the dish and serve.
