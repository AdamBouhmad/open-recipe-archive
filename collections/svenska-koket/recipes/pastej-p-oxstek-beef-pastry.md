## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless beef roast
- 1.5 dcl meat sauce
- 100 g flour for the butter dough
- 3/4 dcl water (scant)
- 150 g butter

## Directions

1. Cut the roast into thin, even slices and warm the sauce slightly.
2. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 34, roll out to 1/2 cm thickness and line a smooth or fluted baking form with it.
3. The dough should hang 2 cm over the edge of the form.
4. Place the slices in the form and pour the meat sauce over them.
5. Fold the dough edge that hangs over the form over the meat and place a dough lid on top.
6. Decorate the dough lid with dough figures and brush with whisked egg white.
7. Place the pastry in a warm oven and it is done when the butter dough is baked.
8. Serve with meat sauce.
