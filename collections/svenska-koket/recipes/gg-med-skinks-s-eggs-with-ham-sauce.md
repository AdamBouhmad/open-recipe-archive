## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 1–1/2 deciliters cream
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tablespoon clarified butter (20 gr.)
- 1/2 hectogram lean, smoked ham
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 deciliter broth
- 3 deciliters milk
- 1 egg yolk
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Peel the eggs and cut them lengthwise in half; carefully remove the yolks and cut them into fine cubes.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the thin cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then carefully fold in the crumbled egg yolks and season the filling with salt and white pepper.
3. Fill the egg halves with the filling and pour the clarified butter over.
4. Cut the ham into fine cubes and fry in butter. Stir in the flour, add the broth and milk gradually. Let the sauce simmer for 8 minutes, stir in the egg yolk and let it simmer and season.
5. Place the eggs in a frying pan and put in the oven to heat through; then arrange on a warm serving dish and pour the ham sauce over.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve for the smorgasbord.
