## Ingredients

- 1-2 rutabagas
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 1-2 tablespoons syrup
- broth or water
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Peel the rutabagas and cut into oblong squares.
2. Heat the cream, add the rutabagas, and pour the syrup over them.
3. Brown the roots while stirring until they turn a nice brown color.
4. Add broth or water and let the roots cook slowly until completely soft.
5. Serve with salt meat or sausage.
