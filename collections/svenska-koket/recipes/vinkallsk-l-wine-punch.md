## Ingredients

- 150 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 liter water
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 1/4 liter crushed ice
- 5 dcl. white or red wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar, water, lemon slices and ice in the soup bowl and let stand 1/2 hour or until the sugar is melted.
2. Add the wine and lemon juice, taste the punch and serve immediately with potato starch pastries or rusks.
