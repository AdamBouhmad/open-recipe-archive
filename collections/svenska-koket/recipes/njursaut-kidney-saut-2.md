## Ingredients

- 2 calf kidneys
- 1/2 liter water for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 deciliters brown broth
- 1/2 wine glass Madeira

## Directions

1. Blanch the kidneys, that is, place them in cold salted water, let them come to a boil and boil uncovered for 3-4 minutes, after which they are placed on a sieve and allowed to cool.
2. Slice them thinly, sprinkle with the spices and turn in the flour.
3. Slice the mushrooms.
4. Heat the butter in a pan: when it is light brown, add the kidney and mushroom slices and brown them evenly while shaking.
5. Dilute with the broth and let the stew boil for 5 minutes, after which the wine is added and the sauté is allowed to cook an additional 8-10 minutes.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish with fried potatoes.
