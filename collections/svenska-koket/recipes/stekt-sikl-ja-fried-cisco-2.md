## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg. siklöja (cisco)
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 4 msk. mjöl (40 gr.)
- 1 hg. smör (butter)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish (see page 171) and score it on both sides, with 1 1/2 cm. between each score.
2. Rub it with salt and fry it on top of the stove as in recipe no. 330.
3. Serve with mashed potatoes or with stewed sugar peas mixed with one-third cooked, fresh carrots in cubes.
