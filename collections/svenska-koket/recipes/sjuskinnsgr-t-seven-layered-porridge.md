## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover rice groats or barley groats porridge
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the dish

## Directions

1. Grease a heatproof dish with the butter.
2. Place one layer of the porridge on it and sprinkle thickly with the sugar mixed with cinnamon.
3. Heat a pancake turner until glowing and use it to brown the sugar, then place another layer of porridge on top, sprinkle with sugar and brown it.
4. Proceed in the same way until there are 7 layers, hence the name.
5. Do not brown the top layer of sugar with the pancake turner; instead place the dish in a hot oven so that the sugar becomes nicely brown and the porridge is heated through.
6. Serve with milk.
