## Ingredients

- 1 litre fresh green gooseberries or 1/2 litre preserved gooseberries
- 2 litres water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 g)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse them, place them in boiling water together with the lemon zest and let them cook until soft
2. Strain the soup
3. Cook it and serve as in the preceding recipe
