## Ingredients

- Sweetbread from 1 calf
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed croutons
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Place the sweetbread in water so the blood is drawn out, blanch it thereafter, take it up and let it drain. Cut it into thin slices, these are brushed with egg and turned in crushed croutons.
2. Fry the slices thereafter in butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Place them on the serving dish, wipe the pan with a little bouillon or water, and pour the sauce over the sweetbread. Serve the arrangement with peas.
