## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg red bream
- 1 1/2 vinegar
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)

## Directions

1. For cooking, choose large red breams
2. Place them on a dish, brush with a little vinegar, let them rest for 1/2 hour, then scrape them well with a knife
3. Remove the gills and the innards through an incision made at the head
4. Carefully scrape away the dark membrane and cut off the fins
5. Rinse the fish very well, dry it, rub it with salt and let it rest for 1 hour, then dry it again
6. Cook it as in recipe no. 327 and requires great attention during cooking, as it is done in a few minutes
7. Place on a warm dish with the white side up, garnish with parsley and serve with wine sauce, parsley sauce, or crayfish sauce
8. Halibut is cleaned and cooked in the same way but requires longer cooking time. Serve with melted butter, grated horseradish and boiled potatoes
