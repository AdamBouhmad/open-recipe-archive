## Ingredients

- 1½ kg breast of beef
- salt, white pepper
- ½ hg prunes
- 1 hg dried apples
- 60 gr butter
- ½–1 liter broth or boiling water
- 1 part cream
- beef stock
- 1⅜ tbsp wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, remove all membranes and sinews.
2. Beat the meat well and rub it in with salt and white pepper.
3. Rinse the dried fruit and spread it out on half of the meat.
4. Fold the other half over, and sew the edges together.
5. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the meat in, and when it has taken on good color on all sides, dilute it with boiling broth or water.
6. Let the meat thereafter slowly cook 3–4 hours. When the meat is done, remove the strings, cut the meat into nice pieces, place on a dish and garnish with the fruit.
7. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
