## Ingredients

- 1/2 dcl. rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 50 gr. butter
- 5 hg. smoked ham

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft, approximately 15-20 minutes.
2. Pour them into a colander and let the water drain off well.
3. Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in the butter in a frying pan, then add the grains.
