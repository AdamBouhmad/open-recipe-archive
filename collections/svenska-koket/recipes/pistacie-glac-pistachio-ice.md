## Ingredients

- 75 grams pistachios
- 60 grams sweet almonds
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 liter boiling cream
- Base cream recipe
- Green food coloring (optional)
- A couple tablespoons arrack

## Directions

1. Blanch the pistachios and almonds, grind through a mill and pound with 1 tablespoon milk until fine.
2. Place in the boiling cream and let steep, covered, for 20 minutes.
3. Prepare the cream according to the base recipe, strain and whisk until cold.
4. If desired, color green with a little caramel coloring and flavor with a couple tablespoons arrack.
5. Freeze as in recipe no. 929.
