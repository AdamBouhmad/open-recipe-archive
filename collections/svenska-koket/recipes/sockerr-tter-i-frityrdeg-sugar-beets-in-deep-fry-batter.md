## Ingredients

- 24 fine, even sugar beets
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 80 grams dry flour
- 3 grams salt
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter (10 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter of the cooking liquid
- 1/2 deciliter milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 kilogram lard

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sugar beets as in the previous recipe and let drain on linen.
2. For the deep fry batter whisk together all ingredients into a thick pancake batter, which should stand and swell for about 2-3 hours.
3. Cut the sugar beets in half, take with a fork and dip into the batter, which must completely cover them, and cook in the lard until they are nicely golden brown.
4. Let them drain on gray paper, place on a platter with a folded napkin or parchment paper and serve very hot with melted butter.
