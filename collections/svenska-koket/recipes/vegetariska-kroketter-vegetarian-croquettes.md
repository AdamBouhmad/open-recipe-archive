## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 deciliter rice grains
- 1 liter milk
- 2 deciliter mushroom broth
- 1 can mushrooms (1/2 liter)
- 2 1/2 tablespoon butter (50 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt for breading
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil for breading
- 1 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1 kg. lard for frying
- 1 lettuce head for garnish
- 2-3 tomatoes for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the rice grains in milk with the mushroom broth until soft.
2. Add the canned mushrooms.
3. Stir in the egg yolks and season with salt and white pepper.
4. Form the croquettes into elongated shapes.
5. Bread and fry in lard as in recipe no. 49.
6. Serve as a course with meat or mushroom sauce and garnish with lettuce and tomatoes.
