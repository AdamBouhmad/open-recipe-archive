## Ingredients

- 4 liters fresh mushrooms: button mushrooms, chanterelles, or milk mushrooms, or 1 can button mushrooms (0.5 liters)
- 100 g butter
- 80 g flour
- 2 dl mushroom stock
- 3 dl boiling milk
- 5–6 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 0.5 tbsp butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp grated breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, rinse them and blanch them until soft. Pour them onto a sieve and let them drain well, then cut them into cubes and fry them in 30 g of butter until clear. Pour off the mushrooms and prepare the soufflé as in recipe no. 59 (Sparrissufflé), serve immediately after removing from the oven as a course with hollandaise or melted butter.
