## Ingredients

- 20 gr. gelatin (approximately 12 leaves)
- pineapple syrup and water together 1 liter
- juice of 1 lemon
- 225 gr. sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 dcl. white wine
- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr.)

## Directions

1. Open the pineapple can first, carefully reserve the syrup, and cut the pineapple into beautiful slices.
2. Prepare the jelly as in no. 924, and when it is clear, lay the pineapple slices in the mold in the same manner as described for grapes in the same number.
