## Ingredients

- 3 liters beautiful rose hips
- 1 liter water
- 0.5 to 1 kg granulated sugar
- 2 parts vinegar
- 10 cloves
- 1 piece cinnamon, 1 decimeter long

## Directions

1. Clean the rose hips and carefully remove all seeds.
2. From the water, vinegar, sugar and spices cook a thin brine, in which the rose hips are given a short boil.
3. Pour the preserve into jars and cover them.
