## Ingredients

- 2 large chickens approximately 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 hg. bread panade
- 2 hg. butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pluck the chickens, clean them, rinse very well, dry with a cloth and skin. Remove all meat from the bones and scrape it thoroughly free from sinews and membranes. Grind the meat 6 times through a meat grinder and pound it together with the seasonings with a wooden pestle in a mortar until the forcemeat becomes fine and smooth. Remove it and pound the panade (see no. 626) with the butter into an even dough, add the forcemeat and pound the mixture very well. Place it in a bowl and add the whisked eggs together with the yolks under continuous vigorous stirring. Pass the forcemeat through a hair sieve and stir again for approximately 1/2 hour.
