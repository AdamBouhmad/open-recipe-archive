## Ingredients

- Young partridges, roasted
- Beef and veal stock, about 1.5 liters
- Gelatin, dissolved in a little water
- Eggs
- Cold cooked mayonnaise sauce
- Finely chopped beets
- Poached eggs
- Oysters

## Directions

1. Roast the partridges until they are completely loose in texture
2. Boil stock from beef and veal, which when ready should yield about 1.5 liters
3. Strain the stock, skim well, and return to heat
4. Add the dissolved gelatin to the stock
5. When the stock is warm, whisk in the eggs and bring to a good boil while whisking, then strain through a cloth wrung out in hot water
6. Pour half of the clear aspic onto a flat dish to set
7. From the roasted, cooled partridges, cut 4-5 slices on each side of the breastbone
8. Lay these on the set aspic, pour over the remaining aspic, and set out to cool completely
9. Separate the meat slices with a knife so a little aspic remains on each slice
10. Arrange them in a wreath on a platter, fill the center with cold cooked mayonnaise sauce, and sprinkle finely chopped beets over it
11. Garnish the arrangement with poached eggs and beets, or with oysters
