## Ingredients

- 450 gr. fresh champignons
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- a little white pepper
- 3-4 dcl. cream or broth
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms as in the previous recipe, but do not cut away the stems; instead trim and scrape them
2. If the mushroom is large, cut it into pieces
3. Rinse quickly and let drain on cloth
4. Melt the butter and sauté the mushroom until the butter is clear
5. Season the mushroom and add the cream a little at a time while letting the mushroom cook until soft
6. If a thicker consistency is desired, sauté 15 gr. butter with the flour, add to the stew, and cook under stirring 5 minutes
7. Taste and adjust seasoning
8. Serve with smorgasbord, in omelet, in pastry shells, tartlets, etc.
