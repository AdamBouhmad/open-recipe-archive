## Ingredients

- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- 4 eggs
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 2 dcl. cream
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 1 tsp. potato flour (5 gr.)
- 2 dcl. granulated sugar for the mold (170 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. boiling water

## Directions

1. Melt the sugar in a buttered, slightly warm frying pan, dilute it with the boiling water, and use this to coat a smooth ring mold.
2. Scald the almonds, dry them in the oven and pass them through an almond mill.
3. Stir the egg yolks and sugar in a saucepan for 1/4 hour.
4. Bring the cream to a boil and pour it onto the eggs with vigorous whisking, after which let the cream simmer over the fire.
5. Remove the saucepan from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
6. Then add the almonds, lemon zest and flour.
7. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
8. Pour the mixture into the coated mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 3/4 hour.
9. Turn out the pudding after 10 minutes and serve warm with vanilla or lemon sauce, if desired.
