## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Water
- Egg yolks
- Heavy cream
- Zest of 1 orange
- Juice of 2 oranges
- Carmine coloring

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water together
2. Remove from heat and add egg yolks one at a time while whisking
3. Let the cream simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until thick and fluffy
4. Remove from heat and whisk until completely cold
5. Whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the cream
6. Color the cream red with carmine
7. Flavor with the zest of 1 orange and the strained juice of 2 oranges
8. Freeze in the mold as in the basic recipe
