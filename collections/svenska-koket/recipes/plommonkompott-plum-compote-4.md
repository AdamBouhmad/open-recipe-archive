## Ingredients

- 8 liters plums
- 8 hectograms sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Clean the plums, rinse them and let them drain.
2. Peel them, halve them with a silver knife and remove the pits. The plums should be placed immediately in the sugar solution, otherwise they will become dark.
3. Cook them as ordinary compote for 30 minutes.
