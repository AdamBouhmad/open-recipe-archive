## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- 1 liter water for blanching
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 2 tablespoons thick cream
- salt, white pepper, ginger

## Directions

1. Let the calf brain soak in water, changing it frequently so that all blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch the brain (see recipe no. 47) for 45 minutes and place on a sieve.
3. When cold, remove all membranes and chop the brain with a knife on a cutting board.
4. Melt the butter in a pot, add the brain and fry it well.
5. Add the cream and spices and bring the stew to a good boil.
6. Pour onto warm toast and garnish with small croutons (fried white bread slices).
7. Serve with the smorgasbord.
