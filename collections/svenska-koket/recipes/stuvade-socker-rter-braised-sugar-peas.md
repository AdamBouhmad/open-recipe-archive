## Ingredients

- 2.5 liters sugar pea pods
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1.5 tablespoons chopped parsley
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the pea pods and place in boiling, salted water together with 0.5 tablespoon of the butter.
2. When soft, remove them.
3. Fry the remaining butter with the flour, thin with the stock in which the peas were cooked, and let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes.
4. Add the pea pods and the chopped parsley, and season the stew with sugar and salt.
