## Ingredients

- 1 old hen
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 white peppercorns
- 1 clove
- 1.5 liters light broth
- 65 gr. rice grains
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the hen in the usual way and let it soak in plenty of water for 12 hours.
2. Dry it, cut into 4 pieces, place in cold water with salt, skim well and cook with the spices and 1 liter of broth until the meat is tender, about 4 hours.
3. Rinse the rice grains in hot water and cook them soft in the remaining broth.
4. When the meat is tender, separate it from the bones, which should cook again in the broth until it amounts to 2 liters.
5. Cut the meat into pieces, grind 4 times through a meat grinder and pound in a stone mortar together with 2 tablespoons of the butter and the cooked rice grains.
6. Pass the mixture through a fine sieve.
7. Strain and skim the broth.
8. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the broth little by little, add the mixture and let the purée cook while stirring for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
9. Whip the egg yolks in the soup bowl and pour in the purée while whisking vigorously.
10. Serve with cheese or butter pastry sticks.
