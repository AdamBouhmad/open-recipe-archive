## Ingredients

- 850 gr. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 lit. lukewarm milk
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 40 gr. butter or margarine
- 40 gr. sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as described on page 462 (set aside a small amount of flour for rolling out) and allow it to rise to double its size.
2. Stir the butter with the sugar until white and fluffy and work it into the risen dough, together with more flour if needed.
3. Work the dough on the work surface and divide it into 4 equal parts, which are rolled out into oblong buns or round cakes, which are well fluted.
4. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, allow to rise, brush with cold milk and bake in a warm oven until they have a nice color and feel light.
5. The dough can also be rolled out into rings, spirals and braids, which are brushed with egg and sprinkled with sugar when they have risen.
