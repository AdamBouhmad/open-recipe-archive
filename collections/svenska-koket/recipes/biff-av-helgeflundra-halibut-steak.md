## Ingredients

- 1½ kg halibut
- 2 tbsp salt
- 2 tsk white pepper
- vinegar
- 3 tbsp butter for roasting
- 3 cups fish broth
- 1 tsk flour
- 1½ tsk English mustard (prepared)
- 1 tsk soy

## Directions

1. Free the halibut from skin and bones, and cook broth on these.
2. Cut the fish into 3 cm thick slices, which are lightly pounded and placed on a serving dish.
3. Sprinkle the slices with salt, pepper and, if desired, a little finely chopped onion.
4. Pour over enough vinegar so that it is level with the fish.
5. After two hours, remove the slices and dry well with a cloth.
6. Brown them in the butter and place on a warm dish.
7. Stir the flour in the pan, dilute with the fish broth, add soy and mustard, and let the sauce come to a good boil, taste it and pour it over the fish.
8. Place it on the serving dish and garnish with parsley and small butter pastry squares.
