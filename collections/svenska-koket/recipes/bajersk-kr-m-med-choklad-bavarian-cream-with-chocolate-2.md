## Ingredients

- 2 hg granulated sugar
- 2 dcl water
- 1 vanilla pod
- 1 hg unsweetened chocolate
- 10 leaves gelatin
- 1 dcl hot water for the gelatin
- 7 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar with the water and vanilla until a clear, syrupy syrup, then remove the vanilla pod.
2. Melt the chocolate in a water bath, without adding water, and stir it into the sugar syrup.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, dissolve it in the warm water and add it, then stir the cream until it is cold.
4. Then add the cream whipped until stiff, and pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours to set.
5. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper.
6. The cream can also be poured into a mold with a lid and frozen, but then only 3 leaves gelatin are used.
