## Ingredients

- 500 gr. boneless roasted or cooked meat
- 3 msk. grated cheese
- 1 msk. melted butter (20 gr.)
- 1 1/2 msk. butter for the sauce (30 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 4 dcl. meat gravy or broth
- salt, white pepper
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold
- 3 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices.
2. Butter a heatproof dish or pie pan and sprinkle with half of the rusks.
3. Layer the meat alternately with the cheese and sauce, with the top layer being sauce, sprinkled with the remaining rusks and cheese.
4. Finally pour the clarified butter over it and place the dish in a warm oven to get a nice brown color.
5. For the sauce: fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the meat gravy and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes, then season with spices.
6. Serve with mashed potatoes.
