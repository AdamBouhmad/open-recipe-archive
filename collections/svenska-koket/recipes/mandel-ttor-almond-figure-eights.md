## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almond
- 6 bitter almonds
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 25 grams sweet almond for garnish

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds and grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound together with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work in
3. From the almond mass make small cakes in the shape of figure-eights
4. A scalded sweet almond, cut in half lengthwise, is inserted into each loop
5. Place the cakes on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets and bake in weak oven heat
