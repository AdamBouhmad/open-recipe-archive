## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fatty herrings
- 3/4 liter raw potatoes
- 2 red onions
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times.
2. Skin the herring, remove it from the water, split it down the middle, and remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap them in a fish towel, and cut each herring half into six pieces.
4. Rinse the potatoes, peel them, and cut into thin slices.
5. Peel the onions and chop them finely.
6. Butter an ovenproof omelet pan with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
7. On the bottom of the pan place a layer of potatoes, then a layer of herring, then onions and a little pepper.
8. Continue layering until there are 3 layers of potatoes and 2 layers of herring.
9. Place the butter in flakes on top and sprinkle with rusks.
10. Bake in a good oven heat for approximately 45 minutes and serve as a breakfast dish or with a smorgasbord.
