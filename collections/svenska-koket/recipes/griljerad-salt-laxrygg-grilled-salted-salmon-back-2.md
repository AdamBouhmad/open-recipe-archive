## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg salted salmon back
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed hardtack
- 2 tbsp butter or stock

## Directions

1. Cut the salted salmon back into pieces and soak out.
2. Remove the pieces and dry them well, brush with egg, and roll in crushed hardtack.
3. Fry them in butter or stock and place on brown paper so the excess fat is absorbed.
4. Serve with currant sauce.
