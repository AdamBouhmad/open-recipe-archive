## Ingredients

- 40 gr. butter
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 225 gr. prime wheat flour

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and heat just enough so the butter melts.
2. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with the sugar.
3. Add the milk little by little, and work the dough with a spoon until it is smooth and glossy.
4. Cover it with a cloth and let it rest in a warm place during rolling out.
5. Roll out one-third of the dough at a time.
6. Roll the dough thin and stretch it out on a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet.
7. Prick the cake well and cut with a wheel along a ruler into strips 1 decimeter long and 7 cm. wide.
8. Bake beautifully golden-brown in a not too hot oven, cut along the marked lines and serve with coffee or tea with butter or orange marmalade.
