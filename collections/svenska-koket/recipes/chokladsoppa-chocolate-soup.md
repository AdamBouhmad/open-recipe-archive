## Ingredients

- 100 grams chocolate
- 2 liters milk
- 0.5 to 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons potato flour
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Bring milk to boil, break up the chocolate and add it.
2. Whisk the milk over heat until it boils and chocolate melts.
3. Add sugar and thicken the soup with potato flour mixed with a little water.
4. Bring to a good boil, pour up, and serve with whipped cream.
