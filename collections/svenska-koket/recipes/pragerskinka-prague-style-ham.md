## Ingredients

- 1 lightly smoked ham, approximately 5 kg
- 2 bottles Rhine wine
- water

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in plenty of cold water for 1 day. Brush the skin thoroughly with a soft brush and dry it with a cloth.
2. Place the ham in a pot that is not too large, just large enough to fit the ham snugly. Add wine and enough water so that it reaches the same level as the meat.
3. Cook the ham as in recipe 556. Place it in a deep dish, pour the cooking liquid over it, and let it cool in the liquid.
4. Serve with the skin on and carve at the table.
5. Serve with red cabbage or other vegetables and potatoes.
