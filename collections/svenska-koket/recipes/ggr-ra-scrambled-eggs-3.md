## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 16 tbsp milk
- salt
- 1 tbsp butter

## Directions

1. Eggs, milk and salt are whisked well together.
2. A saucepan is buttered, the egg mixture is poured in, and the saucepan is placed over low heat.
3. As the eggs begin to set, they are carefully lifted up with a spoon.
