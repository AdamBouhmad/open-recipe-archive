## Ingredients

- 130 gr. apples
- 130 gr. currants
- 130 gr. raisins
- 60 gr. candied fruit
- 130 gr. beef suet
- 1 tbsp. wheat flour
- 130 gr. crushed rusks
- 130 gr. sugar
- 1 tsp. salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 part brandy
- 1-1.5 cups water
- 1 tbsp. butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the cores and chop finely.
2. Clean and wash the currants, rinse the raisins, remove the seeds and chop.
3. Cut the candied fruit into thin slices.
4. Free the suet from membranes and chop finely together with the wheat flour.
5. Mix all dry ingredients well together.
6. Gradually add the egg yolks, brandy and water, stirring the mixture vigorously for 2-3 hours.
7. Pack the mixture together, place a plate on top, and let it rest for at least two days.
8. When the pudding is to be cooked, beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the mixture.
9. Pour the batter into a buttered mold, cover with buttered paper and cook in a water bath for at least 3 hours.
10. Serve with flaming sauce.
