## Ingredients

- 2 hg. butter
- 1 hg. granulated sugar
- 4 hg. steam-prepared oat groats
- 40 gr. wheat flour, for rolling out

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir until it becomes white and fluffy, then add the sugar and groats.
2. Roll out the dough to 1/2 cm. thickness and cut with a square, smooth measure into cakes, which are placed on a buttered baking sheet and baked a beautiful light golden in a not too hot oven.
3. The cakes are allowed to cool on the baking sheet.
