## Ingredients

- 6 two-öre almond biscuits
- 1 1/2 hg melted butter
- 1 hg coarsely crushed rock sugar
- 150 gr sugar
- 3 dcl water
- 1 1/2 liters good, tart apples
- 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Grease a copper or tin mold with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks
2. Split the biscuits lengthwise, dip them in the melted butter and turn them in the sugar
3. Line the mold with biscuit slices, fill with apple compote, and place the remaining biscuit slices on top as a lid
4. Bake for approximately 1/2 hour in ordinary oven heat
5. Turn out and serve warm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream
6. The apple compote is prepared as in recipe no. 820. If desired, the compote can be mixed with some jam
