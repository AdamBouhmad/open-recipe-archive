## Ingredients

- 1 liter tender sugar peas, or about 5 liters unpodded
- 10 small, fresh carrots
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.)
- 4-4 1/2 deciliters pea broth
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the peas as in the preceding recipe. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry and cut into fine dice.
2. Place the peas and carrots in boiling water, seasoned with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until they are tender.
