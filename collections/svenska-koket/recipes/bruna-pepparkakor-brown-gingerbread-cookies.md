## Ingredients

- 850 gr. yellow farina
- 1 1/2 dcl. syrup
- 3 dcl. water
- 2 tsp. ginger
- 2 tsp. finely grated orange peel
- 2 tsp. cinnamon
- 2 tsp. cloves
- 22 gr. soda
- 1/2 wine glass cognac
- 530 gr. washed butter
- 2 kg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar, syrup and water, then add the spices and the dissolved soda dissolved in the cognac.
2. Pour this mixture over the butter and stir the dough until it is completely smooth.
3. Work in as much wheat flour as needed to make a firm dough, let it stand overnight.
4. Save the rest of the flour for baking.
5. Roll out the dough thinly and cut with round or oblong, fluted cutters into cookies, place them on a well-buttered sheet and bake in a warm oven.
6. Let the cookies cool on the sheet.
