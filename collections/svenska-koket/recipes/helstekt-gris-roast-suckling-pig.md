## Ingredients

- 1 small tender suckling pig
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 2 teaspoons white pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- apples
- prunes
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the pig with a cloth dipped in hot water, then rub it inside and out with the spices and fill it with apples so that it retains its shape, and sew it closed.
2. Grease a long pan with the butter, place the pig in it with the back down, put it in the oven and cover with wet paper.
3. After 1.5 hours, turn it over and place it on its knees.
4. During roasting, it should be frequently rubbed with a piece of butter placed in a linen cloth (this makes the skin tender and beautifully light brown).
5. Toward the end of roasting, which takes about 2 hours total, add the broth.
6. Strain the sauce, skim it and serve as gravy.
7. Serve the pig with some sour fruits or salads.
