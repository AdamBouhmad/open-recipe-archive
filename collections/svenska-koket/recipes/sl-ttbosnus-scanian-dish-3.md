## Ingredients

- 1/2 twenty-five öre coarse spiced bread
- 125 gr. butter
- 1 1/2 cup raspberry jam
- 2-3 sugar-coated chocolate cakes (at 25 öre each)
- 4-5 dcl. thick cream
- 1 msk. vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. The bread, which should be 3-4 days old, is grated and fried in the butter until it is completely cooked through and crispy, then allowed to cool.
2. Grate the chocolate and whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
3. In a compote bowl, lay the fried bread, jam, chocolate and cream alternately, in the order mentioned.
4. The top layer should be cream, which is garnished with small dollops of jam.
