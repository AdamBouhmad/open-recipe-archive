## Ingredients

- 4—5 hg. boneless, roasted or cooked veal
- 1 red onion
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 3—4 dcl. meat gravy or bouillon
- 2 msk. tomato puree
- a little paprika
- 2 msk. finely cut preserved cucumber
- 1 msk. Madeira
- 1 tsk. cognac
- salt, sugar, 1/2 msk. soy

## Directions

1. The meat is chopped (not ground) finely.
2. The onion is peeled, chopped and browned in the butter together with the flour (see Sauces, p. 210), the meat gravy and tomato puree are thinned in and the sauce is allowed to boil for 5 minutes.
3. The chopped meat is added and the stew is seasoned with paprika, cucumber, wine, cognac, spices and soy.
4. The preparation is heated but must not boil.
