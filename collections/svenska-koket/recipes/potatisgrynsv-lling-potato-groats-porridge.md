## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 1/4 cup potato groats
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil.
2. Shake in the groats, and let the porridge simmer slowly under stirring for 1/2 hour, or until the groats are clear.
3. Taste and serve.
