## Ingredients

- 0.5 celery
- 1 leek
- 0.5 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 2 cups fresh peas
- salt, water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1.5 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- 200 grams spinach
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 part cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the celery, leek and Jerusalem artichokes in the usual way and cook them whole in salted water. Cook the cauliflower, parsley bunch and peas also in salted water in a separate pot.
2. Mix butter and flour together and thin it with the boiling broth. Add the cleaned and rinsed spinach, and let the soup cook for 10 minutes. Add the Jerusalem artichokes together with their broth, but not the celery and leek; also add the broth in which the cauliflower and peas cooked, after which season the soup.
