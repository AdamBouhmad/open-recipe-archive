## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them into thin slices from the stem downward, sprinkle with salt and let them stand in a press for a few hours.
2. Pour off the liquid that has formed, and layer the cucumbers alternately with fine salt in a jar, which is bound closed.
3. When you want to use them, rinse off the salt and place the cucumbers in cold water until they are adequately salty.
