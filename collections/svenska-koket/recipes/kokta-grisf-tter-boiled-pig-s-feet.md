## Ingredients

- 4 pig's feet
- 4 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 gr.)
- soda, the size of a pea

## Directions

1. Pig's feet should lie in plenty of cold water for 1 day.
2. They are then blanched with hot water and scraped well, put in cold water with the salt and soda added, skimmed and allowed to boil until a whisk easily passes through the meat.
3. They are immediately taken out of the broth and cooled in cold water to become white.
4. The soda is used during cooking for this same reason.
5. Kept in brine.
6. Served with beetroot.
