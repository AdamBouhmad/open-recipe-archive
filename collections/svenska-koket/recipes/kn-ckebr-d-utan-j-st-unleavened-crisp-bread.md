## Ingredients

- 2 hectograms wheat flour
- 2 hectograms rye sieve or coarse rye flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 grams)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)
- 75 grams cold butter
- approximately 2 deciliters milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with sugar and salt.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work in with fingertips.
3. Add enough milk to make a moderately firm dough that is worked until it becomes supple and smooth and releases from the bowl.
4. Roll the dough out very thin, prick lightly, and cut with a pastry wheel following a ruler into oblong rectangles.
5. Place on buttered sheets and bake light brown in a not too hot oven.
6. These make good envelope bread and sandwich bread.
