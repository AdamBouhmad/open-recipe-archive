## Ingredients

- 2 salted herrings
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup crushed rusks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Soak the herring the day before, clean and remove skin and bones.
2. Butter an omelette dish with half of the butter.
3. Roll the herring halves in the crushed rusks and place them in the dish.
4. Place the rest of the butter in flakes on top and place the herring in the oven.
5. When it is halfway done (after 5-10 minutes), pour the cream over and let the herring cook until it is very tender.
