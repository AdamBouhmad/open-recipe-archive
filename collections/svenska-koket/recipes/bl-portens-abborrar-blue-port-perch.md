## Ingredients

- 2 kg perch
- 2 tbsp salt
- 2 tsp vinegar
- 2 hg butter
- 6 tbsp coarsely chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp chopped dill
- 1.5 tbsp flour
- 5-6 tbsp water

## Directions

1. Scale the perch, gut them and rinse well, and rub in inside and outside with a mixture of the salt and vinegar.
2. Grease a casserole with butter, and lay the fish alternately in it; between each layer lay butter, dill, parsley and flour.
3. Last add the water.
4. Let the fish only simmer gently with a tightly fitting lid; the liquid is thereby preserved better.
5. When the fish is cooked, lay it on a platter.
6. Whisk the sauce in the casserole and pour it over the fish, without either straining or thinning it.
