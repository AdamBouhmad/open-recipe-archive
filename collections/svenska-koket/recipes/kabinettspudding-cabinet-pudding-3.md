## Ingredients

- Leftover sponge cake, cut into small cubes (approximately 5 dcl.)
- 1 dcl. currants (60 gr.)
- 30 gr. candied fruits
- 4 eggs
- 50 gr. sugar
- 4 dcl. good milk
- 1 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. The sponge cake, which should be cut into small cubes, is placed in a buttered and breadcrumb-dusted timbal mold in alternating layers with the well-rinsed currants and the chopped candied fruits.
2. The eggs are beaten well with the sugar, the milk is added and the mold is filled with this mixture.
3. The pudding is baked in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour, turned out of the mold and served very hot with warm vanilla sauce (see recipe no. 454).
