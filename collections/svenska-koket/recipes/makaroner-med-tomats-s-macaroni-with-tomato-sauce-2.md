## Ingredients

- 1.5 hectograms macaroni
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1.5 tablespoons tomato purée

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into suitable pieces and cook until completely soft in salted water.
2. Pour off the water, add cold butter and tomato purée, and serve the macaroni with roasted meat.
