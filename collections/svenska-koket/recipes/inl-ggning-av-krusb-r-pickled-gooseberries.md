## Ingredients

- Unripe gooseberries
- Water

## Directions

1. Snip and rinse the unripe gooseberries
2. Place them in boiling water for approximately 1 minute
3. Drain and allow to dry completely on linen
4. Place in clean, dry bottles
5. Cork and seal the bottles with resin immediately
