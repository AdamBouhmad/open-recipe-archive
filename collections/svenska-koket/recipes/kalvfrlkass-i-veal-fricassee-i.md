## Ingredients

- 1.5-2 kg veal breast
- 1.5 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr)
- 6 deciliters veal broth
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2-3 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 lemon, sliced

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the meat as in the previous recipe, place on a warm plate and cover while the sauce is being made
2. Make the sauce as in recipe number 441 and pour it over the meat
3. Garnish the dish with lemon slices and serve with rice
4. If desired, one can add 2 boiled carrots diced and 2 deciliters boiled peas to the sauce
5. The meat can also be cooked with 0.5 lemon in slices and the sauce seasoned with Madeira instead of lemon juice; then omit the egg yolks
6. The sauce can, if desired, be seasoned with curry instead of lemon and the rice sprinkled with a little dry curry
