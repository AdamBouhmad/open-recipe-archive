## Ingredients

- 3 hg. washed butter
- 150 gr. sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 hg. sweet almond
- 325 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to white foam, then add the egg yolks, one at a time, the blanched and ground almond and finally the flour.
2. Work the dough until it becomes smooth, then let it rest in a cold place for about 2 hours before using.
3. This dough cannot be rolled out but is pressed directly into forms or piped through a piping bag.
