## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg pork meat (preferably loin)
- 425 grams fat pork
- 1.33 liters water
- 2 parts beer
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate
- 2 teaspoons ground white pepper
- 1 teaspoon ground strong pepper
- 5 meters casings (hooked casing)
- 2 tablespoons salt for rubbing
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar for rubbing
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate for rubbing

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water.
