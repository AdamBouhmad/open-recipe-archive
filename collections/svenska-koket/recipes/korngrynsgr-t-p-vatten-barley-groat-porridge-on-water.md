## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 liters water
- 3 deciliters fine crushed barley groats (240 grams)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 grams)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)

## Directions

1. Boil the water, add the rinsed groats under vigorous whisking so the groats do not lump. Let the porridge cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely tender, or 3-4 hours, stirring often so it does not burn. Season with salt and cold butter and serve with milk.
