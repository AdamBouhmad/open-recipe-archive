## Ingredients

- 2 veal kidneys
- 1/2 liter water for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 1 tin mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 deciliters brown beef broth
- 1/2 wine glass Madeira

## Directions

1. Blanch the kidneys, that is, place them in cold salted water, let them come to a boil and cook uncovered 3-4 minutes, then place them in a colander and let them cool.
2. Cut them into thin slices, which are sprinkled with the spices and turned in the flour.
3. Cut the mushrooms into slices.
4. Brown the butter in a pan; when it is light brown, add the kidney and mushroom slices and brown evenly while shaking.
5. Dilute with the broth and let the stew cook 5 minutes, after which add the wine and let the sauté cook an additional 8-10 minutes.
6. Serve as a breakfast course with fried potatoes.
