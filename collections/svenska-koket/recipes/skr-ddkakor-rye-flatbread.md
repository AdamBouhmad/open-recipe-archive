## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rye sifted flour
- 325 gr wheat flour
- 1/2 litre skimmed milk
- 25 gr pressed yeast
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/2 tbsp crushed fennel or anise (2 tbsp syrup)

## Directions

1. From the rye flour, a portion of the wheat flour, the lukewarm milk, and the yeast mixed with salt, make a dough as described on the preceding page
