## Ingredients

- Medium-sized crayfish (20 crayfish per batch)
- 2 liters water per 20 crayfish
- 45 gr. coarse salt per 20 crayfish
- Dill flowers to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish in cold water.
2. Bring water to a vigorous boil and add the crayfish.
3. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
4. Transfer to a dish and let cool in enough of the cooking liquid so it covers them evenly.
5. Arrange high on a round plate, garnish with dill, and serve for the smorgasbord.
