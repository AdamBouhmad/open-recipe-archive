## Ingredients

- 1 kg inner loin or French cut (boneless)
- 2 tsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 small red onions
- 1 liter potatoes
- 3-4 parts weak broth
- ¾-1 part white French wine or beer

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into slices across the grain. Pound the slices, season and brown quickly in the butter.
2. Peel the onion, cut it into slices and brown it likewise. Peel the potatoes and cut them into slices.
3. Butter a form or pan with a tight-fitting lid, place a layer of potatoes in it, then a layer of steaks and a little onion. Continue layering everything, with the top layer being potatoes.
4. Deglaze the frying pan with the broth, and pour this (together with the wine or beer) over the meat. Cover and let it simmer slowly for 2-3 hours.
