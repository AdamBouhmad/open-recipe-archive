## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters water
- 1 deciliter syrup
- 8 hectograms graham flour
- 50 grams pressed yeast
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- approximately 4 hectograms wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the pans

## Directions

1. Boil the water with the syrup and mix with the graham flour.
2. Work the dough quickly, cover well and let it stand overnight.
3. Early the following morning, add the yeast mixed with the salt and 2 tablespoons lukewarm water, then work the dough well and let it rise covered in a warm place until it begins to rise.
