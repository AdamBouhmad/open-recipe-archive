## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 1 vanilla pod
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tsp. potato flour (5 gr.)
- 1 hg. sugar
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 2 dcl. milk
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, milk, vanilla pod, and potato flour in a saucepan and whisk vigorously over the heat until the cream is thick and creamy.
2. Pour out the cream and whisk until it is cold.
3. Remove the vanilla pod, rinse it, and store it in an airtight jar for future use.
4. Add the cream whipped to stiff peaks together with vanilla sugar, if desired.
5. Pour the cream into a compote bowl and serve with pastries or use as filling in cakes.
