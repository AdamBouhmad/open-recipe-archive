## Ingredients

- 2 hectograms lean beef
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 0.25 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon potato flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder, then pound well together with the butter.
2. Mix the meat mixture with the crushed rusks, milk, potato flour, and seasonings, and work until it becomes smooth.
3. Form small meatballs from the mixture, which are cooked in salted water or weak broth for about 10 minutes.
4. Serve with cabbage or strong broth soups in general.
