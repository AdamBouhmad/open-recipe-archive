## Ingredients

- 1 kg veal liver
- 50 g pork fat
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 caul fat
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 6 deciliters boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 g)
- liver sauce
- 1-2 deciliters thick cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 0.5 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. The liver, which should be light and tender, should soak in water for about 0.5 hour, then dry well, clean, and lard with fine strips of fat rolled in a mixture of the salt and pepper
2. Wrap in caul fat and fry in butter until golden brown
3. Pour boiling milk around it and add soy sauce
4. Make the sauce by frying butter and flour together, add liver sauce and thick cream, season with salt, sugar, and white pepper, and finish with cold butter
