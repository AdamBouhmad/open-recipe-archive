## Ingredients

- 10 litres apples
- half the weight of sugar against the peeled apples
- 3 parts water
- juice and zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into sections, and remove the cores.
2. Place them on the heat with the water, sugar and lemon, let them cook with constant stirring until the apples fall apart and form a paste.
3. Pour it warm into a crock and cover, once it has cooled, with a thin layer.
