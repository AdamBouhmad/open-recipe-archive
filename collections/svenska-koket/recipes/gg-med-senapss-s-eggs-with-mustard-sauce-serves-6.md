## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 slices white bread
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- For the mustard sauce: 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon English mustard
- 5 deciliters milk
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 5 minutes, dip in cold water, and shell them.
2. Cut the bread slices into oblong pieces, hollow them out, and fry them nicely golden brown in butter.
3. Place them on a warm plate and lay one egg on each slice.
4. Fry the butter, flour, and mustard together for 2 minutes, add the milk, and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
5. Taste and season with the spices and pour over the eggs.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
