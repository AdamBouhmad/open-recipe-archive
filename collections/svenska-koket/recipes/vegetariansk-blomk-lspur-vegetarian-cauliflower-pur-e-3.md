## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads
- 1/2 root celery
- 1 leek
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower in the usual way and divide into florets, which are soaked in vinegar-mixed water (1 liter water, 1 tablespoon vinegar) for 1 hour
2. Peel the celery, cut into cubes; clean the leek, rinse and cut into pieces
3. Cook the vegetables in water with a little salt until completely tender, then press through a sieve
4. Prepare and serve the purée as in recipe no. 266
