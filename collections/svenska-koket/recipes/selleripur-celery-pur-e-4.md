## Ingredients

- 2 celeries
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 liters secondary broth
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- cayenne
- 2–3 tablespoons sherry
- 2–3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and cut the celeries into small pieces.
2. Set aside half a celery and cut it into dice, which are boiled in salted water and served in the soup.
3. Prepare and serve the purée as in the preceding recipe.
