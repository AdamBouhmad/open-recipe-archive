## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter peas
- 8 deciliters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the peas and rinse them
2. Put them in cold, soft water (see page 338) and let them cook slowly until completely tender, which takes 2 1/2-3 hours
3. Thin with warm water during cooking if needed
4. Season with salt and stir in the cold butter at the end
5. Serve with pickled herring, fried bacon and meat
6. Note: The peas cook more easily if soaked in water the night before, but will then have poor color
