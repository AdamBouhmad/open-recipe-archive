## Ingredients

- 2 kg pears
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Place the pears in boiling water and allow them to cook until almost soft.
2. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup and allow it to cool.
3. Peel the pears, scrape the stems, and cut a cross into each pear.
4. As they are peeled, immediately place them in the sugar syrup.
5. Place on heat and allow to cook until they are clear and soft.
6. Remove the pears and place in a jar or crock.
7. Allow the syrup to cook down until it becomes thick and pour it hot over the fruit.
