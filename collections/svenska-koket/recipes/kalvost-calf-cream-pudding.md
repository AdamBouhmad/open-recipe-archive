## Ingredients

- 1 liter raw milk
- 1/2 liter cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- 15 almonds (2 bitter) (15 gr.) or 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. The milk, cream, sugar, salt and the blanched, ground almonds or cinnamon are whisked well.
2. The mixture is tested in a small pan over gentle heat; if it is too stiff, a little more cream is added, in the opposite case 1-2 whisked eggs.
3. It is then poured into a buttered mold and baked in weak oven heat or in a water bath, in the oven or on top of the stove.
4. Serve with brûlée sauce, meat sauce or sour cream.
5. The Kalvost can also be baked in a glazed mold (see brûlée pudding).
