## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hg. star-shaped macaroni
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 1/2 liters beef broth
- 1 tbsp. grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 dcl. sherry or Madeira
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The macaroni is placed in boiling salted water and allowed to cook until completely soft, then poured into a colander, rinsed with cold water so it does not stick together, and placed in the soup bowl.
2. The broth is brought to a boil and seasoned with the cheese, spices and wine.
3. The egg yolks are beaten with the cream and whisked into the soup, which is then poured over the macaroni.
4. Served very hot.
