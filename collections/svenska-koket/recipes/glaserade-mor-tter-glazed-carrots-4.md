## Ingredients

- 1 liter small carrots (karotts)
- 60 gr. butter
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 4 tsp. sugar (20 gr.)
- 4 dcl. boiling water
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Small pear-shaped carrots, so-called karotts, are best suited for this preparation method
2. Clean the carrots, rinse, brush well, scrape neatly, rinse again, dry in a cloth and cut in half lengthwise or in quarters, depending on size
3. Heat the butter in a saucepan, add the carrots, sprinkle with salt and sugar and fry them well in the butter
4. Pour on the water and let it boil with the lid on until the carrots are almost tender, then remove the lid and let the sauce boil down so that it becomes thick as syrup
5. Shake the carrots gently in the sauce until they are evenly glazed
6. If desired, add the finely chopped parsley at the end
7. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with cooked vegetables
8. If the carrots are large and old they should be shaped like oblong olives, placed in cold salted water, boiled for 5 minutes, drained in a colander and prepared thereafter as above
