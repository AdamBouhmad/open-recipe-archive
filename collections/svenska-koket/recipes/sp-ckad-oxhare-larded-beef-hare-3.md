## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1 hg lard
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- a little white pepper
- 1/2 liter boiling broth

## Directions

1. The hare, which should have hung 4-8 days depending on the season, is prepared and larded (see page 232), rubbed with spices and tied so that it has a beautiful shape.
2. The butter is browned in an iron pot, the meat is placed in it and browned beautifully on all sides, diluted with half of the broth and put in a hot oven to roast about 3/4-1 hour, basting it every 10th minute and diluting as needed.
3. Sliced into thin slices, placed on a hot platter, moistened with a couple of tablespoons of sauce, garnished with parsley and vegetables or vegetable salad in shells and served with strained sauce, see no. 429. The hare can also be diluted with cream instead of broth, served then with browned potatoes and currant jelly.
