## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tablespoon flour (30 gr.)
- 5 deciliter meat or fish broth or 6 deciliter good milk
- 2—3 tablespoon grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry 1 tablespoon of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
2. Gradually add the broth or milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the horseradish together with the remaining butter and season the sauce with seasonings.
4. Scrape the horseradish, rinse it, let it soak in cold water for 1 hour, dry it with a cloth and grate it on a grater.
