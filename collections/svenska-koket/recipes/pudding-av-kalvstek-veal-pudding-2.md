## Ingredients

- 4 hg. fried, boneless veal or chicken meat
- 1 hg. raw, boneless veal meat
- 2 hg. bread panada
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 msk. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the onion
- 5 dcl. cream
- For the mold: 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Chop the fried meat finely, grind the raw meat 3 times through the meat grinder and mix with the fried.
2. The raw meat can be omitted, but the pudding will have a finer flavor if it is used.
3. Lightly brown the panada (see no. 626) in 1 msk. of the butter
