## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work them into the almond mixture
3. Place the mixture on baking sheets with spoons into small cakes
4. Bake in very weak oven heat
