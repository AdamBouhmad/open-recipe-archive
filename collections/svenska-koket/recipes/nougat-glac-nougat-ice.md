## Ingredients

- 125 grams sugar
- 100 grams almonds
- 120 grams of the prepared nougat
- Base vanilla ice cream

## Directions

1. Make nougat from 125 grams sugar and 100 grams almonds (see nougat recipe).
2. Pound 120 grams of the nougat, press it through a sieve and mix into finished cooked cream for vanilla ice.
3. Reserve the remaining nougat for garnish.
4. Freeze as in recipe no. 929.
