## Ingredients

- 35 gr. butter
- 425 gr. sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 425 gr. sugar
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 2 liters apples
- 2 dcl. sugar (170 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. water
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the pan
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir until it is white and fluffy.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, grind them and pound them in a mortar to a fine mass.
3. Whip the egg yolks, egg whites and sugar for 1/2 hour with a wire whisk.
4. Mix the egg mixture with the butter and almonds, and lastly add the flour.
5. The apples should be cooked the day before. Rinse them, dry them, peel them, cut them into fine wedges, remove the cores and cook with water and sugar until they fall apart, then strain and let them cool.
6. Grease a pan or baking dish with the butter mixed with flour, and sprinkle with crushed rusks. Lay first a layer of dough, then the apples, then the rest of the dough, and bake the cake approximately 3/4 hour in ordinary oven heat, or until the dough feels dry when tested with a whisk. Remove the cake and let it stand 1/2 hour, then turn it out and serve warm or cold with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
