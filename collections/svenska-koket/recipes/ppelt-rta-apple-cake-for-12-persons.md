## Ingredients

- 400 gr butter
- 400 gr wheat flour
- 90 gr sweet almond
- 90 gr powdered sugar
- 3 liters apples
- 3 cups sugar
- 1-2 parts water

## Directions

1. From the butter, flour, the blanched and grated almond and sugar prepare a regular butter pastry, which is allowed to cool.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into sections and cook with the sugar and water to a compote, which is beaten up to cool.
3. Roll out the pastry to approximately 1/2 cm thickness.
4. In a form or baking pan bake a thin base of the pastry, which is allowed to cool.
5. Line the edges of the form with pastry, place the apple compote in, and fold the dough edges over the fruit.
6. Place the rest of the dough as a lid on the fruit and press well against the folded edge.
7. Bake the cake brown in moderate oven heat.
8. Remove from the form only after it has almost cooled completely, and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
