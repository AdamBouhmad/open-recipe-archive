## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 decilitres Carolina rice grains (125 gr.)
- 1 1/2 litres water
- 4 oranges
- 1 1/2 decilitres sugar (128 gr.)
- 3 decilitres thick cream
- 5-6 leaves gelatin, 3 tablespoons water
- 2 oranges in segments for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald in hot water, place in boiling water and cook until soft, or about 20 minutes.
2. Strain in a sieve and rinse with cold water, letting it drain well.
3. Peel the oranges, remove the white pith carefully, then cut into small cubes, remove the seeds and mix with sugar.
4. Beat the cream to stiff peaks.
5. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces and dissolve in three tablespoons hot water.
6. Mix the rice grains with the orange cubes and cream, and lastly add the gelatin.
7. Pour the mass into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted form and let it stand on ice about 3 hours, or until it has set.
8. Loosen the pudding around the edge with a pointed knife, turn out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper, garnish with orange segments and serve with orange sauce.
