## Ingredients

- 1 liter cooked potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. The potatoes are peeled, cut into 3 mm. thick slices or even cubes. Half of the butter is browned in a frying pan, the potatoes are added and sprinkled with a mixture of the crushed rusks, salt and sugar. The rest of the butter is laid on top and the potatoes are browned while the pan is shaken frequently. Lastly the parsley is sprinkled over and the potatoes are served immediately with roasted fish and meat.
