## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper
- 8 mushrooms
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tablespoon flour (5 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons mushroom stock
- 3 tablespoons cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the mushrooms and sauté them in the butter until it becomes clear.
2. Sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the mushroom stock and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Place the mushroom in the stew, let it come to a boil and taste for seasoning.
4. Prepare the omelet as in the previous recipe; make a slit in the center of the omelet and fill it with the mushroom stew.
5. If desired, garnish the omelet with 4 fluted mushrooms that have been fried in butter.
