## Ingredients

- ½ liter cooked or roasted chopped meat
- 2 small red onions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1½ tablespoon salt
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 1 cup rice grits
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅓ liter broth
- ½ tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it together with the meat in the butter, then add the seasonings.
2. Cook the rice grits
