## Ingredients

- 1½ kg lamb breast
- water
- 1 tbsp salt
- 2 tbsp butter
- ½ Portuguese onion
- 2-4 carrots
- ½-1 turnip
- 1½ cups Carolina rice
- 2 liters water
- 1 tbsp salt

## Directions

1. Wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into nice pieces.
2. Place to boil in warm water, skim, salt, and cook for 1 hour, then remove and strain the broth.
3. Peel and coarsely chop the onion and brown in half the butter, add the meat and brown likewise.
4. Add one liter of the strained broth and let the meat cook slowly until tender.
5. Clean the vegetables, cut into fine strips and brown in the other half of the butter, then add broth and let the vegetables cook—preferably each separately—until soft.
6. Rinse the rice and place in 2 liters boiling salted water and cook until soft, stirring occasionally with a fork.
7. Strain the rice and place in the warming oven to dry.
8. Place the meat in the middle of the serving platter, and the rice and vegetables in groups around it. Garnish the dish with parsley.
9. Strain the sauce, skim, and thicken if desired.
