## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg fatty whitefish
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 3 parsley stems
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1/2 egg
- 80 g butter
- 1 1/2 deciliters French wine
- 1 1/2 deciliters water
- 1 deciliter broth
- pepper and dill sprigs (if desired)

## Directions

1. If desired, add pepper and some dill sprigs.
2. Arrange the salmon pieces with the meat sides toward each other in such a way that the thick side of the fish is placed against the thin side.
3. Place it thereafter in light press between two cutting boards overnight and the fish is thereafter ready.
4. Cut into slices 1/2 cm thick, arrange them beautifully on a platter, garnish with fish skin halved on the stove or fried, and fresh dill sprigs.
5. Serve with cooking oil, vinegar, powdered sugar, French mustard, salt and pepper.
6. Cured fish can be cut into thin slices and seared in an almost dry frying pan. Serve as above.
