## Ingredients

- 300 gr. sweet almond
- 300 gr. butter
- 300 gr. granulated sugar
- 300 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Blanch the almond, grate or run through an almond mill and pound together with the sugar.
2. Wash the butter and stir until white, and work in the almond mass and flour.
3. Divide the dough into three parts, roll these out to round bottoms, which are baked on well-buttered baking sheets at fairly good oven heat.
4. Let the cakes cool and layer them with jam or cream between each bottom.
5. Glaze the tart with water icing, garnish with fruit and crystallized fruits or with whipped cream.
