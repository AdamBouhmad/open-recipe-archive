## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried green peas
- 3 liters water
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 leek
- 1 small celery
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 part cream
- salt
- (sugar)
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the dried peas and soak them in cold water the evening before.
2. Place them in new cold water to cook until completely soft. If the water is hard, add a small pinch of soda about the size of a pea during cooking.
3. Peel and prepare the vegetables in the usual way, cut into strips, and cook separately in salted water.
4. When the peas are cooked, strain them.
5. Brown the butter and flour together, add the puree, and let the soup simmer 10 minutes.
6. Add the cooked vegetables together with their cooking liquid and taste and adjust seasoning.
7. Whip the cream in the soup bowl and pour in the soup.
