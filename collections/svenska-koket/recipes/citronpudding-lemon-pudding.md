## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- 75-100 grams granulated sugar
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- 4 egg yolks
- 7 leaves gelatin

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream until stiff peaks form.
2. Stir in the sugar, grated lemon zest, lemon juice and egg yolks one by one.
3. Last, add the gelatin dissolved in a little warm water.
4. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set it out to set.
5. When ready to serve, dip the mold in warm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with sponge fingers.
