## Ingredients

- 3-4 stalks rhubarb or 1/2 litre preserved rhubarb
- 2 litres water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 g)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse it, dry it and cut it into 2 cm long pieces
2. Bring the water to a boil, place the rhubarb in it with the lemon zest and let it cook until it falls apart
3. Strain the soup, pour it back into the pan and add the crushed sugar
4. Let it boil again, thicken it with potato flour mixed in 1/2 tablespoon water, and let it cook 2 minutes
5. Beat the egg yolks and powdered sugar in the soup bowl until frothy, and pour the soup in with steady whisking
6. Serve with potato flour cakes or rusks
7. In simpler cases the egg yolks are omitted, but then more potato flour is used
