## Ingredients

- 165 gr. flour
- 2 tsp. baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 160 gr. sugar
- 45 gr. melted butter
- 1 1/2 dcl. cream or milk
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 20 sweet and 5 bitter almonds
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with the baking powder.
2. Stir the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the finely grated lemon zest, butter, cream and finally the flour.
3. Stir the batter well, pour into a well buttered and rusk-strewn ring mold or frying pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 1/2 hour.
