## Ingredients

- 7 hg. potato purée
- 40–50 gr. truffle
- 1 egg for brushing and breading
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 1 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Prepare the potato purée as in recipe no. 680 and mix with the truffle, cut into fine dice.
2. Treat the purée as in recipe no. 680 and roll into the shape of apricots.
3. Dip these in beaten egg, roll in the almond, blanched and cut into the finest slivers.
4. Cook until beautifully golden-brown in hot lard, let drain on blotting paper and serve very hot as a garnish around fried meat.
