## Ingredients

- 4 large white cucumbers
- 6 tsp. salt (30 gr.)
- 2 liters boiling water
- 2 1/2 liters spirit vinegar
- 6 gr. whole white pepper
- pepperroot in small pieces (1 dcl.)
- 4-5 dill crowns
- 1 kg. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the cucumbers as in the preceding recipe, place them in a dish, sprinkle with salt, and let them lie for 24 hours.
2. Take them up and lay them on double linen to drain.
3. Place the cucumbers again in a dish and pour the boiling water over them.
4. After 5 minutes, take them out, wrap them well in dry linen, place them in a crock, and boil 1 1/2 liters of the vinegar and pour it over them.
5. Cover the crock with a doubled cloth and plate and let it stand in a cool place for 3 days.
6. Take out the cucumbers, wrap them in a cloth (discard the vinegar), and lay them in alternating layers with the white pepper, pepperroot, and dill crowns in a jar.
7. Boil the rest of the vinegar with the sugar and pour it hot over the cucumbers, which are covered with double linen and allowed to cool.
8. Bind the jar well. These cucumbers are strong but excellent.
