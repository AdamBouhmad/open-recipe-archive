## Ingredients

- 1 small cauliflower head
- 1 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 6 eggs
- 4 decilitres boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams)

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water for about 1 hour.
2. Place it in boiling, salted water and cook until tender, then remove it, drain it on a sieve and divide into small pieces.
3. Whisk the eggs well with the seasonings, and add the liquid, which should be boiling, while whisking vigorously.
4. Butter a saucepan, pour in the egg mixture and cook in a water bath while stirring.
5. When it is half set, carefully fold in the cauliflower and let the scrambled eggs finish cooking.
6. Serve on a warm platter, garnish with parsley and serve as a main course or with fried meat, fried fish, boiled ham or sausage.
