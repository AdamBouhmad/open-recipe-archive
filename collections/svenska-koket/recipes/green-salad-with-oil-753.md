## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized salad heads
- 150 gr. fresh cucumber, in slices
- 2 tomatoes, in slices
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- a little paprika or white pepper
- 1 1/2 msk. vinegar
- 3-4 msk. fine oil

## Directions

1. Treat the salad heads as in the previous recipe.
2. Peel the cucumber, cut it into slices, rinse the tomatoes, dry them, and cut them likewise into slices.
3. Prepare the salad just before it is served—preferably at the table.
4. In the salad bowl, mix the spices, vinegar, and oil, add the salad, cucumber and tomato slices, and mix gently with the sauce.
5. Serve with roasted meat. In the same way, salad of endive, chicory, cress, young dandelion leaves, and others may be prepared.
