## Ingredients

- 1 liter boiled potatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour (15 gr.)
- 3–4 deciliters good milk
- salt
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes with skins on, let them cool, peel them and cut into thin slices.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
3. Add the milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
4. Add the potato slices and let the stew simmer slowly for about 5 minutes.
5. Season with salt and the finely chopped parsley.
6. Serve with salted salmon, fried fish and meat, smoked sausage, etc.
