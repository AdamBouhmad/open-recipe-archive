## Ingredients

- 1 kg oranges
- 3/4 to 1 kg granulated sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Wipe the oranges with a damp cloth and peel them.
2. Remove all the white from the peel.
3. Cut them into fine strips and cook with a little water for 10 minutes.
4. Strain off the water, pour on new cold water, and allow the peels to cook for a further 10 minutes in this.
5. Strain off the water, and for the third time pour on new water, so much that it stands level over the peels.
6. Cook the peels in this until they become soft.
7. Cut the oranges into wedges, remove all pits and membranes, and place the fruit flesh with the peels along with the sugar.
8. Allow the marmalade to cook until it is clear and fairly thick.
9. During cooking stir the marmalade from time to time and skim carefully.
10. When most of the heat has dissipated, transfer it to warmed glass jars.
