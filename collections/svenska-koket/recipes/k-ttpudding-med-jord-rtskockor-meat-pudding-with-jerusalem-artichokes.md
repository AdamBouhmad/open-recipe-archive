## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipe no. 632
- 1.5 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 0.75 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 4 deciliters cooking liquid
- Salt
- Sugar
- White pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the ground meat as in recipe no. 632.
2. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes clean, scrape them, rinse them and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour to keep them white.
3. Place them in boiling water with salt added and let them cook until soft, then drain them in a colander and reserve the cooking liquid.
4. Prepare the stewing and pudding and bake them as in the preceding recipe.
