## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters broth (made from roasting bones)
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons wine, sherry, port wine, or Madeira (optional)
- 1 hectogram pasta
- 1/2 liter broth

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour
2. Add the broth (which should have a root vegetable flavor) and let the soup cook about 10 minutes
3. Season with salt, white pepper, and, if desired, a little wine
4. Serve the soup with pasta cooked in broth
