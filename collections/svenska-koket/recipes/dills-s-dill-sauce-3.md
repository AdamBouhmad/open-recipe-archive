## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter meat stock
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1.5 tablespoons vinegar
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour together, add the meat stock, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
2. Add the chopped dill and taste and adjust the sauce with sugar and vinegar and salt if needed.
3. Finally finish it with an egg yolk, after which the sauce must not boil.
