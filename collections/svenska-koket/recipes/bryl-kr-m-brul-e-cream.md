## Ingredients

- 125 grams sugar
- 1.5 deciliters boiling water
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 4 deciliters thick cream
- 1/2 teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Melt the sugar in a slightly warm frying pan buttered with 1/2 teaspoon butter; when it has a light brown color, add 1 deciliter of the water, and pour the sugar syrup into a saucepan.
2. Add the vanilla and let it boil, stirring over gentle heat for about 5 minutes, after which it is removed.
3. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in the rest of the water and pour it into the sugar syrup, which is then allowed to cool.
4. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and fold it gently into the sugar syrup.
5. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it set on ice.
6. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a cake dish with cake paper.
7. Garnish with biscuits.
