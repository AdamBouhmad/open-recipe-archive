## Ingredients

- 4–5 hg. boneless, cooked or roasted meat
- 1 small red onion
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 2 dcl. roasting sauce or broth
- 4 eggs
- 1 tsk. wheat flour
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. sugar
- 4 dcl. milk
- 1 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Chop the meat fairly finely.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in the butter, add the meat and brown it well, dust with spices and mix with the sauce or broth.
3. For the egg custard, whip the egg yolks with the flour, a little salt and sugar and the milk, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Place the meat in a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie mold and pour the egg custard over it.
