## Ingredients

- 4 liters fresh mushrooms: button mushrooms, chanterelles, boletus or 1 can button mushrooms (1/2 liter)
- 1 hg butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 dcl mushroom broth
- 3 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, rinse them and blanch them until soft. Pour them into a sieve and let them drain well, then cut them into cubes and fry them in 30 gr of the butter until it is clear. Pour out the mushrooms and prepare the soufflé as in recipe no. 59, serve it immediately after removing it from the oven as a middle course with hollandaise or melted butter.
