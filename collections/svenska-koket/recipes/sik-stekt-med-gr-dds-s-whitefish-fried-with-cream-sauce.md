## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg fat whitefish
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 1 hg butter
- 4 dcl thick cream
- 3 tablespoons coarsely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, rinse well and dry it.
2. Cut it open along the backbone and remove this along with all small bones.
3. (If desired, the skin can be removed, but many consider it a delicacy.)
4. Rub the fillets with salt and let them rest 1 hour.
5. Dry them again and cut into 3 1/2 cm wide pieces across the grain, dip these in the beaten egg and turn in the wheat flour.
6. Brown the fish quickly in half of the butter in a frying pan and layer it with the parsley in a buttered baking dish.
7. Place the rest of the butter in flakes on top and add the cream.
8. Cover the dish and place it in the oven, and let the fish fry for about 20 minutes.
9. Serve from the dish with mashed potatoes. — Instead of parsley, chopped capers can be used.
