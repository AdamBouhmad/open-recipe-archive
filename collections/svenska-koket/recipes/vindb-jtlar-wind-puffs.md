## Ingredients

- 3.5 parts water
- 105 grams wheat flour
- 105 grams butter
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the water, butter, and sugar together, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook until it leaves the pan.
2. When most of the heat has gone, stir in the egg yolks one at a time; last fold in the beaten egg whites.
3. Press the mass through a pastry bag or corrugated piping tube onto a well-cleaned and buttered sheet in the shape of arches or other figures.
4. Bake them in a warm oven.
