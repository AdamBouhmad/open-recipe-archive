## Ingredients

- Juice and peel of 2 oranges
- 150 gr. crushed sugar
- 1 egg, 3 egg yolks
- 1 dcl. milk
- 1 tablespoon sherry
- 2 gr. citric acid
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 5 leaves gelatin, 3 tablespoons water
- For garnish: 1 orange, 50 gr. lady fingers

## Directions

1. Wash and dry the oranges and rub the peel against the sugar in a clean saucepan.
2. Press out and strain the orange juice over the sugar.
3. Add the egg, egg yolks, and milk, and whisk until the sugar is dissolved.
4. Simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until the cream is thick and fluffy.
5. Remove from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
6. Add the sherry and the pounded citric acid.
7. Rinse the gelatin, cut into pieces, and dissolve in 3 tablespoons warm water.
8. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
9. Add the gelatin to the cream while whisking, then gently fold in the whipped cream.
10. Rinse a clean tin mold with cold water and sprinkle with sugar.
11. Pour in the mixture and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours.
12. When serving, loosen from the edge with a pointed knife, unmold onto a flat glass dish with cake paper, and garnish with candied orange slices and lady fingers.
