## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons white sago grains
- 2 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 2 parts cream
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Rinse the grains in cold water, place in the boiling broth and let them cook until they are clear.
2. Season the soup.
3. Whisk the egg yolk with the cream in a soup bowl and pour the soup over while whisking vigorously.
