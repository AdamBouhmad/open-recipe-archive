## Ingredients

- 3 liters cherries
- 6-8 hectograms loaf sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Clean the berries and remove the stems.
2. Rinse the fruit carefully and allow it to drain very well.
3. Cook it then as regular compote for 20 minutes.
