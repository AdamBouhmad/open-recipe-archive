## Ingredients

- 1-2 turnips, water, salt
- 1 liter potatoes
- 0.5-1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter or cream
- salt, sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips and cut them into small pieces and put them on to cook in salted water for about 30 minutes.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut them into slices and add them to cook with the turnips until everything is soft.
3. Drain the water, mash the potatoes and turnips with a fork.
4. Stir in the cold butter together with the milk.
5. Work the mash well and season with the spices.
