## Ingredients

- 2-3 large celeriac roots
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/2 deciliters finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the celeriac, brush them, peel them, place them in cold water with salt and let them cook until soft, which is tested with a whisk. Lay them on a cloth, let them cool and cut them afterwards into 1/2 cm thick slices. These are dipped in beaten egg, turned in crushed rusks and fried a beautiful golden brown in the butter. Serve as a lunch dish or as a garnish around fried meat. If desired, the celeriac can be cooked unpeeled, then they get a stronger celeriac flavor.
