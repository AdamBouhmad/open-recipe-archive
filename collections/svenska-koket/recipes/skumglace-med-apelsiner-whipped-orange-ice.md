## Ingredients

- Zest of 2 oranges
- Juice of 4 oranges
- 7 parts heavy cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 115 gr. sugar
- 1.25 parts water

## Directions

1. The oranges are rinsed, dried well, and zested.
2. All the white pith is removed, and the zest is cut into thin strips.
3. Half of the cream is brought to a boil, the orange zest is added, and the cream is covered and allowed to cool.
4. The egg yolks are whisked in a saucepan with 1 tablespoon of the sugar; the cooked and strained cream is added gradually, and the mixture is allowed to simmer over heat to a thick cream.
5. Meanwhile, the orange juice is cooked with the remaining sugar and water to a syrupy consistency, which is mixed with the cream.
6. The cream whisked to stiff peaks is folded in, and the ice is frozen.
