## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1 lit. lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 1-1½ kg. butter or margarine
- 1 kkp granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 kkp for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and mix in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double size.
5. Melt the butter and work it into the dough together with the sugar, and let the dough rise again.
6. Shape the dough into long, narrow breads or round cakes, which are later scored.
7. Place the breads on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets to rise.
8. When ready to bake, brush them with cold water and place in a hot oven.
