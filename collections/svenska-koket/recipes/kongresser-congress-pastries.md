## Ingredients

- 200 grams wheat flour
- 200 grams butter
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 3 eggs
- 300 grams granulated sugar
- 250 grams sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as simple butter dough (no. 549)
2. Roll out quite thin and use it to line small pastry forms
3. Blanch the almonds and grate or pass through an almond mill
4. Mix the eggs and sugar well together, blend in the almonds, and fill the forms halfway with this mixture
5. From the remaining butter dough cut out approximately 1 cm wide strips and place these crosswise over the forms
6. Bake the pastries in good oven heat
