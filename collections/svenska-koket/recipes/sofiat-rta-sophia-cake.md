## Ingredients

- 7 eggs
- 300 grams granulated sugar
- 50 grams sweet almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- 300 grams semolina
- 150 grams semolina
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar for half an hour.
2. During beating, add 1 tablespoon water twice.
3. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the oven, and grind or pass them through an almond mill, then mix with the semolina.
4. Stir the dry mixture into the egg batter, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan or mold and bake for 1 hour in moderate oven heat.
6. This cake is particularly good with coffee and can, if desired, be glazed with chocolate glaze or garnished as a regular cake.
