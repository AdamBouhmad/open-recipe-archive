## Ingredients

- Heart and lungs of veal, sheep, or pork
- Water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 gr.)
- 1 red onion
- 20 white peppercorns
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3/4 liter of broth
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut open the heart and lungs, rinse them and let them soak in water for 12-15 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Place them in enough cold water to just cover the meat, skim well and add the seasonings; let boil until tender, then remove the windpipe and air tubes and mince the meat very finely.
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth which should be well skimmed, add the meat, add the vinegar and let the purée simmer for 10 minutes, then taste carefully and serve with boiled potatoes and vinegar.
4. If the purée is too thick, thin with a little more broth; if too thin, thicken with a little potato flour mixed in cold water, and then let boil for 2 minutes.
