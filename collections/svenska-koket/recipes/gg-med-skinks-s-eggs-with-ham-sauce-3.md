## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 1–1/2 deciliter cream
- egg yolks from the hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tablespoon clarified butter (20 gr.)
- 1/2 hectogram lean, smoked ham
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 deciliter broth
- 3 deciliters milk
- 1 egg yolk
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Peel the eggs and cut them in half lengthwise; carefully remove the yolks and cut them into fine dice.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the thin cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then carefully fold in the diced egg yolks and season the stewing with salt and white pepper.
3. Fill the egg halves with the stewing and pour the clarified butter over.
4. Cut the ham into fine dice and fry in butter. Stir in the flour, add the broth and milk a little at a time. Let the sauce simmer for 8 minutes, stir in the egg yolk and let it simmer and season to taste.
5. Place the eggs in a frying pan and put in the oven to heat through, then arrange on a hot serving dish and pour the ham sauce over. Garnish with parsley and serve to the smörgåsbord.
