## Ingredients

- 15 liters raspberries
- 3 liters warm water
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 0.25 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. The berries are crushed and placed in a crock.
2. The warm water and vinegar are poured over and the berries are allowed to ferment for about 8 days, or until they float to the surface. Once a day the berries should be stirred.
3. They are then poured into a strainer cloth to drip. The juice is put on with sugar and cooked until it is clear and slightly sticky. It is bottled warm or cold, the bottles are corked and sealed with resin.
