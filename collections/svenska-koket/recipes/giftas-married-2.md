## Ingredients

- 1 deciliter extra thick cream
- 3 deciliters jam
- 3 deciliters pieces of small bread or 18 small sugar rusks

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, mix in the jam and the small bread or rusks (soaked in 1.5 parts milk), and put the cream in a compote bowl.
2. The small bread can be omitted, in which case the cream is piped into cones instead.
