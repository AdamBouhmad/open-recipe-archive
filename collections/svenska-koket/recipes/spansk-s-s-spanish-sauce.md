## Ingredients

- 1 liter strong, dark stock
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 bunch parsley
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- meat stock
- salt
- (sugar)
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Put the stock on with the onion, parsley and carrot and let it cook until it has a good, strong flavor.
2. Strain it and skim carefully.
3. Fry together the butter and flour, add the stock, and let the sauce come to a good boil.
4. Move it then to low heat to simmer for 1.5 hours.
5. Now add the meat stock according to the roast to which the sauce is to be served, and let the sauce come to another boil, then taste and serve.
