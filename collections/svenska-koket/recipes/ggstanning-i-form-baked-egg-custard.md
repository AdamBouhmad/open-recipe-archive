## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs well with the milk and add the seasonings.
2. Butter a metal form or soufflé dish, pour in the egg mixture, cover with a lid and place the form in a water bath on top of the stove.
3. Take careful note that the water does not boil but only stays hot until the custard is properly set, otherwise it will easily become watery.
4. Turn out onto a hot plate, garnish with parsley and serve with brined ham or sausage, fried meat or fish.
5. The egg custard can also be baked in a water bath in the oven.
