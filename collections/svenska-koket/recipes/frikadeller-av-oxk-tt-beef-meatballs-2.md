## Ingredients

- 150 gr. boneless beef
- 20 gr. lard
- 1/2 tablespoon flour (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1—1 1/2 deciliters cream

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and ground together with the lard 4 times through a meat grinder
2. It is then pounded well together with the flour and spices in a mortar and the forcemeat is worked for 1/2 hour, while the cream is added, a little at a time
3. The forcemeat is tasted and formed with 2 teaspoons, dipped in warm water, into very small balls, which are placed in boiling broth or salted water (not too many at once) and allowed to boil 2—3 minutes
4. The meatballs are cleaned before they are placed in the soup bowl
