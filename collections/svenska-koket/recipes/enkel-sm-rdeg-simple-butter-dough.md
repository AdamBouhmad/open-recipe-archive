## Ingredients

- 250 gr butter
- 250 gr wheat flour
- 7-8 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, but do not work it so long that it becomes too soft.
2. Mix the flour with water so that the dough becomes the same consistency as the butter.
3. Roll out the dough to a square cake, somewhat thicker in the middle.
4. Clap out the butter to a round cake and place it in the middle of the dough.
5. Fold the dough over the butter so that it is completely covered.
6. Carefully roll out the butter dough to an oblong square, not too thin.
7. Fold this square together so that 1/4 is folded inward from the left and 1/4 is folded inward from the right.
8. The edges of these two folds meet in the middle of the square.
9. Then fold one half of the dough over the other lengthwise (across the previous folds).
10. Place the dough with the long side toward you and roll it out again to an oblong square.
11. Repeat this four times in succession.
12. The dough is then ready to bake out.
