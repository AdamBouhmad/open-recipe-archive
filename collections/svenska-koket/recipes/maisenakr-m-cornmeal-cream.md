## Ingredients

- 10 cups whole milk
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 3 heaping tablespoons cornmeal
- 4 to 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of the milk with the sugar and cornmeal.
2. Boil the remaining milk with the vanilla.
3. Stir in the mixture and let the cream cook for 5 minutes, then remove from heat and stir in the beaten egg yolks.
