## Ingredients

- Shell and roe of 2 lobsters
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 msk. vinegar
- 2 msk. fine olive oil
- 2 dcl. thick cream
- Salt, sugar, paprika

## Directions

1. Pound the shells, roe, fat from the lobster and butter in a mortar until a fine mass, pass through a hair sieve and wring through muslin.
