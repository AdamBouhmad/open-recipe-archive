## Ingredients

- 3 deciliter white beans
- 1/2 liter water
- 4 deciliter broth
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the beans and rinse them
2. Place in cold, soft water (see page 338) and let simmer slowly until completely tender, which takes about 3 hours
3. When the water has cooked down, dilute with broth
4. Melt the butter in a frying pan, add the onion and beans and fry well with the butter
5. Season with spices
6. Serve preferably with roasted mutton
