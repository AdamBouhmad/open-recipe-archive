## Ingredients

- 4 small salted herrings
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 60 gr. butter (for frying)

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, clean, rinse well and fillet. Remove the backbone and soak the herring in water for approximately 12 hours.
2. Rinse the fillets well and wrap in a fish towel. Remove all small bones carefully and coat the fillets in a mixture of wheat flour and crushed rusks.
3. Fry them until beautifully brown in the butter, place on a warm platter, garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish with freshly boiled unpeeled potatoes.
