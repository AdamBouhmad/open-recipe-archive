## Ingredients

- Wheat bread from the previous day
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into approximately 8 mm thick slices.
2. Place the slices on a rack and toast over gas or on a stove over strong heat until they are light brown on both sides.
3. Do not stack the slices on top of each other as they will become soggy; instead, stand them on edge in a toast rack (available commercially).
4. Serve the bread hot with butter for tea, coffee, small breakfast dishes, or soups.
