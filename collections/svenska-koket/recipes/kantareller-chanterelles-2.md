## Ingredients

- Kantareller (chanterelle mushrooms)
- water
- salt
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut off the stem end slightly, but keep the gills attached
2. Rinse the mushroom well and cut into pieces
3. Blanch in boiling salted water for about 5 minutes (discard the liquid)
4. Drain on cloth
5. Cook as stewed champinjoner, or simply sauté in butter and season with salt and pepper
6. Serve with open-faced sandwiches
