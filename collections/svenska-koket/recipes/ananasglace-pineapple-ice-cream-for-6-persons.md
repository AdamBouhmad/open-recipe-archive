## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple
- 150 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- citric acid
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Cut three-quarters of the pineapple into pieces and pass them together with the sugar.
2. Add the water, and taste the mixture with citric acid and strain.
3. Finally add the egg white.
4. Pour the mixture into an ice cream freezer or ice cream machine and freeze.
5. When the ice cream is half frozen, fold in the rest of the pineapple, cut into small cubes, and finish freezing.
6. Pineapple ice cream is usually served in glasses together with another colored ice cream, such as pistachio or raspberry ice cream.
