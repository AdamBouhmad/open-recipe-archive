## Ingredients

- 105 gr. butter
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 160 gr. wheat flour
- 1 pinch potassium bicarbonate salt
- 75 gr. sweet almond
- 3 eggs
- 150 gr. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortcrust pastry in the usual manner.
2. Blanch the almonds and chop fairly coarsely.
3. Beat the eggs and sugar very well together, and add the almonds.
4. Line small pastry molds with shortcrust pastry and fill with the almond mixture.
5. Place the molds in the oven to bake until the filling is completely set.
6. Turn out immediately.
