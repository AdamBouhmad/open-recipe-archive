## Ingredients

- 600 g boneless beef
- 1 egg
- 3 cups milk
- 1/2 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 100 g butter
- 1 Portuguese onion

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with the lard into cubes and pass 3 times through a meat grinder.
2. Whisk the eggs and milk, add the crushed breadcrumbs and let them swell, then mix them into the forcemeat together with the spices.
3. Work the forcemeat until it becomes cohesive like a dough.
4. Form small thin patties from it.
5. Peel the onion and cut it into thin slices.
6. Brown the butter in a frying pan and fry the patties beautifully brown in it and lay them on a hot platter.
7. Whisk the pan with a little boiling water and pour the sauce over the patties.
8. Brown the onion last in butter and garnish the patties with it.
