## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 80 grams butter
- 115 grams flour
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Heat the milk. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, gradually add the milk while stirring, and let the porridge cook for 15 minutes. Season with salt and serve with milk.
