## Ingredients

- 2 large salt fat herrings
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- ½ tsp. sugar
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the mould

## Directions

1. Remove the innards from the herrings, skin them and soak in water the day before use.
2. Wrap in a fish towel or white paper and carefully remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Cut each fillet into 6 pieces.
4. Butter a fireproof, low-sided mould with butter, lay the herring pieces in it and pour the egg custard over.
5. Finally sprinkle with parsley, place the mould in a warm oven and let it stand until the egg custard has taken on a beautiful, light brown colour.
6. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
