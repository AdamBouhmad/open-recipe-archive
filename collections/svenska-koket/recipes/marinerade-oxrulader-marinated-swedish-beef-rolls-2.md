## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless inner round
- 1 hg. pork fatback
- 1 msk. finely chopped onion
- 1 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 2 tsk. anchovy sauce
- Marinade: 2 msk. vinegar
- 2 msk. cooking oil
- 1 msk. white wine or Madeira
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 tsk. anchovy sauce
- For braising: 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 6 dcl. broth or water
- 2 msk. red or white wine

## Directions

1. The meat is treated as in the previous recipe, cut into 12 slices, which are well tenderized, placed on a platter, covered with the marinade and left to lie 1 hour, during which they are turned once.
2. For the marinade all ingredients are well mixed and supplemented, if desired, with 1/2 msk. finely chopped onion.
3. The fatback is cut into 12 strips, which are rolled in a mixture of the onion and parsley.
4. The rolls are well browned in the butter, sprinkled with flour, the boiling broth or water mixed with wine is poured on, and they are left to slowly braise with a tight lid, 2 1/2-3 hours.
5. The pot is shaken often so the rolls do not burn.
6. They are placed on a warm carrot, most of the fat is skimmed from the sauce, which is thinned if needed, and poured over the meat.
7. Served with boiled potatoes.
