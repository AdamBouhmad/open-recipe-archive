## Ingredients

- 2 large salted herrings
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons grated rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 parts thick cream
- 1 large Portuguese onion for garnish
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g) for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the innards from the herring and let it soak in plenty of water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Remove the skin.
