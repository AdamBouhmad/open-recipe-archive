## Ingredients

- Zest and juice of 1 small lemon
- 2 dcl crushed sugar (190 gr)
- 1 dcl French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl water
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it, rub the zest against the sugar, then place the sugar in a well-scoured pot.
2. Press out the lemon juice and mix it with the sugar.
3. Add the wine, water and egg yolks and whisk the mixture until the sugar has melted.
4. Move the pot over the heat and let the cream simmer under vigorous whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
5. Pour the cream out and whisk it until it is cold.
6. If desired, fold in the cream which has been whipped to stiff peaks.
7. Serve in a compote bowl with pastries.
8. Half the recipe is enough to fill a large cake. The cream should then be omitted.
