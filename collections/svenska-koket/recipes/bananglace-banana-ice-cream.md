## Ingredients

- 1 liter mashed fruit pulp of bananas (approximately 1 1/2 kg. unpeeled fruit)
- 6 dcl sugar syrup
- 1/4 dcl maraschino liqueur
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Pass through a fine sieve
