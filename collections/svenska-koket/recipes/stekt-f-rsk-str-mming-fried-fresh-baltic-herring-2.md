## Ingredients

- 1/2 pail strömming (Baltic herring)
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 cup coarse rye flour
- 3 tablespoons butter or lard

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the strömming well and remove the bones, if desired, and cut
2. Rub them with salt and let them sit for a while; then coat them in the coarse rye flour and fry in butter or lard
3. Fresh herring is fried in the same way
