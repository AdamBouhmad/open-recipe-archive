## Ingredients

- 2 hg calf sweetbreads
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tsk salt
- 2 msk butter (40 gr)
- 4 msk flour (40 gr)
- 1 dcl sweetbread stock
- 4 dcl cream
- 2-3 truffles
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 12-14 crisps

## Directions

1. The calf sweetbreads should soak in water for 1 hour so that the blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch them (place in cold, lightly salted water and let them cook in it for 10 minutes) and then place in 1/2 litre of boiling water with salt, to cook about 1/2 hour or until they become white and firm.
3. Lay them on a sieve so the stock drains well. Cut the sweetbreads into fine cubes and sauté in the butter until it becomes clear.
4. Squeeze it out of the butter with a spoon. Then sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the sweetbread stock and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add the calf sweetbreads and the finely cut truffle, and let the stew come to a boil and taste it.
6. Lastly stir in the whisked egg yolk, let the stew simmer and fill the crisps immediately.
7. Serve as a side dish.
