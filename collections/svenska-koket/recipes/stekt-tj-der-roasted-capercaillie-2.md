## Ingredients

- 1 capercaillie
- 1 hectogram lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ½-1 liter broth or thin cream
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- meat dripping
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the capercaillie (the hen is finest), singe, clean out, rinse well and dry. If it has been frozen, soak it in milk overnight. Lard it with lard cut into fine strips and truss.
2. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the capercaillie in it with the breast up, brown it well and salt it. Roast it on low heat and baste occasionally with broth or thin cream. Roasting takes 2-3 hours.
3. Strain and skim the meat dripping, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules.
