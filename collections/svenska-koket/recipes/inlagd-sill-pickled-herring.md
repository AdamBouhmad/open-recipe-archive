## Ingredients

- 2 salted herrings
- 3-4 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp chopped onion

## Directions

1. Soak the herring the day before, clean and remove skin and bones
2. Cut it into strips on the bias and place in a salad bowl
3. Mix vinegar, pepper and sugar and pour over the herring
4. Garnish with the peeled and chopped onion
