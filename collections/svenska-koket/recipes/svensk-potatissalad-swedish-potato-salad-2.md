## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter small potatoes
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 3-4 dcl thin cream
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar
- a little Cayenne
- 1-2 tbsp capers
- 1/2 tbsp finely chopped chervil or cress

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them clean and cook them in salted water with the skins on; peel and let them cool, then cut them
