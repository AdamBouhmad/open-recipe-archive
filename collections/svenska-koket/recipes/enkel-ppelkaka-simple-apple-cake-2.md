## Ingredients

- 1 liter loose, good apples
- 50 gr. sugar
- 50 gr. butter
- 50 gr. flour
- 4 dcl. boiling milk
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. finely grated lemon zest or 10 sweet almonds
- 3 eggs
- 1 tbsp. melted butter (20 gr.) for the pan
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, halve them, remove the cores and cut them into thin slices, which are layered alternately with the sugar in a bowl and allowed to stand for approximately 1 hour.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk, and let the batter cook for 5 minutes, after which the sugar and lemon zest or almond are added, and the batter is allowed to cool.
3. Then add the eggs, one at a time, and stir the batter for 10 minutes, after which the apple slices are added and the mass is stirred carefully.
4. Grease a sheet pan or frying pan with the butter mixed with flour, and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
5. Pour in the mass, and bake the cake in good oven heat for approximately 3/4 hour, or until it has risen and the apples feel soft, which is tested with a whisk.
6. Turn the cake out immediately, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm.
