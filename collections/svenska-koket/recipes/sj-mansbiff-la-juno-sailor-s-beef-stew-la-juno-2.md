## Ingredients

- 750 g ox chuck or trimmed beef
- 1 calf kidney
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 2-3 red onions
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 hg cooked ham
- 1 liter potatoes
- 4 dcl beef broth
- 3 dcl pilsener beer

## Directions

1. Treat the meat as for beef (see recipe no. 483).
2. Cut the calf kidney into slices and sprinkle these along with the patties with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Cut the ham into slices, which are layered with the remaining meat and potatoes.
4. Prepare the beef as in the previous recipe. Only pilsener can be used but the flavor will be milder if it is mixed with broth.
