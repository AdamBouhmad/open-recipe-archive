## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Carolina rice groats
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 3 parts thick cream
- 1/4 kg butter
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and put them on in boiling water and let them cook until they are soft.
2. Then add the cream whipped to stiff peaks as well as the butter and taste the porridge with sugar and salt.
