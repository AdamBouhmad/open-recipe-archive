## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked or fried meat
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter broth or meat gravy
- 1 small salt cucumber
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into appropriately large pieces. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry in butter. Fry the flour in it, and add the broth or meat gravy together with the salt cucumber sliced thin.
