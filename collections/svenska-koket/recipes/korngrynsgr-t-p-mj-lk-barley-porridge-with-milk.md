## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 2 1/2 liters milk
- 2 1/2 deciliters fine crushed barley (2 hectograms)
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot and pour in the milk.
2. Bring to a boil, then add the rinsed barley under vigorous whisking so that it does not become lumpy.
3. Let the porridge simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until the barley is completely soft, or 3-4 hours, stirring often so that it does not burn on the bottom.
4. Thin with boiling milk if needed.
5. Season with salt and serve with milk.
