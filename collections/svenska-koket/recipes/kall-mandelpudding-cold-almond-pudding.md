## Ingredients

- 40 gr. sweet almonds
- 3 bitter almonds
- 4 egg yolks
- 50 gr. sugar
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water
- 3 dcl. extra thick cream

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, dry them and pass them through an almond mill.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes, after which add the almonds.
3. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the hot water.
4. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks.
5. Gradually stir the gelatin into the egg mixture and then fold in the cream very carefully.
6. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with tart paper.
8. Garnish with fruit or jelly.
