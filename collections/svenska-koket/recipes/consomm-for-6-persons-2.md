## Ingredients

- 1 kg marrow bone
- 0.5 kg veal brisket
- 1 chicken
- 4 liters broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken in the usual way and brown it over a strong flame, but do not cook it through.
2. Wash the beef and veal meat with a cloth dipped in cold water, and cut it into small pieces.
3. Place it together with the chicken and salt in the cold broth, let it slowly come to a boil and skim it, after which add the cleaned vegetables.
4. Let the broth then slowly simmer for 4-5 hours and clarify it afterwards in the usual way.
5. Serve consommé with small pastries, vol-au-vents, macaroni or vermicelli.
