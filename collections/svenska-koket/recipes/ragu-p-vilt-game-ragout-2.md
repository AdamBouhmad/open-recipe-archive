## Ingredients

- 3-4 hg. cooked, boneless meat from poultry or game
- 8 dcl. broth or water
- 2 cloves (salt)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 g)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 g)
- 5 dl of the broth
- 1/2 tbsp. lingonberry jelly
- 1 tsp. red wine

## Directions

1. Game ragout is usually used as a filling in vol-au-vents, pâtés, rissoles and croquettes, etc.
2. Remove the meat from the bones and cut it into small cubes or elongated pieces; crush the bones and cook them in the broth (or water with added ingredients).
