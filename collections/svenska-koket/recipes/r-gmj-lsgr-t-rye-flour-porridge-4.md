## Ingredients

- 1.3 liters water
- 4 dcl. ground rye flour (2 hg.)
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with the salt, whisk the flour vigorously during full boil, and let the porridge thereafter simmer gently with a lid for about 1 hour.
2. Season with more salt if desired, and serve with milk.
