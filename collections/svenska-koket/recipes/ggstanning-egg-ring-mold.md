## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold
- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks
- 1 cup cream
- 1 can omelette asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 1/2 tbsp butter

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are combined and mixed with cream to make a loose batter.
2. When most of the heat has subsided, egg yolks are added one at a time with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Last, egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
4. The batter is poured into a buttered and bread-crumbed ring mold and baked in a water bath in an ordinary oven for about 20 minutes.
5. Turn out onto a round platter.
6. The hole in the middle is filled with braised asparagus.
7. The egg mold is garnished around with the heated asparagus spears and served with melted butter.
