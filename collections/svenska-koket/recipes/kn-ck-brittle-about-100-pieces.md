## Ingredients

- 2 hg. sweet almonds
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 3 dcl. syrup
- 3 hg. granulated sugar
- 1 1/4 dcl. finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and chop them finely.
2. In a pot, beat together the cream, syrup, and sugar and let it cook while stirring until the mass, when tested in cold water, shows a tendency to set (about 25 minutes).
3. Then add the finely crushed rusks and let the mass cook an additional 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the almonds and let the mass come to a rapid boil.
5. Pour it into small pleated paper molds.
