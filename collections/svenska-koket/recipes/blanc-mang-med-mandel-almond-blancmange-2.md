## Ingredients

- 95 gr. sweet almonds
- 3 bitter almonds
- 3 dcl. milk
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 125 gr. sugar
- Zest and juice of 1 orange or a little orange essence
- 6 leaves gelatin, 1 dcl. warm water

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, grind them through a mill and pound well with a little of the milk in a stone mortar.
