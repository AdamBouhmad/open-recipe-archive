## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast (gödkalvbringa)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 egg
- 1 deciliter crushed breadcrumbs (stötta skorpor)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the meat as in the previous recipe, bone it, brush it with the beaten egg, and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
2. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the veal breast in it with the outer side up, and brown it in a hot oven, basting it often with the butter in the pan.
3. Serve with browned butter, boiled potatoes and salads, or with cooked or braised vegetables, or with potato purée.
