## Ingredients

- 10 coffee cups unskinned milk
- 3 heaping tablespoons cornstarch flour
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 4-6 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one coffee cup of milk with the sugar and cornstarch flour.
2. Boil the remaining milk with the vanilla.
3. Stir in the mixture and let the cream cook for 5 minutes, then remove from heat and stir in the beaten egg yolks.
4. Remove the vanilla pod and pour the cream into a water-rinsed mold.
5. When completely cold, turn it out and serve with jam or fruit sauce.
