## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg whitefish
- 1 msk salt (15 gr.)
- 4 msk flour (40 gr.)
- 1 hg butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish as described on page 171 and score both sides with 1 1/2 cm between each score
2. Rub with salt and fry on the stove as in recipe no. 330
3. Serve with mashed potatoes or with braised peas mixed with one-third cooked, fresh diced carrots
