## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless inner thigh
- 100 gr. lard
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 2 teaspoons anchovy sauce
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 tablespoon white wine or Madeira
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 teaspoon anchovy sauce
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 6 dcl. broth or water
- 2 tablespoons red or white wine

## Directions

1. The meat is treated as in the previous recipe, cut into 12 slices, which are well beaten, placed on a platter, covered with the marinade and allowed to lie 1 hour, during which they are turned once.
2. All the marinade ingredients are well mixed together and combined, if desired, with 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped onion.
3. The lard is cut into 12 strips, which are rolled in a mixture of the onion and parsley and the anchovy sauce.
4. The meat slices are removed from the marinade, the lard strips are placed on each slice, and the rolls are tightly rolled up and bound or fastened as in recipe 480.
5. The rolls are well browned in butter and sprinkled with flour; the broth or water mixed with wine is poured on, and they are allowed to simmer slowly with a tight lid, 2 1/2–3 hours.
6. They are placed on a warm platter, the strings are cut away, the sauce is whisked well, thinned if necessary, tasted and poured over the rolls.
7. Served with boiled potatoes.
