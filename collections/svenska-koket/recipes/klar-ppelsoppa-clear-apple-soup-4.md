## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 litres sour apples
- 1 1/2 litres water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 1/2 decilitre French wine or sherry
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (80 g)
- 2 tablespoons red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the apples
2. Do not peel one litre of them; cut them into quarters, removing the blossom, stem and core
3. Place them in cold water and let them cook until soft but not falling apart or mushing
4. Pour them onto a cloth and let the liquid drip through
5. Peel the rest of the apples, cut them into 6 quarters and cook them with the lemon zest, sugar and wine in the apple liquid until soft
6. Thicken the soup with potato flour mixed in 1/2 tablespoon cold water and let it cook 2 minutes
7. Taste it, let it cool and add small pieces of currant jelly
8. Serve with rusks or biscuits
