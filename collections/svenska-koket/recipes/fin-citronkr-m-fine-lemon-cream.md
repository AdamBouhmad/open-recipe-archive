## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 160 grams sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 7 sheets gelatin
- 3 tablespoons hot water

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in the hot water.
3. Whip the egg whites to stiff foam.
4. Let the gelatin cool and stir it gradually into the egg mixture along with the egg white foam.
5. Pour the mixture into a compote bowl and let it stand on ice for 3-4 hours to set.
6. Serve with almond shavings.
