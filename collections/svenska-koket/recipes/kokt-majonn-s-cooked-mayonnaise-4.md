## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp English mustard
- 3 dcl water
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp fine salad oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 dcl thick cream
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the butter, flour, and mustard for 2 minutes.
2. Add the water gradually, little by little, and let the sauce boil while stirring for 10 minutes.
3. Pour it out and stir until it has cooled, then add the egg yolks, one at a time, and work the sauce with a spoon for about 1/2 hour while gradually adding the vinegar, oil, and lemon juice with steady stirring.
4. Fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks and season carefully with the spices.
5. This sauce is used instead of true mayonnaise for those who do not tolerate salad oil.
