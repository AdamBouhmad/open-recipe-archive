## Ingredients

- 2 hectograms loaf sugar
- zest of 2 lemons
- 6 parts water
- 4 parts French or Rhine wine
- 4 parts Madeira
- 40 grams gelatin
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Lemons are zested against the sugar.
2. This is placed on together with the water, lemon juice and Rhine wine, and when the whole becomes warm, the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the beaten egg whites are stirred in.
3. The mixture is given a brief boil with whisking, and the Madeira is added.
4. The jelly is strained through a napkin wrung out in hot water.
5. The clear jelly is then poured into a water-rinsed mold and set out to solidify.
6. The wine jelly is turned out and garnished with a little chopped jelly.
