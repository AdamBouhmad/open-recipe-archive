## Ingredients

- 1 pig's head (= 4 kg)
- 3 kg calf meat
- water
- 12 spice peppercorns
- 12 white peppercorns
- 7 tablespoons salt
- 3 teaspoons ground white pepper

## Directions

1. Split the pig's head, wash and scrape it and then soak it in water for one day.
2. Change the water a couple of times.
3. Place the head and calf meat in boiling water so that it covers the meat.
4. After skimming, add 3 tablespoons of salt and the peppercorns.
5. Let the meat simmer until it is tender and falls from the bones.
6. Wring out a towel in hot water and spread it out in a bowl.
7. Cut the rind into the largest possible pieces and spread it on the towel with skin side down.
8. Cut the meat and fat into slices while hot.
9. Next to the rind, place a layer of calf meat sprinkled with pepper and salt, and then a layer of fat, continuing this way with seasonings between each layer.
10. Place a fat rind on top and tie the towel tightly.
11. Dip the aspic in the boiling broth and then place it in a press for about 24 hours.
12. Store it in brine.
