## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped capers

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in half of the butter and place on a warm plate.
2. Brown the remaining butter lightly, season with vinegar and pour over the eggs.
3. Mix the parsley and capers and sprinkle over.
4. Serve to the smorgasbord.
