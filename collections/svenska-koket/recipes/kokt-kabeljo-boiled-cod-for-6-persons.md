## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. cod
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and soak out the salt.
2. Take it up and scrape the skin until it becomes white.
3. Place the fish on the fire in cold water and let it simmer very slowly until cooked.
4. Take it up immediately and serve with clarified butter and chopped eggs or with milk sauce.
