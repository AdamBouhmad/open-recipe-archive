## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. lamb breast
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 gr. saltpetre

## Directions

1. Crack the ribs and rub the breast with a mixture of salt, saltpetre and sugar.
2. Place it in a deep dish and let it stand 3—4 days, turning it frequently in the brine that forms.
3. Wash the meat thoroughly with a cloth dipped in cold water.
4. Place in cold water, bring quickly to the boil, skim well and cook for 15 minutes at 100°, after which cool the liquid to 82°-83° and let the meat rest in it without boiling until tender, about 1 1/2-2 hours.
5. Serve with browned turnips, stewed vegetables or potato purée. The broth can be used for soup or purée.
6. Alternatively, once cooked, the meat can be brushed with beaten egg, sprinkled with finely crushed breadcrumbs, placed in a baking pan whose bottom is covered with broth, and put in a warm oven to gratinée for 15—20 minutes. Serve as above.
