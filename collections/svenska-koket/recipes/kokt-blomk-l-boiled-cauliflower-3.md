## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads (1 kg.)
- 1 1/4 litres water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 40 gr. melted butter or 3 decilitres hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. The cauliflower heads should be white and firm.
2. Remove the green leaves and cut away the bottom of the stalk.
3. Rinse the cauliflower well and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water (see page 338) for 1 hour.
4. Rinse again, place in boiling salted water with the flower side down, and cook 15-20 minutes, or until just tender, which is tested with a whisk.
5. Place the cauliflower on a warm dish with a slotted spoon, pour the butter or sauce over it, and serve the cauliflower very hot as a vegetable side dish or with roasted meat, salted brisket, or hamburger brisket.
6. Cauliflower is also used as a garnish around meat together with other vegetables.
7. Cauliflower becomes whiter if cooked in half milk and half water.
