## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 1/2 parts cream
- 1 tsp powdered sugar
- jam
- 1 tsp butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites.
2. Whisk the yolks together with the cream and sugar.
3. Fold in the egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the batter immediately into a well-buttered omelette pan and bake until light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
5. Turn it out onto a plate immediately, place the jam on half of the omelette, and fold the other half over.
