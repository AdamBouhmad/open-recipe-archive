## Ingredients

- 2 small cauliflower heads (3/4 kg)
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr)
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr) for the form
- 2 tablespoons toasted breadcrumbs
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese (if served as a side dish)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the cauliflower heads as in recipe no. 709, let drain in a sieve, divide into florets, which are laid in a buttered and bread-strewn, heat-resistant form.
2. For the egg custard: whisk the egg yolks with the flour, salt and sugar, and the milk, after which the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
3. Pour the egg custard over the cauliflower and sprinkle with parsley, if desired.
4. Place the form in regular oven heat and let stand until the egg custard is baked and has a nice color.
5. Serve with forcemeat, fried meat, or as a side dish. In the latter case, sprinkle the egg custard with 1 tablespoon grated cheese instead of parsley before placing in the oven.
