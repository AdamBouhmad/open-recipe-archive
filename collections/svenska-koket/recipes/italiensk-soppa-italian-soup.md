## Ingredients

- 2 kg meat bones
- 3 tablespoons fat
- 4 liters water
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 carrots
- 1 small bunch parsley stems
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 cup star-shaped pasta
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese (preferably Roquefort)
- 2 tablespoons sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Brown the meat bones in the fat
2. Add 4 liters of water and bring to a boil
3. Skim well when it boils, then add seasonings, carrots, and the parsley bunch
4. Cook covered with a tight-fitting lid until the broth has good flavor, about 4–5 hours
5. Strain and skim the broth well
6. Fry together the butter and flour, add the broth, and add the rinsed star-shaped pasta
7. Let the broth cook about 20 minutes, or until the pasta is completely soft and swollen
8. Add the grated cheese and wine, taste and adjust seasoning
