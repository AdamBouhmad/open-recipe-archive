## Ingredients

- 1¼ liters fresh sauerkraut
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper
- ½ kilogram fresh pork
- 1 carrot
- 1 small red onion
- 1 clove
- 20 grams juniper berries
- 50 grams lard
- 4 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 liters beef broth

## Directions

1. Do not soak fresh sauerkraut in water. If the sauerkraut is old, soak it about 12 hours, then drain it well in a sieve before arranging.
2. Mix the cabbage with salt and pepper.
3. Cut the pork into slices and line the bottom and sides of an iron saucepan with them.
4. Add the cabbage along with the peeled carrot cut into four pieces, the peeled onion with the clove inserted in it, the juniper berries (tied in muslin) if desired, the lard, and the parsley stems tied together with the bay leaf.
5. Layer the remaining pork slices on top.
6. Pour in the broth so that it stands above the cabbage.
7. Bring the cabbage to a boil, then place the saucepan in a warm oven.
