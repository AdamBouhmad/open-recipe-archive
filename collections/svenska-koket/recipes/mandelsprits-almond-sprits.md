## Ingredients

- 280 gr. butter
- 210 gr. caster sugar
- 1 egg
- 35 gr. grated bitter almond
- 425 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and blend together with the other ingredients and the sifted wheat flour on the baking board.
2. When the dough is well worked, press it through a pastry bag and form into S-shapes or wreaths.
3. Bake the cookies in moderate oven heat on well-dried baking sheets.
