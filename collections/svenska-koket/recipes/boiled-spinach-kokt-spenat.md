## Ingredients

- 750 g spinach
- 3 liters water
- 4 teaspoons salt (20 g)
- 80 g butter, melted

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach (remove the coarse stalks), then rinse it very thoroughly, place on a sieve and let it drain.
2. Blanch it over fresh fire (place in boiling, salted water) 5-7 minutes, place immediately in cold water to retain the color, place on a sieve and let it drain well.
3. Place on warm carrot, pour over the hot butter and serve with fried fish or meat.
