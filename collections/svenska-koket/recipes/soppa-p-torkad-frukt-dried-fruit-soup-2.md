## Ingredients

- 100 g dried apples
- 100 g dried pears
- 50 g raisins
- 2 liters water
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- zest of ½ lemon
- 1½-2 teaspoons crushed sugar
- 25 g rice or potato flour
- ½ deciliter sherry or French wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit very well in lukewarm water and let it soak overnight in the water in which it is to be cooked.
2. Place it in the pot together with the cinnamon, lemon zest, and sugar and let cook until soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the rice flour, stirred into ½ cup cold water, and let cook 5 minutes.
4. Season with the wine and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or biscuits.
