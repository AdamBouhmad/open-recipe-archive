## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 5 deciliters fish stock or broth
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 2 tablespoon chopped capers
- 1—2 tablespoon caper vinegar
- salt
- white pepper
- 1—2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes.
2. Fish stock and cream are added and the sauce is allowed to cook for 5 minutes.
3. Capers and vinegar are added and the sauce is brought to a boil.
4. Season with spices and blend in the beaten egg yolk.
5. If desired, add the cold butter at the end.
6. Serve mainly with fish but also with boiled veal or lamb breast.
