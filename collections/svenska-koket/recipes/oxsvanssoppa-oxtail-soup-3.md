## Ingredients

- 1 oxtail
- 1/2 kg beef neck
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 1-2 red onions
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 liters beef broth or water
- 15 g cornstarch
- salt, sugar, cayenne
- 1/2 deciliter dry sherry
- 1 tablespoon tomato puree (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the oxtail in cold water, scald in boiling water, dry with a cloth and cut into pieces at the joints, splitting the largest pieces crosswise.
2. Rinse the meat quickly, dry and cut into smaller pieces.
3. Peel the onions, trim and cut the vegetables into pieces, rinse the parsley and tie together with the bay leaf.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the meat and vegetables and brown over gentle heat for approximately 1 hour.
5. Add 1/4 liter of boiling broth, which under steady stirring should boil down to a jelly. When the jelly has turned a beautiful dark brown color without burning, add the remaining broth and let the soup cook slowly with the lid tightly closed for approximately 3 hours or until the meat is completely tender.
6. Remove the meat with a slotted spoon, trim the oxtail pieces, cut the meat into small pieces and place in the soup bowl.
7. Strain the soup, skim well, bring to a boil, thicken with the cornstarch mixed with 1/2 cup cold water, and let it boil under stirring for 5 minutes.
8. Season with the spices, wine and tomato puree and pour over the meat in the soup bowl. Serve very hot.
9. If desired, a cooked carrot can be cut into decorative shapes and served as garnish in the soup.
