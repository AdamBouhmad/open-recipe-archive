## Ingredients

- 4 large white cucumbers
- 3 liters water, 1 tsp. salt (15 gr.) for blanching
- 1 1/4 kg. loaf sugar
- 1 liter spirit vinegar
- 2 gr. whole mace
- 2 gr. whole white pepper
- 3 pieces ginger (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut them lengthwise in half, remove the seeds with a silver spoon, and cut the cucumber halves crosswise.
2. Place some cucumbers at a time in boiling salted water and let them simmer slowly until soft, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon, lay them on linen to drain, and then lay them in alternating layers in a jar.
4. Cook the sugar, vinegar, and spices tied in muslin to a clear simmering syrup, which is poured hot over the cucumbers.
5. Cover the jar with a doubled cloth and plate.
6. Place the spice bag on top of the plate.
7. After 3 days, strain off the syrup, boil it together with the spices, and pour it cold over the cucumbers, which are carefully covered.
8. Proceed in this manner two more times with six-day intervals, after which the cucumbers are ready.
9. Bind the jar well.
