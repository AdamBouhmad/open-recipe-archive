## Ingredients

- 1 firm, medium-sized fresh cucumber of about 1/2 kg.
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr.)
- 1 deciliter vinegar
- 4 teaspoons sugar (20 gr.)
- 2-3 tablespoons fine table oil
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 teaspoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumber from the flower end to the stem end with not too thin peel, cut into 3 mm. thick slices, which are sprinkled with salt and left to stand for about 1 hour.
2. Beat out the juice, place the slices in a salad bowl and pour over them a mixture of the vinegar, sugar, table oil if desired, and white pepper, and sprinkle with the parsley.
3. Serve with the sandwich board and with roasted meat.
