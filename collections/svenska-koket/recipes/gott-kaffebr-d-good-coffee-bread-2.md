## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 185 gr. sugar
- 2 hg. melted butter
- 115 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. sweet almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, cut them into thin slices lengthwise and lightly toast them in the oven.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes.
3. Add the butter and flour.
4. Pour the batter into a small long pan that is well cleaned and greased with cold butter.
5. The batter should reach 1 cm. high in the pan.
6. Sprinkle the almonds evenly over the cake and bake in a not-too-hot oven until it has a nice light brown color on both sides.
7. Turn the cake out when it has cooled and cut into pieces approximately 6 cm. long and 2 cm. wide. Serve fresh with coffee or tea.
