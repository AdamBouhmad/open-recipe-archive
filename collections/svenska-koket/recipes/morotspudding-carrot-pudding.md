## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg carrots
- 6 tablespoons grated soft bread
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 parts milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Half-cook the carrots and grate them.
2. Mix the other ingredients well together, stir in the grated carrots.
3. Pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Place in moderate oven heat to set and brown.
5. Serve with fish and meat dishes in general.
