## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked fish (1/2 kg pike)
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 5 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Grate the cooked fish finely like rice grains with a fork.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Pour into a dish; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
4. Mix in the fish along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Taste the mixture, pour into a buttered and bread-crumbed pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
6. If using a baking sheet form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour and turn out.
7. Serve immediately when removed from the oven as a side course with crayfish or lobster sauce, hollandaise or lemon sauce.
