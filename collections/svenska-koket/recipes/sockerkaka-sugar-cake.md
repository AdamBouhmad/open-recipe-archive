## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 320 gr. sugar
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- 8 bitter almonds
- 1 cup milk
- 3 tbsp. clarified butter
- 300 gr. wheat flour
- 3 heaping tsp. baking powder
- 0.5 tbsp. washed butter
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Stir the eggs together with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy, approximately 0.5 hour.
2. Add the lemon peel, blanched and grated almond, milk and the melted butter.
3. Finally stir down the flour, sifted with the baking powder.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined form and bake at fairly good oven heat.
