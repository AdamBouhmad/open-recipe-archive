## Ingredients

- 2 kg rowan berries of sweet rowan (about 3 liters)
- 1 1/2 kg sugar
- 4 dcl water

## Directions

1. The berries are picked when ripe and juicy.
2. The jam is then made as in recipe 1031. The jam is poured into small glass jars, which are covered the following day.
3. Served as a salad or on butter and bread with tea.
