## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon currants (50 gr.)
- 6 1/2 deciliters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon sugar or syrup
- 1—2 tablespoon vinegar
- salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Currants are cleaned and rinsed very well in several lukewarm waters.
2. They are placed in 6 1/2 deciliters cold water and allowed to cook until soft.
3. Drain in a colander and use the currant liquid for the sauce.
4. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes.
5. Currant liquid is added and the sauce is allowed to cook for 5 minutes.
6. Currants are added, the sauce is seasoned with sugar or syrup, vinegar and salt.
7. If desired, blend in the beaten egg yolk.
8. Finally stir in the cold butter.
