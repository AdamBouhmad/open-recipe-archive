## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 1-1½ kg. butter or margarine
- 1 cup granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 cups for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and stir in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
5. Melt the butter and work it into the dough together with the sugar, and work the dough again and set it to rise again.
6. Shape the dough into long, narrow breads or round cakes, which are later scored. It can also be baked into envelope bread.
7. Place the breads on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets to rise.
8. When ready to bake, brush them with cold water and place in a hot oven.
9. This type of wheat bread is also used for sandwich bread, but in that case omit the sugar.
