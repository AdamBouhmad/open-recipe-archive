## Ingredients

- 60 gr. block chocolate with sugar
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 tbsp. potato flour (8 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1-2 tbsp. powdered sugar (15 gr.)
- cognac or liqueur (optional)

## Directions

1. Divide the chocolate finely into the cream, which is brought to boil with whisking so that the chocolate melts.
2. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar and potato flour to a foam.
3. Add the boiling milk with vigorous whisking and let the sauce then simmer over the heat with continued whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
4. Serve both warm and cold with puddings and cakes and can if desired be flavored with a little cognac or liqueur.
5. The sauce can also be mixed with 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
