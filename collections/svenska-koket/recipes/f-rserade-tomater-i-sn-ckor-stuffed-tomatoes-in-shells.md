## Ingredients

- 6 large tomatoes
- 20 gr. dried mushrooms
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for shells
- 2 tbsp. crushed bread crusts for shells

## Directions

1. Dip the tomatoes 1 minute in boiling water and peel off the skin.
2. Open the fruit at the stem and carefully remove the seeds with a teaspoon handle.
3. Soak the mushrooms in water for several hours, place in boiling lightly salted water and simmer approximately 1/2 hour, then drain in a colander and let drip off.
4. Cut them into fine dice and fry in half of the butter until clear.
5. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, thin the cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
6. Add the beaten egg yolk and let the sauce simmer.
7. Add the mushrooms and parsley, and season the stew with the spices.
8. Fill the tomatoes with the stew.
9. Grease shells or small heat-resistant molds with butter and place one tomato in each.
10. Sprinkle with crushed bread crusts and pour melted butter over them.
11. Place the shells in a long pan with coarse salt, put in a warm oven and gratine 10-15 minutes.
12. Serve as a side dish or with roasted meat.
