## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg beef steak (loin or tenderloin)
- 3½ oz smoked ham
- 2 veal kidneys
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- ½ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsp ground white pepper
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 cup water
- Butter dough: 125 g butter, 125 g flour, 3-4 tbsp water

## Directions

1. The butter dough is prepared as simple butter dough.
2. The onion is blanched for 10 minutes. The veal kidneys are blanched and cut into thin slices; likewise the onion and ham.
3. From the beef steak, thin, well-beaten steaks are prepared, which are turned in a mixture of salt, white pepper and flour.
4. In a fireproof form of clay or porcelain, first a layer of onion is placed, over it a layer of beef slices, then a layer of ham and veal kidney, and so it continues until the form is filled.
5. The water is added, and 3/4 of the butter dough is placed over the pie as a lid. With a larding needle, holes are made in a few places in the dough so that gases formed during baking can escape.
6. The pie is brushed with egg white. The remaining butter dough is rolled out thin and cut into narrow strips. With it the pie is lined in a grid pattern, or it is garnished with small leaves cut from the butter dough. Finally, the pie's edges are also covered with a strip of butter dough.
7. It is brushed over with beaten egg or water and baked in a hot oven for approximately 1½ hours.
