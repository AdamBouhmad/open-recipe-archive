## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed hare, about 3 1/2 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- Marinade: 1 bottle red wine
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 allspice berries
- 1/4 teaspoon thyme
- For stewing: 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- the marinade
- 8 pickled onions, in wedges
- 10 mushrooms, in slices
- 8 pitted olives

## Directions

1. Prepare the hare as in the previous recipe, cut into smaller pieces, which are rubbed with the spices and placed in a stoneware pot.
2. Mix all the ingredients for the marinade, then pour it over the hare, which is left to rest until the following day.
3. Remove the meat, wrap it well in cloth and strain the marinade.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the meat, brown it and sprinkle with flour.
5. Dilute with the marinade and let the meat simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid for about 1 hour, during which shake the pot often so that the meat does not stick to the bottom.
6. Add the onion, mushrooms and olives, cut into thin rings, and let the stew simmer until everything is tender, or about 1/2 hour.
7. Place the meat on a warm carrot, whisk up the sauce, taste it and pour it over.
8. Garnish the dish with small triangular bread croutons and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
