## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 hg. of the meat
- 50 gr. lard
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 msk. flour
- salt, white pepper
- 1 tsk. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 msk. finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1 1/2—2 dcl. thick cream
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.) for frying
- 1 msk. flour (10 gr.)
- 3 dcl. broth
- 2 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see page 232) and cut across into 12 even slices, which are lightly flattened.
2. Cut the lard into strips, which are dipped in a mixture of salt and pepper, and with this lard the meat slices.
3. Roll them up and tie at two places with fine twine, dipped in boiling water.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the rolls in and brown nicely.
