## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. salted beans
- 8 dcl. water
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. bean liquid
- 3 dcl. cream or milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the beans in a colander, rinse with cold water and let them soak in plenty of water for approximately 12 hours.
2. Rinse them again, place in cold water and let them simmer slowly until they are completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. Drain off the liquid and reserve it.
4. For the sauce, make a roux with butter and flour, add the bean liquid and cream, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
5. Temper the sauce with the egg yolk and let it just simmer, then add the beans and season the stew with sugar.
6. Serve with boiled sausage, salted meat etc.
