## Ingredients

- 2 kilograms perch
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 teaspoons vinegar
- 1½ kilograms butter
- 6 tablespoons coarsely chopped parsley
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- 5-6 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Scale the perch, gut them and rinse them well and rub them inside and out with a mixture of the salt and vinegar.
2. Place the fish in layers in a casserole rubbed with butter; between each layer place butter, parsley and flour.
3. Last add the water.
4. Let the fish only simmer with a tightly fitting lid.
5. When it is cooked, place it on a plate, whisk the sauce in the casserole and pour it over the fish, without straining or diluting it.
