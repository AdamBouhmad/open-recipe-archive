## Ingredients

- 350 gr. sweet almond
- 8 bitter almonds
- 425 gr. powdered sugar
- 4—5 egg whites
- wafer paper
- cooked, pitted sour plums or yellow plums for filling
- jelly in various colors for filling

## Directions

1. Blanch the almond, dry it and grind it.
2. Pound it then with the sugar and one egg white to a fine and smooth mass.
3. Whip the remaining egg whites to stiff peaks and carefully fold into the almond mixture, which should be quite loose.
4. Cut a round bottom from wafer paper, as large as the dish or bowl on which the cake shall be served.
5. Spread half of the dough on it.
6. Press the remaining dough through a pastry bag in squares over the bottom.
7. Bake the cake in a not too hot oven until it is beautifully golden brown but must not be completely dry.
8. When the cake has cooled, fill the squares with cooked, pitted sour plums or yellow plums and jelly in various colors.
