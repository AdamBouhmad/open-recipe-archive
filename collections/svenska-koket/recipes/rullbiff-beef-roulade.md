## Ingredients

- 1⅔ kg boneless beef (flank)
- ½ tsp white pepper
- ½ tbsp salt
- 1 red onion
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 liter broth or water
- ⅛ tbsp butter (for sauce)
- 1⅛ tbsp flour
- beef stock
- 1 part thin cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Cut it thereafter into a larger oblong slice, which is sprinkled with pepper and salt and with the peeled and finely chopped red onion.
3. Roll the meat together and bind it well with twine (previously dipped in boiling water) and brown it in the butter.
4. Dilute with boiling broth or water, and let the meat roast 3–4 hours. During roasting it should often be diluted and basted with the broth.
5. Strain the meat stock and skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
