## Ingredients

- 2 hg butter
- 1 hg sugar
- 2 hg flour
- 10 gr sweet almonds (10 pieces)
- 5 bitter almonds
- 1 egg
- 5 rhubarb stalks (approximately 7 hg)
- 3 dcl castor sugar (255 gr)
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the pan
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. The butter is washed and worked together with the sugar, flour, the blanched and ground almonds and egg into a smooth dough, which is left to rest in a cold place for approximately 2 hours.
2. The rhubarb is peeled thinly and cut into 3 cm long pieces.
3. Two-thirds of the dough is rolled out and with it a greased baking sheet or frying pan is lined, after which the rhubarb is added in alternating layers with the sugar, and the dough edge is folded over.
4. The rest of the dough is rolled out to a lid, with which the pie is covered, and the edges are pressed down well.
5. The pie is baked in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1 hour, or until the rhubarb is soft and the dough is baked through when tested with a whisk.
6. It is taken out and left to stand for a quarter hour, after which it is turned out and served warm or cold with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
