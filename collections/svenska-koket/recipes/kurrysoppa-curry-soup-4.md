## Ingredients

- 1 small celeriac
- 1 carrot
- 1 1/2 liters stock
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2-3 egg yolks
- curry powder to taste
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 deciliter rice grains (1 hg)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 g salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the celeriac and carrot in the usual way, cut into fine strips and cook until soft in the stock, which is then strained.
2. Place the vegetables in the soup bowl.
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and allow to boil while stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Beat the egg yolks and whisk into the soup, then season with curry powder, salt and cayenne pepper and pour over the vegetables in the soup bowl.
5. Rinse the rice grains, place in boiling salted water and allow to cook until soft, about 15 minutes.
6. Drain in a colander, rinse with cold water and let stand in the oven for a few minutes, stirring with a fork to make them dry and warm.
7. Arrange on a platter and serve with the soup.
