## Ingredients

- 100 grams chocolate
- 2 liters milk
- 0.5 to 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons potato starch
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil and break the chocolate into pieces and add it.
2. Whisk the milk over the heat until it boils and the chocolate melts.
3. Add the sugar and thicken the soup with potato starch dissolved in a little water.
4. Bring to a good boil again, pour up, and serve with whipped cream.
