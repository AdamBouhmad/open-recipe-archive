## Ingredients

- 400 gr. boneless broth meat
- 1-2 red onions
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 dcl. meat gravy or broth
- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 1/2-2 dcl. boiling milk
- 2 eggs
- salt, sugar
- 1 msk. finely crushed rusks
- 1-2 msk. grated cheese
- 2 msk. melted butter (20 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. butter for the dish (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely (do not grind).
2. Peel and chop the onions, fry in butter, add the meat and fry well, sprinkle with spices and add the boiling meat gravy or broth.
3. Peel the potatoes raw, cut into pieces, boil quickly, drain well and press through a potato ricer.
4. Place the mash in a saucepan, add the cold butter immediately and work with a fork, add the boiling milk, add the beaten egg yolks and stir until white and fluffy.
5. Season with salt and sugar and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Butter a heatproof dish or pie pan, place the meat in it, spread the mashed potatoes over the meat, sprinkle with rusks mixed with cheese, and finally pour the melted butter over it.
7. Place the dish in a warm oven and bake for 20-30 minutes.
8. Serve with pan sauce or melted butter. If desired, the meat can be browned in cream instead of butter; in the mashed potatoes, butter and eggs can be omitted.
