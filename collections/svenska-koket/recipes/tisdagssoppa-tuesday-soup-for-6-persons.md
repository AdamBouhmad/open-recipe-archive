## Ingredients

- 30 gr pearl barley
- 1 1/2 liters pork or meat broth
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 leek
- 1/2 liter potatoes
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr)
- 1/2 liter milk
- 2 dcl thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse the pearl barley, add it to boiling broth and allow to cook for about 1 1/2 hours.
2. Sauté the roots and leek, cut into strips and add to the soup.
3. Peel the potatoes, cut into cubes and add, then allow the soup to cook until the vegetables are tender.
4. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, thin the milk and cream, add the soup and allow to cook with stirring for 5 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper and finally add the parsley.
6. If a finer soup is desired, omit the pearl barley and whisk 2-3 egg yolks in the soup bowl.
