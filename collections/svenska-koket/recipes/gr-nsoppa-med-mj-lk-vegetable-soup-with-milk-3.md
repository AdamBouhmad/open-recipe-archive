## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 root celeriac
- 1 small leek
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 liter boiling water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons fresh peas
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 liter milk
- 1/2 hectoliter spinach
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon cold butter (20 g)

## Directions

1. Prepare the roots in the usual way and cut into small cubes; cut the leek into fine strips
2. Clean the cauliflower, divide into florets and let soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour
3. Sauté the root vegetables and leek well in half the butter, add to the boiling water with salt and cook until half tender
4. Add the rinsed cauliflower and peas and cook until done
5. Remove the vegetables with a skimming ladle to the soup bowl; use the broth for the soup
6. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes; gradually add the broth and boiled milk
7. Add the cleaned, rinsed and skimmed spinach and cook the soup while stirring for 5 minutes
8. Add the parsley and season with spices
9. Whip the egg yolks and cream together in the soup bowl and add the soup while whisking
10. Let simmer and pour over the vegetables and cold butter in the soup bowl
11. In simpler versions the eggs and cream can be omitted
