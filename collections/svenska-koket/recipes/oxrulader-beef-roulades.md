## Ingredients

- 1⅜ kg boneless inner loin
- 1½ hg lard
- ½ tbsp salt
- ⅛ tsk white pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into thin slices, which are well beaten.
2. On each one is placed a thin, well-seasoned slice of lard, and the roulades are rolled up and bound tightly in two places.
3. Brown them in the butter, and sprinkle flour over.
4. Dilute the roulades thereafter with the boiling liquid and let them slowly cook with a tightly closed lid until they feel tender when tested with a whisk.
5. Remove the twines, place the roulades high on a carrot platter, and pour the sauce over.
