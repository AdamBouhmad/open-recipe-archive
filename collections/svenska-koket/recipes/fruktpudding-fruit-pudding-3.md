## Ingredients

- 2½ parts milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 stick vanilla
- 100 grams sugar
- 1 hectogram biscuits
- 2 parts preserved fruit: pineapple, peaches or apples, pears, plums
- ½ liter thick cream
- 5 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Let the milk, egg yolks, vanilla, and sugar boil over the fire with whisking until the cream thickens.
2. Continue whisking until the cream has cooled.
3. Then add the biscuits, broken into pieces, and the fruit, likewise cut into pieces, and the cream whipped to peaks.
4. Finally stir in the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water.
5. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set it out to stiffen.
6. When it is to be served, dip the mold for a moment in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with fruit.
