## Ingredients

- 6 large pork chops
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 9 beautiful, sour apples
- 1 decilitre broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as in the preceding recipe, rub in the seasonings and brown quickly on both sides in half of the butter.
2. Peel the apples, cut into thin slices, which are placed on a buttered, heatproof dish or preferably one of silver.
3. Place the half-fried chops on top and cover with the remaining butter, distributed in flakes.
4. Deglaze the frying pan with the broth, and pour the sauce onto the dish, which is then placed in a warm oven, and in it the chops and apples are fried until they are tender.
5. Serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
