## Ingredients

- 1 liter cooked potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 grams)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut into 3 mm thick slices or even cubes.
2. Brown half of the butter in a frying pan, add the potatoes and sprinkle with a mixture of the crushed rusks, salt and sugar.
3. Place the remaining butter on top and brown the potatoes while shaking the pan frequently.
4. Finally sprinkle with parsley and serve the potatoes immediately with fried fish and meat.
