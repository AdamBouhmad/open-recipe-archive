## Ingredients

- 3 kg. rhubarb
- 1 liter red currants
- 2 liters water
- 650 gr. sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the rhubarb, dry it, cut it into pieces, place it together with the rinsed currants in water and let it cook soft.
2. Strain the mass and let it drip until the following day.
3. Weigh the juice, dissolve the sugar in the juice, and treat it as in recipe no. 1046.
