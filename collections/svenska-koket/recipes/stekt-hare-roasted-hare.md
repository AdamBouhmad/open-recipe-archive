## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- ½ lemon
- 1 hg. lard
- 2 tbsp. butter
- ½ tbsp. salt
- ½–1 liter broth or water
- 1 tbsp. butter for sauce
- 1½ tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 1–2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The hare should have hung a few days to become tender. Skin it, and remove all membranes very carefully. Cut the head off at the shoulders, as well as the thin parts on the sides. Break the back into 5–6 connected pieces, likewise break the hind legs. Tuck one hind leg between the tendons in the other. Rinse the hare well, dry with a cloth and rub with the lemon until the meat has a fresh and sour smell. Lard it thereafter with the lard cut into fine strips in four rows on each side of the back and on the meatiest part of the leg. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, brown the hare therein on all sides, salt and dilute with broth little by little. Let it then roast until done with a tightly closed lid, for which 1–1½ hours are required.
2. The hare can either be served with its stock, or the sauce can be thickened according to general rules for sauces.
