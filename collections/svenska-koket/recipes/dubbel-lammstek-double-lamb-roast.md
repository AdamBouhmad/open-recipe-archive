## Ingredients

- 4 kg. lamb roast
- 3 hg. lard
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 hg. butter
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. From one young lamb, cut off the two connected roasts. The shanks are cut off — but roasted with — and some of the fat is trimmed.
2. The meat is treated, roasted and served as in the previous recipe.
3. The roast is carved, the slices are placed together again so that it looks whole, and is garnished with cooked vegetables (legumes).
