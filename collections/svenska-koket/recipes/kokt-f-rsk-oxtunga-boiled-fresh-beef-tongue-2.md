## Ingredients

- 1 beef tongue
- water
- 3-4 tbsp salt
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 1 carrot

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue and soak it in water for a couple of hours.
2. Place it in boiling water, which should just cover it, skim well, and add the spices and the carved carrot.
3. Let the tongue cook slowly for 2 1/2-3 hours, or until the skin can be easily pulled off.
4. After the skin is removed, place it back in the broth to keep it warm until serving.
5. Slice the tongue on the bias into 1/2 cm thick slices, arrange them so that the tongue appears whole on the platter at serving.
6. Garnish with parsley or salad and serve with cooked or stewed vegetables or with a sauce, such as brown mushroom, morel, or olive sauce, and potatoes.
7. Leftovers of cooked tongue can be sliced, dipped in egg and breadcrumbs, and fried in butter.
8. They can also be served in brown caper sauce made from 4 dcl meat stock, 1-2 tsp cornstarch, 1 tbsp finely chopped capers, 1/2 tbsp Madeira, salt, and sugar. When the sauce has boiled for 5 minutes, add the tongue slices and let them boil for 5 minutes. Serve with browned potatoes as a breakfast dish.
