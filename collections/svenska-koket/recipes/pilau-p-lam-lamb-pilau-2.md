## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb breast
- water
- 1 tbsp salt
- 2 tbsp butter
- 0.5 Portuguese onion
- 2-4 carrots
- 0.5-1 rutabaga
- 1.5 cups Carolina rice
- 2 liters water
- 1 tbsp salt

## Directions

1. Wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into nice pieces. Bring to a boil in warm water, skim, salt, and simmer for 1 hour, then remove and strain the broth. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it in half the butter, add the meat and brown it likewise. Add one liter of the strained broth, and let the meat simmer slowly until tender. Prepare the root vegetables, cut into fine strips and brown in the other half of the butter, then add broth and let the vegetables simmer—preferably separately—until soft.
2. Rinse the rice and place in 2 liters boiling salted water and simmer until soft, stirring occasionally with a fork. Drain and place in a warm oven to dry. Arrange the meat in the center of the serving plate, and rice and vegetables in groups around it. Garnish with parsley.
3. Strain the sauce, skim, and thicken if desired.
