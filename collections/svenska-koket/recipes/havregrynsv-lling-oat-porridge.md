## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons oat flour (50 g)
- 1/4 liter water
- 1 3/4 liters milk
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with water and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Bring the milk to a boil, pour in the flour mixture while whisking and let the porridge simmer while whisking for 15 minutes.
3. Season with salt and sugar and serve as a breakfast or evening dish.
