## Ingredients

- 3/4 litre small potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 3-4 decilitres thin cream
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a little Cayenne
- 1-2 tablespoons capers
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped chervil or cress

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them well clean and cook with skins on in salted water; peel and let cool, whereupon cut them into thin, even slices, which are placed in a salad bowl.
2. The potatoes can also be cut into cubes.
3. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the cream is diluted in, and the sauce is allowed to cook for 5 minutes.
4. The sauce is seasoned with vinegar and spices and poured over the potato slices, which are carefully mixed with the sauce.
5. Let the salad cool, garnish with the capers and chervil or cress.
6. Serve as the preceding.
