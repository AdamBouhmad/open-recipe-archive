## Ingredients

- 1 veal liver
- 1½ kg lard
- ½ Portuguese onion
- 4 eggs
- 1 hectogram butter
- 1 teaspoon wheat flour
- 4 hectograms lard for the mold
- white pepper
- thyme
- marjoram
- salt
- 1 large truffle
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the veal liver and cut it into slices.
2. Cut the lard likewise into slices, blanch the onion and cut it into slices.
3. Pass everything three times through the meat grinder.
4. Add the eggs along with the sliced, but cooled butter, flour, and spices.
5. Work the forcemeat well and pass it through a sieve.
6. Lastly add the truffle, cut into large cubes, and the cream beaten stiff.
7. Pour the mixture into a mold lined with lard slices, cover with lard slices and tie over with buttered paper.
8. Bake the pâté in a water bath in the oven for approximately 2 hours.
