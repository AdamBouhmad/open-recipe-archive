## Ingredients

- 1 young duck
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½-1 liter broth
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- 1 part thick cream
- meat dripping

## Directions

1. Pluck the duck, singe, clean out, rinse well and dry. Rub the inside with half the salt and truss.
2. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the bird in it with the breast up and brown well on all sides. Sprinkle with the remaining salt and add the broth. Let the duck braise slowly for 1½-2 hours.
3. Strain and skim the meat dripping, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules.
4. The duck can also be roasted in the oven like a goose, stuffed with apples and prunes.
