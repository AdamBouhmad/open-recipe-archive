## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- 1 liter water for blanching
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons thick cream
- salt, white pepper, ginger

## Directions

1. Let the calf brain soak in water, changing the water frequently so that all blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch the brain for 45 minutes and place on a sieve.
3. When cold, remove all membranes and chop the brain with a knife on a cutting board.
4. Brown the butter in a pot, add the brain and brown it well.
5. Add the cream and seasonings and let the stew come to a good boil.
6. Pour onto warm toast and garnish with small croutons (fried bread slices).
7. Serve at the smorgasbord.
