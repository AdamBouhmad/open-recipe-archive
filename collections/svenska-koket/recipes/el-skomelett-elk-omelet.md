## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 4 deciliters sour cream
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped leek
- 3 hectograms brined side bacon

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with salt and flour for 10 minutes, then add the milk and leek.
2. Cut the bacon into thin slices and crisp it lightly in a hot pan.
3. Remove it, pour off the fat and wipe the pan well with white paper so that no salt remains.
4. Pour the fat back into the pan, which is again placed over the fire to become hot, then pour in the egg mixture and place the bacon on top.
5. When the omelet is cooked on one side, turn it onto a lid, tip it back into the pan and cook on the other side.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
7. Smoked ham can also be used.
