## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cherries
- 1 liter water
- 1 1/2 hg sugar
- 3 tbsp mondamin (45 gr) or 3 tbsp potato flour (45 gr)

## Directions

1. Clean the cherries, rinse and let drain.
2. Crush half of the berries in a stone mortar.
3. Put the whole and crushed berries in the cold water together with the sugar and let boil until the berries are soft, then strain through a colander.
4. Pour the juice back into the pot, taste and thicken with the flour mixed in 1 dcl cold water.
5. Let the cream boil for 5 minutes while stirring.
6. Pour into a compote bowl rinsed with warm water, sprinkle with sugar so as not to form skin, let cool and serve with cream or milk.
