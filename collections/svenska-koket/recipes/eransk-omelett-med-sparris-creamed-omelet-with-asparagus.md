## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper
- 10 fresh asparagus tips
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. flour (5 gr.)
- 1 dcl. cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the asparagus in salted water and let it drain.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Taste with the spices and carefully blend in the asparagus.
4. Prepare the omelet as in recipe no. 24 and just before the eggs have fully set, add the asparagus stewing. Form the omelet with a fork, turn it out onto a warm dish and garnish, if desired, with a few blanched asparagus tips which have been browned in butter.
5. The blanched asparagus can also be mixed with a few tablespoons of hollandaise and filled in the omelet after it has been turned out onto the dish.
