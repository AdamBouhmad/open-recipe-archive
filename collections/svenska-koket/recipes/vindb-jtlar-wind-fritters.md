## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 3 dcl. water or milk
- 125 gr. wheat flour
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Butter and water or milk are brought to a boil, flour is added with steady whisking and the batter is allowed to boil until it releases from the pan.
2. It is poured out, the whisk is squeezed out well, and one egg at a time is worked in.
3. The mass is allowed to rest for 1 hour, then pressed through a piping bag or pastry bag onto a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet in the form of bumps or small oblong buns, which are baked in regular oven heat with a baking sheet underneath.
4. During baking, the oven door must not be opened for 10 minutes, because the draft will cause the cakes to collapse and become flat.
5. Served with consommé or bouillon.
