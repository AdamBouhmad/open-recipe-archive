## Ingredients

- 2.5 hectograms pears
- 6 pieces ginger
- 1.8 kg lump sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, remove the blossom end, and cut a cross into each pear.
2. Scrape the stems and cut them down to half length.
3. After peeling, place the pears in fresh water.
4. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup.
5. Place the pears in along with the crushed ginger, tied in a thin cloth, and allow to cook slowly for 3-4 hours, or until they are completely soft and clear.
6. Place the pears in jars and pour the hot syrup over.
7. If the syrup does not have sufficiently strong ginger flavor, add a little grated ginger.
