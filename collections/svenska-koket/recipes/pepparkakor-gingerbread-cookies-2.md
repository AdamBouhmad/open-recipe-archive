## Ingredients

- 850 g syrup
- 1/3 liter clarified butter
- 212 g sugar
- 25 g potash
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 tsp candied orange peel
- 2 tsp ginger
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp cloves
- 2 tsp allspice
- 1 1/2 kg wheat flour

## Directions

1. Cook the syrup and then stir it with the clarified butter until cold.
2. Then stir in the sugar, potash, egg yolks, the finely chopped candied orange peel, and the ground spices.
3. Last, work in the flour, and let the dough stand until the next day.
4. Roll out quite thin and cut cookies with a measure, which are baked in good oven heat.
