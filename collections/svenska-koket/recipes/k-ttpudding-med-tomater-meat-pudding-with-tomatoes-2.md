## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipe no. 631 or no. 632
- 6 large tomatoes
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare the ground meat according to the aforementioned recipes.
2. Clean the tomatoes, rinse them, and cut them into slices, which are sprinkled with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Grease a pudding mold with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
4. Layer the ground meat and tomatoes in the mold, alternating; the first and last layer shall be meat.
5. Cook or bake the pudding as in recipe no. 638 and serve with meat sauce (see recipe no. 429) or clarified butter as well as potatoes.
