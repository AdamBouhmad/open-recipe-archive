## Ingredients

- 1 kg. marrow pipe
- 0.5 kg. veal brisket
- 1 hen
- 4 liters broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip

## Directions

1. Clean the hen in the usual way and brown it on high heat, but do not cook it through.
2. Wash the beef and veal with a cloth dipped in cold water, and cut into small pieces.
3. Place the meat and salt in the cold broth, let it slowly come to a boil and skim.
4. Add the cleaned vegetables.
5. Let the broth simmer slowly for 4-5 hours and then clarify in the usual way.
6. Serve with pastries, vol-au-vents, macaroni or vermicelli.
