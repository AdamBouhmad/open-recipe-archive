## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 0.25 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Sift flour into a bowl, whisk eggs and mix in with the sifted butter, salt, and sugar.
2. Work the mixture into a smooth dough.
3. Bring milk to boil with cinnamon and sugar.
4. Press the dough through a colander into the boiling milk.
5. Remove from heat and let stand covered with a lid at the side for about 10 minutes.
6. Season and serve.
