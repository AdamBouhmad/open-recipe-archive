## Ingredients

- 4 baked apples
- 2 egg whites
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 kilogram powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg whites until barely frothy.
2. Pass the baked apples through a sieve and fold into the egg white along with the sugar and lemon juice.
3. Stir the mixture for 1 hour with uneven speed—that is, stir the spoon one turn quickly through the mixture and one turn slowly.
4. When the cream has turned into white foam, it is ready.
5. Place on serving dish and, if desired, garnish with carmine sugar.
