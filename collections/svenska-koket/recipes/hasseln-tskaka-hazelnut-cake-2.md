## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 150 gr. sugar
- 1/2 kg. blanched, ground hazelnuts
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks and sugar for 1/2 hour, then add the blanched and ground nuts and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
2. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn mold and bake in a gentle oven heat for about 1 hour.
