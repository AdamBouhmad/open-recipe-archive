## Ingredients

- 1 dozen oysters
- 5 dcl. light stock
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tbsp. thick cream
- salt, white pepper
- sherry, lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the oysters, pour the beard and water from the oysters into a pan together with the stock
2. Let the stock simmer for 10-15 minutes and strain; it should yield 5 dcl.
3. Blanch the oysters in lightly salted water for 2-3 minutes
4. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes
5. Add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
6. Add the egg yolks whisked with cream and let the sauce simmer
7. Add the blanched and cleaned oysters and season with spices, sherry and lemon juice
8. Serve with fish dishes, chicken or veal
