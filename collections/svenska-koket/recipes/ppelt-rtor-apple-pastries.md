## Ingredients

- 4 hg. simple butter dough
- 1 lit. good, loose apples
- 1 part granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as in recipe no. 34.
2. Peel the apples, remove the cores with a melon baller, and cut the apples into thin slices.
3. Roll out the dough into a square of approximately 8 mm. thickness and cut in half with a wheel.
4. On one half, layer the apple slices alternating with sugar, brush the edge with water, place the other half of dough on top, and press the edges together well.
5. Bake in a warm oven until well-baked and the apples are soft, which is tested with a whisk.
6. When the cake is cold, cut it into 4 cm. wide and 8 cm. long pastries, which are sprinkled with icing sugar if desired.
7. This recipe makes approximately 18 pastries.
