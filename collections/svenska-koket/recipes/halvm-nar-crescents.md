## Ingredients

- 150 gr. butter
- 160 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- 110 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. butter for the pan
- jelly or jam for filling
- 2 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp. water

## Directions

1. The butter is melted, allowed to cool and is stirred with the sugar until it becomes white and fluffy.
2. The eggs are added alternately with the flour.
3. A waffle pan is placed in the oven to become hot, is then brushed with melted butter, and 1/2 tbsp. of the batter is placed in each space.
4. The waffle pan is placed in the oven, and when the cakes are baked, they are taken out.
5. They are laid two and two on top of each other with jelly or jam between them.
6. The cake is glazed with water glaze and is then cut in two halves.
