## Ingredients

- 2 kg. short-cut saddle
- 1 1/2 hg. lard
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 dcl. broth
- 3 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. With a sharp knife, remove all membranes from the back without taking any meat with them.
2. Lard the saddle with finely thin strips of lard, dipped in a mixture of the salt and pepper.
3. Place in a long pan, cover with the butter, distributed in flakes.
4. Place in a warm oven and let stand 15 minutes.
5. Then add the hot broth and cream and let roast until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours, basting every 10 minutes, otherwise it will become dry.
6. Carve the saddle as lamb saddle, garnish with browned potatoes, small tartlets filled with lingonberry jelly, or with vegetable salad in small shells of raw cabbage leaves.
7. Serve with reduced sauce (see no. 429), mixed with 1 msk. lingonberry jelly.
8. Cutlets can be cut from the saddle and fried as veal cutlets, with or without breading.
9. Serve with stewed mushrooms or champignons, potato croquettes or chestnut purée.
