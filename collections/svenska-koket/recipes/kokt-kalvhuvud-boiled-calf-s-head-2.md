## Ingredients

- 1 veal head
- 1.5-2 liters water
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 0.5 lemon, sliced
- 1 red onion
- 5 parsley stems
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 1 can mushrooms (0.25 liter)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 4 deciliters of broth
- 0.5 deciliter mushroom broth
- 1-2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tablespoon Madeira
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. The veal head is split (the tongue and brain are removed, the tongue is cooked with the head, the brain is used for stewing, see recipe no. 107), rinsed and soaked in water overnight.
2. It is well cleaned, placed, together with the tongue, in boiling water and skimmed; the seasonings, the prepared carrot, the lemon slices (freed from seeds), the peeled onion and the parsley are added, after which the meat is allowed to cook until tender.
3. The head is removed and allowed to cool; the meat is separated from the bones and cut into even pieces.
4. The broth is strained and skimmed.
5. The butter is melted, the mushrooms, skimmed and sliced, are sautéed in the butter until clear, they are then pressed out with a spoon and the flour is sautéed with the butter for 2 minutes, the meat broth and mushroom broth are added and the sauce is allowed to cook for 5 minutes.
6. It is finished with the egg yolk, the cut meat and the mushrooms are added and the dish is seasoned with spices and a little wine, if desired.
7. Arranged on a warm carrot, sprinkled with parsley and served with boiled potatoes.
8. If the meat is cut finer, the stew can be used in tarts or vol-au-vent.
