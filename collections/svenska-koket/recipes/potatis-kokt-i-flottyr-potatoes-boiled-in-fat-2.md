## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 kg. fat

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush well clean, peel thinly and cut into 1 cm. thick, even strips, which are placed in water as they are cut.
2. Dry them well in a cloth, place in hot fat and let boil therein, until they are tender and nicely golden-brown on the surface but soft inside.
3. Lay up on absorbent paper to drain, sprinkle with salt and serve very hot with cutlets etc.
