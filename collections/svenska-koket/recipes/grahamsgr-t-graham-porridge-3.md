## Ingredients

- 1.3 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 3 deciliters graham flour (165 g)

## Directions

1. Boil the water with salt
2. Whisk in the flour
3. Let the porridge cook slowly with a lid for about 3/4 hour
4. Taste and add more salt if desired
5. Serve with cream or milk
