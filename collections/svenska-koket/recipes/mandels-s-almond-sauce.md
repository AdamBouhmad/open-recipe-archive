## Ingredients

- 50 gr. almonds, of which 6 bitter
- 5 dcl. cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 40 gr. caster sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, grind them through an almond mill and pound them in a stone mortar with 2 teaspoons of cream to a fine paste.
2. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes.
3. Heat the remaining cream with the almond paste and strain through muslin.
4. Heat the cream again and pour it over the egg mixture while whisking vigorously.
5. Let the sauce simmer with continued whisking until thick and foamy, then pour it out and whisk until cold.
6. Taste the sauce and, if desired, season with a little arrack, wine or liqueur, and it may also be colored green with spinach juice or red with carmine.
