## Ingredients

- 210 gr fine butter
- 4 eggs
- 150 gr sugar
- 150 gr flour
- Peel and juice of 1/2 small lemon
- 1/2 msk butter for the iron (10 gr)

## Directions

1. Stir the butter until it becomes white and fluffy, then add the egg yolks, sugar, flour, and lemon peel, and stir the mixture 1/2 hour.
2. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Grease the iron only for the first waffles.
4. Bake the waffles and serve as in recipe no. 775.
