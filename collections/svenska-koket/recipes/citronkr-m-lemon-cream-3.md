## Ingredients

- 1 coffee cup lump sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 part white French wine
- 1 part water
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix the lump sugar, wine, water, grated lemon zest, lemon juice, and egg yolks in a saucepan and whisk vigorously over heat until the cream thickens. Remove the pan from heat and continue whisking until the cream cools.
