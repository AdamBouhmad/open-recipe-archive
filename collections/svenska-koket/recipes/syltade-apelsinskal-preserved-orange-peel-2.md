## Ingredients

- 1 kg. lump sugar to each kg. cooked orange peel
- orange peel
- water

## Directions

1. Soak the orange peels in water overnight.
2. Cook them in water for 3–4 hours, or until they are soft.
3. Remove and weigh them.
4. From the sugar and a little of the cooking liquid, cook a clear sugar syrup.
5. Place the peels in this and cook until they are completely clear.
6. Pour into glass jars.
7. The sugar syrup should completely cover the peels.
