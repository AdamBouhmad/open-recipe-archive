## Ingredients

- 1 litre sliced fresh or 1 litre preserved rhubarb
- 2 litres water
- 1½–2 cup crushed sugar
- ⅓ lemon (optional)
- 1¼ tablespoon potato flour

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse it well and cut into pieces.
2. Bring the water to a boil, add rhubarb and sugar.
3. When the soup comes to a boil, skim it and let it cook until the rhubarb is soft.
4. Mix the potato flour with a little cold water and use it to thicken the soup, which should then be brought to a good boil and tasted.
5. Serve with whipped cream if desired.
6. If the rhubarb is less sour, add juice and peel of ⅓ lemon or vinegar if lemon is not available.
