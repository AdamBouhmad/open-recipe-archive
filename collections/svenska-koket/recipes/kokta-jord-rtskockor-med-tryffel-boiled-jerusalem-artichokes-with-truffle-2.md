## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 3-4 truffles
- 2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes clean, scrape, rinse well, shape round and let soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon, arrange on a round platter with a folded napkin.
4. Scatter over with truffle cut into fine strips.
5. Serve very hot with melted butter.
