## Ingredients

- 2 hg. smoked pork loin
- 1 box sardines
- 12 slices white bread
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The pork loin should be lightly salted and only hot-smoked for about 2 hours.
2. Cut it into 12 slices, which are trimmed even at the edges.
3. On each slice place a sardine fillet, roll the slices together and lay the rolls closely next to each other in a frying pan.
4. From the bread slices, which should be 2 cm. thick, cut away the edges, so that they have the same shape as the rolls.
5. Fry them beautifully golden brown in the butter and place on a warm serving dish.
6. Put the rolls into a hot oven and take them out when they are lightly browned.
7. Lay them on the croutons (bread slices) and serve immediately for the smörgåsbord.
