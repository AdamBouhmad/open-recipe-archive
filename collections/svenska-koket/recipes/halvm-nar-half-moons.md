## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 140 gr. caster sugar
- 210 gr. potato starch
- 420 gr. wheat flour
- 1 egg for brushing
- 25 gr. sweet almond
- 1 dcl. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and work out the water thoroughly
2. Stir it until it is white and fluffy, then work in the sugar and both types of flour
3. Set aside 2 dcl. of the wheat flour for dusting
4. Roll out the dough thinly and cut out with a round mold into cookies
5. Fold the cookies almost in half so they resemble half-moons
6. Brush with whipped egg, sprinkle with blanched and ground almond and pearl sugar
7. Bake until golden brown in a not-too-hot oven
