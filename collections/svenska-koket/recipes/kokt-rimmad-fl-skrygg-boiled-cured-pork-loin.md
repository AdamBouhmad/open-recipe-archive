## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pork loin
- 3 liters water

## Directions

1. Let the pork loin soak in water for 12-24 hours, depending on how heavily it is salted.
2. Break it, place it in cold water, bring quickly to a boil, skim and let it boil for 15 minutes at 100°.
3. Cool the liquid to 82°-83°, and at this temperature let the pork remain until it is tender, or approximately 2 hours.
4. Cut it into nice pieces, place on a warm serving dish, garnish with parsley and serve with root vegetable puree, potato puree or dumplings.
