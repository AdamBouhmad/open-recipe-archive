## Ingredients

- 2 kg boneless inner loin
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 large carrot
- 1 shallot
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 liter broth
- For the stuffing: 5 hectograms of the meat
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 2-2 1/2 deciliters cream
- 1 tin truffle (1/8 liter)
- 2 olives
- 25 g salt ox tongue
- For the rice base: 3 hectograms cheap rice grains
- 1 1/2 liters water
- For garnish: 3 carrots
- 1 tin peas (1 liter)
- 1 tin haricots vert (1/2 liter)
- 1 tin asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 2 cauliflower heads
- 4 tbsp clarified butter (80 g)
- French potatoes, green salad, parsley

## Directions

1. Beat the meat, prepare it and lard it all around (see page 232), then cut it on one short side so it forms a large pocket.
2. Carefully cut out so much meat that it becomes 5 hectograms, and from that prepare stuffing as in recipe 325.
3. Cut the truffle into strips, the olives into small triangles and the tongue into fine cubes and add them last to the stuffing, which should be quite firm and carefully seasoned.
4. Fill the meat with the stuffing and sew the opening well closed.
5. Brown it in the butter together with the prepared carrot cut into slices, the peeled onion and the parsley and sprinkle with a mixture of salt and pepper.
6. Add the boiling broth and let the meat roast slowly, with the lid tightly closed, until tender, or about 3 hours, during which time it should be basted often and turned several times.
7. Cut the roast into thin slices, which are laid together so it appears whole, then place it on a base of rice grains.
8. Rinse the rice grains, scald in hot water, put on in cold water and let cook until soft, or about 20 minutes, then drain in a colander and let drain well.
9. Form the rice on the serving platter into a 3-4 cm high, even base, which should stand a few hours so it becomes firm.
10. Around the meat garnish with the cooked carrots, beautifully cut with a vegetable knife, the peas, beans and asparagus, reheated in a water bath, the cooked cauliflower, French potatoes, green salad and parsley.
11. Pour the clarified butter over the vegetables.
12. Serve the strained sauce, with thick cream added (see recipe 429).
