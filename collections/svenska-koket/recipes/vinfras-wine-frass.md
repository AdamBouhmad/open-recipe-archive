## Ingredients

- 125 gr. loaf sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 15 gr. gelatin (approximately 9 sheets)
- 1 dcl. hot water for the gelatin
- 1 1/2 dcl. French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl. sherry

## Directions

1. Boil a clear syrup from the sugar and water, skim it off and immediately mix with the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice, and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in the hot water and the wine.
2. Stir the mixture well and pour it into a stretched cheesecloth, previously wrung out in hot water, and let it drip through. As long as the jelly runs turbid through, pour it back into the cheesecloth. See further page 452.
3. Pure gooseberry wine can be used to advantage for this jelly. The jelly can be whipped in a large bowl placed on ice until it resembles snow. Then pour into a bowl or use for garnishing clear jellies.
