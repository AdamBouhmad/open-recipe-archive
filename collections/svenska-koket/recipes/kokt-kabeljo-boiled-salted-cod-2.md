## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg salted cod (salt tongue)
- 2 1/2 liters water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish piece into 4 pieces and let them soak in plenty of cold water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Scrape the skin well and place the fish in 2 1/2 liters cold water and let it simmer for about 1 hour.
3. Place on a hot dish with a slotted spoon, garnish with fresh parsley and serve with sauce made from butter, flour, fish stock, 1 raw egg yolk and 2 hard-boiled eggs.
4. Serve with boiled potatoes.
