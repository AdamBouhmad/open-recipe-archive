## Ingredients

- 6 parts rose hips
- 2¼–3 litres water
- 1–1½ cup crushed sugar
- 1⅓ tablespoon potato flour
- 25 grams sweet almond

## Directions

1. Rinse the rose hips and put them in cold water to cook until soft, approximately 2 hours.
2. During cooking, whisk the soup vigorously from time to time.
3. Press it through a sieve, add the sugar, and thicken with potato flour mixed with a little water.
4. Bring to a good boil and taste.
5. Serve with the blanched almond, cut into long strips.
