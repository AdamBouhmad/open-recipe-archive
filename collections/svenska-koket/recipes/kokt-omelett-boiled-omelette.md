## Ingredients

- 5 decilitres milk
- 40 grams butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 60 grams wheat flour
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Bring the milk, butter, and sugar to a boil.
2. Whisk the flour carefully in while at a full boil and let the mass cook until it leaves the sides of the pan.
3. Remove from heat and stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks and pour the mixture into a well-buttered omelette pan.
5. Bake in a warm oven until the omelette has set and turned a beautiful golden-brown colour.
6. Turn out and fill either with finely sliced roast in its sauce or with braised calf's kidney.
