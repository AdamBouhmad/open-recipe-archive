## Ingredients

- 150 gr. "idealmakaroner" (ideal macaroni)
- 1 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 4 hg. rökt skinka (smoked ham)
- 4 ägg (eggs)
- 3 kkp mjölk (milk)
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. socker (sugar)
- 1/2 msk. smör (butter) - 10 gr.
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor (grated breadcrumbs)

## Directions

1. Rinse the macaroni, place in boiling salted water and let cook 15-20 minutes.
2. Drain in a colander and let the water drain well.
3. Cut the ham into small cubes, mix well with the macaroni and place the mixture in a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie form.
4. Whisk the eggs well with the milk and spices, and pour the egg mixture over the preparation in the form.
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
