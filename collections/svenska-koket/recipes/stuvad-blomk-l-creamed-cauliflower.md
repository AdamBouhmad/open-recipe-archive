## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads (1 kg)
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 3 deciliters cream or milk
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 1 egg yolk (optional)
- 2 deciliters cauliflower cooking liquid
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the cauliflower heads as in the previous recipe and place on a sieve to drain.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cooking liquid and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, then finish with the egg yolk if desired.
3. Season the sauce carefully with the seasonings.
4. Place the cauliflower on a warm serving dish and pour the sauce over it. Serve with sausages, ground meat, roasted meat, etc.
