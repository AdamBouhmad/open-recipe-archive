## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal loin
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 large bunch parsley
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.) for filling
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.) for frying
- 1 dcl. broth
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and treat as in the previous recipe and sprinkle the meat slices with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse it, wring it in a cloth, chop it coarsely and work it into the butter.
3. Divide the parsley butter into 12 parts and place one part on each meat slice, which is then rolled up and bound in two places with fine twine.
4. Fry and serve the chickens as in the previous recipe.
