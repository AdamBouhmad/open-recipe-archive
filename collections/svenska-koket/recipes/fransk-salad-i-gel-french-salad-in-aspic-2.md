## Ingredients

- 2 hg. cooked chicken meat (leftovers)
- 1 hg. salted ox tongue
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 3 medium-sized, cooked potatoes
- 2 tomatoes
- 2 small apples
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsp. salt, cayenne
- 2 dcl. fine salad oil
- 1 tbsp. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
- 1 tbsp. boiling water
- 1/2 liter good bouillon
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 1 tsp. vinegar
- 1 egg white and the rinsed shells
- 1 tbsp. cold water

## Directions

1. The chicken meat and tongue are cut into strips.
2. The cucumber, the peeled potatoes, tomatoes and apples are cut into small, even cubes.
3. The mayonnaise is prepared as in no. 10 and left in a cold place for a few hours so that it becomes quite firm.
4. The meat and other ingredients are carefully mixed into the mayonnaise.
5. The aspic is prepared as in no. 10 and allowed to set until half-set.
6. Twelve small timbale molds are glazed with it and placed on ice to set.
7. The salad is filled into the molds and a little aspic is poured over.
8. The molds are placed again on ice until the aspic is firm, then they are turned out and garnished with leftover salad and aspic as well as parsley.
9. Serve to the smorgasbord or as a course.
