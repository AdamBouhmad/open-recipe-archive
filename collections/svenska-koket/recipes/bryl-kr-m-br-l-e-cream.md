## Ingredients

- 125 gr. socker
- 1 1/2 dcl. kokande vatten
- 1/2 vanilj böna
- 15 blad gelatin
- 4 dcl. tjock grädde

## Directions

1. Melt the sugar in a somewhat warm frying pan, buttered with 1/2 teaspoon butter; when it has a light brown color, add 1 deciliter of water, and pour the sugar mixture into a saucepan.
2. Add the vanilla and let it boil, stirring over low heat for about 5 minutes, then remove it.
3. Rinse the gelatin, cut into pieces, dissolve in the remaining water and pour into the sugar mixture, which then is allowed to cool.
4. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the sugar mixture.
5. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let set on ice.
6. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn out onto a pastry dish with pastry paper. Garnish with biscuits.
