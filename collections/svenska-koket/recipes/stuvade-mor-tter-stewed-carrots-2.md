## Ingredients

- 750 gr. carrots
- 1/2 litre water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 4 1/2 - 5 decilitres carrot stock
- 10 gr. cold butter
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse the carrots, brush clean, scrape, rinse again, dry and cut into oblong pieces, which are placed in boiling water, seasoned with salt and sugar, and boiled until soft.
2. Drain in a colander and reserve the stock.
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the carrot stock and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
4. Add the carrots, bring the stew to a boil and season with the cold butter as well as salt, sugar and parsley.
5. Serve with salt meat and fish.
6. Carrots can also be stewed with milk.
