## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon lobster butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 4 cups broth or fish stock
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 large lobster or 0.5 can preserved lobster
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 2-4 tablespoons thick cream
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together, thin with the broth, and add the seasonings.
2. Cut the lobster meat into pieces and add it, and let the sauce come to a good boil.
3. Pass the mass through a sieve and mix with the egg yolks, the beaten egg, cream, and a little cayenne pepper.
4. Pour the mass into small, buttered pastry molds and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1.5 hours.
5. Turn the pastries out and serve with fish soups.
