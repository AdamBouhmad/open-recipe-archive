## Ingredients

- 30 Baltic herrings
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 3 deciliters warm water

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, scrape away the dark skin, rinse well and wrap in a fish towel.
2. Rub it inside and out with salt and layer it in a pot brushed with butter, sprinkling flour and parsley between each layer.
3. Distribute the remaining butter in flakes between the layers.
4. Finally add the warm water and let the fish simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid for about 10 minutes.
5. Arrange the herring on a plate, whisk up the sauce, taste and adjust seasoning, and pour over.
6. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
