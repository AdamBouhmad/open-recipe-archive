## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb breast
- 2 liters water
- 1.5 tablespoons salt
- a few dill stalks
- For the sauce: 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter meat broth
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1.5 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Break the breast and wash with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Place in boiling water, skim, salt, and add the dill stalks.
3. Let the meat cook slowly with the lid closed until tender, approximately 2 hours.
4. Remove and cut into appropriately sized pieces.
5. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
