## Ingredients

- 750 gr. boneless pork loin
- 1 small msk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 hg. butter
- 2 tsk. finely chopped parsley
- 1 tsk. lemon juice
- French potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth, wring out in hot water, cut into 1 cm. thick slices, which are pounded on both sides and sprinkled with pepper and salt.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the steaks in and fry over high heat and place on a warm serving dish.
3. Whisk out the pan with a little boiling broth or water and pour the sauce over the steaks.
4. On each steak place a small slice of maître d'hôtel butter, which is prepared as in recipe no. 95.
5. Garnish the dish with potatoes cooked in cream and parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
