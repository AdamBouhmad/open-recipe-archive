## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2.5 parts cream
- 1 teaspoon caster sugar
- jam
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks and whites.
2. Whip the egg yolks together with the cream and sugar, then fold in the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the batter at once into a well-buttered omelette pan and bake light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
4. Turn it out at once, spread the jam on half of the omelette, and fold the other half over.
