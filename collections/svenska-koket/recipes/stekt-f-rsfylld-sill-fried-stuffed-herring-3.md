## Ingredients

- 6 fresh herrings (= 2 kg)
- 12 anchovies
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 1/2 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 cup coarse rye flour
- 3 tablespoons lard

## Directions

1. Open the herring along the back, remove the entrails and backbone; rinse the herring afterwards, dry well and salt lightly
2. Clean the anchovies and remove the bones
3. Make a forcemeat from the anchovies, bread, milk and seasonings, with which the herring is stuffed
4. Turn it in the flour, place in a pan greased with lard and fry slowly in the oven or on the stove for 1/2 hour
5. Serve with currant sauce
