## Ingredients

- Chanterelle mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. The light yellow or common chanterelle has finer flavor than the reddish variety
2. Trim the foot slightly, but leave the gills intact
3. Rinse the mushroom well and cut into pieces
4. Blanch in boiling salted water for about 5 minutes (discard the cooking liquid)
5. Let drain on cloth
6. Prepare as braised champignons, or simply fry in butter and season with salt and pepper, then serve with smorgasbord
