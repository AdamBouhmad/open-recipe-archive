## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. water
- 65 gr. refined sugar
- zest of 1 1/2 oranges
- juice of 3 oranges
- 2—3 tablespoon Rhine or French wine
- 2 teaspoons potato flour (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. From the water and sugar, cook a light sugar syrup, which is skimmed well.
2. Wash and dry the oranges. Grate the zest from 1 1/2 orange neatly and mix with the sugar syrup together with the pressed orange juice.
3. Add the wine and bring the juice to a boil, season and dilute with a little water if desired.
4. Thicken the sauce as in recipe 460 and serve both warm and cold.
