## Ingredients

- 2.5 liters veal broth
- 1 celery
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the celery, scrape and cut into small cubes, blanch and place in cold water to preserve its color.
2. Remove and drain, then cook in 0.5 liters of the broth first on very low heat for 0.5 hour, then for another hour on stronger heat, while the broth reduces so the celery becomes glazed.
3. Add the remaining broth, bring the soup to a boil, season and serve.
