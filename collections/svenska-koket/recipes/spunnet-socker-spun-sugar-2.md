## Ingredients

- 250 gr. rock sugar
- 1 1/4 dcl. water
- 8 gr. glucose
- oil for preparation
- caramel coloring (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook rock sugar, water, and glucose to caramel sugar (180°). Cool rapidly by dipping the pan briefly in cold water, then place tilted in a ring of straw.
2. Lightly oil a marble slab or 2-3 baking sheets. Oil a thin stick and place it on the slab under a weight (such as a heavy weight) so it stands above the slab.
3. Using a broken whisk or broad fork, fling the sugar back and forth over the stick until you have the desired amount.
4. Cut the threads with a heated knife and immediately wrap around the dish to be garnished. The sugar can be colored with various caramel colors.
