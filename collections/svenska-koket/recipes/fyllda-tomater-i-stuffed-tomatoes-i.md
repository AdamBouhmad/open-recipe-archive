## Ingredients

- 6 large, beautiful tomatoes
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- salt, white pepper
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes, halve them and remove some of the interior with a teaspoon.
2. Chop the mushrooms finely and fry them in the butter until it is clear, then press them out of the butter with a spoon and mix with the thickest part of the interior.
3. Season the mixture with salt and white pepper and fill the hollowed-out tomatoes, which are placed on a platter, sprinkle with grated cheese and pour a little butter over them.
4. Place the platter in a roasting pan with coarse salt, put in a hot oven, and bake the tomatoes 10-15 minutes, or until the filling is beautifully golden brown.
5. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat.
