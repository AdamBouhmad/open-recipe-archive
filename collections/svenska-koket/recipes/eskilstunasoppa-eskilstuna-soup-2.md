## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. crushed grain
- 2 lit. water
- 1 lit. fresh, sour apples
- 1-1 1/2 kkp crushed sugar
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- 1 kkp gooseberry jam for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the grain, set it on to boil in cold water and let it cook with a tight lid.
2. Rinse the apples, dry them, cut them into wedges, and remove the cores.
3. Add them to the soup when it has cooked 1/2 hour.
