## Ingredients

- 6 tomatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 4 decilitres bouillon
- 1 decilitre cream
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Skin the tomatoes and strain them.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the bouillon, tomato purée and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Bind it with the whisked egg yolk, let it simmer and season with the spices.
