## Ingredients

- 2 large veal tongues
- 3/4 msk. salt
- water
- 1 carrot
- 1 piece celery
- 1 leek
- 1 1/2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. flour
- 2-3 kkp of the cooking liquid
- 1 1/2 msk. chopped capers
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 eggs for breading
- 2 kkp crushed rusks for breading
- 1/4 kg. fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Let the tongues soak in cold water for 1 hour, then scald and scrape well.
2. Place them in boiling water and skim carefully when the liquid boils up.
3. Add salt and vegetables, and let the tongues simmer slowly until tender.
4. When done, remove the skin immediately, and cut the meat into fine cubes.
5. Make a roux with butter and flour, thin with the cooking liquid in which the tongues were cooked, add the tongue and finely cut capers, and continue the preparation of the croquettes according to the method described for sweetbread croquettes.
