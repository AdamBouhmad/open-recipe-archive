## Ingredients

- 1 medium bunch kale
- 6-8 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters bone broth
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the kale and blanch in salted boiling water for about 0.5 hour.
2. Drain the water, rinse the kale with cold water and place on a sieve to drain.
3. Chop it finely together with the flour and place in a pot with a little of the broth.
4. Dilute with the rest of the broth with frequent stirring.
5. Let the kale cook for 0.5 hour and season with salt and white pepper.
6. Serve with meatballs, sausage or poached eggs.
