## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1-1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 liter broth or water
- 1/2 tbsp butter
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour
- meat gravy
- 2 tsp tomato puree

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water
