## Ingredients

- 3 liters lingonberries
- 6 parts water
- 6 liters preserved pears
- 1.8 kg crushed sugar
- 20 grams cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries, rinse them and cook with the water until they fall apart.
2. Pour them into a cheesecloth to drip.
3. Peel the pears, cut a cross into each pear, scrape the stems and cut them down to half length.
4. After peeling, place them in fresh water.
5. Cook the lingonberry juice with the sugar and cinnamon to a thick syrup.
6. Place the pears in and allow to cook very slowly until they are soft and clear.
7. Place them in a crock or jar.
8. If the syrup is not sufficiently thick, cook it down and pour it over the pears.
