## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 300 gr. granulated sugar
- 200 gr. sweet almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- 100 gr. butter
- 4 medium potatoes
- 1 tbsp. washed butter for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan
- 1 tbsp. water for the glaze
- 100 gr. granulated sugar for the glaze
- 2 tsp. lemon juice
- 25 gr. pistachio almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Blanch and grate the sweet almonds, pound them in a mortar together with the washed butter, and blend into the egg mixture.
3. Peel the cooked potatoes, grate them, and stir last into the batter.
4. Pour the batter into a well-buttered and bread-crumb-strewn pan and bake in gentle oven heat for about 1/2 hour.
5. When the cake has cooled, glaze it and sprinkle with blanched and chopped pistachio almonds.
