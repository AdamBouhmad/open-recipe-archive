## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1¼ tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- 1¼ cups crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon clarified crayfish butter
- 2 parts cream

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, rinse and dry well and fillet it.
2. Remove the skin and bones carefully, and rub the fish with vinegar and salt and let it lie for a while.
3. Dry it well with a cloth, brush with melted butter and the beaten egg yolk, and sprinkle on both sides with grated cheese and crushed rusks.
4. Place it in a roasting pan greased with cold butter, pour the melted crayfish butter and cream over it, and roast quickly in a hot oven until it gets a golden-brown color.
5. Baste it often with its drippings during roasting.
6. Serve with mushroom sauce mixed with the sauce in which the fish was roasted.
