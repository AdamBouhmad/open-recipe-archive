## Ingredients

- horseradish (approximately 60 gr. grated)
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 2-3 tsk. sugar
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- vinegar to taste

## Directions

1. Scrape the horseradish, rinse it, let it soak in cold water for 1 hour, dry with a cloth, grate on a grater, weigh and cover, otherwise it will darken.
2. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, add the horseradish immediately and season the sauce with sugar, salt and vinegar.
3. Serve with boiled bream, chicken, veal and lamb.
