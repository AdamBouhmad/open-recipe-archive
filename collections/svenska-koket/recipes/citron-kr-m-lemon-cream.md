## Ingredients

- Zest and juice of 1 small lemon
- 2 dcl. crushed sugar (190 gr.)
- 1 dcl. French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl. water
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it; rub the zest against the sugar, which is then placed in a well-scoured saucepan.
2. Press out the lemon juice and mix it with the sugar.
3. Add the wine, water, and egg yolks, and whisk the mixture until the sugar has melted.
4. Move the saucepan over the heat, and let the cream simmer under vigorous whisking until it is thick and creamy.
5. Pour out the cream and whisk it until it is cold.
6. If desired, add the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
7. Serve in a compote bowl with pastries. Half the batch is sufficient for filling a large cake; the cream should then be omitted.
