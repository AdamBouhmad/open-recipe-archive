## Ingredients

- 560 gr. butter
- 230 gr. powdered sugar
- 30 gr. sweet almond
- 30 gr. bitter almond
- 6 hg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir until it is white and fluffy, then add the sugar, the scalded and ground almond, and the sifted wheat flour.
2. The dough is piped through a pastry bag onto a buttered baking sheet in the form of small wreaths, which are baked a beautiful light golden in a not too hot oven.
