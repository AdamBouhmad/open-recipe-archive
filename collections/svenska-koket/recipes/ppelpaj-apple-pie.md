## Ingredients

- For the pie dough: 2 1/2 dcl. flour (125 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 125 gr. butter
- 3-4 tbsp. cold water
- For the filling: 1 1/2 liters good, mild apples
- 2 tbsp. water
- 2 tbsp. white wine
- 1 1/4 dcl. sugar (108 gr.)
- For the form: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and sugar into a bowl, distribute the butter, which should be firm, in flakes into the flour and work it in with the fingertips until the mixture is even in color and without lumps.
2. Then add the water and work the mixture quickly.
