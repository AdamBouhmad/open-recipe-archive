## Ingredients

- 5 hg. powdered sugar
- 3 1/2 dcl water
- 1 liter juice or purée of fresh raspberries
- juice of 1 lemon (if desired)

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as strawberry ice cream with the same proportions
