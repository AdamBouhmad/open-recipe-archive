## Ingredients

- 400 gr. butter
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 600 gr. wheat flour
- 1 egg
- 7 egg whites for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 1/4 cup pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour on the work table.
2. Distribute the washed butter in flakes and place on top of the flour along with sugar and the whisked egg.
3. Work all ingredients together into a smooth, cohesive dough and set aside to cool.
4. Roll out, not too thin, and cut out small cakes with an oblong, fluted measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with almonds and sugar.
6. Bake in good oven heat.
