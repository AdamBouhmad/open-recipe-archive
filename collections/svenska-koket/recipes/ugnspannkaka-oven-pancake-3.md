## Ingredients

- 2-3 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- approximately 160-180 gr. flour
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Prepare the pancake batter and let it swell for 2 hours.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and grease the pan with it.
3. Stir the batter well and pour one-third of the batter into the pan.
