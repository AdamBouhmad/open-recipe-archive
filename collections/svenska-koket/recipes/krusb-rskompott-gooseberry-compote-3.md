## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters granulated sugar (255 g)
- 1 deciliter water
- 1 liter half-ripe, green, thin-skinned gooseberries (about 500 g)

## Directions

1. Cook a clear, syrupy syrup from the sugar and water
2. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse well, and drain on cloth
3. Place in the sugar syrup and cook until they begin to crack but do not cook to pieces
4. Arrange gooseberries with a slotted spoon in a compote bowl
5. Cook the syrup until it is syrupy and pour over the berries
6. Serve cold with whipped cream
