## Ingredients

- 6 large pork chops
- 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 2 deciliter finely crushed bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 2 1/2 tablespoon butter for frying (50 gr.)
- 2 deciliter broth

## Directions

1. If the chops are small, one should calculate 2 per person for a meal.
2. The chop rack is dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water, cut so that a bone follows with each chop, which is then lightly pounded, trimmed and shaped.
3. The chops are sprinkled with the lemon juice, rubbed with the spices and left to rest on each other for 1/4 hour, after which they are brushed with the butter and turned in a mixture of the crushed bread crumbs and parsley.
4. The butter is browned in a frying pan, the chops are placed in and fried slowly without a cover about 7 minutes on each side.
