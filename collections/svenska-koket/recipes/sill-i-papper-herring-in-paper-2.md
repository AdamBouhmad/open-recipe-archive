## Ingredients

- 4 small salted herrings
- 80 gr. butter
- 2 sheets white writing paper
- 3 hectograms (300 gr.) bouillon for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times. Skin, clean, rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
2. Remove head and tail and also the backbone if desired. Divide the paper sheets, cut into ovals and brush with butter on as large an area as the herring occupies.
3. Place the herring on the paper, fold the edges tightly over each other, then place the herring in a frying pan with hot bouillon and fry for approximately 5 minutes.
4. Place the pan in a warm oven until the paper puffs up like a balloon. Serve with boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
