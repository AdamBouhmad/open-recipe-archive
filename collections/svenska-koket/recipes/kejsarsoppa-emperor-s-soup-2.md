## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 g)
- 1 1/2 liters clear broth
- salt
- cayenne
- 1/2 deciliter sherry or madeira
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling broth and let it cook with stirring for 5 minutes, then season with spices and wine.
2. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl and pour the soup in with vigorous whisking. Serve very hot with egg balls.
