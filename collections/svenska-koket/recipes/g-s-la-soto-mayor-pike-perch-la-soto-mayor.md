## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pike-perch
- 2 tsk salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk white pepper
- 2 tsk finely chopped red onion
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 msk cooking oil
- 1 1/2 dcl milk
- 1/2 tsk salt (3 gr.)
- 3 msk wheat flour (30 gr.)
- 2 hg puff pastry

## Directions

1. Scale the pike-perch, clean as described on page 171, remove the backbone but keep the head and tail.
2. Cut off the gills and fins and rub the fish in with salt and white pepper.
3. Rub with finely chopped red onion, lemon juice and cooking oil.
