## Ingredients

- 3 parts milk
- 1/2 hectogram unsweetened chocolate (cocoa powder)
- 1 1/2 coffee cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 6 leaves gelatin

## Directions

1. Pour the milk, chocolate, sugar and eggs into a saucepan and simmer with vigorous whisking over heat.
2. Remove from heat but continue whisking until the cream cools.
3. Add the gelatin dissolved in a little warm water and the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a mold rinsed with cold water and sprinkled with sugar, and let it set in a cold place for about 2 1/2 hours.
5. When the cream is to be turned out, hold the mold briefly in warm water.
6. Serve with whipped cream.
