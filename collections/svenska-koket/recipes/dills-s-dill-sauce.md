## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 liter meat stock
- 2 tbsp chopped dill
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 1/2 tbsp vinegar
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour, add the meat stock, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
2. Add the chopped dill, and season the sauce with sugar and vinegar and salt if needed.
3. Finally finish it with an egg yolk, after which the sauce must not boil.
