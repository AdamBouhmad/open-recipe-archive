## Ingredients

- 1 hg. flour
- 3/4 dcl. water (scant)
- 150 gr. butter
- leftover cooked fish (about 3 dcl.)
- 1 can lobster (1/4 lit.)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 dcl. fish stock
- 2 dcl. milk
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp. water

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry dough as in no. 34, roll it out thinly, cut it with a round measure into cakes, with which smooth pastry forms are lined. The dough can be baked first in the same way as for tartlets, see no. 41, but the ragout can also be filled directly into the lined forms.
2. Cut the fish and lobster into dice. Sauté the butter and flour for 2 minutes, then add the fish stock and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes. Gently stir in the fish and lobster together with the beaten egg yolk. Let the ragout simmer, season with the spices and when it has cooled somewhat, fill it into the lined forms. Cut the remaining dough into strips, which are laid in a lattice pattern over the top.
