## Ingredients

- 1 dozen oysters
- 5 dcl. light broth
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tbsp. thick cream
- salt, white pepper
- sherry, lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the oysters, the beard and the water from the oysters are poured into a pan together with the broth, which is allowed to boil for 10–15 minutes; strain and should yield 5 dcl.
2. Blanch the oysters in lightly salted water for 2–3 minutes
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, dilute with the broth and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes
4. Add the egg yolks whipped with cream and let the sauce simmer
5. Add the blanched and cleaned oysters and season with the seasonings, sherry and lemon juice and serve with fish dishes, chicken or veal
