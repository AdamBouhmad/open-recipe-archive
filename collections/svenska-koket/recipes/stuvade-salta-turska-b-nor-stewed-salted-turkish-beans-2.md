## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. salted beans
- 8 deciliters water
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters bean cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters cream or milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the beans in a colander, rinse with cold water and let them soak in plenty of water for approximately 12 hours.
2. Rinse them again, place in cold water and let them simmer slowly until they are completely soft, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. Pour off the cooking liquid and reserve it.
4. For the sauce, melt butter with flour, add the cooking liquid and cream, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
5. Mix in the egg yolk and let it simmer, then add the beans and season the stew with sugar.
6. Serve with boiled sausage, salted meat, etc.
