## Ingredients

- Base cream recipe
- 1 large vanilla pod
- 2 deciliters thick cream, beaten to stiff peaks (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with 1 large vanilla pod.
2. Let the vanilla pod steep in the cream for about 20 minutes.
3. Prepare the cream according to the base recipe description.
4. When cold, if desired, fold in 2 deciliters of thick cream beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Freeze as in recipe no. 929.
6. Serve with ice cream bread.
