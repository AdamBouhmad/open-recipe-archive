## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter or 2 tablespoons crayfish butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter thin cream
- 2 hectograms crayfish tails
- sugar
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are cooked together
2. Cream is added
3. The sauce is allowed to boil for 10 minutes
4. The crayfish tails are added
5. The sauce is seasoned with sugar and salt
6. Finally it is finished with the egg yolk, after which it must not boil
7. If salted crayfish tails are used, they must first be well soaked
