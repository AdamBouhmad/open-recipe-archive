## Ingredients

- 250 grams caster or granulated sugar
- 3 decilitres water
- 12 good, evenly-sized apples
- 25 grams sweet almond

## Directions

1. A clear syrup is made from the sugar and water and allowed to cool.
2. The apples are washed, dried, peeled, and the cores are removed with a tube.
3. The apples are placed directly in the sugar syrup and allowed to boil until they are soft and clear, then they are removed with a slotted spoon and arranged in a pyramid on a gelatin mold.
4. If desired, the holes left by the cores can be filled with apple or red currant jelly.
5. The almond is blanched, cut into fine dice, lightly toasted in the oven and sprinkled over the pyramid.
6. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream. If the pyramid is served cold, it can be, if desired, garnished with 3 decilitres of cream whipped to stiff peaks.
