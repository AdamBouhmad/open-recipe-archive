## Ingredients

- 18 Baltic herrings
- 1/2 tbsp. salt
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 1/2 tsp. French mustard
- 2 tbsp. vegetable oil
- 1 dcl. vinegar
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped dill

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, rinse well, skin, remove the insides, and scrape away the dark membrane carefully.
2. Rinse the fillets, wrap in a fish towel and lay in a shallow dish alternating with the spices.
3. Stir the mustard with the oil and vinegar and pour this marinade over the herring.
4. Sprinkle the dill over and let the herring stand in a cold place for 1 hour.
5. It is then cured. Serve to the smorgasbord.
