## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters fish stock
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as in the previous recipe.
2. Cut the lobster into small pieces; after it is added, the sauce should be stirred very carefully so that the lobster does not fall apart.
3. If fresh lobster is used, allow one lobster for six persons.
4. From the shell, lobster butter is prepared and the sauce is cooked with lobster stock.
