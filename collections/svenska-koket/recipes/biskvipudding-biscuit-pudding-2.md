## Ingredients

- 75 gr. biscuits
- 4 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. boiling water
- 4 dcl. extra thick cream
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 6 ränklor (compote) for garnish
- 2 tbsp. red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Dry the biscuits in a warm oven and crush them, not too finely, in a mortar. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the water. Whip the cream with 1 tbsp. of the sugar to stiff peaks, and gradually fold in the cooled gelatin, the remaining sugar, vanilla sugar and the crushed biscuits. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass plate with parchment paper. Garnish with fruit and jelly.
