## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 185 gr. sugar
- 2 hg. melted butter
- 115 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. sweet almond for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almond, cut it into thin slices lengthwise and lightly toast in the oven.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the butter and flour.
3. Pour the batter into a small long pan that is well cleaned and buttered with cold butter.
4. The batter should reach 1 cm. high in the pan.
5. Sprinkle the almond evenly over the cake, which is baked in a not too hot oven until it gets a nice light brown color on both sides.
6. Turn the cake out when it has cooled and cut it into about 6 cm. long and 2 cm. wide pieces.
7. Serve fresh with coffee or tea.
