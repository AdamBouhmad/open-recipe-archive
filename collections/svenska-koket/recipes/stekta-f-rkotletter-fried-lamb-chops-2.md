## Ingredients

- 12 lamb chops
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1 1/2 deciliter meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. The lamb chops should be cut with long bones and be approximately 2 cm thick.
2. Dry them with a cloth wrung out in hot water, clean them, scrape the bones and beat the chops on both sides, then rub with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, place the chops in and fry 5–8 minutes on each side.
4. Place them on a warm serving plate, whisk the frying pan with the boiling meat stock and pour the sauce over the chops.
5. Serve with fried potatoes, mashed potatoes or vegetables.
6. The chops can also be breaded and fried in the same way.
