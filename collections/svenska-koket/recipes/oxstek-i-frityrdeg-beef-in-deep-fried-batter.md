## Ingredients

- 6 hg. boneless, fried beef
- 80 gr. dry flour
- 3 gr. salt
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 1—1 1/2 dcl. cream
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Slice the beef across into beautiful even slices.
2. For the batter, beat all ingredients together into a thick batter and let it stand and swell for about 2 hours.
3. Dip the beef slices in the batter, which should completely cover them.
4. Place them immediately in hot fat and fry beautifully golden brown.
5. Allow them to drain on absorbent paper.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with browned butter and vegetables and salads.
