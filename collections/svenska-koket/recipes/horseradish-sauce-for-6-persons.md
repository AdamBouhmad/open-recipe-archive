## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat or fish stock or 6 dcl. good milk
- 2-3 tbsp. grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Sauté 1 tbsp. of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the stock or milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the horseradish along with the remaining butter and season the sauce with spices.
3. Scrape, rinse and soak the horseradish in cold water for 1 hour, dry with a cloth and grate on a grater.
