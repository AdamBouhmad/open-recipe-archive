## Ingredients

- 5 tbsp. oat flour (50 gr.)
- 1/4 lit. water
- 1 3/4 lit. milk
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the flour with water and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Bring the milk to a boil, pour in the flour mixture while whisking, and let the porridge boil while continuing to whisk for 15 minutes.
3. Season with salt and sugar and serve as a breakfast or evening dish.
