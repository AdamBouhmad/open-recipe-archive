## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef
- 1 kg pork meat
- 1.5 kg lard
- 150 grams salt
- 18 grams ground strong pepper
- 1 small tablespoon potassium nitrate
- 1 generous tablespoon powdered sugar
- 2 parts cognac
- 4 meters straight casings (ox casing)
- 8 tablespoons salt for rubbing
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar for rubbing
- 2 teaspoons potassium nitrate for rubbing

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut it into pieces.
2. Remove sinews and membranes, and grind the meat 5 times through a meat grinder.
3. Cut the lard into fine, even cubes.
4. Work the ground meat by itself for 0.5 hours.
5. Then mix in the finely ground seasonings, sugar, saltpeter (likewise finely ground), cognac, and pork cubes.
6. Work the mixture for another 0.5 hours.
7. Stuff the farce as tightly as possible into straight ox casings with the tallow side turned out.
8. Tie off the finished sausages, rub them with salt, sugar, and saltpeter, and let them lie for 8 days.
9. Turn them morning and evening and rub them again with salt, sugar, and saltpeter each time.
10. Then smoke the sausages for 3 days.
11. Store them hanging in a cool place or packed in juniper.
