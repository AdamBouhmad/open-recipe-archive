## Ingredients

- 30 strömmingar (Baltic herring)
- 1 1/2 msk. salt
- 75 gr. mjöl (flour)
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1 dcl. ljumt vatten (lukewarm water)
- 10 gr. smält smör (melted butter)
- 1 äggvita (egg white)
- 1/2 kg. flottyr (lard for frying)

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring as in recipe no. 163.
2. For the batter: dry the flour well in the oven and sift with salt in a bowl.
3. Add the water and melted butter, stirring the batter as little as possible so it does not become tough.
4. Just before use, fold in the stiffly beaten egg white.
5. Wrap the herring in a fish cloth and roll them up with the meat side out.
6. Dip in the batter and cook in hot lard until brown and cooked through, which is tested with a whisk.
7. Place on gray paper to drain.
8. Place on a warm serving dish, garnish with parsley cooked in lard, and serve with cooked mayonnaise as breakfast.
