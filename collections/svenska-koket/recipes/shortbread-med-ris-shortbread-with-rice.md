## Ingredients

- 120 gr. butter
- 60 gr. caster sugar
- 70 gr. rice flour
- 130 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Blend the sifted flour and sugar well together.
2. Knead in the butter, and work everything together into a smooth dough.
3. Roll this out quite thinly, prick it, and cut out small triangular cookies with a fork, which are baked a beautiful golden brown in weak oven heat.
