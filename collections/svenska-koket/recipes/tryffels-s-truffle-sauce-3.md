## Ingredients

- 1 can truffle (1/8 liter) truffle broth
- 1 deciliter Madeira or sherry
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch (15 grams)
- 5 deciliters strong, brown meat stock
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut the truffle into small cubes and cook in the broth and wine, covered, for approximately 10 minutes.
2. Mix the cornstarch with 1/2 deciliter of the meat stock.
3. Add the remaining meat stock to the mushroom and bring to boil.
4. Thicken with the cornstarch mixture.
5. Let the sauce cook, stirring, for 5 minutes, and season with the spices.
6. The sauce may also be thickened with butter and flour as in recipe no. 428.
