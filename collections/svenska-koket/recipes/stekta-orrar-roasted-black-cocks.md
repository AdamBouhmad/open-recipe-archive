## Ingredients

- 2 black cocks
- 1 hg. lard
- 3—4 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt
- 3 dcl. bouillon
- 5 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The female black cock has lighter meat and is considered finer than the male.
2. They are treated and prepared as black grouse but are roasted in 1—1 1/2 hours.
3. The larding can advantageously be omitted, and the birds are instead covered with lard slices when they are trussed.
4. Carve, garnish (see page 300) and serve with poured sauce, mixed with thick cream (see no. 429).
