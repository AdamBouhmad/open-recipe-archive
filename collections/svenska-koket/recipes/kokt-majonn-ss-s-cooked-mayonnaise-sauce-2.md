## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter
- 4 tbsp. flour
- 1/2 tbsp. mustard
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 parts stock or fish stock
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tbsp. fine olive oil
- 3 tbsp. vinegar
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The butter, flour, mustard and white pepper are fried together in a saucepan, the stock is thinned in, and the sauce is allowed to simmer until it is thick as thin porridge.
2. When it is cooked, it is strained through a sieve and stirred until it has cooled.
3. Then the sugar and egg yolks are added one at a time, as well as the oil and vinegar drop by drop under constant stirring.
4. Finally the cream, whipped to stiff peaks, is folded in.
