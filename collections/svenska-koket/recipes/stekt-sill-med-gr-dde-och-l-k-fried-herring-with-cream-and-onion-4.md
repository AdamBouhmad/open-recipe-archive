## Ingredients

- 2 large salt herrings
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 deciliters thick cream
- 1 large Portuguese onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g) for garnish

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in plenty of water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Remove the skin, fillet the herring, remove the backbone and all small bones, and wrap the herring in a fish towel.
3. Coat the fillets in a mixture of flour and crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Fry them a beautiful golden brown on both sides and place on a warm serving dish.
5. Deglaze the pan with cream and let the sauce come to a boil.
6. Peel the onion, cut it into thin slices and brown it well in butter in a separate pan.
7. Place the herring on a warm serving dish, pour the sauce over it and garnish with the browned onion and parsley.
8. Serve as breakfast with freshly boiled, unpeeled potatoes.
