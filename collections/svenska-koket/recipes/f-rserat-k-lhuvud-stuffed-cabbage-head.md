## Ingredients

- 1 large cabbage head
- Water and salt for cooking
- 300 grams boneless beef
- 200 grams boneless veal or pork
- 50 grams lard
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 deciliter crushed breadcrumbs
- 4-5 deciliters cream or milk
- 100 grams suet
- 100 grams lard in one large slice
- 1 liter bouillon

## Directions

1. Place the cabbage head in boiling water and let it cook until soft.
2. Remove it immediately and let it drain on a cloth.
3. Remove the outer green leaves and hollow out the head from the top.
4. Prepare the meat mixture as in recipe no. 637 and it should be fairly loose.
5. Fill the cabbage head with the meat mixture (but not completely full, as the meat will rise during cooking), cover it with a few large cabbage leaves and tie it up.
