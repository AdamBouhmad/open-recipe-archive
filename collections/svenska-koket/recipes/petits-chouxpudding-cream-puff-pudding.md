## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thin cream
- 4 eggs
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 dcl. sugar for the mold
- 2 tbsp. boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a buttered, slightly warm frying pan, brown lightly, then add the boiling water, and with this coat a fireproof mold.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, thin with the cream, and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes, then pour it out and let it cool.
3. Stir in the egg yolks one by one together with the sugar, the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
4. Finally, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into the coated mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
6. Serve the pudding immediately when ready with vanilla, lemon, or fruit sauce. — If desired, the pudding can be turned out.
