## Ingredients

- 500 g boned roasted meat of beef, veal or lamb
- 800 ml broth or water
- 1 red onion
- 40 g butter (2 tbsp)
- 40 g flour (4 tbsp)
- 500 ml of the broth
- 2 tbsp finely chopped pickled cucumber
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones into even, attractive pieces.
2. Crush the bones and cook as in the preceding recipe.
3. Peel the onion, chop finely and brown lightly in the butter.
4. Fry the flour with the butter and onion for 2 minutes; add the strained broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add the meat and pickled cucumber and warm in the sauce but do not boil.
6. Season the dish with spices, place on a hot platter and garnish with fried potatoes.
