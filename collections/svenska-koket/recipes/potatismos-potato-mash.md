## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 2-2 1/2 deciliters boiling milk

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them, peel thinly and cook.
2. Drain the water well and press the potatoes through a puree press or mash with a fork until mashed.
3. Add the cold butter immediately while stirring vigorously, and then add the boiling milk, a little at a time.
4. Season the mash with salt and a little sugar if desired.
5. Potato mash should be served immediately after it is prepared, as it becomes sticky and gluey if it stands too long.
