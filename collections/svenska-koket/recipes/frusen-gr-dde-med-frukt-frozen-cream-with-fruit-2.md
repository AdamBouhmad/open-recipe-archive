## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter thick cream
- 2 cups raspberry preserve or other preserve

## Directions

1. The cream is whisked to stiff peaks and is then blended with the preserve.
2. It is then poured into a freezing mold and frozen, though not too hard.
3. The frozen cream is packed in a freezing mold, which is placed on ice until it is to be served, when it is turned out and garnished with fruit.
