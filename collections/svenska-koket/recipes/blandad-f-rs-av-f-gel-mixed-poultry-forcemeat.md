## Ingredients

- I. Chicken forcemeat: 1 hen, 100 gr. butter, 2 eggs, 1 part cream, twelve-öre wheat bread for panading, 2 parts boiling milk, salt, white pepper, 1.75 glas truffle at 1.50 kr. by weight against the forcemeat
- II. Game forcemeat: 3 partridges, 100 gr. butter, 2 eggs, 1 part cream, twelve-öre wheat bread for panading, 2 parts boiling milk, salt, white pepper, 0.25 glas truffle at 1.50 kr. half the weight against the forcemeat
- Sauce: 1 tbsp. butter, 2 tbsp. flour, game stock, 1 part thick cream, 0.5 glas truffle at 1.50 kr.

## Directions

1. Pluck the hen, clean it and rinse it well. Loosen the meat from the bones (cook stock on the carcass), cut it into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder.
2. Prepare the panade as in the previous recipe and pass it through a sieve.
3. Pound the meat and butter well together in a stone mortar and pass through a fine sieve.
4. Gradually blend the whisked egg yolks, cream and seasonings into the forcemeat, which is worked for 1 hour. Then add the finely cut truffle and egg whites beaten until stiff.
5. If the forcemeat is too stiff, add a little cream, otherwise an egg yolk.
6. Prepare the game forcemeat in the same way.
7. Butter a fine pipe or à-la-daube mold and coat it with bread. In the mold, arrange alternating layers of the light chicken forcemeat.
