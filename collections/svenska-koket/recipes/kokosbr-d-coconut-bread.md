## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 1/2 kilogram grated coconut
- 2 1/2 cups granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Separate egg yolks and whites
2. Stir the egg yolks and 1 egg white with the sugar to a foam
3. Beat the remaining egg whites to stiff peaks and blend into the batter together with the grated coconut
4. Form the mass into small round cakes, and bake these on well-buttered baking sheets in very weak oven heat
