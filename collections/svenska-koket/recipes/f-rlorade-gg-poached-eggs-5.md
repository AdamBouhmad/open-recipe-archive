## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Fill a well-cleaned frying pan with water to 2 cm height; add ½ teaspoon salt and ½ teaspoon vinegar.
2. When the water boils, carefully break one egg at a time into a cup and let it slide slowly into the water, which should only simmer gently.
3. Once the white has set, remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and plunge quickly into cold water to remove the foam.
4. Trim with a drinking glass and serve as a breakfast dish on toasted bread slices, or as garnish for other dishes.
