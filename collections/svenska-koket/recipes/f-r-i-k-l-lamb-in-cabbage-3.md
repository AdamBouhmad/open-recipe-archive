## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg lamb breast or saddle
- 1 small head of cabbage
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoon flour
- 2 1/2 liters cold water

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into pieces.
2. Brown it lightly together with the coarsely cut cabbage.
3. Sprinkle flour over, add salt and pepper, and mix everything well.
4. Add the cold water, and let the soup simmer slowly for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
5. Carefully skim off all the fat, taste the dish, and serve.
