## Ingredients

- Chanterelles
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the chanterelles lightly, cutting the base slightly but leaving the slices intact
2. Rinse well and cut into pieces
3. Blanch in boiling salted water for about 5 minutes (do not use the cooking liquid)
4. Drain on cloth and prepare as stewed mushrooms
5. Alternatively, fry only in butter, season with salt and pepper, and serve at the smorgasbord
