## Ingredients

- 0.5 liters thick cream
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 hg. biscuits
- 5 sheets gelatin
- redcurrant jelly and biscuits for garnish

## Directions

1. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks, and fold in the gelatin, rinsed and dissolved in a little lukewarm water, and the crushed biscuits.
2. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
3. When it is to be served, dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water and turn out the pudding.
4. Garnish with biscuits and redcurrant jelly.
