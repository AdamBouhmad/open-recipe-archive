## Ingredients

- 6 large veal cutlets
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 msk. clarified butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 msk. Parmesan cheese
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 150 gr. good macaroni
- 2 lit. water
- 1 1/2 msk. salt (23 gr.)
- 2 dcl. tomato purée or 30 gr. melted butter

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as in no. 514, season with spices, dip in egg yolks whisked with butter, turn in a mixture of breadcrumbs and cheese and fry as in no. 514.
2. Break the macaroni into 3 cm. long pieces, rinse in cold water, place in boiling salted water and let simmer 15–20 minutes.
3. Drain in a colander, let drain and mix with the tomato purée (which has been warmed in a water bath) or with the melted butter.
4. Place the macaroni in the center of a warm serving platter, the cutlets in a ring around and garnish the dish with parsley.
