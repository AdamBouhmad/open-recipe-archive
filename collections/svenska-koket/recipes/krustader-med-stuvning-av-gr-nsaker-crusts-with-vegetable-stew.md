## Ingredients

- 12-14 crusts
- 1 bunch tender carrots
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 6 wax beans
- 1 1/2 cup fresh peas
- 12 asparagus tips
- 8 deciliters water
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1/2 liter vegetable stock
- 1/2 teaspoon meat extract
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons thick cream

## Directions

1. The crusts are prepared as in no. 46.
2. The vegetables are cleaned, placed in boiling water, added with sugar, and allowed to cook until soft.
3. Pour them into a colander and reserve the stock.
4. Divide the cauliflower into small pieces; cut the other vegetables nicely with a vegetable knife.
5. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes; stir in the vegetable stock, mixed with meat extract and salt, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
6. Finish it with the whipped egg yolk and cream, let it simmer and taste it with salt.
7. Carefully mix the vegetables with the sauce and fill the stew into the warm crusts.
8. Serve as an intermediate course or as a garnish to smoked salmon, fried fish, etc.
