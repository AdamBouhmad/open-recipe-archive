## Ingredients

- 3 sole, approximately 4 hg. each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 4 hg. pike for forcemeat
- 150 gr. scraped fish meat
- 75 gr. butter
- 10 gr. flour
- 1 1/2–2 dcl. cream
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 truffle
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.) for cooking
- fish stock
- hollandaise or crawfish sauce for garnish
- 2 slices truffle
- parsley
- 12 crawfish or shrimp tails

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the sole as in recipe no. 371.
2. Flatten the fillets with a moistened meat mallet and rub with salt; let them lie on top of each other for a couple of hours.
3. Prepare the fish forcemeat as in recipe no. 356 and stir in the truffle cut into fine strips.
4. Dry the fillets again with a fish towel, trim them, and cover the inner side with a thin layer of fish forcemeat.
5. Roll them up with the forcemeat inside and place them close together in a low saucepan with a flat bottom, buttered with the cold butter.
6. Smooth the forcemeat that protrudes at the upper end with a spoon dipped in warm water.
7. Add enough fish stock (cooked from head, bones, and skin) so it just covers the rolls.
8. Cover the saucepan and place in a regular oven.
