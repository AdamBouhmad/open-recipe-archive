## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 1 tablespoon oil

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 12 hours. Skin it if desired, clean it, rinse it well and dry it with a fish towel.
2. Brush it with the oil, place it in a double grilling rack that can be turned, and grill it over coals on both sides.
3. Place on a warm plate, garnish with parsley and serve with boiled unpeeled potatoes or with unpeeled potatoes fried in the oven as a breakfast dish.
