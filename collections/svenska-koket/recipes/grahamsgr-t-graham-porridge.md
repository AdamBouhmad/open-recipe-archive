## Ingredients

- 1.3 liters water
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 3 dcl. graham flour (165 gr.)

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with the salt, whisk in the flour, and let the porridge thereafter cook slowly with a lid for about 3/4 hour.
2. Season with more salt if desired, and serve with cream or milk.
