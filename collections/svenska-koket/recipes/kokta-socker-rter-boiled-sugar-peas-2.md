## Ingredients

- 2 liters large, beautiful sugar peas
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 2 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. The peas are cleaned and rinsed well, and allowed to drain on cloth.
2. They are placed in boiling water, seasoned with salt and sugar, and allowed to simmer slowly until completely tender.
3. The liquid is strained off and the peas are carefully mixed with the cold butter and parsley.
4. Served with ham and roasted meat.
