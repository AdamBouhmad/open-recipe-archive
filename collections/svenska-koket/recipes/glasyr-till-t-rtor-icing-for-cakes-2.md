## Ingredients

- Water icing: 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons water, 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- Wine icing: 1 cup powdered sugar, 3 tablespoons wine

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with water, flavored with a little lemon juice or with wine, and stir the mixture until the sugar has melted.
2. Spread this icing over the cake with a knife.
3. Garnish the cake only after the icing has dried.
4. If you want white icing, use finely sifted sugar instead of powdered sugar.
