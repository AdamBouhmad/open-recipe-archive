## Ingredients

- 250 g lump sugar
- 3 parts water
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 10 oranges (of which 3 blood oranges)
- 2 oranges for garnish

## Directions

1. Make a clear and thick syrup from the sugar and water.
2. Stir in the lemon and orange juice, and set the mixture out to cool.
3. When completely cold, pour it into an ice cream machine or ice cream mold and freeze, but not too hard.
4. Pack it in an ice cream form, which in turn is packed in ice.
5. Turn out and garnish with orange wedges.
