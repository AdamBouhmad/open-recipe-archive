## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fatty herrings
- 3 anchovies
- 40 g butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 pinch cayenne
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped chives
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, remove it, rinse, cut into fillets and remove the backbone and all small bones.
2. Place the herring in lukewarm milk and let it rest for about 12 hours.
3. Remove the fillets, wrap well in a cloth or clean white paper, and cut into beautiful, oblong even pieces.
4. Place any trimmed pieces (should equal 1 fillet) in a mortar and pound together with the cleaned and boned anchovies and butter.
5. Pass the mixture through a sieve and blend well with the egg yolk, a pinch of cayenne, parsley and chives.
6. Spread the farce thinly on each herring piece and roll up into small rolls.
7. Butter a fireproof mold with cold butter and arrange the rolls tightly in it.
8. Sprinkle with crushed rusks and pour the clarified remaining butter over.
9. Place the mold in a warm oven for 10-15 minutes.
10. Serve immediately as breakfast with freshly boiled potatoes.
