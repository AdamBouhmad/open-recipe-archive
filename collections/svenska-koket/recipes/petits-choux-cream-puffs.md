## Ingredients

- 3 decilitres water
- 100 gr. butter
- 2 tsp. granulated sugar
- 100 gr. wheat flour
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Bring water, butter, and sugar to a boil, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook until it leaves the pan.
2. Once most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg yolks one by one.
3. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. On well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets, place large spoonfuls of the batter.
5. Bake at good oven heat, taking care not to open the oven door before 10 minutes have passed.
6. Once the pastries have cooled, cut a lid into them and fill with cream or whipped cream.
