## Ingredients

- 4 to 5 cups sweet or sour juice
- 1 liter water
- 4 to 5 cups lemon juice with 2 cups sugar
- 4 to 5 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Boil the juice and water (and sugar), and skim.
2. Stir the potato flour in a little cold water and add it, then bring the cream to a short boil.
3. Taste for seasoning and pour into a jelly mold or carrot mold to cool.
