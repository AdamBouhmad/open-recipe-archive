## Ingredients

- 3 hg. washed butter
- 150 gr. sugar
- 3 hg. rye sifted flour or coarse rye flour
- 3 hg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Stir the butter for 10 minutes
2. Add the sugar and flour, which should be sifted (2 dcl. of the wheat flour is set aside for dusting)
3. Roll out the dough to 3 mm thickness, prick well and cut out with a round mold into small cookies with a hole on one side
4. Place on buttered baking sheet and bake until golden brown in a not-too-hot oven
