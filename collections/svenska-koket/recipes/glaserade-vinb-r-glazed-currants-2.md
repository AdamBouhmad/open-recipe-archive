## Ingredients

- 1 liter large, beautiful bunches of red or white currants
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (128 g)

## Directions

1. Place the bunches in a colander, pour cold water over them and let them drain on cloth.
2. Whip the egg whites until light foam forms.
3. Dip the currant bunches first in the egg white, then in the sugar, until they are evenly glazed.
4. Arrange on a sieve and let stand in gentle oven heat until they are dry.
5. Serve as dessert or for a buffet.
