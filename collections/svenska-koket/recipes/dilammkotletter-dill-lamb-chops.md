## Ingredients

- 9 chops
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare, arrange and serve the chops as in the previous recipe but do not fry for more than 3 minutes on each side.
