## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 1 dl. water or milk
- 60 gr. flour
- 25 gr. grated cheese
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 kg. broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Boil the butter and water together, add the flour with steady whisking and let the mixture boil until it leaves the pan, then pour it out and press the whisk well.
2. When the mixture has cooled, add the grated cheese and work in the eggs one at a time.
3. Stir the mixture for 10 minutes, then shape it with two teaspoons, which are dipped in broth, into small balls the size of a hazelnut.
4. Cook these to a golden-brown color in the broth and place on brown paper to drain.
5. The broth must not be too hot, as the balls will not then puff up.
6. Serve lukewarm, arranged on cake paper, with broth.
