## Ingredients

- 100 grams sweet almond
- 4 grams bitter almond
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- 6 parts water
- 6 parts thick cream
- 12 grams gelatin
- zest of 1 orange
- juice of 2 oranges
- carmine
- spinach green

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and grate them.
2. Make a syrup from the sugar and water, in which the almond is allowed to boil 10 minutes.
3. To further draw out the almond flavor, let the syrup stand beside the fire for another 20 minutes, then strain it.
4. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks, add the sugar syrup, and finally the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water.
5. Divide the mixture into three equal parts.
6. Flavor one part with the zest and juice of the oranges; color the second part with carmine and the third with spinach green.
7. Rinse a pretty mold with cold water and sprinkle with sugar.
8. Place the green mixture on the bottom and set it out to stiffen.
9. On top of this place the white mixture, and when it has set, the red mixture.
10. When the blanc-mangé is to be served, dip the mold in lukewarm water and turn out the pudding.
