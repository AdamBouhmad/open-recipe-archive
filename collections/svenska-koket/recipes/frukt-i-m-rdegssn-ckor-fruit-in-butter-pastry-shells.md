## Ingredients

- 40 gr. sweet almond
- 3 bitter almonds
- 105 gr. fine butter
- 65 gr. sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 85 gr. flour
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the shells
- 1 egg white for garnish
- 40 gr. powdered sugar for garnish
- 15 pistachio almonds for garnish
- compote of apples, pears or cloudberries for filling
- vanilla sauce for filling

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and grind them through an almond mill.
2. Wash the butter and work it in a bowl together with the almond, sugar, egg yolk and flour into a smooth dough, which is allowed to rest for 2–3 hours in a cool place.
3. Grease small shells on the outside with a little butter.
4. Roll out the dough to 3 mm. thickness and cut out with a large round cutter for cakes, with which the shells are lined on the outside.
5. Place the shells, with the dough side up, on a baking sheet and bake in regular oven heat until nicely light brown, then remove them, let them cool and turn them out.
6. Beat the egg white to stiff peaks, dust with powdered sugar and place pistachios on top.
7. Fill the shells with fruit compote and vanilla sauce.
