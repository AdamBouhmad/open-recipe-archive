## Ingredients

- 1 kg oranges
- 0.75-1 kg powdered sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Dry the oranges with a damp cloth and peel them.
2. Remove all the white from the peels.
3. Cut them into fine strips and cook with a little water for 10 minutes.
4. Strain off the water, pour on new cold water, and let the peels cook another 10 minutes in this.
5. Strain off the water, and pour on new water a third time, so much that it stands level with the peels.
6. Cook the peels in this until they become soft.
7. Cut the oranges into wedges, remove all seeds and membranes, and place the fruit flesh with the peels together with the sugar.
8. Let the marmalade cook until it is clear and fairly thick.
9. Stir the marmalade occasionally during cooking and skim it carefully.
10. When most of the heat has passed, ladle it into warmed glass jars.
