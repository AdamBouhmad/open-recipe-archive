## Ingredients

- 300 g cooked or fried, boneless meat
- 100 g fat, smoked bacon
- 1 red onion
- 150 g cooked, grated potato
- 1-2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g) for the onion
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg for breading
- 1 egg white
- 2 dcl breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g) for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely together with the bacon.
2. Peel the onion, chop finely, sauté in butter and work into the forcemeat with a chopping knife, together with the potato, spices, the whisked egg and egg yolk.
3. Shape the forcemeat into oblong cutlets, 1 cm thick.
4. Dip in the whisked egg and egg white and turn in breadcrumbs.
5. Fry in butter over low heat until nicely golden brown.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with stewed vegetables or macaroni.
