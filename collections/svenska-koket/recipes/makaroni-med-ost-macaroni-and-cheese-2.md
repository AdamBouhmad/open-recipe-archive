## Ingredients

- 4 cups broken macaroni
- water
- salt
- 2 cups grated cheese
- 6 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 3-4 parts cream
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The macaroni is broken into 5 cm pieces and is parboiled in boiling salted water, then drained.
2. It is then placed in a buttered heatproof mold, layered with the grated cheese.
3. From 1 tbsp butter, flour and cream, a sauce is made and poured over the macaroni.
4. The mold is sprinkled with crushed rusks, and the remaining butter is dotted on top.
5. The mold is placed in the oven.
6. When the dish has a golden brown color, it is removed and served.
