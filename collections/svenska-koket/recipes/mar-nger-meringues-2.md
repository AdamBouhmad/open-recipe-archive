## Ingredients

- 180 gr. flour sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 20 gr. sweet almond
- 1 tbsp. fine powdered sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them well and chop them very finely.
2. Sift the sugar through a fine sieve.
3. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and carefully blend the sugar with a fork into the egg white foam.
4. Pipe with a smooth piping tube or with two teaspoons onto a well buttered baking sheet in wreaths or round balls, which are sprinkled with the almonds and sugar.
5. Bake the meringues in gentle oven heat until they are yellow and hard on the surface but not completely dry.
6. Loosen them with a knife from the sheet while they are warm.
