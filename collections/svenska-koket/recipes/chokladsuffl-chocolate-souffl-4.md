## Ingredients

- 50 g unsweetened chocolate
- 150 g sugar
- 4 dcl milk
- 15 g potato flour
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g) for the mold

## Directions

1. Whisk the chocolate, sugar, milk, and flour in a saucepan over heat until the mixture boils.
2. Remove the saucepan from heat and immediately add the egg yolks one at a time, whisking vigorously.
3. When the mixture is cold, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered, heat-resistant form and bake in a water bath in ordinary oven heat for approximately 40 minutes.
5. Serve immediately from the form with vanilla sauce.
