## Ingredients

- 12 thin fine leeks
- 1.5 liters water
- 10 grams salt

## Directions

1. Trim the leeks in the usual way and cut off some of the green parts.
2. Rinse them extremely well so that no sand or dirt remains between the leaves, place them in boiling salted water and cook until they are tender, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon and serve very hot on a platter with a folded napkin.
4. Serve creamed butter with it.
