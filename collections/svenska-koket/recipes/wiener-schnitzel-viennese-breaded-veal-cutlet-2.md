## Ingredients

- 6 hg. boneless veal sirloin
- 3 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 eggs for breading
- 1 1/2 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 g.) for frying
- 1 dcl. broth
- 6 lemon slices for garnish
- 6 anchovy fillets
- 1 tbsp. chopped capers

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into 6 slices, which are flattened to half the thickness and cleaned.
