## Ingredients

- 2.5-3 liters fresh tender nettles
- 3 liters water for pre-boiling
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 small bunch chives
- 1 handful chervil or 1/2 teaspoon ground fennel
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 liters beef broth
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon cold butter

## Directions

1. The nettles are cleaned and rinsed very thoroughly.
2. They are pre-boiled for 15 minutes as in recipe 257, placed on a sieve, rinsed with cold water and allowed to drain well.
3. The chervil and chives are cleaned, rinsed, wrung in a cloth and chopped finely together with the nettles, after which they are passed through a sieve.
4. Butter, nettles and flour are fried for 5 minutes, the boiling broth is thinned gradually, and the soup is allowed to cook with the lid slightly ajar for about 1/2 hour.
5. It is carefully seasoned with the spices (with fennel, if chervil is lacking) and served with fish quenelles, poached eggs or egg dumplings.
6. Canned nettles can be used with advantage. The soup can be made in the same way from chopped canned spinach.
