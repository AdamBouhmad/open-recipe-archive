## Ingredients

- 850 gr. syrup
- 1/3 liter clarified butter
- 212 gr. sugar
- 25 gr. potash
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 tsp. orange peel
- 2 tsp. ginger
- 2 tsp. cinnamon
- 2 tsp. cloves
- 2 tsp. allspice
- 1 1/2 kg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil up the syrup and then stir it with the clarified butter until it becomes cold.
2. Then stir in the sugar, potash, egg yolks, the finely chopped orange peel and the ground spices.
3. Last work in the flour, and let the dough stand until the next day.
4. Then roll it out quite thinly, and with a measure cut out cookies, which are baked in good oven heat.
