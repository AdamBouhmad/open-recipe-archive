## Ingredients

- Hachis of veal: 3 hectograms veal steak (leftovers), 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.), 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.), 5 deciliters broth, 1 tablespoon white wine, salt, white pepper
- Omelet: 6 eggs, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, 2 teaspoons flour, 6 deciliters milk
- For the omelet pan: 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the veal steak into fine cubes.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, dilute with the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the meat and warm it in the sauce, but do not let it boil; season the stew with the wine and seasonings.
4. For the omelet, whisk the eggs well with the salt, sugar, flour and milk.
5. Heat the omelet pan and butter it well.
6. Fry 2 omelets from the egg mixture, which are done when they have a light brown color.
7. Turn the first omelet out onto a round platter, spread the meat stew on it, turn the second omelet onto a buttered lid and place it thereafter over the stew.
8. Serve the turtle with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
