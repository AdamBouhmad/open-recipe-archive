## Ingredients

- 750 gr. fresh beans
- 7 dcl. water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 3 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.) - optional

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse them, let them drain on cloth, and cut them into 4 mm. thick pieces, sliced across (somewhat at an angle).
2. Place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft, then drain off excess liquid and mix the beans with parsley and cold butter.
3. If a thicker sauce is desired, mix the flour with half of the butter, add the cooking liquid, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes, then add the beans and mix with parsley and remaining butter.
4. Serve with boiled ham, cooked sausage and roasted meat.
