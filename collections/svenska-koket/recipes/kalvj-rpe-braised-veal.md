## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal loin
- 1 hg. lard
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 dcl. broth
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see page 232) and cut across into 12 even slices, which are lightly flattened.
2. Cut the lard into strips, which are dipped in a mixture of salt and pepper, and with this lard the meat slices.
3. Roll them up and tie at two places with fine twine, dipped in boiling water.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the rolls in and brown nicely, sprinkle with the rest of the spice mixture, thin with the boiling broth and cream and let them stew slowly with a tight lid, until they are tender, or about 3/4 hour.
5. Remove them, cut off the strings, place in a warm carrot, whisk up the sauce, taste and adjust and add a little thick cream if needed, and pour over the rolls.
6. Serve with boiled or mashed potatoes and lingonberries and onion.
