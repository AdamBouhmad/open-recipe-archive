## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. mushroom liquor
- 5 dcl. cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the mushrooms into pieces or slices and fry them in the butter until it is clear, then press them with a spoon
2. Fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes
3. Add the mushroom liquor and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
4. Add the mushroom and let the sauce simmer
