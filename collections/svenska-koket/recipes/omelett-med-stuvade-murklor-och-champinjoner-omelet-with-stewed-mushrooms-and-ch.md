## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 0.5 liters sour cream
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan
- 5-6 large mushrooms (fresh or dried)
- 1 can champignons (0.25 liters)
- 4-5 medium fresh champignons
- 2 tablespoons butter for stew
- 4 tablespoons wheat flour for stew
- 1.5 deciliters cream
- champignon liquid
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs together with salt for half an hour.
2. Add the flour and milk.
3. Season the batter, pour it into a well-buttered omelet pan and bake light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
4. Tip the omelet out immediately, place the stew on half of the omelet, and fold the other half over.
5. For the stew: Wash the mushrooms and fresh champignons and place them in cold water to cook until soft.
6. Fry the butter and flour together, add the sour cream, and let the stew boil for 10 minutes.
7. Cut the mushrooms and champignons into suitable pieces and mix them into the stew together with the champignon liquid and some of the mushroom liquid.
8. Season the stew with salt and white pepper; finally stir in the egg yolk and let the stew simmer over the heat.
