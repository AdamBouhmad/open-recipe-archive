## Ingredients

- 15 large and beautiful apples
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup crushed sugar
- 1 cup finely crushed rusks
- 50 g sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Dry and peel the apples and remove the seed cavities.
2. Melt the butter; crush the sugar so that it resembles pearl sugar; blanch and chop the almonds.
3. Mix the sugar, crushed rusks and almonds together.
4. Brush the apples with the butter and roll them in the mixture.
5. Butter a pie form with a little butter, place the apples in it and set in good oven heat.
6. When they are soft and have taken on a beautiful light brown color, place them on a glass dish and let them cool.
7. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
