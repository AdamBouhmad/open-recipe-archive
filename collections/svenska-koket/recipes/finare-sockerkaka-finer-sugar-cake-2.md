## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 250 gr. sugar
- 60 gr. wheat flour
- 60 gr. potato flour
- juice of ½ lemon
- 6 bitter almonds or vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy.
2. Add the potato flour little by little, and then the wheat flour.
3. Flavor the batter with lemon, almond or vanilla, pour it into a buttered and bread-crumb-coated mold or cake pan and bake in good oven heat.
