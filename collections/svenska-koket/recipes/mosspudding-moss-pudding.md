## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 dcl. sherry
- 120 gr. sugar
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks, wine, sugar, finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice in a saucepan and whip until the sugar melts.
2. Let the cream then simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and foamy, then remove from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in the hot water.
4. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
5. First add the gelatin to the cream, then fold in the cream very carefully.
6. Fill the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it sit on ice for 3-4 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass plate lined with parchment paper and garnish with cookies.
