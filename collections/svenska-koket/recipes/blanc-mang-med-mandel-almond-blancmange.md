## Ingredients

- 95 gr. sweet almond
- 3 bitter almonds
- 3 dcl. milk
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 125 gr. sugar
- zest and juice of 1 orange or a little orange essence
- 6 leaves gelatin
- 1 dcl. warm water

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, grind them through a mill and pound them well with a little of the milk in a mortar.
2. Mix the pounded almonds, milk, cream, sugar and the finely grated orange zest and orange juice in a clean saucepan.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in the warm water and add it.
4. Let the mixture come to a boil while stirring, strain through muslin, pour into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it set on ice.
5. The bottom of the mold can be garnished with red wine jelly.
6. Instead of almond, lemon, coffee, nuts etc. can be used.
