## Ingredients

- 1 small root celery
- 1 carrot
- 1 1/2 liters beef broth
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 2-3 egg yolks
- curry to taste
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 deciliter rice grains (1 hectogram)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 grams salt

## Directions

1. The celery and carrot are browned in the usual way, cut into fine strips and cooked soft in the broth, which is then strained
2. The roots are placed in the soup bowl
3. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the broth is added and allowed to cook while stirring for 5 minutes
4. The egg yolks are beaten and whisked into the soup, which is then seasoned with curry powder, salt and cayenne pepper and poured over the vegetables in the soup bowl
5. The rice grains are rinsed, put into boiling salted water and allowed to cook until soft or approximately 15 minutes
6. They are drained in a colander, rinsed over with cold water and allowed to stand in the oven for a few minutes, while they are stirred with a fork to become dry and warm
7. Placed on a carrot and served with the soup
