## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 5 parsley stalks
- 2 deciliters rice grains
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 30 g butter
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 6 deciliters of broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter
- 3 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the breast with a cloth wrung out in hot water, chop it into even, beautiful pieces, which are placed in so much boiling water that it stands level with the meat; skim well and add the spices, the seared carrot and the rinsed parsley stalks.
2. Let the meat thereafter cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until it is tender, or about 1 1/2 hours.
3. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, place them in boiling salted water and let them cook 15–20 minutes, after which they are poured into a colander, let them drain well and are mixed, while they are hot, with the cold butter.
4. For the sauce, fry butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, after which it is seasoned with the spices and finished with the egg yolks (see page 209).
5. Grease a fireproof dish with cold butter and sprinkle with half of the crushed breadcrumbs, then layer the meat, rice and sauce alternately.
