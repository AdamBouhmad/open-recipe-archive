## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms boiled, finely minced meat
- 1 hectogram boneless beef
- 1 hectogram boneless veal
- 1/2 hectogram lard
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 2-3 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Run the beef, veal and lard three times through a meat grinder and mix with the egg, heavy cream, the onion fried in 1 teaspoon butter and the seasonings to make a regular meat forcemeat. Add the boiled and finely minced meat, and mix everything well together.
2. Form small cutlets from the forcemeat in the shape of hearts, which are quickly fried in butter. If desired, the cutlets can be brushed with egg and sprinkled with crushed rusks before frying.
3. Serve with brown sauce.
