## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 210 gr. powdered sugar
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1 hg. potato flour
- 3 egg whites
- 30 gr. melted butter for the molds
- 1 dcl. crushed biscuits for the molds
- 1/2 portion vanilla cream or 1/2 portion lemon cream for filling
- 1 hg. powdered sugar for glaze
- 1 tbsp. water for glaze
- 1/2 tsp. lemon or orange juice for glaze
- 3 candied orange peels for garnish
- 30 gr. pistachios for garnish

## Directions

1. Brush small fluted pastry molds with the melted butter, let it set, then sprinkle with crushed, sifted biscuits.
2. Whisk three whole eggs and three egg yolks for 45 minutes, then add the finely grated lemon peel or 15 gr. scalded and ground sweet almond and the flour.
3. Lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Fill the molds to 3/4 with batter, and bake the pastries in a not too warm oven.
5. Turn them out, let them cool, split them and fill with cream, glaze them, sprinkle with the finely chopped orange peels and pistachios and serve as dessert or with coffee and tea.
