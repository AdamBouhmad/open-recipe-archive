## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoon breadcrumbs
- 80 gr. butter
- 3 dcl. boiling broth
- 1/2 dcl. white wine
- Mushroom sauce
- Pressed potatoes
- Apple sauce

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike (see page 171), leaving the head but removing the gills
2. Rub with salt and let lie 1 hour
3. Dry well, dip in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs, and roast in oven for about 1/2 hour as in recipe 338
4. Serve with mushroom sauce and pressed potatoes
5. Alternatively, cut the pike into fillets and remove head, backbone, and skin (use these to cook broth for basting the fish), then roast the same way
6. Roasted pike can also be served with apple sauce
7. Pike can also be smoked in the oven using a special smoking plate now available in stores
