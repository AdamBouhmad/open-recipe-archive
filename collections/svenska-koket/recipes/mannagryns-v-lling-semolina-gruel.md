## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 2 lit. milk
- 1 dcl. semolina (76 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 tsp. salt

## Directions

1. Heat the milk with the butter, sprinkle in the semolina, and stir well.
2. Cook while stirring until the gruel thickens.
3. Season with sugar and salt and serve.
