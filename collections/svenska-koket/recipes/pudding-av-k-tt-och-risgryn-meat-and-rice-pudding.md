## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless fried or cooked meat
- 1-2 red onions
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3-4 deciliters gravy or good broth
- 1 1/2 deciliters rice grains (150 g)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices or chop finely (do not grind). Peel, chop and brown the onion in the butter in a frying pan, add the meat and brown it well, season it and mix with the hot gravy or broth.
2. Rinse the rice grains with cold water, scald in hot water, add to the boiling milk and let cook until soft, then drain, immediately mix with the cold butter and let cool. Add the beaten egg yolks with the salt and finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. Butter a pie mold and sprinkle with crushed rusks. Layer the meat and rice alternately in the mold, the top layer should be rice, sprinkled with crushed rusks. Bake the pudding in the oven for about 30-40 minutes. Serve with gravy or clarified butter.
4. Instead of onion, finely chopped salt or pickled cucumber can be used. The pudding can be made simpler by using cream instead of butter and omitting the eggs in the rice.
