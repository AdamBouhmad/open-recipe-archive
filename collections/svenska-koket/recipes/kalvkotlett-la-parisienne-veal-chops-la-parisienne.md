## Ingredients

- 4 veal chops
- 2 truffles
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 g)
- small round cooked or fried potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the chops with a cloth wrung out in hot water, trim them, pound well on both sides and lard with the truffle, cut into strips.
2. Rub with the spice mixture.
3. Brown the butter in a frying pan, place the chops in and fry 5 minutes on each side.
4. Transfer to a warm platter, pour the butter over and garnish the dish with small round cooked or fried potatoes and parsley.
5. Serve as a breakfast dish.
