## Ingredients

- 2 kg beef brisket, chuck, or marrowbone
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/4 liter meat broth or milk
- 3 liters water
- 2 tbsp salt
- 3-4 tbsp grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water
2. Place in boiling water, skim, salt, and let simmer slowly with a tightly fitting lid for 2-3 hours
3. Prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
4. Do not let the horseradish cook with the sauce, as this makes it taste bitter
