## Ingredients

- 1 1/3 hg butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Hard boil the eggs, cut and chop them.
2. Clarify the butter, remove the white foam.
3. Place the chopped eggs in the sauce bowl and pour the clarified butter over.
