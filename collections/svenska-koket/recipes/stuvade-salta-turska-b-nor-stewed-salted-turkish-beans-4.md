## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. salted beans
- 8 dcl. water
- White sauce: 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. bean cooking liquid
- 3 dcl. cream or milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the beans in a colander, rinse with cold water and let them soak in plenty of water for approximately 12 hours.
2. Rinse again, place in cold water and let them simmer slowly until completely soft, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. Drain the cooking liquid and reserve it.
4. For the sauce, brown the butter and flour together, add the cooking liquid and cream, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
5. Mix in the egg yolk and let it simmer, then add the beans and season the stew with sugar.
6. Serve with cooked sausage, salted meat, etc.
