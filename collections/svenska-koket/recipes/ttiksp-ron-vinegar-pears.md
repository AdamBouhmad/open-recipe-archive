## Ingredients

- 3 kg peeled pears
- 1 1/2 kg granulated sugar
- 3 dcl vinegar
- 2 dcl white wine

## Directions

1. Peel the pears with thin, even peels, halve them if they are large, and remove the cores.
2. Scrape the stems well.
3. Layer the pears and sugar alternately in a jam pot.
4. Add the vinegar and wine, and let the pears simmer slowly with a tight-fitting lid until they are soft and clear, or approximately 2 1/2 hours.
5. During this time skim them when necessary.
6. Arrange them with a slotted spoon on a flat dish to cool and place them thereafter in a jar previously rinsed with brandy.
7. Let the syrup cook down without a lid until it is quite thick, then let it cool somewhat and pour it over the pears.
