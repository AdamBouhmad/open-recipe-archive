## Ingredients

- 12 anchovies
- 2 eggs
- 2 dcl. cream
- 1 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the dish
- 1 tbsp. crushed rusks for the dish

## Directions

1. Skin the anchovies, remove the insides, split down the middle and remove the bone.
2. Grease a small, fireproof dish with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
3. Lay the fillets in it, pour over with the eggs, well whipped with the cream, and sprinkle with the parsley.
4. Bake the casserole in a good oven heat and serve to the smorgasbord.
