## Ingredients

- 250 gr. fine prunes (about 22 pieces)
- 2 tablespoons sugar (80 gr.)
- 3 decilitres water
- 4 eggs
- 100 gr. sugar
- 75 gr. butter
- 4 decilitres boiling cream
- 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the form

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes well in several lukewarm waters and place in a bowl alternating with sugar. Add water and let the prunes soak overnight.
2. Cook them thereafter about 5 minutes, let them cool, remove the pits, cut into strips, which are placed in a buttered pie form.
3. Crush the pits, place the almonds in boiling water, peel, dry in the oven, chop finely and sprinkle over the prunes.
4. Beat egg yolks, sugar and butter for 20 minutes, add the boiling cream under vigorous whisking together with the lemon zest, and continue whisking until the cream is cold, then carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the cream over the prunes and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven about 3/4 hour, or until the mass is set when tested with a whisk.
6. Serve warm or cold with or without whipped cream.
