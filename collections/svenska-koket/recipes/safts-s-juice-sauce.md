## Ingredients

- 1 teacup sweet juice
- 8 teacups water
- 1 tablespoon potato flour

## Directions

1. Juice and water are boiled up.
2. Potato flour is stirred out with a little water and added, after which the sauce is brought to a boil for a short time.
3. It is seasoned with sugar and lemon juice, if needed.
