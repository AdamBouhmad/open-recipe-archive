## Ingredients

- 3–4 leaves gelatin, 2 tbsp. warm water
- 5 dcl. extra thick cream
- 3 tbsp. caster sugar (45 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar or 1 tsp. vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the warm water.
2. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks, add the vanilla sugar or essence, and gradually pour in the gelatin, whisking vigorously, into the cream.
3. Pour the cream into a compote bowl, let it set on ice and garnish with jam.
4. Serve with biscuits or almond flakes.
