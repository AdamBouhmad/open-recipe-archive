## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hectograms good macaroni
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 80 gr. butter
- 1 finely chopped red onion
- 50 gr. lean veal (in fine cubes)
- 40 gr. flour
- 8 deciliters boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.) for the mold
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 3 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni in salted water until tender, drain and set aside.
2. Make the béchamel sauce: melt the butter, sauté the finely chopped red onion and veal cubes, sprinkle with flour and stir for 2 minutes.
3. Gradually add the boiling milk while stirring constantly, season with salt, white pepper and a little grated nutmeg.
4. Mix the cooked macaroni with the béchamel sauce.
5. Butter a mold, pour the macaroni mixture into it.
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese and crushed breadcrumbs.
7. Bake in the oven until golden brown and serve.
