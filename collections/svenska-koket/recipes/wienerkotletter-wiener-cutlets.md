## Ingredients

- 500 gr. boneless veal bottom round
- 200 gr. cooked, cold potato
- 4 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 1 deciliter heavy cream
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 deciliters crushed bread crumbs
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 2 deciliters broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, cut into pieces and grind 4 times through a meat grinder, the last time together with the potato.
2. Mix the parsley, spices and cream into the meat mixture, stir well and form into 15 oblong cutlets.
3. Even out with a knife, brush with egg whisked with milk, and turn in the crushed bread crumbs.
4. Brown butter in a frying pan, add the cutlets, brown quickly in butter and place in a pot.
5. Whip out the frying pan with the boiling broth, pour sauce over the cutlets, which then braise with the lid tightly closed for about 10 minutes.
6. Place on a warm serving dish, whip up the sauce and pour over the cutlets, which are garnished with parsley and served with snap peas, spinach or mashed potatoes.
