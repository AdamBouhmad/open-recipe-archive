## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized cauliflower heads
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2-3 cups cabbage broth
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and place it in vinegar-mixed water for about 1/2 hour.
2. Place it in salted boiling water (just over 1 liter) and let it simmer slowly until it feels tender.
3. Remove it and let it drain.
4. Fry the butter and flour together, add the cabbage broth and milk a little at a time, and let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Cut the cauliflower into wedges and add it to the sauce, and season the stew.
6. Place the stew on a plate and serve.
