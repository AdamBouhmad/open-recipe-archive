## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. cherries
- 1 liter water
- 1 1/2 hg. sugar
- 3 tbsp. mondamin (45 gr.) or 3 tbsp. potato starch (45 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the cherries, rinse them and let them drain.
2. Crush half of the berries in a stone mortar.
3. Put both the whole berries and the crushed berries into the cold water together with the sugar and let them cook until the berries are soft.
4. Strain through a colander.
5. Pour the juice back into the saucepan, taste it and thicken with the flour mixed with 1 dcl. cold water.
6. Let the cream cook while stirring for 5 minutes.
7. Pour into a compote dish rinsed with lukewarm water, sprinkle with sugar to prevent a skin from forming, let cool and serve with cream or milk.
