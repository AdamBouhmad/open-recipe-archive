## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg small Portuguese onions
- 6 decilitres light stock
- 60 gr butter
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, place in the boiling stock together with butter and sugar and let boil, until they are completely soft, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Remove with a slotted spoon, and reduce the sauce to a jelly, place the onions in it and glaze, while shaking the pot gently.
3. Serve as the preceding dish.
