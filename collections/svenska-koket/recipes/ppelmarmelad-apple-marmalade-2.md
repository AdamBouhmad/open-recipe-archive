## Ingredients

- 10 liters apples
- 1.5 liters water
- 1/4 kg granulated sugar for each kg of apple sauce
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 parts white French wine

## Directions

1. Wash the apples (but do not peel), cut them into pieces, and remove the cores.
2. Place them in cold water and allow them to cook until they become soft and fall apart, then press them through a sieve.
3. Place the sauce back in the pot, add the sugar, and allow the sauce to cook under stirring for 2-3 hours.
4. Add the lemon juice and wine.
5. When the mass releases from the spoon in flakes, the marmalade is ready.
