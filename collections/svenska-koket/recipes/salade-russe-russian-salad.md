## Ingredients

- 1 hg. cooked potato
- 1 hg. cooked carrots
- 1 hg. canned Turkish beans
- 50 gr. truffle
- 50 gr. lean, smoked ham
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 lit.)
- 1 hg. canned peas
- 2 msk. capers
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- cayenne
- 1/4 tsk. French mustard
- 1 1/2 dcl. cooking oil
- 1 msk. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 msk. lemon juice
- 1 msk. boiling water
- 1-2 beetroots for garnish
- 1 lettuce head for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the potato, carrots, beans, truffle, ham and pickled cucumber into fine strips.
2. Cut the mushrooms into thin slices.
3. Prepare the mayonnaise sauce as in recipe no. 10.
4. Mix all ingredients carefully with the sauce.
5. Place the salad in a crystal bowl and garnish with beetroot and lettuce.
6. Serve with cold, roasted meat or as a side dish.
