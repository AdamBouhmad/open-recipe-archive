## Ingredients

- Meat mixture as in recipe no. 632
- 1 1/2 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 3/4 liter water
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 3 msk. butter
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 4 dcl. cooking liquid from artichokes
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat mixture as in recipe no. 632.
2. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes clean, scrape them, rinse and soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour to keep them white.
3. Place them in boiling water with salt and cook until tender, then drain in a colander and save the cooking liquid.
4. Prepare the braised vegetables and pudding and bake as in the preceding recipe.
