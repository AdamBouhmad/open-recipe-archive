## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- wheat flour equal to the weight of 3 small eggs (= 180 gr.)
- granulated sugar equal to the weight of 4 eggs (= 240 gr.)
- grated zest of 1 lemon or 3 drops almond essence
- 2 tbsp. washed butter for the molds
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Fine wheat flour is dried and sifted.
2. The egg yolks are stirred with the sugar until the mixture becomes white.
3. The flour is added together with the lightly grated lemon zest or almond essence.
4. The egg whites are beaten to stiff peaks and carefully folded into the batter.
5. Sponge-cake molds or regular smooth pastry molds are greased with washed butter, sprinkled with sugar and filled to three-quarters with batter.
6. The cakes are placed in a fairly warm oven to bake through and get a beautiful golden color.
7. They are turned out of the molds while still warm, sugared over and allowed to cool.
