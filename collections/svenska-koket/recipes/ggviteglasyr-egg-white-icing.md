## Ingredients

- 1 cup sifted powdered sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 tsp lemon juice

## Directions

1. Whip the egg white, add the sugar and lemon juice, and stir the mixture until it is smooth and shiny.
2. Coat the cake with the icing and place it for a moment in a warm oven so that the icing dries.
