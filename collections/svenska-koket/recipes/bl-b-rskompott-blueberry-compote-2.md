## Ingredients

- 1.5 deciliters sugar (128 grams)
- 2 deciliters water
- 1 liter blueberries

## Directions

1. Cook the water and sugar together to make a thin, clear liquid
2. Clean the blueberries, rinse in a sieve and allow to drain well
3. Place them in the sugar liquid and cook for 4-5 minutes
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and place in a warm compote bowl
5. Cook the syrup until it is clear and syrupy, then pour immediately over the berries
6. Serve cold with whipped cream
