## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. carrots (4-5 pieces)
- 1/2 litre water
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse and brush the carrots well clean, but do not scrape them, place in cold salted water and let them boil until they are soft, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Remove the carrots, peel the skin immediately, then cut them into small pieces or slices with a vegetable knife.
3. Place them in a serving dish, sprinkle with sugar, cover with the cold butter and let them stand in a water bath until they are heated through, then serve as a garnish around fried meat or together with cooked vegetables.
