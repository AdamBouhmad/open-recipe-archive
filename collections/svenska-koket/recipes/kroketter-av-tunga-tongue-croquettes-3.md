## Ingredients

- 1 calf tongue
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 piece celery
- 1/2 leek
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 4 deciliter tongue stock
- 1/2 teaspoon meat extract
- 1 tablespoon capers or 3 olives
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt, white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt for breading
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Let the tongue soak in cold water for a few hours, then scald in hot water and scrape well.
2. Place it in boiling salted water and skim the stock well when it comes to a boil, then add the prepared root vegetables and let the tongue cook slowly until tender, about 2 hours.
3. Remove the skin and let the tongue cool.
4. Cut the tongue into fine cubes.
5. Strain the stock, skim it, and mix 3 deciliters of it with the meat extract.
6. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
7. Stir in the tongue and finely chopped capers or olives together with the whipped egg yolks and the gelatin that has been rinsed and dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water.
8. Season the mixture with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
9. Form the croquettes into oblong shapes.
10. Serve as a side course with peas or mixed vegetables.
