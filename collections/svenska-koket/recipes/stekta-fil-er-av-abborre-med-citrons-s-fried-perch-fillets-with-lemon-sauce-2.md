## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg perch
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 3 msk flour (30 gr.)
- 4 msk butter (80 gr.)

## Directions

1. Do not scale the perch; clean as described on page 171, cut into fillets and remove the backbone and all small bones.
2. Place fillets with skin side against cutting board and cut the meat from the skin with a pointed knife.
3. Rub fillets with salt and let rest 1 hour, then dry again with a fish towel.
4. Turn fillets in flour and fry them a nice golden brown in butter on the stovetop as in recipe no. 330.
5. Place on a warm plate and pour sauce (see recipe no. 416) over them.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and fluted lemon slices and serve with mashed potatoes.
