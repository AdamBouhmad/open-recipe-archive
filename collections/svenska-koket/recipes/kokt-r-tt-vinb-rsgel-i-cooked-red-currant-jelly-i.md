## Ingredients

- 3 liters red currants
- 4 parts water
- 3/4 to 1 kg sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the currants and allow them to drain.
2. Strip the berries and cook them with the water on very low heat for approximately 20 minutes, stirring.
3. Pour the berries into a cheesecloth and allow the juice to drip without pressing.
4. Weigh the juice, add the sugar and stir until the sugar is somewhat melted.
5. Bring slowly to a boil and allow to cook slowly for 8-10 minutes.
6. Skim the jelly well during cooking.
7. Immediately pour into warm jars rinsed with cognac.
