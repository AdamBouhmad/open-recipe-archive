## Ingredients

- 250 grams wheat flour
- 425 grams butter
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon spirits
- 1 egg for brushing
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 hectogram sweet almonds
- 3 parts thick cream, vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as fine butter dough (no. 548)
2. Roll out the dough and cut out into narrow strips, which are wound around metal tubes
3. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with finely chopped almonds and sugar
4. Bake the pastries in a hot oven
5. When done, take them out and remove the metal tubes
6. When cooled, fill with cream whipped to stiff peaks and mixed with vanilla sugar
