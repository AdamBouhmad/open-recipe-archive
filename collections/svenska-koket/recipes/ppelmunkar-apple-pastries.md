## Ingredients

- 7 beautiful apples
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- 125 grams butter
- 125 grams wheat flour
- 3 3/4 to 4 tablespoons water
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and remove the cores
2. Place them in a buttered pie form, fill with sugar, set in the oven to fry until they are half soft, and then set out to cool
3. Prepare the butter dough as simple butter dough (no. 549) and roll out to approximately 1 cm thickness
4. Cut out into squares, place one apple on each square, and fold the corners over each other so that they form a bundle
5. Turn the pastries, place on a sheet, brush with egg and bake in a hot oven
