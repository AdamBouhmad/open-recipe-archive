## Ingredients

- 3 dl. water
- 100 gr. butter
- 2 tsp. caster sugar
- 100 gr. wheat flour
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Bring water, butter, and sugar to a boil, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook until it leaves the sides of the pan.
2. When most of the heat has passed, stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
3. Lastly, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. On well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets, place large spoonfuls of the batter.
5. Bake in good oven heat, taking care not to open the oven door before 10 minutes have passed.
6. When the pastries have cooled, cut a lid in each, and fill them with cream or whipped cream.
