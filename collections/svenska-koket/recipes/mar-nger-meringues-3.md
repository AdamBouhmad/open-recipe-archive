## Ingredients

- 180 gr. fine sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 20 gr. sweet almond
- 1 tablespoon fine granulated sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. The almond is blanched, dried well and chopped very fine.
2. The sugar is sifted through a fine sieve.
3. The egg whites are whisked to stiff peaks and the sugar is gently mixed with a fork into the egg white foam.
4. Through a smooth piping bag or with two teaspoons are laid on well buttered sheet cransar or round buns, which are sprinkled with the almond and sugar.
5. The meringues are baked in gentle oven heat until they are yellow and hard on the surface but not completely dried through.
6. They are loosened with a knife from the sheet while they are still warm.
