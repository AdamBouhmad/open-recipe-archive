## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter German lentils
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 1 shallot onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- lemon juice to taste
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lentils
2. Place in cold water and let cook for approximately 1 1/2 hours
3. Peel and finely chop the onion and sauté it in butter together with the flour
4. Add the lentil cooking liquid and bring to a boil
5. Add the lentils and let simmer slowly until completely tender, or approximately 1/2 hour
6. Season with salt and lemon juice and sugar if desired
7. Serve with fried pork and meatballs. Lentils can also be prepared like brown beans
