## Ingredients

- 8 salted herrings
- water
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 oz. butter

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water the day before it is to be used.
2. Remove it, free it from skin and innards and split it down the middle, then remove the backbone and as many small bones as possible.
3. Cook the eggs for 10 minutes, shell them and chop them finely.
4. Place the herring in boiling water.
