## Ingredients

- 1 grouse
- 1 hectogram lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5-1 liter broth or thin cream
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the grouse, remove organs, rinse well and dry. If it has been frozen, soak it in milk overnight.
2. Lard it with lard cut into fine strips and truss.
3. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the grouse breast-side up, brown well and salt. Cook on low heat and baste occasionally with broth or thin cream.
4. Roasting takes 2-3 hours.
5. Strain the meat gravy, skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
