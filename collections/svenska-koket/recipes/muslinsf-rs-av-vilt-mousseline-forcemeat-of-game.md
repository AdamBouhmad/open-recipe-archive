## Ingredients

- 1 large hare, approximately 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsp. salt (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 5-6 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the hare. Cut off head, paws and slaughter sides, then rinse the hare very well, dry it and remove all membranes.
2. Cut all meat loose from the bones, scrape it free from sinews and membranes, mix with the seasonings and pass 6 times through a meat grinder.
3. Pound the forcemeat thereafter in a stone mortar with a wooden pestle until it becomes even and smooth, then add the whipped egg and egg yolk with vigorous stirring.
4. Pass the forcemeat through a hair sieve, place in a mold or smaller saucepan, which is set in a bowl with ice and let it stand 1 hour, or until it is completely cooled.
5. Stir it then, let it remain on the ice and add the cream a little at a time with even and vigorous stirring.
6. Test the forcemeat as in the previous recipe.
7. Forcemeat prepared in this way is especially fine and delicate and is well suited for finer side dishes, garnishes, etc.
8. Meat of veal, fowl, game birds, fish and shellfish can be used for this.
