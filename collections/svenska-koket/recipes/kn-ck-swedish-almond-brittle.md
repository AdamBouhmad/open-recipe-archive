## Ingredients

- 2 hg. sweet almonds
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 3 dcl. syrup
- 3 hg. granulated sugar
- 1 1/4 dcl. crushed and sifted rusks

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and chop finely.
2. Mix cream, syrup, and sugar in a pan and bring to a boil with stirring, cooking until the mixture shows a tendency to harden when tested in cold water (approximately 25 minutes).
3. Add the finely crushed and sifted rusks and cook the mixture for a further 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the almonds and bring the mixture to a quick boil.
5. Pour into small fluted paper forms.
