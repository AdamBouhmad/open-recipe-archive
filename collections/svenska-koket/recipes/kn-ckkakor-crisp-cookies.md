## Ingredients

- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. baking powder (3 gr.)
- 3 hg. butter
- 150 gr. sugar
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, set aside one kkp for dusting, and mix the rest well with the baking powder
2. Wash the butter and stir it with the sugar for about 15 minutes
3. Add the eggs, one at a time
4. Add the flour and work the dough until it is even and smooth
5. Cover and let rest for about 2 hours
6. Roll out to 3 mm thickness, prick well and cut out into small round cookies with a hole on one side
7. Place on buttered baking sheet and bake until golden brown in a not-too-hot oven
