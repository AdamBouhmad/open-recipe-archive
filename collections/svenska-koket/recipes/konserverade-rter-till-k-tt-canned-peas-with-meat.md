## Ingredients

- 1 can peas (1/2 litre)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Pour the peas with their liquid into a saucepan and bring to a boil.
2. Drain the liquid and mix the peas with the cold butter and parsley.
3. Serve as in recipe 717.
