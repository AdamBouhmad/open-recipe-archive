## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg lamb brisket
- 0.25 kg bacon
- 30 gr. butter
- 4–5 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon beef steak sauce
- 2 carrots
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 6 deciliters meat stock or broth
- 0.5 liter potatoes
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 0.5 can fine peas (0.25 liter)

## Directions

1. Treat the meat as in the previous recipe, cut into even beautiful pieces, which are well browned in hot bacon.
2. Brown the butter and flour in a pot, add the meat, sprinkle with spices and pour in the boiling broth or meat stock along with the tomato paste and beef steak sauce.
3. Allow to slowly boil with a lid for approximately 1 hour.
4. Prepare the carrots and potatoes and cut into oblong pieces, peel the onion and cut into wedges.
5. Place the vegetables and onion on top of the meat and allow the dish to boil further for approximately 30 minutes, or until everything is tender.
6. Then add the peas and allow the stew to come to a boil, taste, and serve very hot.
