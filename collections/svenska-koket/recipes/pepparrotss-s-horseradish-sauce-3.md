## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter meat stock or milk
- 3-4 tablespoons grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the horseradish in water for a couple of hours, scrape and grate it.
2. Fry butter and flour together, add the meat stock or milk, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
3. Add the grated horseradish and taste and adjust the sauce.
4. The horseradish must not cook with the sauce, as it will then give the sauce a bitter taste.
