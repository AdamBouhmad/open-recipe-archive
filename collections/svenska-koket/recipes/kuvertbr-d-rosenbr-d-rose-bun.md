## Ingredients

- 5 hg. prime wheat flour
- 50 gr. butter
- 3 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 15 gr. pressed yeast
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 5-6 cardamom pods
- 1 egg for brushing
- 1 tbsp. poppy seeds

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl. Distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work in with fingertips, then stir in the milk and yeast, mixed with the salt and sugar and the finely crushed cardamom pods, if desired.
2. Work the dough very well and roll out into 15 round buns, in which a sharp knife is used to make a cross.
3. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, allow to rise about 1 1/2 hours, brush with beaten egg or milk, sprinkle with poppy seeds and bake in a hot oven 20-25 minutes.
4. The breads may be formed as horns and treated as above.
5. The dough may also be rolled out into 30 thin sticks, which are cut on the slant, allowed to rise, brushed with milk, sprinkled with salt and baked a lovely brown in a hot oven.
6. Instead of yeast, 2 teaspoons of baking powder may be used, but the breads are then baked immediately after they are rolled out.
