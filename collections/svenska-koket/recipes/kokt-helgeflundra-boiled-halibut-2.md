## Ingredients

- 1 kg halibut
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 5 tablespoons salt (75 g)
- 3 liters ice-cold water
- 0.5 lemon
- 10 g coarse salt per liter of water for cooking

## Directions

1. Clean the fish piece; pour vinegar over the dark side and scrape well.
2. Dissolve the salt in water and let the fish piece soak in it for 1 hour.
3. Remove it, wrap it in cloth, extract through an incision made at the head, scrape away the dark membrane carefully and cut off the fins.
4. Rinse the fish very well, dry it, rub it with salt and let it rest for 1 hour, then dry it again.
5. Boil as in recipe no. 327, requiring great attention during cooking as it is done in a few minutes.
6. Place on a warm dish with the white side up, garnish with parsley and serve with wine sauce, parsley sauce or crayfish sauce.
7. Serve with melted butter, grated horseradish and boiled potatoes.
