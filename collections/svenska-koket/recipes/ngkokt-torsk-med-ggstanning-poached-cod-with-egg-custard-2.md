## Ingredients

- 2 fish, 8 hectograms each
- 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 grams)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the form
- 4 eggs
- 3 deciliters cream milk
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the fish as in no. 384.
2. Cut off the head and tail and do not use them.
3. Rub the fish with salt, let lie 2 hours, dry well, cut into 4 1/2 cm. wide pieces across, which are placed in a butter-greased ovenproof form, preferably with a lid, together with the roe and liver, cut into pieces.
4. Whisk the eggs well with the milk, mix in the parsley and pour the egg custard over the fish, place the lid on or bind the form with butter-greased, double butter paper.
5. Cook the arrangement in a water bath in the oven approximately 40 minutes or until the fish feels tender.
6. Serve it from the form with melted butter, added with lemon juice and boiled potatoes.
