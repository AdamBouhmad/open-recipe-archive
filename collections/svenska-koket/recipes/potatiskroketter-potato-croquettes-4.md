## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 50 grams butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- sugar
- 2 eggs for breading, 8 cups crushed rusks
- 1/2 kg cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush and cook them.
2. Peel them, press them through a puree press and mix with the butter and egg and season with salt and sugar.
3. Form small round balls from the potato puree; these are breaded and cooked beautifully golden brown in the cream.
