## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 small tsp. English powdered mustard
- 1/2 liter fine olive oil
- 3-4 tbsp. vinegar or lemon juice
- 1/2 tbsp. water

## Directions

1. The raw egg yolks, salt and mustard are stirred together in a bowl, which is placed in ice water to keep sufficiently cold.
2. The oil, which should likewise be very cold, is poured in drop by drop to the egg yolks, alternating with the vinegar or lemon juice under constant stirring in one direction.
3. From time to time a few drops of water are likewise added, as otherwise the sauce easily breaks.
