## Ingredients

- 2–3 eggs
- 3 dcl cream
- approximately 160 g flour
- 7 dcl milk
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- 1½ msk melted butter (30 g)
- 2 msk butter for the pan (40 g)
- 2 dcl crushed sugar
- 2 dcl water
- ½ tsk finely grated lemon zest (optional)
- 1 litre peeled apples
- 2 msk butter for the pan (40 g)

## Directions

1. Prepare the pancake batter as in the previous recipe and let it rest and rise
2. Boil sugar and water to make a clear syrup, flavoured with lemon zest if desired
3. Peel the apples, remove the cores, and cut into 3 mm thick slices
4. Place the apple slices directly in the sugar syrup and cook until half soft
5. Heat the pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it
6. Stir the batter well and when it is poured on the pan, place one apple slice in each section
7. Fry the pancakes on both sides, place on a warm plate, sprinkle with sugar and serve hot without jam
