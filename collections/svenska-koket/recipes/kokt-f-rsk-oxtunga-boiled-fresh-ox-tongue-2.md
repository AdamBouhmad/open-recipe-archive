## Ingredients

- 1 ox tongue
- water
- 3-4 tablespoons salt
- 10 peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 1 carrot

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue and let it soak in water for a couple of hours.
2. Place in boiling water, which should cover it just level, skim well, and add the spices and the prepared carrot.
3. Let the tongue simmer slowly for 2½–3 hours, or until the skin can be easily peeled off.
4. After the skin is removed, place it back in the broth to keep it warm until serving.
5. Slice the tongue diagonally into ½ cm thick slices, which are arranged so that the tongue appears whole on the platter when served.
6. Garnish with parsley or salad and serve with boiled or stewed vegetables or with a sauce, such as brown mushroom, morel, or olive sauce, and potatoes.
