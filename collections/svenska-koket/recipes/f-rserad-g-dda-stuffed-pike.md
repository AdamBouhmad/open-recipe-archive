## Ingredients

- 1.4 kg pike (= 600 g scraped fish meat)
- 300 g butter
- 40 g flour
- 6 parts cream
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- sugar
- 1 egg for breading
- 2 tbsp crushed hardtack for breading
- 2 tbsp butter for frying
- fish stock

## Directions

1. Scale the pike and cut it open along the back, remove the innards and wash the fish well
2. Remove the backbone and carefully scrape the meat from the skin
3. Sew the skin together with a surgical stitch and leave a small opening at the head
4. Force the finely scraped fish meat together with butter three times through a meat grinder and then pound with the egg yolks in a mortar until the mass is fine and cohesive
5. In a bowl mix the flour, cream and seasonings
6. When everything is well mixed, work the mixture into the forcemeat tablespoon by tablespoon
7. Work the forcemeat for about 1 hour, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
8. Test the forcemeat and fill through a pastry tube into the prepared fish skin
9. The fish should not be overfilled as the forcemeat will swell from the beaten egg white and the fish may burst
10. Brush the pike with egg, sprinkle with crushed hardtack and bake in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours
11. Serve with mushroom, crayfish or lobster sauce
