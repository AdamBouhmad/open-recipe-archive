## Ingredients

- 1 tsp. minced shallot onion
- 1/2 gr. white pepper
- 2 gr. salt
- 4 tbsp. good vinegar
- 5 egg yolks
- 180 gr. melted butter
- cayenne
- 20 gr. minced tarragon stems
- 3 tbsp. good meat gravy or fish broth

## Directions

1. Mix the onion, pepper, salt and vinegar in the sauce pot and reduce by cooking until 1/3 remains.
2. Move the pot to the side, add the whipped egg yolks and let the sauce simmer under vigorous whisking, then add the melted, slightly cooled butter gradually.
3. Strain the sauce through fine cloth, season with cayenne and finish with the minced tarragon stems and, if desired, with the meat gravy or fish broth.
4. Heat in a bain-marie until lukewarm and serve immediately. If heated too much, it will break.
