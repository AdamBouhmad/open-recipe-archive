## Ingredients

- 1 pike, 1 1/2 kg
- 1 small salt herring
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 3 decilitres boiling water
- 3 whole gingerbread cookies
- 2 decilitres cream

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; the head may remain but remove the gills
2. Clean, rinse, and dry the salt herring that has not been soaked, and place it inside the pike
3. Brush the pike with egg and sprinkle with crushed hardtack
4. Grease a long baking pan with half the cold butter on a spot as large as the fish occupies; place the pike in and distribute the remaining butter in flakes on top
5. Roast the fish in a warm oven for approximately 1/2 hour, basting it frequently and moistening with water
6. Crush the gingerbread cookies finely and mix with cream
7. Remove the herring from the pike, which is placed on a warm platter and garnished with parsley
8. Deglaze the pan sauce with the dissolved gingerbread, bring to a boil, strain, and serve in a separate sauceboat
