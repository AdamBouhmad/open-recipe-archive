## Ingredients

- 9 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 3 dcl. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 5 tablespoons butter (1 hg.)

## Directions

1. Wipe the cutlet rack with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Cut out the cutlets so that one rib bone remains in each.
3. Remove the outer membrane and backbone, scrape the meat from the tip of the rib bones and cut them even.
4. Pound the cutlets with a damp meat mallet, shape and rub in with a mixture of salt and pepper.
5. They can be fried with or without breading.
6. If they are to be breaded, dip them in the beaten eggs and turn them in the crushed breadcrumbs.
7. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter lightly in it, lay in the cutlets and fry about 7 minutes on each side, or until they are beautifully brown and tender.
8. Lay them up on a warm dish and pour the browned butter over.
9. If desired, put paper cuffs on the bones.
10. Serve with fried potatoes or vegetables. If the cutlets are large, one can serve each person and the other ingredients are reduced accordingly.
