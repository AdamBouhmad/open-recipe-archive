## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 root celery
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the Jerusalem artichokes and celery as in recipe no. 269.
2. Place them in cold water with salt added and allow to cook until completely tender, then pass through a sieve.
3. Fry 2 tablespoons of butter with flour for 2 minutes.
4. Gradually add the purée and cream, little by little.
5. Allow the soup to cook for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
6. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl, add the remaining butter and pour the soup in while whisking.
7. Serve with toasted bread or cheese croutons.
