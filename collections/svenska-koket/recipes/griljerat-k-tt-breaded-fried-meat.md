## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked or roasted meat
- 1 egg
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 cup crushed bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices.
2. Dip these in the whisked egg and milk, turn in the crushed bread crumbs and fry golden-brown in the butter.
3. Serve with potato purée or vegetables.
