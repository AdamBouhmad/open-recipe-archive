## Ingredients

- 4 pork feet
- 4 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 gr.)
- baking soda, of the size of a pea
- 1 egg
- 2 deciliters finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The pork feet are cooked as in the preceding recipe and as soon as they are done the large bone is removed, after which they are placed in a press between two cutting boards and allowed to cool. They are dipped in beaten egg, rolled in crushed breadcrumbs, fried to a beautiful golden brown in butter and served with fried potatoes.
