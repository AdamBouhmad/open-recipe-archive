## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg chestnuts
- coarse salt

## Directions

1. Cut a cross in the pointed end of each chestnut
2. In a frying pan or long pan bed the chestnuts down in salt and put in the oven to roast 25-30 min., or until they easily shed their shells
3. Place them up in a broken napkin and serve warm with cold butter
