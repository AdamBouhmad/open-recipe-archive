## Ingredients

- 1 kg. large lingonberries
- 15 cloves
- 750 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 dcl. water

## Directions

1. Select large lingonberries, rinse them, let them drain, prick them afterwards with sewing needles threaded through a cork, and layer them alternately with the cloves in a jar.
2. Cook the sugar and water to a clear, syrupy liquid, which is poured hot over the berries.
3. Cover the jar with a doubled cloth and plate and let it stand until the next day.
4. If the liquid is then too thin, pour it off, cook it up and pour it cold over the berries.
5. Bind with parchment paper.
