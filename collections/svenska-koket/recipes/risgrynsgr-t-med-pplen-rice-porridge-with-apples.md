## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice groats
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 liters fresh apples or 1 1/2 hectogram dried apples
- 1–1 1/2 cups crushed sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice groats in lukewarm water
2. Bring the water to a boil; add the groats, the peeled and cut apples and the sugar
3. Let the porridge cook about 2 hours
4. Season and serve
5. If using dried apples, rinse them and soak a few hours before cooking
6. Crushed groats can be used instead of rice groats
