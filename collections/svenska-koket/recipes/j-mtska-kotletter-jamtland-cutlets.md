## Ingredients

- 3 hg. cooked or fried, boneless meat
- 1 hg. fat, smoked bacon
- 1 red onion
- 150 gr. cooked, grated potato
- 1—2 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the onion
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg for breading and frying
- 1 egg white
- 2 dcl. grated breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely together with the bacon.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely, fry it in butter and work it into the mixture with a chopping knife, together with the potato, spices, the beaten egg and egg yolk.
3. Form the mixture into oblong cutlets, 1 cm. thick.
4. Dip them in the beaten egg and egg white and turn them in grated breadcrumbs.
5. Fry them in butter over low heat until they are beautifully golden brown.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with stewed vegetables or macaroni.
