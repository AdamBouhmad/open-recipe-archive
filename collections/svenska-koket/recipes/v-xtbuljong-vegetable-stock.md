## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 0.5 celery root
- 0.5 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 0.5 rutabaga
- 1 cauliflower head
- 1 leek
- 3 teaspoons fresh or 0.25 liter canned peas
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 3 liters water
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)

## Directions

1. Braise the roots and vegetables in the usual way, cut them into pieces and place them along with the salt and butter in the cold water to cook slowly with a tight lid for 4–5 hours.
2. Strain the stock, taste and adjust seasoning, and color if desired with a little Japanese soy sauce.
3. Serve with rice or egg pasta, use for julienne soup, clear stock, or purée.
