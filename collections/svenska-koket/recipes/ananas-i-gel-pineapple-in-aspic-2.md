## Ingredients

- 20 gr. gelatin (about 12 sheets)
- pineapple juice
- 1 liter water
- juice of 1 lemon
- 225 gr. sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 dcl. white wine
- 1 can pineapple (about 750 gr.)

## Directions

1. Open the pineapple can first, carefully reserve the juice, and cut the pineapple into beautiful slices.
2. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 924 and when it is clear, lay the pineapple slices in the mold in the same way as described for grapes in the same recipe.
