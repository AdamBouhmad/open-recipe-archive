## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram dried apples, pears or apricots
- 5 dcl. water
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 teaspoon potato flour (5 gr.)
- lemon zest or lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the dried fruit in several changes of lukewarm water and let it soak for a few hours in the water in which it is to be cooked.
2. Put it on with the sugar and let it cook until soft, then press the fruit through a sieve.
3. Thicken the purée with the potato flour, dissolved in 1 tablespoon cold water, and let it boil for 2 minutes.
4. Season the sauce and add a little lemon zest or lemon juice if desired.
5. Serve warm or cold.
