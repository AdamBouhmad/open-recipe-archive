## Ingredients

- 1 liter loose, good apples
- 50 gr. sugar
- Dough: 50 gr. butter
- 50 gr. flour
- 4 dcl. boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest or 10 sweet almonds
- 3 eggs
- For the form: 1 tablespoon melted butter (20 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The apples are rinsed, dried, peeled, halved, freed from the cores and cut into thin slices, which are layered alternately with the sugar in a bowl and left to stand for about 1 hour.
2. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the milk is added, and the mixture is allowed to boil for 5 minutes, after which the sugar and lemon zest or almond are added, and the mixture is allowed to cool.
3. Then the eggs are added, one at a time, and the mixture is stirred for 10 minutes, after which the apple slices are added and the mass is stirred gently.
4. A baking sheet or pan is greased with the butter mixed with flour, and sprinkled with crushed rusks.
5. The mass is poured in, and the cake is baked in good oven heat for about 3/4 hour, or until it has risen and the apples feel soft, which is tested with a whisk.
6. The cake is immediately turned out, sprinkled with sugar and served warm.
