## Ingredients

- 60–70 g finely crushed rusks
- 4 dcl milk
- 1/2 litre blood
- 2 msk syrup
- 3 msk melted butter (60 g)
- 1 msk salt (15 g)
- 1/2 tsk white pepper
- 1 tsk ginger
- 1/2 tsk finely ground marjoram
- 1 finely chopped red onion (optional)
- 1/2 msk butter for the onion (optional)
- 3 msk butter (60 g) for the pan

## Directions

1. Soak the rusks in milk for 1 hour to swell.
2. Strain the blood through a fine sieve, whisk well and mix with the rusk mixture.
3. Add the syrup, melted butter and spices together with the onion if desired, and stir the batter well.
4. Heat a pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it.
5. Stir the batter well and fry thin pancakes from it, which are placed on a warm serving dish.
6. Serve with melted butter and lingonberry preserves.
