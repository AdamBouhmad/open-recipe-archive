## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- water, salt
- 2 1/2 parts water
- 30 grams butter
- 125 grams flour
- 4 eggs
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 kg cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cook them soft and press them through a puree press.
2. Boil water and butter, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook until it releases from the pan.
3. When most of the heat has passed, stir in the eggs one at a time and the pressed potatoes, and season the batter with sugar and salt.
4. Form small balls with a spoon; these are cooked golden brown in cream.
5. Place them on gray paper and let them drain.
