## Ingredients

- 2 capercaillie
- 1 hg. lard
- 3–4 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 5 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The capercaillie hen has lighter meat and is considered finer than the cock.
2. They are treated and prepared as black grouse but are roasted in 1–1½ hours.
3. The larding can advantageously be omitted, and the birds are instead covered with slices of lard when they are trussed.
4. Cut them, garnish (see p. 300) and serve with reduced sauce, added with thick cream (see no. 429).
