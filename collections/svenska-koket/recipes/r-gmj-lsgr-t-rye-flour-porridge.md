## Ingredients

- 2 liters water
- 5 cups rye flour
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with the salt.
2. Whisk in the flour carefully so that the porridge does not become lumpy.
3. Let it cook with frequent stirring for approximately 1.5 hours.
