## Ingredients

- 25 g sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- rice grits
- milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1/2 kg butter for frying

## Directions

1. Scald the rice grits and cook them in milk until soft.
2. Beat the porridge, and once most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg, grated almonds and lemon zest.
3. Form small round balls with a spoon, turn them in egg and crushed rusks and fry them golden brown in butter.
