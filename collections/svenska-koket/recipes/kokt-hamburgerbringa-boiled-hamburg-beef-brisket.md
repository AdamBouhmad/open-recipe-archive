## Ingredients

- 3 kg. salted and smoked beef brisket
- water

## Directions

1. Brush the brisket well with lukewarm water, crack it and let it soak in plenty of cold water for approximately 12 hours.
2. Place it in cold water, bring it quickly to a boil, skim well and cook as in recipe 497.
3. Let the brisket remain in its broth for 1/2 hour before serving, as it will thereby become more tender.
4. Serve, cut into even, beautiful slices, with vegetables, vegetable pastries and some of the broth, mixed with a little good broth, or also with mixed meat dishes.
5. The finest is considered cold Hamburg brisket. It is then allowed to cool in the broth, cut into thin slices, which are arranged on a base of dark gelée and garnished with gelée in molds or gelée croutons and parsley. Serve with potato or vegetable salad.
