## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Place the eggs and egg yolk in a metal bowl, season lightly and beat with a fork until well blended.
2. Heat the omelet pan, melt the butter in it but do not let it brown, pour in the eggs and stir quickly with a broad fork so that the entire mixture is evenly heated.
3. When the omelet begins to set, gently insert a fork back and forth through it and shake the pan so that the omelet does not stick to the bottom or form a hard crust.
4. When the omelet is set, shape it with a fork and place it quickly over the heat again, then turn it out onto a warm dish.
5. Make a slit in the center of the omelet and fill it with some stewed filling.
6. The stewed filling can also be added just before the eggs are fully set, after which the omelet is shaped and turned out onto a dish.
