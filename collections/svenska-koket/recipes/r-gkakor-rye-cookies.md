## Ingredients

- 400 gr. butter
- 200 gr. caster sugar
- 320 gr. wheat flour
- 300 gr. rye sifted flour
- 2 tsp. baking powder

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, and sift the flour with the baking powder.
2. Work all ingredients well together on the baking board, and let the dough set to harden.
3. Roll it out and prick it, and with a measure cut out round cookies, with a small cabbage-shaped piece at one side.
4. Bake the cookies golden brown in not too strong oven heat.
