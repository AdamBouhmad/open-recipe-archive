## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 2½ liters broth
- 1 knife-tip celery seed
- ½ tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- white pepper
- salt
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and cook in 1 liter of the broth until soft, then pass through a sieve.
2. Fry together butter and flour, thin with the purée and the remaining broth, and let the soup cook 10 minutes.
3. Season, whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl, and pour in the soup with vigorous whisking.
