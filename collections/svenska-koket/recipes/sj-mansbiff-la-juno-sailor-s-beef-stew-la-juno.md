## Ingredients

- 750 g beef chuck or cut beef
- 1 veal kidney
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 2-3 red onions
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 hg cooked ham
- 1 liter potatoes
- 4 dcl beef broth
- 3 dcl pilsener beer

## Directions

1. Treat the meat as for beef (see recipe no. 483).
2. Cut the veal kidney into slices and sprinkle these along with the beef patties with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Cut the ham into slices, which are layered alternately with the remaining meat and potatoes.
4. Prepare the beef as in the preceding recipe. Only pilsener can be used but the flavor will be milder if it is mixed with broth.
