## Ingredients

- 1 dcl crushed grain
- 2 liters water
- 1 liter fresh, tart apples
- 1-1.5 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 dcl thick cream
- 1 tablespoon gooseberry jam for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the grain and place in cold water and cook with a tight lid.
2. Rinse the apples, dry them, cut into wedges and remove the cores.
3. Add them to the soup when it has cooked 1/2 hour, and let them cook until they fall apart, then strain the soup.
4. Add the sugar and let the soup come to a boil.
5. Beat the egg yolks and cream together in a soup bowl and whisk until light, then pour the soup in while stirring constantly.
6. Add the gooseberry jam to the soup and serve warm with biscuits.
