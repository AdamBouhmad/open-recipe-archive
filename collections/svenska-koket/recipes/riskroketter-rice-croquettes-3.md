## Ingredients

- 200 grams rice grains
- 1.5 liters milk
- 1 egg
- 25 grams sweet almond
- 4 bitter almonds
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- 1 egg for breading
- 0.5 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 0.5 kilogram lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice grains and cook them in the milk until soft.
2. Drain the porridge, and when most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg, the grated almond and lemon peel.
3. With a spoon form small round balls, turn them in egg and crushed rusks and fry golden brown in lard.
