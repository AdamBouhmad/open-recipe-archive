## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms cooked, finely chopped meat
- 1 hectogram boneless beef
- 1 hectogram boneless veal
- 1/2 hectogram lard
- 1 egg
- 1.5 cups cream milk
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 2-3 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Drive the beef, veal and lard three times through a meat grinder and mix afterwards with the egg, cream milk, the onion fried in 1 teaspoon butter and the seasonings to make a regular meat forcemeat. Add the cooked and finely chopped meat, and mix everything well together.
2. Form small cutlets in the shape of hearts from the forcemeat, which are quickly fried in butter. If desired, the cutlets may be brushed with egg and sprinkled with crushed hardtack before frying.
3. Serve with brown sauce.
