## Ingredients

- Beets
- salt
- sugar
- raw vinegar
- caraway (optional)

## Directions

1. Do not rinse the beets, only rub them with a rough towel.
2. Place in cold water with a little salt and let them cook slowly until tender, which takes 3-4 hours.
3. Drain the water immediately and let the beets cool.
4. Peel them and cut into thin slices.
5. Layer them with sugar in a pot.
6. Pour raw vinegar over and it should stand well above the beets.
7. Do not cover the pot, as the beets will easily mold.
8. If desired, add a little caraway.
9. Alternatively, beets can be advantageously roasted in the oven like potatoes and then treated as above.
