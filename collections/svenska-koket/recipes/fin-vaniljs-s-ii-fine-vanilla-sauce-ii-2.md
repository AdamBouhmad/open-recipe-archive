## Ingredients

- 6 parts heavy cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 5-6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cream is boiled with the vanilla pod and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, after which it is strained through a fine sieve.
2. It is poured back into the pan, and the egg yolks are whisked in one at a time.
3. The pan is placed over the heat again, and the sauce simmers while whisking until it becomes adequately thick.
4. Whisking is continued until the sauce is cold.
