## Ingredients

- 2 kg mutton roast (from the loin)
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 2 deciliters vinegar
- 1 deciliter water
- 1 deciliter white wine
- 3 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 small red onion, in slices

## Directions

1. The roast should have hung so that it is tender. The bone and the thin meat are removed and the roast is larded (see page 232) with the lard strips, dipped in a mixture of the salt, pepper and ginger, and placed in a deep dish.
2. For the marinade, all the ingredients are mixed well, whereupon it is poured over the meat, which is then allowed to stand 3-4 days, during which it is turned 2 times daily.
3. The marinade is brought to a boil with so much water that it stands level with the meat. The roast is placed in and allowed to cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until it is tender, or approximately 3 hours.
4. It is removed and allowed to cool, cut into thin slices and garnished with stuffed eggs (see no. 8) as well as green salad or parsley and served with mayonnaise.
