## Ingredients

- 1 liter large, beautiful clusters of red or white currants
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (128 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the clusters in a sieve, rinse with cold water, and let drain on a cloth.
2. Whip the egg whites until light foam forms.
3. Dip the currant clusters first in the egg white, then in the sugar, until evenly glazed, arrange on a sieve, and let stand in gentle oven heat until dry.
4. Serve as a dessert or for a buffet.
