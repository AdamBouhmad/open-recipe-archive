## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 200 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 50 gr. sugar
- 2 liters blueberries
- 1½ cups caster sugar

## Directions

1. Work all ingredients together on the work surface for the shortcrust pastry.
2. Line a fireproof form with this dough.
3. Clean and rinse the blueberries and place them in the form, layered alternately with the sugar.
4. Place a lid of dough over the berries.
5. Bake the pie in moderate oven heat.
6. Serve cold with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
