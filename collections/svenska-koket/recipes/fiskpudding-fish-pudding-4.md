## Ingredients

- 1 bg butter
- 1 bg flour
- 5 parts unskimmed milk
- 1/2 liter fish (cooked and finely chopped)
- 4 eggs
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for mold

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and flour together, and add the milk while stirring constantly, so that the mixture does not become lumpy.
2. Let it cook for 10 minutes, then remove to cool.
3. Add the finely chopped fish, together with the spices and egg yolks.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
