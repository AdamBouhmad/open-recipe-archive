## Ingredients

- 75 gr. sötmandel (sweet almonds)
- 2 hg. spanska ell. hasselnötter (Spanish or hazelnut)
- 1 helt ägg (1 whole egg)
- 2 äggulor (2 egg yolks)
- 2 hg. strösocker (granulated sugar)
- 6 blad gelatin (gelatin leaves)
- 3 msk. varmt vatten, till gelatinet (warm water for the gelatin)
- 4 dcl. tjock grädde (thick cream)

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and dry them in the oven.
2. Peel the nuts and chop them finely together with the almonds.
3. Whisk the whole egg and egg yolks well in a bowl.
4. Place the sugar in a buttered and slightly warm pan and stir with a wooden spoon over heat until melted.
5. Immediately add the almonds and nuts to the melted sugar, let it come to a boil, and pour under stirring onto the whisked eggs.
6. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in the warm water and add it.
7. Stir the mixture until it is cold, then fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
8. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours.
9. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and unmold onto a flat glass dish with parchment paper.
10. Garnish with candied nuts and ladyfingers.
