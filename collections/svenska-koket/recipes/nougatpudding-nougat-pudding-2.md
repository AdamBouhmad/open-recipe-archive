## Ingredients

- 75 gr. sweet almonds
- 2 hg. Spanish or hazelnuts
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 hg. granulated sugar
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water for the gelatin
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and dry them in the oven. Peel the nuts and chop them finely together with the almonds. Whisk the whole egg and egg yolks well in a bowl. Place the sugar in a buttered and slightly warm frying pan and stir with a wooden spoon over the heat until melted. Add the almonds and nuts immediately and bring to a boil, then pour under stirring onto the beaten eggs. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in the hot water and add it. Stir the mixture until it is cold, then fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and unmold onto a flat glass dish with cake paper. Garnish with candied nuts and ladyfingers.
