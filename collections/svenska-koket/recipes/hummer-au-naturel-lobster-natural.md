## Ingredients

- 1 fresh lobster (4-5 hectograms)
- 2.5 liters water
- 1 hectogram coarse salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Mixed greens or parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Select a fresh lobster where the tail retains its full firmness and the claws are movable; the smoothest and heaviest is best
2. Bring water and coarse salt to a boil
3. Place the lobster head-first into the boiling water—it dies immediately—and boil for 15-20 minutes
4. Lay the lobster on its back to cool
5. Split the cooled lobster lengthwise into two halves just before serving
6. Carefully crack the claws, remove the intestinal tract and the stomach sac located at the head
7. Arrange the lobster on a platter, garnish with mixed greens or parsley
8. Serve with oil, vinegar and pepper
