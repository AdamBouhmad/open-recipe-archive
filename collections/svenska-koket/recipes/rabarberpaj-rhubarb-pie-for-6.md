## Ingredients

- For pastry: 200 gr. flour
- 200 gr. butter
- 25 gr. caster sugar
- 1 small egg
- For filling: 6 parts fresh rhubarb
- 3.5 parts caster sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter if it is salted.
2. Make an ordinary short pastry from the butter, flour, egg and sugar, and set aside to harden.
3. Roll out half of the dough and with it line a baking sheet or cast iron pan.
4. Peel the rhubarb, cut into pieces and place alternately with the sugar in the lined pan.
5. Roll out the remaining dough, cut out a lid, and cover the pie with it.
6. Bake in good oven heat for 0.75-1 hour, turn out and serve cold or warm.
