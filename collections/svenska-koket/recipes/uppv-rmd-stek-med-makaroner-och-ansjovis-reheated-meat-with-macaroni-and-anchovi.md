## Ingredients

- 3 hg. fried meat (leftovers)
- 1 hg. macaroni
- 1 lit. water
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 2 dcl. meat gravy
- 2 dcl. broth
- 1 anchovy or 2 tsk. anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small even pieces.
2. Break the macaroni into pieces, place in boiling salted water and let cook 15-20 minutes or until tender.
3. Rinse over with cold water, let drain and cut into small pieces.
4. Let the broth and meat gravy come to a boil, add the meat and macaroni and warm in the sauce without boiling.
5. Season the dish with a cleaned and strained anchovy or a little anchovy sauce and serve as a breakfast dish.
