## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 grams)
- 6 decilitres meat stock
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2-3 teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the whisked egg yolks and let the sauce simmer under whisking.
3. Season with spices and lemon juice and serve over the white meat.
