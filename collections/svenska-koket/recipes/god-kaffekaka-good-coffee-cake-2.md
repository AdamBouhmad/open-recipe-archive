## Ingredients

- 165 grams flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 160 grams sugar
- 45 grams melted butter
- 1.5 deciliters cream or milk
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 20 sweet and 5 bitter almonds
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Combine the flour with baking powder.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar together, add the melted butter and cream or milk, mix in the flour and baking powder.
3. Season with lemon zest or ground almonds.
4. Pour into a buttered and crumb-lined cake pan and bake in a not too hot oven.
