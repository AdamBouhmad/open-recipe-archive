## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter peas
- 8 parts water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the peas, rinse them, place in cold, soft water (see page 338) and let them cook slowly until they are completely soft, which takes 2 1/2-3 hours.
2. Add warm water during cooking if needed.
3. Season with salt, and finally stir in the butter.
