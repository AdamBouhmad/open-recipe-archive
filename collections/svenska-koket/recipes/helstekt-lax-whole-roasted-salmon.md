## Ingredients

- 4 kg salmon
- 4 msk salt
- parsley and lemon for garnish

## Directions

1. Brush, trim, and wash the salmon thoroughly, then scale it.
2. Rub it with the salt and allow it to rest for a while.
3. Heat butter in a long pan, place the salmon in it, and brown it in a good oven heat.
4. Add boiling water and allow the salmon to finish cooking, basting it occasionally with its sauce.
5. Place it on the serving dish, pour the sauce over it, and garnish with parsley and lemon.
