## Ingredients

- 2 kg apples, cut into pieces
- 1 liter water
- 750 gr crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Tart apples of the same variety are washed, dried, cut into pieces, freed from wormy and damaged parts and weighed.
2. They are placed in cold water and cooked until they are completely soft, then passed through a hair sieve.
3. The sauce is then cooked with sugar while stirring until it is of the right consistency, or 1/2-1 hour.
4. Poured while hot into warm jars rinsed with cognac, preferably not larger than what can be used at once.
5. When the sauce is cold, it is covered with a thin layer of melted paraffin or mutton fat, or sprinkled with 1 tbsp cognac mixed with 1/4 gram salicylic acid.
