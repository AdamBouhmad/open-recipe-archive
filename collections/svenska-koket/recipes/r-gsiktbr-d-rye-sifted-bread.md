## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg fine rye sifted flour
- 0.5 kg wheat flour
- 1 tsk salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 70 gr pressed yeast
- 1 hg butter or margarine
- 2.5 parts syrup
- 2 msk crushed fennel
- 1 msk crushed anise
- 2 msk finely cut orange peel

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Place the sifted rye flour in a bowl, add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and stir in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough smooth and glossy.
3. Place in a warm spot to rise to double size.
4. Add the well-washed and stirred butter, syrup, the crushed spices and the finely chopped orange peel, and work in the wheat flour until the dough becomes quite firm.
5. Let it rise again.
6. Finally work the dough on the baking board and shape into oblong buns, place on heated and floured cloths to rise.
7. When well risen, place on buttered sheets, brush with cold water and bake in a warm oven.
8. During baking, brush the buns twice with hot water, likewise when the finished buns are removed from the oven.
9. Cover them well so that the crust does not become too hard.
