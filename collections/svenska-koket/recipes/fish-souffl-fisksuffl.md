## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked fish (1/2 kg. pike)
- 80 gr. butter
- 80 gr. flour
- 5 deciliter boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoon crushed bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Grate the cooked fish finely like rice grains with a fork.
2. Melt butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the batter for 10 minutes.
4. Mix in the fish together with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Taste the mixture, pour into a buttered and bread crumb-lined pie mold and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
6. If using a sheet metal mold, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour and turn out.
7. Serve the soufflé immediately after taking it from the oven as a side course with crayfish or lobster sauce, hollandaise or lemon sauce.
8. Instead of fish, veal sweetbread, poultry or game may be used.
