## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg chestnuts
- 1/2 kg cream
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 3-4 deciliters broth
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a cross 1 1/2 cm deep into the pointed end of the chestnuts.
2. Boil them for 2 minutes in hot cream so the shells will loosen.
3. Remove both the outer and inner shells while the chestnuts are warm.
4. Melt the butter in a pot, add the chestnuts, sprinkle with salt and sugar.
5. Add enough broth so it just covers them and let cook slowly with a lid until almost soft.
6. Remove the lid and reduce the broth until it becomes thick as syrup.
7. Gently shake the chestnuts in it so they become evenly glazed.
8. Season with more salt and sugar and a little white pepper if desired.
9. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with cooked Brussels sprouts as a side dish.
