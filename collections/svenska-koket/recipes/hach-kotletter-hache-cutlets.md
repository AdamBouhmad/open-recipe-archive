## Ingredients

- 4 cups cooked, chopped meat
- 2 cups cooked, chopped potato
- 2 small red onions
- 1-1.5 cups meat broth or milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Mix the meat, potato and the blanched and finely chopped onion well together. Add the meat broth together with salt and pepper.
2. Form oblong cutlets from the mixture, which are fried golden brown in butter.
3. Serve with stewed carrots.
