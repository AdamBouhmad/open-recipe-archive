## Ingredients

- 1 liter strong, dark bouillon
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 bunch parsley
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- meat stock
- salt
- sugar (optional)
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Put the bouillon on to cook together with the onion, parsley and carrot and let it cook until it has acquired a good, strong flavor.
2. Strain it and skim carefully.
3. Fry butter and flour together, add the bouillon, and let the sauce come to a good boil.
4. Then move it to low heat to simmer for 1.5 hours.
5. Add the meat stock according to the roast for which the sauce is to be served, and let the sauce come to another boil, then taste and adjust and serve.
