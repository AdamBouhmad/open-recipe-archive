## Ingredients

- 6 eggs (= 360 gr.)
- 360 gr. butter
- 360 gr. granulated sugar
- 360 gr. wheat flour
- red currant jelly for filling
- 1 hg. sifted sugar
- 2 tbsp. water

## Directions

1. The eggs and sugar are stirred well.
2. The butter is melted, allowed to cool and is stirred until it becomes white, then mixed into the egg mixture.
3. Finally the flour is added.
4. The batter is spread out quite thinly on a well-buttered baking sheet and placed in the oven to bake.
5. When it is baked, it is cut in half, and one half is brushed with jelly.
6. The other half is then lifted onto the first, the cake is glazed and cut into diagonal squares.
