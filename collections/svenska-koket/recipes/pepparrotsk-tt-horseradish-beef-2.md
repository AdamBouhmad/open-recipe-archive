## Ingredients

- 2 kg beef (rib, brisket, or marrow pipe)
- 3 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter meat broth or milk
- 3-4 tablespoons grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water
2. Place it in boiling water, skim, salt, and let it simmer slowly with a tightly fitting lid for 2-3 hours
3. Prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
4. Do not let the horseradish cook with the sauce, as this would make it taste bitter
5. Add the grated horseradish to the finished sauce
