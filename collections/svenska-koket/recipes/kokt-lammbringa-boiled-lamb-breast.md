## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg lamb breast
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 grams)
- 4 dill or parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Crack the breast well and dry it with a cloth.
2. Wring it out in hot water, place it in boiling water, skim well, salt it, and add the dill or parsley.
3. Let it cook slowly with a tightly closed lid for 1 1/2-2 hours.
4. Remove it and cut into beautiful, even pieces, which are laid on a warm platter and garnished with dill or parsley.
5. Strain the stock, skim it well, and use it for sauce and for simpler soups.
6. Serve with dill sauce or with caper or light mushroom sauce. In the former case cook the breast with dill, in the latter case with parsley.
