## Ingredients

- 650 grams oranges
- 1 lemon
- 650 grams powdered sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Dry the oranges and lemon well with a damp cloth and peel them.
2. Carefully remove all the white from the peel.
3. Cut the peel into fine strips and the fruit flesh into thin slices, removing all seeds.
4. Place everything in the cold water and let it stand 24 hours.
5. Place it on the fire and let it cook 1 hour.
6. Then add the sugar, and let the marmalade cook an additional 1 hour, or until it is clear and fairly thick.
7. Skim it carefully during cooking.
8. When most of the heat has passed, pour it into warm glass jars.
