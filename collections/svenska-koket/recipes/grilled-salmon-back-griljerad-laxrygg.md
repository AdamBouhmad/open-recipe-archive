## Ingredients

- approximately 1 1/2 kg salted salmon back
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 4 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon back (or head if desired) into pieces and rinse very thoroughly, then let them soak in milk overnight.
2. Wrap the pieces in cloth, dip them in egg whisked with cream, turn them in crushed rusks, and fry them to a beautiful brown color in butter.
3. If less fatty salmon is desired, place the pieces on absorbent paper as they are fried.
4. Arrange on a warm platter, garnish with parsley, and serve with currant sauce and potatoes or with creamed potatoes.
