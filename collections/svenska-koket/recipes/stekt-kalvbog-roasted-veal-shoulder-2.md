## Ingredients

- 2 kg. veal shoulder
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 4 fresh or 1/2 hg. dried apples
- 3 tbsp. butter
- 3/4 liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water. The shoulder blade is carefully removed and cut into pieces; then broth is cooked to dilute the roast with.
2. The meat is rubbed with the seasonings and filled with the peeled and sliced fresh apples or with the well-rinsed dried apple rings and bound together.
3. It is then roasted in an oven or pot (see no. 498 or 500). Served with boiled potatoes and strained sauce (see no. 429), mixed with cream.
