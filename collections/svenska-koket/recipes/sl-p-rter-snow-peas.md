## Ingredients

- 3 liters tender snow peas
- water
- salt
- 100 grams butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the tender but fully mature pea pods well.
2. Place in boiling, salted water (short broth) and let simmer slowly until completely tender.
3. Remove and let drain.
4. Serve with clarified butter.
