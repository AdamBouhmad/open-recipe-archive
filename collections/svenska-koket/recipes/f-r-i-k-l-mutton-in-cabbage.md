## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg mutton brisket or saddle
- 1 small head of cabbage
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 1/2 liters cold water

## Directions

1. Rinse the mutton with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into pieces.
2. Brown it lightly together with the coarsely cut cabbage.
3. Sprinkle flour over, add salt and pepper, and mix well.
4. Add the cold water and let the soup slowly cook for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
5. Carefully skim off all the fat, taste and adjust seasoning, then serve.
