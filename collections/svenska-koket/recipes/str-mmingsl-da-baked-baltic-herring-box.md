## Ingredients

- 24 Baltic herrings
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 12 anchovies
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g) for the dish
- 1 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs for the dish

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, bone them, scrape away the dark skin, rinse them well and wrap them in a fish cloth.
2. Sprinkle them inside and outside with salt and let them rest for a while.
3. Clean the anchovies, bone them and split them in half.
4. Grease a heat-resistant omelet dish with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Place an anchovy half in each herring, roll the herrings tightly and arrange them in the dish close together.
6. Place the butter in flakes on top; finally sprinkle the crushed breadcrumbs over them.
7. Place the dish in good oven heat, and let the herrings fry until they are nicely brown, about 25 minutes.
8. Serve for the smorgasbord or with fried potatoes as a breakfast dish.
