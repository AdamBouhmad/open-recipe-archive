## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 2 hectograms wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 egg
- 3 hectograms smoked ham
- 1 small red onion
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the potatoes, drain the water, and mash with a fork or press through a ricer and let cool.
2. Add the salt, egg and most of the flour (save some flour for baking).
3. Work the dough very little on the baking board and form into round cakes.
4. Peel the onion; cut the ham and onion into cubes and brown them and season.
5. Make a hole in the middle of each cake and fill with a teaspoon of the browned ham, then firmly close the hole.
6. Place the cakes in boiling salted water and let them cook slowly until they float up, or about 10 minutes.
7. Serve the kroppkakor with clarified butter.
