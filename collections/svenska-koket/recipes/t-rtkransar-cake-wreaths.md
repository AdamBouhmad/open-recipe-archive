## Ingredients

- 1 kg. wheat dough
- 1/2 kg. butter
- pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Work the well-washed butter into the fine wheat dough, raised for the second time, with a rolling pin.
2. Roll out the dough to 1 cm thickness and cut out wreaths with a measure.
3. Dip in coarse sugar and bake in a hot oven.
