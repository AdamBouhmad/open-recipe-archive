## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. water
- 65 gr. lump sugar
- zest of 1 1/2 oranges
- juice of 3 oranges
- 2—3 tsp. Rhine or French wine
- 2 tsp. potato starch (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cook a light sugar syrup from the water and sugar, skimming well.
2. Wash and dry the oranges.
3. Grate the zest from 1 1/2 oranges and mix with the sugar syrup along with the pressed orange juice.
4. Add the wine, bring the sauce to a boil.
5. Season and dilute with a little water if desired.
6. Thicken the sauce as in recipe no. 460.
7. Serve warm or cold.
