## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp English mustard
- 3 dcl water
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp fine oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 dcl thick cream
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry butter, flour and mustard for 2 minutes.
2. Dilute the water gradually and let the sauce simmer with stirring for 10 minutes.
3. Pour it out and stir until cooled, then add the egg yolks one at a time and work the sauce with a spoon for about 1/2 hour while gradually adding the vinegar, oil and lemon juice with constant stirring.
4. Stir in the cream whipped to stiff peaks and season carefully with spices.
5. This sauce is used instead of true mayonnaise for those who do not tolerate oil.
