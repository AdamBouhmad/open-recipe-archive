## Ingredients

- 12 pork chops
- 1 lemon
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 cups finely crushed breadcrumbs (sifted)
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 3 tablespoons butter for cooking

## Directions

1. Trim the pork chops and pound them well, sprinkle with lemon juice and let them lie on each other for 1.5 hours.
2. Brush them with the beaten eggs and turn them in a mixture of the salt, white pepper, crushed breadcrumbs and parsley.
3. Heat the butter in a frying pan, place the chops in and brown well.
4. Place a lid on and let the chops cook through for about 1.5 hours.
5. Place them on a warm dish, whisk out the pan with a little boiling water, and pour the sauce over the chops.
6. If desired, a little lemon juice can be added to the sauce.
