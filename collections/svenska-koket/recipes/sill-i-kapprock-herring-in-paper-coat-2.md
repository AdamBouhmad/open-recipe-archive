## Ingredients

- 2 large salted herrings
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 red onions
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 1 sheet white writing paper
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 g) for frying

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times.
2. Remove the skin, cut off the head and tail.
3. Cut open along the back, remove the bones and innards, rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
4. Sprinkle the inside with pepper and add the peeled onion sliced thinly.
5. Reassemble so it maintains its shape.
6. Wrap each herring well with 1/2 sheet of paper, butter it with 2 tablespoons of butter.
7. Fry in butter until the paper is brown, about 15 minutes.
8. Serve in the paper with creamed potatoes as a breakfast dish.
