## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg small Portuguese onions
- 6 deciliters light broth
- 60 grams butter
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and place in the boiling broth together with butter and sugar and let cook until they are completely soft, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Remove them with a slotted spoon, and reduce the sauce to a jelly, lay the onions in it and glaze, while the saucepan is shaken gently.
3. Serve as the preceding dish.
