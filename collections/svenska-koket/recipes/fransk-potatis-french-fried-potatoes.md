## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters potatoes
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5 kilogram cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, rinse them, cut them into slices, strips or long rectangles and dry them well in a cloth.
2. Heat the cream, add the potatoes and let them cook until they are beautifully golden brown.
3. Remove them, let them drain on grey paper and place them on a serving dish, then sprinkle with a little salt.
