## Ingredients

- 8 lettuce heads
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 tsk mustard
- 1-2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the salad, rinse and cut into large pieces
2. Hard boil the eggs and peel; separate yolks and whites
3. When the yolks have cooled, crush them together with the raw egg yolk, sugar, mustard and white pepper
4. Add the vinegar and cream gradually, so that the sauce does not become lumpy
5. Mix the salad well with this sauce and garnish with the chopped egg whites
