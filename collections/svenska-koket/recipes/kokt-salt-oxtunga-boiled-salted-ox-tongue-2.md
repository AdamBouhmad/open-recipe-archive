## Ingredients

- 1 salt ox tongue
- water

## Directions

1. The tongue, which should be lightly salted, is placed in chilled water for a couple of hours
2. Place in cold water, skim, and boil for 15 minutes at 100°
3. Cool the broth to 82°-83° and keep the tongue at this temperature for 3 1/2 hours, or until the skin can easily be removed
4. Place in a stone crock with flat bottom, pour the broth over, and let the tongue cool in it—it becomes juicier if cooled in the broth
5. If to be served warm, warm it in the broth after the skin is removed
6. If the tongue is very salty, it should soak in water for about 12 hours
