## Ingredients

- 8 hg. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 liter skim milk or water
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar (8 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. melted butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and set aside a little of it for finishing.
2. Heat the milk or water to about 40 degrees and mix into the flour together with the yeast, dissolved with salt and sugar, and the butter.
3. Work the dough until it becomes smooth and glossy and quite firm.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
5. Turn the dough out onto a floured baking board, work it, and divide it into 5 parts and divide each fifth thereafter into 6 parts; these are shaped into French breads, which are placed on well-buttered baking sheets to rise again.
6. Brush the breads with milk and bake in a warm oven.
7. The butter and sugar can be omitted if the breads are used the same day.
8. The dough can be formed into oblong breads which are brushed with milk. From the dough, a twist is made which is placed lengthwise along the bread. The rolls are then brushed with egg, sprinkled with poppy seeds, and allowed to rise, after which they are again brushed with milk before being placed in the oven.
9. The dough can also be placed in two buttered bread pans and allowed to rise 2-2½ hours. Bake in a very warm oven for about 1 hour. Brush with hot water when removed.
