## Ingredients

- 2 kg. lamb roast (the loin)
- 1 1/2 hg. lard
- 2 teaspoon salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoon butter (60 gr.)
- 3/4 liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. The roast, which should have hung to become tender, is dried, pounded, larded, and the leg is removed.
2. It is larded mostly on the outer leg and back, broken to facilitate carving, and then the roast is rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. A long pan is heated, the butter is browned in it along with some of the lard, the roast is placed in with the inner leg up and browned for about 1/2 hour in a warm oven, basting frequently with the fat in the pan.
4. It is thinned with the boiling broth or water and allowed to roast until tender, or about 1 1/2-2 hours, basting every 10 minutes.
5. Place on a warm serving platter and carve at the table.
6. Serve with white beans and vegetables or vegetable patties, and with the strained, skimmed meat gravy or with thickened sauce.
