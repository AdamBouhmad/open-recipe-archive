## Ingredients

- 20 fried sprats
- 1 teaspoon allspice berries
- 4 bay leaves
- 1 chopped red onion
- 2 cups vinegar
- 3 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Place the fried, cold sprats in a jar, alternating with the spices.
2. Mix the vinegar with a little sugar and pour over the sprats.
3. The liquid should cover them well.
