## Ingredients

- 850 gr. prima wheat flour
- 1/2 liter unskimmed milk
- 40 gr. compressed yeast
- 75 gr. sugar
- 2 hg. butter
- 20 gr. almonds, of which 5 bitter
- 1 egg for brushing
- 1 hg. sweet almonds for filling
- 5 bitter almonds for filling
- 2 hg. powdered sugar for filling
- 1/2-1 tablespoon cold water for filling

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as in recipe no. 950 and it should be quite loose.
2. When it has risen for the second time, work it well on the bakeboard and divide it into 14-16 parts, which are formed into round buns and placed on well-buttered baking sheets.
3. Brush with beaten egg and let rise quickly, preferably over water vapor.
4. Bake the buns in a very hot oven.
5. When cool, cut a lid in each bun, remove some of the contents, and fill the buns with almond filling.
6. For the almond filling: blanch the almonds, dry them, pass through an almond mill and pound with sugar and a little water until smooth and pliable.
7. If desired, the almond filling can be mixed with 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream, beaten to stiff peaks, before filling the buns.
8. The almond filling can be stored several days if rolled in powdered sugar and kept well covered in a cool place.
