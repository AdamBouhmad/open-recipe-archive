## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 celeriac root
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Treat the Jerusalem artichokes and celeriac as in recipe no. 269.
2. Add to cold water with salt and let cook until completely tender, then strain.
3. Brown 2 tablespoons of butter with flour for 2 minutes; thin the purée and cream gradually, and let the soup cook 5 minutes, then season with spices.
4. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl, add the remaining butter and keep the soup warm while whisking.
5. Serve with toasted bread or cheese croûtons.
