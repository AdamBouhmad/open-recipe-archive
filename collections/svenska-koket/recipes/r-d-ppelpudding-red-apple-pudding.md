## Ingredients

- 2 liters apples
- peel of 1 1/2 lemons
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 liter water
- 25 grams red gelatin

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into wedges, and remove the cores.
2. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water; cook the apples in this syrup together with lemon peel and lemon juice until they fall apart, but do not mash completely.
3. Dissolve the gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir it in; pour everything into a water-rinsed mold and set aside to congeal.
4. Dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water.
5. Turn out the pudding and serve with vanilla sauce or cream.
6. Preferably make this the day before.
