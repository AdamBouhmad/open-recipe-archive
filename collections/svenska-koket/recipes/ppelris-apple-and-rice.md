## Ingredients

- For the apple compote: 150 grams sugar, 4 deciliters water, 1 liter good, tart apples, juice of 1/2 lemon or 1 tablespoon vinegar
- For the rice: 1.5 deciliters Carolina rice, 1.5 liters water
- For the meringue: 2 large egg whites, 70 grams caster sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the apple compote as in recipe 820.
2. Treat and cook the rice as Indian rice (see recipe 795), but omit the salt.
3. When the rice has been removed from the oven, mix it with the apple liquid.
4. Arrange the rice in a ring on a heat-resistant plate, or preferably a silver plate.
5. Arrange the compote in a pyramid inside the ring of rice.
6. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form, gently fold in the sifted caster sugar.
7. Pipe the meringue mixture through a fluted piping tube over the apples.
8. Let the arrangement stand in a gentle oven heat until the meringue is half-dry and has a light golden color.
9. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
