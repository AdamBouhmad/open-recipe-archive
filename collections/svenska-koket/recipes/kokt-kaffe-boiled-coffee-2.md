## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 75 grams (10 tablespoons) ground coffee of medium strength
- a small piece of eggwhite

## Directions

1. Boil the water.
2. The ground coffee, which should have the coarseness of small oat grains, is added together with the eggwhite, and the coffee is allowed to boil very slowly for 3-5 minutes.
3. A little cold water is then poured into the pan, which is set to the side of the stove, so that the grounds can settle.
4. If the coffee is to be served in a pot, this should first be warmed with a little hot water.
