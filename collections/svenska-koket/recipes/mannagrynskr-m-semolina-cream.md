## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. semolina (114 gr.)
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 lit. milk
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 1 1/2 msk. sugar (23 gr.)
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 2–3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Treat the semolina and cook the porridge as in the previous recipe, but it should cook for about 3/4 hour.
2. When the semolina is soft, add the remaining butter, seasonings, lemon zest and the whisked egg yolks, then let the cream simmer.
3. Pour into a mold and garnish, when it is cold, with jam or jelly.
4. Serve with cream, milk or fruit sauce.
