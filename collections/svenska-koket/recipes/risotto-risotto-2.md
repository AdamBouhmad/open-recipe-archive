## Ingredients

- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 150 gr. butter
- 180 gr. Carolina rice grains
- 1 gr. crushed saffron
- 1/2 liter good stock
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 hectogram smoked ham
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, chop it finely and fry it well in 1 hectogram of the butter in a saucepan.
2. Add the rinsed rice grains together with the finely crushed saffron and stir over low heat until they are well mixed with the butter.
3. Then add the stock, little by little, while stirring the grains frequently with a wooden spoon.
4. Let the rice grains then cook slowly with a lid, stirring them often, until the grains are soft and begin to break apart.
5. Season the dish with the remaining butter, Parmesan cheese and the ham cut into fine cubes, and salt.
6. Serve as a breakfast or intermediate course.
