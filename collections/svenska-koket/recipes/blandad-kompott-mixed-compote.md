## Ingredients

- Fruits, cooked separately according to the above descriptions
- Mixed, reduced sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Arrange fruits cooked separately in an attractive manner in a compote bowl and cover with the mixed, reduced sugar syrup.
2. Serve with frozen or whipped cream.
