## Ingredients

- 1 kg. sik or gös (whitefish)
- 3 tbsp. salt
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 can lobster (1 liter)
- 1 1/2 liters of fish stock
- 15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tbsp. cold water
- 4 tbsp. good white wine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 truffle
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scale, gut, rinse well and dry the fish with a fish towel.
2. Rub the fish with half the salt and let it rest for 1/2 hour, then dry it again.
3. Place it in cold water with the remaining salt and spices, and let it cook slowly until done, about 15 minutes.
4. Remove immediately, peel off the skin and divide the fish into 2 fillets.
5. Place the bone in the stock and let it cook an additional 20 minutes.
6. Cut the fish into beautiful pieces and the lobster likewise.
7. Prepare the gelée from the fish stock as in recipe no. 181.
8. When cold, add the wine.
9. Prepare the dish as in the previous recipe and serve at the smörgåsbord.
