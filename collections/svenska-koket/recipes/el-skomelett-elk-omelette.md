## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 4 decilitres creamed milk
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped leek
- 300 grams cured side pork

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with salt and flour for 10 minutes, then add the milk and leek.
2. Cut the pork into thin slices and brown lightly in a hot pan.
3. Remove the pork, pour off the fat, and wipe the pan thoroughly with white paper so that no salt remains.
4. Pour the fat back into the pan and heat it over the fire. Pour in the egg mixture and lay the pork on top.
5. When the omelette is cooked on one side, turn it onto a plate, slide it back into the pan, and cook on the other side.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish. Smoked ham can also be used.
