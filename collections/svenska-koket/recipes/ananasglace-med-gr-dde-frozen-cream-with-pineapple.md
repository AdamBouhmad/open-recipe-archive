## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 2 parts water
- 110 g sugar
- 0.5 liter thick cream
- 1 small can pineapple

## Directions

1. The egg yolks, water and sugar are whisked over the heat until the cream thickens, then the pan is lifted off the heat.
2. Whisking is continued until the mixture is cold.
3. The cream, whipped to stiff peaks, is then whisked in together with half of the pineapple, cut into small pieces, and the pineapple syrup.
4. The mixture is not frozen too hard, is arranged on a jelly dish and is garnished with the remaining pineapple, cut into slices, and small biscuits.
