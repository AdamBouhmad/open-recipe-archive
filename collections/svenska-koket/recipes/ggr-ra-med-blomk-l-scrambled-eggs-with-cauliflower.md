## Ingredients

- 1 small head of cauliflower
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt
- 6 eggs
- 4 dcl. boiling milk
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water for about 1 hour.
2. Place it in boiling salted water and cook until tender, then remove it, let it drain in a sieve and divide into small pieces.
3. Whisk the eggs well with the seasonings, and add the liquid, which must be boiling, while whisking vigorously.
4. Butter a saucepan, pour in the egg mixture and cook in a water bath while stirring.
5. When it is half set, carefully stir in the cauliflower and let the scrambled eggs finish cooking.
6. Arrange with a flat spoon on a hot plate, garnish with parsley and serve as a main dish or with fried meat, fried fish, brined ham or sausage.
