## Ingredients

- 120 gr. fine butter
- 1/4 tsp. lemon juice
- 2 gr. table salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter just before serving and season, if desired, with salt and lemon juice.
2. If the butter is allowed to stand and clarify, it loses in flavor and becomes hard-melted.
3. If the butter is used for fried fish, let it cook until it has taken on a golden color and has a faint hazelnut aroma.
4. Then season it with lemon juice and pour it at once over the fish, which has been previously sprinkled with chopped parsley.
