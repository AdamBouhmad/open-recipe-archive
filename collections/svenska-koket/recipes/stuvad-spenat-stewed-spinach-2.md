## Ingredients

- 1 kg. spinach
- 4 liters water for blanching
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.)
- 2-3 deciliters cream, milk or bouillon
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 hard-boiled eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Treat and blanch the spinach as in the previous recipe, place in a colander, immediately pour over cold water, and carefully press out the water with a spoon.
2. Chop the spinach finely.
