## Ingredients

- 1 12-penny wheat bread
- 1 hectogram butter
- 1 egg white
- 1 hectogram grated cheese, sharp in flavor

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices, and on one side spread butter.
2. Beat the egg white to a light foam and mix with the grated cheese.
3. Spread the mass on the sandwiches.
4. Place them on a sheet and put in weak oven heat to get a nice golden brown color.
5. Serve with meat, fish, and vegetarian soups.
