## Ingredients

- 1⅔ kg boneless beef in one cohesive bundle
- ½ Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1½ hg lard
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- ½ tbsp salt
- 1 hg anchovy
- 2 tbsp butter
- ⅛ liter boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into thin slices, which are beaten well, but not so that they break apart.
2. Peel the onion and chop it finely together with the parsley, and with this sprinkle the meat slices.
3. Cut the lard into thin slices, which are sprinkled with pepper and salt and placed on top of the meat slices.
4. On top is placed cleaned and boned anchovy.
5. Roll the meat slices tightly together and bind them in two places with fine sailcloth twine (previously dipped in hot water) and place them close together in an iron roasting pan brushed with butter.
6. Thereafter set the rolls on good heat to brown.
7. Dilute with the boiling water and let them roast 1–1½ hours.
8. Strain the sauce, skim it and serve as jus.
