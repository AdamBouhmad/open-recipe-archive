## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 hg. salt salmon
- 1 liter skim milk
- 1 liter cooked, cold potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons flour (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. heavy cream
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the salmon, cut it into slices and let it soak in the skim milk for about 3 hours, take it out and wrap it in a cloth or white paper.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut them into slices and arrange them alternately with the salmon in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold.
3. Beat the eggs with the flour, pour the milk mixture and the egg mixture into the mold.
4. Sprinkle the pudding with parsley and bake in the oven for about 40 minutes.
5. Serve with melted butter or currant sauce.
