## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock of about 5 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- 1 1/2 kg chestnuts
- 3/4 liter broth
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Treat the turkey as in no. 605 and rub with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. Peel the chestnuts and let them lie in hot water until the inner thin skin can be removed with a cloth.
3. Place the peeled chestnuts in the broth together with the butter and seasonings and cook until tender but do not let them fall apart.
4. Drain in a colander and let cool.
5. Fill the turkey with the chestnuts, treat thereafter as in the preceding recipe and roast either in the oven or in a pot.
6. Carve it, garnish with small sausages, browned potatoes and green salad.
7. Serve with gravy or prepared sauce (see no. 429).
