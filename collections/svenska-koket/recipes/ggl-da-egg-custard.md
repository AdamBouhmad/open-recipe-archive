## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 cups milk
- sugar
- salt
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. The eggs are whisked together with the milk and seasonings, and the mixture is poured into a buttered baking dish or heatproof porcelain mold.
2. The egg custard is placed in a water bath in the oven to bake 20-30 minutes, or until completely set.
