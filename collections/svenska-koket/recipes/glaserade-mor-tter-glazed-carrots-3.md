## Ingredients

- 1 liter small carrots (carrots)
- 60 gr. butter
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 4 tsp. sugar (20 gr.)
- 4 dcl. boiling water
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Small pear-shaped carrots, so-called carrots, are best suited for this cooking method.
2. Clean, rinse and brush the carrots well, scrape neatly, rinse again, dry in a cloth and cut them in half lengthwise or in quarters, depending on their size.
3. Heat the butter in a saucepan, add the carrots, sprinkle with salt and sugar and fry them well in the butter.
4. Add the water and let it simmer with the lid on until the carrots are almost tender, then remove the lid and let the sauce reduce until it becomes thick as syrup.
5. Shake the carrots gently in the sauce until they become evenly glazed.
6. If desired, add the finely chopped parsley at the end.
7. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with cooked vegetables. If the carrots are large and old, they should be formed like oblong olives, put in cold, salted water, boil for 5 minutes, drain in a colander and then prepare as above.
