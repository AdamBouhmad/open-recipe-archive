## Ingredients

- Leftover mutton roast
- 3 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 small parsnip
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 portion cream

## Directions

1. Cut the finest meat from the roast into cubes
2. Crush the rest of the meat together with the bone
