## Ingredients

- 60 asparagus spears
- 1.5 liters water
- 35 g salt
- 100 g butter

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus, wash in cold water, and tie together in bundles with all tips pointing in one direction.
2. Place in boiling, salted water and cook for 15-20 minutes.
3. As soon as the asparagus is done, remove it immediately and drain.
4. Arrange on an asparagus dish, a napkin, or on a large slice of toasted bread dipped in the water in which the asparagus was cooked.
5. Serve with creamed butter.
