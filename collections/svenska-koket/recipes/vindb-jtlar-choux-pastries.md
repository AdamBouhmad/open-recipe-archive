## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 deciliters water
- 105 grams wheat flour
- 105 grams butter
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Bring water, butter and sugar to a boil
2. Whisk in the flour and let the batter cook until it releases from the pan
3. Once most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg yolks one at a time; finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
4. Press the batter through a pastry bag or corrugated piping cone onto a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet in the shape of swans or other figures
5. Bake in a hot oven
6. During baking, the oven door should be opened as infrequently as possible, as the draft will cause the pastries to collapse and become flat
7. If the bottom heat of the oven is too strong, use double baking sheets
