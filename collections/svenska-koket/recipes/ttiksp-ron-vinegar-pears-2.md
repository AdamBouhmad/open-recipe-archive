## Ingredients

- 3 kg. skalade päron
- 1 1/2 kg. strösocker
- 3 dcl. ättika
- 2 dcl. vitt vin

## Directions

1. Peel the pears with thin, even peels, split them if they are large, and remove the cores.
2. Scrape the stems well.
3. Layer the pears with the sugar in a jam pot.
4. Add the vinegar and wine, and let the pears cook slowly with a tight-fitting lid until they are soft and clear, or approx. 2 1/2 hours.
5. Skim during cooking as needed.
6. Lift out the pears with a slotted spoon onto a flat dish to cool.
7. Place them in a jar that has been rinsed with cognac.
8. Let the cooking liquid boil down without a lid until it is quite syrupy, then let it cool slightly and pour over the pears.
