## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 hg of the veal
- 50 g lard
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt, white pepper
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1 1/2–2 decilitres thick cream
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 3 decilitres broth
- 2 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal and cut it crosswise into 12 even slices, which are gently flattened.
2. Cut the lard into strips, dip them in a mixture of salt and pepper, and use them to lard the veal slices.
3. Roll them up and bind them in two places with fine twine, dipped in boiling water.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the rolls in and brown beautifully, sprinkle with the rest of the spice mixture, dilute with the boiling broth and cream and let simmer slowly with a tight lid until tender, approximately 3/4 hour.
5. Remove them, cut the twine, place in warm carrot, whisk up the sauce, taste and add a little thick cream if needed, and pour over the rolls.
6. Serve with boiled or mashed potatoes, lingonberries, and onion.
