## Ingredients

- 2 liters green, unripe tomatoes
- 1 kilogram loaf sugar
- 4 deciliters vinegar
- 3 deciliters vinegar
- 15 cloves
- 1/4 pod Spanish pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the tomatoes, rinse them, prick them with a small silver fork, scald in boiling water until they feel soft when tested with a whisk, then place on linen to drain.
2. When they are cold, place them in a jar.
3. Make a syrup from the sugar, vinegar and spices, which is strained hot over the tomatoes.
4. Cover the jar with a folded towel, place a plate and weight on top, and let the contents stand for three days, after which the liquid is strained, reheated and poured hot over the fruit again.
5. Repeat this two more times with six-day intervals; the last time let the liquid cool before pouring it on.
6. The liquid should stand well over the tomatoes.
7. Bind the jar well.
