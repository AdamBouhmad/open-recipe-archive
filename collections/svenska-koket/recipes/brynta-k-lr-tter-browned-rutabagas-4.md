## Ingredients

- 2 large rutabagas
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.) for blanching
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 4 dcl. boiling meat broth
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. The rutabagas are peeled, cut into fairly large cubes or oblong squares, which are blanched 3-4 minutes with the lid open.
2. They are poured into a colander and allowed to drain on a cloth.
3. The butter is browned in an iron saucepan, the roots are added, dusted with sugar and salt and browned, while they are frequently stirred, until they take on a nice color.
4. Then the broth is added and the roots are allowed to cook slowly until they are completely soft, or about 1 1/2 hours.
5. They are seasoned with a little pepper, if desired, and served with salt meat.
