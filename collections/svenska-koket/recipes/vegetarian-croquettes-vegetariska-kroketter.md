## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 deciliters rice grains
- 1 liter milk
- 2 deciliters mushroom stock
- 1 can mushrooms (1/2 liter)
- 2 1/2 tablespoon butter (50 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt
- 2 teaspoon vegetable oil
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 kg. cooking fat
- 1 lettuce head for garnish
- 2-3 tomatoes for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald in hot water and cook in the milk and mushroom stock until a thick porridge forms, then pass through a sieve immediately.
2. Cut the mushrooms into cubes and fry in butter until it becomes clear, then stir into the porridge along with the beaten egg yolks.
3. Season the mixture with salt and white pepper and treat as in recipe no. 49.
4. Shape the croquettes into oblong forms.
5. Garnish with lettuce and tomato wedges and serve as a middle course with fresh or preserved peas.
