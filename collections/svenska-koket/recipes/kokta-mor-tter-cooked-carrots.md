## Ingredients

- ½ kg. carrots (4-5 pieces)
- ½ liter water
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse and brush the carrots clean well, but do not scrape them, place them in cold salted water and let them boil until they are tender, which is tested with a whisk
2. Take up the carrots, remove the skin immediately, then cut them into small pieces or slices with a vegetable knife
3. Place them in a serving dish, sprinkle with the sugar, top with the cold butter and let them stand in a water bath until they are heated through, then serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with cooked vegetables
