## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked fish (1/2 kg pike)
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 5 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Grate the cooked fish finely with a fork until it resembles rice grains.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
3. Add the milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
4. Pour into a bowl; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
5. Mix in the fish along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten until stiff peaks form.
6. Taste and adjust seasonings.
7. Pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold and bake in regular oven heat for about 45 minutes.
8. If using a metal form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour and turn out.
9. Serve immediately upon removal from the oven as a middle course with crayfish or lobster sauce, hollandaise or lemon sauce.
