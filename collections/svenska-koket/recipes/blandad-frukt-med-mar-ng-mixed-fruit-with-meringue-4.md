## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram dried apricots
- 1 liter fresh apples
- 2 hectograms prunes
- 2 cups crushed sugar
- 1/4 liter water
- Meringue mass: 3 egg whites, 3-6 tablespoons powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well and soak them in water for about 1 hour.
2. From this water and the sugar, boil a clear syrup.
3. Peel the apples, cut them into 4 parts, place them directly in the syrup (to maintain their color) and cook until clear.
4. Remove them with a slotted spoon, place the apricots in and let them likewise cook until clear, then remove them.
5. Finally place the prunes in and let them cook until soft.
6. When the fruit is cold, arrange it alternately in a pyramid shape on a heat-resistant dish.
7. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, and carefully fold in the sugar.
8. Pipe the meringue mass through a fluted piping bag over the fruit, and place the dish in gentle oven heat.
9. When the meringue mass is dry and has taken on a light yellow color, remove it, and serve the dish cold with whipped cream.
