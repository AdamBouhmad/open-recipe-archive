## Ingredients

- 6 parts rosehips
- 2½–3 litres water
- 1–1½ cups crushed sugar
- 1⅜ tablespoons potato flour
- 25 grams sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Rinse the rosehips and place in cold water to cook soft for about 2 hours.
2. During cooking, whisk the soup vigorously from time to time.
3. Press through a sieve, add the sugar.
4. Thicken the soup with potato flour mixed with a little water and bring to a good boil.
5. Taste and serve with the blanched almonds, cut into long strips.
