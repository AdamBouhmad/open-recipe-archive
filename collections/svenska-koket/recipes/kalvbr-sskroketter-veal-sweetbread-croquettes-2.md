## Ingredients

- 3 hg. veal sweetbread
- 1 msk. butter
- 1 hg. salted ox tongue
- 1 hg. mushrooms
- 1 1/2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. flour
- 2 parts broth
- 2 parts cream
- salt, white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 eggs for breading
- 2 kkp crushed rusks for breading
- 3/4 kg. fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Let the veal sweetbread soak in water for a couple of hours so the blood is drawn out, then blanch it and let it drain.
2. Brown it in the butter, remove it and let it cool, then cut it into small cubes.
3. Cut the tongue and mushrooms likewise into cubes.
4. Make a roux with butter and flour, thin with broth and cream, and let the mixture cook for 5 minutes.
5. Remove the pan from the heat, add the seasonings and egg yolks.
6. Mix in the sweetbread, tongue and mushrooms, and spread the mixture on a flat platter to cool.
7. Form oblong croquettes from it, roll these in crushed rusks, dip in egg and roll again in crushed rusks, then let them dry for 1/2 hour.
8. Heat the fat, cook the croquettes golden brown in it and place on paper towels to drain.
9. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or tomato sauce.
