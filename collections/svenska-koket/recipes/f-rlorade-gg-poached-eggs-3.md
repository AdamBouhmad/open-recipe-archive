## Ingredients

- water to 2 cm height
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Fill a well-cleaned frying pan with water to 2 cm height.
2. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon vinegar to the water.
3. Bring the water to a boil, ensuring it only barely boils.
4. Carefully break an egg into a cup and let it slide slowly into the water.
5. When the white has set, remove the egg with a slotted spade and immerse quickly in cold water to remove the foam.
6. Polish the egg with a drinking glass and serve as a breakfast dish, laid on fried bread slices, or as garnish for other dishes.
