## Ingredients

- 2 kkp linser (lentils)
- 1 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1 portug. lök (Portuguese onion)
- 2 msk. smör (butter)
- 1 kkp stötta skorpor (breadcrumbs)
- 1 kkp majsflingor (corn flakes)
- 2 ägg (eggs)
- 1 msk. salt (salt)
- 1/2 tsk. närsalt (nutmeg salt)
- 4 msk. smör (butter for frying)

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lentils, place in soft cold water and let cook until soft.
2. Peel the onion, cut into cubes and brown in butter.
3. Press the lentil mush twice through a meat grinder together with the breadcrumbs and the onion.
4. Let the corn flakes cook a few minutes in 1/2 liter of water; when they have cooled, stir them into the lentil mush along with the beaten eggs.
5. Season the mixture and form into small cutlets, which are fried a nice brown in butter.
6. Serve with stewed parsnips or fried potatoes as a breakfast or dinner dish.
