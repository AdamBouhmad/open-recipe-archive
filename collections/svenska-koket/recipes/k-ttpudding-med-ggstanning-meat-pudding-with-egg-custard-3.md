## Ingredients

- 4–5 hg. boneless cooked or fried meat
- 1 small red onion
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g.)
- 2 tsp. salt (10 g.)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 2 dcl. meat gravy or broth
- 4 eggs
- 1 tsp. wheat flour
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. sugar
- 4 dcl. milk
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Chop the meat fairly finely.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in the butter; add the meat and brown well, sprinkle with the seasonings and mix with the gravy or broth.
3. For the egg custard, whisk the egg yolks with the flour, a little salt and sugar, and the milk; then fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Place the meat in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie mold; pour the egg custard over it and sprinkle with parsley.
5. Bake the pudding in the oven until it has a nice golden-brown color, or approximately 1/2 hour.
6. Serve with boiled potatoes and melted butter.
