## Ingredients

- 212 grams butter
- 6 eggs
- 212 grams powdered sugar
- 212 grams potato flour
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 1-2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the pan (10 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and beat it to white foam, then alternately beat in 1 egg yolk, 1 tablespoon sugar and 1 tablespoon flour, until all ingredients are worked in.
2. Season the mixture with finely grated lemon zest or vanilla sugar and beat for an additional 20 minutes.
3. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, and pour the batter into a buttered and crumb-lined ring mold and bake for about 45 minutes in a not too hot oven.
