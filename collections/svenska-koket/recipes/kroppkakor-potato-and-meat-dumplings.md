## Ingredients

- 1½ liters potatoes
- 2 hectograms wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 egg
- 3 hectograms smoked ham
- 1 small red onion
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the potatoes, drain the water, and mash with a fork or press through a ricer and let cool.
2. Add the salt, egg and most of the flour (save some flour for rolling out).
3. Work the dough very little on the board and form into round cakes.
4. Peel the onion; cut the ham and onion into cubes and fry and season.
5. Make a hole in the middle of each cake and fill with a teaspoon of the fried ham mixture, then press the hole well closed.
6. Place the dumplings in boiling salted water and let simmer slowly until they float up, or approximately 10 minutes.
7. Serve with clarified butter.
