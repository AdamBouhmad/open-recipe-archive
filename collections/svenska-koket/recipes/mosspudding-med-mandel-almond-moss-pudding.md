## Ingredients

- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 part milk
- 2 eggs
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 1/2 liter thick cream
- biscuits for garnish

## Directions

1. The almonds are blanched, grated and pounded together with the sugar into a fine paste.
2. The almond paste, milk and eggs are whipped together in a pot and the mixture is simmered over heat while whipping until the cream thickens.
3. The gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water is added, and when the mixture is cold, the cream whipped to stiff peaks is stirred in.
4. The cream is poured into a rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
5. When it is to be served, the mold is held for a moment in lukewarm water, the pudding is turned out and garnished with jam and biscuits.
