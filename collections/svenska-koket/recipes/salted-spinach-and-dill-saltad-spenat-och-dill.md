## Ingredients

- Spinach or dill
- 4-5 hg coarse rock salt per kg greens

## Directions

1. Prepare and salt in the same manner as salted fresh beans.
2. Layer alternately with salt in a wooden tub or crock and press down gently.
3. Place a thick layer of salt on top.
4. Add a bottom and a light weight, cover the vessel well and store in a cellar.
5. When the greens are to be served, remove the undissolved salt crystals, then boil in several changes of water until they reach the desired saltiness.
6. In the last water, add a little sugar.
