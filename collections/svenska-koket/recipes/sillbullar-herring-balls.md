## Ingredients

- 2 salt herrings
- ½ liter cooked, cold potatoes
- 1½ cups cooked, chopped meat
- 1 cup milk
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 msk butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings the day before; clean them in the usual way and skin them.
2. Chop them finely together with the potatoes; mix in meat, milk and pepper.
3. Form balls from the forcemeat, roll them in crushed breadcrumbs and fry golden brown in butter or lard.
4. Serve with currant sauce.
