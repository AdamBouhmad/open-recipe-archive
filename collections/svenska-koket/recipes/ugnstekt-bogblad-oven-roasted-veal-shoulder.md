## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal shoulder
- ½ tbsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 8 medium-sized apples
- 2 tbsp butter
- ¼ liter boiling water or broth

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into two equal-sized pieces, which are rubbed with salt and white pepper.
2. Dry the apples well, remove the blossom end, and place them between the shoulder pieces, which are then tied together with the meaty side outward.
3. Place the roast in a butter-lined long roasting pan and put it in the oven to brown, then moisten it with boiling water or broth.
4. The roasting takes about 2 hours, during which the meat is basted every ten minutes.
5. Strain the meat gravy, skim it, and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
6. (If desired, it can be finished as an ordinary roasting sauce.)
