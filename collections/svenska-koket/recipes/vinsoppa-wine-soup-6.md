## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 gr)
- 2 dcl French wine
- 1.5 dcl sherry
- 4 egg yolks
- 150 grams granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the water with the lemon peel.
2. Mix in the potato flour.
3. Add the French wine and sherry.
4. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar in a soup bowl until light and frothy, then pour the soup in while stirring constantly.
5. Serve warm.
