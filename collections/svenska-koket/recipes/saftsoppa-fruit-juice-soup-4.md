## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter sour or sweet fruit juice
- 1 1/2 liters water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1 1/2–2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato starch (30 g)

## Directions

1. Mix the juice, water, lemon peel, cinnamon, and sugar (if the juice is sweet, omit the sugar) in a pot, bring to a boil, and cook for 5 minutes.
2. Skim the soup and thicken with potato starch stirred in 1/2 tablespoon cold water, then let it cook for 2 minutes.
3. Serve with whipped cream, dumplings, yeast powder rusks, or biscuits.
