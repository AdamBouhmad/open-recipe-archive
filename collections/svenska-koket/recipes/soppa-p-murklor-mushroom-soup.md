## Ingredients

- 1 lit. fresh or 1/2 lit. canned porcini mushrooms
- 2 msk. butter
- 2 lit. broth
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 1 six-öre French bread
- cayenne pepper
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the mushrooms very well (if they have not been canned, in which case the broth is important for the fine taste of the soup).
2. Fry half of the mushrooms in 1 msk. of the butter and then let them cook together with the raw mushrooms in the broth, and add the salt.
3. When the mushrooms are tender, strain them out.
4. Cut the wheat bread into approximately 2 cm. cubes, which are fried in the rest of the butter.
5. Add the fried bread to the soup, which should cook a further 15 minutes, then season with a very little cayenne pepper.
6. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl, and pour the soup in while whisking.
