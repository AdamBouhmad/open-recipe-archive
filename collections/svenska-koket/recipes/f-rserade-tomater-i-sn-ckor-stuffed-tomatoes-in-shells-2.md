## Ingredients

- 6 large tomatoes
- 20 gr. dried morels
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp finely chopped parsley
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr.) for the shells
- 2 tbsp crushed hard biscuits for the shells

## Directions

1. Dip the tomatoes for 1 minute in boiling water and peel off the skin. Open the fruit at the stem and carefully remove the seeds with the handle of a teaspoon.
2. Let the morels soak in water for several hours, place them in boiling lightly salted water and let them boil for about 1/2 hour, then drain them in a colander and let them drip dry.
3. Cut them into fine cubes and fry them in half the butter until it is clear.
4. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, dilute with the cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
5. Add the whisked egg yolk and let the sauce simmer. Add the morels and parsley, and season the stew with the spices.
6. Fill the tomatoes with the stew. Butter shells or small ovenproof forms and place one tomato in each.
7. Sprinkle with crushed hard biscuits and pour over clarified butter.
8. Place the shells in a long pan with coarse salt, put them in a warm oven and gratinee for 10-15 minutes.
9. Serve as a side dish or with roasted meat.
