## Ingredients

- 165 gr. icing sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 75 gr. candied block chocolate
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in the previous recipe and place in the form of small wreaths or ordinary meringues on a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet, which are sprinkled with a little sugar and baked in weak oven heat until they are almost completely dry and release from the sheet.
2. Dissolve the chocolate in a water bath, without the addition of water. Blanch the almond, cut into thin slices, which are lightly roasted with 2 tsp. sugar in the oven.
3. When the meringues are cold, they are placed on a flat glass bowl with baking paper, alternately with the melted chocolate, so that they form a pyramid. The pyramid is covered with chocolate and sprinkled with the roasted almond.
4. The cream is whipped with the sugar until stiff peaks form. Half of it is flavored with vanilla sugar, the other half is colored pink with carmine. The white and pink cream are piped, each separately, through a fluted piping bag in beautiful garlands over the pyramid. The Swiss should not be garnished until 1/2 hour before it is to be served.
