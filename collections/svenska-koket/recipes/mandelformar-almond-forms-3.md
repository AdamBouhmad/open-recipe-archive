## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 210 gr. flour sugar
- 75 gr. sweet almond
- 10 gr. bitter almond
- 2 egg yolks
- 210 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and beat it with the sugar until it is white and fluffy, then add the blanched and ground almonds and the flour, and work the dough until it becomes even and smooth.
2. Let it thereafter stand in a cool place for approximately 2 hours.
3. Roll out the dough into a narrow rope, which is cut into pieces, with which small fluted pastry forms are lined internally.
4. Place the forms on a baking sheet, and bake the pastries in a not too hot oven until they are beautifully golden brown on both sides.
5. Take them out, turn them out immediately and let them cool on the turned-over forms.
6. Serve with jam or with coffee or tea. — The pastries can be filled with applesauce or pitted strained plum compote, which is piped over with meringue, sprinkled with sugar and let stand in gentle oven heat until the meringue has a light yellow color, or else garnish the pastries with whipped cream.
