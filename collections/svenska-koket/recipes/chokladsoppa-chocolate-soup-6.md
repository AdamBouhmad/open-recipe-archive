## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 liters whole milk
- 1 hg. chocolate
- 30 gr. cocoa
- 1 tbsp. mondamin flour (15 gr.)
- sugar to taste
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Heat the milk; when it reaches boiling point, pour off 2/3 of it and in the remaining milk dissolve the chocolate broken into small pieces and the cocoa.
2. Add the remaining milk and when the soup boils, thicken it with the mondamin flour stirred in 1/2 cup cold milk.
3. Let the soup boil while stirring for 5 minutes and add sugar to taste; pour it into the soup bowl and place the cream whipped to stiff peaks on top.
4. Water can be used instead of milk if desired.
