## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 2 hectograms broth fat
- water
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. The potatoes are rinsed, brushed well clean and raw-peeled. They are then formed with a smooth potato ball cutter into small round balls, which are boiled in water with salt until they are half-cooked.
