## Ingredients

- 3 veal chops
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 g.)
- 12 small raw potatoes
- 1 tsp. salt

## Directions

1. Dry the chops with a cloth wrung out in hot water, clean them, beat well on both sides and rub with the spice mixture.
2. Use three small, low, ovenproof forms with lids. Brown the butter in the forms, place one chop in each form and brown nicely.
3. Peel the potatoes and form them round; lay four pieces around each chop after it is browned.
4. Sprinkle salt over the potatoes and place the lid on the form.
5. Put the forms in a hot oven; let the chops and potatoes fry until tender, or about 15 minutes.
6. Serve in the forms as a breakfast dish.
