## Ingredients

- 2 kg. fish
- 2 tbsp. salt
- 2 tsp. vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it and wash it well, then rub it with the vinegar and salt.
2. Cut it into pieces and place it on the fire in hot, but not boiling water (short broth).
3. When the broth comes to a boil, skim it well, and let the fish simmer afterwards at the side of the fire for approximately 15 minutes, or until the fins loosen easily.
4. Place it immediately on a platter or plate and garnish with parsley.
5. Serve with simple fish sauce, mustard sauce or parsley sauce.
