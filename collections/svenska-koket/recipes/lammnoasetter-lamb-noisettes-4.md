## Ingredients

- 750 gr. lamb fillet
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 1.5 hectograms lard
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 3 deciliters broth
- For garnish: 1 hectogram goose liver
- 1 can asparagus (0.25 liter)
- 0.5 liter dwarf potatoes, cooked in stock

## Directions

1. Dry the fillets with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into 12 slices, which are lightly flattened, rubbed with the spices, rolled up and wrapped in thinly cut lard slices.
2. Brown them in the butter, add the boiling broth and let them braise with a tight lid until they are tender, about 45 minutes.
3. Remove the lard slices and arrange the noisettes in a ring on a round serving plate and place a small round slice of goose liver on each noisette.
4. Place the asparagus in the center of the plate, heated in a water bath, and arrange the dwarf potatoes around it.
5. Garnish the arrangement with parsley and serve with truffle sauce (see recipe 433).
