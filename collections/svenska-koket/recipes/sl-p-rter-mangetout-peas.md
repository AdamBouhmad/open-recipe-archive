## Ingredients

- 2 1/2-3 litres half-grown field peas
- 1 litre water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar (8 gr.)
- 120 gr. butter for serving

## Directions

1. Rinse the pea pods well and let drain on cloth.
2. Place them in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly with a tight lid until completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. Place them on a serving platter with a slotted spoon, cover with a napkin and serve very hot with softened butter.
