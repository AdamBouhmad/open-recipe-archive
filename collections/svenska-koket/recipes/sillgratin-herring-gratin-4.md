## Ingredients

- 2 large salt fat herrings
- 3/4 liter raw potatoes
- 2 red onions
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold
- 1 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times.
2. Fillet it, remove it, split it lengthwise, then remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap them in a fish towel and cut each herring half into six pieces.
4. Rinse the potatoes, peel them and cut them into thin slices.
5. Peel the onion and chop it finely.
6. Butter a fireproof omelette mold with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
7. At the bottom of the mold, place a layer of potatoes, then a layer of herring, then onion and a little pepper.
8. Continue until there are 3 layers of potatoes and 2 layers of herring.
9. Place the butter in flakes on top and sprinkle breadcrumbs over it.
10. Bake in a hot oven for about 45 minutes and serve as breakfast or with the smorgasbord.
