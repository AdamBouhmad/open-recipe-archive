## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 300 gr. caster sugar
- 200 gr. sweet almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- 100 gr. butter
- 4 medium potatoes
- 1 tbsp. washed butter for the pan
- 2 tbsp. breadcrumbs for the pan
- 1 tbsp. water for the glaze
- 100 gr. caster sugar for the glaze
- 2 tsp. lemon juice
- 25 gr. pistachios for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Blanch and grate the sweet almonds, pound in a mortar together with the washed butter, and blend into the egg mixture.
3. Peel the cooked potato, grate it, and fold it last into the mixture.
4. Pour the batter into a well-buttered and breadcrumb-lined baking pan and bake in gentle oven heat for approximately 1/2 hour.
5. When the cake has cooled, glaze it and sprinkle with blanched and chopped pistachios.
