## Ingredients

- 5 parts cream
- 3 tablespoons granulated sugar (or vanilla sugar)
- 4 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream with sugar to stiff peaks.
2. Dissolve the gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir carefully into the whipped cream until everything is well mixed.
3. Pour the blanc-mangé into a gelatin mold to set, after which garnish it with jam and biscuits.
