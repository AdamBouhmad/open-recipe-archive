## Ingredients

- Fresh or preserved roe from sturgeon
- Half sliced lemons, fluted
- Toasted bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Fresh roe is finest, but it is also purchased preserved.
2. Caviar is garnished with halved, fluted lemon slices and served with toasted bread and butter.
