## Ingredients

- 1 kg apples, grated
- 6 eggs
- 150 grams powdered sugar
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- For the form: 1/2 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cook and strain the apples.
2. Mix the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture is white and fluffy; blend in the applesauce, the grated lemon zest and lemon juice.
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and carefully fold in.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered pie form and place in the oven for approximately 30 minutes.
5. Serve the soufflé immediately with whipped cream.
