## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 1/2 dcl. cognac
- 210 gr. granulated sugar
- 840 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and work out the water well.
2. Stir it then with the sugar for approximately 15 minutes, then add the cognac and the sifted flour, after which the dough is worked until it becomes even and smooth.
3. Roll it out in very thin lengths, which are laid double and twisted together, cut into approximately 7 mm long pieces, which are fastened together in the form of wreaths.
4. Bake the cakes immediately beautifully golden brown in a not too warm oven.
