## Ingredients

- 1 kg raspberries
- 3/4 kg loaf sugar
- 2 1/2 to 3 parts water

## Directions

1. Clean the raspberries.
2. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup that should be so well cooked that a cooled sample threads between the fingers or that a drop on a plate does not immediately run out.
3. Add the berries, and let the preserve come to a boil, lift off, shake and skim.
4. Set it back on, let it come to a boil, lift off, shake and skim.
5. Repeat this 4 to 5 times, or until the berries are clear and the syrup is shimmering.
6. Shake the preserve until it has cooled somewhat.
7. Then pour it immediately into the prepared jars.
