## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- 2 liters apples
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour in a bowl and mix with salt.
2. Whisk the eggs with the milk and stir into the flour with vigorous whisking.
3. Let the batter stand for 1 hour so the flour can swell.
4. Peel the apples, cut into pieces, and fry in the butter with the sugar until almost soft.
5. Pour into the pan lined with cold butter, give the batter a vigorous whisking and pour over the apples.
6. Bake the pancake in a good but not too strong oven heat.
7. Turn out onto a plate and sprinkle with sugar.
