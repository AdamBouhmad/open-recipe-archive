## Ingredients

- Heart and lungs of calf, sheep or pork
- water
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 1 red onion
- 20 white peppercorns
- 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 0.75 liter of broth
- 1–2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut up the heart and lungs, rinse them and soak in water for 12–15 hours, changing the water twice.
2. Place in enough cold water to just cover the meat, skim well and add the spices; let cook until tender, then clean away the esophagus and windpipe and chop the meat very finely.
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth (which should be well skimmed), add the meat, add the vinegar and let the purée simmer while stirring for 10 minutes, then taste and correct the seasoning.
4. Serve with boiled potatoes and vinegar.
