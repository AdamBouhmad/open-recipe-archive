## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 2 tsk. wheat flour
- 4 dcl. cream milk
- 1 tsk. finely chopped leek
- 3 hg. cured side pork

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with the salt and flour for 10 minutes, then add the milk and leek.
2. Cut the pork into thin slices and lightly brown in a hot pan. Remove it, drain off the fat and wipe the pan thoroughly with white paper so no salt remains.
3. Pour the fat back into the pan, which is again placed over the heat to warm, then pour in the egg mixture and place the pork on top.
4. When the omelet is cooked on one side, turn it onto a lid, turn it back into the pan and cook on the other side. Serve as a breakfast dish. Smoked ham can also be used.
