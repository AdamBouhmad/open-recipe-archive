## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 3 dcl. boiling milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- a little white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk gradually, and let the batter cook for 5 minutes.
2. Pour into a bowl; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time, and stir the batter for 10 minutes.
3. Season with spices, and gently fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mass into a well-buttered ring mold and bake in a water bath in low oven heat for about 40 minutes.
5. Turn out onto a hot plate and serve immediately with clear or finished soups.
