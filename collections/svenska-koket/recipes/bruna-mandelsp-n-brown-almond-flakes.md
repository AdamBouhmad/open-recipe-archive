## Ingredients

- 100 grams sweet almond
- 3 bitter almonds
- 1 egg
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, dry them and grate finely
2. Stir one egg with the sugar until the mixture becomes frothy, stir in the almonds and the egg whites beaten to soft peaks
3. Wipe a flat baking sheet carefully dry and grease it with washed butter
4. Spread the batter thinly with a spatula, and put the sheet in weak oven heat
5. When the batter has set and taken on a nice brown color, remove the sheet from the oven door
6. Cut out flakes of approximately 1 decimeter square and roll them up diagonally while they are still properly hot, otherwise they break
