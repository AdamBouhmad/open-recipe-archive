## Ingredients

- 2 msk. currants (50 gr.)
- 6 1/2 dcl. water
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 msk. sugar or syrup
- 1—2 msk. vinegar, salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 msk. cold butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Currants are cleaned and rinsed very well in several warm waters.
2. They are placed in 6 1/2 dcl. cold water and allowed to boil until soft.
3. Strained through a colander and the currant liquid is used for the sauce.
4. Butter and flour are fried 2 minutes.
5. Currant liquid is added and the sauce is allowed to boil 5 minutes.
6. Currants are added, the sauce is seasoned with sugar or syrup, vinegar and salt and finished, if desired, with the whisked egg yolk.
7. Finally the cold butter is stirred in.
