## Ingredients

- 20 sprats
- 1 teaspoon French mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2-3 parts strong vinegar
- 1-2 parts vegetable oil
- 2 parts water
- 1 bunch dill

## Directions

1. Clean the sprats, rinse, scale and fillet them.
2. Make a strong brine from the spices, vegetable oil, vinegar and water.
3. Place the sprats in it and sprinkle over with chopped dill.
4. After 1/2 hour it is ready to serve.
