## Ingredients

- 1 kg mutton roast (leftovers)
- 1/4 Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2-2 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts broth, cooked on mutton bones
- pepper and salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry in butter, stir in flour, and add the broth together with seasonings.
2. Let the sauce cook about 10 minutes, then add the meat. Finish the stew with egg yolk and cream.
3. Garnish, if desired, with poached eggs.
