## Ingredients

- 350 grams chestnuts
- water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 teaspoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 liters broth
- sugar (optional)
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Free the chestnuts from the outer shell and place them in boiling water for a few minutes, then remove the inner skin easily.
2. Place the chestnuts in cold water with half of the butter and 1/2 tablespoon salt and let them cook slowly until they are soft enough to strain, about 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
3. Fry the remaining butter with the onion and flour, add the purée, and add the broth.
