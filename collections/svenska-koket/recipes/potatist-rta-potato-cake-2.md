## Ingredients

- 150 gr. good butter
- 3 eggs
- 3 hg. sugar
- 2 hg. cooked, cold grated potato
- 150 gr. sweet almond
- 6 bitter almonds
- 15 gr. washed butter for the pan
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir until it is white and fluffy.
2. Whip the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes and then mix with the butter.
3. Add the potato together with the blanched and ground almond and pour the mixture into a well-buttered and breadcrumb-strewn baking pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 40 minutes.
4. Remove the cake from the oven and let it stand for 10 minutes before turning it out.
5. When the cake is cold, glaze it with water glaze and, if desired, sprinkle with 20 gr. finely chopped pistachios.
6. Serve as a dessert with vanilla or lemon sauce.
