## Ingredients

- 250 gr. marmalade or tutti frutti
- 70 gr. melted butter
- 4 eggs
- 4 dcl. cream
- 3/4 dcl. crushed rusks
- 40 gr. crushed biscuits
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 2 dcl. sugar for the pan
- 2 tbsp. hot water for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the sugar in a pan, add the water, and line a heatproof pan with this mixture.
2. Mix the marmalade well with the butter.
3. Whip the egg yolks with the cream and mix with the crushed rusks and biscuits.
4. Add the egg mixture to the marmalade, a little at a time, and stir the mixture well.
5. Finally, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Fill the mixture into the lined pan and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven approximately 1 hour.
7. Serve warm or cold with vanilla sauce.
