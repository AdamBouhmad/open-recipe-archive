## Ingredients

- 320 gr. sweet almond
- 320 gr. flour sugar
- 7 egg whites
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan
- 3 candied orange peel for garnish
- fruit and jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, run them through a mill and mix with the sugar.
2. Thereafter carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mass into a well buttered and breadcrumb-strewn baking pan and bake the cake in gentle oven heat for approximately 1 hour.
4. Turn the cake out when it has cooled and garnish, when it is cold, with fruit and jelly and crystallized sugar if desired.
5. Serve with vanilla sauce.
