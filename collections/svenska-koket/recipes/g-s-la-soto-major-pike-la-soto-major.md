## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1 1/2 msk salt
- 1/2 tsk white pepper
- 1 small red onion
- 1/4 lemon
- 4 msk olive oil
- 1 del thin cream
- 3 msk flour
- 2 1/2 msk good stock
- fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Scale, rinse, and dry the fish.
2. Cut it open along the back, remove the entrails, and wash it thoroughly.
3. Remove the bones, but leave the head and tail attached.
4. Mix together the salt, white pepper, chopped onion, lemon juice, and olive oil, and pour this over the fish, allowing it to rest for a couple of hours.
5. Carefully remove the seasonings, dip the fish in cream, dredge it in flour, and fry it golden-brown in stock.
6. Place it on gray paper (to draw out the excess fat), serve it on fried bread or a napkin, and garnish with fried parsley.
7. Serve as hot as possible with mushroom sauce.
