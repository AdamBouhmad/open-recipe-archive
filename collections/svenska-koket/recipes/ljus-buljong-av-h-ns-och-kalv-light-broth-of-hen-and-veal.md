## Ingredients

- 1 old hen
- 1 kg. veal top round
- 1 kg. veal neck
- 4 liters water
- 10 gr. salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1 small leek
- 2 hectograms boneless lean veal for clarification
- 1 egg white and the rinsed eggshell for clarification

## Directions

1. Pluck the hen, singe it, remove it, rinse it and let it soak in water for 12 hours.
2. Dry it with a cloth and cut it into smaller pieces.
3. Rinse the veal quickly, dry it, cut it into small pieces and crack the bones well.
4. Cook the broth as in recipe no. 222.
5. Clarify it in the same way as described in recipe no. 223, and use it for light soups.
