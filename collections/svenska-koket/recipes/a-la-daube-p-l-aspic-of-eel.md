## Ingredients

- 2 kg eel
- 2 msk salt
- a few allspice berries
- dill stalks
- 4 msk vinegar
- 1 liter water
- ¾ liter fish stock
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Skin, clean, rinse and wash the eel well.
2. Rub it with salt, cut into pieces and place on the heat in hot water together with the spices and dill stalks and let it simmer slowly until done.
3. Remove the fish and place it in a beautiful aspic mold.
4. Clarify the stock with egg whites, strain through a cloth or cheesecloth and pour over the fish.
5. Place the aspic in a cold place to set, turn out and garnish with dill.
