## Ingredients

- 250 g spinach
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1.5 liters stock
- 250 ml thin cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the spinach, wrap in cloth and cut into pieces
2. Sauté the spinach in butter until clear, then add flour and mix well with the butter
3. Add the boiling stock gradually along with the cream
4. Let the soup simmer while stirring for 10 minutes
5. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl and pour in the soup while whisking
6. Serve with croutons (fried bread slices)
