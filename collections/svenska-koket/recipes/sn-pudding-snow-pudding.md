## Ingredients

- 2 parts thin cream
- 4 parts thick cream
- 50 g sugar
- 0.5 vanilla pod
- 2 egg yolks
- 40 g biscuits (or preserved fruit)

## Directions

1. The thin cream, sugar and vanilla are brought to a boil until the cream has acquired vanilla flavor.
2. The egg yolks are added one at a time while whisking vigorously, and the cream is allowed to simmer over the heat until it thickens.
3. The pan is then lifted off the heat, and whisking is continued until the cream is completely cold.
4. The thick cream is then whisked to stiff peaks and folded into the cream.
5. This is poured into a freezing mold and frozen until half frozen, then packed alternately with the crushed biscuits or fruit in a water-rinsed mold with a tight lid.
6. The mold is placed in crushed ice and salt.
7. The ice and salt should lie under, around and over the mold for 2-3 hours.
8. The pudding is turned out and garnished with biscuits or fruit.
