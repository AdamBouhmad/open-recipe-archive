## Ingredients

- Cream (same as previous recipe)
- Puree of strawberries or raspberries
- Maraschino liqueur
- Cocoa

## Directions

1. Prepare the cream as in the previous recipe and divide into three equal parts.
2. Add a little puree or juice of strawberries or raspberries to one part, pour into a beautiful tin mold rinsed with cold water and sprinkled with granulated sugar, and let it set on ice.
3. When the red cream is set, flavor the second part with maraschino and pour it into the mold.
4. Add cocoa to the third part and pour it last into the mold.
5. Let the cream set on ice for 2-3 hours, turn out of the mold, and garnish with lady fingers.
