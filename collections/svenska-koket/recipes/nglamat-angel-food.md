## Ingredients

- 4 parts thick cream
- 2 coffee cups jam
- 20 small rusks or cake pieces

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream until stiff peaks form.
2. Mix in jam and cake pieces.
3. Place the cream in a glass bowl and it is then ready to serve.
