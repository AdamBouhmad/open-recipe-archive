## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 cups fish broth
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, open it and clean it, rinse well.
2. Cut it open along the back, remove the backbone and all small bones, and rub the fillets with vinegar and salt.
3. Cook broth on the head and bones.
4. Brush the fillets with the beaten egg and turn them in the crushed rusks so that these completely cover them.
5. Melt the butter in a roasting pan, place the fillets in it and put in the oven to get a nice golden-brown color.
6. As soon as the fish is browned, add the hot fish broth and let it roast for about 20 minutes.
7. Place it on the serving platter and garnish with parsley.
8. Add cream to the sauce and let it come to a good boil; strain and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
