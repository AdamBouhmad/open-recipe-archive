## Ingredients

- 300 g fresh asparagus or 1 can omelette asparagus (0.5 liters)
- 80 g butter
- 80 g flour
- 2 dl asparagus stock
- 3 dl boiling milk
- 5–6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 0.5 tbsp butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp grated breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus, rinse it, cut it into three parts and cook it soft in 4 dl water with 0.5 tsp salt. If using canned asparagus, cut it in half and place it on a sieve to drain.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling asparagus stock and milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
3. Mix in the well-drained asparagus together with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Taste the mixture, pour it into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
4. If using a metal form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour and turn it out. Serve the soufflé immediately after removing from the oven as a course with hollandaise or melted butter.
