## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg lamb breast
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 3 parsley stems
- 2 carrots
- 1 small rutabaga
- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 2 deciliters rice grains
- 2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 g)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the breast as in the previous recipe but remove it when it is half tender, or after 45 minutes.
2. Clean the vegetables and cut them into strips or nice pieces with a vegetable knife or vegetable cutter; peel the onion and chop it coarsely.
3. Remove the breast and cut it into even, nice pieces. Strain the broth and skim it.
4. Brown the onion in half of the butter together with the meat, then place it in a pot and add 1/2 liter of the broth. Brown the vegetables in the remaining butter and place them on top of the meat, then let everything simmer gently.
5. Cook the rice grains as in the previous recipe and arrange them in a pyramid on a round serving dish, arrange the meat and vegetables in groups around it and garnish the dish with parsley.
6. Strain the broth, skim it, let it boil, taste it and serve it in a sauce bowl.
