## Ingredients

- 1 hg. flour
- 3/4 dcl. water (scarcely)
- 150 gr. butter
- leftover cooked fish (approximately 3 dcl.)
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 dcl. fish stock
- 2 dcl. milk
- salt, sugar
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg yolk, 1 tbsp. water (for brushing)

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff pastry as in no. 34, roll out thinly, cut out with a round measure into cakes, with which smooth pastry forms are lined. The pastry can be baked first, in the same manner as for tartlets, see no. 41, but the stew can also be filled directly into the lined forms.
2. Cut the fish and lobster into dice. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the fish stock and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes. Carefully mix in the fish and lobster together with the beaten egg yolk. Let the stew simmer, season with the spices and when it has cooled slightly, fill it into the lined forms. Stretch out the rest of the pastry into strips, which are laid in squares over the top.
