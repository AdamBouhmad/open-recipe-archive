## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- approximately 150 gr. flour
- 1 1/2 liters loose apples
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. sugar (45 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter for the pan (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Prepare the pancake batter as in recipe no. 769.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into thin wedges and fry them in butter along with sugar until they are half soft.
3. Heat a frying pan slowly, brush it with butter, and add the apples.
4. Whisk the batter vigorously and pour it over the apples, after which the pancake is baked in a warm oven until it is beautifully golden brown.
5. Turn it out onto a round dish and sprinkle with sugar.
6. This batch yields three large pancakes.
