## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. rice grains (125 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1/2 vanilla pod or the grated zest of 1/4 lemon
- 60 gr. granulated sugar
- 4 leaves gelatin
- 2 tbsp. warm water
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, add to the boiling milk and let them cook with the vanilla or lemon zest, stirring, until they are completely soft.
2. Thin with more milk if needed.
3. The porridge, which should be thick, is poured out and allowed to cool, after which the sugar is added.
4. Rinse the gelatin, cut into pieces, dissolve in the warm water and stir into the porridge together with the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Fill the mass into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled timbale mold and let it set on ice for 3-4 hours.
6. Unmold, garnish with redcurrant jelly and serve with apricot sauce or strawberry juice.
7. The rice can be mixed with cherries, peaches or other fruits, cut into dice. On the bottom of the mold, 1 dcl. wine jelly can be poured, colored red with carmine, which is allowed to set, after which the rice mass is added and allowed to set on ice.
8. The rice mass can be filled into a ring mold, set on ice, unmolded onto a round dish, preferably of silver, the hole in the middle filled with compote of strawberries, raspberries or apricots, and the rice ring garnished with berries or fruit.
