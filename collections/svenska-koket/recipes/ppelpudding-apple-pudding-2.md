## Ingredients

- ½ liter thick cream
- grated zest of ½ lemon
- 4 parts apple sauce (sweetened)
- 7 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Mix the apple sauce with the grated lemon zest and with the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water.
2. Finally carefully stir in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set it out to stiffen.
4. When it is to be served, dip the mold for a moment in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with fruit and biscuits.
