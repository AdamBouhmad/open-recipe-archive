## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1 lit. lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 300 gr. butter or margarine
- 2-4 kkp granulated sugar
- ½ hg. sweet almonds
- 1 egg for brushing
- 4 hg. sweet almonds for almond paste
- 25 gr. bitter almonds for almond paste
- 4 hg. granulated sugar for almond paste
- 2 tsk. ground cinnamon for almond paste (Mandelmassa I)
- 100 gr. butter for almond paste (Mandelmassa I)
- 1 lit. thin cream for almond paste (Mandelmassa I)
- 1 lit. thick cream for almond paste (Mandelmassa II)

## Directions

1. Make the dough as for fine wheat bread (no. 642).
2. Shape it into round buns.
3. When well risen, brush with egg and bake in good oven heat.
4. When cool, cut a lid in each bun, remove some of the interior, and fill the buns with almond paste.
5. For Mandelmassa I: Scald the almonds and grate them, then pound with the sugar to a fine paste. Boil the cream and butter, mix in the almond paste, cinnamon, and interior from the buns, and when well mixed, fill into the buns.
6. For Mandelmassa II: Scald the almonds and grate them, then pound in a mortar together with the sugar. Mix in the cream beaten to stiff peaks together with the interior from the buns.
