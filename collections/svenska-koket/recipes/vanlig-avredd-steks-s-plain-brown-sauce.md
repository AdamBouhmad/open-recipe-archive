## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 liter meat stock
- salt
- white pepper
- (1 part cream, optional)

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour, add the meat stock gradually with stirring, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes, then taste.
2. If desired, a little cream can also be added to the sauce.
3. Instead of butter, the skimmed fat can be used.
