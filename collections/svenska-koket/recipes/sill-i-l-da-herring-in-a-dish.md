## Ingredients

- 2 salted herrings
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/2 cup crushed rusks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Soak the herring the day before, clean and remove skin and bones
2. Butter an omelet dish with half of the butter
3. Turn the herring halves in the crushed rusks and lay them in the dish
4. Place the rest of the butter in flakes on top and put the herring in the oven
5. When it is half done (after 5-10 min.), pour the cream over and let the herring fry until it is completely tender
