## Ingredients

- 3 dcl. sweet or sour fruit juice
- 2 dcl. water
- 2 tsp. potato starch (10 gr.)
- sugar (if using sour juice)
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the juice and water and bring to a boil, then move to the side of the stove.
2. If sour juice is used, add sugar to taste.
3. Mix the potato starch with 2 tsp. cold water and add to the juice while stirring.
4. Let the sauce boil for 2 minutes.
5. Season with more sugar and lemon juice if desired.
6. Serve warm or cold.
