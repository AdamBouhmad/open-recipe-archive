## Ingredients

- 1 lit. large, beautiful bunches of red or white currants
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (128 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the bunches in a sieve, rinse with cold water, and let drain on cloth.
2. Whip the egg whites until soft foam.
3. Dip the currant bunches first in the egg white, then in the sugar, until evenly glazed.
4. Place on a sieve and let stand in weak oven heat until dry.
5. Serve as dessert or for buffet.
