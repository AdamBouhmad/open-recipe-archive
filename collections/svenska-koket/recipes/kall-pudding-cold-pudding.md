## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 25 grams butter
- 25 grams sugar
- 25 grams grated sweet almond
- 25 grams cornstarch flour
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, butter, sugar and grated sweet almond until the milk has acquired sufficient almond flavor.
2. Whisk the cornstarch flour and eggs together and add to the milk with vigorous whisking.
3. Let the cream boil briefly and pour into a water-rinsed mold to cool.
4. Serve with jam or fruit sauce.
