## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 2-4 cup granulated sugar
- 3 kg. butter or margarine
- 2 eggs for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 cup pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 cups for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and stir in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
5. Work the butter creamed to a white foam and the sugar into the dough.
6. Let the dough rise again and shape it into wreaths or braids, which are placed on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets.
7. When the breads have risen, brush them with the beaten eggs and sprinkle with the blanched and chopped sweet almonds mixed with the pearl sugar.
8. Kringles and snails can also be baked from this dough, but these are only brushed with egg or water.
9. Bake the breads in a hot oven.
