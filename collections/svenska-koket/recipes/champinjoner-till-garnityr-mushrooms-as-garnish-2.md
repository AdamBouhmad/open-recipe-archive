## Ingredients

- 450 gr. small, fresh champignons
- 1/2 litre water
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 30 gr. butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the champignons by carefully removing the gills, leaving the skin on tender mushrooms
2. Cut the stems level with the caps
3. Rinse the mushroom quickly and dry on cloth
4. Score the caps beautifully with a sharp knife
5. Place immediately in boiling water seasoned with salt, butter, and lemon juice
6. Let cook 5-10 minutes
7. Remove with a slotted spoon and use as garnish for finer dishes
8. The mushrooms can be stored 2-3 days in a cool place if covered with butter-coated butter paper
9. The cooking liquid can be used for sauces and stews
