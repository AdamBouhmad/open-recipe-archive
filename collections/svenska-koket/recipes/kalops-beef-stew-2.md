## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef (loin, rib or marrow bone)
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3 tbsp flour
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter
- 12 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 6 cups water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water. Cut it into slices approximately 1½ cm thick across the grain. Free the slices from coarser connective tissue and fat and pound lightly.
2. Mix white pepper, salt and flour on a plate, and turn the meat slices in this. Peel the onion and cut it into slices.
3. Brown the meat and onion quickly in the butter. Place the browned meat in a pot, deglaze the frying pan with a little boiling water, which is poured over the meat.
4. Add the allspice berries, bay leaves and the remaining water, and let the stew simmer slowly with a tight-fitting lid until the meat feels tender.
5. Place it in a serving dish, and skim the sauce.
