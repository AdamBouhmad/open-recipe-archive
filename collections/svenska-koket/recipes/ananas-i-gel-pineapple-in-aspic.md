## Ingredients

- 20 gr. gelatin (approximately 12 sheets)
- pineapple juice
- 1 litre water
- juice of 1 lemon
- 225 gr. sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 dcl. white wine
- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr.)

## Directions

1. Open the pineapple can first, carefully collect the juice, and cut the pineapple into beautiful slices.
2. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 924 and when it is clear, place the pineapple slices in the mold in the same manner as is described for grapes in the same recipe.
