## Ingredients

- 130 gr. apples
- 130 gr. currants
- 130 gr. raisins
- 60 gr. candied fruit
- 130 gr. beef suet
- 1 tbsp. wheat flour
- 130 gr. crushed rusks
- 130 gr. sugar
- 1 tsp. salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 part brandy
- 1 to 1½ cups water
- 1 tbsp. butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, core them and chop finely.
2. Clean and wash the currants, rinse the raisins, remove the seeds and chop them, cut the candied fruit into thin slices.
3. Free the beef suet from membranes and chop finely together with the wheat flour.
4. Mix all dry ingredients well together.
5. Add the egg yolks, brandy and water gradually, working the mixture vigorously for 2½ to 3¾ hours.
6. Pack the mixture together, place a plate on top, and let it rest at least two days.
7. When the pudding is to be cooked, beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the mixture.
8. Pour the batter into a buttered mold, cover with buttered paper and cook in a water bath for at least 3 hours.
9. Serve with flaming sauce.
