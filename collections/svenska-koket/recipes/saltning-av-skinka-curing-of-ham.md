## Ingredients

- 1 ham
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate
- 12 liters water
- 1.3 kg butter salt
- 26 grams potassium nitrate
- 160 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the ham with a mixture of salt, nitrate and sugar.
2. From the water, salt, nitrate and sugar, cook a brine, which is skimmed well and poured out to cool.
3. When the brine is cold, place the ham in it and it should remain for 14 days.
