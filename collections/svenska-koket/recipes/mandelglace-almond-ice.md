## Ingredients

- 1 hg. sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 1 litre cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grind through a mill and pound with a little water in a mortar to a fine paste.
2. Add this paste to the boiling cream and let it stand covered for 20 minutes to infuse.
3. Strain the cream through muslin.
4. Prepare the cream as described in the basic recipe.
5. Freeze in a machine as in recipe n:o 929.
