## Ingredients

- 750 gr. parsnips
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 decilitres boiling broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 decilitre cream
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the parsnips, brush clean, scrape, rinse again, dry and cut with a vegetable knife into beautiful pieces.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the parsnips, sprinkle with sugar and flour, add the broth and boil until soft.
3. Finish with the egg yolks whisked with cream, and let the stew simmer.
4. Season with spices and serve with meat.
