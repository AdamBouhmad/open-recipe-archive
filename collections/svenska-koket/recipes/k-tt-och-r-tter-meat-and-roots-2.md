## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg beef chuck or brisket
- 1 liter water
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)
- 1/4 tsp crushed black pepper
- 1 large rutabaga
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into oblong pieces of appropriate size for serving.
2. Bring the water to boil with the salt, add the meat and skim when it boils.
3. Add the pepper. Let the meat cook slowly with the lid on until tender, approximately 2 1/2 hours, adding boiling water if needed.
4. Peel the rutabaga, cut into even cubes, and place on top of the meat 1/2 hour before it is done.
5. In a sauce pan, fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, pour in the meat broth and strain, then let the sauce boil for 5 minutes and taste for seasoning.
6. Arrange the dish on a warm serving platter and pour the sauce over it.
7. Alternative: the butter may be omitted; in that case, stir the flour in a little cold water and add to the stew, which then should boil for 8 minutes.
