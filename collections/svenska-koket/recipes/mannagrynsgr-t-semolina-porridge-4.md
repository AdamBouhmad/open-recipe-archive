## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. semolina grits (152 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 bitter almonds
- 10 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Place the semolina grits in a sieve, rinse with cold water and let drain.
2. Melt half of the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, whisk in the grits, and let the porridge cook while whisking until it thickens, then move to a lower heat and let simmer gently for about 1 hour.
3. Season the porridge with the seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds and the rest of the butter.
4. Serve with cream or milk.
