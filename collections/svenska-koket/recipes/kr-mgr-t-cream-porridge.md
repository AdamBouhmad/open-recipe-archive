## Ingredients

- 3 msk. smör (60 gr.)
- 1 lit. mjölk
- 2 dcl. vetemjöl (1 hg.)
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tsk. socker (10 gr.)
- 2 äggulor

## Directions

1. Prepare according to the standard method for cream porridge.
2. Serve with jam or compote as a dessert.
