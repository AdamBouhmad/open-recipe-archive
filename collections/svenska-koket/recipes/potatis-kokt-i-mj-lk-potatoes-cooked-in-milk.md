## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 litres potatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 5-6 decilitres good milk
- salt
- finely chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel small, even potatoes raw and shape them oblong with a vegetable knife.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, add the potatoes and let them cook slowly with a lid in the oven until soft, at which point the sauce should be quite simmery.
3. Season the stew with salt and with parsley, if desired.
4. Serve with salted meat and boiled sausage.
