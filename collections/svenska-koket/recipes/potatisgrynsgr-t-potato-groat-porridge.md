## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 1 cup potato groats
- 1 tablespoon wheat flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil
2. Shake in the groats and let the porridge cook with stirring for 1/2 hour, or until the groats are clear
3. Finish the porridge with the flour mixed in a little cold milk, bring to a good boil
4. Taste and serve
