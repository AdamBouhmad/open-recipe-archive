## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless veal bottom round
- 1 dcl crushed rusks
- 200-250 ml heavy cream
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 1 dcl meat stock (preferably from game)
- 300 ml heavy cream
- salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, cut into pieces and grind 4 times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the crushed rusks with the cream and let swell for 1 hour.
3. Work the meat well with the rusk mixture, season with the spices and form into 15 round patties.
4. Brown the patties quickly in butter and place in a pot.
5. Cut the mushrooms into slices and sauté in butter in the frying pan, add flour and mix well with the butter and mushrooms.
6. Add the meat stock and cream and bring the sauce to a good boil, season with spices.
7. Pour the sauce over the patties, which then cook slowly with a tightly closed lid for 10-15 minutes.
8. Place on a warm serving dish, whisk up the sauce and pour over the patties.
9. Serve with mashed potatoes or fried potatoes.
