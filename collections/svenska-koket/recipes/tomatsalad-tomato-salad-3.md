## Ingredients

- 4-5 medium, firm tomatoes
- 2 tsk. chopped tarragon leaves
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- a few grains paprika
- 2-3 msk. fine cooking oil
- 1-1 1/2 msk. vinegar or wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Dip the tomatoes briefly in hot water, peel them and cut in half crosswise.
2. Gently squeeze the tomato halves so they are freed from juice and seeds.
3. Cut them into fine strips, place in a salad bowl and pour the sauce over them, which is prepared as in recipe no. 448.
4. Sprinkle the salad with the chopped tarragon leaves and serve with finer meat dishes.
