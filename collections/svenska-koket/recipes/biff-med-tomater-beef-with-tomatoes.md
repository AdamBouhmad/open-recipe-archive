## Ingredients

- 6 hg. beef chuck or boneless sirloin
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- 1 tsk. white pepper
- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 2 large, ripe tomatoes
- 1 msk. flour (10 gr.)
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling water
- 1/2 tsk. meat extract

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into 1 cm thick slices, which are pounded on both sides.
2. Peel the onion and cut into slices.
3. Rinse the tomatoes, dry them, cut into slices, which are sprinkled with pepper and salt and turned in wheat flour.
4. Brown half the butter in a frying pan, add the tomato slices and onion and brown nicely, then place on a warm serving dish.
5. Season the beef slices, fry in the remaining butter and place one slice on each tomato slice.
6. Mix the water and meat extract and pour into the pan, then let the sauce simmer 3-4 minutes.
7. Add 1 tsk. cold butter and pour the sauce over the beef slices.
8. Serve with potatoes as a breakfast dish.
