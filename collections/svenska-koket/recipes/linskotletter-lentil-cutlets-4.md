## Ingredients

- 2 cups lentils
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 cup crushed bread crumbs
- 1 cup cornflakes
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.) for frying

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lentils, place them in soft, cold water and let them cook until they become a mush.
2. Peel the onion, cut it into cubes and brown it in the butter.
3. Pass the lentil mush twice through a meat grinder together with the crushed bread crumbs and onion.
4. Let the cornflakes cook for a few minutes in 1/2 liter of water; when they have cooled, stir them into the lentil mush along with the beaten eggs.
5. Season the mixture and form it into small cutlets, which are fried until beautifully brown in the butter.
6. Serve with stewed pâté sandwiches or fried potatoes as a breakfast or main course dish.
