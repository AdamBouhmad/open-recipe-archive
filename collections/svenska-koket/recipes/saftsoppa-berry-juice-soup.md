## Ingredients

- 3–4 cups sour berry juice
- 1¼ litres water
- 1–1½ cups crushed sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon (optional)
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 10 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Bring the juice, water and cinnamon to a boil, add sugar and let it cook 5–10 minutes, then skim.
2. Mix the potato flour with a little cold water and add it; bring to a good boil.
3. Blanch the almonds, cut into strips and place in the soup bowl.
4. Serve the soup preferably cold with rusks or rusks. If sweet juice is used, omit the sugar.
