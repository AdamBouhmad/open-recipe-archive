## Ingredients

- Murklor (morels)
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the murklor and rinse extremely well so that all sand and soil is removed
2. Blanch as you would chanterelles
3. Fry in butter for 15-20 minutes, or until the butter is clear
4. Season with salt and pepper and a little lemon juice if desired
5. Serve at the smorgasbord or in sauces and stews, or stew as mushrooms
