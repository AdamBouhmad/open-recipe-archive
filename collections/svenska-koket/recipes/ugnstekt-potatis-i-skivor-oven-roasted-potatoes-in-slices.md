## Ingredients

- 1 liter evenly-sized, oblong potatoes
- 1 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 grams)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as described on page 337 and following and cut into 1/2 cm thick slices, which are stacked so that the potatoes retain their form.
2. Butter a frying pan or omelette form with half of the butter, place the potato slices tightly next to each other, place the remaining butter in flakes on top, and finally sprinkle a mixture of the crushed breadcrumbs and cheese.
3. Roast in the oven until soft and beautifully golden brown.
4. Place on a warm dish (preferably silver) and garnish around with cooked vegetables.
5. Serve with roasted meat.
