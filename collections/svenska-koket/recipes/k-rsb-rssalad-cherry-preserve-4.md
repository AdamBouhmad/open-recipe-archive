## Ingredients

- 3 liters cherries
- 1 1/2 liters vinegar
- 3/4 kg. sugar for every kg. of berries
- a few pieces cinnamon
- 1 tsp. whole cloves
- 15 cherry pits

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries, pit them, weigh them and let them lie in vinegar for 24 hours, then strain off the vinegar.
2. Layer the cherries alternately with granulated sugar, spices and pits in a jar, and add enough of the vinegar so that it stands over the berries.
3. Bind the jar closed and let it stand in a cool place.
4. After 6 days, strain off the liquid, cook it until it is syrupy, let it cool and pour it back over the berries.
5. Repeat this one more time if the liquid seems thin.
