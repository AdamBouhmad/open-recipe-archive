## Ingredients

- 2 liters fish broth
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 celery
- 1 leek
- 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped parsley
- 10 white peppercorns
- 3 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 hectogram grated cheese

## Directions

1. Bring the broth to a boil and skim well.
2. Prepare the carrot, celery and leek in the usual way, cut into fine strips and add to the broth together with the chopped parsley and spices.
3. Let the soup slowly cook with a tightly closed lid for 1 1/2 hours.
4. Fry butter and flour together, add to the soup and let it cook for 10-15 minutes, then add the cheese and taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl and add the soup with vigorous whisking.
