## Ingredients

- 1 kg coarse salt
- 3 liters water
- 100 small West Aros cucumbers
- cherry and currant leaves
- dill flower heads
- 2-3 tablespoons spice peppercorns
- 3 liters vinegar
- 1.5 liters water (for the brine)

## Directions

1. Mix the salt and water cold together.
2. When the salt is completely dissolved, place the cucumbers in the brine and let them lie there for three days.
3. Take up the cucumbers and layer them in a tub or stone crock alternately with leaves, dill flower heads, and peppercorns. Each cucumber should be wrapped with leaves so they do not lie tightly together (otherwise they become spotted). The first and last layers should be leaves.
4. Cook a brine from the vinegar and water, which is poured hot over the cucumbers.
5. Place a weighted bottom on top and bind the crock closed. The brine should cover the cucumbers.
6. When the cucumbers are clear when cut in half, they are ready.
7. To help the cucumbers keep better, you can cover them with yellow mustard, sewn into a small bag.
