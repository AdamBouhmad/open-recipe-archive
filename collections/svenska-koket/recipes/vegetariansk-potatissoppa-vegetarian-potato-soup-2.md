## Ingredients

- 1 liter potato
- 0.5 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 0.25 celery root
- 3 liters water
- 2.5 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar (optional)
- 2 egg yolks
- 0.25 cup cream

## Directions

1. Peel the potato raw; peel the Jerusalem artichokes and celery root in the usual manner and put them on in cold, salted water.
2. When the root vegetables are soft, pass them through a sieve.
3. Melt 1.5 tablespoons of the butter with the flour, add the passed mass, and let the soup cook for 10-15 minutes, then taste and adjust seasoning.
4. Whip the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl, add the cold butter, and mix the purée in with vigorous whisking.
5. Serve with toasted bread.
