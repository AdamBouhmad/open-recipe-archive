## Ingredients

- 1 salmon, 1.75–2 kg
- 1.5 tbsp salt (23 g)
- 0.5 tsp white pepper
- For breading: 1 egg
- 3 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs
- For frying: 1 hg butter

## Directions

1. Clean the salmon as in recipe 331 and score both sides with 2 cm between each score, rub with the seasonings, and let sit in a cool place for a couple of hours.
2. Dry again, brush with the whisked egg, and sprinkle with finely crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Butter a long pan with cold butter on an area as large as the fish occupies.
4. Place the salmon with the back side up and sides spread out and fry.
