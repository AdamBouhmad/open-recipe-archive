## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almond
- 6 bitter almonds
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scald and dry the almonds, grate or grind them and pound in a mortar together with the sugar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and stir in last
3. Pipe the almond mass onto well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets as éclairs
4. Bake in very weak oven heat
5. If desired, they can be decorated with red and white icing
