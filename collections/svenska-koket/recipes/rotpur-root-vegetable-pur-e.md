## Ingredients

- 1 small rutabaga
- 2 carrots
- 2 parsnips
- 1 small celery root
- 1 leek
- 1 liter water
- 2 liters broth
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- salt, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the roots, prepare in the usual way, cut into pieces and place in cold water to cook soft, after which they are passed through a sieve.
2. Fry butter and flour together, add the purée and broth, and let the soup cook for 10 minutes, after which it is seasoned.
3. Whip the egg yolk and cream in the soup bowl, and pour in the soup with vigorous whisking.
