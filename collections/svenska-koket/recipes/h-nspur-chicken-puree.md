## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 3 liters veal broth or water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons rice grains
- 1 tablespoon flour
- white pepper
- sugar
- 1 part cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken, singe it, remove the innards, rinse well and dry. Cook it in broth or, if unavailable, in water with salt. Scrape the meat carefully, pass it through a meat grinder and pound together with half of the butter.
2. Rinse the rice grains well, parboil in water, cook tender in a little broth and pound well together with the chicken mixture. Add a little broth so that the mixture can easily be passed. Strain and skim the broth.
3. Fry the remaining butter with the flour, add the broth and puree, and let the soup cook for about 10 minutes, then season carefully. Whip the cream and egg yolks in a soup tureen and pour the puree into it while whisking vigorously.
