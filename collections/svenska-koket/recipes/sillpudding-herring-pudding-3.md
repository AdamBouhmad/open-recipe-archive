## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fatty herrings
- 1 lit. cooked, cold potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 2 tsk. flour (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. milk
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1-2 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Skin the herrings, remove the insides, rinse well, clean, remove the backbone and lay the fillets in water the night before they are to be used.
2. Wrap them in a fish towel or white paper, remove all small bones and cut into strips or 3 cm. wide pieces crosswise.
3. Prepare the pudding thereafter and serve as in recipe no. 399.
