## Ingredients

- 850 grams small green cucumbers
- 0.5 kg coarse salt
- 425 grams fine salt
- 25 small blanched Spanish onions
- 6 shallot onions
- 20 grams tarragon
- 4 slices Spanish pepper
- 20 grams fennel
- 1.5 liters wine vinegar
- 0.5 part cognac or strong spirits

## Directions

1. Shake the cucumbers in a towel with coarse salt, brush and clean them.
2. Layer them alternately with the coarse ground salt in a crock.
3. After 6 hours, take them up, let them drain, and place them in wide-necked bottles together with the blanched onion and spices.
4. Boil the vinegar, mix it with the cognac, and pour it over to cool.
5. Pour the mixture over the cucumbers.
6. Cork the bottles and bind them well with parchment paper.
7. The spices can be omitted, but the onion should always be kept.
