## Ingredients

- 150 gr. good butter
- 3 eggs
- 300 gr. sugar
- 200 gr. cooked, cold grated potato
- 150 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 15 gr. washed butter for the pan
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir it until it is white and fluffy. Whip the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes and then mix with the butter. Add the potato together with the blanched and ground almonds and fill the batter into a well-buttered and breadcrumb-strewn baking pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 40 minutes. Take the cake out of the oven and let it stand for 10 minutes before turning it out. When the cake is cold, glaze it with water glaze (see no. 961) and sprinkle, if desired, with 20 gr. finely chopped pistachios. Serve as a dessert with vanilla or lemon sauce.
