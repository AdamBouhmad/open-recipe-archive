## Ingredients

- 165 gr. icing sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 8 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Sift the sugar through a fine sieve.
2. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and carefully fold in the sugar with a fork into the egg white foam.
3. Spread the mixture on a well-cleaned and buttered sheet in the form of round bases, which should be uniform if the meringue cake is to be made cylindrical, but larger and smaller if a pyramidal shape is desired.
4. Pipe a ring of small meringues close together around the edge of each base.
5. Dust the meringues lightly with sugar and bake in gentle oven heat until they are almost dry and release from the sheet, or about 1 hour.
6. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks, then add the vanilla sugar.
7. When the meringue cakes are cold, layer them together with cream between each.
8. Cover the outside of the cake with whipped cream, smoothed flat with a spoon.
9. Divide the remaining cream into two parts; color one part pink with a little dissolved carmine.
10. Pipe the white and pink cream through a fluted piping bag in beautiful garlands over the cake.
11. Carefully transfer to a flat glass dish with parchment paper.
12. The meringue cake should not be decorated until 1/2 hour before it is to be served.
13. The meringue cake can be decorated with only white cream and preserved fruits if desired.
