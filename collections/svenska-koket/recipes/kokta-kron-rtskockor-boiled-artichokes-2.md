## Ingredients

- 6 large artichokes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes by removing the stem and the lower hard leaves and cutting off the sharp prickles on the remaining leaves.
2. Rinse them well and tie them with string so they do not fall apart during cooking.
3. Place them in lightly salted boiling water, but only enough to cover them one-third of the way.
4. Boil them rapidly with a tight-fitting lid for approximately 15-20 minutes.
5. Place them on a warm sieve to drain and cut off the string.
6. Place the artichokes upside down on a warm platter with a folded napkin.
7. Serve with the same type of sauce as asparagus.
