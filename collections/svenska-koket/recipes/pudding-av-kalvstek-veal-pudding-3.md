## Ingredients

- 4 hg. cooked, boneless veal or chicken meat
- 1 hg. raw, boneless veal meat
- 2 hg. bread panada
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g.)
- 4 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g.) for the onion
- 5 dcl. cream
- For the mold: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat finely, grind the raw meat 3 times through a meat grinder and mix with the cooked. — The raw meat can be omitted, but the pudding will have a finer taste if it is used. — Brown the panada (see no. 626) lightly in 1 tbsp. of the butter.
