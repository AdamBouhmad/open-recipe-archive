## Ingredients

- 18 fried herring
- 2 teaspoons spice peppercorns
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 red onion
- 3 decilitres vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Place the cold herring in a jar, layering alternately with the spices.
2. Peel and finely chop the onion, place in a pot with the vinegar and sugar.
3. Bring the brine to a boil and let cool.
4. Pour the cooled brine over the herring.
5. After 1 hour it is ready to serve on the smorgasbord.
