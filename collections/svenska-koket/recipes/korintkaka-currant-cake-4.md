## Ingredients

- 212 gr. butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 212 gr. powdered sugar
- 212 gr. currants
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped candied citrus peel or 1 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 250 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the mold
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir until it is white and fluffy.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the melted butter.
