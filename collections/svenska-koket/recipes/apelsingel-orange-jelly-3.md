## Ingredients

- 15 g gelatin (approximately 9 leaves)
- 3 1/2 dcl warm water
- 175 g sugar
- peel of 1/2 orange
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 4 oranges (totaling 3 dcl juice)
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 dcl white wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in the warm water
2. Add the sugar, orange and lemon peels with all the white removed, and press out the juice
3. Add the juice to the mixture
4. Let the mixture come to a boil and then let it stand for 10 minutes at the side of the stove
5. Whisk the egg whites in a clean bowl together with the white wine, and add the gelatin mixture under vigorous whisking
6. Prepare and serve the jelly as in no. 924
