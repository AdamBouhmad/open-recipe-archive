## Ingredients

- 2 small cauliflower heads (3/4 kg)
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cauliflower heads as in recipe 709, let drain on a sieve, and divide into florets
2. Place the florets in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined baking dish
3. For the egg custard, whisk the egg yolks with flour, salt, sugar, and milk
4. Fold in the egg whites that have been beaten to stiff peaks
5. Pour the egg custard over the cauliflower and sprinkle with parsley if desired
6. Place the mold in a regular oven and bake until the custard is set and has a nice color
7. Serve with farces, roasted meat, or as an intermediate course
8. If serving as an intermediate course, sprinkle with 1 tablespoon grated cheese instead of parsley before placing in the oven
