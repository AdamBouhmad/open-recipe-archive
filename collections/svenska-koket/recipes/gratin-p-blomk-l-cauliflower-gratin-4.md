## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized, beautiful cauliflower heads
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 grams salt
- vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter cauliflower cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the cauliflower and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Rinse again and place in boiling salted water and cook until a whisk passes easily through it.
3. Remove immediately with a slotted spoon, let drain, and place on a buttered ovenproof dish or silver platter.
4. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cauliflower cooking liquid and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Season with spices, bind with egg yolk, and pour over the cauliflower.
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese and crushed rusks, pour a little clarified butter over.
7. Place the platter in a water bath with coarse salt and gratinée in a warm oven for approximately 15 minutes.
8. Serve immediately when removed from the oven.
