## Ingredients

- 1 kg ox chuck
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 2 red onions or 1 Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 liter potatoes
- 4 dcl good beef broth
- 1 tbsp flour (10 g)
- Pie crust: 1 hg cooked, cold grated potatoes, 1 hg butter, 1 hg flour

## Directions

1. If a beef cooker is lacking, the beef can be arranged in a fireproof so-called pie form with a dough lid, and is then called beef pie.
2. Prepare it as in recipe 485, with or without potatoes. In the latter case, serve the potatoes with the pie.
3. It can also be supplemented with cooked ham or calf kidney, according to taste.
4. Whisk the broth with the flour and add last.
5. For the pie crust, blend all ingredients on the work surface and roll out the dough to 1 cm thickness.
6. Cut a lid for the form, brush the edges of the form with water, and press the dough lid in place.
7. With a fork or larding needle make 4-6 holes in the dough so that the gases formed during baking can escape.
8. Brush the pie with egg white and garnish with the rest of the dough cut into beautiful figures, which are also brushed with egg white.
9. Bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1 1/2 hours. Serve from the form.
