## Ingredients

- 750 gr. boneless veal
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 dcl. broth
- 2 tbsp. Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see page 232), cut into 12 slices, pound them well, shape with a knife, sprinkle with spices and turn in flour
2. Brown the butter in a frying pan, lay the cutlets in and fry until beautifully brown on both sides, place on a warm dish
3. Whisk the pan with the boiling broth and wine, then pour the sauce over the cutlets
4. Serve with stewed vegetables
