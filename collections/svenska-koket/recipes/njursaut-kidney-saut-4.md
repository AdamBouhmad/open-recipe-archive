## Ingredients

- 2 veal kidneys
- 1/2 liter water for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr.)
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 deciliters brown broth
- 1/2 wine glass Madeira

## Directions

1. Blanch the kidneys by placing them in cold, salted water, bring to a boil and cook uncovered for 3-4 minutes, then place on a sieve and let cool.
2. Cut them into thin slices, which are sprinkled with seasonings and turned in the flour.
3. Cut the mushrooms into slices.
4. Brown the butter in a pan: when it is light brown, add the kidney and mushroom slices and brown evenly while shaking.
5. Dilute the broth and let the stew cook for 5 minutes, then add the wine and let the sauté cook an additional 8-10 minutes.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish with fried potatoes.
