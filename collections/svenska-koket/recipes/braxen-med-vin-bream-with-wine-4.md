## Ingredients

- 2 kg bream
- 1 kg butter
- 1 cup crushed hardtack
- 4 parts French wine
- 1 tbsp salt
- 2 tsp sugar
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, remove the insides, rinse well and dry it.
2. Fry the butter in a fish kettle without a sieve, place the fish in it and sprinkle with the crushed hardtack; add salt and sugar, dilute with the wine, and let the fish cook slowly on low heat.
3. When the fish is done, remove it. Strain the sauce and thicken with the egg yolks.
