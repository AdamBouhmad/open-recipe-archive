## Ingredients

- 2 kg. berries
- 2 kg. powdered sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Treat the berries as in the previous recipe.
2. Break the sugar into small pieces, place it in the pot, and add the water.
3. When the sugar is nearly melted, cook it while skimming to a clear syrup, which is ready when it threads between the fingers.
4. Remove the pot from the fire, pour in the berries and shake them down well in the syrup.
5. Put the pot back on and let the jam cook slowly while often shaking the pot and skimming the jam until it is clear or about 1/2 hour.
6. Treat as in the previous recipe.
