## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh or 0.5 liter canned stone mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 liters broth
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 1 six-öre French bread
- cayenne pepper
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the mushrooms very thoroughly (if they have been canned, the liquid is important for the soup's fine flavor).
2. Fry half of the mushrooms in 1 tablespoon of the butter and then cook them together with the raw mushrooms in the broth, and add the salt.
3. When the mushrooms are tender, strain them out.
4. Cut the bread into approximately 2 cm cubes and fry them in the remaining butter.
5. Add the fried bread to the soup, which should cook for another 15 minutes, then season with a very little cayenne pepper.
6. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl and add the soup while whisking.
