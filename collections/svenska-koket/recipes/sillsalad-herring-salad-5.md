## Ingredients

- 1½ salt herring
- 10 medium potatoes
- 6 beets
- 2 apples
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 25 gr. veal
- 2 tbsp. vinegar
- ½ tsp. white pepper
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 2 hard-boiled eggs for garnish
- 1 beet in dice for garnish
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, remove the innards, rinse well and let soak in water overnight.
2. Cut into fillets and carefully remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well and wrap in a fish towel.
4. Cut the fillets into fine dice, as well as the potatoes, beets, apples, pickled cucumber and veal.
5. Mix all these ingredients together lightly and season with the vinegar and spices.
6. Pack the herring salad tightly into a water-rinsed mould à-la-daube, turn out and garnish with hard-boiled eggs and beets.
7. Serve with sharp sauce or whipped cream as a breakfast dish or for the smörgåsbord.
