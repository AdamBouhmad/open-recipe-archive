## Ingredients

- 2 celery roots
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 liters secondary broth
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- salt
- cayenne
- 2-3 tbsp sherry
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Rinse the celery, peel and cut into small pieces.
2. One half celery is removed and cut into cubes, which are cooked in salted water and served in the soup.
3. The purée is prepared and served as in the previous recipe.
