## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 3 lemons
- 3 liters water
- 2 kg sugar

## Directions

1. Dry the oranges and lemons well with a damp cloth and cut them in halves.
2. Cut each half into thin slices, and carefully remove the seeds.
3. Pour the cold water over the fruit, which is then allowed to stand for 12 hours.
4. The next day let everything cook until the peels become transparent, and then let it stand another 24 hours.
5. Then add the marmalade together with the sugar and let it cook under skimming until it becomes clear.
6. When most of the heat has passed, pour it into small glass jars.
