## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg spareribs
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. The spareribs, which should be thinly cut, are broken, dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water, rubbed with a mixture of salt, white pepper and ginger and left to rest until the following day.
2. The butter is browned in a roasting pan, the spareribs are placed in with the meat side up and browned nicely, basting frequently with the fat in the pan.
3. The boiling broth or water is added and the spareribs are roasted with continued basting until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours.
4. It is cut into nice pieces and served warm or cold, garnished with prunes and parsley.
5. The meat gravy is strained, skimmed, seasoned with meat extract and spices and served in a gravy boat.
