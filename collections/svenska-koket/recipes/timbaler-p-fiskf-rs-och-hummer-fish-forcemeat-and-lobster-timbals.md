## Ingredients

- 4 kg pike
- 150 grams scraped fish meat
- 75 grams butter
- 15 grams flour
- 1 1/2 decilitres cream
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the molds
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 3 decilitres cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 litre)
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish forcemeat as in the previous recipe.
2. Butter small timbal molds holding about 2 decilitres and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
3. Line them with a layer of fish forcemeat about 1 cm thick.
4. Fill with lobster stew, place a lid of forcemeat over the stew, and bind the molds with butter-brushed butcher paper.
5. Bake the timbals in a water bath in the oven about 1/2 hour.
6. Turn out after 10 minutes, place on a round platter, garnish with parsley and serve as a course.
7. The timbals can be filled with stew of crayfish, shrimp or chicken.
