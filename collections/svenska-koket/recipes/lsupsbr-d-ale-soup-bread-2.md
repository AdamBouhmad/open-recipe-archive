## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre milk
- 1 kg wheat flour
- 1 litre ale or drink
- 2 kg rye sifted flour
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr)
- 3 decilitres syrup
- 2 tablespoons crushed fennel
- 1 tablespoon crushed anise
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped orange peel

## Directions

1. Whip so much wheat flour into the milk that it becomes a thick slurry.
2. Boil the ale, add the slurry and let the mixture come to a good boil while stirring.
3. Pour into a baking pan and stir in enough rye flour to make a loose dough.
4. When cooled to about 40°, add the yeast mixed with salt and enough flour to make a fairly stiff dough.
5. Let it rise to double size.
6. Boil the syrup with the spices, let cool and work into the dough along with wheat flour so the dough becomes firm.
7. Let it rise again.
8. Shape, unbake and bake as in recipe no. 939.
