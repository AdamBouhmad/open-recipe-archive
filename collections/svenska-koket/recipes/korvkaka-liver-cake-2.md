## Ingredients

- 5 hg. ox liver
- 3 hg. pork
- 1 1/2 kkp rice grains
- 1 1/2 liter milk
- 2 msk. syrup
- 2 1/4 msk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 kkp raisins
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the liver well and let it soak in water for approximately 1/4 hour.
2. Then parboil it and let it cool, after which grate or finely chop it.
3. Rinse the rice grains in warm water and let them cook soft in the milk.
4. Finely chop the pork and mix it together with the liver, seasonings, syrup and the rinsed raisins in the cooled porridge.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-covered mold, sprinkle crushed rusks over the top and bake in a good oven heat for approximately 1 hour.
6. Serve with clarified butter.
