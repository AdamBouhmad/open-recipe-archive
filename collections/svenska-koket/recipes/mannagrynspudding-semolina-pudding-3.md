## Ingredients

- 150 gr. semolina
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 20 sweet almonds, 10 bitter almonds
- 40 gr. common raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat the semolina and cook the porridge as in no. 791 but it is ready after 20 minutes. Then pour it out and add the rest of the butter immediately along with seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds and the well-rinsed, blanched and pitted raisins. When the porridge is cold, stir in the whisked egg yolks and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated cake form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour. Serve immediately after removing from the oven, with jam, compote or fruit sauce.
