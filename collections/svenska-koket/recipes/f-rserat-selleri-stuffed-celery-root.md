## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 smaller celery roots
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 2 dcl. cream
- 1/2 tbsp. salt
- 2 hg. beef
- 1/2 hg. veal
- 1/2 hg. lard
- 1/2 tbsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 3 chopped pickled onions or 1/2 tbsp. chopped Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.) for frying
- 3 dcl. broth

## Directions

1. Peel the celery roots, rinse well, place in boiling salted water and cook for about 1 hour, or until almost soft, then remove and let cool.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass together with the lard 3 times through a meat grinder. Then add the flour, egg, seasonings, and cream little by little, and work the forcemeat for about 1/2 hour. Stir in the onion and taste the forcemeat.
3. Cut out a lid from the celery roots and hollow them from above. Fill them with the forcemeat, place the lid on top and tie the celery roots with string.
