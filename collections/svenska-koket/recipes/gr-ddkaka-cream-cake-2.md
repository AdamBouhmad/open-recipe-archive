## Ingredients

- 6 sweet and 3 bitter almonds or 1/4 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 4 eggs
- 4 tbsp. sugar (60 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick sweet or sour cream
- 50 gr. dry wheat flour
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are mixed for 2 minutes, the cream and milk are added, the sugar and chocolate are added, and the mixture is brought to a good boil and allowed to cool.
2. The egg yolks are added thereafter, one at a time, with vigorous stirring, and lastly the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
3. The mixture is poured into a glazed mold and the pudding is baked in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
4. Unmold and serve warm with vanilla sauce.
