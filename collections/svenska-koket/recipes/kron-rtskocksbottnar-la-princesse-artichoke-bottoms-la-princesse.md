## Ingredients

- 12 large canned artichoke bottoms
- 1 can asparagus tips (1/4 liter)
- 1 can French peas (1/4 liter)
- 3/4 liter boiling water
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g)
- 5 deciliters mousseline sauce
- 3 truffles
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Place the bottoms in the water with the butter added; when it boils again, remove them with a slotted spoon and let them drain.
2. Warm the asparagus and peas in a water bath, remove them and let them drain.
3. Place the asparagus on 6 of the bottoms and peas on the others.
4. Pour the mousseline sauce onto a hot silver dish; sprinkle the finely chopped truffle over it, arrange the bottoms in a ring on it, baste with the clarified butter and serve very hot.
