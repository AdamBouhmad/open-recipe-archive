## Ingredients

- 12 oranges
- 1/2 liter thick cream
- zest of 1 orange
- juice of 4 oranges
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 4 leaves gelatin

## Directions

1. Select 12 equal, beautiful oranges.
2. Rinse and dry them well.
3. Remove the zest from half of each orange, leaving a 1/2 cm. wide strip to form a handle.
4. Scoop out the fruit flesh carefully, and carve the basket edges and handle into a pretty pattern.
5. Soak the baskets in cold water for 1/2 hour, drain and fill with orange snow.
6. To make the orange snow, whisk the cream until stiff peaks form.
7. Remove all the white from the orange zest, chop the zest finely and mix into the cream with the orange juice, sugar and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little warm water.
8. Set the baskets in a cool place so the cream sets.
9. Garnish with whipped cream and serve with pastries, for example almond cakes.
