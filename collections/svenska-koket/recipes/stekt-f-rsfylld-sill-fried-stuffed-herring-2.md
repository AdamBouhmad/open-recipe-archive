## Ingredients

- 6 fresh herrings (= 2 kg)
- 12 anchovies
- 1 cup crushed hardtack
- 1/2 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 cup coarse rye flour
- 3 tbsp stock

## Directions

1. Open the herring along the back, remove the entrails and backbone; rinse it well, dry it, and salt it lightly.
2. Clean and debone the anchovies.
