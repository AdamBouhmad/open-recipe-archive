## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 170 gr. caster sugar
- 1 egg
- 600 gr. wheat flour
- 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1 cup pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with the sugar until it becomes white.
2. Add the egg, and lastly work in the flour.
3. Roll the dough into small wreaths, dip these in a mixture of cinnamon and pearl sugar and bake in moderate oven heat.
