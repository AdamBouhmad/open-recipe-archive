## Ingredients

- 12 small carrots
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the carrots and cook in salted water until soft.
2. Drain the water and remove the skin while still warm.
3. Melt the butter in a pot together with the sugar.
4. Add the carrots and when well coated with glaze, remove them.
5. Serve as vegetables with meat dishes.
