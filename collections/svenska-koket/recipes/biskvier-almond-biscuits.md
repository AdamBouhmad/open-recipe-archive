## Ingredients

- 150 gr. sweet almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 150 gr. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven and drive through an almond mill.
2. Whip the egg whites and sugar for 1/2 hour, then add the almonds and stir the mass for another 10 minutes.
3. Place the dough with a teaspoon in small clumps on a well-cleaned, buttered and floured baking sheet.
4. Bake the biscuits in weak oven heat until they are light yellow and release from the baking sheet.
