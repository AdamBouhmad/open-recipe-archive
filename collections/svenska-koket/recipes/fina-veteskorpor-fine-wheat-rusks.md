## Ingredients

- 3 kg wheat flour
- 1 tsk salt
- 2 liters lukewarm milk
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 6 hg butter or margarine
- 2-4 kkp sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for finer wheat bread (no. 642).
2. Bake out into small round buns.
3. When they have risen, bake them in good oven heat.
4. When cooled, cut them in half into upper and lower rusks.
5. Place them in medium oven heat to get a beautiful golden-brown color.
6. Remove them, let them cool and place in weak oven heat to become completely dry and crispy.
