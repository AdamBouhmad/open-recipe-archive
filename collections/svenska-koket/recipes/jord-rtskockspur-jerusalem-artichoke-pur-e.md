## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 2½ liters broth
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- salt, white pepper
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the Jerusalem artichokes in the usual way and then cook them in the broth until soft enough to pass through a sieve.
2. Fry butter and flour together, add the passed purée and let it cook for 10-15 minutes. Skim the soup and season.
3. Whip the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl, and pour in the purée with vigorous whisking.
4. Serve with small cooked Jerusalem artichokes or with toasted bread.
