## Ingredients

- 1 calf tongue
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- white pepper
- 3 cups good broth
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 4 deciliters meat stock
- 1 teaspoon mondamin flour
- 1 tablespoon Madeira
- 2 tablespoons finely cut, browned mushrooms

## Directions

1. Brush the tongue well, rinse it, place it in the boiling water with salt added, and let it boil until the skin can be easily pulled off.
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the tongue in and brown it nicely on all sides, salt it and dilute with the broth, with soy sauce added.
3. Let it cook slowly until it is tender, approximately 1 hour, and baste it every ten minutes so it does not dry out.
4. Slice it, arrange it on a warm serving dish and garnish with small bread croutons.
5. Thicken the meat stock with the mondamin flour, stirred in the wine, and let the sauce boil approximately 5 minutes.
6. Add the mushrooms and season the sauce with salt and pepper.
7. Pour a little of the sauce over the tongue and pour the rest into a sauce boat.
8. Serve as a breakfast dish with browned potatoes.
