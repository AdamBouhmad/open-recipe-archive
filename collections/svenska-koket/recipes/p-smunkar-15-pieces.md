## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 12 tbsp. cream milk
- 120 gr. wheat flour
- 20 gr. potato starch
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Bring butter and milk to a boil, stir in the flour.
2. Let the batter cook until it leaves the sides of the pan, then remove from heat.
3. Stir in one egg yolk immediately, and when the batter has cooled, fold in the remaining egg yolks and lastly the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Take a full teaspoon of batter for each pastry.
5. Cook the pastries in deep fat or bake them in a pastry pan in the oven and sprinkle with sugar as soon as they are done.
6. Serve with jam.
