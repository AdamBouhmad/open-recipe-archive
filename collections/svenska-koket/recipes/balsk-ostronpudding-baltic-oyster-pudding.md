## Ingredients

- 4-5 good herring milt
- 1 liter skim milk
- 2 deciliters finely crushed rusks
- 5 deciliters cream milk
- 80 gr. clarified butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a little nutmeg
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the herring milt well and let it soak in the skim milk for about 2 hours.
2. Wrap it in a fish towel, chop it finely and mix it with the finely crushed rusks, cream milk, clarified butter, well-whipped eggs and spices.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined baking form and bake in the oven until it has taken on a beautiful golden-brown color, about 45 minutes.
4. Serve with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
