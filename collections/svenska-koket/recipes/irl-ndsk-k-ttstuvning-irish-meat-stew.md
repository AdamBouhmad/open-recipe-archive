## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg lamb
- 2 red onions
- 1 tbsp salt
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- 6 cups water
- 1½ liters potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut into small pieces and place in the bottom of a pot.
2. Peel the onion, parboil, cut into slices and place with the seasonings on top of the meat.
3. Add the boiling water and let the meat cook slowly for ¼ hour.
4. Then add the peeled and sliced potatoes, and let the stew cook another ½ hour approximately.
5. Serve the meat in the middle of the platter, potatoes around it, and pour the sauce over.
