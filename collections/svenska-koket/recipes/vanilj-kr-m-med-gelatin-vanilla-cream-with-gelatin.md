## Ingredients

- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. water
- 1 egg
- 110 gr. sugar
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 vanilla pod
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut it into pieces, and dissolve it in 3 tbsp. boiling water.
2. Mix it with the egg, egg yolks, sugar, milk, and vanilla in a well-scoured saucepan.
3. Whisk the mixture vigorously over the heat until the cream is thick and creamy.
4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
5. Add the cream whipped to stiff peaks and pour the cream into a mold rinsed with water and sprinkled with sugar.
6. Let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours to set.
7. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a sharp knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper, and garnish with fruit or biscuits.
8. Half the cream can be colored green with pistachio paste, poured into the mold, and allowed to set somewhat on ice, after which the rest of the light yellow cream is poured in. The cream is set on ice, turned out, and served with raspberry or strawberry sauce.
