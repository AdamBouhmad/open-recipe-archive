## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 lit. unwhipped milk
- 1 hg. chocolate
- 30 gr. cocoa
- 1 tbsp. mondamin flour (15 gr.)
- sugar to taste
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Heat the milk; when it reaches boiling point, pour off two-thirds of it into the remaining third and dissolve the chocolate, broken into small pieces, and cocoa.
2. Add the reserved milk and when the soup boils, thicken it with the mondamin flour, dissolved in 1/2 cup cold milk.
3. Let the soup boil while stirring for 5 minutes and add sugar to taste.
4. Pour into the soup bowl and place the stiffly whipped cream on top.
5. Alternatively, water can be used instead of milk.
