## Ingredients

- 3 large pears
- 1/2 coffee cup sugar
- 6 parts thin cream
- 75 grams granulated sugar
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 3 parts water
- 3 teaspoons potato flour
- 4-5 egg yolks
- berries for garnish
- 100 grams dried apricots
- 3 parts water
- 100 grams granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor.
2. Thicken with potato flour mixed in a little cream, let boil briefly and remove from heat.
3. Whisk the egg yolks together in a bowl and add the cream with vigorous whisking.
4. Return the cream to heat and let it simmer but not boil.
5. When thickened, pour onto a round platter.
6. Peel and halve the pears.
7. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water; add the pears and cook until soft, then arrange in a ring on the cream.
8. Soak the apricots in warm water for a few hours before cooking, then cook in the same water.
9. When soft, strain them and season the sauce with sugar and pour over the pears.
10. Place a halved berry on each pear.
11. Put the platter in a moderate oven for about 10 minutes, then serve.
