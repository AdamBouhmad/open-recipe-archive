## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg veal roast (leftovers)
- 1 hectogram cooked pork
- 1/4 Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts broth, cooked on veal bones
- pepper and salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 part cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and pork into cubes and mix well. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry in butter, stir in flour, and add the broth together with seasonings.
2. Let the sauce cook a few minutes, then add the meat. Finish the stew with egg yolk and cream and season with lemon juice.
3. Garnish, if desired, with poached eggs.
