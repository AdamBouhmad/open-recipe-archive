## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg spare ribs
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 apples
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 hectogram prunes
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- meat gravy
- 1 tablespoon flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Break the spare ribs, then wash them with a cloth dipped in hot water and rub them in with pepper and salt.
2. Rinse the apples and dry them well, remove the cores and cut them into slices, which are dipped in sugar.
3. Spread out the spare ribs with the bony side up and cover with apple slices and the rinsed prunes.
4. Roll it up and tie it in three places with sail twine, previously dipped in boiling water.
5. Brown the meat in a roasting pot, add boiling water and let it cook with a tightly fitted lid until the meat is tender, which takes about 2 hours.
6. Remove it, remove the sail twine, and cut the meat into nice pieces, which are placed high on a warm platter and garnished with apple slices and prunes.
7. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
