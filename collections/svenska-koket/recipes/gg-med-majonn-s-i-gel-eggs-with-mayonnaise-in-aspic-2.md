## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 12 shrimp, parsley
- 2 fresh eggs for mayonnaise
- 1/2 tsp. salt, cayenne
- 1/4 tsp. French mustard
- 1-1/2 parts fine salad oil
- 1 tbsp. tarragon vinegar
- 1 tbsp. lemon juice
- 1 tbsp. boiling water
- 1 leaf gelatin for mayonnaise
- 1/2 liter veal broth
- 5 leaves gelatin for aspic
- 1 tsp. vinegar
- 2 egg whites and the rinsed shells
- 1 tbsp. cold water

## Directions

1. The mayonnaise should preferably be prepared the day before it is to be used to become sufficiently firm.
2. Separate the egg yolks from the whites (the whites and shells are used for the aspic), place in a large bowl together with the seasonings and mustard and stir with a wooden spoon for 15 minutes.
3. Add the oil thereafter drop by drop, alternating with the vinegar and lemon juice; finally stir in the boiling water.
4. Taste the mayonnaise and let it stand in a cold place for a few hours.
5. One hour before it is to be used, add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1/2 tbsp. hot water.
6. The eggs, which should be cold, are peeled, the white is trimmed at the ends and each egg is cut into 4 slices, which are placed on a deep platter.
7. In the middle of each slice place a peeled shrimp tail and a small sprig of parsley.
8. Pipe the mayonnaise through a fine fluted pastry tube in a beautiful ring around the egg yolk.
9. Pour the broth into a saucepan together with the rinsed gelatin, the vinegar and the egg whites lightly beaten with water, mixed with the crushed eggshells.
10. The mixture is brought to a good boil with whisking, the lid is placed on the saucepan, which is removed from the heat and allowed to stand at the side of the stove for about 15 minutes.
11. Strain the broth through a cloth or napkin wrung out in hot water.
12. Let the clear aspic cool and pour it carefully over the garnished eggs.
13. When the dish has set, remove the egg slices with a round cutter so that a border of aspic extends beyond the egg edge.
14. Arrange them on an elongated platter, garnish with parsley or salad and serve at the smörgåsbord.
