## Ingredients

- 1 fresh ham, approximately 5 kg
- 40 g salt
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 2 dl crushed juniper berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 red onions, in slices
- 1 celery, in cubes
- 2 liters weak stock water
- 1 bottle red wine
- Water
- 8 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 piece ginger

## Directions

1. Remove the skin neatly. Rub the ham well with salt and pepper and place it in a deep dish. Sprinkle with juniper berries, bay leaves, red onion and celery and pour weak stock and enough water over it so it covers the meat.
2. Let the ham marinate for approximately one week, turning it daily.
3. Cook it in wine and water with the spices as in recipe 556, and serve hot or cold.
