## Ingredients

- Meringue mass: 8 large egg whites, 400 grams powdered sugar
- Frozen cream: 1/2 liter thick cream, 1 tablespoon powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar, 5 preserved pears with their juice
- For garnish: 1/2 liter thick cream, carmine

## Directions

1. Cut 6 strips from stiff paper, which are joined together into rings of different sizes.
2. The first should be 12 cm in diameter, each of the others 1 cm smaller than the preceding one.
3. Butter the paper rings with washed butter or with cooking oil, and on the outer side press the meringue mass through a piping bag in the form of meringue upon meringue.
4. The size of the meringues diminishes according to the papers.
5. The last paper should be a round disk on which a few leaves are formed with the piping bag.
6. Sprinkle the meringues with sugar and bake in gentle oven heat, remove and let them cool.
7. Carefully remove the paper so that the meringues do not break.
8. Then arrange wreath upon wreath until they form a pyramid.
9. Cut the preserved pears into pieces.
10. Whip the cream together with the sugar until stiff peaks form, blend in the pears with their juice, and freeze the cream.
11. Fill the pyramid with this and garnish with whipped cream beaten until stiff and colored with a little carmine.
