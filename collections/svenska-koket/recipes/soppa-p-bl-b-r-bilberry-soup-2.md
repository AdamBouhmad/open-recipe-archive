## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh bilberries or 4 deciliters dried bilberries
- 2 liters water
- Zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm.)
- 1 1/2–2 coffee spoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (80 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them in a colander, and let them drain.
2. Put them in boiling water with the lemon zest and cinnamon, and let them cook until loose.
3. Strain the berries out, add sugar to the liquid, bring it to a boil, and thicken with the potato flour mixed in 1/2 coffee spoon cold water.
4. Let the soup cook 2 minutes, taste it, and pour it up to cool.
5. Serve with klimp, whipped cream, and rusks or biscuits.
6. If using dried bilberries, rinse them very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they will be cooked.
