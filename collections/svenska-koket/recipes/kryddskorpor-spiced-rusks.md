## Ingredients

- 50 g. cream
- 1 liter unskimmed milk
- 5 hg. rye sifted flour
- 1.2 kg. wheat flour
- 60 g. compressed yeast
- 2 tsp. salt (10 g.)
- 4 dcl. syrup
- 4 tbsp. finely chopped orange peel
- 2 tbsp. crushed fennel
- 3 eggs
- 150 g. butter

## Directions

1. Melt the cream in the milk — the rusks become thereby softer. — The dough is made as described on p. 462 and 1 dcl. syrup is added at once. When the dough has risen to double its size, it is worked again with the rest of the syrup, the orange peel, the fennel, the beaten eggs, the melted butter and the rest of the flour except 1 hg. of the wheat flour, which is saved for baking. The dough is turned out onto a floured work surface, kneaded and divided into 12 parts, which are worked well, rolled out into long, thin buns, which are laid on buttered pans, allowed to rise and baked in a warm oven. When the buns are cold, they are cut into 1 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are split into upper and lower rusks, cut, if desired, and dried as wheat rusks (see no. 957).
