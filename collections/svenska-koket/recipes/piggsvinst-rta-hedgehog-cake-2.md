## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- 70 grams potato flour
- shell and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the pan
- 50 grams sifted sugar for glaze
- 1 part white French wine for glaze
- 50-100 grams sweet almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Then add the lemon peel and juice, as well as the flour.
3. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn form and bake in weak oven heat.
5. Glaze with the prepared glaze.
6. Blanch the sweet almonds, cut into fine strips, and stick them thickly into the cake so that it resembles a hedgehog.
