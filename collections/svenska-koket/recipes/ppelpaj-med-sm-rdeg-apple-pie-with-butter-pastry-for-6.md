## Ingredients

- For pastry: 2 cups flour
- 8 tbsp. butter
- 1.5 tbsp. brandy
- For filling: 2 liters apples
- 1.5 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour on a pastry board, wash the butter and distribute finely in the flour, working it in with fingertips.
2. Then quickly add the brandy, and as soon as the dough is cohesive, set it aside to become cold.
3. Peel the apples, remove the cores and cut into thin slices and place in a heatproof mold with sugar between each layer.
4. The last layer should be fruit.
5. If the apples are not juicy, add 0.5 cup water.
6. Roll out the pie dough to 0.5 cm. thickness.
7. Cut a lid for the mold, brush the edge of the mold with water and cover with a thick strip of dough, which is pressed firmly on the edge and brushed with water.
8. Place the dough lid on and press firmly against the inner edge of the mold, then trim the outer edges of the lid and strip with a sharp knife.
9. Prick the edge with the back of the knife and crimp with the tip of the knife.
10. Make four holes in the lid with a coarse whisk.
11. Brush the pie with beaten egg and place in good oven heat to bake until beautifully golden brown for about 1 hour.
12. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
