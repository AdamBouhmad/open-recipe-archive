## Ingredients

- 2 raw egg yolks
- 8 anchovies
- 1 small Portuguese onion or 2 large red onions

## Directions

1. Place the egg yolks on a small oblong dish.
2. Clean the anchovies, remove the bones, chop finely and arrange in a ring around the yolks.
3. Around this, place the finely chopped onion.
4. At the table, blend all ingredients together with a fork.
