## Ingredients

- 240 gr. butter
- 1 tsk. crushed cloves
- 1 tsk. ground cinnamon
- 2 msk. finely chopped orange peel
- 4 hg. sugar
- 1½ kkp sour cream
- 6 eggs
- 450 gr. wheat flour
- 2 tsk. baking soda
- 1 tsk. butter for the pan
- 1 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until it becomes white.
2. Add the sugar, spices, eggs, and wheat flour sifted with baking soda.
3. Last, fold in the sour cream beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pan and bake in moderate oven heat.
