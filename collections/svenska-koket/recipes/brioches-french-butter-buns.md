## Ingredients

- 5 hg. prima wheat flour
- 15 gr. compressed yeast
- 1 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 5 gr. salt
- 45 gr. sugar
- 3-4 cardamom pods (optional)
- 3 eggs
- 3 hg. washed butter
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Sift the flour.
2. In one third of the flour crumble the yeast, then add the milk and work the dough quickly (it should be loose), roll it on the bakeboard into a ball with a cross cut on top.
3. Place the dough in a bowl, cover and let it rise in a warm place about 1 hour.
4. Then work in the salt, sugar, cardamom (if desired), and the eggs one at a time.
5. Between each addition of egg, work the dough 5 minutes and add more flour so the dough becomes properly firm.
6. Roll out the dough and incorporate the butter as in the previous recipe, then place in a bowl, cover and let it rise in a moderately warm room 6-8 hours.
7. After 3 hours, stop the rising by placing the dough on a floured bakeboard and patting it with your hand.
8. Place it back in the bowl and let it rise another 4-5 hours.
9. Place the dough in brioche molds, brush with egg, let it rise and bake in a warm oven.
10. It can also be formed into small round buns with a small indentation in the center, in which place a small ball the size of a small nut.
