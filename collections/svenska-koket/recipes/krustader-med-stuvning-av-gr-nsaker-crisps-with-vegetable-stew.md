## Ingredients

- 1 bunch tender carrots
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 6 wax beans
- 1 1/2 cup fresh peas
- 12 asparagus tips
- 8 dcl water
- 12-14 crisps

## Directions

1. Prepare vegetables as indicated in the ingredient list.
2. Cook in water until tender.
3. Prepare stew and fill crisps following the method described in the previous crisps recipes.
