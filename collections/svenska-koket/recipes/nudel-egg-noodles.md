## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tbsp. good cream
- 1 1/2 tbsp. clarified butter (30 gr.)
- flour, as much as needed for a firm dough

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with the cream for 10 minutes, add the butter and mix in as much flour as needed to make a firm dough.
2. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a five-ore coin and let it rest in a cold place to stiffen overnight.
3. Prick it out into 1 1/2 cm. wide strips, which are cut across into fine strips.
4. Let these dry well and can be stored for several weeks in a tin box with a tightly closed lid.
