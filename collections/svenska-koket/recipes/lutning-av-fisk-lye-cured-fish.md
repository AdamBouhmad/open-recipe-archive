## Ingredients

- 2 kg. spillånga or gråsej
- 1.4 lit. kalk
- 150 gr. soda
- vatten after behov

## Directions

1. Saw the fish into two or more pieces depending on size, and let it lie in cold water for a week, changing the water twice.
2. Brush it thoroughly clean with a scrub brush, especially on the skin side.
3. Spread a layer of lime in the bottom of a tub, then place the thickest fish pieces and sprinkle with lime, then fish again, and continue until all fish is laid down, but reserve about 4 dcl. of the lime to sprinkle over during the luring.
4. Pound the soda and dissolve it in 1 lit. lukewarm water.
5. Mix the soda solution with enough cold water that it stands over the fish.
6. Add it carefully around the edge of the tub so that the lime is not washed away from the fish.
7. As the fish swells, add more water and sprinkle a little more of the reserved lime.
8. The smallest pieces are usually ready after 2 days, the large ones after 4 days.
9. Remove them as they become ready and let them lie in fresh water for about 4 days, changing the water once a day.
10. This batch is sufficient for about 30 persons.
