## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms fresh salmon
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg for breading
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 150 grams butter for frying and serving
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the salmon piece and dry with a cloth dipped in cold water, cut into slices 1 1/2 cm thick, which are flattened with a knife blade and rubbed with salt and pepper.
2. Dip in the egg beaten with water, dredge in a mixture of flour and breadcrumbs and fry until golden brown in half the butter.
3. Serve the cutlets as a breakfast dish with the remaining butter, browned and mixed with lemon juice.
