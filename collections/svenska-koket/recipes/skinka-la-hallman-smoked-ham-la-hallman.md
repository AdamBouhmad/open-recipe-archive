## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. smoked ham
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- parsley
- lost eggs or fried potatoes for serving

## Directions

1. Cut the ham into thin slices and trim away the rind neatly.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly, place the ham in it and brown beautifully on both sides, then place on a warm serving platter.
3. Whisk out the pan with the cream and let the sauce come to a boil, then pour it over the ham.
4. Garnish with parsley and serve with poached eggs or fried potatoes as a breakfast dish.
