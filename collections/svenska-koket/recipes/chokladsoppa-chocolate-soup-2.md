## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 lit. unskim­med milk
- 1 hg. chocolate
- 30 gr. cocoa
- 1 tbsp. mondamin flour (15 gr.)
- sugar to taste
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Heat the milk; when it reaches boiling point, pour off two-thirds of it and dissolve the chocolate, broken into small pieces, and the cocoa in the remaining milk.
2. Add the reserved milk and when the soup boils, thicken it with the mondamin flour stirred into 1/2 cup cold milk.
3. Let the soup boil while stirring for 5 minutes and add sugar to taste; pour into the soup bowl and top with the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Water can be used instead of milk if desired.
