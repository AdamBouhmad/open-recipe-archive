## Ingredients

- 1 kg. cooked or fried meat
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- 1/2 liter broth or pan sauce
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into appropriately sized pieces.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in the butter.
3. Fry the flour in it, and add the broth or pan sauce together with the cucumber cut into thin slices.
4. Let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes, then add the meat and bring to a boil.
5. Season the dish with pepper and salt.
6. If fried meat is used, it should not simmer in the sauce, as it will become tough.
