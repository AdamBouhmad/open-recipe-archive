## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter thin cream
- 1 hectogram sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 3 egg yolks
- 50-100 g sugar
- 0.5 part arrack
- green caramel coloring
- 30 pistachio almonds
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The sweet almonds and bitter almonds are blanched, passed through an almond grinder or grated and boiled with the thin cream for approximately 10 minutes, after which the mixture is strained.
2. The cream is brought to a boil again and blended with vigorous whisking into the egg yolks.
3. The mixture is then allowed to simmer over the heat until it thickens, after which it is lifted from the heat.
4. It is flavored with sugar and arrack, colored and put into an ice cream machine to freeze.
5. When the frozen mixture is half frozen, the blanched and chopped pistachio almonds and the cream whipped to peaks are folded in, after which freezing is continued.
6. Pistachio frozen cream is served in glasses together with some white frozen cream.
