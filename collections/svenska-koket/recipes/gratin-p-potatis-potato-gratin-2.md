## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. peeled potatoes (approximately 3/4 liter unpeeled)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper (a little grated nutmeg)
- 1 egg
- 3–4 deciliters boiled milk
- 20–30 gr. fresh, grated Emmental cheese
- 1 slice Portuguese onion
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons grated Emmental cheese

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as described previously, peel and place in water as they are peeled, dry in a cloth and cut into thin slices, which are placed in a dish.
2. Add the seasonings, the beaten egg, the boiled milk and the cheese and mix the mixture well.
3. Rub an ovenproof dish with the onion slice and butter it with half of the butter.
4. Add the potato slices, sprinkle with cheese and cover with the remaining butter, distributed in flakes.
5. Bake the gratin in a warm oven until the potatoes are soft, or approximately 45 minutes.
6. Serve with the smorgasbord or with smoked or boiled sausage.
