## Ingredients

- 200 gr. sweet almond
- 200 gr. powdered sugar
- 2-3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and pass through a mill.
2. Stir the egg whites and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the almonds.
3. If the mass is too hard, add a little more egg white and work the dough for another 10 minutes.
4. Pipe the dough through a fairly coarse star tip in the form of S or wreaths onto a well-buttered baking sheet or on oblate.
5. Bake the pastries in not too warm an oven until they are half-dry and have a light yellow color.
6. If desired, garnish after they are cold with red and white icing.
