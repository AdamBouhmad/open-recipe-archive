## Ingredients

- 750 g fresh or 300 g dried apples
- 2 liters water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1½-2 teaspoons crushed sugar
- 25 g rice or potato flour
- ½ deciliter French wine or sherry

## Directions

1. The apples for this should be tart and not fully ripe. Rinse, dry, do not peel, cut into 6 wedges; remove the flower, stem, and core.
2. Place the apples in cold water together with the lemon zest and cinnamon and let cook until they fall apart, then pass through a coarse sieve.
3. Let the fruit purée come to a boil with the sugar and thicken with the rice flour, stirred into ½ cup cold water, then let it cook while stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Season the soup with the wine and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or toasted bread. If using dried apples, rinse these very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked.
