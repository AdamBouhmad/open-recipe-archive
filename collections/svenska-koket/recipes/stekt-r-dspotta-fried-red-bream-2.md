## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg red bream
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr)
- 3 dcl milk for breading
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr)
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil
- salt, white pepper
- 1 1/2 cup crushed rusks
- 1/2 kg lard for frying
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the red breams as in the preceding recipe, but cut off the head and remove the dark skin by scraping the tail fin upward with a pointed knife until enough skin loosens that you can wind it around the knife blade, then roll the knife toward the head and wind the skin around it
2. Rub the fish with salt, let rest for 1 hour, dry again and bread as in recipe no.
