## Ingredients

- 1 kg. cooked, boned fish meat or 1 1/4 kg. pike
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 4 decilitres fish stock
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 6 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the fish meat into beautiful, even slices.
2. Grease a fireproof dish or silver dish with half of the butter and lay the fish slices on it, distributing the remaining butter in flakes on top of the fish.
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the fish stock, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, season with salt, sugar and white pepper, pour it out and let it cool.
4. Add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the sauce for 10 minutes, lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the sauce over the fish, sprinkle the grated cheese on top and place the dish in a hot oven to gratinée for 15-20 minutes.
6. Serve, if desired, with lemon or mushroom sauce.
