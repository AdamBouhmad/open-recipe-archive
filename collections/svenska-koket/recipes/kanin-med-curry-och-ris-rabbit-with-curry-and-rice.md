## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed rabbit, about 3 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 red onion in slices
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1-2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1/2 liter broth
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons thick cream

## Directions

1. The rabbit is treated and cut as in the previous recipe.
2. The butter is browned in an iron pot, the meat is added, browned nicely and removed with a slotted spoon.
3. The onion, flour and curry powder are added and fried well with the butter in the pot, the broth is diluted, and the sauce is brought to a boil.
4. The meat is placed in the sauce, the lemon juice is added and the meat is allowed to simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until it is tender, or about 1 hour.
5. Place it on a warm plate, whisk up the sauce with the cream, bring it to a boil, taste it and pour it over the meat.
6. Around the meat garnish with boiled rice, sprinkled with 1-2 teaspoons curry powder.
