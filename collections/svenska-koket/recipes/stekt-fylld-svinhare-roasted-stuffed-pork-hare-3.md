## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork hare
- 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3/4 tsp. ginger
- 3 fine, tart apples
- 1 hg. prunes
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. solid meat extract (4 gr.)

## Directions

1. Free the hare from most of the fat, dry with a cloth wrung out in hot water, rub with the lemon juice and the spices, well mixed.
