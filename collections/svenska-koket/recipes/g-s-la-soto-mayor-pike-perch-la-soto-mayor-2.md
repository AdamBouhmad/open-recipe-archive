## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. pike-perch
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 2 tsk. finely chopped red onion
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 msk. salad oil
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 3 msk. wheat flour (30 gr.)
- 2 hg. puff pastry

## Directions

1. Scale the pike-perch, clean according to page 171, cut away the backbone but keep the head and tail. Cut off the gills and fins and rub the fish with salt and white pepper, finely chopped red onion and lemon juice; let stand 30 minutes.
