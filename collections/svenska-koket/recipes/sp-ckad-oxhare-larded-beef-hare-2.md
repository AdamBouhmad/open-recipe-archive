## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1-1½ hg lard
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 liter broth or water
- ½ tbsp butter (for sauce)
- 1¼ tbsp flour
- beef stock
- 2 tsp tomato paste

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and lard it thereafter in dense rows with finely cut lard strips.
2. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, and brown the meat in it, salt it and dilute with half of the boiling broth.
3. Let the hare thereafter slowly roast with a tightly closed lid for 2 hours, during which it is occasionally diluted and basted with the broth.
4. Slice it into thin slices, lay it together again and place on the serving platter and garnish with vegetables.
5. Strain and skim the beef stock, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
