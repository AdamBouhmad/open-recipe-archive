## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. sugar
- 2 1/2 dcl. water
- 1 tbsp. glucose (15 gr.)
- 10 drops peppermint oil
- carmine for coloring
- oil for preparation

## Directions

1. Cook sugar, water, and glucose to crack stage (see sugar cooking). Add peppermint oil.
2. Pour half the sugar onto a lightly oiled marble slab and continuously fold the edges inward with an oiled knife until cooled.
3. Pull the cooled sugar between oiled hands or on a hook until white, then stretch into a thin length.
4. Cook the remaining sugar to caramel stage, color red with carmine, pour out, roll out, and place on the white sugar.
5. Twist and cut the combined sugar diagonally into 2-3 cm. long candies.
