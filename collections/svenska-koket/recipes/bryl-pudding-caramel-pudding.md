## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 40 gr. sugar
- 1/2 liter cream-milk
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 3 egg whites
- 3 dcl. sugar (255 gr.) for the mold
- 1/2 dcl. water for the mold

## Directions

1. Brylépudding is best if prepared the day before it is to be served.
2. Cook the sugar with the water until it is dry, then stir it until it becomes liquid again and has a nice light brown color.
3. Pour some of the sugar mixture into a pie mold, turning it around so that the mixture covers both the bottom and sides.
4. The remaining sugar mixture is thinned with water and served as sauce with the pudding.
5. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar for 20 minutes.
6. Heat the cream-milk (2 dcl. cream, 3 dcl. milk) with the vanilla bean for about 5 minutes and add it to the stirred egg yolks under vigorous whisking; continue whisking until the cream is cold, then remove the vanilla bean and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
7. Pour the cream into the coated mold, and let the pudding boil in a water bath in ordinary oven heat for about 1 1/2 hours.
8. Let it stand in the mold until the next day, or until it is completely cold. Turn out and serve with the remaining caramel sauce.
