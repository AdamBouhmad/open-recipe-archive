## Ingredients

- Shortcrust pastry: 400 gr butter, 400 gr wheat flour, 90 gr sweet almond, 90 gr powdered sugar
- Rhubarb filling: 1 kg fresh rhubarb, 200 gr sugar
- Meringue mixture: 5 egg whites, 8 tablespoons powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
