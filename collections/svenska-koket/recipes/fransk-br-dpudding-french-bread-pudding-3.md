## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-cent wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 30 sweet almonds
- 6-8 bitter almonds
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 0.5 liter cream milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar for the mold
- 5-6 tablespoons boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a frying pan and dilute with the boiling water, then use this to line a mold.
2. Slice the bread and spread butter on both sides.
3. Layer the slices in the mold alternately with the blanched and chopped almonds and grated lemon zest.
4. Whisk the eggs and milk together, add the sugar, and pour this mixture over the bread.
5. Let the mold stand for about 1 hour or until the bread is thoroughly soaked, then place in the oven to bake.
6. When it has set and taken on a beautiful light yellow color, remove it and turn it out onto a dish or bowl.
7. Serve cold.
