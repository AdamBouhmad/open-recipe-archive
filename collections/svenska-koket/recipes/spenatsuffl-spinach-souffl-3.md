## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. spinach
- 3 lit. water for blanching
- 4 tsk. salt (20 gr.)
- 1 1/2 msk. flour (15 gr.)
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2-2 dcl. cream, milk or broth
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1-2 tsk. sugar
- 2-3 eggs
- 1/2 msk. butter for mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Treat and prepare the spinach as in the previous recipe, place in a colander and let it cool, then stir in the egg yolks, one at a time.
2. When the spinach is cold, fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites, pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumbed pudding mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
3. Turn out and serve immediately as an appetizer with creamed butter, or garnish it with small boiled carrots, browned potatoes and peas and serve with fried meat.
4. The mixture can also be baked in small pastry molds (in a water bath in the oven, 20-30 minutes). Turn out and serve as garnish around fried meat, together with other vegetables.
