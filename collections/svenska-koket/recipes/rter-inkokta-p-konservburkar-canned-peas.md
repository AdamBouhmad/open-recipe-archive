## Ingredients

- Peas
- Cold cooked salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and separate the smaller ones from the larger ones, then rinse the peas. Place them in canning jars and cover with cold cooked salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water). Screw the jars shut or seal them and cook according to general rules for 1.5 to 1 hour, depending on the size of the peas.
2. Alternatively, the peas may be briefly blanched for 3 minutes in lightly salted water with a little sugar added before filling the jars. To preserve the color, add a knife's point of bicarbonate of soda and 1 bunch of parsley to the cooking water. Treat them as described above.
