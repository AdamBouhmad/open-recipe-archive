## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef (French cut)
- 3 pieces of lard with rind
- 1 calf's foot
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
- 8 white peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 1 large onion
- 3 carrots
- 2 parts French wine
- 1 part cognac
- 1 1/2 liters weak broth
- 5 large fried onions for garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water
2. Cut the lard into strips, roll in salt and pepper, and lard the meat in dense rows
3. Tie the meat with string (first dipped in boiling water) and place in a pot together with the blanched pork rind, blanched calf's foot, remaining salt, white peppercorns, cloves, wine, cognac, peeled onion, scraped carrots, and broth
4. Bring to a boil and skim well
5. Cover with a tightly fitting lid and let the meat simmer slowly for 3-4 hours
6. When tender, remove the meat and take off the string
7. Slice the meat and place on a warm plate, sprinkle with a little salt, and garnish with sliced carrots and fried onions, serve with French potatoes
8. Strain the broth, skim well, taste, and serve with the meat
9. The sauce can be finished according to general sauce-making rules if desired
