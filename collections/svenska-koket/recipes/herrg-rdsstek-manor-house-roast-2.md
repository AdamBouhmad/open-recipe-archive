## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg beef chuck or brisket
- 1.5 hg lard
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 liter boiling beef broth
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 small red onions
- 6 allspice berries
- 2 anchovies
- 2 tbsp cognac
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and lard it (see page 232), rub with a mixture of white pepper and salt and tie with twine (previously dipped in hot water) so it has a beautiful shape.
2. Brown the butter in a braising pot, which should be just large enough for the meat to fit.
3. Place the roast in and brown until a beautiful crust forms, then add the broth.
4. Place the bay leaf, peeled onions, allspice berries, cleaned and boned anchovies on top of the roast, and finally add the cognac, vinegar and syrup.
5. Cover and let the meat braise slowly until tender, about 2.5-3 hours, turning it several times.
6. Place the roast on a warm platter and carve at the table.
7. Serve with vegetable pastries and salads as well as reduced sauce with cream added (see no. 429).
