## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 210 gr. caster sugar
- 125 gr. sweet almond
- 375 gr. wheat flour
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 25 gr. sweet almond
- 1 dcl. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and work out the water thoroughly
2. Stir it with the sugar for 5 minutes
3. Add the blanched and ground almond
4. Finally work in the sifted flour, except for 75 gr., which is set aside for dusting
5. Roll out the dough to 1/2 cm thickness
6. Cut out with a pointed mold into cookies
7. Brush with lightly beaten egg white and sprinkle with blanched and ground almond and sugar
8. Bake until golden brown in a not-too-hot oven
