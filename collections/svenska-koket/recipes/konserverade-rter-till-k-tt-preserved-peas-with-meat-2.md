## Ingredients

- 1 can peas (1/2 liter)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. The peas together with the liquid are poured into a saucepan and brought to a boil.
2. The liquid is drained off, and the peas are mixed with the cold butter and the parsley.
3. Served as in recipe no. 717.
