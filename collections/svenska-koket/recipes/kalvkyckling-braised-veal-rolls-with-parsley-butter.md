## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal loin
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 large bunch parsley
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.) for frying
- 1 dcl. broth
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and treat as in the previous recipe and sprinkle the meat slices with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse, wring out in a cloth, chop coarsely and work into the butter.
3. Divide the parsley butter into 12 parts and place one part on each meat slice, which is then rolled up and tied at two places with fine twine.
4. Fry and serve as in the previous recipe.
