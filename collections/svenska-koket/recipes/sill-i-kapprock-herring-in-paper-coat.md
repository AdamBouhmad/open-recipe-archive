## Ingredients

- 2 large salt herrings
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 red onions
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 1 sheet white writing paper
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 g) for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times
2. Remove the skin and cut off the head and tail
3. Cut open along the back, remove the bones and innards, rinse well and dry with a fish towel
4. Sprinkle the herring inside with pepper and sliced peeled red onion
5. Reassemble so that it retains its shape
6. Wrap each herring well with 1/2 sheet of paper, butter with 2 tablespoons of the butter
7. Fry in the butter until the paper is brown or approximately 15 minutes
8. Serve in the paper with creamed potatoes as a breakfast dish
