## Ingredients

- 150 grams thin, fine egg macaroni
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/3 liter boiled milk
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- canned fruit: apples, pears, and plums

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into even pieces, cook until soft in water, and drain on a sieve.
2. Melt the butter and flour together in a saucepan; gradually add the milk, and bring the mixture to a good boil.
3. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
4. Add lemon peel and sugar to taste.
5. Mix the well-drained macaroni into the mixture and let it simmer over heat until it thickens.
6. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
7. Arrange the fruit high on a dish with the cooled macaroni around it.
