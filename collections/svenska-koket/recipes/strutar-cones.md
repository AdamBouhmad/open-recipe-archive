## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour equal to the weight of 1 egg (slightly more)
- sugar equal to the weight of 2 eggs
- 1/4 tsp. powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. The eggs are whisked together with the sugar, and the flour and cinnamon are added.
2. The batter is spread out on a well-buttered baking sheet.
