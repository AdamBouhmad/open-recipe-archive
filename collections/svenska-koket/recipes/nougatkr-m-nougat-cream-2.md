## Ingredients

- 40 gr. sweet almonds
- 125 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 tsp. butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 50 gr. sugar
- 2 dcl. thin cream
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- nougat for garnish
- 1 hg. green grapes

## Directions

1. Blanch and finely chop the almonds.
2. Place the sugar in a slightly warm pan, greased with 1/2 tsp. butter, and stir with a wooden spoon over heat until melted.
3. Add the almonds at once and bring to a boil, then pour onto a well-cleaned and greased baking sheet.
4. When the nougat has cooled somewhat, cut it into even squares with a hack knife, grease with butter, and loosen from the sheet.
5. Finely crush half of the nougat in a stone mortar; reserve the other half for garnish.
6. For the cream, beat the egg yolks and sugar for 15 minutes.
7. Heat the thin cream with the vanilla bean and whip it vigorously into the beaten egg yolks.
8. Allow the cream to simmer over heat with continued whisking.
9. Remove the pan from heat and whisk the cream until cold.
10. Add the rinsed gelatin that has been dissolved in the hot water and the crushed nougat.
11. Last, gently fold in the cream that has been whipped to stiff peaks.
12. Pour the cream into a rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it stand on ice for about 3 hours.
13. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edges with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with parchment paper.
14. Garnish with the remaining nougat and grapes, and macaroons if desired.
