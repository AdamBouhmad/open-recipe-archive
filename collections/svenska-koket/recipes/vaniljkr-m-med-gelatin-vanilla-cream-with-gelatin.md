## Ingredients

- 6 blad gelatin
- 3 msk. vatten
- 1 ägg
- 110 gr. socker
- 1 1/2 dcl. mjölk
- 1 stång vanilj
- 2 äggulor
- 4 dcl. tjock grädde
- 1 msk. vaniljsocker (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut into pieces, dissolve in 3 tablespoons hot water and mix with the egg, egg yolks, sugar, milk and vanilla in a polished saucepan.
2. Whisk the mixture vigorously over the fire until the cream is thick and fluffy, then remove the saucepan and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Add the whipped heavy cream and pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let stand on ice 2-3 hours to set.
4. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn out onto a flat glass dish with pastry paper and garnish with fruit or biscuits.
5. Half of the cream can be colored green with ground pistachios, poured into the mold and allowed to set somewhat on ice, after which the rest of the light yellow cream is poured in. The cream is allowed to set on ice, turned out and served with raspberry or strawberry sauce.
6. The vanilla can be omitted and the cream flavored instead with crushed hazelnuts, cocoa, liqueur, punch, coffee, lemon or fruit.
