## Ingredients

- 6 poached eggs
- 6 slices wheat bread
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.) for sauce
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1 tbsp. good meat stock
- salt, white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg. smoked ham
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the bread slices into oblong shapes, hollow them out and fry them nicely golden brown in the butter.
2. Arrange them on a warm platter and place one egg on each slice.
3. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the cream and meat stock, then let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Season the sauce with the seasonings and pour it over the eggs.
5. Cut the ham into fine strips, fry it in the butter and sprinkle it in a ring around each egg.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
