## Ingredients

- 360 gr. wheat flour
- 1 1/2 tsk. bicarbonate of soda (4 1/2 gr.)
- 2 eggs
- 325 gr. yellow sugar
- 1 hg. melted butter
- 3 dcl. thick sour cream
- 80 gr. coarsely chopped sweet almonds
- 1 tsk. crushed cloves
- 1 tsk. crushed cinnamon
- 1 tsk. crushed ginger
- 1 tsk. crushed cardamom
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 3 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and mix well with the bicarbonate of soda.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar for 15 minutes.
3. Add the cooled melted butter, the cream beaten to stiff peaks, the almonds, and the spices.
4. Fold in the flour last and pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined large ring or bread pan and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 45 minutes.
