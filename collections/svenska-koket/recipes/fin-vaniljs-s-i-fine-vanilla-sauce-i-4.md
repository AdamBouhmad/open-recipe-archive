## Ingredients

- 5 parts thick cream
- 1/4 vanilla pod
- 1 1/2 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 3-4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Half of the cream is boiled together with the vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, after which the saucepan is lifted from the heat.
2. One egg yolk at a time is whisked in, and the sauce is placed over heat again to simmer under whisking.
