## Ingredients

- 2 large root celeries
- 100 g beef
- 100 g veal
- 50 g lard
- 1 tablespoon crushed bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped pickled onion
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Scrape and rinse the celeries well, and cook them half-tender in water.
2. Remove them, cut out a lid, and hollow out the celeries from the top.
3. Fill them with well-worked and seasoned ground meat, and place the lids on.
4. Heat the butter in a pot, and place the celeries in it.
5. Let them slowly fry with a tightly closed lid while basting with good broth, until the celeries are completely soft and the meat is cooked through.
6. Remove them and serve with the sauce.
