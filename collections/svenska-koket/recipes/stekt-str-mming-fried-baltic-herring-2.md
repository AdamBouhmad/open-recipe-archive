## Ingredients

- 30 Baltic herrings
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed crackers
- 80 grams butter for frying
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams) for sauce
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, remove the backbone, scrape away the dark membrane carefully and rinse very well, then wrap in a fish towel.
2. Rub it with salt, dip in the beaten egg and turn in a mixture of flour and crushed crackers.
3. Heat the butter and fry the herring until golden.
4. For the sauce, mix the butter with chopped parsley and lemon juice and serve alongside.
