## Ingredients

- 75 gr. flour
- 1/2 dl. water (scant)
- 125 gr. fine butter
- 40 gr. fine, grated cheese
- 1 egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff pastry as in recipe no. 33.
2. The last time it is rolled out, sprinkle a little of the grated cheese on the rolled-out dough and repeat this until the dough is fully rolled out, then let it rest 1 hour.
3. Roll it out to an oblong strip of 1 cm. thickness, which is cut out into 8 cm. long sticks.
4. Twist them 2 turns or leave them straight, brush with egg yolk, place on a well-cleaned baking sheet and bake to a golden-brown color in a warm oven.
5. Serve with consommé or clear broth.
