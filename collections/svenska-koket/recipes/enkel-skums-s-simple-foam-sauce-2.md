## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- zest of 0.5 lemon
- 3 parts white wine
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Sugar, flour, lemon zest and wine are whisked over strong heat with a stiff whisk until the mixture thickens.
2. The pan is removed from the heat.
3. The eggs are added one at a time with vigorous whisking.
4. The sauce is poured up, and whisking is continued a little while longer.
