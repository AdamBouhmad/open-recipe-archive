## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 300 gr. butter or margarine
- 2-4 cup granulated sugar
- ½ hg. sweet almonds
- 1 egg for brushing
- Almond paste (choice of two recipes): Recipe I: 4 hg. sweet almonds, 25 gr. bitter almonds, 4 hg. granulated sugar, 2 tsp. ground cinnamon, 100 gr. butter, 1 liter thin cream, filling from the buns. Recipe II: 4 hg. sweet almonds, 25 gr. bitter almonds, 4 hg. granulated sugar, 1 liter thick cream, filling from the buns

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for fine wheat bread (no. 642).
2. Shape it into round buns.
3. When they have risen well, brush them with egg and bake in good oven heat.
4. When they are cold, cut a lid from each bun, remove some of the filling, and fill the buns with almond paste.
5. For Almond Paste I: Blanch the almonds and grate them, then pound them with the sugar to a fine paste. Boil the cream and butter, stir in the almond paste, cinnamon, and filling, and when the mixture is well combined, fill it into the buns.
6. For Almond Paste II: Blanch the almonds and grate them, then pound them in a mortar together with the sugar. Mix in the cream whipped to stiff peaks together with the filling. When the mixture is well combined, fill it into the buns.
