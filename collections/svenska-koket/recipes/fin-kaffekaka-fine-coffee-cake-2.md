## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- powdered sugar equal in weight to the eggs
- wheat flour, 1/2 the weight of the eggs
- lemon zest or vanilla sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the pan (10 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and sugar with a steel whisk for about 45 minutes.
2. Sift the flour and add it, then season the batter with 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest or 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar.
3. Pour into a buttered and crumb-lined ring mold or cake pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 40 minutes.
