## Ingredients

- 1.5 hectograms macaroni
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 15 tablespoons tomato puree

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into appropriately sized pieces and cook completely tender in salted water.
2. Drain off the water, add cold butter and tomato puree, and serve the macaroni with fried meat.
