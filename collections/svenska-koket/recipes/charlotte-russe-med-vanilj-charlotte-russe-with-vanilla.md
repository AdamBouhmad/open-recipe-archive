## Ingredients

- 0.25 liters thick cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 75 gr. sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 10 gr. gelatin
- sponge fingers
- 0.75 egg white

## Directions

1. Boil half of the cream, place the vanilla pod in it, and let the cream stand by the side of the heat until it has taken on a good vanilla flavor, then remove the vanilla pod.
2. Add the sugar and egg yolks, and whip everything together over heat until the cream begins to simmer and thicken.
3. Remove the pan from heat and continue whisking until the cream has cooled.
4. Then add the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the remaining cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
5. Line a mold with sponge fingers, which on the inside have been lightly brushed with lightly whipped egg white.
6. Pour the cream in and set out on ice to stiffen.
7. When the pudding is to be turned out, dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water.
