## Ingredients

- 750 gr rhubarb
- Sugar at a ratio of 750 gr per kg of rhubarb

## Directions

1. The stems are washed, cut into pieces, weighed and layered alternately with sugar in an enameled preserving pan.
2. The mixture is then cooked while stirring until it falls in flakes from the spoon.
3. Poured into small warm jars, which are covered when the marmalade is cold.
