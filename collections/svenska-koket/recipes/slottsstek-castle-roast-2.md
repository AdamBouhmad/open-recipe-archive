## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg inside round of beef
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 liter water
- 8 allspice berries
- 4 white peppercorns
- 1/2 tablespoon syrup
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 3 anchovies
- 1 small red onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1/2 deciliter cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roast with a cloth dipped in hot water
2. Brown it in the heated butter, then salt it and add boiling water
3. Add the spices, cleaned anchovies, and peeled but whole onion
4. Let the roast simmer slowly for approximately 3 hours
5. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
