## Ingredients

- 80 fine, even sugar beets
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 1/2 lemon
- 2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Scrape the sugar beets, rinse well and let soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Bundle them together, 6 in each bundle.
3. Place them in boiling salted water mixed with the juice of 1/2 lemon and let cook until they are soft, which is tested with a whisk.
4. Remove them with a slotted spoon, place on a long platter with a folded napkin.
5. Serve very hot with melted butter or hollandaise mixed with the juice of 1 blood orange.
