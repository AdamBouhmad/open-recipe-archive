## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 small fat herring
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 3 dcl. boiling water
- 3 gingerbread cookies
- 2 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; keep the head but remove the gills
2. Clean the unsoaked herring, rinse, dry, and place inside the pike
3. Brush with egg and coat with crushed rusks
4. Grease a long pan with half the cold butter in an area sized to the fish; place the pike in and dot with remaining butter
5. Roast in a hot oven for about 1/2 hour, basting often and adding water as needed
6. Crush the gingerbread finely and mix with cream
7. Remove the herring from the pike and place on a warm platter, garnish with parsley
8. Deglaze the pan sauce with the dissolved gingerbread mixture, bring to a boil, strain, and serve in a separate sauce bowl
