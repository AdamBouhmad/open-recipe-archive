## Ingredients

- 210 gr. fine butter
- 4 eggs
- 150 gr. sugar
- 150 gr. flour
- zest and juice of 1/2 small lemon
- 1/2 msk. butter for the iron (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Stir the butter until it becomes white and fluffy, then add the egg yolks, sugar, flour and lemon zest and stir the mixture 1/2 hour.
2. Last, fold in the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Cook the waffles and serve as in recipe no. 775.
4. Grease the iron only for the first waffles.
