## Ingredients

- 20 ripe apricots
- 6 dcl sugar syrup
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Split the apricots and remove the stones
2. Crush the stones and save the kernels
3. Cook the kernels in the sugar syrup
4. Let the syrup cool and strain it
5. Pass the apricots through a sieve
6. Mix them into the syrup together with the lemon juice
