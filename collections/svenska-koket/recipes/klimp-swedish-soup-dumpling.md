## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr.)
- 4 deciliters boiling milk
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 6 almonds (2 bitter)
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes
2. milk is added and the mixture is allowed to boil while stirring for 10 minutes
3. sugar, the scalded and grated almond, and the whisked eggs are added and the mass is allowed to simmer over heat until it becomes properly thick
4. the klimp is poured into a water-rinsed mold and allowed to cool
5. it is loosened carefully at the edge, turned out onto a plate or round dish and served to broth or fruit soups
6. if desired, it is sprinkled with 1 tablespoon chopped parsley when used with broth, otherwise it is garnished with 20 gr. scalded sweet almond, cut into strips
