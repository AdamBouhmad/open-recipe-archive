## Ingredients

- 1 calf tongue
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 piece celery
- 1/2 leek
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 4 deciliter tongue broth
- 1/2 teaspoon meat extract
- 1 tablespoon capers or 3 olives
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt
- white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt for breading
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil for breading
- 1 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue in cold water for a few hours, then blanch in hot water and scrape well.
2. Place in boiling salted water and skim the broth well when it comes to a boil, then add the prepared vegetables and let the tongue cook slowly until tender, approximately 2 hours.
3. Remove the skin and let the tongue cool.
4. Cut into fine dice.
5. Strain and skim the broth, and mix 3 deciliters of it with the meat extract.
6. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
7. Stir in the tongue and finely chopped capers or olives together with the whisked egg yolks and rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water.
8. Season the stew with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
9. Form the croquettes into elongated shapes.
10. Serve as a course with peas or mixed vegetables.
