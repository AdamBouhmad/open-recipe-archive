## Ingredients

- 250 gr. lean, fresh pork
- 2 msk. crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 dcl. cream
- 2 dcl. water
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 kkp crushed breadcrumbs for breading
- 2 1/2 msk. butter for frying
- 1 1/2 dcl. broth or water

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and grind 6 times through the meat grinder.
2. Whisk the egg with the cream and water and add the crushed breadcrumbs to it.
3. Add the breadcrumb mixture little by little to the forcemeat, which is worked for 1 hour.
4. Season well with the spices and the finely chopped parsley and let stand until the next day.
5. Stir the forcemeat 15 minutes, form into oblong round cutlets, which are turned in crushed breadcrumbs and fried a nice brown in butter.
6. Wipe out the pan with a little broth and pour the sauce over the cutlets.
7. Serve with preserved peas or browned potatoes as the course.
