## Ingredients

- 1 hg. dried apples, pears, or apricots
- 5 dcl. water
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 tsp. potato starch (5 gr.)
- lemon zest or lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the dried fruit in several portions of lukewarm water and let it soak for a few hours in the water in which it will be cooked.
2. Add the sugar and cook until the fruit is soft.
3. Press the fruit through a sieve to make a purée.
4. Thicken the purée with potato starch mixed with 1 tsp. cold water and let it boil for 2 minutes.
5. Season and add a little lemon zest or lemon juice if desired.
6. Serve warm or cold.
