## Ingredients

- 450 gr. small, fresh mushrooms
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 30 gr. butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. The mushrooms are cleaned, as soon as they are picked, in such a way that the scales are removed carefully, the outer skin is not removed from tender mushrooms, the stems are cut off level with the caps, whereupon the mushroom is quickly rinsed and dried on a cloth.
2. The caps are nicely fluted with a sharp knife, placed immediately in boiling water, seasoned with salt, butter and lemon juice and are allowed to cook 5-10 minutes.
3. They are taken out with a slotted spoon and used as garnish for finer dishes.
4. The mushrooms can be stored 2-3 days in a cool place, if they are covered with butter-spread so-called butter paper.
5. The broth is used for sauces and stews.
6. Small French, canned mushrooms can be fluted in a similar way, placed in their own broth, seasoned with 1 tablespoon butter, allowed to cook about 2 minutes and used as above.
